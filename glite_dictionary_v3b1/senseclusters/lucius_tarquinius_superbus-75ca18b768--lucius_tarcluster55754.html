Lucius Tarquinius Superbus

proper noun
legendary last king of Rome
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈluːʃəs tɑːrˈkwɪni.əs suːˈpɜːrbəs / (AmE), / ˈluːsi.əs tɑːˈkwɪni.əs suːˈpɜːbəs / (BrE)
the seventh and last legendary king of Rome, reputed for his tyranny, who was expelled from the throne and reigned from 534 to 510 BC.
