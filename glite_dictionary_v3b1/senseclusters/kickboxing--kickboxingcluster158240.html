kickboxing

noun
martial arts sport
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪkˌbɑksɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɪkˌbɒksɪŋ / (BrE)
a sport or exercise that combines kicking and punching techniques, often practiced for fitness or competition

Examples

Kickboxing class
She attends kickboxing classes to stay in shape.
Many people enjoy kickboxing as a fun way to improve their fitness and coordination.
The kickboxing tournament attracted athletes from all over the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boxing - a sport where two people fight using only their fists
  • synonym: karate - a Japanese martial art using punches, kicks, and open-handed techniques
  • antonym: wrestling - a sport involving grappling and throws without striking
