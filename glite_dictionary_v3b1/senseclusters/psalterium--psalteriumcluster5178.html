psalterium

noun
third stomach compartment of ruminant
9.9
Pronunciation: /sɑːl.ˈtɪr.iː.əm/ /; / /sɔːl.ˈtɪr.iː.əm/ (AmE), /sɒl.ˈtɪə.riəm/ /; / /sɔːl.ˈtɪə.riəm/ (BrE)
