signorial

adjective
of a lord's authority
Pronunciation: / saɪˈnɔːriəl / (AmE), / sɪˈnɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to a lord or the authority and rights of a lord, especially in feudal systems

Examples

Signorial rights
The peasants were subject to various signorial duties under the local lord.
Many medieval villages had a signorial manor at their center.
The historian studied the signorial privileges granted during the feudal era.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feudal - relating to the social system of feudalism
  • synonym: lordly - characteristic of or befitting a lord
