Scheherazade

noun
skilled storyteller
Pronunciation: / ʃəˌhɛrəˈzɑːd / (AmE), / ʃəˌhɛərəˈzɑːd / (BrE)
a person, especially a woman, who is an entertaining or skillful storyteller

Examples

A modern Scheherazade
With her vivid imagination, she became the Scheherazade of every family gathering.
The author was often called a modern Scheherazade for her captivating novels.
He listened, spellbound, as the Scheherazade of the group spun another fantastic tale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raconteur - a person who tells anecdotes in a skillful and amusing way
  • synonym: storyteller - someone who tells stories, especially as a profession
