GIF

noun
animated image file
Pronunciation: / dʒɪf / (AmE), / dʒɪf / (BrE)
a digital image file format, often used for short animations or simple graphics online

Examples

Funny GIF online
He replied to the message with a hilarious GIF that made everyone laugh.
You can easily find a GIF to express almost any emotion online.
The website allows users to upload and share their favorite GIFs with friends.
He attached a funny GIF to the email to make his colleagues laugh.
The website's logo was saved as a GIF to keep the file size small.
Many social media users share animated GIFs to express their reactions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: JPEG - a common digital image file format for photographs
  • synonym: PNG - a digital image file format supporting lossless compression and transparency
  • synonym: meme - an image, video, or phrase spread widely online, often humorous
