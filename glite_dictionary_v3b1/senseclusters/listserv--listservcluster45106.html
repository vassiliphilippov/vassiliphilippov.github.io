listserv

noun
email list software
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪstˌsɜrv / (AmE), / ˈlɪstˌsɜːv / (BrE)
a service or software for managing group email lists, or the email list itself used for group communication

Examples

A listserv email
I joined the department listserv to receive updates about upcoming events and meetings.
The teacher sent the assignment details to the class listserv so everyone would get the message.
If you want to unsubscribe from the listserv, follow the instructions at the bottom of the email.
If you want to stay informed about upcoming events, join the club's listserv.
The professor sent the assignment details to the entire class through the listserv.
Please reply to the listserv so everyone can see your response.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mailing list - a list of email addresses used to send messages to multiple people
  • synonym: newsletter - a regular email sent to subscribers with news or updates
