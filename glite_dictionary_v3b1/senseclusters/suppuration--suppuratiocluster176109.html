suppuration

noun
formation or discharge of pus
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌpjəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsʌpjʊˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of forming or discharging pus, usually because of infection or inflammation

Examples

Chronic suppuration may be a sign of an underlying infection.

Chronic suppuration often indicates an ongoing infection that needs to be treated.
