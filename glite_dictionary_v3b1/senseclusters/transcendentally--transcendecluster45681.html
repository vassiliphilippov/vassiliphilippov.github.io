transcendentally

adverb
in a transcendental manner
Pronunciation: / trænˌsɛnˈdɛntəli / (AmE), / trænˌsɛnˈdɛntəli / (BrE)
In a transcendental manner; to a transcendental extent.

Examples

Understood transcendentally by mind
Mathematics is sometimes described as being understood transcendentally by the mind.
Some believe that truth can only be known transcendentally, beyond mere sensory experience.
The philosopher argued transcendentally about the nature of reality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transcendently - in a way that surpasses ordinary limits; exceedingly
