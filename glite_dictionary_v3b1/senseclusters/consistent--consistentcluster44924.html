consistent

adjective
capable of being reproduced; reliable in results
1.0
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɪstənt / (AmE), / kənˈsɪstənt / (BrE)
(of results or actions) capable of being reproduced with the same outcome; yielding similar or reliable results each time.

Examples

Consistent results
The scientist was pleased that the experiment produced consistent results across multiple trials.
These findings are only valuable if they are consistent every time the procedure is repeated.
We need to ensure our testing method is consistent so the data can be trusted.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reproducible - (of results) able to be duplicated under the same conditions
