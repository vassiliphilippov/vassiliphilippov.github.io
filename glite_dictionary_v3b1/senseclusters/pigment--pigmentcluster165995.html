pigment

noun
substance used to color or coat surfaces
5.4 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪɡ.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈpɪɡ.mənt/ (BrE)
a substance or powder used to give color to paints, inks, cosmetics, or other materials

Examples

Pigment powder for paint
After mixing the pigment with oil, he applied it as a thick paint on the canvas.
Some protective coatings contain a waterproof pigment that extends the life of the surface.
The artist carefully chose each pigment to achieve the perfect shade for her landscape painting.
Some cosmetics contain natural pigment extracted from plants and minerals.
The artist carefully blended the red pigment with oil to create a vibrant color.
The restoration process involved matching the original pigment used in the ancient frescoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dye - a substance used to change the color of something (especially textiles)
  • synonym: paint - a colored substance spread over a surface that dries to leave a thin decorative or protective coating
