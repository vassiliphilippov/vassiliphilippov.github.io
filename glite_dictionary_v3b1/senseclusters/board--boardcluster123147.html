board

noun
display surface for public information
1.0 14/m
Pronunciation: / b…îrd / (AmE), / b…îÀêd / (BrE)
a flat, usually vertical surface on which information is displayed for public viewing, such as a notice board or bulletin board

Examples

Notice board
Please check the notice board for your exam schedule.
The announcement was posted on the board near the entrance.
They pinned their holiday photos to the board in the kitchen.
