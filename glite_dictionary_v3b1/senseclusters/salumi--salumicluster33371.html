salumi

noun
Italian cured meats
7.4
Pronunciation: / sɑːˈluːmi / (AmE), / sæˈluːmi / (BrE)
Italian cured meats, such as prosciutto, salami, and similar products, often served as appetizers.

Examples

Italian salumi platter

A platter of salumi and cheeses is perfect for sharing at parties.
