sky dive

verb
jump from an airplane with acrobatics before parachuting
Pronunciation: / skaɪ daɪv / (AmE), / skaɪ daɪv / (BrE)
to jump from an airplane and perform acrobatic maneuvers before opening a parachute

Examples

To sky dive from plane
He has always wanted to sky dive over the Grand Canyon.
Many thrill-seekers learn to sky dive as a hobby.
The instructor showed us how to sky dive safely before our first jump.
