blue daisy

noun
flowering plant with blue petals
Pronunciation: / bluː ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / bluː ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
a hairy subshrub native to South Africa or Australia, with daisylike flowers that have blue petals

Examples

A blue daisy flower
I planted a blue daisy next to the roses to add a splash of color.
Many South African gardens feature the blue daisy as a decorative perennial.
The blue daisy is prized for its vibrant blue flowers in garden borders.
