throne sniffer

noun
sycophant to powerful people
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊn ˌsnɪfɚ / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊn ˌsnɪfə / (BrE)
A person who excessively flatters or fawns over those in positions of authority or power in order to gain favor.

Examples

Throne sniffer flatters boss
He gained a reputation as a throne sniffer after always agreeing with the boss.
People disliked the throne sniffer for always flattering those in charge.
Rather than speaking his mind, he chose the path of a throne sniffer at court.
