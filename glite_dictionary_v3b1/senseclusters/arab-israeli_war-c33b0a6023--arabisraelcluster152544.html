Arab-Israeli War

noun
1967 war between Israel and Arab states
Pronunciation: / ɛrəb ɪzˈreɪli wɔr / (AmE), / ˈærəb ɪzˈreɪli wɔː / (BrE)
A brief war fought in June 1967 between Israel and neighboring Arab countries, resulting in a major Israeli victory and increased regional influence.

Examples

1967 Arab-Israeli War
Many books analyze the causes and outcomes of the Arab-Israeli War.
The Arab-Israeli War of 1967 dramatically altered the map of the Middle East.
Veterans of the Arab-Israeli War often recount their personal experiences during the conflict.
