headstand

noun
balancing on one's head (often with hands)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈhɛd.stænd / (BrE)
an act of balancing on one's head, typically with the support of the hands

Examples

A headstand pose
Children attempted a headstand on the grass, laughing as they toppled over.
She practiced her headstand every morning as part of her yoga routine.
The gymnast held a perfect headstand for several seconds during her routine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: handstand - an acrobatic position where one balances on the hands with the body inverted
