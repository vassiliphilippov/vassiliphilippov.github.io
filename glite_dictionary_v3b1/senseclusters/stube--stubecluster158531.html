stube

noun
cozy room or tavern
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːbə / (AmE), / ˈʃtuːbə / (BrE)
a small, cozy room or tavern, especially found in German-speaking regions

Examples

A cozy stube
After a long hike, they relaxed in a traditional stube with hot drinks and local food.
The old village stube was filled with laughter and the smell of fresh bread.
Locals often gather at the stube to share stories and enjoy each other's company.
