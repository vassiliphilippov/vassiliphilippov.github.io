tumblerful

noun
glassful amount
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌmblərˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈtʌmbləˌfʊl / (BrE)
the amount of liquid that fills a tumbler glass

Examples

A tumblerful of juice
She drank a tumblerful of orange juice with her breakfast.
He offered his guest a tumblerful of cold water on a hot day.
The recipe calls for a tumblerful of milk to be added at the end.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cupful - the amount that fills a cup
  • synonym: glassful - the amount that fills a glass
