spermatogonium

noun
sperm-producing cell
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌspɜːrmətəˈɡoʊniəm / (AmE), / ˌspɜːmətəˈɡəʊniəm / (BrE)
a cell in the testes that divides to produce sperm cells during the process of sperm formation (spermatogenesis)
