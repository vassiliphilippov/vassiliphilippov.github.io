heaviness

noun
persistent emotional sadness
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvi.nəs /; / ˈhɛvinəs / (AmE), / ˈhɛv.i.nɪs /; / ˈhɛvinɪs / (BrE)
a feeling of sadness, emotional burden, or being weighed down mentally

Examples

Heaviness in the heart
A sense of heaviness filled the room during the memorial service.
She couldn't shake the heaviness that settled over her after the breakup.
Writing about her feelings helped ease the heaviness in her heart.
He spoke of the heaviness he felt after losing his job.
The heaviness of her responsibilities made it difficult for her to enjoy the holiday.
There's a certain heaviness in the air whenever bad news is about to be delivered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burden - a heavy load or duty; a cause of hardship
  • antonym: lightness - The quality of having little weight; also relief or lack of emotional burden
  • synonym: sadness - The state of being unhappy or sorrowful
