kilometer

noun
metric unit of length (1,000 metres)
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɑːmɪtər /; / kɪˈlɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / kɪˈlɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a metric unit of length equal to 1,000 metres or approximately 0.62 miles

Examples

Ten kilometers distance
It is about ten kilometers from my house to the city center.
The marathon course is over forty-two kilometers long.
They measured the distance and found it was exactly one kilometer.
He ran a marathon, which is just over forty-two kilometres long.
Speed limits in many countries are measured in kilometres per hour.
The nearest town is five kilometres away from here.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meter - the base unit of length in the metric system
  • antonym: mile - imperial unit of distance equal to 1,609.34 metres
