railway locomotive

noun
engine-powered train vehicle
1.9 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪl.weɪ ˌloʊ.kəˈmoʊ.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈreɪl.weɪ ˌləʊ.kəˈməʊ.tɪv / (BrE)
