pleasantry

noun
an agreeable or amusing remark
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛzəntri / (AmE), / ˈplɛzəntri / (BrE)

Examples

Their conversation was filled with light pleasantries and easy laughter.
We exchanged pleasantries before beginning the meeting.
