crowder

noun
variety of cowpea
Pronunciation: / ˈkraʊdər / (AmE), / ˈkraʊdə / (BrE)
a type of cowpea with seeds that are crowded together in the pod, commonly eaten as a vegetable in the southern United States

Examples

Cooked crowder beans
She cooked a pot of fresh crowder with bacon and onions for supper.
Farmers in the South often grow crowder for their hearty flavor and high yield.
A bowl of seasoned crowder is a traditional side dish at many family gatherings.
