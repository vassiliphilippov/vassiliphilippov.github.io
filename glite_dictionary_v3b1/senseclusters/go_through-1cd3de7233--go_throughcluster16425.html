go through

verb
undergo a difficult experience
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˈθruː / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈθruː / (BrE)
to experience or endure a difficult or unpleasant situation, especially over a period of time

Examples

To go through hard times
After losing his job, he had to go through a very tough period.
She went through a painful divorce last year.
We all go through challenges at some point in our lives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endure - to suffer patiently through something difficult or unpleasant
  • synonym: undergo - to experience or be subjected to something (usually unpleasant or difficult)
