acetanilid

noun

Pronunciation: /ə.siː.tə.ˈnɪl.ɪd/ /; / /æ.sɪ.tə.ˈnɪl.ɪd/ (AmE), /ə.siː.tə.ˈnɪl.ɪd/ /; / /æ.sɪ.tə.ˈnɪl.ɪd/ (BrE)
A white, odorless crystalline compound formerly used as a painkiller and currently used in the manufacture of dyes, rubber accelerators, and other chemicals.
