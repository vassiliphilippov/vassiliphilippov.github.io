Zuiderzee

proper noun
former inlet of the North Sea in the Netherlands
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈzaɪ.dər.zeɪ/ /; / /ˈzaɪ.dər.ziː/ (AmE), /ˈzaɪ.də.zeɪ/ /; / /ˈzaɪ.də.ziː/ (BrE)
