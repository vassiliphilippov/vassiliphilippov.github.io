fire marshal

noun
head or official of a fire department
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ ˌmɑrʃəl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌmɑːʃəl / (BrE)
an official responsible for fire safety, investigating fires, and enforcing fire regulations
