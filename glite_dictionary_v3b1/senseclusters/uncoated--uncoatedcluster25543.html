uncoated

adjective
not having a coating
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkəʊtɪd / (BrE)
Not covered with a layer of another material; lacking a coating.

Examples

Uncoated cookware
I prefer uncoated cookware because it does not contain synthetic chemicals.
The pills are given in uncoated form to dissolve more quickly.
We print our brochures on uncoated paper for a more natural feel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coated - covered with a layer of another material
