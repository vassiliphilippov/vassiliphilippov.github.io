ready

verb
prepare or make suitable in advance
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdi / (AmE), / ˈrɛdi / (BrE)
to make something or someone prepared or suitable for use, action, or a particular purpose; to prepare

Examples

To ready the team
She spent the morning readying the room for her guests.
The chef readied the ingredients before the dinner rush.
They are readying themselves for the big presentation tomorrow.
Could you ready the soup while I set the table?
He readied breakfast quickly so we wouldn't be late.
She readied the vegetables for roasting before the guests arrived.

  • synonym: equip - to provide with what is needed for use or action
  • synonym: heat - to make something hot or warm, especially food
  • synonym: prepare - to make ready for use or consideration
