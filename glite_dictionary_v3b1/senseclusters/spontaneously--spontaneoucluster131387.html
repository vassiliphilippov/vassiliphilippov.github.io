spontaneously

adverb
without advance preparation
4.7 3.6
Pronunciation: /spɑːn.ˈteɪ.niː.əs.liː/ /; / /spɑːn.ˈteɪ.nɪ.əs.lɪ/ (AmE), /spɒn.ˈteɪ.niː.əs.liː/ /; / /spɒn.ˈteɪ.nɪ.əs.lɪ/ (BrE)
in a natural, unplanned, or unforced way; without advance preparation or being made to do it

Examples

The children started singing spontaneously, surprising their parents with an impromptu concert.
