electroencephalographic

adjective
relating to an electroencephalograph
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊɪnˌsɛfələˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊɪnˌsɛfələˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving an electroencephalograph or the recording of electrical activity in the brain

Examples

An electroencephalographic study
An electroencephalographic study was performed to investigate the patient's seizures.
Researchers compared the electroencephalographic patterns of alert and sleeping subjects.
The neurologist analyzed the electroencephalographic data for signs of abnormal brain activity.
