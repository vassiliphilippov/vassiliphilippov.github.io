alkalify

verb
make or become more alkaline
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.kə.ləˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈal.kə.lɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
to make or become more alkaline; to decrease the acidity of a substance

Examples

To alkalify a solution
Adding baking soda will alkalify the solution, reducing its acidity.
The process of alkalifying water can improve its suitability for certain industrial uses.
They alkalified the soil to make it more suitable for gardening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neutralize - to make chemically neutral
