intra-arterial

adjective
within an artery
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrə ɑrˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrə ɑːˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
occurring within or administered directly into an artery

Examples

Intra-arterial injection
The doctor used an intra-arterial catheter to monitor the patient's blood pressure continuously.
Certain cancer treatments are delivered through intra-arterial infusion for targeted therapy.
An intra-arterial injection can provide rapid delivery of medication to specific organs.
