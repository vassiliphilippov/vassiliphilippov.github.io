stupid person

noun
person lacking intelligence
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːpɪd ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈstjuːpɪd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who lacks intelligence or shows poor judgment

Examples

A stupid person
Calling someone a stupid person is both rude and unhelpful.
He acted like a stupid person when he ignored all the warning signs.
No one wants to be thought of as a stupid person at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who behaves unwisely or imprudently
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual ability
  • synonym: idiot - a person of very low intelligence
