expostulation

noun
expression of strong protest or objection
9.2
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌspɑstʃəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˌspɒstjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
an act or expression of strong disagreement or protest, especially in an earnest or reasoned way
