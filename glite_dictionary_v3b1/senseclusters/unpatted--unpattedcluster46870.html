unpatted

adjective
not checked by patting
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpætɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpætɪd / (BrE)
not having been checked or searched by patting with the hands, especially for weapons or contraband

Examples

Unpatted bags at security
The security guard noticed the unpatted bags left near the entrance.
Passengers with unpatted coats were asked to step aside for further screening.
He realized his backpack was still unpatted after passing through the checkpoint.

Related Terms

  • antonym: patted - having been checked by patting with the hands
  • antonym: searched - having been checked for prohibited items
