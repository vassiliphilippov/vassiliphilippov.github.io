defrauder

noun
person who commits fraud
9.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfrɔdər / (AmE), / dɪˈfrɔːdə / (BrE)
a person who gains money or advantage by deceiving others or committing fraud

Examples

A defrauder of money
Laws are in place to protect investors from any potential defrauder.
She lost a considerable amount of money to a skilled defrauder.
The police arrested the defrauder after months of careful investigation.
