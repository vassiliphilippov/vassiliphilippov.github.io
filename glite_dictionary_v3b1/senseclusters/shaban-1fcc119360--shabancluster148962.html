Sha'ban

noun
eighth month in the Islamic calendar
9.3
Pronunciation: / ʃɑːˈbɑːn / (AmE), / ʃɑːˈbɑːn / (BrE)

Examples

Sha'ban month
Many Muslims prepare for Ramadan during Sha'ban by increasing their acts of worship.
Some people fast during Sha'ban as a tradition before the holy month of Ramadan.
The date of Sha'ban changes each year because the Islamic calendar is lunar.
