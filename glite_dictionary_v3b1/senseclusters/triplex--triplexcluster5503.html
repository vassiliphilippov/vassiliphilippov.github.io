triplex

noun
three-unit building
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪˌplɛks / (AmE), / ˈtrɪplɛks / (BrE)
a building divided into three separate homes or apartments, each with its own entrance

Examples

A triplex building
They decided to invest in a triplex so they could live in one unit and rent the others.
The city has many triplexes in residential neighborhoods, offering housing for several families.
After renovating the old triplex, the owner quickly found tenants for all three apartments.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: apartment building - a building containing multiple separate apartments
  • degree lower: duplex - a building divided into two separate homes or apartments
