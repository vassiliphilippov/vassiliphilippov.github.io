Ascension of the Lord

noun
Christian feast marking Jesus's ascension
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛnʃən əv ðə lɔrd / (AmE), / əˈsɛnʃən əv ðə lɔːd / (BrE)
(in Christianity) the celebration of Jesus Christ's ascension into heaven, observed on the 40th day after Easter.

Examples

The Ascension of the Lord celebration
Many Christians observe the Ascension of the Lord forty days after Easter Sunday.
The church was beautifully decorated for the Ascension of the Lord celebration.
We attended Mass to mark the Ascension of the Lord with our parish community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ascension Day - Christian festival commemorating Christ's ascension
