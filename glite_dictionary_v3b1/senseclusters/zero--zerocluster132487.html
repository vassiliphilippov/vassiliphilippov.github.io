zero

noun
sight setting for firearm accuracy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈziɹoʊ / (AmE), / ˈzɪərəʊ / (BrE)
the sight adjustment on a firearm that causes a projectile to hit the center of the target under standard conditions, such as with no wind.

Examples

Rifle zero
Adjusting your zero is essential when environmental conditions change.
After cleaning his firearm, he checked the zero to maintain shooting accuracy.
Before firing, make sure the rifle's zero is properly set for this range.
