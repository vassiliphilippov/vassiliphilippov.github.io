order Temnospondyli

noun
extinct order of prehistoric amphibians
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər tɛmnəˈspɒndɪlaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə tɛmˌnɒspɒnˈdɪlaɪ / (BrE)
a group of large, extinct amphibians that lived during the Carboniferous and Permian periods, characterized by vertebrae with some unfused elements
