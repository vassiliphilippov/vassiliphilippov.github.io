button fern

noun
fern with buttonlike leaflets
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtən fɝn /; / ˈbʌt.ən fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈbʌtən fɜːn /; / ˈbʌt.ən fɜːn / (BrE)
