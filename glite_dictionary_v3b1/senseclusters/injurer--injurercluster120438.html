injurer

noun
person causing harm
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndʒərər / (AmE), / ˈɪndʒərə / (BrE)
a person who causes harm or injury to another

Examples

The injurer pays
The court decided that the injurer must pay for the damages caused.
Insurance companies often investigate to determine who the injurer is in an accident.
The injurer apologized to the person he had accidentally hurt.
The injurer was required to pay compensation to the victim.
In court, the lawyer argued that the injurer acted negligently.
Laws are designed to hold the injurer accountable for their actions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offender - a person who commits an illegal act
  • antonym: victim - a person who is harmed or injured
