annoyingness

noun
quality of being irritating
4.7
Pronunciation: / əˈnɔɪɪŋnəs / (AmE), / əˈnɔɪɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being irritating or causing mild anger

Examples

The annoyingness of noise
The annoyingness of the buzzing mosquito kept me awake all night.
He apologized for the annoyingness of his constant interruptions during the meeting.
Some people are bothered by the annoyingness of loud chewing in public places.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irritation - the state of being annoyed or made slightly angry
  • antonym: pleasantness - the quality of being enjoyable or pleasing
