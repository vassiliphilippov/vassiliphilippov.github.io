nonplastic

adjective
without plastic
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈplæstɪk / (AmE), / nɒnˈplæstɪk / (BrE)
not made of or containing synthetic plastic materials

Examples

Nonplastic packaging
The company switched to nonplastic packaging to reduce environmental impact.
Many customers prefer nonplastic straws made from paper or metal.
She bought a set of nonplastic containers for her kitchen.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plastic - made of or containing plastic
  • synonym: plastic-free - not containing any plastic
