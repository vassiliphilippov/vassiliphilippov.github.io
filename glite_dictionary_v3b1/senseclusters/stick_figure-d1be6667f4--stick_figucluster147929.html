stick figure

noun
very simple drawing using lines and circles
Pronunciation: / stɪk ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / stɪk ˈfɪɡjə / (BrE)
a simple drawing of a person or animal, with the head as a circle and the body and limbs as straight lines
