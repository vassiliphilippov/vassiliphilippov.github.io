satisfiability

noun
ability to be satisfied
Pronunciation: / ˌsætɪsfaɪəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌsætɪsfaɪəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being able to be satisfied or fulfilled, especially in meeting requirements or conditions

Examples

Satisfiability of requirements
The satisfiability of the contract terms was discussed at length during the meeting.
Engineers tested the satisfiability of the new safety requirements before implementation.
The satisfiability of customer demands is crucial for the success of any business.

Related Terms

  • antonym: impossibility - the state of not being possible or achievable
  • synonym: satisfaction - the state of being pleased or content with something
