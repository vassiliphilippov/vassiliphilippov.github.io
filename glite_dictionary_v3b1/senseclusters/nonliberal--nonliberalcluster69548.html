nonliberal

adjective
opposed to liberalism
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈlɪbərəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈlɪbərəl / (BrE)
not supporting or characterized by liberal ideas, policies, or values

Examples

Nonliberal policies
The government introduced several nonliberal policies restricting freedom of the press.
Her nonliberal views on education set her apart from her colleagues.
Critics argued that the reforms were too nonliberal for a modern democracy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conservative - favoring traditional views and values; opposed to change
  • antonym: liberal - supporting individual freedoms and progressive reforms
