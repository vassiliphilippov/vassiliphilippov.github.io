mason wasp

noun
solitary wasp building mud nests
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪsən wɑsp / (AmE), / ˈmeɪsən wɒsp / (BrE)
a solitary wasp that builds nests from hardened mud or clay for its young
