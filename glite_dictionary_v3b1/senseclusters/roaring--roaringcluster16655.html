roaring

adjective
very lively or profitable (business, trade)
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a trade, business, or period) very lively, successful, and profitable.

Examples

Roaring success

Since launching their new product, they've been doing a roaring trade.
The city experienced a roaring boom in tourism last year.
