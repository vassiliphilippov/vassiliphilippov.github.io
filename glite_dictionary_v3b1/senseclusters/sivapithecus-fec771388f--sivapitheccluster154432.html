Sivapithecus

noun
extinct Miocene primate genus
Pronunciation: / ˌsiːvəpɪˈθiːkəs /; / sɪˌvɑːpɪˈθiːkəs / (AmE), / ˌsɪvəpɪˈθiːkəs /; / sɪˌvapiːˈθiːkəs / (BrE)
an extinct genus of primates, whose fossils have been found in South Asia, believed to be closely related to the orangutan
