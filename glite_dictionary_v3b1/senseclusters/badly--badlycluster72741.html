badly

adverb
in a naughty or disobedient way
Pronunciation: / ˈbædli / (AmE), / ˈbadli / (BrE)
in a disobedient, naughty, or morally wrong way

Examples

Children behave badly
If you act badly during dinner, you'll be sent to your room.
She was scolded for speaking badly to her teacher.
The child often behaved badly when he didn't get his way.
He knew it was wrong to treat his friends badly and later apologized.
People who act badly often cause harm to others.
The villain in the story behaved badly throughout his life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: naughtily - in a naughty or misbehaving way
  • antonym: well - in a proper or obedient way
