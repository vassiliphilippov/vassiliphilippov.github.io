diplodocus

noun
large long-necked Jurassic dinosaur
5.2 7.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈplɑːdəkəs, daɪˈploʊdəkəs / (AmE), / dɪˈplɒdəkəs, daɪˈpləʊdəkəs / (BrE)
a large, long-necked herbivorous dinosaur of the late Jurassic period, known for its long tail and found in western North America.
