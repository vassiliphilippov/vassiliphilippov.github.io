milkily

adverb
feebly or timidly
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪkɪli / (AmE), / ˈmɪkɪli / (BrE)
in a weak, ineffectual, or timid manner; feebly or without strength or confidence

Examples

He speaks milkily
He answered the difficult question milkily, barely raising his voice and avoiding direct statements.
During the meeting, she milkily suggested a change but quickly withdrew her idea.
The student milkily defended his project, lacking the confidence to speak up clearly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boldly - in a confident and courageous way
  • synonym: feebly - in a weak or ineffective way
  • synonym: timidly - in a shy or lacking confidence manner
