lily family

noun
a group of flowering plants including lilies
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪli ˌfæməli / (AmE), / ˈlɪli ˌfæmɪli / (BrE)
a large group of flowering plants, formerly classified as Liliaceae, including lilies and related genera, now often divided among several plant families

Examples

The lily family plants
Botanists have reclassified some plants once included in the lily family into separate families.
Many popular garden flowers belong to the lily family, admired for their vibrant blooms.
Members of the lily family often have six petaled flowers and grow from bulbs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amaryllis family - a family of flowering plants related to lilies
  • synonym: lily - a kind of flowering plant, often with large showy flowers
