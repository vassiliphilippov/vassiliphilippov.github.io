glove

verb
to put on gloves
Pronunciation: / ɡlʌv / (AmE), / ɡlʌv / (BrE)
to put on or cover the hands with gloves, especially for protection or hygiene

Examples

To glove your hands
Before entering the sterile room, the surgeon was gloving carefully to avoid contamination.
Workers must glove their hands before handling hazardous materials in the lab.
She quickly gloved her hands to protect them from the cold wind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unglove - to remove gloves from the hands
