preemie

noun
prematurely born infant
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːmi / (AmE), / ˈpriːmi / (BrE)
an infant that is born prematurely, especially before 37 weeks of gestation

Examples

A preemie baby
After spending two months in the neonatal intensive care unit, the preemie was finally allowed to go home.
Doctors carefully monitored the preemie for signs of breathing difficulties.
The hospital has special facilities to care for preemies born at less than 30 weeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: premature baby - a baby born before the usual term of pregnancy
