rocky

adjective
full of difficulties; problematic
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑki / (AmE), / ˈrɒki / (BrE)
full of difficulties or problems; involving hardship or uncertainty

Examples

Rocky relationship

The team's path to the championship was long and rocky.
Their relationship had a rocky start but eventually settled down.
