smallpox virus

noun
virus causing smallpox in humans
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɔlˌpɑks ˈvaɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːlpɒks ˈvaɪərəs / (BrE)
the virus (Variola virus) that causes smallpox in humans, sometimes considered a potential bioweapon
