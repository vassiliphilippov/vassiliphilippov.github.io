fractal geometry

noun
branch of mathematics studying fractals
Pronunciation: / ˈfræk.təl dʒiˈɑː.mə.tri / (AmE), / ˈfræk.təl dʒɪˈɒm.ɪ.tri /; / ˈfræk.təl dʒiˈɒm.ɪ.tri / (BrE)
the branch of mathematics that studies fractals and patterns that repeat at different scales (self-similarity)

  • synonym: fractal - a complex geometric figure made up of patterns that repeat at every scale
