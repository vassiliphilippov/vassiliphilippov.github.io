withdrawer

noun
person who avoids social interaction
8.4
Pronunciation: / wɪðˈdrɔɚ / (AmE), / wɪðˈdrɔːə / (BrE)
a person who withdraws from social interaction, especially habitually or characteristically; an individual who tends to avoid social contact

Examples

Social withdrawer
After experiencing a traumatic event, she became more of a withdrawer and rarely interacted with her peers.
As a withdrawer, he often declined invitations to parties or social gatherings.
Many withdrawers find comfort in spending time alone rather than mingling with large groups.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extrovert - a person who is outgoing and enjoys socializing
  • synonym: recluse - a person who lives a solitary life and tends to avoid other people
