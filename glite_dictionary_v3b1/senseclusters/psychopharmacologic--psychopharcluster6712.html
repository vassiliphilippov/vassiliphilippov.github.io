psychopharmacologic

adjective
mind-affecting drugs
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊˌfɑːrməkəˈlɑːdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊˌfɑːməkəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study or use of drugs that affect mental processes or behavior

Examples

Psychopharmacologic treatment
The doctor recommended a psychopharmacologic approach to help manage the patient's anxiety.
Recent advances in psychopharmacologic research have led to new treatments for mental illness.
She specializes in psychopharmacologic therapies for mood disorders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pharmacological - relating to the study or use of drugs
  • synonym: psychotropic - affecting mental activity, behavior, or perception
