nonresiny

adjective
not containing resin
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈrɛzəni / (AmE), / nɒnˈrɛzəni / (BrE)
not containing or characterized by resin; not resiny.

Examples

Nonresiny plant stems
Some plants produce nonresiny stems, making them less sticky to the touch.
The carpenter preferred nonresiny wood for making furniture because it was easier to finish.
This variety is prized for its nonresiny texture, especially in paper manufacturing.
