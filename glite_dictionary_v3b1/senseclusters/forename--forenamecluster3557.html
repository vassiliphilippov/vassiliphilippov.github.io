forename

noun
person's first name
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrˌneɪm / (AmE), / ˈfɔːneɪm / (BrE)
A person's first name; the name that comes before the family name or surname.

Examples

A forename and surname
Her forename was carefully chosen by her grandmother.
Many people have more than one forename listed on their passport.
Please write your forename and surname on the application form.

Related Terms

  • synonym: given name - first name, often assigned at birth or baptism
  • antonym: surname - family name, occurring after the forename
