tzatziki

noun
Greek yogurt dip
Pronunciation: / tsɑːˈtsiːki / (AmE), / tsætˈsiːki / (BrE)
a Greek dip or sauce made from yogurt, cucumber, garlic, and herbs, often served with bread or grilled meats

Examples

Greek tzatziki sauce
We served tzatziki with warm pita bread at the party.
The grilled chicken was delicious with a side of tzatziki and fresh salad.
She made homemade tzatziki using Greek yogurt, cucumber, and garlic for the barbecue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hummus - a Middle Eastern dip made from chickpeas and tahini
  • synonym: raita - an Indian yogurt-based condiment with vegetables and spices
