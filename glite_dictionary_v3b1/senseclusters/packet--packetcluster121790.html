packet

noun
small package or bundle
Pronunciation: / ˈpæk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈpæk.ɪt / (BrE)
a small package or container for holding or transporting things (such as food, documents, or medicine)

Examples

A packet of sugar
Each visitor received a packet containing samples and brochures.
I found a packet of seeds in the garden shed.
She opened a packet of biscuits and offered them around the table.
Each guest received a welcome packet containing brochures and maps.
He found a packet of seeds in the drawer and decided to plant them.
She tore open the packet of sugar and poured it into her coffee.
