little

adjective
not important; trivial (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬.əl / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl / (BrE)
small in significance or importance; not important or serious (informal)

Examples

A little problem
He dismissed the argument as a little misunderstanding that wasn't worth his time.
Let's not worry about such little problems right now.
She spent the afternoon dealing with little details instead of the main issue.

Related Terms

  • antonym: important - of great significance or value
  • synonym: petty - of little importance; trivial
  • synonym: trivial - of little value or importance
