lazaretto

noun
hospital for infectious diseases
Pronunciation: / ˌlæzəˈrɛtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌlæzəˈrɛtəʊ / (BrE)
a hospital or building used for quarantine, especially for people with infectious diseases

Examples

Quarantine lazaretto
During the outbreak, the city established a lazaretto on an isolated island.
Historical records show that the lazaretto treated thousands of patients with contagious diseases.
The ship was required to stop at the lazaretto before passengers could go ashore.
Many ships' passengers were required to stay in the lazaretto before entering the country.
The old lazaretto stood on an island just outside the city harbor.
The restored lazaretto attracts visitors interested in medical history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leprosarium - hospital specifically for treating people with leprosy
  • synonym: quarantine - a period or place of isolation to prevent spread of disease
