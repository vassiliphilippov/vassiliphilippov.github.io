kleptomaniacal

adjective
compelled to steal
9.8
Pronunciation: /klep.tə.mə.ˈnaɪ.ə.kəl/ (AmE), /klep.tə.mə.ˈnaɪ.ə.kəl/ (BrE)
having a strong or uncontrollable urge to steal, especially without need or personal gain

Examples

A kleptomaniacal person
The kleptomaniacal child could not resist taking small items from the store.
Her kleptomaniacal tendencies worried her family and friends.
A kleptomaniacal impulse led him to steal things he did not even want.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compulsive - driven by an irresistible urge
  • antonym: honest - not likely to steal or cheat
