maidenhood

noun
state or period of being a maiden
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdənˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈmeɪd(ə)nhʊd / (BrE)
the state or period of being a maiden; a girl's childhood or youth, especially before marriage

Examples

Her maidenhood years
Her maidenhood was spent in a quiet village before she moved to the city.
In some cultures, maidenhood is celebrated with special ceremonies marking a girl's passage into adulthood.
Poets often write nostalgically about the innocence of maidenhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: virginity - the state of never having had sexual intercourse, especially in reference to a woman
  • degree higher: womanhood - the state or condition of being a woman, often after 'maidenhood'
