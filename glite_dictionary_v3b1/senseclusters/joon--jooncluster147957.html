joon

noun
term of endearment
Pronunciation: / dʒuːn / (AmE), / dʒuːn / (BrE)
a Persian term of endearment meaning 'dear' or 'beloved', sometimes used in English to show affection

Examples

Sweet joon
She smiled and called her friend joon after hearing the good news.
In their family, it is common to add joon to names as a sign of love.
He whispered 'goodnight, joon' before leaving the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beloved - a person who is dearly loved
  • synonym: dear - a term used to address someone affectionately
