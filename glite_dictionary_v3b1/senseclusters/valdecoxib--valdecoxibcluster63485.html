valdecoxib

noun
Cox-2 inhibitor pain-relief drug
Pronunciation: / ˌvæl.dɪˈkɑːs.ɪb / (AmE), / ˌvæl.dɪˈkɒk.sɪb / (BrE)
A nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (Cox-2 inhibitor) used to relieve pain and inflammation, formerly marketed under the trade name Bextra.

Examples

Valdecoxib pain relief
Due to safety concerns, valdecoxib was withdrawn from major markets but is still discussed in medical literature.

The doctor prescribed valdecoxib for her chronic joint pain after other medications caused stomach issues.
