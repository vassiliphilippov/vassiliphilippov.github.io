flying mouse

noun
small gliding marsupial (sugar glider)
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪɪŋ ˌmaʊs / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ ˌmaʊs / (BrE)
a small gliding marsupial, also known as a flying phalanger or sugar glider, native to Australia and New Guinea

Examples

A flying mouse pet
A flying mouse is sometimes kept as an exotic pet due to its cute appearance.
Researchers studied the diet of the flying mouse in its natural habitat.
The flying mouse can glide from tree to tree using the membrane between its limbs.
