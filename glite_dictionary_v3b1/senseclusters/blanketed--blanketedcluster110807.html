blanketed

adjective
covered with or as if with a blanket
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈblæŋ.kə.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈblaŋ.kɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
covered with or as if with a blanket; completely covered or enveloped.

Examples

Fog blanketed the city
A thick fog blanketed the city for most of the morning.
She found the valley blanketed with vibrant wildflowers in the spring.
The fields were blanketed in snow after last night's storm.

  • antonym: exposed - not covered or protected
