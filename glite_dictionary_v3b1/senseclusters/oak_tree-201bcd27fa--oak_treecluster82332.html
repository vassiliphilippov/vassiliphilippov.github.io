oak tree

noun
large deciduous tree with acorns
1.0
Pronunciation: / oʊk tri /; / oʊk triː / (AmE), / əʊk triː / (BrE)
a large tree of the genus Quercus, with lobed leaves and acorns, known for its strong wood
