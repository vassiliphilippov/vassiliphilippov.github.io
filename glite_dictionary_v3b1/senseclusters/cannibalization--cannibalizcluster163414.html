cannibalization

noun
eating own species
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkænəbələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkænɪbələˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of an animal or person eating a member of its own species

Examples

Animal cannibalization
The sudden lack of food led to cannibalization among the isolated animal population.
Scientists observed cannibalization in the insect colony when resources became scarce.
The documentary showed rare footage of cannibalization in wild rodent communities.

Related Terms
