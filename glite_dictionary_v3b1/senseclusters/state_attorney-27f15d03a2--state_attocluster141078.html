state attorney

noun
public prosecutor at state level (U.S.)
2.6
Pronunciation: / steɪt əˈtɝni / (AmE), / steɪt əˈtɜːni / (BrE)
A lawyer who represents the government in criminal prosecutions at the state level, especially in the United States.

Examples

State attorney prosecutes crimes
After reviewing the evidence, the state attorney decided to proceed with the prosecution.
The state attorney announced new charges against the defendant during the press conference.
The state attorney met with victims to explain the upcoming trial process.
