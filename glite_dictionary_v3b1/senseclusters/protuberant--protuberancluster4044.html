protuberant

adjective
bulging outward; sticking out
6.3
Pronunciation: / proʊˈtuːbərənt / (AmE), / prəˈtjuːbərənt / (BrE)
bulging or sticking out beyond the surrounding surface; curving outward

Examples

A protuberant belly
He had a protuberant belly after the large meal.
The ancient sculpture features a protuberant nose that draws attention.
The frog's protuberant eyes allow it to see in almost all directions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bulging - sticking out in a rounded shape
  • antonym: recessed - set back or pressed inward
