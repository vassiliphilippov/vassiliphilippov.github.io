medlar fruit

noun
edible fruit of the medlar tree
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛd.lər frut / (AmE), / ˈmɛd.lə fruːt / (BrE)
the edible fruit of the medlar tree, brownish and soft when ripe, traditionally eaten after bletting

Examples

Ripe medlar fruit
In late autumn, the branches are heavy with medlar fruit.
She made a delicious jam using ripe medlar fruit.
The medlar fruit should be left to blet before it can be eaten.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medlar - the small deciduous tree producing the medlar fruit
