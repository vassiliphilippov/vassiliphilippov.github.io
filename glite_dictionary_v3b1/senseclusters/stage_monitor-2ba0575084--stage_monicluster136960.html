stage monitor

noun
speaker for performers on stage
3.7 2.8
Pronunciation: / steɪdʒ ˈmɑːnɪtər / (AmE), / steɪdʒ ˈmɒnɪtə / (BrE)
A loudspeaker on stage that faces performers, allowing them to hear their own performance clearly during a live event.

Examples

Stage monitor speaker
The band positioned each stage monitor carefully to ensure every member could hear the mix.
The guitarist asked the sound engineer to turn up the stage monitor so she could hear herself better.
Without a stage monitor, singers might struggle to stay in tune during a live show.
