protostome

adjective
mouth forms first
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊtəˌstoʊm / (AmE), / ˈprəʊtəstəʊm / (BrE)
relating to animals whose embryonic development involves the mouth forming before the anus.

Examples

Protostome development
In biology class, we learned about protostome development in worms and insects.
The scientist explained the differences between protostome and deuterostome animals.
Mollusks are a common example of protostome organisms in the animal kingdom.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deuterostome - relating to animals whose anus forms before the mouth during development
