famousness

noun
state of being famous
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪməsnəs / (AmE), / ˈfeɪməsnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being well known and recognized by many people

Examples

The famousness of a person
The famousness of the author grew after her novel won a major award.
He was unprepared for the sudden famousness that came with his viral video.
Despite her famousness, she remained humble and approachable to her fans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fame - the state of being known or recognized by many people
  • antonym: obscurity - the state of being unknown or not well known
