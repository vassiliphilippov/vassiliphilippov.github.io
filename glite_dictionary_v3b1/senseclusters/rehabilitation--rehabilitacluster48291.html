rehabilitation

noun
treatment to restore physical abilities
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.həˌbɪ.ləˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌriː.həˌbɪl.ɪˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of helping someone to recover physical abilities after illness or injury, especially through treatment such as massage, exercises, or electrotherapy

Examples

Physical rehabilitation
After his surgery, he underwent rehabilitation to regain movement in his leg.
She credits her speedy rehabilitation to the dedicated physical therapy team.
The hospital offers comprehensive rehabilitation programs for stroke patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: physiotherapy - treatment of disease or injury by physical methods
  • synonym: restoration - the act of returning something to its original state
