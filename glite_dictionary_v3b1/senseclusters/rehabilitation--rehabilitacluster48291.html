rehabilitation

noun
treatment to restore physical abilities

Examples

Physical rehabilitation
After his surgery, he underwent rehabilitation to regain movement in his leg.

Synonyms

    3.4 0.1/m
    physical rehabilitation uses clinical therapies to restore movement and strength after injury, while rehabilitation names the overall recovery process.
  • physical restoration
    4.1 0.00/m
    physical restoration focuses on reducing bodily disability to return basic function, while rehabilitation covers the broader medical recovery journey.
  • therapeutic rehabilitation
    4.8 0.00/m
    therapeutic rehabilitation uses treatment to improve daily function and quality of life, while rehabilitation is the physical recovery process.
  • reconditioning
    4.8 0.00/m
    reconditioning rebuilds fitness, strength, or stamina through exercise after illness, while rehabilitation is a wider medical therapy program.
