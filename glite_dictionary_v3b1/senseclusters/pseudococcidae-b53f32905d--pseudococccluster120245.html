Pseudococcidae

noun
family of insects known as mealybugs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdoʊˈkɒksɪdiː / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊˈkɒksɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of small, soft-bodied insects known as mealybugs, some of which are agricultural pests

Examples

Pseudococcidae mealybugs
Infestations of Pseudococcidae can weaken plants and encourage the growth of mold.
Several species within the Pseudococcidae family are notorious agricultural pests affecting fruit crops.
The Pseudococcidae are often found on plant stems and leaves, where they feed on sap.
