cargo door

noun
door for loading or unloading cargo
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrɡoʊ dɔr / (AmE), / ˈkɑːɡəʊ dɔː / (BrE)
a door on a vehicle, aircraft, or ship used for loading or unloading cargo

Examples

The cargo door
Make sure the cargo door is fully secured before departure.
The plane's cargo door was opened to unload the containers.
Workers gathered by the cargo door to organize the incoming shipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hatch - an opening or door in a ship, aircraft, or vehicle for access or loading
