noncoding

adjective
not coding for proteins
6.7
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈkoʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈkəʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
describing DNA or RNA that does not contain instructions for making proteins

Examples

Noncoding DNA
Scientists study noncoding DNA to understand its role in gene regulation.

The discovery of noncoding RNA has changed our understanding of genetics.
