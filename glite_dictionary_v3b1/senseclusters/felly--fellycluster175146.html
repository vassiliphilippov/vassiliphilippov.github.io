felly

noun
rim segment of a wheel for fitting spokes
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛli / (AmE), / ˈfɛli / (BrE)
the outer rim or a segment of the rim of a wheel, into which the spokes are fitted

Examples

The felly of a wheel
Each spoke must fit snugly into its slot in the felly.
The blacksmith carefully replaced the broken felly on the wagon wheel.
Traditional wooden wheels are made by joining several fellies together.
