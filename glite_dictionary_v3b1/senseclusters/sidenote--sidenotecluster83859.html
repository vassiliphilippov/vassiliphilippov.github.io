sidenote

noun
additional comment
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌnoʊt / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌnəʊt / (BrE)
a brief comment or piece of information added separately from the main topic

Examples

A sidenote comment
As a sidenote, the museum will be closed on Mondays for maintenance.
He added a quick sidenote to clarify his earlier statement during the presentation.
Let me mention, as a sidenote, that parking is free after 6 PM.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aside - a remark not directly related to the main subject
  • synonym: footnote - an additional note at the bottom of a page
  • antonym: main point - the central or most important idea
