Turanian

noun
person from Turan
Pronunciation: / tuˈreɪniən / (AmE), / tjʊˈreɪniən /; / tjʊəˈreɪniən / (BrE)
a person belonging to the Turanian peoples or an outdated term for a supposed racial group from Central Asia (historical/obsolete)
