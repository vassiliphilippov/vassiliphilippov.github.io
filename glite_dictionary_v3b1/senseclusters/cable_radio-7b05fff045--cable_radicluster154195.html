cable radio

noun
radio delivered via coaxial cable
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪbəl ˈreɪdioʊ / (AmE), / ˈkeɪbəl ˈreɪdiəʊ / (BrE)
A system for delivering radio broadcasts to homes and businesses via coaxial cable rather than over-the-air transmission.

Examples

Cable radio system
Cable radio provided clearer reception than traditional over-the-air broadcasts in some urban locations.
Many apartment complexes offered cable radio as part of their utilities package in the 1980s.
She remembers listening to her favorite stations on cable radio before FM became widely available.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cable television - television delivered via coaxial cable
  • antonym: terrestrial radio - radio transmission over-the-air via electromagnetic waves
