great yellowcress

noun
a European riverside perennial herb
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt ˈjɛloʊˌkrɛs / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈjɛləʊˌkrɛs / (BrE)
a perennial herb found on streams and riversides throughout much of Europe, with yellow flowers; sometimes classified in the genus Nasturtium

Examples

The great yellowcress plant
