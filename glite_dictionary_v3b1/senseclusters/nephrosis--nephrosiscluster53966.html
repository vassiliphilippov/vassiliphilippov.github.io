nephrosis

noun
kidney disease (non-inflammatory)
7.5
Pronunciation: / nɛˈfroʊsɪs / (AmE), / nɛˈfrəʊsɪs /; / nɪˈfrəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a non-inflammatory disease of the kidneys, often causing swelling and large amounts of protein in the urine
