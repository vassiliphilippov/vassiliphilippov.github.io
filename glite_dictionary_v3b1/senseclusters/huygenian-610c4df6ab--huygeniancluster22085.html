Huygenian

noun
follower of Huygens
Pronunciation: / haɪˈɡeɪniən / (AmE), / haɪˈɡeɪniən / (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the scientific theories of Christiaan Huygens, especially his wave theory of light

Examples

A Huygenian scientist
As a dedicated Huygenian, she argued for the wave theory of light in her lectures.
The debate between the Huygenians and the Newtonians shaped the history of physics.
He was proud to be called a Huygenian during the scientific revolution.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Newtonian - a person who supports the scientific theories of Isaac Newton
