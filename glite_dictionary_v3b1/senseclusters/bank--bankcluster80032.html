bank

noun
row or tier of similar objects
Pronunciation: / bæŋk / (AmE), / bæŋk / (BrE)
a set of similar objects arranged in a row or tier, especially for a specific function

Examples

A bank of lights
A bank of elevators served the busy office building.
She installed a bank of solar panels on the roof to increase efficiency.
The engineer checked the bank of monitors for any warning lights.
