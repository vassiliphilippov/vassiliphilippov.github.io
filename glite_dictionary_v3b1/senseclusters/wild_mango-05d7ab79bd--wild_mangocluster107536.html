wild mango

noun
African tree with edible mango-like fruit
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈmæŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / waɪld ˈmæŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
an African tree (Irvingia gabonensis) with edible yellow fruit similar to mangoes, valued for its oil-rich seeds and termite-resistant wood

Examples

African wild mango tree
Researchers are studying the oil extracted from wild mango seeds for its health benefits.
The termite-resistant wood of wild mango is prized for making durable furniture.
The villagers harvest wild mango for its sweet fruit and nutritious seeds.
