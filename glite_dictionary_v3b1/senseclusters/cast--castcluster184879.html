cast

noun
throwing dice in a game
4.7
Pronunciation: / kæst / (AmE), / kɑːst / (BrE)
the act of throwing dice in a game of chance

Examples

A dice cast
He watched nervously as she made her cast and the dice rolled across the table.
The outcome of the game depended on the final cast of the dice.
With a single cast, he lost all his winnings in the game.
