guyline

noun
supporting rope or cable
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a rope or cable used to steady or support a structure such as a tent or pole

Examples

A guyline rope

He tripped over a guyline while walking around the campsite at night.
