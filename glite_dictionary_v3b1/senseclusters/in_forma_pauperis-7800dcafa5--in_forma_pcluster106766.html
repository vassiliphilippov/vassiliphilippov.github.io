in forma pauperis

x
legal fee waiver
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈfɔrmə ˈpɔpərɪs / (AmE), / ɪn ˈfɔːmə ˈpɔːpərɪs / (BrE)
a legal status allowing a person to proceed in court without paying fees due to lack of money

Examples

Obtain in forma pauperis status
The judge approved her request to file in forma pauperis because she could not afford the court fees.
Many prisoners submit appeals in forma pauperis when they lack the funds for legal costs.
He was able to proceed with his lawsuit in forma pauperis after proving his financial hardship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affidavit of indigency - a sworn statement declaring inability to pay court costs
  • synonym: indigent - lacking money or resources
