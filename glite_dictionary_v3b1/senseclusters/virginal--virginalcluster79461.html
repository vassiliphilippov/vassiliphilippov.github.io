virginal

adjective
characteristic of a virgin; pure or innocent
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝːdʒənl̩ /; / ˈvɜrdʒɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdʒɪnəl / (BrE)
pure, untouched, or unspoiled; fresh and innocent

Examples

Virginal white dress
His room maintained a virginal neatness that impressed his guests.
She wore a virginal white dress to the ceremony, symbolizing innocence.
The snow covered the landscape in a virginal blanket of white.
She walked through a virginal forest, untouched by human hands.
The artist sought a virginal canvas, free from any previous sketches or marks.
The mountain air was so virginal that it felt like we were the first to breathe it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: innocent - not corrupted by evil, sin, or wrongdoing
  • synonym: pure - free from contamination; morally spotless
  • antonym: tainted - spoiled or affected with something bad
  • synonym: untouched - not changed or affected, still in its original state
