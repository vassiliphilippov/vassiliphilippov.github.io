gratuitously

adverb
without good reason; needlessly
7.5 6.3
Pronunciation: /grə.ˈtuː.ə.təs.liː/ /; / /grə.ˈtjuː.ə.təs.liː/ (AmE), /grə.ˈtjuː.ɪ.təs.liː/ /; / /grə.ˈtʃuː.ɪ.təs.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that is unwarranted or done without good reason or justification.

Examples

To gratuitously insult
He gratuitously insulted his colleague during the meeting, surprising everyone.
She was offended by his gratuitously harsh remarks.
The critic argued that the film contained scenes of violence that were gratuitously included.

Related Terms

  • antonym: justifiably - in a manner that is justified or warranted
  • synonym: needlessly - without necessity or reason
