percutaneously

adverb
through the skin
8.9
Pronunciation: /pər.kjuː.ˈteɪ.nɪ.əs.liː/ /; / /pər.kjʊ.ˈteɪ.nɪ.əs.liː/ (AmE), /pə.kjuː.ˈteɪ.nɪ.əs.liː/ /; / /pə.kjʊ.ˈteɪ.nɪ.əs.liː/ (BrE)
by means of or through the skin, especially using a needle or medical instrument

Examples

Inserted percutaneously with needle
The doctor inserted the catheter percutaneously to avoid making a large incision.
Some vaccines can be delivered percutaneously rather than by traditional injection.
The procedure was performed percutaneously to reduce the patient's recovery time.
