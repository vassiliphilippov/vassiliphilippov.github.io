cravenness

noun
cowardly or contemptible behavior
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkrævənnəs / (AmE), / ˈkrævənnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being cowardly, especially in a contemptible or mean-spirited way

Examples

His cravenness showed
His cravenness during the crisis was shocking to everyone on the team.
No one could excuse his cravenness in the face of danger.
She despised the cravenness that kept him from standing up for his friends.

  • synonym: cowardice - lack of bravery
