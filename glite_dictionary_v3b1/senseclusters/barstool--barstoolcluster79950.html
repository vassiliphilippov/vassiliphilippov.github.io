barstool

noun
tall stool for bars
2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑːr.stuːl/ (AmE), /ˈbɑː.stuːl/ (BrE)
a tall stool, usually used at a bar or high counter, often with a footrest and sometimes without a back or arms
