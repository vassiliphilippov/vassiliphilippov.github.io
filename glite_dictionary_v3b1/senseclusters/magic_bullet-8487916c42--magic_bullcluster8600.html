magic bullet

noun
ideal cure; perfect remedy
Pronunciation: / ˌmædʒɪk ˈbʊlət / (AmE), / ˌmadʒɪk ˈbʊlɪt / (BrE)
a drug or therapy that can cure or prevent a disease quickly and effectively, often regarded as a perfect or ideal solution

Examples

A magic bullet drug
He hoped the new drug would be the magic bullet the medical community was waiting for.
Scientists are searching for a magic bullet to halt the spread of the virus.
Unfortunately, there is no magic bullet that can cure all types of cancer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cure-all - a remedy that is believed to cure any disease or difficulty
  • synonym: panacea - a solution or remedy for all difficulties or diseases
