twistable

adjective
can be twisted
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪstəbəl / (AmE), / ˈtwɪstəbəl / (BrE)
able to be bent or turned without breaking

Examples

Twistable cable
The new phone charger uses a twistable cable that resists tangling.
She bought a twistable toy for her cat to play with.
This metal rod is surprisingly twistable despite its thickness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexible - able to bend easily without breaking
  • antonym: rigid - not able to be bent or changed easily
