breakdown

noun
analysis into exclusive categories
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈbreɪk.daʊn / (BrE)
an analysis or classification into distinct, exclusive categories, often used to show the components of a whole

Examples

Breakdown by category
Can you provide a breakdown of the survey responses by age group?
The document offers a breakdown of each product's market share.
The financial report included a breakdown of expenses by department.

  • antonym: summary - brief statement of the main points
