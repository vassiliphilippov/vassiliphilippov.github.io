animalistic

adjective
having qualities typical of animals
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈmælɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌænɪˈmælɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of animals, especially in a primal, instinctual, or non-rational way

Examples

Animalistic energy
Her dance had an animalistic energy that captivated the audience.
The villain's animalistic rage frightened everyone in the room.
Under stress, people may sometimes act in surprisingly animalistic ways.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: animal - a living organism that feeds on organic matter
  • synonym: beastly - cruel or savage in a way that resembles a beast
