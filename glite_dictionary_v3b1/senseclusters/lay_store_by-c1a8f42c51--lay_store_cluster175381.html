lay store by

verb
consider important or valuable
8.9
Pronunciation: / leɪ stɔr baɪ / (AmE), / leɪ stɔː baɪ / (BrE)
to regard something as important, valuable, or worthy of trust; to count on or value highly

Examples

To lay store by advice
Many investors lay store by expert opinions when making decisions about their portfolios.
She has always laid store by honesty and hard work in her team.
Some people lay store by old superstitions, while others dismiss them entirely.
