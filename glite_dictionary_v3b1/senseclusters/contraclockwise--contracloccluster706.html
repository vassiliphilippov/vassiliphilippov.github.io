contraclockwise

adjective
in the opposite direction to a clock's hands
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑntrəˈklɑkˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrəˈklɒkˌwaɪz / (BrE)
(of movement) in a direction opposite to the way the hands of a clock move.

Examples

Contraclockwise movement
Astronomers observed the planet rotating contraclockwise about its axis.
The dancers began moving in a contraclockwise circle around the bonfire.
Turn the knob contraclockwise to loosen it.
