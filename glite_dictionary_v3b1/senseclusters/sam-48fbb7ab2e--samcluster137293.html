SAM

noun
US federal vendor registry (SAM.gov)
8.3
Pronunciation: /sæm/ /; / /es eɪ em/ (AmE), /sæm/ /; / /es eɪ em/ (BrE)
Acronym for the US System for Award Management (SAM.gov), the federal online registry that consolidates vendor registration, procurement data, and contract opportunities for government contractors and agencies.

Examples

SAM registration system
Before bidding on federal contracts, vendors must be registered in SAM.
I searched SAM to find open solicitations for small businesses last night.
Their company renewed its registration in SAM to keep eligibility for government contracts.
