catafalque

noun
decorated funeral platform for a coffin
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkæt̬.ə.fælk / (AmE), / ˈkat.ə.falk / (BrE)
A decorated platform or framework on which a coffin rests during a funeral or while lying in state.

Examples

The catafalque platform
Soldiers stood guard around the catafalque throughout the night.
The coffin was displayed on a catafalque in the cathedral for the public to pay their respects.
The ornate catafalque was draped in black velvet during the memorial service.
