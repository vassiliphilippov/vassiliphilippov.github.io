crabbedness

noun
ill-tempered disposition
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkræbɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈkræbɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being ill-tempered, irritable, or difficult to please

Examples

His crabbedness shows
Despite his reputation for crabbedness, his friends knew he had a kind heart beneath the gruff exterior.
His crabbedness made working with him a daily challenge for the entire team.
She was known for her crabbedness and sharp retorts, especially before her morning coffee.
