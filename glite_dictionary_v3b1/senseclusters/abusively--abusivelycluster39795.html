abusively

adverb
in an abusive manner
3.9
Pronunciation: / əˈbjusɪvli / (AmE), / əˈbjuːsɪvli / (BrE)
In an abusive or cruel manner, especially using violent or insulting language or behavior.

Examples

Speak abusively to others
He often spoke abusively to those who disagreed with him.
She was surprised when her friend reacted abusively during the argument.
The manager treated his staff abusively, leading to several complaints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abusive - using or involving harmful, insulting, or cruel language or behavior
  • antonym: kindly - in a kind or compassionate manner
