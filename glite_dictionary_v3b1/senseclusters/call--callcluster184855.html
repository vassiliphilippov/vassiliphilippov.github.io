call

verb
declare or demand a card or suit (games)
4.2 3.17/m
Pronunciation: / k…îl / (AmE), / k…îÀêl / (BrE)
(in cards or similar games) to demand, declare, or name a card, suit, or action as part of gameplay.

Examples

To call a card
During the poker game, he called for a show of hands to settle the dispute.
The player confidently called her card, hoping to win the round.
When the bidding reached him, Tom called spades as the trump suit.

Synonyms

  • declare
    7.4 0.52/m
    declare names the trump suit or no-trump at the end of bidding, while call can mean naming or demanding a card or suit.
  • bid
    5.1 0.08/m
    bid makes a formal statement of value or suit in the auction, while call often simply names or demands a card or suit.

Related Terms
