demasculinize

verb
to reduce masculinity
8.8
Pronunciation: / diːˈmæskjəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / diːˈmæskjʊlənaɪz / (BrE)
to make someone or something less masculine in character, appearance, or behavior; to remove or reduce masculine qualities

Examples

The organization worked to demasculinize the culture of the sports club.

The new fashion trend aims to demasculinize traditional men's clothing styles.
Some people fear that changing gender roles will demasculinize certain professions.
The director chose to demasculinize the character to challenge stereotypes.
