phosphorous

adjective
containing or relating to phosphorus
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑsfərəs / (AmE), / ˈfɒsfərəs / (BrE)
containing or characteristic of phosphorus, especially relating to compounds in which phosphorus has a lower oxidation state (such as +3)

Examples

Phosphorous fertilizers
Phosphorous fertilizers are applied to improve plant growth.
A phosphorous compound is used as a reagent in the laboratory.
The water sample was tested for phosphorous contamination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: phosphoric - containing or characteristic of phosphorus, especially in higher oxidation state (+5)
