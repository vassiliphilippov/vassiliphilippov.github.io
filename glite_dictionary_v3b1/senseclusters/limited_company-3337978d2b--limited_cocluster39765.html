limited company

noun
business entity with limited liability
5.7 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪm.ɪ.tɪd ˈkʌm.pə.niː/ /; / /ˈlɪm.ə.tɪd ˈkʌm.pə.niː/ (AmE), /ˈlɪm.ɪ.tɪd ˈkʌm.pə.nɪ/ (BrE)
a company whose owners' liability is limited to the amount of their investment

Examples

A limited company
A limited company must file annual accounts and comply with certain legal regulations.
Many small firms convert to a limited company as they expand their operations.
The family business registered as a limited company to protect personal assets from business debts.
