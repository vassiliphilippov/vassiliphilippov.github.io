modularly

adverb
in separate parts
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːdʒələrli / (AmE), / ˈmɒdjʊləli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or uses separate modules or parts that can be combined or changed independently

Examples

Modularly build furniture
The furniture was designed modularly so that each piece could be rearranged easily.
By building the software modularly, developers can update individual features without affecting the whole program.
The course is structured modularly, allowing students to complete different sections at their own pace.

Related Terms

  • antonym: holistically - in a way that considers the whole rather than separate parts
  • synonym: systematically - in an organized and methodical way
