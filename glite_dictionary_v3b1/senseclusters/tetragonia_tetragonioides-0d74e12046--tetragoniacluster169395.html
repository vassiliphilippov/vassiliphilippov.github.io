Tetragonia tetragonioides

noun
Australasian plant grown for edible leaves
Pronunciation: / tɛtrəˈɡoʊniə tɛtrəˌɡoʊniˈɔɪdiz / (AmE), / tɛtrəˈɡəʊniə tɛtrəˌɡəʊniˈɔɪdiːz / (BrE)
a coarse, sprawling plant native to Australasia, with red or yellow flowers, cultivated for its edible young shoots and succulent leaves; also known as New Zealand spinach
