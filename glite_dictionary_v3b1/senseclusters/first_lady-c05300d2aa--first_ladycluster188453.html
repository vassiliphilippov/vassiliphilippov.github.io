first lady

noun
wife of a president or leader
Pronunciation: / ˌfɝst ˈleɪdi / (AmE), / ˌfɜːst ˈleɪdi / (BrE)
the wife of a president or other head of state, especially in a republic; also sometimes used for the wife of a governor or other high-ranking official.

Examples

The first lady of the country
As first lady, she championed several important social causes.
Many people admire the charitable work done by the first lady of the country.
The first lady attended the opening ceremony of the new hospital.

Related Terms

  • synonym: president - an elected head of a republic, especially in government
