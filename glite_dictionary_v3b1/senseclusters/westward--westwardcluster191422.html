Synonyms

  • west
    1.0 20/m
    west names the direction or point on a map and can mean toward the west, while westward emphasizes movement toward the west.
  • westbound
    2.8 0.4/m
    westbound describes things moving or scheduled to move toward the west, while westward simply means in that west direction.
  • westerly
    3.1 0.03/m
    westerly often means coming from or toward the west, especially for winds, while westward focuses only on movement toward the west.
