westward

adverb
toward the west
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstwɚd /; / ˈwɛstwɚdz / (AmE), / ˈwɛstwəd /; / ˈwɛstwədz / (BrE)
toward the west; in the direction of the west.

Examples

Travel westward
Many birds migrate westward during the autumn season.
The clouds drifted westward as the wind picked up.
The pioneers moved westward in search of new land.
At sunset, the birds began flying westwards in a perfect V formation.
The river flows westwards before emptying into the bay.
We decided to drive westwards to explore the coastal towns.
