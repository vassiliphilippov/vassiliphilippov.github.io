saccule

noun
small sac, especially in the inner ear
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsæk.juːl / (AmE), / ˈsak.juːl / (BrE)
A small sac or pouch, especially the smaller chamber of the membranous labyrinth in the inner ear.

Examples

The saccule in ear
Damage to the saccule can affect a person's sense of balance.
Scientists studied the structure of the saccule in various animals to understand its function.
The saccule helps detect vertical movements as part of the vestibular system.
