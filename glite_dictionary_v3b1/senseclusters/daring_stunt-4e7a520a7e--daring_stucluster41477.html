daring stunt

noun
bold and risky performance
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛrɪŋ stʌnt / (AmE), / ˈdɛːrɪŋ stʌnt / (BrE)
An action or performance that involves great boldness and risk, often intended to impress or entertain.

Examples

A daring stunt performance
Crowds gathered to watch the daring stunt at the air show.
Her latest movie features a breathtaking daring stunt on top of a skyscraper.
The motorcyclist performed a daring stunt by jumping over ten cars in a row.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feat - an achievement that requires great courage, skill, or strength
  • antonym: safe move - an action that avoids risk or danger
  • synonym: trick - an action intended to deceive or amuse, sometimes dangerous
