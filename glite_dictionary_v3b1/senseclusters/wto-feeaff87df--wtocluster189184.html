WTO

noun
World Trade Organization; global trade body
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌdʌbəl.juː tiː ˈoʊ / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəl.juː tiː ˈəʊ / (BrE)
an international organization based in Geneva that monitors and enforces rules governing global trade between nations

Examples

The WTO organization
Membership in the WTO can provide countries with access to broader markets.
The WTO recently ruled on a dispute between two major trading partners.
The policies established by the WTO influence global economic growth.
