discourteously

adverb
in an impolite manner
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈkɝːtiəsli / (AmE), / dɪsˈkɜːtiəsli / (BrE)
In an impolite or rude manner.

Examples

Behave discourteously
He was criticized for behaving discourteously during the meeting.
She reacted discourteously to his polite request, surprising everyone present.
The waiter spoke discourteously to the customers, which made them uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impolitely - in an impolite manner
  • antonym: politely - in a courteous manner
