Christmassy

adjective
like Christmas
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪsməsi / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsməsi / (BrE)
having the qualities, atmosphere, or feeling associated with Christmas

Examples

A Christmassy feeling
The living room looked especially Christmassy with all the lights and ornaments.
She wore a Christmassy sweater decorated with reindeer and snowflakes to the party.
The music and scents in the shop made everything feel so Christmassy.
