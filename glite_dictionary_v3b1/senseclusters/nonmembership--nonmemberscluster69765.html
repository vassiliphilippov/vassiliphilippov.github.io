nonmembership

noun
lack of membership
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈmɛmbərʃɪp / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈmɛmbəʃɪp / (BrE)
the state of not being a member or not belonging to a group, organization, or club

Examples

His nonmembership in group
His nonmembership in the association prevented him from attending the annual meeting.
Due to her nonmembership, she was not eligible for the special discounts offered to club members.
The policy applies to both members and those with nonmembership status.
Her nonmembership in the club meant she could not attend the private event.
The policy applies to both membership and nonmembership groups within the organization.
In mathematics, the symbol indicates the nonmembership of an element in a set.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exclusion - the act of keeping someone or something out
  • antonym: membership - the state of being a member of a group or organization
