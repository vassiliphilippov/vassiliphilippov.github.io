skirting

noun
wall base trim
4.5 0.15/m

a strip of material, usually wood or plastic, covering the lower part of an interior wall where it meets the floor

Examples

Wooden skirting board
The carpenter installed new skirting along the base of the living room walls.
Dust often collects on the skirting, so it needs to be cleaned regularly.
They chose white skirting to create a contrast with the dark wooden floor.

Synonyms

  • skirting board
    4.8 0.01/m
    skirting board is the common full name for the wooden or plastic board along a wall base, while skirting is a shorter term.
  • mopboard
    8.7 0.01/m
    mopboard is a narrow molding that protects the wall from knocks and dirt, while skirting is the general lower wall strip.
