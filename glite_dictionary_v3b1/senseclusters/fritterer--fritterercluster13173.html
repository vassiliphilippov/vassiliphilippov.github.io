fritterer

noun
person who wastes resources
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪtərər / (AmE), / ˈfrɪtərə / (BrE)
a person who wastes time, money, or resources on unimportant things

Examples

A careless fritterer
As a notorious fritterer, he never managed to save any money for emergencies.
The manager warned the team not to be fritterers of valuable company time.
Instead of being a fritterer, she learned to prioritize her tasks and budget wisely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conserver - a person who uses resources carefully and avoids waste
  • synonym: spender - a person who spends money freely or carelessly
  • synonym: waster - a person who uses something carelessly or without purpose
