monger

verb
to spread harmful ideas
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
to promote, spread, or encourage something, especially something undesirable such as rumors or fear

Examples

To monger rumors
Some media outlets have been accused of mongering panic during the crisis.
He was criticized for mongering rumors that had no basis in fact.
The group was known to monger fear to gain political advantage.
Some people monger rumors to create fear in the community.
Politicians should not monger hatred or misinformation for personal gain.
He was accused of trying to monger panic during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: promote - to encourage the growth or acceptance of something
  • synonym: spread - to cause something to become known or used by more people
  • antonym: suppress - to prevent something from being known or expressed
