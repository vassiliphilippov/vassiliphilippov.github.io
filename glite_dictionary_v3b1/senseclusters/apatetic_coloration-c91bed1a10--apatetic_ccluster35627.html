apatetic coloration

noun
animal camouflage coloration
7.7
Pronunciation: / əˌpæˈtɛtɪk ˌkʌləˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌpəˈtɛtɪk ˌkʌləˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
