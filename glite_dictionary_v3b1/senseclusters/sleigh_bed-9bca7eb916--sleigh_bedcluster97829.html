sleigh bed

noun
bed with curved headboard and footboard
7.6
Pronunciation: / sleɪ bɛd / (AmE), / sleɪ bɛd / (BrE)
A bed with a solid headboard and footboard that curve outward at the top, resembling a sleigh.

Examples

A sleigh bed frame
Her favorite piece of furniture is the cherry sleigh bed by the window.
The ornate sleigh bed in the master bedroom was more than a century old.
We decided to buy a wooden sleigh bed for our new house.
