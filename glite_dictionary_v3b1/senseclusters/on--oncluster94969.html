on

adjective
scheduled to happen (events)
1.2
Pronunciation: / ɑn / (AmE), / ɒn / (BrE)
(of events) arranged, planned, or scheduled to take place; not cancelled.

Examples

On for tonight
Do you know if the meeting is on for tomorrow?
Our plans for dinner are on as scheduled.
The concert is still on despite the weather warnings.
