silverplate

verb
to coat with a thin layer of silver
7.3 7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvərˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəˌpleɪt / (BrE)
to coat (an object) with a thin layer of silver, usually by chemical or electrochemical means

Examples

To silverplate a spoon
Many old spoons were silverplated instead of made entirely from solid silver.
The craftsman will silverplate the candlesticks to restore their original shine.
They decided to silverplate the antique watch to enhance its value.
