featural

adjective
based on features
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːtʃərəl / (AmE), / ˈfiːtʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to or based on features, especially as individual properties in analysis or linguistics

Examples

A featural analysis
The featural analysis of language helps linguists understand how sounds are constructed.
A featural approach can reveal patterns that are not obvious in holistic methods.
In computer vision, featural methods focus on identifying key points in images.
