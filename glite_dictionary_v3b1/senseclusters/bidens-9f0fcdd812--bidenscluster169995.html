Bidens

noun
a genus of plants (bur marigolds)
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌdɛnz /; / ˈbaɪdɛnz / (AmE), / ˈbɪdɛnz /; / ˈbaɪdɛnz / (BrE)
a genus of flowering plants in the daisy family (Asteraceae), commonly known as bur marigolds or beggarticks, with yellow, daisy-like flowers and barbed seeds that stick to animals or clothing
