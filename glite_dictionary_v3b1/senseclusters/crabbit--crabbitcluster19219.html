crabbit

noun
irritable person
Pronunciation: / ˈkræbɪt / (BrE)
a person who is bad-tempered or easily irritated, especially in Scottish or regional dialects

Examples

A crabbit in town
The old man was known as a crabbit who shouted at children playing outside.
After a long, stressful day, she became a real crabbit at home.
Nobody wanted to sit next to the crabbit on the bus because he always complained.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful person - someone who is happy and pleasant
  • synonym: grouch - a person who complains a lot or is often in a bad mood
  • synonym: grump - someone who is easily annoyed or complains often
