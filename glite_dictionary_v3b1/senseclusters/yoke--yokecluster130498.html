yoke

noun
pair of draft animals joined together
4.9
Pronunciation: / joʊk / (AmE), / jəʊk / (BrE)
A pair of draft animals, such as oxen, that are joined together by a yoke and work as a team.

Examples

A yoke of oxen
A single yoke can accomplish much more than an individual animal working alone.
The wagon was pulled by a yoke of sturdy oxen.
They purchased a new yoke to help plow the fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: team - a group of animals pulling together
