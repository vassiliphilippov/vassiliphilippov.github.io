exfil

verb
to extract covertly
Pronunciation: / ˈɛksfɪl / (AmE), / ˈɛksfɪl / (BrE)
to remove or withdraw people or data from a dangerous, hostile, or sensitive area, especially in a covert operation

Examples

To exfil a team
The special forces unit managed to exfil the informant without being detected.
After collecting the data, the hackers needed to exfil it before the system locked down.
The plan was to exfil all personnel from the embassy by midnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evacuate - to remove people from a dangerous place
  • synonym: extract - to remove someone or something from a place, especially with effort
  • antonym: infiltrate - to enter or gain access to a place secretly
