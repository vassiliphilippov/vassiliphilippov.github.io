vulturous

adjective
preying on others; like a vulture
Pronunciation: / ˈvʌltʃərəs / (AmE), / ˈvʌltʃərəs / (BrE)
living by preying on other animals, especially by catching and consuming living prey; resembling a vulture in predatory habits or appearance.

Examples

Her vulturous gaze made everyone at the table uncomfortable.

The vulturous hawk circled above, searching for its next meal.
