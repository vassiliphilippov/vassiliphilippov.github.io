preadult

noun
not yet adult
Pronunciation: / priːˈædʌlt / (AmE), / priːˈædʌlt / (BrE)
an organism that has not yet reached full adult development

Examples

An insect preadult
The preadult stage is crucial for the insect's growth.
Scientists observed the behavior of preadult frogs in the laboratory.
Nutrition during the preadult period can affect an animal's health as an adult.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adult - a fully developed and mature organism
  • synonym: juvenile - a young organism not yet fully grown
