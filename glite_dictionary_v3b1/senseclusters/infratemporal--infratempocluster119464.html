infratemporal

noun
area below temporal bone
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfrəˈtɛmpərəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnfrəˈtɛmpərəl / (BrE)
the region or fossa located beneath the temporal bone of the skull, often referenced in anatomy or surgery

Examples

Infratemporal fossa
The surgeon accessed the infratemporal to reach the deep-seated tumor.
Injuries to the infratemporal can affect important nerves and blood vessels.
Detailed knowledge of the infratemporal is essential for maxillofacial surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infratemporal fossa - the anatomical space beneath the temporal bone
  • antonym: temporal fossa - the shallow depression on the side of the skull above the zygomatic arch
