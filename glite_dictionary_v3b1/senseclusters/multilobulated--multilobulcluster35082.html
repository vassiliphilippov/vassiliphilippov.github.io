multilobulated

adjective
with many lobes
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈloʊbjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈləʊbjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
having or divided into several lobes or rounded sections

Examples

A multilobulated mass
The MRI revealed a multilobulated mass in the patient's liver.
Doctors noted that the cyst was multilobulated, making surgery more complex.
A multilobulated structure can sometimes indicate a benign tumor in medical imaging.
