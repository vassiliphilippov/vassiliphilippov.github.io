eccentrically

adverb
in a way not centered; off-center
8.5 5.8
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈsen.trɪ.kəl.i/ /; / /ɪk.ˈsen.trɪ.kliː/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈsen.trɪ.kəl.i/ /; / /ɪk.ˈsen.trɪ.kliː/ (BrE)
