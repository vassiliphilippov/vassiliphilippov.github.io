Pyrrhonian

noun
skeptical philosopher
Pronunciation: / pɪˈroʊniən / (AmE), / pɪˈrəʊniən / (BrE)
a follower or advocate of Pyrrhonism, a philosophy that promotes extreme skepticism and doubts the possibility of certain knowledge

Examples

A Pyrrhonian thinker
The Pyrrhonian questioned every claim made during the debate, refusing to accept anything as certain.
As a Pyrrhonian, he believed that true knowledge is impossible to attain.
Many philosophers have been influenced by the Pyrrhonian approach to skepticism.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dogmatist - a person who lays down principles as undeniably true
  • synonym: skeptic - a person who questions or doubts accepted opinions
