winterization

noun
preparing for winter
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌwɪntərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌwɪntəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of preparing something, such as a building or vehicle, for use or protection during winter conditions

Examples

Winterization of a house
The winterization of the house included sealing windows and insulating pipes.
Proper winterization of your car can prevent engine problems during cold weather.
Farmers invest in winterization to protect their equipment from freezing temperatures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insulation - material or process used to prevent heat loss
  • synonym: preparation - the act of getting something ready
