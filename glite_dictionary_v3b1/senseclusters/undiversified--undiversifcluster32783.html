undiversified

adjective
not varied or diversified
3.1 5.7
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dɪ.ˈvɜːr.sɪ.faɪd/ (AmE), /ʌn.dɪ.ˈvɜː.sɪ.faɪd/ (BrE)
Not involving a variety of different types; lacking diversification.

Examples

An undiversified economy
An undiversified economy can be severely affected by the failure of a single industry.
Her skills were too undiversified for the demands of a modern workplace.
The company's undiversified portfolio left it vulnerable to market changes.
