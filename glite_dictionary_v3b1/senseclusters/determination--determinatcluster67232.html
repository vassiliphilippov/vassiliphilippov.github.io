determination

noun
an official decision or judgment
Pronunciation: / dɪˌtɜrmɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌtɜːmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
an official decision or judgment made after consideration

Examples

Legal determination
After much discussion, the judge made her final determination on the matter.
The committee's determination was announced at the end of the hearing.
The panel's determination reflected all the available evidence.
