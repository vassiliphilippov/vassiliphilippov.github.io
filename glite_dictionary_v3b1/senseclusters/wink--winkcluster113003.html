wink

noun
a very short period of time
Pronunciation: / wɪŋk / (AmE), / wɪŋk / (BrE)
An extremely short period of time; a moment.

Examples

In a wink
He finished the test in a wink, surprising everyone in the room.
She promised to call back in a wink, but it took her hours.
The magician vanished in a wink, leaving the audience amazed.
