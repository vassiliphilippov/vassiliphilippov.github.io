sea swallow

noun
common tern, a seabird
Pronunciation: / si ˈswɑloʊ / (AmE), / si ˈswɒləʊ / (BrE)
a common tern of Eurasia and America with white, black, and grey plumage

Examples

A sea swallow bird
During migration, thousands of sea swallows can be spotted along the coast.
Fishermen often refer to the common tern as the sea swallow because of its flight pattern.
The sea swallow was seen diving gracefully over the waves.
