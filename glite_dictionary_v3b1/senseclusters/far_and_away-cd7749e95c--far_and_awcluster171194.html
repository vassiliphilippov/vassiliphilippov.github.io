far and away

adverb
by a considerable margin
Pronunciation: / fɑr ənd əˈweɪ / (AmE), / fɑː ənd əˈweɪ / (BrE)
by a very large amount; used to strongly emphasize that something is much more or much better than others

Examples

Far and away the best
She is far and away the most qualified candidate for the position.
That was far and away the best performance of the evening.
This is far and away the most comfortable chair I've ever owned.
His latest film is far and away more successful than his previous works.
She was far and away the most talented artist at the exhibition.
This is far and away the best pizza I've ever tasted.
