macroglobulinemia

noun
high blood proteins

Pronunciation: /mæk.rə.ɡloʊ.bjʊ.lɪ.ˈniː.mɪ.ə/ (AmE), /mæk.rə.ɡlɒb.jʊ.lɪ.ˈniː.mɪ.ə/ (BrE)
