macroglobulinemia

noun
high blood proteins
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmækroʊˌɡlɑːbjəˌlɪˈmiːə / (AmE), / ˌmakrəʊˌɡlɒbjʊlɪˈmiːə / (BrE)
a rare medical condition where there is too much of a large protein (macroglobulin) in the blood, which can cause health problems
