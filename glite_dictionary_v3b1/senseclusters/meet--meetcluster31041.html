meet

noun
athletic or sporting event
Pronunciation: / mit / (AmE), / miːt / (BrE)
an event at which a number of athletic or sporting contests take place

Examples

A swimming meet
Dozens of athletes gathered at the regional gymnastics meet last weekend.
She qualified for the state swimming meet after her strong performance.
The school hosted an annual track meet every spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tournament - a series of contests in a particular sport
