codefendant

noun
person jointly charged in a legal case
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊ.dɪˈfɛndənt / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.dɪˈfɛndənt / (BrE)
a person who is charged alongside one or more others in the same legal case

Examples

A codefendant in court
During the trial, each codefendant had their own attorney representing them.
The judge addressed both codefendant and lead defendant about their charges.
The prosecutor questioned the codefendant about his role in the crime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defendant - a person or group against whom a legal action is brought
