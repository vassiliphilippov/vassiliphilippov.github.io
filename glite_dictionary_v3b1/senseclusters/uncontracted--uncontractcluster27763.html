uncontracted

adjective
not tightened
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈtræktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈtræktɪd / (BrE)
not tightened, shortened, or drawn together; in a relaxed or extended state, especially of muscles or tissues

Examples

Uncontracted muscle
After the massage her calf muscles were uncontracted and felt much more relaxed.
The doctor noted that the patient's arm remained uncontracted during the examination.
Yoga helps keep the body flexible and the muscles uncontracted.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contracted - tightened or drawn together
  • synonym: relaxed - not tense or tight
