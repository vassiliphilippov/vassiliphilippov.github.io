doing business as

A name under which a business or corporation operates that is not its legal, registered name.

Examples

Doing business as a name
All legal documents must include the corporation's registered name and its doing business as name.
She registered her freelance graphic design company doing business as "Creative Expressions".
The café operates under the name "Sunrise Eats" doing business as "Sunrise Café, LLC".
