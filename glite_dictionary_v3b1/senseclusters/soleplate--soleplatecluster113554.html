soleplate

noun
base of iron or wall
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsoʊl.pleɪt/ (AmE), /ˈsəʊl.pleɪt/ (BrE)
the flat metal base of an iron or the bottom horizontal plate in construction, such as at the base of a wall

Examples

Soleplate of the iron
The soleplate of the iron must be kept clean for smooth ironing.
Carpenters installed the wooden soleplate before building the wall frame.
If the soleplate is damaged, the iron may not glide properly over clothes.
