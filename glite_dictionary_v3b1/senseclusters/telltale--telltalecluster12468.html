telltale

noun
person who reveals secrets or informs on others
2.1 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtel.teɪl/ (AmE), /ˈtel.teɪl/ (BrE)
a person, especially a child, who reveals secrets or informs on others, often indiscreetly or out of spite

Examples

A telltale child
In the playground, being called a telltale was considered an insult among the children.
No one wanted to play with the telltale who always ran to the teacher with gossip.
She accused her little brother of being a telltale after he revealed her secret.

Related Terms

  • synonym: informer - a person who informs, especially to authorities
  • synonym: snitch - a person who betrays others by disclosing secrets or informing
