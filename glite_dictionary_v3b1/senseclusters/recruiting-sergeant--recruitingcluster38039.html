recruiting-sergeant

noun
sergeant in charge of enlistment
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkrutɪŋ ˌsɑrdʒənt / (AmE), / rɪˈkruːtɪŋ ˌsɑːdʒənt / (BrE)
A sergeant whose duty is to enlist new members, especially for military service.

Examples

A recruiting-sergeant speech
As a recruiting-sergeant, his persuasive speeches attracted many volunteers.
In the nineteenth century, the role of a recruiting-sergeant was crucial during wartime.
The recruiting-sergeant stood in the town square, encouraging young men to join the army.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recruiter - a person who enlists people as new members of an organization, especially the military
