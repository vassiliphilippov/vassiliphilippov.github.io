stairstep

noun
step in staircase
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛrˌstɛp / (AmE), / ˈsteəˌstɛp / (BrE)
a single step in a staircase or something that has the shape of such a step

Examples

A wooden stairstep
She tripped on the last stairstep as she hurried downstairs.
The old house had a creaky stairstep that always made noise at night.
The mountain path rose in a stairstep pattern up the hillside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: riser - the vertical part between two steps in a staircase
  • synonym: tread - the horizontal part of a step on a staircase
