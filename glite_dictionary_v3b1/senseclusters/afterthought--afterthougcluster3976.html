afterthought

noun
a subsequent reconsideration or thought
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈæf.tɚˌθɔt / (AmE), / ˈɑːf.təˌθɔːt / (BrE)
a thought or thing that comes to mind or is added after something else has already been decided or done

Examples

An afterthought idea
After submitting her application, she had an afterthought about adding more details to her resume.
He bought the expensive watch on impulse but soon had an afterthought about whether he really needed it.
The guest room was clearly an afterthought in the design of the house.
Her apology seemed like an afterthought rather than something she truly meant.
Originally, there was no deck; it was built as an afterthought.
They added a sunroom as an afterthought to the house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addition - something added to something else
  • synonym: second thought - a feeling of doubt or reconsideration about a decision
