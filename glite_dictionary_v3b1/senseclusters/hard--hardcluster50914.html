hard

adjective
excessively indulging, especially in alcohol
Pronunciation: / hɑrd / (AmE), / hɑːd / (BrE)
(of a person) tending to indulge excessively in bodily appetites, especially drinking alcohol to excess.

Examples

Hard drinker
After years of being a hard smoker, he finally quit.
His uncle was known as a hard drinker throughout the small town.
She warned him not to become a hard gambler like his father.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heavy - describing a person who frequently engages in an activity to excess (e.g., heavy drinker)
