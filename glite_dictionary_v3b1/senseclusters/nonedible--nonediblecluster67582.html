nonedible

adjective
not safe to eat
4.0
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈɛdəbl̩ / (AmE), / nɒnˈɛdəbl̩ / (BrE)

Examples

Nonedible berries

Some household products contain nonedible ingredients that are dangerous if swallowed.
