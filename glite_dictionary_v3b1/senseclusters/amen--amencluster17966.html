amen

interjection
prayer ending; agreement
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɑːˈmɛn / (AmE), / ɑːˈmɛn / (BrE)
a word used at the end of a prayer or to express strong agreement or affirmation

Examples

Say amen after prayer
The congregation ended the prayer with a heartfelt amen.
When she finished her speech, several people in the audience responded with a loud amen.
He nodded and said amen to show his strong agreement with the proposal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: so be it - an expression meaning 'let it be so'
  • synonym: truly - used to express sincerity or agreement
