Bidone award

noun
annual satirical football award (Italy)
8.9
Pronunciation: / biˈdoʊn əˈwɔrd / (AmE), / bɪˈdəʊn əˌwɔːd / (BrE)
An annual satirical award given to the most disappointing football player in Serie A (Italian football league) for that season.

Examples

The Bidone award winner
After a poor season, the striker was nominated for the Bidone award.
Many players hope to avoid being associated with the Bidone award at all costs.
The Bidone award is infamous among football fans for highlighting the league's greatest disappointments.
