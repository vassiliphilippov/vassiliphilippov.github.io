anti-narcotics

noun
drug prevention efforts
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌænti nɑrˈkɑtɪks / (AmE), / ˌænti nɑːˈkɒtɪks / (BrE)
measures, policies, or actions intended to prevent illegal drug use, distribution, or trafficking

Examples

Anti-narcotics operations

Several anti-narcotics campaigns were launched to educate young people about the dangers of drugs.
International cooperation is essential for effective anti-narcotics enforcement.
