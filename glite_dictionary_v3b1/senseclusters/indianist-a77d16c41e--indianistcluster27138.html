Indianist

noun
student of Native cultures
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiənɪst / (AmE), / ˈɪndiənɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies, imitates, or is interested in Native American cultures

Examples

Indianist studies Native Americans
The museum hired an Indianist to curate the new exhibit.
As an Indianist, she spent years researching Native American art and traditions.
Many Indianists have contributed to the preservation of indigenous languages and customs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropologist - a person who studies human societies and cultures
  • synonym: ethnologist - a person who studies different cultures and peoples
