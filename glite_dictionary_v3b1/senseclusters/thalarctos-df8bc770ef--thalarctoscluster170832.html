Thalarctos

noun
genus of polar bears (taxonomy)
Pronunciation: / θəˈlɑrk.toʊs /; / θəˈlɑrktəs / (AmE), / θəˈlɑːk.tɒs /; / θəˈlɑːktɒs / (BrE)
a former or alternative genus name for polar bears, sometimes not considered different from the genus Ursus
