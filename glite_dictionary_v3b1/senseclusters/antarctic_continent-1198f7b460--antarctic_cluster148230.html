Antarctic continent

noun
the icy southernmost continent
2.1
Pronunciation: / ænˈtɑːrk.tɪk ˈkɑːn.tɪ.nənt / (AmE), / ænˈtɑːk.tɪk ˈkɒn.tɪ.nənt / (BrE)
the extremely cold continent surrounding the South Pole, almost entirely within the Antarctic Circle and covered by a thick ice cap
