godforsaken

adjective
remote or desolate place
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑd.fərˌseɪ.kən / (AmE), / ˈɡɒd.fəˌseɪ.kən / (BrE)
(of a place) remote, dismal, or neglected; lacking any signs of activity or comfort.

Examples

A godforsaken place
It’s a godforsaken building, crumbling and empty since the factory closed.
She grew up in a godforsaken village miles from the nearest town.
We drove through a godforsaken stretch of desert that seemed to go on forever.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abandoned - left empty or deserted, no longer cared for
  • synonym: desolate - deserted and bleak, especially of a place
