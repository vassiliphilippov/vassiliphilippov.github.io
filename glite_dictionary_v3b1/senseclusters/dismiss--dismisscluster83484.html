dismiss

verb
refuse to consider or pay attention to
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈmɪs / (AmE), / dɪsˈmɪs / (BrE)
to decide that something or someone is not worth thinking about or considering; to refuse to give attention or credence to

Examples

Dismiss an idea
Many people dismiss myths without considering their historical significance.
She quickly dismissed his comments as irrelevant to the discussion.
The committee chose to dismiss the allegations due to a lack of evidence.

  • synonym: ignore - to pay no attention to
