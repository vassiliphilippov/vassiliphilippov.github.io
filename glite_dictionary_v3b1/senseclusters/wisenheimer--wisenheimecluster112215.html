wisenheimer

noun
someone making snide, conceited remarks
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪzənˌhaɪmər / (AmE), / ˈwaɪz(ə)nˌhaɪmə / (BrE)
A person who makes conceited, sardonic, or insolent remarks; a smart-aleck.

Examples

A wisenheimer remark
His wisenheimer attitude quickly got on everyone's nerves during the meeting.
Stop being such a wisenheimer and let your sister finish talking.
Teachers often have to deal with at least one wisenheimer in every class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smart aleck - a person who is irritatingly clever or sarcastic
  • synonym: wise guy - a person who is annoying because they think they know everything
