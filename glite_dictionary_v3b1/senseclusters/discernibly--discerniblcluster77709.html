discernibly

adverb
clearly noticeably
5.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɜrnəbli / (AmE), / dɪˈsɜːnəbli / (BrE)
in a way that can be seen, noticed, or recognized clearly

Examples

More discernibly bright
The mountain peaks were discernibly brighter after the clouds cleared.
Her mood had discernibly improved since the good news arrived.
The quality of the new product is discernibly better than the old version.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indistinctly - in a way that is not clear or easy to recognize
  • synonym: noticeably - in a way that is easy to see or recognize
