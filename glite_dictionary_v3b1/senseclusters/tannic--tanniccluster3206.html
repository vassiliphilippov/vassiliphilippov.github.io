tannic

adjective
relating to or containing tannin
9.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtæn.ɪk/ (AmE), /ˈtæn.ɪk/ (BrE)
Containing or derived from tannin; having the properties of tannin, especially relating to an astringent taste.

Examples

Tannic taste
Some teas are more tannic than others, producing a dry, puckering sensation.
The tannic acid in oak barrels influences the flavor of aged spirits.
The wine had a tannic aftertaste that lingered on the palate.
