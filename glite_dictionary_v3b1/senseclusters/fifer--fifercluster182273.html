fifer

noun
fife player
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪfər / (AmE), / ˈfaɪfə / (BrE)
a person who plays the fife, especially as part of a military band

Examples

The fifer led the soldiers with a bright melody during the parade.
As a skilled fifer, she performed at many historical reenactments.
The band included a fifer, two drummers, and a bugler.
The fifer marched at the front of the regiment, playing a spirited melody.
During the ceremony, a skilled fifer performed traditional military tunes.
Young musicians admired the fifer for his precise and lively playing.
