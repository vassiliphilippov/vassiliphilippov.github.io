compassionless

adjective
without sympathy
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpæʃənləs / (AmE), / kəmˈpæʃənləs / (BrE)
showing no sympathy or concern for the suffering of others

Examples

A compassionless decision
The manager's compassionless decision upset many employees who expected more understanding.
Her compassionless response to the tragedy shocked everyone in the community.
A compassionless leader often fails to inspire loyalty among their followers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compassionate - showing sympathy and concern for others
  • synonym: heartless - showing no kindness or compassion
  • synonym: merciless - showing no mercy or pity
