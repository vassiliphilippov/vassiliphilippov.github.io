tour guide

noun
person who leads and informs tourists
Pronunciation: / tʊr ɡaɪd / (AmE), / tʊə ɡaɪd / (BrE)
a person employed to show tourists around places of interest and provide information about them

Examples

A friendly tour guide
Our tour guide explained the history of the old castle in great detail.
The tour guide answered all of our questions during the museum visit.
We followed the tour guide through the narrow streets of Rome.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guide - someone who shows others the way or gives information
