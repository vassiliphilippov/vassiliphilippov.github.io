stell

noun
animal enclosure
Pronunciation: / stɛl / (AmE), / stɛl / (BrE)
a small enclosure or shelter for animals, especially used in dialectal English

Examples

Sheep stell
The shepherd built a simple stell to protect the sheep from the wind.
At night, the cows were gathered into the stell for safety.
Old farmhouses often had a stone stell beside the main barn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pen - an enclosure for confining animals
  • synonym: stall - a compartment for an animal in a stable or barn
