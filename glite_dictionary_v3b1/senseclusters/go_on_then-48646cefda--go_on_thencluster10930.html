go on then

interjection
expression of encouragement or permission
1.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /goʊ ɑːn ðen/ /; / /goʊ ɑːn ðən/ /; / /goʊ ɔːn ðen/ (AmE), /gəʊ ɒn ðen/ /; / /gəʊ ɒn ðən/ (BrE)
