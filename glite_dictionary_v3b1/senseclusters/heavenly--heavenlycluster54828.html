Synonyms

  • celestial
    6.9 2.15/m
    celestial is used for things in the sky or related to heaven, while heavenly describes something belonging to the divine.
  • supernal
    9.9 0.10/m
    supernal sounds formal and poetic for divine matters, while heavenly is more general and common in speech.
  • empyrean
    9.9 0.03/m
    empyrean names the highest, sky-like heaven in poetry, while heavenly often covers general divine or spiritual things.
  • paradisal
    8.6 0.06/m
    paradisal suggests a blissful, garden-like paradise feeling, while heavenly means belonging to the divine or spiritual realm.
  • ultraterrene
    9.9 0.00/m
    ultraterrene implies not of the earth and otherworldly in feeling, while heavenly specifically links to heaven or the divine.
  • ethereal
    8.8 1.68/m
    ethereal feels very light, delicate, or perfect for this world, while heavenly means directly of the divine or heaven.
