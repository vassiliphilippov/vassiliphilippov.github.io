bar hop

verb
visit several bars or pubs in succession
6.5
Pronunciation: / bɑr hɑp / (AmE), / bɑː hɒp / (BrE)
to go from one bar or pub to another, typically drinking alcohol at each place, often as a social activity

Examples

To bar hop with friends
On weekends, many students enjoy bar hopping with friends.
They bar hopped all night during their trip to New Orleans.
We decided to bar hop downtown to celebrate Jane's birthday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pub crawl - to visit a series of pubs or bars in one session
