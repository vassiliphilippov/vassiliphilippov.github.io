kindergartener

noun
child in preschool or kindergarten
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪndɚˌɡɑrdnɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɪndəˌɡɑːdnə / (BrE)
a child who attends a preschool or kindergarten, typically around five or six years old
