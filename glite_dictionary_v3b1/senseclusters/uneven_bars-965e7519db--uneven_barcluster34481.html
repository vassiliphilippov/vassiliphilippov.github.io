uneven bars

noun
gymnastics apparatus with bars at different heights
7.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈivən bɑrz / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːvən bɑːz / (BrE)
a pair of parallel bars set at different heights, used in women's gymnastics for exercises and routines
