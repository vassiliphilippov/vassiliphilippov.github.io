bang out

verb
produce quickly or with little effort
Pronunciation: / bæŋ aʊt / (AmE), / bæŋ aʊt / (BrE)
to produce something quickly or with little effort, especially in a mechanical or casual way

Examples

To bang out a report
He likes to bang out a few songs on his guitar when he's bored.
I can bang out this report in an hour if I focus.
She managed to bang out three assignments before lunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crank out - to produce large quantities of something quickly, often with less care
  • synonym: knock out - to produce or complete something quickly, especially without care; informal
