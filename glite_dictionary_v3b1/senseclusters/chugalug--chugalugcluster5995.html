chugalug

interjection
drinking encouragement
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌɡəˌlʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌɡəˌlʌɡ / (BrE)
an exclamation used to encourage someone to drink quickly, especially in a social setting

Examples

Chugalug! Drink up!
Everyone at the table shouted chugalug before downing their drinks together.
He raised his glass and yelled chugalug, making everyone laugh at the party.
When someone called out chugalug, the group started a friendly drinking contest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheers - an exclamation used to toast before drinking
