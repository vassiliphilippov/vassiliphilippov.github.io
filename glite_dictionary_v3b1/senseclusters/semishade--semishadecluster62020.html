semishade

noun
partial shade
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmiˌʃeɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɛmiˌʃeɪd / (BrE)
a condition of partial shade, especially suitable for plants that require limited sunlight

Examples

In semishade
Many woodland flowers thrive in semishade rather than in direct sunlight.
The gardener recommended planting the herbs in semishade to prevent their leaves from burning.
Hostas prefer semishade and will wilt if exposed to too much sun.
