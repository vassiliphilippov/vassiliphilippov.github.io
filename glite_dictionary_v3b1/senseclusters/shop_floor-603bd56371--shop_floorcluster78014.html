shop floor

noun
factory production area
Pronunciation: / ʃɑp flɔr / (AmE), / ʃɒp flɔː / (BrE)
the area in a factory where manufacturing or assembly work is done, typically housing the machinery and production workers

Examples

The shop floor area
All new employees must spend time working on the shop floor before moving to management positions.
Automation has transformed the shop floor in many modern factories.
Union representatives regularly visit the shop floor to discuss concerns with workers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: workshop - room or building where manufacturing or repair takes place
