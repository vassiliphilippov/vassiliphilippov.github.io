Synonyms

  • lassitude

    lassitude is a deep physical or mental tiredness that makes movement hard, while languor often feels calm, pleasant stillness.
  • lethargy
    6.3 0.98/m
    lethargy is a heavy, sleepy tiredness often caused by illness, while languor can be a gentle, relaxed lack of energy.
  • listlessness
    6.8 0.19/m
    listlessness is a lack of interest and enthusiasm that makes life feel dull, while languor usually includes calm, pleasant stillness.
  • sluggishness
    4.4 0.26/m
    sluggishness is slow, inactive movement or work that lacks speed, while languor often means quiet, dreamy tiredness rather than slowness.
