rigor mortis

noun
post-mortem muscle stiffness
Pronunciation: / ˌrɪɡər ˈmɔrtɪs / (AmE), / ˌrɪɡə ˈmɔːtɪs / (BrE)
the temporary stiffening and rigidity of the muscles and joints that happens a few hours after death
