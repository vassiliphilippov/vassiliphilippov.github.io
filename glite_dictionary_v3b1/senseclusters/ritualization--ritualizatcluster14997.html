ritualization

noun
development of animal signals
9.0
Pronunciation: /rɪt.ʃuː.ə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪt.juː.ə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /rɪt.ʃuː.ə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪt.juː.ə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process by which ordinary actions in animals become fixed, exaggerated signals used for communication or display

Examples

Animal ritualization
The ritualization of courtship dances helps birds attract mates and avoid conflict.
Scientists observed the ritualization of feeding gestures in certain primate groups.
Through ritualization, simple movements can become important signals in animal societies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: display - a behavior or action shown by animals to communicate
