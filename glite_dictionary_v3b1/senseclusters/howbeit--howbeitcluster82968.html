howbeit

conjunction
although; even though
9.9
Pronunciation: / haʊˈbiːɪt / (AmE), / haʊˈbiːɪt / (BrE)
although; even though—used to introduce a contrast or concession, mainly in older or literary English

Examples

Howbeit tired
The journey was long, howbeit rewarding for those who completed it.
He accepted the offer, howbeit with some hesitation.
The castle stood strong, howbeit weathered by centuries of storms.
