lock

verb
build canal locks for navigation
6.8
Pronunciation: / lɑk / (AmE), / lɒk / (BrE)
to construct or pass through a lock in a canal or river to allow boats to move between different water levels

Examples

They plan to lock the canal, making it easier for barges to travel between towns.
Several barges locked upstream during the heavy rains.
The boat will lock through the canal next morning.
We had to lock through three times before reaching the lake.
