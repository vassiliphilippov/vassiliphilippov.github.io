work

verb
to function or operate as intended
Pronunciation: / wɝk /; / wɝːk / (AmE), / wɜːk / (BrE)
to function, operate, or be effective (machines, plans, systems, etc.)

Examples

The machine works well
If your phone doesn’t work, try charging it for a while.
The elevator didn’t work this morning, so I had to take the stairs.
This old clock still works after all these years.
He was hired to work the soundboard during the concert.
The campaign manager asked her to work the phones and call potential voters.
Volunteers work the soup kitchen every weekend to help the community.
He quickly learned how to work the cash register at his new job.
She showed me how to work the new coffee machine in the office.
The technician expertly worked the control panel to restart the system.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fail - to be unsuccessful; not to work as expected
  • synonym: function - to work or operate in the correct way
  • antonym: malfunction - to fail to work properly
  • synonym: operate - to control the functioning of a machine or device
