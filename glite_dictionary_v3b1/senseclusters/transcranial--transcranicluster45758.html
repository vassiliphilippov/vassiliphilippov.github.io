transcranial

adjective
across the skull
7.4
Pronunciation: / trænsˈkreɪniəl / (AmE), / trænzˈkreɪniəl / (BrE)
passing through or performed across the skull, especially referring to medical procedures or techniques

Examples

Transcranial magnetic stimulation
The neurologist recommended transcranial magnetic stimulation as a non-invasive treatment option.
Researchers are studying the effects of transcranial electrical stimulation on memory and learning.
A transcranial Doppler ultrasound can help assess blood flow in the brain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intracranial - located or occurring within the skull
