underground market

noun
illegal or unofficial trade network
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˌɡraʊnd ˈmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˌɡraʊnd ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
A system or network of buying and selling goods or services that operates illegally or unofficially, outside of government regulation.

Examples

An underground market for goods
After the ban, an underground market for cigarettes quickly emerged in the city.
Authorities cracked down on the underground market that was selling counterfeit electronics.
Collectors often turn to the underground market to find rare coins that are unavailable legally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: black market - illegal trade in goods or services
