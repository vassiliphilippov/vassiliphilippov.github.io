reorganized

adjective
arranged again; restructured
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzd /; / riˈɔrɡəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɔːɡənaɪzd /; / riˈɔːɡənaɪzd / (BrE)
arranged or structured again, often to improve efficiency or adapt to new conditions

Examples

A reorganized company
After the merger, the company became a reorganized entity with a new management structure.
Many businesses emerged as reorganized and more efficient after the financial crisis.
The reorganized files made it much easier to find the documents I needed.
After the merger, the staff was completely reorganised into new teams.
Our department is now reorganised to improve communication and productivity.
The company emerged stronger after being reorganised last year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disorganized - not properly arranged or lacking organization
  • synonym: reorganized - organized again or differently (American spelling)
  • synonym: restructured - organized differently or rebuilt, often for improvement
