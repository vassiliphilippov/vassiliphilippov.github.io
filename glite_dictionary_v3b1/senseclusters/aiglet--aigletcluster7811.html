aiglet

noun
sheath at end of shoelace or ribbon
8.7 9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈeɪ.glət/ /; / /ˈeɪ.glet/ (AmE), /ˈeɪ.glət/ /; / /ˈeɪ.glet/ (BrE)
