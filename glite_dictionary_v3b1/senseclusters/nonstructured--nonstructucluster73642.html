nonstructured

adjective
without clear structure
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈstrʌktʃərd / (AmE), / nɒnˈstrʌktʃəd / (BrE)
not organized or lacking a clear or defined structure, plan, or pattern

Examples

The scientist observed a nonstructured material that did not form any regular patterns.
Unlike crystals, glass is a nonstructured solid with atoms arranged randomly.
The team struggled to analyze the nonstructured data because it lacked any clear organization.
The meeting was completely nonstructured, allowing everyone to speak freely.
Researchers found it difficult to analyze the nonstructured data from various sources.
Her teaching style is intentionally nonstructured to encourage creativity among students.
The workshop was intentionally nonstructured to allow participants to explore topics freely.
He enjoys nonstructured activities where creativity is encouraged over strict guidelines.
A nonstructured interview can help reveal more honest and spontaneous answers.
