Richard Nixon

proper noun
37th U.S. President; resigned after Watergate
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃərd ˈnɪksən / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃəd ˈnɪksən / (BrE)
(1913–1994) The 37th President of the United States (1969–1974), previously Vice President (1953–1961) under Eisenhower; resigned after the Watergate scandal.
