dish

verb
to gossip
6.8
Pronunciation: / dɪʃ / (AmE), / dɪʃ / (BrE)
to talk about other people's private lives or share gossip, often in a casual or informal way

Examples

To dish gossip
They love to dish about celebrities over lunch.
After the meeting, everyone gathered to dish on the latest office rumors.
She called her friend to dish about what happened at the party.

  • antonym: conceal - to keep something secret or hidden
  • synonym: gossip - to talk about the private lives of others
