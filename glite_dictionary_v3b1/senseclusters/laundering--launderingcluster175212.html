laundering

noun
washing or disguising money
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔndərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɔːndərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of washing clothes or making illegally obtained money appear legal

Examples

Money laundering
The laundering of dirty clothes is a weekly chore in many households.
Authorities are investigating the laundering of funds through offshore accounts.
Proper laundering of uniforms is required for all employees in the hospital.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleaning - the act of making something clean, especially by washing
  • synonym: money laundering - the process of making illegally gained money appear legal
