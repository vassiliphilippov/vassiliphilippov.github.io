caddy

noun
golf assistant or container
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkædi / (AmE), / ˈkædi / (BrE)
a person who carries a golfer's clubs and gives advice during a game, or a small container for storing items such as tea or cutlery

Examples

Golf caddy
He hired a caddy to help him during the golf tournament.
She keeps her favorite teas in a decorative caddy on the kitchen shelf.
The caddy offered advice on which club to use for the next shot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: container - an object used for holding or storing things
  • synonym: golf assistant - a person who helps a golfer by carrying clubs and giving advice
