day lark

noun
person who is active during the day
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪ ˌlɑrk / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ ˌlɑːk / (BrE)
A person who is naturally active, alert, or awake during the day, especially in the morning.

Examples

A day lark person
Although he's a true day lark, he has no trouble waking up early for work.
My sister is a day lark and loves going for a run at sunrise.
Unlike most college students, Emma is a day lark who finishes assignments before noon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: night owl - a person who is habitually active or awake at night
