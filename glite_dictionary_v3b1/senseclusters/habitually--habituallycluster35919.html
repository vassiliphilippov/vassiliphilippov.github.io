habitually

adverb
in a habitual manner; regularly by habit
3.5
Pronunciation: / həˈbɪtʃ.u.əli / (AmE), / həˈbɪt.ju.əli / (BrE)
in a way that is done regularly or as a habit; according to custom or usual practice

Examples

To habitually interrupt
Despite her efforts, he habitually interrupts people during meetings.
He habitually arrives at the office before anyone else.
She habitually reads for an hour before going to bed each night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: regularly - at uniform intervals; commonly or on a regular schedule
