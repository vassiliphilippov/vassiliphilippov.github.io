hockshop

noun
shop for stolen goods
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑkˌʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈhɒkˌʃɒp / (BrE)
a shop where stolen goods are knowingly bought and sold; a place that acts as a fence for stolen items

Examples

Stolen goods hockshop
The detective suspected the old hockshop was a front for stolen electronics.
After the burglary, the thieves tried to sell the jewelry at a local hockshop.
Neighbors complained that the hockshop attracted suspicious activity to the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fence - a person or business that buys and sells stolen goods
  • degree lower: pawnshop - a shop where people can pawn or sell items, not necessarily stolen
  • antonym: police station - a place where crimes are reported and investigated
