noun

6.1 0.8/m
Pronunciation: / É‘ps / (AmE), / É’ps / (BrE)
Informal short for 'operations': the department or team responsible for running, maintaining, and supporting systems, services, or business operations.

Examples

Ops team meeting

Startups often outsource Ops tasks until they can hire a full operations team.
