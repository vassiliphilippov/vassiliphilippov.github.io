Ops

noun
operations department (informal)
6.1
Pronunciation: /ɑːps/ (AmE), /ɒps/ (BrE)
Informal short for 'operations': the department or team responsible for running, maintaining, and supporting systems, services, or business operations.

Examples

Ops team meeting
The Ops team coordinated with engineering to resolve the outage within minutes.
When the database failed, Ops rolled back the deployment and restored the previous version quickly.
Startups often outsource Ops tasks until they can hire a full operations team.
