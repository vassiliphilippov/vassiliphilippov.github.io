engineless

adjective
without an engine
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɛn.dʒɪn.ləs / (AmE), / ˈɛn.dʒɪn.ləs / (BrE)
not having an engine or not powered by an engine

Examples

An engineless glider
The engineless glider soared silently above the fields.
They designed an engineless vehicle that relies entirely on gravity for movement.
Many old boats were left engineless after their motors were removed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: motorless - not having a motor or engine
  • antonym: powered - having or using an engine or power source
