forasmuch

conjunction
since; because
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrəzˈmʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˌfɔːrəzˈmʌtʃ / (BrE)
because; since (used to introduce a reason, especially in older or formal English).

Examples

Forasmuch as it was true
Forasmuch as the evidence was clear, the jury reached a quick verdict.
He was excused from duty forasmuch as he was unwell that day.
Forasmuch as you have completed the task, you may leave early.
