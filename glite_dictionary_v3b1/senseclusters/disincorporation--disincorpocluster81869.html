disincorporation

noun
removal of corporate status
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɪnˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˌkɔːpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which a company, organization, or municipality loses its official legal status and is dissolved
