handbuild

verb
make pottery by hand without a wheel
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌbɪld / (AmE), / ˈhændˌbɪld / (BrE)
to make (a ceramic or pottery object) by hand rather than using a potter's wheel

Examples

To handbuild a bowl
In class, we learned how to handbuild a bowl using the coil method.
Many ancient pots were handbuilt before the invention of the wheel.
This famous potter handbuilds all of her vessels from local clay.

Related Terms
