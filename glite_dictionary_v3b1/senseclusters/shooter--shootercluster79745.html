shooter

noun
player attempting to score a goal or basket
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuːt̬ər / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtə / (BrE)
a player who attempts to score points by driving, kicking, or shooting the ball toward the goal or basket, especially in sports such as soccer or basketball

Examples

Soccer shooter
As a shooter, she has a powerful and accurate kick that often finds the net.
During the penalty, the shooter calmly directed the ball past the goalkeeper.
The team's best shooter scored 30 points in the basketball game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defender - a player who prevents the opposition from scoring
  • synonym: scorer - a player who scores points or goals
