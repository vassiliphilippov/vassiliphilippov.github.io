clock face

noun
surface of a clock showing the time
2.1 0.20/m
Pronunciation: / klÉ‘k feÉªs / (AmE), / klÉ’k feÉªs / (BrE)
the flat surface of a clock on which the hours and minutes are shown, usually with numbers and hands

Examples

The clock face shows time
She wiped the dust off the clock face to see what time it was.
The antique clock face was beautifully painted with roman numerals.
The hands on the clock face pointed to midnight as the room fell silent.
