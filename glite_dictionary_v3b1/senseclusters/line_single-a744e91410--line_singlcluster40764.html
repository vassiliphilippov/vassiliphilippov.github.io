line single

noun
baseball: single from a line drive
6.1
Pronunciation: /laɪn ˈsɪŋ.gəl/ (AmE), /laɪn ˈsɪŋ.gəl/ (BrE)
(in baseball) a hit in which the batter reaches first base by hitting a line drive.

Examples

A line single hit

The batter's line single drove in two runs to tie the game.
With two outs, she delivered a sharp line single past the shortstop.
