line single

noun
baseball: single from a line drive
6.1
Pronunciation: / laɪn ˈsɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / laɪn ˈsɪŋɡəl / (BrE)
(in baseball) a hit in which the batter reaches first base by hitting a line drive.

Examples

A line single hit
He hit a line single to center field in the third inning.
The batter's line single drove in two runs to tie the game.
With two outs, she delivered a sharp line single past the shortstop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: line drive - a batted ball that travels in a nearly straight line
  • synonym: single - a hit that allows the batter to reach first base
