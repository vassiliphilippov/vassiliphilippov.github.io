workforce

noun
people available for work
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːk.fɔɹs /; / ˈwɝrkˌfɔrs / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk.fɔːs /; / ˈwɜːkˌfɔːs / (BrE)
all the people who are employed or available for work in a company, industry, or country

Examples

A skilled workforce
A skilled workforce is essential for maintaining high productivity.
The company expanded its workforce to meet the growing demand for its products.
The government is introducing new training programs to prepare the workforce for technological changes.
Automation is expected to reduce the need for a large workforce in manufacturing.
The company's workforce has grown rapidly over the past decade.
Women now make up over half of the national workforce.
