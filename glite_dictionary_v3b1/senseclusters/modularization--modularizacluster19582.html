modularization

noun
dividing into modules
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːdʒələrɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɒdjʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of dividing something into separate, independent modules or parts

Examples

Thanks to modularization, the company could quickly adapt its products to different markets.
