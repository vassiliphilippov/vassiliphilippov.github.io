kaf

preposition
Hebrew 'as' prefix
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɑf / (AmE), / kɑːf / (BrE)
a Hebrew or Aramaic preposition, written as the letter כ, used as a prefix meaning 'as' or 'like'

Examples

Kaf prefix in Hebrew
In Biblical Hebrew, the prefix kaf is attached to nouns to mean 'like' or 'as'.
The word 'kohen' with a kaf prefix becomes 'like a priest' in Hebrew.
Students learned how the kaf prefix changes the meaning of Hebrew words in class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: as - used to compare or show similarity
  • synonym: like - having the same characteristics as; similar to
