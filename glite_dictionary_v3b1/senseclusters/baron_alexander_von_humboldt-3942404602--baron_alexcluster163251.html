Baron Alexander von Humboldt

proper noun
German naturalist and explorer (1769–1859)
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbærən ˌælɪɡˈzændər vɒn hʊmˈboʊlt / (AmE), / ˈbærən ˌælɪɡˈzandə vɒn ˈhʊmbɔːlt / (BrE)
(1769–1859) A German naturalist and explorer known for his scientific expeditions in Central and South America and for his influential work describing the natural world.
