Wakashan

noun
member of Indigenous Wakashan-speaking peoples
9.9
Pronunciation: / wɑːˈkɑːʃən / (AmE), / wəˈkɑːʃən / (BrE)
a member of any of the Indigenous peoples of British Columbia and Washington State who speak or historically spoke the Wakashan languages

Examples

Wakashan people
He is a proud Wakashan with deep roots in the region.
Several Wakashan tribes maintain their cultural traditions along the Pacific coast.
The museum exhibits artifacts from early Wakashan settlements.
