muggle

noun
unskilled outsider
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌɡəl / (AmE), / ˈmʌɡl̩ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who lacks knowledge or skill in a particular activity, subject, or community

Examples

A muggle at coding
As a complete muggle in coding, she relied on colleagues to fix every technical bug.
He felt like a muggle at the chess club, surrounded by experienced players.
Don't worry if you're a muggle when it comes to photography; everyone starts somewhere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expert - a person with a high level of knowledge or skill
  • synonym: novice - a person new to or inexperienced in a field
