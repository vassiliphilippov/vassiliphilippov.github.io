order of payment

noun
document directing payment of money
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər əv ˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə əv ˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
a formal document instructing a bank or person to pay a specified sum of money to a named party

Examples

An order of payment document
An order of payment was issued after the court ruled in favor of the plaintiff.

The company submitted an order of payment to the bank to settle its supplier's invoice.
