order of payment

noun
document directing payment of money
4.8 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…îrd…ôr …ôv Ààpe…™m…ônt / (AmE), / Àà…îÀêd…ô …ôv Ààpe…™m…ônt / (BrE)
a formal document instructing a bank or person to pay a specified sum of money to a named party

Examples

An order of payment document
An order of payment was issued after the court ruled in favor of the plaintiff.
She received an order of payment authorizing her to collect the funds from the local branch.
The company submitted an order of payment to the bank to settle its supplier's invoice.

Synonyms

  • bill of exchange
    7.5 0.03/m
    a bill of exchange instructs one person or bank to pay a third party at a set future time, while an order of payment names the payee more generally.
  • draft
    5.8 0.27/m
    a draft is an order drawn by one person or bank on another to pay money, while an order of payment is a general formal instruction.
  • check
    2.1 13/m
    a check is a written order to a bank to pay from the drawer's account on demand, while an order of payment is a general formal payment instruction.
