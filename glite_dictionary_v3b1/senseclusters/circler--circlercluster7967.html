circler

noun
person who circles
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrklər / (AmE), / ˈsɜːklə / (BrE)
a person who marks text or objects by drawing a circle around them to highlight or indicate something

Examples

Text circler
The teacher assigned a circler to highlight all the key points in the article.
As the official circler, she carefully marked every error in the document.
During the review, the circler drew attention to important details by circling them in blue ink.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annotator - a person who adds notes or comments to text
  • synonym: marker - a person who marks or highlights text
