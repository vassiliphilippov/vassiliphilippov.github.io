fiduciarily

adverb
in a fiduciary way
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɪˈduːʃiˌɛrɪli / (AmE), / fɪˈdjuːʃɪərɪli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to trust or fiduciary responsibility, especially in managing someone else's assets

Examples

Act fiduciarily for clients
The lawyer acted fiduciarily to protect her client's interests.
All assets must be handled fiduciarily according to the terms of the trust.
He was required to manage the estate fiduciarily for the beneficiaries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: negligently - in a careless or irresponsible manner
  • synonym: trustworthily - in a trustworthy or reliable manner
