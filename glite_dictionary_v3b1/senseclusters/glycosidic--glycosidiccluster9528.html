glycosidic

adjective
relating to glycosides
Pronunciation: / ˌɡlaɪkəˈsɪdɪk / (AmE), / ˌɡlaɪkəˈsɪdɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving a glycoside or the chemical bond formed by a glycoside

Examples

Glycosidic bond
The glycosidic bond connects the sugar to the base in DNA.
Enzymes can break the glycosidic linkages in complex carbohydrates during digestion.
A mutation may affect the stability of the glycosidic connection in certain molecules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glycoside - a compound formed from a simple sugar and another compound
