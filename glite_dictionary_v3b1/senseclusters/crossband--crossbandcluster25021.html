crossband

verb
to reinforce with veneer
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌbænd / (BrE)
to apply a crosswise layer of veneer to a wood panel to strengthen or stabilize it

Examples

To crossband wood panels
The carpenter decided to crossband the table top to prevent it from warping over time.

Many high-quality plywood sheets are crossbanded to improve their durability and stability.
