drop

verb
(of animal) give birth to young
Pronunciation: / drɑp / (AmE), / drɒp / (BrE)
(of an animal) to give birth to a young animal, especially to bring forth a calf or similar offspring.

Examples

The cow dropped a calf
Farmers watched as the mare dropped her foal in the pasture.
The cow dropped a healthy calf in the early hours of the morning.
The ewe is expected to drop her lambs sometime this week.
