first moment

noun
mean value in statistics or mathematics
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˌfɝst ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˌfɜːst ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
the mean of a set of values; the sum of the values divided by their number, especially in statistics and mathematics.

Examples

The first moment of data

When analyzing data, calculating the first moment is often the first step.
