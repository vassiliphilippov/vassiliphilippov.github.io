WIP

noun
unfinished inventory
5.4
Pronunciation: / wɪp / (AmE), / wɪp / (BrE)
goods that are partially completed and still in the production process, counted as inventory

Examples

WIP inventory
The company increased its WIP to meet the growing demand for its products.
Delays in receiving raw materials caused a buildup of WIP on the factory floor.
Managers regularly review the amount of WIP to improve production efficiency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: finished goods - products that have completed the production process and are ready for sale
  • synonym: inventory - all goods and materials held by a business for sale or production
