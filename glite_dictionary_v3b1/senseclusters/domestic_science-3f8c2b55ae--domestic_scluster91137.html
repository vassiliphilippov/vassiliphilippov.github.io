domestic science

noun
subject on homemaking skills
1.1 3.9
Pronunciation: / dəˈmɛstɪk ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
The study of the theory and practice of homemaking, including cooking, cleaning, sewing, and household management, taught as a subject in schools.
