slummer

noun
visitor to poor areas
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌmər / (AmE), / ˈslʌmə / (BrE)
a person who visits poor urban areas, often out of curiosity or for entertainment rather than necessity

Examples

Tourist slummer
The documentary followed a group of slummers exploring the city's poorest neighborhoods.
Some critics argue that slummers treat poverty as a spectacle rather than understanding its challenges.
He was accused of being a slummer when he visited the area just to take photographs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: resident - a person who lives in a particular place
  • synonym: tourist - a person who travels for pleasure
