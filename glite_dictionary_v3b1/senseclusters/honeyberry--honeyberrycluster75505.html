honeyberry

noun
edible blue berry
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌniˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈhʌniˌbɛri / (BrE)
an edible blue berry from certain honeysuckle plants, especially Lonicera caerulea, with a sweet-tart flavor

Examples

Fresh honeyberries
She sprinkled fresh honeyberries on her morning yogurt for a burst of flavor.
The farmer grows honeyberry bushes because the fruit ripens earlier than most berries.
You can make delicious jam from honeyberries harvested in late spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blueberry - a small, sweet blue fruit from the Vaccinium genus
  • synonym: honeysuckle - a climbing or shrubby plant with sweet-smelling flowers, some of which produce edible berries
