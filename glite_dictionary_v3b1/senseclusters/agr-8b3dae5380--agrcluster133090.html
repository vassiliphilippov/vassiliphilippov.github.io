US military program
Pronunciation: / eɪ dʒiː ɑːr / (AmE), / eɪ dʒiː ɑː / (BrE)
Active Guard/Reserve, a US military program where National Guard or Reserve members serve full time in support or administrative roles.

Examples

AGR program
After basic training, he applied for the AGR to work full time with the National Guard.
Many soldiers in the AGR program handle administrative duties and support missions year-round.
She transferred from the Reserves to the AGR for a more stable military career.

Related Terms

  • synonym: active duty - full-time service in the armed forces
  • antonym: reserve - military personnel not in full-time service but available if needed
