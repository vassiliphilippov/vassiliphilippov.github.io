patched

adjective
updated with fixes or security patches
4.3
Pronunciation: /pætʃt/ (AmE), /pætʃt/ (BrE)
(of software, firmware, or a system) having had one or more patches or updates applied to fix bugs, close security vulnerabilities, or improve behavior.

Examples

Patched software
The patched firmware now resists the recently disclosed exploit after the update.
After the security audit, the company shipped a patched version of the router to customers.
Keep your system patched to protect against ransomware and other common internet threats.
