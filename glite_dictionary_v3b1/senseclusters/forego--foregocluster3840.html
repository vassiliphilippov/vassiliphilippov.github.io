forego

verb
to occur or come before

to occur or exist before something in time; to precede

Examples

Forego earlier events
Ancient customs often forego written records in many cultures.
Debate on the new law must forego the vote in parliament.
The festival foregoes the harvest season, marking the start of celebrations.
