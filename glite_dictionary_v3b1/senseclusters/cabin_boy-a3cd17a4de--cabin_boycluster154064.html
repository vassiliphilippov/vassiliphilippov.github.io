cabin boy

noun
young servant on a ship
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkæb.ɪn bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈkæb.ɪn bɔɪ / (BrE)
A young male servant who works on a ship, performing domestic tasks and errands for officers or passengers.

Examples

The cabin boy serves
In many novels, the cabin boy dreams of adventure beyond his humble duties.
The cabin boy was always the first to rise and the last to sleep.
The captain called for the cabin boy to bring him some fresh water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deckhand - a person who assists with work on the deck of a ship
