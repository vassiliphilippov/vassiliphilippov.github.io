chargeback

noun
reversed payment
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɑːrdʒ.bæk/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɑːdʒ.bæk/ (BrE)
a reversal of a payment, returning money to the payer after a dispute or error

Examples

Bank chargeback
After the disputed transaction, the bank issued a chargeback to the customer’s account.
Merchants try to avoid chargebacks by providing clear records of every sale.
A chargeback can occur if a customer claims they never received the product.

Related Terms

  • antonym: payment - the act of giving money for goods or services
  • synonym: refund - money returned to a customer after a purchase
