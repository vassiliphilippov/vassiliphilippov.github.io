subarachnoid space

noun
space in the meninges containing cerebrospinal fluid
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbəˈræk.nɔɪd speɪs / (AmE), / ˌsʌbəˈræk.nɔɪd speɪs / (BrE)
the space in the meninges, beneath the arachnoid membrane and above the pia mater, that contains cerebrospinal fluid

Examples

The subarachnoid space fluid
A hemorrhage into the subarachnoid space can cause sudden, severe headaches and neurological symptoms.
Doctors examined the MRI for abnormalities in the subarachnoid space after the patient experienced trauma.
The cerebrospinal fluid circulates through the subarachnoid space to cushion the brain and spinal cord.
