guilty pleasure

noun
something enjoyable you feel guilty about
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪlti ˈplɛʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪlti ˈplɛʒə / (BrE)
something that someone enjoys, even though they feel it is not generally approved of or they feel a little ashamed or guilty about it

Examples

A guilty pleasure activity
Binge-watching romantic comedies is my guilty pleasure after a long week.
He admitted that eating ice cream at midnight was his guilty pleasure.
Listening to cheesy pop songs is definitely her guilty pleasure.
