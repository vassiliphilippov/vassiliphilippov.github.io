caffeine

noun
stimulant compound in coffee and tea
2.2
Pronunciation: / kæˈfiːn / (AmE), / ˈkæf.iːn / (BrE)
A bitter, stimulating compound found in coffee, tea, and some other beverages and foods.

Examples

Bitter caffeine in coffee
I try to avoid drinking caffeine late at night so I can sleep better.
Many people rely on caffeine for a morning energy boost.
Some soft drinks also contain caffeine, not just coffee and tea.

Related Terms
