cabler

noun
telegraph message sender
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪblər / (AmE), / ˈkeɪblə / (BrE)
a person who sends or transmits messages by cable or cablegram, especially in the context of telegraphy

Examples

Cabler sends messages
The cabler worked late into the night sending urgent news across the ocean.
In the early 1900s, a skilled cabler was essential for international communication.
The embassy relied on a trusted cabler to transmit confidential messages quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: messenger - a person who delivers messages
  • synonym: telegrapher - a person who operates a telegraph
