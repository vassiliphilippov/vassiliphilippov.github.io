simulate

verb
create an imitation or model
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪmjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to create a representation or imitation of something, especially in order to study or test it

Examples

To simulate a test
The experiment was designed to simulate conditions found on Mars.
The software can simulate complex weather patterns for research purposes.
Video games often simulate real-world physics to enhance realism.
