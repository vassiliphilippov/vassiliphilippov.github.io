humor

verb
to indulge wishes
4.9 1.08/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàhjuÀêm…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàhjuÀêm…ô / (BrE)
to go along with or indulge someone's wishes, often to please them or avoid conflict

Examples

To humor a child
She humored her little brother by playing his favorite game even though she was tired.
The teacher humored the students' request for an extra break during the hot afternoon.
He humored his friend's strange ideas just to keep the peace.
She humored her father by pretending to enjoy the old family stories during dinner.
He humored his little brother by playing the same game for the third time.
The teacher humored the students' request for an extra break, even though she was busy.
