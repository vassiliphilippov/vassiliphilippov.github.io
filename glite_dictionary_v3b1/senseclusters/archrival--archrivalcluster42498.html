archrival

noun
main competitor
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrtʃˌraɪvəl / (AmE), / ˈɑːtʃˌraɪvəl / (BrE)
a person's or group's main and most important rival, often in a long-standing competition

Examples

The archrival team
The football team finally defeated their longtime archrival in a dramatic final match.
For years, the two companies have been archrivals in the technology industry.
She trained hard all season to outperform her archrival at the championship.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ally - a person or group that cooperates with another
  • synonym: nemesis - a long-standing rival or arch-enemy
  • synonym: opponent - someone who competes against another
