tightness

noun
a feeling of constriction in the body
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtnəs / (BrE)
a sensation of being squeezed or constricted in a part of the body, often causing discomfort or difficulty

Examples

Tightness in the chest
After running up the stairs, he noticed a tightness in his chest and decided to rest.
The doctor asked if she had experienced any tightness in her throat during the allergy attack.
Yoga helps relieve the tightness in my shoulders after a long day at the computer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: constriction - a narrowing or tightening sensation, especially in the body
  • antonym: looseness - the state of being loose, the opposite of tightness
