seasonality

noun
regular seasonal change
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsiːzəˈnæləti / (AmE), / ˌsiːzəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
the quality of changing or varying in a regular pattern according to the season or time of year

Examples

Seasonality of prices
Seasonality affects the price of many fruits and vegetables throughout the year.
Tourism in the region is influenced by the seasonality of the climate.
Retailers must plan their inventory carefully to account for seasonality in demand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cyclicality - the quality of occurring in cycles or repeated patterns
  • antonym: irregularity - the state of not following a regular pattern
