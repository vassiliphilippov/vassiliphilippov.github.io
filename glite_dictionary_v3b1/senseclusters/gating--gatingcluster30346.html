gating

verb
to install a gate
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
actively providing with a gate or controlling access by installing a gate or barrier

Examples

To gate a road
The community is gating the main road to improve security for residents.
Workers are gating the playground to keep it closed after dark.
By gating the parking lot, the school can control who enters the premises.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barring - to block or prevent entry
  • antonym: opening - to make accessible or remove barriers
