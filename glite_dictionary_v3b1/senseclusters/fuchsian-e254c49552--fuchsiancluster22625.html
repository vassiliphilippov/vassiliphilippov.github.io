Fuchsian

adjective
mathematics of Fuchs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːksiən / (AmE), / ˈfʊksiən / (BrE)
relating to the mathematical work of Fuchs, especially in geometry or group theory, such as Fuchsian groups in hyperbolic geometry

Examples

Fuchsian group
A Fuchsian group is a type of discrete group acting on the hyperbolic plane.
The mathematician presented a new result about Fuchsian differential equations.
Many important concepts in geometry are named after Fuchsian work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hyperbolic - relating to hyperbolic geometry or space
