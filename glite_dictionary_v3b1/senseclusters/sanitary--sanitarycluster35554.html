sanitary

adjective
clean; free from disease-causing agents
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsænɪˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈsænɪtəri / (BrE)
clean and free from dirt, germs, or anything that could cause disease; promoting health and hygiene

Examples

A sanitary hospital
All hospital equipment must be kept sanitary to prevent infections.
Good handwashing habits are essential to maintain a sanitary environment.
The restaurant was praised for its sanitary food preparation area.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unsanitary - not clean or hygienic; likely to cause disease
