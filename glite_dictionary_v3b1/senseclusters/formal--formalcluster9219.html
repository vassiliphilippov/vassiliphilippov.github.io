formal

noun
lavish dance requiring formal attire
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔr.məl / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.məl / (BrE)
A large social dance or ball where formal attire is required, especially in schools or universities.

Examples

School formal dance
Attending the senior formal was one of the highlights of my high school experience.
Our school is hosting its annual formal next month and everyone's excited.
She bought a beautiful dress to wear to the spring formal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prom - a formal dance held for a high school class at the end of the academic year
