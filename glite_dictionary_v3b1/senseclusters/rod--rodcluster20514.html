rod

verb
to punish with a rod
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɑd / (AmE), / rɒd / (BrE)

Examples

To rod a child

The master rodded the servant as punishment, a practice now considered cruel.
He rodded the boy for disobeying, following the harsh customs of the time.
