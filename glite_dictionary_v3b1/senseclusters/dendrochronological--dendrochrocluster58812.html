dendrochronological

adjective
tree ring dating
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛndroʊˌkrɑnəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌdɛndrəkˌrɒnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl /; / ˌdɛndrəʊˌkrɒnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the scientific study of tree rings to date events, objects, or understand past environmental conditions

Examples
