dendrochronological

adjective
tree ring dating
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛndroʊˌkrɑnəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌdɛndrəkˌrɒnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl /; / ˌdɛndrəʊˌkrɒnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the scientific study of tree rings to date events, objects, or understand past environmental conditions

Examples

A dendrochronological study
The dendrochronological study provided precise dates for the ancient wooden structure.
Researchers used dendrochronological evidence to track climate changes over centuries.
A dendrochronological approach helped confirm the age of the archaeological site.

Researchers used dendrochronological methods to date the wooden beams in the ancient structure.
A dendrochronological approach can help scientists understand how forests responded to past climate changes.

  • synonym: paleoclimatic - relating to the climate of past geological ages
  • synonym: radiocarbon dating - a method of dating objects by measuring radioactive carbon
