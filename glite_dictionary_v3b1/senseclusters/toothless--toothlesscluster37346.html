toothless

adjective
having no teeth
Pronunciation: / ˈtuθ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈtuːθ.ləs / (BrE)
Having no teeth; lacking teeth.

Examples

A toothless smile
After having most of her teeth removed, the grandmother was almost completely toothless.
Conservationists discovered a toothless fish species in the remote lake.
The snake's toothless jaws make it harmless to humans.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dentate - having teeth or toothlike projections
