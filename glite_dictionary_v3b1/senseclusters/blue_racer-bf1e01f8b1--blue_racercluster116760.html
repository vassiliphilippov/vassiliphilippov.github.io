blue racer

noun
bluish-green snake found in US Midwest/South
5.4
Pronunciation: / bluː ˈreɪsər / (AmE), / bluː ˈreɪsə / (BrE)
A bluish-green, nonvenomous snake (Coluber constrictor foxii) native to regions from Ohio to Texas in the United States.

Examples

The blue racer snake
Farmers appreciate the presence of the blue racer because it helps control rodent populations.
The blue racer is known for its speed and agility in chasing prey.
While hiking, we spotted a blue racer slithering quickly across the path.
