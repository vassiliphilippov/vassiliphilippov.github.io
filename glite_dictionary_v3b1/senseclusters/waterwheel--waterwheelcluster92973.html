waterwheel

noun
wheel powered by water for driving machinery
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌwiːl /; / ˈwɔt̬ɚˌwiːl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌwiːl / (BrE)
a large wheel turned by flowing or falling water, used to provide power to machines or to lift water

Related Terms
