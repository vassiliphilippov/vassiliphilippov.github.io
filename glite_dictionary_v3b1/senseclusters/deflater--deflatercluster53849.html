deflater

noun
device to release air
4.8
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfleɪtər / (AmE), / dɪˈfleɪtə / (BrE)
a device or person that lets air out of something, making it less inflated

Examples

Tire deflater
The mechanic used a deflater to reduce the tire pressure.
After the game, the coach handed the deflater to a player to pack up the balls.
A simple deflater can help you quickly release air from inflatable mattresses.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inflater - a device or person that puts air into something
