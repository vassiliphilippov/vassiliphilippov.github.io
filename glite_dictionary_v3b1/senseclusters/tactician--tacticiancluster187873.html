tactician

noun
person skilled at planning tactics
4.5
Pronunciation: / tækˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / tækˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
A person who is skilled in planning and executing tactics, especially in military or strategic contexts.

Examples

A skilled tactician
A skilled tactician always considers several possible outcomes before making a move.
She gained a reputation as a political tactician who could outmaneuver her opponents.
The general was a brilliant tactician who won many battles through careful planning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strategist - a person skilled in planning long-term actions or policies
