hyperfocal

noun
lens focus distance
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈfoʊkəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈfəʊkəl / (BrE)
the nearest distance at which a camera lens can be focused so that objects at infinity are acceptably sharp
