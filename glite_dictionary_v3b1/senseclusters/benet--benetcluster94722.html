benet

noun
naive or foolish person
an old-fashioned term for a simple, naive, or foolish person

Examples

A foolish benet
In the old village, people would sometimes call a gullible child a benet.
The story described a benet who trusted everyone he met, no matter what they said.
She worried that acting like a benet would make others take advantage of her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • synonym: simpleton - a person lacking in common sense or intelligence
  • antonym: wise - having or showing experience, knowledge, and good judgment
