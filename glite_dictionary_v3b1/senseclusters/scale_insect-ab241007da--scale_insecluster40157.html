scale insect

noun
sap-sucking insect on plants
2.1 7.3
Pronunciation: / skeɪl ˈɪn.sɛkt / (AmE), / skeɪl ˈɪn.sɛkt / (BrE)
A small sap-sucking insect that lives on plants and secretes a protective waxy covering.

Examples

A scale insect pest
Certain species of scale insect can cause serious harm to fruit crops.
She used an insecticidal soap to control the scale insects on her houseplants.
The gardener discovered a cluster of scale insects damaging the rose bushes.
