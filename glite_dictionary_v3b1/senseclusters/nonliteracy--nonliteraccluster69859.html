nonliteracy

noun
lack of literary knowledge
8.4
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈlɪt̬ərəsi / (AmE), / nɒnˈlɪtərəsi / (BrE)
a lack of familiarity with, interest in, or knowledge of literary works and literary culture

Examples

Nonliteracy in literature
His nonliteracy in poetry made it difficult for him to appreciate the class discussions.
The museum aims to address nonliteracy in literature among young adults.
Her nonliteracy regarding famous authors was evident during the quiz.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illiteracy - the inability to read or write
  • antonym: literacy - the ability to read and write
