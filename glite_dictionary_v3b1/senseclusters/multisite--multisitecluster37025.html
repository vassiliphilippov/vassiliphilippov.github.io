multisite

noun
study at multiple locations
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a research study or clinical trial conducted at more than one location to increase sample size or diversity

Examples

Multisite trial
The multisite included hospitals from three different countries to ensure a diverse participant pool.
Researchers designed a multisite to compare results across various geographic regions.
Funding was secured for a large multisite to improve the reliability of the clinical trial findings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: multicenter - a study conducted at several centers or institutions
  • antonym: single-site - a study conducted at only one location
