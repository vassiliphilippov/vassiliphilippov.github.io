multisite

noun
study at multiple locations
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌl.tɪ.saɪt/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tə.saɪt/ (AmE), /ˈmʌl.tɪ.saɪt/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tə.saɪt/ (BrE)

Examples

Multisite trial

Researchers designed a multisite to compare results across various geographic regions.
