academical

adjective
relating to education
Pronunciation: / ˌækəˈdɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌækəˈdɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to education, scholarship, or academic institutions; archaic form of 'academic'

Examples

Academical robes
He wore his academical robes at the graduation ceremony.
The university's traditions include several academical customs no longer widely practiced.
In old texts, you may find references to academical pursuits instead of academic ones.
