cloudiness

noun
the state of being cloudy
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈklaʊdi.nɪs / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdɪnɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being cloudy, especially with reference to the sky or a substance's lack of clarity

Examples

Cloudiness in water
Doctors were concerned about the cloudiness observed in the patient's urine sample.
In spring, the lake's cloudiness increases as melting snow washes in sediment.
The cloudiness of the sky suggested that rain was likely later in the day.

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear
