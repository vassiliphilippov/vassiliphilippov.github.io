embattle

verb
fortify with battlements
5.7 7.3
Pronunciation: /em.ˈbæt.əl/ /; / /ɪm.ˈbæt.əl/ /; / /əm.ˈbæt.əl/ (AmE), /em.ˈbæt.əl/ /; / /ɪm.ˈbæt.əl/ /; / /əm.ˈbæt.əl/ (BrE)
to prepare or fortify (a place, structure, or group) for battle or defense

Examples

To embattle a place
Architects would sometimes embattle even churches, preparing them for possible attack.
