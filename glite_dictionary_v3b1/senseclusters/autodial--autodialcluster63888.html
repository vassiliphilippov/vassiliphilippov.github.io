autodial

noun
automatic dialing device
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːtoʊˌdaɪəl / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊˌdaɪəl / (BrE)
a device or computer system that automatically dials telephone numbers without manual input

Examples

An autodial device
The company uses an autodial to contact hundreds of customers every day.
After installing the autodial, the call center improved its efficiency significantly.
The technician programmed the autodial to call emergency numbers in case of fire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autodialer - a device or software that dials telephone numbers automatically
  • antonym: manual dial - the act of dialing a telephone number by hand
