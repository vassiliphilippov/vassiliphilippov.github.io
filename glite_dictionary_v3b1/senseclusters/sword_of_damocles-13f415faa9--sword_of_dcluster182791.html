sword of Damocles

noun
imminent or constant threat
Pronunciation: / sɔrd əv dəˈmoʊkliːz / (AmE), / sɔːd əv dəˈmæklɪz / (BrE)
a situation of constant and impending danger or threat, especially one that is looming over someone

Examples

The sword of Damocles threat
For many small business owners, economic uncertainty is a sword of Damocles hanging over their heads.
Living under the constant sword of Damocles of job cuts can be incredibly stressful for employees.
The threat of nuclear war remained a sword of Damocles over the world during the Cold War.

  • synonym: threat - a potential source of danger or harm
