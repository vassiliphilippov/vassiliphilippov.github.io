American smooth dogfish

noun
a small shark species of the Atlantic coast
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən smuːð ˈdɔːɡˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən smuːð ˈdɒɡˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a species of small shark found along the Atlantic coast of the Americas, known for its smooth skin and slender body
