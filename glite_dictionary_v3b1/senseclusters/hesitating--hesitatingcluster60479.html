Examples

Synonyms

  • hesitant
    3.2 5.60/m
    hesitant is a short adjective for someone slow to act from doubt, while hesitating describes the actual slow action.
  • irresolute
    8.2 0.18/m
    irresolute sounds formal and shows lack of firm purpose, while hesitating focuses on a moment of slow action.
  • indecisive
    4.3 0.79/m
    indecisive suggests a general inability to choose confidently, while hesitating describes a temporary pause before acting.
  • wavering
    5.3 0.00/m
    wavering shows moving between choices or feelings, while hesitating describes a single slow act caused by doubt.
