Galictis vittatus

noun
species of grison, a mustelid mammal
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlɪktɪs vɪˈteɪtəs / (AmE), / ɡəˈlɪktɪs vɪˈteɪtəs / (BrE)
A carnivorous mammal found in Central and South America, resembling a weasel, with a greyish-white back and dark underparts; also known as the greater grison.
