uhuru

proper noun
East African name
9.9
Pronunciation: / uˈhuːruː / (AmE), / uˈhuːruː / (BrE)
a personal name used in East Africa, derived from the Swahili word meaning freedom

Examples

Uhuru Kenyatta
The president's first name is Uhuru, which means freedom in Swahili.
Many parents in Kenya choose Uhuru as a name to celebrate independence.
At the ceremony, Uhuru was called to the stage to receive his award.
