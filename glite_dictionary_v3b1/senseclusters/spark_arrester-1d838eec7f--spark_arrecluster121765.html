spark arrester

noun
device reducing electrical sparking
Pronunciation: / spɑrk əˈrɛstər / (AmE), / spɑːk əˈrɛstə / (BrE)
a device used to reduce or prevent sparking when electrical contacts are opened or closed

Examples

Electrical spark arrester
After installing the spark arrester, the technician noticed a significant reduction in visible sparks.
Many electrical panels are equipped with a spark arrester to enhance operational safety.
The spark arrester is crucial for preventing dangerous electrical fires in industrial equipment.
