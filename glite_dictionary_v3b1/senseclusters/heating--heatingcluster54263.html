heating

noun
increase in temperature; becoming warm
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of becoming warm or causing something to become warm, especially the increase in temperature

Examples

Rapid heating of water
Rapid heating can sometimes damage delicate materials.
The heating of the room was slow after the windows had been opened.
The climate is changing, and global heating is a major concern.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cooling - the process of becoming cooler; a falling temperature
