man-to-man

adjective, adverb
in a direct and honest manner
Pronunciation: / ˌmæn tə ˈmæn / (AmE), / ˌmæn tə ˈmæn / (BrE)
in a direct, honest, or frank way, especially in private between two people

Examples

Man-to-man talk
He spoke man-to-man with his son about responsibility.
Let's discuss this man-to-man and sort out our differences.
They wanted a man-to-man conversation to clear the air.
After a man-to-man talk, they resolved their differences honestly and with respect.
He appreciated his boss’s man-to-man approach during their review meeting.
Sometimes a man-to-man conversation is the best way to address sensitive topics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candid - truthful and straightforward; frank
  • antonym: evasive - tending to avoid commitment or self-revelation, especially by responding only indirectly
  • synonym: forthright - direct and outspoken; straightforward and honest
