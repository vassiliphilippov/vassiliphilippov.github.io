refrigerating

adjective
causing cooling or freezing
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfrɪdʒəˌreɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɪdʒəreɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
causing cooling or freezing; having the effect of reducing temperature or making something cold.

Examples

A refrigerating system
A refrigerating system is essential for transporting frozen foods safely.
The refrigerating effect of the ice pack helped reduce swelling.
This warehouse is equipped with several refrigerating units to store perishable goods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cooling - reducing temperature; becoming less warm
  • antonym: heating - making or becoming hot or warm
