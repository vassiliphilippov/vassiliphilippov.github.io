hatchet job

noun
malicious attack on someone's reputation
4.1 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈhætʃ.ət dʒɑːb/ /; / /ˈhætʃ.ət dʒoʊb/ (AmE), /ˈhætʃ.ɪt dʒɒb/ /; / /ˈhætʃ.ɪt dʒəʊb/ (BrE)
a malicious and unfair criticism, report, or attack intended to damage someone's reputation or misrepresent their actions

Examples

The article was nothing more than a hatchet job on the senator's record.
