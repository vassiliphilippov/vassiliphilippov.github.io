preregister

verb
to enroll in advance
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈrɛdʒɪstər / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈrɛdʒɪstə / (BrE)
to sign up or enroll for something before it officially starts

Examples

To preregister for a class
You should preregister for the workshop to guarantee your spot.
Many students preregister for classes before the semester begins.
It is possible to preregister online for the upcoming marathon.
You should preregister online to reserve your spot at the conference.
Participants are encouraged to preregister to avoid long lines on the event day.
If you preregister, you will receive a confirmation email with further instructions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: register - to sign up or enroll officially
  • antonym: withdraw - to remove oneself from participation
