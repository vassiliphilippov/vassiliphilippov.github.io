Sechuana

noun
language of the Tswana people
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɛˈtʃwɑːnə / (AmE), / sɛˈtʃwɑːnə / (BrE)
the Bantu language of the Tswana people, spoken chiefly in Botswana and South Africa; also called Setswana.

Examples

Sechuana language
