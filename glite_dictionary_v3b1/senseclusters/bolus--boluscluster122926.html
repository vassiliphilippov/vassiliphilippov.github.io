bolus

noun
small rounded mass, esp. chewed food
5.5 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈboʊ.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈbəʊ.ləs/ (BrE)
a small rounded mass of something (such as chewed food or medicine) that is ready to be swallowed or given as a dose

Examples

A bolus of food
After chewing thoroughly, the food formed a bolus ready to be swallowed.
Each bolus passes from the mouth to the esophagus during eating.
The technician analyzed a bolus of chewed bread under the microscope.
The doctor gave the patient a bolus of insulin to quickly lower his blood sugar.
After chewing, the food forms a bolus that is easy to swallow.
A bolus of contrast dye was injected before the scan began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dose - a measured amount of medicine taken at one time
  • synonym: tablet - a small, solid, round form of medicine
