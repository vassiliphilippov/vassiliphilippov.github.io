kudo

noun
Japanese martial art
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkuːdəʊ / (BrE)
a Japanese hybrid martial art that combines striking and grappling techniques, emphasizing both physical and mental discipline

Examples

Kudo martial art
After years of training, he earned his black belt in kudo.
The kudo tournament attracted martial artists from around the world.
She practiced kudo twice a week to improve her striking and grappling skills.
