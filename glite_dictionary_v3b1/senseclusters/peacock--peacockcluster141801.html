peacock

noun
male peafowl with striking tail
3.1 1.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpiː.kɑːk/ (AmE), /ˈpiː.kɒk/ (BrE)
The male peafowl, notable for its crested head and very large, fan-shaped tail with iridescent eye-like spots.

Examples

A peacock displays feathers
A peacock strutted proudly across the garden, attracting everyone's attention.
The peacock displayed its magnificent tail feathers during the mating ritual.
The vibrant colors of the peacock make it a symbol of beauty in many cultures.
