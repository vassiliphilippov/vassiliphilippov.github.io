bubbliness

noun
quality of giving off bubbles
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌb.li.nəs / (AmE), / ˈbʌb.li.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being full of bubbles or giving off bubbles

Examples

The bubbliness of champagne
She commented on the bubbliness of the champagne at the celebration.
The bubbliness of the sparkling water made it very refreshing.
Too much bubbliness in the soda can make it difficult to pour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: effervescence - the rapid escape of gas from a liquid, producing bubbles
