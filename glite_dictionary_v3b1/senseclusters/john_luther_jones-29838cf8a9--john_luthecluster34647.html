John Luther Jones

proper noun
famous U.S. railroad engineer, subject of a ballad
7.5
Pronunciation: / dʒɑn ˈluːðɚ dʒoʊnz / (AmE), / dʒɒn ˈluːθə ˈdʒəʊnz / (BrE)
(1864–1900) United States railroad engineer, famously known as "Casey Jones," who died trying to stop his train from crashing into another, inspiring a well-known ballad.
