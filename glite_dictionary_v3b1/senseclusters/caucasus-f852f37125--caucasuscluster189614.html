Caucasus

proper noun
region between Black and Caspian Seas
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːkəsəs / (AmE), / ˈkɔːkəsəs / (BrE)
a large region situated between the Black Sea and the Caspian Sea, known for the Caucasus Mountains and rich oil resources

Examples

Caucasus region
Geologists are interested in the Caucasus for both its mountains and its significant oil reserves.
Many ancient cultures developed in the Caucasus due to its strategic location and diverse landscape.
The Caucasus is home to a wide variety of ethnic groups and languages.
