mill

noun
machine for grinding or crushing materials
4.4 1.9
Pronunciation: /mɪl/ (AmE), /mɪl/ (BrE)
a machine or device for grinding, crushing, or pulverizing materials such as grain, coffee, or ores

Examples

A coffee mill
The coffee beans are placed in a mill before brewing to enhance their flavor.
The old flour mill still operates using water power from the river.
Workers fed grain into the mill to produce fresh flour every morning.
