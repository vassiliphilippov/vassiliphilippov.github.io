unnie

noun
older sister or female friend (Korean use)
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈuːni / (AmE), / ˈuːni / (BrE)
(in Korean culture) a term used by a female to address or refer to an older sister or an older female friend.

Examples

Unnie in Korea
If you visit Korea, you might hear younger girls referring to their elder sisters as unnie.
In the K-pop group, she always calls her bandmate unnie out of respect.
My Korean friend told me to use unnie when talking to older girls.

Related Terms

  • antonym: noona - a term used by males to address an older female in Korean culture
  • antonym: oppa - a term used by females to address an older male in Korean culture
