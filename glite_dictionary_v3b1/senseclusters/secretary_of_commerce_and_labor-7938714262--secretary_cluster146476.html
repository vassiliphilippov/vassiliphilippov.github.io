Secretary of Commerce and Labor

noun
head of former U.S. Commerce and Labor department
8.4
Pronunciation: / səˈkrɛtɛri əv ˈkɑːmɚs ənd ˈleɪbɚ / (AmE), / sɪˈkrɛtəri əv ˈkɒmɜːs ənd ˈleɪbə / (BrE)
the head of a former U.S. executive department, established in 1903 and divided into the Departments of Commerce and Labor in 1913
