stromatolite

noun
layered microbial rock
9.9
Pronunciation: / stroʊˈmætəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / strəˈmatəˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a layered rock structure formed by the activity and growth of microorganisms, especially cyanobacteria

Examples

Ancient stromatolite fossils
Scientists discovered ancient stromatolite fossils in the desert.
A stromatolite can reveal information about early life on Earth.
The museum displayed a large stromatolite collected from a remote lake.

  • synonym: limestone - a sedimentary rock mainly composed of calcium carbonate
