structure

noun
something built from parts; complex entity
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstrʌk.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈstrʌk.tʃə / (BrE)
a thing that is built or assembled from parts, especially a complex entity composed of many parts

Examples

A tall structure
Coral reefs are an important marine structure providing shelter for many ocean species.
Engineers designed the bridge's structure to withstand strong winds and earthquakes.
The museum is housed in a structure dating back to the 18th century.

  • antonym: ruin - the remains of a destroyed structure
