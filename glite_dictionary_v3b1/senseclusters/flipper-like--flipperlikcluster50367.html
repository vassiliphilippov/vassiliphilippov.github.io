flipper-like

adjective
having limbs resembling flippers
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪpɚ laɪk / (AmE), / ˈflɪpə laɪk / (BrE)
having limbs or parts that resemble or function like flippers, especially in animals adapted for swimming.

Examples

Flipper-like limbs
Her costume included flipper-like appendages to mimic a sea creature.
Some dinosaurs from the Cretaceous period developed flipper-like forelimbs for swimming.
The seal's flipper-like limbs help it move gracefully through the water.

  • synonym: flippered - having or provided with flippers
