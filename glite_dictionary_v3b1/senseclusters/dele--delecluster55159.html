dele

verb
to mark for deletion
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàdiÀêli / (AmE), / ÀàdiÀêle…™ / (BrE)
to mark text for deletion or to remove text, especially during proofreading or editing

Examples

To dele a word
