dele

verb
to mark for deletion
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːli / (AmE), / ˈdiːleɪ / (BrE)
to mark text for deletion or to remove text, especially during proofreading or editing

Examples

To dele a word
The editor asked me to dele the extra sentence from the manuscript.
When proofreading, use the correct symbol to dele any unnecessary words.
Please dele all repeated information before submitting your final draft.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delete - to remove or erase text or data
  • antonym: insert - to add text or material
