filterable

adjective
able to be filtered
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪltərəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪltərəbl / (BrE)
able to be filtered or capable of passing through a filter

Examples

Filterable particles
Only filterable particles will be removed by this device.
The laboratory tested whether the bacteria were filterable through the membrane.
This process separates filterable solids from the liquid in the solution.

Related Terms

  • antonym: impermeable - not allowing substances to pass through
  • synonym: permeable - allowing liquids or gases to pass through
