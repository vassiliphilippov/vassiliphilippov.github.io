traumatically

adverb
in a harmful way
7.5
Pronunciation: / trɔːˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / trɔːˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that causes or relates to severe emotional or psychological harm or trauma

Examples

Traumatically affected emotionally
The child was traumatically affected by the sudden loss of her pet.
He remembers the event traumatically, even years after it happened.
Many people were traumatically impacted by the natural disaster in the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gently - in a kind or mild way
  • synonym: painfully - in a way that causes pain or distress
