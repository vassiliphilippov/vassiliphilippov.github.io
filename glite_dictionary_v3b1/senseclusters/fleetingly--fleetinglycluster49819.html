fleetingly

adverb
briefly
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfliːtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈfliːtɪŋli / (BrE)
for a very short time; briefly

Examples

To fleetingly think
She fleetingly considered moving abroad before deciding to stay close to her family.
His eyes met hers fleetingly across the crowded room before he looked away.
The sun fleetingly appeared from behind the clouds before disappearing again.
He looked fleetingly at the painting before moving on to the next exhibit.
The sun appeared fleetingly from behind the clouds before it started to rain again.
She considered the idea fleetingly but quickly dismissed it as impossible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: briefly - for a short time
  • synonym: momentarily - for a moment; very briefly
  • antonym: permanently - in a way that lasts forever
