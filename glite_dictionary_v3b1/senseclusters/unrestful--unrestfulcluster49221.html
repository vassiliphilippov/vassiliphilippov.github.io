unrestful

adjective
not calm or peaceful
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɛstfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛstfəl / (BrE)
not peaceful or calm; causing or marked by restlessness or disturbance

Examples

An unrestful night
After an unrestful night, she felt tired and irritable in the morning.
The unrestful atmosphere in the office made it hard to concentrate on work.
He found the hotel bed unrestful and struggled to fall asleep.

Related Terms

  • antonym: restful - peaceful and calm; promoting rest
  • synonym: restless - unable to relax or be still
