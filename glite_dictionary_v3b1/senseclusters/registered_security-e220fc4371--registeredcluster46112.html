registered security

noun
security recorded in owner's name
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒɪstɚd sɪˈkjʊrəti / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstəd sɪˈkjʊərəti / (BrE)
A security, such as a stock or bond, for which the name of the owner is recorded by the issuing company or its agent.

Examples

A registered security certificate
The certificate for the registered security was mailed to the new owner after the transaction was completed.
To receive dividend payments, the shareholder must be listed as the owner of the registered security.
Unlike a bearer bond, a registered security provides a record of ownership maintained by the issuer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bearer security - a security not registered in the owner's name, ownership passes by delivery
