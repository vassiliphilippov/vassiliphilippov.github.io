underutilization

noun
insufficient use
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˌjuːtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˌjuːtɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or state of not using something as much as it could or should be used

Examples

The underutilization of resources
The underutilization of public transportation leads to more traffic and pollution in the city.
Many employees felt frustrated by the underutilization of their skills in the new project.
The report highlighted the underutilization of available resources in the healthcare system.
The underutilization of public transport leads to increased traffic congestion in the city.
Many employees are frustrated by the underutilization of their skills at work.
Efforts are being made to address the underutilization of renewable energy sources.

Related Terms

  • antonym: overuse - the act of using something too much
  • antonym: overutilization - the state of being used too much or excessively
  • synonym: underuse - the state of not being used enough
