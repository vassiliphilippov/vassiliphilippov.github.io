cannibalistically

adverb
in a self-destructive way
9.9
Pronunciation: /kæn.ɪ.bə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /kæn.ɪ.bə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a way that attacks, harms, or consumes members of one's own group, often causing self-destruction.

Examples

Compete cannibalistically with teammates
The two companies competed cannibalistically, weakening their own market share instead of growing it.
During the heated debate, team members argued cannibalistically rather than presenting a united front.
If a group acts cannibalistically, it may end up destroying itself from within.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cooperatively - in a way that works together for mutual benefit
  • synonym: self-destructively - in a way that causes harm to oneself or one's own group
