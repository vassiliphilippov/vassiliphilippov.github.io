Yorkshire caviar

noun
gourmet fish roe delicacy from Yorkshire
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈjɔrkʃər ˈkæviɑr / (AmE), / ˈjɔːkʃə ˈkæviɑː / (BrE)
A local delicacy from Yorkshire made from fish roe, typically regarded as a gourmet dish.

Examples
