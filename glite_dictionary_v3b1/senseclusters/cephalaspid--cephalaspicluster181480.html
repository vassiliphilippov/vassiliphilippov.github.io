cephalaspid

noun
extinct armored Devonian jawless fish
Pronunciation: / sɛfəˈlæspɪd / (AmE), / sɛfəˈlaspɪd / (BrE)
an extinct genus of jawless fish from the Devonian period, characterized by a heavily armored head

Examples

Fossil cephalaspid species
Fossils of cephalaspid species provide valuable information about early vertebrate evolution.
Scientists discovered a new cephalaspid fossil in the Devonian strata of eastern Europe.
The cephalaspid had a flattened body and a shield-like head for protection.
