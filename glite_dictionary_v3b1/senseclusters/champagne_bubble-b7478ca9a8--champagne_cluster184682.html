champagne bubble

noun
bubble in carbonated champagne
5.3 3.2
Pronunciation: /ʃæm.ˈpeɪn ˈbʌb.ər/ (AmE), /ʃæm.ˈpeɪn ˈbʌb.əl/ (BrE)
