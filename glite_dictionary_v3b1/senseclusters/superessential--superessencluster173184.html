superessential

adjective
beyond essential nature
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərɪˈsɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌsuːpərɪˈsɛnʃəl / (BrE)
transcending or existing beyond the essence or essential nature of something, especially in philosophy or theology

Examples

Superessential being
In some philosophical texts, God's nature is described as superessential and beyond human understanding.
The concept of a superessential reality challenges our usual definitions of existence and essence.
Some theologians argue that divine attributes are superessential, not limited by any created nature.

Related Terms

  • antonym: essential - absolutely necessary or fundamental
  • synonym: transcendent - going beyond ordinary limits or experience
