Examples

To suckle a baby
She sat quietly as her baby suckled at her breast.
The kittens continued to suckle until they were several weeks old.
The newborn calf began to suckle minutes after birth.
In some traditions, wet nurses would suckle the babies of noble families.

Synonyms

  • suck
    2.5 3.57/m
    suck means giving milk to a baby or young animal from the breast or udder, while suckle covers feeding or being fed with milk.
  • give suck
    9.8 0.02/m
    give suck is an older phrase for a woman or female animal nursing young, while suckle is the general verb for feeding with milk.
  • nurse
    2.3 4.59/m
    nurse means feeding a baby at the breast and caring for it, while suckle focuses on feeding or being fed with milk.
  • breastfeed
    2.6 2.14/m
    breastfeed means feeding a baby with milk directly from the mother's breast, while suckle applies to feeding or being fed by any female.
