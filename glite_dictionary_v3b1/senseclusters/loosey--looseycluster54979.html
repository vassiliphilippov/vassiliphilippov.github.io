loosey

adjective
not strict
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈluːsi / (AmE), / ˈluːsi / (BrE)
informal; not strict, precise, or tightly controlled; lax in discipline or enforcement.

Examples

Loosey rules
The manager's loosey approach to deadlines made the team less productive.
Parents worried that the school's loosey rules would lead to chaos.
His loosey attitude toward safety regulations concerned his coworkers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lax - not strict or severe; careless
  • antonym: strict - demanding that rules are closely followed
