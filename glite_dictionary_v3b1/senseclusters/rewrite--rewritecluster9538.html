rewrite

write again for improvement or adaptation

to write something again, often to improve it or change its form or content

Examples

To rewrite an essay
She decided to rewrite her essay to make it clearer and more concise.
The committee had to rewrite the policy to address recent changes.
They will rewrite the script so it appeals to a younger audience.
After receiving feedback, she decided to rewrite her essay from the beginning.
He rewrote the instructions so that they would be easier to follow.

Synonyms

  • redraft
    3.5 0.17/m
    redraft is writing a new draft to improve content or structure, while rewrite is the general act of writing something again.
  • revise
    3.4 20/m
    revise means making changes to correct or improve writing, while rewrite focuses on writing the text again, often more fully.
  • recast
    5.6 1.43/m
    recast means presenting text in a different form or style, while rewrite usually means writing the same content again to change it.
