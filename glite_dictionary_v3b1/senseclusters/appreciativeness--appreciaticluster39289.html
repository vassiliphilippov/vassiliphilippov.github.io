appreciativeness

noun
warm feeling of gratitude
Pronunciation: / əˈpriːʃieɪtɪvnəs / (AmE), / əˈpriːʃɪətɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of showing warm, friendly feelings of gratitude or thankfulness

Examples

Great appreciativeness shown
He showed great appreciativeness when his friends helped him move to a new house.
Her appreciativeness for their support was clear in her heartfelt speech.
The teacher's appreciativeness inspired the students to put forth their best effort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gratitude - the quality of being thankful; readiness to show appreciation
  • antonym: ingratitude - lack of gratitude
