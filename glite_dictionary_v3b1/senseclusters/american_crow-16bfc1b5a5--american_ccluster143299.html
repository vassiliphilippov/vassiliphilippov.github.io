American crow

noun
common black crow of North America
6.1
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən kroʊ / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən krəʊ / (BrE)
a large, glossy black bird of North America (Corvus brachyrhynchos) known for its intelligence and loud cawing call

Examples
