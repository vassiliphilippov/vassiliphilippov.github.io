area bombing

noun
large-scale indiscriminate bombing
Pronunciation: / ˈɛriə ˈbɑmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈeəriə ˈbɒmɪŋ / (BrE)
an extensive and systematic bombing campaign designed to destroy a large target area, rather than aiming at specific locations or facilities

Examples

Area bombing campaign
Historians debated the effectiveness and morality of area bombing in World War II.
The city suffered extensive damage due to area bombing during the war.
The military shifted its strategy from area bombing to more targeted airstrikes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpet bombing - indiscriminate bombing covering a wide area
  • antonym: precision bombing - bombing intended to destroy a specific target with minimal collateral damage
