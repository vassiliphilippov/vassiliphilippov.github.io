rolandic

adjective
of the central sulcus
9.9
Pronunciation: / roʊˈlændɪk / (AmE), / rəʊˈlændɪk / (BrE)
relating to the Rolandic area of the brain (the central sulcus and its surrounding regions)

Examples

Doctors often reassure parents that rolandic seizures usually disappear by adolescence.
