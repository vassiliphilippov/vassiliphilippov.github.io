Strauss the Younger

proper noun
Austrian composer known as the 'waltz king'
8.4
Pronunciation: /straʊs ðə ˈjʌŋ.ər/ /; / /straʊs ðiː ˈjʌŋ.ər/ (AmE), /straʊs ðə ˈjʌŋ.ə/ /; / /straʊs ðiː ˈjʌŋ.ə/ (BrE)
(1825–1899) Austrian composer, the son of Strauss the Elder, celebrated for his many famous waltzes and known as the 'waltz king'.

Examples

Strauss the Younger composer
Many people consider Strauss the Younger to be the greatest composer of Viennese waltzes.
The music of Strauss the Younger is still played at Vienna’s New Year’s Concert each year.
Unlike his father, Strauss the Younger achieved international fame during his lifetime.
