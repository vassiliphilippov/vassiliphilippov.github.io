asunder

adverb
into pieces; apart
7.5
Pronunciation: / əˈsʌndɚ / (AmE), / əˈsʌndə / (BrE)
Into pieces; apart or divided.

Examples

Torn asunder
After years of conflict, their partnership was torn asunder.
The ancient statue was smashed asunder by vandals.
Thunder split the silence asunder in the dead of night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apart - separated by distance or into pieces
