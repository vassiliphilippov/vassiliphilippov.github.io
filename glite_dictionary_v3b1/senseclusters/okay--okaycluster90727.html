okay

adverb
in a satisfactory or adequate way
Pronunciation: / oʊˈkeɪ / (AmE), / əʊˈkeɪ / (BrE)
in a way that is satisfactory or adequate; well enough but not exceptionally

Examples

Did okay in school
Even though he was nervous, he performed okay in his first interview.
I didn't study much for the exam, but I think I did okay.
She handled the difficult situation okay, considering the pressure she was under.
