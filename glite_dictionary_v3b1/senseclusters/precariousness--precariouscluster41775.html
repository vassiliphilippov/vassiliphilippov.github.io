precariousness

noun
state of being unstable or insecure
5.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /prɪ.ˈkeə.rɪ.əs.nəs/ (AmE), /prɪ.ˈkeə.rɪ.əs.nəs/ (BrE)
the state of being unstable, insecure, or uncertain, often involving risk or dependence on chance
