seven-spot

noun
playing card with seven pips
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛv.ən.spɑt / (AmE), / ˈsɛv.ən.spɒt / (BrE)
One of the four playing cards in a standard deck that has seven pips on its face.

Examples

The seven-spot card
He drew the seven-spot of diamonds and smiled at his luck.
The winning card was the seven-spot of clubs.
There are four seven-spots in a standard deck of cards.
