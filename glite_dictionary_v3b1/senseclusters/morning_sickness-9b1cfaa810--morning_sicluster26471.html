morning sickness

noun
nausea in early pregnancy
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˌsɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˌsɪknəs / (BrE)
Nausea and vomiting that typically occurs in the morning, especially during the early months of pregnancy.

Examples

Morning sickness during pregnancy
Her morning sickness was so severe that she had to miss work for several days.
Many women experience morning sickness during the first trimester of pregnancy.
Some find that eating small snacks helps ease morning sickness symptoms.
