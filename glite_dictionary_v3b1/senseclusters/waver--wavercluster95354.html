waver

noun
a pause or hesitation
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪvər / (AmE), / ˈweɪvə / (BrE)
a pause or uncertain hesitation, especially when making a decision or speaking

Examples

A moment of waver
He made his choice without a single waver or doubt.
The pitch of his speech caught a waver when he mentioned her name.
There was a noticeable waver in her voice as she spoke.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hesitation - a pause before saying or doing something, usually because of uncertainty
