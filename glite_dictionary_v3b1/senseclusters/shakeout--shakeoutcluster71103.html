shakeout

noun
short easy pre-race run
Pronunciation: / c883e6aakcca8at / (AmE), / c883e6aka8at / (BrE)
a short, easy run (usually before a race or intense workout) done to loosen muscles and prepare the body.

Examples

Pre-race shakeout
Runners completed a light shakeout before the marathon to ease tired legs.
Before the race, he did a five-minute shakeout to loosen his calves and calm his nerves.
After a long travel day, the team went for a short shakeout to wake up their muscles.
