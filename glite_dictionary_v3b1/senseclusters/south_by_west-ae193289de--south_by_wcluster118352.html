south by west

noun
compass point slightly west of south
Pronunciation: / saʊθ baɪ wɛst / (AmE), / saʊθ baɪ west / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point west of due south, or at an angle of 191°15′ from due north.

Examples

South by west direction
According to the map, our next bearing should be south by west for five miles.
The ship was sailing south by west to avoid the rocky coastline.
The wind shifted to south by west during the afternoon, bringing warmer air.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: south-southwest - the compass point between south and southwest
