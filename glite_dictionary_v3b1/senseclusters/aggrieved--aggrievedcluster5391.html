aggrieved

adjective
unfairly treated
7.2
Pronunciation: / əˈɡrivd / (AmE), / əˈɡriːvd / (BrE)
feeling upset, angry, or hurt because of unfair treatment or a wrong suffered

Examples

After the misunderstanding, she felt aggrieved and avoided talking to her friends for a while.

He felt aggrieved when he was passed over for the promotion.
