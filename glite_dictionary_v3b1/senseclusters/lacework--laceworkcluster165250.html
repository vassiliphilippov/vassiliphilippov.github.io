lacework

noun
fabric or decoration made like lace
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪswɜrk / (AmE), / ˈleɪswɜːk / (BrE)
A delicate fabric or decorative pattern consisting of or resembling lace, typically made by weaving threads in intricate open designs.
