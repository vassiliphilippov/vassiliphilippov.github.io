pickler

noun
person who makes pickles
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪklər / (AmE), / ˈpɪklə / (BrE)
a person who makes pickles from vegetables or fruits, often by preserving them in vinegar or brine

Examples

Expert pickler
The local pickler sold jars of spicy cucumbers at the farmers market.
As a skilled pickler, she experimented with new flavors every season.
The pickler taught a workshop on how to preserve vegetables at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preserver - a person who preserves food by canning or pickling
