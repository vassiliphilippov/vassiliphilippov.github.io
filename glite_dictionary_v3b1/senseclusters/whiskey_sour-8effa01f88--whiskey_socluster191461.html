whiskey sour

noun
cocktail with whiskey, citrus, and sugar
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪski ˌsaʊɚ /; / ˈwɪski saʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɪski ˌsaʊə /; / ˈwɪski ˈsaʊə / (BrE)
a cocktail made with whiskey, lemon or lime juice, sugar, and sometimes egg white, typically served over ice and often garnished with a slice of fruit or a cherry.
