microtonal

adjective
smaller than semitone
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈtoʊnəl / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈtəʊnəl / (BrE)
relating to musical intervals that are smaller than a semitone

Examples

Microtonal music
The composer is known for his use of microtonal harmonies in modern classical music.
Some traditional instruments are capable of producing microtonal notes not found on the piano.
She attended a workshop on microtonal music to learn about new tuning systems.

Related Terms

  • antonym: atonal - not based on any key or tonal center
  • synonym: chromatic - using notes outside the standard scale, often by semitones
