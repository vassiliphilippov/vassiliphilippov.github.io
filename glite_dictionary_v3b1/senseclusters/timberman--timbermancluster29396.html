timberman

noun
male owner or manager in lumber industry
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪmbərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈtɪmbəmæn / (BrE)
a person who works in the timber industry, such as cutting, processing, or selling timber

Examples

A timberman at work
As a successful timberman, he expanded his business throughout the state.
The timberman oversaw the operations at several logging camps in the region.
The meeting was attended by every major timberman interested in sustainable forestry practices.
The experienced timberman taught the new workers how to safely use the chainsaw.
After years as a timberman, he knew every tree in the forest by sight.
The timbermen gathered at dawn to begin their work deep in the woods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forester - a person in charge of managing forests
  • synonym: logger - a person who cuts down trees for timber
  • synonym: lumberjack - a person whose job is cutting down trees for timber
