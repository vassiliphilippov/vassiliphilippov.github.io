electroluminescent

adjective
emitting light electrically
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊluːməˈnɛsənt / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊluːmɪˈnɛsənt / (BrE)
emitting light as a result of an electric current or electric field

Examples

Electroluminescent display
The dashboard was illuminated by an electroluminescent display that was easy to read at night.
Engineers chose electroluminescent materials for the new safety signs because they are visible in the dark.
Her watch face uses an electroluminescent panel to provide a soft blue glow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fluorescent - emitting light while exposed to radiation, especially ultraviolet light
  • synonym: phosphorescent - emitting light without heat, especially after exposure to radiation
