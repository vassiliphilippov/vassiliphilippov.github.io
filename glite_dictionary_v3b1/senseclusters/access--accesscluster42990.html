access

verb
obtain or retrieve data, esp. on computer
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈækˌsɛs /; / ˈæksɛs / (AmE), / ˈæksɛs /; / ˈækses / (BrE)
to obtain, reach, or use something (such as a place, resource, or information), especially when permission is needed
