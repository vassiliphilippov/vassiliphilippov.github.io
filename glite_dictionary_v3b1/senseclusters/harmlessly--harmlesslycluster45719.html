harmlessly

adverb
without causing harm
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrmləsli / (AmE), / ˈhɑːmləsli / (BrE)
In a way that does not cause harm or injury.

Examples

Laugh harmlessly
He laughed harmlessly at the joke, meaning no offense.
The chemicals dissipated harmlessly into the atmosphere.
The snake passed by harmlessly, ignoring the hikers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harmfully - in a way that causes harm
