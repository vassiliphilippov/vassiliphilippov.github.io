Amazonian

adjective
tall and strong (woman)
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌæməˈzoʊniən / (AmE), / ˌæməˈzəʊniən / (BrE)
physically powerful, tall, or imposing, especially in reference to a woman

Examples

Amazonian woman
Her Amazonian presence commanded attention wherever she went.
The athlete's Amazonian build gave her an advantage in the competition.
People admired her Amazonian strength and confidence on the stage.
