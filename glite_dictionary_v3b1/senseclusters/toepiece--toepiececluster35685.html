toepiece

noun
protective toe plate
7.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààto äÀåpis / (AmE), / Ààt…ô äÀåpiÀês / (BrE)
a metal or structural part that covers or protects the front part of a boot, armor, or artificial foot

Examples

Metal toepiece on armor
The knight's toepiece was engraved with his family's crest for decoration and identification.
Modern work boots often include a steel toepiece to protect against heavy falling objects.
The restoration team carefully polished the ancient toepiece found at the archaeological site.
The technician adjusted the toepiece of the artificial foot to improve balance during walking.
A damaged toepiece can affect the comfort and function of a prosthetic foot.
Modern prosthetic designs often include a flexible toepiece for a more natural walking motion.

Synonyms

  • toecap
    5.7 0.02/m
    toecap is a protective cover on the toe of a shoe used for safety or decoration, while toepiece is a stronger metal part covering a boot's front.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heelpiece - a part that covers or reinforces the heel of a shoe or boot
  • synonym: prosthesis - an artificial device that replaces a missing body part
  • synonym: toe cap - a protective covering for the toe of a shoe or boot
