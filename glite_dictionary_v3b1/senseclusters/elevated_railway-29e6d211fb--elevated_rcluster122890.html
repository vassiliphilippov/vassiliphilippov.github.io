elevated railway

noun
railway system raised above the street
1.9 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈel.ə.veɪ.tɪd ˈreɪl.weɪ/ /; / /ˈel.ə.veɪ.təd ˈreɪl.weɪ/ (AmE), /ˈel.ə.veɪ.tɪd ˈreɪl.weɪ/ /; / /ˈel.ə.veɪ.təd ˈreɪl.weɪ/ (BrE)
