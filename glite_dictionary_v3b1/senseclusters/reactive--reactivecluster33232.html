reactive

adjective
responding to a stimulus or influence
Pronunciation: / riˈæktɪv / (AmE), / riˈæktɪv / (BrE)
responding readily to a stimulus or influence; showing a response or reaction when stimulated or affected by something.

Examples

Reactive behavior
Chemically reactive substances must be handled with great caution in the laboratory.
Her skin became more reactive to sunlight after the treatment.
Some people have highly reactive immune systems that trigger allergies easily.

Related Terms

  • antonym: passive - not reacting visibly to something that might be expected to produce manifestations of an emotion or feeling; antonym for reactive
  • synonym: responsive - reacting quickly and positively; synonym for responsive to stimuli
