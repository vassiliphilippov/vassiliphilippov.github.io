connection

noun
device or component that joins parts
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / kəˈnɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
a device or component that joins or links parts together so that electricity, signals, or substances can pass between them

Examples

An electrical connection
Make sure each connection is tight before operating the device.
The plumber checked the pipe connection for any signs of leaks.
The technician replaced the faulty connection to restore power to the machine.
