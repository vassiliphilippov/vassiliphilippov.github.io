boom

noun
deep, loud, prolonged noise
4.4
Pronunciation: / buːm / (AmE), / buːm / (BrE)
A deep, prolonged, and loud noise.

Examples

The boom of thunder
The boom of the cannon shook the entire harbor.
The distant boom of thunder echoed across the valley.
We heard a sudden boom as the fireworks exploded overhead.
