unlinked

adjective
not genetically connected
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɪŋkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪŋkt / (BrE)
not genetically connected; located on different chromosomes and inherited independently.

Examples

Unlinked genes
The two traits are unlinked, so they are inherited independently in offspring.
Scientists discovered that the mutations were unlinked and appeared on separate chromosomes.
Because the genes are unlinked, their inheritance does not affect each other.

Related Terms

  • antonym: linked - genetically connected; inherited together
