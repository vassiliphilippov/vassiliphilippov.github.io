measly portion size

noun
very small amount of food
8.5 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmizli ˈpɔrʃən saɪz / (AmE), / ˈmiːzli ˈpɔːʃn saɪz / (BrE)
an amount of food that is much smaller than expected or desired, often considered inadequate

Examples

A measly portion size served
Despite the high prices, they served a measly portion size that barely covered half the plate.
He complained to the waiter about the measly portion size of his main course.
We left the restaurant hungry because of the measly portion size.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scanty - smaller than what is needed; insufficient
