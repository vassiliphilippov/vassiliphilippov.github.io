green snake

noun
nonvenomous African green colubrid snake
1.9
Pronunciation: / ɡrin sneɪk / (AmE), / ɡriːn sneɪk / (BrE)
Any of various nonvenomous colubrid snakes of Africa that are typically green in color.

Examples

African green snake
Many bird species prey on the eggs of the green snake in the wild.
Researchers observed a green snake basking on a tree branch near the river.
The green snake is often found in the grasslands of sub-Saharan Africa.
