Madonna

noun
Mary in art
Pronunciation: / məˈdɑnə / (AmE), / məˈdɒnə / (BrE)
a painting or statue of Mary, the mother of Jesus, especially as depicted in Christian art

Examples

A Madonna painting
The church features a stunning Madonna painted by a famous Renaissance artist.
Many museums in Italy display beautiful Madonnas from different historical periods.
She admired the delicate details in the marble Madonna at the cathedral.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Virgin Mary - the mother of Jesus in Christian tradition
  • synonym: icon - a religious image or representation, especially in Eastern Christianity
