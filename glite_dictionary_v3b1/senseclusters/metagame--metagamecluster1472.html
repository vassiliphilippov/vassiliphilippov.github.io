metagame

verb
to use outside strategies
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtəˌɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəˌɡeɪm / (BrE)
to use strategies, knowledge, or expectations from outside the immediate game to gain an advantage while playing

Examples

To metagame opponents
Experienced players often metagame by predicting their opponents' next moves based on previous tournaments.
Some gamers dislike when others metagame instead of playing only by the rules of the current match.
He tried to metagame by researching the most popular strategies before the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: improvise - to create or use strategies spontaneously
  • synonym: outthink - to gain an advantage by thinking more strategically
