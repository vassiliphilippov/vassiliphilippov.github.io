extramedullary

adjective
outside spinal cord or marrow
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəˈmɛdʒəˌlɛri / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈmɛdjʊləri / (BrE)
located or occurring outside the spinal cord or bone marrow

Examples

An extramedullary mass
The doctor explained that the extramedullary mass was pressing against the patient's spinal cord.
Researchers are studying new treatments for extramedullary tumors in cancer patients.
An extramedullary lesion was detected during the MRI scan.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intramedullary - located or occurring within the spinal cord or bone marrow
