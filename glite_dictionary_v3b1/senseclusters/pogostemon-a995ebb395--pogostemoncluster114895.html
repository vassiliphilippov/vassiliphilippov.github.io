Pogostemon

noun
genus of fragrant Asiatic shrubs and trees
9.9
Pronunciation: / poʊˈɡoʊstəˌmɒn /; / poʊˈɡɑːstəˌmɒn / (AmE), / pəˈɡɒstɪmɒn /; / pəˈɡɒstəmɒn / (BrE)
