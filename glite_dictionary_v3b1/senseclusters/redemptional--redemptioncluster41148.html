redemptional

adjective
pertaining to redemption
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdɛmpʃənəl / (AmE), / rɪˈdɛmptʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or resulting in redemption, especially in a religious, moral, or philosophical sense.

Examples

A redemptional message
Her speech had a deeply redemptional message that resonated with the audience.
Many theological discussions focus on the redemptional purpose of suffering.
The novel explores the redemptional aspects of forgiveness and personal transformation.
