hornwort

noun
non-vascular plant with hornlike capsules

Pronunciation: /ˈhɑːrn.wɑːrt/ /; / /ˈhɔːrn.wɔːrt/ (AmE), /ˈhɔːn.wɔːt/ /; / /ˈhɔːn.wɒt/ (BrE)
A small non-vascular plant of the division Anthocerotophyta, characterized by slender, hornlike spore capsules.

Examples

Unlike mosses, hornworts have a single large chloroplast in each cell.
