effeminate style

noun
feminine manner of dress or behavior
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛmənɪt staɪl / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛmɪnət staɪl / (BrE)
a manner of dress or behavior regarded as characteristic of or resembling that of women

Examples

Effeminate style of dress
His effeminate style surprised everyone at the party.
In conservative circles, an effeminate style of dress is often frowned upon for men.
The performer's effeminate style challenged traditional notions of masculinity on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feminine - having qualities or appearance traditionally associated with women
  • antonym: masculine - having qualities or appearance traditionally associated with men
