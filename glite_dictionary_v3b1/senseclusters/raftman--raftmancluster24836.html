raftman

noun
man who travels by raft
Pronunciation: / ˈræftˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈrɑːftˌman / (BrE)
a man who travels by or works on a raft, especially for transporting people or goods by river

Examples

A raftman on river
During the spring floods, a raftman was an essential figure in the logging camps.
Many stories from the frontier feature a hardy raftman navigating dangerous rapids.
The seasoned raftman guided the makeshift vessel down the winding river.
