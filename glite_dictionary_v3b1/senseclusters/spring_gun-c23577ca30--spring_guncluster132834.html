spring gun

noun
trap-activated gun
9.6 7.3
Pronunciation: /sprɪŋ ɡʌn/ (AmE), /sprɪŋ ɡʌn/ (BrE)
A gun that is rigged to discharge automatically when someone triggers a connected wire or mechanism, typically used as a trap against intruders.

Examples

A spring gun trap
He read about how poachers would use a spring gun to protect their illicit traps in remote woods.
Setting a spring gun is illegal in many countries due to the risk to innocent people.
The old farmhouse still had a spring gun hidden by the door as a deterrent to trespassers.

  • synonym: booby trap - an explosive device intended to harm when triggered
