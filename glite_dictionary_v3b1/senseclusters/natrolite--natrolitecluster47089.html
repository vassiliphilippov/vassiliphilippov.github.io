natrolite

noun
a mineral of the zeolite group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnæt.rəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈnatrəlʌɪt / (BrE)
a white or colorless mineral of the zeolite group consisting of a hydrous silicate of sodium and aluminum
