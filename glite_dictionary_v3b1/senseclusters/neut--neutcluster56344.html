neut

noun
abbreviation for neutron
9.9
Pronunciation: / nuːt / (AmE), / njuːt / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for 'neutron', the neutral subatomic particle found in atomic nuclei

Examples

A neut particle
The scientist noted the presence of a single neut in the experiment.
In particle physics discussions, researchers sometimes refer to a neutron as a neut.
The detector was calibrated to identify every neut passing through the chamber.
