Gemini program

proper noun
US manned spaceflight program, 1965–66
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛmɪnaɪ ˌproʊɡræm / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛmɪni ˌprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
the US spaceflight program (1965–66) that sent manned missions into Earth orbit as preparation for the Apollo missions
