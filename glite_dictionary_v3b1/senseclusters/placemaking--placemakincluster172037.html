placemaking

adjective
community space design
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪsˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪsˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to activities or design aimed at creating or improving public spaces for community benefit.

Examples

Placemaking design ideas
The city launched a placemaking initiative to transform empty lots into vibrant parks.
A placemaking approach encourages local residents to participate in designing public spaces.
Successful placemaking projects often reflect the unique character of a neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: community development - efforts to improve the well-being of a community
  • synonym: urban planning - the process of designing and organizing urban spaces
