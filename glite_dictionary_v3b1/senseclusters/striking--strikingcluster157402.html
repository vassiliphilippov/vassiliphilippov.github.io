striking

adjective
very noticeable or impressive
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstraɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
attracting attention because of being unusual, impressive, or beautiful

Examples

Striking beauty
She bore a striking resemblance to her grandmother at the same age.
The museum displayed a striking piece of modern art that captured everyone’s gaze.
What makes the building truly striking is its unusual glass facade.
She has a striking resemblance to her grandmother in that old photograph.
The actress wore a striking red dress to the awards ceremony.
The painting's striking colors drew everyone's attention as soon as they entered the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remarkable - worthy of attention; striking
  • antonym: unremarkable - not particularly interesting or notable
