Swedish language

noun
Scandinavian language of Sweden and Finland
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈswiːdɪʃ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈswiːdɪʃ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the North Germanic language that is the official language of Sweden and one of the two official languages of Finland
