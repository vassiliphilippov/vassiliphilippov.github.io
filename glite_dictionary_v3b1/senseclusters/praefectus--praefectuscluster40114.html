praefectus

noun
church official
9.9
Pronunciation: / praɪˈfɛktəs / (AmE), / prɪˈfɛktəs / (BrE)
an ecclesiastical official responsible for overseeing a church department, congregation, or religious institution

Examples

Church praefectus of a department
The praefectus was responsible for managing the church's finances and staff.
A new praefectus was appointed to oversee the religious education department.
The congregation respected the praefectus for his dedication and leadership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bishop - a senior member of the Christian clergy in charge of a diocese
  • synonym: prefect - an official in charge of a particular area or department
