unregretting

adjective
not feeling regret
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈɡrɛt.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈɡrɛt.ɪŋ / (BrE)
not feeling or showing regret for something done or not done.

Examples

An unregretting choice
Despite the consequences, she remained unregretting about her decision to leave.
He spoke in an unregretting tone, as if certain he had made the right choice.
The memoir presents an unregretting account of a life lived boldly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: regretful - feeling or expressing regret
  • antonym: remorseful - deeply regretful; showing remorse
