preceptor

noun
university or college teacher
Pronunciation: / priˈsɛptər / (AmE), / prɪˈsɛptə /; / priˈsɛptə / (BrE)
a teacher or instructor, especially one who supervises students in a university, college, or practical setting

Examples

A preceptor supervises students
At Oxford, it was customary for each student to be assigned a personal preceptor.
She was appointed as a preceptor at the university shortly after earning her doctorate.
The young scholar admired his preceptor for both his knowledge and his kindness.
The medical preceptor guided the interns through their first patient examinations.
Each nursing student was assigned a preceptor to help them learn clinical skills.
Her preceptor provided valuable feedback after every teaching session.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instructor - a person who teaches something
  • synonym: mentor - an experienced person who guides and advises someone less experienced
  • antonym: student - a person who is studying
  • synonym: teacher - a person who helps others to learn
