doctor-fish

noun
spa pedicure fish (Garra rufa)
5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈdɑːk.tər.fɪʃ/ /; / /ˈdɔːk.tər.fɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈdɒk.tə.fɪʃ/ (BrE)
A small freshwater fish (typically Garra rufa) used in spa pedicures to nibble dead skin from customers' feet and hands.

Examples

Doctor-fish pedicure
She booked a doctor-fish spa pedicure in Istanbul to remove dead skin.
Health inspectors warned that some doctor-fish spas might spread infections if hygiene is poor.
At the market she admired a small tank holding several lively doctor-fish nibbling skin gently.
