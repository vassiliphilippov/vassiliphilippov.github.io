query

noun
a question or inquiry
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪri / (AmE), / ˈkwɪəri / (BrE)
A question, especially one addressed to an official or organization, or an expression of doubt about something.

Examples

A query about payment
If you have any queries, please don't hesitate to contact customer support.
She had a query about the new company policy that needed clarification.
The student submitted his query to the professor after the lecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inquiry - an act of asking for information
  • synonym: question - a sentence or phrase used to find out information
