overpack

verb
to pack too much
4.2
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈpæk/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈpæk/ (BrE)
to pack more items than necessary or to fill a container too tightly

Examples

To overpack a suitcase
If you overpack your suitcase, it might be too heavy to carry comfortably.
He always tries not to overpack when traveling for business.
It is easy to overpack for a short weekend trip, but try to bring only essentials.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overfill - to fill something beyond its capacity
  • antonym: underpack - to pack fewer items than needed
