Spartan

noun
resident of ancient Sparta
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːrtən / (AmE), / ˈspɑːtən / (BrE)
A person who comes from or lives in the ancient Greek city-state of Sparta.

Examples

A famous Spartan
Leonidas was a famous Spartan king known for his leadership at the Battle of Thermopylae.
Many Spartan customs have become legendary in Western history.
The Spartans were renowned for their military discipline and bravery.
