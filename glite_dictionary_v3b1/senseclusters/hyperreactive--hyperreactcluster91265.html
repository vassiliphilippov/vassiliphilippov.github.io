hyperreactive

adjective
overly responsive
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərriˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpərriˈæktɪv / (BrE)
having an abnormally strong or exaggerated response to stimuli or situations

Examples

Hyperreactive to pollen
Some people are hyperreactive to pollen and develop severe allergy symptoms.
The patient's immune system was found to be hyperreactive after the treatment.
A hyperreactive response to stress can make daily life more challenging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypersensitive - having an extreme or excessive sensitivity to stimuli
  • antonym: unresponsive - not reacting or responding to stimuli
