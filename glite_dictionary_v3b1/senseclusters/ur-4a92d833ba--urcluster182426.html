Ur

proper noun
ancient Sumerian city in Mesopotamia
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɜːr / (AmE), / ɜː / (BrE)
an ancient city of Sumer in southern Mesopotamia, located near a former channel of the Euphrates River, known for its archaeological and historical significance.

Examples

Ancient city Ur
Archaeologists have discovered many artifacts at Ur, shedding light on early Sumerian civilization.
Many historians regard Ur as one of the world's earliest urban centers.
The ziggurat at Ur is one of the best-preserved structures from ancient Mesopotamia.
