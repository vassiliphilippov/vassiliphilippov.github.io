splenic

adjective
relating to the spleen
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsplɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˈspliːnɪk / (BrE)

Examples

Splenic biopsy
Doctors sometimes perform a splenic biopsy to diagnose certain diseases.

The splenic artery supplies blood to the spleen and part of the stomach.
