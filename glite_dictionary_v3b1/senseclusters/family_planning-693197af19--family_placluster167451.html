family planning

noun
control of the number of children in a family
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ˌplænɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfæməli ˌplænɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of controlling the number and spacing of children in a family, typically by using contraception or other methods

Examples

Family planning services
Access to family planning services can improve women’s health and economic opportunities.
He works for a clinic that provides family planning advice and contraception.
Many governments promote family planning to help manage population growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: birth control - the use of methods to prevent pregnancy
  • synonym: contraception - the deliberate use of artificial methods or other techniques to prevent pregnancy
