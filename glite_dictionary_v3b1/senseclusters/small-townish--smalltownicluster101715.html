small-townish

adjective
characteristic of a small town; friendly or close-knit
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɔlˌtaʊnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːlˌtaʊnɪʃ / (BrE)
having the qualities or atmosphere typical of a small town, especially in being friendly, close-knit, or old-fashioned
