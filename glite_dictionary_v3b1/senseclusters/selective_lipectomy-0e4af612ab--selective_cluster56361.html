selective lipectomy

noun
surgical removal of targeted fat deposits
Pronunciation: / sɪˈlɛktɪv laɪˈpɛktəmi / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛktɪv lɪˈpɛktəmi / (BrE)
a plastic surgery operation that involves removing specific areas of fatty tissue from the body

Examples

Selective lipectomy surgery

The patient opted for a selective lipectomy to address stubborn fat pockets.
