spar

verb
(of birds) fight with spurs
Pronunciation: / spɑr / (AmE), / spɑː / (BrE)
to fight or practice fighting, especially in boxing or between animals, often as training or sport

Examples

He often spars with his friend to stay in shape.
The two fighters began to spar in the ring before the real match started.
We practiced our techniques by sparring lightly every morning.
