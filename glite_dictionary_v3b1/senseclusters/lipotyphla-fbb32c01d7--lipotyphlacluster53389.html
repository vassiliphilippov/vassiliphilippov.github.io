Lipotyphla

noun
former mammal order: moles, shrews, hedgehogs
Pronunciation: / lɪˈpoʊtəflə /; / ˌlɪpoʊˈtɪflə / (AmE), / lɪˈpɒtɪflə /; / ˌlɪpəˈtɪflə / (BrE)
a former zoological group (order or suborder) of small, insect-eating mammals, including moles, hedgehogs, and shrews, now obsolete in modern classification
