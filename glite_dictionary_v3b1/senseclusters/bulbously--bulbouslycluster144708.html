bulbously

adverb
in a swollen way
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌlbəsli / (AmE), / ˈbʌlbəsli / (BrE)
in a manner that is round, swollen, or shaped like a bulb

Examples

Bulbously shaped root
The old tree's roots spread bulbously across the ground, making the soil uneven.
His nose was shaped bulbously, giving his face a distinctive look.
The glass vase expanded bulbously at the base, making it stable on the table.
