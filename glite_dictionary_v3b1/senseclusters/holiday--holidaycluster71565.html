holiday

noun
day when work is suspended
1.0 26/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…ëÀêl…™Àåde…™ / (AmE), / Ààh…íl…™de…™ / (BrE)
A day when most people do not have to work, usually because of a law or custom; a public holiday.

Examples

A national holiday
Because it was a holiday, the streets were quieter than usual.
Most businesses will close for the national holiday next week.
Our family always travels during the winter holidays.

Synonyms

  • public holiday
    1.4 0.11/m
    a public holiday is a formally recognized day when work stops by law, while a holiday is the common everyday word for the same.
  • legal holiday
    1.8 0.13/m
    a legal holiday is a day officially set by law when official business stops, while a holiday is the general word for such days.

Related Terms
