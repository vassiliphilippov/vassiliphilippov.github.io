sweat of one's brow

noun
hard work or effort to achieve something
7.4
Pronunciation: / swɛt əv wʌnz braʊ / (AmE), / swɛt əv wʌnz braʊ / (BrE)
hard work or effort, especially as a means of earning a living or accomplishing something

Examples

They built their small business from scratch by the sweat of their brow.
