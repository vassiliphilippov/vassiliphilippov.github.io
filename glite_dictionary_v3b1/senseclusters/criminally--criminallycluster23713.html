criminally

adverb
in a criminal manner
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪmɪnəli / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪnəli / (BrE)
in a way that involves a violation of the law or is characteristic of a crime

Examples

Criminally responsible
Altering the records was deemed criminally fraudulent by the court.
He was prosecuted for criminally misappropriating funds from the charity.
The company was found to have acted criminally negligent in its safety procedures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illegally - in a manner not allowed by law
  • antonym: lawfully - in a manner permitted by law
