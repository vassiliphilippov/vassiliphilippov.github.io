Synonyms

  • thunder
    3.2 2/m
    thunder makes a loud, deep, rolling noise like a distant storm, while boom is a sudden, heavy impact sound.
  • roar
    1.0 9/m
    roar is a loud, continuous sound from animals or engines, while boom is often a short, deep single blast.
  • rumble
    2.5 6/m
    rumble is a low, long, vibrating sound like distant thunder, while boom is a short, sharp, powerful noise.
  • resound
    5.3 1/m
    resound fills a place as sound echoes and carries, while boom is a single, heavy noise from a strong strike.
  • ring out
    3.6 2/m
    ring out means to sound loudly and clearly for some time, while boom is a deep, sudden, heavy noise.
