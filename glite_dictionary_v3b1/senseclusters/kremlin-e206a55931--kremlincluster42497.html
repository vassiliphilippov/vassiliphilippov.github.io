Kremlin

noun
the Russian government's citadel in Moscow
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛmlɪn / (AmE), / ˈkrɛm.lɪn / (BrE)
the citadel in Moscow that is the seat of the Russian government; often used to refer to the Russian government itself

Examples

The Kremlin government
During the Cold War, many important decisions were made behind the walls of the Kremlin.
Journalists waited outside the Kremlin for news about the summit.
The Kremlin denied involvement in the incident, according to official statements.
