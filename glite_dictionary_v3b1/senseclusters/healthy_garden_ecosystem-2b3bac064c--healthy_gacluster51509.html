healthy garden ecosystem

noun
balanced environment for plant growth in a garden
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlθi ˈɡɑrdn ˌikoʊˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθi ˈɡɑːdn ˌiːkəʊˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a balanced and functioning environment in a garden that supports plant growth and sustains beneficial organisms

Examples

A healthy garden ecosystem supports plants
Attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies helps create a healthy garden ecosystem.
Composting is one way to maintain a healthy garden ecosystem by enriching the soil naturally.
Overuse of pesticides can disrupt the balance of a healthy garden ecosystem.

  • synonym: ecosystem - a biological community of interacting organisms and their physical environment
