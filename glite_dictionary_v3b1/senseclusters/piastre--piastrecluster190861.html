piastre

noun
a currency unit equal to 1/100 lira (Turkey)
6.5
Pronunciation: / piˈæstər /; / ˈpaɪˌæstər / (AmE), / piˈæstə /; / piˈastə /; / ˈpɪəstə / (BrE)
a small unit of money equal to one hundredth of the main currency, used in countries such as Turkey, Egypt, Lebanon, Sudan, and Syria

Examples
