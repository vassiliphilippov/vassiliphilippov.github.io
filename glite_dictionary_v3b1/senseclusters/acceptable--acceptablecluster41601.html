Synonyms

  • satisfactory
    2.3 7/m
    satisfactory means it meets required standards in a clear test or task, while acceptable is a broader allowance.
  • adequate
    2.7 30/m
    adequate gives just enough quality or amount for a need, while acceptable can be a wider, more general approval.
  • decent
    2.3 20/m
    decent feels fairly good, respectable in social terms, while acceptable simply means it can be tolerated or used.
  • passable
    4.4 0.7/m
    passable is barely good enough to pass or be accepted, while acceptable means it meets a needed standard.
