scuff

noun
surface mark
2.6
Pronunciation: / skʌf / (AmE), / skʌf / (BrE)
a mark or scratch on a surface caused by rubbing, scraping, or friction

Examples

A scuff mark
After moving the furniture, I noticed several scuffs on the hardwood floor.
He tried to clean the scuff from his new shoes with a damp cloth.
The wall had a visible scuff where the suitcase had rubbed against it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: polish - to make a surface smooth and shiny
  • synonym: scratch - a thin mark or line caused by scraping a surface
  • synonym: stain - a mark that is difficult to remove from a surface
