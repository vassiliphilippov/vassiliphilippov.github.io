diestrus

noun
stage of sexual inactivity (animal reproductive cycle)

A phase in the reproductive cycle of some animals, characterized by sexual inactivity between periods of estrus (heat).

Examples

The diestrus phase
During diestrus, hormone levels in the female dog begin to return to normal as her body rests.
The period of diestrus follows estrus and is marked by a lack of sexual behavior.
Veterinarians often monitor the length of diestrus to help diagnose reproductive issues in animals.
