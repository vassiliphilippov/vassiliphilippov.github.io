round

noun
single bullet or shell for a gun
Pronunciation: / raʊnd / (AmE), / raʊnd / (BrE)
a single charge of ammunition, such as a bullet or shell, for firing from a gun or other weapon

Examples

A round of bullets
Police recovered several live rounds at the scene of the incident.
She found a spent round on the shooting range.
The soldier loaded another round into his rifle before taking aim.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cartridge - a case containing a charge for a firearm
