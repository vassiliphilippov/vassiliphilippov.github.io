tin

verb
to coat with tin
Pronunciation: / tɪn / (AmE), / tɪn / (BrE)
to coat or cover a surface (especially metal) with a thin layer of tin or solder

Examples

To tin a pan
Before using the pot for cooking, you should tin its interior.
Manufacturers often tin copper wires to prevent corrosion.
The craftsman carefully tinned the steel plate before soldering.
Before attaching the components, remember to tin the wire ends for a better connection.
She practiced tinning small metal pieces as part of her training in electronics.
To ensure a strong joint, he carefully tinned the copper pipe before brazing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electroplate - to coat a metal object by electrolysis with a thin layer of another metal
  • synonym: solder - to join or coat metal with solder
