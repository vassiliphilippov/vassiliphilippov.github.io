incidence

noun
rate or frequency of occurrence
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.sɪ.dəns / (AmE), / ˈɪn.sɪ.dəns / (BrE)
the rate or frequency at which something happens, especially something undesirable

Examples

Incidence of disease
Smoking raises the incidence of heart disease among adults.
The incidence of childhood obesity has increased dramatically over the past decade.
The city is working to reduce the incidence of violent crime in its neighborhoods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prevalence - the total number of cases in a population
