urban

adjective
relating to cities or towns
2.3 2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɝː.bən /; / ˈɜːr.bən / (AmE), / ˈɜː.bən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a city or town (not rural)

Examples

Urban city life
She prefers the excitement of urban living to the quietness of rural areas.

The government launched a new plan to revitalize urban neighborhoods.
Many young professionals prefer urban living for easier access to work and amenities.
The city council is focused on improving urban transportation systems.
Urbanization has led to the rapid expansion of urban areas in many countries.
