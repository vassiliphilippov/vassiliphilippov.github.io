anticlastic

adjective
opposite curvatures
Pronunciation: / ˌæntɪˈklæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntɪˈklæstɪk / (BrE)
having opposite curvatures in two perpendicular directions, such as a surface that curves like a saddle

Examples

An anticlastic surface
The sculptor created an anticlastic form that twisted elegantly in space.
Engineers studied the anticlastic properties of the new bridge's support structure.
The stadium's anticlastic roof allows for both strength and a unique visual effect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: synclastic - having the same curvature in all directions, like a dome
