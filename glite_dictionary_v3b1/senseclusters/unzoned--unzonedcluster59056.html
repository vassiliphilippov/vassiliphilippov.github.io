unzoned

adjective
not assigned zoning
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈzoʊnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈzəʊnd / (BrE)
not assigned to a zoning category or not divided into zones, especially regarding land use or development

Examples

Unzoned land
The city council debated whether the unzoned land should be used for housing or commercial purposes.
Because the property was unzoned, developers had more flexibility in their building plans.
Some rural areas remain unzoned, allowing a mix of residential and industrial activities.
The new development area is unzoned, allowing for a mix of residential and commercial buildings.
Because the park is unzoned, visitors can use any section for picnics or sports.
The city council debated whether to keep the district unzoned or introduce new regulations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: zoned - assigned to a specific zoning category or regulation
