Tahiti

proper noun
a major island in French Polynesia, South Pacific
Pronunciation: / təˈhiti / (AmE), / təˈhiːti / (BrE)
an island in the South Pacific Ocean, the largest and most important island of French Polynesia, known for its tropical scenery and cultural significance

Examples

Tahiti island
After a long journey, the explorers finally arrived on the shores of Tahiti.
I have always dreamed of visiting Tahiti to see its beautiful beaches and clear blue waters.
Many artists, including Paul Gauguin, found inspiration in the landscapes of Tahiti.
