valuable

adjective
of great worth or importance
Pronunciation: / ˈvæljəbəl / (AmE), / ˈvæljʊəbl / (BrE)
worth a lot or having great importance, usefulness, or merit.

Examples

Valuable advice
Honesty is a valuable quality in a friend.
She gave me some valuable advice before my interview.
The documents are extremely valuable for understanding our history.
