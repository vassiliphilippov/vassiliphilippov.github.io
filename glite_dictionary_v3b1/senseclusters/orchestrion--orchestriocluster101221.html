orchestrion

noun
mechanical orchestra instrument
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrkəˈstriːən / (AmE), / ˌɔːkɪˈstriːən / (BrE)
a large mechanical musical instrument that plays music imitating the sound of an orchestra, often using pipes, drums, and other devices

Examples

A orchestrion machine
The museum displayed a restored orchestrion that once entertained guests in grand hotels.
Visitors were amazed by the complex mechanisms inside the old orchestrion.
The sound of the orchestrion filled the hall with lively music from a bygone era.
