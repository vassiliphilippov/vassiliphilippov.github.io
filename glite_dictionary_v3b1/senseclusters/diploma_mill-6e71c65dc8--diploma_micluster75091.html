diploma mill

noun
organization selling unearned degrees
5.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈploʊmə mɪl / (AmE), / dɪˈpləʊmə mɪl / (BrE)
an organization or institution that awards academic degrees or diplomas with little or no academic requirements, often for a fee

Examples

Diploma mill organization
Laws have been enacted in several states to crack down on diploma mills.
Many employers check the accreditation status to avoid hiring graduates from diploma mills.
She unknowingly spent thousands of dollars on a degree from a diploma mill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: degree mill - an institution offering degrees with insufficient educational requirements
