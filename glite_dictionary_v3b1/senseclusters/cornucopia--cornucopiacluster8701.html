cornucopia

noun
horn-shaped symbol of abundance
8.5 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / Àåk…îrn.j…ôÀàko ä.pi.…ô / (AmE), / Àåk…îÀê.njuÀêÀàk…ô ä.pi.…ô / (BrE)
A decorative container shaped like a goat's horn, overflowing with fruits, flowers, and grains, symbolizing abundance and prosperity.

Examples

A decorative cornucopia
She placed a ceramic cornucopia on the mantelpiece as part of the autumn decorations.
The cornucopia has long been used as a symbol of prosperity and abundance in art.
The Thanksgiving table was decorated with a cornucopia overflowing with fresh fruit and vegetables.

Synonyms

  • horn of plenty
    7.1 0.02/m
    horn of plenty is the common name for the overflowing symbolic horn, while cornucopia is the decorative container shaped like that horn.
