dextrously

adverb
in a skillful or deft manner
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛkstrəsli / (AmE), / ˈdɛkstrəsli / (BrE)
In a skillful or deft manner; with dexterity.

Examples

To dextrously repair
She dextrously repaired the jewelry with tiny tools.
The knots were dextrously untied within seconds.
The magician moved his hands dextrously to perform the card trick.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsily - in a clumsy manner, lacking dexterity
  • synonym: skillfully - in a skillful manner
