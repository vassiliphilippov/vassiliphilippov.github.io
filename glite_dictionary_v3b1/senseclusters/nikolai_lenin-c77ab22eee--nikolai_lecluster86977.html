Nikolai Lenin

proper noun
Russian revolutionary and first Soviet leader
4.9
Pronunciation: /nɪk.ə.ˈlaɪ ˈle.nɪn/ /; / /nɪk.ə.ˈlaɪ ˈle.nən/ (AmE), /nɪk.ə.ˈlaɪ ˈle.nɪn/ /; / /nɪk.ə.ˈlaɪ ˈle.nən/ (BrE)
