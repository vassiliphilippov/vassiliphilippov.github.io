hydrodynamically

adverb
in a fluid-flow manner
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdroʊdaɪˈnæmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdrəʊdaɪˈnæmɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the movement or flow of fluids, especially water

Examples

Moves hydrodynamically in water
The new submarine moves hydrodynamically to reduce resistance in the water.
Engineers tested the model hydrodynamically to ensure it would perform well in strong currents.
The fish's body is shaped hydrodynamically for efficient swimming.

Related Terms
