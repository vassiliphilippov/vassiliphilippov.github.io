presocratic

adjective
before Socrates; ancient Greek philosophy
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːsoʊˈkrætɪk / (AmE), / ˌpriːsəˈkrætɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the period or philosophers in ancient Greece before the time of Socrates.

Examples

Presocratic philosophers
He wrote a thesis on presocratic views of nature and change.
Many presocratic thinkers tried to explain the origin of the universe using natural principles.
The presocratic philosophers were among the first to question traditional mythology.
