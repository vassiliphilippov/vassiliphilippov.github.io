jodhpur shoe

noun
short riding boot with side buckle
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑd.pɚ ˌʃu / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒd.pə ˌʃuː / (BrE)
A short leather riding boot that fastens with a strap and buckle at the side, often worn with jodhpurs.

Examples

A pair of jodhpur shoes
She wore her jodhpur shoes with matching breeches for the riding lesson.
The jodhpur shoe is designed to offer both comfort and support to equestrians.
You can easily recognize a jodhpur shoe by its distinctive ankle strap and buckle.
