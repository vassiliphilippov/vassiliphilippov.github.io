street fighter

noun
person skilled in street fighting
Pronunciation: / ˈstɹit ˌfaɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈstriːt ˌfaɪtə / (BrE)
a person who fights skillfully in street situations, often without formal training in boxing or martial arts

Examples

A tough street fighter
Despite his reputation as a street fighter, he always avoided unnecessary confrontations.
Her years as a street fighter taught her to expect the unexpected.
The champion admitted he started out as a street fighter before entering professional boxing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brawler - a person who fights noisily in public places
