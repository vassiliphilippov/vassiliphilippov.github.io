Synonyms

  • blockage
    4.1 2/m
    a blockage is a physical barrier that stops flow in a pipe or road, while closure names that act or obstruction.
  • obstruction
    4.4 2/m
    an obstruction is a thing that blocks movement on a path or road, while closure means blocking or the act of closing.
  • occlusion
    8.7 0.1/m
    an occlusion is a medical or technical blockage inside a vessel or pipe, while closure is any blocking or closing action.
  • stoppage
    5.0 0.01/m
    a stoppage is a sudden failure that stops liquid or gas flow in a pipe, while closure means blocking or closing more broadly.
  • closing off
    5.2 0.1/m
    closing off is the act of isolating an area by preventing access, while closure can mean blocking or ending something.
  • closing
    2.3 14/m
    closing is bringing an event, door, or business to an end, while closure refers to blocking or an obstruction in passage.
