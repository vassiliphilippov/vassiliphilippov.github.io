labor

verb
to be in childbirth
2.6 4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪbɚ /; / ˈleɪbər / (AmE), / ˈleɪbə / (BrE)
(of a woman) to experience the process of giving birth; to undergo the efforts and pains of childbirth.

Examples

To labor in childbirth
Many mothers labor for several hours before giving birth.
She began to labor late in the evening and delivered a healthy baby by morning.
The nurse stayed with her while she labored through the night.
Many women choose to labour at home before heading to the hospital.
She laboured for several hours before the child was born.
The mother began to labour late in the night and delivered her baby by dawn.
