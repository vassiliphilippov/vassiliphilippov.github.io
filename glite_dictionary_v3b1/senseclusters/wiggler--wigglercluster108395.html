wiggler

noun
someone who can't stay still, especially a child
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪɡ.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɪɡ.lə / (BrE)
a person, especially a child, who cannot sit or stay still; someone who fidgets or squirms repeatedly

Examples

Little wiggler
It's hard for a wiggler to sit through a long movie without moving around.
My niece, always a wiggler, kept jumping up and down while waiting in line.
The toddler was such a wiggler during dinner that he couldn't stay in his seat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fidget - a person who fidgets or has trouble staying still
