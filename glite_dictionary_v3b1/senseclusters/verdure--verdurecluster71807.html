verdure

noun
lush green vegetation
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrdʒər /; / ˈvɝːdʒər / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdjə / (BrE)
lush green vegetation or the fresh green color and vitality of flourishing plants

Examples

Fresh verdure
After a long drought, rain restored the verdure to the parched fields.
In spring, the verdure of the countryside is especially vibrant and fresh.
The hikers admired the thick verdure along the riverbanks as they walked.
Even in the heart of summer, the forest retained its deep verdure.
The garden was filled with a rich verdure after the spring rains.
They admired the verdure of the hills stretching into the distance.
