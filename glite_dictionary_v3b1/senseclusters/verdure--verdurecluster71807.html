verdure

noun
lush green vegetation
8.9 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààv…úrd í…ôr /; / Ààv…ùÀêd í…ôr / (AmE), / Ààv…úÀêdj…ô / (BrE)
lush green vegetation or the fresh green color and vitality of flourishing plants

Examples

Fresh verdure
After a long drought, rain restored the verdure to the parched fields.
In spring, the verdure of the countryside is especially vibrant and fresh.
The hikers admired the thick verdure along the riverbanks as they walked.
Even in the heart of summer, the forest retained its deep verdure.
The garden was filled with a rich verdure after the spring rains.
They admired the verdure of the hills stretching into the distance.

Synonyms

  • verdancy
    8.6 0.01/m
    verdancy shows a rich green growth across plants, while verdure describes lush green vegetation full of life.
  • greenness
    3.3 0.21/m
    greenness refers to the green color and freshness of plants, while verdure focuses on lush living vegetation and vitality.
  • lush green foliage
    3.5 0.02/m
    lush green foliage describes dense healthy leaves on plants, while verdure means broad wide areas of flourishing green vegetation.
  • greenery
    2.2 1.92/m
    greenery often names plants used to decorate rooms or events, while verdure means natural, flourishing green growth across landscapes.
