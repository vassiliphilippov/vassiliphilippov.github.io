scrappily

adverb
in a combative or contentious way
Pronunciation: / ˈskræpɪli / (AmE), / ˈskræpɪli / (BrE)
In a combative or argumentative manner; showing readiness to fight or argue.

Examples

To fight scrappily
Despite being smaller, she fought scrappily and never backed down from a challenge.
The debate turned scrappily as both candidates argued passionately over the issue.
The underdog team played scrappily, refusing to give their opponents an easy win.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggressively - in a forceful or hostile manner
  • antonym: peacefully - in a peaceful or calm manner
