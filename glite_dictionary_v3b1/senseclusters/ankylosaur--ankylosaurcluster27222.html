ankylosaur

noun
armoured herbivorous dinosaur
9.9 8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋkəloʊˌsɔr / (AmE), / ˈaŋkɪləsɔː / (BrE)
a large herbivorous dinosaur of the late Jurassic and Cretaceous periods, having a heavily armoured body and a club-like tail

Examples

The ankylosaur dinosaur
Researchers believe that the ankylosaur's armor protected it from even the most fearsome predators.
The fossilized remains of an ankylosaur were discovered in the Montana badlands.
Unlike other dinosaurs, the ankylosaur had a bony club at the end of its tail for defense.
