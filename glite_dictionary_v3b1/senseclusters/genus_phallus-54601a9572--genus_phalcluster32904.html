genus Phallus

noun
genus of fungi with hanging cap
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈfæl.əs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈfal.əs / (BrE)
A genus of fungi characterized by a cap or pileus that hangs free around the stem, including several well-known stinkhorn species.
