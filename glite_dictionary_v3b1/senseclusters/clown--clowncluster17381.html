clown

noun
a rude or vulgar fool
Pronunciation: / klaʊn / (AmE), / klaʊn / (BrE)
A person who behaves in a foolish, rude, or vulgar way; a fool or buffoon.

Examples

A clown in class
Don't be such a clown; show some respect for the occasion.
He acted like a total clown during the meeting, making offensive jokes.
Nobody takes him seriously because he's known as the office clown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buffoon - a ridiculous but amusing person; a clown
  • synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
