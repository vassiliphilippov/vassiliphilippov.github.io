crampoon

noun
device for lifting heavy objects
Pronunciation: / kræmˈpun / (AmE), / kræmˈpuːn / (BrE)
a hinged pair of curved iron bars used for lifting or raising heavy objects

Examples

A crampoon lifts stones
A crampoon is essential for handling large stone blocks at the quarry.
The iron crampoon gripped the object firmly as it was hoisted.
The workers used a crampoon to lift the beam into place safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cramp - a device for fastening objects together
