delineative

adjective
depicting clearly and recognizably
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪniˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪniətɪv / (BrE)
showing or depicting something clearly and recognizably; serving to delineate or represent accurately

Examples

A delineative style
Her delineative approach contrasts with abstract techniques, emphasizing recognizable forms.
In the exhibition, most paintings were highly delineative, focusing on realistic landscapes.
The artist's delineative style made every figure appear lifelike and full of character.

Related Terms

  • synonym: representational - depicting things as they appear in real life, not abstract
