ideational

adjective
related to ideas or concepts
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪdiˈeɪʃənl / (AmE), / ˌaɪdiːˈeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
Relating to or concerned with ideas, concepts, or mental representations rather than physical objects.

Examples

An ideational concept
Her ideational framework helped structure the discussion around abstract concepts.
His ideational abilities made him excel in philosophy and theory-based disciplines.
The study focused on the ideational aspects of language development in children.
