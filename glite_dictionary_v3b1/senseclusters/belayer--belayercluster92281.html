belayer

noun
climbing rope controller
8.4
Pronunciation: / bɪˈleɪər / (AmE), / bɪˈleɪə / (BrE)
a person responsible for controlling the safety rope for a climber during climbing activities

Examples

Climbing belayer
The belayer must always pay close attention to the climber's movements.
Before starting the climb, she checked that her belayer was ready and alert.
A good belayer can make a big difference in a climber's safety and confidence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: climber - a person who climbs, especially as a sport
  • synonym: spotter - a person who watches and assists a climber for safety
