directionally

adverb
in relation to direction
5.3
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛkʃənəli / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛkʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or depends on direction.

Examples

Directionally sensitive microphone
The new microphone is directionally sensitive, picking up sound only from one side.
Birds often migrate directionally in response to seasonal changes.
