beshrew

verb
wish harm on, curse (archaic)
Pronunciation: / bɪˈʃru / (AmE), / bɪˈʃruː / (BrE)
(archaic) to wish harm or invoke evil upon; to curse.

Examples

To beshrew someone
'Beshrew me,' he muttered, frustrated by his own folly.
In the play, the character did beshrew the villain's name for all his misdeeds.
She exclaimed, 'Beshrew my heart for believing his lies!'

Related Terms

  • synonym: curse - to invoke harm or misfortune upon someone or something
