feathertop grass

noun
African grass with feathery panicles
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛðərˌtɑp ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈfɛðətɒp ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A tropical African grass plant characterized by feathery, plume-like panicles.

Examples

Feathertop grass plant
Farmers in the region often encounter feathertop grass growing wild along irrigation channels.
