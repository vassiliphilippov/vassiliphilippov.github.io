ornithomimid

noun
type of lightly built dinosaur
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɔːrˌnɪθəˈmɪmɪd / (AmE), / ɔːˌnɪθəˈmɪmɪd / (BrE)
A lightly built, medium-sized dinosaur with extremely long limbs and neck, a small head, large eyes, and a relatively large brain; member of the family Ornithomimidae.

Examples

The ornithomimid dinosaur
Fossil evidence suggests that ornithomimid dinosaurs were among the fastest runners of the Cretaceous period.
Some paleontologists believe that ornithomimids may have had feathers similar to modern birds.
The museum display featured a life-size reconstruction of an ornithomimid skeleton.
