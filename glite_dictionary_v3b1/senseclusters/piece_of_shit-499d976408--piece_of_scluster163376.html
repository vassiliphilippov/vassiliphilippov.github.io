piece of shit

noun
something extremely bad or worthless (vulgar slang)
9.9
Pronunciation: / pis əv ʃɪt / (AmE), / piːs əv ʃɪt / (BrE)
a thing or person regarded as extremely bad, worthless, or contemptible, often used as an insult

Examples

A piece of shit insult

I can't believe I wasted my money on this piece of shit car.
That movie was a complete piece of shit; I walked out halfway through.
