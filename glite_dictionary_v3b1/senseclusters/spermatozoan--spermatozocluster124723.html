spermatozoan

noun
male reproductive cell
Pronunciation: / ˌspɝː.mə.təˈzoʊ.ən / (AmE), / ˌspɜː.mə.təˈzəʊ.ən / (BrE)
A male reproductive cell or gamete, typically motile and capable of fertilizing a female egg cell.

Examples

A spermatozoan cell
A healthy spermatozoan is vital for successful fertilization.
Each spermatozoan carries genetic information from the father to the offspring.
Under the microscope, the spermatozoan could be seen swimming toward the egg cell.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ovum - the female reproductive cell or gamete
  • synonym: sperm - a male reproductive cell or gamete
