medevac

verb
to airlift for treatment
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdəˌvæk / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪˌvæk / (BrE)
to transport someone by helicopter or aircraft for emergency medical treatment, especially from a dangerous or remote location

Examples

To medevac a patient
After the accident, the injured worker was medevaced to a trauma center within minutes.
The rescue team medevacs patients from remote areas during natural disasters.
Several soldiers were medevaced out of the combat zone for urgent care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airlift - to transport people or goods by aircraft, especially in emergencies
  • synonym: evacuate - to remove people from a dangerous place
