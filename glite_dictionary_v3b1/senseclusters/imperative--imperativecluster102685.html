imperative

adjective
very important; urgent
5.9
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɛrətɪv / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɛrətɪv / (BrE)
extremely important and needing immediate attention or action.

Examples

Imperative action
Finding a solution to the crisis is now imperative for the organization.
It is imperative that all students submit their assignments by the deadline.
With the storm approaching, taking shelter became imperative.

Related Terms

  • antonym: optional - not required or mandatory
  • synonym: urgent - requiring immediate action or attention
