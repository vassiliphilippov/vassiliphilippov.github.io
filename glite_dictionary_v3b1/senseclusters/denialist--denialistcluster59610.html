denialist

noun
person who rejects facts
8.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈnaɪəlɪst / (AmE), / dɪˈnaɪəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who refuses to accept established facts, truth, or scientific consensus, especially in science or history
