burr

verb
remove sharp edges from metal, etc.
7.6 7.3
Pronunciation: /bɜːr/ (AmE), /bɜː/ (BrE)
to remove burrs (sharp edges or roughness) from an object, especially metal, usually after cutting or drilling

Examples

To burr sharp edges
Before painting the frame, make sure to burr all the cut edges thoroughly.
It's important to burr the metal after drilling to prevent injuries.
The machinist carefully burred each hole to ensure a smooth finish.
