ulcerative

adjective
relating to or marked by ulcers
Pronunciation: / ˈʌlsɚˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈʌlsərətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by ulcers or ulceration

Examples

Ulcerative lesions

Research continues into better treatments for ulcerative diseases affecting the digestive tract.
