eat one's own dog food

verb
use one's own product or service
Pronunciation: / it wʌnz oʊn dɔɡ fuːd / (AmE), / iːt wʌnz əʊn dɒɡ fuːd / (BrE)
(of a company or its employees) to use its own products or services to demonstrate their quality and gain better insight into them.

Examples

To eat one's own dog food regularly

If you truly believe in your solution, you should eat your own dog food every day.
The CEO urged his team to eat their own dog food and test the new app thoroughly.
