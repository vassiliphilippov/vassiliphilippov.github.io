casein

noun
water-based paint made from milk protein
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪsiɪn / (AmE), / ˈkeɪsiːn / (BrE)
a water-based paint made from a milk protein, used as an artist's medium or binder

Examples

Casein paint for art
After mixing the pigment with casein, she applied the paint to the wood panel.
Many artists prefer casein paints because they combine the traits of watercolor and oil paint.
The mural was painted using casein for its quick-drying properties and matte finish.
