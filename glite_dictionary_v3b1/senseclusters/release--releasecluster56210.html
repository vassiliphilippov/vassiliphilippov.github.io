release

noun
the act of expressing emotions or creativity
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlis / (AmE), / rɪˈliːs / (BrE)
an activity or act that allows creative energy or emotions to be freely expressed

Examples

A release of stress
He found physical exercise to be an effective release for his frustration.
Painting was a form of release for her after a stressful day.
The music provided a much-needed emotional release for the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outlet - a means of expressing emotions or creative energy
  • synonym: vent - an outlet or means of release for emotions
