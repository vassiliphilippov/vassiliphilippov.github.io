sundown

noun
time when sun sets
1.2 2.17/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆsÊŒnËŒdaÊŠn / (AmE), / ËˆsÊŒn.daÊŠn / (BrE)
the time in the evening when the sun disappears below the horizon

Examples

At sundown time
At sundown, the sky was filled with beautiful shades of orange and red.
Farmers often finish their work at sundown when the fields grow dark.
We should try to get home before sundown to avoid getting lost in the dark.
