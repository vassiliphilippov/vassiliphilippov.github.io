wildwood

noun
untamed forest
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪldˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldˌwʊd / (BrE)
a natural, uncultivated forest, especially one that is dense and untouched by humans

Examples

The wildwood forest
The children imagined magical creatures living deep in the wildwood behind their house.
Legends say the wildwood is home to ancient spirits and hidden treasures.
After hours of hiking, they finally emerged from the dense wildwood into a sunny clearing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forest - a large area covered chiefly with trees and undergrowth
  • synonym: wilderness - an area of land that has not been changed by people
