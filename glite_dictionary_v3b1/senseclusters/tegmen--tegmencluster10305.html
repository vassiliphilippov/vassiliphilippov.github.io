tegmen

noun
bony roof of cavity
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛɡmən / (AmE), / ˈtɛɡmən / (BrE)
a thin plate of bone forming the roof of a body cavity, especially the bony roof of the middle ear

Examples

Ear tegmen
The surgeon carefully examined the tegmen to check for any signs of damage.
A fracture in the tegmen can lead to complications in the middle ear.
During the procedure, the doctor noted that the tegmen was unusually thin.
