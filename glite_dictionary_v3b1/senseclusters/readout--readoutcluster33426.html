readout

noun
device or display showing electronic data
6.2 0.65/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàriÀêda ät / (AmE), / ÀàriÀêda ät / (BrE)
an electronic device or screen that displays information, especially data or measurements from an instrument

Examples

A readout screen
Engineers waited anxiously for the first results to appear on the readout.
She glanced at the readout on the monitor to check the patient's vital signs.
The digital readout showed that the temperature had reached 100 degrees.
