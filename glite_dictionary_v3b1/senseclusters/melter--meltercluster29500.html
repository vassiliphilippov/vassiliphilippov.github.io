melter

noun
person who melts substances (metal, wax, etc.)
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛltər / (AmE), / ˈmɛltə / (BrE)
a worker who melts substances such as metal or wax, typically as part of an industrial or manufacturing process.

Examples

Skilled melter
As a skilled melter, she was responsible for producing high-quality molten wax for the candles.
Several melters worked night shifts in the factory to keep up with demand.
The melter carefully monitored the temperature in the furnace to ensure the metal liquefied evenly.

Related Terms
