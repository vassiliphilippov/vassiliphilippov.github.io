on the wing

adverb
in flight; flying through the air
9.5
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə wɪŋ / (AmE), / ɒn ðə wɪŋ / (BrE)
while flying or in flight (especially of birds or insects)

Examples

On the wing flying
Swallows are agile hunters, catching insects on the wing at remarkable speed.
The photographer captured a butterfly on the wing, its wings blurred by motion.
We watched a flock of geese on the wing as they migrated south for the winter.
Migrating geese are most often seen on the wing at dawn and dusk.
The photographer captured a stunning image of swans on the wing over the lake.
We spotted dozens of butterflies on the wing in the summer meadow.
