pancake turner

noun
kitchen tool for flipping pancakes
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpænˌkeɪk ˈtɜrnɚ / (AmE), / ˈpæn.keɪk ˈtɜː.nə / (BrE)
A flat kitchen utensil with a broad blade used for turning or lifting pancakes and other foods while cooking.

Examples

A pancake turner tool
He reached for the pancake turner to serve the golden brown pancakes.
She used a pancake turner to flip the hotcakes without breaking them.
The pancake turner was resting on the edge of the frying pan after breakfast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spatula - a broad, flat, blunt blade used for mixing or turning foods
