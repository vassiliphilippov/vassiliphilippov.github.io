Toraja

proper noun
region in Indonesia
Pronunciation: / toʊˈrɑːdʒə / (AmE), / tɒˈrɑːdʒə / (BrE)
a mountainous region and cultural area in South Sulawesi, Indonesia, known for its unique traditions and the Toraja people

Examples

Toraja region
Many tourists visit Toraja to experience its traditional funeral ceremonies and unique architecture.
The people of Toraja are known for their elaborate wooden houses and rich cultural heritage.
During their trip to Indonesia, they spent several days exploring the highlands of Toraja.
