daube

noun
French beef stew
Pronunciation: / doʊb / (AmE), / dəʊb / (BrE)
a French stew made with beef braised slowly in wine with vegetables and herbs

Examples

A beef daube
For the special occasion, the chef served a rich daube with red wine and fresh herbs.
She learned to make daube while living in the south of France.
The aroma of simmering daube filled the kitchen on a cold winter evening.
