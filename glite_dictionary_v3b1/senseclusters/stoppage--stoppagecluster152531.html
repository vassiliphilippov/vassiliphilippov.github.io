stoppage

noun
obstruction in a pipe or tube
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːpɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈstɒpɪdʒ / (BrE)
an obstruction that prevents the normal flow of liquid or gas in a pipe or tube

Examples

Pipe stoppage
A plumber found a stoppage deep within the main sewer pipe.
Heavy rainfall sometimes leads to a stoppage in old drainage systems.
The stoppage in the sink was caused by grease buildup.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blockage - something that blocks a pipe or tube
  • synonym: obstruction - something that obstructs or blocks passage
