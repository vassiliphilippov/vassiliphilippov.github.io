gunnysack

noun
a coarse burlap bag for storage or transport
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌniˌsæk / (AmE), / ˈɡʌniˌsæk / (BrE)
a large bag made of coarse burlap, typically used for transporting or storing goods such as grain or potatoes

Examples

A gunnysack bag
She carried her belongings in an old gunnysack as she walked down the road.
The children made a game out of racing while standing in gunnysacks.
The farmer stored the potatoes in a gunnysack in the barn.
