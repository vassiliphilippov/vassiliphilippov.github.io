connective

noun
a word that joins parts of sentences
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛktɪv / (AmE), / kəˈnɛktɪv / (BrE)
a word, especially a conjunction or other function word, used to link words, phrases, clauses, or sentences

Examples

The word connective
In the sentence "She likes tea and coffee," the word "and" acts as a connective.
The teacher explained that "because" is a connective that introduces a reason.
Writers often rely on connective words like "however" or "therefore" to improve coherence.

  • synonym: conjunction - a word used to connect clauses or sentences
