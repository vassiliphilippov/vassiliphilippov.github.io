come a cropper

verb
fail or suffer a sudden mishap
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌm ə ˈkrɑːpər / (AmE), / ˈkʌm ə ˈkrɒpə / (BrE)
to fail badly or suffer a sudden mishap, often unexpectedly

Examples

To come a cropper badly
He worked hard but still came a cropper in the final exam.
Janet came a cropper when she tried skiing for the first time.
Several new companies came a cropper during the economic downturn.
