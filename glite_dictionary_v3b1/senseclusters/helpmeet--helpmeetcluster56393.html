helpmeet

noun
helpful partner or spouse
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlpˌmit / (AmE), / ˈhɛlpˌmiːt / (BrE)
a person who is a helpful and supportive partner, especially a spouse.

Examples

A helpmeet partner
He regarded his assistant not just as an employee, but as a trusted helpmeet.
She was a devoted helpmeet to her husband throughout his career.
The novel describes the wife as an ideal helpmeet and confidant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partner - person who takes part in an activity or relationship with another
  • synonym: spouse - a husband or wife
