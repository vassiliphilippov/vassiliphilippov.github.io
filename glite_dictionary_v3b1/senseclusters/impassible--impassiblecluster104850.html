impassible

adjective
emotionless; expressionless
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpæsəbəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɑːsɪbəl / (BrE)
showing no emotion or feeling; not affected by what happens; expressionless

Examples

Impassible face
Despite the shocking news, his impassible expression did not change.
The judge remained impassible throughout the entire trial, never revealing her thoughts.
She kept an impassible face even when everyone else was laughing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotional - showing strong feelings
  • synonym: impassive - not showing emotion or feeling
