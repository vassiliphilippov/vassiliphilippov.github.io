sinogram

noun
CT scan data graph
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnəɡræm / (BrE)
a graphical representation of raw data collected during a computed tomography (CT) scan, used to reconstruct images

Examples

CT sinogram image
The radiologist examined the sinogram before reconstructing the final CT images.
A clear sinogram is essential for accurate diagnosis in computed tomography procedures.
Researchers developed new software to improve the quality of the sinogram in CT scans.

  • synonym: tomogram - an image produced by a tomography scan
