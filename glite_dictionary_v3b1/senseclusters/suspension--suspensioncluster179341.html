suspension

noun
the act of hanging something
4.7
Pronunciation: / səˈspɛnʃən / (AmE), / səˈspɛnʃən / (BrE)
the act of hanging something from above so that it moves freely

Examples

Suspension of a lamp
Careful suspension of the lights ensured they could sway slightly with the breeze.
The suspension of the chandelier gave the room a sophisticated atmosphere.
They admired the suspension of the painting from the tall ceiling beams.
