Brahmanism

noun
modern caste-based ideology favoring Brahmin dominance

A modern socio-political ideology or attitude that promotes or preserves Brahmin (upper-caste) dominance in cultural, political, or institutional life; used critically to describe caste-based privilege and exclusion.
