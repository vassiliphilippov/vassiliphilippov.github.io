codswallop

noun
nonsense; foolish talk
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑdzˌwɑləp / (AmE), / ˈkɒdzwɒləp / (BrE)
nonsense; language or ideas that are foolish or meaningless.

Examples

Pure codswallop
Don’t believe a word of it—it's just codswallop from start to finish.
Everything he said about the project was pure codswallop.
She dismissed the rumor as utter codswallop and refused to discuss it further.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rubbish - something that is nonsensical or worthless (informal, esp. British)
