cultigen

noun
plant bred by humans
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltɪˌɡɛn /; / ˈkʌltɪˌdʒɛn / (AmE), / ˈkʌltɪˌɡɛn /; / ˈkʌltɪdʒən / (BrE)
