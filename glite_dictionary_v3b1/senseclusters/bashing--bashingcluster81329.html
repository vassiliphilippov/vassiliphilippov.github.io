bashing

noun
violent beating
Pronunciation: / ˈbæʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbæʃɪŋ / (BrE)
an act of violently hitting or beating someone, or of severely damaging something by striking it

Examples

A violent bashing
After the heated argument, he received a severe bashing from the angry crowd.
The old car took quite a bashing during the hailstorm last night.
She was shocked by the bashing her proposal received in the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attack - an aggressive and violent action against a person or place
  • synonym: beating - an act of hitting someone repeatedly and violently
  • antonym: praise - the expression of approval or admiration
