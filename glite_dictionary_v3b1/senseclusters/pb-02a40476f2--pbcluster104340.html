Pb

noun
chemical symbol for lead (element)
6.7
Pronunciation: / pi bi / (AmE), / piː biː / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for the element lead, a soft, heavy, toxic, malleable metal that is bluish-white when freshly cut but tarnishes to dull grey
