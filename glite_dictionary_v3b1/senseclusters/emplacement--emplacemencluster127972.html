emplacement

noun
fortified position for weapon placement
9.9 7.6
Pronunciation: /ɪm.ˈpleɪs.mənt/ /; / /əm.ˈpleɪs.mənt/ (AmE), /ɪm.ˈpleɪs.mənt/ /; / /əm.ˈpleɪs.mənt/ (BrE)
a fortified or prepared position where a weapon, especially a gun or artillery piece, is placed for use in combat

Examples

Artillery emplacement
