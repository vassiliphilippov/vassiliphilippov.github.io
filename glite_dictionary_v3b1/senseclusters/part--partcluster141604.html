part

noun
portion of a whole
1.0 388/m
Pronunciation: / p…ërt / (AmE), / p…ëÀêt / (BrE)
a piece, segment, or component of something that, together with others, forms a whole (can be physical or abstract)

Examples

A part of the puzzle
Each student was responsible for a different part of the group project.
She left out an important part of the story in her summary.
The last part of the movie was the most exciting.
Everyone played a part in making the project a success.
She felt like she was missing an important part of the conversation.
The engine has several crucial parts that work together to make it run.
Each replacement part is carefully inspected for quality control.
He realized he was missing a crucial part for his model airplane.
Please order the correct part number to repair the device.

Synonyms

  • element
    3.0 115/m
    an element is a basic component named in lists or science, while a part is any piece that helps make a whole.
  • component
    3.4 91/m
    a component is a self-contained item used in machines or systems, while a part is the general word for any piece.
  • constituent
    5.6 3.00/m
    a constituent is an essential component often needed for something to work, while a part can be any member of the whole.
  • piece
    1.0 26/m
    a piece is an individual physical item, like a puzzle or tool, while a part can also be abstract or a smaller share.
  • segment
    3.0 33/m
    a segment is a clear section cut from something, such as a market or film, while a part is any piece of the whole.
  • section
    1.0 153/m
    a section is a marked division, for example in a book or room, while a part is the more general word for a piece.
  • unit
    1.1 46/m
    a unit is a single thing or measure used in a system, while a part is any piece that joins with other pieces.
  • portion
    2.1 46/m
    a portion is a share or amount served or given, often measured, while a part simply names any piece of a whole.

Related Terms

  • synonym: portion - a part or share of something larger
  • synonym: section - a distinct part that can be separated from something
  • antonym: whole - something that is complete and not divided into parts
