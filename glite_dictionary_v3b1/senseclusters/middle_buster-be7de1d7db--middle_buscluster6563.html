A type of moldboard plow with two moldboards, used to move soil to either side of a central furrow, often for creating planting rows or harvesting root crops.

Examples

A middle buster plow
A middle buster is ideal for breaking open the soil and making wide furrows for irrigation.
He attached the middle buster to his tractor to dig up sweet potatoes in the fall.
The farmer used a middle buster to create neat rows for planting potatoes.
