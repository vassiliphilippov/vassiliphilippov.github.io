swing

noun
square dance move involving two dancers
1.0
Pronunciation: / swɪŋ / (AmE), / swɪŋ / (BrE)
A square dance move in which a pair of dancers join hands and revolve around a pivot point between them.

Examples

A square dance swing
During the square dance, each couple performed a swing before moving to the next position.
She enjoyed the lively swing as they spun gracefully around the floor.
The caller instructed us to do a swing with our partners.
