metroscope

noun
uterus examination tool
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtrəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrəˌskəʊp / (BrE)
a medical instrument used to examine the inside of the uterus

Examples

A metroscope device
The gynecologist inserted a metroscope to check for abnormalities in the uterus.
A metroscope allows doctors to view the uterine lining during a medical examination.
After experiencing unusual symptoms, she underwent a procedure using a metroscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hysteroscope - an instrument for inspecting the uterine cavity
