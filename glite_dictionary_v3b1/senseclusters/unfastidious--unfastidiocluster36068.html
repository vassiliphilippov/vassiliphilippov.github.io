unfastidious

adjective
not attentive to detail or cleanliness
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.fæˈstɪd.i.əs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.fæˈstɪd.i.əs / (BrE)
not picky, not concerned with details, cleanliness, or being demanding
