skint

adjective
having no money; broke (British slang)
9.9
Pronunciation: / skɪnt / (AmE), / skɪnt / (BrE)
Having little or no money; broke.

Examples

To be skint
After losing his job, he was so skint that he had to borrow money from friends.
I can't come out tonight—I'm completely skint until payday.
She spent all her savings at the festival and returned utterly skint.
