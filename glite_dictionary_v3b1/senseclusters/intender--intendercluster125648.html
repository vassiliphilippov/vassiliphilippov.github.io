intender

noun
person who plans
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛndər / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛndə / (BrE)
a person who intends or plans to do something

Examples

An intender to apply
Each intender must submit their application by Friday.
The committee welcomed every intender who expressed interest in joining the project.
As an intender, she carefully outlined her goals before taking action.

Related Terms

  • antonym: doer - a person who actively does things rather than just planning
  • synonym: planner - a person who makes plans
