groin

noun
area between abdomen and thigh
Pronunciation: / ɡrɔɪn / (AmE), / ɡrɔɪn / (BrE)
the area between the abdomen and the inner part of the thigh, often including the crease at their junction and the external genitals

Examples

Pulled groin muscle
He pulled a muscle in his groin while playing football.
She complained of pain in her groin after running the marathon.
The doctor examined the swollen area in his groin for signs of infection.
