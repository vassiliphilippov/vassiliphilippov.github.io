senselessness

noun
the quality of having no meaning or purpose
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsləsnɪs / (BrE)
the state of having no meaning, sense, or purpose; the quality of lacking sense or reason

Examples

The senselessness of violence
Many critics commented on the senselessness of the film's plot.
She was overwhelmed by the senselessness of the tragedy, unable to find any reason behind it.
The novel explores the senselessness of war through the eyes of its main character.

  • synonym: pointlessness - lack of purpose or point
  • antonym: significance - the quality of having meaning or importance
