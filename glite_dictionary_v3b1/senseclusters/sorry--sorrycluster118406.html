sorry

adjective
feeling regret or sorrow
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːri / (AmE), / ˈsɒri / (BrE)
feeling or showing regret or sorrow for something that has happened or been done, especially a mistake or wrong

Examples

I am sorry for that
He was sorry for the harsh words he had spoken.
I'm truly sorry that I missed your call last night.
She looked sorry as she apologized for the mistake.

Related Terms

  • antonym: proud - feeling deep pleasure or satisfaction as a contrast to feeling sorry
  • synonym: regretful - feeling regret or sorrow about something past
  • synonym: remorseful - feeling deep regret or guilt for a wrong committed
