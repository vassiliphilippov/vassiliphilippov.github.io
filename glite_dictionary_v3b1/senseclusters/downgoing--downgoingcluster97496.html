downgoing

adjective
falling pitch
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
having a falling pitch or characterized by a downward movement in voice intonation, especially in phonetics

Examples

Downgoing intonation
The teacher explained that a downgoing intonation is common in statements.
In some languages, a downgoing tone can change the meaning of a word.
Her voice had a clear downgoing pattern at the end of each sentence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: falling - having a pitch that moves downward
  • antonym: rising - having a pitch that moves upward
