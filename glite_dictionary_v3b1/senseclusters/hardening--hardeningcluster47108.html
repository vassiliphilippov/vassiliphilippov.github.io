Synonyms

  • sclerosis
    8.3 2.26/m
    sclerosis is a medical term for tissue that becomes abnormally hard or thick, while hardening is the general name for this change.
  • induration
    9.9 0.07/m

  • sclerotization
    9.9 0.00/m

  • thickening
