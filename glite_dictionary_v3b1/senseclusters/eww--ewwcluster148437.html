eww

verb
to express disgust
9.6
Pronunciation: / uː / (AmE), / juː / (BrE)
to say 'eww' or make a similar sound to show that something is disgusting or unpleasant

Examples

To eww at bugs
The kids ewwed loudly when they found a bug in their soup.
She couldn't help but eww at the sight of the moldy bread.
Whenever he sees something gross, he always ewws and turns away.
