spud

noun
tool for digging out roots and weeds
Pronunciation: / spʌd / (AmE), / spʌd / (BrE)
a sharp, narrow-bladed tool used for digging out roots and weeds, especially in gardening

Examples

Garden spud tool
After sharpening his trusty spud, Jim tackled the thick roots under the hedge.
He used a spud to remove the stubborn dandelion from the garden.
The gardener reached for her spud to quickly dig up the weeds.
