silvervine

noun
ornamental Asian vine with silver-marked leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvərˌvaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəˌvaɪn / (BrE)
An ornamental vine of eastern Asia with yellow edible fruit and leaves marked with silver-white patterns; known for its effect on cats similar to catnip.

Examples

Ripe silvervine fruit
He used ripe silvervine fruit in a traditional dish from eastern Asia.
Researchers studied the effects of silvervine leaves on feline behavior.
The silvervine in her garden attracted both visitors and neighborhood cats.
