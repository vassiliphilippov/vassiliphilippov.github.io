postprandial

adjective
after a meal
8.9
Pronunciation: / poʊstˈprændiəl / (AmE), / pəʊstˈprændɪəl / (BrE)
Occurring or done after a meal, especially dinner.

Examples

A postprandial walk
He enjoyed a postprandial stroll through the garden to aid digestion.
Some people like to relax with a postprandial coffee after dinner.
The doctor ordered a postprandial blood glucose test after the patient finished eating.
