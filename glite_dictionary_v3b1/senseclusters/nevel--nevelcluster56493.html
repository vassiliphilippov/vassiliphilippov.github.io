nevel

noun
ancient Hebrew instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪvɛl / (AmE), / ˈniːvɛl / (BrE)
an ancient Hebrew stringed instrument, similar to a harp or psaltery, mentioned in the Bible

Examples

Ancient nevel music
The musicians played the nevel during the temple ceremony in ancient Jerusalem.
A beautifully crafted nevel was discovered among the archaeological ruins.
The psalms mention the nevel as an instrument of praise.
