big band

noun
large jazz or dance music band
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ bænd / (AmE), / bɪɡ band / (BrE)
a large group of musicians that plays jazz or dance music, typically featuring sections of brass, woodwind, and rhythm instruments, with improvised solos by lead players

Examples

A big band jazz
Many famous jazz musicians got their start in a big band before going solo.
She enjoys dancing to live music, especially when a big band is playing.
The big band performed classics from the swing era at the concert hall.
