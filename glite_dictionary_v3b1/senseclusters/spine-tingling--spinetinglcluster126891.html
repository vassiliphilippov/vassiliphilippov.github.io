spine-tingling

adjective
causing excitement or fear
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪn ˌtɪŋɡlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪn ˌtɪŋɡlɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

There was a spine-tingling silence just before the storm hit.
