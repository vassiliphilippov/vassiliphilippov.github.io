potentiate

verb
make something more effective; intensify effect
Pronunciation: / poʊˈtɛnʃiˌeɪt / (AmE), / pəˈtɛnʃɪeɪt / (BrE)
to increase the power, effectiveness, or effect of something, especially a drug or biochemical process

Examples

To potentiate drug effect
Combining these chemicals will potentiate the desired physiological response.
Taking caffeine alongside certain drugs may potentiate their stimulating effects.
The new medication can potentiate the effects of the existing treatment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enhance - to improve or intensify
