little grebe

noun
a small European and Asian water bird
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬.əl ɡrib / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl ɡriːb / (BrE)
a small water bird of the grebe family, common in Europe and Asia, with a short neck and brownish plumage (Tachybaptus ruficollis)

Examples

A pair of little grebes
She photographed a pair of little grebes building their floating nest on the pond.
The little grebe is known for its distinctive whinnying call during the breeding season.
We spotted a little grebe diving for food near the riverbank this morning.
