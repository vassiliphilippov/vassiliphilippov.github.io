toot

noun
Fediverse microblog post
Pronunciation: / tuːt / (AmE), / tuːt / (BrE)
(informal, on the Fediverse) a short message or microblog post published on Mastodon or similar decentralized social networks.

Examples

A Mastodon toot
She shared a sketch and wrote about it in a toot for her followers.
After the conference, he posted a short toot summarizing the main ideas.
New users often explain the platform by comparing a toot to a tweet or status update.

Related Terms

  • synonym: post - an item of content published on a blog or social network
  • synonym: status update - a brief message sharing what someone is thinking or doing on social media
  • synonym: tweet - a short message posted on Twitter / X; similar in function to a toot
