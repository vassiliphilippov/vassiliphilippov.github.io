Agnus Dei

noun
lamb symbol of Christ
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡnəs ˈdeɪ.i / (AmE), / ˌaɡnəs ˈdeɪ.i / (BrE)
A representation of a lamb, often with a halo and flag, used as a symbol of Jesus Christ in Christian art and worship.

Examples

Agnus Dei in art
In medieval paintings, the Agnus Dei is often shown holding a banner.
The altar was adorned with an Agnus Dei carved in white marble.
The choir sang a beautiful version of the Agnus Dei during the service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Lamb of God - another name for Agnus Dei, referring to Christ in Christian theology
