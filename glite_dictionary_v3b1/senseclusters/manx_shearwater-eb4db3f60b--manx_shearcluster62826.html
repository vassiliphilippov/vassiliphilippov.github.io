Manx shearwater

noun
Atlantic black-and-white seabird
8.2
Pronunciation: / mæŋks ˈʃɛrˌwɔtɚ / (AmE), / mæŋks ˈʃɪəˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
a small black-and-white seabird of the petrel family, common in the northeastern Atlantic, known for its long migratory flights
