    4.6 0.01/m
    An incident disclosing confidential information to unauthorized recipients, often unintentional
    vs breach: an incident in which confidential or sensitive information is disclosed to unauthorized parties, often unintentionally or due to a security failure
  • compromise
    6.5 0.5/m
    Involves unauthorized access to systems, accounts, or records, often altering integrity
    vs breach: (in information security) an incident in which systems, accounts, or data are accessed, exposed, or altered without authorization, reducing confidentiality or integrity.
  • leakage
    4.6 0.1/m
    Refers to unauthorized or accidental disclosure of confidential information in digital contexts
    vs breach: the unauthorized or accidental disclosure of confidential or private information, especially in digital, cybersecurity, or data contexts.
  • leak
    4.8 8/m
    Means making secret information publicly known, often lacking official approval
