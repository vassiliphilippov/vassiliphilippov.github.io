Euplectella

noun
genus of glass sponges
Pronunciation: / juːˈplɛktələ / (AmE), / juːˈplɛktələ / (BrE)
A genus of glass sponges in the class Hyalospongiae, known for their delicate, lattice-like silica skeletons.
