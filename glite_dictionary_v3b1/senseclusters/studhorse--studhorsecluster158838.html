studhorse

noun
male horse kept for breeding
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌdˌhɔrs / (AmE), / ˈstʌdˌhɔːs / (BrE)
An adult male horse kept specifically for breeding purposes.

Examples

A studhorse for breeding
Keeping a healthy studhorse requires special care and training.
The breeder selected his best studhorse for the upcoming mating season.
They traveled to another farm to use a renowned studhorse with an excellent pedigree.
