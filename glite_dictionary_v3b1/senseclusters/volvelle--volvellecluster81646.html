volvelle

noun
circular chart with rotating parts
Pronunciation: / vɑlˈvɛl / (AmE), / vɒlˈvɛl / (BrE)
A circular device with rotating parts, often made of paper, used to calculate or display information by aligning different sections.

Examples

A volvelle device
By turning the volvelle, readers could quickly find the date of Easter in old almanacs.
She constructed a volvelle for her science project to show the lunar phases.
The museum displayed an ancient volvelle used by astronomers to chart the stars.
