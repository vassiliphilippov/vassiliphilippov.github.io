mire

verb
to soil with mud or muck
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪə / (BrE)
to cover or soil with mud, dirt, or wet, sticky substance

Examples

To mire shoes
She accidentally mired her dress when she slipped on the muddy path.
Splashing water from the rainstorm mired his pants and shoes before he made it home.
The truck became stuck after the wheels were mired in the swampy field.
