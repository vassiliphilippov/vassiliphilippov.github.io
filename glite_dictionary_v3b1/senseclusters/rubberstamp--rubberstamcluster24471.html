rubberstamp

verb
mark officially with a rubber stamp
4.5 3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌb.ər.stæmp/ (AmE), /ˈrʌb.ə.stæmp/ (BrE)
to mark a document with a rubber stamp, usually as a form of official approval or confirmation

Examples

To rubberstamp a document
Every proposal was rubberstamped without any real discussion.
She spent the morning rubberstamping attendance forms for her supervisor.
The manager must rubberstamp each invoice before payment is processed.
