artillery fire

noun
military gunfire from artillery weapons
3.6 5.5
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.ˈtɪl.ə.riː ˈfaɪ.ər/ /; / /ɑːr.ˈtɪl.ə.riː faɪr/ (AmE), /ɑː.ˈtɪl.ə.riː ˈfaɪ.ə/ (BrE)

Examples
