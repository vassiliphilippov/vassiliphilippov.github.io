desert willow

noun
flowering tree of southwestern North America
9.9 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈde.zərt ˈwɪl.oʊ/ /; / /ˈdɪz.ərt ˈwɪl.oʊ/ (AmE), /ˈde.zət ˈwɪl.əʊ/ /; / /ˈdɪz.ət ˈwɪl.əʊ/ (BrE)
a small shrubby tree of dry regions in southwestern North America, with long narrow leaves, showy purplish flowers, and elongated seed pods; not a true willow

A desert willow tree
