sprong

noun
jump or leap
9.9
Pronunciation: / sprɒŋ / (AmE), / sprɒŋ / (BrE)
an act of jumping or leaping, especially in an energetic or sudden way

Examples

A quick sprong
With a quick sprong, the cat cleared the fence and disappeared into the garden.
The athlete made a powerful sprong over the stream during the competition.
He startled everyone with a sudden sprong onto the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jump - an act of pushing oneself off a surface and into the air
  • synonym: leap - a long or high jump
