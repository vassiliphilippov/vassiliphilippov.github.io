apparat

noun
political organization
Pronunciation: / ˈæpəˌræt / (AmE), / ˈæpəˌræt / (BrE)
a powerful and often secretive administrative or political organization, especially within a communist or authoritarian system

Examples

Party apparat
The party's apparat controlled every aspect of daily life.
He rose quickly through the ranks of the apparat thanks to his loyalty.
Critics accused the apparat of suppressing dissent and limiting freedom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bureaucracy - a system of government or management with many complicated rules and officials
  • synonym: establishment - the group in power within a society or organization
