Yemeni fils

noun
Yemeni currency subunit
Pronunciation: / jəˈmɛni fɪls / (AmE), / jɛˈmiːni fɪls / (BrE)
a monetary subunit of Yemen, equal to one hundredth of a Yemeni rial

Examples

A Yemeni fils coin
He collected coins from around the world, including a rare Yemeni fils.
One Yemeni fils is almost worthless compared to the Yemeni rial.
The price was listed as fifty Yemeni fils for a loaf of bread.
