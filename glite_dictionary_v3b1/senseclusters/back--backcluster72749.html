Synonyms

  • rear
    1.0 11/m
    rear names the part toward the back in neutral or formal use, while back is the common everyday word.
  • posterior
    6.1 2.29/m
    posterior is a formal or anatomical term for the back area, while back is casual and used in everyday speech.
  • rearward
    7.0 0.20/m
    rearward means toward the back or moving in that direction, while back simply describes the position at the rear.
  • rearmost
    5.9 0.06/m
    rearmost sits at the very back as the furthest point, while back can mean any rear part not necessarily the last.
  • backmost
    6.0 0.01/m
    backmost stands at the farthest rear position, often informal or regional, while back simply refers to the rear area.
  • hindmost
    7.7 0.06/m
    hindmost is mainly used for animals or groups to show the very back, whereas back is more general and common.
