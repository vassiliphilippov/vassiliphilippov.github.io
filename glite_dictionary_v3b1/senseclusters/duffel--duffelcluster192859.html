duffel

noun
large cylindrical cloth bag
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌfəl / (AmE), / ˈdʌfəl /; / ˈdʌf(ə)l / (BrE)
a large cylindrical bag made of sturdy cloth, used for carrying personal belongings, often when traveling or camping

Examples

A duffel bag
After the gym, she carried her duffel back to her car.
He packed all his clothes and gear into a sturdy duffel for the camping trip.
The soldier slung his duffel over his shoulder as he boarded the bus.
He packed all his clothes into a blue duffle before leaving for college.
She tossed her duffle onto the bed as soon as she arrived at the hotel.
The soldiers carried their gear in worn duffles during the march.
