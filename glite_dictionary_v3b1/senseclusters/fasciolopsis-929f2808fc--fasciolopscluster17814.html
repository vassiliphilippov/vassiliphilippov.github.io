Fasciolopsis

noun
genus of parasitic flatworms
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfæʃiəˈlɑpsɪs /; / fæsiəˈlɑpsɪs / (AmE), / ˌfasɪəˈlɒpsɪs /; / fæsiəˈlɒpsɪs / (BrE)
a genus of parasitic flatworms (trematodes) in the family Fasciolidae, including Fasciolopsis buski, which infects the intestines of mammals (especially humans and pigs)
