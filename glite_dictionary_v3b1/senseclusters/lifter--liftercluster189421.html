lifter

noun
a thief who steals from stores
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪftər / (AmE), / ˈlɪftə / (BrE)
a thief who steals goods from a store, typically by concealing them

Examples

A store lifter
She was accused of being a lifter after the missing merchandise was found in her bag.
Store owners have become more vigilant in identifying potential lifters.
The security guard caught the lifter trying to leave the store with unpaid items.
