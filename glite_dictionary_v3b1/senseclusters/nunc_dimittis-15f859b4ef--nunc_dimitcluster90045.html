Nunc dimittis

noun
Christian canticle (Song of Simeon)
9.9
Pronunciation: / nʌŋk dɪˈmɪtɪs / (AmE), / nʌŋk dɪˈmɪtɪs / (BrE)
a canticle beginning with the words 'Nunc dimittis,' also known as the Song of Simeon, used in Christian liturgy and based on Luke 2:29–32.

Examples

The Nunc dimittis canticle
At evening prayer, the choir sang the Nunc dimittis with great solemnity.
Many composers have set the Nunc dimittis to music over the centuries.
The words of the Nunc dimittis express gratitude and peaceful acceptance.
