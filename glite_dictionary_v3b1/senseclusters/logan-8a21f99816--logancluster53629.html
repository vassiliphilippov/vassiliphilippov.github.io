Logan

proper noun
highest mountain in Canada
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊɡən / (AmE), / ˈləʊɡən / (BrE)
a mountain peak in the St. Elias Range in southwestern Yukon Territory, Canada; at 5,959 meters (19,550 feet), the highest mountain in Canada.

Examples

Mount Logan peak
Mount Logan attracts climbers from around the world due to its extreme height and remoteness.
Temperatures on Logan can drop drastically even in summer months.
The snow-capped peak of Logan was clearly visible from the airplane window.
