smokelike

adjective
resembling smoke
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or similar to smoke in appearance or quality

Examples

A smokelike haze
A smokelike haze hung over the city after the fireworks display.
The artist used soft, smokelike strokes to create a mysterious atmosphere in the painting.
Early morning fog gave the valley a smokelike appearance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - easy to see through; not cloudy or smoky
  • synonym: hazy - unclear or filled with haze
  • synonym: misty - full of or resembling mist
