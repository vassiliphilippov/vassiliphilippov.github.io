brand

verb
create and manage public identity
3.7
Pronunciation: /brænd/ (AmE), /brænd/ (BrE)
to create, shape, and promote a distinctive public identity or image for a person, product, or company, especially through marketing or social media.

Examples

To brand a company
Entrepreneurs increasingly brand themselves online, crafting a distinctive image to attract clients.
The company plans to brand its new line as eco-friendly to reach younger buyers.
She spent months branding her profile to appear more professional to recruiters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: market - to promote or sell a product or service to customers
  • synonym: promote - to publicize or advance a product, person, or idea to increase its success or reputation
