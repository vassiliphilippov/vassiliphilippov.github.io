petulantly

adverb
in an irritable or sulky manner
6.7 6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛtʃələntli / (AmE), / ˈpɛtjʊləntli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows impatience, irritability, or childlike sulkiness.

Examples

To speak petulantly
He answered petulantly, obviously upset by the question.
She spoke petulantly when her ideas were ignored.
The child petulantly refused to eat his vegetables at dinner.
