BOGO

noun
buy-one-get-one offer
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊɡəʊ / (BrE)
a sales promotion in which buying one item allows you to get another item free or at a discount

Examples

BOGO offer
The supermarket is offering a BOGO deal on breakfast cereals this week.
During the holiday sale, many customers took advantage of the BOGO promotion on winter coats.
If you buy one pizza, the BOGO offer lets you get a second one free.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discount - a reduction in the usual price of something
  • synonym: two-for-one - a deal where you get two items for the price of one
