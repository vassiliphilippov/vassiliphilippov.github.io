Zinnia grandiflora

noun
a species of yellow-flowered zinnia
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪni.ə ˌɡrændəˈflɔrə / (AmE), / ˈzɪni.ə ˌɡrændɪˈflɔːrə / (BrE)
A species of subshrub with short leafy stems and numerous small, nearly round yellow-orange flower heads, native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.
