freakazoid

noun
eccentric person
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːkəˌzɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈfriːkəzɔɪd / (BrE)
a person who behaves in a very strange, eccentric, or unconventional way

Examples

A freakazoid person
Everyone at the party thought he was a real freakazoid because of his wild dancing.
My brother acts like a total freakazoid when he gets excited about his hobbies.
Some people called her a freakazoid, but she was just being herself.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normal - a person who behaves in a typical or expected way
  • synonym: oddball - a person who behaves in an unusual or peculiar way
  • synonym: weirdo - a person who acts in a strange or unusual way
