sturgeon

noun
large fish prized for flesh and roe
7.1 1.17/m
Pronunciation: / Ààst…ùÀêd í…ôn / (AmE), / Ààst…úÀêd í…ôn / (BrE)
A large primitive fish found mainly in northern temperate regions, valued for its flesh and roe (caviar).

Examples

The sturgeon fish
