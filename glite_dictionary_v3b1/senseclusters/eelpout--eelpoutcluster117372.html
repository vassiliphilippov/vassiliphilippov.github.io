eelpout

noun
marine eellike bottom-dwelling fish
Pronunciation: / ˈiːlˌpaʊt / (AmE), / ˈiːlˌpaʊt / (BrE)
a marine fish resembling an eel, typically found dwelling on the sea bottom in northern seas

Examples

Marine eelpout fish
Biologists study the eelpout to understand how marine species adapt to cold environments.
Fishermen in Arctic regions often catch eelpout during the winter months.
The eelpout is known for its elongated body and habit of lurking on the ocean floor.
