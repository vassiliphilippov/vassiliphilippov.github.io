bastard

noun
illegitimate child (offensive)
Pronunciation: / ˈbæstərd / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstəd / (BrE)
a person born to parents who are not married to each other (now considered offensive); illegitimate child

Examples

A bastard child
He discovered he was a bastard, born before his parents were married.
In medieval times, being called a bastard could affect one's rights and inheritance.
The term bastard is now considered offensive and should be avoided.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illegitimate - born to parents not married to each other
  • antonym: legitimate - born to married parents; lawful
