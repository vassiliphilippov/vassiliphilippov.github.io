thong

noun
narrow strip of leather for tying or lashing
Pronunciation: / θɔŋ / (AmE), / θɒŋ / (BrE)
a narrow strip of leather, used for fastening, tying, or as part of a whip

Examples

Leather thong strap
Ancient sandals were often fastened with a simple thong.
She tied the bundle tightly with a leather thong.
The craftsman used a thong to secure the handle to the axe head.
A braided thong gives the whip both strength and flexibility.
He replaced the worn thong to restore the whip's effectiveness.
The handle and the thong of the whip were both finely crafted.
