tot

noun
small potato piece
3.5
Pronunciation: / tɑt / (AmE), / tɒt / (BrE)
a small piece of food, especially made from potato and shaped into a ball or cube, often served as a snack or side dish

Examples

Potato tots
She served crispy tots with ketchup for lunch.
The children enjoyed a plate of golden tots as an afternoon snack.
Would you like to add some tots to your meal instead of fries?

Related Terms

  • synonym: tater tot - a small cylinder of grated potato that is deep-fried
