ultranationalist

noun
extreme nationalist
9.3
Pronunciation: /ʌl.trə.ˈnæ.ʃə.nə.lɪst/ (AmE), /ʌl.trə.ˈnæ.ʃə.nə.lɪst/ (BrE)
a person who has extreme or fanatical loyalty to their own nation, often rejecting or opposing other countries or foreign influence

Examples

An ultranationalist leader
The ultranationalist called for strict policies to protect the country's borders.
Many feared that the rise of ultranationalists would lead to increased tensions with neighboring nations.
The party's ultranationalist views were criticized by international organizations.
The ultranationalist party gained support by promising to put the country first in all decisions.
Critics accused the leader of being an ultranationalist who refused to work with neighboring nations.
Protesters marched against the ultranationalist movement, calling for more openness to the world.

  • degree lower: nationalist - a person who strongly supports their own nation
