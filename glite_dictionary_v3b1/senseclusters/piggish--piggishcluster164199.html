piggish

adjective
like a pig; gluttonous or greedy
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡɪʃ / (BrE)
(of a person or their behavior) resembling a pig, especially in being gluttonous, greedy, or coarse.

Examples

Piggish behavior
After a day of piggish behavior, he felt uncomfortably full.
His piggish appetite was obvious from the way he devoured his food.
She found his piggish habits at the table quite off-putting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: porcine - relating to or resembling pigs
  • synonym: swinish - coarse, greedy, or brutish like a pig
