Examples

To abrade the surface
If you use steel wool, it might abrade the surface of the table.
Sand particles can slowly abrade rocks along the riverbank.

Synonyms

  • rub off
    3.4 0.28/m
    rub off comes away from a surface when you rub it, while abrade means the surface itself is worn by friction.
  • scrape
    1.2 4.40/m
    scrape is to scratch or remove material with a hard edge or tool, while abrade means wear a surface away by friction.
  • wear away
    3.8 0.41/m
    wear away acts slowly by water, use, or time to reduce something, while abrade stresses rubbing or scraping action.
  • rub down
    4.4 0.01/m
    rub down smooths or cleans a surface with hard rubbing, while abrade means to wear the surface away by friction.
  • grind down
    6.1 0.15/m
    grind down smooths or reduces a surface by strong abrasion or tools, while abrade can be gentler rubbing or scraping.
  • corrade
    9.9 0.00/m
