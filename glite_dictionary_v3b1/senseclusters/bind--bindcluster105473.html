bind

verb
cause constipation (of food/medicine)
6.5
Pronunciation: / baɪnd / (AmE), / baɪnd / (BrE)
(of food or medicine) to cause constipation; to make the bowels difficult to move.

Examples

Food binds the bowels
Bananas are believed by some people to bind you if eaten in large amounts.
Doctors may warn that certain painkillers can bind patients and cause digestive issues.
Some medications can bind the bowels and lead to discomfort.
