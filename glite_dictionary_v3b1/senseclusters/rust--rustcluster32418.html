rust

adjective
reddish-brown color
9.7
Pronunciation: / rʌst / (AmE), / rʌst / (BrE)
of a reddish-brown color resembling that of iron oxide (rust).

Examples

Rust jacket
He picked a rust scarf to match the autumn leaves.
She wore a rust sweater that brought out the color of her hair.
The living room was decorated in warm, rust tones and soft golds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rusty - of the color of rust; also corroded with rust (when describing objects)
