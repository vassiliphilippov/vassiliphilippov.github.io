kite tail

noun
tail attached to a kite for balance
3.3
Pronunciation: / kaɪt teɪl / (AmE), / kaɪt teɪl / (BrE)
a strip or series of strips attached to the end of a kite to provide balance and stability during flight

Examples

A kite tail strip
He made a colorful kite tail out of ribbons to decorate his homemade kite.
The added kite tail helped stabilize the kite in the strong wind.
Without a kite tail, the kite spun out of control and crashed.
