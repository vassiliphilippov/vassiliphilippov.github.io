belly flop

noun
dive where abdomen hits the water painfully
4.1 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆbÉ›li flÉ‘p / (AmE), / ËˆbÉ›li flÉ’p / (BrE)
A dive into water during which the front of the body, especially the abdomen, strikes the surface flatly and forcefully, often causing pain.

Examples

A painful belly flop

She won the contest for the loudest belly flop in the summer camp competition.
The children laughed when Tom performed a huge belly flop at the pool party.
