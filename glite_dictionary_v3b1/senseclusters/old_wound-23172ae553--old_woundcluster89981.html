old wound

noun
past emotional pain or trauma
6.0
Pronunciation: / oʊld wuːnd / (AmE), / əʊld wuːnd / (BrE)
a past emotional or psychological pain that continues to cause distress when remembered or discussed

Examples

An old wound hurts
Her words touched an old wound he thought had healed years ago.
Therapy helped her deal with the old wounds that still affected her relationships.
Whenever he talked about his childhood, it seemed to reopen an old wound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scar - lasting mark of past hurt or injury (figurative)
  • synonym: trauma - emotional or psychological injury
