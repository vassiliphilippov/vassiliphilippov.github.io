uncharacteristically

adverb
not in a typical or usual manner
5.4 5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌkærɪktəˈrɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌkærɪktəˈrɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is not typical of a person or thing; not in keeping with usual behavior or characteristics.

Examples

Speak uncharacteristically harshly
He spoke uncharacteristically harshly to his friends, unlike his usual gentle self.
She was uncharacteristically late to the meeting, which surprised her colleagues.
The weather was uncharacteristically warm for this time of year.
