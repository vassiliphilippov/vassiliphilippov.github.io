Crock Pot

noun
electric slow cooker (proprietary name)
3.5
Pronunciation: / krɑk pɑt / (AmE), / krɒk pɒt / (BrE)
A proprietary name for an electric cooker designed to cook food slowly at a relatively low, steady temperature; often used generically for any such appliance.

Examples

A Crock Pot cooker
Many recipes can be easily adapted for a Crock Pot with just a few adjustments.
She inherited her grandmother’s old Crock Pot, and it still works perfectly.
We put the stew in the Crock Pot before leaving for work so it would be ready by dinner.
