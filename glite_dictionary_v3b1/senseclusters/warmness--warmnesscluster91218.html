warmness

noun
the quality of being warm
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːrm.nəs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːm.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being warm; moderate heat.

Examples

The warmness of the room

The warmness of the sun made the afternoon pleasant for everyone in the park.
