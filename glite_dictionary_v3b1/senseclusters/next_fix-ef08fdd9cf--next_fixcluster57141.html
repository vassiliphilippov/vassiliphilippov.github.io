next fix

noun
next dose of addictive drugs
Pronunciation: / nɛkst fɪks / (AmE), / nɛkst fɪks / (BrE)
the next dose of an addictive drug that someone craves or is seeking, especially as used by drug addicts

Examples

The next fix craving
After a few hours without heroin, he grew desperate for his next fix.
Many drug addicts spend their days thinking only about getting their next fix.
She stole money from her family to pay for her next fix.
