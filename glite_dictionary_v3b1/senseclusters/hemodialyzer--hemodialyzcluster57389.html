hemodialyzer

noun
machine for blood purification by dialysis
7.1 6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːmoʊˈdaɪlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˌhiːməʊˈdaɪlaɪzə / (BrE)
a machine used in medical treatment to remove impurities and waste products from a patient's blood through dialysis, before returning the blood to the body

Examples

A hemodialyzer machine
During kidney failure, a hemodialyzer performs the essential function of removing toxins from the bloodstream.
Modern hemodialyzers are designed to operate efficiently and minimize complications.
The patient was connected to a hemodialyzer for four hours to cleanse his blood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dialyzer - a device that filters waste from the blood during dialysis
