nouveau-riche

noun
person who is newly rich
9.2 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / nuÀêÀåvo äÀàriÀê É / (AmE), / ÀånuÀêv…ô äÀàriÀê É / (BrE)
A person who has recently acquired wealth, typically one perceived as lacking the culture or refinement associated with long-established upper classes.

Examples

A nouveau-riche millionaire
After winning the lottery, he became a nouveau-riche and spent extravagantly on luxury cars.
She was determined not to behave like a stereotypical nouveau-riche despite her sudden fortune.
The old money families looked down on the nouveau-riche who recently moved into the neighborhood.

Synonyms

  • arriviste
    9.9 0.10/m
    arriviste often shows off to climb society, while nouveau-riche names someone newly rich seen as lacking class and refinement.
  • parvenu
    9.9 0.10/m
    parvenu suddenly gains wealth and social rank, while nouveau-riche is a person seen as newly rich and lacking traditional refinement.
  • upstart
    7.4 0.59/m
    upstart rises quickly and may seem arrogant, while nouveau-riche specifically refers to someone newly wealthy and lacking refinement.
  • new money
    4.8 0.64/m
    new money describes families or groups who recently became rich, while nouveau-riche names an individual seen as uncultured.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parvenu - a person of obscure origin who has gained wealth and position suddenly
