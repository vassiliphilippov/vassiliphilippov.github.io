atap

noun
roofed shelter
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːtɑːp / (AmE), / ˈɑːtæp / (BrE)
a small shelter or roofed structure, often open at the sides, providing shade or protection

Examples

River atap shelter
The villagers built an atap near the river to rest during the hot afternoons.
We enjoyed our lunch under the shade of the atap while watching the rain.
Children played games beneath the old atap at the edge of the field.

  • synonym: gazebo - a small open-sided structure in a garden or park
