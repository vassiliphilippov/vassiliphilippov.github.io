hobo

verb
to travel as a hobo
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊboʊ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊbəʊ / (BrE)
to travel or live as a hobo, often moving from place to place without a permanent home or job

Examples

To hobo across America
After losing his job, he spent a year hoboing along the west coast.
They dreamed of hoboing across the country, seeing new places every week.
In the 1930s, many people survived by hoboing from town to town in search of work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: settle - to stay in one place and establish a home
  • synonym: wander - to travel aimlessly from place to place
