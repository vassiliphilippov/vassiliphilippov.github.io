antipyretic

adjective
reducing or preventing fever
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌpaɪˈrɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌpaɪˈrɛtɪk / (BrE)
(of a drug or substance) preventing or reducing fever.

Examples

Antipyretic medicine
An antipyretic drug can help lower a patient's body temperature during an infection.
Paracetamol is commonly used as an antipyretic treatment for mild fevers.
Some herbal remedies are believed to have antipyretic properties, helping to reduce fever naturally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: febrifuge - a medicine that reduces fever
