PIR

noun
private data retrieval
9.9
Pronunciation: /pɪr/ /; / /ˈpiː.aɪ.ɑːr/ (AmE), /pɪə/ /; / /ˈpiː.aɪ.ɑː/ (BrE)

Examples

PIR protocol

Researchers are working to make PIR protocols more efficient for large-scale systems.
Using PIR, clients can access sensitive information without exposing their interests.
