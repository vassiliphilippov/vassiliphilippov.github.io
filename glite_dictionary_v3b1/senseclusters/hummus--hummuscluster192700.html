hummus

noun
Middle Eastern chickpea dip
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊməs, ˈhʌməs /; / ˈhuːməs / (AmE), / ˈhʊm.əs, ˈhʌm.əs /; / ˈhʊm.ɒs / (BrE)
a creamy Middle Eastern spread or dip made from mashed chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic
