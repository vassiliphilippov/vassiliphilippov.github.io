victualling

noun
supplying provisions
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪtəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɪtəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of supplying food and provisions, especially for ships or military forces

Examples

Victualling of the fleet
The navy assigned a team to handle the victualling of the fleet before departure.
Proper victualling was essential to ensure the soldiers had enough food during the campaign.
The port had special warehouses dedicated to the victualling of merchant ships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: provisioning - the act of supplying food and other necessities
  • synonym: supplying - the act of providing something needed
