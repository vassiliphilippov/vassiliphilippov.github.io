nuclease

noun
enzyme that breaks down nucleic acids
7.1 7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnuː.kli.eɪs / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.klɪ.eɪz / (BrE)
an enzyme that catalyzes the hydrolysis of nucleic acids by cleaving the chains of nucleotides into smaller units

Examples

Restriction nuclease enzymes are vital tools in genetic engineering.
