pease pudding

noun
savory dish of split peas and egg
Pronunciation: / piz ˈpʊd.ɪŋ / (AmE), / piːz ˈpʊd.ɪŋ / (BrE)
A savory dish made from boiled split peas that are strained and mixed with egg, often served as an accompaniment to meat.

Examples

Pease pudding dish
She served slices of ham with a generous portion of pease pudding on the side.
The recipe calls for yellow split peas to make homemade pease pudding.
Traditional pease pudding is often enjoyed with bacon in parts of northern England.

Related Terms
