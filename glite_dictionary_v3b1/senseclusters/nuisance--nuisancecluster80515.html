nuisance

noun
person or thing causing annoyance or inconvenience
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːsəns / (AmE), / ˈnjuːsəns / (BrE)
a person, thing, or situation that causes annoyance, inconvenience, or trouble

Examples

A noisy nuisance
Please don't be a nuisance and let your dog bark all night.
Some people find telemarketing calls to be an annoying nuisance.
The loud construction work next door is a real nuisance when I'm trying to study.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annoyance - something that causes irritation or mild anger
  • antonym: pleasure - a feeling of happiness or enjoyment, opposite of inconvenience or annoyance
