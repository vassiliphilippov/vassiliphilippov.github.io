Jacquard

noun
loom for weaving complex patterns
9.9 0.08/m
Pronunciation: /  í…ôÀàk…ërd / (AmE), /  íaÀàk…ëÀêd / (BrE)
a type of loom with a mechanism that controls individual warp threads, allowing the weaving of complex patterns into fabrics

Examples

A Jacquard loom
A Jacquard loom can produce detailed patterns that are impossible with simpler machines.
Modern weaving mills often still use versions of the Jacquard for luxury fabrics.
The Jacquard revolutionized the textile industry by automating intricate designs.

Synonyms

  • Jacquard loom
