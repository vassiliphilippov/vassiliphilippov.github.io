maj

noun
Major (abbreviation)
8.4
Pronunciation: / meɪdʒ / (AmE), / meɪdʒ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for the military rank Major, used as a title before or after a name

Examples

Maj Smith
The letter was addressed to Maj Thompson, who led the battalion last year.
After his promotion, everyone started calling him Maj Carter.
The ceremony honored Maj Smith for his years of dedicated service.
