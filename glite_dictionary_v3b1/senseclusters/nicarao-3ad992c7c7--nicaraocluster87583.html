Nicarao

proper noun
legendary chief
Pronunciation: / nikaˈɾa.oʊ / (AmE), / nikaˈɾaʊ / (BrE)
a historical or legendary chief of the Nicarao people, often associated with the origin of the name Nicaragua

Examples

Chief Nicarao
According to some historians, Nicarao was a prominent leader in the region now known as Nicaragua.
The name of the country Nicaragua is believed by some to be linked to Chief Nicarao.
Spanish explorers wrote about their encounters with Nicarao and his people in the sixteenth century.
