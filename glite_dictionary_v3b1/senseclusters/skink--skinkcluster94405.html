skink

noun
a type of smooth, agile lizard
6.6
Pronunciation: / skɪŋk / (AmE), / skɪŋk / (BrE)
A small, agile lizard with a smooth, shiny skin, often with reduced limbs, found in moist tropical regions worldwide.

Examples

A skink lizard
A skink darted across the forest floor and disappeared into the undergrowth.
Several species of skink can be found in Southeast Asia's rainforests.
The shiny scales of the skink help it glide smoothly through leaf litter.
