wavery

adjective
unsteady or trembling
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪvəri / (AmE), / ˈweɪvəri / (BrE)
moving or appearing in an unsteady, trembling, or fluctuating way

Examples

Wavery movement
The wavery lines on the old map made it hard to read.
Her voice sounded wavery as she spoke in the cold night air.
A wavery shadow danced across the wall as the wind blew the curtains.

Related Terms

  • antonym: steady - firm, stable, or not changing
  • synonym: wavering - moving back and forth or fluctuating
