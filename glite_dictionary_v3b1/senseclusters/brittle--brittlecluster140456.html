brittle

noun
hard caramelized sugar candy
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪɾl̩ / (AmE), / ˈbrɪt.əl / (BrE)
A hard, thin sheet of caramelized sugar, often with nuts, eaten as a sweet.

Examples

Peanut brittle
Peanut brittle is a traditional treat during the winter months.
She made a batch of almond brittle for the holiday party.
The brittle cracked easily when he bit into it.
