gur

noun
unrefined sugar
Pronunciation: / ɡʊr / (AmE), / ɡʊə / (BrE)
a type of unrefined sugar made from sugarcane or palm sap, commonly used in South Asia

Examples

A piece of gur
She sweetened her tea with a piece of gur.
In many Indian desserts, gur is used instead of refined sugar for a richer flavor.
Farmers often eat gur with roti as a simple and energizing meal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jaggery - unrefined sugar made from sugarcane or palm sap
  • degree higher: sugar - a sweet substance used to sweeten food and drinks
