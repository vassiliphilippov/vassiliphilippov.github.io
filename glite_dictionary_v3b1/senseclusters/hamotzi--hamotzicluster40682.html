hamotzi

noun
Jewish bread blessing
Pronunciation: / hɑːˈmoʊtsi / (AmE), / hɑːˈməʊtsi / (BrE)
a Jewish blessing recited over bread before meals

Examples

The hamotzi blessing
Before the meal began, the family gathered to say the hamotzi together.
At the Shabbat dinner, Rachel led everyone in reciting the hamotzi over the challah.
The children learned to sing the hamotzi at their Hebrew school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grace - a prayer of thanks said before or after a meal
  • synonym: kiddush - a Jewish blessing recited over wine to sanctify the Sabbath or a holiday
