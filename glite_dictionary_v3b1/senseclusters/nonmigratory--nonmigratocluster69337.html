nonmigratory

adjective
not migrating (animal)
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈmaɪɡrəˌtɔri / (AmE), / nɒnˈmʌɪɡrəˌtəri / (BrE)
(of an animal) not migrating; tending to remain in the same area throughout the year.

Examples

Nonmigratory animal species
Many songbirds are migratory, but the nonmigratory species stay in the region all year.
The island is home to several nonmigratory mammals that have adapted to its unique environment.
Unlike geese, penguins are nonmigratory and do not travel long distances seasonally.

Related Terms

  • antonym: migratory - (of an animal) migrating or prone to migrate
