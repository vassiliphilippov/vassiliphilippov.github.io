stokehole

noun
compartment for ship's furnaces (nautical)
9.7 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊkˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈstəʊkˌhəʊl / (BrE)
(Nautical) The chamber or compartment in which a ship's furnaces are stoked or fired, especially on steam-powered vessels.
