disgorgement

noun
the act of vomiting
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɡɔrdʒmənt / (AmE), / dɪsˈɡɔːdʒmənt / (BrE)
the act of ejecting the contents of the stomach through the mouth; vomiting

Examples

Disgorgement of stomach
After eating spoiled food, disgorgement of the stomach contents occurred within the hour.
Certain chemicals can induce disgorgement as a protective reflex.
The patient's sudden disgorgement alarmed the attending nurse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emesis - the medical term for vomiting
  • synonym: vomiting - the act of ejecting stomach contents through the mouth
