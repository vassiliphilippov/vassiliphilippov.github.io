ohhhh

interjection
realization exclamation
Pronunciation: / oʊːːː / (AmE), / əʊːːː / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express realization, surprise, or sudden understanding

Examples

Ohhhh I see
When she saw the answer on the board, she exclaimed, "ohhhh, now it makes sense!"
He paused for a moment and then said, "ohhhh, I remember where I left my keys."
"Ohhhh, so that's why you were late!" she said, finally understanding.
