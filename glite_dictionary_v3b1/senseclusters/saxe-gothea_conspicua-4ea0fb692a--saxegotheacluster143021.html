Saxe-gothea conspicua

noun
an ornamental evergreen tree from southern Chile
Pronunciation: / ˌsæks goʊˈθiːə kənˈspɪkjuə / (AmE), / ˌsaks ɡəʊˈθiːə kənˈspɪkjʊə / (BrE)
A species of evergreen conifer, native to the mountains of southern Chile, with attractive foliage and partially weeping branches, cultivated as an ornamental yew.

Examples

Saxe-gothea conspicua tree
Botanists traveled to Chile to study the natural habitat of Saxe-gothea conspicua.
Many arboretums cultivate Saxe-gothea conspicua as a rare ornamental species.
The Saxe-gothea conspicua is prized in gardens for its graceful, weeping branches and evergreen foliage.
