Allegheny mountain spurge

noun
a perennial herb native to the southern U.S.
Pronunciation: / ˌæl.əˈɡeɪ.ni ˈmaʊn.tən spɜrdʒ / (AmE), / ˌæl.ɪˈɡeɪ.ni ˈmaʊn.tɪn spɜːdʒ / (BrE)
A low semi-evergreen perennial herb with small spikes of white or pinkish flowers, native to the southern United States but cultivated elsewhere.

Examples

Allegheny mountain spurge plant
Despite its origins in the South, Allegheny mountain spurge can thrive in cooler northern climates as well.
