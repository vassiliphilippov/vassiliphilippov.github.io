Anthemis nobilis

noun
Eurasian medicinal plant; Roman chamomile
9.2
Pronunciation: / ænˈθɛmɪs ˈnoʊbɪlɪs / (AmE), / anˈθɛmɪs ˈnɒbɪlɪs / (BrE)
A Eurasian plant with apple-scented leaves and white-rayed flowers, used medicinally; also known as Roman chamomile.

Examples

Anthemis nobilis plant
Gardeners value Anthemis nobilis for its fragrant, feathery foliage and delicate white flowers.
Some herbal teas contain dried Anthemis nobilis flowers to promote relaxation and sleep.
The essential oil of Anthemis nobilis is often used in aromatherapy and skincare products.
