selenium

noun
essential mineral
Pronunciation: / səˈliniəm / (AmE), / sɪˈliːniəm / (BrE)
a mineral essential for health that helps protect cells in the body from damage

Examples

Selenium in food
Brazil nuts are one of the richest natural sources of selenium.
A deficiency in selenium can lead to health problems over time.
Doctors recommend getting enough selenium through a balanced diet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vitamin E - a vitamin that helps protect cells from damage
  • synonym: zinc - an essential mineral important for immune function
