Tsuga mertensiana

noun
mountain hemlock tree species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtsuːɡə mərˌtɛnsiˈænə / (AmE), / ˈtsuːɡə mɛːˌtɛnsiˈɑːnə / (BrE)
A large evergreen conifer, commonly known as the mountain hemlock, native to western North America with wood harder than other hemlocks.
