a word in someone's ear

noun
to speak privately to someone
3.0
Pronunciation: / ə wɝːd ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz ɪr / (AmE), / ə wɜːd ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz ɪə / (BrE)
to speak to someone privately, often to give advice, information, or a warning

Examples

A word in his ear
I had a word in his ear before the meeting started so he knew what to expect.
The coach pulled the player aside for a word in his ear after practice.
Whenever I need help, Karen always has a word in my ear to guide me.
