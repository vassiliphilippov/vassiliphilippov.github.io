bizz

verb
to make buzzing sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪz / (AmE), / bɪz / (BrE)
to make a short, low buzzing or humming sound, like that of a small motor or insect

Examples

Motors bizz quietly
Late at night the old motor would bizz whenever someone switched it on.
A tiny insect began to bizz near my ear as I tried to sleep.
The phone started to bizz quietly on the table, alerting her to a new message.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buzz - to make a continuous humming or vibrating sound
  • synonym: hum - to make a low, steady continuous sound
  • antonym: silence - the absence of sound
