exponentiate

verb
to grow rapidly
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌspoʊnənˈʃieɪt / (AmE), / ɪkˌspəʊnənˈʃieɪt / (BrE)
to increase or cause to increase very rapidly, especially in a way that resembles exponential growth

Examples

Exponentiate growth
After the viral post, their follower count began to exponentiate within days.
If left unchecked, the problem could exponentiate and become much harder to solve.
The company's profits started to exponentiate after launching the new product line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expand - to become larger in size, number, or amount
  • synonym: multiply - to increase greatly in number or amount
