approx.

adjective
abbreviation for approximate
4.4
Pronunciation: / əˈprɑks / (AmE), / əˈprɒks / (BrE)
abbreviation of 'approximate', used before a noun to show that a value or measurement is not exact

Examples

Approx. value
The meeting will start at approx. 3 p.m., but it might be a few minutes late.
The distance between the two cities is approx. 200 miles according to the map.
He gave an approx. number of attendees since the final list was not ready.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approximate - not exact; close to the actual value
  • antonym: exact - completely correct or accurate
