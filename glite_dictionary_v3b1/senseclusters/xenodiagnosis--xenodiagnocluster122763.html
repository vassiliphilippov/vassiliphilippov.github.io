xenodiagnosis

noun
diagnosis using test animals
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌziːnoʊdaɪˈæɡnəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌzɛnəʊdaɪˈæɡnəsɪs / (BrE)
a method of diagnosing infection by transferring material from a patient to a test animal to observe if the animal becomes infected

Examples

Xenodiagnosis test method
The laboratory performed xenodiagnosis to confirm the presence of the parasite in the blood sample.
In rare cases, xenodiagnosis is used when standard diagnostic tests are inconclusive.
Researchers rely on xenodiagnosis to study certain infectious diseases in controlled environments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biopsy - the removal and examination of tissue to diagnose disease
  • synonym: diagnosis - the identification of a disease or condition
