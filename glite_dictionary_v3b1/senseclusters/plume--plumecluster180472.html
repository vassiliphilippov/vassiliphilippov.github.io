plume

noun
a feather or cluster of feathers
7.3
Pronunciation: / plum /; / pluːm / (AmE), / pljuːm /; / pluːm / (BrE)
a large or decorative feather, or a group of feathers, especially used for decoration or as part of a bird's covering

Examples

A plume of feathers
Several smaller birds preened their plumes in the morning sun.
She wore a hat decorated with a bright red plume from an exotic bird.
The peacock's magnificent plume caught everyone's attention at the zoo.
She added a colorful plume to her hat for the costume party.
The dancer's costume was adorned with a magnificent plume of ostrich feathers.
The knight wore a shining helmet with a brilliant plume on top.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feather - the flat appendage forming the plumage of birds
