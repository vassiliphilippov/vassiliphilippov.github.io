grappa

noun
Italian brandy from pressed grape residue
4.7 7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɑːpə / (AmE), / ˈɡrapə / (BrE)
A strong Italian brandy made from the residue of grapes after they have been pressed for wine.
