shipwright

noun
person who builds and repairs ships
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈʃɪp.raɪt / (BrE)
a person skilled in the construction and repair of ships, especially wooden vessels

Examples

An experienced shipwright
As a young apprentice, he learned his trade from an experienced shipwright.
Many generations of shipwrights have worked in this coastal town.
The skilled shipwright oversaw the construction of the new sailing vessel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boatbuilder - a person who builds boats
