noncarbon

adjective
without carbon
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈkɑrbən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkɑːbən / (BrE)
not containing, made of, or relating to carbon

Examples

Noncarbon fuel source
Scientists are developing noncarbon alternatives to traditional fuels to reduce emissions.
The new battery uses noncarbon materials to improve efficiency and safety.
Many minerals are classified as noncarbon compounds in chemistry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carbon-based - containing or made of carbon
