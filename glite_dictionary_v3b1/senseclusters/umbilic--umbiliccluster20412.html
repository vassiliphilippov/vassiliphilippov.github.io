umbilic

noun
navel; central point
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʌmˈbɪlɪk / (AmE), / ʌmˈbɪlɪk / (BrE)
the navel or a central point, especially in anatomy or geometry

Examples

The umbilic area
The doctor examined the patient's umbilic for signs of infection.
In geometry, the umbilic is considered the central point of a surface.
The incision was made just below the umbilic during the procedure.

Related Terms
