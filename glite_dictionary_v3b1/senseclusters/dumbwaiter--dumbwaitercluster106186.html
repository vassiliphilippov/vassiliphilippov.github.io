dumbwaiter

noun
small elevator for food or goods
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmˌweɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdʌmˌweɪtə / (BrE)
a small elevator used to transport food or goods between floors in a building

Examples

A dumbwaiter elevator
The broken dumbwaiter caused a delay in delivering meals to the upper floor.
The restaurant uses a dumbwaiter to send dishes up from the kitchen to the dining room.
They installed a dumbwaiter in the house to make laundry transport easier between floors.
