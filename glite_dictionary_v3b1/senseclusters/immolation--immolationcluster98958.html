immolation

noun
sacrifice by killing or burning
Pronunciation: / ˌɪməˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪməˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of killing or offering someone or something as a sacrifice, especially by burning

Examples

Human immolation
His dramatic immolation shocked the world and drew attention to the cause.
Stories of voluntary immolation as a protest have appeared throughout history.
The ancient ritual involved the immolation of animals to appease the gods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sacrifice - the act of offering something to a deity as a religious rite
