Saite

noun
person from Sais
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsaɪt / (BrE)
a person from the ancient Egyptian city of Sais or a member of Egypt's 26th (Saite) dynasty

Examples

A Saite ruler
The museum displayed artifacts belonging to a prominent Saite official from the 26th dynasty.
Historians study the influence of Saite rulers on ancient Egyptian culture and politics.
A Saite inscription was discovered on the temple walls during the excavation.
