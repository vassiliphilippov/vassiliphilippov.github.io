lantern

noun
portable light with protective case
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlæn.tɚn / (AmE), / ˈlæn.tən / (BrE)
A portable light source with a transparent protective case, often carried or hung outdoors.

Examples

A lantern glows
He hung a lantern from the tree branch to light the campsite.
She bought a decorative lantern for the garden party.
The old metal lantern cast a warm glow on the snowy porch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lamp - a device for giving light, especially one that has a covering
