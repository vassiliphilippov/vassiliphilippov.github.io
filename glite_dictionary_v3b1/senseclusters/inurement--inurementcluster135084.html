inurement

noun
becoming accustomed
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnjʊrmənt / (AmE), / ɪˈnjʊəmənt / (BrE)
the process of becoming accustomed to something, especially something unpleasant or difficult

Examples

Inurement to pain
Years of working outdoors led to his inurement to harsh weather conditions.
The soldiers' inurement to discomfort made the long march more bearable.
Her inurement to criticism allowed her to stay focused on her goals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adaptation - the process of adjusting to new conditions
  • synonym: habituation - the process of becoming used to something through repeated exposure
  • antonym: sensitivity - the quality of being easily affected by something
