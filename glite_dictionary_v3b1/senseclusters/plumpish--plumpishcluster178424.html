plumpish

adjective
slightly chubby
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌmpɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈplʌmpɪʃ / (BrE)
somewhat or slightly fat in a soft and rounded way

Examples

A plumpish baby
The baby had a plumpish face that made everyone smile.
She wore a dress that flattered her plumpish figure at the party.
After a winter of little exercise, he felt a bit plumpish around the waist.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: plump - having a full, rounded shape; slightly fat
  • antonym: thin - having little fat on the body
