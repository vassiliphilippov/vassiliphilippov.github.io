velcro

verb
fasten using Velcro
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛlkroʊ / (AmE), / ˈvɛlkrəʊ / (BrE)
to fasten or attach with Velcro, a type of fastening material consisting of two strips that stick together when pressed

Examples

To velcro pockets shut
Please velcro the pockets shut to keep everything secure.
She quickly velcroed her shoes before leaving the house.
The baby’s bib velcros at the back for easy removal.
