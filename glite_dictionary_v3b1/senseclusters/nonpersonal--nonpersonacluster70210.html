nonpersonal

adjective
lacking personal characteristics
2.2
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈpɜːrsənl̩ / (AmE), / nɒnˈpɜːs(ə)nəl / (BrE)
not relating to, characteristic of, or involving a particular person; lacking personal qualities or individuality.

Examples

A nonpersonal message
His feedback felt nonpersonal and objective rather than directed at anyone individually.
Many natural disasters are caused by nonpersonal forces beyond human control.
The company used a nonpersonal approach in its automated customer service replies.

Related Terms
