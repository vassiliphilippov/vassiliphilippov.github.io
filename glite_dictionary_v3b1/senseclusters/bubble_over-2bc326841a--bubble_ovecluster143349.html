bubble over

verb
overflow with emotion or excitement
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌbəl ˈoʊvɚ /; / ˈbʌb.əl ˈoʊ.vɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌbəl ˈəʊvə /; / ˈbʌb.əl ˈəʊ.və / (BrE)
(of a person) to be so full of strong emotion or excitement that it is clearly shown and cannot be contained

Examples

He bubbled over with joy
He bubbled over with enthusiasm as he described his new project.
She was bubbling over with ideas after the conference.
The children bubbled over with excitement when they saw the puppy.
During the award ceremony, her gratitude began to bubble over in her speech.
His enthusiasm for the project bubbled over and inspired the whole team.
The children couldn't help but bubble over with excitement on Christmas morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bubble up - to surge or well up, especially of emotion or liquid
  • synonym: overflow - to be so full that it spills out
