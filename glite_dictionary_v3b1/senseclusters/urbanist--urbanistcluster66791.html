urbanist

noun
city planning expert
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrbənɪst / (AmE), / ˈɜːbənɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies, designs, plans, or promotes the development and improvement of cities and urban areas

Examples

Urbanist designs parks
The urbanist proposed a new park to make the city more livable.
Many urbanists believe in creating walkable neighborhoods with easy access to public transport.
As an urbanist, she studies how cities can become more sustainable and efficient.
The urbanist presented a new vision for sustainable public transportation in the city.
As an urbanist, she advocates for more green spaces in densely populated neighborhoods.
Many urbanists study how architecture affects the daily lives of city residents.
The urbanist gave a lecture on how to make cities more sustainable and livable.
As a passionate urbanist, he advocates for better public transportation and green spaces in cities.
Many urbanists believe that walkable neighborhoods improve the quality of urban life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: architect - a person who designs buildings and often oversees construction
  • synonym: city planner - a person who plans and designs urban areas
  • antonym: ruralist - a person who prefers or studies rural areas
  • antonym: suburbanite - a person who lives in or prefers the suburbs
  • synonym: urban designer - a person who designs the physical features of cities
