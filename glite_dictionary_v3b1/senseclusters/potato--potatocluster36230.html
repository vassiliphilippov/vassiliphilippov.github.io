An annual plant native to South America, grown for its edible starchy tubers, which are widely cultivated as a vegetable; the rest of the plant is poisonous.

Examples

Potato plant
After the blight destroyed the potato crop, farmers faced a difficult season.
The potato plant has green leaves above ground but is valued for its tubers below ground.
We planted potatoes in the garden this spring and harvested them in the fall.
