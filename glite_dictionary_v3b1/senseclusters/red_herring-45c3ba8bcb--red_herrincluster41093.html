red herring

noun
preliminary financial prospectus
9.9 7.3
Pronunciation: /red ˈhe.rɪŋ/ (AmE), /red ˈhe.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
a preliminary version of a prospectus filed with regulators, distinguished by a disclaimer that its details may change before the final version is issued

Examples

Red herring prospectus
Investors reviewed the red herring to assess the company's potential before the release of the final prospectus.
Regulations require all changes between the red herring and the final prospectus be clearly disclosed.
The company filed a red herring with the SEC before its official IPO.
