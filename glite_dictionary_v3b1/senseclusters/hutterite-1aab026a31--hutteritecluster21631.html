Hutterite

adjective
of the Hutterites
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌtəˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈhʌtəˌraɪt / (BrE)
relating to the Hutterites, their beliefs, customs, language, or communal way of life

Examples

Hutterite traditions
The Hutterite community is known for its communal living and shared resources.
She studied Hutterite traditions as part of her research on religious minorities.
Many Hutterite colonies are located in rural areas of North America.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mennonite - relating to the Mennonites, a similar Anabaptist group
