muskie

noun
large North American fish
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌski / (AmE), / ˈmʌski / (BrE)
a large predatory freshwater fish of North America, also known as the muskellunge

Examples

A large muskie fish
He spent hours on the lake hoping to catch a muskie before sunset.
The muskie is prized by anglers for its size and strength.
She was thrilled when she finally landed a massive muskie during her vacation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: muskellunge - a large North American freshwater fish, also called muskie
  • synonym: pike - a large predatory freshwater fish related to the muskie
