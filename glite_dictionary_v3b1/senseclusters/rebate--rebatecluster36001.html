rebate

verb
give a price reduction or partial refund
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈriˌbeɪt / (AmE), / ˈriːbeɪt / (BrE)
to give a partial refund or reduction in the price of a product, often during a special sale or promotion

Examples

To rebate the price
Some manufacturers rebate part of the price directly to the customer after the sale.
The company decided to rebate the installation fee as part of its promotion.
The electronics store will rebate $50 on any new laptop purchased this weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discount - to reduce the price of something; a reduction in price
