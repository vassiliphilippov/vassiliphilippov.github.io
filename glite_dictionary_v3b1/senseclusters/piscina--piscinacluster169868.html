piscina

noun
ornamental fish pond
Pronunciation: / pɪˈsiːnə / (AmE), / pɪˈsiːnə / (BrE)
an ornamental pool or pond used for keeping fish, especially in historical or medieval contexts

Examples

Stone piscina
The old monastery grounds still feature a stone piscina filled with water lilies and goldfish.
During the Middle Ages, a piscina was often used to supply fresh fish for the estate's kitchen.
Visitors admired the restored piscina in the castle gardens, once home to rare carp.
