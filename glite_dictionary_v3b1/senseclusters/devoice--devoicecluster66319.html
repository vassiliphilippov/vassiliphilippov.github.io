devoice

verb
pronounce without vocal cord vibration
9.0
Pronunciation: / diˈvɔɪs / (AmE), / diˈvɔɪs / (BrE)
to pronounce a sound without vibrating the vocal cords; to make a voiced sound voiceless

Examples

To devoice final consonants
In some dialects, speakers devoice final consonants in words.
Linguists observe that children sometimes mistakenly devoice voiced sounds when learning to speak.
The teacher demonstrated how to devoice the syllable during the lesson.
