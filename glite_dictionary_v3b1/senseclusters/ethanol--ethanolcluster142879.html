ethanol

noun
a type of alcohol used in drinks, fuels, and industry
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθ.əˌnɑl / (AmE), / ˈiːθ.ənɒl / (BrE)
a colorless volatile flammable liquid that is the active ingredient of alcoholic drinks and is also used as a solvent, fuel, or in industry

Examples

ethanol fuel
Consuming large quantities of ethanol can be hazardous to health.
Gasoline mixed with ethanol can reduce vehicle emissions.
The laboratory uses ethanol as a solvent for many chemical reactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alcohol - a colorless volatile flammable liquid also known as ethanol, used as a beverage and solvent
