faded

adjective
having lost color or brightness
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˈfeɪdɪd / (BrE)
no longer having the original brightness, freshness, or strength of color; having become dull or washed out

Examples

Faded blue jeans
She wore her favorite pair of faded jeans to the party.
The curtains in the living room were faded after years of sunlight.
The old photograph looked faded but held many memories.
