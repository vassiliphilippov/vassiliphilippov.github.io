unreel

verb
to unwind from a reel
7.4 0.11/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàril / (AmE), /  ånÀàriÀêl / (BrE)
to unwind or release (something) from or as if from a reel

Examples

To unreel a film
As the story continued, her memories seemed to unreel like an old film.
He began to unreel the fishing line to check for tangles.
The technician had to unreel several meters of cable for the new connection.

Synonyms

  • unspool
    6.3 0.09/m
    unspool means to pull thread or film off a spool or reel, while unreel means to release something from a reel.
  • reel
    3.4 2.78/m
    reel can mean to wind or unwind material onto a spool, while unreel specifically means to release it from a reel.
  • unwind
    3.7 0.45/m
    unwind means to loosen or straighten something by reversing twists, while unreel implies releasing material from a reel or spool.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reel - a cylinder or device for winding and storing line, tape, film, etc.
  • antonym: wind - to wrap or coil (something) around a reel or spool
