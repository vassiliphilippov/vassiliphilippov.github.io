interleague

adjective
between leagues
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌliːɡ / (AmE), / ˈɪntəlˌliːɡ / (BrE)
describing games or events that take place between teams from different leagues

Examples

Interleague play
The baseball season features several interleague matchups that attract large crowds.
Fans were excited to see their team play an interleague game for the first time.
Television ratings often increase during interleague competitions between rival cities.
