semidivine

noun
partly divine being
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈdaɪvaɪn / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈdɪvaɪn / (BrE)
a being who is partly divine, often considered to have some godlike origin or powers but not fully a god

Examples

A semidivine being
In ancient legends, a semidivine often acted as a bridge between gods and humans.
The villagers believed the healer was a semidivine with special powers.
Many myths feature a semidivine hero who performs extraordinary feats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demigod - a being with partial divine status, often the offspring of a god and a mortal
  • antonym: mortal - a being who is not divine and is subject to death
