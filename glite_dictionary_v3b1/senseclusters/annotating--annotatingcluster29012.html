annotating

noun
labeling data for machine learning
3.9
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u00e6n\u0259\u02ccte\u026at\u026a\u014b / (AmE), / \u02c8an\u0259\u02ccte\u026at\u026a\u014b / (BrE)
(in data science and machine learning) the process of labeling or tagging data items (for example, images, text, or audio) to create training and evaluation datasets for models.

Examples

Annotating images
Data scientists spend hours annotating thousands of images to prepare training datasets for the new model.
For accurate results, the team developed guidelines before annotating audio clips for the speech-recognition project.
Freelancers are often hired for annotating large text corpora to create labeled examples for evaluation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annotation - a note or label added to data or text for explanation or analysis
  • synonym: labeling - the act of assigning labels to data items for use in machine-learning models
