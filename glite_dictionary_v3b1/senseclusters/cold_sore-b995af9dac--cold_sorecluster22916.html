cold sore

noun
painful blister from herpes simplex virus
Pronunciation: / koʊld sɔr / (AmE), / kəʊld sɔː / (BrE)
a small, painful blister or group of blisters, usually on the lips or around the mouth, caused by infection with the herpes simplex virus

Examples

A cold sore on lips
A cold sore can be uncomfortable and take a week or more to heal.
He tried to hide the cold sore on his lip with makeup before the meeting.
Stress and fatigue can sometimes trigger a cold sore outbreak in people with the virus.
