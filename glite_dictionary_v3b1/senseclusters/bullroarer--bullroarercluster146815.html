bullroarer

noun
ceremonial whirring instrument
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌrɔrər / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌrɔːrə / (BrE)
a flat piece of wood attached to a cord or string that makes a whirring sound when spun, used for signaling or in ceremonies
