sickout

noun
organized worker protest
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪkˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈsɪkˌaʊt / (BrE)
an organized protest in which workers call in sick to pressure their employer for change or to express disagreement

Examples

Worker sickout protest
Teachers organized a sickout to demand better classroom resources and smaller class sizes.
The hospital was short-staffed after a sickout by nurses protesting new policies.
Management quickly responded to the sickout by meeting with union representatives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strike - a work stoppage by employees as a form of protest
  • synonym: walkout - a protest in which workers leave their workplace as a group
