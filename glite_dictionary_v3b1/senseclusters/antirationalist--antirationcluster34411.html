antirationalist

noun
opponent of rationality
9.4
Pronunciation: /æn.tɪ.ˈræʃ.ə.nəl.ɪst/ /; / /ən.tɪ.ˈræʃ.ə.nəl.ɪst/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.ˈræʃ.ə.nəl.ɪst/ /; / /ən.tɪ.ˈræʃ.ə.nəl.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who opposes or rejects the use of reason or rationality as the main basis for belief or action

Examples

During the discussion, an antirationalist perspective emphasized the importance of intuition over analysis.
