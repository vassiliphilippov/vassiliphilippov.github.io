professor

noun
senior university academic staff member
1.0
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɛsɚ / (AmE), / prəˈfɛsə / (BrE)
a senior teacher and researcher at a college or university, often holding the highest academic rank

Examples

University professor
Dr. Smith is a professor of biology at the university.
She became a professor after many years of teaching and research.
The professor assigned extra reading for next week's seminar.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: assistant professor - a lower academic rank than full professor in some universities
  • degree lower: associate professor - an intermediate academic rank between assistant and full professor
  • degree lower: lecturer - someone who gives lectures, often a lower academic rank
