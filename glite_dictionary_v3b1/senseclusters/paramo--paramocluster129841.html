paramo

noun
Andean high plateau
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrəˌmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpærəməʊ / (BrE)
a high, treeless plateau or grassland found in the Andes mountains, often with a cold and misty climate

Examples

The paramo grassland
The unique plants of the paramo have adapted to survive in harsh conditions.
Hiking across the paramo can be challenging due to the cold and thin air.
Many rivers in South America begin in the high paramo regions of the Andes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: savanna - a grassy plain in tropical and subtropical regions
  • synonym: steppe - a large area of flat unforested grassland
