unfixity

noun
indecisiveness; inconstancy
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪksəti / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪksɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being changeable, indecisive, or lacking firmness in mind, feeling, or purpose

Examples

Mental unfixity
His unfixity made it difficult for the team to agree on a final decision.
The unfixity of her emotions left her friends unsure how to support her.
A leader's unfixity can undermine confidence in their ability to guide a group.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inconstancy - the quality of not staying the same or being unreliable
  • synonym: indecision - the inability to make decisions quickly
  • antonym: steadfastness - the quality of being firm and unwavering
