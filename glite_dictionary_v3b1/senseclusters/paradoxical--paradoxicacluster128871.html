paradoxical

adjective
seeming contradiction or impossibility
7.0 7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpær.əˈdɑk.sɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌpær.əˈdɒk.sɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Seeming impossible or self-contradictory, but possibly true or valid.

Examples
