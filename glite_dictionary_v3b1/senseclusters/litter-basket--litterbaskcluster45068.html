litter-basket

noun
public rubbish container
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtər ˌbæskət / (AmE), / ˈlɪtə ˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a container, typically in or outside a public building, for people to put rubbish in

Examples

A litter-basket outside
Please put your empty cup in the litter-basket by the door before you leave.
Someone carelessly tossed their sandwich wrapper next to the litter-basket instead of inside it.
The park is very clean because there is a litter-basket every few meters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trash can - a container for rubbish, especially one with a lid
  • synonym: wastebasket - a basket for holding wastepaper, especially indoors
