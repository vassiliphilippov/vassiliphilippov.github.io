ballast

noun
stabilizing influence on character or emotions
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbæl.əst / (AmE), / ˈbæl.əst / (BrE)
a quality, characteristic, or influence that provides mental or moral stability, helping to steady and support a person's character or emotions.

Examples

Emotional ballast
A sense of humor can serve as a ballast against the stresses of everyday life.
Her family provided the emotional ballast she needed during difficult times.
His strong principles acted as a ballast, keeping him steady amid pressure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anchor - something that provides stability or confidence
