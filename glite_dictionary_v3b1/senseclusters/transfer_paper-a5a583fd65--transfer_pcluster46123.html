transfer paper

noun
paper for transferring designs
4.1 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtræns.fər ˈpeɪ.pər/ (AmE), /ˈtræns.fə ˈpeɪ.pə/ /; / /ˈtrɑːns.fə ˈpeɪ.pə/ (BrE)
A special paper coated with a material that allows a design, image, or pattern to be transferred onto another surface.

Examples

Special transfer paper
Architects sometimes rely on transfer paper to trace complex designs onto blueprints.
She used transfer paper to move her sketch onto the fabric before painting.
When making custom T-shirts, transfer paper helps apply images using heat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carbon paper - paper coated with a layer of a loosely bound pigment to transfer written or typed marks
