crapware

noun
unwanted software
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkræpˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈkræpˌweə / (BrE)
unwanted or low-quality software that is often pre-installed on new computers or devices

Examples

Pre-installed crapware
My new laptop came loaded with crapware that I had to uninstall.
Many users complain about the amount of crapware included on their smartphones.
He spent hours removing crapware before his computer ran smoothly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bloatware - software that uses a lot of resources and is often unnecessary
  • antonym: malware - software designed to harm or exploit computers
