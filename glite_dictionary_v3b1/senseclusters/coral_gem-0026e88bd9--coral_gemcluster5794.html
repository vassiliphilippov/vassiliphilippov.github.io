coral gem

noun
a flowering perennial plant, often with scarlet or purple blooms
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəl dʒɛm / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəl dʒɛm / (BrE)
A low-growing, much-branched perennial plant from the Canary Islands, with orange-red, scarlet, or purple flowers, now naturalized in the United States.

Examples

Gardeners in warmer climates often plant the coral gem for its colorful blossoms.
