trend-setting

adjective
initiating or popularizing a trend
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛndˌsɛtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrɛndˌsɛtɪŋ / (BrE)
initiating or popularizing a new fashion, idea, or activity that others subsequently follow

Examples

This trend-setting technology will shape the industry for years to come.
