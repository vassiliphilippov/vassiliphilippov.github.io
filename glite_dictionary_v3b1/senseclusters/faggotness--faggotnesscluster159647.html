faggotness

noun
offensive term for flamboyant behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈfæɡətˌnɛs / (AmE), / ˈfæɡətnɪs / (BrE)
A highly offensive term describing a style or behavior perceived as excessively flamboyant, exaggerated, or lacking seriousness, often used as a slur against LGBTQ+ individuals.

Examples

His faggotness style
Many people were offended by his faggotness during the performance.
She disliked the faggotness that some colleagues brought to the workplace.
The film was criticized for portraying the character’s faggotness as a joke.
