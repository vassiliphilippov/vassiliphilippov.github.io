pycnotic

adjective
relating to condensed cell nuclei (pyknosis)
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɪkˈnɑtɪk / (AmE), / pɪkˈnɒtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or exhibiting pyknosis; showing the condensation of cell nuclei, especially as seen in degenerating or dying cells.

Examples

A pycnotic nucleus
A pycnotic nucleus is often seen in cells affected by necrosis.
The pathologist noted several pycnotic nuclei in the tissue sample.
Under the microscope, pycnotic changes indicated that the cells were undergoing apoptosis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pyknosis - the condensation of a cell's nucleus during cell death
