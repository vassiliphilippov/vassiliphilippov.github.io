electrochemist

noun
scientist in electrochemistry
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈkɛmɪst / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈkɛmɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies the chemical effects of electricity and the interactions between electrical and chemical processes

Examples

The electrochemist studies electricity
The electrochemist presented her findings at the international conference on battery technology.
As an electrochemist, he focuses on developing more efficient fuel cells for electric vehicles.
Many industries rely on the expertise of an electrochemist to improve their energy storage systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemist - a scientist who studies or works with chemicals
  • antonym: physicist - a scientist who studies physics
