torch

noun
device producing a hot flame for welding, cutting
3.3
Pronunciation: / tɔrtʃ / (AmE), / tɔːtʃ / (BrE)
a device that mixes air and gas to produce a very hot flame, used for tasks such as welding or cutting

Examples

A welding torch
A propane torch is ideal for soldering copper pipes in plumbing work.
Please ensure the gas supply is off before lighting the torch.
The welder used a torch to cut through the steel beams.

Related Terms
