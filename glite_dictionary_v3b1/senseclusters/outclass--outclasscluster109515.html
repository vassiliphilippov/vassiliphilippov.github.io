outclass

verb
surpass greatly; be much better than
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈklæs / (AmE), / aʊtˈklɑːs / (BrE)
to be much better than someone or something; to surpass or excel so clearly that others seem inferior in comparison

Examples

To outclass rivals
She outclassed all her rivals to win the championship.
The new phone easily outclassed its competitors in speed and design.
Their team outclassed every opponent they faced this season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excel - to do extremely well or be superior to others
  • synonym: outshine - to be much more impressive or outstanding than
  • synonym: surpass - to be greater or better than
