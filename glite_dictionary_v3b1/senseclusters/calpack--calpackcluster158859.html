calpack

noun
traditional high-crowned men's cap
Pronunciation: / ˈkælˌpæk / (AmE), / ˈkalˌpak / (BrE)
a high-crowned black cap, usually made of felt or sheepskin, traditionally worn by men in Turkey, Iran, and the Caucasus

Examples

A black calpack
He wore a black calpack as part of his national costume during the festival.
Historians believe the calpack symbolized both status and identity in Central Asian societies.
The museum displayed a traditional calpack alongside other regional garments.
