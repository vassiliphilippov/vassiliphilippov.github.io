all-knowing

adjective
knowing everything; omniscient
Pronunciation: / ˌɔlˈnoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɔːlˈnəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
having complete or infinite knowledge; knowing everything.

Examples

An all-knowing person
Her mother seemed all-knowing when it came to the children's mischief.
Many religions believe in an all-knowing deity who observes humanity.
The novel features an all-knowing narrator who reveals every character's thoughts and secrets.
