weatherwoman

noun
female weather forecaster
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛðərˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈwɛðəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman whose job is to present weather forecasts on television, radio, or other media.

Examples

A weatherwoman on TV
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a weatherwoman on national television.
The weatherwoman predicted rain for most of the week.
Viewers praised the weatherwoman for her clear and accurate forecasts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: weatherman - a man who presents weather forecasts
  • synonym: weatherperson - a person who presents weather forecasts, gender-neutral term
