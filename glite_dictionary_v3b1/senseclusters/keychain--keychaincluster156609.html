keychain

noun
digital password storage
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkiː.tʃeɪn/ (AmE), /ˈkiː.tʃeɪn/ (BrE)
a secure digital storage system that saves passwords, authentication tokens, or cryptographic keys for easy access and management

Examples

Digital keychain
The browser saved my login details in the keychain, so I did not have to type them.
If you forget your password, you can often retrieve it from your device's keychain.
Many apps ask for permission to access the keychain to autofill your credentials securely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: password manager - a tool that stores and manages passwords securely
