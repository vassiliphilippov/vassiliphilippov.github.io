watch the world go by

verb
observe life around you passively
Pronunciation: / wɑtʃ ðə wɝld ɡoʊ baɪ / (AmE), / wɒtʃ ðə wɜːld ɡəʊ baɪ / (BrE)
to spend time observing life and activities happening around you, especially in a relaxed or passive way, without becoming involved

Examples

To watch the world go by slowly
On Sunday afternoons, I like to sit by the window and watch the world go by.
She spent her lunch break quietly watching the world go by from the park bench.
Some people travel to bustling cities just to watch the world go by from a busy café.
