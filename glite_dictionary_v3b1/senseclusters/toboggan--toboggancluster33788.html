toboggan

noun
a sled without runners, used on snow
5.5
Pronunciation: / təˈbɑɡən / (AmE), / təˈbɒɡən / (BrE)
a long, narrow sled made from a single flat board or several boards, curving up at the front and without runners, used for sliding downhill over snow or ice

Examples

A wooden toboggan
After the first big snowfall, the park was filled with kids and their toboggans.
The children rode a toboggan down the snowy hill, laughing all the way.
We brought our old wooden toboggan to the winter festival for the race.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sled - a vehicle of various forms mounted on runners for use on snow, ice, or grass
