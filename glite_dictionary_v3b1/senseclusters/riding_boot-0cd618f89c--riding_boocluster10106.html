riding boot

noun
tall boot for horse riding
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪ.dɪŋ buːt / (AmE), / ˈraɪ.dɪŋ bʊt / (BrE)
A tall, sturdy boot without laces, designed to be worn when riding horses, typically forming part of traditional riding attire.

Examples

A riding boot pair
He polished his riding boot before heading to the stables.
She pulled on her riding boots and mounted her horse.
Traditional equestrian outfits often include a pair of black riding boots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jodhpur boot - an ankle-high riding boot worn with jodhpurs
