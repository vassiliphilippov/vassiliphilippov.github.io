herald a new era

verb
announce major change or new period
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrəld ə nu ˈɛrə / (AmE), / ˈhɛrəld ə njuː ˈɪərə / (BrE)
to announce or signal the beginning of a period of major change or development

Examples

To herald a new era boldly
Many hope that renewable energy will herald a new era of environmental responsibility.
The invention of the smartphone heralded a new era in communication.
The peace treaty heralded a new era of cooperation between the two countries.
