Bovidae

noun
family of hoofed mammals (antelopes, cattle, goats, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊvɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊvɪdiː / (BrE)
A large family of hoofed mammals that includes antelopes, cattle, sheep, goats, and related species.

Examples

The Bovidae family
Researchers study the evolutionary history of Bovidae to understand their diverse adaptations.
Sheep, goats, and buffalo all belong to the biological group known as Bovidae.
The Bovidae family includes both domesticated cattle and wild antelopes.
