pap

noun
soft food requiring little chewing
6.7
Pronunciation: / pæp / (AmE), / pæp / (BrE)
soft, bland food that requires little or no chewing, often given to people who are ill or have difficulty swallowing

Examples

Soft pap food
After his surgery, he was put on a pap diet until he healed.
Infants often start with pap before moving on to solid foods.
The elderly residents were served pap each morning for breakfast.
