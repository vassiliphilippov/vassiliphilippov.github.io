tank farm

noun
area for storing petroleum in tanks
5.8
Pronunciation: / tæŋk fɑrm / (AmE), / tæŋk fɑːm / (BrE)
An area of land used for the storage of large quantities of petroleum or other liquids in tanks.

Examples

A tank farm area
After the spill, inspections at every tank farm in the region were intensified.
Safety regulations around the tank farm are especially strict to prevent leaks or fires.
The oil company built a new tank farm near the harbor to increase storage capacity.
