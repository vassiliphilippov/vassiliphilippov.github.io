ingrained

adjective
deeply rooted or firmly fixed

(of a habit, belief, or attitude) deeply rooted; firmly established and difficult to change.

Examples

An ingrained habit
His ingrained distrust of outsiders made it difficult for him to make new friends.
It can take years to break an ingrained habit such as smoking.
Many older traditions remain ingrained in rural communities.
