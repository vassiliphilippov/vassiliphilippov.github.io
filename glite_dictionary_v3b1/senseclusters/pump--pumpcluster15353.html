pump

verb
shoot or deliver rapidly (e.g., bullets)
Pronunciation: / pʌmp / (AmE), / pʌmp / (BrE)
to shoot or deliver something, especially bullets or other projectiles, in rapid succession or with force

Examples

To pump bullets
She pumped shots at the target during her training exercises.
Soldiers pumped round after round into the barricade as they advanced.
The gunman pumped bullets into the car before fleeing the scene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fire - to shoot a bullet or other projectile from a weapon
