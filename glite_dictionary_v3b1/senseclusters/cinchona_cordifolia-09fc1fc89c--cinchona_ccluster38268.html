Cinchona cordifolia

noun
cinchona tree species; source of Cartagena bark
Pronunciation: / sɪŋˈkoʊnə kɔːrdɪˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / sɪŋˈkəʊnə kɔːdɪˈfəʊlɪə / (BrE)
A species of cinchona tree native to Colombia, known as a source of Cartagena bark used for medicinal purposes.
