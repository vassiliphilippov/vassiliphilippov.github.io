Guangdong

proper noun
province in southern China
Pronunciation: / gwɑŋˈdɔŋ / (AmE), / gwæŋˈdɒŋ / (BrE)
A province in southern China, known for its economic significance and coastal location; its capital is Guangzhou.

Examples

Guangdong province
Cantonese cuisine originated in Guangdong province, influencing Chinese food worldwide.
Many businesses in Guangdong benefit from its proximity to the South China Sea.
The economic boom in Guangdong has attracted millions of migrant workers.
