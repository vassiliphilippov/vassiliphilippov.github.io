mothproof

adjective
resistant to moth damage
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːθˌpruːf / (AmE), / ˈmɒθˌpruːf / (BrE)
Resistant to damage or destruction by moths, especially in reference to fabrics or materials.

Examples

Mothproof fabric
After treatment, the blanket became completely mothproof and safe to store.
I prefer buying mothproof clothing to avoid any unwanted holes.
The company sells mothproof wool carpets that last for years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: moth-eaten - damaged or worn out by moths
  • synonym: waterproof - impervious to water
