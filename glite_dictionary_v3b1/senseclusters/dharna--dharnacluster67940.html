dharna

noun
nonviolent sit-in protest
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdɑːr.nə/ /; / /ˈdʌr.nə/ (AmE), /ˈdɑː.nə/ /; / /ˈdʌ.nə/ (BrE)
a nonviolent sit-in protest, especially in South Asia, used to demand action or redress

Examples

A peaceful dharna
The activists organized a dharna in front of the government building to demand justice.
Hundreds of farmers held a peaceful dharna to protest new agricultural laws.
The union called for a dharna until their demands were addressed by management.
