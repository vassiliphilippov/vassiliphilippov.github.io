castanet

noun
percussion instrument
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkæstəˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˌkæstəˈnɛt / (BrE)
a small percussion instrument, usually used in pairs and clicked together by hand to make rhythmic sounds, especially in Spanish music and dance
