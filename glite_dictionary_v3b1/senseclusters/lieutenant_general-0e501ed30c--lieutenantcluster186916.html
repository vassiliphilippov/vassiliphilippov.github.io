lieutenant general

noun
military rank above major general
5.2
Pronunciation: / luˈtɛnənt ˈdʒɛnərəl / (AmE), / lɛfˈtɛnənt ˈdʒɛnərəl / (BrE)
A high military rank above major general and below full general.

Examples

A lieutenant general rank
After years of distinguished service, she was promoted to lieutenant general in the army.
He reported directly to the lieutenant general during the strategic planning meeting.
The lieutenant general led the division through some of its toughest battles.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: general - an officer ranking just above lieutenant general
  • degree lower: major general - an officer ranking just below lieutenant general
