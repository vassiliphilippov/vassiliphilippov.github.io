Voltairean

noun
follower of Voltaire
9.5
Pronunciation: /vɑːl.ˈte.rən/ /; / /vɑːl.ˈteə.rən/ (AmE), /vɒl.ˈteə.rən/ /; / /vɒl.ˈte.rən/ (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the ideas of Voltaire, especially those promoting reason, tolerance, and criticism of superstition

Examples

A Voltairean thinker
As a proud Voltairean, she valued free speech and rational debate above all else.
The club attracted many Voltaireans who enjoyed discussing philosophy and challenging old beliefs.
He was called a Voltairean for his outspoken criticism of superstition and support for tolerance.
