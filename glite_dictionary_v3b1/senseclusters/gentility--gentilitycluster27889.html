gentility

noun
refined and elegant manners
3.3
Pronunciation: / dʒɛnˈtɪlɪti / (AmE), / dʒɛnˈtɪlɪti / (BrE)
elegance and refinement of manner; the quality of being polite, graceful, or socially superior

Examples

High gentility
Despite his wealth, he lacked the gentility expected at such formal gatherings.
Her gentility was evident in the way she greeted every guest with poise and warmth.
The old manor's furnishings reflected a time of elegance and gentility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elegance - refined grace or dignified propriety
  • antonym: vulgarity - lack of refinement or taste
