retrospect

noun
review or contemplation of the past
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛtrəˌspɛkt / (AmE), / ˈrɛtrəʊˌspɛkt / (BrE)
the act of thinking about or reviewing past events or situations

Examples

In retrospect now
In retrospect, she realized how significant that meeting was to her career.
Looking back in retrospect, we can see how the plan could have been improved.
The decision seemed unwise in retrospect, but it felt right at the time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anticipation - the act of looking forward; expectation of future events
  • synonym: recollection - the action or faculty of remembering something
