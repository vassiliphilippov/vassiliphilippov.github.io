play on fears

verb
exploit or manipulate fear
Pronunciation: / pleɪ ɑn fɪrz / (AmE), / pleɪ ɒn fɪəz / (BrE)

Examples

To play on fears of crime

Rumors were spread to play on fears of economic instability in the community.
The advertisement sought to play on fears about personal safety to sell security systems.
