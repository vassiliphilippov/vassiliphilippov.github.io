fluoroscopic

adjective
using fluoroscopy
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌflʊrəˈskɑpɪk / (AmE), / ˌflɔːrəˈskɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to or using fluoroscopy, a technique for viewing moving internal body structures in real time

Examples

Fluoroscopic images
The surgeon relied on fluoroscopic images to guide the placement of the implant.
A fluoroscopic examination allows doctors to observe organs in motion during the procedure.
The technician adjusted the fluoroscopic equipment before starting the diagnostic test.

Related Terms

  • synonym: radiographic - relating to the use of X-rays to view internal structures
