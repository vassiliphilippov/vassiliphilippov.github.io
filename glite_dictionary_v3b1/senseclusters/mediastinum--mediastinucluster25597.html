mediastinum

noun
the central area in the chest between the lungs
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌmiːdiəˈstaɪnəm / (AmE), / ˌmiːdɪəˈstaɪnəm / (BrE)
the central compartment of the thoracic cavity, located between the lungs, and containing the heart, major blood vessels, esophagus, trachea, and thymus.

Examples

The mediastinum cavity
Enlargement of the mediastinum can be seen in some chest X-rays.
Infections of the mediastinum are rare but can be life-threatening.
The surgeon examined the mediastinum to locate the tumor.
