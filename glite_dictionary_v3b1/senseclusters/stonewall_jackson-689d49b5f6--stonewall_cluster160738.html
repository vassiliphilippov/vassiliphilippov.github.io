Stonewall Jackson

proper noun
Confederate general (American Civil War)
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊnwɔl ˈdʒæksən / (AmE), / ˈstəʊnwɔːl ˈdʒæksən / (BrE)
(1824–1863) A famous general in the Confederate Army during the American Civil War, known for his troops' steadfastness at the First Battle of Bull Run.
