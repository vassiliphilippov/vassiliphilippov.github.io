bandanna

verb
to tie with bandanna
5.3
Pronunciation: / bænˈdænə / (AmE), / bænˈdænə / (BrE)
to tie or fasten a bandanna around someone or something, often for decoration or protection

Examples

To bandanna a head
They bandannaed the horses before the parade to make them look festive.
She bandannaed her hair to keep it out of her face while working.
The hikers bandannaed their necks to protect themselves from the sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wrap - to cover or enclose something with material
