harpy

noun
large tropical American eagle
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːr.pi / (AmE), / ˈhɑː.pi / (BrE)
a large, powerful black-and-white eagle of tropical American forests, known for its prominent crest

Examples

Harpy eagle
Researchers tracked a harpy in the Amazon rainforest for conservation purposes.
The harpy is one of the largest and most powerful predatory birds in the world.
With its striking crest, the harpy is easily recognizable among tropical eagles.
