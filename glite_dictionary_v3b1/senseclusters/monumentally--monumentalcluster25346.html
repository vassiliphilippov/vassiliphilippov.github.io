monumentally

adverb
extremely impressively
4.4 0.46/m

to a very great or extreme degree; extremely or hugely (often emphasizing how big, impressive, or memorable something is)

Examples

A monumentally big project
The project was monumentally expensive, requiring years of planning and construction.
She was monumentally disappointed when the concert was canceled at the last minute.
His decision turned out to be monumentally important for the future of the company.
The project was monumentally successful, far exceeding everyone's expectations.
She was monumentally bored during the long, repetitive meeting.
It would be monumentally difficult to finish this task without help.
