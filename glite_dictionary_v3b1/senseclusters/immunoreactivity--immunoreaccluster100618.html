immunoreactivity

noun
immune response ability
8.2
Pronunciation: /ɪm.juː.noʊ.riː.æk.ˈtɪv.ə.tiː/ /; / /ɪm.juː.noʊ.rɪ.æk.ˈtɪv.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /ɪm.juː.nəʊ.riː.æk.ˈtɪv.ə.tiː/ /; / /ɪm.juː.nəʊ.rɪ.æk.ˈtɪv.ə.tiː/ (BrE)
the ability or degree to which a substance, cell, or tissue provokes or reacts with an immune response, often measured in laboratory tests

  • antonym: immunodeficiency - a weakened or insufficient immune response
  • synonym: immunogenicity - the ability of a substance to provoke an immune response
  • synonym: immunoresponsiveness - the capacity of the immune system to respond to stimulation
