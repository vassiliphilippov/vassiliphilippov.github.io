kukui

noun
Hawaiian tree nut
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / kuËËˆkuËi / (AmE), / kuËËˆkuËi / (BrE)

Examples

Kukui nut lei
The kukui nut is often strung into leis for special Hawaiian ceremonies.
Traditional Hawaiian dishes sometimes include roasted kukui as an ingredient.
The oil from the kukui nut was once used to light lamps in ancient Hawaii.
