cobble

noun
rectangular stone used for road paving
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒbl / (BrE)
a roughly rectangular stone with a rounded top, used in the past for paving roads and streets

Examples

Old cobble streets
Many European cities still have roads paved with cobbles.
The old street was lined with cobbles that made walking uneven but charming.
Workers removed several cobbles to repair the underground pipes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cobblestone - a small, round stone used for paving streets
