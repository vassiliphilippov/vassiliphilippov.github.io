vindaloo

proper noun
British football song
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪndəˈluː / (AmE), / ˌvɪndəˈluː / (BrE)
the title of a 1998 British novelty football song by Fat Les, commonly sung by fans at sporting events

Examples

Vindaloo football song
Fans erupted into singing vindaloo as the players entered the stadium.
The pub was filled with the sound of vindaloo after England scored a goal.
He learned all the words to vindaloo before attending his first football match.
