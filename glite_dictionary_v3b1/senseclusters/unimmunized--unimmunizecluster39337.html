unimmunized

adjective
not vaccinated
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈɪmjəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈɪmjʊnaɪzd / (BrE)
not having received a vaccine to protect against a specific disease

Examples

Unimmunized children
Many unimmunized children are at greater risk during outbreaks of infectious diseases.
Travelers who are unimmunized may be required to get certain vaccines before entering some countries.
The school reported that only a small percentage of students remained unimmunized after the vaccination campaign.

Related Terms

  • antonym: immunized - having received a vaccine to protect against a disease
  • synonym: unvaccinated - not having received a vaccine
