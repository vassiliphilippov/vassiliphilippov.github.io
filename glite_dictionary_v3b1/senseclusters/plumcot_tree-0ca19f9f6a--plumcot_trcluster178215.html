plumcot tree

noun
hybrid tree of plum and apricot
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌm.kɑt ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈplʌm.kɒt ˌtriː / (BrE)
A fruit tree that is a hybrid between a plum (Prunus domestica) and an apricot (Prunus armeniaca), bearing fruits with characteristics of both.

Examples

A plumcot tree fruit
A mature plumcot tree can yield a generous harvest if well cared for.
Farmers are increasingly planting plumcot trees because of their adaptability and unique flavor.
The plumcot tree in her garden produces delicious, juicy fruits every summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pluot - hybrid fruit from plum and apricot, different varieties than plumcot
