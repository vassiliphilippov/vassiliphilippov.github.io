unlockable

adjective
can be unlocked
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɑːkəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɒkəbəl / (BrE)
able to be unlocked or opened, especially with a key, code, or device

Examples

Unlockable door
The new phone has an unlockable screen that responds to your fingerprint.
Only authorized staff have access to the unlockable storage cabinets in the office.
The treasure chest is unlockable if you solve the puzzle and find the key.

Related Terms

  • antonym: locked - secured with a lock and not able to be opened
  • synonym: openable - able to be opened
