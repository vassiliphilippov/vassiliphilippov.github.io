thrombolysis

noun
medical breakdown of blood clots
7.8
Pronunciation: / θrɑːmˈbɑːləsɪs / (AmE), / θrɒmˈbɒlɪsɪs / (BrE)
the dissolution or breaking down of blood clots, especially by medical treatment

Examples

Thrombolysis treatment

Early thrombolysis can significantly improve outcomes after a stroke.
The patient underwent thrombolysis to dissolve the blockage in his artery.
