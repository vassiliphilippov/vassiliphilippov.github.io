via

noun
route or method
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪə / (AmE), / ˈviːə / (BrE)
a route, way, or method of achieving something or reaching a place (sometimes used for ancient roads)

Examples

The via to Rome
The shipment arrived in Paris via London to avoid delays.
You can access the building via the main entrance or the side door.
The information was sent to us via email yesterday.
The messenger traveled along the ancient via to reach the mountain village before sunset.
Archaeologists discovered a well-preserved Roman via beneath the modern city streets.
Merchants used the via to transport goods between distant towns in the empire.

Related Terms

  • antonym: destination - the place to which someone is going
  • synonym: method - a way of doing something
  • synonym: path - a track or way for walking or traveling
  • synonym: route - a way or course taken to get from one place to another
