formlessly

adverb
lacking definite shape or structure
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrmləsli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmləsli / (BrE)
In a way that lacks a definite shape or structure.

Examples

Formlessly without shape
Fog drifted formlessly over the landscape, obscuring the view.
The child's clay sculpture collapsed formlessly before it could be baked.
The fabric hung formlessly from the hanger, showing no sign of structure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amorphously - in a shapeless or unstructured way
