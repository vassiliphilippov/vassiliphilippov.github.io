shake

noun
wooden shingle for roofing or siding
8.9
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪk / (AmE), / ʃeɪk / (BrE)
a split wooden shingle used as siding or roofing material, typically made from cedar or another durable wood.

Examples

Cedar shake roof
Cedar shakes are a popular choice for rustic-style homes.
The roof was covered in weathered shakes that lasted for decades.
They replaced the old siding with new wooden shakes to preserve the house's historic look.
