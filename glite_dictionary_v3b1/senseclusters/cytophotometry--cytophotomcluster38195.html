cytophotometry

noun
quantitative study of cell compounds using a cytophotometer
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪtəfəˈtɑmətri / (AmE), / ˌsʌɪtəfəˈtɒmɪtri / (BrE)
the quantitative study of chemical compounds within cells using a cytophotometer

Examples

Cytophotometry chemical study
Advances in cytophotometry have enabled more detailed chemical mapping of single cells.
Researchers used cytophotometry to determine the concentration of DNA in the tumor cells.
Training in cytophotometry is essential for lab technicians working in cell biology.
