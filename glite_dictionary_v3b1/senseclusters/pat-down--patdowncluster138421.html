pat-down

verb
to search by patting
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpætˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈpætˌdaʊn / (BrE)
to search someone by patting the outside of their clothes, usually to check for hidden items or weapons

Examples

To pat-down a person
The security guard had to pat-down everyone before they entered the building.
At the airport, officers may pat-down passengers if the scanner detects something unusual.
The police pat-down suspects to ensure they are not carrying any dangerous items.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frisk - to search someone by running hands over their clothing
  • synonym: search - to look through or examine someone or something to find something
