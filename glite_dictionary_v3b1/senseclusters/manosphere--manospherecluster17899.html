manosphere

noun
anti-feminist online network
Pronunciation: / ˈmænəˌsfɪr / (AmE), / ˈmænəˌsfɪə / (BrE)
online communities or spaces where men discuss men's issues, masculinity, and gender roles, often including anti-feminist or misogynistic views

Examples

The manosphere online
Many discussions in the manosphere criticize modern feminism and promote traditional gender roles.
Researchers have studied how the manosphere influences attitudes toward women online.
Some websites in the manosphere have been linked to coordinated harassment campaigns.
Many discussions in the manosphere center on topics like fatherhood and men's mental health.
He became interested in the manosphere after searching for advice on relationships and masculinity.
Some critics argue that the manosphere can promote both positive support and controversial views.
