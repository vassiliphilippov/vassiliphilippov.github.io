whaleman

noun
whale hunter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪlmən / (AmE), / ˈweɪlmən / (BrE)
a person who hunts whales or works in the whaling industry, especially on a whaling ship

Examples

A whaleman hunts whales
The experienced whaleman shared stories of dangerous encounters at sea.
In the 19th century, a whaleman could spend years away from home.
The young recruit dreamed of becoming a skilled whaleman like his father.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fisherman - a person who catches fish for a living
  • synonym: whaler - a person or ship involved in hunting whales
