schmatte

noun
old rag or cloth
Pronunciation: / ʃˈmɑtə / (AmE), / ʃˈmætə / (BrE)
an old piece of cloth or rag, especially used in Yiddish-influenced English

A cleaning schmatte

My grandmother always had a schmatte handy for cleaning up spills in the kitchen.
