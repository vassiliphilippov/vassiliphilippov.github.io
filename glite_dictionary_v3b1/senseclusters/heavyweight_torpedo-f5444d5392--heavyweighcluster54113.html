heavyweight torpedo

noun
large torpedo used by submarines
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhev.ɪ.weɪt tɔːr.ˈpiː.doʊ/ (AmE), /ˈhev.ɪ.weɪt tɔː.ˈpiː.dəʊ/ (BrE)
a large, powerful torpedo carrying a warhead of at least 450 kg, typically used by submarines as a standoff weapon

Examples

A heavyweight torpedo weapon
Loading a heavyweight torpedo requires special handling equipment due to its sheer size and mass.
Several navies around the world use the heavyweight torpedo as their principal anti-ship and anti-submarine weapon.
The submarine was equipped with the latest heavyweight torpedo for maximum strike capability.
