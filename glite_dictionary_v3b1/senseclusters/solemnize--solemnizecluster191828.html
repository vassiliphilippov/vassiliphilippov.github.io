solemnize

verb
perform a wedding ceremony formally
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːləmˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɒləmˌnaɪz /; / ˈsɒlɪmnaɪz / (BrE)
to perform a wedding ceremony with the proper formalities or ceremonies

Examples

To solemnize a marriage
Only a registered official may solemnize a legal wedding ceremony in this country.
The priest will solemnize the marriage on Saturday afternoon.
They invited their families and friends to witness the minister solemnize their union.
He was honoured to solemnise the union of his two close friends.
Only officially recognised clergy are permitted to solemnise weddings in this country.
The priest will solemnise the marriage in the cathedral tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celebrate - to perform a religious or formal ceremony
  • synonym: officiate - to act as an official in charge at a ceremony, especially a wedding
