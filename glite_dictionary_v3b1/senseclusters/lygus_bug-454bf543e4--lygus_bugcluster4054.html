lygus bug

noun
plant-feeding insect pest; genus Lygus
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪɡəs bʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪɡəs bʌɡ / (BrE)
A small plant-feeding insect, especially of the genus Lygus, that can act as a vector of viral plant diseases and is a pest of various crops.

Examples

The lygus bug pest
Farmers often struggle with infestations of lygus bug in their cotton fields.
Researchers developed new strategies to control lygus bug populations without pesticides.
The lygus bug can transmit viral diseases to tomatoes, causing significant crop losses.
