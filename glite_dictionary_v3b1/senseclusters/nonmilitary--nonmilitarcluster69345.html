nonmilitary

adjective
not related to the military
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈmɪləˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈmɪlɪtəri / (BrE)
Not connected with or related to soldiers, armed forces, or the military.

Examples

Nonmilitary operations
His role focuses solely on nonmilitary operations within the government.
The conference included both military and nonmilitary experts.
The organization provides nonmilitary aid to countries affected by natural disasters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: civilian - a person not in the armed services; nonmilitary
  • antonym: military - related to or characteristic of soldiers or the armed forces
