architecture

noun
the profession of designing buildings
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃər /; / ˈɑrkɪˌtɛktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈɑːkɪtɛktʃə / (BrE)
the art, science, or profession of designing and constructing buildings and other structures, especially with regard to aesthetics and function
