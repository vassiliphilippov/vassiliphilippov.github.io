the p-word

noun
euphemism for a sensitive 'p' word
9.7
Pronunciation: / ði ˈpiː ˌwɝːd / (AmE), / ði ˈpiː ˌwɜːd / (BrE)
A euphemism used to refer indirectly to a word beginning with 'p' that is considered sensitive, taboo, or offensive.

Examples

Use of the p-word
Some people find even the use of the p-word offensive in public discussions.
Teachers are cautious about mentioning the p-word in front of students.
The politician deliberately avoided saying the p-word on national television.
