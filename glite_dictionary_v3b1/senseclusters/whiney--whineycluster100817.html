Synonyms

  • whiny
    3.9 2.33/m
    whiny is the common standard form used in speech and writing, while whiney is a less common, informal variant.
  • gripey
    8.4 0.01/m
    gripey often means persistent grumbling in an annoyed tone, while whiney suggests childish, high-pitched complaining.
  • querulous
    9.4 0.31/m
    querulous is formal and means habitually complaining in a sulky way, while whiney sounds childish and annoying.
