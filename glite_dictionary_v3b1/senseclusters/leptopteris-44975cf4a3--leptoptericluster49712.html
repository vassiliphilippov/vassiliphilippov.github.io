Leptopteris

noun
a genus of ferns
Pronunciation: / lɛpˈtɑptərɪs / (AmE), / lɛpˈtɒptərɪs / (BrE)
a genus of ferns native to regions such as Australasia, sometimes classified under the genus Todea.

Examples

Leptopteris ferns
Botanists debated whether to include certain ferns in Leptopteris or the genus Todea.
Several species within the genus Leptopteris are prized by fern collectors for their delicate fronds.
The slender, feather-like leaves of Leptopteris make it distinctive among Australasian ferns.
