churchyard

noun
ground surrounding a church, often for burials
3.5 1.19/m

an area of ground adjoining a church, often used as a burial ground

Examples

Synonyms

    1.8 5.46/m
    graveyard is a place for burials often near a church, while churchyard specifically means the ground next to a church.
  • God's acre
    9.9 0.00/m
    God's acre is an old poetic name for a church burial area, while churchyard is the common, neutral word.
  • burial ground
    3.9 0.80/m
    burial ground is a general term for land used to bury the dead, while churchyard is land next to a church.
  • burying ground
    6.1 0.04/m
    burying ground is an older or less common name for a cemetery, while churchyard usually means the graveyard beside a church.
