murderously

adverb
in a violently deadly manner
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝː.dɚ.əs.li /; / ˈmɝːdɚəsli / (AmE), / ˈmɜː.dər.əs.li /; / ˈmɜːdərəsli / (BrE)
in a way that shows or suggests a strong intention to seriously harm or kill; with violent or deadly intent
