sweet

adjective
kind and angelic in nature
Pronunciation: / swit / (AmE), / swiːt / (BrE)
having a kind, gentle, and endearing nature; resembling the innocence or goodness of an angel

Examples

Sweet little child
His sweet nature made him everyone's favorite in the class.
People often describe her as a sweet soul who never has a harsh word for anyone.
She is known for her sweet disposition and always puts others at ease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angelic - having the qualities of an angel; very kind and pure
  • synonym: cherubic - innocent-looking and sweet like a cherub
  • antonym: harsh - unpleasantly rough or cruel in character
