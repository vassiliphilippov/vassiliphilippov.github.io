cam

verb
to use cam mechanism
7.2
Pronunciation: / kæm / (AmE), / kæm / (BrE)
to rotate or move a part using a cam mechanism, especially in machinery or engines

Examples

To cam an engine
The mechanic cammed the engine to increase its horsepower.
After the machine was cammed, its movements became much smoother.
They cam the valve system to optimize fuel efficiency in the new model.
