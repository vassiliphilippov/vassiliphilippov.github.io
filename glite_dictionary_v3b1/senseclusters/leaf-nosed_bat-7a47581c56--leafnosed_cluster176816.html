leaf-nosed bat

noun
bat with a leaflike nose flap
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlif ˌnoʊzd bæt / (AmE), / ˈliːf ˌnəʊzd bæt / (BrE)
A bat that has a leaflike flap of skin on the end of its nose, especially those belonging to the families Phyllostomatidae, Rhinolophidae, and Hipposideridae.

Examples

A leaf-nosed bat
Scientists have discovered a new species of leaf-nosed bat in the rainforest.
The leaf-nosed bat uses its nose flap to help it navigate and locate prey.
Unlike other bats, the leaf-nosed bat has a distinctive protrusion on its nose.

  • synonym: bat - a flying nocturnal mammal
