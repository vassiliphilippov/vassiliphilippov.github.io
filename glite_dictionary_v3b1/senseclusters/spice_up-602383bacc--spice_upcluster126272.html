spice up

verb
make more interesting or lively
1.2
Pronunciation: / spaɪs ʌp / (AmE), / spaɪs ʌp / (BrE)
to make something more interesting, exciting, or lively

The chef decided to spice up the soup with some fresh herbs and chili flakes.
They played upbeat music to spice up the atmosphere at the event.
Try adding a new activity to spice up your daily routine.
