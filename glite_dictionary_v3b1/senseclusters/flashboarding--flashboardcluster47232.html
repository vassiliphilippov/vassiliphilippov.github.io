flashboarding

noun
boarding on a dam to increase height
Pronunciation: / ˈflæʃˌbɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflæʃˌbɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
A boarding or structure placed along the top of a dam to increase its height temporarily and raise the water level.

Examples

Dam flashboarding
After heavy rains, the crew checked the flashboarding to ensure the dam could safely contain the higher water.
Dam maintenance includes removing old flashboarding before flood season begins.
Temporary flashboarding was installed to raise the reservoir level for irrigation season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flashboard - a board used at the top of a dam to control water level
