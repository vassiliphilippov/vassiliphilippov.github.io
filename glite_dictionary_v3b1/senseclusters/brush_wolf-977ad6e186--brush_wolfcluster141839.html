brush wolf

noun
a small North American wolf (coyote)
9.9
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ wʊlf / (AmE), / brʌʃ wʊlf / (BrE)
a small wolf native to western North America, also known as the coyote

Examples

The brush wolf howls
A brush wolf can often be heard howling on quiet nights in the prairie.
Early settlers sometimes mistook the brush wolf for a young gray wolf.
The ranchers reported seeing a brush wolf lurking near their cattle at dusk.
