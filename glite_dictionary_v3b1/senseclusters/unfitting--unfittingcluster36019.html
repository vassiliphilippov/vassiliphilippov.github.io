unfitting

adjective
not appropriate; unsuitable
2.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not suitable or appropriate for a particular purpose, situation, or occasion

Examples

An unfitting choice
His casual attire was unfitting for such a formal ceremony.
It seemed unfitting to laugh during the solemn occasion.
The painting's bright colors are unfitting for a calming bedroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inappropriate - not suitable or proper in the circumstances
  • synonym: inapt - not suitable or appropriate in the circumstances
  • antonym: suitable - appropriate for a particular purpose, person, or occasion
