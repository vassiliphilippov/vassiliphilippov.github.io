vital organ

noun
organ essential for life
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪtəl ˈɔrɡən / (AmE), / ˈvaɪt(ə)l ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
an organ in the body that is necessary for life, such as the heart, lungs, or brain

Examples

A vital organ like heart
Damage to a vital organ such as the heart can be life-threatening.
During the surgery, the doctors worked carefully to avoid any vital organs.
The bullet missed all vital organs, which greatly increased his chances of survival.
