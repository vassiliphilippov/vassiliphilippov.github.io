Yelper

noun
one who yelps
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛlpər / (AmE), / ˈjɛlpə / (BrE)
a person or animal that gives a short, high-pitched cry, often from pain, fear, or surprise

Examples

A noisy Yelper
The puppy was a constant Yelper whenever someone knocked on the door.
As soon as the thunder started, the little Yelper hid under the bed and whimpered.
The vet recognized the Yelper by its distinctive high-pitched cries during checkups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barker - an animal, especially a dog, that barks
  • synonym: crier - someone or something that cries out loudly
