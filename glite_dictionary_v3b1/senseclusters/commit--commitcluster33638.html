commit

verb
to carry out or perform an act
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈmɪt/ (AmE), /kə.ˈmɪt/ (BrE)
to give, dedicate, or put yourself or your resources (such as time, money, or effort) into something or someone

Examples

To commit time
He wants to commit himself fully to the project and see it succeed.
The company was accused of having committed numerous violations of environmental laws.
