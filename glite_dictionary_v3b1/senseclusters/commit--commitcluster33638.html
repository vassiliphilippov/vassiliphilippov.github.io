Synonyms

  • devote
    3.7 40/m
    devote means giving your time, effort, or energy fully to a person or cause, while commit means offering yourself or resources generally.
  • dedicate
    3.7 21/m
    dedicate means giving time or work completely to a person, project, or cause, while commit means to give resources or yourself more broadly.
  • invest
    3.5 52/m
    invest means putting money, time, or effort in with hope of gain, while commit means giving yourself or resources without that profit focus.
