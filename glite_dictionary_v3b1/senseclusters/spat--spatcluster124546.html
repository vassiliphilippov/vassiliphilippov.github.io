spat

verb
mollusk/oyster: attach to surface
6.6
Pronunciation: / spæt / (AmE), / spæt / (BrE)
(of mollusks or oysters) to become permanently attached to a surface after the larval stage.

Examples

Oysters spat on rocks
After the larvae mature, they will spat and become fixed in place.
The presence of clean shells encourages oysters to spat in aquaculture farms.
Young oysters eventually spat onto rocks or other hard surfaces.
