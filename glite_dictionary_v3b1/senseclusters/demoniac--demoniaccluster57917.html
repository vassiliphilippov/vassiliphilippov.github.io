demoniac

noun
person said to be possessed by a demon
5.3 8.8
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈmoʊ.nɪ.æk/ /; / /dɪ.ˈmoʊ.naɪ.æk/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈməʊ.nɪ.æk/ /; / /dɪ.ˈməʊ.naɪ.æk/ (BrE)
A person who behaves as if possessed by a demon; one believed to be controlled or driven by evil spirits.

Examples

A wild demoniac

The priest offered prayers to help the demoniac find peace.
The villagers believed that the wild-eyed demoniac was responsible for the strange happenings.
