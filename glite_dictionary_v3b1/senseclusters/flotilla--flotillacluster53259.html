flotilla

noun
group of small ships or boats
6.1 5.8
Pronunciation: /flə.ˈtɪl.ə/ /; / /floʊ.ˈtɪl.ə/ (AmE), /flə.ˈtɪl.ə/ /; / /flɒ.ˈtɪl.ə/ (BrE)
a group of small ships, boats, or similar vehicles (such as drones) operating together as a fleet

Examples

Engineers coordinated three flotillas of agricultural drones to survey the region and spray fertilizer.
