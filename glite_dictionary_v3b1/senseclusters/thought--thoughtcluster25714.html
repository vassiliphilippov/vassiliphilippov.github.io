Examples

Deep thought

Synonyms

  • thinking
    1.8 72/m
    thinking is the active process of using your mind to consider things, while thought can mean that process or a single idea.
  • notion
    3.7 60/m
    notion is a vague or simple idea that someone has, while thought covers clear ideas, opinions, or the act of thinking.
  • opinion
    1.0 119/m
    opinion is a personal judgment often about facts or feelings, while thought can mean any idea, belief, or mental process.
  • view
    1.5 181/m
    view is a personal way of seeing something, often stated publicly, while thought can be any private idea or the thinking process.
