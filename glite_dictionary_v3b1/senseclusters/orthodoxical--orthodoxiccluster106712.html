orthodoxical

adjective
of Orthodox Church
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrθəˈdɑksɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəˈdɒksɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the beliefs, practices, or institutions of the Orthodox Church, especially Eastern Orthodox Christianity

Examples

Orthodoxical church
The ancient church is famous for its orthodoxical architecture and religious icons.
Many orthodoxical traditions are still observed in the monastery today.
He wrote a book about the orthodoxical teachings of Eastern Christianity.
