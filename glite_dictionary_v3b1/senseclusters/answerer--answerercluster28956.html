answerer

noun
person who responds
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.sər.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈɑːn.sər.ər / (BrE)
a person who responds to a question, request, or challenge

Examples

The answerer responds
As the answerer hesitated, the audience waited in silence.
In the interview, each answerer was given two minutes to respond.
The answerer provided detailed explanations to all the questions.
