herbary

noun
book of herbs
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜːrbəri / (AmE), / ˈhɜːbəri / (BrE)
a book that describes herbs and their properties, especially their medicinal uses; a herbal, mainly historical

Examples

Old herbary book
The botanist treasured an ancient herbary filled with detailed illustrations of medicinal plants.
She discovered a rare herbary in the library's special collections section.
In medieval times, a herbary was an essential reference for healers and apothecaries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herbal - a book listing and describing herbs and their uses
  • synonym: pharmacopoeia - an official book listing medicinal drugs and their effects
