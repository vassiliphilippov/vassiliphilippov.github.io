artful

adjective
cunning; not straightforward
6.9 1.80/m

clever or skillful, especially in a sly, cunning, or indirect way

Examples

An artful explanation
He gave an artful explanation that avoided the main issue entirely.
Her artful questions managed to uncover secrets without ever seeming intrusive.
The artful politician skillfully dodged direct answers during the debate.
He offered an artful reply that avoided answering the reporter's question directly.
Her artful handling of the negotiations impressed everyone at the meeting.
The artful arrangement of colors in the painting drew the viewer's eye instantly.
