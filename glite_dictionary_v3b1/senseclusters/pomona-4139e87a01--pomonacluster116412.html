Pomona

proper noun
place name; Roman goddess
8.0
Pronunciation: / poʊˈmoʊnə / (AmE), / pəˈməʊnə / (BrE)
a proper name used for various places, especially cities, and for the Roman goddess of fruit in mythology

Examples

Pomona city festival
The city of Pomona is known for its annual art festival.
In Roman mythology, Pomona was the goddess who protected fruit trees and gardens.
Many towns in the United States are named Pomona after the Roman goddess.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ceres - the Roman goddess of agriculture, grain crops, and fertility
  • synonym: Demeter - the Greek goddess of agriculture and harvest
