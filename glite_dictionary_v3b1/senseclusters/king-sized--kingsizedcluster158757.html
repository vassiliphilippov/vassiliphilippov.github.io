king-sized

adjective
extra large in size
4.3 2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋ.saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋ.saɪzd / (BrE)
Of a size larger than the standard; especially used to describe a bed or item that is extra large.

Examples

A king-sized bed
He ordered a king-sized burger that was almost too big to eat.
They replaced the couch with a king-sized one to fit their entire family.
We bought a king-sized bed for the guest room to ensure maximum comfort.
