Mahonia

noun
a genus of evergreen shrubs and small trees
9.7
Pronunciation: / məˈhoʊniə / (AmE), / məˈhəʊniə / (BrE)
a genus of evergreen shrubs and small trees native to North and Central America and Asia, known for their spiny or pinnate leaves and yellow flowers
