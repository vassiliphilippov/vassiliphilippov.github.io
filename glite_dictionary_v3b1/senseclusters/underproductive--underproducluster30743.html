underproductive

adjective
not producing enough
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərprəˈdʌktɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌndəprəˈdʌktɪv / (BrE)
producing less than is expected, needed, or normal

Examples

An underproductive team
The team was underproductive this quarter and failed to meet its sales targets.
If a farm is underproductive, it may need better soil or new equipment.
She worried that her underproductive habits would affect her performance review.

Related Terms

  • antonym: productive - producing a large amount or achieving a lot
  • synonym: unproductive - not producing or achieving much
