library

noun
collection of books for use or borrowing
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪˌbrɛri / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.brəri / (BrE)
a collection of books or other literary works kept for reference or borrowing

Examples

A library collection
Members are allowed to borrow up to five books from the library at a time.
She spent her afternoon doing research in the university library.
The public library has an extensive collection of rare manuscripts.
