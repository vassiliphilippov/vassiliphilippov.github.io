morbidity

noun
gloomy or unhealthy state of mind
8.4 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / m…îÀêrÀàb…™d…ôti / (AmE), / m…îÀêÀàb…™d…™ti / (BrE)
A state of mind characterized by excessive gloominess or preoccupation with unwholesome or disturbing subjects.

Examples

A sense of morbidity
He spoke with a morbidity that made his friends worry about his mental health.
Her poems are often marked by a subtle morbidity that unsettles the reader.
There is a certain morbidity to his fascination with tragic events.

Synonyms

  • morbidness
    8.8 0.02/m
    morbidness is the general state of being unhealthily interested in unpleasant topics, while morbidity describes a gloomy, preoccupied state of mind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: melancholy - a deep, persistent sadness or gloom
