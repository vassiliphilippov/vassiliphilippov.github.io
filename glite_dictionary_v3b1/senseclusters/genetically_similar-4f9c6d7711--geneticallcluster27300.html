genetically similar

adjective
having similar genetic traits
1.0
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈnɛtɪkli ˈsɪmələr / (AmE), / dʒɪˈnɛtɪkli ˈsɪmɪlə / (BrE)
having similar genetic traits or genetic makeup

Examples

Genetically similar species
Chimpanzees are genetically similar to humans, sharing over 98% of our DNA.
Researchers found that the plants in this region are genetically similar due to years of cross-pollination.
The two breeds are genetically similar, but their appearance is quite different.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genetically distinct - having different genetic traits
  • degree higher: genetically identical - having exactly the same genetic traits
