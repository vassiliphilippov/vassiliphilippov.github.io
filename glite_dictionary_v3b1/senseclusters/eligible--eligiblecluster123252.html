eligible

adjective
qualified or suitable to be chosen
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlɪdʒəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪdʒɪbəl / (BrE)
having the right qualifications or meeting the necessary conditions to do or be chosen for something

Examples

Eligible to vote
Only citizens over the age of 18 are eligible to vote in the election.
She became eligible for the scholarship after her second year at university.
The organization is searching for the most eligible candidate for the position.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ineligible - not qualified or suitable to be chosen
