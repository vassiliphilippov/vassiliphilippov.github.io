identity theft

noun
fraudulent use of another person's personal information
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdɛntəti θɛft / (AmE), / aɪˈdɛntɪti θɛft / (BrE)
The fraudulent acquisition and use of someone else's personal information, such as their name or financial details, without their knowledge.

Examples

Identity theft crime
After the data breach, several customers became victims of identity theft.
Banks are urging people to protect themselves from identity theft by never sharing their PINs.
He reported the identity theft to the police as soon as he noticed suspicious activity on his credit card.

  • synonym: fraud - wrongful or criminal deception for financial or personal gain
