finger-paint

verb
apply paint with fingers
2.8 3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡərˌpeɪnt / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌpeɪnt / (BrE)
to apply paint to a surface using the fingers, rather than a brush or other tools, especially as a creative or artistic activity

Examples

To finger-paint bright colors
Children love to finger-paint bright colors onto large pieces of paper.
In preschool, we often finger-paint to explore shapes and textures.
She finger-painted a sunny landscape during her art class.
