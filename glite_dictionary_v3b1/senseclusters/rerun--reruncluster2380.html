rerun

verb
run again as a candidate
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈrʌn / (AmE), / ˌriːˈrʌn / (BrE)
to run as a candidate for office again after previously holding or seeking the position

Examples

To rerun for office

If he chooses to rerun for office, he'll face tough competition from new candidates.
The mayor announced she would rerun in hopes of securing another term.
