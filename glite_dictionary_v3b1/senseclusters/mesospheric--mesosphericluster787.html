mesospheric

adjective
of the mesosphere
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛsəˈsfɪrɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛsəˈsfɪərɪk / (BrE)
relating to the mesosphere, the deep region of Earth's mantle beneath the asthenosphere in geology

Examples

Mesospheric mantle
The mesospheric region plays a crucial role in the movement of tectonic plates.
Scientists study mesospheric properties to better understand the Earth's internal structure.
Recent research revealed new details about the mesospheric composition beneath the Pacific Ocean.

Related Terms

  • antonym: asthenospheric - relating to the asthenosphere, the upper layer of the Earth's mantle
  • synonym: mantle - the layer of the Earth between the crust and the core
