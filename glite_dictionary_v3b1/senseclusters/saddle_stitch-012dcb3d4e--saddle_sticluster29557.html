saddle stitch

noun
decorative sewing or embroidery stitch
Pronunciation: / ˈsædəl stɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈsædl stɪtʃ / (BrE)
a decorative sewing or embroidery stitch resembling an overcast or running stitch, often done in a contrasting color

Examples

Careful saddle stitching
Careful saddle stitching made the homemade napkins look professionally finished.
She added a saddle stitch around the edge of the pillow as a decorative detail.
The saddle stitch in red thread gave the white fabric an eye-catching border.
