Lahu language

noun
a language of the Lahu ethnic group in China and nearby
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑ hu ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈlɑː huː ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Loloish language spoken primarily in Southwest Yunnan province of China and surrounding regions; the language of the Lahu people

Examples

Lahu language spoken
He is fluent in both Mandarin and the Lahu language.
Linguists have published several studies on the structure of the Lahu language.
The Lahu language is an important part of the Lahu people's cultural heritage.
