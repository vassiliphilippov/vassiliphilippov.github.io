mombie

noun
exhausted mother from lack of sleep
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːm.bi / (AmE), / ˈmɒm.bi / (BrE)
A mother who is extremely tired and absent-minded due to lack of sleep, especially from caring for a child.

Examples

A mombie mother
After staying up all week with her sick child, she turned into a mombie by Friday.
If I don't get any rest tonight, I'll turn into a mombie at work tomorrow.
Many new mothers joke that they've turned into a mombie during those sleepless first months.

Related Terms
