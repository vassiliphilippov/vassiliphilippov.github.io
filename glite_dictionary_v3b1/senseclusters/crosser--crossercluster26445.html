crosser

noun
player delivering crosses
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɔː.sər/ /; / /ˈkrɑː.sər/ (AmE), /ˈkrɒ.sə/ (BrE)
a player who delivers a cross, especially a wide pass into the penalty area, in team sports such as football (soccer)

Examples

A skilled crosser
The team's best crosser set up two goals with precise passes from the right wing.
As a skilled crosser, she often creates scoring opportunities for her teammates.
Coaches value a reliable crosser who can consistently deliver the ball into the box.
