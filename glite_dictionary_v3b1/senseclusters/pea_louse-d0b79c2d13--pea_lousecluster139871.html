pea louse

noun
pest insect infesting pea plants
Pronunciation: / piː laʊs / (AmE), / piː laʊs / (BrE)
a small insect that infests and feeds on pea plants, often regarded as a pest in agriculture and gardening

Examples

Pea louse infestation
Gardeners often inspect pea plants for signs of pea louse infestation.
The farmer lost much of his crop to pea louse damage last season.
The use of natural predators can help control the spread of pea louse in organic gardens.
