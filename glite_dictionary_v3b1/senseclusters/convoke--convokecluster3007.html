convoke

verb
formally call together
5.2
Pronunciation: / kənˈvoʊk / (AmE), / kənˈvəʊk / (BrE)
to call people together for a meeting or assembly, usually formally

Examples

To convoke a meeting
The chairman decided to convoke a special session to address the urgent issues.
The president may convoke Parliament in times of crisis.
The school board will convoke a meeting of parents next week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convene - to come together or bring people together for a meeting
  • antonym: dismiss - to send people away from a place or meeting
