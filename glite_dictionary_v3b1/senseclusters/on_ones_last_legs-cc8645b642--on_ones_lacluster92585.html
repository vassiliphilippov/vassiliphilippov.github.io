on one's last legs

adjective
close to collapse or exhaustion
4.6
Pronunciation: / ɑn wʌnz læst lɛɡz / (AmE), / ɒn wʌnz lɑːst lɛɡz / (BrE)
very tired, weak, or close to collapse, especially after exertion or long use

Examples

On my last legs after running
After the marathon, I was on my last legs and could barely walk.
By the time the meeting ended, everyone felt on their last legs.
The old truck is on its last legs and needs to be replaced soon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhausted - extremely tired
  • antonym: fresh - not tired or fatigued; full of energy
