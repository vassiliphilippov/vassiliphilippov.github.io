superkid attitude

noun
exceptional positive mindset
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsuː.pɚ.kɪd ˈæt̬.ɪ.tuːd / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pə.kɪd ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
