piccata

noun
Italian meat dish
Pronunciation: / pɪˈkɑːtə / (AmE), / pɪˈkɑːtə / (BrE)
an Italian dish of thinly sliced meat, usually chicken or veal, cooked in a lemon and caper sauce

Examples

Chicken piccata dish
For dinner, she prepared a delicious piccata using fresh chicken breasts and a tangy lemon sauce.
The restaurant's veal piccata is served with a generous portion of capers and parsley.
He learned to make piccata while taking a cooking class in Italy last summer.
