standard

noun
a board measure (1980 board feet)
Pronunciation: / ˈstændərd / (AmE), / ˈstændəd / (BrE)
A fixed unit of measure for sawn timber, equal to 1980 board feet.

Examples

A standard of timber
Each standard of timber must be measured before shipment overseas.
The company sold oak planks by the standard to large-scale buyers.
The shipment contained over twenty standards of high-quality pine lumber.
