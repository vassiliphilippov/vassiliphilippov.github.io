rein

noun
strap for guiding a horse
5.5
Pronunciation: / reɪn / (AmE), / reɪn / (BrE)
one of a pair of long narrow straps attached to a horse's bit or headpiece, used to guide or control the animal

Examples

Horse rein
He gently pulled the rein to slow the horse to a walk.
She let the reins fall loosely while the horse grazed.
The reins snapped as the horse bolted suddenly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bridle - the headgear used to control a horse, including bit, reins, and straps
