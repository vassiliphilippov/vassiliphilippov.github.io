Synonyms

  • mental attitude
    4.1 0.2/m
    mental attitude is a more formal phrase about a person's thoughts and readiness to act, while attitude is the everyday word for feelings and behavior.
  • mindset
    4.3 11/m
    mindset describes a habitual way of thinking that shapes responses, while attitude covers a wider mix of feeling and outward behavior.
  • outlook
    3.5 11/m
    outlook is a general view of life or the future, while attitude refers to a settled feeling or way of behaving about specific things.
