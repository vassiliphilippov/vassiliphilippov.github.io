kingdom

noun
sphere where something dominates
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋdəm / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm / (BrE)
a domain, area, or sphere where a particular thing is dominant or prevailing, often used figuratively

Examples

Business kingdom
For years, the company maintained its kingdom in the world of mobile technology.
His imagination ruled over a vast kingdom of fantastical stories and characters.
She found peace in the tranquil kingdom of her garden each morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domain - a specified sphere of activity or knowledge
  • synonym: realm - an area or domain, especially one related to abstract ideas
