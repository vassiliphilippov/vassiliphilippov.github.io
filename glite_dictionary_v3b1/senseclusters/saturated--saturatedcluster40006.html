saturated

adjective
completely soaked
Pronunciation: / ˈsætʃəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈsætʃəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
completely filled with or soaked in a liquid or another substance so that no more can be absorbed

Examples

Saturated ground
After the heavy rain, the ground was saturated with water.
The sponge became saturated and could not absorb any more liquid.
Her clothes were saturated after she got caught in the storm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dry - not wet or containing no moisture
  • synonym: soaked - completely wet or filled with liquid
