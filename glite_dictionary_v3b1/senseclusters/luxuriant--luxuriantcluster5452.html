luxuriant

adjective
rich and abundant growth
Pronunciation: / lʌgˈʒʊriənt / (AmE), / lʌgˈzjʊəriənt / (BrE)
(of plants or hair) growing thickly and abundantly; lush and rich in growth.

Examples

Luxuriant hair
After the heavy rains, the hillside became covered in luxuriant green grasses.
She admired the luxuriant hair that fell in waves down his back.
The garden was filled with luxuriant ferns and vibrant flowering shrubs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lush - (of plant growth) rich and profuse; luxuriant
  • antonym: sparse - thinly dispersed or scattered; the opposite of luxuriant for growth
