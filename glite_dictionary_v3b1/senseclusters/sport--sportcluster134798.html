sport

verb
play or frolic energetically
Pronunciation: / spɔrt / (AmE), / spɔːt / (BrE)
to play or frolic in a lively, energetic, or boisterous way

Examples

Children sport in water
Children sported in the fountain, splashing water everywhere on a hot summer day.
The lambs sport around the field at sunrise, full of playful joy.
The puppies sport on the lawn every afternoon, chasing each other with boundless energy.
