metacarpal

adjective
relating to the bones of the hand
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈkɑrpəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈkɑːpəl / (BrE)
Relating to the metacarpus, the group of five bones of the hand between the wrist and the fingers.

Examples

Metacarpal region pain
A break in a metacarpal bone can seriously affect hand function.
Protective gear can help prevent metacarpal injuries in athletes.
The surgeon examined the metacarpal region for any signs of fracture.

Related Terms
