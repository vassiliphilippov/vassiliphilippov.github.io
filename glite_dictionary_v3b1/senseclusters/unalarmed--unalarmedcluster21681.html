unalarmed

adjective
not worried or warned
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈlɑrmd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈlɑːmd / (BrE)
not feeling worried, frightened, or disturbed, especially in a situation that might cause concern

Examples

Unalarmed person
Despite the loud noise outside, she remained unalarmed and continued reading her book.
The hikers were unalarmed by the distant thunder, believing the storm would pass them by.
He walked into the dark room unalarmed, unaware that a surprise party was waiting for him.
Despite the sudden thunderstorm, the children remained unalarmed and continued playing indoors.
He was unalarmed by the unexpected announcement and calmly asked for more details.
The dog stayed unalarmed even when strangers entered the house.
