master of ceremonies

noun
event host or presenter
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmæstɚ əv ˈsɛrəˌmoʊniz / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstə əv ˈsɛrɪˌməʊniz / (BrE)
a person who acts as host at a formal event, making introductions and guiding proceedings

Examples

Master of ceremonies at event
He prepared carefully for his role as master of ceremonies at the charity gala.
She served as the master of ceremonies for the conference, welcoming guests and introducing the speakers.
The awards show always chooses a popular comedian as its master of ceremonies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emcee - a person who acts as host at formal occasions; abbreviation of master of ceremonies
