nondangerousness

noun
lack of danger
7.9
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈdeɪndʒərəsnəs / (AmE), / nɒnˈdeɪndʒərəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of not being dangerous or posing no threat of harm

Examples

The nondangerousness of chemicals
The nondangerousness of the chemical was confirmed by several independent studies.
Her lawyer argued for her release based on her nondangerousness to the community.
The product's nondangerousness makes it suitable for use around children and pets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dangerousness - the quality of being likely to cause harm or danger
  • synonym: harmlessness - the quality of causing no harm
  • synonym: safety - the condition of being protected from harm or danger
