mesmerize

verb
captivate or fascinate completely
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛzməˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɛzməraɪz / (BrE)
to capture and hold the complete attention of someone as if by magic; to fascinate deeply

Examples

To mesmerize an audience
Children are often mesmerized by the dazzling lights of the city.
Her beautiful voice mesmerized everyone in the room, making them pause in silence.
The magician seemed to mesmerize the entire audience with his performance.
Children were mesmerised by the bright colors and fast movements of the parade.
His deep voice seemed to mesmerise everyone in the room.
The magician managed to mesmerise the entire crowd with her performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bore - to cause someone to feel weary and uninterested
  • synonym: captivate - to attract and hold the interest and attention of; charm
  • synonym: fascinate - to attract and hold the attention of someone deeply
