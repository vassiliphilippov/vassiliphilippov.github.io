ornately

adverb
in a highly decorated manner

In a way that is highly or elaborately decorated.

Examples

To ornately decorate
Each column in the cathedral was ornately carved to depict scenes from mythology.
She wore a dress that was ornately embroidered with silver thread and tiny crystals.
The ballroom was ornately decorated with golden chandeliers and intricate tapestries.

Synonyms

  • elaborately
    4.5 1.63/m
    elaborately describes careful, detailed decoration or effort, while ornately suggests richly decorated surfaces or objects with much ornament.
  • baroquely
    9.1 0.02/m
    baroquely means decorated in an old Baroque style, while ornately means highly decorated but without a specific historical style.
  • intricately
    6.3 1.50/m
    intricately shows many small connected details and complexity, while ornately highlights showy decoration and surface ornament.
  • fancily
    3.9 0.03/m
    fancily looks stylish or decorative in a casual way, while ornately suggests careful, rich decoration often in art or architecture.
