degree day

noun
unit estimating heating or cooling needs
8.8 5.5
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈgriː deɪ/ /; / /də.ˈgriː deɪ/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈgriː deɪ/ /; / /də.ˈgriː deɪ/ (BrE)
a unit for measuring the extent to which the outside temperature falls below (or rises above) a standard level, used to estimate heating or cooling requirements in buildings
