car race

noun
competition involving racing cars
1.0
Pronunciation: / kɑr reɪs / (AmE), / kɑː reɪs / (BrE)
a competition in which cars, typically high-performance, race against each other to reach a finish line first

Examples

A car race event
He dreams of competing in a professional car race one day, inspired by his favorite drivers.
Spectators cheered as the car race sped into its final, dramatic lap.
The city hosted its first international car race last summer, drawing crowds from all over the country.

  • synonym: motor race - a race involving motor vehicles, especially cars
