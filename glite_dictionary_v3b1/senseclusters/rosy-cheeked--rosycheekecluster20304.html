rosy-cheeked

adjective
having pink or red, healthy-looking cheeks
2.8 0.13/m
Pronunciation: / Ààro äzi t Éikt / (AmE), / Ààr…ô äzi t ÉiÀêkt / (BrE)
(of a person) having cheeks that are pinkish or red, typically as a sign of health or freshness.

Examples

Rosy-cheeked child
After spending a few minutes in the cold, his face became rosy-cheeked.
She was a rosy-cheeked woman, always smiling and full of life.
