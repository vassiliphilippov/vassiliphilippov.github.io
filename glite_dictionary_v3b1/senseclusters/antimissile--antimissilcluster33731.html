antimissile

noun
missile defense weapon
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈmɪsəl / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈmɪsaɪl / (BrE)
a weapon or defense system designed to detect, intercept, and destroy incoming missiles

Examples

Antimissile system
The government invested in a new antimissile system to protect the capital from attacks.
Engineers are working to improve the accuracy of the antimissile technology.
During the demonstration, the antimissile successfully intercepted a simulated threat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interceptor - a missile or aircraft designed to destroy enemy missiles or aircraft
  • antonym: missile - a weapon that is self-propelled and guided to a target
