concentrator

noun
mineral processing plant
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnsənˌtreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsənˌtreɪtə / (BrE)
a facility or device that separates valuable minerals from ore to produce a higher-grade concentrate

Examples

Ore concentrator
The mining company built a new concentrator to improve the efficiency of ore processing.
After extraction, the ore is sent to the concentrator for separation of valuable minerals.
Modern concentrators use advanced technology to maximize the yield of precious metals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: refinery - a facility where raw materials are purified
  • synonym: smelter - a plant where metal is extracted from ore by heating
