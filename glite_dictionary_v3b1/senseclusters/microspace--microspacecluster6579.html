microspace

noun
compact living area
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌspeɪs / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌspeɪs / (BrE)
a very small living or working area designed to use space efficiently, often found in urban environments

Examples

Urban microspace
She decorated her microspace with foldable furniture to make the most of every inch.
Living in a microspace requires creative storage solutions and careful organization.
Many young professionals choose a microspace in the city to save on rent and commute time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mansion - a very large and luxurious house
  • synonym: microapartment - a very small apartment designed for maximum efficiency
  • synonym: studio - a small apartment with a combined living and sleeping area
