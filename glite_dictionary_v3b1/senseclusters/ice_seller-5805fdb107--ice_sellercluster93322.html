ice seller

noun
person who cuts and delivers ice
2.9
Pronunciation: / aɪs ˈsɛlɚ / (AmE), / aɪs ˈsɛlə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to cut and deliver ice, especially before the widespread use of refrigeration

Examples

The ice seller delivers
During summer, the children eagerly awaited the ice seller's cart.
In the past, an ice seller was a common sight in the village.
The ice seller arrived each morning before the sun grew too warm.
