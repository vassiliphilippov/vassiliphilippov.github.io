conversionist

noun
data format converter
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɜːrʒənɪst / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who converts documents, files, or data from one format to another

Examples

File conversionist
The company hired a conversionist to update all their old files to a modern format.
As a skilled conversionist, she quickly transformed the scanned documents into editable text.
We needed a conversionist to help migrate our data to the new system.
