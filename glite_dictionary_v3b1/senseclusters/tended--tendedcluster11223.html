tended

adjective
looked after by a caretaker
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛn.dɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.dɪd / (BrE)
Having a caretaker, watcher, or someone who looks after or manages it.

Examples

A well-tended garden
A well-tended garden can be the pride of any homeowner.
Many animals thrive best in tended enclosures where their needs are met daily.
The patients in the recovery room were always tended by skilled nurses.
