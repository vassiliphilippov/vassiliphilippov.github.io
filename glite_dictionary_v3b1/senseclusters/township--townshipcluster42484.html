township

noun
small rural community
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊnʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈtaʊnʃɪp / (BrE)
a small town or community, especially one located outside a city or in a rural area

Examples

A rural township
She moved to a peaceful township to escape the noise of the city.
The township organizes a festival every summer for local residents.
Many families in the township work together to maintain the community park.

Related Terms

  • antonym: city - a large and important town
  • synonym: village - a small group of houses in a country area, smaller than a town
