sizzler

noun
very hot day
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪzlər / (AmE), / ˈsɪzlə / (BrE)
something, especially a day, that is extremely hot, or something that is very exciting or impressive

Examples

A sizzler day
Yesterday was a real sizzler with temperatures over 100 degrees.
The new movie is a sizzler that kept everyone on the edge of their seats.
Bring plenty of water if you plan to be outside during a sizzler like this.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chiller - something that is very cold
  • synonym: heatwave - a period of unusually hot weather
  • synonym: scorcher - an extremely hot day
