fuzzball

noun
small ball of fuzz
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌzbɔl / (AmE), / ˈfʌzbɔːl / (BrE)
a small, soft ball made of loose fibers, lint, or fuzz

Examples

A soft fuzzball
The kitten chased a fuzzball across the floor, batting it with her paws.

He rolled a tiny fuzzball between his fingers while thinking about his homework.
