Juncaginaceae

noun
family of monocotyledonous bog herbs
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒʌŋkəˌdʒɪˈneɪsiː /; / dʒʌŋˌkeɪdʒɪˈneɪsi.i / (AmE), / ˌdʒʌŋkəˌdʒɪˈneɪsiː /; / dʒʌŋˌkeɪɡɪˈneɪsiː.iː / (BrE)
