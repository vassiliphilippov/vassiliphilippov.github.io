stain

noun
mark of discoloration
Pronunciation: / steɪn / (AmE), / steɪn / (BrE)
a patch or mark of discoloration, especially one that is difficult to remove

Examples

A coffee stain
Removing the coffee stain from the carpet proved to be challenging.
The old book's pages were yellowed and marked with a water stain in the corner.
There was a noticeable stain on the front of her shirt after lunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blemish - a small mark or flaw that spoils the appearance of something
  • synonym: spot - a small round mark or stain
