spleenwort

noun

8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspliːnwɜːrt / (AmE), / ˈspliːnwɜːt / (BrE)
any of a group of ferns, chiefly growing on rocks or walls, of the genus Asplenium

Examples

The spleenwort fern
