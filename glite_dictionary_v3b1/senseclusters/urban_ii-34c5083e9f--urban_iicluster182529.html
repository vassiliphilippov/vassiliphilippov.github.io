Urban II

proper noun
Pope who launched the First Crusade
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɝːbən ðə ˌsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈɜːbən ðə ˌsɛkənd / (BrE)
(c. 1042–1099) The pope from 1088 to 1099, known for calling for the First Crusade in a sermon at the Council of Clermont.

Examples

Urban II pope
Urban II is best remembered for initiating the First Crusade in 1095.
Many historians study how Urban II shaped the course of medieval Europe.
The speech delivered by Urban II at Clermont inspired thousands to join the crusade.
