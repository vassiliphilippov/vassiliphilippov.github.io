Dictyoptera

noun
insect group: cockroaches and mantids
Pronunciation: / dɪkˈtiːəptərə /; / dɪk.tiˈɑp.tə.rə / (AmE), / dɪkˈtɪəptərə /; / dɪk.tiˈɒp.tərə / (BrE)
a former order or group of insects that included cockroaches and mantids, now usually divided into the orders Blattodea and Mantodea
