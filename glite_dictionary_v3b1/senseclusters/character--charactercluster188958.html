character

noun
written symbol; letter or sign
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛrɪktɚ / (AmE), / ˈkærɪktə / (BrE)
a written or printed symbol, such as a letter, numeral, or punctuation mark, used to represent speech or writing

Examples

A character in writing
Chinese writing uses thousands of unique characters.
Each character on the license plate must be clearly visible.
The document contained a strange character that my computer couldn't display.

Related Terms

  • synonym: letter - a written symbol representing a speech sound in an alphabet
