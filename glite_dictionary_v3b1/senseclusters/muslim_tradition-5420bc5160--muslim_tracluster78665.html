Muslim tradition

noun
custom or practice in Islam
Pronunciation: / ˈmʊzləm trəˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmʊzlɪm trəˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
a custom, practice, or ritual observed within the Islamic faith and culture

Examples

Muslim tradition of charity
Charity is a key Muslim tradition emphasized during the holy month of Ramadan.
During Ramadan, many families follow a Muslim tradition of breaking the fast together at sunset.
The wearing of the hijab is a Muslim tradition observed by women in various countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Islamic custom - a practice or observance specific to the religion of Islam
