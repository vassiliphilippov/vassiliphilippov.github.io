pigskin

noun
football; pig's skin
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡˌskɪn / (BrE)
the skin of a pig, or informally, a football made from or resembling pig's skin

Examples

Throw the pigskin
He tossed the pigskin across the field during practice.
The wallet was made from genuine pigskin and felt very durable.
Fans cheered as the quarterback threw the pigskin for a touchdown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: football - an oval ball used in American football
  • synonym: leather - animal skin prepared for use as material
