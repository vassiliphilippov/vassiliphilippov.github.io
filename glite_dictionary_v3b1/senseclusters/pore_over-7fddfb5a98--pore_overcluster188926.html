pore over

verb
read or examine attentively
9.3
Pronunciation: / pɔr ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / pɔː ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to read or examine something carefully and attentively, especially to gain information or remember details

Examples

To pore over documents
He enjoys poring over old photographs and reminiscing about the past.
She spent hours poring over the documents in search of important clues.
Students were poring over their textbooks before the big exam.

Related Terms

  • antonym: skim - to read quickly without close attention to detail
  • synonym: study - to devote time and attention to gaining knowledge
