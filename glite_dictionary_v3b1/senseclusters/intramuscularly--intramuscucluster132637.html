intramuscularly

adverb
by injection into a muscle
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.trəˈmʌs.kjə.lɚ.li / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trəˈmʌs.kjʊ.lə.li / (BrE)
In a way that involves injection or administration directly into a muscle.

Examples

Injected intramuscularly
Doctors decided to deliver the antibiotic intramuscularly instead of orally.
Pain relief medication is often injected intramuscularly for faster absorption.
The vaccine was administered intramuscularly in the upper arm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subcutaneously - by injection under the skin
