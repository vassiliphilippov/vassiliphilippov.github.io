buriti

noun
South American palm
9.8
Pronunciation: / buːˈriːti / (AmE), / buːˈriːti / (BrE)
a tropical palm tree native to South America, valued for its edible fruit and oil

Examples

Buriti palm tree
The buriti grows in wetland areas and provides food for many animals.
Local communities harvest buriti fruit to make juice and sweets.
Oil extracted from the buriti is used in cosmetics and cooking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: palm - a type of tropical tree with large leaves and edible fruit
