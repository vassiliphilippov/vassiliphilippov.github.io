ehh

interjection
mild indifference
Pronunciation: / ɛ / (AmE), / ɛ / (BrE)
used to show mild indifference, lack of enthusiasm, or that something is unimpressive or unimportant

Examples

Ehh, whatever
When asked if he liked the movie, he just said ehh and changed the subject.
She looked at the menu and gave an ehh because nothing seemed appealing.
I studied hard, but my test results were just ehh this time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meh - used to express indifference or lack of interest
  • antonym: wow - used to express surprise or admiration
