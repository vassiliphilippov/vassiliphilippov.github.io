kettle

noun
metal vessel for boiling water or food
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈkɛt.əl / (BrE)
A container, usually made of metal and with a lid, used for boiling water or stewing food, often with a handle and spout.

Examples

Boil water in a kettle
Before making tea, he always rinses the kettle to ensure it's clean.
She filled the kettle with water and set it on the stove.
The old iron kettle hung over the open fire, boiling stew for dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: teapot - a vessel with a spout used specifically for brewing and serving tea
