claim cooties

verb
pretend to have imaginary germs (children's usage)
9.5
Pronunciation: / kleɪm ˈkuːtiz / (AmE), / kleɪm ˈkuːtiz / (BrE)
to say, usually jokingly, that one has an imaginary contagious illness (cooties), especially in children's games or banter
