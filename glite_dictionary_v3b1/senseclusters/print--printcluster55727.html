print

verb
produce or publish text or images
Pronunciation: / prɪnt / (AmE), / prɪnt / (BrE)
to produce text or images on paper, especially using a printer or printing press (such as for books, newspapers, or magazines)

Examples

To print a document
Please do not print these comments in your article.
Some magazines refuse to print misleading advertisements.
The newspaper decided to print the allegations despite potential backlash.
Books are usually printed in large quantities for sale.
She plans to print the invitations tomorrow morning.
The company will print your photos in high quality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: erase - to remove or delete recorded or printed text or images
  • synonym: publish - prepare and issue text for public distribution
