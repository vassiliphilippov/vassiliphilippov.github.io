broach

verb
bring up a subject for discussion
Pronunciation: / broʊtʃ / (AmE), / brəʊtʃ / (BrE)
to begin to discuss or mention a subject, especially one that may be sensitive or difficult

Examples

To broach a topic
During the meeting, Sarah decided to broach the idea of flexible working hours.
He was nervous to broach the subject of moving to a new city with his parents.
It can be awkward to broach difficult topics with close friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: introduce - to bring a topic into discussion for the first time
  • synonym: raise - to bring up for consideration or discussion
