ring-around-the-rosy

noun
children's circle game with song and actions
Pronunciation: / ˌrɪŋ əˈraʊnd ðə ˈroʊzi / (AmE), / ˌrɪŋ əˈraʊnd ðə ˈrəʊzi / (BrE)
A children's game in which players hold hands and dance in a circle, squatting or falling down at a specific cue, often while singing a rhyme.
