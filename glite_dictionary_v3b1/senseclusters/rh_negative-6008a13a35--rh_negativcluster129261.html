Rh negative

adjective
lacking the Rh factor in blood type
8.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.səs ˈne.gə.tɪv/ /; / /ɑːr eɪtʃ ˈne.gə.tɪv/ (AmE), /ˈriː.səs ˈne.gə.tɪv/ /; / /ɑː eɪtʃ ˈne.gə.tɪv/ (BrE)
having a blood type whose red blood cells do not possess the Rh factor (Rh antigen)

Examples

Rh negative blood type
All pregnant women are tested to see if they are Rh negative or Rh positive.
If a person with Rh negative blood receives Rh positive blood, it may cause a reaction.
She learned she was Rh negative during her first prenatal checkup.
