filing cabinet

noun
office furniture for storing documents
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪlɪŋ ˌkæbɪnət / (AmE), / ˈfaɪlɪŋ ˌkæbɪnət / (BrE)
A piece of office furniture with drawers for storing and organizing documents or papers.

Examples

A filing cabinet drawer
After moving to a new office, we bought a new filing cabinet to keep our records organized.
He searched through every drawer in the filing cabinet but couldn't find the missing file.
The legal documents are all stored in the filing cabinet next to her desk.
