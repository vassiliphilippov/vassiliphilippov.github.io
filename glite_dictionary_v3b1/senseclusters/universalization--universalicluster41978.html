universalization

noun
making universal
Pronunciation: / juːnɪˌvɜrsələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / juːnɪˌvɜːsəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something universal or applicable to everyone or all cases

Examples

The universalization of rights
The universalization of healthcare remains a major challenge for many developing nations.
Advocates argue that the universalization of human rights is essential for global peace.
Technological advances have contributed to the universalization of access to information.
The universalization of human rights has become a central goal in international law.
Critics warn against the universalization of cultural values without considering local traditions.
Her research explores the universalization of educational standards across different countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generalization - the act of forming a general principle from specific cases
  • synonym: globalization - the process of making something worldwide in scope or application
  • antonym: particularization - the process of making something specific to a particular group or case
