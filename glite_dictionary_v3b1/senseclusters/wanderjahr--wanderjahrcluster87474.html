wanderjahr

noun
apprentice travel year
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːndərˌjɑːr / (AmE), / ˈvandəˌjɑː / (BrE)
a period (often a year) spent traveling or working in different places to gain experience, especially after finishing school, university, or during training

Examples

During the wanderjahr
During his wanderjahr, he learned new techniques from craftsmen in several European cities.
The tradition of the wanderjahr allowed young workers to broaden their skills and experience.
She spent her wanderjahr traveling and working in different workshops across the country.
Many students choose to spend their wanderjahr volunteering in different countries.
He returned from his wanderjahr with new skills and a broader perspective on life.
Taking a wanderjahr before starting university helped her decide on her future career.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apprenticeship - a period of training in a trade or profession
  • synonym: gap year - a year taken off, often for travel or work, between life stages
