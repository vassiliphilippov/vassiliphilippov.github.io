pixelation

noun
visible image pixels
4.8
Pronunciation: /pɪk.sə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /pɪk.sə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a visual effect in which an image appears as large, visible pixels, often due to low resolution or digital processing

Examples

Image pixelation
The old computer game showed obvious pixelation on modern screens.
Zooming in too much on the photo caused noticeable pixelation.
The artist used pixelation as a creative effect in her digital artwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blurring - a visual effect where an image loses sharpness and detail
  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and easy to see
