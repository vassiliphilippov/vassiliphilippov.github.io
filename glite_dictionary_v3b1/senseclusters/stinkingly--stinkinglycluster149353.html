stinkingly

adverb
with a bad smell
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that smells very bad or is extremely unpleasant to smell

Examples

Stinkingly bad smell
The kitchen was stinkingly foul after the food spoiled in the heat.
He found the locker room stinkingly unpleasant after the game.
The trash bins were stinkingly full and needed to be emptied immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pungently - in a way that has a strong, sharp smell or taste
