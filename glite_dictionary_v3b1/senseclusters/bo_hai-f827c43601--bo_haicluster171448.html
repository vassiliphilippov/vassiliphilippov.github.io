Bo Hai

proper noun
inlet of the Yellow Sea in eastern China
7.7
Pronunciation: / boʊ haɪ / (AmE), / bəʊ haɪ / (BrE)
an inlet of the Yellow Sea located on the eastern coast of China, often considered the innermost gulf of the Bohai Sea region.

Examples

Ships cross the Bo Hai
Cargo ships regularly pass through the Bo Hai to reach northern Chinese ports.
Major rivers such as the Yellow River flow into the Bo Hai.
Winter temperatures in the Bo Hai can lead to the formation of sea ice.
