artillery plant

noun
tropical American plant with explosive pollen discharge
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪlɚi plænt / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪlɪəri plɑːnt / (BrE)
A tropical American plant (Pilea microphylla) of the nettle family, noted for its explosive discharge of pollen.

Examples

The artillery plant flower
Despite its name, the artillery plant is completely harmless to touch.
Many gardeners appreciate the artillery plant for its unique pollination method.
The artillery plant releases its pollen with a small but impressive burst.
