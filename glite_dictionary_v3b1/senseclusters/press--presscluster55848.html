noun

Examples

Synonyms

  • news media
    2.3 5.29/m
    news media cover events across newspapers, TV, and online, while press tends to mean newspapers, magazines, and journalists collectively.
  • public press
    3.2 0.12/m
    public press means newspapers and magazines as organizations, while press can also mean individual journalists and their reporting.
  • fourth estate
    5.7 0.18/m
    fourth estate names the press as a powerful social force, while press simply names newspapers, magazines, and journalists.
  • newspeople
    3.1 0.13/m
    newspeople are the journalists who report stories in media outlets, while press can mean both those people and the publications.
