bin Laden

proper noun
founder of al-Qaeda, Arab terrorist leader

Pronunciation: / bɪn ˈlɑdən / (AmE), / bɪn ˈlɑːdən / (BrE)
Osama bin Laden (1957–2011), an Arab terrorist who founded al-Qaeda and orchestrated numerous attacks, including those on September 11, 2001.
