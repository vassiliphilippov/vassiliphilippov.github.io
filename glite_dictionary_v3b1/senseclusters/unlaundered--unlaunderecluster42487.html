unlaundered

adjective
not disguised illegally
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɔːndərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɔːndəd / (BrE)
not processed to hide the illegal origin of money or assets; still directly linked to criminal activity

Examples

He was arrested after attempting to transfer large amounts of unlaundered cash overseas.
