mulled wine

noun
wine heated with sugar and spices
6.8 3.2
Pronunciation: / mʌld waɪn / (AmE), / mʌld waɪn / (BrE)
wine that is heated with spices (and sometimes sugar or fruit) and served warm, especially in winter

Examples

Hot mulled wine
Every December, our family gathers to enjoy homemade mulled wine by the fireplace.
He served mulled wine with slices of orange and cinnamon sticks at the party.
The aroma of mulled wine filled the market during the winter festival.
She prepared mulled wine with cinnamon and cloves for her guests on a snowy evening.
The aroma of mulled wine filled the kitchen as it simmered on the stove.
We enjoyed a cup of mulled wine by the fireplace during the holiday party.
