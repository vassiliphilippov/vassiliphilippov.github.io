faience

noun
glazed, colored earthenware
Pronunciation: / faɪˈɑns / (AmE), / faɪˈɑːns / (BrE)
Glazed earthenware decorated with opaque colored glazes, especially that made in France, Italy, and the Middle East.

Examples

Glazed faience pottery
Archaeologists unearthed fragments of faience jewelry from the burial site.
She admired the intricate blue-and-green patterns on the faience bowl.
The museum's ancient Egyptian collection includes several beautiful pieces of faience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: majolica - Italian glazed pottery, often brightly colored
