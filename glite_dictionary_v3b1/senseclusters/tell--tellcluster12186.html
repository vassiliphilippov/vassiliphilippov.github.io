Synonyms

    1.3 30/m
    order is a strong command from someone in power, while tell is a more general instruction or information.
  • direct
    1.6 20/m
    direct means guiding or giving clear steps to someone, while tell is a broader way to give orders or facts.
  • instruct
    2.8 14/m
    instruct is to teach or show exactly how to do something, while tell can be a simple order or request.
  • enjoin
    8.6 2/m
    enjoin is a formal, often legal command used in courts, while tell is everyday speech for giving instructions.
