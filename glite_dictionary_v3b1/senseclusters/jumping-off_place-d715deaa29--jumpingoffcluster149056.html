jumping-off place

noun
starting point for a journey or venture
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ ˌɔf ˌpleɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ ˌɒf ˌpleɪs / (BrE)
a place or point from which a journey, enterprise, or activity is started or launched

Examples

A jumping-off place for trips
His essay uses World War II as a jumping-off place to discuss modern politics.
San Francisco served as the jumping-off place for their cross-country road trip.
The conference was the perfect jumping-off place for her career in technology.

Related Terms

  • antonym: destination - the place to which someone or something is going
  • synonym: starting point - initial place or position from which something begins
