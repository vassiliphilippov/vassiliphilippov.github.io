contrib

noun
contributed item
Pronunciation: / kənˈtrɪb / (AmE), / kənˈtrɪb / (BrE)
an item, file, or work that someone has contributed to a project, especially in open-source or collaborative contexts

Examples

A new contrib
She uploaded her first contrib to the plugin repository last night.
The project welcomes any contrib that improves its documentation or features.
Each contrib is reviewed by the maintainers before being added to the main codebase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contribution - something that is given or added to a project or cause
  • synonym: submission - a file or work sent in for consideration or inclusion
