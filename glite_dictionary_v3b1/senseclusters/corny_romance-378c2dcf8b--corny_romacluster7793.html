corny romance

noun
overly sentimental romance story or film
3.3 3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːrni roʊˈmæns / (AmE), / ˈkɔːni rəʊˈmæns / (BrE)
A romance story, film, or novel that is excessively sentimental or overly emotional, often perceived as lacking originality or subtlety.

Examples

A corny romance movie
She rolled her eyes at the idea of watching another corny romance on Valentine's Day.
Some people enjoy a corny romance now and then, especially when they're in the mood for something light.
That movie was such a corny romance, but I have to admit I cried at the ending.
