downstage

noun
front part of a theatre stage
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌsteɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌsteɪdʒ / (BrE)
the part of a theatre stage nearest to the audience

Examples

Front downstage area
