paddle box

noun
enclosure for paddlewheel on a steamboat
6.5 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpæd.əl bɑːks/ (AmE), /ˈpæd.əl bɒks/ (BrE)
a wooden structure enclosing the upper section of a paddlewheel on a steamboat, designed to protect it and reduce spray.

Examples

A paddle box on boat
A broken paddle box can cause water to splash onto the deck.
Engineers inspected the paddle box for signs of rot before the ferry could operate.
The old steamboat's paddle box was carefully restored to its original condition.
