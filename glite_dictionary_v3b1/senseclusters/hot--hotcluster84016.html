hot

adjective
very near to what is being sought (in games)
Pronunciation: / hɑt / (AmE), / hɒt / (BrE)
(of a seeker, in a game or search) very close to finding the object sought.

Examples

Hot guess
"Am I close?" "You're hot—almost on top of it!"
In the treasure hunt, I told her she was hot and just inches away from the prize.
You're getting hot—the keys must be very close by.
