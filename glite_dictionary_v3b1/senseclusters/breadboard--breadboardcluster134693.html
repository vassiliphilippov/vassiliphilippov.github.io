breadboard

noun
temporary circuit board
5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbred.bɔːrd/ (AmE), /ˈbred.bɔːd/ (BrE)
a board with holes used to build and test temporary electronic circuits without soldering

Examples

Electronic breadboard
The students used a breadboard to assemble their first electronic project in class.
Before finalizing the design, she tested the circuit on a breadboard to make sure it worked.
He learned how to connect components on a breadboard without using any solder.
