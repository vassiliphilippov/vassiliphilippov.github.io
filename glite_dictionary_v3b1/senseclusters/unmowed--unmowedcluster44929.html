unmowed

adjective
not cut (grass/crops)
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmoʊd / (AmE), / ʌnˈməʊd / (BrE)
not cut or mown; left in its natural, uncut state (especially about grass, crops, or fields)

Examples

An unmowed lawn
The unmowed lawn grew wild and tall after weeks of rain.
Children played in the unmowed field behind the school, hiding among the tall grass.
We could barely see the path through the unmowed meadow during our hike.
The field remained unmowed all summer, allowing wildflowers to bloom.
After the storm, the park's unmowed grass made it difficult to walk along the paths.
Neighbors complained about the unmowed lawn attracting insects and rodents.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mowed - cut or trimmed with a mower
  • antonym: mown - cut down, especially grass or crops
  • synonym: uncut - not cut or trimmed
