routinize

verb

6.4
Pronunciation: /ruː.ˈtiː.naɪz/ /; / /ˈruː.tɪ.naɪz/ (AmE), /ruː.ˈtiː.naɪz/ /; / /ˈruː.tɪ.naɪz/ (BrE)
