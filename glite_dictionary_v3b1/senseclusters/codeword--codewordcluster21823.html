codeword

noun
secret signal word
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊdwɝːd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊdwɜːd / (BrE)
a word or phrase used to convey a secret, hidden, or special meaning understood only by a particular group

Examples

Secret codeword
The agents used a codeword to confirm their identities during the mission.
In the club, mentioning the codeword at the door granted you special access.
Teachers sometimes use a codeword to signal students to pay attention.
The spy whispered the codeword to confirm his identity to the contact.
During the meeting, the manager used a codeword to discreetly signal a change in plans.
Only those who knew the codeword were allowed to enter the secret room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: password - a secret word used to gain access or prove identity
  • antonym: plain language - words or phrases with their usual, non-secret meaning
  • synonym: signal - a gesture, action, or sound used to convey information or instructions
