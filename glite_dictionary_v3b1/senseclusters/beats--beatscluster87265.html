beats

noun
members of the 1950s Beat Generation subculture
7.3 6.8
Pronunciation: /biːts/ (AmE), /biːts/ (BrE)
members of a U.S. youth subculture of the 1950s known for rejecting conventional values, embracing communal living, modern jazz, and alternative lifestyles; also called the Beat Generation.

Examples

The beats generation
Many beats were inspired by jazz musicians like Charlie Parker.
The beats gathered in coffeehouses to discuss literature and philosophy.
The influence of the beats can still be seen in countercultural movements today.
