instructive

adjective
giving useful information or guidance
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstrʌktɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈstrʌktɪv / (BrE)
Providing useful information or guidance; giving instruction or insight.

Examples

An instructive experience
It was an instructive experience to visit the historical museum and learn about ancient cultures.
She found the documentary both entertaining and instructive.
The teacher's instructive comments helped me understand the topic more clearly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: informative - providing useful or interesting information
  • antonym: unenlightening - not providing useful information or insight
