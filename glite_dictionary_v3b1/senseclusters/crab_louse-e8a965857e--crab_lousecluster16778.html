crab louse

noun
parasitic insect infesting pubic hair
9.9
Pronunciation: / kræb laʊs / (AmE), / kræb laʊs / (BrE)
a small parasitic insect that infests the pubic hair and other hairy parts of the human body, causing irritation

Examples

A crab louse infestation
A single crab louse can cause significant itching and discomfort in the affected area.
Doctors diagnosed her with an infestation of crab louse after she experienced severe itching.
The crab louse is commonly transmitted through close physical contact.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pubic louse - another name for the crab louse, scientific name Pthirus pubis
