opaque gem

noun
non-transparent gemstone
Pronunciation: / oʊˈpeɪk ʤɛm / (AmE), / əʊˈpeɪk dʒɛm / (BrE)
A gemstone that does not let light pass through it; not transparent or translucent.

Examples

An opaque gem
The jeweler showed me an opaque gem with a unique green hue.
Turquoise is a popular opaque gem often used in Southwestern jewelry.
Unlike diamonds, an opaque gem does not sparkle when held to the light.

Related Terms

  • antonym: translucent gem - a gemstone that allows some light to pass through but not completely clear
  • antonym: transparent gem - a gemstone that allows light to pass through
