bookstand

noun
book-selling stall
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌstænd / (BrE)
a stall, table, or display where books are sold or shown, often in a shop or market

Examples

A busy bookstand
She bought a rare edition from a tiny bookstand near the train station on Saturday.
The bookstand at the market offered a wide selection of novels and travel guides.
He set up a bookstand outside the library to sell used textbooks to students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bookstall - a small shop or stand selling books
  • synonym: newsstand - a stand or stall selling newspapers and magazines
