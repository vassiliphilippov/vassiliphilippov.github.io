oncocytic

adjective
cells with granular cytoplasm
Pronunciation: / ˌɑŋkəˈsɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒŋkəˈsɪtɪk / (BrE)
describing cells that have abundant granular cytoplasm, often found in certain tumors

Examples

Oncocytic tumor cells
The biopsy revealed oncocytic cells, which helped the doctors diagnose the tumor.
Pathologists often note oncocytic features in certain types of thyroid tumors.
The presence of oncocytic changes can indicate a specific subtype of neoplasm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eosinophilic - describing cells that stain readily with eosin dye
