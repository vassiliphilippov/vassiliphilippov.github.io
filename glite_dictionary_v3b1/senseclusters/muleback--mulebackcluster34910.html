muleback

noun
journey on a mule
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈmjuːlˌbæk / (BrE)
the back of a mule, or a ride or journey on a mule, especially as a means of transport

Examples

A muleback ride
The explorers traveled by muleback through the rugged mountain paths.
Supplies were delivered to the remote village on muleback due to the lack of roads.
After hours of muleback riding, they finally reached the ancient ruins.
The villagers transported goods over the mountain on muleback because there were no roads.
She traveled through the narrow trails by muleback during her adventure in the Andes.
Supplies for the remote camp arrived each week on muleback.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foot - traveling by walking
  • synonym: horseback - a ride or journey on the back of a horse
