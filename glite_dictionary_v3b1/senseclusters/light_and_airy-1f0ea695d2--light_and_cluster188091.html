light and airy

adjective
not heavy or restrictive; comfortable
3.7 2.8
Pronunciation: /laɪt ən ˈer.iː/ /; / /laɪt ænd ˈer.iː/ (AmE), /laɪt ən ˈeə.riː/ /; / /laɪt ænd ˈeə.riː/ (BrE)
(of clothing, rooms, or similar) pleasantly lightweight and not heavy or confining; comfortable due to being not thick or restrictive.

Examples

A light and airy room
His new shirt is so light and airy that it's perfect for hiking.
She chose a light and airy dress for the summer party.
The curtains made the room feel light and airy even on cloudy days.
