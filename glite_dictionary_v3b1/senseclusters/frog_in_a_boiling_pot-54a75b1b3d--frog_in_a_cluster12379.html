frog in a boiling pot

noun
person unaware of growing danger
Pronunciation: / frɔɡ ɪn ə ˈbɔɪlɪŋ pɑt / (AmE), / frɒɡ ɪn ə ˈbɔɪlɪŋ pɒt / (BrE)
A person who fails to recognize a gradually increasing danger or problem until it is too late.

Examples

A frog in a boiling pot
Don't become a frog in a boiling pot; notice the warning signs before it's too late.
Many people ignored the company's decline, like a frog in a boiling pot unaware of danger.
She realized she had been a frog in a boiling pot, slowly accepting worsening work conditions.

Related Terms
