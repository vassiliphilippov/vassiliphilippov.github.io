commerce

noun
buying and selling of goods and services
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.mɚs / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ɜːs / (BrE)
the activities involved in buying and selling goods and services, especially on a large scale

Examples

International commerce
International commerce has grown rapidly over the last few decades.
Local businesses depend on commerce for their survival and growth.
The internet has transformed the way commerce operates globally.
