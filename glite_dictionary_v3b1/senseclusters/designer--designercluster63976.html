designer

noun
a person who creates designs or plans
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪnər / (AmE), / dɪˈzʌɪnə / (BrE)
a person who creates the plans or designs for something, especially buildings, clothing, or other objects

Examples

Famous designer of buildings
As a leading designer, she has contributed to several award-winning buildings.
Many people dream of becoming a famous fashion designer.
The company hired a designer to create the plans for their new headquarters.
