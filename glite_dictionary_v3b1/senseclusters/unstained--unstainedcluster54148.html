unstained

adjective
not spoiled or tarnished (esp. of reputation)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsteɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsteɪnd / (BrE)
(of reputation or character) not spoiled, blemished, or discredited; pure or untarnished.

Examples

Unstained reputation
Despite many accusations, his honor remained unstained in the eyes of the community.
He maintained an unstained reputation throughout his long legal career.
The judge was known for her unstained integrity and fairness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stained - marked or tainted (esp. of reputation)
  • antonym: tarnished - spoiled or made less good (esp. reputation)
  • synonym: unsullied - not spoiled or made impure (of reputation, character)
  • synonym: untarnished - not made less valuable or respected; not damaged (esp. reputation)
