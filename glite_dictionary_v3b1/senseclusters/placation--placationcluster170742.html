Synonyms

  • appeasement
    7.4 2/m
    appeasement is giving in to demands to avoid trouble, while placation is calming someone often by yielding to them.
  • mollification
    8.4 0.01/m
    mollification means softening someone's anger with gentle words or acts, while placation often involves calming by yielding demands.
  • pacification
    7.7 0.01/m
    pacification is calming or bringing peace to a person or place after conflict, while placation focuses on soothing one person's anger.
  • propitiation
    9.9 0.2/m
    propitiation is offering something to regain favor after a wrong, while placation is calming someone to stop their anger.
  • conciliation
    6.7 0.9/m
    conciliation is a mutual peace process, often with a neutral third party, while placation is one person's act of calming another.
