cool

adjective
describing greens, blues, or violets in color
Pronunciation: / kul / (AmE), / kuːl / (BrE)
(of a color) giving an impression of coolness, typically describing greens, blues, and violets.

Examples

Cool colors
Her dress had a cool shade that made it look refreshing in the summer heat.
Interior designers often use cool colors to create a calm, relaxing atmosphere.
The artist chose cool tones of blue and green for the background.

Related Terms

  • antonym: warm - (of a color) giving an impression of warmth, typically reds, oranges, and yellows
