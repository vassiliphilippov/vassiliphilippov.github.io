Francisco José de Goya

proper noun
Spanish painter and satirist (1746–1828)
6.3
Pronunciation: / frænˌsɪskoʊ hoʊˌseɪ də ˈɡɔɪə / (AmE), / frænˌsɪskəʊ həʊˌseɪ də ˈɡɔɪə / (BrE)
(1746–1828) A Spanish painter renowned for his portraits and satirical works, considered one of the most important artists of the late 18th and early 19th centuries.
