paring

noun
piece of fruit or vegetable cut off while peeling
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeərɪŋ / (BrE)
a small, thin piece or fragment cut from the surface of something, especially the skin or peel of fruit or vegetables

Examples

Apple parings
A pile of apple parings collected on the cutting board as he worked.
Always discard the orange parings before juicing the fruit.
She swept the potato parings into the compost bin after making dinner.
I swept the apple parings into the trash after peeling the fruit.
She used a small knife to remove the potato parings before boiling them.
Wood parings covered the floor of the workshop after hours of carving.

  • synonym: rind - the tough outer layer of certain fruits and vegetables
