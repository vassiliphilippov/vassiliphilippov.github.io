diabolo

noun
juggling spool toy
9.9
Pronunciation: / diˈæbəˌloʊ / (AmE), / diˈabələʊ / (BrE)
a juggling toy with an hourglass-shaped spool spun and tossed on a string stretched between two sticks

Examples

Juggling diabolo tricks
He amazed the crowd with his diabolo tricks at the festival.
Learning to balance the diabolo on the string takes patience and practice.
She bought a colorful diabolo as a souvenir from the circus show.
