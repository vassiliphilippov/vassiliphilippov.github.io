Grand Canal

proper noun
main waterway in Venice, Italy
4.0
Pronunciation: / ɡrænd kəˈnæl / (AmE), / ɡrænd kəˈnal / (BrE)
the main waterway running through the city of Venice, Italy, known for its iconic S-shape and historic significance.
