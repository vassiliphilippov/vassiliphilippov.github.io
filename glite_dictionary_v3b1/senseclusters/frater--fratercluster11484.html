frater

noun
religious title
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈfrɑːtə / (BrE)
a title or form of address used before the name of a member of certain religious or fraternal orders, meaning 'Brother'

Examples

Frater John
The new member was introduced as frater John during the ceremony.
In the monastery, everyone respectfully addressed him as frater Thomas.
The letter was signed by frater Michael, the head of the order.
