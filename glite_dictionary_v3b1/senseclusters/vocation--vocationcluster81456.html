vocation

noun
group of people in the same profession
Pronunciation: / voʊˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / vəʊˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
A group or community of people engaged in the same type of profession or occupation.

Examples

The medical vocation
He found companionship in the vocation he shared with other scientists.
Teaching is a vocation that attracts people who want to make a difference.
The medical vocation has a long tradition of public service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: profession - a paid occupation, especially one that requires advanced education or training
