extrafloral

adjective
outside the flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəˈflɔrəl / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈflɔːrəl / (BrE)
located or occurring outside the flowers, especially referring to parts of a plant such as nectaries

Examples

Extrafloral nectaries
Some plants have extrafloral nectaries that attract ants for protection.
Researchers studied the extrafloral glands found on the stems of certain tropical species.
The presence of extrafloral structures can help plants defend against herbivores.
