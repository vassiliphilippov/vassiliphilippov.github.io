Humean

noun
follower of Hume
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuːmiən / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmiən / (BrE)
a person who follows or supports the philosophical views of David Hume

Examples

A Humean thinker
As a dedicated Humean, he often questioned the certainty of scientific knowledge.
The professor explained the Humean approach to causation in her philosophy lecture.
Many modern philosophers consider themselves at least partly Humean in their thinking.

Related Terms
