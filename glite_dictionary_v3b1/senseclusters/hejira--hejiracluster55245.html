hejira

noun
mass journey to escape danger
Pronunciation: / hɛˈdʒɪrə / (AmE), / hɛˈdʒɪərə / (BrE)
A journey, especially by a large group, to escape from a dangerous or hostile environment; an exodus.

Examples

A hejira journey
After the outbreak of violence, a massive hejira changed the region's demographics.
The film tells the story of a family's hejira from their war-torn homeland.
The persecuted minority embarked on a hejira to seek safety across the border.
