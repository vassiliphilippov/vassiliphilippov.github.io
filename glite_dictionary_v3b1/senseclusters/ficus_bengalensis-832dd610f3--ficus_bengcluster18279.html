Ficus bengalensis

noun
species of banyan tree
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪkəs bɛnˈɡælənsɪs / (AmE), / ˈfʌɪkəs bɛnˌɡalˈɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A large East Indian tree that produces aerial shoots growing down into the soil to form additional trunks; commonly known as the banyan tree.

Examples

The Ficus bengalensis tree
Many villages in India have a revered Ficus bengalensis at their center.
Scientists studied how the aerial roots of Ficus bengalensis developed into additional trunks.
The shade beneath the Ficus bengalensis was a welcome relief from the midday sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banyan - East Indian fig tree with aerial roots forming trunks
