bendy

adjective
curving or winding
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛndi / (AmE), / ˈbɛndi / (BrE)
curving or winding, having many bends or turns, especially of roads, paths, or shapes

Examples

Bendy road
The children loved riding their bikes along the bendy path through the park.
We drove carefully on the bendy mountain road during the rainstorm.
The artist drew a bendy line to show the river's winding course.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curvy - having many curves or bends
  • antonym: straight - not curving or bending; direct
