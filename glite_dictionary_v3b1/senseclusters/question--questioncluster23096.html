question

verb
to ask someone questions formally

to ask someone questions, especially to get information or test knowledge

Examples

To question a witness
After the meeting, reporters questioned the mayor on the new policy.
She was questioned for hours by the customs officers at the airport.
The detective questioned the witness about what he saw that night.
Several reporters questioned the senator following his speech at the conference.
She spent the morning questioning local residents for the radio segment.
The journalist will question the celebrity about her new film during the live broadcast.
Journalists question public officials to obtain important information for their readers.
She nervously questioned her professor after the lecture to clarify a point.
The police began to question the witnesses about the events of that night.
