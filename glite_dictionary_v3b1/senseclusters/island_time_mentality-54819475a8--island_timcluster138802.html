island time mentality

noun
a relaxed, unhurried attitude toward time
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.lənd taɪm mɛnˈtæləti / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.lənd taɪm mɛnˈtælɪti / (BrE)
an attitude characterized by a relaxed or unhurried approach to time and daily life, often associated with island cultures

Examples

Island time mentality attitude
After spending a month in the Caribbean, he adopted the island time mentality and stopped rushing everywhere.
She explained her new island time mentality as prioritizing relaxation over strict schedules.
The island time mentality made the event start much later than planned, but no one seemed to mind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: punctuality - the quality of being on time
