frenchy

noun
informal term for French person
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛntʃi / (AmE), / ˈfrɛntʃi / (BrE)
an informal and sometimes offensive term for a person from France or of French descent

Examples

A frenchy at school
Some people called him frenchy because of his strong accent and Parisian style.
The nickname frenchy was used by his teammates, though he did not like it.
She was sometimes teased as frenchy after moving to the new school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Frenchman - a man from France
  • synonym: Frenchwoman - a woman from France
