uninclusive

adjective
not including all
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈkluːsɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈkluːsɪv / (BrE)
not including or not allowing all people or things; not open to everyone

Examples

An uninclusive group
The club's uninclusive rules prevented many students from joining.
Critics argued that the event was uninclusive and failed to represent the whole community.
Her uninclusive attitude made some team members feel left out.

Related Terms
