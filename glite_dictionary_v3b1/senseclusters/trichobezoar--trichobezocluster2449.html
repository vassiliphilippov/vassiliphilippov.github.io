trichobezoar

noun
mass of swallowed hair in the stomach
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtrɪkoʊˈbizoʊˌɑr / (AmE), / ˌtrɪkəʊˈbiːzəʊˌɑː / (BrE)
a compact mass of hair found in the stomach or intestines, usually as a result of swallowing hair.

Examples

A trichobezoar mass
Persistent hair pulling and ingestion can lead to the formation of a trichobezoar.
The surgeon removed a large trichobezoar from the patient's stomach.
Veterinarians often treat cats for trichobezoars caused by excessive self-grooming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bezoar - a mass found in the digestive tract, usually of indigestible material
