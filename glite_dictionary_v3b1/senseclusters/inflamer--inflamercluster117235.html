inflamer

noun
one who provokes conflict
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfleɪmər / (AmE), / ɪnˈfleɪmə / (BrE)
a person who provokes conflict or stirs up controversy

Examples

An inflamer of conflict
He was labeled an inflamer after his comments sparked animosity at the meeting.
Rather than seeking compromise, she acted as an inflamer in the negotiations.
Social media can give every inflamer a platform to spread discord to a wide audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instigator - a person who initiates something, especially trouble or conflict
  • antonym: peacemaker - a person who tries to make peace or resolve conflicts
