manipulator

noun
controlling person
Pronunciation: / məˈnɪpjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / məˈnɪpjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who controls or influences others, often in a clever or unfair way to achieve their own goals

Examples

Clever manipulator
The skilled manipulator convinced everyone to support his plan without revealing his true intentions.
She realized too late that her friend was a manipulator who only cared about personal gain.
In politics, a clever manipulator can shape public opinion to their advantage.
She realized too late that her business partner was a skilled manipulator who always got his way.
The story warns readers to be cautious of manipulators who use charm to deceive others.
As a child, he learned to spot a manipulator by watching how people twisted the truth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: influencer - a person who has the power to affect others' decisions or actions
  • synonym: schemer - a person who makes secret plans to achieve something, often by tricking others
  • antonym: victim - a person who is harmed or controlled by someone else
