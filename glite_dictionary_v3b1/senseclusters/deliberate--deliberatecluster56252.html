deliberate

adjective
carefully thought out and intentional
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪbərət / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪbərət / (BrE)
done consciously and intentionally, after careful thought or planning

Examples

A deliberate choice
His movements were slow and deliberate, showing how carefully he planned each step.
She gave a deliberate answer after weighing all the options.
The attack on the database was a deliberate act of sabotage.
His deliberate attempt to mislead the committee was eventually uncovered.
She made a deliberate decision to confront her boss about the issue.
The attack was a deliberate act of aggression, not a random incident.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accidental - happening by chance, not intended
  • synonym: intentional - done on purpose or deliberately
