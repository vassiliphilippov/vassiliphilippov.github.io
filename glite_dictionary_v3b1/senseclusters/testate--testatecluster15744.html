testate

adjective
having made a valid will
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɛsteɪt / (BrE)
(of a person) having made a legally valid will before death.

Examples

Testate person
It is important to be testate to ensure your wishes are respected after your death.
The estate was distributed smoothly because the deceased died testate.
The law provides different procedures for testate and intestate estates.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intestate - dying without having made a valid will
