wasp

noun
stinging insect with slender body
3.4
Pronunciation: / wɑsp / (AmE), / wɒsp / (BrE)
A social or solitary insect with a slender body, a narrow waist, and a powerful sting, usually with yellow and black coloring.

Examples

A wasp sting
A wasp flew into the kitchen and we had to open a window to let it out.
During the picnic, a swarm of wasps gathered around the soda cans.
She was stung on the arm by a wasp while gardening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bee - flying insect related to wasps, but usually hairier and non-aggressive, produces honey
  • synonym: hornet - a large, aggressive type of wasp
