exceedingly

adverb
to a very great extent
7.1
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsiːdɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪkˈsiːdɪŋli / (BrE)
to an extreme degree; extremely; exceptionally

Examples

An exceedingly rare event
It is exceedingly rare to see this species of bird in the city.
She was exceedingly grateful for all the help she received during her recovery.
The test was exceedingly difficult for most students to complete in the allotted time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extremely - to a very high degree; very
  • antonym: moderately - to a limited degree; not very
  • degree lower: rather - to a somewhat high degree (less than exceedingly)
