timely

adjective
occurring at the right time
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪmli / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmli / (BrE)
happening or done at the right or most suitable time

Examples

A timely advice
Her timely advice helped me avoid making a costly mistake.
The article provided a timely analysis of the current political situation.
The firefighters arrived with a timely response that prevented further damage.
Her timely intervention saved the company from a major financial loss.
Submitting your application in a timely manner increases your chances of acceptance.
The timely arrival of the firefighters prevented the fire from spreading to neighboring houses.
The manager's timely decision helped the company avoid a major loss.
A timely reminder ensured that everyone arrived at the meeting on schedule.
Her timely arrival meant we could start the event without any delay.

Related Terms

  • antonym: late - happening after the expected or usual time
  • synonym: prompt - done without delay; immediate
  • synonym: punctual - happening or doing something at the agreed or proper time
  • synonym: seasonable - appropriate or happening at a suitable or opportune moment
  • antonym: untimely - happening or done at an unsuitable or premature time
