endurance

noun
ability to withstand hardship or stress
4.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʊrəns / (AmE), / ɪnˈdjʊərəns / (BrE)
the ability to withstand hardship or stress over a long period of time

Examples

Physical endurance
Completing the mountain trek required extraordinary endurance and mental strength.
She admired his endurance through years of tough training.
Winter in the Arctic is a true test of endurance for both people and animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stamina - physical or mental energy for sustaining effort
