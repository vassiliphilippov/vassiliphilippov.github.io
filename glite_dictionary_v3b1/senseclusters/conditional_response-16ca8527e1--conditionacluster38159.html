Examples

The dog salivates as a conditional response to the ringing bell.

Synonyms

  • conditioned response
    4.8 0.17/m
    conditioned response is the standard psychology term for a learned reaction to a stimulus, while conditional response is a less common variant.
  • acquired reflex
    6.5 0.00/m
    acquired reflex is the physiology term for a response learned to a specific stimulus, while conditional response is the psychology phrase for the same idea.
  • learned response
    5.7 0.08/m
    learned response is a general reaction gained by experience or training, while conditional response is a specific reaction tied to one stimulus.
