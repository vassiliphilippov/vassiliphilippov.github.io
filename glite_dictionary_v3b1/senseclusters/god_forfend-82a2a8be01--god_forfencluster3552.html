God forfend

interjection
May God prevent (something)
Pronunciation: / ɡɑd fərˈfɛnd / (AmE), / ɡɒd fəˈfɛnd / (BrE)
May God prevent or forbid something undesirable from happening.

Examples

God forfend that
God forfend that we ever experience another war like that.
If, God forfend, anything happens, please call me immediately.
The villagers prayed, saying, "God forfend the river should flood again this year."

Related Terms

  • synonym: God forbid - May God prevent something unwanted; interjection expressing hope something will not happen
