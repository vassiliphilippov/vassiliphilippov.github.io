riotously

adverb
in an exuberant or unrestrained way
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪ.ə.təs.li / (AmE), / ˈraɪ.ət.əs.li /; / ˈraɪ.ə.təs.li / (BrE)
in a very lively, wild, or unrestrained way, often with noise and excitement

Examples

Laugh riotously
Guests laughed riotously as the comedian took the stage.
The garden was riotously colorful in the spring sunlight.
The room was riotously decorated for the festive celebration.
After the announcement, the room erupted riotously with laughter and applause.
The fans cheered riotously as the winning goal was scored in the final seconds.
The parade moved riotously through the streets, with music and colorful costumes everywhere.

  • synonym: tumultuously - in a way that involves a lot of noise, confusion, or disorder
