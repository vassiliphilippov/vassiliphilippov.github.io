particularize

verb
describe in detail; specify
2.9
Pronunciation: / pərˈtɪkjələrˌaɪz /; / pərˈtɪkjələˌraɪz / (AmE), / pəˈtɪkjʊləraɪz / (BrE)
to mention or describe someone or something specifically and in detail

Examples

To particularize details
Could you particularize exactly where the error occurred?
She asked him to particularize his objections to the proposal.
The report failed to particularize the methods used in the experiment.
In your application, please particularise any relevant work experience you have.
She asked the witness to particularise the events leading up to the incident.
The report does not particularise how the funds were allocated.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generalise - to make something more general and less specific
  • synonym: specify - to state something clearly and exactly
