paratactically

adverb
without conjunctions
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈtæktɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈtæktɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that places clauses or phrases side by side without using conjunctions

Examples

To write paratactically
The author often writes paratactically, using short, simple sentences placed side by side.
In her essay, ideas are presented paratactically rather than connected with complex conjunctions.
He described the events paratactically, listing them one after another without linking words.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hypotactically - in a manner that uses subordination and conjunctions to link clauses
