nonfluctuating

adjective
unchanging; constant
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈflʌktʃuˌeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈflʌktʃuːeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not changing or varying; remaining constant over time.

Examples

A nonfluctuating level

A nonfluctuating exchange rate can help stabilize the economy.
The scientist observed a nonfluctuating pattern in the data over several weeks.
