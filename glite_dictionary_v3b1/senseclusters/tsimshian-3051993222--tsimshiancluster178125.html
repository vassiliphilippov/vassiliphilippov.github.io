Tsimshian

noun
member of an Indigenous people of British Columbia
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃɪmˈʃiːən / (AmE), / tʃɪmˈʃiːən / (BrE)
a member of an Indigenous people originally inhabiting river and coastal areas of British Columbia and southern Alaska.
