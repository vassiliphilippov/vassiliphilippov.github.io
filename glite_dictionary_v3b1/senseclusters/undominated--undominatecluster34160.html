undominated

adjective
not controlled
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdɑːməˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdɒmɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
not controlled, ruled, or influenced by anyone or anything

Examples

Undominated land
For centuries, the mountain region remained undominated by any foreign power.
The artist's unique style is undominated by current trends or popular opinion.
Many wild areas are still undominated by human development and remain untouched.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dominated - controlled or ruled by someone or something
  • synonym: independent - not controlled by others; self-governing
