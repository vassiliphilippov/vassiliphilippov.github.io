pointillistic

adjective
relating to pointillism or made of small dots
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌpɔɪn.t̬əˈlɪs.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɔɪn.tɪˈlɪs.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling pointillism, especially in using small distinct dots or points to compose a whole image or effect.

Examples

Pointillistic painting style
Her latest novel has a pointillistic structure, made up of tiny, vivid scenes.
Some composers have written music with a pointillistic texture, using short, separated notes.
The artist's pointillistic technique created a shimmering effect on the canvas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pointillism - a painting technique using tiny dots of color to create an image
