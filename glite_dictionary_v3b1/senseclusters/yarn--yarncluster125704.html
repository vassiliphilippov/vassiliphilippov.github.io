yarn

noun
a long or imaginative story
Pronunciation: / jɑrn / (AmE), / jɑːn / (BrE)
a long or imaginative story, often one that is exaggerated or difficult to believe

Examples

A wild yarn
Grandpa is famous for spinning a good yarn at every family gathering.
He told a fascinating yarn about his adventures as a sailor.
The journalist was skeptical of the politician's wild yarn about his past.

Related Terms

  • synonym: story - an account of imaginary or real people and events told for entertainment
  • synonym: tale - a story, especially one that is imaginatively recounted
