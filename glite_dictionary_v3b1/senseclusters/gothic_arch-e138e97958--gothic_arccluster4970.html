Gothic arch

noun
pointed arch in Gothic architecture
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːθɪk ɑrt͡ʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒθɪk ɑːt͡ʃ / (BrE)
a pointed arch, characteristic of Gothic architecture, with a joint at the apex instead of a keystone
