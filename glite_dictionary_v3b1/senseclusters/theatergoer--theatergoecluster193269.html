theatergoer

noun
person who attends the theater
Pronunciation: / ˈθiət̬ɚˌɡoʊ.ɚ /; / ˈθiət̬ɚˌɡoʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈθɪətəˌɡəʊ.ə /; / ˈθɪətəˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
a person who attends the theater, especially regularly or as a habit

Examples

A theatergoer attends plays
As an avid theatergoer, she never misses a new stage production in town.
Being a theatergoer has introduced him to a wonderful community of drama enthusiasts.
The city is a paradise for the theatergoer seeking both classic and modern performances.
As an avid theatregoer, she rarely missed a new production in the city.
Discounts were offered to frequent theatregoers throughout the theatre festival.
The theatregoer enjoyed discussing the performance during intermission.
