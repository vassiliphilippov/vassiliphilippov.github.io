horsewhip

verb
strike with a horsewhip
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌwɪp / (AmE), / ˈhɔːswɪp / (BrE)
to strike or flog someone or something with a whip intended for horses

Examples

To horsewhip a person
He claimed he would horsewhip anyone who insulted his honor.
She was shocked to see the farmer horsewhipping the stubborn mule in the field.
The cruel trainer threatened to horsewhip the animal if it disobeyed.
