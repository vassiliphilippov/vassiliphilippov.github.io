puff paste

noun
dough for light, flaky pastry
6.2 4.0
Pronunciation: / pʌf peɪst / (AmE), / pʌf peɪst / (BrE)
a dough made of flour, butter, and water, used to create very light, flaky pastry layers, especially in puff pastry

Homemade puff paste

You need to chill the puff paste before rolling it out for the best flaky texture.
