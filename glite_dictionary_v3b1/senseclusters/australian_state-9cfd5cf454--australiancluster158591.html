Australian state

noun
state of Australia
2.0
Pronunciation: / əˈstreɪljən steɪt / (AmE), / ɒˈstreɪliən steɪt / (BrE)
one of the main territorial divisions that make up the Commonwealth of Australia, each with its own government.

Examples

Each Australian state government
Every Australian state has its own legislation on education and healthcare.
Queensland is one Australian state known for its tropical climate and Great Barrier Reef.
The capital city of each Australian state is often its economic center.
