backsplash

noun
liquid splashing back
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌsplæʃ / (AmE), / ˈbækˌsplæʃ / (BrE)
a splash or spray of liquid that bounces back from a surface toward someone or something

Examples

A muddy backsplash
The passing lorry caused a muddy backsplash that soaked his shoes and trouser cuffs.
She wiped the backsplash from the wall after washing the dishes.
Rainwater hit the pavement and created a backsplash that splattered onto the window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: splashback - liquid that splashes back from a surface
  • synonym: spray - a mass of small drops of liquid sent through the air
