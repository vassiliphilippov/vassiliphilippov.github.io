backsplash

noun
liquid splashing back
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbæk.splæʃ/ (AmE), /ˈbæk.splæʃ/ (BrE)
a splash or spray of liquid that bounces back from a surface toward someone or something

Examples

A muddy backsplash

She wiped the backsplash from the wall after washing the dishes.
