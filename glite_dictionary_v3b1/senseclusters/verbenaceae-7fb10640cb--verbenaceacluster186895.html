Verbenaceae

noun
family of tropical plants (verbena family)
Pronunciation: / vərˌbiːnəˈsiː.iː /; / vɚˌbiːnəˈsiː.iː / (AmE), / vɜːˌbiːnəˈsiː.iː / (BrE)
a family of tropical and subtropical herbs, shrubs, and trees, known as the verbena family, found mainly in the New World

Examples

The Verbenaceae family
Botanists have classified several shrubs in South America as part of the Verbenaceae.
Some species in the Verbenaceae are valued for their medicinal properties.
The Verbenaceae family includes many ornamental garden plants such as verbena and lantana.
Several species in the Verbenaceae are used in traditional herbal medicine.
The Verbenaceae family contains a wide variety of flowering plants found in the Americas.
