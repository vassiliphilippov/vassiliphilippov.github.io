Moqui

proper noun
former name for Hopi
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊki / (AmE), / ˈməʊki / (BrE)
an old term formerly used to refer to the Hopi people of the southwestern United States

Examples

The Moqui people
Early explorers referred to the Hopi as Moqui in their journals.
The term Moqui is now considered outdated and is rarely used in modern texts.
Artifacts labeled as Moqui often actually belong to the Hopi people.
