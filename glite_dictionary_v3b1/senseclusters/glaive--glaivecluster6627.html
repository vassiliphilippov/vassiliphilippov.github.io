glaive

noun
fantasy throwing weapon
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡleɪv / (AmE), / ɡleɪv / (BrE)
a bladed throwing weapon, often star- or disk-shaped, especially found in fantasy or science fiction stories

Examples
