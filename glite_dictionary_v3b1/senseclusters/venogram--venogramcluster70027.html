venogram

noun
X-ray image of a vein with contrast medium
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈviːnəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈviːnəɡram / (BrE)
a radiographic image of a vein after injection with a contrast medium to make it visible on X-rays

Examples

A venogram image
A venogram can help diagnose deep vein thrombosis when ultrasound results are inconclusive.
After reviewing the venogram, the radiologist noticed a narrowing in the vein.
The doctor ordered a venogram to check for blood clots in her leg.
