snack food

noun
light food eaten between meals
3.8
Pronunciation: / snæk fuːd / (AmE), / snæk fuːd / (BrE)
A light food typically eaten between regular meals, often as a quick or convenient option.

Examples

Healthy snack food
I try to keep healthy snack food in my office so I'm not tempted by candy.
Many children enjoy eating snack food after school.
Potato chips are a popular snack food at parties.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: junk food - food that is high in calories but low in nutritional value
  • synonym: snack - a small amount of food eaten between meals
