rumba

verb
to dance the rumba
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌmbə / (AmE), / ˈrʌmbə / (BrE)
to dance the rumba, a ballroom dance of Cuban origin with a slow, rhythmical movement

Examples

To rumba together
At the party, she invited me to rumba with her to the lively Latin music.
Couples love to rumba at weddings because of its passionate rhythm.
They rumbaed across the polished dance floor, impressing everyone with their graceful steps.
