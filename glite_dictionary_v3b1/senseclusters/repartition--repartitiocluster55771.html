repartition

noun
dividing again
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriːpɑːrˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːpɑːˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of dividing something again into parts, especially a computer disk or a territory

Examples

Disk repartition
The IT specialist performed a repartition of the hard drive to create more storage space.
After the war, the repartition of the disputed region was discussed by both countries.
You may need a repartition if you want to install a new operating system on your computer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partition - the act of dividing something into parts
  • antonym: unification - the process of joining parts together into a whole
