conversationalist

noun
AI program that converses
Pronunciation: / \u02cck\u0251\u02d0n.v\u0259r\u02c8se\u026a.\u0283\u0259n.\u0259l\u026ast / (AmE), / \u02cck\u0252n.v\u0259\u02c8se\u026a.\u0283\u0259n.\u0259l\u026ast / (BrE)
(in computing) a software program or AI agent designed to hold natural-language conversations with users, often to answer questions or perform tasks.

Examples

AI conversationalist
The new customer service AI acts as a conversationalist, guiding users through complex queries.
Researchers trained a conversationalist to suggest diet plans using natural dialog and personal data.
Many companies deploy a conversationalist on their websites to handle routine customer questions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chatbot - a computer program designed to simulate conversation with human users
  • synonym: conversational agent - software that interacts with users using natural language, often powered by AI
