soft-soap

verb
persuade with insincere flattery
Pronunciation: / sɔft soʊp /; / ˈsɔft ˌsoʊp / (AmE), / sɒft səʊp /; / ˈsɒft ˌsəʊp / (BrE)
to try to persuade or influence someone by using insincere praise or flattery
