amateur

adjective
lacking professional skill or expertise
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈæmətʃɚ /; / ˈæməˌtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈæmətə, ˈæmətjʊə /; / ˈamətə, ˈamətʃə / (BrE)
not professional; done by someone who is not a professional or does not have expert skill

Examples

Amateur work
His amateur attempt at fixing the sink only made things worse.
The repairs to the car looked very amateur and unsafe.
The website had an amateur design that lacked polish.
Many amateur athletes participate in competitions simply for the joy of the sport.
She is an amateur photographer who loves capturing landscapes in her free time.
The club welcomes both amateur and professional actors to audition for its plays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inexpert - lacking expertise or skill
  • antonym: professional - having or showing professional skill or expertise
  • synonym: unskilled - not having special skill or training
