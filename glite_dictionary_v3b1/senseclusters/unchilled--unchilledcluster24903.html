unchilled

adjective
not cold
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃɪld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɪld / (BrE)
not made cold or not kept at a low temperature

Examples

Unchilled white wine
He served the white wine unchilled, which surprised his guests.
Some people prefer their water unchilled rather than with ice.
The dessert was left unchilled on the counter before being served.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chilled - made cold or kept at a low temperature
  • degree higher: warm - having a temperature slightly higher than room temperature
