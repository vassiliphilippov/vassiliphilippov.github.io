parenthesize

verb
to add parentheses
7.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈrɛnθəˌsaɪz / (AmE), / pəˈrɛnθəsaɪz / (BrE)

Examples

To parenthesize a word

In academic writing, it is common to parenthesize references after a quotation.
