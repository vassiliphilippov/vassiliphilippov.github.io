parenthesize

verb
to add parentheses
Pronunciation: / pəˈrɛnθəˌsaɪz / (AmE), / pəˈrɛnθəsaɪz / (BrE)
to enclose something within parentheses in writing or printing

Examples

To parenthesize a word
The editor asked the writer to parenthesize the dates for better clarity.
You should parenthesize additional information that is not essential to the main sentence.
In academic writing, it is common to parenthesize references after a quotation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enclose - to surround or close off on all sides
