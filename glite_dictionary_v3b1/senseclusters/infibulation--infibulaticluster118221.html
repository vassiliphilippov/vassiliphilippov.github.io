infibulation

noun
closure of genitalia
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌfɪbjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˌfɪbjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a surgical procedure involving the closure or sewing together of the external genitalia, often for cultural or traditional reasons

Examples

Female infibulation procedure
The World Health Organization strongly opposes the practice of infibulation due to its health risks.
Many countries have laws banning infibulation and similar procedures.
Medical professionals work to educate communities about the dangers of infibulation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: female genital mutilation - the partial or total removal of external female genitalia for non-medical reasons
