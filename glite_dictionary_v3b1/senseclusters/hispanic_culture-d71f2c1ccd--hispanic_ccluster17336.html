Hispanic culture

noun
customs and traditions of Spanish-speaking peoples
2.5
Pronunciation: / hɪˈspænɪk ˈkʌltʃɚ / (AmE), / hɪˈspænɪk ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
The customs, traditions, social practices, and values characteristic of Spanish-speaking communities, especially those of Latin America and Spain.
