mizuna

noun
Japanese leafy vegetable
Pronunciation: / miˈzuːnə / (AmE), / mɪˈzuːnə / (BrE)
a Japanese leafy green vegetable with feathery leaves and a mild, peppery flavor, often used in salads and stir-fries

Farmers' markets often sell bunches of mizuna alongside other leafy greens.
The chef recommended adding mizuna to the stir-fry for extra flavor and color.
