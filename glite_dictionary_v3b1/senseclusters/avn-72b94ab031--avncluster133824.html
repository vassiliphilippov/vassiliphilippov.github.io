AVN

noun
bone tissue death
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ viː ˈɛn / (AmE), / ˌeɪ viː ˈɛn / (BrE)
a medical abbreviation for avascular necrosis, a condition in which bone tissue dies due to a lack of blood supply

Examples

AVN diagnosis
The MRI confirmed that the patient had AVN in his left femur.
Early diagnosis of AVN can help prevent further joint damage.
Doctors often monitor patients on steroids for signs of AVN.

Related Terms

  • synonym: avascular necrosis - death of bone tissue due to lack of blood supply
  • synonym: osteonecrosis - death of bone tissue caused by reduced blood flow
