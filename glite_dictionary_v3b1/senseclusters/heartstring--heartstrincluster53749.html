heartstring

noun
cord in heart
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑːrt.strɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈhɑːt.strɪŋ/ (BrE)
a tendinous cord inside the heart that helps control the movement of the heart valves

Examples

A heartstring in surgery
The surgeon carefully inspected the torn heartstring before repairing the valve.
Damage to a heartstring can affect how the heart valves function.
During the operation, the doctor replaced a ruptured heartstring to restore normal blood flow.
