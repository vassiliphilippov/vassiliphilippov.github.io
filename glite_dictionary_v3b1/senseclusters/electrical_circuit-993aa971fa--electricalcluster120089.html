electrical circuit

noun
closed path for electric current
3.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl ˈsɜrkɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl ˈsɜːkɪt / (BrE)
A closed path or network through which electric current flows, typically consisting of wires and electrical components.

Examples

An electrical circuit path
