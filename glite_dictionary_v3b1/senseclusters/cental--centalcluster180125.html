cental

noun
US weight unit equal to 100 pounds
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛntəl / (BrE)
A unit of weight in the United States equal to 100 pounds.

Examples

One cental weight
A shipment of potatoes weighed one cental.
Farmers commonly used the cental when recording crop yields in the early 20th century.
The price per cental of cotton varied depending on the season.
