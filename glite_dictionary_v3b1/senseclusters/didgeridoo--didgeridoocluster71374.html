didgeridoo

noun
Australian wind instrument
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪdʒəriˈduː / (AmE), / ˌdɪdʒəriˈduː / (BrE)
a long wooden wind instrument from Australia, traditionally played by Aboriginal people, that produces a deep, droning sound
