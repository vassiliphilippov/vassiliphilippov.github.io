virtuosic

adjective
exceptionally skilled
Pronunciation: / ˌvɜrtʃuˈɑsɪk / (AmE), / ˌvɜːtjʊˈɒsɪk / (BrE)
showing exceptional skill or technique, especially in music or the arts

Examples

A virtuosic performance
The pianist delivered a virtuosic performance that left the audience in awe.
Her virtuosic guitar solo demonstrated years of dedicated practice and talent.
Critics praised the dancer's virtuosic technique and expressive movements on stage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amateurish - lacking professional skill or expertise
  • synonym: masterful - showing great skill or expertise
