redolent of

adjective
strongly smelling of or reminiscent of
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdələnt ʌv / (AmE), / ˈrɛdələnt ɒv / (BrE)
having a strong smell of or strongly reminiscent of something, especially a particular quality or atmosphere

Examples

Redolent of childhood memories
Her writing is redolent of childhood memories and nostalgia.
The air was redolent of spices from the nearby street market.
The old library was redolent of leather-bound books and polished wood.
