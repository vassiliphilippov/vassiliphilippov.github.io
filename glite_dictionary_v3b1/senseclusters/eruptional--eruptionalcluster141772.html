eruptional

adjective
tooth emergence
8.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrʌpʃənəl / (AmE), / ɪˈrʌpʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the emergence of teeth through the gums or the process of tooth eruption

Examples

Eruptional tooth growth
The dentist monitored the child's eruptional development to ensure healthy tooth growth.
Certain eruptional problems can cause discomfort during childhood.
A delay in eruptional timing may indicate underlying dental issues.
