bomp

noun
playful thumping sound
Pronunciation: / bɑmp / (AmE), / bɒmp / (BrE)
a playful, thumping sound or the act of making such a sound, often in music or play

Examples

A loud bomp
The drummer added a loud bomp to the end of the song.
With each step, his boots made a soft bomp on the wooden floor.
The children laughed every time the balloon made a bomp against the wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bang - a sudden loud noise, often from an impact
  • synonym: thump - a heavy, dull sound made by something hitting a surface
