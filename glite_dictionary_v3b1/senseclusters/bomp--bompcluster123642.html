bomp

noun
playful thumping sound
8.4 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / bÉ‘mp / (AmE), / bÉ’mp / (BrE)
a playful, thumping sound or the act of making such a sound, often in music or play

Examples

The children laughed every time the balloon made a bomp against the wall.
