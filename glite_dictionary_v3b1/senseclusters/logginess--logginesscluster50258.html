logginess

noun
dull or sluggish state due to weariness
9.5 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈlɑːg.ɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈlɔːg.ɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈlɒg.ɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
A state of feeling dull, sluggish, or listless, often as a result of tiredness or lack of energy.
