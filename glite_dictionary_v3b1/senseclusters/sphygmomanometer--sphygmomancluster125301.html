sphygmomanometer

noun
device for measuring blood pressure
5.8 8.8
Pronunciation: / sfɪɡmoʊməˈnɑmɪtər / (AmE), / sfɪɡməʊməˈnɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument for measuring blood pressure, typically consisting of an inflatable cuff to restrict blood flow and a manometer to measure the pressure
