sphygmomanometer

noun
device for measuring blood pressure
5.8
Pronunciation: / sfɪɡmoʊməˈnɑmɪtər / (AmE), / sfɪɡməʊməˈnɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument for measuring blood pressure, typically consisting of an inflatable cuff to restrict blood flow and a manometer to measure the pressure

Examples

Digital sphygmomanometer device
A digital sphygmomanometer is often used for quick and accurate readings in clinics.
He learned how to use a sphygmomanometer during his first week of medical training.
The nurse wrapped the sphygmomanometer cuff around my arm to take my blood pressure.
