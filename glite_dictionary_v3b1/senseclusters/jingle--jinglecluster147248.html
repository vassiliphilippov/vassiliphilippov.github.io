jingle

noun
a light metallic ringing sound
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
a light, metallic ringing sound, often produced by small metal objects striking each other

Examples

A jingle of coins
I heard a jingle every time he moved his pocket of loose change.
She enjoyed the cheerful jingle of the holiday bells.
The jingle of keys echoed through the empty hallway.
