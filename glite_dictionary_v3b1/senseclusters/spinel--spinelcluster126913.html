spinel

noun
a mineral used as a gemstone
Pronunciation: / spɪˈnɛl / (AmE), / spɪˈnɛl / (BrE)
a hard, glassy mineral composed of magnesium and aluminum oxides, found in various colors and often used as a gemstone

Examples

A spinel gemstone
A rare blue spinel was recently discovered in the mountains of Myanmar.
Collectors prize spinel for its vibrant range of colors and brilliance.
The crown was adorned with a large red spinel that gleamed in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ruby - a red gemstone, sometimes consisting of spinel as well as corundum
