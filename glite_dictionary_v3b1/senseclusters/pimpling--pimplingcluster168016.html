pimpling

noun
surface bumps
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪmplɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪmplɪŋ / (BrE)
small raised bumps or irregularities appearing on a surface, such as wood, metal, or paint

Examples

Pimpling on wood
The old paint on the door developed pimpling after years of exposure to moisture.
Careless application of the finish resulted in noticeable pimpling on the wooden surface.
Inspect the metal for any signs of pimpling before applying a new coat of paint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blistering - formation of small raised areas or blisters on a surface
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of having an even, flat surface without bumps
