Guinea worm

noun
parasitic roundworm causing dracunculiasis
8.7 7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈgɪn.ɪ wɜːrm/ /; / /ˈgɪn.iː wɜːrm/ (AmE), /ˈgɪn.ɪ wɜːm/ /; / /ˈgɪn.iː wɜːm/ (BrE)
