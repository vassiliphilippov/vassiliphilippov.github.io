patriotically

adverb
in a patriotic manner
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪtriˈɑtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpætriˈɒtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows love, devotion, or support for one's country.

Examples

To patriotically support
He patriotically supported local industries by buying only products made in his country.
She patriotically volunteered for service during a time of national crisis.
The crowd sang the national anthem patriotically at the start of the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patriotic - having or expressing devotion to and vigorous support for one's country
  • antonym: unpatriotically - in an unpatriotic manner
