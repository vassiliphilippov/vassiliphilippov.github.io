start of the day

noun
the beginning of the day
Pronunciation: / stɑrt əv ðə deɪ / (AmE), / stɑːt əv ðə deɪ / (BrE)
the beginning of the day, especially the early morning, when daily activities typically commence

Examples

The start of the day
At the start of the day, the team gathers for a brief meeting.
She always enjoys a cup of coffee at the start of the day.
Waking up at the start of the day helps me feel productive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beginning - the point in time or place at which something starts
  • antonym: end of the day - the conclusion or final part of the day
