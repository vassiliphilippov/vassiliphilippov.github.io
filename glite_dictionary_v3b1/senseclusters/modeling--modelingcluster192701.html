modeling

noun
act of creating a representation
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːdəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɒdəlɪŋ /; / ˈmɒd(ə)lɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of creating a representation of something, often on a smaller scale or as a prototype
