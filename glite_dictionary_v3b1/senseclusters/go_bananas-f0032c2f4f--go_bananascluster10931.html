go bananas

verb
become extremely excited or crazy
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ bəˈnænəz / (AmE), / ɡəʊ bəˈnænəz / (BrE)
to become extremely excited, angry, or crazy

Examples

To go bananas with excitement
Everyone went bananas after the team scored the winning goal in the last minute.
She went bananas when she found out she had won the lottery.
The children went bananas when their favorite singer walked onto the stage.
If you tell her the news right now, she'll go bananas.
My parents went bananas when they saw my grades.
The crowd went bananas when the team scored the winning goal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: go crazy - to lose control emotionally
  • synonym: go wild - to become uncontrollably excited
  • synonym: lose it - to lose one's self-control, especially becoming angry or upset
