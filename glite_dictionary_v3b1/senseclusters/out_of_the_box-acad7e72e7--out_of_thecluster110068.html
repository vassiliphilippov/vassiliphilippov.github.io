out of the box

adjective
ready to use without setup
4.0
Pronunciation: /aʊt əv ðə bɑːks/ (AmE), /aʊt əv ðə bɒks/ (BrE)
ready to use immediately after purchase or installation, without requiring additional setup, configuration, or modification.

Examples

Works out of the box
The new laptop arrived and worked perfectly out of the box with no installation required.
This smart thermostat is out of the box compatible with our heating system, so setup was fast.
Buyers appreciated that the furniture came out of the box assembled, saving them hours of work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: customize - to change something to suit particular needs, the opposite of being ready to use
  • synonym: plug-and-play - designed to work as soon as it is connected, with no additional setup
  • synonym: ready-to-use - able to be used immediately without further preparation or modification
