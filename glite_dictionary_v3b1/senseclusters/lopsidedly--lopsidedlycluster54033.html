lopsidedly

adverb
in an uneven or crooked manner
7.9
Pronunciation: / lɑpˈsaɪdɪdli / (AmE), / lɒpˈsaɪdɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that is uneven, crooked, or lacking balance; not symmetrical.

Examples

To smile lopsidedly
He grinned lopsidedly, showing he wasn’t quite sure what to say.
She tied the bow lopsidedly, giving the present a quirky look.
The painting hung lopsidedly on the wall, bothering everyone who noticed.
