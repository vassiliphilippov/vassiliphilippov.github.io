ski resort

noun
place offering lodging and facilities for skiing
Pronunciation: / ski rɪˈzɔrt /; / ski ɹɪˈzɔɹt / (AmE), / ski rɪˈzɔːt /; / ski ɹɪˈzɔːt / (BrE)
a place or area with accommodation and facilities where people go to ski and enjoy winter sports
