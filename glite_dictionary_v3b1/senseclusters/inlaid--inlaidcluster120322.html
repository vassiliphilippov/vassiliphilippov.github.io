inlaid

adjective
decorated with set-in materials
4.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈleɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈleɪd / (BrE)
decorated by having pieces of a different material set into its surface, usually in a pattern or design

Examples

Inlaid woodwork
She admired the inlaid woodwork on the floor of the historic building.
The jewelry box, inlaid with intricate patterns, was a family heirloom.
The top of the old writing desk was beautifully inlaid with mother-of-pearl and brass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inlay - to decorate a surface by embedding different material into it
