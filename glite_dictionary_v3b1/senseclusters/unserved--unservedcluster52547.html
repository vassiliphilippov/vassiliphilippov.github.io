unserved

adjective
not legally delivered
7.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈsɜːrvd/ /; / /ən.ˈsɜːrvd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈsɜːvd/ /; / /ən.ˈsɜːvd/ (BrE)

Examples

Unserved legal papers
The lawsuit could not proceed because the defendant remained unserved with the complaint.
If a party is unserved, the court may delay the hearing until proper notice is given.
