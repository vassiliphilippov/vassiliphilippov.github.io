bronze

adjective
made of bronze alloy
5.9
Pronunciation: / brɑːnz / (AmE), / brɒnz / (BrE)
(of objects) made from or consisting of bronze, a yellowish-brown alloy of copper and tin.

Examples

Bronze statue
A magnificent bronze plaque was mounted on the wall.
She wore a bronze bracelet that gleamed in the sunlight.
The museum displays a bronze statue from ancient Greece.
