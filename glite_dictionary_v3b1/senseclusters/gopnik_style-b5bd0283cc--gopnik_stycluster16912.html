gopnik style

noun
fashion typical of the gopnik subculture
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑpnɪk staɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡɒpnɪk staɪl / (BrE)
A fashion style characterized by tracksuits, sneakers, and other elements associated with the 'gopnik' subculture, especially in Russia and Eastern Europe.

Examples

Gopnik style tracksuit
He showed up in full gopnik style, wearing a tracksuit and squatting outside the store.
Many people adopt gopnik style as a playful nod to Eastern European street culture.
She recognized his gopnik style immediately from his flat cap and Adidas sneakers.

Related Terms
