Eschrichtiidae

noun
family of gray whales
Pronunciation: / ɛsˌkrɪkˈtiːɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ɛsˌkrɪkˈtiːɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of baleen whales that includes only the gray whale.

Examples

The Eschrichtiidae family
Fossil evidence suggests that members of the Eschrichtiidae once inhabited both the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans.
Marine biologists have conducted extensive studies on the migration patterns of the Eschrichtiidae.
The Eschrichtiidae are unique among whales in having a single extant species, the gray whale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gray whale - a large baleen whale of the species Eschrichtius robustus
