Eschrichtiidae

noun
family of gray whales

Examples

The Eschrichtiidae family

Marine biologists have conducted extensive studies on the migration patterns of the Eschrichtiidae.
The Eschrichtiidae are unique among whales in having a single extant species, the gray whale.
