critical review

noun
scholarly literature survey and critique
4.2 0.17/m

A scholarly article or essay that surveys, summarises, and critically evaluates the existing research and literature on a particular topic.

Examples

A critical review of research
The journal commissioned a critical review of recent studies on urban air pollution.
For her thesis, she wrote a comprehensive critical review that identified gaps in the research.
Our department journal published a critical review comparing methodologies across climate-change papers.

Synonyms

  • review article
    3.9 0.95/m
    a review article evaluates or summarises a single work or paper, while a critical review surveys and evaluates wider research literature on a topic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: literature review - a survey of scholarly publications on a specific topic that summarises and often evaluates findings and debates
  • synonym: review article - a scholarly article that summarises recent developments, methods, and debates in research on a particular subject
