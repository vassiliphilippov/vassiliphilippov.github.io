rubbery

adjective
tough, elastic, hard to chew
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌb.ɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈrʌb.ər.i / (BrE)
(of food) having a tough, elastic texture that is difficult to chew.

Examples

Rubbery eggs
If the eggs are cooked too long, they can become rubbery and unpleasant to eat.
She complained that the calamari was rubbery and overcooked.
The steak was so rubbery that it took me minutes to chew one bite.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tender - (of food) easy to cut or chew
  • synonym: tough - (of food) difficult to cut or chew
