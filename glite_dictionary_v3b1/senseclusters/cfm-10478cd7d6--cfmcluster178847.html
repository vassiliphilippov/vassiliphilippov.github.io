CFM

sym
airflow unit
5.1
Pronunciation: / si ɛf ɛm / (AmE), / si ɛf ɛm / (BrE)
an abbreviation for cubic feet per minute, a unit measuring the rate of airflow

Examples

400 CFM airflow
The air conditioner is rated at 400 CFM, which is suitable for a medium-sized room.
To improve ventilation, the engineer recommended a fan with a higher CFM rating.
Always check the CFM of a device to ensure it meets your airflow needs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: L/min - liters per minute, a metric unit for measuring flow rate
