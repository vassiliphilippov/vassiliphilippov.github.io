sinapism

noun
mustard plaster used as a counterirritant
8.7
Pronunciation: / sɪˈneɪ.pɪzəm / (AmE), / sɪˈneɪ.pɪzəm / (BrE)
A plaster containing powdered black mustard, applied to the skin as a counterirritant or rubefacient.

Examples

A sinapism plaster
