tusky

adjective
having tusks
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌski / (AmE), / ˈtʌski / (BrE)
having tusks or resembling a tusk in appearance or form

Examples

Tusky elephant
The tusky elephant used its long teeth to dig up roots.
Hunters were wary of the tusky wild boar roaming the forest.
The artist sculpted a tusky creature for the fantasy exhibit.
