exhibitioner

noun
person who organizes an exhibit
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˌzɪbɪˈʃənər / (AmE), / ɪɡˌzɪbɪˈʃənə / (BrE)
a person who organizes or presents an exhibit for others to see

Examples

An exhibitioner at fairs
As an experienced exhibitioner, she traveled to art fairs across the country.
The gallery praised the exhibitioner for his innovative approach to design.
The leading exhibitioner curated a display of rare manuscripts at the museum.
