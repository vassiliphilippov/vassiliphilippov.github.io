fanjet

noun
aircraft powered by a fanjet engine
5.6 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈfæn.dʒet/ (AmE), /ˈfæn.dʒet/ (BrE)
a jet engine with a large fan (turbofan), or an aircraft powered by such an engine

Examples
