nanowire

noun
ultra-thin wire
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnænoʊˌwaɪr / (AmE), / ˈnænəʊˌwaɪə / (BrE)
a very thin wire with a diameter measured in nanometers, used in electronics, nanotechnology, or produced by microorganisms
