treat

verb
address or discuss a subject
Pronunciation: / trit / (AmE), / triːt / (BrE)
to deal with a subject or topic in speech, writing, or artistic expression

Examples

To treat a topic
Her latest documentary treats the challenges faced by immigrants.
The novel treats the theme of forgiveness in a subtle way.
This lecture will treat the origins of European democracy.
