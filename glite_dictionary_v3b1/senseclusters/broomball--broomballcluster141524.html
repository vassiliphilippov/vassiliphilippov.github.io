broomball

noun
ice sport with brooms
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːmˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈbruːmˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a team sport similar to ice hockey, played on ice with brooms and a ball instead of sticks and a puck

Examples

A broomball match
Every winter, our community organizes a broomball tournament on the local rink.
Unlike hockey, players in broomball use special shoes instead of skates.
She joined a broomball league to stay active during the cold months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ice hockey - a fast-paced team sport played on ice with sticks and a puck
