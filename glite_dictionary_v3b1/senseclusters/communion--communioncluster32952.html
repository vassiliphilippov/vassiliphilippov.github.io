communion

noun
group of Christians with shared faith
4.2
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunjən / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnjən / (BrE)
a group of Christians who share the same religious faith and rites

Examples

A communion of churches
After the schism, a new communion of believers was formed.
He was raised in the Lutheran communion and attended services every Sunday.
The Anglican communion includes churches from different countries around the world.
