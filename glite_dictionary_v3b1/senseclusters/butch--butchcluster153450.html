butch

noun
masculine lesbian (slang, sometimes offensive)
Pronunciation: / bʊtʃ / (AmE), / bʊtʃ / (BrE)
(slang, sometimes offensive) A lesbian who displays masculine appearance or behavior.

Examples

A butch lesbian
In some lesbian communities, the butch identity is embraced as a source of pride.
Many people described her as a butch because of her short hair and masculine clothes.
She never liked being labeled a butch, even though she dressed in a more masculine style.

Related Terms

  • antonym: femme - a lesbian with feminine appearance or behavior
