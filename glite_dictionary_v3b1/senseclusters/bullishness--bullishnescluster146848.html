bullishness

noun
optimistic confidence
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈbʊlɪʃnəs / (BrE)
a confident or optimistic attitude, especially the belief that prices or markets will rise

Examples

Market bullishness
The recent bullishness in the market has encouraged more people to invest.
Her bullishness about the company's future impressed the board of directors.
Periods of strong bullishness are often followed by market corrections.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bearishness - a pessimistic attitude, especially the belief that prices or markets will fall
  • synonym: confidence - a feeling of certainty or trust in something
  • synonym: optimism - a hopeful or positive attitude about the future
