Synapsida

noun
a group of extinct reptiles ancestral to mammals
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnæpsɪdə / (AmE), / sɪˈnapsɪdə / (BrE)
an extinct group of reptiles from the Permian to Jurassic periods, regarded as ancestors of mammals
