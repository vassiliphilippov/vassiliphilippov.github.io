Gulf of Lepanto

proper noun
inlet of the Ionian Sea, Greece
Pronunciation: / ɡʌlf əv ləˈpæntoʊ / (AmE), / ɡʌlf əv lɪˈpantoʊ / (BrE)
an inlet of the Ionian Sea situated between central Greece and the Peloponnesus

Examples
