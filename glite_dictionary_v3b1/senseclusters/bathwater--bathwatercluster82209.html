bathwater

noun
water in a bathtub
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæθˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
the water in a bathtub, especially after someone has bathed in it

Examples

Warm bathwater
After her long soak, the bathwater was filled with bubbles and rose petals.
He accidentally dropped his toy into the bathwater while playing in the tub.
The bathwater was still warm when she stepped out of the bathroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bathtub - a large container for holding water in which a person bathes
