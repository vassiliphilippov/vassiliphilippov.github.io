segment

noun
a part into which something divides
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛɡ.mənt /; / ˈsɛɡmənt / (AmE), / ˈsɛɡ.mənt /; / ˈsɛɡmənt / (BrE)
a part or section of something that is divided from the whole
