superhero

noun
fictional character with superhuman powers
Pronunciation: / ˈsuː.pɚˌhɪr.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pəˌhɪə.rəʊ / (BrE)
a fictional character, especially in comic books or movies, who possesses abilities beyond those of ordinary people and typically uses them to fight evil or protect the public

Examples

Comic book superhero
Children all over the world dream of becoming a superhero and saving the day.
He collects vintage comics and has an impressive superhero figurine display.
The new film features a female superhero with the power to control the weather.
