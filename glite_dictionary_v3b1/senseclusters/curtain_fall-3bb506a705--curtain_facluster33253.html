curtain fall

noun
end of a performance or event
6.0 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…úÀêrt…ôn f…îÀêl / (AmE), / Ààk…úÀêtn f…îÀêl / (BrE)
the end of a theatrical performance or event, especially marked by the lowering of the stage curtain; the conclusion of something

Examples

The curtain fall
The actor took a final bow as the curtain fall signaled the end of the play.
The festival's organizers promised surprises until the very curtain fall.
There was a standing ovation at the curtain fall of the performance.

Synonyms

  • closing curtain
    3.8 0.00/m
    closing curtain signals the final scene and lowering of the stage cloth, while curtain fall names the end of the performance.
  • final curtain
    4.6 0.12/m
    final curtain marks the last moment of a show in a simple, often dramatic way, while curtain fall refers to that theatrical ending.
