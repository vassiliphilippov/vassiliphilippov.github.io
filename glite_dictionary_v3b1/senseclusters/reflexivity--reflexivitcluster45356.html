reflexivity

noun
relation of reflexive pronoun to antecedent
9.1 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåriÀêfl…õkÀàs…™v…ôti / (AmE), / ÀåriÀêfl…õkÀàs…™v…™ti / (BrE)
(Linguistics) The relationship in which a reflexive pronoun refers back to its antecedent within the same clause or sentence.

Examples

Reflexivity in pronouns
Teachers often explain reflexivity using examples with subjects referring to themselves.
The reflexivity in the sentence 'She saw herself in the mirror' is displayed by the pronoun 'herself'.
Understanding reflexivity is essential for studying sentence structure in English grammar.

Synonyms

  • reflexiveness
    9.4 0.02/m
    reflexiveness names the grammatical property of a pronoun referring back to its subject, while reflexivity is the technical term used by linguists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reflexive pronoun - a pronoun that refers back to the subject (e.g. 'himself')
