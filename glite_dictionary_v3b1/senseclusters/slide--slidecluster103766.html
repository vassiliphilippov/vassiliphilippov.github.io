slide

a mass of earth or rock descending a slope

Examples

Synonyms

    3.3 2.30/m

  • landslip
    7.5 0.00/m
    landslip is a mass of earth or rock that moves down a slope, often from a cliff, while slide refers to any large moving mass.
  • downslide
    6.5 0.00/m
    downslide is a sudden downward movement of earth, rocks, or debris, while slide can be slower or include snow.
