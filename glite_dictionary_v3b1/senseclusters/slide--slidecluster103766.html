slide

noun
a mass of earth or rock descending a slope
2.3
Pronunciation: / slaɪd / (AmE), / slaɪd / (BrE)
a large mass of earth, rock, snow, or other material moving or falling down a slope

Examples

A rock slide
Geologists studied the area to understand the cause of the recent slide.
Several homes were damaged when a slide occurred on the hillside.
The mountain road was blocked by a massive slide after the heavy rain.
