girder

noun
main steel support beam in construction
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɝː.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡɜː.dər / (BrE)
a long, strong beam, usually made of steel, used as the main support in buildings and bridges

Examples

Steel girder
A crane lifted the steel girder into position at the top of the new building.
During construction, workers bolted each girder securely into place.
The massive girder supported the entire weight of the bridge above the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beam - a long, sturdy piece of squared timber or metal supporting part of a structure
