plate armor

noun
historical body armor of metal plates
4.3 6.4
Pronunciation: /pleɪt ˈɑːr.mər/ (AmE), /pleɪt ˈɑː.mə/ (BrE)
a type of personal armor made from large metal or modern material plates, worn for protection in battle or against bullets

  • synonym: body armor - protective clothing or equipment worn to stop or reduce damage from bullets and other threats
  • synonym: body armour - protective clothing or equipment worn to reduce injury from bullets, knives, or shrapnel
  • antonym: chainmail - armor consisting of small metal rings linked together
  • antonym: mail - flexible armor made of interlinked metal rings
