prerequisite

adjective
required as a prior condition
7.4 5.3
Pronunciation: /priːr.ˈe.kwə.zət/ /; / /prɪr.ˈe.kwɪ.zɪt/ (AmE), /priːr.ˈe.kwɪ.zɪt/ /; / /prɪr.ˈe.kwɪ.zɪt/ (BrE)
Required as a prior condition for something else to happen or be done.

Examples

Prerequisite skills needed
A valid driver's license is prerequisite to renting a car in most countries.
Certification in basic first aid is often prerequisite for working as a camp counselor.
Completion of the introductory course is a prerequisite for enrollment in advanced classes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: optional - not required; left to choice
