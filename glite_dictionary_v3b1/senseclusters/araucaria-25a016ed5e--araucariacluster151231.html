Araucaria

noun
genus of evergreen coniferous trees
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌærəˈkɛriə / (AmE), / ˌarɔːˈkɛːrɪə / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen coniferous trees in the family Araucariaceae, native to the southern hemisphere and including species such as the monkey puzzle tree.
