gropple

verb
to grope or struggle
to grope or struggle, especially using the hands, often in the dark or when searching for something

Examples

To gropple for keys
He had to gropple around in the dark to find his keys.
She groppled through her bag, trying to find her phone.
The children groppled in the sand, searching for hidden shells.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fumble - to use the hands clumsily while doing or searching for something
  • synonym: grope - to search for something by feeling with the hands
