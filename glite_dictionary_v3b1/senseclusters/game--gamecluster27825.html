game

noun
activity regarded as trivial or not serious
Pronunciation: / ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ɡeɪm / (BrE)
an activity or situation regarded as frivolous or not to be taken seriously; something trivial or inconsequential

Examples

School is a game
For him, school was always just a game, never something to worry about.
She realized that her future was at risk and this was no longer a game.
The negotiations were no game—there were real stakes involved.

Related Terms

  • antonym: serious business - something regarded as important or requiring earnest consideration
  • synonym: trifle - something of little value or importance
