littered

adjective
covered with scattered objects or rubbish
3.6 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪt.ərd/ (AmE), /ˈlɪt.əd/ (BrE)
covered or scattered with a large number of things in an untidy way, especially rubbish or debris.

Examples

Street littered with rubbish
After the parade, the street was littered with confetti and empty bottles.
Her desk is always littered with papers and old coffee cups.
The beaches were littered with plastic waste after the storm.

Related Terms
