auction pitch

noun
card game where opening lead sets trump
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔkʃən pɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɔːkʃən pɪtʃ / (BrE)
a card game, based on all-fours, in which the first card led determines the trump suit

Examples

Auction pitch card game
In auction pitch, the player who wins the bid leads the first card, establishing the trump suit.
Many families in the Midwest have a tradition of gathering for games of auction pitch during the holidays.
They spent the afternoon playing auction pitch with friends at the community center.
