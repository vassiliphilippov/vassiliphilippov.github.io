euchre

verb
to trick or defeat
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːkər / (AmE), / ˈjuːkə / (BrE)
to defeat or trick someone, especially by outsmarting them, originally in the card game euchre.

Examples

To euchre an opponent
He felt completely euchred after his opponent played the winning card.
They realized too late that they had been euchred by a clever trick.
In the final round, she euchred her rivals with a surprising move.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deceive - to trick or mislead someone
  • antonym: lose - to be defeated or fail to win
  • synonym: outwit - to defeat by being more clever
