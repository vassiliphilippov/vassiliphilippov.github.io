Examples

Easily comprehensible text
His handwriting is hardly comprehensible to anyone except himself.

Synonyms

    3.7 0.01/m
    comprehendible is a less common variant seen in some texts, while comprehensible is the standard modern word people use.
  • understandable
    1.6 11/m
    understandable is the common everyday word used by most speakers, while comprehensible sounds slightly more formal and book-like.
  • intelligible
    4.4 2.21/m
    intelligible is clear and formal, often in writing or study, while comprehensible is more general and used in speech.
  • explainable
    3.8 0.46/m
    explainable can be made clear by giving reasons or steps, while comprehensible means already easy to understand in itself.
  • explicable
    5.5 0.39/m
    explicable can be accounted for or justified by facts, while comprehensible focuses on being easily understood by the mind.
  • interpretable
    5.7 0.32/m
    interpretable can have different meanings or be read in many ways, while comprehensible means it is clear and understood.
  • accessible
    3.4 5.01/m
    accessible is written or spoken so most people can read and enjoy it, while comprehensible means mentally clear and understood.
  • graspable
    3.8 0.15/m
    graspable is easy to take in mentally or picture, while comprehensible means it can be fully understood.
  • fathomable
    4.9 0.02/m
    fathomable is something you can understand after study or thought, while comprehensible implies it is clear without much effort.
  • scrutable
    7.0 0.00/m
    scrutable is open to close study or interpretation, while comprehensible means it is plainly understood by the mind.
  • apprehensible
    7.5 0.04/m
    apprehensible can be mentally grasped but sounds rare or formal, while comprehensible is the common modern word people use.
