nocturia

noun
excessive nighttime urination
Pronunciation: / nɑkˈtʊriə / (AmE), / nɒkˈtjʊəriə / (BrE)
a condition characterized by excessive urination at night, especially during sleeping hours

Examples

Night nocturia condition
Limiting fluid intake in the evening can sometimes help reduce nocturia.
Older adults often experience nocturia, which can disturb their sleep and daily functioning.
The doctor diagnosed him with nocturia after he reported frequent nighttime trips to the bathroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enuresis - involuntary urination, especially at night
