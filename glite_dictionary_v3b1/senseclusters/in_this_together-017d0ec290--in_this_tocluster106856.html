in this together

adjective
sharing a difficult situation or experience
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪn ðɪs təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / ɪn ðɪs təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
used to express that people are sharing the same difficult situation, problem, or experience and must support each other.

Examples

We are in this together
As teammates, we know we're in this together and must work as one unit.
During the crisis, the company reminded employees that they were in this together.
Whatever happens, remember that we're all in this together.
