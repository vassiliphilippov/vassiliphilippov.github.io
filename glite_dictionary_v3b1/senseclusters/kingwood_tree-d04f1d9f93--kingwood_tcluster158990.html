kingwood tree

noun
Brazilian tree supplying decorative hardwood
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋ.wʊd triː / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋ.wʊd triː / (BrE)
A small Brazilian tree (Dalbergia cearensis) that produces hard, richly colored wood used especially in fine cabinetmaking.

Examples

The kingwood tree wood
Furniture makers prize the wood of the kingwood tree for its beauty and durability.
The kingwood tree is native to Brazil and valued in fine woodworking.
The ornate desk was crafted from the rare kingwood tree.
