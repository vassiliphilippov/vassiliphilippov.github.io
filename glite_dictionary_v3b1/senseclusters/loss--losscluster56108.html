loss

noun
thing or amount that is lost
Pronunciation: / lɑs / (AmE), / lɒs / (BrE)
an amount or thing that is lost, especially as the result of an accident, theft, or other misfortune

Examples

Insurance loss
After the burglary, their insurance covered the loss of jewelry and electronics.
The drought led to a significant loss of crops for the farmers.
The fire caused a complete loss of the old warehouse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deficit - an amount by which something is less than expected or required
  • antonym: gain - an increase or addition; something gained
