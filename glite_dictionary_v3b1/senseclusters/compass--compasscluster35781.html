compass

verb
to surround fully
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmpəs / (AmE), / ˈkʌmpəs / (BrE)

Examples

Walls compass the garden

The city was compassed by a wide river that protected it from invaders.
Thick fog compassed the mountain, making it difficult to see the path ahead.
