interwar

adjective
between two wars
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈwɔr / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈwɔː / (BrE)
relating to the period between two wars, especially the time between World War I and World War II

Examples

Interwar years
The interwar period saw significant changes in European politics and society.
Many artists experimented with new techniques during the interwar years.
Economic instability was a major issue in several countries throughout the interwar era.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postwar - relating to the period after a war
  • antonym: prewar - relating to the period before a war
