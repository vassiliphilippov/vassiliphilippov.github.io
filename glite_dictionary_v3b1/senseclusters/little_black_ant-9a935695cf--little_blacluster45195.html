little black ant

noun
a tiny shiny black ant species
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬.əl blæk ænt / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl blæk ænt / (BrE)
a very small, shiny black ant (Monomorium minimum) that nests outdoors but often invades houses in search of food

Examples

A little black ant colony
A colony of little black ants had built a nest under the garden stones.
The little black ant is a common household pest during summer months.
We found a trail of little black ants marching across the kitchen floor.
