symbologist

noun
expert in symbols
Pronunciation: / sɪmˈbɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / sɪmˈbɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or interprets symbols and their meanings

Examples

The symbologist studies symbols
The symbologist was called to help decode the mysterious carvings found in the temple.
As a symbologist, she often gave lectures on the meanings behind ancient religious icons.
The novel features a famous symbologist who solves puzzles using his knowledge of symbols.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryptologist - a person who studies codes and ciphers
  • synonym: semiotician - a person who studies signs and symbols and their use
