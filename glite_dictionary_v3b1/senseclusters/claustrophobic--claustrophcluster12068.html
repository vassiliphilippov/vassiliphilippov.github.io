claustrophobic

adjective
afraid of confined or small spaces
6.7 5.6
Pronunciation: /klɔːs.trə.ˈfoʊ.bɪk/ (AmE), /klɔːs.trə.ˈfəʊ.bɪk/ (BrE)
suffering from or affected by claustrophobia; having an abnormal fear of confined or enclosed spaces.

Examples

Claustrophobic person
During the flight, her claustrophobic tendencies became apparent as she struggled to stay calm.
She feels extremely claustrophobic in elevators and always avoids them when possible.
The small, windowless room made him so claustrophobic that he had to leave immediately.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agoraphobic - someone with a fear of open or crowded spaces
  • synonym: claustrophobia - the fear of closed spaces
