respect

noun
courteous expression of esteem
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈspekt/ /; / /riː.ˈspekt/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈspekt/ (BrE)
polite actions, expressions, or attitudes that show regard or consideration for others

Examples

Show respect
As a sign of respect, she stood when the judge entered the courtroom.

She always treats her colleagues with respect, no matter their position.
The children showed great respect for their teacher during the ceremony.
