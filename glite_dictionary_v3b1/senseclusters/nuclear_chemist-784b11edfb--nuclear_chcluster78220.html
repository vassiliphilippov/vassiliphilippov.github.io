nuclear chemist

noun
chemist specializing in nuclear chemistry
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːkliɚ ˈkɛmɪst / (AmE), / ˈnjuːkliə ˈkɛmɪst / (BrE)
a chemist who specializes in the study of chemical processes involving radioactive elements and nuclear reactions

Examples

A nuclear chemist studies atoms
After earning her PhD, she became a respected nuclear chemist at the national laboratory.
Many nuclear chemists contribute to advancements in medical imaging technology.
The nuclear chemist explained the process of radioactive decay to the students.
