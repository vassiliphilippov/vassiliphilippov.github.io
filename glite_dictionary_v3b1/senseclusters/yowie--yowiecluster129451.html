yowie

interjection
exclamation of pain
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjaʊi / (AmE), / ˈjaʊi / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express surprise, pain, or disgust

Examples

Yowie! That hurts
He stubbed his toe on the table and shouted yowie in pain.
When the cold water hit her back, she let out a loud yowie.
Seeing the mess in the kitchen, she exclaimed yowie in disgust.
