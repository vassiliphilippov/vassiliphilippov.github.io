angel cake

noun
light sponge cake without egg yolks
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪndʒəl keɪk / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəl keɪk / (BrE)
A light, airy sponge cake made without egg yolks, typically using only egg whites, flour, and sugar.

Examples

A angel cake slice
Have you ever tried making an angel cake from scratch without using any egg yolks?

The angel cake was so light and fluffy that it almost melted in our mouths.
