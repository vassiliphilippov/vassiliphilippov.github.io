recall

noun
military bugle call for returning troops
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɔl / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːl / (BrE)
a bugle call that signals troops to return to their quarters or assembly point

Examples

Military recall signal
As soon as the recall sounded, the soldiers began making their way back to camp.
At the sound of the recall, all units were required to regroup at headquarters.
The bugler played the recall to signal the end of the exercise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: retreat - a bugle call signaling the end of the day's activities in the military
  • antonym: reveille - a bugle call used to wake military personnel at sunrise
