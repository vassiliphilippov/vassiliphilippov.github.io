suite

noun
set of connected rooms in a hotel
Pronunciation: / swit / (AmE), / swiːt / (BrE)
A set of connected rooms, especially in a hotel, used as a single living unit.

Examples

Hotel suite
The presidential suite offers breathtaking views of the city skyline.
Their honeymoon suite included a private balcony and a hot tub.
We booked a spacious suite for our family vacation at the resort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apartment - a set of rooms used as a dwelling
  • degree lower: room - a single enclosed space in a building
