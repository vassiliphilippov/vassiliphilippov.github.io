mad-dog skullcap

noun
North American medicinal herb
Pronunciation: / ˈmæd dɔɡ ˈskʌlˌkæp / (AmE), / ˈmad dɒɡ ˈskʌl.kæp / (BrE)
a North American herb (Scutellaria lateriflora) of the mint family that was formerly used as a remedy for epilepsy and other nervous disorders
