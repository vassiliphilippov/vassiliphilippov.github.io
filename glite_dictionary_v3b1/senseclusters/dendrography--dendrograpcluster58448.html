dendrography

noun
tree illustration science
Pronunciation: / dɛnˈdrɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / dɛnˈdrɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the art or science of describing or illustrating trees, especially their forms and structures

Examples

Dendrography in textbooks
The textbook included several examples of dendrography to help identify tree species.
She became interested in dendrography after taking a course on plant illustration.
Accurate dendrography is essential for field guides used by botanists and nature enthusiasts.
