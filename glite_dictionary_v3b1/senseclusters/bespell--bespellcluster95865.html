bespell

verb
to enchant magically
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪˈspɛl / (AmE), / bɪˈspɛl / (BrE)
to enchant or put someone or something under a magical spell

Examples

To bespell with magic
The mysterious woman seemed to bespell everyone she met at the festival.
Legends say the wizard could bespell objects to make them move on their own.
He feared the old book might bespell anyone who dared to read it aloud.
The old witch tried to bespell the prince with her magic.
Legends say the forest is bespelled and no one who enters returns unchanged.
She felt as if the music had bespelled her, making her forget everything else.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bewitch - to cast a spell over someone or to attract irresistibly
  • synonym: enchant - to fill someone with great delight or to put under a spell
  • antonym: free - to release from captivity or a spell
