pad

verb
add unnecessary details to inflate content
Pronunciation: / pæd / (AmE), / pæd / (BrE)
to add unnecessary or exaggerated details, information, or content to something, especially to make it seem more impressive or substantial

Examples

To pad a report
He decided to pad his resume with extra skills he didn’t really have.
The company was accused of padding its expense reports to increase reimbursements.
Writers sometimes pad their stories with unnecessary information to meet word counts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exaggerate - to describe as greater than is actually the case
  • synonym: inflate - to make something appear larger by exaggeration
  • antonym: streamline - to make something simpler by removing unnecessary details
