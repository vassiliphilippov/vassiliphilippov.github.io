befit

verb
to be suitable for; to suit
6.1 5.5
Pronunciation: /bɪ.ˈfɪt/ /; / /bə.ˈfɪt/ (AmE), /bɪ.ˈfɪt/ /; / /bə.ˈfɪt/ (BrE)
to be suitable or appropriate for someone or something; to befit means to be worthy of or in keeping with.

Examples

His calm demeanor befits someone in a leadership role.
