breechcloth

noun
loincloth garment
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːtʃˌklɔθ / (AmE), / ˈbriːtʃˌklɒθ / (BrE)
a strip of cloth worn around the loins, especially as a traditional garment by some Indigenous peoples

Examples

A breechcloth garment
He fashioned a simple breechcloth from a strip of woven fabric.
Museum exhibits display traditional breechcloths made from animal hides.
The chief wore a decorated breechcloth during the ceremonial dance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loincloth - a single piece of cloth worn around the hips and groin
