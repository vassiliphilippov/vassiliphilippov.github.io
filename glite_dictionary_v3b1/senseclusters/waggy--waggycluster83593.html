waggy

adjective
tending to wag
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwæɡi / (AmE), / ˈwæɡi / (BrE)
having a tendency to wag or move back and forth quickly, especially referring to a tail

Examples

A waggy tail
The puppy greeted us with a waggy tail and lots of excitement.
Her dog is always so waggy when she comes home from work.
You could tell the cat was happy by its waggy movements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waggish - playful or mischievous, like a wag
