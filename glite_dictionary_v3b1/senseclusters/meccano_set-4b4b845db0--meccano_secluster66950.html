Meccano set

noun
metal construction toy set
7.4
Pronunciation: / məˈkɑːnoʊ sɛt / (AmE), / mɪˈkɑːnəʊ sɛt / (BrE)
A construction toy consisting of metal strips, plates, wheels, and other parts that can be connected with nuts and bolts to build mechanical models.
