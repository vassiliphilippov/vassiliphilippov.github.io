pinheaded

adjective
very small-headed
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
having an unusually small or very small head, especially in comparison to the body

Examples

Pinheaded statue
The ancient figurine had a strangely pinheaded appearance, its tiny skull barely larger than a bead.
Some rare animals are born pinheaded, making their heads look out of proportion to their bodies.
The cartoon character was drawn with a pinheaded look to make it appear comical.

Related Terms

  • synonym: microcephalic - having an abnormally small head
