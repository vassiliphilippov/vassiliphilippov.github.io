duo

noun
pair of two people or things
2.5 0.70/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàduÀê.o ä / (AmE), / ÀàdjuÀê.…ô ä / (BrE)
A pair of people or things that are closely associated or regarded together.

Examples

A duo of rings
She wore a striking duo of bracelets on her wrist.
The comedy duo has entertained audiences for decades.
They make a great duo when working on projects together.
