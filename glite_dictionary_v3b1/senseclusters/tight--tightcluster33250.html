tight

adjective
slang: excellent, impressive
Pronunciation: / taɪt / (AmE), / taɪt / (BrE)
(slang, informal) very good, impressive, or skillfully performed; effective because precise or well rehearsed.

Examples

Tight performance
The band's new single was tight, every note perfectly timed and energetic.
Her routine was so tight that the judges gave her a standing ovation.
After weeks of practice, the team's defense looked tight and almost impossible to score against.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excellent - of very high quality or exceptionally good
  • antonym: lame - not good or impressive; disappointing
