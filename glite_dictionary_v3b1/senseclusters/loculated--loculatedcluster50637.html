loculated

adjective
divided into chambers
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːkjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɒkjʊleɪtɪd /; / ˈlɒkjʊˌleɪtɪd / (BrE)
divided into small chambers or compartments, especially in a medical or biological context
