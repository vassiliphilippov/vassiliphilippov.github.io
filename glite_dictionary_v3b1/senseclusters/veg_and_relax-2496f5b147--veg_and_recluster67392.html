veg and relax

verb
do nothing and rest
4.8
Pronunciation: / vɛdʒ ənd rɪˈlæks / (AmE), / vɛdʒ ənd rɪˈlæks / (BrE)
to spend time doing nothing and resting, usually to recover from stress or tiredness

Examples

To veg and relax at home
After a busy week at work, I just want to veg and relax this weekend.
It's important to give yourself time to veg and relax when life gets overwhelming.
She ordered some pizza, put on a movie, and decided to veg and relax for the evening.
