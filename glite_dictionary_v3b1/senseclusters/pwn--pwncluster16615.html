pwn

verb
to hack and control
9.9
Pronunciation: / poʊn / (AmE), / pəʊn / (BrE)
to gain unauthorized access to or take control of a computer, network, or online account, often by hacking

Examples

After discovering a security flaw, the team realized their system had been pwned.
