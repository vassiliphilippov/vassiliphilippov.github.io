tapu

adjective
sacred or forbidden
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːpuː / (AmE), / ˈtɑːpuː / (BrE)
considered sacred or forbidden by custom, and protected from use, touch, or disturbance

Examples

Tapu land
The ancient forest was considered tapu and no one dared to enter it.
Certain objects in the village are tapu and must not be touched by outsiders.
The ceremony took place on tapu ground, reserved only for elders and priests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: permitted - allowed or not forbidden
  • synonym: sacred - regarded with great respect and reverence
  • synonym: taboo - prohibited or restricted by social custom
