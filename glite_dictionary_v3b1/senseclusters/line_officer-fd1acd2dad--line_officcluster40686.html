line officer

noun
commissioned officer in combat unit
5.3 6.1
Pronunciation: / laɪn ˈɑfəsər / (AmE), / laɪn ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a commissioned officer in a combat unit, as opposed to a staff or supply officer

Examples

A line officer in combat
After years as a line officer, she moved to a staff position at headquarters.
Only a line officer may issue direct orders to troops in combat situations.
Promotion from line officer to staff officer often comes with broader administrative duties.
