word

noun
a brief conversation or discussion
1.2
Pronunciation: / wɝd / (AmE), / wɜːd / (BrE)
a conversation or exchange of views, especially a brief one

Examples

A word with someone
After a word with her manager, she felt much better about the situation.
Can I have a word with you about the meeting later?
They stopped outside the office for a quick word before lunch.
