valet

noun
manservant; personal male attendant
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈvælɪt / (AmE), / ˈvæleɪ; ˈvælɪt / (BrE)
a male servant who acts as a personal attendant to his employer, performing tasks such as dressing, grooming, and looking after clothes

Examples

A valet helps dress
His father once worked as a valet in a grand London hotel.
In many classic novels, the valet is a discreet but essential household member.
The wealthy gentleman always relied on his valet to help him dress for dinner.
