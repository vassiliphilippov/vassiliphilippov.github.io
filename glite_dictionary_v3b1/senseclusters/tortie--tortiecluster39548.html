tortie

adjective
tortoiseshell-patterned
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔːrti / (AmE), / ˈtɔːti / (BrE)
having a tortoiseshell coat or pattern, especially with mottled patches of brown, black, and orange

Examples

Tortie fur pattern
Their new cat is a beautiful tortie with striking orange and black fur.
She wore a tortie scarf that complemented her autumn outfit.
The tortie kitten quickly became the favorite at the animal shelter.
