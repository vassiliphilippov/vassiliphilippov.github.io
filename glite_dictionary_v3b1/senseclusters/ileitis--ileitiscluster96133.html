ileitis

noun
inflammation of the ileum
Pronunciation: / ˌɪliˈaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌɪliˈaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the ileum, the last part of the small intestine.

Examples

Chronic ileitis
Chronic ileitis can be associated with Crohn's disease.
The doctor diagnosed him with ileitis after reviewing his test results.
Treatment of ileitis may include medication to reduce inflammation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enteritis - inflammation of the intestine, especially the small intestine
