Mount Everest

proper noun
the world's highest mountain
Pronunciation: / maʊnt ˈɛvərɪst /; / maʊnt ˈɛvɚɪst, ˈɛvrəst / (AmE), / maʊnt ˈɛvərɪst /; / maʊnt ˈɛvərɪst, ˈɛvrɪst / (BrE)
the highest mountain in the world, located in the Himalayas on the border between Nepal and Tibet/China, rising to 8,848 meters (29,029 feet)
