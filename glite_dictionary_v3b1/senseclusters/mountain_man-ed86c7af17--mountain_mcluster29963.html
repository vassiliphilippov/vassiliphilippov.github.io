mountain man

noun
man living in the wilderness/frontier
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən mæn / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn mæn / (BrE)
a man living in the wilderness, especially on the frontier, typically skilled in survival and hunting

Examples

A mountain man hunter
He admired the independent spirit of a true mountain man.
Many mountain men worked as fur trappers in the early nineteenth century.
The old mountain man told stories of surviving harsh winters in the Rockies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frontiersman - a man who lives on the frontier, especially in early American history
