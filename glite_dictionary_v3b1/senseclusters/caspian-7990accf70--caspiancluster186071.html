Caspian

proper noun
largest inland sea between Iran and Russia
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkæspiən / (AmE), / ˈkaspɪən / (BrE)
the largest inland body of water in the world, a saltwater lake located between southeastern Europe and western Asia, bordered by countries including Iran and Russia

Examples
