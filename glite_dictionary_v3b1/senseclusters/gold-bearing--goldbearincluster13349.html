gold-bearing

adjective
containing gold
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldˌbɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌbɛərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of rock, soil, etc.) containing gold; having gold within it.

Examples
