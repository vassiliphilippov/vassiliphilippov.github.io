Gikuyu language

noun
Bantu language of central Kenya
9.1
Pronunciation: / giˈkuːju ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / gɪˈkuːjuː ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Bantu language spoken primarily by the Kikuyu people in central Kenya.

Examples
