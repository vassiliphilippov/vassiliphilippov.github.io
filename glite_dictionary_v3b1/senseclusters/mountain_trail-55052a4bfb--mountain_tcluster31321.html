mountain trail

noun
path through mountainous areas
3.0 2.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmaʊn.tən treɪl/ /; / /ˈmaʊn.tɪn treɪl/ (AmE), /ˈmaʊn.tɪn treɪl/ /; / /ˈmaʊn.tən treɪl/ (BrE)
a path or route for walking, hiking, or riding in a mountainous area

Examples
