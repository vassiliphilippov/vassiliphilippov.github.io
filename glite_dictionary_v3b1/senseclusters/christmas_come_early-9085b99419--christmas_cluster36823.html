Christmas come early

noun
unexpected good fortune
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪs.məs kʌm ˈɜr.li / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.məs kʌm ˈɜː.li / (BrE)
an unexpected but very welcome event or piece of good fortune, as if receiving gifts before Christmas

Examples

Getting an all-expenses-paid trip felt like Christmas come early to him.
Her promotion was Christmas come early after months of hard work.
