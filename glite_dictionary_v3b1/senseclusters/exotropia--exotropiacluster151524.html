exotropia

noun
outward-turning eye condition
8.1 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛk.soʊˈtroʊ.pi.ə / (AmE), / ˌɛk.səʊˈtrəʊ.pi.ə / (BrE)
a type of strabismus in which one or both eyes turn outward

Examples

Exotropia eye condition
Children with exotropia may have trouble focusing on objects straight ahead.
Her doctor diagnosed her with exotropia after noticing her right eye turned outward.
Surgery is sometimes required to correct severe cases of exotropia.
