gau

noun
German administrative district
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡaʊ / (AmE), / ɡaʊ / (BrE)
a regional administrative district in Germany, especially one of the territorial divisions used during the Nazi period

Examples

German gau district
Each gau was governed by a party official known as a Gauleiter.
The country was divided into several gaus for administrative purposes during that era.
Historical records show that the boundaries of a gau often changed over time.
