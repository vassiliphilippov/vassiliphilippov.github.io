A high, short fly ball in baseball that is easy for fielders to catch.

Examples

Baseball pop-up
He was frustrated after hitting a pop-up instead of driving the ball to the outfield.
The batter hit a pop-up to the infield, and the shortstop made an easy catch.
With two outs, a pop-up ended the inning and stranded the runners on base.
