reporting

noun
presentation of news by journalists
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɔrtɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈpɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the presentation of news and information by journalists for newspapers, radio, television, or other media

Examples

News reporting
Many viewers criticized the television station's reporting as being one-sided.
She won an award for her excellent investigative reporting.
The newspaper was praised for its reporting of international affairs.
