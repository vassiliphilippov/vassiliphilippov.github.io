Synonyms

  • welfare
    2.3 78/m
    welfare is government help given through organized programs, while social welfare refers broadly to public support for people or groups in need.
  • public assistance
    2.5 1.29/m
    public assistance is direct financial aid from government to individuals or families, while social welfare includes both money and other support services.
  • social assistance
    3.2 0.14/m
    social assistance is government benefits paid to ensure minimum income levels, while social welfare covers income help plus wider services for people or groups.
