aircraft

noun
flying vehicle
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌkræft / (AmE), / ˈeə.krɑːft / (BrE)
A vehicle that is able to fly, such as an airplane, helicopter, or glider.

Examples

Historic aircraft
During the airshow, several historic aircraft were displayed to the public.
Modern aircraft are designed for greater fuel efficiency and lower emissions.
The aircraft landed safely after a long flight across the Atlantic.

  • synonym: helicopter - a type of aircraft with rotating blades
