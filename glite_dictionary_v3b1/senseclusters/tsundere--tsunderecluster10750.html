tsundere

noun
a character who warms up over time
8.0
Pronunciation: / tsunˈdɛr.eɪ /; / tsuːnˈdɛɹeɪ / (AmE), / tsʊnˈdɛr.eɪ /; / tsʊnˈdɛɹeɪ / (BrE)
a fictional character, especially in anime or manga, who is initially cold or hostile toward someone but gradually becomes warmer and more affectionate

Examples

A tsundere character
At first, she seemed rude, but her tsundere side eventually won everyone over.
He has a thing for tsundere characters in romance anime.
The main female lead is a classic tsundere, often acting cold but secretly caring.
Fans love the tsundere character type because it adds depth to romantic storylines.
She called him a tsundere after he blushed and refused to admit he cared.
The main heroine is a classic tsundere, acting hostile at first before revealing her true feelings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yandere - a character who is obsessively loving and may become violent
