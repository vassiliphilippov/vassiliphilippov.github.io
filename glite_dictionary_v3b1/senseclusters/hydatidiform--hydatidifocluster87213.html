hydatidiform

adjective
like a hydatid cyst
Pronunciation: / haɪˌdætɪdɪˈfɔrm / (AmE), / haɪˌdætɪdɪˈfɔːm / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a hydatid (a cyst, especially in medical contexts, such as abnormal growths in pregnancy)

Examples

Hydatidiform cyst
The doctor identified a hydatidiform mass during the ultrasound examination.
A hydatidiform cyst can develop in organs such as the liver or lungs.
The pathology report described the growth as hydatidiform in appearance.
The ultrasound revealed a hydatidiform mass in the patient's uterus.
Doctors monitor women with a hydatidiform mole for possible complications.
A hydatidiform pregnancy requires special medical attention and follow-up.
