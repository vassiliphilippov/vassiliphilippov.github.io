nonspecialist

adjective
not expert
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈspɛʃəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈspɛʃəlɪst / (BrE)
not having or requiring special knowledge or expertise in a particular subject

Examples

Nonspecialist advice
The museum offers nonspecialist tours designed for visitors with no background in art history.
She wrote a nonspecialist article to make the science accessible to everyone.
This book is suitable for both experts and nonspecialist readers interested in the topic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: general - not specialized or limited to one area
  • antonym: specialist - having or requiring expert knowledge in a particular area
