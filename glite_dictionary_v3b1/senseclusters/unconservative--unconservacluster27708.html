unconservative

adjective
not conservative
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈsɜːrvətɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈsɜːvətɪv / (BrE)
not supporting conservative political or social views; favoring progressive or liberal ideas and change

Examples

Unconservative views
The party's unconservative platform attracted many young voters interested in social change.
Her unconservative views on education reform sparked debate within the community.
He was criticized for his unconservative approach to economic policy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservative - holding traditional values and cautious about change
  • synonym: liberal - open to new behavior or opinions and willing to discard traditional values
  • synonym: progressive - favoring social reform or new ideas
