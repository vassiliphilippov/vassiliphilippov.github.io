asleep at the switch

adjective
not attentive; neglecting duty
5.1
Pronunciation: / əˈslip ət ðə swɪtʃ / (AmE), / əˈsliːp ət ðə swɪtʃ / (BrE)
not paying attention when you should be, especially so as to neglect your responsibilities or miss something important

Examples

Asleep at the switch in duty
After the security breach, the manager was criticized for being asleep at the switch.
Don't be asleep at the switch—double-check every detail of the contract.
She missed the crucial call because she was asleep at the switch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alert - paying close attention to potential problems
  • synonym: inattentive - not paying attention
