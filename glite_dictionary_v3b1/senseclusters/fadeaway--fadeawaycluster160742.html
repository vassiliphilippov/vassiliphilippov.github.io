fadeaway

noun
basketball jump shot
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪdəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪdəˌweɪ / (BrE)
a basketball shot made while jumping backward away from the basket to avoid or create space from a defender

Examples

A basketball fadeaway

Practicing the fadeaway helped him shoot over taller defenders.
Her signature move was a quick fadeaway from the left side of the court.
The player made a difficult fadeaway just before the buzzer sounded.
His signature move is the fadeaway, which is hard for defenders to block.
She practiced her fadeaway every day to improve her scoring ability.
