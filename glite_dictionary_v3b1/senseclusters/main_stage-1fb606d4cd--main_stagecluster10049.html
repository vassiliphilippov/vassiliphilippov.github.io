main stage

noun
central place of focus
Pronunciation: / meɪn steɪdʒ /; / ˈmeɪn steɪdʒ / (AmE), / meɪn steɪdʒ /; / ˈmeɪn steɪdʒ / (BrE)
the most important area or platform at an event or venue where the main events or performances happen
