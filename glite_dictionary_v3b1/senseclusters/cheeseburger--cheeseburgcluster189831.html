cheeseburger

noun
hamburger with melted cheese
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːzˌbɝːɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzˌbɜːɡə / (BrE)
a hamburger topped with melted cheese, usually served in a bun

Examples

A cheeseburger with cheese

The cheeseburger was topped with extra onions and pickles.
