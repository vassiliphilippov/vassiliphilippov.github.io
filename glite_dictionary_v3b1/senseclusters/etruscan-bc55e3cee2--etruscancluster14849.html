Etruscan

proper noun
ancient Italian people
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtrʌskən / (AmE), / ɪˈtrʌskən / (BrE)
a member of an ancient people who lived in central Italy and greatly influenced Roman culture

Examples

Etruscan civilization
The Etruscan civilization left behind remarkable tomb paintings and sculptures.
Archaeologists discovered an Etruscan artifact in the ruins of an ancient city.
Many Roman customs were influenced by the earlier Etruscan culture.
