derelict car

noun
abandoned, neglected car
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛrəlɪkt kɑr / (AmE), / ˈdɛrəlɪkt kɑː / (BrE)
a car that has been abandoned and left in a state of neglect or disrepair

Examples

A derelict car
A derelict car blocked the driveway and was covered in graffiti.
Children often played around a derelict car in the abandoned lot near our house.
The city issued a notice for the removal of the derelict car that had been sitting in the alley for months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abandoned vehicle - a vehicle that has been left unattended and is considered deserted
  • synonym: junk car - a car that is no longer functional and considered worthless
