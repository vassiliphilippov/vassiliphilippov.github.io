oil-bearing

adjective
containing oil (especially of rocks or soil)
5.2
Pronunciation: / ɔɪlˌbɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ɔɪlˌbeərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of rock, soil, or other material) containing oil, especially crude oil.

Examples

Oil-bearing rock

The geologists discovered an oil-bearing formation deep beneath the desert.
