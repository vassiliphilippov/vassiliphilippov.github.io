orderly

adjective
tidy and well-organized
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdɚli / (AmE), / ˈɔːdəli / (BrE)
arranged or organized in a tidy, methodical way; clean and neat

Examples

Orderly desk
She keeps her desk orderly and free of clutter.
The workshop was surprisingly orderly after a busy day of projects.
We admired the orderly rows of books on the shelf.

Related Terms

  • antonym: messy - untidy or disorganized
  • synonym: neat - tidy and orderly in appearance or habits
