non-metric

adjective
not based on the metric system
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / nɒnˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
Not based on the meter or the metric system as a standard of measurement.

Examples

Non-metric system
Many engineers prefer metric, but some industries still use non-metric standards.
The United States uses primarily non-metric units like feet and pounds.
The recipe provides non-metric measurements alongside metric ones for convenience.
