pony

noun
small glass for liquor measure
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊni / (AmE), / ˈpəʊni / (BrE)
A small glass or measure, typically holding about one ounce, used for serving a single swallow of liquor such as whiskey.

Examples

A pony of whiskey
At the tasting, everyone received just a pony of the house special rum.
He ordered a pony of whiskey to sip before dinner.
The bartender poured a pony of brandy into the glass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shot glass - a small glass used to serve strong alcoholic drinks
