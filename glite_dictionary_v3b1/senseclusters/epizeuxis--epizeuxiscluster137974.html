epizeuxis

noun
word repetition device
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈzjuːksɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈzjuːksɪs / (BrE)
a rhetorical device in which a word is repeated immediately for emphasis or effect
