Pre-Raphaelite

adjective
relating to the Pre-Raphaelite artistic movement
9.9
Pronunciation: / priː ˈræfiəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / priː ˈræfɪəˌlaɪt / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the Pre-Raphaelites, a group of English artists and writers of the mid-19th century who aimed to return to the detail, vivid colors, and complex compositions of art before the time of Raphael.

Examples

Pre-Raphaelite painting style
Her collection includes several beautifully illustrated Pre-Raphaelite books.
Many Pre-Raphaelite artists sought inspiration from medieval literature and nature.
The painting's Pre-Raphaelite style is evident in its vivid colors and intricate details.
