beach buggy

noun
vehicle for driving on sand or beaches
Pronunciation: / bitʃ ˈbʌɡi / (AmE), / biːtʃ ˈbʌɡi / (BrE)
a small recreational vehicle with large tires designed for driving on beaches or sand dunes

Examples

A beach buggy ride
Driving a beach buggy is a popular activity on this tropical island.
The old beach buggy bounced over the sandy tracks with ease.
We rented a beach buggy to explore the sand dunes along the coast.
