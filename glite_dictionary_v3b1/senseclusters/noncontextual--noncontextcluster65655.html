noncontextual

adjective
not context-dependent
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkənˈtɛkstʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈtɛkstʃuəl / (BrE)
not dependent on or influenced by the surrounding context or situation

Examples

Noncontextual knowledge
The test was designed to measure noncontextual knowledge, focusing only on facts.
A noncontextual approach ignores the influence of surrounding circumstances.
Some definitions are noncontextual and remain the same in every situation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absolute - not relative or dependent on anything else
  • antonym: contextual - dependent on or related to the surrounding context
