nontextual

adjective
not in text form
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈtɛkstʃuəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈtɛkstjʊəl / (BrE)
not consisting of, involving, or relating to written or printed text; made up of images, audio, video, or other non-text elements

The presentation used nontextual aids such as charts and photographs to explain the data.

Modern advertising frequently combines nontextual elements with written slogans for greater impact.
