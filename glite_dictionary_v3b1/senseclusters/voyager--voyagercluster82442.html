voyager

noun
person undertaking a long journey, esp. by sea
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔɪ.ədʒ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪ.ɪdʒ.ə / (BrE)
a person who undertakes long and adventurous journeys, especially by sea or to distant lands

Examples

Long voyagers at sea
Many voyagers wrote detailed journals about their experiences at sea.
Marco Polo was a famous voyager who traveled from Europe to Asia.
The legendary voyager set sail across the Atlantic in search of new lands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: explorer - a person who travels to unfamiliar places to discover new things
  • synonym: traveler - someone who travels to different places
