ratty

adjective
resembling or characteristic of a rat
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈræti / (AmE), / ˈræti / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of a rat; having qualities or an appearance similar to a rat.

Examples

Ratty features
He wore a ratty coat that had holes and torn sleeves.
Stray cats prowled the alleys, giving the neighborhood a slightly ratty feel.
The old, ratty building looked abandoned and unsafe.
