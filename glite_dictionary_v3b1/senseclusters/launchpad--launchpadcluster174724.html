launchpad

noun
platform for launching rockets or spacecraft
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔntʃˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈlɔːntʃˌpad / (BrE)
A platform from which rockets or spacecraft are launched into the air or space.

Examples

Rocket launchpad
After months of preparation, the spacecraft was finally moved to the launchpad for its mission.
Crowds gathered to watch the historic launch from the launchpad at Cape Canaveral.
The rocket sat silently on the launchpad as engineers finished their final checks.
