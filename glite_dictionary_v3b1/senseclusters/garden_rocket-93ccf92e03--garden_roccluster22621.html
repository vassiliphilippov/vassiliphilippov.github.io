garden rocket

noun
edible salad plant with peppery leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdən ˈrɑkɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˈrɒkɪt / (BrE)
an annual European plant with peppery-tasting young leaves, often grown for use in salads

Examples

Garden rocket leaves
Garden rocket thrives in well-drained soil and plenty of sunlight.
He added fresh garden rocket to the salad for a peppery flavor.
Many chefs prefer to use garden rocket as a garnish because of its distinctive taste.
