bouzouki

noun
Greek stringed instrument
8.6
Pronunciation: / buˈzuːki / (AmE), / buːˈzuːki / (BrE)
a Greek stringed musical instrument with a long neck and a rounded body, played with a plectrum

Examples

A bouzouki instrument
The musician played a lively tune on his bouzouki during the concert.
She learned to play the bouzouki after visiting Greece last summer.
The sound of the bouzouki filled the air at the traditional Greek wedding.
