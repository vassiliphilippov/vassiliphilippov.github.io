orangery

noun
glass-walled living room/extension
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrəndʒəri / (AmE), / ˈɒrəndʒəri / (BrE)
A glass-walled room or single-storey extension, often attached to a house, used as a living or social space rather than primarily for growing citrus plants.

Examples

Modern orangery room
They converted the old orangery into a bright, glass-walled living space.
We spent the afternoon reading in the new orangery that overlooks the garden.
The couple added an elegant orangery to connect the kitchen and patio.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conservatory - a room with glass walls and roof used for growing plants or as a sunlit living space
  • synonym: greenhouse - a glass building for growing plants; historically closely related to orangeries
  • synonym: sunroom - a room with large windows designed to admit sunlight, used for relaxation or social activities
