alpha fetoprotein

noun
serum tumour marker
6.8
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u00e6l.f\u0259 \u02ccfi\u02d0.to\u028a\u02c8pro\u028a.ti\u02d0n / (AmE), / \u02c8al.f\u0259 \u02ccfi\u02d0.t\u0259\u028a\u02c8pr\u0259\u028a.ti\u02d0n / (BrE)
a serum protein measured in adults and used as a tumour marker to help detect or monitor certain cancers, especially hepatocellular carcinoma and some germ cell tumours.

Examples

Cancer alpha fetoprotein level
Clinicians monitor alpha fetoprotein levels to help diagnose and manage liver cancer.
An elevated alpha fetoprotein result prompted further imaging to check for possible hepatocellular carcinoma.
During follow-up, her alpha fetoprotein concentration fell after successful chemotherapy for a germ cell tumor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tumor marker - a substance in blood or tissues used to detect or monitor cancer
