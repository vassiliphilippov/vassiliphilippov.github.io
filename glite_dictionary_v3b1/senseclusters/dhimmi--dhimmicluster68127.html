dhimmi

noun
protected non-Muslim
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdɪ.mi/ /; / /ˈðɪ.mi/ /; / /ˈdiː.mi/ (AmE), /ˈdɪ.mi/ /; / /ˈðɪ.mi/ /; / /ˈdiː.mi/ (BrE)
