animalization

noun
making cruel or less human
Pronunciation: / ˌænəmələˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌænɪmələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌænɪmələˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making someone or something cruel or lacking normal human qualities, especially by treating them in a dehumanizing way

Examples

Animalization of people
Some critics argue that violent video games contribute to the animalization of young people.
The brutal conditions in the prison led to the animalization of many inmates.
The novel explores the animalization of society during times of war and chaos.
His experiences in prison led to a gradual animalisation that alarmed his family and friends.
Many fear that constant exposure to violence results in the animalisation of society.
The animalisation of the soldiers during war was a cause for concern among their commanders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brutalization - the process of making someone cruel or violent
  • synonym: dehumanization - the process of depriving a person or group of positive human qualities
  • antonym: humanization - the process of making something more humane
