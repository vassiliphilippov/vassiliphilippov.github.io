nonmajority

adjective
not a majority
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.məˈdʒɔr.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.məˈdʒɒr.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
not constituting or relating to a majority; not forming more than half of a group or total.

Examples

Nonmajority opinion
The committee's decision was influenced by the nonmajority members who voiced strong concerns.
A nonmajority opinion can still have a significant impact on the outcome.
Policies were changed after a nonmajority group raised important issues at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: majority - more than half of a group or total
  • synonym: minority - a group that is less than half of the total
