tung

noun
tree producing tung oil
7.6
Pronunciation: / tʌŋ / (AmE), / tʌŋ / (BrE)
A Chinese tree (Vernicia fordii) whose seeds produce tung oil, used especially in wood finishing and paints.

Examples

tung tree oil
Farmers in southern China cultivate tung trees for their valuable oil.
The tung is valued both for its ornamental flowers and for its industrial uses.
The furniture was polished using oil pressed from the tung tree seeds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tung oil - oil derived from the seeds of the tung tree
