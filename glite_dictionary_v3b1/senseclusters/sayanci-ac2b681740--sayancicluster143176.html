Sayanci

noun
Chadic language of northern Nigeria
Pronunciation: / saɪˈjɑːn.tʃi / (AmE), / saɪˈjæn.tʃi / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in northern Nigeria, especially in Bauchi State, Tafawa Balewa LGA

Examples

Sayanci language
Efforts are underway to preserve and revitalize the Sayanci language among the younger generation.
Linguists have documented the Sayanci language in several communities around Bauchi State.
She learned to speak Sayanci fluently after moving to northern Nigeria.
