smacking

noun
an open-handed blow
Pronunciation: / ˈsmækɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsmækɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of striking something or someone with an open hand, typically making a sharp sound

Examples

He received a stinging smacking for talking back to his teacher.
The child's loud smacking of his hands on the table startled everyone in the room.
