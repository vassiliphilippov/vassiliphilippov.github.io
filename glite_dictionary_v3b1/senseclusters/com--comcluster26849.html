com

noun
internet domain ending
3.2
Pronunciation: / kɑm / (AmE), / kɒm / (BrE)
the internet top-level domain used at the end of website addresses, mainly for commercial organizations

Examples

Com website address
Most large companies want their website to have a com address for global recognition.
She registered her business with a com domain to attract more customers online.
The com extension is one of the most popular choices for website addresses.

Related Terms

  • antonym: net - internet domain ending originally for network providers
  • antonym: org - internet domain ending for organizations, especially non-profits
