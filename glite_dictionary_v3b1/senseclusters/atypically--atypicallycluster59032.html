atypically

adverb
in an untypical or unusual way
Pronunciation: / əˈtɪpɪkli / (AmE), / əˈtɪpɪkəli / (BrE)
In a way that is not typical or usual.

Examples

He was atypically late
He was atypically late to the meeting, surprising everyone.
She spoke atypically softly, barely above a whisper.
The weather has been atypically warm for this time of year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: typically - in a way that is usual or expected
