attaint

verb
bring shame or dishonor upon
7.2
Pronunciation: / əˈteɪnt / (AmE), / əˈteɪnt / (BrE)
to bring shame, dishonor, or disgrace upon someone or something

Examples

To attaint a reputation
Fearing he might attaint his family's good name, he confessed his mistakes.
The knight was attainted by rumors of treachery.
The scandal threatened to attaint his reputation in the court.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disgrace - cause shame or loss of respect
  • antonym: honor - to show great respect or bring pride to
