Mt. McKinley

proper noun
former name for Denali, Alaska's highest mountain
Pronunciation: / maʊnt məˈkɪnli / (AmE), / maʊnt məˈkɪnli / (BrE)
the former name of the highest mountain in North America, located in south central Alaska; now officially called Denali, it rises to 20,310 feet (6,190 meters)

Examples

Mt. McKinley mountain peak
Mt. McKinley was officially renamed Denali by the U.S. government in 2015.
Many climbers attempt to reach the summit of Mt. McKinley each year.
The breathtaking view from Mt. McKinley attracts tourists from all over the world.
