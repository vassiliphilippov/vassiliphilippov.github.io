flan

noun
caramel custard dessert
Pronunciation: / flɑn / (AmE), / flæn / (BrE)
a sweet, creamy dessert made with eggs and milk, often topped with caramel sauce

Examples

Caramel flan
She made a delicious flan for dessert, and it disappeared quickly at the dinner table.
The restaurant is famous for its creamy flan topped with golden caramel.
After the meal, everyone enjoyed a slice of homemade flan with coffee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crème caramel - a custard dessert with a layer of soft caramel on top
  • synonym: pudding - a sweet, creamy dessert, often made with milk and eggs
