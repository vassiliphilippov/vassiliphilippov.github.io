territorial division

noun
administrative district or area
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛr.əˈtɔr.i.əl dɪˈvɪʒ.ən / (AmE), / ˌtɛr.ɪˈtɔː.ri.əl dɪˈvɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
a district or area defined for administrative or governmental purposes

Examples

Local territorial divisions
Each state is further broken down into several territorial divisions for the purposes of local government.
France’s administrative structure includes several types of territorial division, such as regions and departments.
The boundaries of the territorial division were redrawn after the census results were published.

  • synonym: province - a principal administrative division of certain countries or empires
