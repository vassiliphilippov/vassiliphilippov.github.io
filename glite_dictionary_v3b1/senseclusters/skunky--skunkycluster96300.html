skunky

adjective
resembling a skunk
Pronunciation: / ˈskʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈskʌŋki / (BrE)
having markings, coloration, or appearance similar to that of a skunk, especially black-and-white or striped patterns

Examples

Skunky stripes
The kitten's skunky fur made it stand out among its siblings.
She wore a black-and-white scarf that looked quite skunky with her outfit.
The puppy's tail had a skunky stripe running down the middle.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plain - without markings or patterns
  • synonym: striped - having long bands of color
