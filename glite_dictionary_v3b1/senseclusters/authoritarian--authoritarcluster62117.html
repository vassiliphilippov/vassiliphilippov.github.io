authoritarian

adjective
demanding strict obedience
2.7
Pronunciation: / əˌθɔr.əˈtɛr.i.ən / (AmE), / ɔːˌθɒr.ɪˈteə.ri.ən / (BrE)
expecting, demanding, or enforcing strict obedience to authority, often at the expense of personal freedom

Examples

Authoritarian leader
Her authoritarian teaching style left little room for student creativity or input.
The authoritarian manager expected his staff to follow every order without question.
They grew up in a highly authoritarian household where speaking out was discouraged.
His authoritarian style left little room for dissent among employees.
Many people fled the authoritarian government in search of freedom.
The country was governed by an authoritarian regime for over two decades.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autocratic - relating to a ruler with absolute power
  • antonym: democratic - relating to a government by the people
  • synonym: dictatorial - having characteristics of a dictator; expecting total obedience
  • antonym: permissive - allowing much freedom; not strict or controlling
