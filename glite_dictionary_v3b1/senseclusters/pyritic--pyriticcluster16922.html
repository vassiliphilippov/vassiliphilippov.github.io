pyritic

adjective
containing pyrite
Pronunciation: / paɪˈrɪtɪk / (AmE), / paɪˈrɪtɪk / (BrE)
containing, relating to, or resembling pyrite, a yellow mineral also known as fool's gold

Examples

Pyritic rock layer
The geologist identified the pyritic layer by its metallic luster and yellowish color.
Miners often encounter pyritic rocks that can be mistaken for valuable ore.
The soil in that region is rich in pyritic minerals, giving it a distinctive appearance.
