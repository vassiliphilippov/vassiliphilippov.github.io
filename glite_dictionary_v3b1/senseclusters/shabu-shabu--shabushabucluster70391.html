shabu-shabu

noun
Japanese hot pot dish
Pronunciation: / ˌʃɑːbuˈʃɑːbu / (AmE), / ˌʃæbuˈʃæbu /; / ˌʃæbuːˈʃæbuː / (BrE)
a Japanese hot-pot dish where thin slices of meat and vegetables are quickly cooked in hot broth at the table
