preinstruction

noun
preliminary guidance
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːɪnˈstrʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːɪnˈstrʌkʃən / (BrE)
information or guidance given before the main teaching or activity begins

Examples

Teacher's preinstruction
The teacher gave preinstruction to ensure everyone understood the goals of the lesson.
Effective preinstruction can help students feel more confident before starting a new topic.
Before the experiment, the instructor provided preinstruction on safety procedures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: introduction - the beginning part that prepares for what follows
  • synonym: preparation - the act of getting ready for something
