federate

verb
join a digital federation
Pronunciation: / \u02c8f\u025bd\u0259\u02ccre\u026at / (AmE), / \u02c8f\u025bd\u0259re\u026at / (BrE)
(in computing) to join or configure a service, device, account, or server so it can participate in a federated network, allowing cross-domain communication, sharing, or authentication.

Examples

To federate servers
The new server can federate with other nodes in the Fediverse to share posts.
To enable single sign-on, the company chose to federate its identity provider with partners.
Engineers spent the weekend configuring the app to federate across domains for message exchange.

Related Terms

  • synonym: federated authentication - an authentication method that lets users log in across multiple systems using a shared identity provider
  • synonym: federation - a group of services, servers, or organizations that agree to interoperate or share data under common rules
