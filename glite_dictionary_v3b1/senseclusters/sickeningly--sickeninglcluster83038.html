sickeningly

adverb
in a disgusting or revolting manner
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪkənɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsɪkənɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that causes disgust or nausea; to a degree that is offensive or revolting.

Examples

Sickeningly arrogant behavior
He was sickeningly arrogant, boasting about his wealth all evening.
She smiled sickeningly as she accepted the praise she clearly didn't deserve.
The food was sickeningly sweet, making it hard to enjoy more than a bite.
