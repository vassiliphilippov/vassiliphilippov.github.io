Tswana

noun
a member of a southern African ethnic group
Pronunciation: / ˈtswɑːnə / (AmE), / ˈtswɑːnə / (BrE)
a member of a Bantu ethnic group living mainly in Botswana and western South Africa

Examples

A Tswana person
Many Tswana live both in Botswana and nearby areas of South Africa.
She met a Tswana man while studying in Botswana.
The Tswana are known for their rich traditions and cultural heritage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Bantu - a group of indigenous African peoples sharing related languages
