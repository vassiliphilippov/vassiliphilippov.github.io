executively

adverb
in an implementing way
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪvli / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner typical of an executive, especially by making decisions, showing authority, or carrying out actions effectively

Examples

To act executively
The new policy was enforced executively to ensure immediate results across all departments.
She handled the transition executively, focusing on the practical steps needed for success.
Tasks were assigned and completed executively to keep the project on schedule.
She managed the team executively during the crisis.
The project was handled executively to ensure all deadlines were met.
He spoke executively when outlining the company's new strategy.

  • synonym: practically - in a way that relates to actual doing or use
