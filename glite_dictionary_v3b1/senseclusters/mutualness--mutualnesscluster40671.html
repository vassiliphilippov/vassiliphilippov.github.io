mutualness

noun
reciprocal quality or feeling
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuː.tʃu.əl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmjuː.tʃu.əl.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being mutual; the condition in which two or more parties share the same feelings, actions, or relationship reciprocally.

Examples

The mutualness of respect
The mutualness of respect in their partnership made their collaboration very successful.
There was an undeniable mutualness between the two friends that everyone admired.
Without mutualness, relationships often struggle to thrive or deepen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mutuality - the sharing of a feeling or action by two or more parties
