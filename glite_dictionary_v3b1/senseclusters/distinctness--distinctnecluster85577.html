distinctness

noun
clarity or sharpness
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɪŋktnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪŋktnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being sharply defined, clear, or easily distinguishable

Examples

Distinctness of the image
Good lenses enhance the distinctness of the image on the screen.
The distinctness of her speech made it easy for everyone to understand.
Through the fog, the distinctness of the mountain's outline gradually faded away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarity - the quality of being clear, in particular clarity of sound, vision, or thought
  • antonym: vagueness - the quality of being unclear or not precisely defined
