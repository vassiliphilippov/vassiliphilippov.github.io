boisterously

adverb
noisily and energetically
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪstərəsli / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪstə rəsli / (BrE)
in a noisy, energetic, and cheerful way; with lively enthusiasm

Examples

Cheer boisterously
Fans cheered boisterously when their team scored the winning goal.
The children laughed boisterously as they splashed in the pool.
The parade moved boisterously through the busy downtown streets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: quietly - in a quiet manner
