struggles of the proletariat

noun
hardships faced by the working class
6.8 6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈstrʌg.əlz əv ðə proʊ.lə.ˈteə.ri.ət/ (AmE), /ˈstrʌg.əlz əv ðə prəʊ.lɪ.ˈteə.ri.ət/ (BrE)
the hardships and collective difficulties experienced by the working class, especially in the context of social and economic systems

Examples

The struggles of the proletariat under capitalism
In his speech, he addressed the struggles of the proletariat under capitalist regimes.
Many artists throughout history have depicted the struggles of the proletariat in their work.
The novel deals with the struggles of the proletariat in an industrial city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: working class - social group consisting of people employed for wages, especially in manual or industrial work
