chili sauce

noun
spicy condiment made with tomatoes and peppers
3.5 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɪl.iː sɔːs/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɪl.iː sɔːs/ (BrE)
A spicy or piquant condiment made from tomatoes, onions, and peppers (sweet or hot), simmered with vinegar, sugar, and various seasonings.

Examples

A bottle of chili sauce
She poured some chili sauce over her noodles to give them an extra kick.
The recipe calls for a tablespoon of chili sauce to add flavor to the marinade.
You can find chili sauce in most supermarkets, either mild or hot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hot sauce - spicy sauce made from chili peppers, often thinner and hotter than chili sauce
