milking shorthorn

noun
breed of dairy cattle from shorthorn stock
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkɪŋ ˈʃɔrthɔrn / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkɪŋ ˈʃɔːthɔːn / (BrE)
a breed of dairy cattle evolved from shorthorn beef cattle, known for its milking ability as well as beef quality
