kyanite

noun
blue or greenish-blue aluminum silicate mineral
8.4 9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkaɪ.ə.naɪt/ (AmE), /ˈkaɪ.ə.naɪt/ (BrE)
A typically blue or greenish-blue mineral composed of aluminum silicate, found in metamorphic rocks and used in refractory and ceramics industries.

Examples

Kyanite mineral
Kyanite is prized for its use in heat-resistant ceramics and electrical insulators.
