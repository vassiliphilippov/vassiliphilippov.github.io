nightwalker

noun
large earthworm used as fishing bait
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtˌwɔkər / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtwɔːkə / (BrE)
A large earthworm that emerges at night, especially when the ground is wet, and is commonly used as bait by anglers.

Examples

A nightwalker worm
Nightwalkers can be found on the lawn most evenings in early spring.
She used a handful of nightwalkers to catch more fish at the lake.
The fisherman dug for nightwalkers after a heavy rain to use as bait.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nightcrawler - a large earthworm, especially one used as bait for fish
