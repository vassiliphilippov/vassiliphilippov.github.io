dead in the water

adjective
unable to succeed or progress
4.2
Pronunciation: / dɛd ɪn ðə ˈwɔtər / (AmE), / dɛd ɪn ðə ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
completely unable to succeed or make progress; having no chance of continuing effectively

Examples

Dead in the water project
After the budget was rejected, the project was dead in the water.
His campaign was dead in the water before it even began.
The company's expansion plans are dead in the water due to lack of funding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hopeless - having no hope; impossible to succeed
