interservice

adjective
between software services
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈsɜːrvɪs / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
relating to interaction, communication, or coordination between separate software services or components.

Examples

Interservice communication
The engineers improved interservice communication to make the application more reliable.
Security policies must address interservice data transfers in distributed systems.
Effective interservice coordination is essential for maintaining a scalable microservices architecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intersystem - relating to communication or coordination between different systems
  • antonym: intraservice - relating to processes within a single service
