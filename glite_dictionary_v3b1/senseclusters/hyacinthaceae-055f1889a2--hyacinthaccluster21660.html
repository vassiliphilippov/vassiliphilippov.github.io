Hyacinthaceae

noun
a plant family including hyacinths (botany)
Pronunciation: / haɪəˌsɪnˈθeɪsi.i /; / haɪəˌsɪnθiˈeɪsiː / (AmE), / haɪəˌsɪnˈθeɪsiː /; / haɪəˌsɪnθɪˈiːsiː / (BrE)
a former or sometimes-recognized family of flowering plants that includes the hyacinths
