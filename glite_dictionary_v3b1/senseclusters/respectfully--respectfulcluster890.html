respectfully

adverb
in a respectful manner
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɛktfəli / (AmE), / rɪˈspɛktfəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows respect or politeness toward someone or something

Examples

To respectfully greet elders
He always respectfully greets his elders when he enters the room.
She respectfully declined the invitation to the event.
The students listened respectfully as the principal addressed the school.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disrespectfully - in a way that shows a lack of respect
