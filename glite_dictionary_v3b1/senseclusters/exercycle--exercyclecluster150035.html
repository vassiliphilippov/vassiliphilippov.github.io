exercycle

noun
stationary exercise bicycle
Pronunciation: / ˈɛksərˌsaɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈɛksəˌsaɪkəl / (BrE)
an exercise machine that looks like a bicycle and is used for stationary cycling workouts

Examples

An exercycle machine
After his knee surgery, he used an exercycle for low-impact rehabilitation.
Many gyms have several types of exercycle to suit different training needs.
She prefers to work out at home using her exercycle while watching TV.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stationary bike - an exercise device for indoor cycling, similar to a bicycle
  • antonym: treadmill - an exercise machine for walking or running while staying in one place
