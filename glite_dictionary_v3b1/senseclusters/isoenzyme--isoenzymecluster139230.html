isoenzyme

noun
enzyme variant
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsoʊˌɛnzaɪm / (AmE), / ˈaɪsəʊˌɛnzaɪm / (BrE)
an enzyme that exists in multiple forms within a species but carries out the same chemical function

Examples

A specific isoenzyme
The laboratory identified a specific isoenzyme to help diagnose the patient's heart condition.
Different tissues in the body may contain a unique isoenzyme of the same enzyme.
Researchers compared the activity of each isoenzyme to understand their roles in metabolism.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: enzyme - a protein that speeds up chemical reactions in living things
  • synonym: isozyme - an alternative form of an enzyme with the same function
