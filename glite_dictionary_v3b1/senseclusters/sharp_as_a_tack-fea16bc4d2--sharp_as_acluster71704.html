sharp as a tack

adjective
very intelligent or quick-witted
7.0
Pronunciation: / ʃɑrp æz ə tæk / (AmE), / ʃɑːp æz ə tæk / (BrE)
very intelligent or mentally quick; extremely mentally acute or perceptive

Examples

Sharp as a tack mind

The new intern is sharp as a tack and picks up new skills very quickly.
