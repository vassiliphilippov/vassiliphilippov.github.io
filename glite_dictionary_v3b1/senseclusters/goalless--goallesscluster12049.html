goalless

adjective
with no goals scored
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊl.ləs / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊl.ləs / (BrE)
(of a game or period) having no goals scored by either side.

Examples

A goalless match
After a goalless first half, the players looked frustrated as they left the pitch.
It was their third consecutive goalless draw, disappointing fans hoping for an exciting game.
The football match remained goalless despite several close chances from both teams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scoreless - having no points or goals scored
