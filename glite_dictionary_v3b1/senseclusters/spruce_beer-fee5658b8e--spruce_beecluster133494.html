spruce beer

noun
drink made with fermented spruce sap
Pronunciation: / sprus bɪr / (AmE), / spruːs bɪə / (BrE)
a beverage made by fermenting molasses and other sugars with the sap or extract of spruce trees, sometimes including malt

Examples

A glass of spruce beer
In colonial times, spruce beer was a common drink during the long winter months.
She was curious to try spruce beer after reading about its unique flavor in a travel magazine.
The brewery introduced a new seasonal spruce beer inspired by historic recipes.
