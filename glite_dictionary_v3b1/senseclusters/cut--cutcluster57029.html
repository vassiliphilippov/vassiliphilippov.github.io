cut

noun
unexcused class absence
6.6
Pronunciation: / kʌt / (AmE), / kʌt / (BrE)
an instance of being absent from class without permission; an unexcused absence from school or college

Examples

An unexcused cut
He missed too many cuts and failed the course.
Students who accumulate excessive cuts may face disciplinary action.
The teacher warned that every cut would lower their final grade.
