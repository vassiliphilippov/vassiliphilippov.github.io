snuff

verb
inhale through the nose
6.6
Pronunciation: / snʌf / (AmE), / snʌf / (BrE)
to inhale a substance through the nose, especially powdered tobacco or drugs

Examples

To snuff tobacco
He would snuff a pinch of tobacco every morning.
She snuffed the herbal remedy before her yoga class.
Some people snuff cocaine at parties, which is dangerous and illegal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inhale - to breathe in (air, gas, etc.)
  • synonym: sniff - to draw air audibly through the nose
