welting

noun
raised skin mark
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈwel.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈwel.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
a raised, swollen mark or ridge on the skin caused by a blow, pressure, or allergic reaction

Examples

Skin welting
After the cat scratched him, a long red welting appeared down his forearm.
She developed a welting on her leg after brushing against the stinging nettles.
The allergic reaction caused a painful welting to form on his arm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bruise - an area of skin discoloration caused by injury
  • antonym: scar - a mark left on the skin after a wound has healed
  • synonym: welt - a raised, red mark on the skin from injury or irritation
