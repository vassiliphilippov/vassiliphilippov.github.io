runner

noun
sliding part or surface
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɚ / (AmE), / ˈrʌnə / (BrE)
a part or device on which something slides or moves smoothly, such as the narrow strips on the bottom of a sled or sliding drawer

Examples

Drawer runner
Many modern drawers have a metal runner to help them slide smoothly.
The bottom runner of the sled was damaged when it hit a rock.
The door moves along a runner at the top of the frame.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slide - a flat, smooth surface on which something moves or is moved
