SHF

noun
super high frequency range (3–30 GHz)
Pronunciation: / ˌɛs.eɪtʃˈɛf / (AmE), / ˌɛs.eɪtʃˈɛf / (BrE)
super high frequency; a range of radio frequencies from 3 to 30 gigahertz, used for radar, satellite communication, and microwave transmission.
