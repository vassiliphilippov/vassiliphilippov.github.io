barkingly

adverb
harshly or abruptly
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbɑːkɪŋli / (BrE)
in a harsh, abrupt, or barking manner (especially when speaking or making a sound)

Examples

Speak barkingly at someone
The coach spoke barkingly to the players after their mistake on the field.
He replied barkingly, making everyone in the room fall silent.
Orders were given barkingly, leaving no room for questions or hesitation.
He barkingly called out her name, startling everyone in the room.
The coach barkingly ordered the players to line up for practice.
She barkingly laughed at the joke, her voice echoing through the hall.
