transhumant

noun
seasonal livestock herder
9.9
Pronunciation: / trænzˈhjuːmənt / (AmE), / trænzˈhjuːmənt / (BrE)
a person who moves seasonally with their livestock between different grazing areas, typically in search of better pastures.

Examples

A transhumant farmer
The transhumant led his cattle up the mountain each spring to find fresh grass.
For centuries, transhumants have shaped the landscape through their seasonal migrations.
A transhumant community relies on moving between valleys and highlands to sustain their herds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nomad - a person who moves from place to place without a permanent home
  • synonym: pastoralist - a livestock farmer who raises animals on natural pastures
  • antonym: settler - a person who stays in one place and does not move seasonally
