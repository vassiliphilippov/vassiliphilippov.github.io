weight

verb
to burden or make heavy with a load
4.0
Pronunciation: / weɪt / (AmE), / weɪt / (BrE)
to burden or press down with a heavy load or weight; to make heavy by adding weight

Examples

To weight a bag
She felt weighted by the responsibilities she carried.
Snow weighted the branches of the old tree until they bent.
The truck was weighted down with boxes for delivery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burden - to load heavily or oppress
  • antonym: lighten - to make less heavy or less burdened
