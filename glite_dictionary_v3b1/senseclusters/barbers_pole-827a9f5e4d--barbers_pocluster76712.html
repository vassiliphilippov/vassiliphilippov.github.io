barber's pole

noun
striped pole outside a barbershop
5.4 2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrbərz poʊl / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbəz pəʊl / (BrE)
a pole with spiral red and white stripes, traditionally displayed outside a barber's shop to signify that it is a place where barbering services are offered

Examples

A barber's pole
He looked for the red-and-white barber's pole before stepping in for a trim.
In many countries, the barber's pole is a symbol of hairdressing and shaving.
The traditional barber's pole spun in the breeze outside the corner barbershop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbershop - a place where barbers work and provide hair-cutting and shaving services
