auditor

noun
person who listens attentively
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːdɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪtə / (BrE)
a person who listens attentively or is a member of an audience

Examples

Auditor in the audience
As an auditor at the conference, she was not allowed to participate in discussions.
Each auditor in the hall was required to remain silent and attentive.
The auditor sat quietly and absorbed every word of the lecture.
