navel-gazing

noun
excessive self-absorption or introspection
9.9 7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈneɪ.vəl ˈgeɪ.zɪŋ/ /; / /ˈneɪ.bəl ˈgeɪ.zɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈneɪ.vəl ˈgeɪ.zɪŋ/ (BrE)
self-indulgent or excessive contemplation of oneself or a single issue, at the expense of a wider view or action

Examples

Navel-gazing behavior
His endless navel-gazing made it hard for him to focus on real-world problems.
Some critics accuse modern art of being too much about navel-gazing and not enough about society.
The meeting turned into an exercise in navel-gazing with little progress made.

Related Terms

  • antonym: outward-looking - having interest in things beyond oneself; opposite of introspection
  • synonym: self-absorption - the state of being preoccupied with one's own feelings and interests
  • synonym: self-reflection - the process of introspection or examining one's own thoughts
