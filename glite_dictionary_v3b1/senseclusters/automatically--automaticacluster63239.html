automatically

adverb
without conscious thought; by reflex
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is done without conscious thought; as a reflex or by instinct.

Examples
