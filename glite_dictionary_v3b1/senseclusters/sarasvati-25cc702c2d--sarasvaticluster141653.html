Sarasvati

proper noun
Hindu goddess of knowledge and arts
9.5
Pronunciation: / səˈrɑːsvəˌti / (AmE), / səˈrɑːsvəti / (BrE)
In Hinduism, the goddess of knowledge, music, art, wisdom, and learning.

Examples

Sarasvati goddess of knowledge
Images of Sarasvati often show her playing the veena, a traditional musical instrument.
Many students pray to Sarasvati for success in their studies and artistic pursuits.
The festival of Vasant Panchami is dedicated to honoring Sarasvati in many parts of India.
