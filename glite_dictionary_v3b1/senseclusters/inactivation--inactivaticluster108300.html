inactivation

noun
disbanding of a military unit
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˌæk.tɪˈveɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.æk.tɪˈveɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of disbanding a military unit, typically through the transfer or discharge of its members

Examples

Military unit inactivation
After decades of service, the squadron faced inactivation due to military restructuring.
During inactivation, personnel were reassigned to other units or discharged from service.
The inactivation of the regiment was announced at the ceremony last Friday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disbandment - the act of breaking up an organized group, especially a military unit
