evocativeness

noun
power to evoke feelings
8.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvoʊkətɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈvəʊkətɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of bringing strong images, memories, or feelings to mind

Examples

Critics praised the evocativeness of the film's soundtrack for stirring deep emotions.
He admired the evocativeness of the old photographs, which brought back childhood memories.
