fusionist

noun
fusionism supporter
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːʒənɪst / (AmE), / ˈfjuːʒənɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates fusionism, combining libertarian economic ideas with traditional conservative values

Examples

A political fusionist
The political party attracted many fusionists who wanted both free markets and strong family values.
As a fusionist, he believed in balancing economic freedom with respect for tradition.
Debates between fusionists and other conservatives shaped the direction of the movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conservative - a person who supports traditional values and institutions
  • synonym: libertarian - a person who advocates for minimal government and maximum individual freedom
