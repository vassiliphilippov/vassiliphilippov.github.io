land mine of controversy

noun
potentially very controversial situation
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlænd ˌmaɪn əv kənˈtrɝː.vɚ.si / (AmE), / ˈlænd ˌmaɪn əv kənˈtrɒ.və.si / (BrE)
