cruel plant

noun
flowering shrub that traps moths at night
Pronunciation: / ˈkruːəl plænt / (AmE), / ˈkruːəl plɑːnt / (BrE)
a robust twining shrub, Wattakaka volubilis, with racemes of fragrant white or pink flowers that temporarily trap nocturnal moths until dawn

Examples

The cruel plant flowers
Gardeners often cultivate cruel plant for its beautiful, fragrant flowers.
