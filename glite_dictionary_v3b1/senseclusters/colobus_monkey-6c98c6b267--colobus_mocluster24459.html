colobus monkey

noun
African tree-dwelling monkey with silky fur
9.2 5.6
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈloʊ.bəs ˈmʌŋ.kɪ/ (AmE), /kə.ˈlɒ.bəs ˈmʌŋ.kɪ/ /; / /kə.ˈləʊ.bəs ˈmʌŋ.kɪ/ (BrE)
