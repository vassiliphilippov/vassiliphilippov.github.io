untouchable

adjective
lowest caste; socially defiled (esp. South Asian context)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʌtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʌtʃəbl / (BrE)
(historical, chiefly South Asian) Regarded as belonging to the lowest social caste, and thus considered defiled or unacceptable for physical contact or social mixing.

Examples

Untouchable caste
In some traditional societies, certain people were considered untouchable and faced severe discrimination.
She spoke out against the unjust treatment of untouchable communities in her homeland.
The book explores the harsh realities experienced by those labeled as untouchable in rural India.
