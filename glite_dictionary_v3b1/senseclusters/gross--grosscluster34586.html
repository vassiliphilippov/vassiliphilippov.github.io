gross

adjective
extremely obvious and unacceptable
5.1 3.5
Pronunciation: /groʊs/ (AmE), /grəʊs/ (BrE)
extremely obvious, total, or glaring (used for errors, injustice, or emphasizing something bad)

Examples

Gross mistake
His repeated errors showed a gross lack of attention to detail.
The company was found guilty of gross negligence in failing to protect its workers.
There was gross injustice in how the law was applied to different communities.
Her accusation was a gross distortion of the truth.
It would be a gross injustice to punish the innocent.
The company was found guilty of gross negligence in maintaining safety procedures.
