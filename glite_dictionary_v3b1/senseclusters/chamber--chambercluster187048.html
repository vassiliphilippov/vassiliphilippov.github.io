chamber

noun
legislative or deliberative assembly
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪmbə / (BrE)
a group of people forming a legislative, deliberative, administrative, or judicial body

Examples

A legislative chamber
The Supreme Court convened in its chamber to hear the case.
The city council operates as a legislative chamber for local government.
The legislation must pass through both the lower and upper chambers of parliament.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assembly - a group of people gathered for a common purpose
  • synonym: house - a legislative or deliberative body or chamber
  • synonym: senate - the upper chamber of a legislative body, especially in the US or ancient Rome
