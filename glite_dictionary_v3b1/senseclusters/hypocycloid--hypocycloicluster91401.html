hypocycloid

noun
type of mathematical curve
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪ.poʊˈsaɪ.klɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪ.pəʊˈsʌɪ.klɔɪd / (BrE)
A plane curve traced by a point on the circumference of a circle that rolls without slipping inside a larger fixed circle.

Examples

A hypocycloid curve
