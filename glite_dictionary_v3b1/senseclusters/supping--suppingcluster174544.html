supping

noun
the act of drinking, especially with a spoon
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of drinking or consuming liquid food, especially with a spoon or by sipping

Examples

Slow supping of soup
After a day in the cold, she found comfort in the slow supping of hot soup.
He took great pleasure in the gentle supping of herbal tea before bed.
Their supping of broth was accompanied by quiet conversation around the fire.
