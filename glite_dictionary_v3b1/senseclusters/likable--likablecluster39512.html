likable

adjective
pleasant and easy to like
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪkəbəl / (AmE), / ˈlaɪkəbl /; / ˈlaɪkəbəl / (BrE)
pleasant and easy to like; attractive or agreeable in a way that makes people feel friendly or approving
