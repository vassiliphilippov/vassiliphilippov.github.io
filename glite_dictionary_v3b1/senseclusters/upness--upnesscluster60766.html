upness

noun
state of being up
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpnəs / (AmE), / ˈʌpnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being up, upward, or elevated.

Examples

The upness of a peak
The upness of the mountain peak made it visible from miles away.
She admired the upness of the skyscraper as it towered above the city.
The balloon's upness made it float above the crowd.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downness - the quality or state of being down or downward
  • synonym: elevation - the height above a given level, especially sea level
  • synonym: height - the measurement of how tall something is
