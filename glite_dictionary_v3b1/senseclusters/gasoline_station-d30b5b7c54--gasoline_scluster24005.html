gasoline station

noun
place to buy fuel for vehicles
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæsəliːn ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈɡasəliːn ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A place where fuel, especially gasoline, is sold and vehicles can have their tanks filled.

Examples

A gasoline station stop
After the long drive, they desperately needed a gasoline station for both fuel and snacks.
The nearest gasoline station is five miles away from here.
We stopped at a gasoline station to fill up the tank before leaving town.

Related Terms

  • synonym: petrol station - a service station that sells petrol (primarily British English)
  • synonym: service station - a place that sells fuel and provides services for vehicles
