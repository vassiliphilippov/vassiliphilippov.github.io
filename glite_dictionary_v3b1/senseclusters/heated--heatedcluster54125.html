heated

adjective
emotionally intense or vehement
4.2 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàhiÀêt…™d / (AmE), / ÀàhiÀêt…™d / (BrE)
(of a situation or discussion) marked by intense emotion, anger, or excitement; vehement.

Examples

Heated argument
She regretted her heated response after she calmed down.
The debate became heated as each side refused to compromise.
We had a heated discussion about politics last night.
