Herodian

adjective
of Herod's dynasty
Pronunciation: / həˈroʊdiən / (AmE), / həˈrəʊdiən / (BrE)
relating to Herod the Great, his dynasty, or their period of rule in ancient Judea

Examples

Herodian architecture
The Herodian architecture in Jerusalem is famous for its massive stone blocks.
Many coins from the Herodian era have been discovered by archaeologists.
The historian wrote a book about Herodian politics in ancient Judea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Herod - the name of several rulers of Judea in ancient times
