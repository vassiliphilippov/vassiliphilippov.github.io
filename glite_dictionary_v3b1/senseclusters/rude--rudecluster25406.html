rude

adjective
primitive or crude in development
9.8 5.7
Pronunciation: /ruːd/ (AmE), /ruːd/ (BrE)
existing at an early stage of technical or social development; simple or crude in form, construction, or function.

Examples

Rude tools
Archaeologists discovered rude pottery shards that dated back thousands of years.
Early settlers built their homes with rude tools and basic materials found in the forest.
The mountain villagers survived with rude shelters and little access to modern conveniences.
