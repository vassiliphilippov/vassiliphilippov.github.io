Behmenism

noun
mystical doctrines of Jakob Boehme
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪmɪnˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈbeɪmənɪzəm / (BrE)
the mystical theological doctrines of Jakob Boehme, particularly his views on the nature of God and creation, which influenced later religious movements such as the Quakers
