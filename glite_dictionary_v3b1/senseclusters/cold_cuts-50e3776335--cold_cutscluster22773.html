cold cuts

noun
sliced assorted cold meats
8.4 1.8
Pronunciation: /koʊld kʌts/ (AmE), /kəʊld kʌts/ (BrE)
Sliced cooked or cured meats, typically served cold and often assorted, used in sandwiches or as part of a platter.

Examples

We ordered a platter of cold cuts and cheese for the party.
