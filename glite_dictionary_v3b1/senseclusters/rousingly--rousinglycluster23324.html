rousingly

adverb
in an energizing way
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊzɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈraʊzɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that excites, inspires, or stirs people to action or strong emotion

Examples

To speak rousingly
The speaker addressed the crowd rousingly, inspiring everyone to take action.
The band played rousingly at the festival, getting the audience on their feet.
He cheered rousingly for his team as they scored the winning goal.
The national anthem was performed rousingly, filling the stadium with energy.
She spoke rousingly about the need for change in the community.
The team was cheered rousingly by their fans after the victory.
