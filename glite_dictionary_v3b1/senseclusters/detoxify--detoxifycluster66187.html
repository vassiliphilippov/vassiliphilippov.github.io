detoxify

verb
remove toxins or poisons
3.3
Pronunciation: / diˈtɑksɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / diˈtɒksɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to remove toxic or harmful substances from something, especially to render it harmless

Examples

To detoxify a substance
Doctors worked quickly to detoxify the patient after a drug overdose.
Some plants can naturally detoxify contaminated soil through a process called phytoremediation.
Special processes are used to detoxify industrial waste before it is released into the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: purify - to make pure by removing contaminants
