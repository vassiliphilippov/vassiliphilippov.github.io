elliptic leaf

noun
simple leaf shaped like an ellipse
9.9 5.5
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈlɪp.tɪk liːf/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈlɪp.tɪk liːf/ (BrE)
a simple leaf whose shape resembles an ellipse, being widest at the middle and tapering equally towards both ends

Examples

A elliptic leaf shape
A botanist collected a specimen with a single, smooth-edged elliptic leaf for further study.
Some botanists classify the elliptic leaf as distinct from an oval leaf due to its symmetrical shape.
The magnolia tree is easily identified by its large elliptic leaves.
