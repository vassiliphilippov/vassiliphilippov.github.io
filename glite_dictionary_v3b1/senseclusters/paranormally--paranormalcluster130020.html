paranormally

adverb
beyond scientific explanation
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈnɔrməli / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈnɔːməli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to phenomena beyond scientific explanation or the normal laws of nature

Examples

To act paranormally
Some people believe the old castle is paranormally active during thunderstorms.
The documentary explored whether the events could be explained paranormally or had a scientific basis.
She claimed her dreams were paranormally influenced by the mysterious artifact.

Related Terms
