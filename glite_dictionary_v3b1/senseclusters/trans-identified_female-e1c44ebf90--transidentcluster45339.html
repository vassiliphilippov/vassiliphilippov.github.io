trans-identified female

noun
female person who identifies as transgender
Pronunciation: / trænz aɪˈdɛntəˌfaɪd ˈfiːmeɪl / (AmE), / trænz ʔɪˈdɛntɪfaɪd ˈfiːmeɪl / (BrE)
a female who identifies as transgender, often specifically referring to an assigned-female-at-birth person who identifies with a gender identity different from their sex assigned at birth

Examples

Trans-identified female person
Many organizations are working to support trans-identified females in their communities.
Research on trans-identified females contributes to better understanding of gender diversity.
The panel discussed healthcare needs relevant to trans-identified females.
