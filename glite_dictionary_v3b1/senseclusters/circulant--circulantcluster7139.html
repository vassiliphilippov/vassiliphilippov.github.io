circulant

adjective
cyclically shifted rows
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrkjələnt / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊlənt / (BrE)
having a structure, especially in matrices, where each row is a cyclic shift of the previous row

Examples

A circulant matrix
A circulant matrix is often used in digital signal processing applications.
The algorithm exploits the circulant property to simplify complex computations.
Researchers analyzed the circulant structure to find efficient solutions in mathematics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cyclic - recurring in cycles or having a repeating pattern
