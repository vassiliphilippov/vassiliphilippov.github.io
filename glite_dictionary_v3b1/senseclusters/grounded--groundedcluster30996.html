grounded

adjective
not allowed to fly
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈgraʊn.dɪd/ (AmE), /ˈgraʊn.dɪd/ (BrE)
kept on the ground and not allowed to fly or operate, especially referring to aircraft or people with flying privileges

Examples

Grounded flights
All flights were grounded due to the heavy fog covering the airport.
After the incident, the pilot was grounded until further notice.
The space agency grounded its astronauts while investigating the technical issue.
