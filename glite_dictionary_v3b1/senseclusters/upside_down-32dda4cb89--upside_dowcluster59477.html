upside down

adverb
in an inverted or overturned manner

in a position such that the top is where the bottom normally is; in an inverted or overturned manner.

Examples

Upside down position
After the storm, many of the boats were left upside down on the shore.
She realized the painting had been hanging upside down for weeks.
Turn the jar upside down to get the last bit of sauce out.

Synonyms

  • upturned
    4.5 0.05/m
    upturned is turned so the bottom faces up and often looks gently flipped, while upside down means fully inverted.
  • inverted
    4.8 0.02/m
    inverted is a formal word for something with top and bottom reversed, while upside down is common in everyday speech.
  • overturned
    3.6 0.00/m
    overturned often describes things flipped by force or accident, while upside down focuses on position without cause.
  • upended
    4.4 0.00/m
    upended usually means set on end or disrupted suddenly, while upside down describes a simple inverted position.
