sizzlingly

adverb
extremely hot or exciting
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪzəlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsɪzəlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely hot, intense, or exciting

Examples

Sizzlingly hot day
The chef brought out a sizzlingly hot pan of fajitas that filled the room with aroma.
Her sizzlingly passionate performance captivated everyone in the audience.
The summer sun beat down sizzlingly on the crowded beach all afternoon.
