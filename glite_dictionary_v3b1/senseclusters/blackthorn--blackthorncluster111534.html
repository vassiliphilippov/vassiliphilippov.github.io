blackthorn

noun
American hawthorn shrub with pear-shaped berries
4.9 8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌθɔrn / (AmE), / ˈblækˌθɔːn / (BrE)
An erect, almost thornless American hawthorn with somewhat pear-shaped berries, distinguished from the European blackthorn.

Examples

Botanists compared the American blackthorn to similar hawthorn varieties native to the region.

Unlike European species, the American blackthorn has few thorns and smoother branches.
