incongruence

noun
lack of harmony
8.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈkɑːn.ɡruː.əns/ /; / /ɪn.ˈkɑːn.ɡrʊ.əns/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈkɒn.ɡruː.əns/ /; / /ɪn.ˈkɒn.ɡrʊ.əns/ (BrE)
a lack of agreement, harmony, or compatibility between things or ideas

Examples

Incongruence between ideas
There was a clear incongruence between his promises and his actions.
The incongruence of their opinions made it difficult to reach a decision.
She noticed an incongruence between the data and the conclusions in the report.

Related Terms

  • antonym: congruence - agreement or harmony between things
  • synonym: discord - disagreement or lack of harmony
  • synonym: inconsistency - the state of being contradictory or not in agreement
