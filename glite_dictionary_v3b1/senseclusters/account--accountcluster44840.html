account

noun
itemized statement of money owed
Pronunciation: / əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
a record, statement, or arrangement related to money, such as a bill, bank account, or financial statement

Examples

Bank account
She settled the account with the supplier by bank transfer.
The account listed every item he had purchased over the month.
The company sent an account to the client for payment at the end of the project.
She opened a new account at the local bank to manage her savings.
The company suspended his account after fraudulent activity was detected.
To access online services, you must first create an account with your email address.
She checked her account to see if the payment was processed.
The account shows all deposits and withdrawals from this quarter.
The bank emailed my monthly account for review.
