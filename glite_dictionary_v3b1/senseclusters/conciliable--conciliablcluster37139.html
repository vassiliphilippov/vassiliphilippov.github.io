conciliable

adjective
able to be pacified or reconciled
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɪliəbl̩ / (AmE), / kənˈsɪliəbl̩ / (BrE)
capable of being pacified or won over; able to be reconciled

Examples

A conciliable nature
Despite their differences, his conciliable nature helped restore peace within the team.
Some conflicts are not conciliable, no matter how hard people try to mediate.
The manager found the usually conciliable employee unusually resistant after the policy change.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irreconcilable - impossible to reconcile or bring into agreement
