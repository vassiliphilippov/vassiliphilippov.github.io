Synonyms

    2.7 0.5/m
    glittery is covered in small shiny bits that stick to surfaces, while sparkly describes a casual, playful glittering look.
  • glittering
    3.0 0.08/m
    glittering shines with bright, flashing points and sounds more formal or strong, while sparkly feels light and playful in use.
  • sparkling
    1.3 0.2/m
    sparkling shows lively, bright flashes and can describe people or drinks, while sparkly mainly refers to a glittery surface.
  • twinkling
    3.3 0.00/m
    twinkling shines with small, quick flashes like stars at night, while sparkly often describes a glittery object or cloth.
  • glinting
    3.6 0.00/m
    glinting gives brief bright flashes from smooth surfaces like metal, while sparkly shows many steady, small points of light.
  • scintillating
    8.2 0.01/m
    scintillating flashes with very bright, brilliant light and often sounds formal or literary, while sparkly is casual and visual.
