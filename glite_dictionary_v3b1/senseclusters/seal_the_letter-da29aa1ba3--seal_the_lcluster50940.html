seal the letter

verb
securely close a letter, often with wax
1.8 2.3
Pronunciation: /siːl ðə ˈlet.ər/ (AmE), /siːl ðə ˈlet.ə/ (BrE)
to close a letter securely, often by melting wax or using adhesive to ensure it cannot be opened unnoticed

Examples

To seal the letter shut
After writing his apology, he used glue to seal the letter shut.
She carefully pressed the wax to seal the letter before mailing it.
Traditionally, people would seal the letter with a personal stamp.

Related Terms
