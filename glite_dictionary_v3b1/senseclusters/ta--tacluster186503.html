ta

interjection
informal thank you (British)
Pronunciation: / tɑ / (AmE), / tɑː / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly British) An informal way of saying 'thank you.'

Examples

Ta very much
He handed her the parcel and she replied, "ta very much."
Just a quick ta for helping me with my homework yesterday!
She smiled at the driver and said ta as she got off the bus.
