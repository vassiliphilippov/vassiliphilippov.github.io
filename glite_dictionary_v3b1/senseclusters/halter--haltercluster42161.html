halter

verb
to put on a halter
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔltər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːltə / (BrE)
to put a halter on an animal, especially a horse or cow, to control or lead it

Examples

To halter a cow
The rancher haltered the young calf before taking it to the barn.
She carefully halters each horse before their morning exercise.
After the storm, the farmer haltered the nervous cow to keep it calm.
