Sterculia apetala

noun
large tropical tree (the Panama tree)
9.9
Pronunciation: /stɜːr.ˈkjuː.lɪ.ə ə.ˈpe.tə.lə/ (AmE), /stɜː.ˈkjuː.lɪ.ə ə.ˈpe.tə.lə/ (BrE)
a large deciduous tree native to Central and South America, notable for its densely leafy crown and bare trunk; known as the Panama tree and believed to have given Panama its name
