Cracticidae

noun
family of Australian birds
9.9
Pronunciation: / krækˈtɪsɪdiː / (AmE), / krakˈtɪsɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of Australian birds that were formerly classified with shrikes, including species such as butcherbirds and currawongs.
