immodest

adjective
lacking decency or propriety
6.2 5.0
Pronunciation: /ɪm.ˈmɑː.dɪst/ /; / /ɪm.ˈmɑː.dəst/ (AmE), /ɪm.ˈmɒd.ɪst/ /; / /ɪm.ˈmɒd.əst/ (BrE)
lacking in decency or propriety; not conforming to accepted standards of behavior or dress, especially in regard to modesty

Examples

Immodest dress

It would be immodest of me to claim all the credit for this project.
Some people considered her dress immodest at the conservative event.
