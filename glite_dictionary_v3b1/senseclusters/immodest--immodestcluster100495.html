immodest

adjective
lacking decency or propriety
6.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɑdəst / (AmE), / ɪˈmɒdɪst / (BrE)
lacking in decency or propriety; not conforming to accepted standards of behavior or dress, especially in regard to modesty

Examples

Immodest dress
His immodest remarks offended several members of the audience.
It would be immodest of me to claim all the credit for this project.
Some people considered her dress immodest at the conservative event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indecent - not conforming with generally accepted standards of behavior or propriety; obscene
  • antonym: modest - having or showing a moderate or humble estimate of one's merits; not showy
