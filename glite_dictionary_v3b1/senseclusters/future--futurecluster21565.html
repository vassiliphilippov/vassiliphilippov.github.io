future

noun
the time yet to come
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːtʃər / (AmE), / ˈfjuː.tʃə / (BrE)
the time that will come after the present; the period of time following the moment of speaking or writing

Examples

The future ahead
No one can predict the future with absolute certainty.
She is saving money for the future.
Technological advances will shape our future in unexpected ways.

Related Terms

  • antonym: past - the time before the present; earlier times
  • antonym: present - the period of time occurring now
