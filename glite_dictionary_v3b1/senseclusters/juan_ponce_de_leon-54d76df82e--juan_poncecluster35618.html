Juan Ponce de Leon

proper noun
Spanish explorer, discovered Florida
Pronunciation: / hwan ˈpɔns də leɪˈɒn / (AmE), / ˈwæn ˈpɒns də leɪˈɒn / (BrE)
(1460–1521) Spanish explorer who accompanied Columbus on his second voyage in 1493 and, in 1513, became the first European to discover Florida while searching for the legendary Fountain of Youth.

Examples

Juan Ponce de Leon explorer
Juan Ponce de Leon is credited with the European discovery of Florida in 1513.
Juan Ponce de Leon's expeditions played a significant role in the Spanish colonization of the Americas.
Many legends surround Juan Ponce de Leon and his search for the Fountain of Youth.
