Cynocephalus

noun
genus of flying lemurs (colugos)
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪnoʊˈsɛfələs /; / saɪˌnoʊsɪˈfæləs / (AmE), / ˌsɪnəʊˈsɛfələs /; / sɪˌnəʊsɪˈfæləs / (BrE)
a genus of mammals in the family Cynocephalidae, known as flying lemurs or colugos
