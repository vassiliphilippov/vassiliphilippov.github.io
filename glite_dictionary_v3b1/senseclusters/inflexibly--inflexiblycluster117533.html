In a way that does not change or adapt; in a rigid or unyielding manner.

Examples

To inflexibly refuse
He inflexibly refused to change his opinion despite new evidence.
She applied the rules inflexibly, leaving no room for exceptions.
The company's policies are enforced inflexibly across all departments.

Synonyms

    4.3 2.01/m

  • unyieldingly
    7.4 0.02/m
    unyieldingly means holding firm under pressure and not giving way, while inflexibly means not changing or adapting in any situation.
  • adamantly
    6.4 1.78/m
    adamantly often means stating a strong refusal or firm view, while inflexibly focuses on refusing to change behaviour or rules.
