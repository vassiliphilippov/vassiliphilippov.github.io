overdress

verb
wear too many clothes; dress too warmly
5.1 3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈdrɛs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈdrɛs / (BrE)
to put on more clothing than necessary, especially so as to be too warm

Examples

To overdress in winter
Children often overdress in winter and end up feeling too hot indoors.
I tend to overdress for autumn hikes, then have to remove layers as I warm up.
If you overdress your baby, she might become uncomfortable and fussy.
