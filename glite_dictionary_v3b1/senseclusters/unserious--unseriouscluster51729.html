unserious

adjective
not serious
4.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɪriəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪəriəs / (BrE)
not showing a serious attitude or not treating something with seriousness

Examples

Unserious attitude
His unserious attitude during the meeting annoyed his colleagues.
The teacher warned the class not to be unserious about their assignments.
She gave an unserious answer to a very important question.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frivolous - not having any serious purpose or value
  • antonym: serious - showing careful thought or not joking
