palette

noun
armpit armor plate on a suit of armor
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpælət / (AmE), / ˈpælɪt / (BrE)
one of the rounded armor plates located at the armpits of a suit of armor, designed to protect the wearer

Examples

Armor palette
Conservators restored the dented palette from the 15th-century suit of armor.
Each palette was carefully shaped to allow for arm movement while offering protection.
The knight's palette deflected the blade away from his vulnerable armpit.
