smugness

noun
excessive self-satisfaction
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsmʌɡ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsmʌɡ.nəs / (BrE)
Excessive or irritating satisfaction with oneself or one's achievements.

Examples

His smugness showed
He could not hide his smugness when he answered every question correctly.
Her smugness after winning the award irritated her colleagues.
There was a hint of smugness in his smile as he revealed the news.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modesty - lack of self-satisfaction or boasting
  • synonym: self-satisfaction - pleasure in one's own achievements or qualities
