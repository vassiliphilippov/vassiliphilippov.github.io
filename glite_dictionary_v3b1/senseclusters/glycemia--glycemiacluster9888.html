glycemia

noun
blood sugar level
Pronunciation: / ɡlaɪˈsiːmiə / (AmE), / ɡlɪˈsiːmiə /; / ɡlʌɪˈsiːmɪə / (BrE)
the presence or concentration of sugar (especially glucose) in the blood

Examples

Blood glycemia level
Doctors monitor glycemia closely in patients with diabetes.
A sudden drop in glycemia can cause dizziness and weakness.
Maintaining normal glycemia is important for overall health.
Doctors monitor glycemia to help manage diabetes.
A sudden drop in glycemia can cause dizziness and confusion.
Regular exercise can help maintain healthy glycemia levels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blood sugar - the amount of glucose present in the blood
  • degree higher: hyperglycemia - an abnormally high level of sugar in the blood
  • degree lower: hypoglycemia - an abnormally low level of sugar in the blood
