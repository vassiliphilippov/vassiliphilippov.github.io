Examples

Silk filament
Biologists studied each filament to understand how the cells functioned together.
The light bulb's tungsten filament glowed brightly when switched on.
Under the microscope, a single filament of algae appeared as a chain of tiny cells.
A single filament of silk can be incredibly strong for its size.
Synthetic filaments are widely used in the production of textiles and plastics.
Thick ropes are often made of many smaller filaments twisted together.
