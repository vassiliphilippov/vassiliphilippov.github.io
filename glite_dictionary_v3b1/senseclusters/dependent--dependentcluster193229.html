dependent

adjective
contingent on something else
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
(of something) determined or controlled by something else; contingent upon another factor or condition.

Examples

Dependent on weather
Economic growth in the area is dependent upon new infrastructure investments.
His recovery is largely dependent on following the doctor's advice.
The success of the project is dependent on teamwork and cooperation.
Access to the bonus is dependant upon meeting specific performance targets.
The company's future is dependant on securing new contracts this year.
Whether we can go hiking tomorrow is dependant on the weather forecast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dependent - contingent on something else
  • antonym: independent - not depending on something else; free from external control
