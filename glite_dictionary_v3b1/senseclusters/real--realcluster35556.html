real

noun

A number that is either rational or irrational, including all the numbers on the number line, except imaginary numbers.

Examples

Real number line
In calculus, we often deal with functions defined over the reals.
The set of all reals includes both rational and irrational numbers.
Zero is considered a real, as it is neither positive nor negative.
