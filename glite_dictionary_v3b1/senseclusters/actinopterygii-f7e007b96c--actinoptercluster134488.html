Actinopterygii

proper noun
ray-finned fish class
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæktɪnɑptəˈrɪdʒiaɪ / (AmE), / ˌæktɪnɒptəˈrɪdʒɪaɪ / (BrE)
the scientific class of ray-finned fishes, which includes most modern fish species

The Actinopterygii make up the largest class of vertebrates in the animal kingdom.
Scientists study the evolution of Actinopterygii to understand the diversity of modern fish.
