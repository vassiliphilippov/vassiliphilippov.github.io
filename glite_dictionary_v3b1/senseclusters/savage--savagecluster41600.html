(of people, customs, or conditions) lacking sophistication, culture, or the influences of civilization; considered primitive or uncivilized.

Examples

Savage tribe
Early laws were designed to bring order to what was seen as a savage frontier.
Some colonial authors wrote harshly about the savage customs they observed in distant lands.
The explorers encountered a savage tribe that had never seen outsiders before.

Synonyms

  • uncivilized
    5.9 1.08/m
    uncivilized describes people or societies without social or cultural development, while savage suggests rough, primitive behavior or customs.
  • barbaric
    6.3 2.76/m
    barbaric describes cruel, violent, or brutal actions, while savage refers more to being primitive and lacking culture.
  • barbarian
    7.6 0.36/m
    barbarian names a person seen as uncivilized or violent, while savage describes a primitive, uncultured quality or behavior.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbaric - uncivilized or primitive, lacking refinement or culture
