savage

adjective
primitive or uncivilized; lacking civilization
Pronunciation: / ˈsævɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsavɪdʒ / (BrE)
(of people, customs, or conditions) lacking sophistication, culture, or the influences of civilization; considered primitive or uncivilized.

Examples

Savage tribe
Early laws were designed to bring order to what was seen as a savage frontier.
Some colonial authors wrote harshly about the savage customs they observed in distant lands.
The explorers encountered a savage tribe that had never seen outsiders before.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbaric - uncivilized or primitive, lacking refinement or culture
  • antonym: civilized - having an advanced culture, refined, not primitive
