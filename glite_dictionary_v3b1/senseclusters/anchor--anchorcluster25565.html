anchor

noun
person or thing providing support or stability
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋkɚ / (AmE), / ˈaŋkə / (BrE)
a person or thing that provides stability, support, or confidence, especially in difficult situations

Examples

Emotional anchor
Faith has been the anchor that kept him steady through all difficulties.
For the team, their captain was the anchor they could always count on.
She relied on her husband as an anchor during times of crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keystone - the central, most important element
  • synonym: linchpin - a vital person or thing that holds something together
  • synonym: mainstay - a chief support or main source of stability
