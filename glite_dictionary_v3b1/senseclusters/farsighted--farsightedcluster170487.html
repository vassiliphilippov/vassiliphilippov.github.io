farsighted

adjective
able to plan wisely for the future
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrˌsaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfɑː.saɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
able to plan wisely for the future; showing thought for what may happen later.

Examples

Farsighted leader
Her farsighted approach to education reform was praised by experts.
Only a farsighted leader would implement policies that benefit future generations.
The company's farsighted investments helped it weather the economic downturn.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shortsighted - lacking consideration for future consequences
