mindless

adjective
lacking attention or awareness
5.4 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪnd.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪnd.ləs/ (BrE)
not using thought, intelligence, or awareness; lacking sense or consideration

Examples

Mindless behavior
Doing mindless tasks can sometimes help relax your mind after a stressful day.
He wandered through the museum in a mindless daze, barely noticing the exhibits.
She gave a mindless answer without considering the consequences.
She was disturbed by the mindless destruction caused by the vandals.
The protesters condemned the mindless violence that erupted in the streets.
Watching hours of mindless television shows left him feeling bored and empty.
He found the repetitive task to be completely mindless and boring.
She stared with a mindless look, lost in daydreams.
The movie was entertaining, but it was basically just mindless action from start to finish.
