reedbird

noun
small European warbler
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdbɝːd / (AmE), / ˈriːdbɜːd / (BrE)
A small European warbler that breeds among reeds and sedges and winters in Africa.

Examples

European reedbird
Each autumn, thousands of reedbirds migrate south to spend the winter in Africa.
Ornithologists spotted a flock of reedbirds nesting by the riverbank.
The reedbird is well known for its melodious song during the breeding season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sedge warbler - a small brown and cream European warbler often called 'reedbird'
