slouch

noun
stooped posture or stance
5.5 0.33/m
Pronunciation: / slaÊŠtÊƒ / (AmE), / slaÊŠtÊƒ / (BrE)
A posture in which the shoulders and head are bent forward, giving an awkward or ungainly appearance while standing or walking.

Examples

A tired slouch
Her slouch made her look much shorter than she actually was.
The teacher told him to fix his slouch and stand up straight.
Years of working at a desk gave him a noticeable slouch.
