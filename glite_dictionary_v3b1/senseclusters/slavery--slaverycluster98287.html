slavery

noun
the state of being controlled by another
6.1 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsleɪ.və.riː/ /; / /ˈsleɪ.vrɪ/ (AmE), /ˈsleɪ.və.rɪ/ /; / /ˈsleɪ.vrɪ/ (BrE)
the condition of being entirely under the control of another person; a situation in which a person is forced to obey someone else and lacks personal freedom

Examples

Life in slavery
After years in slavery, he finally managed to escape and live freely.
Many activists fought tirelessly to abolish slavery in the nineteenth century.
She described her overwhelming workload as a form of modern slavery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: freedom - the state of being free rather than under the control of another
  • synonym: servitude - the state of being a servant or completely subject to someone more powerful
