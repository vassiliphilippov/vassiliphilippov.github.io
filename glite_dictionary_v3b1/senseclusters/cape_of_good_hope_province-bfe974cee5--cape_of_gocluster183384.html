Cape of Good Hope Province

proper noun
former province of South Africa
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌkeɪp əv ɡʊd ˈhoʊp ˈprɑvɪns / (AmE), / ˌkeɪp əv ɡʊd ˈhəʊp ˈprɒvɪns / (BrE)
a former province in southern South Africa, established by Dutch settlers in 1652, ceded to Great Britain in 1814, and divided into three new provinces in 1994

Examples
