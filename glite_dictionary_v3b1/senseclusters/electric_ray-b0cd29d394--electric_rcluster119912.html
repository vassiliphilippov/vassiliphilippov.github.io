electric ray

noun
ray fish with electric organs
8.8
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk reɪ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk reɪ / (BrE)
a slow-moving, bottom-dwelling ray of the order Torpediniformes that has a rounded body and electric organs capable of producing strong electric discharges

Examples

The electric ray fish
Researchers studied the powerful shock produced by the electric ray in shallow waters.
The electric ray stunned its prey before swallowing it whole.
We spotted a large electric ray resting on the ocean floor during our dive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: torpedo - another name for electric ray, especially of the genus Torpedo
