uncatechized

adjective
not instructed
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈtɛkɪˌzaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈtɛkɪzaɪzd / (BrE)
not instructed or initiated in the basic principles or practices of a particular system, field, or tradition, often used figuratively.

Many students felt uncatechized when they started the advanced course without any prior background.
