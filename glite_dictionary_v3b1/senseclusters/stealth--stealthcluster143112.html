stealth

noun
secretive or cautious movement to avoid detection
Pronunciation: / stɛlθ / (AmE), / stɛlθ / (BrE)
the act of moving, proceeding, or acting in a secretive or cautious way to avoid being detected

Examples

Military stealth technology
The aircraft is designed for stealth to evade enemy radar.
The cat moved with such stealth that the mouse didn't notice a thing.
Thieves rely on stealth to slip past security without being caught.

Related Terms

  • synonym: furtiveness - secretive behavior intended to avoid notice or attention
  • antonym: open - done or carried out without attempt to hide; not secretive
