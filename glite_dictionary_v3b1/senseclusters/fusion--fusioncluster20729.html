fusion

noun
nuclear reaction combining nuclei
5.9 5.96/m
Pronunciation: / Ààfju í…ôn / (AmE), / ÀàfjuÀê í(…ô)n / (BrE)
a nuclear reaction in which atomic nuclei combine to form a heavier nucleus, releasing energy

Examples

Nuclear fusion
Scientists are working to achieve controlled fusion to generate clean energy.
The Sun's energy comes from the process of nuclear fusion.
Unlike fission reactors, fusion reactors have the potential to produce less radioactive waste.

Synonyms
