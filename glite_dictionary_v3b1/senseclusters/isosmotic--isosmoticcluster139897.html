isosmotic

adjective
having equal osmotic pressure
2.6 7.8
Pronunciation: /aɪ.soʊz.ˈmɑː.tɪk/ /; / /ɪ.soʊz.ˈmɑː.tɪk/ (AmE), /aɪ.səʊz.ˈmɒ.tɪk/ /; / /ɪ.səʊz.ˈmɒ.tɪk/ (BrE)
(of solutions) having the same osmotic pressure as another solution, especially when separated by a semipermeable membrane.
