trisodium

adjective
with three sodium atoms
Pronunciation: / traɪˈsoʊdiəm / (AmE), / traɪˈsəʊdiəm / (BrE)
containing three sodium atoms or ions in its chemical structure

Examples

Trisodium phosphate cleaner
The cleaning agent contains trisodium phosphate to help remove grease and stains.
Scientists analyzed a compound with a trisodium structure for its potential industrial uses.
Some processed foods list trisodium citrate as a preservative on their ingredient labels.

  • degree lower: monosodium - containing one sodium atom or ion
