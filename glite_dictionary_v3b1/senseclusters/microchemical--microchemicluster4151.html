microchemical

adjective
small-scale chemical
8.1
Pronunciation: /maɪ.krə.ˈkem.ɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /maɪ.krə.ˈkem.ɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
relating to chemical analysis or reactions involving very small amounts of substances

Examples

A microchemical technique
A microchemical technique allows scientists to analyze substances using only a few milligrams of material.
The laboratory specializes in microchemical methods for detecting trace elements in water samples.
Because the sample was so small, a microchemical approach was necessary for accurate results.

Related Terms

  • antonym: macrochemical - relating to chemical analysis involving large quantities
  • synonym: microanalysis - chemical analysis of very small samples
