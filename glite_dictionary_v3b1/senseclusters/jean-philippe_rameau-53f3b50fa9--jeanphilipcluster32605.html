Jean-Philippe Rameau

proper noun
French composer and music theorist
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʒɑ̃ fiˈlip raˈmoʊ / (AmE), / ʒɑ̃ fiˈliːp raˈmoʊ / (BrE)
