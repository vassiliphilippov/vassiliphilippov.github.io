capisce

interjection
do you understand
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈpiʃ / (AmE), / kəˈpiːʃ / (BrE)
used to ask if someone understands or agrees, often at the end of a statement for emphasis

Examples

Listen, capisce?
You need to finish your homework before going out, capisce?
No one is allowed in this room after midnight, capisce?
We meet at the usual place, and you bring the documents, capisce?

Related Terms

  • synonym: got it - informal way to confirm understanding
  • synonym: understand - to grasp the meaning or significance of something
