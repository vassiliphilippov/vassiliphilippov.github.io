Brahmanic

adjective
of Brahmanism or Brahmins
9.5
Pronunciation: /brɑː.ˈmɑː.nɪk/ /; / /brə.ˈmæ.nɪk/ (AmE), /brɑː.ˈmɑː.nɪk/ /; / /brə.ˈmæ.nɪk/ (BrE)
relating to Brahmanism, the Brahmin caste, or the ritual traditions and texts (such as the Brahmana sections of the Vedas) in Hinduism

Examples

Brahmanic rituals
The ancient texts were written in a Brahmanic style that reflected the traditions of the priestly class.
Many Brahmanic rituals are still practiced in temples across India today.
The festival included several Brahmanic ceremonies led by local priests.
The ancient text contains many Brahmanic explanations of religious ceremonies.
He gave a lecture on the Brahmanic traditions found in early Indian literature.
Researchers analyzed Brahmanic rituals to understand their influence on later Hindu practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Brahminical - relating to Brahmins or their traditions
  • synonym: Vedic - relating to the Vedas, the oldest Hindu scriptures
  • antonym: non-Brahmanic - not relating to Brahmanism or the Brahmin caste
  • synonym: ritualistic - relating to or characteristic of rituals
