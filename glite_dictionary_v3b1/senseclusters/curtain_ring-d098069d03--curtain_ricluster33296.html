curtain ring

noun
ring used to hang curtains
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜrtən rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtən rɪŋ / (BrE)
a circular ring, usually of metal or plastic, used to attach and support a curtain on a rod

Examples

A curtain ring on rod
A decorative curtain ring can add a stylish touch to your window treatment.
She slid each curtain ring onto the pole before hanging the new drapes.
The old curtain rings started to rust and needed replacement.
