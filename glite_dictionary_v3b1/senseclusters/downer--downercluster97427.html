downer

noun
cause of disappointment
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnər / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnə / (BrE)
something that causes sadness, disappointment, or a negative mood

Examples

A real downer moment
Hearing about the canceled trip was a real downer for the whole family.
The movie ended on a downer, leaving the audience feeling sad.
It is such a downer when your plans fall through at the last minute.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disappointment - a feeling of sadness because something was not as good as expected
  • synonym: letdown - something that disappoints or fails to meet expectations
  • antonym: uplift - something that makes you feel happier or more positive
