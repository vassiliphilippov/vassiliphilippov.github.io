recyclable

noun
item for recycling
3.5
Pronunciation: / riˈsaɪkləbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈsaɪkləbəl / (BrE)
an item or material that can be collected and processed to be used again instead of being thrown away

Examples

Sort the recyclables
Please separate all recyclables from regular trash before putting them in the bin.
The city collects recyclables every Tuesday morning from each neighborhood.
Glass bottles and paper are common recyclables in most households.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reusable - able to be used again for the same or a different purpose
  • antonym: waste - unwanted or unusable materials discarded after use
