malolactic

adjective
wine fermentation process
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌmæloʊˈlæktɪk / (AmE), / ˌmæləʊˈlæktɪk / (BrE)
relating to a fermentation process in winemaking that converts malic acid into lactic acid

Examples

Malolactic fermentation
Many red wines undergo malolactic fermentation to create a softer mouthfeel.
The winemaker explained that the malolactic process reduces the wine's acidity.
Chardonnay often benefits from malolactic fermentation, resulting in a creamier texture.
