orgy

noun
wild party with excessive drinking and sex
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdʒi / (AmE), / ˈɔːdʒi / (BrE)
A wild party characterized by excessive drinking and unrestrained sexual activity.

Examples

A wild orgy
He was arrested after the police raided the orgy in the penthouse apartment.
Rumors of an orgy taking place after the festival shocked the conservative town.
The tabloids reported that the celebrities were involved in a secret orgy at the mansion.
