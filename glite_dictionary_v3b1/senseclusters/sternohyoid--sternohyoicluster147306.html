sternohyoid

noun
neck muscle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌstɜrnoʊˈhaɪɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌstɜːnəʊˈhaɪɔɪd / (BrE)
a muscle in the neck that connects the sternum to the hyoid bone and helps lower the hyoid during swallowing

Examples

Sternohyoid muscle in neck
The sternohyoid is important for swallowing and speaking.
During neck surgery, the sternohyoid muscle may need to be moved aside.
Injury to the sternohyoid can affect the movement of the hyoid bone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: omohyoid - a neck muscle connecting the shoulder blade to the hyoid bone
  • synonym: sternothyroid - a neck muscle connecting the sternum to the thyroid cartilage
