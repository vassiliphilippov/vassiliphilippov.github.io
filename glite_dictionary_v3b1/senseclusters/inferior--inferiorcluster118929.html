inferior

adjective
of lower quality or value
1.1 3.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈfɪr.iː.ər/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈfɪə.riː.ə/ (BrE)
of lower quality or value; not as good as something else

Examples

Inferior product quality
Many claimed that the new software was inferior to its predecessor in terms of features and speed.
Students complained that the facilities in the old building were inferior to those in the new one.
The company recalled the product because it was made with inferior materials.
He was worried his project would be judged as inferior to the others.
Some medicines sold online may be inferior and potentially unsafe.
The materials used were of inferior quality and broke easily.
