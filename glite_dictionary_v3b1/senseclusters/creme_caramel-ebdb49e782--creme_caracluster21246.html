creme caramel

noun
custard dessert with caramel topping
6.1
Pronunciation: / krɛm kɑˈrɑmɛl, krim karəˈmɛl / (AmE), / krɛm kaːˈramɛl, krɛm kaːrəˈmɛl / (BrE)
A dessert consisting of a sweet baked custard topped with a layer of soft caramel.

Examples

A creme caramel dessert
For dessert, she made a delicious creme caramel topped with berries.
He brought a homemade creme caramel to the dinner party and everyone loved it.
The restaurant’s signature creme caramel was smooth and rich in flavor.
