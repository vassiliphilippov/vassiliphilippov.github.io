limekiln

noun
kiln for producing lime from limestone
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪmˌkɪln / (AmE), / ˈlaɪmˌkɪln / (BrE)
A furnace or kiln used to produce lime from limestone by heating it to a high temperature.

Examples

A limekiln furnace
Historically, every village had at least one limekiln for agricultural and construction needs.
The old limekiln by the river has stood unused for over a century.
Workers loaded limestone into the limekiln to begin the lime production process.
