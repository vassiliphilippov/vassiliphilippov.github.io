prayer

noun
act of addressing a deity
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛr / (AmE), / ˈpreə / (BrE)
the act of speaking to God or another deity, especially to ask for help or give thanks

Examples

Daily prayer
Every morning, he begins his day with prayer and meditation.
She closed her eyes and offered a silent prayer for her family's safety.
The congregation bowed their heads in prayer during the ceremony.
He began his day with a quiet prayer for guidance and strength.
Many people find comfort in offering a prayer during difficult times.
She wrote a prayer to express her gratitude after the good news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: supplication - the action of asking or begging for something earnestly or humbly
