gold-worker

noun
artisan who works with gold
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊld ˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊld ˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
an artisan who crafts jewelry or other objects out of gold

Examples

The gold-worker crafts jewelry
Every piece crafted by the gold-worker displayed exceptional attention to detail.
She apprenticed with a renowned gold-worker to master the art of making intricate bracelets.
The ancient city was famous for the skill of its gold-workers.
