Synonyms

  • admonishing
    6.5 0.00/m
    admonishing is direct and meant to correct someone's behavior, while admonitory describes a warning or mild reprimand.
  • reproving
    7.4 0.00/m
    reproving expresses clear disapproval and censure toward a person, while admonitory describes a warning or cautionary remark.
  • monitory
    9.6 0.00/m
    monitory serves to warn or give a cautionary notice in formal contexts, while admonitory implies a rebuke or warning tone.
  • cautionary
    5.6 2.30/m

  • warning
