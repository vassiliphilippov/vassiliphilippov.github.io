ampullary

adjective
relating to an ampulla (anatomical dilation)
Pronunciation: / æmˈpʊləri / (AmE), / æmˈpʌləri / (BrE)
relating to or resembling an ampulla, especially pertaining to a duct, cavity, or anatomical structure having an ampulla-like dilation

Examples

Ampullary carcinoma
An ampullary carcinoma often affects the region where the bile duct and pancreatic duct join.
The endoscope reached the ampullary segment of the duct without difficulty.
The surgeon identified an ampullary tumor during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ampulla - a flask-shaped dilation, especially in anatomical structures
