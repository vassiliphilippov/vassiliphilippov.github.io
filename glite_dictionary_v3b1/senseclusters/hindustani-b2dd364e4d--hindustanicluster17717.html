Hindustani

noun
form of Hindi-Urdu spoken in Northern India
6.4 6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪnduˈstɑni / (AmE), / ˌhɪnduˈstɑːni / (BrE)
the form of Hindi-Urdu spoken chiefly in Northern India, especially around Delhi, serving as the basis for standard Hindi and Urdu

Examples

Hindustani language
Linguists often study Hindustani as the basis for both modern Hindi and Urdu.
Many Bollywood films are produced in Hindustani to appeal to a wider audience across India and Pakistan.
My grandmother speaks Hindustani, which makes it easy for her to communicate in both India and Pakistan.
