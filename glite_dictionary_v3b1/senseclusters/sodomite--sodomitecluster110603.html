sodomite

noun
person who engages in anal intercourse
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːdəˌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɒdəˌmaɪt / (BrE)
a person who engages in anal intercourse, especially a man who does so with another man; historically, also used as an insulting term

Examples

A sodomite man
He was unfairly labeled a sodomite by his critics in an era of intolerance.
In medieval texts, the term sodomite was often used in legal accusations.
The word sodomite has a long history of being used as a slur against gay men.
