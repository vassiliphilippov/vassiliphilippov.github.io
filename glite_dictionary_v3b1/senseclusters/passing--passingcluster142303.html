passing

noun
motion of one object past another
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋ / (BrE)
the motion of one object moving past or in relation to another, especially in scientific or astronomical contexts

Examples

Stellar passing event
Stellar passings can significantly alter the orbits of small celestial bodies.
The passing of a comet near Earth is a rare astronomical event.
The scientist studied the passing of meteors through the atmosphere.
