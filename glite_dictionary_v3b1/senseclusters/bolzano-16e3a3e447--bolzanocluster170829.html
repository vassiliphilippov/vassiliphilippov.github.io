Bolzano

proper noun
Italian city in Trentino-Alto Adige
5.3
Pronunciation: / bolˈtsɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / bɒlˈtsɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
A city in northern Italy, located in Trentino-Alto Adige near the Austrian border, known as a resort and for its Alpine scenery.

Examples

Bolzano city
Bolzano is famous for its blend of Italian and Austrian cultures, reflected in local architecture and cuisine.
Many tourists visit Bolzano each year to enjoy its stunning mountain views and charming old town.
The train from Verona to Bolzano offers travelers scenic views of the Alps along the route.
