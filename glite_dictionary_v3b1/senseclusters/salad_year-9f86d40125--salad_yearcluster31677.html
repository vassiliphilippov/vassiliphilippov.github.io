salad year

noun
a year of plentiful fresh produce
Pronunciation: / ˈsæləd jɪr / (AmE), / ˈsæləd jɪə / (BrE)
A year characterized by an abundance of fresh produce or garden vegetables.

Examples

A salad year
After a salad year, the family enjoyed making fresh meals every day.
Looking back, she remembered 2019 as a salad year, when the crops exceeded all expectations.
This has truly been a salad year for local gardeners.
