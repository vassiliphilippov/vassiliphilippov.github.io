plowwright

noun
person who makes or repairs plows
Pronunciation: / ˈplaʊˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈplaʊˌraɪt / (BrE)
a person who makes and repairs plows, especially as a traditional occupation

Examples

A plowwright makes plows

The village once relied on its skilled ploughwright for maintaining farming equipment.
