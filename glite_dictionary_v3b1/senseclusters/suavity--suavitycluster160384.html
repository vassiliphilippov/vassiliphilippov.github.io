suavity

noun
smooth and gracious manner
3.9 7.2
Pronunciation: /suː.ˈæv.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /swɑː.ˈvɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /suː.ˈæv.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /swɒ.ˈvɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
the quality of being smooth, gracious, and polite in manner, often in a way that seems charming or ingratiating
