cookshop

noun
kitchenware store
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊkˌʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈkʊkˌʃɒp / (BrE)
a shop that sells cooking utensils, cookware, and other kitchen equipment

Examples

Cookshop for pans
She bought a cast-iron pan at the old cookshop on the high street yesterday.
The cookshop downtown has a wide selection of baking tools and pots.
If you need a new frying pan, the local cookshop is the best place to look.
