Mary Pickford

proper noun
Canadian-born American silent film actress
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛri ˈpɪkfərd / (AmE), / ˈmɛːri ˈpɪkfəd / (BrE)
(1893–1979) A Canadian-born American film actress best known for her roles in silent films and as a pioneering figure in early Hollywood.

Examples

Mary Pickford silent films
Mary Pickford was regarded as one of the most influential actresses of the silent film era.
