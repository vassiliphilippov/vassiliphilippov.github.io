louvered window

noun
window with adjustable glass slats
5.6 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàluÀêv…öd Ààw…™ndo ä / (AmE), / ÀàluÀêv…ôd Ààw…™nd…ô ä / (BrE)
A window fitted with horizontally arranged, angled glass slats (louvers) that can be adjusted to control ventilation and light.

Examples

A louvered window
A louvered window allows air to flow in even when it's raining outside.
She cleaned each glass slat in the louvered window to let in more light.
We decided to install a louvered window in the bathroom for better ventilation.

Synonyms

  • jalousie
    9.9 0.03/m
    jalousie is a common name for a window with adjustable slats, while louvered window is a more descriptive technical term.
