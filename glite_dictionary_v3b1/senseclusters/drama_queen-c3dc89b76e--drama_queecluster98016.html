drama queen

noun
person who overreacts or exaggerates emotions
1.0 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈdræ.mə kwiːn/ /; / /ˈdrɑː.mə kwiːn/ (AmE), /ˈdrɑː.mə kwiːn/ (BrE)
