fulfilled

adjective
completed or fully achieved
2.5
Pronunciation: / fʊlˈfɪld / (AmE), / fʊlˈfɪld / (BrE)
Having been brought to completion or achieved to the highest standard; fully realized or accomplished.

Examples

Fulfilled dreams
His ambitions were fulfilled when he published his first novel.
The promise was fulfilled to perfection, exceeding everyone's expectations.
Years of training left her feeling fulfilled after finally winning the gold medal.
