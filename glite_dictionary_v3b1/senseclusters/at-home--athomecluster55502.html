at-home

adjective
designed for home use
Pronunciation: / æt hoʊm / (AmE), / æt həʊm / (BrE)
(attributive) designed for use in the home; suitable for domestic or home-based use rather than professional or institutional settings.

Examples

An at-home test
The new at-home testing kit allowed patients to check their blood sugar without visiting the clinic.
They offer an at-home meal-prep service that delivers ingredients and recipes every Sunday morning.
Our company developed an at-home version of the software suited for small business owners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domestic - relating to the home or household
  • synonym: home-based - intended to be done or used at home rather than at a workplace or institution
