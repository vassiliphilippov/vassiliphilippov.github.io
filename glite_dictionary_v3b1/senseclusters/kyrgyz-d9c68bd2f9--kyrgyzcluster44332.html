Kyrgyz

proper noun
language of Kyrgyzstan
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪrɡɪz / (AmE), / ˈkɪːɡɪz / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken by the Kyrgyz people and one of the official languages of Kyrgyzstan

Examples

Kyrgyz language
She learned Kyrgyz to communicate with her grandparents in Bishkek.
The official documents in Kyrgyzstan are available in both Russian and Kyrgyz.
Many traditional songs are performed in Kyrgyz at cultural festivals.
