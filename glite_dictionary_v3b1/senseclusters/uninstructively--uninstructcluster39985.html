uninstructively

adverb
in an uninformative manner
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.ɪnˈstrʌk.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈstrʌk.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In an uninformative or unhelpful manner; without providing instruction or valuable information.

Examples

Answered uninstructively
He answered the journalist's questions uninstructively, leaving important issues unaddressed.
The manual was written so uninstructively that no one could operate the device properly.
The teacher spoke uninstructively, giving little guidance to the confused students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: uninformatively - in an uninformative manner
