briefing

noun
detailed instructions or information session
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbriːfɪŋ / (BrE)
a meeting or session at which detailed instructions or information are given, especially before undertaking a task or operation

Examples

A briefing meeting
After the security briefing, all employees knew what to do in an emergency.
The general held a morning briefing to outline the day's objectives.
The team attended a briefing before setting out on the rescue mission.

Related Terms

  • antonym: debriefing - a meeting to review or discuss an operation after it has occurred
  • synonym: instruction - detailed direction or order given to someone
