Asiatic cockroach

noun
species of cockroach originally from Asia
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪʒiˈætɪk ˈkɑːkˌroʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˌeɪʃiˈatɪk ˈkɒkˌrəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a species of dark brown cockroach, originally from Asia, now found in many parts of the world

Examples

The Asiatic cockroach species
Many homes in the southern United States have seen an increase in Asiatic cockroach infestations.
The Asiatic cockroach can be mistaken for the German cockroach due to their similar appearance.
Unlike other species, the Asiatic cockroach is often attracted to lights and can fly considerable distances.
