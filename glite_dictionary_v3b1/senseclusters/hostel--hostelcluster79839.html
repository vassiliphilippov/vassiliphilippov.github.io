hostel

noun
inexpensive lodging for travelers, often youths
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːstl̩ /; / ˈhɑːstəl / (AmE), / ˈhɒstl̩ / (BrE)
a building or place that provides cheap accommodation for travelers or students, often with shared rooms and facilities

Examples

A hostel for students
Many students choose to live in a hostel because it is affordable and sociable.
The hostel offers basic facilities but is safe and clean for travelers.
We stayed in a small hostel near the city center during our trip to Berlin.
During her backpacking trip across Europe, Maria stayed in a different hostel every night.
He chose a budget hostel instead of an expensive hotel for his weekend getaway.
The city center hostel offers free breakfast and Wi-Fi to all its guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dormitory - a large bedroom for a number of people in a school or institution
  • degree higher: hotel - an establishment providing more expensive accommodation and services for travelers and tourists
