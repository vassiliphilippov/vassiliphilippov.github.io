exacerbating

verb
making worse or more severe
8.6
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzæsərˌbeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɑːsəˌbeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Making a problem, situation, or feeling worse or more severe.

Examples

To exacerbate a problem
Pollution is exacerbating the effects of climate change in urban areas.
She realized that arguing further was only exacerbating the situation.
The lack of communication is exacerbating the conflict between the two teams.
