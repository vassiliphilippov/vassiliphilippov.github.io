featherweight

noun
boxer in the 126–139 lbs weight class
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛðɚˌweɪt /; / ˈfɛðərˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɛðəˌweɪt /; / ˈfɛðəweɪt / (BrE)
