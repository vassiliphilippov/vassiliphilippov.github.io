promotability

noun
suitability for promotion
Pronunciation: / prəˌmoʊtəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / prəˌməʊtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or degree to which something is suitable for promotion or likely to be successfully marketed

Examples

Promotability of a product
The marketing team discussed the promotability of the new product before launching the campaign.
High promotability can make a movie more attractive to investors and distributors.
They assessed the promotability of each idea to decide which one to develop further.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appeal - the quality of being attractive or interesting
  • synonym: marketability - the quality of being easy to sell or promote
  • antonym: unsuitability - the quality of not being appropriate or fit for a purpose
