Benjamin bush

noun
aromatic shrub of eastern US
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛn.dʒə.mɪn bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɛn.dʒə.mɪn bʊʃ / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States, known for its highly aromatic leaves and bark, yellow flowers, and scarlet or yellow berries.

Examples

Benjamin bush with berries
