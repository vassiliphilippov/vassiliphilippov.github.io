kitchen sink

noun
basin for washing in a kitchen
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtʃən sɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃɪn sɪŋk / (BrE)
A large basin with a water supply, installed in a kitchen and used for washing dishes and other kitchen tasks.

Examples

Dirty kitchen sink
Dishes piled up in the kitchen sink after the party.
She rinsed the vegetables in the kitchen sink before preparing dinner.
The plumber came to fix the leaking kitchen sink yesterday.
