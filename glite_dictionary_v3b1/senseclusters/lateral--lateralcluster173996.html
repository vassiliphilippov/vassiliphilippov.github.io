lateral

verb
to pass sideways
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlæt̬ərəl / (AmE), / ˈlætərəl / (BrE)
to throw or pass a ball sideways or backward, especially in American football

Examples

To lateral the ball
During the final play, the running back lateraled the ball to avoid being tackled.
The coach taught the players how to lateral effectively during practice.
He quickly lateraled to his teammate, keeping the play alive.
