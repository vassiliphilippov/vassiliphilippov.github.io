wheeler-dealer

noun
shrewd or unscrupulous dealmaker
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlɚ ˌdiːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈwiːlə ˌdiːlə / (BrE)
a person who is skilled at making deals, often using shrewd or sometimes dishonest methods

Examples

A wheeler-dealer person
Many believed the wheeler-dealer skated close to the edge of legality.
No one was surprised when the wheeler-dealer managed to close the complicated merger within days.
She has a reputation as a wheeler-dealer who can get almost anything done in the industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: operator - a person who is adept at making successful deals, often in a shrewd manner
  • antonym: straight shooter - a person who is honest and direct in business
