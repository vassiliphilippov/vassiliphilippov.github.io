plethysmographic

adjective
measured by plethysmography
Pronunciation: / plɛθˌɪzməˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / plɛθˌɪzməˈɡrafɪk / (BrE)
relating to or measured by plethysmography, a technique for measuring changes in volume within an organ or body part

Examples

The plethysmographic readings
The plethysmographic readings indicated a significant change in the patient's blood flow.
Researchers used plethysmographic equipment to monitor lung volume during the experiment.
A plethysmographic test can help diagnose certain circulatory disorders.
