sawdusty

adjective
like sawdust
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːˌdʌsti / (AmE), / ˈsɔːˌdʌsti / (BrE)
having a texture, smell, or quality similar to sawdust

Examples

Sawdusty smell
The old attic had a sawdusty smell that reminded me of a carpenter's workshop.
After moving the lumber, his clothes felt rough and sawdusty to the touch.
The air in the shed became thick and sawdusty after hours of sawing wood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - free from dirt or unwanted substances
  • synonym: dusty - covered with or resembling dust
