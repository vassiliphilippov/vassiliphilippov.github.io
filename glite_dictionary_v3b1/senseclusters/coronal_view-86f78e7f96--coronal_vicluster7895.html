coronal view

noun
anatomical view dividing body into front and back
Pronunciation: / kəˈroʊ.nəl vjuː / (AmE), / kəˈrəʊ.nəl vjuː / (BrE)
an anatomical perspective based on a vertical plane dividing the body into front (anterior) and back (posterior) sections

Examples

Coronal view of body
By examining the skull from the coronal view, doctors can better visualize the brain's frontal lobe.
Diagrams in the textbook clearly show organs as they appear in the coronal view.
The MRI images were analyzed in the coronal view to assess any abnormalities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sagittal view - an anatomical plane dividing the body into left and right sections
  • synonym: transverse view - an anatomical plane dividing the body into upper and lower sections
