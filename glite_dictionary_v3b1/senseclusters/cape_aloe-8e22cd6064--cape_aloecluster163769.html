cape aloe

noun
South African prickly succulent plant

A much-branched South African succulent plant (Aloe ferox) with reddish, prickly leaves, often used for medicinal purposes.

Examples

Cape aloe plant
Cape aloe is cultivated both for its ornamental value and its medicinal uses.
Some herbal remedies include extracts from cape aloe leaves.
The bitter sap of cape aloe is thought to have potent laxative properties.
