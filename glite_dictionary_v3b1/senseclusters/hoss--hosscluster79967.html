hoss

noun
informal male address
Pronunciation: / hɑs / (AmE), / hɒs / (BrE)
an informal way to address a man, meaning friend or buddy, especially in the southern United States

Examples

Hey hoss!
Hey hoss, can you help me move this sofa this afternoon?
Thanks for the advice, hoss, I really appreciate it.
You did a great job fixing the car, hoss!

Related Terms

  • synonym: buddy - an informal term for a friend
  • synonym: pal - an informal word for a close friend
  • antonym: stranger - a person you do not know
