sun god

noun
deity representing the sun
Pronunciation: / sʌn ɡɑd / (AmE), / sʌn ɡɒd / (BrE)
a god who represents or is associated with the sun, especially in mythology or religion

Examples

The sun god Ra
In ancient Egypt, Ra was considered the most important sun god.
Many ancient cultures had their own version of a sun god in their mythology.
The Aztecs worshipped a sun god to honor the power of the sun.

Related Terms

  • antonym: moon god - a god associated with the moon
  • synonym: solar deity - a deity representing the sun
