unrequitedly

adverb
without being returned
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈkwaɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈkwaɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is not returned or reciprocated, especially referring to feelings such as love or affection

Examples

To love unrequitedly
He admired her unrequitedly, never revealing his true feelings.
For years, she cared unrequitedly for someone who barely noticed her.
The poet wrote unrequitedly about his secret love in many of his verses.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mutually - in a way that is shared by two or more parties
  • synonym: unreciprocated - not returned or given back in kind
