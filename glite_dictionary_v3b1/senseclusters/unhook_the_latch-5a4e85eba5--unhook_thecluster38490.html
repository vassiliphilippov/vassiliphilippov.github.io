unhook the latch

verb
release a latch
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhʊk ðə lætʃ / (AmE), / ʌnˈhʊk ðə lætʃ / (BrE)
to release or disengage a latch by removing its hook or fastening

Examples

She carefully unhooked the latch on the window to let in some fresh air.
