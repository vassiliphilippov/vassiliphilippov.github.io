primary amenorrhea

noun
absence of menstruation by age 18
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪˌmɛːri əˌmɛnəˈriːə / (AmE), / ˈpraɪməri əˌmɛnəˈriːə / (BrE)
a condition in which menstruation has not started by the age of 18

Examples

Primary amenorrhea diagnosis
A diagnosis of primary amenorrhea is made when a girl has not started menstruating by the age of 18.
Doctors used hormonal tests to determine the cause of her primary amenorrhea.
She visited an endocrinologist due to concerns about primary amenorrhea and delayed puberty.
