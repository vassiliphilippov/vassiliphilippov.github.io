sadism

noun
sexual pleasure from inflicting pain
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪ.dɪ.zəm / (AmE), / ˈseɪ.dɪ.zəm / (BrE)
sexual pleasure derived from inflicting pain or suffering, either physical or psychological, on others.

Examples

Sadism in sex
He was disturbed by her open discussion of sadism as a source of excitement.
In some cases, sadism and masochism are present together in people's fantasies.
The film explored the sadism of the character who enjoyed tormenting others.
