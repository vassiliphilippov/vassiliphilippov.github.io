unendangered

adjective
not at risk
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈdeɪndʒərd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈdeɪndʒəd / (BrE)
not at risk of extinction, harm, or disappearance; safe or secure from danger.

Examples

Unendangered species
The panda is no longer considered unendangered because its population has declined.
Many common birds are classified as unendangered due to their stable numbers worldwide.
Thanks to conservation efforts, the species is now listed as unendangered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: safe - protected from danger or harm
  • synonym: secure - free from threat or danger
