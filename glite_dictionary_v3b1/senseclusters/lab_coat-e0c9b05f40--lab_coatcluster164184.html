lab coat

noun
protective coat worn in laboratories
1.0
Pronunciation: / læb koʊt / (AmE), / læb kəʊt / (BrE)
A light, usually white, coat worn over clothing to protect it from spills, chemicals, or other substances in a laboratory environment.

Examples

A lab coat
All students must wear a lab coat during chemistry experiments for safety.
She spilled acid on her lab coat, but her clothes were unharmed.
The scientist hung his lab coat on the wall before leaving the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laboratory gown - a garment worn to protect clothing in a laboratory
