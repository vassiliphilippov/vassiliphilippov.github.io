prima

adjective
most important performer or role
6.6 8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːmə / (AmE), / ˈpriːmə / (BrE)
used before a noun to indicate the most important performer or principal role, especially in artistic or theatrical contexts.

Examples

The prima ballerina
His ambition was always to be the prima actor on stage, never content with supporting roles.
She was cast in the prima role of the production, a position coveted by many dancers.
The prima violinist delivered a breathtaking solo during the concert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leading - most important or prominent in a group or activity
  • synonym: principal - first in order of importance; main
