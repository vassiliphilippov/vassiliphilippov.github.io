sleepless

adjective
unable to sleep; awake at night
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsliː.pləs/ (AmE), /ˈsliː.pləs/ (BrE)
unable to sleep or having difficulty sleeping; marked by wakefulness during usual sleeping hours.

Examples

Sleepless night
After drinking too much coffee, I spent a sleepless night staring at the ceiling.
He wrote poetry during his long, sleepless hours until morning.
The worries of the coming exam left her feeling sleepless and anxious.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rested - having had enough sleep or rest
  • synonym: restless - unable to rest, relax, or remain still
  • synonym: wakeful - remaining awake; not sleeping
