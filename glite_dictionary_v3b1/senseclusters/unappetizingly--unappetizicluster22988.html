unappetizingly

adverb
in an unappealing way
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈæpɪˌtaɪzɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈæpɪˌtaɪzɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is not appealing or attractive, especially to eat or taste

Examples

Look unappetizingly dull
The leftovers sat unappetizingly on the plate, untouched by anyone at the table.
The soup was served unappetizingly cold, making it difficult to enjoy.
The vegetables looked unappetizingly overcooked and gray on the buffet.
The vegetables were unappetizingly overcooked and lost their color.
The room was unappetizingly decorated, making guests feel uncomfortable.
His presentation was unappetizingly disorganized, so the audience quickly lost interest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: appetizingly - in a way that looks or smells appealing to eat
  • antonym: attractively - in a way that is appealing or pleasing
  • synonym: unpleasantly - in a way that is not enjoyable or pleasing
