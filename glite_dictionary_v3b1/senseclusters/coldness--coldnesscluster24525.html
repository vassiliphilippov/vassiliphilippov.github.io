coldness

noun
lack of warmth or affection
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊld.nəs / (AmE), / ˈkəʊld.nəs / (BrE)
an absence of warmth or friendliness in manner, showing a lack of affection or enthusiasm

Examples

Coldness in her voice
He was surprised by the coldness in her reply to his invitation.
Her coldness towards her colleagues was obvious at the meeting.
There was a noticeable coldness between the two old friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aloofness - a state of being emotionally distant or reserved
  • antonym: warmth - friendliness and affection in manner
