blast

verb
shrivel or wither (plants)
Pronunciation: / blæst / (AmE), / blɑːst / (BrE)
(of a plant) to shrivel, wither, or mature imperfectly, often due to disease, drought, or other damaging conditions.

Examples

Leaves blast early
A severe drought can cause the leaves to blast early in the season.
If the crops blast before harvest, the yield will be poor.
The flowers have blasted, turning brown before they fully opened.
