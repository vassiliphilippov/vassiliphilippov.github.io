banquet song

noun
song sung at a banquet
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋkwət sɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈbaŋkwɪt sɒŋ / (BrE)
a song, often improvised, sung by guests during a banquet

Examples

A banquet song sung
At the end of the evening, the guests joined in a cheerful banquet song.
He composed a witty banquet song for his friend's wedding feast.
The tradition of singing a banquet song dates back centuries in some cultures.
