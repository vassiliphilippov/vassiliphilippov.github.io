personal expense

noun
cost of personal or family living
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝsənl ɪkˈspɛns / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl ɪkˈspɛns / (BrE)
an amount of money spent on personal or family living, not related to business or professional costs

Examples

Personal expenses records
Employees must keep records of their personal expenses in case they are audited.
She reduced her personal expenses to save more for her holiday trip.
Some personal expenses can be deducted from your taxable income depending on local laws.
