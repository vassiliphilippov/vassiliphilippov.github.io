precipitable

adjective
able to become precipitation
8.8
Pronunciation: / prɪˈsɪpɪtəbəl / (AmE), / prɪˈsɪpɪtəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being condensed from the atmosphere and falling as precipitation such as rain, snow, or sleet

Examples

Precipitable water vapor
Meteorologists measured the amount of precipitable water in the atmosphere before the storm.
High precipitable moisture levels often lead to heavy rainfall events.
The region experienced unusually low precipitable water, resulting in a dry season.

Related Terms
