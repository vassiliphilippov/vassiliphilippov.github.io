humaneness

noun
compassion; consideration for others
4.2
Pronunciation: / hjuˈmeɪn.nəs / (AmE), / hjuːˈmeɪn.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of showing compassion, kindness, or consideration for others, especially in treating people or animals

Examples

Acts of humaneness
Acts of humaneness can make a significant difference in the lives of vulnerable individuals.
Her humaneness was evident in the way she treated the injured stray dog.
The judge's humaneness influenced his decision to recommend rehabilitation instead of prison.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cruelty - callous indifference to or pleasure in causing pain and suffering
  • synonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate
