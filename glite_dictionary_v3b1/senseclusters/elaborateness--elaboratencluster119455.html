elaborateness

noun
the quality of being detailed or intricate
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlæbərət.nəs /; / ɪˌlæbərətnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈlæb(ə)rət.nəs /; / ɪˌlæbərətnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being intricate, detailed, or carefully worked out

Examples

The elaborateness of design
She admired the elaborateness of the clock's design, with its intricate gears and carvings.
The elaborateness of his explanation made the topic seem more complicated than it was.
The elaborateness of the wedding decorations amazed all the guests.
He admired the elaborateness of her wedding gown, covered in detailed lace and tiny pearls.
Some critics felt that the elaborateness of the design overwhelmed its original simplicity.
The elaborateness of the palace's interior was breathtaking, with gold accents and intricate patterns everywhere.
