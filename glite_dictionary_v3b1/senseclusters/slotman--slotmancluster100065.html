slotman

noun
casino slot operator
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑtmæn / (AmE), / ˈslɒtmæn / (BrE)
a person whose job is to operate, maintain, or supervise slot machines in a casino

Examples

The slotman fixes machines
The slotman quickly fixed the malfunctioning slot machine on the casino floor.
If you have a problem with a machine, ask the slotman for help.
The slotman made regular rounds to ensure all the slot machines were working properly.
