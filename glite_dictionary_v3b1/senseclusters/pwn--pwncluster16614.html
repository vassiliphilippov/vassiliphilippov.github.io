pwn

verb
to defeat completely
Pronunciation: / poʊn / (AmE), / pəʊn / (BrE)
to defeat or dominate someone completely, especially in online gaming or competitions

Examples

To pwn the enemy team
She pwned the other team in the online tournament last night.
If you practice more, you can pwn your friends at this game.
He bragged about how he pwned everyone in the final round.

  • antonym: lose - to be defeated in a contest or competition
