rattly

adjective
making a harsh sound
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrætli / (AmE), / ˈrætli / (BrE)
making a rattling noise, usually because parts are loose or shaking

Examples

A rattly noise
The old car made a rattly noise every time it went over a bump.
After his cold, his cough sounded especially rattly in the quiet room.
She spoke in a rattly voice after cheering loudly at the concert.
The old bicycle was so rattly that everyone could hear me coming down the street.
She tried to fix the rattly window, but it still made noise when the wind blew.
After years of use, the washing machine became quite rattly during the spin cycle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noisy - making a lot of noise
  • antonym: quiet - making little or no noise
  • synonym: raspy - having a rough or harsh sound
  • antonym: silent - making no sound at all
  • antonym: smooth - having a soft or even sound
