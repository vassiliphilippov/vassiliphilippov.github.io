outcropping

noun
visible part of a rock formation
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌkrɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌkrɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
a part of a rock formation that is visible above the surface of the surrounding land

Examples

Rock outcropping
A small outcropping jutted out from the hillside above the river.
Fossils were discovered embedded in a limestone outcropping near the village.
The hikers stopped to examine an outcropping of ancient granite along the trail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outcrop - a visible exposure of bedrock or ancient superficial deposits on the surface of the Earth
