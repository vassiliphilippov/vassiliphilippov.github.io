snow house

noun
shelter made of snow
3.4
Pronunciation: /snoʊ haʊs/ (AmE), /snəʊ haʊs/ (BrE)
a shelter or small house constructed from blocks of snow, typically built for fun or protection from the cold

Examples

A snow house shelter
After the heavy snowfall, the children spent hours building a snow house in the park.
The explorers constructed a temporary snow house to protect themselves from the freezing wind.
Each winter, the festival features a giant snow house that visitors can walk through.
