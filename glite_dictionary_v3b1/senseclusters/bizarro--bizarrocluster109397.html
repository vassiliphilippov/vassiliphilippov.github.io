bizarro

noun
opposite counterpart
Pronunciation: / bɪˈzɑːroʊ / (AmE), / bɪˈzɑːrəʊ / (BrE)
an inverted, distorted, or opposite version of someone or something, often used humorously or as a parody

Examples

Bizarro version

The comedian created a bizarro world where everything was the opposite of reality.
