hackwork

noun
uninspired, formulaic professional work
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhækˌwɜrk /; / ˈhækˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈhækˌwɜːk / (BrE)
writing, art, or other professional work produced quickly or mechanically, usually lacking creativity or quality
