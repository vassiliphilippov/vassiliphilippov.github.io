cocona

noun
South American fruit
9.9
Pronunciation: / koʊˈkoʊnə / (AmE), / kəˈkəʊnə / (BrE)
a tropical fruit native to South America, especially the Amazon region, also known as Solanum sessiliflorum

Examples

Fresh cocona fruit
The cocona is often used in Amazonian cuisine for its tangy flavor.
Farmers in Peru grow cocona alongside other tropical fruits.
You can make a refreshing juice from fresh cocona during the hot season.
