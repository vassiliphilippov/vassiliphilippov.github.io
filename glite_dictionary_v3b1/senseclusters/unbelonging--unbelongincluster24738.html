unbelonging

adjective
feeling out of place
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnbɪˈlɔŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnbɪˈlɒŋɪŋ / (BrE)
not feeling accepted or part of a group, place, or community

Examples

An unbelonging student
After moving to a new school, he often felt unbelonging among his classmates.
The sense of unbelonging made her hesitant to join any clubs or activities.
Many immigrants experience feelings of unbelonging when adapting to a different culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alienated - feeling isolated or estranged from others
  • antonym: included - feeling accepted as part of a group
