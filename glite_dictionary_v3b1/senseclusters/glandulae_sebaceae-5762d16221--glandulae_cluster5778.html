glandulae sebaceae

noun
sebaceous glands of the skin
Pronunciation: / ɡlænˈdjuːli siːˈbeɪsiː / (AmE), / ɡlænˈdjuːli siːˈbeɪsiː / (BrE)
The sebaceous glands of the skin, which secrete sebum to lubricate hair and skin, usually opening into hair follicles.

Examples

Glandulae sebaceae of skin
Excessive activity of the glandulae sebaceae may cause oily skin.
Inflammation of the glandulae sebaceae can result in acne.
The glandulae sebaceae help protect the skin by secreting oily substances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sebaceous gland - a small gland in the skin which secretes sebum
