coup de main

noun
sudden surprise attack
Pronunciation: / ku də ˈmeɪn / (AmE), / kuː də ˈmæ̃ / (BrE)
a sudden, surprise attack made without warning, especially used in a military context

Examples

A coup de main attack
Historical records describe a daring coup de main on the fortress at dawn.
The general orchestrated a coup de main that caught the enemy completely off guard.
The victory was secured by a swift coup de main rather than a prolonged siege.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blitzkrieg - a swift, sudden military offensive
  • antonym: frontal assault - a direct attack on a position
