operable

adjective
treatable by surgery
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɑpərəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɒpərəbəl / (BrE)
(of a disease) able to be treated or removed by surgical operation.

Examples

Operable tumor
Doctors explained that her condition was operable if detected early.
Fortunately, the cancer is operable and surgery is scheduled for next week.
The tumor was found to be operable, giving hope to the patient.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inoperable - not able to be treated or removed by surgery
