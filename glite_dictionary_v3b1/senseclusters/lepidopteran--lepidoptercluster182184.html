lepidopteran

noun
insect order including butterflies and moths
6.4 7.7
Pronunciation: /lep.ɪ.ˈdɑːp.tər.ən/ /; / /ləp.ɪ.ˈdɑːp.tər.ən/ (AmE), /lep.ɪ.ˈdɒp.tə.rən/ /; / /ləp.ɪ.ˈdɒp.tə.rən/ (BrE)
An insect of the order Lepidoptera, characterized by having four wings usually covered with minute scales; includes butterflies and moths.
