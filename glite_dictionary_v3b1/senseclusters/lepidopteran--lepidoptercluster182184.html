lepidopteran

noun
insect order including butterflies and moths
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛpɪˈdɑptərən / (AmE), / ˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərən / (BrE)
An insect of the order Lepidoptera, characterized by having four wings usually covered with minute scales; includes butterflies and moths.

Examples

A lepidopteran insect
A lepidopteran typically undergoes metamorphosis during its life cycle.
Many lepidopterans are important pollinators in various ecosystems.
The scientist observed a rare species of lepidopteran in the rainforest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butterfly - an insect with large, brightly colored wings, a type of lepidopteran
  • synonym: moth - an insect similar to a butterfly, also a lepidopteran
