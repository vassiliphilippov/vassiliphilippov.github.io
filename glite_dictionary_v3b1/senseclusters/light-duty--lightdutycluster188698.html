light-duty

adjective
not designed for heavy work
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌduːti / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌdjuːti / (BrE)
designed for tasks that do not require much strength or are not demanding; not intended for heavy or intensive use.

Examples

A light-duty truck
A light-duty truck may not be suitable for moving large construction materials.
This light-duty cleaner is perfect for everyday household spills and messes.
We used a light-duty extension cord for the lamp in the bedroom.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heavy-duty - designed for heavy or demanding work
