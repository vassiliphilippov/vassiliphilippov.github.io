dream up

verb
to invent or imagine something
Pronunciation: / drim ʌp / (AmE), / driːm ʌp / (BrE)

Examples

To dream up ideas
He managed to dream up a creative excuse at the last minute.
The team worked all night to dream up a solution to the problem.
Who would have dreamed up such a bizarre idea for a movie?
