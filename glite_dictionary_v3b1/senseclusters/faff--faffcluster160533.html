faff

verb
to waste time
9.9
Pronunciation: / fæf / (AmE), / fæf / (BrE)
to spend time doing unimportant or unnecessary things instead of what needs to be done

Examples

To faff about tasks
He tends to faff about in the mornings instead of getting ready quickly.
We wasted an hour faffing around before finally starting the project.
Stop faffing and help me set the table, please.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dawdle - to waste time or move slowly
  • antonym: focus - to concentrate on what needs to be done
  • synonym: procrastinate - to delay doing something that needs to be done
