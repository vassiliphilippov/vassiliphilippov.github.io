discordancy

noun
geological misalignment
9.9
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈskɔːr.dən.siː/ /; / /dɪs.ˈkɔːr.dən.siː/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈskɔː.dən.siː/ /; / /dɪs.ˈkɔː.dən.siː/ (BrE)
a lack of alignment or conformity between rock layers or geological structures, such as when an intrusion or fault cuts across bedding planes

Examples

Geological discordancy
The discordancy between the granite intrusion and the surrounding sedimentary rocks was clearly visible in the outcrop.
Geologists studied the discordancy to understand the sequence of geological events in the region.
Mapping the discordancy helped the team identify ancient fault lines beneath the surface.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conformity - alignment or agreement of rock layers or structures
  • synonym: unconformity - a surface between rock layers representing a gap in the geological record
