wainscoting

noun
wooden panels for walls
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪnskətɪŋ, ˈweɪnskoʊtɪŋ /; / ˈweɪnˌskoʊtɪŋ /; / ˈweɪnskətɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪnskətɪŋ, ˈweɪnskəʊtɪŋ /; / ˈweɪnskətɪŋ / (BrE)
