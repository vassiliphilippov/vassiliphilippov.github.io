desk phone

noun
telephone set for desk or table
Pronunciation: / dɛsk foʊn / (AmE), / dɛsk fəʊn / (BrE)
a telephone set designed to sit on a desk or table, typically featuring a handset and keypad

Examples

A desk phone set
He reached over and picked up the desk phone to call his assistant.
Her desk phone rang several times before she answered it.
Many offices are replacing traditional desk phones with internet-based communication systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: landline phone - a telephone connected by wires to a network
  • antonym: mobile phone - a portable telephone using cellular network
