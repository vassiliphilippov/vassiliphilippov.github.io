wantonly

adverb
in a cruel or reckless manner
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːntənli / (AmE), / ˈwɒntənli / (BrE)
in a deliberate and unrestrained manner, often with cruelty or without justification

Examples

Wantonly destroying property
He was accused of acting wantonly when he broke the windows for no reason.
The ancient statues were wantonly defaced during the invasion.
The soldiers destroyed the village wantonly, leaving nothing behind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mercifully - in a compassionate or forgiving manner
  • synonym: recklessly - without regard for consequences
