writing desk

noun
desk for writing
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪ.t̬ɪŋ ˌdɛsk /; / ˈraɪtɪŋ dɛsk / (AmE), / ˈraɪ.tɪŋ ˌdɛsk /; / ˈraɪtɪŋ dɛsk / (BrE)
a desk or portable case with a surface for writing, often with drawers or compartments for stationery
