passaggio

noun
musical transition
9.8
Pronunciation: / pəˈsɑːdʒioʊ / (AmE), / paˈsadʒioʊ / (BrE)
a passage or transition in a piece of music, especially the transition between vocal registers in singing

Examples

The passaggio in singing
The singer practiced the passaggio to improve her vocal transitions.
Mastering the passaggio is essential for classical vocalists.
She struggled with the passaggio when moving from her chest voice to her head voice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: break - the point where a singer's voice shifts between registers
  • synonym: transition - a change from one state or condition to another
