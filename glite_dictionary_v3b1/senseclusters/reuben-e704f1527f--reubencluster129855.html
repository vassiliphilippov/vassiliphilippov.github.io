Reuben

proper noun
eldest son of Jacob in the Old Testament
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈruː.bən/ (AmE), /ˈruː.bən/ (BrE)
(In the Old Testament) the eldest son of Jacob and the forefather of one of the twelve tribes of Israel.

Examples

Biblical Reuben
Reuben was the firstborn son of Jacob and Leah in the Hebrew Bible.
According to tradition, the tribe of Reuben settled east of the Jordan River.
In many biblical stories, Reuben is portrayed as a protector of his younger brother Joseph.
