amperometric

adjective
measuring electric current
Pronunciation: / ˌæmpəroʊˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌæmpərəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of electric current, especially in chemical or biological analysis

Amperometric measurement method

An amperometric sensor can provide rapid results in chemical detection.
