overmastering

noun
complete domination
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈmæstərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈmɑːstərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of gaining complete control or dominance over someone or something

Examples

Overmastering of emotions
His overmastering of fear gave him the confidence to speak to the large crowd.
The general's overmastering of the enemy forces led to a swift victory.
She struggled against the overmastering influence of her strict upbringing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domination - the exercise of control or influence over someone or something
  • synonym: subjugation - the act of bringing someone or something under control
  • antonym: submission - the act of accepting the control of someone else
