onshore

adjective
domestic; within one's country
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːn.ʃɔːr/ /; / /ˈɑːn.ʃoʊr/ (AmE), /ˈɒn.ʃɔː/ (BrE)
Located, produced, or operated within one's own country; domestic, especially of services, manufacturing, or financial activities, as opposed to offshore.

Examples

Onshore production
The government offered incentives for onshore production to boost local employment and investment.
After moving its call centers onshore, the company reported faster response times and higher customer satisfaction.
Many manufacturers are bringing onshore assembly lines back to reduce shipping costs and supply delays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domestic - relating to or originating within a person's own country
  • antonym: offshore - located or carried out in a different country, often to reduce costs or taxes
