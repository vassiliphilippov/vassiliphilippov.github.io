bleachers

noun
outdoor tiered seating at sports venues
8.4 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈbliː.tʃərz/ (AmE), /ˈbliː.tʃəz/ (BrE)
Open, tiered rows of seats, usually outdoors and without a roof, for spectators at sporting events or other public gatherings.

Examples

Outdoor bleachers seats
After hours in the bleachers, everyone was sunburned but happy.
The students cheered loudly from the bleachers during the game.
We found seats high up in the bleachers to get a better view of the field.
