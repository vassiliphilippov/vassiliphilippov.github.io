dullness

noun
slowness of understanding
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌlnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʌlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being slow to understand, or lacking quickness of mind

Examples

Mental dullness
Despite his dullness, he tried hard to keep up with his peers.
Her apparent dullness made the instructions difficult to follow.
The teacher became frustrated by the students' dullness during the lesson.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sharpness - the quality of being quick to notice or understand things
  • synonym: stupidity - lack of intelligence or quickness of mind
