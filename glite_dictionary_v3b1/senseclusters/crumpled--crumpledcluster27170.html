crumpled

adjective
bent or crushed into folds or creases
6.5 3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌmpəld / (AmE), / ˈkrʌmpəld / (BrE)
(of a material, especially metal, paper, or fabric) pressed or crushed into creases or folds; bent or twisted out of shape.

Examples

A crumpled piece of paper
She handed me a crumpled piece of paper she had found in her pocket.
The crumpled metal beams showed how severe the collision was.
The hood of the car was crumpled after the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creased - marked by lines, folds, or wrinkles
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface, free from folds or indentations
  • synonym: wrinkled - having small lines or folds, typically as a result of being crushed or folded
