trimodality

noun
three treatment methods
Pronunciation: / traɪmoʊˈdæləti / (AmE), / traɪməʊˈdælɪti / (BrE)
the use or presence of three different modes or methods, especially in medical treatment

Examples

Trimodality cancer treatment
The doctor recommended a trimodality approach combining surgery, chemotherapy, and radiation.
Recent studies show that trimodality therapy can improve survival rates in certain cancers.
The hospital specializes in trimodality treatments for complex medical conditions.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bimodality - the use or presence of two different modes or methods
  • synonym: multimodality - the use of multiple methods or approaches, especially in treatment
