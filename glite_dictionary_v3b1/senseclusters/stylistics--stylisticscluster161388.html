stylistics

noun
study of language style
7.2
Pronunciation: / staɪˈlɪstɪks / (AmE), / staɪˈlɪstɪks / (BrE)
the study and analysis of style in language, especially in literary texts

Examples

Stylistics in language
Stylistics examines how authors use language to create different effects in their writing.

The professor introduced stylistics as a way to understand how authors create mood and tone.
A knowledge of stylistics can help readers appreciate the unique features of different genres.
