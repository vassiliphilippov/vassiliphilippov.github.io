jaw

noun
a part of a tool for gripping objects
Pronunciation: / dʒɑ / (AmE), / dʒɔː / (BrE)
either of the two parts of a tool or device that move together to grip, hold, or clamp an object firmly

Examples

Jaws of the pliers
Be careful not to pinch your fingers between the jaws of the pliers.
The jaw of the clamp closed tightly around the wooden board.
The machinist adjusted the jaw of the vise to secure the metal rod.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clamp - a tool for fastening or holding things together tightly
  • synonym: vise - a mechanical screw apparatus used for holding or clamping a workpiece
