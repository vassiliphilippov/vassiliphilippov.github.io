media

noun
growth substances
6.1 2.56/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmiÀêdi…ô / (AmE), / ÀàmiÀêdi…ô / (BrE)
substances or prepared environments used for growing microorganisms, cells, or tissues in a laboratory setting

Examples

Growth media
The scientist poured the prepared media into petri dishes to grow bacterial colonies.
Different types of media are required to culture various microorganisms in the laboratory.
Before starting the experiment, she checked that all media were sterile and ready for use.

Synonyms

  • culture medium
    4.1 0.17/m
    culture medium is a nutrient liquid or gel used to grow microbes in a lab, while media refers to prepared growth substances used in labs.
  • medium
    6.5 11/m
    medium is a general word for any surrounding substance or environment, while media specifically means growth substances used for cells or microbes in labs.

Related Terms
