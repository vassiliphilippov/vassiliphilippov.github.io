media

noun
growth substances
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːdiə / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiə / (BrE)
substances or prepared environments used for growing microorganisms, cells, or tissues in a laboratory setting

Examples

Growth media
The scientist poured the prepared media into petri dishes to grow bacterial colonies.
Different types of media are required to culture various microorganisms in the laboratory.
Before starting the experiment, she checked that all media were sterile and ready for use.
