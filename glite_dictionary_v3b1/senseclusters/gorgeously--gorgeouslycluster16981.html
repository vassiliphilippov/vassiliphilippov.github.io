gorgeously

adverb
in an impressively beautiful way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔrdʒəsli / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːdʒəsli / (BrE)
In a way that is impressively beautiful or splendid.

Examples

To gorgeously decorate
She decorated the hall gorgeously, impressing all the guests.
The bride was gorgeously dressed for her wedding ceremony.
The sky was gorgeously painted with hues of orange and pink at sunset.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beautifully - in a beautiful manner
