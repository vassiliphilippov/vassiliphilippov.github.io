stock ticker

noun
machine for printing stock prices on tape
5.1 4.3
Pronunciation: / stɑk ˈtɪkər / (AmE), / stɒk ˈtɪkə / (BrE)
a device or display (mechanical or electronic) that shows real-time stock prices and financial information

Examples

An old stock ticker
Collectors sometimes display antique stock tickers as reminders of financial history.
In the early twentieth century, every brokerage office had a stock ticker in a prominent location.
The stock ticker relayed the latest share prices directly to the trading floor.
The financial website displays a live stock ticker across the bottom of its homepage.
Traders watched the stock ticker nervously as prices changed during the volatile session.
During the broadcast, the channel scrolled an international stock ticker showing major markets' indices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: live price feed - a continuous electronic stream of market prices and quotations
  • synonym: ticker - a display showing current stock prices or financial quotations
  • synonym: ticker tape - narrow strip of paper used to record information from a ticker
