welcoming committee

noun
group that greets newcomers
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlkəmɪŋ kəˌmɪti / (AmE), / ˈwɛlkəmɪŋ kəˈmɪti / (BrE)
a group of people appointed to greet and assist newcomers to a community, organization, or event

Examples

The welcoming committee greets
Our neighborhood's welcoming committee organizes events for people moving into the area.
She joined the welcoming committee to help international students settle in at the university.
The welcoming committee greeted new residents with homemade cookies and helpful information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reception committee - a committee to receive and welcome guests or newcomers
