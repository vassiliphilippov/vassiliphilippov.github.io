superhumanly

adverb
beyond human ability
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈhjuːmənli / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəhjuːˈmænli /; / ˌsuːpəhjuːmənli / (BrE)
in a way that is beyond normal human ability or to an extraordinary degree

Examples

To superhumanly run fast
He ran superhumanly fast to rescue the child from danger.
The team worked superhumanly to complete the project before the deadline.
She lifted the heavy box superhumanly, surprising everyone in the room.
She worked superhumanly hard to finish the project before the deadline.
The athlete ran superhumanly fast, breaking the world record by a wide margin.
He remained superhumanly calm during the crisis, impressing everyone around him.
