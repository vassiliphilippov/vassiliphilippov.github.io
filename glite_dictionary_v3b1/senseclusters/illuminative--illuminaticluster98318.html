illuminative

adjective
clarifying or enlightening
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈluːmɪˌneɪtɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈluːmɪnətɪv / (BrE)
providing light or making something clearer or easier to understand

Examples

Illuminative explanation
The professor's illuminative explanation made the difficult concept much easier to grasp.
Her illuminative insights brought new understanding to the discussion.
The documentary was highly illuminative for viewers unfamiliar with the subject.
