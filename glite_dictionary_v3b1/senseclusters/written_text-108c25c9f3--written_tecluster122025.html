written text

noun
material in written form, especially transcribed
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtn tɛkst / (AmE), / ˈrɪtn tɛkst / (BrE)
something that is written, especially material that has been transcribed or copied, such as a typewritten version of dictation.
