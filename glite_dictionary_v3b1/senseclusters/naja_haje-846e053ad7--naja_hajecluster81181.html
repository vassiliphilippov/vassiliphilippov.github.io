Naja haje

noun
a species of African cobra (Egyptian cobra)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌneɪ.dʒə ˈhɑː.dʒeɪ / (AmE), / ˌneɪ.dʒə ˈhɑː.dʒiː / (BrE)
