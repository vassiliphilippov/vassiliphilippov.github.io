Synonyms

  • seep
    2.7 4/m
    seep is to move slowly through small openings or pores into nearby places, while ooze implies a thicker, slower flow.
  • exude
    6.5 0.2/m
    exude is to give off a steady small amount like drops, while ooze usually means to leak or flow slowly through holes.
