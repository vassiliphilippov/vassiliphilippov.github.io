chapel

noun
religious service in a chapel
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæpəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃæpəl / (BrE)
a religious service held in a place of worship, typically one with its own altar, especially as part of a school or institution

Examples

Morning chapel service
After chapel, the school gathered for announcements in the main hall.
Every student was required to attend chapel every morning.
He missed chapel and had to explain himself to the headmaster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: service - a meeting for public worship
