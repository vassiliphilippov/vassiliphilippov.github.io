round-bottom flask

noun
spherical laboratory glass vessel
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊnd ˌbɑːtəm flæsk / (AmE), / ˈraʊnd ˌbɒtəm flɑːsk / (BrE)
a glass vessel with a spherical body and a narrow neck, commonly used in laboratories for heating or mixing chemicals

Examples

A round-bottom flask used
A round-bottom flask is often preferred because its shape distributes heat evenly.
She carefully poured the mixture into the round-bottom flask to begin the distillation process.
The solution was heated gently in a round-bottom flask during the experiment.
