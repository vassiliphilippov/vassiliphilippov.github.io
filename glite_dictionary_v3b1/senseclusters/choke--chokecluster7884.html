Synonyms

  • suffocate
    6.1 2.83/m
    suffocate means failing to breathe because of little air or smothering, while choke often means blocking the throat or airway.
  • asphyxiate
    9.3 0.49/m
    asphyxiate means stop breathing by reducing oxygen and often leads to death, while choke can be temporary or caused by blockage.
  • stifle
    6.6 0.68/m
    stifle means stop someone from breathing or hold back sounds or feelings in speech, while choke is usually physical blockage of air or liquid.
  • strangle
    6.2 5.30/m
    strangle means squeeze or press the throat to kill or injure, while choke covers many ways air or liquid become blocked.
  • throttle
    8.8 0.17/m
    throttle means try to kill by forcing pressure on the neck, while choke also includes accidental or internal airway blockages.
