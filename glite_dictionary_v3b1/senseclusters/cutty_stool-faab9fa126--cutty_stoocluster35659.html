cutty stool

noun
low Scottish stool; church seat for public rebuke
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk åti stuÀêl / (AmE), / Ààk åti stuÀêl / (BrE)
A low stool, especially in Scotland; formerly, a seat in a church where someone was publicly rebuked for an offense.
