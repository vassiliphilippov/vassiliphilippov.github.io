hoopskirt

noun
stiffened skirt with hoop framework
Pronunciation: / ˈhuːpˌskɝːt / (AmE), / ˈhuːpˌskɜːt / (BrE)
A long skirt expanded and stiffened by a framework of hoops, typically worn by women in the 19th century.

Examples

A hoopskirt dress
Fashion in the 1800s often featured the dramatic silhouette of a hoopskirt.
She struggled to fit her hoopskirt through the narrow doorway at the ball.
The museum display included a beautiful Victorian hoopskirt trimmed with lace.
