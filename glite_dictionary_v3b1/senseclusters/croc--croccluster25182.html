croc

noun
informal for crocodile
5.4
Pronunciation: / krɑk / (AmE), / krɒk / (BrE)
an informal word for a crocodile, a large reptile found in rivers and swamps

Examples

A wild croc
We spotted a massive croc swimming quietly near the riverbank.
The guide warned us not to get too close to the croc resting in the sun.
Children at the zoo were excited to see the huge croc open its jaws.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crocodile - a large, predatory reptile with a long snout and powerful jaws
