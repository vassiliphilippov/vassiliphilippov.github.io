topstitch

noun
decorative stitching
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtɑːp.stɪtʃ/ (AmE), /ˈtɒp.stɪtʃ/ (BrE)
a visible line of stitching sewn on the outside of a garment or item, often for decoration or reinforcement

Examples

A neat topstitch
The designer used a contrasting topstitch to highlight the edges of the jacket.
A neat topstitch can make simple clothing look more professional and finished.
She admired the careful topstitch running along the hem of the dress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embroidery - decorative needlework on fabric
  • synonym: seam - the line where two pieces of fabric are sewn together
