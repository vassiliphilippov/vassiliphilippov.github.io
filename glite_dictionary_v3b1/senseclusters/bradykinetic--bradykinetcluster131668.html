bradykinetic

adjective
abnormally slow movement
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌbreɪdiˈkɪnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌbrædɪkɪˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by abnormally slow movement, especially in a medical context

Examples

Bradykinetic patient
The neurologist noted that the patient appeared bradykinetic during the examination.
People with Parkinson's disease often become bradykinetic as the condition progresses.
Her movements were so bradykinetic that daily tasks took much longer than usual.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: akinetic - lacking movement or motion
  • antonym: hyperkinetic - characterized by excessive movement
