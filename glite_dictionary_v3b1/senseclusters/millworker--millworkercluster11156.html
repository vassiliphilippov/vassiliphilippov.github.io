millworker

noun
factory machine operator
2.9 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…™lÀåw…úrk…ôr / (AmE), / Ààm…™lÀåw…úÀêk…ô / (BrE)
a person who works in a mill or factory, especially operating machinery to process materials

Examples

A millworker operates machines
The experienced millworker carefully shaped the wooden panel for the furniture.
As a millworker, she learned to operate several types of milling machines.
The factory hired a new millworker to help increase production efficiency.
The millworker arrived early every morning to start the machines.
Many families in the town depended on the local millworker jobs for their livelihood.
As a skilled millworker, she was responsible for maintaining the equipment.
The millworker carefully monitored the grinding stones to ensure the flour was fine.
Generations of his family had been millworkers in the old textile mill by the river.
As a young millworker, she learned to repair the machinery when it broke down.
