by-and-by

noun
an indefinite future time
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ ən ˈbaɪ / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ ən ˈbaɪ / (BrE)
A future time that is not specifically defined; an indefinite time in the future.

Examples

In the near by-and-by
He promised that he would fix the fence in the by-and-by, but weeks went by.
Many people believe that everything will be better in the by-and-by.
We'll discuss those details in the by-and-by rather than right now.

  • synonym: future - the time yet to come
