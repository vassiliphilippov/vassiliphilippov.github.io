microbiotic

adjective
relating to microbes
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊbaɪˈɑtɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊbaɪˈɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of very small living organisms, especially bacteria or microbes

Examples

Microbiotic life forms
The scientist studied the microbiotic composition of the water sample.
A healthy gut depends on a balanced microbiotic environment.
Researchers are exploring how microbiotic factors influence plant growth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: macrobiotic - relating to large living organisms or a diet based on whole grains and vegetables
  • synonym: microbial - relating to or caused by microbes
