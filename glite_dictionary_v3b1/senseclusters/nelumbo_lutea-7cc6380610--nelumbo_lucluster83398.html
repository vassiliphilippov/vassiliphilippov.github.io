Nelumbo lutea

noun
North American water lily species
9.1
Pronunciation: / nɪˈlʌmboʊ luˈtiə / (AmE), / nɪˈlʌmbəʊ luːˈtiːə / (BrE)
A water lily native to eastern North America, known for its pale yellow flowers and edible, nut-like seeds.

Nelumbo lutea water lily
