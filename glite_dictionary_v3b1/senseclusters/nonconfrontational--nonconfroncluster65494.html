nonconfrontational

adjective
avoiding conflict
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkɑnfrənˈteɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkɒnfrənˈteɪʃənəl / (BrE)
tending to avoid conflict, arguments, or direct confrontation with others

Examples

A nonconfrontational attitude
His nonconfrontational approach made it easier to resolve disagreements in the team.
She prefers a nonconfrontational style when discussing sensitive topics with her friends.
A nonconfrontational attitude can help maintain harmony in difficult situations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aggressive - ready or likely to confront or attack
  • antonym: confrontational - tending to seek conflict or direct confrontation
  • synonym: peaceful - not involving conflict or violence
