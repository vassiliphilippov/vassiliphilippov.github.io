genderqueer

noun
non-binary identity
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛndərˌkwɪr / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛndəˌkwɪə / (BrE)
a person whose gender identity does not fit within traditional categories of male or female

Examples

A genderqueer person
Alex identifies as genderqueer and prefers they/them pronouns.
The organization welcomes people who are genderqueer and supports gender diversity.
As a genderqueer individual, Sam often educates others about non-binary identities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cisgender - a person whose gender identity matches their sex assigned at birth
  • synonym: nonbinary - a person who does not identify exclusively as male or female
