jury duty

noun
requirement to serve on a jury
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɝːi ˌduːti / (AmE), / ˈdʒʊəri ˌdjuːti / (BrE)
the civic responsibility of serving as a member of a jury in a court of law when summoned

Examples

Jury duty service
After receiving a letter in the mail, she reported for jury duty at the courthouse.
He missed work because he was selected for jury duty this week.
Many people try to postpone jury duty, but it is an important civic responsibility.
