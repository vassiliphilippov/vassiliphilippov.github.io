epitaxially

adverb
in an epitaxial way
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈtæksiəli / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈtæksiəli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to epitaxy, where a crystal layer grows on the surface of another crystal.

Examples

To grow epitaxially
The semiconductor layer was deposited epitaxially to ensure high crystal quality.
Researchers grew the thin film epitaxially on the sapphire substrate for better performance.
The material was carefully aligned and added epitaxially to match the underlying structure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epitaxy - the growth of a crystal layer on a substrate crystal
