retributor

noun
giver of punishment
9.4
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtrɪbjətər / (AmE), / rɪˈtrɪbjʊtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that gives punishment or retribution for wrongdoing

Examples

Masked retributor
The mysterious retributor appeared whenever injustice occurred in the city.
In ancient myths, a god often acted as the retributor of evil deeds.
Some believe that fate itself is the ultimate retributor for our actions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: avenger - someone who seeks revenge or justice
  • antonym: forgiver - a person who pardons or excuses wrongdoing
  • synonym: punisher - a person who inflicts punishment
