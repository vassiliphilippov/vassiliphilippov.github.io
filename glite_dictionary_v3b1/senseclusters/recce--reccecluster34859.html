recce

noun
informal for reconnaissance, inspection or scouting

an inspection or exploration, especially to gather information, often used in military or filmmaking contexts as a short form of reconnaissance.

Examples

A recce of location
Before shooting, the film crew did a quick recce of the location to plan their setup.
The commander ordered a recce of the area before moving the troops forward.
We went out on a recce yesterday to survey the wildlife in the park.
