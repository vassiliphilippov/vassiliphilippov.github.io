Cinerama

noun
immersive film process
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪnəˈræmə / (AmE), / ˌsɪnəˈræmə / (BrE)
a widescreen film projection process developed to create immersive cinema experiences using multiple projectors and a curved screen

Examples

Cinerama projection system
The old theater was renovated to show classic films in Cinerama for a new generation of moviegoers.
Many people were amazed by the immersive visuals when they first experienced Cinerama in the 1950s.
Film historians often discuss the impact of Cinerama on the development of modern cinema technology.

Related Terms
