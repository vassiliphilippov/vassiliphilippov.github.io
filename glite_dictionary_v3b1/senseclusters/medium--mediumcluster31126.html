medium

noun
nutrient substance for growing micro-organisms
2.5 6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːdiəm / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiəm / (BrE)
a substance or environment in which something exists, grows, is preserved, or takes place (such as for growing cells, preserving specimens, or as a surrounding material)
