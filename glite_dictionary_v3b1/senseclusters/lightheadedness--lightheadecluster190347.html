  • headedness
    7.8 0.26/m
    headedness means feeling light-headed or unsteady and often leads to faintness, while lightheadedness is a milder dizzy feeling.
  • wooziness
    5.4 0.04/m
    wooziness is a fuzzy, slightly confused dizzy feeling, while lightheadedness feels more like near fainting and loss of balance.
  • faintness
    4.9 0.10/m

  • giddiness
    4.6 0.16/m

  • vertigo
    7.8 2.02/m
    vertigo is a strong spinning sensation that makes surroundings move, while lightheadedness is a faint, wobbling feeling.
