zombie

noun
compromised device in a botnet
6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈzɑːm.biː/ /; / /ˈzɑːm.bɪ/ (AmE), /ˈzɒm.biː/ /; / /ˈzɒm.bɪ/ (BrE)
A computer or other device that has been infected by malware and is remotely controlled by an attacker as part of a botnet.

Examples

Zombie computer attack
The company detected several zombie PCs sending spam from the infected network.
During the security audit, they found a dozen zombies acting as part of a botnet.
After the malware outbreak, my laptop became a zombie controlled remotely by attackers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bot - a program or automated agent that performs tasks, often under remote control; used for malicious activity by attackers.
  • synonym: botnet - a network of compromised computers controlled by an attacker, often used to send spam or perform distributed attacks.
