stutterer

noun
person who stutters while speaking
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌt̬.ɚ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈstʌt.ər.ə / (BrE)
a person who speaks with involuntary pauses and repetitions, typically as a result of a speech disorder
