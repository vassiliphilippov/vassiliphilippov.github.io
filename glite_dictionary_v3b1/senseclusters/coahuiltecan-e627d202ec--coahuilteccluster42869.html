Coahuiltecan

noun
indigenous language family
Pronunciation: / koʊ.əˈwiːl.tɪ.kən / (AmE), / kəʊ.əˈwiːl.tɪ.kən / (BrE)
a language belonging to the Coahuiltecan family, historically spoken by indigenous peoples in southern Texas and northeastern Mexico
