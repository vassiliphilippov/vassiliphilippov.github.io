microcellular

adjective
small-area wireless
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈsɛljələr / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈsɛljʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or using microcells, especially for providing wireless coverage in very small areas such as buildings or city blocks.

Examples

Microcellular network system
The company installed a microcellular network to improve coverage inside the shopping mall.
A microcellular system is ideal for providing strong signals in densely populated urban areas.
Engineers recommended a microcellular solution to eliminate dead zones in the office building.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cellular - relating to a mobile phone system that uses cells to provide coverage
  • degree higher: macrocellular - relating to large-area wireless coverage using macrocells
