United States Virgin Islands

proper noun
US territory in the Caribbean Sea
Pronunciation: / ˌjuː.nəˈtaɪ.t̬ɪd steɪts ˈvɜːrdʒɪn ˈaɪ.ləndz / (AmE), / ˌjuː.nɪ.tɪd steɪts ˈvɜː.dʒɪn ˈaɪ.ləndz / (BrE)
