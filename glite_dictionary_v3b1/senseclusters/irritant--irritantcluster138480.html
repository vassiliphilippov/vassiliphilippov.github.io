irritant

noun
something that causes irritation or annoyance
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrɪtənt / (AmE), / ˈɪrɪtənt / (BrE)
a substance or factor that causes irritation, discomfort, or annoyance

Examples

A irritant substance
Exposure to dust is a common irritant for people with allergies.
His constant interruptions were a major irritant during the meeting.
Some cleaning products can be a strong irritant to sensitive skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annoyance - something that causes slight anger or irritation
  • antonym: soothing agent - something that calms or relieves irritation
