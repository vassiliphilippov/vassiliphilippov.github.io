ozonize

verb
convert oxygen into ozone
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.zəˌnaɪz /; / ˈoʊzəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.zəˌnaɪz /; / ˈəʊzənaɪz / (BrE)
