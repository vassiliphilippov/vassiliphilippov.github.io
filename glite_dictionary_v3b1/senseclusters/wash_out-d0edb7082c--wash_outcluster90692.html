wash out

verb
be cancelled/interrupted due to rain
Pronunciation: / wɑʃ aʊt / (AmE), / wɒʃ aʊt / (BrE)
(of an event or activity) to cancel or interrupt because of rain.

Examples

Rain washes out games

The baseball game was washed out after heavy rain flooded the field.
