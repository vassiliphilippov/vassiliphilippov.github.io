alert

verb
to warn about danger or problems
2.3 13/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàl…ùt / (AmE), / …ôÀàl…úÀêt / (BrE)
to warn someone about a possible danger, problem, or emergency, or to make someone aware of something that requires attention

Examples

To alert the police
Doctors were alerted when the patient's symptoms worsened unexpectedly.
Please alert me if you notice any unusual activity around the building.
The city officials alerted residents to the impending storm.

Synonyms

    1.0 57/m
    warn tells people about possible danger so they can be careful, while alert makes someone aware of danger or urgent attention.
  • caution
    3.0 8.67/m
    caution warns someone to act carefully in risky situations, while alert is used to warn or make people aware of danger or problems.
  • forewarn
    4.8 1.18/m

  • notify
    3.1 15/m
