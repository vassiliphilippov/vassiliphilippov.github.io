scoop up

verb
pick up quickly in a sweeping motion
2.8 3.46/m
Pronunciation: / skuÀêp  åp / (AmE), / skuÀêp  åp / (BrE)
to lift or pick up something quickly or in a sweeping motion, typically using a rounded or cupped hand or a container

Examples

To scoop up souvenirs
After the game, fans scooped up souvenirs from the field.
She scooped up the toys from the floor and put them in the basket.
The dog quickly scooped up the crumbs that had fallen on the ground.

Synonyms

  • snatch up
