juglone

noun
walnut-derived dye
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌɡloʊn / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌɡləʊn / (BrE)
a brown dye obtained from walnut trees, especially from their husks, used to stain cloth or wood

Examples

Juglone dye for wood
The craftsman used juglone to give the wooden bowl a deep, natural brown color.
Some artists prefer juglone for staining fabrics because of its rich, earthy tone.
She extracted juglone from walnut husks to create a homemade dye for her project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dye - a substance used to change the color of something
  • synonym: pigment - a natural or artificial substance that gives color to materials
