juglone

noun
walnut-derived dye
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒʌg.loʊn/ /; / /ˈdʒuːg.loʊn/ (AmE), /ˈdʒʌg.ləʊn/ /; / /ˈdʒuːg.ləʊn/ (BrE)
