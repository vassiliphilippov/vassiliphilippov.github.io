weaponeer

noun
arms dealer
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛpəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌwiːpəˈnɪə / (BrE)
a person who supplies, trades in, or deals with weapons, especially illegally

Examples

Illegal weaponeer sells guns
The police arrested a notorious weaponeer who had been supplying illegal firearms for years.
Rumors spread quickly about a new weaponeer operating in the city’s underground market.
Authorities are working to track down the weaponeer responsible for the recent surge in gun violence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arms dealer - a person who buys and sells weapons
  • antonym: law enforcement officer - a person who upholds and enforces the law
  • synonym: smuggler - a person who illegally moves goods, especially across borders
