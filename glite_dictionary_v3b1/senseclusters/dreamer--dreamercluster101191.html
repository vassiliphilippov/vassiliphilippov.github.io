dreamer

noun
undocumented immigrant covered by DACA/DREAM Act
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdriː.mər/ (AmE), /ˈdriː.mə/ (BrE)
(in U.S. politics) an undocumented immigrant who was brought to the United States as a child and is or would be covered by the DREAM Act or DACA protections.

Examples

Young dreamer status
Advocates argued that every dreamer deserves a path to legal status and opportunity.
As a dreamer, she worries about the future whenever immigration policy changes are discussed.
Lawmakers debated whether to extend protections for dreamer applicants during the immigration bill vote.

Related Terms

  • synonym: DACA recipient - a person granted temporary protection from deportation and a work permit under the Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals program
  • synonym: undocumented immigrant - a person living in a country without official immigration authorization; Dreamers are a specific group within this category
