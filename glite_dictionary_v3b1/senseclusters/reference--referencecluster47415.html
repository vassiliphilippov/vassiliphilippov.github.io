reference

noun
a cited publication or passage
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈref.ər.əns/ /; / /ˈref.əns/ (AmE), /ˈref.ər.əns/ /; / /ˈref.əns/ (BrE)
the act of mentioning, citing, or pointing to a source of information, or a statement or note that does this

Examples

A reference in a paper
She listed several references at the end of her paper for further reading.
The professor asked for a reference to verify the statistics in the report.
This article includes a reference to an important historical document.
A reference to the dictionary clarified the meaning of the word.
Before answering, she made a quick reference to her notes.
