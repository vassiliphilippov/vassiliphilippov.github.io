Synonyms

  • citation
    5.1 6.40/m
    citation is a formal note that shows exactly where information comes from, while reference is any mention pointing to a source.
  • mention
    2.5 18/m
    mention is a short, casual note often made in passing, whereas reference is a formal mention that points to a source.
