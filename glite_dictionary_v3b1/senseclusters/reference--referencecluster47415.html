reference

noun
a cited publication or passage
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛf.ɚ.əns /; / ˈrɛfɚəns / (AmE), / ˈrɛf.ər.əns /; / ˈrɛf.ərəns /; / ˈrɛfərəns / (BrE)
the act of mentioning, citing, or pointing to a source of information, or a statement or note that does this

Examples

A reference in a paper

This article includes a reference to an important historical document.

Before answering, she made a quick reference to her notes.
The manual should be available for reference at all times.
Please include a reference for every source you use in your research paper.
She checked the reference to confirm the original source of the quotation.
The book contains a comprehensive list of references at the end of each chapter.
