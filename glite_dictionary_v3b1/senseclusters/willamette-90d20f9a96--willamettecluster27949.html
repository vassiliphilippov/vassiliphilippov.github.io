Willamette

proper noun
river in western Oregon, US
Pronunciation: / wɪˈlæmɪt / (AmE), / wɪˈlæmɪt / (BrE)
a river in western Oregon, United States, that flows north into the Columbia River near Portland

Examples

Willamette River
