Easter Sunday

noun
Christian festival day (Easter Sunday)
1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈiː.stər ˈsʌn.deɪ/ /; / /ˈiː.stər ˈsʌn.diː/ (AmE), /ˈiː.stə ˈsʌn.deɪ/ /; / /ˈiː.stə ˈsʌn.diː/ (BrE)
The Sunday on which the Christian festival of Easter is celebrated, commemorating the resurrection of Jesus Christ; it falls on a date between late March and late April.

Examples

Easter Sunday celebration
Easter Sunday is marked by joyful celebrations in many countries around the world.
This year, Easter Sunday falls in early April.
We always go to church as a family on Easter Sunday.
