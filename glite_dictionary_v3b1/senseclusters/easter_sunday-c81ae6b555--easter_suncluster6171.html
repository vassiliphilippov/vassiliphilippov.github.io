Easter Sunday

noun
Christian festival day (Easter Sunday)
1.0 0.76/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆistÉš ËˆsÊŒn.deÉª / (AmE), / ËˆiËstÉ™ ËˆsÊŒn.deÉª / (BrE)
The Sunday on which the Christian festival of Easter is celebrated, commemorating the resurrection of Jesus Christ; it falls on a date between late March and late April.

Examples

Easter Sunday celebration
Easter Sunday is marked by joyful celebrations in many countries around the world.
This year, Easter Sunday falls in early April.
We always go to church as a family on Easter Sunday.

Synonyms

  • Easter Day
    1.0 0.10/m
    Easter Day is the single festival day in March or April, while Easter Sunday names that specific Sunday in the Christian calendar.
  • Easter
    1.0 0.33/m
    Easter is the whole Christian festival and its season, while Easter Sunday is the single Sunday when Christians celebrate the resurrection.
