Hipposideridae

noun
family of Old World leafnose bats
Pronunciation: / hɪˌpɑsɪˈdɛrɪdiː /; / hɪpəˌsɪdəˈraɪdiː / (AmE), / ˌhɪpəʊsɪˈdɛrɪdiː /; / hɪpəˌsɪdərˈɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of Old World bats known as leafnose bats, distinguished by a leaf-shaped structure on their noses
