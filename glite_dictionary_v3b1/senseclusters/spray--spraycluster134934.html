spray

noun
flowering branch or arrangement
Pronunciation: / spreɪ / (AmE), / spreɪ / (BrE)
a small branch or stem with flowers and leaves, often used as a simple flower arrangement

Examples

A spray of flowers
A delicate spray of roses adorned the bride's hair.
She placed a spray of lilies on the table for decoration.
The florist created a beautiful spray with fresh wildflowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bouquet - an attractively arranged bunch of flowers, especially one presented as a gift
