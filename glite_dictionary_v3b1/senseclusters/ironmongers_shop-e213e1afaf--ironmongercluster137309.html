ironmonger's shop

noun
British hardware store
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪərnmʌŋɚz ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈaɪənmʌŋɡəz ʃɒp / (BrE)
(British) A shop that sells hardware such as tools, nails, and household metal goods.
