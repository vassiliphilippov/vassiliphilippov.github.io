stamp

verb
crush or grind with force
Pronunciation: / stæmp / (AmE), / stæmp / (BrE)
to crush, suppress, or extinguish something forcefully

Examples

To stamp out fire
She watched the workers stamp the seeds to separate the husks.
The villagers used to stamp grapes to make fresh wine.
You can stamp the herbs with a pestle to release their oils.
Activists are working tirelessly to stamp racism from society.
It took years of reform to finally stamp the illegal trade into oblivion.
The government vowed to stamp out corruption in public offices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crush - press or squeeze with force to break or distort
  • antonym: encourage - to give support or confidence to; the opposite of suppressing
  • synonym: eradicate - to completely remove or destroy something bad
  • synonym: grind - to reduce to small particles by crushing
