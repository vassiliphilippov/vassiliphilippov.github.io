overmastering

adjective
impossible to resist
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈmæstərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈmɑːstərɪŋ / (BrE)
so strong or powerful that it cannot be resisted or controlled

Examples

Overmastering fear
He struggled against an overmastering sense of fear as he entered the dark room.
The novel describes an overmastering passion that changes the main character's life.
She was driven by an overmastering desire to succeed, no matter the obstacles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irresistible - too attractive or strong to be resisted
  • antonym: mild - not strong or intense
  • synonym: overwhelming - very great in effect or force; difficult to resist
