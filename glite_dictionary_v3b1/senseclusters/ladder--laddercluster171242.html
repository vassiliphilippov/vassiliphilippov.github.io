ladder

noun
structure with rungs for climbing
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlædɚ / (AmE), / ˈlædə / (BrE)
A structure with steps or rungs between two long side pieces, used for climbing up or down.

Examples

Climb a ladder
Be careful not to stand on the top rung of the ladder.
He climbed the ladder to fix the broken roof tile.
The painter set up his ladder against the wall before starting work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elevator - a device used for raising and lowering people or things between floors
  • synonym: staircase - a set of stairs for going from one level to another
