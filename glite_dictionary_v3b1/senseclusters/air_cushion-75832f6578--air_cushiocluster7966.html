air cushion

noun
shock-absorbing device using air
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr ˌkʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌkʊʃən / (BrE)
a device or object filled with air to provide cushioning, support, or absorb shocks (such as in shoes or seat pads)

Examples

Air cushion in shoes
Many modern trainers include an air cushion in the sole for extra comfort.
The car's air cushion reduces the impact when driving over potholes.
The engineer recommended installing an air cushion to protect the delicate equipment from vibrations.
She sat down on the air cushion to make the hard bench more comfortable.
The stadium seats were equipped with a small air cushion for each guest.
When camping, an air cushion is useful for resting and keeping warm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: air mattress - an inflatable mattress filled with air
  • synonym: shock absorber - device that absorbs and dampens shock impulses
