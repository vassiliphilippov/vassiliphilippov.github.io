queerness

noun
non-heteronormative sexual orientation or identity
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪrnəs / (AmE), / ˈkwɪənəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being homosexual or, more broadly, of not conforming to traditional heterosexual norms

Examples

LGBTQ+ queerness
He wrote an essay exploring the role of queerness in literature.
Her sense of queerness became an important part of her identity in college.
Many people celebrate queerness as a form of resistance to heteronormativity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heterosexuality - sexual attraction to people of the opposite sex
  • synonym: homosexuality - sexual attraction to people of the same sex
