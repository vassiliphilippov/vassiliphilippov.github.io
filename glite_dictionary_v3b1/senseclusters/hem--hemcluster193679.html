hem

verb
fold and sew the edge of cloth
Pronunciation: / hɛm / (AmE), / hɛm / (BrE)
to fold the edge of cloth over and sew it down to prevent unravelling or to finish the edge

Examples

To hem a skirt
Could you hem this dress for me by Friday?
She learned to hem her own skirts in sewing class.
The tailor will hem your trousers to the right length.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sew - to join or attach with needle and thread
