free

adjective
not restrained or limited
1.0 78/m
Pronunciation: / friÀê / (AmE), / friÀê / (BrE)
not limited, controlled, or held back by restrictions, rules, or obligations; able to act or be as one wishes

Examples

Free time
After years of studying, she finally had a free afternoon to relax.
In a free society, people can express their opinions openly.
You are free to leave whenever you wish.

Synonyms

  • unrestricted
    4.1 2.23/m
    unrestricted allows use or access without limits or control, while free means able to act without rules or obligations.
  • unfettered
    7.3 2.11/m
    unfettered suggests being released from chains or limits and living fully, while free means not held back by rules.
  • untrammeled
    8.3 0.27/m
    untrammeled paints a picture of open, natural movement without barriers, while free means able to act without restrictions.
  • at liberty
    3.6 1.26/m
    at liberty sounds like being allowed to do something by others, while free focuses on acting without control or obligation.
  • unrestrained
    5.7 1.50/m
    unrestrained often shows strong, uncontrolled feelings or actions, while free mainly means not limited by rules or duties.
