cultural diversity

noun
variety of cultures within a society
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltʃɚəl daɪˈvɝsəti / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəl daɪˈvɜːsəti / (BrE)
the existence of a variety of cultural or ethnic groups within a society

Examples

Cultural diversity in society
A workplace that values cultural diversity often benefits from a wider range of perspectives and ideas.
Many universities celebrate cultural diversity through international festivals and events.
Teachers are encouraged to incorporate cultural diversity into their lesson plans to reflect their students' backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: homogeneity - the quality of being made up of the same kind; lack of diversity
  • synonym: multiculturalism - an approach or policy recognizing and promoting multiple cultures within a society
