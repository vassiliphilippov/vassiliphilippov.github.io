in general

adverb
usually; broadly speaking
1.0 1.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn ˈdʒen.ər.əl/ /; / /ən ˈdʒen.ər.əl/ (AmE), /ɪn ˈdʒen.ər.əl/ /; / /ən ˈdʒen.ər.əl/ (BrE)
used to say that something is true in most cases or generally, not about specific details
