donnybrook

noun
noisy public fight
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑniˌbrʊk / (AmE), / ˈdɒnibrʊk / (BrE)
a noisy and chaotic fight or argument, especially one that happens in public

Examples

A political donnybrook
The city council meeting erupted into a donnybrook when the budget cuts were announced.
After the controversial call, a donnybrook broke out among the players on the field.
The festival ended in a donnybrook that required police intervention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brawl - a rough or noisy fight, usually involving several people
  • synonym: melee - a confused fight or scuffle involving many people
  • antonym: peace - a state of calm and absence of conflict
