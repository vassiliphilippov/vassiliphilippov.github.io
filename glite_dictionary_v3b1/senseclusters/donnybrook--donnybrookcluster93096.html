donnybrook

noun
noisy public fight
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdɑː.nɪ.brʊk/ (AmE), /ˈdɒn.ɪ.brʊk/ (BrE)

Examples

A political donnybrook

After the controversial call, a donnybrook broke out among the players on the field.
The festival ended in a donnybrook that required police intervention.
