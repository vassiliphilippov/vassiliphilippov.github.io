John James Audubon

proper noun
American ornithologist and bird artist
7.1
Pronunciation: / dʒɑn ˈdʒeɪmz ˈɔdəˌbɑn / (AmE), / dʒɒn ˈdʒeɪmz ˈɔːdəbɒn / (BrE)
(1785–1851) An American ornithologist and artist renowned for his detailed illustrations of North American birds, particularly in his work 'The Birds of America'.
