arbitrarily

adverb

In a random or inconsistent manner, without a specific pattern, reason, or system.

Examples

Rules arbitrarily applied
Some rules seemed to be arbitrarily enforced, while others were ignored entirely.
Students were placed into groups arbitrarily by the computer, without considering their preferences.
The manager assigned tasks arbitrarily, so employees often felt confused about their roles.
