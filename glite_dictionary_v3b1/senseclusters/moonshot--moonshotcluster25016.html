moonshot

noun
ambitious, high-risk project or goal
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌʃɒt / (BrE)
an ambitious or innovative project or goal, especially one with a high likelihood of failure or difficulty

Examples

An ambitious moonshot
Many thought the mission to Mars was just another moonshot, but the team pressed on.
Starting your own tech company can feel like a real moonshot in today's competitive market.
The company launched a moonshot to develop a cure for the rare disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: long shot - an attempt or guess with only a slight chance of success
