transform

verb
change by mathematical transformation
Pronunciation: / trænsˈfɔrm / (AmE), / trænsˈfɔːm / (BrE)
To change (a mathematical object or data set) by applying a mathematical function or operation, especially so as to express it in a different form.

Examples

To transform data
Data can be transformed to better fit the assumptions of the statistical model.
In this equation, the function is transformed using the Fourier transform.
The signal was transformed into the frequency domain for further analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convert - to change (something) into a different form or properties
