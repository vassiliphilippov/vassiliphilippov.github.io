sadomasochistic

adjective
relating to sadomasochism
Pronunciation: / ˌseɪdoʊˌmæsəˈkɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌseɪdəʊˌmæsəˈkɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by sadomasochism, especially in sexual practices or psychological behavior.

Examples

Sadomasochistic behavior

The film was criticized for its depiction of sadomasochistic fantasies.
They discussed sadomasochistic behaviors in the context of alternative sexualities.
