banca

noun
Philippine outrigger boat
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋkə / (AmE), / ˈbæŋkə / (BrE)
a small boat, often with outriggers, commonly used in the Philippines for fishing or transport

Examples

A fishing banca
Fishermen set out at dawn in their banca to catch fish for the market.
Tourists enjoyed a ride along the coast in a traditional banca painted with bright colors.
The villagers used a banca to transport supplies across the river during the rainy season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canoe - a narrow boat pointed at both ends and moved by paddles
  • synonym: ferry - a boat used to carry people or goods across water
