landing skid

noun
part of a helicopter's landing gear
Pronunciation: / ˈlændɪŋ skɪd / (AmE), / ˈlændɪŋ skɪd / (BrE)
one of the two long, horizontal supports on the underside of a helicopter that allow it to land and rest on the ground

Examples

Damaged landing skids
Damaged landing skids can make it dangerous for a helicopter to take off or land.
Technicians inspected each landing skid for cracks before giving the aircraft clearance to fly.
The helicopter settled gently onto its landing skid after a smooth descent.
