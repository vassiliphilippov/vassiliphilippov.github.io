purple

adjective
overly ornate or flowery (writing/speech)

(of writing or speech) excessively elaborate, ornate, or showy in style; flowery or over-embellished.

Examples

Purple prose
Editors often suggest cutting purple descriptions to improve clarity and conciseness.
His essay was criticized for its purple language, which distracted from the main argument.
The novel is full of purple passages that make the plot difficult to follow.
