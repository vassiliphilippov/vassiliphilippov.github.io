muni

noun
municipal bond (informal)
9.3 0.65/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmjuÀêni / (AmE), / ÀàmjuÀêni / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for a municipal bond, a debt security issued by a local government to raise money for public projects
