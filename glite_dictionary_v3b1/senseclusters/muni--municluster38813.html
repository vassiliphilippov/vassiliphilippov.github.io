muni

noun
municipal bond (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːni / (AmE), / ˈmjuːni / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for a municipal bond, a debt security issued by a local government to raise money for public projects

Examples

A muni bond
Many investors choose a muni for its tax advantages and relative safety.
She added another muni to her portfolio to diversify her investments.
The city issued a new muni to fund the construction of a public library.
Many investors prefer munis because the interest is often exempt from federal taxes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: corporate bond - a bond issued by a corporation
  • synonym: municipal bond - a bond issued by a city or local government
