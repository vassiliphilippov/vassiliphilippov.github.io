boatlike

adjective
resembling a boat
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbəʊtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or similar to a boat in shape or appearance

Examples

A boatlike shape
The architect designed a boatlike pavilion that seemed to float above the water.
Her sculpture had a distinctly boatlike form, curving gently at both ends.
We noticed a boatlike shadow moving slowly across the surface of the pond.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boat-shaped - having the shape of a boat
