eppy

noun
lab microtube
Pronunciation: / ˈɛpi / (AmE), / ˈɛpi / (BrE)
a small plastic microcentrifuge tube, especially an Eppendorf tube, used in laboratories for storing or spinning tiny liquid samples

Examples

A lab eppy
The scientist placed the DNA sample into a clean eppy before starting the experiment.
After centrifuging, she carefully removed the liquid from the eppy using a pipette.
Each eppy was labeled with the date and sample number for easy identification.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Eppendorf tube - a brand of small plastic tube used in laboratories
  • synonym: microcentrifuge tube - a small tube used in laboratory centrifuges for liquid samples
