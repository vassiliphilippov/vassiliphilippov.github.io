nativeness

noun
quality of belonging by origin or birth
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪ.tɪv.nəs / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.tɪv.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of belonging to or originating from a particular place by birth or origin

Examples

Cultural nativeness
His strong sense of nativeness made him an active participant in local traditions.
Many plants were chosen for the garden based on their nativeness to the region.
Scientists study the nativeness of various species to preserve the local ecosystem.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foreignness - the quality of being from another place or country; not native
  • synonym: indigeneity - the fact of originating or occurring naturally in a particular place; indigenousness
