rerelease

noun
new issue of work
3.6
Pronunciation: /rɪ.rɪ.ˈliːs/ /; / /riː.rɪ.ˈliːs/ (AmE), /rɪ.rɪ.ˈliːs/ /; / /riː.rɪ.ˈliːs/ (BrE)
