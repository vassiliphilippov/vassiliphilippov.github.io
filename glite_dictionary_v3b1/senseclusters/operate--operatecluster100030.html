operate

verb
manage or control a project or business
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɑp.əˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒp.ə.reɪt / (BrE)
to control, manage, or direct the functioning of a business, project, or organization

Examples

To operate a business
She operates a successful bakery downtown.
The charity operates several programs to support local families.
They operated the business for over twenty years before retiring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manage - to be in charge of or run a business, organization, etc.
