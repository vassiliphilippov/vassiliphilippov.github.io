shoreside

adjective
near the shore
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔːrˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːsaɪd / (BrE)
located or happening near the edge of a shore or coastline

Examples

Shoreside hotel
They enjoyed a shoreside walk as the sun set over the water.
The hotel offers beautiful shoreside views from every room.
A small shoreside café served fresh seafood to visitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coastal - situated on or near a coast
  • antonym: inland - situated away from the coast or shore
