rough-haired

adjective
having coarse or rough hair
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌfˌhɛrd / (AmE), / ˈrʌfˌheəd / (BrE)
having hair that feels coarse or rough to the touch

Examples

A rough-haired pony
Her rough-haired pony was popular with children at the riding school.
Some breeds develop a rough-haired texture that's ideal for protection against the cold.
The rough-haired terrier needs regular grooming to keep its coat clean.
