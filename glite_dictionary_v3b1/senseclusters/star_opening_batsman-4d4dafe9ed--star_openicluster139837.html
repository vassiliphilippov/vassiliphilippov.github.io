star opening batsman

noun
top batsman who opens in cricket
Pronunciation: / stɑr ˈoʊpənɪŋ ˈbætsmən / (AmE), / stɑː ˈəʊpənɪŋ ˈbætsmən / (BrE)
the leading or best player who bats first in the lineup in cricket, often relied upon for a strong start

Examples

The star opening batsman
After the retirement of their star opening batsman, the team struggled to find a suitable replacement.
Every young cricketer dreams of becoming the star opening batsman for their country one day.
The team relied heavily on their star opening batsman to set a strong foundation for the innings.
