heroic

adjective
gaming: higher difficulty level
6.8
Pronunciation: /hɪ.ˈroʊ.ɪk/ /; / /hə.ˈroʊ.ɪk/ (AmE), /hɪ.ˈrəʊ.ɪk/ /; / /hə.ˈrəʊ.ɪk/ (BrE)
(in video games) denoting a higher or more difficult game mode, dungeon, or raid intended for experienced players and offering tougher enemies and better rewards.

Examples

Heroic mode
Players attempted the heroic dungeon to earn tougher enemies and better loot.
The guild only runs heroic raids on weekends because they require experienced players and coordination.
When you select heroic mode, enemies deal more damage but drop higher-quality rewards.
