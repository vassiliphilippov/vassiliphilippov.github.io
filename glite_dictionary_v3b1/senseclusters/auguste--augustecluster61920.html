auguste

noun
comic clown character
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈɡuːst / (AmE), / əʊˈɡuːst / (BrE)
a clown character with exaggerated makeup and comic behavior, typically playing the fool in a clown duo

Examples

Auguste clown
The auguste stumbled across the stage, making the audience burst into laughter.
In the circus, the auguste often plays tricks on the more serious whiteface clown.
Children eagerly waited for the auguste to perform his next silly routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clown - a performer who entertains with comic acts and costumes
  • antonym: whiteface - a clown character with white makeup, often the straight man in a duo
