Gulf of Aden

proper noun
arm of the Indian Ocean, near Yemen and Somalia
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌlf əv ˈeɪdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌlf əv ˈeɪdn̩ / (BrE)
a gulf of the Indian Ocean situated between Yemen on the Arabian Peninsula and Somalia in eastern Africa, forming the entrance to the Red Sea

Examples

The Gulf of Aden coast
Many oil tankers pass through the Gulf of Aden on their way to Europe.
Piracy has been a significant concern for vessels navigating the Gulf of Aden.
The Gulf of Aden connects the Arabian Sea with the Red Sea.
