Sittidae

noun
family of birds: nuthatches
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪtɪˌdi / (AmE), / ˈsɪtɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of small passerine birds known as nuthatches, characterized by their ability to climb down trees head first.

Examples

The Sittidae family
Many ornithologists study Sittidae to learn about their unique foraging behavior.
Members of Sittidae are found in both the Northern and Southern Hemispheres.
The family Sittidae includes several species of agile, tree-climbing birds called nuthatches.
