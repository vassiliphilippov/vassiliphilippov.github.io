greater

adjective
of more considerable size or importance
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtə / (BrE)
of more considerable size, importance, or degree than something else; comparative form of 'great'.

Examples

The greater part
For the greater part of his career, he worked as a teacher.
She hopes her work will bring about greater understanding between the two countries.
The new building is of greater height than the old one.
