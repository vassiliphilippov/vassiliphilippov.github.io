goatee

noun
pointed chin beard
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊˈti / (AmE), / ɡəʊˈtiː / (BrE)
A small, usually pointed beard grown only on the chin, resembling the beard of a goat.

Examples

A goatee beard
After shaving off his beard, he left just a small goatee on his chin.
He decided to grow a goatee during his summer vacation.
Her father's distinctive goatee made him easy to spot in a crowd.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: beard - hair growing on the chin, cheeks, and neck of men
  • synonym: mustache - hair grown on the upper lip
