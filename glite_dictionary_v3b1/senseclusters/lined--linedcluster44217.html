lined

adjective
bordered by a row of objects
3.8
Pronunciation: / laɪnd / (AmE), / laɪnd / (BrE)
having a border or edge formed by a row of objects, especially trees or similar items.

Examples

Tree-lined street
Many historic towns have shop-lined streets bustling with visitors.
The city is famous for its tree-lined avenues and beautiful parks.
We walked down a lined pathway, with flowers on each side.
