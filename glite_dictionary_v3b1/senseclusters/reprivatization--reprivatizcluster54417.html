reprivatization

noun
return to private ownership
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌpraɪvətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌpraɪvətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of returning property or businesses to private ownership after they have been nationalized by the government

Examples

The reprivatization process
The government announced the reprivatization of several major industries to encourage competition.
Debates over reprivatization often focus on its impact on workers and consumers.
After years of state control, the reprivatization of the bank was completed last month.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nationalization - the process of bringing private assets under government ownership
  • synonym: privatization - the transfer of ownership from the public sector to private individuals or companies
