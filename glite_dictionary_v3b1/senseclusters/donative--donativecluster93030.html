donative

adjective
relating to gifts
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊnətɪv / (AmE), / ˈdəʊnətɪv / (BrE)
given or provided as a gift, especially voluntarily or as a donation.

Examples

Donative gift
The museum received a donative painting from a local artist.
Many organizations rely on donative support to fund their programs.
The donative nature of the gift made it especially meaningful to the recipients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charitable - relating to giving help or money to those in need
  • antonym: commercial - concerned with making a profit
  • synonym: philanthropic - showing a desire to help others, especially by giving money
