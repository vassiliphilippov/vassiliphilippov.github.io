bated breath

noun
anxious or excited anticipation
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪtəd ˈbrɛθ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪtɪd ˈbrɛθ / (BrE)
in a state of anxious or excited anticipation, holding one's breath in suspense

Examples

Bated breath waiting
Fans listened to the announcement about the concert with bated breath.
She waited for the judge's decision with bated breath.
The children watched the magician's trick with bated breath, eager to see what would happen next.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anticipation - the act of expecting or looking forward to something
