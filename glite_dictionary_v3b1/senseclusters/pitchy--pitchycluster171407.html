pitchy

adjective
resembling pitch or tar
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtʃi / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃi / (BrE)
having the characteristics of pitch or tar; resembling or covered in pitch; sticky, dark, and thick.

Examples

Pitchy tar
A pitchy substance oozed from the trunk of the pine tree.
His hands were left pitchy and black from handling the tar barrels.
The workers' clothes became pitchy after hours of repairing the old roof.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tar-like - resembling tar in appearance or texture
