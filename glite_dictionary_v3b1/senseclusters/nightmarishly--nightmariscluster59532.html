nightmarishly

adverb
in a terrifying way
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtˌmɛrɪʃli /; / ˈnaɪtmɛrɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtˌmeərɪʃli /; / ˈnaɪtmɛːrɪʃli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely unpleasant, frightening, or like a nightmare

Examples

Nightmarishly crowded city
The city was nightmarishly crowded during the festival, making it hard to move anywhere.
He described the accident as nightmarishly chaotic and frightening.
The computer system failed nightmarishly just before the important deadline.
The traffic was nightmarishly heavy, making us late for the meeting.
She found the exam nightmarishly difficult and struggled to finish on time.
The project became nightmarishly complicated after several unexpected problems arose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horribly - in a very unpleasant or terrible way
  • antonym: pleasantly - in a way that is enjoyable or pleasing
  • synonym: terribly - to a very great or severe degree
  • synonym: terrifyingly - in a way that causes great fear
