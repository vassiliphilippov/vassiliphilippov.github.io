helpfully

adverb
in a helpful manner
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlpfəli / (AmE), / ˈhɛlpfəli / (BrE)
In a way that provides help or is useful.

Examples

He helpfully reminded
He helpfully reminded me about the meeting tomorrow.
She helpfully provided directions to the lost tourists.
The instructions are helpfully illustrated with diagrams.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unhelpfully - in a way that is not helpful
