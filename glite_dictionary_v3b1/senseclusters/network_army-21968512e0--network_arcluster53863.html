network army

noun
group united online for shared aim
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛt.wɝk ˈɑr.mi / (AmE), / ˈnɛt.wɜːk ˈɑː.mi / (BrE)
a group of people connected and organized through the internet to achieve a common purpose, often exerting significant influence in social, political, or business spheres

Examples

A network army of activists
A network army of environmental activists coordinated a global protest through social media.
A network army quickly rallied online to support the cause after the story went viral.
The company's success relied on a network army of freelance developers working remotely.
