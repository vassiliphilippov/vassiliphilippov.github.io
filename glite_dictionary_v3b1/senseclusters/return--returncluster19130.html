return

noun
tennis stroke returning the ball
6.4
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtɝn / (AmE), / rɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
(in sports) an action of sending or running something back, such as hitting a ball back in tennis or running the ball back in football

Examples

A tennis return
After a weak serve, she made an easy return to win the point.
His backhand return is one of the best in the tournament.
She hit a powerful return that her opponent could not reach.
He scored a touchdown on a spectacular return during the first quarter.
Her 70-yard return electrified the crowd and changed the momentum of the game.
The team's defense set up a great field position with an interception return.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kickoff return - an act of returning the ball after a kickoff
  • synonym: punt return - an act of returning the ball after a punt
  • antonym: serve - the act of starting play in tennis by hitting the ball
