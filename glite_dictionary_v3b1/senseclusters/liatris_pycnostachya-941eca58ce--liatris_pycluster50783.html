Liatris pycnostachya

noun
a US perennial plant with dense purple flower spikes
Pronunciation: / laɪˈætrɪs pɪkˌnoʊˈstækiə / (AmE), / laɪˈætrɪs pɪkˌnɒˈstækɪə / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to southeastern and central United States, with very dense spikes of purple flowers, often grown for cut flowers; also known as prairie blazing star.
