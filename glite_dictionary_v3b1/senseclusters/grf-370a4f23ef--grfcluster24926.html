GRF

sym
ground reaction force
Pronunciation: / dʒiː ɑːr ɛf / (AmE), / dʒiː ɑː f / (BrE)
an abbreviation for ground reaction force, the force exerted by the ground on a body in contact with it, especially in biomechanics

Examples

GRF in jumping
Researchers measured the GRF during each phase of the athlete's jump.
A sudden increase in GRF can lead to injuries in runners.
The study compared GRF values between walking and running on different surfaces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ground reaction force - the force the ground exerts on a body in contact with it
