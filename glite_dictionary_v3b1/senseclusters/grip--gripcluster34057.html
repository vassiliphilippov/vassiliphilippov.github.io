grip

noun
handle or part to hold
Pronunciation: / ɡɹɪp / (AmE), / ɡɹɪp / (BrE)
the part of an object specifically shaped or designed to be held in the hand when using or moving it

Examples

Bicycle grip
Always keep a firm hold on the bicycle's grip when you ride.
The suitcase still had a sturdy grip despite its age.
The umbrella's grip was covered in soft foam for comfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handle - part of an object designed to be held or manipulated
