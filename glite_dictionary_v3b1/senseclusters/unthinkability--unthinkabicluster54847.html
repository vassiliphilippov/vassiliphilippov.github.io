unthinkability

noun
impossibility to imagine
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌθɪŋkəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌθɪŋkəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being impossible to imagine, consider, or accept

Examples

The unthinkability of loss
The unthinkability of losing their home made the family work even harder to save it.
For many, the unthinkability of such a disaster meant they were unprepared when it happened.
He spoke about the unthinkability of peace in the region after years of conflict.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impossibility - the state of being impossible
  • synonym: inconceivability - the state of being impossible to imagine or comprehend
  • antonym: possibility - the state of being possible
