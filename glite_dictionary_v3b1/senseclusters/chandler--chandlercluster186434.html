chandler

noun
a maker or seller of candles and soap
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃændlər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːndlɐ / (BrE)
a person whose occupation is to make or sell candles, soap, oils, and sometimes paints

Examples

Candle chandler
Before electric lighting, a good chandler was essential to running a large estate.
He apprenticed as a chandler, learning to mix oils for lamps and molds for candles.
The village chandler supplied everyone with candles and soap throughout the year.
