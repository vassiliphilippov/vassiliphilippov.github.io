flying bomb

noun
jet-propelled missile carrying a bomb
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪɪŋ bɑm / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ bɒm / (BrE)
a small jet-propelled missile with wings, designed to deliver a bomb to a target

Examples

A flying bomb attack
British cities suffered several attacks from flying bombs in the 1940s.
Experts found the remains of an old flying bomb in the forest.
The V-1 was a type of flying bomb used during World War II.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruise missile - guided missile that can fly at low altitude along a planned route
  • synonym: missile - projectile weapon propelled to a target
