choppily

adverb
with uneven chopping
6.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt É…ëÀêp…™li / (AmE), / Ààt É…íp…™li / (BrE)
in a way that is uneven, jerky, or interrupted, either in physical movement or in flow (such as video or speech)

Examples

To cut choppily
The chef sliced the vegetables choppily, making the pieces different sizes.
Because of the wind, the boat moved choppily across the water.
He spoke choppily, pausing often and breaking his sentences into short bursts.
The video played choppily because of the slow internet connection.
Waves hit the shore choppily during the storm, making it hard to swim.
He spoke choppily, pausing often as he tried to remember the words.

Synonyms

  • lurchingly

  • brokenly
    4.9 0.09/m
