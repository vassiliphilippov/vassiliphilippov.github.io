sang

noun
North American herb, American ginseng
9.9
Pronunciation: / sæŋ / (AmE), / sæŋ / (BrE)
A North American woodland herb (Panax quinquefolius) similar to and used as a substitute for Chinese ginseng.

Examples

Wild sang plant
She learned to identify sang in the forest during the autumn harvest.
Some traditional healers use sang in herbal remedies for its energizing effects.
The export of wild sang is regulated due to its popularity and value.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ginseng - an Asian herb whose root is used as a stimulant and tonic
