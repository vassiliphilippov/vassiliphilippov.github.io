remoteness

noun
aloof or unsympathetic manner
6.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmoʊtnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈməʊtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being distant and unsympathetic in manner or attitude; aloofness

Examples

Emotional remoteness
Despite his brilliance, his remoteness made it difficult for colleagues to approach him.
Her remoteness during the meeting created an uncomfortable atmosphere among the team.
There was a certain remoteness in his manner that kept others at a distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aloofness - the quality of being emotionally distant or detached
  • antonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly and approachable
