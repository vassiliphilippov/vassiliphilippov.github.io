meum

noun
archaic for 'mine'
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːəm / (AmE), / ˈmiːəm / (BrE)
an archaic word meaning something that belongs to me; my property or possession

Examples

His meum
He argued that the field was meum and refused to let anyone else use it.
In old legal documents, you may find the term meum referring to personal property.
The dispute centered on what was meum and what was shared among the family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mine - something that belongs to me
  • antonym: thine - archaic word for 'yours'
