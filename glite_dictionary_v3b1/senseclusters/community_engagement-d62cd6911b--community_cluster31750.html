community engagement

noun
active involvement in community activities
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunəti ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪti ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the process of actively participating in activities to improve or support a community

Examples

Effective community engagement
Effective community engagement can lead to positive changes in neighborhoods and schools.
Her strong belief in community engagement inspired others to get involved in local projects.
The company encouraged community engagement by supporting local volunteering events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: civic participation - involvement in political or community activities by citizens
  • antonym: disengagement - lack of involvement in activities or relationships
