doubleheader

noun
two consecutive games between same teams
Pronunciation: / ˌdʌbəlˈhɛdər / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəlˈhɛdə / (BrE)
A sporting event in which two games are played consecutively between the same teams on the same day, especially in baseball.

Examples

A doubleheader game
After the first doubleheader of the season, the players were exhausted.
Fans enjoyed concessions and activities between games during the doubleheader.
The baseball teams played a doubleheader to make up for yesterday's rainout.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single game - a single match or contest between teams
