ramp

verb
stand with raised forelegs/arms menacingly
3.9
Pronunciation: / ræmp / (AmE), / ræmp / (BrE)
(of an animal, especially in heraldry or zoology) to stand with the forelegs or arms raised, as if in a threatening or menacing posture.

Examples

A bear ramps up
Many startled bears ramp on their hind legs if threatened.
The horse appeared to ramp, rearing up on its hind legs in alarm.
The lion on the coat of arms is shown to ramp above the shield.
