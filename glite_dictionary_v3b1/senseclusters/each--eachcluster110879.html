each

adverb
to or from every one considered individually
3.8
Pronunciation: / itʃ / (AmE), / iːtʃ / (BrE)
For or to every one of two or more people or things considered separately.

Examples

A gift each
The children were given a slice of cake each.
They paid thirty dollars each for the tickets.
We were handed two gifts each at the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: every - all individuals or things in a group without exception
