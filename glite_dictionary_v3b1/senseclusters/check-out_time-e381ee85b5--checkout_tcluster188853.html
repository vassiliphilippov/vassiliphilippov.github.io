check-out time

noun
latest hotel departure time
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛk aʊt taɪm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛk aʊt taɪm / (BrE)
the latest time by which a guest must leave their room and vacate a hotel

Examples

The check-out time
Please be aware that missing the check-out time may result in an extra charge.
She requested a late check-out time to accommodate her afternoon flight.
The hotel check-out time is usually 11 a.m., so plan accordingly.

Related Terms
