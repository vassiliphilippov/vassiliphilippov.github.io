crossover of genres

noun
blend of different genres or styles
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔːsˌoʊvɚ əv ˈʒɑnrz / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌəʊvə əv ˈʒɒnz / (BrE)
a musical or artistic work that blends elements from two or more different genres or styles

Examples

A crossover of genres novel
Her novel is a crossover of genres, mixing elements of science fiction and romance.
The band's latest album is a true crossover of genres, blending jazz with electronic sounds.
The festival showcases films that are a crossover of genres, attracting a wide range of audiences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fusion - the process or result of joining two or more things together to form a single entity, often used in music
  • degree lower: genre - a category of artistic composition, as in music or literature, characterized by similarities in form, style, or subject matter
