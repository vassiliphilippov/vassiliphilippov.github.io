Sigyn

proper noun
Norse mythological wife of Loki
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːɡɪn / (AmE), / ˈsiːɡɪn / (BrE)
In Norse mythology, the wife of Loki, known for her loyalty in comforting him during his punishment by holding a bowl to catch dripping poison.

Sigyn in Norse mythology
Sigyn is celebrated for her unwavering devotion to Loki in Norse legends.
Sigyn's story reflects the themes of loyalty and sacrifice in mythology.
