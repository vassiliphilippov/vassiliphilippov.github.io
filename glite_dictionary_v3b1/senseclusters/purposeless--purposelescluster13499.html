purposeless

adjective
without purpose or meaning
1.0 4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpɜːr.pə.sləs/ (AmE), /ˈpɜː.pə.sləs/ (BrE)
having no purpose, aim, or useful result; serving no useful function

Examples

A purposeless life
After losing his job, he wandered the city in a purposeless daze.
She felt her tasks had become purposeless, offering her no real satisfaction.
The conversation seemed purposeless and eventually faded into silence.
Many critics called the film's violence excessive and purposeless.
She wandered through the empty house in a purposeless way, unsure what to do next.
The meeting felt purposeless because no decisions were made by the end.
