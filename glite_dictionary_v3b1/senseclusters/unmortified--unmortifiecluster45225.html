unmortified

adjective
not embarrassed
7.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɔrtɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɔːtɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not feeling embarrassed, ashamed, or humiliated in a situation where such feelings might be expected

Examples

Unmortified after a mistake
Despite the mistake, he appeared completely unmortified in front of the audience.
She was unmortified by the criticism and continued with her presentation.
Even after the awkward silence, they remained unmortified and kept talking.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mortified - feeling extremely embarrassed or ashamed
  • synonym: unashamed - not feeling shame or embarrassment
  • synonym: unembarrassed - not feeling embarrassed
