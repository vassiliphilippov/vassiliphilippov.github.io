contractive

adjective
causing contraction
7.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈtræktɪv / (AmE), / kənˈtræktɪv / (BrE)
causing or producing contraction, especially of muscles or bodily tissues.

Examples

Contractive muscle effect
The medication has a contractive effect on smooth muscle tissue.
Doctors monitored the contractive response of the patient's heart after the procedure.
Certain exercises can increase the contractive strength of your abdominal muscles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: constrictive - causing something to become narrower or tighter
  • antonym: relaxant - causing relaxation, especially of muscles
