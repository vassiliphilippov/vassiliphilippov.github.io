meditatio

noun
Lectio Divina stage
9.9
Pronunciation: /me.dɪ.ˈteɪ.ʃoʊ/ /; / /me.dɪ.ˈtɑː.tiː.oʊ/ (AmE), /me.dɪ.ˈteɪ.ʃəʊ/ /; / /me.dɪ.ˈtɑː.tiː.əʊ/ (BrE)
a stage in the Christian practice of Lectio Divina involving prayerful reflection on a passage of scripture

Examples

Scripture meditatio
During the retreat, each monk set aside time for meditatio on the day's Gospel reading.
The practice of meditatio helps deepen one's understanding of scripture in Lectio Divina.
After reading the passage, she entered into meditatio to reflect on its meaning for her life.
