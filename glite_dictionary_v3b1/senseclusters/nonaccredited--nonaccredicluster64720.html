nonaccredited

adjective
not legally qualified
7.9
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ə.ˈkred.ɪ.tɪd/ /; / /nʌn.ə.ˈkred.ɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /nɒn.ə.ˈkred.ɪ.tɪd/ /; / /nə.ˈkred.ɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
not meeting the official legal criteria to be recognized as an accredited investor, and therefore ineligible for certain private investments.

Examples

Nonaccredited investor
As a nonaccredited investor, he was not allowed to participate in the private equity offering.
The company could only accept funds from accredited investors, so nonaccredited individuals were excluded.
Many investment opportunities are unavailable to nonaccredited investors due to regulatory restrictions.
