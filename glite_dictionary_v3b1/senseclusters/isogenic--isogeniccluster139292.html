isogenic

adjective
same genetic makeup
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
having the same genetic makeup or originating from the same genetic source

Examples

Isogenic mice
Scientists used isogenic mice to ensure that genetic differences would not affect the experiment.
The two cell lines are isogenic, differing only by a single gene mutation.
Researchers often compare isogenic organisms to study the impact of specific genetic changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genetically identical - having exactly the same genetic material
  • antonym: heterogenic - having a different genetic origin
