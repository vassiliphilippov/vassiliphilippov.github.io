sharklet

noun
aircraft wing extension
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːrklət / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːklət / (BrE)
a small, upward-pointing extension at the tip of an aircraft wing designed to improve aerodynamic efficiency

Airplane sharklet
