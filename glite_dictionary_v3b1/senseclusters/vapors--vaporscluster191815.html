vapors

noun
aerosol from e-cigarettes
3.8
Pronunciation: / ve6ap5az / (AmE), / ve6ap59z / (BrE)
The aerosol produced by electronic cigarettes or other vaping devices; the visible cloud of tiny liquid droplets exhaled during vaping.

Examples

Vapors from e-cigarettes
Many offices ban the vapors emitted by e-cigarettes in enclosed spaces to protect workers.
She waved her hand and the sweet-smelling vapors from the vape dissipated quickly outdoors.
Researchers are studying whether inhaling vapors from flavored e-liquids affects lung health long term.
