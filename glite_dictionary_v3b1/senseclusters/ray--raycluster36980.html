ray

noun
branch of an umbel (botany)
Pronunciation: / reɪ / (AmE), / reɪ / (BrE)
A primary division or branch of an umbel or umbel-like flower cluster in plants.

Examples

Flower rays
Botanists count the number of rays to distinguish between related plant species.
Each ray in the umbel holds a small cluster of blossoms.
The flower heads have long rays that spread from a central point.

Related Terms

  • synonym: umbel - a cluster of flowers with stalks of nearly equal length that spring from a common center
