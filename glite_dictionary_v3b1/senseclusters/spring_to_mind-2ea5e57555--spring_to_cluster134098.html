spring to mind

verb
to be immediately remembered
4.3 2.9
Pronunciation: /sprɪŋ tə maɪnd/ /; / /sprɪŋ tuː maɪnd/ (AmE), /sprɪŋ tə maɪnd/ /; / /sprɪŋ tuː maɪnd/ (BrE)
to come into your mind quickly or without effort (about an idea, memory, or thought)

Examples

It springs to mind
Her kindness always springs to mind when I think about good friends.
No solutions immediately spring to mind, but let's keep thinking.
Whenever I hear that song, memories of my childhood spring to mind.
If you need ideas for dinner, pasta often springs to mind.
No other candidates spring to mind for the position at this moment.
Whenever someone mentions Paris, the Eiffel Tower immediately springs to mind.
