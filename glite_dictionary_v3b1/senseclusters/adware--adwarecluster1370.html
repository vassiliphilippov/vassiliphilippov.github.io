adware

noun
advertising software
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈæd.wer/ (AmE), /ˈæd.weə/ (BrE)
software that automatically shows or downloads advertising material, often without the user's consent

Examples

Adware software
Many free programs come bundled with adware that displays pop-up ads on your computer.
He installed an antivirus tool to remove the adware slowing down his laptop.
Some adware tracks your browsing habits to show you targeted advertisements.
After downloading the free game, my computer was filled with adware that showed constant pop-up ads.
Many users install security programs to protect their devices from adware and other unwanted software.
The technician removed the adware that was slowing down my laptop.
Many free mobile apps include adware that displays banners while you use them.
He removed the adware from his computer because the pop-up ads were too annoying.
Some users prefer to pay for software rather than deal with adware interruptions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freeware - software available for use at no cost
  • synonym: malware - software designed to harm or exploit computers
  • synonym: spyware - software that secretly gathers user information
