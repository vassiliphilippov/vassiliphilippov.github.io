glucuronide

noun
conjugated liver compound
Pronunciation: / ɡluːˈkjʊrəˌnaɪd /; / ɡluˈkjʊrəˌnaɪd / (AmE), / ɡluːˈkjʊərənaɪd /; / ɡluˈkjʊərənaɪd / (BrE)
a compound formed when a substance combines with glucuronic acid (usually in the liver) to help the body remove drugs or toxins
