recessed

adjective
set back like an alcove
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɛst / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛst / (BrE)
(of a space or part) set back or formed so as to resemble an alcove or nook; indented into a surface.

Examples

Recessed window seat
A recessed window seat created a cozy reading nook.
The living room has a recessed shelf perfect for displaying art.
They installed lights in the recessed areas above the fireplace.

Related Terms

  • antonym: protruding - extending outward from a surface
