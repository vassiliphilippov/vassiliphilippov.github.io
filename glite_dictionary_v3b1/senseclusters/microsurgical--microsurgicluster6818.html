microsurgical

adjective
extremely precise
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈsɜrdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈsɜːdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
extremely careful, precise, and detailed in action or analysis, as if performed with the accuracy of microsurgery

Examples

Microsurgical editing
The editor took a microsurgical approach to revising the manuscript, ensuring every sentence was clear.
His microsurgical attention to detail made the project a success.
With microsurgical precision, she organized the data for the final report.

  • synonym: meticulous - showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
