iconoclast

noun
someone who challenges traditional beliefs or institutions
Pronunciation: / aɪˈkɑnəˌklæst / (AmE), / aɪˈkɒnəklæst / (BrE)
a person who attacks or criticizes cherished beliefs or established institutions

Examples

A modern iconoclast
As an outspoken iconoclast, she often challenges the status quo in her articles.
His iconoclast views made him unpopular among conservative politicians.
The artist gained fame for being an iconoclast in a field dominated by tradition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heretic - a person holding beliefs contrary to orthodox doctrine
  • antonym: traditionalist - a person who supports established customs or beliefs
