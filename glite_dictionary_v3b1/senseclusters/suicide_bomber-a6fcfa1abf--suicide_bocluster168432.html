suicide bomber

noun
a person who kills themselves and others with a bomb
1.0 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːəˌsaɪd ˈbɑːmər / (AmE), / ˈsuːɪsʌɪd ˈbɒmə / (BrE)
a person who carries out a bomb attack intending to kill others and themselves, usually for ideological or political reasons

Examples

Suicide bomber attack
Authorities increased security after threats of suicide bombers targeting public transport.
The city was shocked by the attack carried out by a suicide bomber in the crowded market.
The film depicted the life of a suicide bomber and the consequences of their actions.

  • synonym: terrorist - someone who uses violence for political aims, especially against civilians
