spaceborne

adjective
in outer space
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪsˌbɔrn / (AmE), / ˈspeɪsˌbɔːn / (BrE)
carried, operating, or existing in outer space

Examples

Spaceborne telescope
The spaceborne telescope sent back detailed images of distant galaxies.
Scientists analyzed data from a spaceborne satellite to study Earth's atmosphere.
A spaceborne observatory can avoid the distortion caused by Earth's atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airborne - carried or transported by air
  • antonym: ground-based - located or operating on the ground
