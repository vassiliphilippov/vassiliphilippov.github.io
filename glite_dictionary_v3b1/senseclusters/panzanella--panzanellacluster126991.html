panzanella

noun
Italian bread salad
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːntsəˈnɛlə / (AmE), / ˌpæntsəˈnɛlə / (BrE)
an Italian salad made with pieces of bread, tomatoes, onions, olive oil, and sometimes other vegetables

Examples

A panzanella salad
For lunch, we shared a bowl of panzanella filled with juicy tomatoes and crunchy bread.
The chef recommended the panzanella as a refreshing starter on a hot summer day.
She learned to make authentic panzanella while traveling in Tuscany last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caprese - an Italian salad of mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil
  • synonym: salad - a cold dish of mixed vegetables, sometimes with added meat or fish
