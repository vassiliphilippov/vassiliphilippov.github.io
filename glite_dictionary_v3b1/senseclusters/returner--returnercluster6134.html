returner

noun
person who comes back
2.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtɜrnər / (AmE), / rɪˈtɜːnə / (BrE)
a person who comes back to a place, organization, or situation after being away for some time

Examples

A returner to work
After several years working abroad, the returner found it challenging to readjust to life at home.
The company organized a special event to welcome all the returners back to the team.
Many returners bring valuable international experience to their communities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emigrant - a person who leaves their own country to settle elsewhere
  • synonym: returnee - a person who returns, especially to their home country
