teat

noun
nipple-like projection on a mammary gland
Pronunciation: / tit / (AmE), / tiːt / (BrE)
the small, nipple-like projection on a mammary gland through which milk is suckled, especially in animals

Examples

Animal teats
Each udder has several teats used for feeding young.
Farmers regularly check the health of a cow's teat to prevent infection.
The newborn calf instinctively found its mother's teat and began to nurse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nipple - the small projection on a mammary gland, especially in humans
