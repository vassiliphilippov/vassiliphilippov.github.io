present perfect

noun
verb tense for completed actions with present relevance
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛzənt ˈpɜrfɪkt / (AmE), / ˈprɛzənt ˈpɜːfɪkt / (BrE)
a verb tense used to express an action that has been completed at the present time or has relevance to the present
