smalt

verb
to apply blue pigment
Pronunciation: / smɔlt / (AmE), / smɔːlt / (BrE)
to color or coat something with smalt, a blue glass pigment, especially in ceramics or painting

Examples

To smalt pottery
Artisans would smalt the pottery to achieve a deep blue color before glazing.
The restoration team decided to smalt the faded sections of the mural for authenticity.
Historically, craftsmen would smalt glass beads to create vibrant blue jewelry.
