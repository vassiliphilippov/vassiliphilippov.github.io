Blackbeard

proper noun
notorious English pirate (c. 1680–1718)

Pronunciation: /ˈblæk.bɪrd/ (AmE), /ˈblæk.bɪəd/ (BrE)
(c. 1680–1718) The nickname of Edward Teach (or Thatch), a notorious English pirate who raided ships in the Caribbean and along the American Atlantic coast.
