Gaultheria hispidula

noun
a North American/Japanese evergreen shrub
Pronunciation: / ɡɔlˈθɪriə hɪˈspɪdjulə / (AmE), / ɡɔːlˈθɪəriə hɪˈspɪdjʊlə / (BrE)
A slow-growing, low evergreen shrub native to northern North America and Japan, with white flowers and small white hairy berries; also known as creeping snowberry.
