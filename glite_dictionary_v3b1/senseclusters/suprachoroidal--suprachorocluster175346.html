suprachoroidal

adjective
above eye's choroid
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəkəˈrɔɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəkəˈrɔɪdəl / (BrE)
located or occurring above the choroid layer of the eye

Examples

The suprachoroidal space
The surgeon carefully injected the drug into the suprachoroidal space to treat the patient's eye condition.
Researchers are studying new therapies that use the suprachoroidal route for drug delivery in ophthalmology.
Inflammation in the suprachoroidal area can lead to serious vision problems if not treated promptly.
