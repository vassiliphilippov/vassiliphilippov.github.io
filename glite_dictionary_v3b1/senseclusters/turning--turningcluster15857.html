turning

noun
act of changing direction
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːnɪŋ /; / ˈtɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
an act or process of changing direction or position, either physically or in a general sense

Examples

A sharp turning

The driver signaled before making a turning at the busy intersection.
The turning of the seasons brings new opportunities each year.
The sudden turning of the conversation caught everyone by surprise.
We felt a turning of the tide as the weather began to change.
