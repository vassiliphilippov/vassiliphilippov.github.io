dab

noun
a light or quick touch
7.8
Pronunciation: / dæb / (AmE), / dab / (BrE)
a light or quick touch, often made gently with a hand, cloth, or other object

Examples

A gentle dab
Apply a small dab of cream to the affected area.
He gave the painting a gentle dab with his brush.
She removed her tears with a quick dab of her handkerchief.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pat - a light tap with the hand or a flat object
  • synonym: tap - a light touch or gentle knock
