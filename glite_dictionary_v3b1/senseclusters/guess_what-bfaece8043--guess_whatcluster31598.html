guess what

interjection
used to introduce surprising news
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɛs wʌt / (AmE), / ɡɛs wɒt / (BrE)
used to introduce surprising, interesting, or important news, often to prompt curiosity in the listener

Examples

Guess what happened
Guess what? Emily just got engaged!
Guess what? The test has been postponed until Friday.
