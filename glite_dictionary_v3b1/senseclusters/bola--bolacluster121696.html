bola

noun
hunting tool: weighted rope for entangling animals
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlə / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlə / (BrE)
A rope with weights attached to the ends, designed to be thrown to entangle the legs of animals, especially as used by South American gauchos.

Examples

A bola weapon
In the museum, you can see a traditional bola used by Argentine gauchos.
The bola wrapped tightly around the horse’s legs, bringing it to a sudden stop.
The hunter skillfully threw the bola to capture the running ostrich.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bolas - plural form of bola; sometimes used as a variant
