misframing

noun
incorrect frame construction
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈfreɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈfreɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the incorrect construction or installation of a structural frame in carpentry or building work

Examples

Misframing a wall
The building failed inspection due to significant misframing in several walls.
Careless misframing can lead to structural problems and costly repairs.
The contractor had to redo the work after discovering misframing around the doorways.

Related Terms

  • synonym: framing error - a mistake made during the construction of a frame
  • antonym: proper framing - the correct construction of a structural frame
