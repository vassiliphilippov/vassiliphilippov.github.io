greaseless

adjective
without grease
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːsləs / (AmE), / ˈɡriːsləs / (BrE)
not containing, producing, or covered with grease or oil

Examples

Greaseless lotion
He bought a greaseless lotion that would not leave any residue on his skin.
The chef preferred using greaseless pans for healthier cooking.
After repairing the car, she cleaned her hands with a greaseless soap.

Related Terms

  • antonym: greasy - covered with or containing grease
  • synonym: oil-free - not containing any oil
