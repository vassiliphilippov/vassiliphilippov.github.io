camomile

noun
plant used in herbal medicine and tea
Pronunciation: / ˈkæməˌmil / (AmE), / ˈkæm.ə.miːl / (BrE)
A Eurasian plant with feathery leaves and white, daisy-like flowers, often used in herbal medicine and tea for its calming effects.

Examples

Camomile tea
Drinking camomile tea before bed can help promote relaxation and better sleep.
Many people use dried camomile in homemade remedies for mild digestive problems.
The garden is filled with the sweet scent of blooming camomile flowers.
