unsharpened

adjective
not sharpened; lacking a sharpened edge or point
3.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃɑrpənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɑːpənd / (BrE)
not made sharp; having no edge or point sharpened for use.

Examples

An unsharpened knife
Because the scissors were unsharpened, they wouldn't cut through the paper.
She handed me an unsharpened pencil before the exam started.
The chef refused to use the unsharpened knife to cut the vegetables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunt - having a worn-down edge or point; not sharp
  • antonym: sharp - having a fine edge or point
