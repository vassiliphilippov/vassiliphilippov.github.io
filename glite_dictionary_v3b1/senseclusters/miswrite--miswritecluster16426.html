miswrite

verb
to write incorrectly
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈraɪt / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈraɪt / (BrE)
to write something incorrectly or with mistakes

Examples

To miswrite a date
She realized she had miswritten the date on the invitation and had to send a new one.
If you miswrite the answer, the teacher may mark it as incorrect.
He miswrote his friend's phone number, so he could not reach him.

Related Terms

  • antonym: correct - to make something right or free from errors
  • synonym: misspell - to spell a word incorrectly
