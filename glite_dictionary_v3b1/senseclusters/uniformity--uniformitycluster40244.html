uniformity

noun
lack of variation; sameness
1.3 3.7
Pronunciation: /juː.nə.ˈfɔːr.mə.tiː/ /; / /juː.nɪ.ˈfɔːr.mɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /juː.nɪ.ˈfɔː.mɪ.tiː/ /; / /juː.nə.ˈfɔː.mə.tiː/ (BrE)
