up to now

adverb
until the present moment
Pronunciation: / ʌp tu naʊ / (AmE), / ʌp tu naʊ /; / ʌp tə naʊ / (BrE)
until now; up to the present moment

Examples

Up to now

Up to now, the investigation has produced few results.
He had never seen snow up to now.
Up to now, everything has gone according to plan and there have been no problems.
No solution has been discovered up to now, but research is still ongoing.
She had never traveled abroad up to now, so this trip is very exciting for her.
