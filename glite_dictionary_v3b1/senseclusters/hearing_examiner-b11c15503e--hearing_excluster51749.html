hearing examiner

noun
official who conducts government hearings
2.6 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈhiː.rɪŋ ɪg.ˈzæm.ɪ.nər/ /; / /ˈhɪ.rɪŋ ɪg.ˈzæm.ɪ.nər/ (AmE), /ˈhɪə.rɪŋ ɪg.ˈzæm.ɪ.nə/ /; / /ˈhɪː.rɪŋ ɪg.ˈzæm.ɪ.nə/ (BrE)
