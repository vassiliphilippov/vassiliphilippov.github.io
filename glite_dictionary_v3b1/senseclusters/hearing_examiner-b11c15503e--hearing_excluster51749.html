hearing examiner

noun
official who conducts government hearings
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪrɪŋ ɪɡˌzæmɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɪərɪŋ ɪɡˌzæmɪnə / (BrE)
an official appointed by a government agency to conduct investigations or administrative hearings and make recommendations or decisions on cases

Examples

Hearing examiner decides case
After listening to testimony, the hearing examiner decided in favor of the complainant.
If you disagree with the agency’s decision, you may request a review by a hearing examiner.
The hearing examiner presided over the case and issued a detailed report with recommendations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrative law judge - an official who adjudicates disputes in administrative law proceedings
