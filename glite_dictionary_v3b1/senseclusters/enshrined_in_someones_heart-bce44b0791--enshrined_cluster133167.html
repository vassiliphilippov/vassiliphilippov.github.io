enshrined in someone's heart

adjective
deeply cherished or remembered
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈʃraɪnd ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz hɑrt / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃraɪnd ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz hɑːt / (BrE)
Deeply cherished or remembered; held in lasting affection or reverence.

Examples

Enshrined in someone's heart forever
Her late grandmother's wisdom is forever enshrined in her heart.
That special summer is still enshrined in my heart after all these years.
The sacrifices of the fallen will remain enshrined in our hearts for generations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgotten - no longer remembered
