automatic teller machine

noun
cash-dispensing banking machine (ATM)
Pronunciation: / əˌtɑmətɪk ˈtɛlɚ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ɔːtəˌmætɪk ˈtɛlə məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine, usually located outside banks, that allows people to withdraw cash and use banking services by inserting a card and entering a PIN, without needing a bank teller

Examples

Automatic teller machine card
Many travelers rely on an automatic teller machine to access cash while abroad.
She withdrew $100 from the automatic teller machine near her office.
The automatic teller machine was out of service, so I had to go inside the bank.
