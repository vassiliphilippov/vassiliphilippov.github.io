crisscross

noun
intersecting lines pattern
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪsˌkrɔs / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsˌkrɒs / (BrE)
a pattern or arrangement of lines that cross each other, forming a network or lattice

Examples

A crisscross of wires
Sunlight shone through the crisscross of wires on the fence, casting patterns on the ground.
The map was filled with a crisscross of roads and railway lines.
The wooden table was covered with a crisscross of knife marks and scratches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crosshatch - a pattern made of intersecting sets of parallel lines
