scaremonger

noun
person spreading fear through rumors
8.2 6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsker.mʌŋ.gər/ (AmE), /ˈskeə.mʌŋ.gə/ (BrE)

Examples

A scaremonger spreads fear

Health officials warned the public not to listen to scaremongers spreading false information online.
The newspaper dismissed rumors of economic collapse as the work of a scaremonger.
