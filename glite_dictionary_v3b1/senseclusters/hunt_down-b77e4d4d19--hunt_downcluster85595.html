hunt down

verb
pursue and catch wild animals
1.0
Pronunciation: / hʌnt daʊn / (AmE), / hʌnt daʊn / (BrE)

Examples

To hunt down wild animals

Wolves instinctively hunt down weaker animals in the herd.
