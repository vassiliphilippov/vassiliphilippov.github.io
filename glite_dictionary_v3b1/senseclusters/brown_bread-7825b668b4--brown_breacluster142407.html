brown bread

noun
whole wheat bread
5.3 1.0
Pronunciation: /braʊn bred/ (AmE), /braʊn bred/ (BrE)
bread made from whole wheat or whole grain flour, darker and denser than white bread, sometimes with other ingredients like cornmeal or molasses
