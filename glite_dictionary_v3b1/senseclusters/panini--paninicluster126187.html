panini

noun
Italian grilled sandwich
Pronunciation: / pəˈniːni / (AmE), / paˈniːni / (BrE)
an Italian sandwich made with bread, usually grilled and filled with meat, cheese, or vegetables

Examples

A grilled panini
She ordered a chicken panini with pesto and mozzarella for lunch.
The café serves a variety of panini filled with fresh ingredients.
He enjoyed a hot panini while sitting outside in the sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sandwich - two slices of bread with filling between them
  • synonym: toastie - a toasted sandwich, especially in British English
