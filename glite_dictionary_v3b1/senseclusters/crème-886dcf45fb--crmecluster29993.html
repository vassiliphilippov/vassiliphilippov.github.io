crème

noun
thick part of milk
Pronunciation: / krɛm / (AmE), / krɛm / (BrE)
the thick, fatty part of milk that rises to the top and is used in cooking or as a topping

Examples

Crème in coffee
She added a spoonful of crème to her coffee for a richer taste.
The recipe calls for fresh crème to make the sauce smooth and creamy.
At the bakery, they topped the cake with whipped crème and strawberries.
