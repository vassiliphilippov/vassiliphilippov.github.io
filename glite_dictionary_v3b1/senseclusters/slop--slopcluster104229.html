slop

verb
walk through mud or mire
2.9 5.9
Pronunciation: /slɑːp/ (AmE), /slɒp/ (BrE)
to walk through mud or mire, often making a splashing or squelching sound

Examples

To slop through mud

We had to slop through the soggy field to reach the barn.
