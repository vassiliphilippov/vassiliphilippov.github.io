Nicene

adjective
of or relating to Nicaea, ancient city
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪˌsin / (AmE), / nʌɪˈsiːn / (BrE)
of or relating to the ancient city of Nicaea in Asia Minor, especially in reference to the church councils held there

Examples

Nicene church council
Scholars still debate the historical context of the Nicene decisions.
Several important councils were held in the Nicene city during the early centuries.
The Nicene Creed remains a central statement of Christian faith.
