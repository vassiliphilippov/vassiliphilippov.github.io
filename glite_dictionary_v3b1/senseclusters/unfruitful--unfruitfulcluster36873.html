unfruitful

adjective
not productive or fertile
1.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfruːt.fəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfruːt.fəl / (BrE)
not producing good results, crops, or benefits; not productive or fertile

Examples

An unfruitful effort
Despite their efforts, the negotiations proved unfruitful and no agreement was reached.
The land was unfruitful, yielding only a small harvest each season.
Years of unfruitful research discouraged the scientists, but they persisted nonetheless.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fruitful - producing good results or crops; productive or fertile
