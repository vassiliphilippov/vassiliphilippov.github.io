raw

adjective
untreated or unfinished wood/furniture
Pronunciation: / rɔ /; / rɑː /; / rɑ / (AmE), / rɔː / (BrE)
in a natural, unprocessed, or unrefined state; not cooked, treated, or finished

Examples

Raw vegetables

Honey in its raw form still contains pollen and enzymes.
The factory discharges raw sewage into the river, causing pollution.
We import raw materials and manufacture finished goods locally.
Consuming raw eggs can pose a risk of foodborne illness.
She prefers to eat raw carrots as a healthy snack.
The fish was served raw in a traditional Japanese dish.
