  • unrefined
    4.4 0.17/m
    unrefined refers to materials not purified or smoothed, while raw covers any natural or unfinished state, including food.
  • in the raw
    5.4 0.01/m
    in the raw appears as a phrase for unprocessed or unedited things, while raw is the single-word adjective used more widely.
  • uncooked
    1.0 0.69/m
    uncooked describes food not prepared by heat, while raw also means natural or unfinished beyond just being not cooked.
  • crude

    crude feels rough or rude and often lacks skill, while raw focuses on being natural, unprocessed, or not finished.
