resolver

noun
rotary position sensor
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɑːlvər / (AmE), / rɪˈzɒlvə / (BrE)
a rotary electrical device that converts angular position or motion into an electrical signal for precise position feedback in control systems

Examples

Motor resolver
The engineer installed a resolver to provide accurate feedback on the motor's position.
Modern CNC machines often use a resolver for precise control of moving parts.
A faulty resolver can cause errors in the robotic arm's movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encoder - a device that converts position or motion into a coded signal
  • synonym: transducer - a device that converts one form of energy into another
