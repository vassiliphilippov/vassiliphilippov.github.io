mosquito fern

noun
small aquatic floating fern plant
Pronunciation: / məˈskiːtoʊ fɜrn / (AmE), / məˈskiːtəʊ fɜːn / (BrE)
A small free-floating aquatic fern native to the eastern United States and tropical America, often naturalized in other regions.
