Crossref

noun
scholarly metadata service
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌrɛf / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌrɛf / (BrE)
an online organization and database that provides metadata and links for scholarly publications, including DOIs and citation information

Examples

Crossref database access
Many academic publishers register their articles with Crossref to ensure proper citation and discoverability.
You can use Crossref to look up the DOI of a journal article for your bibliography.
Libraries often rely on Crossref to verify the metadata of scholarly publications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: DOI - a unique digital identifier for scholarly works
  • synonym: PubMed - a database of biomedical literature and citations
