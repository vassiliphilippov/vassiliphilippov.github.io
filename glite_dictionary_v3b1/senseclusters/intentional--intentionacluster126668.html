intentional

adjective
done on purpose; deliberate
3.4 3.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈten.ʃə.nəl/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈten.ʃə.nəl/ (BrE)
done on purpose; deliberate, not accidental

Examples

An intentional act
Her actions were intentional and calculated, not the result of a misunderstanding.
The company conducted an intentional review of its hiring policies to ensure fairness.
The lawyer argued that the intentional damage to the property warranted severe penalties.
He made an intentional choice to take the longer but more scenic route home.
Her comments were intentional, meant to provoke a specific reaction from the audience.
The artist's use of color was clearly intentional throughout the painting.
