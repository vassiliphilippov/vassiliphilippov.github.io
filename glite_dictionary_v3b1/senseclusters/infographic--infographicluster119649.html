infographic

adjective
visual information style
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfoʊˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪnfəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
presented in the style of an infographic or designed to display information visually using graphics and text

Examples

Infographic style design
The report included several infographic charts to make the data easier to understand.
She prefers infographic presentations because they combine images and text for quick learning.
The teacher used an infographic worksheet to help students visualize the process.

Related Terms

  • antonym: textual - relating to written or printed words
  • synonym: visual - relating to seeing or sight
