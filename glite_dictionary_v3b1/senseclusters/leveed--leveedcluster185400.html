leveed

verb
to build a levee
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvid / (AmE), / ˈlɛvid / (BrE)
built, reinforced, or surrounded with a levee or embankment to protect from flooding.

Examples

To leveed a river
After the heavy rains, the town quickly leveed the river to prevent flooding.
The engineers leveed the entire stretch of the riverbank last year.
Many low-lying areas have been leveed to protect homes from seasonal floods.
