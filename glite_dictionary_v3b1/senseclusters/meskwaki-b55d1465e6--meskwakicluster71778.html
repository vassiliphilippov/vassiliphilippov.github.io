Meskwaki

noun
Algonquian language
Pronunciation: / mɛsˈkwɑːki / (AmE), / mɛsˈkwɑːki / (BrE)
the Algonquian language spoken by the Meskwaki people, also known as the Fox people, of North America

Examples

Meskwaki language
The Meskwaki language is taught in local schools to help preserve cultural heritage.
She is fluent in both English and Meskwaki, allowing her to communicate with elders in her community.
Linguists have documented many traditional stories in Meskwaki to ensure they are not lost.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Algonquian - a family of Native American languages spoken in North America
