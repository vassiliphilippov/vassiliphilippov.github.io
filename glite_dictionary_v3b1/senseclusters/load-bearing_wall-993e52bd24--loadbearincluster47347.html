load-bearing wall

noun
a wall supporting structural weight
3.6 0.05/m

A wall that supports the weight of parts of a building above it, such as floors or a roof.

Examples

A load-bearing wall
It is dangerous to knock down a load-bearing wall without proper reinforcement.
The blueprints show which load-bearing walls cannot be removed during renovation.
They hired an engineer to ensure the load-bearing wall could support the new extension.

Synonyms
