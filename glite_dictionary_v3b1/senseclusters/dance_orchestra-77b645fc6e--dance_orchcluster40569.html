dance orchestra

noun
musicians playing dance music
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdæns ɔɹˈkɛstɹə / (AmE), / ˈdɑːns ɔːˈkɛstrə / (BrE)
a group of musicians who play popular music specifically for dancing, often at social events or gatherings

Examples

A dance orchestra plays
During the 1930s, swing music was often performed by a dance orchestra.
The hotel ballroom hired a dance orchestra to entertain guests at the annual gala.
They danced late into the night to the lively tunes of the dance orchestra.
