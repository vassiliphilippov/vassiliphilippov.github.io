antiaircraft gun

noun
gun designed to shoot down aircraft
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈɛrkræft ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈeəkrɑːft ɡʌn / (BrE)
A large gun designed to fire at and destroy aircraft from the ground.

Examples

An antiaircraft gun battery
An old antiaircraft gun is displayed in the military museum.
The soldiers manned the antiaircraft gun as enemy planes approached.
They positioned the antiaircraft gun on the hill for better coverage.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cannon - a large, heavy gun typically mounted on wheels
  • synonym: flak - bursting anti-aircraft fire, especially from WWII
