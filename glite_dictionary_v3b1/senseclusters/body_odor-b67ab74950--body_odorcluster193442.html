body odor

noun
unpleasant smell from the body
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdi ˌoʊdər /; / ˈbɑːdi ˌoʊdɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˌəʊdə / (BrE)
An unpleasant smell from the human body, typically caused by perspiration and by bacteria on the skin breaking down sweat; it may be linked to a lack of personal hygiene.

Examples

Body odor smell
During the hot summer months, body odor can become more noticeable.
Regular bathing and deodorant can help prevent body odor.
She was self-conscious about her body odor after exercising at the gym.
He became self-conscious about his body odour after working out at the gym.
Some people use deodorant daily to manage their body odour.
Wearing breathable fabrics can help reduce body odour during hot weather.
