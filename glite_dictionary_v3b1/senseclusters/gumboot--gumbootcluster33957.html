gumboot

noun
rubber waterproof boot
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmˌbuːt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmˌbuːt / (BrE)
a waterproof boot made of rubber, worn to keep feet dry in wet or muddy conditions

Examples

A pair of gumboots
He put on his gumboots before heading out into the rain-soaked garden.
Children love splashing in puddles while wearing their gumboots.
Farmers often rely on sturdy gumboots to keep their feet dry in muddy fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rain boot - a waterproof boot worn in wet weather
  • synonym: wellington boot - a knee-high waterproof rubber boot
