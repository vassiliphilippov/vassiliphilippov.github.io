radius of curvature

noun
radius of best-fitting circle to a curve at a point
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdiəs əv kərˈveɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈreɪdɪəs əv kɜːˈveɪtʃə / (BrE)
the radius of the circle that best approximates a given curve at a particular point, equal to the reciprocal of the curvature at that point

Examples

The radius of curvature value
A smaller radius of curvature indicates a sharper bend in the road.
At each point along the track, engineers calculate the radius of curvature to ensure safety.
The radius of curvature of the lens determines how strongly it bends light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curvature - the amount by which a geometric object deviates from being flat or straight
  • degree lower: radius - a straight line from the center to the circumference of a circle
