Synonyms

  • uplift
    7.0 0.08/m
    uplift is a broad geological rise of land over large areas, while upthrust is a localized tectonic lift tied to mountain building.
  • upthrow
    9.9 0.00/m
    upthrow is a fault-related upward movement of one side of rock, while upthrust is a general tectonic uplift in mountains.
  • upheaval
    7.3 0.08/m
