head game

noun
psychological manipulation tactic
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛd ˌɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈhɛd ˌɡeɪm / (BrE)
an act of psychological manipulation intended to confuse, deceive, or unsettle someone

Examples

A head game of tricks
His threats were just a head game to make her nervous before the interview.
She realized too late that their arguments were all part of his head games.
Stop playing head games and tell me what you really want.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mind game - an action or statement intended to manipulate or confuse someone mentally
