American gray birch

noun
birch tree of eastern North America
6.0
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ɡreɪ bɝːtʃ /; / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ɡreɪ bɜrtʃ / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ɡreɪ bɜːtʃ /; / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ɡreɪ bɜːtʃ / (BrE)
A medium-sized birch tree (Betula populifolia) native to eastern North America, with white or pale grey bark and low-value wood, often found in second-growth forests.
