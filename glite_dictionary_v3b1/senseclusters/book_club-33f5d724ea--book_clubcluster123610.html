book club

noun
club for buying discounted books
1.5
Pronunciation: / bʊk klʌb / (AmE), / bʊk klʌb / (BrE)
an organization that people join to buy selected books at reduced prices, usually offered by mail or online

Examples

A book club membership
I joined a book club to get novels at lower prices.
She received a special edition from her book club subscription.
The book club sent me a new bestseller every month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: literary club - a club for people to discuss books and literature
