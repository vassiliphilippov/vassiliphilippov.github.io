quantizer

noun
signal converter
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːntaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈkwɒntaɪzə / (BrE)
a device or algorithm that converts a continuous signal into a set of discrete values

Examples

Audio quantizer
The audio quantizer reduces the signal to manageable digital levels.
A high-quality quantizer is essential for accurate digital sound reproduction.
Engineers adjusted the quantizer settings to minimize distortion in the processed image.
