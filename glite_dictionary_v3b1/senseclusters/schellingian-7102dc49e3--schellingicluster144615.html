Schellingian

noun
follower of Schelling
Pronunciation: / ʃɛˈlɪŋiən / (AmE), / ʃɛˈlɪŋiən / (BrE)
a person who follows, supports, or studies the philosophical ideas of Friedrich Wilhelm Joseph Schelling

Examples

A Schellingian thinker
As a devoted Schellingian, he often referenced nature as a central theme in his essays.
The conference attracted many Schellingians interested in German idealism and its influence on modern thought.
Her research into absolute idealism marked her as a prominent Schellingian among her peers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Hegelian - a follower of the philosophy of Georg Wilhelm Friedrich Hegel
  • synonym: Kantian - a follower of the philosophy of Immanuel Kant
