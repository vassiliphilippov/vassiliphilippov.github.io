flanging

noun
audio swirling effect
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈflændʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflændʒɪŋ / (BrE)
an audio effect that produces a swirling or whooshing sound by mixing two identical signals with a slight delay

Examples

Flanging audio effect
The producer used flanging to give the vocals a dreamy, swirling quality.
Classic rock songs often feature flanging on the guitar for a psychedelic sound.
You can achieve a unique effect by applying flanging to the drum track.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chorus - an audio effect that thickens sound by mixing delayed copies
  • synonym: phasing - an audio effect that creates a sweeping sound by shifting phase relationships
