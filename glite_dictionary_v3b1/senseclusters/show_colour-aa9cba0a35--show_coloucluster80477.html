show colour

verb
display vibrant colors or express style
Pronunciation: / ʃoʊ ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / ʃəʊ ˈkʌlə / (BrE)

Examples

To show colour brightly

She likes to show colour through her bold fashion choices.
The tulips in the garden will show colour in early April.
