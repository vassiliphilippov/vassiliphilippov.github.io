Serbo-Croatian

noun
South Slavic language of Serbs and Croats
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsɜːr.boʊ kroʊ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈsɜːr.boʊ krəʊ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈsɜː.bəʊ krəʊ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
