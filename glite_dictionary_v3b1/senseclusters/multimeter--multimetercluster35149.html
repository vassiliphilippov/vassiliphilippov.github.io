multimeter

noun
electrical measuring device
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈmʌltɪˌmiːtə / (BrE)
an electronic device used to measure voltage, current, and resistance in electrical circuits

Examples

A multimeter device
The electrician used a multimeter to diagnose the problem in the circuit.
Before repairing the appliance, she checked the fuse with a multimeter.
Learning how to use a multimeter is essential for anyone working with electronics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ammeter - a device for measuring electric current
  • synonym: ohmmeter - a device for measuring electrical resistance
  • synonym: voltmeter - a device for measuring electrical voltage
