swellness

noun
state of being swollen
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛlnəs / (AmE), / ˈswɛlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being swollen or enlarged, especially in a part of the body

Examples

Swellness of the ankle
The doctor checked the swellness of her ankle after the injury.
Ice packs can help reduce the swellness caused by a sprain.
He noticed some swellness around the insect bite on his arm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inflammation - a condition in which a part of the body becomes red, swollen, and painful
  • synonym: swelling - an area of the body that has become larger due to injury or illness
