jeep

noun
four-wheel-drive vehicle for rough terrain
2.0
Pronunciation: / dʒiːp / (AmE), / dʒiːp / (BrE)
a small, sturdy motor vehicle with four-wheel drive, designed for traveling over rough or uneven ground

Examples

A jeep vehicle
After the storm, a jeep was the only vehicle that could reach the remote village.
The muddy road was no problem for the powerful jeep.
They rented a jeep to explore the mountains during their vacation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: SUV - a sport utility vehicle, often used for similar purposes
