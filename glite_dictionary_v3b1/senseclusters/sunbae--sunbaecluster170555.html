sunbae

noun
senior (in Korean hierarchy)
6.6
Pronunciation: / sun-beɪ / (AmE), / sʌn-beɪ / (BrE)
(in Korean culture) a person who is older or more experienced, especially in an educational or professional context.

Examples

A sunbae at work
As a new employee, she relied on her sunbae to help her settle into the job.
He treated his sunbae with great respect, following traditional customs.
In our club, I always seek advice from my sunbae when I face challenges.
