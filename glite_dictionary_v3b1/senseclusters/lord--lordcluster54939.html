lord

verb
to confer lordship upon
Pronunciation: / lɔrd / (AmE), / lɔːd / (BrE)
to give someone the title or rank of a lord; to make someone a lord

Examples

To lord a knight
After decades of dedication to the crown, he was finally lorded and given an estate.
She was lorded by the monarch after her heroic deeds during the war.
The king decided to lord his most trusted advisor for years of loyal service.
