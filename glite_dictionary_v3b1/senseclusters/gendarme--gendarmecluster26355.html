gendarme

noun
French police officer (often military)
6.5
Pronunciation: / ʒɑnˈdɑrm / (AmE), / ʒɒnˈdɑːm / (BrE)
A police officer in France, especially one belonging to a military police force.

Examples

A gendarme officer
After the accident, a gendarme arrived to direct the traffic.
During our trip to Paris, we asked a gendarme for directions.
The museum was guarded by a vigilant gendarme standing at the entrance.
