modularity

noun
separate parts design
6.6
Pronunciation: /mɑːd.jə.ˈlær.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /mɑːd.jʊ.ˈlær.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /mɒd.jʊ.ˈlær.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /mɒd.jə.ˈlær.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
the quality of being made up of separate, interchangeable, or semi-independent parts that can work together as a whole

Examples
