eagle

noun
emblem of power or authority
Pronunciation: / ˈiɡəl / (AmE), / ˈiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
A representation of an eagle used as an emblem of power, authority, or imperial sovereignty.

Examples

An eagle emblem
In medieval heraldry, the eagle was often depicted to signify power and dominance.
The eagle atop the flagpole represents national strength and heritage.
The Roman army marched beneath the eagle, a symbol of their empire's might.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lion - an emblem commonly representing courage or royalty
