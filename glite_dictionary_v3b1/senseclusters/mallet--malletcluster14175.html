mallet

noun
tool with a large head used for striking
4.5 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmæl.ɪt/ /; / /ˈmæ.leɪ/ (AmE), /ˈmæl.ɪt/ /; / /ˈmæ.leɪ/ (BrE)
a hammer-like tool with a large, typically wooden head, used for driving objects or shaping materials without damaging them

Examples

Wooden mallet
A wooden mallet is often used to assemble furniture without damaging it.
She struck the chisel with a mallet to carefully shape the stone.
The chef used a mallet to tenderize the meat before cooking.
