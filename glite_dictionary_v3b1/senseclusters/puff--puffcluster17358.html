puff

verb
praise or promote extravagantly
Pronunciation: / pʌf / (AmE), / pʌf / (BrE)
to praise or promote something or someone in an exaggerated or extravagant manner

Examples

To puff a product
Critics often puff mediocre films with too much enthusiasm.
The author was unfairly puffed in the local press.
The magazine puffed the new product as the best on the market.

Related Terms

  • antonym: criticize - to express disapproval or point out faults
  • synonym: flatter - to praise excessively, often insincerely
