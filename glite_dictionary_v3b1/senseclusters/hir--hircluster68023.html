hir

pronoun
gender-neutral pronoun
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɪr / (AmE), / hɪə / (BrE)
a gender-neutral pronoun used in place of 'him' or 'her' to refer to a person without specifying gender

Examples

Give it to hir
If anyone forgets hir ticket, please let the organizer know at the entrance.
The teacher asked each student to submit hir assignment by Friday.
Jordan prefers that people use hir chosen pronouns in conversation.
