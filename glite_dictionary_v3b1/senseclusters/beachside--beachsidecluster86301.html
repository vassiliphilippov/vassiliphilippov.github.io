beachside

adjective
near the beach
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtʃsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃsaɪd / (BrE)
located next to or very close to a beach

Examples

Beachside hotel
They rented a beachside apartment for their summer vacation.
The beachside restaurants are always busy during the holiday season.
We took a long walk along the beachside path at sunset.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inland - located away from the coast or beach
  • synonym: seaside - located by or near the sea
