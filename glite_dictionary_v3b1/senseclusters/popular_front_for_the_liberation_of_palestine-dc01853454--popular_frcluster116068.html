Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine

proper noun
Palestinian militant Marxist-Leninist organization (founded 1967)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑpjələr frʌnt fər ðə ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən əv ˌpæləsˈtaɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊlə frʌnt fə ðə ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən əv ˌpælɪsˈtiːn / (BrE)
a Palestinian Marxist-Leninist organization, founded in 1967, known for combining Palestinian nationalism with leftist ideology and for its use of militant actions against Israel
