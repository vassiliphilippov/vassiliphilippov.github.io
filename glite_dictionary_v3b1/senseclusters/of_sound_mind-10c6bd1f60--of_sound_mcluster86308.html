of sound mind

adjective
legally mentally competent
2.8 5.3
Pronunciation: /əv saʊnd maɪnd/ /; / /ə saʊnd maɪnd/ (AmE), /əv saʊnd maɪnd/ /; / /ə saʊnd maɪnd/ (BrE)
having full mental capacity and able to think, understand, and make decisions clearly, especially as required by law.

Examples

Of sound mind and memory
In her will, she declared herself of sound mind and memory at the time of signing.
The court must determine if the defendant is of sound mind before proceeding with the trial.
The doctor testified that the patient was not of sound mind when the agreement was made.
