seat belt

noun
belt for safety in vehicles
Pronunciation: / ˈsit ˌbɛlt / (AmE), / ˈsiːt ˌbɛlt / (BrE)
A safety belt in a car, plane, or other vehicle that keeps a person in their seat and helps protect them in the event of an accident or sudden stop.

Examples

Fasten the seat belt
Always fasten your seat belt before the car starts moving.
The flight attendant checked to ensure everyone’s seat belt was securely fastened.
Wearing a seat belt can significantly reduce the risk of injury in an accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: safety belt - belt device used to hold someone safely in a seat
