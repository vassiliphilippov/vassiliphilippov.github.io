guayacan

noun
aromatic tree resin
Pronunciation: / ˌgwaɪəˈkɑːn / (AmE), / ˌgwaɪəˈkæn / (BrE)
a dark, aromatic resin or gum obtained from the guayacan tree, formerly used in medicine and for varnishes

Examples

Guayacan resin finish
The craftsman used guayacan to create a glossy finish on the wooden sculpture.
In the past, doctors sometimes prescribed guayacan as a remedy for certain illnesses.
Collectors value old bottles of guayacan for their historical significance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gum - a sticky substance from plants used in products or medicine
  • synonym: resin - a solid or semi-solid organic substance from plants, often aromatic
