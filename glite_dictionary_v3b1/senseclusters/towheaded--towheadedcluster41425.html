towheaded

adjective
having very light blond hair
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈtəʊˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
(of a person’s hair) having very light blond or almost white hair.

Examples

Towheaded child
As a child, she was so towheaded that people often mistook her for being albino.
Several towheaded children played on the beach, their hair almost white against the sand.
The little boy was towheaded, his pale hair shining in the sunlight.
