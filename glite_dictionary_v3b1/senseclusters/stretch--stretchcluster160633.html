stretch

verb
extend limbs or body
3.3 1.5
Pronunciation: / stɹɛtʃ / (AmE), / stɹɛtʃ / (BrE)
to extend your body or a part of your body to its full length, especially to make your muscles less tight or for exercise

Examples

To stretch your arms
After sitting at his desk for hours, Tom stood up to stretch his legs.
It's important to stretch before and after exercising to avoid injury.
She reached upward, stretching her arms toward the ceiling with a satisfied sigh.
After sitting at his desk for hours, he stood up and stretched his arms above his head.
Every morning, she takes a moment to stretch before starting her run.
The cat loves to stretch out on the sunny windowsill.

  • synonym: reach - to extend your arm or hand in order to touch or get something
