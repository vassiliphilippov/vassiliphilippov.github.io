shit out

verb
produce something quickly or carelessly (vulgar, slang)
Pronunciation: / ʃɪt aʊt / (AmE), / ʃɪt aʊt / (BrE)
To produce or express something quickly and carelessly, often with little effort or attention to quality.

Examples

She shits out ideas all day, but rarely follows through with them.
