schoolie

noun
schoolchild or pupil
Pronunciation: / ˈskuːli / (AmE), / ˈskuːli / (BrE)
a pupil or schoolchild, especially one attending primary or secondary school

Examples

A young schoolie
The schoolie nervously arranged her books before entering the classroom for the first day.
Every schoolie wore a uniform and carried a heavy backpack to class.
During the field trip, the schoolies listened carefully to the guide's instructions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: student - a person who is studying at a school or college
  • antonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school
