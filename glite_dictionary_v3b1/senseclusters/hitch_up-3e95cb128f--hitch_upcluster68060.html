hitch up

verb
pull up, especially clothing
7.8
Pronunciation: / hɪtʃ ʌp / (AmE), / hɪtʃ ʌp / (BrE)
to pull something, especially a piece of clothing, upwards to a higher position on the body

Examples

To hitch up trousers

She hitched up her skirt so she could wade through the stream.
The boy hitched up his backpack higher onto his shoulders.
