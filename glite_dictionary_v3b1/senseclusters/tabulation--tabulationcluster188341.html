tabulation

noun
information arranged in table form
Pronunciation: / ˌtæbjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtabjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of arranging information in a table, or the information arranged in this way

Examples

Careful tabulation of results
Careful tabulation of the results revealed several interesting patterns.
Her report included a concise tabulation of survey responses.
The sales data was presented in a clear tabulation for the board meeting.
Accurate tabulation is essential for compiling scientific data.
Election officials are responsible for the tabulation of all votes cast.
The tabulation of survey results provided clear insight into public opinion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: table - an arrangement of data in rows and columns
