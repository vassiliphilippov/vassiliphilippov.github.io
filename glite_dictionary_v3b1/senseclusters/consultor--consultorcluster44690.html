consultor

noun
expert adviser
8.3
Pronunciation: / kənˈsʌltər / (AmE), / kənˈsʌltə / (BrE)
a person who gives expert advice, especially in official or religious matters

Examples

A consultor gives advice
The board hired a consultor to help develop their new strategy.
In the church, a consultor is often called upon for important decisions.
As a legal consultor, she provided valuable guidance to the organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advisor - someone who gives advice in a particular field
  • synonym: consultant - a person who provides expert advice professionally
