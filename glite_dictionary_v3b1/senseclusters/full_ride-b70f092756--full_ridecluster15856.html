full ride

noun
scholarship covering all costs
Pronunciation: / fʊl raɪd / (AmE), / fʊl raɪd / (BrE)
a scholarship that covers all educational costs, including tuition, fees, and often room and board

Examples

A full ride scholarship
He earned a full ride to college thanks to his academic achievements.
Many students dream of receiving a full ride to their top-choice school.
The university offered her a full ride for her outstanding athletic abilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scholarship - financial aid awarded to a student
