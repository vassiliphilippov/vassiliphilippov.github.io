Goodman

proper noun
surname or archaic title
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʊdmən / (AmE), / ˈɡʊdmən / (BrE)
a surname or, in archaic use, a respectful title for a man, similar to 'Mister'

Examples

Mr Goodman
The letter was addressed to Goodman Brown, who lived at the edge of the village.
In old English literature, a man might be called Goodman as a sign of respect.
The family name Goodman has been passed down for generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mister - a polite title used before a man's surname or full name
