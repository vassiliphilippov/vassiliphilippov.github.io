bubblejet

noun
type of inkjet printer using heated ink bubbles
6.3 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌb.əl.dʒet/ (AmE), /ˈbʌb.əl.dʒet/ (BrE)
a type of inkjet printer that uses heat to create bubbles of ink, which are then sprayed onto paper to form an image or text

Examples

A bubblejet printer
Canon was one of the first companies to popularize bubblejet technology.
Many home users prefer bubblejet printers for their affordable, high-quality color printing.
The office replaced its old laser printer with a new bubblejet model.

Related Terms
