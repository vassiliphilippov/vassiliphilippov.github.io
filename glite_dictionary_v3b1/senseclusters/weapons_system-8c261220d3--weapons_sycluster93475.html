weapons system

noun
collective group of weapons in a force
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛpənz ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈwɛpənz ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
all weapons of a particular military force or organization considered collectively as a unit, especially their integrated use and design.

Examples

Each component of the weapons system must work seamlessly together for maximum effectiveness.
The military unveiled its latest weapons system designed for modern combat operations.
Upgrading the entire weapons system will enhance the army's defensive capabilities.
