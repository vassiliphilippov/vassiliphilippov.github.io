nigiri

noun
sushi with fish on rice
7.0 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / niÀà…°iri / (AmE), / niÀà…°iÀêri / (BrE)
a type of sushi made with a slice of fish or seafood placed on top of a small ball of vinegared rice

Examples

Nigiri sushi
At the Japanese restaurant, I ordered tuna nigiri for my appetizer.
He prefers nigiri over maki because he likes the taste of fresh fish on rice.
The chef carefully shaped the rice before placing the salmon on the nigiri.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maki - sushi rolled with rice and seaweed, often with fish or vegetables
  • antonym: sashimi - thinly sliced raw fish or seafood, served without rice
