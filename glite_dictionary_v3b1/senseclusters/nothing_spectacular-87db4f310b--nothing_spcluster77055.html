nothing spectacular

adjective
not impressive; unremarkable
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌθɪŋ spɛkˈtækjələr / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ spɛkˈtakjʊlə / (BrE)
not particularly impressive or remarkable; ordinary or unexceptional.

Examples

Nothing spectacular to see
Her grades were solid, nothing spectacular, but enough to get into college.
The movie was entertaining, although the ending was nothing spectacular.
The restaurant's decor was clean but nothing spectacular.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mediocre - of only average quality; not very good
  • synonym: ordinary - with no special or distinctive features; normal
  • antonym: spectacular - impressive or strikingly large or obvious
