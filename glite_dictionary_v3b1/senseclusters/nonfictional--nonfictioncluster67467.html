nonfictional

adjective
not fictional; factual or real
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈfɪkʃənl̩ / (AmE), / nɒnˈfɪkʃənl / (BrE)
Not invented or imaginary; based on facts or real events.

Examples

A nonfictional story
Her latest book is a nonfictional account of her experiences as a journalist.
I prefer watching nonfictional documentaries to fictional movies.
The museum features nonfictional exhibits that tell the true story of the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: factual - concerned with what is actually the case rather than interpretations
  • antonym: fictional - invented or imaginary; not real
