Examples

To be under the influence of alcohol

Synonyms

  • intoxicated
    4.2 1.82/m
    intoxicated is caused by alcohol or drugs and is used in medical or legal contexts, while under the influence means impaired and possibly illegal.
  • drunk
    1.6 42/m
    drunk describes someone who drank too much alcohol and shows clear loss of control, while under the influence covers alcohol or drugs and legal impairment.
  • inebriated
    6.5 0.34/m
    inebriated is a more formal or polite word for being drunk after drinking alcohol, while under the influence refers to legal or functional impairment.
