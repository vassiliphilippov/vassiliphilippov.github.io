release

a legal document or formal statement giving up a right, claim, or property

Examples

A legal release
Before closing on the sale, they obtained a release from the previous lienholder.
The debtor signed a release confirming that all obligations had been satisfied.
The lawyer prepared a release for the settlement of the client's debt.
All participants are required to submit a release form before joining the event.

Synonyms

  • waiver
    6.0 6.28/m
    waiver is a written statement where someone freely gives up a right, while release is a legal document freeing a claim.
  • quitclaim
    9.9 0.05/m
    quitclaim is a property document that transfers any claim without guarantee, while release is a general legal statement giving up rights.
  • renunciation
    8.2 0.37/m
    renunciation is the formal act of giving up a right or possession, while release is usually a signed legal document.
  • discharge
    5.2 8.66/m
    discharge is an official freeing from duty or obligation and is often recorded, while release is a document giving up a right.
  • acquittance
    9.9 0.00/m
    acquittance is a written receipt showing a debt or obligation is paid and ended, while release is a broader legal document.
  • quittance
    9.9 0.00/m
    quittance is a formal receipt that shows someone is freed from a debt and sounds old-fashioned, while release is a legal document.
