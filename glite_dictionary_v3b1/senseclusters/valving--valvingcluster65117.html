valving

noun
valve arrangement
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvælvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvælvɪŋ / (BrE)
the design, arrangement, or adjustment of valves in a machine or device that affects its performance

Examples

Engine valving system
The engineer improved the engine's performance by modifying the valving system.
Proper valving is essential for smooth operation in hydraulic machinery.
Adjusting the valving can change how a shock absorber responds to different road conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tuning - the adjustment of a machine or device for better performance
  • synonym: valve - a device that controls the flow of a fluid or gas
