grope for

verb
search with hands in the dark
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊp fɔr / (AmE), / ɡrəʊp fə / (BrE)
to search for something by feeling with the hands, often in the dark or when visibility is poor

Examples

To grope for a light
During the power outage, everyone was groping for their phones to use as flashlights.
He groped for his glasses on the nightstand, half asleep.
She had to grope for the light switch in the pitch-black room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: search for - to try to find something by looking or otherwise seeking
