shoo away

verb
drive away by saying 'shoo'
Pronunciation: / ʃu əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ʃuː əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to make someone or something leave a place by saying 'shoo' or making a similar gesture or noise

Examples

To shoo away birds
He waved his hand to shoo away the curious children.
She tried to shoo away the birds from the windowsill.
The gardener shooed away the stray cat from the flower beds.
