preshrink

verb
to prevent fabric shrinkage
4.8
Pronunciation: / priːˈʃrɪŋk / (AmE), / priːˈʃrɪŋk / (BrE)
to wash or treat fabric or garments before use or sale to prevent them from shrinking later
