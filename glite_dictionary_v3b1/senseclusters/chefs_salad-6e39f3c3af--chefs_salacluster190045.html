chef's salad

noun
salad with meats, cheese, and eggs
2.7
Pronunciation: / ʃɛfs ˈsæləd / (AmE), / ʃɛfs ˈsaləd / (BrE)
a salad typically consisting of mixed greens, meats such as ham or turkey, cheese, hard-boiled eggs, and assorted vegetables, often served as a main course

Examples

A chef's salad bowl
For lunch, I ordered a chef's salad because I wanted something light but filling.
She prepared a big chef's salad for the family picnic.
The restaurant's signature chef's salad comes with turkey, ham, cheese, and hard-boiled eggs.
