unstruck

adjective
not hit or touched
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstrʌk / (AmE), / ʌnˈstrʌk / (BrE)
not hit, touched, or affected by a blow or impact

Examples

Unstruck bell
The ancient bell remained unstruck for decades, gathering dust in the old tower.
His face was the only one unstruck by the flying snowballs during the game.
The match lay unstruck on the table, as no one wanted to light the candle.

Related Terms

  • antonym: struck - hit or affected by a blow
  • synonym: untouched - not affected or changed by something
