normed

adjective
having a defined norm
9.1
Pronunciation: / nɔrmd / (AmE), / nɔːmd / (BrE)
having a norm defined, especially referring to a mathematical object such as a vector space equipped with a function that assigns lengths or sizes

Examples

Normed vector space
A normed space allows mathematicians to measure the size of vectors in a consistent way.
The concept of a normed vector space is fundamental in functional analysis.
In their research, they worked with several normed linear spaces to prove the theorem.
