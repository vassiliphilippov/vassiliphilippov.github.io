exotic pet

noun
non-native or unusual animal kept as a pet
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɑtɪk pɛt / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɒtɪk pɛt / (BrE)
an animal that is unusual or not native to the area, kept at home as a pet

Examples

A exotic pet lizard
After researching, she realized caring for an exotic pet requires special knowledge.
Local laws sometimes restrict which kinds of exotic pets can be owned.
Some people keep an exotic pet like a snake or a parrot instead of a dog or cat.
