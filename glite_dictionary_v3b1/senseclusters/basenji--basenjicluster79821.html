basenji

noun
African breed of dog that does not bark
Pronunciation: / bəˈsɛn.dʒi / (AmE), / bəˈsɛn.dʒi / (BrE)
a small, smooth-haired breed of dog from Africa, known for its tightly curled tail and inability to bark

Examples

A basenji dog
She adopted a basenji from the local animal shelter last year.
The basenji has a distinctive curly tail and is very intelligent.
The basenji is often called the 'barkless dog' because it does not bark like other breeds.
