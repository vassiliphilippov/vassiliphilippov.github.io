unfeminist

adjective
not supporting feminism
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɛmɪnɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɛmɪnɪst / (BrE)
not supporting or not in line with feminist ideas or principles

Examples

Unfeminist opinion
Many considered the politician's comments to be unfeminist and outdated.
The movie was criticized for its unfeminist portrayal of female characters.
She argued that the policy changes were unfeminist and harmful to gender equality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: feminist - supporting or relating to feminism
  • synonym: sexist - showing prejudice or discrimination based on sex
