abortion-inducing drug

noun
drug that causes abortion
7.5
Pronunciation: / əˈbɔrʃən ɪnˈdusɪŋ drʌɡ / (AmE), / əˈbɔːʃən ɪnˈdjuːsɪŋ drʌɡ / (BrE)
a drug or chemical agent that causes an abortion by terminating a pregnancy

Examples

An abortion-inducing drug
Doctors may prescribe an abortion-inducing drug during the early stages of pregnancy.
Some states have restricted access to abortion-inducing drugs in recent years.
The safety and regulation of abortion-inducing drugs are frequently debated topics in healthcare.
