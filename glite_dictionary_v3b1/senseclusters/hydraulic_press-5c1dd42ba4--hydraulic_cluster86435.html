hydraulic press

noun
machine that uses fluid pressure to press materials
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /haɪ.ˈdrɔː.lɪk pres/ (AmE), /haɪ.ˈdrɒ.lɪk pres/ (BrE)
A machine using a liquid, usually oil or water, to multiply force and press or shape materials.

Examples

A hydraulic press machine
A hydraulic press can apply much more pressure than a mechanical one of the same size.
He operated a hydraulic press to crush the old car for recycling.
The hydraulic press is used to shape metal parts with great force.

Related Terms

  • synonym: press - a machine for applying pressure to form or shape something
