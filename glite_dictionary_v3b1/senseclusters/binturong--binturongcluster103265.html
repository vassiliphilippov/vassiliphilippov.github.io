binturong

noun
large arboreal Asian civet; bearcat
Pronunciation: / bɪnˈtʊrɒŋ /; / bɪnˈtʊr.ɒŋ / (AmE), / bɪnˈtʊərɒŋ /; / bɪnˈtʊər.ɒŋ / (BrE)
