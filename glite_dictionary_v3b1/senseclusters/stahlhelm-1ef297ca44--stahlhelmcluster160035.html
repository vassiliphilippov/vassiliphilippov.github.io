Stahlhelm

proper noun
German veterans' group
Pronunciation: / ˈʃtɑːlˌhɛlm / (AmE), / ˈʃtɑːlˌhɛlm / (BrE)
a German nationalist veterans' and paramilitary organization formed after World War I and active during the Weimar Republic

Examples

Stahlhelm veterans group
Many former soldiers joined the Stahlhelm to find a sense of belonging after World War I.
The Stahlhelm played a significant role in German politics during the Weimar period.
Historians often discuss the influence of the Stahlhelm on the rise of nationalist movements in Germany.
