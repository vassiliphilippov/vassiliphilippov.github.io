Basque

proper noun
ethnic group or language
Pronunciation: / bæsk / (AmE), / bɑːsk / (BrE)
a member of a people from a region in northern Spain and southwestern France, or their unique language

Examples

Basque people
The Basque people have a rich cultural heritage and unique traditions.
She is fluent in both Spanish and Basque after living in the region for years.
Many linguists are fascinated by the origins of the Basque language.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Catalan - a person from Catalonia or their language
  • antonym: Spanish - a person from Spain or the Spanish language
