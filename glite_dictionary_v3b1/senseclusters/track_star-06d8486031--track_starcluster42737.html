track star

noun
outstanding athlete in track and field
2.6
Pronunciation: / træk stɑr / (AmE), / træk stɑː / (BrE)
A highly successful or outstanding athlete in track and field sports, particularly one who excels in running events.

Examples

A track star athlete
After breaking the national record, she quickly gained fame as a track star.
He balanced academics and athletics to become a celebrated track star in college.
The high school hopes their new track star will win the state championship this year.
