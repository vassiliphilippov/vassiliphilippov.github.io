stoked

verb
to add fuel to fire
Pronunciation: / stoʊkt / (AmE), / stəʊkt / (BrE)
added fuel to a fire or tended it so that it burned more strongly

Examples

Stoked the fire
After collecting kindling, he stoked the campfire until the flames burned steadily.
She carefully stoked the fireplace to keep the room warm throughout the night.
The old caretaker stoked the furnace every morning during the winter months.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extinguish - to put out a fire
  • synonym: fuel - to supply with material to burn
