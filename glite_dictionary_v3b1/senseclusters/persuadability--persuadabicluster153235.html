persuadability

noun
ease of being convinced
7.6
Pronunciation: /pɜːr.sweɪ.də.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ /; / /pɜːr.suː.ə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /pə.sweɪ.də.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ /; / /pə.suː.ə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (BrE)
the quality or degree of being easily convinced or influenced by others

Examples

High persuadability
