certify

verb
guarantee payment of a check

(of a bank) to guarantee the payment of a check by verifying that sufficient funds are available and stamping the check as certified.

Examples

To certify a check
Before mailing the payment, he asked the teller to certify the check to reassure the landlord.
Once the bank certifies a check, the funds are set aside until it is cashed.
The bank will certify your check if you request it at the counter.
