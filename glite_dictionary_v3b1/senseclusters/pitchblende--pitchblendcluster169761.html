pitchblende

noun
a mineral ore of uranium
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtʃˌblɛnd / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃˌblɛnd / (BrE)
A mineral form of uraninite, consisting chiefly of uranium oxide; the principal ore of uranium.

Examples

The pitchblende mineral
Large deposits of pitchblende were discovered in the Czech Republic in the 19th century.
Marie Curie extracted radium from pitchblende during her pioneering research.
The mine produces significant amounts of pitchblende for use in nuclear energy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: uraninite - A mineral composed largely of uranium oxide
