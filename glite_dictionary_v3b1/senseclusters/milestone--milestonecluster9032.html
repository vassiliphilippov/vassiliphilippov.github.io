milestone

noun
important event or stage
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪlstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈmaɪlstəʊn / (BrE)
an important event or achievement in the development of something, especially in a person's life or a project

Examples

A major milestone
Buying their first house was a significant milestone for the young couple.
Graduating from college was a major milestone in her life.
The company reached an important milestone when it launched its first product.
Graduating from university was a major milestone in his life.
Learning to walk is a developmental milestone for babies.
The launch of their new product marked an important milestone for the company.
