sweetener

noun
a substance that makes food sweeter
Pronunciation: / ˈswiːt̬ənər / (AmE), / ˈswiːtənə / (BrE)
a substance added to food or drink to make it taste sweeter, especially in place of sugar

Examples

Artificial sweetener
Honey can be used as a sweetener in baking recipes.
Many diet drinks contain an artificial sweetener to reduce calories.
She prefers using a natural sweetener instead of sugar in her coffee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artificial sweetener - substance used instead of sugar to sweeten foods or drinks
  • synonym: sugar - sweet crystalline substance used as a sweetener
