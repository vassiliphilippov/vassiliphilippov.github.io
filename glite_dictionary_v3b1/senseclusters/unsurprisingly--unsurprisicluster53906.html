unsurprisingly

adverb
as expected
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsərˈpraɪzɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəˈpraɪzɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is not surprising or as one would expect

Examples

Unsurprisingly, the result
Unsurprisingly, the team won the match after leading the entire season.
The movie was, unsurprisingly, a huge success given the popular cast.
Unsurprisingly, traffic was heavy during the holiday weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expectedly - in a way that is expected
  • synonym: predictably - in a way that can be predicted or expected
  • antonym: surprisingly - in a way that causes surprise or is unexpected
