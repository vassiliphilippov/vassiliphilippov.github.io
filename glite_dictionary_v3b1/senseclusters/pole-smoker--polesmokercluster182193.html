pole-smoker

noun
derogatory slang for a homosexual man
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊl ˌsmoʊkər / (AmE), / ˈpəʊl ˌsməʊkə / (BrE)
(offensive, vulgar, slang) A derogatory term for a homosexual man or someone regarded as overly flamboyant or sexual.

Examples

A pole-smoker insult
He was unfairly labeled a pole-smoker by his classmates.
Please avoid calling anyone a pole-smoker; it is highly offensive.
The comedian was criticized for using the term pole-smoker in his routine.
