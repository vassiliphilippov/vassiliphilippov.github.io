glamourless

adjective
without charm
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈglæm.ər.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈglæm.ə.ləs/ (BrE)
lacking glamour, charm, excitement, or attractiveness; plain or unromantic

Examples
