glamourless

adjective
without charm
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæmərləs / (AmE), / ˈɡlæmələs / (BrE)
lacking glamour, charm, excitement, or attractiveness; plain or unromantic

Examples

A glamourless office
The office building was functional but completely glamourless in appearance.
Despite its glamourless setting, the restaurant served excellent food.
She preferred a simple, glamourless lifestyle away from the spotlight.
The film offers a glamourless look at the daily struggles of factory workers.
Her glamourless outfit stood out among the sparkling dresses at the party.
He described his childhood in a glamourless but honest way.
