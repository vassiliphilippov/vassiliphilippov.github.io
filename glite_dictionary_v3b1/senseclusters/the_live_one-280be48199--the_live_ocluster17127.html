the live one

noun
the most entertaining person in a group
Pronunciation: / ðə laɪv wʌn / (AmE), / ðə laɪv wʌn / (BrE)
the most lively or entertaining person in a group, often the source of excitement or fun

Examples

The live one in group
At every family reunion, Grandpa was the live one, keeping everyone entertained with his antics.
Whenever we go out, Mark is always the live one, telling stories and cracking jokes.
You can count on her to be the live one at any gathering, making sure everyone has a good time.

Related Terms
