summoner

noun
one who calls spirits
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmənər / (AmE), / ˈsʌmənə / (BrE)
a person or character who calls forth spirits, creatures, or magical forces, especially in stories, games, or by using spells

Examples

A summoner casts spells
The summoner raised his staff and called forth a mighty dragon to aid the heroes.
In many fantasy novels, a summoner can control magical creatures to fight in battles.
Players often choose the summoner class for its unique ability to bring allies into the game.
The summoner raised his staff and began chanting in the ancient language.
In the story, a powerful summoner brings mythical creatures to aid the heroes.
Legends say that only a skilled summoner can control the spirits they call forth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conjurer - a person who performs magic or calls up spirits
  • synonym: necromancer - a person who claims to communicate with the dead
