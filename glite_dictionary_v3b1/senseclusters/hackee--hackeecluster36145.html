hackee

noun
eastern North American chipmunk
Pronunciation: / ˈhæki / (AmE), / ˈhæki / (BrE)
A small striped squirrel native to eastern North America, known for its cheek pouches; commonly called the eastern chipmunk.

Examples

The hackee squirrel
A hackee gathered acorns and stored them in its burrow for winter.
The striped hackee is common in eastern American woodlands.
We saw a hackee darting across the forest floor, its cheeks stuffed with seeds.
