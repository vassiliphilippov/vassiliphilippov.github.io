tear-stained

adjective
marked by tears
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪrˌsteɪnd / (AmE), / ˈtɪəˌsteɪnd / (BrE)
marked or wet with tears, showing signs of having cried

Examples

Tear-stained cheeks
He noticed her tear-stained cheeks as she tried to smile.
The tear-stained pillow showed she had been crying all night.
She clutched a tear-stained photograph, remembering happier times.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dry-eyed - not crying or showing tears
  • synonym: tearful - full of or showing tears
