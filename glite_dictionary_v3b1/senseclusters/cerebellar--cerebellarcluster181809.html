cerebellar

adjective
relating to the cerebellum of the brain
4.4 6.3
Pronunciation: /se.rə.ˈbel.ər/ (AmE), /se.rə.ˈbel.ə/ (BrE)
relating to or associated with the cerebellum, a part of the brain that controls coordination and balance.

Examples

Cerebellar artery
A stroke affecting the cerebellar artery can lead to dizziness and difficulty walking.
Problems with balance and coordination can indicate cerebellar dysfunction.
The doctor noted a cerebellar lesion in the MRI scan results.
