breakdancing

verb
to perform street dance
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkˌdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkˌdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
to perform energetic street dance moves that involve acrobatics, spins, and footwork, typical of breakdancing style

Examples

She learned breakdancing from online videos and practiced every weekend.
