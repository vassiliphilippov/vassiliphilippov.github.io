abyme

noun
recursive image or story
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈbiːm / (AmE), / əˈbiːm / (BrE)
a recursive or self-reflective image or story within itself, especially in art or literature

Examples

Abyme in art
The novel uses an abyme by including a story within a story.
A painting that shows itself being painted is an example of abyme in art.
The film's plot creates an abyme effect by repeating scenes within scenes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mise en abyme - a formal technique of placing a copy of an image within itself
  • synonym: recursion - the process of repeating items in a self-similar way
