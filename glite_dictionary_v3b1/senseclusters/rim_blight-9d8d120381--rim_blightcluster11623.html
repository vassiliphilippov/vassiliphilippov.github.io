rim blight

noun
disease affecting edges of tea leaves
Pronunciation: / rɪm blaɪt / (AmE), / rɪm blaɪt / (BrE)
a disease of tea plants marked by yellowing and browning of the leaf margins

Examples

Rim blight disease
Rim blight can significantly reduce the yield of tea crops if not managed properly.
Farmers are advised to inspect their plantations regularly for signs of rim blight.
The yellowing and browning of leaf margins are early symptoms of rim blight in tea plants.
