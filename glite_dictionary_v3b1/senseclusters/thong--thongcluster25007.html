thong

noun
type of backless sandal
4.9
Pronunciation: / θɔŋ / (AmE), / θɒŋ / (BrE)
A backless sandal held to the foot by a strap passing between the big toe and the second toe.

Examples

Beach thong sandals
During the summer, many people wear thongs to the beach.
He lost one of his thongs while walking along the boardwalk.
She slipped on her thongs before stepping outside.
