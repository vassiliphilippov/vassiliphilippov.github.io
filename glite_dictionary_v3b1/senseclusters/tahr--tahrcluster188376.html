tahr

noun
Asian wild goat
Pronunciation: / tɑr / (AmE), / tɑː / (BrE)
a large wild goat with curved horns, native to the mountains of southern Asia

Examples

The Himalayan tahr
The Himalayan tahr is known for its thick reddish-brown coat and curved horns.
We spotted a group of tahr climbing effortlessly along the steep cliffs.
Conservation efforts are helping to protect the endangered tahr in its natural habitat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goat - a domesticated or wild animal with horns and a beard
  • synonym: ibex - a wild mountain goat with long, curved horns
