jointed

adjective
having joints or jointed segments
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪntɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪntɪd / (BrE)
having one or more joints or jointed segments; consisting of or provided with joints.

Examples

Jointed legs
Crabs have jointed legs that help them walk sideways on the sand.
She admired the jointed arms of the action figure, which could bend in many directions.
The centipede has a jointed body that allows it to move easily through narrow spaces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: jointless - without joints or jointed segments
