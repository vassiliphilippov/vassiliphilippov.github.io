biparentally

adverb
with both parents
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪpəˈrɛntəli / (AmE), / ˌbaɪpəˈrɛntəli / (BrE)
in a way that involves both parents sharing responsibility for raising or caring for a child

Examples

Biparentally raised children
The twins were biparentally cared for, with both parents taking equal roles in their upbringing.
In some cultures, children are not always raised biparentally due to various family structures.
The study compared children raised biparentally with those raised by a single parent.
