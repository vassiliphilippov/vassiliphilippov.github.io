Relating to or ordered by the pope or the papacy, regarded as the successor of the Apostles.

Examples

Apostolic authority
Certain privileges may only be granted through apostolic authority within the Catholic Church.
He requested an apostolic blessing for the new cathedral.
The order was given in an apostolic letter signed by the pope himself.
