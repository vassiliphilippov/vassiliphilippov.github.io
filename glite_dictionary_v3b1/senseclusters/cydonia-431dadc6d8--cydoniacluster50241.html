Cydonia

noun
genus of trees including quince
9.9
Pronunciation: / saɪˈdoʊniə / (AmE), / sɪˈdəʊniə / (BrE)
A genus of small trees in the rose family, best known for the quince (Cydonia oblonga).

Examples

Cydonia oblonga tree
Cydonia oblonga is widely cultivated for its aromatic fruit, known as quince.
Botanists often refer to the quince tree by its scientific name, Cydonia.
The genus Cydonia includes only one living species today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quince - the edible fruit of Cydonia oblonga
