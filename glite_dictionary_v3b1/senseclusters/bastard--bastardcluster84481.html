bastard

noun
variation that is irregular or inferior
Pronunciation: / ˈbæs.tɚd / (AmE), / ˈbɑː.stəd / (BrE)
A something irregular, inferior, or not genuine; a variation of dubious origin, often viewed as not authentic.

Examples

Bastard version
He criticized the device as a bastard version of the original, filled with cheap materials.
Some linguists consider the dialect a bastard offspring of several regional languages.
The villa featured a bastard form of Baroque design, lacking the style’s usual elegance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genuine - authentic; actually being what it is claimed to be
  • synonym: imitation - a copy or reproduction that is not genuine; made to look like something else
