nonindependent

adjective
not self-sufficient
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɪndɪˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɪndɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
not independent; unable to act, function, or exist alone without support or control from others

Examples

Nonindependent organization
The organization is considered nonindependent because it relies on funding from a parent company.
A nonindependent committee must seek approval before making any major decisions.
Some students are still nonindependent and need help managing their daily routines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dependent - relying on someone or something else for support
  • antonym: independent - able to act or function alone without help
