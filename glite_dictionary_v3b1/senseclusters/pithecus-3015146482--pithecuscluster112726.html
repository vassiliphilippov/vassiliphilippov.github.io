Pithecus

noun
ape genus name
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɪˈθiːkəs / (AmE), / pɪˈθiːkəs / (BrE)
a genus name used in scientific classification for certain apes or monkeys

Examples

Genus Pithecus
The fossil was classified under the genus Pithecus by the research team.
Scientists debated whether the newly discovered skull belonged to Pithecus or another genus.
In early taxonomy, Pithecus was used for several species of ancient apes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Homo - the genus that includes modern humans and their close relatives
  • synonym: Pan - the genus that includes chimpanzees and bonobos
