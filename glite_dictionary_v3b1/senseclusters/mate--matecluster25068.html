mate

verb
chess term: defeat an opponent by checkmate
8.0 5.7
Pronunciation: /meɪt/ (AmE), /meɪt/ (BrE)
(in chess) to put an opponent's king in a position from which it cannot escape capture, thus ending the game.

Examples

To mate the king
After a brilliant sequence, she mated her opponent in only 25 moves.
If you are not careful, he will mate you before you realize your mistake.
The grandmaster mated his rival with a stunning rook sacrifice at the end.
