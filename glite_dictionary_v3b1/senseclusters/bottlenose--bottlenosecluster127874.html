bottlenose

noun
type of dolphin with rounded forehead
7.4 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑː.təl.noʊz/ (AmE), /ˈbɒt.əl.nəʊz/ (BrE)
a dolphin with a rounded forehead and a well-developed beak, found mainly in the northern Atlantic and Mediterranean.

Examples

The bottlenose is easily recognized by its rounded forehead and prominent beak.
