unexpected roadblock

noun
sudden obstacle or delay
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪkˈspɛktɪd ˈroʊdˌblɑk / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈspɛktɪd ˈrəʊdblɒk / (BrE)
an obstacle, difficulty, or delay that occurs without warning and interrupts progress

Examples

An unexpected roadblock appears
Her project hit an unexpected roadblock when the team lost their main investor.
The negotiations faced an unexpected roadblock, causing significant delays in the process.
We encountered an unexpected roadblock during our drive and had to find an alternate route.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obstacle - something that blocks one's way or hinders progress
  • synonym: roadblock - a barrier or obstacle on a road
