economizer

noun
energy-saving device
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkɑːnəˌmaɪzər / (AmE), / ɪˈkɒnəˌmaɪzə / (BrE)
a device that reduces energy use or increases efficiency, especially in heating, cooling, or engines

Examples

The building manager installed an economizer to help reduce electricity bills.

Many modern heating systems include an economizer to improve overall efficiency.
The factory installed an economizer to lower its fuel costs and improve efficiency.
By adding an economizer to the heating system, the building reduced its energy consumption.
Modern engines often include an economizer to make better use of fuel.
