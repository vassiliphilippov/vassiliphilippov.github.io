levirate

noun
custom of marrying a deceased brother's widow
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛv.əˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈliː.vɪˌreɪt / (BrE)
a custom or law requiring a man to marry the widow of his deceased, childless brother to preserve the brother's family line
