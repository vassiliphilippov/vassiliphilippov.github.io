neorealist

noun
modern realist supporter
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌniːoʊˈriːəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊˈrɪəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or studies a modern form of realism, especially in art, literature, or politics

Examples

Famous neorealist director
The Italian film movement was shaped by several influential neorealists after World War II.
As a neorealist, she focused on depicting everyday life in her paintings.
Many political theorists identify as neorealists in international relations.
