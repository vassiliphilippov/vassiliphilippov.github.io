neorealist

noun
modern realist supporter
Pronunciation: / ˌniːoʊˈriːəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊˈrɪəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or studies a modern form of realism, especially in art, literature, or politics
