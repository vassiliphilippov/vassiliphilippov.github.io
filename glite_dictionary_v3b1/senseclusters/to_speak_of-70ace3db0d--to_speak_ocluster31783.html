to speak of

verb
worth mentioning; significant (in negatives)
5.5
Pronunciation: / tə spiːk əv / (AmE), / tə spiːk ɒv / (BrE)
used after a negative to mean worth mentioning or important; significant enough to be noted

Examples

Nothing to speak of
She received no training to speak of before starting the job.
There was no rain to speak of during the entire summer.
We had no problems to speak of with the new software.
