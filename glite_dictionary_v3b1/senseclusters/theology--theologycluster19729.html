theology

noun
academic study of religion and God
Pronunciation: / θiˈɑːlədʒi /; / θiˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / θiˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the academic study of God, religious beliefs, and the influence of religion

Examples

Theology degree
His interest in theology led him to attend a seminary after graduation.

She decided to pursue theology as her field of study at university.
Many debates in theology revolve around questions of faith versus reason.
She decided to study theology at university to better understand world religions.
The professor gave a fascinating lecture on medieval theology and its impact on Western thought.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divinity - the study of religion, especially as an academic discipline
  • antonym: philosophy - the study of fundamental questions about existence, knowledge, and ethics; sometimes contrasted with theology
