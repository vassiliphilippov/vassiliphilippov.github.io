Catacala

noun
genus of underwing moths
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkætəˈkeɪlə / (AmE), / ˌkatəˈkeɪlə / (BrE)
A genus of moths in the family Erebidae, whose larvae are cutworms known as underwings.

Examples

Catacala moth larvae
Larvae of the Catacala genus can cause damage to agricultural crops.
Scientists observed several Catacala species during the nocturnal survey in the forest.
The Catacala moth is known for the vivid patterns on its underwings.
