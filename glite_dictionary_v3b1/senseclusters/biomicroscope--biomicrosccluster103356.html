biomicroscope

noun
microscope for living tissue
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊˈmaɪkrəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊˈmʌɪkrəskəʊp / (BrE)
a microscope designed for examining living tissues, especially used to inspect the eye

Examples

A biomicroscope for eyes
The ophthalmologist examined the patient's eye with a biomicroscope to check for any abnormalities.
A biomicroscope allows doctors to observe living tissues in great detail during an eye exam.
Using a biomicroscope, the specialist detected a small scratch on the cornea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: microscope - an instrument used to see objects that are too small for the naked eye
  • synonym: slit lamp - a device combining a microscope and a light source for examining the eye
