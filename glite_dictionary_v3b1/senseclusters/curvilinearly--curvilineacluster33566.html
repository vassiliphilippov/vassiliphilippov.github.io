curvilinearly

adverb
in a curved manner
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌkɜːrvɪˈlɪnɪrli / (AmE), / ˌkɜːvɪˈlɪnɪəli / (BrE)
in a way that follows or forms a curved line

Examples

To move curvilinearly
The road winds curvilinearly along the edge of the mountain.
The artist drew the pattern curvilinearly to create a sense of movement.
The path of the comet appeared to move curvilinearly across the night sky.

  • antonym: straight - in a direct or linear manner
