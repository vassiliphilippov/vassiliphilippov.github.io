dust

verb
remove dust from a surface
3.3
Pronunciation: / dʌst / (AmE), / dʌst / (BrE)
to remove dust from the surface of something by wiping or brushing it

Examples

To dust the shelves
After the construction work, we had to dust every surface in the house.
Before setting the table, he quickly dusted the dining room.
She dusted the shelves every Saturday morning to keep them looking neat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polish - to make a surface smooth and shiny by rubbing it
