prefecture

noun
the office or area of a prefect
9.3 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpriː.fek.tʃər/ /; / /ˈprɪ.fek.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈpriː.fek.tʃə/ /; / /ˈpriː.fek.tjʊə/ (BrE)
an administrative district or area governed by a prefect (an official), especially in countries like France, Japan, or ancient Rome

Examples

The prefecture government
Changes in leadership at the prefecture affected local administration.
He was appointed to the prefecture after many years of dedicated service.
The prefecture was responsible for maintaining order in the region.
During the Roman Empire, each prefecture was governed by an appointed official.
Kyoto is the capital city of Kyoto prefecture in Japan.
The southern prefecture is known for its beautiful coastal landscapes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: municipality - a city or town with its own local government
  • synonym: prefect - an official in charge of a particular area or group
  • synonym: province - an administrative division, often larger and less specific than a prefecture
