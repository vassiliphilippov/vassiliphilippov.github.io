stalwartly

adverb
with strength and loyalty
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːlwərtli / (AmE), / ˈstɔːlwətli / (BrE)
in a strong, loyal, and determined way

Examples

To stalwartly support
She stalwartly supported her team even when they were losing.
The volunteers stalwartly worked through the night to help those in need.
Despite criticism, he stalwartly stood by his beliefs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loyally - in a loyal and faithful way
  • synonym: steadfastly - in a firm and unwavering manner
  • antonym: weakly - in a weak or unsteady way
