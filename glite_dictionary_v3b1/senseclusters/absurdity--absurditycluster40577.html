absurdity

noun
quality of being ridiculous or unreasonable
6.1 5.19/m
Pronunciation: / …ôbÀàs…ùÀêd…ôti / (AmE), / …ôbÀàs…úÀêd…ôti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being ridiculous, illogical, or wildly unreasonable; something that goes against common sense or logic

Examples

The absurdity of rumors

The absurdity of his suggestion made everyone laugh in disbelief.
The play's humor lies in its deliberate absurdity and illogical situations.
Many political arguments descend into absurdity when they lack evidence.

Synonyms

  • ludicrousness
    7.5 0.12/m
    ludicrousness makes people laugh at how unreasonable something looks, while absurdity stresses both the laugh and serious lack of logic.
  • irrationality
    6.2 1.78/m
    irrationality shows a lack of reason or logic in thought, while absurdity adds a strong sense of being strange or ridiculous.
  • farcicality
    9.9 0.00/m

  • unreasonableness
    6.0 0.08/m
    unreasonableness is behavior or choices that lack fairness or sense, while absurdity often reads as strikingly illogical or ridiculous.
