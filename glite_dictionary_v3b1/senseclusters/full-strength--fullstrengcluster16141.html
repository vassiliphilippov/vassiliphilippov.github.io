full-strength

adjective
undiluted (of a drink, esp. alcohol)
Pronunciation: / fʊl ˈstrɛŋθ / (AmE), / fʊl ˈstrɛŋθ / (BrE)
(of an alcoholic drink) served without being diluted with water or other mixers; undiluted.

Examples

A full-strength drink
He asked for a full-strength gin, refusing any tonic or water.
She prefers her whiskey full-strength rather than mixed with soda.
The bar serves cocktails, but you can also order full-strength spirits if you wish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neat - (of an alcoholic drink) served undiluted or without ice
