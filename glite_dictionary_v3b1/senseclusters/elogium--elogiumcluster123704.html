elogium

noun
praise for the dead
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈloʊdʒiəm / (AmE), / ɪˈləʊdʒiəm / (BrE)
a short speech or inscription in praise of someone, especially one who has died

Examples

An elogium on tomb
The ancient tomb bore an elogium honoring the fallen soldier.
A beautifully carved elogium was placed at the base of the statue.
Historians studied the elogium to learn more about the city's famous citizens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eulogy - a speech or piece of writing praising someone who has died
