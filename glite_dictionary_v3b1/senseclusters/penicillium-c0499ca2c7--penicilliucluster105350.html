Penicillium

noun
genus of fungi (mold); source of penicillin
8.4 6.2
Pronunciation: /pen.ɪ.ˈsɪl.ɪ.əm/ /; / /pə.nɪ.ˈsɪl.ɪ.əm/ (AmE), /pen.ɪ.ˈsɪl.ɪ.əm/ /; / /pə.nɪ.ˈsɪl.ɪ.əm/ (BrE)
a genus of fungi that appear as blue or green molds on decaying food, some species used in cheese-making and as a source of penicillin

Examples

Penicillium mold colonies
