vav

particle
Hebrew prefix 'and'
Pronunciation: / vɑv / (AmE), / vɑːv / (BrE)
a prefix in Hebrew, represented by the letter vav, used to mean 'and' or to link clauses, sometimes affecting verb tense in Biblical Hebrew
