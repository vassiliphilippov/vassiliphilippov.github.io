'til

preposition
up to a time
Pronunciation: / tɪl / (AmE), / tɪl / (BrE)
an informal contraction of 'until', meaning up to the time of a particular event or point.

Examples

Wait 'til morning
We waited outside the theater 'til the doors finally opened.
You can stay at the party 'til midnight if you want.
He worked on his project 'til he was completely satisfied with the results.

  • synonym: until - up to the time of an event or point
