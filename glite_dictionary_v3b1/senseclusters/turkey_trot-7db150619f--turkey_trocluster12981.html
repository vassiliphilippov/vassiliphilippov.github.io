turkey trot

noun
Thanksgiving charity run
Pronunciation: / \u02c8t\u025d\u02d0ki tr\u0251t / (AmE), / \u02c8t\u025c\u02d0ki tr\u0252t / (BrE)
a short road race or fun run, usually held on or around Thanksgiving and often organised to raise money for charity.

Examples

Thanksgiving turkey trot
Every Thanksgiving morning our neighborhood hosts a turkey trot to raise money for food banks.
Thousands of runners signed up for the city's annual turkey trots, many choosing the five-kilometer route.
She trained all autumn so she could finish the local turkey trot without stopping.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charity run - a running event organised to raise money for a charitable cause
  • synonym: fun run - a short, usually informal run held for enjoyment or charity
