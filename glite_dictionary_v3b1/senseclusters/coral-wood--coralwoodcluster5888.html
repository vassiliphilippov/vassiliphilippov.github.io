coral-wood

noun
East Indian ornamental tree with yellow-white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəl wʊd / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəl wʊd / (BrE)
an East Indian tree (Adenanthera pavonina) with racemes of yellow-white flowers, cultivated as an ornamental plant

Examples

The coral-wood tree
Carpenters sometimes use wood from the coral-wood for making small decorative objects.
Many botanical gardens in tropical regions have a coral-wood tree among their collections.
The coral-wood is prized for its attractive flowers and decorative value in gardens.
