outfitter

noun
shop selling specialized equipment
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌfɪtər / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌfɪtə / (BrE)
a shop or business that sells clothing and equipment for a particular activity, such as camping, hunting, or hiking

Examples

Outdoor outfitter shop
Before their canoe trip, they consulted an outfitter who knew the river well.
The outfitter specializes in gear for fishing trips and mountain expeditions.
We stopped at the local outfitter to rent some hiking boots and backpacks.
