tag

verb
attach a tag or label to
Pronunciation: / tæɡ / (AmE), / tæɡ / (BrE)
to attach a tag or label to something for identification or classification, either physically or digitally (including mentioning someone in social media)

Examples

To tag a suitcase
Make sure to tag your luggage with your name and address.
Please tag each sample before sending them to the lab.
She tagged the boxes with their contents for easy unpacking.
She tags her friend in the photo on Instagram to share the memory.
They tagged the conference speakers in the post so everyone could see their profiles.
I'm tagging all my classmates in the event invitation to make sure they get it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: label - to attach a label to something for identification
  • synonym: mention - to refer to or name someone in a post or message
  • synonym: notify - to give someone a message or alert about something; often automatic after a tag
