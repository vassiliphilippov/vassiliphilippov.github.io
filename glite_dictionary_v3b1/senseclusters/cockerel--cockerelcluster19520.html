cockerel

noun
young male chicken
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːkərəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒkərəl / (BrE)
A young male domestic chicken, usually less than one year old.

Examples

The cockerel crows
Every morning, the cockerel crows before sunrise.
That cockerel will soon be mature enough to join the roosters.
The farmer bought three cockerels to add to his flock.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hen - an adult female domestic chicken
  • degree higher: rooster - an adult male domestic chicken
