lend

verb
give temporarily, expecting return
Pronunciation: / lɛnd / (AmE), / lɛnd / (BrE)
to give something to someone for a short time, expecting it to be returned

Examples

To lend a book
Can you lend me your notes for the class?
I'm always happy to lend a hand when someone needs help.
She lent her umbrella to a coworker during the storm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: borrow - to take and use something belonging to someone else with the intention of returning it
  • synonym: loan - to lend something, especially money, to someone
