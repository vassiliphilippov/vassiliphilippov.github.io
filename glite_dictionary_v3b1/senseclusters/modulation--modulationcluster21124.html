modulation

noun
musical passage changing key
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑdʒəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɒdjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a musical passage or process in which the key is changed from one to another

Examples

Key modulation in music
During the symphony, there's a sudden modulation that adds excitement to the movement.
The modulation from C major to G major gave the piece an unexpected brightness.
The composer employed a clever modulation to transition between sections of the work.
