unworking

adjective
not employed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
not employed or not currently having a job.

Examples

An unworking person
After the company downsized, he found himself unworking for the first time in years.
Many unworking people attend job fairs to look for new opportunities.
She became unworking after her contract ended last month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unemployed - not having a paid job
  • antonym: working - having a paid job
