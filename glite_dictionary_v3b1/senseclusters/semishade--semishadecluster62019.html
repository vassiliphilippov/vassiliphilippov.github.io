semishade

adjective
partly shaded
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmiˌʃeɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɛmiˌʃeɪd / (BrE)
partly shaded; receiving only limited sunlight, often used to describe areas suitable for certain plants

Examples

Semishade garden
Some ferns grow best in semishade rather than in full sun.
The garden bench was placed in a semishade area to keep it cool during the afternoon.
Choose semishade locations for plants that cannot tolerate strong sunlight.
