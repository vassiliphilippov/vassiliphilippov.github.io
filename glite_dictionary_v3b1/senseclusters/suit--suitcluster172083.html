suit

noun
set of matching outerwear garments
1.0 53/m
Pronunciation: / suÀêt / (AmE), / suÀêt / (BrE)
a set of matching garments, typically consisting of a jacket and trousers or a skirt, made from the same fabric and color, worn as outerwear

Examples

A business suit
He wore his best suit to the interview and made a great impression.
She bought a navy blue suit for her new job.
The store offers custom-tailored suits for both men and women.

Synonyms

  • suit of clothes
    3.0 0.24/m
    suit of clothes is a longer phrase that clearly refers to a matching jacket and trousers, while suit is the shorter everyday word for the same outfit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: costume - a set of clothes worn for a particular occasion or purpose
  • antonym: dress - a one-piece garment for women or girls
  • synonym: tuxedo - formal evening suit for men
