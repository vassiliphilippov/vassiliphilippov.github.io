suit

noun
set of matching outerwear garments
Pronunciation: / suːt / (AmE), / suːt / (BrE)
a set of matching garments, typically consisting of a jacket and trousers or a skirt, made from the same fabric and color, worn as outerwear

Examples

A business suit
He wore his best suit to the interview and made a great impression.
She bought a navy blue suit for her new job.
The store offers custom-tailored suits for both men and women.
