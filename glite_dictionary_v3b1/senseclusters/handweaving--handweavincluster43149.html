handweaving

noun
cloth woven by hand
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌwiːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌwiːvɪŋ / (BrE)
cloth or textile that is made by weaving threads together by hand rather than by machine

Examples

A beautiful handweaving
The artisan spent weeks creating a colorful handweaving for the exhibition.
Many traditional cultures have unique styles of handweaving passed down through generations.
She learned handweaving as a hobby and now sells her textiles at local markets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handmade - made by hand, not by machine
  • antonym: machine weaving - weaving done by machine rather than by hand
