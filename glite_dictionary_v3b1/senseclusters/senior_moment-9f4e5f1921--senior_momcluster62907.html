senior moment

noun
temporary lapse of memory, often in older people
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːniɚ ˈmoʊmənt /; / ˈsinjɚ ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈsiːniə ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a brief period when someone, especially an older person, temporarily forgets something or becomes confused, often due to aging
