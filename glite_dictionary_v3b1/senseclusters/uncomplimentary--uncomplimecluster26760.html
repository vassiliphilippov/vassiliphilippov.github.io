uncomplimentary

adjective
expressing criticism or disrespect
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌkɑːmpləˈmɛntəri /; / ˌʌn.kəmˈplɪm.ənˌtɛr.i / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌkɒmplɪˈmɛntəri /; / ˌʌn.kəmˈplɪm.ən.tər.i / (BrE)
expressing criticism, disapproval, or lack of praise; not favorable or flattering

Examples

Uncomplimentary comments
He found the comments in the article rather uncomplimentary toward his work.
She made several uncomplimentary remarks about his appearance.
The critic's review was both harsh and uncomplimentary.
He was upset by the uncomplimentary remarks made about his outfit at the party.
Her comments about the presentation were rather uncomplimentary and hurt the speaker's feelings.
The critic wrote an uncomplimentary review of the film, pointing out its many flaws.

Related Terms

  • antonym: complimentary - expressing praise, approval, or admiration
  • synonym: unflattering - not making someone or something look attractive or good
