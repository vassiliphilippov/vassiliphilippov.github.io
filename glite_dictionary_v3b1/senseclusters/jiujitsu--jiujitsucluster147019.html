jiujitsu

noun
sport grappling (Brazilian jiu-jitsu)
6.5
Pronunciation: /dʒuː.ˈdʒɪt.suː/ /; / /dʒiː.ˈdʒɪt.suː/ (AmE), /dʒuː.ˈdʒɪt.suː/ /; / /dʒiː.ˈdʒɪt.suː/ (BrE)
A sport-focused form of grappling (especially Brazilian jiu-jitsu) that emphasizes ground fighting and submission techniques used in training and competitions.

Examples

Brazilian jiujitsu match
She earned a silver medal in jiujitsu at the international martial arts competition.
He trains five nights a week to improve his jiujitsu and prepare for local tournaments.
Many mixed martial arts fighters started with jiujitsu before moving to striking disciplines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Brazilian jiu-jitsu - A modern competitive grappling martial art focusing on ground fighting and submissions; often abbreviated BJJ.
  • synonym: grappling - Hand-to-hand fighting techniques that involve holds, throws, and ground control rather than striking.
