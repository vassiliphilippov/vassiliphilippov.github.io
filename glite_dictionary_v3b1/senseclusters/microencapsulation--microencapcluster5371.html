microencapsulation

noun
tiny particle coating
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊɪnˌkæpsjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊɪnˌkæpsjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of coating or enclosing very small particles or droplets with a thin layer to form tiny capsules, usually to protect them or control their release
