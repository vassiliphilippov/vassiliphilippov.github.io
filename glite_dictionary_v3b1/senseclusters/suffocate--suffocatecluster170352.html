Synonyms

    6.6 0.3/m
    Prevent breathing normally, often used for partial or temporary restriction
    vs suffocate: to prevent someone from breathing normally; to suffocate or asphyxiate
  • asphyxiate
    9.3 0.6/m
    Refers to oxygen deprivation causing breathing failure, often medical
    vs suffocate: to prevent someone or something from breathing, usually by depriving them of oxygen, causing suffocation or even death
  • smother
    6.4 1/m
    Cause lack of air by covering or enclosing, often leading to death
    vs suffocate: to deprive (someone) of air and prevent them from breathing, typically causing death by suffocation
  • choke
    3.7 12/m
    Block the airway by an object or squeeze, often sudden and mechanical
    vs suffocate: to be unable to breathe or to make someone unable to breathe by blocking the throat, airway, or by squeezing the neck; or to become blocked so that air or liquid cannot pass

Related Terms

  • synonym: choke - to have difficulty breathing because of a blockage in the throat
  • synonym: smother - to kill by covering the nose and mouth, preventing breathing
