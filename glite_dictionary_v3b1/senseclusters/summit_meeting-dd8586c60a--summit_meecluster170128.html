summit meeting

noun
meeting of heads of government
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmɪt ˈmitɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌmɪt ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a meeting between heads of government or high-level officials to discuss important issues

Examples

A summit meeting between leaders
After months of tension, the two countries agreed to hold a summit meeting to discuss peace.
The G7 summit meeting brought together leaders to address the challenges of climate change.
The president is preparing for a crucial summit meeting with leaders from across the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conference - a formal meeting for discussion, especially among experts or representatives
