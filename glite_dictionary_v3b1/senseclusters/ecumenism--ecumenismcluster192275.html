ecumenism

noun
movement for unity among Christian churches
7.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkjuːməˌnɪzəm /; / ɪˈkjuməˌnɪzəm /; / ˌiːˈkjuː.məˌnɪ.zəm / (AmE), / iːˈkjuːmənɪz(ə)m /; / ɪˈkjuːmənɪz(ə)m /; / ˌiːˈkjuː.mə.nɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the principle or aim of promoting unity and cooperation among Christian churches or, more broadly, among different religious groups
