consolidationist

adjective
favoring centralization
9.8
Pronunciation: /kən.sɑː.lə.ˈdeɪ.ʃən.ɪst/ (AmE), /kən.sɒl.ɪ.ˈdeɪ.ʃən.ɪst/ (BrE)
favoring or supporting the combining or centralizing of institutions, assets, or authority

Examples

Consolidationist policy
The new government took a consolidationist approach to unify the country's various agencies.
Many business leaders are consolidationist when it comes to merging smaller companies for efficiency.
Critics argue that a consolidationist policy can reduce local autonomy and diversity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centralizing - favoring the concentration of control or power
  • antonym: decentralizing - favoring the distribution of power away from a central authority
