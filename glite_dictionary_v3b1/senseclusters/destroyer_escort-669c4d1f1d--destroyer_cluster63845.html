destroyer escort

noun
small warship for escorting fleets or convoys

Examples

A destroyer escort ship
A destroyer escort is less heavily armed than a typical destroyer but plays a vital role in anti-submarine warfare.

The aging destroyer escort was finally decommissioned after decades of service.
