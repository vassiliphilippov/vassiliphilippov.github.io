destroyer escort

noun
small warship for escorting fleets or convoys
6.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstrɔɪər ˈɛskɔrt / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɔɪə ˈɛskɔːt / (BrE)
a small warship, smaller than a destroyer, designed to escort and protect fleets or convoys at sea

Examples

A destroyer escort ship
A destroyer escort is less heavily armed than a typical destroyer but plays a vital role in anti-submarine warfare.
During World War II, the U.S. Navy commissioned hundreds of destroyer escorts to protect transatlantic convoys.
The aging destroyer escort was finally decommissioned after decades of service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corvette - a small, fast warship, often used for escort duties
  • degree higher: destroyer - a large, fast, and maneuverable warship
  • synonym: frigate - a medium-sized warship, historically used for escort and patrol
