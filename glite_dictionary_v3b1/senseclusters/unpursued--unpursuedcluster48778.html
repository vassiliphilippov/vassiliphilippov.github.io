unpursued

adjective
not followed up
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnpərˈsuːd / (AmE), / ˌʌnpəˈsjuːd / (BrE)
not continued, followed up, or acted on, especially referring to a plan, idea, or investigation.

Examples

Unpursued investigation
The investigation into the missing documents remained unpursued due to a lack of evidence.
Many creative ideas go unpursued because people are afraid to take risks.
The company left several promising projects unpursued after the leadership change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abandoned - left behind or given up completely
  • synonym: neglected - not given proper attention or care
  • antonym: pursued - continued or followed up with effort
