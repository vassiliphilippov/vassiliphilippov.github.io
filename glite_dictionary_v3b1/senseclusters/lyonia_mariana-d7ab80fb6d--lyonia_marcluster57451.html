Lyonia mariana

noun
poisonous shrub of eastern US with pinkish-white flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / laɪˈoʊniə məˈriːənə / (AmE), / laɪˈəʊniə məˈriːənə / (BrE)
a deciduous shrub native to the coastal plains of the eastern United States, known for its nodding pinkish-white flowers and toxicity to livestock
