back order

verb
to order out-of-stock items
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbæk ˌɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈbæk ˌɔːdə / (BrE)
to request goods that are currently out of stock so they will be delivered when available

Examples

To back order items

Customers can choose to back order products that are temporarily out of stock.
