patterning

noun
forming patterns
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpætərnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpætənɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or result of forming patterns or repeated designs on a surface or within a structure

Examples

Textile patterning
The textile features intricate patterning inspired by traditional motifs.
Careful patterning on the walls created a sense of movement in the room.
The bird's feathers show natural patterning that helps it blend into its environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decoration - the act of adding ornament or embellishment
  • synonym: design - a decorative or artistic arrangement of elements
