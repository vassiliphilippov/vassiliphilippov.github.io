beltline

noun
road around city
4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbelt.laɪn/ (AmE), /ˈbelt.laɪn/ (BrE)
a road or railway that goes around the edge of a town or city, often used to connect different areas or avoid city traffic

Examples

City beltline road
The new beltline helps drivers avoid downtown traffic during rush hour.
Factories are located along the beltline to make shipping goods easier.
We took the beltline to get to the other side of the city quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bypass - a road built to go around a town or busy area
  • synonym: ring road - a road that circles around a city or town
