Joe

noun
ordinary man
2.5
Pronunciation: / dʒoʊ / (AmE), / dʒəʊ / (BrE)
an ordinary or typical man, often used to refer to any regular person

Examples

Average Joe
The bar was filled with friendly Joes enjoying the game.
He is just a regular Joe who works hard every day.
Many Joes across the country are struggling with rising costs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: everyman - an ordinary or typical person
