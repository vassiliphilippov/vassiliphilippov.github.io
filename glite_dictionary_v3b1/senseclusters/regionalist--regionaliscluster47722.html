regionalist

adjective
relating to a region
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdʒənəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈriːdʒənəlɪst / (BrE)
relating to the distinctive features, culture, or interests of a particular region rather than the whole country.

Examples

Regionalist culture
The regionalist policies aimed to protect the unique traditions of the mountain villages.
A regionalist perspective can help address the specific needs of different communities.
The festival had a regionalist flavor, celebrating local foods and crafts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: local - relating to a particular area or neighborhood
  • antonym: national - relating to a whole nation or country
