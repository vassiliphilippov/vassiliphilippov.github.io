slippery floor

noun
floor surface likely to cause slipping
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪpəri flɔr / (AmE), / ˈslɪpəri flɔː / (BrE)
a floor surface that is difficult to walk on because it is likely to cause slipping

Examples

A slippery floor
She put up a warning sign to alert visitors to the slippery floor.
The janitor quickly mopped up the spill to prevent a slippery floor.
Watch out for the slippery floor near the entrance after it rains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slippery - difficult to stand or move on because it is smooth, wet, or polished
