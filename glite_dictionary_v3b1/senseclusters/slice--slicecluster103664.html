slice

noun
a share or portion
Pronunciation: / slaɪs / (AmE), / slaɪs / (BrE)
a share or portion of something, such as money, support, or influence

Examples

A slice of profits
Each department fights for its slice of the annual budget.
He was promised a substantial slice of the profits if the venture succeeded.
Tourism provides a significant slice of the nation's income.

Related Terms

  • synonym: portion - a part of something that is shared or divided
  • synonym: share - a part of something that has been divided between several people
