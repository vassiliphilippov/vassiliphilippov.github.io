mealberry

noun
evergreen shrub with red berries
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːlˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈmiːlˌbɛri / (BrE)
an evergreen, mat-forming shrub found in North America and northern Eurasia, with small white flowers, red berries, and leaves that turn red in autumn

Examples

The mealberry shrub
In autumn, the leaves of the mealberry turn a vivid red, adding color to the landscape.
The garden was covered in mealberry, providing a beautiful carpet of greenery year-round.
Wildlife are often attracted to the bright red berries produced by the mealberry.
