compilation

noun
the act of gathering and assembling items
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːm.pəˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒm.pɪˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of gathering and assembling information, documents, or items into a single collection, such as a book, file, or list

Examples

Compilation of data
She created a compilation of her favorite poems to give as a gift.
The annual report was a careful compilation of financial data from all departments.
This music album is a compilation of the band's greatest hits over the past decade.
