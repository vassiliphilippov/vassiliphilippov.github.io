fra

noun
title for friar
Pronunciation: / frɑː / (AmE), / frɑː / (BrE)
a title used before the name of an Italian friar or monk

Examples

Fra Giovanni
Fra Bartolomeo was known for his beautiful Renaissance paintings.
The monastery was home to Fra Giovanni and several other monks.
Many art historians admire the works of Fra Filippo Lippi.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brother - a title for a male member of a religious order
  • antonym: sister - a title for a female member of a religious order
