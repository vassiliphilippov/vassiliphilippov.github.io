Academy Award

noun
prestigious annual film award
Pronunciation: / əˈkædəmi əˈwɔrd / (AmE), / əˈkædəmi əˈwɔːd / (BrE)
An annual award presented by the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences recognizing outstanding achievements in the film industry.

Examples
