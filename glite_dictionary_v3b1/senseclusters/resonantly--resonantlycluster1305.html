resonantly

adverb
evocatively, with impact
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛzənəntli / (AmE), / ˈrɛzənəntli / (BrE)
in a way that evokes strong emotion, significance, or a lasting effect

Examples

Music resonantly echoes
The music echoed resonantly through the hall, leaving the audience in awe.
Her words rang resonantly in his mind long after the conversation ended.
The film's message was delivered resonantly, making a deep impression on viewers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evocatively - in a way that brings strong images or feelings to mind
  • antonym: flatly - in a way that lacks emotion or impact
  • synonym: powerfully - in a way that has a strong effect
