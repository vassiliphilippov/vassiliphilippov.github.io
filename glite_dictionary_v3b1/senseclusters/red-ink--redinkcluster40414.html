red-ink

verb
mark with red ink
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛd ˌɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈrɛd ˌɪŋk / (BrE)
to mark or highlight something with red ink, especially to indicate errors, restrictions, or to draw attention to specific sections

Examples

Editors often red-ink passages that need revision before publication.
Please red-ink the confidential areas on the diagram so they are clearly visible.
The teacher decided to red-ink all the spelling mistakes in the essays.
