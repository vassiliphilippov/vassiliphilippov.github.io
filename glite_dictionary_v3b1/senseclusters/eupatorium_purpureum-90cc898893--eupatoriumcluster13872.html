Eupatorium purpureum

noun
a North American herbaceous plant
Pronunciation: / juˌpeɪtɔriəm pɝːˈpjuriəm / (AmE), / juːˌpatɔːriəm pjʊəˈpjʊəriəm / (BrE)
A tall North American herb with whorled leaves and terminal clusters of purple-spotted flowers, commonly known as Joe-Pye weed.
