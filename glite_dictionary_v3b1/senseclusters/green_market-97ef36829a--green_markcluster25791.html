green market

noun
outdoor market selling farm produce
Pronunciation: / ɡriːn ˈmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
an open-air market where fresh farm products, such as fruits and vegetables, are sold directly to consumers

Examples

Local green market
Every Saturday, we buy fresh vegetables at the green market downtown.
She loves the lively atmosphere of the green market and always finds seasonal fruit there.
The city council encourages local farmers to participate in the green market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farmers' market - open-air market where local farmers sell their produce
