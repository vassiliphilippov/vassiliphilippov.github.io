Red Hand Defenders

proper noun
Northern Ireland loyalist paramilitary group
Pronunciation: / rɛd hænd dɪˈfɛndɚz / (AmE), / rɛd hænd dɪˈfɛndəz / (BrE)
a loyalist paramilitary group in Northern Ireland, composed mainly of Protestants, known for violent attacks against Catholic civilians and opposition to political settlement with the Irish Republic
