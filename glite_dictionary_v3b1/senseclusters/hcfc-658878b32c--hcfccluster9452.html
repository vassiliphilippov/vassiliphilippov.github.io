any of a group of hydrochlorofluorocarbons, chemical compounds used as refrigerants and propellants that are less damaging to the ozone layer than chlorofluorocarbons (CFCs).

Examples

HCFC chemical compounds
Although HCFCs are less harmful than CFCs, their use is still regulated.
Many countries have limited the use of HCFC compounds to protect the ozone layer.
The technician recommended replacing the old coolant with an HCFC alternative.
