dharmic

adjective
relating to dharma
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑrmɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɑːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to dharma, especially the moral and religious duties in Indian religions such as Hinduism, Buddhism, and Jainism

Examples

Dharmic traditions
Many dharmic traditions emphasize compassion and nonviolence.
The festival is celebrated by followers of several dharmic religions across South Asia.
He studied the dharmic principles that guide ethical behavior in Hinduism and Buddhism.

Related Terms

  • antonym: secular - not connected with religious or spiritual matters
  • synonym: spiritual - relating to religion or the soul
