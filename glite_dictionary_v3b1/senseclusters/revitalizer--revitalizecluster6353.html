revitalizer

noun
source of renewal
5.1
Pronunciation: / riˈvaɪtəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / riˈvaɪtəlaɪzə / (BrE)

Examples

She was known as a revitalizer who could inspire any team to achieve more.
