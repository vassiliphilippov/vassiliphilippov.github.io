multicultural Marxism

noun
pejorative political label
9.9
Pronunciation: /mʌl.tɪ.ˈkʌl.tʃər.əl ˈmɑːrks.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /mʌl.tiː.ˈkʌl.tʃər.əl ˈmɑːrks.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /mʌl.tɪ.ˈkʌl.tʃə.rəl ˈmɑːks.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a pejorative label used in political rhetoric to accuse multicultural or diversity policies of being driven by Marxist ideology rather than neutral scholarship.

Examples

Multicultural Marxism accusation
Some commentators call new diversity initiatives multicultural Marxism, accusing universities of promoting a political agenda.
He dismissed the training as multicultural Marxism, saying it replaced objective history with ideological lessons.
Right-wing blogs often use multicultural Marxism to suggest a coordinated attack on traditional values.
