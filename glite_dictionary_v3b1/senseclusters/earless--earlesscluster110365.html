earless

adjective
without external ears
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrləs / (AmE), / ˈɪəlɪs / (BrE)
having no external ears or lacking visible ears.

Examples

Earless cat breed
Some breeds of cats are almost earless due to genetic mutations.
The earless seal is well adapted to aquatic life.
The statue appeared strangely earless after years of weathering.
