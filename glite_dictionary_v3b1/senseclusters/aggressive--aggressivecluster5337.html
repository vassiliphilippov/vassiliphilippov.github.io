aggressive

adjective
determined and energetic in pursuing goals
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈɡrɛsɪv / (AmE), / əˈɡrɛsɪv / (BrE)
showing determination and energetic pursuit of one's goals, often in a forceful or proactive way

Examples

Aggressive strategy
Her aggressive approach to negotiations resulted in a beneficial deal.
The athlete is known for his aggressive style of play on the court.
The company hired an aggressive manager to lead their expansion into new markets.
