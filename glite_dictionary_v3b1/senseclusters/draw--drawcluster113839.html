draw

noun
random selection; act of drawing lots
6.5
Pronunciation: / drɔ / (AmE), / drɔː / (BrE)
an act of choosing or being chosen at random, especially by lot or chance

Examples

A prize draw
He won the prize in the draw, purely by chance.
It's just the luck of the draw whether you go first or last.
The teams were assigned their opponents in the draw held yesterday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lot - an object or token used to decide something at random; act of drawing lots
  • synonym: raffle - a means of making a random selection, usually for a prize or opportunity
