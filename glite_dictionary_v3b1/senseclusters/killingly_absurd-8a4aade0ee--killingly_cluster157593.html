killingly absurd

adjective
extremely ridiculous; intensely absurd
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪlɪŋli əbˈsɝd / (AmE), / ˈkɪlɪŋli əbˈzɜːd / (BrE)
Extremely ridiculous or nonsensical, to an intense or hilarious degree.

Examples

A killingly absurd story
Her explanation was so killingly absurd that even the teacher had to smile.
The plot of the play was killingly absurd, drawing laughter from everyone in the audience.
We found ourselves in a killingly absurd argument over who left the spoon on the table.
