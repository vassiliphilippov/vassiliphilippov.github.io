Synonyms

  • undestroyable
    8.2 0.00/m
    undestroyable means impossible to destroy by normal means, while indestructible implies a very strong, lasting resistance to damage.
  • unbreakable
    2.7 1.14/m
    unbreakable resists breaking in normal use, while indestructible suggests it really cannot be destroyed by any force.
  • imperishable
    7.3 0.24/m
    imperishable does not decay and lasts forever, while indestructible mainly focuses on resisting physical damage or destruction.
  • invulnerable
    7.7 1.04/m
    invulnerable is safe from harm or attack, while indestructible means it cannot be physically destroyed or broken.
