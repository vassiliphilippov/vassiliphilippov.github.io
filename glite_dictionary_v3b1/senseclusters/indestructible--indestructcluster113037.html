indestructible

adjective
impossible to destroy
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈstrʌktəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈstrʌktəbəl /; / ˌɪndɪˈstrʌktɪbəl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to destroy or damage; very durable or long-lasting

Examples

An indestructible object
Her spirit proved indestructible in the face of adversity.
The superhero's shield was said to be indestructible, surviving every attack thrown at it.
These tires are made with an indestructible compound that resists punctures and cuts.
Ancient myths speak of an indestructible shield that could withstand any attack.
Despite years of harsh weather, the statue appeared almost indestructible.
She admired her grandmother's indestructible spirit in the face of adversity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: destructible - able to be destroyed; not indestructible
  • synonym: durable - able to withstand wear and pressure; lasting
  • antonym: fragile - easily broken or damaged
