advertorial

noun
advertisement styled as an editorial
5.3 6.1
Pronunciation: /æd.vər.ˈtɔːr.iː.əl/ (AmE), /æd.və.ˈtɔː.riː.əl/ (BrE)
an advertisement written and presented in the style of an editorial piece, intended to resemble journalistic content

Examples

An advertorial article
Companies often use advertorial content to subtly market their products within credible-looking articles.
Some readers were unaware that the article they enjoyed was actually an advertorial paid for by a brand.
The magazine was filled with advertorials promoting travel destinations disguised as personal stories.
