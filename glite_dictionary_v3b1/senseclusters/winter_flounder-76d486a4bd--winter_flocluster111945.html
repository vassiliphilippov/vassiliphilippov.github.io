winter flounder

noun
a North American flatfish, Pseudopleuronectes americanus
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tɚ ˈflaʊn.dɚ /; / ˈwɪntər ˈflaʊndər / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə ˈflaʊn.də /; / ˈwɪntə ˈflaʊndə / (BrE)
a flatfish found in the northwestern Atlantic Ocean, valued as a food fish and usually caught in winter
