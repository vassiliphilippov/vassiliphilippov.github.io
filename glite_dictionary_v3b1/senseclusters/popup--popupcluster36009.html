popup

noun
sudden on-screen window
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːpˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈpɒpˌʌp / (BrE)
a small window or box that appears suddenly on a computer or device screen, often to display information or advertisements

Examples

Popup window
When I clicked the link, a popup appeared asking me to subscribe to the newsletter.
Many websites use popups to display advertisements or special offers to visitors.
You can adjust your browser settings to block unwanted popups from interrupting your browsing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dialog box - a small window that prompts the user to make a decision or enter information
  • synonym: notification - a message that appears on a device to alert the user
