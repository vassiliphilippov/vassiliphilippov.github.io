popup

noun
sudden on-screen window
3.3 0.56/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ëÀêpÀå åp / (AmE), / Ààp…ípÀå åp / (BrE)
a small window or box that appears suddenly on a computer or device screen, often to display information or advertisements

Examples

You can adjust your browser settings to block unwanted popups from interrupting your browsing.
