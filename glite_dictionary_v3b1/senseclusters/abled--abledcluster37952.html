abled

adjective
not disabled
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪbəld / (AmE), / ˈeɪbəld / (BrE)
having physical or mental abilities, especially in contrast to being disabled

Examples

Abled children
The new playground is designed to be accessible for both abled and disabled children.
Some sports events are open to abled athletes as well as those with disabilities.
The organization promotes equal opportunities for abled and disabled employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: able-bodied - physically healthy and not disabled
  • antonym: disabled - having a physical or mental condition that limits abilities
