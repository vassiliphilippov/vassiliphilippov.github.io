foster son

noun
male child raised by non-biological parents
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑstər sʌn / (AmE), / ˈfɒstə sʌn / (BrE)
A boy or man who is raised as one's son, though not biologically related, typically as part of a foster family arrangement.

Examples

The foster son
After years in the system, he finally became their foster son at age ten.
Social workers praised the couple for their dedication to their foster son.
The foster son formed a close bond with his new family over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adopted son - male child legally adopted and raised as a son
  • antonym: foster daughter - female child raised by non-biological parents
