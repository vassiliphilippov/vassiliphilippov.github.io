rejuvenescence

noun
renewal of youthfulness or vitality
Pronunciation: / rɪˌdʒuːvəˈnɛsəns / (AmE), / rɪˌdʒuːvɪˈnɛsəns / (BrE)
The process or state of being renewed with youthful qualities, energy, or vitality.

Examples

A rejuvenescence process
Physical exercise can bring about a sense of rejuvenescence in people of all ages.
The art movement brought a cultural rejuvenescence to the entire region.
The city experienced a period of rejuvenescence after the redevelopment projects were completed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rejuvenation - the act of making someone or something look or feel younger
