forward-looking

(in law and finance) describing statements, projections, or estimates about future events or performance, typically presented with hedges, assumptions, and risk disclosures; used as a conventional label in reports, filings, and press releases.

Examples

Forward-looking statement
The annual report contains several forward-looking statements about expected revenue and growth over the next year.
Regulators require companies to label speculative projections as forward-looking and to include appropriate risk disclosures.
Investors were warned that forward-looking estimates are uncertain and depend on market and operational assumptions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: historical - relating to past events rather than future possibilities
  • synonym: projection - a calculation or estimate of future trends, revenues, or performance
