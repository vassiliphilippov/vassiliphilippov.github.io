zoom the camera

verb
adjust camera lens to change magnification
Pronunciation: / zum ðə kəˈmɛrə / (AmE), / zuːm ðə ˈkæmərə / (BrE)
to adjust the camera lens in order to make the subject appear closer or farther away in the frame

Examples

To zoom the camera in
He asked the photographer to zoom the camera so they could see the details.
She forgot to zoom the camera during the performance, so the footage was too wide.
When filming wildlife, you may need to zoom the camera quickly to catch sudden movements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: zoom in - move the camera lens to make the subject appear larger
  • antonym: zoom out - move the camera lens to make the subject appear smaller
