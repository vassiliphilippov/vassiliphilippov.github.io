moronically

adverb
foolishly
7.6
Pronunciation: / məˈrɑːnɪkli / (AmE), / məˈrɒnɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is very foolish or shows a lack of good sense

Examples

To moronically forget
He moronically forgot to lock the door before leaving the house.
She moronically tried to fix the computer by hitting it repeatedly.
They moronically ignored the warning signs and got lost in the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foolishly - in a way that lacks good sense or judgment
  • antonym: sensibly - in a way that shows good sense or judgment
