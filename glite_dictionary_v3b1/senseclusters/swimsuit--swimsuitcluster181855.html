swimsuit

noun
tight-fitting garment for swimming
1.0 2.88/m
Pronunciation: / Ààsw…™mÀåsuÀêt / (AmE), / Ààsw…™msjuÀêt / (BrE)
a piece of clothing designed to be worn for swimming, covering the body more or less closely

Examples

A swimsuit for swimming
He forgot his swimsuit, so he had to sit out during the water games.
She bought a new swimsuit for her summer vacation at the beach.
The pool requires all swimmers to wear a swimsuit while swimming.

Synonyms

  • bathing suit
    1.0 3.84/m
    a bathing suit is a close-fitting single garment for swimming, while a swimsuit can mean any garment worn to swim.
  • swimming costume
    1.3 0.03/m
    a swimming costume usually fits closely in the water for swimming, while a swimsuit is a general swimming garment.
  • bathing costume
    3.4 0.04/m
    a bathing costume is a tight-fitting garment that focuses on a close fit, while a swimsuit can cover more or fit less tightly.
  • swimwear
    1.0 0.37/m
    swimwear names all clothes used for swimming at pools or beaches, while a swimsuit usually means a single garment for swimming.
