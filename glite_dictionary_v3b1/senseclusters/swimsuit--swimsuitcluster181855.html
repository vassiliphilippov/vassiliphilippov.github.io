swimsuit

noun
tight-fitting garment for swimming
1.5 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈswɪm.suːt/ /; / /ˈswɪm.sjuːt/ (AmE), /ˈswɪm.sjuːt/ /; / /ˈswɪm.suːt/ (BrE)

Examples
