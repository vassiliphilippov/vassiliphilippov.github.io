shantung

noun
heavy silk or imitation cotton fabric
5.5
Pronunciation: / ʃænˈtʌŋ / (AmE), / ʃænˈtʌŋ / (BrE)
a heavy silk fabric with a rough, irregular surface, sometimes imitated in cotton.

Examples

A shantung fabric
Designers often choose shantung for its unique texture and elegant drape.
She wore a shimmering shantung dress to the evening gala.
The jacket is made from navy blue shantung and lined with silk.
