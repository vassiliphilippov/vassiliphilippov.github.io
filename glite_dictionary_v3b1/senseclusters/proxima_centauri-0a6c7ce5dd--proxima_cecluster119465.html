Proxima Centauri

proper noun
nearest star to the Sun
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑksɪmə sɛnˈtɔri, ˈproʊk‑ / (AmE), / ˈprɒksɪmə sɛnˈtɔːraɪ / (BrE)
the closest known star to the Sun, located about 4.3 light-years from Earth in the constellation Centaurus

Examples

Proxima Centauri star
Proxima Centauri is visible through powerful telescopes from the southern hemisphere.
Astronomers discovered a planet orbiting Proxima Centauri in 2016.
Many stargazers hope to someday visit Proxima Centauri using advanced spacecraft.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Alpha Centauri - the closest star system to the Solar System, of which Proxima Centauri is a part
