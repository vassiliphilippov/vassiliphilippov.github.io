turakoo

noun
large, crested African bird
Pronunciation: / təˈreɪkuː / (AmE), / tjʊəˈrɑːkuː / (BrE)
a large, brightly crested bird native to Africa, known for its vivid plumage and distinctive crest

Examples

A turakoo bird
Our guide pointed out a turakoo perched near the riverbank during the safari.
The striking green feathers on the turakoo make it easy to spot in the treetops.
Turakoos are famous for their loud calls and the vivid colors in their crests.
