felucca

noun
Mediterranean sailing boat
9.6
Pronunciation: / fəˈlʌkə / (AmE), / fəˈlʌkə / (BrE)
A fast, narrow sailing boat of the Mediterranean, traditionally rigged with one or more lateen sails.

Examples

A felucca boat
Dozens of feluccas lined the harbor, their sails catching the afternoon sun.
The felucca glided swiftly across the water, steered by an experienced captain.
We sailed down the Nile in a traditional felucca, enjoying the gentle breeze.
