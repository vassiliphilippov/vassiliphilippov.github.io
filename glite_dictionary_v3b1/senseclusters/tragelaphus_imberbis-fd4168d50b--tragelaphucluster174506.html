Tragelaphus imberbis

noun
a species of African antelope (lesser kudu)
9.9
Pronunciation: / trædʒɪˈlæfəs ɪmˈbɜːrbɪs / (AmE), / trædʒɪˈlæfəs ɪmˈbɜːbɪs / (BrE)
A species of African antelope, known as the lesser kudu, characterized by its slender build and spiral horns.

Examples

The Tragelaphus imberbis antelope
Researchers observed a Tragelaphus imberbis grazing quietly by a waterhole.
The Tragelaphus imberbis inhabits dry, scrub woodlands in eastern Africa.
Unlike the greater kudu, the Tragelaphus imberbis has a more slender appearance and shorter horns.
