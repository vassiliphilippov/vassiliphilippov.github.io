symposium

noun
meeting for discussion of a topic
1.1
Pronunciation: / sɪmˈpoʊziəm / (AmE), / sɪmˈpəʊziəm / (BrE)
a meeting or conference at which experts or specialists discuss a particular topic and often make presentations

Examples

Academic symposium
Experts from various fields gathered for the annual symposium to share knowledge and insights.
She presented her research findings at an international symposium.
The university will host a symposium on climate change next month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conference - a meeting for discussion or information exchange
  • synonym: seminar - a class or meeting for discussion or training
