chevrotain

noun
small hornless deer-like mammal
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃɛv.rəˌteɪn / (AmE), / ʃɛv.rəˈtɛɪn / (BrE)
A very small, hornless, deer-like ruminant found in tropical Asia and West Africa.

Examples

A chevrotain animal
Researchers observed a family of chevrotains feeding near the riverbank at dusk.
The elusive chevrotain darted through the dense undergrowth of the rainforest.
Unlike most deer, the chevrotain lacks antlers and is very small in size.
