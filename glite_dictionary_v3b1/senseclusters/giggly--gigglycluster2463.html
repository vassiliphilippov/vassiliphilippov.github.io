giggly

adjective
prone to giggling
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪɡli / (AmE), / ˈɡɪɡli / (BrE)
often laughing in a light, silly, or uncontrollable way, especially from amusement or nervousness

Examples

A giggly laugh
The group of giggly teenagers could not stop laughing at the joke.
She always gets giggly when she is nervous before a presentation.
After hearing the funny story, everyone at the table became giggly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: playful - full of fun and high spirits
  • antonym: serious - not joking or playful
