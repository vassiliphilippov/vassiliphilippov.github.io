distempered

adjective
infected with distemper
8.2
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtɛmpərd / (AmE), / dɪsˈtɛmpəd / (BrE)
affected with distemper, a contagious viral disease that commonly infects dogs and some other animals

Examples

Distempered dog
The veterinarian quickly isolated the distempered dog to prevent the disease from spreading.
Shelter workers wore protective gear when caring for distempered animals.
Symptoms in a distempered puppy can include fever, coughing, and nasal discharge.

Related Terms

  • antonym: healthy - not affected by disease; in good health
  • synonym: infected - having a disease caused by germs or viruses
