potability

noun
drinkable quality
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊtəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌpəʊtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)

Examples

Water potability test

Regular testing ensures the potability of the city's drinking water supply.
Concerns about potability led residents to boil their tap water before use.
