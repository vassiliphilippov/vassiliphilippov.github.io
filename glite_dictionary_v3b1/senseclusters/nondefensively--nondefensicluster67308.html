nondefensively

adverb
without being defensive
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.dɪˈfɛnsɪvli / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dɪˈfɛnsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that is open and not defensive, especially when receiving feedback or criticism

Examples

Listen nondefensively to feedback
She listened nondefensively to the feedback, eager to learn and improve.
The team played nondefensively, focusing on scoring rather than protecting their lead.
He responded nondefensively to the criticism, showing maturity and openness.
He answered the difficult questions nondefensively, showing he was open to feedback.
During the meeting, she responded nondefensively to criticism from her team.
It is important to listen nondefensively when someone shares their concerns with you.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defensively - in a way that is protective or intended to guard against attack or criticism
  • synonym: openly - in an honest and direct way
