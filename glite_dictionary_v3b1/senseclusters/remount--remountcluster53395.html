remount

verb
mount again, especially a horse or vehicle
5.5 6.7
Pronunciation: / riˈmaʊnt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈmaʊnt / (BrE)
to get on a horse, bicycle, or other vehicle again after being dismounted or after having got off; to mount again.

Examples

To remount a bicycle
After a brief rest, she remounted her bicycle and continued along the trail.
He remounted the motorcycle, determined to finish the race despite the setback.
The knight quickly remounted after being thrown from his horse in the joust.

Related Terms
