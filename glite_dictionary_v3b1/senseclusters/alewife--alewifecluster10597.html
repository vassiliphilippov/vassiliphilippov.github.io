alewife

noun
a migratory North American food fish
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪlˌwaɪf / (AmE), / ˈeɪlˌwaɪf / (BrE)
a North American fish of the herring family (Alosa pseudoharengus), often found in rivers and used as food (especially salted or smoked)
