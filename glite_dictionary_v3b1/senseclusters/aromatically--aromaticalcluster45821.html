aromatically

adverb
in an aromatic manner
Pronunciation: / ˌɛrəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌærəˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to aromatic compounds or displays properties typical of aromatic systems in chemistry, such as ring stabilization.

Examples

Aromatically stable compound
The compound reacted aromatically, maintaining the stability of its benzene ring.
Some molecules behave aromatically under certain reaction conditions in organic chemistry.
The catalyst allowed the process to proceed aromatically, preserving the structure of the aromatic ring.
