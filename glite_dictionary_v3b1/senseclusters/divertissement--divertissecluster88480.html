divertissement

noun
light entertainment piece
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˌvɜrtɪsˈmɑ̃ / (AmE), / dɪˌvɛːtɪsˈmɒ̃ /; / diˌvɛːtɪsˈmɒ̃ /; / dɪˌvɛːtɪsˈmɑ̃ / (BrE)
a short, light performance or activity for entertainment, often as part of a larger show or as a pleasant distraction
