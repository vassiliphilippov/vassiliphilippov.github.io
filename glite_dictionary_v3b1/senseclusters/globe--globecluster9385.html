globe

noun
a spherical map model of the earth
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡloʊb / (AmE), / ɡləʊb / (BrE)
a spherical model on which a map, especially of the earth or another celestial body, is represented

Examples

A world globe
A dusty globe sat in the corner of his office, untouched for years.
She spun the globe to show her students where Africa is located.
The children enjoyed tracing their fingers along the raised mountains of the classroom globe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atlas - a book of maps or charts
