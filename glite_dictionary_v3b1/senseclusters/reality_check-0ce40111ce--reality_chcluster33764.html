reality check

noun
occasion revealing the true situation
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈæ.lə.tiː tʃek/ /; / /rɪ.ˈæl.ɪ.tiː tʃek/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈæl.ɪ.tiː tʃek/ /; / /riː.ˈæ.lə.tiː tʃek/ (BrE)
