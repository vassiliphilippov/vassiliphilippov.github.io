spouse

noun
husband or wife
1.0
Pronunciation: / spaʊs / (AmE), / spaʊs / (BrE)
a husband or wife; a person's partner in marriage

Examples

Each spouse has rights
Each spouse has equal rights in the property acquired during the marriage.
It is important for each spouse to support the other through challenges.
She attended the ceremony with her spouse, who greeted everyone warmly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ex-spouse - a former husband or wife
  • synonym: partner - a person with whom someone shares an intimate relationship, including marriage
