unpersuasively

adverb
not convincingly
6.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.pɜːr.ˈsweɪ.sɪv.liː/ /; / /ən.pɜːr.ˈsweɪ.sɪv.liː/ (AmE), /ʌn.pə.ˈsweɪ.sɪv.liː/ /; / /ən.pə.ˈsweɪ.sɪv.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that does not convince others or fails to change their opinion

Examples

Speak unpersuasively at meeting
He spoke unpersuasively at the meeting, so few people supported his proposal.
The lawyer argued unpersuasively and the jury remained unconvinced.
Her explanation was delivered unpersuasively, leaving everyone with doubts.
