cupronickel

noun
copper-nickel alloy
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌkjuːproʊˈnɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌkjuːprəʊˈnɪkəl / (BrE)
an alloy consisting mainly of copper and nickel, typically used for coins, marine engineering, and other applications requiring corrosion resistance

Examples

A cupronickel alloy
