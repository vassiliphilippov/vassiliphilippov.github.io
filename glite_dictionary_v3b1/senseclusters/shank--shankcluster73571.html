shank

noun
cut of meat from the upper leg
8.9
Pronunciation: / ʃæŋk / (AmE), / ʃæŋk / (BrE)
a cut of meat from the upper part of the leg of an animal such as beef, veal, mutton, or lamb

Examples

The chef slowly braised the shank to make it tender and flavorful.
