underlit

adjective
lit from below
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈlɪt / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈlɪt / (BrE)
illuminated from below, often creating dramatic or unusual shadows

Examples

Underlit statue
The statue appeared mysterious when it was underlit by hidden lights at the base.
In horror films, faces are often underlit to create a spooky effect.
The artwork was underlit, casting dramatic shadows on the gallery wall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: overlit - illuminated from above
  • synonym: uplighted - illuminated by light directed upward
