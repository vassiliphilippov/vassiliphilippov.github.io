mandarin orange tree

noun
tree bearing mandarin oranges
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmændərɪn ˈɔːrɪndʒ triː / (AmE), / ˈmændərɪn ˈɒrɪndʒ triː / (BrE)
a small tree or shrub native to southeastern Asia that produces mandarin oranges, known for their sweet, aromatic pulp and loose, thin orange rind
