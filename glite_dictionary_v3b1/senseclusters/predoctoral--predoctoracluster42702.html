predoctoral

adjective
before doctoral degree
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈdɑːktərəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈdɒktərəl / (BrE)
relating to the period or status before earning a doctoral degree

Examples

Predoctoral fellowship
She received a predoctoral fellowship to support her research before starting her PhD.
Many predoctoral students attend conferences to present their early findings.
The university offers special programs for predoctoral candidates preparing for their dissertations.
