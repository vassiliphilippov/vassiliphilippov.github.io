Silesian

noun
Slavic language of Silesia
Pronunciation: / saɪˈliːʒən / (AmE), / saɪˈliːziən / (BrE)
a West Slavic language or group of dialects spoken in the Silesia region of Central Europe

Examples

Silesian language
Many older residents still speak Silesian at home with their families.
The university offers courses in Silesian language and culture.
She wrote her thesis on the grammar of Silesian dialects.
