signable

adjective
can be signed
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnəbəl / (BrE)
able to be expressed or communicated using sign language.

Examples

Signable message
The teacher made sure every instruction was signable for her Deaf students.
Some complex scientific terms are not easily signable without specialized vocabulary.
The poem was beautifully adapted into a signable version for the performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: translatable - able to be expressed in another language
  • antonym: unsignable - not able to be expressed in sign language
