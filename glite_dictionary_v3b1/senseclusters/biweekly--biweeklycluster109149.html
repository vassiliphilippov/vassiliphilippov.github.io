biweekly

adjective
every two weeks
Pronunciation: / baɪˈwiːkli / (AmE), / baɪˈwiːkli / (BrE)
Happening or produced every two weeks.

Examples

Biweekly paycheck
She receives a biweekly paycheck from her employer.
The biweekly staff meeting will be held every other Monday afternoon.
We publish our newsletter on a biweekly basis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fortnightly - occurring or appearing every two weeks
  • degree higher: monthly - occurring or appearing every month
  • degree lower: weekly - occurring or appearing every week
