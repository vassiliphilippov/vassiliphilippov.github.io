liege lord

noun
feudal lord entitled to allegiance
9.3 9.6
Pronunciation: /liːdʒ lɔːrd/ /; / /liːʒ lɔːrd/ (AmE), /liːdʒ lɔːd/ /; / /liːʒ lɔːd/ (BrE)
a feudal lord to whom allegiance and service are owed, especially in the context of medieval European society

Examples

The liege lord

The knight pledged loyalty to his liege lord above all others.
