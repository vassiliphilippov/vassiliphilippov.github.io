blasphemous

adjective
irreverent or disrespectful toward the sacred
6.0 8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈblæsfəməs / (AmE), / ˈblɑːsfəməs /; / ˈblasfəməs / (BrE)
showing disrespect or lack of reverence for something considered sacred or holy (including actions or language)

Examples

Blasphemous words

Many people found his blasphemous comments offensive during the ceremony.
The book was banned for its blasphemous depiction of sacred figures.
