spring in someone's step

noun
energetic, lively manner of walking
9.9
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz stɛp / (AmE), / sprɪŋ ɪn ˈsʌmwənz stɛp / (BrE)
an energetic and lively way of walking, often showing happiness or confidence

Examples
