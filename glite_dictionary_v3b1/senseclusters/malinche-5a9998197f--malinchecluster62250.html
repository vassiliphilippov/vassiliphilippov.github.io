Malinche

proper noun
Mexican volcano and park
9.6
Pronunciation: / maˈlintʃeɪ / (AmE), / maˈlintʃeɪ / (BrE)
a stratovolcano and national park in central Mexico, located on the border of Puebla and Tlaxcala, known for hiking and scenic views

Examples

Climb Malinche mountain
Many tourists climb Malinche to experience its breathtaking views and natural beauty.
The national park surrounding Malinche is home to diverse wildlife and forests.
From the summit of Malinche, you can see several other famous Mexican volcanoes.
