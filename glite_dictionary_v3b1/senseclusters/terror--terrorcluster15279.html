terror

noun
use of extreme fear to coerce
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrər / (AmE), / ˈtɛrə / (BrE)
the use of extreme fear to control or coerce people, especially for political purposes

Examples

Reign of terror
During the revolution, the population lived under a climate of terror.
The dictator used terror to silence all opposition.
Throughout history, some regimes have maintained power through terror and intimidation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: terrorism - the unlawful use of violence and intimidation, especially against civilians, in the pursuit of political aims
