springily

adverb
in a bouncy way
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋɪli / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is elastic, lively, or bouncy

Examples

Jump springily on trampoline
The children jumped springily on the new trampoline in the backyard.
She walked springily down the street, full of excitement for the day ahead.
The puppy bounded springily across the grass, chasing after its favorite toy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stiffly - in a rigid or inflexible manner
