signal

adjective
striking; out of the ordinary
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡ.nəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nəl / (BrE)
notably out of the ordinary; striking or distinguished

Examples

A signal achievement
His resignation was seen as a signal event that changed the company's direction.
She received a signal honor at the awards ceremony for her contributions.
The discovery marked a signal achievement in the field of medicine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ordinary - with no special or distinctive features; normal
  • synonym: remarkable - worthy of attention; striking
