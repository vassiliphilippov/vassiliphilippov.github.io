ADHD

noun
neurodevelopmental disorder causing inattention and hyperactivity
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ ˌdiː ˌeɪtʃ ˈdiː / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˌdiː ˌeɪtʃ ˈdiː / (BrE)
a neurodevelopmental disorder marked by patterns of inattentiveness, hyperactivity, and impulsivity that interfere with daily functioning or development

Examples

ADHD symptoms
Many children with ADHD benefit from structured routines and clear expectations.
She attended a support group for parents of kids with ADHD.
The doctor diagnosed him with ADHD after noticing his difficulty focusing in school.
