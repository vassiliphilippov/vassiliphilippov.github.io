moxie plum

noun
evergreen shrub with white flowers and seeds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑksi plʌm / (AmE), / ˈmɒksi plʌm / (BrE)
a slow-growing, low evergreen shrub of northern North America and Japan with white flowers and numerous rough, hairy, white, fleshy seeds

Examples

Botanists study the moxie plum for its unique white, fleshy seeds.
