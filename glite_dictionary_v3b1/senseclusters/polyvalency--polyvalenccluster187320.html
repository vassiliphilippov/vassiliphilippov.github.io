polyvalency

noun
valency greater than two (chemistry)
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːliˈveɪlənsi / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈveɪlənsi / (BrE)
(Chemistry) The state of having a valency greater than two.

Examples

Polyvalency in metals
Researchers studied the polyvalency of the element to determine its potential uses in new alloys.
Some transition metals display polyvalency, allowing them to form compounds with different oxidation states.
The concept of polyvalency is important in understanding certain chemical reactions and bonding patterns.
