nutbrown

adjective
warm brown color like nuts
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtˌbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌbraʊn / (BrE)
Of a rich, warm brown color similar to that of nuts.

Examples

Nutbrown color
He admired the nutbrown warmth of the old wooden table.
Her nutbrown hair gleamed in the sunlight as she walked through the garden.
The artist chose a nutbrown hue for the horse in his painting.
