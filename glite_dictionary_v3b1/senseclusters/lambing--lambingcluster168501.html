lambing

verb
to give birth (sheep)
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlæmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlæmɪŋ / (BrE)
to give birth to lambs, used of a sheep.

Examples

Ewes lambing tonight
The farmer stayed up all night because several ewes were lambing in the barn.
Spring is the busiest season for lambing on the sheep farm.
She learned how to assist with lambing during her agricultural internship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calving - to give birth to calves, used of cattle
  • synonym: foaling - to give birth to a foal, used of a horse
