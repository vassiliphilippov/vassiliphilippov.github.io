disincorporate

verb
to dissolve legal status
9.3
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ɪn.ˈkɔːr.pə.reɪt/ (AmE), /dɪs.ɪn.ˈkɔː.pə.reɪt/ (BrE)
to officially end the legal status of a corporation, organization, or town, so it no longer exists as a legal entity

Examples

The government decided to disincorporate the town after its population dropped below the required minimum.

Legal procedures must be followed to disincorporate a municipality in this state.

Legal procedures must be followed to properly disincorporate a company.

If the population drops too low, the state may require the town to disincorporate.
Residents debated whether to disincorporate and let the county manage local services.
