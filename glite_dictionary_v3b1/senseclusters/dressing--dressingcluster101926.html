dressing

noun
material applied to a wound
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
a piece of material or cloth placed on a wound to protect it or help it heal

Examples

Wound dressing
After cleaning the cut, he covered it with a sterile dressing.
It is important to change the dressing regularly to prevent infection.
The nurse carefully applied a fresh dressing to the patient's wound.
Before applying the dressing, be sure to sterilize the wound area thoroughly.
She tried not to let the dressing get wet while she was showering.
The nurse changed the dressing on his arm every morning to keep the wound clean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bandage - a strip of material used to bind a wound or injury
