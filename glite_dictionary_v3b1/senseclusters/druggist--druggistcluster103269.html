druggist

noun
qualified person who prepares and sells medicines
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌɡɪst / (AmE), / ˈdrʌɡɪst / (BrE)
a person qualified to prepare and sell medicines and drugs, especially one who works in a pharmacy; a pharmacist

Examples

The druggist sells medicine
In the early 1900s, a druggist might also make home remedies for common illnesses.
My grandfather worked as a druggist in a small town pharmacy for over forty years.
The druggist filled my prescription with care and explained the dosage instructions.
