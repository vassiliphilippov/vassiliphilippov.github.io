Synonyms

  • unlawfully
    4.3 0.6/m
    unlawfully is used in legal or formal texts to mean not allowed by law, while illegally is general everyday wording.
  • illicitly
    6.8 0.1/m
    illicitly often describes secret or morally wrong acts, while illegally simply means doing something against the law.
  • impermissibly
    8.1 0.04/m
    impermissibly sounds formal and covers rules or permission, while illegally focuses on actions that break the law.
  • lawlessly
    7.2 0.02/m
    lawlessly describes acting with no regard for law or order, while illegally means doing something that breaks a specific law.
