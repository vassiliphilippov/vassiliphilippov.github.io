horehound

noun
aromatic herb of the genus Marrubium
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈhɔːˌhaʊnd / (BrE)
any of several aromatic herbs of the genus Marrubium, especially Marrubium vulgare, with wrinkled leaves and small white flowers, traditionally used as a flavoring or medicinal plant

Ancient remedies often included extracts from the horehound plant.
