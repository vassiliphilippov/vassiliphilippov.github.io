set to music

verb
compose music for words or a text
2.1
Pronunciation: / sɛt tə ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / sɛt tə ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
to compose music for a text, poem, or set of words so that it can be sung or performed

Examples

To set to music a poem
Many of Shakespeare's sonnets have been set to music by various composers.
She decided to set to music the letter she found in her grandmother's attic.
The poet's words were set to music and performed at the concert.
