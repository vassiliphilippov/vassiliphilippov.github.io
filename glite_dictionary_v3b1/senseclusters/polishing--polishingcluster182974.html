polishing

noun
the act of making something shiny or smooth
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑlɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making a surface smooth and shiny by rubbing, buffing, or applying a finish

Examples

Regular polishing of floors
Regular polishing helps keep the wood floors looking new.
She spent the afternoon polishing her silverware until it gleamed.
The polishing of the marble statue took several hours to complete.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buffing - the act of polishing something using a soft cloth or machine
  • degree lower: cleaning - the act of removing dirt or impurities
