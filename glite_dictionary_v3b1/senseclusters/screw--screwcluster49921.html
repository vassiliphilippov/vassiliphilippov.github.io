screw

verb
cheat or deceive (someone)
6.7
Pronunciation: / skruː / (AmE), / skruː / (BrE)

Examples

To screw someone over

It feels like I got screwed in that deal, and there’s nothing I can do.
The company tried to screw me by not paying my full wages.
