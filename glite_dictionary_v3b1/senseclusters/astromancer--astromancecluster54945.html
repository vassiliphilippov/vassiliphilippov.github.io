astromancer

noun
star-based magician
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæstrəˌmænsər / (AmE), / ˈæstrəˌmænsə / (BrE)
a magician or person (real or fictional) who uses the power of stars or interprets the stars to perform magic or divination

Examples

A powerful astromancer
The astromancer raised her staff and summoned a shower of shooting stars.
Legends say an astromancer once saved the kingdom by reading the night sky.
In the novel, the young apprentice dreams of becoming a powerful astromancer.
The village astromancer was consulted before every important decision.
Legends tell of an astromancer who could read destinies in the night sky.
Many doubted the predictions of the astromancer, but some still believed in his powers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astrologer - a person who studies the positions of stars to predict events
  • synonym: necromancer - a magician who communicates with the dead
  • antonym: skeptic - a person who doubts or questions claims of magic or divination
  • synonym: soothsayer - a person who claims to predict the future
  • synonym: sorcerer - a person who practices magic, especially in stories
  • synonym: wizard - a man in stories who has magical powers
