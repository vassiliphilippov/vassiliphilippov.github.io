cogged

adjective
having cogs or teeth (esp. on wheels/gears)
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɑgd / (AmE), / kɒgd / (BrE)
having cogs; furnished or equipped with cogs (teeth, usually on a wheel or gear).

Examples

A cogged belt
A cogged belt provides better grip than a smooth one in machinery.
Inspect the cogged sprocket for any worn or broken teeth before using the bike.
The old clock was powered by a series of cogged wheels and gears.
