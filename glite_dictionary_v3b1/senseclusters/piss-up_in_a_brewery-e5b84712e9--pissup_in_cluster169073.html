piss-up in a brewery

noun
an example of incompetence or poor organization
9.9

(chiefly British, informal) Used to emphasize that someone is very poor at organizing things, especially in the form 'couldn't organize a piss-up in a brewery', meaning someone is incompetent or disorganized.
