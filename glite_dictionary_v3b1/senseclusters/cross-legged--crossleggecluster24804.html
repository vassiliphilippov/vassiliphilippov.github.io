cross-legged

adverb
with legs crossed while sitting
2.3 3.3
Pronunciation: / krɔs ˈlɛgd / (AmE), / krɒs ˈlɛgd / (BrE)
in a way that involves sitting with one leg crossed over the other and both knees bent outward, usually with each foot resting underneath the opposite knee or thigh

Examples

Sitting cross-legged
He listened cross-legged on the grass, enjoying the warm afternoon sun.
She usually does her homework cross-legged on the living room rug.
The children sat cross-legged in a circle, eagerly waiting for the story to begin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lotus position - a cross-legged sitting posture in meditation, especially in yoga
