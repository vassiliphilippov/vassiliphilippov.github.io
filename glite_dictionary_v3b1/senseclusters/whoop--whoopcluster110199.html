whoop

verb
shout with joy or excitement
3.7 1.43/m
Pronunciation: / wup /; / wuÀêp / (AmE), / wuÀêp / (BrE)
to give a loud, excited cry or shout, especially to show joy or enthusiasm

Examples

To whoop with excitement
After hearing the good news, she couldn't help but whoop with happiness.
The children whooped and ran through the playground, celebrating the last day of school.
The fans whooped and clapped as their team scored the winning goal.
The children whooped with excitement when they saw the fireworks light up the sky.
Fans whooped and clapped as their team scored the final goal.
She whooped in delight when she heard the good news.
