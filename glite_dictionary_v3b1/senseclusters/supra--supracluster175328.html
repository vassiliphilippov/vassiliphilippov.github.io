supra

adverb
above, earlier in a document
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsuÀêpr…ô / (AmE), / ÀàsuÀêpr…ô / (BrE)
(in writing) at an earlier point in the same document; above.

Examples

Refer to section 3 supra for additional information.
The issue, mentioned supra, is critical for this argument.
