supra

adverb
above, earlier in a document
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːprə / (AmE), / ˈsuːprə / (BrE)
(in writing) at an earlier point in the same document; above.

Examples

As discussed supra
As discussed supra, the case law supports this interpretation.
Refer to section 3 supra for additional information.
The issue, mentioned supra, is critical for this argument.

Related Terms

  • antonym: infra - at a later place in the same document
