box beam

noun
hollow rectangular structural beam
6.1
Pronunciation: / bɑks bim / (AmE), / bɒks biːm / (BrE)
a beam constructed from boards and having a hollow rectangular cross section, used in building and engineering structures

Examples

A box beam structure
A box beam offers excellent strength-to-weight ratio in modern buildings.
Carpenters assembled the box beam using plywood sheets and adhesive.
The architect specified a box beam for the long span across the living room.
