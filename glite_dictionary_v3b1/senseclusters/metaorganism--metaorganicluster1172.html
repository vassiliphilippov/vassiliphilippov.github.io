metaorganism

noun
organism plus microbes
9.9
Pronunciation: /met.ə.ˈɔːr.gə.nɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /met.ə.ˈɔː.gə.nɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a biological system consisting of an organism together with all its associated microorganisms

Examples

A metaorganism system
Scientists study the human gut as a metaorganism to understand the interactions between the host and its microbes.
The concept of a metaorganism highlights the importance of microorganisms in animal health.
Each plant can be seen as a metaorganism because of its close relationship with soil bacteria and fungi.
