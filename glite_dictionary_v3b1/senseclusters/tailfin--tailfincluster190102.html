Examples

Synonyms

  • vertical stabilizer
    5.9 0.01/m
    vertical stabilizer is the standard technical name for the upright rear surface that keeps an aircraft steady, while tailfin is a common name.
  • vertical fin
    4.3 0.02/m
    vertical fin is a simple term for a vertical stabilizing surface on the tail, while tailfin is more informal and common.
  • vertical tail
    6.0 0.02/m
    vertical tail is the whole upright tail assembly including fin and rudder, while tailfin usually names the single vertical fin.
