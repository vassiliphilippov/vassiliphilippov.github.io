nom

verb
to eat enthusiastically
Pronunciation: / nɑm / (AmE), / nɒm / (BrE)
to eat something, especially quickly or with obvious enjoyment, often used informally in speech or online

Examples

To nom a snack
After the long hike, everyone was ready to nom on the sandwiches.
She couldn't wait to nom the fresh cookies straight from the oven.
During the movie, they nommed a huge bowl of popcorn together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: devour - to eat something quickly and eagerly
  • antonym: nibble - to eat something by taking small bites
