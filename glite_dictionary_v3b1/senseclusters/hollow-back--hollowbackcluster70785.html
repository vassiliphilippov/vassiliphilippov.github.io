hollow-back

noun
abnormal inward spine curvature
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑloʊˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈhɒləʊˌbæk / (BrE)
An abnormal inward curvature of the spine, especially in the lower back; lordosis.

Examples

A hollow-back curve
Physical therapy can help alleviate discomfort caused by hollow-back.
The doctor diagnosed her with hollow-back after observing her unusual posture.
Wearing improper footwear for years may contribute to developing hollow-back.
