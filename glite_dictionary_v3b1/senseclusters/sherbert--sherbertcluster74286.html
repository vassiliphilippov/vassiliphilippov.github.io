sherbert

noun
a type of frozen dessert
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɜrbɚt / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːbət / (BrE)
A frozen dessert made from fruit juice, sugar, and often milk, egg white, or gelatin.

Examples

A scoop of sherbert
I ordered a scoop of raspberry sherbert for dessert.
Many children love the sweet, tangy flavor of sherbert.
This homemade orange sherbert is light and refreshing on a hot day.
