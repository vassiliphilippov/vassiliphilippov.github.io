pneumatic drill

noun
power drill operated by compressed air
5.0
Pronunciation: / nuˈmæt̬ɪk drɪl / (AmE), / njuːˈmætɪk drɪl / (BrE)
a power drill that is operated using compressed air, typically used for breaking up road surfaces or concrete

Examples

A pneumatic drill machine
The sound of the pneumatic drill echoed through the street early in the morning.
The workers used a pneumatic drill to break up the concrete sidewalk.
You must wear ear protection when operating a pneumatic drill due to the loud noise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jackhammer - a powerful tool that combines a hammer and a drill, typically powered by compressed air
