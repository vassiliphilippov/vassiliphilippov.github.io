skiddoo

noun
quick departure
Pronunciation: / skɪdˈduː / (AmE), / skɪdˈduː / (BrE)
a sudden or hurried departure, especially to leave quickly or escape from a place or situation

Examples

A quick skiddoo
After the argument he made a quick skiddoo from the party to calm down.
When the police arrived, the suspects made a hasty skiddoo from the scene.
Sensing trouble, she decided it was time for a skiddoo before things got worse.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arrival - the act of coming to a place
  • synonym: getaway - a quick escape or departure
