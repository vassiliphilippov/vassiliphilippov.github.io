spoony

adjective
overly sentimental in love
Pronunciation: / ˈspuːni / (AmE), / ˈspuːni / (BrE)
excessively sentimental or foolishly in love, especially in an old-fashioned or naive way

Examples

Spoony about someone
In his youth, he was quite spoony about every girl he met.
The old novel describes a spoony young man writing endless love letters.
She laughed at his spoony declarations, finding them charmingly naive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infatuated - experiencing an intense but short-lived passion for someone
  • antonym: practical - dealing with things sensibly and realistically
  • synonym: romantic - showing strong feelings of love or affection
