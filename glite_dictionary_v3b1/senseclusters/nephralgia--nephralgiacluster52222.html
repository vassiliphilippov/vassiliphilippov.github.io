nephralgia

noun
pain in the kidney
Pronunciation: / nɛˈfrældʒiə / (AmE), / nɛˈfraldʒɪə / (BrE)
Pain originating in the kidney, typically felt in the loin region.

Examples

Chronic nephralgia pain
Chronic nephralgia may indicate underlying kidney disease.
Doctors prescribed medication to alleviate her severe nephralgia.
The patient complained of persistent nephralgia after his surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: renal colic - severe pain from kidney stones
