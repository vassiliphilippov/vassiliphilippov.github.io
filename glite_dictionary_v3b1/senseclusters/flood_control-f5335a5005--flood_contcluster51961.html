flood control

noun
rate-limiting mechanism in IT
3.3
Pronunciation: / fl\u028cd k\u0259n\u02c8tro\u028al / (AmE), / fl\u028cd k\u0259n\u02c8tr\u0259\u028al / (BrE)
(in computing and online services) a mechanism that limits the rate or volume of messages, connections, or requests to prevent overload, spam, or abuse.

Examples

Flood control for messages
The chat platform uses flood control to limit rapid messages and prevent spam.
Channel operators enabled flood control to stop users from sending too many messages at once.
When the server detected repeated requests, it activated flood control to protect performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rate limiting - techniques to restrict the number of requests or messages over time
  • synonym: throttling - intentionally slowing or restricting data flow or service use to control load
