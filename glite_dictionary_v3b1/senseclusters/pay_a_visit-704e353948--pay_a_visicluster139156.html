pay a visit

verb
to visit someone or someplace
1.4
Pronunciation: / peɪ ə ˈvɪzɪt / (AmE), / peɪ ə ˈvɪzɪt / (BrE)
to visit a person or place, usually briefly or for a short period

Examples

To pay a visit to friends
Tourists often pay a visit to the famous cathedral in the city.
We should pay a visit to your parents before leaving town.
Whenever he's in the area, he likes to pay a visit to his old teachers.
