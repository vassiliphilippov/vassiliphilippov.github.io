concentrate

noun
purified mineral after removing impurities
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnsənˌtreɪt /; / ˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪt /; / ˈkɑːn.sənˌtreɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsəntreɪt /; / ˈkɒnsənˌtreɪt /; / ˈkɒn.sənˌtreɪt / (BrE)
a substance that is denser or purer than usual because water or other elements have been removed (especially in food, drinks, or minerals)
