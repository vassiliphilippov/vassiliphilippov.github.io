round

noun
stage of company fundraising
5.8
Pronunciation: /raʊnd/ (AmE), /raʊnd/ (BrE)
a stage in which a company raises money from external investors; often specified by type (seed, Series A, Series B) or purpose.

Examples

A round of funding
The startup secured a new round of financing to accelerate growth.
Investors led the round that valued the company at more than fifty million dollars.
They closed a seed round to fund product development and hire initial staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: financing round - a round of investment in which a company raises capital from investors
  • synonym: fundraising - the activity of seeking and obtaining capital or donations, especially for a company or organization
