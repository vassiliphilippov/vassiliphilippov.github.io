Republic of Haiti

proper noun
Caribbean country on Hispaniola
3.0
Pronunciation: / həˈiti / (AmE), / haɪˈiːti / (BrE)
A country in the Caribbean, occupying the western part of the island of Hispaniola; became independent from France in 1804.
