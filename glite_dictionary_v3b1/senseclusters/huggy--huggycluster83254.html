huggy

adjective
fond of hugging
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌɡi / (AmE), / ˈhʌɡi / (BrE)
fond of giving or receiving hugs, or having a tendency to hug often

Examples

Huggy child
My little sister is always in a huggy mood when she comes home from school.
The huggy puppy jumped into everyone's arms at the animal shelter.
After the good news, the whole team became very huggy and cheerful.
