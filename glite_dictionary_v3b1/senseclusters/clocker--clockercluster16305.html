clocker

noun
timekeeper at work
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈklɑː.kər/ (AmE), /ˈklɒ.kə/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to operate a time clock or record employees' working hours for payroll or attendance

Examples

Payroll clocker
The clocker made sure every employee signed in at the start of their shift.
After the meeting, the clocker updated the attendance records for the week.
If you forget to punch in, the clocker will need to adjust your hours manually.
