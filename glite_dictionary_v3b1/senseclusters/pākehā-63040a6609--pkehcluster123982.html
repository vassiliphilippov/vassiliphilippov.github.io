Pākehā

adjective
non-Māori New Zealand
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːkɛhɑː / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkəhɑː / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of non-Māori New Zealanders, especially those of European descent or their culture

Examples

Pakeha culture
The Pākehā community has played a significant role in shaping modern New Zealand society.
Some traditional Pākehā customs differ greatly from those of the Māori population.
The festival celebrated both Māori and Pākehā heritage through music and food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: European New Zealander - a New Zealander of European descent
  • antonym: Māori - relating to the indigenous Polynesian people of New Zealand
