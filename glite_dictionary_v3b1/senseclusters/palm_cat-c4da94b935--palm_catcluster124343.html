palm cat

noun
arboreal civet of Southeast Asia
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɑm kæt / (AmE), / pɑːm kæt / (BrE)
a spotted or striped tree-dwelling civet native to Southeast Asia and the East Indies

Examples

The palm cat animal
Because of its distinctive markings, the palm cat is often confused with other small wild cats.
Farmers sometimes see a palm cat searching for food near their plantations at night.
The palm cat is well-adapted to life in the trees of the tropical forests.
