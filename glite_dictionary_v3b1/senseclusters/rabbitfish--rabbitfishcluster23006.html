rabbitfish

noun
large European chimaera fish
Pronunciation: / ˈræbɪtˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈræbɪtˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a large European chimaera, a type of cartilaginous fish found in deep waters

Examples

Deep-sea rabbitfish
Marine biologists study the unique features of the rabbitfish.
The rabbitfish is known for its elongated body and large head.
The deep-sea trawler caught a rabbitfish off the coast of Norway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chimaera - a cartilaginous fish related to sharks and rays
