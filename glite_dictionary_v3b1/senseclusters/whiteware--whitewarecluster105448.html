whiteware

noun
ceramic tableware
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtˌweə / (BrE)
ceramic dishes and tableware, such as plates, bowls, and cups, typically used for serving food

Examples

Ceramic whiteware plates
The restaurant served every meal on elegant whiteware to create a clean, modern look.
She bought a new set of whiteware for her kitchen after moving into her apartment.
After the party, they carefully washed all the whiteware and put it back in the cupboard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chinaware - dishes and tableware made of porcelain
  • synonym: stoneware - durable ceramic ware made from stoneware clay
