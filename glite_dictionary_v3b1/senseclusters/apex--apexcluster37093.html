apex

noun
direction of the sun's motion in astronomy
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.pɛks / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.pɛks / (BrE)
the point on the celestial sphere toward which the sun and solar system appear to be moving relative to the fixed stars

Examples

Solar apex location
Astronomers have determined the exact coordinates of the solar apex using advanced telescopes.
Knowledge of the apex helps scientists understand the motion of our solar system through space.
The sun is moving toward the constellation Hercules, in the direction of the apex.
