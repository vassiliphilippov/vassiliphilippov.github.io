sphingine

adjective
like a sphinx; enigmatic
Pronunciation: / ˈsfɪn.dʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈsfɪŋ.gɪn / (BrE)
Resembling a sphinx, especially in being enigmatic or mysterious.

Examples

A sphingine expression
His sphingine silence made everyone in the room uncomfortable.
She gave me a sphingine smile that I couldn't decipher.
The detective maintained a sphingine expression as she listened to the suspect.
