clear and present danger

noun
legal test for restricting free speech
3.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /klɪr ənd ˈprez.ənt ˈdeɪn.dʒər/ /; / /klɪr ænd ˈprez.ənt ˈdeɪn.dʒər/ (AmE), /klɪə ənd ˈprez.ənt ˈdeɪn.dʒə/ /; / /klɪə ænd ˈprez.ənt ˈdeɪn.dʒə/ (BrE)
a legal principle allowing restriction of free speech if it poses an imminent and obvious threat to public safety or order

Examples

A clear and present danger
