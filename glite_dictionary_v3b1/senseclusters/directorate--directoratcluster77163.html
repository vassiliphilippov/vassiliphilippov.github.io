directorate

noun
managing group in a corporation or institution
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛktərət / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛktərət / (BrE)
a group of people chosen to manage or govern the affairs of a corporation, institution, or organization

Examples

Company directorate
He was appointed to serve on the hospital's directorate last year.
The directorate decided to implement new policies to improve efficiency.
The company's directorate met to discuss the annual budget and long-term strategy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: board of directors - a group of individuals elected to oversee the activities of a company or organization
