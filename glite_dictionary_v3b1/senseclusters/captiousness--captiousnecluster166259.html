captiousness

noun
tendency to find fault
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkæp.ʃəs.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈkæp.ʃəs.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being overly critical and finding fault with small or unimportant things

Examples

Constant captiousness
His constant captiousness made it hard for the team to enjoy their success.
