pulmonologist

noun
lung specialist doctor
Pronunciation: / ˌpʌlməˈnɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌpʊlməˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating diseases of the lungs and respiratory system

Examples

A pulmonologist doctor
The pulmonologist ordered several tests to better understand her breathing problems.
After months of coughing, he finally saw a pulmonologist for a thorough examination.
A pulmonologist can help manage chronic conditions like asthma and COPD.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cardiologist - a doctor who specializes in diseases of the heart
  • synonym: respiratory physician - a doctor specializing in diseases of the respiratory system
