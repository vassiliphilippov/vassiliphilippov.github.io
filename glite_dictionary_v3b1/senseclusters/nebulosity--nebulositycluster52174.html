nebulosity

noun
cloud-like mass
Pronunciation: / ˌnɛbjəˈlɑsəti / (AmE), / ˌnɛbjʊˈlɒsəti /; / ˌnɛbjʊˈlɒsɪti / (BrE)
a cloudy, misty, or hazy mass or appearance, especially in space or in a liquid

Examples

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear
  • synonym: haze - a slight obscuration of the lower atmosphere, typically caused by fine suspended particles
  • synonym: nebula - a cloud of gas and dust in space
