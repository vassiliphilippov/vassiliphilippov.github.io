Garbage State

noun
derogatory term for littered or mismanaged area
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ steɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ steɪt / (BrE)
an insulting nickname for an area considered notably littered or poorly governed

Examples

The Garbage State nickname
After years of neglect, the riverfront gave rise to the nickname Garbage State among residents.
Local newspapers criticized officials for allowing the city to become known as the Garbage State.
No one wants their hometown branded as a Garbage State in national headlines.
