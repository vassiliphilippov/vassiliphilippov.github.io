former; previous time
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛstər / (AmE), / ˈjɛstə / (BrE)
a combining form used before nouns or adjectives to mean 'of a previous time' or 'former', as in yesteryear or yesternight

Examples

Yester-year
The poet used yester- in phrases like yesteryear to give a nostalgic tone.
Words like yesternight and yestermorn are rarely used in modern English.
The old song spoke of yester-days filled with laughter and joy.

Related Terms
