move

noun
player's action in a game
Pronunciation: / muːv / (AmE), / muːv / (BrE)
an action taken by a player during their turn in a game, as permitted by the rules

Examples

A chess move
Each player is allowed only one move per turn in this board game.
Her brilliant move won her the chess match.
It's your move, so please decide what to do next.

Related Terms

  • synonym: turn - one's opportunity to act in sequence in a game or activity
