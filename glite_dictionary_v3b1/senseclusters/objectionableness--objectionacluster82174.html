objectionableness

noun
quality of being objectionable or offensive
9.5
Pronunciation: / əbˈdʒɛkʃənəblnəs / (AmE), / əbˈdʒɛkʃənəblnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being objectionable, offensive, or causing dislike or hatred.

Examples

The objectionableness of behavior
His behavior was characterized by a level of objectionableness that made collaboration impossible.
She spoke out against the objectionableness of the language used in the advertisement.
The objectionableness of the proposal led to widespread criticism from the community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acceptability - the quality of being acceptable
  • synonym: offensiveness - the quality of being offensive or annoying
