dimwit

noun
a stupid or unintelligent person
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪmˌwɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɪmˌwɪt / (BrE)
a person who is stupid or lacks intelligence

Examples

A dimwit person
Don’t be such a dimwit—double-check your answers before turning in the test.
Only a dimwit would try to fix electrical wiring without turning off the power first.
She called him a dimwit after he forgot his own birthday.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genius - an exceptionally intelligent person
  • synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
