raggy

adjective
torn or shabby
Pronunciation: / ˈræɡi / (AmE), / ˈræɡi / (BrE)
looking torn, worn out, or resembling something made of rags

Examples

Raggy old coat
The stray dog had a raggy coat and sad eyes.
He wore a raggy sweater that was full of holes.
After the long hike, their clothes looked dirty and raggy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neat - tidy and in good order
  • synonym: shabby - in poor condition through long use or lack of care
  • synonym: tattered - old and torn; in poor condition
