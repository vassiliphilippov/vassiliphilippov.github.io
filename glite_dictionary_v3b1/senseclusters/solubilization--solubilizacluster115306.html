solubilization

noun
making soluble
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊljəbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌsɑːljəbɪləˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɒljʊbɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making a substance soluble or more soluble in a liquid or solvent

Examples

Adding certain chemicals can aid the solubilization of oils in cleaning products.
