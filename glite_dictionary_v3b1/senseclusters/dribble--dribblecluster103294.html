dribble

noun
moving a ball by repeated taps or kicks
3.3 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈdrɪb.əl/ (AmE), /ˈdrɪb.əl/ (BrE)
the act of moving a ball forward by repeatedly tapping, bouncing, or kicking it, especially in sports such as soccer or basketball

Examples

A dribble in soccer
A well-timed dribble can break through a tight defense.
His impressive dribble past three defenders led to a goal.
The coach praised her quick dribble up the court.
