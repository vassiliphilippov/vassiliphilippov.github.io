dander

noun
tiny skin flakes causing allergies
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdændər / (AmE), / ˈdændə / (BrE)
Tiny flakes or particles from animal skin, hair, or feathers, which can trigger allergic reactions in some people.

Examples

Cat dander allergy
Cat dander is one of the most common indoor allergens.
People with pet allergies often react to dander rather than to the animal itself.
Regular cleaning can help reduce the amount of dander in your home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allergen - something that causes an allergic reaction
