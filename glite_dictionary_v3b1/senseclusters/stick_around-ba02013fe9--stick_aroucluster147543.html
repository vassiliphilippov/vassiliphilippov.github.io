stick around

verb
remain in a place; not leave
2.3 2.5
Pronunciation: /stɪk ə.ˈraʊnd/ /; / /stɪk ə.ˈraʊn/ (AmE), /stɪk ə.ˈraʊnd/ /; / /stɪk ə.ˈraʊn/ (BrE)
to stay in a place and not leave, often waiting for something to happen
