naturistic

adjective
characteristic of naturism; advocating nudity
Pronunciation: / ˌnætʃəˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnatʃʊˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of naturism, especially advocating or practicing social nudity as a lifestyle or philosophy

Examples

Naturistic lifestyle
She enjoys painting in a naturistic environment, feeling at one with nature.
Some people seek out naturistic communities to embrace a lifestyle of social nudity.
The resort is known for its naturistic policy, which encourages guests to relax without clothing.
