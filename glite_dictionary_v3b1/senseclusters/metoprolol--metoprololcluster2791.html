metoprolol

noun
a beta-blocker drug for heart conditions
5.9
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈtoʊ.prə.lɑːl/ /; / /mɪ.ˈtoʊ.prə.lɑːl/ (AmE), /mɪ.ˈtɒ.prə.lɒl/ (BrE)
