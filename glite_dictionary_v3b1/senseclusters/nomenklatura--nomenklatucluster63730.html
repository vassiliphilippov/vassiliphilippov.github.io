nomenklatura

noun
elite ruling group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊmɛŋkləˈtʊrə / (AmE), / ˌnəʊmɛnkləˈtjʊərə / (BrE)
a group of people who hold important administrative or influential positions in government or organizations, especially in a communist system

Examples

The nomenklatura elite
During the Soviet era, the nomenklatura controlled access to the most powerful positions.
Membership in the nomenklatura often meant special privileges and influence.
The new leader promised to reduce the power of the nomenklatura in government.
