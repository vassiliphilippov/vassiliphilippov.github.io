nonexploratory

adjective
not exploratory; not involving exploration
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.ɪkˈsplɔr.əˌtɔr.i / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.ɪkˈsplɔː.rə.tər.i / (BrE)
Not involving or characterized by exploration or investigation.

Examples

A nonexploratory role
His role on the team was largely nonexploratory, focusing on routine analysis instead of new research.
Our project is in a nonexploratory phase, so no new areas will be investigated.
The study followed a nonexploratory approach, strictly testing predetermined hypotheses.

  • antonym: exploratory - involving or relating to exploration or investigation
