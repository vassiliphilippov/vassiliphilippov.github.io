caseinate

adjective
containing caseinate
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪsiəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈkeɪsiəneɪt / (BrE)
made from or containing caseinate, especially used to describe products or ingredients with a caseinate compound

Examples

Caseinate powder ingredient

Some people avoid caseinate additives due to milk allergies.
