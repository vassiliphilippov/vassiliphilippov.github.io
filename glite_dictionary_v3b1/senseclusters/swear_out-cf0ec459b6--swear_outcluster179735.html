swear out

verb
officially issue (a warrant or summons)
Pronunciation: / swɛr aʊt / (AmE), / swɛə aʊt / (BrE)
to officially issue (a warrant or summons) by making a sworn statement, especially in a legal context

Examples

To swear out a summons
After the theft, the shopkeeper swore out a summons against the suspect.
She threatened to swear out a complaint if the harassment continued.
The police decided to swear out a warrant for his arrest after gathering enough evidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: issue - to formally give out (especially a legal document)
