Synonyms

  • careful consideration
    3.5 0.8/m
    careful consideration means thorough, organized thinking and review before choosing, while consideration names the general act of thinking before a decision.
  • deliberation
    4.9 7/m
    deliberation is careful, often group discussion before a decision, while consideration can be any careful thinking or talking alone.
  • weighing
    4.1 0.5/m
    weighing is measuring pros and cons to judge options, while consideration is the broader act of thinking before making a choice.
  • reflection
    3.7 18/m
    reflection is quiet, personal thinking about past events or choices, while consideration focuses on thinking or discussing before decisions.
  • contemplation
    4.8 4/m
    contemplation is long, deep looking or thinking often without deciding, while consideration is thinking or discussing before making a decision.
