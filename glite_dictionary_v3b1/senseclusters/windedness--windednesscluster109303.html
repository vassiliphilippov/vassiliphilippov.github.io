windedness

noun
shortness of breath
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪn.dɪd.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈwɪn.dɪd.nəs/ (BrE)
the state of being out of breath or having difficulty breathing after exertion

Examples

Sudden windedness
After climbing the steep hill, she felt a sudden windedness and had to stop to catch her breath.
The player's windedness was obvious after sprinting across the field several times.
People with asthma may experience windedness even after light exercise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breathlessness - the condition of being unable to breathe easily
  • synonym: dyspnea - medical term for difficult or labored breathing
