tarboosh

noun
a conical red felt cap with tassel
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / t…ërÀàbu É / (AmE), / t…ëÀêÀàbuÀê É / (BrE)
A man's red felt cap shaped like a flat-topped cone, usually with a tassel hanging from the crown.

Examples

A tarboosh cap
He wore a tarboosh with a gold tassel for the ceremony.
In old photographs, many Ottoman officials can be seen wearing a tarboosh.
The tarboosh added a traditional touch to his formal attire.
