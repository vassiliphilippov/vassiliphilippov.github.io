convector

noun
space heater using air convection
6.6 6.5
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈvek.tər/ (AmE), /kən.ˈvek.tə/ /; / /kɒn.ˈvek.tə/ (BrE)
A device or space heater that warms air and circulates it through a room by convection.
