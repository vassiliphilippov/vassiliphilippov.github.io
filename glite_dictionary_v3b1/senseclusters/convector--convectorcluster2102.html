convector

noun
space heater using air convection
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɛktər / (AmE), / kənˈvɛktə / (BrE)
A device or space heater that warms air and circulates it through a room by convection.

Examples

Electric convector heater
During the winter, the convector kept the living room warm and comfortable.
He adjusted the settings on the convector to increase the room temperature.
The office was fitted with a modern convector to provide efficient heating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: radiator - a heating device that transfers heat mainly by radiation
