thrash out

verb
discuss thoroughly to reach agreement
4.9 4.3
Pronunciation: /θræʃ aʊt/ (AmE), /θræʃ aʊt/ (BrE)
to discuss a problem or issue thoroughly in order to reach a solution or agreement

Examples

To thrash out a problem
