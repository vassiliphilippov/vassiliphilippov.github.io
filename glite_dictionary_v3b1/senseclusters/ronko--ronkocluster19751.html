ronko

noun
small flat-bottomed boat
a small flat-bottomed boat used on rivers and lakes for fishing or short transport

Examples

Fishing ronko boat
Fishermen paddled their ronko quietly across the misty lake at sunrise.
The children enjoyed a ride in the old ronko along the riverbank.
They repaired the wooden ronko before using it to transport supplies across the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canoe - a narrow, lightweight boat pointed at both ends and paddled
  • synonym: dinghy - a small open boat often used for fishing or as a tender
