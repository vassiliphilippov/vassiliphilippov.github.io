paint

noun
cosmetic powder for cheeks
2.8
Pronunciation: / peɪnt / (AmE), / peɪnt / (BrE)
Makeup consisting of a red or pink powder applied to the cheeks to give color.

Examples

Cheek paint
In Victorian novels, a woman's use of paint was often considered scandalous.
She applied a touch of paint to her cheeks before the evening party.
The actress's paint gave her a youthful, rosy glow on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blusher - modern term for cosmetic applied to color the cheeks
  • synonym: rouge - red or pink cosmetic applied to the cheeks
