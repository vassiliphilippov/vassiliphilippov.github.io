frostbiting

verb
to injure with cold
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔstˌbaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfrɒstˌbaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
to cause injury to body tissue due to exposure to extreme cold.

Examples

The cold frostbiting skin
The icy wind was frostbiting his cheeks as he walked through the snowstorm.
Without gloves, the cold air quickly started frostbiting her hands.
Climbers must protect their skin to avoid frostbiting during high-altitude expeditions.
