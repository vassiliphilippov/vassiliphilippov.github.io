publishable

adjective
suitable for publication
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪʃəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪʃəbl̩ / (BrE)
suitable or fit to be published, especially in print or online media.

Examples

Publishable manuscript
He worked tirelessly to ensure his manuscript was publishable by industry standards.
Her research findings are not only interesting but also highly publishable in academic journals.
The editor decided the story was finally publishable after several rounds of revisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: printable - suitable for being printed or published
  • antonym: unpublishable - not suitable for publication
