trifle with

verb
treat lightly or carelessly
1.9 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtraɪ.fəl wɪð/ /; / /ˈtraɪ.fəl wɪθ/ (AmE), /ˈtraɪ.fəl wɪð/ /; / /ˈtraɪ.fəl wɪθ/ (BrE)
