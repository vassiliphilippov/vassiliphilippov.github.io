valet parking

noun
service where an attendant parks guests' cars
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌvæleɪ ˈpɑːrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌvæleɪ ˈpɑːkɪŋ / (BrE)
a service at establishments such as restaurants, clubs, or airports, where an attendant parks and retrieves guests' cars for them

Examples

Valet parking service
He handed the keys to the attendant at the valet parking stand outside the restaurant.
Many airports now provide valet parking services for busy travelers.
The hotel offers valet parking for all its guests, making arrival convenient and stress-free.

Related Terms
