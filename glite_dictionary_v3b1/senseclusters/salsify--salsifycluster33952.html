salsify

noun
a Mediterranean plant with edible root
Pronunciation: / ˈsælsəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsælsɪfi / (BrE)
A Mediterranean plant with long edible white roots and purple flowers, cultivated as a vegetable.

Examples

Salsify plant flowers
In the spring, purple flowers appear atop the salsify plant.
She pulled a salsify root from the garden to use in her soup.
The restaurant served roasted salsify alongside potatoes and carrots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scorzonera - another root vegetable plant sometimes called black salsify
