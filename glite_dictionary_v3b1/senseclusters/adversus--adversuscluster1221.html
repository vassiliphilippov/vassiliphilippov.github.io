adversus

x
Latin 'against' in law
9.9
Pronunciation: / ædˈvɜːrsəs / (AmE), / ædˈvɜːsəs / (BrE)
a Latin preposition meaning 'against', used in legal case names to indicate opposition between parties

Examples

Brown adversus Green
The court case was officially titled Brown adversus Green in the legal records.
In older legal documents, you may find adversus used instead of 'versus' between party names.
The judge referred to the matter as Johnson adversus Smith during the proceedings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: versus - used to indicate opposition in legal cases or contests
