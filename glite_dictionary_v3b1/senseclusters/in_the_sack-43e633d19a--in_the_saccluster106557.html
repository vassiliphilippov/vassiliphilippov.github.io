in the sack

adverb
asleep or in bed
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɪn ðə sæk / (AmE), / ɪn ðə sæk / (BrE)
asleep or resting, especially in bed

Examples

To be in the sack

I tried calling Lisa, but she was already in the sack by ten o'clock.
