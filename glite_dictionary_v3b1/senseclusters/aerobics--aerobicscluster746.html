aerobics

noun
exercise to improve cardiovascular fitness
Pronunciation: / ɛˈroʊbɪks / (AmE), / ɛəˈrəʊbɪks / (BrE)
a system of physical exercises, often performed to music, designed to improve the efficiency of the body's cardiovascular system by increasing oxygen intake

Examples

To do aerobics exercises
Regular aerobics can help boost your energy and improve your overall health.
She attends aerobics classes at the local gym every Monday and Thursday.
The instructor led the group through a challenging aerobics routine set to upbeat music.

  • synonym: exercise - physical activity to maintain or improve health and fitness
