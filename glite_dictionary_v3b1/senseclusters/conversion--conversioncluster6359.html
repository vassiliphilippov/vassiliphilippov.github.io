conversion

noun
change from one form or unit to another

Examples

Synonyms

    4.1 0.67/m

  • switchover
    3.7 0.07/m
    switchover is the moment or short process of switching services or systems, while conversion can mean a broader change of form or units.
  • transformation
    3.7 27/m
    transformation is a complete change in form, nature, or character, while conversion can be a partial or functional change between forms.
  • transition
    3.8 5.56/m
    transition is the act of moving from one state or activity to another, while conversion focuses on changing form, use, or unit.
  • transmutation
    8.6 0.22/m
