preregistration

noun
early sign-up
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of signing up or enrolling for something before the official start or deadline

Examples

Preregistration for classes
Preregistration for the summer camp is required to reserve your spot.
The university encourages preregistration to help organize class sizes in advance.
Due to high demand, preregistration for the workshop filled up quickly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cancellation - the act of calling off or withdrawing from something
  • synonym: enrollment - the process of officially joining or signing up for something
  • synonym: registration - the act of signing up or enrolling for something
