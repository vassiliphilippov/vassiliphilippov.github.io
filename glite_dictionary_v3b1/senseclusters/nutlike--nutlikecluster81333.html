nutlike

adjective
resembling a nut in shape/size
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌt.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈnʌt.laɪk / (BrE)
Resembling a nut in shape or size.

Examples

Nutlike shape
She found a small, nutlike object lying on the forest floor.
The chef used a spice with a nutlike flavor to enhance the dish.
The seeds have a nutlike appearance and are often mistaken for actual nuts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nutty - having a flavor or aroma like that of nuts
