retail store

noun
shop selling goods to consumers
1.0 2.40/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàriÀê.te…™l st…îÀêr / (AmE), / ÀàriÀê.te…™l st…îÀê / (BrE)
A shop or business where goods are sold directly to consumers, usually in small quantities.

Examples

A retail store shop
Many people prefer to shop in a retail store rather than online.
The new retail store on Main Street attracts hundreds of customers every day.
This company plans to open several new retail stores across the country next year.

Synonyms

  • shop
    1.0 65/m
    shop is often a small, local place selling goods directly to people, while retail store is a general term for such businesses.
  • store
    1.0 168/m
    store is a general place that sells goods to the public, while retail store often refers to businesses selling directly in small quantities.
  • sales outlet
    2.6 0.04/m
    sales outlet is a formal business term for a place that sells to customers, while retail store feels more everyday and local.
  • outlet
    2.3 6.92/m
    outlet is often a brand or discount shop selling surplus goods, while retail store usually means any shop selling items directly to consumers.
