fibrousness

noun
quality of being fibrous
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbɹəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈfaɪbɹəsnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of containing or consisting of fibers, especially in texture or structure.

Examples

The fibrousness of paper
Paper quality can be affected by the fibrousness of its raw materials.
Some cuts of meat are avoided due to their fibrousness.
The fibrousness of the vegetable made it difficult to chew.

  • synonym: fibrous - containing or resembling fibers
