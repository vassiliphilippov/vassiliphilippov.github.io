faith-based charity

noun
charity based on religious beliefs
3.3 3.1
Pronunciation: /feɪθ beɪst ˈtʃær.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /feɪθ beɪst ˈtʃær.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
