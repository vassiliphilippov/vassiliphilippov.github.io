sloop

noun
single-masted sailing boat
8.1 0.71/m
Pronunciation: / sluÀêp / (AmE), / sluÀêp / (BrE)
a small sailing boat with a single mast set forward of the centre, usually equipped with a fore-and-aft mainsail and a single headsail

Examples

A sloop boat
After repairing the mast, they took the sloop out for its maiden voyage.
He sailed his sloop across the bay every summer weekend.
The harbor was filled with sloops of various sizes and colors.

Synonyms

  • sailboat
    1.1 3.10/m
    sailboat is a small boat moved by sails in general, while sloop is a specific single-masted sailboat with a fore-and-aft mainsail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cutter - a sailing vessel similar to a sloop but with a mast set further aft and more than one headsail
