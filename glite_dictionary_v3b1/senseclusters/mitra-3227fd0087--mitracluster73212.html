Mitra

proper noun
Hindu god of friendship and alliances
Pronunciation: / ˈmiː.trə / (AmE), / ˈmiː.trə / (BrE)
In Hindu mythology, the god of friendship and alliances, often invoked with Varuna as a supporter of heaven and earth.

Examples

Mitra god of friendship
Mitra is worshipped as a deity of friendship and harmony in Hindu tradition.
Scholars study the role of Mitra in Vedic rituals and mythology.
The ancient hymns often invoke Mitra and Varuna as protectors of cosmic order.
