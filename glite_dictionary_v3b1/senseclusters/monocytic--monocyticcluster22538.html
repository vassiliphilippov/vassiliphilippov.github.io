monocytic

adjective
of monocytes
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnəˈsaɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəˈsaɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving monocytes, which are a type of white blood cell

Examples

Monocytic white cells
The laboratory report showed a monocytic response to the infection.
Doctors observed an unusually high number of monocytic cells in the patient's blood.
Certain diseases can cause monocytic infiltration in body tissues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: granulocytic - relating to granulocytes, a group of white blood cells
  • synonym: lymphocytic - relating to lymphocytes, another type of white blood cell
