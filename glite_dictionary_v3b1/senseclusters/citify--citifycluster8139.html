citify

verb
make or become urban; accustom to city life
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪt̬.ɪ.faɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪt.ɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
to make or become urban in character; to accustom to city life or ways

Examples

To citify a town
As more people moved in from the city, local businesses started to citify their offerings.
Rapid development has citified many previously quiet rural areas.
The influx of new technology began to citify the small agricultural town.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ruralize - make (something) more rural
  • synonym: urbanize - make (something) more urban
