Zygomycota

noun
a former group of fungi producing zygospores
Pronunciation: / zaɪˌɡoʊmaɪˈkoʊtə /; / zaɪɡoʊmaɪˈkoʊtə / (AmE), / zaɪˌɡəʊmaɪˈkəʊtə /; / zaɪɡəʊmaɪˈkəʊtə / (BrE)
a group of fungi (formerly a division) characterized by producing zygospores during sexual reproduction
