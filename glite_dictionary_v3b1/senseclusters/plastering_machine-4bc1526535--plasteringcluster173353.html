plastering machine

noun
machine for applying plaster to walls
4.8 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈplæ.stər.ɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (AmE), /ˈplɑː.stə.rɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (BrE)
a machine used to apply plaster or render to walls and ceilings, typically in construction or building work

Examples
