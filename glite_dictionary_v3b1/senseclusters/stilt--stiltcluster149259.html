stilt

noun
pole with foot support for walking high
5.6
Pronunciation: / stɪlt / (AmE), / stɪlt / (BrE)
one of a pair of tall wooden or metal poles with supports for the feet, used to walk high above the ground

Examples

Walk on stilts
Circus performers often use stilts to tower above the crowd and entertain.
The children had fun learning to walk on stilts at the summer fair.
Workers used stilts to paint the high ceilings without scaffolding.
