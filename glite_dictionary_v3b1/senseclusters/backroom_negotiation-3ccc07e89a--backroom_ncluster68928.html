backroom negotiation

noun
secretive or informal discussion to make a deal
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌruːm nəˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈbækruːm nəˌɡəʊʃiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a secretive or informal discussion, often held behind the scenes, to reach an agreement or decision, especially in politics or business

Examples

Backroom negotiation between leaders
After hours of backroom negotiation, the two companies finally agreed to merge.
Political decisions are often shaped by backroom negotiation rather than public debate.
The outcome was determined by a backroom negotiation between party leaders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backroom deal - an agreement made privately and secretly, often dishonestly
  • synonym: negotiation - discussion aimed at reaching an agreement
