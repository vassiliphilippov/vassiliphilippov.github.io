arbor

verb
to plant trees around
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrbɚ / (AmE), / ˈɑːbə / (BrE)
to provide an area with trees or shelter it by planting trees around it

Examples

To arbor a street
They arbored the narrow lane last spring to give it summer shade and privacy.
The city plans to arbor the new park to create a peaceful green space.
Volunteers arbored the playground, making it cooler and more inviting for children.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deforest - to remove trees from an area
  • synonym: shade - to provide shade or shelter
