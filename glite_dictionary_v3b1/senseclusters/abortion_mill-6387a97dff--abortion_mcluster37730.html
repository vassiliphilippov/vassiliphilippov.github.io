abortion mill

noun
abortion clinic (pejorative)
9.3
Pronunciation: / əˈbɔɹʃən mɪl / (AmE), / əˈbɔːʃən mɪl / (BrE)
an abortion clinic, especially one criticized as being unethical or running as a high-volume business; often used pejoratively

Examples

An abortion mill clinic
Local protesters demanded the closure of the so-called abortion mill downtown.
Some activists use the term abortion mill to criticize clinics they believe operate unethically.
The article accused the doctor of turning his clinic into an abortion mill.
