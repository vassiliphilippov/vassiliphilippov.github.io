credit

verb
ascribe an achievement to someone
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛdɪt / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪt / (BrE)
to publicly acknowledge or recognize someone as responsible for something or as the source of information

You should credit your colleague for helping with the project.
