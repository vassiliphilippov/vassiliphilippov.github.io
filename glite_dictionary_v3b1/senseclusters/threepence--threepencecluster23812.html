threepence

noun
old British coin worth three pennies
Pronunciation: / ˈθriːpəns / (AmE), / ˈθrɛpəns / (BrE)
a former British coin worth three pennies, used before decimalisation in the United Kingdom

Examples

A threepence coin
Before decimalisation, a loaf of bread might cost a threepence in England.
Collectors are often interested in rare threepence coins from the Victorian era.
He found an old threepence in his grandfather’s coin collection.
