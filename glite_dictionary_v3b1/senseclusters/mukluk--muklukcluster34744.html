mukluk

noun
Arctic soft boot
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌkˌlʌk / (AmE), / ˈmʌkˌlʌk / (BrE)
a soft, warm boot made from animal skin or fabric, traditionally worn in Arctic regions to protect against cold

Examples

A pair of mukluks

Traditional mukluks are often decorated with colorful patterns and beads.

In the harsh Arctic winter, she wore mukluks to keep her feet warm and dry.
Traditional mukluks are often decorated with colorful beadwork and fur trim.
He bought a pair of mukluks before his trip to Alaska to prepare for the cold.
