undilated

adjective
not expanded
6.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdaɪleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdaɪleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not widened or expanded; remaining in a normal or contracted state

Examples

The undilated pupil
The nurse observed that the patient's pupils remained undilated after the medication was given.
During the eye exam, the doctor checked if the eyes were undilated before proceeding.
In low light, most people's pupils are not undilated but become larger to let in more light.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dilated - widened or expanded
