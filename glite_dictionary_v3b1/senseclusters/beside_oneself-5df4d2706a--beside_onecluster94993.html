beside oneself

adjective
overcome with strong emotion
6.7
Pronunciation: / bɪˈsaɪd wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / bɪˈsaɪd wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
overwhelmed by strong emotion, such as anger, joy, or worry, to the point of losing self-control

Examples

Beside himself with anger
After losing his keys, he became beside himself with frustration.
She was beside herself with excitement when her book was published.
When he heard the shocking news, he was beside himself with grief.

Related Terms

  • synonym: out of one's mind - temporarily not in control of one's thoughts or behavior because of emotion
