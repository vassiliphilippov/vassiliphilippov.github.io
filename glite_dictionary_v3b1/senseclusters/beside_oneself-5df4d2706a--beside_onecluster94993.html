beside oneself

adjective
overcome with strong emotion
6.7 5.6
Pronunciation: /bɪ.ˈsaɪd wʌn.ˈself/ /; / /bə.ˈsaɪd wʌn.ˈself/ /; / /bɪ.ˈsaɪd wən.ˈself/ (AmE), /bɪ.ˈsaɪd wʌn.ˈself/ /; / /bə.ˈsaɪd wʌn.ˈself/ (BrE)
overwhelmed by strong emotion, such as anger, joy, or worry, to the point of losing self-control
