surcharge

noun
an additional charge or fee
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.tʃɑːrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.tʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
an extra charge added to the usual cost, often as a penalty or for additional services

Examples

A fuel surcharge
A fuel surcharge is added to the cost of airline tickets during times of high oil prices.
Customers will be required to pay a surcharge for late payment of their bills.
There was a surcharge for requesting expedited shipping.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fee - a payment made for a service
  • synonym: penalty - a punishment or consequence for breaking a rule or law
