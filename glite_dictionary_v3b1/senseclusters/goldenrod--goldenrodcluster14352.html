goldenrod

noun
a plant with yellow flower clusters
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldənˌrɑd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldənˌrɒd / (BrE)
a tall plant with clusters of small, bright yellow flowers, commonly found in North America, especially of the genus Solidago

Examples

A bunch of goldenrod
A bunch of goldenrod brightened the kitchen table.
In late summer, the fields are filled with blooming goldenrod.
Many people confuse goldenrod with plants that cause hay fever, but it's not a significant allergen.
