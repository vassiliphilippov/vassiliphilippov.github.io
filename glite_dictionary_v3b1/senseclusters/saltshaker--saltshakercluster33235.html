saltshaker

noun
container for sprinkling salt
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔltˌʃeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈsɒltˌʃeɪkə / (BrE)
A container with a perforated top used for sprinkling salt onto food.

Examples

A saltshaker on table

The saltshaker on the table was completely empty.
