heap on praise

verb
give excessive praise
Pronunciation: / hip ɑn preɪz / (AmE), / hiːp ɒn preɪz / (BrE)
to give someone a large amount of praise, often more than is necessary or appropriate

Examples

To heap on praise generously
She's not one to heap on praise unless it's truly deserved.
Sometimes parents heap on praise to boost their children's confidence.
The coach didn't hesitate to heap on praise after the team's stunning victory.

Related Terms

  • antonym: criticize - express disapproval or find fault
  • synonym: praise - express approval or admiration
