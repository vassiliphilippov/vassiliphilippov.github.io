denseness

noun
the degree of compactness in space
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛns.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdɛns.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of having a high amount of matter or units packed within a given space; density

Examples

Denseness of the metal
Scientists measured the denseness of the metal to determine its composition.
The denseness of the crowd caused delays at the event entrance.
The denseness of the forest made it hard to see very far.
