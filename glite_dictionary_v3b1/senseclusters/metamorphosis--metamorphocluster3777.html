Synonyms

  • transformation
    3.7 30/m
    transformation is a common, general change in form or nature used in many contexts, while metamorphosis implies a dramatic, often magical, complete change.
  • transmutation
    8.6 0.2/m
    transmutation is a formal or scientific change of substance or nature, while metamorphosis suggests a living or magical change in shape or character.
