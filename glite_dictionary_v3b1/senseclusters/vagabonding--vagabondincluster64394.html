vagabonding

noun
wandering lifestyle
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈvæɡəˌbɑndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvæɡəbɒndɪŋ / (BrE)
a lifestyle or act of traveling freely and living as a wanderer, often for extended periods

Examples

A life of vagabonding
After quitting his job, he embraced vagabonding and explored dozens of countries over several years.
Many people dream of vagabonding but find it difficult to leave their routines behind.
Her book describes the joys and challenges of vagabonding through remote regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nomadism - the practice of moving regularly from place to place
  • antonym: settling - establishing a permanent home in one place
  • synonym: wandering - traveling from place to place without a fixed home
