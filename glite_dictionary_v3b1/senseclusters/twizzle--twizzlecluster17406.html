twizzle

verb
to twist quickly
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈtwɪzəl / (BrE)

Examples

To twizzle a pen
She liked to twizzle her pen while thinking about the answer.
He would twizzle the keychain in his hand when he was bored.
The magician began to twizzle the coin between his fingers before making it disappear.
