Cymbopogon

noun
aromatic plant oil
9.9
Pronunciation: /sɪm.ˈbɑː.pə.gən/ (AmE), /sɪm.ˈbɒp.ə.gən/ (BrE)
an essential oil obtained from plants of the Cymbopogon genus, used in perfumery, aromatherapy, and as an insect repellent
