Cymbopogon

noun
aromatic plant oil
Pronunciation: / sɪmˈbɑpəˌɡɑn / (AmE), / sɪmˈbɒpəˌɡɒn / (BrE)
an essential oil obtained from plants of the Cymbopogon genus, used in perfumery, aromatherapy, and as an insect repellent

Examples

Cymbopogon oil
The laboratory analyzed the Cymbopogon oil to identify its compounds used in natural repellents.
Many natural perfumes use Cymbopogon for its fresh, lemony scent.
Farmers often plant Cymbopogon to produce oils that help keep insects away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: citronella oil - an essential oil from Cymbopogon species used as an insect repellent
  • synonym: lemongrass oil - an essential oil from lemongrass, a Cymbopogon species
