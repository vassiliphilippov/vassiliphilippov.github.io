outreach

noun
the act of reaching out or extending effort
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌriːtʃ /; / ˈaʊtˌritʃ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌriːtʃ / (BrE)
the act of reaching out to help, communicate, or provide services or support to others, especially those who might not otherwise have access

Examples

Community outreach program
His outreach to former colleagues was met with enthusiasm and gratitude.
The organization's outreach to the local community made a significant impact.
The university launched an outreach program to encourage more students to study science.
The hospital's outreach program provides free checkups to people in rural areas.
Volunteers organized an outreach event to distribute food and clothing to the homeless.
The library's outreach efforts include visiting local schools to encourage reading.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aid - help or support, especially in a practical form
  • synonym: assistance - help or support given to people
  • synonym: outstretch - to stretch out or extend, especially the hand or arm
