Surma

proper noun
Ethiopian ethnic group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsʊrmə / (AmE), / ˈsɜːmə / (BrE)
an ethnic group of southwestern Ethiopia and South Sudan, known for pastoralism, body painting, and traditional rituals

Examples

Surma people
The Surma are famous for their elaborate body painting and lip plates.
Anthropologists have studied the cattle-centered culture of the Surma for decades.
Many tourists visit southwestern Ethiopia to learn about the traditions of the Surma people.
