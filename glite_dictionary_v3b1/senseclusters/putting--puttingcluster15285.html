putting

noun
hitting a golf ball on the green with a putter
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌtɪŋ / (BrE)
the action or skill of hitting a golf ball lightly on the green with a putter, aiming to get it into the hole

Examples

Consistent putting stroke
Consistent putting is essential for a low golf score.
His putting has improved greatly since he started practicing every day.
She blamed poor putting for losing the tournament.

Related Terms

  • synonym: putt - a gentle stroke made to roll the ball on the green in golf
