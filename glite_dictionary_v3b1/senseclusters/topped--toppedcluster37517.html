topped

adjective
having a top of a particular kind
3.1 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / tÉ‘pt / (AmE), / tÉ’pt / (BrE)
having a top or upper surface of a specified kind or covered with a particular substance, often as a combining form (e.g., snow-topped, cream-topped).

Examples

Topped with cream
Her signature drink is a latte topped with spiced whipped cream.
The cake was beautifully topped with fresh strawberries and mint leaves.
We hiked up a snow-topped mountain during our vacation.

Synonyms

  • capped
    3.9 0.00/m
    capped means covered on top like a lid or crown, while topped describes an upper surface often covered by something.
  • surmounted
    6.2 0.00/m
    surmounted means having a distinct object placed on top, while topped describes an upper surface or covering of something.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capped - covered as if with a cap or top
