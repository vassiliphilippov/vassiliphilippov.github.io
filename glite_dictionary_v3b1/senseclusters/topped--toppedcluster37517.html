topped

adjective
having a top of a particular kind
1.6 2.6
Pronunciation: /tɑːpt/ (AmE), /tɒpt/ (BrE)
having a top or upper surface of a specified kind or covered with a particular substance, often as a combining form (e.g., snow-topped, cream-topped).

Examples

Topped with cream
Her signature drink is a latte topped with spiced whipped cream.
The cake was beautifully topped with fresh strawberries and mint leaves.
We hiked up a snow-topped mountain during our vacation.
