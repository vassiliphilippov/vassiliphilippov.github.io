youth

noun
period between childhood and adulthood
3.5 1.0
Pronunciation: /juːθ/ (AmE), /juːθ/ (BrE)
the period or state of being young, between childhood and adulthood

Examples

In his youth
During his youth, he traveled extensively and learned several languages.
She spent most of her youth dreaming of becoming a writer.
The memories of my youth are filled with summer adventures and laughter.
He regretted some of the mistakes he made in his youth.
Her youth gave her the confidence to try new things without fear.
The energy and enthusiasm of youth are often admired by older generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adolescence - the transitional stage from childhood to adulthood
  • degree higher: adulthood - the state of being fully grown or mature
  • degree lower: childhood - the period of being a child
  • antonym: maturity - the state of being fully grown or developed
