washbasin

noun
fixed bathroom sink for washing
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃˌbeɪsən / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃˌbeɪs.ən /; / ˈwɒʃˌbeɪsɪn / (BrE)
a fixed bowl in a bathroom or washroom, used for washing your hands and face

Examples

Bathroom washbasin
Please rinse your hands thoroughly in the washbasin before dinner.
She splashed cold water from the washbasin onto her face to wake up.
The hotel room comes with a small washbasin next to the shower.
He rinsed his hands quickly at the washbasin before dinner.
She installed a modern washbasin in her newly renovated bathroom.
The washbasin in the guest bathroom was sparkling clean.

Related Terms
