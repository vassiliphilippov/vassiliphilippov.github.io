tent

verb
to live or shelter in a tent
Pronunciation: / tɛnt / (AmE), / tɛnt / (BrE)
to live or take shelter in a tent or as if in a tent, especially temporarily

Examples

To tent outdoors

They chose to tent in the backyard rather than book a hotel room.
We tented by the lake for a week during our vacation.
