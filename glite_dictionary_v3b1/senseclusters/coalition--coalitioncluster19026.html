Synonyms

  • alliance
    4.0 36/m
    an alliance is a formal agreement between groups or countries to work together, while a coalition is often a temporary group for shared goals.
  • consortium
    6.7 7.15/m
    a consortium is a group of companies or organizations formed for a specific project, while a coalition joins different actors for a common cause.
  • partnership
    2.7 24/m
    a partnership is a cooperative relationship often between two parties sharing responsibility, while a coalition brings many groups together for a purpose.
  • confederation
    6.0 3.09/m
    a confederation is a loose union of states or groups that keep their independence, while a coalition joins for a shared goal.
  • combination
    3.4 4.01/m
    a combination is a simple joining of parts or people for a task, while a coalition is an organized alliance of groups.
  • alignment
    4.6 2.14/m
