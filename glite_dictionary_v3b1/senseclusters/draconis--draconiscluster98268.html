Pronunciation: / drəˈkoʊnɪs / (AmE), / drəˈkəʊnɪs / (BrE)
the Latin genitive form meaning 'of the dragon', used in scientific names and terminology.

Examples

Draconis in species name
The species name 'Draco draconis' means 'dragon of the dragon' in Latin.
In astronomy, the term 'caput draconis' refers to the dragon's head.
Many scientific names use draconis to indicate a connection to dragons.
