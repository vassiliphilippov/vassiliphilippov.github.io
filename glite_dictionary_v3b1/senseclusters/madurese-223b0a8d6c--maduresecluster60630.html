Madurese

noun
person or language of Madura
Pronunciation: / ˌmædʊˈriːz / (AmE), / ˌmadjʊˈriːz / (BrE)
a person from Madura Island in Indonesia or the Austronesian language spoken there

Examples

A Madurese speaker
The Madurese have a rich tradition of music and dance unique to their island.
She learned to speak Madurese while living in eastern Java.
Many Madurese migrate to other parts of Indonesia for work.
