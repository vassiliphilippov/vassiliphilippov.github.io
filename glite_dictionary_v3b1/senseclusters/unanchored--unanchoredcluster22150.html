unanchored

adjective
not fixed in place
6.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈæŋkərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈæŋkəd / (BrE)
not attached, fixed, or held in place; lacking a physical or figurative anchor

Examples

Unanchored boat
The boat drifted away because it was unanchored during the storm.
She felt unanchored after moving to a new city where she knew no one.
Without clear goals, his plans remained unanchored and uncertain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anchored - firmly fixed or held in place
  • synonym: detached - not connected or joined to anything
