churchy

adjective
excessively religious
8.7 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt É…úÀêrt Éi / (AmE), / Ààt É…úÀêt Éi / (BrE)
disapproving: excessively or affectedly religious, especially in a way that is moralizing or narrow-minded

Examples

Churchy attitude
Her churchy attitude made some people feel uncomfortable at the party.
He always gives churchy advice, even when no one asks for it.
The film was criticized for its churchy tone and lack of humor.
