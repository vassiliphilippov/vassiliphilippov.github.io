ticket

noun
document granting access or right
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɪk.ɪt / (BrE)
a printed or electronic document that gives its holder the right to enter an event, travel on transportation, or receive a service

Examples

A concert ticket
He bought two tickets for the concert next Saturday night.
Please show your ticket to the conductor before boarding the train.
Without a valid ticket, you cannot enter the movie theater.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: invitation - a written request to attend an event
  • synonym: pass - a document allowing a person to enter a place or use a service
  • degree higher: wristband - a band worn around the wrist as a form of event entry
