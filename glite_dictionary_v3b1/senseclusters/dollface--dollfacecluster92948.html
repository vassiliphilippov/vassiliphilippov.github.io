dollface

noun
term of endearment
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑlˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈdɒlˌfeɪs / (BrE)
an affectionate term for someone, usually a woman, with a pretty or youthful face

Examples

Hey, dollface
He greeted her with a cheerful 'Good morning, dollface!' as she entered the room.
Whenever she felt sad, her grandfather would call her dollface to make her smile.
The actor winked and said, 'See you later, dollface,' in the old movie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: darling - a term of endearment for someone you love
  • synonym: sweetheart - an affectionate term for a loved one
