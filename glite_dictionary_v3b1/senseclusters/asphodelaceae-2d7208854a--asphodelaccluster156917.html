Asphodelaceae

noun

Pronunciation: / æsfəˌdɛləˈsiː.iː /; / ˌæsfəˌdɛləˈsiː.i / (AmE), / æsfəˌdɛlɪˈsiː.iː /; / ˌæsfədɒˈliːsiː / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants in the order Asparagales, including asphodels, aloes, and related genera, formerly classified as a subfamily within Liliaceae or Xanthorrhoeaceae
