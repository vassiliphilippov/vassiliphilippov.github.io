bald eagle

noun
large North American eagle with white head and tail
Pronunciation: / bɔld ˈiɡəl / (AmE), / bɔːld ˈiːɡl / (BrE)
a large eagle of North America with a white head and tail and dark brown body and wings, the national bird of the United States

Examples

The bald eagle
The bald eagle is a symbol of strength and freedom in the United States.
The conservation efforts helped restore the bald eagle population across North America.
We spotted a majestic bald eagle soaring above the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eagle - a large bird of prey with a massive hooked bill and long broad wings
