source of life

noun
origin or basis of existence
Pronunciation: / sɔrs əv laɪf / (AmE), / sɔːs əv laɪf / (BrE)
the place, thing, or person from which life originates or is sustained

Examples

The source of life water
Many ancient myths describe water as the source of life and fertility.
Scientists study the ocean as a potential source of life on other planets.
The sun is often considered the source of life for all living things on Earth.
