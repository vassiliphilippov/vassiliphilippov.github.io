antialiasing

noun
computer graphics edge-smoothing technique
9.2 6.6
Pronunciation: /æn.tɪ.ə.ˈleɪ.sɪŋ/ /; / /ˈæn.tɪ.ə.leɪ.sɪŋ/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.ə.ˈleɪ.sɪŋ/ /; / /ˈæn.tɪ.ə.leɪ.sɪŋ/ (BrE)
A technique in computer graphics used to smooth jagged edges in curves and diagonal lines, making them appear smoother.

Examples

Antialiasing technique

The antialiasing filter improved the quality of the rendered images.
