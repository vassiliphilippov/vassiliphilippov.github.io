Achaemenid

adjective
ancient Persian dynasty
Pronunciation: / əˈkiːmənɪd / (AmE), / əˈkiːmənɪd / (BrE)
relating to the ancient Persian dynasty that ruled from about 550 to 330 BC

Examples

Achaemenid Empire
The Achaemenid Empire was one of the largest empires in ancient history.
Many Achaemenid artifacts have been discovered in modern-day Iran.
The Achaemenid kings were known for their administrative skills and tolerance of different cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Persian - relating to ancient Persia or its people
