zombie spirit

noun
voodoo supernatural force reanimating bodies
Pronunciation: / ˈzɑːmbi ˌspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈzɒmbi ˌspɪrɪt / (BrE)
(in voodoo) a supernatural force or spirit believed to reanimate a dead body.

Examples

A zombie spirit in voodoo
In the story, a zombie spirit possesses the body and forces it to roam the night.
Some believers attribute unexplained deaths to the work of a zombie spirit.
The priest claimed he could call forth a zombie spirit during the ritual.
