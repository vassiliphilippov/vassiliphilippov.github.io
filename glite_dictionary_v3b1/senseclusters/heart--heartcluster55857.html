heart

noun
central part; core
Pronunciation: / hɑrt / (AmE), / hɑːt / (BrE)
the central or most important part of something (such as a place, idea, or activity)

Examples

The heart of the city
At the heart of the debate was the question of fairness for all parties involved.
Rescue teams made their way into the heart of the disaster zone to provide aid.
They spent the afternoon in the heart of the city, exploring its historic sites.
At the heart of the debate is the issue of fairness and equality.
She reached the heart of the problem after hours of discussion.
The heart of his message was about hope and perseverance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: center - the middle point or part of something
  • synonym: core - the central, most important part of something
  • synonym: essence - the intrinsic nature or indispensable quality
  • antonym: periphery - the outer limits or edge of an area
