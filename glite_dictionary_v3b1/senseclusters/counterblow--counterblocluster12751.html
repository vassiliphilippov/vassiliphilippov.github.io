counterblow

noun
a retaliatory strike or blow
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌbloʊ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌbləʊ / (BrE)
A blow given in return to an attack; a retaliatory strike.

Examples

A counterblow in boxing
Every time he was struck, he seemed to deliver a precise counterblow in return.
The boxer's counterblow caught his opponent off guard, turning the tide of the match.
The general responded to the enemy's attack with a swift counterblow.

  • synonym: retaliation - the act of returning an attack
