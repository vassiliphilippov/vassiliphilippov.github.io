agba

noun
West African tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːɡbɑː / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡbɑː / (BrE)
a large tree native to West Africa, valued for its durable wood used in carving and construction

Examples

Agba wood carving
The craftsman carved a statue from agba wood.
Furniture made from agba is known for its strength and beauty.
In some West African villages, houses are built using timber from the agba tree.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mahogany - a tropical hardwood tree valued for its strong, reddish-brown wood
