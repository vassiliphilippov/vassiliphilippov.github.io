beauty bush

noun
ornamental flowering shrub
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːti bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈbjuːti bʊʃ / (BrE)
A Chinese deciduous shrub (Kolkwitzia amabilis) with yellow-throated pinkish flowers and bristly fruit, often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Examples

The beauty bush flowers
After blooming, the beauty bush produces bristly fruit that adds interest to the landscape.
Many landscapers recommend planting a beauty bush for its showy blossoms and easy care.
The beauty bush in our garden is covered with delicate pink flowers each spring.
