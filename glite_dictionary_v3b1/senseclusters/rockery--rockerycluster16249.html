rockery

noun
garden area with rocks and small plants
5.1 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈrɑːk.ə.riː/ (AmE), /ˈrɒk.ə.riː/ (BrE)
A part of a garden arranged with rocks and typically planted with alpine or other small plants.

Examples

A rockery garden
The rockery requires little maintenance compared to the rest of the flower beds.
The children enjoyed exploring the rockery for interesting insects and tiny alpine flowers.
We decided to create a rockery in the corner of our yard to add visual interest.
