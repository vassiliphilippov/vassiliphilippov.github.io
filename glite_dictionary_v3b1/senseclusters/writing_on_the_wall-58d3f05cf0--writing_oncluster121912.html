writing on the wall

noun
sign of impending trouble or failure
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtɪŋ ɑn ðə wɔl / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ ɒn ðə wɔːl / (BrE)
a clear sign or warning that something bad or unsuccessful will happen

Examples

The writing on the wall
Despite the improvements, the writing on the wall was clear: the company was heading for bankruptcy.
Many investors saw the writing on the wall and pulled out before the market crashed.
She ignored the writing on the wall and continued to invest in the failing business.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harbinger - a person or thing that announces or signals the approach of another
  • synonym: omen - an event regarded as a sign of good or evil
  • synonym: warning - a statement or event indicating possible danger or problem
