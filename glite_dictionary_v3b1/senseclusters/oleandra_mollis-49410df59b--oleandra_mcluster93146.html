Oleandra mollis

noun
tropical fern species from Asia to Polynesia
Pronunciation: / oʊˈliːɑːndrə ˈmɑlɪs / (AmE), / əʊˈliːændrə ˈmɒlɪs / (BrE)
A tropical fern with leathery, oleander-like fronds, native to regions from Asia to Polynesia.

Examples

Oleandra mollis fern
Botanists discovered a rare specimen of Oleandra mollis in the rainforests of Borneo.
The leathery leaves of Oleandra mollis make it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
Unlike many ferns, Oleandra mollis thrives in both humid and slightly drier environments.
