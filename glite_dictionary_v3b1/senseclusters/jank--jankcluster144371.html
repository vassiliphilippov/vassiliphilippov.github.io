jank

noun
sudden jerking movement
9.9
Pronunciation: /dʒæŋk/ (AmE), /dʒæŋk/ (BrE)
a sudden, sharp, or awkward jerking movement or twitch, especially of a machine or vehicle

Examples

After hitting the brakes suddenly, the car gave a noticeable jank forward.
He felt a strange jank in the machinery as the engine started up.
