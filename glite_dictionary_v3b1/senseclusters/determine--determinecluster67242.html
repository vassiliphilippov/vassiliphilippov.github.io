determine

verb
shape or influence outcome or direction
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɜrmɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪn / (BrE)
to shape or influence something; to decide or establish the way in which something will happen or develop

Examples

Factors determine success
Cultural factors can determine how a community responds to change.
Environmental conditions often determine which species will thrive in an area.
His upbringing largely determined his future ambitions and values.

Related Terms

  • synonym: control - to exercise power over; to direct
  • antonym: follow - to come as a result or consequence
  • synonym: influence - to affect or change how someone or something develops or behaves
  • synonym: mold - to shape or influence development
