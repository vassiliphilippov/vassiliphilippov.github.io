graffitied

adjective
marked with graffiti
4.7
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈfiːtid / (AmE), / ɡrəˈfiːtid / (BrE)
covered with graffiti, such as writing or drawings applied to a surface without permission

Examples

Graffitied walls
The abandoned building was completely graffitied with colorful tags and murals.
City workers spent hours cleaning the graffitied subway station walls.
Tourists took photos of the famous graffitied alley in the city center.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - free from marks, dirt, or graffiti
  • synonym: defaced - damaged or spoiled in appearance, often by writing or drawing
