depressed

adjective
flattened downward; dorsoventrally compressed
Pronunciation: / dɪˈprɛst / (AmE), / dɪˈprɛst / (BrE)
(of a surface or structure) flattened downward or pressed in from above; having a dorsoventrally flattened shape.

Examples

Depressed surface
In some beetles, the body is noticeably depressed to aid in burrowing.
The depressed discs can be seen clearly under the microscope.
The shell of this species is depressed rather than domed.
