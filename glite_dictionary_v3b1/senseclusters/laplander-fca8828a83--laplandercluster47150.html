Laplander

proper noun
outdated term for Sami
Pronunciation: / ˈlæpˌlændər / (AmE), / ˈlæpˌlændə / (BrE)
an old term for a native or inhabitant of Lapland, a region in northern Europe, now considered outdated

Examples

A Laplander in Lapland
In the 19th century, travelers described the traditions of the Laplanders in their journals.
The term Laplander is rarely used today, as 'Sami' is preferred.
Old maps often labeled the northern regions as the home of the Laplanders.
