gourde

noun
Haitian currency unit
Pronunciation: / ɡʊrd / (AmE), / ɡɔːd / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Haiti, equal to 100 centimes.

Examples

The gourde currency
She converted her dollars into gourdes before traveling to Haiti.
The price of rice has increased by fifty gourdes per sack this year.
The vendor asked for 100 gourdes for the handmade bracelet.
