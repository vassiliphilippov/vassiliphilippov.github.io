upcanyon

noun
upper canyon area
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌkænjən / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌkænjən / (BrE)
the upper part or higher region within a canyon, often farther from the mouth or entrance

Examples

The upcanyon camp
The hikers set up camp in the upcanyon to avoid the crowds near the entrance.
Rare plants were discovered growing only in the cooler, shaded upcanyon regions.
As the sun rose, light slowly reached the upcanyon walls above the river.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downcanyon - the lower part or region closer to the mouth of a canyon
