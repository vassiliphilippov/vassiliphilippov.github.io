starfire

noun
burst of inspiration
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a sudden, intense burst of creative inspiration, excitement, or energy

Examples

Starfire of ideas
A sudden starfire of ideas helped the team solve the problem in record time.
He felt a starfire of excitement as he stepped onto the stage for the first time.
Whenever she paints, a starfire of creativity seems to light up her mind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apathy - lack of interest, enthusiasm, or energy
  • synonym: flash - a sudden burst of light or inspiration
  • synonym: spark - a small burst of energy or creativity
