cablecast

verb
to broadcast by cable
6.3
Pronunciation: /keɪ.bəl.ˈkæst/ (AmE), /keɪ.bəl.ˈkɑːst/ (BrE)
to transmit a television or radio program by means of cable television systems

Examples

To cablecast a show
The city council meeting will be cablecast live to all local subscribers.
They plan to cablecast the championship game for viewers who cannot attend in person.
Last night, the documentary was cablecast on the community access channel.
