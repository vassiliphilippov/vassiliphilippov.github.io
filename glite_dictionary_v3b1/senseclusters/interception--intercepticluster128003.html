interception

noun
capture/inspection of data or requests
Pronunciation: / cc6an.t5ac8s5bp.8359n / (AmE), / cc6an.t59c8s5bp.8359n / (BrE)
(computing) the capture, inspection, and sometimes modification of data, messages, or requests as they pass through a software layer, network, or system.

Examples

Network interception
The security team detected an interception of encrypted packets by a malicious proxy on the network.
Developers logged every interception of API requests to diagnose why the service returned errors.
Browser extensions can perform interception of web requests to modify headers or block trackers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intercept - to stop or seize something on its way
  • synonym: packet capture - the recording of network packets for analysis (used to inspect network traffic)
