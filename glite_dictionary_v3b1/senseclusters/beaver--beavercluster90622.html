beaver

noun
piece of armor protecting lower face
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːvər / (AmE), / ˈbiːvə / (BrE)
a movable piece of armor attached to a medieval helmet, designed to protect the lower part of the face

Examples

Helmet beaver

Medieval helmets often featured a beaver for added protection during combat.
The knight lowered his beaver before entering battle.
