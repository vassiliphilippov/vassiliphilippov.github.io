cartoonishly

adverb
exaggeratedly, unrealistically
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈtuːnɪʃli / (AmE), / kɑːˈtuːnɪʃli / (BrE)
in an exaggerated or unrealistic way, similar to how things appear in cartoons

Examples

Move cartoonishly on stage
The actor moved cartoonishly across the stage, making the audience laugh at his silly gestures.
Her eyes widened cartoonishly when she saw the enormous cake on the table.
The villain's mustache curled cartoonishly as he plotted his next move.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exaggeratedly - in a way that makes something seem larger or more extreme than it really is
  • antonym: realistically - in a way that is true to life or reality
