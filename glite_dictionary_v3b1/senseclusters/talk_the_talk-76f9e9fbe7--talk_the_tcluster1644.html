talk the talk

verb
speak convincingly or confidently
7.8 5.9
Pronunciation: /tɔːk ðə tɔːk/ /; / /tɔːk ðiː tɔːk/ /; / /tɑːk ðə tɑːk/ (AmE), /tɔːk ðə tɔːk/ /; / /tɔːk ðiː tɔːk/ (BrE)
to speak convincingly or credibly, especially when suggesting expertise or confidence, often without necessarily performing the actions to match one's words
