eddy

verb
move in a circular current
7.0 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈe.diː/ /; / /ˈe.dɪ/ (AmE), /ˈe.dɪ/ /; / /ˈe.diː/ (BrE)

Mist began to eddy around the trees in the early morning light.
Tiny fish eddy in small pools formed by the stream's uneven flow.
