eddy

verb
move in a circular current
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdi / (AmE), / ˈɛdi / (BrE)
(of a liquid or gas) to move or flow in a circular motion or current.

Examples

Leaves eddy around
Leaves eddied in the wake of the passing car.
Mist began to eddy around the trees in the early morning light.
Tiny fish eddy in small pools formed by the stream's uneven flow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swirl - to move in a twisting or spiraling pattern
