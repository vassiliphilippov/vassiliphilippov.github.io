abrasion

noun
erosion or scraping by friction
9.4
Pronunciation: / əˈbreɪʒən / (AmE), / əˈbreɪʒən / (BrE)
the process of wearing away or scraping by friction, especially on a surface

Examples

Surface abrasion
Frequent cleaning can lead to the abrasion of delicate surfaces.
The constant movement of sand caused significant abrasion to the rocks over time.
The geologist explained how wind contributes to the abrasion of exposed cliffs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: erosion - the gradual destruction of something by natural forces such as water, wind, or friction
