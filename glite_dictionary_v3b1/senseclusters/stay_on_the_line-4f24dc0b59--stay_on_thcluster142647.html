stay on the line

verb
remain connected on the telephone
Pronunciation: / steɪ ɑn ðə laɪn / (AmE), / steɪ ɒn ðə laɪn / (BrE)
to remain connected on the telephone while waiting to continue or be transferred

Examples

To stay on the line waiting
If you need further assistance, stay on the line and someone will help you.
Please stay on the line while I check your details.
The operator asked me to stay on the line during the transfer.

Related Terms
