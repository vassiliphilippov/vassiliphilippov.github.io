classwide

adjective
affecting entire class
Pronunciation: / ˈklæsˌwaɪd / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsˌwaɪd / (BrE)
involving or affecting all members of a class as a group

Examples

A classwide reward system
The teacher implemented a classwide reward system to encourage good behavior.
A classwide discussion was held to address the students' concerns about the project.
They introduced a classwide reading challenge to improve literacy skills among all students.

Related Terms

  • antonym: individual - relating to a single person or thing, not a group
  • synonym: schoolwide - involving or affecting all members of a school
