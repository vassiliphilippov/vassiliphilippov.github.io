birchwood

noun
wood from birch tree
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrtʃˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈbɜːtʃˌwʊd / (BrE)
the wood obtained from a birch tree, commonly used in making furniture or for construction

Examples

Smooth birchwood furniture
The craftsman selected birchwood for its smooth texture and light color.
Many Scandinavian chairs are made from strong birchwood.
They built a small cabin using mostly birchwood from the nearby forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oak - wood from an oak tree, used in furniture and construction
  • synonym: pinewood - wood from a pine tree, often used in building and furniture
