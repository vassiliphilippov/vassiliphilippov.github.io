come up roses

verb
turn out well; end successfully
Pronunciation: / kʌm ʌp ˈroʊzɪz / (AmE), / kʌm ʌp ˈrəʊzɪz / (BrE)
to turn out very well or result in a positive or successful outcome

Examples

To come up roses in life
After months of planning, the wedding came up roses and was a huge success.
Despite a difficult year, everything came up roses for the company in the end.
If you work hard, things are much more likely to come up roses for you.
