inker

noun
printing press worker
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkə / (BrE)
a worker in a printing press who applies ink to type, plates, or rollers before printing

Examples

Skilled inker at work
The experienced inker made sure the plates were evenly coated before each print run.
As an inker, she took pride in preparing the press for high-quality prints every morning.
The inker worked closely with the pressman to ensure the colors were consistent throughout the job.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pressman - a worker who operates a printing press
  • synonym: printer - a person who operates a printing press
