operationally

adverb
in relation to operation or functioning
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənəli / (BrE)
In terms of operation or relating to the way something works or is used.

Examples

To be operationally ready
She ensured that the equipment was operationally efficient before the launch.
The building was operationally ready ahead of schedule.
The two systems are operationally identical, though they differ in design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: functionally - in a way that relates to function or operation
