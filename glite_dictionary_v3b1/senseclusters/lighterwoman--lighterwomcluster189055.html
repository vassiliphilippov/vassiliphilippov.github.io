lighterwoman

noun
female barge operator
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtərˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman whose occupation is operating or working on a lighter (a type of barge used to transport goods).

Examples

A lighterwoman on a barge
As a lighterwoman, she was responsible for transporting goods between ships and the shore.
She trained as a lighterwoman on the busy river docks in London.
The lighterwoman skillfully maneuvered the barge through the narrow canal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lighterman - a person (traditionally male) who operates a lighter (barge)
