red-eye

noun
overnight flight or resulting fatigue
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌaɪ / (BrE)
an overnight flight, typically one that departs late at night and arrives early the next morning; also, the fatigue or weary state resulting from such a flight

Examples

A red-eye flight
I booked a red-eye to New York to make my early morning meeting.
She tried to catch up on sleep after her exhausting red-eye.
Taking a red-eye can save time but often leaves you feeling tired the next day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overnight flight - a flight that travels during the nighttime hours, usually arriving in the morning
