Sturnus vulgaris

noun
the common starling (bird, scientific name)
8.2
Pronunciation: / stɝː.nəs vʌlˈɡɛər.ɪs / (AmE), / ˈstɜː.nəs vʌlˈɡɑː.rɪs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the common starling, a widely distributed, gregarious bird with glossy dark plumage, known for nesting near human dwellings.
