genus Alnus

noun
genus of trees/shrubs called alders
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈælnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈal.nəs / (BrE)
A genus of deciduous trees and shrubs known as alders, commonly found in temperate climates and characterized by toothed leaves and catkins.
