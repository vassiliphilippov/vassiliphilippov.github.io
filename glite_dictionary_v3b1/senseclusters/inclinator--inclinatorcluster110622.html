inclinator

noun
tilting machine
9.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkleɪnətər / (AmE), / ɪnˈkleɪnətə / (BrE)
a machine or device that tilts or changes the angle of an object or surface, especially in industrial settings

Examples

Industrial inclinator device
The workers used an inclinator to safely tilt the large metal sheets during assembly.
Installing an inclinator improved efficiency in the warehouse by making it easier to pour materials.
The engineer checked the inclinator to ensure it could handle the weight of the new molds.
