hireable

adjective
suitable for employment
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪərəbəl / (AmE), / ˈhaɪərəbl / (BrE)
suitable or qualified to be hired for a job or position

Examples

Hireable candidate
After completing the training program, she became much more hireable in the competitive job market.
Companies look for candidates who are immediately hireable and ready to start work.
Improving your interview skills can make you more hireable to potential employers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: employable - having the skills or qualities needed to get a job
  • antonym: unemployable - not able to be employed
