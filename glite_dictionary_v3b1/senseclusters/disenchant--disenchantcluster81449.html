(in fantasy and gaming) to remove magical properties or enchantments from an object, often to make it non-magical or to recover magical components.

Examples

To disenchant a sword
The mage decided to disenchant the cursed sword to reclaim its spellbound gems for crafting.
They disenchanted several artifacts at the workshop and sold the recovered runes to collectors.
After hours of disenchanting, she finally removed the enchantment that prevented the door from opening.
