antalgic

adjective
pain-relieving
Pronunciation: / ænˈtældʒɪk / (AmE), / ænˈtældʒɪk / (BrE)
relieving or reducing pain; having an effect that lessens discomfort

Examples

Antalgic medicine
The doctor recommended an antalgic medication to help manage her chronic back pain.
After the injury, he adopted an antalgic posture to avoid discomfort while walking.
Physical therapists often teach patients antalgic movements to reduce pain during recovery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analgesic - relieving pain without causing loss of consciousness
  • antonym: painful - causing physical pain or discomfort
