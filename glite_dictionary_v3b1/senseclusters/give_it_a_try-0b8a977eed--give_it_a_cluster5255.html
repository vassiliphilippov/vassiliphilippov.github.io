give it a try

verb
to attempt something, usually once
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv ɪt ə traɪ / (AmE), / ɡɪv ɪt ə traɪ / (BrE)
to try to do something, especially for the first time, to see if you can do it or if you like it

Examples

To give it a try now
Even though she was nervous about public speaking, she decided to give it a try.
I've never eaten sushi before, but I'm willing to give it a try.
You should give it a try before deciding it's not for you.
I've never played tennis before, but I'll give it a try.
If you're not sure you can finish the puzzle, just give it a try anyway.
She was nervous about public speaking, but decided to give it a try at the event.
