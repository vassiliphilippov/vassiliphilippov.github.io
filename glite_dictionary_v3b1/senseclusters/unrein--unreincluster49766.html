unrein

verb
to remove restraints
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈreɪn / (AmE), / ʌnˈreɪn / (BrE)
to remove or loosen restraints or reins, especially from a horse, to allow more freedom

Examples

To unrein a horse
The handler carefully unreined the horse before letting it run in the field.
After the storm, workers unreined the tarps covering the supplies.
She unreined the boat from its moorings and set off down the river.
After a long ride, she decided to unrein the horse so it could rest and graze.
The stable hand carefully unreined each horse before leading them to the pasture.
He paused by the river to unrein his mare and let her drink freely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rein - to control or guide a horse using reins
  • antonym: restrain - to hold back or keep under control
  • synonym: unharness - to remove a harness from a horse or other animal
  • synonym: unleash - to release from restraint or control
