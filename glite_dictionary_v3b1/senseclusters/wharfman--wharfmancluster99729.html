wharfman

noun
dock worker
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrfmən / (AmE), / ˈwɔːfmən / (BrE)
a person who works at a wharf, loading and unloading ships or handling cargo on the dock

Examples

Wharfman loads cargo
The experienced wharfman quickly secured the ropes as the ship docked.
Several wharfmen worked together to unload the heavy crates from the vessel.
After years as a wharfman, he knew every part of the busy harbor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: longshoreman - a worker who loads and unloads ships at a dock
  • synonym: stevedore - a person employed to load and unload ships
