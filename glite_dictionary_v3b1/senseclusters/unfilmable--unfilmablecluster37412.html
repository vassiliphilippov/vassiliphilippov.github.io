unfilmable

adjective
impossible to film
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪlməbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪlməbəl / (BrE)
not able to be filmed, usually because of restrictions, safety concerns, or lack of access

Examples

Unfilmable location
The director called the dangerous mountain scene unfilmable due to the extreme weather conditions.
Some private events are strictly unfilmable because of confidentiality agreements.
The author believed his complex novel was unfilmable and could never be adapted for the screen.

Related Terms

  • antonym: filmable - able to be filmed or recorded
  • synonym: unrecordable - not able to be recorded in any form
