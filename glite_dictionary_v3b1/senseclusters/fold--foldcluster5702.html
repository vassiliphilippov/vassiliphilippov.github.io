fold

noun
group of sheep or goats
Pronunciation: / foʊld / (AmE), / fəʊld / (BrE)
an enclosure or group for sheep or goats

Examples

A sheep fold
A stray lamb was found outside the main fold and gently guided back.

The shepherd led the entire fold to a new pasture for grazing.
During the storm, the sheep huddled together in the fold for shelter.
The shepherd led the flock back to the fold before nightfall.
The stone walls of the old fold have stood for centuries.
