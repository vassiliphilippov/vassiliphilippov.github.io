asexual

adjective
not involving sexual activity
Pronunciation: / eɪˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / eɪˈsɛkʃuːəl / (BrE)
not involving sexual activity; occurring or reproducing without sex.

Examples

Asexual reproduction in plants
Many plants reproduce through asexual means such as budding or fragmentation.
Some species are capable of both asexual and sexual reproduction, depending on environmental conditions.
The asexual production of spores allows some fungi to spread rapidly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sexual - involving sex or sexual reproduction
