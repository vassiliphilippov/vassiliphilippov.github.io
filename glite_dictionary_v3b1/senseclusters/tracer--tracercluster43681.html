tracer

noun
bullet or shell that leaves a visible trail
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪsər / (AmE), / ˈtreɪsə / (BrE)
A type of bullet or shell that leaves a visible trail of smoke or light so its trajectory can be observed.

Examples

Tracer bullet
A bright tracer streaked across the sky during the battle.
The soldiers loaded their rifles with tracer rounds for the night exercise.
Watching the path of the tracer helped the shooter adjust their aim.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tracer bullet - bullet that leaves a luminous or smokey trail
