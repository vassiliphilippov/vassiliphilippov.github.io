indissolubly

adverb
in a non-soluble way
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈsɑːljəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈsɒljʊbli /; / ˌɪndɪˈsɒljəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be separated, broken, or undone

Examples

Indissolubly joined
The two chemicals were indissolubly bonded, making separation impossible.
The fabric was indissolubly stained after the experiment in the lab.
Some compounds are indissolubly linked and cannot be broken down by water.
The two companies are indissolubly connected through their long-standing partnership.
Her memories of childhood are indissolubly linked to the old family home.
Tradition and culture are often indissolubly woven into a community's identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inseparably - in a way that cannot be separated
  • synonym: insolubly - in a way that cannot be dissolved
  • synonym: permanently - in a way that lasts forever
  • antonym: temporarily - for a limited time only
