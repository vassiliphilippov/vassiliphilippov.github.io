signified

noun
meaning expressed by a sign or word
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪ.nəˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nɪˌfaɪd / (BrE)
the concept or meaning expressed by a word, sign, or symbol, especially in linguistics and semiotics

Examples

The signified meaning
Each symbol in the system has both a physical form and a signified meaning.
In linguistics, the signified is the concept or idea that a word represents.
The relationship between the signifier and the signified is fundamental to the study of semiotics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: signifier - the form which a sign takes in linguistics; the physical form of a word or symbol
