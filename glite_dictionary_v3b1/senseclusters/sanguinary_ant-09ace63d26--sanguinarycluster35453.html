sanguinary ant

noun
a slave-making ant species
Pronunciation: / ˈsæŋɡwəˌnɛri ænt / (AmE), / ˈsaŋɡwɪnəri ænt / (BrE)
a species of slave-making ant widely distributed over the Northern Hemisphere, known for raiding other ant colonies to capture their brood
