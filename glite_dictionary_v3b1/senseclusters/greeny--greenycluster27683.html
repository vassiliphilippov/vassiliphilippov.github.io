greeny

noun
environmentalist; greenish thing
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːni / (AmE), / ˈɡriːni / (BrE)
a person who supports environmental causes or something that is greenish in color

Examples

A proud greeny
He is a proud greeny who volunteers for local clean-up projects.
The walls were painted a soft greeny shade that made the room feel fresh.
Some people call her a greeny because she always talks about recycling and climate change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: environmentalist - a person who is concerned with protecting the environment
  • degree lower: green - having the color of growing grass or leaves
