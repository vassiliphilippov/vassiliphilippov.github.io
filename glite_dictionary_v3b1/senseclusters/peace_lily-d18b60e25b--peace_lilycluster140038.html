peace lily

noun
tropical houseplant with white spathe flowers
4.8 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / pis Ààl…™li / (AmE), / piÀês Ààl…™li / (BrE)
An evergreen plant of the genus Spathiphyllum, commonly grown indoors for its glossy green leaves and characteristic white spathe surrounding a spike of small flowers.

Examples

A peace lily plant
I keep a peace lily on my desk because it helps purify the air.
She gave her friend a peace lily as a housewarming gift.
The flowers of the peace lily are elegant and last for weeks.

Synonyms
