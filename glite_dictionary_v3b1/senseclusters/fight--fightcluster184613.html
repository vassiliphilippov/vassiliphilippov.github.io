fight

noun
intense argument or dispute
1.0
Pronunciation: / faɪt / (AmE), / faɪt / (BrE)
a heated argument or serious disagreement between people, groups, or organizations, especially one that is public or prolonged

Examples

A fight over money
A fight over funding erupted at the board meeting last night.
Their friendship ended after a bitter fight about politics.
There was a fight between the two senators during the heated debate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agreement - a situation in which people share the same opinion
  • synonym: argument - an exchange of diverging or opposite views, typically a heated one
  • synonym: debate - a formal or informal discussion on a particular topic
