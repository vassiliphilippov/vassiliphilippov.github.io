Bluetooth

proper noun
wireless data technology
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːtuːθ / (AmE), / ˈbluːtuːθ / (BrE)
a wireless technology standard for exchanging data over short distances using radio waves

Examples

Bluetooth connection
You need to turn on Bluetooth to connect your wireless speaker to your phone.
Many modern cars allow you to make hands-free calls using Bluetooth technology.
She transferred the photos from her laptop to her tablet via Bluetooth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Wi-Fi - a wireless networking technology for connecting devices to the internet
  • synonym: infrared - a wireless technology using infrared light for short-range communication
