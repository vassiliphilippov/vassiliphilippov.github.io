demonetization

noun
removal of online monetization
7.5
Pronunciation: /diː.mə.nə.taɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /dɪ.mə.nə.taɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /diː.mə.nə.taɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /diː.mʊ.nə.taɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /diː.mʌ.nə.taɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
The act of removing a channel's, account's, or piece of online content's ability to earn money on a digital platform.

Examples

YouTube demonetization notice
After the policy change, several small creators reported that demonetization had destroyed their main income.
The company warned that videos violating rules could face immediate demonetization without prior notice to the uploader.
Debate about algorithm transparency grew after unexplained demonetization affected dozens of educational channels last month.

Related Terms

  • antonym: remonetization - Restoring the ability to earn money from content on a platform; opposite of demonetization.
