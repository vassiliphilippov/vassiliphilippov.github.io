sewage farm

noun
farm using sewage for irrigation and fertilization
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːɪdʒ fɑrm / (AmE), / ˈsjuːɪdʒ fɑːm / (BrE)
A farm where land is irrigated and fertilized with untreated or partially treated sewage for the disposal of waste and to promote plant growth.

Examples

A sewage farm
Environmental regulations now limit the use of raw sewage on a sewage farm.
Farmers on the sewage farm monitor soil and water quality regularly.
The city built a new sewage farm on the outskirts to manage waste sustainably.

Related Terms

  • synonym: treatment plant - a facility for treating sewage or wastewater
