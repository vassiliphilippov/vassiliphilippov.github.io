foppish

adjective
excessively concerned with appearance (for men)
9.9 8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfɑːp.ɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈfɒp.ɪʃ/ (BrE)
(of a man) excessively concerned with his clothes and appearance; behaving or dressing in an affectedly elegant or refined way.

Examples

Foppish young man
Actors in period dramas often adopt a foppish manner to portray aristocratic characters.
Despite his foppish appearance, he was respected for his sharp intellect.
He wore a foppish suit with bright colors and a silk cravat to the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dandyish - displaying the characteristics of a dandy; affectedly fashionable or elegant
  • antonym: masculine - having qualities traditionally associated with men, especially strength and aggressiveness
