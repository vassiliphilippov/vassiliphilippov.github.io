card-house

noun
fragile structure made from playing cards
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrd haʊs / (AmE), / ˈkɑːd haʊs / (BrE)
A structure built by balancing playing cards together, typically fragile and easily collapsed.

Examples

A card-house collapses
After an hour of careful construction, the card-house collapsed with the slightest breeze.
Building a card-house requires patience and a steady hand.
The tower he made turned out to be as fragile as a card-house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: house of cards - a structure made from playing cards balanced together; also used metaphorically for something unstable
