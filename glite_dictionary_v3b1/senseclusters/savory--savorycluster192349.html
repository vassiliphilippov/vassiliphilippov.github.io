savory

adjective
pleasant-tasting; appetizing
7.8 4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvəri / (AmE), / ˈseɪvəri / (BrE)
having a pleasant, appetizing, spicy, or salty taste (not sweet)

Examples

Savory snacks
The chef is famous for her savory pies and quiches.
The soup had a savory flavor that everyone enjoyed.

She prefers savoury snacks like crackers and cheese over sweet treats.

The chef prepared a savoury sauce that perfectly complemented the grilled chicken.
