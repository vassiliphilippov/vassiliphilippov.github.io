cityless

adjective
without cities
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪtiləs / (AmE), / ˈsɪtiləs / (BrE)
having no cities or lacking any urban centers

Examples

A cityless area
The cityless landscape stretched for miles, dotted only with farms and forests.
Many people enjoy the peace and quiet of a cityless environment.
The country remained largely cityless until the late nineteenth century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rural - relating to the countryside rather than towns or cities
  • antonym: urban - relating to a city or town
