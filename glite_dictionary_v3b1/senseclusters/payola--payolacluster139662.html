payola

noun
illegal payment to promote records
9.2 8.5
Pronunciation: /peɪ.ˈəʊ.lə/ /; / /peɪ.ˈoʊ.lə/ (AmE), /peɪ.ˈəʊ.lə/ (BrE)
the illegal practice of paying a disc jockey or broadcaster to promote certain recordings in order to influence their popularity
