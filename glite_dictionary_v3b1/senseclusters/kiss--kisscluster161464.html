kiss

noun
an act of touching with the lips
1.0
Pronunciation: / kɪs / (AmE), / kɪs / (BrE)
an act of touching someone or something with one's lips as a sign of affection, greeting, or respect

Examples

A goodnight kiss
His first kiss was awkward but unforgettable.
She gave her mother a gentle kiss on the cheek before leaving.
The couple shared a kiss under the mistletoe at the holiday party.
