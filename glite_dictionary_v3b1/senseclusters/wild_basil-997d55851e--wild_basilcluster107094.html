wild basil

noun
aromatic herb with small pink or whitish flowers
4.2
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈbæzəl / (AmE), / waɪld ˈbæzl / (BrE)
an aromatic herb with clusters of small pink or whitish flowers, widely found in the United States, Europe, and Asia

Examples

Wild basil plant
Wild basil grows naturally along forest edges and meadows across Europe and North America.
She used dried wild basil leaves to add flavor to the stew.
The garden was filled with the scent of wild basil in late summer.
