unmaintainable

adjective
cannot be kept up
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnmeɪnˈteɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnmeɪnˈteɪnəbl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to keep in good condition or continue supporting

Examples

Unmaintainable building
After years of neglect, the building became unmaintainable and had to be demolished.
The software's outdated code made it unmaintainable for the current team.
If a system is too complex, it can quickly become unmaintainable and costly to fix.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maintainable - able to be kept in good condition or supported
  • synonym: unsustainable - unable to be continued or maintained over time
