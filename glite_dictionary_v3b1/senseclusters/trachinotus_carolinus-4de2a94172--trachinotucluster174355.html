Trachinotus carolinus

noun
Florida pompano, a marine fish species
9.9
Pronunciation: / trəˌkɪnoʊtəs ˌkæroʊˈlaɪnəs / (AmE), / trəˌkɪnətəs ˌkarəʊˈlaɪnəs / (BrE)
A species of edible marine fish, commonly known as the Florida pompano, found in coastal waters from New England to Brazil, except in the clear waters of the West Indies.
