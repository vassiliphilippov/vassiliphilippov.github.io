orthopedic insole

noun
shoe insert for foot support
3.2 3.4
Pronunciation: /ɔːr.θə.ˈpiː.dɪk ˈɪn.soʊl/ (AmE), /ɔː.θə.ˈpiː.dɪk ˈɪn.səʊl/ (BrE)
A specially designed shoe insert that provides support and corrects foot alignment to alleviate pain or discomfort.

Examples

Orthopedic insoles for support
If you suffer from flat feet, orthopedic insoles can provide essential support.
My doctor recommended wearing orthopedic insoles to relieve my foot pain.
She bought new orthopedic insoles for her running shoes after her injury.
