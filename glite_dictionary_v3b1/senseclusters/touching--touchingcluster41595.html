touching

adjective
emotionally moving or affecting
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
causing strong feelings of sympathy, tenderness, or sadness; emotionally moving.

Examples

A touching story
Her speech was so touching that many people in the audience were moved to tears.
I found the reunion scene between the family members especially touching.
It was a touching tribute to those who had made sacrifices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moving - causing strong feelings of emotion, especially sadness or sympathy
  • synonym: poignant - evoking a keen sense of sadness or regret
  • antonym: unfeeling - not showing emotion or sympathy
