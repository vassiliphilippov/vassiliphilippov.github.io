common purse

noun
shared funds among a group
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən pɝs / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən pɜːs / (BrE)
money or funds that are shared by members of a group for mutual expenses or projects

Examples

The common purse
Each roommate adds money to the common purse to pay for shared groceries and bills.
The committee agreed to use the common purse for travel expenses to the conference.
They established a common purse so that no one would have to pay for activities alone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kitty - a sum of money gathered from a group to be used collectively
  • synonym: pool - a common fund of money for a shared purpose
