star begonia

noun
a begonia with star-shaped leaves and pink flowers
6.3
Pronunciation: / stɑr bɪˈɡoʊniə / (AmE), / stɑː bɪˈɡəʊniə / (BrE)
A rhizomatous begonia with leaves shaped like stars and pink flowers, often grown as an ornamental plant.

Examples

A star begonia plant
His grandmother grew a beautiful star begonia that bloomed with delicate pink flowers every spring.
She placed the star begonia on the windowsill so its unique leaves could catch the sunlight.
The garden center offers several varieties, but the star begonia is always popular for its bold foliage.
