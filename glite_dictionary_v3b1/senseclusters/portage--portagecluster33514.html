portage

verb
to carry boat overland
8.6 0.19/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…îrt…™d í / (AmE), / Ààp…îÀêt…™d í / (BrE)
to carry a boat or its cargo over land between bodies of water

Examples

To portage a canoe
We portaged the canoe across the narrow strip of land to reach the next lake.
The group had to portage their kayaks for half a mile around the waterfall.
After a long day of paddling, they portaged their gear to the campsite.

Synonyms

  • port
    7.4 0.02/m
