pearly everlasting

noun
a North American plant with white pearly flower heads
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpɝːli ˌɛvɚˈlæs.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpɜːli ˌɛvəˈlɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
a North American perennial plant (Anaphalis margaritacea) with soft, woolly leaves and clusters of small white flower heads surrounded by pearly bracts

Examples

Dried pearly everlasting
Dried pearly everlasting is popular in floral arrangements because it retains its color and shape.
Gardeners often plant pearly everlasting for its attractive, long-lasting white flowers.
The pearly everlasting bloomed along the roadside, its silvery leaves shimmering in the sunlight.
