retromandibular vein

noun
vein behind the mandible in the face
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛtroʊˌmænˈdɪbjələr veɪn /; / ˌriːtroʊˌmændɪˈbjulər veɪn / (AmE), / ˌrɛtrəʊˌmænˈdɪbjʊlə veɪn /; / ˌriːtrəʊˌmændɪˈbjuːlə veɪn / (BrE)
a vein behind the lower jaw (mandible), formed by the joining of the superficial temporal and maxillary veins, and serving as a branch of the facial vein
