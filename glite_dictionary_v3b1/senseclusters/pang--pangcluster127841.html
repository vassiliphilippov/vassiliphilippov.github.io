pang

verb
to feel sudden pain
6.5
Pronunciation: / pæŋ / (AmE), / pæŋ / (BrE)
to cause or experience a sudden sharp feeling of pain or distress

Examples

To pang with guilt
She was panged by guilt after forgetting his birthday.
A sudden memory panged him as he walked past the old house.
He panged with regret when he realized his mistake.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: ache - to feel a continuous or dull pain
  • synonym: sting - to cause a sharp pain or discomfort
