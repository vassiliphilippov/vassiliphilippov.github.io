deciliter

noun
metric unit, one tenth of a liter
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛsɪˌlitər /; / ˈdɛsɪˌliːtər / (AmE), / ˈdɛsɪˌliːtə / (BrE)
A metric unit of volume equal to one tenth of a liter (0.1 liter).

Examples

He drank two decilitres of water before the race.
The recipe calls for half a decilitre of olive oil.
