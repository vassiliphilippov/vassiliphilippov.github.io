sender

noun
person or entity sending a message or item
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛndər / (AmE), / ˈsɛndə / (BrE)
a person or organization that sends a message, letter, or parcel to someone

Examples

Letter sender
In any communication, both the sender and the recipient play important roles.
Please write the sender's address clearly on the envelope.
The email bounced back because the sender's address was invalid.

Related Terms

  • antonym: recipient - person or entity that receives something
  • synonym: transmitter - a person or device that transmits something
