beachcomb

noun
person searching beaches
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtʃˌkoʊm / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃˌkəʊm / (BrE)
a person who searches along a beach for items of value or interest, such as shells or lost objects

Examples

A beachcomb finds shells
Every morning, the dedicated beachcomb walked the shore looking for interesting shells and driftwood.
As a beachcomb, he often found coins and small treasures after storms.
The local beachcomb shared stories about the unusual objects she had discovered over the years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collector - a person who gathers or accumulates items of a particular kind
  • synonym: scavenger - a person who searches for and collects discarded items
