jawbone

noun
bone forming the jaw
2.5 0.69/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd í…ëÀåbo än / (AmE), / Ààd í…îÀêb…ô än / (BrE)
The bone that forms the structure of the jaw and is hinged to open and close the mouth in vertebrates.

Examples

A broken jawbone
He fractured his jawbone in a biking accident and needed surgery.
The dentist examined my lower jawbone before deciding on the treatment.
The fossilized jawbone of a dinosaur was discovered by paleontologists in the desert.

Synonyms

  • mandible
    6.5 0.26/m
    mandible is the formal anatomical name for the lower jaw, while jawbone is the common everyday word for that bone.
  • lower jaw
    2.2 0.51/m
    lower jaw is the common phrase for the bottom hinged part of the mouth, while jawbone is a general name for that bone.
  • submaxilla
    9.9 0.00/m
    submaxilla is a technical name used in anatomy for the lower jaw bone, while jawbone is the general, everyday term.
