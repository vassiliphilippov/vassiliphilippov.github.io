whited sepulcher

noun
hypocrite; outwardly good, inwardly corrupt person
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtɪd ˈsɛpəlkɚ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtɪd ˈsɛp(ə)lkə /; / ˈwaɪtɪd ˈsɛpəlkə / (BrE)
A person who pretends to be virtuous or morally upright but is actually corrupt or wicked inside; a hypocrite.
