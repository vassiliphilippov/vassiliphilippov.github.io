nontreatable

adjective
cannot be treated
8.2
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈtriːtəbəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈtriːtəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be treated or cured by medical or other means

Examples

Nontreatable condition
Unfortunately, some conditions remain nontreatable despite advances in modern medicine.
Doctors explained that the disease was nontreatable and recommended supportive care.
Researchers are searching for therapies for currently nontreatable illnesses.
