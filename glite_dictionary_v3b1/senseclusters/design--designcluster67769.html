design

verb
intend or have as a purpose
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
to intend or have as a purpose; to plan with a particular aim in mind

Examples

To design a result
He designed that his company would one day lead the market.
She designed to leave her mark on history through her charitable works.
They designed to finish the project before the end of the year.

Related Terms
