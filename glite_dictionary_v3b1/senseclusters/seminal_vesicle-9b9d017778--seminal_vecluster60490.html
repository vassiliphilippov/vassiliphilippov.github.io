seminal vesicle

noun
male gland near bladder that secretes seminal fluid
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmɪnəl ˈvɛsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛmɪnəl ˈvɛsɪkəl / (BrE)
either of a pair of glands in males, located near the bladder, that secrete fluid contributing to semen during ejaculation

Examples

The seminal vesicle gland
During surgery, care must be taken not to damage the seminal vesicle.
Inflammation of a seminal vesicle can cause pain or discomfort in the pelvic region.
The seminal vesicle produces a significant portion of the fluid found in semen.
