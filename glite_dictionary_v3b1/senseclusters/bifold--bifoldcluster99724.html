bifold

adjective
folding in two
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈbaɪˌfəʊld / (BrE)
folding into two parts, especially used to describe doors or wallets that have two panels or sections

Examples

Bifold door design
We installed a bifold door to save space in the laundry room.
He prefers a bifold wallet because it fits easily in his pocket.
The closet has bifold panels that open smoothly and quietly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: folding - able to be bent or folded
  • antonym: single - consisting of one part or section
