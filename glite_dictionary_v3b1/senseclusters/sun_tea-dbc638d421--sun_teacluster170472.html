sun tea

noun
tea brewed in the sun
4.8 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / s ån ti / (AmE), / s ån tiÀê / (BrE)
Tea made by steeping tea leaves in water exposed to sunlight, usually served cold with ice.

Examples

Sun tea on porch
On hot summer days, my grandmother always makes sun tea on the porch.
Some people prefer sun tea for its mellow flavor compared to traditionally brewed tea.
We enjoyed a refreshing glass of sun tea after gardening all afternoon.
