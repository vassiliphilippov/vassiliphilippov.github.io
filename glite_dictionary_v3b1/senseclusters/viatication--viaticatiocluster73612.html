viatication

noun
buying insurance from terminally ill policyholders
8.8 9.9
Pronunciation: /vaɪ.ə.tɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /viː.ə.tɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /viː.ə.tɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /vaɪ.ə.tɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
