hazan

noun
synagogue official who chants prayers
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɑːˈzɑːn, ˈhɑːzən / (AmE), / hɑːˈzɑːn, ˈhɑːzən / (BrE)
an official of a synagogue who leads the liturgical part of the service and chants prayers, often as a soloist

Examples

The hazan leads prayers
As a child, she admired the voice of the hazan in her local synagogue.
Every Friday evening, the hazan prepares to chant the ancient melodies for the Sabbath service.
The hazan led the congregation in a moving rendition of the prayer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cantor - an official who leads the singing in a synagogue or church
