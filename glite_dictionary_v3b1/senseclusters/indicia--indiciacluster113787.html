indicia

noun
postage markings
9.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈdɪ.ʃə/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈdɪ.ʃə/ (BrE)
printed markings on mail that show postage has been paid, used instead of stamps

Examples

Mail indicia
The envelope had a printed indicia instead of a traditional postage stamp.
Many businesses use indicia to save time when sending large volumes of mail.
The postal worker checked the indicia to confirm that the postage was paid.

Related Terms

  • synonym: postmark - an official mark stamped on mail to show the date and place of posting
  • antonym: stamp - a small piece of paper showing postage has been paid
