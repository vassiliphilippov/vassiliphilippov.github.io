wave front

noun
surface just reached by a wave
Pronunciation: / weɪv frʌnt /; / ˈweɪv frʌnt / (AmE), / weɪv frʌnt /; / ˈweɪv frʌnt / (BrE)
a surface connecting all points reached at the same time by a moving wave, such as light or sound
