talonid

noun
part of lower molar
Pronunciation: / ˈtælənɪd / (AmE), / ˈtælənɪd / (BrE)
the posterior part of a lower molar tooth in some mammals, often involved in grinding food

Examples

The talonid part
The paleontologist examined the talonid to determine the animal's diet.
Damage to the talonid can affect how a mammal grinds its food.
Researchers compared the size of the talonid across different fossil species.
