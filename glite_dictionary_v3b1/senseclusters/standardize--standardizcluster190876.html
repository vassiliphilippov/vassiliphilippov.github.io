standardize

verb
make conform to a standard
Pronunciation: / ˈstændərˌdaɪz /; / ˈstændərdaɪz /; / ˈstændɚˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈstændədaɪz / (BrE)
to make something conform to a set standard or norm, often for consistency or uniformity

Related Terms

  • synonym: standardize - to cause to conform to standard or norm; chiefly American spelling
