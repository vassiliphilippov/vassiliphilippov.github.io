arteria brachialis

noun
main artery of the upper arm
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈtɪəriə brəˈkaɪəlɪs / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə brəˈkaɪəlɪs / (BrE)
the main artery of the upper arm, continuing from the axillary artery and dividing into the radial and ulnar arteries at the elbow

Examples

The arteria brachialis artery
An injury to the arteria brachialis can result in severe blood loss.
During surgery, care must be taken not to damage the arteria brachialis.
The arteria brachialis supplies blood to the muscles of the upper arm.
