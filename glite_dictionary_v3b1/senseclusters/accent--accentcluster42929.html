accent

verb
to stress or pronounce with an accent
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈækˌsɛnt / (AmE), / ˈæk.sənt / (BrE)
to put stress on a particular syllable or word when speaking; to pronounce with a particular accent

Examples

To accent a word

In French, you accent the final syllable in some words.
You should accent the main points in your speech for clarity.
