Privine

noun
trademarked medication for nasal congestion/eye irritation
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪviːn / (AmE), / ˈprɪviːn / (BrE)
A trademarked vasoconstrictor medication used as a nasal spray to relieve nasal congestion and as eyedrops for eye irritation.

Examples

Privine nasal spray
After catching a cold, she used Privine to relieve her nasal congestion.
It is important not to overuse Privine, as it can cause rebound congestion.
The doctor recommended Privine eyedrops for the patient's irritated eyes.
