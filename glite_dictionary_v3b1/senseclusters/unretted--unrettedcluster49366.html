unretted

adjective
not processed by retting
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɛtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛtɪd / (BrE)
not having undergone retting, the process of separating fibers from plant stems by soaking or rotting

Examples

The unretted hemp
The factory could not use the unretted hemp because the fibers were still too tough.
Processing unretted flax requires more effort than using retted material.
Farmers stored the unretted stalks until they were ready for the retting process.

Related Terms

  • antonym: retted - having undergone retting to separate fibers from plant stems
