flop

noun
complete failure
1.2
Pronunciation: / flɑp / (AmE), / flɒp / (BrE)
a complete failure, especially of a project, performance, or person

Examples

Despite his ambition, everyone saw him as a flop in the business world.
She feared becoming a flop after her project failed to impress anyone.
