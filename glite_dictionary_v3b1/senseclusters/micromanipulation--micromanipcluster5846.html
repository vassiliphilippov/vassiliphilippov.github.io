micromanipulation

noun
precise handling of tiny objects
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˌmænɪpjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˌmænɪpjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the use of specialized instruments to handle or alter extremely small objects, such as cells or microscopic structures

Examples

Cell micromanipulation
The scientist used micromanipulation to transfer a single chromosome into the egg cell.
Advances in micromanipulation have made it possible to study the behavior of individual molecules.
Training in micromanipulation is essential for researchers working with delicate biological samples.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manipulation - the act of handling or controlling something
  • synonym: microsurgery - surgery performed on very small structures using a microscope
