seek

verb
inquire; ask for (information/advice)
1.0 90/m
Pronunciation: / siÀêk / (AmE), / siÀêk / (BrE)
to ask for or try to obtain information, advice, or help from someone

Examples

To seek advice
If you get lost, you should seek directions from someone nearby.
The organization encourages everyone to seek help if they are struggling.
Tourists often seek advice from hotel staff when exploring a new city.
