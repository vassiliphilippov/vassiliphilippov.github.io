Zantedeschia aethiopica

noun
South African plant with white spathe; arum lily
9.9
Pronunciation: / zæn.tɪˈdɛski.ə ˌiːθiˈɑpɪkə / (AmE), / zan.tɪˌdɛski.ə ˌiːθɪˈɒpɪkə / (BrE)
a South African plant with large arrow-shaped leaves and showy funnel-shaped white flowers, widely grown as an ornamental; also called arum lily or calla lily.
