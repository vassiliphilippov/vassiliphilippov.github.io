perspectively

adverb
involving perspective
7.4
Pronunciation: / pərˈspɛktɪvli / (AmE), / pəˈspɛktɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to or involves perspective, especially in art or visual representation

Examples

Perspectively arranged objects
The artist arranged the figures perspectively to give the painting a sense of depth.
In the diagram, the buildings are drawn perspectively to show their relative positions.
The objects appear perspectively smaller as they recede into the background.

Related Terms

  • synonym: visually - in a way that relates to seeing or sight
