atelectasis

noun
lung collapse or incomplete expansion
Pronunciation: / ˌætəˈlɛktəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌætɛlˈɛktəsɪs / (BrE)
collapse or closure of a lung resulting in reduced or absent gas exchange, or the failure of the lung to expand at birth.

Examples

Atelectasis of lung
A chest X-ray revealed atelectasis in the patient's right lung.
Prompt intervention is required to prevent complications from atelectasis.
The newborn was diagnosed with atelectasis after difficulty breathing was observed.
