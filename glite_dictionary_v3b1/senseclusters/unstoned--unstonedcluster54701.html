unstoned

adjective
not stoned (by rocks)
9.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstoʊnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈstəʊnd / (BrE)
not having been pelted with stones or subjected to stoning as a form of punishment or attack

Examples

An unstoned prisoner
The accused man was found innocent and left the court unstoned.
In some ancient societies, few people accused of such crimes remained unstoned.
Despite the angry crowd, the protestors walked away unstoned and unharmed.
