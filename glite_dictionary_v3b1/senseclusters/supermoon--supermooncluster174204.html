supermoon

noun
large, bright full moon
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsuː.pər.muːn/ (AmE), /ˈsuː.pə.muːn/ /; / /ˈsjuː.pə.muːn/ (BrE)
a full moon that appears larger and brighter than usual because it is closer to Earth in its orbit

Examples

A supermoon night
Many people gathered at the beach to watch the spectacular supermoon rise over the ocean.
Photographers prepared their cameras to capture the rare supermoon lighting up the night sky.
The news reported that tonight's supermoon would be the brightest of the year.
Crowds gathered in the park to watch the rare supermoon rise above the city skyline.
Photographers stayed up late to capture the stunning brightness of the supermoon last night.
The supermoon made the night seem almost as bright as day.

Related Terms
