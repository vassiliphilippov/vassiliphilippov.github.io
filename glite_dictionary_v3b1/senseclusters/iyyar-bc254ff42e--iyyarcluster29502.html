Iyyar

noun
Jewish calendar month, April–May
9.9
Pronunciation: / iˈjɑr / (AmE), / iˈjɑː / (BrE)
The eighth month of the civil year and the second month of the ecclesiastical year in the Jewish calendar, usually falling in April and May.

Examples

The month Iyyar
According to the Jewish calendar, the new moon marks the beginning of Iyyar.
Many families commemorate anniversaries that fall in the month of Iyyar.
The festival of Lag BaOmer takes place in Iyyar each year.
