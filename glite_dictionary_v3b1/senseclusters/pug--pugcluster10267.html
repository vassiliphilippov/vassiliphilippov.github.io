pug

verb
to knead clay
Pronunciation: / pʌɡ / (AmE), / pʌɡ / (BrE)
to mix or knead clay or a similar material into a smooth, workable mass, usually with a machine.

Examples

To pug clay
The workers spent hours pugging the clay to prepare it for the pottery class.
Before shaping the bricks, the factory uses a machine for pugging the raw material.
She learned the importance of pugging clay to achieve a smooth texture for her sculptures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knead - to work a substance, such as dough or clay, into a uniform mixture by pressing and folding
  • synonym: mix - to combine two or more substances so that they become a single substance
