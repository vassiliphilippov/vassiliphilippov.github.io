statics

noun
branch of mechanics: forces in equilibrium
Pronunciation: / ˈstætɪks / (AmE), / ˈstætɪks / (BrE)
the branch of mechanics that deals with forces and their effects on bodies at rest or in equilibrium

Examples

Statics in engineering
Engineers must understand statics when designing stable structures.
I found the statics portion of the physics course particularly challenging.
In statics, we analyze how forces balance to keep an object at rest.
