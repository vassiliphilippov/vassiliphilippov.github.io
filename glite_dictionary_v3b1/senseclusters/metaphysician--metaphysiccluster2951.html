metaphysician

noun
expert in metaphysics
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəfəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəfəˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who studies or is an expert in metaphysics, the branch of philosophy concerned with the nature of reality

Examples

A metaphysician debates reality
The metaphysician spent years pondering the fundamental nature of existence.
As a metaphysician, he often discussed abstract concepts like time and space.
Many students found the lectures by the metaphysician both challenging and inspiring.
The metaphysician challenged the audience to question the nature of existence.
As a respected metaphysician, she published several books on the concept of being.
Many students attended the seminar led by a renowned metaphysician from the university.

Related Terms

  • synonym: philosopher - a person engaged in the study of philosophy
  • antonym: scientist - a person who studies or practices science
