trendy

adjective
fashionable; up to date
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛndi / (AmE), / ˈtrɛndi / (BrE)
very fashionable or up to date with the latest trends, styles, or ideas

Examples

A trendy outfit
She likes to wear trendy outfits that follow the latest styles.
They discussed several trendy ideas for the new advertising campaign.
This restaurant became popular for its trendy atmosphere and modern decor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fashionable - popular or in style at a particular time
  • antonym: outdated - no longer in fashion or use
