gobo

noun
light-shaping device
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊboʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊbəʊ / (BrE)
a device or plate placed in front of a light source to shape, block, or project patterns of light, especially in stage lighting or photography
