corticotropin

noun
pituitary hormone that stimulates adrenal cortex
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔrtɪkoʊˈtroʊpɪn /; / ˌkɔːrtɪkəˈtroʊfɪn / (AmE), / ˌkɔːtɪkəʊˈtrəʊpɪn /; / ˌkɔːtɪkəˈtrəʊfɪn / (BrE)
a hormone produced by the anterior pituitary gland that stimulates the adrenal cortex to secrete corticosteroids
