cantina

noun
small casual bar
3.9
Pronunciation: / kænˈtinə / (AmE), / kænˈtiːnə / (BrE)
a small bar or restaurant, especially one serving casual food and drinks, often with a relaxed atmosphere

Examples

Lively cantina
We stopped at a lively cantina for some snacks and cold drinks after our hike.
The local cantina is famous for its live music and friendly atmosphere.
She met her friends every Friday night at the neighborhood cantina.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar - a place where alcoholic drinks are served
  • synonym: pub - a public house serving drinks and sometimes food
  • synonym: restaurant - a place where meals are served to customers
