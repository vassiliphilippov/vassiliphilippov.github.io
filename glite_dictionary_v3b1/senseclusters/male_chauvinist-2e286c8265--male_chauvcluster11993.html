male chauvinist

noun
man with sexist beliefs
Pronunciation: / meɪl ʃoʊˈvɪnɪst / (AmE), / meɪl ˈʃəʊvɪnɪst / (BrE)
a man who believes that men are superior to women and who acts in a way that shows this belief

Examples

A male chauvinist attitude
She refused to work for a male chauvinist who didn't respect female colleagues.
Some people still label traditional attitudes as male chauvinist even today.
The meeting was disrupted by a male chauvinist making inappropriate remarks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sexist - a person who discriminates based on gender, especially against women
