Battle of Karbala

proper noun
historic 680 CE battle near Karbala, Iraq
9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈbæt.əl əv kɑːr.ˈbɑː.lə/ (AmE), /ˈbæt.əl əv kɑː.ˈbɑː.lə/ (BrE)
a battle fought in 680 CE near Karbala (in present-day Iraq), in which Husayn ibn Ali, the grandson of Muhammad, and his followers were killed by the forces of the Umayyad caliphate

Scholars often discuss the aftermath of the Battle of Karbala in the context of Islamic history.
