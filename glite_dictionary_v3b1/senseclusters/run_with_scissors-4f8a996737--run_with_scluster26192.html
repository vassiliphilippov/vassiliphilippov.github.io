run with scissors

verb
act recklessly or dangerously
Pronunciation: / rʌn wɪð ˈsɪsərz / (AmE), / rʌn wɪð ˈsɪzəz / (BrE)
to engage in reckless or dangerous behavior, especially without regard for the possible consequences
