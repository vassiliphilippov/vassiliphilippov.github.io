umbilical cord

noun
cord connecting fetus and placenta
6.0 2.6
Pronunciation: / ʌmˈbɪlɪkəl kɔrd / (AmE), / ʌmˈbɪlɪkəl kɔːd /; / ʌmˌbɪlɪkəl ˈkɔːd / (BrE)
the long, flexible cord or tube that connects a fetus to the placenta, carrying nutrients and oxygen from the mother and removing waste during pregnancy
