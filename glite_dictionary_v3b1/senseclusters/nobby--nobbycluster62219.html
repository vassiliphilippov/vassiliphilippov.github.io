nobby

noun
traditional fishing boat
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɒbi / (AmE), / ˈnɒbi / (BrE)
a small traditional sailing fishing boat used off the northeast coast of England

Examples

Fishing nobby boat
The fishermen set out in a small nobby to check the lobster pots before dawn.
A restored nobby is now displayed at the local maritime museum.
Many coastal villages once relied on the nobby for their daily catch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trawler - a fishing boat that uses a trawl net
