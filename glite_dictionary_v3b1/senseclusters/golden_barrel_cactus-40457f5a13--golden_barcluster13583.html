golden barrel cactus

noun
a round cactus with golden spines
8.1 6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən ˈbærəl ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈbærəl ˈkaktəs / (BrE)
a large, round cactus native to central Mexico, with golden-yellow spines and pale yellow flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental plant
