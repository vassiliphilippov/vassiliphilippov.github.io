sheikha

noun
wife or female counterpart of a sheik
4.4
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪˈkɑ / (AmE), / ʃeɪˈkɑː / (BrE)
the wife or female counterpart of a sheik in Arab society, often holding a position of respect

Examples

The sheikha of Dubai

The sheikha attended the cultural festival alongside her husband, the sheik.
