round-nose

adjective
with a rounded tip
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊnd ˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈraʊnd ˌnəʊz / (BrE)
having a rounded end or tip, especially used to describe tools or objects

Examples

Round-nose tool
The jeweler selected a round-nose plier to shape the delicate wire.
A round-nose shovel is ideal for digging in soft soil.
He bought a set of round-nose scissors for his young daughter to use safely.
