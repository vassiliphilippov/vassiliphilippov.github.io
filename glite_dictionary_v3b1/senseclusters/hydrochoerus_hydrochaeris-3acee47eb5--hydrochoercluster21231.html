Hydrochoerus hydrochaeris

noun
largest living rodent; capybara
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdrəˈkɪərəs ˌhaɪdrəˈkɪərɪs / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪdrəˈkɪərəs ˌhʌɪdrəˈkɪərɪs / (BrE)
a large, tailless South American rodent, also known as the capybara, which lives near water and has partly webbed feet; the largest living rodent.
