astrologer

noun
one who predicts by stars and planets
1.0 6.3
Pronunciation: / əˈstrɑː.lə.dʒɚ / (AmE), / əˈstrɒl.ə.dʒə / (BrE)
a person who claims to foretell the future by interpreting the positions and movements of planets, the Sun, and the Moon

Examples

An astrologer reads stars
Many people in ancient cultures relied on an astrologer for advice and guidance.
She consulted an astrologer to learn what her future might hold.
The astrologer explained that Mercury in retrograde could influence communication.

Related Terms

  • antonym: astronomer - a scientist who studies the universe beyond Earth's atmosphere
  • synonym: fortune-teller - a person who claims to predict other people's futures
