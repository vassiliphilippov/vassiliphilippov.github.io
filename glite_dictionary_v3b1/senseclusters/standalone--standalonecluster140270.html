standalone

noun
independent device
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈstænd.ə.loʊ/ (AmE), /ˈstænd.ə.ləʊ/ (BrE)
a self-contained device, system, or product that can operate independently without needing to be connected to others

Examples

A standalone printer
The new printer is a standalone that does not require a computer to function.
Many people prefer a standalone air purifier for each room in their house.
This software can be used as a standalone or as part of a larger system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: independent - not connected to or relying on others
  • antonym: integrated - combined with or dependent on other systems
