superstore

noun
large retail store
2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsuː.pər.stɔːr/ (AmE), /ˈsuː.pə.stɔː/ (BrE)
a very large retail store that sells a wide variety of goods, often including groceries, clothing, and household items

Examples

A superstore shopping
We bought all our groceries at the superstore.
The new superstore attracts hundreds of shoppers every day with its wide selection.
You can find electronics, clothes, and food all under one roof at the superstore.
The new superstore opened last week and attracted hundreds of shoppers on the first day.
You can find everything from electronics to fresh produce at the local superstore.
Many families do their weekly shopping at a superstore because of the wide selection and low prices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypermarket - a very large store combining a supermarket and a department store
