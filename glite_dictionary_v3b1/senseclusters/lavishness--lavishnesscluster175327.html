lavishness

noun
quality of being excessively luxurious or expensive
Pronunciation: / ˈlæv.ɪʃ.nəs /; / ˈlævɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈlæv.ɪʃ.nəs /; / ˈlævɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
