kinglike

adjective
having qualities or rank of a king
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋ.laɪk / (BrE)
Having the qualities, rank, or manner of a king; regal or majestic.

Examples

Kinglike manner
His kinglike demeanor impressed all the guests at the ceremony.
She displayed a kinglike sense of responsibility in her leadership role.
The artist gave the statue a kinglike expression to convey authority.
