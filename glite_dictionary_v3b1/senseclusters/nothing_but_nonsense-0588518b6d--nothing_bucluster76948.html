nothing but nonsense

x
utter rubbish or insincerity
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌθɪŋ bət ˈnɑnsɛns / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ bə ˈnɒnsəns / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is completely untrue, worthless, or insincere; merely rubbish or pointless talk or claims.

Examples

Nothing but nonsense talk
His excuses sounded like nothing but nonsense to me.
The advertisement promised quick results, but it turned out to be nothing but nonsense.
The rumors about the company's bankruptcy were nothing but nonsense.
