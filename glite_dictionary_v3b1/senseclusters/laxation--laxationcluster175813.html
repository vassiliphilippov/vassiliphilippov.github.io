laxation

noun
the act of loosening
Pronunciation: / lækˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / lækˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of making something less tight or loosened

Examples

Laxation of cables
Engineers allowed slight laxation in the cables to reduce tension.
Manual laxation of the band was necessary during the procedure.
The laxation of the corset provided immediate relief.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tightening - the act of making something tight or tighter
