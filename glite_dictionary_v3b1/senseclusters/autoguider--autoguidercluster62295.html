autoguider

noun
automatic telescope tracker
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːtoʊˌɡaɪdər / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊˌɡaɪdə / (BrE)
a device that automatically keeps a telescope aimed at a target by making small adjustments

Examples

The autoguider device
The autoguider made it possible to photograph faint galaxies with long exposures.
Without an autoguider, the stars in the image would appear as streaks instead of points.
She set up the autoguider to keep the telescope perfectly aligned with the moving comet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guidescope - a small telescope used to help aim a larger telescope
