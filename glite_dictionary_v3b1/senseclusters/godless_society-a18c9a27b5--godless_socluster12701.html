godless society

noun
society lacking religious belief
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑd.ləs səˈsaɪ.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈɡɒd.ləs səˈsaɪ.ə.ti / (BrE)
a society in which religious belief or belief in God is absent or rejected

Examples

A godless society
Debates about secularism often focus on the consequences of living in a godless society.
Some argue that a godless society lacks a sense of shared purpose and meaning.
The author describes a godless society where moral values are constantly questioned.
