admirably

adverb
in an admirable manner
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.mɚ.əb.li / (AmE), / ˈæd.mɪr.əb.li / (BrE)
In a way that deserves respect or approval; in an admirable manner.

Examples

Handled admirably
Despite the difficulties, she handled the situation admirably.
He adapted to the new environment admirably, making friends quickly.
The team performed admirably under pressure and won the match.
