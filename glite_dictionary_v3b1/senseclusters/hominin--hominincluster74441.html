hominin

noun
human lineage member
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑː.mɪ.nɪn/ /; / /ˈhɑː.mə.nɪn/ (AmE), /ˈhɒ.mɪ.nɪn/ /; / /ˈhɒ.mə.nɪn/ (BrE)
