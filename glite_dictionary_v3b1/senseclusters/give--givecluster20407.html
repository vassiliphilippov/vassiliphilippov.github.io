give

noun
elasticity; ability to stretch and recover
6.5 5.9
Pronunciation: /gɪv/ (AmE), /gɪv/ (BrE)
the ability of a material or object to stretch or bend slightly and return to its original shape; elasticity or flexibility

Examples

A shoe with give
Leather shoes often have more give than synthetic ones, making them easier to break in.
The rope showed just enough give to absorb the shock of the fall.
This cushion has a lot of give, making it very comfortable to sit on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexibility - the quality of bending easily without breaking
  • antonym: rigidity - the quality of being stiff and unable to bend or be forced out of shape
