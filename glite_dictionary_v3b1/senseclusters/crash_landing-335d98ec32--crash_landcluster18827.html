crash landing

noun
emergency, often damaging landing of an aircraft
5.9
Pronunciation: / kræʃ ˈlændɪŋ / (AmE), / kræʃ ˈlændɪŋ / (BrE)
an emergency landing made under circumstances where a normal landing is impossible, often resulting in damage to the aircraft

Examples

A crash landing emergency
Investigators are examining the wreckage following the crash landing at the small airfield.
Thanks to the skill of the pilot, all passengers survived the crash landing with only minor injuries.
The airplane made a crash landing after its engines failed over the mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emergency landing - a landing made in response to an emergency situation
