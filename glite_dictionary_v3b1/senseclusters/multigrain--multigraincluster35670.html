multigrain

noun
food with mixed grains
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌɡreɪn / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌɡreɪn / (BrE)
a food product, such as bread or cereal, made from more than one type of grain

Examples

A multigrain cereal
She prefers multigrain bread because it is healthier than white bread.
The bakery offers a delicious multigrain loaf packed with seeds and oats.
For breakfast, he usually eats a bowl of multigrain cereal with fresh fruit.

Related Terms

  • antonym: white bread - bread made from refined wheat flour
  • synonym: whole grain - food made from the entire grain kernel
