sage green

noun
a soft greyish-green color
5.5
Pronunciation: / seɪdʒ ɡriːn / (AmE), / seɪdʒ ɡriːn / (BrE)
A soft, muted greyish-green color resembling the leaves of the sage plant.

Examples
