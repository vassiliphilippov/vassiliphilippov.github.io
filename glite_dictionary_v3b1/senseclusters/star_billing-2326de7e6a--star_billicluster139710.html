star billing

noun
prominent credit for a leading performer
5.9
Pronunciation: / stɑr ˈbɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / stɑː ˈbɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
prominent credit or position given to a leading actor or performer in a production

Examples

Star billing in a show
Despite being a newcomer, he received star billing alongside the veterans.
Directors sometimes argue over which performer deserves star billing in ensemble films.
The leading actress was thrilled to have star billing in the new production.
