flower girl

noun
young girl in a wedding procession with flowers
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊər ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈflaʊə ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a young girl who carries flowers and leads the procession at a wedding ceremony

Examples

Wedding flower girl
At her cousin's wedding, Emily was thrilled to be chosen as the flower girl.
The flower girl walked ahead of the bride, scattering rose petals along the aisle.
The flower girl wore a white dress and held a small basket of flowers.
