spackle

verb
to fill surface holes
Pronunciation: / ˈspækəl / (AmE), / ˈspækəl / (BrE)
to fill holes or cracks in a wall or ceiling with a special paste before painting or finishing

Examples

He spackled the holes left by the old picture frames to make the wall smooth again.
