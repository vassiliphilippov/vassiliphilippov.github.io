prelaunch

adjective
before official launch
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːlɔntʃ / (AmE), / ˈpriːlɔːntʃ / (BrE)
happening or done before an official launch, especially of a product, service, or event

Examples

Prelaunch event
The company organized a prelaunch event to build excitement for its new smartphone.
We received valuable feedback during the prelaunch testing phase.
Their prelaunch marketing strategy focused on social media influencers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postlaunch - happening after an official launch
  • synonym: preliminary - happening before something important as a preparation
