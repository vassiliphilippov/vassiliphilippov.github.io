blackthorn

noun
thorny Eurasian shrub with plumlike fruits
9.5 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈblæk.θɑːrn/ /; / /ˈblæk.θɔːrn/ (AmE), /ˈblæk.θɔːn/ (BrE)
