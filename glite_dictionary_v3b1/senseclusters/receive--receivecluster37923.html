Synonyms

  • welcome
    1.0 14/m
    welcome is used to accept someone warmly and with pleasure, while receive is a more general act of taking someone in.
  • greet
    1.0 16/m
    greet means to say hello or welcome someone, while receive focuses on admitting or accepting them into a place or group.
  • meet
    1.0 59/m
    meet means to be present to greet someone when they arrive, while receive means to accept or admit them into a place or group.
