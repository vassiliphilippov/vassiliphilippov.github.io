cartoon

noun
animated film made from drawings
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈtun / (AmE), / kɑːˈtuːn / (BrE)
a film made by photographing a sequence of drawings or images to create the illusion of movement; an animated film

Examples

A cartoon movie
As a child, she loved watching Saturday morning cartoons on television.
Classic Disney cartoons like 'Snow White' are enjoyed by people of all ages.
The studio released a new cartoon featuring talking animals and colorful backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animation - the technique of photographing successive drawings to create motion
