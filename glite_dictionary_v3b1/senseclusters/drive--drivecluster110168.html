drive

noun
device for reading or writing data
Pronunciation: / draɪv / (AmE), / draɪv / (BrE)
(computing) A device that reads from or writes data to a storage medium, such as a hard disk, CD, or USB device.

Examples

USB drive
Please insert the USB stick into the appropriate drive to transfer your files.
She upgraded her laptop with a faster solid-state drive for better performance.
The computer would not boot because the drive was damaged.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disk drive - a drive that uses a disk as a storage medium
  • synonym: hard drive - a drive containing a hard disk for storage
