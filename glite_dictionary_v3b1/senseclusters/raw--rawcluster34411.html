raw

adjective
brutally unfair or harsh
Pronunciation: / rɔ / (AmE), / rɔː / (BrE)
(of a situation, treatment, or experience) brutally unfair or harsh.

Examples

Raw deal
After all his dedication, being dismissed so suddenly seemed especially raw.
She felt it was a raw deal when she was blamed for their mistakes.
The workers complained about the raw treatment they received from management.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harsh - cruel or severe
  • synonym: unfair - not based on or behaving according to the principles of equality and justice
