overvaliant

adjective
excessively brave or bold
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈvæliənt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈvæliənt / (BrE)
having or showing excessive or undue bravery or boldness, often to a foolish or reckless degree

Examples

An overvaliant act
Her overvaliant attempts often led her into unnecessary danger.
Only an overvaliant knight would challenge the dragon alone.
The general's overvaliant strategy worried his cautious advisers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reckless - without regard for danger or consequences; heedless
  • degree lower: valiant - brave, courageous
