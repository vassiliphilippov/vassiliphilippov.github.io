meadow saffron

noun
bulbous autumn-flowering herb, also called autumn crocus
7.4 7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈme.doʊ ˈsæf.rən/ (AmE), /ˈme.dəʊ ˈsæf.rən/ (BrE)
