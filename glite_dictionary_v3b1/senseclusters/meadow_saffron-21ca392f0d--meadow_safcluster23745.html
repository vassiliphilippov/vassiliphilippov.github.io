meadow saffron

noun
bulbous autumn-flowering herb, also called autumn crocus
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdoʊ ˈsæfrən / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊ ˈsæfrən / (BrE)
a bulbous autumn-flowering herb of western and central Europe, with white, purple, or lavender-and-white flowers; also known as autumn crocus

Examples

The meadow saffron flower
Clusters of meadow saffron appeared in the field, signaling the end of summer.
Gardeners should be cautious when planting meadow saffron, as all parts of the plant are toxic if ingested.
In the wild, meadow saffron often carpets damp meadows with its striking purple blooms in autumn.
