book

noun
set of stamps, tickets, etc. bound together
1.6
Pronunciation: / bʊk / (AmE), / bʊk / (BrE)
a collection of items fastened together along one edge, such as tickets, stamps, or playing cards

Examples

A book of stamps
He bought a book of stamps at the post office.
I always keep a book of subway tickets in my bag for convenience.
The cashier handed her a book of coupons to use on future visits.
In canasta, a book consists of at least seven cards of the same rank.
Players compete to create as many books as possible during each round.
She formed a book of sevens to score extra points.
