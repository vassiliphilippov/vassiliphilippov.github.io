oversweet

adjective
excessively sweet or sentimental
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈswiːt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈswiːt / (BrE)
excessively sweet in taste or overly sentimental in tone or style

Examples

An oversweet dessert
The dessert was so oversweet that I could only eat a few bites.
Her oversweet letter made him feel uncomfortable because it seemed insincere.
Some critics found the movie's ending oversweet and lacking in realism.
The lemonade was so oversweet that I had to add more water.
Some people find this cake oversweet, but children usually love it.
He complained that the sauce was oversweet and masked the flavor of the meat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bitter - having a sharp, unpleasant taste; lacking sweetness
  • synonym: cloying - too sweet or sentimental to the point of being unpleasant
  • synonym: saccharine - excessively sweet or sentimental
  • synonym: sickly - unpleasantly sweet in taste or smell
  • degree lower: sweet - having a taste like sugar or honey
