boxing glove

noun
padded mitten worn in boxing
1.0 2.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑːk.sɪŋ glʌv/ (AmE), /ˈbɒk.sɪŋ glʌv/ (BrE)
A thickly padded leather mitten worn on the hand during boxing to protect both the fighter's hand and the opponent from serious injury.

Examples

A pair of boxing gloves
A pair of boxing gloves was hanging on the gym wall.
He laced up his boxing gloves before stepping into the ring.
Modern boxing gloves are designed to minimize injury to both fighters.
