cunningly

adverb
in a clever or crafty manner
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌnɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkʌnɪŋli / (BrE)
In a clever and deceitful or crafty way, often using skill to achieve one's aims or to trick others.

Examples

To cunningly plan
He cunningly devised a plan that no one suspected until it was too late.
She cunningly manipulated the conversation to get what she wanted.
The fox cunningly escaped the hunters by doubling back on its trail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craftily - in a cunning or sly manner
  • antonym: guilelessly - without cunning or deceit
