climate denialist

noun
someone who rejects climate change science
6.3 7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪ.mət dɪˈnaɪ.ə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˈklaɪ.mət dɪˈnaɪ.ə.lɪst / (BrE)
a person who refuses to accept the scientific consensus on climate change, often rejecting evidence of global warming and its causes

Examples

Climate denialists reject science
Media outlets sometimes give climate denialists airtime to present opposing views.
She accused the panelist of being a climate denialist despite his lack of scientific background.
The climate denialist refused to accept the overwhelming evidence presented by scientists.
