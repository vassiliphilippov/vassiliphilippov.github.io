Señora

noun
Spanish title for a married woman
4.3
Pronunciation: / seɪˈnjɔɾə / (AmE), / seɪˈnjɔːrə / (BrE)
a Spanish title or form of address for a married woman; equivalent to 'Mrs.' or 'madam'

Examples

Senora Ramirez
As soon as Señora Ramirez entered the room, everyone stood up to welcome her.
The children greeted their teacher with a respectful Señora González.
The invitation was addressed to Señora Maria López and her family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mrs. - English title for a married woman
  • antonym: Señorita - a Spanish title for an unmarried woman
