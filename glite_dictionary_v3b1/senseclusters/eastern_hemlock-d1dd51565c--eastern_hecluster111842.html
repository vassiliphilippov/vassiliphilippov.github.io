eastern hemlock

noun
a North American evergreen tree
7.4 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààist…ôrn Ààh…õml…ëk / (AmE), / ÀàiÀêst…ôn Ààh…õml…ík / (BrE)
a large evergreen tree (Tsuga canadensis) native to eastern North America, commonly found in forests and used especially for pulpwood

Examples

The eastern hemlock tree
Many birds and mammals depend on the shelter provided by the eastern hemlock.
Paper companies often harvest eastern hemlock for use as pulpwood.
The forest was dense with towering eastern hemlock trees, their needles carpeting the ground below.

Synonyms

  • Tsuga canadensis
    8.9 0.02/m
    Tsuga canadensis is the scientific name used by botanists, while eastern hemlock is the usual common name in everyday speech.
  • Canadian hemlock
    7.9 0.03/m
    Canadian hemlock is a common name used in wood and trade, while eastern hemlock names the same forest tree.
