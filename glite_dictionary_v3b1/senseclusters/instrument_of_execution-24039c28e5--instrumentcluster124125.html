instrument of execution

noun
tool for carrying out execution
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnstrəmənt əv ˌɛksəˈkjuʃən / (AmE), / ˈɪnstrəmənt əv ˌɛksɪˈkjuːʃən / (BrE)
A device or tool specifically designed and used to put a condemned person to death as part of a legal execution.

Examples

An instrument of execution
Debate continues over whether any instrument of execution can be considered humane.
The guillotine was a notorious instrument of execution during the French Revolution.
Throughout history, societies have employed various instruments of execution, such as the gallows and the electric chair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electric chair - a chair used to execute condemned prisoners by electrocution
  • synonym: gallows - a structure for hanging people
  • synonym: guillotine - a device for beheading by dropping a heavy blade
