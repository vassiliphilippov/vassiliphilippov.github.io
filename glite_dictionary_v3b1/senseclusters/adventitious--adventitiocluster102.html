adventitious

adjective
occurring by chance; not inherent
Pronunciation: / ˌædvənˈtɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌædvɛnˈtɪʃəs / (BrE)
Happening or appearing by chance rather than as an essential part; not inherent or intrinsic.

Examples

An adventitious event
His role in the project was adventitious, as he had not been originally scheduled to participate.
Some of the decorations were adventitious, added at the last minute for color and variety.
The scientist explained that the substance's effect was purely adventitious and not a result of its chemical structure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incidental - occurring as a minor accompaniment or by chance
  • antonym: inherent - existing as a permanent, essential, or characteristic attribute
