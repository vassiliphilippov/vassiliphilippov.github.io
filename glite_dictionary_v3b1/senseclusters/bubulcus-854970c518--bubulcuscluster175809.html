Bubulcus

noun
genus of small white herons (cattle egrets)
Pronunciation: / buˈbʌlkəs / (AmE), / bjuːˈbʌlkəs / (BrE)
A genus of small white herons known as cattle egrets, commonly found near grazing animals.
