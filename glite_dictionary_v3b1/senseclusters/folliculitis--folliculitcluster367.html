folliculitis

noun
inflammation of a hair follicle
6.5 6.9
Pronunciation: /fɑː.lɪ.kjʊ.ˈlaɪ.tɪs/ /; / /fɑː.lɪ.kjuː.ˈlaɪ.tɪs/ (AmE), /fɒl.ɪ.kjʊ.ˈlaɪ.tɪs/ /; / /fɒl.ɪ.kjuː.ˈlaɪ.tɪs/ (BrE)
A medical condition in which a hair follicle becomes inflamed, often resulting in a red, swollen area on the skin.

Examples

Folliculitis on skin
The doctor diagnosed his red scalp bumps as folliculitis.
Treatment for folliculitis usually involves topical antibiotics or antiseptics.
Wearing tight clothing can sometimes cause folliculitis on the thighs.
