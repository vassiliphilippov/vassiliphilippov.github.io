radiolarian

noun
single-celled marine protozoan
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdiəˈlɛriən / (AmE), / ˌreɪdɪəˈleəriən / (BrE)
A type of single-celled marine protozoan with a siliceous skeleton and long, radiating pseudopods.

Examples

A radiolarian cell
Scientists study radiolarians to understand past ocean conditions.
The skeleton of a radiolarian is often beautifully intricate and delicate.
Tiny radiolarians drift in the open seas, forming part of the plankton community.
