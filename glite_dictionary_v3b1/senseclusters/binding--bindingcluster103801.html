binding

noun
strip sewn on for reinforcement or decoration
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbaɪndɪŋ / (BrE)
something that covers, secures, or holds the edge of an object, such as fabric, a book, or a bandage
