masquerade

verb
pretend to be someone else; disguise oneself
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌmæskəˈreɪd / (AmE), / ˌmɑːskəˈreɪd / (BrE)
to pretend to be someone or something that you are not, often in order to deceive; to disguise oneself

Examples

To masquerade as a friend
At the party, several guests masqueraded as famous movie stars.
She was tired of masquerading as someone she was not.
The spy tried to masquerade as a local villager to gain information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impersonate - to pretend to be another person
  • synonym: pretend - to act as if something is true when it is not
