arrhythmically

adverb
without regular rhythm
Pronunciation: / əˈrɪðmɪkli / (AmE), / əˈrɪðmɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that lacks a regular or normal rhythm or pattern (such as in music, movement, speech, or medically)

Examples

To play arrhythmically
The drummer played arrhythmically, making the song difficult to follow.
During the test, the patient's heart was beating arrhythmically and required immediate attention.
The lights flickered arrhythmically during the storm, creating an unsettling atmosphere.
The lights flickered arrhythmically, making the room feel eerie and unpredictable.
She tapped her fingers arrhythmically on the table while waiting for the call.
The dancers moved arrhythmically, breaking away from the traditional choreography.
