consign

verb
send or deliver goods to a place
6.3
Pronunciation: / kənˈsaɪn / (AmE), / kənˈsaɪn / (BrE)
to send or deliver something, especially goods, to a person or place, often officially or for sale

Examples

To consign goods
All orders are consigned by courier to minimize the risk of loss.
He consigned the clothing to a local shop for resale.
The company will consign the packages to your new address next week.

Related Terms
