sprawly

adjective
spread out in an untidy or irregular way
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɑːli / (AmE), / ˈsprɔːli / (BrE)
spreading out in an irregular or untidy way, especially over a wide area; sprawling.

Examples

A sprawly handwriting
Her handwriting was so sprawly that it filled the entire page.
The sprawly suburbs made it difficult to get around without a car.
They moved from a compact apartment to a sprawly house in the countryside.

  • synonym: sprawling - spreading out in an untidy or irregular way
