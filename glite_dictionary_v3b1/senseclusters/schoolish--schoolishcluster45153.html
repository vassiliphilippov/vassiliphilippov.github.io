schoolish

adjective
like a school
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈskuː.lɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈskuː.lɪʃ/ (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of school or schooling, often in a way that is overly formal or academic

Examples

Schoolish tone
Her schoolish tone made the training session feel more like a lecture than a discussion.
The report was criticized for its schoolish language and lack of practical advice.
He avoided a schoolish approach, choosing instead to make the workshop interactive and fun.
