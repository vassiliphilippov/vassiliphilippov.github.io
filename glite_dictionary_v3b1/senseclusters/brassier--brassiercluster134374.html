brassier

adjective
more bold or showy
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbræsiər / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːsiə / (BrE)
more brazen, bold, or showy in manner or appearance than before.

Examples

Brassier style
His jokes became brassier as the evening went on, making everyone laugh.
The new design is much brassier than last year's subtle look.
She wore a brassier outfit to stand out at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bolder - more confident and willing to take risks
  • antonym: subtler - less obvious or showy in manner
