valuable

noun
item of value, often valuable objects
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈvæljəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈvæljʊəbl̩ / (BrE)
an object or objects, especially small items, that are worth a lot of money or are important for personal reasons

Examples

Keep your valuables safe
Hotel guests are advised to keep their valuables in the safe provided in each room.
Remember to pack your valuables in your carry-on luggage rather than your checked bag.
She lost all her valuables when her bag was stolen on the train.

Related Terms

  • synonym: valuables - personal possessions of value; plural form commonly used in same sense
