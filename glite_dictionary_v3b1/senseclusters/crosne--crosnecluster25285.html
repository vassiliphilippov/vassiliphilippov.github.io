crosne

noun
edible tuber plant
Pronunciation: / kroʊn / (AmE), / krəʊn / (BrE)
a herbaceous plant of the mint family, Stachys affinis, grown for its small edible tubers

Examples

Crosne plant
The chef added sautéed crosne to the salad for a unique crunchy texture.
Farmers in some regions have started cultivating crosne as a specialty crop.
She harvested a basket of crosne tubers from her garden in late autumn.
