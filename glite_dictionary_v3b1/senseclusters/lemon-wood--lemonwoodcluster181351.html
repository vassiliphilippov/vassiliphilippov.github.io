lemon-wood

noun
South African evergreen tree with hard wood
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛmən wʊd / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən wʊd / (BrE)
A South African evergreen tree with hard, tough wood, often used in carpentry and woodworking.

Examples

South African lemon-wood
