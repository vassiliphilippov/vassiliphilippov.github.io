dielectrolysis

noun
movement of charged particles in a colloid by electric field
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /daɪ.ə.lek.ˈtrɑːl.ɪ.sɪs/ /; / /daɪ.ə.lek.ˈtrɔːl.ɪ.sɪs/ (AmE), /daɪ.ə.lek.ˈtrɒl.ɪ.sɪs/ (BrE)
