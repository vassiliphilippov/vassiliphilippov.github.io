Procavia capensis

noun
species of hyrax (rock hyrax)
9.9
Pronunciation: / proʊˈkeɪ.vi.ə kəˈpɛn.sɪs / (AmE), / prəˈkeɪ.vɪə kəˈpen.sɪs / (BrE)
A species of hyrax that inhabits rocky areas in Africa and the Middle East, commonly known as the rock hyrax.
