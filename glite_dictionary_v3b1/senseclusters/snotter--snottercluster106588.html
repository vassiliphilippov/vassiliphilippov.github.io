snotter

noun
sailing rope fitting
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɑtər / (AmE), / ˈsnɒtə / (BrE)
a short rope or fitting used to fasten a sail or spar on a boat

Examples

A snotter rope

A worn snotter can make it difficult to control the sail properly.
