prewriting

noun
early scribbles
Pronunciation: / ˈpriˌraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the early marks, scribbles, or drawings made by children before they learn to write actual letters or words

Examples

Child's prewriting marks
The teacher encouraged prewriting activities to help children develop their fine motor skills.
Many children go through a prewriting stage before they can form recognizable letters.
Parents proudly displayed their child's colorful prewriting on the refrigerator.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scribbling - making random marks or lines, especially by young children
  • degree higher: writing - the act of forming letters and words to communicate
