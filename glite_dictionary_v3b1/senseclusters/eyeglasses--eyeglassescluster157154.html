eyeglasses

noun
corrective lenses worn on the face
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌɡlæsɪz / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌɡlɑːsɪz / (BrE)
A pair of lenses set in a frame that rests on the nose and ears, used to correct or assist defective eyesight.

Examples

A pair of eyeglasses
His eyeglasses broke during the basketball game and he had to tape them together.
Modern eyeglasses come in a wide variety of styles and colors.
She forgot her eyeglasses at home and struggled to read the menu.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: contact lenses - lenses worn directly on the eyes for vision correction
  • synonym: spectacles - glasses for correcting vision
