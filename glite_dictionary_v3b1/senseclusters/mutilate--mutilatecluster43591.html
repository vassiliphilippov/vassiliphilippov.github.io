mutilate

verb
to damage or injure severely
7.4 2.97/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmjuÀêt…ôÀåle…™t / (AmE), / ÀàmjuÀêt…™le…™t / (BrE)
to damage or change something so badly that it is ruined, disfigured, or hard to recognize, often by cutting or destroying parts

Synonyms

  • mangle
    6.1 1.08/m
    mangle means tearing, crushing, or cutting something so badly it becomes twisted and broken, while mutilate means cutting away parts and ruining it.
  • maim
    7.0 1.79/m
    maim means seriously injuring a person and causing lasting harm or loss of limbs, while mutilate often means cutting away parts and disfiguring.
  • disfigure
    5.8 1.70/m
    disfigure spoils someone's or something's appearance by marking or deforming it, while mutilate implies severe cutting or removal of parts.
