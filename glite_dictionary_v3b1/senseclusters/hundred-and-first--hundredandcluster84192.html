hundred-and-first

adjective
ordinal number: 101st
Pronunciation: / ˌhʌndrəd ən ˈfɜrst / (AmE), / ˌhʌndrəd ənd ˈfɜːst / (BrE)
the ordinal number corresponding to 101st in a sequence; coming before all others except for 100 in order or rank

Examples

The hundred-and-first place
She finished in hundred-and-first place out of a thousand runners.
The hundred-and-first page of the book contained a major plot twist.
This is the hundred-and-first anniversary of the school's founding.
