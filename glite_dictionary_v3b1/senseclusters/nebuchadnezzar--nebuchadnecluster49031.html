nebuchadnezzar

noun
large wine bottle (holds 20 bottles)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɛbjəkədˈnɛzər / (AmE), / ˌnɛbjʊkədˈnɛzə / (BrE)
a very large wine bottle holding the equivalent of 20 standard bottles (15 liters), often used for display purposes

The restaurant keeps a nebuchadnezzar on display as a conversation piece for wine enthusiasts.
