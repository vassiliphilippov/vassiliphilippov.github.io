corncake

noun
cornmeal cake
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌkeɪk / (BrE)
a small, flat cake made from cornmeal, usually fried or baked and often served as a side dish

Examples

A corncake with honey
For breakfast, we enjoyed a warm corncake topped with honey and butter.
She learned to make a traditional corncake recipe from her grandmother.
The restaurant served spicy chili with a side of corncakes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cornbread - a bread made from cornmeal, typically baked
  • synonym: pancake - a thin, flat cake made from batter and cooked on a griddle or frying pan
