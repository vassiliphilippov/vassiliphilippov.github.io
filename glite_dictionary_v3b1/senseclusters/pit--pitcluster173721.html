pit

noun
a hollow or indentation in a surface
5.7 4.3
Pronunciation: /pɪt/ (AmE), /pɪt/ (BrE)
a hollow or depression in a surface, especially a small anatomical indentation or concavity

Examples

A pit in skin
Scars left pits on his cheek after the illness.
The pit on the surface of the fruit is a natural indentation.
The human skull has a small pit called the sella turcica at its base.
