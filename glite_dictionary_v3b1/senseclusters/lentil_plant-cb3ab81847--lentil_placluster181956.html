lentil plant

noun
annual herb grown for edible seeds
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛn.tɪl ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈlɛn.tɪl ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
a Eurasian annual herb (Lens culinaris) widely grown for its edible flattened seeds and leafy stalks used as fodder

Examples

The lentil plant seeds
Farmers harvested the lentil plant in late summer to supply local markets.
The lentil plant is cultivated in many countries for its nutritious seeds.
The leaves and stems of the lentil plant are often used as animal fodder after harvesting the seeds.
