flat wash

noun
laundry suitable for machine ironing
7.8
Pronunciation: / flæt wɑʃ / (AmE), / flæt wɒʃ / (BrE)
laundry items, such as sheets and towels, that can be ironed by machine rather than by hand

Examples

A flat wash load
Pillowcases and sheets are considered part of the flat wash in most commercial laundries.
The hotel sends all its flat wash to an outside laundry service.
The new machine can handle up to fifty pounds of flat wash at a time.
