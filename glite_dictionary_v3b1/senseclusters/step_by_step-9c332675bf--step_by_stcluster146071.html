step by step

adverb
in a gradual, steady manner
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛp baɪ ˈstɛp /; / stɛp baɪ stɛp / (AmE), / ˌstɛp baɪ ˈstɛp /; / stɛp baɪ stɛp / (BrE)
in a gradual way, doing each part or stage one after another
