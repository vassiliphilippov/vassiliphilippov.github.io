rearer

noun
one who raises young
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪrər / (AmE), / ˈrɪərə / (BrE)
a person or animal that raises or brings up young animals or children

Examples

A rearer of animals
The rearer of the puppies made sure they were healthy and well-fed.
As a skilled rearer of exotic birds, she has won several awards.
In some cultures, the mother is traditionally the main rearer of children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breeder - a person who breeds animals
  • synonym: caretaker - a person who takes care of others, especially children or animals
