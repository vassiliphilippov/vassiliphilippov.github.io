Synonyms

  • adulation
    8.4 1.25/m
    adulation is very strong, often public praise used to flatter powerful people, while flattery is general insincere praise for gain.
  • smarm
    9.7 0.02/m
    smarm is oily, false charm aimed at winning favor in close situations, while flattery is broad insincere praise for gain.
  • fawning
    7.4 0.01/m
    fawning is showing eager praise or attention to please someone directly, while flattery is any insincere praise to gain advantage.
  • fulsomeness
    9.9 0.01/m
    fulsomeness is heavy, often old-fashioned praise that sounds forced or excessive, while flattery simply means insincere praise for gain.
