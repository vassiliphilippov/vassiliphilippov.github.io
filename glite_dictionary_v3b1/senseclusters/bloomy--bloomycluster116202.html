bloomy

adjective
with powdery surface
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːmi / (AmE), / ˈbluːmi / (BrE)
having a soft, powdery surface or covered with a natural bloom, as on fruit or leaves

Examples

Bloomy plums
The plums were bloomy with a delicate white coating that protected their skin.
She admired the bloomy grapes hanging in clusters on the vine.
After the rain, the leaves appeared bloomy and fresh in the morning light.

Related Terms

  • antonym: glossy - having a shiny or smooth surface
  • synonym: powdery - covered with or resembling powder
