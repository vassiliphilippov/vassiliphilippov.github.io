supertrain

noun
large freight train
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsuː.pər.treɪn/ (AmE), /ˈsuː.pə.treɪn/ (BrE)
a very long or unusually heavy freight train designed to carry large volumes of goods over long distances

Examples

A supertrain carries cargo
The new supertrain can transport hundreds of containers in a single journey.
A supertrain is often used to move bulk goods across vast distances efficiently.
Engineers designed the supertrain to handle heavier loads than standard freight trains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freight train - a train used for transporting goods rather than passengers
  • synonym: locomotive - a powered railway vehicle that pulls trains
