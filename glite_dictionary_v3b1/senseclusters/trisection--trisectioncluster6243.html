trisection

noun
one of three parts
8.0
Pronunciation: / traɪˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / traɪˈsɛkʃən / (BrE)
one of the three parts created by dividing something into three, often equal, sections

Examples

After the trisection of the land, each sibling received a separate plot.
