carceral

adjective
prison-related
9.7 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ërs…ôr…ôl / (AmE), / Ààk…ëÀês…ôr…ôl / (BrE)
relating to prisons, imprisonment, or the system of incarceration

Examples

The carceral system
The new law aims to reform the carceral system and reduce incarceration rates.
Activists are challenging the carceral approach to dealing with social problems.
Her research focuses on the effects of carceral policies on marginalized communities.

Synonyms

  • correctional
    5.8 2.54/m
    correctional refers to institutions and programs for punishing or rehabilitating offenders, while carceral denotes prisons and the wider system of incarceration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: penal - relating to punishment or prisons
  • synonym: prison - a place where people are kept as punishment for a crime
