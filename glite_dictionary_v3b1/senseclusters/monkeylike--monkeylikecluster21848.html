monkeylike

adjective
resembling a monkey
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋkiˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋkiˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having characteristics of a monkey, especially in appearance or behavior

Examples

Monkeylike face
The child made a monkeylike face to make his friends laugh.
Her monkeylike agility allowed her to climb the tree quickly and easily.
He moved through the playground with monkeylike energy, swinging from bar to bar.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humanlike - resembling a human
  • synonym: simian - relating to or resembling monkeys or apes
