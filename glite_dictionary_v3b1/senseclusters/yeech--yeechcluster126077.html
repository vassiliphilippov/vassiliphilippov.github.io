yeech

interjection
disgust exclamation
Pronunciation: / jiːtʃ / (AmE), / jiːtʃ / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to express disgust or distaste

Examples

Yeech, gross food
He opened the old lunchbox and immediately said, "yeech, what is that smell?"
When she saw the bug in her soup, she exclaimed, "yeech!"
The muddy water looked so dirty that he just muttered, "yeech, no way am I drinking that."
