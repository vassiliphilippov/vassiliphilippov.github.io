cooling system

noun
system that cools a vehicle's engine
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːlɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈkuːlɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a system of equipment or components designed to remove heat and keep a machine, vehicle, building, or environment at a safe or comfortable temperature

Examples

The cooling system works
Regular maintenance of the cooling system can prevent costly engine repairs.
She flushed the cooling system to remove any accumulated debris.
The car broke down because its cooling system failed during the summer heat.
During the summer, the building's cooling system works overtime to keep everyone comfortable.
Many modern computers use a liquid cooling system for better temperature control.
The car's cooling system needs regular maintenance to prevent the engine from overheating.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heating system - a system for providing warmth
  • synonym: radiator - a device that transfers heat from the engine to the air in a cooling system
  • synonym: refrigeration - the process of cooling or keeping something cold
