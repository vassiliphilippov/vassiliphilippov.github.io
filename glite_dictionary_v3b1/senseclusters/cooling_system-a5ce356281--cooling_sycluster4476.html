cooling system

noun
system that cools a vehicle's engine
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkuː.lɪŋ ˈsɪs.təm/ (AmE), /ˈkuː.lɪŋ ˈsɪs.təm/ (BrE)
a system of equipment or components designed to remove heat and keep a machine, vehicle, building, or environment at a safe or comfortable temperature

Examples

Regular maintenance of the cooling system can prevent costly engine repairs.
