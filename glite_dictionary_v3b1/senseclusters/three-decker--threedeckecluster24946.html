three-decker

noun
sandwich with three slices of bread
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈθriˌdɛkər / (AmE), / ˈθriːˌdɛkə / (BrE)
A sandwich made with three slices of bread, usually toasted, with filling between each pair of slices.

Examples

A three-decker sandwich
I couldn't finish the massive three-decker they served for brunch.
She ordered a three-decker for lunch, filled with turkey, ham, and cheese.
The restaurant's specialty is a towering three-decker sandwich.

Related Terms

  • synonym: club sandwich - A sandwich with three slices of bread and two layers of filling
