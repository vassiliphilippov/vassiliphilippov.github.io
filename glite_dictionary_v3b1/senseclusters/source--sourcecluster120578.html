source

noun
process supplying energy or substance to a system
2.6 110/m
Pronunciation: / s…îrs / (AmE), / s…îÀês / (BrE)
the place, person, thing, or process from which something originates, is obtained, or is caused (such as energy, supplies, inspiration, or the start of something)

Examples

A source of energy
Exhaust fumes are a significant source of air pollution in cities.
The power plant serves as a source of electricity for the entire region.
The sun is the primary source of energy for life on Earth.
Historians study the source of the tradition to understand its roots.
Pittsburgh is the source of the Ohio River.
The Nile is considered the source of much of Egypt's water supply.
The factory became a key source of electricity for the surrounding area.
The library is an important source of information for students.
This well is the main source of water for the village.
