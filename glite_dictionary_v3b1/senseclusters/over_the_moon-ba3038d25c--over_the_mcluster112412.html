over the moon

adjective
extremely happy; elated
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvər ðə ˈmun / (AmE), / ˌəʊvə ðə ˈmuːn / (BrE)

Examples

They were over the moon about the birth of their first child.
When my team won the final, I was over the moon and celebrated all night.
