squirrely

adjective
unstable or hard to control
8.6 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààskw…úrli / (AmE), / Ààskw…úÀêli / (BrE)
unstable or likely to swerve, making something difficult to control, especially a vehicle or object

Examples

Squirrely steering
The car felt squirrely on the icy road, making it hard to keep in a straight line.
After the repairs, the bike was less squirrely and much easier to handle.
If your skateboard gets squirrely at high speeds, check the wheels and trucks for problems.

Synonyms

  • drifty
    7.1 0.00/m

  • wonky
    7.7 0.2/m
    wonky is not steady or straight and often sits crooked, while squirrely implies sudden swerving that makes control difficult.
