kanaka

noun
offensive slur
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɑːkə / (AmE), / kəˈnɑːkə / (BrE)
an offensive term for a Pacific Islander, especially a Hawaiian; considered a slur and should not be used

Examples

A kanaka slur
The word kanaka is now recognized as an offensive term and should be avoided.
Using kanaka to refer to someone is disrespectful and hurtful.
Many people are unaware that kanaka is considered a slur in modern usage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: islander - a person from an island, especially in the Pacific
