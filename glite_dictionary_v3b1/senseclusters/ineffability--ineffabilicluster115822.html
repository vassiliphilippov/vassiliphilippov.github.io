ineffability

noun
impossibility to express
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ne.fə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɪ.nə.fə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ɪ.ne.fə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɪ.nə.fə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
the quality of being impossible to express or describe fully in words

Examples

The ineffability of love

Many poets try to capture the ineffability of love in their verses.

Many poets try to capture the ineffability of beauty in their verses.
The ineffability of the tragedy made it difficult for anyone to speak about it openly.
