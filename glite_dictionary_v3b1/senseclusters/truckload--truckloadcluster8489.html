truckload

noun
large quantity
2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtrʌk.loʊd/ /; / /ˈtrʌ.kloʊd/ (AmE), /ˈtrʌk.ləʊd/ /; / /ˈtrʌ.kləʊd/ (BrE)
