again and again

adverb
repeatedly, many times
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈɡɛn ənd əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / əˈɡɛn ənd əˈɡɛn / (BrE)

Examples

Again and again repeatedly

He asked the same question again and again until he got an answer.
The coach emphasized the importance of practice again and again during the training session.
