relation

noun
narration or recounting of events
4.7
Pronunciation: / rɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a spoken or written account or narration of an event or series of events

Examples

A relation of events
His relation of what happened that night differed from everyone else's version.
She wrote a vivid relation of her journey across the desert.
The historian's detailed relation of the battle captured the imagination of his readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: account - a report or description of an event or experience
  • synonym: narration - the action of narrating a story or event
  • antonym: silence - the absence of narration or speech
