yaxche

noun
Maya world tree
Pronunciation: / jɑːʃˈtʃeɪ / (AmE), / jɑːʃˈtʃeɪ /; / jæʃˈtʃeɪ / (BrE)
a tropical tree (ceiba or kapok) native to Central America, considered sacred in Maya culture and believed to connect the heavens, earth, and underworld as the 'world tree'
