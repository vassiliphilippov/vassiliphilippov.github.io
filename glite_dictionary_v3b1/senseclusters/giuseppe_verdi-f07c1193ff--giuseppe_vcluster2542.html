Giuseppe Verdi

proper noun
Italian opera composer (1813–1901)
Pronunciation: / dʒuˈzɛpeɪ ˈvɛrdi / (AmE), / dʒuːˈzɛpeɪ ˈvɛːdi / (BrE)
