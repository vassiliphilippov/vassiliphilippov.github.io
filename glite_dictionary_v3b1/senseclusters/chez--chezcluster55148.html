chez

preposition
at someone's home
9.5
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪ / (AmE), / ʃeɪ / (BrE)
used to indicate being at the home or place of a particular person, especially in French expressions used in English.

Examples

Dinner chez Marie
We are having dinner chez Marie tonight.
The invitation said the party would be chez the Smiths on Saturday evening.
He always feels welcome chez his grandparents during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: at - in or near a particular place
