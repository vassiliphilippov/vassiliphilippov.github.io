overtell

verb
to repeat excessively
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈtɛl / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈtɛl / (BrE)
to tell something too often or with excessive detail, making it repetitive or tedious

Examples

To overtell a story
He tends to overtell stories, repeating the same details.
If you overtell the joke, it loses its humor and becomes boring.
She realized she might overtell her experiences when her friends stopped listening.
