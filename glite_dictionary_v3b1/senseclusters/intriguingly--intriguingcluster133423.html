intriguingly

adverb
curiously; interestingly
4.1
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtriːɡɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈtriːɡɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that arouses curiosity or interest

Examples

Intriguingly mysterious story
Intriguingly, the old diary was written in a secret code no one could understand.
The story unfolds intriguingly, keeping readers guessing until the very end.
He spoke intriguingly about his travels to remote and mysterious places.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boringly - in a way that causes boredom or lack of interest
  • synonym: interestingly - in a way that is interesting or attracts attention
