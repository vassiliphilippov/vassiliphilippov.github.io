pedometer

noun
device for counting steps walked
5.8
Pronunciation: / pəˈdɑmɪtər / (AmE), / pɪˈdɒmɪtə / (BrE)
A device that counts the number of steps taken by the wearer while walking or running.

Examples

A pedometer device
He clipped the pedometer to his belt before leaving for his morning walk.
Her pedometer showed she had walked over 10,000 steps by lunchtime.
The new fitness app syncs with your pedometer to track daily steps.
