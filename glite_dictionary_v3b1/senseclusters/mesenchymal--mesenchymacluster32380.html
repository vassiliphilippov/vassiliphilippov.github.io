mesenchymal

adjective
of connective tissue
Pronunciation: / mɛˈzɛŋkɪməl / (AmE), / mɛˈzɛŋkɪməl /; / mɛˈsɛŋkɪməl / (BrE)
relating to mesenchyme (the embryonic connective tissue) or to cells and tissues that develop from it

Examples

The tumor was found to have a mesenchymal origin, indicating it developed from connective tissue.
