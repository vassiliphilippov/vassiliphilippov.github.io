self-employed

adjective
working for oneself
1.8 2.8
Pronunciation: /self ɪm.ˈplɔɪd/ /; / /self əm.ˈplɔɪd/ (AmE), /self ɪm.ˈplɔɪd/ /; / /self əm.ˈplɔɪd/ (BrE)
working for oneself, rather than for an employer; earning income directly from one's own business or profession.

Examples

Self-employed person
He has been self-employed as a graphic designer for over ten years.
Many self-employed people enjoy the flexibility of setting their own work hours.
The government offers tax benefits to self-employed individuals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: employed - having a paid job working for an employer
  • synonym: freelance - working for oneself, often taking jobs from multiple clients
