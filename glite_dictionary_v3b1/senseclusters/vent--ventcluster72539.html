vent

noun
means of expressing emotion or energy
3.2
Pronunciation: / vɛnt / (AmE), / vɛnt / (BrE)
a means or activity by which strong emotions or energy can be released or expressed

Examples

A vent for anger
Physical exercise provides a healthy vent for nervous energy.
She used painting as a vent for her emotions after a stressful day.
Writing poetry became a vent for his frustration and loneliness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outlet - a way of expressing or releasing emotion, energy, or creativity
  • synonym: release - the act of letting go or discharging emotions or energy
  • antonym: repression - the act of holding back or suppressing an emotion
