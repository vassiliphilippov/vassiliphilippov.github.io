Synonyms

  • critical analysis
    5.1 0.90/m
    critical analysis gives a detailed, structured examination of a text or work, while criticism judges its merits and faults.
  • critique
    5.6 15/m
    critique offers a detailed evaluation often of art or ideas, while criticism makes general judgments of merits and faults.
  • critical appraisal
    6.3 0.14/m
    critical appraisal is a careful, systematic evaluation used in formal reports, while criticism is a broader judgment of merits and faults.
  • critical review
    4.1 0.12/m
    critical review is a written article that assesses a book or film, while criticism can mean any judgment of merits and faults.
  • evaluation
    3.7 57/m
    evaluation is the formal process of judging value or quality, while criticism focuses on analyzing merits and faults with opinion.
  • assessment
    2.8 118/m
    assessment is a measured judgment often used for tests or performance, while criticism addresses merits and faults more broadly.
  • scrutiny
    5.5 13/m
    scrutiny is a very close inspection of details, while criticism goes beyond inspection to judge merits and faults.
