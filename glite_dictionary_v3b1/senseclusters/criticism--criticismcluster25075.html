criticism

noun
serious examination and judgment
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪt̬.ɪˌsɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈkrɪt.ɪ.sɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the act of carefully analyzing and making a judgment about the merits and faults of something

Examples

Fair criticism
Art students are taught how to give and receive criticism.
Constructive criticism can help people improve their skills.
Her manager offered helpful criticism of her presentation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: praise - expression of approval or admiration
  • synonym: review - an evaluation or assessment of something
