family Apterygidae

noun
bird family including kiwis
Pronunciation: / ˌæptəˈrɪdʒɪdiː / (AmE), / ˌæptəˈrɪdʒɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of flightless birds native to New Zealand, including the kiwis, and forming the only family in the order Apterygiformes.

Examples

The family Apterygidae
All living species in the family Apterygidae are endemic to New Zealand.
Scientists have studied the evolution of the family Apterygidae to better understand flightless birds.
The family Apterygidae is best known for its only extant members, the kiwi birds of New Zealand.
