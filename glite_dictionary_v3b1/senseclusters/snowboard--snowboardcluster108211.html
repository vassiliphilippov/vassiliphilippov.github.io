snowboard

verb
glide down snow on a snowboard
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌbɔːd / (BrE)
to glide down a snow-covered slope while standing and balancing on a snowboard

Examples

To snowboard down a hill
He learned to snowboard before he could ski.
Many people travel to the Alps each winter to snowboard on the fresh powder.
They spent the afternoon snowboarding with friends at the mountain resort.
