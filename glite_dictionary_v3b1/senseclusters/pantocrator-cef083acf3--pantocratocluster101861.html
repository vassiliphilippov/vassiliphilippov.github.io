Pantocrator

proper noun
Corfu's highest mountain
Pronunciation: / ˌpæn.təˈkreɪ.tər / (AmE), / ˌpæn.təˈkrɑː.tɔː / (BrE)
the highest mountain on the Greek island of Corfu, famous for its panoramic views and historic monastery

Examples

Mount Pantocrator
The view from the summit of Pantocrator stretches across the entire island of Corfu.
Many tourists visit Pantocrator to explore the ancient monastery at its peak.
On a clear day, you can see Albania from the top of Pantocrator.
