swallower

noun
one who swallows
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑloʊər / (AmE), / ˈswɒləə / (BrE)
a person or animal that swallows something, especially food or drink

Examples

A fast swallower of food
The pelican is a skilled swallower of large fish.
As a competitive swallower, he could finish a glass of water in seconds.
Some snakes are remarkable swallowers and can eat prey much larger than their heads.

  • synonym: eater - a person or animal that eats
