slide

noun
glass plate for microscope specimens
Pronunciation: / slaɪd / (AmE), / slaɪd / (BrE)
a small, flat, rectangular piece of glass used to mount specimens for examination under a microscope

Examples

Glass slide
Each slide was labeled with the specimen's name for easy identification.
She prepared a new slide to study the plant cells in more detail.
The scientist carefully placed the sample on the slide to observe it under the microscope.
