multivalve

noun
organism with many valves
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltivaɪv / (AmE), / ˈmʌltɪvɑːlv / (BrE)
an organism or structure, especially a shell, that has multiple valves or hinged plates

Examples

A multivalve shell
The scientist examined a rare multivalve collected from the deep sea.
Some seed capsules are classified as multivalves because they split into several sections.
The shell of this ancient mollusk is a classic example of a multivalve structure.

Related Terms
