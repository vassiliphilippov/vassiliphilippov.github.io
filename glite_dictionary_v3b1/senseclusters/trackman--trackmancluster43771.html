trackman

noun
track maintenance worker
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtrækmæn / (AmE), / ˈtrækmən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to maintain or repair a running track, racecourse, or other sports track

Examples

Racecourse trackman
The trackman arrived early to inspect the racecourse before the event started.
After the storm, the trackman worked hard to clear debris from the running track.
Each morning, the trackman checks the surface for any damage or hazards.
