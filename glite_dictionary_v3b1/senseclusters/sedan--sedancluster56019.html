sedan

noun
type of passenger car with a closed body
2.8 2.1
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈdæn/ (AmE), /sə.ˈdæn/ (BrE)
a passenger car with a closed body, separate trunk, and typically front and rear seats and two or four doors

Examples

A family sedan
He prefers driving a sedan because of its comfort and ample seating space.
The sedan easily accommodated their family of five for the weekend trip.
They purchased a new sedan with four doors and a spacious trunk.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: SUV - a sport utility vehicle, larger than a sedan, often with off-road capability
  • degree lower: coupe - a two-door car with a fixed roof and a sloping rear
  • degree lower: hatchback - a car with a door across the full width at the back end
  • degree higher: wagon - a car with an extended rear cargo area (station wagon/estate)
