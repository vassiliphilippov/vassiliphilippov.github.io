tip

noun
small monetary reward for service
1.0 5.73/m
Pronunciation: / tÉªp / (AmE), / tÉªp / (BrE)
a small amount of money given to someone for a service they have performed, in addition to the basic payment

Examples

A tip for the waiter
It is customary to give a tip to the housekeeping staff at a hotel.
She was delighted to receive a large tip from the satisfied customers.
We left a generous tip for the server after an excellent meal.
