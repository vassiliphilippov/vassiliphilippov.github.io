geek

noun
socially awkward or eccentric person
6.8 11/m
Pronunciation: / …°ik / (AmE), / …°iÀêk / (BrE)
a person who is regarded as socially awkward or eccentric, often having unusual interests or hobbies

Examples

A computer geek

He's always been a bit of a geek, obsessed with collecting rare insects.
In high school, she was labeled a geek because of her passion for computers.
