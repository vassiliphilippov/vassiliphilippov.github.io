pellucidly

adverb
in a clear and lucid manner

In a way that is clear and easy to understand; lucidly.

Examples

To speak pellucidly
He expressed his opinion pellucidly, leaving no room for confusion.
Her essay was written so pellucidly that even those unfamiliar with the subject could understand it.
The professor explained the complex concept pellucidly, making it easy for the students to grasp.
