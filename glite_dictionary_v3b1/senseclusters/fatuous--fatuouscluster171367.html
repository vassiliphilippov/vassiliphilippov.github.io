fatuous

adjective
extremely silly or foolish
9.9 7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfætʃ.uː.əs/ /; / /ˈfæt.juː.əs/ (AmE), /ˈfæt.juː.əs/ /; / /ˈfætʃ.uː.əs/ (BrE)

Examples
