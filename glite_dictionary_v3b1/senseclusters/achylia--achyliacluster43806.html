achylia

noun
absence of gastric juices
6.2
Pronunciation: / əˈkaɪliə / (AmE), / əˈkɪlɪə / (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by the absence or severe reduction of gastric juice secretion in the stomach.

Examples

Achylia of stomach
Diagnosis of achylia requires careful analysis of gastric secretions in the patient.
Long-term achylia may lead to digestive difficulties and nutritional deficiencies.
The physician explained that the cause of her symptoms was achylia.
