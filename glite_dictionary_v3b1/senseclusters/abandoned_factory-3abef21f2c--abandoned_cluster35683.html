abandoned factory

noun
a deserted, unused factory
1.3
Pronunciation: / əˈbændənd ˈfæk.tɚ.i / (AmE), / əˈbændənd ˈfæk.tər.i / (BrE)
a factory building that is no longer in use and has been deserted by its owners or workers

Examples

An abandoned factory building
Developers plan to turn the abandoned factory into modern apartments within the next year.
The city council voted to demolish the abandoned factory after years of complaints from residents.
We explored the abandoned factory and found old machinery covered in dust.
