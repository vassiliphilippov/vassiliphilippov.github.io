homeopathically

adverb
in a homeopathic way
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊmiəˈpæθɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhəʊmiəˈpæθɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or uses homeopathy, a system of alternative medicine based on treating diseases with highly diluted substances

Examples

Treated homeopathically
The patient was treated homeopathically for her allergies, using natural remedies.
Some people prefer to address minor illnesses homeopathically rather than with conventional medicine.
The doctor explained how the medicine was prepared homeopathically according to specific principles.
