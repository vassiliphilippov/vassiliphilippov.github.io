acidification

noun
process of becoming acidic
6.3
Pronunciation: / əˌsɪdəfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌsɪdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of becoming acidic or being converted into an acid

Examples

The acidification of lakes
Industrial emissions can lead to the acidification of nearby lakes and rivers.
Ocean acidification is a growing concern for marine ecosystems worldwide.
The acidification of rainwater has damaging effects on buildings and plant life.
