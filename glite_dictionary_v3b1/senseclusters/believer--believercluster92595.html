believer

noun
someone who accepts or supports something as true
Pronunciation: / bəˈlivər / (AmE), / bɪˈliːvə / (BrE)
a person who accepts or supports something as true, especially in religion or philosophy

Examples

A believer in God
Despite the challenges, he remained a believer in their cause.
Many believers gathered to support the new initiative.
She is a firm believer in the power of education.
As a committed believer, she attends church every Sunday without fail.
He is a believer in the value of forgiveness and compassion.
Many believers gather for communal worship during religious festivals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: follower - a person who supports and admires a particular person or set of ideas
  • antonym: nonbeliever - someone who does not have religious faith or belief
  • antonym: skeptic - a person inclined to doubt or question accepted opinions
