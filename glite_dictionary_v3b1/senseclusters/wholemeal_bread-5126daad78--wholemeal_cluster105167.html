wholemeal bread

noun
bread made from whole wheat flour
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊlˌmil brɛd / (AmE), / ˈhəʊlˌmiːl brɛd / (BrE)
Bread made from whole wheat flour, retaining all parts of the grain.

Examples

A slice of wholemeal bread
Many supermarkets offer a range of wholemeal bread options alongside white and seeded varieties.
She prefers toasts made with wholemeal bread for a healthier breakfast.
The recipe calls for fresh wholemeal bread crumbs to be used as a coating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whole wheat bread - bread made from the entire wheat kernel; common American term
