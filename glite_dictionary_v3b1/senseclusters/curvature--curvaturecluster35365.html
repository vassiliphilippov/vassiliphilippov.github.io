curvature

noun
the state or degree of being curved
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝː.vɚ.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɜː.və.tʃər / (BrE)
the condition of being curved or the degree to which something bends, especially a line or surface

Examples

Curvature of the surface
Scientists analyzed the curvature of the newly discovered planet's surface.
She measured the curvature of the metal beam to ensure it met the specifications.
The curvature of the road makes it dangerous to drive at high speeds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curve - a line or surface that bends smoothly; not straight
  • antonym: straightness - the quality of being straight; not curved
