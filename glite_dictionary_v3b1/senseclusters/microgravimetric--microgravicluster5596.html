microgravimetric

adjective
measuring tiny weights
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˌɡrævəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˌɡrævɪˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of very small changes in weight or mass

Examples

Microgravimetric analysis of mass
The microgravimetric analysis revealed a slight increase in the sample's mass after the reaction.
Scientists use microgravimetric techniques to detect extremely small amounts of residue.
A microgravimetric sensor can measure changes in weight at the microgram level.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gravimetric - relating to the measurement of weight
