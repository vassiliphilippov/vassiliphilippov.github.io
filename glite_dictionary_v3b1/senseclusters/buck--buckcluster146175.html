buck

noun
male of certain animals, esp. deer
5.8
Pronunciation: / bʌk / (AmE), / bʌk / (BrE)
A mature male of certain animals, especially deer, antelope, or rabbits.

Examples

A male buck
During the autumn, the dominant buck protects his territory from rivals.
The buck raised its antlers before darting away into the trees.
We spotted a large buck grazing near the edge of the forest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: doe - female of certain animals, esp. deer
  • synonym: stag - adult male deer, especially red deer
