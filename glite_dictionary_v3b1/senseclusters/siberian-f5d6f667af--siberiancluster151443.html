Siberian

noun
someone from Siberia
Pronunciation: / saɪˈbɪriən / (AmE), / saɪˈbɪəriən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Siberia.

Examples

A Siberian traveler

Many Siberians are adapted to extremely cold climates.
