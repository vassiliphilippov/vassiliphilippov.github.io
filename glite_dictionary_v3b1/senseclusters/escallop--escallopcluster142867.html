escallop

noun
edible muscle of a fan-shaped shell mollusk
Pronunciation: / ɪˈskæləp /; / ɛˈskæl.əp / (AmE), / ɪˈskæl.əp / (BrE)
an edible marine mollusk with a fan-shaped shell, or its muscle used as food
