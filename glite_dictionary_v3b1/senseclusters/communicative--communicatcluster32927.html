communicative

adjective
relating to communication
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunɪˌkeɪtɪv / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪkətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or involving the act of communication; concerned with exchanging information, ideas, or feelings

Examples

Communicative skills
Her job requires strong communicative skills and collaboration with others.
The communicative arts include writing, speaking, and media studies.
The teacher uses communicative methods to encourage student participation.
