sinlessness

noun
the state of being free from sin
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪn.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪn.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
the state of being free from sin or moral wrongdoing; innocence or purity of character

Examples

Childlike sinlessness
Her childlike sinlessness made her beloved by all who met her.
Many religions regard sinlessness as an ideal state to be aspired to.
The legend speaks of the hero's sinlessness and unwavering virtue throughout his life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sinfulness - the quality or state of being sinful; guilty of sin
