The perpendicular distance from the base of a geometric figure to the opposite vertex or side.

Examples

Triangle altitude
In a triangle, the altitude is drawn from one vertex perpendicular to the opposite side.
The longest altitude of a right triangle meets the hypotenuse at a right angle.
To find the area of a parallelogram, multiply the base by its altitude.
