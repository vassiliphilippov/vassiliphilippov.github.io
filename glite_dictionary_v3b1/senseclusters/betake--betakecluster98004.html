betake

verb
to turn to for help
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪˈteɪk / (AmE), / bɪˈteɪk / (BrE)
to go to or turn to someone or something, especially for help, comfort, or a course of action.

Examples

To betake oneself to prayer
In times of trouble, people often betake themselves to prayer or meditation.

When the storm hit, the villagers betook themselves to the safety of the church.
