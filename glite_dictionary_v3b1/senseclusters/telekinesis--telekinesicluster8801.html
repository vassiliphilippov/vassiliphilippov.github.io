telekinesis

noun
supposed power to move objects mentally
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛlɪkɪˈnisɪs / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪkɪˈniːsɪs / (BrE)
the supposed ability to move objects at a distance by mental power without physical interaction

Examples

Telekinesis power
Science fiction often features telekinesis as a rare and powerful skill.
Some claim to have experienced telekinesis, but there is no scientific evidence for it.
The character in the novel possesses telekinesis, allowing her to move items without touching them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychokinesis - supposed ability to move or influence objects with the mind
