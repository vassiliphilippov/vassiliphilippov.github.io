pet

adjective
especially favored or preferred
Pronunciation: / pɛt / (AmE), / pɛt / (BrE)
(of a project, subject, etc.) particularly favored or preferred above others and given special attention or partiality.

Examples

Pet project
His pet theory about the origins of life was not widely accepted by the scientific community.
She assigned her pet student all the best roles in the school play.
The CEO funded his pet project despite objections from the board.

Related Terms

  • antonym: side - (as in 'side project'; a secondary or less important interest)
