devotionally

adverb
in a religious way
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvoʊʃənəli / (AmE), / dɪˈvəʊʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows deep religious devotion, prayer, or spiritual commitment

Examples

To sing devotionally
She sang the hymn devotionally, her eyes closed in deep concentration.
The book is written devotionally, encouraging readers to reflect on their faith.
He approaches his daily meditation devotionally, setting aside time for quiet prayer.
He prayed devotionally every morning before starting his day.
The choir sang the anthem devotionally, moving the entire congregation.
She read the scriptures devotionally, reflecting on their meaning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: casually - in a relaxed or unconcerned manner
  • synonym: reverently - in a deeply respectful or worshipful manner
  • antonym: secularly - in a non-religious or worldly manner
