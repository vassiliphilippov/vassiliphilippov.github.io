walk

noun
path for walking, esp. near a building
Pronunciation: / wɑk / (AmE), / wɔːk / (BrE)
a path or passage designed for people to walk on, often in a garden or near a building

Examples

Garden walk
She slipped on the icy walk in front of her house.
The garden walk winds between rows of flowers and shrubs.
Workers cleared the walk of leaves every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sidewalk - a paved path for pedestrians at the side of a road
