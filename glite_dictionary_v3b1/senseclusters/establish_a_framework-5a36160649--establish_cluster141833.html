establish a framework

verb
create a foundational structure or system
Pronunciation: / ɪˈstæblɪʃ ə ˈfreɪmˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ɪˈstæblɪʃ ə ˈfreɪmˌwɜːk / (BrE)
to create a basic structure or system that serves as a foundation for further development or organization

Examples

To establish a framework for rules
Before implementing new policies, the committee decided to establish a framework for decision-making.
It is important to establish a framework to ensure all project requirements are met efficiently.
The government worked to establish a framework for economic cooperation between the countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: establish a foundation - to create the base for further development
  • synonym: set up a framework - to organize or arrange a structure as a basis for something
