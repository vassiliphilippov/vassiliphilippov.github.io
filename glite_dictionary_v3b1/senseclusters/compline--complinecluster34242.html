compline

noun
last canonical hour of prayer
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmplaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɒmplɪn / (BrE)
the last of the seven canonical hours, said or sung before retiring for the night in certain Christian traditions

Examples
