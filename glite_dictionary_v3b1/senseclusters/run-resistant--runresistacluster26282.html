run-resistant

adjective
resistant to runs or ladders in hosiery
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌn rɪˌzɪstənt / (AmE), / ˈrʌn rɪˌzɪstənt / (BrE)
(of hosiery or fabric) designed or treated to prevent runs (US) or ladders (UK) from forming or spreading.

Examples

Run-resistant tights
I always buy run-resistant tights because they last much longer than regular ones.
She appreciated the run-resistant fabric when her cat tried to claw her pantyhose.
The new line of run-resistant stockings is very popular among office workers.
