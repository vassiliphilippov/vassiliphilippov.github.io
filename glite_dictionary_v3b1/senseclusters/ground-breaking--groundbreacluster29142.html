ground-breaking

adjective
Innovative or ceremonial start of construction
4.0 4.1
Pronunciation: /graʊnd ˈbreɪ.kɪŋ/ /; / /ˈgraʊnd.breɪ.kɪŋ/ (AmE), /graʊnd ˈbreɪ.kɪŋ/ /; / /ˈgraʊnd.breɪ.kɪŋ/ (BrE)
