unrealistic

adjective
not practical or sensible; not based on reality
2.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˌriːəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ʌnˌrɪəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
not having or showing a practical or sensible idea of what can be achieved or expected; not based on reality

Examples

An unrealistic idea
His movie plots are often criticized for being completely unrealistic.
It is unrealistic to expect a child to stay quiet for hours.
The company set unrealistic goals for the first year of operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: practical - concerned with actual use or practice; sensible
  • antonym: realistic - having or showing a sensible and practical idea of what can be achieved or expected
