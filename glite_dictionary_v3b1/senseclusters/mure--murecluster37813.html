mure

noun
old word for wall
Pronunciation: / mjʊr / (AmE), / mjʊə / (BrE)
an old or literary word for a wall, especially one that encloses or surrounds an area

Examples

Stone mure
The ancient mure surrounded the ruined castle.
Legends say the city was protected by a mighty mure built centuries ago.
He leaned against the cold stone mure and gazed at the stars above.
