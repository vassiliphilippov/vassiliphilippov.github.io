acinous

adjective
relating to small sacs in glands
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæs.ə.nəs / (AmE), / ˈæs.ɪ.nəs / (BrE)
relating to or resembling small sacs, especially referring to the acini of compound glands.

Examples

Acinous glands
The pancreas is made up of many acinous structures responsible for enzyme secretion.
This type of tumor typically arises from acinous cells in salivary glands.
Under the microscope, the acinous glands showed signs of inflammation.

  • synonym: acinar - relating to or composed of acini
