safety

noun
device to prevent accidents or injuries
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti / (BrE)
a device or mechanism designed to prevent injury or accidents, especially by controlling or limiting the operation of equipment

Examples

Safety switch
Always check that the safety is engaged before storing power tools.
Most modern cars include a variety of safeties to protect passengers in the event of an accident.
The safety on the handgun must be switched off before firing.
