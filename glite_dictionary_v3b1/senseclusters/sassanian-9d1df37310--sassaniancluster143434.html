Sassanian

noun
member of Sassanid Empire
9.9
Pronunciation: / səˈseɪniən / (AmE), / səˈseɪniən / (BrE)
a person who lived in or belonged to the Sassanid Empire, especially a member of its ruling dynasty in ancient Persia

Examples

A Sassanian ruler
The Sassanian was known for his skills in both administration and warfare.
Artifacts from the Sassanian period reveal a rich artistic tradition.
A Sassanian general led the army north during the sixth-century campaign against the Romans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Persian - a person from ancient Persia
  • antonym: Roman - a person from ancient Rome
