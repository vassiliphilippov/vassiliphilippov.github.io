wiggle nail

noun
corrugated steel fastener for wood joints
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪɡəl neɪl / (AmE), / ˈwɪɡəl neɪl / (BrE)
a small strip of corrugated steel with sharp points on one side, used in rough carpentry to join pieces of wood by hammering it across joints

Examples

A wiggle nail strip
He secured the wooden frame with a wiggle nail for extra support.
The carpenter hammered a wiggle nail across the joint to fasten the boards together.
We used several wiggle nails to quickly assemble the crates.
