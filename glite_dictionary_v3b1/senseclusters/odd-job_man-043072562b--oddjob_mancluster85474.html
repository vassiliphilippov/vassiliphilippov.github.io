odd-job man

noun
man doing small jobs or repairs
5.6
Pronunciation: / ɑd dʒɑb mæn / (AmE), / ɒd dʒɒb mæn / (BrE)
a man who is employed to do various small jobs or minor repairs, often requiring a range of basic skills

Examples

An odd-job man tends to have a wide set of practical skills.

We hired an odd-job man to paint the fence and fix the gate.
