unoperated

adjective
not surgically treated
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɑːpəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɒpəˌreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having undergone a surgical operation or not acted upon by an operation

Examples

An unoperated tumor
The doctor recommended surgery for the unoperated tumor.
Her unoperated shoulder continued to limit her movement.
Many patients with unoperated conditions require careful monitoring.

Related Terms

  • antonym: operated - having undergone a surgical operation
  • synonym: untreated - not having received medical treatment
