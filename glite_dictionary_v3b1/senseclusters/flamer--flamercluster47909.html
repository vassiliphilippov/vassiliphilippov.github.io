flamer

noun
flame-producing tool
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfleɪ.mər/ (AmE), /ˈfleɪ.mə/ (BrE)
a tool or weapon that produces a controlled, directed flame, such as a blowtorch or flamethrower

Examples

Industrial flamer
The technician used a flamer to quickly remove the old paint from the metal surface.
Firefighters sometimes train with a flamer to understand how flames behave in different conditions.
The welder picked up his flamer to heat the pipes before bending them into shape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blowtorch - a tool that produces a hot flame for heating or welding
  • synonym: flamethrower - a weapon that projects a stream of burning fuel
