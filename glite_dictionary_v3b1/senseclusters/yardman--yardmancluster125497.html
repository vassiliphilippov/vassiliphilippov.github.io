yardman

noun
man hired for outdoor or yard work
4.9 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈjɑːrd.mən/ (AmE), /ˈjɑːd.mən/ (BrE)
a man employed to do outdoor work such as mowing lawns or maintaining grounds

Examples

Garden yardman
As a yardman, he was responsible for raking leaves and clearing snow from the walkways.
The yardman arrived early in the morning to cut the grass and trim the hedges.
We hired a yardman to help maintain the gardens during the summer.
