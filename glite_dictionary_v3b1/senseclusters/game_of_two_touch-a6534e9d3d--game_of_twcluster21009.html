game of two touch

noun
a variant of touch football with two-touch rule
7.1
Pronunciation: / ɡeɪm əv tu tʌtʃ / (AmE), / ɡeɪm əv tuː tʌtʃ / (BrE)
a casual form of touch football in which a player is considered tackled after being touched twice by an opponent

Examples

A game of two touch football
A quick game of two touch is perfect for large groups who want to play football with minimal contact.
During summer camp, the kids played a game of two touch instead of the standard football game.
On sunny afternoons, we often organize a game of two touch at the local field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: touch football - a game in which players are 'tackled' by being touched rather than physically brought to the ground
