pampa

noun
South American grassland
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpæmpə / (AmE), / ˈpæmpə / (BrE)
a large, flat, grassy plain in South America, especially in Argentina and surrounding countries

Examples

The pampa grassland
Gauchos ride horses across the vast pampa in Argentina.
The pampa is known for its fertile soil and cattle ranching.
Many wildflowers bloom each spring on the open pampa.

Related Terms

  • synonym: steppe - a large area of flat unforested grassland, especially in Eurasia
