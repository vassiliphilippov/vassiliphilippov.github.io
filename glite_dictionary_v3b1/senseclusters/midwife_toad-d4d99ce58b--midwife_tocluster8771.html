midwife toad

noun
European toad with unique egg-carrying behavior
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪd.waɪf toʊd /; / ˈmɪdˌwaɪf ˌtoʊd / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.waɪf təʊd /; / ˈmɪdˌwaɪf ˌtəʊd / (BrE)
a small European toad (genus Alytes) whose male carries fertilized eggs on its body until they hatch

Examples

Unlike most toads, the midwife toad does not lay its eggs directly in water.
