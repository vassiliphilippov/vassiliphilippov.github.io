bougie

adjective
slang for pretentious
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbuː.ʒiː/ /; / /ˈbuː.dʒiː/ (AmE), /ˈbuː.ʒiː/ /; / /ˈbuː.dʒiː/ (BrE)
slang for behaving in a way that is seen as pretentious or associated with wealth and upper-class status

Examples

A bougie dinner
He ordered the most expensive wine just to seem bougie in front of his friends.
Some people think using designer brands is a bit bougie for our small town.
Her bougie taste in restaurants surprised everyone at the office.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humble - not proud or not acting as if one is better than others
  • synonym: pretentious - trying to appear more important or wealthy than one is
  • synonym: snobbish - showing the attitude of people who think they are better than others
