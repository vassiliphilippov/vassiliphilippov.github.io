bougie

adjective
slang for pretentious
9.3 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbuÀê íi / (AmE), / ÀàbuÀê íi / (BrE)
slang for behaving in a way that is seen as pretentious or associated with wealth and upper-class status

Examples

A bougie dinner

Her bougie taste in restaurants surprised everyone at the office.
