bisque

noun
creamy shellfish soup
Pronunciation: / bɪsk / (AmE), / bɪsk / (BrE)
a rich, creamy soup, usually made with shellfish and sometimes vegetables, blended until smooth

Examples

A bisque soup
Crab bisque is a favorite starter at many French bistros.
She served a creamy shrimp bisque as the first course at dinner.
The restaurant’s lobster bisque was rich and full of flavor.
The restaurant's lobster bisque was smooth, flavorful, and served with a slice of fresh bread.
She learned to make a delicious shrimp bisque from her grandmother's recipe.
Many people enjoy a bowl of hot bisque on a cold winter evening.
