range hood

noun
exhaust device above a kitchen stove
8.3 3.0
Pronunciation: /reɪndʒ hʊd/ (AmE), /reɪndʒ hʊd/ (BrE)
a device fitted above a kitchen stove to remove smoke, steam, and cooking odors by exhausting air outdoors or filtering it
