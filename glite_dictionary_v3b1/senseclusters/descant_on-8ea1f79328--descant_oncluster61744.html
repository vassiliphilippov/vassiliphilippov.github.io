descant on

verb
sing or play a descant over a melody
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛs.kænt ɑn / (AmE), / ˈdɛs.kænt ɒn / (BrE)
to sing or play a descant as a counter-melody to a main tune or melody

Examples

To descant on a song
Several sopranos enjoyed descanting on the traditional carol, bringing extra brightness to the music.
She learned how to descant on the melody, adding a beautiful counterpoint to the song.
The choir was asked to descant on the main hymn during the performance.
