geostrategist

noun
global strategy expert
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːoʊˈstrætədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəʊˈstrætədʒɪst / (BrE)

As a geostrategist, she studies how geography influences international relations.
The conference featured a panel of geostrategists discussing global security challenges.
