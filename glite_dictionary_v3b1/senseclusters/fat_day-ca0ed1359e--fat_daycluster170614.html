fat day

noun
a day of feeling overweight or overeating
Pronunciation: / fæt deɪ / (AmE), / fæt deɪ / (BrE)
An informal term for a day when one feels overweight or indulges in excessive eating.

Examples

A fat day eating
After eating an entire pizza by herself, she declared it a fat day.
He laughed about his fat day after realizing he'd finished a box of donuts.
Whenever I have a fat day, I try to go for a long walk the next morning.
