Examples

Synonyms

  • urinary system
    3.8 0.03/m
    urinary system is the common medical name for the organs that make, store, and remove urine, while urinary tract often refers to the tubes.
  • urinary apparatus
    6.3 0.00/m
    urinary apparatus is a technical term that sometimes includes nearby reproductive organs, while urinary tract usually means the urine-carrying tubes and bladder.
