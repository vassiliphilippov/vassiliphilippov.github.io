economic process

noun
process in production or management of wealth
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkəˈnɑmɪk ˈproʊsɛs / (AmE), / ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪk ˈprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
a process that involves the production, development, or management of material wealth within an economy

Examples

The economic process of growth
Inflation can disrupt the normal economic process by affecting both supply and demand.
The industrial revolution marked a dramatic shift in the economic process of many countries.
Understanding the economic process is essential for policy makers seeking to stimulate growth.
