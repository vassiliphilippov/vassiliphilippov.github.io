in the flesh

adverb
in person; physically present
5.1 3.4
Pronunciation: /ɪn ðə fleʃ/ /; / /ɪn ðiː fleʃ/ /; / /ən ðə fleʃ/ (AmE), /ɪn ðə fleʃ/ /; / /ɪn ðiː fleʃ/ /; / /ən ðə fleʃ/ (BrE)
