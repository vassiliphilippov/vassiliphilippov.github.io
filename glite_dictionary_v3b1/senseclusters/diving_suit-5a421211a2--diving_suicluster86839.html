diving suit

noun
airtight garment worn by underwater divers
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪvɪŋ suːt / (AmE), / ˈdaɪvɪŋ sjuːt / (BrE)
A garment that is weighted and airtight, supplied with air, and worn by divers for underwater activities.

Examples

A diving suit set
Before entering the water, he checked that his diving suit was completely sealed.
She wore a diving suit to explore the shipwreck at the bottom of the sea.
The old-fashioned diving suit had a large metal helmet and was connected to the surface by an air hose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spacesuit - an airtight suit worn by astronauts, analogous to a diving suit but for space
  • degree lower: wetsuit - a close-fitting suit made of neoprene, worn by swimmers and divers, offering thermal insulation but not airtight
