greater scaup

noun
a large North American duck species
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪtər ˈskɑːp / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtə ˈskɔːp / (BrE)
a large North American diving duck, the male of which has a greenish iridescent head

Examples

Male greater scaup
Male greater scaup can be identified by the greenish gloss on their heads.
The greater scaup is often seen in large flocks on coastal bays during winter.
Unlike the lesser scaup, the greater scaup prefers larger bodies of open water.
