TSS

noun
toxic shock syndrome; acute bacterial infection
9.0 0.14/m
Pronunciation: / ti …õs …õs / (AmE), / tiÀê …õs …õs / (BrE)
a serious and sometimes fatal acute condition caused by bacterial infection (often Staphylococcus), characterized by high fever, rash, low blood pressure, and organ failure, commonly associated with tampon use or surgical wounds
