lick

noun
a stroke or touch with the tongue
Pronunciation: / lɪk / (AmE), / lɪk / (BrE)
an act of touching or passing over something with the tongue

Examples

A quick lick
After a quick lick with his tongue, the cat resumed grooming its fur.
She took a lick of ice cream from the cone on a hot summer day.
The puppy gave my hand a gentle lick before curling up to sleep.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lap - an act of lapping with the tongue, especially by an animal
