purchaser

noun
person who buys something
2.8 3.38/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ùÀê.t É…ô.s…ö / (AmE), / Ààp…úÀê.t É…ô.s…ô / (BrE)
a person who buys something, especially goods or property

Examples

The purchaser pays
Please provide a receipt for the purchaser at the checkout counter.
The purchaser is responsible for paying any additional taxes on the property.
The purchaser of this artwork wishes to remain anonymous.

Synonyms

  • buyer
    1.0 28/m
    buyer is a common word for someone who pays for goods or services, while purchaser sounds more formal and legal.
  • vendee
    9.5 0.08/m
    vendee is a legal term for a party who buys in a contract, while purchaser is a broader, everyday buyer term.
  • emptor
    9.8 0.30/m
    emptor is a formal commercial or legal name for a buyer, while purchaser is more standard English used for property or goods.
  • customer
    1.0 133/m
    customer is someone who regularly buys from a shop or company, while purchaser often refers to a one-time buyer of goods or property.
  • acquirer
    5.9 0.13/m
    acquirer is a person or company that gains ownership, often by investment or takeover, while purchaser usually means someone who buys goods.
