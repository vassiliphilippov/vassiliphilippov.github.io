giant kangaroo

noun
large Australian kangaroo species
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt kænˈɡuːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt kæŋˈɡuːrəʊ / (BrE)
A very large greyish-brown species of Australian kangaroo, formerly numerous in open wooded regions.

Examples

The giant kangaroo species
Researchers studied fossil remains of the extinct giant kangaroo to learn about Australia's prehistory.
The giant kangaroo once roamed vast areas of Australia in great numbers.
Unlike smaller species, the giant kangaroo could reach heights over two meters.
