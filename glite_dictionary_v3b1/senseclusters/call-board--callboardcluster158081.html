call-board

noun
backstage theater bulletin board
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔl bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːl bɔːd / (BrE)
A bulletin board located backstage in a theater, used for posting notices, schedules, and announcements for the cast and crew.

Examples

The call-board backstage
Every morning, the stage manager updates the call-board with the day's schedule.
Notices about the new cast meeting were posted on the call-board.
The actor checked the call-board for updates on rehearsal times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bulletin board - a board for posting public notices
