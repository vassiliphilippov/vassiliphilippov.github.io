avariciousness

noun
extreme greed for wealth
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌævəˈrɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / ˌævərˈɪʃəsnəs / (BrE)
extreme greed for wealth or material gain; the quality of being excessively desirous of accumulating riches.

Examples

Extreme avariciousness
His avariciousness led him to exploit his workers for personal gain.
She was known for her avariciousness and relentless pursuit of luxury.
The novel explores the avariciousness of society's elite during the economic boom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: greed - intense and selfish desire for something, especially wealth or power
