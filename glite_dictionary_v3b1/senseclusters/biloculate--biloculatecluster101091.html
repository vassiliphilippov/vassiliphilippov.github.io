biloculate

adjective
having two cells or chambers
9.3
Pronunciation: / baɪˈlɑkjələt / (AmE), / baɪˈlɒkjʊleɪt / (BrE)
divided into or containing two cells or chambers, especially used in biology and medicine.

Examples

A biloculate capsule
A biloculate capsule was observed during the plant’s dissection.
The cyst appeared biloculate on the MRI scan, with each chamber separated by a thin wall.
The fruit is biloculate, containing two separate seed chambers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: multilocular - having many compartments or chambers
  • antonym: unilocular - having only one compartment or chamber
