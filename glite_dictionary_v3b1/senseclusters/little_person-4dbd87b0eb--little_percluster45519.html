little person

noun
a person of unusually short stature (esp. with dwarfism)
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬l ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
A person of unusually short stature, especially someone with dwarfism; sometimes used figuratively to refer to a marginalized or powerless individual.
