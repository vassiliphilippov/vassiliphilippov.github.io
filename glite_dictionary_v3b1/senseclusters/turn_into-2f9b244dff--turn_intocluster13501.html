turn into

verb
become or transform into something else
Pronunciation: / tɝn ˈɪntu / (AmE), / tɜːn ˈɪntuː /; / tɜːn ˈɪntu / (BrE)
to become something different, or to cause something or someone to become different; to transform or change into

Examples

To turn into a business
After years of hard work, her small shop finally turned into a thriving business.
If you leave milk out in the sun, it will turn into cheese.
The disagreement quickly turned into a heated argument.
Over the years, the small town has turned into a busy city.
The caterpillar will turn into a butterfly after a few weeks.
What started as a hobby is now turning into a successful business.

Related Terms

  • synonym: become - to start to be; to begin to be
  • synonym: change - to make or become different
  • antonym: remain - to continue to be the same
  • synonym: transform - to change completely the appearance or character of something or someone, especially so that that thing or person is improved
