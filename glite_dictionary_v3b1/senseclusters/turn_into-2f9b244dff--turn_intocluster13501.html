Examples

After years of hard work, her small shop finally turned into a thriving business.
If you leave milk out in the sun, it will turn into cheese.

Synonyms

  • change
    1.0 509/m
    change means becoming or making something different in many situations, while turn into often shows a clear new form or state.
  • become
    1.0 860/m
    become starts to be a new state or role over time, while turn into can show both starting and causing that same change.
  • transform
    3.4 48/m
    transform means changing something strongly in form or character, while turn into is more general and can be a small or large change.
  • convert
    3.7 5.64/m
    convert means change something for a new use, system, or belief often in formal contexts, while turn into is more everyday and general.

Related Terms

  • antonym: remain - to continue to be the same
