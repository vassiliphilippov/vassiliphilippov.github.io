freebooting

noun
unauthorized video sharing
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːˌbuːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌbuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of copying and sharing online videos or content without permission, often for personal gain or profit

Examples

Online freebooting
The platform updated its policies to combat freebooting and protect original content creators.
Many creators lose revenue because of widespread freebooting on social media sites.
He was accused of freebooting after uploading someone else's video without permission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: copyright infringement - the violation of the rights given to the creator of original work
  • synonym: piracy - the unauthorized use or reproduction of someone else's work
  • degree lower: sharing - the act of distributing something to others
