freebooting

noun
unauthorized video sharing
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfriː.buː.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈfriː.bə.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfriː.buː.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈfriː.bə.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
