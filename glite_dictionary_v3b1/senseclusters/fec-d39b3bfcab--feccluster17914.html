FEC

proper noun
Florida railway company
Pronunciation: / ɛf i si / (AmE), / ɛf iː siː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for Florida East Coast Railway, a railroad company operating along the east coast of Florida

Examples

FEC railway company
The FEC has played a major role in the development of Florida's east coast.
Freight trains operated by the FEC deliver goods to cities along the Atlantic shoreline.
Many communities grew rapidly after the FEC extended its railway line southward.
