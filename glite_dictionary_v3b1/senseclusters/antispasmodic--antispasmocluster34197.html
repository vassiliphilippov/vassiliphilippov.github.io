antispasmodic

noun
drug to relieve muscle spasms
3.2 6.0
Pronunciation: /æn.tɪ.spæz.ˈmɑː.dɪk/ /; / /æn.tə.spæz.ˈmɑː.dɪk/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.spæz.ˈmɒd.ɪk/ /; / /æn.tə.spæz.ˈmɒd.ɪk/ (BrE)
