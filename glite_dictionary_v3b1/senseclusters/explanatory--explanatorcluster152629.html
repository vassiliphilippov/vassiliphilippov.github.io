Synonyms

  • explicatory
    7.1 0.01/m
    explicatory explains the meaning clearly, often in notes or commentary, while explanatory is a general term used to make things clear.
  • elucidative
    7.5 0.00/m
    elucidative sheds light on difficult points and makes them easier to follow, while explanatory simply aims to make things clear.
  • expository
    7.0 0.60/m
    expository presents information or ideas in a clear, organized way, while explanatory focuses mainly on explaining a single point.
  • expositive
    8.1 0.00/m
    expositive sets out facts or ideas to explain something formally, while explanatory is more general and often less formal.
  • expositional
    6.9 0.06/m
