unstruck

adjective
not stamped or minted
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstrʌk / (AmE), / ʌnˈstrʌk / (BrE)
not stamped, pressed, or impressed by a die, especially referring to coin blanks or medals that have not been minted.

Examples

Unstruck coin
The collector was excited to find an unstruck coin blank in the old mint.
An unstruck medal is rare because most are pressed soon after production.
The display included several unstruck planchets alongside finished coins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blank - a coin or medal before it is stamped or impressed
  • antonym: minted - stamped or produced as a coin or medal
