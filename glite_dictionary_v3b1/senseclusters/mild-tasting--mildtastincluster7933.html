mild-tasting

adjective
having a gentle, not strong flavor
Pronunciation: / maɪld ˈteɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / maɪld ˈteɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
Having a gentle or not strong flavor; not intense or spicy in taste.

Examples

Mild-tasting food
If you prefer mild-tasting foods, try the grilled chicken with steamed vegetables.
The soup is mild-tasting, so you might want to add some pepper for extra flavor.
This cheese is known for its mild-tasting and creamy texture, making it popular with children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bland - lacking strong flavor or character
  • antonym: spicy - having a strong, pungent flavor from spices
