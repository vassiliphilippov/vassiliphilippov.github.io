analogist

noun
person using analogies
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈnælədʒɪst / (AmE), / əˈnælədʒɪst / (BrE)

Examples

An analogist compares ideas

His reputation as an analogist made his lectures both engaging and accessible.
The analogist explained the theory by drawing parallels to everyday experiences.
