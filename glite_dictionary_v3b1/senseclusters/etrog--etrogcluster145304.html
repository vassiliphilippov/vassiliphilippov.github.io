etrog

noun
Jewish ritual citrus
Pronunciation: / ˈɛtrɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈɛtrɒɡ / (BrE)
a yellow citrus fruit used in Jewish religious rituals, especially during the festival of Sukkot

Examples

Sukkot etrog
Each family brought a perfect etrog to the synagogue for the Sukkot celebration.
The etrog is carefully inspected for blemishes before it is used in the ritual.
He explained the significance of the etrog to his children during the holiday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: citron - a large, yellow citrus fruit with thick rind
