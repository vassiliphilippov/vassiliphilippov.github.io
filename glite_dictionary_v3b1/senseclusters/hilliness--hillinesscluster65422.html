hilliness

noun
the state of being hilly
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪli.nəs / (AmE), / ˈhɪlɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having many hills or being hilly

Examples

The hilliness of land
The hilliness of the region affects local weather patterns and agriculture.
The hilliness of the terrain made cycling challenging for beginners.
Tourists were captivated by the hilliness of the English countryside.
