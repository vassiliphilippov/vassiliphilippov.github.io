narrow wale

noun
corduroy with narrow ribs
Pronunciation: / ˈnæroʊ weɪl / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ weɪl / (BrE)
Corduroy fabric characterized by closely spaced, narrow ribs.

Examples

Narrow wale corduroy fabric
Designers often prefer narrow wale for shirts and trousers due to its softer texture.
Her narrow wale pants are comfortable and stylish for both work and casual outings.
The jacket is made of narrow wale corduroy, giving it a sleek, refined look.
