grand tour

noun
historic European educational journey
3.2
Pronunciation: / ɡrænd tʊr / (AmE), / ɡrænd tɔː / (BrE)
an extended journey through Europe, especially undertaken by wealthy young Englishmen in the 18th century as part of their education and cultural enrichment

Examples

A grand tour of Europe
After finishing university, Henry embarked on his grand tour of the European continent.
Many famous artists and writers were inspired by experiences during their grand tour in the 1700s.
The tradition of the grand tour influenced the development of art collections and museums in England.
