million-dollar question

noun
a crucial or key question
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪljən ˈdɑlər ˈkwɛstʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪljən ˈdɒlə ˈkwɛstʃən / (BrE)
an important or decisive question whose answer is highly sought after or significant

Examples

The million-dollar question is
Finding a solution to climate change is the million-dollar question for scientists worldwide.
Her presentation started with the million-dollar question everyone wanted answered.
The million-dollar question is whether the company can turn a profit next year.
