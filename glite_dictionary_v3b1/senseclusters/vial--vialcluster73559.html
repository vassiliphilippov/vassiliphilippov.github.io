vial

noun
small container for liquid medicines
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪəl / (AmE), / ˈvaɪ.əl / (BrE)
a small container, usually made of glass, used to hold liquid medicines, especially for injections

Examples

A vial of medicine
He carefully labeled each vial before sending them to the laboratory.
Please dispose of the used vial in the designated sharps container.
The nurse filled the syringe from the vial of vaccine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ampoule - a small sealed vial used to contain and preserve a sample, often for injections
