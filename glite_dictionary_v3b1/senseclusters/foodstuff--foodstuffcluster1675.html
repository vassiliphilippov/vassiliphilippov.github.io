foodstuff

noun
substance used or prepared as food
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːd.stʌf /; / ˈfuːdˌstʌf / (AmE), / ˈfuːd.stʌf / (BrE)
a substance used as food, especially one of the basic ingredients in people's diet or a product sold or processed as food

Examples

Basic foodstuff
Dried fruits and nuts are important foodstuffs in Middle Eastern cuisine.
Many basic foodstuffs such as rice and wheat have become more expensive.
The government imposed strict controls on imported foodstuffs.
Rising prices of basic foodstuffs have put many families under financial strain.
Supermarkets offer a wide variety of organic foodstuffs.
The government imposed strict quality controls on imported foodstuffs.
