turn of the screw

noun
added complication increasing tension
8.5
Pronunciation: / tɝn əv ðə skru / (AmE), / tɜːn əv ðə skruː / (BrE)
an event or development that makes a stressful, difficult, or tense situation even more so

Examples

A turn of the screw event
Each revelation in the investigation seemed to be a new turn of the screw for the embattled politician.
The judge's harsh words were another turn of the screw for the anxious defendant.
When the company announced layoffs, it was a cruel turn of the screw for the workers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complication - a factor that makes a situation more difficult or confusing
  • synonym: twist - an unexpected development in a story or situation
