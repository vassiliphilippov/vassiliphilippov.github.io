podunk

noun
insignificant town
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊdʌŋk / (AmE), / ˈpəʊdʌŋk / (BrE)
a small, unimportant, or isolated town, often regarded as dull or insignificant

Examples

A quiet podunk
After moving from a podunk in the Midwest, she was amazed by the city's energy.
He always dreamed of leaving his podunk and seeing the world.
They joked that their college was in the middle of podunk nowhere.
