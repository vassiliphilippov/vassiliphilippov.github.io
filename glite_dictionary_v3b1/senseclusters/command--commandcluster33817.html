command

verb
deserve and obtain (respect, fee, etc.)
1.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈmænd / (AmE), / kəˈmɑːnd / (BrE)
to deserve and obtain something, such as respect, attention, or a price, especially because of merit or status

Examples

To command respect
His integrity and hard work command the admiration of everyone he meets.
The new product commands a premium price in the market.
With years of experience, she commands great respect among her colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deserve - to be worthy of or entitled to
