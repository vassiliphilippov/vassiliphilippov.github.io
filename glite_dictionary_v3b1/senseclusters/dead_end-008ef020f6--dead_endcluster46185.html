dead end

noun
road or passage closed at one end
4.6 2.3
Pronunciation: /ded end/ (AmE), /ded end/ (BrE)
A passage or road that is closed at one end and does not lead anywhere.

Examples

A dead end street
She parked her car at the dead end and walked the rest of the way.
The new development is being built at the far end of a dead end street.
They realized too late that the alley was a dead end and had to turn back.
