maniacally

adverb
in a wild or frenzied manner
Pronunciation: / məˈniː.ə.kli / (AmE), / məˈnɪ.ək.li / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely wild, frenzied, or exhibiting irrational enthusiasm or obsession.

Examples

Waves arms maniacally
He waved his arms maniacally to get everyone's attention.
She laughed maniacally at the villain's obvious mistake.
The scientist worked maniacally through the night to complete his invention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maniacal - showing extremely wild or violent behavior
