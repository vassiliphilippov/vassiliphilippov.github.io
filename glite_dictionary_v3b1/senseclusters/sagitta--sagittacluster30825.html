sagitta

noun
height of an arc
Pronunciation: / səˈdʒɪtə / (AmE), / səˈdʒɪtə / (BrE)
a line segment in geometry that joins the midpoint of an arc to the midpoint of its chord, representing the height of the arc

Examples

The sagitta length
To find the radius of a circle, you can use the length of the sagitta and the chord.
The geometry teacher explained how the sagitta determines the height of an arc.
In the diagram, the sagitta is drawn from the midpoint of the chord to the arc above.
