headlamp

noun
vehicle front light
6.4 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈhed.læmp/ (AmE), /ˈhed.læmp/ (BrE)
a powerful light with a reflector attached to the front of a vehicle, such as a car or locomotive, to illuminate the road ahead

Examples

Car headlamp
He turned on the headlamp to see through the foggy night.
Modern vehicles often use LED headlamps for better visibility.
One of the car's headlamps was broken after the accident.
