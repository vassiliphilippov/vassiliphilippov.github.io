G-series agent

noun
type of nerve agent used in chemical warfare
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒi ˌsɪriːz ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː ˌsɪəriːz ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
any of a group of highly toxic, non-persistent nerve agents, discovered and first synthesized before or during World War II, including agents such as tabun, sarin, and soman
