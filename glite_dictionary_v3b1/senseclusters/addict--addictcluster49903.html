addict

verb
cause to become dependent or hooked
Pronunciation: / əˈdɪkt / (AmE), / əˈdɪkt / (BrE)
to cause someone or oneself to become dependent on a substance or activity, especially a narcotic drug

Examples

To addict someone to drugs
He worried that video games might addict his younger brother.
Many painkillers can addict patients if not taken as prescribed.
She never imagined a simple prescription would addict her to opioids.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hook - to cause someone to become addicted or strongly attracted to something
