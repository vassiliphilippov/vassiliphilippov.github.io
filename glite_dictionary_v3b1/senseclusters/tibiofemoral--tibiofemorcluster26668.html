tibiofemoral

adjective
of tibia and femur
Pronunciation: / ˌtɪbioʊˈfɛmərəl / (AmE), / ˌtɪbiəʊˈfɛmərəl / (BrE)
relating to the tibia and femur, especially to the knee joint where these bones meet

Examples

Tibiofemoral joint
The tibiofemoral joint is the main hinge of the human knee.
Doctors measured the tibiofemoral angle to assess the patient's leg alignment.
Injuries to the tibiofemoral area can affect walking and running.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knee joint - the joint connecting the thigh and lower leg
