blindingly

adverb
extremely brightly
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈblaɪndɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈblaɪndɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely bright or intense, making it hard to see or bear

Examples

Blindingly bright light
The snow reflected the sun blindingly, making it hard to keep my eyes open.
She smiled blindingly as the cameras flashed all around her.
The stadium lights shone blindingly during the night game.
The headlights were blindingly bright as the car approached on the dark road.
She was blindingly angry after hearing the unfair decision.
The new paint was blindingly white, making the room look much larger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dazzlingly - in a way that is extremely bright or impressive
  • antonym: dimly - in a way that is not bright or clear
  • antonym: mildly - in a gentle or not intense way
