junkyard dog

noun
fiercely aggressive or defensive person
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋkˌjɑrd dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋkˌjɑːd dɒɡ / (BrE)
a person or team who is fiercely aggressive or defensive, especially when protecting their interests or territory

Examples

Like junkyard dogs
After the halftime speech, the team came out fighting like junkyard dogs and turned the game around.
Don't mess with her; she can be a real junkyard dog when it comes to her friends.
He gained a reputation as a junkyard dog for the way he protected his territory in the ring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fighter - a determined competitor or person who fights
  • antonym: pushover - a person who is easy to defeat or influence
