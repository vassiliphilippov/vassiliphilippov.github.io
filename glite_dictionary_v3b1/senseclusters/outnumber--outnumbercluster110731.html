outnumber

verb
be greater in number
3.4
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈnʌmbɚ / (AmE), / aʊtˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
to be greater in number than someone or something else

Examples

To outnumber the enemy
The defenders were outnumbered by the attacking force two to one.
Tourists often outnumber locals during the summer months.
Women outnumber men in many college courses nowadays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outweigh - to be heavier or more important than something else
