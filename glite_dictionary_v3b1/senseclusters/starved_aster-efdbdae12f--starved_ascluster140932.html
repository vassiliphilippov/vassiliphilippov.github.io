starved aster

noun
a North American species of aster
Pronunciation: / stɑrvd ˈæstər / (AmE), / stɑːvd ˈæstə / (BrE)
a North American plant of the daisy family (Symphyotrichum lateriflorum), characterized by its small white flower heads on slender stems

Examples

Starved aster plant
Gardeners may seek out starved aster for its delicate white blooms and adaptability to shade.
Native bees are often attracted to the small flowers of the starved aster in the fall.
The woodland was dotted with clusters of starved aster late in the autumn.
