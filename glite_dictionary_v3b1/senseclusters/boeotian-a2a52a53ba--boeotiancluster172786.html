Boeotian

adjective
dull or uncultured
Pronunciation: / biˈoʊʃən / (AmE), / biˈəʊʃən / (BrE)
describing a person as dull, unimaginative, or lacking in culture or refinement

Examples

Boeotian attitude
His Boeotian attitude made him uninterested in the art exhibition.
She dismissed the critics as Boeotian for not appreciating modern poetry.
Despite his Boeotian reputation, he surprised everyone with his insightful comments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sophisticated - having refined tastes and manners
  • synonym: uncultured - lacking education or refinement
