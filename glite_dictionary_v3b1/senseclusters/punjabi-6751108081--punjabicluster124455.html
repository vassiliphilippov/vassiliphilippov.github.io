Punjabi

noun
a person from Punjab (Pakistan or India)
Pronunciation: / pʌnˈdʒɑːbi / (AmE), / pʌnˈdʒɑːbi / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the Punjab region of Pakistan or India, or a member of the Punjabi people

Examples

A Punjabi person
Many Punjabis celebrate both religious and regional festivals with great enthusiasm.
My best friend is a Punjabi who grew up in Lahore.
The Punjabi was proud to share stories about his heritage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Sikh - a member of a religious community originating in the Punjab region
