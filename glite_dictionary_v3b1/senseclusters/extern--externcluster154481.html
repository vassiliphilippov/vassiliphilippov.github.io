extern

noun
nonresident hospital doctor/student
8.8 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…õkÀåst…ùn / (AmE), / Àà…õkÀåst…úÀên / (BrE)
A medical doctor or student who is attached to a hospital but does not reside there.

Examples

A medical extern
As an extern, he was responsible for observing surgeries and participating in rounds.
During her rotation as an extern, she gained valuable clinical experience.
The hospital welcomed several new externs this semester to assist with patient care.
