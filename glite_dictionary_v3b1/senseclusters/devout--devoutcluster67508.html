devout

adjective
deeply religious or pious
7.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaʊt / (AmE), / dɪˈvaʊt / (BrE)
having or showing deep religious feeling or commitment

Examples

A devout Christian
Although he was raised in a religious family, he became less devout as he grew older.
Her grandmother was a devout Catholic who never missed Sunday mass.
The devout members of the community gathered for the evening prayer service.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irreverent - not showing respect for religious practices or beliefs
  • synonym: pious - having or showing religious devotion
