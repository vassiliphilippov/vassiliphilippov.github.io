streeter

noun
local resident
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːtər / (AmE), / ˈstriːtə / (BrE)
a person who lives on or is closely associated with a particular street or neighborhood

Examples

Local streeter
The streeter on our block organizes the yearly street clean-up and barbecue.
As a longtime streeter, she knows everyone who lives in the neighborhood.
Each streeter contributed ideas for improving safety on their road.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neighbor - a person living near or next door to another
  • antonym: outsider - a person who does not belong to a particular group or place
