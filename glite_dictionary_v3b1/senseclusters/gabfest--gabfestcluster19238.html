gabfest

noun
lively informal conversation or gathering
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæbˌfɛst / (AmE), / ˈɡab.fɛst / (BrE)
an informal gathering for lively conversation or chat, especially among friends

Examples

A lively gabfest
After the conference, the lobby was filled with a lively gabfest among old friends.
The book club meeting turned into a gabfest that lasted well into the evening.
Their lunch breaks often become a gabfest about TV shows and family life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chitchat - casual small talk or light conversation
  • synonym: conversation - talking with others, exchange of ideas
  • antonym: silence - absence of sound or conversation
