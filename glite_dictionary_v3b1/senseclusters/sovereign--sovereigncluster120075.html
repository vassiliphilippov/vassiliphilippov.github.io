sovereign

adjective
supreme in status or authority
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːvərə(n) / (AmE), / ˈsɒvərɪn / (BrE)
having the highest status, authority, or power; supreme or paramount.

Examples

Sovereign authority
Her sovereign power over the organization was never questioned by her peers.
The committee made a sovereign decision that could not be appealed.
The court acts with sovereign authority in constitutional matters.
