dopehead

noun
slang drug user
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊpˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈdəʊpˌhɛd / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who habitually uses drugs, especially illegal ones

Examples

A dopehead on drugs
The film depicted a young dopehead trying to escape his troubled past.
She was worried her brother would become a dopehead if he kept hanging out with that crowd.
The neighborhood struggled with a rise in dopeheads and related crime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addict - a person who is dependent on a substance, especially drugs
  • synonym: junkie - a person addicted to drugs, especially heroin
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who never drinks alcohol or uses drugs
