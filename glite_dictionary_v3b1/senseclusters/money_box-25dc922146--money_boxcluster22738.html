money box

noun
container for storing coins or money
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌni bɑks / (AmE), / ˈmʌni bɒks / (BrE)
a container, usually with a slot in the top, used for storing coins or money

Examples

A money box for coins
As a child, I saved my allowance in a money box shaped like an elephant.
He shook the money box to see how many coins were inside before opening it.
They bought a new money box to encourage their daughter to save her pocket money.
After counting the coins in his money box, Tom had enough for a new toy.
Sarah dropped her spare change into the money box on her desk every day.
The charity placed a money box near the entrance for donations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: piggy bank - a small container, often shaped like a pig, for saving coins
  • degree higher: safe - a strong, lockable box for storing valuables
