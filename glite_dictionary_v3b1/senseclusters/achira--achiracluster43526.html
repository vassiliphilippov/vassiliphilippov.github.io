A tropical American plant (Canna edulis) grown for its edible rootstock, from which arrowroot starch is obtained.

Examples

Achira plant root
Achira is cultivated in South America for its starchy tubers used in cooking.
Farmers often harvest achira to process its root into arrowroot starch.
In Peru, achira leaves are also used to wrap traditional pastries.
