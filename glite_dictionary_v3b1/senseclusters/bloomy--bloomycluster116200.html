bloomy

adjective
full of flowers
3.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbluÀêmi / (AmE), / ÀàbluÀêmi / (BrE)
covered with or full of flowers; having many blooms.

Examples

Bloomy meadow
The hillside was especially bloomy in spring, with wildflowers stretching as far as the eye could see.
After weeks of rain, the park became bloomy and vibrant with color.
She painted a bloomy landscape filled with roses and sunflowers.
