carpentry

noun
the craft of making things from wood
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrpəntri / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpəntri / (BrE)
The skill or activity of making and repairing wooden objects and structures.

Examples

Wooden carpentry work
After the storm, we hired a professional for the carpentry repairs on our roof.
He learned carpentry from his father and built furniture by hand.
Her interest in carpentry led her to create beautiful wooden sculptures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: woodworking - the process of making things from wood
