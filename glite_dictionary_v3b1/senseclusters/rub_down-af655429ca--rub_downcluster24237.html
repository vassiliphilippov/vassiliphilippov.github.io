rub down

verb
wear away by rubbing
Pronunciation: / rʌb daʊn / (AmE), / rʌb daʊn / (BrE)
to wear away the surface of something by rubbing, typically to make it smooth

Examples

To rub down wood
She carefully rubbed down the sculpture to remove rough edges.
The old paint was rubbed down until the wall felt perfectly smooth.
Use fine sandpaper to rub down the wooden surface before painting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sand - to smooth or wear away with sandpaper or a similar abrasive
