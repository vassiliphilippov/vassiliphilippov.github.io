pad

noun
fleshy cushion on foot or finger
Pronunciation: / pæd / (AmE), / pad / (BrE)
the fleshy cushion-like part on the underside of the foot of an animal or on the tip of a human finger

Examples

Cat's pad
The cat silently walked across the room on its soft pads.
The dog injured the pad of its paw during the hike.
You can feel the pad of your finger when you press it against a surface.

Related Terms

  • antonym: callus - a thickened and hardened part of the skin, often on feet or hands
