Leninist

adjective
of Lenin or Leninism
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛnənɪst / (AmE), / ˈlɛnɪnɪst / (BrE)
relating to the ideas, policies, or political practices of Lenin or Leninism

Examples

Leninist ideology
The party adopted a strictly Leninist approach to organizing its members.
Her research focuses on the influence of Leninist thought in modern political movements.
Some critics argue that Leninist policies led to significant changes in twentieth-century history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Marxist - relating to the ideas or policies of Karl Marx or Marxism
  • antonym: anti-communist - opposed to communist ideology or policies
