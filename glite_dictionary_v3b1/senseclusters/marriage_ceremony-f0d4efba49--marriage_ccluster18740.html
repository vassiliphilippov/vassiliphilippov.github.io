marriage ceremony

noun
wedding ceremony; nuptial ritual
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmærɪdʒ ˌserəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / ˈmærɪdʒ ˈserɪməni / (BrE)
a formal event or ritual in which two people are united in marriage

Examples

A marriage ceremony
Many couples choose to personalize their marriage ceremony to reflect their cultures.
The marriage ceremony took place in a beautiful garden setting.
They invited their closest friends and family to the marriage ceremony.

Related Terms

  • antonym: divorce - the legal dissolution of a marriage
  • synonym: wedding - a marriage ceremony, especially considered as a social event
