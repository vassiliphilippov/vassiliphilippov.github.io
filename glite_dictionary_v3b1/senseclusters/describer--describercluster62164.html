describer

noun
person giving description
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskraɪbər / (AmE), / dɪˈskraɪbə / (BrE)
a person or thing that gives a description of someone or something

Examples

A describer of scenes
The describer used colorful language to help the audience imagine the landscape.
As a skilled describer, she made the characters come alive in her stories.
Every good tour needs a knowledgeable describer to explain the history of the place.
The travel writer is an excellent describer of exotic places and cultures.
As a describer, she paints vivid pictures with her words.
Every good scientist must also be a clear describer of their findings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: explainer - a person who makes something clear or easy to understand
  • synonym: narrator - a person who tells a story or describes events
