loftsman

noun
ship design layout worker
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔftsmən / (AmE), / ˈlɒftsmən / (BrE)
a person who lays out the lines and patterns for building ships, especially in a shipyard

Examples

The loftsman draws lines
The loftsman spent hours ensuring the ship's curves were perfectly drawn on the workshop floor.
Before construction began, the loftsman checked every measurement twice for accuracy.
A skilled loftsman is essential for building ships that are both strong and seaworthy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shipwright - a person who builds and repairs ships
