varyingly

adverb
in changing ways
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛriɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈveəriɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that changes or differs at different times or in different situations

Examples

Varyingly warm and cool
The weather in this region is varyingly warm and cool throughout the year.
Students responded varyingly to the new teaching methods introduced by the school.
The movie was varyingly received by critics, with some praising it and others criticizing it.
