prick

verb
(of ears) to stand up sharply
9.3
Pronunciation: / prɪk / (AmE), / prɪk / (BrE)
(of an animal's ears, etc.) to stand erect or move upwards sharply, usually in response to a sound or alertness.

Examples

A fox pricks its ears
The fox pricked its ears and listened intently for any sign of danger.
The horse's ears pricked as it heard the distant thunder.
Whenever she called, the dog's ears pricked up in excitement.
