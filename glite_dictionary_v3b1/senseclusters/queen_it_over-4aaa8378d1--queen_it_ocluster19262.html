queen it over

verb
act in a domineering way
Pronunciation: / kwiːn ɪt ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / kwiːn ɪt ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to act in an arrogant or domineering way toward others, as if presiding with superiority

Examples

To queen it over friends
Even after his promotion, he never queened it over his old friends.
She tried to queen it over her classmates by bragging about her achievements.
The manager would often queen it over her staff, making everyone uncomfortable.
