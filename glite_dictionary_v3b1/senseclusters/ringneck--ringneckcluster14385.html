ringneck

noun
small snake with neck ring
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋˌnɛk / (BrE)
a small, usually harmless snake with a pale ring around its neck, especially the species Diadophis punctatus

Examples

Ringneck snake
The children were excited to spot a ringneck slithering through the grass near the woods.
A harmless ringneck was hiding beneath the pile of leaves in our backyard.
We gently moved the ringneck to a safer place away from the walking path.

Related Terms

  • synonym: garter snake - a common, harmless North American snake
  • synonym: ring-necked snake - a small North American snake with a yellow or orange ring around its neck
