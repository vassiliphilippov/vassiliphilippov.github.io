hundred-and-fortieth

adjective
ordinal number: 140th
Pronunciation: / ˌhʌndrəd ænd ˈfɔrtiəθ / (AmE), / ˌhʌndrəd ən ˈfɔːtiəθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number corresponding to one hundred and forty, used to indicate position in a sequence.

Examples

This was the hundred-and-fortieth anniversary of the school's founding.
