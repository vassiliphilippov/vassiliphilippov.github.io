ragout

noun
well-seasoned meat and vegetable stew
6.0
Pronunciation: / ræˈɡu / (AmE), / ræˈɡuː / (BrE)
A highly seasoned stew of meat and vegetables, typically cooked slowly to blend the flavors.

Examples

A ragout stew

The chef prepared a delicious ragout with lamb, carrots, and aromatic herbs.
