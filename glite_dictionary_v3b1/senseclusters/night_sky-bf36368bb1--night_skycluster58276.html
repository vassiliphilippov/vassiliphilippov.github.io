night sky

noun
the sky at night with stars, moon, etc.
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪt skaɪ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt skaɪ / (BrE)
the sky as it appears during the night, often with visible stars, the moon, and other celestial bodies

Examples

The night sky stars
Astronomers often study the night sky to observe planets and distant galaxies.
The full moon shone brightly in the clear night sky.
We lay on the grass and stared at the night sky filled with countless stars.
