burnin'

verb
to be on fire
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːnɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɜːnɪn / (BrE)
informal or dialect spelling of 'burning', meaning to be on fire or undergoing combustion and producing flames or smoke

The house is burnin'
The old barn was burnin' when we arrived, flames already pouring from the roof.
He shouted that the toast was burnin' in the kitchen and rushed to turn off the stove.
We could smell something burnin' from the neighbor's backyard late last night.
