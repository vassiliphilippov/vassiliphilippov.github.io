hot rod

noun
car modified for speed
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑt ˌrɑd /; / ˈhɑt rɑd / (AmE), / ˈhɒt ˌrɒd /; / ˈhɒt rɒd / (BrE)
a car that has been specially modified for speed, performance, and often a unique style
