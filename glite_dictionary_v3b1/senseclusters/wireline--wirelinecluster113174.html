wireline

noun
oil well cable
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪərˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəlaɪn / (BrE)
a thin cable used to lower instruments or tools into an oil or gas well, or the work performed using this cable

Examples

Oil well wireline
The engineers used a wireline to lower sensors deep into the oil well.
Routine maintenance often involves running tools down the wireline to check for blockages.
Modern drilling operations rely on wireline technology for accurate measurements.
