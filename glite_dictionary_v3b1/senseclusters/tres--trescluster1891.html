tres

adverb
very (French-style)
Pronunciation: / treɪ / (AmE), / treɪ / (BrE)
used informally in English to mean 'very', imitating the French word 'très'.

Examples

Tres chic style
Her new apartment is tres modern and stylish.
He thought the restaurant was tres expensive for what it offered.
The party was tres lively, with music and dancing all night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: slightly - to a small degree; a little
  • synonym: very - to a high degree; extremely
