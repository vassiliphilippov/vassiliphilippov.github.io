dormered

adjective
with dormer windows
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔrmərd / (AmE), / ˈdɔːməd / (BrE)
having one or more dormer windows projecting from the roof

Examples

A dormered roof
The dormered cottage looked charming in the countryside.
They bought a dormered house to have more light in the upstairs rooms.
From the street, the dormered roof gave the building a classic appearance.
