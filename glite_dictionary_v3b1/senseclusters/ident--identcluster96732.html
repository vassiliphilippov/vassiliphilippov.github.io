ident

noun
station identification
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌdɛnt / (AmE), / ˈaɪdɛnt / (BrE)
a short recorded or live announcement, jingle, or visual used by a radio or television station to identify itself

Examples

TV ident jingle
The radio station played its ident before every news bulletin.
Viewers recognized the channel instantly by its colorful ident at the start of each program.
A catchy ident helps a TV station build its brand identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bumper - a short segment used to separate parts of a broadcast
  • synonym: jingle - a short catchy tune used in advertising or broadcasting
