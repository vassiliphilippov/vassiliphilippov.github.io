bra burner

noun
strenuous or sweat-inducing workout (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑː ˌbɝːnər / (AmE), / ˈbrɑː ˌbɜːnə / (BrE)
an informal term for a physically demanding workout or activity that induces significant sweating, especially one done by women

Examples

A bra burner workout
After hiking up the mountain, we all agreed we'd had a serious bra burner.
Her trainer promised the next session would be a real bra burner.
That spin class was such a bra burner—my shirt was soaked by the end.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exercise - physical activity to improve fitness
  • synonym: workout - a session of physical exercise
