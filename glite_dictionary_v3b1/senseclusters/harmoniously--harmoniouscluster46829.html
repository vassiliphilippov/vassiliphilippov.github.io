harmoniously

adverb
in a peaceful or agreeable way
4.1 0.34/m
Pronunciation: / h…ëÀêrÀàmo äni…ôsli / (AmE), / h…ëÀêÀàm…íni…ôsli / (BrE)
in a way that is peaceful, friendly, or in agreement, without conflict or discord

Examples

Live harmoniously together

The orchestra played so harmoniously that the performance mesmerized the audience.
The two departments worked harmoniously to complete the project on time.
