Darjeeling

noun
a distinctive black tea from northern India
5.1
Pronunciation: / dɑrˈdʒiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / dɑːˈdʒiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
a fine variety of black tea grown in the Darjeeling district of northern India, renowned for its distinctive flavor and aroma

Examples

A cup of Darjeeling tea
Many tea connoisseurs appreciate the delicate aroma of Darjeeling.
She brewed a pot of Darjeeling to enjoy with her afternoon snack.
Would you like some Darjeeling or do you prefer a different type of tea?

Related Terms

  • synonym: Assam - a variety of Indian black tea from Assam, noted for its bold flavor
  • synonym: Ceylon tea - a type of black tea from Sri Lanka with a distinctive taste
  • antonym: green tea - tea made from unfermented leaves, usually lighter than black tea
