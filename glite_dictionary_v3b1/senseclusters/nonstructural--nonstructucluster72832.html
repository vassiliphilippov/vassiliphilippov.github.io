nonstructural

adjective
not relating to structure
4.1 5.9
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈstrʌktʃərəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈstrʌktʃərəl / (BrE)
not relating to or affecting the structure of something, especially a building, system, or organism; not structural.

Examples

Nonstructural protein
Some proteins in the virus are classified as nonstructural because they do not form part of the main structure.
The cracks were determined to be nonstructural and did not affect the building's safety.
The renovations focused only on nonstructural changes like paint and new fixtures.
