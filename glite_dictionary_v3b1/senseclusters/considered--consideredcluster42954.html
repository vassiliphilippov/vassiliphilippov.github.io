considered

adjective
carefully thought-out
1.8 2.8
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈsɪd.ərd/ (AmE), /kən.ˈsɪd.əd/ (BrE)
(of a decision, opinion, or action) formed after careful thought; carefully weighed or deliberated.

Examples

A considered decision

It is important to make a considered choice when applying for jobs.
The committee released a considered statement after reviewing all the evidence.

Related Terms
