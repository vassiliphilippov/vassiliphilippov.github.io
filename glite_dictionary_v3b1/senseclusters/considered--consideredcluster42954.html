considered

adjective
carefully thought-out
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɪdɚd / (AmE), / kənˈsɪdəd / (BrE)
(of a decision, opinion, or action) formed after careful thought; carefully weighed or deliberated.

Examples

A considered decision
Her argument was not hasty, but a considered response to the complex issue.
It is important to make a considered choice when applying for jobs.
The committee released a considered statement after reviewing all the evidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deliberate - done consciously and intentionally; carefully thought out
  • antonym: impulsive - acting or done without forethought
