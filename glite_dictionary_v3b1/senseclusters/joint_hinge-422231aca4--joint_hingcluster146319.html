joint hinge

noun
hinge with two long straps for doors or lids
6.4
Pronunciation: / dʒɔɪnt hɪndʒ / (AmE), / dʒɔɪnt hɪndʒ / (BrE)
a hinge with two long straps, one attached to a moving part (such as a door or lid) and the other to a stationary frame

Examples

A joint hinge strap
After years of use, the old joint hinge on the lid had become rusty and stiff.
The carpenter chose a joint hinge to securely fasten the barn door to its frame.
To install the gate, align the joint hinge so that the straps are flush with both surfaces.
