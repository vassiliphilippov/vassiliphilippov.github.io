cautious expectation

noun
guarded hope or anticipation
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔʃəs ˌɛkspɛkˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɔːʃəs ˌɛkspɛkˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a feeling of hope or anticipation tempered by carefulness or hesitation about a possible outcome
