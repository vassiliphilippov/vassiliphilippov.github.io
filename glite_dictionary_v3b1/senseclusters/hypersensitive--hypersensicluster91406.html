hypersensitive

adjective
abnormally sensitive; easily affected, esp. to allergens
4.6 6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsətɪv / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəˈsɛnsɪtɪv / (BrE)
having an abnormal or excessive sensitivity or reaction to specific substances or stimuli, often resulting in an allergic response

Examples

Hypersensitive to pollen
After the rash appeared, he realized he was hypersensitive to that new laundry detergent.
She is hypersensitive to peanuts and must always carry an epinephrine injector.
Some children become hypersensitive to pollen during the spring months.
