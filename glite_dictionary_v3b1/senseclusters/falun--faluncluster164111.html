falun

noun
Falun Gong symbol
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːlʊn / (AmE), / ˈfɑːlʊn / (BrE)
a circular spiritual symbol, also known as a dharma wheel, used by the Falun Gong movement

Examples

Falun Gong falun
The golden falun is displayed prominently at Falun Gong gatherings.
She wore a pendant shaped like a falun as a sign of her beliefs.
The falun is often seen in artwork related to the Falun Gong movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dharma wheel - a symbolic representation of Buddhist teachings and the cycle of life
