continency

noun
self-restraint in sexual matters

Examples

Sexual continency

Synonyms

  • continence
    9.9 0.10/m
    continence is the standard modern form for self-control, while continency is an older, rarer variant meaning the same restraint.
  • sexual abstention
    7.1 0.00/m
    sexual abstention means deliberately not having sex for religious or personal reasons, while continency emphasizes self-control or moderation in sexual behaviour.
  • abstinence
    6.4 6.75/m
    abstinence is the practice of avoiding pleasures like alcohol or sex, while continency can also mean moderation and self-restraint.
  • chastity
    8.1 1.74/m
    chastity means not having sexual activity for moral or religious reasons, while continency focuses on self-restraint and moderation.
  • celibacy
    6.8 2.51/m
    celibacy is the state of not having sexual relations, often as a long-term vow, while continency means self-restraint or moderation.
