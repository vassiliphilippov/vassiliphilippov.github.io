cougar

noun
large American wild cat (mountain lion)
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːɡər / (AmE), / ˈkuːɡə / (BrE)
a large wild cat of North and South America, also called mountain lion, with a tawny coat and no spots or stripes

Examples

Wild cougar sighting
A cougar was spotted prowling near the outskirts of town yesterday.
Park rangers warned hikers to watch for signs of cougar activity along the trail.
The cougar is also known as the mountain lion or puma in different regions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: jaguar - a large spotted feline native to the Americas, related but distinct from the cougar
  • synonym: mountain lion - another name for the cougar, a large American wild cat
  • synonym: panther - another term sometimes used for the cougar in North America
