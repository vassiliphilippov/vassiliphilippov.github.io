pullover

noun
sweater put on over the head
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊlˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈpʊlˌəʊvə / (BrE)
A sweater or lightweight garment that is put on by pulling it over the head, rather than fastening it at the front.

Examples

A pullover sweater
He took off his pullover and hung it on the back of the chair.
In winter, I always wear a thick woolen pullover to stay warm.
She bought a green pullover for her hiking trip.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cardigan - a knitted sweater that opens at the front, usually with buttons
  • synonym: sweater - a knitted garment typically with long sleeves, worn on the upper body
