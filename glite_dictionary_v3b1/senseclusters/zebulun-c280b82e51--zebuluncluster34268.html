Zebulun

proper noun
biblical tribe and figure
Pronunciation: / ˈzɛbjələn / (AmE), / ˈzɛbjʊlən / (BrE)
a son of Jacob in the Bible and the ancestor of one of the twelve tribes of ancient Israel

Examples

Jacob's son Zebulun
In the Old Testament, Zebulun is listed as one of Jacob's twelve sons.
The territory of the tribe of Zebulun was located in northern ancient Israel.
Many biblical scholars study the history and significance of Zebulun and his descendants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Israel - the collective name for the descendants of Jacob
  • synonym: Judah - another son of Jacob and a tribe of ancient Israel
