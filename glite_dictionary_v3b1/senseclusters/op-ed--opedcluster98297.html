op-ed

adjective
opinion-based writing
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɑpˈɛd / (AmE), / ˌɒpˈɛd / (BrE)
describing a piece of writing or column that expresses an individual's opinion rather than the publication's official view

Examples

Op-ed column
The newspaper published an op-ed piece by a local activist about climate change.
She was invited to write an op-ed article on education reform for the Sunday edition.
Many readers responded to the op-ed column with letters to the editor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commentary - a written or spoken expression of opinions or explanations
  • antonym: editorial - an article expressing the official opinion of a publication
