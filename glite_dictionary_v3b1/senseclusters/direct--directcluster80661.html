direct

verb
to aim an activity or product at a specific group
2.6
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛkt, daɪˈrɛkt / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛkt, daɪˈrɛkt / (BrE)
to plan or aim a product, event, or activity specifically for a particular group of people

Examples

To direct a product
The company decided to direct its new product toward college students.
They are directing the event specifically at families with children.
This advertising campaign is directed at young professionals interested in technology.
