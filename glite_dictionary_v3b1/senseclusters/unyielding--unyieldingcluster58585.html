unyielding

adjective
firm; not giving way
6.8 5.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈjiːldɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈjiːldɪŋ / (BrE)
not willing to change, give up, or be influenced; firm or resolute, especially in the face of opposition or difficulty.

Examples

Unyielding attitude
She remained unyielding in her decision despite mounting pressure from her colleagues.
The mountain presented an unyielding barrier to early explorers.
With unyielding determination, he pursued his dream until he finally succeeded.
