ecophysiology

noun
organism-environment physiology
Pronunciation: / ˌiːkoʊˌfɪziˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌiːkəʊˌfɪziˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of how the physiological processes of organisms interact with and adapt to their environment

Examples

Ecophysiology research
Researchers in ecophysiology investigate how animals survive in extreme climates.
The field of ecophysiology helps explain how plants respond to drought and heat.
Advances in ecophysiology have improved our understanding of species adaptation to environmental change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecology - the study of relationships between organisms and their environment
  • synonym: environmental physiology - the study of how environmental factors affect physiological processes
  • synonym: physiology - the study of how living organisms function
