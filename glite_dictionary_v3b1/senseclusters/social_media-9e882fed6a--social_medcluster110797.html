social media

noun
online platforms for sharing and interaction
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊʃəl ˈmiːdiə /; / ˈsoʊʃəl ˈmiːdiə / (AmE), / ˌsəʊʃl ˈmiːdiə /; / ˈsəʊʃəl ˈmiːdiə / (BrE)
websites or applications that allow people to create and share content or interact with others online

Examples

Social media platforms
Many people get their information from social media rather than traditional news sources.

Many companies use social media to market their products and connect with customers online.
Teens today spend hours each day scrolling through social media feeds.
