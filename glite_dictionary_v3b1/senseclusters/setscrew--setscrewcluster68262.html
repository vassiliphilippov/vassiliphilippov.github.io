setscrew

noun
screw for securing parts together
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛtˌskru /; / ˈsɛt.skuː / (AmE), / ˈsɛtskruː /; / ˈsɛt.skruː / (BrE)
a screw, often without a head, used to secure or adjust the position of one object within or against another object
