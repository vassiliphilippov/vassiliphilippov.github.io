disinterest

noun
lack of interest
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɪntrəst / (AmE), / dɪsˈɪntrɪst / (BrE)
the state of not being interested or lacking concern in something

Examples

Disinterest in class
A sense of disinterest could be felt during the lengthy presentation.
His disinterest in politics is well known among his friends.
The manager's disinterest in the team's concerns led to low morale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indifference - lack of interest, concern, or sympathy
  • antonym: interest - the feeling of wanting to know or learn about something
