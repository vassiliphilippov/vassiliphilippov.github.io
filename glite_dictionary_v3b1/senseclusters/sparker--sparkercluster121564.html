sparker

noun
device producing sparks
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑrkər / (AmE), / ˈspɑːkə / (BrE)
a person or device that produces sparks, especially to ignite something

Examples

Engine sparker
The mechanic used a sparker to ignite the engine during the repair.
A simple sparker can light a gas stove safely and quickly.
He became known as the best sparker in the fireworks factory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: igniter - a device used to start a fire or engine
  • synonym: lighter - a small device for producing a flame
