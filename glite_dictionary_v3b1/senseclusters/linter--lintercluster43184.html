linter

noun
cottonseed fibers
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪn.tər/ (AmE), /ˈlɪn.tə/ (BrE)
the short, fine fibers that remain on cottonseed after the ginning process, often used to make cellulose or paper

Examples
