Examples

Glacier calving event
Scientists recorded increasing rates of calving along the glacier's seaward edge during summer melt.
Massive calving events can cause tsunamis when large icebergs fall into the ocean near shore.
Satellite images help researchers measure seasonal calving patterns and predict future sea-level contributions.
