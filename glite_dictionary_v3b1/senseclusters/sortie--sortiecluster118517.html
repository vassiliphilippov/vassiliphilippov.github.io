sortie

noun
military attack by troops leaving a defensive position
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːrti / (AmE), / ˈsɔːti / (BrE)
a sudden attack by troops coming out from a defensive position, especially from a besieged place

Examples

A sortie attack
After several days of siege, the garrison attempted a desperate sortie.
The general ordered a surprise sortie to disrupt the attackers' lines.
The troops launched a sortie against the enemy's position at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sally - a sudden charge out of a besieged place against the enemy
