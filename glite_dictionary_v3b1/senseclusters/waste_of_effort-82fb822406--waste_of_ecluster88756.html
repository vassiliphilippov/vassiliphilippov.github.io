waste of effort

noun
pointless or fruitless effort
Pronunciation: / weɪst əv ˈɛfɚt / (AmE), / weɪst əv ˈefət / (BrE)
an action or work that produces no useful result; pointless or fruitless effort

Examples

A waste of effort
Completing extra paperwork was a waste of effort since no one read it.
She realized her attempts to change his mind were a complete waste of effort.
Trying to fix the old laptop turned out to be a waste of effort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: futile - incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
  • synonym: waste of time - something that is not a worthwhile use of time
