High Commission

noun
embassy between Commonwealth countries
4.0
Pronunciation: / haɪ kəˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / haɪ kəˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
An official diplomatic mission representing one Commonwealth country in another.

Examples

The High Commission office
Delegates met at the Australian High Commission to discuss trade agreements.
She worked at the Indian High Commission during her stay in South Africa.
The Canadian High Commission in London provides services to Canadians living in the UK.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embassy - official residence or offices of an ambassador in a foreign country
