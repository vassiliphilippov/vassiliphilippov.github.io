negativist

noun
a person with a negative, defeatist attitude
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛɡətɪvɪst / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡətɪvɪst / (BrE)
a person who habitually sees only the negative side of things or is resigned to defeat without offering positive suggestions

Examples

A gloomy negativist
As a negativist, he seldom offered solutions and focused mostly on problems.
It can be difficult to work with a negativist who consistently dismisses new ideas.
The team's negativist attitude affected the morale of everyone involved.

Related Terms

  • antonym: optimist - someone who expects the best in all things
  • synonym: pessimist - someone who habitually expects the worst
