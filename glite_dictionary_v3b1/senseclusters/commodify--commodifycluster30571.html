commodify

verb
to make into a commodity
8.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɑːdəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / kəˈmɒdɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to turn something into a product or service that can be bought or sold, often removing its special qualities or meaning

Examples

There is concern that art galleries may commodify cultural artifacts by focusing only on their market value.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commercialize - to make something available for sale or profit
  • antonym: decommodify - to remove from the status of being a commodity
  • synonym: devalue - to reduce or remove the value or importance of something
  • synonym: monetize - to earn money from something
