heartstrings

noun
deepest emotions or feelings
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrtˌstrɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtˌstrɪŋz / (BrE)
used to refer to a person's deepest emotions, especially feelings of love or compassion.

Examples

Pull heartstrings
Charities often use powerful stories to tug at the public's heartstrings.
Seeing the lost puppy shivering in the rain touched her heartstrings.
The sad movie pulled at my heartstrings and brought tears to my eyes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compassion - sympathetic pity and concern for others
  • synonym: emotions - strong feelings such as love, anger, or joy
