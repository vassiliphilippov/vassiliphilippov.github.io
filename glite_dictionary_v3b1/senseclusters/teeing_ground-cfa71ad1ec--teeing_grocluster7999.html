teeing ground

noun
golf: starting area for each hole
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːɪŋ ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈtiːɪŋ ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
the area at the beginning of a hole in golf where play begins

Examples

The teeing ground
After shaking hands, they walked to the teeing ground to start the game.
The teeing ground was wet and muddy after last night’s rain.
You must tee off from within the boundaries of the teeing ground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tee - small stand used to support a golf ball for the first stroke of a hole
