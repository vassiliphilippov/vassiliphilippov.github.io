renewability

noun
ability to be renewed
Pronunciation: / rɪˌnuːəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / rɪˌnjuːəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being able to be renewed, extended, or continued, especially for contracts or agreements

Examples

Renewability of a contract
The renewability of your passport depends on the regulations of your country.
Many streaming services highlight the renewability of their monthly subscriptions.
Before signing the lease, check the renewability clause to avoid future issues.
