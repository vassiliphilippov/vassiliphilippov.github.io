pleasure house

noun
establishment for entertainment or vice
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛʒɚ haʊs / (AmE), / ˈplɛʒə haʊs / (BrE)
A place where people go for entertainment or to enjoy activities often considered immoral or adult, such as gambling or sexual services.

Examples

The pleasure house
He spent many evenings in the pleasure house of the city, mingling with guests.
Stories about the infamous pleasure house spread quickly through town.
The old building was once a notorious pleasure house offering music and gambling.
