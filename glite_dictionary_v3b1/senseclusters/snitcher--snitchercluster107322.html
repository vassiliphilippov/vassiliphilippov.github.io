snitcher

noun
non-violent thief
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈsnɪtʃə / (BrE)
a thief who steals without using violence

Examples

A street snitcher
Police suspected that the snitcher was responsible for the string of pickpocket incidents downtown.
The snitcher quietly slipped a wallet from the distracted shopper's bag.
Unlike a mugger, a snitcher avoids confrontation and relies on stealth.

  • synonym: thief - a person who steals
