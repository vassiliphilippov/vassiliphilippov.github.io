barococo flair

noun
style mixing baroque and rococo elements
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːrəˈkoʊkoʊ flɛr / (AmE), / ˌbærəˈkəʊkəʊ fleə / (BrE)
a style that combines elements of baroque and rococo design, often characterized by ornate, decorative, and elaborate details

Examples

Barococo flair style
She decorated her living room with a barococo flair, using gilded mirrors and pastel colors.
The designer's collection is famous for its barococo flair and extravagant ornamentation.
The new hotel's lobby features a barococo flair that immediately draws the eye.
