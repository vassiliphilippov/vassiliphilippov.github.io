sternward

adjective
toward the rear
8.4 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààst…úrnw…ôrd / (AmE), / Ààst…úÀênw…ôd / (BrE)
directed toward or located at the stern or rear of a ship or vehicle.

Examples

Sternward position
The captain ordered everyone to move to the sternward section of the deck.
A sternward glance revealed the approaching storm clouds behind the ship.
The cargo was stored in the sternward hold to balance the vessel.
