Limicolae

noun
group of migratory shorebirds (taxonomic term)
9.9
Pronunciation: / lɪˈmɪkəˌli / (AmE), / lɪˈmɪkəˌli / (BrE)
a former taxonomic group used for migratory shorebirds, especially those now placed in the Charadrii

Examples

The Limicolae group
Few contemporary ornithologists still use the term Limicolae in their research.
Modern classifications have replaced Limicolae with more precise groupings like Charadrii.
The Limicolae were once thought to comprise all common wading birds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Charadrii - modern taxonomic group for many shorebirds
