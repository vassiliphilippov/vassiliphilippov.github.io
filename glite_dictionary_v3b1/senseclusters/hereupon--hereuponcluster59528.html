hereupon

adverb
immediately after this
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪrəˈpɑn / (AmE), / ˌhɪərəˈpɒn / (BrE)
immediately after this; at this point as a consequence.

Examples

And hereupon said
Hereupon, the committee decided to end the meeting.
He disagreed with the proposal; hereupon a debate ensued.
The door was locked; hereupon they knocked loudly.
