goat

noun
domesticated animal with beard and horns
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊt / (AmE), / ɡəʊt / (BrE)
A hardy domesticated animal related to the sheep, typically having a beard and straight horns.

Examples

A mountain goat
A goat climbed effortlessly up the steep rocky hillside.
The farmer keeps a goat for its milk and meat.
They watched as the goat nibbled leaves from the bush.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sheep - a domesticated ruminant animal related to the goat, usually kept for its wool or meat
