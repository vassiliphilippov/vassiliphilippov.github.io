polyploid

adjective
having multiple sets of chromosomes
5.7 8.2

(of a cell or organism) having more than two complete sets of chromosomes.

Examples

Polyploid plants
Many cultivated plants, such as wheat, are polyploid and have numerous chromosome sets.
Researchers discovered a polyploid strain of fish that shows enhanced tolerance to environmental stress.
Some cancers involve cells becoming polyploid, which is linked to genetic instability.
