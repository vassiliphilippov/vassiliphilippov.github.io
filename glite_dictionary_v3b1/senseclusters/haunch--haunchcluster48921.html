haunch

noun
the loin and leg of a quadruped
Pronunciation: / hɑntʃ / (AmE), / hɔːntʃ / (BrE)
the hindquarter, especially the loin and upper leg, of a four-legged animal, often used as a cut of meat

Examples

A haunch of meat
A wolf crouched on its haunches, ready to spring at any moment.
The chef roasted a haunch of venison for the main course.
They carried the haunch of meat back to camp for dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thigh - the part of a person's leg between the hip and the knee, or similar part in animals
