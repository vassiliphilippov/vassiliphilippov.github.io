bolt the door

verb
secure a door with its bolt
Pronunciation: / boʊlt ðə dɔr / (AmE), / bəʊlt ðə dɔː / (BrE)
to secure a door by sliding its bolt into place so it cannot be opened

Examples

To bolt the door quickly
After hearing a strange noise, Tom quickly bolted the door to feel safer.
Please remember to bolt the door when you leave the house.
She always bolts the door before going to bed at night.
