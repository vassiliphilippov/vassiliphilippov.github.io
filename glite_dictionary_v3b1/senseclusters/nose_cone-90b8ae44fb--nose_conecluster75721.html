nose cone

noun
conical front section of a missile, rocket, or aircraft
5.3 6.5
Pronunciation: / noʊz koʊn / (AmE), / nəʊz kəʊn / (BrE)
the pointed, usually conical front section of a missile, rocket, or aircraft, designed to protect the payload and reduce air resistance during flight

Examples

The nose cone of missile
A radar system was installed in the nose cone to improve the missile's targeting accuracy.
Technicians inspected the nose cone for any damage before the next test flight.
The nose cone of the rocket was designed to withstand extreme temperatures during reentry.
