jimjams

noun
nervousness; pajamas
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪmˌdʒæmz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪmˌdʒæmz / (BrE)
a slang term for feelings of nervousness or anxiety, or in British English, pajamas

Thinking about the exam tomorrow gives me the jimjams every time.
