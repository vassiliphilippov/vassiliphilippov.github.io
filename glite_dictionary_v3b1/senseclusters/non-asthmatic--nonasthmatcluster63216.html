non-asthmatic

noun
person without asthma
4.8
Pronunciation: / nɑn æsˈmædɪk / (AmE), / nɒn æsˈmædɪk / (BrE)
a person who does not have asthma.

Examples

A non-asthmatic person
The study compared lung function between non-asthmatics and those diagnosed with asthma.
As a non-asthmatic, she did not require any special medication during allergy season.
Researchers recruited both asthmatics and non-asthmatics to participate in the clinical trial.
