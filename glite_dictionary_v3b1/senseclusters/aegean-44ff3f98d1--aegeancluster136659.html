Aegean

adjective
relating to prehistoric Aegean civilization
Pronunciation: / ɪˈdʒiən / (AmE), / iˈdʒiːən / (BrE)
relating to the prehistoric civilization of the region bordering the Aegean Sea, especially as it existed before classical Greek culture

Examples

Aegean civilization
Archaeologists have uncovered new Aegean artifacts on the island of Crete.
Many Aegean ruins date back to the second millennium BCE.
The Aegean culture greatly influenced later Greek art and mythology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Minoan - relating to the Bronze Age civilization on Crete and other Aegean islands
