cord

noun
unit for measuring firewood
6.1
Pronunciation: / kɔrd / (AmE), / kɔːd / (BrE)
a unit for measuring cut firewood, equal to 128 cubic feet (about 3.62 cubic metres)

Examples

A cord of wood
He stacked a full cord neatly beside the shed for easy access.
The truck delivered three cords of wood to our cabin yesterday.
We ordered a cord of firewood to last through the winter.
