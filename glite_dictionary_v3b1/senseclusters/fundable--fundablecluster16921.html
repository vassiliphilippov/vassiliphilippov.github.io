fundable

adjective
eligible for funding
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌndəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfʌndəbəl / (BrE)
able to receive or qualify for financial support or investment

Examples

A fundable project
The new project is highly fundable due to its strong business plan and experienced team.
Many research proposals are not fundable because they lack clear objectives or measurable outcomes.
Startups with innovative solutions are often considered more fundable by investors.
