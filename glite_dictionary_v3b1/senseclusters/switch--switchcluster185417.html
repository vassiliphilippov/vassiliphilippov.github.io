switch

noun
thin stick for whipping as punishment
9.8
Pronunciation: / swɪtʃ / (AmE), / swɪtʃ / (BrE)
a thin, flexible branch or stick used for corporal punishment, especially by whipping

Examples

Punishment switch
Discipline was often enforced with a switch in rural schools.
He cut a switch from the tree before returning home.
The teacher used a switch to discipline the students in the past.
