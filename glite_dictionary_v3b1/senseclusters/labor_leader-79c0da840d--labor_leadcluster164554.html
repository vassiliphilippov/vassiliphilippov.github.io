labor leader

noun
person who leads a labor movement
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪbər ˌliːdər / (AmE), / ˈleɪbə ˌliːdə / (BrE)
a person who leads or organizes workers, especially within a labor union or labor movement

Examples

Labor leader negotiates
As a labor leader, she negotiated successfully for higher wages and safer workplaces.
Many politicians consult the labor leader before introducing new employment laws.
The labor leader organized a strike to demand better working conditions for the employees.

  • synonym: union leader - a person who leads a labor union
