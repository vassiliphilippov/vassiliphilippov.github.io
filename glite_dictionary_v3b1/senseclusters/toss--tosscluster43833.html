toss

noun
the act of flipping a coin
1.1
Pronunciation: / tɑs / (AmE), / tɒs / (BrE)
the act of throwing a coin into the air, especially to decide something by chance; a coin flip

Examples

A toss for sides
Before every match, there is a toss to determine who serves first.
Let's settle this with a toss—heads means you win, tails means I do.
The referee started the game with a toss to decide which team would kick off.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coin toss - the act of throwing a coin in the air to make a decision; coin flip
  • synonym: flip - act of turning over, often refers to a coin
