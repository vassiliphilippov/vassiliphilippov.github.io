bittersweet

adjective
tasting both bitter and sweet
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌbɪt̬ɚˈswit / (AmE), / ˌbɪtəˈswiːt / (BrE)
Having a taste that is a combination of bitterness and sweetness.

Examples

Bittersweet chocolate
He savored the bittersweet taste of the orange peel candy.
The chocolate had a bittersweet flavor that lingered on her tongue.
This coffee is surprisingly bittersweet, balancing the dark roast with a hint of sugar.
