headcheese

noun
sausage or loaf made from animal head meat
7.6 8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈhed.tʃiːz/ (AmE), /ˈhed.tʃiːz/ (BrE)
A sausage or jellied loaf made from the chopped meat of a calf's or pig's head, and sometimes its feet or tongue.

Examples

A headcheese loaf
Making headcheese requires boiling the pig's head until the meat falls off the bone.
She nervously tasted the headcheese offered at the rural market.
The platter included headcheese along with traditional sausages and cold cuts.
