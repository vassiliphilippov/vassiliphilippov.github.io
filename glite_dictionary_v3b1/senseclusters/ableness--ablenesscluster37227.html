ableness

noun
capability or ability
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪbəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈeɪbəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being able or having the capacity to do something

Examples

Her ableness to solve
Her ableness to solve complex problems made her a valuable team member.
The manager praised his ableness during the challenging project.
Despite his young age, his ableness was evident to everyone in the room.

  • antonym: incapacity - lack of ability or power
