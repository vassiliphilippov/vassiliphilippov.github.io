wildcat venture

noun
risky or speculative business investment
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪldˌkæt ˈvɛn.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldˌkæt ˈvɛn.tʃə / (BrE)
a business project or investment that involves high risk and is often conducted without assurance of success or proper investigation

Examples

A wildcat venture project
His quick fortune came from a wildcat venture that most people doubted would succeed.
Many lost money after backing a wildcat venture in the oil industry.
Starting a wildcat venture is not for the faint of heart or the risk-averse.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sound investment - a safe or low-risk financial commitment
  • synonym: speculative venture - an undertaking involving high risk for high potential profit
