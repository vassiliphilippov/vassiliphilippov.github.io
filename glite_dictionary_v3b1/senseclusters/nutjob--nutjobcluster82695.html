nutjob

noun
slang for crazy person
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtˌdʒɑb / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌdʒɒb / (BrE)
an informal and insulting term for a person who behaves in a very strange, foolish, or irrational way

Examples

A nutjob person
Everyone thought he was a nutjob for swimming in the lake during a thunderstorm.
She called her neighbor a nutjob after he painted his house bright orange.
Only a true nutjob would try to skateboard down those steep stairs.
Some people dismissed the protester as a nutjob because of his wild conspiracy theories.
The radio host called his opponent a nutjob during the heated debate.
She was worried her family would think she was a nutjob for believing in aliens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fanatic - a person with extreme and uncritical enthusiasm or zeal
  • synonym: lunatic - a person who is crazy or behaves in a wild way
  • synonym: maniac - a person who behaves in a very wild or dangerous way
  • antonym: moderate - a person with reasonable or centrist views
  • antonym: sane person - a person who behaves in a normal and reasonable way
