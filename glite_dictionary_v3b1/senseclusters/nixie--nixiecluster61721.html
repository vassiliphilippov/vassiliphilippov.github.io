nixie

noun
undeliverable mail
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪksi / (AmE), / ˈnɪksi / (BrE)
a piece of mail that cannot be delivered because the address is incorrect or insufficient and is returned to the sender

Examples

Returned nixie letter
The post office labeled the letter as a nixie and sent it back to the sender.
If the address is incomplete, your package may become a nixie and never reach its destination.
Clerks sort nixies separately to ensure they are returned to the correct sender.

Related Terms

  • synonym: returned mail - mail sent back to the sender because it could not be delivered
  • synonym: undeliverable - unable to be delivered to the intended recipient
