marginalize

verb

to treat a person, group, or idea as unimportant or insignificant, pushing them to the outer edge of society or a community

Examples

To marginalize minority groups
Activists often speak out against systems that marginalize minority groups.
New policies aim to support communities that were previously marginalized by the government.
Society should not marginalize individuals based on their background or beliefs.
Her ideas were often marginalised during meetings, despite their value.
Many refugees feel that society has marginalised them, leaving them without support or opportunities.
Policies that marginalise certain communities can have lasting negative consequences.
