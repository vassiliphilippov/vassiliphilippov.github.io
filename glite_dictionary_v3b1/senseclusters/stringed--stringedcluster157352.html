stringed

verb
to thread objects
3.4
Pronunciation: / strɪŋd / (AmE), / strɪŋd / (BrE)
simple past and past participle of 'string': to thread or attach objects on a string, or to fit something with a string

Examples

She stringed pearls
She carefully stringed the pearls to make a beautiful necklace for her mother.
The tennis player stringed his racket before the big match to ensure the best performance.
They stringed colorful lights across the garden for the party.

Related Terms
