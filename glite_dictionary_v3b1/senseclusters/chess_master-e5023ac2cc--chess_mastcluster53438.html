chess master

noun
expert chess player
2.2
Pronunciation: / tʃɛs ˈmæs.tɚ / (AmE), / tʃɛs ˈmɑː.stə / (BrE)
a person who plays chess at an advanced or expert level, often recognized with an official title or rating

Examples

A chess master player
After years of training, she finally became a chess master and competed internationally.
As a chess master, he is known for his creative opening strategies and strong endgame skills.
The local club invited a chess master to give a simultaneous exhibition against ten players.
