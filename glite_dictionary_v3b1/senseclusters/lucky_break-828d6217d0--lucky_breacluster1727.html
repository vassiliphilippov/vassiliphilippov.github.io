lucky break

noun
unexpected good fortune or opportunity
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌki breɪk / (AmE), / ˈlʌki breɪk / (BrE)
an unexpected event or opportunity that brings good fortune or success

Examples

A lucky break in career

Sometimes a single lucky break can make all the difference in a career.
Winning the scholarship was a lucky break that changed her life.
