preimmigration

noun
before immigration
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˌɪmɪˈɡreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːˌɪmɪˈɡreɪʃən / (BrE)
the period or activities that take place before a person immigrates to another country

Examples

Preimmigration workshop
The government offers preimmigration workshops to help newcomers prepare for life in their new country.
Many families go through a preimmigration process that includes gathering documents and learning the language.
She found the preimmigration orientation helpful for understanding the healthcare system abroad.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postimmigration - the period or activities after a person has immigrated
