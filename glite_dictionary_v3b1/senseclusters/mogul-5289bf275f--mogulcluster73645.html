Mogul

noun
member of India's former Muslim ruling dynasty
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊɡəl / (AmE), / ˈməʊɡəl / (BrE)
A member of the Muslim dynasty that ruled India from the early 16th century until 1857; a Mughal.

Examples

The Mogul dynasty
Babur, the founder of the Mogul dynasty, invaded India from Central Asia in the sixteenth century.
By the mid-nineteenth century, British rule ended the reign of the Moguls in India.
The empire established by the Moguls significantly shaped the culture and architecture of the Indian subcontinent.
