manhood

noun
state of being a man; manly qualities
4.7 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmæn.hʊd/ (AmE), /ˈmæn.hʊd/ (BrE)
the state of being a man (not a boy), including adulthood or qualities like courage and strength

Examples
