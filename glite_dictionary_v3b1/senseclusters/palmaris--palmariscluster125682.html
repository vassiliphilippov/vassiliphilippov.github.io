palmaris

adjective
of the palm
Pronunciation: / pælˈmɛrɪs / (AmE), / pælˈmɑːrɪs / (BrE)
relating to the palm of the hand or located on or toward the palm.

Examples

Palmaris surface
The palmaris muscle is absent in some people without affecting hand function.
Doctors examined the palmaris region for signs of injury after the accident.
A strong palmaris tendon can be useful for certain surgical procedures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dorsal - relating to the back or upper side, especially of the hand
  • synonym: palmar - relating to the palm of the hand
