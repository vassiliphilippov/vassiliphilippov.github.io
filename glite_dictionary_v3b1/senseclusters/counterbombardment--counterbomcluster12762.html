counterbombardment

noun
attack to neutralize enemy weapons
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərbəmˈbɑrdmənt / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəbɒmˈbɑːdmənt / (BrE)
an attack by artillery or other heavy weapons, aimed at destroying or neutralizing an enemy's weapons or batteries

Examples

Fierce counterbombardment attack
During the battle, both sides engaged in fierce counterbombardment operations.
Successful counterbombardment can significantly reduce the effectiveness of opposing forces.
The artillery unit launched a counterbombardment to suppress the enemy's heavy guns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bombardment - a continuous attack with bombs, shells, or other missiles
