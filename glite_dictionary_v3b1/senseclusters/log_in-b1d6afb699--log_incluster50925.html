log in

verb
access a computer system or website
1.0
Pronunciation: / lɑɡ ˈɪn /; / lɑɡ ɪn / (AmE), / lɒɡ ˈɪn /; / lɒɡ ɪn / (BrE)
to enter a computer system or online account by providing your username and password

Examples

To log in online
After logging in, she checked her messages and calendar.
He forgot his password and couldn't log in to the company server.
Make sure to log in before you try to access your email.
After forgetting her password, Maria couldn't log in to the company website.
He always makes sure to log in early for his online classes.
You need to log in before you can access your email account.

  • antonym: log out - to disconnect from a computer system or website
  • synonym: sign in - to access a computer system or service by entering credentials
  • antonym: sign out - to exit a computer system or account, synonym of 'log out'
