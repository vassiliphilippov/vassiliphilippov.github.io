cradleboard

noun
Native infant carrier
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪdəlˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkreɪdəlˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a traditional wooden frame used by some Native American peoples to carry and protect infants

Examples

A cradleboard for babies
The mother secured her baby in a cradleboard before heading out to gather food.
Museum visitors admired the beautifully decorated cradleboard on display.
A traditional cradleboard allows parents to keep their infants close while working.
