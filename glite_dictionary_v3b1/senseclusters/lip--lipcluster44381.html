lip

noun
insolent or cheeky speech
Pronunciation: / lɪp / (AmE), / lɪp / (BrE)
(informal) Insolent or cheeky speech; impudent talk, especially as a reply to authority.

Examples

Give lip
Don't give me any lip if you want to stay out of trouble.
She was grounded because she talked back and gave her mother some lip.
The student got detention for giving the teacher too much lip.

Related Terms

  • antonym: respect - polite regard, opposite of insolence
  • synonym: sass - impudent or cheeky speech or reply
