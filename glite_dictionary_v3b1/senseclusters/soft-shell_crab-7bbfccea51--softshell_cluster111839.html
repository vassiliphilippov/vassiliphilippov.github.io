soft-shell crab

noun
recently molted edible crab with a soft shell
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔft ˌʃɛl kræb /; / ˈsɔft ʃɛl kræb / (AmE), / ˈsɒft ˌʃɛl kræb /; / ˈsɒft ʃɛl krab / (BrE)
a crab that has recently shed its old shell and has a soft, tender shell, often eaten whole as a delicacy

Examples

A soft-shell crab dish

She learned how to clean and cook soft-shell crab during her culinary training.
The restaurant's specialty tonight is soft-shell crab served with lemon butter sauce.
