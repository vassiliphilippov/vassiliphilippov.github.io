loud

adjective
showy or gaudy in appearance
8.0
Pronunciation: / laʊd / (AmE), / laʊd / (BrE)
(of colors, clothes, etc.) having a very bright or showy appearance in a way that is considered unpleasant or tasteless.

Examples

Loud colors
His loud shirt made him stand out at the formal event.
She preferred simple jewelry to anything too loud or flashy.
The living room was decorated with loud colors that clashed painfully.

Related Terms

  • synonym: garish - unpleasantly bright or showy
  • synonym: gaudy - extravagantly bright or showy, typically so as to be tasteless
  • antonym: subtle - not obvious or showy in appearance
