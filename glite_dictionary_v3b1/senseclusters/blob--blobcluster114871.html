blob

verb
to drop in lumps
Pronunciation: / blɑb / (AmE), / blɒb / (BrE)
to drop, apply, or form something as a soft, shapeless mass or lump

Examples

To blob paint
She blobbed paint onto the canvas to create a unique effect.
The sauce accidentally blobbed onto the edge of the plate.
He blobs cream on top of his dessert before serving it.
The paint started blobbing on the canvas, creating uneven spots.
Raindrops blobbed together on the window before sliding down.
She accidentally blobbed glue onto the paper, making a sticky mess.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clump - to form into a thick, clustered mass
  • synonym: dab - to touch or apply something lightly in small amounts
  • synonym: smear - to spread or apply something messily or unevenly
  • antonym: spread - to extend or distribute over an area
