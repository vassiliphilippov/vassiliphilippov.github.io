dress hat

noun
formal men's hat with a tall crown
Pronunciation: / drɛs hæt / (AmE), / drɛs hæt / (BrE)
a man's formal hat with a tall crown, typically covered with silk or beaver fur, worn on dress occasions

Examples

A dress hat with silk
He wore a dress hat and a black tuxedo to the opera opening.
The old photograph shows him proudly tipping his dress hat to the ladies.
Traditionally, a dress hat completed a gentleman’s formal attire for evening events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: top hat - a tall, flat-crowned, broad-brimmed hat, especially worn by men in the 19th and early 20th centuries
