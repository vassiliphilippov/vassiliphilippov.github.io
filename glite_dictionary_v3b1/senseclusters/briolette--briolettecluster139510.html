briolette

adjective
pear-shaped, faceted
Pronunciation: / ˌbriːoʊˈlɛt / (AmE), / ˌbriːəˈlɛt / (BrE)
cut or shaped in an elongated, faceted pear-like form, especially used for gemstones or jewelry.
