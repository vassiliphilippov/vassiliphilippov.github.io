inviability

noun
impracticality or impossibility
8.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn.vaɪ.ə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ /; / /ɪn.vaɪ.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /ɪn.vaɪ.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /ɪn.vaɪ.ə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ (BrE)
the state of being impossible or impractical to achieve, succeed, or carry out

Examples

Scientists discussed the inviability of the species in the harsh new environment.
