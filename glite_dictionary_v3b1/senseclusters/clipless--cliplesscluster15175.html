clipless

adjective
without clips
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪpləs / (AmE), / ˈklɪpləs / (BrE)
not having or using a clip, clasp, or fastener to hold something in place

Examples

Clipless hairstyle
She wore her hair clipless for the wedding, letting it fall naturally over her shoulders.
The new design features a clipless closure for a sleeker look.
Many cyclists prefer clipless pedals for better efficiency and control.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clipped - secured or held in place with a clip
  • synonym: unfastened - not secured or held by a fastener
