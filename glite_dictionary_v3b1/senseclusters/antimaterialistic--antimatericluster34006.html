antimaterialistic

adjective
opposed to materialism
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌænti məˌtɪriəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌænti məˌtɪəriəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
rejecting the belief that only physical matter exists, emphasizing the importance of mind, spirit, or non-material entities

Examples

Antimaterialistic philosophy
Her antimaterialistic views led her to focus on personal growth rather than possessions.
The philosopher's antimaterialistic arguments challenged the idea that only matter is real.
Many religions promote an antimaterialistic perspective, valuing spiritual over physical things.

Related Terms

  • antonym: materialistic - believing that only physical matter exists or valuing material possessions
  • synonym: spiritual - relating to the soul or spirit rather than physical things
