Unable to develop a normal immune response after exposure to an antigen, often due to a deficiency in the immune system.

Examples

Immunodeficient patient
Because she was immunodeficient, even common infections posed a serious threat to her health.
Certain genetic disorders can cause a patient to become immunodeficient from birth.
The researchers used immunodeficient mice to study the effects of the new treatment.

Synonyms

  • immunocompromised
    7.5 0.22/m
    immunocompromised describes a generally weakened immune system in many situations, while immunodeficient means an inability to mount a normal response to an antigen.
  • immunosuppressed
    7.4 0.06/m
    immunosuppressed describes medically or disease-caused lowered immune activity, while immunodeficient refers to an inability to mount a normal response to an antigen.
