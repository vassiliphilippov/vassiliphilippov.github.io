meteorology

noun
study of weather and forecasting
Pronunciation: / ˌmitiəˈrɑlədʒi /; / ˌmiːtiəˈrɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌmiːtiəˈrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the atmosphere and weather, especially for forecasting

Examples

Meteorology helps people prepare for dangerous weather conditions such as hurricanes or blizzards.
Advancements in meteorology have greatly improved the accuracy of weather forecasts.

Advances in meteorology have made weather forecasts more reliable in recent years.

The science museum offered a workshop on meteorology for students interested in learning about weather.
