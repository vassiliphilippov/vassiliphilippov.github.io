expired

adjective
no longer valid or effective
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspaɪɚd / (AmE), / ɪkˈspaɪəd / (BrE)
no longer valid or having reached the end of its designated period; no longer effective after a set time has passed.

Examples

An expired passport
He was turned away at the airport because his passport was expired.
My driver's license was expired when I tried to renew it at the DMV.
You should always check if the medication is expired before taking it.

  • antonym: valid - legally or officially acceptable; effective
