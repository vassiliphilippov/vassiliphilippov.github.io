cone-bearing

adjective
producing cones (typically evergreen)
6.0 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkoʊn.ber.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkəʊn.beə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
(of a tree or shrub) producing cones and usually having evergreen leaves.

Examples

Cone-bearing trees
Many cone-bearing plants remain green throughout the winter.
Pine and spruce are common cone-bearing trees in northern forests.
The botanist studied several cone-bearing shrubs in the alpine region.
