hectogram

noun
metric unit of 100 grams
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛktəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈhɛktəˌɡram / (BrE)
a metric unit of mass equal to 100 grams

Examples

A hectogram of flour

Scientists measured the specimen’s weight as half a hectogram.
The package contains exactly one hectogram of cheese.
