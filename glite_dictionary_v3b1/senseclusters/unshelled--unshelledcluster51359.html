unshelled

adjective
without shell; shell removed
8.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃɛld / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɛld / (BrE)
(of animals or fruits) Having no shell, or with the shell removed.

Examples

Unshelled walnuts
At the market, they sell both shelled and unshelled walnuts.
He prefers to buy unshelled peanuts for his recipes.
The dish requires unshelled shrimp to save preparation time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shelled - having a shell or with the shell intact
