scope

noun
optical device for magnifying distant objects
9.5
Pronunciation: / skoʊp / (AmE), / skəʊp / (BrE)
an optical device used to magnify images of distant objects, such as a telescope or rifle sight

Examples

A scope for stars
Astronomers use a powerful scope to observe distant planets.
She adjusted the scope on her rifle to improve her aim.
The hunter peered through the scope to spot deer on the far hill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: microscope - an instrument for viewing small objects in detail
  • synonym: telescope - an instrument for viewing distant objects
