robotize

verb
to automate with robots
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊbəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈrəʊbətaɪz / (BrE)
to make something automatic or controlled by robots, often replacing human labor with machines

Examples

To robotize production
The factory decided to robotize its packaging process to increase efficiency.
Many industries are looking to robotize repetitive tasks to reduce costs.
As technology advances, more companies will robotize their production lines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automate - to make a process operate automatically
  • antonym: manual - done by hand or with human effort
  • synonym: mechanize - to use machines instead of people for work
