rootlessness

noun
lack of belonging
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈruːt.ləs.nəs/ /; / /ˈruːt.lɪs.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈruːt.ləs.nəs/ /; / /ˈruːt.lɪs.nəs/ (BrE)
the state of having no permanent home or feeling of belonging anywhere

Examples

A feeling of rootlessness
After years of traveling from city to city, he struggled with a sense of rootlessness.
Many immigrants experience rootlessness when adapting to a new culture and environment.
Her rootlessness made it difficult for her to form lasting friendships.

Related Terms

  • antonym: belonging - a feeling of being accepted and part of a group or place
  • synonym: homelessness - the state of having no home
