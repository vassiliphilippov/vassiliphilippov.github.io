uncontestable

adjective
impossible to dispute
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈtɛstəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈtɛstəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to dispute, challenge, or deny

Examples

An uncontestable fact
The scientist presented uncontestable proof that changed the course of the research.
Her victory in the competition was uncontestable due to her outstanding performance.
The contract terms are uncontestable and must be accepted by all parties.

Related Terms

  • antonym: debatable - open to question or dispute
  • synonym: indisputable - impossible to question or doubt
  • synonym: undeniable - impossible to deny or refute
