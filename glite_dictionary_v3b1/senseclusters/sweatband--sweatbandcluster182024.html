sweatband

noun
band to absorb sweat
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛtˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈswɛtˌbænd / (BrE)
A strip of absorbent material worn around the forehead or wrist to absorb sweat, especially during physical activity.

Examples

Sports sweatband
He always brings an extra sweatband to practice in case one gets soaked.
She wore a sweatband during her run to keep perspiration out of her eyes.
The tennis player adjusted his sweatband before serving the ball.

Related Terms

  • synonym: headband - band worn around the head
  • synonym: wristband - band worn around the wrist
