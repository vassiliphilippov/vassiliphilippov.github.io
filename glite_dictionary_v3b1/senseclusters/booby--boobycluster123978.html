booby

noun
an ignorant or foolish person
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːbi / (AmE), / ˈbuːbi / (BrE)
A stupid, ignorant, or foolish person.

Examples

A silly booby
Don't be a booby; double-check your answers before submitting.
Everyone thought he was a booby for believing such an obvious lie.
She laughed at her own mistake, calling herself a booby in jest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person lacking sense or judgment
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence or ability
  • synonym: simpleton - a person lacking in common sense
