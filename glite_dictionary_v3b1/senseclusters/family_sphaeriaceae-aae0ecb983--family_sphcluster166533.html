family Sphaeriaceae

noun
family of parasitic fungi
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli sfɪəriˈeɪsiaɪ / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli sfɪəˌrɪˈeɪsiːˌaɪ / (BrE)
A family of parasitic fungi characterized by globose, and sometimes necked or beaked, perithecia.

Examples

Family Sphaeriaceae fungi
Members of the family Sphaeriaceae are known for their distinctive beaked perithecia.
Researchers discovered a new fungus belonging to the family Sphaeriaceae on decaying wood.
The family Sphaeriaceae includes several species important to plant pathology.
