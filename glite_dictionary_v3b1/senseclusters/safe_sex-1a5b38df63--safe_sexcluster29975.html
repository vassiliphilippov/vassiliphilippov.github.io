safe sex

noun
sexual activity with measures to prevent disease
Pronunciation: / seɪf sɛks / (AmE), / seɪf seks / (BrE)
sexual activity in which precautions, such as using condoms, are taken to reduce the risk of sexually transmitted infections, especially HIV/AIDS.

Examples

Practice safe sex
Practicing safe sex is important for reducing the risk of HIV and other infections.
The public health campaign encouraged teenagers to learn about safe sex.
They discussed the importance of safe sex during their relationship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protected sex - sexual activity using protection to reduce STI risk
