misally

verb
form an unsuitable alliance
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈælaɪ / (AmE), / mɪsˈælaɪ / (BrE)
to form an alliance that is unsuitable or unwise; to ally in an inappropriate manner

Examples

To misally nations
If nations misally, it can result in unintended diplomatic consequences.
The board realized too late that they had misallied with unreliable partners.
They misallied themselves with a rival company, leading to years of conflict.
