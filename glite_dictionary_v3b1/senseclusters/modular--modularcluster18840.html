modular

adjective
built with interchangeable units for flexibility
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑdʒjələr / (AmE), / ˈmɒdjʊlə / (BrE)
constructed with standardized units or dimensions that allow for flexibility, customization, and variety in use

Examples

Modular office partitions
The company offers modular office partitions that can be rearranged according to your needs.
Their new apartment was filled with sleek, modular furniture designed for small spaces.
We assembled the modular shelving units in under an hour without any tools.

Related Terms

  • antonym: custom-built - built to individual specifications rather than from standardized units
  • synonym: prefabricated - manufactured in advance, especially in sections that can be easily assembled
