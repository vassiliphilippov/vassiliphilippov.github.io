fashionably

adverb
in a stylish or trendy manner
Pronunciation: / ˈfæʃ.ən.əb.li / (AmE), / ˈfæʃ.ən.əb.li / (BrE)
In a manner that follows the latest styles or trends; stylishly.

Examples

Arrive fashionably late
He arrived fashionably late to the party, making a dramatic entrance.
She always dresses fashionably no matter the occasion.
The restaurant is fashionably decorated with modern art and velvet chairs.
