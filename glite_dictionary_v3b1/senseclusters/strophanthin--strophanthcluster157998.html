strophanthin

noun
toxic plant glycoside; cardiac stimulant
8.8
Pronunciation: / stroʊˈfænθɪn / (AmE), / strəʊˈfænθɪn / (BrE)
a bitter, highly toxic glycoside obtained from Strophanthus plants, used in small amounts as a cardiac stimulant and in larger doses as a poison

Examples

Strophanthin poison
Native hunters once used extracts containing strophanthin to poison their arrows.
Researchers study the effects of strophanthin on heart muscle contractions.
The doctor explained that strophanthin could be dangerous if not administered properly.
