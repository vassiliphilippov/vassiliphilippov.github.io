microloan

noun
small business loan
3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ.loʊn/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ.ləʊn/ (BrE)
a very small loan, typically given to individuals or small businesses who do not have access to traditional banking services

Examples

A microloan for business
Many entrepreneurs in developing countries rely on a microloan to start their businesses.
She received a microloan to buy equipment for her tailoring shop.
A microloan can make a big difference for someone who cannot get a traditional bank loan.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: loan - an amount of money that is borrowed and expected to be paid back
  • synonym: microcredit - the lending of small amounts of money to poor borrowers
