flash

verb
communicate or make known rapidly
1.4
Pronunciation: / flæʃ / (AmE), / flæʃ / (BrE)
to make known or cause to appear with great speed, especially by quickly communicating information

Examples

To flash news
As soon as the information was confirmed, it was flashed to headquarters.
The news agency flashed the election results across all major networks.
Urgent updates are often flashed to all mobile devices in the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broadcast - to transmit information or content to a large audience
  • synonym: notify - to inform someone officially
