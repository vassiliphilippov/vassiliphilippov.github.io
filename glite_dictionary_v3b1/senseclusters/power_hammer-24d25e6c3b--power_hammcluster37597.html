power hammer

noun
power tool giving repeated blows for shaping or drilling
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊɚ ˌhæmɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə ˌhamə / (BrE)
a machine tool that uses compressed air or electricity to deliver repeated blows, especially for drilling or shaping metal or rock

Examples

A power hammer machine
Engineers employed a power hammer to drill into the bedrock during the tunnel construction.
The blacksmith used a power hammer to shape the heated metal into a horseshoe.
With the help of a power hammer, the task of breaking through concrete was significantly faster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hammer drill - a power tool with a hammering action for drilling through hard materials
  • synonym: jackhammer - a powerful machine tool used for breaking up rock and pavement
