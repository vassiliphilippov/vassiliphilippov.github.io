harvest mite

noun
mite larva causing skin irritation
4.7 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑːr.vəst maɪt/ /; / /ˈhɑːr.vɪst maɪt/ (AmE), /ˈhɑː.vəst maɪt/ /; / /ˈhɑː.vɪst maɪt/ (BrE)
