dutchman

noun
woodwork repair piece
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdʌtʃ.mən/ /; / /ˈdʌtʃ.mæn/ (AmE), /ˈdʌtʃ.mən/ /; / /ˈdʌtʃ.mæn/ (BrE)
