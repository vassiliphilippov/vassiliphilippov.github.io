inrushing

adjective
moving inward quickly
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌrʌʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌrʌʃɪŋ / (BrE)
flowing or rushing inward, especially suddenly or with great force

Examples

Inrushing water
The inrushing tide quickly covered the sand as the storm approached.
Engineers worked to stop the inrushing air from damaging the equipment.
He was startled by the inrushing crowd as the doors opened.
