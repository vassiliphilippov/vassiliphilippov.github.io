mercurial

adjective
relating to or containing mercury
Pronunciation: / mərˈkjʊriəl / (AmE), / məˈkjʊəriəl / (BrE)
(of a substance or condition) relating to, containing, or caused by mercury.

Examples

Mercurial compounds in science
Exposure to mercurial fumes can be highly toxic to humans.
The diagnosis revealed a mercurial poisoning due to contaminated water.
The laboratory was required to handle all mercurial compounds with extreme caution.
