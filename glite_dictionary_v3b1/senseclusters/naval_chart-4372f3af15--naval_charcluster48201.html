naval chart

noun
map for maritime navigation
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪvəl tʃɑrt / (AmE), / ˈneɪvəl tʃɑːt / (BrE)
a map used by navigators that displays meteorological, hydrographic, and navigational conditions of oceans and seas

Examples

A naval chart map
Before setting sail, they reviewed the naval chart to avoid shallow waters.
Modern naval charts include detailed information about underwater hazards and currents.
The captain spread the naval chart on the table to plot the ship’s course.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nautical chart - a map designed specifically for maritime navigation
