crowfoot

noun
flowering plant of the genus Ranunculus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkroʊˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊˌfʊt / (BrE)
any of several flowering plants of the genus Ranunculus, typically with divided leaves resembling a bird's foot

Examples

The crowfoot plant
In the wet meadows, crowfoot blooms with clusters of bright yellow flowers in spring.
Some species of crowfoot are commonly known as buttercup due to their golden blossoms.
The leaves of the crowfoot plant are deeply divided, much like a bird’s foot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buttercup - common name for several species of Ranunculus with yellow flowers
