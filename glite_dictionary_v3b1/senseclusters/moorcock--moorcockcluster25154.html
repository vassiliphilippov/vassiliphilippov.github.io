moorcock

noun
male red grouse (bird)
Pronunciation: / ˈmʊr.kɑk / (AmE), / ˈmʊə.kɒk / (BrE)
A male red grouse, a bird found on moorlands, especially in Britain.

Examples

A moorcock bird
A flash of reddish-brown signaled that a moorcock was startled from the bracken.
Gamekeepers were counting the number of moorcocks on the estate before hunting season.
The distinctive call of the moorcock echoed across the heather-covered hills.
