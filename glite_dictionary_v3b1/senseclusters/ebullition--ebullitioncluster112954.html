ebullition

noun
sudden emotional outburst
Pronunciation: / ˌɛbəˈlɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛbʊˈlɪʃən / (BrE)
a sudden, intense expression of emotion or feeling

Examples

An ebullition of applause
His speech ended in an ebullition of applause and cheers from the audience.
In a sudden ebullition of joy, she hugged everyone in the room.
There was an ebullition of anger when the results were announced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outburst - a sudden release of strong emotion
