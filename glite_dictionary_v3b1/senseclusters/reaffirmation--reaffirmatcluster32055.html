reaffirmation

noun
the act of affirming again
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌæfɚˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌæfəˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of affirming something again or renewing a previous confirmation or statement

Examples

A reaffirmation of support
Her speech was a powerful reaffirmation of her dedication to social justice.
The annual report began with a reaffirmation of the company’s core values.
The treaty included a reaffirmation of each nation's commitment to peace.
