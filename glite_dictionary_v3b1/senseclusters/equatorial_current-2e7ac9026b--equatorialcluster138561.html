equatorial current

noun
ocean current flowing west at the equator
Pronunciation: / ɪˌkwætəriəl ˈkɝːrənt / (AmE), / ɪˌkwætəˈrɪəl ˈkʌrənt / (BrE)
any of the ocean currents that flow westward along the equator, driven mainly by trade winds.
