anti-corruption

adjective
against corruption
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌænti kəˈrʌpʃən / (AmE), / ˌænti kəˈrʌpʃən / (BrE)
opposing or intended to prevent bribery and dishonest or illegal practices, especially in government or organizations

Examples

Anti-corruption campaign
The government launched a new anti-corruption campaign to restore public trust.
Many organizations have strict anti-corruption policies to ensure ethical behavior.
International agencies support anti-corruption efforts in countries with high levels of bribery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: corrupt - dishonest or fraudulent, especially by those in power
  • synonym: honest - free of deceit and untruthfulness; sincere
