calender

noun
machine for smoothing paper or cloth
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæləndər / (AmE), / ˈkælɪndə / (BrE)
a machine that smooths or glazes paper or cloth by pressing it between plates or passing it through rollers

Examples

Paper calender machine
He operated the calender every day, ensuring the cloth came out perfectly polished.
Modern textile factories rely on automatic calenders to produce smooth fabrics.
The freshly made paper was passed through a calender to achieve a glossy finish.
