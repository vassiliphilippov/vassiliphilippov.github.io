disarticulation

noun
unclear speech
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɑːrˌtɪkjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɑːˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
difficulty or inability to produce clear and distinct speech sounds.

Examples

Speech disarticulation
After the accident, he suffered severe disarticulation and struggled to make himself understood.
The therapist worked with the child to improve her disarticulation and help her speak more clearly.
Stroke patients may experience disarticulation as part of their speech difficulties.
