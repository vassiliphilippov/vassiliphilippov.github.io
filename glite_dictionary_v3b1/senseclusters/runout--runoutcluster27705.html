runout

noun
cricket dismissal method
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌn.aʊt/ (AmE), /ˈrʌn.aʊt/ (BrE)
a method of dismissing a batsman in cricket when the fielding team breaks the wicket while the batsman is outside the crease

Examples

Cricket runout
The team's hopes faded after a crucial runout in the last over of the match.
A quick throw from the fielder resulted in a dramatic runout at the non-striker's end.
The batsman was disappointed to lose his wicket to a runout after a mix-up with his partner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bowled - a cricket dismissal when the ball hits the stumps and dislodges the bails
  • synonym: stumping - a cricket dismissal when the wicketkeeper puts down the wicket while the batsman is out of his ground
