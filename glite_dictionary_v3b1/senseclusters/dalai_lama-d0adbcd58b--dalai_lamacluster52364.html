Dalai Lama

proper noun
spiritual leader of Tibetan Buddhism
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌdɑːlaɪ ˈlɑːmə / (AmE), / ˌdælaɪ ˈlɑːmə / (BrE)
the spiritual leader of Tibetan Buddhism, traditionally believed to be the reincarnation of a line of revered religious teachers, and formerly the political ruler of Tibet

Examples

Dalai Lama speaks
Many people gather to listen when the Dalai Lama speaks about mindfulness and kindness.
The Dalai Lama often travels the world spreading messages of peace and compassion.
The current Dalai Lama was recognized as the reincarnation of his predecessor as a child.
