clasp

noun
fastening device or hook
Pronunciation: / klæsp / (AmE), / klɑːsp / (BrE)
a device, such as a buckle or hook, used to fasten or hold two things together

Examples

A necklace clasp
He struggled for a moment with the tiny clasp on his watch band.
She fastened the necklace with a delicate clasp at the back of her neck.
The briefcase has a sturdy metal clasp that keeps it securely closed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buckle - fastening device for joining two ends, often on a belt
  • synonym: fastener - device that fastens things together
  • synonym: hook - curved device used for catching, holding, or pulling
