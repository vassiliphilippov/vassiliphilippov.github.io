United Arab Emirates

proper noun
country in the Middle East, federation of emirates
Pronunciation: / juːˈnaɪt̬ɪd ˌɛrəb ˈɛmɪrɪts / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪtɪd ˌærəb ˈɛmɪərɪts / (BrE)
A country in the Middle East formed by a federation of seven emirates on the eastern Arabian Peninsula, known for its significant oil reserves.

Examples

The United Arab Emirates country
Oil exports have greatly contributed to the development of the United Arab Emirates.
