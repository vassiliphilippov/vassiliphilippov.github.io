sting operation

noun
undercover police trap for criminals
Pronunciation: / stɪŋ ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃən /; / stɪŋ ˌɑpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / stɪŋ ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
an undercover law enforcement operation where agents use deception to catch someone committing a crime

Examples

A sting operation arrest
A successful sting operation led to the arrest of several drug traffickers last month.
Authorities planned the sting operation for weeks before finally apprehending the suspects.
The FBI conducted a sting operation to catch the corrupt officials in the act.
A sting operation was launched to expose fraudulent financial advisers.
Several suspects were arrested during the sting operation at the warehouse.
The police carried out a sting operation to catch drug dealers in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entrapment - the act of tricking someone into committing a crime to secure their prosecution
  • synonym: undercover operation - a police or investigative action where true identities are concealed
