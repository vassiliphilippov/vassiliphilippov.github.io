sting operation

noun
undercover police trap for criminals
2.6 4.1
Pronunciation: /stɪŋ ɑː.pə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /stɪŋ ɒ.pə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
