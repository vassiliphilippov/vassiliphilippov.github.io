news

noun
current events program on TV or radio
1.0 121/m
Pronunciation: / nuÀêz / (AmE), / njuÀêz / (BrE)
A program on radio or television that reports and discusses current events, often including interviews and commentary.

Examples

Evening news program
She reported the story live on the evening news.
The news often features interviews with experts on important topics.
We watch the 7 o'clock news every night to stay informed.

Synonyms

  • news program
    1.9 1.32/m
    a news program is a scheduled TV or radio broadcast reporting events and interviews, while news refers to that type of program.
  • news show
    1.0 1.09/m
    a news show is a TV or radio program that presents current events and interviews, while news names that kind of program.
  • newscast
    2.9 3.49/m
    a newscast is a formal radio or TV broadcast of news and commentary, while news can mean the program itself.

Related Terms
