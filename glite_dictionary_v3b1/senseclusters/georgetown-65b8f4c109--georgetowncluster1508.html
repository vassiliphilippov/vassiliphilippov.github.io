Georgetown

proper noun
capital city of Guyana
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔr(d)ʒˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒˌtaʊn / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Guyana, a port city on the Atlantic coast.

Examples

Georgetown, Guyana

Many visitors to Guyana begin their travels in Georgetown, the country's lively capital.
The Demerara River runs through Georgetown, making it an important port city for exports.
