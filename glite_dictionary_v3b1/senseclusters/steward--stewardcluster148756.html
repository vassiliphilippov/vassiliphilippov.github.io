steward

verb
to manage resources
3.9 0.2/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàstuÀê…ôrd / (AmE), / ÀàstjuÀê…ôd / (BrE)
to manage, look after, or take responsibility for something, such as property, resources, or an event

Examples

To steward resources
She was chosen to steward the community's shared garden.
It is important to steward natural resources for future generations.
The organization stewards several historic buildings in the city.
She stewarded the charity event with great care and attention to detail.
The museum was stewarded by a dedicated team of volunteers.
He has stewarded the organization through many changes over the years.
