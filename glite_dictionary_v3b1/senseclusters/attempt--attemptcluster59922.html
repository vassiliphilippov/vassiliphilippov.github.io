attempt

noun
an act of trying to harm or kill
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈtɛmpt / (AmE), / əˈtɛmpt / (BrE)
an act of trying to harm or kill someone, especially in the phrase 'an attempt on someone's life'

Examples

Attempt on his life
He survived an attempt on his life and increased his security.
The police prevented an attempt on the ambassador's life last night.
There was another attempt on the king's life during the parade.
