milkhouse

noun
farm milk building
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a small building or room on a farm used for storing and sometimes processing milk and other dairy products

Examples

A milkhouse on farm
The farmer cleaned the milkhouse every morning to keep the milk fresh.
Buckets of milk were cooled in the old stone milkhouse behind the barn.
Visitors toured the historic milkhouse to learn about traditional dairy farming.
The old farmhouse still has a milkhouse where fresh cream is kept cool.
Before modern refrigeration, families relied on a milkhouse to store butter and cheese safely.
She cleaned the milkhouse every morning to keep the dairy products fresh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dairy - a place on a farm where milk is collected and processed
  • synonym: pantry - a small room for storing food
