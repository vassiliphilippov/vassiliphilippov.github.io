nonscholastic

adjective
not academic
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnskəˈlæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnskəˈlæstɪk / (BrE)
not related to schools, academics, or formal education

Examples

Nonscholastic programs
The organization provides nonscholastic programs to help children develop social skills.
Many students participate in nonscholastic sports teams outside of school hours.
Her interests are mostly nonscholastic, such as painting and volunteering in the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extracurricular - not part of the regular academic curriculum
  • antonym: scholastic - relating to schools or education
