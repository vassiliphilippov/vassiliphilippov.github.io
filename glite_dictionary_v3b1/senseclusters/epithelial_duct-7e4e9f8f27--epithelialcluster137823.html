epithelial duct

noun
tube in the body lined with epithelial cells
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈθili.əl dʌkt / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈθiːlɪ.əl dʌkt / (BrE)
A bodily passage or tube lined with epithelial cells that conveys a secretion or other substance.

Examples

An epithelial duct passage
Damage to an epithelial duct may interfere with normal secretion flow.
Researchers studied the structure of the epithelial duct in salivary glands.
The obstruction of the epithelial duct can cause swelling in the affected gland.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duct - a tube or passage in the body for fluids or other substances
