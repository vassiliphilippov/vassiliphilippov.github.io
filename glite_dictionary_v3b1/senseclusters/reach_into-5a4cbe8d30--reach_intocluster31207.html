reach into

verb
extend or enter into something
Pronunciation: / riːtʃ ˈɪntu / (AmE), / riːtʃ ˈɪntuː / (BrE)
to extend or stretch into a space or area; to enter or penetrate into something.

Examples

To reach into a box
As he reached into the box, he felt something soft and furry.
She reached into her bag to find her keys.
The roots reach into the soil searching for water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reach in - to extend a hand, arm, or instrument inside something
