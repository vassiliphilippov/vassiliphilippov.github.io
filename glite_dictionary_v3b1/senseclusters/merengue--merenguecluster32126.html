merengue

noun
Real Madrid supporter
9.3
Pronunciation: / məˈrɛŋɡeɪ / (AmE), / məˈrɛŋɡeɪ / (BrE)
an informal term for a supporter or member of the Spanish football club Real Madrid, referring to the team's white shirts

Examples

A Real Madrid merengue
After moving to Madrid, he quickly became a passionate merengue and attended every home game.
The stadium was filled with cheering merengues celebrating another victory for their team.
She proudly wore her white jersey, showing everyone she was a true merengue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Madridista - a fan or member of Real Madrid football club
  • antonym: culé - a supporter of FC Barcelona football club
