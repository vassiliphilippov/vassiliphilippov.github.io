Again and again; many times at regular or irregular intervals.

Examples

Said repeatedly
She stressed the importance of honesty repeatedly during her speech.
The alarm went off repeatedly throughout the night, disturbing everyone's sleep.
The same question was asked repeatedly, but no answer was given.

Synonyms

  • over and over
    1.0 27/m

  • time and again

  • time and time again
    1.8 3.47/m
    time and time again repeats the idea of happening very often for emphasis, while repeatedly is a simple neutral adverb.
  • recurrently
    4.3 0.11/m
    recurrently means happening again at set or usual intervals, while repeatedly covers many repeats at regular or irregular times.
  • continually
    2.9 16/m
    continually suggests almost nonstop action with short breaks, while repeatedly means happening again and again at separate times.
  • frequently
    1.0 60/m
    frequently means happening often, usually in a short time, while repeatedly stresses the idea of doing something again and again.
  • often
    1.0 476/m
    often means on many occasions or at regular times, while repeatedly focuses on multiple repeats rather than overall frequency.
  • regularly
    1.0 40/m

  • on a regular basis

  • repetitively
