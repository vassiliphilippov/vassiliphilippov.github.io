brunette

noun
person with dark brown or black hair
Pronunciation: / bruːˈnɛt / (AmE), / bruːˈnɛt / (BrE)
a person, usually a woman or girl, with dark brown or black hair

Examples

A natural brunette
As a natural brunette, he rarely dyes his hair.
She was a striking brunette with bright green eyes and a confident smile.
Three brunettes and two blondes auditioned for the lead role.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blonde - a person with pale yellow or golden hair
