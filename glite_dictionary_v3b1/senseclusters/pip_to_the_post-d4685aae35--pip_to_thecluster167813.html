pip to the post

verb
to narrowly win at the last moment
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɪp tə ðə poʊst / (AmE), / pɪp tə ðə pəʊst / (BrE)
to narrowly defeat an opponent or achieve success just before the end of a competition or contest

Examples

To pip to the post in race
After trailing for most of the match, she managed to pip to the post in the final seconds.
He was disappointed to be pipped to the post after leading the race for so long.
The underdog team pipped to the post their rivals with a last-minute goal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: edge out - to narrowly defeat someone, especially in a competition
  • antonym: lose out - to fail to win in a competition, especially at the last moment
