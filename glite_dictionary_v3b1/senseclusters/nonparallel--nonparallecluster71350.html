nonparallel

adjective
not involving simultaneous processing
7.8 6.3
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈpɛrəˌlɛl / (AmE), / nɒnˈparəlɛl / (BrE)
relating to the sequential processing of operations rather than their simultaneous execution, especially in computing.

Examples

Nonparallel processing

Switching from a nonparallel to a parallel model significantly improved processing speeds.
The program used a nonparallel approach, handling each request one after the other.
