Carya myristiciformis

noun
species of hickory tree
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛəriə ˌmaɪrɪstɪˈfɔrmɪs / (AmE), / ˈkeəriə ˌmɪrɪstɪˈfɔːmɪs / (BrE)
A species of hickory tree native to the southern United States and Mexico, known for its hard, nutmeg-shaped nuts.

The Carya myristiciformis produces nuts that are similar in shape to nutmeg seeds.
