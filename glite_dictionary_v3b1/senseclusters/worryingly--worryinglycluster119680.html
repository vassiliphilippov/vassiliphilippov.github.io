worryingly

adverb
in a manner that causes concern
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝː.i.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈwʌr.i.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that causes anxiety or concern.

Examples

Increasing worryingly
Crime rates have increased worryingly in the past year.
She looked worryingly pale after hearing the news.
The results of the experiment were worryingly inconsistent across different trials.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alarmingly - in a way that causes alarm or fear
  • antonym: reassuringly - in a way that makes someone feel less worried
