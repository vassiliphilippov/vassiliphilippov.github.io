chocolate fondue

noun
melted chocolate dish for dipping fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑk.lət fɑnˈdu / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒk.lət fɒnˈdjuː / (BrE)
A dessert dish consisting of chocolate melted with milk or cream, served hot for dipping fruit or other treats.

Examples

A chocolate fondue dessert
At the party, everyone gathered around the chocolate fondue to dip marshmallows and fruit.
The restaurant's chocolate fondue is a popular choice for sharing after dinner.
We prepared a chocolate fondue and served it with strawberries and bananas for dessert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fondue - A dish where foods are dipped into a hot, melted substance
