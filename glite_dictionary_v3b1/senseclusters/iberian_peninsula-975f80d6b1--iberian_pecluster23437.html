Iberian Peninsula

proper noun
peninsula in southwestern Europe
Pronunciation: / aɪˈbɪriən pəˈnɪnsələ / (AmE), / aɪˈbɪəriən pəˈnɪnsjʊlə / (BrE)
a large peninsula in southwestern Europe, occupied by Spain and Portugal

Examples

The Iberian Peninsula countries
Portugal and Spain are the two main countries located on the Iberian Peninsula.
The Iberian Peninsula is known for its rich cultural heritage and diverse landscapes.
The climate of the Iberian Peninsula varies from Mediterranean in the south to oceanic in the north.
