test paper

noun
chemically treated paper for tests
Pronunciation: / tɛst ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / tɛst ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
Paper treated with a chemical indicator, used to test for the presence of specific substances in a solution.

Examples

Litmus test paper
A strip of test paper turned blue, indicating an alkaline substance was present.
Before conducting the experiment, he prepared several pieces of test paper for use.
She dipped the test paper into the solution to check for acidity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: litmus paper - paper treated with litmus, used as a pH indicator
