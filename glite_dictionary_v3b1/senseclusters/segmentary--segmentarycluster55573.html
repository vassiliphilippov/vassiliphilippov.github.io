segmentary

adjective
made of segments
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛɡmənˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈsɛɡməntri / (BrE)
relating to or composed of separate parts or segments

Examples

A segmentary system
The anthropologist studied the segmentary lineage system of the community.
In biology, some worms have a segmentary body structure with repeated units.
The organization followed a segmentary model, dividing responsibilities among independent teams.

Related Terms

  • antonym: continuous - without breaks or divisions
  • synonym: segmented - divided into separate parts or sections
