sparid

noun
fish of the family Sparidae
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛr.ɪd / (AmE), / ˈspær.ɪd / (BrE)
A fish of the family Sparidae, spiny-finned fishes of warm waters with well-developed teeth, many of which are valued as food.

Examples

A sparid fish
Divers observed several species of sparid swimming among the coral reefs.
Many sparids have laterally compressed bodies and are popular in commercial fisheries.
The Mediterranean sparid is highly prized for its taste and delicate texture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sea bream - a common name for several fishes in Sparidae
