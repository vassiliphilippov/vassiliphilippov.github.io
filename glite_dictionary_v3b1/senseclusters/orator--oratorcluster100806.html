orator

noun
person skilled at public speaking
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrətər / (AmE), / ˈɒrətə / (BrE)
a person who delivers formal speeches, especially someone skilled in public speaking

Examples

A skilled orator
He is known as a brilliant orator who motivates people with his speeches.
The keynote orator captivated the audience with her inspiring words.
The young lawyer hoped to become as skilled an orator as his mentor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: audience - the people who listen to a speech
  • synonym: lecturer - someone who gives a lecture or formal talk
  • synonym: speaker - a person who speaks; a person giving a speech or lecture
