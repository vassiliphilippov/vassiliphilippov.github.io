disorder

noun
lack of order; mess
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɔrdər / (AmE), / dɪsˈɔːdə / (BrE)
a state in which things are not arranged properly; a lack of order or organization

Examples

Total disorder
After the earthquake, the city was thrown into chaos and disorder.
His desk was in such disorder that he could barely find anything.
The children left their toys in complete disorder all over the living room.
