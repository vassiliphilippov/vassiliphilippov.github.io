dreamed

adjective
hoped-for or imagined
Pronunciation: / driːmd / (AmE), / driːmd / (BrE)
conceived of, imagined, or longed for; existing only in hopes or dreams

Examples

A dreamed invention
He worked tirelessly on his dreamed invention, hoping it would change the world.
The artist finally completed her dreamed masterpiece after years of planning.
Their dreamed vacation became a reality when they won the lottery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imagined - formed in the mind; not real
  • antonym: realized - brought into actual existence
