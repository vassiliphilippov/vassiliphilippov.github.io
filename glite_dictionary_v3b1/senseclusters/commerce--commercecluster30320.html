commerce

noun
social exchange of ideas or attitudes
2.8 0.77/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀê.m…ùÀês / (AmE), / Ààk…ím.…úÀês / (BrE)
communication or dealings between people, especially the exchange of ideas, opinions, or sentiments.

Examples

Commerce of ideas
Poetry thrives on the commerce of emotions between writer and reader.
The commerce of great minds often leads to groundbreaking ideas.
Throughout the evening, lively commerce of opinions filled the room.
