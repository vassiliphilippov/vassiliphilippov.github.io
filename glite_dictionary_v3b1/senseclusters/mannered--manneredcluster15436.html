mannered

adjective
artificial, affected (behavior)
Pronunciation: / ˈmænərd / (AmE), / ˈmænəd / (BrE)
(of a person's behavior, speech, or writing) artificial or affected; showing unnatural mannerisms.

Examples

A mannered speech
Her speech sounded mannered and insincere to the audience.
I found the writing style overly mannered and lacking in authenticity.
The actor's performance was too mannered to feel convincing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affected - artificial or pretentious in behavior or speech
  • antonym: natural - not artificial or affected, genuine
