flingy

adjective
casually romantic
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪŋi / (AmE), / ˈflɪŋi / (BrE)
inclined to have short, casual romantic or sexual relationships; given to brief affairs.

Examples

Flingy dating style
After his divorce, he became quite flingy and dated several people in a short time.
Her friends teased her for being flingy after she went on three dates in one week.
Some people go through a flingy phase after a long relationship ends.

Related Terms

  • antonym: faithful - remaining loyal to one partner
  • synonym: promiscuous - having many casual sexual relationships
