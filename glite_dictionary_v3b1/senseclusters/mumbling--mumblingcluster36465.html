Examples

Nervous mumbling
During the ceremony, there was a lot of nervous mumbling from the participants.
His constant mumbling made it difficult to understand what he was saying.
I couldn't follow her instructions because of her quiet mumbling.

Synonyms

  • muttering
    4.6 0.25/m
    muttering is quiet speech often showing complaint or secrecy, while mumbling is unclear because words are not well formed.
  • murmuring
    3.4 0.42/m
    murmuring is soft, steady sound by people or nature, while mumbling is unclear human speech with poor pronunciation.
