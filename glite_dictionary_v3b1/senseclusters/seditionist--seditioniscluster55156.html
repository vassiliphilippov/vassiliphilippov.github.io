seditionist

noun
rebellion supporter
9.4
Pronunciation: / səˈdɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / sɪˈdɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who encourages or supports rebellion against authority or government

Examples

A seditionist leader
The government arrested several seditionists after the protest.
As a known seditionist, he was closely monitored by the authorities.
The pamphlet was distributed by a group of seditionists seeking to inspire revolt.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loyalist - a person who remains loyal to the established government
  • synonym: rebel - a person who rises in opposition against authority
