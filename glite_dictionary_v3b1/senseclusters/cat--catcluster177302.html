cat

noun
leaves of Catha edulis used as a stimulant
4.4 8.6
Pronunciation: /kæt/ (AmE), /kæt/ (BrE)
the leaves of the shrub Catha edulis, which are chewed for their stimulating and euphoric effects or used to make tea, especially in eastern Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

Examples

Cat leaves
In some cultures, it is common for people to chew cat leaves in the afternoon for their stimulating effect.
Locals brewed tea from dried cat leaves during traditional gatherings.
The trade and consumption of cat is regulated or banned in many countries due to its psychoactive properties.
