mousehole

noun
hole made by mouse
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊsˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈmaʊshəʊl / (BrE)
a small hole in a wall or floor made or used by a mouse to enter or exit

Examples

A mousehole in the wall
We discovered a tiny mousehole near the baseboard in the living room.
The cat waited patiently by the mousehole for hours, hoping to catch a mouse.
After patching the mousehole, we noticed there were no more signs of rodents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burrow - a hole or tunnel dug by a small animal for shelter
  • synonym: tunnel - an underground passage, often made by animals
