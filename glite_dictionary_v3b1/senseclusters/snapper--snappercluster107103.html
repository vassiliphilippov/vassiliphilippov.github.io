snapper

noun
flesh of certain food fishes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæpɚ / (AmE), / ˈsnæpə / (BrE)
The flesh of any of various important food fishes of warm seas, especially valued as food.

Examples

Snapper fillet
Fresh snapper makes an excellent choice for ceviche.
He bought two pounds of snapper at the fish market for dinner.
The restaurant served grilled snapper with a tangy lemon sauce.
