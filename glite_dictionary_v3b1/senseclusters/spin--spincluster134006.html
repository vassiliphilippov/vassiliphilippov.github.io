spin

verb
to prolong or extend in duration
4.9
Pronunciation: / spɪn / (AmE), / spɪn / (BrE)
to make something last longer than necessary; to prolong or extend an activity, event, or process

Examples

To spin out time
He decided to spin his holiday by taking extra days off.
She managed to spin her story long enough to avoid answering the question directly.
They tried to spin out the meeting for as long as they could.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prolong - to make something last longer
  • antonym: shorten - to make something last for less time
