turbinado

adjective
containing coarse brown sugar
Pronunciation: / ˌtɜːrbɪˈnɑːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˌtɜːbɪˈnɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
made with or resembling turbinado sugar, typically slightly coarse and light brown in appearance or texture
