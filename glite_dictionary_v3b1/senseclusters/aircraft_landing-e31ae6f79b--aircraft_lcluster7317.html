aircraft landing

noun
bringing an aircraft safely to the ground
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr.kræft ˈlæn.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛə.krɑːft ˈlæn.dɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of bringing an aircraft to the ground safely

Examples

An aircraft landing safely
Emergency procedures were followed during the unexpected aircraft landing.
The pilot executed a smooth aircraft landing despite the poor weather.
Training for a safe aircraft landing is crucial for all student pilots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: landing - the act of coming to the ground after a flight
  • antonym: takeoff - the act of an aircraft becoming airborne
