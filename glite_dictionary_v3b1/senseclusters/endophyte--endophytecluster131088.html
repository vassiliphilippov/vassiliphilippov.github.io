endophyte

noun
microbe living in plants
Pronunciation: / ˈɛndoʊˌfaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛndəʊfaɪt /; / ˈɛndəʊˌfaɪt / (BrE)
a microorganism, especially a fungus or bacterium, that lives inside a plant without causing harm

Examples

Related Terms

  • antonym: parasite - an organism that lives in or on another and benefits at the host's expense
  • antonym: pathogen - an organism that causes disease
  • synonym: symbiont - an organism that lives in close association with another
