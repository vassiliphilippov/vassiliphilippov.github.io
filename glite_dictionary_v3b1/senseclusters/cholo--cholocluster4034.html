cholo

noun
person of mixed ancestry
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊləʊ / (BrE)
in some Andean countries, a person of mixed indigenous and European ancestry, often used as an ethnic or social term

Examples

Andean cholo identity
In the highlands she identified as a cholo, proud of her mixed indigenous and Spanish heritage.
The term cholo is sometimes used to describe cultural identity in Peru and Bolivia.
He learned about his family's history as cholos living in the Andean region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mestizo - a person of mixed European and indigenous American descent
