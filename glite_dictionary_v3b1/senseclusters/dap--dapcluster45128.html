dap

verb
to fish by touching water
9.9
Pronunciation: / dæp / (AmE), / dæp / (BrE)
to fish by letting a baited hook or fly lightly touch the surface of the water

Examples

To dap for trout
He learned to dap for trout by letting the fly skim the water's surface.
The angler skillfully dapped his bait to attract fish in the shallow stream.
On calm mornings, she prefers to dap rather than cast her line far out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cast - to throw a fishing line or net into the water
  • synonym: dab - to touch something lightly and quickly
