multilingualism

noun
use of multiple languages
4.8
Pronunciation: /mʌl.tɪ.ˈlɪŋ.gwə.lɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /mʌl.tɪ.ˈlɪŋ.gwə.lɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the use or presence of two or more languages within a community, institution, or individual

Examples

Community multilingualism
The country's multilingualism is evident in its official documents, which are published in several languages.
Schools that encourage multilingualism help students connect with different cultures and communities.
Her family's multilingualism allowed her to communicate easily while traveling across Europe.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monolingualism - the use or knowledge of only one language
