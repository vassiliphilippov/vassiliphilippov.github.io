nonplastic

adjective
not moldable
7.0
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈplæstɪk / (AmE), / nɒnˈplæstɪk / (BrE)
not able to be shaped, molded, or changed in form; lacking plasticity

Examples

Nonplastic clay
The dried clay was completely nonplastic and could not be shaped by hand.
Materials that are nonplastic are often used for building strong, stable structures.
After being exposed to air, the dough became nonplastic and difficult to work with.
The clay was nonplastic and crumbled easily when pressure was applied.
Some metals are nonplastic at low temperatures and will fracture rather than bend.
Engineers prefer materials that are not nonplastic for parts that must absorb impact.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brittle - easily broken or fractured without significant deformation
  • antonym: plastic - capable of being shaped or molded
  • synonym: rigid - not flexible or easily changed in shape
