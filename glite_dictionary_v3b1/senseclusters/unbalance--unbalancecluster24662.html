unbalance

noun
lack of stability
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbæləns / (AmE), / ʌnˈbæləns / (BrE)
a state in which things are not steady, equal, or properly proportioned

Examples

Financial unbalance
The unbalance in the budget caused concern among the board members.
Engineers detected an unbalance in the rotating machinery during the inspection.
A sudden unbalance in the team led to confusion during the project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: balance - a state where things are steady and equal
  • synonym: imbalance - a lack of balance or proportion
