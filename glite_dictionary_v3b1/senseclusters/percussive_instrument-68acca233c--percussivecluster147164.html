percussive instrument

noun
musical instrument played by striking
Pronunciation: / pərˈkʌsɪv ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / pəˈkʌsɪv ˈɪnstrʊmənt / (BrE)
a musical instrument that produces sound by being struck, shaken, or scraped
