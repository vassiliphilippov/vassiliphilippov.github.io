streptococcal sore throat

noun
bacterial throat infection from streptococcus
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌstrɛptəˈkɑkəl sɔr θroʊt / (AmE), / ˌstrɛptəˈkɒkəl sɔː θrəʊt / (BrE)
A bacterial infection of the throat and tonsils caused by streptococcus bacteria, marked by a sore, red throat, and often fever.
