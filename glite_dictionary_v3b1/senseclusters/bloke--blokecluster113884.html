bloke

noun
informal term for a man (British English)
6.0
Pronunciation: / bloʊk / (AmE), / bləʊk / (BrE)
a man; a fellow, especially in informal British English.

Examples

A friendly bloke
He's a really friendly bloke—everyone gets along with him.
The bloke fixing the car is my neighbor.
You wouldn't believe what that bloke did at the party last night.
