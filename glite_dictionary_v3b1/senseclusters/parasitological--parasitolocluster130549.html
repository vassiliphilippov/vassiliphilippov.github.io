parasitological

adjective
study of parasites
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəsɪtəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpærəsɪtəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the scientific study of parasites and their interactions with hosts

Examples

Parasitological tests
The laboratory conducted several parasitological tests to identify the cause of the infection.
Her parasitological research focused on the life cycle of tapeworms in livestock.
Advances in parasitological methods have improved the diagnosis of tropical diseases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parasitic - relating to or caused by parasites
  • synonym: zoological - relating to the scientific study of animals
