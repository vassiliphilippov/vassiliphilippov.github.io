multilayer

noun
object with several layers
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌleɪər / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌleɪə / (BrE)
a manufactured object or material made of several layers, often used in electronics or packaging for added strength or function

Examples

Multilayer circuit board
The new smartphone uses a multilayer circuit board to improve performance and durability.
Researchers developed a multilayer film to protect food from moisture and oxygen.
A multilayer design allows the packaging to be both strong and lightweight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laminate - a material made by bonding together several layers
  • antonym: monolayer - a single, one-layered material or structure
