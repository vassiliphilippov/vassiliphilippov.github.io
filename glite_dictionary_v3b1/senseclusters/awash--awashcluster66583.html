awash

adjective
covered with water; flooded
Pronunciation: / əˈwɑʃ / (AmE), / əˈwɒʃ / (BrE)
covered or flooded with water; level with or submerged by water

Examples

Basement awash with water
After the heavy rainfall, the whole basement was awash.
During the voyage, the deck was almost awash with seawater.
The streets were awash following the sudden storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flooded - covered with water, especially unexpectedly
