energy

noun
vigor; forceful exertion
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnɚdʒi / (AmE), / ˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
physical or mental strength and vitality; the ability to be active and not get tired

Examples

High energy level
After a good night's sleep, I felt renewed energy for the day's challenges.
Lisa completed the marathon with remarkable energy despite the hot weather.
The children were bouncing around the playground, full of energy.
After a good night's sleep, he woke up full of energy.
Children often have more energy than adults can keep up with.
She has so much energy that she can work all day without a break.

  • synonym: vitality - physical or mental liveliness and energy
