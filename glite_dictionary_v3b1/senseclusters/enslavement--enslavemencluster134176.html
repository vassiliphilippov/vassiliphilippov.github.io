enslavement

noun
the state of being a slave
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsleɪvmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈsleɪvmənt / (BrE)
the state of being a slave or the act of making someone a slave

Examples

The enslavement of people
After years of enslavement, the country finally abolished slavery.
He wrote a book about the enslavement and liberation of his ancestors.
The enslavement of millions of people was a tragic part of human history.
Many activists fight against modern forms of enslavement around the world.
The enslavement of entire populations was a tragic part of history.
Their resistance delayed the enslavement of their people by the invaders.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emancipation - the act of freeing someone from slavery or oppression
  • antonym: freedom - the state of being free, not under control or in bondage
  • synonym: slavery - the system or condition of being a slave
