dark bread

noun
bread made with whole wheat flour
9.3 2.3
Pronunciation: /dɑːrk bred/ (AmE), /dɑːk bred/ (BrE)
Bread made with whole wheat flour or other dark flours, often resulting in a denser and more flavorful loaf.

Examples

A loaf of dark bread
At the bakery, she bought a loaf of freshly baked dark bread.
He prefers eating dark bread for breakfast because it is richer in fiber.
Many European sandwiches are traditionally made with dark bread rather than white bread.

Related Terms

  • antonym: white bread - bread made from refined wheat flour
  • synonym: wholemeal bread - bread made from whole wheat flour, especially in British English
