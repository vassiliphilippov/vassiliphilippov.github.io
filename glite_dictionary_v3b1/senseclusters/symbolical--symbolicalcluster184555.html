symbolical

adjective
serving as a symbol; representing the abstract
Pronunciation: / sɪmˈbɑlɪkəl / (AmE), / sɪmˈbɒlɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or serving as a symbol; representing something abstract through symbols

Examples

Symbolical meaning
The dove is symbolical of peace in many cultures.
The statue's raised arm is symbolical of hope for the future.
Wearing white at weddings is symbolical of purity and new beginnings.
Symbolical representations are often used in religious art to convey spiritual concepts.
Her writing takes a highly symbolical approach to exploring cultural traditions.
The ceremony was rich in symbolical meaning, with each gesture carefully chosen.
