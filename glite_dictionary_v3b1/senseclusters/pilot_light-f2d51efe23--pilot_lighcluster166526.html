pilot light

noun
small flame that ignites a larger gas burner
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪlət laɪt / (AmE), / ˈpaɪlət laɪt / (BrE)
a small gas burner that remains continuously ignited to light a larger burner when needed

Examples

Gas stove pilot light
After the storm, the pilot light on our stove went out and needed to be relit.
If the furnace isn't working, it could be due to an extinguished pilot light.
The technician checked the pilot light before servicing the water heater.
