scot-free

adverb
without punishment or penalty
Pronunciation: / skɑt fri /; / skɑt ˈfri / (AmE), / skɒt friː /; / skɒt ˈfriː / (BrE)
without being punished or suffering any consequences for doing something wrong or illegal

Examples

Go scot-free

After the trial, he walked out scot-free, to everyone's shock.
If you break the rules, don't expect to get away scot-free.
Many people felt it was unfair that the company got off scot-free after the scandal.
