appropriation

noun
money set aside for a purpose; government budgeting
5.9
Pronunciation: / əˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌprəʊpriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
an amount of money officially set aside for a specific purpose, especially by a government or legislature

Examples

Government appropriation
The city council approved a special appropriation to renovate the old library.
The museum received a federal appropriation to expand its collection.
This year’s appropriation for education was higher than last year’s.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allocation - the process of distributing something, especially official funds
  • synonym: budget - an estimate of income and expenses for a period; a plan for spending
