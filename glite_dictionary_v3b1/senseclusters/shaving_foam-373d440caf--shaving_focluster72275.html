shaving foam

noun
foamy substance for use before shaving
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪvɪŋ foʊm / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪvɪŋ fəʊm / (BrE)
A toiletry product in the form of a frothy substance, used to soften facial hair and facilitate shaving.

Examples

Shaving foam for face
He squirted some shaving foam onto his hand before spreading it over his beard.
I ran out of shaving foam, so I had to use soap instead.
The hotel bathroom was stocked with toiletries, including shaving foam and toothpaste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shaving cream - a thick cream used for softening hair before shaving
