style

verb
arrange or design in a particular fashion
2.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /staɪl/ (AmE), /staɪl/ (BrE)
to design, arrange, or present something or someone in a particular way, especially in terms of appearance, fashion, or formatting

Examples

To style hair
He likes to style his beard differently every month.
She asked her hairstylist to style her hair for the wedding.
The designer will style the new collection to reflect current trends.
Please style your references using APA format for this paper.
She spent hours styling her report to ensure consistency throughout.
The editor will style the manuscript according to the publisher's guidelines.
He always styles his hair carefully before going out.
She styled her living room with modern furniture and colorful rugs.
The artist styled the portrait in a bold, graphic way.

  • synonym: fashion - to make or shape something, especially with a particular style or technique
  • synonym: format - arrange or organize according to a particular format
