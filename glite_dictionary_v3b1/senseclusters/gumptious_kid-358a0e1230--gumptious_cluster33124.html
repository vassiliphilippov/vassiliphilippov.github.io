gumptious kid

noun
resourceful, lively child
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmpʃəs kɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmpʃəs kɪd / (BrE)
an energetic and resourceful child, especially one showing initiative and determination

Examples

A gumptious kid volunteers
As a gumptious kid, she eagerly volunteered for every class project.
Everyone admired the gumptious kid for finding solutions to problems on the playground.
The gumptious kid started a lemonade stand to raise money for the local animal shelter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resourceful child - a child able to deal well with new situations and difficulties
  • antonym: timid child - a child lacking in courage or self-confidence
