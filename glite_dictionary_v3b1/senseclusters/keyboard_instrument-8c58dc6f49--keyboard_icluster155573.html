keyboard instrument

noun
musical instrument played with a keyboard
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkiː.bɔrd ˌɪn.strə.mənt / (AmE), / ˈkiː.bɔːd ˌɪn.strə.mənt / (BrE)
a musical instrument that is played using a keyboard, such as a piano, organ, or synthesizer

Examples

A keyboard instrument player
During the concert, he demonstrated his skill on various keyboard instruments.
She learned to play several keyboard instruments, including the organ and the harpsichord.
The synthesizer is a modern type of keyboard instrument used in pop and electronic music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: organ - a large keyboard instrument producing sound by forcing air through pipes or using electronic circuits
  • synonym: piano - a large keyboard musical instrument with strings that are struck by hammers
  • antonym: string instrument - a musical instrument that produces sound by vibrating strings
