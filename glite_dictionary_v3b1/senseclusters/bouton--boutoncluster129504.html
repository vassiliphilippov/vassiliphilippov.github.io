bouton

noun
plant bud or swelling
9.9
Pronunciation: / buːˈtɒn / (AmE), / buːˈtɒn / (BrE)
a small bud or swelling on a plant from which a leaf or flower develops

Examples

A flower bouton
In early spring, each branch produced a tiny bouton that later opened into a white blossom.
The gardener carefully examined each bouton to check for signs of disease.
After the rain, every bouton on the rosebush seemed ready to burst into bloom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bud - a small growth on a plant that develops into a leaf or flower
