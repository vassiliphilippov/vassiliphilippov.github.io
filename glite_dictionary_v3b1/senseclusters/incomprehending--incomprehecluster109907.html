incomprehending

adjective
not understanding
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkɑːmprɪˈhɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɪnkɒmprɪˈhɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
not understanding or showing a lack of understanding of something

Examples

An incomprehending look
He stared at the complex diagram with an incomprehending expression on his face.
The teacher noticed the incomprehending looks from several students during the lesson.
She remained incomprehending even after the instructions were repeated twice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confused - unable to think clearly or understand
  • antonym: understanding - showing comprehension or grasp of something
