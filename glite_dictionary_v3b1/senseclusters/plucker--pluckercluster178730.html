plucker

noun
remover of feathers or fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌkər / (AmE), / ˈplʌkə / (BrE)
a person or device that removes something by pulling or picking it off (such as feathers, fruit, etc.)

Examples

Chicken plucker
The plucker made the process of preparing chickens much faster for the kitchen staff.
She worked as a plucker during the harvest season, removing ripe apples from the trees.
The factory uses a mechanical plucker to remove feathers from poultry efficiently.
The chicken plucker made preparing the birds much faster for the farmers.
She used a plucker to remove the unwanted hairs from her eyebrows.
During harvest, a skilled plucker can pick hundreds of apples in a day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harvester - a person or machine that gathers crops
  • synonym: picker - a person or device that picks fruit or other items
  • synonym: remover - a person or thing that takes something away
