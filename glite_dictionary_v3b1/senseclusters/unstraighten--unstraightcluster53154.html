unstraighten

verb
to bend again
6.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstreɪtən / (AmE), / ʌnˈstreɪtən / (BrE)
to make something not straight or to bend it after it has been straightened

Examples

To unstraighten a paperclip

If you unstraighten the metal rod, it may lose its original strength.
The artist decided to unstraighten the wire to create a more dynamic sculpture.
