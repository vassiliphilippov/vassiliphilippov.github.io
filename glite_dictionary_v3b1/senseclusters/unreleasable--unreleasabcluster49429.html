unreleasable

adjective
not able to be released
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈliː.sə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈliː.sə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be released, published, or made available to the public

Examples

Unreleasable secret report
The confidential report was deemed unreleasable by the government.
Due to technical problems, the software update remained unreleasable for several months.
The director was disappointed when the movie was labeled unreleasable by the studio.

Related Terms

  • antonym: releasable - able to be released or made available
  • synonym: unpublishable - not suitable or allowed to be published
