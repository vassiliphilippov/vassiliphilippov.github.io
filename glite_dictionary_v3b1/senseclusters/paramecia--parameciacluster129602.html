paramecia

noun
plural of paramecium (single-celled organism)
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈmiʃiə / (AmE), / ˌparəˈmiːsɪə / (BrE)
Plural of paramecium; any member of the genus Paramecium, a single-celled freshwater organism with a slipper-like shape, commonly studied in biology.

Examples

Freshwater paramecia
Biology students often culture paramecia to study their feeding and movement.
The pond water sample was teeming with paramecia and other microorganisms.
Under the microscope, we observed several paramecia moving swiftly across the slide.
