gameness

noun
bravery and determination
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪmnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being brave, determined, and willing to continue despite difficulty or danger

Examples

Her gameness under pressure
The athlete's gameness inspired her teammates to keep fighting until the end.
Despite the injury, his gameness never wavered throughout the competition.
Everyone admired the gameness she showed in facing such a difficult challenge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courage - the ability to face danger or pain without fear
  • antonym: cowardice - lack of bravery or courage
  • synonym: determination - the quality of continuing to try despite difficulties
