summer house

noun
house used during summer months
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmɚ haʊs / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə haʊs / (BrE)
A house, usually in the country, used for relaxing or living during the summer months.

Examples

A summer house in garden
The old family summer house was filled with memories of carefree holidays.
They spent every August at their summer house by the lake.
We plan to renovate our summer house before next season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cottage - a small simple house, typically in the countryside
