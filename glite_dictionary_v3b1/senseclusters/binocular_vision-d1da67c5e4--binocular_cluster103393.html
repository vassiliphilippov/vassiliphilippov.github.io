binocular vision

noun

Examples

Binocular vision development
Children develop binocular vision as their eyes and brains learn to coordinate together.
Owls have excellent binocular vision that helps them judge distances accurately when hunting at night.
Wearing an eyepatch may temporarily reduce binocular vision and make tasks requiring depth perception more difficult.
Researchers equipped the drone with binocular vision to estimate distances during mapping flights.
The autonomous rover relied on binocular vision to avoid obstacles on the rocky terrain.
Engineers used binocular vision algorithms to reconstruct a 3D model from synchronized camera images.
