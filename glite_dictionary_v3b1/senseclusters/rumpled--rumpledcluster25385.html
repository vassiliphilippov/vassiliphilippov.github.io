rumpled

adjective
wrinkled or untidy in appearance
4.2 1.52/m

(of fabric, clothing, or appearance) creased, wrinkled, or untidy; in a state of disarray.

Examples

Rumpled clothes
After a long nap, her hair was rumpled and messy.
He wore a rumpled shirt that looked like it hadn't been ironed in days.
The rumpled bedspread showed someone had been tossing and turning all night.

Synonyms

  • disheveled
    6.1 2.09/m
    disheveled looks untidy with messy hair, loose clothes, or a messy room, while rumpled shows creased, wrinkled or bunched clothing.
  • tousled
    5.3 0.59/m
    tousled means hair or clothes look casually ruffled, while rumpled refers to clothing or fabric that is creased and wrinkled.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wrinkled - having small lines or folds, especially from being crushed or crumpled
