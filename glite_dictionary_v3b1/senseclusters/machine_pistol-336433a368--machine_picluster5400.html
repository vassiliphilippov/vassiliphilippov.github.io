machine pistol

noun
fully automatic handgun
Pronunciation: / məˈʃin ˈpɪs.təl / (AmE), / məˈʃiːn ˈpɪs.təl / (BrE)
a small, fully automatic handgun that can fire multiple rounds rapidly, often considered a type of submachine gun

Examples

A machine pistol gun
Carrying a machine pistol without proper authorization is illegal in many countries.
Collectors are interested in rare models of the machine pistol manufactured in the 20th century.
The security forces confiscated a loaded machine pistol from the suspect during the raid.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: pistol - a small firearm designed to be held in one hand
  • synonym: submachine gun - a lightweight, automatic gun that fires pistol ammunition
