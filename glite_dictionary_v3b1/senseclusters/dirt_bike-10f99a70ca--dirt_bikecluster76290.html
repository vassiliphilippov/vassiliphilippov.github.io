dirt bike

noun
off-road motorcycle
Pronunciation: / dɜrt baɪk / (AmE), / dɜːt baɪk / (BrE)
a lightweight motorcycle designed for off-road use, typically equipped with rugged tires and suspension for riding over rough, unpaved ground

Examples

A dirt bike ride
He rode his dirt bike along the winding trails through the forest.
Many teenagers dream of owning their first dirt bike and exploring off-road tracks.
The rough terrain was perfect for testing the dirt bike's suspension and handling.
