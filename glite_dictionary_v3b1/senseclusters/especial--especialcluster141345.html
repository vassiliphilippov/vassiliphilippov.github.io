especial

adjective
more important; exceptional
Pronunciation: / ɪˈspɛʃəl / (AmE), / ɪˈspɛʃəl / (BrE)
More important or significant than others; exceptional or surpassing what is usual or expected.

Examples

An especial care
He gave especial care to the details of the project.
She has an especial interest in Renaissance literature.
The invitation was sent with especial urgency to the committee members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exceptional - unusually good; much greater than usual
  • antonym: ordinary - with no special or distinctive features; normal
  • synonym: special - better, greater, or different from what is usual
