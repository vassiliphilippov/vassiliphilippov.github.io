drill site

noun
site where drilling takes place
5.1
Pronunciation: / drɪl saɪt / (AmE), / drɪl saɪt / (BrE)
A location where a drill hole is made, especially for extracting minerals, oil, or gas.

Examples

A drill site location
Strict safety precautions must be followed at every drill site to prevent accidents.
The engineers arrived at the drill site early to prepare the equipment for extraction.
The remote drill site was only accessible by helicopter due to its location.
