pseudoreligious

adjective
falsely religious
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdoʊrɪˈlɪdʒəs / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊrɪˈlɪdʒəs / (BrE)
having the appearance or qualities of religion but not genuinely religious

Examples

Pseudoreligious ceremony
The ceremony had a pseudoreligious feel, though it was not connected to any faith.
Critics argued that the group's pseudoreligious practices were designed to manipulate followers.
Some political rallies adopt pseudoreligious symbols to inspire loyalty among supporters.

  • synonym: spurious - not genuine or authentic
