American blight

noun
a pest of apple and other trees (woolly aphid)
8.3
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈmer.ɪ.kən blaɪt/ /; / /ə.ˈmɜːr.ɪ.kən blaɪt/ (AmE), /ə.ˈme.rɪ.kən blaɪt/ (BrE)
