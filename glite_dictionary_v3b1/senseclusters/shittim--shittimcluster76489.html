shittim

noun
a thorny tree of the southeastern US
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪt.ɪm / (AmE), / ˈʃɪt.ɪm / (BrE)
A shrubby, thorny, deciduous tree native to the southeastern United States, having white flowers and small black drupaceous fruit.
