timeful

adjective
timely; opportune
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪmfəl / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmfəl / (BrE)
happening or done at a suitable or advantageous moment; timely or opportune.

Examples

Timeful decision
Her timeful remark arrived just as the meeting needed direction.
The manager appreciated his timeful intervention during the crisis.
A timeful warning can prevent many problems before they start.

Related Terms

  • synonym: opportune - occurring at a favorable or useful time
  • synonym: timely - happening at the right or appropriate time
  • antonym: untimely - happening at an unsuitable or wrong time
