pollution

noun
contamination of the environment with harmful substances
1.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈluːʃən /; / pəˈluʃən / (AmE), / pəˈluːʃən / (BrE)
the presence or introduction of harmful substances into the environment, making it unsafe or causing harm
