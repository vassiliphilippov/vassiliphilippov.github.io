intention

noun
aim or purpose, esp. regarding marriage proposal
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈten.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈten.ʃən/ (BrE)
(usually intentions) a person's aim or purpose, especially in regard to proposing marriage or forming a serious romantic relationship.

Examples

Marriage intentions
He told her that his intentions were serious and honorable.
Rumors spread quickly about his intentions toward the mayor's daughter.
She doubted his intentions until he finally proposed.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: commitment - the state or quality of being dedicated to a cause, often used of marriage
  • degree lower: proposal - an act of asking someone to marry you
  • synonym: purpose - the reason for which something is done or created
