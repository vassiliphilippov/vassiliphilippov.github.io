champion

adjective
having won first place; prizewinning
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæm.pi.ən / (AmE), / ˈtʃæm.pi.ən / (BrE)
Having won first place in a competition or contest; prizewinning.

Examples

Champion bulldog
He bred a champion bulldog, renowned at national dog shows.
That vineyard is famous for producing champion wines year after year.
The racehorse was a true champion, winning every race that season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prizewinning - having won a prize in a contest; successful
