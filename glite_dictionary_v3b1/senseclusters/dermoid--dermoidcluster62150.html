dermoid

noun
benign cystic tumor
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdɜːr.mɔɪd/ (AmE), /ˈdɜː.mɔɪd/ (BrE)
a benign cystic tumor present from birth that contains tissues such as skin, hair, or teeth

Examples

Ovarian dermoid cyst
The doctor explained that the dermoid in her ovary was not cancerous and could be removed safely.
A dermoid may contain hair, skin, or even teeth, making it unusual among cysts.
After surgery, the patient recovered quickly from the removal of a dermoid near her eye.
