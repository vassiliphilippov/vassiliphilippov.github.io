keep up with the Joneses

verb
try to match others' status or lifestyle
5.8
Pronunciation: / kip ʌp wɪð ðə ˈdʒoʊnzɪz / (AmE), / kiːp ʌp wɪð ðə ˈdʒəʊnzɪz / (BrE)
to try to match one's neighbors or social peers in status, possessions, or lifestyle, often for appearance's sake
