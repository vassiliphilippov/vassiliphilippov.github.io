manbot

noun
human-robot hybrid
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmænˌbɑt / (AmE), / ˈmænˌbɒt / (BrE)
a being that is part human and part robot, or a robot designed to look or act like a man

Examples

Surgical manbot
The scientist unveiled a new manbot capable of performing delicate surgeries with precision.
In the movie, the main character is a manbot struggling to remember his human emotions.
Some people fear that manbots could blur the line between humans and machines in the future.
The company unveiled a new manbot that can assist with heavy lifting in factories.
Children were fascinated by the lifelike movements of the manbot at the science exhibition.
Engineers programmed the manbot to respond to simple voice commands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: android - a robot designed to look and act like a human
  • synonym: cyborg - a being with both organic and biomechatronic body parts
  • antonym: gynoid - a humanoid robot designed to resemble a woman
  • antonym: robot - a machine capable of carrying out complex actions automatically
