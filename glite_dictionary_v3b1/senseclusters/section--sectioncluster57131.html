section

noun
segment of a citrus fruit
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
Each of the naturally divided parts of a citrus fruit, such as an orange, separated by thin membranes.

Examples

A section of orange
He handed me a section of grapefruit from his breakfast plate.
I prefer to eat oranges by separating them into sections.
She peeled the tangerine and ate one juicy section at a time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: segment - a part or section into which something is divided, especially of fruit
