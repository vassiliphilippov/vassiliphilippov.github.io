bask in praise

verb
enjoy receiving compliments or admiration
7.5
Pronunciation: / bæsk ɪn preɪz / (AmE), / bɑːsk ɪn preɪz / (BrE)
to take great pleasure from the admiration or compliments of others

Examples

To bask in praise
After the ceremony, she took a moment to bask in praise from her colleagues.
He tends to bask in praise instead of sharing credit with his team.
The artist loves to bask in praise whenever his work is exhibited.
