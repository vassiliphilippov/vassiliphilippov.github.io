national

adjective
owned or run by national government
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃənəl / (AmE), / ˈnæʃənəl / (BrE)
owned or managed for the public by the national government rather than by local authorities or private individuals

Examples

National university
He attended a national university, which is supported by public funds.
Many museums are national institutions funded by the federal government.
The government expanded the system of national parks to protect more wildlife habitats.

Related Terms

  • antonym: private - owned by individuals or companies, not by the government
  • degree lower: state - owned or managed by a regional or state government
