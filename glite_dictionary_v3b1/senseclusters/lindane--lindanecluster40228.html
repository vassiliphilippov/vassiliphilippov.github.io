lindane

noun
insecticide and medicinal compound
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪnˌdeɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɪndeɪn / (BrE)
a white crystalline compound used as an agricultural insecticide and in some medicinal preparations to treat lice and scabies

Examples

lindane insecticide lotion
Doctors sometimes prescribed lindane lotions for severe cases of head lice.
Farmers relied on lindane as an effective method to control crop pests.
The use of lindane has been restricted in many countries due to environmental concerns.
