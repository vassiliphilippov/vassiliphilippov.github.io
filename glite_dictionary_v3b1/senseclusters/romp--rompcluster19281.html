romp

noun
lively, carefree play or activity
Pronunciation: / rɑmp / (AmE), / rɒmp / (BrE)
a lively and carefree play or activity, especially involving physical movement and fun

Examples

A fun romp
After finishing their homework, the siblings enjoyed a quick romp in the backyard before dinner.
The children had a romp in the park, laughing and chasing each other around the playground.
The puppy's joyful romp through the field delighted everyone watching.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frolic - a playful and lively activity
  • synonym: play - activity done for enjoyment and recreation
  • synonym: revel - to take great pleasure in a lively activity or party
