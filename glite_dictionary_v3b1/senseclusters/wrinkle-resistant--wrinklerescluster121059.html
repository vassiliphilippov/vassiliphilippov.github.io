wrinkle-resistant

adjective
not easily wrinkled (of fabric)
4.5 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪŋ.kəl rɪ.ˈzɪs.tənt/ /; / /ˈrɪŋ.kəl riː.ˈzɪs.tənt/ /; / /ˈrɪŋ.kəl rə.ˈzɪs.tənt/ (AmE), /ˈrɪŋ.kəl rɪ.ˈzɪs.tənt/ /; / /ˈrɪŋ.kəl rə.ˈzɪs.tənt/ (BrE)
(of fabric) not easily creased or wrinkled, retaining a smooth appearance after use or washing.

Examples

Wrinkle-resistant fabric
Many uniforms are designed to be wrinkle-resistant for a professional appearance.
She prefers wrinkle-resistant shirts because they look neat all day without ironing.
The travel bag is made from wrinkle-resistant fabric, perfect for journeys.
