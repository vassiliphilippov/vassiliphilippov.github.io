subcase

noun
inner container
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌkeɪs / (BrE)
a small case or container placed inside a larger case, used for storing or protecting items

Examples

A velvet subcase
She kept the jewelry in a velvet subcase inside the travel suitcase to prevent scratches.
The technician opened the subcase to access the delicate electronic components.
Each subcase was labeled to help organize the tools within the main toolbox.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: case - a container for holding or protecting something
  • synonym: compartment - a separate section within a larger container
