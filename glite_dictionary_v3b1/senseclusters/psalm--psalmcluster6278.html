psalm

noun
biblical song or hymn
Pronunciation: / sɑm / (AmE), / sɑːm / (BrE)
a sacred song or hymn, especially one from the biblical Book of Psalms

Examples

A psalm of praise
Every morning, she reads a psalm from the Old Testament.
Many people find comfort in reciting a psalm when they are anxious.
The choir sang a beautiful psalm during the church service.
He found comfort in reciting his favorite psalm each morning.
Many psalms express gratitude and praise towards God.
The choir sang a beautiful psalm during the Sunday service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hymn - a song of praise, especially a religious one
  • synonym: prayer - words spoken to God or a deity, especially in worship
