bur

noun
seed vessel with hooks or prickles
Pronunciation: / bɝː / (AmE), / bɜː / (BrE)
a seed vessel or fruit with hooks or prickles that attach to fur or clothing

Examples

Sticky bur on clothes
A bur can be difficult to remove from clothing due to its tiny hooks.
Children often find bur stuck to their socks after playing outdoors.
The dog's fur was full of burs after our walk through the meadow.
