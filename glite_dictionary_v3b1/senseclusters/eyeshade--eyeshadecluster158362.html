eyeshade

noun
sleep mask
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈaɪ.ʃeɪd/ (AmE), /ˈaɪ.ʃeɪd/ (BrE)
a piece of material worn over the eyes to block out light, especially to help with sleeping

Examples

A sleeping eyeshade
She put on an eyeshade to block out the morning sunlight and sleep longer.
The airline provided each passenger with an eyeshade for the overnight flight.
Wearing an eyeshade can help people who are sensitive to light fall asleep more easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blindfold - a piece of cloth used to cover someone's eyes
  • synonym: sleep mask - a soft covering for the eyes used to block light while sleeping
