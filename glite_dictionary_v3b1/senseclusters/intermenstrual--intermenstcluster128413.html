intermenstrual

adjective
between periods
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmɛnstruəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmɛnstruəl / (BrE)
occurring between menstrual periods

Examples

Intermenstrual spotting
The doctor asked if she had experienced any intermenstrual spotting in the past month.
Some women may notice intermenstrual pain as part of their normal cycle.
He explained that intermenstrual bleeding can have several possible causes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: menstrual - relating to menstruation or a menstrual period
