upsetter

noun
unexpected winner; defeats the favorite
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈsɛtər / (AmE), / ʌpˈsɛtə / (BrE)
a person or team that unexpectedly defeats the favored opponent or competitor

Examples

The upsetter team
Fans cheered as the upsetter knocked the top seed out of the competition.
In a shocking turn of events, the little-known runner emerged as the upsetter in the finals.
The underdog team became the upsetter of the tournament by beating the defending champions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: underdog - a competitor thought to have little chance of winning a fight or contest
