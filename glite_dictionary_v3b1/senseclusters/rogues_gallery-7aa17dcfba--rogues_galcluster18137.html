rogue's gallery

noun
group of disreputable or criminal people
6.1
Pronunciation: / roʊɡz ˈɡæləri / (AmE), / rəʊɡz ˈɡaləri / (BrE)

The detective reviewed the rogue's gallery in search of a suspect for the recent burglary.
