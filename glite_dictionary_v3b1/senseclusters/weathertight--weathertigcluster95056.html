weathertight

adjective
sealed against weather
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛðərˌtaɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɛðətaɪt / (BrE)
preventing water, wind, or weather from entering or passing through

Examples

Weathertight cabin
The cabin was built to be weathertight so that no rain or wind could get inside.
Make sure the roof is weathertight before the winter storms arrive.
They installed new doors to keep the house weathertight during harsh weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airtight - not allowing air to enter or escape
  • antonym: leaky - allowing liquid or gas to pass through
  • synonym: watertight - preventing water from entering or passing through
