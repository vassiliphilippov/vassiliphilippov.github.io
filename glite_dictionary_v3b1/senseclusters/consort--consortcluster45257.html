consort

verb
go together harmoniously
9.0 5.6
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈsɔːrt/ /; / /kɑːn.ˈsɔːrt/ (AmE), /kən.ˈsɔːt/ /; / /kɒn.ˈsɔːt/ (BrE)

Examples
