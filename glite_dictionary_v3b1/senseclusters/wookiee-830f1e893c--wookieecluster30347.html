Wookiee

proper noun
Star Wars alien species
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊki / (AmE), / ˈwʊki / (BrE)
a tall, hairy, fictional alien species from the Star Wars universe, known for strength and loyalty

Examples

Wookiee Chewbacca
Chewbacca is a well-known Wookiee who travels with Han Solo in the Star Wars films.
The Wookiee home planet, Kashyyyk, is covered in giant trees and dense forests.
Many fans dress up as a Wookiee at science fiction conventions.
