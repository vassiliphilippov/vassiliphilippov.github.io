Bolsheviki

noun
old plural of Bolshevik
9.9
Pronunciation: / boʊlʃəˈviːki / (AmE), / bɒlʃəˈviːki /; / bɒlʃɪˈviːki / (BrE)
members of the Russian Communist Party (the Bolsheviks), the majority faction led by Lenin that led the 1917 Russian Revolution

Examples

The Bolsheviki party
The Bolsheviki played a crucial role in the October Revolution of 1917.
Many historians debate the strategies used by the Bolsheviki to consolidate power.
After the revolution, the Bolsheviki established a new government in Russia.
The Bolsheviki played a crucial role in the success of the October Revolution in 1917.
Many historians debate the strategies used by the Bolsheviki to gain popular support.
After seizing power, the Bolsheviki established a new government based on communist principles.
