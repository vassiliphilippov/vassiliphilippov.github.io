laughability

noun
quality of being funny
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌlæfəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌlɑːfəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being amusing or ridiculous enough to make people laugh

Examples

The laughability of excuses
The laughability of his excuse made it impossible to stay angry at him.
Critics questioned the laughability of the new comedy show.
She doubted the laughability of the joke before telling it to the group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: seriousness - the quality of being serious and not funny
