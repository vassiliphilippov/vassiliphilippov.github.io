Synonyms

  • fall
    1.0 170/m
    fall is a general downward movement by gravity or force, while drop down describes falling from a higher place, suddenly or slowly.
  • drop
    1.0 50/m
    drop often means to let something fall or to fall by accident, while drop down focuses on moving from higher to lower.
  • fall down
    1.0 12/m
    fall down means to lose an upright position and hit the ground, while drop down can mean any object falling from a higher place.
  • descend
    2.7 7/m
    descend is a more formal word for moving down stairs or levels, while drop down is more common in spoken use.
  • come down
    2.8 10/m
    come down means to move or fall to a lower position or level, while drop down can be sudden or gradual.
  • go down
    1.9 15/m
    go down is used for things or numbers becoming lower, while drop down focuses on physical movement from a higher place.
  • plummet
    5.8 4/m
    plummet means to fall very fast and steeply, while drop down allows both sudden drops and slow downward movements.
  • plunge
    3.8 4/m
    plunge means to fall or move down very quickly, often with force, while drop down can be less forceful or slow.
  • sink
    1.0 17/m
    sink often means to move slowly down into a liquid or lower level, while drop down can be faster or not liquid-related.
