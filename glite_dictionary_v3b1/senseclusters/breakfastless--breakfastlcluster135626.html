breakfastless

adjective
without breakfast included
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛkfəstləs / (AmE), / ˈbrɛkfəstləs / (BrE)
not including breakfast, especially referring to accommodation or arrangements where a morning meal is not provided

Examples

Breakfastless hotel
They chose a breakfastless hotel to have more flexibility in their morning plans.
The travel package was breakfastless, so guests had to find their own morning meals.
A breakfastless stay is often cheaper than one that includes food.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bed and breakfast - accommodation including breakfast with the room
  • synonym: room only - accommodation that does not include meals
