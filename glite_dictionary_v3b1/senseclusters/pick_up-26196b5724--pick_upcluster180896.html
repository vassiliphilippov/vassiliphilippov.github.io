pick up

verb
eat by pecking at, like a bird
1.0
Pronunciation: / pɪk ʌp / (AmE), / pɪk ʌp / (BrE)
To eat small amounts of food by pecking at it, like a bird.

Examples

Birds pick up crumbs
She would pick up bits of bread from her plate like a bird.
Sparrows often pick up crumbs left on outdoor tables.
The chickens pick up grains scattered on the ground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nibble - to take small bites of something
