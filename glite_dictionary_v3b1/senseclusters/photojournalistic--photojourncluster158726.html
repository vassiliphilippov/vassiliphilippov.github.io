photojournalistic

adjective
of photojournalism
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˌdʒɜːrnəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˌdʒɜːnəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of photojournalism or photojournalists, especially in style or approach

Examples

Photojournalistic style
The magazine is known for its photojournalistic coverage of world events.
She developed a photojournalistic approach to documenting everyday life in the city.
His photojournalistic images often appear in major newspapers and online news sites.
