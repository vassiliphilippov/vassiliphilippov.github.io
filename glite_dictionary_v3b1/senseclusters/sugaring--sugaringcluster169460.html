sugaring

noun
coating with sugar
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌɡərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌɡərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of coating, glazing, or preserving food with sugar, especially to make it sweet or glossy

Examples

Sugaring fruit
The chef demonstrated sugaring by rolling the fruit in fine crystals before baking.
Traditional recipes often include sugaring to preserve berries for the winter months.
After sugaring, the pastries had a beautiful, shiny finish and a sweet taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candying - the process of preserving or glazing food with sugar
  • synonym: glazing - the act of coating food with a shiny or glossy substance
