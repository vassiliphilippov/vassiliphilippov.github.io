squeeze

noun
a firm grip or pressing action
2.2
Pronunciation: / skwiz / (AmE), / skwiːz / (BrE)
an act of gripping or pressing something firmly with the hand or fingers

Examples

A quick squeeze
A quick squeeze of the lemon added flavor to the dish.
He felt a reassuring squeeze on his shoulder as he stepped on stage.
She gave his hand a gentle squeeze before letting go.
