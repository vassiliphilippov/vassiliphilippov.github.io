proportioned

adjective
balanced in size
Pronunciation: / prəˈpɔrʃənd / (AmE), / prəˈpɔːʃənd / (BrE)
having parts or elements that are balanced in size, amount, or degree relative to each other

Examples

Well proportioned body
The artist created a proportioned sculpture that looked realistic from every angle.
A well-proportioned meal includes vegetables, protein, and grains in the right amounts.
The new building is beautifully proportioned, making it stand out in the city skyline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balanced - having parts in correct or pleasing proportions
  • antonym: disproportionate - not in correct or equal proportion
  • synonym: symmetrical - having similarity in size, shape, and position on both sides
