coarticulation

noun
overlapping movements
8.7
Pronunciation: /koʊ.ɑːr.tɪ.kjuː.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kəʊ.ɑː.tɪ.kjuː.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
