sharp-angled

adjective
having sharp corners or angles
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑrp ˌæŋɡəld / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːp ˌæŋɡəld / (BrE)
having sharp or acute corners or angles; not rounded or obtuse.

Examples

A sharp-angled object
Care should be taken when handling sharp-angled objects to avoid injury.
The artist preferred to use sharp-angled shapes in his modern paintings.
The furniture in the room was mostly sharp-angled, giving the space a contemporary feel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acute - having a sharp or severe angle; less than 90 degrees
  • antonym: obtuse - having a blunt or wider angle; more than 90 degrees
