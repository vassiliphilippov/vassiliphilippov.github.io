carpenter's mallet

noun
wooden mallet used by carpenters
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːr.pən.tɚz ˈmæl.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pən.təz ˈmæl.ɪt / (BrE)
