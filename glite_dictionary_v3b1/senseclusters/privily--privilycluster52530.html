privily

adverb
secretly; in a confidential manner
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪvɪli / (AmE), / ˈprɪvɪli / (BrE)
In a confidential or secret manner; secretly.

Examples

To privily arrange
He privily arranged the meeting, fearing it might cause public concern.
She whispered privily to her companion, careful not to be overheard.
The documents were privily removed from the office late at night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: openly - in an open or public manner
  • synonym: secretly - in a secret manner
