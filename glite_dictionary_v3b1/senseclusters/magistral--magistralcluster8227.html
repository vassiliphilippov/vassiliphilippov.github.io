magistral

adjective
custom-prescribed medicine
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmædʒɪstrəl / (AmE), / ˈmædʒɪstrəl / (BrE)
relating to a medicine prepared specifically according to a doctor's prescription rather than a standard formula

Examples

Magistral medicine
The doctor ordered a magistral ointment to be made for the patient's unique skin condition.
Unlike standard medications, a magistral preparation is tailored to the individual's needs.
Pharmacists must follow precise instructions when creating magistral solutions for patients.
