soil-building

adjective
crops that improve soil quality
6.3 4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsɔɪl.bɪl.dɪŋ/ /; / /sɔɪl ˈbɪl.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈsɔɪl.bɪl.dɪŋ/ /; / /sɔɪl ˈbɪl.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
(of crops) grown to improve and enrich the quality of the soil.

Examples

Soil-building crops
Farmers often plant soil-building crops like clover to enrich their fields naturally.
Rotating soil-building plants with regular crops can maintain soil health over many years.
Using soil-building legumes helps reduce fertilizer needs and supports sustainable agriculture.
