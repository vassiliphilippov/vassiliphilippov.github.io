Examples

Synonyms

  • residence
    2.5 14/m
    a residence is where someone lives long-term and appears in formal or legal contexts, while a dwelling names any living place.
  • abode
    5.8 1.90/m
    an abode sounds a bit old-fashioned or literary and means a home, while a dwelling is a neutral term for any living place.
  • home
    1.0 412/m
    a home feels like the place you belong with family and memories, while a dwelling simply means a place someone lives.
  • habitation
    5.1 0.77/m
    a habitation often refers to where people or animals live, sometimes in technical or legal texts, while a dwelling is a general living place.
