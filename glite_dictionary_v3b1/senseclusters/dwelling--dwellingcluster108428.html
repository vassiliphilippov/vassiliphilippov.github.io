dwelling

noun
place where someone lives
Pronunciation: / ˈdwɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdwɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
A place where someone lives; a house, apartment, or other residence.

Examples

A dwelling place
After years of saving, they purchased their own dwelling in the countryside.
Local laws regulate what kind of animals can be kept in a dwelling.
The small dwelling at the end of the road belongs to an artist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: home - the place where one lives permanently
  • synonym: housing - houses, apartments, etc., considered collectively
  • synonym: residence - a place where someone lives
