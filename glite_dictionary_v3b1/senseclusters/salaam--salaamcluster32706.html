salaam

noun
deep bow; Muslim salutation
2.8
Pronunciation: / səˈlɑm / (AmE), / səˈlɑːm / (BrE)
a deep bow of respect or a traditional Muslim greeting or gesture of salutation

Examples

A respectful salaam
He greeted the elder with a respectful salaam before entering the room.
In some cultures, it is customary to offer a salaam as a sign of respect.
The visitors responded to the host’s welcome with a graceful salaam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bow - a bending of the body as a sign of respect
  • synonym: greeting - an expression or gesture of welcome or salutation
