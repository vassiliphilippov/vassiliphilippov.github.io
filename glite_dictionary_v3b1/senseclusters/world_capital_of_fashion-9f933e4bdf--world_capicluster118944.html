world capital of fashion

noun
global center for fashion industry
Pronunciation: / wɝːld ˈkæp.ɪ.tl̩ əv ˈfæʃ.ən / (AmE), / wɜːld ˈkæp.ɪ.tl əv ˈfæʃ.ən / (BrE)
A city recognized as a global center of the fashion industry, influencing trends and style worldwide.

Examples

A world capital of fashion
Milan has long been considered a world capital of fashion, attracting designers and models from around the globe.
New York is proudly viewed as a world capital of fashion, especially during its internationally renowned Fashion Week.
Though many cities are stylish, only a few can truly claim the status of world capital of fashion.
