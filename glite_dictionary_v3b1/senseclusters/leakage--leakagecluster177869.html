leakage

noun
the escape of fluid or gas from a container
4.7 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈliː.kɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈliː.kə.dʒ/ (AmE), /ˈliː.kɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈliː.kə.dʒ/ (BrE)
