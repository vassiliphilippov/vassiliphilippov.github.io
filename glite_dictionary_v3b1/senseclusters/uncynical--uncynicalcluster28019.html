uncynical

adjective
sincere or trusting
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɪnɪkəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪnɪkəl / (BrE)
showing sincerity, trust, or innocence rather than doubt or suspicion

Examples

An uncynical person

Despite many disappointments, she remained uncynical and believed in people's good intentions.
