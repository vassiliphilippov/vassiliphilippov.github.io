uncynical

adjective
sincere or trusting
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɪnɪkəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪnɪkəl / (BrE)
showing sincerity, trust, or innocence rather than doubt or suspicion

Examples

An uncynical person
His uncynical approach to life inspired those around him to be more open and honest.
The film was praised for its uncynical portrayal of friendship and hope.
Despite many disappointments, she remained uncynical and believed in people's good intentions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cynical - distrustful of others' motives or sincerity
  • synonym: sincere - genuine and honest in feeling or expression
  • synonym: trusting - willing to rely on others; not suspicious
