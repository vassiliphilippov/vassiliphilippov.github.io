illustrate

verb
clarify by giving an example
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪləˌstreɪt /; / ˈɪl.əˌstreɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪləstreɪt /; / ˈɪl.ə.streɪt / (BrE)
