bedding geranium

noun
type of ornamental geranium plant
9.9 5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdɪŋ dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˈbɛdɪŋ dʒəˈreɪniəm / (BrE)
an upright geranium with scalloped leaves showing a broad colored zone near the margin, and white, pink, or red flowers, often grown as ornamental bedding plants.

Examples

Bright bedding geraniums
Every spring, she plants bright red bedding geraniums along the garden path.
The nursery offered a variety of bedding geraniums with leaves edged in contrasting colors.
White bedding geraniums can add a touch of elegance to any flower bed.
