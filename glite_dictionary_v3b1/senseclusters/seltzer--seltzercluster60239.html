Synonyms

  • carbonated water
    2.3 0.12/m
    carbonated water is water made fizzy by dissolved gas, while seltzer is the common, everyday name for that fizzy water.
  • sparkling water
    1.0 0.43/m
    sparkling water is often bottled or mineral water with fine bubbles and a fresh taste, while seltzer is plain fizzy water.
  • soda water
    1.2 0.21/m
    soda water can contain added salts or be flavored and used as a mixer, while seltzer is unsweetened plain carbonated water.
