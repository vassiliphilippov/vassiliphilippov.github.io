maimed

adjective
permanently injured or disabled
Pronunciation: / meɪmd / (AmE), / meɪmd / (BrE)
Having a part of the body permanently injured, crippled, or disabled.

Examples

Maimed war veteran
He returned from the war maimed but determined to rebuild his life.
She helped care for the maimed animals rescued from the wildfire.
The accident left several passengers permanently maimed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crippled - having a severe physical disability in a limb or limbs
  • antonym: unharmed - not injured or damaged
