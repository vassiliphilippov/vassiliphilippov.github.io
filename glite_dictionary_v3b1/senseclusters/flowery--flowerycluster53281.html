flowery

adjective
ornate or elaborate in speech or writing
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊəri / (AmE), / ˈflaʊəri / (BrE)
(of writing or speech) using elaborate, ornate, or decorative language, often with excessive detail or embellishment.

Examples

Flowery language
His writing is sometimes criticized as too flowery for scientific publications.
She preferred a more direct style, avoiding flowery language whenever possible.
The politician's flowery speech was filled with metaphors and elaborate expressions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ornate - (of style) elaborate or highly decorated
  • antonym: plain - (of style) simple and unadorned
