dirndl

noun
traditional Alpine dress with bodice and full skirt
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪrndəl /; / ˈdɜrndl̩ / (AmE), / ˈdɜːndl̩ / (BrE)
a traditional women's dress with a tight-fitting bodice and a full skirt, originally worn in Alpine regions of Germany, Austria, and Switzerland
