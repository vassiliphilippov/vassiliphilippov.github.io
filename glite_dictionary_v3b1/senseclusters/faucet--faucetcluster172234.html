faucet

noun
device for controlling water flow
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔsɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɔːsɪt / (BrE)
A device for controlling the flow of liquid, especially water, from a pipe or container.

Examples

Kitchen faucet
He turned on the faucet and waited for the water to heat up.
Please make sure the faucet is tightly closed after you use it.
The kitchen faucet has been leaking for days, and we need to fix it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tap - device for controlling the flow of liquid from a pipe (chiefly British English)
