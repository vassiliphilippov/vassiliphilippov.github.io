angelus bell

noun
bell rung for Angelus prayer
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈændʒələs bɛl / (AmE), / ˈændʒələs bɛl / (BrE)
the sound of a bell rung in Roman Catholic churches to signal the recitation of the Angelus prayer

Examples

The angelus bell rings
Hearing the distant angelus bell became a daily reminder of her childhood in the village.
The angelus bell rang at noon, inviting parishioners to pause for prayer.
The priest waited for the angelus bell before leading the congregation in reciting the prayer.
