glycosuria

noun
presence of sugar in urine
Pronunciation: / ˌɡlaɪkəˈsjʊriə / (AmE), / ˌɡlʌɪkəˈsjʊərɪə / (BrE)
the presence of abnormally high levels of sugar in the urine, often indicating diabetes or kidney problems

Examples

High glycosuria levels
Patients with glycosuria may also experience increased thirst and urination.
Routine tests revealed glycosuria, leading the doctor to suspect a possible case of diabetes.
The presence of glycosuria in a child's urine sample prompted further investigation into underlying health issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diabetes - a disease in which blood glucose levels are abnormally high
