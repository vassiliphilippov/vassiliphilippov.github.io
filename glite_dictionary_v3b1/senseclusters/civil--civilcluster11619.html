civil

adjective
polite, but not especially friendly
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪvəl / (BrE)
polite in a way that is adequate or minimally courteous but not overly friendly or warm

Examples

Civil conversation
Although the two colleagues disagreed, they remained civil during the meeting.
He was only civil to his neighbors, never making an effort to be friendly.
She gave a civil reply but did not try to continue the conversation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polite - showing good manners or consideration
  • antonym: rude - not showing respect or consideration
