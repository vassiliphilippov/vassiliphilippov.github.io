dice roll

noun
risky or unpredictable outcome/decision
4.8
Pronunciation: / daɪs roʊl / (AmE), / daɪs rəʊl / (BrE)
an action or decision whose outcome is uncertain or unpredictable, often involving risk

Examples

A dice roll decision
Investing in that startup was a real dice roll for her.
Sometimes picking a movie is a total dice roll and you end up loving it.
The weather during mountain climbing is always a bit of a dice roll.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gamble - an action involving risk or chance
  • antonym: sure thing - something certain to happen or succeed
