sand dollar

noun
flat, round sea urchin
Pronunciation: / sænd ˈdɑlər / (AmE), / sænd ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
a flat, round sea urchin with a rigid skeleton, typically found on sandy sea bottoms

Examples

A sand dollar shell
A sand dollar is often found washed up on the shore, resembling a silver coin.
Divers observed a live sand dollar partially buried in the sand on the ocean floor.
The children collected a sand dollar from the beach during their summer vacation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sea urchin - a spiny, globular marine animal related to the sand dollar
