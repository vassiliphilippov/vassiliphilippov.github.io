gac

noun
red fruit extract
9.9
Pronunciation: /gæk/ (AmE), /gæk/ (BrE)
a deep red oil or aril extracted from the gac fruit, used as a natural food coloring and in traditional medicine

Examples

Gac oil coloring
She added a spoonful of gac to the sticky rice to give it a vivid red color.
Traditional medicine sometimes uses gac for its supposed health benefits.
The vibrant color of the dish came from the addition of gac extract.
