categorically

adverb
absolutely and without qualification
Pronunciation: / ˌkætəˈɡɔrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌkatəˈɡɒrɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is clear and without any doubt or qualification; absolutely and explicitly.

Examples

To categorically refuse
He categorically refused to answer any more questions.
She categorically denied any involvement in the incident.
The spokesman categorically stated that the policy would not change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flatly - in a firm and unequivocal manner
  • synonym: unequivocally - in a way that leaves no doubt
