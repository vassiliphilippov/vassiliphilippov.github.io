ram

verb
strike or crash into forcefully
Pronunciation: / ræm / (AmE), / ræm / (BrE)
to strike or crash into something with great force

Examples

To ram a car
He accidentally rammed his bike into a parked car.
Protesters rammed a metal gate with heavy objects to break through.
The truck rammed the guardrail during the storm.
A truck rammed into the barriers, causing a loud crash.
He tried to ram the crate into the corner with the forklift.
The police had to ram the door open during the raid.

  • synonym: crash - to collide violently with an object or person, causing damage
  • synonym: slam - to shut or strike something forcefully and loudly
