untenanted

adjective
not occupied or leased by a tenant
5.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɛnən.tɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɛn.ə.tɪd / (BrE)
(of a property or land) not leased to or occupied by a tenant; vacant.

Examples

An untenanted house
After the previous tenants left, the apartment is completely untenanted again.
Large portions of the countryside remain untenanted to this day.
The old manor stood untenanted for years before being restored.

Related Terms

  • antonym: occupied - being used by someone; not vacant
  • synonym: vacant - not occupied by anyone; empty
