sillily

adverb
in a foolish way
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlɪli / (AmE), / ˈsɪlɪli / (BrE)
in a silly or foolish manner

Examples

To act sillily
The children laughed as their teacher acted sillily to get their attention.
He answered the question sillily, making everyone in the room smile.
She waved her arms sillily to cheer up her friend after a bad day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foolishly - in a way that lacks good sense or judgment
  • antonym: seriously - in a thoughtful or earnest manner
