virial

adjective
energy in particle systems
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪriəl / (AmE), / ˈvɪəriəl / (BrE)
relating to the energy, forces, or equilibrium in a system of particles, especially in physics or chemistry

Examples

Virial equation in physics
The virial coefficient helps describe the behavior of real gases at different temperatures.
Researchers used virial analysis to study the stability of the star cluster.
Understanding virial equilibrium is essential in astrophysics and thermodynamics.
