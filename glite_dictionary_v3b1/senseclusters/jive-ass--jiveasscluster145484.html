jive-ass

adjective
slang for foolish or pretentious person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪvˌæs / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪvˌas / (BrE)
(slang) Foolish, pretentious, or annoying, often in a way that is insincere or not to be taken seriously.

Examples

A jive-ass excuse
Don't listen to his jive-ass excuses; he never means what he says.
She called him a jive-ass for constantly bragging about things he never did.
That jive-ass attitude won't get you anywhere in this company.
