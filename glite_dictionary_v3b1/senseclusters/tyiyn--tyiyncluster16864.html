tyiyn

noun
unit of currency in Kyrgyzstan
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / tiÀàjin / (AmE), / tiÀàjiÀên / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Kyrgyzstan, equal to one one-hundredth of a som.
