plumbago

noun
form of graphite; industrial use
9.9
Pronunciation: / pləmˈbeɪgoʊ / (AmE), / plʌmˈbeɪgəʊ / (BrE)
A form of graphite, used as a lubricant and in the moderation of nuclear reactors.

Examples

Plumbago lubricant
In nuclear reactors, plumbago can serve as a neutron moderator.
The engineer applied plumbago to lubricate the machinery's moving parts.
The pencils were originally made using plumbago instead of modern graphite.
