transit

noun
a journey, especially by ship
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtræn.sɪt / (AmE), / ˈtræn.zɪt / (BrE)
a journey from one place to another, especially by ship

Examples

Ship transit
During the ship’s transit, the crew encountered several storms.
Insurance covers damage to goods during transit between ports.
The cargo was lost in transit across the Atlantic Ocean.
