dromos

noun
ancient passageway
Pronunciation: / ˈdroʊməs / (AmE), / ˈdrəʊmɒs / (BrE)
a passage or avenue leading to a tomb or ancient building, especially in ancient architecture

Examples

Tomb dromos entrance
The archaeologists discovered a long dromos leading to the burial chamber.
Visitors walked through the ancient dromos before entering the main temple.
The dromos was lined with statues that guided the way to the tomb.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entrance - a way into a place, such as a door or passage
