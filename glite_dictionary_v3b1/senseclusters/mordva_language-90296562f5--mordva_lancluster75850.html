Mordva language

noun
language spoken by the Mordvinians
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrdvə ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɔːdvə ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Uralic language or languages spoken by the Mordvinian people of Russia, comprising Erzya and Moksha.

Examples

Mordva language speakers
He is studying the Mordva language to communicate with speakers in the Volga region.
Linguists have documented the complex grammar of the Mordva language in recent years.
The Mordva language is divided into two major dialects: Erzya and Moksha.
