Botticelli

proper noun
Italian Renaissance painter
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːtəˈtʃɛli / (AmE), / ˌbɒtɪˈtʃɛli / (BrE)
(c. 1445–1510) Italian painter renowned for his mythological and religious works, including 'The Birth of Venus' and 'Primavera'.
