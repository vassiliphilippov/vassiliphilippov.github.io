maximizer

noun
audio loudness tool
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmæksɪˌmaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈmæksɪˌmaɪzə / (BrE)
a device or software tool in audio production that increases the perceived loudness of a sound by limiting its peaks

Examples

Audio maximizer plugin
The sound engineer applied a maximizer to make the final mix louder without distortion.
Many mastering plugins include a built-in maximizer for boosting track volume.
Using a maximizer can help your music stand out on streaming platforms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compressor - a device or software that reduces the dynamic range of audio
  • synonym: limiter - a device or software that prevents audio from exceeding a set level
