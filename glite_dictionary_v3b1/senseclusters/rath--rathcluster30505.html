rath

noun
ancient Irish fort
Pronunciation: / ræθ / (AmE), / rɑːθ / (BrE)
an ancient circular earthwork or fort, especially found in Ireland

Examples

Ancient rath
The archaeologists discovered a well-preserved rath on the outskirts of the village.
Many legends in Irish folklore are set around the mysterious rath on the hill.
Farmers often find ancient artifacts when plowing fields near a rath.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ringfort - a circular fortified settlement, especially in Ireland
