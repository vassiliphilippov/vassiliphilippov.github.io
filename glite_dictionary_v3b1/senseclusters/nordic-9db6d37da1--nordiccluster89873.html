Nordic

adjective
relating to Scandinavia or Northern Europe
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrdɪk / (AmE), / ˈnɔːdɪk / (BrE)
relating to Scandinavia, Finland, Iceland, and often the northern Germanic peoples or countries of Northern Europe.

Examples

Nordic countries
She participated in a Nordic skiing competition last winter.
The Nordic countries are known for their high quality of life and natural beauty.
The Nordic model of welfare is admired by many other nations.
