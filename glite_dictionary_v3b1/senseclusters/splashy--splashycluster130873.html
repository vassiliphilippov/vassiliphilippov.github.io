splashy

adjective
marked by splashing liquid
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsplæʃi / (AmE), / ˈsplæʃi / (BrE)
characterized by splashes of liquid, especially water, flying about in a disorderly or vigorous way

Examples

Splashy water fight
After a splashy water balloon fight, the backyard was soaked.
She wore a raincoat to protect herself from the splashy waves at the shore.
The children had a splashy good time playing in the fountain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: watery - full of or resembling water
