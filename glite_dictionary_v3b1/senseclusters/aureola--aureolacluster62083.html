aureola

noun
halo or aura
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈriːələ / (AmE), / ɔːˈriːələ / (BrE)
a surrounding atmosphere or quality that seems to come from and mark a person or thing, like an aura or halo of reputation

Examples

An aureola of fame
The artist painted a golden aureola around the saint's head in the mural.
Her kindness gave her an aureola that made everyone feel comfortable in her presence.
The scientist's discoveries created an aureola of brilliance that inspired young researchers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aura - a distinctive atmosphere or quality that seems to surround a person or thing
  • synonym: halo - a circle of light appearing to surround a person, often as a symbol of holiness
