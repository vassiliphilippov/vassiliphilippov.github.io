rowdily

adverb
in a noisy or disorderly way
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊdɪli / (AmE), / ˈraʊdɪli / (BrE)
In a loud, noisy, or disorderly manner.

Examples

To rowdily play
Children played rowdily in the backyard all afternoon.
Protesters marched rowdily through the city center, attracting police attention.
The fans cheered rowdily as their team scored the winning goal.
