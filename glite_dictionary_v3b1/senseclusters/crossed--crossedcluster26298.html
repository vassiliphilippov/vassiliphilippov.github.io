crossed

adjective
placed crosswise; intersecting
1.6 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / krÉ”st / (AmE), / krÉ’st / (BrE)
placed or arranged so that one part passes over or across another; lying or fitted crosswise.

Examples

Crossed arms
A pair of crossed swords decorated the family crest above the fireplace.
He answered her question with his fingers crossed behind his back.
She sat at her desk with her arms crossed, waiting for the meeting to begin.

Synonyms

  • crosswise
    4.0 0.00/m
    crosswise means lying or placed across at right angles, while crossed describes something with one part lying over another.

Related Terms

  • antonym: uncrossed - not crossed; arranged in a parallel or open position
