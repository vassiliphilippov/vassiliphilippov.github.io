mortgage holder

noun
person or institution holding a mortgage
4.4 0.32/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…îr…°…™d í Àåho äld…ôr / (AmE), / Ààm…îÀê…°…™d í Àåh…ô äld…ô / (BrE)
a person or institution that holds the legal right to a mortgage, often receiving payments from the borrower until the debt is repaid

Examples

The mortgage holder receives payments
It's important to notify your mortgage holder if you change your insurance provider.
The mortgage holder has the right to foreclose if the borrower defaults on the loan.
When we refinanced our house, a new bank became the mortgage holder.
