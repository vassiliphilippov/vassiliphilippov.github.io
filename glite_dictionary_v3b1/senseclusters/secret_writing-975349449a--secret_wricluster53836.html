secret writing

noun
writing in code or cipher
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːkrət ˈraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːkrɪt ˈraɪtɪŋ /; / ˈsiːkrət ˈraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
writing that uses codes or ciphers to keep messages hidden from people who are not supposed to read them
