koala

noun
Australian arboreal marsupial
1.0 1.14/m
Pronunciation: / ko äÀà…ëÀêl…ô / (AmE), / k…ô äÀà…ëÀêl…ô / (BrE)
a slow-moving, tailless Australian marsupial with grey fur and large ears that lives in trees and feeds on eucalyptus leaves and bark

Examples

A koala bear
Because of habitat loss, the koala population has declined in recent years.
Children at the zoo were excited to see the koala eating leaves.
The koala spent most of the day sleeping in the eucalyptus tree.

Synonyms

  • koala bear
    1.0 0.06/m
    koala bear is a common informal name adding 'bear', while koala is the standard term used by scientists and guides.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marsupial - mammal of the order Marsupialia, typically carrying young in a pouch
