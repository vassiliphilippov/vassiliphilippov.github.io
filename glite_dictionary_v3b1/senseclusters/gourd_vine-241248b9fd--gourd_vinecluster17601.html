gourd vine

noun
climbing plant of the gourd family
7.6
Pronunciation: / ɡɔrd vaɪn / (AmE), / ɡɔːd vaɪn / (BrE)
Any climbing or trailing plant of the gourd family (Cucurbitaceae) that produces fruits with hard, durable rinds.

Examples

A gourd vine plant
A single gourd vine can produce dozens of bottle gourds in one season.
Farmers rely on the gourd vine for its durable fruits and attractive foliage.
The gourd vine in our backyard covers the entire fence by midsummer.
