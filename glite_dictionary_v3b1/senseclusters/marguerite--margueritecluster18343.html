marguerite

noun
a type of daisy-like flowering plant
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑrɡjəˈrit / (AmE), / ˌmɑːɡjəˈriːt /; / ˌmɑːɡjʊˈriːt / (BrE)
a plant with daisy-like flowers, often grown in gardens for its white or pale yellow blooms
