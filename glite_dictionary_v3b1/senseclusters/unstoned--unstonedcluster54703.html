unstoned

adjective
without gemstones
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstoʊnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈstəʊnd / (BrE)
not set with gemstones or stones, especially referring to jewelry.

Examples

Unstoned ring
She preferred an unstoned band for its simple, classic look.
The jeweler showed him an elegant unstoned ring made of white gold.
Many people choose unstoned necklaces for a more understated style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plain - simple in design, without decoration
