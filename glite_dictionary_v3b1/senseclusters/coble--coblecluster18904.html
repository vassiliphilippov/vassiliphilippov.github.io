coble

noun
northern fishing boat
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒbəl / (BrE)
a flat-bottomed fishing boat with a high bow and stern, traditionally used in northern England and Scotland

Examples

A coble fishing boat
The fishermen rowed their coble out into the early morning mist.
A traditional coble is well suited to the rough waters off the North Sea coast.
Tourists admired the old wooden coble displayed at the harbor museum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dinghy - a small open boat, often used for fishing or recreation
  • antonym: trawler - a large fishing boat that drags a net through the water
