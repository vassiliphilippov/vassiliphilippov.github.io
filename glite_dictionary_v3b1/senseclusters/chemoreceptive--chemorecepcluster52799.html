chemoreceptive

adjective
detecting chemical stimuli

relating to or capable of detecting chemical stimuli, especially through chemoreceptors.

Examples

Highly chemoreceptive organs

Specialized chemoreceptive cells in the nose respond to airborne chemicals.
The olfactory system is a classic example of a chemoreceptive sense in mammals.
