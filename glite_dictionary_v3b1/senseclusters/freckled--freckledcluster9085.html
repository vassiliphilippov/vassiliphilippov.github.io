freckled

adjective
having small brownish skin spots
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛküld / (AmE), / ˈfrɛkld / (BrE)
having freckles on the skin; covered with small, light brown spots, often caused by exposure to the sun.

Examples

Freckled face
Her freckled face always reminded me of summertime adventures.
She smiled shyly, revealing her freckled cheeks.
The boy had freckled arms from spending hours outdoors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freckle - small, light brown spot on the skin
  • antonym: spotless - without spots or marks
