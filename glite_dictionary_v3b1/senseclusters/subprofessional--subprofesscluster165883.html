subprofessional

noun
worker below professional
7.3
Pronunciation: /sʌb.prə.ˈfeʃ.ə.nəl/ /; / /səb.prə.ˈfeʃ.ə.nəl/ (AmE), /sʌb.prə.ˈfeʃ.ə.nəl/ /; / /səb.prə.ˈfeʃ.ə.nəl/ (BrE)
a worker whose job duties or qualifications are below those of a fully qualified professional in a field

Examples

A subprofessional worker
The hospital relied on a subprofessional to handle basic patient care and administrative duties each day.
Many schools employ subprofessionals to assist teachers with classroom management and support tasks.
A subprofessional may perform routine work that does not require a full professional license.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paraprofessional - a person trained to assist professionals but not licensed as one
  • antonym: professional - a person with specialized training and qualifications in a profession
