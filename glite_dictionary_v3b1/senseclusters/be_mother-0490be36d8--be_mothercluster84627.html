be mother

verb
act as caretaker or server (esp. tea)
6.0
Pronunciation: / bi ˈmʌðɚ / (AmE), / bi ˈmʌðə / (BrE)
to act in a nurturing or caring role, especially by serving others (traditionally used when serving tea)

Examples

To be mother at tea
At tea time, someone always asks, 'Who will be mother and pour the tea?'
During the meeting, Tom volunteered to be mother and handed out the coffee.
She likes to be mother whenever friends come over for a chat.

Related Terms
