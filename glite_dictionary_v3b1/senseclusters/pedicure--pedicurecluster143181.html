pedicure

noun
professional foot and toenail care
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛdɪˌkjʊr / (AmE), / ˈpɛdɪˌkjʊə / (BrE)
a professional treatment for the feet and toenails, usually involving cleaning, shaping, and often painting the nails

Examples

A pedicure treatment
After a long hike, a relaxing pedicure felt wonderful.
I'm treating myself to a pedicure at the new salon this weekend.
She regularly gets a pedicure to keep her feet looking neat and healthy.
