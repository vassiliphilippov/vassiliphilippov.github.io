orthopedic

adjective
relating to orthopedics (medicine)
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrθəˈpidɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəˈpiːdɪk / (BrE)
relating to the branch of medicine concerned with the correction of deformities or functional impairments of the skeletal system and associated muscles, joints, and ligaments, especially the spine and joints.

Examples

Orthopedic shoes
He wears orthopedic shoes to support his arches.
The doctor recommended orthopedic surgery to correct her spinal curvature.
The hospital is renowned for its orthopedic department specializing in joint replacements.
After the accident, he required extensive orthopaedic treatment for his fractured spine.
She was prescribed orthopaedic shoes after her foot surgery.
