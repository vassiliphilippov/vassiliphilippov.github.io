breakability

noun
quality of being easily broken
Pronunciation: / ˌbreɪkəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌbreɪkəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being easily broken, damaged, or destroyed

Examples

Product breakability test
Manufacturers test products for breakability to ensure they are safe for consumers.
The breakability of the package contents was clearly labeled to alert the movers.
You should be aware of the breakability of glass when handling these dishes.
