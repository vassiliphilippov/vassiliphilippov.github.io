Chinese privet

noun
an evergreen shrub native to East Asia

Pronunciation: /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz ˈprɪ.vɪt/ /; / /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz ˈprɪ.vət/ (AmE), /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz ˈprɪ.vɪt/ /; / /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz ˈprɪ.vət/ (BrE)
