grocery list

noun
list of groceries to buy
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊsəri lɪst / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊsəri lɪst / (BrE)
a written or mental list of items to be bought at a grocery store, or by extension, any list of things to get or do

Examples

A grocery list of items
I forgot to add eggs to my grocery list before going to the store.
My phone has an app for creating a digital grocery list.
She always makes a grocery list to stay organized and avoid impulse buys.
He checked off each item as he found it on the grocery list in his hand.
She quickly jotted down eggs, milk, and bread on her grocery list before leaving for the store.
The manager presented the board with a grocery list of necessary improvements.
