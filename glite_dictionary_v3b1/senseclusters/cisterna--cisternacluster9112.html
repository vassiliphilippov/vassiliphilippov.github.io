cisterna

noun
fluid-filled cavity
9.8
Pronunciation: / sɪsˈtɜrnə / (AmE), / sɪsˈtɜːnə / (BrE)
a fluid-filled sac or cavity in the body, especially within cells or tissues

Examples

Golgi cisterna
The Golgi apparatus is made up of several cisternae that modify and package proteins.
A cisterna can store important substances within a cell until they are needed.
Scientists observed the movement of fluid inside the cisterna under a microscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cavity - an empty space within a solid object or body
  • synonym: vesicle - a small fluid-filled sac in a cell
