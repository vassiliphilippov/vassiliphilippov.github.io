membraned

adjective
having a membrane
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛmˌbreɪnd / (AmE), / ˈmɛmˌbreɪnd / (BrE)
having or covered by a membrane.

Examples

Membraned cell
The cell is membraned to protect its internal components.
Some eggs are membraned to prevent dehydration in dry environments.
The membraned wings of the insect help it glide silently through the air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: membranous - having the nature or appearance of a membrane
