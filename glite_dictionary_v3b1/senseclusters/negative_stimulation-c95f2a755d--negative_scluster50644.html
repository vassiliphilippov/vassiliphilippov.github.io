negative stimulation

noun
unpleasant or aversive influence or input
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛɡəˌtɪv ˌstɪmjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡətɪv ˌstɪmjʊˈleɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
unpleasant or aversive influence or input that causes antagonism, discomfort, or loss of interest

Examples

Negative stimulation effect
Children exposed to negative stimulation may develop avoidance behaviors in similar situations.
Prolonged negative stimulation can lead to disengagement and decreased performance at work.
The experiment measured participants' reactions to both positive and negative stimulation.
