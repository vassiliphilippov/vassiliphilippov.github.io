decompressing

noun
restoring compressed data (computing)

Pronunciation: /diː.kəm.ˈpres.ɪŋ/ /; / /də.kəm.ˈpres.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /diː.kəm.ˈpres.ɪŋ/ /; / /də.kəm.ˈpres.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
(computing) the process of restoring data from a compressed digital form to its original or usable form.
