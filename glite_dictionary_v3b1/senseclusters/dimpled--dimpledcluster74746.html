dimpled

adjective
with small indentations
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪmpəld / (AmE), / ˈdɪmpəld / (BrE)
having small, rounded indentations or depressions on the surface

Examples

Dimpled golf ball
The dimpled surface of the golf ball helps it travel farther through the air.
She smiled, revealing her dimpled cheeks to everyone in the room.
The old metal tray was dimpled from years of use and wear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pitted - marked with small holes or depressions
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
