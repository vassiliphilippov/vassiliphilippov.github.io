gun worm

noun
tool for clearing musket barrels
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡʌn wɜrm / (AmE), / ɡʌn wɜːm / (BrE)
a corkscrew-shaped tool used for extracting unspent charges from the barrel of a musket or firearm.

Examples

A gun worm tool
Before the invention of modern firearms, the gun worm was an essential part of every soldier's kit.
Collectors often display examples of the gun worm alongside other antique firearm tools.
The soldier used a gun worm to safely remove the cartridge from the musket barrel.
