coffee

noun
plant yielding coffee beans
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔfi / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
a tropical shrub or small tree of the genus Coffea, especially Coffea arabica or Coffea canephora, cultivated for its seeds that yield coffee beans

Examples

Coffee plant
Botanists studied the different species of coffee to improve bean quality.
Farmers in Brazil grow vast fields of coffee for export to the world market.
The coffee in her greenhouse was thriving, with new blossoms appearing every week.
