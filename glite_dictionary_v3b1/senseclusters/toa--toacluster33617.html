toa

noun
Polynesian warrior
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊ.ɑ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.ɑː / (BrE)
a warrior or champion, especially in Maori and other Polynesian cultures, admired for bravery and skill

Examples

A brave toa
The villagers honored the aged toa who had led them bravely in many battles.
In the ceremony, each young man aspired to become a respected toa in his community.
Legends tell of a fearless toa who protected the island from invaders.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
  • synonym: hero - a person admired for courage or noble qualities
  • synonym: warrior - a person who fights in battles or wars
