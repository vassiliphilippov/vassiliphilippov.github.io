venereal

adjective
relating to the sexual organs or sex-related disease
Pronunciation: / vəˈnɪriəl / (AmE), / vɪˈnɪəriəl / (BrE)
relating to sexual intercourse or the sexual organs, especially with reference to diseases transmitted sexually.

Examples

Venereal disease
A number of venereal viruses can be prevented through vaccination.
During the twentieth century, public awareness of venereal risks increased due to health campaigns.
Some infections, such as venereal diseases, require specialized medical treatment.
