day-to-day

adjective
happening or used every day
Pronunciation: / ˌdeɪ tə ˈdeɪ / (AmE), / ˌdeɪ tə ˈdeɪ / (BrE)
happening, used, or done every day; relating to the ordinary or routine aspects of daily life

Examples

We handle all the day-to-day operations of the business.
