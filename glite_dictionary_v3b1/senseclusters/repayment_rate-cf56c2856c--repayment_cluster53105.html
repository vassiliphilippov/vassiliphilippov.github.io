repayment rate

noun
rate of paying back money over time
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpeɪmənt reɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈpeɪmənt reɪt / (BrE)
the amount of money paid back over a particular period of time, especially in the context of loans or debts

Examples

High repayment rate
A higher repayment rate helps borrowers clear their debts faster.
The bank lowered the repayment rate to make the loan more affordable for customers.
The terms of the loan specify the repayment rate and the payment schedule.
