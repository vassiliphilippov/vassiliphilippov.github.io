viziership

noun
office of a vizier
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪziərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈvɪziəʃɪp / (BrE)
the position, office, or term of a vizier (a high-ranking political advisor or minister, especially in Muslim countries)
