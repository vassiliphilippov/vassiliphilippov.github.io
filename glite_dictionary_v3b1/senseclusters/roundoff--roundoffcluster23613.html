roundoff

noun
gymnastics move
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊndˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈraʊndˌɒf / (BrE)
a gymnastics movement like a cartwheel, but ending with both feet landing together facing the direction of entry

Examples

A perfect roundoff
The gymnast began her routine with a flawless roundoff on the mat.
He practiced his roundoff every day to improve his tumbling skills.
A strong roundoff is essential for connecting to a back handspring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cartwheel - a sideways rotary movement of the body with hands and feet touching the ground in turn
