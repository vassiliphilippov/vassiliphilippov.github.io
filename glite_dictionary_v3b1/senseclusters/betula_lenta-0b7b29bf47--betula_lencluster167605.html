Betula lenta

noun
species of birch tree from eastern North America
Pronunciation: / bəˈtʃuːlə ˈlɛntə / (AmE), / bɛˈtjuːlə ˈlɛntə / (BrE)
a species of birch tree native to eastern North America, with dark, spicy-scented bark and hard wood used for furniture and flavoring
