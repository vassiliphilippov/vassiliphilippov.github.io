mop

noun
floor-cleaning tool with absorbent head
Pronunciation: / mɑp / (AmE), / mɒp / (BrE)
a cleaning implement consisting of absorbent material attached to a handle, used for washing floors

Examples

A mop for floors
After the spill, he grabbed a mop to soak up the juice.
Every evening, the janitor uses a fresh mop to clean the hallways.
She dipped the mop into the soapy water before cleaning the kitchen floor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: broom - a long-handled brush used for sweeping floors
