two for two

noun
deal offering two items for a set price
3.8
Pronunciation: / tuː fəɹ tuː / (AmE), / tuː fə tuː / (BrE)
a promotional offer in which two items are sold together for a set total price, usually two units for two dollars or equivalent currency

Examples

Two for two deal
Many clothing stores run two for two promotions to attract more customers.
The supermarket advertised a two for two special on energy drinks this week.
We bought bagels during the two for two sale at our favorite bakery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: two for one - a promotional offer in which two items are sold for the price of one
