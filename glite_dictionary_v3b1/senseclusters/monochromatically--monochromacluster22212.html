monochromatically

adverb
using one color
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnəkroʊˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəkrəˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that uses or displays only one color or hue

Examples

To decorate monochromatically
The designer decorated the living room monochromatically to create a calm and unified atmosphere.
For her photography project, she shot all the portraits monochromatically to emphasize light and shadow.
The film was presented monochromatically, giving it a timeless and artistic feel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: colorfully - in a way that uses many colors
  • synonym: monochrome - a picture or design in one color or shades of one color
