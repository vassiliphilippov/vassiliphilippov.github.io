action mechanism

noun
part transmitting power in a mechanism
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.ʃən ˈmɛk.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈæk.ʃən ˈmɛk.ə.nɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the mechanical part of a device that transmits power and initiates movement, especially in musical instruments or machinery
