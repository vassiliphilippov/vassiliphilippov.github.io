thermochemical

adjective
heat in chemical reactions
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜːrmoʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the heat produced or absorbed during chemical reactions

Examples

Thermochemical reaction heat
The thermochemical properties of the compound were measured in the laboratory.
Scientists study thermochemical reactions to understand how energy changes during chemical processes.
A thermochemical equation shows the amount of heat involved in a chemical reaction.

  • synonym: exothermic - releasing heat during a chemical reaction
