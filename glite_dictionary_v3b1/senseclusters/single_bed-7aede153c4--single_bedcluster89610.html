single bed

noun
a bed for one person
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəl bɛd / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡəl bɛd / (BrE)
a bed designed for one person to sleep in

Examples

A single bed
He booked a room with a single bed for his business trip.
Many college dormitories provide each student with a single bed and a desk.
She prefers a single bed because it saves space in her apartment.

Related Terms
