adorableness

noun
quality of being adorable
5.3
Pronunciation: / əˈdɔɹ.ə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / əˈdɔː.rə.bəl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely attractive or charming in a way that inspires affection

Examples

The adorableness of the puppy melted everyone's heart.
The movie relies heavily on the adorableness of its animal characters to win over the audience.
