adhesively

adverb
using an adhesive
4.6
Pronunciation: / ədˈhiːsɪvli / (AmE), / ədˈhiːsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that sticks or attaches to a surface, usually using something sticky like glue

Examples

To adhesively attach labels
The labels were adhesively attached to each product for easy identification.
Tiles can be adhesively fixed to the wall instead of using nails or screws.
The artist adhesively mounted the photographs onto the display board.
The poster was attached adhesively to the wall and did not fall off.
She sealed the envelope adhesively to ensure its contents stayed secure.
The bandage stuck adhesively to his skin, making it difficult to remove.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glue - to join or attach with glue
  • antonym: loosely - in a way that is not firmly attached
  • antonym: mechanically - using physical means or devices rather than adhesives
  • synonym: stickily - in a sticky or adhesive manner
