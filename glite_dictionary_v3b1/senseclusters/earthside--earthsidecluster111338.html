earthside

adjective
facing the Earth
Pronunciation: / ˈɜːrθsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈɜːθsaɪd / (BrE)
on or facing the side of something that is toward the Earth, especially in relation to celestial bodies

Examples

Earthside surface
Astronomers studied the earthside features of the Moon using powerful telescopes.
The satellite's earthside antenna ensures a constant connection with ground stations.
From the observatory, we could only see the earthside of the distant planet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earthward - directed or facing toward the Earth
  • antonym: farside - the side of something that is farthest from the Earth
