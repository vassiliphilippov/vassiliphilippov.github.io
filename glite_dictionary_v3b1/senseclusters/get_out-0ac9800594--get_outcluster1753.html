get out

verb
(of news) become known
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt aʊt / (AmE), / ɡɛt aʊt / (BrE)
(of information or news) to become known by the public, especially when it was meant to be secret.

Examples

Once rumors get out, they are almost impossible to contain.
The surprise party plans managed to get out despite everyone's efforts to keep it a secret.
