snowmobile

noun
motor vehicle for travel on snow
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊmoʊˌbiːl / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊməʊˌbiːl / (BrE)
a motorized vehicle designed for travel on snow, typically having skis at the front and tracks at the rear

Examples

A snowmobile ride
Heavy snowfall meant that only a snowmobile could reach the remote cabin.
The guide led our group across the icy lake on his snowmobile.
We rented a snowmobile to explore the frozen landscape.
