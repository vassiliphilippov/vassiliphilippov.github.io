Examples

Synonyms

  • Olympic Games
    1.1 3.69/m
    Olympic Games is the full formal name for the modern international sports event, while Olympics is the shorter everyday name people use.
  • Olympiad
    6.3 0.55/m
    Olympiad refers to the four-year period or sometimes the event itself, while Olympics refers to the games held every four years.
