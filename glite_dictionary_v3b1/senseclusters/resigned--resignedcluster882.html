resigned

adjective
accepting without protest
4.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzaɪnd / (AmE), / rɪˈzaɪnd / (BrE)
accepting something unpleasant or undesirable without protest or resistance

Examples

Resigned look
He gave a resigned shrug when he heard the disappointing news.
With a resigned expression, she accepted the changes to her schedule.
They listened in resigned silence as the decision was announced.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defiant - openly resisting or refusing to obey
  • synonym: submissive - willing to accept orders or instructions without resistance
