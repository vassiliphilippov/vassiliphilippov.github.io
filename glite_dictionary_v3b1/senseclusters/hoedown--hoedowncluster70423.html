hoedown

noun
country dance event
6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊ.daʊn/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊ.daʊn/ (BrE)
a lively social event with country or folk music and dancing, often in a rural or Western setting

Examples

A big hoedown party
Everyone dressed in plaid shirts for the annual hoedown at the community center.
The lively music at the hoedown got people of all ages up and dancing.
After dinner, the guests enjoyed a traditional hoedown with square dancing and fiddles.
The local community organized a hoedown to celebrate the end of the harvest season.
Everyone dressed in plaid shirts and boots for the annual hoedown at the town hall.
Live country music and square dancing made the hoedown a memorable night for all ages.
The community center hosted a hoedown with live country music and square dancing.
At the annual hoedown, everyone enjoyed traditional dances and homemade food.
She learned to line dance at her first hoedown last summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barn dance - a social event with folk or country dancing, often held in a barn
