slitty

adjective
narrow or slit-like
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪti / (AmE), / ˈslɪti / (BrE)
having narrow openings or resembling a slit in shape or appearance

Examples

Slitty eyes
The cat's slitty eyes glowed in the darkness as it watched silently.
Light streamed through the slitty cracks in the old wooden door.
She wore a dress with a slitty neckline that added a touch of elegance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gaping - wide open, often in a way that is obvious or unpleasant
  • synonym: slit - a long, narrow opening or cut
