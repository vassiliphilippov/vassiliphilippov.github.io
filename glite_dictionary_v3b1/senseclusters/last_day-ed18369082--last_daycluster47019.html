Last Day

noun
Christian day of final judgment
Pronunciation: / læst deɪ / (AmE), / lɑːst deɪ / (BrE)
In Christian belief, the day at the end of time when God judges every human according to their deeds.

Examples

The Last Day of judgment
Artists throughout history have depicted scenes of the Last Day in their works.
Some Christians believe the resurrection of the dead will occur on the Last Day.
The preacher warned that everyone would be held accountable on the Last Day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Armageddon - the final battle at the end of the world, according to Christian belief
  • synonym: Judgment Day - the day of God's final judgment of all people
