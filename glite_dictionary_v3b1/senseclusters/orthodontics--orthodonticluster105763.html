orthodontics

noun
branch of dentistry for correcting teeth
8.7 4.1
Pronunciation: /ɔːr.θə.ˈdɑːn.tɪks/ (AmE), /ɔː.θə.ˈdɒn.tɪks/ (BrE)
the branch of dentistry that deals with the prevention and correction of irregularities of the teeth and jaws
