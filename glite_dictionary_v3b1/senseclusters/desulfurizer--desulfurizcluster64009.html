desulfurizer

noun
sulfur-removal device
Pronunciation: / diːˈsʌlfəˌraɪzər / (AmE), / diːˈsʌlfəraɪzə / (BrE)
a device or substance used to remove sulfur from a material, gas, or process

Examples

A desulfurizer device
The power plant installed a desulfurizer to reduce harmful emissions from burning coal.
A modern desulfurizer can significantly lower the sulfur content in industrial gases.
Technicians checked the desulfurizer regularly to ensure it was operating efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: purifier - a device that removes impurities from substances
  • synonym: scrubber - a device that removes pollutants from gases
