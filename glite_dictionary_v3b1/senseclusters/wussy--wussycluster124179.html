wussy

adjective
timid or weak
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊsi / (AmE), / ˈwʊsi / (BrE)

Examples

Wussy attitude
He was too wussy to try the roller coaster with his friends.
Don't be wussy about speaking up if you have a good idea.
She called him wussy for refusing to watch the scary movie.
