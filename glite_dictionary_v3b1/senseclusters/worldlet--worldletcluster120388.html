worldlet

noun
small planet
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrldlət / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldlɪt / (BrE)
a very small world or planet, especially in science fiction contexts

Examples

A distant worldlet
The explorers landed on a distant worldlet orbiting a red star.
Scientists discovered a frozen worldlet beyond the edge of the solar system.
In the novel, each worldlet had its own unique ecosystem and atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: planet - a large celestial body orbiting a star
  • synonym: planetoid - a small celestial body resembling a planet
