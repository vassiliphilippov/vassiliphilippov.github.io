isoelectronic

adjective
same electron structure
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsoʊɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəʊɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk / (BrE)
having the same number of electrons or the same electronic structure as another atom, ion, or molecule

Examples

Isoelectronic atoms
Neon and sodium ions are isoelectronic because they have the same number of electrons.
The oxide ion is isoelectronic with neon, sharing an identical electron configuration.
Scientists often compare isoelectronic species to understand trends in chemical properties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: isostructural - having the same or similar crystal structure
