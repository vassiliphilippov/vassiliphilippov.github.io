sportingly

adverb
in a sporting or fair manner
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔrtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner showing fairness, generosity, or a sportsmanlike spirit; in a sporting manner

Examples

Behave sportingly
Despite losing the match, the team behaved sportingly throughout.
He accepted his defeat sportingly and congratulated the winner.
She sportingly offered her place to a teammate who had never played before.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fairly - in a fair or just manner
  • antonym: unsportingly - in a manner not showing a sportsmanlike spirit
