wand

noun
image-editing selection tool
3.7
Pronunciation: / wɑnd / (AmE), / wɒnd / (BrE)
(in image-editing software) a selection tool that automatically selects parts of an image by grouping pixels with similar color, brightness, or tone; often called the “magic wand.”

Examples

Magic wand tool
She selected the blue sky quickly with the wand and filled it with a soft gradient.
Using the wand, he removed the product photo background before uploading the image.
Beginners often rely on the wand to select similar tones but must refine edges manually.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magic wand (tool) - another name for the wand selection tool that selects similar pixels automatically
  • synonym: selection tool - a tool in graphics software used to select areas of an image
