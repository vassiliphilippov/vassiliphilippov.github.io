coral tree

noun
ornamental shrub or tree of the genus Erythrina
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəl ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəl ˌtriː / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree of the genus Erythrina, with trifoliate leaves and racemes of scarlet to coral-red flowers, often grown as an ornamental plant

Examples

A coral tree flower
Farmers often plant coral trees as windbreaks and for shade in the fields.
The coral tree in her garden was covered in vibrant red blossoms every spring.
The distinctive flowers of the coral tree attract a variety of birds and insects.
