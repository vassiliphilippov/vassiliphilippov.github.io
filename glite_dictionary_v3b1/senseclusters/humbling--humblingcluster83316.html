humbling

adjective
causing humility or modesty
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌm.blɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʌm.blɪŋ / (BrE)
making you realize your own weaknesses, limitations, or lack of importance

Examples

A humbling experience
Climbing the mountain was a truly humbling experience, reminding us of our place in nature.
Receiving the award was humbling because she knew how much her team had helped her succeed.
The devastation left by the storm was humbling for the entire community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exalting - making someone feel very proud or happy; elevating in rank or status
  • synonym: humiliate - to make someone feel ashamed and lose the respect of others
