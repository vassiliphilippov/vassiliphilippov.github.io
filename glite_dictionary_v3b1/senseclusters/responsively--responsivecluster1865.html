responsively

adverb
in an adaptive way
4.5
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɑːnsɪvli / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒnsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that reacts or adapts quickly and appropriately to changes or needs, especially in technology or design

Examples

Website adapts responsively
The website adjusts responsively to different screen sizes, making it easy to use on any device.
She answered the customer's questions responsively, ensuring their concerns were addressed quickly.
Modern apps are designed to behave responsively when users interact with them in various ways.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adaptively - in a way that adjusts to new conditions
  • synonym: flexibly - in a way that is able to change or be changed easily
  • antonym: rigidly - in a way that does not change or adapt
