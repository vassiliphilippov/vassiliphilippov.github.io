responsively

adverb
in an adaptive way
4.5
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈspɑːn.sɪv.liː/ /; / /rə.ˈspɑːn.sɪv.liː/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈspɒn.sɪv.liː/ /; / /rə.ˈspɒn.sɪv.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that reacts or adapts quickly and appropriately to changes or needs, especially in technology or design

Examples

Modern apps are designed to behave responsively when users interact with them in various ways.
