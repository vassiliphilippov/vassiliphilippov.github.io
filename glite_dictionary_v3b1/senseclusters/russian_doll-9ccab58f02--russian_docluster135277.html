Russian doll

noun
set of nested wooden dolls from Russia
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌʃən dɑl / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃ(ə)n dɒl / (BrE)
a set of wooden dolls of decreasing size placed one inside another, originating from Russia

Examples

A Russian doll set
As a gift, he received a hand-painted Russian doll from his grandmother.
She placed the Russian doll on the mantelpiece, admiring its intricate design.
The children enjoyed taking apart the Russian doll to find the smaller ones hidden inside.
