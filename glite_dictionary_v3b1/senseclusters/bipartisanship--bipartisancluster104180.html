bipartisanship

noun
cooperation between parties
7.2
Pronunciation: / baɪˈpɑːrtɪzənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / baɪˈpɑːtɪzənʃɪp / (BrE)
cooperation or agreement between two political parties that usually oppose each other

Examples

Rare bipartisanship in Congress
The new law was passed thanks to rare bipartisanship in Congress.
Many citizens hope for more bipartisanship on important national issues.
Without bipartisanship, it is difficult to achieve lasting political reforms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cooperation - the process of working together for a common goal
  • antonym: partisanship - strong support for one political party, often without compromise
