curse

noun
an invocation of supernatural harm
4.7
Pronunciation: / kɝs / (AmE), / kɜːs / (BrE)
a supernatural spell or utterance intended to bring harm or punishment to someone or something

Examples

A curse on the house
He feared that he had brought a curse upon his family.
Many people believe that the tomb is protected by a curse.
The old woman placed a powerful curse on the village.
She feared the old book contained a powerful curse if anyone disturbed it.
The story describes a prince who lived under a curse for many years.
The villagers believed that a curse had been placed on the entire valley.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blessing - a prayer or utterance asking for divine protection or favor
  • synonym: hex - an evil spell or charm, especially in folklore
