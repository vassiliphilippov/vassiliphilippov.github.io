lead

noun
graphite and clay core in a pencil
1.0
Pronunciation: / lɛd / (AmE), / lɛd / (BrE)
a mixture of graphite and clay used as the marking core in a pencil, varying in hardness.

Examples

Pencil lead
Don't chew on the lead of your pencil—it's made of graphite, not real lead.
He replaced the broken lead in his mechanical pencil before finishing the drawing.
The lead in this pencil is softer, making it ideal for sketching.
