Rota

noun
Catholic Church's supreme appeals tribunal
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊtə / (AmE), / ˈrəʊtə / (BrE)
the highest ecclesiastical tribunal of the Roman Catholic Church, to which cases may be appealed from lower church courts

Examples

The Rota tribunal
He studied canon law before serving on the Rota in Rome.
Only the most complex cases are submitted to the Rota for judgment.
The case was finally brought before the Rota after several appeals in lower courts.
