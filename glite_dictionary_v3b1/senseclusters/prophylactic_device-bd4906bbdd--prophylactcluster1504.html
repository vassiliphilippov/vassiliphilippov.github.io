prophylactic device

noun
device used to prevent conception
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊfləˈklæktɪk dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ˌprɒfləˈklæktɪk dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
a device designed to prevent conception, such as a condom or diaphragm

Examples

A prophylactic device condom
Choosing the right prophylactic device is an important aspect of responsible sexual health.
She used a prophylactic device as a precaution against unwanted pregnancy.
The clinic provides information on every type of prophylactic device available.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contraceptive - a drug, device, or practice that prevents pregnancy
