premiership

noun
top sports league
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛmiɚˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈprɛmiəʃɪp / (BrE)
the highest division or league in a particular sport, especially football in some countries

Examples

The premiership title
After years of hard work, the team finally reached the premiership this season.
Many young athletes aspire to compete in the premiership of their sport.
The club was relegated from the premiership after a disappointing year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: championship - a contest for the position of champion or the highest league
  • degree lower: division - a group of teams in a sports league ranked by level
