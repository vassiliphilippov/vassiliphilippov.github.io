motionlessness

noun
lack of movement or motion
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊʃənləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈməʊʃənləsnəs / (BrE)
the state of being completely still, without any movement

Examples

Complete motionlessness
She maintained perfect motionlessness during the meditation exercise.
The motionlessness of the forest at dawn was almost eerie.
The predator relied on its motionlessness to avoid detection by its prey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immobility - the state of not moving or being unable to move
  • antonym: movement - the act of moving
  • synonym: stillness - the absence of movement; calmness
