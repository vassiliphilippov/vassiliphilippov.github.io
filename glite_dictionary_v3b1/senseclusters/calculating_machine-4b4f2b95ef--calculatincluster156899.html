calculating machine

noun
device for mathematical calculations
1.5 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkælkjəˌleɪtɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈkælkjʊˌleɪtɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
