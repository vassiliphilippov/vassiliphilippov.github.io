popularizer

noun
someone who makes ideas accessible to the public
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːpjələˌraɪzər / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊləˌraɪzə / (BrE)
a person who makes a subject, idea, or activity appealing or accessible to the general public
