obsessed

adjective
preoccupied or controlled by a strong emotion or idea
4.1
Pronunciation: / əbˈsɛst / (AmE), / əbˈsɛst / (BrE)
having a very strong and persistent interest or concern about someone or something

Examples

To be obsessed with cars
He's completely obsessed with vintage cars and spends every weekend working on them.
Lately, she has become obsessed with learning new languages.
The media seemed obsessed with the actor's private life.
Ever since she read that book, she has been obsessed with learning more about the author.
He became obsessed with perfecting his painting technique.
The media seemed obsessed with the celebrity's private life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addicted - physically or mentally dependent on a particular substance or activity
  • antonym: indifferent - having no particular interest or concern; apathetic
  • synonym: preoccupied - Engrossed in thought or absorbed by something to the exclusion of other concerns
  • antonym: unconcerned - Not interested or worried; indifferent
