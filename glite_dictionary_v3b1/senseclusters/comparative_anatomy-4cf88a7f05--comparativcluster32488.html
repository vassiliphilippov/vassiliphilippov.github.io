comparative anatomy

noun
study of anatomical differences between species
Pronunciation: / kəmˌpɛrətɪv əˈnætəmi / (AmE), / kəmˌparətɪv əˈnatəmi / (BrE)
the scientific study of similarities and differences in the anatomical structures of different species of animals

Examples

Comparative anatomy study
Comparative anatomy reveals how certain structures have adapted to different functions in various species.
She is conducting research in comparative anatomy to understand evolutionary relationships among mammals.
The textbook includes detailed diagrams to illustrate principles of comparative anatomy.
