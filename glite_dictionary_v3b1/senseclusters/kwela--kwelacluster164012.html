kwela

noun
South African dance music with pennywhistle
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɛl.ə / (AmE), / ˈkwɛl.ə / (BrE)
A type of danceable, jazzy music originating in South Africa, characterized by the use of a pennywhistle and influenced by jazz and traditional rhythms.

Examples

Kwela music style
Many jazz musicians cite kwela as a major influence on their style.
She learned to play the pennywhistle to join a local kwela band.
The energetic rhythms of kwela filled the streets during the festival.
