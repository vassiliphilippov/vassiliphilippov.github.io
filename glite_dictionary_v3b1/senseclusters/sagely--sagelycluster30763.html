Synonyms

  • wisely
    2.3 7.03/m
    wisely describes practical good choices in everyday life, while sagely suggests calm, thoughtful advice often like an older, experienced person.
  • sagaciously
    8.9 0.03/m
    sagaciously shows keen, shrewd judgment in difficult matters and decisions, while sagely gives calmer, more reflective advice.
  • sapiently
    9.3 0.00/m
    sapiently sounds formal and bookish, showing learned wisdom in writing, while sagely feels more natural, calm, and practical.
  • judiciously
    6.5 0.82/m
    judiciously means using good care and caution in decisions, while sagely suggests wise, calm thought rather than cautious planning.
  • insightfully
    4.8 0.25/m
    insightfully means seeing deep, clear truths about a problem, while sagely focuses on calm, wise, experience-based advice.
