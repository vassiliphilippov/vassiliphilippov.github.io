shyster

noun
dishonest or unethical person, esp. lawyer or politician
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaɪstər / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪstə / (BrE)
a person, especially a lawyer or politician, who behaves in a dishonest, unscrupulous, or unethical way to achieve a goal

Examples

A shyster lawyer
After being cheated by a shyster, she decided to handle her legal issues herself.
Everyone warned me not to trust that shyster of a politician with my vote.
The company hired a shyster to help them escape legal trouble, but it backfired.

Related Terms

  • synonym: con artist - person who deceives others for personal gain
  • synonym: crook - dishonest person, often involved in crime
  • antonym: honest - free of deceit and untruthfulness; sincere
