diagramming

noun
the act of making diagrams or charts
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəˌɡræmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəɡræmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of representing information, ideas, or systems by means of diagrams or charts

Examples

Good diagramming of data
Good diagramming of data flow can help clarify how each part of the organization functions.
Software tools make diagramming complex systems much more efficient than drawing by hand.
The teacher spent hours diagramming the process on the whiteboard for her students.
