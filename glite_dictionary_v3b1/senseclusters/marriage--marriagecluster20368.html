marriage

noun
close union or joining of things
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmærɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmarɪdʒ / (BrE)
a close or intimate union or joining of two things, especially in a harmonious or effective way

Examples

A marriage of ideas
Her novel is a marriage of wit and wisdom.
The marriage of classical architecture and modern design created a unique building.
This dessert is a perfect marriage of sweet and sour flavors.
