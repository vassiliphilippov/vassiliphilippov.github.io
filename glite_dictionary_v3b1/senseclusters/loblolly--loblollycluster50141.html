loblolly

noun
pine tree or swamp
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːbˌlɑːli / (AmE), / ˈlɒbˌlɒli / (BrE)
a type of pine tree native to the southeastern United States, or a muddy, swampy area, especially in the southern US

Examples

A muddy loblolly
The loblolly is one of the most common pines in the southeastern United States.
After the heavy rain, the field turned into a muddy loblolly that was hard to cross.
Many birds build their nests in the tall branches of the loblolly.

  • synonym: swamp - an area of low, wet, muddy ground
