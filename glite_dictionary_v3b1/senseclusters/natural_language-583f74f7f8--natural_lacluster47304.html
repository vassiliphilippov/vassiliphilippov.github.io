natural language

noun
a language used by humans for communication
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃ.ɚl ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈnætʃ.rəl ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a language spoken or written by humans for general communication, as opposed to artificial or computer languages

Examples

Natural language learning
Children learn their first natural language without any formal instruction.
English, Chinese, and Arabic are all examples of natural languages.
Machine translation systems attempt to translate from one natural language to another.

Related Terms

  • antonym: computer language - an artificial language used to communicate with computers
