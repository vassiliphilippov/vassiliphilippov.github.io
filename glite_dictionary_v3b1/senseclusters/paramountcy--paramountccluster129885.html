paramountcy

noun
supreme authority; preeminence
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrəˌmaʊn(t)si / (AmE), / ˈparəmaʊnsi / (BrE)
the state or condition of being paramount; supreme authority or preeminence

Examples

The paramountcy of power
During colonial rule, the British claimed paramountcy over several princely states in India.
He argued for the paramountcy of environmental concerns in policy-making.
The paramountcy of the constitution is recognized in all democratic nations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: primacy - the state of being first in importance or rank
  • synonym: supremacy - the state of being supreme or having the highest authority
