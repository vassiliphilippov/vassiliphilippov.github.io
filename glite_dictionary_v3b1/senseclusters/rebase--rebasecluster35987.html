rebase

verb
to refit denture base
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbeɪs / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbeɪs / (BrE)
to replace or refit the base of a denture so that it fits the mouth more closely

Examples

To rebase dentures
The dentist recommended that she rebase her dentures to improve their comfort and fit.
After several years of use, it became necessary to rebase the lower denture.
Patients are often advised to rebase their dentures when the fit becomes loose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reline - to add new material to the fitting surface of a denture
