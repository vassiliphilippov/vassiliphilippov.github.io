fall

noun
the autumn season (esp. in US English)
Pronunciation: / fɑl / (AmE), / fɔːl / (BrE)
(North American) The season of the year between summer and winter, when leaves fall from the trees; autumn.

Examples

In the fall leaves drop
Many students return to school in early fall each year.
The trees are especially beautiful in the fall, with their red and gold leaves.
We went apple picking in the fall when the weather turned cool.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autumn - the season between summer and winter, used in British English
