cassino

noun
a card game involving matching and capturing cards
2.5
Pronunciation: / kəˈsiːnoʊ / (AmE), / kəˈsiːnəʊ / (BrE)
a card game in which players match cards in their hands with cards facing up on the table to capture them, aiming to score points.

Examples

Play cassino cards
Every Saturday night, our family gathers around to play cassino for fun.

The tournament featured several classic card games, including cassino and bridge.
