steppe

noun
treeless grassland plain
Pronunciation: / stɛp / (AmE), / stɛp / (BrE)
a large, flat, unforested grassland, especially in southeastern Europe or Siberia

Examples

The steppe grassland
Herds of wild horses roamed freely across the vast steppe.
Many nomadic tribes once lived on the Eurasian steppe.
The Russian steppe experiences extreme temperatures throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prairie - extensive flat grassland, especially in North America
  • synonym: savanna - grassy plain in tropical and subtropical regions
