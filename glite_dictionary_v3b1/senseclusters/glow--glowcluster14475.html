glow

noun
a warm or positive feeling
Pronunciation: / ɡloʊ / (AmE), / ɡləʊ / (BrE)
a strong feeling of warmth, pleasure, or satisfaction

Examples

A warm glow
After hearing the good news, a warm glow spread across his face.
She felt a glow of pride as her name was announced.
The room was filled with the glow of friendship during the celebration.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chill - a feeling of coldness or lack of warmth (also emotionally)
  • synonym: radiance - a warm, bright, or glowing quality or feeling
