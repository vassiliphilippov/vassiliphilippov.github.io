sleep late

verb
to wake up later than usual
Pronunciation: / sliːp leɪt / (AmE), / sliːp leɪt / (BrE)
to remain asleep longer than is usual or expected, especially in the morning

Examples

To sleep late in morning
After staying up all night studying, he slept late the next day.
I usually sleep late on weekends when I don't have to work.
She sleeps late during holidays and enjoys a relaxed morning.
