open

verb
to have an opening to another space
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.pən / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.pən / (BrE)
(of a room, door, window, etc.) to have an opening, passage, or outlet to another space.

Examples

Room opens onto garden

Several smaller rooms open off the main corridor.
The balcony doors open into the living room, letting in plenty of light.
