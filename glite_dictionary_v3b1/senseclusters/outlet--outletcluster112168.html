outlet

noun
shop selling goods to the public
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊt.lɛt / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.lɛt / (BrE)
A shop or place of business where goods are sold to the public, especially one focusing on a particular brand or discounted items.

Examples

Factory outlet store
Many tourists visit the outlets to find bargains on well-known labels.
The new shopping center includes an outlet for designer clothing brands.
They opened an outlet in the city to sell excess inventory at lower prices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shop - a place where goods are sold to customers
  • synonym: store - a retail establishment selling items to customers
