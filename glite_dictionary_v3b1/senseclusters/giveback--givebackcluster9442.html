Examples

Customer giveback
The company announced a giveback to reward loyal customers with a special discount.
Shareholders were pleased with the unexpected giveback in the form of a cash dividend.
The government offered a tax giveback to stimulate consumer spending during the recession.

Synonyms

  • refund
