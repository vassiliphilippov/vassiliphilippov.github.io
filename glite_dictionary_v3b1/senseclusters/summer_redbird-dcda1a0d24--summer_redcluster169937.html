summer redbird

noun
North American songbird with red male and yellow female
9.5 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌm.ər ˈred.bɜːrd/ (AmE), /ˈsʌm.ə ˈred.bɜːd/ (BrE)
a bird of the middle and southern United States, with the male having deep rose-red plumage and the female mostly yellow; also known as the summer tanager
