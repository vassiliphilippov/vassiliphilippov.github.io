rhinologic

adjective
of the nose
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌraɪnəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌraɪnəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the nose or the medical study and treatment of nasal diseases

Examples

Rhinologic nasal diseases
The hospital has a department dedicated to rhinologic disorders and treatments.
She published a paper on rhinologic advances in allergy management.
A rhinologic examination revealed a chronic sinus infection.
