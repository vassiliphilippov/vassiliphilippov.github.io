dominicker

noun
barred-feathered chicken
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑmɪˌnɪkər / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪˌnɪkə / (BrE)
a chicken with black-and-white barred feathers, especially one of the Dominique breed

Examples

A dominicker chicken
The old barn was home to a proud dominicker rooster that crowed every morning.
She admired the unique black-and-white pattern on her favorite dominicker hen.
At the county fair, his prize-winning dominicker attracted a lot of attention from visitors.

Related Terms
