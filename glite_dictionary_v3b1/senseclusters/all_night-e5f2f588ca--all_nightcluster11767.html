all night

adverb
throughout the entire night
Pronunciation: / ɔl ˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈnaɪt / (BrE)
For the entire duration of the night; from evening until morning.

Examples

Study all night
He studied all night to prepare for the big exam.
The store is open all night for last-minute shoppers.
They danced all night at the wedding reception.
