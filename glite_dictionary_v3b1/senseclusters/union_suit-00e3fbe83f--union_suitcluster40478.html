union suit

noun
one-piece undergarment
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːniən suːt / (AmE), / ˈjuːniən suːt / (BrE)
A one-piece undergarment combining a shirt and drawers, usually with long sleeves and legs, originally worn as underwear.

Examples

A union suit garment
He laughed when he realized he had put his union suit on backwards.
My grandfather used to wear a union suit during the winter to keep warm.
The classic red union suit with buttons is a staple of old cartoons.
