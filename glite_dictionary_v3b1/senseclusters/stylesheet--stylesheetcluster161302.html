stylesheet

noun
style and formatting guide
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪlˌʃiːt / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlʃiːt /; / ˈstaɪlˌʃiːt / (BrE)
a document or file that sets rules for the style, formatting, or appearance of writing, documents, or webpages

Examples

A stylesheet file
The publisher updated its stylesheet to include new rules for digital content.

If you want to change the font size, edit the stylesheet directly.
A single stylesheet can control the look of multiple pages on a site.

Many designers create a custom stylesheet to give their site a unique appearance.
