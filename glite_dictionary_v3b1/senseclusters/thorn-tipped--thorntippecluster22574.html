thorn-tipped

adjective
ending in a thorn
Pronunciation: / θɔrn tɪpt / (AmE), / θɔːn tɪpt / (BrE)
(of a plant or part of a plant) ending in or having a tip that is a thorn.

Examples

Thorn-tipped plant leaves
Many desert plants are thorn-tipped to protect themselves from herbivores.
The thorn-tipped leaves of this bush can easily scratch your skin.
The botanist described the new species as having thorn-tipped branches.
