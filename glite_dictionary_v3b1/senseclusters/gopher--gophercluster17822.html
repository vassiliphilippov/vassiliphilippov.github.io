gopher

noun
a burrowing rodent with cheek pouches
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊ.fər /; / ˈgoʊfər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ.fər /; / ˈɡəʊfə / (BrE)
a burrowing rodent with cheek pouches, found in North and Central America

Examples

A gopher tunnel
A gopher popped its head out of the tunnel in the backyard.

Watching the gopher pop up from its burrow delighted the children.
