spirit

noun
distilled alcoholic drink
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
a strong alcoholic drink made by distilling fermented substances such as grains, fruits, or vegetables

Examples

A glass of spirits
The restaurant serves a variety of spirits, including whiskey, vodka, and gin.
He prefers to drink spirits rather than beer when celebrating special occasions.
Many countries have their own traditional spirits made from local ingredients.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beer - an alcoholic drink made from fermented grains, usually lower in alcohol than spirits
  • synonym: liquor - a strong alcoholic drink distilled from grains or other plants
  • antonym: wine - an alcoholic drink made from fermented grapes or other fruits
