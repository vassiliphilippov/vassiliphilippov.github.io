beaverboard

proper noun
fiberboard brand name
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːvərˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbiːvəbɔːd / (BrE)
a trademarked brand name for a type of fiberboard used for wall paneling and building construction

Examples

Beaverboard brand panels
The walls of the cottage were covered with beaverboard to give a smooth finish.
During renovations, they discovered old beaverboard panels behind the wallpaper.
Many homes built in the early 20th century used beaverboard for interior walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drywall - a panel made of gypsum used for interior walls
  • synonym: fiberboard - a type of engineered wood product made from wood fibers
