sternohyoid

adjective
of the neck muscle
Pronunciation: / ˌstɜrnoʊˈhaɪɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌstɜːnəʊˈhaɪɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to the sternohyoid muscle or its fibers in the neck, which connects the sternum and hyoid bone

Examples

Sternohyoid fibers in neck
The sternohyoid muscle plays a role in swallowing by lowering the hyoid bone.
During the procedure, the doctor identified the sternohyoid fibers to avoid damaging them.
An injury to the sternohyoid area can affect neck movement and speech.
