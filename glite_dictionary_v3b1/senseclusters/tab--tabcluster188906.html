tab

noun
tablet of medicine (informal)
Pronunciation: / tæb / (AmE), / tab / (BrE)
a small, solid dose of medicine, usually shaped like a pellet, designed for oral administration; a tablet.

Examples

A tab of medicine
Always check the dosage before taking an unfamiliar tab.
He swallowed the antibiotic tab with a glass of water.
The doctor gave me a tab to take every night before bed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tablet - a small, solid dose of medicine for swallowing
