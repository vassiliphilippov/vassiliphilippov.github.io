Trionyx

noun
genus of soft-shelled turtles
9.9
Pronunciation: / traɪˈɑːnɪks / (AmE), / traɪˈəʊnɪks /; / traɪˈɒnɪks / (BrE)
a genus of soft-shelled freshwater turtles (the type genus of the family Trionychidae), found in Africa and Asia, including both living and fossil species
