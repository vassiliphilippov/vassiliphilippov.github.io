heart of the home

noun
central, most important part of a home
Pronunciation: / hɑrt əv ðə hoʊm / (AmE), / hɑːt əv ðə həʊm / (BrE)
the central or most important part of a home, often referring to a person, place, or thing that provides warmth, comfort, or focus to family life

Her warm personality makes her the true heart of the home for her family.
The kitchen is often considered the heart of the home where everyone gathers for meals and conversation.
