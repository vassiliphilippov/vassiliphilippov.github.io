retrace

verb
to go back over the same path
Pronunciation: / riˈtreɪs / (AmE), / rɪˈtreɪs / (BrE)
to go back over the same route, path, or sequence that was previously taken

Examples

To retrace your steps

Detectives often retrace the victim's movements to gather clues about a crime.
The hiker decided to retrace the trail to find his lost backpack.
