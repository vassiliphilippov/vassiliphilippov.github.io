condoler

verb
to express sympathy
8.8
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈdoʊ.lər/ (AmE), /kən.ˈdəʊ.lə/ (BrE)
to express sympathy or sorrow to someone who is grieving or suffering a loss

Examples

To condoler with friends
He would condoler with families who had lost loved ones, offering gentle words of comfort.
In old letters, people often condoler with friends after tragic events.
The priest made time to condoler with the grieving widow after the funeral.
