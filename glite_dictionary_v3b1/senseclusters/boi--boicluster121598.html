boi

noun
masculine-presenting person
Pronunciation: / bɔɪ / (AmE), / bɔɪ / (BrE)
a person, especially in queer communities, who expresses a masculine gender style or identity, often used by young masculine-presenting lesbians, transgender men, or non-binary people
