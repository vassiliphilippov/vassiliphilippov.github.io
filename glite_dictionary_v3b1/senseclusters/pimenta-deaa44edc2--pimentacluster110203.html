Pimenta

noun
genus of tropical trees; allspice tree
9.5
Pronunciation: /pɪ.ˈmen.tə/ (AmE), /pɪ.ˈmen.tə/ (BrE)
A genus of tropical trees in the myrtle family, including the allspice tree (Pimenta dioica), native to Central America and the West Indies.
