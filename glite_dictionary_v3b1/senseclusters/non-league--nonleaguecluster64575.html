non-league

adjective
outside main league
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈliːɡ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈliːɡ / (BrE)
not belonging to the main professional sports league, especially in football (soccer)

Examples

Non-league football team
He joined a non-league team after leaving the professional club.
Many talented players start their careers in non-league football.
The cup match featured a non-league side against a top-division team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amateur - not professional; done for enjoyment rather than as a job
  • antonym: league - a group of sports clubs that compete with each other
