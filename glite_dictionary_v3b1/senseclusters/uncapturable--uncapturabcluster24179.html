uncapturable

adjective
impossible to capture
8.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkæptʃərəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkæptʃərəbl / (BrE)
impossible to capture, record, or represent accurately, especially in a photo, video, or other medium

Examples

An uncapturable moment
The beauty of the sunset was uncapturable no matter how many photos she took.
Some emotions are so deep they feel uncapturable in words or music.
The magic of that night was truly uncapturable and lived only in their memories.

Related Terms

  • antonym: capturable - able to be captured or recorded
  • synonym: elusive - difficult to find, catch, or achieve
  • synonym: inexpressible - impossible to express in words or other forms
