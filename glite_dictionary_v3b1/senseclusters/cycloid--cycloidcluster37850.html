cycloid

noun
mathematical curve traced by a rolling circle
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪˌklɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈsaɪ.klɔɪd / (BrE)
a curve traced by a point on the circumference of a circle as it rolls along a straight line without slipping
