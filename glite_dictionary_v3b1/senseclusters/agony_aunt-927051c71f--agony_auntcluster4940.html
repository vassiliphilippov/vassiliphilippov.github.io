agony aunt

noun
female advice columnist
Pronunciation: / ˈæɡəni ænt / (AmE), / ˈæɡəni ɑːnt / (BrE)
a woman who writes a column in a newspaper or magazine, giving advice to people who write in about their personal problems

Examples

Agony aunt column
Many newspapers have a dedicated agony aunt to address readers' personal dilemmas.
She became a popular agony aunt after offering thoughtful advice on relationships and family issues.
The teenager sent a letter to the agony aunt hoping for guidance about her friendship troubles.
