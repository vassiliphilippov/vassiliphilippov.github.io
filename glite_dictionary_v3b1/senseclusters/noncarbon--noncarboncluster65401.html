noncarbon

noun
inorganic material
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈkɑrbən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkɑːbən / (BrE)
a material or substance that does not contain carbon atoms, typically referring to inorganic materials.

Examples

Noncarbon material
The new engine uses a noncarbon composite to withstand extreme heat.
Scientists are exploring noncarbon alternatives for safer battery technology.
A noncarbon filter was installed to improve water purification efficiency.
