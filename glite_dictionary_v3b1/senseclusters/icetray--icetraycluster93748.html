icetray

noun
tray for making ice cubes
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsˌtreɪ / (AmE), / ˈaɪsˌtreɪ / (BrE)
A tray with compartments used to make cubes of ice in a refrigerator or freezer.

Examples

An icetray with cubes
I filled the icetray with water before putting it in the freezer.
She popped a few cubes from the icetray into her drink.
The plastic icetray cracked when I twisted it to get the ice out.
