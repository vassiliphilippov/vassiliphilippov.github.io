clinicopathological

adjective
clinical and pathological
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌklɪnɪkoʊˌpæθəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌklɪnɪkəʊˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to both the clinical and pathological aspects of a disease or medical condition

Examples

Clinicopathological findings
The clinicopathological findings helped doctors understand the progression of the illness.
Researchers compared the clinicopathological characteristics of different patient groups.
A detailed clinicopathological analysis was performed to guide treatment decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clinical - relating to the observation and treatment of patients
  • synonym: pathological - relating to the study of disease
