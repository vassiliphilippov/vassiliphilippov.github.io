pignut

noun
edible woodland tuber
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡˌnʌt / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡˌnʌt / (BrE)
a small edible tuber found in woodlands, or the plant that produces it

Examples

Wild pignut tuber
While walking in the woods, we found a pignut and tasted its nutty flavor.
The children dug up a pignut beneath the old oak tree during their nature lesson.
Some animals rely on the pignut as a food source in the forest.
