spear up

verb
thrust upward like a spear
Pronunciation: / spɪr ʌp / (AmE), / spɪə ʌp / (BrE)
to rise or thrust upward like a spear

Examples

To spear up sharply
A solitary pine branch speared up against the evening sky.
New buildings spear up on the city skyline every year.
Several tall grasses spear up along the edge of the pond.
