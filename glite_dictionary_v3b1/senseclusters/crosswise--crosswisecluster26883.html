crosswise

adjective
lying or placed across; transverse
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔs.waɪz /; / ˈkrɔsˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkrɒs.waɪz /; / ˈkrɒsˌwaɪz / (BrE)
lying or placed across something; arranged at right angles or transversely

Examples

Crosswise lines
He noticed a pattern of cracks running crosswise over the surface.
She cut the fabric crosswise to make strips for quilting.
The beams were laid crosswise to provide extra support.
A long beam was placed crosswise on top of the supports.
He drew a line crosswise across the page to divide it in half.
The tablecloth had stripes running crosswise rather than lengthwise.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lengthwise - in the direction of the length
  • antonym: longitudinal - placed lengthwise rather than across
  • synonym: transverse - lying or extending across something
