Cystophora cristata

noun
hooded seal of Arctic and Atlantic waters
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˌstɑfərə krɪˈsteɪtə / (AmE), / sɪˌstɒfərə krɪˈsteɪtə / (BrE)
A medium-sized blackish-grey seal with a large inflatable sac on its head, native to Arctic and northern Atlantic waters; also known as the hooded seal.
