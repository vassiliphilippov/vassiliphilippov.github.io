polisher

noun
tool for buffing surfaces
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑː.lɪ.ʃər/ (AmE), /ˈpɒ.lɪ.ʃə/ (BrE)
a person or machine that makes a surface smooth and shiny by rubbing or buffing it

Examples

Electric polisher
After waxing the car, he went over it with an electric polisher to make it shine.
Industrial floors are often maintained with a heavy-duty floor polisher.
The woodworker used a polisher to give the table a glossy finish.
The polisher made the wooden floor gleam like new.
She used an electric polisher to shine her shoes before the interview.
A skilled polisher can restore old silverware to its original luster.
The worker used a polisher to restore the shine on the marble floor.
She bought a new polisher to keep her wooden furniture looking bright.
A professional polisher can make old silverware look almost new again.
