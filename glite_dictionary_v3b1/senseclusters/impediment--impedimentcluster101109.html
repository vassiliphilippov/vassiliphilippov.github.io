impediment

noun
obstacle or barrier to progress
5.7
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɛdəmənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɛdɪmənt / (BrE)
something that makes progress or action difficult or slow

Examples

A impediment to progress
Bureaucratic delays can become an impediment to timely decision making.
His nervousness proved to be an impediment during the interview.
Lack of funding is a serious impediment to the project's success.
Heavy traffic can be a serious impediment during morning commutes.
His fear of public speaking became an impediment to his career advancement.
The lack of funding was a major impediment to the project's completion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aid - something that helps or promotes progress
  • synonym: barrier - a structure or circumstance that prevents movement or access
  • synonym: hindrance - a thing that provides resistance, delay, or obstruction
  • synonym: obstacle - something that blocks or hinders progress
