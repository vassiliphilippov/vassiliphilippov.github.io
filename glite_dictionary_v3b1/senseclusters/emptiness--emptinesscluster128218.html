emptiness

noun
quality of being valueless or futile
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmptinəs / (AmE), / ˈɛmptinɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having no value, purpose, or meaning; futility

Examples

A deep emptiness
After years of chasing wealth, he was left with a profound sense of emptiness.
Many critics commented on the emptiness of the movie's storyline.
She was troubled by the emptiness at the core of her daily routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: futility - the quality of being pointless or useless
  • antonym: meaningfulness - the quality of having meaning or value
