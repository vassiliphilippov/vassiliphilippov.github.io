degree

noun
unit of temperature measurement
Pronunciation: / dɪˈɡri / (AmE), / dɪˈɡriː / (BrE)
A unit of measurement for temperature on a specified scale, such as Celsius or Fahrenheit.

Examples

Ten degrees Celsius
It was below zero degrees Celsius during the hike.
She set the oven to 180 degrees before baking the cake.
The doctor measured my fever at 102 degrees Fahrenheit last night.
