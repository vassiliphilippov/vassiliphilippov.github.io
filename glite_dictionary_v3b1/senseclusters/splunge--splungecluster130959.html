splunge

noun
noisy splashy fall
Pronunciation: / splʌndʒ / (AmE), / splʌndʒ / (BrE)
a noisy plunge or fall into water or another liquid, producing a large splash

Examples

A splunge into water
The diver made a dramatic splunge into the pool, sending water everywhere.
With a loud splunge, the dog jumped into the muddy pond after the ball.
Everyone laughed when the watermelon landed in the river with a huge splunge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plunge - a sudden fall or jump into water or another substance
  • synonym: splash - the sound or action of something hitting liquid and causing water to fly about
