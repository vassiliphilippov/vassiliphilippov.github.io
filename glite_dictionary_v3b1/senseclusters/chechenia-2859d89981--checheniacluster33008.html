Chechenia

proper noun
autonomous republic in southwestern Russia
Pronunciation: / tʃɛˈtʃɛniə / (AmE), / tʃɛˈtʃiːniə / (BrE)
an autonomous republic in southwestern Russia, located in the northern Caucasus Mountains and known for its declaration of independence in the 1990s and subsequent conflict with Russia; also known as Chechnya
