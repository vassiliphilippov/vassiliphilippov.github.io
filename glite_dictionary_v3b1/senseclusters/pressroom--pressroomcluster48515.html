pressroom

noun
room with presses
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpres.ruːm/ (AmE), /ˈpres.ruːm/ (BrE)
a room in a factory, workshop, or print shop where mechanical presses are operated for printing or shaping materials

Examples

Factory pressroom
The new pressroom was equipped with state-of-the-art printing machines for faster production.

After the tour, the students watched the machines in the pressroom shape sheets of metal.
