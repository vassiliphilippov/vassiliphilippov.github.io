mysid

noun
shrimp-like crustacean
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪsɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɪsɪd / (BrE)
a small shrimp-like marine or freshwater crustacean belonging to the family Mysidae

Examples

A mysid shrimp
The scientist observed a group of mysids swimming near the surface of the lake.
Many fish species feed on mysid populations in coastal waters.
The aquarium displayed several mysids in a special tank for visitors to see.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crustacean - an aquatic animal with a hard shell, such as a crab or shrimp
  • synonym: shrimp - a small, slender marine crustacean
