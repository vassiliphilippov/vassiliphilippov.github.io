nail

noun
thin metal fastener hammered into materials
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /neɪl/ (AmE), /neɪl/ (BrE)
a thin, pointed piece of metal, usually with a flat head, that is hammered into wood or other materials to fasten them together

Examples

Hammer a nail
Be careful not to step on a rusty nail in the yard.
He hammered a nail into the wall to hang the picture.
The carpenter used a nail to fasten the boards together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: screw - a metal fastener with a helical ridge used to join materials
