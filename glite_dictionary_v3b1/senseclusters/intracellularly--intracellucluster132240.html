intracellularly

adverb
inside a cell
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈsɛljələrli / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈsɛljʊləli / (BrE)
in a manner that takes place inside a cell

Examples

Multiply intracellularly
The bacteria multiply intracellularly, making them harder to eliminate with antibiotics.
Some drugs are designed to act intracellularly to target specific cellular processes.
The protein is transported intracellularly before it is secreted from the cell.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extracellularly - in a manner that takes place outside a cell
