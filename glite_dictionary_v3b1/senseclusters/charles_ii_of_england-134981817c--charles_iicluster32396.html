Charles II of England

proper noun
King of England during the Restoration
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrlz səˈkənd əv ˈɪŋɡlənd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːlz ðə ˈsɛkənd ɒv ˈɪŋɡlənd / (BrE)
