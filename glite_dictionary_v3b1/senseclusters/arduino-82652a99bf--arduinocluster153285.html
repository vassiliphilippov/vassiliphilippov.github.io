Arduino

proper noun
electronics company
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈduːinoʊ / (AmE), / ɑːˈduːiːnəʊ / (BrE)
the company or organization that designs, manufactures, and supports Arduino hardware, software, and its community

Examples

Arduino company team
The Arduino team released a new open-source board for hobbyists and educators.
Many schools use Arduino products to teach students about electronics and programming.
Arduino has built a large community of makers and developers around the world.
