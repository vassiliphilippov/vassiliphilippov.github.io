inebriated

adjective
drunk from alcohol
9.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˈniː.bri.eɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ɪˈniː.brɪ.eɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
having drunk too much alcohol; drunk.

Examples

To be inebriated
After several drinks, she became visibly inebriated and started to sing loudly.
He was so inebriated that he could barely stand upright.
The police questioned the inebriated driver after the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intoxicated - affected by alcohol or drugs, drunk
  • antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; not drunk
