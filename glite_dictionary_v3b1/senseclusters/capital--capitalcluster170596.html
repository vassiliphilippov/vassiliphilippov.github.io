capital

noun
leading center for a specific activity
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈkapɪtəl / (BrE)
a place regarded as a leading center for a specified activity or product

Examples

Fashion capital
New York is often called the fashion capital of the world.
The city quickly became the tech capital of the region, attracting startups from everywhere.
With its lively music scene, Austin is hailed as the live music capital of the United States.
