skelp

noun
hard slap or blow
9.9
Pronunciation: / skɛlp / (BrE)
a slap or hard blow, especially with the open hand, chiefly in Scottish English

Examples

A quick skelp
He gave his brother a quick skelp on the back of the head for teasing him.
The teacher threatened a skelp if anyone misbehaved in class.
After the argument he felt the sting from the sudden skelp across his cheek.

Related Terms

  • antonym: punch - a forceful blow with a closed fist
  • synonym: slap - a sharp blow with the open hand
