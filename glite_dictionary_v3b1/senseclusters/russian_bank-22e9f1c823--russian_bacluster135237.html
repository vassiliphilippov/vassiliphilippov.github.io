Russian bank

noun
two-player card game using separate decks
7.7 7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌʃ.ən bæŋk/ /; / /ˈrʌʃ.ən beɪŋk/ (AmE), /ˈrʌʃ.ən bæŋk/ /; / /ˈrʌʃ.ən beɪŋk/ (BrE)
A card game for two players, each using a separate deck, in which the object is to get rid of all one's cards by playing them onto foundation piles.

Examples

Russian bank card game
Russian bank requires each player to focus on their own cards while racing to build the foundations.
He taught his sister how to play Russian bank using two decks of cards.
We spent the evening playing Russian bank by the fireplace.
