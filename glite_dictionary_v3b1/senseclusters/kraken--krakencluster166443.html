kraken

noun
powerful force
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɑːkən / (AmE), / ˈkrɑːkən / (BrE)
a powerful or dangerous force, person, or organization, often used figuratively to suggest something large and uncontrollable

Examples

Corporate kraken rises
After the merger, the company became a kraken in the technology sector.
Many feared the new law would unleash a regulatory kraken on small businesses.
The team unleashed their inner kraken during the championship game and dominated their opponents.
