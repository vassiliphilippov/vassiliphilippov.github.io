padrón

noun
official register
9.8
Pronunciation: / pɑːˈðɾoʊn / (AmE), / paˈðɾɒn / (BrE)
an official register or list of residents or voters, especially in Spanish-speaking countries

Examples

City padrón list
The local government maintains a padrón to keep track of all residents in the city.
Before elections, officials update the padrón to ensure all eligible voters are included.
You must be listed in the padrón to access certain public services in Spain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: register - an official list or record of names or items
  • synonym: roll - an official list of names, especially of voters or members
