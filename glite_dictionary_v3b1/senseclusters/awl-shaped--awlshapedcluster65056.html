awl-shaped

adjective
narrow and pointed, like an awl
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɔl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈɔːl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
narrow and tapering to a point like an awl; shaped like an awl.

Examples

Awl-shaped point

The cactus has awl-shaped leaves that taper to a sharp point.
The tool's awl-shaped tip made it ideal for piercing leather.
