positivistic

adjective
relating to positivism (philosophy)
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːzətɪˈvɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɒzətɪˈvɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of positivism, especially as a philosophical approach that emphasizes observable facts and scientific methods.

Examples

A positivistic approach
His arguments were criticized for being too positivistic and ignoring moral considerations.
Modern sociology often distances itself from positivistic methods, favoring interpretive approaches.
The researcher adopted a positivistic approach to ensure objectivity and clarity in the findings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: positivist - a person who adheres to the philosophy of positivism
