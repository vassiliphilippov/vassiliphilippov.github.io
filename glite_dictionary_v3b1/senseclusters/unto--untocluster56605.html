unto

preposition
concerning; with regard to
8.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtu / (AmE), / ʌnˈtuː / (BrE)
concerning or with regard to; used to indicate reference or relation, especially in older or formal English.

Examples

Speak unto the people
She addressed her remarks unto the matter of school funding during the meeting.
The king spoke unto his people with great authority and wisdom.
Let your actions be guided unto kindness and understanding at all times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: about - on the subject of; concerning
  • synonym: regarding - concerning; with respect to
