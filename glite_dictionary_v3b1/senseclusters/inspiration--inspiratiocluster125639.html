inspiration

noun
the act of inhaling
6.4 3.4
Pronunciation: /ɪn.spə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪn.spɪ.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪn.spə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪn.spɪ.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of drawing air or other gases into the lungs; inhalation, especially as part of the process of breathing

Examples

Inspiration of air
During strenuous exercise, the rate of inspiration increases to supply more oxygen to the body.
His deep inspiration was followed by a slow, controlled expiration.
The doctor measured the patient's rate of inspiration and expiration during the examination.
