wounding

noun
the act of inflicting a wound
3.5 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈwuːn.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈwuːn.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
The act of causing an injury that cuts or breaks the skin or body; the process of inflicting a wound.

Examples
