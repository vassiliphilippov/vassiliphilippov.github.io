draw

noun
card(s) taken or dealt in a game
Pronunciation: / drɔ / (AmE), / drɔː / (BrE)
(in card games) a playing card or cards taken from the deck that are dealt to a player.

Examples

A draw card
After the draw, she decided to fold her hand.
He was lucky with his draw and ended up with a straight flush.
You can discard two cards and request a new draw.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deal - the distribution of cards to players in a card game
