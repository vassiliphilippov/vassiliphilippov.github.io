bastide

noun
French country house
Pronunciation: / bæˈstid / (AmE), / bæˈstiːd / (BrE)
a large country house or farmhouse in southern France, especially in Provence, often used as a rural estate or manor

Examples

Provence bastide house
They spent their vacation in a beautifully restored bastide overlooking the vineyards.
The old bastide was surrounded by fields of lavender and olive trees.
Many artists have found inspiration while staying in a traditional Provençal bastide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farmhouse - a house attached to a farm, especially in the countryside
  • synonym: villa - a large and luxurious country house
