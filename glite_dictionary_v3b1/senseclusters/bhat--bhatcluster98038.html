bhat

noun
South Asian bard
9.9 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / b…ët / (AmE), / b…ëÀêt / (BrE)

Examples

Bhat storyteller

A respected bhat was invited to compose a poem for the royal celebration.
In some villages, the bhat is responsible for keeping important historical records.
