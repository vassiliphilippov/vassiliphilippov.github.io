bhat

noun
South Asian bard
Pronunciation: / bɑt / (AmE), / bɑːt / (BrE)
a member of a traditional South Asian community of bards, genealogists, or poets who preserve and recite family histories

Examples

Bhat storyteller
The bhat recited the family's genealogy at the wedding, tracing ancestors back generations.
A respected bhat was invited to compose a poem for the royal celebration.
In some villages, the bhat is responsible for keeping important historical records.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bard - a poet and storyteller, especially in ancient times
  • synonym: genealogist - a person who studies or traces family ancestries
