bosie

noun
deceptive cricket ball (googly)
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊzi / (AmE), / ˈbəʊzi / (BrE)
A cricket ball bowled so as to appear to break in one direction but actually breaks in the opposite direction; a googly.

Examples

A bosie ball
He learned to bowl a bosie after years of practice.
Many cricketers find it challenging to read a bosie coming out of the bowler's hand.
The spinner surprised the batsman with a perfectly disguised bosie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: googly - a ball in cricket that looks like a normal leg break but moves in the opposite direction
