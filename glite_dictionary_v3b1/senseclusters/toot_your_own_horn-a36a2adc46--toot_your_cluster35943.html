toot your own horn

verb
boast about oneself
9.4
Pronunciation: / tuːt jʊr oʊn hɔrn / (AmE), / tuːt jɔːr əʊn hɔːn / (BrE)
to boast or brag about your own achievements or abilities

Examples

To toot your own horn loudly
If you never toot your own horn, people might not notice your strengths.
It's okay to toot your own horn when you've worked hard on a project.
She doesn't like to toot her own horn, but her accomplishments speak for themselves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blow your own trumpet - to boast about your achievements (chiefly British)
