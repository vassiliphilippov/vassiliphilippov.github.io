schwannoma

noun
benign nerve tumor
9.9 0.15/m
Pronunciation: /  Éw…ëÀêÀàno äm…ô / (AmE), /  Éw…ôÀàn…ô äm…ô / (BrE)
a usually benign tumor that develops from Schwann cells (cells that cover nerves) in the nervous system

Examples
