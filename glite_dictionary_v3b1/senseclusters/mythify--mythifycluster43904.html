mythify

verb
to make legendary
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪθɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪˌfaɪ / (BrE)
to make something or someone seem like a myth or legendary figure, often by exaggerating their qualities or achievements

Examples

To mythify a hero
The documentary tried to mythify the life of the famous explorer, making him seem larger than life.

Historians warn against efforts to mythify political leaders, as it can distort the truth.
