physis

noun
bone growth plate
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfaɪ.sɪs/ /; / /ˈfiː.sɪs/ (AmE), /ˈfaɪ.sɪs/ /; / /ˈfiː.sɪs/ (BrE)
the growth plate in a long bone where new bone is produced in children and adolescents

Examples

Child's physis injury
Damage to the physis can result in abnormal bone growth in children.
Doctors monitor the physis to ensure healthy development during adolescence.
An X-ray showed that the physis was still open, indicating the patient was still growing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epiphyseal plate - the cartilage plate at the ends of long bones where growth occurs
