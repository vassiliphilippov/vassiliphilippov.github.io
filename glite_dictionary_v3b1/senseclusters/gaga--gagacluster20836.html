gaga

adjective
mentally weakened with age
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːɡɑː / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːɡɑː / (BrE)
No longer able to think or behave normally because of old age; feeble-minded or mentally confused, especially due to age.

Examples

Gaga in old age
After turning ninety, her great-uncle became a bit gaga and often forgot familiar faces.
Don't mind her odd stories—she's gone gaga in her old age.
The children were amused by their gaga grandfather's eccentric behavior.

Related Terms

  • synonym: senile - showing weaknesses of old age, especially mental decline
  • antonym: vigorous - strong and healthy, especially mentally or physically
