view

noun
digital view count
3.0 4.13/m
Pronunciation: / vjuË / (AmE), / vjuË / (BrE)
the recorded number of times an online item (such as a video, webpage, or post) has been watched, loaded, or otherwise accessed; a view count used to measure online engagement.

Examples

A video view
The band's new single has reached ten million views across all streaming platforms this week.
Her tutorial gained thousands of views within hours, pushing it onto the site's front page.
Advertisers examine the number of views to judge how popular a video campaign is.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impression - a metric for how many times content or an ad is displayed to users (related but sometimes measured differently)
  • synonym: view count - the total number of times a piece of online content has been viewed
