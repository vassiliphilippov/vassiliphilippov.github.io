cry than wool

x
more fuss than substance
Pronunciation: / kraɪ ðæn wʊl / (AmE), / kraɪ ðan wʊl / (BrE)
used to describe a situation where there is a lot of noise or complaint but little actual substance or result

Examples

More cry than wool
Despite all her objections, it was more cry than wool at the meeting.
His threats were simply cry than wool and nobody took them seriously.
When the team protested the decision, it turned out to be just cry than wool.

Related Terms

  • synonym: all bark and no bite - saying a lot without taking real action
