let out a curse

verb
utter a curse or swear word
3.8
Pronunciation: / lɛt aʊt ə kɝs / (AmE), / lɛt aʊt ə kɜːs / (BrE)
to utter a curse or swear word, especially as an expression of anger, frustration, or pain

Examples

To let out a curse loudly
Frustrated by his mistake, he let out a curse under his breath.
She accidentally dropped the plate and let out a curse in the kitchen.
Whenever he stubs his toe, he can't help but let out a curse.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bless - to speak words of approval or wish good upon someone, opposite of cursing
  • synonym: swear - to use offensive language, especially as an expression of anger
