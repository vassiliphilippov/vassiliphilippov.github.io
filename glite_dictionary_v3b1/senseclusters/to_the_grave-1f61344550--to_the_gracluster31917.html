to the grave

adverb
keep something secret until death
5.1
Pronunciation: / tə ðə ɡreɪv / (AmE), / tə ðə ɡreɪv / (BrE)
to keep (something) secret until death; never to reveal it while alive

Examples

Keep secret to the grave
He took her confession to the grave, never telling another soul.
She swore she would keep the truth to the grave.
Some secrets are so painful that people carry them to the grave.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carry to the grave - to keep a secret for one's whole life
