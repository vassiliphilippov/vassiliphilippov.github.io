stotinka

noun
Bulgarian currency unit, 1/100 lev
9.9
Pronunciation: / stoˈtɪŋkə / (AmE), / stɒˈtɪŋkə / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Bulgaria, equal to one hundredth of a lev.

Examples

A stotinka coin
Coins of one stotinka are rarely used in daily transactions.

The price of the postcard was fifty stotinka.
