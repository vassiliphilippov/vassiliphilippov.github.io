backcourt

noun
rear court area
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbæk.kɔːrt/ (AmE), /ˈbæk.kɔːt/ (BrE)
the area at the rear of a court in racket sports, near the baseline and farthest from the net

Examples

Backcourt rally
She moved quickly to the backcourt to return the deep shot.
A strong serve can push your opponent into the backcourt and give you an advantage.
Players often practice hitting from the backcourt to improve their defensive skills.

  • antonym: net - the central dividing barrier in racket sports
