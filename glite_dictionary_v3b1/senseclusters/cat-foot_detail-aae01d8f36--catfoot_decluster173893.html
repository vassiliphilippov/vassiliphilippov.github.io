cat-foot detail

noun
ornamental paw-shaped furniture detail
4.9
Pronunciation: / kæt fʊt diːteɪl / (AmE), / kat fʊt diːteɪl / (BrE)
a decorative element in furniture design that resembles the paw of a cat, often carved at the foot of a table or chair leg

Examples

cat-foot detail on furniture
Restorers carefully cleaned the intricate cat-foot detail on the damaged table.
The antique dresser featured a delicate cat-foot detail at the base of each leg.
You can recognize Victorian-era chairs by their distinctive cat-foot detail.

Related Terms
