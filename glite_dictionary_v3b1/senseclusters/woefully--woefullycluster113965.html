woefully

adverb
in a very bad or serious way
Pronunciation: / ˈwoʊ.fəli / (AmE), / ˈwəʊ.fəli / (BrE)
In a way that is very bad, serious, or deplorable; to a degree that causes concern or regret.

Examples

To be woefully unprepared
She was woefully unprepared for the difficult exam.
The building's safety measures were woefully inadequate for an emergency situation.
The newly released app was woefully lacking in essential features.

Related Terms

  • antonym: happily - in a fortunate or pleasing manner
  • synonym: sadly - in an unfortunate or regrettable manner
