environmental impact

noun
effect on the environment from an activity
1.0 3.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəl ˈɪmpækt / (AmE), / ɪnˌvʌɪrənˈmɛntəl ˈɪmpakt / (BrE)

Examples
