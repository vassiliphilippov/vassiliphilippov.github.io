cheapo

noun
low-quality item
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːpəʊ / (BrE)
an object that is inexpensive and often of low quality, especially one considered cheap in a negative way

Examples

A cheapo umbrella
He bought a cheapo umbrella that broke the first time it rained.
The hotel room was a real cheapo, but at least it was clean.
I would rather save up than waste money on another cheapo gadget.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bargain - something bought for less than the usual price
  • synonym: junk - something of little value or poor quality
  • antonym: luxury - something expensive and of high quality
