fashion

noun
way something is done
Pronunciation: / ˈfæʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈfæʃ.ən / (BrE)
a particular or usual way of doing something; a manner or method

Examples

In quick fashion
She always completes her assignments in a timely fashion.
The ceremony was conducted in an old-fashioned fashion.
The report was presented in a highly detailed fashion.
It was his fashion to take a walk every morning before breakfast.
She answered all questions in her usual fashion, with calm confidence.
The students worked in their own fashion, each using a different method.

Related Terms

  • synonym: custom - a traditional and widely accepted way of behaving or doing something
  • synonym: manner - a way in which something is done or happens
  • synonym: mode - a particular way or style of doing something
  • synonym: style - a distinctive way of doing something
