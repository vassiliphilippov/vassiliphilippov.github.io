Mesoamerica

proper noun
region including central Mexico and Central America
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛsoʊəˈmɛrɪkə / (AmE), / ˌmɛsəʊəˈmɛrɪkə / (BrE)
A region extending from central Mexico through Central America, known for its ancient civilizations before Spanish colonization.

Examples

Mesoamerica ancient civilizations
Mesoamerica was home to many advanced pre-Columbian societies.
Archaeologists have found remarkable ruins throughout Mesoamerica.
The ancient Maya civilization flourished in Mesoamerica for centuries.
