fudge packer

noun
offensive slang term; derogatory insult
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌdʒ ˌpækər / (AmE), / ˈfʌdʒ ˌpakə / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) An insulting term used to refer to a homosexual man; also, an offensive or slang term applied to someone who avoids making difficult decisions or is evasive.

Examples

A fudge packer insult
Don't be such a fudge packer—make a decision already!
He was cruelly called a fudge packer by some classmates, which was deeply hurtful.
The comedian faced criticism for using the term fudge packer in his routine.
