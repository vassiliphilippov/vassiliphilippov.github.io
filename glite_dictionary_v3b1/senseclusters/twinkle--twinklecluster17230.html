twinkle

noun
brief flash of light
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪŋkəl / (AmE), / ˈtwɪŋkəl / (BrE)
a brief or rapid flash of light; a sudden sparkle or glimmer

Examples

A twinkle of light
A twinkle of light caught her eye as she opened the box.
He noticed a faint twinkle in the distance, coming from the lighthouse.
The twinkle of the stars was visible through the clear night sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glimmer - a faint or brief light
  • degree higher: glow - steady light, stronger and longer than a twinkle
  • synonym: sparkle - a series of small flashes of light
