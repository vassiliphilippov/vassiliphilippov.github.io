laddering

noun
run in fabric
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈlæd.ər.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈlæd.ə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
the formation of a run or long unravelling line in knitted fabric, especially in stockings or tights
