patricide

noun
killing of one's father
Pronunciation: / ˈpætrɪˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈpætrɪsaɪd / (BrE)
the act of killing one's father

Examples

To commit patricide
His motives for committing patricide were debated at length during the trial.
In law, patricide is considered one of the most serious crimes.
The ancient legend tells of a prince accused of patricide after his father's mysterious death.

  • synonym: parricide - the killing of a parent or close relative
