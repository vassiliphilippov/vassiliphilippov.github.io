down payment

noun
initial payment on a purchase, balance paid later

an initial payment made when something is bought on credit, with the remaining amount to be paid later in installments

Examples

A down payment amount
The car dealer required a ten percent down payment to secure the purchase.
We had to make a down payment before moving into the new apartment.
Without a down payment, it's difficult to get approved for a mortgage.

Synonyms

  • down
    3.7 2.84/m
    down is an informal short word for a cash payment made at purchase, while down payment is the full term for that initial credit payment.
  • deposit
    3.3 11/m
    deposit is money paid in advance often as security or to a bank, while down payment is the specific initial purchase payment on credit.
