grim

adjective
harshly ironic or sinister (esp. of humor or jokes)
5.6 4.3
Pronunciation: /grɪm/ (AmE), /grɪm/ (BrE)
(of humor, jokes, or laughter) characterized by harsh, unsettling irony or a sinister tone, especially involving serious or disturbing subjects.

Examples

Grim humor
Her grim joke about the accident made everyone at the table uncomfortable.
Many stand-up comedians use grim humor to comment on social issues.
There was a grim laughter in his voice as he recounted the disaster.
