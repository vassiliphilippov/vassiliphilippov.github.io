attract

verb
to draw interest or attention
Pronunciation: / əˈtrækt / (AmE), / əˈtrækt /; / əˈtrakt / (BrE)
to cause someone to notice, like, or be interested in something; to draw attention or appeal

Examples

To attract attention
Bright colors often attract children's attention.
The aroma of freshly baked bread attracted people to the bakery.
The museum hopes to attract more visitors with its new exhibition.
Bright colors are used to attract the attention of shoppers walking by.
She hopes her innovative ideas will attract investors to fund her project.
The new museum exhibit is sure to attract large crowds this summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appeal - to be attractive or interesting
  • antonym: repel - to drive or force back; to be distasteful to
