ridgy

adjective
with many ridges
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪdʒi / (AmE), / ˈrɪdʒi / (BrE)
having many ridges or raised lines on the surface

Examples

The ridgy bark
The ridgy bark of the old tree was rough to the touch.
After the earthquake, the ground became uneven and ridgy in places.
She ran her fingers over the ridgy pattern carved into the stone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ridged - having a raised line or lines on the surface
  • antonym: smooth - having an even surface without raised lines
