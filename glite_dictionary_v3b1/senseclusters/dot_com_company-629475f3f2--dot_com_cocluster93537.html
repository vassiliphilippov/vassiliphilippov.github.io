dot com company

noun
internet-based business using '.com' domain
Pronunciation: / ˌdɑt ˈkɑm ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˌdɒt ˈkɒm ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
A company that conducts most of its business via the internet, typically identified by a URL ending in '.com'.

A dot com company website
