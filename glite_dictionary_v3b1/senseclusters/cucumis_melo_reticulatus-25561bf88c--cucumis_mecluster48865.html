Cucumis melo reticulatus

noun
a muskmelon with netted rind and green flesh
Pronunciation: / kuˈkjuː.mɪs ˈmɛloʊ rɛˌtɪk.jʊˈleɪ.təs / (AmE), / kjuːˈkjuː.mɪs ˈmiː.ləʊ rɛˌtɪk.jʊˈleɪ.təs / (BrE)
A variety of muskmelon plant with a thin netted rind and sweet green flesh, cultivated for its edible fruit.

The Cucumis melo reticulatus thrives in warm climates and produces sweet, succulent fruit.
