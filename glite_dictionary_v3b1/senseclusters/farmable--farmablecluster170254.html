farmable

adjective
can be exploited repeatedly
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrməbəl / (AmE), / ˈfɑːməbəl / (BrE)
able to be repeatedly exploited or harvested for resources, rewards, or experience, especially in gaming contexts

Examples

Farmable area in games
This area is highly farmable, so players often return to collect rare items.
The boss drops are farmable, making it easier to upgrade your equipment.
Many gamers look for farmable dungeons to quickly gain experience points.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grindable - able to be repeatedly played or used to gain rewards
  • antonym: unfarmable - not able to be repeatedly exploited for resources or rewards
