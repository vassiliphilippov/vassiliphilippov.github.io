Molson muscle

noun
beer belly from excessive beer, esp. Molson
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊlsən ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / ˈmɒlsən ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
a humorous term for a protruding stomach or beer belly resulting from excessive beer consumption, especially associated with Molson beer

Examples

The Molson muscle
After quitting beer for a year, his Molson muscle finally disappeared.
He wore his Molson muscle with pride at family gatherings.
They teased him about his growing Molson muscle on the beach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beer belly - a large, protruding stomach caused by excessive consumption of beer
