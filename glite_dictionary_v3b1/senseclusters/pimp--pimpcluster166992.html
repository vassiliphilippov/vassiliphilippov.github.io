pimp

noun
person who manages prostitutes
Pronunciation: / pɪmp / (AmE), / pɪmp / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who controls prostitutes and arranges clients for them, taking part of their earnings

Examples

A pimp controls women
He acted as a pimp, taking money from the women he managed.
The movie depicted the harsh life of a pimp in the 1970s.
The police arrested the pimp during a raid on the city center.
