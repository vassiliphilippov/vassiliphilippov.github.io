bun divider

noun
machine for dividing and shaping dough
Pronunciation: / bʌn dɪˈvaɪdɚ / (AmE), / bʌn dɪˈvaɪdə / (BrE)
a machine used to cut raw dough into equally sized portions and roll them into balls of uniform weight and shape

Examples

A bun divider machine
A bun divider ensures every roll is the same size and shape.
After mixing the dough, she placed it into the bun divider for portioning.
The bakery invested in a new bun divider to speed up bread production.
