splodge

noun
an irregularly shaped spot or mark
Pronunciation: / splɑdʒ / (AmE), / splɒdʒ / (BrE)
A large or irregularly shaped spot, mark, or stain, typically one made accidentally.

Examples

A muddy splodge
A muddy splodge appeared on the kitchen floor after the dog came in.
She tried to wipe the ink splodge from the worksheet.
There was a bright red splodge of paint on his shirt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blotch - a large, irregular spot or patch
  • degree lower: speckle - a small spot or mark
