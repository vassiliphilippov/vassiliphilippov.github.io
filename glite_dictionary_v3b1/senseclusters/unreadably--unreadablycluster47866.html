unreadably

adverb
in an illegible way
3.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈriːdəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈriːdəblɪ / (BrE)
In a way that is impossible or very difficult to read; in an illegible manner.

Examples

Written unreadably fast
He wrote unreadably quickly during the meeting, making his notes hard to decipher.
The faded ink made the old letter unreadably smudged in several places.
The signature on the document was unreadably scrawled across the bottom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illegibly - in a way that is difficult or impossible to read
