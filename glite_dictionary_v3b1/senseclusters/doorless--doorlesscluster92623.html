doorless

adjective
without a door
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔrləs / (AmE), / ˈdɔːləs / (BrE)
without a door or not having any doors

Examples

A doorless entrance
The doorless entrance made the café feel more welcoming to passersby.
They installed a doorless shower to create a modern look in the bathroom.
A doorless wardrobe can make it easier to access your clothes quickly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: closed - shut or covered by a door
  • synonym: doorway - an opening for a door, especially one without a door
  • synonym: open - not closed or covered by a door
