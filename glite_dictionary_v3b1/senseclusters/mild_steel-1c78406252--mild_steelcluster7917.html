mild steel

noun
low-carbon steel for construction/manufacturing
3.7 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / ma…™ld stil / (AmE), / ma…™ld stiÀêl / (BrE)
Steel containing less than about 0.15% carbon, making it relatively soft and easily shaped, often used in construction and manufacturing.

Examples

Mild steel sheet
He prefers mild steel because it is easy to weld and shape.
Many household appliances contain components made from mild steel.
The bridge was built using mild steel for its main beams.

Synonyms

  • low-carbon steel
    3.8 0.01/m
    low-carbon steel is a common type with low carbon and good weldability, while mild steel is the everyday name used in construction and manufacturing.
  • soft-cast steel
    5.1 0.00/m
    soft-cast steel is very soft and easy to shape for casting jobs, while mild steel is the general construction and manufacturing term.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carbon steel - steel with carbon as the main alloying element
