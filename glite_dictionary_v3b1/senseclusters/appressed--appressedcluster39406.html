appressed

adjective
pressed closely against something
Pronunciation: / əˈprɛst / (AmE), / əˈprɛst / (BrE)
(especially of an object or part) pressed closely against or lying flat upon another surface.

Examples

Appressed leaves
The appressed leaves help the plant retain moisture in dry environments.
The bark is covered with appressed scales that are difficult to remove.
The cat's ears were appressed to its head in fear.
