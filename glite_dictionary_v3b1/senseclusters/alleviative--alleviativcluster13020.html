alleviative

adjective
providing relief or easing pain
6.5
Pronunciation: / əˈliːviˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / əˈliːviətɪv / (BrE)
reducing or easing pain, suffering, or difficulty; having a soothing or relieving effect

Examples

Alleviative effect
Many people turn to music as an alleviative measure during stressful times.
The alleviative effects of the medication were noticeable within an hour.
The doctor suggested an alleviative treatment to minimize her joint pain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exacerbating - making pain or difficulty worse
  • synonym: relieving - making pain or distress less severe
  • synonym: soothing - having a calming or relieving effect
