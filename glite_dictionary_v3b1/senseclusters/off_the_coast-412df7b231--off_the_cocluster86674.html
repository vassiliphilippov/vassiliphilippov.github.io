off the coast

preposition
in the sea near land
Pronunciation: / ɔf ðə koʊst / (AmE), / ɒf ðə kəʊst / (BrE)
in the sea near the land or shoreline; located close to the coast of a country or continent

Examples

Off the coast of Spain
A group of dolphins appeared just off the coast early this morning.
An oil spill occurred off the coast last night, threatening local wildlife.
Several small islands can be seen off the coast from the beach.
