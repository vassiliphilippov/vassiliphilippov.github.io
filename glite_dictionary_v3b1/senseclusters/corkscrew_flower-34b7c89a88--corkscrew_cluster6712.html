corkscrew flower

noun
tropical vine with spiral-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrkˌskru ˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːk.skruː ˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
a perennial tropical American vine grown for its racemes of showy yellow and purple flowers, with a corolla keel coiled like a snail shell

Examples

The corkscrew flower petals
Gardeners admire the unique spiral shape of the corkscrew flower's petals.
She cultivated several corkscrew flowers along the fence for their vibrant colors.
The corkscrew flower makes an attractive addition to any tropical garden.
