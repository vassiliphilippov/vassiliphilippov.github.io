drop out

verb
withdraw from conventional life or society
4.4 0.35/m
Pronunciation: / drÉ‘p aÊŠt / (AmE), / drÉ’p aÊŠt / (BrE)
to withdraw from society or stop participating in conventional life, especially because of disillusion with its values

Examples

To drop out of society

She felt disconnected from conventional values, so she eventually dropped out.
