slip road

noun
road for entering or leaving a motorway
3.8 2.8
Pronunciation: /slɪp roʊd/ (AmE), /slɪp rəʊd/ (BrE)
a short road that allows vehicles to join or leave a major road such as a motorway or expressway

Examples

The slip road exit
Drivers should slow down before leaving the motorway via the slip road.
Take the slip road at the next junction to join the motorway.
The new development will include a slip road to improve access to the highway.
