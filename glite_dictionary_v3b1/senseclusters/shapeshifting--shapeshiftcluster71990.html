shapeshifting

noun
changing form or shape
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪpˌʃɪftɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪpˌʃɪftɪŋ / (BrE)
the ability or act of changing one's physical form or appearance, especially in myths or stories

Examples

Power of shapeshifting
Many ancient myths describe gods with the power of shapeshifting into animals or humans.
The movie's villain uses shapeshifting to trick the heroes and escape capture.
Stories about shapeshifting creatures are common in folklore from around the world.

  • synonym: transformation - a complete change in form or appearance
