work party

noun
workplace social event
3.2
Pronunciation: /wɜːrk ˈpɑːr.ti/ /; / /wɜːrk ˈpɑːr.tiː/ (AmE), /wɜːk ˈpɑː.ti/ /; / /wɜːk ˈpɑː.tiː/ (BrE)
a social event organized by an employer or workplace for staff to celebrate, relax, or build team spirit; not primarily intended for doing tasks.

Examples

Company work party
The company organized a work party after the product launch to thank the whole team.
Employees dressed casually for the work party and stayed late chatting with colleagues.
Her manager announced a small work party to celebrate reaching the quarterly sales goal on Friday.
