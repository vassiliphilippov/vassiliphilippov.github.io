illiterate

adjective
unable to read or write
5.8
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɪtəɹət / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪt.ər.ət / (BrE)

Examples

Illiterate children
Many people in remote areas remain illiterate due to a lack of access to education.
She grew up illiterate, but later became a successful writer.
The organization runs classes to help illiterate adults learn to read and write.
