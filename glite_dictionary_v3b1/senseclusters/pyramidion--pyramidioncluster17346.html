pyramidion

noun
pyramid-shaped cap
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪrəˈmɪdiən / (AmE), / ˌpɪrəˈmɪdiən / (BrE)
a small, pyramid-shaped stone or decorative cap placed on top of a pyramid, obelisk, or other structure, especially in ancient Egyptian architecture
