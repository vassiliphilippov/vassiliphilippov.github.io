warthog

noun
African wild pig with facial warts and tusks
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrthɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈwɑːthɒɡ / (BrE)
A wild African pig with warty lumps on its face and large, curved tusks.

Examples

A warthog in Africa
A group of warthogs grazed quietly by the waterhole.
The warthog uses its tusks for digging and self-defense.
We spotted a warthog while on a safari in Kenya.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wild boar - a wild ancestor of the domestic pig
