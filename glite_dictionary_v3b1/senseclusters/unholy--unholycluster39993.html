unholy

adjective
not holy or consecrated
6.1 0.26/m
Pronunciation: / ÊŒnËˆhoÊŠli / (AmE), / ÊŒnËˆhÉ™ÊŠli / (BrE)
Not hallowed or consecrated; not regarded as holy or sacred.

Examples

Unholy ground
He was accused of building his house on unholy land.
The ceremony cannot be held on unholy ground.
The ruins are an unholy site, avoided by worshippers for centuries.

Synonyms

  • unsanctified
    8.9 0.02/m

  • unhallowed
    9.9 0.06/m
    unhallowed is not made holy and often lacks blessing or rites, while unholy is not regarded as holy or sacred.
  • unconsecrated
    9.0 0.11/m
    unconsecrated is not formally dedicated for religious use and lacks blessing, while unholy simply means not sacred or hallowed.
  • nonsacred
    6.1 0.02/m
    nonsacred describes ordinary things not linked to religion or worship, while unholy implies something is not hallowed or sacred.
  • profane
    6.3 0.58/m
    profane treats sacred things with disrespect or is simply secular and everyday, while unholy only means not hallowed or consecrated.
