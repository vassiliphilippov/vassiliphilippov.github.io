fresh foods

noun
foods not preserved or processed
Pronunciation: / frɛʃ fuːdz / (AmE), / frɛʃ fuːdz / (BrE)
foods that have not been preserved by methods such as canning, dehydration, freezing, or smoking, and are typically consumed soon after harvesting or production

Examples

Fresh foods from market
After the storm, the community struggled to access fresh foods for several days.
Eating fresh foods can improve your overall health and nutrition.
The market is known for its wide selection of organic fresh foods.
