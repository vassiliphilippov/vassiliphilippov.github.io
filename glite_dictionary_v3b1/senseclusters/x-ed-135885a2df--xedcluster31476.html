X-ed

verb
to cross out with X
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɛksˌt / (AmE), / ˈɛksˌt / (BrE)
to mark, delete, or cross out something by drawing an 'X' over it

Examples

X-ed the wrong answer
She X-ed the incorrect answers before turning in her homework.
The editor X-ed out several sentences that needed to be rewritten.
He quickly X-ed the names that were no longer relevant to the list.

Related Terms
