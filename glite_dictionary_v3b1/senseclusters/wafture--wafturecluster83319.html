wafture

noun
hand movement used as a signal
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑftʃər / (AmE), / ˈwɒftjʊə / (BrE)
A movement of the hand or arm intended as a signal or gesture.

Examples

A casual wafture
He dismissed the idea with a casual wafture.
The wafture of his arm was barely noticeable but carried great meaning.
With a gentle wafture of her hand, she signaled them to be silent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gesture - a movement of part of the body to express an idea or meaning
