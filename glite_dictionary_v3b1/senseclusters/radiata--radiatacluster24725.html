radiata

noun
Monterey pine tree
7.3
Pronunciation: / reɪdiˈɑːtə / (AmE), / reɪdiˈɑːtə / (BrE)
a fast-growing pine tree, the Monterey pine (Pinus radiata), widely cultivated for timber production

Examples

Radiata pine forest
The plantation was filled with rows of young radiata ready for harvest in a few years.
Many furniture companies use wood from radiata because it grows quickly and is easy to work with.
After the fire, new radiata seedlings were planted to restore the forest.
