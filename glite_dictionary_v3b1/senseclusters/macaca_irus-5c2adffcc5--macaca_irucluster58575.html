Macaca irus

noun
a Southeast Asian monkey species
Pronunciation: / məˈkɑːkə ˈaɪrəs / (AmE), / məˈkɑːkə ˈɪərəs / (BrE)
a species of monkey native to Southeast Asia, Borneo, and the Philippines, also known as the long-tailed macaque or crab-eating macaque.
