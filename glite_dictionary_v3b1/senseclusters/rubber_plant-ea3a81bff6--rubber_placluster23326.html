rubber plant

noun
tropical tree, popular houseplant
8.4 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌb.ər plænt/ (AmE), /ˈrʌb.ə plɑːnt/ (BrE)
A large tropical Asian tree (Ficus elastica) often grown indoors as a houseplant, and also used as a source of natural rubber.

Examples

A large rubber plant
Many people mistake the rubber plant for a purely decorative species, not realizing its industrial use.
