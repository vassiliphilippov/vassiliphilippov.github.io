criminally

adverb
outrageously or shamefully (emphatic)
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪmɪnəli / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪnəli / (BrE)
in a way that is outrageously, shamefully, or extremely bad, often used for emphasis rather than literal criminality

Examples

Criminally careless
He was criminally careless with confidential information, putting the project at risk.
It was criminally unfair to blame her for the team's mistakes.
The apartment was criminally overpriced considering its poor condition and location.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shamefully - in a manner worthy of shame or disgrace
