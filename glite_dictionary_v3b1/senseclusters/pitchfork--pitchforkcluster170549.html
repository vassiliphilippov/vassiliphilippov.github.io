pitchfork

noun
long-handled tool for lifting hay
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtʃˌfɔrk / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃˌfɔːk / (BrE)
a long-handled tool with widely spaced, sharp prongs, used for lifting and tossing hay or other materials

Examples

A pitchfork in the barn
After the storm, she grabbed her pitchfork to clear debris from the yard.
At the barn, an old rusted pitchfork leaned against the wall.
The farmer used a pitchfork to toss hay onto the wagon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fork - a tool with two or more prongs used for lifting or digging
