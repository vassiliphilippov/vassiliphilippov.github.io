fight

verb
take part in a struggle or conflict
Pronunciation: / faɪt / (AmE), / faɪt / (BrE)
to take part in a physical or verbal struggle against someone or something; to engage in conflict

Examples

To fight an enemy
Neighbors sometimes fight over parking spaces on the crowded street.
She has always fought for what she believes is right.
The two teams will fight for the championship title this weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: battle - to fight in a battle or conflict
  • synonym: contend - to struggle to surmount a difficulty or opposition
  • antonym: surrender - to stop fighting and admit defeat
