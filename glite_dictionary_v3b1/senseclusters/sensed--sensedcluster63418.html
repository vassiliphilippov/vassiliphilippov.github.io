sensed

adjective
detected by sensors
4.1
Pronunciation: / sənst / (AmE), / sənst / (BrE)
detected or registered by electronic sensors or measuring instruments rather than by human perception.

Examples

Sensed movement
The autopilot flagged a sensed obstacle based on sensor data.
A sensed increase in cabin pressure triggered the alarm and slowed the aircraft.
The maintenance dashboard showed several sensed temperature spikes near the server rack overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detect - to discover or identify the presence of something, especially by using instruments or careful observation
  • synonym: measure - to determine the size, amount, or degree of something using instruments or devices
