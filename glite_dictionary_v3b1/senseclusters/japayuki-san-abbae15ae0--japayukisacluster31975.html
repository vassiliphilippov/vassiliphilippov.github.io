Japayuki-san

noun
Japanese female entertainer abroad
7.6
Pronunciation: / dʒɑː.paɪˈjuːki sɑːn / (AmE), / dʒæ.paɪˈjuːki sæn / (BrE)
A Japanese woman, especially one working as an entertainer or bar hostess abroad, particularly in other Asian countries.

Examples

Japayuki-san workers abroad
Her documentary explores the lives of several Japayuki-san in the Philippines.
Many Japayuki-san remitted money home to support their families.
The term Japayuki-san highlights social issues surrounding migrant workers from Japan.
