tansy leaf aster

noun
wild North American aster with purple flowers
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtænzi liːf ˈæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈtænzi liːf ˈastə / (BrE)
A wild North American aster with fernlike leaves and narrow bright purple ray flowers, native from Alberta to Texas and Mexico.

Examples

tansy leaf asters bloom purple
Gardeners often plant tansy leaf asters to attract pollinators to their wildflower beds.
The tansy leaf aster brightens prairies from Alberta to Mexico with its vibrant purple blooms.
The fernlike leaves of the tansy leaf aster make it easily distinguishable from other wildflowers.
