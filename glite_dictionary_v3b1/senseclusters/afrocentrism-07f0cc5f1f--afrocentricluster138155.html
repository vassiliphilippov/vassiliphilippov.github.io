Afrocentrism

noun
African-centered movement
Pronunciation: / ˌæfroʊˈsɛntrɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌæfrəʊˈsɛntrɪzəm /; / ˌafrəʊˈsɛntrɪzəm / (BrE)
a movement or way of thinking that emphasizes the importance and value of African culture, history, and identity, especially for people of African descent
