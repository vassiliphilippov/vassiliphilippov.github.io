Saint Nicholas

proper noun
4th-century bishop; basis for Santa Claus
4.2
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈnɪkələs / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈnɪkələs / (BrE)
a Christian saint from the 4th century who is the basis for Santa Claus and is associated with bringing gifts to children at Christmas
