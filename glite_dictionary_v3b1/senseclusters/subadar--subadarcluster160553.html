subadar

noun
army officer rank
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːbədɑːr / (AmE), / ˈsuːbədɑː / (BrE)
a junior commissioned officer rank in the Indian and Pakistani armies, typically commanding a platoon or company

Examples

A subadar rank
The experienced subadar was respected by both his soldiers and his superiors.
After years of service, he was promoted to the rank of subadar in the army.
The subadar led his platoon with discipline and courage.
