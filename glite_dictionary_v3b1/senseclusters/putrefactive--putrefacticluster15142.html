putrefactive

adjective
causing or relating to putrefaction
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpjuːtrəˈfæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌpjuːtrɪˈfæktɪv / (BrE)
causing or promoting the process of putrefaction, especially by bacterial action; relating to putrefaction

Examples

Putrefactive odor
A putrefactive odor filled the room, indicating that the meat had spoiled.
Proper refrigeration inhibits putrefactive changes in perishable foods.
The scientist studied the putrefactive bacteria responsible for decomposing organic matter.
