inshore

adjective
near the shore
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌʃɔr / (AmE), / ˈɪnʃɔː / (BrE)
situated or occurring close to the shore, especially of the sea.

Examples

Inshore waters
A strong inshore breeze made sailing difficult.
Environmental groups are monitoring inshore waters for pollution.
Many small boats operate inshore during the fishing season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: offshore - situated at sea some distance from the shore
