Synonyms

  • nearshore
    3.5 0.55/m
    nearshore describes sea areas close to the coastline, while inshore means areas even nearer to the shore and surf.
  • coastal
    2.4 18/m
    coastal refers to land or places by the sea, while inshore describes parts of the sea that lie very close to the shore.
