nonvocal

adjective
not spoken
5.7
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈvoʊkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈvəʊkəl / (BrE)
not involving or using the voice; silent or not spoken

Examples

Nonvocal communication
After the operation, the patient relied on nonvocal communication to express her needs.
The teacher encouraged nonvocal participation, such as raising hands or using gestures.
Some animals use nonvocal signals, like body movements, to communicate with each other.

Related Terms

  • synonym: silent - not making any sound
  • antonym: spoken - expressed using the voice
