antisocially

adverb
in an unfriendly way
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈsoʊʃəli / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈsəʊʃəli / (BrE)
in a way that avoids, harms, or goes against social interaction or society's rules

Examples

Act antisocially
He acted antisocially by refusing to speak to anyone at the meeting.
The children were warned not to behave antisocially in public places.
Ignoring your neighbors completely can be seen as living antisocially.
The teenagers behaved antisocially by vandalizing public property in the park.
He was fined for acting antisocially and disturbing his neighbors late at night.
People who drive antisocially put others at risk on the road.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criminally - in a way that involves breaking the law
  • synonym: hostilely - in a hostile or unfriendly manner
  • antonym: sociably - in a friendly and social manner
  • antonym: socially - in a way that relates to society or social interaction
  • synonym: unfriendly - not kind or sociable toward others
