bittersweetly

adverb
happily and sadly
5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪt.ər.swiːt.liː/ /; / /ˈbɪt.ər.swiːt.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈbɪt.ə.swiːt.liː/ /; / /ˈbɪt.ə.swiːt.lɪ/ (BrE)
