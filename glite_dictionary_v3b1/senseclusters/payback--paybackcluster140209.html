payback

noun
return on investment; recouping of initial outlay
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈpeɪˌbæk / (BrE)
the financial return gained from an investment, especially when it equals the original amount invested

Examples

Investment payback
Investors were pleased to see a quick payback on their initial outlay.
The project's payback period is estimated at just two years, making it very attractive to investors.
We calculated the payback to ensure the investment would be worthwhile.
