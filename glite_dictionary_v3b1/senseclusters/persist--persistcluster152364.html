persist

verb
remain in a place; not leave
Pronunciation: / pərˈsɪst / (AmE), / pəˈsɪst / (BrE)
(formal) To remain in a place and not leave; to stay for an extended period.

Examples

Symptoms persist
If symptoms persist, consult your doctor before continuing with any activities.
Several protesters chose to persist inside the building even after they were asked to leave.
The fog persisted over the hills for hours, refusing to lift.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depart - to leave a place
  • synonym: stay - to remain in a place instead of leaving
