sprinkling

noun
small amount scattered about
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋklɪŋ / (BrE)
a small number or thin layer of things spread or scattered over an area

Examples

A sprinkling of applause
A sprinkling of applause followed her announcement, polite but not enthusiastic.
He added a sprinkling of herbs to the soup before serving.
There was a sprinkling of snow on the ground this morning.
