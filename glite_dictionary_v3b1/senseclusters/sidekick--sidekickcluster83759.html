sidekick

noun
close companion or assistant
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌkɪk / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌkɪk / (BrE)
a person’s close companion, especially one who accompanies and assists them in their activities

Examples

A trusted sidekick
In many stories, the hero relies on advice from a trusted sidekick.
She never goes anywhere without her loyal sidekick by her side.
The detective and his sidekick solved the crime together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: companion - a person with whom one spends a lot of time or accompanies another
  • synonym: partner - a person who takes part in an activity with another
