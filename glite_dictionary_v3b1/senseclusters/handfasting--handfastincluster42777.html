handfasting

noun
historic marriage promise
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌfæstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌfɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
a traditional or historical ceremony in which two people join hands or have their hands tied together to symbolize their union or promise to marry
