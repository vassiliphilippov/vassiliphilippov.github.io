tenner

noun
ten-pound note
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnər / (AmE), / ˈtɛnə / (BrE)
a British slang term for a ten-pound banknote

Examples

A tenner note
He found a tenner in the pocket of his old jacket.
Can you lend me a tenner until I get paid next week?
She paid for the meal with a tenner and got some change back.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: fiver - a British slang term for a five-pound banknote
  • synonym: ten-pound note - a banknote worth ten pounds sterling
  • degree higher: twenty - a British slang term for a twenty-pound banknote
