painkilling

adjective
relieving pain
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪnˌkɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪnˌkɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
relieving or reducing physical pain, especially by means of medication

Examples

Painkilling medicine
The nurse gave him a painkilling injection to help with his injury.
After the operation, she was prescribed a painkilling medication to manage discomfort.
Many over-the-counter drugs have painkilling properties for minor aches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soothing - having a calming or relieving effect
