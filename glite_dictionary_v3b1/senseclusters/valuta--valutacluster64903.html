valuta

noun
effective transaction date
Pronunciation: / vəˈluːtə / (AmE), / vəˈluːtə / (BrE)
the date on which a bank transfer or financial transaction becomes effective for the availability of funds and interest calculation

Examples

Valuta date
The valuta for the wire transfer is set for the next business day.
Interest on the deposit will be calculated from the valuta specified by the bank.
Please check the valuta to ensure your funds are available when needed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: settlement date - the date when a transaction is completed and funds are transferred
  • synonym: value date - the date when funds from a transaction become available
