goof

verb
make a silly mistake
Pronunciation: / ɡuːf / (AmE), / ɡuːf / (BrE)
to make a careless or silly mistake

Examples

To goof badly
He really goofed when he forgot to save his work before closing the program.
I can't believe I goofed on such an easy question during the test.
Try not to goof this time; double-check your calculations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunder - to make a serious mistake, usually due to carelessness or confusion
  • synonym: err - to make an error or go astray in conduct, thought, or belief
