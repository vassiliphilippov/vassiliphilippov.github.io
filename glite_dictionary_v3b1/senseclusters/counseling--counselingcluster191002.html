counseling

noun
professional advice or guidance
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkaʊn.səl.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkaʊn.səl.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of giving professional advice or guidance to help someone make decisions or deal with problems, especially of a personal or emotional nature

Examples

The university offers free counseling services for students experiencing stress or anxiety.

Marriage counselling can help couples resolve their conflicts effectively.
The university offers free counselling services to all its students.
