aphid

noun
small sap-sucking insect; plant pest
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.fɪd / (AmE), / ˈæf.ɪd / (BrE)
A small insect that feeds by sucking sap from plants, often regarded as a pest to crops and gardens.

Examples

Green aphids on plants
An infestation of aphids can damage young vegetable plants, leading to stunted growth.
Gardeners often struggle to control aphids because they multiply so quickly.
The rose bushes were covered in aphids, so we had to spray them to protect the flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: greenfly - an aphid, especially a green species commonly found on plants
  • synonym: plant louse - another term for aphid, especially in North American English
