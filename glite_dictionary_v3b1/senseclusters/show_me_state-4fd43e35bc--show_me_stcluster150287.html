Show Me State

proper noun
nickname for Missouri (U.S. state)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊ mi steɪt / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ mi steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the U.S. state of Missouri, referring to the reputed skeptical and practical nature of its residents

Examples

Show Me State nickname
He grew up in the Show Me State and was proud of his Midwestern roots.
Many people refer to Missouri as the Show Me State because of its residents' reputation for skepticism.
The baseball team traveled across the Show Me State during their summer tour.
