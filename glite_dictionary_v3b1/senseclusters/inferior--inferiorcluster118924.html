inferior

noun
person of lower rank or status
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɪriɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɪəriə / (BrE)
a person who is considered to be lower in rank, status, or quality than others

Examples

His inferiors at work
Despite his title, he never looked down on his inferiors or treated them with disrespect.
In the rigid hierarchy, inferiors had few opportunities for advancement.
The company expects its inferiors to follow the instructions of their superiors precisely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subordinate - a person under the authority or control of another
  • antonym: superior - a person of higher rank or quality
