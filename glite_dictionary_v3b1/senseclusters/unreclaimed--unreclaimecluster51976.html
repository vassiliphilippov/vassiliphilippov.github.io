unreclaimed

adjective
not collected
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈkleɪmd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈkleɪmd / (BrE)
not claimed, collected, or taken back by the rightful owner; left uncollected or unused.

Examples

Unreclaimed luggage
Several unreclaimed bags were left at the airport after the flight arrived.
The lost property office stores all unreclaimed items for three months before disposing of them.
At the end of the auction, a few unreclaimed goods remained on the shelves.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reclaimed - taken back or recovered by the rightful owner
  • synonym: unclaimed - not claimed or collected by anyone
