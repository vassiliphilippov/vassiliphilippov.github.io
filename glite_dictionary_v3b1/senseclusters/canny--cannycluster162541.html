canny

adjective
shrewd, clever, and cautious
4.4 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkæn.iː/ /; / /ˈkæn.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈkæn.iː/ /; / /ˈkæn.ɪ/ (BrE)
