galvanic

adjective
sudden, powerful, thrilling effect
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɡælˈvænɪk / (AmE), / ɡælˈvanɪk / (BrE)
producing a sudden and powerful emotional effect, as if by an electric shock; thrilling or energizing.

Examples

Galvanic effect on audience
Her galvanic performance brought the theater to its feet in applause.
The speaker's galvanic presence inspired the entire audience to action.
The team's comeback had a galvanic impact on their supporters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electric - having an intense, exciting, or thrilling effect (figurative sense)
  • synonym: stirring - causing excitement or strong emotion
  • antonym: unexciting - not causing excitement or strong emotion
