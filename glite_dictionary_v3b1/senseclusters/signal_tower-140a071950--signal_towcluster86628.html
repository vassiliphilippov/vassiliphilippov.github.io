signal tower

noun
telecommunications mast or tower
3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪg.nəl ˈtaʊ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈsɪg.nəl ˈtaʊ.ə/ /; / /ˈsɪg.nəl ˈtəʊ.ə/ (BrE)
a tall structure (often a mast or lattice tower) that carries antennas and equipment to transmit or receive radio, mobile phone, or other telecommunications signals.

Examples

Mobile signal tower
The new signal tower on the hill greatly improved reception for nearby residents.
Engineers inspected the signal tower after the storm and replaced the damaged antennas.
Several signal towers were removed to make way for the highway expansion project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cell tower - a tower that supports cellular network antennas for mobile phone communication
  • synonym: radio mast - a mast supporting radio antennas for broadcasting or communication
  • synonym: transmission tower - a tall structure used to support antennas or transmission lines for broadcasting or communications
