sandless

adjective
without sand
Pronunciation: / ˈsændləs / (AmE), / ˈsændləs / (BrE)
lacking sand or containing no sand at all

Examples

A sandless surface
After the construction, the playground became completely sandless and was covered with grass.
The sandless beach surprised tourists who expected to see golden shores.
Some plants thrive in sandless soil that retains more moisture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sandy - containing or covered with sand
