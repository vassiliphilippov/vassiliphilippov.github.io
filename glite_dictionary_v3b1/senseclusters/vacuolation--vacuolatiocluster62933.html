vacuolation

noun
formation of vacuoles in cells
Pronunciation: / ˌvæk.ju.əˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌvak.jʊ.əˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the formation or presence of vacuoles within a cell or tissue

Examples

Vacuolation in cells
Microscopic examination revealed vacuolation in the liver cells.
Neural vacuolation can be an indicator of certain neurological diseases.
The degree of vacuolation varied between different tissue samples.
