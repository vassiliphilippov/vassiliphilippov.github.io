hundred-percenter

noun
an extreme nationalist
8.8 8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhʌn.drər pər.ˈsen.tər/ (AmE), /ˈhʌn.drəd pə.ˈsen.tə/ (BrE)
A person who holds extreme nationalist views, especially one who aggressively promotes the interests of their own country above all else.

Examples

A hundred-percenter nationalist
Her rhetoric bordered on that of a hundred-percenter, leaving little room for compromise with other countries.
In early twentieth-century politics, hundred-percenters often called for strict loyalty tests for new citizens.
The senator was accused of being a hundred-percenter during the heated debate on immigration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jingoist - an extreme nationalist who advocates for aggressive foreign policy
  • synonym: nationalist - a person who strongly identifies with their nation and seeks to promote its interests
  • degree lower: patriot - a person who loves and strongly supports their country
