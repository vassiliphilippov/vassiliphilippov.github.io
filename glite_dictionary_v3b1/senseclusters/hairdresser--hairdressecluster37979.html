Synonyms

  • hairstylist
    2.1 0.65/m
    hairstylist focuses on modern cutting and creative styling for clients, while hairdresser is the general professional who cuts and styles hair.
  • stylist
    2.8 1.82/m
    stylist often means a professional who creates fashionable hair looks, while hairdresser emphasizes the practical cutting and salon work.
  • styler
    5.8 0.01/m
    styler is a less common, more informal term for someone who shapes hair, while hairdresser is the standard professional name used in salons.
