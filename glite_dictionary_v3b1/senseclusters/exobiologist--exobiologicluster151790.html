exobiologist

noun
scientist studying extraterrestrial life
8.6
Pronunciation: /ek.soʊ.baɪ.ˈɑː.lə.dʒɪst/ (AmE), /ek.əʊ.baɪ.ˈɒl.ə.dʒɪst/ (BrE)
a scientist who studies the possibility and nature of life beyond Earth, especially on other planets

Examples

The exobiologist studies planets
The exobiologist presented new findings about possible microbial life on Europa.
As an exobiologist, she collaborates with space agencies to design experiments for Mars missions.
Many exobiologists are interested in how life could adapt to extreme environments on other planets.
The exobiologist presented her findings about possible microbial life on Europa at the conference.
As an exobiologist, he spends much of his time analyzing data from space missions.
A team led by an exobiologist designed experiments to detect life on distant planets.
