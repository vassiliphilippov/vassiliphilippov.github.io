platting

noun
braiding process
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈplæt.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈplæt.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the action or process of braiding or weaving hair, straw, rope, or similar materials into plaits

Examples

Hair platting
The artisan demonstrated traditional platting techniques for making decorative cords.
She spent the afternoon platting long strands of straw into a sturdy basket.
In some cultures, platting hair is an important part of ceremonial dress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braiding - the process of interlacing three or more strands
  • synonym: plaiting - the act of forming material into plaits
  • antonym: unraveling - the process of undoing or separating braided or woven material
