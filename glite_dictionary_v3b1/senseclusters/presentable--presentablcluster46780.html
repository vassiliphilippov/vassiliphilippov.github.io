presentable

adjective
fit to be seen; acceptable in appearance
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɛntəbəl / (AmE), / prɪˈzɛntəbl / (BrE)
clean, tidy, or suitable enough to be seen in public; acceptable in appearance.

Examples

A presentable appearance
He quickly tidied his desk to make it presentable before the guests arrived.
Please wear something presentable to the interview tomorrow.
She didn’t feel presentable without fixing her hair.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shabby - worn out or untidy in appearance; not presentable
  • synonym: tidy - neat and in order; presentable
