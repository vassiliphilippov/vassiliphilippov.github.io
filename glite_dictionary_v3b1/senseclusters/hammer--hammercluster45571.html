hammer

noun
striking part of a gun's firing mechanism
6.9 7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmɚ / (AmE), / ˈhamə / (BrE)
the part of a gun's firing mechanism that strikes the percussion cap or firing pin when the trigger is pulled

Examples

Gun hammer
If the hammer is not cocked properly, the gun will not fire.
Older revolvers often have an exposed hammer that must be manually pulled back before shooting.
The hammer fell with a sharp click as he pulled the trigger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firing pin - a part of a gun that strikes the primer of a cartridge to ignite it
