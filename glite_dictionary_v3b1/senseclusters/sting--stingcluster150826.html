sting

verb
cause sharp emotional pain
4.2
Pronunciation: / stɪŋ / (AmE), / stɪŋ / (BrE)
to cause a sharp emotional pain or hurt, as if by stinging

Examples

Words sting deeply
Her criticism really stung him, though he tried not to show it.
It can sting when a friend cancels plans at the last minute.
The memory of his harsh words still stings after all these years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hurt - to cause physical or emotional pain
  • antonym: soothe - to make someone feel calmer or less upset
