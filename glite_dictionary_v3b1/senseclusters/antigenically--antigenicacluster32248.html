antigenically

adverb
in relation to antigens
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌæntɪdʒəˈnɪkli / (AmE), / ˌæntɪdʒɪˈnɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the properties or effects of antigens

Examples

To be antigenically similar
The two viruses are antigenically similar, so the same vaccine may work for both.
Researchers compared the strains antigenically to determine if they would trigger the same immune response.
The new variant differs antigenically from previous versions, affecting vaccine effectiveness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immunologically - in a way that relates to the immune system
