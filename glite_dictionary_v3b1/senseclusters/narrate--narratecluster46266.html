narrate

verb
provide commentary for a film or broadcast
Pronunciation: / nəˈreɪt /; / nəˈreɪt, ˈnæˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈnæreɪt /; / nəˈreɪt, ˈnæˌreɪt / (BrE)
to tell a story or describe events, either by speaking or writing, including providing commentary for a film or performance

Examples

To narrate a story
He was chosen to narrate the award-winning documentary about climate change.
She enjoys narrating stories for children's audio books and television shows.
The actor will narrate the film, describing the events as they unfold.
He enjoys narrating stories to his grandchildren every evening.
She narrated the film, providing insights into each scene.
The tour guide narrated the history of the castle as we walked through its halls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commentate - to provide a spoken account of events as they happen, especially on radio or television
  • synonym: describe - to give an account of something with details
  • synonym: relate - to recount or tell; to give an account of
  • antonym: summarize - to give a brief statement of the main points; to sum up
