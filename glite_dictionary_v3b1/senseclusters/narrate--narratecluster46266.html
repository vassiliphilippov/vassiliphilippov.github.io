Examples

To narrate a story

Synonyms

  • tell a story
    1.1 4.29/m
    tell a story focuses on sharing an account or imaginative tale for listeners, while narrate can also give film commentary or formal description.
  • recount
    4.1 9.30/m
    recount gives detailed, often factual events from experience, while narrate covers storytelling and broader description in speech or writing.
  • tell
    1.0 387/m
    tell is a general verb for saying information aloud, while narrate implies a full story or described sequence of events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commentate - to provide a spoken account of events as they happen, especially on radio or television
  • synonym: describe - to give an account of something with details
  • synonym: relate - to recount or tell; to give an account of
  • antonym: summarize - to give a brief statement of the main points; to sum up
