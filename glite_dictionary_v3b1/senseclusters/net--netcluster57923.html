net

verb
to earn as net profit
Pronunciation: / nɛt / (AmE), / nɛt / (BrE)
to earn or achieve as net profit after expenses have been deducted

Examples

To net a profit
After taxes, her freelance work nets her a comfortable living.
Last year, the company netted over $2 million in revenue after all expenses.
The investment netted him a tidy sum in just a few months.
After expenses, the company netted over half a million dollars last quarter.
Her investments net substantial returns each year.
The film netted an impressive sum at the box office despite stiff competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clear - to earn as clear profit, after deductions
  • antonym: gross - earn amount before any deductions
