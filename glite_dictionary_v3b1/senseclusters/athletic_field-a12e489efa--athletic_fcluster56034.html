athletic field

noun
area for playing sports
5.2
Pronunciation: / əˈθlɛtɪk fiːld / (AmE), / əˈθlɛtɪk fiːld / (BrE)
An area of land specially prepared and maintained for playing sports or athletic games.

Examples

The athletic field
Due to the rain, the athletic field was too muddy for practice.
She volunteers every spring to help maintain the athletic field in her neighborhood.
The high school built a new athletic field for the soccer and football teams.
