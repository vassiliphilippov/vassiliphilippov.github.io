accommodation ladder

noun
portable ship's ladder for boarding
Pronunciation: / əˌkɑːməˈdeɪʃən ˈlædər / (AmE), / əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃən ˈlædə / (BrE)
(nautical) a portable stairway or ladder hung over the side of a ship to provide access to boats or a pier alongside.

Examples

The accommodation ladder on ship
A safety line was rigged along the accommodation ladder to help people during rough seas.
Inspectors climbed the accommodation ladder to board the vessel for a routine check.
The crew lowered the accommodation ladder so that passengers could board the waiting boat.
