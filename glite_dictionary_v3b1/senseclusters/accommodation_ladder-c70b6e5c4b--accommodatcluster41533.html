accommodation ladder

noun
portable ship's ladder for boarding
Pronunciation: / əˌkɑːməˈdeɪʃən ˈlædər / (AmE), / əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃən ˈlædə / (BrE)
(nautical) a portable stairway or ladder hung over the side of a ship to provide access to boats or a pier alongside.

Examples
