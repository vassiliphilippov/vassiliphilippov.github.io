parfait glass

noun
tall glass for serving parfaits
4.0
Pronunciation: / pɑrˈfeɪ ɡlæs / (AmE), / pɑːˈfeɪ ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a tall, slender glass with a short stem, typically used for serving parfaits and similar layered desserts.

Examples

A parfait glass set
For the dinner party, she bought a new set of elegant parfait glasses for dessert.
He carefully layered fruit and yogurt in a parfait glass to create a beautiful dessert.
The ice cream sundae looked especially appealing when served in a tall parfait glass.
