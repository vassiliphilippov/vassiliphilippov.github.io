OAM

noun
network management procedures
8.6
Pronunciation: / oʊ eɪ ɛm / (AmE), / əʊ eɪ ɛm / (BrE)
an abbreviation for operations, administration, and maintenance, referring to procedures and tools used to monitor and manage computer networks or telecommunications systems

Examples

OAM tools
The technician used OAM tools to monitor the performance of the new network.
Proper OAM is essential for maintaining reliable telecommunications services.
Engineers rely on OAM procedures to quickly detect and fix network issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: network management - the process of administering and managing computer networks
  • synonym: troubleshooting - identifying and resolving problems in systems or equipment
