order Pholidota

noun
taxonomic order of pangolins
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər foʊˌlɪˈdoʊtə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌfəʊlɪˈdəʊtə / (BrE)
the taxonomic order containing pangolins, previously classified under Edentata in older systems

Examples

Order Pholidota mammals
Members of the order Pholidota are known for their overlapping scales and insectivorous diet.
Scientists have reclassified certain extinct mammals that were once placed in the order Pholidota.
Unlike most other mammals, species in the order Pholidota possess large protective keratin scales.
