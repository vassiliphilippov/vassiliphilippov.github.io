earthly paradise

noun
a perfect, idyllic place on earth
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɝːθli ˌpærəˈdaɪs / (AmE), / ˈɜːθli ˌpærəˈdaɪs / (BrE)
a place on earth that is ideal, beautiful, and seemingly perfect in every way; often associated with delight and happiness

Examples

An earthly paradise place
Many writers have described the secluded valley as an earthly paradise.
They hoped to turn the old farmhouse into an earthly paradise of peace and tranquility.
Tourists see the remote beach as something close to an earthly paradise.
