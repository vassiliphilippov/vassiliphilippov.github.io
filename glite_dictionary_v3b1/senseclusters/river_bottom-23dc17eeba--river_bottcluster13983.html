river bottom

noun
ground at the bottom of a river channel
3.6 2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvər ˌbɑt̬əm / (AmE), / ˈrɪvə ˌbɒtəm / (BrE)
the ground at the lowest part of a river channel, especially where the river flows or once flowed

Examples

The river bottom ground

Years of erosion had exposed ancient fossils in the dry river bottom.
