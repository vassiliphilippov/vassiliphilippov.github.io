skillet corn bread

noun
cornbread with ham or bacon, cooked in a skillet
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪlɪt kɔrn ˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈskɪlɪt kɔːn ˌbrɛd / (BrE)
A type of cornbread, often with ham or bacon bits, cooked in a skillet, giving it a crisp crust and rich flavor.
