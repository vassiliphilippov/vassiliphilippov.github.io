hairband

noun
band for holding hair
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈheəˌbænd / (BrE)
a band or loop, often elastic or decorative, worn to hold hair back or keep it in place

Examples

A hairband for ponytails
She wore a bright red hairband to keep her hair out of her eyes during class.
He bought a velvet hairband as a gift for his sister.
A simple hairband can add a stylish touch to any outfit.
She wore a pink hairband to keep her hair out of her face during class.
Before playing tennis, he put on a hairband to stop sweat from dripping into his eyes.
The little girl lost her favorite hairband at the playground yesterday.
She always keeps a hairband on her wrist in case she needs to tie her hair back.
Before playing tennis, he pulled his hair into a ponytail with a hairband.
The little girl chose a pink hairband to match her dress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hair tie - an elastic band used to fasten hair into a ponytail or other style
  • synonym: headband - a band worn around the head to keep hair in place or absorb sweat
  • synonym: scrunchie - a fabric-covered elastic hair tie
