tertial

adjective
third in order
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrʃəl / (AmE), / ˈtɜːʃəl / (BrE)
third in order, or relating to a third part or level of something

Examples

Tertial section
The report was divided into primary, secondary, and tertial sections for clarity.
In the new curriculum, students progress from basic to advanced and finally to tertial modules.
Each tertial phase of the project builds on the previous two stages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: primary - first in order or importance
  • antonym: secondary - second in order or importance
