backbiting

adjective
maliciously gossipy
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌbaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbækˌbaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
characterized by malicious gossip or criticism about someone who is not present

Examples

Backbiting remarks
The backbiting comments in the group made it difficult for anyone to trust each other.
She left the backbiting environment at her old job for a more positive workplace.
Rumors and backbiting behavior can quickly destroy team spirit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gossipy - fond of talking about other people's private lives
  • synonym: slanderous - making false and damaging statements about someone
  • antonym: supportive - giving encouragement or help instead of criticism
