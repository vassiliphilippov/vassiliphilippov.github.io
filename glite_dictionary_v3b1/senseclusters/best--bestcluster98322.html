best

noun
the most outstanding person
1.0
Pronunciation: / bɛst / (AmE), / bɛst / (BrE)
the person who is most outstanding or excels above all others in a group or field

Examples

The best in class
Although she was new to the team, she quickly became the best in her class.
Even the best sometimes make mistakes under pressure.
He trained hard because he wanted to be the best in his field.

  • synonym: winner - a person who wins a competition or achieves first place
