vesication

noun
formation of blisters in the skin
9.2 9.6
Pronunciation: /vez.ɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /və.zɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /vez.ɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /və.zɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of forming blisters (vesicles) in or beneath the skin, especially as a result of disease or certain chemicals

Examples
