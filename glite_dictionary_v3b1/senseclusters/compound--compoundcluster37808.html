compound

noun
enclosed area with residences and buildings
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑm.paʊnd / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.paʊnd / (BrE)
an area enclosed by a fence or wall and containing residences and other buildings, especially in some Asian or African countries

Examples

A guarded compound
After entering the main gates, visitors must check in with security at the compound office.
Several families share the large compound, which includes houses, gardens, and a playground.
The workers live together in a secure compound on the outskirts of the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courtyard - an unroofed area enclosed by buildings
  • synonym: enclosure - an area that is surrounded by a barrier
