sparklingly

adverb
in a lively way
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːrklɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈspɑːklɪŋli / (BrE)
in a lively, witty, or brilliant manner, especially in speech, performance, or personality

Examples

Sparklingly funny speech
The comedian spoke sparklingly during the interview, making everyone laugh.
Her essay was written sparklingly, full of clever ideas and humor.
He performed sparklingly on stage, captivating the entire audience.
