greenware

noun
eco-friendly products
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːnwɛr / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnwɛə / (BrE)
products or goods that are designed to be environmentally friendly, often made from recycled or sustainable materials

Examples

Eco-friendly greenware products
The company introduced a new line of greenware made entirely from biodegradable materials.
Many consumers prefer greenware because it helps reduce environmental impact.
Restaurants are switching to greenware to minimize plastic waste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eco-friendly - not harmful to the environment
  • antonym: polluting - causing harm to the environment
  • synonym: sustainable - able to be maintained without harming the environment
