neuroprotective

adjective
protecting nerves
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊprəˈtɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊprəˈtɛktɪv / (BrE)
helping to prevent or reduce damage to the brain or nervous system

Examples

Neuroprotective effect
Scientists are developing neuroprotective therapies to help patients after a stroke.
Certain vitamins may have neuroprotective effects against age-related memory loss.
The new drug showed strong neuroprotective properties in laboratory tests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neurotoxic - harmful or poisonous to the nervous system
