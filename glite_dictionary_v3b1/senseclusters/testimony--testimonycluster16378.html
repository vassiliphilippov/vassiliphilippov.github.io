testimony

noun
evidence or proof of something
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛstəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / ˈtɛstɪməni / (BrE)
something that shows or proves that a particular fact is true or exists; evidence of a fact or quality.

Examples

Testimony to success
Every achievement stands as testimony to her perseverance and talent.
Her constant support is a clear testimony to their close friendship.
The award is testimony to his years of hard work and dedication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evidence - facts or information that show something is true
  • synonym: proof - evidence or argument establishing a fact or the truth of a statement
