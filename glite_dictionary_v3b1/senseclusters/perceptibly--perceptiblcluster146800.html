perceptibly

adverb
in a noticeable or detectable way
6.8
Pronunciation: / pərˈsɛptəbli / (AmE), / pəˈsɛptəbli / (BrE)
In a way that can be seen, heard, or noticed; in a noticeable manner.

Examples

Changed perceptibly
Her partner's mood improved perceptibly after hearing the good news.
The pain eased perceptibly after she took the medication.
The temperature has dropped perceptibly since last night, making it much colder outside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: imperceptibly - in a way that cannot be noticed or detected
  • synonym: noticeably - in a way that is easily seen or noticed
