double-propeller plane

noun
aircraft with two counter-rotating propellers
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌb.əl prəˈpɛl.ɚ pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʌb.əl prəˈpel.ə pleɪn / (BrE)
