transhuman

adjective
enhanced by technology
9.2
Pronunciation: / trænzˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / trænzˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
having abilities or characteristics enhanced beyond normal human limits, especially through advanced technology

Examples

Transhuman abilities
The concept of a transhuman society raises important ethical questions about the future.
Some believe that transhuman enhancements could eliminate many human limitations.
A transhuman athlete might use advanced implants to improve performance beyond natural ability.

Related Terms
