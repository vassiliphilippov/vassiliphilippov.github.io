worthwhileness

noun
the quality of being worthwhile
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːθˌwaɪl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈwɜːθˌwaɪl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of having enough value or importance to justify the time or effort spent

Examples

The worthwhileness of effort

She questioned the worthwhileness of continuing the long process.
The worthwhileness of the project became apparent after seeing its positive impact.
