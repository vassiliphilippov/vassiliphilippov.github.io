Synonyms

  • flowered
    2.0 0.01/m
    flowered describes objects covered in a pattern of flowers, while floral describes anything generally related to or decorated with flowers.
  • floriated
    8.2 0.00/m
    floriated means decorated with carved or painted flower designs, while floral is a broader word for things related to flowers.
  • flowerlike
    3.3 0.03/m
    flowerlike looks similar in shape to a flower, while floral refers more widely to flower patterns, scent, or relation.
  • flowery
    3.5 0.47/m
    flowery suggests the smell or a rich, ornate style, while floral mainly describes things that look or smell like flowers.
