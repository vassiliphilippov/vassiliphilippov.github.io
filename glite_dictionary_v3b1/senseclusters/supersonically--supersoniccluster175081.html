supersonically

adverb
very quickly
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈsɑːnɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈsɒnɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely fast or with great intensity, often used figuratively to mean very quickly.

Examples

To finish supersonically
The team finished the assignment supersonically, impressing everyone with their speed.
He responded supersonically to every email, never keeping anyone waiting.
The news spread supersonically through social media, reaching thousands in minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rapidly - very quickly or at a great rate
  • antonym: slowly - at a low speed; not quickly
