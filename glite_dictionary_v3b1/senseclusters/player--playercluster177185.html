player

noun
person who participates in an activity
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in a particular activity, especially one who is active or influential within a field or organization

Examples

Key player
He became a key player in international affairs after the agreement.
She is considered a major player in the local real estate business.
The new company is now a significant player in the technology market.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bystander - a person who is present but not participating
  • synonym: participant - a person who takes part in an activity
