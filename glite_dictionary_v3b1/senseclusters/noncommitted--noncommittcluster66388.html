noncommitted

adjective
not finalized
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəˈmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəˈmɪtɪd / (BrE)
not finalized, confirmed, or made permanent, especially regarding changes, transactions, or actions in a system

Examples

Noncommitted changes pending
The updates were still noncommitted until the administrator approved them.
You can review your noncommitted changes before saving them to the system.
Any noncommitted transactions will be lost if the application closes unexpectedly.
