infectiousness

noun
ability to spread disease
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɛkʃəsnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɛkʃəsnəs / (BrE)

Examples

Wearing masks can help reduce the infectiousness of respiratory illnesses in crowded places.
