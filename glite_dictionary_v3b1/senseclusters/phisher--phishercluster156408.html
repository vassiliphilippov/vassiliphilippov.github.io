phisher

noun
online scammer
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃə / (BrE)
a person who tries to trick people online into giving away personal or financial information, usually by pretending to be someone else

Examples

A phisher sends emails
A phisher sent fake emails to thousands of people, hoping to steal their bank details.
If you receive a suspicious message, it might be from a phisher trying to get your information.
The company warned employees about the dangers of clicking links sent by a phisher.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fraudster - a person who commits fraud
  • synonym: scammer - a person who cheats others by trickery
  • antonym: victim - a person who is tricked or harmed by a phisher
