titler

noun
person who gives titles
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪtlər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtlə / (BrE)
a person who confers or bestows a formal title, rank, or honor on someone

Examples

Royal titler
The king served as the official titler during the annual honors ceremony.
As titler, she had the authority to grant knighthood to distinguished citizens.
The titler announced the new title in front of the gathered audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bestower - a person who gives or presents something
  • antonym: recipient - a person who receives something
