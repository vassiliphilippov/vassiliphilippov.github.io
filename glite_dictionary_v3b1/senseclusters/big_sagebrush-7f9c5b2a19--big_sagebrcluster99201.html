big sagebrush

noun
aromatic shrub of western North America
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ ˈseɪdʒˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈseɪdʒˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
an aromatic shrub (Artemisia tridentata) native to arid regions of western North America, having silvery-grey leaves and commonly found in sagebrush steppe ecosystems
