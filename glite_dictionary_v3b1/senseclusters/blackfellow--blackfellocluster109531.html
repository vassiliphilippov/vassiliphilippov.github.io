blackfellow

noun
offensive term for Aboriginal man
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌfɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈblækˌfɛləʊ / (BrE)
an offensive and outdated term formerly used to refer to an Aboriginal Australian man

Examples

The blackfellow term
The term blackfellow is now considered highly offensive and should not be used.
Historical documents sometimes refer to Aboriginal men as blackfellows, reflecting outdated attitudes.
Modern writers avoid using blackfellow due to its offensive and derogatory nature.
