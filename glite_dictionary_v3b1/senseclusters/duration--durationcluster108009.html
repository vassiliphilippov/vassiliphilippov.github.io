Synonyms

  • elapsed time
    3.4 0.27/m
    elapsed time is the amount of time that has passed during an event or between events, while duration is the length of that event.
  • time interval
    3.3 0.84/m
    time interval is a measurable period between two specific points or events, while duration describes how long something lasts.
  • period
    2.1 192/m
