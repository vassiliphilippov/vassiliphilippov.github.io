wifelike

adjective
having typical qualities of a wife
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪfˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈwaɪf.laɪk / (BrE)
Having qualities or characteristics regarded as typical of a wife.

Examples

A wifelike manner
He sought a partner with gentle manners and a wifelike sense of responsibility.
Her wifelike devotion and support impressed everyone who met her.
The novel describes a heroine who is both independent and wifelike in her care for the family.
