self-

x
prefix meaning 'of/by oneself'
Pronunciation: / sɛlf / (AmE), / sɛlf / (BrE)
Used as a prefix to form words meaning 'of oneself' or 'by oneself.'

Examples

Self-taught musician
He is a self-taught musician who plays several instruments by ear.
Her self-confidence improved dramatically after she started her new job.
The self-checkout machines make shopping much faster for many customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auto- - prefix meaning 'self', from Greek
