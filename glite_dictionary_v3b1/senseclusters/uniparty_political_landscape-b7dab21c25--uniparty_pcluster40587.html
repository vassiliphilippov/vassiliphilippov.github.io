uniparty political landscape

noun
system dominated by one political party
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːniˌpɑrti pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈlænd.skeɪp / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪˌpɑːti pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈlan(d).skeɪp / (BrE)
A political system or environment in which a single political party dominates, often resulting in limited competition or choice for voters.

Examples

A uniparty political landscape
Some analysts argue that a uniparty political landscape restricts true democratic choice.
The rise of one dominant party has led to a uniparty political landscape in the country.
Voters expressed frustration with the uniparty political landscape during the recent election cycle.
