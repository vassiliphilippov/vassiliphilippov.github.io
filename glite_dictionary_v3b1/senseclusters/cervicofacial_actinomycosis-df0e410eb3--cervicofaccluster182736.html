cervicofacial actinomycosis

noun
actinomycosis of face and neck
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜrvɪkoʊˈfeɪʃəl ˌæktɪnoʊmaɪˈkoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌsɜːvɪkəʊˈfeɪʃəl ˌæktɪnəʊmaɪˈkəʊsɪs / (BrE)
the commonest and least severe form of actinomycosis, affecting the face and neck regions

Examples

cervicofacial actinomycosis infection
Early diagnosis of cervicofacial actinomycosis is important for effective treatment.
Patients with cervicofacial actinomycosis often present with abscesses in the face and neck regions.
The most frequent manifestation of cervicofacial actinomycosis involves swelling in the lower jaw area.
