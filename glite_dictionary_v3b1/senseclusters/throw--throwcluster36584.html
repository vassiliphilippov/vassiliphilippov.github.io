throw

noun
decorative lightweight blanket or cover
Pronunciation: / θɹoʊ / (AmE), / θɹəʊ / (BrE)
A lightweight blanket or cloth covering, often used decoratively and casually draped over furniture or a bed.

Examples

A soft throw
I always keep a throw nearby for chilly evenings on the porch.
She placed a soft throw over the back of the armchair for added comfort.
The living room sofa was brightened by a colorful throw and a few cushions.
