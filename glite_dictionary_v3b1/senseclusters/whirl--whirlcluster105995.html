whirl

noun
an attempt; a try
7.9
Pronunciation: / wɝl / (AmE), / wɜːl / (BrE)
(informal) An attempt or try at doing something, especially something new or unfamiliar.

Examples

Give it a whirl
After studying French for months, he finally gave conversation a whirl with a native speaker.
I've never played tennis before, but I'll give it a whirl.
If you're not sure about sushi, you should at least give it a whirl.

  • synonym: try - an attempt to do something
