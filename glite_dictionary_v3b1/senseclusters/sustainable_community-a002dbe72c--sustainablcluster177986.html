sustainable community

noun
community designed for environmental sustainability
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈsteɪ.nə.bəl kə.ˈmjuː.nə.tɪ/ (AmE), /sə.ˈsteɪ.nə.bəl kə.ˈmjuː.nɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
a community planned or developed to minimize environmental impact and support long-term ecological balance

Examples

A sustainable community plan
Living in a sustainable community encourages residents to reduce waste and support local food production.
Many people are moving to a sustainable community that uses solar power and promotes eco-friendly transportation.
The city council is working to transform the old neighborhood into a sustainable community with green spaces and energy-efficient homes.
