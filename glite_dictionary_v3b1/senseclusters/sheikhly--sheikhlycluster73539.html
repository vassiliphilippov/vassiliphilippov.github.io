sheikhly

adjective
of a sheikh
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪkli / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪkli / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a sheikh, especially in style, manner, or appearance

He wore a sheikhly robe that made him stand out at the ceremony.
The sheikhly manner in which he greeted his guests impressed everyone at the gathering.
