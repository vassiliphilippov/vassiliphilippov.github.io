mulita

noun
young or small mule
9.9
Pronunciation: / muˈliːtə / (AmE), / muˈliːtə / (BrE)
a young or small mule, which is a hybrid animal born from a male donkey and a female horse

Examples

A young mulita
The farmer gently brushed the mulita before letting it out to graze.
Children at the ranch loved to watch the playful mulita run around the field.
A veterinarian examined the sick mulita and gave it some medicine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foal - a young horse or related animal
  • synonym: mule - the hybrid offspring of a male donkey and a female horse
