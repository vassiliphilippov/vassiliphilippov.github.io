loadout

noun
equipment set
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈloʊ.daʊt/ (AmE), /ˈləʊ.daʊt/ (BrE)
a set or collection of equipment, supplies, or items prepared or carried for a specific activity, mission, or by a vehicle

Examples

A loadout bag
Before the hiking trip, she carefully packed her loadout to include food, water, and a first aid kit.
The soldier double-checked his loadout to ensure he had all the necessary equipment for the mission.
In the game, players can customize their loadout to suit different strategies and challenges.
The military team checked their loadout before boarding the helicopter for the mission.
Each truck's loadout was carefully balanced to avoid exceeding weight limits.
The video game allows players to customize their character's loadout with different weapons and gear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cargo - goods or items transported by a vehicle or ship
  • synonym: gear - equipment or apparatus for a particular activity
  • synonym: kit - a set of tools or equipment for a particular purpose
  • synonym: payload - the total weight of cargo or equipment carried by a vehicle
