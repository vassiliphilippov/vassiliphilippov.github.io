Pythagorean triangle

noun
right triangle with integer sides
8.7 6.1
Pronunciation: / paɪˌθæɡəriən ˈtraɪæŋɡəl / (AmE), / paɪˌθaɡəriːən ˈtraɪaŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
