postmark

verb
stamp mail with official mailing date/address
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstˌmɑːk / (BrE)
to stamp (a letter or package) with an official mark showing the date and place of mailing

Examples

To postmark a package
All outgoing packages are now postmarked electronically at the facility.
Be sure to postmark your application envelope before tomorrow’s deadline.
The clerk postmarked the letter before sending it overseas.
