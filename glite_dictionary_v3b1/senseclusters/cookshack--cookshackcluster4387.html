cookshack

noun
small informal eatery
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊkˌʃæk / (AmE), / ˈkʊkˌʃæk / (BrE)
a small, informal roadside or neighborhood restaurant or food stall, often serving grilled or smoked food

Examples

Roadside cookshack
We stopped at a cookshack on the highway to try their famous barbecue.
Locals love the grilled chicken served at the neighborhood cookshack every weekend.
The cookshack by the beach is known for its fresh seafood and relaxed atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diner - a small, informal restaurant serving simple meals
  • synonym: food stall - a small stand or booth selling food, often outdoors
