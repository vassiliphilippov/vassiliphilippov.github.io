Fenusa

noun
genus of leaf-mining sawflies
Pronunciation: / fəˈnuːsə / (AmE), / fəˈnjuːsə / (BrE)
a genus of small sawflies whose larvae are known as birch leaf miners, feeding inside the leaves of birch and sometimes other trees
