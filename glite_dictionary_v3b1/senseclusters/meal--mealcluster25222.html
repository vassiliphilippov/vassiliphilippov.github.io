meal

noun
an occasion for eating food
3.9
Pronunciation: / miːl / (AmE), / miːl / (BrE)
an occasion when people eat food, especially at a regular time such as breakfast, lunch, or dinner

Examples

A big meal
His favorite meal of the day is breakfast.
They gathered together for a Sunday meal with the whole family.
We usually have our evening meal at seven o'clock.
He skipped his breakfast meal because he was running late.
The hotel offers three meals a day to its guests.
We usually eat our evening meal together as a family.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: feast - a large, elaborate meal often for a celebration
  • degree lower: snack - a small amount of food eaten between main meals
