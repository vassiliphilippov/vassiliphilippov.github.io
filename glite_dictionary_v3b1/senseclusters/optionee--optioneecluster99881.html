optionee

noun
person granted an option
8.3
Pronunciation: /ɑːp.ʃə.ˈniː/ /; / /əp.ʃə.ˈniː/ (AmE), /ɒp.ʃə.ˈniː/ /; / /əp.ʃə.ˈniː/ (BrE)

Examples

As an optionee, she had the right to buy shares at a fixed price.
