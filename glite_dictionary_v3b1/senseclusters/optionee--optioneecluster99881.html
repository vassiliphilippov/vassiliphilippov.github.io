optionee

noun
person granted an option
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːpʃəˈniː / (AmE), / ˌɒpʃəˈniː / (BrE)
a person who has been granted an option, especially the right to buy something such as shares or property

Examples

The optionee buys shares
The optionee decided to purchase the property before the contract expired.
As an optionee, she had the right to buy shares at a fixed price.
The agreement specified the responsibilities of both the optionor and the optionee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: option holder - a person who possesses an option contract
  • antonym: optionor - a person who grants an option to another
