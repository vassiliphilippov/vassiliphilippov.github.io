adjutant general

noun
chief military administrative officer
Pronunciation: / ˌædʒ.ə.tənt ˈdʒen.ə.rəl / (AmE), / ˌæd.jʊ.tənt ˈdʒen.ər.əl / (BrE)
the chief administrative officer in an army, or a senior staff officer who assists a commanding general
