call forwarding

noun
diverting incoming calls to another number
Pronunciation: / kɔl ˈfɔrwərdɪŋ / (AmE), / kɔːl ˈfɔːwədɪŋ / (BrE)
A telephone service feature that allows incoming calls to be redirected to another phone number chosen by the subscriber.

Examples

Call forwarding service
Many people use call forwarding so they don't miss important calls when they are away from the office.
The call forwarding feature can be a lifesaver while you're traveling abroad.
To activate call forwarding, simply follow the instructions provided by your phone service provider.

Related Terms

  • synonym: call divert - British term for call forwarding
  • synonym: call waiting - telephone service that alerts a user to an incoming call while they are already on the line
