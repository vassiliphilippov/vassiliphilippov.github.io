promiser

noun
person who makes a promise
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːməsər / (AmE), / ˈprɒmɪsə / (BrE)
a person who makes a promise or gives an assurance that something will be done

Examples

The promiser keeps word
As the promiser, you have a responsibility to keep your word.
The promiser is legally bound to fulfill the terms of the contract.
The agreement requires the promiser to deliver the goods within thirty days.
