fleet

verb
disappear or fade gradually
Pronunciation: / fliːt / (AmE), / fliːt / (BrE)
(literary or archaic) To disappear or pass away gradually; to fade slowly.

Examples

Feelings fleet away
After the excitement of the party, the feeling of joy began to fleet from her heart.
As the years fleet, memories of childhood slowly fade away.
The pain in his chest fleeted overnight, much to his relief.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fade - to gradually disappear or lose intensity
  • antonym: linger - to last for a long time, delaying disappearance
