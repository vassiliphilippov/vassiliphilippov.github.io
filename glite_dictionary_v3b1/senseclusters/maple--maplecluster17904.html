maple

noun
a deciduous tree of the genus Acer
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪ.pəl / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.pəl / (BrE)
a tree or shrub of the genus Acer, native to the north temperate regions, known for its lobed leaves and winged seeds, and often valued for its wood and sap

Examples

A maple tree
A large maple tree provided welcome shade for the children playing below.
Canadian flags often feature the distinctive maple leaf as a national symbol.
The leaves of the maple turn brilliant red and orange in autumn.
