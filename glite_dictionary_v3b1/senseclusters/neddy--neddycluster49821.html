neddy

noun
slang for donkey
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛdi / (AmE), / ˈnɛdi / (BrE)
a slang term for a donkey, or in some regions, a foolish person

Examples

An old neddy
The children laughed as the old neddy brayed loudly in the yard.
He bought a neddy to help carry supplies up the hill.
In some places, calling someone a neddy means you think they are being silly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ass - another word for a donkey
  • synonym: donkey - a domesticated hoofed mammal related to the horse
