Boletus pulverulentus

noun
a species of edible bolete mushroom
Pronunciation: / boʊˈliːtəs ˌpʌlvəˈruːlɛntəs / (AmE), / bəʊˈliːtəs ˌpʌlvjʊˈruːlɛntəs / (BrE)
An edible species of bolete mushroom with a broadly convex, blackish-brown cap, yellow pore surface that darkens with age, and a thick stalk enlarging toward the base.

Examples

Boletus pulverulentus mushroom
Identifying Boletus pulverulentus is easier when the cap is still young and the pore surface remains bright yellow.
The chef prepared a seasonal soup using wild Boletus pulverulentus collected that morning.
We foraged in the forest and found several Boletus pulverulentus growing near the old oak tree.
