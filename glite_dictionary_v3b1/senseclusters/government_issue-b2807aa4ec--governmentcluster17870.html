government issue

noun
official government-issued supplies
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌvərnmənt ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvənmənt ˈɪʃu / (BrE)
supplies such as food, clothing, or equipment officially distributed by the government, especially to members of the armed forces.

Examples

A government issue uniform
Each soldier received a government issue uniform and boots upon enlistment.
Old surplus stores often sell unused government issue equipment to the public.
The food rations were strictly government issue and tasted rather bland.

Related Terms

  • synonym: standard issue - the usual equipment or clothing officially supplied
