string bean

noun
very tall, thin person (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɪŋ biːn / (AmE), / ˈstrɪŋ biːn / (BrE)
A very tall and thin person, used humorously or informally.

Examples

Tall string bean kid
Everyone joked that Mark was such a string bean after his recent growth spurt.
My brother was always called a string bean in high school because of his height.
Standing next to the basketball team, I felt like a real string bean.
