lotting

noun
dividing into parcels
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɒtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of dividing land or property into separate lots or parcels

Examples

Lotting farmland
The lotting of the farmland allowed several families to purchase their own plots.
After the lotting process, the company sold each parcel to different developers.
City officials approved the lotting plan for the new residential neighborhood.
