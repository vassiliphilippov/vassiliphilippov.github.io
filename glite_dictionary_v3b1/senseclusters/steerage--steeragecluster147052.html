steerage

noun
the act of steering a ship
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈstɪərɪdʒ / (BrE)
the act or practice of steering a ship or other vessel

Examples

Steerage of the ship
Accurate steerage is essential when maneuvering through narrow channels.
Successful steerage of the sailing ship depended on quick decisions by the helmsman.
The crew's skill in steerage was tested during the violent storm.
