smarm

noun
excessive, insincere flattery
Pronunciation: / smɑrm / (AmE), / smɑːm / (BrE)
excessive and insincere flattery or charm, especially to gain favor

Examples

Excessive smarm
His constant smarm quickly made him unpopular with the rest of the team.
Politicians are sometimes criticized for relying on smarm instead of genuine connection.
She saw through the smarm in his compliment and refused to trust him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flattery - excessive praise or compliments, often for self-serving purposes
