crossroads

noun
crucial decision point
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌroʊdz /; / ˈkrɑsˌroʊdz / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌrəʊdz / (BrE)
a point in life or a situation where an important decision must be made that will affect the future

Examples

At a crossroads in life
After losing his job, he found himself at a crossroads in his career and life.
She realized she was at an emotional crossroads and needed to choose a new direction.
The country stands at a crossroads, facing decisions that will shape its future.
After years in the same job, Maria found herself at a crossroads in her career.
He realized he was at a crossroads, needing to choose between two very different paths in life.
The company faces a crossroads as it decides whether to expand or stay small.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crisis - a time of intense difficulty or danger
  • synonym: turning point - a time at which a decisive change in a situation occurs
