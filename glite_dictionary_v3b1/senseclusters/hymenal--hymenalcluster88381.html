hymenal

adjective
of or relating to the hymen
9.9
Pronunciation: / haɪˈmɛnəl / (AmE), / hɪˈmiːnəl / (BrE)
relating to the hymen, a thin membrane that partially covers the external vaginal opening

Examples

Hymenal membrane
A hymenal tear can occur for various reasons, not solely from sexual activity.
The doctor noted the condition of the hymenal tissue during the examination.
There is significant variation in hymenal appearance among individuals.
