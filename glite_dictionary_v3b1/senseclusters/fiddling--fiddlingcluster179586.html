fiddling

adjective
of little importance; trivial
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪd.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪd.lɪŋ / (BrE)
(informal) Small and of little importance; trivial.

Examples

A fiddling amount
Arguing about fiddling sums of money wastes everyone's time.
She spent hours arguing over a fiddling detail that didn't really matter.
The amount he was owed was so fiddling that he didn't bother to collect it.
