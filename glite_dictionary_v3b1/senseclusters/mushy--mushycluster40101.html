mushy

adjective
excessively sentimental or emotional
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌʃi / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃi / (BrE)
excessively or insincerely emotional, especially in a way that seems silly or sentimental

Examples

Mushy love letter
His mushy letter made her laugh more than it moved her.
I find romantic movies too mushy for my taste.
She tends to get mushy when talking about her childhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maudlin - self-pityingly or tearfully sentimental, often through drunkenness
  • synonym: sentimental - having or arousing feelings of tenderness, sadness, or nostalgia
  • antonym: unsentimental - not influenced by, showing, or expressing feelings or sentiment
