alluringly

adverb
attractively, charmingly
Pronunciation: / əˈlʊrɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈljʊərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is attractive, charming, or tempting and draws attention

Examples

Smiles alluringly
She smiled alluringly across the room, catching everyone's attention.
The model walked alluringly down the runway, captivating the audience.
He spoke alluringly about his travel adventures, making everyone want to join him.
The jewelry sparkled alluringly in the shop window, drawing the attention of passersby.
She smiled alluringly at the camera, making the advertisement more appealing.
The aroma of fresh bread wafted alluringly from the bakery each morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: temptingly - in a way that arouses desire or interest
  • antonym: unattractively - in a way that does not attract or charm
