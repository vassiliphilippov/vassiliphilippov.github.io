Ovambo

proper noun
southern African ethnic group
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈvɑːmboʊ / (AmE), / əʊˈvæmbəʊ / (BrE)
a member of a Bantu ethnic group from southern Africa, especially Namibia and Angola, or the language they speak
