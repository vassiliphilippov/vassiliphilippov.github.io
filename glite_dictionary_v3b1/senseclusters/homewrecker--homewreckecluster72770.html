homewrecker

noun
person who breaks up relationships
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌrɛkər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌrɛkə / (BrE)
a person who is blamed for causing the breakup of a marriage or romantic relationship

Examples

A homewrecker blamed
The tabloids called her a homewrecker after the celebrity couple announced their divorce.
He was unfairly labeled a homewrecker even though the marriage was already in trouble.
Some people blame the so-called homewrecker, but relationships are often more complicated than they appear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adulterer - a person who has a sexual relationship outside their marriage
  • antonym: faithful partner - a person who remains loyal in a relationship
