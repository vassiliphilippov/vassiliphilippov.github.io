bloodbeat

noun
rhythmic pulsing sensation
8.1 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààbl ådÀåbiÀêt / (AmE), / Ààbl ådÀåbiÀêt / (BrE)
a rhythmic pulsing or throbbing sensation in the body, similar to a heartbeat

Examples

The bloodbeat in temples
She could feel the bloodbeat in her temples as she ran up the hill.
The intense silence made the bloodbeat in his ears seem even louder.
After the workout, his bloodbeat was strong and steady in his wrists.

Synonyms

  • heartbeat
    2.3 5.99/m
    heartbeat is the regular strong movement of the heart felt in the chest, while bloodbeat is a more general pulsing sensation.
  • pulse
    2.3 13/m
    pulse is the rhythmic beat felt at the wrist or neck, while bloodbeat is a softer whole-body pulsing sensation.
  • pulsation
    5.6 0.14/m
    pulsation is a technical word for rhythmic throbbing in tissues, while bloodbeat is a common felt pulsing in the body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pulse - the regular throbbing of arteries as blood is propelled through them
