toadlike

adjective
resembling a toad
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊdˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtəʊdˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having characteristics of a toad, especially in appearance or texture

Examples

Toadlike rough skin

He drew a toadlike monster with warts and bulging eyes for the storybook.
Some amphibians have toadlike features even though they are not true toads.
