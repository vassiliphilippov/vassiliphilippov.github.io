bad company

noun
negative or harmful associates
Pronunciation: / bæd ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / bæd ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
the presence or association of people who are considered to have a negative or harmful influence

Examples

Bad company leads trouble
His parents worried that spending time in bad company would lead him astray.
She left the group when she realized they were bad company.
Teachers often advise students to avoid bad company to focus on their studies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: good company - association with pleasant or positive people
