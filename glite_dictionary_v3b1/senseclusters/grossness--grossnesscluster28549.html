grossness

noun
lack of taste or refinement
4.1 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈgroʊ.snəs/ (AmE), /ˈgrəʊ.snəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being lacking in taste, refinement, or delicacy; coarseness or vulgarity

Examples

The grossness of jokes
Critics often lament the grossness and lack of subtlety in some modern comedies.
His behavior at the dinner table revealed a certain grossness that made others uncomfortable.
The grossness of the decor clashed with the otherwise sophisticated atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coarseness - the quality of being rough or lacking refinement
  • antonym: refinement - elegance or cultured taste, opposite of grossness
  • synonym: vulgarity - lack of sophistication or good taste; unrefined quality
