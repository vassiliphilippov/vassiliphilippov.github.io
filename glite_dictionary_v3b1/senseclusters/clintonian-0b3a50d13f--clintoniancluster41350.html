Clintonian

adjective
of Hillary Clinton
Pronunciation: / klɪnˈtoʊniən / (AmE), / klɪnˈtəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to the policies, political style, or public persona of Hillary Rodham Clinton, former U.S. Secretary of State and presidential candidate

Examples

Clintonian policy style
The senator's Clintonian approach emphasized careful planning and detailed policy proposals.
Many voters appreciated her Clintonian focus on experience and pragmatic solutions.
Critics argued that his speech had a distinctly Clintonian tone, prioritizing stability over bold change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Obamanian - relating to the policies or style of Barack Obama
  • antonym: Trumpian - relating to the policies or style of Donald Trump
