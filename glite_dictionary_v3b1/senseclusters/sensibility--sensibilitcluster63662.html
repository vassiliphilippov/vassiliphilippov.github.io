sensibility

noun
refined sensitivity or emotional awareness
4.3 5.4
Pronunciation: /sɛn.sɪ.ˈbɪ.lɪ.tiː/ /; / /sɛn.sə.ˈbɪ.lɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /sɛn.sɪ.ˈbɪ.lɪ.tiː/ /; / /sɛn.sə.ˈbɪ.lɪ.tiː/ (BrE)

Examples
