wild vanilla

noun
fragrant-leaved perennial plant
2.7
Pronunciation: / waɪld vəˈnɪlə / (AmE), / waɪld vəˈnɪlə / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to the southeastern United States, known for its leaves that have a fragrance resembling vanilla.
