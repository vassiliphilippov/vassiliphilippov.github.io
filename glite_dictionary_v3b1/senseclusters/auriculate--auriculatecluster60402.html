auriculate

adjective
having ear-shaped lobes or appendages
4.9
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈrɪkjələt / (AmE), / ɔːˈrɪkjʊlət / (BrE)
having ear-shaped appendages or lobes (auricles), especially as applied to leaves, petals, or anatomical structures

Examples

Auriculate leaves
