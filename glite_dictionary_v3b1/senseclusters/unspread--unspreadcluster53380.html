unspread

verb
to fold up
8.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsprɛd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsprɛd / (BrE)
to fold up or gather together something that has been spread out; to reverse the action of spreading.

Examples

To unspread a cloth
After the picnic, she unspread the blanket and packed it away.
He carefully unspread the map before putting it back in his bag.
Once the event was over, the volunteers unspread the banners and stored them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fold - to bend or lay something so that one part covers another
  • antonym: spread - to open or stretch something out
