unspread

verb
to fold up
8.1
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈspred/ /; / /ə.ˈspred/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈspred/ /; / /ə.ˈspred/ (BrE)

Examples

To unspread a cloth

Once the event was over, the volunteers unspread the banners and stored them.
