ditchmoss

noun
submerged freshwater plant
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪtʃˌmɑs / (AmE), / ˈdɪtʃˌmɒs / (BrE)
a submerged freshwater plant, especially of the genus Fontinalis, commonly found in streams and ditches

Examples

ditchmoss in streams
During the summer, ditchmoss covers many shallow parts of the old canal.
Researchers collected samples of ditchmoss to study its effect on water quality.
The stream banks were thick with ditchmoss swaying gently in the current.
