phonetician

noun
a specialist in phonetics
Pronunciation: / foʊˌnɛˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / fəʊˌnɛˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who specializes in the study of phonetics, the science of speech sounds

Examples

A phonetician studies sounds
As a trained phonetician, she can distinguish subtle variations in pronunciation.
Many language researchers consult a phonetician when developing new speech recognition software.
The phonetician analyzed the differences in vowel sounds among various dialects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phonologist - expert in phonology, the study of sound systems
