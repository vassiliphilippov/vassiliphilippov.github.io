ill

adjective
not well; suffering from a disease
Pronunciation: / ɪl / (AmE), / ɪl / (BrE)
suffering from a disease or not in good health; affected by an impairment of normal physical or mental function.

Examples

Feeling ill
He felt ill after eating food from the street vendor.
Many employees called in ill after the flu swept through the office.
She has been ill for several days and needs to see a doctor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: healthy - in good health; not suffering from illness
  • synonym: sick - affected by disease or ill health
