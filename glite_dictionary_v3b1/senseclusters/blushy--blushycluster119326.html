blushy

adjective
easily blushing
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌʃi / (AmE), / ˈblʌʃi / (BrE)
easily blushing or tending to show a red face from embarrassment or shyness

Examples

A blushy face
He became blushy whenever someone mentioned his achievements in front of the class.
Her blushy cheeks made it obvious she was embarrassed by the attention.
The blushy teenager avoided eye contact after making a mistake during the presentation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confident - feeling or showing certainty and self-assurance
  • synonym: flushed - having a red or pink face, often from embarrassment
  • synonym: shy - nervous or timid in the company of others
