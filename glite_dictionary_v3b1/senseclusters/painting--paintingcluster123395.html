painting

noun
a work of art made with paint
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
A picture or artistic composition created by applying paints to a surface such as canvas, paper, or a wall.

Examples

A famous painting
She spent the afternoon working on a landscape painting in her studio.
The museum is famous for its impressive collection of paintings by European masters.
They admired the colorful painting hanging above the fireplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drawing - a picture made with pen, pencil, or another writing instrument
  • synonym: picture - a painting, drawing, or photograph; a visual representation
