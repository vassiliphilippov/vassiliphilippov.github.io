departmentally

adverb
by department
5.6
Pronunciation: / dɪˌpɑːrtˈmɛntəli / (AmE), / dɪˌpɑːtˈmɛntəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or is organized by departments within an organization

Examples

Organized departmentally
The budget was allocated departmentally to ensure each team had the resources they needed.
Decisions are often made departmentally rather than by the company as a whole.
Performance reviews are conducted departmentally at the end of each quarter.
