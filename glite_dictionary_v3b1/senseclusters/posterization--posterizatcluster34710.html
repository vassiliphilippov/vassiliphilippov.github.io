posterization

noun
making a large poster
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of reproducing an image or design as a large printed poster for display

Examples

Posterization for an exhibition
The artist used posterization to create a striking display for the exhibition.
After posterization, the photograph was visible from across the entire hall.
Many companies offer posterization services for turning digital images into large prints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enlargement - the process of making something larger, especially a photograph
  • antonym: miniaturization - the process of making something much smaller
