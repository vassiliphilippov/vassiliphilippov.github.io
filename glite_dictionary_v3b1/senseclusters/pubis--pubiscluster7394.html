pubis

noun
front part of the pelvic bone
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuːbɪs / (AmE), / ˈpjuːbɪs / (BrE)
the forward-facing part of the pelvic bone, forming the front section of the pelvis

Examples

The pubis bone
Pain in the pubis may indicate an injury to the front of the pelvic region.
The pubis joins with the other pelvic bones to form the complete hip structure.
The surgeon explained that the fracture was located along the pubis and required careful attention.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: ilium - the broad, uppermost part of the hip bone
  • degree lower: ischium - the lower, rear portion of the hip bone
  • synonym: pelvis - the bony structure at the base of the spine, to which the legs are attached
