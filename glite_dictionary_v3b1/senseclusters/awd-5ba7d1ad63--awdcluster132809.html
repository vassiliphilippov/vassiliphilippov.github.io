AWD

adjective
all-wheel drive
4.4
Pronunciation: /eɪ ˈdʌb.əl.juː diː/ (AmE), /eɪ ˈdʌb.əl.juː diː/ (BrE)
used to describe a vehicle with a system that sends power to all four wheels for better traction

Examples

AWD vehicle
The new SUV comes standard with AWD for improved handling in all weather conditions.
Many drivers prefer AWD vehicles when living in areas with heavy snowfall.
He chose an AWD model to feel safer on slippery mountain roads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: 4WD - a vehicle with four-wheel drive, often for off-road use
  • antonym: FWD - a vehicle with front-wheel drive
  • antonym: RWD - a vehicle with rear-wheel drive
