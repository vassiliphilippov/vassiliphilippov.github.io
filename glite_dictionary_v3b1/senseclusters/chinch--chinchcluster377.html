chinch

noun
bedbug that infests beds and feeds on blood
9.3
Pronunciation: / tʃɪntʃ / (AmE), / tʃɪntʃ / (BrE)
a small bug found in temperate regions, especially known for infesting beds and feeding on human blood; a bedbug.

Examples

A chinch bug
