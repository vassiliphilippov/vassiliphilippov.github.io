myopically

adverb
without foresight
Pronunciation: / maɪˈɑːpɪkli / (AmE), / maɪˈɒpɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a lack of foresight or concern for the future

Examples

To act myopically in business
The board made decisions myopically, focusing only on immediate profits.
He myopically ignored the potential long-term effects of his actions.
Investors who act myopically may miss out on future opportunities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prudently - in a way that shows careful planning for the future
  • synonym: shortsightedly - in a way that does not consider long-term consequences
