screever

noun
street pavement artist
Pronunciation: / ˈskriːvər / (AmE), / ˈskriːvə / (BrE)
a person who draws pictures or writes on pavement, especially with chalk, often to earn money

Examples

Street screever draws
The screever spent hours creating a beautiful chalk mural on the busy sidewalk.
Tourists stopped to watch the screever as he finished his latest pavement masterpiece.
Children gathered around the screever to admire the colorful drawings on the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: busker - a person who performs music or other entertainment in public places for money
  • synonym: street artist - an artist who creates art in public spaces
