malty

adjective
tasting of malt
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔlti / (AmE), / ˈmɔːlti / (BrE)
having the flavor, aroma, or qualities of malt, often used to describe foods or drinks like beer, bread, or cereals

Examples

She prefers cereals with a strong malty flavor for her breakfast.
