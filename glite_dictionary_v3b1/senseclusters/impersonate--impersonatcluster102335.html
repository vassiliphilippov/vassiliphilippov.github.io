impersonate

verb
pretend to be another person
6.1 5.8
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɜrsəˌneɪt /; / ɪmˈpɜːrsəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɜːsəneɪt / (BrE)
to pretend to be another person, especially by copying their appearance, voice, or behavior, often to entertain or deceive
