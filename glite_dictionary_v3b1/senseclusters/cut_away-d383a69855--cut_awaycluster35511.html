cut away

verb
remove or diminish emotionally
2.3
Pronunciation: / kʌt əˈweɪ / (AmE), / kʌt əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to lessen or remove something intangible, such as feelings or spirit, often causing emotional pain or emptiness

Examples

Cut away her confidence
His constant criticism began to cut away her confidence over time.
Over the years, loss and disappointment would slowly cut away at her joy.
The shocking news seemed to cut away his sense of security.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cut out - to remove completely, also used figuratively for excluding something or someone
