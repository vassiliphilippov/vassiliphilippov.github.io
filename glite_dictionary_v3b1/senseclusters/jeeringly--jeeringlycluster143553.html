jeeringly

adverb
with mockery or scorn; in a jeering way
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪrɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪə.rɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a manner showing mockery or contempt by jeering; with scornful laughter or ridicule.

Examples

To speak jeeringly
He answered jeeringly, making it clear he didn't take her question seriously.
The crowd laughed jeeringly as the speaker stumbled over his words.
The students shouted jeeringly from the back of the classroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mockingly - in a mocking manner
