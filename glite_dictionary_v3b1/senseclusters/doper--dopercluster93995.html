doper

noun
person who gives drugs
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊpər / (AmE), / ˈdəʊpə / (BrE)
a person who gives or administers drugs, especially to enhance performance or enable cheating in sports or competitions

Examples

Doper gives drugs
The cycling team was disqualified after a doper was caught supplying illegal substances.
Officials are working hard to catch any doper trying to cheat in the tournament.
The veterinarian was accused of being a doper for giving racehorses banned drugs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheater - a person who acts dishonestly to gain an advantage
  • antonym: clean athlete - an athlete who does not use performance-enhancing drugs
  • synonym: drugger - a person who gives drugs to others
