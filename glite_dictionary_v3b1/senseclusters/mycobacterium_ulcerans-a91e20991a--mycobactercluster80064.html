Mycobacterium ulcerans

noun
bacteria causing Buruli ulcer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkoʊbækˈtɪəriəm ʌlˈsɜːrænz / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkəʊbækˈtɪəriəm ʌlˈsɜːrænz / (BrE)
a species of bacteria found in water that causes Buruli ulcer, a chronic skin disease, in humans and animals
