striping

noun
act of marking with stripes
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstraɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of marking something with stripes, or the stripes themselves on a surface

Examples

The striping on uniforms
The striping on the team's uniforms was bold and colorful.
The road was closed for striping to add new lane markings.
Zebras' natural striping helps them blend into their environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stripe - a long, narrow band or strip, typically one of several that are parallel or close together
