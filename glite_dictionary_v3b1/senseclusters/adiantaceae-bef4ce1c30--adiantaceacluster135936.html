Adiantaceae

noun
a family of ferns in some classifications
9.9
Pronunciation: /eɪ.diː.æn.ˈteɪ.siː/ /; / /ə.diː.æn.ˈteɪ.siː/ (AmE), /æ.dɪ.æn.ˈteɪ.siː/ /; / /eɪ.dɪ.æn.ˈteɪ.siː/ (BrE)

Examples

Adiantaceae family plants
