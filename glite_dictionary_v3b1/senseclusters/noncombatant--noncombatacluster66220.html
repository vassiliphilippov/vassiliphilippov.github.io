noncombatant

adjective
not engaged in fighting; civilian in war
5.0 5.8
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.kəm.ˈbæt.ənt/ (AmE), /nɒn.kəm.ˈbæt.ənt/ (BrE)
(of a person) not engaged in fighting, especially during a war; used to refer to civilians or others not involved in military combat.

Examples

Noncombatant civilians
During the evacuation, all noncombatant personnel were given priority.
The Red Cross vehicles transported noncombatant refugees to safety.
Under international law, targeting noncombatant populations is considered a war crime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: civilian - a person not in the armed services or police force
  • antonym: combatant - a person actively engaged in fighting, especially in war
