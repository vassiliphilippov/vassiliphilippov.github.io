glacial landscape

noun
land shaped by glaciers
Pronunciation: / ˈɡleɪʃəl ˈlændˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈɡleɪsɪəl ˈlændskeɪp / (BrE)
an area of land whose features have been shaped or formed by the action of glaciers

Examples
