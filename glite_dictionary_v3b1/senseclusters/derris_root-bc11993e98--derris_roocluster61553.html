derris root

noun
root of derris plant; source of rotenone
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛrɪs ruːt / (AmE), / ˈdɛrɪs ruːt / (BrE)
the root of a woody vine from the genus Derris, used as a source of the natural insecticide rotenone
