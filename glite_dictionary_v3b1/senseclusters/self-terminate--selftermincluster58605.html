self-terminate

verb
end or cancel something oneself; cease, often automatically
3.3 9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf tərˈmeɪt / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf təˈmɛɪt / (BrE)
to cancel, end, or discontinue something oneself or to cease functioning automatically in response to certain conditions; also, euphemistically, to end one's own life.

Examples

To self-terminate accounts
After the final episode aired, many viewers chose to self-terminate their service accounts.
Feeling overwhelmed, he talked openly about a desire to self-terminate.
The system is designed to self-terminate if an error is detected.
