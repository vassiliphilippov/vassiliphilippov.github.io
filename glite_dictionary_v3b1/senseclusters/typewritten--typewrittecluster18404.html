typewritten

adjective
made with typewriter
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪpˌrɪtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpˌrɪtn̩ / (BrE)
produced or written using a typewriter rather than by hand or printed by computer

Examples

Typewritten letter
The official document was typewritten and signed by the manager.
He found an old typewritten manuscript in the attic.
All applications must be typewritten to be considered for the position.

Related Terms

  • antonym: handwritten - written by hand rather than by machine
  • synonym: typed - produced using a keyboard or typewriter
