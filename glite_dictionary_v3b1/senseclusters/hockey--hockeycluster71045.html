hockey

noun
team stick sport
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑki / (AmE), / ˈhɒki / (BrE)
a team sport played on ice or a field in which players use sticks to hit a puck or ball into a goal

Examples

A hockey team
He enjoys watching hockey games with his friends every weekend.
Many children start playing hockey at a young age in Canada.
The local team won the hockey championship after a thrilling final match.

Related Terms

  • synonym: field hockey - a form of hockey played on grass or turf with a ball
  • antonym: football - a team sport played with a ball, known as soccer in some countries
  • synonym: ice hockey - a form of hockey played on ice with a puck
