pulling teeth

x
something very difficult to do
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊlɪŋ tiːθ / (AmE), / ˈpʊlɪŋ tiːθ / (BrE)
used to describe something that is very difficult or unpleasant to do, often involving much effort or reluctance from others

Examples

Like pulling teeth
Getting him to admit his mistake was like pulling teeth.
Organizing the team meeting felt like pulling teeth this week.
Sometimes persuading children to eat their vegetables can be as hard as pulling teeth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: effort - a vigorous or determined attempt
  • synonym: struggle - a forceful or violent effort to get free of restraint or resist attack
