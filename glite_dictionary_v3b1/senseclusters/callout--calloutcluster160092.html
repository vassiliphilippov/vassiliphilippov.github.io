callout

noun
diagram label or note
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔlˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈkɔːlˌaʊt / (BrE)
a label or note on a diagram, image, or illustration that highlights or explains a specific part or detail

Examples

Diagram callout
Each part of the engine was identified with a callout in the technical drawing.
The textbook uses callouts to highlight important terms in the diagrams.
A callout on the map pointed to the location of the new museum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annotation - a note added to explain or comment on something
  • synonym: caption - a brief explanation or description accompanying an illustration or photo
