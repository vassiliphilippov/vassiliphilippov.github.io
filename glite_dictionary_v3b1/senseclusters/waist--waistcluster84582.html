waist

noun
narrow part of a shoe's sole
Pronunciation: / weɪst / (AmE), / weɪst / (BrE)
the narrow part of a shoe connecting the heel and the wider part of the sole

Examples

Shoe waist
He noticed the waist was too narrow for his comfort.
Some modern shoes feature a reinforced waist to increase durability and stability.
The cobbler carefully shaped the waist of the shoe for better support.
