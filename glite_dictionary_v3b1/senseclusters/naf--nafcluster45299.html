naf

interjection
disapproval exclamation
9.9
Pronunciation: / næf / (AmE), / næf / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to express disapproval, contempt, or that something is of poor quality

Examples

Naf at ugly decor
When he saw the outdated decorations, he just said naf and walked away.
She looked at the cheap souvenir and muttered naf under her breath.
After tasting the bland soup, he shrugged and commented, naf.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cool - an exclamation expressing approval or admiration
  • synonym: ugh - an exclamation expressing disgust or dislike
