break it to someone

verb
inform someone of bad news
Pronunciation: / breɪk ɪt tu ˈsʌmwʌn / (AmE), / breɪk ɪt tʊ ˈsʌmwʌn / (BrE)
to tell someone something unpleasant or unwelcome, often with care or tact

Examples

To break it to a friend

Sometimes it's best to break it to someone gently rather than all at once.
