intravenous drip

noun
direct vein fluid infusion
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈvinəs drɪp / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈviːnəs drɪp / (BrE)
a slow, continuous infusion of fluids, medications, or nutrients directly into a vein, delivered drop by drop

Examples

An intravenous drip treatment
Doctors started an intravenous drip to administer the antibiotics directly into her bloodstream.
Each intravenous drip must be carefully monitored by nursing staff to ensure accurate fluid delivery.
The patient received fluids through an intravenous drip after becoming severely dehydrated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: IV - abbreviation for intravenous; used informally for intravenous drip
  • synonym: infusion - the process of introducing a substance, such as a drug, into the body via a vein
