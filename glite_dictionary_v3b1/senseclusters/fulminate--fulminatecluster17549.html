fulminate

noun

A salt or ester of fulminic acid, often used as an explosive compound in primers and detonators.

Examples

Mercury fulminate compound
Chemists must handle fulminate compounds with great care due to their instability.
Silver fulminate is extremely sensitive to shock and used in toy caps for toy guns.
The laboratory synthesized a small amount of mercury fulminate for testing purposes.
