Chrysanthemum frutescens

noun
a Canary Islands subshrub with pale yellow daisy flowers
9.6
Pronunciation: /krɪ.ˈsæn.θə.məm fruː.ˈtes.əns/ (AmE), /krɪ.ˈsæn.θə.məm fruː.ˈtes.əns/ (BrE)
A perennial subshrub native to the Canary Islands, often cultivated for its pale yellow daisylike flowers; also known as the marguerite daisy.
