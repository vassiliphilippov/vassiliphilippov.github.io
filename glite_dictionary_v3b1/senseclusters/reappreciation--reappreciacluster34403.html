reappreciation

noun
renewed recognition
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːəˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of reassessing and recognizing the value, quality, or importance of something again

Examples

Reappreciation of work
The documentary led to a reappreciation of the scientist's overlooked discoveries.
After many years, there was a reappreciation of traditional crafts in the community.
The anniversary event sparked a reappreciation for the band's early albums.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appreciation - recognition of the value or quality of something
  • antonym: neglect - failure to care for or recognize the value of something
  • synonym: reevaluation - the act of assessing something again
