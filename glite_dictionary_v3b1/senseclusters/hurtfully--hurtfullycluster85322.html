hurtfully

adverb
in a painful manner
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜrtfəli / (AmE), / ˈhɜːtfəli / (BrE)
in a way that causes pain or emotional distress to someone

Examples

To hurtfully ignore
She hurtfully ignored his efforts to apologize after their disagreement.
The teacher spoke hurtfully to the student, making him feel embarrassed in front of the class.
He hurtfully dismissed her ideas without giving them any consideration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruelly - in a way that shows a lack of kindness or compassion
  • antonym: kindly - in a way that shows kindness or care
