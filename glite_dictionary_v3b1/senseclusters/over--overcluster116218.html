over

adverb
across to the other side
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvɚ /; / ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
above or across something; moving or located from one side to the other, or in a position higher than something else (as an adverb)

Examples

A plane flew over the mountains as the sun was rising.
She hung her coat over the back of the chair before sitting down.
The cat jumped over the fence and disappeared into the garden.
