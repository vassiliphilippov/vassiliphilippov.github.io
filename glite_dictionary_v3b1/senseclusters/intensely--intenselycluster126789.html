intensely

adverb
to a high or extreme degree
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛnsli /; / ɪnˈtɛn(s)li / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛnsli /; / ɪnˈtɛn(t)sli / (BrE)
in a very strong, extreme, or focused way; with great force or feeling

Examples

She stared intensely at the painting, trying to understand its meaning.
They argued intensely about the best approach to the problem.
