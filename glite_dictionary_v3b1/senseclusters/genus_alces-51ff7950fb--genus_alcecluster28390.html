genus Alces

noun
genus of large deer (moose/elk)
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈælˌsiːz / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈæl.siːz / (BrE)
A genus of large deer that includes the moose or elk, native to northern regions of North America and Eurasia.

Examples
