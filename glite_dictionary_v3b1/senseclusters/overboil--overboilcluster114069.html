overboil

verb
overflow while boiling
3.0 5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈbɔɪl / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈbɔɪl / (BrE)
(of liquid) to overflow or cause to overflow while boiling.

Examples

Soup overboils on stove
Be careful not to let the soup overboil or you'll have a mess to clean up.
If you overboil the milk, it will spill onto the stove.
The pot overboiled while I was distracted by a phone call.
