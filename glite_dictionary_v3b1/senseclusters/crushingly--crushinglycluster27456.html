crushingly

adverb
in an overwhelming or devastating way
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkrʌʃɪŋli / (BrE)
In an extremely overwhelming or devastating manner.

Examples

To be crushingly disappointed
She was crushingly disappointed when she didn't get the job.
The new evidence was crushingly convincing, leaving no room for doubt.
They lost the championship game crushingly, unable to score a single point.
