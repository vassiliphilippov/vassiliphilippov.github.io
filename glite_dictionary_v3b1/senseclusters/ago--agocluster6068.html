ago

adjective, adverb
indicating a time in the past
Pronunciation: / əˈɡoʊ / (AmE), / əˈɡəʊ / (BrE)
used after a time expression to show how much time has passed since something happened

Examples

A long time ago
I saw that movie a long time ago, but I still remember it well.
She left for Paris three years ago.
Two hours ago, it was raining heavily outside.
It seems like just yesterday, but it was actually months ago.
She left for college five years ago.
The company was founded a century ago.
