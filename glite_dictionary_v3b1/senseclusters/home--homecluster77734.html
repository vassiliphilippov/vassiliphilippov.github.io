home

verb
provide with or send to a home
5.5
Pronunciation: / hoʊm / (AmE), / həʊm / (BrE)
to provide someone with a home or to send someone to live in a place of care, such as an institution or foster home.

Examples

To home a kitten
After the rescue, volunteers worked to home the abandoned kittens with loving families.
Due to family issues, he was homed in an institution for several years.
The shelter aims to home as many stray dogs as possible each year.
