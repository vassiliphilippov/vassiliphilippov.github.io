dance-off

noun
competitive dance contest
Pronunciation: / ˈdæns ˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈdɑːns ˌɒf / (BrE)
a contest in which two or more people or groups compete by dancing to determine who is the best dancer

Examples

A dance-off contest
Many movies feature a dramatic dance-off scene to settle disputes.
She challenged him to a dance-off at the school talent show and won.
The crowd cheered loudly as the final dance-off began between the two rival crews.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dance competition - an event where dancers compete
