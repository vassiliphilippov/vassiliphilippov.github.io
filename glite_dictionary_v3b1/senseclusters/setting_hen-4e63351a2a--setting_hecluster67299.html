setting hen

noun
domestic hen ready to brood
9.9 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈset.ɪŋ hen/ (AmE), /ˈset.ɪŋ hen/ (BrE)
A domestic hen that is ready to brood eggs or is sitting on them to incubate.

Examples

A setting hen
A setting hen needs a quiet and comfortable place to raise her chicks.
It's important not to disturb a setting hen while she incubates her eggs.
The farmer separated the setting hen from the rest to make sure her eggs were safe.
