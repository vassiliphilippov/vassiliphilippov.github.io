Synonyms

    fittingly feels natural for describing a correct match or moment, while aptly also suggests a clever or well-chosen fit.
  • appropriately
    3.5 12/m

  • suitably
    3.0 1.68/m

  • properly
    1.0 47/m

  • befittingly
    6.8 0.01/m
