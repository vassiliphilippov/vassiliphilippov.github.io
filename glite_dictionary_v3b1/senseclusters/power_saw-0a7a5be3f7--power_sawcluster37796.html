power saw

noun
machine tool for cutting wood
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊər sɔ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə sɔː / (BrE)
a machine-driven tool with a toothed blade used for cutting wood or other materials

Examples

A power saw blade
Always wear safety goggles when operating a power saw.
He used a power saw to quickly cut through the wooden planks.
The carpenter bought a new power saw for his workshop.
