joyfully

adverb
in a very happy or delighted way
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪ.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪ.fʊ.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows or expresses great happiness or delight.

Examples

To joyfully celebrate success
After the final exam, the students joyfully celebrated their success.
She joyfully accepted the award on stage, beaming with pride.
The children laughed joyfully as they played in the park.
