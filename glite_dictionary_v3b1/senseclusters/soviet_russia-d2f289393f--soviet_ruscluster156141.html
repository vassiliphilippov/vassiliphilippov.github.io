Soviet Russia

proper noun
the largest republic of the former USSR
Pronunciation: / soʊˈviː.ət ˈrʌʃ.ə / (AmE), / səʊˈviː.ət ˈrʌʃ.ə / (BrE)
the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, the largest constituent republic of the former USSR, occupying eastern Europe and northern Asia
