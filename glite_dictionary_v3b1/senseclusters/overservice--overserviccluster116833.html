overservice

noun
excessive service
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvɪs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
the act of providing more service than is necessary, especially serving too much alcohol to customers

Examples

Alcohol overservice
The bar was fined for repeated overservice of alcohol to its patrons.
Some customers complained about the overservice they received, feeling overwhelmed by constant attention.
Strict laws are in place to prevent overservice in restaurants and bars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overprovision - the act of supplying more than is needed
  • antonym: underservice - the act of providing less service than is needed
