margining

noun
writing in margins
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrdʒɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːdʒɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of writing notes or comments in the margins of a text

Examples

Margining a book
Her careful margining of the novel made it easier to review for the exam.
Teachers often encourage margining as a way to engage more deeply with the reading material.
He found that margining helped him organize his thoughts while studying complex articles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annotating - adding notes or comments to a text
  • synonym: highlighting - marking important parts of a text
