basin

noun
wide, bowl-shaped container
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsən / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsɪn / (BrE)
a round, wide container with sloping sides, used for holding liquids or food, or the amount it can hold

Examples

A basin of water
Please put the fruit in that basin on the counter.
She washed the vegetables in a large basin before cooking.
The artist filled the basin with water to clean his brushes.
He carried a basin of soapy water out to wash the car.
She poured a basin of warm water into the sink for washing dishes.
The recipe calls for a basin of flour to make the dough.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basinful - the amount that a basin will hold
  • synonym: bowl - a round, deep dish for food or liquid
