Basque

noun
a member of an ethnic group in the western Pyrenees
Pronunciation: / bæsk / (AmE), / bask / (BrE)
a member of an ethnic group of unknown origin, traditionally living in the western Pyrenees region of France and Spain

Examples

A Basque person
He is proud to identify as a Basque, with deep roots in the region.
Many Basques still speak the ancient Basque language, known as Euskara.
The Basque have a unique culture that sets them apart from their neighbors.
