focusable

adjective
can be made sharp
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊkəsəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfəʊkəsəbəl / (BrE)
able to be brought into clear focus or made sharp in appearance

Examples

Focusable lens
This microscope has a focusable lens that allows you to see tiny details clearly.
The projector is easily focusable so the image stays sharp on the screen.
Make sure the camera is focusable before you try to take close-up photos.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjustable - able to be changed or set to a different position
  • antonym: blurred - not clear or sharp in appearance
