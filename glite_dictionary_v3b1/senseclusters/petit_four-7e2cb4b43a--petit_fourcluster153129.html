petit four

noun
small decorated cake
Pronunciation: / pəˌti ˈfʊr / (AmE), / ˌpeti ˈfʊə / (BrE)
A small fancy cake, typically decorated with icing and ornaments, often served as a dessert or with tea.

Examples

A petit four cake
At the party, each guest was served a colorful petit four with their cup of tea.
She arranged the petit fours on a silver platter for the afternoon reception.
The bakery specializes in intricately decorated petit four pastries.
