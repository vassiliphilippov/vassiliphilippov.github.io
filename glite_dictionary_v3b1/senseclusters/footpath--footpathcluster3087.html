footpath

noun
narrow path for pedestrians
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌpæθ /; / ˈfʊtpæθ / (AmE), / ˈfʊt.pɑːθ /; / ˈfʊtpɑːθ / (BrE)
a narrow path for people to walk on, often found away from roads or through the countryside
