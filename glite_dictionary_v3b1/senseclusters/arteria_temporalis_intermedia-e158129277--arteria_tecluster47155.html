arteria temporalis intermedia

noun
branch of temporal artery to middle temporal lobe
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪəriə tɛmˌpɔːrælɪs ˌɪntərˈmiːdiə / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə tɛmˌpɔːrælɪs ˌɪntəˈmiːdiə / (BrE)
A branch of the temporal artery supplying blood to the middle part of the cerebral cortex in the temporal lobe.
