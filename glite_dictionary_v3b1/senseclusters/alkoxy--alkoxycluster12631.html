relating to or containing an alkoxy group (—OR), where R is an alkyl group, in a chemical compound

Examples

Alkoxy compound
The alkoxy group increases the solubility of the compound in organic solvents.
Chemists often use alkoxy substituents to modify the properties of aromatic rings.
An alkoxy chain can affect the reactivity of a molecule in chemical reactions.
