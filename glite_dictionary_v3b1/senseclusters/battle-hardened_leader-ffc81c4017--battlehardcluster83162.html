battle-hardened leader

noun
experienced and resilient leader
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬l ˌhɑrdənd ˈliːdər / (AmE), / ˈbætl ˌhɑːdnd ˈliːdə / (BrE)
a leader who has gained experience and toughness through facing many conflicts or challenges

Examples

A battle-hardened leader
After years of crisis management, he became a battle-hardened leader trusted by his team.
As a battle-hardened leader, she remained calm under pressure and led her troops to victory.
Only a battle-hardened leader could guide the company through such turbulent times.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inexperienced leader - a leader lacking experience or resilience
  • synonym: seasoned leader - a leader with significant experience
