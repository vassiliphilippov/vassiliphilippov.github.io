nonguidance

noun
withholding guidance
9.5
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈɡaɪdəns / (AmE), / nɒnˈɡaɪdəns / (BrE)
a deliberate refusal or policy of not providing guidance or instructions, often to encourage independence

Examples

Nonguidance teaching style
The teacher's approach of nonguidance allowed students to explore solutions on their own.
Some leaders believe that nonguidance fosters creativity and independent thinking among employees.
A policy of nonguidance can be risky if team members lack experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autonomy - independence or freedom in making decisions
  • antonym: guidance - advice or information aimed at resolving a problem or difficulty
