Synonyms

  • rebellion
    4.1 8/m
    rebellion is large organized armed resistance by many people, while uprising is a general act of resistance that can be smaller or looser.
  • insurrection
    9.1 2/m
    insurrection is a violent attempt to overthrow authority, while uprising is a broader term for any organized resistance against power.
  • revolt
    4.6 8/m
    revolt is a forceful, often sudden challenge to a ruler or government, while uprising can mean organized resistance over time.
  • rising
    6.8 0.6/m
    rising is a somewhat older or historical word for an organized attempt to oppose authority, while uprising is more general and modern.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rebellion - an act of violent or open resistance to an established government or ruler
  • synonym: revolt - an attempt to overthrow the authority of a government
  • degree higher: revolution - a forcible overthrow of a government or social order for a new system
