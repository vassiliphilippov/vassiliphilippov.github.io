palm

verb
to conceal in hand
6.8
Pronunciation: / pɑm / (AmE), / pɑːm / (BrE)
to hide or hold something in the palm of your hand, often secretly or for a trick

Examples

To palm a coin

She tried to palm the key during the game without anyone noticing.
He was caught palming a coin during the trick.
