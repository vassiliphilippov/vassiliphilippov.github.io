jackshaft

noun
power-transmitting shaft
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒækˌʃæft / (AmE), / ˈdʒækˌʃɑːft / (BrE)
a shaft in a machine that transmits motion or power between different parts

Examples

The jackshaft in engine
The mechanic replaced the worn jackshaft in the engine.
A broken jackshaft can cause the entire machine to stop working.
The engineer inspected the jackshaft for signs of wear and tear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: axle - a rod or shaft that rotates and supports wheels
  • synonym: driveshaft - a shaft that transmits power from an engine to the wheels
