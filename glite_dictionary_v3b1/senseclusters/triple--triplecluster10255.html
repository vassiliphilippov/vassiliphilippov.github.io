triple

adjective
having three parts or units
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpəl /; / ˈtrɪp.əl / (BrE)
consisting of three parts, or three times as much or as many

Examples

A triple layer of chocolate makes this cake especially rich and indulgent.

He achieved a triple score compared to last year’s result.
The company reported a triple increase in profits this quarter.
The penalty allowed the plaintiff to claim triple damages for the wrongdoing.
