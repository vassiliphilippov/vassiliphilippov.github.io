family Trionychidae

noun
family of soft-shelled turtles
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli traɪˌɒnɪˈkɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli traɪˌɒnɪˈkɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of soft-shelled turtles characterized by their leathery shells and aquatic habits, found mainly in Asia, Africa, and North America.
