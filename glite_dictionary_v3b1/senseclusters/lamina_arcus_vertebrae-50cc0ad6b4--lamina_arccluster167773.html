Anatomical term for the flattened posterior part of the vertebral arch, from which the spinous process extends.

Examples

The lamina arcus vertebrae anatomy
Damage to the lamina arcus vertebrae can affect the integrity of the entire vertebral arch.
During surgery, the lamina arcus vertebrae may be removed to relieve pressure on the spinal cord.
The spinous process extends directly from the lamina arcus vertebrae in the spinal column.
