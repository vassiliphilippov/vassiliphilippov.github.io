Synonyms

  • vascular bundle
    5.5 0.02/m
    a vascular bundle is the common botanical term for a strand of xylem and phloem, while a vascular strand names the same structure more descriptively.
  • fibrovascular bundle
    8.7 0.00/m
    a fibrovascular bundle has extra fibrous support and conducts xylem and phloem, while a vascular strand is the general name for that tissue.
  • vein
    4.4 0.96/m
    a vein forms the branching ribs in a leaf and carries vascular tissue, while a vascular strand names the internal xylem and phloem strand in stems or leaves.
