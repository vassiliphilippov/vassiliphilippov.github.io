visitator

noun
archaic visitor
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪzɪˌteɪtər / (AmE), / ˈvɪzɪˌteɪtə / (BrE)
an individual who visits someone or a place for a short period; a visitor (archaic or rare)

Examples

A friendly visitator
The old records mention a visitator who inspected the estate every spring.
An unexpected visitator arrived at the manor to stay for an afternoon.
In the letter, she described her excitement at meeting a foreign visitator.

Related Terms

  • antonym: host - a person who receives or entertains guests
  • synonym: visitor - a person who visits a person or place
