wingsuit

verb
to glide in jumpsuit
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋˌsuːt / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋˌsuːt / (BrE)
to fly or glide through the air by wearing a special jumpsuit with fabric between the arms and legs.

Examples

To wingsuit off cliffs
He trained for months before he dared to wingsuit from the mountain peak.
Many extreme sports enthusiasts dream of wingsuiting over breathtaking landscapes.
After learning to skydive, she wanted to wingsuit for an even greater thrill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paraglide - to glide through the air using a fabric wing
  • synonym: skydive - to jump from an aircraft and free-fall before opening a parachute
