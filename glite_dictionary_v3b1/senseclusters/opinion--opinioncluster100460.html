A belief or sentiment held by most people; the collective view or prevailing sentiment of a community or group.

Examples

Public opinion
His statement went against the prevailing opinion in the community.
Public opinion can have a strong influence on government decisions.
The survey was conducted to measure opinion on environmental issues.

Synonyms

  • public opinion
    2.4 11/m
    public opinion shows what most people in a society think about an issue, while opinion is any belief held by a group.
  • popular opinion
    2.9 1.16/m
    popular opinion is the majority view seen in everyday talk and media, while opinion may refer to any view within a community.
  • vox populi
    8.9 0.04/m
    vox populi names the voice of the people in a strong, sometimes poetic way, while opinion is the group's general belief.
  • thought
    5.2 25/m
    thought covers broad ideas or trends across time or groups, while opinion refers to a specific belief held by a community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: view - a belief or way of thinking about something
