air cleaner

noun
device that filters dust from air
3.4 0.26/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…õr Àåklin…ôr / (AmE), / Ààe…ô ÀåkliÀên…ô / (BrE)
A device with a filter that removes dust and other particles from air passing through it.

Examples

A air cleaner device
He bought a portable air cleaner to reduce allergens in his apartment.
The air cleaner in my car needs to be replaced every year to ensure optimal performance.
The technician suggested installing an air cleaner to improve indoor air quality at the office.

Synonyms

  • air filter
    2.8 0.53/m
    an air filter is the replaceable element that traps dust inside systems, while an air cleaner is the whole device with that filter.
  • purifier
    3.5 0.39/m
    a purifier removes impurities from air or water and often uses filters or chemical methods, while an air cleaner mainly removes particles by filtering.
