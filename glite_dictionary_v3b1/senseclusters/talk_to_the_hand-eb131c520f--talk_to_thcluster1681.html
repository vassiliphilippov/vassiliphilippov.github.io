talk to the hand

x
dismissive phrase indicating disinterest
Pronunciation: / tɑk tə ðə hænd / (AmE), / tɔːk tə ðə hænd / (BrE)
used to tell someone that you do not want to listen to them or that their opinion does not matter to you; a dismissive expression indicating disinterest or refusal to engage.

Examples

Talk to the hand phrase
If you're looking for sympathy, you can just talk to the hand.
Rolling her eyes, Jessica made the 'talk to the hand' gesture and walked away.
When he tried to explain, she simply said, 'talk to the hand.'
