clumpy

adjective
full of lumps
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌmpi / (AmE), / ˈklʌmpi / (BrE)
having or forming lumps, clusters, or thick uneven masses

Examples

The forest floor was covered with clumpy patches of moss and leaves.
