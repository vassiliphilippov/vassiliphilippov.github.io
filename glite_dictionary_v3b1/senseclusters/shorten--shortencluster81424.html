shorten

verb
become shorter in length or duration
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrtən / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːtn̩ / (BrE)

Examples

Days shorten in autumn
As autumn approaches, the days shorten and the nights grow longer.
The skirt will shorten if you wash it in hot water.
With each passing year, the meetings seem to shorten in duration.
