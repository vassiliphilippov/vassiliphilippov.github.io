haul away

verb
remove by vehicle
6.5
Pronunciation: / hɔl əˈweɪ / (AmE), / hɔːl əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to take away or remove something, especially large or heavy items, using a vehicle

Examples

To haul away equipment
After the concert, workers hauled away the stage equipment by truck.
The company will haul away our old refrigerator tomorrow morning.
They need to haul away all the debris left after the storm.
