kingly

adjective
resembling or suitable for a king
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋli / (BrE)
Having the qualities, manner, or rank of a king; regal or royal.

Examples

Kingly dignity
He carried himself with a kingly dignity that impressed everyone in the court.
Her kingly robes shimmered in the candlelight during the ceremony.
The palace was decorated in a kingly fashion, with golden accents and luxurious fabrics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: regal - befitting or resembling a king or queen
  • synonym: royal - of or relating to a king, queen, or monarchy
