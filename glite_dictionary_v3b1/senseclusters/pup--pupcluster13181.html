pup

noun
inexperienced young person
6.5
Pronunciation: / pʌp / (AmE), / pʌp / (BrE)
an inexperienced or naive young person, especially one regarded with slight condescension

Examples

A young pup
Don't underestimate her just because she's a pup; she's got real talent.
He may act tough, but he's still just a pup in the business world.
When I started here, I was a young pup eager to learn from the older staff.

  • antonym: veteran - a person with long experience in a field
