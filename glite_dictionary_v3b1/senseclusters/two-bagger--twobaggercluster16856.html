two-bagger

noun
baseball term for double hit
Pronunciation: / ˈtuː ˌbæɡər / (AmE), / ˈtuː ˌbæɡə /; / ˈtuː ˌbaɡə / (BrE)
(baseball) a hit that allows the batter to reach second base; a double

After smacking a two-bagger, she slid safely into second base.

The rookie's first two-bagger of the season drove in two runs.
