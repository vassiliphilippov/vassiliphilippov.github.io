mycophagy

noun
the eating of fungi, especially mushrooms
Pronunciation: / maɪˈkɑːfəˌdʒi / (AmE), / mʌɪˈkɒfəˌdʒi / (BrE)
the practice of eating fungi, especially mushrooms, often those collected in the wild

Examples

Mycophagy of mushrooms
Her fascination with mycophagy led her to attend mushroom foraging workshops.
Interest in mycophagy has grown as people seek edible wild mushrooms.
The study focused on communities where mycophagy is a traditional part of the local diet.
