paganization

noun
making more pagan
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpeɪɡənʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something more pagan in character, influence, or practice

Examples

The paganization of festivals
Historians have studied the paganization of certain Christian festivals over the centuries.
The paganization of local traditions was met with resistance by some community leaders.
Some scholars debate whether the paganization of art forms was intentional or accidental.
