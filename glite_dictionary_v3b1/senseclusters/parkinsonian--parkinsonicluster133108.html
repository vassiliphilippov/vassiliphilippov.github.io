parkinsonian

noun
person with Parkinson's
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑrkɪnˈsoʊniən / (AmE), / ˌpɑːkɪnˈsəʊniən / (BrE)
a person who has Parkinson's disease or a related parkinsonian syndrome

Examples

A parkinsonian patient
The clinic treats every parkinsonian with individualized therapy to maintain mobility.
Support groups can help a parkinsonian cope with daily challenges.
A parkinsonian may experience tremors and difficulty with movement.
