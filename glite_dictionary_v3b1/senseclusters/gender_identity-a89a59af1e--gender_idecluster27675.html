gender identity

noun
a person's personal sense of gender
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒen.dər aɪ.ˈden.tɪ.ti/ /; / /ˈdʒen.dər aɪ.ˈden.tə.ti/ (AmE), /ˈdʒen.də aɪ.ˈden.tɪ.ti/ /; / /ˈdʒen.də aɪ.ˈden.tə.ti/ (BrE)
a person's internal sense of their own gender, which may or may not match the sex assigned at birth
