safe period

noun
least fertile phase of menstrual cycle
3.4
Pronunciation: / seɪf ˈpɪr.i.əd / (AmE), / seɪf ˈpɪə.ri.əd / (BrE)
the time during a woman's menstrual cycle when conception is least likely to occur, typically just before or after menstruation

Examples

The safe period in cycle
Doctors often warn that calculating the safe period can be unreliable for some women.
It is important to realize that the safe period is not a foolproof method of contraception.
Some couples rely on the safe period for natural family planning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fertile period - the time during a woman's menstrual cycle when conception is most likely to occur
