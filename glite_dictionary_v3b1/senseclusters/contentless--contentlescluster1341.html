contentless

adjective
without substance
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑntɛntləs / (AmE), / ˈkɒntɛntləs / (BrE)
lacking meaningful information, substance, or value

Examples

Contentless report
The report was so contentless that no one learned anything new from it.
Critics described the movie as visually stunning but ultimately contentless.
His apology sounded sincere but felt contentless to those who were hurt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: empty - having nothing inside; lacking meaning or substance
  • synonym: meaningless - having no meaning or significance
  • antonym: substantial - having real importance or value
