furry

noun
fan of animal characters
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜri / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəri / (BrE)
a person who is interested in or part of a community focused on anthropomorphic animal characters, often involving costumes or art

Examples

Furry convention
Many furries create their own animal costumes and attend conventions together.
Online communities allow furries to share artwork and stories about their favorite characters.
Some furries enjoy designing original animal personas called fursonas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cosplayer - a person who dresses up as characters from fiction
