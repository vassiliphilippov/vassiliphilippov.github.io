plantain tree

noun
tropical tree producing starchy plantains
3.1 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈplæn.teɪn triː/ /; / /ˈplæn.tɪn triː/ (AmE), /ˈplæn.teɪn triː/ /; / /ˈplæn.tɪn triː/ (BrE)
A tropical tree that produces large, angular greenish starchy fruits called plantains, similar to bananas, grown in the tropics and subtropics.

Examples

The plantain tree fruit
Farmers harvested the ripe fruits from the plantain tree early in the morning.
The plantain tree is a common sight in tropical regions where its fruit is a dietary staple.
Unlike sweet bananas, the fruit of the plantain tree is often cooked before eating.
