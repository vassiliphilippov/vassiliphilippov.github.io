lumberer

noun
timber merchant
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌmbərər / (AmE), / ˈlʌmbərə / (BrE)
a person who sells or supplies timber and sawn wood; a timber merchant

Examples

Local lumberer supplies wood
The construction company bought all their wood from a reliable lumberer in the area.
As a lumberer, he supplied high-quality timber to furniture makers across the city.
They ordered the floorboards from a local lumberer known for good prices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lumberjack - a person whose job is cutting down trees for timber
  • synonym: timber merchant - a person or business that sells wood and timber products
