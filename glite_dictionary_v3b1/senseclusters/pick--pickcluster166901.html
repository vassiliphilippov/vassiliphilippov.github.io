pick

noun
thin, sharp tool for removing material
Pronunciation: / pɪk / (AmE), / pɪk / (BrE)
a thin, sharp tool used for removing unwanted material or debris from a surface or cavity

Examples

Dental pick
A dental pick is often used to clean tight spaces in your mouth.
He used a pick to dislodge the food stuck between his teeth.
The archaeologist carefully scraped away soil with a small pick.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awl - a small pointed tool for making holes, especially in leather
  • synonym: probe - a slender instrument for exploring wounds or body cavities
