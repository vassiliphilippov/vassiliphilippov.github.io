sailplane

noun
engine-less glider aircraft
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlˌpleɪn / (AmE), / ˈseɪlˌpleɪn / (BrE)
a glider aircraft designed to fly without an engine, supported only by the lift generated by air flowing over its wings

Examples

A sailplane glides
He learned to fly a sailplane before trying powered aircraft.
Many aviation clubs offer sailplane lessons to introduce beginners to gliding.
The sailplane soared gracefully on the rising thermals above the valley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glider - an aircraft without an engine, designed to glide on air currents
