mannerist

noun
Mannerism artist
9.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmæn.ər.ɪst/ (AmE), /ˈmæn.ər.ɪst/ (BrE)
an artist or writer who follows the Mannerism style, especially in art or literature of the late Renaissance

Examples

A mannerist painter
The museum's new exhibition highlights the work of a renowned mannerist from Florence.
As a mannerist, she preferred exaggerated poses and complex compositions in her paintings.
Many critics consider El Greco to be a leading mannerist of his time.
