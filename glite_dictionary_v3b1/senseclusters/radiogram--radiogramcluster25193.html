radiogram

noun
message sent by radio telegraphy
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdiəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊɡram / (BrE)
a message sent by radio, especially one transmitted by wireless telegraphy

Examples

Urgent radiogram
During the war, families often waited anxiously for a radiogram from soldiers overseas.
He sent a radiogram to announce his safe arrival in New York.
The ship received a radiogram with urgent instructions from the mainland.

Related Terms

  • synonym: telegram - a message sent by telegraph
