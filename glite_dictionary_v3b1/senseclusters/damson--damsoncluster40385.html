damson

noun
a type of dark purple plum
Pronunciation: / ˈdæmzən / (AmE), / ˈdæmzən / (BrE)
a small, dark purple plum from the damson tree, often used for preserves and cooking

Examples

A damson plum
A bowl of fresh damsons brightened up the kitchen table.
She picked ripe damsons from the tree in the orchard.
The tartness of a damson makes it perfect for jams and jellies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plum - a sweet fruit with a smooth skin and a stone, of which the damson is a variety
