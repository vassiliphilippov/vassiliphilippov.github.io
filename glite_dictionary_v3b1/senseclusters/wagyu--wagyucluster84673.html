wagyu

noun
Japanese marbled beef
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːɡju / (AmE), / ˈwæɡjuː / (BrE)
a type of high-quality beef from Japanese cattle, known for its rich marbling and tenderness

Examples

Premium wagyu beef

Many food lovers travel to Japan just to taste authentic wagyu beef.
He was amazed by the tenderness and flavor of the wagyu served at the restaurant.
