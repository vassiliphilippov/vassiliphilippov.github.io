bricklayer

noun
person who builds with bricks
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪkˌleɪər / (AmE), / ˈbrɪkleɪə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to build walls, houses, and other structures with bricks

Examples

A bricklayer builds walls
During the restoration, a team of bricklayers worked carefully to match the original design.
His father was a skilled bricklayer who taught him the trade.
The bricklayer completed the garden wall in just two days.

Related Terms
