automorphic

adjective
unchanged under transformation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˈmɔrfɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmɔːfɪk / (BrE)
describing something (especially in mathematics) that remains unchanged or maps onto itself under a specific transformation or symmetry

Examples
