fuck it

interjection
expression of resignation or indifference
Pronunciation: / fʌk ɪt / (AmE), / fʌk ɪt / (BrE)
used to express resignation, indifference, or the decision to stop caring about the consequences of an action

Examples

Fuck it phrase
After struggling with the project all night, he finally said, fuck it and went to bed.
I'm tired of arguing about this—fuck it, let's do it your way.
She missed her exit on the highway and thought, fuck it, I'll just take the next one.
