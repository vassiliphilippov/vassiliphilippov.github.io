notable

adjective
worthy of notice or attention
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtəbəl /; / ˈnəʊtəbl / (BrE)
deserving attention because of being important, remarkable, or well known

Examples

A notable achievement
Her dedication to the project was notable and inspired everyone around her.
The scientist received an award for his notable contribution to medical research.
There has been a notable improvement in the city's air quality this year.
Several notable scientists attended the international conference.
She became a notable figure in the movement for climate action.
The museum's collection includes some notable pieces of modern art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: famous - known about by many people
  • synonym: noteworthy - worthy of notice or attention
  • antonym: obscure - not well known or difficult to understand
  • antonym: ordinary - not special or distinctive
  • synonym: remarkable - worthy of being noticed or extraordinary
