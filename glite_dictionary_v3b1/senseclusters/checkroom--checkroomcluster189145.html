checkroom

noun
room for storing coats or parcels temporarily
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛkˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛkˌruːm / (BrE)
a room in a public place where visitors can temporarily leave their coats, baggage, or parcels for safekeeping

Examples

Theater checkroom
Please leave your coat in the checkroom before entering the theater.
She picked up her umbrella from the checkroom after the conference.
The hotel lobby has a checkroom for guests' luggage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cloakroom - a room where coats and other belongings may be left temporarily, especially in public buildings
