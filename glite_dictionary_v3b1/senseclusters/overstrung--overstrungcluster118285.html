overstrung

adjective
strung or stretched too tightly
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈstrʌŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈstrʌŋ / (BrE)
strung too tightly or with excessive tension, especially referring to a stringed instrument or object under strain

Examples

Overstrung piano
An overstrung racquet may break more easily during intense matches.
Be careful not to leave your bow overstrung, as it can cause damage to the wood.
The violin sounded odd because it was overstrung and under too much tension.
