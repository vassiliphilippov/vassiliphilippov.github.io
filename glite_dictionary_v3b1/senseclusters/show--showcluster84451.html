Synonyms

  • display
    1.9 54/m
    display arranges items so people can look at them, while show is a general act of making something visible to others.
  • exhibit
    3.4 27/m
    exhibit presents things publicly in a formal space like a museum, while show is a more general or casual act of making visible.
  • present
    2.8 104/m
    present introduces or gives information to an audience in a formal way, while show focuses on making something visible or apparent.
