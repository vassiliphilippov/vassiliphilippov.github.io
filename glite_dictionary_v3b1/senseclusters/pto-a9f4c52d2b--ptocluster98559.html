PTO

noun
power-transfer device on vehicles
8.0
Pronunciation: / pi ti oʊ / (AmE), / piː tiː əʊ / (BrE)
a device for transferring power from an engine, especially on a tractor or other vehicle, to operate another piece of equipment

Examples

Tractor PTO shaft
Make sure the PTO is disengaged before performing any maintenance on the machine.
The farmer used the PTO on his tractor to power the hay baler.
The new attachment connects directly to the PTO for efficient operation.
