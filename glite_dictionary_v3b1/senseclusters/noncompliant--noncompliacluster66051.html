noncompliant

adjective
not obeying rules or authority
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.kəmˈplaɪ.ənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.kəmˈplaɪ.ənt / (BrE)
refusing or failing to obey rules, regulations, or requests; not compliant

Examples

Noncompliant behavior
Her refusal to follow the team's instructions was seen as noncompliant behavior.
Many patients were noncompliant with their medication schedules, leading to complications.
The school has a strict dress code, and students who are noncompliant may receive detention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliant - willing to comply with rules or requests
