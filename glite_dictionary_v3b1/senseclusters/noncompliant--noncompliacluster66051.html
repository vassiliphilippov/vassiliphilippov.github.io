Synonyms

  • disobedient
    4.0 0.96/m
    disobedient openly refuses to obey a person's orders or rules, while noncompliant is a more neutral failure to follow rules.
  • insubordinate
    8.1 0.30/m
    insubordinate refuses to obey orders and shows no respect to superiors, while noncompliant simply does not follow rules.
  • nonadherent
    7.8 0.01/m
    nonadherent does not stick to a required treatment or standard in medicine, while noncompliant is a broader failure to follow rules.
  • defiant
    5.6 4.36/m
    defiant shows bold, open resistance or anger toward authority, while noncompliant can be passive or just fail to follow requests.
  • recalcitrant
    8.1 1.30/m
    recalcitrant stubbornly resists control and refuses orders, while noncompliant may only fail to follow rules without such lasting resistance.
