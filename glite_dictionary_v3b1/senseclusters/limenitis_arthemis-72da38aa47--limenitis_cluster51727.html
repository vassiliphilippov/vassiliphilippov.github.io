Limenitis arthemis

proper noun
a North American butterfly species
Pronunciation: / lɪˌmɛnətɪs ɑrˈθɛmɪs / (AmE), / lɪˌmɛnɪtɪs ɑːˈθɛmɪs / (BrE)
