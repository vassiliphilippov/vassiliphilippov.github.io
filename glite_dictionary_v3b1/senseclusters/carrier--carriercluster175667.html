carrier

noun
vehicle rack for luggage, skis, etc.
Pronunciation: / ˈkæriər / (AmE), / ˈkæriə / (BrE)
a rack attached to a vehicle, such as a car or bicycle, used for carrying luggage, skis, or similar items

Examples

Bicycle carrier
She secured her suitcase to the bicycle carrier before leaving for the train station.
The van's rear carrier made it easy to transport extra camping gear.
We mounted a carrier on top of our car to hold our skis for the trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bike rack - a rack for carrying bikes on a vehicle
  • synonym: roof rack - a framework attached to the roof of a vehicle for carrying luggage
