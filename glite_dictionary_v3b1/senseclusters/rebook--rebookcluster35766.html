rebook

verb
to arrange again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbʊk / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbʊk / (BrE)
to arrange a new booking or reservation after a change, cancellation, or delay

If your appointment is canceled, you can easily rebook online.

The travel agent helped us rebook our hotel room when our plans changed unexpectedly.
