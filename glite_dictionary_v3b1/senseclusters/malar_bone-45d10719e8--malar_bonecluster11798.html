malar bone

noun
the cheekbone beneath the eye
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪ.lɑr ˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.lɑː ˌbəʊn / (BrE)
the bone forming the prominence of the cheek, situated below the eye; also known as the cheekbone or zygomatic bone

Examples

He felt pain and swelling over his left malar bone after being hit with the ball.
High malar bones are often considered a sign of beauty in many cultures.
The surgeon carefully repaired the fracture in her malar bone after the accident.
