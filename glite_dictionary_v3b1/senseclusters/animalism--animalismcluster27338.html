animalism

noun
focus on bodily appetites over the spiritual or intellectual
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.ɪ.məˌlɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈæn.ɪ.mə.lɪz.əm / (BrE)
preoccupation with or emphasis on the satisfaction of physical drives and appetites, especially as opposed to spiritual or intellectual pursuits

Examples

Uncontrolled animalism
His unrestrained animalism was evident in his pursuit of pleasure without thought for consequences.
She rejected animalism in favor of a more contemplative, spiritual life.
Some critics accuse modern society of animalism and a lack of higher values.

Related Terms

  • antonym: asceticism - severe self-discipline and avoidance of all forms of indulgence
  • synonym: hedonism - the pursuit of pleasure as a way of life
