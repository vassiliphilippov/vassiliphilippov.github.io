spiral out of control

verb
become unmanageable or chaotic
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪrəl aʊt əv kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / ˈspaɪərəl aʊt əv kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
to become impossible to manage or contain, often worsening rapidly and uncontrollably

Examples

If we don't address these issues soon, the situation could spiral out of control.
