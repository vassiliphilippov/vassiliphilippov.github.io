stormer

noun
outstanding success
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔrmər / (AmE), / ˈstɔːmə / (BrE)
an outstanding or very exciting performance, person, or event that is a great success

Examples

A stormer performance
Her solo was a stormer that had the whole audience on their feet by the end.
The new product launch was a real stormer and exceeded all expectations.
He played a stormer in the final match, scoring three goals for his team.
