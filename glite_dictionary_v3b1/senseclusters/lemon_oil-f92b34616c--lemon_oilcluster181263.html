lemon oil

noun
fragrant oil from lemon peel
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛmən ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən ɔɪl / (BrE)
a fragrant yellow oil obtained from the peel of lemons, used in cooking, perfumery, and aromatherapy

Examples

Lemon oil scent
Aromatherapists often use lemon oil to create a refreshing and uplifting environment.
Many natural cleaning products contain lemon oil for its fresh scent and antibacterial properties.
The baker added a few drops of lemon oil to enhance the flavor of the cake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lemon essence - a concentrated extract from lemon, usually used for flavoring
  • degree lower: orange oil - fragrant oil obtained from orange peel
