poststructuralist

adjective
relating to poststructuralism
9.9
Pronunciation: /poʊst.ˈstrʌk.tʃər.əl.ɪst/ (AmE), /pəʊst.ˈstrʌk.tʃə.rəl.ɪst/ (BrE)
relating to poststructuralism, especially theories that question fixed meanings, identities, and power structures in language or culture

Examples

Poststructuralist theory
The professor gave a poststructuralist interpretation of the novel, focusing on shifting meanings.
Her poststructuralist approach challenged traditional ideas about identity and language.
Many critics use poststructuralist theories to analyze cultural texts and power relations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deconstructive - relating to deconstruction, a method of critical analysis questioning fixed meanings
  • antonym: structuralist - relating to structuralism, a theory focusing on underlying structures in culture or language
