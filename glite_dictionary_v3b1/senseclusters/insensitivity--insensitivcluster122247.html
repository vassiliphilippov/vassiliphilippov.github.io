insensitivity

noun
lack of sensitivity to others
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˌsɛnˈsɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌɪn.senˈsɪv.ə.ti / (BrE)
lack of concern for the feelings or needs of others; inability to respond appropriately to emotional or social cues.

Examples

Emotional insensitivity
He was criticized for his insensitivity toward the victims' families.
Her insensitivity to his feelings made the situation worse.
The doctor's insensitivity surprised many of his patients.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sensitivity - the quality of being sensitive to others' feelings or needs
