lebkuchen

noun
German gingerbread biscuit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪpˌkuːkən /; / ˈleɪbˌkuːkən / (AmE), / ˈleːpˌkuːxən / (BrE)
a traditional German spiced gingerbread biscuit or cookie, often eaten at Christmas and sometimes decorated or made with honey and nuts
