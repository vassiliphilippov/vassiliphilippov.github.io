tackiness

noun
quality of being tasteless or vulgar
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtæki.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtæk.i.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being cheap, vulgar, or lacking in taste

Examples

Tackiness of decorations
Critics complained about the tackiness of the film’s special effects.
The tackiness of the decorations made the party feel less elegant than intended.
There was a certain tackiness to the souvenir shop’s merchandise, with glitter and plastic on everything.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elegance - the quality of being graceful and stylish in appearance or manner
  • synonym: vulgarity - the state or quality of being vulgar
