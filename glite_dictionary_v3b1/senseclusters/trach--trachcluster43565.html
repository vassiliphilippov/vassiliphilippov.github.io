trach

noun
windpipe; informal
9.6
Pronunciation: / træk / (AmE), / træk / (BrE)
an informal term for the trachea, the tube that carries air to and from the lungs

Examples

The trach is blocked
The doctor checked the patient's trach for any signs of blockage.
After the accident, she had trouble breathing through her trach.
A nurse explained how to care for a trach tube at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trachea - the main airway tube connecting the throat to the lungs
