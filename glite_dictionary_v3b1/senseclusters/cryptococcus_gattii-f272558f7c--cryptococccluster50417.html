Cryptococcus gattii

noun
environmental yeast species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪptəˈkɑːkəs ˈɡætiːaɪ / (AmE), / ˌkrɪptəˈkɒkəs ˈɡætiːaɪ / (BrE)
an environmental yeast species found in soil, decaying wood, and on certain trees such as eucalyptus

Examples

Cryptococcus gattii in soil
