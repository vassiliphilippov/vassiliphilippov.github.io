BC

adverb
before COVID-19 (informal)
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbiː.siː/ (AmE), /ˈbiː.siː/ (BrE)
(informal) Used to mark time as occurring before the COVID-19 pandemic; 'Before COVID-19'.

Examples

Life BC

People jokingly refer to pre-pandemic travel as the BC era when flights were easier.
