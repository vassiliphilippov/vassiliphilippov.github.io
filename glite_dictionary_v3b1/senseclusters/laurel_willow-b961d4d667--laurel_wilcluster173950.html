laurel willow

noun
European willow tree with glossy leaves
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔrəl ˈwɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈlɔːrəl ˈwɪləʊ / (BrE)
A European willow tree (Salix pentandra) with glossy, leathery leaves, widely naturalized in the eastern United States.

Examples

The laurel willow tree
Botanists have studied how the laurel willow adapts to environments outside its native Europe.
The park was lined with laurel willow, providing cool shade in the summer.
We planted a laurel willow by the pond to attract more wildlife.
