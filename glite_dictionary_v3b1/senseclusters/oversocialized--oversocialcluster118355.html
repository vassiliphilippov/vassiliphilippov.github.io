oversocialized

adjective
excessively influenced by norms
8.8
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈsoʊ.ʃə.laɪzd/ /; / /oʊ.vər.ˈsoʊ.ʃəl.aɪzd/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈsəʊ.ʃəl.aɪzd/ /; / /əʊ.və.ˈsəʊ.ʃə.laɪzd/ (BrE)
influenced excessively by social norms or expectations, often resulting in a lack of independence or individuality

Examples

Oversocialized children
Some theorists argue that modern children are oversocialized and lack independence.
The author claims that an oversocialized person may struggle to express their true feelings.
Critics worry that strict rules in schools can lead to oversocialized students who fear standing out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conformist - following accepted rules or standards too closely
  • antonym: independent - not influenced by others or by social expectations
