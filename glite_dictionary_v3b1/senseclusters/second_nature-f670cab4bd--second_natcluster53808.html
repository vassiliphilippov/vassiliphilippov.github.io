second nature

noun
an acquired habit that feels instinctive
1.3 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛkənd ˈneɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˌsekənd ˈneɪtʃə /; / ˌsɛkənd ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
a habit, skill, or behavior that feels automatic or instinctive because it has been practiced a lot

Examples

Second nature to someone
After years of working in customer service, patience had become second nature to her.
For a professional pianist, reading sheet music is second nature.
Wearing a seatbelt whenever he got in a car was second nature to him.
After so many years of painting, mixing colors became second nature to her.
Typing quickly is now second nature for most office workers.
With enough repetition, safe driving will become second nature to you.

Related Terms

  • synonym: habit - something done regularly and automatically
  • synonym: instinct - an innate, typically fixed pattern of behavior
