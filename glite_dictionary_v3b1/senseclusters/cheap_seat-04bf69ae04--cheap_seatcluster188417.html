cheap seat

noun
inexpensive seat at event
5.0
Pronunciation: / tʃip sit / (AmE), / tʃiːp siːt / (BrE)
a seat available at a low price, usually with a less desirable location or view at an event or performance

Examples

The cheap seat section
Fans in the cheap seat section cheered just as loudly as those near the field.
He couldn't see much of the stage from his cheap seat, but he enjoyed the concert anyway.
The stadium was packed except for a few cheap seats near the top.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nosebleed seat - the highest, most distant seat in a stadium or auditorium
