prolotherapy

noun
injection therapy for healing
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊloʊˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌprəʊləʊˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
a treatment in which substances are injected into injured tissues to stimulate healing and tissue repair

Examples

Prolotherapy treatment
Many athletes try prolotherapy to help recover from tendon injuries.
Her doctor recommended prolotherapy as an alternative to surgery for her knee pain.
Some studies suggest that prolotherapy may reduce chronic back pain in certain patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corticosteroid injection - an injection of steroids to reduce inflammation in tissues
  • synonym: regenerative medicine - medical treatments that stimulate the body's own repair mechanisms
