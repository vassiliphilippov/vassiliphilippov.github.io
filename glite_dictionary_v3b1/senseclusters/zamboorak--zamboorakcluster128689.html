zamboorak

noun
camel-mounted small cannon; historical cavalry unit
Pronunciation: / zæmˈbʊəræk / (AmE), / zæmˈbʊəræk / (BrE)
A small cannon mounted on a camel and used as mobile artillery by early modern armies in the Middle East and South Asia.

Examples

A zamboorak cannon
Historians have noted the psychological impact that zambooraks had on opposing troops unfamiliar with camel-mounted artillery.
In the 18th century, zambooraks became a signature feature of Persian military tactics.
The army deployed a squadron of zambooraks during the battle to surprise the enemy infantry.
