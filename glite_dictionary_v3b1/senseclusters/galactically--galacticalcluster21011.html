galactically

adverb
on a cosmic scale
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlæktɪkli / (AmE), / ɡəˈlæktɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to galaxies or on an extremely vast, cosmic scale

Examples

Galactically distant stars
The two teams were galactically different in terms of skill and experience.
Her ideas were galactically ambitious, aiming to change the world.
The stars in the night sky are galactically distant from our planet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astronomically - in an extremely large or vast way
  • antonym: locally - in a way that relates to a small or nearby area
