aide-memoire

noun
diplomatic memorandum; summary of an agreement
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪd meɪmˈwɑr / (AmE), / ˌeɪdmɛmˈwɑː / (BrE)
A written summary or memorandum, especially detailing the main points of an agreement, often used in diplomatic communications.

Examples

An aide-memoire note
After the meeting, an aide-memoire was circulated among the delegates for their records.
The ambassador presented an aide-memoire to summarize the terms of the proposed agreement.
The foreign minister referred to the aide-memoire during the negotiations to clarify each country's commitments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: memorandum - a written message in business or diplomacy
