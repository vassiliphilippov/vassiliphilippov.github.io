KO punch

noun
a punch that causes a knockout
4.0 4.7
Pronunciation: /keɪ oʊ pʌntʃ/ /; / /keɪ oʊ pʌnʃ/ (AmE), /keɪ əʊ pʌntʃ/ /; / /keɪ əʊ pʌnʃ/ (BrE)
