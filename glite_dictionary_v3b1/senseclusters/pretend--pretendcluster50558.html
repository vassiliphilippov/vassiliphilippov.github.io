pretend

adjective
imaginary; make-believe
4.5
Pronunciation: / prɪˈtɛnd / (AmE), / prɪˈtɛnd / (BrE)
imaginary; not real; existing only in play or fantasy

Examples

Pretend food
Children often serve pretend food at their imaginary tea parties.
She caught a pretend fish while playing by the lake.
The classroom corner was set up for pretend shopping with toy fruits and cash registers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: make-believe - imaginary; not real; created for play or fantasy
  • antonym: real - actually existing; not imaginary or pretend
