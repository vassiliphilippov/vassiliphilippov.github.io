substance abuse

noun
harmful use of drugs or alcohol
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbstəns əˈbjus / (AmE), / ˈsʌbstəns əˈbjuːs / (BrE)
the excessive or improper use of drugs or alcohol, especially when it leads to addiction or other harmful effects

Examples

Substance abuse problems
Raising awareness about substance abuse can help prevent addiction in young people.
She overcame her history of substance abuse and now helps others in recovery.
The clinic offers counseling services for those struggling with substance abuse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addiction - a condition of being addicted to a substance or activity
  • antonym: sobriety - the state of not being affected by alcohol or drugs
