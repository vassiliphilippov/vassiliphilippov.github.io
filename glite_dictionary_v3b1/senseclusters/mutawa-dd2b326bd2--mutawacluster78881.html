Mutawa

noun
member of Saudi religious police
9.9
Pronunciation: / muːˈtɑːwə / (AmE), / muːˈtɑːwə / (BrE)
A member of the religious police in Saudi Arabia responsible for enforcing strict adherence to Islamic law and codes of conduct.

Examples

Mutawa religious police
Critics argue that the Mutawa sometimes overstep their authority when dealing with foreigners.
During his visit to Riyadh, he encountered the Mutawa at a shopping mall.
The Mutawa patrol public spaces to enforce dress codes and public behavior.
