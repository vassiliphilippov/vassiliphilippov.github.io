somniloquism

noun
talking during sleep
9.0
Pronunciation: / sɑmˈnɪləkwɪzəm / (AmE), / sɒmˈnɪləkwɪzəm / (BrE)
the act or habit of talking or uttering speech while asleep

Examples

Somniloquism during sleep

People with somniloquism may be unaware they talk during the night.
