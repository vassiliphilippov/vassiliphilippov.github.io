regality

noun
royal quality
8.6
Pronunciation: / rɪˈɡæləti / (AmE), / rɪˈɡælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being royal, kingly, or majestic

Examples

Queen's regality
The queen entered the hall with an unmistakable regality that impressed everyone present.
His speech was delivered with such regality that the audience listened in awe.
The artist captured the regality of the ancient king in her painting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humbleness - the quality of being modest or humble
  • synonym: majesty - impressive beauty or dignity, often associated with royalty
  • synonym: royalty - the status or power of a king, queen, or royal family
