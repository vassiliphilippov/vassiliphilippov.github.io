at home

adverb
on the home team's field (sports)
2.8
Pronunciation: / ət hoʊm / (AmE), / ət həʊm / (BrE)
(in sports) on one's own team's field rather than at the opponent's field.

Examples

Play at home
After three consecutive games at home, the club faces a tough away schedule.
He always performs better when he's at home in front of the local crowd.
The team will be playing at home next Saturday against their biggest rivals.
