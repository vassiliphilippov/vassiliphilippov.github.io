low-calorie diet

noun
diet restricting calorie intake
1.0
Pronunciation: / loʊ ˈkæləri ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / ləʊ ˈkæləri ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet that restricts calorie intake to promote weight loss or improve health

Examples

A low-calorie diet
Doctors often recommend a low-calorie diet for patients with obesity.
Following a low-calorie diet helped her lose weight in a healthy way.
He struggled to stick to the low-calorie diet during the holidays.
