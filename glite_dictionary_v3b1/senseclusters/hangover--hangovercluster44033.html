hangover

noun
remaining thing from the past
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈhæŋˌəʊvə / (BrE)
something that remains from an earlier time or that has survived from the past

Examples

A cultural hangover
Many of the city's traditions are actually hangovers from colonial days.
That law is a hangover from a time when society was very different.
The ceremony felt like a hangover from the empire's glory years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remnant - a small remaining part from something larger
