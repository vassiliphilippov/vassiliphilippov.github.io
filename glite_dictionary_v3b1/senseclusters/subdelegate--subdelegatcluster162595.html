subdelegate

noun
assistant delegate
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌdɛlɪɡət / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌdɛlɪɡət / (BrE)
a person who acts as a delegate under the authority of another delegate

Examples

Team subdelegate
The subdelegate was responsible for organizing the team's daily meetings.
After the main delegate left, the subdelegate took over the negotiations.
Each committee appointed a subdelegate to assist with the conference arrangements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delegate - a person chosen to represent others
  • synonym: deputy - a person appointed to assist or act for another
  • antonym: superior - a person of higher rank or authority
