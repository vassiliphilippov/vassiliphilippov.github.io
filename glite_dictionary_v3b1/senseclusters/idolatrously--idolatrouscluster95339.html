idolatrously

adverb
in an idolatrous manner
3.6 7.9
Pronunciation: /aɪ.ˈdɑː.lə.trəs.liː/ /; / /ɪ.ˈdɑː.lə.trəs.liː/ (AmE), /aɪ.ˈdɒl.ə.trəs.liː/ /; / /ɪ.ˈdɒl.ə.trəs.liː/ (BrE)
In a manner that shows excessive admiration or reverence for idols or objects, often to the point of worship.

Examples

To idolatrously admire
He idolatrously admired every word and action of his mentor.
Some rulers of the past were idolatrously worshipped by their followers.
The crowd idolatrously cheered the celebrity as if she were a deity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: devoutly - in a deeply religious or pious manner
  • antonym: irreverently - in a disrespectful or irreligious manner
