witloof

noun
salad vegetable, type of chicory
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪt.luːf /; / ˈwɪtˌloʊf / (AmE), / ˈwɪt.luːf / (BrE)
a type of leafy vegetable (chicory or endive) with pale or blanched leaves, used in salads and cooking
