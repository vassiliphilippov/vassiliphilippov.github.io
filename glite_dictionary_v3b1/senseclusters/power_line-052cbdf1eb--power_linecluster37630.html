power line

noun
cable distributing electricity
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊ.ɚ laɪn / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə laɪn / (BrE)
A cable or set of cables used for transmitting and distributing electricity from a power source to consumers.

Examples

A power line cable
Never touch a fallen power line, as it may still be live and dangerous.
The storm knocked down a power line, causing a blackout in the neighborhood.
Workers repaired the damaged power line to restore electricity to the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: power grid - a network of power lines that distributes electricity
  • synonym: transmission line - a conductor or system for transmitting electric power or signals
