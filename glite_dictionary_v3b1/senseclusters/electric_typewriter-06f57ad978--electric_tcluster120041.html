electric typewriter

noun
typewriter powered by electricity
1.9 3.5
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈlek.trɪk ˈtaɪp.raɪ.tər/ /; / /ə.ˈlek.trɪk ˈtaɪp.raɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈlek.trɪk ˈtaɪp.raɪ.tə/ /; / /ə.ˈlek.trɪk ˈtaɪp.raɪ.tə/ (BrE)

Examples

The electric typewriter made typing long documents much easier.
