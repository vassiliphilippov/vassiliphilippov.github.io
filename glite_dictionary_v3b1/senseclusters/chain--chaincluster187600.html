chain

noun
unit of length (66 feet)
8.6
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪn / (AmE), / tʃeɪn / (BrE)
A unit of length equal to 66 feet (about 20.12 meters), commonly used in land surveying.

Examples

A surveyor's chain

In old land records, distances are often given in chains and links.
