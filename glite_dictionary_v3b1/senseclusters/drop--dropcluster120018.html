drop

verb
to say (something) casually or indirectly
Pronunciation: / drɑp / (AmE), / drɒp / (BrE)
to say something casually, especially when suggesting or revealing something indirectly

Examples

To drop a hint
During dinner, she dropped a remark about traveling to Europe soon.
He likes to drop famous names during conversations to impress people.
She decided to drop a hint about wanting a promotion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hint - a slight or indirect suggestion
  • synonym: mention - to refer to something briefly or casually
