Culex quinquefasciatus

noun
tropical mosquito species; disease vector
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːlɛks ˌkwɪŋkwəfəˈsiaɪɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈkjuːlɛks ˌkwɪŋkwɪfəˈsɪːətəs / (BrE)
