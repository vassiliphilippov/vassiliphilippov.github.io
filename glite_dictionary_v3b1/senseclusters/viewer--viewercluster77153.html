viewer

noun
person who watches TV or videos
1.0 1.2
Pronunciation: /ˈvjuː.ər/ /; / /vjuːr/ (AmE), /ˈvjuː.ə/ /; / /vjuː/ (BrE)
a person who watches or observes something, such as television, films, shows, or events

Examples

TV viewer
Some viewers complained about the quality of the subtitles during the broadcast.
Television viewers were shocked by the breaking news segment.
The documentary attracted millions of viewers on its first night.
As a viewer of the game, she cheered with the rest of the crowd.
Television ratings are calculated based on how many viewers watch a particular program.
The museum welcomed over a thousand viewers to the new art exhibition on opening night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audience - a group of people watching or listening to something
  • antonym: participant - someone who takes part in something (opposite of observer)
  • synonym: spectator - a person who watches an event, show, or activity
