dollhouse

noun
miniature toy house for children
1.0 0.63/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd…ëÀêlÀåha äs / (AmE), / Ààd…ílÀåha äs / (BrE)
A small model of a house, typically furnished and used as a toy by children.

Examples

A furnished dollhouse
As a child, she spent hours decorating her dollhouse with tiny furniture.
He bought a new set of miniature dishes for his sister's dollhouse.
The antique dollhouse in the museum is over a hundred years old.
