ergot

noun
a fungus infecting cereals, source of alkaloids
9.1 8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈɜːr.gət/ /; / /ˈɜːr.gɑːt/ (AmE), /ˈɜː.ɡɒt/ /; / /ˈɜː.ɡət/ (BrE)
