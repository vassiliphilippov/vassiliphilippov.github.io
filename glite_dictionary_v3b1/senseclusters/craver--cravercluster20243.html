craver

noun
person who begs
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪvər / (AmE), / ˈkreɪvə / (BrE)
a person who strongly desires or longs for something

Examples

In old stories, a craver might approach the king to plead for mercy.
She was known as a craver of knowledge, always seeking new books to read.
As a craver of adventure, she traveled the world.
The bakery was filled with cravers of sweet pastries early in the morning.
He became a craver of knowledge, always reading new books and articles.

  • synonym: petitioner - a person who formally requests something
  • synonym: seeker - a person who looks for or tries to get something
