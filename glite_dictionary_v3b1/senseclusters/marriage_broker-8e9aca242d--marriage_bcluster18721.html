marriage broker

noun
one who arranges marriages for others
Pronunciation: / ˈmærɪdʒ ˈbroʊkər / (AmE), / ˈmærɪdʒ ˈbrəʊkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to arrange marriages between others, typically for a fee

Examples

Marriage broker arranges weddings
In some cultures, a marriage broker plays a crucial role in arranging traditional weddings.
The marriage broker promised to introduce her to several eligible bachelors.
Their grandmother consulted a marriage broker to find suitable matches for her grandchildren.

Related Terms

  • synonym: matchmaker - a person who arranges marriages or assists others in finding a romantic partner
