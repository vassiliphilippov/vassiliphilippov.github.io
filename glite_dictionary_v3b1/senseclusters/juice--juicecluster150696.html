juice

noun
game audiovisual feedback
Pronunciation: / dʒus / (AmE), / dʒuːs / (BrE)
(informal, in video games) Visual and audio effects and other small touches added to interactions to make actions feel more satisfying, responsive, or impactful.

Examples

Extra juice in the game
The developer added extra juice to the hit effects so each strike felt powerful and clear.
Players praised the update for adding subtle juice like sound cues and screen shake to combat.
Indie studios often focus on small touches of juice to make simple mechanics feel satisfying and responsive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feedback - information (visual, audio, tactile) given to a player about the result of an action.
  • synonym: game feel - the overall tactile and sensory quality of interactions in a game; often achieved through visual and audio feedback.
