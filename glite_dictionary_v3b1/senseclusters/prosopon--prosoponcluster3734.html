prosopon

noun
theatrical persona
Pronunciation: / prəˈsoʊpɒn /; / ˈprɑːsəˌpɒn / (AmE), / prəˈsəʊpɒn /; / ˈprɒsəˌpɒn / (BrE)
a character or role in a play, especially in ancient Greek theater, or the mask worn by actors to represent that character or emotion
