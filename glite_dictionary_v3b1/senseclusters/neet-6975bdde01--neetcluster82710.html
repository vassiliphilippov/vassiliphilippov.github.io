NEET

proper noun
Indian medical entrance exam
Pronunciation: / niːt / (AmE), / niːt / (BrE)
the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test, an Indian national examination for admission to undergraduate medical courses

Examples

The NEET exam
After months of preparation, she finally took the NEET to pursue her dream of becoming a doctor.
Many students across India compete in the NEET each year for limited medical college seats.
Scoring well on the NEET is essential for admission to top medical universities in India.

Related Terms

  • synonym: MCAT - Medical College Admission Test, a standardized exam for medical school admission in the US and Canada
