smoking compartment

noun
train section for smokers
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊkɪŋ kəmˌpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkɪŋ kəmˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a section or car on a train designated for passengers who wish to smoke

Examples

A smoking compartment on train
He mistakenly reserved seats in a smoking compartment despite being a non-smoker.
In the past, travelers could choose a smoking compartment if they wanted to smoke during their journey.
The smoking compartment was often located at the end of the train to minimize smoke exposure to other passengers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: non-smoking compartment - passenger train section where smoking is prohibited
