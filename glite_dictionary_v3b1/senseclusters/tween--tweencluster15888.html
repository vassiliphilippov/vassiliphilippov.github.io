tween

verb
to create animation frames
8.3
Pronunciation: /twiːn/ (AmE), /twiːn/ (BrE)
to create intermediate frames or images between key frames in animation or computer graphics to produce smooth motion

Examples

To tween frames
The artist used a special tool to tween the character's movement between two key poses.
Modern animation software can automatically tween frames to create smoother transitions.
She learned how to tween simple shapes to make her first animated short film.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animate - to make drawings or images move in a sequence
  • synonym: interpolate - to insert or estimate intermediate values between two known values
