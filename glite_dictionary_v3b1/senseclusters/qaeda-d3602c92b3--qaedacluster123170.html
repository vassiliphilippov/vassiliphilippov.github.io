Qaeda

noun
Islamist militant organization
9.3
Pronunciation: / kɑːˈeɪdə / (AmE), / kɑːˈeɪdə / (BrE)
A militant Islamic organization founded in 1988, known for orchestrating terrorist attacks and opposing the United States and its allies.

Examples

Qaeda members
After years of searching, the military captured several high-ranking Qaeda members.
Intelligence officials warned of a potential Qaeda plot targeting major cities.
The government intensified its efforts to dismantle Qaeda operations within its borders.
