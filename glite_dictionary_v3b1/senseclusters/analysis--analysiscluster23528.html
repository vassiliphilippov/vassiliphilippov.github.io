analysis

noun
examining parts of something to understand it
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈnæləsɪs /; / əˈnæl.ə.sɪs / (AmE), / əˈnæləsɪs /; / əˈnæl.ɪ.sɪs / (BrE)
the process of examining something by breaking it into parts to understand it or draw conclusions

Examples

Detailed analysis of data
Detailed analysis of the painting showed its true age and origins.
Her analysis helped the team understand the project's strengths and weaknesses.
The scientist's analysis of the data revealed several important patterns.
Our financial analysis revealed several unexpected trends in last year's sales data.
The scientist conducted a thorough analysis of the soil samples.
Through careful analysis of the novel, the professor uncovered its deeper themes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: examination - the act of inspecting or analyzing in detail
  • antonym: synthesis - the combination of parts to form a whole
