finger

verb
touch or examine with fingers
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡɚ /; / ˈfɪŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
to touch or handle something with your fingers, often in a deliberate or curious way

Examples

To finger the fabric
She fingered the delicate lace, admiring its intricate patterns.
The detective fingered the envelope, searching for hidden evidence inside.
While waiting anxiously, he fingered the loose thread on his sleeve.
As they waited, Julia fingered the pearls around her neck.
He absentmindedly fingered the strings of his guitar while thinking.
She fingered the fabric to check its texture before buying it.

  • synonym: touch - to make contact with something, especially with a part of the body
