overvoting

noun
invalid ballot selection
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈvoʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈvəʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of selecting more choices than permitted on a ballot, causing the vote to be invalidated

Examples

Overvoting on a ballot
The election officials discarded the ballot because of overvoting in the city council section.
Voters are instructed to avoid overvoting to ensure their choices are counted.
A high rate of overvoting can affect the outcome of a close election.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spoiled ballot - a ballot that is invalid due to marking errors
  • antonym: undervoting - selecting fewer choices than allowed on a ballot
