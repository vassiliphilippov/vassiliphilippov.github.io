deerfly

noun
biting outdoor fly
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪrˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈdɪəˌflaɪ / (BrE)
a small biting fly, often found outdoors, that feeds on the blood of humans and animals

Examples

A deerfly bite
While camping, a deerfly landed on my arm and bit me.
Farmers often wear hats with nets to protect themselves from deerflies in the summer.
The bite from a deerfly can be very itchy and annoying.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horsefly - a large biting fly that feeds on blood
  • synonym: mosquito - a small flying insect that bites and sucks blood
