anagnorisis

noun
moment of realization
9.9
Pronunciation: /æn.ə.ɡ.ˈnɑːr.ɪ.sɪs/ (AmE), /æn.ə.ɡ.ˈnɒ.rɪ.sɪs/ (BrE)
the moment in a story or play when a character suddenly realizes something important, especially about themselves or their situation
