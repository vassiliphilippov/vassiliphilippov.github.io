thoroughwort

noun
herbaceous plant of southeastern US
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜːroʊˌwɜːrt / (AmE), / ˈθʌrəwɜːt / (BrE)
A perennial herb native to the southeastern United States, with white-rayed flower heads, once used in folk medicine.

Examples

The thoroughwort plant
Botanists studied the medicinal properties of thoroughwort in the region's wetlands.
Native Americans traditionally used thoroughwort in remedies for fevers and colds.
The meadow was dotted with blooming thoroughwort among other wildflowers.
