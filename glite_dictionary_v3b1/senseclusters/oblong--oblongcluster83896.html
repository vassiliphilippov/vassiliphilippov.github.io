oblong

noun
elongated rectangular or oval shape
Pronunciation: / ˈɑb.lɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈɒb.lɒŋ / (BrE)
A plane figure that is longer in one direction than the other, especially a rectangle with unequal adjacent sides.

Examples

An oblong shape
She drew an oblong on the chalkboard to represent the garden plot.
The architect designed windows in the shape of an oblong.
The tablecloth was patterned with rows of yellow oblongs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oval - a plane curve resembling a stretched circle
  • synonym: rectangle - a four-sided figure with opposite sides equal and all angles right angles
  • antonym: square - a plane figure with four equal sides and four right angles
