rend

verb
tear or be torn violently
Pronunciation: / rɛnd / (AmE), / rɛnd / (BrE)
to tear something violently or be torn apart forcefully

Examples

To rend clothes
In ancient rituals, mourners would rend their garments as a sign of grief.
She could not bear to rend the old letters, though their words brought only pain.
The earthquake caused the ground to rend open, leaving a deep crevice.
