digital clock

noun
clock that shows time with digits
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒɪt̬əl klɑk / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl klɒk / (BrE)
A clock that shows the time using digits rather than hands on a dial.

Examples

A digital clock display
He prefers a digital clock because it's easier to read in the dark.
She glanced at the digital clock on her bedside table and realized she was late.
The digital clock in the classroom showed 10:25 AM.

Related Terms

  • antonym: analog clock - a clock that displays time with hands on a dial
