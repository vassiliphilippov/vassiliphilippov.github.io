neck bone

noun
vertebra in the neck; cervical vertebra
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛk ˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈnɛk bəʊn / (BrE)
one of the vertebrae located in the neck region of the human spine; also called a cervical vertebra.

Examples

The neck bone vertebra
Children often sing about the neck bone in the song 'Dem Bones.'
He suffered a fracture to his neck bone in the car accident.
The X-ray revealed that one of her neck bones was out of alignment.

Related Terms
