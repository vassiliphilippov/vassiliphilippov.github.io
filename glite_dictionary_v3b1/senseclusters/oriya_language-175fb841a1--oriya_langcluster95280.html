Oriya language

noun
language of Odisha, India
Pronunciation: / oʊˈriːjə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɒˈrɪə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Indo-Aryan language spoken mainly in the Indian state of Odisha (formerly Orissa) and by the Oriya people

Oriya language spoken in Odisha
