sappiness

noun
foolishness
Pronunciation: / ˈsæpinəs / (AmE), / ˈsæpinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being foolish, lacking good sense, or showing poor judgment

Examples

His sappiness showed
His sappiness was obvious when he believed the scam email without question.
The movie was criticized for its sappiness and lack of realistic characters.
She regretted her sappiness after trusting someone she barely knew.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foolishness - lack of good sense or judgment
  • antonym: wisdom - the quality of having experience, knowledge, and good judgment
