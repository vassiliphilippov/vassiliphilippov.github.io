lordless

adjective
without a lord or master
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔrd.ləs / (AmE), / ˈlɔːd.ləs / (BrE)
Without a lord, master, or governing authority; lacking a person in charge or ownership.

Examples

Lordless villages
Medieval chroniclers described lordless villages abandoned after the plague.
She wrote a poem about lordless knights wandering in search of honor.
The lordless land quickly became a haven for rebels and outlaws.
