durum

noun
hard wheat variety used for pasta and bread
9.1 5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʊr.əm / (AmE), / ˈdjʊə.rəm / (BrE)
a type of hard wheat with dark kernels, high in gluten, used for making bread and pasta

Examples

Hard durum wheat
Farmers in North Dakota cultivate durum for both domestic use and export.
The bakery uses durum flour to make hearty loaves of bread.
Traditional Italian pasta is often made from durum wheat because of its high gluten content.
