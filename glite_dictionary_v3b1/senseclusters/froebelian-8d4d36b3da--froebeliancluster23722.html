Froebelian

adjective
of Froebel's methods
Pronunciation: / froʊˈbɛliən / (AmE), / frəʊˈbɛliən / (BrE)
relating to the educational theories or methods developed by Friedrich Froebel, especially for young children

Examples

Froebelian approach
The preschool's Froebelian philosophy emphasizes play and creativity in early learning.
Many teachers incorporate Froebelian principles when designing activities for young children.
A Froebelian classroom often features blocks and hands-on materials for exploration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Montessorian - relating to the educational methods of Maria Montessori
  • antonym: traditional - following established or long-standing educational methods
