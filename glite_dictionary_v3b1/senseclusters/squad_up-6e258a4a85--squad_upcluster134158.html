squad up

verb
form or join a small team (informal, gaming)
8.9
Pronunciation: / skwɑd / (AmE), / skwɒd / (BrE)
(informal) to form or join a small, coordinated group for a particular task or activity, especially in online gaming.

Examples

To squad up before game
Before the raid, the players decided to squad up and plan their roles carefully.
We usually squad up on weekends to try the new co-op challenges together.
If you want better loot, squad up with experienced teammates who know the routes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: team up - to join with others to work together on a task
