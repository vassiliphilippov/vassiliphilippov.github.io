naughtily

adverb
in a disobedient or mischievous way
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔːtɪli / (AmE), / ˈnɔːtɪli / (BrE)
In a way that is disobedient or mischievous, especially in a manner that breaks rules or behaves badly.

Examples

To naughtily break rules
She looked naughtily at her brother after playing the prank.
The child giggled naughtily when he hid his father's keys.
The puppy naughtily chewed on the slippers despite being told not to.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mischievously - in a playful or teasingly naughty way
  • antonym: obediently - in a way that shows willingness to obey
