drip down

verb
fall or flow downward in drops
1.8
Pronunciation: / drɪp daʊn / (AmE), / drɪp daʊn / (BrE)
(of a liquid) to fall or flow downward in small drops.

Examples

To drip down slowly
Rainwater would drip down from the leaky roof after every storm.
Sweat began to drip down his forehead during the intense workout.
The candle wax dripped down the side of the old brass holder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trickle down - to flow slowly and in a small stream downward
