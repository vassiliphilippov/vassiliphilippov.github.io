Nikkei

noun
Japanese-Peruvian cuisine
8.4
Pronunciation: / niːˈkeɪ / (AmE), / niːˈkeɪ / (BrE)
a style of cuisine developed by Japanese immigrants in Peru that combines Japanese cooking techniques with Peruvian ingredients and flavors

Examples

Nikkei cuisine
The new restaurant in town serves authentic Nikkei dishes that highlight both Japanese and Peruvian flavors.
