Nikkei

noun
Japanese-Peruvian cuisine
8.4
Pronunciation: / niːˈkeɪ / (AmE), / niːˈkeɪ / (BrE)
a style of cuisine developed by Japanese immigrants in Peru that combines Japanese cooking techniques with Peruvian ingredients and flavors

Examples

Nikkei cuisine
The new restaurant in town serves authentic Nikkei dishes that highlight both Japanese and Peruvian flavors.
She learned to prepare Nikkei cuisine after traveling to Lima and studying with local chefs.
Many food critics praise Nikkei for its creative use of fresh seafood and Peruvian spices.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: Japanese cuisine - the traditional food and cooking style of Japan
  • degree lower: Peruvian cuisine - the traditional food and cooking style of Peru
  • synonym: fusion cuisine - a style of cooking that blends elements from different culinary traditions
