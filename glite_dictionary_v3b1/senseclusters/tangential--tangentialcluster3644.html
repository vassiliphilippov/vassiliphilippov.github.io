tangential

adjective
relating to a tangent or tangency
6.0 6.8
Pronunciation: /tæn.ˈdʒen.ʃəl/ (AmE), /tæn.ˈdʒen.ʃəl/ (BrE)
relating to or acting along the direction of a tangent; touching a curve or surface at a single point without crossing it.

Examples

Tangential line to a circle
In geometry class, we learned how a line can be tangential to a circle at just one point.
The engineer explained how tangential forces act on the spinning wheel.
The moon's tangential velocity prevents it from falling into the Earth.
