concert piano

noun
large grand piano for concerts
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnsɚt piˈænoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsət piˈænəʊ / (BrE)
a large, high-quality grand piano designed for use in concert performances

Examples

A concert piano
