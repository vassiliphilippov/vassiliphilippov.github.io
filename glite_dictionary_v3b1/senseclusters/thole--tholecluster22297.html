thole

noun
rowing oar holder on a boat
Pronunciation: / θoʊl / (AmE), / θəʊl / (BrE)
A pin or holder on the gunwale of a boat that keeps the oar in place and serves as a pivot when rowing.

Examples

The thole pin
The rower secured the oar in the thole before setting out on the lake.
Without a proper thole, it is difficult to row efficiently.
Years of use had left grooves in the wooden tholes of the old boat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oarlock - a device that holds an oar in place on a boat
