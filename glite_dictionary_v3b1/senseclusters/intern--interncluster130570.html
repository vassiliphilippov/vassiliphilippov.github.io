intern

noun
student trainee
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌtɝn / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌtɜːn / (BrE)
a student or recent graduate who works, often temporarily, to gain practical experience in a profession

Examples

A summer intern
The intern assisted the marketing team with their latest campaign.
As an intern, he gained valuable experience in a busy law office.
Each summer, the company hires several interns to help with special projects.
The hospital hired several interns to assist doctors during the busy summer months.
As an intern, she learned valuable skills that helped her secure a full-time job.
Many companies offer intern positions to help students gain real-world experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apprentice - a person learning a trade from a skilled employer
  • antonym: mentor - an experienced person who guides and advises someone less experienced
  • antonym: supervisor - a person who oversees the work of others
  • synonym: trainee - a person undergoing training for a job
