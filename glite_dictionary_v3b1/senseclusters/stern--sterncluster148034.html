stern

adjective
strict or forbidding in appearance
Pronunciation: / stɝn / (AmE), / stɜːn / (BrE)
serious, strict, and showing no signs of weakness or giving in; unrelenting in attitude or manner

Examples

Stern warning
He gave me a stern look when I broke the rule.
Her stern expression made it clear she would tolerate no nonsense.
The teacher's stern voice silenced the class immediately.

The teacher was known for her stern approach to discipline in the classroom.
Under her stern supervision, the team's performance improved significantly.
