Endecott

proper noun
English founder of Massachusetts Bay Colony
Pronunciation: / ˈɛndɪˌkɑt / (AmE), / ˈɛndɪkət / (BrE)
John Endecott (1588–1665), an English colonial administrator who served as a founder and early governor of the Massachusetts Bay Colony.

Examples

Endecott served several terms as governor of the Massachusetts Bay Colony.
A statue of Endecott stands in Boston to honor his legacy.
