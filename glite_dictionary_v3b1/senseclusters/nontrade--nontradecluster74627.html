nontrade

adjective
not involving trades
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑnˌtreɪd / (AmE), / ˈnɒnˌtreɪd / (BrE)
not relating to a skilled manual trade or to workers with specific trade qualifications

Examples

Nontrade workers

Many nontrade positions, such as cleaners and security guards, are essential to the project's success.
Unlike electricians or plumbers, nontrade workers do not require specialized trade qualifications.
