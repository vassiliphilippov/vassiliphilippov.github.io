Sokoro language

noun
Chadic language of Chad
Pronunciation: / soʊˈkoʊroʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / səʊˈkɔːrəʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in the Guera Region of Chad, particularly in the Bahr Signaka department and surrounding areas.

Examples

Sokoro language spoken in Chad
Linguists have studied the Sokoro language to preserve its unique vocabulary and grammar.

The Sokoro language is still spoken by thousands of people in the Guera Region of Chad.
