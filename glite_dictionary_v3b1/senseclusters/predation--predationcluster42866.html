predation

noun
the act of plundering or pillaging
Pronunciation: / prɛˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / prɪˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of attacking and robbing or plundering; the practice of pillaging or marauding.

Examples

Bandit predation
Centuries ago, predation by roving bands shaped the history of entire regions.
The predation of the coastal villages left many families homeless and afraid.
The company's aggressive takeovers were seen as a kind of corporate predation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: peace - freedom from disturbance; absence of war or violence
  • synonym: pillaging - the act of robbing a place using violence, especially in wartime
  • synonym: plunder - to steal goods, typically using force and in a time of disorder
