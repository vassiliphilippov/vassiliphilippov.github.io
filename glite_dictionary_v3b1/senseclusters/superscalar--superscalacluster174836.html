superscalar

noun
multi-instruction processor
9.9
Pronunciation: /suː.pər.ˈskeɪ.lər/ /; / /ˈsuː.pər.skeɪ.lər/ (AmE), /sjuː.pə.ˈskeɪ.lə/ /; / /suː.pə.ˈskeɪ.lə/ (BrE)

Examples

A superscalar chip

A superscalar can increase efficiency by executing multiple operations in parallel.
