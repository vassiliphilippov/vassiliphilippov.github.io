dottore

noun
commedia dell'arte doctor
Pronunciation: / doʊˈtɔːreɪ / (AmE), / dɒˈtɔːreɪ / (BrE)
a stock character in Italian commedia dell'arte, typically portrayed as a pompous and foolish doctor

Examples

The dottore character
The dottore entered the stage, boasting about his knowledge but confusing everyone with nonsense.
In traditional Italian theater, the dottore is often the target of jokes for his foolishness.
The actor played the dottore with exaggerated gestures and a booming voice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doctor - a person qualified to practice medicine
  • synonym: pantalone - a stock character in commedia dell'arte, usually a greedy old man
