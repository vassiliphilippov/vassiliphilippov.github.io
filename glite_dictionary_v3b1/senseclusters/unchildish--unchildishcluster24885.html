unchildish

adjective
not childlike
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃaɪldɪʃ / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃaɪldɪʃ / (BrE)
not typical of a child or not showing childlike qualities such as innocence or immaturity

Examples

Unchildish attitude
His unchildish attitude impressed the adults at the meeting.
She handled the difficult situation in a very unchildish way for her age.
It was unchildish of him to apologize so sincerely after the argument.

Related Terms

  • antonym: childish - typical of a child, especially in a negative way
  • synonym: mature - showing adult qualities or behavior
