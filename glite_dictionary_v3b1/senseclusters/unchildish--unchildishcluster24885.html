unchildish

adjective
not childlike
7.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈtʃaɪl.dɪʃ/ /; / /ən.ˈtʃaɪl.dɪʃ/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈtʃaɪl.dɪʃ/ /; / /ən.ˈtʃaɪl.dɪʃ/ (BrE)

Examples

Unchildish attitude

She handled the difficult situation in a very unchildish way for her age.
