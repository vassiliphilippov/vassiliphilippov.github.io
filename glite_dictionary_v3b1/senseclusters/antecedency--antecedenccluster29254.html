antecedency

noun
state of preceding in time
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tɪˈsiː.dən.si / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈsiː.dən.si / (BrE)
The state or condition of being earlier or preceding in time.

Examples

The antecedency of events
In legal disputes, the antecedency of contracts is often examined.
The antecedency of the event made it significant for historical analysis.
Understanding the antecedency of causes helps explain complex phenomena.

Related Terms

  • antonym: posteriority - the state of coming after in time or order
  • synonym: precedence - the condition of coming before in order or importance
