significant figure

noun
meaningful digit in a number; precision of measurement
Pronunciation: / sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt ˈfɪɡjə / (BrE)
each of the digits of a number that are meaningful in terms of its precision, starting with the first non-zero digit and ending with either the last non-zero digit or a zero that is known with certainty.

Examples

Significant figures in numbers
Ensure your final calculation retains only the correct number of significant figures.
Scientists often report measurements using three significant figures.
The answer should be rounded to two significant figures for accuracy.
