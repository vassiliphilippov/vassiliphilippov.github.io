playa

noun
beach or dry lake bed
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈplaɪ.ə/ /; / /ˈpleɪ.ə/ (AmE), /ˈplaɪ.ə/ /; / /ˈpleɪ.ə/ (BrE)
a flat area of land, either a beach (especially in Spanish-speaking regions) or a dry lake bed in a desert

Examples

Sandy playa
We spent the afternoon relaxing on the playa near the coast.
The festival takes place on a vast, flat playa in the desert.
Many migratory birds stop at the playa when it fills with water after heavy rains.
The car sped across the dusty playa under the hot sun.
After the rare rainstorm, a shallow lake formed on the playa before quickly evaporating.
Scientists studied the unique plants that grow along the edge of the playa.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beach - a sandy or pebbly shore by a body of water
  • synonym: dry lake - a lake bed that is dry most of the time
  • synonym: lake bed - the bottom of a lake, often dry in arid regions
  • synonym: salt flat - a flat area covered with salt left by evaporated water
