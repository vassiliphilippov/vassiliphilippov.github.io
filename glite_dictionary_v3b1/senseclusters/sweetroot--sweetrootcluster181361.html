sweetroot

noun
plant with sweet root
Pronunciation: / ˈswiːtˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈswiːtˌruːt / (BrE)
a plant with a sweet-tasting root, such as licorice or sweet cicely, often used for flavoring or medicinal purposes
