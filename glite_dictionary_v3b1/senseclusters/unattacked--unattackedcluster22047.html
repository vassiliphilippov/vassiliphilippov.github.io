unattacked

adjective
not attacked
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈtækt / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈtækt / (BrE)
not having been attacked or remaining unharmed by an attack or threat

Examples

An unattacked island
The remote island remained unattacked throughout the entire conflict.
Several houses were left unattacked after the wildfire swept through the area.
The rare painting was one of the few artifacts that stayed unattacked by vandals.
