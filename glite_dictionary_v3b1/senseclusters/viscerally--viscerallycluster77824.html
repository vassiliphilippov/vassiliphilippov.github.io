viscerally

adverb
in an instinctive, gut-driven manner
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪsərəli / (AmE), / ˈvɪsərəli / (BrE)
In a way that is based on deep, instinctive feelings rather than logical reasoning.

Examples

To viscerally feel
He viscerally disliked dishonesty, finding it intolerable on a gut level.
She reacted viscerally to the disturbing news, feeling anger before she could rationalize it.
The film's violence affects viewers viscerally, stirring strong emotions deep within.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instinctively - in a manner based on instinct
  • antonym: rationally - in a logical or reasoned manner
