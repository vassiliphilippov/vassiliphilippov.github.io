concert-goer

noun
person who attends concerts
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːnsɚtˌɡoʊər / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsətˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
a person who regularly attends musical concerts

Examples

A concert-goer fan
As a passionate concert-goer, she has seen dozens of live performances this year.
Many concert-goers bought merchandise to remember the unforgettable evening.
The venue was crowded with concert-goers excited to hear the famous band.
