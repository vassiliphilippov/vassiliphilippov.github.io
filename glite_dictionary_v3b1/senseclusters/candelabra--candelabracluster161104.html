candelabra

noun
ornamental branched candlestick holder
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˌkændəˈlɑːbrə / (AmE), / ˌkandɪˈlɑːbrə / (BrE)
A large branched candlestick holder designed to hold several candles, often used as an ornamental centerpiece.

Examples

A candelabra holder
A pair of antique candelabra stood on either side of the fireplace.
She polished the silver candelabra before the guests arrived for dinner.
The ornate candelabra in the dining hall held dozens of flickering candles.

  • synonym: candlestick - a holder for a single candle
