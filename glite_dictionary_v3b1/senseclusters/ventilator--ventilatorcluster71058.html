a machine that helps a person breathe by moving air in and out of the lungs, typically used when they are unable to do so unaided

Examples

Hospital ventilator
During the pandemic, hospitals faced a shortage of ventilators for critically ill patients.
She recovered well enough to be taken off the ventilator after several days.
The patient was placed on a ventilator to help him breathe after the surgery.

Synonyms

  • breathing machine
    3.4 0.12/m
    a breathing machine moves air to help a patient breathe in hospital, while a ventilator is the clinical machine used for that support.
  • respirator
    4.9 0.80/m
    a respirator provides artificial breathing over a long time, while a ventilator is the hospital machine that forces air into the lungs.
  • breathing apparatus
    4.4 0.13/m
    a breathing apparatus is any equipment to help breathing in hard situations, while a ventilator is the machine used in hospitals.
  • breathing device
    3.9 0.03/m
    a breathing device is a general medical tool to support breathing, while a ventilator is the specific machine that moves air in lungs.
