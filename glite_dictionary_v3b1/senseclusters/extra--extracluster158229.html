extra

noun
minor background actor in film/TV
5.3 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈek.strə/ (AmE), /ˈek.strə/ (BrE)
a minor actor in a film or television show who appears in a non-speaking or background role, especially as part of a crowd scene

Examples

A movie extra
Being an extra is a great way to experience a film set without acting experience.
She worked as an extra on several television shows before landing a speaking role.
The director needed a hundred extras to fill the stadium for the movie scene.
