Myles Standish

proper noun
English military leader of the Plymouth Colony
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪlz ˈstændɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪlz ˈstændɪʃ / (BrE)
(1584–1656) An English military officer and leader who played a key role in the establishment of the Plymouth Colony in America, serving as an advisor and military leader to the Pilgrims.
