garnish

verb
decorate food with other items
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːnɪʃ / (BrE)
to decorate food, especially with small amounts of other foods for added color or flavor

Examples

To garnish a salad
She likes to garnish her salads with slices of fresh orange and mint.
The chef will garnish the soup with a sprinkle of chopped parsley before serving.
You can garnish the cake with berries or chocolate shavings for a beautiful presentation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decorate - to make something look more attractive by adding items to it
