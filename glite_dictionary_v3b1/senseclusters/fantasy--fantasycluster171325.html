fantasy

noun
imagination divorced from reality
Pronunciation: / ˈfæn.tə.si /; / ˈfæntəsi / (AmE), / ˈfæn.tə.si /; / ˈfæntəsi / (BrE)
the act or result of imagining things that are unlikely or impossible; an imagined event, world, or story

Examples

A wild fantasy
As a child, her fantasy worlds were full of dragons and magic.
He escapes the stresses of daily life through fantasy and storytelling.
The novel blends elements of fantasy with harsh realities.
As a child, she created a vivid fantasy world filled with magical creatures.
Winning the lottery is just a fantasy for most people.
He often escapes into a fantasy of being a famous musician.

Related Terms

  • synonym: daydream - a pleasant imagined thought or wish during waking hours
  • synonym: imagination - the ability to form mental images or concepts not present to the senses
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
