Okinawa Island

proper noun
largest island of the central Ryukyu Islands
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊ.kɪˈnɑː.wə ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / ˌəʊ.kɪˈnɑː.wə ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
the largest of the central Ryukyu Islands in Japan, known for its historical significance and subtropical climate
