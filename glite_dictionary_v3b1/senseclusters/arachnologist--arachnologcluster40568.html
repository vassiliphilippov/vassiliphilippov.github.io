arachnologist

noun
spider scientist
8.3
Pronunciation: / əˌræknəˈlɑːdʒɪst / (AmE), / əˌræknəˈlɒdʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies spiders and related animals such as scorpions and ticks

Examples

The arachnologist studies spiders
The arachnologist spent years researching the behavior of rare tarantulas in South America.
At the museum, an arachnologist explained how spiders help control insect populations.
After publishing her findings, the young arachnologist became well known in the scientific community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entomologist - a scientist who studies insects
  • synonym: zoologist - a scientist who studies animals
