erythemal

adjective
causing skin redness
Pronunciation: / ˌɛrɪˈθiːməl / (AmE), / ˌɛrɪˈθiːməl / (BrE)
relating to or causing erythema, which is redness of the skin

Examples

The erythemal effect
The erythemal effect of sunlight can be measured to assess skin damage risk.
Doctors monitor the erythemal response in patients after certain treatments.
High levels of ultraviolet radiation increase the erythemal potential of sunlight.
