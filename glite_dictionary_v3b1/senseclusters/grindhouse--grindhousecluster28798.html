grindhouse

noun
cinema for exploitation films
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪndˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪndˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a movie theater that shows low-budget, sensational, or exploitation films, often with graphic content

Examples

A grindhouse theater
The old grindhouse downtown was famous for its midnight horror movie screenings.
Fans of cult cinema often seek out rare grindhouse films from the 1970s.
He wrote a book about the history of grindhouse theaters in New York City.
