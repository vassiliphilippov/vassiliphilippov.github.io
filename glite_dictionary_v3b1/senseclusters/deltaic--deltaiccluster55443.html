deltaic

adjective
of a river delta
Pronunciation: / dɛlˈteɪɪk / (AmE), / dɛlˈteɪɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a river delta, especially in terms of landforms or sediments

Examples

The deltaic plain
The deltaic plain was rich in nutrients, making it ideal for farming.
Scientists studied the deltaic deposits to understand the river's history.
Many bird species nest in the deltaic wetlands each spring.
