whush

verb
to move with rushing sound
Pronunciation: / wʊʃ / (AmE), / wʊʃ / (BrE)
to move quickly with a soft rushing or whooshing sound

Examples

Leaves whush past
The leaves whushed across the pavement as the wind picked up.
A train whushed past the station, startling the waiting passengers.
She heard the water whush through the pipes after turning on the tap.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rush - to move with great speed
  • synonym: whoosh - to move quickly making a rushing sound
