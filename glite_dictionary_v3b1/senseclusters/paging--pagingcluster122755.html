paging

noun
system for numbering pages
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the arrangement or system of assigning numbers to the pages of a book, document, or publication

Examples

Book paging
Incorrect paging can cause confusion when referencing sections of a report.
She checked the paging before sending the manuscript to the publisher.
The paging in this journal follows a continuous sequence throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pagination - the process of dividing a document into discrete pages, especially with numbering
