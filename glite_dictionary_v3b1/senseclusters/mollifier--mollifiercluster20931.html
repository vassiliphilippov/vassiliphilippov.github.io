mollifier

noun
person who soothes
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːlɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈmɒlɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a person who calms, soothes, or pacifies others, especially in tense situations

Examples

A mollifier in arguments
During the heated debate, she acted as a mollifier to keep everyone calm.
The manager played the role of mollifier when tensions rose among the team members.
As a skilled mollifier, he was often called upon to resolve family disputes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agitator - a person who stirs up trouble or provokes others
  • synonym: appeaser - a person who tries to calm or placate others, often by making concessions
  • synonym: peacemaker - a person who helps others resolve conflict and restore peace
