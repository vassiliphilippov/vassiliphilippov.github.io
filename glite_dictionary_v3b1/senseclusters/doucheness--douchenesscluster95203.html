doucheness

noun
offensive or obnoxious behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈduːʃ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈduːʃ.nɪs / (BrE)
Offensive or obnoxious behavior or attitude, especially that which is irritating or contemptible.

People get tired of that kind of doucheness really quickly.
