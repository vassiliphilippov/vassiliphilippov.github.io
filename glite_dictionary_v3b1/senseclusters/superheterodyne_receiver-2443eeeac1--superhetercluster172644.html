superheterodyne receiver

noun
type of radio receiver using intermediate frequency
9.9 9.2
Pronunciation: /suː.pər.ˈhet.ə.rə.daɪn rɪ.ˈsiː.vər/ /; / /suː.pər.ˈhet.ə.rə.daɪn rə.ˈsiː.vər/ (AmE), /suː.pə.ˈhet.ə.rə.daɪn rɪ.ˈsiː.və/ /; / /suː.pə.ˈhet.ə.rə.daɪn rə.ˈsiː.və/ (BrE)
a radio receiver in which incoming signals are combined with a locally generated frequency to produce a fixed intermediate frequency for easier amplification and demodulation
