Jabat al-Tahrir al-Filistiniyyah

proper noun
Palestinian terrorist group (founded 1977)
Pronunciation: / dʒæˈbɑːt æl tɑːˈriːr æl fɪlɪstiːˈnijə / (AmE), / dʒəˈbɑːt æl tɑːˈriːə æl fɪlɪstiːˈnijə / (BrE)
A Palestinian terrorist group formed in 1977 after splitting from the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine, later becoming a satellite of al-Fatah and known for attacks on Israel across the Lebanese border.
