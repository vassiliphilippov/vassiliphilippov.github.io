White Tai

noun
Tai language variety spoken in northern Vietnam
Pronunciation: / waɪt taɪ / (AmE), / waɪt taɪ / (BrE)
a branch of the Tai languages spoken mainly in northern Vietnam, along the Red and Black rivers, particularly in Lai Chau, Dien Bien, Lao Cai, and Son La provinces, and some in Lam Dong Province in the south

White Tai language

The White Tai language is spoken by several ethnic groups in northern Vietnam.
