upright piano

noun
vertical-framed piano
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʌp.raɪt piˈænoʊ / (AmE), / ˈʌp.raɪt piˈæn.əʊ / (BrE)
a piano with a vertical frame and strings, designed to be more compact than a grand piano

Examples

An upright piano
She learned to play on an old upright piano in her family's living room.
The upright piano takes up less space than a grand piano, making it ideal for small apartments.
They donated a upright piano to the local community center for music lessons.

Related Terms

  • antonym: grand piano - a large piano with a horizontal frame and strings
