after-shave

noun
fragrant lotion used after shaving
Pronunciation: / ˈæf.tɚ ˌʃeɪv / (AmE), / ˈɑːf.tə ˌʃeɪv / (BrE)
A scented lotion applied to the face after shaving to soothe and refresh the skin.

Examples

A bottle of after-shave
He splashed some after-shave on his cheeks and admired himself in the mirror.
Some people find after-shave soothing, while others prefer not to use it at all.
The scent of his after-shave lingered long after he left the room.
