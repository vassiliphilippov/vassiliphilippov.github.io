nucellus

noun
central part of a plant ovule
5.2
Pronunciation: / nuːˈsɛləs / (AmE), / njuːˈsɛləs / (BrE)
the central part of a plant ovule, containing the embryo sac

Examples

The nucellus tissue

The nucellus surrounds and nourishes the developing embryo sac within the ovule.
