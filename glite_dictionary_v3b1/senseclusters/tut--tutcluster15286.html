tut

interjection
mild disapproval sound
Pronunciation: / tʌt / (AmE), / tʌt / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express mild disapproval, annoyance, or impatience

Examples

A quiet tut
When he saw the mess in the kitchen, he let out a quiet tut and shook his head.
"Tut, you really should have finished your homework before playing games," the teacher said.
She gave a little tut when her friend arrived late again.
