isotopically

adverb
involving isotopes
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsəˈtoʊpɪkli / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈtəʊpɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or involves isotopes.

Examples

To be isotopically labeled
The water samples were isotopically labeled to trace their movement through the environment.
Scientists isotopically analyzed the minerals to learn about the planet's history.
The reaction was monitored isotopically to detect any changes in the atomic composition.
