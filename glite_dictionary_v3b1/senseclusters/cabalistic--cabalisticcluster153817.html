cabalistic

adjective
having a secret or hidden meaning
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈbæl.ɪst.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌkæb.əˈlɪs.tɪk / (BrE)
having a secret or mysterious meaning, often relating to or resembling a code or system understood by only a few

Examples

A cabalistic meaning
His writings were so cabalistic that few could make sense of their true purpose.
Many believed the alley was marked with cabalistic symbols to ward off intruders.
The ancient manuscript was full of cabalistic diagrams that puzzled even the most experienced scholars.
