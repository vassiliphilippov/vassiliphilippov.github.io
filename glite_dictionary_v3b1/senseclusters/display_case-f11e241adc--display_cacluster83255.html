display case

noun
glass container for displaying items
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspleɪ keɪs / (AmE), / dɪˈspleɪ keɪs / (BrE)
a glass container or cabinet with transparent sides used to display and protect items such as merchandise, collectibles, or artifacts
