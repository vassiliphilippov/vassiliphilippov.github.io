malintent

noun
harmful intention
Pronunciation: / ˌmælɪnˈtɛnt / (AmE), / ˌmælɪnˈtɛnt / (BrE)
the intention to do harm or act with bad motives

Examples

Malintent to harm
The manager believed the employee acted without malintent during the incident.
Detectives searched for evidence of malintent in the suspect's actions.
She apologized, insisting there was no malintent behind her words.

Related Terms

  • antonym: goodwill - friendly or helpful intentions
  • synonym: malice - the desire to harm others
