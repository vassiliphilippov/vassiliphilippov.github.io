confiscation

noun
seizure of property by authority
5.4 1.12/m

the act of taking property or goods by authority, especially by a government, usually as a penalty

Examples

The confiscation of property
After the company's assets were frozen, confiscation of the property followed swiftly.
The government ordered the confiscation of all illegal firearms in the region.
Threats of confiscation made the smugglers reconsider their actions.

Synonyms

  • forfeiture
    8.0 0.47/m

  • seizure
    6.1 4.90/m
    seizure is taking control or possession often by force or legal order, while confiscation is taking property as a government penalty.
