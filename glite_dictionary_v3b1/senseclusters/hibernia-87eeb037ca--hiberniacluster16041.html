Hibernia

proper noun
historical name for Ireland
Pronunciation: / haɪˈbɜrniə / (AmE), / haɪˈbɜːniə / (BrE)
a historical and poetic name for the island of Ireland, comprising the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland

Examples

Hibernia island
In classical literature, Hibernia is often depicted as a distant, mysterious land.
Many ancient maps refer to Ireland as Hibernia.
The poet wrote nostalgically about Hibernia and its green hills.
