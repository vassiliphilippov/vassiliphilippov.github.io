Zea mays amylacea

noun
type of corn with soft, starchy kernels
9.9
Pronunciation: / ziː.ə ˈmeɪz ˌæm.ɪˈleɪ.si.ə / (AmE), / ziː.ə ˈmeɪz ˌæm.ɪˈleɪ.si.ə / (BrE)
A variety of corn with kernels composed almost entirely of soft starch, used mainly for flour.
