fanatical

adjective
extremely enthusiastic or devoted
7.2
Pronunciation: / fəˈnætɪkəl / (AmE), / fəˈnatɪkəl / (BrE)
Showing extreme and uncritical enthusiasm or devotion for a cause, idea, or activity.

Examples

Fanatical about sports
He is fanatical about environmental issues, dedicating all his free time to the cause.
Her fanatical support for the team made her travel to every away game.
The fanatical devotion of the followers alarmed the organizers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: moderate - keeping within reasonable limits; not extreme or excessive
  • synonym: zealous - showing great energy or enthusiasm in pursuit of a cause
