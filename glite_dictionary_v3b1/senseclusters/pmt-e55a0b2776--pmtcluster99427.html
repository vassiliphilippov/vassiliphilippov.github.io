PMT

noun
abbreviation for payment
Pronunciation: / piː ɛm tiː / (AmE), / piː ɛm tiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for payment, used on invoices, bank statements, and forms to indicate an amount paid or due

Examples

PMT due
The invoice listed a PMT of $200 due by the end of the month.
Please check your bank statement for the recent PMT to confirm the transaction.
The accountant marked the PMT as received on the financial report.

Related Terms

  • synonym: payment - the act of paying money for something
