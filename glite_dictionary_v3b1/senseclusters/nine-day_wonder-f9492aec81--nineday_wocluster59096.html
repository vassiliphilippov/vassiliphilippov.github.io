nine-day wonder

noun
something briefly popular, soon forgotten
8.0 6.7
Pronunciation: /naɪn deɪ ˈwʌn.dər/ (AmE), /naɪn deɪ ˈwʌn.də/ (BrE)
Something that is briefly popular or attracts great attention but is soon forgotten.

Examples

A nine-day wonder story
Her viral video became a nine-day wonder, but the fame quickly faded.
Many internet memes are just a nine-day wonder in today's fast-paced world.
The invention was a nine-day wonder and was soon forgotten by the public.
