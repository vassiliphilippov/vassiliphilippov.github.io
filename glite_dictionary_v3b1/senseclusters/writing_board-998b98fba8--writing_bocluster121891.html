writing board

noun
portable flat board for writing on lap
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
A lightweight, flat board used as a portable surface for writing, often placed across the lap.

Examples

A writing board on lap
A writing board is helpful when you don't have access to a desk.
She placed the paper on her writing board before starting to jot down notes.
The students used writing boards during the outdoor class to complete their worksheets.
