ivory plant

noun
stemless palm tree with ivory nuts
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪvəri plænt / (AmE), / ˈaɪvəri plɑːnt / (BrE)
A stemless palm tree native to Brazil and Peru, known for producing ivory nuts.

Examples

Ivory plant palm tree
Artisans often use nuts from the ivory plant as an eco-friendly alternative to elephant ivory.
Collectors prize the ivory plant for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
The ivory plant thrives in the tropical rainforests of South America.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ivory nut - the nut from the ivory plant, used as a substitute for ivory
