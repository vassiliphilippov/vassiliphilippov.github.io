mythographer

noun
myth scholar or writer
Pronunciation: / mɪˈθɑːɡrəfər / (AmE), / mɪˈθɒɡrəfə / (BrE)
a person who collects, studies, or writes about myths

Examples

The mythographer studies myths
The famous mythographer published a comprehensive book on Greek legends.
As a mythographer, she traveled widely to gather stories from different cultures.
Many ancient tales would have been lost without the work of a dedicated mythographer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: folklorist - a person who studies or collects folklore
  • synonym: mythologist - a person who studies myths and mythology
