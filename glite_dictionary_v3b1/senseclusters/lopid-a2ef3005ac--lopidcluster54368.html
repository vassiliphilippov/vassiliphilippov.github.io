Lopid

noun
trademarked medicine for lowering triglycerides
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊpɪd / (AmE), / ˈləʊpɪd / (BrE)
a trademarked medicine (gemfibrozil) used to reduce triglyceride levels in the blood

Examples

Lopid medicine
