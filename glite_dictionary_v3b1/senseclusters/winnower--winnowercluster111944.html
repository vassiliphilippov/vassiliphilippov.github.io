winnower

noun
selector or eliminator
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪnoʊər / (AmE), / ˈwɪnəʊə / (BrE)
a person or thing that separates what is valuable or suitable from what is not, especially by careful selection or elimination

Examples

A careful winnower
The committee acted as a winnower, narrowing down the list of applicants to the most qualified.
In the competition, the first round served as a winnower to filter out weaker participants.
As a winnower of ideas, she helped the team focus on the most promising solutions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eliminator - a person or thing that removes or excludes others
  • antonym: includer - a person who includes or accepts all
  • synonym: selector - a person or thing that chooses from a group
