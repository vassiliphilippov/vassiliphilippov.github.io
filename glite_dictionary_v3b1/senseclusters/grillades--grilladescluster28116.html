grillades

noun
meat for grilling
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪˈjɑːdz / (AmE), / ɡrɪˈjɑːdz / (BrE)
cuts or small pieces of meat, such as steaks or chops, cooked on a grill or griddle and served as a dish

Related Terms

  • synonym: roast - meat cooked by dry heat, usually in an oven or over a fire
  • synonym: steak - a thick slice of meat, usually beef, suitable for grilling or frying
