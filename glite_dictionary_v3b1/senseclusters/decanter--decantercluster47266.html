decanter

noun
glass bottle for serving drinks
8.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkæntər / (AmE), / dɪˈkɑːntə / (BrE)
A decorative glass bottle with a stopper, used for serving wine or other drinks.

Examples

A glass decanter
He polished the silver decanter before placing it on the dining room sideboard.
She poured the red wine from the decanter into crystal glasses.
The water on the table was served in a beautiful decanter.
