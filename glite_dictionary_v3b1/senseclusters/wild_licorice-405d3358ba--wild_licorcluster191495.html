wild licorice

noun
North American plant similar to licorice
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈlɪkərɪʃ /; / waɪld lɪˈkɔrɪʃ /; / waɪld lɪˈkɒrɪʃ / (AmE), / waɪld ˈlɪkərɪʃ /; / waɪld lɪˈkɒrɪʃ / (BrE)
a plant with sweet, licorice-like roots used for flavoring or medicinal purposes
