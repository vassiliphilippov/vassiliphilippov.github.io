skew

adjective
set at a slant or angle
Pronunciation: / skju / (AmE), / skju / (BrE)
Having an oblique, slanting, or not straight direction or position; set at an angle.

Examples

Skew shelves
Because the shelves were skew, the books kept sliding off.
His tie was slightly skew, giving him a rather disheveled appearance.
The old painting looked skew on the living room wall until we adjusted it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: straight - following a direct or uncurved line or direction
