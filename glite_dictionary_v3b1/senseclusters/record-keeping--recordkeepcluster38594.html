record-keeping

adjective
related to records
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkərd ˌkiːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛkɔːd ˌkiːpɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to the making, maintenance, or management of records, especially as part of an organized system.

Examples

Record-keeping system
The company improved its record-keeping system to ensure all files were up to date.
Accurate record-keeping practices are essential for financial audits.
The new software simplifies record-keeping tasks for small businesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrative - relating to the organization and running of something
  • synonym: documentary - relating to documents or records
