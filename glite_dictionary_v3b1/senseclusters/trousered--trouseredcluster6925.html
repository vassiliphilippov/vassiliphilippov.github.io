trousered

adjective
wearing trousers
Pronunciation: / ˈtraʊzərd / (AmE), / ˈtraʊzəd / (BrE)
Wearing trousers; dressed in trousers rather than other types of clothing.

Examples

Trousered man
He was the only trousered boy at the formal event.
She arrived at the meeting smartly trousered and ready for business.
The women in the group were all trousered, defying the village norms.
