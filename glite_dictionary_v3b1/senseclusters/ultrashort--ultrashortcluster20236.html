ultrashort

adjective
extremely short
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈʌltrəˌʃɔrt / (AmE), / ˈʌltrəˌʃɔːt / (BrE)
extremely short in length or height, especially used to describe clothing, hair, or objects

Examples

The designer released a line of ultrashort jackets perfect for spring evenings.
