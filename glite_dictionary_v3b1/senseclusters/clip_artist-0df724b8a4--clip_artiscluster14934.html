clip artist

noun
creator/editor of short video clips
4.9
Pronunciation: /klɪp ˈɑːr.tɪst/ /; / /klɪp ˈɑːr.təst/ (AmE), /klɪp ˈɑː.tɪst/ /; / /klɪp ˈɑː.təst/ (BrE)
an informal term for a person who creates, edits, or curates short-form video clips for social media or other online platforms.

Examples

Social media clip artist
The influencer hired a clip artist to craft viral reels for her channel.
Several clip artists offer freelance editing services to streamers and small brands on social platforms.
After gaining attention for short edits, he worked as a professional clip artist for sports channels.
