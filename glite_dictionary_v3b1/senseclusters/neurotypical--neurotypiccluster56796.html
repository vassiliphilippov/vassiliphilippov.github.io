neurotypical

noun
person with typical neurology
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈtɪpɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈtɪpɪkəl / (BrE)
a person whose neurological development and functioning are considered typical, not showing signs of neurodivergence such as autism or ADHD

Examples

A neurotypical person
The classroom included both neurotypicals and students with autism, encouraging understanding and inclusion.
Some social situations may feel easier for a neurotypical than for someone who is neurodivergent.
She explained that as a neurotypical, she wanted to learn more about different perspectives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neurodivergent - a person whose neurological development differs from what is considered typical
