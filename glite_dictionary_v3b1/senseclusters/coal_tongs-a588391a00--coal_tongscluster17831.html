coal tongs

noun
tongs for handling burning coals
6.5
Pronunciation: / koʊl tɑŋz / (AmE), / kəʊl tɒŋz / (BrE)
a pair of tongs used to handle burning coals, especially in a fireplace or stove

Examples

Coal tongs handle coals
He used the coal tongs to add more fuel to the fire.
Please use the coal tongs instead of your hands to adjust the burning logs.
The ornate coal tongs stood next to the old cast-iron fireplace.
