betweenness

noun
network centrality measure
8.9
Pronunciation: / bɪˈtwiːnnəs / (AmE), / bɪˈtwiːnnəs / (BrE)
a measure in network analysis showing how often a node lies on the shortest paths between other nodes, indicating its influence or control in the network

Examples

Betweenness centrality score
The betweenness of a node helps identify key connectors in a social network.
Nodes with high betweenness can control the flow of information between groups.
Researchers analyzed the betweenness scores to find the most influential people in the network.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centrality - the importance of a node within a network
  • synonym: closeness - a measure of how near a node is to all other nodes in a network
