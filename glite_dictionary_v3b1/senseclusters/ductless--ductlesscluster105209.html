ductless

adjective
having no duct (esp. of glands)
7.7 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆdÊŒktlÉ™s / (AmE), / ËˆdÊŒktlÉ™s / (BrE)
(of a gland or organ) having no duct to carry away its secretions, especially referring to glands that release hormones directly into the bloodstream.

Examples

Ductless glands
Endocrine glands are sometimes referred to as ductless glands.
The thyroid is a classic example of a ductless gland.
Unlike sweat glands, ductless glands release their products directly into the bloodstream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endocrine - relating to glands that release hormones directly into the blood; ductless
  • antonym: exocrine - relating to glands that release their secretions through ducts
