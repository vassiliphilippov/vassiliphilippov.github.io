hedge in one's options

verb
protect against loss by diversifying
8.1 5.9
Pronunciation: / hɛdʒ ɪn wʌnz ˈɑpʃənz / (AmE), / hɛdʒ ɪn wʌnz ˈɒpʃənz / (BrE)
to protect oneself against potential loss by pursuing multiple strategies or opportunities, especially by diversifying investments or choices

Examples

To hedge in one's options carefully

She always tries to hedge in her options in case her first choice doesn't work out.
