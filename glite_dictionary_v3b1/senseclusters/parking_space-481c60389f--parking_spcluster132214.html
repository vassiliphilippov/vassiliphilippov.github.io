parking space

noun
a designated area to park a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrkɪŋ ˌspeɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɪŋ ˌspeɪs / (BrE)
an area marked or designated for parking a single vehicle, usually within a parking lot, garage, or along a street
