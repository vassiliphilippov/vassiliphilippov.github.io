hybridization

noun
combining to create new
5.5
Pronunciation: /haɪ.brə.də.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /haɪ.brɪ.daɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /haɪ.brɪ.daɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /haɪ.brə.də.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of combining different elements or species to create something new or mixed

Examples

Hybridization of styles
The hybridization of traditional and modern art styles resulted in a fresh new movement.
Scientists study hybridization to develop plants with improved resistance to disease.
The hybridization of languages can be seen in many words borrowed from different cultures.
