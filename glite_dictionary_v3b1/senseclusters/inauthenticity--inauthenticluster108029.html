inauthenticity

noun
lack of genuineness
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnɔːˌθɛnˈtɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌɪnɔːˌθɛnˈtɪsəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being not genuine, real, or true

Examples

The inauthenticity of painting
The inauthenticity of the painting was revealed after a detailed examination.
Many critics pointed out the inauthenticity in the actor's emotional scenes.
She disliked the inauthenticity of mass-produced souvenirs sold to tourists.

Related Terms

  • antonym: authenticity - the quality of being genuine or real
  • synonym: falseness - the quality of being untrue or fake
