shelf

noun
flat support for holding items
1.6
Pronunciation: / ʃɛlf / (AmE), / ʃɛlf / (BrE)
a flat, horizontal board or surface fixed to a wall or within a frame, used for supporting objects

Examples

A book shelf
He built a wooden shelf for his growing collection of trophies.
She placed the books neatly on the top shelf above her desk.
The kitchen shelf was crowded with jars and spices.
