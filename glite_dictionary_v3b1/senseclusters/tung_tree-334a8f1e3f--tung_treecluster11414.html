tung tree

noun
Chinese tree producing tung oil
7.1 9.1
Pronunciation: / tʌŋ tri / (AmE), / tʌŋ triː / (BrE)
A tree native to China whose seeds produce tung oil, used in wood finishing and paints.

Examples

Tung oil, extracted from the seeds of the tung tree, is highly valued by woodworkers.
