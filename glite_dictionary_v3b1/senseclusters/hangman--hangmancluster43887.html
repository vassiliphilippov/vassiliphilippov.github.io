hangman

noun
word guessing game
2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhæŋ.mən/ (AmE), /ˈhæŋ.mən/ (BrE)
a word game in which players guess letters to reveal a hidden word, with each wrong guess bringing a stick-figure closer to being 'hanged'

Examples

We often play hangman in English class to practice spelling and vocabulary.
