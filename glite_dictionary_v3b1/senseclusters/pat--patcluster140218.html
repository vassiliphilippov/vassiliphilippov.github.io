pat

noun
a gentle touch or stroke
Pronunciation: / pæt / (AmE), / pæt / (BrE)
a quick, gentle touch or stroke with the hand or a flat object, usually to show affection, approval, or comfort

Examples

A gentle pat on the head
He gave the dog a gentle pat on the head before leaving.
She finished icing the cake, then gave it a final pat to smooth the sides.
With a reassuring pat on her shoulder, he signaled that everything would be alright.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tap - a light touch or blow, especially with a slight sound
