tomato hornworm

noun
a caterpillar pest of tomato and potato plants
4.8 6.8
Pronunciation: /tə.ˈmeɪ.toʊ ˈhɔːrn.wɜːrm/ /; / /tə.ˈmɑː.toʊ ˈhɔːrn.wɜːrm/ (AmE), /tə.ˈmɑː.təʊ ˈhɔːn.wɜːm/ /; / /tə.ˈmeɪ.təʊ ˈhɔːn.wɜːm/ (BrE)
