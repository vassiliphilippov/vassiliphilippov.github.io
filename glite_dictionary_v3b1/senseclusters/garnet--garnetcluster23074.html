garnet

noun
hard mineral used as a gemstone and abrasive
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrnət / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːnɪt / (BrE)
any of a group of hard, glassy minerals (silicates of various metals) typically red or reddish-brown, used as gemstones and abrasives
