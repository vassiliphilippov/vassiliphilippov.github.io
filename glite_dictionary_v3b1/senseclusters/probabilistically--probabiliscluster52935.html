probabilistically

adverb
using probability theory
9.4
Pronunciation: / proʊˌbæbəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / prəʊˌbæbɪˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or uses probability theory; using principles of probability

Examples

Modeled probabilistically
He modeled the system probabilistically to account for uncertainty in the measurements.
Particles in quantum mechanics behave probabilistically rather than deterministically.
The algorithm decides probabilistically whether to accept or reject the data point.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deterministically - in a way that is determined by initial conditions without chance
