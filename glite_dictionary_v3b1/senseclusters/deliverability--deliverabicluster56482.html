deliverability

noun
readiness for delivery
7.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˌlɪvərəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / dɪˌlɪvərəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the suitability or readiness of a fetus or birth situation to be delivered, especially by vaginal birth

Examples

Fetal deliverability
The doctor evaluated the deliverability of the fetus before deciding on the safest method of birth.
Concerns about deliverability led the medical team to recommend a cesarean section.
Ultrasound results can help determine the deliverability of the baby in complicated pregnancies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: viability - the ability of a fetus to survive outside the womb
