dissimulator

noun
person who conceals true feelings or beliefs
7.5 7.3
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈsɪm.jə.leɪ.tər/ /; / /dɪ.ˈsɪm.jʊ.leɪ.tər/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈsɪm.jʊ.leɪ.tə/ /; / /dɪ.ˈsɪm.jə.leɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person who disguises or conceals their true feelings, motives, or beliefs by professing opinions or attitudes they do not hold

Examples

A dissimulator hides feelings
In politics, a skilled dissimulator can often win support through calculated statements.
She accused him of being a dissimulator, always hiding his true intentions behind polite words.
The dissimulator managed to deceive everyone by pretending to agree with all sides.
