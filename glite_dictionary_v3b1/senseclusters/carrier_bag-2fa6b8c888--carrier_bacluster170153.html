carrier bag

noun
paper or plastic shopping bag
Pronunciation: / ˈkæriɚ bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈkæriə bæɡ / (BrE)
a bag, typically made of paper or plastic, used by shops to hold customers' purchases for carrying them home

Examples

A carrier bag
Could you please put the apples in a carrier bag?
She reused an old carrier bag to carry her library books.
The supermarket now charges five pence for each carrier bag.
