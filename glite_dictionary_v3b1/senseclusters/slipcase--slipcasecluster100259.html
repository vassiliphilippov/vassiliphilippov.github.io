slipcase

noun
protective media box
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪpˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈslɪpˌkeɪs / (BrE)
a protective box or sleeve, open on one side, used to hold and protect books or other media items

Examples

A book slipcase
The collector kept his rare books in a sturdy slipcase to protect them from dust.
Each volume of the series fits neatly into the decorative slipcase provided by the publisher.
She admired the artwork printed on the slipcase of the limited edition DVD set.
The collector's edition came in a sturdy slipcase.
She carefully slid the rare book back into its slipcase to keep it safe.
Many luxury books are sold with a decorative slipcase for added protection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: box set - a set of items, such as books or DVDs, packaged together in a box
  • synonym: dust jacket - a removable paper cover for a book
