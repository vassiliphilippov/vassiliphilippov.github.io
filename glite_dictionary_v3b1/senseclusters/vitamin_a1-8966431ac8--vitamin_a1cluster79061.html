vitamin A1

noun
an unsaturated alcohol (retinol); a form of vitamin A
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌvaɪtəmɪn ˈeɪ wʌn / (AmE), / ˌvɪtəmɪn ˈeɪ wʌn / (BrE)
an unsaturated alcohol, also known as retinol, that occurs in marine fish-liver oils and is biologically synthesized from carotene

Examples

Marine vitamin A1
Marine fish-liver oils are a rich source of vitamin A1, essential for healthy vision.
Scientists discovered that vitamin A1 is synthesized in the body from carotene.
The deficiency of vitamin A1 can lead to night blindness in humans.
