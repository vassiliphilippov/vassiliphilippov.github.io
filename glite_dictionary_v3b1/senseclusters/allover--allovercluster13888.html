allover

adjective
covering the entire surface
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɔlˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈɔːlˌəʊvə / (BrE)
(of a pattern, color, etc.) covering the entire surface of something; extending uniformly over an area.

Examples
