tactilely

adverb
by touch
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtæktaɪlli / (AmE), / ˈtæktaɪlli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or involves the sense of touch

Examples

To explore tactilely
The artist explored the sculpture tactilely to understand its shape and texture.
Some fabrics can only be distinguished tactilely because they look identical.
Blind students learn to read Braille tactilely using their fingertips.

Related Terms

  • synonym: physically - in a manner relating to the body or physical contact
  • antonym: visually - in a way that relates to seeing
