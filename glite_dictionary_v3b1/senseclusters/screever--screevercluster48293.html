screever

noun
public scribe
Pronunciation: / ˈskriːvər / (AmE), / ˈskriːvə / (BrE)
a person who writes letters or documents for others, often for a fee; a public scribe

Examples

Village screever writes
The illiterate villagers relied on the screever to write important documents for them.
She became a screever in the market, helping people send letters to their families.
A screever sat at a small table, offering to write for anyone who needed help.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scribe - a person who copies or writes documents by hand
  • synonym: secretary - a person employed to write letters and manage records
