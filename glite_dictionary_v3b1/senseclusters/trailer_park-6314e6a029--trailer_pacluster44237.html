trailer park

noun
area for parked trailers/mobile homes
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪlər pɑrk / (AmE), / ˈtreɪlə pɑːk / (BrE)
An area of land where spaces for house trailers or mobile homes are rented, usually with shared utilities and amenities.

Examples

A trailer park community
After the storm, several homes in the trailer park were damaged.
She grew up in a trailer park on the outskirts of the city.
The trailer park offers affordable housing options for many families.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mobile home park - area where mobile homes are stationed and rented
