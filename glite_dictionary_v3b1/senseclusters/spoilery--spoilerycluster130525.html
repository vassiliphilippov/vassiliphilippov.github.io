spoilery

adjective
revealing plot details
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔɪləri / (AmE), / ˈspɔɪləri / (BrE)
containing or revealing important plot details that could spoil a story or surprise for someone who has not experienced it yet

Examples

A spoilery article
I stopped reading the article because it was getting too spoilery about the ending.
Please avoid spoilery comments when discussing the new season online.
The trailer was surprisingly spoilery and showed all the major twists.
The review was so spoilery that it ruined the movie for me before I even saw it.
Please avoid spoilery comments when discussing the latest episode online.
He tends to make spoilery remarks that give away the ending of every book.

Related Terms

  • antonym: secretive - not revealing information or details
  • synonym: spoiler - information that reveals important plot details
