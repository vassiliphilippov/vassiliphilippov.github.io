supplement

noun
additional section in a publication
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌpləmənt / (AmE), / ˈsʌplɪmənt / (BrE)
an additional section or material added to a publication, often at the end, to provide extra information or updates

Examples

Sunday supplement
A new supplement was added to the textbook containing updated statistics.
Readers will find the glossary in the supplement at the end of the report.
The encyclopedia includes a supplement with recent scientific discoveries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addendum - an additional item at the end of a publication
  • synonym: appendix - supplementary material at the end of a book
