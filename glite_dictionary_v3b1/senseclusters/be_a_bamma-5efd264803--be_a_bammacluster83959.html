be a bamma

verb
act foolishly or ineptly
9.9
Pronunciation: / bi ə ˈbæmə / (AmE), / biː ə ˈbamə / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or inept manner, especially in a way that causes annoyance or inconvenience to others.

Examples

To be a bamma in class
He forgot his homework again—always trying to be a bamma in class.
If you be a bamma at work, you might get written up.
Please don’t be a bamma when we meet my parents tonight.
