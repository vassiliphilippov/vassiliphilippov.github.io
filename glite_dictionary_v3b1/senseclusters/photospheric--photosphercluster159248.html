photospheric

adjective
of a star's surface
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtəˈsfɪrɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəˈsfɪrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the visible surface layer of a star, especially the sun

Examples

Photospheric layer
Scientists study photospheric activity to understand solar flares and sunspots.
The photospheric temperature of the sun is about 5,500 degrees Celsius.
Changes in photospheric brightness can indicate magnetic disturbances on the sun.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coronal - relating to the outermost layer of a star's atmosphere
  • synonym: solar - relating to the sun
