bellhop

noun
hotel luggage carrier and errand runner
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛlˌhɑp / (AmE), / ˈbɛl.hɒp / (BrE)
a hotel employee whose job is to carry guests' luggage and run errands

Examples

The bellhop carries luggage
I gave the bellhop a tip for helping us with our suitcases.
The bellhop quickly brought our bags up to the room after we checked in.
Whenever guests arrived, the bellhop was always there to greet them and carry their luggage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: porter - a person employed to carry luggage, especially in hotels or transportation terminals
