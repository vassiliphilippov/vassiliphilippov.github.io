redshirt

noun
Garibaldi follower
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈred.ʃɜːrt/ (AmE), /ˈred.ʃɜːt/ (BrE)
a member of a volunteer military or political group identified by wearing a red shirt, especially a follower of Giuseppe Garibaldi in 19th-century Italy

Examples

The history book described how the redshirts became a symbol of Italian nationalism.
