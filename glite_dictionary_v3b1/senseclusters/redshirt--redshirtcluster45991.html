redshirt

noun
Garibaldi follower
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌʃɜrt / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌʃɜːt / (BrE)
a member of a volunteer military or political group identified by wearing a red shirt, especially a follower of Giuseppe Garibaldi in 19th-century Italy

Examples

Garibaldi redshirt fighter
The museum displayed a worn uniform that had belonged to a redshirt from the 1860 campaign.
Many redshirts fought bravely alongside Garibaldi during the unification of Italy.
The history book described how the redshirts became a symbol of Italian nationalism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Garibaldian - a supporter or follower of Giuseppe Garibaldi
