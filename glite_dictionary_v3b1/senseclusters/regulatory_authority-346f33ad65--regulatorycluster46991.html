regulatory authority

noun
government agency that regulates businesses
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛɡjəˈleɪtɔri əˈθɔrəti / (AmE), / ˌrɛɡjʊˈleɪtəri ɔːˈθɒrɪti / (BrE)
an official organization or agency with the power to control or supervise particular business activities in the public interest

Examples

The regulatory authority agency
A report was submitted to the regulatory authority concerning safety violations at the plant.
Every airline must comply with the rules set out by the relevant regulatory authority.
The new financial regulatory authority was established to oversee banking practices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deregulation - the removal of regulations or restrictions
  • synonym: regulator - an official who supervises a particular industry or activity
