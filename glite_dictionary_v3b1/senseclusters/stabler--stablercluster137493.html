stabler

noun
stable worker
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪblər / (AmE), / ˈsteɪblə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to take care of horses and other animals in a stable

Examples

A stabler feeds horses
The stabler arrived early each morning to feed and brush the horses.
After the competition, the stabler carefully checked each animal for injuries.
A skilled stabler is essential for keeping the stable clean and the animals healthy.
