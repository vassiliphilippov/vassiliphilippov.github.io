Lunaria annua

noun
plant with silver seedpods used in decoration
9.1
Pronunciation: /luː.ˈnɑːr.ɪ.ə ˈæn.juː.ə/ (AmE), /luː.ˈnɑː.rɪ.ə ˈæn.juː.ə/ (BrE)
