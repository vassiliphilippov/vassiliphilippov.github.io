Nintendo

noun
video game console
Pronunciation: / nɪnˈtɛndoʊ / (AmE), / nɪnˈtɛndəʊ / (BrE)
an informal name for a home video game console made by the company Nintendo

Examples

Play on the Nintendo
He spent the afternoon playing his favorite games on the Nintendo with his friends.
After saving up for months, she finally bought a new Nintendo for her birthday.
Many classic games were first released on the original Nintendo console.

Related Terms

  • synonym: PlayStation - a popular home video game console by Sony
  • synonym: Xbox - a home video game console by Microsoft
