praying mantid

noun
predatory insect with prayer-like posture
9.9 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpreɪ.ɪŋ ˈmæn.tɪd/ (AmE), /ˈpreɪ.ɪŋ ˈmæn.tɪd/ (BrE)
A large, slender predatory insect with forelegs bent and held together at an angle that suggests the position of prayer; also known as a praying mantis.

Examples

A praying mantid insect
A praying mantid sat motionless on the garden fence, waiting for its next meal.
I was fascinated as the praying mantid turned its head to follow the movement of my finger.
Many gardeners appreciate the praying mantid for its ability to control pests.
