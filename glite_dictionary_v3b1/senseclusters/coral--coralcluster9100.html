coral

adjective
pink or yellowish-pink color
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəl / (BrE)
Of a strong pink to yellowish-pink color, similar to that of red coral.

Examples

Coral dress
Her lips were painted a bright coral shade that matched her earrings.
She wore a beautiful dress in coral for the summer party.
The artist chose a coral background to make the painting feel warm and lively.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peach - a soft pinkish-yellow color, like the flesh of a peach
  • degree lower: pink - a pale red color
  • synonym: salmon - a pinkish-orange color, similar to the flesh of a salmon
  • degree higher: vermilion - a brilliant red or scarlet color
