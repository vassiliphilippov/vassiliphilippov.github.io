ashrama

noun
spiritual retreat
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːʃrəmə / (AmE), / ˈɑːʃrəmə / (BrE)
a spiritual hermitage or monastery, especially in Indian religions, where people live and practice meditation or religious study

Examples

A peaceful ashrama
After years in the city, she decided to spend time at an ashrama to find inner peace.
The ashrama welcomed visitors who wanted to learn about meditation and yoga.
He wrote a book about his experiences living in an ashrama in southern India.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hermitage - a place where a hermit lives in seclusion
  • synonym: monastery - a building where monks or nuns live and worship
