Ganesa

proper noun
Hindu god of wisdom and remover of obstacles
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈneɪsə / (AmE), / ɡəˈneɪsə / (BrE)
A Hindu god of wisdom and prophecy, known as the remover of obstacles, usually depicted with an elephant's head.

Examples

Ganesa statue
A statue of Ganesa stood at the entrance, symbolizing wisdom and protection.
During festivals, people decorate their homes with images of Ganesa to invite good fortune.
Many devotees pray to Ganesa before starting a new venture, seeking his blessings for success.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ganesha - alternate spelling of Ganesa, Hindu elephant-headed god
