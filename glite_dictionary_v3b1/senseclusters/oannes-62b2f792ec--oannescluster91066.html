Oannes

proper noun
Mesopotamian mythic teacher
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈæniz / (AmE), / əʊˈæniz / (BrE)
a figure in Mesopotamian mythology believed to have emerged from the sea to teach humans knowledge and civilization

Examples

Oannes in mythology
According to legend, Oannes taught early humans how to write and build cities.
Scholars debate the origins of the Oannes myth in ancient Mesopotamian culture.
The story of Oannes describes a being who emerged from the sea to share wisdom.
