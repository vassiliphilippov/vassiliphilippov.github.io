whoops

interjection
exclamation for mistake
7.4
Pronunciation: / wʊps / (AmE), / wʊps / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to show surprise or apology after a small mistake or accident

Examples

Whoops my mistake
I accidentally sent the wrong file—whoops!
Whoops, I forgot to lock the door before leaving the house.
He spilled his drink and said, 'whoops!' with a smile.
Whoops, I dropped my keys on the floor.
He said whoops when he realized he had sent the wrong email.
Whoops, I almost forgot your birthday present!

Related Terms

  • synonym: oops - an exclamation used after a minor error or accident
  • synonym: sorry - used to express apology or regret
  • synonym: uh-oh - an exclamation expressing concern or realization of a problem
