gallerist

noun
art gallery manager
Pronunciation: / ˈɡælərɪst / (AmE), / ˈɡælərɪst / (BrE)
a person who owns, manages, or operates an art gallery

Examples

The gallerist manages gallery
The gallerist introduced the artist at the exhibition opening.
After years as an art dealer, she became a successful gallerist in New York.
The gallerist carefully selected works for the upcoming modern art show.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artist - a person who creates art
  • synonym: curator - a person in charge of a museum or art collection
