toxicologically

adverb
relating to toxins
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑksɪkəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌtɒksɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the effects, study, or science of poisons or toxins

Examples

To toxicologically evaluate drugs
The new drug was toxicologically evaluated before being released to the public.
Researchers examined the toxicologically significant effects of the chemical on the environment.
All ingredients must be toxicologically assessed to ensure consumer safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemically - in a way that relates to chemicals or chemistry
  • synonym: medically - in a way that relates to medicine or medical treatment
