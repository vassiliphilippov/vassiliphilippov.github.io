Maliki

adjective
of Maliki school
Pronunciation: / ˈmælɪki / (AmE), / ˈmælɪki / (BrE)
relating to the Maliki school, one of the main Sunni Islamic legal traditions

Examples

Maliki law school
The scholar gave a lecture on Maliki principles in Islamic law.
Many North African countries follow the Maliki tradition in their legal systems.
He is studying Maliki jurisprudence at the university.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Hanafi - relating to the Hanafi school, another major Sunni Islamic legal tradition
  • antonym: Hanbali - relating to the Hanbali school, another major Sunni Islamic legal tradition
  • antonym: Shafi'i - relating to the Shafi'i school, another major Sunni Islamic legal tradition
