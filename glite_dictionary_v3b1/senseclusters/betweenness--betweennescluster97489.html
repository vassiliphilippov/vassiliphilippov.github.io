betweenness

noun
intermediate position
Pronunciation: / bɪˈtwiːnnəs / (AmE), / bɪˈtwiːnnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being in an intermediate or middle position between two or more things

Examples

Point betweenness
In geometry, the concept of betweenness helps define the order of points on a line.
The betweenness of the mediator allowed both sides to reach an agreement.
Social network analysis often measures the betweenness of individuals to determine their influence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centrality - the quality of being central or in the center
  • synonym: intermediacy - the state of being in the middle or between things
  • antonym: separation - the state of being apart or divided
