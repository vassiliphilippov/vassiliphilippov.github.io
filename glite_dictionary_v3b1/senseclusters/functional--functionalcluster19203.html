functional

noun
mathematical mapping
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋkʃənəl / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋkʃənəl / (BrE)
a mathematical object that assigns a single number to a function, often used in advanced mathematics and physics

Examples

A functional in math
In calculus of variations, a functional maps a function to a real number.
The energy of a physical system can often be described by a specific functional.
Mathematicians study the properties of a functional to solve optimization problems.

Related Terms
