volunteer

noun
person who freely joins the military
2.1 1.9
Pronunciation: /vɑː.lən.ˈtɪr/ /; / /və.lən.ˈtɪr/ (AmE), /vɒ.lən.ˈtɪə/ /; / /və.lən.ˈtɪə/ (BrE)
a person who offers to do something freely, especially without being paid or forced

Examples

A volunteer helper
As a volunteer, he was motivated by a sense of duty rather than obligation.
Many men served as volunteers during the war, choosing to fight for their country.
The army is seeking volunteers for a new peacekeeping mission.
As a volunteer, she read stories to children at the local library.
Every Saturday, the hospital relies on at least one volunteer to help with patient care.
The charity is seeking more volunteers for its upcoming fundraising event.
