Sonoran

adjective
of Sonora region
Pronunciation: / səˈnɔːrən / (AmE), / səˈnɔːrən / (BrE)
relating to the Sonora region of northwestern Mexico or its desert

Examples

Sonoran desert
The Sonoran landscape is famous for its towering saguaro cacti.
Many unique animal species inhabit the Sonoran Desert.
She studied the traditional foods of Sonoran cuisine for her research.
