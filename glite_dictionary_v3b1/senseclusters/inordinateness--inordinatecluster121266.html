inordinateness

noun
the state of being excessive or immoderate
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɔrdnənəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈɔːdɪnətnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being excessive, immoderate, or beyond reasonable limits.

Examples

The inordinateness of spending
His inordinateness in spending quickly depleted his savings.
She was criticized for the inordinateness of her demands during the negotiation.
The novel explores the inordinateness of human desire and ambition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excessiveness - the quality of exceeding a normal or permitted limit
  • antonym: moderation - the quality of being moderate and avoiding excess
