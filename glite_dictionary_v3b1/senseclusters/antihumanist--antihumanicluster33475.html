antihumanist

adjective
opposed to humanism
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈhjuːmənɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈhjuːmənɪst / (BrE)
opposed to humanism or the belief that human values and interests should be central.

Examples

Antihumanist philosophy
The philosopher's antihumanist views challenged the idea that people are the center of meaning.
Some critics described the film as antihumanist because it downplayed the value of individual lives.
Her essay took an antihumanist stance, questioning the importance of human experience in art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anti-individualist - opposed to the importance of the individual
  • antonym: humanist - supporting human values and interests as central
