beefburger

noun
sandwich with a minced beef patty
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /biːf ˈbɜːr.gər/ (AmE), /biːf ˈbɜː.gə/ (BrE)
A sandwich made with a fried or grilled minced beef patty served in a split bun, typically with additional ingredients such as lettuce, tomato, or cheese.

Examples

A beefburger sandwich
He ordered a beefburger and fries at the café.
I prefer my beefburger with extra cheese and pickles.
The menu offers both a chicken burger and a beefburger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hamburger - a sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat, usually beef, in a split bun
