disengaged

adjective
not connected or locked
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒd / (BrE)
not physically connected, attached, or locked in place; released from engagement, especially of machine parts or mechanisms

Examples

Disengaged gear
Before starting the repair, make sure the clutch is disengaged to avoid accidents.
The safety mechanism keeps the gears disengaged when the machine is not in use.
He checked that all parts were disengaged before removing the cover.

Related Terms

  • antonym: engaged - connected or locked in place, especially of machine parts
  • synonym: unlocked - not fastened or secured by a lock
