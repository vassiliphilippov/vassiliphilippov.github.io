fibrinous

adjective
containing or resembling fibrin
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪ.brɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfɪb.rɪ.nəs / (BrE)
containing, consisting of, or resembling fibrin; especially relating to tissue or exudate marked by the presence of fibrin.

Examples

A fibrinous coating
A fibrinous coating developed on the surface of the inflamed membrane.
Pathologists noted the presence of thick, fibrinous deposits in the tissue sample.
The patient's lung showed a fibrinous exudate under microscopic examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fibrous - resembling or containing fibers; made of or characterized by fibers
