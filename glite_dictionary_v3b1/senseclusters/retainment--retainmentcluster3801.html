retainment

noun
advance service fee
7.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈteɪnmənt / (AmE), / rɪˈteɪnmənt / (BrE)
a fee paid in advance to secure or keep the services of a professional or adviser for a period of time

Examples

A lawyer's retainment fee
The lawyer requested a retainment before starting any work on the case.
They paid a monthly retainment to keep the consultant available for advice.
Without a retainment, the adviser was not obligated to provide ongoing support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: retainer - a fee paid in advance to secure professional services
