sour

adjective
unpleasantly turned or spoiled (taste)
1.9 1.0
Pronunciation: /saʊər/ /; / /saʊr/ (AmE), /saʊə/ (BrE)
(of food or drink) having turned bad and having an unpleasant smell or taste, especially due to fermentation or spoilage.

Examples

Sour milk smell
After a few days in the heat, the juice turned sour.
The cream had gone sour and was no longer safe to eat.
You should throw out any sour milk from the fridge immediately.
