four times

adverb
by a factor of four
Pronunciation: / fɔr taɪmz / (AmE), / fɔː taɪmz / (BrE)
By a factor of four; multiplied by four.

Examples

Four times higher
House prices in the city are now four times what they were a decade ago.
The company's profits increased four times after launching the new product line.
Water usage dropped to just four times per week during the drought.
