Simarouba

noun
tropical American tree genus
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪməˈruːbə / (AmE), / ˌsɪməˈruːbə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical American trees and shrubs with pale, soft wood and bitter bark, forming the type genus of the Simaroubaceae family.
