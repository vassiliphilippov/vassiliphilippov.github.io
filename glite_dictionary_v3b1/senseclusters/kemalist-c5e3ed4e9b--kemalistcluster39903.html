Kemalist

adjective
of Atatürk's ideology
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈke.mə.lɪst/ /; / /kə.ˈmæl.ɪst/ (AmE), /ˈke.mə.lɪst/ /; / /kə.ˈmæl.ɪst/ (BrE)
relating to the principles, policies, or ideology of Mustafa Kemal Atatürk, especially secularism, nationalism, and modernization in Turkey

Examples

Kemalist reforms
The new constitution reflected a strong Kemalist influence, emphasizing secularism and national unity.
Many reforms in the early Turkish Republic were inspired by Kemalist principles.
The party's Kemalist stance shaped its approach to education and government policy.
