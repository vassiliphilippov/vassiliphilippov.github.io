poacher turned gamekeeper

noun
person who switches from rule-breaker to enforcer
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊtʃər tɝnd ˈɡeɪmˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈpəʊtʃə tɜːnd ˈɡeɪmˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person who shifts from breaking the rules or acting illegally to upholding or enforcing them, especially someone who moves from illegal hunting to wildlife conservation.

Examples

A poacher turned gamekeeper
After years of illegal fishing, he became a poacher turned gamekeeper and now protects local rivers.
Her journey from activist hacker to cybersecurity consultant is a classic poacher turned gamekeeper story.
The politician, once accused of corruption, is seen as a poacher turned gamekeeper after his anti-fraud campaign.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gamekeeper - a person employed to take care of game and prevent poaching on an estate
