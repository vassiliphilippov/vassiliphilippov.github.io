recenter

verb
to refocus attention
5.1
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈsen.tər/ /; / /rə.ˈsen.tər/ (AmE), /riː.ˈsen.tə/ /; / /rə.ˈsen.tə/ (BrE)
to change the focus or emphasis of something back to its main subject, goal, or priority

Examples

To recenter the discussion
After a long break, the team needed to recenter their efforts on the main project.
She paused to recenter her thoughts before answering the difficult question.
The teacher helped the class recenter on the lesson after a noisy interruption.

Related Terms

  • synonym: refocus - to direct attention or effort back to something important
