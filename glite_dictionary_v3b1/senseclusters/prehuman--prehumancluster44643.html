prehuman

noun
early human ancestor
Pronunciation: / priˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / priˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
an early ancestor of modern humans that lived before Homo sapiens

Examples

A prehuman ancestor
Scientists found the remains of a prehuman in the African desert.
The documentary explores how prehuman species adapted to changing climates.
Some tools discovered at the site were likely made by a prehuman ancestor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hominid - a member of the biological family that includes humans and their ancestors
  • antonym: human - a member of the species Homo sapiens
