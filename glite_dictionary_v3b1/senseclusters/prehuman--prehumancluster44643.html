prehuman

noun
early human ancestor
7.7 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / priÀàhjuÀêm…ôn / (AmE), / priÀàhjuÀêm…ôn / (BrE)
an early ancestor of modern humans that lived before Homo sapiens

Examples

A prehuman ancestor
Scientists found the remains of a prehuman in the African desert.
The documentary explores how prehuman species adapted to changing climates.
Some tools discovered at the site were likely made by a prehuman ancestor.

Synonyms

  • protohuman
    9.3 0.01/m
    protohuman is an early ancestor shown in scientific texts as a step toward modern people, while prehuman means any ancestor before Homo sapiens.
  • ape-man
    9.1 0.01/m
    ape-man is a popular or dramatic term for a primitive, ape-like ancestor, while prehuman is a neutral scientific name for ancestors before Homo sapiens.
