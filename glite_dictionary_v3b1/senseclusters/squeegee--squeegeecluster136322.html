squeegee

noun
tool for removing water from surfaces
5.1 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈskwiː.dʒiː/ /; / /skwiː.ˈdʒiː/ (AmE), /ˈskwiː.dʒiː/ (BrE)
A T-shaped tool with a rubber blade, used for removing liquid, especially water, from a surface such as windows.

Examples

A squeegee for windows
After his shower, he ran a squeegee over the glass door to prevent streaks.
She used a squeegee to remove the soapy water from the window.
The car wash attendant wiped the windshield with a squeegee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wiper - a tool for cleaning or wiping, such as a windshield wiper
