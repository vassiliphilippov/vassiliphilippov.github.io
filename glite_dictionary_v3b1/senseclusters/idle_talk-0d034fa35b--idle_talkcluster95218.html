idle talk

noun
pointless or trivial conversation
2.5 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈaɪ.dəl tɔːk/ (AmE), /ˈaɪ.dəl tɔːk/ (BrE)
conversation that is pointless, trivial, or lacking in purpose; gossip or chatter with little value

Examples

Idle talk and gossip
He prefers action to idle talk and wants to get things done.
The meeting deteriorated into idle talk once the main agenda was over.
They spent the afternoon in idle talk instead of working on their project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chatter - rapid, trivial, or meaningless talk
  • synonym: gossip - casual or unconstrained conversation about others, often involving details that are not confirmed as true
