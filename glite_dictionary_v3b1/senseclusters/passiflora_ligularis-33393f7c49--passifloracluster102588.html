Passiflora ligularis

noun
a type of passionflower with edible fruit
Pronunciation: / ˌpæsəˈflɔrə lɪˌɡjuːˈlærɪs / (AmE), / ˌpæsɪˈflɔːrə lɪˌɡjuːˈlærɪs / (BrE)
a species of passionflower native to South America, cultivated for its sweet edible fruit, also known as sweet granadilla
