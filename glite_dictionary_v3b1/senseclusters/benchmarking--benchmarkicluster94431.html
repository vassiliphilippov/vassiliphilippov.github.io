benchmarking

noun
performance testing process
4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbentʃ.mɑːr.kɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbentʃ.mɑː.kɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of running standard tests to measure and compare the performance of computers, software, or other systems

Examples

Benchmarking computer speed
The IT department spent the week benchmarking several new laptops to find the fastest model.
Accurate benchmarking helps companies choose the best software for their needs.
Before launching the app, the developers performed benchmarking to ensure it ran smoothly on all devices.
