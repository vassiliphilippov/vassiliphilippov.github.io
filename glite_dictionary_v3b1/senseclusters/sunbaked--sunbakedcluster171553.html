sunbaked

adjective
hardened or dried by the sun
7.8 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌn.beɪkt/ (AmE), /ˈsʌn.beɪkt/ (BrE)
baked, dried out, or hardened by strong sunlight

Examples

Sunbaked dry fields
After months without rain, the fields were hard and sunbaked.

We sat on the sunbaked terrace enjoying the warmth and a cool drink.
The sunbaked streets shimmered in the midday heat.
The old farmhouse stood alone in the sunbaked countryside.
We picnicked on the sunbaked grass, enjoying the warm summer afternoon.
