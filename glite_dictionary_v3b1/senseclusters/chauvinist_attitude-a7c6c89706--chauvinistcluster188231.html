chauvinist attitude

noun
belief in group or male superiority
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊvənɪst əˈtɪtjud / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊvənɪst əˈtɪtjuːd / (BrE)
a strong or aggressive belief in the superiority of one's group, especially relating to male dominance or extreme patriotism

Examples

Chauvinist attitude toward women
His chauvinist attitude often led him to dismiss the suggestions of his female colleagues.
Many criticized the politician's chauvinist attitude during the heated debate on national identity.
She refused to tolerate his chauvinist attitude any longer and called him out in front of everyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: egalitarianism - belief in equal rights and opportunities for all
  • synonym: patriotism - devotion to and vigorous support for one's country
  • synonym: sexism - prejudice or discrimination based on a person's sex or gender
