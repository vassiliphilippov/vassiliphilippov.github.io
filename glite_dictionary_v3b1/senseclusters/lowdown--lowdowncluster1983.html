lowdown

noun
dishonest act
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a mean, dishonest, or unfair action or trick, especially one intended to harm or deceive someone

Examples

A lowdown on a friend
It was a real lowdown to spread those lies about his friend.
Everyone was shocked by the lowdown he used to win the competition.
She could not believe he would resort to such a lowdown just to get ahead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: betrayal - an act of being disloyal or unfaithful
  • antonym: favor - an act of kindness or help
  • synonym: trick - a cunning or deceitful act
