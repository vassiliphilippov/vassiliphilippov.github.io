narrowcast

noun
targeted broadcast
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈnæ.roʊ.kæst/ (AmE), /ˈnæ.rəʊ.kɑːst/ (BrE)
a broadcast, program, or transmission intended for a specific, limited audience rather than the general public

Examples

A narrowcast to fans
The marketing team created a narrowcast to reach only their most loyal customers.
A narrowcast can be more effective than a general broadcast for specialized topics.
The channel's narrowcast focused on science fiction fans in the local area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: podcast - a digital audio program available to a specific audience
