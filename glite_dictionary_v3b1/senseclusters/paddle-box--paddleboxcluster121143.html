paddle-box

noun
cover for the upper part of a paddlewheel
Pronunciation: / ˈpædəl bɑːks / (AmE), / ˈpædəl bɒks / (BrE)
a wooden structure that covers the upper part of a paddlewheel on a paddle steamer or similar vessel, protecting it from the elements and debris

Examples

The paddle-box cover
After the accident, the damaged paddle-box had to be replaced before the ship could sail again.
The old steamer's paddle-box was intricately carved and painted.
The riverboat's twin paddle-boxes gave it a distinctive silhouette against the sunset.
