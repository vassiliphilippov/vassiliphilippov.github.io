keep back

verb
withhold or refuse to share
5.6
Pronunciation: / kip ˈbæk / (AmE), / kiːp ˈbæk / (BrE)
to withhold or refuse to give or share something; to prevent something from being made known or distributed

Examples

To keep back information
She tried to keep back her emotions during the farewell ceremony.
The company decided to keep back important details about the project until the official announcement.
The teacher kept back some test results until all the students had finished.

Related Terms
