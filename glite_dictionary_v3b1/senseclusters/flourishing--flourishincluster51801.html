flourishing

adjective
thriving; prospering successfully
Pronunciation: / ˈflɝːɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflʌrɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
Developing rapidly and successfully; thriving and prosperous.

Examples

A flourishing business
She runs a flourishing business selling handmade furniture online.
The flourishing tech sector has attracted investors from around the world.
Tourism was once a flourishing industry in the coastal town.

Related Terms

  • antonym: failing - not successful; struggling or going out of business
  • synonym: prosperous - successful, usually by earning a lot of money
  • synonym: thriving - growing, developing, or being successful
