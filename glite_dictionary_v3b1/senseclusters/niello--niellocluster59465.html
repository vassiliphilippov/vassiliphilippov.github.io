niello

verb
to inlay with niello
9.9
Pronunciation: / niˈɛloʊ / (AmE), / niˈɛləʊ / (BrE)
to decorate metal by engraving a design and filling the engraved lines with niello, a black metallic inlay

Examples

To niello a pendant
The jeweler nielloed the silver ring, creating a striking contrast with the dark inlay.
Ancient craftsmen often nielloed swords and armor to add intricate patterns.
She admired the beautifully nielloed box, its engraved lines filled with black enamel.
The artisan skillfully nielloed the silver pendant with intricate floral patterns.
They decided to niello the bracelet to highlight the engraved details.
After he nielloed the sword hilt, the dark lines made the design stand out beautifully.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engrave - to cut or carve a design on a hard surface
  • synonym: inlay - to decorate an object by embedding pieces of a different material
