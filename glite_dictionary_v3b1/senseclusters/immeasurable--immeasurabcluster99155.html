immeasurable

adjective
impossible to measure or quantify
2.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɛʒ.ər.ə.bəl /; / ɪˈmɛʒərəbəl / (AmE), / ɪˈmɛʒ.ər.ə.bəl /; / ɪˈmɛʒ(ə)rəb(ə)l / (BrE)
too large, great, or extreme to be measured or calculated; impossible to measure

Examples

The impact of the new policy on the community was immeasurable and far-reaching.
The love parents have for their children is often described as immeasurable.
