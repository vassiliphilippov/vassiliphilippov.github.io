illuminist

noun
member of Illuminati
Pronunciation: / ɪˈluːmənɪst / (AmE), / ɪˈluːmɪnɪst / (BrE)
a member or supporter of the Bavarian Illuminati, an 18th-century secret society promoting enlightenment ideals

Examples

Bavarian illuminist member
The historian discovered letters written by an illuminist in the late 1700s.
Some conspiracy theories claim that an illuminist influence still exists today.
He studied the beliefs and rituals of every known illuminist group from the Enlightenment era.
