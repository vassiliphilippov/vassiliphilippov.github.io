overemphatic

adjective
excessively forceful
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɛmˈfætɪk / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɛmˈfætɪk / (BrE)
expressed with too much emphasis or force, often making it seem exaggerated or insincere

Examples

An overemphatic praise
Her overemphatic praise made everyone feel uncomfortable during the meeting.
The actor's overemphatic gestures distracted from the seriousness of the scene.
He gave an overemphatic response that sounded more defensive than convincing.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: emphatic - expressed with emphasis or force
  • synonym: exaggerated - made to seem greater or more intense than it really is
  • antonym: understated - expressed in a restrained or subtle way
