ad-lib

noun
spontaneous remark; improvised comment
Pronunciation: / ˈæd lɪb / (AmE), / ˈæd lɪb / (BrE)
a remark or speech made spontaneously without prior preparation

Examples

A funny ad-lib
He often throws in an ad-lib during public speeches to connect with listeners.
Her quick ad-lib turned an awkward moment into a funny one.
The comedian's ad-libs kept the audience laughing throughout the show.

Related Terms
