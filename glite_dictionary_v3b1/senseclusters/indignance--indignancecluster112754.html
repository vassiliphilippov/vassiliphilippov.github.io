indignance

noun
anger at injustice
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɪɡnəns / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪɡnəns / (BrE)
a feeling of anger or annoyance caused by something perceived as unfair or unjust

Examples

Feel indignance at unfairness
Her indignance was clear when she spoke out against the unfair policy.
The students showed their indignance by organizing a peaceful protest.
He could not hide his indignance after being blamed for something he did not do.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indignation - anger or annoyance at unfair treatment
  • synonym: resentment - bitter indignation at having been treated unfairly
