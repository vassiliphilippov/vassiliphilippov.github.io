Paprilus alepidotus

noun
a species of Atlantic butterfish
Pronunciation: / pəˈprɪləs æˌlɛpɪˈdoʊtəs / (AmE), / pəˈprɪləs æˌlɛpɪˈdəʊtəs / (BrE)
A species of butterfish found in Atlantic waters, ranging from Chesapeake Bay to Argentina, growing up to about a foot long.
