Relating to or resembling a chimera; fanciful, imaginary, or wildly improbable. Also, composed of parts of different origin or nature, especially in biology or genetics.

Examples

Chimeric features
In myths, monsters often have chimeric features, combining parts from various animals.
The scientist engineered a chimeric protein using genes from two different species.
Their plan for world peace was dismissed as a chimeric dream by the critics.
