compilation

noun
collection of gathered material
3.5 2.79/m
Pronunciation: / Àåk…ëÀêm.p…ôÀàle…™. É…ôn / (AmE), / Àåk…ím.p…™Ààle…™. É…ôn / (BrE)
a collection of items, such as stories, songs, or data, gathered and organized into a single book, album, or file

Examples

A compilation album
She released a compilation of her greatest hits to celebrate her career anniversary.
The encyclopedia is a compilation of articles written by experts from various fields.
This report is a compilation of data collected over the last five years.

Synonyms

  • collection
    1.4 87/m
    a collection is a group of related things gathered together, while a compilation is an arranged set put into one book, album, or file.
  • compendium
    8.8 1.38/m
    a compendium is a book that gathers and often summarizes facts on a subject, while a compilation is a general collected set of items.
  • digest
    4.7 3.32/m
    a digest is a short publication that summarizes material from many sources, while a compilation gathers original items together in one book, album, or file.
