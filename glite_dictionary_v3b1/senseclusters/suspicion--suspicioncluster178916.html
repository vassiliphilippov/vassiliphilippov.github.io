suspicion

noun
quality of being suspicious
5.5
Pronunciation: / səˈspɪʃən / (AmE), / səˈspɪʃən / (BrE)

Examples

Mutual suspicion
A marriage can be ruined by mutual suspicion and lack of trust.
Her constant suspicion made it hard for her to form close relationships.
The manager's suspicion caused him to double-check every employee's work.
