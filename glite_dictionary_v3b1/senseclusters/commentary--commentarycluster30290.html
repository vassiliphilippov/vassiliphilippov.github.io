commentary

noun
explanatory discussion
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmənˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈkɒməntri / (BrE)
a spoken or written explanation or discussion that gives opinions or information about an event or subject

Examples

Live commentary
The political commentary on the news channel sparked a lively debate among viewers.
During the football match, the commentary helped me understand the strategies used by each team.
Her insightful commentary on the novel made the class discussion much more interesting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analysis - a detailed examination or study of something
  • synonym: report - an account or statement describing an event or situation
  • antonym: silence - the absence of speech or sound
