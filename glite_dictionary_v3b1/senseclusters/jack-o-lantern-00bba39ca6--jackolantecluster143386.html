jack-o'-lantern

noun
a poisonous orange-capped luminescent mushroom
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒæk oʊ ˌlæn tɚn / (AmE), / ˈdʒæk ə ˌlæn tən / (BrE)
A large poisonous mushroom with orange caps, clustered slender stalks, and luminescent gills, found in woods and fields.

Examples

Glowing jack-o'-lantern mushroom
Deep in the woods, we found a jack-o'-lantern glowing faintly at night.
Foragers should avoid the jack-o'-lantern mushroom, as it is highly poisonous.
The bright orange caps of the jack-o'-lantern make it easy to spot among the leaf litter.
