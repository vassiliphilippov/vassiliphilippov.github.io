Nyctimene

noun
genus of East Indian fruit bats
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɪkˈtɪmɪni / (AmE), / nɪkˈtɪməni / (BrE)
A genus of East Indian fruit bats known for their tubular nostrils, belonging to the family Pteropodidae.

Examples

Nyctimene genus
Scientists discovered a new species within the Nyctimene genus in Papua New Guinea.
The Nyctimene are easily recognized by their distinctive tubular nostrils.
Unlike other fruit bats, Nyctimene use their unique noses to help with their sense of smell.
