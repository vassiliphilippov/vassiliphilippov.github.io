illiberality

noun
unwillingness to be generous; lack of liberality
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɪbəˈræləti / (AmE), / ɪˌlɪbəˈrælɪti / (BrE)
an unwillingness to be generous with money; lack of generosity or liberality

Examples

His illiberality with money
Despite his wealth, his illiberality made him unpopular in charitable circles.
Her illiberality was well-known, as she rarely contributed to group expenses.
The company's illiberality towards its employees caused dissatisfaction among the staff.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generosity - the quality of being generous; willingness to give
  • synonym: stinginess - an unwillingness to spend money; excessive frugality
