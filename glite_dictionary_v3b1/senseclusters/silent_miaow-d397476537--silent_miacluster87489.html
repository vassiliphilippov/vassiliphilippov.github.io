silent miaow

noun
barely audible cat sound
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪlənt miˈaʊ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪlənt mɪˈaʊ / (BrE)
a very soft or barely audible sound made by a cat, often with the mouth opening but little or no sound heard

Examples

A kitten's silent miaow
The kitten greeted me with a gentle silent miaow when I entered the room.
She watched her cat give a silent miaow, opening its mouth but making almost no sound.
Sometimes, older cats communicate with a silent miaow instead of a loud meow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hiss - a sharp sibilant sound made by a cat, often when threatened
  • synonym: meow - the typical vocal sound made by a cat
