bogeyed

adjective
strange or scary-eyed
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊɡid / (AmE), / ˈbəʊɡid / (BrE)
having eyes that look strange, frightening, or unnaturally large, often in a way that resembles a monster or ghost

Examples

Bogeyed eyes
The old painting showed a bogeyed creature lurking in the shadows.
Children were frightened by the bogeyed mask at the haunted house.
In the story, the witch had bogeyed eyes that glowed in the moonlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goggle-eyed - having eyes that are wide open or protruding
  • antonym: normal-eyed - having eyes of normal appearance
