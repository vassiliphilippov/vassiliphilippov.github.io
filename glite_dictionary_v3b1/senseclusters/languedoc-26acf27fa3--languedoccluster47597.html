Languedoc

proper noun
Occitan dialect
Pronunciation: / ˈlæŋɡəˌdɒk / (AmE), / ˈlɒ̃ɡdɔk / (BrE)
the variety of the Occitan language traditionally spoken in the Languedoc region of southern France

Examples

Languedoc language
Linguists have preserved many old songs written in Languedoc from the Middle Ages.
The poet chose to write in Languedoc to celebrate his regional heritage.
Some villages in southern France still teach children the basics of Languedoc.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Occitan - a Romance language spoken in southern France and nearby regions
  • synonym: Provençal - a dialect of Occitan spoken in Provence
