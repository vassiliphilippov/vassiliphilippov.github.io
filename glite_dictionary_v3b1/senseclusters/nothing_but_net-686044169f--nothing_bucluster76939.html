nothing but net

noun
perfect basketball shot (no rim or backboard)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌθɪŋ bət ˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ bət ˈnɛt / (BrE)
(in basketball) a perfect shot where the ball goes through the net without touching the rim or backboard.

Examples

Nothing but net shot
Every shot she took in practice was nothing but net, amazing her coach.
The crowd erupted as the three-pointer sailed in for nothing but net.
With only seconds left, his shot was nothing but net, winning the game for his team.
