redistribution

noun
the act of distributing again
Pronunciation: / ˌridɪstrɪˈbjuʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən / (BrE)
the act of distributing something again, especially resources, wealth, or land, often to achieve greater equality

Examples

The redistribution of wealth
After the merger, there was a redistribution of roles within the company.
Many politicians debate the impact of redistribution on economic growth.
The new law led to a redistribution of government spending across regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distribution - the action of sharing something out among a number of recipients
