lacto-ovo vegetarianism

noun
vegetarian diet including eggs and dairy
6.8 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈlæk.toʊ ˈoʊ.voʊ vedʒ.ə.ˈter.ɪ.ə.nɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈlæk.təʊ ˈəʊ.vəʊ vedʒ.ə.ˈteə.rɪ.ə.nɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
A type of vegetarianism that includes eggs and dairy products but excludes meat, fish, and poultry.

Examples

Lacto-ovo vegetarianism diet
He adopted lacto-ovo vegetarianism to improve both his health and animal welfare.
Many people choose lacto-ovo vegetarianism because it allows for greater dietary variety than veganism.
Restaurants are increasingly offering options suitable for lacto-ovo vegetarianism.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: veganism - a diet excluding all animal products
  • degree lower: vegetarianism - a diet excluding meat, sometimes including dairy or eggs
