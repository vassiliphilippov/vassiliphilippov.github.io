cep

noun
vine trunk or stump
Pronunciation: / sɛp / (AmE), / sɛp / (BrE)
the trunk or main stem of a vine or tree, especially the stock or stump of a vine

Examples

Vineyard cep stump
The vineyard workers carefully pruned each cep to encourage healthy new growth.
After the harvest, only the old ceps remained standing in the field.
Each cep in the orchard showed the marks of many years of careful tending.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stump - the base part of a tree or plant left after the rest has been removed
  • synonym: trunk - the main woody stem of a tree
