wanderer

noun
person who moves from place to place
3.9 1.39/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…ëÀênd…ôr…ôr / (AmE), / Ààw…índ…ôr…ô / (BrE)
a person who travels from place to place without a fixed home or purpose

Examples

A lonely wanderer
After years as a wanderer, he decided to settle down in a small village.
Legends spoke of a mysterious wanderer who brought news from distant lands.
She lived as a wanderer, never staying in one place for more than a few months.

Synonyms

  • drifter

  • wayfarer
    7.6 0.20/m
