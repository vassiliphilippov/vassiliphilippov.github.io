tenderloin

noun
a very tender cut of meat
8.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈten.dər.lɔɪn/ (AmE), /ˈten.də.lɔɪn/ (BrE)
A cut of tender meat from the loin muscle along either side of the spine, often considered the most tender part of the animal.

Examples

Beef tenderloin steak
Beef tenderloin is often the highlight of a special dinner menu.
He ordered the grilled pork tenderloin with a side of roasted vegetables.
The chef prepared a perfectly cooked tenderloin for the main course.
