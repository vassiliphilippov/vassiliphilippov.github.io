erraticism

noun
unpredictable quality
8.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrætɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪˈrætɪˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
the quality or state of being unpredictable, inconsistent, or irregular in behavior or action

Examples

The erraticism of behavior
The erraticism of the weather this spring made it hard to plan outdoor events.
Investors are concerned about the erraticism of the stock market in recent months.
Her erraticism in decision-making often led to confusion among her colleagues.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consistency - the quality of always behaving or performing in a similar way
  • synonym: unpredictability - the state of being impossible to predict
