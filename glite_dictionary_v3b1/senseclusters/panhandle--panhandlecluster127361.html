panhandle

verb
ask strangers for money in the street
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpænˌhændl̩ / (AmE), / ˈpanˌhandl̩ / (BrE)
to beg for money by approaching people in public places, especially on the street

Examples

To panhandle money
He lost his job and had to panhandle on the streets to survive.
It's illegal to panhandle in certain parts of the city.
Tourists were often approached by people who would panhandle near the busy subway station.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beg - to ask people for money or food, usually because you are poor
