local hoon

noun
person known for reckless, antisocial behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊkl̩ huːn / (AmE), / ˈləʊkl huːn / (BrE)
A person in a community who is known for antisocial or reckless behavior, especially dangerous driving.

Examples

The local hoon drives recklessly
Everyone knows to keep their distance when the local hoon shows up at parties.
People in the neighborhood have complained about the noise made by the local hoon and his car.
The local hoon was caught speeding through the town center again last night.
