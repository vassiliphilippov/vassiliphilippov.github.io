disposer

noun
person allocating assets
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspoʊzər / (AmE), / dɪˈspəʊzə / (BrE)
a person who transfers, sells, or gives away property or assets, especially with the authority to make final decisions about them

Examples

Disposer of property
As the disposer of the estate, he was responsible for selling the family home.
The court appointed her as the official disposer of the company's remaining assets.
A disposer must act fairly when distributing property among heirs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beneficiary - a person who receives assets or property from a will or trust
  • synonym: executor - a person appointed to carry out the terms of a will
