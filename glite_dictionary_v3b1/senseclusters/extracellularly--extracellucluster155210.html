extracellularly

adverb
outside a cell
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəˈsɛljələrli / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈsɛljʊləli / (BrE)
in a manner occurring or located outside a cell or cells

Examples

Act extracellularly
The medication acts extracellularly to prevent harmful substances from entering the cells.
Certain enzymes function extracellularly to break down large molecules before absorption.
The bacteria release toxins extracellularly, affecting the surrounding tissue.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intracellularly - in a manner occurring or located inside a cell
