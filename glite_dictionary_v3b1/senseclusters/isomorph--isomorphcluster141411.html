isomorph

noun
substance with same structure
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsəˌmɔrf / (AmE), / ˈaɪsəmɔːf / (BrE)
something that has the same form or structure as another thing, especially in science or mathematics

Scientists studied the isomorph to understand how the organism maintains its shape across generations.
