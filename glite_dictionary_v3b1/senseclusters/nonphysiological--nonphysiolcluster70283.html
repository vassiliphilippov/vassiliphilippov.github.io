nonphysiological

adjective
not due to normal biology
9.1
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.fɪz.ɪ.ə.ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /nɒn.fɪz.ɪ.ə.ˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
not related to or caused by normal biological or bodily functions

Examples

Nonphysiological pain
The doctor concluded that the patient's pain was nonphysiological and not caused by any detectable disease.
Researchers found that the reaction was nonphysiological, resulting from external factors rather than natural body processes.
Some symptoms may appear nonphysiological when no physical explanation can be identified.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abnormal - not typical or usual; deviating from the norm
  • antonym: physiological - related to normal biological or bodily functions
