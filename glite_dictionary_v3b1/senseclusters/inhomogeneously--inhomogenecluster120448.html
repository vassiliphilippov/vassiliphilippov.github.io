inhomogeneously

adverb
not uniformly
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˌhoʊməˈdʒiːniəsli / (AmE), / ˌɪnhəˈmɒdʒɪnəsli /; / ˌɪnˌhɒməˈdʒiːniəsli / (BrE)
in a way that is not uniform or consistent throughout

Examples

Applied inhomogeneously
The paint was applied inhomogeneously, resulting in patches of different colors.
Minerals are often distributed inhomogeneously within the rock layers.
The temperature changed inhomogeneously across the large hall.
The chemical was distributed inhomogeneously across the surface, leading to uneven reactions.
In this experiment, the temperature changed inhomogeneously throughout the material.
The light was scattered inhomogeneously by the particles in the solution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heterogeneously - in a way that consists of different or diverse elements
  • antonym: homogeneously - in a way that is uniform or the same throughout
  • synonym: unevenly - in an irregular or unequal way
  • antonym: uniformly - in a consistent or even way throughout
