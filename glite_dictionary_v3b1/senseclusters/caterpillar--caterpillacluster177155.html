caterpillar

noun
tracked earth-moving vehicle
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkæt̬ɚˌpɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈkatəˌpɪlə / (BrE)
a large vehicle with continuous tracks instead of wheels, used mainly in construction or farming for moving earth

Examples

Caterpillar tractor
A massive caterpillar rumbled past us, flattening the old road.
Farmers often rely on a caterpillar when working on large fields.
The construction crew used a caterpillar to clear the land before building began.
