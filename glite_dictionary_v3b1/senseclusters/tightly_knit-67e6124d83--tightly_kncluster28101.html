tightly knit

adjective
closely integrated or united group
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪtli nɪt / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtli nɪt / (BrE)
Closely and firmly integrated; having strong social, emotional, or organizational bonds within a group.

Examples

A tightly knit team
A tightly knit team can overcome even the toughest challenges together.
She grew up in a tightly knit neighborhood where everyone knew each other well.
The chess club is a tightly knit group that supports each other both in and out of competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: close-knit - closely bound together, especially socially or emotionally
