fictionist

noun
story fabricator
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪk.ʃən.ɪst/ (AmE), /ˈfɪk.ʃən.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who invents or tells false or exaggerated stories, often presenting them as true

Examples

A fictionist of lies
The journalist was accused of being a fictionist after several stories were found to be fabricated.
No one believed his wild tales, calling him a fictionist behind his back.
She gained a reputation as a fictionist because her stories were always too dramatic to be true.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fabulist - a person who invents fables or lies
  • synonym: liar - a person who tells lies
  • antonym: truth-teller - a person who always tells the truth
