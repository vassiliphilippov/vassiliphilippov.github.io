floating

noun
the act of remaining afloat on water
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfləʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of remaining on or near the surface of a liquid, especially water, without sinking

Examples

Floating in the pool
Children enjoy floating on inflatable rafts at the beach.
He practiced floating on his back before learning to swim.
The sensation of floating in the pool was very relaxing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swimming - the sport or activity of moving through water by using the limbs
