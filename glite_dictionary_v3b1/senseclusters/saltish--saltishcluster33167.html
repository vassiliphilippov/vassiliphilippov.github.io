saltish

adjective
somewhat salty
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔltɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɔːltɪʃ / (BrE)
Somewhat salty in taste or character.

Examples

A saltish taste
Her homemade seaweed snacks are pleasantly saltish without being overpowering.
I noticed a saltish aftertaste in the water from that well.
The soup has a saltish flavor, but it's still quite enjoyable.
