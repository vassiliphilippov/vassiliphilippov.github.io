deflationist

adjective
minimizing theory
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfleɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / dɪˈfleɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
supporting or relating to a theory or approach that minimizes or simplifies the importance of a concept, especially in philosophy or semantics

Examples

Deflationist theory
The philosopher presented a deflationist account of truth during the lecture.
Her deflationist perspective challenges traditional views on the meaning of language.
Many modern theories in semantics adopt a deflationist approach to complex concepts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inflationist - supporting or relating to a theory that expands or emphasizes the importance of a concept
  • synonym: minimalist - favoring simplicity and minimal elements
