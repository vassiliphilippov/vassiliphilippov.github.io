Synonyms

  • take to the skies
    4.6 0.15/m
    take to the skies is a vivid phrase for beginning to fly into open air, while take off is the neutral aviation term.
  • lift off
    3.9 1.47/m
    lift off is used for rockets or spacecraft rising from the ground or a pad, while take off suits aircraft or birds leaving the ground.
  • take wing
    5.8 0.27/m
    take wing is a more literary phrase often for birds or sudden beginnings, while take off is the common, neutral aviation term.
  • take flight
    3.5 0.84/m
    take flight often means to run away quickly or start flying suddenly, while take off focuses on an aircraft leaving the ground.
