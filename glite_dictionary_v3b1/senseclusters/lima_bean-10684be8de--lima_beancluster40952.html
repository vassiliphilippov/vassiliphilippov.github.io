lima bean

noun
an edible flat bean, eaten cooked
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪmə ˌbin / (AmE), / ˈliːmə ˌbiːn / (BrE)
a plant that produces flat, pale green or white edible beans, or the bean itself, usually eaten cooked

Examples

Lima beans cooked
Lima beans should always be cooked thoroughly before eating.
My grandmother serves delicious stews made with lima beans and fresh herbs.
She added some lima beans to the soup for extra flavor and texture.
Many gardeners prefer growing lima bean plants because of their rich harvest.
She added chopped lima beans to the vegetable stew for extra flavor and texture.
The recipe called for both corn and lima beans as the main ingredients.
I always disliked lima beans as a child, but now I enjoy their buttery flavor.
She added cooked lima beans to the vegetable stew for extra protein.
The farmer grows both green beans and lima beans in his fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butter bean - another name for the lima bean, especially in the southern US and Britain
