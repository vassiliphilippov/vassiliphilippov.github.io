planning board

noun
advisory group for planning and development
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈplænɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈplænɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
a group of officials appointed to advise on and oversee the development and use of land and resources, typically in a local government or administrative context

Examples

The planning board meeting
After a long debate, the planning board approved the construction of the new shopping center.
Citizens are encouraged to attend planning board meetings to voice their opinions on local development.
The city council will meet with the planning board to discuss the new housing project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: planning commission - an official body that makes recommendations or decisions about planning matters
