pony cart

noun
light cart pulled by a pony
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊni kɑrt / (AmE), / ˈpəʊni kɑːt / (BrE)
a light two-wheeled or four-wheeled cart, drawn by a pony, typically with seats for two people

Examples

A pony cart ride
A small pony cart waited at the gate, ready to take them to town.
She decorated the pony cart with ribbons for the parade.
The children took a ride around the farm in the pony cart.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carriage - a wheeled vehicle for people, usually horse-drawn
