girly

noun
informal young woman
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɜrli / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːli / (BrE)
an informal term for a girl or young woman, often one who is seen as very feminine

Examples

A girly loves pink
When she was younger, everyone called her a girly because she loved pink and sparkles.
The group of girlies laughed together at the café after school.
He teased his sister by calling her a girly for wearing a frilly dress.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boy - a male child or young man
  • synonym: girl - a female child or young woman
