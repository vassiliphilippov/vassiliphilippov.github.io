unassertive

adjective
not confident or forceful; lacking assertiveness
3.8 5.0
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ə.ˈsɜːr.tɪv/ /; / /ən.ə.ˈsɜːr.tɪv/ (AmE), /ʌn.ə.ˈsɜː.tɪv/ /; / /ən.ə.ˈsɜː.tɪv/ (BrE)
not confident or forceful in expressing one's opinions or desires; lacking assertiveness

Examples

An unassertive person
Despite her talent, she remained unassertive in meetings and rarely spoke up.
He has an unassertive manner that often leads others to overlook his ideas.
The teacher encouraged unassertive students to participate more in class discussions.
