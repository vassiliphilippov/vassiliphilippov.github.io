Cucurbita argyrosperma

noun
a species of squash plant
Pronunciation: / kuːˈkɝːbɪtə ˌɑːrɡiəˈspɝːmə / (AmE), / kjuːˈkɜːbɪtə ˌɑːɡɪəˈspɜːmə / (BrE)
A species of squash plant cultivated for its round to oval fruits with grey, green, and white striped, often warty rinds.
