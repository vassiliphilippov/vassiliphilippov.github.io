embanked

adjective
with raised banks
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbæŋkt / (AmE), / ɪmˈbæŋkt / (BrE)
having a bank or raised edge built up, especially to prevent water from overflowing

Examples

An embanked canal
The embanked canal kept the nearby fields safe from flooding during the storm.
Engineers inspected the embanked section of the river for signs of erosion.
After the improvements, the once dangerous stream became a secure, embanked waterway.
