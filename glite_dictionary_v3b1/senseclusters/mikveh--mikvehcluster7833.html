mikveh

noun
Jewish ritual bath
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪkvə / (AmE), / ˈmɪkvə / (BrE)
a ritual bath used for purification in Jewish religious practice

Examples

The mikveh ritual bath
Before her wedding, she immersed herself in the mikveh as part of the tradition.
