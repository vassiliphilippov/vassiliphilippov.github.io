jauntily

adverb
in a lively, confident or stylish way
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔn.tɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːn.tɪ.li / (BrE)
In a lively, cheerful, and self-confident manner, often with a fashionable or stylish flair.

Examples

To walk jauntily
He tipped his hat jauntily before stepping onto the train.
She walked jauntily down the street, greeting everyone with a bright smile.
The young boy skipped jauntily ahead of his parents, full of excitement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jaunty - having a lively, cheerful, and self-confident manner
