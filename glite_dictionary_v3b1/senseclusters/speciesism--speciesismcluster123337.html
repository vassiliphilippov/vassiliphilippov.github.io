speciesism

noun
belief in species superiority
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈspiː.ʃiː.zɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈspiː.siː.zɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈspiː.ʃiː.zɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈspiː.siː.zɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the belief that one species (especially humans) is superior to others and deserves different or greater moral consideration or rights
