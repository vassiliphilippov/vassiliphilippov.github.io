apoliticism

noun
indifference or opposition to politics
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ.pəˈlɪt.ɪˌsɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.pəˈlɪt.ɪ.sɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the state or quality of having no interest in or hostility to political affairs or affiliations

Examples

Apoliticism in politics
Her apoliticism was evident; she refused to discuss any party or policy.
Some critics argue that apoliticism can be a barrier to necessary social change.
The rise of apoliticism among young voters has led to declining participation in elections.
