apoliticism

noun
indifference or opposition to politics
4.5 7.7
Pronunciation: /eɪ.pə.ˈlɪt.ɪ.sɪ.zəm/ /; / /ə.pə.ˈlɪt.ɪ.sɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /eɪ.pə.ˈlɪt.ɪ.sɪ.zəm/ /; / /ə.pə.ˈlɪt.ɪ.sɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
