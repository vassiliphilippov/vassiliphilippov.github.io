overcook

verb
cook for too long
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈkʊk/ /; / /əʊ.vər.ˈkʊk/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈkʊk/ (BrE)
to cook food for too long, making it spoiled, dry, or less tasty

Examples

To overcook the pasta
If you overcook the pasta, it will become mushy and lose its texture.
She tends to overcook chicken, making it dry and tough.
The recipe warns not to overcook the sauce or it will lose its flavor.

Related Terms
