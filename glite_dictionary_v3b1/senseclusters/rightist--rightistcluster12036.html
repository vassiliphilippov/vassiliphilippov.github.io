rightist

adjective
supporter of the political right
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪ.tɪst / (AmE), / ˈraɪ.tɪst / (BrE)
Believing in or supporting the views and policies of the political right, especially in politics.

Examples

Rightist views
In recent years, support for rightist politicians has grown in several countries.
She holds rightist views on economic policies and prefers limited government intervention.
The party's rightist wing called for stricter immigration controls.
