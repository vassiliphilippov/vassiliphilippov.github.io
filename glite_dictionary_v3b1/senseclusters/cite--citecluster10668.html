cite

verb
officially summon to appear, esp. in court
Pronunciation: / saɪt / (AmE), / saɪt / (BrE)
to officially summon someone to appear, especially before a court or tribunal

Examples

To cite someone to court
He was cited to appear on charges of contempt of court next week.
The company was cited by regulators for failing to comply with safety standards.
The court cited her as a witness in the ongoing trial.

Related Terms

  • synonym: summon - to order someone to appear, especially in a law court
