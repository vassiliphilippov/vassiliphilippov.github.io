whole wheat bread

noun
bread made from whole wheat flour
3.5 1.0
Pronunciation: / hoʊl wit brɛd / (AmE), / həʊl wiːt brɛd / (BrE)
Bread made from flour that uses the whole wheat grain, resulting in a denser and more nutritious product than white bread.

Examples

A slice of whole wheat bread
Making sandwiches with whole wheat bread is a healthier choice for many people.
She prefers whole wheat bread to white bread for its richer flavor and higher fiber content.
The bakery offers fresh whole wheat bread every morning.
