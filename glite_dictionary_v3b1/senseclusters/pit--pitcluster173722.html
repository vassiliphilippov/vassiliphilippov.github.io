pit

verb
mark with small scars or hollows
Pronunciation: / pɪt / (AmE), / pɪt / (BrE)
to mark or scar with small indentations or hollows, often as a result of disease, injury, or wear

Examples

To pit the surface
The disease had pitted her arms so badly that the scars never completely faded.
The hailstorm pitted the car's surface, leaving it covered in small dents.
Years of acne pitted his cheeks with tiny scars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pock - to mark with pocks, usually from disease
  • synonym: scar - to mark permanently with a heal or blemish
