uptilt

verb
to tilt upward
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌtɪlt / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌtɪlt / (BrE)
to tilt or raise something so that it points upward.

Examples

To uptilt the mirror
He uptilted the mirror so he could check the ceiling of the room.
The photographer uptilted the camera to capture the tall building in the frame.
She uptilted her head to look at the stars above.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lower - to move something to a lower position
  • synonym: raise - to lift or move something to a higher position
