homophobic attitude

noun
prejudiced feeling or belief toward LGBTQ+ people
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊməˈfoʊbɪk ˈætɪtud / (AmE), / ˌhəʊməˈfəʊbɪk ˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
a negative feeling, belief, or disposition toward LGBTQ+ people, stemming from prejudice or fear

Examples

Homophobic attitude toward LGBTQ+
A homophobic attitude can lead to discrimination in the workplace.
Media coverage has helped reduce the prevalence of homophobic attitude in recent years.
The principal condemned any homophobic attitude expressed at school assemblies.
