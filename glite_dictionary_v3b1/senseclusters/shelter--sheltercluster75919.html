shelter

noun
state of being protected
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛltɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃɛltə / (BrE)
the state or condition of being protected from danger, trouble, or bad weather

Examples

Seek shelter from danger
During the conflict, families found temporary shelter in underground bunkers.
Many animals rely on dense forests for shelter against predators.
The villagers sought shelter from the storm in the church.
