hook

verb
make addicted or dependent
Pronunciation: / hʊk / (AmE), / hʊk / (BrE)
to cause someone to become addicted or dependent on something, especially a narcotic drug.

Examples

To hook on drugs
She became hooked after only a few weeks of using it.
The dealer tried to hook young people on the drug.
The new painkiller hooked him before he realized what was happening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addict - to make someone dependent on a substance or activity
  • antonym: detox - to remove a drug dependency; overcome addiction
