stencil

verb
to mark using a stencil
4.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsten.səl/ /; / /ˈsten.sɪl/ (AmE), /ˈsten.sɪl/ /; / /ˈsten.səl/ (BrE)
to make a design, pattern, or lettering on a surface by applying paint or ink through a stencil

Examples

To stencil a pattern
Each box was stenciled with a unique identification number for inventory purposes.
The artist carefully stenciled the pattern onto the wall above the fireplace.
We spent the afternoon stenciling holiday designs onto plain T-shirts for the children.
She spent the afternoon stenciling flowers onto the kitchen walls for a fresh look.
The artist is stenciling a logo onto each T-shirt for the event.
He carefully stenciled numbers on the boxes to keep them organized.
