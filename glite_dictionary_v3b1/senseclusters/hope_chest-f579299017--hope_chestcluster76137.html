hope chest

noun
chest for storing trousseau items before marriage
5.3 5.6
Pronunciation: /hoʊp tʃest/ (AmE), /həʊp tʃest/ (BrE)
a chest used to store clothing, linens, and other household items collected in anticipation of marriage, especially by a young woman
