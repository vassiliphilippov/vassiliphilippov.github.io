scribbly

adjective
messy or irregular
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɪbli / (AmE), / ˈskrɪbli / (BrE)
covered with or resembling messy, irregular, or winding lines, like careless writing or drawing

Examples

scribbly lines
The child's drawing was full of scribbly lines and bright colors.
She handed me a scribbly note that was hard to read.
The wall was covered in scribbly graffiti after the festival.
The artist used a scribbly style to give the drawing a sense of movement.
We noticed scribbly patterns on the bark of the eucalyptus trees in the park.
Her notes were so scribbly that no one else could read them.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neat - tidy and orderly in appearance or style
  • synonym: scrawly - written or drawn in a thin, messy, or awkward way
  • synonym: scribbled - covered with careless or irregular writing or marks
  • antonym: smooth - having an even surface without marks or lines
