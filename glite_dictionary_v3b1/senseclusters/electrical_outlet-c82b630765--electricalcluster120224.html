electrical outlet

noun
socket for electrical plugs
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl ˈaʊtlɛt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl ˈaʊtlət / (BrE)
