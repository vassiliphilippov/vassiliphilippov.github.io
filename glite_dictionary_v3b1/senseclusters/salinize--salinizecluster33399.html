salinize

verb
to add salt for preservation
Pronunciation: / ˈsæləˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsælɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to treat or preserve something, especially food, by adding salt to make it salty for preservation

Examples

To salinize meat
Before refrigeration, people would salinize meat to keep it from spoiling.
The chef decided to salinize the fish before cooking it over the fire.
Farmers sometimes salinize vegetables to preserve them for the winter months.

Related Terms

  • antonym: desalinate - to remove salt from something
  • synonym: salt - to add salt to food for flavor or preservation
