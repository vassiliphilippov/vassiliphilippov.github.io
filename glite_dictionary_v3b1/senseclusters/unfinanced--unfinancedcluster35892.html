unfinanced

adjective
without funding
5.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfaɪnænst / (AmE), / ʌnˈfaɪnɑːnst / (BrE)
not provided with financial support or funding

Examples

An unfinanced project
The new community center remained unfinanced after the city council rejected the proposal.
Many small businesses struggle when their projects are unfinanced for long periods.
Without government support, the research initiative was left unfinanced and could not proceed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: funded - provided with financial support
  • synonym: unfunded - not having funds or financial backing
