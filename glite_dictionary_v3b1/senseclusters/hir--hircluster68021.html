hir

pronoun
gender-neutral possessive
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɪr / (AmE), / hɪə / (BrE)
a gender-neutral possessive determiner used before a noun to indicate that something belongs to someone, instead of 'his' or 'her'

Examples

Hir backpack
Please return hir book to the lost and found by Friday.
Each student should bring hir own laptop to the workshop.
The teacher praised hir for the creative project idea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: her - possessive determiner for a female person
  • synonym: his - possessive determiner for a male person
  • synonym: their - gender-neutral possessive determiner for one or more people
