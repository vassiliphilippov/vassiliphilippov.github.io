Avalokiteshvara

proper noun
Buddhist Bodhisattva of compassion
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˌvoʊloʊˌkiːtɛʃˈvɑːrə / (AmE), / əˌvəʊlɒˌkiːtɛʃˈvɑːrə / (BrE)
A Bodhisattva in Mahayana Buddhism embodying the compassion of all Buddhas, often depicted as a male figure and widely revered across Asia.
