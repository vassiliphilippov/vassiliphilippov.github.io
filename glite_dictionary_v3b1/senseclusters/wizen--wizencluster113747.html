wizen

adjective
shriveled or wrinkled from age or illness
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪzən / (AmE), / ˈwɪz(ə)n / (BrE)
Having a dry, shriveled, and wrinkled appearance, especially as a result of aging or illness.

Examples

A wizened face
After years in the sun, her hands became wizened and thin.
His wizened features gave him a look of both wisdom and weariness.
The wizened face of the old gardener revealed decades of hard work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shrivel - to become dry and wrinkled, often from loss of moisture
  • synonym: wither - to become dry and shriveled, especially from loss of moisture
