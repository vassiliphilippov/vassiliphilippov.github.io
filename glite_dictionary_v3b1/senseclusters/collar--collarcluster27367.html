collar

noun
marking or band on an animal's neck
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑlər / (AmE), / ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
(Zoology) An encircling band or distinctive marking around the neck of an animal.

Examples

Snake collar
Some snakes have a colorful collar just behind their heads.
The jaguar's collar is visible as a dark stripe around its neck.
The robin's distinctive collar makes it easy to identify among other birds.
