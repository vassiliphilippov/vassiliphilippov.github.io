unsuspectingly

adverb
without suspecting; without suspicion
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsəˈspɛktɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəˈspɛktɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of suspicion; without suspecting anything.

Examples

Unsuspectingly share information
Unsuspectingly, the company disclosed sensitive information to the fake inspector.
He walked unsuspectingly into the trap the group had set for him.
She unsuspectingly handed over the package, unaware of its contents.

Related Terms

  • antonym: suspiciously - in a way that arouses suspicion
