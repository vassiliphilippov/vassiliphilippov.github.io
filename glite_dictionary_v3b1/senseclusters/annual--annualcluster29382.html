annual

noun
yearly publication or book
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈænjuəl / (AmE), / ˈanjʊəl / (BrE)
A book or publication issued once each year, often containing information, reports, or records for that year.

Examples

A comic annual
Children often looked forward to receiving their favorite comic annual each Christmas.
I bought the company's annual to review their financial progress.
The school publishes an annual that highlights students' achievements throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: almanac - an annual calendar containing important dates and statistical information
