depth

noun
distance from surface to bottom
Pronunciation: / dɛpθ / (AmE), / dɛpθ / (BrE)
The distance from the top or surface to the bottom of something, or the extent inward or backward from an edge.

Examples

Depth of the ocean
Divers measured the depth of the lake before entering the water.
Please check the depth of the shelf to make sure the boxes will fit.
The closet's depth allows you to store bulky items easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: width - the measurement or extent of something from side to side
