botchy

adjective
of poor quality; clumsily done
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːtʃi / (AmE), / ˈbɒtʃi / (BrE)
Done in a clumsy or unskillful way; of poor quality or workmanship.

Examples

A botchy job

The repairs to the car were botchy and only made things worse.
We had to redo the painting because the first job was far too botchy.
