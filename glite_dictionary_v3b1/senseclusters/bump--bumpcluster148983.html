bump

verb
meet by chance; come upon unexpectedly
Pronunciation: / bʌmp / (AmE), / bʌmp / (BrE)
to meet someone or something unexpectedly, especially by chance or accident

Examples

To bump into a friend
I didn't expect to bump into my old professor at the airport.
They bumped into a fascinating little café while wandering through the city.
You never know whom you might bump into at these large gatherings.
