drenched in

adjective
completely covered or filled with something
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛntʃt ɪn / (AmE), / ˈdrɛntʃt ɪn / (BrE)

Examples

The city was drenched in color during the vibrant festival.
The garden was drenched in golden sunlight after the storm.
