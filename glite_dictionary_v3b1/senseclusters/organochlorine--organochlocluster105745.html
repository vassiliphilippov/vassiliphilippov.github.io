organochlorine

adjective
containing carbon and chlorine
Pronunciation: / ɔːrˌɡænoʊˈklɔːriːn / (AmE), / ɔːˌɡænəʊˈklɔːriːn / (BrE)
containing both carbon and chlorine, or relating to chemical compounds with these elements

Examples

Organochlorine compound
The researchers analyzed organochlorine pesticides found in the soil samples.
Many organochlorine compounds are persistent in the environment and can accumulate in living organisms.
The study focused on the health effects of organochlorine chemicals in drinking water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chlorinated - containing chlorine or treated with chlorine
  • synonym: organic - relating to or derived from living matter; containing carbon
