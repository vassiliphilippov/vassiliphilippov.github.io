unreachably

adverb
beyond reach
8.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈriːtʃəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈriːtʃəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be reached or attained

Examples

Unreachably deep
The treasure was hidden unreachably deep beneath the ocean floor.
Her dreams seemed unreachably distant after the setback.
The castle stood unreachably atop the steep, rocky cliffs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attainably - in a way that can be reached or achieved
  • synonym: inaccessibly - in a way that cannot be accessed or reached
