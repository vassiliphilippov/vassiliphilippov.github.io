secret

adjective
mysterious or beyond ordinary understanding
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːkrət / (AmE), / ˈsiːkrɪt / (BrE)
(of knowledge, information, or meaning) not easily understood or explained; mysterious or beyond ordinary understanding.

Examples

Secret meaning
Ancient texts are often filled with secret meanings and hidden references.
The philosopher spoke in secret terms that few could understand.
There is a secret wisdom in these rituals that escapes ordinary explanation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mysterious - difficult or impossible to understand, explain, or identify
  • antonym: obvious - easily perceived or understood; clear, self-evident
  • synonym: occult - relating to mystical, supernatural, or magical powers or phenomena
