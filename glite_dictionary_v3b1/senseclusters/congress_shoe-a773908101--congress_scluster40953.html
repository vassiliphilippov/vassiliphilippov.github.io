congress shoe

noun
ankle-high shoe with elastic sides
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑŋɡrəs ʃu / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡrəs ʃuː / (BrE)
an ankle-high shoe with elastic gussets at the sides, making it easy to slip on and off
