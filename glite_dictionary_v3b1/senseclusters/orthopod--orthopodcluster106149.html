orthopod

noun
orthopedic doctor
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrθəˌpɑd / (AmE), / ˈɔːθəˌpɒd / (BrE)
an informal term for an orthopedic doctor or surgeon

Examples

The orthopod doctor
After his injury, he made an appointment with the best orthopod in town.
The orthopod recommended surgery to repair the torn ligament in her knee.
Many athletes trust their orthopod to help them recover from sports injuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orthopedist - a doctor specializing in the treatment of bones and joints
  • synonym: surgeon - a doctor who performs operations
