repaint

verb
redraw a UI component
5.4
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈpeɪnt/ /; / /rɪ.ˈpeɪnt/ (AmE), /riː.ˈpeɪnt/ /; / /rɪ.ˈpeɪnt/ (BrE)
(in computing) to cause a graphical user‑interface component or a region of the screen to be redrawn, typically after its contents change.

Examples

To repaint the screen
The GUI framework will repaint the panel after the new data arrives and the model updates.
When the window was resized, the browser repainted the affected region to show the updated layout.
Efficient graphics code avoids unnecessary repainting by only invalidating small areas that changed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: redraw - to draw the contents of a screen area or UI element again
  • synonym: refresh - to update or reload a display or its data; often used for UI updates
