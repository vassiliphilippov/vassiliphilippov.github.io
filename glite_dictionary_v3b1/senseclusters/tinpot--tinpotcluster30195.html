tinpot

adjective
petty or insignificant (especially of leaders)
9.9 8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪnˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈtɪnˌpɒt / (BrE)
(especially of a leader or institution) unimportant, petty, or lacking in real power or authority; inferior or contemptible.

Examples

A tinpot leader
Critics called the new committee a tinpot organization with no real influence.
He dismissed his rival as just another tinpot official seeking attention.
The country was ruled by a tinpot dictator for decades.

Related Terms

  • antonym: important - having great significance or value
  • synonym: insignificant - not important or worthy of attention
  • synonym: petty - of little importance, especially of a person who is small-minded or trivial
