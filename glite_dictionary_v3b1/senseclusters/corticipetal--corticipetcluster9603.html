corticipetal

adjective
passing toward the cerebral cortex
9.9 9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔrtɪˈpɛtəl / (AmE), / ˌkɔːtɪˈpiːtəl / (BrE)
(of a nerve fiber or impulse) originating outside and passing toward the cerebral cortex.

Examples

Corticipetal nerve fibers
Corticipetal nerve fibers carry sensory information toward the cerebral cortex for processing.
Damage to corticipetal pathways can affect how the cortex receives external signals.
The neurologist explained the path of the corticipetal impulses in the brain.
