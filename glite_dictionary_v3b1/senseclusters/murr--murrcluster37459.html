murr

verb
soft sound made by a content cat
5.5
Pronunciation: / mɜr / (AmE), / məː / (BrE)
(of a cat) to make a low, soft, rumbling sound expressing contentment.

Examples

Cats murr softly
At night, she listened to the kittens murring softly in their basket.
The old tomcat murred contentedly beside the warm fireplace.
When I scratched his ears, the cat began to murr with delight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: purr - a low continuous vibratory sound a cat makes when content
