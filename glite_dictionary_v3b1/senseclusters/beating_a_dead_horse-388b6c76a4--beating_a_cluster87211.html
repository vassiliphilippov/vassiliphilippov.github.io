beating a dead horse

To continue pursuing or discussing a matter that is already settled or pointless, especially when any further effort is futile.

Examples

Beating a dead horse again
Discussing that topic again would just be beating a dead horse.
He kept beating a dead horse by bringing up the same complaint over and over.
There's no sense in beating a dead horse; we've already made our decision.
