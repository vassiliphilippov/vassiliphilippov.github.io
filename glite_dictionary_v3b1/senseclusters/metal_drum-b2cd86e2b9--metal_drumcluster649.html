metal drum

noun
large cylindrical metal container
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtəl drʌm / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəl drʌm / (BrE)
A large cylindrical container made of metal, commonly used for storing or transporting liquids or bulk materials.

Examples

A metal drum container
A metal drum rolled across the warehouse floor, rattling noisily as it moved.
The factory stores chemicals in large metal drums to ensure safe handling.
They shipped the oil overseas in sealed metal drums to prevent leaks.
