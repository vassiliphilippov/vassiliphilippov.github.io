(astronomy) The rising of a star or celestial object above the horizon.

Examples

Star ascension
Astronomers carefully track the ascension of various stars throughout the year.
Just before dawn, the ascension of Venus can be observed over the eastern horizon.
The ascension of Sirius marks the beginning of the ancient Egyptian calendar.
