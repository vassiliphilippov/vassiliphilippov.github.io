embalm a body

verb
preserve a dead body from decay
6.2
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbɑlm ə ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / ɪmˈbɑːm ə ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
to preserve a deceased body using chemicals or other methods to prevent decomposition

Examples

To embalm a body carefully

It is common practice to embalm a body for an open-casket funeral.
Some religions have specific rituals they follow when they embalm a body.
