yours

pronoun
typical of someone
4.8
Pronunciation: / jɔɹz / (AmE), / jɔːz / (BrE)
used informally to say that something is very typical of someone or perfectly suits their style or personality

Examples

That style is yours
That colorful jacket is so yours; it matches your unique sense of style perfectly.
When I saw the vintage car, I immediately thought, 'That's totally yours!'
This playlist is yours all over—every song fits your taste exactly.
