muffy

adjective
soft and fluffy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌfi / (AmE), / ˈmʌfi / (BrE)
slang for soft, fluffy, or resembling a muff in texture or appearance

Examples

Muffy scarf
She wore a muffy scarf to keep warm in winter.
The kitten's muffy fur made it irresistible to pet.
He bought a pair of muffy slippers for extra comfort at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fluffy - soft and light in texture
  • synonym: fuzzy - covered with soft, fine hair or fibers
  • antonym: rough - having an uneven or coarse surface
