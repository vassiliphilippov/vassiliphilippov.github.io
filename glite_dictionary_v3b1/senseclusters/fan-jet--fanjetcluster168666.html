fan-jet

noun
airplane with fanjet engine
Pronunciation: / ˈfænˌdʒɛt / (AmE), / ˈfanˌdʒɛt / (BrE)
An airplane powered by a fanjet engine, which uses a jet propulsion system with a large fan to generate additional thrust.

Examples

Fan-jet airplane
A fan-jet can travel at higher speeds than most propeller-driven aircraft.
Engineers designed a new fan-jet that reduces noise while increasing thrust.
The airline upgraded its fleet to include the latest fan-jet models for improved efficiency.
