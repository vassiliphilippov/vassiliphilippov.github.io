Cuna

proper noun
indigenous group, language
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːnə / (AmE), / ˈkuːnə / (BrE)
an indigenous people of Panama and Colombia, or the language spoken by this group

Examples

Cuna people
The Cuna have a rich tradition of textile art known as molas.
Many Cuna people live on the San Blas Islands off the coast of Panama.
She is studying the Cuna language as part of her research on indigenous cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Guna - another name for the Cuna people and their language
  • synonym: indigenous - originating in and characteristic of a particular region or country
