repeat oneself

verb
say or do the same thing again
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpit wʌnˌsɛlf / (AmE), / rɪˈpiːt wʌnˌsɛlf / (BrE)
to say or do the same thing again, especially unnecessarily or in a way that is tiresome

Examples

To repeat oneself often
He tends to repeat himself during long meetings, which can be frustrating for listeners.
Please listen carefully so I don’t have to repeat myself again.
The speaker apologized for having to repeat herself due to the interruption.

Related Terms

  • antonym: paraphrase - to express the meaning of something using different words
  • synonym: reiterate - to say something again, often for emphasis or clarity
