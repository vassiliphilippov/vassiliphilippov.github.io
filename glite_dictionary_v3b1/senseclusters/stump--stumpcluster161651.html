stump

noun
raised speaker's platform, esp. political
Pronunciation: / stʌmp / (AmE), / stʌmp / (BrE)
a platform raised above the surrounding ground to give prominence to the speaker, especially in political contexts

Examples

Political stump
He was known for his passionate oratory from the stump.
The candidate climbed the stump and began addressing the cheering crowd.
Town squares often had a wooden stump for political speeches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: platform - a raised level surface on which people or things can stand
