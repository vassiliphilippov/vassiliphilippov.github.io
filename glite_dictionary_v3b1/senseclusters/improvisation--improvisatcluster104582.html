improvisation

noun
spontaneous, unplanned solution or creation
4.7
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌprɑːvəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌɪm.prə.vəˈzeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌɪmprəvəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪmˌprɒvɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ɪmˌprɒv.ɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or result of creating or performing something spontaneously, without planning, especially as a solution or in creative activities
