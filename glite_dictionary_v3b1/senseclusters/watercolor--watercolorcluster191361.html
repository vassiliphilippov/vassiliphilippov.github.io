watercolor

noun
paint with water-soluble pigments
5.5 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔː.tər.kʌ.lər/ /; / /ˈwɑː.tər.kʌ.lər/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.tə.kʌ.lə/ (BrE)
a type of paint made with pigments suspended in water, used especially for art on paper

Examples

A set of watercolors
Many artists prefer watercolors for their delicate transparency and layering effects.
She painted a beautiful landscape with watercolor on textured paper.

The artist exhibited a new watercolor depicting a peaceful lakeside scene.
