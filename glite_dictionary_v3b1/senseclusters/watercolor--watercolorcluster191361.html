watercolor

noun
paint with water-soluble pigments
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚˌkʌlɚ /; / ˈwɔː.t̬ɚˌkʌl.ɚ /; / ˈwɔt̬ɚˌkʌlɚ /; / ˈwɔːtərˌkʌlɚ /; / ˈwɔt̬ərˌkʌlɚ /; / ˈwɑtərˌkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌkʌlə /; / ˈwɔː.təˌkʌl.ə /; / ˈwɔːtəkʌlə / (BrE)
a type of paint made with pigments suspended in water, used especially for art on paper
