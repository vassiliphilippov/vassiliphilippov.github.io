unmalted

adjective
not converted into malt
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɔltɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɔːltɪd / (BrE)
(of grain) not having been converted into malt.

Examples

Unmalted grain
Certain beers are brewed using a blend of unmalted and malted grains.
Some traditional whiskeys use unmalted barley in their distillation process.
The recipe calls for both unmalted and malted barley to create a unique flavor.
