redbone

noun
slang for light-skinned person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌbəʊn / (BrE)
a slang term for a person, especially a woman, with light brown or reddish skin, often of mixed Black and other ancestry

Examples

A redbone woman
The artist wrote a song about a beautiful redbone he met at the party.
Some people use the word redbone to describe someone with a light brown complexion.
She was often called a redbone because of her unique skin tone and features.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dark-skinned - having a dark skin tone
  • synonym: mulatto - a person of mixed white and Black ancestry
