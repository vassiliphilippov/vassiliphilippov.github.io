regulatory agency

noun
government body overseeing regulation
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjələˌtɔri ˈeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊlətəri ˈeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
a government authority responsible for supervising and enforcing rules and standards in specific sectors to protect the public interest

Examples

A regulatory agency approves products
Every pharmaceutical product must be approved by a regulatory agency before it enters the market.
The regulatory agency imposed new safety standards on chemical manufacturers.
The environmental regulatory agency monitors pollution and enforces compliance with the law.

Related Terms

  • synonym: regulator - a person or body that supervises an industry or activity
