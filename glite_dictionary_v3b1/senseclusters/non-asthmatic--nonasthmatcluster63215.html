non-asthmatic

adjective
without asthma
3.8
Pronunciation: / nɑn æsˈmædɪk / (AmE), / nɒn æsˈmætɪk / (BrE)

Examples

Non-asthmatic symptoms

A non-asthmatic child may not experience breathing difficulties during exercise.
