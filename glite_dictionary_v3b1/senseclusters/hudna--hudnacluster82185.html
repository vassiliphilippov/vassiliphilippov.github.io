hudna

noun
temporary truce
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊdnə / (AmE), / ˈhʊdnə / (BrE)
a truce or temporary ceasefire, especially in the context of Middle Eastern conflicts

Examples

A hudna truce
The leaders declared a hudna to give both sides time to negotiate.
During the hudna, humanitarian organizations delivered food and medical supplies to civilians.
A fragile hudna was established after weeks of intense fighting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceasefire - an agreement to stop fighting for a period of time
  • antonym: hostilities - acts of fighting in a war
  • synonym: truce - an agreement between enemies to stop fighting for a time
