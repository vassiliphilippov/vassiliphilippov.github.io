shower

verb
to bestow something abundantly
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊɚ /; / ˈʃaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊə /; / ˈʃaʊ.ə / (BrE)
to give something to someone in large amounts or with great generosity, especially affection or gifts

Examples

To shower someone with gifts
He showers his children with affection every day.
Her friends showered her with gifts on her birthday.
The athletes were showered with confetti after winning the championship.
Fans showered the winning team with confetti as they celebrated their victory.
On her birthday, her friends showered her with thoughtful gifts and flowers.
The new employee was showered with praise after finishing his first big project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deluge - to flood or overwhelm with a great quantity
  • synonym: lavish - to give something in great amounts or without limit
  • degree lower: praise - to express warm approval or admiration
  • antonym: spare - to refrain from giving or using generously
