toasting

noun
temporary on-screen notification
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊstɪŋ / (BrE)
(in computing) the act of showing brief, transient on-screen notifications (called 'toasts') that provide unobtrusive feedback to the user.

Examples

App toasting alerts
The app uses toasting to confirm actions without interrupting the user's workflow.
During testing, she logged every toasting so developers could check message timing and content.
Android's design guidelines recommend brief toasting for simple feedback, not long interactive dialogs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: notification - a message or alert that informs a user about an event or action
  • synonym: toast (notification) - a small, temporary on-screen message shown by software to inform the user
