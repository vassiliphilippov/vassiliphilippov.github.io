bitter

adjective
expressing intense grief or regret
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtər /; / ˈbɪt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɪtə / (BrE)
feeling or showing strong negative emotions such as anger, resentment, grief, or hostility; or expressing these emotions in speech or actions

Examples

Bitter feelings
He gave a bitter sigh, unable to forget his mistake.
She wept bitter tears at the news of her loss.
The letter was filled with bitter words of regret and longing.
After the breakup, she spoke in a bitter tone whenever his name was mentioned.
He remained bitter about the outcome of the trial for many years.
Their bitter feelings towards each other made collaboration impossible.
After the bitter debate, neither side was willing to compromise.
She spoke of their bitter rivalry that destroyed what friendship they had left.
The two companies have been engaged in a bitter legal battle for years.
