infernal

adjective
used as an intensifying expletive
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɝːnl̩ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɜːnl / (BrE)
(informal, usually before a noun) Used for emphasis to express annoyance, anger, or irritation.

Examples

Infernal noise
I wish that infernal machine would stop making so much noise.
She couldn't stand the infernal heat in the crowded room.
That infernal alarm woke me up again at 5 a.m.
