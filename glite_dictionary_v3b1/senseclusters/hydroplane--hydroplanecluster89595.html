hydroplane

noun
aircraft that operates on water
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdroʊˌpleɪn / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrəˌpleɪn / (BrE)
an aircraft designed to take off from and land on water

Examples

A hydroplane lands on water
Pilots need special training to safely operate a hydroplane in windy conditions.
The museum displayed a restored wooden hydroplane from the 1920s.
The old hydroplane gently landed on the surface of the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seaplane - an aircraft that can take off from and land on water
