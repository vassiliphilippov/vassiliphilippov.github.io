rocaille

noun
decorative garden rockwork
9.9
Pronunciation: / roʊˈkaɪ / (AmE), / rɒˈkaɪ / (BrE)
an artificial rockwork or decorative rockery in a garden, often adorned with shells, plants, or small statues

Examples

Garden rocaille feature
The landscape designer created a beautiful rocaille at the edge of the garden.
Visitors admired the intricate shells and ferns arranged in the rocaille near the fountain.
They added a small statue of a nymph to the rocaille to enhance its charm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grotto - a small cave or artificial structure in a garden
  • synonym: rockery - an area in a garden arranged with rocks and plants
