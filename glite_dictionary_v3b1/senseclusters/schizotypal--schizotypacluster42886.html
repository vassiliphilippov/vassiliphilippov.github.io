schizotypal

adjective
personality disorder traits
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌskɪtsəˈtaɪpəl / (AmE), / ˌskɪtsəˈtɪpəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a personality disorder marked by odd behavior, thinking, and difficulties in social relationships

Examples

Schizotypal personality disorder
The psychologist noted several schizotypal features in the patient's behavior and speech.
People with schizotypal personality disorder often have unusual beliefs or magical thinking.
Her schizotypal mannerisms made social interactions challenging for her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paranoid - relating to excessive suspicion or mistrust of others
  • synonym: schizoid - relating to a personality disorder with emotional detachment and limited social interaction
