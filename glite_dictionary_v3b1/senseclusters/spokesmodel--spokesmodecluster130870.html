spokesmodel

noun
public representative
5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈspoʊks.mə.dəl/ /; / /ˈspoʊks.mɑː.dəl/ (AmE), /ˈspəʊks.mə.dəl/ /; / /ˈspəʊks.mɒd.əl/ (BrE)
a person, often a model, hired to publicly represent and promote a brand, product, cause, or organization

Examples

A spokesmodel for a brand
The organization hired a well-known spokesmodel to increase public awareness of their campaign.
As a spokesmodel, she attended events and gave interviews to promote the charity's mission.
The new spokesmodel quickly became the recognizable face of the environmental movement.
The cosmetics company hired a well-known spokesmodel to appear in their advertisements.
As a spokesmodel, she attends events and promotes the brand's latest products.
He became the official spokesmodel for the new athletic wear campaign.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ambassador - a person who represents and promotes a particular activity or organization
  • synonym: brand ambassador - a person who promotes a brand and represents its image
  • synonym: spokesperson - a person who speaks on behalf of an organization or group
