family Anguillidae

noun
a family of migratory freshwater eels
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli æŋˌɡwɪˈlɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli æŋˌwɪˈlɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of eels that live in fresh water as adults but migrate to the sea to spawn
