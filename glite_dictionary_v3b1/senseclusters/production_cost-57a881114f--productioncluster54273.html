production cost

noun
costs to manufacture goods
1.0
Pronunciation: / prəˈdʌkʃən kɔst / (AmE), / prəˈdʌkʃən kɒst / (BrE)
the total cost of raw materials and labor required to manufacture goods

Examples

Total production cost
By reducing waste, the factory was able to lower its overall production cost.
Labor accounts for a significant portion of the total production cost in the automotive industry.
The rising production cost has forced many companies to increase the price of their products.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manufacturing cost - the expense incurred in manufacturing a product, often used interchangeably with production cost
