polo

noun
collared casual shirt
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊləʊ / (BrE)
a casual shirt with a collar, a few buttons at the neck, and usually short sleeves

Examples

A polo shirt
He wore a white polo to the company picnic because it looked neat but comfortable.
Many schools require students to wear a polo as part of their uniform.
She bought several colorful polos for her summer wardrobe.

Related Terms

  • antonym: T-shirt - a casual shirt without a collar or buttons
  • antonym: dress shirt - a formal shirt with a collar and full-length buttons
