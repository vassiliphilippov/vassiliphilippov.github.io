seasonless

adjective
without seasonal change
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːzənləs / (AmE), / ˈsiːzənləs / (BrE)
having little or no noticeable change in weather or conditions throughout the year

Examples

Seasonless climate
The tropical island is famous for its seasonless climate, making it a year-round destination.
Many plants thrive in a seasonless greenhouse where temperature and light remain constant.
Some people prefer living in seasonless regions to avoid extreme weather changes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: temperate - having mild temperatures with moderate seasonal changes
  • synonym: unchanging - not changing or varying
