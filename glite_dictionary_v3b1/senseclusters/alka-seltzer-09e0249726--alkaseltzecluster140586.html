Alka-Seltzer

noun
effervescent antacid and pain relief tablet
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈælkəˌsɛltzər / (AmE), / ˈalkəˌsɛltzə / (BrE)
a trademarked effervescent tablet containing an antacid and aspirin, used to relieve heartburn, upset stomach, and pain

Examples

An Alka-Seltzer tablet
After a heavy meal, she took an Alka-Seltzer to settle her stomach.
He dropped two Alka-Seltzer tablets into a glass of water and watched them fizz.
Some people use Alka-Seltzer for relief from headaches as well as indigestion.
