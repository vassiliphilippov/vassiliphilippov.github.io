viceroy

noun
governor ruling for a king or sovereign
4.8 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈvaɪ.sə.rɔɪ/ (AmE), /ˈvaɪ.sə.rɔɪ/ (BrE)
A governor of a country or province who rules as the representative of a king or sovereign.

Examples

British viceroy
After the rebellion, the new viceroy enacted sweeping administrative reforms.
During British rule, the viceroy of India lived in a grand palace in Delhi.
The Spanish crown appointed a viceroy to administer its colonies in the New World.
