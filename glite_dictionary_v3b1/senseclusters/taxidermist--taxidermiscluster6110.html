taxidermist

noun
person who stuffs and mounts animal skins
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtæksɪˌdɜrmɪst / (AmE), / ˈtaksɪˌdɜːmɪst / (BrE)
a person who practices taxidermy by preparing, stuffing, and mounting the skins of animals for display

Examples

A taxidermist mounts animals
As a professional taxidermist, she has preserved everything from fish to exotic birds.
Many hunters take their trophies to a skilled taxidermist for mounting.
The taxidermist spent hours carefully preparing the deer for the museum exhibit.
