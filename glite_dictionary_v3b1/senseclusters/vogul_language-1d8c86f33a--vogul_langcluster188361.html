Vogul language

noun
Ugric language of the Vogul (Mansi) people
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊɡəl ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈvəʊɡʊl ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Ugric language, related to Hungarian, traditionally spoken by the Vogul (Mansi) people of western Siberia.
