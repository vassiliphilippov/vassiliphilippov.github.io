consociationalism

noun
power-sharing system
9.9
Pronunciation: /kən.soʊ.sɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃən.əl.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /kən.səʊ.sɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃən.əl.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a political system or theory in which power is shared among different social, ethnic, or religious groups to maintain stability and fair representation

Examples
