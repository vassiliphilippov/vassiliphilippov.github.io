vapidly

adverb
in a dull or spiritless manner
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈvæpɪdli / (AmE), / ˈvapɪdli / (BrE)
In a dull, uninteresting, or lifeless manner; lacking in liveliness or spirit.

Examples

To smile vapidly
He smiled vapidly, showing no real interest in the conversation.
She spoke vapidly about the weather, offering little insight or enthusiasm.
The lecture was delivered so vapidly that many students struggled to stay awake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dully - In a dull or uninteresting manner
  • antonym: lively - In an animated or spirited manner
