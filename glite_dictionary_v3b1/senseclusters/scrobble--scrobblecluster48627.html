scrobble

noun
music play record
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɑbəl / (AmE), / ˈskrɒbəl / (BrE)
a record showing that a particular song or audio track has been played and logged by a music-tracking service

Examples

A scrobble count
Her profile showed a new scrobble for every song she played that day.
The app automatically creates a scrobble each time you finish listening to a track.
He checked his total scrobbles to see how many songs he had listened to this month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: listen - an instance of hearing a song or audio track
  • synonym: play count - the number of times a song has been played
