attachment

noun
file sent with an email
2.8
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈtætʃ.mənt/ (AmE), /ə.ˈtætʃ.mənt/ (BrE)
a file (for example a document, photo, or video) that is sent with an email or other electronic message.

Examples

Email attachment
She forwarded the attachment from the email as a PDF for review.
Please download the attachments before the meeting so you can follow the report.
He forgot to include the attachment, so the client did not receive the invoice.
