steric

adjective
spatial arrangement (chemistry)
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛrɪk / (AmE), / ˈstɪərɪk / (BrE)
relating to the spatial arrangement of atoms in a molecule and how this affects chemical reactions or properties

Examples

The steric effect
The steric effects of bulky groups can prevent certain chemical reactions from occurring.
Scientists study steric hindrance to understand why some molecules react more slowly than others.
The enzyme's activity was reduced due to steric clashes with the substrate.
