workfolk

noun
working class people
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkˌfoʊk / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌfəʊk / (BrE)
people who work, especially manual or industrial workers or employees at a place

  • synonym: workforce - all the people employed in a company or industry
