confessionary

adjective
self-revealing style
8.2
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɛʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / kənˈfɛʃənəri / (BrE)
expressed in a personal, candid, and self-revealing way, like a confession

Examples

Confessionary writing style
The author's confessionary memoir touched readers with its honesty and vulnerability.
Her poetry often takes a confessionary approach, sharing intimate details of her life.
He adopted a confessionary tone in his speech, admitting past mistakes to the audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reserved - not revealing one's thoughts or feelings easily
