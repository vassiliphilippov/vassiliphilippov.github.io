irritatedly

adverb
with annoyance
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrɪˌteɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ˈɪrɪteɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows annoyance or impatience

Examples

Answered irritatedly
He answered irritatedly when interrupted for the third time during the meeting.
The customer tapped her foot irritatedly while waiting in the long line.
She glanced irritatedly at her watch as the bus was late again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angrily - in a way that shows anger
  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful or relaxed way
