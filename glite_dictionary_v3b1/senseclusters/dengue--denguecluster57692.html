dengue

noun
mosquito-borne tropical viral disease
7.4 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈdeŋ.geɪ/ /; / /ˈdeŋ.giː/ (AmE), /ˈdeŋ.geɪ/ /; / /ˈdeŋ.giː/ (BrE)
a tropical infectious disease transmitted by mosquitoes, causing fever, rash, and severe pain in the head and joints

Examples

Severe dengue fever
After recovering from dengue, she experienced fatigue for several weeks.
Doctors warned that dengue cases were rising due to increased mosquito activity.
The city launched an awareness campaign to prevent the spread of dengue during the rainy season.
