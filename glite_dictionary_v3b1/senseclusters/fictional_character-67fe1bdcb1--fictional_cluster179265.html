fictional character

noun
imaginary person in fiction
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪkʃənl ˈkærəktɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɪkʃənl ˈkærəktə / (BrE)
A person invented by an author and represented in a work of fiction such as a novel, play, or film.

Examples

A fictional character in a story
Every fictional character in the film was inspired by real people.
Harry Potter is a fictional character created by J.K. Rowling.
The story features a fictional character who can talk to animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: character - a person in a novel, play, or film
