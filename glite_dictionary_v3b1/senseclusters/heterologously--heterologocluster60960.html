heterologously

adverb
from different species
9.9
Pronunciation: /he.tə.ˈrɑː.lə.dʒə.sliː/ /; / /he.tə.ˈrɔː.lə.dʒə.sliː/ (AmE), /he.tə.ˈrɒ.lə.dʒə.sliː/ (BrE)

Examples
