heterologously

adverb
from different species
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛtəˈrɑːləɡəsli / (AmE), / ˌhɛtəˈrɒləɡəsli / (BrE)
in a way that involves different species or sources, especially in scientific or biological contexts

Examples

To heterologously express genes
The gene was heterologously expressed in yeast cells to study its function.
Scientists often produce proteins heterologously to analyze their properties in a controlled environment.
The enzyme was heterologously produced in a different organism for medical research.

Related Terms

  • antonym: homologously - in a way that involves the same species or similar sources
