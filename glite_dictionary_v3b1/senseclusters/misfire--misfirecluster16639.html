misfire

noun
failed attempt to hit or succeed
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɪsˌfaɪə / (BrE)
an instance of failing to achieve the intended result, especially a failure to hit a target or succeed in an attempt

Examples

A plan misfire
Despite careful planning, their strategy was ultimately a misfire.
His attempt at humor was a complete misfire and nobody laughed.
The launch of the new product turned out to be a misfire, with disappointing sales.

Related Terms

  • synonym: failure - lack of success in doing or achieving something
  • antonym: success - the achievement of a goal or target
