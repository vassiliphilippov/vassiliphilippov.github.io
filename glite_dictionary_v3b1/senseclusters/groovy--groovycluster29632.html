groovy

adjective
excellent; very fashionable or attractive
Pronunciation: / ˈɡruvi /; / ˈɡruːvi / (AmE), / ˈɡruːvi / (BrE)
very fashionable, stylish, or excellent (informal, used to show strong approval)

Examples

A groovy style
I think your new apartment looks absolutely groovy!

That new song is really groovy and everyone is dancing to it.
She wore a groovy dress that turned heads at the party.
The designer’s groovy style was featured in the fashion magazine.
Those groovy new shoes really make a statement!
