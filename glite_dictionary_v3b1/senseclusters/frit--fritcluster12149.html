frit

noun
glassmaking material
9.3
Pronunciation: / frɪt / (AmE), / frɪt / (BrE)
a fused mixture of materials, often ground into powder, used in making glass or ceramics

Examples

Glass frit powder
The potter added frit to the clay to improve its strength and finish.
Manufacturers use frit as a key ingredient in producing colored glass.
The artist experimented with different types of frit to achieve unique textures in her ceramics.

  • synonym: raw material - basic material used to produce goods
