Synonyms

    synchronous describes things that match in timing or rhythm, while simultaneous means happening at exactly the same moment.
  • coincident
    5.6 0.02/m
    coincident means events meet or overlap in time, often by chance, while simultaneous means they occur exactly at the same time.
  • concurrent
    6.6 1.0/m
    concurrent describes things happening during the same period, often independently, while simultaneous stresses the exact same single moment.
  • co-occurrent
