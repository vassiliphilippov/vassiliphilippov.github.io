simultaneous

adjective
happening at the same time
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪməlˈteɪniəs / (AmE), / ˌsɪməlˈteɪnɪəs / (BrE)
happening or done at exactly the same time as something else

Examples

A simultaneous translation
A simultaneous translation was provided during the international conference.
The athletes began their runs in simultaneous fashion to ensure fairness.
The two concerts were simultaneous, making it impossible for me to attend both.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coincident - occurring at the same time; simultaneous
  • synonym: concurrent - happening or existing at the same time
  • antonym: successive - coming one after another; following in order
