twopence

noun
UK coin worth two pennies
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːˌpɛns / (AmE), / ˈtuːpəns / (BrE)
a coin of the United Kingdom worth two pennies, formerly made of silver and later of bronze as a decimal coin

Examples

A twopence coin
He found a twopence in the street on his way to school.
She added a twopence to the collection box at the museum.
The old twopence was made of silver before decimalisation.
