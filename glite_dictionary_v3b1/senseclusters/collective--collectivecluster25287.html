collective

noun
cooperative group or organization
5.0 3.5
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈlek.tɪv/ (AmE), /kə.ˈlek.tɪv/ (BrE)

Examples

The artist collective organized a public mural project in the city’s downtown area.
