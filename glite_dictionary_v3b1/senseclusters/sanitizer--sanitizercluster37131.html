sanitizer

noun
germ-killing product
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsænəˌtaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsænɪtaɪzə / (BrE)
a substance or product used to kill germs and reduce the spread of bacteria or viruses on hands or surfaces

Examples

Hand sanitizer
During flu season, many people use sanitizer to keep their hands clean when soap is not available.
The school placed bottles of sanitizer at every entrance for students and staff.
He wiped down the table with a sanitizer before starting his work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleaner - a substance used to remove dirt or impurities
  • synonym: disinfectant - a chemical used to destroy harmful microorganisms
