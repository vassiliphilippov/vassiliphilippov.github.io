ejectable

adjective
can be ejected
Pronunciation: / ɪˈdʒɛktəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒɛktəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be ejected or designed to be released from something, especially for safety or emergency purposes

Examples

An ejectable seat
The fighter jet is equipped with an ejectable seat for pilot safety.
This device has an ejectable battery that can be replaced easily.
In case of malfunction, the capsule is ejectable from the main spacecraft.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixed - firmly attached and not able to be removed or ejected
  • synonym: removable - able to be taken off or separated from something
