separator

noun
device separating particles by centrifugal force
1.0 4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsep.ə.reɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈsep.ə.reɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a device that uses centrifugal force to separate different components, especially solid particles from a liquid suspension

Examples

Milk separator
A dairy separator is commonly used to separate cream from milk.
The laboratory technician placed the test tubes in the separator to extract the plasma.
This industrial separator can process hundreds of liters of liquid suspension per hour.
