beehive

noun
conical piled-up hairstyle
Pronunciation: / ˈbiˌhaɪv / (AmE), / ˈbiːˌhʌɪv / (BrE)
a women's hairstyle in which the hair is piled high in a conical shape, resembling a beehive

Examples

Beehive hairstyle
Amy Winehouse often wore her signature beehive hairstyle on stage.
She was famous for her towering beehive that reached almost a foot above her head.
The retro beehive made a comeback at the fashion show last night.
