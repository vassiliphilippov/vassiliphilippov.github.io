Pamiri

noun
person from Pamir region
Pronunciation: / pɑːˈmiːri / (AmE), / pəˈmɪəri / (BrE)
a person who comes from the Pamir region or belongs to one of the Pamiri ethnic groups

Examples

A Pamiri person
The Pamiri explained the traditions of his community to the visitors.
Many Pamiris speak several languages, including their own local dialects.
A Pamiri family invited us to share a traditional meal in their mountain home.
