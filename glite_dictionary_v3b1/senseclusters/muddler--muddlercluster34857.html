muddler

noun
inept or confused person
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌd.lər/ (AmE), /ˈmʌd.lə/ (BrE)
a person who is confused or inept and makes situations more complicated or less effective

Examples

A careless muddler
He was a notorious muddler who often made simple tasks unnecessarily complicated.
Instead of solving the problem, the muddler only added to the confusion.
The team struggled because their leader was a muddler with no clear plan.
