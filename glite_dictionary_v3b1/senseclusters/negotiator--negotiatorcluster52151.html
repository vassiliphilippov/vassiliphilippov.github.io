negotiator

noun
person who negotiates agreements
Pronunciation: / nəˈɡoʊʃiˌeɪtər / (AmE), / nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪtə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in formal discussions or bargaining in order to reach an agreement or settlement

Examples

Skilled negotiator
As a professional negotiator, he is often called upon to resolve complex disputes.
She proved herself to be a skilled negotiator during the international peace talks.
The company's chief negotiator worked tirelessly to secure a fair contract for the employees.
