pressroom

noun
journalists' workroom
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛsˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈprɛsˌruːm / (BrE)
a room in a building where journalists work, gather information, or prepare news reports

Examples

Busy pressroom
The mayor held a press conference in the pressroom after the city council meeting.
Journalists hurried into the pressroom to file their stories before the deadline.
The stadium's pressroom was filled with reporters covering the championship game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: newsroom - a room where news is prepared and edited
