gridding

verb
to align using grid
5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈgrɪ.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈgrɪ.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
to arrange or align visual elements using a grid to ensure consistent placement and proportion

Examples

Gridding a blueprint
The architect spent hours gridding the blueprint to ensure every room was perfectly aligned.
By carefully gridding the poster, the designer achieved a balanced and professional look.
Teachers sometimes use chalk lines for gridding the board before writing important notes.

  • synonym: organize - to arrange or order things systematically
