sappiness

noun
excessive sentimentality
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsæpinəs / (AmE), / ˈsæpinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being overly sentimental or emotional, often in a way that seems excessive or insincere

Examples

Movie sappiness
The film's sappiness made some viewers roll their eyes, while others found it touching.
He tried to avoid sappiness in his speech, keeping his words simple and sincere.
Some people enjoy the sappiness of romantic comedies, while others find it overwhelming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: melodrama - exaggerated emotional behavior or sentiment
  • antonym: realism - the quality of representing things as they are, without exaggeration
  • synonym: sentimentality - the quality of being overly emotional or tender
