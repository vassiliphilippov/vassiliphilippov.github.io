embargo

noun
government trade restriction

an official government order that restricts or prohibits trade with a particular country or the exchange of specific goods

Examples

A trade embargo
Due to the embargo, many factories faced shortages of essential materials.
The United Nations voted to lift the oil embargo after the peace agreement was signed.
The government imposed an embargo on all arms shipments to the country.

Synonyms

  • trade embargo
    5.9 0.48/m
    trade embargo is a government ban on trade with a particular country, while embargo is a general official order that restricts or prohibits trade.
  • trade stoppage
    7.0 0.00/m
    trade stoppage is an official order that halts or limits trade to enforce policy, while embargo is a targeted government ban on trade.
  • interdiction
    8.9 1.29/m
    interdiction is a formal order that forbids actions by law, while embargo is a government order that specifically targets trade or goods.
  • ban
    1.2 17/m
    ban is an official rule that forbids something in general, while embargo is a specific government order stopping trade with a country.
  • prohibition
    4.4 8.48/m
    prohibition is the formal act of legally forbidding something, while embargo is a government order that blocks trade or specific goods.

  • synonym: boycott - an act of refusing to do business with a country, organization, or person
