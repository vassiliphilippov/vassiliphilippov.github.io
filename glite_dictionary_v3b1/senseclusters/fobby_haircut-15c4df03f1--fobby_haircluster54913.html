fobby haircut

noun
immigrant-associated hairstyle
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːbi ˈhɛrˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈfɒbi ˈheəkʌt / (BrE)
A hairstyle, often considered unfashionable or outdated in mainstream society, commonly associated with recent immigrants or those perceived as retaining non-Western cultural influences.

Examples

A fobby haircut
He was self-conscious about his fobby haircut on the first day of school.
Some people embraced their fobby haircut as a sign of pride in their roots.
The old yearbook photo showed her with a classic fobby haircut.
