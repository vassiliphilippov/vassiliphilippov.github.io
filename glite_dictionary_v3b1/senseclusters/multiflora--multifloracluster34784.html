multiflora

noun
a rose with clusters of small flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌl.tɪˈflɔr.ə / (AmE), / ˌmʌl.tɪˈflɔː.rə / (BrE)
A vigorous rose plant that produces clusters of many small flowers, often used for hedges or as a rootstock for grafting.

Examples

A multiflora rose
Many species of multiflora are valued for their abundant blossoms in spring.
The gardener planted a multiflora hedge along the back fence for added privacy.
This variety of multiflora rose can be used as a rootstock for grafting other roses.
