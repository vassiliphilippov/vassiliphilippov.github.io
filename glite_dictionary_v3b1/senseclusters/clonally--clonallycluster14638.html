clonally

adverb
by cloning
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊnəli / (AmE), / ˈkləʊnəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or results from cloning, especially producing identical copies of an organism or cell

Examples

To clone plants clonally
The scientists propagated the plants clonally to maintain their genetic traits.
Some bacteria reproduce clonally, resulting in identical offspring.
The vineyard's grapevines were selected and grown clonally for consistent wine quality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asexually - in a way that does not involve sexual reproduction
