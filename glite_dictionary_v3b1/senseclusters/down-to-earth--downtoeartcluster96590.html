down-to-earth

adjective
practical and sensible
Pronunciation: / ˌdaʊn.tuˈɝθ /; / ˌdaʊn tə ˈɜrθ / (AmE), / ˌdaʊn.tuˈɜːθ /; / ˌdaʊn tʊ ˈɜːθ / (BrE)
practical, sensible, and not pretentious; realistic in manner or attitude

Examples

Related Terms

  • antonym: impractical - not sensible or realistic
  • synonym: practical - concerned with actual use or practice
  • antonym: pretentious - attempting to impress by affecting greater importance or merit than actually possessed
  • synonym: realistic - having or showing a sensible and practical idea of what can be achieved or expected
