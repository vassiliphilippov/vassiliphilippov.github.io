elliptically

adverb
in an oval path
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɪptɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪptɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that follows the shape or path of an ellipse or oval.

Examples

Moves elliptically
The planet orbits the sun elliptically, coming closer at some points than others.
The racetrack was designed elliptically to allow for longer straight sections.
The comet moved elliptically through the solar system, following a stretched oval path.

Related Terms

  • antonym: circularly - in a circular manner or path
  • synonym: ovally - in the shape or manner of an oval
