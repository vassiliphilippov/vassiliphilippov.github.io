colonic

adjective
relating to the colon (large intestine)
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɑnɪk / (AmE), / kəˈlɒnɪk / (BrE)
of or relating to the colon, the part of the large intestine extending from the cecum to the rectum.

Examples

Colonic health
A diet high in fiber can help maintain colonic health.
Researchers are studying the effects of probiotics on colonic bacteria.
The doctor noted that the patient had a colonic obstruction requiring immediate attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colon - the main part of the large intestine
