pericardiocentesis

noun
removal of heart sac fluid
9.0
Pronunciation: /pə.rɪ.kɑːr.diː.oʊ.sen.ˈtiː.sɪs/ /; / /pe.rɪ.kɑːr.dɪ.oʊ.sen.ˈtiː.sɪs/ (AmE), /pə.rɪ.kɑː.dɪ.əʊ.sen.ˈtiː.sɪs/ /; / /pe.rɪ.kɑː.dɪ.əʊ.sen.ˈtiː.sɪs/ (BrE)
