nickel

noun
five dollars worth of a drug (slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈnɪkəl / (BrE)
(slang) Five dollars worth of an illicit drug, especially when referring to a small bag or quantity sold for that amount.

Examples

A nickel bag
Back in the day, you could get a nickel of heroin for just five bucks.
He bought a nickel of weed from his friend behind the school.
The dealer only had nickel bags available for sale last night.
