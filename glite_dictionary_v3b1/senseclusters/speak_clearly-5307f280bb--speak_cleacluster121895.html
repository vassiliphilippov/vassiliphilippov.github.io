speak clearly

verb
articulate words to be easily understood
1.0
Pronunciation: / spiːk ˈklɪrli / (AmE), / spiːk ˈklɪəli / (BrE)
to articulate words in a way that is easy to understand; to enunciate clearly when speaking

Examples

To speak clearly in class
During the presentation, please speak clearly so everyone can follow your points.
He learned to speak clearly after years of practicing public speaking.
My teacher always reminds us to speak clearly during oral exams.
