air conditioning

noun
system for cooling and dehumidifying air
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /er kən.ˈdɪʃ.ən.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /eə kən.ˈdɪʃ.ən.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
