air conditioning

noun
system for cooling and dehumidifying air
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr kənˌdɪʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈeə kənˌdɪʃənɪŋ / (BrE)
a system for controlling the temperature, humidity, and ventilation of indoor air, usually to keep it cool and comfortable
