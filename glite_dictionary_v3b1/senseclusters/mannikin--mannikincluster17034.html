mannikin

noun
female fashion model
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.ɪ.kɪn / (AmE), / ˈmæn.ɪ.kɪn / (BrE)
a woman who models clothes for display in fashion shows or advertisements

Examples

Fashion mannikin
Casting directors are searching for a new mannikin for their upcoming fall fashion campaign.
She dreamed of becoming a famous mannikin in Paris one day.
The mannikin strutted confidently down the runway, showing off the designer's latest collection.
