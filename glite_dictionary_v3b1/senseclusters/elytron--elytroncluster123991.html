elytron

noun
hardened forewing of beetles/insects
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlɪˌtrɑn / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪˌtrɒn / (BrE)
either of the hardened, protective forewings of beetles and some other insects that cover the functional hind wings

Examples

Beetle elytron wing
Beetles protect their delicate hind wings beneath their hardened elytra.
The bright red elytra of ladybirds make them easy to identify in a garden.
When threatened, the beetle quickly closes its elytra over its body for defense.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wing case - a hardened covering that protects the wings of some beetles and insects
