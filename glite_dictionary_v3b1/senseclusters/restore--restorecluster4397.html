restore

verb
to give back or return to original state
2.5
Pronunciation: / rɪˈstɔr / (AmE), / rɪˈstɔː / (BrE)
to give back something that was lost, stolen, or taken to its original owner; to bring back to a former condition or position

Examples

To restore lost property

Please restore all borrowed books to the library by next week.
The museum will restore the ancient sculpture to its rightful place.
