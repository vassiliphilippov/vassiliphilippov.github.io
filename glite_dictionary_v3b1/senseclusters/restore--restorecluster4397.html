Examples

To restore lost property
After years apart, the children were restored to their family.

Synonyms

  • return
    1.0 47/m
    return means giving something back to its owner or place, while restore often adds fixing or returning to a former condition.
  • give back
    1.0 14/m
    give back is a casual way to return an item to its owner after use, while restore often means returning to a former condition.
  • restitute
    8.8 0.01/m
    restitute is a formal legal word for returning property or rights to the proper owner, while restore is more general and can mean repair.
  • bring back
    1.7 11/m
    bring back means to take or cause something to return to its starting place, while restore stresses returning to a former state or owner.
