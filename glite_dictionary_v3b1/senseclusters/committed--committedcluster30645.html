committed

adjective
dedicated or pledged to a cause or belief
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɪt̬ɪd / (AmE), / kəˈmɪtɪd / (BrE)
feeling or showing dedication to a cause, activity, or belief; pledged or bound to a particular course or group

Examples

Committed supporter
As a committed teacher, he always puts his students' needs first.
Only truly committed members attend every meeting, rain or shine.
She is a committed environmentalist who works tirelessly for conservation causes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dedicated - devoted to a task or purpose
  • antonym: detached - not feeling or showing emotional involvement
