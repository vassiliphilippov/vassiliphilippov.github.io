unthreateningly

adverb
without seeming dangerous
5.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθrɛtənɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈθrɛtənɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that does not seem dangerous or likely to cause harm

Examples

To act unthreateningly
The cat approached the children unthreateningly, making them feel at ease.
He smiled unthreateningly to show he meant no harm.
The stranger waved unthreateningly from across the street to greet his neighbor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmlessly - in a way that causes no harm
  • antonym: menacingly - in a way that seems threatening or dangerous
