assuringly

adverb
with confidence or comfort
Pronunciation: / əˈʃʊrɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈʃʊərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that gives confidence or comfort to someone

Examples

Speak assuringly
He spoke assuringly to help his friend feel better about the situation.
The doctor nodded assuringly before explaining the simple procedure.
She placed her hand assuringly on his shoulder to show her support.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anxiously - in a way that shows worry or nervousness
  • synonym: calmly - in a peaceful and relaxed way
  • synonym: reassuringly - in a way that removes doubt or fear
