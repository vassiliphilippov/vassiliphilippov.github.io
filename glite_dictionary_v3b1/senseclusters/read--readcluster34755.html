read

verb
criticize or insult sharply (slang)
Pronunciation: / rid / (AmE), / riːd / (BrE)
(slang) to criticize, insult, or rebuke someone sharply, often by pointing out their faults in a witty or harsh way.

Examples

To read someone harshly
She read him for filth after he lied about the numbers.
Comedians often read each other playfully during roast nights at the club.
Everyone was shocked when the critic read the performer in front of the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insult - to say or do something that offends or shows a lack of respect for someone
