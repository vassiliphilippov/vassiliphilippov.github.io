circa

adverb
approximately; about
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrkə / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkə / (BrE)
used to indicate that a number, date, or amount is approximate or not exact

Examples

Circa 1880
The painting was created circa 1880, though the exact year is unknown.
The population of the city was circa 500,000 at the start of the decade.
The manuscript dates to circa the twelfth century, according to historians.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approximately - used to show that a number or amount is not exact
  • antonym: exactly - in a precise or accurate way
