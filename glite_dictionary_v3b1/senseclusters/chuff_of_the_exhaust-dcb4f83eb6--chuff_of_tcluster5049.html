chuff of the exhaust

noun
sound made by an engine's exhaust
Pronunciation: / tʃʌf əv ði ɪɡˈzɔst / (AmE), / tʃʌf əv ði ɪɡˈzɔːst / (BrE)
a short, muffled sound produced by an engine's exhaust, often heard as a repetitive or rhythmic noise

Examples

Chuff of the exhaust sound
Only the chuff of the exhaust broke the morning silence as the boat started up.
She could tell the train was getting closer by the distinctive chuff of the exhaust in the distance.
The old tractor made a steady chuff of the exhaust as it idled in the yard.
