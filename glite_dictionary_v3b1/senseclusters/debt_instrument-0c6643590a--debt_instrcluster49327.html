debt instrument

noun
document acknowledging a loan
6.0

a formal document or digital record that shows someone owes money and explains how and when it will be paid back (such as a bond or a token on a blockchain)

Related Terms

  • synonym: bond - a debt security in which an investor loans money to an entity for a defined period at fixed interest
  • synonym: promissory note - a written promise to pay a specified sum of money to a certain person at a future date
  • synonym: security token - a digital token that represents ownership of an underlying financial asset and is issued on a blockchain
