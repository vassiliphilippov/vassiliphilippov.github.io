fire

noun
the act of shooting weapons
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
the act of shooting weapons or artillery, especially towards an enemy

Examples

Open fire
The ceasefire ended, and both sides resumed fire across the border.
The soldiers immediately opened fire when they spotted the enemy.
We were caught in heavy fire as we crossed the open field.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ceasefire - an agreement to stop shooting or fighting
  • synonym: gunfire - firing of guns or repeated shooting
