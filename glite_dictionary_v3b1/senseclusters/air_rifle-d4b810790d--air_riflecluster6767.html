air rifle

noun
gun powered by compressed air
1.5 3.1
Pronunciation: /er ˈraɪ.fəl/ (AmE), /eə ˈraɪ.fəl/ (BrE)
a gun that uses compressed air or gas to fire pellets or other projectiles, rather than gunpowder

Examples

An air rifle gun
He practiced shooting cans in the backyard with his air rifle.
Strict safety measures are required at the air rifle range.
The air rifle is a popular choice for beginners learning marksmanship.
