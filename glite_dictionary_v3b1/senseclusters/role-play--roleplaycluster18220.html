role-play

noun
acting exercise
3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊl.pleɪ/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊl.pleɪ/ (BrE)
an activity in which people act out characters or situations for practice, learning, or entertainment

Examples

A role-play activity
The language class included a role-play where students pretended to be at a hotel reception.
During the workshop, participants engaged in role-play to practice handling difficult customers.
Teachers often use role-play to help students develop communication skills in real-life scenarios.

Related Terms

  • synonym: improvisation - the act of creating or performing spontaneously without preparation
  • synonym: simulation - an activity that imitates a real situation for training or practice
