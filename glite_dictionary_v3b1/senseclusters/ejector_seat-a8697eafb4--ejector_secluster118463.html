ejector seat

noun
emergency pilot seat ejected from aircraft
Pronunciation: / ɪˈdʒɛktər siːt / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒɛktə siːt / (BrE)
a seat in an aircraft, especially a military jet, designed to be ejected with its occupant in an emergency, enabling the pilot to parachute to safety

Examples

The ejector seat activates
Instructions for operating the ejector seat are clearly marked in the cockpit.
The fighter jet was equipped with an ejector seat in case of emergencies.
The pilot survived thanks to his quick use of the ejector seat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ejection seat - a seat designed to be ejected from an aircraft
