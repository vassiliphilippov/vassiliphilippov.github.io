khan

noun
inn with courtyard for caravans
Pronunciation: / kɑn / (AmE), / kɑːn / (BrE)
(in some eastern countries) an inn with a large courtyard, providing accommodation for caravans and travelers.

Examples

A busy khan
Merchants and their camels filled the khan every evening during the trading season.
The ancient khan stood at the heart of the bustling bazaar, offering shelter to all.
The weary travelers decided to rest at a khan along the dusty road.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caravanserai - an inn with a central courtyard for travellers in Asia or North Africa
