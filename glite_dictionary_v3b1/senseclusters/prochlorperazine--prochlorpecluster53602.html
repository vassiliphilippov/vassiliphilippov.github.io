prochlorperazine

noun
antipsychotic and antiemetic drug
9.4
Pronunciation: / proʊˌklɔːrˈpɛrəˌziːn / (AmE), / prəʊˌklɔːpəˈræziːn / (BrE)
A drug used as an antipsychotic and antiemetic, particularly for treating schizophrenia, nausea, and vomiting.

Examples

Prochlorperazine medicine
After taking prochlorperazine, her symptoms of dizziness and vomiting improved significantly.
Patients with schizophrenia may be treated with antipsychotic medications such as prochlorperazine.
The doctor prescribed prochlorperazine to help control his severe nausea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chlorpromazine - another phenothiazine antipsychotic drug
  • synonym: haloperidol - another antipsychotic drug used to treat schizophrenia
