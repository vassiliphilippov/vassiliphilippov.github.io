TTY

noun
teletypewriter device
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtiː ˈtiː ˈwaɪ / (AmE), / ˈtiː ˈtiː ˈwaɪ / (BrE)
an electromechanical teletypewriter used to send and receive typed messages over telegraph or telephone lines, especially before computers

Examples

Old TTY machine
The old newsroom still had a TTY in the corner for receiving urgent bulletins.
Before email, reporters often relied on the TTY to get breaking news updates.
A TTY was once essential for sending messages over long distances quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: telegraph - a system for transmitting messages over long distances using wires
  • synonym: teletype - a machine for sending and receiving typed messages over wires
