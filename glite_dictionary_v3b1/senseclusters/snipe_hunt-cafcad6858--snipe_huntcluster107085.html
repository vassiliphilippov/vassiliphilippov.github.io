snipe hunt

noun
elaborate practical joke involving a fake hunt
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsnaɪp ˌhʌnt / (AmE), / ˈsnaɪp ˌhʌnt / (BrE)
a practical joke or prank where someone is sent on a pointless or impossible search for something that does not exist
