Ziploc

noun
resealable plastic bag
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪpˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈzɪpˌlɒk / (BrE)
a resealable plastic bag, typically used for storing food or small items, often used generically for any brand

Examples

A Ziploc bag
She packed her sandwich in a Ziploc to keep it fresh for lunch.
After sorting the puzzle pieces, he stored them in a large Ziploc.
Travelers often use a Ziploc to organize toiletries in their luggage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: container - an object used for holding or storing things
  • synonym: plastic bag - a bag made of thin, flexible plastic
