glume

noun
small bract in grasses and sedges
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡlum / (AmE), / ɡluːm / (BrE)
a small, dry, membranous bract found at the base of the grass spikelet in plants of the grass and sedge families

Examples

Small glumes on grass
Certain species of grass can be identified by the size and shape of their glumes.
Each spikelet of the wheat plant is protected by a pair of glumes.
The botanist carefully removed the glume to examine the seed within.
