rupiah

noun
Indonesian currency unit
7.5
Pronunciation: / ruːˈpiːə / (AmE), / ruːˈpiːə / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Indonesia, equal to 100 sen

Examples

The rupiah currency
She exchanged her dollars for rupiah before traveling to Bali.
The price of coffee in Jakarta is listed in rupiah rather than U.S. dollars.
The souvenir cost 25,000 rupiah at the market.
