incrementally

adverb
gradually, in small steps
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪŋkrəˈmɛntəli / (AmE), / ˌɪŋkrɪˈmɛntəli / (BrE)
in a way that increases or progresses gradually by small amounts

Examples

To increase incrementally
The company increased its production capacity incrementally to meet the growing demand.
She improved her language skills incrementally by practicing a little every day.
The software is updated incrementally to add new features without major changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gradually - slowly over time, not suddenly
  • antonym: suddenly - quickly and unexpectedly
