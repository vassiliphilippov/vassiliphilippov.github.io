romantic getaway

noun
trip or holiday for romance
Pronunciation: / ɹoʊˈmæntɪk ˈɡɛtəˌweɪ / (AmE), / rəʊˈmæntɪk ˈɡɛtəweɪ / (BrE)
A holiday or short trip taken by a couple with the intention of spending quality romantic time together.

Examples

A romantic getaway trip
A weekend romantic getaway can help couples reconnect and escape daily routines.
Many hotels offer special packages for a romantic getaway including candles, champagne, and spa treatments.
They booked a romantic getaway to celebrate their anniversary on a quiet island.

Related Terms

  • synonym: honeymoon - a holiday taken by newlyweds immediately after their wedding
