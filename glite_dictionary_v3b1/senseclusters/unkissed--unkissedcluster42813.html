unkissed

adjective
untouched; unaffected
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɪst / (BrE)
not touched, affected, or marked by something, often used poetically to mean untouched by a particular force or experience.

Examples

Unkissed by frost
The garden remained unkissed by the morning frost, its flowers still vibrant and fresh.
She felt as if her dreams were unkissed by disappointment, full of hope and possibility.
The remote beach was unkissed by tourists, leaving the sand smooth and undisturbed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: marked - affected or changed by something
  • synonym: pristine - in its original, unspoiled condition
  • synonym: untouched - not affected or changed by something
