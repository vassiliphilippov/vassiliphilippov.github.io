epicness

noun
quality of being grand or heroic
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛpɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪknəs / (BrE)
The quality of being impressive, grand, or heroic in scale or character.

Examples

The epicness of victory
Fans celebrated the epicness of the team's comeback victory.
She admired the epicness of the mountain landscape surrounding them.
The epicness of the battle scene left the audience in awe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grandeur - the quality of being grand or magnificent
  • antonym: humdrum - lacking excitement or variety; dull
