poorish

adjective
somewhat poor
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊrɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːrɪʃ / (BrE)
somewhat poor in quality or condition; fairly bad or below the usual standard

Examples

Poorish weather
The weather was poorish for our picnic, with clouds and a chilly breeze.
His performance in the exam was poorish, but he still managed to pass.
We stayed at a poorish hotel that needed some renovation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: good - of high quality or standard
  • synonym: mediocre - of only average or moderate quality; not very good
  • degree higher: poor - of low quality or standard
