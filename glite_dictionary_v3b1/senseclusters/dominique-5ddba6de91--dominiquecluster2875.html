Dominique

noun
American breed of barred grey chicken
Pronunciation: / dəˈmiːniˌk / (AmE), / dəˈmiːniːk / (BrE)
an American breed of chicken with barred grey feathers, raised for meat and brown eggs

Examples

Dominique chicken breed
Many farmers prefer Dominique chickens because of their hardiness and productivity.
The Dominique is easily recognized by its striking barred plumage.
We added two Dominiques to our backyard flock for their tasty eggs and calm nature.
