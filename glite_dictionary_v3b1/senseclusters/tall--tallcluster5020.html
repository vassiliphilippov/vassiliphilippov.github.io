tall

adjective
of greater than average height
1.0
Pronunciation: / tɔl / (AmE), / tɔːl / (BrE)
having a greater than average height; high in vertical dimension or stature.

Examples

A tall building
Several tall skyscrapers dominate the city skyline.
That oak tree is the tallest in the park.
The tall basketball player easily reached the rim for a slam dunk.
