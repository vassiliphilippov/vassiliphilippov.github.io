ahead of schedule

Examples

Finished ahead of schedule

Synonyms

  • ahead of time
    2.3 4.70/m
    ahead of time means happening earlier than planned in general, while ahead of schedule specifically means finishing before the planned deadline.
  • in advance
    1.8 18/m
    in advance is used for doing or arranging something before it happens, while ahead of schedule says a task is finished earlier than expected.
