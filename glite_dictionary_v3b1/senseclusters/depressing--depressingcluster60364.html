depressing

adjective
making you feel sad or hopeless
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈprɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈprɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
making you feel very sad and without hope; causing feelings of gloom or inadequacy.

Examples

A depressing story
Her story about losing her job was truly depressing to hear.
I find that empty house incredibly depressing to walk past every day.
The constant rain made the whole weekend depressing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - happy and showing positive feelings
  • synonym: sad - unhappy or showing unhappiness
  • antonym: uplifting - making someone feel happier or more hopeful
