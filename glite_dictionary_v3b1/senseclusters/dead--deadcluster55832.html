dead

adjective
lacking physical sensation; numb
7.2 3.3
Pronunciation: /ded/ (AmE), /ded/ (BrE)
having no feeling or response; numb or emotionally unresponsive

Examples

Dead arm
After the injection, her arm felt completely dead and she couldn't move her fingers.
His foot had gone dead from sitting cross-legged for too long.
The dentist waited until the area was dead before starting the procedure.
After years in the job, he had grown dead to the suffering around him.
She seemed completely dead to criticism from her colleagues.
They were dead to the children's cries and focused only on their phones.

  • synonym: insensitive - lacking sensitivity to the feelings of others
  • synonym: numb - unable to feel sensation; lacking physical feeling
  • antonym: sensitive - quick to respond to physical stimuli; able to feel sensations
