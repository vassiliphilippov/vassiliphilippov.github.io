reroute

noun
alternative route
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈraʊt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈruːt / (BrE)
an act or instance of directing something or someone along a different route than originally planned

Examples

A bus reroute
Due to construction, the city announced a reroute for all downtown buses.
The marathon organizers planned a reroute to avoid the flooded streets.
Drivers followed the posted reroute signs to reach the highway safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detour - a temporary route taken instead of the usual one
  • degree lower: shortcut - a quicker or more direct route than the usual one
