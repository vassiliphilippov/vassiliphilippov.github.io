farolito

noun
paper holiday lantern
Pronunciation: / ˌfɑːroʊˈlitoʊ / (AmE), / ˌfærəʊˈliːtəʊ / (BrE)
a small paper lantern, often used in Southwestern US holiday celebrations, especially during Christmas

Examples

A farolito lantern
During the Christmas season, families place farolitos along their rooftops and walkways.
The soft glow of each farolito created a magical atmosphere at the festival.
Children helped light the farolitos before the holiday parade began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lantern - a portable light with a protective enclosure
  • synonym: luminaria - a traditional Southwestern US holiday lantern, often made from a paper bag with a candle
