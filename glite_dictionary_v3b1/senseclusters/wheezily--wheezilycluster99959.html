wheezily

adverb
in a wheezing manner
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːzɪli / (AmE), / ˈwiːzɪli / (BrE)
In a manner characterized by a wheeze; with a wheezing sound or quality.

Examples

To breathe wheezily
After climbing the stairs, he breathed wheezily and clutched his chest.
She read the story wheezily, struggling with each word due to her cold.
The old man laughed wheezily, his voice raspy and strained.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wheezy - making a wheezing sound
