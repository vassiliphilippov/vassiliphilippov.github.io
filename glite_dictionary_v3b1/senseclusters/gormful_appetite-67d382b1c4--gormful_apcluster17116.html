gormful appetite

noun
large or insatiable appetite
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔːrmfəl ˈæpəˌtaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːmfʊl əˈpɛtɪt / (BrE)
an exceptionally large or insatiable appetite for food or drink

Examples

A gormful appetite
Despite his slim build, Mark always had a gormful appetite at family gatherings.
Her gormful appetite was matched only by her love of good conversation.
The chef prepared extra portions, knowing the critics were known for their gormful appetites.

  • synonym: voracious appetite - an intensely eager or insatiable desire for food
