ace

verb
to score a hole in one (in golf)
9.8
Pronunciation: / eɪs / (AmE), / eɪs / (BrE)
to score a point directly with a single action in sports, such as serving in tennis or hitting a hole in one in golf

Examples

To ace a serve

He aced the serve and won the match.
She managed to ace her opponent in the final game with a powerful serve.

If you ace your rival on match point, you win in spectacular style.
She managed to ace her opponent with three powerful serves in a row.
