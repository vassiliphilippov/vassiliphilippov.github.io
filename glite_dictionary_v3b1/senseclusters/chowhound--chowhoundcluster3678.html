chowhound

noun
enthusiastic eater
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaʊˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈtʃaʊˌhaʊnd / (BrE)
a person who loves eating, especially in large quantities or with great enthusiasm

Examples

A chowhound loves food
My brother is a real chowhound and never misses a chance to try new dishes.
At the party, the chowhound quickly found his way to the snack table.
If you are a chowhound, this all-you-can-eat restaurant is perfect for you.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foodie - a person with a particular interest in food
  • synonym: glutton - a person who eats or drinks excessively
