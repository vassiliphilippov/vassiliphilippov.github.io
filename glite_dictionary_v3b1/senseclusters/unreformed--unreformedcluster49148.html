unreformed

adjective
not affected by the Reformation (historical/religious)
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈfɔrmd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈfɔːmd / (BrE)
Not changed or influenced by the religious reforms of the Reformation period.

Examples

Unreformed church
He grew up in an unreformed parish that kept its pre-Reformation rituals.
Several unreformed monasteries continued their traditional practices well into the modern era.
The Catholic Church in Ireland remained largely unreformed throughout the sixteenth century.
