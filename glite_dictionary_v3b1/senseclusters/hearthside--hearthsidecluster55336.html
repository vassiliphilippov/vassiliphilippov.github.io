hearthside

noun
space beside fireplace
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrθˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːθˌsaɪd / (BrE)
the area beside a fireplace, or the warm, comfortable atmosphere and activities of home life, especially around the fireplace

Examples

Hearthside comfort
The family spent the evening talking and laughing by the hearthside.
On winter nights, she liked to read a book at the hearthside.
Children gathered at the hearthside to listen to their grandfather's stories.
After a long day, she enjoyed relaxing by the hearthside with her family.
The old cottage was filled with laughter and warmth at the hearthside each evening.
He missed the simple joys of hearthside gatherings during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fireside - the area near a fireplace, often associated with warmth and comfort
  • synonym: home - the place where one lives, especially with family
