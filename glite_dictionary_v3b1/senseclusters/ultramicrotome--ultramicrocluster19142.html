ultramicrotome

noun
device for thin slicing
9.9
Pronunciation: /ʌl.trə.ˈmaɪ.krə.toʊm/ /; / /ˈʌl.trə.maɪ.krə.toʊm/ (AmE), /ʌl.trə.ˈmaɪ.krə.təʊm/ /; / /ˈʌl.trə.maɪ.krə.təʊm/ (BrE)
a device used to cut extremely thin slices of material for examination under a microscope, especially in electron microscopy

Examples

An ultramicrotome device

An ultramicrotome allows scientists to obtain slices thin enough for detailed cellular analysis.
