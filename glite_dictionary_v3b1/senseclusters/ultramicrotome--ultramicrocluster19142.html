ultramicrotome

noun
device for thin slicing
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrəˈmaɪkrəˌtoʊm / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈmaɪkrəˌtəʊm / (BrE)
a device used to cut extremely thin slices of material for examination under a microscope, especially in electron microscopy

Examples

An ultramicrotome device
The technician used an ultramicrotome to prepare tissue samples for electron microscopy.
An ultramicrotome allows scientists to obtain slices thin enough for detailed cellular analysis.
Proper use of the ultramicrotome is essential for producing high-quality microscopic images.

Related Terms

  • synonym: microtome - an instrument for cutting thin sections of material for microscopic study
