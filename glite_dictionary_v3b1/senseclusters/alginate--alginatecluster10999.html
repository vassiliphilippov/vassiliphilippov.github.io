alginate

adjective
containing alginate
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.dʒəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈæl.dʒɪ.neɪt / (BrE)
containing or made from alginate, especially referring to materials or products that include alginate as a component.

Examples

Alginate impression material
The dentist used an alginate impression material to take a mold of the patient's teeth.
Wound dressings with alginate are often chosen for their ability to absorb fluids.
Some dietary supplements contain alginate fibers to help with digestion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alginic - relating to or derived from alginic acid
