Mennonite

proper noun
Christian group member
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛnəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛnənaɪt / (BrE)
a member of a Christian group known for simple living, pacifism, and community values

Examples

Mennonite community values
The Mennonite community is respected for its commitment to peace and helping others.
Many Mennonite families continue to practice traditional farming methods.
A Mennonite choir performed beautiful hymns at the local festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Amish - a Christian group related to Mennonites, known for simple living and reluctance to adopt modern technology
  • synonym: Quaker - a member of the Religious Society of Friends, known for pacifism and simple living
