hovercar

noun
floating toy vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌvərkɑːr / (AmE), / ˈhʌvəkɑː / (BrE)
a small toy or model vehicle that floats slightly above a surface, usually for play or demonstration

Examples

Toy hovercar race
The children raced a hovercar across the living room, amazed by how it glided above the floor.
He received a remote-controlled hovercar for his birthday and played with it all afternoon.
At the science fair, a student demonstrated a homemade hovercar that floated on a cushion of air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hovercraft - a vehicle that travels over land or water on a cushion of air
  • synonym: toy car - a small model car used for play
