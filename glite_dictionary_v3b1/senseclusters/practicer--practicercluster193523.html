practicer

noun
person who practices
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpræktɪsər / (AmE), / ˈpræktɪsə / (BrE)
a person who regularly does a particular activity, profession, or skill, often to improve at it.

Examples

A practicer of music
As a daily practicer of yoga, he has seen great improvement in his flexibility.
The practicer spent hours each week perfecting her violin technique.
Every successful athlete is a dedicated practicer of their sport.
As a dedicated practiser of meditation, he spends time each morning in silence.
The school recognized her as the most consistent practiser of good study habits.
Every practiser of the martial art must follow strict discipline and training routines.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amateur - a person who engages in an activity for pleasure rather than as a profession
  • antonym: novice - a person new to and inexperienced in an activity
  • synonym: practitioner - a person actively engaged in an art, discipline, or profession
