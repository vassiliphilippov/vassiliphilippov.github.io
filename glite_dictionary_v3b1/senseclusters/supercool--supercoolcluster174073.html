supercool

verb
to cool below freezing
7.5
Pronunciation: /suː.pər.ˈkuːl/ /; / /ˈsuː.pər.kuːl/ (AmE), /suː.pə.ˈkuːl/ /; / /ˈsuː.pə.kuːl/ (BrE)
to cool a liquid below its normal freezing point without it turning into a solid

Examples

To supercool a liquid
Scientists were able to supercool water so that it remained liquid even below zero degrees Celsius.
If you supercool a liquid, it can stay unfrozen until disturbed or seeded with a crystal.
The experiment required them to supercool the solution before adding the catalyst.
