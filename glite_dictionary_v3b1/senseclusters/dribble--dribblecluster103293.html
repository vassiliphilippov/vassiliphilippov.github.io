Synonyms

  • drool
    2.1 1.54/m
    drool is when saliva runs from the mouth without control, while dribble can mean any slow small flow of liquid.
  • drip
    1.0 11/m
    drip means liquid falls in separate drops, while dribble often suggests a slow, light flow or small drops from mouth or object.
  • trickle
    3.4 5.63/m
    trickle is a thin, steady flow or stream, while dribble usually means small drops or a weak, irregular flow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gush - to flow out quickly and in large amounts
  • synonym: trickle - to flow in a small stream or in drops
