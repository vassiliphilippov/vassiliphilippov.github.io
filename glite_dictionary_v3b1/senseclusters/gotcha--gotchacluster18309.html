gotcha

noun
trap question
8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈgɑː.tʃə/ (AmE), /ˈgɒ.tʃə/ (BrE)
a question, action, or remark designed to catch someone off guard or embarrass them, especially in a debate or interview

Examples

A tough gotcha question

She prepared carefully so she would not fall for any gotcha during the debate.
That last gotcha in the meeting made everyone laugh, but it was a little embarrassing.
