keener

noun
funeral mourner
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkiː.nər/ (AmE), /ˈkiː.nə/ (BrE)
a person, often hired, who laments loudly and mourns at a funeral, especially by wailing or crying.

Examples

A funeral keener
The keener led the mourners in a traditional song of grief at the funeral.
In some cultures, a keener is hired to express sorrow on behalf of the family.
The sound of the keener wailing echoed through the quiet cemetery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mourner - a person who attends a funeral to grieve for the deceased
  • synonym: wailer - someone who cries or wails loudly, especially at funerals
