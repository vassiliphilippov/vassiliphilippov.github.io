lawnmower

noun
grass-cutting machine
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔnˌmoʊər / (AmE), / ˈlɔːnˌməʊə / (BrE)
a machine with rotating blades used to cut the grass on a lawn

Examples

Electric lawnmower
He pushed the lawnmower across the yard to keep the grass short.
After the rain stopped, she used the lawnmower to tidy up the garden.
The old lawnmower made a loud noise but still worked well.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mower - a machine or person that cuts grass or crops
  • synonym: trimmer - a tool used for cutting the edges of grass or hedges
