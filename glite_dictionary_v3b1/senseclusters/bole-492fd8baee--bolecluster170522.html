Bole

noun
a Chadic language of northern Nigeria
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊleɪ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊleɪ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in northern Nigeria, closely related to Hausa, with dialects such as Bara and Fika

Examples
