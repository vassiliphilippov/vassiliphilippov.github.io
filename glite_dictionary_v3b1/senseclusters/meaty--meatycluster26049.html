meaty

adjective
like or containing meat
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːti / (AmE), / ˈmiːti / (BrE)
containing meat or resembling meat in taste, texture, or substance

Examples

Meaty flavor
After weeks of eating salads, I craved something meaty for dinner.
The restaurant's special is a meaty stew, filled with chunks of beef and savory spices.
This burger tastes surprisingly meaty even though it is made from soy protein.

Related Terms

  • antonym: vegetarian - containing no meat or derived from plants
