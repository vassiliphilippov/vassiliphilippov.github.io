truffle

noun
creamy chocolate confection
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌfəl / (AmE), / ˈtrʌf.əl / (BrE)
A rich, creamy chocolate confection, typically made with a ganache center coated in chocolate, cocoa powder, or nuts.

Examples

Chocolate truffle candy
He bought a box of assorted truffle candies as a gift.
She offered me a homemade truffle filled with dark chocolate ganache.
The dessert tray included a selection of truffles dusted with cocoa powder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chocolate - a sweet food made from roasted and ground cacao seeds
