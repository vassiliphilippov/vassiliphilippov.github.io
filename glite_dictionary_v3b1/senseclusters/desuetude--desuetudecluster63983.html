desuetude

noun
state of disuse or inactivity
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdeswɪˌtuːd / (AmE), / ˈdɛswɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
The state of being no longer used or practiced; disuse.

Examples

Fall into desuetude
Many traditional rituals have lapsed into desuetude in modern society.
The law remained on the books, though it had long passed into desuetude.
The old railway station fell into desuetude after the new line was built.
