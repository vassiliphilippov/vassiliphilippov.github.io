faggoting

noun
openwork embroidery technique
Pronunciation: / ˈfæɡətɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfæɡətɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of embroidery in which groups of parallel threads are joined or decorated with fagot stitches to create openwork patterns.

Examples

Faggoting embroidery style
Learning faggoting can enhance the appearance of linens and garments.
She used faggoting along the seams to add a decorative touch to the blouse.
The vintage handkerchief featured delicate faggoting around its borders.
