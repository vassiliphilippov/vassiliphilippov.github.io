engagement

noun
mechanical connection or fitting together
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the action of a part or device fitting or coming into contact with another to perform a mechanical function

Examples

Engagement of gears
Precise engagement of the gears ensures the machine operates without slipping.
When starting the car, the engagement of the clutch must be smooth to prevent stalling.
You may hear a click that signals the engagement of the locking mechanism.
