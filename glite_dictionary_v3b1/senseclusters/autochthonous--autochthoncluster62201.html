autochthonous

adjective
formed in original place; not transported
9.8 9.9
Pronunciation: /ɑː.ˈtɑːk.θə.nəs/ /; / /ɔː.ˈtɑːk.θə.nəs/ (AmE), /ɔː.ˈtɒk.θə.nəs/ (BrE)
originating or formed in the place where it is found; native, not transported from elsewhere
