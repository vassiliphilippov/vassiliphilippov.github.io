DPT

noun
triple vaccine
3.1
Pronunciation: /diː.piː.tiː/ (AmE), /diː.piː.tiː/ (BrE)
a vaccine that protects against diphtheria, pertussis (whooping cough), and tetanus

Examples

DPT vaccine shot
Most children receive the DPT vaccine before starting school.
The doctor explained the importance of the DPT shot to the parents.
After getting the DPT vaccine, some children may have mild side effects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: DTaP - a similar vaccine for diphtheria, tetanus, and acellular pertussis
  • synonym: Tdap - a booster vaccine for tetanus, diphtheria, and pertussis
