talar

adjective
of the ankle bone
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlɑr / (AmE), / ˈteɪlɑː / (BrE)
relating to the talus, the ankle bone in the foot.

Examples

Talar joint injury
The doctor noted a talar fracture after reviewing the X-ray images.
Arthritis can sometimes affect the talar surface, causing pain and swelling.
Surgery was required to repair the damaged talar cartilage in his ankle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: talus - the large bone in the ankle that connects the leg and the foot
