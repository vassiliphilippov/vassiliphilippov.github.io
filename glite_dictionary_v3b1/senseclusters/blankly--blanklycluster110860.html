blankly

adverb
in an expressionless or uncomprehending way
Pronunciation: / ˈblæŋkli / (AmE), / ˈblaŋkli / (BrE)
in a way that shows no expression, understanding, or emotion

Examples

To stare blankly
She stared blankly at the wall, lost in thought.
The students looked blankly at their teacher, confused by the instructions.
When asked the question, he replied blankly as if he hadn't heard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vacantly - in a way that shows lack of thought or expression
