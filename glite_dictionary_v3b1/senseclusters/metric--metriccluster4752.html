metric

adjective
relating to poetic meter or rhythm
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the rhythmic arrangement of syllables in verse; of or pertaining to poetic meter.
