Synonyms

  • implausible
    7.0 2.34/m
    implausible seems unlikely or unreasonable in a fact-based way, while far-fetched feels exaggerated and hard to accept.
  • unbelievable
    4.9 13/m
    unbelievable sounds like something extremely unlikely or amazing, while far-fetched suggests a weak, overstated idea hard to believe.
  • fanciful
    6.0 1.29/m
    fanciful is imaginative and unreal, while far-fetched describes an idea that is exaggerated and unlikely in real life.
  • preposterous
    7.3 3.59/m
    preposterous is so unreasonable it feels laughable, while far-fetched is unlikely but less strongly insulting or comic.
  • absurd
    4.4 17/m
    absurd is wildly ridiculous and often insulting, while far-fetched means unlikely or exaggerated without strong ridicule.
