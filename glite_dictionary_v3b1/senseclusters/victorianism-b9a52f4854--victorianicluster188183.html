Victorianism

noun
Victorian era revival
8.9
Pronunciation: /vɪk.ˈtɔːr.i.ə.nɪz.əm/ (AmE), /vɪk.ˈtɔː.ri.ə.nɪz.əm/ (BrE)
the values, attitudes, styles, or features typical of the Victorian era, especially strict morals, social codes, and distinctive artistic styles
