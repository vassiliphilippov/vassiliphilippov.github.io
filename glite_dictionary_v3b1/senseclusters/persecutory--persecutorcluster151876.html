persecutory

adjective
feeling attacked
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrsɪkjəˌtɔri / (AmE), / pəˈsɪkjʊtəri / (BrE)
relating to the feeling or belief that one is being unfairly attacked, harassed, or persecuted

Examples

Persecutory thoughts
The patient described experiencing persecutory thoughts during periods of stress.
People with certain mental health conditions may develop persecutory beliefs about those around them.
Her persecutory feelings made it difficult for her to trust her coworkers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paranoid - having an unreasonable fear that others are trying to harm you
  • antonym: protective - feeling the need to guard oneself from harm
