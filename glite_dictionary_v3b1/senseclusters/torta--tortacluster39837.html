torta

noun
Filipino egg dish
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrtə / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtə / (BrE)
a Filipino omelette or egg-based dish, often mixed with vegetables, meat, or seafood

Examples

Filipino torta omelette
For lunch, she prepared a delicious torta with ground pork and diced potatoes.
The restaurant's signature torta includes shrimp, onions, and bell peppers.
He learned to make his grandmother's favorite torta using leftover vegetables and eggs.

Related Terms
