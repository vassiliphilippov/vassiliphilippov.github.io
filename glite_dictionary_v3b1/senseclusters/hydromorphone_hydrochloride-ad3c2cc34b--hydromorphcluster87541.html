hydromorphone hydrochloride

noun
narcotic pain-relieving drug (Dilaudid)
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdroʊˈmɔːrfoʊn ˌhaɪdrəˈklɔːraɪd / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdrəʊˈmɔːfəʊn ˌhaɪdrəˈklɔːraɪd / (BrE)
a powerful narcotic analgesic drug used to relieve moderate to severe pain, commonly known by the trade name Dilaudid

Examples

Hydromorphone hydrochloride pain relief

Due to its potency, hydromorphone hydrochloride is prescribed only for moderate to severe pain that does not respond to other treatments.
The hospital administered hydromorphone hydrochloride to manage the patient's severe postoperative pain.
