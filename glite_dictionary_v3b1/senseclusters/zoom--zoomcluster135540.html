zoom

noun
a rapid increase or rise
Pronunciation: / zuːm / (AmE), / zuːm / (BrE)
a rapid and significant increase, especially in price, level, or activity

Examples

A zoom in prices
The past decade saw a remarkable zoom in the city’s population.
There was a sudden zoom in stock market prices after the announcement.
We have witnessed a zoom in demand for electric cars this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surge - a sudden powerful forward or upward movement, especially by a crowd or by a natural force
