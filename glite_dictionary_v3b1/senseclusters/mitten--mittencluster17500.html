mitten

verb
to put on mittens
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪtənd / (AmE), / ˈmɪtənd / (BrE)
to put mittens on someone or something, or to cover hands or paws with mittens.

Examples

To mitten a child
She quickly mittened the baby before stepping out into the cold playground.
Before taking the puppy outside, he mittened its tiny paws to keep them warm.
In winter, parents often mitten their children to protect their hands from frostbite.
