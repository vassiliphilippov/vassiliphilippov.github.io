coigue

noun
Chilean evergreen tree used for thatching
4.6
Pronunciation: / koʊˈiːɡeɪ / (AmE), / kəʊˈiːɡeɪ / (BrE)
A Chilean evergreen tree whose leafy branches are used for thatching roofs.

Examples

A coigue tree
A dense grove of coigue trees bordered the path through the Chilean forest.
Local artisans weave baskets from the flexible twigs of the coigue.
The villagers gathered branches from the coigue to repair their roofs before the rainy season.
