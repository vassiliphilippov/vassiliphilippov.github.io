manat

noun
currency of Turkmenistan
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑː.nɑːt / (AmE), / ˈmæn.æt / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Turkmenistan, equal to 100 tenge.

Examples

Turkmen manat note
She exchanged dollars for manats at the bank before her trip to Turkmenistan.
The manat has fluctuated in value over the past year.
The price of a loaf of bread is one manat in Ashgabat.
