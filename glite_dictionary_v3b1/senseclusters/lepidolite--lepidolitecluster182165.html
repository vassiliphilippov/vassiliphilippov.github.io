lepidolite

noun
a lithium-bearing mica mineral
Pronunciation: / lɪˈpɪd.əˌlaɪt / (AmE), / lɪˈpɪd.ə.laɪt / (BrE)
A lilac or rose-colored mineral of the mica group that is an important source of lithium.

Examples

Lepidolite mineral
Lepidolite is often used as a major source for extracting lithium in industrial processes.
A collection of colorful minerals included shimmering pieces of lepidolite and quartz.
The geologist discovered a large deposit of lepidolite in the remote mining area.
