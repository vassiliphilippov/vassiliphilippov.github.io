denounce

verb
formally declare end of treaty or agreement
Pronunciation: / dɪˈnaʊns / (AmE), / dɪˈnaʊns / (BrE)
to announce the formal termination of an agreement or treaty

Examples

To denounce a treaty
After repeated violations, the state formally denounced the treaty.
The country decided to denounce the long-standing trade agreement.
The government plans to denounce all bilateral pacts that no longer serve its interests.
