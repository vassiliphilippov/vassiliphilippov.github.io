tractable

adjective
easy to manage or influence
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtræk.tə.bəl /; / ˈtræktəbəl / (AmE), / ˈtræk.tə.bəl /; / ˈtræktəbl / (BrE)
easy to manage, control, or influence; willing to accept suggestions or directions

Examples

A tractable child
Children can be more tractable when given clear instructions and positive reinforcement.

Some chemicals are more tractable than others, making them easier to process industrially.

The problem is complex but still tractable with the right approach.
