Don Juan

noun
a man who is a successful womanizer
Pronunciation: / dɑn ˈwɑn /; / ˌdɑn ˈwɑn / (AmE), / dɒn ˈwɑːn /; / ˌdɒn ˈwɑːn / (BrE)
a man who is known for seducing many women; a womanizer (from the legendary Spanish nobleman Don Juan)

Examples

A Don Juan man
Despite his charming looks, she wasn't interested in another Don Juan.
He has a reputation as a Don Juan who never settles down with one woman.
The tabloids labeled the actor a modern Don Juan after his string of affairs.
He was often called a Don Juan because of his reputation with women.
Mozart composed an opera based on the life of Don Juan.
The main character became a modern Don Juan, charming everyone he met.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Casanova - a man who behaves in a promiscuous or amorous manner
  • antonym: faithful - remaining loyal and steadfast to one partner
  • synonym: philanderer - a man who frequently enters into casual sexual relationships with women
