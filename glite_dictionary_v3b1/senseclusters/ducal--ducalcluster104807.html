ducal

adjective
relating to a duke or duchy
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈduːkəl / (AmE), / ˈdjuːkəl / (BrE)
of or belonging to a duke or duchy; suitable for a duke.

Examples

A ducal crest
A ducal crest was engraved above the entrance to the estate.
The grand ballroom retained much of its original ducal splendor.
They hosted an elegant dinner at their ducal residence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duke - a male ruler of a duchy or member of the noble rank below a prince
