European bream

noun
European freshwater fish (silvery, flattened body)
Pronunciation: / jʊˈroʊpiən briːm / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən briːm / (BrE)
A European freshwater fish with a flattened body and silvery scales, valued little as food.

Examples

The European bream fish
Although common, European bream are not highly prized for their flavor.
Her field research focused on the spawning habits of the European bream.
The fisherman caught a large European bream near the riverbank.
