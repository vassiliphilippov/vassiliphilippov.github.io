buss

verb
archaic verb meaning to kiss
Pronunciation: / bʌs / (AmE), / bʌs / (BrE)
(archaic) To kiss someone, especially as a gesture of affection or greeting.

Examples

To buss the cheek
He busses his wife every morning before heading to work.
She leaned over and bussed her daughter goodnight before leaving the room.
The actor bussed his co-star on the cheek after a successful performance.
