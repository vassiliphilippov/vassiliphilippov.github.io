musk

verb
to scent with musk
8.6
Pronunciation: / mʌsk / (AmE), / mʌsk / (BrE)
to apply musk to something or to scent or mark with a musky odor

Examples

To musk perfume
The perfumer musked the sample to recreate the animalic base note for the new perfume.
In ancient times, people musked their clothing to mask unpleasant odors.
The room was musked with a subtle, earthy fragrance after the incense was burned.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deodorize - to remove or mask an unpleasant smell
  • synonym: perfume - to apply a pleasant fragrance to something
