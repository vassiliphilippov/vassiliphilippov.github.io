praetorian

adjective
relating to a Roman praetor
Pronunciation: / priˈtɔːriən / (AmE), / priːˈtɔːrɪən / (BrE)
relating to a Roman praetor or to the office, authority, or duties of a praetor.

Examples

The praetorian guard
Ancient manuscripts described the praetorian process of governance in detail.
Some officials exercised praetorian powers that influenced legal and military affairs.
The city was under praetorian administration during the early Roman Empire.
