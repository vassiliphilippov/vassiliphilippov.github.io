degradation

noun
loss of dignity or status
1.0 5.1
Pronunciation: /dɪ.grə.ˈdeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /dɛ.grə.ˈdeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /dɛ.grə.ˈdeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
A state of having lost dignity, honor, or position; a low or humiliated condition.

Examples

Personal degradation
After the scandal, she withdrew from public life in a period of emotional degradation.
His repeated failures led him to a state of degradation he could never have imagined.
The cruel punishment subjected the prisoner to considerable degradation in front of his peers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abasement - a lowering in rank, dignity, or self-esteem
  • antonym: exaltation - a feeling or state of extreme happiness or elevation
  • synonym: humiliation - the act or state of being made to feel ashamed or degraded
