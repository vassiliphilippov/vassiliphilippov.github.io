insect repellant

noun
chemical that repels insects
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.sɛkt rɪˈpɛl.ənt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.sekt rɪˈpel.ənt / (BrE)
a chemical substance applied to skin, clothing, or other surfaces to discourage insects from landing or biting

Examples

The shop sells a natural insect repellant that is safe for children.
