attribute

verb
to assign as a cause or source
2.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈtrɪ.bjuːt/ /; / /ə.ˈtrɪ.bjət/ (AmE), /ə.ˈtrɪ.bjuːt/ /; / /ə.ˈtrɪ.bjət/ (BrE)
to say or think that something is caused by or belongs to a particular person, thing, or source
