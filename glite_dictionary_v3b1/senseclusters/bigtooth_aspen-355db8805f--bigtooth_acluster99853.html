bigtooth aspen

noun
a North American aspen tree species
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪɡˌtuθ ˈæspən / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡˌtuːθ ˈæspən / (BrE)
a North American aspen tree (Populus grandidentata) with a narrow crown and large, coarse-toothed leaves

Examples

A bigtooth aspen tree
A stand of bigtooth aspen thrives along the edge of the forest near the lake.
Foresters often plant bigtooth aspen to aid reforestation in eastern North America.
The leaves of the bigtooth aspen are easily recognised by their large, coarse teeth.
