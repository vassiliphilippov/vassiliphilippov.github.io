anti-racketeering law

noun
law against organized crime and racketeering
8.2 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈæn.tiː ræk.ɪt.ˈɪr.ɪŋ lɑː/ /; / /ˈæn.taɪ ræk.ɪt.ˈɪr.ɪŋ lɔː/ (AmE), /ˈæn.tɪ ræk.ɪt.ˈɪə.rɪŋ lɔː/ /; / /ˈæn.tiː ræk.ɪt.ˈɪə.rɪŋ lɔː/ (BrE)
A law enacted to combat organized crime by imposing strict penalties and allowing the seizure of assets gained through illegal activities.

Examples

The government passed a new anti-racketeering law to cripple criminal organizations.
