subfraction

noun
smaller subgroup
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˈfrækʃən / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈfrækʃən / (BrE)
a smaller group or faction that exists within a larger political, social, or organizational group

Examples

Political subfraction
A subfraction of the committee disagreed with the final decision and voiced their concerns.
The organization split into several subfractions after the leadership change.
Within the student council, a subfraction advocated for more environmental initiatives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faction - a group within a larger organization with different ideas or interests
  • antonym: majority - the larger part of a group
  • synonym: subgroup - a smaller group within a larger group
