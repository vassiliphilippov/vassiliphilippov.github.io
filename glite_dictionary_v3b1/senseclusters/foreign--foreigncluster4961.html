Synonyms

  • nonnative
    4.9 0.53/m
    nonnative describes a species or person from elsewhere, while foreign is the general word for not belonging naturally.
  • nonindigenous
    7.2 0.22/m
    nonindigenous refers especially to organisms introduced from another region, while foreign is broader and used for many things.
  • nonendemic
    7.1 0.00/m
    nonendemic means not limited to one area and often used for species, while foreign means not native or local.
  • alien
    4.1 24/m
    alien can feel strange or legal and sometimes hostile, while foreign simply means not native or introduced.
