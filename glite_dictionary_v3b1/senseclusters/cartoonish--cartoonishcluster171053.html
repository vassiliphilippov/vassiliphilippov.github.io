cartoonish

adjective
exaggerated like a cartoon
4.2
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈtuːnɪʃ / (AmE), / kɑːˈtuːnɪʃ / (BrE)
looking exaggerated, silly, or resembling the style of a cartoon

Examples

Cartoonish style
The villain in the film had a cartoonish laugh and exaggerated facial expressions.
Her cartoonish drawings made everyone smile at the art show.
The costumes were so cartoonish that even adults found them amusing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comic - funny or resembling a comic strip in style
  • antonym: realistic - representing things as they are in real life
