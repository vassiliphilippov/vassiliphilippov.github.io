fifth wheel

noun
spare wheel for a vehicle
Pronunciation: / fɪfθ wil / (AmE), / fɪfθ wiːl / (BrE)
a spare wheel and tire carried as an extra for use on a four-wheel vehicle

Examples

A spare fifth wheel
Before starting their road trip, they made sure the fifth wheel was properly inflated and securely stowed.
If you get a flat tire, having a fifth wheel can be a real lifesaver.
Older trucks often carried a fifth wheel mounted on the back for emergencies.
