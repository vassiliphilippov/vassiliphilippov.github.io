boat paddle

noun
oar for propelling small boats
2.6
Pronunciation: / boʊt ˈpædəl / (AmE), / bəʊt ˈpadl̩ / (BrE)
a short, light oar used without an oarlock to propel a canoe or small boat

Examples

A boat paddle grip
Each kayaker gripped a boat paddle as they prepared to start the race.
He used a boat paddle to steer the canoe down the river.
The old wooden boat paddle was worn smooth from years of use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oar - pole with a flat blade used to row or steer a boat
