Presidential Directive

noun
directive issued by the President of the U.S.
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl dəˈrɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˌprɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl dɪˈrɛktɪv / (BrE)
an official instruction issued by the President of the United States, usually addressed to heads of federal departments and agencies

Examples

A Presidential Directive issued
After the terrorist attacks, a Presidential Directive was issued to reorganize homeland security.
All federal agencies must comply with the new Presidential Directive concerning cybersecurity.
The Presidential Directive outlined new environmental policies for government departments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: executive order - an official order by the President of the United States; closely related term
