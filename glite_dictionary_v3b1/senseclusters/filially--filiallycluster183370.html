filially

adverb
as a son or daughter
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪliəli / (AmE), / ˈfɪliəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or is appropriate for a son or daughter

Examples

Filially caring for parents
She supported her mother filially during the long illness.
He always spoke to his father filially, with respect and care.
The siblings visited their parents filially every weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dutifully - in a way that shows a sense of duty or responsibility
  • antonym: parentally - in a way that relates to a parent
