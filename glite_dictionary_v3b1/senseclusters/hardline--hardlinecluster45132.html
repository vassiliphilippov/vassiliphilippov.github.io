hardline

adjective
firm and uncompromising
6.8 0.83/m

strict, firm, and unwilling to change position or make compromises, especially regarding policies or attitudes

Examples

A hardline approach
Negotiators warned that a hardline approach would make compromise impossible.
Some members of the committee hold hardline views on immigration policy.
The new government adopted a hardline stance on crime and punishment.

  • hardheaded
