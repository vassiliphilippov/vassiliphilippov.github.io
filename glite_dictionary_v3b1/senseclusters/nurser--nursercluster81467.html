nurser

noun
plant caretaker
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɜːrsər / (AmE), / ˈnɜːsə / (BrE)
a person who cares for young plants or seedlings, especially in a nursery

Examples

Nurser of seedlings
The nurser carefully watered each row of seedlings every morning.
As a skilled nurser, she knew how to spot early signs of plant disease.
The young nurser learned how to transplant saplings without damaging their roots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gardener - a person who tends and cultivates a garden
  • synonym: horticulturist - a person skilled in the science of growing plants
