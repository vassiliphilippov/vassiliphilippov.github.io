smiling

adjective
showing a smile; cheerful-looking
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsmaɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsmaɪlɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

A smiling person

The smiling children greeted their teacher at the door every morning.
