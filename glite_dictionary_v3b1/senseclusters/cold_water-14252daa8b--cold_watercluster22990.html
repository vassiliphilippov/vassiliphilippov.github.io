cold water

noun
discouragement; disapproval of a plan
2.3 5.1
Pronunciation: /koʊld ˈwɔː.tər/ /; / /koʊld ˈɑː.tər/ (AmE), /kəʊld ˈwɔː.tə/ (BrE)
