softback book

noun
book with a paper cover
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑftˌbæk bʊk / (AmE), / ˈsɒftˌbæk bʊk / (BrE)
A book with a flexible paper cover, rather than a hard cover.

Examples

A softback book
She prefers reading softback books because they are lighter to carry than hardbacks.
The store had a special offer on classic novels in softback book format.
You can buy the new mystery novel as either a hardcover or a softback book.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hardback book - A book with a rigid protective cover
  • synonym: paperback - A book bound in flexible paper covers
