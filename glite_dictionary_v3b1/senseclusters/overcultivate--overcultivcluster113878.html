To make land less productive by cultivating it too intensively or frequently, exhausting its resources.

Examples

To overcultivate the soil
Efforts are underway to restore areas damaged by overcultivating the soil.
If farmers overcultivate their fields, the soil quickly loses its fertility.
The land was overcultivated for years, resulting in poor crop yields.
