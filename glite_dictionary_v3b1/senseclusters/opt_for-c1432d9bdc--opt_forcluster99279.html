opt for

verb
to choose or select something
3.5 7.88/m
Pronunciation: / É‘pt fÉ”r / (AmE), / É’pt fÉ™ / (BrE)
to choose or decide in favor of a particular option from among several possibilities

Examples

To opt for a choice
After much consideration, she opted for the vegetarian meal at the restaurant.
Companies often opt for remote work to reduce overhead costs.
Many students opt for online courses due to their flexibility.
