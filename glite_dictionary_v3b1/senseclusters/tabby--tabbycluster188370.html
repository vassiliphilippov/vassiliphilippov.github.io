tabby

noun
a cat with a striped or mottled coat
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtæbi / (AmE), / ˈtæbi / (BrE)
a cat, especially a female, with a grey or tawny coat marked with stripes, spots, or swirling patterns, often mottled with black

Examples

A striped tabby
Her old tabby curls up on the sunny windowsill every afternoon.
I used to have a tabby that loved to chase butterflies in the garden.
The shelter has several tabby kittens available for adoption.
