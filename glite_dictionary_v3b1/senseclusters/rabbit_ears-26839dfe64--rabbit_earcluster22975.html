rabbit ears

noun
indoor TV antenna with extendible rods
Pronunciation: / ˈræbɪt ɪrz / (AmE), / ˈræbɪt ɪəz / (BrE)
an indoor television antenna consisting of two extendible metal rods angled to form a V-shape, used to receive TV signals

Examples

TV rabbit ears
Old televisions often relied on rabbit ears to pick up local broadcasts.
She covered the rabbit ears with aluminum foil to try to improve the reception.
We had trouble getting a clear picture, so we adjusted the rabbit ears on top of the TV.
