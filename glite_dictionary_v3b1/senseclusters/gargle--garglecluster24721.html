gargle

verb
utter in a gargling or burbling way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrɡəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːɡl / (BrE)
to utter words or sounds with a gargling or burbling quality

Examples

To gargle words
He gargled something unintelligible and stormed out of the room.
She tried to gargle a reply, but her mouth was full of water.
The actor gargled his lines for comedic effect during the rehearsal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gurgle - make a bubbling sound in the throat
