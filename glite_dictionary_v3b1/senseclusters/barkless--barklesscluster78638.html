barkless

adjective
not barking
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrkləs / (AmE), / ˈbɑːkləs / (BrE)
not producing a barking sound; unable or unwilling to bark.

Examples

Barkless dog breed
The Basenji is a famous barkless dog breed known for its quiet nature.
After the surgery, the once loud dog became completely barkless.
Despite the excitement outside, the puppy remained barkless and calm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: noisy - making a lot of noise
  • synonym: silent - not making any sound
