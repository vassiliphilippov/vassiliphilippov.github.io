bloodedly

adverb
without emotion
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdɪdli / (AmE), / ˈblʌdɪdli / (BrE)
in a cold, emotionless, or unfeeling manner

Examples

Bloodedly ignore feelings
He bloodedly delivered the news, not caring about anyone's feelings.
The manager bloodedly dismissed the employee without a second thought.
She bloodedly ignored his apology, showing no sign of forgiveness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coldly - in an unemotional or unfriendly way
  • synonym: emotionlessly - without showing any emotion
  • antonym: warmly - in a friendly or affectionate way
