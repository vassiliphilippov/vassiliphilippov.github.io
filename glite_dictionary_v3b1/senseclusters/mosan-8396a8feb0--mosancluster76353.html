Mosan

noun
a family of Amerindian languages in the Pacific Northwest
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.sæn / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.sæn / (BrE)
a proposed family of Amerindian languages spoken in Washington and British Columbia, including Salishan, Wakashan, and Chimakuan languages

Examples

Mosan language family
Few fluent speakers of Mosan languages remain today due to historical changes in the region.
Some believe that Salishan, Wakashan, and Chimakuan languages are all part of the Mosan group.
The classification of the Mosan language family has been debated among linguists for decades.
