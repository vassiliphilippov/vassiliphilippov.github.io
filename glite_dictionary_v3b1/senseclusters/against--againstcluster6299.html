against

preposition
in comparison with
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈɡɛnst / (AmE), / əˈɡɛnst / (BrE)
in comparison with or considered in relation to something else

Examples

Against a background
The team's performance was impressive when judged against the league's top players.
Her achievements stand out against the background of a challenging year.
The new policy was evaluated against international standards to ensure quality.
