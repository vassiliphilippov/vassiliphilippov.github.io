fecal occult test

noun
test for hidden blood in stool
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːkəl əˈkʌlt tɛst /; / ˈfiː.kəl əˈkʌlt tɛst / (AmE), / ˈfiːkəl əˈkʌlt tɛst /; / ˈfiː.kəl əˈkʌlt tɛst / (BrE)
