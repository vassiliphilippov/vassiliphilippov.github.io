audit

verb
attend a course without getting credit
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɑdɪt / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪt / (BrE)
to attend a class or course without receiving academic credit or a grade

Examples

To audit a class
Many retirees choose to audit university lectures to keep learning without worrying about grades.
She decided to audit a philosophy course during her summer break.
Students can audit the class if they prefer not to take the final exam.
