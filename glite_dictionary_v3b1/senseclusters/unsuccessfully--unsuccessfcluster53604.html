    fruitlessly describes doing something that produces no useful result, while unsuccessfully means failing to achieve the desired result.
  • ineffectively
    4.0 0.1/m
    ineffectively means acting in a way that does not work, while unsuccessfully focuses on failing to reach the intended outcome.
  • futilely
    7.4 0.1/m
    futilely describes effort that has no hope of success, while unsuccessfully simply states that an attempt failed.
  • vainly
    5.5 0.2/m
    vainly suggests trying with no result and a sense of wasted effort, while unsuccessfully just means not succeeding.
  • in vain

  • abortively
    7.9 0.00/m
