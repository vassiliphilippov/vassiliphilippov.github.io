nonaffiliated

noun
person with no group ties
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˈfɪlieɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəˈfɪlieɪtɪd / (BrE)
a person who is not connected with or a member of any organization, group, or political party

Examples

A nonaffiliated voter
As a nonaffiliated, she preferred to stay out of political debates.
The club welcomed both members and nonaffiliated guests to the event.
Many young voters now identify as nonaffiliated rather than joining a political party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: independent - a person not belonging to any group or party
  • antonym: member - a person who belongs to a group or organization
