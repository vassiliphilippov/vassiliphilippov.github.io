television monitor

noun
monitor used to view broadcasts in a studio
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən ˈmɑnɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən ˈmɒnɪtə / (BrE)
