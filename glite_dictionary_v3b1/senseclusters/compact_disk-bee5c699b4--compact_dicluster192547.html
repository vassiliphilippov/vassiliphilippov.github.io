compact disk

noun
small optical disc for digital data
5.5 2.2
Pronunciation: /kəm.ˈpækt dɪsk/ /; / /ˈkɑːm.pækt dɪsk/ (AmE), /kəm.ˈpækt dɪsk/ /; / /ˈkɒm.pækt dɪsk/ (BrE)
