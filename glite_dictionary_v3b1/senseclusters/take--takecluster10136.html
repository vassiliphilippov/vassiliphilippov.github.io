Synonyms

  • accept
    1.0 44/m
    accept is agreeing to receive an offered gift, invitation or membership, while take is broader and can mean getting or grabbing.
  • have
    1.0 856/m
    have means to possess or own something now, while take means to receive or accept something as an action.
  • admit
    2.8 12/m
    admit is to allow someone to join an organization, while take means to receive or accept someone as a member.
