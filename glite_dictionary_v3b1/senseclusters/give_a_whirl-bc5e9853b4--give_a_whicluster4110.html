give a whirl

verb
try something out
6.6
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv ə wɜrl / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə wɜːl / (BrE)
to try something, especially for the first time, to see if you like it or are good at it

Examples

To give a whirl at yoga
If you’re unsure about yoga, you could give it a whirl at the community class.
She’d never tried mountain biking before, but decided to give it a whirl last Saturday.
You might like chess—why not give it a whirl?
