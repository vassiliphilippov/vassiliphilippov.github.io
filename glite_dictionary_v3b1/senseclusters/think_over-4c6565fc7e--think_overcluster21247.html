think over

verb
consider carefully; reflect on
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪŋk ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈθɪŋk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)

Examples

To think over a plan
I need some time to think over your proposal before I decide.
She thought over her friend's advice very carefully.
The night before the interview, he kept thinking over what he was going to say.
