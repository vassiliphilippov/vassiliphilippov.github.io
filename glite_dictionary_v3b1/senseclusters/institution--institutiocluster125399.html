institution

noun
hospital for mental illness or disability
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.stəˈtu.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.stɪˈtjuː.ʃən / (BrE)
a hospital for people with mental illnesses or intellectual disabilities, especially one providing long-term care

Examples

Mental health institution
After his diagnosis, he was admitted to an institution for treatment and care.
Many former patients of the old institution recalled the harsh conditions they experienced.
The government has reformed the mental health system, closing many large institutions in favor of community care.
