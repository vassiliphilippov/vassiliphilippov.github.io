institution

noun
hospital for mental illness or disability
5.6 7.38/m
Pronunciation: / Àå…™n.st…ôÀàtu. É…ôn / (AmE), / Àå…™n.st…™ÀàtjuÀê. É…ôn / (BrE)
a hospital for people with mental illnesses or intellectual disabilities, especially one providing long-term care

Examples

Mental health institution
After his diagnosis, he was admitted to an institution for treatment and care.
Many former patients of the old institution recalled the harsh conditions they experienced.
The government has reformed the mental health system, closing many large institutions in favor of community care.

Synonyms

  • mental institution
    3.6 1.01/m
    mental institution is an older everyday phrase for long-term psychiatric care, while institution is a neutral formal term for such long-stay facilities.
  • mental hospital
    2.8 1.58/m
    mental hospital treats people with psychiatric disorders in a hospital setting, while institution often implies long-term residential care.
  • psychiatric hospital
    3.0 1.11/m
    psychiatric hospital offers medical treatment and therapy for acute mental health problems, while institution here stresses long-term custodial care.
