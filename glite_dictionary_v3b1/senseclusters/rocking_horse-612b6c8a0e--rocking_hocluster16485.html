rocking horse

noun
children's horse-shaped rocking toy
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑkɪŋ hɔrs / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪŋ hɔːs / (BrE)
A children's toy in the shape of a horse mounted on curved rockers, on which a child can sit and rock back and forth.

Examples

A rocking horse toy
He gave his daughter a beautiful rocking horse for her birthday.
The old rocking horse in the attic once belonged to my grandfather.
The toddler laughed as she rocked back and forth on her rocking horse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hobby horse - a toy consisting of a stick with a horse's head, which a child rides astride
