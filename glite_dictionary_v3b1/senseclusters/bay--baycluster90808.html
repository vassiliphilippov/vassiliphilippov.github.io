bay

verb
bark or howl noisily (of dogs)
7.7 5.5
Pronunciation: /beɪ/ (AmE), /beɪ/ (BrE)
(of a dog or wolf) to bark or howl loudly and persistently

Examples

To bay at night
At midnight, the dog would bay at the moon for several minutes.
Hunters listened as their dogs bayed from deep within the woods.
The hounds began to bay loudly as they picked up the scent in the forest.
A lone hound would often bay in the distance at night, breaking the silence.
The dogs bayed loudly, alerting the hunters to the scent of their prey.
The wolves began to bay at the moon as darkness fell.
