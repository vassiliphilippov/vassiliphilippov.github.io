noninterruptive

adjective
without causing disruption
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪn.təˈrʌptɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪn.təˈrʌptɪv / (BrE)
not causing interruption or disruption to a process or activity

Examples

A noninterruptive process
The software update was completely noninterruptive and did not affect users' work.
We aim to provide noninterruptive service even during system maintenance.
The new backup process is noninterruptive, allowing employees to continue working as usual.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disruptive - causing interruption or disturbance
  • synonym: uninterrupted - continuing without being stopped or disturbed
