Slinky

noun
coil toy for stairs
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈslɪŋki / (BrE)
a toy made of a flexible metal or plastic coil that moves down steps by stretching and reforming its shape

Examples

A Slinky toy
The children watched the Slinky flip end over end as it traveled down the stairs.
She received a shiny new Slinky for her birthday and played with it all afternoon.
A classic Slinky can entertain kids for hours with its simple movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spring - a coiled piece of metal that can be compressed or stretched
