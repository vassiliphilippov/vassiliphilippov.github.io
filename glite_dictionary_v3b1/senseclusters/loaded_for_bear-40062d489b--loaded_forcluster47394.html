loaded for bear

adjective
well-prepared for confrontation
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊdɪd fər ˈber / (AmE), / ˈləʊdɪd fə ˈbeə / (BrE)
Well-prepared and ready to face an intense challenge, confrontation, or difficult situation.

Examples

To be loaded for bear
He walked into the boardroom loaded for bear, having prepared every possible counterpoint.
She arrived at the negotiations loaded for bear, determined to win every argument.
The lawyer was loaded for bear when she faced the opposing counsel.
