early spider orchid

noun
a species of spring-flowering orchid
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɝːli ˈspaɪdɚ ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈɜːli ˈspaɪdə ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
