craftsman

noun
a skilled manual worker or artisan
Pronunciation: / ˈkræfts.mən / (AmE), / ˈkrɑːfts.mən / (BrE)
a person who is highly skilled in a particular manual craft or trade

Examples

Skilled craftsman worker
Becoming a craftsman takes years of practice and dedication to the art.
She admired the delicate jewelry carved by a skilled craftsman.
The wooden chest was made by a master craftsman using traditional techniques.
Every craftsman in the workshop takes pride in their work.
She admired how the craftsman carved intricate designs into the wood.
The furniture was made by a skilled craftsman using traditional techniques.
A true craftsman takes pride in every stage of the building process.
Each stained glass window was created by a master craftsman over several months.
The furniture was built by a local craftsman known for his attention to detail.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amateur - a person who engages in a pursuit, especially a sport or art, on an unpaid rather than a professional basis
  • degree lower: apprentice - a person learning a trade from a skilled employer
  • synonym: artisan - a worker skilled in a trade, especially one that involves making things by hand
  • synonym: craftswoman - a woman who is skilled in a particular craft or manual trade
