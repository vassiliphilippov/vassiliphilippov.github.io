Magian

adjective
of the Magi
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdʒiən / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdʒiən / (BrE)
relating to the Magi, the ancient priestly caste of Persia, or their religious practices

Examples

Magian rituals
The ancient text described a Magian ceremony held at dawn.
He wore robes with symbols of Magian origin during the festival.
Legends often mention Magian wisdom and mysterious rituals.
