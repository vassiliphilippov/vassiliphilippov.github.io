enfeeble

verb
make or become very weak
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfiːbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈfiːbəl / (BrE)
to make someone or something very weak

Examples

To enfeeble the body
The disease began to enfeeble her, making daily tasks difficult.
The oppressive heat seemed to enfeeble the athletes during the marathon.
Years of neglect had enfeebled the old mansion, leaving it in a state of disrepair.
