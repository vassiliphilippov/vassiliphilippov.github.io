red silk cotton

noun
fiber from the red silk-cotton tree
7.0 7.9
Pronunciation: /red sɪlk ˈkɑː.tən/ /; / /red sɪlk ˈkɔː.tən/ (AmE), /red sɪlk ˈkɒt.ən/ (BrE)
