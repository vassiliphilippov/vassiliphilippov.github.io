fatuus

adjective
foolish or silly
Pronunciation: / ˈfætʃuːəs / (AmE), / ˈfætjuːəs / (BrE)
archaic term meaning foolish, silly, or lacking good sense

Examples

Fatuus mistake
In the ancient manuscript, the king was described as a fatuus unfit to rule.
The poet mocked the fatuus nobleman for his lack of wisdom.
Historians often refer to the ruler as a fatuus in their accounts of the era.
