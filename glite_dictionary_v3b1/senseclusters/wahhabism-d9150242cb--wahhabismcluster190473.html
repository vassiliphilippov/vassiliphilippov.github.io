Wahhabism

noun
conservative movement in Sunni Islam
Pronunciation: / wɑˈhɑːbɪzəm /; / wəˈhɑːbɪzəm / (AmE), / wəˈhɑːbɪzəm /; / wəˈhæbɪzəm / (BrE)
a strict and conservative movement within Sunni Islam, originating in the 18th century and influential in Saudi Arabia
