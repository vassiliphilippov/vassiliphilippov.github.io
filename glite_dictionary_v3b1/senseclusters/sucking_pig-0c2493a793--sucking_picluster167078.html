sucking pig

noun
unweaned piglet
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌkɪŋ pɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈsʌkɪŋ pɪɡ / (BrE)
A young piglet that is still feeding on its mother's milk and has not been weaned.

Examples

A sucking piglet
Farmers usually keep the sucking pigs with the sow until they are weaned.
The chef prepared a traditional roast sucking pig for the banquet.
The market was known for selling fresh sucking pig during the holiday season.
