jogger

noun
person who runs for exercise
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑɡər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒɡə / (BrE)
a person who runs at a steady, moderate pace, usually for exercise

Examples

Morning jogger
Every morning, the park is filled with joggers enjoying the fresh air.
She's a dedicated jogger who completes five kilometers every evening after work.
The jogger slowed down to tie his shoe but quickly resumed his pace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: runner - a person who runs, especially in races
