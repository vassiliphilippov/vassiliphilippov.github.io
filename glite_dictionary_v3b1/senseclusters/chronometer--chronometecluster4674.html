chronometer

noun
highly accurate clock for navigation
Pronunciation: / krəˈnɑː.mə.tər / (AmE), / krəˈnɒm.ɪ.tə / (BrE)
an extremely accurate clock, especially one used for navigation or scientific purposes

Examples

A chronometer watch
A marine chronometer is essential for accurate ocean navigation.
She inherited a nineteenth-century chronometer from her grandfather, who was a naval officer.
The captain relied on a chronometer to determine the ship's longitude at sea.
