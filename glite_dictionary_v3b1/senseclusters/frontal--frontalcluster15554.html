frontal

noun
front part of a building
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌn.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˈfrʌn.tl̩ / (BrE)
the front part or face of a building; the principal exterior side.

Examples

Building frontal
Restorations to the building's frontal revealed its original architectural details.
The frontal was decorated with columns and intricate sculptures.
The ornate frontal of the cathedral drew the attention of many tourists.
