prospect

noun
person considered for a role or honor
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.spɛkt / (AmE), / ˈprɒs.pɛkt / (BrE)
a person who is considered likely to be chosen for a position, prize, honor, or similar opportunity

Examples

A top prospect
Each prospect was interviewed individually by the selection committee.
She was a leading prospect for the scholarship award.
The scouts believe he is the best prospect in this year's draft.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candidate - a person who applies for or is nominated for a job, position, or honor
