arteria metacarpea

noun
artery along metacarpal bones in the hand
9.6
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.ˈtɪr.ɪə met.ə.ˈkɑːr.pi.ə/ (AmE), /ɑː.ˈtɪə.ri.ə met.ə.ˈkɑː.pi.ə/ (BrE)
Any of the arteries in the hand that run along the metacarpal bones, supplying blood to the dorsal or palmar regions.
