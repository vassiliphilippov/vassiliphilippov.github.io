telephone circuit

noun
path for communication in a telephone network
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlɪˌfoʊn ˈsɜrkɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn ˈsɜːkɪt / (BrE)
a connection or path in a telephone network that enables communication between two or more points

Examples

A telephone circuit connection
A dedicated telephone circuit is required for the new fax machine.

The technician repaired the telephone circuit to restore service to the office.
