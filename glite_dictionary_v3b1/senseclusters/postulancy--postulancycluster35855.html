postulancy

noun
trial membership period
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑːs.tjə.lən.siː/ /; / /ˈpɑːs.tə.lən.siː/ (AmE), /ˈpɒs.tjʊ.lən.siː/ /; / /ˈpɒs.tə.lən.siː/ (BrE)
the period when someone is a candidate for membership in a group or organization, especially before joining a religious order

Examples

He reflected on his experiences during his postulancy before taking the next step.
