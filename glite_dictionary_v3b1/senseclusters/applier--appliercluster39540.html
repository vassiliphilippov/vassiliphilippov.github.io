applier

noun
device for applying a substance
Pronunciation: / əˈplaɪɚ / (AmE), / əˈplaɪə / (BrE)
a device used to apply a substance to a surface or object

Examples

Mascara applier
A mascara applier often comes with the product for convenience.

This handy glue applier ensures precise placement without a mess.
