Wikipedian

noun
Wikipedia contributor
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌwɪkɪˈpiːdiən / (AmE), / ˌwɪkɪˈpiːdiən / (BrE)

Examples

Active Wikipedian
The experienced Wikipedian spent hours improving the accuracy of several articles.
Many Wikipedians collaborate online to ensure information is up to date.
As a new Wikipedian, she learned the guidelines before making her first edit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: editor - a person who prepares or revises content for publication
