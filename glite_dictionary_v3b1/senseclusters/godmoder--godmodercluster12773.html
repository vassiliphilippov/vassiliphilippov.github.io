godmoder

noun
chat or game moderator with extra authority
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑdˌmoʊdər / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌməʊdə / (BrE)
A moderator in an online chat or gaming environment with special authority or control over participants.

Examples

The godmoder controls chat
Many respected the godmoder for keeping the chat civil and organized.
The godmoder issued a warning to users who broke the chat rules.
When spam flooded the forum, the godmoder quickly took action.
