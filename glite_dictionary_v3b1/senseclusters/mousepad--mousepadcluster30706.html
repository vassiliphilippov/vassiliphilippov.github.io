mousepad

noun
pad used under a computer mouse
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊsˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈmaʊsˌpad / (BrE)
A small, flat pad placed under a computer mouse to improve its movement and accuracy.

Examples

A mousepad under mouse
She replaced her old mousepad with a new, more comfortable one.
The design on my mousepad matches the rest of my desk accessories.
Using a good mousepad can make your mouse movements much smoother.
