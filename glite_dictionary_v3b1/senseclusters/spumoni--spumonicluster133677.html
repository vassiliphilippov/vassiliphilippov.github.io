spumoni

noun
layered Italian ice cream
7.6
Pronunciation: / spuˈmoʊni / (AmE), / spuˈmɒni / (BrE)
an Italian ice cream dessert made with layers of different flavors, often including fruits and nuts

Examples

A slice of spumoni
After dinner, we enjoyed a slice of spumoni with pistachio, chocolate, and cherry layers.
The restaurant's homemade spumoni was the perfect end to our Italian meal.
He ordered spumoni for dessert because he loves the combination of fruit and nut flavors.

  • synonym: neapolitan - ice cream with three separate flavors in layers
