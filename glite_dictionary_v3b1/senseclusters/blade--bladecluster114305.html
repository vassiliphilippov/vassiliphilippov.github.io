blade

noun
thin, narrow piece (e.g., of lint, grass)
Pronunciation: / bleɪd / (AmE), / bleɪd / (BrE)
a thin, narrow piece of something, especially one resembling a blade of grass.

Examples

Blade of confetti
Each blade of confetti shimmered as it floated to the ground.
He brushed a blade of lint off his jacket before the meeting.
There was a single blade of straw stuck to her boot after the walk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filament - a slender threadlike object or fiber
  • synonym: strand - a single thin length of something such as thread, fiber, or hair
