paparazzi

noun
celebrity photographers
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːpəˈrɑːtsi / (AmE), / ˌpæpəˈrætsi / (BrE)
photographers who follow celebrities to take their pictures, often without their permission

Examples

The paparazzi chase stars
The paparazzi waited outside the hotel for the movie star.
She wore sunglasses to avoid being recognized by the paparazzi at the airport.
Many celebrities complain about the constant attention from paparazzi in public places.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photographer - a person who takes photographs
  • synonym: reporter - a person who reports news for newspapers or broadcasts
