uneconomic

adjective
not economically efficient or profitable
7.5 5.4
Pronunciation: /ə.ne.kə.ˈnɑː.mɪk/ /; / /ʌ.ne.kə.ˈnɑː.mɪk/ (AmE), /ə.ne.kə.ˈnɒm.ɪk/ /; / /ʌ.ne.kə.ˈnɒm.ɪk/ (BrE)
not making efficient or profitable use of resources; not resulting in financial gain or benefit.

Examples

An uneconomic project
Building an airport in such a remote location would be uneconomic.
Extraction of the mineral was halted after it was found to be uneconomic.
The company decided to close the factory because it had become uneconomic to operate.
