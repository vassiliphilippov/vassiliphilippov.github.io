bench

noun
flat shelf of land on a slope
8.5
Pronunciation: / bɛntʃ / (AmE), / bɛntʃ / (BrE)
a flat or gently sloping area of land along a slope or hillside, often forming a terrace with steeper land above and below

Examples

A hillside bench
A narrow bench provided a perfect spot to set up their tents away from the steep drop.
Deposits of ancient soils were visible on the bench midway up the hillside.
The hikers stopped to rest on a bench that overlooked the valley below.
