tsarina

noun
powerful, influential woman
Pronunciation: / z51d0c8rid0n59 / (AmE), / z51d0c8rid0n59 / (BrE)
A (often informal) woman who has great power, authority, or influence in a particular area or organization; a dominant female leader.

Examples

Tech tsarina
After the merger, she was hailed as the tech czarina who transformed the entire industry.
Critics accused the acting mayor of acting like a czarina, making decisions without consulting the council.
Many newspapers called the Nobel laureate a science czarina for reshaping research priorities worldwide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: czar - an authoritative or powerful person (often used figuratively for someone in control of a policy area)
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group, organization, or country
