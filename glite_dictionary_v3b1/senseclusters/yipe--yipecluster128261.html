yipe

verb
to cry out sharply
9.5
Pronunciation: /jaɪp/ (AmE), /jaɪp/ (BrE)
to utter a short, sharp cry, especially from surprise, fear, or excitement

Examples

To yipe loudly
The puppy would yipe whenever it heard a loud noise in the house.
She couldn't help but yipe when the cold water splashed on her feet.
If you sneak up behind him, he might yipe in surprise.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: scream - to make a loud, high-pitched cry
  • antonym: whisper - to speak very softly without using the vocal cords
  • synonym: yelp - to give a short, sharp cry, especially of pain or alarm
