aromatherapy

noun
the use of aromatic oils as therapy
Pronunciation: / əˌroʊməˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / əˌrəʊməˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
a treatment that uses aromatic plant extracts and essential oils for therapeutic benefit, often through massage or baths

Examples

Aromatherapy massage
Many people have turned to aromatherapy to help manage stress and promote relaxation.
She booked an aromatherapy massage featuring lavender and eucalyptus oils.
Some spas offer aromatherapy treatments that claim to boost mood and well-being.
