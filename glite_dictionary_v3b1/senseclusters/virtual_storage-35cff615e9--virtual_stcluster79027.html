Cloud-based or networked data storage provided on demand as a virtualized pool of space, allowing users or organizations to store and retrieve files over the internet.

Examples

Cloud virtual storage
She backed up her vacation photos to virtual storage offered by the cloud provider last night.
Companies move archival data to virtual storage to reduce on-site hardware costs and improve accessibility.
The app syncs new files automatically with virtual storage so users can access documents from any device.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cloud storage - online service that stores data on remote servers accessible via the internet; often scalable and managed by a provider.
  • synonym: remote storage - storage located on another machine or server, accessed over a network rather than locally.
