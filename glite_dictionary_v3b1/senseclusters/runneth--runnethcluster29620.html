runneth

verb
archaic 'runs'
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪθ / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪθ / (BrE)

Examples

The fountain runneth clear
The ancient fountain still runneth clear water after all these years.
His cup runneth over with joy in the old hymn.
The clock in the tower runneth as it did centuries ago.
