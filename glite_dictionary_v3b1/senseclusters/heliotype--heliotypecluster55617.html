heliotype

noun
photographic printing process or its print
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàhiÀêli…ôÀåta…™p / (AmE), / ÀàhiÀêl…™…ô äta…™p / (BrE)

Examples

A heliotype print
