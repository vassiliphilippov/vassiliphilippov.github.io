mortal

noun
human being
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrtəl / (AmE), / ˈmɔːtəl / (BrE)
a human being who is subject to death, as opposed to a god or immortal being

Examples

All mortals
Unlike the gods, all mortals must eventually die.
The legend tells of a mortal who challenged the gods and paid the price.
As mortals, we all have limited time on this earth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: human - a person; a member of the species Homo sapiens
  • antonym: immortal - a being that cannot die
