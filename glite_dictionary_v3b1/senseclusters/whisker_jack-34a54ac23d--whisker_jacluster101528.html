whisker jack

noun
northern North American jay (bird)
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪskər ˌdʒæk / (AmE), / ˈwɪskə ˌdʒæk / (BrE)
A jay of northern North America with a black-capped head and no crest, known for its boldness in stealing food.

Examples

A whisker jack bird
During our camping trip, a whisker jack swooped down and stole a piece of bread from the table.
Spotting a whisker jack is considered a lucky sign by many northern hikers.
The children laughed as a curious whisker jack hopped right up to their picnic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Canada jay - official name for the same bird species (Perisoreus canadensis)
  • synonym: gray jay - another common name for the same bird species
