decisive

adjective
clear and undeniable
4.5 2.7
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈsaɪ.sɪv/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈsaɪ.sɪv/ (BrE)
so clear or obvious that it leaves no doubt or room for question; unmistakable or undeniable.

Examples

Her victory in the debate was secured by a decisive argument that everyone agreed with.
The team held a decisive lead going into the final quarter, making their win almost certain.
