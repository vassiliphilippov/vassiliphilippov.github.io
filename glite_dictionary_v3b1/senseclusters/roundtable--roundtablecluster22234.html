roundtable

noun
peer discussion meeting
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊn(d)ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈraʊn(d)ˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
a meeting where people of equal status discuss and exchange ideas openly, often to reach a consensus or explore different viewpoints

Examples

A roundtable discussion
Experts from various fields participated in the roundtable to share their insights.
She attended a roundtable to discuss strategies for improving workplace diversity.
The conference began with a roundtable on climate change policies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: panel discussion - a formal discussion with selected speakers before an audience
