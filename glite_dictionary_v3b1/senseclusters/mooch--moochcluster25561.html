mooch

verb
ask for and get things for free
8.5 0.4/m
Pronunciation: / muÀêt É / (AmE), / muÀêt É / (BrE)
to ask for and obtain something, especially food, money, or help, without paying or giving anything in return; to act as a parasite

Examples

To mooch a meal
He always tries to mooch free meals from his friends.
She managed to mooch a ride home after the concert.
Stop trying to mooch off my hard work and do something yourself.

Related Terms
