isochronic

adjective
simultaneous timing
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsəˈkrɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈkrɒnɪk / (BrE)
happening at exactly the same time; simultaneous in timing or occurrence

Examples

Isochronic events

The dancers moved in an isochronic pattern, each step matching the others exactly.
