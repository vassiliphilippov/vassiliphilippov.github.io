A chronic progressively worsening disease of the nervous system in which the myelin sheath surrounding nerve fibers in the brain and spinal cord is damaged, often resulting in physical and cognitive disability.

Examples

People with multiple sclerosis may experience muscle weakness, vision problems, and difficulty walking.

  • disseminated multiple sclerosis
