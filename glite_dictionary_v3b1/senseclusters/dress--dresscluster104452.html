Synonyms

  • decorate
    1.9 19/m
    decorate is to add ornaments or color to make something pretty, while dress means arrange or style something to look presentable.
  • dress something up
    3.4 2.04/m
    dress something up is a more casual phrase for making things look special for events, while dress means make items tidy or attractive.
  • trim
    4.1 2.14/m
    trim often means adding small ornaments or neat edges to improve appearance, while dress means arrange or decorate broadly for presentation.
