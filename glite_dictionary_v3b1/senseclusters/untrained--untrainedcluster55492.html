untrained

adjective
not trained; lacking instruction or skill
3.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtreɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtreɪnd / (BrE)
not having received training or instruction; lacking skill, discipline, or experience as a result.

Examples

An untrained voice
Judges could easily recognize an untrained singing voice during auditions.
Many untrained volunteers helped at the shelter during the busy season.
The new recruits were untrained and struggled with the complex equipment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: trained - having received training or instruction
