untrained

adjective
not trained; lacking instruction or skill

not having received training or instruction; lacking skill, discipline, or experience as a result.

Examples

An untrained voice

Synonyms

    7.8 0.34/m
    untutored means not having formal lessons and often learning by oneself, while untrained means not having had training or practice for the job.
  • uninstructed
    6.9 0.05/m
    uninstructed describes people who have not been taught basic methods or rules, while untrained emphasizes lacking formal training or experience.
  • untaught
    6.8 0.05/m
    untaught simply means not taught at all, while untrained suggests lacking specific training and practical skill for the task.
  • unskilled
    2.9 0.52/m
    unskilled lacks the needed ability or skill to do a job, while untrained focuses on not having received training or instruction.
  • inexperienced
    3.4 5.01/m
    inexperienced lacks practical time and exposure to do something well, while untrained stresses absence of formal training or instruction.
