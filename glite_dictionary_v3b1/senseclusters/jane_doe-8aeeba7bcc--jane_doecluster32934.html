Jane Doe

noun
unidentified or anonymous woman
Pronunciation: / dʒeɪn doʊ / (AmE), / dʒeɪn dəʊ / (BrE)
a name used for an unknown or unidentified woman, especially one involved in legal proceedings

Examples

A Jane Doe in court
A Jane Doe filed a lawsuit to protect her identity in court.
Hospitals often use Jane Doe for unidentified female patients.
The police could not identify the victim, so they listed her as Jane Doe.

Related Terms

  • antonym: John Doe - an unknown or fictitious man who is a party to legal proceedings
