subequatorial

adjective
near the equator
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbɪˌkwɑːtɔːriəl /; / ˌsʌbɪˈkwætəriəl / (AmE), / ˌsʌbɪˌkwætɔːriəl /; / ˌsʌbɪˈkwætəriəl / (BrE)
located near, but not directly on, the equator; relating to regions just outside the equatorial zone, often with distinct wet and dry seasons
