Examples

To mold clay
She molded the wax into the shape of a delicate flower.
The artist carefully molded a sculpture out of wet clay.
You can mold the dough into any shape you like before baking.
The glass was heated and then molded to the unique curves of the sculpture.
The yoga pants mold to her body, providing both comfort and support.

Synonyms

  • form
    1.6 17/m
    form means to make or give structure in a general way, while mold usually means shaping by hand or fitting closely to a shape.
  • shape
    2.0 35/m
    shape means to give a particular form for use or appearance, while mold often involves shaping by hand or fitting closely.
  • model
    4.1 18/m
    model means to create a representation often smaller or conceptual, while mold focuses on forming real objects by hand or casting them.
