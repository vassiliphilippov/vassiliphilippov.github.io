neven

noun
marigold plant
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛvɛn / (AmE), / ˈnɛvɛn / (BrE)
a marigold plant (Calendula officinalis), especially as known in South Slavic regions, used for decoration and traditional remedies

Examples

A neven flower
She planted a row of neven along the sunny border for color and scent.
Traditional ointments in the region often use extracts from neven flowers.
The bright orange petals of neven are a common sight in village gardens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marigold - a bright orange or yellow garden flower (Calendula officinalis)
