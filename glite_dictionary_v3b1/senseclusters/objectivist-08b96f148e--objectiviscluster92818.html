Objectivist

noun
follower of Objectivism
Pronunciation: / əbˈdʒɛktɪvɪst / (AmE), / əbˈdʒɛktɪvɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the philosophy of Objectivism, especially as developed by Ayn Rand

Examples

Ayn Rand Objectivist
As an Objectivist, she believes in rational self-interest and individual rights.
The debate featured an Objectivist arguing against government intervention in the economy.
Many Objectivists are inspired by the writings of Ayn Rand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: philosopher - a person engaged in the study of philosophy
  • antonym: subjectivist - a person who believes that knowledge is subjective
