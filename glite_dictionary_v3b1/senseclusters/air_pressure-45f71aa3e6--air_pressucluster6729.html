air pressure

noun
pressure from the atmosphere
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr ˌprɛʃər / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌprɛʃə / (BrE)
the pressure exerted by the weight of the atmosphere on a surface

Examples

Air pressure changes
Changes in air pressure can affect the weather and your comfort.
Low air pressure often signals that a storm is coming.
The airplane cabin is specially designed to maintain safe air pressure for passengers.
