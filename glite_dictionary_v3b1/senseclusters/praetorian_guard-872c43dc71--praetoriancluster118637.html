Praetorian Guard

noun
elite Roman Emperor's bodyguard unit
Pronunciation: / priˈtɔriən ɡɑrd /; / preɪˈtɔriən ɡɑrd / (AmE), / priːˈtɔːrɪən ɡɑːd / (BrE)
