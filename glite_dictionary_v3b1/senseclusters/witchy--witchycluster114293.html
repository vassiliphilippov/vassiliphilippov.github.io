witchy

adjective
magical or mysterious
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtʃi / (AmE), / ˈwɪtʃi / (BrE)
having qualities that are magical, mysterious, or reminiscent of witches

Examples

Witchy black dress
She wore a witchy black dress to the party, complete with a pointed hat.
The forest had a witchy atmosphere, especially when the fog rolled in at dusk.
He gave her a witchy smile that made everyone wonder what he was planning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magical - having the qualities of magic or enchantment
  • synonym: mysterious - difficult to understand or explain; full of mystery
  • antonym: ordinary - not unusual or special; normal
