son of the morning

noun
person symbolizing hope or promise
Pronunciation: / sʌn əv ðə ˈmɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / sʌn əv ðə ˈmɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
a metaphorical expression for a person who brings hope, brightness, or a sense of a promising future

Examples

The son of the morning
In the novel, she described her brother as the son of the morning, always inspiring those around him.
Many believed the young scientist was a true son of the morning in the field of research.
The new mayor was often called the son of the morning for his optimistic vision.
