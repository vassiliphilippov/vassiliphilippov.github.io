trojniak

noun
Polish mead variety (honey and water)
Pronunciation: / ˈtroʊ.njak / (AmE), / ˈtrɔɪ.njak / (BrE)
A traditional Polish mead made by fermenting one part honey with two parts water.

Examples

Traditional trojniak mead
At the cultural festival, we sampled traditional trojniak along with other regional beverages.
He brought a bottle of trojniak back from his trip to Poland as a souvenir.
The distinctive flavor of trojniak comes from its unique balance of honey and water.
