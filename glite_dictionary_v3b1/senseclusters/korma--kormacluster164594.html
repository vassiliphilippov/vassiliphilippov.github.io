korma

noun
creamy South Asian dish
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrmə / (AmE), / ˈkɔːmə / (BrE)
a mild, creamy South Asian curry dish made with meat or vegetables, cooked with yogurt or cream and aromatic spices
