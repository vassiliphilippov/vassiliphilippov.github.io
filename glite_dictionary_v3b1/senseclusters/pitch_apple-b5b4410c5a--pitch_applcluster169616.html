pitch apple

noun
tropical American tree with white or rose flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtʃ ˌæp.əl / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃ ˌæp.əl / (BrE)
a tropical American tree (Clusia rosea) with thick, leathery leaves and solitary white or rose-colored flowers
