mushroom

noun
mushroom-shaped cloud after explosion
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌʃˌruːm, ˈmʌʃˌrʊm / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃ.rʊm, ˈmʌʃ.ruːm / (BrE)
a large, mushroom-shaped cloud of debris and dust rising after an explosion, especially a nuclear bomb

Examples

Mushroom cloud
A giant mushroom rose into the sky after the explosion.
The atomic blast created a mushroom that could be seen for miles around.
The news footage showed the distinctive mushroom forming moments after the detonation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mushroom cloud - a cloud shaped like a mushroom formed after a large explosion, especially nuclear
