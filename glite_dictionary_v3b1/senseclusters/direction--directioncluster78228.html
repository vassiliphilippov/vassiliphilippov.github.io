Synonyms

  • heading
    3.0 0.97/m
    heading shows the direction a vehicle or person is moving or facing, while direction is the general way something points or moves.
  • course
    2.0 51/m
    course is the route or path something follows over time, while direction is the simple line or way it points or moves.
  • orientation
    3.9 8.34/m
    orientation gives the position or alignment of something relative to other things, while direction means the way it points or moves.
  • bearing
    5.0 1.31/m
    bearing shows a measured angle or compass direction often used in navigation, while direction is the general way something faces or moves.
