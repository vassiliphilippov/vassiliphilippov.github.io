untamable

adjective
impossible to control
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈteɪməbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈteɪməbəl / (BrE)
impossible to control, domesticate, or make gentle

Examples

An untamable wild horse

The wild stallion was completely untamable despite the trainer's best efforts.
Some people believe that creativity is an untamable force that cannot be restricted.
The jungle seemed untamable with its dense vegetation and unpredictable wildlife.
