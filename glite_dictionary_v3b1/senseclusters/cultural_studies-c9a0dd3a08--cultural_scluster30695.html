cultural studies

noun
academic field analyzing culture and society
1.0 4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌl.tʃɚ.əl ˈstʌd.iz / (AmE), / ˈkʌl.tʃər.əl ˈstʌd.iz / (BrE)
an interdisciplinary academic field focusing on the analysis of cultural practices, power relations, and social constructs, often drawing from various theoretical perspectives
