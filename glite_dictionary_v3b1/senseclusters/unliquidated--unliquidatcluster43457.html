unliquidated

adjective
not converted to cash
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈlɪkwɪˌdeɪtɪd /; / ʌnˈlɪkwɪˌdeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈlɪkwɪdeɪtɪd /; / ʌnˈlɪkwɪdeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not yet settled, determined, or converted into cash (especially about money, assets, or companies)
