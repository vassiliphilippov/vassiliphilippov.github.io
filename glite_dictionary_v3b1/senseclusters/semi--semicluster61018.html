Synonyms

  • tractor trailer
    3.0 0.95/m
    a tractor trailer is a heavy truck unit that pulls a long trailer, while a semi is the North American name for the same vehicle.
  • trailer truck
    3.2 0.18/m
    a trailer truck is a tractor unit pulling one or more trailers on roads, while a semi is the common North American term for it.
  • articulated lorry
    5.4 0.01/m
    an articulated lorry is the British term for a truck with a jointed trailer, while a semi is the North American name for that vehicle.
  • semitrailer
    4.8 0.09/m
    a semitrailer is the wheeled trailer needing a tractor for support, while a semi in North America can mean that trailer or the whole truck.
  • trucking rig
    4.6 0.00/m
    a trucking rig is a large tractor and trailer joined for long hauls, while a semi is the North American short name for such rigs.
  • rig
    5.5 2.92/m
    a rig often means any large truck or vehicle used for hauling, while a semi in North America specifically names the truck or its trailer.
