semi

noun
trailer with rear wheels, front supported by vehicle
8.1 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈse.miː/ /; / /ˈse.maɪ/ (AmE), /ˈse.miː/ (BrE)
(North American) a large truck for transporting goods, or the trailer part of such a truck

Examples

A semi truck
A fleet of semis lined up at the loading dock, waiting to be filled with cargo.
The truck driver attached the semi to his cab before beginning his cross-country journey.
They transported the heavy machinery using a large semi rented from a local hauling company.
After driving the semi all night, the trucker finally stopped for coffee at dawn.
Construction crews had to reroute traffic when a semi overturned on the interstate.
The semi rumbled down the highway, hauling a load of produce to the next city.
