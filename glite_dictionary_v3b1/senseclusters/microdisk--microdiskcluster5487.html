microdisk

noun
small data storage disk
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌdɪsk / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌdɪsk / (BrE)
a small, flat disk used for storing digital data, especially in computers

Examples

Computer microdisk
In the 1980s, many computer programs were sold on a microdisk.
She found an old microdisk containing backup files from her first computer.
The microdisk could store much less data than modern USB drives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compact disc - a small optical disc used to store digital data
  • synonym: floppy disk - a thin, flexible magnetic storage disk for computers
