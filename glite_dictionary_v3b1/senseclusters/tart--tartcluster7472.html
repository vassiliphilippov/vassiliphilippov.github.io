tart

adjective
having a sharp, sour taste
Pronunciation: / tɑrt / (AmE), / tɑːt / (BrE)
having a sharp or sour taste, similar to that of a lemon or unripe fruit

Examples

Tart lemon taste
She prefers tart apples to sweet ones for baking pies.
The lemonade was too tart for my taste, so I added more sugar.
The sauce had a pleasantly tart flavor that complemented the fish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sour - having an acidic taste like lemon or vinegar
  • antonym: sweet - having the taste characteristic of sugar or honey
