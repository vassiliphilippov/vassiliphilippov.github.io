Bambi

noun
naive or timid person
Pronunciation: / ˈbæmbi / (AmE), / ˈbæmbi / (BrE)
an inexperienced, naive, or easily frightened person, especially someone new to a situation

Examples

A Bambi at work
When she started her first job, she felt like a total Bambi in the office.
The coach could tell he was a Bambi on the field, nervous and unsure of himself.
Don't worry if you seem like a Bambi at first; everyone learns with time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: novice - a person new to and inexperienced in a job or situation
  • synonym: rookie - a person who is new to an activity or job
  • antonym: veteran - a person with long experience in a particular field
