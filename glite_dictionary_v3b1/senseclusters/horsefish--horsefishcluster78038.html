horsefish

noun
a type of flat-bodied marine fish
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a name for several species of silvery marine fish with very flat bodies, often used for certain pipefish or sea horses
