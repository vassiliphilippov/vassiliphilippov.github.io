boot camp

noun
military training camp for recruits
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːt kæmp / (AmE), / ˈbuːt kæmp / (BrE)
a camp where new members of the armed forces receive intensive basic training

Examples

Military boot camp
After graduating from high school, he enlisted in the Marines and went straight to boot camp.
She wrote letters home every week describing her experiences at boot camp.
The recruits found boot camp both physically and mentally challenging.
