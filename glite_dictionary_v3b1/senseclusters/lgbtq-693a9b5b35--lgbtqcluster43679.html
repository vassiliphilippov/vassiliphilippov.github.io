LGBTQ

noun
the community of non-heterosexual and/or non-cisgender people
3.5 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Àå…õl d íiÀê biÀê tiÀê ÀàkjuÀê / (AmE), / Àå…õl d íiÀê biÀê tiÀê ÀàkjuÀê / (BrE)
