daycare

noun
daytime care for adults
1.4
Pronunciation: /ˈdeɪ.keər/ /; / /ˈdeɪ.kər/ (AmE), /ˈdeɪ.keə/ /; / /ˈdeɪ.kə/ (BrE)
A daytime service or facility that provides supervision, social activities, and basic support for adults who need care, such as older people or people with disabilities.

Examples

Adult daycare service
Every weekday, her father goes to daycare where staff lead exercises and social activities.
The family enrolled him in daycare for dementia patients to give caregivers a regular break.
Local health services offer daycare programs that provide meals, transport, and basic nursing support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adult day care - a program providing daytime supervision, activities, and support for adults with care needs
  • synonym: respite care - short-term care provided to give a regular caregiver temporary relief
