bereaved person

noun
someone grieving a loved one's death
6.7 0.18/m
Pronunciation: / b…ôÀàrivd Ààp…úrs…ôn / (AmE), / b…™ÀàriÀêvd Ààp…úÀês…ôn / (BrE)
someone who has suffered the death of a loved one; a person experiencing mourning or grief due to a personal loss.

Examples

A bereaved person grieving
Flowers and messages often offer little solace to a bereaved person in the first days of grief.
Support groups for the bereaved person can provide comfort and community during difficult times.
The counselor spent hours supporting the bereaved person after their unexpected loss.

Synonyms

  • bereaved
    7.8 0.12/m
    bereaved is a short term often used in formal writing for someone who lost a loved one, while bereaved person is a clearer everyday phrase.
  • griever
    7.2 0.06/m
    griever is someone actively feeling deep sorrow after a loss, while bereaved person names anyone who suffers the death of a loved one.
  • mourner
    4.0 2.99/m
    mourner attends funerals or shows grief publicly, while bereaved person refers to anyone mourning a death privately or publicly.
