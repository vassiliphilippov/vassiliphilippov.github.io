vicenary

adjective
relating to twenty
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪsəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈvɪsənəri / (BrE)
Relating to the number twenty; based on, consisting of, or grouped in twenties.

Examples

A vicenary system
A vicenary system is less common than a decimal system, but still used in some cultures.
The ancient counting system was vicenary, grouping numbers in sets of twenty.
The language uses vicenary numerals, making calculations quite unique.
