majorly

adverb
to a great extent
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdʒərli / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdʒəli / (BrE)
in a very significant or extreme way; to a great extent, used informally.

Examples

Majorly disappointed
I was majorly disappointed when the concert was canceled at the last minute.
He majorly underestimated how difficult the exam would be.
The new policy has majorly improved working conditions for employees.
