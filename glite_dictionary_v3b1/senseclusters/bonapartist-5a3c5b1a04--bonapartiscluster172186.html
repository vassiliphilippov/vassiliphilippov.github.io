Bonapartist

adjective
of Napoleon Bonaparte
Pronunciation: / ˌboʊnəˈpɑːrtɪst / (AmE), / ˌbəʊnəˈpɑːtɪst / (BrE)
relating to Napoleon Bonaparte, his family, or the political movement and style of government associated with him

Examples

Bonapartist government style
The country adopted a Bonapartist system that emphasized strong central leadership.
Historians debated whether the reforms were truly Bonapartist in nature or simply authoritarian.
Many veterans supported the Bonapartist ideals after the fall of the monarchy.
