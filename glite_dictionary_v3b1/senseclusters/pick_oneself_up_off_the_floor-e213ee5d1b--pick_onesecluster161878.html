pick oneself up off the floor

verb
recover after a setback or failure
2.4
Pronunciation: / pɪk wʌnˈsɛlf ʌp ɔf ðə flɔr / (AmE), / pɪk wʌnˈsɛlf ʌp ɒf ðə flɔː / (BrE)
