Partial or total loss of the ability to hear in one or both ears.

Examples

Hearing impairment in children
Early detection of hearing impairment in children can greatly improve their language development.
She uses special devices at work to help manage her hearing impairment.
The conference offered resources for people living with hearing impairment and their families.

Synonyms

  • hearing loss
    2.7 2.17/m
    hearing loss means a partial or total inability to hear, while hearing impairment is a medical term for reduced hearing.
  • deafness
    2.7 1.06/m
    deafness usually means complete or very severe inability to hear, while hearing impairment covers partial or total loss.
  • hearing disorder
    3.3 0.02/m
    hearing disorder is a clinical label for many ear problems that reduce hearing, while hearing impairment specifically names loss of hearing.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: deafness - complete or severe loss of hearing
