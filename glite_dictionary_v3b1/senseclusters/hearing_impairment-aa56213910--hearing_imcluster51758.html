Synonyms

  • hearing loss
    2.7 0.6/m
    hearing loss means a partial or total inability to hear, while hearing impairment is a medical term for reduced hearing.
  • deafness
    2.7 0.5/m
    deafness usually means complete or very severe inability to hear, while hearing impairment covers partial or total loss.
  • hearing disorder
    3.3 0.01/m
    hearing disorder is a clinical label for many ear problems that reduce hearing, while hearing impairment specifically names loss of hearing.
