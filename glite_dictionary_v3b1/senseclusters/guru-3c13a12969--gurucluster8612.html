Guru

noun
Sikh spiritual leader; one of the first ten leaders of Sikhism
6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈguː.ruː/ /; / /ˈgʊ.ruː/ /; / /ˈgə.ruː/ (AmE), /ˈguː.ruː/ /; / /ˈgʊ.ruː/ /; / /ˈgə.ruː/ (BrE)

Examples

The Guru Granth Sahib
