morsel

noun
small piece or mouthful of food
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔr.səl / (AmE), / ˈmɔː.səl / (BrE)
a small piece or amount of something, especially food

Examples

A morsel of food
He offered the stray dog a morsel from his sandwich.
Not a single morsel of food was wasted at the picnic.
She took a delicate morsel of cake and savored the taste.
He savored every morsel of the delicious chocolate cake.
She offered her dog a morsel of cheese as a treat.
There was not a single morsel of evidence to support the claim.
