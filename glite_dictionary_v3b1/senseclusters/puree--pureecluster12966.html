puree

noun
smooth, blended food preparation
6.7 2.5
Pronunciation: / pjʊˈreɪ / (AmE), / ˈpjʊəreɪ / (BrE)
a smooth food made by cooking and then crushing or blending fruit, vegetables, or other ingredients until soft and smooth

Examples

Apple puree
She spooned some apple puree into the baby's bowl for breakfast.
The chef prepared a delicious carrot puree to accompany the roasted meat.
You can freeze leftover tomato puree for later use in soups or sauces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mash - food that has been crushed into a soft mass, especially potatoes
