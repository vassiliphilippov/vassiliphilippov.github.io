contact arm

noun
rotating conducting arm in switches
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.tækt ɑrm / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tækt ɑːm / (BrE)
a conducting arm that rotates over a set of fixed contacts and comes to rest on an outlet, typically used in rotary switches or similar mechanisms

Examples

The contact arm rotates
If the contact arm is misaligned, the switch may not function correctly.
The contact arm rotates and connects the circuit to different outputs.
The technician replaced the worn contact arm in the rotary selector.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wiper - a movable contact in electrical switches or potentiometers
