patcher

noun
circuit connection device
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpætʃ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈpætʃ.ə/ (BrE)
a device or piece of equipment used to create temporary connections between electronic, audio, or telecommunications circuits

A new patcher was installed to improve the flexibility of the telecommunications system.
