patcher

noun
circuit connection device
Pronunciation: / ˈpætʃər / (AmE), / ˈpætʃə / (BrE)
a device or piece of equipment used to create temporary connections between electronic, audio, or telecommunications circuits

Examples

Audio patcher device
The technician used a patcher to quickly reroute the audio signals during the concert.
After the old patcher failed, the studio experienced several connection issues.
A new patcher was installed to improve the flexibility of the telecommunications system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patch bay - a panel for connecting and routing audio or electronic signals
  • synonym: switchboard - a device for connecting telephone calls or circuits
