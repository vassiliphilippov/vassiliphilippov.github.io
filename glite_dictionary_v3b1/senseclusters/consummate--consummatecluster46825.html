consummate

adjective
complete or perfect; highly skilled
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnsəmət /; / ˈkɑnsəˌmeɪt, kənˈsʌmɪt /; / ˈkɑnsəˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsjʊmət /; / ˈkɒnsjʊmət, kənˈsʌmɪt / (BrE)
showing the highest degree of skill, quality, or perfection; complete in every way
