mousiness

noun
signs of mice
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊsinəs / (AmE), / ˈmaʊsinɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being infested with mice or showing signs of mice, such as droppings or a musty smell

Examples

Mousiness in the attic
The mousiness of the old barn was clear from the smell and scattered droppings.
Despite her efforts to clean, a sense of mousiness lingered in the basement.
They called pest control after noticing increasing mousiness in the pantry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cleanliness - the state of being clean and free from dirt or pests
  • synonym: infestation - the presence of large numbers of unwanted animals or insects
