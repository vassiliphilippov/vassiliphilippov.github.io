kildeer

noun
a North American plover with a loud call
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪlˌdɪr / (AmE), / ˈkɪldəː / (BrE)
A North American plover (Charadrius vociferus) found near inland waters and fields, known for its loud, distinctive call.

Examples

A kildeer bird
A pair of kildeers ran swiftly through the field at sunset.
The loud, piercing call of the kildeer echoed across the wetlands.
We spotted a kildeer protecting its nest by pretending to have a broken wing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plover - a type of wading bird with a short bill and long legs
