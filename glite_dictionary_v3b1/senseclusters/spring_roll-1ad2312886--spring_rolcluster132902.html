spring roll

noun
minced vegetables and meat fried in a thin wrapper
6.7 2.2
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ roʊl / (AmE), / sprɪŋ rəʊl / (BrE)
A small cylindrical pastry filled with minced vegetables and sometimes meat, wrapped in a thin dough and fried, commonly served as an appetizer in East Asian cuisine.

Examples

A plate of spring rolls
I ordered a plate of crispy spring rolls to start our meal.
She learned how to make homemade spring rolls filled with shrimp and vegetables.
The restaurant serves delicious vegetarian spring rolls with a tangy dipping sauce.
