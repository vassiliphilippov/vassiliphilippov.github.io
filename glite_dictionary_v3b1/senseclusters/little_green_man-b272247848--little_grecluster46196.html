little green man

noun
unmarked soldier used for deniable operations
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪt.əl griːn mæn/ (AmE), /ˈlɪt.əl griːn mæn/ (BrE)
(informal, politics/military) an unmarked soldier or paramilitary fighter in green uniform, deployed so the sponsoring power can deny responsibility.

Examples

Unmarked little green men in uniform
During the crisis, locals reported sightings of unidentified little green men near the border checkpoints.
Western governments accused the country of deploying little green men to seize strategic buildings while denying involvement.
The photo showed a single masked little green man carrying a rifle and no insignia on his uniform.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paramilitary - an organized group that is not part of a country's official military but uses military-style structure and tactics
  • synonym: unmarked soldier - a member of the armed forces without insignia or identifying markings
