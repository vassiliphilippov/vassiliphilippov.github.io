merchant marine

noun
commercial shipping fleet of a country
5.2 3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈmɜːr.tʃənt mə.ˈriːn/ (AmE), /ˈmɜː.tʃənt mə.ˈriːn/ (BrE)
The commercial ships of a country that are used for transporting goods and passengers, rather than for military purposes.

Examples
