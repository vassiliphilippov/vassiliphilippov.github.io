(of a political body or region) having the right or power to govern itself without outside control.

Examples

A self-governing territory
After years of negotiation, the island became a self-governing territory within the commonwealth.
Greenland has a self-governing parliament that manages most of its domestic affairs.
Many indigenous communities demand the right to be self-governing in matters of local law and education.

Synonyms

  • independent
    2.8 23/m
    independent means a country or group is free from outside rule and acts alone, while self-governing means it controls its own affairs.
  • sovereign
    7.0 7.15/m
    sovereign is a state with full independent authority recognized by others, while self-governing emphasizes governing itself without outside control.
  • autonomous
    6.3 8.37/m
    autonomous means having local freedom to make decisions, often inside a larger state, while self-governing means full control over its own governance.
