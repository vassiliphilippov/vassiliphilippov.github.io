self-governing

adjective
having autonomy; governing itself
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈɡʌvərnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈɡʌvənɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a political body or region) having the right or power to govern itself without outside control.

Examples

A self-governing territory
After years of negotiation, the island became a self-governing territory within the commonwealth.
Greenland has a self-governing parliament that manages most of its domestic affairs.
Many indigenous communities demand the right to be self-governing in matters of local law and education.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autonomous - having self-government, acting independently
  • synonym: sovereign - having supreme authority within a territory
