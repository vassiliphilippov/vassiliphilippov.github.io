pituitary body

noun
the endocrine gland at the base of the brain
Pronunciation: / pɪˈtuː.əˌtɛr.i ˈbɑː.di / (AmE), / pɪˈtjuː.ɪˌtɛə.ri ˈbɒd.i / (BrE)
the small, oval endocrine gland at the base of the brain that regulates other glands in the endocrine system
