brewmaster

noun
beer expert
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈbruːˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who is highly skilled in making beer, especially one in charge of a brewery

Examples

The brewmaster selects hops
The brewmaster carefully selected the hops for the new craft beer.
After years of training, she became the head brewmaster at the local brewery.
Visitors enjoyed a tour led by the experienced brewmaster who explained the brewing process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brewer - a person who makes beer
