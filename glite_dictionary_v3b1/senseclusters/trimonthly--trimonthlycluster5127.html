trimonthly

adjective
every three months
Pronunciation: / traɪˈmʌnθli / (AmE), / traɪˈmʌnθli / (BrE)
happening every three months or occurring three times in a month, depending on context

Examples

Trimonthly meetings
The magazine is published on a trimonthly basis, so there are four issues each year.
Our team holds trimonthly meetings to review progress and set new goals.
The trimonthly reports help us track changes in customer satisfaction over time.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: annual - happening once a year
  • degree higher: monthly - happening every month
  • synonym: quarterly - happening every three months
