Moctezuma II

proper noun
Aztec emperor killed by Cortés
Pronunciation: / ˌmoʊktəˈzuːmə ðə ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˌmɒktəˈzuːmə ðə ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
the last Aztec emperor of Mexico, overthrown and killed by Hernán Cortés in 1520

Examples

Moctezuma II emperor
