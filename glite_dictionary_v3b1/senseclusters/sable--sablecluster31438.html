sable

noun
mammal with luxurious dark fur
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈseɪbəl / (BrE)
a small mammal (Martes zibellina) of northern Asian forests, valued for its soft, dark brown fur

Examples

Wild sable
The sable is prized for its rich, dark brown fur.
The luxurious coat was lined with sable to provide warmth in the winter.
Traders once crossed vast distances to collect rare sable pelts.
