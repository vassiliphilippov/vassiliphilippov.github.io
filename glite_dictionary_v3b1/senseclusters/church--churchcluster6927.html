church

noun
congregation; church members
1.0 39/m
Pronunciation: / t É…ùt É / (AmE), / t É…úÀêt É / (BrE)
the group of people who regularly attend or belong to a specific Christian congregation

Examples

The church gathers
Every Sunday, the entire church gathers for morning worship.
She has been a dedicated member of her local church for over twenty years.
The church organized a charity fundraiser for the community.

Synonyms

  • congregation
    3.7 16/m
    congregation is a group who meet for worship in one place, while church means the people who belong to that same group.
  • fold
    7.5 3.00/m
    fold is an older or poetic word for a group of believers who meet, while church names the people who belong to that congregation.
  • parish
    4.1 15/m
    parish is a local church community or area with its own boundaries and services, while church means the people who attend it.
