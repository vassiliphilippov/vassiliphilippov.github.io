Edomite

adjective
of ancient Edom
Pronunciation: / ˈiːdəˌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈiːdəˌmaɪt / (BrE)
relating to Edom, an ancient kingdom in the region south of the Dead Sea, or its people, culture, or language

Examples

Edomite pottery
The museum displayed several Edomite artifacts from the Iron Age.
Researchers studied Edomite inscriptions to learn more about the region's history.
The Edomite language is known from a few ancient texts and inscriptions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Israelite - relating to the ancient people of Israel
  • synonym: Moabite - relating to Moab, an ancient kingdom near Edom
