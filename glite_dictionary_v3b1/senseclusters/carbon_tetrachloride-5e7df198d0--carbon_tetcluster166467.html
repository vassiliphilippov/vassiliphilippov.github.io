carbon tetrachloride

noun
toxic, colorless chemical compound
3.1 6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.bən tet.rə.ˈklɔːr.aɪd/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.bən tet.rə.ˈklɔː.raɪd/ (BrE)
