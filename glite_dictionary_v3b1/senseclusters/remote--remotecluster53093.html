Synonyms

  • distant
    2.8 20/m
    distant is far away in space and used for physical distance, while remote often suggests being far from people or cities.
  • faraway
    2.8 3/m
    faraway sounds more poetic and describes a place far in space or time, while remote is neutral and practical.
  • far-off
    4.2 2/m
    far-off describes something very distant or in the distant past, while remote focuses on being away from people or cities.
  • outlying
    5.0 0.4/m
    outlying describes areas far from a central place or city, while remote highlights distance from people or towns.
  • far
    1.0 20/m
    far means at a great distance in space or time, while remote often adds the idea of being away from people or cities.
  • isolated
    3.8 5/m
    isolated is cut off and alone with little contact, while remote just means far away from cities or people.
  • far-flung
    6.3 2/m
    far-flung describes places spread over a wide area and far apart, while remote simply means distant from people or towns.
  • out of the way
    4.0 0.4/m
    out of the way is a phrase meaning in a secluded or inconvenient place, while remote focuses on being distant from people.
