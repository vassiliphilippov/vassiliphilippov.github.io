    4.2 2.77/m

  • outlying

  • far
    1.0 23/m
    far means at a great distance in space or time, while remote often adds the idea of being away from people or cities.
  • isolated
    3.8 4.60/m
    isolated is cut off and alone with little contact, while remote just means far away from cities or people.
  • far-flung
    6.3 1.91/m
