working dog

noun
dog bred for practical tasks
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkɪŋ dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkɪŋ dɒɡ / (BrE)
A dog bred and trained to perform practical tasks such as guarding, guiding, herding, or pulling loads.

Examples

Working dog breeds
Guide dogs are a type of working dog trained to assist the visually impaired.
Many working dogs require plenty of exercise and mental stimulation.
The farm relied on a working dog to help round up the sheep.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: herding dog - a dog bred to help herd livestock
  • synonym: service dog - a dog trained to assist people with disabilities
