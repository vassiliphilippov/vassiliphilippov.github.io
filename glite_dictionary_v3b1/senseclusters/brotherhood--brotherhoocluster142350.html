brotherhood

noun
group sharing a profession or interest
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌðərˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈbrʌðəhʊd / (BrE)
a group of people sharing a common profession, interest, or purpose, often seen as having close relationships or a sense of camaraderie

Examples

Medical brotherhood
Many doctors feel a strong sense of brotherhood with their colleagues.
She was welcomed into the brotherhood of engineers after passing her final exams.
The brotherhood of firefighters held a ceremony for their retiring chief.
