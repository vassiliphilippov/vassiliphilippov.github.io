Synonyms

  • tattered
    4.1 0.4/m
    tattered shows many old tears and loose threads on fabric, while ragged means clothes or people have rough, hanging edges.
  • tatty
    6.0 0.1/m
    tatty looks faded, shabby, and worn from use, while ragged implies torn edges and a more damaged appearance.
  • ratty
    5.4 1.0/m
