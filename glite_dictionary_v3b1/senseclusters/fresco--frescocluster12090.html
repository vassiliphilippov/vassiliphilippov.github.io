fresco

verb
paint onto wet plaster (art technique)
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛs.koʊ / (AmE), / ˈfrɛs.kəʊ / (BrE)
to paint a picture or design onto wet plaster on a wall or ceiling, using the fresco technique

Examples

To fresco a ceiling
Historical buildings often feature beautifully frescoed ceilings from the Renaissance period.
She spent months frescoing the dome with images of the heavens.
The artist decided to fresco an elaborate scene on the chapel's main wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mural - a large painting applied directly to a wall or ceiling
