newscasting

noun
news presentation
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzˌkæstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌkɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or profession of presenting news on television, radio, or other media as a broadcast program

  • synonym: broadcasting - the transmission of programs or information by radio or television
  • synonym: journalism - the activity of gathering, assessing, and presenting news
  • synonym: news broadcast - a program in which news is reported to the public
