gooseflesh

noun
bumps on skin due to cold or emotion
4.2 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈguːs.fleʃ/ (AmE), /ˈguːs.fleʃ/ (BrE)
small, raised bumps on the skin caused by the involuntary contraction of muscles at the base of hair follicles, typically in response to cold, fear, or excitement; also known as goosebumps.

Examples

Gooseflesh on skin
He shivered, feeling gooseflesh rise on his arms as the wind blew.
The chilling story gave her gooseflesh despite the warm room.
Whenever she spoke in public, gooseflesh appeared along her skin.
