alpenhorn

noun
Swiss wooden horn
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈælpənˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈælpənˌhɔːn / (BrE)
a long wooden horn traditionally played by Alpine herders, especially in Switzerland, to communicate across mountains

Examples

The alpenhorn sound
The musician played the alpenhorn at the festival, filling the valley with its deep sound.
Tourists gathered to watch a demonstration of the traditional alpenhorn in the Swiss Alps.
The echo of the alpenhorn could be heard from miles away in the mountains.
