harvestfish

noun
an Atlantic species of butterfish
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrvɪstˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːvɪstˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a species of butterfish, up to about a foot long, found in Atlantic waters from Chesapeake Bay to Argentina
