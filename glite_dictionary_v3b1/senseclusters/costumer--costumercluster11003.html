costumer

noun
person who makes or supplies costumes
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːstjuːmər / (AmE), / ˈkɒstjuːmə / (BrE)
a person who designs, makes, or supplies costumes, especially for plays, movies, or masquerades

Examples

The costumer designs costumes
Our school's costumer spent weeks preparing outfits for the musical.
The movie's elaborate outfits were praised, thanks to the work of their lead costumer.
The theater hired a talented costumer to create period clothing for the play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: costume designer - person who designs costumes for a production
