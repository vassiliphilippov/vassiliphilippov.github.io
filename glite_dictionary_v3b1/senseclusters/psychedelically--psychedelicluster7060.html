psychedelically

adverb
in a psychedelic manner
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkəˈdɛlɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈdɛlɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is bright, surreal, or like a psychedelic experience (with vivid colors or unusual effects)

She dressed psychedelically for the party, wearing clothes with bold, colorful patterns.
The film was psychedelically edited, creating a dreamlike and unusual atmosphere.
The stage was psychedelically lit, with swirling patterns and bright neon colors everywhere.
The artist painted the mural psychedelically, using bold shapes and unexpected color combinations.
The movie's visuals shifted psychedelically during the dream sequence, making everything look unreal.
