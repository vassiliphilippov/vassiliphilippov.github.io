storminess

noun
intense turbulence or passion in behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔrminəs / (AmE), / ˈstɔːminɪs / (BrE)
violent or turbulent passion, especially in speech or behavior; the quality of being marked by intense emotion or outburst.

Examples

Emotional storminess
His storminess in meetings sometimes intimidated his colleagues.
She regretted the storminess that had taken over her during the argument.
The storminess of the debate caught everyone by surprise.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmness - the state of being free from agitation or strong emotion
  • synonym: passion - strong and barely controllable emotion
