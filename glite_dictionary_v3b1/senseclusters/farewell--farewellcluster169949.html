farewell

verb
to say goodbye
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛrˈwɛl / (AmE), / ˌfeəˈwɛl / (BrE)
to say goodbye to someone, often in a formal or ceremonial way

Examples

To farewell a friend
The team gathered to farewell their coach at the end of the season.
We will farewell our friends before they move to another country.
A special dinner was held to farewell the retiring professor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: greet - to welcome or say hello to someone
  • synonym: part - to leave or say goodbye to someone
