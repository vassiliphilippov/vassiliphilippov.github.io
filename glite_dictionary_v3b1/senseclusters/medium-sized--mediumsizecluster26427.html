medium-sized

adjective
of intermediate size
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːdiəm saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiəm saɪzd / (BrE)
Of intermediate size; neither large nor small.

Examples

A medium-sized coffee
I'd like a medium-sized coffee, please.
She adopted a medium-sized dog from the animal shelter last week.
They own a medium-sized company that employs about fifty people.
