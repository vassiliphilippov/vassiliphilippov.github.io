homogenization

noun
making uniform or consistent
9.9
Pronunciation: / həˌmɑdʒənəˈzeɪʃən /; / həˌmɑːdʒənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / həˌmɒdʒɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / hɒˌmɒdʒənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something uniform or consistent throughout in composition or character

Examples

Homogenization process
Critics argue that media homogenization leads to a lack of diversity in content.
Globalization has contributed to the cultural homogenization of cities around the world.
The homogenization of milk ensures that the cream does not separate from the rest of the liquid.
Milk undergoes homogenisation to ensure an even texture and taste.
The homogenisation of products across global markets can diminish local cultures.
There are concerns about the homogenisation of news coverage among different media outlets.

  • synonym: homogenization - the American spelling of 'homogenisation'
  • synonym: uniformity - the state of being uniform or alike throughout
