half-breed

adjective
animal with one purebred parent
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhæfˌbrid / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfˌbriːd / (BrE)
(of animals) having only one purebred parent; not of pure blood on both sides.

Examples

Half-breed puppy
At the show, only purebreds were allowed to compete, so the half-breed dogs could not enter.
Farmers sometimes prefer half-breed animals for certain tasks due to their hybrid vigor.
The half-breed puppy inherited traits from both its Labrador father and its mixed-breed mother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crossbreed - animal or plant resulting from crossing different breeds or varieties
  • antonym: purebred - animal with parents of the same recognized breed
