Martin of Tours

proper noun
French bishop and patron saint (died 397)
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrtɪn əv tʊrz / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtɪn əv tʊəz / (BrE)
(c. 316–397) A French bishop and Christian saint, regarded as the patron saint of France.

Examples

Martin of Tours saint
Martin of Tours is celebrated for his acts of charity and his role in spreading Christianity across Gaul.
