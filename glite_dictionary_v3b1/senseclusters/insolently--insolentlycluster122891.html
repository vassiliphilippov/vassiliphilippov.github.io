insolently

adverb
in a rude or disrespectful way
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.sə.lənt.li / (AmE), / ˈɪn.səl.ənt.li / (BrE)
In a rude or disrespectful way, showing a lack of respect for authority or others.

Examples

To speak insolently

She insolently dismissed his advice as irrelevant.
The student insolently interrupted the teacher during the lecture.
