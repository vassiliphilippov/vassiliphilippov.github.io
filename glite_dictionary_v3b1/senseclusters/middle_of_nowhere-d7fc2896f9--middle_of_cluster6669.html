middle of nowhere

noun
a very remote or isolated place
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪd.əl əv ˈnoʊ.wɛr / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.əl əv ˈnəʊ.weə / (BrE)
a place that is very remote, isolated, or far from towns and cities

Examples

In the middle of nowhere place
It felt like we were hiking through the middle of nowhere with no one else around.
She grew up in the middle of nowhere, miles away from the nearest town.
We ended up stranded in the middle of nowhere when our car broke down.

Related Terms

  • synonym: the back of beyond - an extremely remote place; another idiomatic phrase for isolation
