haphazard

adverb
done without order or planning
9.6
Pronunciation: / hæpˈhæzɚd / (AmE), / hæpˈhæzəd / (BrE)
In a manner lacking any obvious principle or order; without care or planning.

Examples

Thrown haphazard
She threw her clothes haphazard into the suitcase, not bothering to fold them.
The books were piled haphazard on the desk, with no apparent order.
The paintings hung haphazard across the wall, creating an unplanned yet interesting display.

Related Terms

  • synonym: randomly - without method or conscious decision
  • antonym: systematically - using a method or system
