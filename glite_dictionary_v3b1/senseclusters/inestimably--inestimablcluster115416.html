inestimably

adverb
immeasurably
8.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɛstɪməbli / (AmE), / ɪˈnɛstɪməbli / (BrE)
to a degree that is too great to be measured or calculated

Examples

An inestimably great value
The scientist's discovery was inestimably important for the advancement of medicine.
His kindness has been inestimably helpful to everyone in the community.
The value of clean water is inestimably high in many parts of the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immeasurably - to an extent that cannot be measured
  • synonym: incalculably - to a degree that cannot be calculated
  • antonym: slightly - to a small degree or extent
