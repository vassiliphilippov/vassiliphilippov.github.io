quixotically

adverb
in an idealistic or impractical way
Pronunciation: / kwɪkˈsɑːtɪkli / (AmE), / kwɪkˈsɒtɪkli / (BrE)
In an extremely idealistic, unrealistic, or impractical manner.

Examples

To quixotically try
He quixotically tried to reform the entire education system on his own.
She quixotically donated all her savings to a cause few believed in.
They quixotically challenged the corporation despite overwhelming odds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quixotic - extremely idealistic, unrealistic, and impractical
