pressure

noun
force per unit area
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛʃər /; / ˈprɛʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃə / (BrE)
the force applied to a surface, especially in science or when talking about air or water

Examples

Air pressure
Atmospheric pressure affects how water boils at different elevations.

The pressure inside the tank rose sharply as more gas was added.
At higher altitudes, the atmospheric pressure is lower than at sea level.
Changes in pressure can affect the weather dramatically.
The instrument is used to measure air pressure in the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stress - physical force or pressure applied to an object
  • synonym: tension - the state of being stretched tight; force that pulls apart
