inlay

verb
decorate by embedding contrasting material
6.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈleɪ /; / ˈɪnˌleɪ / (AmE), / ɪnˈleɪ /; / ˈɪn.leɪ / (BrE)
to decorate a surface by embedding pieces of a different material into it

Examples

To inlay gold
She is inlaying silver threads into the jewelry box lid as a finishing touch.
The artist decided to inlay the tabletop with pieces of colored glass.
They inlaid the wooden floor with a beautiful geometric pattern.
She plans to inlay the floor tiles with intricate patterns for added beauty.
The craftsmen will inlay mother-of-pearl into the wooden box for an elegant finish.
The jeweler expertly inlaid the ring with small emeralds.
