ungarnished

adjective
not legally withheld
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡɑrnɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡɑːnɪʃt / (BrE)
not subject to legal garnishment or attachment, especially referring to wages or bank accounts that have not been withheld by court order

Examples

Ungarnished wages
The employee was relieved to see his ungarnished wages deposited in his account.
Because her bank account remained ungarnished, she could access all her savings freely.
He was grateful that his income stayed ungarnished after resolving the debt dispute.

Related Terms

  • antonym: garnished - having money legally withheld by court order
