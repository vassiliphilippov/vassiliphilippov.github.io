eccentricity

noun
strange or unconventional behavior
Pronunciation: / ˌɛk.sɛnˈtrɪs.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌɛk.sɛnˈtrɪs.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality of being unconventional or showing strange or unusual behavior

Examples

His eccentricity stands out
His eccentricity was evident in the mismatched shoes he wore to every meeting.
Many artists are known for their eccentricity and unexpected habits.
She regarded his eccentricity as charming rather than bothersome.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normality - the state of being normal or typical
  • synonym: oddity - a strange or peculiar person or thing
