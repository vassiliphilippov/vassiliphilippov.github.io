blaze

noun
white mark on animal or tree trail mark
7.0
Pronunciation: / bleɪz / (AmE), / bleɪz / (BrE)
a white or light-colored marking on an animal's fur, especially on the face, or a mark made on a tree to indicate a trail

Examples

A horse's blaze
A wide white blaze marked the center of the animal's face.
Hikers followed the blaze on the tree to stay on the right path.
The horse has a distinctive blaze running down its forehead.
