Compsognathus

noun
a genus of small Jurassic carnivorous dinosaurs
Pronunciation: / kɒmpˈsɒɡnəθəs / (AmE), / kɒmpˈsɒɡnəθəs / (BrE)
a genus of small, bipedal carnivorous dinosaurs from the late Jurassic period, known for their slender build and fast movement
