demographist

noun
scientist studying population statistics
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑːɡrəfɪst / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒɡrəfɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies the growth, density, and structure of populations and their vital statistics

Examples

A demographist studies populations
As a demographist, she conducted research on urban population shifts over the past decade.
Several demographists forecast a significant rise in the elderly population over the next decade.
The report was compiled by a leading demographist at the national statistics institute.
