Nimiipuu

noun
Nez Perce person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪmiˌpuː / (AmE), / ˈnɪmiˌpuː / (BrE)
a person who is a member of the Nimiipuu people, also known as the Nez Perce, a Native American tribe of the Pacific Northwest

Examples

A Nimiipuu person
A proud Nimiipuu leader shared stories of his ancestors at the cultural festival.
The Nimiipuu have a rich tradition of horse breeding and craftsmanship.
Many Nimiipuu continue to preserve their language and customs today.
