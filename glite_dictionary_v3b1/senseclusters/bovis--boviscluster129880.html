bovis

noun
species name for cattle
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊvɪs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊvɪs / (BrE)
a Latin term used as a species epithet meaning 'of cattle' in scientific names of organisms

Examples

Mycobacterium bovis
The bacterium Mycobacterium bovis is responsible for tuberculosis in cattle.
Scientists identified the parasite as Trypanosoma bovis in the infected herd.
The species name bovis indicates that the organism is associated with cattle.
