nail enamel

noun
colored/shiny cosmetic for nails
5.6
Pronunciation: / neɪl ɪˈnæməl / (AmE), / neɪl ɪˈnæməl / (BrE)
a liquid cosmetic that dries to form a colored or shiny coating on the fingernails or toenails

Examples

Nail enamel polish
It's important to let your nail enamel dry completely between coats.
She chose a bright red nail enamel for her manicure.
The new nail enamel promises a long-lasting, chip-resistant finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nail polish - another term for a cosmetic lacquer applied to fingernails or toenails
  • synonym: nail varnish - term used in British English for a cosmetic nail coating
