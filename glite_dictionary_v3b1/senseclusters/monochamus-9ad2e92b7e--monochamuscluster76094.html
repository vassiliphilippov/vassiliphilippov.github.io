Monochamus

noun
genus of sawyer beetles
Pronunciation: / məˈnɑːkəməs /; / ˌmɒnəˈkeɪməs / (AmE), / məˈnɒkəməs /; / ˌmɒnəˈkeɪməs / (BrE)
a genus of large longhorn beetles (sawyer beetles) whose larvae bore into wood, especially pine trees
