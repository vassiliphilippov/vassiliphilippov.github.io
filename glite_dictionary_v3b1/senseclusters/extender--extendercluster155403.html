extender

noun
additive to increase volume
5.8
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈstend.ər/ /; / /ek.ˈstend.ər/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈstend.ə/ /; / /ek.ˈstend.ə/ (BrE)
a substance added to a product to increase its volume or reduce its cost, often by diluting the main ingredient

Examples

Paint extender
The manufacturer used an extender in the paint to make it cover more area.
Some processed foods contain an extender to reduce production costs.
Rubber products may include an extender to increase their volume without adding more natural rubber.

Related Terms

  • synonym: additive - a substance added to something to improve or preserve it
  • synonym: filler - a material added to bulk up a product
