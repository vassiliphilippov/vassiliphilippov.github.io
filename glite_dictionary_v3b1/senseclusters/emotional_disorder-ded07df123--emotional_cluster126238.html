emotional disorder

noun
a mental disorder involving disturbed emotions
4.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊʃənəl dɪsˈɔrdər / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənəl dɪsˈɔːdə / (BrE)
A mental disorder marked by significant disturbance of emotions, not due to identifiable physical abnormalities of the brain.

Examples

An emotional disorder diagnosis
He sought therapy to better manage his emotional disorder and improve his daily life.
Living with an emotional disorder can be challenging, but support groups provide valuable assistance.
The doctor diagnosed her with an emotional disorder after several months of observation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mood disorder - a type of emotional disorder characterized by mood disturbances
  • degree higher: psychotic disorder - a severe mental disorder with a loss of contact with reality
