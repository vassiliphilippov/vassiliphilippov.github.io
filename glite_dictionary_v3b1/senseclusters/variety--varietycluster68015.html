variety

noun
the quality of being diverse or varied
1.0
Pronunciation: / vəˈraɪ.ə.ti /; / vəˈraɪəti /; / vəˈraɪ.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / vəˈraɪ.ə.ti /; / vəˈraɪəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being different or having many different kinds; an assortment of types or kinds

Examples

A variety of choices
Her wardrobe contains an impressive variety of colors and styles.
The garden boasts a variety of flowers that bloom all year round.
There is a wide variety of cuisines to try in this city.
At the conference, the speakers presented on a variety of topics.
She has a variety of hobbies, including painting, hiking, and playing the guitar.
The bakery offered a variety of breads, cakes, and pastries.
For variety, let's try a different restaurant this week.
She enjoys her job because it offers a lot of variety each day.
Traveling to new places adds variety to our routine.
