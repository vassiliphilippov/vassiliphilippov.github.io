pseudonymous

adjective
using or under an assumed name
Pronunciation: / suːˈdɑː.nə.məs / (AmE), / sjuːˈdɒn.ɪ.məs / (BrE)
bearing or identified by a false or assumed name, especially a pen name

Examples

Pseudonymous author
Mark Twain is the pseudonymous author of many famous American novels.
Several bloggers choose to remain pseudonymous to protect their privacy.
The author's early poems were published under a pseudonymous identity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anonymous - having no known name or identity
  • synonym: nom de plume - a pen name used by an author
