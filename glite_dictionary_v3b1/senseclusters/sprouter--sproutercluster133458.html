sprouter

noun
device for sprouting seeds
Pronunciation: / ˈspraʊtər / (AmE), / ˈspraʊtə / (BrE)
A device used to facilitate the sprouting of seeds, especially for use as food.

Examples

A sprouter device
A good sprouter ensures your seeds have the right conditions for healthy growth.
She cleaned the sprouter thoroughly between each batch of sprouts.
We bought a new sprouter to grow alfalfa sprouts in our kitchen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seed tray - a shallow container used for germinating seeds
