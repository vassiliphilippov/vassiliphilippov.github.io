nards

noun
aromatic plants or oils
Pronunciation: / nɑrdz / (AmE), / nɑːdz / (BrE)
the plural form of nard, referring to aromatic plants of the genus Nardostachys or the fragrant oil derived from them, used in perfumery and medicine

Examples

Fragrant nards oil
The ancient texts mention nards as valuable ingredients in sacred rituals.
She dabbed a little of the perfume, rich with nards and sandalwood, behind her ears.
Traders brought nards from distant mountains to sell in the city markets.
