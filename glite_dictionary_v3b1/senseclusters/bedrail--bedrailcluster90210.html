bedrail

noun
side of bed frame
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbe.dreɪl/ (AmE), /ˈbe.dreɪl/ (BrE)
a long horizontal bar or board on the side of a bed frame that connects the headboard and footboard and supports the mattress

Examples

Wooden bedrail
The old wooden bedrail creaked every time he turned over in his sleep.
She tightened the screws on the bedrail to make the bed more stable.
Without a sturdy bedrail, the mattress kept slipping off the frame.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bed frame - the structure that supports a bed's mattress and base
  • degree lower: footboard - the upright panel at the foot of a bed
  • degree lower: headboard - the upright panel at the head of a bed
