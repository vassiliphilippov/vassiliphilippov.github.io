civilian

noun
nonmilitary person
1.0
Pronunciation: / sɪˈvɪliən / (AmE), / sɪˈvɪliən / (BrE)
a person who is not a member of the armed forces or the police

Examples

A civilian worker
After retiring from the army, he adjusted to life as a civilian.
Emergency assistance was delivered to the civilian population in the disaster area.
The government promised to protect all civilians during the conflict.

Related Terms

  • antonym: soldier - a person serving in an army
