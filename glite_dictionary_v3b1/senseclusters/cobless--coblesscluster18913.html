cobless

adjective
without a cob
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːbləs / (AmE), / ˈkɒbləs / (BrE)
lacking a cob, especially describing corn that has had its central core removed

Examples

Cobless corn
The farmer fed the animals with cobless corn to make it easier for them to eat.
After processing, the kernels were completely cobless and ready for packaging.
She prefers buying cobless corn for making soups and stews.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decobbed - having had the cob removed
