Magritte

proper noun
Belgian surrealist painter
Pronunciation: / məˈɡrit / (AmE), / maˈɡriːt / (BrE)
René Magritte (1898–1967), Belgian surrealist painter known for his witty and thought-provoking images that challenge observers’ perceptions of reality.
