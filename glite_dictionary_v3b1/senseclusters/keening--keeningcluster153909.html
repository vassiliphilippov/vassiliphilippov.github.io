keening

adjective
high mournful sound
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkiːnɪŋ / (BrE)
making or expressing a high, sharp, and mournful sound, often associated with grief or sorrow.

Examples

Keening cry
The keening cry of the mourners echoed through the quiet village.
A keening wind swept across the fields, carrying a sense of sorrow.
Her keening voice filled the room as she sang the lament.

Related Terms

  • synonym: melancholy - having a feeling of deep sadness
  • synonym: wailing - making a long, loud, and sad cry
