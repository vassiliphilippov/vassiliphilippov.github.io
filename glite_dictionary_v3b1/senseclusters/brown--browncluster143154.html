brown

noun
a dark reddish-yellow color
Pronunciation: / braʊn / (AmE), / braʊn / (BrE)
a color that is a mixture of red, yellow, and black, resembling the color of wood or earth

Examples

A deep brown
Chestnuts turn a deep brown when they are roasted.
She chose a warm shade of brown for her living room walls.
The old wooden table was painted a rich brown.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: beige - a very pale brown color
  • degree higher: chocolate - a rich, dark brown color
  • degree higher: mahogany - a deep reddish-brown color
  • degree lower: tan - a light shade of brown
