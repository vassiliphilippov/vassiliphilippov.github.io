Examples

To liquidate assets
Investors often liquidate their holdings during periods of economic uncertainty.
She had to liquidate her business after facing financial difficulties.
The company decided to liquidate several assets to pay off its debts.
After years of losses, the board decided to liquidate the business.

Synonyms

  • liquidize
    7.5 0.00/m
    liquidize means selling assets to turn them into cash quickly, while liquidate focuses on selling to pay debts or close a business.
  • close out
    4.3 0.2/m
    close out means selling remaining stock at reduced prices to end an operation, while liquidate means selling assets to pay debts and close a business.
  • divest
    7.5 0.8/m
    divest means selling part of a company or an investment for strategic reasons, while liquidate means selling assets to pay debts or end a business.
  • sell off
    3.5 3/m
    sell off means selling items quickly, often at low prices to get rid of stock, while liquidate means selling assets mainly to pay debts or close a business.
