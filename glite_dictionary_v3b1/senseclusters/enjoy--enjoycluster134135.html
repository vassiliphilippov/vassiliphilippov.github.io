Synonyms

  • delight
    1.0 4.47/m
    delight feels like strong, joyful pleasure, while enjoy is a general, everyday feeling of pleasure or satisfaction.
  • relish
    4.4 5.01/m
    relish means taking strong, eager pleasure in something, while enjoy is more general and less intense in daily use.
  • savor
    4.1 3.06/m
    savor is to enjoy slowly and fully, paying attention to details, while enjoy can be quicker and more general.
  • like
    1.0 484/m
    like means feel fondness or approval for someone or something, while enjoy emphasizes taking clear pleasure in it.
  • revel
    6.3 2.47/m
    revel means take wild or noisy pleasure, often in parties or trouble, while enjoy is more general and calmer.
