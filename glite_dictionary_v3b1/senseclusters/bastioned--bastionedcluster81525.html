bastioned

adjective
secured with fortifications or bastions
Pronunciation: / ˈbæs.tʃənd / (AmE), / ˈbɑː.stɪənd / (BrE)
(of a building, place, or system) secured or protected with bastions or strong fortifications.

Examples

A bastioned fortress
A bastioned fortress dominated the landscape, making approach nearly impossible for invaders.
The bastioned walls of the old city have withstood many sieges over the centuries.
The organization stood like a bastioned institution against the winds of social change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fortified - made stronger or more secure, often with defensive works
