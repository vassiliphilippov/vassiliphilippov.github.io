vainglory

noun
excessive and boastful pride
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪnˌɡlɔri / (AmE), / ˈveɪnˌɡlɔːri / (BrE)
excessive pride in one's own achievements, abilities, or appearance; boastful conceit

Examples

Excessive vainglory
His speech was filled with vainglory, as he bragged about victories won long ago.
The king's vainglory led him to ignore the advice of his most trusted counselors.
Throughout history, vainglory has often preceded the downfall of leaders.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humility - the quality of having a modest or low view of one's importance
  • synonym: vanity - excessive pride in or admiration of one's own appearance or achievements
