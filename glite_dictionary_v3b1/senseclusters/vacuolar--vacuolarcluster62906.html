vacuolar

adjective
of vacuoles
Pronunciation: / ˌvækjuˈoʊlər / (AmE), / ˌvækjuˈəʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or containing vacuoles, especially within a cell

Examples

Vacuolar structure
The vacuolar structure is essential for storing nutrients in plant cells.
Researchers observed vacuolar changes in the tissue samples under the microscope.
A vacuolar membrane separates the contents of the vacuole from the rest of the cell.
