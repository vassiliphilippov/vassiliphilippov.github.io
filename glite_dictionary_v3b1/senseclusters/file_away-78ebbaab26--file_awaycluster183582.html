file away

verb
organize and store for future reference
6.0 3.3
Pronunciation: / faɪl əˈweɪ / (AmE), / faɪl əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to organize and store documents or items in a place so they can be found and used later

Examples

To file away documents
I usually file away important emails in a separate folder.
It's important to file away your receipts in case you need them for taxes.
She carefully filed away all the patient records in alphabetical order.
After reading the report, she filed away the papers for future reference.
Old receipts are filed away every year to keep the office organized.
Please file away these documents in the cabinet marked 'archive'.
He carefully filed away every letter he ever received for his records.
Please file away these invoices in the cabinet when you have a moment.
She remembered to file away all her tax documents after completing her returns.

  • synonym: archive - to put or store documents in an archive

  • synonym: put away - to place something in its proper location, especially for storage
  • synonym: store away - to put something in a place where it is kept for future use
