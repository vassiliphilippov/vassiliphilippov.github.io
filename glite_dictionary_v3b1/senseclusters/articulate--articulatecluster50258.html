articulate

verb
to pronounce or express clearly
5.6
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪkjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪkjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to express something clearly in speech or to pronounce words distinctly

Examples

To articulate each word
Can you articulate each word clearly so everyone understands?
She struggled to articulate her thoughts during the presentation.
The speech therapist helped him articulate difficult sounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enunciate - to pronounce words clearly and distinctly
  • antonym: mumble - to speak in a quiet or unclear way
