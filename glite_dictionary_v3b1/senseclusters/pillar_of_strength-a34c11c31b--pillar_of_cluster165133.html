pillar of strength

noun
dependable and supportive person
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlər əv ˈstreŋθ / (AmE), / ˈpɪlə əv ˈstreŋθ / (BrE)
a person who provides unwavering support and comfort to others, especially in difficult times

Examples

A pillar of strength
During her illness, her husband was a true pillar of strength for the family.
His calm presence made him the pillar of strength the whole team relied on in tough moments.
She became a pillar of strength to her friends during their time of need.
