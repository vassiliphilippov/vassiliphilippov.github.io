chela

noun
pincer-like structure on an arthropod's limb
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkiː.lə/ /; / /ˈtʃeɪ.lə/ (AmE), /ˈkiː.lə/ /; / /ˈtʃeɪ.lə/ (BrE)
a pincer-like grasping structure found at the end of a limb in certain crustaceans and arthropods, such as crabs and lobsters
