overdevelop

verb
to develop too much
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərdɪˈvɛləp / (AmE), / ˌəʊvədəˈvɛləp / (BrE)
to develop land or an area excessively, often resulting in negative effects such as loss of green space or overcrowding

Examples

To overdevelop a city
Many cities risk harming the environment if they overdevelop natural areas.
The government introduced new laws to prevent companies from overdeveloping coastal regions.
Residents protested plans to overdevelop the historic district with high-rise apartments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overbuild - to construct too many buildings in an area
  • antonym: underdevelop - to develop insufficiently or not enough
