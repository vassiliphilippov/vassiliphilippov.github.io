2.3
Pronunciation: / ɪn jʊr ʃuz / (AmE), / ɪn jɔː ʃuːz / (BrE)
used to say that you would act or feel the same way as someone else if you were in their situation

Examples

If I were in your shoes
If I were in your shoes, I would ask for help immediately.
She tried to imagine herself in your shoes before giving advice.
What would you do in your shoes if you were facing this problem?
