nonwoven

adjective
not woven or knitted
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈwoʊvən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈwəʊvən / (BrE)
made from fibers that are bonded together rather than woven or knitted

Examples

Nonwoven fabric
Many disposable medical gowns are made from nonwoven materials for better protection.
The company developed a new nonwoven fabric for use in air filters.
Reusable shopping bags often use strong nonwoven textiles for durability.

Related Terms

  • antonym: knitted - made by interlocking loops of yarn with needles
  • antonym: woven - made by interlacing threads or fibers
