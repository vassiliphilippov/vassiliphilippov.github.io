C.O.D.

adverb
payment collected upon delivery
Pronunciation: / si oʊ ˈdi / (AmE), / siː əʊ ˈdiː / (BrE)
if goods are sent C.O.D., payment is collected upon delivery rather than in advance; stands for 'cash on delivery'.

Examples

Sent C.O.D. package
Please indicate on the form whether you want the package mailed C.O.D. or prepaid.
The company only accepts C.O.D. orders for first-time customers.
You can have the book shipped C.O.D. if you prefer to pay at your door.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prepaid - goods or services paid for in advance
