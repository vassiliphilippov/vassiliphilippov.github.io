bray

verb
donkey's harsh cry (verb)
8.2
Pronunciation: / breɪ / (AmE), / breɪ / (BrE)
(of a donkey) to utter a loud, harsh cry; to make a characteristic donkey noise.

Examples

Donkeys bray loudly
Children laughed as they heard the donkey braying at the petting zoo.
The donkey began to bray loudly in the early morning, waking up the farm.
When startled, the stubborn animal would often bray and refuse to move.
