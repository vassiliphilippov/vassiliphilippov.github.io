thistlelike

adjective
resembling a thistle
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪsəlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈθɪs(ə)llaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of a thistle, especially in appearance or texture.

Examples

Thistlelike leaves
Her drawing depicted a strange, thistlelike bloom growing from the rocky soil.
Some wildflowers have a thistlelike appearance, making them easy to confuse with true thistles.
The plant has thistlelike leaves covered in fine spines.
