gin

noun
alcoholic spirit flavored with juniper berries
4.7
Pronunciation: / dʒɪn / (AmE), / dʒɪn / (BrE)
a clear alcoholic spirit made from grain or malt and flavored with juniper berries and other botanicals

Examples

A gin and tonic
Many classic cocktails, such as the martini, use gin as the main ingredient.
She ordered a gin and tonic at the bar.
The distinct flavor of gin comes from juniper berries and various botanicals.
