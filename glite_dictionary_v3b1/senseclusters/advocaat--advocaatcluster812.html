advocaat

noun
Dutch lawyer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈædvəˌkɑt / (AmE), / ˈædvəˌkɑːt / (BrE)
a lawyer or legal advocate, especially in Dutch-speaking countries or in archaic English usage

Examples

Dutch advocaat
In the Netherlands, an advocaat must complete specialized training before practicing law.
The old documents referred to him as an advocaat representing merchants in court.
She consulted an advocaat to help with her legal dispute in Amsterdam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrister - a lawyer entitled to represent clients in higher courts
  • antonym: client - a person who is represented by a lawyer
  • synonym: lawyer - a person who practices or studies law
