jocundity

noun
cheerful state; merriment
7.8
Pronunciation: / dʒoʊˈkʌndɪti / (AmE), / dʒɒˈkʌndɪti / (BrE)
A cheerful or lighthearted state; mirth or merriment.

Examples

Bright jocundity
Despite the rain, the children played with unrestrained jocundity in the park.
Her speech was filled with a jocundity that lifted everyone's spirits.
The jocundity of the festival was evident in every smiling face.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gloom - a state of darkness, sadness or depression
  • synonym: mirth - amusement or laughter; great joy or merriment
