urachal

adjective
of the urachus
Pronunciation: / jʊˈreɪkəl / (AmE), / jʊˈreɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the urachus, a fetal structure that connects the bladder to the umbilicus

Examples

Urachal cyst
A urachal cyst may remain undetected until it causes symptoms later in life.
Doctors discovered a urachal remnant during the patient's abdominal surgery.
The presence of a urachal anomaly can sometimes lead to infection in adults.
