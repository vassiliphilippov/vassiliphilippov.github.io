bell founder

noun
person who casts metal bells
6.2
Pronunciation: / bɛl ˈfaʊndɚ / (AmE), / bɛl ˈfaʊndə / (BrE)
a person whose occupation is casting or making metal bells, typically by pouring molten metal into a mold
