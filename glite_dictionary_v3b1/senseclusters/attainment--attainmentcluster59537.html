attainment

noun
achievement of a stage or level
Pronunciation: / əˈteɪnmənt / (AmE), / əˈteɪnmənt / (BrE)
the act of achieving or reaching something, such as a goal, level, or condition

Examples

Attainment of a goal
Her attainment of a leadership position inspired many younger colleagues.
His attainment of a black belt reflected years of dedication to martial arts.
The attainment of independence marked a turning point in the country's history.
Freedom was seen as the ultimate attainment for the oppressed group.
Her attainment of the award was the result of years of hard work.
The attainment of his lifelong dream brought him great joy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: failure - lack of success in reaching a stage or goal; an antonym
