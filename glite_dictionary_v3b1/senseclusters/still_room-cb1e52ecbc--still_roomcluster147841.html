still room

noun
pantry for preparing and storing beverages
4.9 8.8
Pronunciation: /stɪl ruːm/ (AmE), /stɪl ruːm/ /; / /stɪl rʊm/ (BrE)
A pantry or storeroom in a large house, near the kitchen, used for preparing beverages and for storing liquors, preserves, and tea.

Examples

A still room pantry
Historically, a still room was essential for preparing medicines and storing beverages in grand houses.
She brewed her own herbal teas in the old still room by the kitchen.
The housekeeper supervised the still room, making sure the preserves were safely stored.
