fascial

adjective
of connective tissue
Pronunciation: / ˈfæʃəl / (AmE), / ˈfæʃiəl / (BrE)
relating to fascia, the connective tissue that surrounds muscles, organs, and other structures in the body

Examples

Fascial tissue massage
The patient received a massage to release tight fascial tissue in her shoulders.
Doctors studied the fascial layers to better understand chronic pain conditions.
Physical therapy can help relieve fascial pain in the back.

Related Terms

  • synonym: muscular - relating to muscles
  • antonym: skeletal - relating to the skeleton
