consolingly

adverb
in a comforting manner
Pronunciation: / kənˈsoʊlɪŋli / (AmE), / kənˈsəʊlɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that provides comfort or relief to someone who is upset or troubled.

Examples

Speak consolingly to someone
He spoke consolingly to the child, hoping to ease her pain.
She smiled consolingly at her friend after the disappointing news.
The teacher patted the student consolingly on the back after his mistake.
