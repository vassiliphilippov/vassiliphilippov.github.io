foreword

noun
introductory essay in a book
3.2 4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrˌwɝːd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːwɜːd / (BrE)
a short introduction at the beginning of a book, typically written by someone other than the author, that explains its content or context

Examples

The foreword of a book

Most readers skip the foreword and go straight to the main text.
The author's friend wrote the foreword to the new edition of the novel.
