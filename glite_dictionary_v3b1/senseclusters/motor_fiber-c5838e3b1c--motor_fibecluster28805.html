motor fiber

noun

Examples

Motor fiber nerve
Diseases affecting the motor fiber can result in difficulties with movement or coordination.
Each motor fiber transmits signals from the spinal cord to a specific group of muscle fibers.

Synonyms

  • motor nerve fiber
    4.9 0.00/m
    motor nerve fiber is the common phrase for nerves that send impulses to muscles or glands, while motor fiber is a shorter term used in labels.
  • efferent fiber
    8.1 0.01/m
    efferent fiber is the technical medical term doctors use for nerves carrying signals out of the central nervous system, while motor fiber is the general name.
