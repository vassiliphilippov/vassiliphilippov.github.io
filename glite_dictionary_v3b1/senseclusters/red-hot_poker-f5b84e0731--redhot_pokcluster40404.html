red-hot poker

noun
a tall plant with brightly colored flower spikes
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛd hɑt ˈpoʊkər / (AmE), / ˌrɛd hɒt ˈpəʊkə / (BrE)
a tall flowering plant of the genus Kniphofia, grown for its spike of bright red, yellow, or orange tubular flowers resembling a red-hot iron poker
