eat for two

verb
eat extra when pregnant
Pronunciation: / it fɔr tu / (AmE), / iːt fə tuː / (BrE)
to eat more than usual because one is pregnant and needs to nourish both themselves and their baby

Examples

To eat for two during pregnancy
He playfully reminded his partner to eat for two to keep their baby healthy.
It's common for mothers-to-be to say they eat for two during pregnancy.
Since becoming pregnant, she often jokes that she has to eat for two.
