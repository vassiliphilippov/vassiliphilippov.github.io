loss

noun
military personnel lost in action
1.0
Pronunciation: / lɔs / (AmE), / lɒs / (BrE)
the number of military personnel who are killed, wounded, or captured in a conflict or operation

Examples

Heavy loss in battle
Reports indicated that the regiment's loss was the highest among all units.
The army suffered heavy loss in the final battle of the war.
They mourned the loss of several brave soldiers after the operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: casualty - a person killed or injured in a war or accident
