manchineel

noun
poisonous tropical fruit
Pronunciation: / ˌmæn.tʃɪˈniːl / (AmE), / ˌman.tʃɪˈniːl / (BrE)
a small, apple-like fruit from the manchineel tree that is extremely poisonous and dangerous to eat

Examples

Manchineel fruit warning
The guide warned us never to touch or eat the manchineel found along the beach.
Locals know to avoid the manchineel because even its juice can cause severe burns.
Despite its tempting appearance, the manchineel is one of the most dangerous fruits in the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apple - a round fruit with red or green skin and firm white flesh
  • synonym: poison - a substance that can cause illness or death if taken into the body
