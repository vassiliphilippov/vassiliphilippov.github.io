promotor

noun
event organizer
Pronunciation: / prəˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / prəˈməʊtə / (BrE)
a person or company that organizes, advertises, and finances public events such as concerts or shows

Examples

Concert promotor
The promotor arranged for several famous artists to perform at the festival.
After months of planning, the promotor finally announced the lineup for the concert.
A successful promotor must handle both marketing and financial aspects of an event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: organizer - a person who arranges and manages events or activities
  • synonym: sponsor - a person or company that supports an event financially
