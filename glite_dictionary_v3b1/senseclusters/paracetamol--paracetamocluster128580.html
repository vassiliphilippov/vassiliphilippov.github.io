paracetamol

noun
pain and fever medicine
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈsiːtəˌmɑl / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈsiːtəmɒl / (BrE)

Examples

The doctor recommended paracetamol to help lower her fever.
Children should not take more paracetamol than the recommended dose.
