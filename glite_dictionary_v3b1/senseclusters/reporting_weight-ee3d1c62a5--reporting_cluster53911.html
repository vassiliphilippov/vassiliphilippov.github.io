reporting weight

noun
athlete's weight at start of season
1.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɔrtɪŋ weɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈpɔːtɪŋ weɪt / (BrE)
an athlete's body weight at the start of the season, recorded when they first report for practice

Examples

Athlete's reporting weight
Athletes are required to declare their reporting weight before participation in the first practice.
Her reporting weight was five pounds less than last year, showing off-season progress.
The coach recorded each player's reporting weight at the start of training camp.
