well-oiled machine

noun
something working smoothly and efficiently
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛl ˈɔɪld məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˌwɛl ˈɔɪld məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a group, organization, or system that functions smoothly and efficiently, with all parts working well together

Examples

A well-oiled machine
After months of rehearsals, the play's production became a well-oiled machine for opening night.
Every member of the crew knew their part, making the operation a well-oiled machine.
Their customer service department runs like a well-oiled machine, handling issues quickly and politely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chaotic - lacking order or efficiency; the opposite of well-organized
  • synonym: smooth operation - something functioning without problems or delays
