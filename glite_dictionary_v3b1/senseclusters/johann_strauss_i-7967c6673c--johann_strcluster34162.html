Johann Strauss I

proper noun
Austrian composer of waltzes (1804–1849)
6.8
Pronunciation: / joʊˌhɑn ˈstraʊs ðə ˈfɜrst / (AmE), / jəʊˌhan ˈstraʊs ði ˈfɜːst / (BrE)
