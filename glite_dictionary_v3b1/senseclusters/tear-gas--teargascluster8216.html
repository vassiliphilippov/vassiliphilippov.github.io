tear-gas

verb
to use tear gas
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪr ˌɡæs / (AmE), / ˈtɪə ˌɡæs / (BrE)
to use tear gas on people in order to disperse or control them

Examples

To tear-gas protesters
The police threatened to tear-gas the protesters if they did not leave.
Authorities tear-gassed the crowd to break up the demonstration.
During the unrest, several people were tear-gassed while trying to escape the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disperse - to cause a group to break up and go in different directions
  • synonym: gas - to attack or control with gas
