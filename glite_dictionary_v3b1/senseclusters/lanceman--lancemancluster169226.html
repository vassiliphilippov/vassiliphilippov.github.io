lanceman

noun
person who lances wounds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlænsˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈlɑːnsmən / (BrE)
a person who performs lancing, making incisions to drain abscesses or treat wounds

Examples

Village lanceman treats wounds
The village lanceman was called whenever someone had a serious infection or abscess.
Before modern medicine, a lanceman would treat wounds using simple tools and herbal remedies.
People trusted the lanceman to relieve pain by draining boils and cleaning injuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surgeon - a medical professional who performs surgery
