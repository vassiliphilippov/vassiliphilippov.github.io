wonder flower

noun
South African flowering plant used as winter cut flower
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌndɚ ˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈwʌndə ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A South African perennial plant with long-lasting spikes of white blossoms, often used as winter cut flowers in Europe and America.

Examples

The wonder flower plant
