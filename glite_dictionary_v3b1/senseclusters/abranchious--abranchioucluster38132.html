abranchious

adjective
lacking gills (biology)
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈbrænkiəs / (AmE), / əˈbraŋkɪəs / (BrE)
(of an animal) having no gills.

Examples

Abranchious animal
Some parasitic flatworms are abranchious throughout their entire life cycle.
The adult terrestrial salamander is abranchious and relies on lungs for respiration.
Unlike its aquatic relatives, this species develops into an abranchious adult.
