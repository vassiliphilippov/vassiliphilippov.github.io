pityingly

adverb
in a compassionate or sympathetic way
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtiɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈpɪtiɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows pity or compassion for someone; with sympathy for another's suffering or misfortune.

Examples

To speak pityingly
He spoke pityingly to his friend who had just lost his job.
She glanced pityingly at the stray dog shivering in the rain.
The teacher looked pityingly at the student who had forgotten his homework again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compassionately - In a manner showing compassion or concern for others
