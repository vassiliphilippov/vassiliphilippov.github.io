Synonyms

  • logical proof
    5.5 0.01/m
    logical proof uses formal, exact reasoning to show a statement must be true, while demonstration names the act of proving.
  • logical argument
    4.3 0.2/m
    logical argument is a chain of reasons that aims to support or refute a statement, while demonstration claims a final, conclusive proof.
  • proof
    2.5 50/m
    proof can be physical evidence or a reason that shows something is true, while demonstration focuses on logical, step-by-step proving.
