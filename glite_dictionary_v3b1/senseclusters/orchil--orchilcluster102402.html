orchil

noun
purple dye from lichens
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːr.tʃɪl / (AmE), / ˈɔː.tʃɪl / (BrE)
a purple dye obtained from certain lichens, especially used in the past for textiles

Examples

Orchil dye for cloth
In medieval Europe, fabrics were often dyed using orchil extracted from lichens.
She studied the process of making natural orchil dyes in her chemistry class.
The distinctive color of the tapestry was achieved with orchil.
