loop-line

noun
railway branch line that rejoins main line
7.9
Pronunciation: / luːp laɪn / (AmE), / luːp laɪn / (BrE)
a railway branch line that leaves a main railway line and later rejoins it, forming a loop

Examples

A loop-line railway
Due to construction on the main track, all trains were diverted onto the loop-line temporarily.
Engineers constructed a loop-line to improve connectivity between the two regional towns.
The new loop-line allows trains to bypass the busy central station during peak hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: branch line - a secondary railway line that diverges from a main route
