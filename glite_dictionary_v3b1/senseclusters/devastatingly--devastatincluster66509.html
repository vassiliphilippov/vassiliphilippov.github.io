devastatingly

adverb
with great destruction
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛvəˌsteɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəsteɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes great harm, damage, or a very strong effect

Examples

Devastatingly destroyed
The earthquake devastatingly destroyed much of the city in just a few minutes.
She was devastatingly honest about the problems facing the company.
The news was devastatingly difficult for the family to accept.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mildly - in a gentle or not severe way
