solleret

noun
armored plate for the foot (medieval)
7.0 9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsɑː.lə.ret/ (AmE), /ˈsɒ.lə.ret/ (BrE)
A piece of armor consisting of protective plates for the foot, often featuring mail with a solid toe and heel, used in medieval times.
