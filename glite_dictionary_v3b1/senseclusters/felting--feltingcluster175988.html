felting

noun
matted fiber material
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛltɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɛltɪŋ / (BrE)
a fabric or material made by matting, condensing, and pressing fibers together, often used for padding or lining.

Examples

Felting for crafts
She cut a piece of felting to line the inside of the jewelry box.
The craftsman used thick felting as padding beneath the wooden floorboards.
You can buy colored felting for various art and craft projects at the store.
