waters

noun
amniotic fluid released before childbirth
4.3 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔː.tərz/ /; / /ˈwɑː.tərz/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.təz/ (BrE)
the serous fluid in which the embryo is suspended inside the amnion; especially as released when a woman's amniotic sac breaks before childbirth

Examples
