literary pirate

noun
illegal distributor of literary works online
Pronunciation: / \u02c8l\u026at\u0259r\u025bri \u02c8pa\u026ar\u0259t / (AmE), / \u02c8l\u026at\u0259r\u0259ri \u02c8pa\u026ar\u0259t / (BrE)
A person who illegally copies, shares, or distributes copyrighted literary works (especially digitally), for free or for profit.

Examples

A literary pirate shares eBooks
The literary pirate uploaded thousands of eBooks to a torrent site, violating multiple copyright laws.
During the investigation, agents discovered that several literary pirates sold scanned textbooks for profit.
Universities warn students that acting as a literary pirate can lead to lawsuits and career damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plagiarist - a person who takes someone else's writing or ideas and presents them as their own
