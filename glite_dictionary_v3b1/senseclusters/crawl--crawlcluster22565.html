crawl

verb
be full of moving creatures or people
3.3 2.43/m
Pronunciation: / kr…îl / (AmE), / kr…îÀêl / (BrE)
(of a place or thing) to be full of or covered with people or creatures, especially in large numbers and often moving.

Examples

The abandoned house was crawling with spiders and other insects.
The kitchen was crawling with ants after we left food out overnight.
