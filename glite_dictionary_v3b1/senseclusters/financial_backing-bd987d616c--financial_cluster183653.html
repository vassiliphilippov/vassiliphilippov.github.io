financial backing

noun
money or resources to support a project
1.0
Pronunciation: / fəˈnænʃəl ˈbækɪŋ / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃəl ˈbækɪŋ / (BrE)
money or resources supplied to support a project, business, or person

Examples

Financial backing from investors

The event would not have been possible without generous financial backing from sponsors.
Without financial backing, the startup struggled to turn its ideas into reality.
