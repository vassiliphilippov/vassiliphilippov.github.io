prepractice

noun
preparatory session
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈpræktɪs / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈpræktɪs / (BrE)
an activity or session done before the main practice to help prepare participants

Examples

A prepractice meeting
The coach scheduled a prepractice meeting to discuss the day's goals.
Athletes arrived early for prepractice stretching to prevent injuries.
During prepractice, the team reviewed their strategies before starting drills.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cool-down - activities done after exercise to help the body recover
  • synonym: warm-up - a set of exercises or activities to prepare for physical activity
