scorpion shell

noun
marine snail with spiny shell
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔr.pi.ən ʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈskɔː.pi.ən ʃɛl / (BrE)
a tropical marine snail whose adult shell has the outer lip of the opening formed into a series of long, curved spines

Examples

The scorpion shell snail
Many collectors prize the unique shape of the scorpion shell in their shell collections.
The diver found a beautiful scorpion shell on the coral reef during his trip.
The outer spines of a scorpion shell make it easily recognizable among other marine snails.
