skunk

noun
complete shutout defeat in a game
Pronunciation: / skʌŋk / (AmE), / skʌŋk / (BrE)
a defeat in a game or match in which one side fails to score any points

Examples

A skunk loss
The final score was 10-0, which made the loss a total skunk for the visiting team.
They managed to avoid a skunk by finally scoring a single point.
Winning by a skunk can be demoralizing for the losing side.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shutout - a game in which one side prevents the other from scoring
