headgear

noun
orthodontic device
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌɡɪə / (BrE)
a device worn on the head, especially to correct the alignment of teeth or jaws in orthodontic treatment

Examples

Orthodontic headgear
The orthodontist explained how to wear the headgear properly each night.
She felt self-conscious about her headgear but knew it would help her smile.
Wearing headgear is sometimes necessary to correct serious jaw alignment issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braces - a dental device used to straighten teeth
  • synonym: retainer - a device used to keep teeth in position after orthodontic treatment
