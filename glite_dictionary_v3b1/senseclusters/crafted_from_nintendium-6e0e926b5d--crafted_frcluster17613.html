crafted from Nintendium

adjective
made from an imaginary, super-strong material
Pronunciation: / ˈkræftɪd frəm nɪnˈtɛndiəm / (AmE), / ˈkrɑːftɪd frəm nɪnˈtɛndiəm / (BrE)
Humorously said of something that is extremely durable, as if made from an imaginary super-strong material associated with Nintendo products.

Examples

Crafted from Nintendium material
Their new controller is so sturdy, people joke it's crafted from Nintendium.
This old phone feels like it was crafted from Nintendium—it survived dozens of falls without a scratch.
When I dropped my laptop and it barely had a dent, my friends said it must be crafted from Nintendium.
