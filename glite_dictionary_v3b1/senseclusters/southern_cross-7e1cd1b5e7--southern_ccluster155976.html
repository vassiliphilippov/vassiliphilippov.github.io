Southern Cross

noun
a prominent constellation in the Southern Hemisphere
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌðərn krɑs / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən krɒs / (BrE)
a small but prominent constellation in the Southern Hemisphere, also known as Crux, situated near Centaurus in the Milky Way

Examples

The Southern Cross constellation
During our trip to Australia, we identified the Southern Cross in the night sky.
Sailors have long used the Southern Cross for navigation in southern latitudes.
The Southern Cross is an important symbol on several national flags in the Southern Hemisphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Crux - the Latin name for the Southern Cross constellation
