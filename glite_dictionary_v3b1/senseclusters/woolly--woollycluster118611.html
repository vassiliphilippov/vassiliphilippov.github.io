woolly

noun
wool garment
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊli / (AmE), / ˈwʊli / (BrE)
a warm piece of clothing made from wool, such as a sweater or jumper, especially for cold weather

Examples

A woolly jumper
She grabbed her favorite woolly before heading out into the chilly morning.
You should pack a thick woolly if you are visiting Scotland in winter.
He wore a blue woolly to keep warm during the hike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jumper - a knitted woolen garment worn on the upper body
  • synonym: sweater - a knitted garment worn on the upper body for warmth
