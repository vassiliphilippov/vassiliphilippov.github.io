merde

interjection
theater good luck exclamation
Pronunciation: / mɛrd / (AmE), / mɛəd / (BrE)
an exclamation used by performers to wish someone good luck before a performance, especially in theater or dance

Examples

Merde before a show
Before the curtain rose, the dancers whispered merde to each other backstage.
She texted her friend 'merde' just before his big audition.
The cast gathered in a circle and shouted merde for good luck before the show.

Related Terms

  • synonym: break a leg - an English expression wishing good luck to performers
