north-northeast

adverb
in the direction between north and northeast
Pronunciation: / nɔrθ nɔrθˈist / (AmE), / nɔːθ nɔːθˈiːst / (BrE)
in or toward the direction halfway between north and northeast on a compass

Examples

Travel north-northeast
Sail the ship north-northeast for two hours to reach the next port.
The hikers headed north-northeast to avoid the mountain range.
The wind was blowing north-northeast at fifteen miles per hour that morning.
