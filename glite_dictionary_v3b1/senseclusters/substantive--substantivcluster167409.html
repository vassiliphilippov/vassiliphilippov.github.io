substantive

adjective
important and meaningful; thought-provoking
4.2 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌb.stən.tɪv/ /; / /səb.ˈstæn.tɪv/ (AmE), /ˈsʌb.stən.tɪv/ /; / /səb.ˈstæn.tɪv/ (BrE)
having real importance, meaning, or substance; considerable in amount or significance

Examples

Her report provided substantive evidence to support the claims made in the article.
