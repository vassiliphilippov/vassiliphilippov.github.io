monastically

adverb
like a monk
Pronunciation: / məˈnæstɪkli / (AmE), / məˈnæstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that resembles the simple, disciplined, or secluded life of a monk or monastery

Examples

To live monastically
After losing his job, he began to live monastically in a small, quiet apartment.
She chose to spend her summer monastically, focusing on meditation and study.
The writer worked monastically for months, rarely leaving his home or seeing friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascetically - in a strict and self-disciplined way, avoiding pleasure
  • antonym: luxuriously - in a way that involves great comfort or wealth
