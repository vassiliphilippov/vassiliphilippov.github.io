ungood

adjective
not good
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡʊd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡʊd / (BrE)
not good; bad or undesirable, often used humorously or in reference to Orwell's Newspeak.

Examples

An ungood movie
The movie was so ungood that we left before it ended.
His explanation was rather ungood and left everyone confused.
She described the weather as distinctly ungood for a picnic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bad - not good; of poor quality or undesirable
  • antonym: good - of high quality or desirable
