parsimoniously

adverb
in a stingy way
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːrsəˈmoʊniəsli / (AmE), / ˌpɑːsɪˈməʊniəsli / (BrE)
in a way that is very unwilling to spend money or use resources

Examples

Live parsimoniously
He always lived parsimoniously, saving every penny he could.
The organization spent its funds parsimoniously to ensure long-term sustainability.
She parsimoniously avoided unnecessary expenses, even on small comforts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frugally - in a way that uses money or resources carefully
  • antonym: generously - in a way that is willing to give or spend freely
