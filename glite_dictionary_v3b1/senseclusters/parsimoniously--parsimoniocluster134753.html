parsimoniously

adverb
in a stingy way
9.4
Pronunciation: /pɑːr.sɪ.ˈmoʊ.nɪ.əs.liː/ /; / /pɑːr.sə.ˈmoʊ.nɪ.əs.liː/ (AmE), /pɑː.sɪ.ˈməʊ.nɪ.əs.liː/ /; / /pɑː.sə.ˈməʊ.nɪ.əs.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that is very unwilling to spend money or use resources

Examples

Live parsimoniously

The organization spent its funds parsimoniously to ensure long-term sustainability.
She parsimoniously avoided unnecessary expenses, even on small comforts.
