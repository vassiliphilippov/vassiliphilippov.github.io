picnic

verb
to eat outdoors as an outing
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪk.nɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɪk.nɪk / (BrE)
to have a meal outdoors for pleasure, usually as part of an outing

Examples

To picnic by the lake
Isn’t it a lovely day to picnic in the countryside?
They picnicked in the park every Sunday afternoon during the summer.
We decided to picnic by the river after our hike.
