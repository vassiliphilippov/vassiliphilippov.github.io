fictional

adjective
imaginary; related to fiction
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪkʃənl̩ /; / ˈfɪkʃənl / (AmE), / ˈfɪkʃənəl / (BrE)
invented or existing only in stories, books, or imagination; not real

Examples

A fictional character
Many of the characters in this novel are entirely fictional and bear no resemblance to real people.
She prefers to read about fictional worlds instead of true stories.
The movie is set in a fictional town in the American Midwest.
She prefers reading about fictional detectives rather than real-life cases.
That story about the talking cat is entirely fictional.
The movie is based on a fictional city inspired by several real-world locations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imaginary - existing only in the imagination
  • antonym: real - actually existing, not imagined or invented
