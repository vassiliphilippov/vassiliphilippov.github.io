rollaway

noun
folding bed on wheels
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊləˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊləˌweɪ / (BrE)
a folding bed on wheels that can be easily moved and stored when not in use

Examples

A rollaway bed
The hotel staff brought a rollaway to our room for the extra guest.
We keep a rollaway in the closet for when friends stay overnight.
A rollaway is convenient because it can be folded and stored easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cot - a small, portable bed
  • synonym: sofa bed - a sofa that can be converted into a bed
