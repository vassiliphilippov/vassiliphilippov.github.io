rust

noun
reddish-brown coating on iron or steel
4.8
Pronunciation: / rʌst / (AmE), / rʌst / (BrE)
a reddish-brown substance or flaky coating that forms on iron or steel when it is exposed to air and moisture

Examples

Rust on metal
A reddish layer of rust had formed on the abandoned tools.
The old bicycle was covered in rust after being left outside for months.
You should paint the metal to protect it from rust.
Rust weakened the bridge, making it unsafe for cars to cross.
He scrubbed the old tools to remove the thick rust covering the surface.
Leaving your bike in the rain can cause rust to develop on the metal frame.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corrosion - gradual destruction of materials (usually metals) by chemical reaction
  • degree lower: tarnish - a thin layer of corrosion that forms on metals other than iron
