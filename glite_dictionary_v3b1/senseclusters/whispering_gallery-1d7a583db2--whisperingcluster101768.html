whispering gallery

noun
gallery with unusual acoustic effects
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪspərɪŋ ˌɡæləri / (AmE), / ˈwɪspərɪŋ ˌɡæləri / (BrE)
a gallery or space beneath a dome or arch where sounds made at certain points are clearly heard at other distant points, due to the structure's acoustics

Examples

The whispering gallery dome
St Paul's Cathedral in London is famous for its remarkable whispering gallery.
The architect included a whispering gallery in the design to showcase the building's acoustics.
Tourists were amazed by how a quiet remark in the whispering gallery could be heard on the opposite side.
