whispering gallery

noun
gallery with unusual acoustic effects
5.0 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪs.pər.ɪŋ ˈgæl.ə.riː/ /; / /ˈhwɪs.pər.ɪŋ ˈgæl.ə.riː/ (AmE), /ˈwɪs.pə.rɪŋ ˈgæl.ə.rɪ/ /; / /ˈhwɪs.pə.rɪŋ ˈgæl.ə.rɪ/ (BrE)
