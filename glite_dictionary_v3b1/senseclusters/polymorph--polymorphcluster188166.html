polymorph

noun
organism with more than one adult form
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑliˌmɔrf /; / ˈpɑːliˌmɔrf / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪˌmɔːf / (BrE)
a substance or organism that exists in or can change into several different forms or structures (for example, minerals or insects with different physical forms)

  • synonym: polymorphism - the occurrence of different forms among the individuals of a species
