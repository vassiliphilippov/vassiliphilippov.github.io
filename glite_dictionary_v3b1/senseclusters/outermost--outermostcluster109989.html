outermost

adjective
farthest from the center
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtərˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈaʊtəˌməʊst / (BrE)
situated at the greatest distance from the center or inside; farthest on the outside.

Examples

The outermost edge
She placed the fragile vase on the outermost edge of the shelf.
The outermost layer of the skin provides protection against bacteria.
The outermost planet in our solar system is Neptune.

Related Terms

  • antonym: innermost - situated closest to the center or inside
