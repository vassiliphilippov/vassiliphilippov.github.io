meanspirited

adjective
unkind or spiteful in nature
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈminˌspɪrɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈmiːnˌspɪrɪtɪd / (BrE)
having or showing a cruel, unkind, or petty nature; lacking generosity or kindness

Examples

Meanspirited attitude
A meanspirited attitude will only isolate you from others.
His meanspirited remarks ruined the atmosphere at the dinner table.
She found his refusal to help out both petty and meanspirited.
Her meanspirited comments hurt the entire team.
It's meanspirited to mock someone for their mistakes.
The critic's meanspirited review did little to help the struggling artist.

  • antonym: kindhearted - having a kind and sympathetic nature
  • synonym: malicious - intending to do harm
