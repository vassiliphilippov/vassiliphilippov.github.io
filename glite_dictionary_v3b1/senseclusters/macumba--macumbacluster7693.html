macumba

noun
Brazilian dance music with religious roots
Pronunciation: / məˈkʊm.bə / (AmE), / məˈkʌm.bə / (BrE)
A genre of popular Brazilian dance music, originally derived from the practices of the macumba religious cult.

Examples

Macumba dance music
She grew up listening to macumba at family gatherings in Rio de Janeiro.
The band played a lively set of macumba that had everyone dancing.
The rhythms of macumba reflect the influence of African spiritual traditions in Brazil.
