uneditable

adjective
cannot be edited
5.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɛdɪtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈɛdɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
not able to be changed or modified by editing

Examples

An uneditable file
The file was marked as uneditable to prevent accidental changes.
After submitting the form, it became uneditable for all users.
Some sections of the contract are intentionally uneditable to maintain legal accuracy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: editable - able to be changed or modified by editing
  • synonym: locked - secured so that changes cannot be made
