crudity

noun
rudeness or lack of refinement
6.5 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkruː.dɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈkruː.dɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
rude or vulgar behavior that lacks tact or refinement; impoliteness or coarseness in manner

Examples

Social crudity
She was surprised by the crudity of his remarks at the formal event.
Some critics objected to the crudity of the humor in the play.
The dinner conversation was spoiled by his crudity and lack of good manners.
