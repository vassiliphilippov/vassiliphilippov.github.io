grumblingly

adverb
in a rumbling way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrʌmblɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌmblɪŋli / (BrE)
in a complaining or dissatisfied way, often in a low or muttering voice

Examples

To grumble quietly
The old engine started grumblingly as the car moved down the road.
He grumblingly agreed to help, clearly unhappy with the decision.
Thunder rolled grumblingly in the distance as the storm approached.
She grumblingly got out of bed when her alarm rang early in the morning.
The students grumblingly completed the extra homework assigned over the weekend.
He grumblingly accepted the decision, even though he disagreed with it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy and positive way
  • synonym: murmuringly - in a low, continuous, and indistinct manner
  • degree higher: noisily - in a loud or disruptive manner
  • antonym: quietly - in a soft or silent manner
  • synonym: reluctantly - in an unwilling or hesitant way
