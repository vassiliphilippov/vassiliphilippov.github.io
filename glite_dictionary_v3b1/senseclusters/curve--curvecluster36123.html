curve

noun
a spinning baseball pitch that curves
6.7 3/m
Pronunciation: / k…ùv / (AmE), / k…úÀêv / (BrE)
(in baseball) a pitch thrown with a spinning motion so that its path curves as it approaches the batter.

Examples

A curve ball
Batters often struggle to hit a well-thrown curve.
He threw a nasty curve that completely fooled the batter.
The pitcher relied on his curve to get out of tough situations.
