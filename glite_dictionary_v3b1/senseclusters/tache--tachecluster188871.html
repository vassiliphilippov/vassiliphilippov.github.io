tache

noun
small spot or mark
9.9
Pronunciation: / tæʃ / (AmE), / tæʃ / (BrE)
a small spot, stain, or mark, especially on a surface

Examples

A small tache on paper
The artist noticed a tiny tache on the otherwise perfect canvas.
She tried to remove the tache from her white shirt before the meeting.
A single tache on the wall caught everyone's attention during the inspection.

  • antonym: cleanliness - the state of being clean, free from marks or stains
  • synonym: spot - a small round mark or stain
