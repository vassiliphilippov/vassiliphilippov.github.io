diagnostic

noun
test or tool for diagnosis
4.0
Pronunciation: / ccda6a59gc8n51st6ak / (AmE), / ccda6a5961c8n52st6ak / (BrE)
a test, procedure, or tool used to identify the cause of a problem in a patient, device, or system.

Examples

A medical diagnostic
The doctor ordered a chest diagnostic to determine the cause of the patient's persistent cough.
The technician ran a quick diagnostic and found a corrupted driver causing the computer to crash.
Before the road trip she took the car in for a full diagnostic to check for hidden engine problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scan - a medical or technical imaging procedure that looks inside a system or body to find problems
  • synonym: test - a procedure or examination used to discover information or measure performance; often used to identify a problem
