hypercube

noun
multidimensional data structure
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪpərˌkjuːb / (AmE), / ˈhaɪpəkjuːb / (BrE)
a multidimensional data structure used in data analysis to organize and summarize information across several dimensions

Examples

A data hypercube
The analyst used a hypercube to compare sales data across different regions and time periods.
Business intelligence tools often rely on the hypercube structure for fast data retrieval.
By slicing the hypercube, users can view detailed information for specific product categories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: data cube - a three-dimensional array of values used in data analysis
