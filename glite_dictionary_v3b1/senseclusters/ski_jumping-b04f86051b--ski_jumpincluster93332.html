ski jumping

noun
sport of jumping from a ramp on skis
Pronunciation: / ski ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ski ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of jumping with skis from a specially built ramp, with the aim of achieving distance and style points

Examples

Ski jumping competition
He has been training for ski jumping since he was a child.
The Winter Olympics always feature thrilling ski jumping events.
Watching ski jumping on television can be breathtaking.
