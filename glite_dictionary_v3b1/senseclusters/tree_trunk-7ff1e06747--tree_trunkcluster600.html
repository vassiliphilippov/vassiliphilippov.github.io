tree trunk

noun
main stem of a tree
4.3 1.0
Pronunciation: / triː trʌŋk / (AmE), / triː trʌŋk / (BrE)
the main woody stem of a tree, typically covered with bark, supporting the branches and leaves above the ground

Examples

A large tree trunk
