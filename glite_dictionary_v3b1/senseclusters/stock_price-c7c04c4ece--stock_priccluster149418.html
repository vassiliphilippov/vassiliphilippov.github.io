stock price

noun
market value of a company's share
3.0
Pronunciation: / stɑk praɪs / (AmE), / stɒk praɪs / (BrE)
the price at which a share of a company is bought or sold on the stock market at a particular time

Examples

The stock price rises
Analysts predict that the stock price will continue to fluctuate throughout the year.
She regularly checks the stock price before making investment decisions.
The company's stock price soared after the announcement of the new product.

Related Terms

  • synonym: share price - the price of a single share in a company
