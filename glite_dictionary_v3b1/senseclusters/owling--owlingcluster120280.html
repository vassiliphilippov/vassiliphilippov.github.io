owling

noun
searching for owls
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of searching for, observing, or listening to owls, usually at night

Examples

Night owling trip
We spent the evening owling in the forest, hoping to spot a barn owl.
During their vacation, the group tried owling for the first time and heard several owl calls.
Many nature enthusiasts enjoy owling as a peaceful nighttime activity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: birdwatching - the activity of observing wild birds in their natural environment
  • antonym: hunting - the activity of searching for and killing wild animals
