go

verb
be awarded or allotted
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ / (BrE)
to be used, spent, or allocated for a particular purpose (money, resources, prizes)

Examples

Money goes to charity
All the scholarship money went to students in need this year.
Most of his time goes into helping local charities.
The first prize goes to Mary, who worked the hardest.
As soon as he got paid, most of his salary had already gone.
Supplies can go quickly during an emergency.
The money was all gone by the end of the week.
A large portion of the budget goes to staff salaries.
All my savings went towards buying a new car.
Most of his salary goes to paying the rent and groceries every month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: be awarded - to be given as a prize or as recognition
  • synonym: be gone - to cease to exist or be present
  • antonym: be saved - (of money) to be kept and not used
  • synonym: be spent - (of money) to be used up or paid out
  • synonym: run out - to be exhausted (especially about supplies)
