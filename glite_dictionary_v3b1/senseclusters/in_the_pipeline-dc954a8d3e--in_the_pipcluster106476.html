in the pipeline

adjective
in progress or development
3.7
Pronunciation: / ɪn ðə ˈpaɪplaɪn / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈpaɪplaɪn / (BrE)
being planned, prepared, or developed and likely to happen or appear soon

Examples

In the pipeline now

Several job openings are in the pipeline, so we're not hiring just yet.
The marketing team has several campaigns in the pipeline for next quarter.
