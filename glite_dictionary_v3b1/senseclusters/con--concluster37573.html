con

noun
a trick or scam to cheat someone
7.2 4.23/m
Pronunciation: / kÉ‘n / (AmE), / kÉ’n / (BrE)
a trick or scam in which someone is deceived, especially to cheat at gambling or to persuade someone to buy worthless property

Examples

A clever con

The lottery was just another con designed to trick people.
