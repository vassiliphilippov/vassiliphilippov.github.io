Cote d'Ivoire franc

noun
former currency of Ivory Coast
9.8
Pronunciation: / koʊt diː ˈvwɑːr ˈfræŋk / (AmE), / kəʊt diː ˈvwɑː ˈfræŋk / (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in Ivory Coast, used prior to adoption of the West African CFA franc

Examples

The Cote d'Ivoire franc currency
Before 1958, the Cote d'Ivoire franc was the standard currency throughout the country.
Collectors sometimes seek out old Cote d'Ivoire francs for their historical value.
The Cote d'Ivoire franc was replaced by the West African CFA franc in the late 1950s.
