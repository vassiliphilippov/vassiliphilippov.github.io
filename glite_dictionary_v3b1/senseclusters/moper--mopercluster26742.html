moper

noun
idle wanderer
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊpər / (AmE), / ˈməʊpə / (BrE)
a person who moves slowly or idly, often wandering about without purpose

Examples

A slow moper
The old moper spent his afternoons strolling aimlessly through the park.
Instead of helping with chores, he acted like a moper and wasted the whole day.
Teachers encouraged the moper in class to participate more actively in group activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drifter - a person who moves from place to place without a fixed home or purpose
  • antonym: go-getter - a person who is energetic and ambitious
  • synonym: loafer - a person who avoids work and spends time idly
