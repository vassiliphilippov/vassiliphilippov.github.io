gramineous plant

noun
plant of the grass family
9.6 7.1
Pronunciation: /græm.ɪ.ˈniː.əs plænt/ /; / /grə.ˈmɪn.i.əs plænt/ (AmE), /græm.ɪ.ˈniː.əs plɑːnt/ /; / /grə.ˈmɪn.i.əs plɑːnt/ (BrE)
A plant belonging to the grass family, typically with hollow jointed stems and long narrow leaves.

Examples

Bamboo is an unusual gramineous plant because of its woody stem.
Many gramineous plants serve as vital food sources for both livestock and humans.
