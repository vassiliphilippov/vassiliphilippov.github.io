waste of skin

noun
insult for a worthless person
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪst əv skɪn / (AmE), / ˈweɪst əv skɪn / (BrE)
A highly insulting expression referring to a person considered utterly worthless or useless.

Examples

A waste of skin
Calling someone a waste of skin is extremely harsh and offensive.
He was unfairly labeled a waste of skin by his critics.
In the heated argument, she shouted that he was just a waste of skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: good-for-nothing - a person who is lazy or useless
  • synonym: loser - a person who is habitually unsuccessful or lacking in merit
  • antonym: valuable person - someone considered important or useful
