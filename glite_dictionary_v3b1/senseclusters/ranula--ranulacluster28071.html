ranula

noun
cyst on underside of tongue
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈræn.jʊ.lə / (AmE), / ˈran.jʊ.lə / (BrE)
a cyst that forms on the underside of the tongue, often caused by obstruction of a salivary gland

Examples

A ranula cyst
A ranula can cause discomfort and may interfere with speaking or eating.
After noticing a small lump, she scheduled an appointment and learned it was a ranula.
The dentist diagnosed the swelling as a ranula and recommended surgical removal.
