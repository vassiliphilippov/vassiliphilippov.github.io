white mangrove

noun
coastal shrub or tree of West Africa and tropical America
1.5
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈmæŋˌɡroʊv /; / waɪt ˈmæŋɡroʊv / (AmE), / waɪt ˈmæŋˌɡrəʊv /; / waɪt ˈmæŋɡrəʊv / (BrE)
a tropical tree or shrub (Laguncularia racemosa) that grows in brackish coastal waters, important for the environment and sometimes used as a source of tannin
