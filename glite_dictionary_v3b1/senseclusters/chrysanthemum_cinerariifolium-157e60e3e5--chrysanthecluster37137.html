Chrysanthemum cinerariifolium

proper noun
white-flowered Balkan plant, source of pyrethrum
9.9
Pronunciation: /krɪ.ˈsæn.θə.məm/ (AmE), /krɪ.ˈsæn.θə.məm/ (BrE)
A species of white-flowered plant native to the Balkan region, known for its pinnate, silky-hairy leaves and as a primary source of natural pyrethrum insecticide.
