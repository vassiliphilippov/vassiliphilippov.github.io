ohia

noun
Hawaiian tree blossom
Pronunciation: / oʊˈhiːə / (AmE), / əʊˈhiːə / (BrE)
the blossom of the ohia tree, especially known as lehua in Hawaii

Examples

Red ohia flower
The bright red ohia decorated the tables at the traditional Hawaiian feast.
Legend says picking a ohia blossom can bring rain to the island.
Tourists admired the unique beauty of the ohia growing on the volcano's slopes.
