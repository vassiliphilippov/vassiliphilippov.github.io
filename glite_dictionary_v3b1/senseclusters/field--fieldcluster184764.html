field

noun
area containing natural resources
4.1
Pronunciation: / fiːld / (AmE), / fiːld / (BrE)
an area of land or sea where natural resources such as minerals, oil, or gas are found and extracted

Examples

Oil field
Geologists are surveying the gold fields in Western Australia for potential mining sites.
Many towns grew rapidly after fields of valuable minerals were found nearby.
The oil company discovered a new field in the North Sea and began drilling operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deposit - a natural accumulation of minerals or other valuable materials
