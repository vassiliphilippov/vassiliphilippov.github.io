partaker

noun
person who takes part in something
3.6
Pronunciation: / pɑrˈteɪkər / (AmE), / pɑːˈteɪkə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in or shares something with others, especially an activity, experience, or benefit.

Examples

A partaker in activities

Every partaker in the ceremony was given a special token to commemorate the event.
She was an eager partaker of the team's success after months of hard work.
