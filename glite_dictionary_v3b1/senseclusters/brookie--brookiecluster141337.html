brookie

noun
brownie-cookie dessert
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʊki / (AmE), / ˈbrʊki / (BrE)
a dessert made by combining a brownie and a cookie, often baked together as one treat

Examples

Chocolate brookie dessert
I baked a batch of brookies for the party, and everyone loved them.
A brookie is perfect if you can't decide between a brownie and a cookie.
The bakery sells a delicious brookie with a gooey chocolate center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brownie - a small, rich chocolate cake
  • synonym: cookie - a sweet baked treat, usually flat and round
