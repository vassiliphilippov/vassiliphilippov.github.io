hypersensitize

verb
to increase sensitivity
8.4
Pronunciation: /haɪ.pər.ˈsen.sə.taɪz/ /; / /haɪ.pər.ˈsen.sɪ.taɪz/ (AmE), /haɪ.pə.ˈsen.sə.taɪz/ /; / /haɪ.pə.ˈsen.sɪ.taɪz/ (BrE)
to increase the sensitivity of a material or device so it responds to lower levels of light or signal

Examples

Special chemicals are used to hypersensitize sensors for scientific experiments.
Researchers hypersensitized the detector to improve its performance in low-signal environments.
