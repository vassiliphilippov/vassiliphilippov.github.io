fine-leafed

adjective
having slender or narrow leaves
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪn ˌliːft / (AmE), / ˈfaɪn ˌliːft / (BrE)
(of a plant) having slender, narrow, or delicately divided leaves.

Examples

Fine-leafed plants
Some species of acacia are known for their fine-leafed appearance.
The fine-leafed grasses blend beautifully with colorful wildflowers in the garden.
The landscaper recommended fine-leafed plants to create a delicate texture in the flower bed.
