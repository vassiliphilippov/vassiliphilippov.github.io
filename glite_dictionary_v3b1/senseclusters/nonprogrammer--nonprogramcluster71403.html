nonprogrammer

noun
person not coding
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈproʊɡræmər / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈprəʊɡræmə / (BrE)
a person who does not write computer programs or is not skilled in programming

Examples

A nonprogrammer user
The new app is so simple that any nonprogrammer can set it up in minutes.
Workshops are available for nonprogrammers interested in learning the basics of coding.
As a nonprogrammer, she appreciated the user-friendly design of the website.
