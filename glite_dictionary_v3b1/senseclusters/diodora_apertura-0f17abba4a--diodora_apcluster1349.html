Diodora apertura

noun
species of marine limpet
Pronunciation: / di.oʊˈdɔːrə əˈpɝː.tʃʊ.rə / (AmE), / di.əˈdɔː.rə əˈpɜː.tjʊ.rə / (BrE)
A species of marine limpet with a conical shell that has an opening at the apex, belonging to the family Fissurellidae.

Examples

Diodora apertura shell opening
A key feature distinguishing Diodora apertura from other limpets is the oval hole at the shell's apex.
Researchers observed the unique shell opening in Diodora apertura specimens collected from the Pacific coastline.
The Diodora apertura can be found clinging to rocks in intertidal zones.
