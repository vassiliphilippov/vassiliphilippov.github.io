Phallus ravenelii

noun
species of stinkhorn fungus
Pronunciation: / ˈfæləs rævəˈnɛli.aɪ / (AmE), / ˈfaləs rævəˈniːlaɪ / (BrE)
A species of stinkhorn fungus, characterized by a cap with a granular surface at the apex and a smell resembling decaying flesh.
