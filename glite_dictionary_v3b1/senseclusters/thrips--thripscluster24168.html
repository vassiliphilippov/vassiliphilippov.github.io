thrips

noun
tiny plant-sucking insect
9.1 8.2
Pronunciation: /θrɪps/ (AmE), /θrɪps/ (BrE)
A small insect with slender, feathery wings, many species of which feed on plant sap and can cause damage to crops and plants.

Examples
