unaggressively

adverb
without aggression
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈɡrɛsɪvli / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈɡrɛsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that is not aggressive, forceful, or confrontational

Examples

To act unaggressively
The dog approached the children unaggressively, wagging its tail and waiting to be petted.
She spoke unaggressively during the meeting, making her points calmly and respectfully.
The two teams played unaggressively, focusing on fair play rather than competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aggressively - in a forceful or confrontational manner
  • synonym: gently - in a mild or kind manner
