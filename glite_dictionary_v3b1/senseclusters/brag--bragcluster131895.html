brag

noun
boastful statement or behavior
9.1
Pronunciation: / bræɡ / (AmE), / bræɡ / (BrE)
A boastful statement or act; an instance of showing off or talking with excessive pride about something.

Examples

A brag about success
Despite his brag about winning the competition, no one seemed impressed.
Every brag he made was met with rolled eyes from the group.
Her constant brag of having the best grades annoyed her classmates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boast - an act of talking with excessive pride
  • antonym: modesty - lack of boastfulness or humility
