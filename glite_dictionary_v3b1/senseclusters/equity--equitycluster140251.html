equity

noun
fairness; just treatment
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkwɪti / (AmE), / ˈɛkwɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being fair or just; adherence to rules or standards of fairness

Examples

Workplace equity
All employees should be treated with equity in the workplace.
She demanded equity in the division of the inheritance.
The new policy was praised for promoting equity among students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fairness - the quality of being fair and reasonable
  • antonym: inequity - lack of fairness or justice
  • synonym: justice - the quality of being just, impartial, or fair
