eucrite

noun
basaltic meteorite
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈjuː.kraɪt/ (AmE), /ˈjuː.kraɪt/ (BrE)
a type of stony meteorite made mostly of basaltic rock, often originating from the asteroid Vesta

Examples

A eucrite meteorite

Many eucrites are believed to have come from the surface of the asteroid Vesta.
A rare eucrite was discovered in the desert, exciting researchers interested in planetary science.
