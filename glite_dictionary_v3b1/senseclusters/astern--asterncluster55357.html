Synonyms

  • aft
    7.0 2/m
    aft is used as a simple direction toward a ship's rear and for rear parts, while astern means behind the ship.
  • sternward
    8.1 0.00/m
    sternward points directly toward the stern in a formal or technical way, while astern means behind or toward the rear more generally.
  • abaft
    9.9 0.00/m
    abaft describes position toward a ship's stern often with 'of' and a following noun, while astern means simply behind the ship.
