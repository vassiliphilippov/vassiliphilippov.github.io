creamy

adjective
smooth and thick like cream
1.1 5.51/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkriÀêmi / (AmE), / ÀàkriÀêmi / (BrE)
having a smooth, thick, and rich texture like that of cream

Examples

Creamy texture
She prefers creamy desserts like cheesecake and mousse.
The lotion felt creamy as I rubbed it into my skin.
This soup has a creamy texture that makes it very satisfying.

Synonyms

  • buttery
    3.2 1.52/m
    buttery is rich and smooth with a mild butter taste, while creamy focuses more on a thick, silky texture like cream.
  • velvety
    3.8 1.27/m
    velvety feels soft and smooth on the tongue, while creamy suggests a thicker, richer texture like cream.
