make it

verb
to survive after adversity
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪk ɪt / (AmE), / ˈmeɪk ɪt / (BrE)
to succeed in surviving or enduring after adversity, danger, or hardship

Examples

Make it through hard times
Despite all the difficulties she faced, she managed to make it through college.
Many businesses don’t make it past their first year of operation.
The doctors weren’t sure he would make it, but he pulled through after the surgery.
