butterfish

noun
small Atlantic food fish
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ɚˌfɪʃ /; / ˈbʌt̬.ɚˌfɪʃ /; / ˈbʌtərˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌfɪʃ /; / ˈbʌt.əˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
