intrathoracic

adjective
within the chest
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəθəˈræsɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəθəˈræsɪk / (BrE)
located or occurring within the chest cavity

Examples

Intrathoracic tumor
The X-ray revealed an intrathoracic tumor pressing against the lung.
Doctors monitored the patient's intrathoracic pressure during surgery.
An intrathoracic infection can cause severe breathing difficulties.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extrathoracic - located outside the chest cavity
  • synonym: thoracic - relating to the chest or thorax
