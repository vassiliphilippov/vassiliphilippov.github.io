cabriole

noun
ballet leap or curved leg
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkæbriˈoʊl / (AmE), / ˈkæbrɪəʊl / (BrE)
a ballet leap in which one leg is extended and the other beats against it in the air, or a curved furniture leg with an outward and inward curve

Examples

Cabriole furniture leg
The dancer executed a flawless cabriole in the middle of her solo performance.
The antique chair featured elegant cabriole legs that curved gracefully outward and inward.
Learning to perform a cabriole requires strength and precise timing in ballet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jeté - a ballet leap in which a dancer jumps from one foot to the other
