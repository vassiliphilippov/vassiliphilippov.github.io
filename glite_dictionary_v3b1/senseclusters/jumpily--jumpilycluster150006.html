jumpily

adverb
nervously, with startle
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmpɪli / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpɪli / (BrE)
in a nervous, easily startled, or restless way, often with sudden or jerky movements

Examples

Look jumpily around
He glanced jumpily around the room after hearing a loud noise outside.
The cat moved jumpily whenever someone entered the room unexpectedly.
She spoke jumpily, her voice betraying her nervousness during the interview.
The squirrel darted jumpily from branch to branch, searching for food.
She answered the phone jumpily, startled by the sudden ringing.
The puppy moved jumpily around the room, excited to see its owner.

  • synonym: nervously - in an anxious or uneasy way
