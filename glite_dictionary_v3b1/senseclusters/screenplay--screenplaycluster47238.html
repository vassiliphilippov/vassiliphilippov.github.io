screenplay

noun
film script including dialogue and descriptions
Pronunciation: / ˈskrinˌpleɪ / (AmE), / ˈskriːnˌpleɪ / (BrE)
the script of a film, including dialogue and descriptions of characters and settings

Examples

A screenplay script
After reading the screenplay, the director agreed to make the movie.
She spent two years writing the screenplay for her first feature film.
The screenplay received an award for best original script at the festival.
