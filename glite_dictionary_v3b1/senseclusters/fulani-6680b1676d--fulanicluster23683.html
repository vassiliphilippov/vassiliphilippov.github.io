Fulani

noun
West African language family
8.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / fuÀàl…ëÀêni / (AmE), / f äÀàl…ëÀêni / (BrE)
a family of closely related languages spoken by the Fulani people of West Africa, used as a lingua franca from Senegal to Chad

Examples

Fulani language
As a lingua franca, Fulani helps people from different ethnic groups communicate.
She wrote her thesis on the structure of Fulani verbs.
The Fulani language is spoken by millions of people across West Africa.
