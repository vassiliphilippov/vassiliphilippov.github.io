stewy

adjective
anxious or brooding
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːi / (AmE), / ˈstjuːi / (BrE)
feeling anxious, worried, or brooding, especially in a restless or agitated way (informal)

Examples

Feeling stewy inside
He became stewy waiting for the results of his job interview.
Whenever she has too much to do, she gets a little stewy and can't relax.
The team felt stewy before the big match, pacing nervously in the locker room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anxious - feeling worried or uneasy
  • antonym: calm - not feeling nervous or agitated
