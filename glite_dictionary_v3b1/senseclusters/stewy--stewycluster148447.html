stewy

adjective
anxious or brooding
8.6 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàstuÀêi / (AmE), / ÀàstjuÀêi / (BrE)
feeling anxious, worried, or brooding, especially in a restless or agitated way (informal)

Examples
