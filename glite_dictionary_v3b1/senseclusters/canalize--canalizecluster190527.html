canalize

verb
provide an area with canals
9.6 7.1
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈnæl.aɪz/ (AmE), /kə.ˈnæl.aɪz/ (BrE)
To provide (a city or area) with canals for transportation or drainage.

Examples

The city plans to canalize the river to improve transportation and control flooding.
Efforts to canalise the river through the old town have improved transportation.
The engineers were hired to canalise the city to prevent future flooding.
Urban planners proposed to canalise the lowlands to boost local agriculture.
