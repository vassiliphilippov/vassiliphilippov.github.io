go around

verb
move or turn circularly around a center
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ɡəʊ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to move or turn in a circle around a point, axis, or center

Examples

The fan goes around
The ceiling fan goes around all day to keep the room cool.
The planets go around the Sun in elliptical orbits.
When the Ferris wheel goes around, you can see the whole city from the top.

Related Terms

  • synonym: revolve - move in a circular orbit around a central point
  • synonym: rotate - turn in a circle around an axis or center
