go around

verb
move or turn circularly around a center
1.0 1.8
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ɡəʊ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)

Examples

The planets go around the Sun in elliptical orbits.
