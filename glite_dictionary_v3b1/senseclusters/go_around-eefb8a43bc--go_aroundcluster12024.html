Synonyms

  • revolve
    3.4 7.27/m
    revolve is used for objects that move in a steady orbit around a center, while go around is more general movement in a circle.
  • rotate
    3.0 12/m
    rotate is used when something turns on its own axis, while go around describes moving in a circle around a point.
  • circle
    1.8 14/m
    circle means to move all the way around something along a path, while go around can mean more general turning or passing around it.
  • spin around
    2.1 3.16/m
    spin around means to turn very quickly on one's axis, while go around means to move in a circle more generally.
