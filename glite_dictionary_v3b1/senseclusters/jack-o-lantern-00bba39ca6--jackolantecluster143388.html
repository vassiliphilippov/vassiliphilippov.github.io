jack-o'-lantern

noun
carved pumpkin lantern for Halloween
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒæk oʊ ˌlæntɚn / (AmE), / ˈdʒak əʊ ˌlæntən / (BrE)
A lantern made by hollowing out a pumpkin, carving a face into it, and placing a light inside, typically used as a decoration at Halloween.

Examples

Halloween jack-o'-lantern pumpkin
She carefully carved a scary face into the jack-o'-lantern.
The candle inside the jack-o'-lantern flickered in the dark.
We placed a jack-o'-lantern on our porch for Halloween.
