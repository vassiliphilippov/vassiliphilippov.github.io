Ligurian

noun
person from Liguria
Pronunciation: / lɪˈɡjʊriən / (AmE), / lɪˈɡjʊəriən / (BrE)
a person who is an inhabitant or native of Liguria, a region in northwestern Italy

Examples

A Ligurian person
A friendly Ligurian welcomed us to the coastal village and shared stories about the region.
Many Ligurians are proud of their unique traditions and delicious cuisine.
The museum features artifacts from ancient Ligurians who once lived in the area.
