Long Island Sound

proper noun
estuarine body of water between Long Island and Connecticut
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ ˈaɪ.lənd saʊnd / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈaɪ.lənd saʊnd / (BrE)
an estuarine body of water located between Long Island (New York) and the state of Connecticut, forming part of the Atlantic coast of the United States

Examples

Long Island Sound waters
Environmental efforts are underway to protect the waters of Long Island Sound.
The beaches along Long Island Sound are popular destinations in the summer.
The ferry provides a scenic route across Long Island Sound from Connecticut to Long Island.
