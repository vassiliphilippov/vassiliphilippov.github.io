House of Commons

noun
lower house of the British Parliament
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌhaʊs əv ˈkɑːmənz / (AmE), / ˌhaʊs əv ˈkɒmənz / (BrE)
the elected lower chamber of the British Parliament, responsible for legislation and representing the public

Examples
