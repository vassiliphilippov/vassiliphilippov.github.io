mean

noun
mathematical average value
3.5 18/m
Pronunciation: / min / (AmE), / miÀên / (BrE)
the value obtained by dividing the sum of a set of numbers by the number of values in the set; an average

Examples

The mean of numbers
In statistics, the mean is often used to summarize data sets.
The teacher calculated the mean score of the test results for the whole class.
To find the mean, add all the numbers together and divide by the quantity of values.

Synonyms

  • arithmetic mean
    3.0 0.15/m
    arithmetic mean is the sum of numbers divided by their count, while mean is the general word for that average.
  • mean value
    3.4 0.78/m
    mean value is a technical term using a specific formula for average, while mean is the common word for an average.

Related Terms

  • synonym: average - the result obtained by adding several quantities together and then dividing by the number of quantities
  • degree lower: median - the middle value in a list of numbers
  • degree lower: mode - the value that appears most frequently in a data set
