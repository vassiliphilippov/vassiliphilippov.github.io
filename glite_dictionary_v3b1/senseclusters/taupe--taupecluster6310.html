taupe

adjective
dusky brownish-gray color
Pronunciation: / toʊp / (AmE), / təʊp / (BrE)
of a dusky brownish-gray color, often used to describe fabrics, paint, or other materials.

Examples

Taupe curtains
I prefer taupe curtains because the color goes with almost any decor.
She chose a taupe dress for the wedding because it complemented her skin tone.
The living room was painted in a soft taupe that felt both cozy and modern.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: beige - a pale sandy yellow-brown color
  • synonym: brown - a color produced by mixing red, yellow, and blue, and containing no blue or green
  • synonym: gray - a color between black and white
