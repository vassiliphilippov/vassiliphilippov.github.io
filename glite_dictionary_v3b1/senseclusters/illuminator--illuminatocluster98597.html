illuminator

noun
one who explains clearly
5.3
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈluː.mə.neɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈluː.mə.neɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person who explains or clarifies something, making it easier to understand

Examples

A clear illuminator
The professor was an excellent illuminator of complex scientific theories.
As an illuminator, she helped her classmates grasp difficult concepts in mathematics.
Writers often act as illuminators by making challenging topics accessible to a wider audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confuser - a person who makes things more difficult to understand
  • synonym: explainer - a person who makes something clear or easy to understand
  • synonym: teacher - a person who helps others learn or understand
