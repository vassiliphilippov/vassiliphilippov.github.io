Orycteropus afer

noun
African burrowing mammal; aardvark
Pronunciation: / ɔːˌrɪktəˈroʊpəs ˈæfər / (AmE), / ˌɒrɪktəˈrəʊpəs ˈɑːfə / (BrE)
A nocturnal, burrowing mammal native to Africa, known for feeding on termites and being the only living species in the order Tubulidentata.
