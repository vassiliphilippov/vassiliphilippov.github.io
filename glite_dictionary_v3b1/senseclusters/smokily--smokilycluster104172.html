smokily

adverb
in a sultry manner
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊkɪli / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is sultry, husky, or suggestive, especially when describing a voice, look, or expression

Examples

She sings smokily
She spoke smokily, her voice low and full of mystery.
The singer gazed smokily at the audience, captivating everyone in the room.
He smiled smokily, giving her a look that was both inviting and enigmatic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: innocently - in a way that shows a lack of experience or guile
  • synonym: seductively - in a way that is sexually attractive or alluring
