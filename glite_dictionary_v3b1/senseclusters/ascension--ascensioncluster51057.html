ascension

noun
the act of moving upwards
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛnʃən / (AmE), / əˈsɛnʃən / (BrE)
the act of moving or rising upwards, either literally (like climbing) or figuratively (like advancing in a career)

Examples

Career ascension
His rapid ascension in the company surprised many of his colleagues.
The ascension of the hot air balloon was slow and steady.
The rocket's ascension into the sky was watched by thousands of spectators.
Her ascension to the top of the mountain was both challenging and exhilarating.
The rocket's ascension was clearly visible against the morning sky.
The sudden ascension of the hot-air balloon drew gasps from the crowd.

Related Terms

  • antonym: descent - the act of moving downwards
  • synonym: rise - an act of moving upward
