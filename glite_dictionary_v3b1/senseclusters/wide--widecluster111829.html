Synonyms

  • widely
    1.0 50/m
    widely is used to mean by many people or in many places, while wide is an adverb for large extent or distance.
  • extensively
    4.1 10/m
    extensively describes something done over a large area or in great depth, while wide often means as far as possible or very much.
  • broadly
    2.7 3/m
    broadly is used to speak in general or with wide scope, while wide focuses on degree, distance, or full openness.
  • far and wide
    2.6 1/m
    far and wide means in many places across a large area, while wide can mean to a great extent rather than many locations.
