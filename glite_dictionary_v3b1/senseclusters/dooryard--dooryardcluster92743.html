dooryard

noun
yard by the door of a house
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔr.jɑrd / (AmE), / ˈdɔː.jɑːd / (BrE)
A yard or area immediately outside the front or rear door of a house.

Examples

The dooryard outside
Children played in the dooryard until dusk.
She planted tulips in the dooryard every spring.
The house had a small dooryard with a white picket fence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: garden - a piece of ground for growing flowers or vegetables, often near a home
