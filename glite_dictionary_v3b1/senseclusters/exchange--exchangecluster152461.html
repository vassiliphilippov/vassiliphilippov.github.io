exchange

noun
marketplace for buying and selling stocks or commodities
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
a place or organization where stocks, shares, or commodities are bought and sold, typically limited to accredited members

Examples

Stock exchange
He became a member of the commodities exchange after years of training.
The New York Stock Exchange is one of the world's largest financial markets.
Trading on the exchange closes at 4 p.m. every weekday.
