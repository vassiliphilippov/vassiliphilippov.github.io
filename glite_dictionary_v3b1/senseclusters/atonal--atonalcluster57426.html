atonal

adjective
lacking a tonal center (music)
Pronunciation: / eɪˈtoʊnəl / (AmE), / eɪˈtəʊnəl / (BrE)
lacking a key or tonal center; characterized by the avoidance of traditional western tonality, especially in music.

Examples

Atonal music
Many twentieth-century composers experimented with atonal music to break free from traditional harmonies.
She found the atonal composition challenging but fascinating to perform.
The film's soundtrack features an atonal score that creates a sense of unease.
