candied

adjective
fruit or nuts preserved with sugar
Pronunciation: / ˈkændid / (AmE), / ˈkændid / (BrE)
(of fruit, nuts, etc.) preserved or coated with sugar or syrup

Recipes often use candied ginger to add a sweet and spicy flavor.
