tartar steak

noun
raw seasoned ground beef dish
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːrtər steɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɑːtə steɪk / (BrE)
A dish of raw ground beef mixed with raw egg and seasonings such as onions, capers, and anchovies, served uncooked.

Examples

Eating tartar steak
Eating tartar steak requires using only the freshest beef for safety.
He ordered the tartar steak as an appetizer at the French restaurant.
The chef prepared tartar steak with onions, capers, and a raw egg on top.

Related Terms

  • synonym: steak tartare - dish made of raw ground beef mixed with seasonings and egg, served raw
