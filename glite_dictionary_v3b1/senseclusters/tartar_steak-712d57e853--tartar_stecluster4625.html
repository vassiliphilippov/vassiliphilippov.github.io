tartar steak

noun
raw seasoned ground beef dish
8.1 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtɑːr.tər steɪk/ /; / /ˈtɑːr.tɑːr steɪk/ (AmE), /ˈtɑː.tə steɪk/ (BrE)
