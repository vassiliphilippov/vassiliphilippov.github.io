Seidlitz powders

noun
effervescent medicinal laxative powder
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdlɪts ˌpaʊdərz / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdlɪts ˌpaʊdəz / (BrE)
a type of effervescent medicinal powder containing sodium bicarbonate, Rochelle salt, and tartaric acid, formerly used as a laxative
