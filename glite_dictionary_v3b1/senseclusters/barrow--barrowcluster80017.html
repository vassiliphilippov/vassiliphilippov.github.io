barrow

noun
handcart with wheels for small loads
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbæroʊ / (AmE), / ˈbærəʊ / (BrE)
a cart with handles and one or more wheels, used for carrying small loads, especially by hand

Examples

A barrow of bricks
He loaded the sand into the barrow and wheeled it across the yard.
The gardener used a barrow to move soil to the flower beds.
Vendors at the market sell fruit from a barrow on the sidewalk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wheelbarrow - a small cart with a single wheel, used for carrying loads
