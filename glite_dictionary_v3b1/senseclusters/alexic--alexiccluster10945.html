alexic

adjective
relating to alexia (inability to read)
Pronunciation: / əˈlɛksɪk / (AmE), / əˈlɛksɪk / (BrE)
relating to or showing symptoms of alexia (the loss of the ability to read)

Examples

Alexic symptoms
Certain brain injuries may cause an alexic condition affecting reading abilities.
He was diagnosed as alexic after a thorough neurological examination.
The patient exhibited alexic symptoms following the stroke.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alexia - loss of the ability to read, usually due to brain injury
