piece

noun
playing token in board games
1.0
Pronunciation: / pis / (AmE), / piːs / (BrE)
an individual item or object that is part of a set or group, such as a chess piece or a puzzle piece

Examples

Chess piece
Each player selects a piece and places it at the starting position.
She carefully moved her piece across the chessboard and captured her opponent's rook.
The board game was missing one piece, so we couldn't start playing.
Every piece in the puzzle fits perfectly together.
She bought a beautiful piece of pottery at the market.
This piece of equipment is essential for the experiment.

  • synonym: token - an object used to represent a player in a game
  • antonym: whole - something complete, not divided into parts
