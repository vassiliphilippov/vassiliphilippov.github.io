spike moss

noun
plant of the genus Selaginella (fern ally)
5.6
Pronunciation: / spaɪk mɑs / (AmE), / spaɪk mɒs / (BrE)
Any of various small, moss-like plants of the genus Selaginella, which are fern allies found in many habitats worldwide.

Examples

Spike moss plants
Botanists are fascinated by the diversity of spike moss in tropical rainforests.
Many gardeners use spike moss as a ground cover because it thrives in moist, shady areas.
Some species of spike moss can survive periods of drought by curling up and appearing lifeless.
