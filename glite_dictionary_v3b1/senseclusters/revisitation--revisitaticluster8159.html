revisitation

noun
subsequent visit
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌvɪzɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌvɪzɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a subsequent visit to a person, place, or institution, often for follow-up, further treatment, or evaluation

Examples

A revisitation appointment
The patient's revisitation was scheduled two weeks after her initial appointment.
A revisitation of the museum allowed him to notice new exhibits he missed before.
After the repairs, a revisitation by the inspector was required to ensure everything was safe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: follow-up - an additional visit or check after an initial one
  • degree lower: visit - an act of going to see a person or place
