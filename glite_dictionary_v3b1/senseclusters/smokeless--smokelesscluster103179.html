smokeless

adjective
emitting little or no smoke
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊkˌləs / (AmE), / ˈsməʊk.ləs / (BrE)
Emitting or producing little or no smoke.

Examples

Smokeless zones
Many cities now require smokeless zones to improve air quality.
Our company developed a smokeless incinerator for safer waste disposal.
The new stove burns smokeless fuel to reduce air pollution.
