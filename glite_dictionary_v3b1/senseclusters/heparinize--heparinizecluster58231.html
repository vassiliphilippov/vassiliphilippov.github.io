heparinize

verb
to add heparin
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛpəˌrɪnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈhɛpəˌrɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to treat something, especially blood, with heparin to prevent clotting

Examples

To heparinize blood
The nurse will heparinize the catheter to keep it from becoming blocked.
It is important to heparinize blood samples before certain laboratory tests.
Doctors sometimes heparinize medical equipment to prevent blood clots during surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anticoagulate - to prevent blood from clotting
