caller

noun
person initiating a telephone call
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔlər / (AmE), / ˈkɔːlə / (BrE)
a person who makes or initiates a telephone call

Examples

Unknown caller rings
Emergency services can trace the location of the caller in case of an accident.
Please state your name so the caller knows who you are.
The caller left a voicemail when no one answered the phone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: recipient - the person who receives a telephone call
