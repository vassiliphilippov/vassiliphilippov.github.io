sunwise

adverb
clockwise direction
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌwaɪz / (BrE)
in the direction of the sun's apparent movement across the sky, usually clockwise in the Northern Hemisphere

Examples

Move sunwise around
The parade moved sunwise around the town square, following an old tradition.
During the ceremony, everyone walked sunwise to bring good luck.
The children ran sunwise around the tree, laughing and shouting.
