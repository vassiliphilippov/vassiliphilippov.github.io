brumal

adjective
relating to winter
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːməl / (AmE), / ˈbruːməl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of winter; wintry.

Examples

A brumal night
After the first snowfall, the village took on a brumal serenity.
The brumal winds swept across the frosted fields, sending everyone indoors.
The poet described the landscape in brumal hues, evoking the silence of a snowy night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: vernal - relating to spring
  • synonym: wintry - relating to winter; cold and bleak, like winter
