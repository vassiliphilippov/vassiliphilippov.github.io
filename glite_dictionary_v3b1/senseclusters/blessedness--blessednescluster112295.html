blessedness

noun
state of supreme happiness
Pronunciation: / ˈblɛsɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈblɛsɪdnɪs / (BrE)
a state of supreme happiness, especially as a result of spiritual or religious fulfillment

Examples

The blessedness of giving
Many consider the blessedness of giving to be greater than that of receiving.
She felt a profound sense of blessedness as she watched her children play.
The monk spoke of the blessedness he found in a life of contemplation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: happiness - state of being happy
  • antonym: misery - state of great distress or unhappiness
