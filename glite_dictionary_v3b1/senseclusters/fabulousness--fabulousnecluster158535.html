fabulousness

noun
mythical quality
Pronunciation: / ˈfæbjələsnəs / (AmE), / ˈfæbjʊləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extraordinary, mythical, or fantastical, often resembling something from a fable or legend

Examples

The fabulousness of legends
The fabulousness of the ancient tales captured the imagination of every listener.
Her costume radiated such fabulousness that everyone at the party admired her creativity.
The movie's visual fabulousness made it feel like a journey into a magical world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fantasticalness - the quality of being imaginative or unreal
  • antonym: ordinariness - the quality of being normal or unremarkable
