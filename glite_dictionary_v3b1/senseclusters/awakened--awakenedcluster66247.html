awakened

adjective
having been woken up
Pronunciation: / əˈweɪkənd / (AmE), / əˈweɪkənd / (BrE)
Having been woken from sleep; no longer asleep.

Examples

Awakened by noise
After the alarm rang, everyone in the dormitory was awakened and started getting ready.
He felt confused and awakened by the sudden noise outside his window.
The awakened baby began to cry loudly in the nursery.
