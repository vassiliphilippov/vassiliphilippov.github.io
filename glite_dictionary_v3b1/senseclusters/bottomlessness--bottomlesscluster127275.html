bottomlessness

noun
state of being bottomless; infinite depth
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑtəm.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈbɒt.əm.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being extremely deep or seemingly without limit.

Examples

The bottomlessness of a pit
She stared into the bottomlessness of the pit, unable to see the ground below.
Some people are frightened by the bottomlessness of outer space, imagining it goes on forever.
The bottomlessness of the ocean has inspired myths and legends for centuries.

  • synonym: limitlessness - the state of having no limits
  • antonym: shallowness - the property of being shallow
