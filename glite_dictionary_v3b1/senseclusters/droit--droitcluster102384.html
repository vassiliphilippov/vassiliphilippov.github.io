droit

noun
legal right or due
Pronunciation: / drwɑː / (AmE), / drwɑː / (BrE)
a legal right or due, especially in historical or legal contexts

Examples

A lord's droit
The medieval lord claimed his droit over the land.
In some old legal documents, the term droit refers to a feudal right.
The king exercised his droit to collect taxes from the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: duty - a moral or legal obligation
  • synonym: privilege - a special right or advantage
  • synonym: right - a legal or moral entitlement
