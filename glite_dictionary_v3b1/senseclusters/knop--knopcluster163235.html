knop

noun
plant bud or swelling
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɑp / (AmE), / nɒp / (BrE)
a small rounded swelling or bud on a plant from which a leaf or flower may develop

Examples

A plant knop
By mid-April each twig bore a tiny knop that quickly opened into a blossom.
The gardener pointed out the green knops forming along the rose bush.
After the rain, every branch was dotted with a fresh knop ready to grow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bud - a small growth on a plant that can develop into a flower or leaf
  • synonym: shoot - a new growth from a plant, such as a stem or branch
