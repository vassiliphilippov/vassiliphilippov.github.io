work

to manipulate, shape, or prepare materials or substances, often using hands or tools

Examples

To work the dough
He patiently worked the ingredients together to form a consistent paste.
The baker began to work the dough until it became smooth and elastic.
You need to work the clay thoroughly before shaping it into a pot.
Copper works well because it is both soft and ductile.
If the clay is too dry, it won't work properly on the wheel.
This dough does not work easily and needs more flour.
Blacksmiths work metal to create tools and weapons.
He worked the wood until it was smooth and ready for carving.
The sculptor patiently worked the clay into a beautiful figurine.
