galleass

noun
armed Mediterranean ship
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgæl.ɪ.æs/ (AmE), /ˈgæl.ɪ.æs/ (BrE)
a large, fast, heavily armed ship used in the Mediterranean during the 16th and 17th centuries

Examples

Historians studied the remains of a sunken galleass found off the coast of Italy.
