blubberhead

noun
overly tearful person
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌbərˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈblʌbəˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person who cries or sobs loudly, especially in an exaggerated or excessive way

Examples

Crying blubberhead
Stop being such a blubberhead and try to calm down.
He was called a blubberhead after bursting into tears during the movie.
Whenever she gets bad news, she turns into a real blubberhead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crybaby - a person who cries or complains too easily
