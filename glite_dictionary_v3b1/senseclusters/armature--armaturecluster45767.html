armature

noun
support framework
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːr.mə.tʃər/ /; / /ˈɑːr.mə.tjʊr/ (AmE), /ˈɑː.mə.tʃə/ /; / /ˈɑː.mə.tjʊə/ (BrE)
a framework or structure used to support a sculpture, model, or construction while it is being made

Examples

Sculpture armature
The sculptor built a sturdy armature before adding layers of clay to form the statue.
Without a proper armature, the model would collapse under its own weight.
Artists often use wire as an armature to shape and support their creations.
The artist constructed a wire armature to support the clay sculpture.
Without a sturdy armature, the statue would not keep its intended shape.
The armature was hidden beneath layers of plaster and paint in the finished artwork.

  • synonym: skeleton - the supporting structure of a body or object
