semiabstract

adjective
partly abstract in art
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈæbˌstrækt / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈab.strakt / (BrE)
(of art) partly abstract; showing stylized forms that are recognizable but not realistically depicted.

Examples

Semiabstract art style
Critics praised the artist’s semiabstract approach, which made the landscape both familiar and mysterious.
Her sculpture is semiabstract, blending recognizable animal forms with imaginative, distorted lines.
The gallery featured several semiabstract paintings that combined geometric shapes with human figures.
