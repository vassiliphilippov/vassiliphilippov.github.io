crit

noun
video‑game critical hit
9.2
Pronunciation: /krɪt/ (AmE), /krɪt/ (BrE)
(in video games) a critical hit; an attack that deals extra damage or effect, usually determined by chance or game mechanics.

Examples

A big crit hit
The rogue landed a crit on the boss, finishing the fight quickly.
When she rolled the dice, a crit doubled her damage and turned the battle around.
After months of grinding, he finally unlocked a weapon that increases the chance of a crit.
