shaping tool

noun
tool for shaping metal
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪpɪŋ tuːl / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪpɪŋ tʃuːl / (BrE)
a tool used to shape or form metal by cutting, pressing, or grinding

Examples

Automated shaping tools
Modern factories rely on automated shaping tools to mass-produce metal components.
Precision is essential when operating a shaping tool to achieve the desired metal form.
The blacksmith selected a shaping tool to refine the curve of the horseshoe.
