welfare queen

noun
derogatory term for a woman misusing welfare
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈwel.fər kwiːn/ /; / /ˈwel.feər kwiːn/ (AmE), /ˈwel.feə kwiːn/ (BrE)
a derogatory term for a woman, typically portrayed as African-American, who is perceived to exploit or misuse government welfare programs for personal gain.

Examples

welfare queen stereotype
Many activists criticize the use of the phrase welfare queen as harmful and misleading.
Tabloid headlines in the 1980s sensationalized stories about so-called welfare queens.
The politician's speech reignited debate about the stereotype of the welfare queen.
