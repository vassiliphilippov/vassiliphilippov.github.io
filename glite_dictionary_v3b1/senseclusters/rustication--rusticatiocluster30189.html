rustication

noun
temporary suspension from university
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌrʌstəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrʌstɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
Suspension of a student from a university or college for a specific period as a disciplinary measure.

Examples

University rustication
After his involvement in the prank, he faced a term of rustication.
The college handbook details the process for appealing a rustication decision.
The university threatened rustication if the protests continued.
