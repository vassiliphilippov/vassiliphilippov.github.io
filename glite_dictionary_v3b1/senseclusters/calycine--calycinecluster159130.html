calycine

adjective
relating to a calyx (flower part)
Pronunciation: / ˈkæl.ɪ.saɪn / (AmE), / ˈkæl.ɪ.siːn / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a calyx, the outermost whorl of a flower, typically consisting of sepals.
