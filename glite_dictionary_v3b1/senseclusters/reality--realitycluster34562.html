reality

noun
the state of things as they actually exist
Pronunciation: / riˈæləti /; / riˈæl.ə.ti / (AmE), / riˈæləti /; / riˈalɪti /; / rɪˈæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the state of things as they actually exist; what is real and not imagined or wished for
