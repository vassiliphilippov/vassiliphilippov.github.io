Dutch-elm beetle

noun
beetle that spreads Dutch elm disease
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌtʃ ˌɛlm ˈbiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈdʌtʃ ˌɛlm ˈbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a beetle that transmits the fungus responsible for Dutch elm disease, particularly affecting elm trees

Examples

The Dutch-elm beetle insect
If you see a Dutch-elm beetle on your tree, you should report it to local authorities.
Scientists are studying ways to control the Dutch-elm beetle to prevent the spread of disease.
The Dutch-elm beetle is a major threat to urban elm populations across Europe and North America.

Related Terms
