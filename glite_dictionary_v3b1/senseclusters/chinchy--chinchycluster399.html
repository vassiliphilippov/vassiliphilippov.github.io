chinchy

adjective
embarrassingly stingy or miserly
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪntʃi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪntʃi / (BrE)
Unwilling to spend or give; mean or miserly to an embarrassing degree.

Examples

A chinchy person
Her chinchy attitude made even small gatherings awkward when it came time to pitch in.
I can't stand how chinchy my landlord is with repairs and maintenance.
Nobody likes going out with him because he’s so chinchy about splitting the bill.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generous - willing to give or share; unselfish
  • synonym: stingy - unwilling to spend or give; mean or miserly
