daw

noun
a small black-and-grey Eurasian bird (jackdaw)
Pronunciation: / dɔ / (AmE), / dɔː / (BrE)
a small Eurasian bird of the crow family with black and grey plumage, known for its inquisitiveness and tendency to collect shiny objects; also called the jackdaw.

Examples

A daw bird
A flock of daws circled above the old stone tower at dusk.
Farmers sometimes complain that the daw steals fruit from their orchards.
The daw is often seen scavenging for food in city parks across Europe.
