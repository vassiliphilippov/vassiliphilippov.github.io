feel lost

verb
experience confusion or disorientation
3.6 0.78/m
Pronunciation: / fil l…ëst / (AmE), / fiÀêl l…íst / (BrE)
to experience confusion, uncertainty, or disorientation, often in unfamiliar situations or without usual support

Examples

To feel lost alone
After moving to the new city, he began to feel lost without his friends.
She started to feel lost when her plans suddenly changed.
Sometimes I feel lost during complicated meetings at work.
