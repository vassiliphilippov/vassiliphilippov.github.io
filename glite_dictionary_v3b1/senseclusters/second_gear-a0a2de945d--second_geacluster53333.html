second gear

noun
second lowest forward gear in a vehicle's gearbox
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd ɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ɡɪə / (BrE)
the gear in a vehicle used after first gear, for moderate speed and more power

He shifted smoothly from first to second gear after starting the engine.
