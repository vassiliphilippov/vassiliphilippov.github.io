Crete dittany

noun
aromatic shrub native to Crete
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːt ˈdɪtəni / (AmE), / ˈkriːt ˈdɪtəni / (BrE)
a small aromatic shrub (Origanum dictamnus) native to Crete, with woolly leaves and pale purple flowers, used in herbal remedies
