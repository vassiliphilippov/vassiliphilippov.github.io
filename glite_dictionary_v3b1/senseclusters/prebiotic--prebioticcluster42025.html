prebiotic

adjective
promoting gut bacteria
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːbaɪˈɑːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌpriːbaɪˈɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to substances that promote the growth or activity of beneficial microorganisms in the digestive system

Examples

Prebiotic fiber foods
Eating foods with prebiotic fibers can help support a healthy digestive system.
Some nutritionists recommend a prebiotic diet to improve gut health.
Yogurt often contains both probiotic and prebiotic ingredients for better digestion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: probiotic - containing live beneficial bacteria for the digestive system
