AOB

noun
insurance payment arrangement
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ oʊ ˈbi / (AmE), / ˌeɪ əʊ ˈbiː / (BrE)
a legal or insurance arrangement, short for 'assignment of benefits', where a policyholder allows a third party to receive claim payments directly

Examples

AOB form signed
The contractor requested an AOB so they could handle the insurance claim directly.
Signing an AOB allows your doctor to receive payment from your insurance company.
Before repairs began, the homeowner signed an AOB with the restoration company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assignment of benefits - the transfer of insurance claim payments to a third party
