classicize

verb
make classic or classical in style
Pronunciation: / ˈklæs.ɪ.saɪz /; / ˈklæsɪsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈklæs.ɪ.saɪz /; / ˈklæsɪsaɪz / (BrE)
