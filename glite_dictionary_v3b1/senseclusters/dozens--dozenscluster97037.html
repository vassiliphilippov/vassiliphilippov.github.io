dozens

noun
a large, indefinite number or amount
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌz.ənz / (AmE), / ˈdʌz.ənz / (BrE)
a large but indefinite number or amount, typically more than twelve

Examples

Dozens of shoes
She owns dozens of shoes, but still says she has nothing to wear.
There were dozens of birds on the lawn this morning.
They received dozens of letters after their story was published.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: hundreds - a very large number; several hundred
  • synonym: scores - a large amount or number of something (usually twenty or more)
