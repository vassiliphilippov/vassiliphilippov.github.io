macrolide

adjective
antibiotic-related
Pronunciation: / ˈmæk.rəˌlaɪd / (AmE), / ˈmak.rəʊ.laɪd / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of macrolide antibiotics, especially when describing resistance or properties associated with them

Examples

Macrolide resistance
Doctors are concerned about increasing macrolide resistance in certain bacterial infections.
The laboratory tested the samples for macrolide susceptibility before prescribing medication.
A macrolide antibiotic is often chosen when patients are allergic to penicillin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antibiotic - a drug used to treat bacterial infections
