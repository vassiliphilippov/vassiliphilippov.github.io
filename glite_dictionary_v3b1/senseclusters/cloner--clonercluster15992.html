cloner

noun
imitator of style
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊnər / (AmE), / ˈkləʊnə / (BrE)
a person who copies another person's creative work, style, or design without adding originality

Examples

A cloner artist
Many critics dismissed the new artist as just another cloner lacking original ideas.
The fashion industry is full of cloners who replicate popular designs each season.
He was tired of being called a cloner and wanted to develop his own unique style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imitator - a person who copies the behavior or work of others
  • antonym: originator - a person who creates or invents something new
