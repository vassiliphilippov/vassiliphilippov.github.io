Schizaea

noun
genus of small tropical ferns
Pronunciation: / skɪˈziː.ə /; / skɪˈzeɪ.ə / (AmE), / skɪˈziː.ə / (BrE)
a genus of small, slender ferns in the family Schizaeaceae, commonly known as curly grass ferns, found mostly in tropical and subtropical regions
