synovitis

noun
joint inflammation of synovial membrane
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪnəˈvaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌsɪnəʊˈvaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the synovial membrane in a joint, often causing pain and swelling

Examples

Synovitis in joints
MRI scans confirmed the presence of synovitis in the affected hip joint.
The patient experienced severe knee pain due to synovitis after a sports injury.
Treatment for synovitis often includes anti-inflammatory medications and rest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arthritis - painful inflammation and stiffness of the joints
