duplexing

noun
double-sided printing
Pronunciation: / ˈduːplɛksɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdjuːplɛksɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of printing on both sides of a sheet of paper, usually automatically by a printer

Examples

Printer duplexing
Many modern printers support duplexing to help reduce paper usage in offices.
Before printing the report, make sure duplexing is enabled in the printer settings.
Automatic duplexing can save time when producing booklets or double-sided documents.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single-sided printing - printing on only one side of a sheet of paper
