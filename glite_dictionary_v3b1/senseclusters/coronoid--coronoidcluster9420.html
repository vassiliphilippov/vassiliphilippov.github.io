coronoid

adjective
hooked or crown-shaped
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəˌnɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒrənɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to a hooked or crown-shaped anatomical structure, especially in bones

Examples

Coronoid process
The dentist pointed out the coronoid process on the X-ray of the jaw.
A fracture of the coronoid bone can affect elbow movement.
The surgeon carefully avoided the coronoid structure during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coronal - relating to a crown or resembling a crown
