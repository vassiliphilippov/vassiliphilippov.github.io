viridescent

adjective
greenish in color
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪrɪˈdɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌvɪrɪˈdɛsənt / (BrE)
having a greenish color or becoming green in appearance

Examples

Viridescent leaves
The artist chose a viridescent shade to capture the freshness of spring in her painting.
After weeks of rain, the hills became noticeably more viridescent and lively.
The insect's wings shimmered with a viridescent glow in the sunlight.

  • synonym: verdant - covered with green vegetation or lush with greenery
