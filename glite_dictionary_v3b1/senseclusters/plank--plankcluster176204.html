plank

verb
cover or lay with planks
1.7
Pronunciation: / plæŋk / (AmE), / plæŋk / (BrE)
to cover or lay with planks, especially wood boards placed side by side

Examples

To plank a walkway
The backyard path was planked to give it a rustic look.
The old pier was planked with sturdy oak boards to ensure safety.
Workers quickly planked the muddy street to make passage easier for carts.
