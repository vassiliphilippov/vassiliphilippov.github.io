anchoress

noun
female religious recluse
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋkəˌrɛs / (AmE), / ˈaŋkərɪs / (BrE)
a woman who chooses to live in seclusion, often in a small cell, for religious devotion and prayer

Examples

The anchoress in cell
The medieval anchoress spent her life in prayer and solitude.
Pilgrims often visited the anchoress to seek spiritual advice through her window.
Historical records describe an anchoress who lived walled into a church cell for decades.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anchorite - a person who lives in religious seclusion
  • synonym: hermit - someone living in solitude, often for religious reasons
