beech

noun
wood of a beech tree
Pronunciation: / biːtʃ / (AmE), / biːtʃ / (BrE)
the wood from any of several beech trees, used especially for flooring, containers, plywood, and tool handles

Examples

Beech wood furniture
Many tool handles are crafted from beech because of its durability and smooth texture.
The flooring in the dining room is made of polished beech that shines in the sunlight.
They chose to use beech for the kitchen cabinets due to its attractive pale color.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maple - wood of a maple tree, also used in furniture
  • synonym: oak - wood of an oak tree, another hardwood
