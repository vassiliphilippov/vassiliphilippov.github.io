Real Estate Investment Trust

noun
company investing in income-producing real estate
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈriəl ɛˌsteɪt ɪnˈvɛstmənt trʌst / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ɪsˌteɪt ɪnˈvɛstmənt trʌst / (BrE)
