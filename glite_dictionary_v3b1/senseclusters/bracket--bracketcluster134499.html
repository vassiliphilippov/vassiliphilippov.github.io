bracket

verb
to support or fasten with brackets
Pronunciation: / ˈbrækət / (AmE), / ˈbrækɪt / (BrE)
to support or fasten (something) with a bracket or brackets, typically against a wall

Examples

To bracket a shelf
The television was securely bracketed above the fireplace.
To install the curtain rod, you need to bracket it properly to the window frame.
We decided to bracket the shelves to the wall for extra stability.
