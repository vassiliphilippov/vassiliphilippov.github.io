wincingly

adverb
with a wince
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪnsɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈwɪnsɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows a reaction of pain, discomfort, or embarrassment

Examples

She listened wincingly
She listened wincingly as her friend described the embarrassing moment in detail.
He accepted the criticism wincingly, clearly uncomfortable with the feedback.
The child looked wincingly at the nurse before getting the injection.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boldly - in a way that shows courage or confidence
  • synonym: painfully - in a way that causes or shows pain
