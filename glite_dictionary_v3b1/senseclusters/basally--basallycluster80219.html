basally

adverb
at baseline level
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsəli / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsəli / (BrE)
at a baseline or resting level, especially referring to physiological activity when not stimulated

Examples

Basally active hormones
The hormone is basally present in the bloodstream even when the body is at rest.
Researchers measured how much the enzyme is basally produced before any treatment is applied.
Some genes are basally active, providing a constant low level of expression.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resting - not active or stimulated; at rest
  • antonym: stimulated - increased above the normal or resting level
