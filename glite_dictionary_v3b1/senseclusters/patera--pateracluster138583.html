patera

noun
decorative disk or rosette
Pronunciation: / pəˈtɛrə / (AmE), / pəˈtɪərə / (BrE)
a round, flat decorative disk or rosette used as an ornament in architecture, furniture, or metalwork

Examples

Decorative patera on a building
The ancient temple's entrance was adorned with a beautifully carved patera above the doorway.
A bronze patera decorated the center of the ornate table in the palace hall.
Restorers carefully cleaned the marble patera to reveal its original intricate design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medallion - a large, round decorative piece used as an ornament
  • synonym: rosette - a decorative design shaped like a rose, often used as an ornament
