wakeless

adjective
deep, uninterrupted sleep
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪk.ləs / (AmE), / ˈweɪk.lɪs / (BrE)
(of sleep) deep and without interruption; so sound that one does not awaken easily.

A wakeless sleep
