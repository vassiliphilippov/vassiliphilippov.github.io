record album

noun
collection of audio recordings issued together
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkərd ˈæl.bəm / (AmE), / ˈrɛk.əd ˈæl.bəm / (BrE)
a collection of audio recordings issued together, especially as a 12-inch vinyl record, cassette, or compact disc, typically with a distinct cover

Examples
