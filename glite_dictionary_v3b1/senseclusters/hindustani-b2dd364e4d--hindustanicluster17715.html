Hindustani

noun
person from Hindustan or India
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪnduˈstɑni / (AmE), / ˌhɪndʊˈstɑːni / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Hindustan (historically, northern India) or India

Examples

A Hindustani migrant
Many Hindustanis migrated to other parts of the British Empire during the colonial period.
She is proud of her heritage as a Hindustani and celebrates traditional festivals.
The Hindustani community in the region maintains its rich cultural customs.
