ratably

adverb
proportionally distributed
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪtəbli / (AmE), / ˈreɪtəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is proportional or according to a calculated rate or share

Examples

Divided ratably among
The insurance payout was divided ratably among all the policyholders.
Profits from the joint venture were shared ratably based on each partner's investment.
The costs will be allocated ratably to each department according to their usage.
The bonus was divided ratably among all employees based on their hours worked.
Profits will be shared ratably according to each partner's investment in the business.
The insurance payout was distributed ratably to the policyholders after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: equally - in an equal manner or amount
  • synonym: proportionally - in a way that corresponds in size or amount to something else
  • antonym: unequally - in a way that is not the same for all parties
