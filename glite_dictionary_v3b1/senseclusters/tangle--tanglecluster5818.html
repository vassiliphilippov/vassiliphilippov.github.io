tangle

noun
a twisted mass of things
Pronunciation: / ˈtæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈtæŋɡl / (BrE)
a confused mass of things twisted and interwoven together

Examples

A tangle of wires
He worked patiently to sort out the tangle of wires behind the desk.
Her hair was such a tangle after the windy day at the beach.
The hikers struggled to move through the tangle of undergrowth blocking the trail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knot - an interlacement of parts creating a lump or mass; a fastener made by tying together pieces
  • antonym: order - a state in which everything is arranged properly
  • synonym: snarl - a tangle, especially of thread, hair, or similar items
