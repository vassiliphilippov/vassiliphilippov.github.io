lepidopterologist

noun
butterfly and moth specialist
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛpɪˌdɑptəˈrɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌlɛpɪˌdɒptəˈrɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies or collects butterflies and moths

Examples

A lepidopterologist studies butterflies
As a lepidopterologist, she carefully documented the lifecycle of local moths.

The lepidopterologist spent years observing rare butterfly species in the Amazon rainforest.
