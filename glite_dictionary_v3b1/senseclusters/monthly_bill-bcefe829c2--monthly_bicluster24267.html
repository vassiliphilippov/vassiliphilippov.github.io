monthly bill

noun
recurring statement of monthly charges
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌnθli bɪl / (AmE), / ˈmʌnθli bɪl / (BrE)
a statement of charges or payments that is issued every month, typically for services such as utilities, rent, or subscriptions

Examples

Pay the monthly bill
Don't forget to pay your monthly bill before the due date.
Each monthly bill includes an itemized list of all the services used.
My monthly bill for electricity was higher than usual this winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bill - a written statement of charges for goods or services
