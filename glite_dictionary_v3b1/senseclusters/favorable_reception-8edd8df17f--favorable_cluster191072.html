favorable reception

noun
being accepted or welcomed positively
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪvərəbəl rɪˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərəbl rɪˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)

Her novel met with a favourable reception from the critics.
