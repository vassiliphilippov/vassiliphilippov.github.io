nymphic

adjective
of a nymph
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪmfɪk / (AmE), / ˈnɪmfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a nymph, especially in biology or mythology

Examples

nymphic stage
The dragonfly was in its nymphic form before emerging as an adult.
In mythology, certain goddesses are described as having a nymphic beauty.
Scientists observed the nymphic stage of the insect under a microscope.
