hippish

adjective
like a hippie
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪpɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɪpɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a hippie, especially in appearance, style, or attitudes associated with the 1960s counterculture.

Examples

Hippish clothes
The new coffee shop has a hippish atmosphere, with colorful decor and vintage posters on the walls.
She wore a hippish outfit, complete with bell-bottom jeans and a tie-dye shirt.
His hippish attitude made him stand out at the formal business meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bohemian - unconventional and artistic in lifestyle or appearance
  • antonym: conventional - following traditional or accepted standards
