puffy

adjective
blowing in short bursts (of wind)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌfi / (AmE), / ˈpʌfi / (BrE)
(of wind) blowing in short, intermittent bursts or puffs.

Examples

Puffy wind
The clouds drifted quickly across the sky, driven by the puffy spring breezes.
The sailors struggled to steer the boat against the puffy offshore winds.
We decided to postpone the flight due to puffy conditions on the runway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gusty - (of wind) blowing in sudden, brief bursts
