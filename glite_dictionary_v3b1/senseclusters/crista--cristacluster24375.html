crista

noun
anatomical ridge
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɪ.stə/ (AmE), /ˈkrɪ.stə/ (BrE)
a crest or raised ridge on a bone or other anatomical structure, often serving as an attachment point or landmark

Examples

Bone crista
