belt ammunition

noun
ammunition in linked strips for machine guns
Pronunciation: / bɛlt ˌæməˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / bɛlt ˌaməˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
Ammunition (typically of small caliber) arranged in flexible linked strips for use in a machine gun.
