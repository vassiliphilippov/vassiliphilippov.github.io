swaybacked fence

noun
fence sagging in the middle
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsweɪˌbækt ˈfɛns / (AmE), / ˈsweɪˌbækt ˈfɛns / (BrE)
A fence that has sagged or bowed downward in the middle.

Examples

A swaybacked fence
A swaybacked fence surrounded the abandoned farmhouse, adding to its weathered charm.
The old swaybacked fence leaned dangerously after the winter storms.
They decided to replace the swaybacked fence before it collapsed completely.
