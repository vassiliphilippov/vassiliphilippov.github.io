artificial additive

noun
synthetic substance added to food
Pronunciation: / ˌɑɹ.təˈfɪʃ.əl ˈæd.ə.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌɑː.tɪˈfɪʃ.əl ˈæd.ɪ.tɪv / (BrE)
A substance that is added to food to enhance its flavor, appearance, or shelf-life, and is not naturally found in the food.

Examples

Artificial additives in food
Many processed foods contain artificial additives to improve their color and shelf life.
She prefers to buy products that are free from artificial additives.
Some people may be sensitive to certain artificial additives found in soft drinks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: natural additive - an additive derived from natural sources used in food
  • synonym: preservative - a substance used to preserve food or other perishable items
