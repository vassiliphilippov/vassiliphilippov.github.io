Piedmont glacier

noun
type of glacier formed by merging valley glaciers
9.6 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpiːd.mɑːnt ˈgleɪ.ʃər/ /; / /ˈpiːd.mənt ˈgleɪ.ʃər/ (AmE), /ˈpiːd.mɒnt ˈgleɪ.sə/ /; / /ˈpiːd.mɒnt ˈgleɪ.sjə/ (BrE)
