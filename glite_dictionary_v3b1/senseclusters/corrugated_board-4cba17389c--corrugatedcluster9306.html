corrugated board

noun
cardboard with a fluted inner layer
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔr.əˌɡeɪ.tɪd bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkɒr.ʊ.ɡeɪ.tɪd bɔːd / (BrE)
strong cardboard consisting of a fluted corrugated sheet and one or two flat linerboards, commonly used in packaging and boxes
