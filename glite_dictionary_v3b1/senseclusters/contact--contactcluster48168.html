contact

noun
person who can provide help or access
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.tækt /; / ˈkɑːnˌtækt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tækt / (BrE)
a person or organization that you know and can communicate with, especially for help or information

Examples

A business contact
He asked his contact at the embassy to help with the visa issue.
Many journalists depend on a network of contacts for inside information.
She became a valuable contact for us in the local government.
If you need to reach the supplier, I can give you my contact there.
She acted as the main contact between the research team and the hospital.
The embassy provided a local contact to assist us during our stay.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connection - a person through whom one can make useful contacts
  • synonym: liaison - person who maintains communication between groups or organizations
