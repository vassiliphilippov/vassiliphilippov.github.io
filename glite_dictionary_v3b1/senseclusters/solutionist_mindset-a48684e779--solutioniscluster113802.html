solutionist mindset

noun
focus on finding practical solutions
6.0 7.7
Pronunciation: / səˈluːʃənɪst ˈmaɪndˌsɛt / (AmE), / səˈluːʃənɪst ˈmaɪndˌsɛt / (BrE)
an attitude or approach characterized by a strong focus on finding practical solutions to problems, often emphasizing action over analysis

Examples

A solutionist mindset approach
A solutionist mindset can drive innovation but may sometimes overlook deeper systemic challenges.
Companies often value employees with a solutionist mindset who prefer action over endless debate.
Her solutionist mindset allowed her to quickly address unexpected issues during the project.
