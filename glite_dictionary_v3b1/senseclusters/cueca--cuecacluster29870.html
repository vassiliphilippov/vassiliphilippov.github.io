cueca

noun
Chilean folk dance
9.9
Pronunciation: / kuˈeɪkə / (AmE), / kuˈeɪkə / (BrE)
a traditional folk dance and music style from Chile and some other South American countries, often performed at festivals

Examples

The cueca dance

She learned to dance the cueca as a child growing up in Chile.
