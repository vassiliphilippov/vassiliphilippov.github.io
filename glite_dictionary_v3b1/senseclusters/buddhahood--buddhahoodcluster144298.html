buddhahood

noun
state of enlightenment
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊdəˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈbʊdəˌhʊd / (BrE)
the state of having attained full or perfect enlightenment in Buddhism, with the qualities of a Buddha such as wisdom and compassion
