endowed

adjective
supplied with a quality or asset
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdaʊd / (AmE), / ɪnˈdaʊd / (BrE)
having been provided or supplied with a particular quality, ability, or asset, especially by nature or inheritance.

Examples

Naturally endowed abilities
She is naturally endowed with a remarkable memory and quick wit.
The charity was endowed by a generous benefactor many years ago.
The university is endowed with one of the finest libraries in the country.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deprived - lacking a specified benefit or quality
  • synonym: gifted - having exceptional talent or natural ability
