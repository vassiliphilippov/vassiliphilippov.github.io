screensaver

noun
computer idle display
3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈskriːn.seɪ.vər/ (AmE), /ˈskriːn.seɪ.və/ (BrE)
a computer program that displays images or patterns on the screen when the computer is not being used

Examples

A screensaver program

The screensaver activated after ten minutes of inactivity on his laptop.
You can customize your screensaver to show different images or animations.
