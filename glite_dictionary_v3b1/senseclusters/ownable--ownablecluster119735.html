ownable

adjective
able to be owned
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊnəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈəʊnəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be legally possessed or owned by someone

Examples

Ownable land
Only ownable property can be sold or transferred under the law.
Some natural resources are not considered ownable by individuals or companies.
The court decided that the artwork was ownable and could be included in the estate.

  • antonym: unownable - not able to be legally possessed or owned
