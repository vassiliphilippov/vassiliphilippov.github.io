Synonyms

  • influence
    2.2 74/m
    influence slowly changes someone's ideas or behavior over time, while affect is a neutral verb for causing change.
  • act upon
    3.5 1.95/m
    act upon is used in formal writing to show an effect or response, while affect is the common everyday verb meaning to change something.
  • impact
    3.4 27/m
    impact often means to hit or have a strong, clear effect, while affect is a softer, more general verb for causing change.
