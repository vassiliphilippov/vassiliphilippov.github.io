nonwhite

adjective
not classified as white
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈwaɪt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈwaɪt / (BrE)
describing a person or group who is not classified as white, often considered insensitive or outdated

Examples

Nonwhite communities
The study focused on the experiences of nonwhite students in higher education.
Many nonwhite communities have advocated for more inclusive policies.
The term nonwhite is now often avoided in favor of more specific or respectful language.

Related Terms

  • synonym: person of color - a person who is not white, considered a more respectful term
  • antonym: white - classified as belonging to the white racial group
