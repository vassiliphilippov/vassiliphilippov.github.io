undoing

noun
cause of failure
4.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈduːɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈduːɪŋ / (BrE)
an event or action that causes someone's failure, ruin, or destruction

Examples

His undoing in life
His arrogance was ultimately his undoing in the competition.
The company's risky investments led to its undoing during the financial crisis.
Ignoring the warning signs proved to be her undoing in the end.
