aerial

noun
forward pass in football
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɛriəl / (AmE), / ˈeəriəl / (BrE)
In sports, especially American football, a forward pass thrown high through the air to a receiver down the field.

Examples

Aerial pass
His ability to catch long aerials makes him a threat to any defense.
The quarterback threw a perfect aerial that sailed forty yards down the field.
The team's winning touchdown came from a dramatic aerial in the final seconds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pass - an act of throwing the ball to a teammate in sports
