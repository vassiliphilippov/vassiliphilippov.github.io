airsick

adjective
feeling sick from flying in an aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌsɪk / (AmE), / ˈeəˌsɪk / (BrE)
feeling unwell or nauseous due to the movement of an aircraft during flight

Examples

To feel airsick
If you are prone to getting airsick, try sitting near the wings of the plane.
Passengers who become airsick are advised to keep their heads still and look at the horizon.
She felt airsick soon after the turbulence began.
