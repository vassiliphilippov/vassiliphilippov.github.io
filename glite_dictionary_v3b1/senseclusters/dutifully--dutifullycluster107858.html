dutifully

adverb
in a dutiful manner
4.7 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈduː.tə.fəl.iː/ /; / /ˈduː.tɪ.fəl.iː/ (AmE), /ˈdjuː.tɪ.fəl.iː/ /; / /ˈdjuː.tə.fəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a way that shows you are doing what you are expected or required to do by duty

Examples

To dutifully complete tasks
He dutifully completed all the paperwork his manager assigned to him.
She dutifully thanked everyone who helped organize the event.
The students dutifully stood when the principal entered the classroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obediently - in a way that shows willingness to comply with orders or requests
  • antonym: reluctantly - in a way that shows unwillingness or hesitation
