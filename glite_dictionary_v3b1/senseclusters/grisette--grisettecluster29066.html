grisette

noun
young French woman; gray mushroom
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪˈzɛt / (AmE), / ɡrɪˈzɛt / (BrE)
a young working-class woman, especially in 19th-century France, or a small gray mushroom

Examples

A Parisian grisette
The artist fell in love with a charming grisette he met in a Parisian café.
In the forest, we found a delicate grisette growing among the fallen leaves.
Many novels of the era feature a grisette as a symbol of youthful independence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: working girl - a young woman who works, especially in a low-paid job
