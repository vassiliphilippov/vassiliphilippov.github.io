Osmanli

noun
a Turk of the Ottoman dynasty or tribe
Pronunciation: / ɑzˈmɑnli / (AmE), / ɒzˈmɑːnli / (BrE)
A member of the Turkish tribe founded by Osman I, especially in the context of the Ottoman Empire; a Turk of Ottoman descent.

Examples

The Osmanli Empire
Historians often study the rise of the Osmanli in Anatolia.
The Osmanli established a powerful empire that lasted for centuries.
The traditions of the Osmanli continue to influence Turkish culture today.
