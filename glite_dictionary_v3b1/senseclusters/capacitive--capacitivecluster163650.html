capacitive

adjective
relating to capacitance
9.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈpæs.ɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / kəˈpæs.ɪ.tɪv / (BrE)
relating to or involving capacitance; able to store electrical energy in an electric field.

Examples

Capacitive coupling
A capacitive coupling exists between the two conductors in this circuit.
Engineers measured the capacitive properties of the new sensor to ensure accuracy.
The capacitive touch screen responds to the electrical properties of your skin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inductive - relating to electrical inductance
  • antonym: resistive - relating to resistance in an electrical circuit
