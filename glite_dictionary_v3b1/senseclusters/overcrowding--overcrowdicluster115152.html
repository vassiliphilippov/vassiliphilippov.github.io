overcrowding

noun
too many in one place
2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vər.kraʊ.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.kraʊ.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
a situation in which too many people or things are in a space, causing discomfort or problems

Examples

Urban overcrowding
The city is working to reduce overcrowding in public schools by building new classrooms.
During the festival, overcrowding made it difficult to move through the streets.
Many animals suffer from overcrowding in small cages at poorly managed shelters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: congestion - the state of being overcrowded, especially with traffic or people
  • synonym: crowding - the condition of being crowded or packed together
  • antonym: spaciousness - the quality of having plenty of space
