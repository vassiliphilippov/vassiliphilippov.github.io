sea-lavender family

noun
family of salt-tolerant flowering plants
Pronunciation: / siː ˈlævəndɚ ˈfæməli / (AmE), / siː ˈlævəndə ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of perennial herbs, shrubs, and lianas, found worldwide especially in saltwater areas; also known as Plumbaginaceae.
