furring

noun
layer of fur
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜːrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɜːrɪŋ / (BrE)
a layer of soft hair or fur covering a surface, or a lining that looks like fur

Examples

Soft furring
The kitten's furring was especially soft to the touch.
A thin furring lined the inside of the winter boots for extra warmth.
She brushed the furring on her coat to keep it looking neat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bare - without any covering or layer
  • synonym: coating - a layer that covers something
