launch pad

noun
platform for launching rockets or spacecraft
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔntʃ ˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈlɔːntʃ ˌpad / (BrE)
A platform from which rockets or spacecraft are launched into space.

Examples

Rocket launch pad
Engineers checked every system before moving the spacecraft to the launch pad.
Spectators gathered miles away to watch the liftoff from the launch pad.
The rocket stood on the launch pad ready for its historic flight.
