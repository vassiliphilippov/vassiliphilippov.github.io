uncoerced

adjective
not forced or pressured
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkoʊˈɜrst / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəʊˈɜːst / (BrE)
not brought about by coercion or force; done voluntarily and without pressure

Examples

An uncoerced choice
Her agreement to help was entirely uncoerced and genuine.
The court needed proof that his confession was uncoerced before accepting it as evidence.
The witness provided an uncoerced statement to the police.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coerced - brought about by force or threat
  • synonym: voluntary - done of one's own free will
