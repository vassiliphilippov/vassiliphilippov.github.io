blast

verb
create/destroy with explosives
2.2
Pronunciation: / blæst / (AmE), / blɑːst / (BrE)
to create an opening, destroy, or break apart something by using explosives or great force

  • synonym: explode - to burst violently as a result of rapid combustion or excessive internal pressure
