motility

noun
ability to move spontaneously
5.3 6.9
Pronunciation: /moʊ.ˈtɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /mə.ˈtɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /məʊ.ˈtɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /mə.ˈtɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
the ability of a living thing or cell to move by itself
