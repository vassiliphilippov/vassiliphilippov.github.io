(in computing, robotics, and AI) actions or outputs of an automated or autonomous system that deviate from its intended behaviour, causing errors, unsafe outcomes, or other undesired effects.

Examples

Robot misbehavior
During the demo, the drone's misbehavior forced the team to switch to manual control.
Engineers traced the robot's unexpected misbehavior to a sensor calibration error and issued an update.
If the self-driving taxi shows any misbehavior, passengers should contact the operator immediately for safety.
The laboratory team investigated the robot's repeated misbehaviour and updated its safety protocols.
After the update, several self-driving cars showed unexpected misbehaviour in rain, causing the company to pause testing.
Engineers traced the satellite's brief misbehaviour to a software bug in its orientation control module.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bug - a fault or defect in software or hardware that causes incorrect or unexpected behaviour
  • synonym: malfunction - a failure to function normally or as intended
  • antonym: normal operation - the system functioning as intended, without errors or unexpected behaviour
