lawyer

verb
to provide legal help
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔjər /; / ˈlɔjərd / (AmE), / ˈlɔːjə /; / ˈlɔːjəd / (BrE)
to act as a lawyer for someone, such as giving legal advice, representing them, or preparing legal documents
