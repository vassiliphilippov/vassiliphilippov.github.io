unpeaceable

adjective
not inclined to peace; contentious
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpiːsəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpiːsəbəl / (BrE)
not inclined or disposed to be peaceful; tending towards conflict or unrest.

Examples

An unpeaceable nature
Her unpeaceable nature often caused tension among her friends.
In history, unpeaceable rulers tended to provoke frequent wars.
The council struggled to mediate between the unpeaceable factions in the community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: peaceable - inclined to peace; not inclined to argue or fight
