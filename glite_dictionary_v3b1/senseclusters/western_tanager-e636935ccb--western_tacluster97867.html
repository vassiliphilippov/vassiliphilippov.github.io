western tanager

noun
North American songbird with bright plumage
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstərn təˈnædʒər / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən təˈnædʒə / (BrE)
A brightly colored songbird of western North America, the male characterized by black, yellow, and orange-red plumage.

Examples

The western tanager bird
Every spring, birdwatchers eagerly await the return of the western tanager to their region.
The bright orange-red head of the male western tanager is strikingly beautiful.
We spotted a western tanager among the pine trees during our hike.
