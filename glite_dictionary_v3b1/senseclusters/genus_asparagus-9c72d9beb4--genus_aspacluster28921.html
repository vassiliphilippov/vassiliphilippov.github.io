genus Asparagus

noun
a plant genus including asparagus
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs əˈspærəɡəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs əˈspærəɡəs / (BrE)
A large group of Old World perennial herbs, including asparagus, with erect, spreading, or climbing stems, small scalelike leaves, and inconspicuous flowers; sometimes classified in the family Asparagaceae.
