Dutch uncle

noun
one who gives frank, stern advice
Pronunciation: / dʌtʃ ˈʌŋkəl / (AmE), / dʌtʃ ˈʌŋkl̩ / (BrE)
a person who gives blunt, stern, and often frank advice or criticism, especially in a well-meaning way
