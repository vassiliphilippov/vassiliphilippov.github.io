Dutch uncle

noun
one who gives frank, stern advice
9.3
Pronunciation: / dʌtʃ ˈʌŋkəl / (AmE), / dʌtʃ ˈʌŋkl̩ / (BrE)
a person who gives blunt, stern, and often frank advice or criticism, especially in a well-meaning way

Examples

A Dutch uncle advice
After failing my exams, my father acted like a Dutch uncle and told me exactly what I needed to do differently.
He spoke to me as a Dutch uncle, offering stern, honest criticism rather than empty encouragement.
She valued her manager’s Dutch uncle approach, even if the advice was sometimes hard to hear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: critic - a person who expresses an unfavorable opinion of something
  • synonym: mentor - an experienced and trusted adviser
