multimodality

noun
multiple treatment methods
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˌmoʊˈdæləti / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪməʊˈdælɪti / (BrE)
the use of two or more different methods or types of treatment to address a medical condition

Examples

Multimodality treatment plan
The hospital adopted a multimodality approach, combining surgery, chemotherapy, and radiation for cancer patients.
Effective pain management often relies on multimodality, such as medication, physical therapy, and counseling.
Recent studies show that multimodality can improve recovery rates in patients with chronic illnesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: combination therapy - the use of more than one type of treatment for a disease
  • antonym: monotherapy - treatment using only a single method or drug
