mail carrier

noun
person who delivers mail
2.7
Pronunciation: / meɪl ˈkæɹiɚ / (AmE), / meɪl ˈkæɹiə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to deliver letters and parcels to people's homes and businesses.

Examples

The mail carrier delivers letters
As a mail carrier, she walks several miles each day in all kinds of weather.
Our friendly mail carrier always greets us with a smile when delivering our letters.
The mail carrier comes to our street every morning around eleven.

Related Terms

  • synonym: letter carrier - another term for mail carrier, especially in North American English
  • synonym: postman - a person, especially a man, who delivers letters and parcels as a job (Chiefly British)
