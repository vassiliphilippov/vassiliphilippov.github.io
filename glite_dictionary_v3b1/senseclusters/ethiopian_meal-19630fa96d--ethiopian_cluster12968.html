Ethiopian meal

noun
meal typical of Ethiopian cuisine
5.9 3.3
Pronunciation: /iː.θɪ.ˈoʊ.pɪ.ən miːl/ /; / /ɪ.θɪ.ˈoʊ.pɪ.ən miːl/ (AmE), /iː.θɪ.ˈəʊ.pɪ.ən miːl/ /; / /ɪ.θɪ.ˈəʊ.pɪ.ən miːl/ (BrE)
