rillettes

noun
meat spread
Pronunciation: / riˈjɛts / (AmE), / riˈjɛts / (BrE)
a spread made from slow-cooked, shredded meat, usually pork, mixed with fat and served cold

Examples

Homemade rillettes spread
They served homemade rillettes with pickles and fresh bread at the picnic.
The chef recommended pairing the duck rillettes with a crisp white wine.
We spread the pork rillettes generously on slices of toasted baguette.

  • synonym: terrine - a dish of meat or vegetables cooked in a covered dish and served cold
