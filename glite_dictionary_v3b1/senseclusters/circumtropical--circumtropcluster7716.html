circumtropical

adjective
across tropical regions
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜrkəmˈtrɑpɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsɜːkəmˈtrɒpɪkəl / (BrE)
found or distributed throughout all tropical regions of the world

Examples

Circumtropical fish species
The fish species is circumtropical, occurring in warm waters around the globe.
Many marine organisms have a circumtropical distribution due to similar environmental conditions.
Researchers studied the circumtropical spread of the disease among tropical countries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: endemic - native to and restricted to a certain place
  • synonym: pantropical - found throughout the tropical regions of the world
