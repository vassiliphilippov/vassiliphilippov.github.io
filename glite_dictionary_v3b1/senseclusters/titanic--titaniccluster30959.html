titanic

adjective
enormous in strength, size, or power
6.3
Pronunciation: / taɪˈtænɪk / (AmE), / tɪˈtænɪk / (BrE)
Extremely powerful or colossal; of immense strength, size, or force.

Examples

A titanic effort
After years of titanic effort, the scientist finally achieved a breakthrough.
The hurricane’s titanic winds battered the coastline for hours.
Their titanic clash changed the history of the sport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gigantic - very large in size or extent
  • antonym: tiny - very small in size
