bulldozer

noun
large machine for moving earth
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈbʊl.doʊ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈbʊl.dəʊ.zə/ (BrE)
a large, powerful tractor with a broad, heavy blade at the front, used for moving or flattening earth or debris

Examples

A bulldozer machine
A yellow bulldozer rumbled across the field, pushing heaps of dirt aside.
After the storm, a bulldozer helped remove fallen trees from the road.
The construction crew used a bulldozer to clear the land before building began.
