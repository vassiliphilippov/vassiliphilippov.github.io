Examples

Synonyms

  • narrate
    4.0 5.62/m
    narrate is a formal word often used in books or films, while tell a story is more everyday and casual.
  • tell
    1.0 387/m
    tell often means give information or say something aloud, while tell a story means to narrate events as a tale.
  • relate
    2.9 6.99/m
    relate is used to give a short account of events to someone, while tell a story often paints scenes and characters.
  • recount
    4.1 9.30/m
    recount means to describe past events in clear detail, while tell a story can include made-up or imagined events for pleasure.
