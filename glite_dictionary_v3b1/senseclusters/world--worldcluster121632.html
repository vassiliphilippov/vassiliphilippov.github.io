world

noun
someone's personal reality or experience
Pronunciation: / wɜːrld / (AmE), / wɜːld / (BrE)
a person's individual experience or way of perceiving things; the sum of someone's thoughts, feelings, and perspectives

Examples

His world changed
After losing his job, his entire world seemed to collapse overnight.
To a child, the world is filled with wonder and magic.
We may work together, but we come from very different worlds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
  • synonym: universe - all that exists, especially as a personal experience
