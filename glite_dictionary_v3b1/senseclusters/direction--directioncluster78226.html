direction

noun
advice or guidance for action
1.0
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
advice or guidance about what action should be taken or how something should be done

Examples

Mentor's direction
After receiving direction from her mentor, she felt confident to start her career.
His lack of direction made the team unsure about what steps to take next.
We need clear direction from the management before proceeding with the project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confusion - lack of understanding; uncertainty
  • synonym: guidance - advice or information aimed at resolving a problem or difficulty
  • synonym: instruction - detailed information on how something should be done
