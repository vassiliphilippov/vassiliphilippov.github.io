unselfconscious

adjective
not self-conscious; natural
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌsɛlfˈkɑːnʃəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌsɛlfˈkɒnʃəs / (BrE)
not preoccupied with oneself or one's appearance; natural and unaffected by self-awareness or shyness.

Examples

Unselfconscious behaviour
Children often dance in an unselfconscious way, unburdened by embarrassment.
His unselfconscious manner made conversations feel relaxed and genuine.
She gave an unselfconscious laugh that put everyone at ease.

Related Terms

  • antonym: self-conscious - aware and often uncomfortable about oneself or one's actions
