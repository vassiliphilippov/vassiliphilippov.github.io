thermotherapy

noun
use of heat for medical treatment
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌθɝːmoʊˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
the use of heat to treat disease or injury, often to improve circulation or relieve pain

Examples

Thermotherapy treatment
Doctors may recommend thermotherapy to alleviate chronic muscle pain.
Patients undergoing thermotherapy often use heating pads as part of their treatment routine.
Physical therapists incorporate thermotherapy to help reduce muscle stiffness and improve mobility.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cryotherapy - the use of cold to treat disease or injury
