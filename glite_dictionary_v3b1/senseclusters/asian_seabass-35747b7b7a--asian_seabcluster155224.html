Asian seabass

noun
a large edible Indo-Pacific fish species
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪʒən ˈsiːˌbæs / (AmE), / ˈeɪʃən ˈsiːˌbæs / (BrE)
a large species of perch (Lates calcarifer), valued as a game and food fish, found in marine, estuarine, and freshwater habitats of the Indo-Pacific region
