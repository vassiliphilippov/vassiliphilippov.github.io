keep at arm's length

verb
maintain a safe emotional or social distance
6.0
Pronunciation: / kip æt ɑrmz lɛŋkθ / (AmE), / kiːp ət ɑːmz lɛŋθ / (BrE)
to deliberately avoid becoming too involved with someone or something; to maintain a safe or cautious distance

Examples

To keep at arm's length a friend
Despite their friendship, she was careful to keep him at arm's length emotionally.
He always kept at arm's length anyone he didn't fully trust.
She tried to keep at arm's length from office politics to avoid unnecessary drama.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distance oneself - to make a conscious effort to avoid involvement
