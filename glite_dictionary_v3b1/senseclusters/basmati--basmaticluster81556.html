basmati

noun
fragrant long-grain rice
2.8
Pronunciation: / bɑːzˈmɑːti / (AmE), / bɑːzˈmɑːti / (BrE)
a variety of long-grain rice from South Asia, known for its distinctive fragrance and delicate flavor

Examples

A bowl of basmati
She prefers to use basmati when making traditional Indian dishes.
The aroma of cooked basmati filled the entire kitchen.
He bought a large bag of basmati rice for the family dinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brown rice - whole-grain rice with the bran layer intact
  • synonym: jasmine rice - a fragrant long-grain rice from Thailand
