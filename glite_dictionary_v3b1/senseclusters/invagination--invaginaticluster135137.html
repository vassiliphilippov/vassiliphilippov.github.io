invagination

noun
folding in to form a pocket

Pronunciation: /ɪn.və.dʒɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪn.væ.dʒɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪn.væ.dʒɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪn.və.dʒɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
