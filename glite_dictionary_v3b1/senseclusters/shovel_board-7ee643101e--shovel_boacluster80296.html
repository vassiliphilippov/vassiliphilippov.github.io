shovel board

noun
game sliding coins/discs on a board
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌvəl bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈʃʌvəl bɔːd / (BrE)
a game in which coins or discs are slid by hand across a long, smooth board toward a mark, aiming for accuracy or points

Examples

A shovel board game
During the festival, a shovel board competition was held for all ages.
He quickly mastered the rules of shovel board and challenged his friends to a game.
The pub had an old shovel board set that attracted curious visitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shuffleboard - a similar game in which pucks are pushed with cues along a board
