mega

x
prefix for one million
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛɡə / (AmE), / ˈmiːɡə / (BrE)
a prefix meaning one million (10^6), used with units to indicate a factor of one million, as in megawatt or megabyte

Examples

Megawatt power
A megabyte is equal to one million bytes of digital information.
The new power plant can generate a megawatt of electricity for the city.
Scientists measured the earthquake's energy in megatons to compare its strength.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: giga - a prefix meaning one billion (10^9)
  • degree lower: kilo - a prefix meaning one thousand (10^3)
