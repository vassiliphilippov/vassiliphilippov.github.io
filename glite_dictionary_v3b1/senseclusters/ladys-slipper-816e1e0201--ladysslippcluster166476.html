lady's-slipper

noun
a wildflower with a pouchlike lip
9.0 7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈleɪ.diːz.slɪp.ər/ (AmE), /ˈleɪ.diːz.slɪp.ə/ (BrE)
A wildflower, especially of the genus Cypripedium, with a distinctive inflated pouchlike lip, native chiefly to North America.
