nonpharmaceutical

adjective
not a medicine
6.5
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.fɑːr.mə.ˈsuː.tɪ.kəl/ /; / /nɑːn.fɑːr.mə.ˈsjuː.tɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /nɒn.fɑː.mə.ˈsjuː.tɪ.kəl/ /; / /nɒn.fɑː.mə.ˈsuː.tɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
not involving drugs or medications; not classified or regulated as a pharmaceutical product (especially in treatment or prevention)

Examples
