nonpharmaceutical

adjective
not a medicine
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌfɑrməˌsuːtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌfɑːməˈsjuːtɪkəl / (BrE)
not involving drugs or medications; not classified or regulated as a pharmaceutical product (especially in treatment or prevention)

Examples

Nonpharmaceutical treatment options
The clinic offers several nonpharmaceutical treatments for chronic pain, such as physical therapy and acupuncture.
Many people prefer nonpharmaceutical approaches to managing stress, like meditation and exercise.
Some supplements are advertised as nonpharmaceutical alternatives to prescription drugs.
Cognitive behavioral therapy is a nonpharmaceutical method for treating anxiety disorders.
Doctors often recommend nonpharmaceutical interventions like diet and exercise for managing high blood pressure.
Many patients prefer nonpharmaceutical options to avoid potential side effects of medication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drug-free - not using drugs or medications
  • antonym: medicinal - having healing properties; used as medicine
  • antonym: pharmaceutical - relating to drugs or medicines
