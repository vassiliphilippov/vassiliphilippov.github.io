brownish-black

adjective
black tinged with brown
3.1 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbraʊ.nɪʃ blæk/ (AmE), /ˈbraʊ.nɪʃ blæk/ (BrE)
Of a black color tinged with brown; having a brownish tone to black.

Examples

The soil in that area is a deep brownish-black from decaying leaves.
