mint julep

noun
bourbon-based cocktail with mint
Pronunciation: / mɪnt ˈdʒuːləp / (AmE), / mɪnt ˈdʒuːlɪp / (BrE)
a cocktail made of bourbon, sugar, mint, and crushed ice, traditionally served in the southern United States

Examples

A mint julep cocktail
During the Kentucky Derby, many guests enjoy a refreshing mint julep.
He carefully muddled the mint leaves before preparing the perfect mint julep.
She sipped her mint julep while sitting on the porch in the warm evening air.
