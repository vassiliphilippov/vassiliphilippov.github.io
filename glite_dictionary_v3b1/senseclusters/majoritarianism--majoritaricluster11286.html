majoritarianism

noun
rule by majority
9.1
Pronunciation: / məˌdʒɔːrɪˈtɛriənɪzəm / (AmE), / məˌdʒɒrɪˈteəriənɪzəm / (BrE)
the political principle or system in which the majority's will or decisions have authority, sometimes ignoring the rights of minorities
