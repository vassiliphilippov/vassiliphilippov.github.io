Synonyms

  • ambush
    5.2 3.67/m

  • ambuscade
    9.9 0.00/m
    ambuscade means to wait and attack from hiding, while bushwhack is a more informal word for a sudden outdoor ambush.
  • lie in wait
    6.8 0.32/m
    lie in wait means to hide and wait to make a surprise attack, while bushwhack suggests a sudden strike in rough outdoors.
  • waylay
    8.6 0.13/m
