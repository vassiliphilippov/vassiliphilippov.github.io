obliviousness

noun
state of being unaware or forgetting
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈblɪviəsnəs / (AmE), / əˈblɪviəsnɪs / (BrE)
a state of being completely unaware of or forgetting something; total forgetfulness

Examples

Complete obliviousness
He wandered through the crowded street in utter obliviousness of his surroundings.
Her obliviousness to the ongoing argument was almost admirable.
The artist's obliviousness to criticism allowed her to develop her unique style.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awareness - knowledge or perception of a situation or fact
  • synonym: forgetfulness - the quality of being likely to forget things
