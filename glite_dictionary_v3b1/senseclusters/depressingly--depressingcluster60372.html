depressingly

adverb
in a depressing or discouraging way
2.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈpre.sɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈpre.sɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that causes or expresses sadness, discouragement, or gloom.

Examples

Depressingly cold weather
It was depressingly cold and gray outside all weekend.
The book is depressingly accurate about the realities of poverty.
The news was depressingly familiar, offering no hope for improvement.
