bringing close together

noun
act of moving two things nearer, esp. tissues
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪŋɪŋ kloʊs təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / ˈbrɪŋɪŋ kləʊs təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
the act of moving two parts, especially the cut edges of tissue, nearer or into contact with each other, typically in a medical or surgical context.
