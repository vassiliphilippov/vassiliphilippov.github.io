reader

noun
a person able to read
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdər / (AmE), / ˈriːdə / (BrE)
a person who can read, or who is literate

Examples

New reader
New programs are helping adults become readers for the first time.
She is a skilled reader who enjoys challenging novels.
The school aims to ensure that every child becomes a confident reader.
