belly up to the bar

verb
approach a bar to order drinks
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛli ʌp tu ðə bɑɹ / (AmE), / ˈbɛli ʌp tə ðə bɑː / (BrE)
to approach or move up to a bar counter, typically in order to order a drink

Examples

To belly up to the bar counter
After a long day, we like to belly up to the bar and share stories.
He walked into the pub and bellied up to the bar for a pint.
Many locals belly up to the bar as soon as happy hour begins.
