streetlight

noun
lamp for lighting a street
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːtˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈstriːtˌlaɪt / (BrE)
A lamp mounted on a pole, used to illuminate a public street.

Examples

A streetlight pole
A single streetlight flickered at the end of the deserted road.
Children played under the streetlight as dusk settled over the neighborhood.
The city installed a new streetlight on every corner to improve visibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lamppost - a post supporting a streetlight
