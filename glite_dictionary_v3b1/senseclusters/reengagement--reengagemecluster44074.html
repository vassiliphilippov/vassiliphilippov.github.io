reengagement

noun
renewed involvement
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the process of restoring or increasing someone's interest, commitment, or involvement after a period of inactivity or disengagement

Examples

Customer reengagement
The company launched a campaign to encourage reengagement among former customers.
After a long break, her reengagement with the project brought new energy to the team.
Schools are developing strategies for the reengagement of students who have become less active in class.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disengagement - the process of withdrawing from involvement or commitment
  • synonym: engagement - the act of being involved or committed to something
