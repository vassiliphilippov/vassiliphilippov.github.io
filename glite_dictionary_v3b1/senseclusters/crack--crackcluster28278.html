crack

noun
smokable form of cocaine
Pronunciation: / kræk / (AmE), / kræk / (BrE)
A purified and potent form of cocaine that is smoked rather than snorted, known for being highly addictive.

Examples

To smoke crack
He became addicted to crack after just a few weeks of use.
The police seized a large quantity of crack during the raid.
There has been a rise in crack-related crimes in the city.
