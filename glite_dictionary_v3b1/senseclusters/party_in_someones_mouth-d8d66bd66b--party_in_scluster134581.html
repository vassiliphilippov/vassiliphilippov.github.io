party in someone's mouth

noun
an extremely enjoyable taste experience
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrti ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz maʊθ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz maʊθ / (BrE)
An informal expression describing food or drink that provides an extremely enjoyable and exciting taste experience.

Examples

A party in my mouth
Every spoonful of the soup offered a party in my mouth with unexpected bursts of flavor.
The first bite of the spicy dish was a true party in my mouth.
This dessert is so flavorful—it's like having a party in your mouth!
