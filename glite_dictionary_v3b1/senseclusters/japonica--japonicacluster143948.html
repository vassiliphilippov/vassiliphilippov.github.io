japonica

noun
deciduous shrub with red blossoms, native to Japan
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈpɑːnɪkə /; / dʒəˈpɑnɪkə / (AmE), / dʒəˈpɒnɪkə / (BrE)
an ornamental shrub, often grown for its attractive flowers and appearance
