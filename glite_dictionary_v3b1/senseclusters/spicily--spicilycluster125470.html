spicily

adverb
in a spicy manner
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪsɪli / (AmE), / ˈspaɪsɪli / (BrE)
In a way that is strongly flavored with spices or has a spicy quality.

Examples

Cook spicily with peppers
She prefers her food prepared spicily, with plenty of peppers and chilies.
The chef cooked the chicken spicily, delighting those who enjoy bold flavors.
The curry was spicily seasoned, leaving a tingling sensation on my tongue.
