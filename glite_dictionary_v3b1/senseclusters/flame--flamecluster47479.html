flame

verb
criticize harshly online
5.4 6.2
Pronunciation: /fleɪm/ (AmE), /fleɪm/ (BrE)
to criticize or attack someone harshly on the internet or via electronic communication, often using angry or insulting language

Examples

To flame someone online

He was flamed in the comments section for expressing an unpopular view.
It's best not to flame others during online debates, even if you disagree strongly.
