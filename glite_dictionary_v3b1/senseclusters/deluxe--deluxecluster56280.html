deluxe

adjective
luxurious; of superior quality
1.6
Pronunciation: / dəˈlʌks / (AmE), / dɪˈlʌks / (BrE)
of a very high quality, luxurious, or elegant; better and more expensive than usual

Examples

A deluxe hotel
She treated herself to a deluxe spa treatment for her birthday.
The hotel offered a deluxe suite with a private balcony and ocean view.
Their deluxe edition of the album includes bonus tracks and special packaging.
The restaurant offers a deluxe tasting menu featuring rare and expensive ingredients.
This limited edition watch comes in a deluxe presentation box with gold trim.
We booked a deluxe room with a view of the ocean for our anniversary trip.

Related Terms

  • antonym: economy - intended to save money by being less expensive and less luxurious
  • synonym: luxurious - very comfortable and expensive; giving great pleasure
