suppertime

noun
time for the evening meal
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌpərˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈsʌpətaɪm / (BrE)
the usual or scheduled time in the evening when supper is eaten

Examples

At suppertime
She was still at work when suppertime arrived.
The smell of food cooking told him it was almost suppertime.
We always gather as a family at suppertime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dinnertime - the time at which dinner is usually eaten
