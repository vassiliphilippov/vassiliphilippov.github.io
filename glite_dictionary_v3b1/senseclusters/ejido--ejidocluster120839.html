ejido

noun
Mexican communal land group
9.9
Pronunciation: / eˈxiːdoʊ / (AmE), / eˈxiːdəʊ / (BrE)
a community or collective in Mexico that holds and manages communal land under the ejido system

Examples

Ejido community meeting
The ejido decided to plant corn on the communal fields this year.
Many families in the ejido work together to maintain the irrigation system.
After joining the ejido, she participated in meetings about land use and crop planning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commune - a group of people living together and sharing resources
  • antonym: private property - land or belongings owned by an individual or company
