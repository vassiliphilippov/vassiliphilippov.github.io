microvascular

adjective
of small blood vessels
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈvæskjələr / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈvæskjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to the smallest blood vessels, such as capillaries, in the body

Examples

Microvascular damage
Diabetes can cause microvascular damage, leading to problems with vision and kidney function.
Researchers are studying microvascular changes in patients with high blood pressure.
The doctor explained that microvascular complications are common in long-term diabetes.

Related Terms
