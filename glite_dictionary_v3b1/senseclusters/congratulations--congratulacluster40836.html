congratulations

noun
expression of praise or good wishes
Pronunciation: / kənˌɡræ.tʃəˈleɪ.ʃənz / (AmE), / kənˌɡræt.jəˈleɪ.ʃənz / (BrE)
A polite expression used to praise someone or offer good wishes for their success or happiness.

Examples

Congratulations on success
He received many congratulations after announcing his promotion.
I sent my congratulations when I heard she had won the award.
My sincere congratulations on the birth of your baby girl.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commendation - formal expression of praise
  • synonym: praise - expression of approval or admiration
