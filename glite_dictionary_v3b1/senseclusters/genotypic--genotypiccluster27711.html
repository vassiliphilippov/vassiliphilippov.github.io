genotypic

adjective
relating to a genotype
Pronunciation: / dʒiːnəˈtɪpɪk / (AmE), / dʒiːnəˈtɪpɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a genotype; of or pertaining to the genetic constitution of an organism.

Examples

Genotypic analysis
A genotypic analysis revealed patterns that were not evident from external observations.
Researchers focused on the genotypic makeup of the bacteria to understand the source of the outbreak.
The genotypic differences between the two plants explained their varying resistance to disease.
