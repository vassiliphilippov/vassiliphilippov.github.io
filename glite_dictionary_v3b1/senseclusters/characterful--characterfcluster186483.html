characterful

adjective
full of personality
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪktərfəl / (AmE), / ˈkærɪktəfʊl / (BrE)
having many interesting or unusual qualities that make something distinctive or full of personality

Examples

A characterful cafe
The characterful café was decorated with vintage posters and mismatched furniture.
She prefers characterful neighborhoods with old houses and lively markets.
His characterful writing style makes every story memorable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bland - lacking strong features or characteristics and therefore uninteresting
  • synonym: distinctive - having a quality that makes something different and easily noticed
  • synonym: unique - being the only one of its kind; unlike anything else
