meadow rue

noun
delicate perennial herb of the genus Thalictrum
6.6 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmed.oʊ ruː/ (AmE), /ˈmed.əʊ ruː/ (BrE)
any of several perennial herbs of the genus Thalictrum, with delicate, divided leaves and clusters of small purple or yellow flowers, commonly found in damp meadows and stream banks.

Examples

Meadow rue plant
Gardeners often plant meadow rue for its attractive foliage and delicate flowers.
The meadow rue in her garden produced an airy cloud of purple blossoms in the spring.
We found wild meadow rue growing along the shady bank of the stream.
