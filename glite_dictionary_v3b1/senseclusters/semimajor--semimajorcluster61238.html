semimajor

noun
half major axis
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈmeɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈmeɪdʒə / (BrE)
the semimajor axis of an ellipse, meaning half the length of its longest diameter

Examples

The semimajor of an ellipse
The planet's orbit has a semimajor of nearly 10 million kilometers.
To calculate the area of the ellipse, you need both the semimajor and the semiminor axes.
Astronomers often record the semimajor when describing the size of an orbit.

Related Terms

  • antonym: minor axis - the shortest diameter of an ellipse
  • synonym: semimajor axis - half the longest diameter of an ellipse
