booby trap

noun
hidden explosive device
4.4 7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːbi træp / (AmE), / ˈbuːbi træp / (BrE)
An explosive device or mine concealed to detonate when touched, stepped on, or otherwise triggered, often intended to cause injury or damage.

Examples

Hidden booby trap
Clearing the building took hours because every room contained a potential booby trap.
He lost his leg after stepping on a booby trap during the war.
The soldiers moved cautiously to avoid setting off a hidden booby trap in the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: land mine - an explosive device placed on or just under the ground, detonated by pressure or proximity
