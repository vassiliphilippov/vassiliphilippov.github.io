Nothofagus dombeyi

noun
Chilean evergreen tree used for thatching
Pronunciation: / noʊˌθoʊˈfeɪɡəs dɒmˈbeɪ.i / (AmE), / nəʊˌθɒˈfæɡəs dɒmˈbeɪ.i / (BrE)
A large evergreen tree native to Chile and Argentina, whose leafy boughs are often used for thatching; also known as Dombey's beech.
