breeding

adjective
used for producing offspring
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
used to describe animals kept or used for the purpose of producing offspring, or populations involved in reproduction.

Examples

Breeding animals

Selective breeding has been used to improve cattle quality for generations.
