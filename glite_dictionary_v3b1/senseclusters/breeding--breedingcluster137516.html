breeding

adjective
used for producing offspring
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
used to describe animals kept or used for the purpose of producing offspring, or populations involved in reproduction.

Examples

Breeding animals
Only the healthiest dogs are chosen for breeding purposes at the kennel.
Selective breeding has been used to improve cattle quality for generations.
The breeding population of birds increased significantly after habitat restoration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reproductive - relating to or used for reproduction
  • antonym: sterile - unable to produce offspring
