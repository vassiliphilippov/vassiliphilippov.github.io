mistily

adverb
in a misty or hazy manner
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstɪli / (AmE), / ˈmɪstɪli / (BrE)
In a manner characterized by mist or by being indistinct or hazy.

Examples

Mistily lit street
Moonlight shone mistily through the foggy window.
She remembered her childhood mistily, as if it were a distant dream.
The city lights appeared mistily across the damp fields at dawn.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearly - in a clear manner
  • synonym: misty - full of or resembling mist
