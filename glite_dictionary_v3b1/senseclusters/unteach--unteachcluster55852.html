unteach

verb
cause a model to forget learned information
9.5
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈtiːtʃ/ /; / /ən.ˈtiːtʃ/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈtiːtʃ/ /; / /ən.ˈtiːtʃ/ (BrE)
(in machine learning) to cause a trained model to forget or remove specific learned information, patterns, or biases, typically by retraining, modifying, or applying algorithmic procedures that erase the model's knowledge of that information.

Examples

To unteach a model
The engineers tried to unteach the model's bias by fine-tuning with balanced datasets and constraints.
After the audit, they had untaught sensitive associations from the classifier to reduce discriminatory outputs.
Researchers are experimenting with methods for unteaching specific concepts without retraining on the entire dataset.

Related Terms

  • synonym: machine unlearning - algorithms and methods for removing learned information from trained models
  • synonym: unlearn - to discard or reverse previously learned knowledge or habits
