sponge gourd

noun
tropical climbing plant used as a sponge
7.0 7.2
Pronunciation: /spʌndʒ gɔːrd/ /; / /spʌndʒ gʊərd/ (AmE), /spʌndʒ gɔːd/ /; / /spʌndʒ gʊəd/ (BrE)
A tropical climbing plant with large yellow flowers and edible young fruits, whose mature fibrous fruit is used as a sponge.

Examples

Dried sponge gourd
After harvesting, the dried sponge gourd can be used for cleaning and bathing.
Some gardeners grow sponge gourd both for food and for its natural sponges.
The sponge gourd is commonly cultivated in Asia for its edible young fruit.
