time limit

noun
deadline or cutoff time
1.0
Pronunciation: / taɪm ˈlɪmɪt / (AmE), / taɪm ˈlɪmɪt / (BrE)
a period of time by which something must be finished or completed; a deadline

Examples

The time limit expires
All applications must be submitted before the time limit expires.
She finished the marathon just seconds before the time limit.
The test has a strict time limit of two hours for all participants.
