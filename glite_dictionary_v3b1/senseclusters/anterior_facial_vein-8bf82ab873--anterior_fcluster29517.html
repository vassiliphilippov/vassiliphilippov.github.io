anterior facial vein

noun
vein in the face draining into the jugular vein
8.2
Pronunciation: /æn.ˈtɪr.iː.ər ˈfeɪ.ʃəl veɪn/ (AmE), /æn.ˈtɪə.ri.ə ˈfeɪ.ʃəl veɪn/ (BrE)
a vein that continues from the angular vein and joins the retromandibular vein before draining into the internal jugular vein

Examples

The anterior facial vein
Blockage in the anterior facial vein can lead to swelling and discomfort in the cheek area.
During surgery, care was taken not to damage the anterior facial vein.
The anterior facial vein is important in draining blood from the superficial structures of the face.
