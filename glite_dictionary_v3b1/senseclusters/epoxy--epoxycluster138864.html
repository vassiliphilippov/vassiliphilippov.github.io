epoxy

verb
to glue with epoxy resin
Pronunciation: / ɪˈpɑksi / (AmE), / ɪˈpɒksi / (BrE)
to glue or fasten using epoxy resin as an adhesive

Examples

To epoxy two pieces
He epoxied the loose tiles to ensure they wouldn't move again.
Make sure the surfaces are clean before you epoxy the parts in place.
She decided to epoxy the broken vase back together rather than use regular glue.
