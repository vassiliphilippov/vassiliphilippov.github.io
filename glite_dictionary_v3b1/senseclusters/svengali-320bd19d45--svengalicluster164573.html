Svengali

noun
manipulative, controlling person
Pronunciation: / svɛnˈɡɑːli /; / svɛnˈɡɑli / (AmE), / svɛnˈɡɑːli / (BrE)
a person who exerts a controlling or hypnotic influence over another, often for sinister purposes
