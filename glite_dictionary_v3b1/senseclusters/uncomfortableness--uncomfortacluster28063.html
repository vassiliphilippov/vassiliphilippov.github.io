uncomfortableness

noun
state of being uneasy or in pain
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkʌmfərtəblnəs /; / ʌnˈkʌmfɚtəblnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌmfətəblnəs /; / ʌnˈkʌmftəblnəs / (BrE)
the state of being uncomfortable, uneasy, or tense, either physically or emotionally
