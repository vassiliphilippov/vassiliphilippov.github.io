desecrated

adjective
treated with disrespect, esp. something sacred
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛsəˌkreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɛsɪˌkreɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a place or thing) treated with disrespect, especially something sacred or holy.

Examples

Desecrated graves
Several graves were found to be desecrated after the storm had passed.
The ancient temple was desecrated by vandals who destroyed its artifacts.
The community was outraged by the desecrated state of the war memorial.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defiled - dirtied or made impure, especially something sacred
  • antonym: sanctified - made holy; treated as sacred
