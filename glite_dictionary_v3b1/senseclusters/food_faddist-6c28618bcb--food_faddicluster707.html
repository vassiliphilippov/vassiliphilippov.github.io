food faddist

noun
person who often tries new diets
Pronunciation: / fuːd ˈfædɪst / (AmE), / fuːd ˈfædɪst / (BrE)
a person who frequently switches to new, fashionable diets, usually for short periods

Examples

A food faddist tries diets
As a food faddist, she constantly talks about the benefits of each new diet she tries.
He is known as a food faddist who is always experimenting with the latest diet trends.
The food faddist abandoned her low-carb plan after only a week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fad dieter - a person who tries new popular diets for brief periods
