spring

noun
coiled metal device with elasticity
3.5 3.1
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ / (AmE), / sprɪŋ / (BrE)
a piece of metal that can be pressed or pulled but returns to its original shape because it is elastic

Examples

Clock spring
He fixed the clock by replacing the broken spring inside.
The mattress contains a spring that makes it more comfortable to sleep on.
When you press the button, a spring pops it back up again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coil - a looped or spiral shape, often as in a spring
