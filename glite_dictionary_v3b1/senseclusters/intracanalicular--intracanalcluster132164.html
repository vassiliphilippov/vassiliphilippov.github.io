intracanalicular

adjective
within a canal
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəkəˌnælɪˈkjulər / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəkəˌnælɪˈkjʊlə / (BrE)
located or occurring within a canal, especially within a bodily canal or passage

Examples

Intracanalicular lesion
The doctor identified an intracanalicular lesion during the examination.
An intracanalicular tumor can affect hearing if it grows within the ear canal.
Special imaging is required to detect intracanalicular abnormalities in the optic nerve.
