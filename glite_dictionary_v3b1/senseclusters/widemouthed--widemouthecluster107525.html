widemouthed

adjective
broad opening
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪdˌmaʊθt / (AmE), / ˈwaɪdˌmaʊθt / (BrE)
having a mouth or opening that is wider than usual

Examples

A widemouthed jar
The widemouthed jar was perfect for storing cookies.
She poured the soup easily into the widemouthed bowl without spilling.
A widemouthed bottle makes it easier to add ice cubes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broad - wide in extent from side to side
  • antonym: narrow-mouthed - having a mouth or opening that is narrower than usual
