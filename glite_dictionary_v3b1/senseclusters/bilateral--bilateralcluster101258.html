bilateral

adjective
having two sides or involving two parties
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪˈlæt̬.ɚ.əl /; / baɪˈlæt̬.ɚ.əl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪˈlæt.ər.əl /; / baɪˈlæt.ər.əl / (BrE)
involving or affecting two sides, parts, parties, or countries

Examples
