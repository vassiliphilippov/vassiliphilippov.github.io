smotheringly

adverb
overprotectively
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsmʌðərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsmʌðərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is excessively affectionate or protective, restricting someone's freedom or independence

Examples

Smotheringly close hug
He held his daughter smotheringly, not wanting to let her go on her first day of school.
The room felt smotheringly warm, making it hard to breathe comfortably.
She cared for her friend smotheringly, always checking in and rarely giving her space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restrictively - in a way that limits freedom or movement
  • antonym: supportively - in a way that provides encouragement without being overbearing
