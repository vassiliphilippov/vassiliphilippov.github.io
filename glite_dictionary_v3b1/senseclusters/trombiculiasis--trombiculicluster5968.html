trombiculiasis

noun
infestation with chiggers (medical)
9.9
Pronunciation: / trɒmˌbɪkjəˈlaɪəsɪs / (AmE), / trɒmˌbɪkjʊˈleɪəsɪs / (BrE)
infestation of the skin by chiggers, leading to irritation and itching

Examples

Trombiculiasis skin irritation
After hiking through tall grass, several campers developed trombiculiasis characterized by intense itching and red bumps.
Doctors identified trombiculiasis when patients presented with rashes caused by chigger larvae.
Prevention of trombiculiasis includes using insect repellent and wearing long sleeves in infested areas.
