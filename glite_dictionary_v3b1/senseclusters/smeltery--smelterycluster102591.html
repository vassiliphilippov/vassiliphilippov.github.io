smeltery

noun
industrial plant for smelting metals
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɛl.tɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈsmɛl.tər.i / (BrE)
a factory or plant where ores or metal-bearing materials are smelted to extract metals

Examples

An old smeltery
After decades of operation, the old smeltery was finally closed due to environmental concerns.
Many workers in the region found employment at the large aluminum smeltery on the outskirts of town.
The copper ore was processed at the nearby smeltery before being shipped overseas.
