hang glider

noun
light non-motorized flying device for gliding
6.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /hæŋ ˈglaɪ.dər/ (AmE), /hæŋ ˈglaɪ.də/ (BrE)
a light, non-motorized flying device, shaped like a large kite, from which the pilot is suspended while flying or gliding through the air

Examples

A hang glider flight
After launching from the mountain, she smoothly navigated her hang glider through the chilly morning air.
He soared over the cliffs on his hang glider, enjoying breathtaking views of the coastline.
The club offers lessons for anyone interested in learning how to fly a hang glider.
