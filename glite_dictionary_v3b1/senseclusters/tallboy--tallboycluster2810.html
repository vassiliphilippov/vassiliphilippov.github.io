tallboy

noun
large beer can
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔlˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈtɔːlˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a tall can of beer, usually larger than the standard size, often containing 16 or 24 ounces

Examples

Many convenience stores sell tallboys as a popular option for beer drinkers.
She bought a tallboy to enjoy while watching the game on TV.
