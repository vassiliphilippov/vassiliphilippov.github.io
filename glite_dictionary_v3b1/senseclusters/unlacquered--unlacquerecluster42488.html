unlacquered

adjective
without lacquer finish
6.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlækərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlækəd / (BrE)
not coated with lacquer or a glossy protective finish

Examples

Unlacquered wood finish
The craftsman preferred to leave the wood unlacquered for a more natural appearance.
You could see the grain clearly on the unlacquered surface of the cabinet.
Unlike the shiny chairs, these stools were completely unlacquered and felt rough to the touch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lacquered - coated with lacquer or a glossy finish
  • synonym: unfinished - not given a final surface treatment or coating
