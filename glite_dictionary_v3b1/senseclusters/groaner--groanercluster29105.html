groaner

noun
bad or obvious joke
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊnər / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊnə / (BrE)
a joke or pun that is so bad or obvious it makes people groan rather than laugh

Examples

A bad groaner joke
He told a groaner about a chicken crossing the road, and everyone groaned.
Whenever she starts with a groaner, her friends know to expect a terrible pun.
The comedian finished his set with a classic groaner that made the audience laugh and groan at the same time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dad joke - a simple, often predictable joke, typically told by fathers
  • synonym: pun - a joke that plays on words with double meanings
