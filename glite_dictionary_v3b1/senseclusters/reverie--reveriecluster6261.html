reverie

noun
daydreaming; lost in thought
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛvəri / (AmE), / ˈrɛvəri / (BrE)
a state of being pleasantly lost in your thoughts; a daydream

Examples

A reverie of thoughts
A soft melody can often trigger a pleasant reverie.
He gazed out the window, caught in a reverie about distant places.
Lost in reverie, she didn't hear the teacher call her name.
As she listened to the soft music, a peaceful reverie overtook her.
His reverie was interrupted by the sudden ringing of the phone.
She drifted into a reverie during the long, quiet afternoon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concentration - The action or power of focusing attention or mental effort
  • synonym: daydream - a series of pleasant thoughts that distract one's attention from the present
