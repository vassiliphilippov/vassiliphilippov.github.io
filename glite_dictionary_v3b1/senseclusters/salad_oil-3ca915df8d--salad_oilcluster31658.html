salad oil

noun
a light vegetable oil for salads
Pronunciation: / ˈsæləd ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈsæləd ɔɪl / (BrE)
a light, edible vegetable oil used in salad dressings and for culinary purposes

Examples

Salad oil for cooking
Many recipes recommend using salad oil instead of butter for a lighter flavor.
She poured some salad oil into the bowl before adding vinegar and spices.
This salad oil is made from pure sunflower seeds and has a delicate taste.
