teaberry

noun
spicy red berry; source of wintergreen oil
Pronunciation: / ˈtiˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌbɛri / (BrE)
a low-growing North American shrub with aromatic leaves and spicy red berries, or the fruit itself, often used as a source of wintergreen oil
