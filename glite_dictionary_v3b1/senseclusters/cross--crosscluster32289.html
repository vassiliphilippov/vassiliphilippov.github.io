cross

verb
move across or pass over
1.3 1.0
Pronunciation: /krɔːs/ /; / /krɑːs/ (AmE), /krɒs/ (BrE)
to move, travel, or extend across, over, or past something; or to meet/intersect with something or someone

Examples

To cross the street
Hundreds of people cross the river every day to reach the city.
She plans to cross the desert alone next year.
The hikers crossed the bridge at dawn, enjoying the view.
The festival crosses over two weekends in July.
The railway line crosses the entire city from east to west.
This tradition crosses several generations in my family.
Our paths crossed as I was leaving and she was arriving.
The ships crossed each other in the foggy channel.
The two trains crossed just outside the station.

  • synonym: traverse - to travel across or through an area
