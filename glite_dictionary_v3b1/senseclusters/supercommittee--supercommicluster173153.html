supercommittee

proper noun
U.S. budget committee
7.2
Pronunciation: /suː.pər.kə.ˈmɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /suː.pə.kə.ˈmɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
an informal name for the bipartisan Joint Select Committee on Deficit Reduction formed by the U.S. Congress in 2011 to address the federal budget deficit

Examples

The U.S. supercommittee
The supercommittee was unable to reach a bipartisan agreement on reducing the national deficit.
Many Americans followed the progress of the supercommittee closely in 2011.
After months of debate, the supercommittee announced it could not meet its deadline.
