intendment

noun
legal interpretation
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛndmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛndmənt / (BrE)
the meaning or intention, especially as interpreted in legal documents or statutes

Examples

The intendment of law
The judge considered the intendment of the law before making a final decision.
Lawyers debated the intendment of the contract's ambiguous clause in court.
Understanding the intendment of a statute is crucial for proper legal interpretation.

  • synonym: interpretation - the act of explaining or determining the meaning of something
