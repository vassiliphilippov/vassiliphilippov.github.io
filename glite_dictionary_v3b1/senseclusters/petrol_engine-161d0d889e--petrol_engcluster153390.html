petrol engine

noun
engine that runs on petrol (gasoline)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛtroʊl ˈɛn.dʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɛtrəl ˈɛn.dʒɪn / (BrE)
An internal-combustion engine that runs on petrol (gasoline), commonly used in cars and other vehicles.

Examples

A petrol engine car
Many classic British cars are powered by a petrol engine.
She prefers vehicles with a petrol engine because they are quieter than diesel ones.
The new model is available with either a diesel or a petrol engine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diesel engine - an internal-combustion engine that runs on diesel fuel
  • synonym: gasoline engine - an internal-combustion engine that runs on gasoline
