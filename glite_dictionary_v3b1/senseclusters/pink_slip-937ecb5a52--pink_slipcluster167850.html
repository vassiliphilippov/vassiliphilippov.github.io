pink slip

noun
notice of dismissal from a job
Pronunciation: / pɪŋk slɪp / (AmE), / pɪŋk slɪp / (BrE)
an official or written notice given to an employee to inform them that they have been dismissed from their job

Examples

Receive a pink slip
After ten years at the company, Sarah was shocked to receive a pink slip without any warning.
Many workers feared they would find a pink slip in their mailboxes after the restructuring.
The company issued hundreds of pink slips during the economic downturn.
After the company downsized, dozens of employees received pink slips in the mail.
He was shocked when he found a pink slip on his desk after the meeting.
Many workers fear the possibility of getting a pink slip during tough economic times.

  • synonym: termination - the act of being fired or dismissed from a job
