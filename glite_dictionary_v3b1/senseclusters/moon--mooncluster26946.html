moon

verb
rise rapidly in price (crypto/meme-stock slang)
Pronunciation: / muːn /; / mun / (AmE), / muːn / (BrE)
(slang) (of a price, asset, or popularity) to rise very quickly in value or popularity, especially online or in cryptocurrency contexts

Examples

Crypto mooned overnight
The token mooned after the announcement, sending early investors huge unexpected profits.
Many small investors hoped the meme stock would moon after the celebrity endorsement.
When the market rallied overnight, the altcoin started mooning and attracted global headlines.
Speculators believe meme stocks will moon as social hype drives prices higher.
The token mooned overnight after the influencer's post sent buyers rushing in.
If that campaign succeeds, the band's popularity could moon and reach fans worldwide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skyrocket - to increase very quickly to a high level
  • synonym: surge - a sudden large increase or rise
