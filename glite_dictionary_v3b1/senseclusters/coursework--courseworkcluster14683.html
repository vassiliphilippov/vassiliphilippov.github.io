coursework

noun
work done by students as part of a course
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrsˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈkɔːswɜːk / (BrE)
work assigned to and completed by a student as part of a course of study, typically contributing to their overall grade.

Examples

Good coursework
Good coursework can make a significant difference to your final grade.
His teacher praised the quality of his coursework in mathematics.
She spent all weekend finishing her coursework for biology class.
