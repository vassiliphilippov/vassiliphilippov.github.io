postsurgical

adjective
after surgery
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈsɜrdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈsɜːdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the period or care after a surgical operation

Examples

Postsurgical care
The patient required careful postsurgical monitoring to prevent complications.
Proper postsurgical care can speed up the healing process after an operation.
Doctors discussed the postsurgical instructions with the family before discharge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: postoperative - relating to the period after a surgical operation
  • antonym: presurgical - relating to the period before a surgical operation
