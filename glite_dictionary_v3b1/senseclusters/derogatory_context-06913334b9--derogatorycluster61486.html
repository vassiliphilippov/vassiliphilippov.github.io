derogatory context

noun
situation expressing disrespect or negativity
9.9 5.8
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɑɡəˌtɔri ˈkɑn.tɛkst / (AmE), / dɪˈrɒɡə.təri ˈkɒn.tɛkst / (BrE)
a situation or environment in which language or actions express disrespect or negativity toward someone or something

Examples

Derogatory context in speech
