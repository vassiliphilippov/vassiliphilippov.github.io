derogatory context

noun
situation expressing disrespect or negativity
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɑɡəˌtɔri ˈkɑn.tɛkst / (AmE), / dɪˈrɒɡə.təri ˈkɒn.tɛkst / (BrE)
a situation or environment in which language or actions express disrespect or negativity toward someone or something

Examples

Derogatory context in speech
Be cautious when quoting terms that have a derogatory context in academic writing.
Her comment was taken out of its derogatory context and misunderstood by the audience.
The word "geek" used to be employed only in a derogatory context but now is often neutral or positive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pejorative - expressing contempt or disapproval
