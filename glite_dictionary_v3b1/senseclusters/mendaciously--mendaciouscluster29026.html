mendaciously

adverb
in a lying or untruthful way
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɛnˈdeɪʃəsli / (AmE), / mɛnˈdeɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a manner that is untruthful or dishonest; lying or deceitfully.

Examples

To mendaciously lie
He mendaciously assured us that the report was accurate, though he knew it was not.
She mendaciously denied any knowledge of the missing documents.
The witness mendaciously claimed he had never been at the scene of the crime.

Related Terms

  • antonym: honestly - in a truthful or sincere manner
  • synonym: untruthfully - in a way that is not true
