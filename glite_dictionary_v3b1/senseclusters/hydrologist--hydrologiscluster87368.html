hydrologist

noun
scientist studying water systems
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdrɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / haɪˈdrɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies the properties, distribution, and movement of water on the Earth's surface

Examples

A hydrologist studies water
A team led by a hydrologist developed new models for groundwater management.
After years of research, she became a renowned hydrologist studying river basins.
The hydrologist analyzed rainfall data to predict flooding risks in the region.

  • synonym: hydrology - the scientific study of water
