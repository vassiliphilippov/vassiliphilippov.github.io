drop-leaf table

noun
table with foldable leaves for adjusting surface size
8.7 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑp lif ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈdrɒp liːf ˈteɪbl̩ / (BrE)
a table with hinged leaves that can be raised to extend the surface or dropped down when not in use

Examples

A drop-leaf table folds
The old drop-leaf table can be expanded to seat more guests during dinner parties.

With both leaves down, the drop-leaf table takes up very little space in the room.
