eighties

noun
age between 80 and 90 years
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪtiz / (AmE), / ˈeɪtiz / (BrE)
the period of a person's life between the ages of 80 and 90

Examples

In your eighties
Many people maintain a healthy lifestyle throughout their eighties and beyond.
My grandfather stayed active well into his eighties and enjoyed traveling.
She published her final novel in her eighties, inspiring writers of all ages.
