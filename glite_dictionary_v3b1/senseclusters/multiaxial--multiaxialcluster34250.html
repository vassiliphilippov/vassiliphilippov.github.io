multiaxial

adjective
involving several axes
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈæksiəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈæksiəl / (BrE)
involving or relating to more than one axis

Examples

A multiaxial joint
The shoulder is a multiaxial joint, allowing movement in several directions.
Engineers designed a multiaxial testing machine to simulate complex forces.
Some crystals display multiaxial optical properties under polarized light.
