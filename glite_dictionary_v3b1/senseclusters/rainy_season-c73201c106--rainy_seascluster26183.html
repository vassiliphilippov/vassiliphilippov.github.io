rainy season

noun
time of year with most rainfall in tropics
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪni ˌsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈreɪni ˌsiːzn̩ / (BrE)
the time of year in tropical regions when most of the annual rainfall occurs

Examples

The rainy season starts
Farmers eagerly await the rainy season to start planting their crops.
The rainy season usually lasts from May to October in this region.
Travelers often avoid the rainy season because of frequent downpours and flooding.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dry season - the time of year in tropical regions with little or no rainfall
