desk officer

noun
officer assigned to administrative duties
1.9 3.5
Pronunciation: /desk ˈɔː.fɪ.sər/ /; / /desk ˈɔː.fə.sər/ /; / /desk ˈɑː.fɪ.sər/ (AmE), /desk ˈɒf.ɪ.sə/ /; / /desk ˈɒf.ə.sə/ (BrE)
