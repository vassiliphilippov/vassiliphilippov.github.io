desk officer

noun
officer assigned to administrative duties
Pronunciation: / dɛsk ˈɑfəsər / (AmE), / dɛsk ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a military or police officer who performs administrative duties rather than serving in active field operations

Examples

Desk officer at headquarters
After years of field work, she was promoted to desk officer at headquarters.
As a desk officer, his responsibilities included processing reports and managing staff assignments.
Many military personnel become desk officers later in their careers when they transition to administrative roles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: field officer - an officer serving in active field operations
