toggle

noun
rod or pin fastener for ropes or cables
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑɡl̩ / (AmE), / ˈtɒɡl̩ / (BrE)
a fastener consisting of a short rod, pin, or crosspiece that is inserted through a loop or eye at the end of a rope, chain, or cable to fasten it securely

Examples

Rope toggle
He fastened the sail by slipping the toggle through a loop in the canvas.
The anchor was secured using a toggle and rope.
The old trunk had a simple toggle to keep it closed tightly.
