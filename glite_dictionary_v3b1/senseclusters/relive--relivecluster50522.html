Examples

To relive a memory
He tried not to relive the embarrassing moment during the interview, but it kept coming back to him.
In his autobiography, the author relives his greatest triumphs and tragedies on the football field.
Whenever she hears that song, she relives the memories of her childhood.

Synonyms

  • recapture
    6.0 1.83/m
    recapture means feeling a past mood or moment again, while relive means experiencing that memory directly in your mind.
  • recreate
    5.5 1.71/m
    recreate is to build or picture an event again in your mind, while relive is to feel that event as if happening again.
