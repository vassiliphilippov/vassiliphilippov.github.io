relive

verb
experience again in memory or imagination
4.4
Pronunciation: / riˈlɪv / (AmE), / riˈlɪv / (BrE)
to experience something again, especially in one's imagination or memory

Examples

To relive a memory
He tried not to relive the embarrassing moment during the interview, but it kept coming back to him.
In his autobiography, the author relives his greatest triumphs and tragedies on the football field.
Whenever she hears that song, she relives the memories of her childhood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forget - to fail to remember, to cease thinking of
  • synonym: revisit - to consider or look at again, especially in memory or discussion
