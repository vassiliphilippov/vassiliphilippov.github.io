sprinklered

adjective
covered with sprinkler water
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋklərd / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋkləd / (BrE)
having been sprayed or wetted by a sprinkler system or device

Examples

Sprinklered lawn
The sprinklered lawn looked much greener than the areas that had not been watered.
After the fire alarm, the entire office was sprinklered and everyone had to evacuate.
We set up a picnic on the sprinklered grass to stay cool during the hot afternoon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dry - not wet or lacking moisture
  • synonym: watered - having been supplied or covered with water
