IPR

noun
intellectual property rights
6.1
Pronunciation: / aɪ piː ɑːr / (AmE), / aɪ piː ɑː / (BrE)
abbreviation for intellectual property rights, the legal rights protecting creations of the mind such as inventions, works of art, and trademarks

Examples

IPR protection
The startup was careful to secure its IPR before launching its new product.
International agreements help protect IPR across different countries.
Lawyers specializing in IPR often deal with patents and trademarks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: copyright - legal right protecting original works of authorship
  • synonym: intellectual property - creations of the mind such as inventions, literary works, and symbols
  • synonym: patent - exclusive legal right granted for an invention
