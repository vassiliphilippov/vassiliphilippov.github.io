Planck

proper noun
fundamental physics units
Pronunciation: / plæŋk / (AmE), / plæŋk / (BrE)
the name given to several fundamental units in physics, such as Planck length or Planck time, named after Max Planck

Examples

Planck length unit
The Planck length is the smallest meaningful unit of distance in physics.
Scientists use Planck units to describe phenomena at the quantum scale.
The concept of Planck time helps physicists understand the earliest moments of the universe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Planck length - the smallest meaningful unit of length in physics
  • synonym: Planck time - the smallest meaningful unit of time in physics
