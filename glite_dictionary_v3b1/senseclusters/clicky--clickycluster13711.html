clicky

adjective
makes clicking sound
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪki / (AmE), / ˈklɪki / (BrE)
making a noticeable clicking sound, especially when pressed or used

Examples

Clicky keyboard
Many people enjoy using clicky keyboards because of the satisfying sound they make.
The old mouse was very clicky, so everyone in the office could hear when it was used.
He prefers clicky buttons on his gaming controller for better feedback during play.

Related Terms

  • antonym: silent - making little or no sound
  • synonym: tactile - having a physical sensation or feedback when touched
