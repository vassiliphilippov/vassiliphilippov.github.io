Aegospotamos

proper noun
ancient river, site of final Peloponnesian War battle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌiːɡəˈspɑtəˌmɒs /; / iːˌɡɑːspəˈteɪmɒs / (AmE), / ˌiːɡɒspəˈteɪmɒs /; / iːˌɡɒspəˈtɑːmɒs / (BrE)
an ancient river or creek in Thrace (now in Turkey), famous as the site where the Spartan fleet defeated the Athenians in the final battle of the Peloponnesian War
