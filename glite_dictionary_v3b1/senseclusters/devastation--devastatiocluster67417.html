devastation

noun
severe damage or destruction
1.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /de.və.ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /dɪ.və.ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /de.və.ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /dɪ.və.ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
great destruction or damage, especially as a result of war, disaster, or violence
