Examples

Distant past

Synonyms

  • remote
    5.1 3.42/m
    remote describes something very far in time or place and often formal, while distant refers specifically to being far in time.
  • far-off
    4.2 2.77/m
    far-off feels informal or poetic for something very far in time or space, while distant is a neutral descriptive adjective.
  • faraway
    2.8 2.73/m
    faraway paints a picture of a place or time that is very far and dreamy, while distant is more general and factual.
  • far
    1.0 38/m
    far often names a point a long time away or place in speech, while distant stresses separation or remoteness in time.
  • removed
    5.3 0.00/m
    removed often means set apart or without close connection to something, while distant only means far in time.
