cloud-covered

adjective
covered with clouds

(of the sky, weather, or a surface) covered with or obscured by clouds.

Examples

Cloud-covered sky
A cloud-covered sky made the afternoon seem gloomy and gray.
From the plane, the city below was cloud-covered and invisible to us.
The mountaintop remained cloud-covered throughout our hike, hiding the view.

Synonyms

  • cloudy
    1.0 3.19/m
    cloudy is a common word for a sky with many clouds and little sun, while cloud-covered is plain and literal.
  • clouded
    2.1 0.01/m
    clouded often describes something made unclear, like a glass or mind, while cloud-covered focuses on physical sky or surface.
  • overcast
    2.5 0.09/m
    overcast means the sky is fully covered and often gloomy, while cloud-covered simply says clouds hide the sky or surface.

Related Terms
