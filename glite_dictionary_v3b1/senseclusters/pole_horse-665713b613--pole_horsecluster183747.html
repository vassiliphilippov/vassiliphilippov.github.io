pole horse

noun
harness racing horse with innermost position
6.7
Pronunciation: / poʊl hɔrs / (AmE), / pəʊl hɔːs / (BrE)
the horse assigned the innermost starting position next to the inside rail in a harness race

Examples

Pole horse position
Because he drew the pole horse position, his chances of a fast start were much higher.
The announcer noted that the pole horse had a clear advantage on the tight track.
Trainers were excited when their horse was listed as the pole horse for the big race.
