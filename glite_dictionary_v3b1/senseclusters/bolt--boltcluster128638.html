bolt

noun
metal fastener with matching nut
2.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /boʊlt/ (AmE), /bəʊlt/ /; / /bɒlt/ (BrE)
A metal pin or rod with a head at one end, designed to be inserted through holes in assembled parts and secured with a nut to form a fastener.

Examples

A bolt and nut
Each chair leg is attached with a single bolt and nut.
Please hand me a wrench to loosen this rusty bolt.
The mechanic tightened the bolt to secure the two metal plates together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nut - a metal block with a hole and internal threads to receive a bolt
  • synonym: screw - a metal fastener with a helical ridge, sometimes used with a nut
