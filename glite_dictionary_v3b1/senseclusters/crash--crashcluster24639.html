crash

verb
make a sudden loud noise
1.4
Pronunciation: / kræʃ / (AmE), / kræʃ / (BrE)
to make a sudden loud noise, usually as a result of something striking or colliding forcefully

Examples

Applause crashed through
As the curtains fell, applause crashed through the theatre.
The dishes crashed to the floor, startling everyone in the room.
Thunder crashed overhead during the fierce storm last night.
