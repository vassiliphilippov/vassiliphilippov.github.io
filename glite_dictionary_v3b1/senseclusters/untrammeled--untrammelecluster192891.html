untrammeled

adjective
not restricted or limited; free
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtræməld /; / ʌnˈtræmld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtræməld / (BrE)
not restricted or limited in any way; free and unrestricted.

Examples

An untrammeled life
He longed for an untrammeled life, free from routine and expectation.
Her writing is characterized by untrammeled creativity and boldness.
The new law allows for untrammeled access to the national park by the public.
His untrammelled imagination led to some truly innovative inventions.
In the wilderness, wildlife roams untrammelled by human interference.
The artist enjoyed an untrammelled freedom to express her ideas.
