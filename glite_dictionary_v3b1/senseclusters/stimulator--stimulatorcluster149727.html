stimulator

noun
motivator or encourager
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈstɪm.jə.leɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈstɪm.jə.leɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person or thing that encourages, motivates, or increases activity or energy in others

Examples

A stimulator of ideas
The teacher acted as a stimulator for her students, encouraging them to explore new ideas.
Caffeine is a common stimulator that helps people feel more awake and alert.
He became a stimulator of change in the company by introducing innovative strategies.
Caffeine is a well-known stimulator of the central nervous system.
The new teacher acted as a stimulator for the students' interest in science.
Physical therapy devices can serve as stimulators to help muscles recover after injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catalyst - something that causes an event or change to happen more quickly
  • antonym: discourager - a person or thing that causes others to lose confidence or enthusiasm
  • antonym: inhibitor - something that slows down or prevents a process or activity
  • synonym: inspirer - someone who inspires others
  • synonym: motivator - a person or thing that provides motivation
