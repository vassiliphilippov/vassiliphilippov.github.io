imperialistically

adverb
in an imperialist way
8.4
Pronunciation: /ɪm.pɪr.ɪ.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kliː/ /; / /ɪm.pɪr.ɪ.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kə.liː/ (AmE), /ɪm.pɪə.rɪ.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kliː/ /; / /ɪm.pɪə.rɪ.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kə.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that relates to or supports empire-building or domination over other countries or peoples

Examples

Acting imperialistically
The nation expanded its borders imperialistically, seeking to control neighboring territories.
Critics argued that the company operated imperialistically in foreign markets.
Some leaders have acted imperialistically to increase their country's influence abroad.
