spoon

noun
unit of available personal energy
8.2
Pronunciation: /spuːn/ /; / /spʊn/ (AmE), /spuːn/ (BrE)
An informal unit representing a limited amount of physical or mental energy that a person has available for activities; used metaphorically in discussions of chronic illness, fatigue, or disability (see 'spoon theory').

Examples

Counting spoons after work
After her chemo, she counted her remaining spoons and decided to rest all afternoon.
I can't meet tonight — I'm down to one spoon after a busy day at work.
The support group taught her to budget her spoons so she wouldn't overdo activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: energy - the capacity to do physical or mental activity; what a 'spoon' is used to measure metaphorically
