Synonyms

  • kinfolk

    kinfolk is a common collective name for extended relatives, while kinsfolk is a less common variant used in some dialects.
  • kin
    2.6 7.90/m
    kin refers to one's relatives as a group often, while kinsfolk focuses on extended family members together.
  • kinspeople
    6.2 0.02/m
    kinspeople names relatives or family members in plain speech, while kinsfolk is an older collective term for extended relatives.
  • family
    1.0 513/m
    family covers immediate household and legal ties often, while kinsfolk highlights wider blood relatives and extended relations.
  • relative
    1.0 34/m
    relative names one person tied by blood or marriage, while kinsfolk refers to a whole group of extended relatives.
