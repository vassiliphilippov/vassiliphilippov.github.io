Santal

proper noun
indigenous group in India
Pronunciation: / ˈsænˌtɑl / (AmE), / ˈsæntɑːl / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous people living mainly in eastern India and Bangladesh

Examples

A Santal person
The Santal have a rich tradition of storytelling and music.
Many Santal communities celebrate their festivals with colorful dances.
A Santal leader spoke about preserving their language and culture.
