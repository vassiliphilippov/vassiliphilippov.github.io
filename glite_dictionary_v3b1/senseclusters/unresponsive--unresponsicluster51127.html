Synonyms

  • nonresponsive
    6.2 0.10/m
    nonresponsive means not replying to a request or stimulus, while unresponsive also suggests not showing interest or reacting to influence.
  • unreactive
    6.5 0.01/m
    unreactive does not show a physical or emotional response to stimulation, while unresponsive often means ignoring communication or requests.
  • uninterested
    3.5 2.07/m

  • indifferent
    4.4 5.17/m
