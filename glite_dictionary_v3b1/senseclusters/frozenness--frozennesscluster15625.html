frozenness

noun
inability to act
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfroʊ.zən.nəs/ /; / /ˈfroʊz.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈfrəʊ.zən.nəs/ /; / /ˈfrəʊz.nəs/ (BrE)
the state of being unable to move or act, especially due to shock, fear, or indecision

Examples

Frozenness from fear
His frozenness during the emergency prevented him from calling for help.
The frozenness she felt on stage made it impossible to remember her lines.
Moments of frozenness can happen to anyone when faced with unexpected news.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confidence - a feeling of self-assurance and readiness to act
  • synonym: hesitation - the act of pausing before taking action
  • synonym: paralysis - the loss of ability to move or act
