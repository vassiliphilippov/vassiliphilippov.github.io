Weeble

proper noun
wobbling toy brand
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːbəl / (AmE), / ˈwiːbəl / (BrE)
a brand name for a small, round-bottomed toy figure that rocks back and forth but does not fall over

Examples

Weeble brand figure
The toddler giggled as the Weeble spun around but never tipped over.
He collected every type of Weeble from the toy store display.
When pushed, the Weeble wobbled but always returned to an upright position.
