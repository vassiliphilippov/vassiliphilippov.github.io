previous question

noun
motion to end debate and vote immediately
8.9 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààpri.vi.…ôs Ààkw…õs.t É…ôn / (AmE), / ÀàpriÀê.vi.…ôs Ààkw…õs.t É…ôn / (BrE)
