smooch

verb
to kiss affectionately
Pronunciation: / smuːtʃ / (AmE), / smuːtʃ / (BrE)
to kiss someone in a playful or affectionate way

Examples

To smooch your partner
He leaned in to smooch his girlfriend under the mistletoe at the party.
They smooched on the park bench, laughing and enjoying the sunny afternoon.
She gave her puppy a quick smooch before leaving for work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kiss - to touch someone with your lips as a sign of affection
  • degree lower: peck - to kiss someone quickly and lightly
