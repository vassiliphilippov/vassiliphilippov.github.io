vermiculate

adjective
infested or damaged as if by worms
Pronunciation: / vərˈmɪkjələt / (AmE), / vəˈmɪkjʊlət / (BrE)
infested with or damaged as if by worms; showing marks or holes as though eaten by worms

Examples

Vermiculate wood
She examined the painting's frame, noting its vermiculate appearance and tiny, twisting holes.
The archaeologist found vermiculate bones in the ancient burial site, riddled with worm tracks.
The old wooden beams were so vermiculate that they crumbled at the touch.
