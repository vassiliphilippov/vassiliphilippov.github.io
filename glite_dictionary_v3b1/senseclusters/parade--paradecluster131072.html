parade

verb
march in a public procession
Pronunciation: / pəˈreɪd / (AmE), / pəˈreɪd / (BrE)
to march or walk together in a public procession, often to celebrate or display something

Examples

To parade through town
Soldiers parade down the avenue during the national holiday.
The students will parade through the city center for the annual festival.
Thousands of fans paraded in celebration after the team won the championship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: march - to walk with regular steps, often as part of a group or procession
