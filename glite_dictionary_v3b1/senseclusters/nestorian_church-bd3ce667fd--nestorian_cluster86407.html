Nestorian Church

noun
historic Christian sect in Persia and Asia
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɛˈstɔriən tʃɜrtʃ / (AmE), / nɛˈstɔːriən tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a Christian sect founded in the 5th century, emphasizing the independence of the divine and human natures of Jesus Christ, prominent in ancient Persia and Asia
