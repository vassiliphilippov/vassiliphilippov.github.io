towerlike

adjective
resembling a tower
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtaʊəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the shape or qualities of a tower, especially in being tall and narrow

Examples

Tall towerlike cliffs
The towerlike cliffs rose sharply above the valley floor.
A towerlike stack of books nearly reached the ceiling in his office.
The architect designed a towerlike monument to be seen from miles away.

Related Terms

  • antonym: short - having little height
  • synonym: towering - very tall or high
