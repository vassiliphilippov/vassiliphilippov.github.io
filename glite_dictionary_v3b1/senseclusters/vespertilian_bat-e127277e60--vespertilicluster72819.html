vespertilian bat

noun
a bat of the family Vespertilionidae
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛspərˈtɪliən bæt / (AmE), / ˌvɛspəˈtɪliən bæt / (BrE)
A bat belonging to the family Vespertilionidae, typically insectivorous and comprising the largest bat family worldwide.

Examples

A vespertilian bat species
Scientists observed several species of vespertilian bat roosting in the abandoned mine.
The vespertilian bat hunts insects using echolocation during the twilight hours.
Unlike fruit bats, a vespertilian bat typically preys on insects.

Related Terms
