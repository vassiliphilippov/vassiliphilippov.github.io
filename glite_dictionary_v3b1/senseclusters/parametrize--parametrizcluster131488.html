parametrize

verb
to set parameters
8.4
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈræm.ə.traɪz/ (AmE), /pə.ˈræm.ə.traɪz/ (BrE)
to assign or determine specific values for the parameters of a model, function, or system, often to fit data or set configurations

Examples

To parametrize a model
The engineers will parametrize the simulation to match real-world conditions as closely as possible.
Before running the experiment, you need to parametrize the model with accurate input values.
Researchers often parametrize their algorithms to optimize performance for different datasets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: configure - to arrange or set up the parts of something
  • synonym: customize - to modify something to suit a particular individual or task
