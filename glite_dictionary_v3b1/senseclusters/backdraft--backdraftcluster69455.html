backdraft

noun
negative backlash
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌdræft / (AmE), / ˈbækˌdrɑːft / (BrE)
a strong negative reaction or backlash that occurs as a result of an action or policy

Examples

Policy backdraft
The new tax law caused a significant backdraft from business owners across the city.
After the controversial decision, the committee faced an unexpected backdraft from the public.
Implementing the policy without consultation led to a political backdraft that surprised the officials.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backlash - a strong negative reaction to a decision or action
  • synonym: reaction - a response to an action or event
