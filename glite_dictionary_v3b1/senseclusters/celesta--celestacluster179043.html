celesta

noun
keyboard percussion instrument
9.9
Pronunciation: / səˈlɛstə / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛstə / (BrE)
a musical instrument with steel plates that are struck by hammers controlled by a keyboard, producing a delicate, bell-like sound

Examples

The celesta keyboard
In the ballet, Tchaikovsky used the celesta to create a delicate, sparkling sound.
She learned to play the celesta for the school concert.
The celesta added a magical quality to the orchestra's performance.
