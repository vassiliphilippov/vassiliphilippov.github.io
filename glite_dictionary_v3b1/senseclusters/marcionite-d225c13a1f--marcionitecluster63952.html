Marcionite

adjective
of Marcion's teachings
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrsiəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɑːsɪənaɪt / (BrE)
relating to Marcion, his teachings, or the beliefs of the Marcionite movement in early Christianity

Examples

Marcionite teachings
The Marcionite view of scripture differed greatly from that of mainstream Christianity.
Some early church councils condemned Marcionite beliefs as heretical.
A Marcionite gospel excluded many books found in the traditional New Testament.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Gnostic - relating to Gnosticism, an early Christian movement
  • antonym: orthodox - conforming to established or traditional Christian beliefs
