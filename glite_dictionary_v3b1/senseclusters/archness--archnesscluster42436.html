archness

noun
playful or teasing slyness
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrtnəs / (AmE), / ˈɑːtʃnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being playfully teasing, mischievous, or slyly humorous, often in a way that is slightly inappropriate or cheeky

Examples

The archness of reply
He couldn't help but notice the archness in her reply as she teased her friend.
Her archness often lightened the mood during tense meetings.
There was a hint of archness in his smile that made everyone laugh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impudence - the quality of being rude or disrespectful
  • antonym: seriousness - the lack of playfulness or lightness
