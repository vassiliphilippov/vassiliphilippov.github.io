room clerk

noun
hotel employee who assigns rooms
3.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ruÀêm kl…úÀêrk / (AmE), / ruÀêm kl…ëÀêk / (BrE)
a hotel employee responsible for assigning rooms to guests and managing room keys and records

Examples

Room clerk at desk
After a long trip, we checked in with the room clerk at the front desk.
The room clerk handed us our keys and explained the hotel amenities.
The room clerk quickly found an available room for the late-night guest.
