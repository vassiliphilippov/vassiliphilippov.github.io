Kanchenjunga

proper noun
mountain on Nepal-India border, third highest in the world
9.9
Pronunciation: /kæn.tʃən.ˈdʒʊŋ.gə/ /; / /kən.tʃen.ˈdʒʊŋ.gə/ (AmE), /kæn.tʃən.ˈdʒʊŋ.gə/ /; / /kən.tʃen.ˈdʒʊŋ.gə/ (BrE)
