varnish

noun
clear protective finish for surfaces
2.5 3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑrnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈvɑːnɪʃ / (BrE)
a clear, hard, and shiny coating applied to a surface for protection or decorative purposes

Examples

A coat of varnish
After applying a fresh layer of varnish, the floor gleamed beautifully.
The wooden table was coated in varnish to protect it from scratches.
You need to let the varnish dry completely before using the chair.
