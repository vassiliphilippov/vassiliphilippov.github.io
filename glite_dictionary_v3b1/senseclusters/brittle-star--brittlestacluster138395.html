brittle-star

noun
marine animal with slender arms
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪt̬l̩ stɑr / (AmE), / ˈbrɪtl̩ stɑː / (BrE)
a small echinoderm with long, slender, fragile arms radiating from a central disc, resembling a starfish

Examples

A brittle-star animal
During the low tide, we found several brittle-stars clinging to the underside of rocks.
Marine biologists study the unusual movement of the brittle-star in its natural habitat.
The brittle-star quickly retreated under a rock when disturbed by divers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: starfish - a marine animal with arms radiating from a central disc, related to the brittle-star
