securitize

verb
to provide collateral
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊrɪtaɪz / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərɪtaɪz / (BrE)
to make a loan or other financial obligation secure by pledging property or assets as collateral

Examples

To securitize assets
The company decided to securitize its assets to obtain a lower interest rate on the loan.
Banks often securitize mortgages to reduce their financial risk.
To secure the business loan, the owner agreed to securitize it with his property.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collateralize - to provide collateral for a loan or obligation
