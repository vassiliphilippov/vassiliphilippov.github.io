Pronunciation: /ˈpɑːr.təm/ (AmE), /ˈpɑː.təm/ (BrE)
a combining form meaning 'birth', used in medical terms to refer to the act or period of giving birth

Examples

Partum in 'postpartum'
The prefix 'post-' in postpartum means after, so postpartum refers to the period after birth.
Doctors use the term antepartum to describe the time before a baby is born.
In medical language, partum is combined with other elements to form words about childbirth.
