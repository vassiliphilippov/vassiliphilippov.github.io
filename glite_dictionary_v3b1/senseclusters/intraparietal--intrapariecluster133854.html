intraparietal

adjective
within parietal region
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəpəˈraɪətəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəpəˈraɪətəl / (BrE)
located or occurring within the parietal region, especially referring to the parietal area of the brain

Examples

Intraparietal sulcus
The intraparietal sulcus is important for processing visual information in the brain.
Researchers studied the intraparietal cortex to understand its role in attention and perception.
Damage to the intraparietal area can affect spatial awareness and coordination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parietal - relating to the wall of a body part or the parietal bone
