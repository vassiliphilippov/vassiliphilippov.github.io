pageboy

noun
boy messenger or errand-runner
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪdʒ.bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪdʒ.bɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy employed to run errands or act as a messenger, especially in a hotel or large house

Examples

Hotel pageboy
A young pageboy in a smart uniform waited by the entrance to assist guests.
In historical dramas, a pageboy often carries notes for the nobility.
The hotel pageboy delivered the message to my room within minutes.
