flannel

verb
to wrap in flannel
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈflænəl / (AmE), / ˈflænəl / (BrE)

Examples

After his bath, he was flannelled and tucked into bed to keep warm.
During the cold winter, she flannelled her feet to avoid getting chilled.
