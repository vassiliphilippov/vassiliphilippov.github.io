Sagittarius the Archer

proper noun
ninth zodiac sign, the Archer
Pronunciation: / sæˌdʒɪˈtɛɹiəs ði ˈɑɹtʃɚ / (AmE), / sæˌdʒɪˈteəriəs ði ˈɑːtʃə / (BrE)
