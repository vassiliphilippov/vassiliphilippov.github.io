heartbeat

noun
regular movement of the heart
2.3 5.99/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆhÉ‘ËrtËŒbiËt / (AmE), / ËˆhÉ‘ËtËŒbiËt / (BrE)
the regular movement or sound of the heart as it pumps blood, especially as felt in the arteries

Examples

Fast heartbeat
After running up the stairs, he could feel his heartbeat pounding in his chest.
Her heartbeat quickened as she waited for the results.
The doctor listened carefully to the baby's heartbeat during the examination.

Synonyms

  • pulse
    2.3 13/m
    pulse is the beating felt in the wrist or neck, while heartbeat is the heart's regular movement or sound as it pumps blood.
  • pulsation
    5.6 0.14/m
    pulsation is a rhythmic throbbing of arteries, while heartbeat is the heart's movement or sound felt as it pumps blood.
  • heart rhythm
    3.0 0.53/m
    heart rhythm describes the pattern of the heart's beats over time, while heartbeat names each single movement or sound as it pumps.
  • beat
    1.0 20/m
    beat is a regular rhythm or pattern of sound or movement, while heartbeat is the heart's specific movement or sound as it pumps blood.
