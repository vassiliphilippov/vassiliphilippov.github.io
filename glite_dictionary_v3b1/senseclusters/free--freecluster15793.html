free

adjective
not held in slavery or servitude
2.0
Pronunciation: / friː / (AmE), / friː / (BrE)
(of a person) not held in slavery or servitude; having personal liberty.

Examples

A free man
After the Civil War, he was finally a free man.
Many nations fought for the right to make their citizens free.
She was born free and never experienced slavery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enslaved - being held as a slave or in servitude
  • synonym: liberated - having been set free, released from captivity
