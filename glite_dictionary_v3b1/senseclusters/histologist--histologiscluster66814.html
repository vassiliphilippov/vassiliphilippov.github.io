histologist

noun
expert in microscopic tissue study
Pronunciation: / hɪˈstɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist or medical specialist who studies the microscopic structure of animal tissues

Examples

Histologist studies tissues
As a histologist, she contributed vital insights on cell changes in cancerous tissues.
Medical schools often require aspiring histologists to master both practical and theoretical aspects of tissue analysis.
The histologist examined the stained tissue samples under the microscope for signs of disease.
