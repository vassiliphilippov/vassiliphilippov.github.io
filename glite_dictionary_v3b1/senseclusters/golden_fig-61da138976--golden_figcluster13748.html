golden fig

noun
a strangler tree of southern Florida and the West Indies
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən fɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən fɪɡ / (BrE)
a strangler tree native to southern Florida and the West Indies, starting life as an epiphyte and developing thick aerial roots that can cover large areas

Examples

The golden fig tree
Many animals depend on the golden fig for food and shelter in the forest.
