bar-lamb

noun
a gentle or overly kind person
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑr ˌlæm / (AmE), / ˈbɑː ˌlæm / (BrE)
a person who is especially gentle, kind, or soft-hearted, sometimes to a fault.

Examples

A bar-lamb person
Despite his size, he's really a bar-lamb with a heart of gold.
Everyone at the club calls him a bar-lamb because he can't say no to anyone.
She gets teased for being a bar-lamb whenever she forgives people too easily.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hard-nosed - practical and unsentimental, lacking in gentleness
  • synonym: softie - a person who is easily moved to pity or gentle in nature
