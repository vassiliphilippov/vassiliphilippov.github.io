sweat

verb
to excrete perspiration
Pronunciation: / swɛt / (AmE), / swɛt / (BrE)
to excrete perspiration through the pores in the skin, especially as a result of heat, physical exertion, or stress

Examples

To sweat in the sun
He began to sweat heavily after running up the stairs.
I always sweat when I'm nervous before a big presentation.
Some people sweat more than others during exercise.
