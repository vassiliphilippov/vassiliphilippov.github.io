instinctual

adjective
based on instinct
4.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstɪŋktʃuəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈstɪŋktʃuəl / (BrE)
relating to or resulting from instinct rather than conscious thought or reasoning

Examples

Instinctual response
His instinctual response was to protect his child from harm.
Many animals rely on instinctual behaviors to find food and shelter.
She made an instinctual decision without stopping to analyze the situation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deliberate - done consciously and intentionally
  • synonym: instinctive - done automatically or naturally, without thinking
