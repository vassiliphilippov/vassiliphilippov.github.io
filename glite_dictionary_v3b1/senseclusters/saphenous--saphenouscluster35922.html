saphenous

adjective
of leg veins
9.1
Pronunciation: / səˈfiːnəs / (AmE), / səˈfiːnəs / (BrE)
relating to either of the two large veins in the leg, especially the great or small saphenous vein

Examples

Saphenous vein
The doctor examined the patient's saphenous vein for signs of swelling.
A graft was taken from the saphenous vein during the heart surgery.
Ultrasound imaging revealed a blockage in the saphenous vein of her left leg.

Related Terms

  • synonym: femoral - relating to the thigh or femur
