dimly

adverb
in a faint or dim light
1.2 2.40/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆdÉªmli / (AmE), / ËˆdÉªmli / (BrE)
In a way that is only faintly or poorly illuminated; with a dim light.

Examples

Dimly lit room
The hallway was dimly lit by a single bulb.
They could dimly hear the sound of music from across the street.
Through the mist, she could dimly make out the outline of a house.
