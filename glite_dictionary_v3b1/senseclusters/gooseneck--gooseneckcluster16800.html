gooseneck

noun
curved tube or part resembling a goose's neck
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːsˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈɡuːs.nɛk / (BrE)
a slender, curved part or tube resembling the neck of a goose, especially as used in hardware, machinery, or plumbing

Examples

A gooseneck trailer
A gooseneck trailer is ideal for hauling heavy loads due to its stability.
He installed a gooseneck faucet in the kitchen for better reach over the sink.
The lamp's flexible gooseneck allowed her to direct the light exactly where she needed it.
