fast-paced lifestyle

noun
busy, high-speed way of living
4.7 3.5
Pronunciation: / fæst ˈpeɪst ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (AmE), / fɑːst ˈpeɪst ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
A way of living that involves constant activity, urgency, and little time for relaxation, often associated with urban environments.

Examples

A fast-paced lifestyle
After years of living a fast-paced lifestyle, she decided to move to the countryside for a change.
Many people are drawn to the excitement of a fast-paced lifestyle in big cities.
Some find the fast-paced lifestyle exhilarating, while others feel overwhelmed by its demands.
