exponentially

adverb
with rapidly increasing or decreasing rate
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌspoʊnənʃiˈæli / (AmE), / ɪkˌspəʊnənʃiˈæli / (BrE)
In a way that increases or decreases at a rate that becomes ever more rapid in proportion to the growing total number or size.

Examples

Increase exponentially
If data increases exponentially, it can quickly overwhelm traditional storage methods.
New discoveries are causing the database to expand exponentially each year.
The population of the city has grown exponentially over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exponential - rapidly increasing (adjective)
  • antonym: linearly - in a linear manner (at a constant rate)
