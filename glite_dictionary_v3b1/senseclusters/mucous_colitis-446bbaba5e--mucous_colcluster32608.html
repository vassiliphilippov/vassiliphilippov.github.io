mucous colitis

noun
recurrent abdominal pain and bowel disturbance
9.9 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmjuː.kəs kə.ˈlaɪ.tɪs/ (AmE), /ˈmjuː.kəs kə.ˈlaɪ.tɪs/ (BrE)
A medical condition marked by recurrent abdominal pain, diarrhea or constipation, often associated with emotional stress; now commonly referred to as irritable bowel syndrome.

Examples
