Examples

Synonyms

  • unhearable
    8.2 0.01/m
    unhearable is a direct, literal word saying a sound cannot be heard, while inaudible is the standard term used more often.
  • indiscernible
    8.8 0.21/m
    indiscernible is hard to notice by sight or sound and often means unclear, while inaudible refers only to things you cannot hear.
