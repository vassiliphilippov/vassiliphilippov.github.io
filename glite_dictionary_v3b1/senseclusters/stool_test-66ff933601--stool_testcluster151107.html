stool test

noun
medical analysis of a stool sample
3.5 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / stuÀêl t…õst / (AmE), / stuÀêl t…õst / (BrE)
a medical test that involves analyzing a sample of a person's stool to check for blood, infection, or other abnormalities, often used to help detect colorectal cancers or digestive disorders

Examples

A stool test sample
A stool test can help diagnose gastrointestinal infections or detect colon cancer early.
After the doctor recommended a stool test, she collected samples at home for laboratory analysis.
He was relieved when his stool test results came back negative for blood.
