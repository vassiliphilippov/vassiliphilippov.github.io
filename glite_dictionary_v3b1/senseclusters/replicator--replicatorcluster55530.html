replicator

noun
copy maker
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈre.plɪ.keɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈre.plɪ.keɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person or device that makes accurate copies or reproductions of objects, artworks, or materials

Examples

A skilled replicator
The skilled replicator created an exact copy of the ancient vase for the museum display.
A modern replicator can produce detailed models using advanced 3D printing technology.
The artist worked as a replicator, reproducing famous paintings for educational purposes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: copyist - a person who makes copies of documents or artworks
  • synonym: imitator - a person who copies the behavior or work of others
  • antonym: original - the first or earliest form from which copies are made
