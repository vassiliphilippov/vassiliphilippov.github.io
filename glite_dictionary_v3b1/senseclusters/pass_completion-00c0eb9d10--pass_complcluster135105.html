pass completion

noun
successful forward pass (American football)
9.6
Pronunciation: / pæs kəmˈpliːʃən / (AmE), / pɑːs kəmˈpliːʃən / (BrE)
(in American football) a successful forward pass where the ball is caught by a teammate.

Examples

A pass completion in football
A well-timed pass completion can completely change the momentum of a football match.
His first pass completion resulted in a twenty-yard gain for the team.
The quarterback had a record number of pass completions in last night’s game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: completion - the act of finishing or completing something, or (in football) a completed forward pass
  • antonym: incompletion - an unsuccessful forward pass where the ball is not caught (American football)
