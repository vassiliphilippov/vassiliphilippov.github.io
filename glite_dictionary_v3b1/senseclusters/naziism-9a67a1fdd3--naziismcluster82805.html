Naziism

noun
the ideology of the Nazi Party
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːtsiˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈnɑːtsiˌɪzəm / (BrE)
The ideology and policies associated with the Nazi Party, characterized by dictatorial power, nationalism, racism, and suppression of opposition.

Examples

Naziism ideology
She studied the impact of Naziism on European political thought.
The museum exhibit explores the origins and consequences of Naziism.
The rise of Naziism in Germany led to profound changes in world history.

Related Terms

  • antonym: communism - an ideology advocating classless society and collective ownership
  • synonym: fascism - an authoritarian and nationalistic right-wing system of government and social organization
