Pinus densiflora

noun
Japanese red pine species
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪ.nəs ˌdɛn.sɪˈflɔːrə / (AmE), / ˌpaɪ.nəs ˌdɛn.sɪˈflɔː.rə / (BrE)
a species of pine tree native to Japan and Korea, often grown as an ornamental, with a wide-spreading, irregular crown when mature
