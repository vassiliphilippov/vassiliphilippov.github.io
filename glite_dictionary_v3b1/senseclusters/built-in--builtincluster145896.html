Synonyms

  • inbuilt
    4.2 0.09/m
    inbuilt often describes something made as part of a design, while built-in means included as a permanent feature.
  • embedded
    4.4 0.00/m
    embedded is fixed or set deeply inside a larger thing, while built-in means included as an original, permanent part.
  • integral
    5.3 3/m
    integral is necessary and important to make a whole work, while built-in describes a feature already included.
  • inherent
    5.1 7/m
    inherent is a natural quality or trait inside something, while built-in refers to a feature included as part of its design.
