A substance or agent that kills or inhibits the growth of microorganisms, especially those that cause disease.

Examples

An antimicrobial agent
Proper use of antimicrobial agents can help prevent the spread of infections in hospitals.
Scientists are researching new natural antimicrobials to combat drug-resistant pathogens.
This spray contains a powerful antimicrobial that kills bacteria on contact.

Synonyms

  • antimicrobic
    6.9 0.00/m
    antimicrobic names agents like heat, chemicals, or radiation that destroy microbes, while antimicrobial is the general term for such substances.
  • germicide
    7.1 0.01/m
    germicide destroys harmful microbes, often by killing them, while antimicrobial also includes agents that merely stop microbe growth.
  • microbicide
    7.2 0.06/m
    microbicide kills or stops microbes and is often used to prevent infection, while antimicrobial covers any agent that kills or inhibits microorganisms.

Related Terms
