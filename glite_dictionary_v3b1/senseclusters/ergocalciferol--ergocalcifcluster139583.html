ergocalciferol

noun
a form of vitamin D (D2)
8.5 7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɜrɡoʊˌkælsɪˈfɛrɔl / (AmE), / ˌɜːɡəʊˌkælsɪˈfɪərɒl / (BrE)
a fat-soluble vitamin (vitamin D2) used to prevent or treat vitamin D deficiency and rickets

Examples

Ergocalciferol vitamin D2
Children who lack exposure to sunlight are at risk of rickets unless they receive ergocalciferol or other sources of vitamin D.
Doctors sometimes prescribe ergocalciferol to treat patients with severe vitamin D deficiency.
Foods such as fortified cereals may be supplemented with ergocalciferol.
