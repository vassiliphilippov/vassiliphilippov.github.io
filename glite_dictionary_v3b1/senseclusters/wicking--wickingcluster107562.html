wicking

verb
to draw moisture away
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
to draw liquid or moisture through or away from a material by capillary action

Examples

Wicking sweat away
The new athletic shirt is designed for wicking sweat away from your skin during exercise.
Engineers tested materials capable of wicking moisture to keep electronics dry.
Absorbent pads are wicking the spilled oil from the surface efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absorb - to take in or soak up liquid
  • antonym: repel - to drive or force something away
