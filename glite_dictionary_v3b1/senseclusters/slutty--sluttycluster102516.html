slutty

adjective
offensive, sexually provocative
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌti / (AmE), / ˈslʌti / (BrE)
offensive term describing someone, especially a woman, as sexually provocative or promiscuous in a negative way

Examples

Slutty character
The movie was criticized for its portrayal of a slutty character.
Some people use the word slutty in a judgmental and hurtful way.
She disliked being called slutty because of her choice of clothing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modest - behaving in a way that does not attract sexual attention
  • synonym: promiscuous - having or showing many sexual partners
