toil

noun
hard, continuous physical work
Pronunciation: / tɔɪl / (AmE), / tɔɪl / (BrE)
Hard and continuous work, especially physically demanding or laborious work.

Examples

Years of toil
After years of toil in the fields, he finally retired.
Her hands were roughened by a lifetime of manual toil.
The project was completed after months of relentless toil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: labor - work, especially physical work for wages
  • antonym: leisure - free time when not working
