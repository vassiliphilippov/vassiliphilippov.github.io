Sufism

noun
Islamic mysticism
Pronunciation: / ˈsuː.fɪ.zəm / (AmE), / ˈsuː.fɪ.zəm / (BrE)
A form of Islamic mysticism which emphasizes inward search for God and spiritual closeness to the divine.

Examples

Sufism spiritual path
He spent years studying Sufism to better understand its philosophical teachings.
Many Sufism practitioners devote themselves to meditation and poetry.
The influence of Sufism can be seen in the works of Rumi.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mysticism - belief in the possibility of attaining union with the divine through contemplation and self-surrender
