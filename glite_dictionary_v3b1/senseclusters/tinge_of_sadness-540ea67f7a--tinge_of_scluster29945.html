tinge of sadness

noun
a slight trace of sadness
Pronunciation: / tɪndʒ əv ˈsædnəs / (AmE), / tɪndʒ əv ˈsædnɪs / (BrE)
A small or slight amount of sadness; a faint feeling of sorrow.

Examples

A tinge of sadness appears
Every autumn, a tinge of sadness comes over her as the days grow shorter.
He smiled, but I could see a tinge of sadness in his eyes.
There was a tinge of sadness in her voice as she spoke about her childhood.
