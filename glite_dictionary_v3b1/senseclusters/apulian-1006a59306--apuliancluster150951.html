Apulian

noun
person from Apulia
7.9
Pronunciation: / əˈpuːliən / (AmE), / əˈpjuːliən / (BrE)
a person who is from or lives in Apulia, a region in southern Italy

Examples

An Apulian person

Many Apulians celebrate traditional festivals unique to their region.
The chef, a proud Apulian, shared recipes from his hometown in southern Italy.
