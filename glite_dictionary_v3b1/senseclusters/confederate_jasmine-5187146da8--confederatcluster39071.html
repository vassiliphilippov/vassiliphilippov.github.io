confederate jasmine

noun
evergreen Chinese climber with fragrant white flowers
8.4
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɛdərət ˈdʒæzmin / (AmE), / kənˈfɛd(ə)rət ˈdʒazmɪn / (BrE)
an evergreen climbing plant native to China, with shiny dark green leaves and highly fragrant white flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental.

Examples

Confederate jasmine flowers
A trellis covered in confederate jasmine filled the garden with a sweet, intoxicating scent.
The delicate, star-shaped blooms of confederate jasmine are most noticeable in late spring.
They planted confederate jasmine along the fence for its lush greenery and beautiful flowers.
