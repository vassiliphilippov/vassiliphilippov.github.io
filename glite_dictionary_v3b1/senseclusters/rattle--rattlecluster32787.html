rattle

noun
horny segments at end of rattlesnake's tail
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrætl̩ /; / ˈræɾl̩ / (AmE), / ˈræt.əl /; / ˈrætl /; / ˈrætl̩ / (BrE)
