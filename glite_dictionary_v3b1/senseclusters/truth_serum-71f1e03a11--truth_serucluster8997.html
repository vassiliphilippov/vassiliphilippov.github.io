truth serum

noun
drug believed to induce truth-telling
7.4 5.4
Pronunciation: /truːθ ˈsɪ.rəm/ (AmE), /truːθ ˈsɪə.rəm/ /; / /truːθ ˈsiː.rəm/ (BrE)
