in-joke

noun
joke only insiders understand

Pronunciation: /ˈɪn.dʒoʊk/ /; / /ˈən.dʒoʊk/ (AmE), /ˈɪn.dʒəʊk/ /; / /ˈən.dʒəʊk/ (BrE)
