in-joke

noun
joke only insiders understand
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌdʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈɪn.dʒəʊk / (BrE)
A joke that is understood and appreciated only by members of a particular group because of shared knowledge or experiences.

Examples

An in-joke between friends
He explained the story behind the in-joke, making everyone else laugh too.
The team members laughed at the in-joke that no one else in the office understood.
Their podcast is full of in-jokes that fly over new listeners' heads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inside joke - A joke understood only by a restricted group sharing special knowledge
