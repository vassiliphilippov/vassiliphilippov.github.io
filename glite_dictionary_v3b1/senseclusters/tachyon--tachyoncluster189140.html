tachyon

noun
very fast person or thing
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtækiˌɑn / (AmE), / ˈtækiɒn / (BrE)
an informal term for a person or thing that moves extremely quickly

Examples

She is a tachyon
On the track, she moved like a tachyon, leaving all the other sprinters far behind.
He finished the assignment so quickly that his coworkers called him a tachyon.
The new delivery service is a real tachyon compared to the old one.

Related Terms

  • synonym: speedster - a person or thing that moves very fast
