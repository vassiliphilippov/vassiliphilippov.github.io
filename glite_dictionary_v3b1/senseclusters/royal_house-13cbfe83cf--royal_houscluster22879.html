royal house

noun
dynastic royal family
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔɪ.əl haʊs / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪ.əl haʊs / (BrE)
a family or dynasty of royal persons who rule or have ruled a country collectively

Examples

European royal houses
Representatives from several European royal houses attended the state funeral.
The British royal house has had a profound influence on the nation's history.
The ancient royal house traces its ancestry back over a thousand years.
