photochemical

adjective
relating to chemical effects of light
6.4 6.3
Pronunciation: /foʊ.toʊ.ˈke.mɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /fəʊ.təʊ.ˈke.mɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
Relating to or produced by the chemical effects of light, especially as they affect chemical reactions.

Examples

A photochemical process
A photochemical process was used to develop the film in the laboratory.
Scientists studied the photochemical changes that occur when sunlight interacts with certain pollutants.
The photochemical reactions in the atmosphere can lead to the formation of smog.
