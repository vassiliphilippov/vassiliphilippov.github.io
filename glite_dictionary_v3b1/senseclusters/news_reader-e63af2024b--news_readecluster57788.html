news reader

noun
software that fetches and reads news
3.4 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / c8nuz ccrid0d59r / (AmE), / c8njud0z ccrid0d59 / (BrE)
A software program, app, or device that automatically fetches or aggregates news from multiple sources and displays or reads it aloud to users.

Examples

Mobile news reader
The car's dashboard news reader streams headlines aloud as the driver commutes.
She installed a news reader app to listen to morning headlines while making coffee.
Elderly users appreciated that the news reader could read long articles clearly and slowly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: RSS reader - an application that fetches and displays news feed items using RSS or similar protocols
  • synonym: news aggregator - a service or app that collects news stories from many sources in one place
