recommendation

noun
a suggested course of action
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛk.ə.mɛn.dəˈʃɛn /; / ˌrɛkəmɛnˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛk.ə.mɛn.dəˈʃən /; / ˌrɛkəmɛnˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
a suggestion or statement of approval about what someone should do or which person or thing is suitable

Examples

Doctor's recommendation
Following your doctor's recommendation, she started a new exercise program.
He ignored my recommendation and chose a different approach.
The committee's recommendation was to approve the project with minor changes.
Can you give me a recommendation for a good Italian restaurant nearby?
Her boss wrote a glowing recommendation for her job application.
The committee followed the recommendation to hire the most experienced candidate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advice - guidance or recommendations concerning prudent action
  • antonym: criticism - the expression of disapproval or negative judgment
  • synonym: endorsement - an expression of support or approval
  • synonym: suggestion - an idea or plan put forward for consideration
