multiway

noun
multiple-socket adapter
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌweɪ / (BrE)
an electrical adapter or extension lead that allows several devices to be powered from a single wall socket

Examples

Multiway adapter
He plugged his laptop and phone charger into the multiway to use both at the same time.
The office uses a multiway to connect several computers to one power outlet.
Make sure the multiway is not overloaded with too many devices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extension lead - a length of cable with a plug at one end and one or more sockets at the other
  • synonym: power strip - a device with multiple electrical sockets for plugging in several devices
