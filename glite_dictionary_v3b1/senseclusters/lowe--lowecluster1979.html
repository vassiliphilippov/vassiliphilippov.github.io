lowe

noun
flame or glow
9.9
Pronunciation: / loʊ / (AmE), / ləʊ / (BrE)
a flame or bright glow, especially from a fire; chiefly Scots or archaic English

Examples

A warm lowe
The gentle lowe of the lantern guided them through the dark passage.
She watched the lowe flicker and dance in the fireplace on a cold evening.
Legends spoke of a magical lowe that never faded atop the ancient hill.
