rug monkey

noun
person with messy hair or socially awkward
Pronunciation: / rʌɡ ˈmʌŋki / (AmE), / rʌɡ ˈmʌŋki / (BrE)
(slang, derogatory) A person with messy hair or who is perceived as socially awkward.

Examples

A rug monkey at school
After running in the rain, he looked like a total rug monkey at school.
At the party, he felt like a rug monkey compared to everyone else.
She called her brother a rug monkey when his hair stuck up everywhere.
