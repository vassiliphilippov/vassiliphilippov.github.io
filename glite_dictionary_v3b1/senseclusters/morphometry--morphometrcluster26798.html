morphometry

noun
measurement of shapes
Pronunciation: / mɔrˈfɑːmətri / (AmE), / mɔːˈfɒmɪtri / (BrE)
the measurement and analysis of the shape, size, or dimensions of organisms or structures

Examples

Morphometry of bones
Researchers used morphometry to analyze the differences in bone structure between the two populations.
Advances in imaging technology have improved the accuracy of morphometry in medical studies.
The museum's collection includes fossils that have been studied using morphometry to determine their species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biometry - the statistical analysis of biological data
  • synonym: geometry - the branch of mathematics dealing with shapes and their properties
