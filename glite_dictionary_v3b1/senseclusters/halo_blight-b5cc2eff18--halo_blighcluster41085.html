halo blight

noun
disease of bean plants
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪloʊ ˌblaɪt /; / ˈheɪloʊ blaɪt / (AmE), / ˈheɪləʊ ˌblaɪt /; / ˈheɪləʊ blaɪt / (BrE)
a bacterial disease of plants that causes spots with yellow or light-colored halos on the leaves
