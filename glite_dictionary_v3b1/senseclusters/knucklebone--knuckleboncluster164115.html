knucklebone

noun
bone used in games
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌkəlˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈnʌkəlbəʊn / (BrE)
a small bone from a joint (especially the ankle bone of an animal), often used in traditional games or as a die

Examples

The museum displayed a collection of carved knucklebones from different cultures.
