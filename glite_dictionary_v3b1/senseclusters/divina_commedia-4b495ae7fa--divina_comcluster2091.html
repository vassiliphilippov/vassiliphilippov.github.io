Divina Commedia

proper noun
Dante's epic poem describing a journey through the afterlife
Pronunciation: / diˌviːnə kämˈmeːdia / (AmE), / diˌviːnə kɒmˈmeːdɪə / (BrE)
the narrative epic poem by Dante Alighieri, regarded as one of the greatest works of world literature, describing the poet's journey through Hell, Purgatory, and Paradise.
