galena

noun
a lead sulfide mineral; main lead ore
9.4
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈliːnə / (AmE), / ɡəˈliːnə / (BrE)
A soft, blue-grey mineral consisting of lead sulfide, which is the chief ore of lead.

Examples

A galena mineral
A sample of galena is often included in geology collections because of its metallic luster.
The abundance of galena in the region made it a hub for the lead mining industry.
The miners extracted galena from deep beneath the earth to obtain lead.
