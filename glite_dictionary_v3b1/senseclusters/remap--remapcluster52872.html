remap

noun
engine software adjustment
Pronunciation: / ˈriːmæp / (AmE), / ˈriːmæp / (BrE)
an adjustment to a vehicle's engine control unit (ECU) software to change its performance, fuel efficiency, or emissions

Examples

Engine remap
He scheduled a remap to boost his car's acceleration and overall performance.
After the remap, the vehicle used less fuel on long journeys.
Many car enthusiasts opt for a remap to customize their driving experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: modification - a change made to improve or alter something
  • synonym: tune-up - routine maintenance or adjustment to improve a vehicle's performance
