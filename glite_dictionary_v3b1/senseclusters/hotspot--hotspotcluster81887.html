hotspot

noun
public Wi‑Fi access point
3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑːt.spɑːt/ (AmE), /ˈhɒt.spɒt/ (BrE)

Examples

Wi-Fi hotspot

Local councils are installing public hotspots in parks to support remote workers and tourists.
