raise one's hands in victory

verb
raise arms as a sign of triumph
1.2
Pronunciation: / reɪz wʌnz hændz ɪn ˈvɪktəri / (AmE), / reɪz wʌnz hændz ɪn ˈvɪktəri / (BrE)
to lift both arms above one's head as a sign of triumph or celebration upon achieving success

Examples

To raise one's hands in victory proudly
He smiled and raised his hands in victory as the crowd cheered him on.
She couldn't help but raise her hands in victory when she finished the marathon first.
The entire team raised their hands in victory after winning the championship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celebrate - to show happiness about a success or special event
  • antonym: surrender - to give up; opposite gesture of raising hands to indicate triumph
