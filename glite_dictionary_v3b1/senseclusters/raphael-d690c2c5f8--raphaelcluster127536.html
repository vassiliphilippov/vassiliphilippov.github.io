Raphael

proper noun
Italian High Renaissance painter
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈræ.feɪ.əl/ /; / /ˈræ.fɪ.əl/ /; / /rə.ˈfaɪ.əl/ (AmE), /ˈræ.feɪ.əl/ /; / /ˈræ.fɪ.əl/ /; / /rə.ˈfaɪ.əl/ (BrE)
(1483–1520) An Italian painter and architect of the High Renaissance, renowned for his clarity of form and composition in paintings such as 'The School of Athens'.

Examples

Raphael the painter

Many admirers traveled to Florence to study the techniques of Raphael firsthand.
The Vatican commissioned Raphael to decorate several rooms with his masterpieces.
