Macroclemys temmincki

noun
large snapping turtle species (alligator snapping turtle)
Pronunciation: / ˌmækroʊˈklɛmɪs tɛmˈmɪnki / (AmE), / ˌmakrəʊˈklɛmɪs tɛmˈmɪŋki / (BrE)
A large freshwater turtle species with three ridges on its back, native to the southeastern United States; also known as the alligator snapping turtle.
