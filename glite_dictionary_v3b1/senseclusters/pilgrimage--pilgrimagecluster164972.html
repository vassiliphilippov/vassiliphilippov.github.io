pilgrimage

noun
journey to a sacred place
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlɡrɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɪlɡrɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
a journey to a sacred place, often for religious reasons

Examples

A pilgrimage to Mecca
Every year, thousands make the pilgrimage to Mecca as part of their faith.
Her journey to the holy city was a spiritual pilgrimage she will never forget.
The ancient site remains a destination for pilgrimage by people of many religions.
