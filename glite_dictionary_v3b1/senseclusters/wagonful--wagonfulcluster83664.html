wagonful

noun
amount filling a wagon
Pronunciation: / ˈwæɡənˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈwæɡənˌfʊl / (BrE)
an amount that is enough to fill a wagon

Examples

A wagonful of apples
The farmers delivered a wagonful of apples to the market early in the morning.
After the harvest, they collected a wagonful of pumpkins from the field.
He donated a wagonful of firewood to the local community center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cartload - an amount that fills a cart
  • synonym: truckload - an amount that fills a truck
