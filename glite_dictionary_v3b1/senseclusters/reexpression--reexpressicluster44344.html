reexpression

noun
revision of financial figures
8.9
Pronunciation: /riː.ɪk.ˈspreʃ.ən/ /; / /rə.ɪk.ˈspreʃ.ən/ (AmE), /riː.ɪk.ˈspreʃ.ən/ /; / /rɪ.ɪk.ˈspreʃ.ən/ (BrE)
the act of revising and presenting financial statements or figures again according to a different accounting basis, policy, or currency

Examples

Reexpression of accounts
The auditor required a reexpression of the company's financial statements to comply with new regulations.
After the currency changed, the firm performed a reexpression of its annual accounts.
A reexpression was necessary to reflect the updated accounting policies in the report.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjustment - a small change made to financial figures or statements
  • synonym: restatement - the presentation of financial statements again, often to correct errors or update information
