queen of the night

noun
tropical cactus with night-blooming flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌkwiːn əv ðə ˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ˌkwiːn əv ðə ˈnaɪt / (BrE)
A tropical American climbing cactus, especially Selenicereus or Epiphyllum species, cultivated for its large, fragrant, night-blooming flowers and colorful fruit.
