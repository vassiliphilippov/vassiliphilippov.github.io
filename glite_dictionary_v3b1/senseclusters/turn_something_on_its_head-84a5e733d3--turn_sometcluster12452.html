turn (something) on its head

verb
completely change understanding or perspective
Pronunciation: / tɝn ɒn ɪts hɛd / (AmE), / tɜːn ɒn ɪts hɛd / (BrE)
to completely change the way something is understood, regarded, or approached, often by introducing a new perspective or argument

Examples

To turn the debate on its head
Her latest research turned the debate on its head by challenging long-held assumptions.
The discovery turned the debate on its head, forcing everyone to reconsider their views.
When the expert spoke, he turned the debate on its head with a fresh perspective.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reverse - to cause something to move in the opposite direction or have an opposite effect
  • synonym: upend - to turn upside down or overturn
