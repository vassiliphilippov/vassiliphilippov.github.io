community theater

noun
local amateur theatrical productions
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunəti ˈθiətər / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪti ˈθɪətə / (BrE)
theatrical productions performed by local amateur actors, typically for or by members of the community

Examples

Community theater productions
Many aspiring actors gain experience through community theater productions.
Our town's community theater is staging a new play this month.
She got her start performing in community theater before moving to New York.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amateur theater - theatrical performances by non-professional actors
  • antonym: professional theater - theatrical productions by trained, paid actors
