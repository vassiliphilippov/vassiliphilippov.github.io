firebombing

noun
attack with fire bombs
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌbɑːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəbɒmɪŋ / (BrE)
an attack in which fire bombs are used to cause widespread destruction, especially to buildings or cities

Examples

The firebombing attack
The firebombing of the city during the war destroyed thousands of homes.
Many historical buildings were lost in the firebombing that lasted through the night.
Survivors of the firebombing shared their stories with the next generation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: air raid - an attack by aircraft, especially on a city
  • synonym: bombing - an attack using bombs
