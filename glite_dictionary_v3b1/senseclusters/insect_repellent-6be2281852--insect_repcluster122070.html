insect repellent

noun
chemical to keep insects away
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌsɛkt rɪˈpɛl.ənt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.sɛkt rɪˈpel.ənt / (BrE)
A chemical substance applied to skin, clothing, or other surfaces to repel insects.

Examples

Apply insect repellent
Apply insect repellent before hiking in areas where mosquitoes are common.
She always keeps a bottle of insect repellent in her picnic basket.
Some insect repellents contain natural oils instead of synthetic chemicals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bug spray - A spray designed to repel or kill insects
  • antonym: insecticide - A substance used to kill insects
