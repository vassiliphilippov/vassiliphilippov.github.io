fluorescent

noun
lighting fixture with a fluorescent lamp
3.5
Pronunciation: / fluˈɔːrəsɛnt / (AmE), / flʊəˈrɛsənt / (BrE)
a lighting fixture that uses a fluorescent lamp, typically producing bright, energy-efficient light for indoor spaces

Examples

A fluorescent light
He prefers working under a fluorescent because it gives off less heat than incandescent lights.
Maintenance crews replaced the old fluorescent in the hallway with an LED fixture.
The office's fluorescent buzzed faintly throughout the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fluorescent lamp - an electric lamp that produces light by fluorescence
  • antonym: incandescent - an electric light using a filament that glows when heated
