mountain mint

noun
aromatic herb of genus Pycnanthemum
4.9 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmaʊn.tən mɪnt/ /; / /ˈmaʊn.tɪn mɪnt/ (AmE), /ˈmaʊn.tɪn mɪnt/ /; / /ˈmaʊn.tən mɪnt/ (BrE)
any of a group of perennial herbs of the genus Pycnanthemum, native to eastern North America and California, known for their aromatic leaves and resemblance to true mint.
