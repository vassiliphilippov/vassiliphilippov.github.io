non-skier

noun
person who does not ski
4.0
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈskiːər / (AmE), / nɒnˈskiːə / (BrE)
a person who does not ski or does not participate in skiing activities

Examples

A non-skier guest
The mountain resort has plenty of options for non-skiers who want to enjoy the snow.
As a non-skier, she preferred to relax by the fireplace while her friends hit the slopes.
Special tours are organized for non-skiers to explore the winter landscape.
