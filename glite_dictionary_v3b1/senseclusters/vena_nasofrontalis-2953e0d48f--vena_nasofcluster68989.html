vena nasofrontalis

noun
a vein in the anterior part of the eye socket
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə neɪ.sə.frʌn.ˈtæl.ɪs/ /; / /ˈviː.nə neɪ.soʊ.frʌn.ˈtæl.ɪs/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə neɪ.sə.frɒn.ˈtæl.ɪs/ /; / /ˈviː.nə neɪ.səʊ.frɒn.ˈtæl.ɪs/ (BrE)
a vein located in the front-middle part of the eye socket, connecting the superior ophthalmic vein with the angular vein

Examples

The vena nasofrontalis vein
An obstruction of the vena nasofrontalis can lead to complications involving the eye and surrounding tissues.

The vena nasofrontalis plays a crucial role in venous drainage of the orbit.
