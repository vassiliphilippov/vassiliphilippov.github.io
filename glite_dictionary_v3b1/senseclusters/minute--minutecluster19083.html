minute

noun
1⁄60 of a degree (angle)
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
A unit of angular measurement equal to one sixtieth (1⁄60) of a degree.

Examples

Minute of angle
Latitude and longitude are often given in degrees, minutes, and seconds.
One minute of arc is a very small angle in the sky.
The astronomer measured the star's position to the nearest arc minute.
