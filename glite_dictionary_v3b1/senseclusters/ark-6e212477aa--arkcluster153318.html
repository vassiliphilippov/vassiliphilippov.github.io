Ark

noun
sacred chest for the Ten Commandments
8.2 0.59/m
Pronunciation: / …ërk / (AmE), / …ëÀêk / (BrE)
(in Judaism) the sacred chest that contained the two tablets of the Ten Commandments, kept by the ancient Hebrews.

Examples

The Ark of the Covenant
Scholars have debated the historical fate of the Ark for centuries.
The Ark was said to be kept in the innermost part of the Tabernacle.
The ancient Hebrews carried the Ark through the desert as a symbol of God's presence.
