good-temperedness

noun
cheerful, friendly disposition
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd ˈtɛmpɚdnəs / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈtɛmpədnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being cheerful, friendly, and easy to get along with.

Examples

His good-temperedness shines
Despite the stressful situation, his good-temperedness never faltered.
Her good-temperedness made her popular among her colleagues.
The teacher's good-temperedness created a relaxed and encouraging classroom environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: good-naturedness - the state of having a pleasant, amiable disposition
  • antonym: ill-temperedness - the quality of being irritable or easily angered
