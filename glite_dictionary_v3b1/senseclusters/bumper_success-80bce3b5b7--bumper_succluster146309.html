bumper success

noun
exceptionally large or successful outcome
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌmpər səkˈsɛs / (AmE), / ˈbʌmpə səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
an exceptionally large or successful outcome, especially for an event, business, or harvest

Examples

A bumper success story
Her first novel proved to be a bumper success, selling thousands of copies in the first month.
Thanks to perfect weather and careful planning, the harvest was a bumper success this year.
The festival was a bumper success, attracting record crowds from around the region.
