Swiss roll

noun
sponge cake rolled with filling
Pronunciation: / swɪs roʊl / (AmE), / swɪs rəʊl / (BrE)
A thin sheet of sponge cake spread with jam or cream and rolled into a cylindrical shape.

Examples

A Swiss roll cake
I watched a video on how to bake a perfect Swiss roll without cracking the cake.
She served a homemade Swiss roll filled with raspberry jam for dessert.
The bakery displayed several kinds of Swiss roll in the window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jelly roll - a cake similar to Swiss roll, often filled with jelly
