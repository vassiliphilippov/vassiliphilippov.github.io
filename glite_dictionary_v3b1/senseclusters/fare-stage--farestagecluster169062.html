fare-stage

noun
bus route section with fixed fare
5.3 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfeər.steɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈfer.steɪdʒ/ (AmE), /ˈfeə.steɪdʒ/ (BrE)
a section of a bus route for which the fare remains the same, regardless of where travelers get on or off within that section

Examples

A fare-stage section
Passengers should check which fare-stage they are boarding from to pay the correct amount.
The bus fare increases at each fare-stage along the route.
The city introduced a new fare-stage system to simplify ticket pricing.
