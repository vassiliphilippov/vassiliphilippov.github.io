hyperdactyly

noun
having extra fingers or toes
7.0 9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈdæktɪli / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəˈdaktɪli / (BrE)
A medical condition in which a person is born with more than the usual number of fingers or toes.

Examples

Born with hyperdactyly
She was born with hyperdactyly, having six toes on one foot.
Surgical intervention may be recommended in cases of hyperdactyly to improve function.
The doctor explained that hyperdactyly is a rare genetic condition involving extra fingers or toes.
