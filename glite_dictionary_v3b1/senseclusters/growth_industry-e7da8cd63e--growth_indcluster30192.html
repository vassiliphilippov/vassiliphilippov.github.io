growth industry

noun
industry experiencing rapid expansion
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊθ ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / ɡrəʊθ ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
an industry or sector that is expanding rapidly and shows potential for further development and profit

Examples

A growth industry sector
Healthcare is often cited as a major growth industry due to aging populations in many countries.
Information technology became a growth industry in the late 20th century, transforming economies around the world.
The renewable energy sector is considered a growth industry with vast potential for investment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: declining industry - an industry experiencing diminishing growth or contraction
  • synonym: sunrise industry - a new and fast-growing industry, often based on new technology
