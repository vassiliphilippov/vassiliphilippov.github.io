guide word

noun
word at top of dictionary page
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪd ˌwɝd / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪd ˌwɜːd / (BrE)
A word printed at the top of a dictionary or reference book page to indicate the first or last entry on that page.

Examples

A guide word in dictionary
Each page of the handbook included a guide word for quick reference.
She checked the guide word to see if her word appeared on that dictionary page.
The guide word at the top of the page made it easy to locate the right entry.
