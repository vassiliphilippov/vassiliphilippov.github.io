sunscreening

noun
sunlight-blocking material
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌskriːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌskriːnɪŋ / (BrE)
a material, film, or device used to block or reduce sunlight entering buildings or vehicles

Examples

Sunscreening film on windows
The office installed sunscreening to make the workspace cooler and more comfortable.
Modern cars often come with built-in sunscreening to protect passengers from harsh sunlight.
They chose a transparent sunscreening film that blocks UV rays but lets in natural light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curtain - a piece of cloth used to cover a window and block light
  • synonym: window tinting - a film applied to windows to reduce sunlight and glare
