pricelessness

noun
quality of being beyond value
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪsləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈpraɪslɪsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely valuable and impossible to measure in terms of money

Examples

The pricelessness of art
Art lovers often discuss the pricelessness of masterpieces like the Mona Lisa.
He reflected on the pricelessness of memories made with old friends.
The pricelessness of her grandmother's wedding ring made it a treasured family heirloom.
