genus Anser

noun
genus of typical geese
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈænsər / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈænsə / (BrE)
The genus of typical geese in the Anatidae family, which includes species such as the greylag goose and the pink-footed goose.
