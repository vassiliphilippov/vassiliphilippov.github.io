lanky frame

noun
tall and thin body structure
Pronunciation: / ˈlæŋki freɪm / (AmE), / ˈlæŋki freɪm / (BrE)
A tall and thin body structure, often appearing somewhat awkward or loosely built.

Examples

A lanky frame
Despite his lanky frame, he was surprisingly strong and agile.
His lanky frame made it easy for him to reach the top shelf.
She was self-conscious about her lanky frame during her teenage years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slender - thin or gracefully slim
  • antonym: stocky - broad and sturdily built
