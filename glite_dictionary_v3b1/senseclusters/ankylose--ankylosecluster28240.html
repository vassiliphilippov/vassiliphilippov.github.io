ankylose

verb
cause fusion of a joint by surgery
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋ.kəˌloʊz / (AmE), / ˈaŋ.kɪ.ləʊz / (BrE)
to cause ankylosis, especially by surgical means; to stiffen a joint by causing its bones to fuse

Examples

To ankylose a joint
After the infection, the only option was to ankylose the knee surgically.
In rare cases, doctors intentionally ankylose a joint to relieve severe chronic pain.
The surgeon had to ankylose the damaged joint to restore the patient's mobility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ankylosis - stiffening or fusion of a joint
