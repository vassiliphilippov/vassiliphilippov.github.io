unhinged

adjective
mentally unbalanced; insane
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɪndʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɪndʒd / (BrE)
mentally unbalanced; affected with madness or insanity

Examples

Mentally unhinged person
After the stressful meeting, she seemed almost unhinged by the pressure.
Some said his erratic behavior made him appear unhinged to everyone around him.
The movie's villain is portrayed as a completely unhinged individual.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insane - mentally ill or wildly foolish
  • antonym: sane - of sound mind; not mentally ill
