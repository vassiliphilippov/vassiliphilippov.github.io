obliging

adjective
helpful and willing to do favors
5.6
Pronunciation: / əˈblaɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈblaɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
willing and eager to help or do favors for others; helpful and accommodating.

Examples

An obliging person
He is always obliging when someone needs assistance at work.
She has such an obliging nature that everyone enjoys her company.
The obliging staff made our stay at the hotel very pleasant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: helpful - giving or ready to give help
  • antonym: uncooperative - not willing to help or do favors
