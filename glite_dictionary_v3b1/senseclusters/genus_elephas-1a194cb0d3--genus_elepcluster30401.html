genus Elephas

noun
the main genus of elephants
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ɪˈliːfəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ɪˈliːfəs / (BrE)
the type genus of the family Elephantidae, including Asian elephants and related extinct species

Examples

Genus Elephas species
Modern zoology classifies the Asian elephant under the genus Elephas.
Paleontologists discovered fossils belonging to the genus Elephas in the ancient riverbeds.
The genus Elephas includes the modern Asian elephant and several extinct species.
