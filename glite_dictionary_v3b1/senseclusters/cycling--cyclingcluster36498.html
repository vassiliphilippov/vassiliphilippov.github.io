cycling

noun
sport of riding bicycles or motorcycles
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪklɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport or activity of riding a bicycle or motorcycle, especially as a form of exercise or competition.

Examples

Cycling to work
He prefers cycling to work instead of driving.
Professional cycling events attract large crowds throughout Europe.
She took up cycling to improve her fitness and enjoy the outdoors.
