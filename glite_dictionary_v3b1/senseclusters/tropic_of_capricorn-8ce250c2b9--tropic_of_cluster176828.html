Tropic of Capricorn

noun
latitude line 23° south of the equator
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑːpɪk əv ˈkæprɪkɔrn / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪk əv ˈkæprɪkɔːn / (BrE)
the imaginary line of latitude approximately 23°26' south of the equator, marking the southern boundary of the tropics

Examples

The Tropic of Capricorn line
Australia's climate is influenced in part by its position north of the Tropic of Capricorn.
In December, the sun is directly overhead at the Tropic of Capricorn.
The Tropic of Capricorn passes through several countries in the southern hemisphere.
