stumblebum

noun
clumsy or incompetent person
7.0 8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈstʌm.bəl.bʌm/ /; / /ˈstʌm.bəl.bəm/ (AmE), /ˈstʌm.bəl.bəm/ /; / /ˈstʌm.bəl.bʌm/ (BrE)
A person who is clumsy, awkward, or incompetent, especially in a foolish or bumbling way.
