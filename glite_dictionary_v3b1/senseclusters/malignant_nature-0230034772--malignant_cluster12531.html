malignant nature

noun
malevolent or harmful character
6.0
Pronunciation: / məˈlɪɡnənt ˈneɪtʃər / (AmE), / məˈlɪɡnənt ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
a deeply harmful, malevolent, or evil disposition or character trait

Examples

A malignant nature

The author described the villain's malignant nature through chilling dialogue and sinister actions.
