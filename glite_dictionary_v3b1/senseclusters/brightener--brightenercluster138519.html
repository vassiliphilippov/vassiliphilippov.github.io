brightener

noun
substance that whitens
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪtənər / (AmE), / ˈbraɪtənə / (BrE)
a substance or product added to make something appear brighter or whiter, especially in cleaning or laundry

Examples

Laundry brightener
The laundry detergent contains a brightener to help keep your clothes looking new.
Some toothpastes include a brightener to make your smile appear whiter.
Painters sometimes use a brightener to enhance the color of their artwork.
Many laundry detergents include a brightener to help clothes look fresh and clean.
The artist added a brightener to the paint to make the colors stand out more.
To improve the appearance of old curtains, she used a fabric brightener during washing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bleach - a chemical used to whiten or lighten
  • antonym: stain - a substance that changes the color of something, often making it darker
  • synonym: whitener - a substance used to make something whiter
