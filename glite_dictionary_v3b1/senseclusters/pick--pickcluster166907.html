pick

noun
basketball maneuver to block an opponent
Pronunciation: / pɪk / (AmE), / pɪk / (BrE)
(in basketball) a maneuver in which a player uses their body to block or obstruct an opponent, typically to free a teammate for a pass or shot.

Examples

Set a pick
His ability to set a clean pick made him valuable on the court.
She set a strong pick to help her teammate get open for the shot.
The referee called a foul for an illegal pick during the play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: screen - a basketball maneuver in which a player blocks a defender to free a teammate
