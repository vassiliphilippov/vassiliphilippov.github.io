living

noun
means of earning a livelihood
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the money or resources that someone earns or uses to support themselves; livelihood

Examples

Make a living
He struggled to make a living after losing his job.
Many families depend on farming for their living.
She earns her living as a freelance writer in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: employment - the state of having paid work
  • synonym: livelihood - means of supporting one's existence
  • antonym: poverty - state of being extremely poor, lacking means of subsistence
