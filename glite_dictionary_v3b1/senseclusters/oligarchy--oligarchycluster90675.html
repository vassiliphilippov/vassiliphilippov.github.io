oligarchy

noun
government by a small group
7.1 7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈɑː.lɪ.gɑːr.kiː/ /; / /oʊ.lɪ.gɑːr.kiː/ (AmE), /ˈɒl.ɪ.gɑː.kiː/ (BrE)
A form of government or control in which a small group of people holds power.

Examples

The oligarchy rules

Many believe the country is shifting towards an oligarchy controlled by wealthy elites.
The ancient city-state was ruled by a powerful oligarchy of noble families.
