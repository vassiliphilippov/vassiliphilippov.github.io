pinheaded

adjective
narrow-minded or petty
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
narrow-minded, petty, or unwilling to consider different ideas or viewpoints

Examples

Pinheaded thinking
His pinheaded attitude made it impossible to discuss any new suggestions.
The manager's pinheaded approach frustrated the creative team.
She called the rule pinheaded because it ignored all practical concerns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: open-minded - willing to consider new ideas
  • synonym: small-minded - having a limited or narrow outlook
