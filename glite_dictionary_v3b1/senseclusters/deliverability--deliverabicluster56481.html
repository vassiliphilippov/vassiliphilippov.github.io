deliverability

noun
suitability for delivery
7.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˌlɪvərəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / dɪˌlɪvərəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of a financial instrument or commodity that makes it acceptable for delivery under a contract or exchange settlement

Examples

Deliverability of gold
The deliverability of the gold bars was confirmed before the contract was finalized.
Concerns about the deliverability of certain commodities can affect market prices.
Auditors reviewed the deliverability of assets listed in the settlement agreement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acceptability - the quality of being accepted as satisfactory or suitable
  • antonym: nonconformity - the state of not meeting required standards or conditions
