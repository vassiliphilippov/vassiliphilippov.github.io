pistol grip

noun
a handle shaped like a pistol's grip
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪstəl ɡrɪp / (AmE), / ˈpɪstəl ɡrɪp / (BrE)
a handle, especially on a gun or tool, that is shaped like the grip of a pistol, designed to be held in one hand

Examples

A pistol grip handle
He prefers using a saw with a pistol grip because it fits his hand better.
The camera's pistol grip allows photographers to hold it steady while shooting video.
The new shotgun features a pistol grip for improved control and comfort.
