plea

noun
an earnest request for help
1.0
Pronunciation: / pliː / (AmE), / pliː / (BrE)
an earnest or humble request for help or support, especially from someone in authority

Examples

A plea for help
Her plea for leniency touched the judge's heart.
The mayor issued a plea for calm after the protests erupted.
The refugees made a desperate plea for food and shelter.
