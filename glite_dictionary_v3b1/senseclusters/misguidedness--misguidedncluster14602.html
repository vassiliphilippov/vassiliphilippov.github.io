misguidedness

noun
state of being mistaken
7.2
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈɡaɪdɪdnəs / (AmE), / mɪsˈɡaɪdɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being mistaken or led by wrong ideas or beliefs

Examples

Clear misguidedness
Her misguidedness was clear when she ignored all the expert advice.
The project's failure was due in part to the misguidedness of its leaders.
He later admitted that his misguidedness had caused unnecessary problems for everyone.
