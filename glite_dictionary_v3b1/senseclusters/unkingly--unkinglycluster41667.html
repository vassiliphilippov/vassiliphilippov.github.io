unkingly

adverb
without dignity
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is not dignified or noble, or not suitable for a king

Examples

Act unkingly
The prince acted unkingly when he ignored the needs of his people.
Such unkingly behavior shocked everyone at the royal banquet.
He apologized for speaking unkingly to his advisors in public.

Related Terms

  • antonym: kingly - in a manner suitable for a king; noble or dignified
  • synonym: undignified - lacking dignity or respect
