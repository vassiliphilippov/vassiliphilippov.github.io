run wild

verb
act or develop without restraint
Pronunciation: / rʌn waɪld / (AmE), / rʌn waɪld / (BrE)
to behave or develop without restraint, control, or limitation

Examples

To run wild with ideas
Don't let your spending habits run wild or you'll regret it later.
During summer vacation, the children run wild around the neighborhood.
When left alone, her imagination tends to run wild with possibilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: let loose - to allow to behave freely; to set free from restraint
  • antonym: restrain - to keep under control; to limit freedom
