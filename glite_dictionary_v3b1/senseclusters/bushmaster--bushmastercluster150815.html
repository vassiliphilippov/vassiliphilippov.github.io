bushmaster

noun
armored personnel carrier
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊʃˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈbʊʃˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
an Australian-made armored vehicle designed to transport soldiers safely and provide protection in combat zones

Examples

Bushmaster vehicle
The soldiers boarded the bushmaster before heading out on their mission.
A convoy of bushmasters provided protection as they crossed the dangerous terrain.
The new bushmaster models are equipped with advanced communication systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armored vehicle - a vehicle protected by armor, used in military operations
  • antonym: tank - a heavily armored fighting vehicle with tracks and a large gun
