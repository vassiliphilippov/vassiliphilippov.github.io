vocal band

noun
folds in the larynx producing voice
1.5 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈvoʊ.kəl bænd/ (AmE), /ˈvəʊ.kəl bænd/ (BrE)
either of two pairs of folds of mucous membrane in the larynx, involved in the production of voice; also called vocal cords or vocal folds

Examples

Inflammation of the vocal bands can result in loss of voice or difficulty speaking.
Precise movement of the vocal bands is essential for clear speech.
