girly

adjective
feminine or cute
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɜrli / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːli / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of girls, especially in a cute, feminine, or delicate way

Examples

Girly dress style
She wore a girly dress covered in lace and ribbons to the party.
The magazine features girly designs and pastel colors on every page.
He surprised his sister with a girly birthday card decorated with hearts and flowers.
Some people think wearing pink is too girly, but she loves the color.
The coach told the team not to act girly when they hesitated on the field.
He was teased for his girly handwriting in elementary school.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boyish - having qualities traditionally associated with boys
  • synonym: feminine - having qualities traditionally associated with women or girls
  • antonym: manly - having qualities traditionally associated with men, such as strength or bravery
