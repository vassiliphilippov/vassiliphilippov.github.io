exchangeability

noun
ability to be exchanged or interchanged
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌstʃeɪndʒəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ɪksˌtʃeɪndʒəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being able to be exchanged or replaced with something else of equal value or function.

Examples

The exchangeability of parts
Engineers focused on the exchangeability of replacement parts to simplify repairs.
The exchangeability of the two currencies made international trade much easier.
The concept of exchangeability plays a crucial role in financial markets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interchangeability - the quality of being able to be swapped without difference
