Synonyms

  • interchangeability
    7.4 0.02/m
    interchangeability means two items can be swapped without loss of function, while exchangeability means the general ability to be replaced by equivalents.
  • substitutability
    9.4 0.01/m
    substitutability is the ability to take another thing's place in use, while exchangeability stresses being replaced by equal value or function.
  • commutability
    9.4 0.00/m
    commutability is a rarer, more formal term for being replaced or swapped, while exchangeability is the general, neutral word for that quality.
  • fungibility
    9.6 0.03/m
    fungibility means goods or assets can be exchanged for identical items, while exchangeability covers replacing things of equal value or function more broadly.
