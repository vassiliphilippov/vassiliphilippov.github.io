gulping

noun
the act of drinking quickly in large mouthfuls
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌlpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌlpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of swallowing or drinking something quickly and in large amounts, sometimes as a reflex or because of emotion

Examples

Excessive gulping water
Excessive gulping can make you choke if you’re not careful.
Her gulping of the cold drink made everyone laugh on the hot day.
The athlete stopped for some gulping at the water station after the long run.
After running the race, his rapid gulping for air made it obvious he was exhausted.
Her nervousness was betrayed by the gulping in her throat before she spoke.
The loud gulping of water could be heard across the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swallowing - the act of causing or allowing something to pass down the throat
  • synonym: swig - an act of drinking in large gulps
