sling

verb
throw or hurl forcefully
1.2 3.2
Pronunciation: /slɪŋ/ (AmE), /slɪŋ/ (BrE)
to throw, hurl, or toss something forcefully or casually

Examples

To sling a ball

She asked him to sling the keys over to her from across the patio.
