unglaciated

adjective
not affected by glaciers
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈɡleɪsiˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈɡleɪsiˌeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not covered by glaciers or not affected by glaciation during an ice age

Examples

An unglaciated area
The region remained unglaciated while surrounding areas were covered by thick ice sheets.
Scientists study unglaciated landscapes to understand ancient climates and ecosystems.
Many unique plant species survived in unglaciated valleys during the last ice age.

Related Terms

  • antonym: glaciated - covered by glaciers or affected by glaciation
