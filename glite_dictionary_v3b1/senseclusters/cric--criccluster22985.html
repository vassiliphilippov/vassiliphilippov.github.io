cric

noun
emergency airway surgery
9.9
Pronunciation: / krɪk / (AmE), / krɪk / (BrE)
an informal term for a cricothyrotomy, a surgical procedure to create an airway through the throat in an emergency

Examples

Emergency cric procedure
In a life-threatening situation, the paramedic performed a cric to help the patient breathe.
The emergency room team prepared for a possible cric after the airway became blocked.
Doctors are trained to do a cric quickly when other methods fail to restore breathing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cricothyrotomy - a surgical procedure to open the airway through the cricothyroid membrane
  • synonym: tracheotomy - a surgical procedure to create an opening in the trachea to help breathing
