cattleman

noun
man who raises cattle
Pronunciation: / ˈkætl̩ˌmæn /; / ˈkæt̬lˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈkatlman /; / ˈkætl̩ˌmæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to raise or take care of cattle, especially on a ranch or farm

Examples

A cattleman on ranch
As a cattleman, he spent most of his life working on the open range.
Many old Western films portray the life of a cattleman in rugged detail.
The cattleman checked his herd every morning before sunrise.
As a seasoned cattleman, he knew how to spot signs of illness in the livestock.
She grew up on a ranch and admired her father, a hardworking cattleman.
The cattleman rode out early in the morning to check on the herd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cowboy - a man who herds and tends cattle, especially in the western US
  • synonym: rancher - a person who owns or manages a ranch, especially one who raises cattle
  • synonym: stockman - a person who looks after livestock on a farm or ranch
