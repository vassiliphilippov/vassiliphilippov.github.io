string

verb
remove stringy fibers from (beans)
7.3
Pronunciation: / strɪŋ / (AmE), / strɪŋ / (BrE)
to remove the fibrous, stringy parts from (especially beans) before cooking or eating

Examples

To string beans
Before cooking, you should always string the beans to remove the tough fibers.
If you forget to string the beans, they can be unpleasantly chewy.
She spent the afternoon stringing peas and chatting with her grandmother.
