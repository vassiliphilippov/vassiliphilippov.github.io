(proper noun) the Multi-Purpose Logistics Module (MPLM) named Leonardo, a pressurised cargo module used to transfer supplies between the Space Shuttle and the International Space Station.

Examples

Astronauts loaded experiments into the Leonardo module before it departed for the ISS.
The shuttle carried Leonardo back to Earth after completing several cargo deliveries to the station.
