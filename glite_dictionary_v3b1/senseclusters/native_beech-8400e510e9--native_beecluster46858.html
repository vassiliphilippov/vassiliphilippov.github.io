native beech

noun
Australian timber tree with hard wood
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪt̬ɪv ˌbiːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.tɪv ˌbiːtʃ / (BrE)
a tall Australian tree with tough, hard wood used for making staves and similar items

Examples

Tall native beech tree
Conservationists are working to protect stands of native beech in the region.
Many early settlers built fences from the durable wood of native beech.
The furniture was crafted from native beech sourced in Queensland.
