desirableness

noun
quality of being desirable
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪr.ə.bəl.nəs /; / dɪˈzaɪrəbəlnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪərəblnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being desirable or worthy of being desired (including attractiveness)

Examples

The desirableness of skills
Her promotion reflected the desirableness of her unique skills in the company.
The desirableness of this location has increased due to new schools and parks nearby.
The panel discussed the desirableness of various proposals before making a decision.
He envied his friend's easy desirableness at parties, which always seemed effortless.
Her sense of confidence added to her desirableness in the eyes of many.
The actress's undeniable desirableness made her a favorite for leading romantic roles.
