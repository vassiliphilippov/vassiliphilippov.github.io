perpetuation

noun
the act of prolonging or continuing
9.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /pər.pe.tʃuː.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /pər.pe.tjuː.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /pə.pe.tjuː.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /pə.pe.tʃuː.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
