illegal

adjective
not allowed by law or rules
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈliɡəl / (AmE), / ɪˈliːɡəl / (BrE)
not allowed by law or by official or accepted rules; unlawful.

Examples

An illegal action
He was disqualified from the tournament for making an illegal move.
It is illegal to park your car in front of a fire hydrant.
The company was fined for using illegal software.

Related Terms

  • antonym: legal - permitted by law
  • synonym: unlawful - not authorized by law
