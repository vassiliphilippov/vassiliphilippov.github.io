glass

noun
a mirror; dressing mirror (archaic, literary)
4.5
Pronunciation: / ɡlæs / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
A mirror, especially a woman's dressing mirror; now mainly literary or archaic.

Examples

A dressing glass
He polished the ornate glass that once belonged to his grandmother.
In her poem, she refers to the soul as a glass reflecting truth.
She gazed thoughtfully into the glass on her vanity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mirror - a reflective surface, typically of glass, that forms an image of an object placed in front of it
