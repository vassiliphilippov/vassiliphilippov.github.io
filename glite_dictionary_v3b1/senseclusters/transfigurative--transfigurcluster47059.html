transfigurative

adjective
causing transformation
Pronunciation: / trænsˈfɪɡjərətɪv / (AmE), / trænsˈfɪɡjʊrətɪv / (BrE)
causing or relating to a complete or dramatic change in form or appearance

Examples

Transfigurative change
The director's transfigurative vision turned the old play into something entirely new.
Her experience abroad had a transfigurative effect on her outlook and personality.
The novel explores the transfigurative power of love and forgiveness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transformative - causing a significant change or transformation
  • antonym: unchanging - not changing or able to be changed
