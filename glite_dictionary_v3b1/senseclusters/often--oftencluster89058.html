Synonyms

  • frequently
    1.0 60/m
    frequently is a slightly more formal adverb used in reports and writing, while often is common in everyday speech.
  • oftentimes
    4.9 4.80/m
    oftentimes appears in older or poetic speech and sounds less common, while often fits normal modern conversation and writing.
  • time and again
    1.8 3.56/m
    time and again is an idiom that shows repeated occasions over time, while often simply means on many occasions.
  • repeatedly
    1.0 29/m
    repeatedly describes actions done again and again, often with emphasis on the sequence, while often names general frequency.
