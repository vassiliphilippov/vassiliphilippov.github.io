proliferous

adjective
producing new buds
8.4
Pronunciation: /prə.ˈlɪf.ə.rəs/ (AmE), /prə.ˈlɪf.ə.rəs/ (BrE)
producing new individuals, buds, or shoots, or showing rapid and abundant growth or reproduction, especially in plants

Examples
