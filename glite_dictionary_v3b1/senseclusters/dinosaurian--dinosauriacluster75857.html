dinosaurian

adjective
very large or clumsy
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪnəˈsɔːriən / (AmE), / ˌdaɪnəˈsɔːriən / (BrE)
resembling a dinosaur in size, appearance, or being extremely large, heavy, or clumsy

Examples

Dinosaurian machines
The dinosaurian machinery took up most of the factory floor.
His dinosaurian footsteps echoed through the empty hallway.
They struggled to move the dinosaurian sofa into the new apartment.
