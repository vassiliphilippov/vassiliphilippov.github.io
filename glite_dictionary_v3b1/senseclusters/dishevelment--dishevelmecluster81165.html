dishevelment

noun
state of disorder
7.2
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈʃev.əl.mənt/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈʃev.əl.mənt/ (BrE)
a state of being untidy, messy, or in disorder, especially in appearance or arrangement

Examples

Complete dishevelment
After the party, the living room was left in complete dishevelment with cups and plates everywhere.
His hair was in such dishevelment that it looked like he had just woken up.
The office fell into dishevelment during the busy season, with papers piling up on every desk.
After the long journey, his dishevelment was obvious to everyone in the room.
The dishevelment of her hair suggested she had just woken up.
Despite his dishevelment, he made a strong impression at the interview.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mess - a situation or place that is dirty or untidy
  • synonym: messiness - the quality of being messy or disorganized
  • antonym: neatness - the quality of being tidy and orderly
  • antonym: order - a state in which everything is arranged neatly and correctly
