transept

noun
side arm of a cruciform church
9.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtræn.sept/ /; / /ˈtrɑːn.sept/ (AmE), /ˈtræn.sept/ /; / /ˈtrɑːn.sept/ (BrE)
