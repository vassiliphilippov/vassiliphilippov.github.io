galut

noun
person in exile
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡɑːˈluːt / (AmE), / ɡəˈluːt / (BrE)
a person who lives in exile, especially away from their homeland or community

Examples

Stories of the galut often focus on longing for home and cultural identity.
