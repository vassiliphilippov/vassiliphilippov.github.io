instant reply

noun
an immediate response or answer
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnstənt rɪˈplaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɪnstənt rɪˈplaɪ / (BrE)
a reply or response given without delay; an immediate answer

Examples

An instant reply
An instant reply to her message made her feel valued and heard.
The customer support team strives to send an instant reply to all urgent inquiries.
With automated messaging, users can often expect an instant reply from the system.

Related Terms

  • antonym: delayed reply - a reply that is not immediate or is given after some time
  • synonym: prompt reply - a reply given quickly, often almost immediately
