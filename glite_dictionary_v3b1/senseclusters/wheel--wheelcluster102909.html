wheel

noun
device for steering vehicles
6.0
Pronunciation: / wil /; / wiːl / (AmE), / wiːl / (BrE)
a circular object that turns on an axle and is used to help things move or to steer vehicles (such as cars, ships, or in games)

Examples

Steering wheel
Learning to keep both hands on the wheel is important for new drivers.

The captain stood at the wheel, guiding the ship through the storm.
A sudden lurch knocked the wheel from the sailor's hands, causing the boat to veer off course.
She was allowed to steer the yacht by holding the wheel under his supervision.
The captain grasped the wheel firmly as the ship turned into the wind.
In some casinos, the wheel is checked frequently to ensure fair play.
She watched the wheel with anticipation, hoping the ball would land on her number.
The dealer spun the wheel as bets were placed around the table.
