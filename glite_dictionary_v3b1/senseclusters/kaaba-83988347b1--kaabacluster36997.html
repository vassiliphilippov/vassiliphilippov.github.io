Kaaba

noun
Islam's most sacred shrine in Mecca
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːbə / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbə / (BrE)
The most sacred shrine of Islam, a cube-shaped stone building in Mecca, toward which Muslims face to pray.

Examples

The Kaaba shrine
Millions of pilgrims visit the Kaaba every year during the Hajj.
Muslims around the world face the Kaaba in Mecca when performing their daily prayers.
The Kaaba is draped in a black cloth called the kiswah.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mecca - The holy city in Saudi Arabia where the Kaaba is located
