spurrier

noun
one who encourages action
Pronunciation: / ˈspɜːriər / (AmE), / ˈspɜːriə / (BrE)
a person who urges or encourages others to take action or make progress

Examples

A helpful spurrier
As a natural spurrier, he always pushed his friends to pursue their dreams.
The team's spurrier encouraged everyone to keep working hard despite setbacks.
She became the group's spurrier, inspiring others to take on new challenges.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discourager - a person who tries to prevent others from acting
  • synonym: instigator - a person who initiates or provokes action
  • synonym: motivator - a person who inspires others to act
