futural

noun
future tense marker
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːtʃərəl / (AmE), / ˈfjuːtʃərəl / (BrE)
a grammatical form, affix, or marker that indicates future time or expresses the future tense

Examples

A futural marker
The linguist explained how the futural is used to indicate actions that will occur.
Some languages have more than one futural to express different kinds of future events.
Researchers studied the futural in ancient texts to understand how people talked about the future.

Related Terms

  • synonym: future tense - a verb form expressing an action that will happen
  • antonym: past tense - a verb form expressing an action that has already happened
