hard-ass

noun
strict or tough person
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌæs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌɑːs / (BrE)
a person who is very strict, demanding, or tough, especially in enforcing rules or discipline

Examples

A hard-ass manager
Everyone in the office knows the new supervisor is a real hard-ass about following procedures.
My coach was a hard-ass, but his tough attitude helped us win the championship.
You don't have to be a hard-ass to get people to respect you.

Related Terms

  • antonym: softie - a person who is gentle or easily influenced
  • synonym: taskmaster - a person who imposes hard or continuous work
