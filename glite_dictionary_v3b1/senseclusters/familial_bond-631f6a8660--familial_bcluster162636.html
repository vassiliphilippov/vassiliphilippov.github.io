familial bond

noun
relationship based on family connection
8.6
Pronunciation: / fəˈmɪliəl bɑnd / (AmE), / fəˈmɪliəl bɒnd / (BrE)
a close relationship between people based on being members of the same family

Examples

A strong familial bond
Adopted siblings can form a strong familial bond despite not sharing biological parents.
The importance of the familial bond is celebrated in many cultures.
Their familial bond helped them overcome many challenges together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: family tie - a connection or relationship within a family
  • antonym: friendship - a close and supportive relationship between friends
