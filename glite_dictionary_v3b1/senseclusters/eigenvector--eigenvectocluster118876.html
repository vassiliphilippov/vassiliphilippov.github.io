eigenvector

noun
vector in linear algebra
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪɡənˌvɛktər / (AmE), / ˈaɪɡənˌvɛktə / (BrE)
a non-zero vector that, when a linear transformation is applied, only changes in magnitude (is scaled) but not in direction
