gnarled

adjective
rough and knotted in appearance
6.5
Pronunciation: / nɑrld / (AmE), / nɑːld / (BrE)
(of a tree, branch, or person, especially an old one) having a rough, twisted appearance with prominent knots or bumpy areas.

Examples

A gnarled tree
An ancient, gnarled oak stood alone in the middle of the field, its branches twisted and thick.
She placed her gnarled hands gently on the table, her fingers twisted from years of hard work.
The old man's gnarled face broke into a warm, toothless smile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knotted - having protruding knots, lumps, or bumps
  • antonym: smooth - having an even, regular surface; free from roughness or knots
