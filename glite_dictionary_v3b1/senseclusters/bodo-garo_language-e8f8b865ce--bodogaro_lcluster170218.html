Bodo-Garo language

noun
language group of northeastern India
Pronunciation: / ˌboʊdoʊ ˈɡɑːroʊ ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌbəʊdəʊ ˈɡɑːrəʊ ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A group of closely related Kamarupan languages spoken in Assam and surrounding areas in northeastern India.

Examples

Bodo-Garo language family
Linguists study the Bodo-Garo language family to understand its influence in Assam.
Many ethnic groups in northeastern India speak variants of the Bodo-Garo language.
The Bodo-Garo language is notable for its distinct tonal patterns among Kamarupan languages.
