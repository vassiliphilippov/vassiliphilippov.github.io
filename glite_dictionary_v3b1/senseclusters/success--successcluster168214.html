success

noun
state of achieving prosperity or fame
Pronunciation: / səkˈsɛs / (AmE), / səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
the achievement of something you want or have worked for; the state or result of being successful

Winning the championship was the team's greatest success in decades.
After years of hard work, her success was celebrated by friends and family.
He attributed his success in the competition to dedication and practice.
Launching the new product was a huge success for the company.
