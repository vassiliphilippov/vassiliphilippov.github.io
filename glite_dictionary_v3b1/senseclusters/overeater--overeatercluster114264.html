overeater

noun
person who eats too much
3.3 0.13/m
Pronunciation: / Àåo äv…ôrÀàiÀêt…ôr / (AmE), / Àå…ô äv…ôrÀàiÀêt…ô / (BrE)
a person who regularly eats more food than is necessary or healthy

Examples

An overeater habit
As an overeater, he found it difficult to control his portions at family gatherings.
Doctors warned that being an overeater could lead to serious health problems over time.
She joined a support group for overeaters to help manage her eating habits.
