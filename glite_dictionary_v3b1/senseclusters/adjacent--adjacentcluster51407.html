Synonyms

  • next
    1.0 54/m
    next is immediately adjoining with no space between things, while adjacent can mean near or may touch one another.
  • contiguous
    7.0 2.23/m
    contiguous means sharing a border or touching directly, while adjacent can also mean near without an exact shared edge.
  • neighboring
    1.9 0.01/m
    neighboring describes places close by, often beside each other in everyday talk, while adjacent is more neutral and formal.
  • conterminous
    9.5 0.05/m
    conterminous is a formal term for sharing a common boundary, while adjacent is common in speech and writing for near or touching places.
  • side by side
    2.2 8.20/m
    side by side shows two things placed next to each other in a row, while adjacent covers being next to or near more generally.
