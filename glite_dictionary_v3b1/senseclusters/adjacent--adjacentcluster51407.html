adjacent

adjective
next to; having a common boundary
Pronunciation: / əˈdʒeɪsənt / (AmE), / əˈdʒeɪsənt /; / əˈdʒeɪs(ə)nt /; / əˈdʒeɪ.sənt / (BrE)
next to or very near something; possibly sharing a boundary, edge, or point

Examples

Adjacent room

The two countries have adjacent borders along the river.
There is a parking lot adjacent to the restaurant for customers.
A small park lies adjacent to the school, providing space for outdoor activities.
The bakery is adjacent to the grocery store, making shopping very convenient.
They purchased the lot adjacent to their current property for expansion.
A small garden sits adjacent to the restaurant, providing fresh herbs for the kitchen.
The library is adjacent to the science building on campus.
They booked two adjacent rooms for their family vacation.
