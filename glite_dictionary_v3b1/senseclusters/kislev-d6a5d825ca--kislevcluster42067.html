Kislev

proper noun
Jewish calendar month
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪzˌlɛv / (AmE), / ˈkɪz.lɛv / (BrE)
the third month of the civil year in the Jewish calendar, usually falling in November or December

Examples

Kislev in winter
Hanukkah often begins in the month of Kislev on the Jewish calendar.
The first rains of winter usually arrive during Kislev in Israel.
Many Jewish holidays are celebrated in Kislev each year.
