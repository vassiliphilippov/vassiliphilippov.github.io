pureblood

adjective
animal with documented purebred ancestry
Pronunciation: / ˈpjʊrˌblʌd / (AmE), / ˈpjʊəˌblʌd / (BrE)
an animal having documented purebred ancestry, with a list of ancestors proving its breed purity

Examples

Pureblood dog pedigree
Having a pureblood lineage makes the animal more valuable to collectors.
Some horse shows require that all entrants be pureblood Arabians.
The kennel only breeds pureblood dogs with excellent pedigrees.

  • synonym: purebred - an animal of unmixed breed
