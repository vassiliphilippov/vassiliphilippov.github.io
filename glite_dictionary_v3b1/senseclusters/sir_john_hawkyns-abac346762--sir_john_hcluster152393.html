Sir John Hawkyns

proper noun
English naval commander and privateer (1532–1595)

Pronunciation: /sɜːr dʒɑːn ˈhɑː.kɪnz/ /; / /sɜːr dʒɑːn ˈhɔː.kɪnz/ (AmE), /sɜː dʒɒn ˈhɔː.kɪnz/ (BrE)
