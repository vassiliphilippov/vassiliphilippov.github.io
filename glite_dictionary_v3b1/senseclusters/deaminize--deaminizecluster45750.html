deaminize

verb
remove amino group from a compound
8.8
Pronunciation: / diˈæməˌnaɪz / (AmE), / diˈæməˌnaɪz / (BrE)
to remove the amino group from a compound, usually by hydrolysis

Examples

The enzyme acts to deaminize certain amino acids during metabolism.
