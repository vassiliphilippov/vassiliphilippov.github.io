subsection

noun
a division within a section
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌsɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌsɛkʃən / (BrE)
A section that is part of a larger section; a division within a section of a document or text.

Examples

A subsection of text
He rewrote the entire subsection to clarify the instructions.
Refer to subsection 3.2 for further details on this procedure.
The manual is organized into sections and each subsection covers a specific topic.
