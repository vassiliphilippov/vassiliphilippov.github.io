instantiate

verb
represent by a specific example
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstæn.si.eɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈstæn.si.eɪt / (BrE)
to represent or express (an abstract concept or principle) by a concrete example or instance

Examples

Instantiate an idea
Her novel instantiates many of the themes found in contemporary literature.
In object-oriented programming, you must instantiate a class before using its methods.
The researcher chose to instantiate the theory with data from recent experiments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exemplify - to illustrate or clarify by giving an example
