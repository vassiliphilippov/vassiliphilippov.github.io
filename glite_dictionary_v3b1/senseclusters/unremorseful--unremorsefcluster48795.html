unremorseful

adjective
not feeling regret or guilt
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈmɔrs.fəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈmɔːs.fəl / (BrE)
Not feeling or showing any regret or guilt for wrongdoing; not remorseful.

Examples

Unremorseful about actions
Even after hearing about the consequences, he remained unremorseful about his actions.
Her unremorseful attitude shocked everyone in the courtroom.
The apology sounded forced and unremorseful, lacking any real emotion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: remorseful - feeling or expressing regret or guilt
  • synonym: unrepentant - not feeling or showing regret for wrongdoing
