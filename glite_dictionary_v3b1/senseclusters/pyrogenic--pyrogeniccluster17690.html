pyrogenic

adjective
fever-producing or caused by fever
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪrəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌpʌɪrəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
(of a substance or process) producing or resulting from fever.

Examples

Pyrogenic reaction
Researchers are developing tests to detect pyrogenic substances in medical products.
Some bacteria release pyrogenic toxins that can cause a high fever in patients.
The doctor suspected a pyrogenic reaction due to the sudden rise in body temperature.
