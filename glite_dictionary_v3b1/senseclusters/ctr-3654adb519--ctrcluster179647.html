CTR

sym
bank reporting form
8.8
Pronunciation: / si ti ɑr / (AmE), / siː tiː ɑː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for Currency Transaction Report, a government form banks use to report large cash transactions

Examples

Bank CTR form
Banks are required to file a CTR for any cash deposit over $10,000.
The compliance officer reviewed the CTR before submitting it to the authorities.
If a customer makes several small deposits to avoid a CTR, it may be considered suspicious.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Currency Transaction Report - a government form for reporting large cash transactions
  • synonym: SAR - Suspicious Activity Report, a form for reporting suspicious financial activity
