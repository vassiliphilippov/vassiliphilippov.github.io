anterior naris

noun
external opening of the nose; nostril
7.1
Pronunciation: / ænˈtɪriər ˈneɪrɪs / (AmE), / ænˈtɪəriə ˈneərɪs / (BrE)
Either one of the two external openings of the nose leading to the nasal cavity; a nostril.

Examples

The anterior naris opening
A cotton swab was gently inserted into the anterior naris to collect a sample.
During the medical examination, the doctor inspected the anterior naris for any signs of infection.
Obstruction of the anterior naris can make breathing difficult for patients.
