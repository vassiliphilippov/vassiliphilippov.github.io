gaufrette

noun
thin crisp wafer
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊˈfrɛt / (AmE), / ɡəʊˈfrɛt / (BrE)
a thin, crisp wafer, often served as a garnish with desserts or ice cream

Examples

Dessert gaufrette
The chef decorated the dessert with a single gaufrette for an elegant touch.
She enjoyed her coffee with a crunchy gaufrette on the side.
A gaufrette adds both texture and beauty to a bowl of ice cream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wafer - a very thin, crisp biscuit or cookie
