subcommittee

noun
a smaller group within a committee
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌb.kəˈmɪt.i / (AmE), / ˌsʌb.kəˈmɪt.i / (BrE)
a small group formed from among the members of a committee to handle a specific task or issue

Examples

A subcommittee meeting
After lengthy discussion, the project was referred to a subcommittee for further study.
She chairs the health and safety subcommittee at her workplace.
The finance subcommittee will review the annual budget proposal before it's presented to the full committee.
