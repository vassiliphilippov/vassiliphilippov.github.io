gulden

noun
former Dutch currency unit
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʊl.dən / (AmE), / ˈɡʊl.dən / (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in the Netherlands, replaced by the euro in 2002; equal to 100 cents

Examples

Dutch gulden
Before the introduction of the euro, most prices in the Netherlands were given in gulden.
He found an old coin collection containing several rare gulden.
The Dutch gulden was once widely used in international trade.
