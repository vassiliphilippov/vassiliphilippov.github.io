corporatocracy

noun
rule by corporations
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔːrpərəˈtɑːkrəsi / (AmE), / ˌkɔːpərəˈtɒkrəsi / (BrE)
a society or system in which corporations or their leaders have significant control over government or public policy
