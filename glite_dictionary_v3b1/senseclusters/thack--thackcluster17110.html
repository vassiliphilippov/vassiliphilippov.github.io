thack

noun
roofing straw
Pronunciation: / θæk / (AmE), / θæk / (BrE)
straw or similar material used for thatching roofs, especially in older buildings

Examples

Fresh thack bundles
The old barn was repaired with fresh thack after the storm damaged its roof.
Villagers gathered bundles of thack to prepare for the winter months.
In the past, many rural homes were protected from rain by thick layers of thack.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thatch - straw or reeds used to cover roofs
  • antonym: tile - a flat piece of baked clay or other material used for covering roofs
