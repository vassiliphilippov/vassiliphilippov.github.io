Synonyms

  • require
    1.1 364/m
    require is used for rules or formal needs, while demand often shows a strong or urgent request from someone.
  • need
    1.0 1430/m
    need expresses a necessary or personal lack, while demand often implies force or a firm expectation from others.
  • call for
    3.2 20/m
    call for says a situation requires something, while demand usually shows a person or group insisting on it.
  • necessitate
    6.3 5.40/m
    necessitate means to cause something to be needed, while demand means to insist that someone provide or allow it.
