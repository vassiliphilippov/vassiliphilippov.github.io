excessive politicism

noun
overly political behavior or rhetoric
3.7
Pronunciation: / ɪgˈzɛsɪv pəˈlɪtɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪgˈzɛsɪv pəˈlɪtɪˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
Behavior or rhetoric that is overly political, especially in situations where it may not be appropriate or relevant.

Examples

Overt excessive politicism
Critics accused the broadcaster of excessive politicism during what was supposed to be an impartial news program.
Some feel that excessive politicism can distract from genuine discussion of key community issues.
The audience grew weary of the excessive politicism in his address, wanting instead to hear about practical solutions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: politicism - political orientation or the use of politics
