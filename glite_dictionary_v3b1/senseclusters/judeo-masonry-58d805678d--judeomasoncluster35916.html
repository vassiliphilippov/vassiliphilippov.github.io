Judeo-Masonry

noun
conspiracy theory linking Jews and Freemasons
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒuːˌdeoʊˈmeɪsənri / (AmE), / dʒuːˌdeɪəʊˈmeɪsənri / (BrE)
A term referring to a conspiracy theory or alleged alliance between Jews and Freemasons, often invoked in anti-Semitic and conspiratorial contexts.

Examples

Judeo-Masonry conspiracy theory
Scholars studying anti-Semitism often address references to Judeo-Masonry in historical propaganda.
Some extremist groups continue to promote the myth of Judeo-Masonry to justify their beliefs.
The idea of Judeo-Masonry has been widely debunked by historians as a baseless conspiracy theory.
