caliphal

adjective
of a caliph
Pronunciation: / kəˈliːfəl / (AmE), / kəˈliːfəl / (BrE)
relating to a caliph or the office, authority, or period of a caliph in Islamic history

Examples

The caliphal palace
The caliphal palace was renowned for its architectural beauty and grandeur.
During the caliphal era, scholars made significant advances in science and philosophy.
Many laws were established under caliphal rule to govern the expanding empire.
