nursing bottle

noun
bottle for feeding infants
3.3 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈnɜːr.sɪŋ ˈbɑː.təl/ (AmE), /ˈnɜː.sɪŋ ˈbɒt.əl/ (BrE)
A bottle fitted with a flexible teat, used to feed milk or formula to infants and very young children as a substitute for breastfeeding.

Examples

A nursing bottle with milk
She sterilized each nursing bottle before preparing the formula.
The newborn was fed with a nursing bottle every three hours.
They packed extra nursing bottles for their trip with the baby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baby bottle - a bottle with a nipple for feeding infants; another common term for nursing bottle
  • antonym: breastfeeding - the act of feeding a baby with milk directly from the breast; opposite feeding method to nursing bottle
