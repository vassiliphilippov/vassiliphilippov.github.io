oleography

noun
art of printing imitation oil paintings
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊliˈɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˌəʊliˈɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the art or process of creating prints, especially on cloth, that imitate oil paintings by using chromolithography.

Examples

Oleography art prints
Oleography allowed art lovers to enjoy affordable replicas that closely resembled original oil paintings.
He specializes in the restoration of vintage oleography prints.
The museum's collection included a rare oleography of a 19th-century landscape.
