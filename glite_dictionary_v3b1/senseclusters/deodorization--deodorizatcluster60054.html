deodorization

noun
removal of odors
4.8
Pronunciation: / diˌoʊdərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / diˌəʊdəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of removing or neutralizing unpleasant odors from something

Examples

The deodorization process
The deodorization of vegetable oil is an important step to ensure a neutral taste.
Modern factories use steam distillation for the deodorization of edible fats.
Proper deodorization helps extend the shelf life of cooking oils by removing volatile compounds.
The deodorization of the kitchen was necessary after the fire.
Modern air purifiers are designed for effective deodorization of indoor spaces.
Industrial plants often use special equipment for the deodorization of waste gases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disinfection - the process of cleaning something to destroy bacteria
  • synonym: purification - the process of making something clean by removing impurities
  • synonym: refining - the process of purifying a substance, especially in industry
