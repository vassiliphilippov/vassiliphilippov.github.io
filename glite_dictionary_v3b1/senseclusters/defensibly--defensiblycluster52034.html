defensibly

adverb
in a justifiable way
6.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɛnsəbli / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛnsəbli / (BrE)
in a manner that can be defended or justified as reasonable or acceptable

Examples

Act defensibly in court
The company acted defensibly when it recalled the faulty products to protect customers.
Her decision to leave early was defensibly based on the information she had at the time.
The new law was defensibly introduced to address growing safety concerns in the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indefensibly - in a way that cannot be defended or justified
  • synonym: justifiably - in a way that can be shown to be right or reasonable
