pffft

noun
dismissive sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / pfːt / (AmE), / pfːt / (BrE)
a short, informal sound made to express dismissal, disbelief, or lack of interest.

Examples

A loud pffft

The idea was met with a collective pffft from the group.
