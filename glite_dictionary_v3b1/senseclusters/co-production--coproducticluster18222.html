co-production

noun
citizen–authority joint service delivery
3.9
Pronunciation: /koʊ prə.ˈdʌk.ʃən/ /; / /kə prə.ˈdʌk.ʃən/ (AmE), /kəʊ prə.ˈdʌk.ʃən/ /; / /kə prə.ˈdʌk.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process by which public services are designed, delivered, or improved through active collaboration between citizens (or community groups) and public authorities or service providers.

Examples

Community co-production
Researchers argue that co-production between citizens and the council improves local public services.
The city's health department promotes co-production to design services that meet patients' needs.
Volunteers took part in the co-production of a community recycling program last summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaboration - the action of working with someone to produce something
  • synonym: public participation - the involvement of citizens in planning, decision-making, or designing public services
