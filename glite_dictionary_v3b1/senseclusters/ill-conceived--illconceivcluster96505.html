ill-conceived

adjective
not carefully planned; poorly thought out
6.1 0.65/m
Pronunciation: / …™l k…ônÀàsivd / (AmE), / …™l k…ônÀàsiÀêvd / (BrE)
not carefully planned or considered; badly thought out.

Examples

An ill-conceived plan
It was an ill-conceived attempt to solve a complex problem without proper research.
Many believe the project failed because it was ill-conceived from the very beginning.
The committee rejected the ill-conceived proposal before it reached the voting stage.

Synonyms

    6.8 0.38/m

  • ill-judged
    6.3 0.11/m
    ill-judged shows poor judgment about likely consequences, while ill-conceived focuses on being badly planned or not well thought out.
  • ill-advised
    5.4 1.17/m

  • misguided
    4.6 7.88/m

  • uncalculated
    5.6 0.01/m

  • poorly designed
    3.7 0.63/m
