fortunately

adverb
by good luck; luckily
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrtʃənətli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtʃənətli / (BrE)
By good luck; as a result of favorable circumstances or chance.

Examples

Fortunately, the weather
Fortunately, the weather stayed clear during our entire trip.
He missed the last train, but fortunately there was a bus he could catch instead.
The document was lost, but fortunately a backup copy existed.
