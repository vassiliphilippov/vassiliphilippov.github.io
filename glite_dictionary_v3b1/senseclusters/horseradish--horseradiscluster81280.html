horseradish

noun
pungent root used as seasoning
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔːrˌsˌrædɪʃ /; / ˈhɔːrsˌrædɪʃ /; / ˈhɔrsˌrædɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsˌrædɪʃ / (BrE)
