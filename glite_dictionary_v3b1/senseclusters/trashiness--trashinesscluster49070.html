trashiness

noun
cheap imitation or lack of taste
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtræʃi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtræʃi.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being cheaply or tastelessly imitative of something of higher quality; the state of lacking refinement or value

Examples

The trashiness of style
Critics lamented the trashiness of the novel, comparing it unfavorably to classic literature.
Some reality TV shows are popular precisely because of their deliberate trashiness.
The trashiness of the décor made the restaurant feel more like a theme park than a fine dining establishment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elegance - the quality of being graceful and stylish in appearance or manner
  • synonym: tackiness - the quality of cheapness or lack of good taste
