anthozoan

noun
marine animal of the class including corals and sea anemones
Pronunciation: / ˌænθəˈzoʊən / (AmE), / ˌænθəˈzəʊən / (BrE)
a member of a class of marine animals, including corals and sea anemones, that exist as polyps and do not have a medusa stage

Examples

A anthozoan species
Researchers discovered a rare anthozoan species living in the deep sea corals.
Some anthozoans form large colonies that build extensive coral reefs.
The anthozoan attaches itself to rocks on the ocean floor, creating a vibrant marine habitat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coral - a hard, stony substance formed by the skeletons of marine animals; an anthozoan
  • synonym: sea anemone - a brightly colored, flower-like marine animal; an anthozoan
