break the silence

verb
end a period of silence
Pronunciation: / breɪk ðə ˈsaɪləns / (AmE), / breɪk ðə ˈsaɪləns / (BrE)
to suddenly make a noise or speak after a period of silence or quiet

Examples

To break the silence suddenly
A distant thunderstorm broke the silence of the night.
He broke the silence by clearing his throat and beginning his speech.
She finally broke the silence and asked what everyone was thinking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: break the ice - to do or say something to relieve tension or get conversation going
