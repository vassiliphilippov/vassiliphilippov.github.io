Romany language

noun
language of the Romani people
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊ.mə.ni ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.məni ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The language of the Romani people, belonging to the Indo-Aryan branch of the Indo-European family, spoken by Romani communities throughout Europe and beyond.
