cardiotoxic

adjective
harmful to the heart
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑrdioʊˈtɑksɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɑːdiəʊˈtɒksɪk / (BrE)
harmful or toxic to the heart muscle or its function

Examples

Cardiotoxic drugs
Some chemotherapy drugs are known to be cardiotoxic.
The study found that long-term exposure to the chemical was highly cardiotoxic in laboratory animals.
Doctors monitor patients for cardiotoxic effects when prescribing certain medications.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cardioprotective - helping to protect the heart from damage
  • synonym: toxic - harmful or poisonous to living things
