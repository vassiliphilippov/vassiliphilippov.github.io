stretch

noun
an act of reaching or extending
3.2
Pronunciation: / strɛtʃ / (AmE), / strɛtʃ / (BrE)
an act of reaching or extending part of the body, or an exercise to improve flexibility or relieve tension

Examples

A morning stretch
After sitting for hours, a long stretch felt wonderful.
He made a stretch to grab the book on the top shelf.
With a stretch of her arm, she was able to reach the branch.
It’s important to do a good stretch before and after your workout.
She paused during her run for a quick stretch to loosen her muscles.
This yoga class begins with a long stretch for the whole body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexibility - the quality of bending easily without breaking, often improved by stretching
  • synonym: reach - an act of stretching out an arm
  • synonym: warm-up - light activity to prepare for more strenuous exercise
