actinometrical

adjective
relating to the measurement of radiant energy
Pronunciation: / ˌæk.tɪ.noʊˈmɛ.trɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌæk.tɪ.nəʊˈmɛ.trɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to actinometry, the measurement of the intensity of radiant energy, especially sunlight.

Examples

Actinometrical instruments
Improvements in actinometrical instruments have enhanced the accuracy of weather forecasts.
The researchers used actinometrical methods to determine the solar radiation at different altitudes.
Their study focuses on the actinometrical analysis of ultraviolet exposure in urban environments.
