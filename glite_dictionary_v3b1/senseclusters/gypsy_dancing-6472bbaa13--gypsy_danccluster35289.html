gypsy dancing

noun
traditional Andalusian Gypsy dance style
1.0 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒɪp.siː ˈdæn.sɪŋ/ /; / /ˈdʒɪp.sɪ ˈdæn.sɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈdʒɪp.siː ˈdɑːn.sɪŋ/ /; / /ˈdʒɪp.sɪ ˈdɑːn.sɪŋ/ (BrE)
