Muhammad al-Mahdi

proper noun
Islamic messianic figure; restorer before the end times
9.9
Pronunciation: /mʊ.ˈhɑː.məd əl.mɑː.ˈdiː/ /; / /mʊ.ˈhæ.məd əl.mɑː.ˈdiː/ (AmE), /mʊ.ˈhæ.məd əl.mɑː.ˈdiː/ /; / /mʊ.ˈhɑː.məd əl.mɑː.ˈdiː/ (BrE)
(in Islam) the messianic figure who, according to popular Muslim belief, will appear before the end of the world to restore justice and true religion.

Examples

Muhammad al-Mahdi will return
Many Shia Muslims believe that Muhammad al-Mahdi is in occultation and will one day return.
Some historical figures have claimed to be the awaited Muhammad al-Mahdi.
The concept of Muhammad al-Mahdi plays a significant role in Islamic eschatology.
