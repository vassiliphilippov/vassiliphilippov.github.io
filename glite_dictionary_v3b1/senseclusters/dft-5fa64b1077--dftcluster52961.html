DFT

noun
quantum chemistry method
9.9
Pronunciation: / di ɛf ti / (AmE), / diː ɛf tiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'density functional theory', a computational method used in quantum chemistry and physics to study electronic structure

Examples

DFT calculation in chemistry
The research team performed DFT calculations to analyze the electronic properties of the new material.
Many modern studies in computational chemistry rely on DFT for accurate predictions.
She learned how to use DFT during her graduate studies in theoretical physics.
