glenoid

adjective
shoulder socket-related
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɛnɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡliːnɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to a shallow cavity in a bone, especially the socket in the shoulder blade that receives the head of the upper arm bone
