say what

interjection
expression of surprise or disbelief
Pronunciation: / seɪ wʌt / (AmE), / seɪ wɒt / (BrE)
used to express surprise or disbelief at something just heard or said

Examples

Say what in surprise
He wants to move to Antarctica, say what?
They canceled the flight again, say what?
You spent that much on shoes? Say what!

Related Terms

  • synonym: come again - used to ask someone to repeat something, often with surprise or disbelief
