Synonyms

  • disbelieving
    4.7 0.22/m
    disbelieving shows a clear refusal to accept a claim, while incredulous expresses shocked disbelief at surprising news.
  • unbelieving
    5.9 0.26/m
    unbelieving doubts or rejects certain religious or fixed beliefs, while incredulous shows disbelief often at a single surprising event.
  • skeptical
    4.2 19/m
    skeptical questions claims and asks for proof, while incredulous reacts with disbelief and surprise without waiting for reasons.
  • unconvinced
    5.1 1.50/m
    unconvinced remains not persuaded after hearing an argument, while incredulous shows open disbelief or shock at what is said.
