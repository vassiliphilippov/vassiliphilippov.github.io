trundle

noun
a low bed stored under another bed
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌndəl / (AmE), / ˈtrʌndəl / (BrE)
a low bed on wheels or casters that is stored under a higher bed and can be pulled out for use

Examples

A trundle bed
The trundle fits neatly under the main bed when not in use.
The guest room has a trundle that can be pulled out for extra sleeping space.
We bought a trundle for our daughter's friend to use during sleepovers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trundle bed - a bed that can be rolled under another
