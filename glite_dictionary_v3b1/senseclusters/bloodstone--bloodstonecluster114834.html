bloodstone

noun
green chalcedony with red spots
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈblʌdstəʊn / (BrE)
a variety of green chalcedony, a semiprecious stone, with distinctive red spots resembling drops of blood

Examples

A bloodstone gem
Collectors prize bloodstone for its unusual green and red coloration.
She wore a striking ring set with a polished bloodstone.
The museum displayed an ornate cup carved from bloodstone in its gem collection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heliotrope - another name for bloodstone, especially in gemology
