supercow

noun
superpowered cow character
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌkaʊ / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəkau / (BrE)
a fictional or humorous cow character with extraordinary, often superhero-like powers, typically appearing in stories, jokes, or computer Easter eggs

Examples

A flying supercow
The video game features a hidden level where you can play as a flying supercow.
In the comic strip, supercow uses her powers to protect the other animals from danger.
Everyone laughed when the commercial showed a supercow lifting a tractor with one hoof.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cow - a large domesticated animal kept for milk or meat
  • synonym: superhero - a fictional character with superhuman powers who fights evil
