perfume

verb
to fill with a pleasant scent
Pronunciation: / pərˈfjuːm / (AmE), / pəˈfjuːm / (BrE)
(verb) to fill or impregnate (a place or the air) with a pleasant scent.

Examples

Flowers perfume the air
A gentle breeze perfumed the evening air with the scent of nearby roses.
After she baked cookies, the kitchen was perfumed with the smell of chocolate and vanilla.
The blooming lilacs perfumed the entire street in spring.
