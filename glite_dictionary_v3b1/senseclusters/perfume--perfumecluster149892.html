perfume

verb
to fill with a pleasant scent
3.9 0.36/m
Pronunciation: / p…ôrÀàfjuÀêm / (AmE), / p…ôÀàfjuÀêm / (BrE)
(verb) to fill or impregnate (a place or the air) with a pleasant scent.

Examples

Flowers perfume the air
A gentle breeze perfumed the evening air with the scent of nearby roses.
After she baked cookies, the kitchen was perfumed with the smell of chocolate and vanilla.
The blooming lilacs perfumed the entire street in spring.

Synonyms

  • aromatize
    7.2 0.01/m
    aromatize fills a room or the air with a pleasant smell, while perfume appears more often in everyday speech.
  • scent
    3.3 2.76/m
    scent means to apply a fragrance to something or give it a smell, while perfume means to fill a place or the air.
