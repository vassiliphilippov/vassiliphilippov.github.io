Gymnosporangium

noun
genus of fungi causing rust on plants
Pronunciation: / dʒɪmˌnoʊspəˈrændʒiəm /; / dʒɪm.nə.spɔːˈrændʒ.i.əm / (AmE), / dʒɪmˌnɒspəˈrandʒɪəm /; / dʒɪm.nə.spɒˈrændʒ.ɪ.əm / (BrE)
a genus of fungi that produce galls on cedar and other conifer trees and cause rust diseases on apples, pears, and other plants in the rose family
