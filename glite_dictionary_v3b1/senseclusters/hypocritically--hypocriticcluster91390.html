hypocritically

adverb
in a hypocritical way
5.0 5.9
Pronunciation: / hɪˌpɑːkrɪkli / (AmE), / hɪˈpɒkrɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows hypocrisy; in a manner that pretends to have moral standards or beliefs that are not actually possessed.

Examples

To hypocritically criticize others
He hypocritically criticized others for dishonesty while hiding his own secrets.
She hypocritically lectured about healthy eating while snacking on junk food.
The politician hypocritically promised reform, but his actions remained unchanged.

Related Terms

  • antonym: honestly - in a truthful and honorable way
  • antonym: sincerely - in a genuine and honest manner
