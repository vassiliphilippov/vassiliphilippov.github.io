Liao dynasty

noun
Chinese dynasty (947–1125) ruled by the Khitan in Manchuria and NE China
9.5
Pronunciation: /liː.ˈaʊ ˈdaɪ.nə.stɪ/ (AmE), /liː.ˈaʊ ˈdɪn.ə.stɪ/ (BrE)
the ruling dynasty of Manchuria and parts of northeastern China from 947 to 1125, established by the Khitan people

Examples

The Liao dynasty period
Many unique artifacts have been discovered from the Liao dynasty period.
The Liao dynasty controlled large parts of northern China during the 10th and 11th centuries.
The fall of the Liao dynasty marked a significant shift in the region's history.
