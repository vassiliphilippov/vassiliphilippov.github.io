Jun

noun
fermented tea drink
Pronunciation: / dʒʌn / (AmE), / dʒʌn / (BrE)
a sweet, fizzy fermented drink made from green tea and honey, similar to kombucha

Examples

A glass of Jun
She offered her guests a glass of homemade Jun at the garden party.
Many people enjoy drinking Jun as a refreshing alternative to soda.
The café serves both kombucha and Jun brewed in-house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kombucha - a fermented tea drink made with black tea and sugar
