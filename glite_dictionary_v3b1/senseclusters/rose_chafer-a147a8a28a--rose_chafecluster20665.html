rose chafer

noun
North American beetle pest of plants
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊz ˌtʃeɪfər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊz ˌtʃɑːfə / (BrE)
a type of beetle that feeds on the leaves and flowers of plants, especially roses
