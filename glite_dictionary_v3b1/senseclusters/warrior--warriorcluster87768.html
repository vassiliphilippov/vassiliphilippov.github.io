warrior

noun
person experienced in warfare
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔriər / (AmE), / ˈwɒriə / (BrE)
A person who is engaged or experienced in warfare, especially as a member of a fighting force.

Examples

A warrior in battle
He showed the spirit of a true warrior during the difficult battle.
Many legends tell the stories of brave warriors who defended their people.
The ancient tribe honored every fallen warrior with a ceremonial burial.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pacifist - a person who opposes war and violence
  • synonym: soldier - a person serving in an army
