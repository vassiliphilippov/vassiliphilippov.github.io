cricket frog

noun
a small North American frog with a clicking call
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪkɪt frɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪkɪt frɒɡ / (BrE)
either of two small North American frogs, noted for their clicking call, found in or near water

Examples

A cricket frog near water
A cricket frog was calling from the edge of the pond all evening.
The distinctive clicking of the cricket frog can be heard in the springtime.
We spotted a tiny cricket frog hidden among the reeds.
