hogtie

noun
limb restraint
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑɡˌtaɪ / (AmE), / ˈhɒɡˌtaɪ / (BrE)
a method of tying together the limbs of a person or animal to prevent movement

Examples

A hogtie on a calf
The cowboy used a hogtie to keep the calf from escaping during the branding.
After the roundup, the ranch hands released the calf from the temporary hogtie.
The security team applied a hogtie to restrain the unruly animal safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restraint - a device or method that limits movement
  • synonym: shackle - a metal fastening for securing the wrist or ankle
