B-complex vitamin

noun
group of B vitamins important for health
2.9 3.4
Pronunciation: / bi ˈkɑmplɛks ˈvaɪtəmɪn / (AmE), / bi ˈkɒmplɛks ˈvɪtəmɪn / (BrE)
a group of water-soluble vitamins (the B vitamins) that play important roles in cell metabolism and were once thought to be a single vitamin

Examples

A B-complex vitamin group
A deficiency in any B-complex vitamin can lead to various health issues.
Doctors often recommend a daily B-complex vitamin supplement for people with restricted diets.
Most multivitamins contain a B-complex vitamin to support metabolism and energy.
