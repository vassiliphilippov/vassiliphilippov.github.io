rematerialization

noun
recycling into products
8.9
Pronunciation: /riː.mə.tɪr.ɪə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rə.mə.tɪr.ɪə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /riː.mə.tɪə.rɪə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪ.mə.tɪə.rɪə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)

Examples

Rematerialization of waste

Efficient rematerialization of electronic waste helps reduce environmental pollution and conserve resources.
Through rematerialization, discarded plastic bottles were transformed into durable park benches.
