Mediterranean cypress

noun
a tall cypress tree native to the Mediterranean
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən ˈsaɪprəs / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən ˈsʌɪprəs / (BrE)
a tall cypress tree native to the Mediterranean region, with thin grey bark and upward-growing branches, often used as an ornamental or in cemeteries
