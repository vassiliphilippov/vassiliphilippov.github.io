fisherman

noun
person whose job is catching fish
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃərmən / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃəmən / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, whose occupation is catching fish

Examples

A fisherman catches fish
Many fishermen in the village depend on the river for their livelihood.
The fisherman cast his net into the sea at sunrise.
The experienced fisherman proudly displayed his catch at the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angler - a person who fishes with a rod and line
  • synonym: fisherwoman - a woman who catches fish, especially professionally
