wire

noun
message sent by telegraph
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪə / (BrE)
a message sent by telegraph; a telegram

Examples

A wire message
He received a wire from his sister announcing her arrival.
Please send a wire as soon as you reach New York.
They communicated urgent news via wire during the war.

Related Terms
