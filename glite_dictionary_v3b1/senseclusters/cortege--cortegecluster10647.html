cortege

noun
funeral procession
8.0
Pronunciation: / kɔrˈtɛʒ /; / kɔɹˈteɪʒ / (AmE), / kɔːˈtɛʒ /; / kɔːˈtɛːʒ / (BrE)
a solemn procession, especially for a funeral or ceremonial event

Examples

Dignitaries walked behind the president in a formal cortege during the state event.
The royal cortege made its way slowly through the cheering crowds.
