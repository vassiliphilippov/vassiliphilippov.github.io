blue wall

noun
electoral bloc of reliably partisan regions
5.6
Pronunciation: /bluː wɔːl/ /; / /bluː wɑːl/ (AmE), /bluː wɔːl/ (BrE)
A bloc of voters or geographic regions that consistently support the same political party, forming a reliable electoral stronghold for that party.

Examples

Election blue wall
Analysts warned that the blue wall of states would shape the election outcome.
Campaign strategists concentrated resources in the blue wall to secure crucial electoral votes.
After demographic shifts, some commentators argued the blue wall might no longer be reliable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electoral bloc - a group of voters or regions that act together to support a political party or candidate
  • antonym: red wall - a bloc of regions that historically voted for the opposing party; often used as a contrasting political term
