glass of claret

noun
glass of red wine (claret)
Pronunciation: / ɡlæs əv ˈklærət / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs əv ˈklærɪt / (BrE)
A glass containing claret, a type of red wine, especially as served with a meal.

Examples

A glass of claret
During the meal, she offered her guest a glass of claret.
He leisurely sipped his glass of claret by the fireplace.
Would you like a glass of claret with your dinner?
