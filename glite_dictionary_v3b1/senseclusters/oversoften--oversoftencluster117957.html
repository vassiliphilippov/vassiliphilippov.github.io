oversoften

verb
to make too soft
6.1
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈsɑːf.ən/ /; / /oʊ.vər.ˈsɔːf.ən/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈsɒf.ən/ (BrE)
to make something excessively soft or softer than is desirable

Examples

To oversoften butter
If you oversoften the butter, it will be too runny for baking cookies.
Be careful not to oversoften the clay, or it will lose its shape.
Using too much fabric softener can oversoften your towels, making them less absorbent.
