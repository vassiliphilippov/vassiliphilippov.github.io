Pinus ponderosa

noun
a large pine tree native to western North America
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪnəs pɒn.dəˈroʊ.sə / (AmE), / ˌpaɪ.nəs pɒn.dəˈrəʊ.sə / (BrE)
a tall and widely distributed species of pine native to western North America, recognized for its dark green needles in clusters of 2 to 5 and thick, dark brown bark
