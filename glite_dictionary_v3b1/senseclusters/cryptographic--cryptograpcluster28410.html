cryptographic

adjective
relating to cryptography or code-breaking
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪptoʊˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌkrɪptəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving cryptography or the techniques of secure communication and code-breaking.

Examples

A cryptographic key
