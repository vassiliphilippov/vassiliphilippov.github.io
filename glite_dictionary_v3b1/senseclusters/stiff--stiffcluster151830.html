stiff

noun
slang term for a dead body
5.4
Pronunciation: / stɪf / (AmE), / stɪf / (BrE)
(slang) A dead body, especially that of a human being.

Examples

A stiff in movies
In old gangster movies, you often hear them call a dead man a stiff.
Nobody wanted to transport the stiff until the coroner arrived on the scene.
The detectives found a stiff under the old pier early this morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corpse - a dead body, especially of a human
