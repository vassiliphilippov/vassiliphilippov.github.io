heaver

noun
worker who lifts heavy goods, esp. at docks
7.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàhiÀêv…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàhiÀêv…ô / (BrE)
a worker whose job is to lift or move heavy freight or goods, especially at a dockyard

Examples

Dockyard heaver

The heaver loaded crates onto the ship all morning.
Without the help of a skilled heaver, moving the heavy barrels would have taken much longer.
