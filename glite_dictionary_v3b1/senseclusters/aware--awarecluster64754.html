aware

adjective
having knowledge or consciousness of something
Pronunciation: / əˈwɛr / (AmE), / əˈwɛə / (BrE)
having knowledge, understanding, or perception of a situation or fact; conscious or cognizant

Examples

To be aware of facts
Are you aware that the meeting starts in ten minutes?
He suddenly became aware of someone watching him from across the room.
She was fully aware of the risks involved in the operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conscious - aware of and responding to one's surroundings
  • antonym: unaware - not having knowledge or awareness of something
