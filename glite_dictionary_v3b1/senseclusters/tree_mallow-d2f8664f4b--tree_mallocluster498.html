tree mallow

noun
a shrub with lobed leaves and showy flowers
7.9
Pronunciation: / tri ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / tri ˈmaləʊ / (BrE)
A perennial shrub with palmately lobed furry leaves and showy red-purple flowers, native to the southwestern United States.
