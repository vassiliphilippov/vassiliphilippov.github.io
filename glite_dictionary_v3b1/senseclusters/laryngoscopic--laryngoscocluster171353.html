laryngoscopic

adjective
examining the larynx
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌlærɪŋɡəˈskɑpɪk / (AmE), / ˌlærɪŋɡəˈskɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the examination of the larynx using a laryngoscope

Examples

Laryngoscopic examination
The surgeon used a laryngoscopic technique to inspect the patient's vocal cords.
A laryngoscopic examination can help diagnose problems in the throat.
Special training is required to perform laryngoscopic procedures safely and effectively.
