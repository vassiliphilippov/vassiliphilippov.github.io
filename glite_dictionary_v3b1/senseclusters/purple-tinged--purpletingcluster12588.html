purple-tinged

adjective
tinged with purple
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpəl tɪndʒd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl tɪndʒd / (BrE)
Having a slight or noticeable tint or hue of purple added to the main color.

Examples

The sky took on a purple-tinged glow as the sun set behind the mountains.
