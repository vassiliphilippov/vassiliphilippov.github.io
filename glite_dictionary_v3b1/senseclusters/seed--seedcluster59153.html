seed

noun
top-ranked tournament player
7.3 1.45/m
Pronunciation: / sid / (AmE), / siËd / (BrE)
an outstanding player who is given a ranking in a sports tournament, often to ensure they do not meet other top players in the early rounds

Examples

Top seed wins
An unseeded player managed to defeat the top seed in a shocking upset.
Despite being only the fifth seed, she advanced to the finals.
He was the number one seed in the Wimbledon tournament this year.
