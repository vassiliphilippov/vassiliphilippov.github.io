nitpicky

adjective
overly critical of details
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈnɪt.pɪ.kiː/ (AmE), /ˈnɪt.pɪ.kiː/ (BrE)
paying too much attention to small or unimportant details, often in a critical way

Examples

Nitpicky comments
Her nitpicky comments about the presentation made everyone nervous.
He can be so nitpicky that even minor mistakes bother him.
The editor's nitpicky approach slowed down the publishing process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fussy - hard to please and overly concerned with details
