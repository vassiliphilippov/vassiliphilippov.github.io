gustatorily

adverb
relating to taste
Pronunciation: / ɡʌsˈteɪtərɪli / (AmE), / ɡʌsˈteɪtərɪli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to the sense of taste

Examples

To gustatorily enjoy food
The chef prepared a meal that was gustatorily delightful and full of unique flavors.
She described the new dessert as gustatorily adventurous, with unexpected combinations of sweet and sour.
The soup was gustatorily satisfying, leaving everyone wanting a second helping.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tastefully - in a way that shows good taste or is pleasing to the taste
