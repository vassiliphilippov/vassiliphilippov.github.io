100th

adjective
ordinal number for one hundred
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌn ˌhʌndrɪdθ / (AmE), / ˈwʌn ˌhʌndrədθ / (BrE)
Position in a sequence that comes after ninety-ninth and before one hundred and first; the ordinal number corresponding to one hundred.

Examples

The 100th place
She finished in 100th place in the marathon.
This year marks the 100th anniversary of the school’s founding.
We celebrated his 100th birthday with a big party.
