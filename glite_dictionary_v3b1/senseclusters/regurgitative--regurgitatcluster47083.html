regurgitative

adjective
causing regurgitation
Pronunciation: / rɪˈɡɝːdʒɪˌteɪtɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈɡɜːdʒɪtətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or causing regurgitation, especially the backward flow of fluid or food in the body

Examples

Regurgitative murmur
The doctor noted a regurgitative murmur during the heart examination.
Certain heart valve disorders can lead to regurgitative blood flow.
The patient experienced regurgitative episodes after eating too quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reflux - the backward flow of fluid, especially in the body
