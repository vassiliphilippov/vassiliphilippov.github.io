coffin

noun
box for a dead body
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.fɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɒf.ɪn / (BrE)
A long, narrow box, typically made of wood, in which a dead body is buried or cremated.

Examples

A wooden coffin
After the ceremony, the coffin was lowered into the ground.
The family gathered around the coffin during the funeral service.
The ornate coffin was covered with a velvet cloth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: casket - a box for burying a dead body, especially in North American English
