canful

noun
quantity a can holds
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.fəl / (AmE), / ˈkan.fəl / (BrE)
An amount that fills a can.

Examples

A canful of soda
He drank a whole canful of soda in one gulp.
She poured a canful of paint into the tray before starting to paint the wall.
The recipe calls for a canful of soup to be added to the pot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cupful - the amount that fills a cup
