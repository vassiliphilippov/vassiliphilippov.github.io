desolation

noun
total destruction or devastation
6.2 6.1
Pronunciation: /de.sə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /də.sə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /de.sə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a state of complete destruction, emptiness, or ruin, especially in a place or landscape

Examples
