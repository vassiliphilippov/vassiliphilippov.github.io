pod

noun
Kubernetes deployable unit
6.0
Pronunciation: /pɑːd/ (AmE), /pɒd/ (BrE)
(in computing, especially Kubernetes) the smallest deployable unit, a group of one or more containers that share network and storage and run together on a node.

Examples

A Kubernetes pod
The application runs in a pod on the Kubernetes cluster.
During deployment, the team scaled the pod to handle increased traffic quickly.
A pod can contain several containers that share the same network and storage settings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: container - a lightweight, standalone software package that includes an application and its dependencies
