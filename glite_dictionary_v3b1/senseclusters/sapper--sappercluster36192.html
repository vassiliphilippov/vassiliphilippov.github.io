sapper

noun
military engineer specializing in sapping
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsæpər / (AmE), / ˈsapə / (BrE)
a soldier responsible for tasks such as digging trenches, undermining fortifications, and constructing fieldworks; a military engineer specializing in sapping and related works.

Examples

A sapper soldier
Becoming a sapper requires both technical skill and bravery under fire.
During the siege, sappers worked day and night to dig trenches under enemy walls.
The army deployed a sapper unit to clear mines ahead of the infantry.
