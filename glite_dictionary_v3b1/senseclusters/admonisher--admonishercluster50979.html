admonisher

noun
someone who warns or advises
8.9
Pronunciation: / ædˈmɑːnɪʃər / (AmE), / ədˈmɒnɪʃə / (BrE)
a person who warns or advises someone against making a mistake

Examples

An admonisher warns
As an admonisher, she always pointed out potential pitfalls before we committed to a plan.
Every group needs an admonisher to keep everyone on the right path.
The teacher acted as an admonisher, reminding students to think before they speak.
