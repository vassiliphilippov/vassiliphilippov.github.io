caragana

noun
a genus of shrubs or small trees with yellow flowers
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌkærəˈɡænə / (AmE), / ˌkarəˈɡanə / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree of the genus Caragana, with even-pinnate leaves and mostly yellow flowers producing seeds in linear pods, native to Asia and eastern Europe

Examples

A caragana shrub
In some regions, caragana is used as a drought-resistant ornamental shrub.
Several caragana bushes were planted along the eastern fence to act as a windbreak.
The caragana produces bright yellow flowers in late spring each year.
