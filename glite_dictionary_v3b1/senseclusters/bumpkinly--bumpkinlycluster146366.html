bumpkinly

adjective
awkwardly simple or provincial
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌmp.kɪn.li / (AmE), / ˈbʌmp.kɪn.li / (BrE)
Having the manner or appearance of a bumpkin; simple, awkward, or provincial in style or behavior.

Examples

Simple bumpkinly manners
Despite his bumpkinly manners, he was quick-witted and kind.
The guest wore a bumpkinly outfit that contrasted with the elegant attire of everyone else.
The play's humor relies on the bumpkinly behavior of its rural characters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cosmopolitan - familiar with and at ease in many different countries and cultures
  • synonym: rustic - relating to the countryside; simple or unsophisticated
