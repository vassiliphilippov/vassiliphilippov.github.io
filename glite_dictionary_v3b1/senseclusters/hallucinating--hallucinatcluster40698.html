hallucinating

adjective
AI producing false outputs
7.9
Pronunciation: /hə.ˈluː.sɪ.neɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /hə.ˈluː.sə.neɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /hə.ˈluː.sɪ.neɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /hə.ˈluː.sə.neɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
(in computing, especially with AI) producing fabricated, incorrect, or ungrounded information presented as if it were true.

Examples

AI hallucinating facts
During testing, the language model kept hallucinating citations for articles that do not exist.
Many users complained the chatbot was hallucinating and gave confidently wrong answers about legal matters.
After the update, engineers noticed the model started hallucinating realistic but false company statistics.
