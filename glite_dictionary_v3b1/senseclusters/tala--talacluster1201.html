tala

noun
Samoa's currency unit
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːlə / (AmE), / ˈtɑːlə / (BrE)
the standard monetary unit of Samoa, equal to 100 sene.

Examples

One hundred tala
He paid a hundred tala for the handcrafted necklace.
The price of the meal was fifteen tala.
Tourists exchanged their dollars for tala at the airport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dollar - standard unit of currency in several countries, including the US
