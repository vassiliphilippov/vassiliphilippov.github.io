cunningness

noun
clever deceit
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌnɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˈkʌnɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being clever at achieving goals through deceit or evasion

Examples

Criminal's cunningness
The detective admired the criminal's cunningness in covering his tracks.
Her cunningness helped her negotiate a better deal than anyone expected.
He used his cunningness to outsmart his competitors in the business world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craftiness - skill in achieving one's aims by deceit
  • synonym: guile - clever and dishonest behavior to achieve something
  • antonym: honesty - the quality of being truthful and fair
