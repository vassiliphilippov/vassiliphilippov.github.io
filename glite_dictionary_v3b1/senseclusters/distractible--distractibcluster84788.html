distractible

adjective
easily distracted
5.8
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈstræk.tə.bəl/ /; / /dɪ.ˈstræk.tɪ.bəl/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈstræk.tə.bəl/ /; / /dɪ.ˈstræk.tɪ.bəl/ (BrE)
easily drawn away from what one is doing or thinking by other things

Examples

Easily distractible person
Because he is so distractible, he finds it hard to finish his homework on time.
The open office environment made even the most focused employees more distractible.
Some people are naturally less distractible and can concentrate for long periods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attentive - able to focus on something without being easily distracted
  • synonym: distracted - unable to concentrate because one's mind is preoccupied
