tubeless tire

noun
pneumatic tire without an inner tube
7.3 2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːbləs ˈtaɪər / (AmE), / ˈtjuːblɪs ˈtaɪə / (BrE)
a pneumatic tire that is made to be airtight without the need for an inner tube

Examples

Tubeless tires for bikes
Many cyclists prefer tubeless tires because they are less prone to punctures.
Switching to tubeless tires can also lead to a smoother ride on rough terrain.
The new car model is equipped with tubeless tires for improved safety and convenience.
