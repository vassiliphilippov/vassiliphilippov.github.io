archon

noun
supernatural ruler
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrkɑn / (AmE), / ˈɑːkɒn / (BrE)
a powerful supernatural or cosmic ruler, especially in Gnostic, mythological, or fantasy traditions

Examples

Cosmic archon
In the ancient myth, an archon governed the fate of the world from a hidden realm.
The fantasy novel describes a council of archons who control the universe's balance.
Some Gnostic texts speak of an archon as a powerful force opposing human freedom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deity - a god or divine being
  • synonym: overlord - a person or being with great power or authority over others
  • antonym: servant - a person or being who serves another
