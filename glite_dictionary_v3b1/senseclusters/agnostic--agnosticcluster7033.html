agnostic

adjective
platform-neutral; works across systems
Pronunciation: / æɡˈnɑstɪk / (AmE), / æɡˈnɒstɪk / (BrE)
Not specific to a particular platform, operating system, vendor, or technology; designed to work across different systems or environments.

Examples

Agnostic software design
The middleware is agnostic to the underlying OS, allowing cross-platform deployment.
We chose an agnostic API design so our app runs on desktops, phones, and tablets.
To support many clients, the team built an agnostic integration layer that normalizes data.

Related Terms

  • synonym: platform-independent - able to run on multiple computing platforms without modification
  • antonym: platform-specific - designed for or restricted to a particular operating system or platform
