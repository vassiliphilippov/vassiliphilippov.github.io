birdwatching

noun
observing wild birds
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːdˌwɑtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌwɒtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of observing wild birds in their natural environment for enjoyment or study

Examples

Birdwatching in nature
Many people travel to national parks for birdwatching during the spring migration season.
She brought her binoculars and field guide for an afternoon of birdwatching by the lake.
Local clubs often organize birdwatching trips to spot rare species in the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: birding - the practice of observing or identifying wild birds
  • synonym: ornithology - the scientific study of birds
