communicatively

adverb
in an open manner
4.5
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunɪkətɪvli / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪkətɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or relates to communication or sharing information

Examples

To smile communicatively
She smiled communicatively, encouraging everyone to join the discussion.
He nodded communicatively to show he understood the instructions.
The teacher spoke communicatively to make the students feel comfortable sharing their ideas.
The students worked communicatively to solve the problem together.
She always tries to participate communicatively in meetings to share her ideas.
Group projects are more successful when everyone acts communicatively and listens to each other.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expressively - in a way that shows thoughts or feelings clearly
  • synonym: openly - in a way that is honest and not secretive
  • antonym: secretively - in a way that hides information or feelings
  • antonym: silently - in a way that does not involve speaking or making noise
  • synonym: sociably - in a friendly and social manner
