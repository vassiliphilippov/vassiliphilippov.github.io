archiannelid

noun
small primitive marine worm
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːr.kiˈæn.ə.lɪd / (AmE), / ˌɑː.kiˈæn.ə.lɪd / (BrE)
A small, primitive marine worm lacking external segmentation, resembling polychaete larvae.

Examples

Small archiannelids worm
Most archiannelids inhabit sandy marine environments where their small size helps them move between sand grains.
Researchers study archiannelids to learn about the early evolution of segmented worms.
Unlike typical annelids, archiannelids do not show visible segmentation on the outside of their bodies.

Related Terms
