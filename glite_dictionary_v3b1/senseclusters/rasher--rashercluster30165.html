rasher

noun
thin slice of bacon
Pronunciation: / ˈræʃər / (AmE), / ˈræʃə / (BrE)
a thin slice of bacon or ham, usually cooked and eaten as part of a meal (especially breakfast)

Examples

A rasher of bacon
He ordered two eggs and a rasher of bacon for breakfast.
The chef placed a crispy rasher on top of the salad for extra flavor.
She fried a rasher of ham to go with her toast.
He placed a crispy rasher of bacon on his plate next to the eggs.
For breakfast, she enjoyed two rashers of ham with toast and tea.
The chef cooked several rashers until they were golden and delicious.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bacon - salted and smoked meat from the back or sides of a pig
  • synonym: ham - meat from the upper part of a pig's leg
  • synonym: slice - a thin, flat piece cut from something
