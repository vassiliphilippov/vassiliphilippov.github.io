Pyracantha

noun
thorny shrub with red or orange berries
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪrəˈkænθə / (AmE), / ˌpɪrəˈkanθə /; / ˌpɪərəˈkanθə / (BrE)
a thorny shrub with small white flowers and bright red or orange-red berries, also called firethorn
