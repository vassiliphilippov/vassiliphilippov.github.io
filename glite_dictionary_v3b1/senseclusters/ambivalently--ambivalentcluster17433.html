ambivalently

adverb
with mixed feelings
7.2
Pronunciation: / æmˈbɪvələntli / (AmE), / æmˈbɪvələntli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows mixed or conflicting feelings about something

Examples

To answer ambivalently
She answered ambivalently when asked if she wanted to move to a new city.
The committee reacted ambivalently to the proposal, unable to agree on its benefits.
He felt ambivalently about leaving his hometown for a better job opportunity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decisively - in a way that shows clear and firm decision
  • synonym: uncertainly - in a way that shows lack of certainty
