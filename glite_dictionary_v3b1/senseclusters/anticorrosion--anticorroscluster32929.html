anticorrosion

noun
corrosion prevention
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌæntikəˈroʊʒən /; / ˌænti.kəˈroʊʒən / (AmE), / ˌæntikəˈrəʊʒən /; / ˌænti.kəˈrəʊʒən / (BrE)
a substance, treatment, or process that prevents or reduces corrosion, especially on metal surfaces

Examples

Anticorrosion treatment
The engineers recommended an anticorrosion treatment for the steel beams in the new bridge.
Applying an anticorrosion coating can extend the lifespan of metal equipment exposed to moisture.
The shipyard uses advanced anticorrosion products to protect vessels from saltwater damage.
The new paint provides excellent anticorrosion protection for the steel bridge.
Engineers recommend regular anticorrosion treatments for vehicles in snowy regions.
Many pipelines are coated with anticorrosion materials to extend their lifespan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rustproofing - a process or treatment to prevent rust on metal
