legatine

noun
papal representative
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛɡəˌtaɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡətiːn / (BrE)
a papal legate or the office, authority, or jurisdiction of a papal legate

Examples

Legatine office
The bishop was granted legatine authority to oversee the council.
A legatine arrived from Rome to mediate the dispute between the two churches.
During the Middle Ages, a legatine often held significant power in local religious matters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: envoy - a person sent as a diplomatic representative
  • synonym: legate - an official representative sent by the pope
