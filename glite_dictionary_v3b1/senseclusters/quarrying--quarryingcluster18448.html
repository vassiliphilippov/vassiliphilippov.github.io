quarrying

noun
extraction of stone or slate from a quarry
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔːriɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkwɒriɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of extracting building stone, slate, or other materials from an open surface quarry

Examples

Quarrying building stone
Concerns about the environmental impact of quarrying have led to stricter regulations.
Modern quarrying techniques have greatly increased the efficiency of stone extraction.
The region's economy was once heavily dependent on quarrying granite and slate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excavation - the act of digging out material from the ground
  • synonym: mining - the extraction of minerals from the earth
