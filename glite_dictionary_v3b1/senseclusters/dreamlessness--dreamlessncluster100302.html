dreamlessness

noun
absence of dreams
Pronunciation: / ˈdriːmləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈdriːmləsnəs / (BrE)
the state of having no dreams, hopes, or imaginative aspirations

Examples

A life of dreamlessness
After years of routine work she felt a dull dreamlessness that made every day grey.
The novel explores the dreamlessness of a generation without hope for the future.
He was troubled by a sense of dreamlessness that left him feeling empty and unmotivated.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ambition - a strong desire to achieve something
  • synonym: apathy - lack of interest, enthusiasm, or concern
