two-piece

noun
woman's bathing suit with two parts
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtuː pis / (AmE), / ˈtuː piːs / (BrE)
a woman's bathing suit consisting of two separate garments, typically a bra top and brief bottoms

Examples

A two-piece swimsuit
I bought a new two-piece for my vacation in Hawaii.
She wore a stylish two-piece to the beach this summer.
The two-piece she chose was bright red with white polka dots.

Related Terms
