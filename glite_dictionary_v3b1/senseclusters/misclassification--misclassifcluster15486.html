misclassification

noun
incorrect categorization
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsklɑːsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of incorrectly placing something in the wrong category or group, especially in science, statistics, or general contexts
