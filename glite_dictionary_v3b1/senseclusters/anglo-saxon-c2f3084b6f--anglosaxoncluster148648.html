Anglo-Saxon

adjective
relating to Anglo-Saxons or their language
Pronunciation: / ˌæŋɡloʊ ˈsæksən / (AmE), / ˌæŋɡləʊ ˈsæksən / (BrE)
relating to the Anglo-Saxons, their language, or their culture, especially in early medieval England

Examples

Anglo-Saxon culture
He studied early Anglo-Saxon literature at university.
The museum features a rare collection of Anglo-Saxon jewelry and artifacts.
This village dates back to Anglo-Saxon times, making it one of the oldest in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Old English - the early form of the English language spoken by the Anglo-Saxons
