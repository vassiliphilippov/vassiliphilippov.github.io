brush

noun
bushy animal tail, esp. fox
7.1
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ / (AmE), / brʌʃ / (BrE)
a bushy tail or the bushy part of an animal's tail, especially that of a fox

Examples

A fox's brush
A trophy hunter displayed the silver-tipped brush with pride.
She admired the thick brush that marked the animal as a mature fox.
The fox's brush trailed behind it as it darted into the woods.
