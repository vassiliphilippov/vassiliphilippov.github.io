tank ship

noun
ship for transporting crude oil in bulk
2.5
Pronunciation: / tæŋk ʃɪp / (AmE), / tæŋk ʃɪp / (BrE)
a large ship designed to transport crude oil or other liquids in bulk.

Examples

A large tank ship
A new tank ship can carry more than two million barrels of oil.
After the oil spill, regulations for tank ships were tightened considerably.
The harbor was busy as another tank ship arrived loaded with crude oil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tanker - a ship, lorry, or aircraft designed to carry liquids in bulk
