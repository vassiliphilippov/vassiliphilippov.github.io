mojito

adjective
mint-lime flavored
7.5
Pronunciation: / moʊˈhiːtoʊ / (AmE), / məʊˈhiːtəʊ / (BrE)
flavored like or reminiscent of a mojito, typically with mint, lime, sugar, and sometimes rum

Examples

Mojito flavor cupcake
The bakery offered a mojito cupcake topped with lime zest and fresh mint leaves.
I tried a mojito ice cream that was both sweet and refreshing on a hot day.
They served a mojito mocktail that captured the classic flavors without any alcohol.
