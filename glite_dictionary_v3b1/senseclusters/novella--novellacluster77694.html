novella

noun
a short novel; intermediate-length fiction
4.0
Pronunciation: / nəˈvɛlə / (AmE), / nəˈvɛlə / (BrE)
a written work of fiction that is longer than a short story but shorter than a full-length novel

Examples

A novella story
Many famous authors have written at least one memorable novella during their careers.
She published a gripping novella about life in the Arctic last year.
The novella bridges the gap between a short story and a novel in complexity and length.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: novel - an extended work of fiction in prose
  • degree lower: short story - a brief fictional narrative
