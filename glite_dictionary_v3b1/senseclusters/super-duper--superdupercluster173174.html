super-duper

adjective, adverb
excellent; impressive
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈduːpər / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈduːpə / (BrE)
very good, excellent, or extremely (used informally to intensify how good or impressive something is)

Examples

A super-duper idea
That was a super-duper performance by the school choir last night.
He found a super-duper deal on a new laptop during the sale.
The kids were excited to try the super-duper ice cream sundae at the new shop.
The cake she baked was super-duper delicious and everyone wanted a second piece.
He was super-duper excited to see his favorite band perform live for the first time.
This phone is super-duper fast compared to my old one.
