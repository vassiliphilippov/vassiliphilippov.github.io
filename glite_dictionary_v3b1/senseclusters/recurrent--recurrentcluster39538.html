recurrent

adjective
happening repeatedly
4.7 2.91/m
Pronunciation: / r…™Ààk…ùÀêr…ônt / (AmE), / r…™Ààk år…ônt / (BrE)
Occurring or appearing repeatedly or periodically.

Examples

Recurrent problems
Flooding is a recurrent issue in this region during the rainy season.
The patient experienced recurrent headaches throughout the year.
There is a recurrent problem with the software crashing unexpectedly.

Synonyms

  • recurring
    3.3 0.00/m
    recurring happens again over time and can be used for many repeats, while recurrent means appearing repeatedly or periodically.
  • periodic
    3.9 7.30/m
    periodic occurs at regular fixed intervals, while recurrent means appearing repeatedly and may not always be exactly regular.
  • cyclical
    5.1 3.24/m
    cyclical follows a repeating cycle like seasons, while recurrent simply means something that returns or happens again.
  • repeated
    1.0 0.00/m
    repeated happens again one or more times and often lacks a pattern, while recurrent implies repeated occurrences over time or periodically.
