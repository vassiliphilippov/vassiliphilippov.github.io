Bethlehem

proper noun
town near Jerusalem; birthplace of Jesus
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛθlɪˌhɛm / (AmE), / ˈbɛθlɪhɛm / (BrE)
A small town near Jerusalem, traditionally regarded as the birthplace of Jesus and the early home of King David.

Examples

The town Bethlehem
King David is said to have been born in Bethlehem according to ancient tradition.
Many pilgrims travel to Bethlehem each year to visit the Church of the Nativity.
The biblical story of Bethlehem has influenced art and music for centuries.
