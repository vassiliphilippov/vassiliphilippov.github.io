neonatology

noun
branch of medicine for newborn infants
6.1 7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌni.oʊˌneɪˈtɑl.ə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌniː.əʊ.nəˈtɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
The branch of medicine that deals with the care, diagnosis, and treatment of newborn infants, especially the ill or premature.

Examples

Neonatology care unit
Advances in neonatology have greatly improved survival rates for newborns with serious conditions.
After medical school, she specialized in neonatology to care for premature babies.
The hospital's neonatology department is equipped with the latest technology to support critically ill infants.
