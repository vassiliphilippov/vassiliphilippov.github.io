Victorian

noun
person from Queen Victoria's reign
6.3
Pronunciation: / vɪkˈtɔɹ.i.ən / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔː.ri.ən / (BrE)
a person who lived during the reign of Queen Victoria, especially in Britain (1837–1901)

Examples

A Victorian writer
Many Victorians witnessed rapid technological changes during their lifetimes.
Some Victorians held very strict views on social etiquette.
The novel features a family of Victorians adjusting to the industrial revolution.
