superhuman

adjective
beyond human power or ability
1.0 6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
having powers, abilities, or qualities beyond those of a normal human; requiring more than human power, endurance, or capability.

Examples

Superhuman strength
Rescuers made a superhuman effort to save everyone from the burning building.
She endured superhuman levels of pain during her recovery from surgery.
The athlete showed superhuman strength in lifting the heavy weights after his injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heroic - having characteristics of a hero; very brave or great in scale
  • antonym: inhuman - lacking human qualities of compassion or mercy; cruel
