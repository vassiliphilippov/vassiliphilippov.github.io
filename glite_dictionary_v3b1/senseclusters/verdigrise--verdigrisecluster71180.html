verdigrise

verb
to develop green patina
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrdɪˌɡraɪz / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdɪˌɡraɪz / (BrE)
to become covered with or to cause to be covered with verdigris, a greenish patina that forms on copper, brass, or bronze surfaces.

Examples

To verdigrise a statue
The statue slowly verdigrised after years of exposure to rain and wind.
If left outside, copper roofs will eventually verdigrise and turn a distinctive green color.
The artist intentionally let the metal verdigrise to achieve an antique look for the sculpture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oxidize - to undergo or cause to undergo a reaction with oxygen
  • antonym: polish - to make a surface smooth and shiny by rubbing
  • synonym: tarnish - to lose or cause to lose luster, especially as a result of exposure to air or moisture
