rai

noun
Thai area unit
9.9
Pronunciation: / raɪ / (AmE), / raɪ / (BrE)
a unit of area used in Thailand, equal to 1,600 square meters

Examples

A rai of land
The farm covers ten rai of fertile land.
He bought a plot measuring five rai near the river.
The village school sits on two rai of land.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acre - a unit of area used in the US and UK, equal to 4,047 square meters
  • synonym: hectare - a metric unit of area equal to 10,000 square meters
