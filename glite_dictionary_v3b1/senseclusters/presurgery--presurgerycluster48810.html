presurgery

noun
time before surgery
Pronunciation: / priːˈsɜːrdʒəri / (AmE), / priːˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
the period or time immediately before a surgical operation

Examples

Presurgery period
The nurse explained the presurgery instructions to the patient before the operation.
Blood tests are often required during the presurgery phase to check for any health risks.
Anxiety is common in the presurgery period, so support from family is important.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postsurgery - the period after a surgical operation
  • synonym: preoperative - relating to the period before a surgical operation
