supply ship

noun
cargo spacecraft delivering supplies
Pronunciation: / səˈplaɪ ʃɪp / (AmE), / səˈplaɪ ʃɪp / (BrE)
(in spaceflight) a cargo spacecraft used to deliver supplies, equipment, or experiments to a space station or other spacecraft.

Examples

A supply ship brings equipment
The ISS mission relied on a supply ship to deliver experiments and provisions.
NASA launched a supply ship that carried food, scientific equipment, and spare parts to station.
An automated supply ship docked with the orbital lab after a precise autonomous approach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cargo spacecraft - a spacecraft designed to carry cargo, supplies, and equipment to orbiting stations or other spacecraft
  • synonym: resupply vehicle - a vehicle (often robotic) used to bring replacement supplies and equipment to a space station
