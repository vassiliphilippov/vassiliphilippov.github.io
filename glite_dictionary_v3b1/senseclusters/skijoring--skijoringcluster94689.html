skijoring

verb
to ski while pulled
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈskiː.dʒɑː.rɪŋ/ /; / /ˈskiː.dʒɔː.rɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈskiː.dʒɔː.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
