skijoring

verb
to ski while pulled
Pronunciation: / skiˈjɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / skiˈjɒrɪŋ / (BrE)
to travel on skis while being pulled by a horse, dog, or motor vehicle, as a sport or activity

Examples

To skijor with dogs
Every winter, they enjoy skijoring with their huskies across the snowy fields.
He tried skijoring for the first time, being pulled by a horse on a frozen track.
Many adventure seekers travel to Scandinavia to experience skijoring behind snowmobiles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skiing - to travel over snow on skis
  • synonym: sledding - to ride on a sled over snow or ice
