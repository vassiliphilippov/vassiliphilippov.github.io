mulcher

noun
machine for making mulch
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈmʌltʃə / (BrE)
a machine that grinds or shreds plant material into small pieces to make mulch

Examples

Garden mulcher
The gardener used a mulcher to process fallen leaves into useful mulch for the flower beds.
After trimming the trees, they fed the branches into the mulcher to reduce waste.
Renting a mulcher made it easy to clean up the yard after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composter - a device for turning organic waste into compost
  • synonym: shredder - a machine that cuts materials into small pieces
