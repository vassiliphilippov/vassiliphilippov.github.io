herself

pronoun
female reflexive pronoun
1.0
Pronunciation: / hərˈsɛlf / (AmE), / həˈsɛlf / (BrE)
the reflexive form of 'she', used when the female subject and object of a verb are the same person

Examples

Herself in the mirror
She looked at herself in the mirror before leaving for the party.
After the accident, she blamed herself for what happened.
Maria taught herself how to cook by watching online videos.

Related Terms

  • synonym: himself - the reflexive form of 'he'
  • synonym: themselves - the reflexive form of 'they'
