phlebogram

noun
X-ray image of a vein
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflibəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈflɛb.ə.ɡram / (BrE)
an X-ray image of a vein made after injection of a radiopaque contrast medium

Examples

A phlebogram image
A phlebogram can help diagnose deep vein thrombosis.
The doctor reviewed the phlebogram to assess the condition of the patient's veins.
They scheduled a phlebogram to get a clearer picture of the vascular blockage.
