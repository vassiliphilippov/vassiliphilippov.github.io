Ranchero

proper noun
vehicle model name
8.4
Pronunciation: / rænˈtʃɛroʊ / (AmE), / rænˈtʃɛərəʊ / (BrE)
the name of a two-door coupe utility vehicle model, especially the Ford Ranchero, produced in the United States in the mid-20th century

Examples

Classic Ranchero car
He spent years restoring his classic Ranchero to its original condition.
The car show featured a rare 1965 Ford Ranchero that attracted many enthusiasts.
She drove her grandfather's old Ranchero across the country last summer.

Related Terms
