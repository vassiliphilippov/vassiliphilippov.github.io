catatonic

adjective
showing catatonia (muscular rigidity or immobility)
9.4 7.1
Pronunciation: /kæt.ə.ˈtɑː.nɪk/ (AmE), /kæt.ə.ˈtɒ.nɪk/ (BrE)
in a state of immobility and unresponsiveness, often due to illness, shock, or a medical condition

Examples
