pigmy talinum

noun
southwestern US plant with pink ground-level flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡmi təˈlaɪnəm / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡmi təˈlaɪnəm / (BrE)
A low-growing plant native to the southwestern United States, with dense narrow succulent leaves and fairly large deep pink flowers that grow close to the ground.
