student nurse

noun
nurse in training
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːdənt nɜrs / (AmE), / ˈstjuːdənt nɜːs / (BrE)
a nurse who is still in training and has not yet qualified

Examples

A student nurse in training
As a student nurse, he must complete a set number of practical hours before graduating.
During her clinical placement, the student nurse learned how to administer injections safely.
The hospital assigns a mentor to each student nurse to help with their professional development.
