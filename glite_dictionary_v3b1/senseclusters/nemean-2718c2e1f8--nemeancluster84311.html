Nemean

adjective
of Nemea or its lion
Pronunciation: / nɪˈmiːən / (AmE), / nɪˈmiːən / (BrE)
relating to Nemea, an ancient site in Greece, or to the legendary Nemean lion of Greek mythology

Examples

Nemean lion legend
The Nemean Games were held in honor of Zeus at the ancient site of Nemea.
Legends tell of the Nemean lion, which could not be killed by mortal weapons.
The hero wore the Nemean lion's skin as a symbol of his strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herculean - relating to Hercules or requiring great strength
  • synonym: mythological - relating to myths or legends
