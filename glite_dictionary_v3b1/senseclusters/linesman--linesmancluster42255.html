linesman

noun
assistant referee in sports
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪnzmən / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnzmən / (BrE)
an official in sports such as tennis, soccer, or football, who assists the referee, especially by indicating when the ball or puck is out of bounds or when a player is offside

Examples

Soccer linesman
A heated argument erupted after the linesman ruled the ball out of play.
During the match, the linesman was praised for his quick and accurate decisions.
The linesman signaled that the player was offside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: referee - the main official who enforces the rules in sports
