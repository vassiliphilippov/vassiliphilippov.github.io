regrind

noun
recycled ground material
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌɡraɪnd / (AmE), / ˈriːˌɡraɪnd / (BrE)
material obtained by grinding used plastic, rubber, or metal again for reuse in manufacturing processes

Examples

Plastic regrind material
