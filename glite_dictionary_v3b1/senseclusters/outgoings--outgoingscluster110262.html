outgoings

noun
regular expenses
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌɡoʊɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌɡəʊɪŋz / (BrE)
regular payments or expenses that a person or organization must pay, such as bills or rent

Examples

Monthly outgoings
She keeps a record of all her monthly outgoings to manage her budget.
The company's outgoings increased after they moved to a larger office.
It is important to compare your income and outgoings when planning your finances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expenses - money spent on something
  • antonym: income - money received, especially on a regular basis
