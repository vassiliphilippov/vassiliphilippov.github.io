TSA

noun
US government transportation security agency
3.9
Pronunciation: / ti ɛs eɪ / (AmE), / tiː ɛs eɪ / (BrE)
an agency of the United States government established in 2001 responsible for the security of the nation's transportation systems, especially airports

Examples

TSA security screening
I had to arrive early at the airport to go through TSA security screening.
She showed her ID to the TSA agent before boarding the plane.
The TSA implemented new rules for carrying liquids on flights.
