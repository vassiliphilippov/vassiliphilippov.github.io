salad

noun
cold dish of mixed food, often with dressing
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsæl.əd / (AmE), / ˈsæl.əd / (BrE)
A dish consisting of a mixture of small pieces of food, usually including raw vegetables, typically served cold and often with a dressing.

Examples

A salad dish
He tossed the salad with olive oil and balsamic vinegar before serving.
She ordered a chicken Caesar salad for lunch.
The restaurant serves a fresh garden salad with every meal.
