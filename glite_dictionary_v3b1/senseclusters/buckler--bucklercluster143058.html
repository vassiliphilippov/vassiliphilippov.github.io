buckler

noun
small round shield for protection
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌk.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌk.lə / (BrE)
A small round shield held by a handle or worn on the forearm to intercept blows in combat.

Examples

A buckler shield
A buckler was often light enough to allow for quick movements in battle.
During the duel, both fighters carried a buckler on their left arms.
The knight raised his buckler to deflect the enemy's sword.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shield - broad protective device for defense in combat
