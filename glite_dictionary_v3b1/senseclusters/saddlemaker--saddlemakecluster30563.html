saddlemaker

noun
maker of saddles
5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsæd.əl.meɪ.kər/ (AmE), /ˈsæd.əl.meɪ.kə/ (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs saddles and other leather equipment for horses

Examples

The saddlemaker repairs saddles
The village saddlemaker crafted beautiful leather saddles for local riders.
After the strap broke, we took the bridle to the saddlemaker for repair.
Her grandfather was a skilled saddlemaker who worked with leather for decades.
The experienced saddlemaker used traditional tools to create a comfortable saddle.
After years of training, she became a skilled saddlemaker in her community.
The rider thanked the saddlemaker for repairing his favorite saddle so quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blacksmith - a person who makes or repairs things made of iron, especially for horses
  • synonym: harness maker - a person who makes or repairs harnesses for horses
  • synonym: saddler - a person who makes, repairs, or sells saddles and other horse equipment
