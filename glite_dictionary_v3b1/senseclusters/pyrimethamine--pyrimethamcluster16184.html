pyrimethamine

noun
antiparasitic drug
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪrəˈmɛθəˌmiːn / (AmE), / ˌpɪrɪˈmɛθəmiːn / (BrE)
a drug used to treat parasitic infections such as malaria and toxoplasmosis

Examples

pyrimethamine drug treatment
The doctor prescribed pyrimethamine to treat the infection.
Patients with toxoplasmosis are often given pyrimethamine as part of their treatment.
Resistance to pyrimethamine has become a concern in some regions affected by malaria.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antimalarial - a drug used to prevent or treat malaria
  • synonym: chloroquine - a medication used to prevent and treat malaria
