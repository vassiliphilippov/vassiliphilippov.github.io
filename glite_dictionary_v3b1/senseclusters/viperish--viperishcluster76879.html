viperish

adjective
spiteful or treacherous
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪpərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈvaɪpərɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling a viper, especially in being spiteful, treacherous, or malicious

Examples

Viperish remarks
Her viperish remarks made everyone in the meeting uncomfortable.
He flashed a viperish smile before revealing his true intentions.
The novel's antagonist is known for her viperish behavior toward her rivals.

  • synonym: malicious - intending to do harm
  • synonym: venomous - full of malice or spite
