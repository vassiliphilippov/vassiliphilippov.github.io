waggon

noun
long car with rear cargo space
3.2 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈwæg.ən/ (AmE), /ˈwæg.ən/ (BrE)
A car with a long body and a rear door, providing space behind the rear seat for cargo or passengers; also known as a station wagon.

Examples

A family waggon
She prefers driving a waggon because of its extra cargo space.
The family's old waggon easily fit all the groceries and luggage behind the seats.
They packed their bicycles into the back of the waggon for the weekend trip.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sedan - car with a separate compartment for luggage; no rear cargo space
  • synonym: station wagon - automobile with an extended rear cargo area and rear door
