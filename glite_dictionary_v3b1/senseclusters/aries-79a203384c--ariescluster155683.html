Aries

noun
zodiacal constellation between Pisces and Taurus
Pronunciation: / ˈɛriˌiz / (AmE), / ˈɛːriːz / (BrE)
a small constellation of the zodiac, located in the Northern Hemisphere between Pisces and Taurus

Examples

Aries constellation
Aries is located between Pisces and Taurus in the zodiac.
Many astronomers use Aries as a reference point when mapping stars in the northern sky.
The constellation Aries is best visible in the night sky during autumn and early winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Pisces - a zodiacal constellation next to Aries
  • synonym: Taurus - a zodiacal constellation adjacent to Aries
