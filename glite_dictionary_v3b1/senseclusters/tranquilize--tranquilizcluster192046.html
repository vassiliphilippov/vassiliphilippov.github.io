tranquilize

verb
make calm, often with a drug
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtræŋkwəˌlaɪz /; / træŋˈkwɪlaɪz /; / træŋˈkwɪˌlaɪz /; / ˈtræŋkwɪˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈtraŋkwɪlʌɪz /; / ˈtraŋkwɪlaɪz /; / træŋˈkwɪlaɪz /; / træŋˈkwɪlʌɪz /; / ˈtræŋkwɪlaɪz / (BrE)
to make a person or animal calm or quiet, especially by giving a drug

Examples

Doctors sometimes tranquilize patients before minor surgical procedures to reduce anxiety.
The team tranquilized the wild horse to transport it safely to the sanctuary.

The vet needed to tranquillise the wild dog before approaching it.
