missileman

noun
thrower of missiles
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsəlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmɪsaɪlˌmæn / (BrE)
an archaic term for a person who throws missiles, such as stones, spears, or other hand-thrown projectiles in battle

Examples

Ancient missileman
During the siege, the missileman stood atop the wall, ready to defend his city.
In ancient battles, a skilled missileman could turn the tide with well-aimed stones.
The story described a lone missileman bravely hurling spears at the advancing army.
