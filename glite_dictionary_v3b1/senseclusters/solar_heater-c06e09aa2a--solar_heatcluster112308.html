solar heater

noun
device that heats using solar energy
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊlər ˈhiːtər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə ˈhiːtə / (BrE)
A device that uses energy from the sun to generate heat, typically for heating water or air.

Examples

A solar heater system
Installing a solar heater can reduce your electricity bills significantly.
The solar heater provides enough hot water for the entire household even in winter.
They decided to invest in a solar heater for their swimming pool.

Related Terms

  • synonym: solar panel - a panel designed to absorb sunlight and generate electricity or heat
