brachiocephalic

adjective
of arm and head
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌbreɪkioʊsɪˈfælɪk / (AmE), / ˌbrækiəʊsɪˈfælɪk / (BrE)
relating to both the arm and the head, especially in anatomical contexts

Examples

Brachiocephalic artery
The brachiocephalic artery branches off the aorta to supply blood to the right side of the body.
Doctors examined the brachiocephalic vein for possible blockages after the surgery.
A brachiocephalic injury can affect both the arm and the head regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carotid - relating to the main arteries in the neck supplying blood to the head
  • synonym: subclavian - relating to the arteries or veins below the clavicle
