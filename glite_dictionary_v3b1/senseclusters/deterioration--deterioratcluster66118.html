deterioration

noun
gradual decline or worsening
3.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˌtɪriəˈreɪʃən /; / dəˌtɪriəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌtɪəriəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of becoming worse in quality, condition, or strength

Examples

Air deterioration
Experts are concerned about the deterioration in air quality over recent years.
His health showed signs of deterioration after the long illness.
The deterioration of the building was evident from its crumbling walls and broken windows.
Doctors were concerned by the deterioration in his health over recent months.
Economic deterioration can lead to higher unemployment and poverty rates.
The rapid deterioration of the building was evident after years of neglect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decline - gradual and continuous loss of quality, strength, or value
  • antonym: improvement - the process of becoming better
