fashionableness

noun
quality of being fashionable
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfæʃ.ən.ə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfæʃ.ən.ə.b(ə)l.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being in accordance with current styles or trends; stylishness or elegance.

Examples

The fashionableness of clothes
Critics praised the fashionableness of the designer's spring collection.
He always keeps up with the fashionableness of the latest urban trends.
The fashionableness of her new dress drew many admiring glances at the gala.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elegance - the quality of being graceful and stylish in appearance or manner
  • synonym: fashionable - influenced by or in keeping with the latest style
  • antonym: unfashionableness - the quality of not being in accordance with current styles
