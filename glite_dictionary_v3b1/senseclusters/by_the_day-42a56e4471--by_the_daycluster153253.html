by the day

adverb
per day; each day
Pronunciation: / baɪ ðə deɪ / (AmE), / baɪ ðə deɪ / (BrE)
for each day; on a daily basis.

Examples

Charged by the day
Hotel rooms are usually charged by the day rather than by the hour.
She rents out her apartment by the day to tourists visiting the city.
The contractor was paid by the day rather than with a fixed salary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: per diem - for each day; by the day
