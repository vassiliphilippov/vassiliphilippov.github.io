unascribable

adjective
unable to be ascribed or attributed
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnəˈskraɪbəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnəˈskraɪbəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be ascribed or attributed to any particular cause, person, or source.

Examples

An unascribable cause
After much analysis, the phenomenon remained entirely unascribable.
Her success, though impressive, seemed unascribable to any one factor.
The damage was of an unascribable origin, baffling the experts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ascribable - able to be ascribed or attributed
  • antonym: attributable - able to be attributed to a cause or source
