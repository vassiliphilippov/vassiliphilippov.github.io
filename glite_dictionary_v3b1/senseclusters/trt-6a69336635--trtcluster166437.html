TRT

noun
testosterone therapy
6.8
Pronunciation: / ti ɑr ti / (AmE), / ti ɑː ti / (BrE)
an abbreviation for testosterone replacement therapy, a medical treatment for low testosterone levels in men

Examples

TRT treatment
After being diagnosed with low testosterone, he started TRT under his doctor's supervision.
Many men consider TRT to help with symptoms of fatigue and decreased muscle mass.
Before beginning TRT, patients should discuss potential risks and benefits with a healthcare provider.

Related Terms

  • synonym: HRT - hormone replacement therapy, often for women
  • synonym: testosterone replacement therapy - medical treatment to increase testosterone levels
