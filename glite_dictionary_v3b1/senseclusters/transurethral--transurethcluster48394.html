transurethral

adjective
through the urethra
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænzjʊˈriːθrəl / (AmE), / ˌtranzjʊˈriːθrəl / (BrE)
passing through or performed by way of the urethra

Examples

Transurethral procedure
The surgeon performed a transurethral procedure to remove the bladder tumor.
A transurethral approach is often used to treat certain prostate conditions.
Recovery after a transurethral operation is usually faster than with open surgery.
