underfeed

verb
to give too little food
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈfiːd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈfiːd / (BrE)
to give too little food to a person, animal, or plant

Examples

To underfeed a pet
If you underfeed your dog, it may become weak and unhealthy.
Farmers must be careful not to underfeed their livestock during the winter months.
The plant looked pale because it had been underfed for several weeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feed - to give food to a person, animal, or plant
  • antonym: overfeed - to give too much food to someone or something
  • degree higher: starve - to suffer or die from lack of food
