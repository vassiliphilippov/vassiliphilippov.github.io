parasol mushroom

noun
an edible long-stalked woodland mushroom
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpærəˌsɑl ˈmʌʃˌrum / (AmE), / ˈpærəsɒl ˈmʌʃrʊm / (BrE)
an edible mushroom with a long stalk, white flesh, white gills, and spores, typically found in open woodlands during autumn
