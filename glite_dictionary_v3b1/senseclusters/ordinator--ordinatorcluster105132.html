a person or official body that formally issues, enacts, or imposes orders, laws, or regulations

Examples

City ordinator issues rules
The ordinator issued new building codes that all developers had to follow.
As the chief ordinator, she was responsible for enacting the new safety regulations.
A local ordinator can impose rules that affect the entire community.
