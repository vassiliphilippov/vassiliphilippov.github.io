peep

verb
appear slightly from hiding
3.6
Pronunciation: / pip / (AmE), / piːp / (BrE)
to appear or emerge slightly or partially, as if from hiding

Examples

A flower peeps out
A tiny flower peeped out from between the rocks.
She saw a rabbit peep from its burrow before retreating.
The sun began to peep over the horizon at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emerge - to move out of or away from something and become visible
  • antonym: vanish - to disappear suddenly or completely
