setaceous

adjective
having bristles or bristle-like features
8.3
Pronunciation: / sɪˈteɪʃəs / (AmE), / sɪˈteɪʃəs / (BrE)
(of an animal or plant part) having bristles or bristle-like structures; bristly.

Examples

A setaceous caterpillar
Many caterpillars are setaceous, with rows of fine hairs covering their bodies.
Some plants develop setaceous leaves as a defense against herbivores.
The ornithologist noted the setaceous tips of the bird's tail feathers.
