run

noun
foot race; running competition
1.2
Pronunciation: / rʌn / (AmE), / rʌn / (BrE)
a race or act of running on foot

Examples

A charity run
He trained for months before entering the city’s annual charity run.
She won first place in the 400-meter run at the regional track meet.
The athlete set a new record during the final run of the competition.
After his morning run, he felt energized and ready for the day.
She completed a five-mile run to train for the upcoming marathon.
The children went for a quick run around the block before dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: race - a competition of speed, especially in running, riding, driving, or sailing
  • degree higher: sprint - a short race run at full speed
