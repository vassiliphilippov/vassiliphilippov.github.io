midcentury

adjective
middle of a century
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪdˈsɛn.tʃə.ri / (AmE), / ˌmɪdˈsɛn.tʃʊ.ri / (BrE)
relating to or typical of the middle of a century, especially the mid-20th century (about 1940–1960), often in reference to style or design

Examples

Midcentury style furniture
The house features midcentury furniture with clean lines and simple shapes.
Many people admire the midcentury style for its timeless appeal and functionality.
She decorated her living room in a midcentury aesthetic, using vintage pieces from the 1950s.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antique - from an earlier period; old-fashioned
  • synonym: contemporary - belonging to the same time period; modern
  • synonym: modernist - relating to modern style, especially in art or architecture
