cold storage

noun
state of being unused or postponed
5.9
Pronunciation: / koʊld ˈstɔɹ.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / kəʊld ˈstɔː.rɪdʒ / (BrE)
A state in which something is kept unused, inactive, or postponed, often temporarily.

Examples

Plans in cold storage
After the merger talks failed, the project was put into cold storage indefinitely.
His proposal remained in cold storage until the company's priorities changed.
Many creative ideas end up in cold storage when funding runs out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abeyance - temporary inactivity or suspension
  • synonym: on hold - postponed or suspended temporarily
