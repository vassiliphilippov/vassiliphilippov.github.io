banker's draft

noun
a cheque drawn by a bank, guaranteeing payment
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋkərz dræft / (AmE), / ˈbæŋkəz drɑːft / (BrE)
a cheque drawn by a bank on its own funds or on funds deposited in another bank, guaranteeing payment to the recipient

Examples

A banker's draft cheque
A banker's draft offers more security than a regular cheque when making large payments.
She visited her local branch to obtain a banker's draft for the house deposit.
The company requested payment by banker's draft rather than by personal cheque.
