folate

noun
a B vitamin essential for cell growth
5.8 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊleɪt / (AmE), / ˈfəʊleɪt / (BrE)
a B vitamin that is necessary for cell growth and reproduction, found naturally in leafy greens and other foods

Examples

Folate vitamin
A deficiency in folate can lead to certain types of anemia.
Many breakfast cereals are fortified with folate for added nutritional value.
Pregnant women are advised to increase their folate intake to support fetal development.

Related Terms
