appropriative

adjective
relating to taking for oneself
3.3 7.1
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈproʊ.prɪ.ə.tɪv/ (AmE), /ə.ˈprəʊ.prɪ.ə.tɪv/ (BrE)
relating to or characterized by the act of taking something, especially for oneself, often without permission.

Examples

An appropriative act
Her appropriative approach to storytelling sparked a debate among critics and readers alike.
The board condemned the appropriative use of indigenous symbols in the advertising campaign.
The museum criticized the artist's appropriative style for borrowing from other cultures without acknowledgment.
