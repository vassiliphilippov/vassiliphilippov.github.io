sternoclavicular

adjective
of breastbone and collarbone
Pronunciation: / ˌstɜrnoʊkləˈvɪkjələr / (AmE), / ˌstɜːnəʊkləˈvɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to the area or joint between the breastbone (sternum) and collarbone (clavicle)

Examples

sternoclavicular joint pain
The athlete suffered a sternoclavicular injury during the football match.
Swelling in the sternoclavicular joint can indicate inflammation or trauma.
Doctors checked the sternoclavicular area for any signs of dislocation after the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acromioclavicular - relating to the joint between the acromion and the clavicle
