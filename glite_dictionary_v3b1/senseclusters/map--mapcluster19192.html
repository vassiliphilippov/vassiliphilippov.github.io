map

verb
establish a correspondence between sets (math)
6.1
Pronunciation: / mæp / (AmE), / mæp / (BrE)
(mathematics) to establish a correspondence between elements or sets, especially by means of a defined function or relationship.

Examples

To map numbers
The function maps each real number to its square.
The mathematician mapped the set of natural numbers to the set of even numbers.
We can map this complex structure onto a simpler one for analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: correspond - to be similar or equivalent in character, form, or function
