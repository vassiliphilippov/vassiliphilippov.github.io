daily double

noun
bet on winners of two races in one day
8.3 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdeɪ.li ˈdʌb.əl/ (AmE), /ˈdeɪ.li ˈdʌb.əl/ (BrE)
A single bet in which the bettor selects the winners of two designated horse races to be run on the same day.
