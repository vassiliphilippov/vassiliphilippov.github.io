psychotomimetic

adjective
causing psychosis-like effects
9.9
Pronunciation: / saɪˌkɑtəˌmɪˈmɛtɪk / (AmE), / saɪˌkɒtəʊmɪˈmɛtɪk / (BrE)

Psychotomimetic effects
