journey

noun
act of traveling from one place to another
1.1 59/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd í…ùÀêni / (AmE), / Ààd í…úÀêni / (BrE)
An act of traveling from one place to another, especially over a long distance.

Examples

A long journey

She documented every stage of her journey through Asia in her travel blog.
The journey from childhood to adulthood can be challenging and rewarding.
