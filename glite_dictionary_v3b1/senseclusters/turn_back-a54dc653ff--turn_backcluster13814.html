turn back

verb
reverse or turn inside out
Pronunciation: / tɝn bæk / (AmE), / tɜːn bæk / (BrE)
To turn something so that the inside is on the outside or to reverse its orientation.

Examples

Turn back a sleeve
He turned back the shirt to check the stitching on the inside.
She turned back the rug to look for the label underneath.
You need to turn back the blanket before you wash it, so the tags are visible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invert - turn upside down or inside out
