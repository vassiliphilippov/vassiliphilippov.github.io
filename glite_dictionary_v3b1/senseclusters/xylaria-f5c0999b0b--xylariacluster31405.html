Xylaria

noun
a genus of fungi with woody, black fruiting bodies
Pronunciation: / zaɪˈlɛəriə / (AmE), / zaɪˈlɑːrɪə / (BrE)
a genus of fungi in the family Xylariaceae, known for erect, black, woody fruiting bodies with perithecia in the upper parts
