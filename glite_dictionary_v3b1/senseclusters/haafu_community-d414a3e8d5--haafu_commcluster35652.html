haafu community

noun
people of mixed heritage in Japan
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑː.fuː kəˌmjuː.nə.ti / (AmE), / ˈhɑː.fuː kəˌmjuː.nɪ.ti / (BrE)
A group of people in Japan who are of mixed Japanese and non-Japanese heritage.

Examples

The haafu community in Japan
Many in the haafu community are working to challenge traditional views of Japanese identity.
The haafu community in Tokyo often holds cultural exchange events.
