slipknot

noun
a knot that slips easily when pulled
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪp.nɑt / (AmE), / ˈslɪp.nɒt / (BrE)
a knot that can easily be untied by pulling one end of the rope or cord

Examples

A slipknot knot
Before starting the project, she demonstrated how to make a simple slipknot with yarn.
He tied a slipknot so that the rope could be easily undone.
The slipknot allows the loop to tighten or loosen quickly.
