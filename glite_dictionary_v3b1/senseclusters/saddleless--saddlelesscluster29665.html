saddleless

adjective
without a saddle
Pronunciation: / ˈsædələs / (AmE), / ˈsædələs / (BrE)
not equipped with a saddle; without a saddle

Examples

Saddleless horse
She rode the horse saddleless across the field, feeling every movement beneath her.
Performing saddleless tricks requires great balance and confidence from the rider.
The trainer demonstrated how to mount a saddleless pony safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bareback - riding a horse without a saddle
  • antonym: saddled - equipped with a saddle
