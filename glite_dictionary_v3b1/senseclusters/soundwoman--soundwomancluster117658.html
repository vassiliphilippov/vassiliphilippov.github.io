soundwoman

noun
female sound technician
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊndˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈsaʊndˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to amplify sound or produce sound effects, especially for television or radio broadcasts

Examples

She takes pride in her work as a soundwoman for live sporting events.
