Synonyms

  • fix
    1.0 61/m
    fix is a common, direct action to make something work again by repairing parts, while repair is the broader act of restoring items.
  • restore
    3.5 15/m
    restore is to return something to its original or usable condition, while repair focuses on fixing what is broken or not working.
  • recondition
    5.4 0.32/m
    recondition means to overhaul or renew an item so it works like new, while repair can be smaller or less thorough.
  • mend
    1.2 2.01/m
    mend often describes fixing small tears or joins, especially in clothes or soft things, while repair covers many kinds of fixes.
  • patch up
    3.4 0.43/m
    patch up means to make a quick, temporary repair with a patch or simple fix, while repair aims to fully restore function.
