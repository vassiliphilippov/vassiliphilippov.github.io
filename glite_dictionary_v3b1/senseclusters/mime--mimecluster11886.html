mime

noun
performance using gestures, not speech
Pronunciation: / maɪm / (AmE), / maɪm / (BrE)
a theatrical performance in which meaning is conveyed through gestures and body movements without spoken words

Examples

A mime show
At the festival, a group presented a classic mime piece about being trapped in an invisible box.
Children laughed while watching the street artist's mime in the park.
The actor performed a brilliant mime that captivated the entire audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pantomime - a dramatic entertainment in which performers express meaning through gestures without speech
