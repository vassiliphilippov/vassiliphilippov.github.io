postharvest

adjective
after crop gathering
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈhɑrvəst / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈhɑːvɪst / (BrE)
relating to the period or activities after crops have been gathered from the fields

Examples

Postharvest storage methods
Proper postharvest storage is essential to prevent spoilage of fruits and vegetables.
The postharvest treatment of grains can significantly affect their quality and shelf life.
Researchers are developing new postharvest technologies to reduce food loss in developing countries.
