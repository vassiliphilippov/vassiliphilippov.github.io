muang

noun
Southeast Asian principality
9.9
Pronunciation: / muːˈɑːŋ / (AmE), / muːˈɑːŋ / (BrE)
a traditional city, town, or administrative district in parts of Southeast Asia, especially in Thailand and Laos, often historically ruled by a local leader
