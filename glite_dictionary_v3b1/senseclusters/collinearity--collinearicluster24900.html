collinearity

noun
points on one line
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊlɪniˈærəti / (AmE), / ˌkɒlɪniˈærəti / (BrE)
the condition of points or objects lying on the same straight line

Collinearity of points
The collinearity of the three cities made it easy to draw a straight road between them.
In geometry, collinearity is an important concept when analyzing the position of points.
The architect checked for collinearity to ensure the columns were perfectly aligned.
