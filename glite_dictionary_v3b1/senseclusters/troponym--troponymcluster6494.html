troponym

noun
a verb denoting manner or method of action
Pronunciation: / ˈtroʊpəˌnɪm / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpənɪm / (BrE)
a word that denotes a particular manner or method of performing an action, typically in relation to a more general verb

Examples

A troponym of speak
'Whisper' can be considered a troponym of 'speak', as it specifies a quiet way of speaking.
In linguistics, analyzing troponyms helps us understand subtle differences in verb meanings.
The word saunter is a troponym of 'walk', indicating a leisurely manner of walking.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hypernym - a word of more general meaning under which more specific words fall
