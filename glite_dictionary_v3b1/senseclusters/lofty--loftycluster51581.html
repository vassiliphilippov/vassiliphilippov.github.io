lofty

adjective
of great or impressive height
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔfti / (AmE), / ˈlɒfti / (BrE)
Of great height; impressively tall, especially so as to stand out above others.

Examples

Lofty tower
From the balcony, she admired the lofty spires of the ancient castle.
The lofty trees in the forest seemed to touch the sky.
The climbers gazed up at the lofty peaks ahead, feeling both awe and excitement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low estimate of importance or height
  • synonym: towering - extremely high or tall, especially standing out above others
