bay laurel

noun
Mediterranean evergreen tree with aromatic leaves
7.5 4.5
Pronunciation: /beɪ ˈlɔː.rəl/ /; / /beɪ ˈlɑː.rəl/ (AmE), /beɪ ˈlɒ.rəl/ /; / /beɪ ˈlɔː.rəl/ (BrE)
A small Mediterranean evergreen tree (Laurus nobilis) with aromatic glossy leaves used in cooking and, in ancient times, for making ceremonial crowns.
