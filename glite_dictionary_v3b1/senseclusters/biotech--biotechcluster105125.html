biotech

noun
biological technology industry
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪoʊˌtɛk / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəʊˌtɛk / (BrE)
a company or industry that uses biological science and technology to develop products, especially in medicine and agriculture

Examples

Biotech company
Several biotech startups are developing new ways to treat rare diseases.
The biotech sector has attracted significant investment in recent years.
She works for a leading biotech company focused on agricultural innovation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biotechnology - the use of living systems and organisms to develop products
  • synonym: pharmaceutical - a company or product related to the manufacture of drugs and medicines
