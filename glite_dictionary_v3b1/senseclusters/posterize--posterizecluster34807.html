posterize

verb
to dunk over opponent
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstəˌraɪz / (BrE)
to perform a spectacular dunk over an opponent in basketball, often in a way that humiliates them or creates a memorable highlight

Examples

To posterize a defender
The rookie posterized the veteran center with an incredible slam in the third quarter.
Fans cheered as she posterized her opponent, making the highlight reel for the week.
He hopes to posterize someone in tonight's big game and make a name for himself.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dunk - to score by thrusting the ball down through the basket
