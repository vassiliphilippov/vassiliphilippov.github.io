Siberia

proper noun
large Russian region in northern Asia
Pronunciation: / saɪˈbɪriə / (AmE), / saɪˈbɪəriə / (BrE)
a vast region in Russia, stretching from the Ural Mountains to the Pacific Ocean, known for its long, cold winters

Examples

Siberia region
He spent two years working in Siberia as part of a geological survey team.
Temperatures in Siberia can drop below minus forty degrees Celsius in winter.
The vast forests and wilderness of Siberia are home to many unique species.
