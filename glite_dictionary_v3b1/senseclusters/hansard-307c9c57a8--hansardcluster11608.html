Hansard

noun
official report of parliamentary proceedings
Pronunciation: / ˈhænˌsɑrd / (AmE), / ˈhænzɑːd / (BrE)
the official published record of the proceedings and debates of a parliamentary body, especially the British Parliament

Examples

The Hansard record
Debates from yesterday are now available in the Hansard for public review.
Historians often consult the Hansard to trace legislative changes over time.
The Minister's statement was recorded in the Hansard and is part of the official record.
