dramatic

adjective
related to drama or theatrical style
Pronunciation: / drəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / drəˈmatɪk /; / drəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to drama, theatre, or acting; intended to attract attention or create excitement; striking, showy, or impressive
