Synonyms

  • theatrical
    4.8 6.98/m
    theatrical means like an actor on stage, showing practiced emotion, while dramatic also covers general striking or exciting events.
  • spectacular
    3.5 20/m
    spectacular is very large and exciting to watch, while dramatic can be smaller and may mean simply striking or emotional.
  • striking
    3.8 5.88/m
    striking draws the eye by being unusual or beautiful, while dramatic often adds action, emotion, or a sense of theatre.
  • stagey
    8.7 0.06/m
    stagey feels artificial and overdone like a show, while dramatic can be sincere, planned for effect, or tied to theatre.
