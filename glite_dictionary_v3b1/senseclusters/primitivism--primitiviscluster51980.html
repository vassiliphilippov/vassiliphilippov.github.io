primitivism

noun
a wild or unrefined state
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪm.ɪ.tɪˌvɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈprɪm.ɪ.tɪ.vɪz.əm / (BrE)
the condition or quality of being wild, crude, or unrefined; a state lacking sophistication or modern development

Examples

Cultural primitivism
He contrasted the primitivism of the ancient ways with the complexity of modern society.
Some artists sought inspiration in what they saw as the primitivism of nature untouched by technology.
The primitivism of the tribe's early rituals fascinated the visiting anthropologists.

Related Terms

  • antonym: civilization - an advanced state of human society
