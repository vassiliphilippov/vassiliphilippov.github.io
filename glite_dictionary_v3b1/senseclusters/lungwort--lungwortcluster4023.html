lungwort

noun
lichen on tree bark
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌŋˌwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈlʌŋˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a large, leafy lichen (Lobaria pulmonaria) that grows on tree bark in old, unpolluted forests

Examples

Forest lungwort lichen
The forester pointed out a rare patch of lungwort clinging to the trunks of ancient oak trees.
Scientists study lungwort to monitor air quality in old-growth forests.
The vibrant green of lungwort stands out against the rough, dark bark of the trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lichen - a composite organism made of fungi and algae living together
