rockered

adjective
with curved runners
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑkərd / (AmE), / ˈrɒkəd / (BrE)
fitted with curved runners (rockers) that allow rocking back and forth, especially referring to furniture like chairs or cradles

Examples

Rockered chair
The old nursery had a rockered cradle that soothed babies to sleep.
He bought a beautifully rockered chair for the front porch.
Many antique pieces are rockered to provide gentle movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rocking - moving gently back and forth
  • antonym: stationary - not moving or rocking
