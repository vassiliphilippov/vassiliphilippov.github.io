unmixable

adjective
not suitable for DJ mixing
8.6
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈmɪk.sə.bəl/ /; / /ən.ˈmɪk.sə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈmɪk.sə.bəl/ /; / /ən.ˈmɪk.sə.bəl/ (BrE)
(in music and DJ culture) not able to be mixed smoothly with other tracks because of incompatible tempo, key, structure, or production, making transitions awkward or impossible.

Examples

Unmixable tracks
The DJ refused to play the track because it was entirely unmixable with the set.
Many producers mark songs unmixable if their tempo or key changes make transitions awkward.
He learned the track was unmixable only after trying to sync the beats live.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incompatible - not able to exist or work together without conflict; in mixing contexts, causing awkward or impossible transitions
  • antonym: mixable - able to be blended smoothly with other tracks or elements in a DJ set or mix
