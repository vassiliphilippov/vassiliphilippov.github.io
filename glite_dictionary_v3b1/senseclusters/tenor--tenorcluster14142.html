tenor

noun
highest adult male singing voice
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnɚ /; / ˈtɛnər / (AmE), / ˈtɛnə / (BrE)
the highest natural adult male singing voice, or a male singer with this voice range (between baritone and alto)

Related Terms

  • degree lower: baritone - the adult male singing voice below tenor and above bass
