packwoman

noun
female itinerant seller or peddler
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpækˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈpakˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who travels about selling goods, especially on the streets or at fairs and carnivals

Examples

A packwoman selling goods
In the market, a packwoman displayed bright ribbons and trinkets from her cart.
Stories were told of a wandering packwoman who brought exotic spices and tales from distant lands.
The old packwoman traveled from village to village selling her handmade baskets.
