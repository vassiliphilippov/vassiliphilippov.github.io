underwhelmed

adjective
not impressed
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈwɛlmd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈwɛlmd / (BrE)
feeling less impressed or excited than expected, often disappointed by something that was supposed to be impressive

Examples

Be underwhelmed by a movie
Many fans were underwhelmed by the movie after all the hype.
He felt underwhelmed by the meal, expecting something much more flavorful.
Despite the glowing reviews, I was underwhelmed by the concert's performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disappointed - feeling let down or not satisfied
  • antonym: impressed - feeling admiration or respect for something
