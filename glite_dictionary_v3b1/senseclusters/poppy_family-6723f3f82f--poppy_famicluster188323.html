poppy family

noun
family of flowering plants; Papaveraceae
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑpi ˌfæməli / (AmE), / ˈpɒpi ˌfæməli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants, including herbs and shrubs, characterized by milky and often colored sap and capsular fruits; scientifically known as Papaveraceae
