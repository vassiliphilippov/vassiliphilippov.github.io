impostor

noun
person who deceives by pretending to be someone else
1.5
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɑstər / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɒstə / (BrE)
a person who pretends to be someone else in order to deceive others, usually for personal gain

Examples

An impostor pretends
He felt like an impostor after receiving the award, doubting whether he truly deserved it.
Many online scams are perpetrated by an impostor pretending to be a trusted individual.
The police discovered that the new employee was an impostor using a false identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fraud - a person who deceives others for personal gain
  • antonym: genuine person - someone who is what they claim to be
  • synonym: swindler - a person who cheats or defrauds others
