euchre

noun
trick-taking card game
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːkɚ / (AmE), / ˈjuːkə / (BrE)
A card game for usually four players, played with a reduced deck, in which players aim to win at least three out of five tricks, with a trump suit determined on each deal.

Examples

A game of euchre
Every Friday night, the neighbors gather to play a lively game of euchre.
My grandfather taught me the rules of euchre when I was a child.
She lost three hands in a row at euchre but kept her spirits high.
