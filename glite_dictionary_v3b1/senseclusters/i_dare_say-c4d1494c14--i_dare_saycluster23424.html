I dare say

verb
to express likelihood
Pronunciation: / aɪ dɛr seɪ / (AmE), / aɪ deə seɪ / (BrE)
used to express that you think something is likely or probably true, often politely or tentatively

Examples

I dare say he will
I dare say he will join us for dinner if he finishes work early.
I dare say you are right about the weather turning cold soon.
It was a difficult exam, but I dare say you did quite well.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guess - to estimate or believe something without being certain
  • synonym: suppose - to think or believe something is true or possible
