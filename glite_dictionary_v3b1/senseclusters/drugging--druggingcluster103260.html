drugging

noun
the act of administering a drug
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrʌɡɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of administering a drug, especially a sedative, to someone, often without their knowledge

Examples

Secret drugging at parties
Reports of drugging people at parties have increased in recent years.
She was wary of accepting drinks from strangers to avoid the risk of drugging.
The criminal was charged with drugging his victim to commit the robbery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doping - the use of drugs to enhance performance, especially in sport
  • antonym: poisoning - the act of giving a person a harmful or lethal substance
