glander

verb
to infect with glanders
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlændər / (AmE), / ˈɡlændə / (BrE)
to infect an animal, especially a horse, with glanders, a contagious disease

Examples

To glander a horse
The veterinarian warned not to glander the healthy horses during the examination.
Strict hygiene measures are necessary to prevent workers from accidentally glandering the livestock.
Researchers studied how easily one infected animal could glander an entire stable.
