wall hanging

noun
decorative textile or object for a wall
7.5
Pronunciation: / wɑl ˈhæŋɪŋ / (AmE), / wɔːl ˈhæŋɪŋ / (BrE)
a decorative piece, such as a tapestry or textile, designed to be hung on a wall or over a window

Examples

A wall hanging decoration
She bought a beautiful wall hanging to brighten up her living room.
The museum displayed a medieval wall hanging woven with intricate patterns.
They decided to make their own wall hanging using colorful fabrics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curtain - a piece of material hung to cover a window or doorway
  • synonym: tapestry - a thick textile fabric with decorative designs, used as a hanging or covering
