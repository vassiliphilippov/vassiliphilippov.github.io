immaterially

adverb
spiritually; not physically
Pronunciation: / ˌɪməˈtɪriəli / (AmE), / ˌɪməˈtɪəriəli / (BrE)
in a way that is not physical or composed of matter; spiritually or without material substance

Examples

Exist immaterially
The soul is believed by some to exist immaterially beyond the body.
He felt immaterially connected to the universe during meditation.
The story describes a ghost that moves immaterially through walls and doors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: physically - in a way relating to the body or material things
  • synonym: spiritually - in a way relating to the spirit or soul, not the body
