stereo photography

noun
photography producing 3D images using stereoscope
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛrioʊ fəˌtɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˈstɪəriəʊ fəˈtɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
Photography that produces images with the illusion of depth and solidity, created by viewing two slightly different photographs of the same scene with a stereoscope.

Examples

Stereo photography images
Advancements in digital technology have revived interest in stereo photography among modern photographers.
He demonstrated stereo photography techniques at the science fair using a homemade stereoscope.
The museum's collection includes rare examples of stereo photography from the Victorian era.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stereoscopy - the technique of creating or enhancing the illusion of depth in an image
