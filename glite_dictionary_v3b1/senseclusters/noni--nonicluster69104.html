noni

noun
tropical fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊni / (AmE), / ˈnəʊni / (BrE)
a tropical fruit from Southeast Asia, known for its strong smell and use in juices and supplements

Examples

Noni fruit
Many people drink noni juice because they believe it has health benefits.
The market sells fresh noni alongside other tropical fruits like mango and papaya.
Some supplements contain extracts from the noni fruit for their supposed medicinal properties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fruit - the sweet and fleshy product of a tree or plant that contains seeds
  • synonym: morinda - a genus of tropical plants including the noni tree
