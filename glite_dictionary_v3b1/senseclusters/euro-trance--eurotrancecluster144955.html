euro-trance

noun
European style of trance music
Pronunciation: / ˌjʊəroʊˈtræns / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəʊˈtræns / (BrE)
A style of electronic trance music originating in Europe, combining elements of hard trance and Eurodance.

Examples

Euro-trance music style
Many clubs in the late 1990s featured euro-trance nights that attracted large crowds.
Modern festivals often revisit classic euro-trance tracks for nostalgic fans.
The DJ played a mix of euro-trance and hardhouse during his set.
