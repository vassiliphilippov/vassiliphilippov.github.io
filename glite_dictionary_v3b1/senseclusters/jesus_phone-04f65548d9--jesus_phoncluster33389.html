Jesus phone

noun
slang for a highly revered smartphone, esp. iPhone
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːzəs foʊn / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːzəs fəʊn / (BrE)
(slang) A smartphone, especially an iPhone, regarded as having special status, spiritual significance, or being highly desirable.

Examples

The Jesus phone
He waited in line all night to buy the latest Jesus phone.
Many reviewers called the original iPhone the first true Jesus phone of its generation.
The new Jesus phone sold out within hours of its release.
