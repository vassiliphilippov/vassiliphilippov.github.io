hydrograph

noun

7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈhaɪ.drə.græf/ /; / /ˈhaɪ.droʊ.græf/ (AmE), /ˈhaɪ.drə.grɑːf/ /; / /ˈhaɪ.drəʊ.grɑːf/ (BrE)
