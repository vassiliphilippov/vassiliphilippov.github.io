Supreme Being

noun
the monotheistic God; creator and ruler of the universe
Pronunciation: / səˈprim ˈbiːɪŋ / (AmE), / sjuːˈpriːm ˈbiːɪŋ / (BrE)
the perfect, all-powerful, and all-knowing creator and ruler of the universe; the object of worship in monotheistic religions

Examples

The Supreme Being creator
Many philosophers have argued for the existence of a Supreme Being as the creator of the universe.
Monotheistic religions typically regard a Supreme Being as both omnipotent and omniscient.
She sought guidance and strength through her faith in the Supreme Being.
