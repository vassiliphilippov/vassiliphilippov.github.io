brown-noser

noun
excessive flatterer
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbraʊnˌnoʊzər / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnˌnəʊzə / (BrE)
a person who tries excessively to please or flatter someone in authority to gain favor

Examples

A brown-noser at work
Everyone in the office knew Mark as a brown-noser who always complimented the boss.
She was called a brown-noser because she agreed with everything the manager said.
No one likes a brown-noser who tries too hard to impress the teacher.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rebel - a person who resists authority or control
  • synonym: sycophant - a person who acts obsequiously toward someone important
  • synonym: toady - a person who flatters or ingratiates themselves with others for personal gain
