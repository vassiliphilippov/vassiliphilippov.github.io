go-to

noun
person or thing regularly relied on
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊ tu / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ tuː / (BrE)
a person or thing that is regularly relied on or chosen for a particular purpose or in a particular situation

Examples

My go-to person
Chocolate is my go-to snack when I'm feeling stressed.
For quick lunches, sandwiches are always my go-to.
Whenever I need advice, Sarah is my go-to.
