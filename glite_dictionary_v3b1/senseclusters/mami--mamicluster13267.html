mami

noun
informal term for mother
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːmi / (AmE), / ˈmɑːmi / (BrE)
an affectionate or informal term for mother, especially used in Spanish-speaking families

Examples

My mami at home
Every morning, I say goodbye to my mami before heading to school.
My mami makes the best homemade soup when I am sick.
During the holidays, the whole family gathers at mami's house for dinner.
