civilly

adverb
in a polite or courteous manner
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvəli / (AmE), / ˈsɪvəli / (BrE)
In a polite or courteous manner; showing good manners and respect.

Examples

Speak civilly to others
After the divorce, they continued to speak civilly for the sake of their children.
Even though they disagreed, both parties debated civilly throughout the meeting.
She always greets her neighbors civilly, regardless of past arguments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rudely - in an impolite or disrespectful manner
