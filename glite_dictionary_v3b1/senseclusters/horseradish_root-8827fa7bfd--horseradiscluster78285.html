horseradish root

noun
root used as a pungent seasoning
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌrædɪʃ ruːt / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsˌrædɪʃ ruːt / (BrE)
The root of the horseradish plant, typically grated or ground and used as a pungent seasoning or condiment.

Examples

Grated horseradish root
She added freshly grated horseradish root to give the sauce a spicy kick.
Store horseradish root in a cool, dry place to keep it fresh for longer.
Traditionally, horseradish root is served with roast beef in many European countries.
