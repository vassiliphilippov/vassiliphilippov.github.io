unfaced

adjective
not facing forward
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfeɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪst / (BrE)
not turned to face something or oriented so that the front is away from a particular direction

Examples

Unfaced cottage

Many old temples were intentionally unfaced to avoid direct sunlight at certain times of day.
The building remained unfaced toward the main road, blending quietly into the landscape.
