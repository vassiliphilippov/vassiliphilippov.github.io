technophile

noun
person enthusiastic about technology
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛk.nəˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈtɛk.nəˌfaɪl / (BrE)
A person who is enthusiastic about or strongly interested in new technology and technological devices.

Examples

A technophile person
As a proud technophile, she eagerly awaited the release of every new smartphone.
His technophile tendencies led him to pursue a career in software engineering.
Many technophiles camped outside the store to get their hands on the latest gadget.

Related Terms

  • antonym: technophobe - a person who fears or dislikes new technology
