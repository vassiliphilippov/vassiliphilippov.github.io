true fir

noun
type of evergreen tree (Abies genus)
4.8
Pronunciation: / truː fɜːr / (AmE), / truː fɜː / (BrE)
Any evergreen tree of the genus Abies, native to upland or mountainous regions, characterized by needle-like leaves and upright cones.

Examples

A true fir tree
Researchers studied the growth patterns of true fir in high-altitude environments.
Several varieties of true fir are popular as Christmas trees.
The forest was dominated by true fir and pine species.
