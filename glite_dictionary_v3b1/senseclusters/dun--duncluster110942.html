dun

verb
make or become dun-colored
Pronunciation: / dʌn / (AmE), / dʌn / (BrE)
to make or become a dull, brownish-gray color

Examples

Fields dun in autumn
Late summer grasses dun the fields with muted, earthy tones.
Once green leaves slowly dunned as autumn approached.
With age, the paint began to dun and lose its original brightness.
