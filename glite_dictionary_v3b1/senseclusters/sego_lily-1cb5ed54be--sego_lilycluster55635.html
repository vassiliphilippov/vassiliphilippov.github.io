sego lily

noun
North American perennial with white bell-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪɡoʊ ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ˈsiːɡəʊ ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to western North America, having clusters of one to four showy white, bell-shaped flowers and edible bulbs.
