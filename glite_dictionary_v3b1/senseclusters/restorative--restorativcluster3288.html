restorative

noun
device for treating injury or disease
Pronunciation: / rɪˈstɔr.ə.tɪv /; / rɪˈstɔrətɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈstɒr.ə.tɪv /; / rɪˈstɒrətɪv / (BrE)
a medicine, device, or agent that helps restore health, strength, or well-being
