ebony tree

noun
tropical tree with dark, hard wood
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛbəni ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈɛbəni ˌtriː / (BrE)
a tropical tree of southern Asia with hard, dark-colored heartwood used in cabinetwork and inlay work

Examples

Furniture made from the ebony tree is prized for its durability and beautiful dark color.
In the tropical forests of Sri Lanka, the ebony tree grows slowly and reaches impressive heights.
The woodworker carved a statue from the dense heartwood of an ebony tree.
