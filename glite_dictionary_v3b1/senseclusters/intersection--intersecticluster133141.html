intersection

noun
a place where roads cross
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.t̬ɚˈsɛk.ʃən /; / ˌɪntərˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈsɛk.ʃən /; / ˌɪntəˈsɛkʃən / (BrE)
a place where two or more roads, streets, or paths cross each other

Examples

Busy intersection
Pedestrians should always be careful when crossing an intersection in the city.
The traffic lights at the intersection turned red, causing all cars to stop.
There was a small café on the corner of the busy intersection.
Be careful when driving through a busy intersection.
In geometry, the intersection of two lines is a single point.
The two streets meet at an intersection near the school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crossroads - a place where two roads meet and cross each other
  • synonym: junction - a place where roads or railway lines meet or cross
