eer

Pronunciation: /ɪər/ /; / /eər/ (AmE), /ɪə/ /; / /eə/ (BrE)
a suffix used to form nouns referring to a person who does a particular action or job

Examples

The suffix eer
The word 'volunteer' uses the suffix eer to indicate a person who offers to help.
In 'pioneer', the eer ending shows it refers to a person who explores new areas.
Many English words for people who act, like 'engineer', end with the suffix eer.
