beneficent

adjective
generous; doing good or charitable acts
Pronunciation: / bɪˈnɛfəsənt /; / bəˈnɛfɪsənt / (AmE), / bɪˈnɛfɪsənt / (BrE)
doing good or being kind and helpful, especially by giving to others or helping those in need

Examples

Beneficent actions
His beneficent actions brought hope to many homeless people during the harsh winter months.
She is known for her beneficent donations to educational charities across the country.
The foundation was established by a beneficent couple who wanted to support local families in need.
He made a beneficent donation that helped build the new hospital.
Many considered her reign to be a period of beneficent leadership.
The foundation is known for its beneficent activities throughout the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benevolent - well meaning and kindly
  • antonym: maleficent - causing harm or destruction, especially by supernatural means
  • antonym: malevolent - having or showing a wish to do evil to others
