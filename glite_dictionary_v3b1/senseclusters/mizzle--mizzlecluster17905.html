mizzle

verb
rain lightly; drizzle
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪzəl / (BrE)
(of rain) to fall lightly in very fine drops; to drizzle lightly.

Examples

It mizzles gently
Clouds gathered and soon it was mizzling over the quiet village street.
It began to mizzle just as we reached the park, so we opened our umbrellas.
Yesterday afternoon, it mizzled for hours without ever turning into a proper rain.
