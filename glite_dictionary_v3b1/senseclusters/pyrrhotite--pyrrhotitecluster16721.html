pyrrhotite

noun
brownish iron sulfide mineral
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪr.həˌtaɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɪr.əˌtaɪt / (BrE)
A brownish iron sulfide mineral (FeS) with weak magnetic properties, found in many igneous and metamorphic rocks.

Examples

Brown pyrrhotite mineral
Pyrrhotite can sometimes be mistaken for other metallic minerals because of its appearance.
Magnetic anomalies in certain rocks are often due to the presence of pyrrhotite.
The geologist found a deposit rich in pyrrhotite during the survey.
