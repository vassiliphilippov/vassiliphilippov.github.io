carajo

noun
ship's lookout platform
Pronunciation: / kaˈɾaxoʊ / (AmE), / kaˈɾaxəʊ / (BrE)
a small elevated lookout platform on a ship's mast, also known as a crow's nest, used to watch for land or other vessels

Examples

Ship's carajo
The lookout spent hours in the carajo scanning the horizon for signs of land.
During the storm, the sailor held tightly to the carajo as the ship rocked violently.
From the carajo, he spotted another vessel approaching in the distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crow's nest - a lookout platform high on a ship's mast
