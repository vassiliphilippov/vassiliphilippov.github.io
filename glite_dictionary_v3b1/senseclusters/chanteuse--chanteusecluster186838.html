chanteuse

noun
female singer
9.0
Pronunciation: / ʃɑnˈtuːz / (AmE), / ʃɒ̃ˈtɜːz / (BrE)
a female singer, especially one who performs in a stylish or sophisticated way, often in nightclubs or cabarets

Many Parisian clubs feature a talented chanteuse singing classic French songs.
The jazz band was joined by a renowned chanteuse for the evening's show.
