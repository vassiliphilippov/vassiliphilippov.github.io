messy

adjective
dirty or untidy
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛsi / (AmE), / ˈmesi / (BrE)
dirty or untidy; not neat or orderly

Examples

A messy room
Her room was so messy that she couldn't find her homework.
Please clean up after cooking; the kitchen is getting messy.
The children’s messy handwriting made it hard to read their stories.
