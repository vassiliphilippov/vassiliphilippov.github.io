noncontiguous

adjective
not adjacent
7.9
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.kən.ˈtɪg.juː.əs/ /; / /nɑːn.kən.ˈtɪg.jʊ.əs/ (AmE), /nɒn.kən.ˈtɪg.juː.əs/ /; / /nɒn.kən.ˈtɪg.jʊ.əs/ (BrE)
not next to each other or not touching; separated by space, order, or sequence

Examples

Alaska is a noncontiguous state because it does not border the other states.
