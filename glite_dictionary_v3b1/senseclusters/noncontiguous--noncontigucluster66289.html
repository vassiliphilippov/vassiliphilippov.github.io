noncontiguous

adjective
not adjacent
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkənˈtɪɡjuəs / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈtɪɡjuəs / (BrE)
not next to each other or not touching; separated by space, order, or sequence

Examples

A noncontiguous area
The country is made up of several noncontiguous regions separated by mountains.
The data was saved in noncontiguous sections of the hard drive, making retrieval slower.
In the list, the selected items were noncontiguous and scattered throughout the document.
Alaska is a noncontiguous state because it does not border the other states.
The museum's noncontiguous galleries are located in different buildings across the city.
The company owns several noncontiguous parcels of land separated by a river.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adjacent - next to or adjoining something else
  • antonym: contiguous - sharing a common border; touching
  • synonym: disconnected - not joined or linked together
  • synonym: disjointed - lacking connection or continuity
  • synonym: separate - existing or happening independently; not joined
