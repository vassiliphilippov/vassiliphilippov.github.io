Pterocarya

noun
genus of wingnut trees
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrəˈkɛəriə /; / ˌtɛrəˈkɛriə / (AmE), / ˌtɛrəˈkɑːrɪə / (BrE)
a genus of deciduous trees in the walnut family, native to Europe and Asia, commonly known as wingnuts because of their winged fruit
