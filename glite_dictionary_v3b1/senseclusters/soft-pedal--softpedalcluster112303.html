soft-pedal

verb
play down; minimize
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔft ˌpɛdəl /; / ˌsɔftˈpɛdəl / (AmE), / ˈsɒft ˌpɛdəl /; / ˌsɒftˈpɛdəl / (BrE)
to make something seem less important or less severe than it really is; to downplay or minimize
