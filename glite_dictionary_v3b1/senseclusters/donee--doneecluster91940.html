donee

noun
recipient of funds or gifts
8.6 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / do äÀàni / (AmE), / d…ô äÀàniÀê / (BrE)
a person who receives funds, a gift, or other benefits, especially as a beneficiary in legal contexts

Examples

The donee of a gift
As the donee of the trust, she was entitled to receive regular payments from the estate.
Each donee must sign an acknowledgment for tax purposes when accepting a significant gift.
The charity selected a local school as the main donee of its annual donation drive.

Synonyms

  • beneficiary
    5.8 12/m
    beneficiary is a person or group who gets money or benefits from a will, trust or policy, while donee is someone who receives a gift.

Related Terms
