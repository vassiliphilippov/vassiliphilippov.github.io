chimney swallow

noun
a North American bird that nests in chimneys
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪmni ˌswɑloʊ /; / ˈtʃɪmni ˈswɑloʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪmni ˌswɒləʊ /; / ˈtʃɪmni ˈswɒləʊ / (BrE)
a small bird, found in North America and Europe, that often nests in chimneys, barns, or similar structures

Related Terms

  • synonym: chimney swift - North American swift that nests in chimneys and hollow trees
