friggin

adjective, adverb
informal intensifier
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪɡɪn / (AmE), / ˈfrɪɡɪn / (BrE)
used informally to strongly emphasize something, especially to show annoyance, surprise, or intensity (mild swear word)

Examples

A friggin mess
I can't believe this friggin computer crashed again during my presentation.
He forgot his friggin keys and had to wait outside in the rain.
That was the best friggin pizza I've ever tasted in my life.
It was so friggin cold outside that I could barely feel my fingers.
This is the best pizza I have ever tasted, it's friggin delicious.
I can't believe how friggin early we have to wake up tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: damn - used to express annoyance or emphasis
  • synonym: really - used to emphasize a statement or opinion
  • synonym: very - used to emphasize degree or intensity
