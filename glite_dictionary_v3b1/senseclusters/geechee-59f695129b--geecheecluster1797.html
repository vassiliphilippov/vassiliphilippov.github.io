Geechee

noun
coastal African American group
Pronunciation: / ˈgiːtʃi / (AmE), / ˈgiːtʃi / (BrE)
a member of an African American community from the coastal regions of Georgia and South Carolina, known for preserving unique cultural traditions

Examples

A Geechee person
The Geechee have maintained many of their ancestors' traditions for generations.
She is proud to be part of the Geechee community along the Georgia coast.
Researchers study the language and customs of the Geechee people to understand their history.
