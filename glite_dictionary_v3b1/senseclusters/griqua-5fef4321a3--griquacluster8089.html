Griqua

proper noun
South African ethnic group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːkwə / (AmE), / ˈɡriːkwə / (BrE)
a member of a South African ethnic group of mixed Khoikhoi and European descent, or relating to their community

Examples

Griqua people
The Griqua established their own communities in several regions of South Africa.
Many Griqua people are proud of their unique cultural heritage.
The history of the Griqua is an important part of South African society.
