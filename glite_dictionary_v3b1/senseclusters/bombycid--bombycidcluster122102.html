bombycid

noun
silk-producing Asiatic moth
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑːm.bɪ.sɪd/ (AmE), /ˈbɒm.bɪ.sɪd/ (BrE)
A moderate-sized Asiatic moth of the family Bombycidae, whose larvae feed on mulberry leaves and produce silk.
