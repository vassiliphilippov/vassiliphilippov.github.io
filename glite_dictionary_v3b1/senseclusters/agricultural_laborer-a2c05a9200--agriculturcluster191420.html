agricultural laborer

noun
farm worker who does manual labor
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡrəˈkʌltʃərəl ˈleɪbərər /; / ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəl ˈleɪbərər / (AmE), / ˌaɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəl ˈleɪbərə /; / ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəl ˈleɪbərə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to work on farms, especially one who tills the soil or performs manual agricultural tasks

Examples

Agricultural laborer on farm
As an agricultural laborer, he became skilled at planting and irrigating crops.
Many agricultural laborers work long hours in the fields during summer.
The company hired several agricultural laborers to help with the harvest season.
During the harvest season, every agricultural labourer was needed in the fields from dawn until dusk.
For decades, he worked as an agricultural labourer on several large farms in the region.
The government introduced new policies to improve the living conditions of agricultural labourers.
