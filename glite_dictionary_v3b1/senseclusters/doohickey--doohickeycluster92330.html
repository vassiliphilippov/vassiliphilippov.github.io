doohickey

noun
unspecified object; gadget
Pronunciation: / ˈduːˌhɪki / (AmE), / ˈduːˌhɪki / (BrE)
an object whose name you do not know or have forgotten; a gadget or thingamajig

Examples

A doohickey on shelf
Can you pass me that doohickey on the shelf over there?
He fixed his bike with some strange doohickey he found in the garage.
Whenever I can't remember a tool's name, I just call it a doohickey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gizmo - a small mechanical or electronic device
  • synonym: thingamajig - an object or device whose name is unknown or forgotten
  • synonym: whatchamacallit - a thing you cannot recall the name of
