phaeton

noun
light horse carriage
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪ.ə.tən /; / ˈfeɪtən / (AmE), / ˈfeɪ.ə.tən /; / ˈfeɪtən / (BrE)
a light, open, four-wheeled horse-drawn carriage, often used for leisure in the 18th and 19th centuries
