sequel

noun
a work that continues a previous one
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːkwəl / (AmE), / ˈsiːkwəl / (BrE)
a book, film, play, or other work that continues the story of a previous one

Examples

A sequel movie
Fans eagerly awaited the sequel after the cliffhanger ending of the first game.
Her new book is a sequel to the bestselling novel she wrote last year.
The movie studio announced that a sequel would be released next summer.
Fans eagerly awaited the sequel to see what happened to the main characters.
Her first novel was a bestseller, so she decided to write a sequel that picks up the story two years later.
The film's sequel was not as well received as the original movie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: continuation - something that continues a previous story or process
  • antonym: prequel - a work that depicts events preceding those of an existing work
