nonchurchgoing

adjective
not attending church regularly
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑntʃɜrtʃˌɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒntʃɜːtʃˌɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
not attending church regularly or no longer practicing one's faith through church participation.

Examples

Nonchurchgoing people
Although she was raised in a religious family, she is now nonchurchgoing.
As a nonchurchgoing individual, he prefers to spend Sundays hiking rather than attending services.
The survey indicates an increase in the nonchurchgoing population in urban areas.
