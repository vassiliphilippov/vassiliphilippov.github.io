payer

noun
person or entity that pays
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪ.ər / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person or organization that pays money for something, especially in exchange for goods or services

Examples

The payer of bills
As the bill's payer, Jessica kept all the payment receipts.
Employers are required to identify the payer and payee for each transaction.
The tax payer is responsible for submitting accurate information to the authorities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: payee - a person or organization to whom money is paid
