farl

noun
quartered flatbread
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɑrl / (AmE), / fɑːl / (BrE)
a flat, round bread or scone, usually cut into four pieces, commonly served in Scotland or Ireland

Examples

Traditionally, Irish soda bread is cut into four farls before baking.
He enjoyed a buttered farl with his afternoon tea.
