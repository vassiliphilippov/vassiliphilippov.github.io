punk

verb
prank or deceive someone
8.4
Pronunciation: / pʌŋk / (AmE), / pʌŋk / (BrE)
to trick, prank, or humiliate someone, especially as a joke (informal)

Examples

The comedian was known for punking celebrities during his live shows.

They love punking each other with harmless jokes at work.
She realized she was being punked when everyone started laughing.
