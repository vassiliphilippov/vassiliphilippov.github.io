unfaithful

adjective
betraying trust; traitorous
3.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfeɪθfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪθf(ə)l / (BrE)
behaving in a way that betrays trust or loyalty; having the character of a traitor or betrayer

Examples

Unfaithful friend
Her unfaithful conduct during the debate shocked even her closest supporters.
Many saw his actions as unfaithful to his country and comrades.
The agent was discovered to be unfaithful and quickly dismissed for betraying his organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faithless - lacking loyalty or trustworthiness
  • antonym: loyal - showing firm and constant support or allegiance
  • synonym: traitorous - having the character of a traitor; betraying trust
