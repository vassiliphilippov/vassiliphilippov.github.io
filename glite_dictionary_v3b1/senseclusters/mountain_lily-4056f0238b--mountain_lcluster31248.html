mountain lily

noun
New Zealand perennial with white flowers
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən ˌlɪli /; / ˈmaʊntən ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˌlɪli /; / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈlɪli / (BrE)
a decorative plant with showy white flowers, called 'mountain lily', which may refer to a New Zealand native or a Japanese lily (Lilium auratum)
