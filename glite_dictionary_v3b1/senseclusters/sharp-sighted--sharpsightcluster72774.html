sharp-sighted

adjective
having very keen vision
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑrpˌsaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpˌsaɪtɪd / (BrE)
Having very keen or acute vision; able to see details clearly.

Examples

Sharp-sighted eagle
As a photographer, being sharp-sighted helps him notice details others might miss.
Even at night, her sharp-sighted eyes could make out shapes in the darkness.
The sharp-sighted hawk spotted a mouse in the field from high above.
