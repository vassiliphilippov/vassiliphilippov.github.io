maduro

adjective
fully ripe
7.6
Pronunciation: / məˈduɾoʊ / (AmE), / məˈdʊərəʊ / (BrE)
describing fruit or food that is fully ripe or matured, especially used for plantains

Examples

Maduro plantain
The chef prefers to use maduro plantains for making sweet fried dishes.
You can tell the banana is maduro by its deep yellow color and soft texture.
In many Latin American recipes, only maduro fruit is suitable for desserts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ripe - fully developed and ready to eat
  • antonym: unripe - not fully developed or ready to eat
