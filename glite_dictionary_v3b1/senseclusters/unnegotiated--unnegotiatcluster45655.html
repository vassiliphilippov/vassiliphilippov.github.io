unnegotiated

adjective
not agreed by negotiation
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnnɪˈɡoʊʃieɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnnɪˈɡəʊʃieɪtɪd / (BrE)
not discussed or agreed upon through negotiation

Examples
