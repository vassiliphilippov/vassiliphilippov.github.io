wolf-whistle

verb
whistle to express attraction
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊlfˌwɪsəl / (AmE), / ˈwʊlfˌwɪsl̩ / (BrE)
(of a man) to whistle in a way that expresses sexual attraction or approval, usually directed at a woman in public.

Examples

To wolf-whistle loudly
He wolf-whistled loudly when he saw the actress on the red carpet.
It is now considered inappropriate to wolf-whistle at women in public.
Several construction workers stopped to wolf-whistle as she walked by the site.
