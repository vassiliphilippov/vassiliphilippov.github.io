Indian chickweed

noun
annual mat-forming weed with small greenish-white flowers
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈtʃɪkˌwid / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈtʃɪkwiːd / (BrE)
an annual, mat-forming weed with whorled leaves and small greenish-white flowers, found widely in North America

Examples

Indian chickweed plant
Gardeners often struggle to control Indian chickweed because it spreads so rapidly.
She identified the small greenish-white flowers as those of Indian chickweed during her field survey.
The lawn was quickly covered by Indian chickweed after the spring rains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chickweed - a common name for several small-leaved plants of the genus Stellaria
