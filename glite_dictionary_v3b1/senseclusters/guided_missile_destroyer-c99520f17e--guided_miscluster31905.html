guided missile destroyer

noun
naval warship armed with guided missiles
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪdɪd ˈmɪsəl dɪˈstrɔɪər / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdɪd ˈmɪsaɪl dɪˈstrɔɪə / (BrE)
a fast, maneuverable warship equipped with guided missiles as its primary armament, designed for naval combat and air defense

Examples

A guided missile destroyer ship
A guided missile destroyer can track and engage multiple enemy aircraft simultaneously.
During the training exercise, the guided missile destroyer demonstrated its advanced defense systems.
The navy launched a new guided missile destroyer to strengthen its fleet.
