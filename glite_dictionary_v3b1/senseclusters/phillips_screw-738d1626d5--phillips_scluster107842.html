Phillips screw

noun
screw with crossed slot head
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlɪps skru / (AmE), / ˈfɪlɪps skruː / (BrE)
a screw with a cross-shaped slot on its head, designed to be driven by a Phillips screwdriver

Examples

A Phillips screw head
She replaced the old, rusted fasteners with new Phillips screws for better durability.
The mechanic realized he needed a special bit to remove the Phillips screw.
Use a Phillips screw to assemble the furniture quickly and securely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: slotted screw - a screw with a single straight slot on its head
