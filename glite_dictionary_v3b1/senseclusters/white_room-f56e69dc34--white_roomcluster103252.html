white room

noun
very clean laboratory or assembly room
9.5
Pronunciation: / waɪt ruːm / (AmE), / waɪt ruːm / (BrE)
a room kept extremely clean and free from dust or bacteria, used for laboratory work or the assembly of sensitive equipment

Examples

A white room laboratory
All components were assembled in a white room to ensure their purity and reliability.
Only specially trained staff are allowed to enter the white room to avoid contamination.
Visitors must wear protective clothing before entering the white room at the research facility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clean room - a room in which the concentration of airborne particles is controlled
