Roquefort dressing

noun
salad dressing with Roquefort cheese
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊkfərt ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɒkfɔː ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
A salad dressing made with vinaigrette and crumbled Roquefort or blue cheese.

Examples

Roquefort dressing with salad
She ordered a salad topped with Roquefort dressing for lunch.
The tangy flavor of Roquefort dressing goes well with grilled meats.
Would you like Roquefort dressing or vinaigrette on your side salad?
