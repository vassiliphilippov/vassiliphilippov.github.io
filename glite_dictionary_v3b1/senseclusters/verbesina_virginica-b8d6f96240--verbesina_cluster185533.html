Verbesina virginica

noun
a tall, white-flowered perennial herb of eastern US
Pronunciation: / vɜːrˈbiːsɪnə vərˈdʒɪnɪkə / (AmE), / vɜːˈbiːsɪnə vəːˈdʒɪnɪkə / (BrE)
