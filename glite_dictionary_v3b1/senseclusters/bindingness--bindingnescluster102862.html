bindingness

noun
ability to bind
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪndɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˈbaɪndɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality or property of being able to bind or hold things together firmly

Examples

Glue bindingness
The bindingness of the adhesive was tested under different temperatures and conditions.
Engineers evaluated the bindingness of various materials to ensure the parts would stay together.
The bindingness of the contract made it difficult for either party to withdraw.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adhesiveness - the ability to stick or hold things together
  • synonym: cohesion - the action or property of sticking together
  • antonym: looseness - the quality of not being firmly fixed or held together
