scrip

noun
certificate used in place of currency
8.5
Pronunciation: / skrɪp / (AmE), / skrɪp / (BrE)
a certificate representing value, used as a substitute for currency and sometimes exchangeable for money

Examples

A scrip certificate
During the economic crisis, workers were paid in scrip instead of cash.
Pioneer towns often used scrip as a medium of exchange before official currency arrived.
The company issued scrip that employees could redeem at selected stores.

Related Terms

  • synonym: voucher - a document serving as evidence of a transaction or entitling the holder to something
