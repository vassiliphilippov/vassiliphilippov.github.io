snake charmer

noun
person who entertains by controlling snakes
Pronunciation: / sneɪk ˈtʃɑrmər / (AmE), / sneɪk ˈtʃɑːmə / (BrE)
a person who performs tricks or entertains people by appearing to control snakes, typically using music or movements

Examples

A snake charmer plays
In many parts of India, a snake charmer is a common sight during festivals.
The snake charmer moved gracefully, playing his flute to mesmerize the audience and the snake.
The crowd watched in awe as the snake charmer coaxed a cobra out of its basket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snake handler - someone who handles snakes, often for religious or entertainment purposes
