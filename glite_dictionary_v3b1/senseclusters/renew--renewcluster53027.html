renew

verb
make appear in a new form
Pronunciation: / rɪˈnu / (AmE), / rɪˈnjuː / (BrE)
to make something appear in a new, updated, or different form

Examples

To renew a brand
The company decided to renew its brand image to attract a broader audience.
The publisher aims to renew interest in the classic by releasing a special edition.
With a few modern touches, the architect managed to renew the look of the old building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: revamp - to give new and improved form, structure, or appearance to something
