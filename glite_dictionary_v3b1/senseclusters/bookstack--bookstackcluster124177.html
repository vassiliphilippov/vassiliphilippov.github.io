bookstack

noun
library shelves
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkstæk / (AmE), / ˈbʊkstak / (BrE)
a stack or organized arrangement of books, especially shelves of books in a library

Examples

A bookstack of shelves
She searched the bookstack for the novel she needed.
The librarian carefully organized the new arrivals in the bookstack.
Students gathered around the bookstack to find resources for their research project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bookshelf - a shelf for holding books
  • synonym: library - a place where books are kept for reading or borrowing
