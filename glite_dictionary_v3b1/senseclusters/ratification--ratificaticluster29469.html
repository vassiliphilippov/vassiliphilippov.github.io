ratification

noun
formal approval or confirmation
4.2 5.6
Pronunciation: /ræt.ə.fə.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ræt.ə.fə.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the official approval or confirmation of an agreement, treaty, or decision, typically by a formal procedure or vote

Examples

The ratification process
After months of negotiations, the ratification process was finally completed by all member states.
The ratification of the new trade agreement required the approval of both houses of parliament.
Union leaders hailed the ratification of the contract as a major victory for workers.
