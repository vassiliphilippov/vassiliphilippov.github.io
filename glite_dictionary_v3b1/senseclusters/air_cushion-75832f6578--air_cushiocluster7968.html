air cushion

noun
makeup compact with liquid foundation
3.7
Pronunciation: /er ˈkʊʃ.ən/ (AmE), /eə ˈkʊʃ.ən/ (BrE)
a small cosmetic compact containing a sponge or 'cushion' saturated with liquid foundation or concealer, applied to the face with a puff for quick, even coverage.

Examples

Makeup air cushion

The new air cushion foundation provided light coverage and SPF protection for summer.
Makeup artists often recommend the air cushion for quick touch-ups between meetings.
