Tenoretic

noun
antihypertensive combination drug
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛnəˈrɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɛnəˈrɛtɪk / (BrE)
A trademarked antihypertensive drug composed of a fixed combination of atenolol and a diuretic, used to treat high blood pressure.
