citrusy

adjective
like citrus fruits
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪtrəsi / (AmE), / ˈsɪtrəsi / (BrE)
having the taste, smell, or qualities of citrus fruits (like lemons, oranges, or limes), or being fresh and lively like citrus
