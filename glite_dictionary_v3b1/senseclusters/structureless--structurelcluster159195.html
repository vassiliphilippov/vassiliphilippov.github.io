structureless

adjective
without clear structure
Pronunciation: / ˈstrʌktʃərləs / (AmE), / ˈstrʌktʃələs / (BrE)
lacking a clear, organized, or defined structure

Examples

Structureless meeting
The discussion became structureless and people started talking over each other.
His essay was so structureless that it was difficult to follow his main points.
Without a plan, the project meetings often felt structureless and unproductive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chaotic - in a state of complete confusion and disorder
  • synonym: disorganized - not arranged in a neat or orderly way
  • antonym: structured - organized in a clear and definite way
