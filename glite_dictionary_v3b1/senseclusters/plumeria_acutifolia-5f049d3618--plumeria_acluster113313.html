Plumeria acutifolia

noun
a species of Indian frangipani plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / pluːˈmɪəriə əˌkjuːtɪˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / pluːˈmɪəriə əˌkjuːtɪˈfəʊliə / (BrE)
A species of frangipani native to India, having an erect growth habit and conical form, commonly grown in temple gardens.
