vena anastomotica

noun
communicating brain vein
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪ.nə ˌæn.ə.stəˈmɑ.tɪ.kə / (AmE), / ˈviː.nə ˌæn.ə.stəˈmɒ.tɪ.kə / (BrE)
either of two veins that connect different parts of the venous system in the brain, allowing communication between veins.
