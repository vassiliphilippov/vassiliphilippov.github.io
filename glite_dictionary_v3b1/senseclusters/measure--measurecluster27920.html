measure

noun
standard or reference point
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒə / (BrE)
a standard or reference point used for comparison or evaluation

Examples

The measure of success
Her generosity became the measure by which others were judged.
Honesty is the true measure of a person's character.
Test results provided a reliable measure for comparing different teaching methods.
