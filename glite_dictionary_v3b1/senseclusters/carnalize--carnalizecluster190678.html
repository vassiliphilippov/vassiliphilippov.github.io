carnalize

verb
to debase through sensual gratification
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀêrn…ôÀåla…™z /; / Ààk…ërn…ôÀåla…™z / (AmE), / Ààk…ëÀên…ôla…™z / (BrE)
to debase or corrupt by indulging in sensual or sexual pleasures

Examples

To carnalize the mind
He feared that material excess would carnalize the spiritual search for meaning.
Some critics argue that modern culture has carnalized our appreciation of art and beauty.
The author warned that unchecked desire could carnalize even the purest intentions.
His critics accused him of trying to carnalise spiritual beliefs for personal gain.
The writer argued that modern society tends to carnalise even the most sacred traditions.
They feared that commercial influence would carnalise art, stripping it of lofty ideals.

Synonyms

  • debase
    7.7 0.93/m
    debase lowers a person's moral value in many ways, often without sexual detail, while carnalize implies corruption through sexual pleasure.
