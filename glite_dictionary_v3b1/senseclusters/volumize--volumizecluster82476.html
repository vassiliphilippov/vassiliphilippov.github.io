volumize

verb
to increase loudness
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑljəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈvɒljʊˌmaɪz / (BrE)
to increase the loudness or perceived volume of recorded sound or an audio track

Examples

To volumize the music
The sound engineer decided to volumize the guitar track for a stronger effect.
If the dialogue is too quiet, you can volumize it during editing.
She asked the technician to volumize her vocals before the final mix.

  • antonym: soften - to make sound less loud
