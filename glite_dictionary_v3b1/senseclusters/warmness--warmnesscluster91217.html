warmness

noun
affectionate kindness or friendliness
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrm.nəs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːm.nəs / (BrE)
a feeling of kindness, affection, or heartfelt friendliness toward someone or something

Examples

Her warmness to friends
Despite his fame, there was a genuine warmness in his smile.
Her warmness put the nervous guests instantly at ease.
I was moved by the warmness of their welcome.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coldness - a lack of affection or friendliness; emotional distance
  • synonym: warmth - a quality of being emotionally warm, affectionate, or friendly
