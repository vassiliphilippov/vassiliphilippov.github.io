unconcluded

adjective
not finished
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈkluːdɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈkluːdɪd / (BrE)
not finished, decided, or brought to a definite end

Examples

An unconcluded case
The investigation remains unconcluded due to a lack of evidence.
Several important questions were left unconcluded after the debate.
The contract negotiations are still unconcluded after months of discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incomplete - not having all the necessary parts or not finished
  • antonym: resolved - settled or brought to a conclusion
  • synonym: unfinished - not completed or brought to an end
