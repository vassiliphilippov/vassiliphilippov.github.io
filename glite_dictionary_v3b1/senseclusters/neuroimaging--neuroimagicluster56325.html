neuroimaging

adjective
brain imaging techniques
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈɪmɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈɪmɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

Neuroimaging techniques
Recent neuroimaging studies have revealed new insights into how memory works in the brain.
The hospital invested in advanced neuroimaging equipment to improve diagnosis of neurological disorders.
Researchers use neuroimaging data to better understand the effects of medication on brain activity.
