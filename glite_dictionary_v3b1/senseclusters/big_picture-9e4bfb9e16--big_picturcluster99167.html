big picture

noun
the overall perspective or situation
1.0
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ ˈpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
the overall perspective, situation, or the most important factors, rather than details

Examples

The big picture view
She tends to lose sight of the big picture when she becomes too focused on minor issues.
The manager reminded the team to focus on the big picture instead of individual complaints.
When making decisions, it is important to consider the big picture rather than just immediate outcomes.
