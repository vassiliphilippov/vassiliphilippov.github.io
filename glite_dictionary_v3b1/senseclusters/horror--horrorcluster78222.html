horror

noun
something or someone causing horror
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrər / (AmE), / ˈhɒr.ər / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes feelings of intense fear, shock, or revulsion; something or someone that is very unpleasant or shocking

Examples

A horror movie
He called the house a horror after seeing its ruined interior.
The new film was a horror that many viewers found deeply disturbing.
The old abandoned hospital is a real horror at night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: delight - something that causes great pleasure or joy (opposite in emotional effect)
  • synonym: monstrosity - something especially large and ugly; something very unsightly or frightening
