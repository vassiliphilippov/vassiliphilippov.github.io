comprehensive

noun
academic proficiency examination
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnsɪv / (AmE), / ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnsɪv / (BrE)
A comprehensive examination testing a student's proficiency in a particular field of study, often required for an advanced degree.

Examples

Comprehensive exam
After months of preparation, she finally passed her comprehensive in molecular biology.
Graduate students must often take a comprehensive before proceeding to their dissertation.
He was nervous about the upcoming comprehensive, which would cover all aspects of the curriculum.
