bird's-eye maple

noun
maple wood with eye-like grain markings
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝdz aɪ ˈmeɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdz aɪ ˈmeɪpəl / (BrE)
a type of maple wood characterized by a wavy grain with small, eye-like markings, prized for use in fine woodworking and furniture
