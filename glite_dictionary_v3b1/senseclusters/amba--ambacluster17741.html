amba

noun
Ethiopian plateau
Pronunciation: / ˈæmbə / (AmE), / ˈæmbə / (BrE)
a flat-topped mountain or high plateau, especially found in Ethiopia and nearby regions

Examples

Ethiopian amba
The village was built on top of an amba, providing a natural defense against invaders.
From the edge of the amba, you could see the entire valley stretching out below.
Many ancient fortresses in Ethiopia were constructed on an amba for strategic advantage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mesa - a flat-topped hill with steep sides, common in arid regions
  • synonym: plateau - an area of high, flat land
