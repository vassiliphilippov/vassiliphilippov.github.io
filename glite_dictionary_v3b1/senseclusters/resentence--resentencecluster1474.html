resentence

noun
new legal sentence
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈsɛntəns / (AmE), / ˌriːˈsɛntəns / (BrE)
the act or process of imposing a new legal sentence on someone, or the new sentence that is imposed

Examples

A court resentence
After the appeal, the court ordered a resentence for the convicted man.
The resentence was much lighter than the original punishment.
Her lawyer argued that the resentence should reflect the new evidence presented.

Related Terms

  • synonym: retrial - a new trial for a case that has already been judged
  • synonym: sentence - the punishment given to someone found guilty by a court
