mature

verb
(finance) become due for repayment
Pronunciation: / məˈtʃʊr / (AmE), / məˈtjʊə / (BrE)
(of a financial arrangement or investment) to become due for repayment.

Examples

The bond matures
His investment is set to mature at the end of next quarter.
Some certificates of deposit mature after only six months.
The loan will mature in five years, requiring the full amount to be repaid.

Related Terms

  • synonym: come due - reach the time when payment or action is required
