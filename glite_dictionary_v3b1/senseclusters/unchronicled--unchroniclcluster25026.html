unchronicled

adjective
not recorded
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrɑːnɪkəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrɒnɪkəld / (BrE)
not recorded or written down in a historical account or official record

Examples

An unchronicled event
Many important events in rural communities remain unchronicled by historians.
The artist's early years are largely unchronicled and shrouded in mystery.
Countless personal stories go unchronicled despite their significance to local culture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: documented - recorded in writing or officially noted
  • synonym: unrecorded - not written down or officially documented
