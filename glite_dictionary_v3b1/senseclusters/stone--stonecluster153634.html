stone

noun
hard inner part of some fruits
Pronunciation: / stoʊn / (AmE), / stəʊn / (BrE)
the hard, inner layer of certain fruits, such as peaches or cherries, which contains the seed

Examples

A cherry stone
Biting into an olive and finding its stone can be unpleasant.
She carefully extracted the stone from a peach before slicing it.
You should remove the stones from cherries before baking the pie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pit - the hard stone of some fruits
  • degree lower: seed - the part of a plant that can grow into a new plant
