ume

noun
Japanese plum blossom
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈuːmeɪ / (AmE), / ˈuːmeɪ / (BrE)
a Japanese plum tree (Prunus mume) or its pale pink or white flowers, admired for blooming in late winter or early spring

Examples

Ume blossoms
Every February, people gather to view the beautiful ume blooming in the temple gardens.
The delicate scent of ume filled the air as the first blossoms appeared after winter.
Artists often paint ume to symbolize hope and renewal in Japanese culture.
In early spring, the ume trees fill the park with their delicate pink and white blossoms.
Traditional Japanese pickles are often made from the fruit of the ume.
The ancient ume in the temple garden is famous for its beautiful flowers.
