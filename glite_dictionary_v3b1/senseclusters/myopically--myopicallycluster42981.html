myopically

adverb
in a nearsighted way
8.0
Pronunciation: / maɪˈɑːpɪkli / (AmE), / maɪˈɒpɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that shows nearsightedness, either literally or figuratively lacking long-term perspective

Examples

To read myopically
Without her glasses, she read the board myopically and struggled to see the notes.
The company planned myopically, ignoring the potential long-term consequences of their actions.
He myopically focused on immediate profits rather than future growth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: farsightedly - in a way that considers the long-term effects
  • synonym: shortsightedly - in a way that lacks consideration for the future
