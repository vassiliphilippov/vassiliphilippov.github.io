stromatolitic

adjective
like stromatolites
Pronunciation: / stroʊˌmætəˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / strəʊˌmætəˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling stromatolites, which are layered structures formed by microorganisms in sedimentary rocks

Examples

stromatolitic rock layers
The stromatolitic layers in the rock provide evidence of ancient microbial life.
Scientists discovered stromatolitic patterns in the limestone, indicating a prehistoric environment.
The museum displayed a stromatolitic fossil that was over two billion years old.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stromatolite - a layered sedimentary structure formed by microorganisms
