Synonyms

  • halisteresis
    9.9 0.00/m
    halisteresis names the removal of mineral salts from bone in medical texts, while demineralization is the general abnormal loss causing weakening.
  • deossification
    9.9 0.00/m
    deossification describes bone losing its mineral content and structure, while demineralization emphasizes abnormal mineral loss that weakens bone.
  • decalcification
    8.3 0.01/m
    decalcification is loss of calcium from bones or teeth, while demineralization covers loss of many mineral salts often causing bone weakening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demineralization - abnormal loss of mineral salts (especially from bone)
  • antonym: remineralization - the restoration of mineral content, especially in teeth or bones
