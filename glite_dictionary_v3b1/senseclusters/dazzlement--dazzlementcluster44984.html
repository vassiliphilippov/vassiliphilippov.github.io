dazzlement

noun
temporary visual impairment
Pronunciation: / ˈdæzəlmənt / (AmE), / ˈdæzlmənt / (BrE)
a temporary loss or reduction of vision caused by a sudden or intense light; blinding glare.

Examples

Flash dazzlement
The sudden dazzlement from the camera flash left her blinking in confusion.
After stepping outside into the bright sun, he experienced a brief dazzlement before his eyes adjusted.
Drivers should be cautious of dazzlement caused by headlights at night.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: blindness - the state of being unable to see
  • degree lower: dimness - a state of reduced brightness or clarity of vision
  • synonym: glare - a strong, harsh light that makes it difficult to see
