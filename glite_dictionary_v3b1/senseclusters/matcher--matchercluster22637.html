matcher

noun
person or thing that matches
Pronunciation: / ˈmætʃər / (AmE), / ˈmætʃə / (BrE)
a person or thing that matches or finds things that are the same or similar

Examples

Image matcher
The matcher in the software quickly found similar images in the database.
As a skilled matcher, she could always find the right lid for every container.
In the game, the matcher paired cards with the same symbols to win points.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pairer - a person or thing that puts things into pairs
  • synonym: sorter - a person or device that arranges items into groups
