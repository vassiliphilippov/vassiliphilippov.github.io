predictable

adjective
able to be foreseen or expected
1.0
Pronunciation: / prɪˈdɪktəbl̩ / (AmE), / prɪˈdɪktəbl̩ / (BrE)
Able to be foreseen or expected; behaving or happening in a way that is not surprising.

Examples

A predictable reaction
His predictable reaction made it easy to plan our next move.
The ending of the movie was so predictable that everyone guessed it early.
Traffic jams are predictable during rush hour in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expected - likely to happen or be the case
  • antonym: unpredictable - not able to be foreseen or expected
