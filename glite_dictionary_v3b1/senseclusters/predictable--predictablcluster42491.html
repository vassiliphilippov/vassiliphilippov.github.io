Examples

A predictable reaction
His predictable reaction made it easy to plan our next move.
The ending of the movie was so predictable that everyone guessed it early.
Traffic jams are predictable during rush hour in the city.

Synonyms

  • foreseeable
    4.3 4.80/m
    foreseeable refers to events likely to happen soon and that we can plan for, while predictable means not surprising in behavior or results.
  • expectable
    3.5 0.12/m
    expectable is likely to be thought possible or to occur, while predictable often means not surprising and sometimes boring in outcome.
