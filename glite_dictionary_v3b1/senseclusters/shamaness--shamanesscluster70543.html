shamaness

noun
female spiritual healer
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːmənɛs / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːmənɪs / (BrE)
a female shaman, especially a woman who acts as a spiritual healer or guide

Examples

The shamaness heals
The villagers respected the shamaness for her wisdom and healing abilities.
During the ceremony, the shamaness led the group in traditional chants.
People traveled from distant places to seek help from the shamaness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: healer - a person who cures or helps to cure illness or injury
  • synonym: shaman - a person regarded as having access to the spirit world and healing powers
