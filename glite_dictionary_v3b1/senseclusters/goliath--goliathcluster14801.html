goliath

noun
a very large and powerful person or thing
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlaɪəθ / (AmE), / ɡəˈlaɪəθ / (BrE)
a person or thing that is extremely large and powerful, often used figuratively for something overwhelmingly strong or influential

Examples

A goliath company
Despite being a goliath on the field, he remained surprisingly humble off it.
The new corporation is seen as a goliath in the technology sector.
The tiny bookstore struggled to compete with the retail goliaths dominating the market.

  • synonym: giant - a person of unusually great size, strength, or influence
