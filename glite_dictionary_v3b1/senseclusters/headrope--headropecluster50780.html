headrope

noun
top rope of net or sail
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌroʊp / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌrəʊp / (BrE)
a rope running along the top edge of a fishing net or sail, providing support and structure

Examples

The headrope of net
The fishermen inspected the headrope for any signs of wear before heading out to sea.
A damaged headrope can cause the net to lose its shape and effectiveness.
Sailors tightened the headrope to ensure the sail was properly set for the journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boltrope - a rope sewn along the edge of a sail to strengthen it
  • antonym: footrope - a rope along the bottom edge of a net or sail
