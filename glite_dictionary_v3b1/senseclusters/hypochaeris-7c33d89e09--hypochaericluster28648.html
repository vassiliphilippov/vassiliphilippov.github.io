Hypochaeris

noun
a plant genus (cat's ear) in the daisy family
9.9
Pronunciation: / haɪpoʊˈkɪərɪs / (AmE), / hɪˌpɒkəˈɪərɪs / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the daisy family, commonly known as cat's ear, with milky sap and widespread weeds.
