happenstance

noun
a chance occurrence; coincidence
Pronunciation: / ˈhæp.ənˌstæns / (AmE), / ˈhæp.ən.stɑːns / (BrE)
a chance event or occurrence, especially one that seems to have been arranged but is actually accidental

Examples

By pure happenstance
By pure happenstance, they met again at the train station after many years.
Sometimes success can be the result of happenstance rather than careful planning.
The discovery of the artifact was a happenstance that changed the course of history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coincidence - a remarkable concurrence of events without apparent causal connection
