cloud

noun
state of suspicion or distrust
6.1 8.58/m
Pronunciation: / klaÊŠd / (AmE), / klaÊŠd / (BrE)
a situation or feeling of trouble, suspicion, or gloom that affects people or events

Examples

A cloud over him
Ever since the scandal, he has been living under a cloud at work.
Her reputation was affected by a cloud that took years to clear.
The politician resigned while a cloud of suspicion hovered over him.
Although there was excitement in the air, a cloud hung over the celebration due to recent events.
Her cheerful mood was soon overshadowed by a cloud of bad news.
The threat of layoffs cast a cloud over the company's annual meeting.
