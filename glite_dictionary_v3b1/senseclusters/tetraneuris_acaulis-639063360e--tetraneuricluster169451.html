Tetraneuris acaulis

noun
a North American perennial plant with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛtrəˈnjʊrɪs əˈkɔlɪs / (AmE), / ˌtɛtrəˈnjʊərɪs əˈkɔːlɪs / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to west central North America, with tufted basal leaves and short, leafless stalks each bearing a single yellow flower head.
