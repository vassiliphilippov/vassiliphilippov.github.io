interatomic

adjective
between atoms
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərəˈtɑmɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪntərəˈtɒmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between atoms

Examples

Interatomic distance
The interatomic distance affects the physical properties of a material.
Scientists study interatomic forces to understand how molecules are formed.
Changes in temperature can alter the interatomic bonds in a crystal lattice.

Related Terms
