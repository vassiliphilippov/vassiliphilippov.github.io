vertiginous

adjective
causing or experiencing dizziness; extremely steep
Pronunciation: / vərˈtɪdʒənəs / (AmE), / vəˈtɪdʒɪnəs / (BrE)
causing or suffering from a sensation of whirling and dizziness; extremely high or steep, making one feel dizzy

Examples
