subjugator

noun
one who conquers and enslaves others
Pronunciation: / sʌb.dʒəˌɡeɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / sʌb.dʒʊˌɡeɪ.tə / (BrE)
A person or group that conquers and brings others under control, often by force or enslavement.

Examples

A subjugator of nations
History remembers him as both a subjugator and a builder of empires.
Many tales depict the hero rising against a powerful subjugator to free the people.
The ruthless subjugator left a legacy of oppression in the lands he conquered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conqueror - one who conquers
  • antonym: liberator - one who frees people from control
