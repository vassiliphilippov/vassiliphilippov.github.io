moviegoing

noun
going to the cinema
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːviˌɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmuːviˌɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of going to a cinema to watch movies

Examples

Family moviegoing
Weekend moviegoing is a tradition for many families in our town.
The popularity of moviegoing declined when people started streaming films at home.
She missed the excitement of moviegoing with friends on Friday nights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cinemagoing - the activity of going to the cinema to watch films
  • antonym: home viewing - watching movies at home instead of at a cinema
