entrance

noun
a way or opening for entering a place
Pronunciation: / ˈɛntrəns / (AmE), / ˈɛntrəns / (BrE)
a door, gate, or other opening that allows entry to a place

Examples

Main entrance
She stood nervously at the entrance of the auditorium before walking in.
The main entrance to the building is locked after 6 p.m.
Visitors must use the south entrance during renovations.
