blitzer

noun
football defensive rusher
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪtsər / (AmE), / ˈblɪtsə / (BrE)
a defensive player in American football who rushes the quarterback quickly to disrupt a play

Examples

Football blitzer
The blitzer broke through the line and sacked the quarterback.
A skilled blitzer can change the outcome of a game with a single play.
The coach assigned a new blitzer to pressure the opposing team's offense.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defender - a player whose main role is to stop the opposing team from scoring
  • synonym: pass rusher - a defensive player who tries to tackle the quarterback
  • antonym: quarterback - the player who leads the offense and throws passes
