fur

noun
soft hair covering of mammals
7.2 1.0
Pronunciation: /fɜːr/ (AmE), /fɜː/ (BrE)
the soft, thick hair of mammals, or the material or clothing made from it

Examples

Polar bears have thick fur to keep them insulated in the Arctic cold.
