mortarless

adjective
without mortar
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrtərləs / (AmE), / ˈmɔːtələs / (BrE)
built or assembled without using mortar to bind the parts together

Examples

Mortarless stone wall
The ancient wall was constructed using mortarless stone techniques.
Many traditional fences in the countryside are mortarless, relying on careful placement of stones.
A mortarless patio can be easier to repair if any stones become damaged.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dry-stone - constructed by stacking stones without mortar
  • antonym: mortar - a mixture used to bind building materials together
