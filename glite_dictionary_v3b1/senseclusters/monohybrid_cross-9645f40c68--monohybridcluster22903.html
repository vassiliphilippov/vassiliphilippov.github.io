monohybrid cross

noun
genetic cross involving one trait
3.9 6.5
Pronunciation: /moʊ.noʊ.ˈhaɪ.brɪd krɔːs/ /; / /moʊ.noʊ.ˈhaɪ.brɪd krɑːs/ (AmE), /mɒ.nə.ˈhaɪ.brɪd krɒs/ /; / /mə.nə.ˈhaɪ.brɪd krɒs/ (BrE)
