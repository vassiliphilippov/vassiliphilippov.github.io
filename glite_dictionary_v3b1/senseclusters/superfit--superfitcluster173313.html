superfit

adjective
extremely fit
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌfɪt / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌfɪt / (BrE)
extremely physically fit and healthy, especially as a result of regular exercise

Examples

Superfit athlete
After months of training, he became superfit and could easily complete a triathlon.
The superfit instructor led the class through a challenging workout routine.
Many professional athletes must stay superfit to compete at the highest level.

Related Terms

  • synonym: athletic - physically strong, fit, and active
  • degree lower: fit - in good physical condition
  • antonym: unfit - not in good physical condition
