tap

noun
small metal plate on a shoe for tap dancing
Pronunciation: / tæp / (AmE), / tæp / (BrE)
a small metal plate fitted to the toe or heel of a shoe, especially for making a tapping sound in tap dancing

Examples

Shoe taps
Over time, the taps on her shoes became worn and needed replacing.
She nailed a new tap onto the heel of her dance shoe.
The sound of his tap echoed on the wooden stage as he began his routine.
