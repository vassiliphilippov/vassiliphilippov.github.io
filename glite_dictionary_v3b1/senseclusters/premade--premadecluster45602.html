premade

adjective
prepared in advance
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈmeɪd / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈmeɪd / (BrE)
made in advance and ready to use or consume

Examples

Premade meal
She served a premade cake at the party to save time.
Many people buy premade meals when they are too busy to cook.
The store offers a variety of premade sandwiches for lunch.

Related Terms
