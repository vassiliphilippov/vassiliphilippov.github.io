Synonyms

    search means looking carefully in places or records, while look for is the everyday act of trying to find something.
  • seek
    1.0 50/m
    seek is a more formal word for trying to obtain or achieve something, while look for is used in everyday speech about finding.
  • hunt
    1.2 9/m
