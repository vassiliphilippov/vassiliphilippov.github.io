sky

noun
the atmosphere and space seen from earth
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /skaɪ/ (AmE), /skaɪ/ (BrE)
The region of the atmosphere and outer space that is visible from the earth as an expanse overhead, often appearing blue during the day.

Examples

Blue sky
Birds soared high in the sky above the city.
Clouds covered the sky and threatened rain all afternoon.
The sky turned bright orange as the sun began to set.
