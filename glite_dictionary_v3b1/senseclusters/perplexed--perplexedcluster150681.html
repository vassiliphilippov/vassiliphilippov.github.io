perplexed

adjective
confused or puzzled
Pronunciation: / pərˈplɛkst / (AmE), / pəˈplɛkst / (BrE)
completely confused or puzzled; unable to understand something clearly.

Examples

Look perplexed
He looked perplexed when he saw the complicated instructions.
She wore a perplexed expression as she tried to solve the puzzle.
The students felt perplexed by the sudden change in the schedule.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bewildered - very confused and unable to decide what to do
  • antonym: certain - sure, having no doubt or confusion
  • synonym: confused - unable to think clearly or to understand something
