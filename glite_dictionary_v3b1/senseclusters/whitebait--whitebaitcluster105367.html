whitebait

noun
young edible fish, especially herrings or sprats
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtˌbeɪt / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtbeɪt / (BrE)
the small, edible young of certain fish (such as herring, sprats, or minnows), usually eaten whole as food
