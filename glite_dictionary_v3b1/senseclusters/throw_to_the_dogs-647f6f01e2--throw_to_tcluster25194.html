throw to the dogs

verb
discard or abandon something valuable
Pronunciation: / θroʊ tə ðə dɔɡz / (AmE), / θrəʊ tə ðə dɒɡz / (BrE)
to discard or abandon something valuable, especially in a careless or disrespectful way

Examples

To throw to the dogs carelessly
After years of dedication, his contributions were simply thrown to the dogs by the new management.
Don't let your dreams be thrown to the dogs just because of one setback.
She was heartbroken when her proposal was thrown to the dogs during the discussion.
