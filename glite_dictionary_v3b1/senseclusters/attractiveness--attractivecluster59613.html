attractiveness

noun
sexual allure or physical appeal
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈtræktɪvnəs / (AmE), / əˈtræktɪvnɪs /; / əˈtræktɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being attractive or appealing to others (including physical, sexual, or general appeal)

Examples

The attractiveness of the city has brought in many new residents over the past year.
They improved the park's attractiveness by planting more flowers and building new playgrounds.
