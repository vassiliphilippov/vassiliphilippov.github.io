samite

noun
heavy medieval silk fabric
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsæˌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsæmaɪt / (BrE)
A heavy and often ornate silk fabric, sometimes woven with gold or silver threads, used mainly in the Middle Ages for clothing and furnishings.
