oceangoing

adjective
designed for traveling on the open sea
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊʃiˌɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈəʊʃiˌɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a ship or vessel) designed for or capable of traveling on the open sea.

Examples

Oceangoing ship
Only oceangoing yachts can safely handle these conditions far from shore.
The ship was built to be an oceangoing vessel capable of crossing the Atlantic.
They invested in a new oceangoing freighter for international trade routes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seafaring - (of people or vessels) regularly traveling by sea
