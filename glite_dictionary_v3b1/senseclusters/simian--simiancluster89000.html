simian

noun
an ape or monkey
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmiən / (AmE), / ˈsɪmiən / (BrE)
an ape or monkey, especially as a member of the primate group distinguished from humans

Examples

A simian species
Researchers observed several simian behaviors in the troop of baboons.
She became fascinated by the intelligence of simian species during her studies.
The exhibit features fossils of ancient simians dating back millions of years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: human - a member of the species Homo sapiens or relating to people
  • synonym: primate - any member of the group of mammals including humans, apes, monkeys, and others
