jut

noun
outward projection; act of sticking out
9.7
Pronunciation: / dʒʌt / (AmE), / dʒʌt / (BrE)
an outward projection or the act of extending out from a surface

Examples

A jut of rock
From the wall, a small wooden jut held a faded lantern.
The architect designed a dramatic jut in the building’s façade to create visual interest.
The rocky jut above the sea formed a perfect place to stand and admire the view.

Related Terms

  • antonym: recession - the act of receding or moving back
