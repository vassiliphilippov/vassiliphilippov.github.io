barcode

noun
product identification code
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrˌkoʊd / (AmE), / ˈbɑːkəʊd / (BrE)
a pattern of lines or bars printed on products and scanned to identify them or retrieve information

Examples

Product barcode
The cashier scanned the barcode to quickly ring up the item.
Every library book has a barcode for easy tracking and checkout.
You can use your phone to scan the barcode and get more product details.

Related Terms

  • synonym: QR code - a type of matrix barcode that stores information readable by a scanner
