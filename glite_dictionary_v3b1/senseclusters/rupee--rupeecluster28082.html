rupee

noun
currency unit of Seychelles
6.1 2.6
Pronunciation: /ruː.ˈpiː/ (AmE), /ruː.ˈpiː/ (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in several South Asian and Indian Ocean countries (such as India, Pakistan, Nepal, Sri Lanka, Mauritius, and Seychelles)

Examples

Indian rupee
She exchanged her dollars for rupees before traveling to Seychelles.
The hotel bill was charged in rupees, the official currency of the country.
The price of the fruit was fifty rupees in the local market.
Prices in Mauritius are usually listed in rupees and cents.
She exchanged her dollars for Mauritian rupees at the airport.
The cost of the taxi ride was 500 rupees.
A single rupee coin features an image of Mahatma Gandhi.
She exchanged her dollars for rupees at the airport before leaving for India.
The price of tea has risen to 300 rupees per kilogram this year.
