launch pad

noun
starting point for an undertaking
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔntʃ ˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈlɔːntʃ ˌpæd / (BrE)
A place or situation that serves as a starting point for a project, career, or other undertaking.

Examples

Career launch pad
The internship became a valuable launch pad for her career in journalism.
Their small shop acted as a launch pad for their entry into the tech industry.
Winning the competition was the launch pad he needed to start his own business.

Related Terms

  • synonym: springboard - something that helps launch a project or career
