mechanical mixture

noun
mixture with separable components
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈkænɪkəl ˈmɪkstʃɚ / (AmE), / mɪˈkanɪkəl ˈmɪkstʃə / (BrE)
A mixture whose individual components can be physically separated by mechanical methods, such as filtration or sorting.

Examples

A mechanical mixture of vegetables
A salad made of lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumbers is considered a mechanical mixture.
Granite is a good example of a mechanical mixture, as its minerals remain distinct and easily separable.
Unlike a solution, a mechanical mixture allows its components to be mechanically separated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mixture - a substance made by combining two or more different materials
