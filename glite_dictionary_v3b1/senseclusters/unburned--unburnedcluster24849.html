unburned

adjective
not consumed by fire
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɜrnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɜːnd / (BrE)
not consumed or chemically changed by burning or combustion; remaining after an incomplete burn.

Examples

Unburned fuel remains
The engine was less efficient because some unburned fuel remained in the exhaust.
After the wildfire, patches of unburned forest could still be seen among the ashes.
The scientist analyzed the unburned material to determine why the reaction was incomplete.

  • antonym: burned - consumed or changed by fire or combustion
