bec

noun
spout or beak projection
Pronunciation: / bɛk / (AmE), / bɛk / (BrE)
a beak or a beak-shaped projection, such as the mouthpart of a bird or the spout of a teapot

Examples

Bird's bec
The teapot's bec was elegantly curved to prevent drips.
She admired the antique kettle with a long, narrow bec for precise pouring.
Make sure the bec is clean before serving tea to your guests.
The eagle used its sharp bec to tear into its prey.
Some birds have a long, slender bec for reaching nectar in flowers.
The parrot cracked the nut open with its strong bec.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beak - the hard, pointed part of a bird's mouth
  • synonym: spout - a tube or lip for pouring liquid from a container
