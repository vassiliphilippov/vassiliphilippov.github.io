pingback

noun
blog link notification
6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪŋ.bæk/ (AmE), /ˈpɪŋ.bæk/ (BrE)
an automatic notification sent to a website or blog when another site links to one of its posts

Examples

A pingback notification
After publishing her article, she received a pingback from another technology blog.
The blogging platform automatically displays pingbacks below each post.
He checked his notifications and saw a pingback from a popular news site.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: comment - a written response or remark on a blog post or article
  • synonym: trackback - a system for notifying a website when it is linked by another site
