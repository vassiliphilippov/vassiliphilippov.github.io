sulfonamide

noun
synthetic antibacterial compound
7.1
Pronunciation: / sʌlˈfoʊnəˌmaɪd / (AmE), / sʌlˈfɒnəˌmaɪd / (BrE)
any of a group of synthetic organic compounds containing the sulfonamide group, used chiefly as antibacterial agents by inhibiting the growth of certain bacteria.

Examples

Sulfonamide drug
Allergic reactions to sulfonamide drugs are relatively common among certain patients.
Some bacteria have developed resistance to sulfonamides over time.
The doctor prescribed a sulfonamide to help treat her bacterial infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antibiotic - a drug used to treat bacterial infections
