sportfishing

noun
recreational or competitive fishing
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔrtˌfɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtˌfɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of fishing for pleasure or competition rather than for food or profit.

Examples

Sportfishing for fun
He invested in new gear to enhance his sportfishing experience last summer.
Many tourists visit coastal towns specifically for sportfishing opportunities.
Some lakes enforce strict catch-and-release policies for sportfishing enthusiasts.
