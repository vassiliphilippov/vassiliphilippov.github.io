Synonyms

  • bubbly
    3.2 1/m
    bubbly describes drinks that have many lively bubbles and sounds friendly and informal, while fizzy is the common everyday term.
  • effervescent
    7.3 0.03/m
    effervescent is a slightly formal word used in writing and science to describe bubbling liquids, while fizzy is more casual in speech.
  • carbonated
    3.4 0.10/m
