Prince Otto Eduard Leopold von Bismarck

proper noun
German statesman; first Chancellor of Germany

Pronunciation: / ˈoʊtoʊ ˈɛdˌwɑrd liˈoʊpəld vɑn ˈbɪzˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈɒtəʊ ˈɛdwɑːd liˈəʊpɒlt fɒn ˈbɪzmɑːk / (BrE)
