downloadable

noun
digital file for download
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌloʊdəbəl / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌləʊdəbəl / (BrE)
a digital file or piece of content that can be downloaded from the internet.

Examples

A downloadable file
The website offers a free downloadable with instructions for assembling the product.
Each downloadable is checked for viruses before being made available to users.
She purchased a downloadable that included bonus music tracks and artwork.

Related Terms

  • antonym: upload - a file sent from a local system to a remote one
