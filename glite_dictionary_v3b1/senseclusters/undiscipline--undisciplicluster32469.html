undiscipline

noun
lack of discipline or self-control
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈdɪsəplɪn / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈdɪsəplɪn / (BrE)
a lack of self-control or the ability to obey rules; the trait of not being disciplined

Examples

His undiscipline problem
His undiscipline made it difficult for him to stick to a study routine.
Teachers often struggle with undiscipline in large classrooms.
The team's undiscipline was evident in their repeated fouls during the match.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discipline - the ability to control oneself or obey rules
  • synonym: indiscipline - lack of discipline or control
