logothete

noun
Byzantine official
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːɡəˌθit / (AmE), / ˈlɒɡəˌθiːt / (BrE)
a high-ranking official or minister in the Byzantine Empire, often responsible for administrative or financial duties

The logothete official
The logothete played a crucial role in managing the empire's finances and records.
As a trusted logothete, he advised the emperor on important administrative matters.
