recapitulation

noun
restatement of earlier musical themes
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.kəˌpɪtʃ.əˈleɪ.ʃən /; / ˌriːkəˌpɪtʃəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriː.kəˌpɪt.jʊˈleɪ.ʃən /; / ˌriːkəˌpɪtjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
(music) the section in a musical composition, especially in sonata form, where themes introduced earlier are repeated or restated, usually near the end
