conveyer belt

noun
moving band for transporting objects
Pronunciation: / kənˈveɪ.ər bɛlt / (AmE), / kənˈveɪ.ə bɛlt / (BrE)
A continuous moving band used for transporting objects from one place to another, especially in factories or airports.

Examples

A conveyer belt system
Engineers repaired the conveyer belt to keep the production line running smoothly.
She watched her luggage circle on the airport conveyer belt after the flight.
The packages are sorted as they move along the conveyer belt in the warehouse.
