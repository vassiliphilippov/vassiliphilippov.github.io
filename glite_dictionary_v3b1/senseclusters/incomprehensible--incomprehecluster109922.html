incomprehensible

adjective
impossible to understand
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnsəbəl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to understand or grasp

Examples

An incomprehensible message
Her handwriting was so messy that the message was almost incomprehensible.
The plot of the movie became incomprehensible after the first twenty minutes.
The technical jargon made the instructions completely incomprehensible to the average reader.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comprehensible - able to be understood; intelligible
