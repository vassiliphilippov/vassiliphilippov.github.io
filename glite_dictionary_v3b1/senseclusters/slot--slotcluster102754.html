slot

noun
scheduled period of time
Pronunciation: / slɑt / (AmE), / slɒt / (BrE)
a specific period of time allocated for a particular event, activity, or purpose in a schedule or timetable

Examples

A time slot
She reserved a slot on the calendar to meet with her supervisor.
The doctor’s next open slot isn’t until next week.
We were lucky to find an available slot for our presentation at the conference.
