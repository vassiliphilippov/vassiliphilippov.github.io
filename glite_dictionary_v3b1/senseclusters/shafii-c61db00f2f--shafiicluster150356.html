Shafi'i

noun
follower of Shafi'i school
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃɑːˈfiː.i / (AmE), / ʃæˈfiː.i / (BrE)
a follower or adherent of the Shafi'i school of Islamic law, one of the four main Sunni schools

Examples

A Shafi'i follower
As a Shafi'i, he follows specific rules regarding daily prayers and rituals.
The majority of Muslims in Southeast Asia identify as Shafi'i in their religious practice.
Her family has been Shafi'i for generations, observing traditions unique to their school.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Hanafi - a follower of the Hanafi school of Islamic law
  • antonym: Hanbali - a follower of the Hanbali school of Islamic law
  • antonym: Maliki - a follower of the Maliki school of Islamic law
