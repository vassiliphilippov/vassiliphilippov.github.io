Arab states of the Persian Gulf

noun
Arab-majority countries bordering the Persian Gulf
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈærəb steɪts əv ðə pɜrʒən ɡʌlf / (AmE), / ˈærəb steɪts əv ðə ˈpɜːʃən ɡʌlf / (BrE)
the countries in southwestern Asia with Arab-majority populations that border the Persian Gulf, typically including Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, and the United Arab Emirates

Examples

The Arab states of the Persian Gulf region

Tourists are increasingly attracted to the modern cities in the Arab states of the Persian Gulf.
