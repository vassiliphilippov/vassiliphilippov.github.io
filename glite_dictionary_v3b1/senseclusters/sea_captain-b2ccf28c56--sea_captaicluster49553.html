sea captain

noun
officer in command of a merchant ship
1.6 2.7
Pronunciation: /siː ˈkæp.tən/ (AmE), /siː ˈkæp.tɪn/ /; / /siː ˈkæp.tən/ (BrE)
an officer who is licensed to command a merchant ship or other seagoing vessel; the master of a ship at sea

Examples

Experienced sea captain
After years at sea, he finally earned his license to become a sea captain.
The sea captain assembled the crew before departing the harbor.
The experienced sea captain skillfully navigated his vessel through the storm.
