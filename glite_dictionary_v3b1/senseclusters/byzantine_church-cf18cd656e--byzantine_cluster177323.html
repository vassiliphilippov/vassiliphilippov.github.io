Byzantine Church

noun
the Christian Church in the Byzantine Empire
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪz.ən.taɪn tʃɜːrtʜ/ /; / /ˈbɪz.ən.tiːn tʃɜːrtʜ/ (AmE), /ˈbɪz.ən.taɪn tʃɜːtʃ/ /; / /ˈbaɪ.zæn.taɪn tʃɜːtʃ/ (BrE)
