close-minded

adjective
unwilling to consider new ideas
1.0 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkloʊs.maɪn.dɪd/ /; / /ˈkloʊs.maɪn.dəd/ /; / /ˈkloʊz.maɪn.dɪd/ (AmE), /ˈkləʊs.maɪn.dɪd/ /; / /ˈkləʊs.maɪn.dəd/ /; / /ˈkləʊz.maɪn.dɪd/ (BrE)

Examples
