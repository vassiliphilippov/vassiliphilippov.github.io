grad

noun
a school or college graduate
6.4
Pronunciation: / ɡræd / (AmE), / ɡræd / (BrE)
a person who has received a degree or diploma from a school, college, or university

Examples

College grad
As a grad of this university, she was invited to the alumni dinner.
Many recent grads are searching for their first full-time job.
The company is looking to hire new grads with fresh ideas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: undergraduate - a student at a college or university who has not yet received a degree
