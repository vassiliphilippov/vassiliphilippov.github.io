tennis camp

noun
a camp where tennis is taught
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnɪs kæmp / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs kamp / (BrE)
a camp or organized program where participants are taught and practice tennis, often including instruction, drills, and matches

Examples

tennis camp program
Every summer, kids from across the state sign up for tennis camp to improve their skills.
She met her best friend at a tennis camp when they were both learning serves.
The tennis camp offers lessons for all ages and playing levels.
