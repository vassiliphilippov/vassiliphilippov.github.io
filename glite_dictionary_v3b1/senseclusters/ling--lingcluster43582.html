ling

noun
low evergreen shrub; heather
8.8
Pronunciation: / lɪŋ / (AmE), / lɪŋ / (BrE)
A low, evergreen shrub of the heath family (Calluna vulgaris), commonly known as heather, found widely in northern Europe and Asia.

Examples

Wild ling heather
Much of the moorland is dominated by ling and other heath plants.
She pressed a sprig of ling into her diary as a keepsake.
The hills were covered with blooming ling in early autumn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heather - another name for the same plant (Calluna vulgaris)
