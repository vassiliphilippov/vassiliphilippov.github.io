cheapie

noun
stingy person
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːpi / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːpi / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is unwilling to spend money or is very stingy

Examples

Mark is a cheapie
Everyone knows Mark is a cheapie who never wants to split the bill fairly.
If you are a cheapie, you might miss out on some fun experiences.
My friends tease me for being a cheapie when I look for the best deals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheapskate - a person who is unwilling to spend money
  • synonym: miser - a person who hoards money and spends as little as possible
  • antonym: spender - a person who spends money freely
