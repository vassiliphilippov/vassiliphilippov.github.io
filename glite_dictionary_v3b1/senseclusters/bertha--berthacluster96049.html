bertha

noun
large WWI howitzer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrθə / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθə / (BrE)
a very large German siege gun used during World War I, famously known as 'Big Bertha'.

Examples

A giant bertha cannon

Historians often mention the impact of the bertha in changing the course of World War I battles.
Photographs from the era show soldiers standing beside the enormous bertha before it was fired.
