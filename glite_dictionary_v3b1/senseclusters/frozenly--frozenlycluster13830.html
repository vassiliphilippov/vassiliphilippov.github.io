frozenly

adverb
without movement or emotion
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfroʊzənli / (AmE), / ˈfrəʊzənli / (BrE)
in a way that is cold, motionless, or showing no emotion

Examples

Look frozenly still
She stared frozenly at the empty street, unable to move.
He responded frozenly, his face showing no sign of emotion.
The audience sat frozenly as the shocking news was announced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emotionlessly - without showing any emotion
  • synonym: motionlessly - without any movement
  • antonym: warmly - in a friendly or affectionate manner
