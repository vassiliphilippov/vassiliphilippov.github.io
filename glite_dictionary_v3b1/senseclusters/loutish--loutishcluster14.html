loutish

adjective
uncouth or ill-mannered
Pronunciation: / ˈlaʊɾɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈlaʊtɪʃ / (BrE)
Rough, uncouth, and ill-mannered in behaviour or appearance.

Examples

A loutish man
Despite his intelligence, his loutish appearance put off potential employers.
She was appalled by his loutish table manners during the dinner party.
The teenagers' loutish behavior at the concert upset many of the older guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boorish - rude and insensitive in a way typical of a peasant or rustic
  • antonym: refined - elegant and cultured in appearance or behaviour
