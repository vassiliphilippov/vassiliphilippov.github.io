banzai

noun
Japanese cheer or exclamation of triumph
Pronunciation: / ˈbænˌzaɪ / (AmE), / ˈbæn.zaɪ / (BrE)
a Japanese cheer or exclamation expressing enthusiasm or triumph, often shouted in celebration or before charging into action

Examples

Shout banzai cheer
As the parade passed by, the crowd shouted banzai to show their excitement.
He raised his sword and yelled banzai before charging forward.
With a loud banzai, the team celebrated their victory.
