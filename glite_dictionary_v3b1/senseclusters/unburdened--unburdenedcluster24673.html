unburdened

adjective
not carrying a burden
5.3 0.00/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàb…ùÀêd…ônd / (AmE), /  ånÀàb…úÀêd…ônd / (BrE)
Not carrying a physical burden or load; not encumbered.

Examples

Unburdened by weight
He was grateful to be unburdened after unloading the crates from his back.
She walked unburdened by heavy bags through the quiet streets.
The horse trotted freely, unburdened for the first time in weeks.
