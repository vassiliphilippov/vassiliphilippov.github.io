wader

noun
waterproof fishing boot
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪdər / (AmE), / ˈweɪdə / (BrE)
a waterproof boot or garment that covers the legs and sometimes the chest, worn for walking in water

Examples

Fishing waders
Before entering the stream, she pulled on her waders to keep dry.
The fisherman wore chest-high waders while casting his line into the river.
After a long day in the marsh, his waders were covered in mud.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boot - a type of footwear that covers the foot and ankle
  • synonym: galoshes - waterproof overshoes worn to protect shoes from water
