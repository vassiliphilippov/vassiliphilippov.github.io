Pipa

noun
genus of aquatic frogs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːpə / (AmE), / ˈpiːpə / (BrE)
A genus of aquatic frogs in the family Pipidae, found in South America.

Examples

Pipa frog species
Members of the genus Pipa are entirely aquatic and rarely leave the water.
Researchers observed several Pipa frogs in their natural habitat in the Amazon basin.
The Surinam toad, a species of Pipa, is known for its unique reproductive habits.
