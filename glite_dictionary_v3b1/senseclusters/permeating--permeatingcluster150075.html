permeating

adjective
spreading throughout; penetrating
6.0 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpɜːr.miː.eɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpɜː.miː.eɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
spreading throughout something; penetrating or diffusing to every part.

Examples

Permeating the air
A feeling of unease was permeating the entire office after the announcement.
Corruption was permeating every level of the organization.
The aroma of freshly baked bread was permeating the old house.
