Congo franc

noun
currency of the Democratic Republic of the Congo
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑŋgoʊ ˌfræŋk / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋgəʊ ˌfræŋk / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of the Democratic Republic of the Congo

Examples

The Congo franc currency
She deposited her savings in an account denominated in Congo francs.
The price of bread doubled when the value of the Congo franc fell.
Tourists are advised to exchange their dollars for Congo francs upon arrival in Kinshasa.
