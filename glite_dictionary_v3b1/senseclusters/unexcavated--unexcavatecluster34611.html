unexcavated

adjective
not yet dug up
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈɛkskəˌveɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈɛkskəveɪtɪd / (BrE)
not yet dug up, uncovered, or explored, especially in the context of archaeology or construction

Examples

An unexcavated site
The ancient city still has many unexcavated areas that may hold important historical secrets.
Archaeologists are excited about the unexcavated tombs discovered beneath the old temple.
Much of the site remains unexcavated due to lack of funding and resources.

Related Terms

  • synonym: undiscovered - not yet found or revealed
