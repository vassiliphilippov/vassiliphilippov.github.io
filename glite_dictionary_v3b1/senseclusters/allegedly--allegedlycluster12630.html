Used to convey that something is claimed to be the case or have taken place, although there is no proof.

Examples

Is allegedly stolen

The politician was allegedly involved in the scandal, though no evidence was presented.

Synonyms

    purportedly is used in formal reports to present a claim, while allegedly states that something is claimed without proof.
  • supposedly
    3.4 24/m
    supposedly often reflects common belief or rumor, while allegedly emphasizes an external claim that lacks firm proof.
