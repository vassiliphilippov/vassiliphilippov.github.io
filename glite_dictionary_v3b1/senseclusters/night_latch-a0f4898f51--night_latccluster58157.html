night latch

noun
lock operated by knob inside, key outside
7.2 6.4
Pronunciation: /naɪt lætʃ/ (AmE), /naɪt lætʃ/ (BrE)
A type of door lock that can be operated from the inside by a knob and from the outside with a key.

Examples

A night latch lock

The front door is fitted with a night latch for extra security.
You need a key to open the night latch from the outside.
