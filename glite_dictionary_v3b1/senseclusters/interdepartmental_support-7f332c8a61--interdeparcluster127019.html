interdepartmental support

noun
cooperation between departments for support
8.1
Pronunciation: / ɪn.tɚ.dɪˌpɑrt.mən.tl səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ɪn.tə.dɪˌpɑːt.mən.tl səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
assistance in logistics or administration provided by one or more departments or organizations to one or more other departments, especially within government or large institutions

Examples

Effective interdepartmental support
Effective interdepartmental support is essential for coordinating emergency response across agencies.
The lack of interdepartmental support hampered progress on the joint infrastructure project.
Training encouraged more interdepartmental support to increase efficiency and collaboration within the university.

  • synonym: interagency cooperation - collaboration among different government agencies
