hollow ache

noun
deep emotional pain or emptiness
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑloʊ eɪk / (AmE), / ˈhɒləʊ eɪk / (BrE)
a deep, persistent pain that feels empty or desolate, often emotional rather than physical

Examples

He tried to smile, but the hollow ache in his chest lingered.
Hearing the old song brought back a hollow ache he thought he had forgotten.
