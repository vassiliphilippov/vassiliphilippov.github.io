trailside

adjective
beside a trail
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪlˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈtreɪlˌsaɪd / (BrE)
located at or immediately beside a trail or path

Examples

Trailside bench
They enjoyed a picnic at a trailside bench overlooking the forest.
A trailside sign provided information about the local wildlife.
We found a trailside shop selling snacks and maps for hikers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: off-trail - away from or not on a trail
  • synonym: roadside - located at or beside a road
