chill

noun
a sudden feeling of fear or excitement
5.6 3.3
Pronunciation: /tʃɪl/ (AmE), /tʃɪl/ (BrE)
a sudden feeling of fear, excitement, or dread, often felt as a shiver

Examples

A chill down my spine

There was a noticeable chill in the air when he mentioned the accident.
