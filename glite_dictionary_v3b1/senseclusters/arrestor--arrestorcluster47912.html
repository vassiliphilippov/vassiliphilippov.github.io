arrestor

noun
safety stopping device
Pronunciation: / əˈrɛstər / (AmE), / əˈrɛstə / (BrE)
a device that stops or slows the movement of something, especially for safety purposes

Examples

Safety arrestor device
The engineer checked the arrestor before starting the machine.
A spark arrestor is required on all equipment to prevent wildfires in this area.
The aircraft carrier uses a cable arrestor to safely stop landing planes.
The emergency arrestor at the end of the runway stopped the aircraft safely.
A spark arrestor is required on some engines to prevent fires in dry areas.
The engineer checked the arrestor to ensure it would function in case of an emergency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accelerator - a device that increases speed or movement
  • synonym: brake - a device for slowing or stopping movement
  • synonym: stopper - a device that blocks or closes something
