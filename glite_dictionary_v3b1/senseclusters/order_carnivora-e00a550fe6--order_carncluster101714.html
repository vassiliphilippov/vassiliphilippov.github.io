order Carnivora

noun
order of carnivorous mammals (cats, dogs, bears, etc.)
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər kɑrˈnɪvərə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə kɑːˈnɪvərə / (BrE)
