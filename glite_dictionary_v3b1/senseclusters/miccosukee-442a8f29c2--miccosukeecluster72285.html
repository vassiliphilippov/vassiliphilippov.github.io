Miccosukee

noun
Muskogean language
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪkəˈsuːki / (AmE), / ˌmɪkəˈsuːki / (BrE)
the language of the Miccosukee people, belonging to the Muskogean language family and traditionally spoken in Florida

Examples

Miccosukee language
The Miccosukee language is taught in some schools to help preserve cultural heritage.
Researchers are documenting Miccosukee to ensure future generations can learn it.
Few people today speak Miccosukee fluently, making language preservation efforts important.
