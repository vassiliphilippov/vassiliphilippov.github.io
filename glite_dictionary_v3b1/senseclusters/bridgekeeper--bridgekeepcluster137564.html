bridgekeeper

noun
gatekeeper or controller
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪdʒˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈbrɪdʒˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person who guards or controls access to a bridge or place (sometimes used figuratively)

Examples

A bridgekeeper stops travelers
The bridgekeeper asked each traveler a question before letting them cross.
As the bridgekeeper of the club, he decided who could join the exclusive group.
In the meeting, she acted as a bridgekeeper, controlling who could speak next.
The bridgekeeper checked each traveler's pass before letting them cross.
A uniformed bridgekeeper stood at the entrance, watching the traffic carefully.
During the festival, the bridgekeeper allowed only residents to cross the old stone bridge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gatekeeper - a person who controls access to something
  • synonym: guard - a person who protects or watches over something
  • synonym: guardian - someone who protects or defends something
