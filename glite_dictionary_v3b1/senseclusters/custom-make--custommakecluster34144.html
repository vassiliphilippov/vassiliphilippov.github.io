custom-make

verb
make to individual specifications
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstəm ˌmeɪk / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəm ˌmeɪk / (BrE)
to make something according to an individual's particular requirements or specifications

Examples

To custom-make a ring
Many couples choose to custom-make their wedding rings to reflect their unique style.
The chef agreed to custom-make a menu to accommodate her dietary restrictions.
You can custom-make furniture online by selecting the materials and measurements you prefer.
