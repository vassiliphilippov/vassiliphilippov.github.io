boo

verb
express disapproval with a vocal sound
5.4 3.6
Pronunciation: /buː/ (AmE), /buː/ (BrE)
to show disapproval by making a loud, deep noise, often during a performance or public event

Examples

To boo a performer
If you don't like the performance, please don't boo; just remain quiet.
Several fans booed the referee when he made the controversial call.
The audience began to boo loudly after the comedian's offensive joke.
