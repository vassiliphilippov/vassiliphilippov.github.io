reillumination

noun
renewed understanding
8.8
Pronunciation: /riː.ɪ.luː.mɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪ.luː.mɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /riː.ɪ.luː.mɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪ.luː.mɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of gaining renewed understanding, insight, or clarity, especially after a period of confusion or uncertainty

Examples

Reillumination of an idea

After a long period of doubt, she experienced reillumination and felt confident in her beliefs again.
The discussion provided a much-needed reillumination of our goals and direction.
