novelist

noun
person who writes novels
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːvəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈnɒvəlɪst / (BrE)
A person who writes novels as a profession or occupation.

Examples

A novelist writes books
As a young novelist, he struggled to get his first book published.
Many readers consider her the most inventive novelist of her generation.
The novelist spent years researching before completing her historical trilogy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: author - a writer of books or other works
  • antonym: poet - a writer of poems
