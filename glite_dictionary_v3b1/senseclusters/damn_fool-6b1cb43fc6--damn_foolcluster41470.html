damn fool

noun
reckless or foolish person
Pronunciation: / dæm fuːl / (AmE), / dæm fuːl / (BrE)
a person who acts in a very foolish, reckless, or thoughtless way

Examples

He is a damn fool
Only a damn fool would try to fix electrical wiring without turning off the power.
She called him a damn fool for driving so fast in the rain.
Don't be a damn fool and forget your passport before the trip.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence or ability
  • synonym: idiot - a person who acts in a very stupid or foolish way
