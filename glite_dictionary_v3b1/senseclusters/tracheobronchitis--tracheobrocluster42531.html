tracheobronchitis

noun
inflammation of the trachea and bronchi
4.5
Pronunciation: / treɪkioʊˌbrɑːŋˈkaɪtɪs / (AmE), / trəˌkiːəʊbrɒŋˈkaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A respiratory infection characterized by inflammation of the trachea and the bronchi.

Examples

Tracheobronchitis infection
Antibiotics are not always required for tracheobronchitis, especially if it's caused by a virus.
Children are more susceptible to viral tracheobronchitis during the winter months.
The doctor diagnosed the patient with tracheobronchitis after reviewing the symptoms and chest X-ray.
