nebula

noun
cloudiness, especially in urine (medical, rare)
3.0 9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈneb.jə.lə/ /; / /ˈneb.jʊ.lə/ (AmE), /ˈneb.jʊ.lə/ /; / /ˈneb.jə.lə/ (BrE)
a cloudy appearance, especially in the urine, typically indicating the presence of suspended matter; now rare and chiefly medical or historical

Examples

Urine nebula
A nebula in the urine can be caused by the presence of pus, phosphates, or bacteria.
In older medical texts, nebula often referred to cloudiness as a sign of infection.
The doctor noted a pronounced nebula in the collected urine sample, prompting further analysis.
