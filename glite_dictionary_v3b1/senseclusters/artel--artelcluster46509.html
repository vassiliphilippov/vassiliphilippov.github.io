artel

noun
Russian workers' cooperative
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɛl / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɛl / (BrE)
a cooperative group of workers, especially in Russia, who share work and profits collectively

Examples

An artel group
The fishermen formed an artel to share their catch and profits.
During the early 20th century, many Russian peasants joined an artel for mutual support.
Each member of the artel contributed equally to the group's success.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collective - a group of people acting together for a common purpose
  • synonym: cooperative - an organization owned and run jointly by its members
