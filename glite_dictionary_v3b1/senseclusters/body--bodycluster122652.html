body

noun
an organized collection or system (e.g., of knowledge, laws)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdi /; / ˈbɑːdi / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
a collection or group of things, ideas, works, or people considered as a single entity

Examples

Body of work
The student studied the entire body of Shakespeare's works for her thesis.
There is now a comprehensive body of research on climate change.
This law is part of the larger body of international agreements.
A new advisory body was formed to address community issues.
Each body within the committee had a distinct role.
The school's body of students voted for a new president.

  • synonym: corpus - a large collection of written or spoken material; body of work
  • synonym: organization - an organized group with a particular purpose
