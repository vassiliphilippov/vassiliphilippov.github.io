custodianship

noun
position or role of a custodian
6.5 5.6
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈstoʊ.dɪ.ən.ʃɪp/ /; / /kʌ.ˈstoʊ.dɪ.ən.ʃɪp/ (AmE), /kə.ˈstəʊ.dɪ.ən.ʃɪp/ /; / /kʊ.ˈstəʊ.dɪ.ən.ʃɪp/ (BrE)
the role or responsibility of taking care of, managing, or being in charge of something or someone (including things like property, people, or information)
