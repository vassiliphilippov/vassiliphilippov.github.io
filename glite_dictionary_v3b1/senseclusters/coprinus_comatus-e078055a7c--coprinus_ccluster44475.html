Coprinus comatus

noun
a shaggy white edible mushroom
Pronunciation: / koʊˈpraɪnəs koʊˈmeɪtəs / (AmE), / kɒˈprɪnəs kəʊˈmeɪtəs / (BrE)
a common edible mushroom with a long, shaggy white cap and black spores, also known as the shaggy ink cap or lawyer's wig
