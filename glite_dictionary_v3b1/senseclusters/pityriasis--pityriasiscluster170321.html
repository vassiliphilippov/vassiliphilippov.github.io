pityriasis

noun
a skin disease causing flaking
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪtɪˈraɪəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌpɪtɪˈraɪəsɪs / (BrE)
Any of several skin disorders characterized by the shedding of dry, flaky scales from the skin.

Examples

pityriasis skin disorder
Common types of pityriasis include pityriasis rosea and pityriasis versicolor.
The dermatologist diagnosed the patient with pityriasis after examining the flaky patches on his skin.
Treatments for pityriasis focus on relieving symptoms and preventing further skin irritation.
