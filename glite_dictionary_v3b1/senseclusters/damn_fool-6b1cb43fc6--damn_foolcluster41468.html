damn fool

adjective
extremely foolish
Pronunciation: / dæm fuːl / (AmE), / dam fuːl / (BrE)
extremely foolish, reckless, or lacking in good judgment

Examples

A damn fool idea
It was a damn fool idea to try to fix the roof during the storm.
Only a damn fool would ignore the warning signs on the mountain trail.
She called it a damn fool mistake to invest all his money in that scheme.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reckless - showing a lack of care about danger or consequences
  • antonym: sensible - showing good judgment or reason
