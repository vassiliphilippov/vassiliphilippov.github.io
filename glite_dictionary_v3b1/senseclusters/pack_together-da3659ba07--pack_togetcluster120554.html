pack together

verb
compress by pressing together
1.8
Pronunciation: / pæk təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / pæk təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to make something more compact by pressing or compressing its parts closer together

Examples

To pack together tightly
Snowflakes pack together tightly when you make a snowball.
We need to pack together the sand to build a sturdy castle.
You should pack together the clothes to fit everything into one suitcase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compress - make something smaller or more compact by pressing
  • antonym: loosen - make less compact or tight
