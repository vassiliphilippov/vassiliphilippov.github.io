ranchero

adjective
ranch-style; rural
6.7
Pronunciation: / rænˈtʃɛroʊ / (AmE), / rænˈtʃɛərəʊ / (BrE)
relating to a ranch, its lifestyle, or things typical of rural ranch culture, especially in food or music

Examples

Ranchero style food
The restaurant is famous for its ranchero dishes, which are inspired by traditional ranch cooking.
He wore a ranchero hat and boots to the country music festival.
They played lively ranchero music at the outdoor barbecue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rustic - relating to the countryside; simple or rural
  • antonym: urban - relating to a city or town
