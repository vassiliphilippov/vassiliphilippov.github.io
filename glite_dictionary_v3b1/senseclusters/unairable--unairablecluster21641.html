unairable

adjective
not suitable for broadcast
8.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɛrəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈeərəbl / (BrE)
not suitable or permitted to be broadcast on television or radio, often due to content restrictions

Examples

Unairable episode
The network decided the controversial episode was unairable because of its graphic violence.
Some jokes in the comedian's routine were considered unairable by the producers.
Due to strict regulations, the advertisement was deemed unairable in several countries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: broadcastable - suitable or allowed to be broadcast on television or radio
  • synonym: unsuitable - not appropriate or fitting for a particular purpose
