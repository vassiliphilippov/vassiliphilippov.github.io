family Lorisidae

noun
a family of nocturnal Asian primates
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli lɔːˈrɪsɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli lɒˈrɪsɪdiː / (BrE)
a group of slow-moving, usually tailless, omnivorous nocturnal primates found in tropical Asia
