flashbulb

noun
bulb for photographic flash
Pronunciation: / ˈflæʃˌbʌlb / (AmE), / ˈflæʃˌbʌlb / (BrE)
a small electric bulb that produces a brief intense burst of light for taking photographs

Examples

A flashbulb for photos
He collected vintage cameras, each with its own unique flashbulb mechanism.
Older cameras often required a disposable flashbulb for every photograph taken indoors.
The bright burst from the flashbulb startled everyone at the birthday party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flash - a brief burst of bright light used in photography
