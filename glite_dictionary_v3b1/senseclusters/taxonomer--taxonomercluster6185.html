taxonomer

noun
biologist specializing in taxonomy
4.2
Pronunciation: / tækˈsɑnəmər / (AmE), / tækˈsɒnəmə / (BrE)
a biologist who specializes in classifying organisms based on their structure, origin, and behavior

Examples

Taxonomer studies species
As a taxonomer, her research focuses on identifying and grouping newly discovered species.
Many taxonomers use DNA analysis to determine relationships among organisms.
The museum hired a taxonomer to help catalogue their insect collection.
