chargeman

noun
man who sets explosives
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrdʒˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːdʒˌmæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to set and detonate explosives, especially in mining or construction

Examples

A chargeman sets explosives
A skilled chargeman is essential to ensure controlled explosions during construction projects.
As a former chargeman in the quarry, he was familiar with all safety protocols.
The chargeman prepared the dynamite before the blast.
