Coelenterata

noun
obsolete group of aquatic invertebrates (jellyfish, corals, etc.)
9.9
Pronunciation: / siˌlɛntəˈreɪtə /; / siːlɛnˈtɛrətə / (AmE), / siːˌlɛntəˈreɪtə /; / sɪlɛnˈtɛrətə / (BrE)
an obsolete scientific group of aquatic invertebrate animals, including hydras, jellyfish, sea anemones, and corals, now divided into Cnidaria and Ctenophora

Examples
