après

preposition
after an event
7.7
Pronunciation: / æˈpreɪ / (AmE), / ˈæpreɪ / (BrE)
used in English to mean 'after', especially in set phrases referring to activities following a main event

Examples

Après-dinner walk
They planned an après-dinner walk through the city center.
The hotel is famous for its lively après-ski parties every winter evening.
Many guests stayed for the après-conference reception to network and relax.

Related Terms

  • synonym: after - following in time or order
  • antonym: before - earlier in time or order
