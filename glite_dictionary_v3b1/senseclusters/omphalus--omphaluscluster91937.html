omphalus

noun
medical term for the navel
Pronunciation: / ˈɑmfələs / (AmE), / ˈɒmfələs / (BrE)
the scar on the abdomen marking the point where the umbilical cord was attached; the navel

Examples

The omphalus scar
Some ancient cultures regarded the omphalus as a symbol of life and creation.
The baby's omphalus healed completely after the umbilical stump fell off.
The surgeon made a small incision just above the omphalus to begin the operation.
