wheatworm

noun
parasitic roundworm affecting wheat
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːtwɜːrm / (AmE), / ˈwiːtwɜːm / (BrE)
a small roundworm that is parasitic on wheat plants, often causing damage to crops

Examples

A wheatworm infestation
Farmers often inspect young wheat plants for signs of a wheatworm infestation.
The wheatworms destroyed several acres before the problem was discovered and treated.
The presence of wheatworm in the fields led to a significant decrease in wheat yields that year.
