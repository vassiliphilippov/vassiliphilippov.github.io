crab

verb
complain or grumble
9.9
Pronunciation: / kræb / (AmE), / kræb / (BrE)
to complain or grumble, especially in a petty or persistent way

Examples

To crab about work
He would always crab whenever the smallest thing went wrong at work.
There's no point if you just crab about every change we make.
They kept crabbing about the weather all day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complain - to express dissatisfaction or annoyance about something
  • antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration of something or someone
