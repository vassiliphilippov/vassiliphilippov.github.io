commanding officer

noun
officer in charge of a military unit
Pronunciation: / kəˈmændɪŋ ˈɑfəsər / (AmE), / kəˈmɑːndɪŋ ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
an officer who is in charge of a military unit and has authority over its personnel and operations

Examples

The commanding officer reports
She reported her concerns directly to the commanding officer of the regiment.
The commanding officer addressed the soldiers before the mission began.
The battalion's commanding officer was responsible for making critical decisions during the operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: officer - a person holding a position of authority in the armed forces
