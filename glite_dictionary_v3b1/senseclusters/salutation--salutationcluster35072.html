salutation

noun
greeting at start of letter
Pronunciation: / ˌsæləˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsaljʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a word or phrase used as a greeting at the beginning of a letter or speech

Examples

Letter salutation
Please make sure to use an appropriate salutation when writing formal letters.
She started the letter with a warm salutation before moving to the main message.
The most common salutation in business letters is 'Dear Sir or Madam.'
