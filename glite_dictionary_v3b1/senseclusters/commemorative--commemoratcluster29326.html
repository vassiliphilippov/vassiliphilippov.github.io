commemorative

adjective
honoring or celebrating someone or something
7.2
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɛmɚˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / kəˈmɛm(ə)rətɪv / (BrE)
intended to honor or remember a person, event, or occasion, especially by being created or done as a tribute.

Examples

Commemorative stamp
A commemorative stamp was issued to celebrate the anniversary of the event.
He collected several commemorative coins from the Olympic Games.
The museum unveiled a commemorative plaque in honor of the artist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: memorial - something, especially a structure, established to remind people of a person or event
