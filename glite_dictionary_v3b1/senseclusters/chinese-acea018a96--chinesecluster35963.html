Chinese

noun
a person from China
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪˈniz / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of China, including both the People's Republic of China and, historically, the Republic of China (Taiwan)

Examples

A Chinese person
Chinese have greatly influenced international cuisine and culture.
Many Chinese living abroad celebrate Lunar New Year with family traditions.
The new exchange student is a Chinese from Beijing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Chinese people - the people of China collectively
