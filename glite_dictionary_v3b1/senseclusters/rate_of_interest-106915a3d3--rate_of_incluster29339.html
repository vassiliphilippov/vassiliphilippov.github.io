rate of interest

noun
percentage charged for borrowed money
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪt əv ˈɪntrəst / (AmE), / ˌreɪt əv ˈɪntrəst / (BrE)
The percentage of an amount of money charged by a lender for its use, typically expressed annually.

Examples

The rate of interest rises
Mortgages can become expensive if the rate of interest rises suddenly.
The bank increased its rate of interest on personal loans this year.
You should compare the rate of interest offered by different banks before investing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interest rate - the percentage of a sum of money charged for its use
