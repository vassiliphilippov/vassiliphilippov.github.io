fertility

noun
the ability to produce offspring or crops
Pronunciation: / fərˈtɪləti / (AmE), / fəˈtɪləti / (BrE)
The ability to produce offspring or new growth; the quality of being fertile.

Examples

Fertility treatment
Recent advances in medicine have improved fertility treatments for many couples.
Researchers are studying how environmental factors affect animal fertility.
The fertility of the soil allowed the farmers to grow abundant crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fecundity - the ability to produce an abundance of offspring or new growth
  • antonym: infertility - the inability to conceive children or young
