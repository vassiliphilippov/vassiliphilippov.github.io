repeatability

noun
consistency of results
4.5
Pronunciation: / rɪˌpiːtəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / rɪˌpiːtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being able to be done again with the same results each time

Examples

The repeatability of tests
The repeatability of the test ensures that scientists can trust the results.
High repeatability is essential for any scientific experiment to be considered reliable.
The machine was evaluated for its repeatability in producing identical parts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reproducibility - the ability to achieve the same results when an experiment is repeated by others
  • antonym: variability - the quality of being likely to change or vary
