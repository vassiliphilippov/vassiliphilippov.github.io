growlingly

adverb
in a growling manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that resembles or involves growling, often showing anger or threat

Examples

To growlingly warn
The guard dog growlingly warned strangers to stay away from the gate.
She spoke growlingly when she was angry about the unfair decision.
The actor delivered his lines growlingly to create a sense of menace.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gently - in a mild or kind manner
  • synonym: snarlingly - in a manner that resembles snarling
