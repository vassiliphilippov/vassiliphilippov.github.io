worldlet

noun
miniature world model
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrldlət / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldlət / (BrE)
a small model or diorama representing a miniature world, often created for display or study

Examples

A model worldlet
The museum displayed a worldlet showing a tiny village surrounded by forests and rivers.
He spent months crafting a detailed worldlet for his school science project.
Children were fascinated by the colorful worldlet inside the glass dome at the exhibition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diorama - a three-dimensional miniature scene
  • synonym: model - a small copy of something, often used for display
