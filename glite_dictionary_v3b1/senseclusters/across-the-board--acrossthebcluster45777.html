across-the-board

adjective
affecting everyone or everything; all-encompassing
Pronunciation: / əˌkrɔs ðə ˈbɔrd / (AmE), / əˌkrɒs ðə ˈbɔːd / (BrE)
affecting or applying to everyone or everything within a group, without exception; broad in scope

Examples

An across-the-board ban
An across-the-board ban on smoking in public places was implemented last year.
The company announced across-the-board pay raises for all employees.
The senator called for across-the-board budget cuts to reduce the deficit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blanket - covering all cases or instances; lacking exceptions
  • synonym: universal - existing or affecting all; applicable to all members of a group
