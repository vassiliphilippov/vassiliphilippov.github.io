hup

interjection
marching exclamation
9.9
Pronunciation: / hʌp / (AmE), / hʌp / (BrE)
an exclamation used to urge someone to move quickly or to mark the rhythm in marching or exercise

Examples

Hup two three
The drill instructor shouted hup to keep the squad in step during the parade.
With a loud hup, the coach signaled the team to start running laps.
Every time the group needed to speed up, someone would call out hup to encourage them.
