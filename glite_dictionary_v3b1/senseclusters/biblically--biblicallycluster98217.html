biblically

adverb
to an epic degree
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪblɪkli / (AmE), / ˈbɪblɪkli / (BrE)
in an extremely great or severe way, often suggesting a scale or intensity like that found in the Bible.

Examples

Biblically large flood
The city was biblically flooded after days of relentless rain.
He was biblically tired after working three shifts in a row.
The team lost biblically, with a score no one had expected.

  • antonym: mildly - to a small or moderate degree
