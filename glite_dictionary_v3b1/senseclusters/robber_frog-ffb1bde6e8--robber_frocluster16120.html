robber frog

noun
small terrestrial frog of tropical America
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑbər frɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈrɒbə frɒɡ / (BrE)
A small terrestrial frog native to tropical America, belonging to certain genera in the family Craugastoridae.

Examples

Central American robber frog
Scientists have discovered a new species of robber frog in Central America.
The robber frog is commonly found hiding beneath leaf litter in rainforests.
Unlike tree frogs, robber frogs tend to live entirely on the forest floor.
