detective

noun
person who investigates crimes
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɛktɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈtɛktɪv / (BrE)
a police officer or person whose job is to investigate crimes and find information

Examples

A detective officer
She always wanted to be a detective after reading mystery novels as a child.
The detective carefully examined the crime scene for any hidden clues.
The detective questioned several witnesses to reconstruct what happened that night.
A skilled detective must pay close attention to even the smallest detail.
The detective spent weeks piecing together clues from the crime scene.
The promotion transformed her from a uniformed officer into a seasoned detective.

Related Terms

  • synonym: investigator - a person who investigates, especially in official capacity
  • synonym: officer - a person holding a position of authority in the police
  • antonym: suspect - a person thought to be guilty of a crime
