evergreen blueberry

noun
eastern US shrub with evergreen leaves and dark berries
Pronunciation: / ˈɛvɚˌɡrin ˈbluːˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌɡriːn ˈbluːˌbɛri / (BrE)
a shrub native to the eastern United States, with shiny evergreen leaves and bluish-black berries

Examples

The evergreen blueberry shrub
The evergreen blueberry thrives in acidic woodland soils and produces fruit in late summer.
The glossy leaves of the evergreen blueberry make it an attractive ornamental shrub.
We planted an evergreen blueberry to attract birds to our garden year-round.
