punctual

adjective
on time; prompt
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌŋktʃuəl / (AmE), / ˈpʌŋktʃuːəl / (BrE)
arriving, happening, or being done at the agreed or correct time; on time

Examples

Always punctual for meetings
He is always punctual for meetings, rarely arriving even a minute late.
Her punctual attendance impressed the interview panel.
We appreciate employees who are punctual and complete their tasks on time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: late - not arriving or happening at the expected time
  • synonym: prompt - done or acting quickly and without delay
