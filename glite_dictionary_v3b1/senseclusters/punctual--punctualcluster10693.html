punctual

adjective
on time; prompt

arriving, happening, or being done at the agreed or correct time; on time

Examples

Always punctual for meetings
He is always punctual for meetings, rarely arriving even a minute late.
Her punctual attendance impressed the interview panel.
We appreciate employees who are punctual and complete their tasks on time.

Synonyms

  • on time
    1.0 12/m
    on time means arriving at the planned moment, while punctual describes someone or something that is reliably not late.
  • right on time
    1.2 0.87/m
    right on time means exactly at the scheduled minute, while punctual describes being regularly on time without delay.
  • on schedule
    2.0 2.68/m
    on schedule means following the planned timetable, while punctual focuses on a person or event arriving at the agreed time.
