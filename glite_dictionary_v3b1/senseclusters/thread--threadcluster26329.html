thread

verb
pass a thread through
Pronunciation: / θrɛd / (AmE), / θrɛd / (BrE)
to pass thread or something similar through an opening, or to arrange objects onto a string or wire

Examples

Every Christmas, we thread popcorn and cranberries to make garlands for the tree.

She carefully threaded the tiny beads onto the silver wire.
She carefully threaded the tape through the cassette player.
The tailor showed me how to thread the ribbon through the loops.
To start the projector, you need to thread the film properly.
