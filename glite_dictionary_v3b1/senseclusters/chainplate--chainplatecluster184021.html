chainplate

noun
boat rigging fitting
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪnˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪnˌpleɪt / (BrE)
a metal plate on a boat to which the rigging or shrouds are securely attached

Examples

Boat chainplate
The sailor checked the chainplate for signs of corrosion before setting sail.
A damaged chainplate can compromise the safety of the entire rigging system.
During the inspection, they found that the chainplate needed to be replaced due to rust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleat - a fitting on a boat for securing ropes
  • synonym: shroud - a rope or wire supporting a ship's mast
