Turkish tobacco

noun
dark, aromatic cigarette tobacco from eastern Europe
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜr.kɪʃ təˈbækoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.kɪʃ təˈbæk.əʊ / (BrE)
a dark, aromatic tobacco from eastern Europe, especially used in cigarettes
