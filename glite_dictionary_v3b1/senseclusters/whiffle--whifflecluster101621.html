whiffle

noun
small puff of air
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪfəl / (AmE), / ˈwɪfəl / (BrE)
a small puff of air or a faint trace of a smell carried by the air

Examples

A whiffle of perfume
A gentle whiffle of perfume reached her as the train door opened.
He caught a whiffle of fresh bread as he walked past the bakery.
A sudden whiffle stirred the papers on the desk near the open window.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: gust - a strong, brief rush of wind
  • synonym: whiff - a brief, slight smell or puff of air
