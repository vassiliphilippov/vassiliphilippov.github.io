cartridge

noun
container holding photographic film
4.9 0.13/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ër.tr…™d í / (AmE), / Ààk…ëÀê.tr…™d í / (BrE)
A light-tight container that holds photographic film and supplies it for exposure in a camera.

Examples

Film cartridge
Most modern cameras no longer require a film cartridge.
The photographer loaded a new cartridge into his camera before heading outside.
You need to rewind the cartridge before removing it from the camera.

Synonyms

  • magazine
    6.5 0.03/m
    magazine is a removable chamber often used in larger or professional cameras, while cartridge is a compact light-tight film container for smaller cameras.
