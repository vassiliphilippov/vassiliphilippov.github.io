pansexualist community

noun
group promoting sexual orientation inclusivity
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpænˈsɛkʃuəlɪst kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ˌpanˈsɛkʃuəlɪst kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
A group of people advocating for inclusivity and acceptance of all sexual orientations, particularly those identifying as pansexual.

Examples

Pansexualist community events
Members of the pansexualist community often organize events to promote awareness and understanding.
She found acceptance and support after joining the local pansexualist community.
The pansexualist community advocates for equal rights and recognition across all sexual orientations.
