framed

adjective
enclosed with a frame
Pronunciation: / freɪmd / (AmE), / freɪmd / (BrE)
enclosed or bordered with a frame, especially for support or decoration

Examples

A framed certificate
He gave her a beautifully framed certificate for her achievement.
She hung several framed photos on the living room wall.
The gallery displayed an impressive collection of framed paintings.
