experiment

verb
try something new to gain experience
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛrəmənt / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛrɪmənt / (BrE)
to try something new, especially to discover what effect it will have or to gain experience

Examples

To experiment with food
She decided to experiment with a different exercise routine this month.
The company likes to experiment before introducing a new product to the market.
Young chefs often experiment with unusual flavor combinations to create unique dishes.
