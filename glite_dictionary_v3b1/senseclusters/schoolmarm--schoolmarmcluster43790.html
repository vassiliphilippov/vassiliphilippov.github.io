schoolmarm

noun
a strict female schoolteacher
9.8 0.23/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàskuÀêlÀåm…ërm / (AmE), / ÀàskuÀêlÀåm…ëÀêm / (BrE)
A woman schoolteacher, typically one regarded as strict or old-fashioned, especially in a rural or historical context.

Examples

A strict schoolmarm
Her reputation as a schoolmarm made the students sit up straight whenever she entered the classroom.
In the old Western towns, a schoolmarm often taught all ages in a one-room schoolhouse.
The new principal reminded everyone of a classic schoolmarm, strict but fair.
