lexically

adverb
with regard to words or vocabulary
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛksɪkli / (AmE), / ˈlɛksɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to words or the vocabulary of a language, rather than its grammar or meaning

Examples

To use lexically related words
In this context, the word is lexically ambiguous.
The language is lexically rich, with many synonyms for common words.
The two terms are lexically different but similar in meaning.
