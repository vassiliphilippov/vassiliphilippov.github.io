Theophrastaceae

noun
tropical American plant family

Pronunciation: / θiˌɑːfræˈsteɪsiˌi /; / ˌθiː.ə.fræˈsteɪ.siː / (AmE), / θiˌɒfræˈsteɪsiː /; / ˌθiː.ə.fræˈsteɪ.siː / (BrE)
a family of mainly tropical American trees and shrubs, similar to or sometimes included in the Myrsinaceae family
