abysmally

adverb
in an extremely bad way
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈbɪzməli / (AmE), / əˈbɪzməli / (BrE)
In an extremely bad or terrible manner.

Examples

To perform abysmally
He managed the project abysmally, causing significant delays and confusion.
Our team played abysmally in the championship final and lost by a wide margin.
The service at the restaurant was abysmally slow, which made the experience quite unpleasant.
