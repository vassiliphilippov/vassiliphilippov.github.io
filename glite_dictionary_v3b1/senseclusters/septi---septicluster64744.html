septi-

x
prefix meaning seven
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛptaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɛptaɪ / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'seven', used to form words that relate to the number seven

Examples

septi-lateral shape
The prefix septi- is used in words like septilateral, which means having seven sides.
In biology, septi- can appear in terms describing structures with seven parts.
The word septipartite uses the septi- prefix to indicate something divided into seven sections.
