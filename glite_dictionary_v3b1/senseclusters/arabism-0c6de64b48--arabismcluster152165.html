Arabism

noun
Arab cultural identity
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈærəbɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈærəbɪzəm / (BrE)
the cultural identity, traditions, or characteristics associated with Arab people or societies

Examples

Arabism in culture
The festival celebrated Arabism through traditional music, dance, and cuisine.
Her research explored the traces of Arabism in contemporary North African film and music.
Many writers express Arabism in their works by highlighting shared history and values.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Arab culture - the customs, arts, and social institutions of Arab people
  • synonym: Arab nationalism - the political ideology advocating unity among Arab countries
