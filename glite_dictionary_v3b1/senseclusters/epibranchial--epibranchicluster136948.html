epibranchial

adjective
above the gills
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈbræŋkiəl / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈbræŋkɪəl / (BrE)
situated above or relating to the gills or branchial arches in animals

Examples

Epibranchial cartilage in fish
The epibranchial cartilage plays a key role in the structure of fish gills.
Researchers studied the development of epibranchial elements in amphibian larvae.
Damage to the epibranchial region can affect an animal's ability to breathe underwater.

Related Terms

  • synonym: branchial - relating to the gills or gill arches
