chocolate mousse

noun
light, rich chocolate dessert
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑklət mus / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət muːs / (BrE)
A rich, airy dessert made from chocolate, typically combined with eggs, cream, and sugar and whipped to a light texture.

Examples

A chocolate mousse dessert
She served a delicious chocolate mousse for dessert at the dinner party.
The restaurant’s chocolate mousse was incredibly rich and silky.
You can make chocolate mousse ahead of time and chill it before serving.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mousse - A light, airy dessert made with cream and beaten egg whites
