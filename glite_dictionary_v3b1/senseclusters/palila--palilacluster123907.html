palila

noun
Hawaiian finch species
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈliːlə / (AmE), / pəˈliːlə / (BrE)

Examples

The palila bird
The palila is found only on the slopes of Mauna Kea in Hawaii.
Efforts to save the palila include habitat restoration and protection from predators.
Birdwatchers travel to Hawaii hoping to catch a glimpse of the rare palila.
