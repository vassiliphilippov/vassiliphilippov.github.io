adactylia

noun
congenital absence of fingers or toes
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌæ.dækˈtɪl.i.ə / (AmE), / ˌæ.dækˈtɪl.i.ə / (BrE)
A congenital condition characterized by the absence of fingers and/or toes.

Examples

Congenital adactylia condition
Cases of adactylia are extremely rare and often associated with other anomalies.
Her surgery team specializes in reconstructive procedures for children with adactylia.
The geneticist explained that adactylia was present from birth in the affected child.
