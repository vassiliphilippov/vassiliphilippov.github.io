stealthy

adjective
secretive or cautious to avoid detection
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛlθi / (AmE), / ˈstɛlθi / (BrE)
behaving or done in a cautious and secretive manner, so as not to be noticed

Examples

A stealthy glance
She cast a stealthy glance at her phone during the meeting.
The cat moved in a stealthy way, slipping through the shadows unnoticed.
The thieves made a stealthy escape from the museum after midnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: furtive - attempting to avoid notice or attention, typically because of guilt or secrecy
  • antonym: open - done or acting without concealment or secrecy
