classification

noun
arranging into categories or classes
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌklæsəfɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌklɑːsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌklasɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or act of arranging things into categories or classes according to shared qualities or characteristics

Examples

Classification of animals
Her job involves the classification of data for research purposes.
The classification of animals is an important part of biology.
They discussed the classification of books in the library according to subject.
All books are arranged according to a strict classification system in the library.
Recent advances in machine learning have improved the classification of medical images.
The classification of animals is based on shared characteristics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: categorization - the process of placing things into categories
  • antonym: segregation - the act of separating people or things into different groups
