whale

noun
very large or impressive person
Pronunciation: / weɪl / (AmE), / weɪl / (BrE)
A very large or impressive person, often remarkable in size or qualities.

Examples

A whale of a man
Everyone was amazed when the whale of a man squeezed into the tiny car.
In the world of professional wrestling, he's a real whale both in talent and in stature.
That company's CEO is a whale in the industry, dominating every market he enters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dwarf - person of unusually small size
  • synonym: giant - very large and strong person
