maître

noun
French legal title
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmeɪ.trə/ (AmE), /ˈmeɪ.trə/ (BrE)
a French honorific title used before the name of a lawyer or notary, especially in French-speaking countries

Examples

Maître Dupont signs
The contract was reviewed and signed by maître Lefevre, a respected Parisian lawyer.
In court, the judge addressed the attorney as maître Dubois.
French legal documents often refer to notaries as maître followed by their surname.
