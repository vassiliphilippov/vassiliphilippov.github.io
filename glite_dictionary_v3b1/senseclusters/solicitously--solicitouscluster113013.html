solicitously

adverb
in a caring or concerned manner
Pronunciation: / səˈlɪs.ɪ.təs.li / (AmE), / səˈlɪs.ɪ.təs.li / (BrE)
in a way that shows concern or care for someone's well-being

Examples

Solicitously offered help
He solicitously offered his seat to the elderly woman on the train.
She asked solicitously if I needed anything after my long journey.
The doctor solicitously checked on his patient throughout the night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attentively - in a way that shows attention or care
  • antonym: indifferently - in a way that shows a lack of concern
