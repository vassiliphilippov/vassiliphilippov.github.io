laryngopharyngitis

noun
inflammation of larynx and pharynx
Pronunciation: / ləˌrɪŋɡoʊˌfærɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ləˌrɪŋɡəʊˌfærɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of both the larynx and the pharynx, often causing sore throat and hoarseness.

Examples

Laryngopharyngitis sore throat
Doctors treated the child's laryngopharyngitis with medication and advised rest.
She was diagnosed with laryngopharyngitis after complaining of a persistent sore throat and hoarseness.
Smoking can increase your risk of developing laryngopharyngitis, especially if you already have a cold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laryngitis - inflammation of the larynx
  • synonym: pharyngitis - inflammation of the pharynx
