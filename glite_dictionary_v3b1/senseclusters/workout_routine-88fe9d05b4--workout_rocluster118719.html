workout routine

noun
planned exercise program
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːk.aʊt ruːˈtin / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkaʊt ruːˈtiːn / (BrE)
a planned series of exercises or a regular program of physical activity followed to improve fitness or health

Examples

A workout routine plan
Her workout routine includes weightlifting, cardio, and stretching every morning.
I've been following a new workout routine to get ready for the marathon.
It's important to vary your workout routine to avoid hitting a plateau.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fitness routine - a planned series of exercises aimed at improving fitness
