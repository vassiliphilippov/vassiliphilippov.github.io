gold plate

noun
thin layer of gold coated on a surface
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊld pleɪt / (AmE), / ɡəʊld pleɪt / (BrE)
a thin layer of gold applied to the surface of another material, typically for decoration or protection

Examples

Gold plate on jewelry
Over time, the gold plate on the jewelry may fade with frequent use.
The antique watch was covered in a thin gold plate that had started to wear away.
You can add elegance to cutlery by applying a gold plate to the handles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gilding - the application of gold leaf or gold paint to a surface
