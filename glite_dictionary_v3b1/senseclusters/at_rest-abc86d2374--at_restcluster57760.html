at rest

adjective
euphemism for dead
Pronunciation: / æt rɛst / (AmE), / ət rɛst / (BrE)
used as a respectful or gentle way of saying someone is dead; deceased.

Examples

Laid at rest
Friends and family gathered to say goodbye as she was laid at rest.
She spoke softly of her grandfather, now at rest after a long illness.
The inscription read, 'Here lies John Smith, finally at rest.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: dead - no longer alive
  • synonym: departed - having died; deceased
