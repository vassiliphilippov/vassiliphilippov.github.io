destroyed

adjective
ruined or damaged beyond repair
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstrɔɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɔɪd / (BrE)
extremely damaged, ruined, or made unusable (physically, morally, or in reputation)

Examples

The hurricane left many homes completely destroyed.
A destroyed reputation can be difficult to rebuild.
After the hurricane, thousands of homes were destroyed by the flooding.
The old documents were found destroyed in the fire.
