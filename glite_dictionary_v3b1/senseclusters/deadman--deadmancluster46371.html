deadman

noun
corpse; dead person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈdɛdˌmæn / (BrE)
an informal or archaic term for a dead person or a corpse

Examples

A deadman found
The detective discovered a deadman in the abandoned warehouse late at night.
In old stories, a deadman was sometimes found washed up on the shore.
They reported seeing a deadman near the railway tracks early this morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cadaver - a dead human body, often used in medical contexts
  • synonym: corpse - a dead body, especially of a human
  • antonym: living person - someone who is alive
