warhammer

noun
combat hammer weapon
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrˌhæmər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːˌhamə / (BrE)
a weapon with a heavy hammer-like head, designed for use in combat to strike or crush armor

Examples

A warhammer weapon
The knight swung his warhammer at the armored opponent.
A warhammer was especially effective against enemies wearing heavy plate armor.
He admired the ornate warhammer displayed in the museum's medieval weapons collection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mace - a heavy club with a metal head, used as a weapon
  • antonym: sword - a weapon with a long metal blade and a handle
