sadomasochist

noun
person who enjoys both sadism and masochism
Pronunciation: / ˌseɪdoʊˈmæsəkɪst / (AmE), / ˌsædəʊˈmæsəkɪst / (BrE)
a person who derives sexual or psychological pleasure from both inflicting and receiving pain or humiliation

Examples

Sadomasochist in relationships
In some relationships, one partner may identify as a sadomasochist, enjoying both roles.
The documentary explored the lives of several sadomasochists who find fulfillment in their practices.
The novel’s main character is a sadomasochist who both inflicts and receives pain during intimate encounters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: masochist - a person who derives pleasure from having pain or humiliation inflicted on them
  • synonym: sadist - a person who derives pleasure from inflicting pain on others
