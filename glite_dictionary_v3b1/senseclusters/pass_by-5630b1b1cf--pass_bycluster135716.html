pass by

verb
move past without stopping
Pronunciation: / pæs baɪ / (AmE), / pɑːs baɪ / (BrE)
to move past someone or something without stopping

Examples

To pass by a shop
He didn't even notice me as he passed by on his way to work.
I watched the parade pass by my house from the window.
Several cars passed by while we waited at the bus stop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: go by - to move past or be seen while moving past
  • synonym: overtake - to catch up with and move in front of while traveling in the same direction
