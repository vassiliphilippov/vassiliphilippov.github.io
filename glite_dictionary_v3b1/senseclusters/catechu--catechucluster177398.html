catechu

noun
an East Indian tree used for dye and tanning
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkætɪˌtʃu / (AmE), / ˈkætɪˌtjuː / (BrE)
an East Indian tree (Acacia catechu) known for its extract used in dyes, tanning, and medicine

Examples

Catechu tree plantation
A plantation of catechu trees stretched along the riverbank.
The bark of the catechu tree is valued for its medicinal properties.
They harvested catechu to use as a natural dye for fabric.
