unusualness

noun

In data analysis and anomaly detection, a quantitative measure or score that indicates how atypical or outlier-like a particular observation or data point is within a dataset.

Examples

Unusualness score in data
The model assigns an unusualness score to each transaction to flag potential fraud.
Researchers used PCA to compute the unusualness of sensor readings across the time series.
A high unusualness value indicates the observation may be an outlier requiring further investigation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anomaly score - a numerical value indicating how anomalous or unexpected a data point is
  • antonym: commonness - the state or measure of being typical or frequent (opposite of unusualness)
  • synonym: outlier - an observation that differs markedly from other observations in a dataset
