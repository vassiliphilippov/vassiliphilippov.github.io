synapsid reptile

noun
extinct reptile with single skull opening; mammal ancestor
6.0 9.4
Pronunciation: /sɪ.ˈnæp.sɪd ˈrɛp.taɪl/ /; / /sɪ.ˈnæp.sɪd ˈrɛp.taɪ.əl/ (AmE), /sɪ.ˈnæp.sɪd ˈrɛp.taɪl/ /; / /sɪ.ˈnæp.sɪd ˈrɛp.taɪ.əl/ (BrE)
