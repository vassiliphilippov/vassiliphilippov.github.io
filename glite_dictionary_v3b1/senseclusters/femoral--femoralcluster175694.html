femoral

adjective
relating to the thigh or femur
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛmərəl / (AmE), / ˈfiːmərəl / (BrE)
relating to the femur (thigh bone) or the thigh region

Examples

Femoral artery
A femoral fracture often requires immediate medical attention.

The surgeon inserted a catheter into the femoral artery during the procedure.
