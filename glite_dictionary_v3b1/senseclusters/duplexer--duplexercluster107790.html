duplexer

noun
double-sided printing device
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈduː.plek.sər/ /; / /ˈdjuː.plek.sər/ (AmE), /ˈdjuː.plek.sə/ /; / /ˈduː.plek.sə/ (BrE)
a device in a printer or copier that allows automatic printing on both sides of a sheet of paper

Examples

Printer duplexer
The new printer came with a built-in duplexer for efficient double-sided printing.
By using the duplexer, the office reduced its paper consumption significantly.
If your copier has a duplexer, you can easily print booklets without flipping pages manually.
