subungual

adjective
under a nail
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈʌŋɡwəl / (AmE), / sʌbˈʌŋɡwəl / (BrE)
located or occurring under a fingernail or toenail

Examples

Subungual infection
The patient developed a subungual infection after injuring his toe.
A subungual hematoma can cause pain and discoloration beneath the nail.
Doctors often use imaging to detect subungual tumors in the fingers or toes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: periungual - situated around a fingernail or toenail
