plus

adjective
on the positive side or higher than a base level
1.2
Pronunciation: / plʌs / (AmE), / plʌs / (BrE)
on the positive side or at a level above a stated standard, especially used to indicate values greater than zero or a grade slightly higher than a base grade

Examples

A B plus grade
She received a B plus on her final exam for her outstanding effort.
The temperature is expected to rise to plus 7 degrees Celsius this afternoon.
This investment has delivered a plus value for shareholders this quarter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: minus - on the negative side or lower end of a scale
