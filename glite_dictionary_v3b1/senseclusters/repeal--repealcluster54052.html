Examples

Synonyms

    abrogate is a formal act to end a law or agreement, while repeal refers specifically to cancelling a law or rule.

  • rescind
    7.9 2.09/m

  • revoke
    6.2 3.96/m

  • annul
    6.3 0.32/m

  • nullify
    7.2 1.23/m
    nullify makes a law or decision have no legal effect, while repeal officially cancels a law or rule by authority.
  • invalidate
    6.1 2.77/m
    invalidate means to make something legally not valid, while repeal is the formal removal of a law or rule.
  • overturn
    5.6 5.85/m
    overturn reverses a court or authority decision, while repeal cancels a law or rule through official removal.
  • cancel
    1.0 9.36/m
