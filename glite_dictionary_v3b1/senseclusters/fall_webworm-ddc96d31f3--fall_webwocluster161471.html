fall webworm

noun
caterpillar pest that forms webs on trees
6.0 9.9
Pronunciation: / fɔl ˈwɛbˌwɝm / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈwɛbˌwɜːm / (BrE)
a moth caterpillar (Hyphantria cunea) whose larvae spin large webs on deciduous trees in late summer and fall, often causing defoliation

The fall webworm is often seen covering tree branches with its distinctive webs in late summer.
