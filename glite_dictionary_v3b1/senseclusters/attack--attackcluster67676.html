attack

noun
onset of destructive or corrosive process
4.1
Pronunciation: / əˈtæk / (AmE), / əˈtak / (BrE)
the onset of a destructive or corrosive process, especially caused by chemical or environmental agents

Examples

This metal coating offers strong resistance to attack from saltwater environments.
