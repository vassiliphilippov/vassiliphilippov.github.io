overcomer

noun
person who overcomes adversity or opposition
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkʌmər / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkʌmə / (BrE)
a person who succeeds in dealing with or gaining control over difficulties or opposition, often by determination or strength

Examples

The program is designed to help every participant see themselves as an overcomer rather than a victim.
