epidermic

adjective
relating to the outer layer of skin
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈdɜrmɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈdɜːmɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the cuticle or outermost layer of an organism, especially the skin or epidermis.

Examples

epidermic absorption
Some medications are designed for epidermic absorption through the skin.
The epidermic layer acts as the body's first line of defense against pathogens.
The study focused on the structure of epidermic cells in amphibians.
