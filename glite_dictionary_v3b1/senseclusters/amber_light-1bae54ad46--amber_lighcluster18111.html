amber light

noun
warm amber-coloured lighting
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈæmbə laɪt / (AmE), / ˈæmbə laɪt / (BrE)
light with a warm yellow-orange (amber) colour used to create a cosy, warm, or atmospheric effect in photography, film, interiors, or design.

Examples

Warm amber light
In photography, amber light creates warm tones that enhance sunset portraits.
The cafe installed soft amber light to make the space feel intimate in the evenings.
Film crews preferred amber lights to simulate late-afternoon sun on the indoor set.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ambient light - general background lighting in a space; often contrasted with directional or accent lighting
  • synonym: warm light - light with a warm colour temperature that creates a cosy atmosphere
