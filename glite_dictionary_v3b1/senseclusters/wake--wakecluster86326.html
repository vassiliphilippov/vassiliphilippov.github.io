wake

noun
waves left by a boat
7.1
Pronunciation: / weɪk / (AmE), / weɪk / (BrE)
a trail of waves left on water by a moving boat or ship

Examples

Wake behind a boat
Foam formed in the wake of the ferry as it crossed the bay.
Swimmers are advised to avoid the wake of large ships for their safety.
The speedboat's wake rocked the smaller fishing vessel as it passed by.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trail - a mark or a series of signs left by someone or something moving
