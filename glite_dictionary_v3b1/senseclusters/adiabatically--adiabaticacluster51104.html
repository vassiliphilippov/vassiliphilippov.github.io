adiabatically

adverb
without state transitions
9.9
Pronunciation: /eɪ.dɪ.ə.ˈbæ.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /ə.dɪ.ə.ˈbæ.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /æ.dɪ.ə.ˈbæ.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /eɪ.dɪ.ə.ˈbæ.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a manner where a physical system changes so slowly that it remains in the same energy state, with no transitions between states.

Examples

Change adiabatically
The temperature of the gas was increased adiabatically during the experiment.
In quantum mechanics, the system evolved adiabatically to prevent any energy transitions.
The pressure was reduced adiabatically, ensuring the system stayed in its initial state.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irreversibly - in a way that cannot be reversed or undone
  • synonym: reversibly - in a way that can be reversed without loss or change
