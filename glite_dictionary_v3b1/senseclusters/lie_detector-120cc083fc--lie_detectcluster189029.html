lie detector

noun
AI/software deception detector
3.5 0.03/m
Pronunciation: /  / (AmE), /  / (BrE)
(informal) An automated system, often using software or AI, that attempts to detect deception by analysing language, voice, facial expressions, or other behavioural cues rather than measuring physiological signals.

Examples

AI lie detector
The HR team deployed a lie detector that analyzes speech patterns during video interviews.
Researchers developed a smartphone lie detector app to flag deceptive answers in quick surveys.
Critics warn that the new lie detector cannot reliably detect dishonesty across different languages and cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deception detection system - a general term for technologies or methods used to identify possible deception from verbal or non-verbal cues
  • synonym: polygraph - a device that records physiological data (blood pressure, pulse, respiration) and is often called a lie detector
