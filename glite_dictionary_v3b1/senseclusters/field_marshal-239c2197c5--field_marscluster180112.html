field marshal

noun
highest army officer rank
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld ˌmɑrʃəl / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌmɑːʃəl / (BrE)
the highest rank of officer in some armies, usually above general

Examples

A field marshal rank
