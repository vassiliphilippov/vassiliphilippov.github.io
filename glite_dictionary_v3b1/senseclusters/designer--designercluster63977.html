designer

noun
interior design professional
4.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪnər / (AmE), / dɪˈzʌɪnə / (BrE)
A person who plans and decorates the interiors of buildings professionally.

Examples

Interior designer
Many people rely on a professional designer for advice on color schemes and furniture placement.
She hired a renowned designer to transform her living room into a modern space.
The designer presented several mood boards to help the clients visualize their new office.
