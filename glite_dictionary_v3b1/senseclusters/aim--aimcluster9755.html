aim

noun
purpose; intention; goal
2.4
Pronunciation: / eɪm / (AmE), / eɪm / (BrE)
a purpose, intention, or goal that someone wants to achieve

Examples

A clear aim
He achieved his aim after years of hard work.
Her main aim in life is to help others.
The company’s aim is to reduce waste and promote sustainability.
He set a clear aim for himself at the beginning of the year.
Her main aim was to become a doctor and help others.
The project's aim is to reduce pollution in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goal - something you are trying to achieve
  • antonym: means - a method or way of achieving something
  • synonym: objective - something you are planning to achieve
  • synonym: purpose - the reason for which something is done
  • synonym: target - a specific goal or objective
