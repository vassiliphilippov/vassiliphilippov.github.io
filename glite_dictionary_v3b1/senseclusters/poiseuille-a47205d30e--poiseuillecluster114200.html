Poiseuille

proper noun
French physicist
Pronunciation: / pwɑːˈzɪl / (AmE), / pwɑːˈzɪl / (BrE)
the surname of Jean Léonard Marie Poiseuille, a French physicist known for his work on fluid dynamics

Examples

Poiseuille law
Poiseuille made significant contributions to the study of blood flow in capillaries.
The unit of viscosity, the poiseuille, is named after Poiseuille.
Students learn about Poiseuille and his law in introductory physics courses.
