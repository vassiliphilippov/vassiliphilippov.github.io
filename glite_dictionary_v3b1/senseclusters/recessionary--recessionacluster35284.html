recessionary

adjective
relating to an economic recession
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɛʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛʃənəri / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of an economic recession.

Examples

Recessionary economic pressures
Analysts warned that recessionary pressures would slow down corporate profits this year.
During recessionary periods, unemployment rates tend to rise significantly.
The central bank adopted recessionary measures to stabilize the economy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boom - a period of rapid economic growth
  • synonym: recession - a period of temporary economic decline
