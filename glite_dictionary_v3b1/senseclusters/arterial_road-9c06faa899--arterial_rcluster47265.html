arterial road

noun
main road for high-capacity traffic
3.2 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / …ëÀêrÀàt…™ri…ôl ro äd / (AmE), / …ëÀêÀàt…™…ôri…ôl r…ô äd / (BrE)
A major road or route that provides high-capacity traffic movement and connects important areas within an urban region.

Examples

The arterial road connects cities
Emergency services often use the arterial road to reach different parts of town quickly.
New businesses tend to open along the arterial road because of the high volume of passing traffic.
The city council approved the expansion of the arterial road to reduce congestion during rush hour.
