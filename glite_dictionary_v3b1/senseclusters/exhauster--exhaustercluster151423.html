exhauster

noun
air-removal device
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɔstər / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːstə / (BrE)
a machine or device that removes air, gas, or fumes from a space

Examples

Laboratory exhauster
The engineer checked the exhauster to ensure it was working properly in the laboratory.
A powerful exhauster is needed to remove fumes from the paint shop.
They installed a new exhauster to improve ventilation in the underground parking garage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fan - a machine with blades that moves air
  • synonym: ventilator - a device that moves air in and out of a space
