tonguing

noun
use of tongue
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʌŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of using the tongue to touch, move, or control something, especially in playing a musical instrument

Examples

Flute tonguing technique
The flutist improved her tonguing to play faster passages more clearly.
Proper tonguing is essential for producing crisp notes on a trumpet.
He demonstrated different tonguing techniques during the music lesson.

Related Terms

  • synonym: articulation - the clear and precise pronunciation or production of sounds
  • antonym: slurring - playing musical notes smoothly without separating them
