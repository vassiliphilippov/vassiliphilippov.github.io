transfer

noun
application of learned skill in a new context
Pronunciation: / ˈtrænsfɚ / (AmE), / ˈtrænsfɜː / (BrE)
The application of a skill or knowledge learned in one situation to a different but similar situation.

Examples

Transfer of skills
High transfer of skills occurs when training closely matches real-world tasks.
Learning a language involves transfer of grammar rules from one tense to another.
The teacher encouraged transfer of problem-solving skills from mathematics to science.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generalization - the process of applying something learned to new situations
