non-orthogonal

adjective
not at right angles
9.0
Pronunciation: / nɑn ɔrˈθɑɡənl / (AmE), / nɒn ɔːˈθɒɡənl / (BrE)
not at right angles; not perpendicular or not independent in function or direction

Examples

Two non-orthogonal axes
In this experiment, the two axes are non-orthogonal, which affects the results.
The engineer explained that non-orthogonal signals can interfere with each other.
Because the vectors are non-orthogonal, they cannot be separated using standard methods.
