blurp

noun
short silly sound
9.5
Pronunciation: / blɜːrp / (AmE), / blɜːp / (BrE)
a short, sudden sound or brief, often silly, utterance, especially one that is unexpected or amusing

Examples

A funny blurp
A strange blurp came from the old radio, making everyone laugh.
He made a silly blurp with his lips to entertain the baby.
Suddenly, a loud blurp interrupted the quiet meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burp - a noise made by air escaping from the stomach through the mouth
