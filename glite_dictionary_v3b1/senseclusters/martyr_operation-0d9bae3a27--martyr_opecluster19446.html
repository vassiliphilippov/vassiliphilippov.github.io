martyr operation

noun
suicide attack for a cause
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrtər ˌɑpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtə ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
an act of killing or injuring others while deliberately causing one's own death, especially by means of a bomb, typically for a cause or belief

Examples

A martyr operation attack
A martyr operation often involves extensive planning and ideological motivation.
Officials condemned the recent martyr operation as an act of terrorism.
The group claimed responsibility for the martyr operation that targeted the bus station.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suicide attack - an attack in which the attacker intends to kill themselves as well as others
