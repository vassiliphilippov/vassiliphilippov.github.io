spitter

noun
illegal baseball pitch with a doctored ball
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪtər / (AmE), / ˈspɪtə / (BrE)
(Baseball) An illegal pitch in which the pitcher applies a foreign substance such as saliva or Vaseline to the ball to alter its movement.

Examples

A baseball spitter
Legend has it that some old-time pitchers relied on a spitter to win games.
The umpire suspected the pitcher of throwing a spitter during the crucial inning.
Using a spitter is against the rules and can result in suspension.
