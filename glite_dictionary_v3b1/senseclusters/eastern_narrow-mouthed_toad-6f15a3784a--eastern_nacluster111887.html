eastern narrow-mouthed toad

noun
small toad of the southeastern U.S.
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstərn ˈnæroʊ maʊθt toʊd / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈnærəʊ maʊθt təʊd / (BrE)
A small species of toad native to the southeastern United States, known for its narrow mouth.
