disablement

noun
state of being unable due to impairment
6.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈseɪbəlmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈseɪblmənt / (BrE)
the condition of being unable to carry out normal activities due to physical or mental impairment

Examples
