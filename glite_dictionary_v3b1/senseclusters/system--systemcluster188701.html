system

noun
organized or methodical way
1.0 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪs.təm/ (AmE), /ˈsɪs.təm/ (BrE)
an organized way or method of doing something; a set of principles, procedures, or schemes for arranging or managing things

Examples

A system of rules
Establishing a clear system can help reduce confusion and mistakes.
Her system for organizing paperwork made the office much more efficient.
We can't achieve our goals without some system in our approach.
Biologists use a classification system to group plants and animals according to their characteristics.
He designed a unique system for organizing the library's vast collection.
The new filing system made it easier to locate important documents quickly.
Every language is structured by its own unique system of grammar.
Many countries have a legal system that is based on similar principles.
The school introduced a new system for tracking student progress.
