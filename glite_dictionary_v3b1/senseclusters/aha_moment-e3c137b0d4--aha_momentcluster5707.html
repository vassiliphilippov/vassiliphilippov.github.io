aha moment

noun
a sudden insight or realization
Pronunciation: / əˈhɑ ˌmoʊmənt / (AmE), / əˈhɑː ˌməʊmənt / (BrE)
a moment of sudden insight or clear understanding, often after a period of confusion or puzzlement

Examples

An aha moment
After hours of struggling with the problem, I finally had an aha moment and found the solution.
Sometimes, a simple explanation can trigger an aha moment for students learning a difficult concept.
The scientist described the aha moment that led to her groundbreaking discovery.
