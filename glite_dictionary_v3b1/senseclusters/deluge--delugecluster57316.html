Synonyms

  • flood
    1.0 8/m
    flood is common and means water covers land quickly and heavily, while deluge suggests a very large or sudden covering.
  • inundate
    6.8 2/m
    inundate often means to cover with water or with too many things at once, while deluge focuses on heavy flooding.
  • swamp
    4.0 3/m
    swamp means to overwhelm with water or with many things at once, while deluge stresses full covering by water.
  • submerge
    3.9 2/m
    submerge means to put something entirely under the surface of water, while deluge means to cover land with water.
