deluge

verb
to flood or overwhelm
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛl.judʒ / (AmE), / ˈdɛl.juːdʒ / (BrE)
to flood or cover something completely with water

Examples

To deluge a city
After the dam burst, the river deluged the entire valley, forcing residents to evacuate.
During the sale, the store was deluged by shoppers looking for bargains.
Her inbox was deluged with messages after the announcement went public.
Fans deluged the band with letters after the concert.
Heavy rains can deluge entire neighborhoods in just a few hours.
The city was deluged by a sudden wave of tourists during the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flood - to cover with water, especially to overflow a place
  • synonym: inundate - to flood or overwhelm with things or people
  • synonym: overwhelm - to cover completely or overpower, especially with emotion or work
