Macau

proper noun
Chinese territory; former Portuguese colony
4.4
Pronunciation: / məˈkaʊ / (AmE), / məˈkaʊ / (BrE)
A Special Administrative Region of China on the south coast, formerly a Portuguese territory until its return to China in 1999, comprising a peninsula and two islands.

Examples
