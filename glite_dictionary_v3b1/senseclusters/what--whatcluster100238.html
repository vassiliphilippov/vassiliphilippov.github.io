what

determiner
questioning determiner
Pronunciation: / wʌt / (AmE), / wɒt / (BrE)
used before a noun in questions to ask about the identity, kind, or amount of something

Examples

What time is it
What time does the train leave in the morning?
Can you tell me what kind of food they serve at that restaurant?
She asked what reason he had for being late.

Related Terms

  • synonym: which - used to ask about one or more items from a known set
