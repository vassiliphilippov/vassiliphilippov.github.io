critic

noun
person who frequently finds fault
2.2 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɪ.tɪk/ (AmE), /ˈkrɪ.tɪk/ (BrE)

Examples

She's her own worst critic and rarely appreciates her achievements.
