sepulcher

noun
stone burial chamber or tomb
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛpəlkɚ /; / ˈsɛp.əl.kɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛp(ə)lkə /; / ˈsɛp.əl.kə / (BrE)
a small room or chamber, typically made of stone, in which a dead person is buried
