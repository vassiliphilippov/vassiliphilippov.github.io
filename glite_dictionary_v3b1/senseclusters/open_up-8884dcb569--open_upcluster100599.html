open up

verb
start operating or cause to operate
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊpən ˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌəʊpən ˈʌp / (BrE)
to start operating or functioning, or to cause something to start operating or functioning, especially a business or institution

Examples

Open up a shop
After renovations, the theater finally opened up to the public.
The new cafe will open up on Main Street next month.
They plan to open up a branch overseas next year.
