uniform

adjective
not varying; consistent; not differentiated
3.3 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈjuː.nə.fɔːrm/ /; / /ˈjuː.nɪ.fɔːrm/ (AmE), /ˈjuː.nɪ.fɔːm/ (BrE)
not varying; the same in all parts or at all times; showing consistency or sameness (including technical use in computer graphics for something constant)
