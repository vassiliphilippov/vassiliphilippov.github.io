Jove

proper noun
Roman supreme god; counterpart of Zeus

Pronunciation: / dʒoʊv / (AmE), / dʒəʊv / (BrE)
The supreme god in Roman mythology, equivalent to the Greek god Zeus.

Examples

Jove the god
In ancient Roman religion, Jove was worshipped as the king of the gods.
Many expressions in English, such as 'by Jove!', refer to this Roman deity.
Poets often invoked Jove in their works to represent power and authority.
