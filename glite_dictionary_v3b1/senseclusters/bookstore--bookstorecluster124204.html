bookstore

noun
shop selling books
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊk.stɔːr / (AmE), / ˈbʊk.stɔː / (BrE)
A shop where books are sold to the public.

Examples

A bookstore near campus
He bought a novel and a magazine from the bookstore near campus.
Many independent bookstores have special events like author signings and readings.
She spent the afternoon browsing in her favorite bookstore downtown.
