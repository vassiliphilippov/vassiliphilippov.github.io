license plate

noun
car's registration identification plate
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪsəns pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪsəns pleɪt / (BrE)
A metal or plastic plate on a motor vehicle, displaying its official registration number.

Examples

A license plate number
He glanced at the license plate to write down the number.
