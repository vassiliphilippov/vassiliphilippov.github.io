backstab

noun
secret betrayal
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌstæb / (AmE), / ˈbækˌstæb / (BrE)
a secret act of betrayal, especially by someone who is trusted or considered a friend

Examples

A friend's backstab
The sudden backstab by his colleague left him feeling shocked and disappointed.
She never expected a backstab from someone she trusted so much.
Rumors of a backstab spread quickly through the office after the unexpected resignation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: betrayal - an act of being disloyal or unfaithful
  • antonym: loyalty - the quality of being faithful and supportive
  • synonym: treachery - behavior that betrays trust or confidence
