niggle

verb
argue or fuss over petty details
8.2 8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈnɪɡəl / (BrE)
to argue or worry over trivial or minor details; to engage in petty criticism or dispute

Examples

To niggle over details
Instead of appreciating the effort, they kept niggling about the tiniest mistakes.
She tends to niggle with her coworkers about things that don't really matter.
We shouldn't niggle over such minor details when the deadline is so close.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bicker - to argue about petty and trivial matters
  • synonym: quibble - to argue or raise objections about a trivial matter
