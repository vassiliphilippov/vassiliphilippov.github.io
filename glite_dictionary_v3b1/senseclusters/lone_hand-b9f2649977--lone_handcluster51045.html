lone hand

noun
person who acts alone
3.8
Pronunciation: / loʊn hænd / (AmE), / ləʊn hand / (BrE)
a person who prefers to act or be alone, without the company or help of others

Examples

As a lone hand, she found it difficult to fit into group activities.
He always prefers to be a lone hand at work, tackling projects without any help.
The detective was known as a lone hand, rarely partnering with colleagues.
