residential district

noun
area mainly for private homes
2.6 2.6
Pronunciation: /re.zɪ.ˈden.ʃəl ˈdɪs.trɪkt/ (AmE), /re.zɪ.ˈden.ʃəl ˈdɪs.trɪkt/ (BrE)
an area of a city or town where most buildings are homes or private residences, rather than businesses or factories

Examples

A quiet residential district
Heavy traffic is not allowed in this residential district after 8 p.m.
The city council plans to build new schools in the residential district.
They moved to a quiet residential district near the park.
