fettler

noun
metal or ceramic finisher
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfet.lər/ (AmE), /ˈfet.lə/ (BrE)
a worker who cleans, trims, and smooths newly cast metal or ceramic items by removing excess material

Examples

Metal fettler

A fettler is essential in the ceramics industry to ensure products are smooth and ready for sale.
