precure

verb
to partially cure material
Pronunciation: / priːˈkjʊr / (AmE), / priːˈkjʊə / (BrE)
to partially cure a material, such as rubber or resin, before the final processing step

Examples

To precure rubber
The technician needs to precure the resin before applying the final layer.
If you precure the rubber, it will be easier to mold during the next stage.
Some adhesives require you to precure them under heat before full bonding.
