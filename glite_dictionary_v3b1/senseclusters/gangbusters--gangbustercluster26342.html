gangbusters

noun
aggressive crime fighters
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæŋˌbʌstərz / (AmE), / ˈɡæŋˌbʌstəz / (BrE)
people, especially law enforcement officers, who aggressively fight or break up criminal gangs

Examples

Police gangbusters
The new police unit acted like gangbusters and quickly dismantled the criminal network.
Local residents praised the gangbusters for making their neighborhood safer.
After the mayor's reforms, the city saw more gangbusters working to reduce gang violence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crime fighters - people who work to prevent or stop crime
  • synonym: detectives - police officers who investigate crimes
