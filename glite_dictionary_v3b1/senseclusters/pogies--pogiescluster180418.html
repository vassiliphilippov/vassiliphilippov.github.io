pogies

noun
handlebar hand covers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊɡiz / (AmE), / ˈpəʊɡiz / (BrE)
protective covers that fit over bicycle handlebars to keep the rider's hands warm in cold weather

Examples

Pogies for handlebars
Cyclists often use pogies in winter to protect their hands from the cold wind.
He bought a pair of pogies before his long-distance bike trip in January.
With pogies on her handlebars, she could ride comfortably even in freezing temperatures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gloves - hand coverings with separate sections for each finger
  • synonym: mittens - gloves that cover the whole hand but not the fingers separately
