key figure

noun
important or central person
Pronunciation: / ki ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / ki ˈfɪɡjə / (BrE)
an important or central person in a particular situation, organization, or event

Examples

A key figure in history
Dr. Grant emerged as the key figure in the medical breakthrough that saved thousands of lives.
During the investigation, the detective identified the accountant as a key figure in the fraud scheme.
She is considered a key figure in the campaign for education reform.

Related Terms

  • synonym: central figure - a person who plays an important or leading role
  • antonym: minor figure - a person who is not important in a context
