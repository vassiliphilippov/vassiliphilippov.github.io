cheer up

verb
to become or make happier
1.4 1.3
Pronunciation: /tʃɪr ʌp/ /; / /tʃɪr əp/ (AmE), /tʃɪə ʌp/ /; / /tʃɪə əp/ (BrE)
to become happier or to make someone feel happier, especially after feeling sad or disappointed

Examples
