cornel

noun
shrub or tree of the genus Cornus
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnəl / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnəl / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree of the genus Cornus, often with showy white or pink bracts resembling petals, typically found in temperate regions

Examples

A cornel tree
A mature cornel can develop striking red berries later in the year.
She planted a pink-flowering cornel in the corner of the yard.
The garden was lined with blooming cornel trees in the spring.
