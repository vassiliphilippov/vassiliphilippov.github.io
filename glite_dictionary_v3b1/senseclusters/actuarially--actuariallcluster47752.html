actuarially

adverb
by statistical calculation
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæktʃuˈɛrɪəli / (AmE), / ˌæktjʊˈɛərɪəli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the statistical calculation of risk, especially in insurance or finance

Examples

Set actuarially calculated premiums
The company set its premiums actuarially to ensure long-term financial stability.
Retirement benefits are calculated actuarially to match expected future payouts.
The new policy was reviewed actuarially before being approved by the board.

Related Terms

  • synonym: statistically - in a way that involves statistics or data analysis
