book up

verb
reserve all places or tickets
1.2
Pronunciation: / bʊk ʌp / (AmE), / bʊk ʌp / (BrE)
to reserve all available places or tickets for an event, transport, or accommodation

Examples

To book up seats
Popular restaurants often book up months in advance for the holidays.
The hotel was booked up for the entire weekend due to the festival.
We tried to get concert tickets, but they had already booked up by the time we called.
