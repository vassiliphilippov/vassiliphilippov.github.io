A material that does not conduct electricity or heat and is used as an insulator, especially in capacitors.

Examples

A dielectric material
Glass is commonly used as a dielectric in high-voltage equipment.
Porcelain serves as a dielectric in many electrical insulators.
The performance of the capacitor depends greatly on the type of dielectric used.
