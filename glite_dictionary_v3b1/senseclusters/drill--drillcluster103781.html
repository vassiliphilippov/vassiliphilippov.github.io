drill

noun
tool for making holes in materials
1.5
Pronunciation: / drɪl / (AmE), / drɪl / (BrE)
a tool with a sharp point and cutting edges, used for making holes in hard materials, usually by rotating rapidly or striking repeatedly

Examples

Electric drill tool
He used a drill to install the shelves in the kitchen wall.

The dentist picked up the small drill to begin working on my tooth.
