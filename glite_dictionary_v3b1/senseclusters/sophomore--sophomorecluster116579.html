sophomore

adjective
second-year (US high school or college)
3.3 1.81/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…ëÀêfmo är / (AmE), / Ààs…íf…ôm…îÀê / (BrE)
relating to the second year at an American high school or college.

Examples

Sophomore year
Many students find that their sophomore year is more challenging than their freshman year.
She joined the debate team during her sophomore semester.
The university requires all sophomore students to complete a writing course.

Synonyms

  • second-year
    2.4 4.90/m
    second-year describes students in their second school year in plain terms, while sophomore is the common U.S. term for the same students.
  • sophomoric
    8.1 0.01/m
    sophomoric describes qualities or behavior of second-year students, while sophomore names the student or refers to the second year itself.
