running light

noun
navigation light on a vessel
6.5 4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪŋ laɪt / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ laɪt / (BrE)
a light carried on a boat or ship, used to indicate its position and direction at night or in poor visibility, especially as required by navigational regulations

Examples

Boat running lights
It is illegal to operate a vessel at night without proper running lights.
The boat's running lights had to be switched on as dusk fell over the harbor.
The green and red running lights made it easy to spot the approaching sailboat.
