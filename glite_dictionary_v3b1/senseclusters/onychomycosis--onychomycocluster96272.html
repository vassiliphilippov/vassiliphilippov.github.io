onychomycosis

noun
fungal nail infection
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːnɪkoʊmaɪˈkoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌɒnɪkəʊmaɪˈkəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a fungal infection affecting the fingernails or toenails

Examples

Onychomycosis nail infection
The dermatologist confirmed that the thick, discolored toenail was caused by onychomycosis.
Treatment for onychomycosis can take several months to fully clear the infection.
People with diabetes are at higher risk of developing onychomycosis in their toenails.

Related Terms

  • antonym: paronychia - an infection of the skin around the nail
  • synonym: tinea - a general term for fungal skin infections
