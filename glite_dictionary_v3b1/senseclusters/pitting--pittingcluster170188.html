pitting

noun
formation of small pits by corrosion
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process in which small depressions or pits form in a surface, typically as a result of corrosion or wear

Examples

Pitting corrosion damage
Engineers inspected the pipe for signs of pitting that could indicate corrosion damage.
Regular maintenance can help prevent pitting on metal surfaces.
The stainless steel tank showed significant pitting after years of exposure to saltwater.
