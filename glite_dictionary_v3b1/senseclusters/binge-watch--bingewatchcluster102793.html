binge-watch

noun
extended session of watching episodes
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪndʒ.wɑːtʃ/ /; / /ˈbɪndʒ.wɔːtʃ/ (AmE), /ˈbɪndʒ.wɒtʃ/ (BrE)
An extended session of watching several episodes of a television series or program in rapid succession, typically in one sitting.

Examples

A binge-watch weekend
We indulged in a weekend-long binge-watch of a new series.
Their Saturday binge-watch lasted twelve hours and included three complete seasons.
The group planned a binge-watch with snacks and breaks between episodes.
