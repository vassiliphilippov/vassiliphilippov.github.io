weaver

noun
African or Asian bird that weaves nests
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːvər / (AmE), / ˈwiːvə / (BrE)
a type of African or Asian bird known for building elaborate woven nests, belonging to the family Ploceidae

Examples

Colorful weaver bird
Birdwatchers travel to see the brightly colored weaver during its nesting season.
Many weaver species create intricate hanging nests woven from grasses and twigs.
The colony of weavers worked together to build dozens of nests in the tall acacia tree.
