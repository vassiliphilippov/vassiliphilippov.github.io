millie

noun
slang for one million (esp. money)
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪli / (AmE), / ˈmɪli / (BrE)
(slang) A million, especially one million dollars or pounds.

Examples

A millie bet
He bet a millie on the championship game and won.
Landing that big client brought the company another millie in revenue this quarter.
She couldn't believe she earned her first millie at just twenty-four.
