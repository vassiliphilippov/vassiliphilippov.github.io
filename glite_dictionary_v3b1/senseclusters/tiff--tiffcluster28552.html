tiff

noun
small or petty argument
1.1
Pronunciation: / tɪf / (AmE), / tɪf / (BrE)
a slight or petty quarrel, usually between friends or lovers

Examples

A tiff with a friend
After their little tiff, they quickly made up and laughed about it.
Even best friends sometimes have a tiff about silly things.
They had a tiff over who should wash the dishes last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dispute - an argument or disagreement, especially an official one
  • synonym: quarrel - an angry argument or disagreement
