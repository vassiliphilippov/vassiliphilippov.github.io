carpetmaker

noun
carpet manufacturer
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrpɪtˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpɪtˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person or company that makes carpets, especially by hand or as a craft

Examples

The carpetmaker crafts rugs
The carpetmaker spent months creating a beautiful Persian rug for the palace.
Local carpetmakers often use traditional patterns passed down through generations.
She apprenticed with a skilled carpetmaker to learn the art of weaving by hand.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carpenter - a person who makes or repairs wooden objects
  • synonym: weaver - a person who makes fabric by weaving
