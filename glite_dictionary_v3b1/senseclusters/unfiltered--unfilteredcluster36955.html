unfiltered

adjective
not processed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪltərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪltəd / (BrE)
not changed, cleaned, or processed; in a raw or natural state

Examples

Unfiltered water
Drinking unfiltered water can be risky in some areas.
The photographer preferred the look of unfiltered sunlight in her portraits.
He gave an unfiltered opinion, speaking honestly without holding back.

Related Terms

  • antonym: filtered - cleaned or processed to remove unwanted parts
  • synonym: pure - not mixed with anything else; clean
  • synonym: raw - in a natural, unprocessed state
