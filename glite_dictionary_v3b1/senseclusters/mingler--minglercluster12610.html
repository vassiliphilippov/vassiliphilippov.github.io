mingler

noun
person who blends styles
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪŋɡlər / (AmE), / ˈmɪŋɡlə / (BrE)
a person who blends different styles, cultures, ideas, or artistic elements to create something new

Examples

Cultural mingler
The artist is known as a mingler who brings together Eastern and Western influences in her work.
As a mingler, he enjoys combining vintage and contemporary fashion in his outfits.
The festival attracted many minglers interested in exploring new cultural fusions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fusionist - a person who combines different styles or elements
  • antonym: traditionalist - a person who adheres strictly to traditional ideas or practices
