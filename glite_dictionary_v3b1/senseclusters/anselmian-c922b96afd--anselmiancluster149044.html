Anselmian

noun
follower of Saint Anselm
Pronunciation: / ænˈsɛlmiən / (AmE), / ænˈsɛlmiən / (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the philosophical or theological views of Saint Anselm

Examples

An Anselmian thinker
As an Anselmian, she defended the ontological argument in seminars and debates with confidence.
The professor was known as a leading Anselmian in the field of medieval philosophy.
Many Anselmians continue to study and discuss his arguments for the existence of God.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Anselmian - relating to the ideas of Saint Anselm
  • synonym: Thomist - a follower of the philosophy or theology of Thomas Aquinas
