proletarian

adjective
of the working class
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊ.ləˈtɛr.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌprəʊ.lɪˈteə.ri.ən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the working class, especially those who do not own capital and must earn their living by manual labor.

Examples

Proletarian values
He adopted a distinctly proletarian style of dress to show solidarity with workers.
Her research focuses on proletarian culture and everyday life under industrialization.
The novel vividly described the proletarian struggles in the early twentieth century.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bourgeois - relating to the middle class, typically referencing capitalist property owners
  • synonym: working-class - relating to those who do manual labor for wages
