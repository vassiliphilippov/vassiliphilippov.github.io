hoop

noun
framework for spreading out a skirt
Pronunciation: / huːp / (AmE), / huːp / (BrE)
a lightweight, usually circular framework used to extend and support the shape of a skirt or dress

Examples

Dress hoop
Hoops were commonly used in nineteenth-century fashion to support wide skirts.
The actress wore a dress with a hoop to give it more volume during the performance.
The museum display included an elaborate Victorian hoop undergarment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crinoline - a stiffened or structured petticoat designed to hold out a woman's skirt
