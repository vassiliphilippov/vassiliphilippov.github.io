crested

adjective
bearing a heraldic crest
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˈkrɛstɪd / (BrE)
having a crest or decorative ornament, especially on a shield, coat of arms, or helmet

Examples

The knight rode into battle wearing a crested helmet that gleamed in the sunlight.
The museum displayed a crested helmet from the medieval period, complete with its original plume.
