backfire spectacularly

verb
fail dramatically with unexpected bad results
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌfaɪɚ spɛkˈtækjələrli / (AmE), / ˈbækˌfaɪə spɛkˈtakjʊləli / (BrE)
to fail in a dramatic or surprising way, resulting in an unexpected and undesirable outcome

Examples

To backfire spectacularly
Her attempt to impress her boss backfired spectacularly when she forgot her lines during the presentation.
The company's decision to cut costs backfired spectacularly, resulting in even larger losses.
When he tried to fix the problem himself, it backfired spectacularly and made things much worse.
