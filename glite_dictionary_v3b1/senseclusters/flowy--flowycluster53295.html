flowy

adjective
loose and graceful
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊi / (AmE), / ˈfləʊi / (BrE)
hanging or moving in a loose, smooth, and graceful way, often used to describe clothing or fabric

Examples

A flowy dress
She wore a flowy dress that moved with the breeze.
The curtains in the living room are made of a flowy fabric that lets in soft light.
He admired the flowy sleeves on her blouse as she danced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: billowy - swelling outward or puffed up in a soft, rounded way
  • antonym: clingy - fitting closely to the body
