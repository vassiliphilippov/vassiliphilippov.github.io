flowy

Examples

A flowy dress
She wore a flowy dress that moved with the breeze.
The curtains in the living room are made of a flowy fabric that lets in soft light.
He admired the flowy sleeves on her blouse as she danced.

Synonyms

  • fluid
    4.2 1.23/m
    fluid describes smooth, graceful movement or style in many contexts, while flowy refers especially to loose, hanging clothing or fabric.
  • flowing
    3.7 0.01/m
    flowing describes continuous, liquid-like movement or shapes, while flowy describes loose, hanging fabric or clothing with a gentle, graceful look.
