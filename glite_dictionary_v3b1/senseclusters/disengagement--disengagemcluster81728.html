disengagement

noun
the act of detaching or separating
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the act of releasing or separating from an attachment, connection, or involvement

Examples

Disengagement from work
After years of involvement, her disengagement from the project surprised everyone.
Emotional disengagement can harm relationships over time.
The sudden disengagement of the engine caused the machine to stop abruptly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attachment - the action or state of being attached or joined to something
  • synonym: separation - the process or state of moving or being moved apart
