morning sun

noun
sunlight early in the morning
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˌsʌn / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˌsʌn / (BrE)
sunlight that appears in the early part of the day, typically soon after sunrise.

Examples

Enjoying the morning sun
After a long walk, she relaxed on the porch, enjoying the morning sun.
Farmers often start work at dawn, taking advantage of the cool morning sun.
The morning sun streamed through the window, filling the kitchen with golden light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sunrise - the time in the morning when the sun appears above the horizon
