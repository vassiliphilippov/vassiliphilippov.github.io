life buoy

noun
ring-shaped flotation rescue device
Pronunciation: / laɪf ˈbɔɪ /; / ˈlaɪf ˌbu.i, ˈlaɪf ˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / laɪf ˈbɔɪ /; / ˈlaɪf ˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
