tall

noun
garment size for tall people
Pronunciation: / tɔl / (AmE), / tɔːl / (BrE)
a garment size designed for people who are taller than average, with longer measurements than standard sizes

Jeans in tall

Many clothing brands offer a tall for both men and women.
