Cecropia

noun
genus of tropical American trees
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkroʊpiə /; / sɛˈkroʊpiə / (AmE), / sɪˈkrəʊpiə /; / sɛˈkrəʊpiə / (BrE)
a genus of fast-growing tropical American trees used for making rope, tanning leather, and producing rubber
