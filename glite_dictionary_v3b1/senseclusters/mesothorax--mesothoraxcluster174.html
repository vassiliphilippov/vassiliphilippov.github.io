mesothorax

noun
middle thorax segment
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛsəˈθɔːræks / (AmE), / ˌmɛsəʊˈθɔːræks / (BrE)
the middle segment of an insect's thorax, which bears the second pair of legs and wings

Examples

Insect mesothorax segment
The butterfly's wings are attached to its mesothorax.
Scientists examined the mesothorax to study the insect's flight muscles.
The mesothorax is larger in flying insects than in those that do not fly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: metathorax - the third segment of an insect's thorax
  • antonym: prothorax - the first segment of an insect's thorax
