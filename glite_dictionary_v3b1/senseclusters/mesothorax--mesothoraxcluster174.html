mesothorax

noun
middle thorax segment
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛsəˈθɔːræks / (AmE), / ˌmɛsəʊˈθɔːræks / (BrE)
the middle segment of an insect's thorax, which bears the second pair of legs and wings

Examples

The mesothorax is larger in flying insects than in those that do not fly.
