nonsustained

adjective
not prolonged
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnsəˈsteɪnd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnsəˈsteɪnd / (BrE)
not prolonged or held for a long duration, especially referring to a musical note or sound that is short or detached

Examples

The pianist played the chords in a nonsustained manner, making each note distinct.
