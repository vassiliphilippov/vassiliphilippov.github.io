containable

adjective
able to be contained
4.3
Pronunciation: / kənˈteɪnəbl̩ / (AmE), / kənˈteɪnəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be held, stored, or kept within a container or limited space

Examples

Containable in a box
The chemicals are only containable in special sealed containers.
The fire was barely containable by the emergency crew due to strong winds.
Make sure the toys are containable in the storage box before buying them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enclosable - able to be enclosed or shut in
  • antonym: uncontainable - impossible to be contained or held within limits
