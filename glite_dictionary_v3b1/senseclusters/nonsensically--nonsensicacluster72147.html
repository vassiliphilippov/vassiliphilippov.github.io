nonsensically

adverb
without sense or logic
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɛnsɪkli / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɛnsɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that lacks sense, logic, or clear meaning

Examples

Speak nonsensically
He spoke so nonsensically that nobody could understand his point.
The story jumped nonsensically from one event to another without explanation.
She laughed nonsensically at the joke, even though it made no sense.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illogically - in a way that does not follow logical reasoning
  • antonym: sensibly - in a way that shows good sense or judgment
