marginal

noun
social outsider
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrdʒɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈmɑːdʒɪnəl / (BrE)
a person who lives on the edge of society or is excluded from mainstream social, economic, or cultural life

Examples

Urban marginals
The documentary focused on the lives of marginals in the city.
Social programs aim to help marginals find stable housing and employment.
Artists and writers are sometimes considered marginals because of their unconventional lifestyles.

  • synonym: outcast - a person who is rejected or excluded by society
