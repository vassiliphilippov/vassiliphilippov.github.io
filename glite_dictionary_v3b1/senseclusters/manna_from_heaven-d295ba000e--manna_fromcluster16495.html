manna from heaven

noun
unexpected blessing or windfall
Pronunciation: / ˈmænə frəm ˈhɛvən / (AmE), / ˈmænə frəm ˈhɛvən / (BrE)
an unexpected benefit or help, especially one that comes at a critical time; originally, the miraculous food sent by God to the Israelites in the Bible

Examples

manna from heaven surprise
After months of drought, the sudden rainstorm was manna from heaven for the farmers.
For the small business, the new government grant came as manna from heaven.
The unexpected inheritance was a real manna from heaven for the struggling artist.
After days of hunger, the villagers regarded the food delivery as manna from heaven.
The financial aid they received was like manna from heaven for the struggling charity.
The sudden rainfall was manna from heaven for the drought-stricken region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: windfall - a sudden, unexpected gain or advantage
