shove

noun
a strong push
Pronunciation: / ʃʌv / (AmE), / ʃʌv / (BrE)
a strong or sudden push given to someone or something

Examples

A quick shove
She gave her brother a playful shove as they walked down the path.
The crowd surged forward with a collective shove when the doors opened.
With a quick shove, he managed to close the sticking window.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pull - the act of drawing something toward oneself
  • synonym: push - the act of applying force to move something away
