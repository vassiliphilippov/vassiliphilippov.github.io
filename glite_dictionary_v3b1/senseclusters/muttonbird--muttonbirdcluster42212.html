muttonbird

noun
seabird harvested for food
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌtənˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈmʌtənˌbɜːd / (BrE)
a type of seabird (especially a shearwater) whose young are often harvested for food in New Zealand and Australia
