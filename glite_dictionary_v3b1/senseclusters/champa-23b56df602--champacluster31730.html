Champa

proper noun
ancient Vietnamese kingdom
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːmpə / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːmpə / (BrE)
a historical kingdom located on the coast of present-day central and southern Vietnam, known for its maritime trade and Cham culture

Examples

The kingdom of Champa
The ancient kingdom of Champa was famous for its impressive temples and maritime trade.
Historians have uncovered artifacts from Champa that reveal its connections with other Asian civilizations.
Many tourists visit the ruins of Champa to learn about its unique culture and history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Vietnam - a country in Southeast Asia, location of the former Champa kingdom
