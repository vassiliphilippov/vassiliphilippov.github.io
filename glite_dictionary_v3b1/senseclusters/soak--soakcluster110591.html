soak

verb
to overcharge or demand excessive price
9.6
Pronunciation: / soʊk / (AmE), / səʊk / (BrE)
to overcharge or make someone pay an unreasonable price for something, especially in an unfair or dishonest way

Examples

To soak customers

The mechanic really soaked us for the repairs last month.
