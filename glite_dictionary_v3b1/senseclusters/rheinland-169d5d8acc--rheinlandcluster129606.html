Rheinland

noun
region of Germany along the Rhine River
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪnˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈraɪnˌlænd / (BrE)
a picturesque region of western Germany situated along the Rhine River, known for its landscapes, castles, and vineyards

Examples

The Rheinland region
He traveled through the Rheinland, enjoying the scenic views of the Rhine River and the surrounding hills.
Many tourists visit the Rheinland each year to see its beautiful castles and vineyards.
The Rheinland played an important role in European history, especially during the Middle Ages.
