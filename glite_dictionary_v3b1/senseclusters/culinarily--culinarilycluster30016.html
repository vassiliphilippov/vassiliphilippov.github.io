culinarily

adverb
in a cooking-related way
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlɪˌnɛrəli / (AmE), / ˈkʌlɪnərɪli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to cooking or the preparation of food

Examples

To be culinarily adventurous
He is culinarily adventurous and loves trying new recipes from around the world.
The restaurant is culinarily innovative, offering unique dishes each season.
She was culinarily inspired after watching the cooking competition on television.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gastronomically - in a way that relates to the art or science of good eating
