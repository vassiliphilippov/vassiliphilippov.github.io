bitter aloes

noun
purgative from aloe leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtər ˈæloʊz / (AmE), / ˈbɪtə ˈaləʊz / (BrE)
a strong purgative preparation made from the dried juice of certain aloe plants

Examples

A dose of bitter aloes
Excessive consumption of bitter aloes can lead to dehydration and abdominal cramps.
For centuries, bitter aloes were used as a natural remedy for digestive problems.
The herbalist recommended a dose of bitter aloes to treat constipation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aloe vera - a species of aloe plant often used medicinally
  • synonym: purgative - a substance causing evacuation of the bowels
