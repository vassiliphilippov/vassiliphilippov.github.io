expression

noun
look on the face showing feeling
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
a look or sign, especially on the face, that shows what someone is feeling or thinking

Examples

A happy expression
He wore an anxious expression during the interview.
Her face had a joyful expression after she heard the good news.
The teacher's stern expression made the students quiet down.
A sudden blush on his face was an expression of embarrassment.
Anger can be an expression shown through body language.
Her smile was an expression of genuine happiness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: countenance - a person's face or facial expression
  • synonym: look - an act of directing one's gaze; a particular facial appearance
  • synonym: manifestation - an event, action, or object that clearly shows or embodies something
  • antonym: suppression - the act of holding back or restraining
