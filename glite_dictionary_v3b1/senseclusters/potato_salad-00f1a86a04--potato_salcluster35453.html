potato salad

noun
cold salad with chopped potatoes
1.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈteɪdoʊ ˈsæləd / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ ˈsæləd / (BrE)
A cold salad made from chopped cooked potatoes, usually mixed with mayonnaise, vinegar, and other ingredients such as vegetables or herbs.

Examples

Cold potato salad
Her favorite side dish at barbecues is always potato salad.
I made a large bowl of potato salad for the picnic.
You can find many variations of potato salad depending on the region.
