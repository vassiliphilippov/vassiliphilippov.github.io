lunchbox

noun
container for lunch
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌntʃˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈlʌntʃˌbɒks / (BrE)
a container used to carry food for lunch or snacks, especially to school or work

Examples

Her new lunchbox keeps her food fresh until lunchtime every day.

Her colorful lunchbox stood out among all the others in the cafeteria.
Parents often prepare sandwiches and fruit to put in their child's lunchbox.
