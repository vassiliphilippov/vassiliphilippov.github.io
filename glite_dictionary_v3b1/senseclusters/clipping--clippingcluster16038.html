clipping

noun
shortened word
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
a word created by shortening a longer word, usually by removing one part

Examples

A word clipping
The word clipping refers to a new word made by shortening an existing one.
In English, 'exam' is a clipping of 'examination'.
Many students use clipping in informal speech, such as saying 'info' for 'information'.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abbreviation - a shortened form of a word or phrase
  • synonym: acronym - a word formed from the initial letters of other words
