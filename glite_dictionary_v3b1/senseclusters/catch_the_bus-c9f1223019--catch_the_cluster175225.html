catch the bus

verb
board a bus in time
1.0
Pronunciation: / kætʃ ðə bʌs / (AmE), / katʃ ðə bʌs / (BrE)
to board a bus before it departs, especially by arriving in time for its scheduled departure

Examples

To catch the bus on time
He waved and ran to catch the bus as it was about to leave.
I have to hurry if I want to catch the bus to work.
She missed breakfast so she could catch the bus on time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: miss the bus - to fail to board a bus before it leaves
