gent

noun
informal term for a man; gentleman
Pronunciation: / dʒɛnt / (AmE), / dʒɛnt / (BrE)
an informal or old-fashioned word for a man, especially one who is polite or gentlemanly

Examples

A polite gent
He’s a real gent—always holding doors open for others.
Only one other gent was waiting in the lobby when I arrived.
The bathroom marked ‘gents’ is down the corridor to the right.
That gent over there is the new manager.
The gent at the counter offered to help me with my bags.
You don't see many young gents wearing suits these days.

Related Terms
