pochard

noun
a type of diving duck
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊ.tʃərd / (AmE), / ˈpɒtʃ.ɑːd / (BrE)
a heavy-bodied diving duck with a grey-and-black body and a reddish head, found in Eurasia and Africa
