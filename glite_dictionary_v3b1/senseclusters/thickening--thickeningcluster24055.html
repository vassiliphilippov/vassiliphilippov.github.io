thickening

noun
the process of becoming thicker
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪkənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθɪkənɪŋ / (BrE)
the process, result, or area of something becoming thicker or more dense

Examples

Thickening of arteries
A gradual thickening of the arteries can lead to heart disease.
Clouds formed a thickening in the sky as the storm approached.
The thickening of the sauce took several minutes on low heat.
A slight thickening of the skin can occur as a response to repeated friction.
The doctor noticed a thickening in the artery wall during the examination.
The pathologist reported a thickening in the tissue sample that may require further tests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swelling - an abnormal enlargement of a part of the body
  • antonym: thinning - the process of becoming thinner
