state boundary

noun
the dividing line between two states
3.1
Pronunciation: / steɪt ˈbaʊndəri / (AmE), / steɪt ˈbaʊndəri / (BrE)
the official line that separates two states, especially in a country like the United States or India

Examples

The state boundary
A dispute over the state boundary led to legal action between the two governments.
The river marks the state boundary between New York and New Jersey.
They crossed the state boundary during their road trip across the Southeast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: border - an official line separating two political or geographical areas
  • antonym: international boundary - the boundary between two countries
