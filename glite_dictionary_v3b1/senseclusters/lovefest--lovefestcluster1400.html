lovefest

noun
affectionate gathering
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌvˌfɛst / (AmE), / ˈlʌvˌfɛst / (BrE)
an event or situation where people show a lot of affection, praise, or goodwill toward each other, often in an enthusiastic or excessive way

Examples

A lovefest event
The annual family picnic turned into a lovefest with everyone sharing stories and hugs.
After the team's big win, the locker room was a real lovefest of congratulations and cheers.
The awards ceremony became a lovefest as colleagues praised each other's achievements.
The awards ceremony quickly became a lovefest with everyone complimenting each other's achievements.
Their meeting was less a discussion and more of a lovefest filled with praise and agreement.
Some critics called the interview a lovefest because there were no tough questions asked.

Related Terms

  • antonym: argument - a situation marked by disagreement or conflict
  • synonym: celebration - a joyful event or gathering
  • antonym: critique - a detailed analysis and assessment, often critical
  • synonym: mutual admiration society - a group where members praise each other excessively
  • synonym: reunion - a gathering of people who have been apart
