controlled substance

noun
drug or chemical regulated by law
Pronunciation: / kənˈtroʊld ˈsʌbstəns / (AmE), / kənˈtrəʊld ˈsʌbstəns / (BrE)
a drug or chemical whose possession and use are regulated by law due to potential for abuse or harm

Examples

A controlled substance drug
It is illegal to possess a controlled substance without a valid prescription.
Law enforcement seized several types of controlled substances during the investigation.
The doctor explained that the medication is classified as a controlled substance.
