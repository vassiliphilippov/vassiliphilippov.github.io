nailrod

noun
marsh plant with cylindrical seed heads (cattail)
9.5 9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈneɪl.rɑːd/ (AmE), /ˈneɪl.rɒd/ (BrE)
A tall marsh plant with cylindrical seed heads that burst when mature, shedding down; its long, flat leaves are used for making mats and chair seats.

Examples

Tall nailrod plant
As the nailrod matures, its seed head explodes and spreads fluffy down everywhere.
The craftsman collected nailrod leaves to weave sturdy chair seats.
You can find nailrod growing along the edges of the pond in early summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bulrush - another name for marsh plant with similar features
  • synonym: cattail - marsh plant with brown cylindrical seed heads
