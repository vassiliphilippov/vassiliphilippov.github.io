hypothetical creature

noun
creature presumed to exist but never observed
2.4
Pronunciation: / haɪˌpɑːθətɪkəl ˈkriːtʃər / (AmE), / haɪˌpɒθɪtɪkəl ˈkriːtʃə / (BrE)
an animal or being that is imagined or proposed to exist, but has not been observed or proven

Examples

A hypothetical creature in stories
Many myths describe hypothetical creatures that have never been found in the wild.
Scientists sometimes speculate about the existence of hypothetical creatures based on unexplained evidence or folklore.
The search for hypothetical creatures like the Loch Ness Monster fascinates both researchers and the general public.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryptid - an animal whose existence is suggested but not proven
