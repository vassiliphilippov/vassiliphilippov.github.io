nicotine addiction

noun
dependence on nicotine
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪkoʊˌtin əˈdɪkʃən / (AmE), / ˈnɪkətiːn əˈdɪkʃən / (BrE)
a condition characterized by dependence on nicotine, often resulting from regular use of tobacco products

Examples

Nicotine addiction problem
His nicotine addiction began when he started smoking as a teenager.
Medical professionals can offer support to those dealing with nicotine addiction.
She struggled for years to break her nicotine addiction after quitting smoking.
