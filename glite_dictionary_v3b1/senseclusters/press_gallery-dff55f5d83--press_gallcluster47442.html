press gallery

noun
area for reporters in legislative halls
3.2 4.3
Pronunciation: /pres ˈgæl.ə.riː/ (AmE), /pres ˈgæl.ə.ri/ (BrE)
an area, often a balcony, reserved for reporters in a legislative or governmental hall, providing vantage for journalistic observation
