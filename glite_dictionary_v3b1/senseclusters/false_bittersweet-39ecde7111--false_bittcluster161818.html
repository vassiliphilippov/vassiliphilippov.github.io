false bittersweet

noun
North American shrub with yellow capsules and scarlet seeds
Pronunciation: / fɔls ˈbɪtərˌswiːt / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈbɪtəˌswiːt / (BrE)
a North American climbing shrub with yellow capsules that enclose scarlet seeds

Examples
