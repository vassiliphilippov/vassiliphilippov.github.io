Examples

To promise to help
I can't make it tonight, but I promise I'll visit you tomorrow.
She promised to help me finish the project by next week.

Synonyms

  • guarantee
    2.8 53/m
    guarantee assures that something will happen or meet a quality standard, while promise is a general personal commitment to do something.
  • vow
    3.5 10/m
    vow is a serious, often personal and solemn commitment to act or refrain from acting, while promise is more general and everyday.
  • pledge
    4.1 7.71/m
    pledge is a formal public commitment or security often given to support a cause, while promise is a general personal assurance.
  • swear
    4.6 36/m
    swear is a solemn oath often used in court or religious settings, while promise is a normal commitment made in everyday speech.

Related Terms

  • antonym: break - to fail to fulfill a promise or agreement

  • synonym: pledge - to make a formal, serious promise
  • antonym: threaten - state one's intention to harm or punish
  • synonym: vow - to make a solemn promise, especially in a formal or religious context
