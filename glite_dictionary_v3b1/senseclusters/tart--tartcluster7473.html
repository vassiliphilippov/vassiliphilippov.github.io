tart

adjective
sharp or harsh (of speech)
6.7 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / t…ërt / (AmE), / t…ëÀêt / (BrE)
(of a remark or tone) marked by being sharp, biting, or harsh.

Examples

Tart reply
He delivered a tart criticism of the proposed policy.
Her tart reply made everyone fall silent.
The debate became heated with increasingly tart exchanges.
