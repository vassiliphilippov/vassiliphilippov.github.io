thrombophlebitis

noun
vein inflammation with a blood clot
6.9 8.4
Pronunciation: /θrɑːm.boʊ.flə.ˈbaɪ.tɪs/ (AmE), /θrɒm.bə.flə.ˈbaɪ.tɪs/ (BrE)
Inflammation of a vein caused by or accompanied by the formation of a blood clot.

Examples

Thrombophlebitis of vein
Prolonged inactivity can increase the risk of developing thrombophlebitis during long-distance travel.
The patient was diagnosed with thrombophlebitis in his left leg after experiencing pain and swelling.
Treatment for thrombophlebitis often includes anticoagulant medication to prevent further clotting.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: deep vein thrombosis - formation of a blood clot in a deep vein
  • synonym: phlebitis - inflammation of a vein
