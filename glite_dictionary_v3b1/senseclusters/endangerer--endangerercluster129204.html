endangerer

noun
one who causes danger
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdeɪndʒərər / (AmE), / ɪnˈdeɪndʒərə / (BrE)

Examples

Pollution is a major endangerer of marine ecosystems around the world.
The reckless driver became an endangerer to everyone on the road.
