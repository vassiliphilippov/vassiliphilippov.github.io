deferment

noun
postponement; delaying to a future time
9.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɝːmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈfɜːmənt / (BrE)
the act of delaying or postponing something to a later time

Examples

A deferment of payment
Due to unforeseen circumstances, the company announced a deferment of the project launch.
His request for a deferment of the payment was granted by the bank.
The student received a deferment from military service while attending university.
