blood

noun
members of a group, especially new or young
2.6
Pronunciation: / blʌd / (AmE), / blʌd / (BrE)
people considered as members of a particular group, especially new or younger members who bring fresh energy or ideas

Examples

New blood
Bringing in young blood helped the club inject fresh enthusiasm.
The board is always looking for fresh blood to ensure innovation.
The company needed some blood with new perspectives to stay competitive.
