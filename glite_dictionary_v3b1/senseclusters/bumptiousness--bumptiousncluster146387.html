bumptiousness

noun
offensive assertiveness; self-importance
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌmpʃəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈbʌmpʃəsnɪs / (BrE)
offensive or irritating assertiveness and self-confidence

Examples

Bumptiousness annoys people
Despite his talent, his bumptiousness often alienated his colleagues.
Her bumptiousness made it difficult for anyone else to express their opinions during the meeting.
The coach warned the team against letting bumptiousness interfere with their cooperation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogance - proud and unpleasant behaviour; an exaggerated sense of self-importance
  • antonym: modesty - lack of excessive pride; humility
