fatal

adjective
decreed by fate; predetermined
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈfeɪtəl / (BrE)
Controlled or decreed by fate; predetermined and unchangeable.

Examples

Fatal destiny
He considered their meeting to be a fatal coincidence, guided by destiny.
Her life seemed to follow a fatal path, no matter what choices she made.
The novel deals with the idea of a fatal prophecy that cannot be avoided.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fated - determined by fate; certain to happen
  • synonym: predestined - destined to happen in a particular way; predetermined
