trample

noun
sound of heavy footsteps or stomping
Pronunciation: / ˈtræmpəl / (AmE), / ˈtræmpəl / (BrE)
a sound made by heavy, continuous footsteps or stamping, often of a group of people or animals

The trample of hooves filled the air as the horses charged across the field.
We could hear the trample of boots echoing down the hallway.
