yperite

noun
mustard gas agent
Pronunciation: / ˈiːpəˌrit / (AmE), / ˈiːpəˌriːt / (BrE)
a poisonous chemical warfare agent, also known as mustard gas, that causes severe blisters and damage to the lungs

Examples

Deadly yperite gas
The use of yperite in World War I caused long-term health problems for many soldiers.
Protective masks were developed to shield troops from yperite attacks on the battlefield.
Exposure to yperite can result in severe skin burns and respiratory damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mustard gas - a toxic chemical weapon causing blisters and lung damage
  • antonym: sarin - a highly toxic nerve agent used as a chemical weapon
