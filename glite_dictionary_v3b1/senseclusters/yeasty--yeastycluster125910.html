yeasty

adjective
containing or resembling yeast
7.3 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / Ààjisti / (AmE), / ÀàjiÀêsti / (BrE)
Containing, resembling, or characteristic of yeast; often used to describe something frothy, bubbly, or lively.

Examples

Yeasty foam
A yeasty froth appeared on top of the fermenting beer.
The baker's kitchen was filled with the yeasty aroma of fresh bread.
The festival had a yeasty energy, with everyone in high spirits.

Synonyms

  • yeastlike
    5.9 0.00/m
    yeastlike describes a neutral, literal resemblance to yeast, while yeasty suggests a frothy, bubbly, or lively character.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flat - not effervescent or lively; lacking bubbles or spirit
  • synonym: frothy - full of or covered with a mass of small bubbles
