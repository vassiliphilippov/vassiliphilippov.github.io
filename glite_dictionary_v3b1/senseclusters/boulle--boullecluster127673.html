boulle

noun
decorative inlay technique for furniture
9.9
Pronunciation: / bul / (AmE), / buːl / (BrE)
A style of inlay decoration for furniture, using tortoiseshell and metals, especially brass, to form elaborate scroll patterns.

Examples

Boulle work
Collectors admire the intricate boulle work on 18th-century cabinets.
Restoring the damaged boulle inlay required a skilled artisan.
The antique desk featured exquisite boulle marquetry on its surface.
