color chart

noun
chart showing a range of colors
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlər tʃɑrt / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə tʃɑːt / (BrE)
a chart or sheet showing a range of different colors, often used to compare or select colors in design, printing, or manufacturing

Examples

A color chart sheet
Artists often use a color chart to mix and match different pigments.
The designer consulted a color chart to choose the perfect shade for the logo.
You can find a color chart at most paint stores to help select wall colors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: color palette - the range of colors used in a particular context or by an artist
  • synonym: color wheel - a circular arrangement of colors showing their relationships
