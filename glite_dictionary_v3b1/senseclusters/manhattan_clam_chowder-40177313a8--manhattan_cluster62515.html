Manhattan clam chowder

noun
chowder with clams and tomatoes
5.9
Pronunciation: / mænˈhæɾ.n̩ klæm ˈtʃaʊ.dɚ / (AmE), / mænˈhæt.ən klæm ˈtʃaʊ.də / (BrE)
a type of chowder made with clams, tomatoes, and vegetables, seasoned and typically served hot

Examples

A bowl of Manhattan clam chowder
I ordered a bowl of Manhattan clam chowder to start my meal at the seafood restaurant.
The chef's special today features Manhattan clam chowder loaded with fresh vegetables and clams.
Unlike the creamy variety, Manhattan clam chowder has a tomato-based broth.
