clawlike

adjective
resembling a claw
Pronunciation: / ˈklɔˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈklɔːˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the shape or characteristics of a claw

Examples

Clawlike fingers
His fingers curled into a clawlike shape in the cold.
The ancient statue's hand had a clawlike appearance, gripping the stone tightly.
The bird's clawlike talons made it an excellent hunter.
