bundle

noun
a collection of items tied together
3.6 2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌn.dl̩ /; / ˈbʌndəl / (AmE), / ˈbʌn.dl̩ /; / ˈbʌndl̩ /; / ˈbʌnd(ə)l / (BrE)
a group of things tied, wrapped, or packaged together; a collection or package (including software or other items grouped as one)
