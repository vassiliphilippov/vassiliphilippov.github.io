Dijon

proper noun
French mustard
Pronunciation: / diˈʒɑn / (AmE), / diˈʒɒ̃ / (BrE)
a type of mild, spicy mustard originally made in Dijon, France, known for its smooth texture and tangy flavor

Examples

Dijon mustard
She added a spoonful of Dijon to the salad dressing for extra flavor.
The chef recommended using Dijon instead of regular yellow mustard for a richer taste.
He spread Dijon on his sandwich to give it a tangy kick.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mustard - a spicy yellow or brown condiment made from mustard seeds
