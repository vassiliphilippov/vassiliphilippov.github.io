late-night dance marathon

noun
all-night dancing event
3.5
Pronunciation: / leɪt naɪt dæns ˈmɛr.əˌθɑn / (AmE), / leɪt naɪt dɑːns ˈmær.əˌθɒn / (BrE)
an event involving prolonged dancing that takes place late at night, often lasting several hours

Examples

A late-night dance marathon
After midnight, the club hosted a spontaneous late-night dance marathon that lasted until dawn.
The fundraiser was a late-night dance marathon that encouraged everyone to keep moving for charity.
They emerged exhausted from the late-night dance marathon but were still smiling.
