converging

noun
the act of coming together
4.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɝːdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of becoming closer or coming together at a point

Examples

The converging of viewpoints
The converging of multiple viewpoints led to a productive discussion.
Their converging interests made collaboration easy.
We watched the converging of the two rivers as they formed a single stream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convergence - the process or state of converging
  • antonym: diverging - the act or process of moving apart
