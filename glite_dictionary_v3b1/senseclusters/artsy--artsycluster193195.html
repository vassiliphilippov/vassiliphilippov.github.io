artsy

adjective
pretentiously artistic
4.7 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːrt.siː/ (AmE), /ˈɑːt.siː/ (BrE)
pretending to be artistic or creative in a way that seems affected or insincere

Examples

An artsy pose
He decorated his apartment in an artsy style, with abstract paintings and unusual furniture.
Some people find her artsy attitude a bit forced and insincere.
The café has an artsy vibe, filled with handmade crafts and local artwork.
She wore an arty scarf that made her stand out at the gallery opening.
Some cafés try to create an arty atmosphere with abstract paintings and eclectic furniture.
The decor in his apartment is a bit too arty for my taste.
The new restaurant has a very arty vibe, with sculptures and abstract paintings everywhere.
He tries to dress in an arty way to impress his friends at the gallery.
Some people find the film too arty and difficult to understand.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artistic - showing skill or creativity in art
  • antonym: plain - simple and without decoration or artistic style
  • synonym: pretentious - trying to appear more important or cultured than one is
