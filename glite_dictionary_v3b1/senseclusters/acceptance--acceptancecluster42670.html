acceptance

noun
willingness to tolerate or embrace differences
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛptəns / (AmE), / əkˈsɛptəns / (BrE)
a willingness to tolerate or embrace different people, ideas, or situations without protest

Examples

Peaceful acceptance of change
Her calm acceptance of criticism showed her maturity and openness to change.
Promoting acceptance of cultural differences is essential for a harmonious society.
The counselor encouraged acceptance as a way to cope with life's challenges.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intolerance - unwillingness to accept views, beliefs, or behavior that differ from one's own
  • synonym: tolerance - willingness to accept behavior or beliefs different from one's own
