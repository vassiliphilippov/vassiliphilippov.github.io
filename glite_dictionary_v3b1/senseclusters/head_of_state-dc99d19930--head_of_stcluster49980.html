head of state

noun
chief public representative of a country
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛd əv steɪt / (AmE), / ˌhɛd əv steɪt / (BrE)
the chief public representative of a country, such as a president or monarch, who may also be the head of government

Examples

The head of state role
In some countries, the monarch serves as both the head of state and the head of government.
The head of state attended the international summit to discuss trade agreements.
The president is officially recognized as the head of state in the United States.

Related Terms

  • synonym: head of government - the person who runs the government or executive branch of a country
