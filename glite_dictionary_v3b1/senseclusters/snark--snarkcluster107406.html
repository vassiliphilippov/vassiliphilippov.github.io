snark

noun
sarcastic remark
Pronunciation: / snɑrk / (AmE), / snɑːk / (BrE)
a clever, sarcastic, or mocking remark that is often rude or critical

Examples

A witty snark
Her snark made everyone laugh, but some people found it a bit too harsh.
He couldn't resist adding a little snark to his response during the debate.
Online discussions are often filled with snark instead of constructive criticism.
