login

noun
account access credentials
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔːɡˌɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɒɡˌɪn / (BrE)
the information, process, or page used to access a computer system, website, or application (such as a username and password, the login page, or the act of logging in)

  • antonym: logout - the process of ending access to a system or account
  • synonym: sign-in - the process or page for entering credentials to access a system
