stuff

verb
to eat too much; overeat greedily
2.5 4.7
Pronunciation: / stʌf / (AmE), / stʌf / (BrE)
to eat large amounts of food quickly or greedily, especially until one is uncomfortably full

Examples

To stuff yourself

She always stuffs her face with dessert whenever she's stressed.
Try not to stuff yourself before the big game, or you'll feel sluggish.
