unspread

adjective
not unfolded
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsprɛd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsprɛd / (BrE)
not opened out, unfolded, or distributed over an area

Examples

An unspread blanket
The picnic blanket remained unspread as the rain began to fall.
He left the documents unspread on his desk, planning to review them later.
The wings of the butterfly were still unspread after it emerged from its chrysalis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: folded - bent or arranged so that one part covers another
  • antonym: spread - opened out or distributed over an area
