unfrequented

adjective
not often visited or deserted place
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈfriːkwəntɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈfriːkwɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a place) not often visited; deserted or quiet because few or no people or creatures go there.

Examples

An unfrequented road
His cabin stood beside an unfrequented road, far from any bustling towns.
The hikers enjoyed the peace of the unfrequented trail through the forest.
We found an unfrequented beach where we could relax in solitude.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deserted - empty because everyone has left; without people
  • antonym: frequented - (of a place) visited often
  • antonym: populated - inhabited by people or creatures
