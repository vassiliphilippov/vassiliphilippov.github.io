planfulness

noun
careful planning
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈplænfʊlnəs / (AmE), / ˈplænfʊlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being careful and organized in making plans or decisions

Examples

Careful planfulness
Her planfulness ensured that every detail of the event was considered in advance.
The team's planfulness allowed them to avoid last-minute problems during the project.
He admired her planfulness when it came to organizing their family vacation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foresight - the ability to anticipate and prepare for future needs or events
  • antonym: impulsiveness - the tendency to act without careful thought or planning
