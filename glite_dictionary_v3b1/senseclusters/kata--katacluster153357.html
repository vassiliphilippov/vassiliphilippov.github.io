kata

noun
practice exercise
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːtə / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtə / (BrE)
a short, repeatable exercise or routine designed to improve a practical skill through regular practice, especially in programming or teaching

Examples

Coding kata
The programming class started each session with a kata to reinforce basic concepts.
She practiced a new kata every morning to improve her teaching techniques.
Completing a daily kata helped him master complex coding patterns over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drill - a practice exercise to improve a skill
  • synonym: exercise - an activity to develop a particular skill
