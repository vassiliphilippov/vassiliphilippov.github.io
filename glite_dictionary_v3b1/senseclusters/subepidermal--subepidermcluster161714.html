subepidermal

adjective
below the epidermis
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˌɛpɪˈdɜrməl / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˌɛpɪˈdɜːməl / (BrE)
situated or occurring just below the outer layer of skin or tissue

Examples

Subepidermal blister
The doctor identified a subepidermal blister after examining the patient's hand.
Certain skin diseases cause subepidermal separation, leading to painful sores.
A biopsy revealed subepidermal inflammation beneath the surface of the skin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: epidermal - relating to the outer layer of skin
  • synonym: subcutaneous - situated or applied under the skin
