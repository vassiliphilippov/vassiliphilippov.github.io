Synonyms

  • sticking out

  • projecting
    4.2 0.00/m
    projecting means extending outward from a surface or edge in a steady way, while protruding is more general.
  • jutting
    4.8 0.00/m
    jutting describes a sharp or angled extension from a surface, while protruding describes any object sticking out.
  • protrusive
    7.6 0.01/m

  • protuberant
    8.4 0.10/m
