big mouth

noun
person who talks too much or reveals secrets
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ maʊθ / (AmE), / bɪɡ maʊθ / (BrE)
a person who talks too much or is prone to revealing secrets unintentionally

Examples

A big mouth talks
Don't be a big mouth—let them announce the news themselves.
He should learn to stop being such a big mouth in meetings.
She has a big mouth and always spills everyone's secrets.
