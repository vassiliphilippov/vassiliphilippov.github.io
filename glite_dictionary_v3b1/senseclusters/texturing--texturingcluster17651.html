texturing

noun
hair styling technique
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛkstʃərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛkstʃərɪŋ / (BrE)
a hairdressing technique used to thin, layer, or alter the movement and feel of hair

Examples

Hair texturing technique
The stylist suggested texturing to give her straight hair more volume and movement.
After texturing, his hair looked lighter and easier to style every morning.
Many salons offer texturing as part of their standard haircut services.

Related Terms

  • synonym: layering - a method of cutting hair into layers of different lengths
  • synonym: thinning - a technique to reduce the bulk of hair
