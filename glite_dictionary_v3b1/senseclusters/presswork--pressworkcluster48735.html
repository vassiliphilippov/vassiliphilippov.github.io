presswork

noun
metal shaping process
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpre.swɜːrk/ /; / /ˈpres.wɜːrk/ (AmE), /ˈpre.swɜːk/ /; / /ˈpres.wɜːk/ (BrE)
