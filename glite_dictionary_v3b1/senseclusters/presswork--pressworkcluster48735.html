presswork

noun
metal shaping process
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛsˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈprɛsˌwɜːk / (BrE)
the process of shaping, stamping, or cutting metal using a press, or the metal parts produced by this process

Examples

Metal presswork
The factory produced thousands of car doors through precise presswork every month.
Modern presswork allows manufacturers to create complex metal shapes quickly and efficiently.
He inspected the presswork to ensure each metal panel met the required standards.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forging - shaping metal by heating and hammering
  • synonym: stamping - the process of shaping metal using a die and a press
