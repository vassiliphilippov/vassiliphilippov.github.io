stagnantly

adverb
without progress
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈstæɡ.nənt.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈstæɡ.nənt.lɪ/ (BrE)

Examples

Prices remained stagnantly high, causing frustration among local residents.
