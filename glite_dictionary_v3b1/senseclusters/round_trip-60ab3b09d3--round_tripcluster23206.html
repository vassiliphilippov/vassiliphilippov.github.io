round trip

noun
a journey to a place and back
4.2
Pronunciation: / raʊnd trɪp /; / ˈraʊnd ˌtrɪp / (AmE), / raʊnd trɪp /; / ˈraʊnd ˌtrɪp / (BrE)
a journey to a place and back again to the starting point

Examples
