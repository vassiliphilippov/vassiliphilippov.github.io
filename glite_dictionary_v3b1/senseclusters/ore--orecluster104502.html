ore

noun
mineral/rock containing valuable metal
Pronunciation: / ɔːr / (AmE), / ɔː / (BrE)
rock or mineral from which metal can be extracted profitably

Examples

Iron ore
Geologists discovered a new vein of gold ore in the region.
Iron ore is a key raw material in steel production.
The miners extracted copper ore from deep underground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mineral - a naturally occurring substance, usually inorganic, with a definite chemical composition
