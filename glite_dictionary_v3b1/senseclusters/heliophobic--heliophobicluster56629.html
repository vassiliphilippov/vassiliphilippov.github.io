heliophobic

adjective
avoiding sunlight
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːliəˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / ˌhiːliəˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
having a tendency to avoid sunlight, or being sensitive or harmed by sunlight

Examples

Heliophobic plants
Some plants are heliophobic and thrive in shaded areas.
The heliophobic animal remained hidden during the day and only came out at night.
Because of her heliophobic condition, she always wore a wide-brimmed hat outdoors.
Some plants are heliophobic and grow best in shaded areas away from direct sunlight.
Her heliophobic condition requires her to wear protective clothing whenever she goes outside.
Doctors advised the heliophobic patient to avoid sun exposure to prevent skin irritation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heliophilic - thriving in or attracted to sunlight
  • synonym: photophobic - having an aversion to light
  • synonym: photosensitive - sensitive to light, especially sunlight
