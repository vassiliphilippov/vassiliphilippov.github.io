comedo

noun
skin pore blockage (blackhead)
4.4 7.7
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈmiː.doʊ/ /; / /ˈkɑː.mə.doʊ/ (AmE), /kə.ˈmiː.dəʊ/ /; / /ˈkɒm.ə.dəʊ/ (BrE)
A plug of sebum and dead skin cells that blocks a pore of the skin, often appearing as a small bump with a black tip.

Examples

A comedo on skin
A dermatologist explained that a comedo forms when a hair follicle becomes clogged with oil.
Regular cleansing may help prevent the formation of a comedo and other types of pimples.
The patient complained about a persistent comedo on her forehead that would not heal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blackhead - a small dark spot on the skin caused by a blocked pore (medical term for open comedo)
