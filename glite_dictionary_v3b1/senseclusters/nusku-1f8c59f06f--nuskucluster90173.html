Nusku

proper noun
Mesopotamian god of fire and light
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnʊsku / (AmE), / ˈnʊsku / (BrE)
an ancient Mesopotamian god of fire and light, corresponding to the Babylonian Girru

Examples

Nusku god of fire
