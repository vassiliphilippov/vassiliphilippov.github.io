nonepileptic

noun
person without epilepsy
7.7
Pronunciation: / nʌnˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk / (AmE), / nʌnˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk / (BrE)
a person who does not have epilepsy, often used as a control subject in medical studies

Examples
