race

verb
work or move quickly towards a goal
3.0
Pronunciation: / reɪs / (AmE), / reɪs / (BrE)
to move or work very quickly, often in a hurry or to achieve something fast

Examples

To race to finish
Companies are racing to release the first electric self-driving car.
Our team is racing against the deadline to finish the project.
Scientists around the world are racing to develop a new vaccine.
She raced through the crowded airport to catch her flight.
The children raced down the stairs to open their presents.
We had to race to catch the last train home.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dawdle - to move slowly, wasting time
  • synonym: rush - to do something with great speed, often hurriedly
