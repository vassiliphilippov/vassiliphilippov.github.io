lamination

noun
bonding thin sheets together
3.3 4.1
Pronunciation: /læ.mɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ /; / /læ.mə.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /læ.mɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ /; / /læ.mə.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of bonding thin layers together, or the result of this process (a layered structure), often to protect or strengthen something
