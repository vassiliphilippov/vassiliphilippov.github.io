terrain

noun
land's physical features; land type
9.4
Pronunciation: / təˈræn / (AmE), / təˈreɪn / (BrE)
an area of land, especially with regard to its physical features or suitability for a particular purpose

Examples

Mountain terrain
Farmers must consider the terrain before deciding which crops to plant.
The hikers appreciated the variety of terrain along the trail, from forests to open meadows.
The soldiers struggled to navigate the rough terrain of the mountain pass.
