masonry

noun
construction of stone or brick
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪsənri / (AmE), / ˈmeɪsənri / (BrE)
A structure or construction made of stone, brick, or similar materials, typically built by a mason.

Examples

Old masonry walls
Skilled workers repaired the crumbling masonry on the cathedral walls.
The ancient temple's masonry has withstood earthquakes for centuries.
The house features beautiful red brick masonry around its entrance.
