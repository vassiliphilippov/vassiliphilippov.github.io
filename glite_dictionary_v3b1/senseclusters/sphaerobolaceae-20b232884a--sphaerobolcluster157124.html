Sphaerobolaceae

noun
family of fungi ejecting spherical gleba
9.9
Pronunciation: / sfɪroʊboʊˈleɪsiˌi / (AmE), / sfɪərəʊbəʊˈleɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
A family of fungi in which the nearly spherical spore mass (gleba) is forcibly ejected at maturity; contains a single genus.

Examples

Sphaerobolaceae fungi family
Sphaerobolaceae are notable for their fruiting bodies that eject spore masses with force.
Scientists recently discovered a new species within the Sphaerobolaceae family.
The Sphaerobolaceae display a fascinating mechanism of spore dispersal among fungi.
