nudie

noun
naked person
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːdi / (AmE), / ˈnjuːdi / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is not wearing any clothes

Examples

A nudie on the beach
The children laughed when a nudie ran past on the beach.
He accidentally walked into the wrong room and found himself face-to-face with a nudie.
At the art class, the model posed as a nudie for the students to draw.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clothed - wearing clothes
  • synonym: nude - a person who is not wearing clothes
