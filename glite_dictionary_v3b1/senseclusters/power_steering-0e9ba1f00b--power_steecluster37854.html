power steering

noun
steering system with power-assist in vehicles
4.5 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpaʊ.ər ˈstɪr.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpaʊ.ə ˈstɪə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
a system in motor vehicles that uses hydraulic or electric power to assist the driver in turning the steering wheel, making steering easier

Examples

Power steering system
Many modern cars come with power steering to make driving more comfortable.
She noticed something was wrong when her power steering started making a strange noise.
When the power steering failed, it became very difficult to turn the wheel.
