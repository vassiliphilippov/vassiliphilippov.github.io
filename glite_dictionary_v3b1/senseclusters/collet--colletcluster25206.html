collet

noun
cone-shaped chuck for holding objects
6.8 8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.lɪt/ (AmE), /ˈkɒ.lɪt/ (BrE)
A cone-shaped device used to hold a cylindrical object firmly in place in a lathe or other machine tool.

Examples

Machine collet
A collet provides a precise grip on the workpiece during high-speed rotation.
Different sizes of collets are available for various diameters of rods.
The machinist inserted the rod into the collet before tightening it securely.
