tête-à-tête

adjective
private or face-to-face between two people
8.9
Pronunciation: / teɪ.təˈtɛt / (AmE), / teɪt.aˈtɛt / (BrE)
(of an event or situation) involving only two people, especially in private; intimately private or face-to-face.

Examples

A tete-a-tete meeting
After the meeting, the manager invited her for a brief tête-à-tête in his office.
The couple had a romantic tête-à-tête dinner by candlelight.
They enjoyed a quiet tête-à-tête in the corner of the café.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confidential - intended to be kept secret
  • synonym: private - intended for or restricted to the use of one particular person or group of people
