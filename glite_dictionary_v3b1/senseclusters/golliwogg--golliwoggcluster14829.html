golliwogg

noun
offensive caricatured black doll
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑliˌwɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒlɪˌwɒɡ / (BrE)
A soft toy or doll resembling a caricatured black minstrel, with exaggerated features and usually dressed in formal clothes; now regarded as an offensive racist caricature.

Examples

A golliwogg doll
As a child, she owned a golliwogg, though it would be considered inappropriate today.
The book featured a golliwogg character that reflected the prejudices of its era.
The museum removed the golliwogg from its collection after complaints about racist imagery.
