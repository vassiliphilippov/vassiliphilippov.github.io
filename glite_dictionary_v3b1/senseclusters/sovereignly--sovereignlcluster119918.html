sovereignly

adverb
with supreme authority
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːvrənli / (AmE), / ˈsɒvrɪnli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows supreme power, authority, or independence

Examples

Sovereignly rules a country
The president acted sovereignly when making decisions for the nation.
Throughout history, some leaders have ruled sovereignly without regard for others' opinions.
The country declared its independence and governed sovereignly from then on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authoritatively - in a commanding or confident manner
  • antonym: submissively - in a manner showing willingness to obey or yield
