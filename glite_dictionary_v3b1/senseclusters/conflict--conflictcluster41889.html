conflict

noun
incompatibility of events or dates
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.flɪkt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.flɪkt / (BrE)
a situation in which events, dates, or activities cannot occur together due to incompatibility

Examples

Conflict of schedules
A conflict arose when the two committees scheduled their meetings for the same day.
Please check for any conflict with existing appointments before confirming the reservation.
There is a conflict between the dates of the conference and your vacation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clash - an instance of two things happening at the same time causing a scheduling problem
