fuze setter

noun
device for setting artillery fuze timing
8.6
Pronunciation: / fjuːz ˈsɛtər / (AmE), / fjuːz ˈsɛtə / (BrE)
a device used to set the timing on mechanical fuzes of artillery projectiles

Examples

Mechanical fuze setter
After each drill, soldiers were trained to check the fuze setter for calibration and accuracy.
Modern artillery systems often feature an automated fuze setter integrated with fire control computers.
The technician carefully used the fuze setter to prepare the shells before loading them into the gun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fuze - a device that initiates an explosion in ammunition
