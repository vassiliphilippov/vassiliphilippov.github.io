workingwoman

noun
female manual/industrial laborer
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrkɪŋˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkɪŋˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is employed in manual or industrial labor, especially as contrasted with women who do not work outside the home.

Examples

A workingwoman in factory
Many workingwomen fought for better wages and safer conditions in the early 20th century.
The factory hired several workingwomen to help increase production during the busy season.
The organization offers support to workingwomen balancing careers and family life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: housewife - a married woman whose main occupation is caring for her family and home
  • synonym: workingman - a man who works for wages, especially manual labor
  • synonym: workman - a man who does manual or industrial labor
