brei

noun
soft porridge
9.7
Pronunciation: / braɪ / (AmE), / braɪ / (BrE)
a soft porridge or mush, especially eaten in South African or Jewish cuisine

Examples

A bowl of brei
For breakfast, she served a warm bowl of brei with honey and cinnamon.
In many South African homes, brei is a traditional comfort food for children.
He learned to make brei from his grandmother's old recipe book.
