interrater

noun
independent evaluator
8.0
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tə.ˈreɪ.tər/ /; / /ən.tə.ˈreɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈreɪ.tə/ /; / /ən.tə.ˈreɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person who independently judges, scores, or assesses the same items or performances as others in a group

Examples

An interrater scores tests
The research team used two interraters to score each test for greater reliability.
Disagreements between interraters were resolved through discussion and consensus.
Training was provided to all interraters to ensure consistent evaluation standards.
