coccidian

adjective
about coccidia parasites
Pronunciation: / kɑkˈsɪdiən / (AmE), / kɒkˈsɪdiən / (BrE)
relating to or caused by coccidia, a group of microscopic protozoan parasites that infect animals.

Examples

Coccidian infection
The veterinarian diagnosed the bird with a coccidian infection after examining its stool sample.
Researchers studied the effects of coccidian parasites on livestock health in the region.
Proper sanitation can help prevent the spread of coccidian diseases among poultry.
