wonga

noun
Australian ground pigeon
Pronunciation: / ˈwɒŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈwɒŋɡə / (BrE)
a large ground-dwelling pigeon native to eastern Australia, known for its distinctive plumage and shy behavior

Examples

A wonga pigeon
The wonga is often seen foraging quietly on the forest floor in eastern Australia.
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a rare wonga during their morning walk.
A shy wonga rustled through the leaf litter in the rainforest at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dove - a bird closely related to pigeons, often smaller and with similar habits
  • synonym: pigeon - a bird with a stout body and short legs, found worldwide
