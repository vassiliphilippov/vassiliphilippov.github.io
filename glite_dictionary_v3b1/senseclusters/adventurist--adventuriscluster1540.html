adventurist

adjective
reckless or opportunistic
Pronunciation: / ədˈvɛntʃərɪst / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃərɪst / (BrE)
acting in a risky, reckless, or opportunistic way, especially in political, military, or economic situations

Examples

Adventurist policy
The leader's adventurist approach to foreign policy worried many of his advisors.
Critics accused the company of making adventurist investments without proper research.
Some historians blame the war on the government's adventurist decisions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cautious - careful to avoid danger or risk
  • synonym: reckless - not thinking about the possible bad effects of your actions
