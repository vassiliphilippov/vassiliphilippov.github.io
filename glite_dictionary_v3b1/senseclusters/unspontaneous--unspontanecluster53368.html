unspontaneous

adjective
not self-initiating
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnspɑnˈteɪniəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnspɒnˈteɪniəs / (BrE)
not occurring or proceeding without the input of external energy or work; not spontaneous.

Examples

Unspontaneous reaction
The chemical reaction is unspontaneous and will not proceed unless energy is supplied.
Because the process is unspontaneous, it requires constant heating to continue.
Photosynthesis is an unspontaneous reaction that depends on sunlight as an energy source.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonspontaneous - not occurring naturally without external influence
  • antonym: spontaneous - occurring naturally without external cause
