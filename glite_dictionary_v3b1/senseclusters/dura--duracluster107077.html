dura

noun
tough outer membrane of the meninges
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʊr.ə / (AmE), / ˈdjʊə.rə / (BrE)
the outermost and toughest of the three membranes (meninges) covering the brain and spinal cord

Examples

The dura mater
An infection of the dura can lead to serious neurological complications.
Damage to the dura may result in leakage of cerebrospinal fluid.
The surgeon carefully separated the dura from the tissue beneath during the operation.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: arachnoid - the middle of the three meninges
  • degree higher: meninges - the three membranes covering the brain and spinal cord
  • degree lower: pia - the innermost of the three meninges
