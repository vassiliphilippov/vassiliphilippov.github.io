semiclassically

adverb
using classical and quantum
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiklæsɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsɛmɪˈklæsɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or uses both classical and quantum methods or theories

Examples

Semiclassically solved equations
The researchers calculated the energy levels semiclassically to simplify the complex quantum equations.
Some properties of the atom can be explained semiclassically before introducing full quantum theory.
Physicists often analyze particle motion semiclassically when exact quantum solutions are difficult.
