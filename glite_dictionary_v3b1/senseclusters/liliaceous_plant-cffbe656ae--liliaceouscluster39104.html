liliaceous plant

noun
plant of or like the lily family
9.9 6.9
Pronunciation: /lɪ.lɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃəs plænt/ (AmE), /lɪ.lɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃəs plɑːnt/ (BrE)
any plant belonging to or resembling the lily family, typically growing from a bulb, corm, rhizome, or tuber.

Examples

liliaceous plants like lilies
Gardeners often plant liliaceous plants like lilies and hyacinths for their beautiful flowers and easy care.
Many liliaceous plants store nutrients in underground bulbs to survive harsh winters.
The tulip is a well-known example of a liliaceous plant, prized for its vibrant spring blooms.
