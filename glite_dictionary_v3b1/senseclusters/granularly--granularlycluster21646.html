granularly

adverb
in a grainy way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrænjʊlərli / (AmE), / ˈɡranjʊləli / (BrE)
in a way that is made up of or shows small, distinct particles or grains

Examples

Dissolve granularly
The sugar dissolved granularly in the hot water, leaving a slightly rough texture.
The artist applied the paint granularly to create a unique, textured effect on the canvas.
Under the microscope, the material was arranged granularly, with each particle clearly visible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coarsely - in a rough or grainy manner
  • antonym: smoothly - in a way that is even and without grains or roughness
