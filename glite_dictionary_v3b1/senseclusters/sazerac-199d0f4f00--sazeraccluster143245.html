Sazerac

noun
cocktail with whiskey, bitters, and Pernod
Pronunciation: / ˈsæzəˌræk / (AmE), / ˈsæzərak / (BrE)
A cocktail made with rye whiskey or bourbon, bitters, sugar, Pernod (or absinthe), and lemon peel, served over ice.
