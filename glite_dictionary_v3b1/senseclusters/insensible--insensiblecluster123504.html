insensible

adjective
unresponsive or unconscious
7.4 7.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛnsəbəl /; / ɪnˈsɛnsəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛnsəbəl /; / ɪnˈsɛnsəbl̩ / (BrE)
unable to feel or respond to physical sensations; unconscious or without physical feeling

Examples

Insensible to pain
After hours in the freezing cold, he became almost insensible to pain.
He was found insensible on the floor after the accident.
The patient remained insensible despite repeated attempts to wake him.
After the accident, he lay insensible to pain.
An insensible object like a rock cannot feel heat or cold.
The patient was insensible to the cold due to nerve damage.
