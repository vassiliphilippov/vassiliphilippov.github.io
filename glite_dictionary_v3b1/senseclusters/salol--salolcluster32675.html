salol

noun
a medicinal white powder used in pharmacy
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlɔl / (AmE), / ˈseɪlɒl / (BrE)
a white, crystalline powder (phenyl salicylate) used as an antiseptic, preservative, or to coat pills for delayed release

Examples

Salol powder
Salol is rarely used today, but it played a significant role in early pharmaceutical preparations.
Researchers tested the effectiveness of salol as a preservative in the new formulation.
The pharmacist coated the pills with salol for enteric release.
