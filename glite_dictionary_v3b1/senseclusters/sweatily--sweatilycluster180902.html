sweatily

adverb
in a sweaty way
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛtɪli / (AmE), / ˈswɛtɪli / (BrE)
in a way that involves being covered with or producing sweat, due to heat, physical activity, or nervousness

Examples

To sweatily work out
He entered the room sweatily after jogging in the midday sun.
She sweatily wiped her forehead after finishing the intense workout.
The players sweatily celebrated their victory on the hot summer field.
He waited sweatily outside the interview room, wiping his hands on his trousers.
She spoke sweatily in front of the large audience, her voice trembling slightly.
After running to catch the bus, he arrived sweatily and out of breath.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a relaxed and peaceful way
  • antonym: dryly - in a way that is not wet or sweaty
  • synonym: nervously - in a way that shows nervousness or anxiety
  • synonym: perspiringly - in a manner involving perspiration
