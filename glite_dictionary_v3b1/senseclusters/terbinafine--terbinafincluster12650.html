terbinafine

noun
antifungal medication
Pronunciation: / tərˈbɪnəˌfin / (AmE), / təˈbɪnəˌfiːn / (BrE)
an antifungal drug, taken orally or applied topically, used to treat fungal infections such as athlete's foot and nail fungus

Examples

terbinafine antifungal medicine
Many athletes use terbinafine to combat stubborn cases of athlete's foot.
Side effects of terbinafine may include headache and gastrointestinal discomfort.
The doctor prescribed terbinafine to treat the persistent nail infection.
