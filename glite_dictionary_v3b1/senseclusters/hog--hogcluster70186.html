hog

noun
young sheep not yet sheared
5.5 9.8
Pronunciation: / hɑɡ / (AmE), / hɒɡ / (BrE)
A young sheep up to the age of one year, especially one that has not yet been sheared.

Examples

A young hog (sheep)
Before its first shearing, a young sheep is called a hog in some regions.
In spring, the hogs are among the last to be brought into the shearing shed.
The farmer separated the hog from the older sheep for special feeding.
