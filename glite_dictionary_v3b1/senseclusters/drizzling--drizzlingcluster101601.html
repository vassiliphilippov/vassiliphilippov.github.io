drizzling

adjective
light rain in small drops
7.3 1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪzəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrɪzlɪŋ / (BrE)
(of rain) falling lightly in very small drops; characterized by a fine, misty rain.

Examples

Light drizzling rain
It was a cold, drizzling morning that made the city look gray and damp.
The forecast said only drizzling showers, so we left our umbrellas behind.
We walked home in the drizzling rain, a thin mist coating our coats and hair.
