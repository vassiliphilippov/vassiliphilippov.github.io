from hunger

preposition
motivated by hunger
Pronunciation: / frəm ˈhʌŋɡər / (AmE), / frəm ˈhʌŋɡə / (BrE)
because of being hungry; motivated by a feeling of hunger

Examples

Eat from hunger

She fainted from hunger during the long trek through the desert.
Sometimes people steal from hunger when they have no other choice.
