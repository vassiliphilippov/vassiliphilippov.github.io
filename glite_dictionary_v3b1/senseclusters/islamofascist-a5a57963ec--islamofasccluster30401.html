Islamofascist

noun
offensive political slur
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪsləmoʊˈfæʃɪst / (AmE), / ˌɪsləməʊˈfæʃɪst / (BrE)
an offensive slur used to insult or criticize certain Islamic political groups by comparing them to fascists

Examples

Called an Islamofascist
The use of the word Islamofascist in public debate is widely considered offensive and inflammatory.
Many organizations have condemned the term Islamofascist as a slur against Muslim communities.
The article criticized politicians for labeling their opponents as Islamofascists without justification.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Islamist - a person who supports political movements based on Islamic principles
  • synonym: fascist - a person who supports or believes in a dictatorial, right-wing system of government
