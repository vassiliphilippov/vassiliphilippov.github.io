Idesia

noun
genus of East Asian trees (Idesia polycarpa)
9.9
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdiːziə / (AmE), / aɪˈdiːziə / (BrE)
A genus of deciduous trees in the willow family, native to East Asia, containing the species Idesia polycarpa.
