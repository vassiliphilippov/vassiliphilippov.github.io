micromanagement

noun
excessive control at work
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈmænɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
a management style in which a manager closely controls or oversees every small detail of employees' work

Examples

Manager's micromanagement style
The team's productivity dropped because of the manager's constant micromanagement.
Employees often feel stressed when there is too much micromanagement in the workplace.
Good leaders avoid micromanagement and trust their staff to do their jobs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: delegation - the act of giving responsibility or tasks to others
  • synonym: overmanagement - excessive or unnecessary management or supervision
