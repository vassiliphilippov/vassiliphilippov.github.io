scorer

noun
logger who marks trees to be felled
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔɚɚ / (AmE), / ˈskɔːrə / (BrE)
a logger whose job is to mark trees that are to be felled

Examples

Forest scorer marks trees
As a scorer, she carefully marked each trunk for the cutting crew.
The scorer walked through the forest, identifying trees for removal.
The team relied on the scorer's expertise to select which trees to harvest.
