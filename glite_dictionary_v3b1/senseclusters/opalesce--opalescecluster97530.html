opalesce

verb
show colors like an opal
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊ.pəˈlɛs / (AmE), / ˌəʊ.pəˈlɛs / (BrE)
to show or reflect a play of colors like an opal, with a shimmering or iridescent appearance

Examples

To opalesce with colors
As the sunlight streamed through the glass, it began to opalesce beautifully.
Certain minerals opalesce when exposed to ultraviolet light, showing remarkable colors.
The surface of the lake seemed to opalesce at dawn, glimmering with shifting hues.
As the soap bubble drifted through the air, its surface would opalesce beautifully.
Her silk dress seemed to opalesce with every step she took under the ballroom lights.
The dewdrops on the grass began to opalesce in the early morning sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iridesce - to show luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles
  • synonym: opalescent - displaying a milky iridescence like that of an opal
