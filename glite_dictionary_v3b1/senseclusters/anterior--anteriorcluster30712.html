anterior

adjective
situated at the front or head end
6.2
Pronunciation: / ænˈtɪriɚ / (AmE), / ænˈtɪəriə / (BrE)
situated at or toward the front, especially with reference to the head or front part of an organism or structure

Examples

Anterior part
Insects have their eyes located on the anterior section of the head.
The anterior part of the brain is responsible for complex thinking and decision-making.
The surgeon made an incision on the anterior surface of the knee.

Related Terms

  • antonym: posterior - situated at or toward the rear or back, especially in anatomy
