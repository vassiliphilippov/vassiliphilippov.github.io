bottle capper

noun
device for capping bottles
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːt̬l ˌkæpər / (AmE), / ˈbɒtl ˌkapə / (BrE)
a manual or automatic device used to seal bottles with caps

Examples

A bottle capper machine
After filling each bottle, he placed it under the bottle capper to secure the lid tightly.
Home brewers often use a handheld bottle capper to seal their beer bottles.
The brewery invested in a new bottle capper to speed up their packaging process.
