Vishnu

proper noun
Hindu god, the preserver
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʃ.nu / (AmE), / ˈvɪʃ.njuː / (BrE)
A principal Hindu god, worshipped as the preserver and protector of the universe.

Examples

Vishnu god
In Hindu mythology, Vishnu is known for his ten incarnations, including Rama and Krishna.
Many Hindus pray to Vishnu as the protector and sustainer of the universe.
The temple was dedicated to Vishnu, who is depicted holding a conch and a discus.
