connectable

adjective
able to be joined
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛktəbəl / (AmE), / kəˈnɛktəbəl / (BrE)
able to be joined, linked, or attached to something else

Examples

Connectable cable
This cable is connectable to most modern smartphones and tablets.
The new software is connectable with various online platforms for easy data sharing.
All the modules in the system are connectable, allowing for flexible configurations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attachable - able to be fastened or joined to something
  • antonym: detachable - able to be removed or separated
