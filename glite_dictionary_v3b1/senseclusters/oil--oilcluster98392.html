oil

verb
apply oil, esp. ritually
Pronunciation: / ɔɪl / (AmE), / ɔɪl / (BrE)
to put oil on something, either to treat, lubricate, or as part of a ritual

Examples

To oil a machine
In ancient traditions, healers would oil the sick as part of a blessing.
She oiled the child's forehead according to the custom of her faith.
The priest will oil the new altar during the dedication ceremony.
After sanding the wood, she carefully oiled the surface for a smooth finish.
Make sure to oil the hinges to prevent them from squeaking.
The chef oiled the pan before adding the vegetables.

  • synonym: grease - to smear or lubricate with grease
  • synonym: lubricate - to apply an oily or greasy substance to lessen friction
