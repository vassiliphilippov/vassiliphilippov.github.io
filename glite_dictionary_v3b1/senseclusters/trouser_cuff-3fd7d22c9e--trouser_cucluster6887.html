trouser cuff

noun
hem at the bottom of trouser legs
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtraʊzər kʌf / (AmE), / ˈtraʊzə kʌf / (BrE)
the turned-up or finished hem at the bottom of a trouser leg, often sewn in place as a decorative or functional feature

Examples

Sewn trouser cuffs
He paid extra to have trouser cuffs added to his new suit.
Many classic dress pants feature trouser cuffs for added style.
Mud splashed onto his trouser cuffs as he walked down the rainy street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cuff - the end part of a sleeve or pant leg, where the material is turned back or a separate band is sewn on
