take the stand

verb
testify in court
2.7
Pronunciation: / teɪk ðə stænd / (AmE), / teɪk ðə stænd / (BrE)

Examples

To take the stand in court
He was nervous as he prepared to take the stand in the high-profile trial.
Many witnesses refused to take the stand, fearing public scrutiny.
The prosecutor called her to take the stand and recount what she had witnessed.
