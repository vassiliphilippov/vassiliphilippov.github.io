douchey

adjective
obnoxious or arrogant
Pronunciation: / ˈduːʃi / (AmE), / ˈduːʃi / (BrE)
behaving in an obnoxious, arrogant, or unpleasant way, often in a manner that annoys others

Examples

A douchey comment
He sounded really douchey when he kept interrupting everyone at the meeting.
Some people find his douchey attitude hard to tolerate at parties.
Wearing sunglasses indoors can make you look a bit douchey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogant - having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance
  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low view of one's importance
  • synonym: obnoxious - extremely unpleasant or offensive
