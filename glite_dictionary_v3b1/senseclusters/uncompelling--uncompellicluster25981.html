uncompelling

adjective
not interesting or convincing
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəmˈpɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəmˈpɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
not able to attract interest, attention, or convince someone

Examples

An uncompelling story
Despite the impressive cast, the story remained uncompelling throughout the film.
Her explanation was so uncompelling that no one believed her.
The book's plot was uncompelling, and I struggled to finish it.

  • synonym: dull - not interesting or exciting
  • synonym: unconvincing - not able to persuade or convince
