broking

verb
to arrange deals
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
to act as a broker by arranging, negotiating, or facilitating deals or agreements between parties for a fee or commission

Examples

To broking a deal
She has been broking real estate deals for over a decade.
The company specializes in broking agreements between international partners.
He started broking after gaining experience in the financial sector.

  • synonym: mediate - to help two sides reach an agreement
  • synonym: negotiate - to discuss in order to reach an agreement
  • antonym: sell - to give something in exchange for money
