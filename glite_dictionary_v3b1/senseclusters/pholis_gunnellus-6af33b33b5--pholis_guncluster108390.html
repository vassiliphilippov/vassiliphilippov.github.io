Pholis gunnellus

noun
species of slippery Atlantic fish
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊlɪs ɡʌˈnɛləs / (AmE), / ˈfəʊlɪs ɡʌˈnɛləs / (BrE)
A slippery, scaleless food fish of the northern Atlantic coastal waters, also known as the butterfish or rock gunnel.
