trampolining

noun
jumping on trampoline
Pronunciation: / ˈtræmpəˌliːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtræmpəliːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or sport of jumping and performing acrobatic movements on a trampoline

Examples

Trampolining sport
Many children enjoy trampolining as a fun way to exercise and play.
She took up trampolining and quickly learned how to do flips and twists.
Competitive trampolining requires both skill and strong coordination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gymnastics - a sport involving exercises and performances requiring physical strength and flexibility
  • synonym: trampoline - a piece of equipment with a strong fabric for jumping and bouncing
