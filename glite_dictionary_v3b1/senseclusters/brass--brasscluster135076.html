brass

noun
senior management or decision-makers
Pronunciation: / bræs / (AmE), / brɑːs / (BrE)
senior officers or the people who have the power to make decisions in an organization; top management

Examples

Company brass meeting
After the incident, the brass called an emergency meeting with staff.
Getting approval from the brass can sometimes take weeks.
The brass at headquarters made the final decision on the merger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leadership - the action or position of leading a group or organization
  • synonym: management - the people in charge of running an organization
  • antonym: staff - the employees of an organization as a whole
