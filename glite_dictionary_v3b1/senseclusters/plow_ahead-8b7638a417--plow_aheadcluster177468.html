plow ahead

verb
continue resolutely despite difficulties
9.2
Pronunciation: / plaʊ əˈhɛd / (AmE), / plaʊ əˈhɛd / (BrE)
to continue to do something determinedly, especially despite difficulties or opposition

Examples

To plow ahead firmly
Despite the bad weather, the team decided to plow ahead with the construction.
She chose to plow ahead with her plans, even though others advised caution.
We will plow ahead with the project, no matter what challenges arise.
