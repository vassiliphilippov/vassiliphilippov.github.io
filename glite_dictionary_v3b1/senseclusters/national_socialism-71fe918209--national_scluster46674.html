national socialism

noun
Nazi political ideology
Pronunciation: / ˌnæʃənəl ˈsoʊʃəlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌnæʃənəl ˈsəʊʃəlɪzəm / (BrE)
A political ideology characterized by extreme nationalism, racism, dictatorial leadership, and expansionist aims, especially as practiced by the Nazi Party in Germany.

Examples

National socialism ideology
Adolf Hitler rose to power by promoting national socialism in Germany during the 1930s.
Many books have been written analyzing the economic and social aspects of national socialism.
The dangers of national socialism are often discussed in history classes around the world.
