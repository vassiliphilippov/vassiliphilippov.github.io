issueless

adjective
without children
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɪsjuːləs / (AmE), / ˈɪsjuːləs / (BrE)
having no children or descendants; not producing offspring, especially in legal or formal contexts

Examples

Issueless nobleman
The nobleman died issueless, leaving his estate to be divided among distant relatives.
In the will, it was stated that if the heir remained issueless, the property would revert to the crown.
The couple lived a long and happy life but ultimately passed away issueless.

Related Terms

  • synonym: childless - having no children
  • antonym: descendant - a person who is a direct offspring of someone
