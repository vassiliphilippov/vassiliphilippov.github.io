Frisbee

noun
plastic flying disc toy for throwing
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪz.bi / (AmE), / ˈfrɪz.bi / (BrE)
A light, plastic flying disc, typically around 10 inches in diameter, thrown and caught for recreation or in competitive games.

Examples

A Frisbee disc
She caught the speeding Frisbee with one hand, impressing her friends.
The dog loves to chase a Frisbee across the backyard.
We spent the afternoon at the park tossing a Frisbee back and forth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flying disc - a general term for a disc-shaped toy thrown and caught by players
