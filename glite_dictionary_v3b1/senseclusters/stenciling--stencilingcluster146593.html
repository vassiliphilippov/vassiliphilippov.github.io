stenciling

noun
decorating with templates
3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsten.sə.lɪŋ/ /; / /ˈsten.slɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈsten.sə.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of making designs or patterns by applying paint or ink over a cut-out template, or the pattern created this way
