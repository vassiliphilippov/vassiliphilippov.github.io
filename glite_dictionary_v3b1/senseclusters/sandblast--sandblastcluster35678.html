sandblast

noun
air blast carrying sand
Pronunciation: / ˈsændˌblæst / (AmE), / ˈsændˌblɑːst / (BrE)
a strong current of air carrying sand, often eroding surfaces or causing damage

Examples

A desert sandblast
A sudden sandblast forced the hikers to take shelter behind large rocks.
After the sandblast, a fine layer of grit covered everything in sight.
Protective gear is essential when working in areas prone to sandblasts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sandstorm - a strong wind carrying clouds of sand over a large area
