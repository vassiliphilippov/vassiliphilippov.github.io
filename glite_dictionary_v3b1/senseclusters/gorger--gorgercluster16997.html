gorger

noun
person who eats greedily
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔrdʒər / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːdʒə / (BrE)
A person who eats food rapidly and greedily.

Examples

A gorger at buffet
At the buffet, the gorger piled his plate high and ate with astonishing speed.
Despite his slim build, he was a secret gorger at parties.
He was always known as the family gorger, finishing everyone else’s leftovers at dinner.
