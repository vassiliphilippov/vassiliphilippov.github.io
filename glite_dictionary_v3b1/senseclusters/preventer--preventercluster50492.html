preventer

noun
sailing safety device
7.9
Pronunciation: / prɪˈvɛntər / (AmE), / prɪˈvɛntə / (BrE)
a rope or device on a sailboat used to stop the boom from swinging across the deck or causing an accidental jibe

Examples

Sailboat preventer rope
The skipper attached a preventer to the boom before sailing downwind.
Using a preventer can help avoid dangerous accidental jibes in strong winds.
Every crew member learned how to rig the preventer during the safety drill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boom vang - a device used to control the angle and tension of a sailboat's boom
  • antonym: jibe - to change a sailboat's direction so that the stern passes through the wind
