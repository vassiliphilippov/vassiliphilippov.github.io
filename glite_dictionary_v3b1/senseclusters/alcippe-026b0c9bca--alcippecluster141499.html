Alcippe

proper noun
mythological daughter of Ares
9.9
Pronunciation: / ælˈsɪpi / (AmE), / ælˈsɪpi / (BrE)
a figure in Greek mythology, the daughter of Ares whose assault led to Ares' trial on the Areopagus

Examples

Alcippe daughter of Ares
