tilting board

noun
a board on a fulcrum for seesaw play
7.3 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪltɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈtɪltɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
A long, narrow board balanced on a central support, ridden up and down by children sitting at either end; a seesaw.

Examples

Children ride tilting board
As soon as they saw the tilting board, they ran over to take turns riding it.
The children spent hours playing on the tilting board at the park.
The old wooden tilting board creaked as two kids balanced at either end.
