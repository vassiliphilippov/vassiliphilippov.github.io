writhingly

adverb
twisting or squirming manner
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪðɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈraɪðɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that twists or squirms, often because of pain or discomfort

Examples

Snake moves writhingly
The snake moved writhingly through the grass after being disturbed.
He lay writhingly on the floor, clutching his injured leg.
The actor portrayed the character's pain writhingly during the dramatic scene.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful or relaxed manner
