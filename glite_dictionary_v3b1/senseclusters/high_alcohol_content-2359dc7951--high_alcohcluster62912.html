high alcohol content

noun
containment of large amount of alcohol
Pronunciation: / haɪ ˈæl.kəˌhɑl ˈkɑn.tɛnt / (AmE), / haɪ ˈæl.kəˌhɒl ˈkɒn.tɛnt / (BrE)
containing a significant or unusually large amount of alcohol compared to other similar beverages

Examples

High alcohol content drink
Because of its high alcohol content, the spirit should be consumed cautiously.
Beers with high alcohol content are often labeled as 'strong ales'.
The whiskey was noted for its high alcohol content, making it stronger than most brands.
