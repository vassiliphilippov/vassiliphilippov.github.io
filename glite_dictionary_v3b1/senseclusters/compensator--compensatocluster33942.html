compensator

noun
payer of compensation
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmpənˌseɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkɒmpənˌseɪtə / (BrE)
a person or organization that pays compensation or reimburses for loss, damage, or injury

Examples

Insurance compensator
After the accident, the insurance company acted as the compensator for the injured driver.
The government became the compensator for citizens affected by the natural disaster.
If the product is faulty, the manufacturer may serve as the compensator and cover the costs.
