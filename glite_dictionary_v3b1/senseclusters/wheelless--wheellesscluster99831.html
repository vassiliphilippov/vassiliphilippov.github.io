wheelless

adjective
without wheels
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːl.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈwiːl.ləs / (BrE)
Without wheels; not having wheels or not equipped with wheeled vehicles.

Examples

Wheelless sleds
Some remote villages rely on wheelless sleds during winter.
The wheelless cart had to be carried instead of pulled.
The museum displayed a wheelless chariot from ancient times.
