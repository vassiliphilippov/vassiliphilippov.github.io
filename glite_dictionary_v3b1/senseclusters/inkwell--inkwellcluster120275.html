inkwell

noun
container for holding ink
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋkˌwɛl / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkwɛl / (BrE)
a small container used to hold ink, especially for dipping a pen into when writing by hand

Examples

A small inkwell
Artists sometimes use an inkwell when creating detailed drawings with a dip pen.
She carefully dipped her pen into the inkwell before starting the letter.
The antique inkwell on his desk was made of crystal and silver.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inkpot - a small container for ink used especially with dip pens
