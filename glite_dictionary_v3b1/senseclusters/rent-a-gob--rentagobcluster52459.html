rent-a-gob

noun
person who talks excessively or loudly
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛnt ə ɡɑb / (AmE), / ˈrɛnt ə ɡɒb / (BrE)
a person who talks excessively or loudly, often in a way that is humorous or annoying, especially in public or media.

Examples

A rent-a-gob person
At every party, there’s that one rent-a-gob who dominates the conversation.
He’s considered a bit of a rent-a-gob in political debates.
The TV show is always looking for a rent-a-gob willing to air controversial opinions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blabbermouth - a person who talks too much and reveals secrets
  • synonym: loudmouth - a person who talks a lot in a loud, offensive, or boastful way
  • antonym: shrinking violet - a very shy or quiet person
