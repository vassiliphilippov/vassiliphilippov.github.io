reproduce

verb
repeat something exactly from memory
Pronunciation: / ˌriːprəˈduːs / (AmE), / ˌriːprəˈdjuːs / (BrE)
to repeat or recite something exactly as learned or memorized

Examples

To reproduce a speech
During the test, he was able to reproduce entire pages of text from memory.
The student learned the speech so well that she could reproduce it word for word.
You must be able to reproduce these formulas on the exam without looking at your notes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recite - to repeat aloud from memory
  • synonym: regurgitate - to repeat information mechanically, especially from memory
