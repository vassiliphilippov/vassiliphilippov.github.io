shopper

noun
person who shops
1.0 8.94/m
Pronunciation: / Àà É…ëÀêp…ôr / (AmE), / Àà É…íp…ô / (BrE)
a person who visits stores to look for and buy goods

Examples

A regular shopper
As a regular shopper, she always knows where to find the best prices.
Many shoppers prefer to compare products online before making a purchase in store.
The mall was crowded with weekend shoppers looking for good deals.

Synonyms

  • buyer
    1.0 28/m
    a buyer often focuses on completing a purchase, while a shopper visits stores to browse and sometimes buy.
  • purchaser
    2.8 3.38/m
    a purchaser is a more formal buyer used for property or large purchases, while a shopper visits stores to browse and buy everyday goods.
