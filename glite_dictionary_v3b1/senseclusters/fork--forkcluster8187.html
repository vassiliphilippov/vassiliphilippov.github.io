fork

noun
a pronged eating or serving utensil
1.4
Pronunciation: / fɔrk / (AmE), / fɔːk / (BrE)
a utensil with two or more pointed parts (prongs) used for picking up, serving, or eating food

Examples

A dinner fork
He accidentally dropped his fork on the floor during dinner.
Please use a fork to serve the vegetables.
She picked up her salad with a fork and began to eat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knife - a utensil with a cutting edge, used for slicing or spreading
  • synonym: spoon - a utensil with a shallow bowl for scooping or stirring food
