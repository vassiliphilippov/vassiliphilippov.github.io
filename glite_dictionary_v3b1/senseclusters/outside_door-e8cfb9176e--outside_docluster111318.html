outside door

noun
door leading outside a building
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtsaɪd dɔr / (AmE), / ˈaʊtsaɪd dɔː / (BrE)
a door that provides entry to or exit from the exterior of a building

Examples

The outside door
Rain was coming in under the outside door during the storm.
She forgot to lock the outside door when she left the house.
The movers struggled to fit the couch through the narrow outside door.

Related Terms

  • synonym: back door - a door at the rear of a building
  • synonym: front door - the main door at the front of a house or other building
  • antonym: interior door - a door inside a building, not leading to the outside
