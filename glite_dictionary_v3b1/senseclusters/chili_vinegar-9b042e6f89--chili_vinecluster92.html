chili vinegar

noun
vinegar infused with chili peppers
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪli vɪˈnɛɡər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪli vɪˈnɪɡə / (BrE)
Vinegar that has been flavored with chili peppers, giving it a hot and spicy taste.

Examples

Chili vinegar bottle
Bottles of chili vinegar lined the shelves in the Asian market.

The restaurant served homemade chili vinegar as a condiment with the dumplings.
