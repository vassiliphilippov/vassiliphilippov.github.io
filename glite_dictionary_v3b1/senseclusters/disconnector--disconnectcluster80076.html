disconnector

noun
electrical circuit breaker
Pronunciation: / dɪs.kəˈnɛktər / (AmE), / dɪs.kəˈnɛktə / (BrE)
a device used to break a connection, especially to stop the flow of electricity or power for safety or maintenance

Examples

A disconnector is essential for isolating electrical equipment during maintenance work.
The safety manual recommends checking the disconnector before starting any electrical installation.

After the heated discussion, he became a disconnector in the group, avoiding further contact.
