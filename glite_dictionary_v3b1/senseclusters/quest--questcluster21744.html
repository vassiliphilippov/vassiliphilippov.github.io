quest

noun
task or mission in a video game
3.9 3.26/m
Pronunciation: / kwÉ›st / (AmE), / kwÉ›st / (BrE)
(in video games and interactive media) a task, mission, or objective given to a player that must be completed to progress the story, gain experience, or receive rewards.

Examples

A game quest
Many RPGs assign a quest to players to advance the storyline and earn rewards.
She completed the side quests before returning to the main mission to level up.
The tutorial teaches new players how to accept and finish a quest in the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mission - a specific assigned task or operation in a game or other context
  • synonym: objective - a goal or target that a player is meant to achieve
  • synonym: task - a piece of work to be done, often as part of a larger goal
