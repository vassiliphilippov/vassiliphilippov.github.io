waste

adjective
desolate or barren place (adj)
Pronunciation: / weɪst / (AmE), / weɪst / (BrE)
(of a place) desolate, barren, or in a remote area, often empty or uninhabited.

Examples

Waste ground
Ancient ruins were found in a waste stretch at the edge of the forest.
The explorers struggled across the waste land searching for water.
The novel describes a waste plateau, empty and windswept, far from the nearest village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barren - (of land) too poor to produce much or any vegetation
  • synonym: desolate - uninhabited and bleak
