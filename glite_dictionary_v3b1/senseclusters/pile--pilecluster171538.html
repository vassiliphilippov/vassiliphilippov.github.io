pile

noun
large sum of money (informal)
Pronunciation: / paɪl / (AmE), / paɪl / (BrE)
an informal term for a large sum of money

Examples

A pile of cash
After selling his business, Tom walked away with a nice pile.
He made a pile of money investing in tech stocks.
It must take a pile to live in that neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fortune - a large amount of wealth or money
  • antonym: pittance - a very small or inadequate amount of money
