solute

noun
substance dissolved in a solution
6.8 0.16/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…ël.jut / (AmE), / Ààs…íl.juÀêt / (BrE)
A substance that is dissolved in a solvent to form a solution.

Examples

A solute in water
Sugar is a common solute found in many beverages.
The concentration of the solute determines the strength of the solution.
When salt is added to water, the salt acts as the solute while the water is the solvent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: solution - a homogeneous mixture of a solute dissolved in a solvent
  • antonym: solvent - the liquid in which a solute is dissolved to form a solution
