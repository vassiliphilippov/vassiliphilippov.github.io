weigh on someone's conscience

verb
cause someone to feel guilty or troubled
Pronunciation: / weɪ ɑn ˈsʌmwʌnz kɑnʃəns / (AmE), / weɪ ɒn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈkɒnʃəns / (BrE)
to make someone feel guilty or troubled over something they have done or failed to do
