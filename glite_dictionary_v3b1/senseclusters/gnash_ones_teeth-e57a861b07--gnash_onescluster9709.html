gnash one's teeth

verb
grind teeth in anger or frustration
6.8
Pronunciation: / næʃ wʌnz tiθ / (AmE), / næʃ wʌnz tiːθ / (BrE)
to grind or clench one's teeth together, typically as a sign of anger or frustration

Examples

To gnash his teeth
After hearing the unfair verdict, he began to gnash his teeth in anger.
She lay awake at night gnashing her teeth over her missed opportunity.
Some fans will gnash their teeth when their favorite team loses the championship.

Related Terms
