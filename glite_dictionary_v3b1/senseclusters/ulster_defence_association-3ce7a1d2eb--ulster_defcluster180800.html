Ulster Defence Association

proper noun
Protestant loyalist paramilitary group in Northern Ireland
Pronunciation: / ˈʌlstər dɪˈfɛns ˌæsəˈsieɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈʌlstə dɪˈfɛns əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a major Protestant loyalist paramilitary organization in Northern Ireland, active during the Troubles, known for attacks on Catholic civilians and involvement in criminal activity
