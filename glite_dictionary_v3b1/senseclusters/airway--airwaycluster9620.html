Examples

Flight airway

The new airway reduces congestion between major airports.

Synonyms

  • skyway
    6.7 0.03/m
    A skyway is a designated route used by airplanes between airports, while an airway is an official route planes follow in controlled airspace.
  • air lane
    4.3 0.01/m
    An air lane is a marked route between airports for regular flights, while an airway is a designated route planes use in the sky.
  • flight path
    3.9 0.73/m
    A flight path is the path an aircraft actually follows during a journey, while an airway is a fixed route planes use between airports.
