anyhoo

adverb
informal anyway
9.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…õniÀåhuÀê / (AmE), / Àà…õniÀåhuÀê / (BrE)
used informally to mean 'anyway', especially to change the subject or continue a conversation.

Examples

But anyhoo
I forgot to call her back, but anyhoo, let's talk about your weekend plans.
The movie was a bit disappointing. Anyhoo, what do you want for dinner?
We missed the bus. Anyhoo, we can always walk to the park instead.

Synonyms

  • anyhow
    2.1 2.72/m
