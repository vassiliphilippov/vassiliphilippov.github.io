in a bad light

adverb
appearing unfavorable or negative
Pronunciation: / ɪn ə bæd laɪt / (AmE), / ɪn ə bæd laɪt / (BrE)
in a way that makes someone or something appear unfavorable or negative

Examples

In a bad light among people
His comments put the entire project in a bad light among investors.
She felt the documentary showed her in a bad light and complained to the producers.
The report presented the company in a bad light, damaging its reputation.
