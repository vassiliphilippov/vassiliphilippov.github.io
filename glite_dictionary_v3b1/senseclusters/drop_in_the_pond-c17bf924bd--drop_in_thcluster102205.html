drop in the pond

noun
an insignificant amount compared to the whole
8.2
Pronunciation: / drɑp ɪn ðə pɑnd / (AmE), / drɒp ɪn ðə pɒnd / (BrE)
a very small or insignificant amount when compared to a much larger amount or need

Examples

A drop in the pond of water
Offering one solution is merely a drop in the pond when the problems are so complex.
The extra resources turned out to be a drop in the pond during the economic crisis.
Their donation was just a drop in the pond given the scale of the disaster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drop in the ocean - an amount too small to make a significant difference
