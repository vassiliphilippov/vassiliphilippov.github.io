banana peel

noun
skin of a banana, often discarded
1.0 1.1
Pronunciation: / bəˈnænə pil / (AmE), / bəˈnɑːnə piːl / (BrE)
the skin of a banana, especially known for being slippery and often used in jokes about slipping and falling
