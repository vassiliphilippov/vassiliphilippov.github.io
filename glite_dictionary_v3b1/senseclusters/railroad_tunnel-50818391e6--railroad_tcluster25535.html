railroad tunnel

noun
tunnel for trains to pass through
4.5 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈreɪl.roʊd ˈtʌn.əl/ /; / /ˈreɪ.roʊd ˈtʌn.əl/ (AmE), /ˈreɪl.rəʊd ˈtʌn.əl/ (BrE)
