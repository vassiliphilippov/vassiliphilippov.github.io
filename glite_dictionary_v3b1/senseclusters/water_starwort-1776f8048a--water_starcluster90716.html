water starwort

noun
star-shaped aquatic plant genus
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ˈstɑrwɝt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈstɑːwɜːt / (BrE)
Any of several aquatic plants in the genus Callitriche, typically with star-shaped rosettes of floating leaves, found in America, Europe, and Asia.
