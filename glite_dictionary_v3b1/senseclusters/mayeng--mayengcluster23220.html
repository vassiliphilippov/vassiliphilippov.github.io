mayeng

noun
Indian tree with fragrant flowers and reddish wood
Pronunciation: / maɪˈɛŋ / (AmE), / maɪˈeŋ / (BrE)
An Indian tree known for its fragrant nocturnal white flowers and reddish wood used for planking, often grown as an ornamental or shade tree.

Examples

The mayeng tree
Carpenters value the reddish wood from the mayeng for making durable planks.
Many gardens feature a mayeng as an ornamental centerpiece due to its attractive flowers.
The mayeng is prized for the enchanting scent of its nightly blossoms.
