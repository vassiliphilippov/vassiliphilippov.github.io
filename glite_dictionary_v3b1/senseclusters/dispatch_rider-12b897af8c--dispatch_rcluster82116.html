dispatch rider

noun
military messenger (often on motorcycle)
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspætʃ ˌraɪdər / (AmE), / dɪˈspætʃ ˌraɪdə / (BrE)
a messenger who carries official military messages, especially by motorcycle

Examples

A dispatch rider on motorcycle
A dispatch rider must navigate dangerous territories to ensure the message arrives quickly.
During the war, the dispatch rider delivered urgent orders to the front lines.
He volunteered as a dispatch rider and was assigned a fast motorcycle for his missions.

  • synonym: messenger - a person who carries a message or does deliveries
