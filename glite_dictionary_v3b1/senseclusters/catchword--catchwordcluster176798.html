catchword

noun
slogan or characteristic phrase
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkætʃˌwɝd / (AmE), / ˈkatʃˌwɜːd / (BrE)
a word or phrase, often used as a slogan, that is characteristic of or favored by a particular group, organization, or individual

Examples

Popular catchword
'Innovation' has become a popular catchword in the tech industry.
The politician repeated the same catchword throughout his campaign to appeal to voters.
Without substance, a catchword can quickly lose its persuasive power.

Related Terms

  • synonym: motto - a short sentence or phrase expressing a guiding principle
  • synonym: slogan - a memorable phrase used in advertising or promotion
