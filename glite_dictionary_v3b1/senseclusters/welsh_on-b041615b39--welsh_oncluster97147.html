welsh on

verb
fail to fulfill a promise or bet
Pronunciation: / wɛlʃ ɑn / (AmE), / wɛlʃ ɒn / (BrE)
to fail to honor or fulfill a promise, obligation, or bet, especially by not paying money owed

Examples

To welsh on a bet
He promised to help with the move but tried to welsh on it later.
If you welsh on a bet, you may lose your friends' trust.
She hates when people welsh on their commitments, especially at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: renege - to go back on a promise, agreement, or contract
