tropical clime

noun
region with a tropical climate
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑːpɪkəl klaɪm / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪkəl klaɪm / (BrE)
A region or area characterized by a warm, tropical climate.

Examples

A warm tropical clime
Many birds migrate to a tropical clime during the winter months.
She dreamed of retiring to a tropical clime with palm trees and sandy beaches.
The humidity in a tropical clime can be difficult for those used to cooler weather.

Related Terms

  • antonym: temperate climate - A climate with moderate temperatures, not extreme like tropical or polar
  • synonym: tropical climate - A climate typical of the tropics; hot and humid year-round
