Heavenly Jewel

noun
first deity of the Taoist Trinity
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvənli ˈdʒuːəl / (AmE), / ˈhɛv(ə)nli ˈdʒuːəl / (BrE)
In Taoism, the first of the three supreme deities known as the Taoist Trinity.

Examples

The Heavenly Jewel deity
Many Taoists honor the Heavenly Jewel as the source of universal order.
Rituals sometimes begin by invoking the Heavenly Jewel for blessings.
The Heavenly Jewel is often depicted at the top of Taoist shrines.
