roosterfish

noun
Pacific game fish
Pronunciation: / ˈruːstərˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈruːstəˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a large game fish of the eastern Pacific Ocean, recognized by its long, comb-like dorsal fin

Examples

The roosterfish fish
The roosterfish is prized by anglers for its strength and unique appearance.
We spotted a large roosterfish swimming near the surface of the Pacific waters.
Catching a roosterfish was the highlight of their fishing trip to Costa Rica.

Related Terms

  • synonym: game fish - a fish caught for sport rather than food
