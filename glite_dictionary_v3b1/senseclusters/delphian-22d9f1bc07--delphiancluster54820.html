Delphian

adjective
relating to Delphi or its oracles
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛlfiən / (AmE), / ˈdɛlfiən / (BrE)
of or relating to Delphi, especially to the ancient Greek sanctuary and oracles of Apollo.

Examples

The Delphian oracle
Scholars have long debated the meaning of the ancient Delphian prophecies.
The Delphian shrine was visited by thousands seeking guidance.
The play draws on Delphian legends to create an atmosphere of mystery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Delphic - relating to Delphi; often enigmatic or ambiguous, as the Delphic oracle
