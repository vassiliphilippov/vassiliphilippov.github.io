Spiranthes romanzoffiana

noun
a species of spiraling white orchid
9.9
Pronunciation: / spaɪˈrænθiːz roʊˌmænzəˈfɪənə / (AmE), / spaɪˈranθiːz rɒˌmanzəˈfɪənə / (BrE)
A species of orchid native to western North America, characterized by dense clusters of creamy white flowers that spiral gently around the stem, with two upper petals forming a hood.
