aspirational

adjective
caused by inhalation
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæspəˈreɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌæspɪˈreɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by the inhalation of food, liquid, or other material into the lungs, especially in a medical context

Examples

Aspirational pneumonia
The patient was diagnosed with aspirational pneumonia after accidentally inhaling food.
Doctors monitor elderly patients for signs of aspirational complications following swallowing difficulties.
A feeding tube may be used to prevent aspirational risks in certain medical cases.
