blossom

verb
develop or flourish successfully
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑːsəm / (AmE), / ˈblɒsəm / (BrE)

Examples

Confidence blossoms
After joining the art club, her confidence began to blossom.
Friendship often blossoms when people work together toward a common goal.
The small startup quickly blossomed into a successful business.
