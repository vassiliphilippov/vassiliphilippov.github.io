jole

noun
cheek or jaw
Pronunciation: / dʒoʊl / (AmE), / dʒəʊl / (BrE)
an archaic word for the cheek or jaw, especially the lower part of the face

Examples

A blow to the jole
The knight received a blow upon his jole during the duel.
In old poems, a rosy jole was a sign of good health.
He stroked his long beard that covered his jole as he pondered the question.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheek - the soft part of the face below the eye and between the nose and ear
  • synonym: jaw - the lower part of the face that moves when you open your mouth
