annoy

verb
to irritate or bother someone slightly
Pronunciation: / əˈnɔɪ / (AmE), / əˈnɔɪ / (BrE)
to make someone slightly angry or irritated, especially repeatedly or by minor actions

Examples

To annoy someone
Her loud chewing can really annoy me during dinner.
It annoys him when his coworkers arrive late to meetings.
The constant noise from the street was annoying the entire family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bother - to disturb or worry someone
  • synonym: irritate - to make someone slightly angry or impatient
  • antonym: please - to give pleasure or satisfaction to someone
