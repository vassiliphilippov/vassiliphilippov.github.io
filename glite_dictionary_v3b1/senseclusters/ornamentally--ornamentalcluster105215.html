ornamentally

adverb
decoratively, not functionally
7.1 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / Àå…îrn…ôÀàm…õnt…ôli / (AmE), / Àå…îÀên…ôÀàm…õnt…ôli / (BrE)
In a way that serves as decoration and not for practical function; in an ornamental manner.

Examples

To ornamentally decorate
The gates were ornamentally forged with intricate floral patterns.
The windows, though beautiful, were ornamentally designed and did not open.
Vases were placed ornamentally along the staircase to enhance its beauty.

Synonyms

  • decoratively
    3.4 0.08/m
    decoratively is used to make something look attractive or pleasing, while ornamentally means serving mainly as decoration rather than function.
  • ornately
    6.0 0.34/m
    ornately describes being highly or elaborately decorated, while ornamentally means simply serving as decoration rather than having practical use.
