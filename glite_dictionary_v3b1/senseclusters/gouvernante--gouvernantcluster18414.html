gouvernante

noun
female house manager
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈguː.vər.nɑːnt/ /; / /ˈɡʌv.ər.nænt/ (AmE), /ˈguː.və.nɑːnt/ /; / /ˈɡʌv.ə.nænt/ (BrE)
a woman employed to manage domestic staff and supervise housekeeping in a large house, hotel, or similar establishment

Household gouvernante

The gouvernante managed the schedules of the maids and reported directly to the owner.
