second sight

noun
supposed ability to perceive things beyond normal senses
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛkənd ˈsaɪt / (AmE), / ˌsɛkənd ˈsaɪt / (BrE)
the supposed ability to perceive events or things not present to the ordinary senses, especially foreseeing future events; clairvoyance

Examples

Second sight ability
Many legends from Scotland mention people with second sight who predicted disasters.
Skeptics dismissed his talk of second sight as nothing more than superstition.
The old woman claimed to have second sight and warned us of coming trouble.
