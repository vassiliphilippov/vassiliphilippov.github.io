dash

verb
move quickly or rush
Pronunciation: / dæʃ / (AmE), / dæʃ / (BrE)
to run or move quickly or suddenly; to rush

Examples

To dash out quickly
After the meeting, everyone dashed to the cafeteria for lunch.
She dashed across the street to catch the bus.
The cat dashed out of the room when it heard a noise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hurry - to move or act with haste
  • synonym: rush - to move with urgent haste
  • antonym: saunter - to walk in a slow, relaxed manner
