wakening

noun
the act of waking from sleep
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪkənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪkənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of waking or becoming awake from sleep

Examples

The wakening from sleep

She dreaded the sudden wakening that came with her alarm each morning.
The gentle wakening of the forest was accompanied by birdsong.
