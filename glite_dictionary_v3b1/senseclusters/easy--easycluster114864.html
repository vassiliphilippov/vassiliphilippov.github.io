easy

adverb
informal adverb meaning 'easily'
Pronunciation: / ˈiːzi / (AmE), / ˈiːzi / (BrE)
in a gentle, relaxed, or easy way; without difficulty or with little effort

Examples

He finished easy
He passed the test easy, without breaking a sweat.
She gets bored easy when there’s nothing to do.
This old truck breaks down easy in cold weather.
He drove easy along the icy road to avoid an accident.
Please go easy on the corners—it's very slippery today.
The doctor told her to take it easy after the surgery.
After his surgery, the doctor told him to take it easy for a few weeks.
Just go easy with the spices, or the dish will be too strong.
The coach went easy on the new players during their first practice.
