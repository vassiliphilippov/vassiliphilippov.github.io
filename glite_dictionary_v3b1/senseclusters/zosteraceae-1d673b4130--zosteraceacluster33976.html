Zosteraceae

noun
family of marine flowering plants (eelgrass family)
Pronunciation: / zoʊˌstɛr.əˈsi.aɪ / (AmE), / zɒˌstɛr.əˈsiː.iː / (BrE)
A family of marine flowering plants, also known as the eelgrass family, sometimes considered equivalent to Potamogetonaceae in some classifications.

Examples
