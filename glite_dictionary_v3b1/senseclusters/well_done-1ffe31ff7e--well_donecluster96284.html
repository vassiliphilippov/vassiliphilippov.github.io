well done

adjective
done to a high standard or cooked thoroughly
Pronunciation: / wɛl dʌn / (AmE), / wɛl dʌn / (BrE)
Done to a high standard; (of meat) cooked thoroughly so that there is no pinkness left.

Examples

Well done steak
He prefers his burgers well done, with no trace of pink inside.
Her steak was cooked well done, just as she requested.
Your report is well done—I can see you put in a lot of effort.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: medium - (of meat) cooked so the inside is pink but not red
  • antonym: rare - (of meat) lightly cooked so the inside is still red or pink
