extortion

noun
the act of obtaining something by force or misuse of authority
6.9 5.9
Pronunciation: /ek.ˈstɔːr.ʃən/ /; / /ɪk.ˈstɔːr.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈstɔː.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or crime of getting something, especially money, from someone by using threats, force, or misuse of authority
