osteochondritis

noun
bone and cartilage inflammation
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstioʊˌkɑnˈdraɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌɒstiəʊkɒnˈdraɪtɪs / (BrE)
a medical condition characterized by inflammation of bone and cartilage, often causing pain and swelling in joints

Examples

Osteochondritis in knee
The young athlete was sidelined due to osteochondritis in his knee joint.
Early treatment of osteochondritis can help prevent long-term joint damage.
Doctors often use imaging tests to diagnose osteochondritis in patients with persistent joint pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arthritis - inflammation of the joints
  • synonym: osteomyelitis - inflammation of bone caused by infection
