rigger brush

noun
slender artist's brush for fine lines
9.0 7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪg.ər brʌʃ/ (AmE), /ˈrɪg.ə brʌʃ/ (BrE)
a long, slender, pointed artist's brush, typically made of sable, used for fine lines and detail work, especially in painting rigging on ships

Examples

A rigger brush for painting
For painting long, thin branches, a rigger brush is the perfect tool.
She carefully dipped her rigger brush into the ink before sketching fine details.
The artist used a rigger brush to create the delicate lines of the ship's rigging.
