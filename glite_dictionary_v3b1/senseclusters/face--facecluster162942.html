face

noun
contorted facial expression
Pronunciation: / feɪs / (AmE), / feɪs / (BrE)
the expression or look on someone's face that shows their feelings

Examples

Funny face
He made a funny face when he tasted the sour lemon.
Please don’t make that face; it’s impolite.
She pulled a face behind the teacher’s back to make her friends laugh.
He wore a serious face throughout the meeting.
Her face lit up when she received the good news.
You could see the disappointment on his face after the loss.
