compliantly

adverb
following rules
4.6
Pronunciation: / kəmˈplaɪəntli / (AmE), / kəmˈplaɪəntli / (BrE)
in a way that follows rules, laws, or instructions; obediently or according to requirements

Examples

To act compliantly
The company now operates compliantly with all industry safety standards.
She compliantly followed the teacher's instructions during the experiment.
All employees acted compliantly when the new regulations were introduced.
The students listened compliantly as the teacher explained the new rules.
He compliantly handed over his documents when asked by the officer.
She compliantly followed the doctor's advice to rest and drink plenty of water.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defiantly - in a way that shows resistance to rules or authority
  • synonym: obediently - in a way that shows willingness to follow orders or rules
