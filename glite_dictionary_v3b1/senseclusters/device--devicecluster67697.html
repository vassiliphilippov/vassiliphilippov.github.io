device

noun
clever or deceptive plan or trick
5.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
a plan, method, or trick intended to achieve a particular result, especially one that is clever or deceptive

Examples

Clever device
He would stoop to any device to win a point in a debate.
The lawyer's device for delaying the case was ultimately discovered by the judge.
Using humor as a device to diffuse tension often works in meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gimmick - a trick or device intended to attract attention or increase appeal
  • synonym: stratagem - a plan or scheme, especially one used to outwit an opponent
