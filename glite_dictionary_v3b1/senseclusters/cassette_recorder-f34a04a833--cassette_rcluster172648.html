cassette recorder

noun
device that records or plays cassettes
1.5
Pronunciation: / kəˈsɛt rɪˌkɔrdər / (AmE), / kəˈsɛt rɪˌkɔːdə / (BrE)
a device for recording and playing audio cassettes

Examples

Old cassette recorder
He found an old cassette recorder in the attic while cleaning.
She used a cassette recorder to tape her interviews for the magazine.
The teacher played the language lesson using a cassette recorder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cassette player - device for playing audio cassettes (not recording)
  • synonym: tape recorder - device for recording sound on magnetic tape
