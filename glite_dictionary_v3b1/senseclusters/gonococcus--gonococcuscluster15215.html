gonococcus

noun
bacterium causing gonorrhea
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɡoʊ nəˈkɑk əs / (AmE), / ˌɡɒ nəˈkɒk əs / (BrE)
a bacterium (Neisseria gonorrhoeae) that causes gonorrhea, characterized by the production of pus

Examples
