epistemic

adjective
relating to knowledge or epistemology
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈstɛmɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈstiːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to knowledge or to the theory of knowledge (epistemology)

Examples

In linguistics, an epistemic modal expresses the speaker’s degree of certainty about a statement.
The philosopher discussed various epistemic approaches to understanding reality.
