Oncorhynchus nerka

noun
a species of red-fleshed Pacific salmon
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɑːn.kə.ˈrɪŋ.kəs ˈnɜːr.kə/ (AmE), /ɒn.kə.ˈrɪŋ.kəs ˈnɜː.kə/ (BrE)
