FPD

noun
abbreviation for flat panel display
5.8
Pronunciation: / ɛf pi di / (AmE), / ɛf piː diː / (BrE)
An abbreviation for 'flat panel display,' a type of video display that is thin and flat, widely used in devices such as laptop computers and modern televisions.

Examples

FPD technology

Many laptop manufacturers now equip their devices with FPDs for slimmer designs.
The FPD delivers higher image quality compared to older CRT screens.
