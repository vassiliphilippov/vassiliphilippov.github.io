know-it-all

noun
person who acts as if they know everything
2.7 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈnoʊ.ɪt.ɔːl/ /; / /ˈnoʊ.ət.ɔːl/ (AmE), /ˈnəʊ.ɪt.ɔːl/ /; / /ˈnəʊ.ət.ɔːl/ (BrE)
a person who acts as if they know everything and often annoys others by not listening or by sharing their opinions too much
