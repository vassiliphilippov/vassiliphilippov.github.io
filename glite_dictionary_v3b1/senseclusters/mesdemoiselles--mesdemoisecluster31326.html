mesdemoiselles

noun
young ladies (French plural)
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɛdˌmwɑːˈzɛlz / (AmE), / mɛdˌmwɑːˈzɛlz / (BrE)
the French plural form of 'mademoiselle', used to refer to young ladies or misses

Examples

The mesdemoiselles invited
The invitation was addressed to the mesdemoiselles of the household.
At the party, several mesdemoiselles wore elegant dresses and greeted the guests.
The teacher called on the mesdemoiselles to present their project to the class.
