Gerardia pedicularia

noun
North American annual with yellow foxglove-like flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈreɹdiə pɛdɪˌkjulˈɛəriə / (AmE), / dʒəˈrɑːdɪə pɛdɪˌkjʊlˈɛːrɪə / (BrE)
A multi-stemmed annual plant native to North America with solitary, dark golden-yellow flowers resembling those of the foxglove; sometimes classified in the genus Gerardia.
