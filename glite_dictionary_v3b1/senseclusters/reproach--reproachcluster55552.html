reproach

verb
express criticism or disappointment
Pronunciation: / rɪˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / rɪˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
to express criticism or disappointment towards someone, especially for their actions or behavior

Examples

To reproach a friend
Instead of helping, he just reproached me for making mistakes.
She reproached him gently for forgetting her birthday.
The manager reproached the team for missing the deadline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criticize - to express disapproval or point out faults
  • antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration
