organology

noun
study of organs
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrɡəˈnɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡəˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of organs, especially their structure and function in living organisms

Examples

Organology of the body

The textbook on organology included detailed diagrams of the heart, lungs, and liver.
Advances in organology have led to improved treatments for organ failure.
