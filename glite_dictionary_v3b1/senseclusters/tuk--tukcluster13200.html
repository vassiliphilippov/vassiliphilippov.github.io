tuk

verb
to tap lightly
to make a light, sharp knock or tap on something, often producing a brief sound

Examples

To tuk the table
He used a pencil to tuk gently on the desk to get her attention.
Please don't tuk the glass like that during the concert; it will break.
The child liked to tuk on the window to watch the raindrops move.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knock - to hit something, especially a door, to get attention
  • synonym: tap - to strike lightly and quickly
