ballistocardiogram

noun
graphical record of heart-induced body movements
Pronunciation: / bəˌlɪstəˈkɑrdioʊˌɡræm / (AmE), / bəˌlɪstəˈkɑːdiəˌɡræm / (BrE)
a graphical record of the movements of the body generated by the heartbeat, made using a ballistocardiograph

Examples

A ballistocardiogram record
A technician operated the ballistocardiograph to produce a clear ballistocardiogram.
Abnormalities in the ballistocardiogram suggested an underlying heart problem.
The doctor reviewed the patient's ballistocardiogram to assess his cardiac health.
