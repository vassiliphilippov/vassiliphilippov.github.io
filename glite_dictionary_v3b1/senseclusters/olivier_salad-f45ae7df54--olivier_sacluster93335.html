Olivier salad

noun
Russian potato salad
Pronunciation: / oʊˈlɪvi.eɪ ˈsæləd / (AmE), / ɒˈlɪvi.eɪ ˈsæləd / (BrE)
a Russian salad made with potatoes, vegetables, eggs, and mayonnaise, often served at celebrations

Examples

Traditional Olivier salad
At the family gathering, everyone enjoyed the homemade Olivier salad with their meal.
She learned to prepare Olivier salad from her grandmother's traditional recipe.
During New Year's Eve in Russia, it is common to serve Olivier salad as a festive dish.
