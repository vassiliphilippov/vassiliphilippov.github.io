box calf

noun
smooth black calfskin leather
Pronunciation: / bɑks kælf / (AmE), / bɒks kɑːf / (BrE)
A fine, smooth, black calfskin leather tanned with chromium salts, often used in high-quality shoes and leather goods.

Examples

A box calf handbag
Her favorite handbag was made of luxurious box calf, giving it a stylish sheen.

Traditional men's dress shoes are often crafted from polished box calf.
