nonscholarly

adjective
not academic
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈskɑːlərli / (AmE), / nɒnˈskɒləli / (BrE)
not academic or not related to formal study, research, or scholarship

Examples

Nonscholarly articles
The magazine publishes nonscholarly articles that are easy for the general public to understand.
He preferred reading nonscholarly books about history rather than academic journals.
Her presentation was intentionally nonscholarly to make the topic accessible to everyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: academic - relating to education, scholarship, or formal study
  • synonym: informal - not formal or official; casual
