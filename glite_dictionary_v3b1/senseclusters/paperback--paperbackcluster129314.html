paperback

adjective
having a flexible paper cover (of books)
9.1 1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪ.pɚˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ.pə.bæk / (BrE)
(of books) having a flexible cover made of thick paper or thin cardboard, not a hard cover.

Examples

Paperback edition
Many students buy used paperback textbooks to save money.
She prefers paperback editions because they are lighter to carry.
The novel is available in both paperback and hardback formats.
