Synonyms

  • exasperation
    5.7 2.36/m
    exasperation is a strong, impatient anger caused by repeated problems, while frustration is upset from failing to reach a goal.
  • vexation
    7.4 0.26/m

  • annoyance
    2.8 3.33/m
