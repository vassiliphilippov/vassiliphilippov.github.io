dashpot

noun
motion-damping device
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdæʃˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈdæʃˌpɒt / (BrE)
a mechanical device that resists motion by using viscous friction, commonly used to control or damp movement

Examples

A dashpot device
The engineer installed a dashpot to prevent the machine from vibrating excessively.
A dashpot helps the door close smoothly and quietly without slamming.
Many industrial machines use a dashpot to control the speed of moving parts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: damper - a device that reduces or controls movement or vibration
  • synonym: shock absorber - a device that absorbs and dampens shock impulses
