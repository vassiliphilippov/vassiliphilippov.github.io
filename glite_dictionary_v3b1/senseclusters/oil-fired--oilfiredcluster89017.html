oil-fired

adjective
powered by burning oil
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɪl ˌfaɪɚd / (AmE), / ˈɔɪl ˌfaɪəd / (BrE)
(of equipment or systems) powered by burning oil as fuel.

Examples

Oil-fired heating system
Many rural homes still use oil-fired heating systems.
The factory upgraded from a coal-fired to an oil-fired furnace.
The house is heated by an oil-fired boiler.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gas-fired - powered by burning gas as fuel
