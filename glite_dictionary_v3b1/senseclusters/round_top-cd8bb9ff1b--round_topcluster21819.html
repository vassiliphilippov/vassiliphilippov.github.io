round top

noun
large circus tent
Pronunciation: / raʊnd tɑp / (AmE), / raʊnd tɒp / (BrE)
A large canvas tent used to house the audience at a circus performance.

Examples

A round top tent
A sudden storm threatened to tear holes in the round top during the matinee.
Each summer, the traveling circus set up its round top in the center of town.
The performers gathered outside the round top before the evening show began.
