Synonyms

  • humpbacked
    6.5 0.11/m
    humpbacked describes a clear, visible hump on the upper back, while hunchbacked can mean a similar humped or stooped posture.
  • humped
    7.5 0.00/m
    humped means having a single raised area on the back, while hunchbacked suggests a more obvious hump or bent posture.
  • kyphotic
    9.9 0.02/m
    kyphotic is a medical term for an excessive spine curve, while hunchbacked is the common way to describe that curved back.
  • stooped
    4.7 0.00/m
    stooped describes a person bending forward at the shoulders, while hunchbacked often implies a fixed spine curve or hump.
  • round-backed
    5.9 0.01/m
    round-backed means the back and shoulders are smoothly rounded, while hunchbacked often refers to a sharper hump or medical curve.
