thirst

noun
feeling of needing to drink
Pronunciation: / θɝst / (AmE), / θɜːst / (BrE)
the feeling of needing or wanting to drink something; a physiological need for liquid

Examples

Unquenchable thirst
After running in the sun, her thirst became unbearable.
During the drought, people suffered a constant thirst for clean water.
He drank a glass of water to quench his thirst.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hunger - a physiological need for food
