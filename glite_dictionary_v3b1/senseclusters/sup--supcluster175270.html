sup

interjection
casual greeting
Pronunciation: / sʌp / (AmE), / sʌp / (BrE)
an informal greeting used to ask how someone is or what they are doing

Examples

Sup? greeting
He walked into the room and said, "Sup?" to his friends.
When I called her, the first thing she said was "Sup?"
You can just text "Sup?" if you want to start a conversation.
