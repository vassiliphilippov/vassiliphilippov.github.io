genus Pandanus

noun
type genus of screw pines
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs pænˈdeɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs panˈdanəs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Pandanaceae, comprising tropical plants known as screw pines.
