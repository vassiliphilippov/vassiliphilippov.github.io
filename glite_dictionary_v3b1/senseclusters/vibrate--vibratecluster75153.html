Synonyms

  • quiver
    4.5 2/m
    quiver is a slight, quick shaking of the voice or skin, while vibrate means small fast back-and-forth movements.
  • tremble
    2.5 8/m
    tremble is an involuntary shaking from fear, cold, or excitement, while vibrate means small rapid movements not tied to emotion.
  • shake
    1.0 40/m
    shake is a broad, often stronger movement of things or people, while vibrate means small rapid back-and-forth motion.
  • oscillate
    6.5 0.4/m
    oscillate means to move back and forth in a regular rhythm, while vibrate means small, quick shaking that may be less regular.
