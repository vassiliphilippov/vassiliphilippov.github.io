fraternalism

noun
brotherhood system
Pronunciation: / frəˈtɜrnəlɪzəm / (AmE), / frəˈtɜːnəlɪzəm / (BrE)
a system or practice of brotherhood and mutual support among members of a group or organization

Examples

Fraternalism and support
The organization was founded on the principles of fraternalism and mutual aid.
Many labor unions emphasize fraternalism to strengthen the bonds between their members.
Through regular gatherings, the club fostered a sense of fraternalism among its participants.

Related Terms

  • antonym: individualism - the belief in the importance of individual independence and self-reliance
  • synonym: solidarity - unity or agreement among individuals with a common interest
