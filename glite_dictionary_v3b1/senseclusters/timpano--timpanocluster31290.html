timpano

noun
Italian baked dish
9.8
Pronunciation: / tɪmˈpɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / tɪmˈpɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
an Italian baked dish consisting of pasta, rice, or other fillings encased in a pastry shell

Examples

Italian timpano dish

For the festival, the chef baked a large timpano that impressed all the guests.
She learned to make timpano from her grandmother's traditional Italian recipe.
