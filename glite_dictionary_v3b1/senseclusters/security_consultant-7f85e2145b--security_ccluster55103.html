security consultant

noun
cybersecurity adviser
3.5
Pronunciation: /sɪ.ˈkjʊr.ɪ.tiː kən.ˈsʌl.tənt/ /; / /sə.ˈkjʊr.ɪ.tiː kən.ˈsʌl.tənt/ (AmE), /sɪ.ˈkjʊə.rɪ.tiː kən.ˈsʌl.tənt/ /; / /sə.ˈkjʊə.rɪ.tiː kən.ˈsʌl.tənt/ (BrE)
a professional who advises organizations on cybersecurity and information-security measures, including risk assessment, vulnerability testing, incident response, and digital defences.

Examples

Security consultant for computers
The company hired a security consultant to strengthen its cybersecurity defenses.
Several startups contract security consultants to run penetration tests before launching new web services.
As a security consultant, she advised on incident response plans after the ransomware breach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cybersecurity consultant - a specialist who provides advice and services to protect computer systems and networks from digital threats
  • synonym: information security analyst - a professional who monitors and protects an organization's information systems and data
