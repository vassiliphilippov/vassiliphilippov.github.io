pound the table

verb
demand forcefully by hitting a table
Pronunciation: / paʊnd ðə ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / paʊnd ðə ˈteɪbl̩ / (BrE)
to demand attention or action forcefully, often by striking a table with one's hand

Examples

To pound the table firmly
During the heated discussion, she had to pound the table to make her point.
He began to pound the table in frustration when nobody listened to his proposal.
Sometimes you need to pound the table to get people's attention in a noisy meeting.
