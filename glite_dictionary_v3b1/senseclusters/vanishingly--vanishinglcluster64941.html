vanishingly

adverb
to an almost nonexistent degree
Pronunciation: / ˈvæn.ɪ.ʃɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈvæn.ɪ.ʃɪŋ.li / (BrE)
to a degree that is almost nonexistent or extremely small; almost to the point of disappearing or reaching zero.

Examples

A vanishingly small amount
Cases of this disease are now vanishingly rare in developed countries.
The differences between the two products are vanishingly slight.
The probability of such an event occurring is vanishingly small.
