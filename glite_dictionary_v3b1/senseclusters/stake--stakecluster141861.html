stake

noun
money risked in gambling or investing
3.0
Pronunciation: / steɪk / (AmE), / steɪk / (BrE)
an amount of money risked on a bet, game, or investment

Examples

A stake in poker
He placed a stake of $100 on the final race.
She didn't want to gamble unless the stake was small.
The higher the stake, the greater the risk and potential reward.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bet - an agreement to risk money on the outcome of an event
  • synonym: wager - an amount of money bet on the outcome of a game or event
