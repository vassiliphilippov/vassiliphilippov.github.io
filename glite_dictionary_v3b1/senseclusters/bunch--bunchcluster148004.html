bunch

noun
group of friends (informal)
Pronunciation: / bʌntʃ / (AmE), / bʌntʃ / (BrE)
(informal) a group of people, especially friends, considered together.

Examples

A bunch of friends
After the meeting, the whole bunch decided to grab dinner together.
I went to the movies last night with a bunch of friends from work.
She and her bunch have been inseparable since college.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crowd - a group of people (often informal friendship group)
  • synonym: gang - an informal group of people, often friends
