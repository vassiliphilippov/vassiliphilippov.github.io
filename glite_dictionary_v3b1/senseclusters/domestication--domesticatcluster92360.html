domestication

noun
adaptation to domestic life
Pronunciation: / dəˌmɛstəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / dəˌmɛstɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of adapting someone or something to domestic life or home routines

Examples

His domestication at home
After years of travel, he found domestication challenging but rewarding.
Her domestication of the wild artist surprised their friends.
The domestication of former adventurers was a common theme in literature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: taming - the process of making something less wild or easier to live with
