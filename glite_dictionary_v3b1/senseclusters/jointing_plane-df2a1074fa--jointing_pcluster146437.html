A long, flat woodworking tool used to smooth and straighten the edges of boards so they fit together closely.

Examples

A jointing plane tool
A well-sharpened jointing plane can make joints nearly invisible in finished furniture.
He used a jointing plane to create perfectly straight edges before gluing the boards together.
The cabinetmaker reached for his trusty jointing plane to prepare the panels for assembly.
