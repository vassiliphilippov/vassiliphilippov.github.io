perforant

noun
hole-making tool
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜːrfərənt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːfərənt / (BrE)
a tool or instrument used to make holes or perforations in materials such as paper, leather, or fabric

Examples

A perforant tool
The artisan used a perforant to create evenly spaced holes in the leather belt.
Before binding the pages, she made holes with a perforant to thread the string through.
A perforant is essential for crafts that require precise holes in fabric or paper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awl - a pointed tool for piercing small holes in leather or wood
  • synonym: punch - a tool for making holes in materials
