humbleness

noun
quality of being humble
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmbəlnəs /; / ˈhʌm.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈhʌmb(ə)lnəs /; / ˈhʌm.bəl.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being modest and not thinking you are more important than others; humility

Examples

Her humbleness and willingness to listen gained her the respect of her colleagues.
