Hydnaceae

noun
family of tooth fungi
Pronunciation: / hɪdˈneɪsiaɪ /; / hɪdˈneɪsi.iː / (AmE), / hɪdˈneɪsiːiː /; / hɪdˈneɪsiː / (BrE)
a family of fungi known as tooth fungi, with fruiting bodies that have spiny or toothed surfaces instead of gills or pores
