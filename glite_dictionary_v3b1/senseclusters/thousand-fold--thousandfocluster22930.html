thousand-fold

adverb
by one thousand times; multiplied by 1000
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈθaʊzəndˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈθaʊzəndfəʊld / (BrE)
By a factor of one thousand; multiplied by one thousand times.

Examples

Increased thousand-fold
Exposure to light boosted the activity of the compound thousand-fold in the experiment.
Technology has advanced thousand-fold since the early 20th century.
The value of the company increased thousand-fold after the new product launch.
