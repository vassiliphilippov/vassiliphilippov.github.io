reed grass

noun
tall marsh grass of the genus Calamagrostis
5.4
Pronunciation: / rid ɡræs / (AmE), / riːd ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
any of various tall perennial grasses of the genus Calamagrostis, typically with feathery plumes, found in marshlands and wet woodlands of the temperate Northern Hemisphere

Examples

Tall reed grass
In the wet woodland, reed grass dominates the understory with its tall, feathery plumes.
Restoring reed grass habitats supports local wildlife and improves water quality.
The reed grass along the riverbank swayed gently in the breeze.
