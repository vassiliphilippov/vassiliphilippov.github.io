copy

noun
text intended for printing or publication
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑpi /; / ˈkɑːpi / (AmE), / ˈkɒpi / (BrE)
written material prepared for publication, such as in newspapers, magazines, or advertisements

Examples

Magazine copy
Please send me the copy for the advertisement without any images attached.
The designer has already laid out the graphics, but we're still waiting on the final copy.
The magazine editor asked for the copy by Friday morning to meet the printing deadline.
After the press conference, journalists scrambled to produce their copy before deadline.
Breaking news often results in last-minute changes to the copy for the front page.
The editor was looking for copy that would attract more readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manuscript - a writer's original text submitted for publication
  • synonym: text - the written word as opposed to images
