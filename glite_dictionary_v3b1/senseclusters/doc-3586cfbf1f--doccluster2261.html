DoC

noun
abbreviation for U.S. Department of Commerce
6.5
Pronunciation: / di oʊ si / (AmE), / diː əʊ siː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for the United States Department of Commerce, the federal department responsible for promoting and administering domestic and foreign trade, conducting the census, and overseeing the patent office.
