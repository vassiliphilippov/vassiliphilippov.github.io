high-crowned

adjective
having a tall crown (of a hat)
2.4
Pronunciation: / haɪˈkraʊnd / (AmE), / haɪˈkraʊnd / (BrE)
(of a hat) having a tall or high crown.

Examples

A high-crowned hat
She wore a striking high-crowned hat to the wedding.
The cowboy's high-crowned hat protected him from the sun.
Victorian fashion often featured high-crowned women's hats adorned with ribbons.
