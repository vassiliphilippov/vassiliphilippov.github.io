waterboy

noun
person doing menial tasks
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔː.tər.bɔɪ/ /; / /ˈwɑː.tər.bɔɪ/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.tə.bɔɪ/ (BrE)
an informal term for a person who performs menial or low-status tasks for others, often in a subservient role

Examples

Office waterboy
He started as the waterboy, running errands and helping everyone in the office.
During the football game, the waterboy made sure the players stayed hydrated.
Some people use the term waterboy to describe someone who does all the small jobs for others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assistant - a person who helps someone in their work
  • antonym: boss - a person in charge or with authority over others
  • synonym: gofer - a person who runs errands or does minor tasks for others
