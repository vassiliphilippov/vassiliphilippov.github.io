oximetry

noun
measuring blood oxygen
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɑkˈsɪmətri / (AmE), / ɒkˈsɪmətri / (BrE)
the measurement of oxygen levels in the blood or body tissues, usually using a special device

Examples

Related Terms

  • synonym: blood gas analysis - a test measuring oxygen and other gases in the blood
  • antonym: capnometry - the measurement of carbon dioxide levels in respiratory gases
  • synonym: pulse oximetry - a noninvasive method for measuring blood oxygen using a fingertip device
  • antonym: spirometry - the measurement of lung function, especially the amount and speed of air inhaled and exhaled
