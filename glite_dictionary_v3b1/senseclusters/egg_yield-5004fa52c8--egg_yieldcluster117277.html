egg yield

noun
number of eggs produced by an animal
4.2
Pronunciation: / ɛɡ jiːld / (AmE), / ɛɡ jiːld / (BrE)
The number or quantity of eggs produced by a hen or other egg-laying animal, typically over a specific period.

Examples

Egg yield per hen
Farmers keep detailed records to monitor their hens' egg yield throughout the year.
Improving the diet of chickens can result in a higher egg yield.
Researchers are studying methods to increase the egg yield in commercial poultry operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fertility - the ability to produce offspring or eggs
  • synonym: productivity - the rate at which goods or crops are produced
