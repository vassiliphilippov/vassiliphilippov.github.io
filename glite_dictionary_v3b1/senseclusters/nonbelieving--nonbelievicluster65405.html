nonbelieving

adjective
doubtful or skeptical
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.bɪˈlivɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.bɪˈliːvɪŋ / (BrE)
showing doubt or lack of belief in something that has been said or claimed

Examples

Nonbelieving expression
He gave a nonbelieving look when he heard the unbelievable story.
Her nonbelieving expression made it clear she did not accept his explanation.
The audience remained nonbelieving despite the speaker's passionate arguments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doubtful - feeling or showing uncertainty
  • synonym: skeptical - having doubts or not easily convinced
  • antonym: trusting - willing to believe or rely on others
