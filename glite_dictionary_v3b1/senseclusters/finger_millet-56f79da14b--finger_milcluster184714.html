finger millet

noun
a cereal grass; its flour is a food staple
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡɚ ˈmɪlɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə ˈmɪlɪt / (BrE)
A cereal plant of tropical Asia and Africa, whose small seeds are used to make a flour for food.

Examples

Finger millet flour
Farmers in southern India traditionally cultivate finger millet as a major cereal crop.
Nutritious porridge can be made from the flour of finger millet.
Recent studies highlight the health benefits of finger millet for people with diabetes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: millet - a group of grasses cultivated for their edible seeds
