conductorship

noun
role of conductor
Pronunciation: / kənˈdʌktɚˌʃɪp / (AmE), / kənˈdʌktəʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or role of being a conductor, especially of an orchestra or musical group

Examples

The conductorship of orchestra
Her conductorship of the symphony orchestra lasted for over a decade.
He was offered the conductorship of a prestigious European orchestra.
During his conductorship, the choir gained international recognition for its performances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conduct - to direct the performance of musicians
  • synonym: leadership - the position or function of leading a group
