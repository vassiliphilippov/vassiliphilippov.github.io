fall short of

verb
not reach expected level
4.3
Pronunciation: / fɔl ʃɔrt əv /; / fɔl ʃɔrt ʌv / (AmE), / fɔːl ʃɔːt ɒv / (BrE)
to not reach the level or standard that is expected or required

Examples

To fall short of goals
Despite his efforts, his performance fell short of what was needed to win the prize.
Her explanation fell short of convincing the skeptical audience.
The new product fell short of our expectations and received mixed reviews.
Despite his best efforts, his proposal still fell short of what the committee wanted.
Her explanation fell short of convincing the panel of her innocence.
If our sales fall short of the target again this quarter, we may face cutbacks.
