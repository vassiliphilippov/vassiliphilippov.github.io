indigo

adjective
deep purplish-blue color
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪˌɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
of a deep purplish-blue color between blue and violet

Examples

Indigo dress
Rows of indigo flowers bloomed along the garden path in early spring.
She wore an elegant dress of shimmering indigo fabric to the gala.
The artist chose indigo paint to capture the twilight sky.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: blue - a color between green and violet; one of the primary colors
  • degree higher: violet - a color between blue and purple in the spectrum
