lipodystrophy

noun
abnormal fat distribution
9.9
Pronunciation: /lɪp.ə.ˈdɪs.trə.fi/ /; / /laɪ.pə.ˈdɪs.trə.fi/ (AmE), /lɪp.ə.ˈdɪs.trə.fi/ /; / /laɪ.pə.ˈdɪs.trə.fi/ (BrE)
a medical condition where the body has an abnormal or uneven distribution of fat tissue
