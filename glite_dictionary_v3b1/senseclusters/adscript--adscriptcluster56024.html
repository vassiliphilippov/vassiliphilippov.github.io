adscript

adjective
written after and aligned with a character
Pronunciation: / ˈædˌskrɪpt / (AmE), / ˈad.skrɪpt / (BrE)
(of a character or symbol) written or printed immediately following another character and aligned with it.

Examples

Adscript letter
Adscript letters are rarely seen in modern printed texts outside specialized fields.
In this linguistic notation, an adscript character indicates a particular type of vowel quality.
The mathematician used an adscript number to distinguish between the variables x and x₁.
