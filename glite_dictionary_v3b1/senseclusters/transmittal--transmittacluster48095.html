transmittal

noun
the act of sending or conveying something
Pronunciation: / trænsˈmɪt̬.əl / (AmE), / trænsˈmɪt.əl / (BrE)
the act of sending or conveying something, especially a message or official document, from one person or place to another

Examples

Transmittal of documents
A delay in transmittal can affect the entire approval process.
Please include a cover letter with the transmittal of these documents.
The transmittal of the report must be completed by Friday afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transmission - the action or process of transmitting something or the state of being transmitted
