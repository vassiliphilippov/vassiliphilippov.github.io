miswiring

noun
incorrect wiring
7.6
Pronunciation: /mɪs.ˈwaɪ.rɪŋ/ (AmE), /mɪs.ˈwaɪ.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act or result of connecting wires incorrectly, causing a device or system to malfunction

Examples

Electrical miswiring
The electrician discovered a miswiring that caused the kitchen lights to flicker.
Due to a miswiring in the control panel, the machine would not start properly.
Safety checks are important to prevent any miswiring in new electrical installations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: short circuit - an electrical circuit that allows current to travel along an unintended path
  • antonym: wiring - the arrangement or installation of wires in a device or building
