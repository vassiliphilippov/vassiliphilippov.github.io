beachgoer

noun
person at the beach
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtʃˌɡoʊər / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
a person who visits the beach for leisure or recreation

Examples

Many beachgoers relax
During the summer, the city is filled with beachgoers enjoying the sun and sand.
The lifeguard watched as a group of beachgoers played volleyball near the water.
Many beachgoers bring umbrellas and coolers to make their day more comfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sunbather - a person who lies in the sun to get a tan
  • synonym: swimmer - a person who swims for sport or recreation
