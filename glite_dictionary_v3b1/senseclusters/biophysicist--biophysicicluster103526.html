biophysicist

noun
scientist in biophysics
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˈfɪz.ɪ.sɪst / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊˈfɪz.ɪ.sɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who applies the principles and methods of physics to the study of biological systems

Examples

A biophysicist studies cells
As a biophysicist, she spends her days researching how proteins fold in living cells.
Many biophysicists collaborate with biologists and chemists to advance medical research.
The laboratory hired a new biophysicist to analyze muscle contractions from a physical perspective.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biophysics - the science applying principles of physics to biological systems
