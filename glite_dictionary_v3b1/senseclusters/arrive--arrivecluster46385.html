arrive

verb
to reach a destination
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈraɪv / (AmE), / əˈraɪv / (BrE)
to reach a place, especially at the end of a journey

Examples

Arrive at the hotel
Please let me know when you arrive at your hotel safely.
The train will arrive at the station in ten minutes.
We finally arrived at the summit after hours of climbing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depart - to leave, especially to start a journey
  • synonym: reach - to arrive at; to come to a place
