in camera

adverb
in private; behind closed doors
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈkæmərə / (AmE), / ɪn ˈkamərə / (BrE)
(of a meeting, discussion, or court session) held in private, with only those directly involved present; not open to the public.

Examples

In camera meeting
Certain evidence was presented in camera to avoid influencing the jury.
The board met in camera to discuss the confidential merger details.
The judge decided the sensitive testimony would be heard in camera to protect the witness's identity.
