vernissage

noun
art exhibition opening
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɛrˈnɪsɑːʒ / (AmE), / vɛːˈnɪsɑːʒ / (BrE)
a private or formal event marking the opening of an art exhibition, often for invited guests

Examples

Gallery vernissage
The artist invited all her friends to the vernissage of her new exhibition at the downtown gallery.
At the vernissage, guests enjoyed wine and listened to the curator's opening remarks.
He felt honored to attend the exclusive vernissage before the exhibition opened to the public.
We were invited to the vernissage of the artist's latest collection at the museum.
The vernissage offered collectors a first look at the paintings before the public opening.
At the vernissage, guests enjoyed wine and conversation while viewing the new sculptures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: finissage - a closing event for an art exhibition
  • synonym: opening - the first event or ceremony marking the start of something
  • synonym: preview - an advance showing of something before the official opening
