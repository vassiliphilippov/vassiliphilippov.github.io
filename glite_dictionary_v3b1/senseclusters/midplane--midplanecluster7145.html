midplane

noun
median plane dividing left and right sides
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌpleɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌpleɪn / (BrE)
the median plane dividing the body or a body part into equal left and right halves

Examples

The midplane of body
Anatomical diagrams often depict the midplane to show symmetrical structures.
The surgeon made the incision along the midplane of the patient's torso.
To study craniofacial symmetry, researchers first locate the midplane of the skull.
