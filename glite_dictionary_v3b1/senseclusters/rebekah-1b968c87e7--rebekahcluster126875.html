Rebekah

proper noun
wife of Isaac in the Old Testament
Pronunciation: / rɪˈbɛkə / (AmE), / rɪˈbiːkə / (BrE)
(in the Old Testament) the wife of Isaac and the mother of Jacob and Esau.
