vegetatively

adverb
without awareness
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒɪteɪtɪvli / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒɪtətɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that resembles a vegetative state, without conscious awareness or purposeful activity.

Examples

Lying vegetatively
After the accident, he lay vegetatively in the hospital bed for weeks.
She moved vegetatively through her daily routine, barely noticing her surroundings.
The patient stared vegetatively at the ceiling, showing no signs of recognition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consciously - in a way that shows awareness or intention
  • synonym: unresponsively - in a way that shows no reaction or response
