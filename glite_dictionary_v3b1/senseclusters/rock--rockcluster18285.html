rock

verb
move back and forth or sideways
3.4 1.0
Pronunciation: /rɑːk/ (AmE), /rɒk/ (BrE)
to move gently or repeatedly back and forth or from side to side, often to comfort or calm

Examples

To rock a baby
He rocked back and forth nervously while waiting for his interview.
Strong winds rocked the boat from side to side all night.
The baby fell asleep while her mother gently rocked the cradle.

Strong winds rocked the boat from side to side.
