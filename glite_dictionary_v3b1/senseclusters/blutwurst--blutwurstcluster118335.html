blutwurst

noun
German blood sausage
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːtvʊrst / (AmE), / ˈbluːtvʊəst / (BrE)
a type of German blood sausage made from pork, blood, and spices

Examples

A plate of blutwurst
At the Oktoberfest celebration, he enjoyed a plate of blutwurst with mustard and bread.
The chef explained how traditional blutwurst is prepared using fresh pork and spices.
She was hesitant to try blutwurst but found it surprisingly flavorful.
