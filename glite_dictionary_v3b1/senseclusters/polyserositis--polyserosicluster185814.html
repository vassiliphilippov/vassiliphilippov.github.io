polyserositis

noun
inflammation of serous membranes
9.9
Pronunciation: /pɑː.lɪ.sə.rə.ˈsaɪ.tɪs/ (AmE), /pɒ.lɪ.sə.rə.ˈsaɪ.tɪs/ (BrE)
a medical condition in which several serous membranes in the body become inflamed at the same time

Examples

Polyserositis inflammation
The doctor suspected polyserositis when the patient presented with chest and abdominal pain.
Treatment for polyserositis often involves managing inflammation in multiple body cavities.
After several tests, the diagnosis of polyserositis explained the patient's widespread discomfort.
