belt-shaped

adjective
long and narrow in shape, like a belt
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛlt ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈbɛlt ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape of a belt; long and narrow.

Examples

A belt-shaped patch
A belt-shaped patch of grass circles the old oak tree.
Scientists discovered a belt-shaped fossil embedded in the rock.
The satellite images clearly show a belt-shaped cloud formation stretching across the region.
