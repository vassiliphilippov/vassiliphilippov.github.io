swallowwort

noun
tropical herbaceous plant; a weed with red flowers
6.5 8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈswɑː.loʊ.wɜːrt/ (AmE), /ˈswɒl.əʊ.wɜːt/ (BrE)
A tropical herb with orange-red flowers and seed pods resembling a swallow's outspread wings, considered a weed in many tropical regions.

Examples

Tropical swallowwort weed
Gardeners find it difficult to remove swallowwort once it takes root in the soil.
The invasive swallowwort has spread rapidly across the local fields.
The unique pods of the swallowwort inspired its name due to their resemblance to a bird in flight.
