koumiss

noun
fermented mare's milk beverage
9.9
Pronunciation: / kuˈmɪs / (AmE), / kuːˈmɪs / (BrE)
an alcoholic drink made from fermented mare's milk, traditionally consumed by nomads in central Asia

Examples

A glass of koumiss
Mongolian herders often drink koumiss during festivals and celebrations.
The slightly sour taste of koumiss is an acquired one for most visitors.
Traditional koumiss is made by fermenting mare's milk in large leather containers.

Related Terms
