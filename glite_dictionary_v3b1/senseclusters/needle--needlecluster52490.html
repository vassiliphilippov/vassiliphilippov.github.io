needle

noun
phonograph stylus for playing records
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdəl / (AmE), / ˈniːdl̩ /; / ˈniːdl / (BrE)
a long, slender, pointed object or part, such as a stylus for a record player, an indicator on a gauge, or a leaf of a pine tree
