lightheadedness

noun
lack of prudence or seriousness
Pronunciation: / ˌlaɪtˈhɛdɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌlaɪtˈhɛdɪdnɪs / (BrE)
A frivolous or careless lack of prudence or seriousness.

Examples

His lightheadedness caused trouble
He regretted his lightheadedness only after the consequences became clear.
Her lightheadedness at the meeting shocked the more serious members of the board.
The novel portrays a society afflicted by lightheadedness and a lack of responsibility.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prudence - the quality of being cautious and wise
  • synonym: recklessness - a lack of concern for the consequences of actions
