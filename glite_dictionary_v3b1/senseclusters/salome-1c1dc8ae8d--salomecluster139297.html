Salome

proper noun
biblical figure; Herodias's daughter
9.9
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈloʊ.miː/ (AmE), /sə.ˈləʊ.miː/ (BrE)
(in the Bible) the daughter of Herodias whose dancing so pleased Herod Antipas that he granted her request for the head of John the Baptist.

Examples

Salome demands head
In Oscar Wilde’s play, Salome demands the head of John the Baptist as a reward.
Paintings of Salome often depict her holding a platter with the prophet's head.
The legend of Salome inspired many artists and writers throughout history.
