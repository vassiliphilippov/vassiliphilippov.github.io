unfeasibly

adverb
impossibly, extremely difficult
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfiːzəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈfiːzəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is not possible or is extremely difficult to achieve

Examples

An unfeasibly steep mountain
The mountain was unfeasibly steep for most climbers to attempt the ascent.
Building a bridge across the canyon seemed unfeasibly expensive for the small town.
The project deadline was unfeasibly short, making it hard for the team to finish on time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: feasibly - in a way that is possible or practical
  • synonym: impossibly - in a way that cannot be done or achieved
