colonia

noun
border community
Pronunciation: / koʊˈloʊniə / (AmE), / kəˈləʊniə / (BrE)
a small, often rural community along the U.S.-Mexico border, sometimes lacking basic services such as water or electricity

Examples

Border colonia
Many families in the colonia struggle with limited access to clean water.
The government launched a program to improve infrastructure in the local colonia.
She grew up in a colonia near the Texas-Mexico border.

Related Terms

  • synonym: settlement - a small community or village
  • synonym: village - a small group of houses in a rural area
