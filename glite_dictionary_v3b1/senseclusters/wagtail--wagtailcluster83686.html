wagtail

noun
small Old World bird with a wagging tail
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwæɡ.teɪl / (AmE), / ˈwæɡ.teɪl / (BrE)
a small Old World songbird with a long tail that continually wags up and down as it walks, belonging to the family Motacillidae.

Examples

A wagtail bird
A wagtail flitted along the riverbank, its tail bouncing up and down.
Many birdwatchers are delighted to spot the distinctive wagtail on their walks.
We watched a wagtail hunt for insects in the garden yesterday morning.
