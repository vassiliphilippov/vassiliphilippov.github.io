oilskin

noun
waterproof coat made from oiled fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɪlˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈɔɪl.skɪn /; / ˈɔɪlˌskɪn / (BrE)
a strong, waterproof cloth treated with oil or wax, or a coat made from this material, used especially for protection against rain
