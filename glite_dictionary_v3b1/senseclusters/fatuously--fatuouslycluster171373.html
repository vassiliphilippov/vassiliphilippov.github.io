fatuously

adverb
in a foolish or silly manner
6.3 7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfætʃuəsli / (AmE), / ˈfatjʊəsli / (BrE)
In a foolish, silly, or complacently stupid manner; showing a lack of intelligence or awareness.

Examples

To smile fatuously
He smiled fatuously, unaware of how ridiculous he looked to the others.
She fatuously believed that luck alone would bring her success.
The spokesperson fatuously dismissed all criticism without bothering to consider the facts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fatuous - foolish or silly, especially in a complacent way
