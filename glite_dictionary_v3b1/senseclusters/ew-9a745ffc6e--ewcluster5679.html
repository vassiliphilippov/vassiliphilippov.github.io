EW

noun
military use of electromagnetic energy
9.6
Pronunciation: / iː ˈdʌbljuː / (AmE), / iː ˈdʌbljuː / (BrE)
abbreviation for electronic warfare; military operations that use electromagnetic energy to detect, exploit, or prevent enemy use of the electromagnetic spectrum.

Examples

An EW unit
An EW unit was deployed to disrupt enemy communications.
The army's EW capabilities were critical during the conflict.
Training in EW is now required for all modern signal officers.
