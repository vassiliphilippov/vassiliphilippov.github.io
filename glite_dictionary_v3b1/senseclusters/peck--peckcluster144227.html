peck

verb
to hit lightly with a picking motion
4.2 2.3
Pronunciation: /pek/ (AmE), /pek/ (BrE)
to strike or hit lightly and repeatedly with a pointed object or beak, as a bird does

Examples

To peck the ground
He used a pencil to gently peck the dirt off the fossil.
She watched the chicken peck at the ground, looking for seeds.
The woodpecker began to peck at the tree trunk, searching for insects.

Related Terms
