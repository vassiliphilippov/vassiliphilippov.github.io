contract offer

noun
employer's proposal of employment terms
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːnˌtrækt ˈɔːfər / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.trækt ˈɒf.ər / (BrE)
an official proposal by an employer to hire a person at a specified rate or on particular terms

Examples

A contract offer from company
After several interviews, she finally received a contract offer from the company.
He declined the contract offer because the salary did not meet his expectations.
The union negotiated for better terms before accepting the contract offer.
