rim

noun
basketball hoop; metal ring for net
Pronunciation: / rɪm / (AmE), / rɪm / (BrE)
(in basketball) the metal hoop from which the net is suspended and through which the ball must pass to score.

Examples

Basketball rim
He shot the ball, and it bounced off the rim before going in.
Her free throw hit the front of the rim and rolled out.
The player grabbed the rim after dunking the ball.
