spring

noun
season after winter
1.0
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ / (AmE), / sprɪŋ / (BrE)
The season between winter and summer, when plants begin to grow and the weather becomes warmer.

Examples

Spring flowers bloom
After a long winter, everyone looked forward to the arrival of spring.
In spring, many flowers bloom and fill the gardens with color.
We plan to go hiking in the mountains during spring break.

Related Terms

  • antonym: autumn - the season after summer and before winter
  • degree higher: summer - the warmest season of the year, following spring
  • degree lower: winter - the coldest season of the year, before spring
