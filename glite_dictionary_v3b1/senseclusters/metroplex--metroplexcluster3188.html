metroplex

noun
large urban region
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtroʊˌplɛks / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrəʊˌplɛks / (BrE)
a large metropolitan area consisting of several closely connected cities and suburbs

Examples

The metroplex area
The Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex is one of the fastest-growing regions in the United States.
Many people commute across the metroplex for work, taking advantage of the extensive highway system.
The metroplex offers a wide variety of cultural events and entertainment options throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conurbation - an extended urban area formed by the merging of several towns or cities
  • synonym: metropolis - a large and important city
