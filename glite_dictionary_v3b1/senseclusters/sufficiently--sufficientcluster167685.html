sufficiently

adverb
to an adequate or sufficient degree
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: / səˈfɪʃəntli / (AmE), / səˈfɪʃəntli / (BrE)
to a degree that is enough for a particular purpose or need; to an adequate extent

Examples

Is sufficiently dry
Make sure the paint is sufficiently dry before applying a second coat.
She was sufficiently prepared for the exam to pass with ease.
The room was sufficiently large to accommodate all the guests.
