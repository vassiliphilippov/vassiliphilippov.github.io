resistless

adjective
offering no resistance
9.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɪstləs / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪstlɪs / (BrE)
impossible to resist; too strong or powerful to be opposed

Examples

A resistless force
He was overwhelmed by the resistless force of her argument.
The resistless prisoners made no attempt to escape.
The resistless tide swept away everything in its path.
He felt a resistless urge to confess everything to her that night.
She was swept away by a resistless tide of emotion she could not control.
The army advanced with resistless force, overwhelming all opposition in its path.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irresistible - impossible to resist; too attractive or appealing
