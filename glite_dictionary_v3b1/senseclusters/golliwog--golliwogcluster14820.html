golliwog

noun
offensive doll with racist caricature
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑliˌwɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒliˌwɒɡ / (BrE)
a doll or character depicted with exaggerated stereotypical features, especially of a Black person, now regarded as offensive

Examples

A golliwog doll
Collectors sometimes seek out old golliwogs, though their display is often controversial.

The museum removed the golliwog from its toy collection after concerns were raised.
