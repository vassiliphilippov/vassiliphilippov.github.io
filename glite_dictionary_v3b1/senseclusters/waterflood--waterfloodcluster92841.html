waterflood

verb
to flood with water
6.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…ît…ôrÀåfl åd / (AmE), / Ààw…îÀêt…ôÀåfl åd / (BrE)
to flood an area with water, especially to cover land or to increase oil recovery from a reservoir

Examples

To waterflood a field
Farmers sometimes waterflood their fields to prepare the soil for planting.
The engineers planned to waterflood the oil reservoir to boost production.
Heavy rains can waterflood low-lying neighborhoods during the storm season.
