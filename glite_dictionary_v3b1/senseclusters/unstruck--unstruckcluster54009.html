unstruck

adjective
not ignited
7.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstrʌk / (AmE), / ʌnˈstrʌk / (BrE)
not ignited, lit, or set off, especially referring to a match, fuse, or similar device.

Examples

Unstruck match
He kept an unstruck match in his pocket to light the candle later.
The fireworks remained unstruck after the rain ruined the celebration.
She found an unstruck fuse and quickly replaced it before the show began.

Related Terms

  • antonym: struck - ignited or set off by striking
  • synonym: unlit - not lit or burning
