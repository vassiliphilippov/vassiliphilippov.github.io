blueblossom

noun
shrub with blue flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːˌblɑsəm / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌblɒsəm / (BrE)
a shrub or plant with blue flowers, especially one of the genus Ceanothus

Examples

The blueblossom shrub
The blueblossom in her garden attracts many butterflies.
We planted a blueblossom near the fence to add color to the yard.
In spring, the blueblossom produces clusters of bright blue flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceanothus - a genus of shrubs with clusters of small blue or white flowers
  • synonym: lilac - a shrub with fragrant purple or white flowers
