incommunicability

noun
impossibility of communication
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪn.kəˌmjuː.nɪ.kəˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ɪn.kəˌmjuː.nɪ.kəˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being impossible to communicate or express to others

Examples

Incommunicability of feelings
The incommunicability of her grief made it difficult for friends to offer comfort.
Some emotions have an incommunicability that words cannot overcome.
The film explores the incommunicability between generations in a rapidly changing society.

Related Terms

  • antonym: communicability - the ability to be communicated or expressed
  • synonym: inexpressibility - the state of being impossible to express in words
