overexpression

noun
excessive expression
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
the act of expressing feelings, opinions, or style in an excessive or exaggerated way

Examples

Overexpression of feelings
His overexpression of excitement made the announcement seem less genuine to the audience.
The teacher warned against overexpression of emotions during class discussions.
Some people find overexpression of personal opinions inappropriate in professional settings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exaggeration - the act of making something seem more important or serious than it is
  • antonym: restraint - the act of holding back or controlling one's feelings or actions
