phobic neurosis

noun
anxiety disorder with irrational fears
6.9 7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊbɪk nʊˈroʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˈfəʊbɪk njʊˈrəʊsɪs / (BrE)
an anxiety disorder marked by extreme and irrational fear of specific objects, activities, or social situations

Examples

Phobic neurosis disorder
After years of suffering from a phobic neurosis, she finally sought help from a therapist.
Exposure therapy is sometimes used to treat patients with a phobic neurosis.
His phobic neurosis kept him from attending crowded events or social gatherings.
