street performance

noun
public outdoor performance for tips
1.0
Pronunciation: / strit pɚˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / striːt pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
a public performance by an artist or group, typically in an outdoor urban space, usually done for voluntary donations from passers-by

Examples

Street performance in park
Her passion for acting began with street performance outside the local theater.
Some musicians make a living through street performance in busy city squares.
The festival included a variety of street performances, from juggling to live music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: busking - performing music or entertainment in public places for money
  • synonym: street performer - a person who performs publicly on the street for tips
