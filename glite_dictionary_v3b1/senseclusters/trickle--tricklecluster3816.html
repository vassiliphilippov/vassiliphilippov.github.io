Examples

Synonyms

    2.8 1.32/m
    dribble means liquid falls in small drops or runs slowly, while trickle is a thin, slow, irregular flow or stream.
  • drip down
    1.4 0.86/m
    drip down means liquid falls in separate drops downward, while trickle describes a thin or slow irregular flow that can form a stream.
