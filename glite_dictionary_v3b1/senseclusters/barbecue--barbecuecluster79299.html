barbecue

noun
meat cooked with seasoned sauce on a grill
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːr.bɪ.kjuː / (AmE), / ˈbɑː.bɪ.kjuː / (BrE)
Meat that has been cooked on a grill or over an open fire, typically flavored with a highly seasoned sauce.

Examples

Tasty barbecue meat
She ordered a plate of smoked barbecue for lunch at the restaurant.
The aroma of spicy barbecue drifted through the backyard during the party.
We served the barbecue with cornbread and coleslaw at the family picnic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbecued meat - meat that has been cooked on a barbecue
