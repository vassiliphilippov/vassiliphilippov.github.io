Synonyms

  • mortality rate
    3.8 2/m
    mortality rate is the technical statistic showing deaths per population per year, while mortality names the general number of deaths.
  • death rate
    2.7 1/m
    death rate counts deaths per thousand people in a period and is common in reports, while mortality is the formal population measure.
  • fatality rate
    4.9 0.2/m
    fatality rate often refers to deaths among a specific group such as disease cases, while mortality covers total deaths in a population.
