Janjaweed

proper noun
Sudanese militia group
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɑːndʒəˈwiːd / (AmE), / ˌdʒændʒəˈwiːd / (BrE)
an armed militia group active in the Darfur region of Sudan, known for involvement in conflict and violence

Examples

The Janjaweed militia
Many villages in Darfur have been attacked by the Janjaweed in recent years.
International organizations have reported human rights abuses committed by the Janjaweed.
The government was accused of supporting the Janjaweed during the conflict.

Related Terms

  • synonym: militia - an armed group not part of the regular army
  • synonym: rebels - people who fight against an established government or authority
