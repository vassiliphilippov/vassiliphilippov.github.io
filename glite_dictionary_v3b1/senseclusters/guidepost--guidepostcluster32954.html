guidepost

noun
post with sign giving directions
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪdˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdˌpəʊst / (BrE)
a post with a sign that gives directions or indicates the way to a place

Examples

A guidepost sign
A guidepost marked the trailhead at the entrance to the national park.
Each intersection along the hiking route had a guidepost with distances to the next landmark.
We stopped at the guidepost to check which direction we should take.

Related Terms

  • synonym: signpost - a post at the side of a road giving information such as the direction and distance to a place
