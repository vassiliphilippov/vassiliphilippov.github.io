purple osier

noun
a Eurasian willow with purple twigs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpəl ˈoʊziɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈəʊziə / (BrE)
A Eurasian willow (Salix purpurea) with reddish or purple twigs and bark rich in tannins, often used in basketry.

Examples

Purple osier willow
Gardeners often plant purple osier for its attractive twigs and fast growth.
The bark of the purple osier contains tannins useful for traditional crafts.
The basket was woven from purple osier harvested in spring.
