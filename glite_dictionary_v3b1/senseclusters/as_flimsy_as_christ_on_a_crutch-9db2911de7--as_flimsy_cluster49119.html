as flimsy as Christ on a crutch

adjective
extremely weak or unconvincing (idiom)
9.9
Pronunciation: / əz ˈflɪmzi əz kraɪst ɑn ə krʌtʃ / (AmE), / əz ˈflɪmzi əz kraɪst ɒn ə krʌtʃ / (BrE)
(of an argument, excuse, etc.) extremely weak, unconvincing, or lacking in substance.

Examples

As flimsy as Christ on a crutch excuse
His explanation for missing the meeting was as flimsy as Christ on a crutch.
Some politicians offer arguments that are as flimsy as Christ on a crutch when confronted with tough questions.
The evidence presented in court was as flimsy as Christ on a crutch, and the jury quickly dismissed it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flimsy - not strong or solid; easily damaged
  • antonym: robust - strong and convincing
  • synonym: weak - not convincing or powerful; lacking strength
