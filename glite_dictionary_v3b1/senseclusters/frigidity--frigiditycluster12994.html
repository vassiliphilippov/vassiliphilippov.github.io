frigidity

noun
lack of warmth or affection
Pronunciation: / frɪˈdʒɪdəti / (AmE), / frɪˈdʒɪdəti / (BrE)
A lack of warmth, affection, or enthusiasm in manner or feeling.

Examples

Emotional frigidity
Despite the festive occasion, a sense of frigidity lingered in the room.
Her frigidity towards her colleagues made it difficult for her to form close friendships at work.
There was a distinct frigidity in his voice whenever the subject was mentioned.
