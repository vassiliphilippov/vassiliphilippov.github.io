uncontentious

adjective
not argumentative
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈtɛnʃəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈtɛnʃəs / (BrE)
not likely to cause or be involved in arguments, disagreements, or controversy; calm and agreeable

Examples

An uncontentious attitude
Her uncontentious attitude helped resolve many workplace disputes before they escalated.
The meeting went smoothly thanks to the uncontentious nature of all the participants.
He is known for being uncontentious, always seeking compromise rather than conflict.
The committee quickly approved the uncontentious proposal without any objections.
Most people agreed that the changes to the schedule were uncontentious.
It was an uncontentious topic that everyone could support.

  • synonym: peaceful - calm and not wanting conflict
