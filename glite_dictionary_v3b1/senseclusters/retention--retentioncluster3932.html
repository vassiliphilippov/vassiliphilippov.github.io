retention

noun
ability to remember
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtɛn.ʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈtɛn.ʃən / (BrE)
the ability to remember information or past experiences

Examples

Memory retention
As people age, retention of detailed information can become more challenging.
Her excellent retention of names and faces made her a great host.
The training aims to improve the retention of safety procedures among workers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgetfulness - the quality of being unable to remember things
  • synonym: memory - the faculty by which the mind stores and remembers information
