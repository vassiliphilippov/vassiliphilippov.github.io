red flag

noun
a warning flag, signal of danger
Pronunciation: / rɛd flæɡ / (AmE), / rɛd flaɡ / (BrE)
a red flag used as a warning signal, especially to indicate danger or caution (including official alerts for fire danger)

Examples

Red flag warning
A red flag flew at the entrance to the construction site.
She noticed several red flags in the safety inspection report.
The lifeguard raised a red flag to warn swimmers about dangerous currents.
The National Weather Service issued a red flag for the county because of high winds and low humidity.
Campers were asked to avoid open fires during the weekend after officials announced a red flag.
When a red flag is posted, residents should follow evacuation and fire-safety instructions immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Red Flag Warning - an official notice from meteorological agencies indicating critical fire weather conditions
  • synonym: signal - a sound or visual sign giving information or instructions
  • synonym: warning - something that serves to warn of danger
