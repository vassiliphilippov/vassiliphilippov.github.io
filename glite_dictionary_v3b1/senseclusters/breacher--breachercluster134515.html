breacher

noun
violator of rules
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbriː.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈbriː.tʃə/ (BrE)
a person or organization that breaks a law, contract, rule, or formal agreement

Examples

A contract breacher
The company was labeled a breacher after failing to honor the contract terms.
A breacher of the agreement may face legal consequences.
The court ruled that the breacher must pay damages to the other party.
