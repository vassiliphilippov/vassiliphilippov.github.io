breacher

noun
violator of rules

Pronunciation: / ÀàbriÀêt É…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàbriÀêt É…ô / (BrE)

Examples

A breacher of the agreement may face legal consequences.
The court ruled that the breacher must pay damages to the other party.
