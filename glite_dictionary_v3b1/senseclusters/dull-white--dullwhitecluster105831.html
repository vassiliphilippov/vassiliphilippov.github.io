dull-white

adjective
white color lacking brightness or shine
2.5 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdʌl.waɪt/ /; / /ˈdʌl.hwaɪt/ (AmE), /ˈdʌl.waɪt/ (BrE)

Examples
