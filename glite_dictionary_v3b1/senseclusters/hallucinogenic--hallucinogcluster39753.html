hallucinogenic

adjective
producing hallucinations
Pronunciation: / həˌluːsɪnəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / həˌluːsɪnəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
(of a substance) able to produce hallucinations in the mind.

Examples

Hallucinogenic drugs
He was arrested for possession of hallucinogenic substances at the festival.
Some mushrooms contain hallucinogenic compounds that alter perception and mood.
The effects of the hallucinogenic drug included vivid visual distortions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychedelic - (of a drug) causing effects similar to hallucinations, especially intense sensory experiences
  • antonym: stimulant - having an opposite effect (increasing alertness without causing hallucinations)
