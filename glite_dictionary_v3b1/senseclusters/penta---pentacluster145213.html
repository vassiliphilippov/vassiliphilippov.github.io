penta-

x
prefix meaning five
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛntə / (AmE), / ˈpɛntə / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'five', used in compound words to indicate five parts or elements

Examples

The penta- prefix
The prefix penta- is used in 'pentagon' to mean a shape with five sides.
In chemistry, penta- appears in 'pentachloride' to show there are five chlorine atoms.
The word 'pentathlon' uses penta- to refer to an athletic event with five different sports.
