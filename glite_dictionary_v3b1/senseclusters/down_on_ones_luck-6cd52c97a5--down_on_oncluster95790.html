down on one's luck

adjective
experiencing a run of bad luck
2.2
Pronunciation: / daʊn ɑn wʌnz lʌk / (AmE), / daʊn ɒn wʌnz lʌk / (BrE)
experiencing a period of misfortune or bad luck, especially financially or in one's personal life

Examples

Down on his luck man

She tried to stay positive even though she was down on her luck and struggling to pay the bills.
When he saw a friend down on his luck, he offered him a place to stay.
