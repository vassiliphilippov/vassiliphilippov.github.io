404 moment

noun
a moment of confusion or mental lapse
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːɹ oʊ ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈfɔːr əʊ ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
A moment of confusion or mental lapse, especially when unable to find or remember something, likened to an internet '404 error' (page not found).

Examples

A 404 moment of confusion
I had a total 404 moment in the meeting and forgot my point.
She couldn't remember his name and just stood there having a 404 moment.
Whenever I misplace my phone, it's like a 404 moment for my brain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brain freeze - a temporary mental block or lapse in concentration
