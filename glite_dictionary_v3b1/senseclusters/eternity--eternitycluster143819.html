eternity

noun
endless existence, often the afterlife
4.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtɝːnəti / (AmE), / ɪˈtɜːnəti / (BrE)
endless time or existence; the state of lasting forever (often used in religious or philosophical contexts)

Examples

Life in eternity
Many religions promise eternity to the faithful after death.

Some cultures believe the soul lives on for all eternity.
Staring at the clock, it felt as though an eternity had passed.
The monuments seem built to last for eternity.
