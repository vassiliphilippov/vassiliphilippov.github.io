expansion bolt

noun
a bolt that expands to secure itself
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspænʃən boʊlt / (AmE), / ɪkˈspænʃən bəʊlt / (BrE)
a bolt designed with a mechanism that expands when inserted into a surface, securing the bolt in place

Examples

An expansion bolt in wall
Installing the bracket required drilling a hole and inserting an expansion bolt.
The shelves were secured to the wall using expansion bolts for extra strength.
You should use an expansion bolt when fastening heavy items to concrete or brick surfaces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anchor bolt - a bolt used to attach objects or structures to concrete
