discreteness

noun
the state of being separate and distinct
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskritnəs / (AmE), / dɪsˈkriːtnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being separate and distinct from others

Examples

The discreteness of parts
He was fascinated by the discreteness of each note in the musical scale.
Mathematicians study the discreteness of numbers in number theory.
The discreteness of species helps scientists classify living organisms accurately.
