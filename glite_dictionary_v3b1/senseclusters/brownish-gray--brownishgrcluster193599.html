brownish-gray

adjective
grey with a brown tint
Pronunciation: / ˈbraʊnɪʃˌɡreɪ /; / ˈbraʊnɪʃ-ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪʃˌɡreɪ /; / ˈbraʊnɪʃ-ɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a grey color tinged with brown.

Examples

A brownish-gray color
Her coat was a soft brownish-gray, blending in perfectly with the autumn landscape.

He wore a brownish-grey coat that blended well with the autumn landscape.
