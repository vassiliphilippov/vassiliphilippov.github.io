brownish-gray

adjective
grey with a brown tint
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbraʊnɪʃˌɡreɪ /; / ˈbraʊnɪʃ-ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪʃˌɡreɪ /; / ˈbraʊnɪʃ-ɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a grey color tinged with brown.

Examples

A brownish-gray color
Her coat was a soft brownish-gray, blending in perfectly with the autumn landscape.
The walls were painted a subtle brownish-gray that complemented the wooden floors.
We chose a brownish-gray fabric for the sofa because it hides stains well.
He wore a brownish-grey coat that blended well with the autumn landscape.
The puppy's fur turned a soft brownish-grey as it grew older.
The walls were painted a brownish-grey that gave the room a warm, earthy feel.
