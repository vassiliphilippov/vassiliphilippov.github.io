unisexual

adjective
having only one sex or sexual organ
5.7 6.2
Pronunciation: /juː.nɪ.ˈsek.sju.əl/ /; / /juː.nɪ.ˈsek.sə.l/ (AmE), /juː.nɪ.ˈsek.sju.əl/ /; / /juː.nɪ.ˈsek.sə.l/ (BrE)
relating to or denoting organisms that have only one type of sexual organ or are of a single sex, as opposed to hermaphroditic or bisexual forms.

Examples

Unisexual organisms
Insects with unisexual reproductive systems cannot self-fertilize.
Many plants are unisexual, producing either male or female flowers but not both.
The species studied exhibits a unisexual population structure, with only female individuals present.
