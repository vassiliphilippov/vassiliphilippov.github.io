Synonyms

  • bulb-shaped
    3.5 0.01/m
    bulb-shaped is the clear form word for objects that look like a bulb, while bulbous describes that swollen round form.
  • bulblike
    6.0 0.01/m
    bulblike looks like a bulb in shape and tone, while bulbous is the usual adjective for a rounded, swollen form.
  • bulging
    3.6 0.13/m
    bulging suggests something swelling outward because of pressure or force, while bulbous refers to a rounded, bulb-like shape.
  • protuberant
    8.4 0.10/m
    protuberant is a more formal word for a part that sticks out, while bulbous describes a smoothly rounded, swollen shape.
  • bulgy
    4.3 0.03/m
    bulgy is a casual, informal word for something that sticks out slightly, while bulbous sounds more clear and descriptive.
  • bellied
    5.6 0.53/m
    bellied often describes an object with a rounded belly or curve, while bulbous focuses on a bulb-like, swollen round shape.
