bespoken

adjective
custom-made (of clothing)
6.5
Pronunciation: / bɪˈspoʊkən / (AmE), / bɪˈspəʊkən / (BrE)
(of clothing) made to order for a particular customer; custom-made.

Examples

A bespoken suit
All the furniture in the study was bespoken rather than bought off-the-shelf.
His suit was bespoken and tailored perfectly to fit his frame.
The lady requested a bespoken dress for the ball.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bespoke - (of goods, especially clothing) made for a particular customer
