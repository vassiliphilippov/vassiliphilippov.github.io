coulometric

adjective
measuring electric charge
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkuːləˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌkuːləˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of electric charge, especially in chemical analysis

Examples

coulometric analysis
The laboratory used a coulometric technique to analyze the purity of the sample.
A coulometric titration provides precise results by measuring the total electric charge passed.
Researchers developed a new coulometric method for detecting trace amounts of water in oil.

Related Terms
