roller

noun
a small wheel without spokes
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə / (BrE)
a small wheel, typically without spokes, that revolves on an axle and is used to facilitate movement, especially as found on roller skates or similar devices.

Examples

A skate roller
I replaced the bent roller on my old roller skate with a new one.
The roller on my suitcase broke, making it difficult to pull.
The conveyor belt moved smoothly because each roller spun freely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wheel - a circular object that revolves on an axle and forms part of a machine or vehicle
