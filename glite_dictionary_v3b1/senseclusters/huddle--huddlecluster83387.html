huddle

noun
crowded, disorganized group
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌd.əl / (AmE), / ˈhʌd.əl / (BrE)
a group of people or things that are crowded closely together in a disorganized way

Examples

A huddle of people
A huddle of tourists blocked the entrance to the museum.
She spotted a huddle of reporters outside the courthouse.
The children waited in a huddle near the school gate for their parents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crowd - a large number of people gathered together
