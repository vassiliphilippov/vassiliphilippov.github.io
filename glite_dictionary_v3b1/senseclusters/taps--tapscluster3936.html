taps

noun
military bugle call for lights out or funerals
Pronunciation: / tæps / (AmE), / tæps / (BrE)
A bugle call played at night in the military, signaling lights out or as a mark of respect at military funerals.

Examples

Play taps at night
At the end of the memorial service, a lone bugler played taps.
Hearing taps always fills me with a sense of solemn respect.
The sound of taps drifted across the camp as the soldiers prepared for sleep.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reveille - a bugle call used to wake military personnel at sunrise
