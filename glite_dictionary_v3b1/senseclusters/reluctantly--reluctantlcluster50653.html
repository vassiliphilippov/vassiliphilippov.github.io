Synonyms

  • unwillingly
    3.4 0.4/m
    unwillingly expresses a clear lack of desire to act, while reluctantly can also suggest hesitation or delay.
  • grudgingly
    6.1 0.8/m
    grudgingly means doing something with reluctance, often quietly, while reluctantly covers both unwillingness and hesitation.
  • begrudgingly
    6.5 0.3/m
    begrudgingly suggests you do something unwillingly and with resentment, while reluctantly may be less angry and more hesitant.
  • hesitantly
    3.2 0.6/m
    hesitantly shows doubt or pause before acting, while reluctantly adds a stronger sense of not wanting to do it.
  • hesitatingly
    6.1 0.02/m
    hesitatingly describes pausing or unsure movement, while reluctantly implies a deeper unwillingness to act or agree.
