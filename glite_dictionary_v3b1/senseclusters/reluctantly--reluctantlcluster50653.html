reluctantly

adverb
in an unwilling or hesitant way
4.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlʌktəntli / (AmE), / rɪˈlʌktəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows unwillingness or hesitation.

Examples

To reluctantly agree
He reluctantly agreed to attend the meeting despite his reservations.
She reluctantly handed over the keys to her new car.
The child reluctantly apologized after being told to do so by his parents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hesitantly - in a hesitant or uncertain manner
  • antonym: willingly - in a willing or eager manner
