brisk

adjective
invigorating; energizing (weather/air/activity)
3.7 1.00/m
Pronunciation: / brÉªsk / (AmE), / brÉªsk / (BrE)
(of weather, air, or activity) stimulating and refreshing; imparting vitality and energy.

Examples

Brisk morning air
After a brisk swim in the lake, he felt refreshed and alert.
She enjoyed taking brisk morning walks to start her day with energy.
The brisk wind on the coast revived our spirits after a long day.
