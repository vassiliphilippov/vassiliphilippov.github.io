brighten

verb
make more cheerful with color
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈbraɪtn̩ / (BrE)
to make something appear more cheerful or lively, especially by adding color

Examples

To brighten a room
A fresh coat of yellow paint can brighten any kitchen.
Adding a few plants will instantly brighten up your workspace.
She used colorful pillows to brighten the living room.
