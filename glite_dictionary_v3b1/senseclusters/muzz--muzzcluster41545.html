muzz

verb
to confuse
9.9
Pronunciation: /mʌz/ (AmE), /mʌz/ (BrE)
to confuse or make unclear, especially in speech, thought, or concentration

Examples

Noise muzzes thinking
The loud music began to muzz his concentration during the exam.
Fatigue can muzz your thoughts and make it hard to focus.
Her words seemed to muzz the discussion rather than clarify the issue.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarify - to make something easier to understand
  • synonym: confuse - to make someone unable to think clearly
