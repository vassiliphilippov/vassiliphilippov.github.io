Scots Gaelic

noun
the Gaelic language of Scotland
9.6
Pronunciation: / skɑts ˈɡeɪlɪk / (AmE), / skɒts ˈɡaːlɪk / (BrE)
the variety of the Gaelic language spoken in Scotland, distinct from Irish or Manx Gaelic
