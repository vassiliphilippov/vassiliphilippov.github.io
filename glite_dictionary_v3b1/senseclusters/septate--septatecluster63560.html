septate

adjective
having or relating to a septum
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛp.teɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɛp.teɪt / (BrE)
having or relating to a septum or septa; divided by a partition or partitions.

Examples

A septate uterus
A septate uterus can pose complications during pregnancy.
The fungus is characterized by its septate hyphae, making it easy to distinguish from similar species.
Under the microscope, the cells appeared septate, each separated by thin walls.
