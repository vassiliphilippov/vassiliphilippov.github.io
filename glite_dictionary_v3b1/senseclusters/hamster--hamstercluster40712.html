hamster

noun
small burrowing rodent, pet
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhæm.stɚ / (AmE), / ˈhæm.stə / (BrE)
a small, short-tailed rodent with large cheek pouches, often kept as a pet

Examples

A pet hamster
My daughter keeps a hamster in a cage in her bedroom.
The hamster stuffed its cheeks with seeds before running back to its burrow.
We bought a new hamster as a classroom pet for the students to take care of.
