Synonyms

  • reprovision
    7.4 0.01/m
    reprovision is a formal word for equipping people or units again, while resupply is a more general term for providing supplies again.
  • replenish
    4.3 3.23/m
    replenish means to fill or restore something used up, while resupply focuses on sending supplies to people or places.
  • restock
    3.2 0.82/m
    restock is to fill shelves or a shop with goods again, while resupply covers sending supplies to users or units.

Related Terms
