unpartnered

adjective
without a partner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɑːrtnərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɑːtnəd / (BrE)
not having a partner, especially in a romantic or domestic relationship

Examples

Unpartnered adult
After her divorce, she remained unpartnered for several years before dating again.
The survey found that a growing number of adults are choosing to stay unpartnered.
He enjoys his independence and is happy being unpartnered at this stage of life.
