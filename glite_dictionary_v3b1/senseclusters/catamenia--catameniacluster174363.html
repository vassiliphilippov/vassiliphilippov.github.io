catamenia

noun
monthly menstrual discharge
Pronunciation: / ˌkætəˈminiə / (AmE), / ˌkatəˈmiːnɪə / (BrE)
the monthly discharge of blood from the uterus of nonpregnant women, occurring from puberty to menopause; menstruation
