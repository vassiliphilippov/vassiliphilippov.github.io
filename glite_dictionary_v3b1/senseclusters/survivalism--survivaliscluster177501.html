survivalism

noun
emergency preparedness
Pronunciation: / sərˈvaɪvəlɪzəm / (AmE), / səˈvaɪvəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice or belief in preparing for emergencies, disasters, or societal collapse by stockpiling supplies and learning survival skills

Examples

Survivalism preparation skills
Survivalism has gained popularity among people concerned about natural disasters.
He became interested in survivalism after reading about major power outages and food shortages.
Many online forums discuss survivalism and share tips on emergency preparedness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prepping - the act of preparing for emergencies by gathering supplies and skills
  • synonym: self-sufficiency - the ability to provide for oneself without outside help
