put down

verb
to euthanize an animal
4.2
Pronunciation: / pʊt daʊn / (AmE), / pʊt daʊn / (BrE)
to kill an animal humanely, usually to end its suffering

Examples

To put down a dog
After the horse broke its leg, the owners agreed to have it put down.
It was a difficult decision, but they had to put down their elderly cat.
The vet advised that the dog should be put down to prevent further suffering.
After years of illness, they decided to put down their old cat to end its pain.
Please put down your bags by the door when you come in.
She carefully put down her cup on the table and smiled.
