eccyesis

noun
pregnancy outside the uterus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛkˈsaɪəsɪs / (AmE), / ɛkˈsaɪəsɪs / (BrE)
pregnancy in which the embryo implants and develops outside the uterus

Examples

An eccyesis pregnancy
An eccyesis can be life-threatening if not diagnosed and treated promptly.
Doctors suspected an eccyesis after the patient reported severe abdominal pain early in pregnancy.
The ultrasound revealed an eccyesis rather than a normal intrauterine pregnancy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ectopic pregnancy - pregnancy occurring outside the uterus, especially in a fallopian tube
