Ignace Paderewski

proper noun
Polish pianist and first Prime Minister of Poland
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈnɑs pædəˈrɛfski / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnɑːs paːdɛˈrɛfski / (BrE)
(1860–1941) Polish pianist, composer, and statesman who was the first Prime Minister of independent Poland in 1919.

Examples

Ignace Paderewski pianist and leader
Ignace Paderewski was renowned both for his virtuosic piano performances and his leadership in Polish politics.
Following World War I, Ignace Paderewski played a key role in restoring Polish independence.
Many music historians regard Ignace Paderewski as one of the most influential pianists of the early 20th century.
