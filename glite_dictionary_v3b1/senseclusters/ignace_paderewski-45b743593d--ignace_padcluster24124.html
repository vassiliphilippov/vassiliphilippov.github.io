Ignace Paderewski

proper noun
Polish pianist and first Prime Minister of Poland
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɪg.nəs pɑː.dər.ˈuː.skiː/ (AmE), /ˈɪg.nəs pɑː.dər.ˈuː.skiː/ (BrE)
(1860–1941) Polish pianist, composer, and statesman who was the first Prime Minister of independent Poland in 1919.

Examples
