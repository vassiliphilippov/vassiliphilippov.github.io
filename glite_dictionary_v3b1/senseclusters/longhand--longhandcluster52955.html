longhand

adjective
ordinary handwritten form (not shorthand)
8.7 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈlɔːŋ.hænd/ (AmE), /ˈlɒŋ.hænd/ (BrE)
(of writing) written by hand in ordinary letters and words, rather than in shorthand or typed form.

Examples

Longhand writing
His longhand notes were much easier to read than her shorthand ones.
She prefers to write her first drafts in longhand before typing them up.
The contract was entirely written out in longhand.
