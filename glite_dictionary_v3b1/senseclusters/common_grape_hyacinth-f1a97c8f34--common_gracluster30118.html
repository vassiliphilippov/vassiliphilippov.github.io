common grape hyacinth

noun
a plant with blue, grape-like flower clusters
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmən ɡreɪp haɪˈæsɪnθ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ɡreɪp haɪˈæsɪnθ / (BrE)
a small, prolific plant of the species Muscari botryoides, having clusters of dark blue, bell-shaped flowers resembling grapes, commonly grown as an ornamental in gardens
