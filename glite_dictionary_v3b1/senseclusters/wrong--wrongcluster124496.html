wrong

adjective
not appropriate for a purpose or occasion
Pronunciation: / rɔŋ / (AmE), / rɒŋ / (BrE)
not suitable or appropriate for a particular purpose or time; happening at an inappropriate moment

Examples

Wrong time
He realized he had worn the wrong shoes for hiking in the mountains.
It was clearly the wrong moment to ask for a raise.
She felt awkward after saying all the wrong things at dinner.
Arriving at the wrong time can spoil even the best-planned surprises.
He made a joke at precisely the wrong moment, which made the situation worse.
She tried to share her idea, but it was clearly the wrong occasion.
