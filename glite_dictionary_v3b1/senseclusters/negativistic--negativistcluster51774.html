negativistic

adjective
resistant to instructions
Pronunciation: / ˌnɛɡətɪˈvɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɛɡətɪˈvɪstɪk / (BrE)
showing persistent resistance, opposition, or refusal to instructions, requests, or authority

Examples

Negativistic behavior
The patient became negativistic and refused to answer any of the nurse's questions.
A negativistic attitude can make therapy sessions more challenging for both the doctor and the patient.
During the assessment, his negativistic behavior included ignoring simple requests and instructions.
The child's negativistic behavior frustrated his teachers during group activities.
Her negativistic responses made it hard to reach any agreement in the meeting.
A negativistic attitude can prevent progress in collaborative projects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliant - willing to follow instructions or requests
  • antonym: cooperative - willing to work with others or follow requests
  • synonym: defiant - openly resisting or challenging authority
