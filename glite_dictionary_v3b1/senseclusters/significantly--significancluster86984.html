significantly

adverb
in a statistically significant way
Pronunciation: / sɪɡˈnɪfɪkəntli / (AmE), / sɪɡˈnɪfɪkəntli / (BrE)
In a way that is statistically significant; to an extent that is unlikely to have occurred by chance.

Examples

Significantly different data
The test results differ significantly from previous studies.
The treatment group improved significantly compared to the control group.
There was no significantly higher risk found in the analysis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insignificantly - in a way that lacks statistical significance
