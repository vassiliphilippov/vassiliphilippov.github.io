Capsella bursa-pastoris

noun
white-flowered weedy plant with triangular pods
9.9
Pronunciation: / kæpˈsɛlə ˌbɜrsə ˈpæstərɪs / (AmE), / kapˈsɛlə ˌbɜːsə ˈpastərɪs / (BrE)
A white-flowered annual plant of the mustard family, native to Europe and widely naturalized as a weed, bearing triangular notched seed pods.
