knock against

verb
collide with something by accident
2.6
Pronunciation: / nɑk əˈɡɛnst / (AmE), / nɒk əˈɡɛn(st) / (BrE)

Examples

To knock against a table

He knocked against a lamppost while looking at his phone.
She accidentally knocked against the vase and it fell to the floor.
