concessionary

adjective
reduced price
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɛʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / kənˈsɛʃənəri / (BrE)
offered at a reduced price, especially for certain groups such as students, seniors, or the disabled

Examples

Concessionary ticket price
Many museums offer concessionary tickets to students and pensioners.
You must show your ID to receive the concessionary rate at the gym.
The concessionary fare makes public transport more affordable for young people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discounted - sold at a lower price than usual
  • antonym: full-price - sold at the regular, non-reduced price
