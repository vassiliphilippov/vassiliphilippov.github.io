Superwoman

proper noun
female superhero character
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a fictional female superhero character, usually depicted as a woman with superhuman powers and heroic abilities

Examples

Superwoman character
The new movie features Superwoman saving the city from disaster.
Many young readers look up to Superwoman as a symbol of strength and courage.
In the comic series, Superwoman teams up with other heroes to fight evil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Superman - a famous male superhero with superhuman powers
  • synonym: heroine - a female hero, especially in stories or comics
