mand

noun
verbal request
Pronunciation: / mænd / (AmE), / mænd / (BrE)
a verbal request or command made to get something, especially in behavioral psychology

Examples

A mand for toy
During the therapy session, the child made a mand for a toy car.
The therapist reinforced each mand to encourage communication skills.
A mand can be as simple as asking for water when thirsty.
In behavioral therapy, a mand is used to teach children how to ask for what they need.
The therapist reinforced the child's mand for a snack by giving him a cookie.
Teaching mands can help individuals with communication difficulties express their wants more effectively.

Related Terms

  • synonym: request - an act of asking for something
