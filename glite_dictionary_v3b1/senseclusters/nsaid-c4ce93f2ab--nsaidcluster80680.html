NSAID

noun
non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnˌɛsˌeɪˌaɪˈdi / (AmE), / ˈɛnˌɛsˌeɪˌaɪˈdi / (BrE)
a drug that reduces inflammation, pain, and fever but does not contain steroids

Examples

A NSAID drug
Ibuprofen is a commonly used NSAID for treating headaches and muscle aches.
Some people experience stomach irritation when taking NSAIDs regularly.
The doctor recommended an NSAID to help manage her joint pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analgesic - drug for pain relief
  • antonym: steroid - a class of compounds with anti-inflammatory properties
