Synonyms

    1.0 40/m

  • auxiliary
    6.0 0.5/m
    an auxiliary supports a team or service in a special role, while a helper is a general person who gives practical aid.
  • aide
    3.5 17/m
    an aide works for a leader or official in a formal, paid role, while a helper is anyone who offers simple help.
  • help
    1.0 17/m
    help can mean a thing or person that makes a task easier, while a helper refers only to a person who gives aid.
