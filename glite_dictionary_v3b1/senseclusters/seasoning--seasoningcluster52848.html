seasoning

noun
substance added to food for flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːzənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːzənɪŋ / (BrE)
a substance (such as salt, herbs, or spices) added to food to improve its flavor, or the act of adding such substances

Examples

Seasoning for food
She added a pinch of seasoning to the soup before serving it.
The recipe calls for minimal seasoning to let the natural taste of the ingredients shine.
You can experiment with different seasonings to create unique flavors in your dishes.
Proper seasoning can turn a simple meal into something delicious.
She pays close attention to the seasoning when preparing her dishes.
The seasoning of the soup made it much more flavorful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: condiment - a substance such as salt or ketchup added to food for flavor
  • synonym: flavoring - something added to food to give it a different taste
  • synonym: herb - a plant used for flavoring or seasoning food
  • synonym: spice - an aromatic substance used to flavor food
