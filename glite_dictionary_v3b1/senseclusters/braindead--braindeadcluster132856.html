braindead

adjective
irreversible brain death
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪnˌdɛd / (AmE), / ˈbreɪnˌdɛd / (BrE)
having suffered an irreversible loss of all brain function and considered clinically or legally dead

Examples

Braindead patient
After extensive testing, the patient was declared braindead by the medical team.
The hospital followed strict protocols before confirming that he was braindead.
Family members struggled to accept the diagnosis that their loved one was braindead.
