country doctor

noun
doctor in rural areas
2.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌn.trɪ ˈdɑːk.tər/ /; / /ˈkʌn.trɪ ˈdɔːk.tər/ (AmE), /ˈkʌn.trɪ ˈdɒk.tə/ (BrE)
a doctor who works and provides medical care in rural areas, often distant from modern hospitals

Examples

A country doctor visits

The country doctor would often travel miles on horseback to visit his patients.
