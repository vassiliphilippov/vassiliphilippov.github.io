(of sound or audio equipment) using two or more channels to reproduce depth or directionality of sound.

Examples

Stereo sound
He bought a new set of stereo speakers for his home theater.
Some older recordings are only available in mono, not stereo.
The concert was broadcast in stereo for a more immersive listening experience.

Synonyms

  • stereophonic
    6.8 0.07/m
    stereophonic uses separate audio channels to place sounds around the listener, while stereo is the common term for directional multi-channel sound.
  • two-channel
    5.0 0.28/m
    two-channel uses exactly two separate audio channels for left and right sound, while stereo can mean general multi-channel directional sound.
