voiceless

adjective
deprived of rights, especially voting
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔɪsləs /; / ˈvɔɪs.ləs / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪslɪs /; / ˈvɔɪs.lɪs / (BrE)
