patty

noun
small flattened piece of food
Pronunciation: / ˈpæti / (AmE), / ˈpæti / (BrE)
A small, flat mass of chopped or ground food, such as meat or vegetables, often shaped and cooked for eating.

Examples

A beef patty
He placed a beef patty on the grill and waited for it to sizzle.
The chef shaped the salmon mixture into a patty before frying it.
Vegetarians often substitute a bean patty for meat in their burgers.
She ordered a vegetable pattie with her lunch.
The chef prepared spicy chicken patties for the buffet.
He enjoyed a hot pattie served with fresh salad on the side.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: burger - a sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat, usually beef, in a sliced bun
  • synonym: croquette - a small roll of chopped food, typically meat or fish, coated in breadcrumbs and fried
  • synonym: patty - a small, flat cake of minced food, especially meat
