fuss-budget

noun
person who worries about minor problems
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌsˌbʌdʒɪt / (AmE), / ˈfʌsˌbʌdʒɪt / (BrE)
a person who worries excessively or needlessly, often about unimportant matters

Examples

A fuss-budget person
Don’t be a fuss-budget; everything will turn out just fine.
My grandmother is such a fuss-budget that she checks the locks three times every night.
The teacher, known as a fuss-budget, worried about every tiny detail before the event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm person - person who is generally calm and unconcerned
  • synonym: worrywart - person who worries excessively
