stand at attention

verb
stand upright formally, as in the military
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /stænd ə ə.ˈten.ʃən/ /; / /stænd æt ə.ˈten.ʃən/ (AmE), /stænd ə ə.ˈten.ʃən/ /; / /stænd æt ə.ˈten.ʃən/ (BrE)
to stand upright with feet together and arms at the sides in a formal, disciplined manner, especially as a military posture

Examples

To stand at attention firmly
He practiced standing at attention until he could do it perfectly.
The new cadets were told to stand at attention as the officer entered the room.
When the flag was raised, everyone turned and stood at attention.

Related Terms
