yahrzeit

noun
memorial candle
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑrtsaɪt /; / ˈjɑrˌtsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈjɑːtsaɪt / (BrE)
the anniversary of a person's death in Jewish tradition, often marked by lighting a memorial candle

Examples

Light a yahrzeit candle
Each year, the family gathers to light a yahrzeit for their grandfather.
She placed the yahrzeit on the windowsill and remembered her father.
Lighting a yahrzeit is an important tradition in many Jewish households.
Each year, the family lights a candle on the yahrzeit of their grandfather.
The synagogue held a special service to mark the yahrzeit of a beloved community member.
She recited prayers at home on her mother's yahrzeit to honor her memory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anniversary - the yearly recurrence of a date marking an event
  • synonym: memorial - an event or object to remember someone who has died
  • synonym: memorial candle - a candle lit in remembrance of someone who has died
