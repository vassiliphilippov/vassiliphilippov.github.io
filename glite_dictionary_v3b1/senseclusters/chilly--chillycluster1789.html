chilly

noun
a hot, pungent pepper
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪli / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪli / (BrE)
A very hot, finely tapering pepper known for its special pungency; an alternate spelling of 'chili'.

Examples

A red chilly
A single chilly can make a whole pot of stew spicy.
She added a finely sliced chilly to the curry for extra heat.
The market stall sold dried chilly peppers of all shapes and sizes.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bell pepper - a mild, sweet pepper of the species Capsicum annuum
  • synonym: chili - a hot pepper of the species Capsicum, also spelled 'chilli'
  • degree higher: habanero - an extremely hot variety of chili pepper
