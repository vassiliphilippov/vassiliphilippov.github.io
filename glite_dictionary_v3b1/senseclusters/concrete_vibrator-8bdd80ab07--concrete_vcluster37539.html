concrete vibrator

noun
device for consolidating poured concrete
5.3 6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːŋˌkriːt vaɪˈbreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋkriːt vaɪˈbreɪtə / (BrE)
A mechanical device used to consolidate freshly poured concrete, removing trapped air and excess water to ensure it sets firmly in formwork.

Examples

A concrete vibrator tool
After pouring the concrete, the crew operated the concrete vibrator along the formwork.
Proper use of a concrete vibrator helps prevent weak spots in the finished structure.
The workers used a concrete vibrator to ensure the foundation was free of air pockets.
