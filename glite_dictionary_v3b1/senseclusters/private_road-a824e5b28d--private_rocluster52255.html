private road

noun
road to a private property
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪvət roʊd / (AmE), / ˈpraɪvət rəʊd / (BrE)
a road that is owned and maintained by a private individual or organization, typically leading to a private property such as a house

Examples

A private road
A gate blocks entry to the private road after 6 PM.
Only residents are allowed to use the private road that leads to the estate.
They installed new signs to indicate that it is a private road and not open to the public.

Related Terms

  • synonym: driveway - a short road leading from the street to a house or garage
