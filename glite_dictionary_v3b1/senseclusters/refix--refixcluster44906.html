refix

verb
to fix again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈfɪks / (AmE), / ˌriːˈfɪks / (BrE)
to fix or repair something again after it has already been fixed once

Examples

To refix a problem
After the first attempt failed, the plumber had to refix the leaking faucet.
She needed to refix the loose button that kept falling off her coat.
The mechanic promised to refix the engine if the problem returned.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repair - to fix something that is broken or damaged
  • synonym: restore - to bring something back to its original condition
