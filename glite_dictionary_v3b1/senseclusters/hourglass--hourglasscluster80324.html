hourglass

noun
timing device with sand, runs for an hour
4.1 1.31/m
Pronunciation: / Ààa ä…öÀå…°l√¶s / (AmE), / Ààa ä…ôÀå…°l…ëÀês / (BrE)
A device consisting of two glass bulbs connected vertically by a narrow neck, with sand flowing from the upper bulb to the lower in exactly one hour, used to measure time intervals.

Examples

An hourglass timer
She turned the hourglass over to start timing her speech.
The antique hourglass on the mantel was filled with fine white sand.
They used an hourglass during the game to keep track of each player's turn.

Synonyms

  • sandglass
    8.1 0.01/m
    sandglass is an older or less common term for the same two-bulb time device, while hourglass is the usual modern word.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sandglass - a device for measuring time by the flow of sand
  • synonym: timer - a device that measures or records the amount of time taken
