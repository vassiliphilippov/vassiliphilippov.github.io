Examples

To recollect a memory

Synonyms

  • recall

    recall is a common verb used to bring past facts or events to mind, while recollect often suggests active effort.
  • remember
    1.0 396/m
    remember is the everyday verb for keeping past facts or events in mind, while recollect sounds more formal or effortful.
  • retrieve
    4.1 3.98/m
    retrieve is often used in tests or technical talk to get information back, while recollect means remember from personal memory.
  • call up
    3.2 1.09/m

  • call back
    2.3 0.18/m

  • bring back
    3.0 7.20/m
    bring back is used when something causes a person to remember, while recollect often means you bring memories up by trying.
