Mongol empire

proper noun
historic Asian empire founded by Genghis Khan
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑŋˌɡoʊl ˈɛmˌpaɪər / (AmE), / ˈmɒŋˌɡəʊl ˈɛmpaɪə / (BrE)
the vast empire founded by Genghis Khan in the 13th century, which at its height extended across much of Asia and into Europe

Examples

The Mongol empire expansion
The Mongol empire was the largest contiguous land empire in history.
The documentary explored the history of the Mongol empire.
Trade along the Silk Road flourished under the rule of the Mongol empire.
