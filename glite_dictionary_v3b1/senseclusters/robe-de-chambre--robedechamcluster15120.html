robe-de-chambre

noun
dressing gown or lounging robe
Pronunciation: / ˌroʊb də ˈʃɑːmbrə / (AmE), / ˌrəʊb də ˈʃɑːmbrə / (BrE)
A long, loose garment worn over nightwear before dressing or while lounging at home; a dressing gown.

Examples

A robe-de-chambre at home
After a hot bath, he wrapped himself in a plush robe-de-chambre.
He greeted his guests at breakfast still wearing his robe-de-chambre.
The old portrait depicted her in an elegant silk robe-de-chambre.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bathrobe - a loose, absorbent robe worn after bathing
  • synonym: dressing gown - a long, loose robe worn inside the house, especially before getting dressed
