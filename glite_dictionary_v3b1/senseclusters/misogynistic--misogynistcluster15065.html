misogynistic

adjective
hating or prejudiced against women
8.4
Pronunciation: / mɪˌsɑːdʒəˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / mɪˌsɒdʒəˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
Showing hatred, dislike, or prejudice against women.

Examples

Misogynistic remarks
His misogynistic remarks caused outrage among his colleagues.
She spoke out against the misogynistic attitudes prevalent in the industry.
The movie was criticized for its misogynistic portrayal of female characters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: feminist - advocating women's rights and equality
  • synonym: misogyny - dislike of, contempt for, or ingrained prejudice against women
  • synonym: sexist - discriminatory based on sex, especially against women
