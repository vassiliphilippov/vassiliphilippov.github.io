myna

noun
tropical Asian starling bird
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪ.nə / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.nə / (BrE)
A bird of the starling family native to tropical Asia, known for its loud calls and ability to mimic sounds.

Examples

A myna bird
A myna perched on the balcony and began mimicking the sound of a ringing phone.
In parts of India, the common myna is often seen scavenging for food near markets.
The pair adopted a rescued myna that learned to say simple greetings.
