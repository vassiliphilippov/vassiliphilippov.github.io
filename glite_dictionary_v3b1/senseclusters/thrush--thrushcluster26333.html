thrush

noun
songbird with spotted breast
Pronunciation: / θrʌʃ / (AmE), / θrʌʃ / (BrE)
A small to medium-sized songbird, typically having brownish upper feathers and a spotted breast, found in many parts of the world.

Examples

A singing thrush
A wood thrush was singing its distinctive song in the early morning.
The brown thrush builds its nest in low trees or shrubs.
We spotted several thrushes pecking for worms after the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blackbird - a common European thrush with all-black plumage
  • synonym: robin - a type of thrush with a red breast
