chimney corner

noun
corner by a fireplace
6.5 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɪm.niː ˈkɔːr.nər/ /; / /ˈtʃɪm.ə.niː ˈkɔːr.nər/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɪm.niː ˈkɔː.nə/ /; / /ˈtʃɪm.ə.niː ˈkɔː.nə/ (BrE)
