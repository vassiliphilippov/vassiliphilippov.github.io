loam

verb
to cover with loam
Pronunciation: / loʊm / (AmE), / ləʊm / (BrE)
to cover or treat a surface with loam, a mixture of sand, silt, and clay used in building or gardening

Examples

To loam a wall
The builders loamed the cottage walls to improve insulation and keep the interior cool.
In traditional construction, workers often loam the exterior to protect against harsh weather.
After the repairs, the team carefully loamed the surface to restore its original appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plaster - to cover a surface with a smooth mixture for protection or decoration
