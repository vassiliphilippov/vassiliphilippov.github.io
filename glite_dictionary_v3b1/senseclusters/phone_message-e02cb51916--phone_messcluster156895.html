phone message

noun
message relayed by telephone
Pronunciation: / foʊn ˈmɛsɪdʒ / (AmE), / fəʊn ˈmɛsɪdʒ / (BrE)
a message transmitted or received by telephone, especially one left on an answering machine or voicemail system

Examples

Phone message left
I missed your phone message while I was out of the office.
Please check your phone messages as soon as you return.
She left a detailed phone message for her doctor about her symptoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: voicemail - electronic system that stores phone messages
