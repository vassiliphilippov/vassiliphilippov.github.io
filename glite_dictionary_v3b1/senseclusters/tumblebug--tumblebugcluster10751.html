tumblebug

noun
a dung beetle that rolls dung
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌm.bəlˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈtʌm.bəlˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
A type of dung beetle that rolls balls of dung, often for food or as a breeding chamber.

Examples

A tumblebug beetle
Children watched as a pair of tumblebugs struggled with their prize.
Farmers appreciate the work of the tumblebug in keeping their fields clean.
The tumblebug rolled a large ball of dung across the dusty path.

Related Terms
