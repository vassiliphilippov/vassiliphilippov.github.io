good night

noun
evening or bedtime farewell
1.5
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd ˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈnaɪt / (BrE)
A polite or conventional expression used to bid someone farewell in the evening or before going to sleep.

Examples

A warm good night
After finishing their phone call, they both said good night and hung up.
He waved to his friends and wished everyone a good night as he left the party.
She kissed her children and softly said, good night before turning off the light.

Related Terms

  • antonym: good morning - a conventional greeting said in the morning
  • synonym: goodbye - a conventional expression used when parting
