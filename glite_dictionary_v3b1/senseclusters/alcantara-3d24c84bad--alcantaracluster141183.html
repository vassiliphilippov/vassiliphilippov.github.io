Alcantara

proper noun
river in western Spain
Pronunciation: / alˈkɑːntərə / (AmE), / alˈkæntərə / (BrE)
a river in western Spain that flows through Extremadura and joins the Tagus River

Examples

Alcantara river Spain
The Alcantara is known for its scenic beauty as it winds through the Spanish countryside.
Many villages along the Alcantara depend on the river for water and agriculture.
Tourists often visit the Alcantara to enjoy canoeing and hiking in Extremadura.
