gendering

noun
assignment of grammatical gender
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛndərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛndərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of assigning or marking grammatical gender to words, such as classifying nouns as masculine, feminine, or neuter

Examples

Gendering of nouns
The gendering of nouns in German can be challenging for language learners.
Some languages are moving away from strict gendering of pronouns to promote inclusivity.
The textbook explains the historical roots of gendering in Romance languages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gender assignment - the act of designating a grammatical gender to a word
  • antonym: gender neutrality - the absence of gender distinctions in language
