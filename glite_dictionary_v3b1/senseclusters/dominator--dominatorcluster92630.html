dominator

noun
one who controls
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːməˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪˌneɪtə / (BrE)
a person, thing, or node that dominates or has control over others, especially in a system or structure

Examples

In the control flow graph, each dominator determines the structure of the program.
The chess champion was known as a dominator in the world of competitive play.
In the ecosystem, the lion is often seen as the dominator among predators.
