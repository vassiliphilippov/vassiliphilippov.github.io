insensately

adverb
without sensation or rational feeling
2.8 9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛnˌseɪtli / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛnsətli / (BrE)
In a way that lacks physical sensation, consciousness, or rational feeling; without reason or sensitivity.

Examples
