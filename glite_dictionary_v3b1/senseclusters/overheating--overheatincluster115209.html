overheating

noun
excessive heating
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈhitɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈhiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
The process of becoming excessively hot or causing something to become excessively hot.

Examples

Device overheating problem
Prolonged overheating of electronic devices can reduce their lifespan.
The engine shut down to prevent overheating during the long drive.
Wearing heavy clothes in hot weather can cause overheating during exercise.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cooling - the process of making something less hot
