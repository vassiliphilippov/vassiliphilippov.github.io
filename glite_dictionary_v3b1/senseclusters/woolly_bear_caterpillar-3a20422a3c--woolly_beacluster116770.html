woolly bear caterpillar

noun
bristly, hairy caterpillar of various moths
6.7 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈwʊl.ɪ beər ˈkæt.ə.pɪl.ər/ (AmE), /ˈwʊl.ɪ beə ˈkæt.ə.pɪl.ə/ (BrE)
a caterpillar of several moth species, distinguished by a dense covering of bristly hairs; some species feed on plants and can be harmful to crops
