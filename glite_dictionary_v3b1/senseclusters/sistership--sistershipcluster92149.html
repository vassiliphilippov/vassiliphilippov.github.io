sistership

noun
association of women with shared interest
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəʃɪp / (BrE)
an association or society of women united by a common religion, trade, or interest

Examples

Women's sistership group
The sistership held monthly meetings to support women in their community.
The charitable sistership welcomed new members from various professions.
They formed a sistership dedicated to promoting women's rights in the workplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sisterhood - the relationship between sisters or a society of women associated for a common purpose
