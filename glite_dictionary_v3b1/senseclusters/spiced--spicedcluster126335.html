spiced

adjective
made more lively
Pronunciation: / spaɪst / (AmE), / spaɪst / (BrE)

Examples

Spiced story

His stories are always spiced with humor, making them enjoyable to listen to.
The teacher's lessons were spiced with real-life examples to help students understand the material.
