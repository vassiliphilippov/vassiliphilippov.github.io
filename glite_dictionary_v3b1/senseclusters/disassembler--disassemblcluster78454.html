disassembler

noun
machine for taking apart
8.3
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ə.ˈsem.blər/ (AmE), /dɪs.ə.ˈsem.blə/ (BrE)
a machine or tool designed to take apart objects into their component parts, often used in manufacturing or recycling

Examples

Industrial disassembler
The company invested in a new disassembler to process old appliances for recycling.
A robotic disassembler can quickly separate electronic devices into reusable materials.
The technician operated the disassembler to break down the machinery into its basic parts.
