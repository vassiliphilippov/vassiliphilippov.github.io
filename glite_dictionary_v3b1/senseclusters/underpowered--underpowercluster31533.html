underpowered

adjective
not strong enough
5.5
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dər.ˈpaʊ.ərd/ /; / /ʌn.dər.ˈpaʊrd/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.ˈpaʊ.əd/ /; / /ʌn.də.ˈpaʊd/ (BrE)
not having enough power, strength, or effectiveness to do something well

Examples

An underpowered study
