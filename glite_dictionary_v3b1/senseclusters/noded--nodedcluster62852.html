noded

adjective
having nodes
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊdɪd / (AmE), / ˈnəʊdɪd / (BrE)
having nodes or jointed points along its length, especially referring to stems or structures in plants

Examples

Noded bamboo stem
The bamboo stem was noded at regular intervals.
Some grasses have noded stalks that help support their height.
The scientist examined the noded structure of the plant under a microscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jointed - having joints or segments
  • antonym: smooth - having an even surface without nodes or joints
