leaf

noun
main photosynthetic organ of a plant
1.0 13/m
Pronunciation: / lif / (AmE), / liÀêf / (BrE)
the flat, typically green part of a plant that grows from a stem or branch and is the main organ for photosynthesis and transpiration.

Examples

A green leaf
Photosynthesis primarily takes place in the leaf of the plant.
She pressed the leaf between the pages of her book to preserve it.
The maple tree's leaves turned brilliant red in autumn.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: needle - a slender, pointed leaf found in many conifers
  • antonym: petal - a part of a flower, often brightly colored but not a true leaf
