gobo

noun
sound-absorbing panel
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊboʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊbəʊ / (BrE)
a portable screen or panel used in recording studios or on sets to block or absorb unwanted sound and control acoustics

Examples

Studio gobo panel
The sound engineer placed a gobo between the guitar amp and the drum kit to reduce noise bleed.
During the recording session, several gobos were arranged around the vocalist for better sound isolation.
A portable gobo can make a big difference in the acoustics of a small studio.
