local governance

noun
administration of local government
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊkəl ˈɡʌvərnəns / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl ˈɡʌvənəns / (BrE)
the administration and management of local government within a specific area or community

Examples

Effective local governance

Issues with local governance often impact the delivery of public services.
