bonelessness

noun
absence of bones
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊnləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having no bones or lacking firmness or structure

Examples

The bonelessness of chicken
The bonelessness of the chicken made it perfect for sandwiches and salads.
He was surprised by the bonelessness of the toy, which could bend in any direction.
The bonelessness of the fish fillet made it easy for children to eat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rigidity - the quality of being stiff or firm
  • synonym: softness - the quality of being soft or lacking firmness
