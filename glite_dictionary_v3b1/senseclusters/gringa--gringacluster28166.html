gringa

noun
Mexican tortilla dish
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈgriːŋ.gə/ /; / /ˈgrɪŋ.gə/ (AmE), /ˈgrɪŋ.gə/ /; / /ˈgriːŋ.gə/ (BrE)
