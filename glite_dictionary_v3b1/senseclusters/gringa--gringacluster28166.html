gringa

noun
Mexican tortilla dish
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪŋɡə / (BrE)
a Mexican dish made with a flour tortilla filled with cheese and often spiced pork, folded and grilled

Examples

A cheesy gringa
At the street market, I tried a delicious gringa filled with melted cheese and marinated pork.
The restaurant is famous for its gringa, which is always served hot and fresh.
He recommended ordering a gringa if you enjoy cheesy, savory Mexican food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quesadilla - a Mexican dish of a tortilla filled with cheese and other ingredients, then grilled
  • synonym: taco al pastor - a Mexican taco filled with spiced pork cooked on a spit
