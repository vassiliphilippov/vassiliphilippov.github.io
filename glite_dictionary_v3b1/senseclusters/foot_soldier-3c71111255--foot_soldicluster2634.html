foot soldier

noun
infantry soldier who fights on foot
1.8
Pronunciation: / fʊt ˈsoʊldʒɚ /; / fʊt ˈsoʊl.dʒɚ / (AmE), / fʊt ˈsəʊldʒə /; / fʊt ˈsəʊl.dʒə / (BrE)
a soldier who fights on foot, or an ordinary member who does basic or routine work in a group or organization
