keelboatman

noun
keelboat operator
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːlˌboʊtmən / (AmE), / ˈkiːlˌbəʊtmən / (BrE)
a person who works on or operates a keelboat, especially on rivers or inland waterways

Examples

The keelboatman navigates rivers
The experienced keelboatman navigated the tricky currents with ease.
In the early 1800s, a keelboatman played a vital role in river transport.
Each keelboatman was responsible for loading and unloading cargo at every stop.
