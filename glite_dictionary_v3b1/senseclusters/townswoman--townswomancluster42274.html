townswoman

noun
a female resident of a town
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊnˌwʊmən /; / ˈtaʊnzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈtaʊnˌwʊmən /; / ˈtaʊnzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who lives in or comes from a town or city
