dialectal

adjective
relating to a dialect
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəˈlɛktl̩ / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈlɛktl̩ / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a dialect, especially as distinguished from the standard language

Examples

Dialectal expressions
Dialectal expressions can reveal much about a speaker's regional background.
The linguist noted several dialectal differences between the two nearby villages.
This word is considered dialectal and is rarely used in standard English.
