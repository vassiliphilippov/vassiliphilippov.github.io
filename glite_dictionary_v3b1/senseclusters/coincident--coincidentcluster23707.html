coincident

adjective
occupying the same position; matching exactly
Pronunciation: / koʊˈɪnsɪdənt / (AmE), / kəʊˈɪnsɪdənt / (BrE)
(of two or more objects) occupying exactly the same space or position at the same time; matching point for point.

Examples

Coincident points
After careful adjustment, the laser beams became coincident at the focal point.
The two graphs are coincident along this segment.
When superimposed, the circles appear perfectly coincident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concurrent - occurring or existing simultaneously
  • antonym: separate - not touching or occupying the same space
