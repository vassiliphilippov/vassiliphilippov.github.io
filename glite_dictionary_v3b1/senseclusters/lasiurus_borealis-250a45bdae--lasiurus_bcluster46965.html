Lasiurus borealis

noun
North American brick-red bat species
Pronunciation: / læˈsaɪjʊrəs bɔːˈreɪəlɪs / (AmE), / læˈsɪjʊərəs bɔːˈriːəlɪs / (BrE)
A North American bat species with brick-red or rusty red fur tipped in white, commonly known as the eastern red bat.
