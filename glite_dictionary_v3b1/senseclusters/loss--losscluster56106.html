loss

noun
the act of losing someone or something
Pronunciation: / lɔs / (AmE), / lɒs / (BrE)
the act or process of losing someone or something; the state of being deprived of a person or thing; the harm or disadvantage that results from this

Examples

A loss in competition
After his loss in the final, he vowed to train even harder.
She was saddened by the loss of her childhood friend.
The company reported a significant loss in revenue this quarter.
After the accident, he felt the loss of his independence deeply.
Her sudden departure was a significant loss to the company.
The team suffered a great loss when their captain retired.
