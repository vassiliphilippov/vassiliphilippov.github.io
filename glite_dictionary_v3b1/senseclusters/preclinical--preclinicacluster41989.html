preclinical

adjective
before human testing
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈklɪnɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈklɪnɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the stage of research or study before testing on humans, especially in medicine or drug development

Examples

Preclinical drug testing
The vaccine is currently in the preclinical stage and has not yet been tested on people.
Researchers spent years on preclinical studies before moving to human trials.
Many potential treatments fail during the preclinical phase due to safety concerns.
