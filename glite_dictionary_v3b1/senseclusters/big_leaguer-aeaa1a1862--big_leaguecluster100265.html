big leaguer

noun
top professional in a field (informal)
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ ˈliɡər / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈliːɡə / (BrE)
Informal. A very successful or highly ranked professional in a particular field; someone who operates at the top level of their profession.
