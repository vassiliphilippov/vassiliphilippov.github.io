plat

verb
draw a map or plan of land
9.2 8.5
Pronunciation: /plæt/ (AmE), /plæt/ (BrE)
to make a map or plan of (an area of land), especially for the purpose of dividing it into lots

Examples

To plat a property
Developers must plat the land according to city regulations before selling lots.
The company hired a surveyor to plat the new subdivision before construction began.
The town was carefully platted with wide streets and public parks.
