unornamented

adjective
not decorated; plain
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈɔrnəˌmɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈɔːnəməntɪd / (BrE)
not decorated or embellished; plain or lacking ornamentation.

Examples

Plain unornamented style
She preferred unornamented prose that got straight to the point.
The building’s facade is strikingly unornamented, emphasizing clean lines and open space.
The living room was intentionally left unornamented to create a sense of minimalist calm.

Related Terms
