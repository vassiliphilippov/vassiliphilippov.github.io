lickspittle

noun
servile flatterer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkˌspɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪkˌspɪtəl / (BrE)
a person who flatters or behaves in an overly submissive way toward someone in authority to gain favor

Examples

A lickspittle at work
The new manager surrounded himself with lickspittles who never challenged his decisions.
She refused to become a lickspittle just to get a promotion at work.
History is full of lickspittles who gained power by flattering those above them.

Related Terms

  • antonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or opposes someone in authority
  • synonym: sycophant - a person who acts obsequiously toward someone important
  • synonym: toady - a person who flatters or ingratiates themselves with someone important
