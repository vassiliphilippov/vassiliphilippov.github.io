parton

noun
subatomic component
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrtɑn / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtɒn / (BrE)
a subatomic particle that is a constituent of protons and neutrons, such as a quark or gluon

Examples

A parton inside proton
Physicists use powerful accelerators to study the properties of a parton inside a proton.
The theory explains how each parton contributes to the mass of a neutron.
Recent experiments have revealed new details about the behavior of partons in atomic nuclei.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gluon - a particle that binds quarks together inside protons and neutrons
  • synonym: quark - a fundamental particle that makes up protons and neutrons
