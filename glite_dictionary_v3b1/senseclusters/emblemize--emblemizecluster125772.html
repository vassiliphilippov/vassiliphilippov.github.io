emblemize

verb
to mark with emblem
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmbləˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛmblɪmaɪz / (BrE)
to decorate or mark something with an emblem or to represent symbolically with an emblem.

Examples

To emblemize a mural
The artist will emblemize the mural with the city's official seal.
They decided to emblemize the uniforms to show team spirit.
The new coins emblemize the nation's rich cultural heritage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decorate - to make something look more attractive by adding items to it
  • synonym: symbolize - to represent or stand for something as a symbol
