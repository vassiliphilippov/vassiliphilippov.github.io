white globe lily

noun
globe lily with egg-shaped white flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / waɪt ɡloʊb ˈlɪli / (AmE), / waɪt ɡləʊb ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A type of globe lily found in southern California, with open branched clusters of egg-shaped white flowers.
