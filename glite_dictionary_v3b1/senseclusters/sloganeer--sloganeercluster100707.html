sloganeer

noun
person who creates and uses slogans
Pronunciation: / ˌsloʊɡəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌsləʊɡəˈnɪə / (BrE)
a person who invents and uses slogans, especially to promote a cause, idea, or product

The political sloganeer came up with a catchy phrase that was repeated throughout the campaign.
