nonsporting

noun
dog show category
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈspɔrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈspɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a category in dog shows for breeds not used for hunting or sporting purposes

Examples

Nonsporting group
The poodle is one of the most popular breeds in the nonsporting group.
At the dog show, the nonsporting category included breeds of various sizes and temperaments.
Judges awarded the top prize in the nonsporting group to a well-groomed bulldog.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sporting - a dog show group for breeds used in hunting and retrieving
  • antonym: working - a dog show group for breeds developed for jobs such as guarding or rescue
