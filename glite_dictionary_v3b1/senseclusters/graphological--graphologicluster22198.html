graphological

adjective
handwriting analysis
Pronunciation: / ˌɡræfəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɡræfəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study or analysis of handwriting or written symbols

Examples

Graphological analysis of handwriting
The detective requested a graphological report to verify the authenticity of the letter.
Her research focused on the graphological features of medieval manuscripts.
A graphological approach can reveal unique aspects of a person's handwriting style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graphology - the study of handwriting, especially for personality analysis
  • synonym: handwriting analysis - the examination of handwriting to determine characteristics or authenticity
