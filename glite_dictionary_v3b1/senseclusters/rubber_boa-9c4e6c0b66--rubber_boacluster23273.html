rubber boa

noun
a small, smooth-skinned North American snake
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbər ˈboʊə / (AmE), / ˈrʌbə ˈbəʊə / (BrE)
a small, non-venomous snake (Charina bottae) native to western North America, noted for its smooth, rubbery appearance and similar-looking head and tail

Examples

The rubber boa snake
Despite its name, the rubber boa is a real snake, not a toy.
The rubber boa is known to be gentle and rarely bites when handled.
We found a rubber boa curling under a log during our hike.
