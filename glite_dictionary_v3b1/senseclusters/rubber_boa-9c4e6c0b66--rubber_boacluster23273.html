rubber boa

noun
a small, smooth-skinned North American snake
9.9 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌb.ər ˈboʊ.ə/ /; / /ˈrʌb.ər boʊ/ (AmE), /ˈrʌb.ə ˈbəʊ.ə/ /; / /ˈrʌb.ə bɔː/ (BrE)
