poignantly

adverb
in an emotionally touching manner
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪnjəntli / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪnjəntli / (BrE)
In a way that arouses deep emotion, especially sadness or sympathy; touchingly.

Examples

Written poignantly
His letter was written poignantly, expressing both regret and hope.
She gazed at the old photographs poignantly, remembering happier times.
The film ends poignantly, leaving the audience silent in reflection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: touchingly - in a way that evokes strong emotion or sympathy
