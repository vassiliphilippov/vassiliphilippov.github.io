Rudbeckia serotina

noun
species of yellow-flowered plant, state flower of Maryland
Pronunciation: / rʌdˈbɛkiə sərˈoʊtɪnə / (AmE), / rʌdˈbɛkɪə sərˈəʊtɪnə / (BrE)
A species of flowering plant with daisylike yellow to orange flowers and dark centers; the state flower of Maryland, native to central and southeastern United States.

Examples

Rudbeckia serotina flower
In Maryland, Rudbeckia serotina is celebrated as the official state flower.
Many wildflower meadows in the southeastern United States are filled with Rudbeckia serotina.
The Rudbeckia serotina brightened the garden with its vivid yellow petals and dark center.
