Cistus ladanum

noun
shrub yielding fragrant resin used in perfumes
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstəs ləˈdænəm / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəs ləˈdeɪnəm / (BrE)
A shrub with white flowers and sticky stems and leaves, yielding a fragrant resin used in perfumes, especially as a fixative.

Examples
