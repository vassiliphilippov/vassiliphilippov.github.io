yoni

noun
Hindu female symbol
Pronunciation: / ˈjoʊni / (AmE), / ˈjəʊni / (BrE)
a symbol in Hinduism representing the female generative power, often associated with the goddess Shakti

A sacred yoni symbol

In Hindu rituals, the yoni is honored as a symbol of creation and life.
Scholars discussed the cultural significance of the yoni in religious art and architecture.
