bubby

noun
informal friend term
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌbi / (AmE), / ˈbʌbi / (BrE)
an affectionate or familiar term of address for a friend or companion, similar to 'pal' or 'buddy', used informally

Examples

Hey bubby!
Hey bubby, could you give me a hand carrying these bags upstairs?
After not seeing each other for years, he greeted his old bubby with a big smile.
You can always count on your bubby to cheer you up when you are feeling down.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buddy - an informal term for a friend or companion
  • synonym: pal - a close friend; an informal term for a companion
