absolved

adjective
freed from blame or guilt
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɑlvd / (AmE), / əbˈzɒlvd / (BrE)
freed from blame, responsibility, or guilt

Examples

Absolved of guilt
After years of investigation, she was finally absolved of any wrongdoing.
He felt absolved after telling the truth to his family.
The committee declared that the company was absolved from all responsibility in the matter.

Related Terms
