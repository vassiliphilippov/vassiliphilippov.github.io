irregularity

noun
violation of rules or norms
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˌrɛɡjəˈlærəti / (AmE), / ɪˌrɛɡjʊˈlarəti / (BrE)
a behavior or action that violates a rule, etiquette, custom, or moral principle

Examples

Irregularity in procedure
Any irregularity in the admission process must be reported immediately.
He was suspended after allegations of irregularity in his conduct during the exam.
The investigation uncovered several irregularities in the company's financial records.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misconduct - improper or unlawful behavior
  • synonym: transgression - an act that goes against a law or rule
