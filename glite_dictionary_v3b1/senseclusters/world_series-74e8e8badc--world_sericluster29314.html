World Series

noun
Major League Baseball championship series
3.0
Pronunciation: / wɜrld ˈsɪriz / (AmE), / wɜːld ˈsɪəriːz / (BrE)
the annual championship series of Major League Baseball in North America, played between the champions of the American League and the National League
