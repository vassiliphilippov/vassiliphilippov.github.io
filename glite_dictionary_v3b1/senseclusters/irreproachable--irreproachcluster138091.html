irreproachable

adjective
impossible to criticize; blameless
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrəˈproʊtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈprəʊtʃəbəl / (BrE)
So good or blameless as to not deserve any criticism or blame.

Examples

An irreproachable character
Her irreproachable conduct earned her the trust of everyone in the community.
His honesty and irreproachable morals were admired by friends and colleagues alike.
The lawyer presented an irreproachable record throughout his entire career.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blameless - not deserving blame; free from guilt
  • antonym: reproachable - deserving blame or criticism
  • synonym: unimpeachable - not able to be doubted, questioned, or criticized; entirely trustworthy
