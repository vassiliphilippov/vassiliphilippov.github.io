keto

noun
low-carb diet
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkiːtəʊ / (BrE)
a diet that is very low in carbohydrates and high in fat, intended to promote ketosis in the body

Examples

On keto
After reading about its benefits, he switched to a keto to improve his energy levels.
Many people find it challenging to maintain a strict keto for long periods.
Her doctor recommended trying a keto to help manage her blood sugar.

Related Terms

  • antonym: high-carb diet - a diet high in carbohydrates, opposite of keto
  • synonym: ketogenic diet - a diet high in fat and low in carbohydrates to induce ketosis
  • synonym: low-carb diet - a diet that restricts carbohydrates, such as those found in sugary foods and bread
