chrysopoeia

noun
alchemical gold treatise
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪsəˈpiːə / (AmE), / ˌkrɪsəˈpiːə / (BrE)
a written work or treatise on the making or transmutation of gold, especially in alchemy

Examples

A chrysopoeia manuscript
The ancient manuscript contained a detailed chrysopoeia outlining the steps to create gold.
Scholars studied the chrysopoeia to understand historical beliefs about alchemy.
A rare chrysopoeia was discovered in the library's collection of medieval texts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alchemy - a medieval practice aiming to transform base metals into gold
  • synonym: transmutation - the act of changing one substance into another, especially base metals into gold
