passion

noun
strong feeling or emotion
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpæʃ.ən /; / ˈpæʃən / (AmE), / ˈpæʃ.ən /; / ˈpæʃən / (BrE)
a very strong feeling or emotion, such as love, anger, enthusiasm, or a powerful desire that drives someone to act

Examples

Speak with passion
He spoke with great passion about the need for social change.
Her passion for music was evident in everything she did.
The artist poured all his passion into his latest painting.
Even after many years, she still expressed every opinion with the same youthful passion.
He is known for approaching his work with incredible passion and dedication.
Her passion for music was evident in the way she spoke about her favorite composers.
After years of searching, she finally found her true passion in teaching.
He pursued his goals with a passion that inspired everyone around him.
Her passion for painting was evident in every canvas she created.

  • antonym: apathy - lack of interest, enthusiasm, or concern

  • synonym: desire - a strong feeling of wanting to have something or wishing for something to happen
  • synonym: emotion - a strong feeling such as love, anger, joy, hate, or fear
  • synonym: enthusiasm - intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval
  • antonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern
  • synonym: obsession - an idea or thought that continually preoccupies or intrudes on a person's mind
