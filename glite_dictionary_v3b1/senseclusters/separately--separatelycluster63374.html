separately

adverb
apart from others; individually
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛpɚətli / (AmE), / ˈsɛpərətli / (BrE)
In a way that is apart from others; individually rather than together.

Examples

To do separately
All the bills were calculated separately to avoid confusion.
Although they lived in the same house, they preferred to eat separately.
The twins dressed separately for the party, each choosing their own style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: individually - one at a time; singly
  • antonym: together - with others or in combination
