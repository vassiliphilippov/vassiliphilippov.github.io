In a way that is apart from others; individually rather than together.

Examples

To do separately
All the bills were calculated separately to avoid confusion.
Although they lived in the same house, they preferred to eat separately.
The twins dressed separately for the party, each choosing their own style.

Synonyms

  • individually
    2.5 13/m
    individually places focus on each person or thing alone, while separately just means apart rather than together.
  • on an individual basis
    4.4 0.70/m
    on an individual basis treats each case alone in formal settings, while separately is more general and can be less formal.
  • discretely
    4.8 0.22/m
    discretely stresses being distinct or clearly separate, while separately simply means apart from others without that emphasis.
  • singly
    4.6 0.85/m
    singly means one at a time or alone, while separately can mean apart without implying the order of occurrence.
  • one by one
    1.4 9.94/m
    one by one describes items happening in sequence, while separately does not require a specific order and may be simultaneous.
  • severally
    8.2 0.37/m
    severally is formal and often used in legal texts to mean separately, while separately is common everyday language for being apart.

Related Terms
