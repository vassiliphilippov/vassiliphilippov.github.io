analyze

verb
to subject to psychoanalytic treatment
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.əˌlaɪz /; / əˈnælaɪz / (AmE), / ˈæn.əl.aɪz /; / ˈæn.ə.laɪz / (BrE)
to subject (a person) to psychoanalytic treatment in order to explore their unconscious mind or diagnose mental disorders

Examples

To analyze a patient
After her traumatic experience, she decided to be analyzed by a specialist.
He spent two years being analyzed to better understand his recurring nightmares.
Many patients were analyzed at Freud's clinic in the early twentieth century.
He decided to analyse his patient to uncover unconscious motivations.
Many patients are analysed for years before significant breakthroughs are made.
She was analysed by a renowned psychoanalyst during her time in London.
