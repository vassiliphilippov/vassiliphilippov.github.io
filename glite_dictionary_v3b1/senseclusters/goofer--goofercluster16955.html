goofer

noun
playful silly person
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːfər / (AmE), / ˈɡuːfə / (BrE)
a person who acts silly or makes mistakes, often in a playful or lighthearted way

Examples

Silly goofer
My little brother is a real goofer when he tries to make everyone laugh at dinner.
She acted like a goofer during the meeting, making everyone smile with her jokes.
Even the teacher became a goofer on the last day of school, telling funny stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clown - someone who behaves in a comical or silly way
  • synonym: goofball - a silly or foolish person
  • antonym: serious person - someone who behaves in a thoughtful or earnest way
