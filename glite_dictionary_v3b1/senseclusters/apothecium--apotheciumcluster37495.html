apothecium

noun
cup-shaped fruiting body in fungi/lichens
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˈθiːsiəm / (AmE), / ˌapəˈθiːsɪəm / (BrE)
A cup-shaped fruiting body found in many lichens and ascomycetous fungi, containing the asci and spores.

Examples

The apothecium cup
Scientists studied the apothecia to understand the reproductive cycle of the fungus.
The apothecium of the lichen was visible as a tiny cup on the surface.
Under the microscope, the structure of the apothecium revealed its spore-producing function.
