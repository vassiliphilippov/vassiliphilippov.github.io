Synonyms

  • brilliant
    3.0 0.7/m
    brilliant is very intense and shiny in color, while bright is a more general word for strong, easily seen hues.
  • vivid
    3.0 1/m
    vivid is deeply saturated and eye-catching in appearance, while bright often means strong color that is easily noticed.
  • brightly-colored
    1.2 0.7/m
    brightly-colored is used to say something has vivid, strong colors, while bright is a short, more general word for strong color.
  • vibrant
    3.6 1/m
    vibrant is bright and full of life or energy in look, while bright focuses only on a color's strong visual intensity.
  • intense
    3.4 0.7/m
    intense describes very high color saturation and power, while bright is a more general word for strong or easily seen color.
  • colorful
    1.0 4/m
    colorful means having many or varied colors and looks lively, while bright highlights a single color's strong, intense appearance.
