bright

adjective
having strong or vivid color
3.3
Pronunciation: / braɪt / (AmE), / braɪt / (BrE)
having a strong, striking, or vivid color; easily noticed because of its intensity

Examples

Bright colors
A bright yellow door welcomed visitors to the old house.
She wore a bright red scarf that stood out in the crowd.
The room was filled with bright colors that made it feel cheerful and lively.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brilliant - of striking or intense color or light
  • antonym: muted - not strong or striking (of color)
  • synonym: vivid - having intense or clear color
