trisagion

noun
Christian hymn or prayer
Pronunciation: / traɪˈsædʒiən /; / trɪˈsædʒiən / (AmE), / trɪˈsædʒiən / (BrE)
a short hymn or prayer in the Eastern Orthodox Christian tradition, often used in services or as a memorial for the dead
