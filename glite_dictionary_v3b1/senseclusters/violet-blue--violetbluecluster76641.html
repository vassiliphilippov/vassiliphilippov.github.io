violet-blue

adjective
bluish-purple color
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌvaɪəlɪtˈbluː / (AmE), / ˌvaɪəlɪtˈbluː / (BrE)

The artist selected a violet-blue shade to capture the twilight sky.
