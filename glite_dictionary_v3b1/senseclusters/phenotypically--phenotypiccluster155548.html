phenotypically

adverb
in terms of traits
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌfiːnəˈtɪpɪkli / (AmE), / ˌfiːnəˈtɪpɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to observable physical or biological traits of an organism

Examples

Plants are phenotypically different
The plants are phenotypically different due to environmental factors.
Although the animals are phenotypically alike, their DNA reveals significant differences.
Researchers observed that the bacteria changed phenotypically under stress conditions.

Related Terms
