crashingly

adverb
with a loud crash
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkræʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkræʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that involves a loud, crashing noise or with great suddenness and force

Examples

Crashingly loud noise
The vase fell crashingly to the floor, startling everyone in the room.
During the storm, the waves hit the rocks crashingly all night long.
The door swung open crashingly as the wind picked up speed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noisily - in a loud or disruptive manner
  • antonym: quietly - in a way that makes little or no noise
