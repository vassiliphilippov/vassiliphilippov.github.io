coevalness

noun
same age or era
9.9
Pronunciation: /koʊ.ˈiː.vəl.nəs/ (AmE), /kəʊ.ˈiː.vəl.nəs/ (BrE)
the state or quality of being the same age or existing during the same period as something else

Examples

The coevalness of fossils
The coevalness of the fossils indicates that the animals lived during the same geological period.
Historians debated the coevalness of the two ancient manuscripts found in the ruins.
The coevalness of their careers made them frequent rivals in the art world.
