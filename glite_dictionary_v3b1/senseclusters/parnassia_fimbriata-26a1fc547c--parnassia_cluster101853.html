Parnassia fimbriata

noun
a North American bog plant with fringed white flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɑːrˈnæsiə fɪmˈbraɪətə / (AmE), / pɑːˈnasɪə fɪmˈbrɪətə / (BrE)
A bog plant native to western North America, with heart-shaped basal leaves and cream or white, fringed, saucer-shaped flowers.
