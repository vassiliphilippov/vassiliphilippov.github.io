malposition

noun
incorrect or abnormal position
Pronunciation: / ˌmæl.pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌmal.pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
an incorrect or abnormal position, especially of a part of the body or a mechanical object

Examples

A malposition problem

Doctors corrected the malposition of the unborn baby before delivery.
