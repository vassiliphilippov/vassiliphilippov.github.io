Attica

proper noun
ancient region of Greece including Athens
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈætɪkə / (AmE), / ˈætɪkə / (BrE)
A historical region of ancient Greece that included Athens and its surrounding territory, noted for its use of the Ionic dialect.
