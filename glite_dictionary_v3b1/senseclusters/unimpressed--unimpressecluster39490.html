unimpressed

adjective
not affected or impressed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.ɪmˈprɛst / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈprɛst / (BrE)
not feeling admiration, interest, or respect for someone or something; not affected or influenced by what is seen or heard.

Examples

To be unimpressed by a movie
Despite the hype, I was unimpressed by the movie.
He tried to show off his skills, but the audience looked unimpressed.
She listened to his story but remained unimpressed throughout.

Related Terms

  • antonym: impressed - feeling admiration or respect
  • synonym: indifferent - having no particular interest or sympathy; unconcerned
