spotted

adjective
having spots or patches
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑt̬ɪd / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd / (BrE)
having spots or patches of a different color or texture

Examples

A spotted animal
A spotted owl perched quietly on the tree branch, blending into the forest shadows.
She wore a spotted dress with vibrant red and blue dots.
The spotted dog ran across the yard, its black patches standing out on white fur.

Related Terms

  • synonym: speckled - covered or marked with many small spots or patches of color
  • antonym: spotless - without any spots or stains
