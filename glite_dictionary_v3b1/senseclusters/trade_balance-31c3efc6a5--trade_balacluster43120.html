Synonyms

  • balance of trade
    4.4 0.27/m
    balance of trade is the formal term used in reports and textbooks, while trade balance is the common everyday term.
  • visible balance
    7.1 0.00/m

  • trade gap
    3.8 0.06/m
    trade gap often highlights a deficit or surplus in public discussion, while trade balance is a neutral measure of import and export value.
