yeastless

adjective
without yeast
Pronunciation: / ˈjiːstləs / (AmE), / ˈjiːstləs / (BrE)
not containing or made with yeast

Examples

Yeastless bread
She baked a yeastless bread for people with allergies.
Many traditional flatbreads are yeastless and have a dense texture.
If you are avoiding yeast, try this simple yeastless pizza dough recipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unleavened - not made with a raising agent such as yeast
