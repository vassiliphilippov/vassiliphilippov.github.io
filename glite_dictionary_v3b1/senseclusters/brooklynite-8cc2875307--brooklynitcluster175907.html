Brooklynite

noun
person from Brooklyn
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʊkləˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈbrʊkləˌnaɪt / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from Brooklyn, New York City

Examples

A proud Brooklynite
As a lifelong Brooklynite, he knows every corner of the borough.
Many Brooklynites take pride in their diverse community and local culture.
The new restaurant quickly became popular among Brooklynites looking for unique dining experiences.
