faithfulness

noun
quality of being faithful or loyal
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪθfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈfeɪθfəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being loyal, reliable, and true to someone or something; the state of being faithful

Examples

Faithfulness to friends
Her faithfulness to her friends earned her their lifelong trust.
Many stories celebrate the faithfulness of partners in long-term relationships.
The dog's faithfulness was evident in the way it waited patiently for its owner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: infidelity - the act or state of being unfaithful
  • synonym: loyalty - the state of being loyal; allegiance
