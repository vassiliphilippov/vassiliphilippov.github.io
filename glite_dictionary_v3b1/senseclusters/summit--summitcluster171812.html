summit

noun
highest level or degree attained
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌmɪt / (BrE)
the highest attainable level or degree of achievement or development

Examples

Summit of success
His writing had finally attained the summit of literary excellence.
She reached the summit of her profession after years of dedication and hard work.
The company is at the summit of innovation in its industry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bottom - the lowest or least successful position
  • synonym: peak - the point of greatest achievement or success
  • synonym: pinnacle - the most successful point; the culmination
