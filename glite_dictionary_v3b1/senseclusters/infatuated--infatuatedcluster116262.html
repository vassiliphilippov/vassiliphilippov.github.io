infatuated

adjective
possessing irrational, intense attraction
8.8
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfætʃ.uˌeɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈfat.ju.eɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
experiencing an intense but short-lived and often irrational passion or admiration for someone or something

Examples

To be infatuated with someone
He became so infatuated with her that he ignored his old friends.
It was just an infatuated crush, not real love.
Many teenagers get easily infatuated with pop stars or celebrities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: besotted - strongly infatuated; obsessed in a foolish way
  • antonym: disenchanted - no longer feeling admiration or love; having lost illusion
  • synonym: enamored - filled with love or admiration; captivated
