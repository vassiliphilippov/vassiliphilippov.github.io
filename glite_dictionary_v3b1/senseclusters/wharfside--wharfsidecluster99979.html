wharfside

noun
area beside wharf
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrfˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɔːfˌsaɪd / (BrE)
the area of land directly beside a wharf, often including the quay and nearby buildings used for loading or storing goods

Examples

Busy wharfside
Vendors set up their stalls along the wharfside to sell fresh seafood to locals and tourists.
The old warehouses on the wharfside have been converted into trendy cafes and shops.
Fishermen gathered at the wharfside early in the morning to unload their catch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dockside - the area beside a dock, especially where goods are handled
  • synonym: quayside - the area next to a quay where ships are loaded and unloaded
