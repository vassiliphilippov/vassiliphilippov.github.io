Kisumu

proper noun
city in western Kenya on Lake Victoria
Pronunciation: / kiˈsuːmu / (AmE), / kɪˈsuːmuː / (BrE)
A port city in western Kenya, located on the northeastern shore of Lake Victoria, known as a fishing and trading center.

Examples

Kisumu city
Kisumu is the third-largest city in Kenya and an important commercial hub.
The fishing industry plays a significant role in the economy of Kisumu.
Tourists often visit Kisumu to enjoy its vibrant markets and lakeside views.
