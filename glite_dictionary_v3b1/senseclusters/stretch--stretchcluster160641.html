stretch

adjective
with an elongated seating area (of vehicles)
Pronunciation: / strɛtʃ / (AmE), / strɛtʃ / (BrE)
(of a vehicle) having an elongated seating area or extended body, especially to accommodate more passengers.

Examples

Stretch limousine
A stretch limo can seat up to ten passengers comfortably.
She rented a stretch SUV for the bachelorette party.
The couple arrived at the wedding in a stretch limousine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: limousine - an expensive car with a lengthened body, often driven by a chauffeur
