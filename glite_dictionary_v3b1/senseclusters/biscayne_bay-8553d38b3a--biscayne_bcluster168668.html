Biscayne Bay

proper noun
bay in southeastern Florida
Pronunciation: / bɪˈskeɪn ˌbeɪ / (AmE), / bɪˈskeɪn ˌbeɪ / (BrE)
a narrow inlet of the Atlantic Ocean in southeastern Florida, forming a bay near Miami

Examples

Biscayne Bay waters
Endangered manatees can sometimes be seen in the waters of Biscayne Bay.
The city of Miami lies along the western shore of Biscayne Bay.
Tourists enjoy boating and swimming in Biscayne Bay all year round.
