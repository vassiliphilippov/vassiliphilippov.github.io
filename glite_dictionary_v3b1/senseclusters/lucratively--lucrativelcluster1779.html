lucratively

adverb
profitably
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈluːkrətɪvli / (AmE), / ˈluːkrətɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that produces a lot of profit or money

Examples

To lucratively earn money
She managed her business lucratively and quickly expanded to new markets.
The author sold his book lucratively to several international publishers.
They invested lucratively in technology stocks during the boom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: profitably - in a way that makes a profit
  • antonym: unprofitably - in a way that does not make a profit
