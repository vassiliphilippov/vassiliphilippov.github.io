Toll House cookie

noun
chocolate chip cookie
8.4
Pronunciation: / toʊl haʊs ˈkʊki / (AmE), / təʊl haʊs ˈkʊki / (BrE)
A type of cookie containing chocolate chips, originally made famous by the Toll House Inn in Massachusetts.

Examples

A Toll House cookie

She brought homemade Toll House cookies to the school bake sale.
The original recipe for Toll House cookies uses semi-sweet chocolate chips.
