pessimist

noun
person who expects the worst
1.5 3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛsɪmɪst / (AmE), / ˈpɛsɪmɪst / (BrE)
a person who tends to see the worst aspect of things or believes that the worst will happen

Examples

A pessimist person
As a pessimist, she always expects things to go wrong.
Even the most devoted pessimist had to admit things were improving.
The pessimist predicted rain for the picnic, but the sun shone all day.
