stylomastoid

adjective
of styloid and mastoid
Pronunciation: / ˌstaɪloʊˈmæstɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌstaɪləʊˈmæstɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to both the styloid and mastoid processes of the temporal bone in the skull

Examples

The stylomastoid foramen
The facial nerve exits the skull through the stylomastoid foramen.
Damage to the stylomastoid artery can affect facial muscle function.
Surgeons must carefully identify the stylomastoid region during ear operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: temporal - relating to the temple or temporal bone of the skull
