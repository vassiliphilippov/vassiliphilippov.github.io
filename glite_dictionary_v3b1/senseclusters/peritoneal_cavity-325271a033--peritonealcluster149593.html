peritoneal cavity

noun
anatomical space in the abdomen
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛr.ɪ.toʊˈniː.əl ˈkæv.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌpɛr.ɪ.təˈniː.əl ˈkæv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the potential space within the abdomen between layers of the peritoneum, containing a small amount of lubricating fluid

Examples

The peritoneal cavity space
Ascites is the abnormal buildup of fluid in the peritoneal cavity.
Fluid can accumulate in the peritoneal cavity as a result of certain diseases.
The surgeon carefully examined the peritoneal cavity for any signs of infection.
