peritoneal cavity

noun
anatomical space in the abdomen
7.0 8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛr.ɪ.toʊˈniː.əl ˈkæv.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌpɛr.ɪ.təˈniː.əl ˈkæv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the potential space within the abdomen between layers of the peritoneum, containing a small amount of lubricating fluid
