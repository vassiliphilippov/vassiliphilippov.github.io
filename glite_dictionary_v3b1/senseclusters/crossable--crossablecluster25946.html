crossable

adjective
able to be crossed
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsəbəl / (BrE)
able to be crossed or traversed, especially by walking, driving, or moving over.

Examples

Crossable bridge
The old wooden bridge is still crossable despite its age.
During the dry season, the field becomes crossable by foot.
The mountain pass is only crossable in summer when the snow has melted.

Related Terms

  • antonym: impassable - impossible to cross or travel through
  • synonym: passable - able to be passed through or over
