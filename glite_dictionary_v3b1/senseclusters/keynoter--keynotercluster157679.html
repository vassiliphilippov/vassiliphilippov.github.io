keynoter

noun
influential leader
5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkiː.noʊ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈkiː.nəʊ.tə/ (BrE)
a person who sets the main tone, theme, or direction for a movement, debate, or event, often through influence or leadership

Examples

Keynoter of change
As the keynoter of the movement, she inspired others to join the cause.
The keynoter shaped the direction of the debate before anyone else spoke.
Many looked to him as the keynoter who set the agenda for the entire event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: follower - a person who supports and is guided by another
  • synonym: influencer - someone who has the power to affect decisions or opinions
  • synonym: leader - a person who guides or directs a group
