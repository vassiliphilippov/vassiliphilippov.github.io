Prevacid

noun
brand of antacid medication
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛvəˌsɪd / (AmE), / ˈprɛvəˌsɪd / (BrE)
A trademarked antacid medication (lansoprazole) used to reduce or prevent stomach acid production.
