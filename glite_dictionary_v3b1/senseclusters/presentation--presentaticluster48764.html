presentation

noun
formal awarding of something
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the formal act of giving or awarding something, such as a prize or reward, to someone in public

Examples

Presentation of a prize
At the awards ceremony, the mayor made the presentation of the scholarship to the top student.
She nervously prepared for the presentation of the certificate to the contest winner.
The presentation of medals took place at the end of the tournament in front of cheering fans.

  • synonym: award - something given as a prize or recognition
