cadaverously

adverb
in a deathlike way
9.6
Pronunciation: / kəˈdævɚəsli / (AmE), / kəˈdævərəsli / (BrE)
in a manner that is extremely pale, thin, or resembling a dead body

Examples

Cadaverously pale face
The patient looked cadaverously thin after weeks of illness.
He smiled cadaverously, his cheeks sunken and eyes hollow.
Under the harsh lights, her face appeared cadaverously pale and drawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gauntly - in a thin and bony manner
  • antonym: healthily - in a way that shows good health and vitality
