Examples

A inhibitor substance
A corrosion inhibitor was added to the cooling system to protect the pipes.
Many medications act as enzyme inhibitors to treat specific illnesses.
The addition of an inhibitor prevented the reaction from proceeding too quickly.

Synonyms

    6.0 1.47/m
    a blocker is a drug that prevents certain substances from acting in the body, while an inhibitor slows or stops a chemical or biological process.
