blinkered

adjective
narrow-minded
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪŋkərd / (AmE), / ˈblɪŋkəd / (BrE)
having a limited or narrow outlook, unwilling or unable to consider different ideas or possibilities

Examples

Blinkered thinking
Her blinkered attitude made it difficult for her to accept new suggestions from the team.
The debate was unproductive because both sides remained blinkered in their views.
A blinkered perspective can prevent progress in a rapidly changing world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: narrow-minded - unwilling to consider different ideas or opinions
  • antonym: open-minded - willing to consider new ideas or opinions
