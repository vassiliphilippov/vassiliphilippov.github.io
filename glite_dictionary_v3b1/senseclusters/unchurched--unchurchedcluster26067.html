unchurched

noun
people not attending church
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃɜrtʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɜːtʃt / (BrE)
people who do not attend church services or are not affiliated with any religious organization

Examples

The unchurched community
The new outreach program aims to connect with the unchurched in the local community.
Many of the unchurched are interested in spiritual topics but do not attend services.
Volunteers organized events to welcome the unchurched and make them feel included.

Related Terms

  • antonym: congregation - a group of people who regularly attend church services
  • synonym: nonbelievers - people who do not believe in a particular religion
