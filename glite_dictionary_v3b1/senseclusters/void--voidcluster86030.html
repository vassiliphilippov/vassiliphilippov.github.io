(of a contract, agreement, or provision) having no legal effect; not legally binding or enforceable.

Examples

Void contract
Any agreement signed under duress is usually void in the eyes of the law.
If the terms are illegal, that part of the contract is considered void.
The contract was declared void because it lacked a necessary signature.
