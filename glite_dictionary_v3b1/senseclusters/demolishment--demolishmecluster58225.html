demolishment

noun
decisive defeat
6.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑlɪʃmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒlɪʃmənt / (BrE)
a decisive defeat or overwhelming destruction of an opponent, especially in sports or argument

Examples

Total demolishment of rivals
The team's demolishment of their rivals was the talk of the tournament.
After the demolishment in the debate, the losing side struggled to recover their reputation.
Fans cheered the unexpected demolishment of the defending champions in the final match.
