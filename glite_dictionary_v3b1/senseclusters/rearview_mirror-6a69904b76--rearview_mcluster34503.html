rearview mirror

noun
mirror for seeing behind in a car
3.3 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪər.vjuː ˈmɪr.ər/ /; / /ˈriːr.vjuː ˈmɪr.ər/ /; / /ˈrɪər.vjuː mɪr/ (AmE), /ˈrɪə.vjuː ˈmɪr.ə/ /; / /ˈrɪə.vjuː ˈmɪr/ /; / /ˈriːə.vjuː ˈmɪr.ə/ (BrE)
A mirror in a vehicle, usually fixed above the windshield inside the car, that allows the driver to see the area behind the vehicle through the rear window.

Examples

Car rearview mirror
A crack in the rearview mirror made it hard for her to see cars behind.
He checked the rearview mirror before changing lanes on the highway.
The driving instructor told him to always glance at the rearview mirror while reversing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: side mirror - mirror on the outside of a vehicle for viewing traffic to the side and rear
