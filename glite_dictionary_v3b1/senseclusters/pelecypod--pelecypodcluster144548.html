pelecypod

noun
mollusk with a two-part shell (bivalve)
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɛsɪˌpɑd / (AmE), / pɛˈliːsɪˌpɒd /; / pɪˈliːsɪˌpɒd / (BrE)
a mollusk with a soft body and a shell made of two hinged parts, such as a clam, oyster, or mussel (a bivalve)
