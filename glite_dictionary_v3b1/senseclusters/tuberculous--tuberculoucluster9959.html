tuberculous

adjective
afflicted with or relating to tuberculosis
6.0
Pronunciation: / tuˈbɝː.kjə.ləs / (AmE), / tjuːˈbɜː.kjʊ.ləs / (BrE)
afflicted with, relating to, or caused by tuberculosis or the tubercle bacillus

Examples

Tuberculous infection
Doctors monitored the progress of the tuberculous infection through regular chest X-rays.
She developed a tuberculous lesion on her lung after prolonged exposure.
The hospital set up a special ward for tuberculous patients during the outbreak.
