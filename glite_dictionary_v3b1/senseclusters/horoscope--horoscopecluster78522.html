horoscope

noun
astrological prediction of the future
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔːrəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈhɒrəsˌkəʊp / (BrE)
A prediction of a person's future, typically based on the positions of the stars and planets at the time of their birth.

Examples

Daily horoscope reading
According to my horoscope, today is a good day for new beginnings.
He doesn't believe in horoscopes, but sometimes checks them for fun.
She read her horoscope every morning before leaving the house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astrology - the study of movements and relative positions of celestial bodies interpreted as having an influence on human affairs
  • synonym: prediction - a statement about what will happen in the future
