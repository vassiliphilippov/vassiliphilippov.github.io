misread

verb
interpret or understand incorrectly
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈriːd /; / mɪsˈrɛd, ˈmɪsˌriːd / (AmE), / mɪsˈriːd /; / mɪsˈriːd, mɪsˈrɛd / (BrE)
to read or understand something incorrectly; to interpret something in the wrong way

Examples

To misread a sign

I realized I had misread the schedule after I arrived at the wrong time.
She misread the instructions and made an error in the recipe.
