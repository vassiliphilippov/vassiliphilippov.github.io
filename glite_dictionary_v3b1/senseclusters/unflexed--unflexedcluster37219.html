unflexed

adjective
not bent or tensed
5.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈflɛkst / (AmE), / ʌnˈflɛkst / (BrE)
not bent, curved, or tensed; in a relaxed or straight position

Examples

Unflexed arm
He kept his arm unflexed while waiting for the doctor to examine it.
The gymnast's leg remained unflexed during the entire routine.
Leave the hose unflexed to prevent any kinks or blockages.
