Synonyms

  • pass through
    2.7 12/m
    pass through means to travel through a place without stopping, while transit often sounds more formal and can mean moving through for transport.
  • move through
    2.1 4/m
    move through describes going from one area to another as part of a journey, while transit is used more formally.
  • pass
    1.0 90/m
    pass means to go past or across something, while transit is a more formal term for moving through a place or system.
  • pass over
    3.3 1/m
    pass over means go above or skip something physically or in order, while transit focuses on traveling through a place or moving goods.
