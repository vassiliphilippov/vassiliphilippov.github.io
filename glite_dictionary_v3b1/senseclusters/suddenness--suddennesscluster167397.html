suddenness

noun
quality of being sudden; unexpectedness
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌdənnəs / (AmE), / ˈsʌd(ə)nnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of happening quickly and unexpectedly, without warning

Examples

The suddenness of change
He was alarmed by the suddenness with which the doors closed.
Many people commented on the suddenness of her decision to leave.
The suddenness of the storm caught the hikers by surprise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unexpectedness - the quality of being not expected or foreseen
