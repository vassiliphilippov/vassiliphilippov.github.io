irascibly

adverb
in an irritable way
9.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˈræsəbli / (AmE), / ɪˈræsɪbli / (BrE)
in a manner showing a tendency to become easily angered or irritated

Examples

To speak irascibly
She answered the unexpected criticism irascibly, making everyone in the room uncomfortable.
The coach irascibly dismissed the reporter's persistent questions after the game.
When the meeting ran late, he irascibly demanded that everyone hurry up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angrily - in a manner showing anger
  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful or relaxed manner
