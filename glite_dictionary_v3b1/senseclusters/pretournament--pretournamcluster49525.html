pretournament

noun
event before tournament
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈtʊrnəmənt / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈtʊənəmənt / (BrE)
an event or match held before the main tournament, often to prepare participants or determine qualifiers

Examples

A pretournament match
The team used the pretournament to test new strategies before the main event.
Several players were eliminated during the pretournament round and did not advance.
Coaches watched the pretournament closely to assess their opponents' strengths.

Related Terms

  • synonym: qualifier - a match or event to decide who advances to the main competition
  • degree higher: tournament - a series of contests to determine an overall winner
