pinion

noun
large feather of a bird's wing or tail
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnjən / (AmE), / ˈpɪnjən / (BrE)
the large flight feathers or the outer part of a bird's wing, important for flying

Examples

A bird's pinion
A broken pinion can prevent a bird from flying.
The biologist examined the structure of the falcon's pinions under a microscope.
The eagle spread its pinions wide as it soared over the valley.
A falcon with injured pinions soon loses its graceful flight.
In poetry, the word pinion is often used to evoke the image of majestic wings.
The eagle spread its powerful pinions and soared above the mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flight feather - a large feather necessary for a bird's flight
  • synonym: wing - the limb of a bird or aircraft used for flying
