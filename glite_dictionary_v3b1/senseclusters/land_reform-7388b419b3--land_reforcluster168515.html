land reform

noun
redistribution of agricultural land by a government
4.8 3.4
Pronunciation: /lænd rɪ.ˈfɔːrm/ /; / /lænd rə.ˈfɔːrm/ (AmE), /lænd rɪ.ˈfɔːm/ /; / /lænd riː.ˈfɔːm/ (BrE)
the redistribution of agricultural land, especially by government action, often to achieve greater social or economic equity
