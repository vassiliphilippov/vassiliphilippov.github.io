mollycoddle

verb
treat with excessive indulgence
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑliˌkɑdəl / (AmE), / ˈmɒliˌkɒd.əl / (BrE)
to treat someone with excessive care or indulgence, often to their detriment

Examples

To mollycoddle a child
Parents should be careful not to mollycoddle their children, as it can hinder their independence.
Some believe that modern society tends to mollycoddle young adults too much.
The manager refused to mollycoddle his team and instead encouraged self-reliance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pamper - treat with too much care or indulgence
  • synonym: spoil - give too much to or indulge someone, especially a child
