melanic

adjective
darkly pigmented
Pronunciation: / məˈlænɪk / (AmE), / mɛˈlænɪk / (BrE)
having or relating to dark pigmentation, especially caused by the presence of melanin

Examples

A melanic mouse blends easily into the dark volcanic rocks of its habitat.
