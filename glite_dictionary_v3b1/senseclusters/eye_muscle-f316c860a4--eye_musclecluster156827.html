eye muscle

noun
muscle that controls movement of the eyeball
Pronunciation: / aɪ ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / aɪ ˈmʌsl / (BrE)
any of the small muscles around the eye that control the rotation and movement of the eyeball

Examples

The eye muscle moves
Certain vision problems are caused by weakness in an eye muscle.
Exercises can sometimes help strengthen an eye muscle after an injury.
The surgeon needed to repair a damaged eye muscle to restore proper eye movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extraocular muscle - any of the muscles surrounding the eyeball responsible for its movement
  • synonym: ocular muscle - another term for the muscles controlling eye movement
