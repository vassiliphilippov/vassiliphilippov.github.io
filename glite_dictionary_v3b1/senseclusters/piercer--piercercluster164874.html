piercer

noun
tool for making holes
5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪr.sər/ (AmE), /ˈpɪə.sə/ (BrE)
a tool or object with a sharp point used to make holes in materials such as leather, metal, or paper

Examples

Leather piercer
The leatherworker used a piercer to create small holes for the stitching.
She bought a new piercer to help with her jewelry-making projects.
A piercer is essential when working with thick materials that are hard to puncture.
