liang

noun
Chinese weight unit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ljɑːŋ / (AmE), / ljæŋ / (BrE)
a traditional Chinese unit of weight, also known as a tael, equal to about 37 grams

Examples

Liang of silver
The merchant measured the silver in liang before making the sale.
One liang of tea was enough to last the family a week.
He inherited a gold bar weighing five liang from his grandfather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tael - a traditional East Asian unit of weight, about 37 grams
