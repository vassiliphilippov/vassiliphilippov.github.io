arrow

verb
to move swiftly and directly
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈæroʊ / (AmE), / ˈærəʊ / (BrE)
to move swiftly and directly in a straight line, like an arrow in flight

Examples

To arrow forward
The falcon arrowed toward its prey with incredible speed.
A cyclist arrowed down the empty road in the early morning light.
The boat arrowed across the lake, leaving a trail of ripples behind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dart - to move quickly and suddenly in a particular direction
  • degree lower: glide - to move smoothly and continuously without effort
