mung bean plant

noun
plant species grown for edible seeds and bean sprouts
Pronunciation: / mʌŋ ˈbin ˌplænt / (AmE), / mʌŋ ˈbiːn ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
an erect, bushy annual plant (Vigna radiata) widely cultivated in warm regions for its edible seeds, a chief source of bean sprouts used in Asian cuisine

Examples

The mung bean plant seeds
Farmers harvested the seeds from the mung bean plant to use in soups and salads.
The mung bean plant is a major source of fresh bean sprouts in Asian recipes.
The mung bean plant thrives best in warm, tropical climates with plenty of sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mung bean - the edible seed produced by the mung bean plant
  • synonym: soybean plant - another plant grown for edible beans, source of soybeans
