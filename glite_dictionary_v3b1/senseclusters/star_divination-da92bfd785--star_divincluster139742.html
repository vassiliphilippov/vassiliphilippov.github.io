star divination

noun
interpreting the stars to predict the future
5.8
Pronunciation: / stɑr dɪˌvɪn.eɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / stɑː dɪˌvɪn.eɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the practice of attempting to foretell the future by interpreting the positions of the planets, sun, and moon
