henequen

noun
fiber-producing plant
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛnəˈkɛn / (AmE), / ˌhɛnɪˈkɛn / (BrE)
a tropical plant of the agave family whose tough fibers are used to make rope and twine

Examples

Henequen plant fields
The plantation grows henequen for use in making strong ropes and sacks.
Workers harvested the leaves of henequen to extract its valuable fibers.
Traditional industries in Yucatán rely on henequen as a key raw material.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sisal - a plant whose fibers are also used to make rope and twine
