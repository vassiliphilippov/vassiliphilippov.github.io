smack

verb
kiss lightly, often with a slight sound
Pronunciation: / smæk / (AmE), / smæk / (BrE)
to give a light, quick kiss, often making a slight noise

Examples

To smack her lips
He leaned over and smacked her lips with a quick kiss.
She smacked him on the cheek before leaving for work.
The child smacked his mother goodnight and went to bed.
