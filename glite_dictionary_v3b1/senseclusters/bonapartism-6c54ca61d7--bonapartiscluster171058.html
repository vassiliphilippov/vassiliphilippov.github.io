Bonapartism

noun
authoritarian political system
9.9
Pronunciation: /boʊ.nə.ˈpɑːr.tɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /bəʊ.nə.ˈpɑː.tɪ.zəm/ (BrE)

Examples

Bonapartism political movement
