Anoa depressicornis

noun
small wild buffalo of Sulawesi
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈnoʊ.ə dɪˌprɛs.ɪˈkɔːr.nɪs / (AmE), / əˈnəʊ.ə dɪˌprɛs.ɪˈkɔː.nɪs / (BrE)
A small species of wild buffalo native to Sulawesi (the Celebes), Indonesia, having small, straight horns.
