pro rata

adverb
in proportion to rate or share
5.8 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / pro ä Ààre…™t…ô / (AmE), / pr…ô ä Ààr…ëÀêt…ô / (BrE)
In proportion to a calculated rate; in a way that corresponds to each individual's share according to a specific rate or proportion.

Examples

Pro rata payment
Employees who work less than full time are entitled to holiday pay pro rata.
If you cancel halfway through the month, your bill will be reduced pro rata.
The interest will be calculated pro rata based on the number of days you borrow the money.

Synonyms

  • ratably
    8.2 0.01/m
    ratably appears mainly in legal and accounting texts for dividing amounts by each calculated share, while pro rata is the Latin phrase used similarly.
  • proportionately
    4.1 1.27/m
    proportionately is a common adverb for adjusting amounts in everyday speech or reports, while pro rata sounds formal and appears mainly in contracts.
