guestbook

noun
visitor sign-in book
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɛstˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈɡɛstˌbʊk / (BrE)
a book or online form where visitors record their names, comments, or messages at an event or place

Examples

Sign the guestbook
Before leaving the museum, please sign the guestbook at the front desk.
The couple enjoyed reading the kind messages left in their wedding guestbook.
Many websites have a digital guestbook for visitors to leave comments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: logbook - a book for recording events or observations
  • synonym: register - an official list or record of names
