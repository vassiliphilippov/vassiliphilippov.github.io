racing boat

noun
boat designed for competitive racing
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪsɪŋ boʊt / (AmE), / ˈreɪsɪŋ bəʊt / (BrE)
a narrow, lightweight boat propelled by rowers, designed specifically for racing

Examples

A racing boat crew
A racing boat is built to be sleek and fast on the water.
Each racing boat was carefully inspected before the start of the competition.
The team spent months training in their racing boat for the upcoming regatta.

  • synonym: rowboat - a small boat propelled by oars
