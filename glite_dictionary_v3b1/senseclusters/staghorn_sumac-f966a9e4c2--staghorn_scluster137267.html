staghorn sumac

noun
a North American shrub or tree with red autumn leaves and red berries
8.2 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈstæg.hɔːrn ˈsuː.mæk/ /; / /ˈstæg.hɔːrn ˈsʌ.mæk/ (AmE), /ˈstæg.hɔːn ˈsjuː.mæk/ /; / /ˈstæg.hɔːn ˈsuː.mæk/ (BrE)
a North American deciduous shrub or small tree (Rhus typhina) with compound leaves that turn bright red in autumn, dense clusters of greenish-yellow flowers, and crimson, acidic berries
