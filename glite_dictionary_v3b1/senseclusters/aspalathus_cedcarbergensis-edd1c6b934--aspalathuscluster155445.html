Aspalathus cedcarbergensis

noun
South African shrub used for herbal tea
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæspəˈleɪθəs sɪdˌkɑrˈbɜrɡɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˌaspəˈleɪθəs sɪdˌkɑːˈbɜːɡɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A South African shrub with flat, pointed leaves and yellow flowers, whose aromatic dried leaves are used to make herbal tea.
