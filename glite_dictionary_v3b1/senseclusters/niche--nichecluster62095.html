niche

noun
suitable position or role
Pronunciation: / niːʃ, nɪtʃ / (AmE), / niːʃ, nɪtʃ / (BrE)
a job, activity, or position that is especially suitable for someone and in which they can be successful or comfortable

Examples

A career niche
After years of searching, he finally found his niche as a graphic designer.
Many companies try to target a specific niche in the market to stand out.
She carved out a niche for herself in the world of independent filmmaking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calling - a strong urge toward a particular career or vocation
  • antonym: misfit - a person whose abilities or interests do not suit their position
  • synonym: vocation - a person's occupation or strong suitability for a particular career
