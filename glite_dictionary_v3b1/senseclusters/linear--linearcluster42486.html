linear

adjective
of equations: first degree
1.8 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪn.iː.ər/ (AmE), /ˈlɪn.iː.ə/ (BrE)
(Mathematics) Designating or relating to an equation whose terms are of the first degree, such as y = mx + b.

Examples

Linear equation
A linear equation graphs as a straight line on the coordinate plane.
In algebra, students learn to solve linear equations before tackling more complex ones.
The relationship between force and acceleration is linear according to Newton's second law.
