compound

noun
substance of two or more chemically joined elements
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːm.paʊnd /; / ˈkɑm.paʊnd / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.paʊnd / (BrE)
a substance or thing made from two or more elements or parts

Examples

A chemical compound

Salt is a common compound found in every kitchen.
