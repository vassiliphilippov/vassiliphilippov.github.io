meritorious

adjective
deserving praise or reward
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛrɪˈtɔriəs / (AmE), / ˌmɛrɪˈtɔːriəs / (BrE)
Deserving praise, reward, or recognition for being good or worthy.

Examples

A meritorious service
He received a medal for his meritorious military service.
Her meritorious actions during the crisis earned her a public award.
The scholarship is given to students with meritorious academic records.

  • synonym: praiseworthy - deserving approval or admiration
