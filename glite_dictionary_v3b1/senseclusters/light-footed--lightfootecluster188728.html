light-footed

adjective
moving lightly and swiftly
Pronunciation: / ˌlaɪtˈfʊt̬ɪd / (AmE), / ˌlaɪtˈfʊtɪd / (BrE)
moving lightly and quickly, with graceful and agile steps

Examples

A light-footed runner
Despite his size, the basketball player was surprisingly light-footed on the court.
Foxes are known for their light-footed movements when stalking prey.
The light-footed dancer seemed to float across the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nimble - quick and light in movement or action
