precious

adjective
highly valued; treasured
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛʃəs / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃ.əs / (BrE)
highly valued or loved; very important or special, often for emotional reasons

Examples

Precious memories
He shared some precious moments with his childhood friends during the reunion.
The diary was a precious gift from her grandmother and she cherished it dearly.
These old photographs are precious to me because they remind me of my family.
Children are precious and must be protected at all costs.
He gave her a precious gift that she would always treasure.
She kept her grandmother’s ring because it was precious to her.
