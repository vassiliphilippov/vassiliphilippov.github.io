nonspherical

adjective
not shaped like a sphere
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsfɛrɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈsfɛrɪkəl / (BrE)
Not having the shape of a sphere; not spherical.

Examples

Nonspherical shape

The object appeared nonspherical when viewed from different angles.
The scientist explained that the asteroid was nonspherical due to its uneven mass distribution.
