soothsaying

noun
foretelling the future by supernatural means
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːθˌseɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsuːθˌseɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the art or practice of foretelling the future, especially by supernatural or mystical means

Examples

Soothsaying and prophecy
Many cultures throughout history have traditions of soothsaying and prophecy.
She was accused of practicing soothsaying, which was frowned upon by the authorities.
The ancient king relied on soothsaying to guide his decisions during times of war.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divination - the practice of seeking knowledge of the future by supernatural means
  • synonym: fortune-telling - the practice of predicting information about a person's life, usually for entertainment
  • synonym: prophecy - a prediction or the act of predicting what will happen in the future
