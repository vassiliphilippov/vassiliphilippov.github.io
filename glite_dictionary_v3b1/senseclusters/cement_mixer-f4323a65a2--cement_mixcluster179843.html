cement mixer

noun
machine for mixing cement and concrete
6.1
Pronunciation: / səˈmɛnt ˌmɪksər / (AmE), / sɪˈment ˌmɪksə / (BrE)
a machine with a large rotating drum used to mix cement, sand, gravel, and water to make concrete

Examples

A cement mixer machine

We rented a cement mixer to help pour the foundation for the new house.
