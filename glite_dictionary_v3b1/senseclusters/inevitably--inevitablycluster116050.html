inevitably

adverb
unavoidably, as is certain to happen
2.8 3.3
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈev.ə.tə.bliː/ /; / /ɪn.ˈev.ɪ.tə.bliː/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈev.ɪ.tə.bliː/ /; / /ɪn.ˈev.ə.tə.bliː/ (BrE)
