genus Clostridium

noun
bacterial genus found in soil and animal intestines
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs klɒˈstrɪdiəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs klɒˈstrɪdɪəm / (BrE)
A genus of anaerobic or micro-aerophilic, rod-shaped or spindle-shaped bacteria, commonly found in soil, animal intestines, and dung.
