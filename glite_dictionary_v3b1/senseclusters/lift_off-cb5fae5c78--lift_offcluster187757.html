lift off

verb
depart from the ground; take off
2.1
Pronunciation: / lɪft ɔf / (AmE), / lɪft ɒf / (BrE)
(of an aircraft, spacecraft, or rocket) to rise into the air from the ground or a launching pad.

Examples

To lift off the runway
The aircraft lifts off at precisely six o'clock every morning.
The rocket lifted off smoothly from the launch pad.
We watched as the drone lifted off and hovered above the trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: take off - (of an aircraft) to become airborne; leave the ground
