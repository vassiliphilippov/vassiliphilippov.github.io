stroke out

verb
cross out or delete by drawing a line through
Pronunciation: / stroʊk aʊt / (AmE), / strəʊk aʊt / (BrE)
to draw a line through something to indicate that it is to be deleted or ignored

Examples

To stroke out words
After reviewing the document, the manager stroked out several unnecessary paragraphs.
Remember to stroke out any incorrect answers before handing in your test.
She quickly stroked out the old address on the envelope and wrote the new one.
