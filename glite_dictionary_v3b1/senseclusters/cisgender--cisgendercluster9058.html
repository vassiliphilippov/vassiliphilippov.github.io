cisgender

adjective
gender matches birth sex
Pronunciation: / sɪsˈdʒɛndər / (AmE), / sɪsˈdʒɛndə / (BrE)
describing a person whose gender identity matches the sex they were assigned at birth

Examples

Cisgender man
A cisgender woman is someone who was assigned female at birth and identifies as a woman.
The survey asked whether participants identified as cisgender or transgender.
Many cisgender people are learning more about gender diversity and inclusion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: transgender - describing a person whose gender identity does not match their sex assigned at birth
