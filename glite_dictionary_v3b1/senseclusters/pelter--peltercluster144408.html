pelter

noun
a person who throws missiles or objects
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛltər / (AmE), / ˈpɛltə / (BrE)
a person who throws objects, especially missiles such as stones or projectiles

Examples

Stone-throwing pelter
During the protest, the police identified several pelters in the crowd.
The old stadium had a problem with pelters disrupting matches by throwing objects onto the field.
Witnesses claimed that the pelter escaped quickly after hurling a stone at the window.
