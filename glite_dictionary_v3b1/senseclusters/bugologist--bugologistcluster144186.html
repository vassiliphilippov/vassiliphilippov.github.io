bugologist

noun
person who studies insects
Pronunciation: / bʌɡˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / bʌɡˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies insects; an informal term for an entomologist

Examples

A bugologist studies insects
At the museum, a bugologist gave a fascinating talk about butterflies and their habitat.
My son wants to become a bugologist when he grows up because he loves insects.
The bugologist identified hundreds of different beetles during her research trip.
