on one's coattails

adverb
benefiting from another's success
9.9 7.4
Pronunciation: / ɑn wʌnz ˈkoʊt.teɪlz / (AmE), / ɒn wʌnz ˈkəʊt.teɪlz / (BrE)
Following closely after someone or gaining advantage from their success, often undeservedly.

Examples

Win on his coattails
Several new candidates won election on his coattails after his landslide victory.
She advanced in her career largely on her boss's coattails.
The smaller company grew on the coattails of its larger partner's success.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ride someone’s coattails - to achieve success because of another person’s achievements
