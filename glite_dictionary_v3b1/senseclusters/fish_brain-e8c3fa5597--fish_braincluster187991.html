fish brain

noun
forgetful or absent-minded person (slang)
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ breɪn / (AmE), / fɪʃ breɪn / (BrE)
(informal, slang) A forgetful or absent-minded person; used to describe someone who is not attentive or who easily forgets things.

Examples

A fish brain person
After staying up all night, she felt like a total fish brain at her exam.
Don't be such a fish brain—remember to lock the door when you leave.
I completely forgot my password again; I swear I have fish brain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airhead - an unintelligent or silly person; someone forgetful or careless
  • antonym: brainy - very intelligent; having a good memory
  • synonym: scatterbrain - a person who is disorganized or forgetful
