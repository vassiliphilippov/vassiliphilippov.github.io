tangentially

adverb
in a peripheral or indirect way
Pronunciation: / tænˈdʒɛnʃəli / (AmE), / tænˈdʒɛnʃəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to a subject or activity only slightly or indirectly; peripherally.

Examples

Mentioned tangentially in speech
She mentioned the controversy tangentially during her speech, focusing instead on other topics.
The novel tangentially explores themes of identity through its minor characters.
The report only addressed the issue tangentially, without going into detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peripherally - in a way that is only slightly connected with the main thing; indirectly
