effeminately

adverb
in a feminine manner
8.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛmɪnətli / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛmɪnətli / (BrE)
in a way that is considered typical of women or lacking traditional masculinity

Examples

To walk effeminately
He walked effeminately, with graceful and delicate movements.
Some people unfairly judge men who speak effeminately in public.
The actor portrayed the character effeminately to fit the director's vision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: femininely - in a manner characteristic of women
  • antonym: masculinely - in a manner characteristic of men
