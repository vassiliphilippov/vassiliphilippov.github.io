slow-moving

adjective
moving at a slow speed
Pronunciation: / ˈsloʊ ˌmuːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsləʊ ˌmuːvɪŋ / (BrE)
moving at a slow speed; not quick to move or progress

Examples

Slow-moving animals
He enjoys watching documentaries about slow-moving animals such as sloths and turtles.
The slow-moving train delayed our arrival by nearly an hour.
Traffic was heavy and slow-moving during the morning commute.
