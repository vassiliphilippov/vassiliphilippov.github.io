heroic

adjective
showing bravery or courage like a hero
2.2
Pronunciation: / hɪˈroʊɪk /; / həˈroʊɪk / (AmE), / hɪˈrəʊɪk / (BrE)
having or showing great courage, bravery, or determination, especially in difficult situations

Examples

Heroic act
Climbing the mountain in winter was a truly heroic achievement.
She made a heroic effort to save the child from the burning house.
Their heroic actions during the crisis inspired the entire community.
Doctors took heroic measures to keep the patient alive after the accident.
She was praised for her heroic actions during the fire, risking her life to save others.
The rescuers made a heroic effort to reach the stranded hikers despite the dangerous conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brave - showing courage
  • synonym: courageous - not deterred by danger or pain; brave
  • antonym: cowardly - lacking courage; excessively afraid
