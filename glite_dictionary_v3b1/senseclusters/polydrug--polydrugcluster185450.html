polydrug

adjective
multiple drug use
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑliˌdrʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈpɒliˌdrʌɡ / (BrE)
involving or relating to the use of more than one drug at the same time

Examples

Polydrug abuse cases
The study focused on polydrug abuse among young adults in urban areas.
Doctors warn that polydrug interactions can be unpredictable and dangerous.
He was admitted to the hospital after a polydrug overdose involving alcohol and prescription pills.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monodrug - involving only one drug
  • synonym: multidrug - involving several drugs at once
