arteria centralis retinae

noun
central artery of the retina
9.2
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.ˈtɪr.iː.ə sen.ˈtræl.ɪs re.tə.ˈnaɪ/ /; / /ɑːr.ˈtɪr.iː.ə sen.ˈtræl.ɪs re.tə.ˈniː/ (AmE), /ɑː.ˈtɪə.riː.ə sen.ˈtræl.ɪs re.tɪ.ˈnaɪ/ /; / /ɑː.ˈtɪə.riː.ə sen.ˈtræl.ɪs re.tɪ.ˈniː/ (BrE)
the central artery of the retina; a branch of the ophthalmic artery that enters the eyeball with the optic nerve and supplies blood to the retina

Examples
