oscillographic

adjective
of an oscillograph
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːsɪləˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒsɪləˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or produced by an oscillograph, a device that records electrical or mechanical oscillations

Examples

Oscillographic recording device
The technician used oscillographic methods to analyze the voltage fluctuations in the circuit.
An oscillographic record can reveal subtle changes in a machine's vibration patterns.
Researchers examined the oscillographic traces to understand the behavior of the new sensor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oscillograph - a device for recording oscillations
