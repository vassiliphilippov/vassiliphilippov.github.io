talkability

noun
ease of conversation
6.7
Pronunciation: / tɔːkəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / tɔːkəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being easy to talk to or having a tendency to engage in conversation

Examples

Talkability of a person
Her talkability made everyone feel comfortable during the meeting.
The host's natural talkability helped guests open up and share their stories.
Good talkability is an important skill for anyone working in customer service.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aloofness - the quality of being distant or not friendly
  • synonym: approachability - the quality of being easy to approach or talk to
  • synonym: sociability - the quality of enjoying and seeking the company of others
