spline

noun
mathematical/computer-graphics curve
8.2 0.1/m
Pronunciation: / splaÉªn / (AmE), / splaÉªn / (BrE)
A smooth mathematical curve defined by piecewise polynomial functions (for example, cubic splines), used in mathematics, computer graphics, CAD, and data interpolation to create or approximate smooth shapes and paths.
