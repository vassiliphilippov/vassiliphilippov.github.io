spendy

adjective
extravagant with money
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛndi / (AmE), / ˈspɛndi / (BrE)
liking to spend money freely or tending to buy expensive things

Examples

A spendy shopper
My sister is very spendy and loves shopping for the latest gadgets.
If you are feeling spendy, this luxury restaurant is the perfect place to treat yourself.
He became less spendy after he started saving for a house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extravagant - spending more money than necessary
  • antonym: frugal - careful with money and not wasteful
