speak against

verb
express opposition or criticism publicly
4.2 0.92/m
Pronunciation: / spiÀêk …ôÀà…°…õnst / (AmE), / spiÀêk …ôÀà…°…õnst / (BrE)
to express opposition to or criticize something publicly

Examples

To speak against a plan
During the meeting, several members spoke against the changes suggested by management.
Many leaders chose to speak against the proposed law during the public forum.
She was not afraid to speak against injustice, even when others remained silent.

Synonyms

  • speak out against
    4.1 2.89/m
    speak out against is used to publicly say strong opposition to something, while speak against is a more general phrase for opposing.
  • protest against
    3.6 3.37/m
    protest against is a public act or statement of strong objection, while speak against focuses on speaking rather than action.
  • protest
    3.2 18/m
    protest is often an action like marches or slogans to show objection, while speak against is mainly saying opposition aloud.
  • decry
    7.7 3.43/m
    decry is to publicly express strong disapproval in speech or writing, while speak against is a more everyday spoken phrase.
  • denounce
    6.8 9.42/m
    denounce is to strongly and formally criticize someone, often in public, while speak against is less formal and spoken.
  • condemn
    4.0 20/m
    condemn is to declare something morally wrong and reject it strongly in public, while speak against simply expresses opposition.
  • oppose
    2.9 91/m
    oppose is to be against or try to stop something in many ways, while speak against means saying that opposition aloud.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oppose - to actively resist or refuse to comply with
  • antonym: speak for - to express support for something or someone publicly
