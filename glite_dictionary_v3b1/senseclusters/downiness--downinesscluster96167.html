downiness

noun
soft, light quality
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊni.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdaʊni.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being soft and light, like down feathers

Examples

Soft downiness
She enjoyed the downiness of the freshly washed towels against her skin.
The downiness of the baby blanket made it perfect for a newborn.
The downiness of the kitten's fur was delightful to touch.
