cliquey

adjective
exclusive in groups
7.7 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkliÀêki / (AmE), / ÀàkliÀêki / (BrE)
tending to form or belong to small, exclusive groups that do not readily include others

Examples

A cliquey group
Some students find the new club a bit cliquey and hard to join.
Her cliquey attitude made it difficult for newcomers to feel included.
The team became less cliquey after organizing more group activities.

Synonyms

  • cliquish
    8.2 0.06/m
    cliquish describes small, exclusive groups and appears more in writing, while cliquey feels more informal and used in speech.
  • clannish
    8.2 0.11/m
    clannish shows strong, family-like loyalty and close ties, while cliquey means small social groups that exclude newcomers and outsiders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exclusive - not allowing others to join or participate
  • antonym: welcoming - friendly and open to including others
