sweet pepper plant

noun
plant producing bell-shaped sweet peppers
2.4 2.3
Pronunciation: / swit ˈpɛpər plænt / (AmE), / swiːt ˈpɛpə plɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant that bears large, mild, thick-walled, usually bell-shaped fruits used as salad peppers.

Examples

A sweet pepper plant
Each sweet pepper plant can produce several vibrant red and yellow fruits over the summer.
He watered his sweet pepper plants every morning to ensure a healthy harvest.
We planted a sweet pepper plant in the garden to add color to our salads.
