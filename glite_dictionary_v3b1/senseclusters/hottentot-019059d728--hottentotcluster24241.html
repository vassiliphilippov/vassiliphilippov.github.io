Hottentot

noun
offensive term for Khoikhoi
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtənˌtɑt / (AmE), / ˈhɒt.ən.tɒt / (BrE)
an obsolete and now offensive term formerly used for the Khoikhoi people or their language in southern Africa

Examples

The word Hottentot
The term Hottentot is now considered offensive and should not be used.
Early European explorers referred to the Khoikhoi people as Hottentot in their writings.
Linguists once used Hottentot to describe the Khoikhoi language, but this is outdated and inappropriate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Khoikhoi - the indigenous people of southwestern Africa
