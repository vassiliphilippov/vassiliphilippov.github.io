radio observation

noun
astronomical observation with a radio telescope
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdioʊ ˌɑbzɚˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ ˌɒbzəˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
an observation of astronomical objects or phenomena made using a radio telescope that detects radio waves from space
