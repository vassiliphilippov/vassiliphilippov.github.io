reinfestation

noun
return of pests
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌɪnfɛˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌɪnfɛˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of being infested again by pests or parasites after having been treated or cleared

Examples

A reinfestation of termites
After the initial treatment, the house experienced a reinfestation of termites within a few months.
Farmers must monitor their fields to prevent reinfestation by crop-damaging insects.
The veterinarian warned that improper cleaning could lead to a reinfestation of fleas in the home.

Related Terms

  • antonym: eradication - the complete removal or destruction of pests or parasites
  • synonym: infestation - the presence of a large number of pests or parasites
