shadowland

noun
area of deep shade
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈʃæ.doʊ.lænd/ (AmE), /ˈʃæ.dəʊ.lænd/ (BrE)
an area or region covered in deep shade or darkness where little light reaches

Examples

Forest shadowland

He wandered through the shadowland of the alley, searching for the lost cat.
