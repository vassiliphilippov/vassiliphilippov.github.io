penny-wise

adjective
thrifty with small amounts of money
7.5 0.05/m

Careful or thrifty with small amounts of money, but not necessarily with larger sums or in more significant matters.

Examples

Penny-wise but pound-foolish
He is penny-wise, always hunting for the lowest grocery prices but spending lavishly on electronics.
She prides herself on being penny-wise in daily expenses, though she rarely plans for major costs.
Sometimes being penny-wise leads people to miss out on better long-term investments.
