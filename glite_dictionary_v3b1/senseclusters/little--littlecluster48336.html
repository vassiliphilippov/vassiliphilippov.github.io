little

noun
small child or thing
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬əl / (AmE), / ˈlɪtəl / (BrE)
a small child or a small thing, especially used informally or affectionately

Examples

A group of littles
The daycare was filled with happy littles playing together in the sunshine.
She always brings snacks for the littles when she visits her sister's house.
At the family picnic, the littles had their own table decorated with balloons.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adult - a fully grown person
  • synonym: kid - a child, especially a young one
