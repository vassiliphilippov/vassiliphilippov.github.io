kampung

noun
informal settlement
Pronunciation: / kɑmˈpʊŋ / (AmE), / kæmˈpʊŋ / (BrE)
an area or settlement, often poor or informal, typically found within or near a town or city in Southeast Asia

Examples

City kampung area
The family built a small house in the kampung on the edge of the city.
Many children in the kampung play together in the narrow streets after school.
She visited her grandmother who still lives in a traditional kampung outside the capital.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slum - a poor, crowded urban area
  • synonym: village - a small community or group of houses in a rural area
