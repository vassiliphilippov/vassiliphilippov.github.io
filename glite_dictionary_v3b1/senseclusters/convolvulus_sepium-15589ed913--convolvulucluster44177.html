Convolvulus sepium

noun
climbing plant with trumpet-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɑlvjələs ˈsiːpiəm / (AmE), / kɒnˈvɒlvjʊləs ˈsiːpiəm / (BrE)
A common Eurasian and American climbing plant, also known as hedge bindweed, with pink or white trumpet-shaped flowers.

Examples

Convolvulus sepium climbing plant
Convolvulus sepium is sometimes mistakenly identified as other types of bindweed.
Gardeners often struggle to control Convolvulus sepium because of its vigorous growth habit.
The hedgerow was covered with the pink blooms of Convolvulus sepium.
