trainable

adjective
able to be trained
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈtreɪ.nə.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈtreɪ.nə.bəl/ (BrE)
able to be trained, especially by machine-learning or computational methods to adjust parameters or behavior.

Examples

Trainable neural network
The new neural network is trainable on much smaller datasets than before.
Some animals are more trainable than others, making them easier to teach new behaviors.
Modern algorithms are highly trainable and can improve their performance with more data.
