Easy to understand or use without needing instruction; designed so that people can operate or grasp it naturally.

Examples

Intuitive design
The new app has an intuitive layout that lets users start immediately without a tutorial.
Designers worked hard to make the control panel intuitive so technicians could train quickly.
Her explanation was more intuitive than the textbook's, so the students understood the concept fast.
