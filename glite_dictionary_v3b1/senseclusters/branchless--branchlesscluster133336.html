branchless

adjective
without branches
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbræntʃləs / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːntʃləs / (BrE)
(of a tree, plant, or structure) having no branches.

Examples

Branchless tree
A branchless cactus stood alone in the middle of the desert.
He admired the stark, branchless silhouette against the sunset.
The branchless trunk of the tree rose straight into the sky.
