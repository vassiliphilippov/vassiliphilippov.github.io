Barbados gooseberry

noun
West Indian climbing plant with edible fruit
Pronunciation: / bɑrˈbeɪdoʊs ˈɡusˌbɛri / (AmE), / bɑːˈbeɪdɒs ˈɡuːsbəri / (BrE)
a woody climbing plant native to the West Indies, with spiny stems, fragrant white flowers, and small yellow to orange fruit
