reboot

verb
restart a computer or device
Pronunciation: / riˈbut / (AmE), / riːˈbuːt / (BrE)
to cause a computer or other device to load its operating system and start afresh, typically by restarting it

Examples

To reboot the computer
He had to reboot the server to apply the new configuration settings.
If your phone freezes, try to reboot it to fix the problem.
The technician advised me to reboot my computer after installing the updates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restart - to start something again, especially a machine or device
