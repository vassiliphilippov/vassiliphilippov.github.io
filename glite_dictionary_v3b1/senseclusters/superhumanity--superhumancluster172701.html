superhumanity

noun
state of being beyond human capability
7.5 8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərhjuˈmænɪti / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəhjuːˈmænɪti / (BrE)
The state or quality of being beyond what is possible for a human being.

Examples

The superhumanity ideal
She spoke of superhumanity as a philosophical ideal that humanity might one day approach.
Some thinkers believe that striving for superhumanity can inspire people to achieve extraordinary things.
The novel explores the potential for superhumanity through the use of advanced technology.
