protection

noun
trade policy to shield domestic industries
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛkʃən / (AmE), / prəˈtɛkʃən / (BrE)
insurance, financial security, or economic measures taken to guard against loss or insecurity

Examples

Protection insurance
His economic plan called for more protection to defend domestic jobs.
Opponents argue that protection can lead to trade wars and higher consumer prices.
The government increased protection to help local manufacturers compete with imports.
Having a steady job gave her a sense of protection from unexpected expenses.
He relied on his savings as protection during periods of unemployment.
Life insurance provides protection for your family in case something happens to you.

Related Terms

  • antonym: free trade - policy of no restrictions on imports or exports
  • synonym: protectionism - the policy of protecting domestic industries through trade barriers
  • synonym: security - freedom from financial worry; financial safety
