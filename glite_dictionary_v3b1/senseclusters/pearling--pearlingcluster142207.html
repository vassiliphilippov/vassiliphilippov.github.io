pearling

noun
collecting pearls
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or process of collecting or harvesting pearls from oysters or other mollusks

Examples

Traditional pearling was once a major industry in the region.
Many coastal communities depended on pearling for their livelihoods in the early twentieth century.
Modern technology has changed the way pearling is carried out around the world.
