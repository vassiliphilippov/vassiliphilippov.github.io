Mafa

noun
a Chadic language of northern Cameroon
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːfə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːfə / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, especially in the Far North Region of Cameroon, Mayo-Tsanaga division, from Mokolo town northward

Examples

Mafa language
Linguists have documented the grammatical structure of Mafa in several detailed studies.
Many children in the Mayo-Tsanaga division grow up speaking both French and Mafa at home.
The translation of health information into Mafa has improved medical outreach in the region.
