Brabancon griffon

noun
dog breed with a short smooth coat
Pronunciation: / brəˌbæŋkən ɡrɪˈfɒn / (AmE), / brəˌbæŋkən ɡrɪˈfɒn / (BrE)
A variety of Brussels Griffon dog with a short, smooth coat.
