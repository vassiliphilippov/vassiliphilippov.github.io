Examples

Seek asylum abroad

Throughout history, many have risked everything to find asylum from persecution.

Synonyms

  • refuge
    3.3 18/m
    refuge is a general safe place that protects people or things, while asylum often means a formal place of shelter or protection.
  • sanctuary
    4.1 4.03/m
    sanctuary is a protected place, sometimes with religious or legal shelter, while asylum is any refuge from danger or hardship.
  • shelter
    1.1 15/m
    shelter is a building giving temporary housing and protection, while asylum implies broader, often longer refuge from danger.
  • harbor of refuge
    5.2 0.00/m
    harbor of refuge is a poetic or nautical phrase for a safe place near water, while asylum is general shelter from harm.
