ritualistically

adverb
in a ritual manner
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌrɪtʃuəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌrɪtʃuəˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that follows a set routine or established ritual

Examples

To ritualistically light candles
He ritualistically lights a candle before starting his meditation each evening.
The team ritualistically gathers for breakfast before every important match.
She ritualistically arranges her desk the same way every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceremonially - in a way that relates to or is used in a ceremony
  • synonym: habitually - in a way that is done regularly or repeatedly
