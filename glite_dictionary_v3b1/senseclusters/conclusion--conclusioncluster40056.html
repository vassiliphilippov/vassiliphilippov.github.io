conclusion

noun
an intuitive or unwarranted assumption
2.7 1.74/m
Pronunciation: / k…ônÀàklu í…ôn / (AmE), / k…ônÀàkluÀê í…ôn / (BrE)
an idea or judgment that is formed without sufficient evidence, often as an intuitive assumption

Examples

Jump to a conclusion
It's easy to conclusion when you don't have all the facts.
She reached a conclusion based solely on her instincts.
Without evidence, his conclusion was little more than a guess.
