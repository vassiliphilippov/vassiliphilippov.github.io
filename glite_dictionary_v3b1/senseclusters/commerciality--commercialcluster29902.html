commerciality

noun
focus on profit
6.7
Pronunciation: /kə.mɜːr.ˈʃæl.ɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /kə.mɜː.ˈʃæl.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
the quality of being commercial or focused on making money, profit, or business success
