Coleus amboinicus

noun
a species of aromatic herb, Cuban oregano
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkoʊ.li.əs æm.bɔɪ.ˈnɪ.kəs/ (AmE), /ˈkəʊ.li.əs æm.bɔɪ.ˈnɪ.kəs/ (BrE)
