Coleus amboinicus

noun
a species of aromatic herb, Cuban oregano
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊli.əs ˌæmbɔɪˈnɪkəs / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlɪ.əs ˌæmbɔɪˈnɪkəs / (BrE)
a species of aromatic, fleshy herb native to India, Sri Lanka, and South Africa, sometimes classified in the genus Plectranthus; commonly known as Cuban oregano or Indian borage
