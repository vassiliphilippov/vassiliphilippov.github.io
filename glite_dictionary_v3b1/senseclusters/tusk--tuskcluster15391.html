tusk

verb
stab or pierce with a tusk
Pronunciation: / tʌsk / (AmE), / tʌsk / (BrE)
(of an animal) to stab or pierce with a tusk or horn.

Examples

To tusk a tree
In the documentary, an elephant was shown tusking a tree to mark its territory.
The rhinoceros tusked its rival in a territorial battle.
The wild boar tusked the farmer during the attack.
