Synonyms

  • customs duty
    3.5 0.2/m
    customs duty is a tax charged at the border on imported or exported goods, while tariff usually names the official rate or system.
  • duty
    3.0 7/m
    duty is any tax paid to the government and can be general, while tariff refers specifically to trade taxes or their official rate list.
  • impost
    9.6 0.06/m
    impost is an older formal word for a tax on imports, while tariff is the standard modern term and the schedule of rates.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subsidy - government financial support to an industry (opposite of a tax)
