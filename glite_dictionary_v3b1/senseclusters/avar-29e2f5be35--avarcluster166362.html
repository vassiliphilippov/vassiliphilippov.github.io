Avar

noun
Caucasian language
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːvɑr / (AmE), / ˈɑːvɑː / (BrE)
a Northeast Caucasian language spoken by the Avar people in Dagestan and surrounding regions

Examples

The Avar language
She spent a year learning Avar to communicate with villagers in Dagestan.
The Avar language has a rich tradition of oral poetry and storytelling.
Many children in the region grow up speaking both Avar and Russian at home.

Related Terms
