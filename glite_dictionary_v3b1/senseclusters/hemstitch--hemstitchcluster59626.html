hemstitch

noun
decorative openwork embroidery on hems
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛmˌstɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɛm.stɪtʃ /; / ˈhɛmˌstɪtʃ / (BrE)
an embroidery technique or decorative stitch that creates an openwork border by drawing out threads and grouping the remaining threads with stitches
