spectrography

noun
sound frequency analysis
8.6
Pronunciation: /spek.trə.ˈgræf.i/ (AmE), /spek.trə.ˈgræf.i/ (BrE)
the analysis or visual display of sound frequencies using spectrograms, especially in acoustics and phonetics

Examples

Spectrography of sound

Advances in spectrography have improved the analysis of speech in language research.
The laboratory demonstrated spectrography to show how vowel sounds differ in frequency.
