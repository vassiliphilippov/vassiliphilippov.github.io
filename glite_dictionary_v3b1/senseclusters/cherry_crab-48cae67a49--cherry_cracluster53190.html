cherry crab

noun
Asian wild crab apple; red fruit for preserves
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛri kræb / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri krab / (BrE)
An Asian species of wild crab apple, cultivated for its small, acidic, usually red fruit, often used for making preserves.
