corsage

noun
a small bouquet of flowers worn on clothing
Pronunciation: / kɔrˈsɑʒ / (AmE), / kɔːˈsɑːʒ / (BrE)
A small bouquet of flowers, typically worn on a woman's dress or wrist at formal occasions.

Examples

A corsage on dress
Each guest at the luncheon received a small pink corsage as a token of appreciation.
She wore a beautiful corsage on her wrist to the prom.
The groom gave his mother a white corsage before the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boutonnière - a flower or small bouquet worn in a buttonhole, typically by men
