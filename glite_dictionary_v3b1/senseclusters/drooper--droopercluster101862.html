drooper

noun
person or thing that sags
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdruːpər / (AmE), / ˈdruːpə / (BrE)
someone or something that droops, sags, or hangs down limply

Examples

A drooper flower
After a week without water, the flower became a sad drooper in the garden.
He looked like a real drooper after staying up all night studying for exams.
The old flag was a drooper in the windless afternoon, barely moving at all.

Related Terms

  • synonym: droop - to hang or bend down limply
  • antonym: upright - standing straight or not bending down
  • synonym: wilter - someone or something that wilts or becomes limp
