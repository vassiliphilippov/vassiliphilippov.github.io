muss

noun
a state of disorder or mess
Pronunciation: / mʌs / (AmE), / mʌs / (BrE)
A state of confusion or disorder; a mess.

Examples

A big muss
After the party, the living room was in a complete muss.
His hair was in a muss after he took off his hat.
She tried to organize her desk, but it still looked like a muss.
