till

preposition
up to a time
1.0
Pronunciation: / tɪl / (AmE), / tɪl / (BrE)
up to the time of a particular event or point

Examples

Wait till noon
The meeting will last till noon, so please be on time.
You cannot enter the theater till the doors officially open.
She waited till the rain stopped before leaving the house.
