swelteringly

adverb
extremely intensely
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛltərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈswɛltərɪŋli / (BrE)
to an extremely intense or excessive degree, often used figuratively and not limited to heat.

Examples

Swelteringly enthusiastic crowd
The team was swelteringly enthusiastic, cheering louder than anyone expected.
He worked swelteringly hard to finish the project before the deadline.
Her performance was swelteringly intense, leaving the audience breathless by the final scene.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mildly - in a gentle or not extreme way
