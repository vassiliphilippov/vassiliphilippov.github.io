leave off

verb
to stop or cease
4.1 3.92/m
Pronunciation: / liv …îf / (AmE), / liÀêv …íf / (BrE)
to stop doing something or to pause an activity, especially temporarily or at a certain point

Examples

To leave off reading
He left off reading at chapter five and went to bed.
Let's leave off work for today and continue tomorrow.
Remind me where I left off so I can pick up from there.
Let's leave off here and finish the rest tomorrow.
She asked him where they had left off in their story.
You can leave off the address if you want.

  • take time out
