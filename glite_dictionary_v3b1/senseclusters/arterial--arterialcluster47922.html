arterial

noun
major city road
4.4 0.32/m
Pronunciation: / …ëÀêrÀàt…™ri…ôl / (AmE), / …ëÀêÀàt…™…ôri…ôl / (BrE)
a main road or street designed to carry large volumes of traffic through a city or area

Examples

Arterial road network
The city plans to widen the main arterial to reduce traffic congestion during rush hour.
Emergency vehicles often use the arterial because it provides the fastest route across town.
Shops and restaurants line the busy arterial that runs through the business district.

  • main road
