comandante

noun
military rank
8.4
Pronunciation: / koʊmɑnˈdɑnteɪ / (AmE), / kəʊmænˈdænteɪ / (BrE)
a military rank in some Spanish-speaking countries, equivalent to commander or major in English-speaking armies

Examples

Army comandante
After years of dedicated service, he was promoted to comandante in the army.
The comandante led his troops with discipline and courage during the operation.
In some countries, the title of comandante is highly respected within the military.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commander - an officer in charge of a military unit
  • synonym: major - a military officer ranking above captain and below lieutenant colonel
