bendingly

adverb
willingly or compliantly
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛndɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbɛndɪŋli / (BrE)
in a willing, compliant, or yielding manner; showing readiness to adapt or submit.

Examples

Respond bendingly
She responded bendingly to her manager's request, eager to help the team succeed.
He always listens bendingly to advice from his elders, respecting their experience.
The committee acted bendingly to accommodate the new regulations without complaint.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stubbornly - in a way that refuses to change or yield
  • synonym: submissively - in a way that shows willingness to submit or yield
