cragged

adjective
rugged, hilly and rocky (landscape)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkræɡɪd / (AmE), / ˈkræɡɪd / (BrE)
(of land or rock) having many steep, rough, or rugged hills and crags; jagged in appearance.

Examples

The cragged mountains
An eagle soared high above the cragged mountains, searching for prey.
The hikers found the cragged cliffs challenging but beautiful to climb.
The windswept coast is known for its cragged rocks and wild scenery.

Related Terms
