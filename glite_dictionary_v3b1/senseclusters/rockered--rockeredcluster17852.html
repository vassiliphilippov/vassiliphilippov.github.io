rockered

adjective
curved upward ends
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːkərd / (AmE), / ˈrɒkəd / (BrE)
having ends or a middle that curve upward, especially in sports equipment like skis or snowboards

Examples

Rockered snowboard
The new snowboard is rockered to make it easier to turn in deep snow.
Many modern skis feature a rockered design for better performance on powder.
He prefers rockered skates because they allow for quicker turns on the ice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cambered - having a slight upward curve in the middle
