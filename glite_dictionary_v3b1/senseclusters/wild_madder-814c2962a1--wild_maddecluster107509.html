wild madder

noun
Eurasian herb with white flowers
5.4
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈmædər / (AmE), / waɪld ˈmædə / (BrE)
A Eurasian herb (Galium mollugo) with abundant clusters of small white flowers, now naturalized in North America.

Examples

The wild madder plant
Botanists noted that wild madder has become widespread in North America.
She gathered a bunch of wild madder to display in a vase.
The fields were dotted with wild madder blooming in the early summer.
