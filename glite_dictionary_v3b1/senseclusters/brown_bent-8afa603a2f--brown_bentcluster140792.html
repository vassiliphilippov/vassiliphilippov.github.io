brown bent

noun
a grass species with slender stems and narrow leaves
7.2
Pronunciation: / braʊn bɛnt / (AmE), / braʊn bɛnt / (BrE)
a type of common grass (Agrostis canina) with slender stems and narrow leaves, often found in moist meadows and pastures

Examples

brown bent grass
Golf courses sometimes use brown bent for its fine texture and resilience.
She knelt down to study the brown bent growing near the riverbank.
The meadow was covered in brown bent that swayed gently in the summer breeze.
