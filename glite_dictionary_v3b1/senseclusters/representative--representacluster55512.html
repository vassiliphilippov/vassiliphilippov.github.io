representative

noun
person acting on behalf of others
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprəˈzɛntətɪv /; / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (BrE)
a person who acts or speaks on behalf of others, especially in an official capacity

Examples

Company representative

She worked as the company's representative at the conference.
The union representative discussed the workers' concerns with management.
Each company sent a representative to the annual industry conference.
If you have any complaints, please contact your local government representative.
The union representative negotiated better working conditions for the employees.
