incorrupted

adjective
not corrupted; pure
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.kəˈrʌp.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌɪn.kəˈrʌp.tɪd / (BrE)
Not spoiled, contaminated, or morally perverted; pure and untainted.

Examples

An incorrupted character
She was admired for her incorrupted honesty and unblemished character.
The manuscript was preserved in an incorrupted state through the centuries.
They strove to maintain an incorrupted version of the ancient ritual.
