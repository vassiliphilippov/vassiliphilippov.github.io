telex

noun
teleprinter for sending written messages via telegraph
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːˌlɛks / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌlɛks / (BrE)
a teleprinter device used for sending written messages over a telegraph network, operating much like a typewriter

Examples

A telex machine
Before email became common, businesses often relied on telex machines for international communication.
He sent the contract via telex to ensure quick delivery to their partners overseas.
The company received the urgent order by telex late last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: teleprinter - an electromechanical typewriter used to send and receive typed messages over wires
