farinaceous

adjective
resembling or containing starch
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfærəˈneɪʃəs /; / ˌfɛrəˈneɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌfarɪˈneɪʃəs / (BrE)
containing starch or having a texture like flour; mealy or powdery
