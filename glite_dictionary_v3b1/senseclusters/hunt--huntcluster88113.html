hunt

noun
group or club of hunters (for sport)
Pronunciation: / hʌnt / (AmE), / hʌnt / (BrE)
an organization or group of people who regularly join together to hunt animals for sport, especially with hounds

Examples

A local hunt group
Many people in the countryside are members of a traditional hunt.
She joined the local hunt and participated in several fox hunting events.
The hunt met early in the morning to plan the day's activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hunting club - a club or organization devoted to hunting
