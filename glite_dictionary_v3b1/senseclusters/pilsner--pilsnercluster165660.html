pilsner

noun
a pale, hoppy lager beer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlznər / (AmE), / ˈpɪlznə / (BrE)
a type of pale, golden lager beer with a strong hop flavor, originating from the town of Pilsen in Bohemia.

Examples

A cold pilsner
He ordered a cold pilsner to go with his meal.
On a hot summer day, nothing beats a crisp pilsner.
The brewery is famous for its traditional Czech pilsner.
