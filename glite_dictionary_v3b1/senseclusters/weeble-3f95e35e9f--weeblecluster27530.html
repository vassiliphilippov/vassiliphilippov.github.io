Weeble

noun
self-righting toy
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːbəl / (AmE), / ˈwiːbəl / (BrE)
a small, round-bottomed toy that wobbles but always returns to an upright position when pushed

Examples

A Weeble wobbles

He collected every type of Weeble from the vintage toy series.
After a gentle push, the Weeble rocked back to its upright position on the table.
