chilly

adjective
noticeably cold; uncomfortably cool
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪli / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪli / (BrE)
noticeably or unpleasantly cold; uncomfortably cool

Examples

Chilly morning air
Even in summer, the water in the lake is surprisingly chilly.
It's a chilly morning, so remember to wear your jacket.
The room felt chilly after we turned off the heater.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold - having a low temperature, especially compared to the air or to what is normal
  • antonym: warm - at a comfortably high temperature, not cold
