Hegelian

adjective
relating to Hegel or his philosophy
Pronunciation: / həˈɡeɪliən / (AmE), / hɛˈɡeɪlɪən / (BrE)
relating to the German philosopher Georg Wilhelm Friedrich Hegel or to his system of dialectical philosophy

Examples

Hegelian philosophy
Her dissertation examines the Hegelian dialectic and its influence on political thought.
Many modern philosophers have tried to incorporate Hegelian concepts into their theories.
The professor explained the complex Hegelian process of thesis, antithesis, and synthesis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dialectical - relating to logical argumentation or the dialectic method
