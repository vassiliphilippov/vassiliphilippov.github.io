blood transfusion

noun
medical procedure introducing blood into a vein or artery
Pronunciation: / blʌd trænˈsfuːʒən /; / blʌd trænsˈfjuʒən / (AmE), / blʌd trænsˈfjuːʒən /; / blʌd trɑːnsˈfjuːʒən / (BrE)
a medical procedure in which blood or blood products are put into a person's vein to replace lost blood or improve health

Examples
