heights

noun
a high place or area
1.5
Pronunciation: / haɪts / (AmE), / haɪts / (BrE)
a high place or area, especially one offering a broad view of the surroundings

Examples

The heights of mountains
I'm terrified of standing near the edge when I'm in the heights.
Many birds make their nests in the heights of the cliffs.
We climbed up to the heights to watch the sunset over the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: summit - the highest point of a hill or mountain
  • antonym: valley - a low area between hills or mountains
