latinization

noun
conversion to Latin script
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌlætənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌlætɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of converting words or text from another writing system into the Latin (Roman) alphabet

Examples

Latinization of street names
The latinization of the city's street names helped tourists find their way more easily.
Many languages have undergone latinization to make them accessible to a wider audience.
The government announced a plan for the latinization of official documents previously written in Cyrillic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: romanization - the conversion of writing from another script into the Roman alphabet
