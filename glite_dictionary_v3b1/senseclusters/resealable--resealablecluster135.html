resealable

adjective
can be closed again
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈsiːləbəl / (AmE), / ˌriːˈsiːləbəl / (BrE)
able to be closed again after being opened, especially to keep contents fresh or secure

Examples

A resealable bag
The package comes in a resealable pouch to keep the snacks fresh.
She prefers buying coffee in resealable bags for convenience.
Make sure the resealable container is closed tightly after use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reclosable - able to be closed again after opening
  • antonym: sealed - closed tightly to prevent entry or escape
