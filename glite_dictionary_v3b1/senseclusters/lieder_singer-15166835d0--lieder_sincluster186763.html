lieder singer

noun
a singer of German art songs (lieder)
Pronunciation: / ˈliːdər ˌsɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈliːdə ˌsɪŋə / (BrE)
a singer, typically a soloist, who specializes in performing lieder (German art songs).

As a lieder singer, he is renowned for his sensitivity to both poetry and melody.
