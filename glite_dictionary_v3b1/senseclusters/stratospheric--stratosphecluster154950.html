stratospheric

adjective
extremely high
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌstrætəˈsfɪrɪk / (AmE), / ˌstrætəˈsfɛrɪk / (BrE)
extremely high in position, amount, or level, often used figuratively

Examples

Stratospheric prices
The cost of housing in the city has become stratospheric in recent years.
After the announcement, the company's stock price reached stratospheric heights.
His confidence was stratospheric after winning the championship.
