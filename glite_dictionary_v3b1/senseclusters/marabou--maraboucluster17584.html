marabou

noun
large African stork or its feathers
5.9 7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmæ.rə.buː/ /; / /ˈme.rə.buː/ (AmE), /ˈmæ.rə.buː/ (BrE)
A large African stork with black-and-white plumage that feeds on carrion; also, the soft downy feathers from its underwings used to trim garments.

Examples

A marabou stork
A solitary marabou stalked the edge of the watering hole, searching for food.
She wore a hat elegantly trimmed with white marabou feathers.
The zoo's new aviary houses a marabou alongside other exotic birds.
