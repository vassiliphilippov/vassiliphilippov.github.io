degludec

noun
long-acting insulin
Pronunciation: / dɛˈɡluːdɛk / (AmE), / dɛˈɡluːdɛk / (BrE)
a long-acting form of insulin used to help control blood sugar in people with diabetes

Patients using degludec often require fewer daily injections than with other insulins.
