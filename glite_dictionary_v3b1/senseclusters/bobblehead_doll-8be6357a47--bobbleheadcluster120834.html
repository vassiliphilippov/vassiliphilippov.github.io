bobblehead doll

noun
informal term for a mindless follower

(informal, figurative) a person who automatically agrees with others or follows orders, as if nodding like a bobblehead; a mindless follower.

Examples

Bobblehead doll at work
During meetings he acted like a bobblehead doll, agreeing with every suggestion without question.
The columnist accused the council of appointing bobblehead dolls who rubber-stamped every decision.
Parents worried their teenager had become a bobblehead doll, simply repeating friends' opinions at school.
