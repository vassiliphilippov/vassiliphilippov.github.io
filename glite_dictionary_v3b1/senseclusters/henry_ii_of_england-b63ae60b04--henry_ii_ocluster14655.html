Henry II of England

proper noun
first Plantagenet King of England, 1133–1189
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈhen.ri ðə ˈsek.ənd əv ˈɪŋ.glənd/ (AmE), /ˈhen.ri ðə ˈsek.ənd əv ˈɪŋ.glənd/ (BrE)
(1133–1189) The first Plantagenet King of England, known for instituting judicial and financial reforms and for his conflict with Archbishop Thomas Becket over the authority of the Crown.
