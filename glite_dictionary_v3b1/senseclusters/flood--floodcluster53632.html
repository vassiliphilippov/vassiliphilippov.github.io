flood

noun
rising incoming tide
Pronunciation: / flʌd / (AmE), / flʌd / (BrE)
the period during which the tide is rising and water is incoming between low tide and the next high tide

Examples

Flood tide
At flood, the riverbanks were completely underwater, making passage impossible.
Taken at the flood, their opportunity brought great success.
The sailors waited until the flood before attempting to enter the harbor.
