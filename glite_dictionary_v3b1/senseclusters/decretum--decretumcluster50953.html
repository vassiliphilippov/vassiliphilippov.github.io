decretum

noun
formal decree
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkriːtəm / (AmE), / dɪˈkriːtəm / (BrE)
a formal decree or official order, especially in church or legal contexts

Examples

Church decretum
The council issued a decretum outlining new regulations for the clergy.
Scholars studied the ancient decretum to understand the church's historical decisions.
A new decretum was announced, changing the rules for admission to the order.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decree - an official order issued by a legal authority
  • synonym: edict - an official order or proclamation issued by an authority
