nonsecurity

noun
lack of safety
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.sɪˈkjʊr.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.sɪˈkjʊə.rɪ.ti / (BrE)
the state of lacking security, protection, or safety, especially in economic or social situations

Examples

Economic nonsecurity
The government is working to address the problem of nonsecurity in rural areas.
Economic nonsecurity can lead to increased stress and anxiety among families.
Social programs aim to reduce nonsecurity by providing support to those in need.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insecurity - the state of being unsafe or unprotected
  • antonym: security - the state of being protected or safe
