bad feeling

noun
sense of unease or foreboding
Pronunciation: / bæd ˈfiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / bad ˈfiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
A sense of unease, suspicion, or foreboding about something that may happen.

Examples

I have a bad feeling about flying in this weather.
