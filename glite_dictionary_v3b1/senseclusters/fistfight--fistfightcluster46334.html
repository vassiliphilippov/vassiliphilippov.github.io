fistfight

verb
to fight using fists
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪstˌfaɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪstˌfaɪt / (BrE)
to fight with the fists, usually in an angry or physical confrontation rather than with weapons

Examples

To fistfight angrily
During his youth, he often fistfought with classmates over minor disputes.
The two men threatened to fistfight in the parking lot after their argument.
They were caught fistfighting behind the school and were sent to the principal's office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: box - to fight with fists, especially as a sport
  • synonym: brawl - a noisy, rough fight involving several people
