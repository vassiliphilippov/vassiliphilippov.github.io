Mango Mussolini

noun
derogatory nickname for authoritarian leader
Pronunciation: / ˈmæŋˌɡoʊ məˈsoʊlɪni / (AmE), / ˈmaŋɡəʊ məˈsuːlɪni / (BrE)
A derogatory nickname for a political leader known for authoritarian behavior, comparing them to Mussolini and alluding to an orange complexion.

Examples

Mango Mussolini nickname
Critics use the term Mango Mussolini to highlight his alleged authoritarian tendencies.
Many political commentators dubbed the president Mango Mussolini after his latest controversial speech.
Social media was flooded with memes calling him Mango Mussolini during the election cycle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mussolini - Italian fascist dictator (1883–1945)
  • synonym: dictator - an authoritarian ruler with absolute power
