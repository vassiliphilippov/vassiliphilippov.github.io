Guanyin

proper noun
Buddhist Goddess of Mercy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈgwɑnˌjɪn /; / ɡwɑnˈjin / (AmE), / ˈgwæn.jɪn /; / ɡwænˈjɪn / (BrE)
in Buddhism, the Bodhisattva of compassion and mercy, especially venerated in East Asia as a goddess
