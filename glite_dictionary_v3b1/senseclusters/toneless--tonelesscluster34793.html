toneless

adjective
lacking tone or expression
2.4 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtoʊn.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈtəʊn.ləs/ (BrE)
(of a sound, voice, or speech) lacking variation in pitch, emotion, or expression; dull or flat.

Examples

Toneless voice
He read the announcement in a toneless manner, showing no emotion at all.
Her voice was so toneless that it was impossible to tell how she felt.
The teacher's toneless lecture made it difficult for the students to stay engaged.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expressive - showing emotion or variation in tone
  • synonym: monotone - a continuing sound, especially of someone's voice, that is unchanging in pitch
