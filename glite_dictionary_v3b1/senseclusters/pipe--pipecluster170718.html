pipe

verb
play a tune on a pipe instrument
Pronunciation: / paɪp / (AmE), / paɪp / (BrE)

Examples

To pipe a tune
At the festival, the musician piped a lively tune to entertain the crowd.
She loves to pipe old folk tunes on her wooden flute.
The shepherd began to pipe a gentle melody at dusk.
