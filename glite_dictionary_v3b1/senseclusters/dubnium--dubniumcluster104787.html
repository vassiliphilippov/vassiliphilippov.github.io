dubnium

noun
synthetic radioactive chemical element (atomic number 105)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbniəm / (AmE), / ˈdʌbnɪəm / (BrE)
a synthetic, radioactive transuranic element with atomic number 105

Examples

Dubnium element
Dubnium does not occur naturally and must be created artificially in laboratories.
Scientists succeeded in synthesizing dubnium only in particle accelerators due to its short half-life.
The properties of dubnium are still being studied because so little of it can be produced.
