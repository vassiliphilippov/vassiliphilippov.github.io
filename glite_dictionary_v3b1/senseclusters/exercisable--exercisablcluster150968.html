exercisable

adjective
able to be practiced
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɛksərˌsaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɛksəsaɪzəbəl / (BrE)
able to be practiced, trained, or used through physical or mental activity.

Examples

Exercisable skills

Language skills remain exercisable if you practice them regularly.
Muscles are most exercisable when you maintain a consistent workout routine.
