sheep gadfly

noun
fly parasitic on sheep
8.5
Pronunciation: / ʃip ˈɡædˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ʃiːp ˈɡæd.flaɪ / (BrE)
A large fly whose larvae are parasitic on sheep, typically causing irritation and health issues in the host animal.

Examples

Sheep gadflies larvae
Sheep gadflies can cause significant discomfort and health problems for affected sheep.
Proper farm management includes monitoring for pests like the sheep gadfly.
The veterinarian treated the flock for sheep gadfly infestation last spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gadfly - a large fly that bites livestock, especially cattle and horses
