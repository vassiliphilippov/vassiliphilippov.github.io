gift-wrap

verb
wrap a gift attractively
3.2
Pronunciation: / ɡɪft ræp / (AmE), / ɡɪft ræp / (BrE)
to wrap a present in decorative paper and sometimes add ribbons or bows to make it look attractive

Examples

To gift-wrap a box
Can you gift-wrap this box for me, please?
He gift-wrapped the book with colorful paper and a satin ribbon.
She offered to gift-wrap the present before I left the store.
