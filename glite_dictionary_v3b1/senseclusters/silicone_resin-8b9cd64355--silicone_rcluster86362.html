silicone resin

noun
a synthetic polymer material (silicone-based)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlɪˌkoʊn ˈrɛzən / (AmE), / ˈsɪlɪkəʊn ˈrɛzɪn / (BrE)
a synthetic polymer made from silicon, oxygen, carbon, and hydrogen, used for its insulating and water-repellent properties

Examples

A silicone resin seal
A transparent silicone resin was chosen for the waterproof seal on the device.
The electronics industry often uses silicone resin to coat circuit boards, protecting them from moisture.
The paint's durability is improved with the addition of silicone resin as a binding agent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epoxy resin - a synthetic resin used as a binder in adhesives and coatings
  • synonym: polymer - a substance made up of large molecules composed of many repeated subunits
