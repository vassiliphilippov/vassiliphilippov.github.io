shapeshift

verb
to change form
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪpˌʃɪft / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪpˌʃɪft / (BrE)
to change one's physical form or appearance, especially magically or in fiction.

Examples

To shapeshift into animals
In many legends, wizards can shapeshift into animals to escape danger.
The villain was able to shapeshift at will, making him hard to catch.
Some video games let players shapeshift into different creatures to solve puzzles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: morph - to change smoothly from one image or shape to another
  • synonym: transform - to change completely in form or appearance
