unselfconsciousness

noun
state of not being self-conscious
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌsɛlfˈkɑːnʃəsnəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌsɛlfˈkɒnʃəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of not being aware of or concerned about oneself or how one is perceived by others

Examples

Child unselfconsciousness
Children often display remarkable unselfconsciousness when playing together.
He danced with an unselfconsciousness that was both rare and refreshing.
Her unselfconsciousness made everyone feel comfortable around her.

Related Terms

  • antonym: self-consciousness - the quality of being overly aware of oneself or one's appearance
