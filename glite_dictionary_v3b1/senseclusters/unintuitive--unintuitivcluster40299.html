unintuitive

adjective
not easy to understand
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈtuːɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtjuːɪtɪv / (BrE)
difficult to understand or use because it does not follow expected or natural patterns

Examples

An unintuitive design
The controls on this device are unintuitive and make it hard for new users to operate.
Many people found the website's navigation unintuitive and struggled to find the information they needed.
He avoided the software because its unintuitive design slowed down his work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confusing - difficult to understand or unclear
  • antonym: intuitive - easy to understand or use without explanation
