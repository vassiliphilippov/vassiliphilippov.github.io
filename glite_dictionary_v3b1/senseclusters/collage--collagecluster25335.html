collage

noun
artwork made of assembled materials
1.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɑːʒ / (AmE), / kəˈlɑːʒ / (BrE)
A work of art made by assembling and sticking together pieces of paper, photographs, or other materials to create an artistic composition.

Examples

A collage of photos
Her bedroom wall was covered in a collage of family photos and postcards.
The student created a collage using magazine clippings and colored paper.
They admired the artist’s latest collage, which combined newspaper headlines with painted images.

Related Terms

  • synonym: montage - an artistic composition made from various elements
