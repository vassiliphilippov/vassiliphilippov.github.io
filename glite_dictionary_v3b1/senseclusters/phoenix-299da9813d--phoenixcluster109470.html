Phoenix

noun
NASA Mars lander (2008)
Pronunciation: / c8fid0n6aks / (AmE), / c8fid0n6aks / (BrE)
the NASA robotic lander that touched down on Mars in 2008 to study the planet's arctic soil, ice, and climate.

Examples

NASA Phoenix lander
NASA's Phoenix lander touched down near Mars's north polar region in 2008 to study ice.
The team analyzed soil and ice samples transmitted by Phoenix to better understand Martian climate history.
Scientists credited Phoenix with confirming seasonal frost and providing evidence of water ice on Mars.
