PaaS

adjective
cloud service model
6.2
Pronunciation: / pæs / (AmE), / pɑːs / (BrE)
relating to or provided by Platform as a Service, a cloud computing model for delivering applications and services.

Examples

PaaS solution
The company chose a PaaS solution to simplify its software development process.
Many startups rely on PaaS platforms to scale their applications quickly.
Our new project uses a PaaS environment for easier deployment and management.

Related Terms

  • synonym: IaaS - relating to Infrastructure as a Service, a cloud computing service model
  • synonym: SaaS - relating to Software as a Service, a cloud-based software delivery model
