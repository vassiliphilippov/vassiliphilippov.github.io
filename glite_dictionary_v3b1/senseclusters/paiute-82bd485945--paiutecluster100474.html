Paiute

noun
a Native American people of the southwestern U.S.
9.6
Pronunciation: / paɪˈjuːt / (AmE), / paɪˈjuːt / (BrE)
A member of either of two closely related Native American peoples (Northern Paiute and Southern Paiute) originally inhabiting the southwestern United States.

Examples

Paiute tribe member
A delegation of Paiute elders attended the tribal council meeting.
Historians have studied the migration patterns of the Paiute across the Nevada desert.
The Paiute have a rich tradition of storytelling passed down through generations.
