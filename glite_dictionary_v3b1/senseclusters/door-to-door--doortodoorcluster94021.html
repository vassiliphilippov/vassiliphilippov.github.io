door-to-door

adjective
involving visits to each house
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /dɔːr tə dɔːr/ /; / /dɔːr tuː dɔːr/ (AmE), /dɔː tə dɔː/ /; / /dɔː tuː dɔː/ (BrE)
involving visiting each house in an area, especially to sell something, make inquiries, or provide a service
