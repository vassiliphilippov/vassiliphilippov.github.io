noncareer

noun
person in temporary jobs
6.2
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.kə.ˈrɪr/ /; / /nɑːn.kə.ˈrɪə/ (AmE), /nɒn.kə.ˈrɪə/ (BrE)
a person who does not pursue a long-term professional occupation, often working in temporary or short-term jobs

Examples

A noncareer worker
After college, he worked as a noncareer in various part-time positions before deciding on a profession.
Many noncareers value the flexibility of short-term jobs over long-term commitments.
The organization offers training programs specifically designed for noncareer workers seeking new opportunities.
