flap

noun
thin covering attached at one edge
4.4 2.1
Pronunciation: /flæp/ (AmE), /flæp/ (BrE)
a broad, thin, and flexible piece of material attached by one edge, which hangs loose or can be lifted

Examples

Envelope flap
He wrote the address on the flap of the envelope.
Lift the flap on the box to open it.
The coat has a flap to cover the zipper.

Related Terms
