cruft

noun
unwanted code or material
Pronunciation: / krʌft / (AmE), / krʌft / (BrE)
unwanted, leftover, or redundant material, especially in computer code or systems, that is no longer useful

Examples

Cruft in the code

Over time, unnecessary files and cruft can slow down your computer.
It's important to clean out cruft to keep your software running efficiently.
