Wilks score

noun
weightlifting comparison value
8.4
Pronunciation: / wɪlks skɔr / (AmE), / wɪlks skɔː / (BrE)
a numerical value used to compare weightlifting performance between athletes of different body weights

Examples

Wilks score calculation
Her Wilks score improved after months of dedicated training.
