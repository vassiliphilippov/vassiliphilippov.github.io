dorsiflex

verb
to bend upward
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdɔːr.sɪ.fleks/ /; / /ˈdɔːr.sə.fleks/ (AmE), /ˈdɔː.sɪ.fleks/ /; / /ˈdɔː.sə.fleks/ (BrE)
to bend a body part, especially the foot, upward toward the back of the body

Examples

To dorsiflex the ankle

Physical therapists often instruct patients to dorsiflex their feet to improve mobility.
If you dorsiflex your toes, you may reduce the risk of cramping during exercise.
