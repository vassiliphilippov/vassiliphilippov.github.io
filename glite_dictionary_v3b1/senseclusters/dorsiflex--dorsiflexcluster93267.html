dorsiflex

verb
to bend upward
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔrsɪˌflɛks / (AmE), / ˈdɔːsɪˌflɛks / (BrE)
to bend a body part, especially the foot, upward toward the back of the body

Examples

To dorsiflex the ankle
During the stretch, remember to dorsiflex your ankle to feel the muscle working.
Physical therapists often instruct patients to dorsiflex their feet to improve mobility.
If you dorsiflex your toes, you may reduce the risk of cramping during exercise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flex - to bend a limb or joint
  • antonym: plantarflex - to bend the foot downward away from the leg
