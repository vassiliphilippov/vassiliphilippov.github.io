predigital

noun
person before digital era
Pronunciation: / priːˈdɪdʒɪtəl / (AmE), / priːˈdɪdʒɪtəl / (BrE)
a person who lived or grew up before digital technology became widespread

Examples

A predigital person
As a predigital, she prefers reading printed books over using an e-reader.
Many predigitals find it challenging to adapt to new smartphone apps.
The workshop was designed to help predigital employees become more comfortable with online tools.
