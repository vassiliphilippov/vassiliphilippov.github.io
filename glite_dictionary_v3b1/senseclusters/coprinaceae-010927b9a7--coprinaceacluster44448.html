Coprinaceae

noun
former family of inkcap fungi
9.9
Pronunciation: /kɑː.prɪ.ˈneɪ.siː/ (AmE), /kɒ.prɪ.ˈneɪ.siː/ (BrE)
a former family of fungi including the inkcap mushrooms, now usually included in the family Agaricaceae

Examples

Recent studies have shown that Coprinaceae is no longer a valid taxonomic group.
Several species formerly classified in Coprinaceae are now placed in other families.
The family Coprinaceae was characterized by its fragile, inky mushrooms.
