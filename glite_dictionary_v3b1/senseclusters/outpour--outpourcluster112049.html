outpour

noun
sudden flow
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌpɔr / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌpɔː / (BrE)
a large or sudden flow of something, especially emotions, support, or liquid

Examples

Outpour of emotion
The announcement led to an outpour of sympathy from the community.
After the disaster, there was an outpour of donations to help the victims.
She felt an outpour of relief when she heard the good news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flood - a large amount of something that comes quickly
  • synonym: outpouring - a strong and sudden expression or flow of something
