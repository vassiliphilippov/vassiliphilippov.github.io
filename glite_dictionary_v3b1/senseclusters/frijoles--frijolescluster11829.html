frijoles

noun
Mexican beans
Pronunciation: / friˈxoʊleɪz / (AmE), / friˈhɒleɪz / (BrE)
beans, especially those used in Mexican or Latin American cooking, often served as a staple food

Examples

Cooked frijoles dish
The restaurant served delicious frijoles alongside the grilled chicken.
She learned how to cook traditional frijoles from her grandmother.
We enjoyed a simple meal of rice and frijoles after a long day.
