palm-shaped

adjective
leaf with parts radiating like a palm
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑm ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈpɑːm ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
(of a leaf) having leaflets or lobes radiating from a single common point, resembling the shape of a palm.

Examples

Palm-shaped leaves
Many houseplants feature palm-shaped leaves that add a tropical feel to interiors.
The botanist described the new plant's foliage as palm-shaped, with lobes spreading outward.
The maple tree is known for its distinctive palm-shaped leaves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: palmate - (of leaves) having parts spreading out from a single point like fingers from a palm
