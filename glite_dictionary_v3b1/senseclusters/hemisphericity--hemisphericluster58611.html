hemisphericity

noun
brain hemisphere dominance
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛmɪˌsfɛˈrɪsɪti / (AmE), / ˌhɛmɪˌsfɛˈrɪsɪti / (BrE)
the degree to which one cerebral hemisphere of the brain is more dominant or specialized than the other

Examples

Brain hemisphericity
Researchers are investigating how hemisphericity affects language learning in children.
Some theories suggest that hemisphericity influences whether a person is more analytical or creative.
The concept of hemisphericity is often discussed in studies about brain function and specialization.
