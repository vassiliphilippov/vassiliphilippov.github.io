tye

noun
old word for knot
Pronunciation: / taɪ / (AmE), / taɪ / (BrE)
an old word for a knot or something used to tie or fasten objects together

Examples

A rope tye
The sailor fastened the rope with a secure tye before leaving the dock.
In old shipbuilding manuals, a tye is often mentioned as a way to secure sails.
He used a strong tye to keep the bundle of wood together during transport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knot - an interlacing of rope or string used to fasten or bind
  • synonym: tie - a piece of string, rope, or similar used for fastening
