clothes

noun
garments; items worn on the body
Pronunciation: / kloʊðz / (AmE), / kləʊðz / (BrE)
items worn to cover the body; garments or dress in general.

Examples

Clothes for winter
His clothes were soaked after walking through the rain.
She packed all her clothes into a suitcase before leaving for college.
You should wear warm clothes if you're going outside in winter.
