phonically

adverb
relating to sound
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːnɪkli / (AmE), / ˈfəʊnɪkli /; / ˈfɒnɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to sound, speech, or pronunciation

Examples

Phonically alerting users
The new alarm system alerts users phonically with a series of beeps.

The two words are spelled differently but are phonically identical.
Children learn to read by breaking words down phonically into their basic sounds.
Some languages are written phonically, making pronunciation easier to predict from spelling.
