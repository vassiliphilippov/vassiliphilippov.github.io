agger

noun
small anatomical ridge
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæɡər / (AmE), / ˈæɡə / (BrE)
a small ridge or elevation of bone or tissue, especially in the nasal cavity, such as the agger nasi

Examples

Nasal agger
The surgeon examined the agger to assess obstruction in the patient's nasal passage.
A prominent agger can sometimes be seen in detailed scans of the nasal cavity.
During the procedure, the doctor carefully avoided damaging the agger nasi region.

  • synonym: ridge - a long, narrow raised part of a surface
