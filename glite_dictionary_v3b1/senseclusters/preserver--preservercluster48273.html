preserver

noun
person who protects from harm or danger
2.4
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɝːvɚ /; / prɪˈzɜrvər / (AmE), / prɪˈzɜːvə / (BrE)
a person who protects, saves, or keeps something in good condition (such as people, traditions, food, or objects)

The preserver worked meticulously to restore the historic mansion's wooden paneling.
