upturned

adjective
having the end turned upward (esp. nose)
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌpˈtɝnd / (AmE), / ˌʌpˈtɜːnd / (BrE)
(of an object, especially a nose) having the end turned upward.

Examples
