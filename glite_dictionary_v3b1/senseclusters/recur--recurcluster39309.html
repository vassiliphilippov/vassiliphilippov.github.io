recur

verb
return to a subject in thought or speech
7.7
Pronunciation: / rəˈkɜr / (AmE), / rɪˈkɜː / (BrE)
to return to a subject or idea in thought or in conversation

Examples

To recur to an idea
During discussions, he tended to recur to the same topic repeatedly.
Memories of childhood often recur when I'm feeling nostalgic.
No matter how hard she tried, her mind would recur to the accident.
