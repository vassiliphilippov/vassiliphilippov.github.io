in question

adjective
under doubt or suspicion
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈkwɛstʃən / (AmE), / ɪn ˈkwɛstʃən / (BrE)
being under doubt or suspicion; not yet resolved or certain.

Examples

In question loyalty
Her loyalty is in question after the recent events.
The honesty of the witness was in question during the entire trial.
Whether the project will be completed on time is still in question.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doubtful - uncertain, open to doubt or question
  • synonym: dubious - hesitating or doubting; not to be relied upon
