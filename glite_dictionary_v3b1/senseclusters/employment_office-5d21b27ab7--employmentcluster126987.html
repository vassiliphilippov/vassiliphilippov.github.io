employment office

noun
agency that helps people find jobs
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈplɔɪmənt ˈɑːfɪs / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔɪmənt ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
a government or private agency that helps people find jobs or fills vacancies for employers

Examples

Local employment office
After losing his job, he got assistance at the local employment office.
She visited the employment office to search for job vacancies.
The government plans to open a new employment office downtown next year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: job centre - a British English term for a government employment office
