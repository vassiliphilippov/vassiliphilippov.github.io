extravascular

adjective
outside blood vessels
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəˈvæskjələr / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈvæskjʊlə / (BrE)
located or occurring outside the blood vessels

Examples

Extravascular leakage
The nurse checked for any extravascular leakage after administering the medication.
Swelling can occur if fluids collect in the extravascular space.
Certain drugs are designed to remain extravascular to avoid entering the bloodstream directly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intravascular - located or occurring within the blood vessels
