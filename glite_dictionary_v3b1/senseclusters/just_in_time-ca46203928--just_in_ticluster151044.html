Synonyms

  • in the nick of time
    3.5 0.53/m
    in the nick of time means arriving at the last moment to avoid trouble, while just in time is the general phrase.
  • at the last minute
    2.5 4.17/m
    at the last minute means deciding or acting just before a deadline, while just in time means before it is too late.
  • at the eleventh hour
    4.5 0.25/m
    at the eleventh hour sounds more formal and suggests action near a deadline, while just in time is more general and neutral.
  • not a minute too soon
    5.9 0.00/m
    not a minute too soon is said after an event to show relief it barely happened, while just in time states timing.
