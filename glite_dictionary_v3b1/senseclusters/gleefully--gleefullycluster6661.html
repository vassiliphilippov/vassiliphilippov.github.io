gleefully

adverb
in a joyful or delighted manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡliːfəli / (AmE), / ˈɡliːfəli / (BrE)
In a joyous and delighted manner; with great pleasure and excitement.

Examples

To gleefully accept
He gleefully accepted the prize, unable to contain his excitement.
She danced gleefully around the room when she heard the good news.
The children shouted gleefully as they ran through the park's fountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joyfully - in a joyful manner
  • antonym: sadly - in a sad manner
