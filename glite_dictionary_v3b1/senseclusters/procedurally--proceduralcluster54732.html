procedurally

adverb
by following procedures
5.3
Pronunciation: /prə.ˈsiː.dʒə.rə.li/ /; / /prə.ˈsiː.dʒrə.li/ (AmE), /prə.ˈsiː.dʒə.rə.li/ /; / /prə.ˈsiː.dʒrə.li/ (BrE)
in a way that follows a set of steps, rules, or official procedures
