procedurally

adverb
by following procedures
Pronunciation: / prəˈsiːdʒɚəli / (AmE), / prəˈsiːdʒərəli / (BrE)
in a way that follows a set of steps, rules, or official procedures

Examples

Done procedurally step by step
The software was designed procedurally to ensure each step was clear and repeatable.
He solved the puzzle procedurally, following a specific sequence of moves.
In some games, levels are generated procedurally rather than being hand-crafted by designers.
The report was procedurally reviewed before being published to ensure accuracy.
All complaints must be handled procedurally according to company policy.
The software generates new maps procedurally each time you start a game.
The judge dismissed the case procedurally because the correct documents were not filed.
The proposal was rejected procedurally, not because of its content.
They handled the complaint procedurally, following every step required by the regulations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: formally - in a way that follows established rules or conventions
  • antonym: randomly - in a way that lacks a set method or order
  • antonym: substantively - in a way that relates to the essential meaning or importance
  • synonym: systematically - in a methodical and organized way
