grown-up

adjective
mature or responsible
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊn ʌp / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊn ʌp / (BrE)
behaving in a mature, responsible, or adult manner

Examples

Grown-up behavior
She handled the difficult situation in a very grown-up way.
It is important to have a grown-up conversation about finances before moving in together.
His grown-up attitude impressed his teachers and classmates.

Related Terms

  • antonym: childish - behaving in an immature or silly way
  • synonym: mature - showing adult qualities or behavior
