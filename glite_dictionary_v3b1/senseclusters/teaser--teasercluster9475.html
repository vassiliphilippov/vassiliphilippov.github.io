teaser

noun
sports-betting wager adjusting point spreads
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːzər / (AmE), / ˈtiːzə / (BrE)
a sports-betting wager in which the bettor adjusts the point spread or totals for two or more games in exchange for a lower payout and changed odds.

Examples

Football teaser bet
He placed a teaser on the NFL game to move the point spread three points.
Bookmakers offered a six-point teaser allowing bettors to combine two games for smaller payouts.
We used a teaser to lower the total so both games would seem like safer bets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parlay - a multi-game bet that combines selections for a larger payout; similar multi-leg gambling wager.
