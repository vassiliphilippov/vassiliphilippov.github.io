Hayreddin Barbarossa

proper noun
Ottoman admiral and Barbary pirate
Pronunciation: / haɪˈrɛdɪn ˌbɑrbəˈroʊsə / (AmE), / haɪˈrɛdɪn ˌbɑːbəˈrɒsə / (BrE)
(c. 1478–1546) An Ottoman admiral and privateer renowned for commanding the Ottoman navy and for his exploits as a Barbary pirate in the Mediterranean.

Examples

Hayreddin Barbarossa admiral
Historians often cite Hayreddin Barbarossa as one of the greatest naval commanders of his era.
Ottoman dominance in the Mediterranean was secured in part by the actions of Hayreddin Barbarossa.
The exploits of Hayreddin Barbarossa inspired many legends throughout the Mediterranean.
