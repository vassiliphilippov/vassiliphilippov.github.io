comodo

adjective
comfortable tempo (music)
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊməˌdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒməˌdəʊ / (BrE)
in music, indicating that a passage should be played at a comfortable or convenient tempo
