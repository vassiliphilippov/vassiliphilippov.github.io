unilingual

noun
single-language dictionary
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪˈlɪŋɡwəl / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈlɪŋɡwəl / (BrE)
a dictionary that provides definitions or explanations in only one language

Examples

Unilingual dictionary
She preferred using a unilingual for her English studies instead of a bilingual dictionary.
A unilingual is often recommended for advanced language learners to encourage immersion.
The library has a large collection of unilingual dictionaries for different languages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bilingual - a dictionary providing translations between two languages
  • synonym: monolingual - a dictionary using only one language for definitions
