endmost

adjective
at the furthest end
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛndˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈɛnd.məʊst / (BrE)
situated at or forming the furthest end of something.

Examples

The endmost window
From the endmost window, you could see the entire garden.
She placed the vase on the endmost shelf, away from the reach of children.
The endmost seat was unoccupied during the concert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: terminal - forming or situated at the end or extremity of something
