semisolid

adjective
partly solid; between solid and liquid
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈsɑlɪd / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈsɒlɪd / (BrE)
partly solid; having rigidity and viscosity intermediate between a solid and a liquid

Examples

Semisolid food
Jelly is a semisolid food that can wobble but holds its shape.
She mixed the ingredients until the batter became a thick, semisolid paste.
The ointment should have a semisolid consistency, making it easy to apply to the skin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: liquid - a substance that flows freely but is of constant volume
  • antonym: solid - a substance with a firm shape and structure
