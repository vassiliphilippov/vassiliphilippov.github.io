Avesta

noun
Zoroastrian scriptures
Pronunciation: / əˈvɛstə / (AmE), / əˈvɛstə / (BrE)
the sacred scriptures of Zoroastrianism, containing the religious teachings and hymns of the faith

Examples

Avesta scriptures
The Avesta is written in an ancient language known as Avestan.
Priests recite passages from the Avesta during Zoroastrian ceremonies.
Many historical details about ancient Persia are preserved in the Avesta.
