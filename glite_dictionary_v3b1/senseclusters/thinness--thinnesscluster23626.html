thinness

noun
state of having little body fat
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪnnəs / (AmE), / ˈθɪnnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of having little body fat

Examples

Thinness of her body
Her thinness made her appear frail, but she was surprisingly strong.
His extreme thinness concerned his doctors, who worried about his health.
Some people strive for thinness due to societal beauty standards.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fatness - the quality of having a large amount of body fat
  • synonym: leanness - the quality of having little body fat or flesh
