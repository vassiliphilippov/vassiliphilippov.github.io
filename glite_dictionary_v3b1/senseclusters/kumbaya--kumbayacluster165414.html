kumbaya

noun
folk spiritual song
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌkʌmbɑːˈjɑː / (AmE), / ˌkʌmbɑːˈjɑː / (BrE)
a spiritual or folk song, often sung at gatherings, expressing a call for help, unity, or peace

Examples

Sing kumbaya together
The group gathered around the fire and sang kumbaya together.
At the end of the retreat, everyone joined hands and softly sang kumbaya.
The children learned to sing kumbaya at summer camp.

Related Terms

  • synonym: folk song - a traditional song passed down orally among people
  • synonym: spiritual - a religious song of African American origin
