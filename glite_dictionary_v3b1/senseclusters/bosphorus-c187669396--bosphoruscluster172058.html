Bosphorus

proper noun
strait between Europe and Asia in Turkey
6.4 0.24/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ëÀês.f…ôr.…ôs / (AmE), / Ààb…ís.f…ôr.…ôs / (BrE)

Examples
