fidelity

noun
faithfulness; loyalty
4.8
Pronunciation: / fəˈdɛlɪti / (AmE), / fɪˈdɛlɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being loyal or faithful to someone or something; strict observance of promises or duties.

Examples

Fidelity to a friend
Her fidelity to her friends was admired by everyone who knew her.
The lawyer questioned the fidelity of the witness's account.
They doubted his fidelity after he betrayed their trust.

Related Terms

  • antonym: infidelity - unfaithfulness, especially in marriage
  • synonym: loyalty - the quality of being loyal
