wear a helmet

verb
put on and use protective headgear
Pronunciation: / wɛr ə ˈhɛlmət / (AmE), / wɛə ə ˈhɛlmɪt / (BrE)
to put on and use a protective covering for the head, especially for safety during certain activities

Examples

To wear a helmet properly
Cyclists are required by law to wear a helmet in many countries.
He forgot to wear a helmet and injured his head during the game.
My mother always reminds me to wear a helmet when I skate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: helmet - a hard or padded protective hat
  • synonym: wear - to have (clothing or an accessory) on one's body
