thin

adjective
having little body fat; slender
1.4 1.0
Pronunciation: /θɪn/ (AmE), /θɪn/ (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having little or no excess body fat; slim or slender in build.

Examples

A thin person
After months of illness, she looked very thin and pale.
He managed to stay thin despite eating large meals every day.
Models in fashion magazines are often criticized for being unrealistically thin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fat - having a large amount of excess flesh
  • synonym: skinny - very thin, often unattractively so
  • synonym: slender - gracefully thin (of a person); slim
