airline

noun
tube for carrying pressurized air
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈeəˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a flexible hose or tube used to convey air under pressure, typically in pneumatic systems or aquariums

Examples

Aquarium airline tube
Be sure to check each airline for leaks before operating the machinery.
He replaced the damaged airline to restore proper airflow to the compressor.
The aquarium pump is connected to the filter by a clear airline.
