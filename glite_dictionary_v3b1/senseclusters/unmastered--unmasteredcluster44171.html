unmastered

adjective
not learned or controlled
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmæstərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɑːstəd / (BrE)
not learned, controlled, or brought to a level of skill or proficiency

Examples

Unmastered skill
The complex dance routine remained unmastered by most of the students after several rehearsals.
Many of the advanced grammar rules are still unmastered by beginners.
His fear of heights was an unmastered challenge throughout his life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unskilled - not having skill or training
