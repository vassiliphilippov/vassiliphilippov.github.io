shrug

noun
shoulder gesture showing indifference or uncertainty
9.2
Pronunciation: / ʃrʌɡ / (AmE), / ʃrʌɡ / (BrE)
An act of raising and lowering the shoulders to express uncertainty, indifference, or lack of knowledge.

Examples

A quick shrug
A simple shrug told me he didn't know the answer.
She answered my request with only a shrug and a smile.
With a careless shrug, he turned away from the question.
