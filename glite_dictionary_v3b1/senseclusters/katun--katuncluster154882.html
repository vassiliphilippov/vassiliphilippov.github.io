katun

noun
Balkan shepherd community
Pronunciation: / kɑːˈtuːn / (AmE), / kəˈtuːn / (BrE)
a historical social unit or community of semi-nomadic shepherds in the medieval Balkans, often organized as a pastoral clan

Examples

A katun of shepherds
In medieval times, a katun would move seasonally with their flocks across the mountains.
The elders of the katun made important decisions for the entire community.
Historical documents describe how each katun managed its own grazing lands and resources.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clan - a group of families with a common ancestor
  • synonym: tribe - a social group consisting of people with shared ancestry and culture
