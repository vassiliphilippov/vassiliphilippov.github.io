on-brand

adjective
consistent with a brand's identity
Pronunciation: / ɑn ˈbrænd / (AmE), / ɒn ˈbrænd / (BrE)
consistent with or characteristic of a particular brand's identity, style, or values

Examples

On-brand message
Choosing eco-friendly packaging was very on-brand for the sustainable fashion label.
Her social media posts always stay on-brand with the company's message.
The new logo design is completely on-brand and reflects our core values.

Related Terms

  • antonym: off-brand - not consistent with a brand's identity; not characteristic of the brand
