homebred

adjective
bred at home
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌbrɛd / (BrE)
bred, raised, or produced at home or in one's own country rather than imported or brought from elsewhere

Examples

Homebred vegetables
The family takes pride in their homebred vegetables, which are grown in their own garden.
Many racing fans prefer homebred horses over those imported from abroad.
The chef uses only homebred chickens for his signature dishes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domestic - produced or existing within a particular country
  • antonym: imported - brought in from another country
