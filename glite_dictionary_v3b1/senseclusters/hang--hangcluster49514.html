Synonyms

  • fall
    2.6 7.54/m
    fall describes hair or cloth that moves down loosely and often smoothly, while hang is the general verb for any loose arrangement.
  • flow
    2.0 2.97/m
    flow shows hair or fabric moving smoothly and gently as if alive, while hang means being placed or left loose.
  • drape
    4.3 9.12/m
    drape means arranging cloth over something for style or cover, while hang means simply to fall or lie loose.
  • dangle
    2.3 13/m
    dangle means to swing down from a fixed point and move freely, while hang is a broader word for material left loose or draped.
