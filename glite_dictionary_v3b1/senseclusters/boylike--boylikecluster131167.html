boylike

adjective, adverb
resembling or characteristic of a boy
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪˌlaɪk /; / ˈbɔɪ.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪ.laɪk / (BrE)
having or showing qualities typical of a boy; in a way that resembles a boy (youthful or innocent)
