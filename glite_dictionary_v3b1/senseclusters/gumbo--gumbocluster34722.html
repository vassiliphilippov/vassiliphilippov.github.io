gumbo

noun
sticky, fine-grained silty soil
7.6 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈgʌm.boʊ/ (AmE), /ˈgʌm.bəʊ/ (BrE)
A fine-grained, silty soil that becomes waxy and very sticky when saturated with water.
