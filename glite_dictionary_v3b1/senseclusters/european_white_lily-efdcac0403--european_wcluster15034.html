European white lily

noun
European water lily with white flowers
Pronunciation: / jʊˈrəʊpiən waɪt ˈlɪli / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən waɪt ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A species of water lily native to Europe, known for its large white flowers that float on the surface of ponds and lakes.

Examples

The European white lily flower
In traditional folklore, the European white lily symbolizes purity and innocence.
She planted a European white lily in her garden because of its striking beauty.
The pond was covered in blooming European white lilies during the summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: water lily - an aquatic plant with large floating leaves and showy flowers
