cheetah

noun
fast, spotted wild cat
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃi tə / (AmE), / ˈtʃiː tə / (BrE)
a large, slender, spotted wild cat native to Africa and southwestern Asia, known for being the fastest land mammal

Examples

A cheetah runs fast
A cheetah can accelerate from zero to sixty miles per hour in just a few seconds.
The cheetah's distinctive black spots help it blend into the tall grasses of the savanna.
Unlike other big cats, the cheetah hunts mainly during the day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leopard - large, spotted wild cat native to Africa and Asia
  • synonym: tiger - large wild cat with stripes
