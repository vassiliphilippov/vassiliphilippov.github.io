seducer

noun
man who seduces women
6.0
Pronunciation: / sɪˈdusər / (AmE), / sɪˈdjuːsə / (BrE)
a person who tries to attract someone romantically or sexually, often using charm or persuasion

The novel tells the story of a famous seducer who travels across Europe.
He gained a reputation as a seducer because of his charming words and confident manner.
Many people warned her about the seducer who often visited the café.
