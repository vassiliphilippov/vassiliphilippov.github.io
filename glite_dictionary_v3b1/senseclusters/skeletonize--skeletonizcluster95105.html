skeletonize

verb
reduce to outlines or basic structure
1.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈskel.ə.tə.naɪz/ /; / /ˈskel.ə.tən.aɪz/ (AmE), /ˈskel.ə.tə.naɪz/ /; / /ˈskel.ə.tən.aɪz/ (BrE)
to reduce (a drawing, image, or structure) to its essential lines or framework; to outline or simplify to the basic form.

Examples

To skeletonize a drawing
Before adding color, the illustrator decided to skeletonize the drawing to highlight the main features.
Digital artists often skeletonize images to study their underlying structure.
Teachers may ask students to skeletonize the drawing as part of a composition exercise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outline - draw or mark the outer edge or shape of something
