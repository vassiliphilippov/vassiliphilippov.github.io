twitchiness

noun
nervous restlessness
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪtʃinəs / (AmE), / ˈtwɪtʃɪnɪs / (BrE)
a state of being nervous, restless, or prone to making sudden small movements

Examples

Nervous twitchiness
His twitchiness was obvious as he waited for the test results.
The dog's twitchiness made it difficult for the vet to examine him.
She tried to hide her twitchiness during the important presentation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmness - the state of being relaxed and free from nervousness
  • synonym: nervousness - the quality of being anxious or uneasy
