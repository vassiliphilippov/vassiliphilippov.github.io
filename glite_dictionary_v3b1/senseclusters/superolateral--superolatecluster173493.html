superolateral

adjective
above and to the side
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpəroʊˈlætərəl / (AmE), / ˌsuːpərəʊˈlætərəl / (BrE)
located above and to the side of a reference point, especially in anatomical contexts

Examples

The superolateral part
The doctor noted swelling on the superolateral part of the patient's knee.
Pain was reported in the superolateral region of the right shoulder.
The muscle attaches to the superolateral border of the bone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inferomedial - located below and toward the midline
