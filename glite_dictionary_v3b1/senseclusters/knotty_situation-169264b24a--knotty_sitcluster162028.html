knotty situation

noun
a complex or difficult problem
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑti sɪˈtʃueɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈnɒti sɪˈtʃuːeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a complex and difficult problem or situation that is hard to solve

Examples

A knotty situation
Resolving the budget shortfall became a knotty situation for the committee.
She found herself in a knotty situation when the evidence contradicted her statement.
The new regulations put us in a knotty situation we hadn't foreseen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dilemma - a situation in which a difficult choice has to be made between two or more alternatives
  • synonym: predicament - a difficult, unpleasant, or embarrassing situation
