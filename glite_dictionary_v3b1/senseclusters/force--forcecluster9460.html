force

noun
legal validity or effect of a law
6.0 8/m
Pronunciation: / f…îrs / (AmE), / f…îÀês / (BrE)
(of a law or rule) the state of being valid or operative legally; the capacity to have legal effect.

Examples

In force legally
Several outdated rules remain technically in force despite being ignored.
That law is no longer in force after the changes.
The new regulations will come into force next month.
