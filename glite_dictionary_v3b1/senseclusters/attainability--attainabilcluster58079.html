attainability

noun
the state of being achievable
Pronunciation: / əˌteɪnəˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / əˌteɪnəˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The state or quality of being able to be achieved or reached.

Examples

The attainability of goals
She questioned the attainability of perfection in creative work.
The attainability of the company's goals depends on careful planning and resource allocation.
There are concerns about the attainability of universal healthcare in the next decade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: achievability - the state of being possible to achieve
