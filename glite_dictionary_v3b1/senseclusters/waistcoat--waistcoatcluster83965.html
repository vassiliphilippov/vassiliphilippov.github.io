waistcoat

noun
a sleeveless garment worn under a jacket
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪsˌkoʊt / (AmE), / ˈweskət / (BrE)
a sleeveless garment for the upper body, typically worn by men under a jacket as part of a suit

Examples

A waistcoat under jacket
He buttoned his waistcoat before putting on his jacket.
In England, it is traditional to wear a waistcoat with a three-piece suit.
The groom chose a grey waistcoat to match his tie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vest - a sleeveless garment, especially in American English for the same article of clothing
