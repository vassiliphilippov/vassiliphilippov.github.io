Examples

Synonyms

    4.1 7.71/m

  • sunshade
    2.1 0.14/m
    a sunshade is a mainly portable cover that gives shade from the sun, while an awning usually attaches to buildings for sun or rain protection.
  • sunblind
    6.4 0.00/m
    a sunblind is a screen or vertical cover used to block direct sunlight, while an awning extends outward to shelter from sun or rain.
