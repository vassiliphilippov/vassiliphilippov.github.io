anopheline

adjective
relating to malaria-transmitting mosquitoes
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑːfəˌlaɪn / (AmE), / əˈnɒfəˌlaɪn / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of mosquitoes of the genus Anopheles, which transmit malaria.

Examples

Anopheline mosquitoes
Insecticide-treated nets are an effective strategy against anopheline vectors.
Only female anopheline mosquitoes are capable of transmitting malaria to humans.
The researchers studied the anopheline population in areas with high malaria transmission rates.

Related Terms
