lightlessness

noun
total absence of light
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪt.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
the state of having no light at all; total darkness

Examples

Total lightlessness
During the blackout, the entire city was plunged into lightlessness for hours.
He felt a sense of unease in the utter lightlessness of the abandoned house.
The deep cave was filled with lightlessness, making it impossible to see anything.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brightness - the quality of being full of light
  • synonym: darkness - the partial or total absence of light
