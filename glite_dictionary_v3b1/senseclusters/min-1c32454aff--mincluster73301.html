Min

noun
a group of Chinese languages of Fujian province
9.9
Pronunciation: / min / (AmE), / mɪn / (BrE)
any of several Sinitic languages and dialects spoken primarily in Fujian province and parts of southeast China, known collectively as Min Chinese.

Examples

Min Chinese language
Linguists classify Hokkien as a variety of Min Chinese.
Many emigrants from Fujian speak Min dialects at home.
She is fluent in both Mandarin and Min.
