snail butter

noun
butter mix for cooking snails
9.9
Pronunciation: / sneɪl ˈbʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / sneɪl ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
A seasoned butter mixture with shallots, garlic, and parsley, used especially for cooking snails.
