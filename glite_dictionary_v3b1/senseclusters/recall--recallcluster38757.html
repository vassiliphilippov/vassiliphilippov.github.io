recall

noun
manufacturer's request to return a defective product
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɔl / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːl / (BrE)
an official request by a manufacturer for consumers to return a defective product for replacement or repair

Examples

Product recall notice
A massive car recall affected thousands of vehicles due to faulty airbags.
If you receive a letter about a product recall, contact the manufacturer for instructions.
The company issued a recall of its popular toy after safety concerns were raised.
