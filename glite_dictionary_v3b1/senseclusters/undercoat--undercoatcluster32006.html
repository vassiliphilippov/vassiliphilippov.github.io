undercoat

verb
apply a primer before painting
6.7 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈʌn.dər.koʊt/ (AmE), /ˈʌn.də.kəʊt/ (BrE)

Examples

To undercoat a wall
Before painting, you should always undercoat the walls to ensure a smooth finish.
She spent the morning undercoating the doors to prepare them for the top coat.
The carpenter carefully undercoated the wooden panels before applying the final color.
