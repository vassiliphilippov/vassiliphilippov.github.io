nimbostratus

noun
rain-producing cloud
Pronunciation: / ˌnɪmboʊˈstreɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌnɪmbəʊˈstrɑːtəs / (BrE)
a thick, dark, low cloud layer that produces continuous, steady rain or snow

Examples

Thick nimbostratus clouds
A thick layer of nimbostratus covered the sky, bringing hours of steady rain.
The weather forecast predicted nimbostratus clouds and continuous drizzle throughout the afternoon.
Snow fell gently from the nimbostratus that stretched across the entire horizon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cumulonimbus - a tall, dense cloud associated with thunderstorms
  • synonym: stratus - a low, gray, uniform cloud layer
