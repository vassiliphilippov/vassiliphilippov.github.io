artistic

adjective
relating to art or artists
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪstɪk / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to art or artists, or showing creative skill, beauty, or good taste

Examples

Artistic style
He developed an artistic style that set him apart from his peers.
Her artistic talent was evident in every painting she created.
The gallery displayed a range of artistic photographs from local artists.
She admired the artistic arrangement of flowers on the table.
The designer created an artistic centerpiece for the gala dinner.
Their apartment is filled with artistic touches and beautiful furniture.
She arranged the flowers in an artistic manner that impressed everyone.
The mural added an artistic touch to the otherwise plain wall.
The vase was admired for its artistic design and attention to detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aesthetic - concerned with beauty or the appreciation of beauty
  • synonym: artful - showing creative skill or taste
  • synonym: creative - having the ability to create, especially in an artistic context
  • antonym: scientific - relating to science or scientists
  • antonym: unartistic - lacking in artistic quality or skill
