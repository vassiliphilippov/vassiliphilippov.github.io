garden snail

noun
a common land snail considered a garden pest
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdn̩ sneɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn sneɪl / (BrE)
a common land snail, often found in gardens, belonging to the genus Helix and considered a pest due to its destructiveness to plants

Examples

A garden snail crawling
After the rain, a garden snail was crawling across the patio.
Children often collect garden snails in jars to observe their behavior.
The garden snail can cause significant damage to lettuce and other crops.
