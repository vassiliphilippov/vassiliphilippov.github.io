fluttering

noun
the act of moving by quick flaps
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌtərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflʌtərɪŋ / (BrE)
a quick, light movement made by flapping up and down, especially of wings or lightweight objects

Examples

A fluttering of wings
She noticed a fluttering in her chest from excitement.
The sudden fluttering of wings startled the resting cat.
There was a gentle fluttering of leaves as the breeze passed through the trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flapping - the act of moving up and down or to and fro with a noise
