subdwarf

adjective
of a faint star
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌdwɔrf / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌdwɑːf / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a subdwarf star, which is a faint, low-luminosity star.

Examples

Subdwarf star type
The astronomer identified a subdwarf star in the outer region of the galaxy.
Many subdwarf stars are difficult to detect because of their low brightness.
A subdwarf companion was discovered orbiting a more massive star in the binary system.
