flower

noun
the prime or best period
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
the period of greatest prosperity, development, or productivity; the best part or prime of something

Examples

The flower of youth
During the flower of her career, she received many awards for her achievements.
He was still in the flower of youth when he wrote his best works.
The city was in the flower of its prosperity during the Renaissance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decline - the period after the height of success or productivity
  • synonym: prime - the period of greatest vigor or success
