bluffness

noun
good-natured frankness
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌfnəs / (AmE), / ˈblʌfnəs / (BrE)
a quality of being frank and direct in a hearty or good-natured way

Examples

His bluffness style
His bluffness put everyone at ease during the tense meeting.
People admired her bluffness and straightforward attitude, which made her easy to trust.
There was a certain bluffness in his voice that made his criticism feel less harsh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candor - the quality of being open and honest
  • synonym: frankness - quality of being open, honest, and direct
  • antonym: insincerity - lack of honesty or authenticity
