microdevice

noun
very small device made by microfabrication
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊdɪˌvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊdɪˌvaɪs / (BrE)
a very small device manufactured using microfabrication techniques, typically on the scale of micrometres or smaller

Examples

A microdevice chip
A single microdevice can fit on the tip of a needle, revolutionizing medical diagnostics.
Researchers are developing a microdevice capable of detecting toxins in drinking water.
The microdevice was implanted to monitor the patient's heart rate in real time.
