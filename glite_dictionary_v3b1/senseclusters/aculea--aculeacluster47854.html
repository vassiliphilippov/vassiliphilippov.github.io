aculea

noun
small spines on insect wings
Pronunciation: / əˈkjuːliə / (AmE), / əˈkjuːliə / (BrE)
Small, spiny outgrowths found on the wings of certain insects.

Examples

Small aculea on wings
Scientists observed distinct patterns of aculea on the beetle's wing membranes.
The butterfly's wings are covered with tiny aculea that aid in camouflage.
