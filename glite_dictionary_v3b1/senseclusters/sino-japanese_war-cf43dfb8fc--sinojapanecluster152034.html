Sino-Japanese War

proper noun
1894–1895 war between China and Japan
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪnoʊ dʒəˈpæniz wɔr / (AmE), / ˌsʌɪnəʊ dʒəˈpaniːz wɔː / (BrE)
The war fought between China and Japan from 1894 to 1895, primarily over control of the Korean Peninsula, resulting in a decisive Japanese victory.
