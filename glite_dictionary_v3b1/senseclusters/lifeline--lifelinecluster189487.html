lifeline

noun
crease on the palm associated with lifespan
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪfˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈlaɪfˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a crease on the palm, traditionally believed in palmistry to predict the length of a person's life

Examples

Palm lifeline
He examined the shape of his lifeline, curious about what it might reveal.
Some people believe that a long lifeline means a long and healthy life.
The palmist traced the lifeline on her hand and smiled reassuringly.
