Wilsonian

adjective
relating to Woodrow Wilson or his policies
8.5
Pronunciation: /wɪl.ˈsoʊ.nɪ.ən/ (AmE), /wɪl.ˈsəʊ.nɪ.ən/ (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Woodrow Wilson, his ideas, principles, or policies, especially in international relations.

Examples

Wilsonian foreign policy
Her foreign policy proposals are often described as Wilsonian in their idealism.
Many historians debate the impact of Wilsonian principles on modern international relations.
The treaty was crafted with a distinctly Wilsonian emphasis on self-determination and peace.
