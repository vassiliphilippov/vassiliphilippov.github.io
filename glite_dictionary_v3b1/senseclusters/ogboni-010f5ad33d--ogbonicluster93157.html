Ogboni

noun
member of Ogboni society
Pronunciation: / oʊɡˈboʊni / (AmE), / əʊɡˈbəʊni / (BrE)
a member or elder of the Ogboni society, a traditional secret religious and judicial group among the Yoruba people of Nigeria

Examples

An Ogboni elder
The respected Ogboni played a key role in resolving conflicts within the community.
He was honored to be initiated as an Ogboni after many years of service.
The village sought guidance from the Ogboni during important ceremonies.
