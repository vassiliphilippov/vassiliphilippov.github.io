sunshine

noun
pleasant, sunny weather
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌʃaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsʌnʃaɪn / (BrE)
the light and warmth from the sun, or pleasant weather with clear, sunny skies

Examples

Bright sunshine

We decided to go hiking because the sunshine made the weather perfect.
After days of rain, the sunshine made everyone feel cheerful again.
The garden needs plenty of sunshine to thrive.
We sat outside, enjoying the warm sunshine on our faces.

  • synonym: sunlight - the light that comes from the sun
