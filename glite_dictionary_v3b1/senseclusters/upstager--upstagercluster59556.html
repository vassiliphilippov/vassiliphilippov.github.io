upstager

noun
selfish actor who upstages others
5.5 8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌsteɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈʌpsteɪdʒə / (BrE)
An actor who deliberately draws attention to themselves at the expense of others on stage.

Examples

The upstager actor
Everyone in the cast grew annoyed with the upstager who constantly tried to be the center of attention.
No one liked working with him because he had a reputation as an infamous upstager.
The director warned the actors not to become a upstager during rehearsals.
