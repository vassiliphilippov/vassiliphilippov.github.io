Lower Paleolithic

noun
the earliest part of the Paleolithic Age
9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈloʊ.ər peɪ.lɪ.oʊ.ˈlɪθ.ɪk/ /; / /ˈlaʊ.ər peɪ.lɪ.oʊ.ˈlɪθ.ɪk/ /; / /ˈloʊ.ər pæl.lɪ.oʊ.ˈlɪθ.ɪk/ (AmE), /ˈləʊ.ə peɪ.lɪ.əˈlɪθ.ɪk/ /; / /ˈlaʊ.ə peɪ.lɪ.əˈlɪθ.ɪk/ /; / /ˈləʊ.ə pæl.lɪ.əˈlɪθ.ɪk/ (BrE)
the earliest phase of the Paleolithic Age, characterized by the first use of stone tools and the emergence of the hand ax, lasting until about 120,000 years ago

Examples
