quadrupling

noun
increase by four times
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈdruːplɪŋ / (AmE), / kwɒˈdruːplɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of increasing something by four times its original amount

Examples

A quadrupling in number
A quadrupling in the number of applications forced the company to expand its team.
Since 2000, the country has witnessed a quadrupling of its broadband internet usage.
The quadrupling of gas prices caused widespread concern among commuters.
