wound

adjective
coiled or wrapped around
Pronunciation: / waʊnd / (AmE), / waʊnd / (BrE)
(of a string, wire, or similar object) arranged into or forming a coil or coils.

Examples

Wound wire coil
She bought a spool of tightly wound thread for her sewing project.
The clock is powered by a wound spring mechanism.
The electrician used wound copper wire for the transformer.
