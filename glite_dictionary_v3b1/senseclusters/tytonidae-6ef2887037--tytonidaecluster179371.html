Tytonidae

noun
family of barn owls (taxonomy)
9.9
Pronunciation: / taɪˈtɑːnɪdiː / (AmE), / tɪˈtɒnɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of owls comprising only the barn owls, distinguished by their heart-shaped faces and cosmopolitan distribution.
