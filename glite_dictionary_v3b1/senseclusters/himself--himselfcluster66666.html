himself

pronoun
male reflexive pronoun
Pronunciation: / hɪmˈsɛlf / (AmE), / hɪmˈsɛlf / (BrE)
the reflexive form of 'he', used to refer back to a male subject (either when he does something to himself or to emphasize that he did it personally)

Examples

Himself taught guitar
He taught himself to play the guitar by watching online videos.
After the accident, he had to learn how to walk by himself again.
The boy was proud that he finished the puzzle all by himself.
He built the entire shed himself without any help from his friends.
The president himself signed the letter to show its importance.
After the accident, he blamed himself for what happened.
