paedobaptist

noun
infant baptism supporter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːdoʊˈbæptɪst / (AmE), / ˌpiːdəʊˈbæptɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or practices the baptism of infants or young children

Examples

A paedobaptist believer
As a lifelong paedobaptist, she believes children should be baptized soon after birth.
The debate between the paedobaptist and credobaptist traditions has lasted for centuries.
He became a paedobaptist after studying the history of early Christian practices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: credobaptist - a person who supports baptism only of believers
