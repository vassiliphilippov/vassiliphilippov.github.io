Menziesia

noun
genus of deciduous shrubs
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɛnˈziːziə / (AmE), / mɛnˈziːzɪə / (BrE)
A genus of deciduous shrubs found in North America and eastern Asia, formerly recognized in the heath family (Ericaceae), some species now often classified under Rhododendron.

Examples

Menziesia shrubs
Botanists have reclassified some species of Menziesia under the genus Rhododendron.
Many Menziesia plants thrive in acidic, well-drained soil conditions.
The Menziesia shrub adds vibrant color to woodland gardens in the spring.
