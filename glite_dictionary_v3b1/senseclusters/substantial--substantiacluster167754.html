substantial

adjective
strongly built; of solid quality
Pronunciation: / səbˈstænʃəl / (AmE), / səbˈstanʃəl / (BrE)
of strong, solid construction or good quality; sturdily or robustly built

Examples

Substantial wooden door
The museum's substantial oak doors impressed every visitor.
The old bridge was surprisingly substantial, withstanding many storms over the decades.
Their new house is particularly substantial compared to the neighboring cottages.
