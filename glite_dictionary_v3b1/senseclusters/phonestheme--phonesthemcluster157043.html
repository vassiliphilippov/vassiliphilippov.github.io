phonestheme

noun
sound-meaning pattern
9.9
Pronunciation: / fəˈnɛsθiːm / (AmE), / fəˈnɛsθiːm / (BrE)
a sound or group of sounds that is associated with a particular meaning in several words, but is not a morpheme

Examples

Common phonestheme sounds
The 'sn-' in words like 'sniff' and 'sneeze' is a common phonestheme related to the nose.
Linguists study how a phonestheme can link similar meanings across different words.
The 'gl-' phonestheme appears in words connected to light, such as 'glow' and 'glimmer'.
