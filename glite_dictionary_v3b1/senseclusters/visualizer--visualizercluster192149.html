visualizer

noun
person who thinks mainly in images
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒuəˌlaɪzər /; / ˈvɪʒ.u.əˌlaɪ.zɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒuəˌlaɪzə /; / ˈvɪʒ.u.əˌlaɪ.zə / (BrE)
