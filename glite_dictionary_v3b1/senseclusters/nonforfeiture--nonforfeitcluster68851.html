nonforfeiture

noun
insurance policy benefit
9.9
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈfɔːr.fɪ.tʃər/ /; / /nən.ˈfɔːr.fɪ.tʃər/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈfɔː.fɪ.tʃə/ /; / /nən.ˈfɔː.fɪ.tʃə/ (BrE)
a rule or clause (especially in insurance) that protects you from losing all your rights, money, or benefits if you stop paying or if the policy ends
