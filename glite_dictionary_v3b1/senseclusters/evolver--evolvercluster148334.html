evolver

noun
agent of change
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvɑːlvər / (AmE), / ɪˈvɒlvə / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes or brings about change or development in someone or something

Examples

Evolver of ideas
As a mentor and advocate for reform, she became an evolver of workplace culture.
The new software acted as an evolver in the company's digital transformation.
He saw himself as an evolver, always seeking ways to improve the system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: innovator - a person who introduces new ideas or methods
  • antonym: preserver - a person or thing that keeps something in its original state
  • synonym: transformer - someone or something that changes the form or appearance of something
