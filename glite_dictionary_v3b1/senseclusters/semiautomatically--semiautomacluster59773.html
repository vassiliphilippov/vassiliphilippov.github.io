semiautomatically

adverb
partly automatically
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˌɔtəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˌɔːtəˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is partly but not fully automatic, often requiring some human intervention

Examples

Sorted semiautomatically by worker
The documents are sorted semiautomatically, with a worker checking each batch for errors.
This software updates the database semiautomatically, requiring you to approve each change.
The doors open semiautomatically when you press the button, but you must close them yourself.
