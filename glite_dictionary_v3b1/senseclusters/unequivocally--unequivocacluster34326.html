unequivocally

adverb
in an unambiguous or clear way
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪˈkwɪvəkli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈkwɪvəkli / (BrE)
In a way that leaves no doubt; in an unambiguous or clear manner.

Examples

To unequivocally support
She unequivocally supports the policy, leaving no room for doubt.
The CEO unequivocally denied any involvement in the scandal.
The research unequivocally demonstrates the effectiveness of the new treatment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ambiguously - in an ambiguous or unclear manner
