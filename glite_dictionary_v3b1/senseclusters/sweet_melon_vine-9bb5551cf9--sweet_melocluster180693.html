sweet melon vine

noun
vine bearing sweet netted-rind melons
8.6 3.3
Pronunciation: /swiːt ˈmel.ən vaɪn/ (AmE), /swiːt ˈmel.ən vaɪn/ (BrE)
any of several varieties of vine whose fruit has a netted rind, sweet edible flesh, and a musky smell, typically including cantaloupe and muskmelon.

Examples

The sweet melon vine fruit

Farmers often plant sweet melon vine varieties like cantaloupe for their juicy, fragrant melons.
The sweet melon vine sprawled across the garden, producing dozens of delicious fruits during the summer.
