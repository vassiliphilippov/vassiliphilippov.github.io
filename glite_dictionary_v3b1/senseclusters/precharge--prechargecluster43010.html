precharge

verb
to apply initial charge
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːtʃɑrdʒ /; / ˈpriːˌtʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpriːtʃɑːdʒ /; / ˈpriːˌtʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
