record price

noun
highest price ever paid for something
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkərd praɪs / (AmE), / ˈrɛkɔːd praɪs / (BrE)
the highest price that has ever been paid for something

Examples

A new record price
Housing in the city reached a record price this year due to high demand.
The Picasso painting was auctioned off at a record price last night.
The company's shares hit a record price before the market closed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: record low - the lowest price ever paid or recorded for something
