pay out

verb
spend or distribute money
1.0
Pronunciation: / peɪ aʊt / (AmE), / peɪ aʊt / (BrE)

Examples

To pay out money

The insurance policy will only pay out if there is proof of damage.
We have to pay out a large sum every year to maintain our equipment.
