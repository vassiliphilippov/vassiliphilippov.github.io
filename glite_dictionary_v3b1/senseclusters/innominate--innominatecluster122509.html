innominate

adjective
without a name
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɑːmɪnət / (AmE), / ɪˈnɒmɪnət / (BrE)
not named or lacking a specific name, especially used for anatomical parts without a distinct designation

Examples

Innominate artery
The innominate artery is a major blood vessel in the upper chest.
Some bones in the human body are referred to as innominate because they lack a specific name.
In older medical texts, you may find references to the innominate vein.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anonymous - without a known or identified name
  • antonym: named - having a specific or given name
