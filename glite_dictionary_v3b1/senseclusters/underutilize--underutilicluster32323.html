underutilize

verb
to use inefficiently
4.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dər.ˈjuː.tɪ.laɪz/ /; / /ʌn.dər.ˈjuː.tə.laɪz/ /; / /ˈʌn.dər.juː.tɪ.laɪz/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.ˈjuː.tɪ.laɪz/ /; / /ʌn.də.ˈjuː.tə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to use something less than its full potential or capacity

Examples

To underutilize resources
Many companies underutilize the skills of their employees, missing out on valuable contributions.
The new software was underutilized because staff did not receive proper training.
If you underutilize your resources, your project may not reach its full potential.
Many talented employees are underutilized in large organizations.
The new equipment was underutilized because staff had not been trained to use it.
If we underutilize our resources, we may fall behind our competitors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maximize - to use something to its fullest extent
  • synonym: waste - to use something carelessly or without fully using its value
