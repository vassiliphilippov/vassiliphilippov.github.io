piglike

adjective
greedy or dirty
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or behaving like a pig, especially in appearance or manners (such as being greedy, dirty, or rude)

Examples

Piglike manners
His piglike manners at the dinner table shocked everyone present.
The children made piglike messes in the kitchen after baking cookies.
She found his piglike greed for money very unattractive.
The wild boar had a distinctly piglike snout and small eyes.
Her laugh was so loud and piglike that everyone turned to look.
The creature's piglike behavior included rooting around in the dirt for food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beastly - cruel, unpleasant, or lacking in manners
  • antonym: polite - showing good manners and respect for others
  • synonym: porcine - relating to or resembling pigs
  • synonym: swinish - resembling a pig in behavior or appearance
