dramatic play

noun
theatrical play for stage performance
Pronunciation: / drəˈmætɪk pleɪ / (AmE), / drəˈmatɪk pleɪ / (BrE)
A play written to be performed by actors on a stage.

Examples

A dramatic play performance
She prefers reading a dramatic play over a novel because she enjoys the dialogue and stage directions.
The school's theatre troupe performed a dramatic play about ancient myths.
Their group is rehearsing a famous dramatic play for the upcoming festival.
