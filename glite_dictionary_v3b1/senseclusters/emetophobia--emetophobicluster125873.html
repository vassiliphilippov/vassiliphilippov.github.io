emetophobia

noun
fear of vomiting
Pronunciation: / ˌɛmɪtəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌɛmɪtəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
an intense and irrational fear of vomiting or seeing others vomit

Examples

Severe emetophobia fear
Her emetophobia made it difficult for her to eat in public places.
People with emetophobia often avoid situations where vomiting might occur.
Therapy can help individuals manage their emetophobia and reduce their anxiety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease
  • synonym: phobia - an extreme or irrational fear of something
