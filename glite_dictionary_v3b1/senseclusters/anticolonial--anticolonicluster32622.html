anticolonial

adjective
opposing colonial rule
Pronunciation: / ˌænti.kəˈloʊniəl / (AmE), / ˌænti.kəˈləʊniəl / (BrE)
opposing colonial rule or supporting independence from colonial powers

Examples

Anticolonial movement
The anticolonial movement gained momentum after World War II.
Many writers expressed anticolonial ideas in their novels and essays.
Protests with strong anticolonial messages spread across the region in the 1950s.

Related Terms

  • synonym: independence - freedom from control by another country or government
  • antonym: procolonial - supporting colonial rule or policies
