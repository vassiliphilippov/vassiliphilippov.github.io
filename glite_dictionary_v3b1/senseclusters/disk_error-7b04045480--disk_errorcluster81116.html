disk error

noun
computer error with magnetic disk
3.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /dɪsk ˈer.ər/ (AmE), /dɪsk ˈe.rə/ (BrE)
an error that occurs when a magnetic disk fails to read or write data properly, typically due to hardware malfunction or corruption

Examples

Frequent disk errors
Frequent disk errors may indicate that your hard drive is about to fail.
He called tech support after receiving a disk error message several times in a row.
The system could not complete the backup because a disk error occurred during the process.
