whiteface

noun
breed of beef cattle with a white face
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtˌfeɪs / (BrE)
A hardy English breed of cattle, distinguished by its white face, extensively raised in the United States and other countries for beef.
