rise

verb
take part in a rebellion
Pronunciation: / raɪz / (AmE), / raɪz / (BrE)

Examples

To rise in revolt

The villagers decided to rise against the oppressive regime.
Throughout history, people have risen in protest when injustice becomes unbearable.

Related Terms
