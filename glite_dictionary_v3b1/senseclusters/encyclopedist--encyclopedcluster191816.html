encyclopedist

noun
person who compiles encyclopedias
5.8
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪst /; / ɛnˌsaɪkləˈpidɪst / (AmE), / ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪst /; / ɪnˌsʌɪklɪəˈpiːdɪst / (BrE)
