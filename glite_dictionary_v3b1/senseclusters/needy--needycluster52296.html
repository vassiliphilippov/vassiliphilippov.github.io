needy

adjective
requiring excessive attention or affection
2.7 1.93/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàniÀêdi / (AmE), / ÀàniÀêdi / (BrE)
needing constant attention, affection, or reassurance from others, often excessively so

Examples

Needy partner
His needy behavior made it difficult for him to maintain friendships.
She became increasingly needy in the relationship, always asking for reassurance.
The child was so needy that he constantly demanded attention from his parents.

Synonyms

  • clingy
    4.8 1.02/m
    clingy clings emotionally and seeks constant closeness and attention, while needy asks for ongoing affection and reassurance from others.
  • overdependent
    4.7 0.03/m
    overdependent depends too much on others for help or guidance, while needy focuses on seeking constant attention, affection, or reassurance.
  • dependent
    2.8 18/m
    dependent relies on someone for support or basic needs, while needy seeks constant emotional attention, affection, or reassurance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clingy - tending to stay very close to someone and depend on them emotionally
  • antonym: independent - not needing help or reassurance from others
