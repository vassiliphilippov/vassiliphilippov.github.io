seatmate

noun
person sitting beside you
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːtˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈsiːtˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a person who sits next to you, especially in a vehicle, classroom, or other shared seating arrangement

Examples

My seatmate on train

My seatmate on the train offered to share her snacks with me.
During the exam, I asked my seatmate for an extra pencil.
I became good friends with my seatmate after sitting together all semester.
