poker chip

noun
disk used as money substitute in poker
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊkər tʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈpəʊkə tʃɪp / (BrE)
a small, round, flat disk used as a counter to represent money in gambling, especially in the game of poker

Examples

Stack of poker chips
After winning the hand, she stacked her poker chips neatly.
Each player starts the game with an equal number of poker chips.
He placed a single poker chip on the table to call the bet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chip - a small, flat, round piece used in games of chance to represent money
