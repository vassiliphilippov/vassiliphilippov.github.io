objectivize

verb
to make objective
8.4
Pronunciation: /əb.ˈdʒek.tɪ.vaɪz/ /; / /ɑːb.ˈdʒek.tɪ.vaɪz/ (AmE), /əb.ˈdʒek.tɪ.vaɪz/ /; / /ɒb.ˈdʒek.tɪ.vaɪz/ (BrE)
