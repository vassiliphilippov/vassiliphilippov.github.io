objectivize

verb
to make objective
8.4
Pronunciation: / əbˈdʒɛktɪˌvaɪz / (AmE), / əbˈdʒɛktɪvaɪz / (BrE)
to make something objective or treat it as an object rather than as a subject or person

Examples

To objectivize an idea
The philosopher aimed to objectivize moral values in his theory.
It is difficult to objectivize emotions in scientific research.
The report attempts to objectivize the experiences of the participants for analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: objectify - to treat someone or something as an object
  • antonym: subjectivize - to make something subjective or treat as a subject
