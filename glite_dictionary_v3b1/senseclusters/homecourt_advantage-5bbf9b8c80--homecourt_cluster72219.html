homecourt advantage

noun
advantage when playing at home (sports)
5.2 2.9
Pronunciation: /hoʊm kɔːrt ə.ˈvæn.tɪdʒ/ (AmE), /həʊm kɔːt ə.ˈvɑːn.tɪdʒ/ (BrE)
the advantage gained by a team when playing on its own court, typically in front of supportive fans

Examples

The homecourt advantage effect
Despite their homecourt advantage, the team struggled to keep up with their opponents in the first half.
Many analysts believe the homecourt advantage can be the deciding factor in a close playoff series.
The basketball team has a clear homecourt advantage because the crowd always supports them enthusiastically.
