unfreedom

noun
lack of real choice
8.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfriːdəm / (AmE), / ʌnˈfriːdəm / (BrE)
a state or condition where someone does not have freedom or the ability to make choices independently, often because of restrictions or control
