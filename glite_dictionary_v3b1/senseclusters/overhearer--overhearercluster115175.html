overhearer

noun
unintended listener
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈhɪrər / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈhɪərə / (BrE)
a person who hears a conversation or information without being directly addressed or involved

Examples

An overhearer listens secretly
As an overhearer, she picked up details about the surprise party without anyone noticing.
The overhearer accidentally learned about the company's new project during lunch.
Sometimes a casual overhearer can discover important information by chance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eavesdropper - a person who secretly listens to other people's conversations
