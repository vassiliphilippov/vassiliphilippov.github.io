fribble

noun
trivial thing
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈfrɪbəl / (BrE)
a thing that is trivial, unimportant, or of little value

Examples

A useless fribble
He spent his money on a fribble that he quickly forgot about.
The gift looked nice, but it turned out to be just another fribble.
Instead of saving, she wasted her allowance on a silly fribble from the shop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bauble - a small, showy, and usually worthless thing
  • antonym: treasure - something of great value or worth
  • synonym: trifle - something of little importance or value
