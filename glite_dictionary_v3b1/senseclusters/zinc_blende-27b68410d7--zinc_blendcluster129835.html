zinc blende

noun
chief ore of zinc; zinc sulfide mineral
9.4
Pronunciation: / zɪŋk blɛnd / (AmE), / zɪŋk blend / (BrE)
a mineral consisting mainly of crystalline zinc sulfide, which is the chief ore of zinc

Examples

The zinc blende mineral
Geologists identified the crystal as zinc blende due to its structure and composition.
Miners extracted zinc blende from deep underground veins.
The mine produces large quantities of zinc blende for industrial use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sphalerite - the mineral form of zinc sulfide, identical to zinc blende
