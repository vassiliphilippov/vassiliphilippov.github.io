nonworking

adjective
not a work day
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
not involving work or business activity; not scheduled as a working day or period

Examples

Nonworking day
The office will be closed on all nonworking days, including weekends and public holidays.
She scheduled her appointment for a nonworking afternoon to avoid missing any meetings.
Banks often have reduced hours on nonworking holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holiday - a day when most people do not have to work
  • antonym: working - involving work or business activity
