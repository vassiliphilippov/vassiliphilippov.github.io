diascope

noun
microscopy viewing device
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəskəʊp / (BrE)
a device used to examine objects by shining light through them, especially in microscopy

Examples

A diascope device
The technician used a diascope to examine the thin tissue sample.
A diascope allows scientists to observe details in transparent specimens.
Before digital imaging, a diascope was essential for viewing slides in laboratories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: microscope - an instrument used to see objects that are too small for the naked eye
  • synonym: projector - a device that projects images onto a surface
