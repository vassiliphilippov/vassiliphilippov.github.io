hemagglutinate

verb
cause red blood cells to clump
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːməˈɡluːtɪˌneɪt /; / hiːməˈɡluːtəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈɡluːtɪneɪt /; / hiːməˈɡluːtɪneɪt / (BrE)
to cause the clumping together of red blood cells

Examples

To hemagglutinate blood cells
