olive green

noun
a light, yellowish-green color
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlɪv ɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv ɡriːn / (BrE)
a shade of green that is lighter and more vibrant than olive, resembling the color of green olives

Examples

An olive green dress
She wore an olive green dress to match her eyes.
The army camouflage included shades of olive green and brown.
We decided to paint the kitchen walls olive green for a fresh, natural feel.
