stately home

noun
large aristocratic country mansion
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪtli hoʊm / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtli həʊm / (BrE)
A large and impressive country house, especially one that was formerly or is currently occupied by an aristocratic family.
