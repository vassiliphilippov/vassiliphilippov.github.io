culture

noun
shared ideas and customs in a society
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltʃər /; / ˈkʌl.tʃɚ /; / ˈkʌltʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃə /; / ˈkʌl.tʃə / (BrE)
the ideas, customs, social behavior, and artistic achievements shared by a group of people, society, or organization
