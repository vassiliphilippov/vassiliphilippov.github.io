windbag

noun
a person who talks too much and is boring
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn(d)bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈwɪn(d)bæɡ / (BrE)
a person who talks a lot, especially about uninteresting or trivial things, and is considered boring

Examples

A windbag talks
After an hour of listening to the windbag drone on, she finally excused herself.
Everyone avoids George at parties because he's such a windbag about his old job.
The politician was dismissed as a windbag who never offered real solutions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blowhard - a person who boasts or brags a lot
  • synonym: chatterbox - a person who talks a lot, especially a child
  • antonym: listener - a person who listens rather than talks
