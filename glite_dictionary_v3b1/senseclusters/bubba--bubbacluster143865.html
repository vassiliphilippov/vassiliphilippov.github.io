bubba

noun
affectionate male nickname
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌbə / (AmE), / ˈbʌbə / (BrE)
an informal and affectionate term for a younger brother or a close male friend, especially in the southern United States

Examples

Little bubba
My little bubba always wants to play video games with me after school.
Everyone in the neighborhood knows bubba for his big smile and friendly attitude.
She called her younger brother bubba even when they were both adults.
