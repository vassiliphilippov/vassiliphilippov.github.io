Amharic

noun
official language of Ethiopia
Pronunciation: / æmˈhærɪk / (AmE), / amˈharɪk / (BrE)
the official language of Ethiopia, belonging to the Semitic language family and influenced by Cushitic languages

Examples

The Amharic language
Many people in Ethiopia speak Amharic as their first or second language.
She is studying Amharic to communicate better with her Ethiopian colleagues.
The novel was translated from Amharic into English last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Arabic - a Central Semitic language spoken across the Arab world
  • synonym: Hebrew - a Semitic language of the ancient Hebrews and modern Israel
