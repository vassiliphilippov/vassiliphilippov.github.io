Shetland sheepdog

noun
small herding dog breed from the Shetland Islands
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛtlənd ˈʃipˌdɔg /; / ˈʃɛtlənd ˈʃip dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈʃɛtlənd ˈʃiːpdɒg /; / ˈʃɛtlənd ˈʃiːp dɒɡ / (BrE)
a small breed of herding dog with a long, thick coat, originally from the Shetland Islands, that looks like a small collie
