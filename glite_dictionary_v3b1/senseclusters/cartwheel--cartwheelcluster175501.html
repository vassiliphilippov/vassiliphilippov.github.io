cartwheel

verb
perform a sideways acrobatic roll
5.7 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːrt.wiːl/ /; / /ˈkɑːr.twiːl/ (AmE), /ˈkɑːt.wiːl/ /; / /ˈkɑː.twiːl/ (BrE)
to perform a sideways somersault in which the body turns over completely with the hands and feet touching the ground alternately

Examples

To cartwheel across grass
Children love to cartwheel on the grass during recess.
She cartwheeled away from the group, laughing and full of energy.
The gymnast cartwheeled across the mat with perfect form.
