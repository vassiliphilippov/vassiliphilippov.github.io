preparator

noun
funeral body preparer
Pronunciation: / prɪˈpærətɔr / (AmE), / prɪˈpærətə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to prepare dead bodies for burial or a funeral, often by embalming and dressing them

Examples

Funeral preparator
The preparator ensured the deceased looked peaceful for the family viewing.
After the ceremony, the preparator carefully handled all the necessary arrangements.
She trained as a preparator to help families during difficult times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embalmer - a person who preserves dead bodies by treating them with chemicals
  • synonym: undertaker - a person whose business is preparing dead bodies and arranging funerals
