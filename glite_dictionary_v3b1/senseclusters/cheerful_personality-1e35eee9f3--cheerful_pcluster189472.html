cheerful personality

noun
happy and positive character
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪrfəl pɚˌsɑːnˈæləti / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪəfʊl pəˌsɒnˈælɪti / (BrE)
a consistently happy and positive disposition that is friendly and uplifting to others

Examples

A cheerful personality
Even during tough times, his cheerful personality shines through and lifts everyone’s spirits.
Her cheerful personality makes her a favorite among her colleagues.
People are drawn to someone with a cheerful personality at social gatherings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: optimistic - having a hopeful and positive outlook
