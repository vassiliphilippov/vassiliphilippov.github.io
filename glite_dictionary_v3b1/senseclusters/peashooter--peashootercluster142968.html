peashooter

noun
toy tube shooter
Pronunciation: / ˈpiˌʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈpiːˌʃuːtə / (BrE)
a simple toy consisting of a tube used to shoot small objects, such as peas, by blowing through it

Examples

A toy peashooter
As a child, he made a peashooter out of a straw and some dried peas.
The kids played in the yard, each armed with a homemade peashooter.
She laughed as a tiny pea shot from her peashooter and landed on the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blowgun - a tube for shooting small projectiles by blowing
  • synonym: slingshot - a Y-shaped stick with elastic used to shoot small objects
