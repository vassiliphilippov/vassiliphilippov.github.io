roadmap

noun
map of roads
3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊd.mæp/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊd.mæp/ (BrE)
a map, either on paper or digital, that shows roads and routes for travel or navigation

Examples

A roadmap for travel
Before GPS devices, travelers relied on a roadmap to plan their journeys.
She checked the roadmap to find the best route through the mountains.
The old roadmap in the glove compartment was still useful during their road trip.
