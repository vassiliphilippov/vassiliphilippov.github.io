disport

verb
amuse or entertain oneself playfully
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspɔrt / (AmE), / dɪˈspɔːt / (BrE)
to amuse yourself by playing or behaving in a lively, playful, or carefree way

The children disported themselves in the sun-drenched park all afternoon.
