mano

noun
stone grinding tool
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
a hand-held stone tool used for grinding food, especially grains, against a larger stone surface

Examples

Stone mano grinder
The ancient villagers used a mano to grind corn into flour for their meals.
A well-worn mano was discovered alongside pottery shards at the excavation site.
She demonstrated how to use a mano and metate to prepare traditional food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: metate - a large stone slab used as a base for grinding with a mano
  • synonym: pestle - a tool used for grinding substances in a mortar
