Shiite

noun
member of Shia branch of Islam
7.0 7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːˌaɪt / (AmE), / ˈʃiːaɪt / (BrE)
a member of the branch of Islam that holds that Ali was the legitimate successor to Muhammad and rejects the authority of the first three caliphs

Examples

Shiite Muslim community
Many Shiite communities observe the holiday of Ashura with special rituals and ceremonies.

The conflict between Shiite and Sunni groups has shaped the region's history for centuries.
