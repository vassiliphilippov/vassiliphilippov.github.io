fleuret

noun
fencing sword
9.9
Pronunciation: / fluˈreɪ / (AmE), / flœˈʁɛ / (BrE)
a light fencing sword with a flexible blade, used especially for practice and sport, also known as a foil

She bought a new fleuret before joining the university fencing club.
