Tanacetum parthenium

noun
an aromatic medicinal herb (feverfew)
9.9
Pronunciation: /tæn.ə.ˈsiː.təm pɑːr.ˈθe.nɪ.əm/ (AmE), /tæn.ə.ˈsiː.təm pɑː.ˈθe.nɪ.əm/ (BrE)
A bushy, aromatic perennial herb native to Europe, with clusters of white-rayed, button-like flowers, traditionally used for medicinal purposes; also known as feverfew.
