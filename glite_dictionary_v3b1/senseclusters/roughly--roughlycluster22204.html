roughly

adverb
approximately, not exactly
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌf.li / (AmE), / ˈrʌf.li / (BrE)
approximately; not exactly but close to a particular number, amount, or degree.

Examples

Roughly three hours
The journey took roughly three hours by train.
There were roughly twenty people at the meeting last night.
Tickets for the concert cost roughly fifty dollars each.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approximately - close to but not exactly a particular number or amount
  • antonym: exactly - in a precise and accurate way, with no inaccuracy or approximation
