postdevelopmental

adjective
after development
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstdɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstdɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
relating to the period or processes that occur after development has been completed

Examples

Postdevelopmental changes
Researchers observed postdevelopmental changes in the structure of the brain.
The effects of the treatment were only visible during the postdevelopmental stage.
Certain diseases may appear in the postdevelopmental phase of an organism's life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: developmental - relating to the process of development
  • synonym: postnatal - relating to the period after birth
