sit

verb
be located; be situated
3.3
Pronunciation: / sɪt / (AmE), / sɪt / (BrE)
to be located or positioned in a particular place (used for objects, buildings, or things)

Examples

The statue sits outside
A statue of the founder sits in the main courtyard.
Our cabin sits on a hill overlooking the lake.
The famous cathedral sits at the center of the old city.
A thin layer of dust sits on the shelf, undisturbed.
The old car sat unused in the garage for years.
We sat around the kitchen table for hours without doing much.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remain - to stay in the same place or condition
  • synonym: stand - (of a building or object) to be situated in a particular place
