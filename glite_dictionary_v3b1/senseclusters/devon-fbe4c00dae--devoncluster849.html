Devon

noun
breed of red English cattle
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛv.ən / (AmE), / ˈdɛv.ən / (BrE)
a breed of red cattle from Devon, England, valued for both milk and meat production

Examples

Devon cattle
Several prize-winning Devon cows were displayed at the agricultural fair this year.
The distinctive reddish-brown coat makes the Devon easily recognizable among other cattle breeds.
The farmer raised a herd of Devon cattle known for their rich milk and quality beef.
