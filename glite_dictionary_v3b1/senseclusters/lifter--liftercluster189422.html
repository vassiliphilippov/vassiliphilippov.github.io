lifter

noun
person or machine that lifts
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪftər / (AmE), / ˈlɪftə / (BrE)
a person or machine that lifts or raises something, especially heavy objects

Examples

A machine lifter
The lifter moved the heavy crate onto the truck with ease.
As a professional weight lifter, she trains every morning before work.
The factory uses an automated lifter to stack boxes on the shelves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crane - a large machine for lifting and moving heavy objects
  • synonym: hoist - a device used for lifting heavy things
  • antonym: lowerer - a person or device that brings something down
