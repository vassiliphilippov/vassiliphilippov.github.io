appeal

noun
legal request for judgment review
6.1 22/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàpil / (AmE), / …ôÀàpiÀêl / (BrE)
(law) A request made to a higher court to review and change the decision of a lower court.

Examples

A court appeal
After losing the case, they decided to pursue an appeal hoping for a different outcome.
The lawyer filed an appeal to overturn the verdict after new evidence emerged.
Their appeal was denied by the state supreme court, leaving the original decision in place.

Related Terms

  • synonym: petition - a formal written request, especially to an authority
  • degree higher: retrial - a new trial ordered by an appellate court
  • degree lower: trial - a formal examination of evidence by a court
