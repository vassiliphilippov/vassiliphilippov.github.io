Argonaut

noun
warm-sea cephalopod; female has papery shell
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrɡəˌnɔt / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡənɔːt / (BrE)
a small cephalopod mollusk of warm seas, whose female produces a delicate, papery, spiral shell

Examples

Female Argonaut shell
Marine biologists were excited to find a rare Argonaut drifting off the coast.
The female Argonaut creates a delicate shell that she uses as a brood chamber for her eggs.
Unlike other octopuses, the Argonaut floats near the ocean surface.
