Jinghpo language

noun
language of the Jinghpo people
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪŋ.poʊ ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪŋ.pəʊ ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Kachinic language spoken by the Jinghpo people, primarily in Myanmar and parts of China and India
