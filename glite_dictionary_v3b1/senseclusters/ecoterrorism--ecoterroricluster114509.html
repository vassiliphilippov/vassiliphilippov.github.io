ecoterrorism

noun
violence in support of environmental causes
6.1 7.3
Pronunciation: /iː.koʊ.ˈter.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /e.koʊ.ˈter.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /iː.kəʊ.ˈter.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /e.kəʊ.ˈter.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
The use or threat of violence in support of environmental causes, typically to intimidate or coerce for political or social objectives.

Examples

Ecoterrorism acts
Debates over ecoterrorism often center on the line between activism and criminal behavior.
Government agencies have increased security in response to rising concerns about ecoterrorism.
The group was charged with ecoterrorism after setting fire to logging equipment.
