Synonyms

  • ecological terrorism
    8.9 0.00/m
    ecological terrorism is a formal phrase used in reports for violent environmental actions, while ecoterrorism is the shorter public term.
  • eco-warfare
    9.8 0.00/m
    eco-warfare means organized violent campaigns by environmental groups, while ecoterrorism is usually single attacks or threats to scare people.
  • ecological warfare
    9.6 0.00/m
    ecological warfare refers to planned sabotage or large-scale attacks for ecological aims, while ecoterrorism usually means activist threats or smaller violent acts.
