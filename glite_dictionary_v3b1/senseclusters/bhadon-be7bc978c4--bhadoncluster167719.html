Bhadon

noun
sixth month of the Hindu calendar
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːdɔn / (AmE), / ˈbɑːdɒn / (BrE)
the sixth month of the Hindu calendar, usually corresponding to August–September in the Gregorian calendar

Examples

Month of Bhadon
Farmers welcome the arrival of monsoon rains in the month of Bhadon.
The diary entry was dated the third day of Bhadon, 1950.
The festival takes place during Bhadon every year in northern India.
