autarchic

adjective
relating to autarchy or self-sufficiency
8.4
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈtɑrkɪk / (AmE), / ɔːˈtɑːkɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by autarchy; self-sufficient, especially economically or politically independent.

Examples

An autarchic economy
Historically, autarchic systems have proven difficult to maintain in a globalized world.
Some leaders advocated for a more autarchic economic model during the global crisis.
The country's autarchic policies made it less dependent on foreign trade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autarchy - absolute self-sufficiency (especially of a state or economy)
  • synonym: autarkic - relating to autarky; self-sufficient
