autarchic

adjective
relating to autarchy or self-sufficiency
8.4 9.9
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈtɑrkɪk / (AmE), / ɔːˈtɑːkɪk / (BrE)

Examples

The country's autarchic policies made it less dependent on foreign trade.
