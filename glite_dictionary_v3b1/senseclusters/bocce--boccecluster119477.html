bocce

noun
Italian bowling game
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːtʃi / (AmE), / ˈbɒtʃeɪ / (BrE)
An Italian game similar to bowling, played on a long, narrow court with weighted balls.

Examples

Playing bocce balls
Every Sunday, the community gathers at the park to play bocce together.
He learned the rules of bocce during his trip to Italy last summer.
The club is looking for new members to join their bocce league.
