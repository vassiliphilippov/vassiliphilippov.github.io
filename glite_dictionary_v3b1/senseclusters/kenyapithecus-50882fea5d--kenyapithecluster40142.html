Kenyapithecus

noun
extinct genus of primate from Kenya
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɛniəˈpɪθɪkəs / (AmE), / ˌkɛniəˈpɪθɪkəs /; / ˌkɛniːəˈpɪθɪkəs / (BrE)
an extinct genus of primates with large molars and strong chewing muscles, known from fossils found in Kenya
