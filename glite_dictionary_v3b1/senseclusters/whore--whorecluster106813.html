whore

verb
compromise integrity for personal gain
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɔr / (AmE), / hɔː / (BrE)
to compromise one's integrity or principles for money or personal gain, often in a way considered morally degrading

Examples

To whore for fame
In his pursuit of fame, he whored himself to the tabloids.
She refused to whore her talent for commercial success.
Some politicians are accused of whoring themselves for campaign donations.
