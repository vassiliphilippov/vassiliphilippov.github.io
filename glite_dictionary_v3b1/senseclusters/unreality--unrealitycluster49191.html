unreality

noun
the state of being unreal
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnriˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌʌnriˈælɪti /; / ˌʌnriˈaləti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being unreal, imaginary, or not existing in fact
