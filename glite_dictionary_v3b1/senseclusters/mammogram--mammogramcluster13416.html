mammogram

noun
X-ray image of the breast
Pronunciation: / ˈmæməˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈmaməɡram / (BrE)
an X-ray image of the breast, used to detect and diagnose breast diseases, especially cancer

Examples

A mammogram image
Her doctor recommended a mammogram after she turned forty.
It's important for women to schedule a regular mammogram as part of preventive healthcare.
The mammogram revealed a small lump that required further testing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mammography - the technique of using X-rays to examine breast tissue
