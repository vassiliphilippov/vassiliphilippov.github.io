discarnation

noun
separation of soul
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskɑrˈneɪʃən /; / ˌdɪskɑːrˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪskɑːˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or state of a soul or spirit being separated from the physical body, especially after death
