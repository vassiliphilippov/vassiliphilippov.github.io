Artemisia annua

noun

7.4
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.tə.ˈmiː.zi.ə ˈæn.juː.ə/ /; / /ɑːr.tə.ˈmɪz.ɪ.ə ˈæn.juː.ə/ (AmE), /ɑː.tə.ˈmiː.zɪ.ə ˈæn.juː.ə/ /; / /ɑː.tə.ˈmɪz.ɪ.ə ˈæn.juː.ə/ (BrE)
an annual species of wormwood native to southeastern Europe and western Asia, known for its medicinal properties, especially as a source of artemisinin used to treat malaria

Examples

Traditional Chinese medicine has used extracts from Artemisia annua for centuries to treat fevers.
