sign

one of the twelve zodiac divisions in astrology

(Astrology) one of the twelve divisions of the zodiac, each associated with a particular constellation and corresponding dates of the year.

Examples

Zodiac sign
Each astrological sign is said to influence personality traits in different ways.
Many people enjoy reading their sign's horoscope every morning.
She was born under the sign of Leo, known for creativity and leadership.

Synonyms

  • star sign
    3.4 0.14/m
    star sign shows which zodiac sign the sun was in at a person's birth, while sign refers to any zodiac division with dates.
