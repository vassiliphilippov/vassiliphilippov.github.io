coned

adjective
cone-shaped
4.4
Pronunciation: / koʊnd / (AmE), / kəʊnd / (BrE)
having the shape of a cone, being wide at one end and narrow at the other

Examples

A coned mountain
The mountain had a distinct coned peak that could be seen for miles.
She decorated the cake with a coned swirl of whipped cream on top.
The laboratory used a coned flask for the chemical experiment.
