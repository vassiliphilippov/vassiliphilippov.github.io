fisk

noun
detailed rebuttal
9.9
Pronunciation: /fɪsk/ (AmE), /fɪsk/ (BrE)
a detailed, point-by-point criticism or rebuttal of a text or argument, often presented online

Examples

A sharp fisk
The journalist published a thorough fisk of the misleading article on her website.
His lengthy fisk addressed every claim made in the original report.
Many readers appreciated the detailed fisk that clarified the facts point by point.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debunking - exposing the falseness of a claim or argument
  • antonym: endorsement - an act of giving approval or support
  • synonym: refutation - the act of proving a statement or argument to be wrong
