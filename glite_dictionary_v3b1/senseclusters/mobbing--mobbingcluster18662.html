mobbing

noun
group animal harassment
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑː.bɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈmɒb.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the collective harassment or attack by a group of animals, especially birds, against a predator or intruder to drive it away

Examples

Bird mobbing a hawk
The crows engaged in mobbing to chase the owl away from their territory.
Researchers studied mobbing behavior among small birds protecting their nests from predators.
During mobbing, several animals work together to drive off a much larger threat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defense - protection against danger or attack
  • synonym: harassment - persistent attacks or pressure
