preheater

noun
device for preheating
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌhiːtər / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌhiːtə / (BrE)
a device that heats a material or fluid before it enters a process or system

Examples

The preheater device
The preheater ensures the fuel reaches the correct temperature before combustion.
Engineers installed a new preheater to improve the efficiency of the system.
Without a functioning preheater, the process may start at a lower temperature than required.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cooler - a device that lowers temperature
  • synonym: heater - a device that produces heat
