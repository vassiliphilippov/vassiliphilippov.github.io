Elul

noun
month in the Jewish calendar
Pronunciation: / ɛˈluːl / (AmE), / ɛˈluːl / (BrE)
the twelfth month of the civil year and the sixth month of the ecclesiastical year in the Jewish calendar, falling in August and September.

Examples

Elul month
In 2023, Elul began in late August.
Rosh Hashanah preparations begin during Elul.
The month of Elul is a time of spiritual reflection in Judaism.
