industry-wide

adjective, adverb
across an industry
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndəstriˌwaɪd /; / ˈɪndəstri waɪd / (AmE), / ˈɪndəstriˌwaɪd /; / ˈɪndəstri waɪd / (BrE)
affecting, involving, or happening throughout all companies or organizations within a particular industry

Examples

Industry-wide impact
The new regulations will have an industry-wide impact on manufacturing standards.
There was an industry-wide agreement to improve worker safety.
Rising costs have led to industry-wide changes in pricing strategies.
The new safety standards were implemented industry-wide after the government passed new regulations.
Wages increased industry-wide as companies competed to attract skilled workers.
There was an industry-wide shortage of raw materials due to supply chain disruptions.
