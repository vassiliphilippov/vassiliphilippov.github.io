crankily

adverb
in an unsteady way
Pronunciation: / ˈkræŋkɪli / (AmE), / ˈkræŋkɪli / (BrE)
in an irregular, unsteady, or not smoothly functioning manner, often referring to machines or processes.

Examples

Engine runs crankily
The engine ran crankily on cold mornings, making strange noises before warming up.
After years of use, the old printer now works crankily and often jams.
The elevator moved crankily between floors, causing passengers to feel uneasy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: erratically - in an unpredictable or irregular way
  • antonym: smoothly - in a steady and regular way, without problems
