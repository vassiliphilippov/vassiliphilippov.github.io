genus Eimeria

noun
type genus of Eimeriidae (protist pathogens)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs aɪˈmɪə.ri.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs aɪˈmɪə.ri.ə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Eimeriidae, comprising protist organisms including species that are serious animal pathogens.

Examples

Genus Eimeria parasites
Many livestock diseases are caused by parasites from the genus Eimeria.
Researchers study the genus Eimeria to understand coccidiosis in poultry.
Several species in the genus Eimeria pose serious threats to animal health.
