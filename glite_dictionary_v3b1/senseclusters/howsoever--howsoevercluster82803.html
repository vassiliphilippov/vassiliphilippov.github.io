howsoever

adverb
formal contrast word
9.2
Pronunciation: /haʊ.soʊ.ˈev.ər/ /; / /haʊ.ˈsoʊ.ev.ər/ (AmE), /haʊ.səʊ.ˈev.ə/ /; / /haʊ.ˈsəʊ.ev.ə/ (BrE)
used to introduce a statement that contrasts with or limits what has just been said, similar to 'however' (formal or literary)

Examples

Howsoever hard
Howsoever difficult the journey was, he never gave up hope.
She was determined to finish the project, howsoever long it might take.
Howsoever you look at the problem, the solution remains the same.
