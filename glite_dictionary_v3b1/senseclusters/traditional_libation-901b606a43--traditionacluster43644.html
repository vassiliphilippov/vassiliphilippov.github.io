traditional libation

noun
customary ceremonial drink
7.2
Pronunciation: / trəˈdɪʃənəl laɪˈbeɪʃən / (AmE), / trəˈdɪʃənəl laɪˈbeɪʃən / (BrE)
a customary drink, often alcoholic, served or offered during ceremonies or rituals

Examples

A traditional libation ceremony
At the wedding, the elders poured a traditional libation to honor their ancestors.
During the New Year's festival, each family prepared a traditional libation unique to their heritage.
Guests were invited to share a traditional libation as part of the opening ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: libation - a drink poured out as an offering to a deity
