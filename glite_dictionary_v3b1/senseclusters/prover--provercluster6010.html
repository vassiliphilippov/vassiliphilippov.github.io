prover

noun
dough-rising chamber
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpruː.vər/ (AmE), /ˈpruː.və/ (BrE)
a heated cabinet or chamber used by bakers to allow dough to rise before baking

Examples

Dough prover
The baker placed the dough in the prover to ensure it would rise evenly.
A commercial prover helps maintain the perfect environment for yeast activity.
Before baking the loaves, she checked the prover to see if the dough had doubled in size.
