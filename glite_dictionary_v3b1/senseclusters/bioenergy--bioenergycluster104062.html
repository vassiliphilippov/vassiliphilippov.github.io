bioenergy

noun
energy from biomass
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˈɛn.ɚ.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊˈɛn.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
energy produced from organic materials such as plants, agricultural waste, or other biological sources

Examples

Bioenergy from plants
Many countries are investing in bioenergy to reduce their dependence on fossil fuels.
The factory uses agricultural waste to generate bioenergy for its operations.
Scientists are developing new ways to produce bioenergy from algae and other fast-growing plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biomass - organic material used as a source of energy
  • antonym: fossil fuel - energy source from ancient organic remains such as coal or oil
  • synonym: renewable energy - energy from sources that are naturally replenished
