atopic

adjective
prone to allergies
7.9
Pronunciation: /eɪ.ˈtɑːp.ɪk/ (AmE), /eɪ.ˈtɒp.ɪk/ (BrE)
having or relating to a tendency to develop allergic conditions such as eczema, asthma, or hay fever

Examples

Atopic skin condition
Children with atopic skin often need special moisturizers to prevent irritation.
Doctors may recommend allergy testing for patients with an atopic family history.
People with atopic conditions should avoid known triggers to reduce symptoms.
