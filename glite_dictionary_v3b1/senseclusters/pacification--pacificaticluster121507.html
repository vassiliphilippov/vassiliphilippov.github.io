pacification

noun
the act of calming or appeasing someone
5.7 6.1
Pronunciation: /pæs.ə.fək.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /pæs.ɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /pæs.ɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /pæs.ə.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of calming or appeasing someone, especially to make them less angry or hostile

Examples

Pacification of anger
Despite his efforts at pacification, the two parties continued to argue.
Her expertise in the pacification of anxious children was well known among teachers.
The pacification of the angry crowd required both patience and diplomacy.
