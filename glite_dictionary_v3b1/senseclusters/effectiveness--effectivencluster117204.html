effectiveness

noun
the quality of being effective
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛktɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛktɪvnɪs /; / ɪˈfɛktɪvnəs / (BrE)
the ability to produce the desired result or to have a strong effect

Examples

The effectiveness of treatment
Her effectiveness as a leader inspired the whole team.
The effectiveness of the new drug was proven in clinical trials.
We need to measure the effectiveness of our marketing strategy to ensure it's working.
Doctors were surprised by the effectiveness of such a low dose.
Researchers measured the effectiveness of the new drug in treating the disease.
The effectiveness of the cleaning agent was tested in various environments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: efficiency - the ability to achieve a result with minimum wasted effort or expense
  • antonym: ineffectiveness - the lack of ability to produce the desired result
  • synonym: potency - the strength or effectiveness of something, especially a drug or chemical
