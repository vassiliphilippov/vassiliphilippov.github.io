openhandedly

adverb
with open palm
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊpənˈhændɪdli / (AmE), / ˌəʊpənˈhændɪdli / (BrE)
with the palm of the hand open, especially when hitting, pushing, or making a gesture

Examples

Hits openhandedly
She pushed the door openhandedly, making sure not to leave any marks.
During the demonstration, he gestured openhandedly to show he meant no harm.
The referee warned the player after he struck his opponent openhandedly on the shoulder.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fistedly - with a closed fist
  • synonym: flatly - with a flat surface, such as the palm, rather than a fist
