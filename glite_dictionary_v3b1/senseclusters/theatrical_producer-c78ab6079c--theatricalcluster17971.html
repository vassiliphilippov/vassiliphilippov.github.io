theatrical producer

noun
person who produces theatrical performances
Pronunciation: / θiˈætrɪkəl prəˈdusər / (AmE), / θɪˈætrɪkəl prəˈdjuːsə / (BrE)
a person responsible for overseeing and financing the production of a theatrical performance, such as a play or musical.

Examples

A theatrical producer manages shows
Every theatrical producer faces challenges balancing artistic vision with budget constraints.
She built a successful career as a theatrical producer, working with many famous directors and actors.
The theatrical producer took a huge risk in bringing the new play to Broadway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: producer - a person responsible for the financial and managerial aspects of a production
