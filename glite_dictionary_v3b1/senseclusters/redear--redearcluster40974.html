redear

noun
North American sunfish
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdɪr / (AmE), / ˈriːdɪə / (BrE)
a freshwater sunfish of North America, known for its red-edged gill cover, also called redear sunfish

Examples

The redear sunfish
The fisherman proudly displayed the redear he caught at the lake.
Many anglers seek out redear because of their size and fighting ability.
A large redear can be found in warm, weedy waters across the southern United States.
