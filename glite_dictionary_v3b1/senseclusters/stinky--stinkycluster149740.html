stinky

adjective
having an unpleasant smell
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋki / (BrE)
having a strong, unpleasant, or offensive smell

Examples

Stinky socks
After playing outside all day, his socks were stinky and needed to be washed.
The dog rolled in something stinky at the park, so it needed a bath.
You can always tell when the garbage is stinky and has to be taken out.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant smell
  • synonym: smelly - having a strong or unpleasant odor
