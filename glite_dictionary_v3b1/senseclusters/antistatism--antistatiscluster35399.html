antistatism

noun
opposition to government control
9.9
Pronunciation: /æn.tɪ.ˈstæt.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /ə.n.tɪ.ˈstæt.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.ˈstæt.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /ə.n.tɪ.ˈstæt.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
opposition to state power, control, or authority; the belief that government should have little or no involvement in people's lives
