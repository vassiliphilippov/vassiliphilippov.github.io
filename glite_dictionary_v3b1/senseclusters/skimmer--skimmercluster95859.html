skimmer

noun
card data thief
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪmər / (AmE), / ˈskɪmə / (BrE)
a device or person that illegally copies credit or debit card information, usually by reading the card's magnetic strip

Examples

ATM skimmer
The police discovered a skimmer attached to the ATM at the gas station.
Authorities warned customers to check for skimmers before inserting their cards into payment machines.
A skimmer can steal your card information without you even noticing.
