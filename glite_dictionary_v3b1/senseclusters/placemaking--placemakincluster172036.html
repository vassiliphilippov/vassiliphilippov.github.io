placemaking

noun
creating public spaces
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪsˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪsˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of creating or improving public spaces to encourage community use, engagement, and well-being

Examples

Community placemaking project
The city invested in placemaking to turn the old square into a lively community hub.
Successful placemaking projects often involve input from local residents and businesses.
Through placemaking, the abandoned park became a popular spot for families and events.
The city council launched a placemaking initiative to revitalize the old market square.
Effective placemaking can transform neglected areas into lively community hubs.
Workshops on placemaking encouraged residents to share ideas for improving their neighborhood parks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: community development - efforts to improve the well-being of a community
  • synonym: urban planning - the technical and political process of designing cities and towns
  • synonym: urban renewal - the redevelopment of areas within a city
