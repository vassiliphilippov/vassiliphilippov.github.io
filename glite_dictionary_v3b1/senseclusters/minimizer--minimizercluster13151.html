minimizer

noun
person who downplays
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪn.ə.maɪ.zər/ /; / /ˈmɪn.ɪ.maɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈmɪn.ɪ.maɪ.zə/ /; / /ˈmɪn.ə.maɪ.zə/ (BrE)
