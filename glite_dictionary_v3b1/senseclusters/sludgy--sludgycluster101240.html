sludgy

adjective
slow, heavy sound
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌdʒi / (AmE), / ˈslʌdʒi / (BrE)
having a slow, heavy, and thick quality, especially describing music with a dense or distorted sound.

Examples

Sludgy music
The band's new album features sludgy guitar riffs that crawl under the listener's skin.
Many fans love the sludgy sound of early grunge and metal bands.
The song opens with a sludgy bass line that sets a dark mood.
