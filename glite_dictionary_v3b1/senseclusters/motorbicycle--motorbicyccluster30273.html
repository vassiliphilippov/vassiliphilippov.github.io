motorbicycle

noun
old term for motorcycle
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtərˌbaɪsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəbʌɪsɪkəl / (BrE)
an older term for a motorcycle, a two-wheeled vehicle powered by a motor

Examples

Vintage motorbicycle
He restored a vintage motorbicycle from the early 1900s.
The museum displayed an old motorbicycle alongside classic cars and carriages.
In the past, a motorbicycle was a rare sight on rural roads.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bicycle - a two-wheeled vehicle powered by pedals
  • synonym: motorcycle - a two-wheeled motor vehicle
