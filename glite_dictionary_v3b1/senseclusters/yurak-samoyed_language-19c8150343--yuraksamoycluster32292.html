Yurak-Samoyed language

noun
Uralic language of northern Siberia
Pronunciation: / ˈjuˌræk səˈmɔɪɛd ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈjʊəræk səˈmɔɪɛd ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Uralic language formerly spoken by the Samoyed people of northern Siberia; also known as Nenets.

Examples

Yurak-Samoyed language speakers
Few speakers of the Yurak-Samoyed language remain today as most have shifted to Russian.
Linguists study the Yurak-Samoyed language to understand the Uralic language family's history.
The Yurak-Samoyed language is closely related to other Samoyedic languages of Siberia.
