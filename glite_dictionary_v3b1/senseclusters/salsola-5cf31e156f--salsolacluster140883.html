Salsola

noun
genus of saltwort plants
Pronunciation: / sælˈsoʊlə / (AmE), / salˈsəʊlə / (BrE)
A genus of mostly Old World herbs or shrubs known as saltworts, often found in saline environments.

Examples

Salsola plant
Many species of Salsola are used as forage for livestock in arid regions.
Researchers studied the adaptive features of various Salsola species to understand salt tolerance mechanisms.
The desert landscape was dotted with Salsola plants thriving in the salty soil.
