when

conjunction
introducing time clause
1.0
Pronunciation: /wen/ /; / /wɪn/ /; / /hwen/ (AmE), /wen/ /; / /wɪn/ (BrE)

Examples

When you arrive

He always feels nervous when he speaks in public.
The lights went out when the storm started last night.
