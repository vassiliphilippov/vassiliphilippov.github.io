parturient

adjective
relating to giving birth
8.0
Pronunciation: / pɑːrˈtʃʊriənt /; / pɑrˈtʊriənt / (AmE), / pɑːˈtjʊəriənt / (BrE)
(of a woman or female animal) giving birth or about to give birth
