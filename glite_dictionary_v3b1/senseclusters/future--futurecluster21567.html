future

noun
verb tense expressing future actions
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈfjuːtʃə / (BrE)
the verb tense used to express an action or state that will happen after the present time

Examples

Future tense
In English, the future is often formed by using 'will' or 'shall' before the verb.
Learning how to express the future is an important part of mastering English grammar.
She asked her students to write five sentences in the future tense.

Related Terms

  • antonym: present - a verb tense expressing actions or states occurring now
