pontificator

noun
officiating bishop
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɒnˈtɪfɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / pɒnˈtɪfɪkeɪtə / (BrE)
a bishop or high-ranking cleric who leads a pontifical or solemn religious ceremony

Examples

Ceremony pontificator
The pontificator wore elaborate robes during the cathedral's annual Easter service.
As pontificator, he delivered the blessing to the gathered congregation.
The ceremony began when the pontificator entered, followed by a procession of clergy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celebrant - a person who leads a religious ceremony
  • synonym: pontiff - a high-ranking bishop, especially the Pope
