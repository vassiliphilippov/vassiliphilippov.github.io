rustle

noun
soft, light noise (e.g. leaves, silk)
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌsəl / (AmE), / ˈrʌsl / (BrE)
a soft, light sound such as the noise made by dry leaves or silk when they move or are rubbed together

Examples

A rustle of leaves
He heard a faint rustle behind the curtains and turned around.
The rustle of her silk dress echoed as she entered the room.
The rustle of leaves was all she could hear in the quiet forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: murmur - a soft, indistinct sound
  • synonym: swish - a soft, rustling sound like something moving softly through air
