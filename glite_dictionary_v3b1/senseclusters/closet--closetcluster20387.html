closet

noun
toilet or lavatory (British)
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑːzət / (AmE), / ˈklɒzɪt / (BrE)
(British) A toilet or lavatory; formerly used to refer to a small private room containing a toilet.

Examples

A water closet
In older British homes, a water closet was often found outside the main building.
She excused herself to use the closet at the end of the hall.
The estate agent described the property as having a modern bathroom and a separate closet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lavatory - room or fixture for washing and usually a toilet
  • synonym: restroom - public toilet, mainly American
