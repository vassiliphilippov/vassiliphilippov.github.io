unauthorized

adjective
lacking official approval
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈɔθəˌraɪzd /; / ˌʌnˈɑːθəˌraɪzd /; / ˌʌnˈɔːθəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɔːθəraɪzd /; / ˌʌnˈɔːθəraɪzd / (BrE)
not having official permission or approval; not officially allowed

Examples

An unauthorized withdrawal
She was reprimanded for making an unauthorized withdrawal from the account.
The company issued a warning about unauthorized use of its trademark.
The guards detained anyone found in the building after hours as it was an unauthorized presence.
Access to the building is strictly prohibited for unauthorized personnel.
He was accused of making unauthorized changes to the document.
The company took action against unauthorized use of its trademark.
Employees found using unauthorised software may face disciplinary action.
The police quickly removed the unauthorised vehicles from the restricted area.
There was an unauthorised access attempt to the company's confidential files.
