neotraditionalist

noun
supporter of tradition
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌniːoʊtrəˈdɪʃənəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊtrəˈdɪʃənəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or promotes a return to traditional ideas or practices in a modern context

Examples

A neotraditionalist thinker
As a neotraditionalist, she believes in blending classic values with contemporary lifestyles.
The new political party attracted many neotraditionalists who wanted to revive old customs.
His neotraditionalist approach to music combines folk melodies with modern instruments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modernist - a person who supports modern ideas or practices
  • synonym: traditionalist - a person who values or upholds traditional ideas or practices
