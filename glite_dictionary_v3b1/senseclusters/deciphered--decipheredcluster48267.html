deciphered

adjective
converted to intelligible language
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsaɪfərd / (AmE), / dɪˈsaɪfəd / (BrE)
converted from a code or unclear form into intelligible language; made understandable

Examples

To decipher a message
His handwriting was so messy that only a few words could be deciphered.
Once deciphered, the message revealed the location of the treasure.
The ancient script was finally deciphered by a team of linguists.
