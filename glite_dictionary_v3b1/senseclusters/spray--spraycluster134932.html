spray

noun
liquid chemical for spraying
1.0
Pronunciation: / spreɪ / (AmE), / spreɪ / (BrE)
a liquid, especially a pesticide or other chemical, held in suspension or solution and designed to be applied as a fine mist

Examples

Insect spray
Be sure to wear gloves when handling this chemical spray.
Farmers apply a special spray to protect their crops from pests.
The gardener mixed the spray according to the instructions on the label.
