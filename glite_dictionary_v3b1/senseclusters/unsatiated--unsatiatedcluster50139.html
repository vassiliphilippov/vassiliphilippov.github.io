unsatiated

adjective
not fully satisfied
4.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈseɪʃiˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈseɪʃɪeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having been satisfied; still wanting more or lacking fulfillment.

Examples

Unsatiated hunger
Even after the feast, he felt an unsatiated hunger for adventure.
Her unsatiated curiosity drove her to keep asking more questions throughout the night.
The team's unsatiated desire for victory pushed them to train harder every day.
