glossitis

noun
inflammation of the tongue
Pronunciation: / ɡlɑːˈsaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ɡlɒˈsaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the tongue, often causing swelling, redness, and pain

Examples

Glossitis inflammation
The patient was diagnosed with glossitis after complaining of a sore and swollen tongue.
Treatment for glossitis depends on the underlying cause of the inflammation.
Vitamin B12 deficiency is one possible cause of glossitis.
