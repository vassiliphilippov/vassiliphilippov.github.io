garlic press

noun
tool for crushing garlic
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrlɪk prɛs / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːlɪk prɛs / (BrE)
a small kitchen tool used to crush or extract juice from garlic cloves by pressing them through a perforated surface

Examples

A garlic press tool
A good garlic press can save time when preparing meals.
Have you seen my garlic press? I left it by the sink.
She used a garlic press to quickly add flavor to the pasta sauce.
