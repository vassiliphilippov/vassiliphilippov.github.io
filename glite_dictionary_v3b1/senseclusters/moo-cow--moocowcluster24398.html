moo-cow

noun
child's word for a cow
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmuˌkaʊ / (AmE), / ˈmuːkaʊ / (BrE)
a cow; especially used by or for children to refer to a domestic cow

Examples

The moo-cow on farm
In the storybook, the friendly moo-cow says hello to all the barnyard animals.
She gave her toddler a plush moo-cow to cuddle at night.
The children pointed excitedly at the moo-cow grazing in the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cow - a fully grown female animal of a domesticated breed of ox, used as a source of milk or beef
