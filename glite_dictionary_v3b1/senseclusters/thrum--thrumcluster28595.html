thrum

verb
to make a humming sound
Pronunciation: / θrʌm / (AmE), / θrʌm / (BrE)
to make or produce a continuous, low, rhythmic, or vibrating sound

Examples

Engines thrum quietly
As the train started, its engine began to thrum steadily beneath our feet.
She sat quietly, letting her fingers thrum on the tabletop in a rhythmic pattern.
The old air conditioning unit would thrum throughout the night, making it hard to sleep.
His fingers thrummed on the tabletop as he waited impatiently for news.
She could hear her neighbor's music thrumming through the apartment walls.
The engines thrum steadily beneath the deck as the ship moves forward.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drone - to make a continuous low dull humming sound
  • synonym: drum - to make a sound like a drum by beating repeatedly
  • synonym: hum - to make a low, steady continuous sound
