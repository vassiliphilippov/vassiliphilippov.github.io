on a roll

adjective
having a run of success
3.6
Pronunciation: / ɑn ə roʊl / (AmE), / ɒn ə rəʊl / (BrE)
experiencing a period of continuous success or good luck

Examples

On a roll streak
After winning three games in a row, the team was clearly on a roll.
Once the chef started winning awards, he was on a roll for the rest of the year.
She feels unstoppable at work lately—she's really on a roll.

Related Terms

  • antonym: down on one's luck - experiencing a period of bad luck
  • synonym: hot streak - a period of continuous success
  • synonym: winning streak - a sequence of successes
