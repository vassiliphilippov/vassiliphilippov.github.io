fin de siècle

adjective
of or relating to the end of a century, especially the 19th
9.9 8.0
Pronunciation: /fɪn də ˈsiː.kəl/ /; / /fæn də ˈsiː.kəl/ (AmE), /fɪn də ˈsiː.kəl/ /; / /fæn də ˈsiː.kəl/ (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the end of a century, especially the cultural, artistic, or social styles prominent at the end of the 19th century.

Examples

Fin de siècle style
Many artists embraced fin de siècle sensibilities, fascinated by both beauty and decay.
The decadent themes of the novel are typical of fin de siècle literature.
The mood of uncertainty and innovation defined the fin de siècle period in European culture.
