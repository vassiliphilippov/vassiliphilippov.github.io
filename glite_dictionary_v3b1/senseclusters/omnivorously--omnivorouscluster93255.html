omnivorously

adverb
in a varied way
Pronunciation: / ɑmˈnɪvərəsli / (AmE), / ɒmˈnɪvərəsli / (BrE)
in a way that involves eating both plants and animals or showing interest in many different things

Examples

Eat omnivorously at dinner
He ate omnivorously, enjoying both salads and steaks at the buffet.
She reads omnivorously, exploring everything from science fiction to biographies.
The children learned omnivorously, absorbing knowledge from many different subjects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: selectively - in a way that is careful about choosing
  • synonym: variously - in several different ways
