fembot

noun
female robot
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛmˌbɑt / (AmE), / ˈfɛmˌbɒt / (BrE)
a robot or artificial being designed to look and act like a woman

Examples

A fembot character
The science fiction novel introduced a fembot who questioned her own identity.
Engineers are developing a new fembot with advanced speech recognition abilities.
In the film, the villain controlled a group of fembots to carry out his plans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: android - a robot with a human appearance
  • synonym: gynoid - a robot designed to resemble a woman
  • synonym: robot - a machine capable of carrying out complex actions automatically
