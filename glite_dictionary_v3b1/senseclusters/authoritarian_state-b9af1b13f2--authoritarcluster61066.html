authoritarian state

noun
country under strict, non-democratic control
7.6 0.36/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀåŒ∏…îÀêr…ôÀàt…õri…ôn ste…™t / (AmE), / …îÀêÀåŒ∏…ír…™Ààte…ôri…ôn ste…™t / (BrE)
A country governed by a central authority that exercises strict control over public and private life, without being responsible to the people.

Examples

An authoritarian state government
Many citizens fear the rise of an authoritarian state where dissent is not tolerated.
The new laws gave the leader even more power, turning the country into an authoritarian state.
Unlike a democracy, an authoritarian state limits political freedoms and controls many aspects of daily life.

Synonyms

  • authoritarian regime
    7.4 1.80/m
    an authoritarian regime concentrates political power in a small group and runs the country tightly, while an authoritarian state is a controlled country.
  • dictatorship
    4.8 10/m
    a dictatorship gives absolute rule to one leader or small group and limits opposition, while an authoritarian state keeps tight control without public accountability.
  • authoritarianism
    8.7 2.36/m
    authoritarianism describes a political system that concentrates power and stops opposition, while an authoritarian state is a country run by that system.
  • autocracy
    8.2 0.96/m
    an autocracy places unlimited power in one person who decides alone, while an authoritarian state may be ruled by a party or elite.
  • despotism
    9.6 1.33/m
    despotism means cruel and absolute rule by one person or group, while an authoritarian state covers strict control but not always cruel rule.

Related Terms

  • antonym: democracy - system of government by the whole population or all eligible members, typically through elected representatives
  • synonym: dictatorship - government by a dictator with absolute power
