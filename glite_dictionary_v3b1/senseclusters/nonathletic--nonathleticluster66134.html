nonathletic

adjective
not physically fit
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnæθˈlɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnæθˈlɛtɪk / (BrE)
not having the qualities, skills, or interest typical of an athlete or someone who plays sports

Examples

Nonathletic person
Despite his nonathletic build, he enjoyed hiking in the mountains every summer.
She always felt nonathletic compared to her classmates who played on sports teams.
His nonathletic nature made gym class a real challenge for him.
Although he is nonathletic, he enjoys watching football games with his friends.
The school offers clubs for nonathletic students who prefer art or music.
Her nonathletic nature made her choose chess over basketball as an extracurricular activity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inactive - not engaging in physical activity
  • synonym: unfit - not in good physical condition
