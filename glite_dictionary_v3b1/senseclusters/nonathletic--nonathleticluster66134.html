nonathletic

adjective
not physically fit
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnæθˈlɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnæθˈlɛtɪk / (BrE)
not having the qualities, skills, or interest typical of an athlete or someone who plays sports
