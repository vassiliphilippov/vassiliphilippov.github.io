antimilitary

noun
military opponent
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈmɪlɪˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈmɪlɪtəri / (BrE)
a person or group that opposes the military, military policies, or militarism

Examples

An antimilitary activist
The antimilitary spoke passionately against increased defense spending at the town hall meeting.
During the protest, several antimilitary groups marched together calling for peace.
As an antimilitary, she advocated for reducing the influence of armed forces in government.

Related Terms

  • antonym: militarist - a person who supports military policies or a strong military
  • synonym: pacifist - a person who opposes war and violence
