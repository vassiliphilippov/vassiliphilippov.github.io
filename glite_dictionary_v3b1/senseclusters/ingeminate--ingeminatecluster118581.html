ingeminate

verb
repeat; say again
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒɛm.əˌneɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɛm.ɪ.neɪt / (BrE)

Examples

To ingeminate a phrase

She ingeminated her objections despite being ignored by the committee.
The professor would often ingeminate his key points to ensure everyone understood.
