consumer

noun
software component that consumes data
3.8
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈsuː.mər/ (AmE), /kən.ˈsjuː.mə/ /; / /kən.ˈʃuː.mə/ (BrE)
(in computing) a program, process, or device that receives, reads, or processes data or messages produced by another component (a producer).

Examples

Data consumer
The data pipeline uses a consumer process to ingest messages from the queue during peak hours.
Multiple consumers read events from the topic to balance load across the microservices cluster.
As a debugging step, she restarted the consumer to clear stuck offsets and resume processing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: producer - a program, process, or device that creates or sends data or messages consumed by another component
  • synonym: subscriber - a component that registers to receive messages from a publisher or topic (often similar in role to a consumer)
