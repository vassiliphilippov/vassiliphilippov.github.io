Croton tiglium

noun
tropical Asian shrub; source of croton oil
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkroʊ.tən ˈtɪ.gliː.əm/ (AmE), /ˈkrəʊ.tən ˈtɪ.gliː.əm/ (BrE)
