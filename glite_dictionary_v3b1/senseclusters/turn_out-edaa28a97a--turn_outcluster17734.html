turn out

verb
prove or be discovered to be (the case)
1.7
Pronunciation: / tɝn aʊt /; / tɝːn aʊt / (AmE), / tɜːn aʊt / (BrE)
to be discovered, proved, or to happen in a particular way, often unexpectedly
