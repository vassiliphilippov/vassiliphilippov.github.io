intercounty

adjective
between counties
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈkaʊnti / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈkaʊnti / (BrE)
involving, existing, or taking place between two or more counties

Examples

Intercounty agreement
The new intercounty bus service makes commuting much easier for residents.
An intercounty agreement was signed to manage the shared water resources.
The annual intercounty sports competition attracts teams from across the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intracounty - existing or occurring within a single county
