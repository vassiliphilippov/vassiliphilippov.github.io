Synonyms

  • contagious disease
    3.2 0.38/m
    contagious disease spreads easily between people in everyday talk, while contagion names the disease or often its spread.
  • communicable disease
    3.6 0.34/m
    communicable disease is the medical phrase for infections passed between people, while contagion names the disease or its spread.
  • transmission
    4.5 4.98/m
    transmission is the act of passing an infection between hosts, while contagion can mean the disease or its spread.
