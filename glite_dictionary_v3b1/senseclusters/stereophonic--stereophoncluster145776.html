stereophonic

adjective
using two or more audio channels for sound
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛriəˈfɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌstɪəriəˈfɒnɪk / (BrE)
(of sound reproduction) using two or more independent audio channels to create the impression of sound coming from various directions, as experienced in natural hearing.

Examples

Stereophonic sound system
Many classic albums were remastered in stereophonic sound to enhance the listening experience.
She prefers stereophonic headphones because they create a more immersive sensation than mono ones.
The new record player delivers a rich stereophonic experience that fills the room with sound.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mono - sound reproduction using a single channel
