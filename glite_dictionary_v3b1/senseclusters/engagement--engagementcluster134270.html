engagement

noun
user interaction metric (digital)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
(in digital media and product analytics) a measurable indicator of how users interact with online content or a product, for example views, clicks, shares, comments, or time spent.

Examples

User engagement online
After the marketing campaign, the app's engagement increased, with more users liking and commenting daily.
Product managers monitor engagement metrics to decide which features attract the most active users each month.
We improved onboarding to boost engagement, aiming to raise time-on-site and click-through rates by quarter-end.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engagement rate - a metric expressing engagement relative to audience size or impressions
  • synonym: interaction - an action or response by a user that shows involvement with content
