vacillate

verb
move or sway in a wavelike pattern
Pronunciation: / ˈvæsəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈvæsɪleɪt / (BrE)
to move or sway in a rising and falling or wavelike motion

Examples

The boat vacillates
As the boat drifted on the gentle sea, it seemed to vacillate with every wave.
The line on the heart monitor began to vacillate as the patient's condition changed.
Watching the kite vacillate in the gusty wind was mesmerizing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oscillate - to move or swing back and forth at a regular speed
