fourth-rate education

noun
very poor quality education
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrθ reɪt ˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfɔːθ reɪt ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
education of very poor quality

Examples

A fourth-rate education
Despite his intelligence, he suffered from a fourth-rate education at the underfunded rural school.
Many students feel trapped by a fourth-rate education that leaves them unprepared for the workforce.
Politicians criticized the country's fourth-rate education system for failing its children.
