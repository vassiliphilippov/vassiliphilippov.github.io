Synonyms

  • acculturation
    8.2 2.42/m
    acculturation involves adopting another group's cultural traits, while assimilation means fully joining the larger group's customs and identity.
  • absorption
    6.1 0.66/m
    absorption is when one group is merged into another and may lose its identity, while assimilation is the process of adopting the larger group's customs.
  • integration
    4.1 6.91/m
    integration brings different groups into a community as equals, while assimilation focuses more on people adopting the larger group's customs.
