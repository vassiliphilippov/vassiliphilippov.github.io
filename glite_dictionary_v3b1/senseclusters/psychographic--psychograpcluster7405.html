psychographic

adjective
about attitudes and lifestyles
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study or classification of people according to their attitudes, interests, and lifestyles

Examples

Psychographic profile analysis
Marketers use psychographic profiles to better understand their customers' preferences and habits.
A psychographic analysis can reveal why certain groups respond differently to advertising.
The survey included both demographic and psychographic questions to get a complete picture of the audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demographic - relating to the statistical characteristics of populations
