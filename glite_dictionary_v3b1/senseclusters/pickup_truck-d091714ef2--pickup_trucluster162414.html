pickup truck

noun
light open-backed motor vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkʌp ˌtrʌk / (AmE), / ˈpɪkʌp ˌtrʌk / (BrE)
a light motor vehicle with an open cargo area at the back and low sides, often used for transporting goods

Examples

A pickup truck bed
He drove his old pickup truck across the dusty road to the farm.
She loaded the furniture into the back of her pickup truck before moving to her new apartment.
The company uses a fleet of pickup trucks for local deliveries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: truck - a motor vehicle for transporting goods
  • synonym: van - a medium-sized motor vehicle with a boxy shape for transporting goods
