preenclosure

adjective
before enclosure
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːɪnˈkloʊʒər / (AmE), / ˌpriːɪnˈkləʊʒə / (BrE)
existing or occurring before the process of enclosure, especially of land for private use

Examples

Preenclosure fields
The preenclosure fields were shared by villagers for grazing their animals.
Historians study preenclosure communities to understand rural life before land privatization.
Maps from the preenclosure era show a patchwork of open, communal land.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enclosed - surrounded or closed off on all sides
  • synonym: preindustrial - existing or occurring before industrialization
