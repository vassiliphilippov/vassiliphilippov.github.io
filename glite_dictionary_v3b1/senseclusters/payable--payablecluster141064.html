payable

adjective
required to be paid; due in payment
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
(of an amount of money) required to be paid; due in payment, especially as specified by law or agreement.

Examples

Payable amount
Dividends will be payable to shareholders on March 1st.
The amount payable includes all applicable taxes and fees.
The invoice is payable within 30 days of receipt.

Related Terms

  • antonym: receivable - (of money) that may be received; due to be received
