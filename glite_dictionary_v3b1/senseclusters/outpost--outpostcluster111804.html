outpost

noun
remote settlement or camp on the frontier
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈaʊtpəʊst /; / ˈaʊt.pəʊst / (BrE)
a small settlement or station in a remote area, often used for military, exploration, or trading purposes

Examples

The remote outpost was supplied by helicopter once a month.

The enemy tried to capture the outpost in a surprise attack before dawn.
The soldiers were sent to man the remote outpost near the border.
