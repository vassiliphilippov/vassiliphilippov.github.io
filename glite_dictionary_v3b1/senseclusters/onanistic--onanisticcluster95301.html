onanistic

adjective
self-gratifying
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊnəˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌəʊnəˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling self-gratification, especially in a self-absorbed or self-indulgent way

Examples

Onanistic art
The artist's latest work was criticized as onanistic and lacking in broader appeal.
Some found the speech onanistic, focusing more on the speaker's achievements than the audience's needs.
His writing style can be onanistic, filled with self-referential commentary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-indulgent - allowing oneself excessive pleasure or luxury
  • antonym: selfless - putting others' needs before one's own
