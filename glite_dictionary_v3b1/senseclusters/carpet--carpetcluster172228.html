carpet

noun
thick fabric floor covering
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːr.pət / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pɪt / (BrE)
A thick woven or tufted fabric used as a floor covering, usually with a nap or pile.

Examples

A soft carpet
He accidentally spilled coffee on the beige carpet.
The soft carpet muffled the sound of footsteps in the hallway.
We replaced the old carpet in the living room with a new one.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rug - a small piece of thick fabric for covering part of a floor
