saliently

adverb
in a prominent way
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪliəntli / (AmE), / ˈseɪliəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is very noticeable or prominent

Examples

To saliently stand out
The speaker saliently addressed the main issues facing the community.
Her achievements were saliently displayed on the wall of the office.
The differences between the two proposals were saliently highlighted in the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conspicuously - in a way that is easily seen or attracts attention
  • synonym: obviously - in a way that is easy to see or understand
  • antonym: subtly - in a way that is not obvious or easily noticed
