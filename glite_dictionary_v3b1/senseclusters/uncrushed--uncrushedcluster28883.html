uncrushed

adjective
not pressed or broken
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrʌʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrʌʃt / (BrE)
not pressed, broken, or squeezed out of shape; remaining whole or intact

Examples

Uncrushed grapes
The grapes arrived uncrushed after their long journey from the vineyard.
She was relieved to find her hat uncrushed at the bottom of her suitcase.
Despite the heavy box on top, the flowers remained uncrushed in the package.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crushed - pressed or squeezed out of shape
  • synonym: intact - not damaged or impaired; complete
