off-line

adjective
not connected to a network
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌlaɪn / (BrE)
Not connected to or directly controlled by a computer or computer network.

Examples

Off-line mode
Many off-line resources are still valuable for research.
She saved the documents for off-line access during her flight.
The program can be used in both off-line and online modes.
