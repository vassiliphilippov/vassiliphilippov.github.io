suffrutescent

adjective
having a woody perennial base (of a plant)
9.9 0.00/m

(of a plant) having a woody base that does not die down each year.

Examples

Suffrutescent plant base
Botanists identified the shrub as suffrutescent, noting its perennial woody stem.
The suffrutescent herbs in this region survive harsh winters thanks to their woody bases.
Unlike true herbs, suffrutescent plants retain a portion of their structure throughout the year.
