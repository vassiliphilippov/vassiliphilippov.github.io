call on

verb
to request or appeal to someone
1.9
Pronunciation: / kɔl ɑn / (AmE), / kɔːl ɒn / (BrE)
to formally ask or appeal to someone for help, information, or action

Examples

To call on the government
Citizens called on the mayor to address the housing crisis.

During the meeting, the manager decided to call on a team member for their opinion.
If you ever need assistance, feel free to call on me at any time.
The teacher will often call on students to answer questions in class.
