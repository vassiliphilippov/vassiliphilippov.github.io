call on

verb
to request or appeal to someone
3.5 10/m
Pronunciation: / k…îl …ën / (AmE), / k…îÀêl …ín / (BrE)
to formally ask or appeal to someone for help, information, or action

Examples

To call on the government

If you ever need assistance, feel free to call on me at any time.
