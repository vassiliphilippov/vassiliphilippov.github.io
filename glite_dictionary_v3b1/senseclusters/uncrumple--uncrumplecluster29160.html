uncrumple

adjective
smooth, not wrinkled
4.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrʌmpəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrʌmpəld / (BrE)
smooth or free from wrinkles; not crumpled or creased

Examples

Uncrumpled paper
He handed her an uncrumpled piece of paper for the application.
The uncrumpled napkins were stacked neatly on the table.
After ironing, the shirt looked perfectly uncrumpled and ready to wear.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crumpled - creased, wrinkled, or rumpled
  • synonym: smooth - having an even surface without wrinkles or creases
