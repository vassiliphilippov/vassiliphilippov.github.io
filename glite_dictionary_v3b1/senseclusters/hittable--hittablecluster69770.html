hittable

adjective
easy to hit
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈhɪtəbəl / (BrE)
easy to hit or capable of being struck, especially in sports like baseball

Examples

Hittable pitch
The pitcher was having a bad day and every ball he threw was hittable.
That serve was so slow it looked completely hittable to the opponent.
In the final round, the boxer's defense weakened and he became more hittable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unhittable - very difficult or impossible to hit
  • synonym: vulnerable - easily harmed or attacked
