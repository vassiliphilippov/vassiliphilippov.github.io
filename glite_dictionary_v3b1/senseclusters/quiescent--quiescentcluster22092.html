quiescent

adjective
inactive; not active
Pronunciation: / kwiˈɛsənt / (AmE), / kwɪˈɛsənt /; / kwiˈesənt / (BrE)
in a state of inactivity, dormancy, or quietness; not active or at rest

Examples

The patient appeared to be in a quiescent state after the medication was administered.
The volcano has remained quiescent for over a century, showing no signs of eruption.
During the winter months, the volcano remained quiescent, showing no signs of activity.
Her worries became quiescent as she listened to the gentle sound of the rain.

During winter, many animals enter a quiescent state to conserve energy.
