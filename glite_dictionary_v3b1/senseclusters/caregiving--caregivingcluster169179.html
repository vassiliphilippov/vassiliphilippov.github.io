caregiving

adjective
providing care
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛrˌɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkeəˌɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to the act of providing care and assistance to someone, especially those who are ill, elderly, or disabled

Examples

Caregiving services
The hospital provides caregiving services for patients recovering from surgery.
She found a job in a caregiving role at a local retirement home.
Many families rely on caregiving support for their elderly relatives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nursing - providing medical or personal care to someone
  • synonym: supportive - giving help or encouragement
