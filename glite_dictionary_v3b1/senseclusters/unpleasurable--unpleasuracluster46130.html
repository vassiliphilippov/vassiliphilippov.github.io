unpleasurable

adjective
not enjoyable
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈplɛʒərəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈplɛʒərəbəl / (BrE)
not giving or capable of giving pleasure; unpleasant or disagreeable

Examples

An unpleasurable experience
The long wait in the cold was an unpleasurable part of the trip.
He found the bitter taste of the medicine completely unpleasurable.
Some people consider doing taxes to be an unpleasurable task each year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enjoyable - giving pleasure or satisfaction
  • synonym: unpleasant - not enjoyable or agreeable
