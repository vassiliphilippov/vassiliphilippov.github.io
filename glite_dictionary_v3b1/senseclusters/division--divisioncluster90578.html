division

noun
sports league group ranked by quality
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / dɪˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
a category or grouping in a sports league or competition, ranked by level of quality, skill, or performance

Examples

Premier division
Each football club in the top division competes for the league title.
He's hoping to get drafted into a higher division next year.
The team was promoted to the first division after a successful season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conference - a division or group within a larger sports league or association
  • synonym: league - an association of sports clubs or teams that compete together
