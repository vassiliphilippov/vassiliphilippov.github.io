be a scrote

verb
behave selfishly or unhelpfully (slang)
Pronunciation: / bi ə skroʊt / (AmE), / biː ə skrəʊt / (BrE)
(slang) To behave in a selfish or unhelpful way; to act like an unpleasant or inconsiderate person.

Examples

To be a scrote
He tends to be a scrote when people ask him for help.
If you're just going to be a scrote, don't bother coming to the meeting.
Stop being a scrote and let your brother borrow your bike.
