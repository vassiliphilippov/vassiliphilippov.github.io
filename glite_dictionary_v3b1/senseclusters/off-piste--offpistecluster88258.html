off-piste

adjective
skiing away from marked trails
Pronunciation: / ˌɔf ˈpiːst / (AmE), / ˌɒf ˈpiːst / (BrE)
(of skiing) taking place on unmarked or unpatrolled areas, away from prepared ski runs.

Examples

Off-piste skiing
It is important to have avalanche safety gear when going off-piste.
Many experienced skiers seek the powder snow found only off-piste.
She prefers the excitement of skiing off-piste rather than following the crowded runs.
