Bertha

proper noun
female name; large thing
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrθə / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθə / (BrE)
a female given name, sometimes used informally to refer to something large or heavy

Examples

Big old Bertha
They called their old van Bertha because it was so big and slow.
My grandmother's name was Bertha, and she loved to bake cookies for us.
The workers jokingly referred to the huge machine as Bertha on the construction site.
