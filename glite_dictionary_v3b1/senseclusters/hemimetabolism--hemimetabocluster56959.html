the type of insect development characterized by incomplete metamorphosis, where the young (nymphs) resemble adults but lack wings and mature structures until final molting

Examples

Hemimetabolism in insects
Grasshoppers undergo hemimetabolism, developing through nymph stages without forming a pupal stage.
Mayflies are classic examples of insects that exhibit hemimetabolism.
The distinction between hemimetabolism and holometabolism is central to insect classification.
