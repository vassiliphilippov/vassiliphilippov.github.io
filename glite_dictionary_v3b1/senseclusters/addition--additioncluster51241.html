addition

noun
an amount that is added
Pronunciation: / əˈdɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / əˈdɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a person, thing, or amount that is added to something else, especially to improve it or increase the number or quantity

The chef suggested the addition of fresh herbs to the recipe.

We're excited to introduce a new addition to our product line this year.
