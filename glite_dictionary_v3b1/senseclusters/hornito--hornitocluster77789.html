hornito

noun
small clay oven
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɔrˈnitoʊ / (AmE), / hɔːˈniːtəʊ / (BrE)
a small clay or brick oven or kiln, traditionally used for baking or firing pottery

Examples

Clay hornito oven
The artisan built a hornito in her garden to fire her handmade pottery.
Fresh bread was baked every morning in the old hornito behind the house.
They learned traditional techniques for using a hornito during the pottery workshop.
