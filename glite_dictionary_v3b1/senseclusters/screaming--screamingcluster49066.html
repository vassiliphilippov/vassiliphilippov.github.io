screaming

adjective
extremely intense; overwhelming
Pronunciation: / ˈskɹiːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskɹiːmɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely intense or overwhelming, as if likely to provoke screams.

Examples

She was in screaming pain after the accident, unable to find any relief.
