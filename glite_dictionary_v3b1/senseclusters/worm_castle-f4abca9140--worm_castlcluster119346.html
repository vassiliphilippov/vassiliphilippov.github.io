worm castle

noun
structure for worm composting
4.1
Pronunciation: / wɜrm ˈkæsəl / (AmE), / wɜːm ˈkɑːsl̩ / (BrE)
a container or structure where worms are kept for composting organic material and improving soil quality

Examples

A worm castle container
After a few months, the worm castle produced rich compost for our vegetable garden.
The children were fascinated by the different layers inside the worm castle.
We set up a worm castle in the backyard to help recycle our kitchen scraps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vermicompost - the product of composting using worms
  • synonym: worm bin - container for keeping compost worms
