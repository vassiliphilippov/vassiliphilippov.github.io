flamingly

adverb
angrily or abusively
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪmɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈfleɪmɪŋli / (BrE)
in an extremely angry, abusive, or insulting manner, especially in written or online communication.

Examples

Flamingly angry reply
He responded flamingly to the criticism, using harsh words in his email.
The online forum was filled with flamingly negative comments after the announcement.
She posted a flamingly angry reply that surprised everyone in the group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful or relaxed manner
  • synonym: furiously - in an extremely angry or intense way
