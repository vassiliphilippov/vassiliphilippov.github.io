phylloxera

noun
plant louse harmful to grapevines
9.9 9.7
Pronunciation: / fɪˈlɑksərə / (AmE), / fɪˈlɒksərə / (BrE)
A small insect of the genus Phylloxera, especially Phylloxera vitifoliae, which infests and damages the roots and leaves of grapevines.
