foul ball

noun
baseball ball hit outside playing area
2.5 2.4
Pronunciation: /faʊl bɔːl/ /; / /faʊl bɑːl/ (AmE), /faʊl bɔːl/ (BrE)
(in baseball) a ball that is hit outside the foul lines and is therefore not in play.

Examples

Two foul balls
After two quick foul balls, the pitcher finally threw a strike.
Please don't reach over the rail to catch a foul ball for your own safety.
The batter hit a foul ball that soared into the stands behind third base.
