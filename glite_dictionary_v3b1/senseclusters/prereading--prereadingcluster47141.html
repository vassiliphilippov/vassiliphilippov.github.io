prereading

noun
preparation before reading
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
activities or skills done before reading a text to help with understanding or preparation

Examples

Prereading activities
The teacher introduced several prereading strategies to help students understand the story.
Effective prereading can make it easier to grasp the main ideas in a difficult article.
Before starting the novel, the class completed a prereading activity to discuss key vocabulary.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postreading - activities done after reading a text
  • synonym: preview - an activity to look at or discuss something before reading
