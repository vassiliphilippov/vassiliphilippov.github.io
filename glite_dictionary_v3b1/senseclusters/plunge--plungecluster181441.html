plunge

noun
steep, rapid fall
7.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /plʌndʒ/ (AmE), /plʌndʒ/ (BrE)
a steep and rapid fall or drop from a higher to a lower position, often sudden or dramatic

Examples

A steep plunge
After a brief plunge, the roller coaster climbed steeply once again.
He felt his heart take a plunge when he heard the bad news.
The stock market took a plunge after the disappointing earnings report.
