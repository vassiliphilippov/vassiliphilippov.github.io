whoopsie

interjection
playful apology
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊpsi / (AmE), / ˈwʊpsi / (BrE)
an exclamation used to acknowledge a small mistake or accident in a playful or apologetic way

Examples

Whoopsie after a spill
She dropped her pencil during the test and whispered whoopsie so she wouldn't disturb anyone.
After spilling a little water on the floor, he laughed and said whoopsie to his friends.
The child knocked over the blocks and giggled, saying whoopsie as she picked them up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oops - an exclamation used when a small mistake is made
  • synonym: sorry - an expression of apology or regret
