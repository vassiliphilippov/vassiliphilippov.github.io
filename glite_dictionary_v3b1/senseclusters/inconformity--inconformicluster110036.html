inconformity

noun
lack of agreement
9.4
Pronunciation: /ɪn.kən.ˈfɔːr.mə.tɪ/ /; / /ɪn.kən.ˈfɑːr.mə.tɪ/ (AmE), /ɪn.kən.ˈfɔː.mə.tɪ/ (BrE)

Examples
