shiso

noun
Japanese aromatic herb
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈʃiːsəʊ / (BrE)
an aromatic herb native to East Asia, especially Japan, used as a garnish or flavoring in food

Examples

Fresh shiso leaf
The chef added a fresh shiso leaf to the sashimi for a burst of flavor.
I love the unique taste that shiso brings to summer salads and sushi rolls.
At the Japanese market, she bought a bunch of shiso to use in her recipes.
