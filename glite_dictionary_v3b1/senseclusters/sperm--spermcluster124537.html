sperm

noun
male reproductive cell
1.9
Pronunciation: / spɝːm / (AmE), / spɜːm / (BrE)
the male reproductive cell that unites with a female egg cell to form a new organism

Examples

Male sperm cell
A healthy sperm must travel a long distance to reach the egg.
Microscopes allow scientists to observe the movement of each sperm cell.
Some men have a low sperm count, which can affect fertility.
