holy woman

noun
woman noted for exceptional holiness
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊli ˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli ˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman admired for her exceptional holiness, piety, or devotion to religious life

Examples

The holy woman prayed
People from all over the region came to seek guidance from the holy woman who lived on the hill.
They revered the holy woman for her kindness, wisdom, and unwavering faith.
Throughout history, the deeds of this holy woman have inspired countless others to follow a spiritual path.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saint - a person recognized for exceptional holiness and virtue
