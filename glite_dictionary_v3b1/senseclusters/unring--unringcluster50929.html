unring

verb
to stop ringing
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪŋ / (BrE)
to stop or silence a ringing sound, such as from a bell, alarm, or telephone

Examples

To unring an alarm
They were unringing the alarm by carefully disconnecting its power supply.
After the ceremony, the caretaker unrings the church bell to signal the end.
It took several minutes to unring the persistent doorbell after the prank.
She quickly pressed the button to unring the alarm before it disturbed the neighbors.
Is there any way to unring the bell once it has started?
He tried to unring the phone, but the call had already ended.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ring - to make a clear resonant sound, as a bell does
  • synonym: silence - to make something quiet or stop a sound
