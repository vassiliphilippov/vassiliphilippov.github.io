liquid

adjective
smooth and flowing (movement)
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd / (BrE)
(of movement, sound, eyes, or air) smooth, flowing, or shining in quality, like a liquid

Examples

Liquid movement
Her arm made a liquid motion as she drew the paintbrush across the canvas.
The dancer moved with liquid grace across the stage, captivating everyone.
The skater's liquid movements impressed the judges during the competition.
Early morning brought a liquid clarity to the mountain air.
She looked at him with liquid brown eyes that sparkled in the sunlight.
The singer's liquid soprano voice captivated the entire audience.
He looked at her with liquid eyes, emotions barely concealed.
Her liquid eyes glistened as she held back tears at the movie's ending.
The portrait captured a child’s liquid gaze, as if she were about to cry.
