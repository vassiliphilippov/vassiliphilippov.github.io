vegetative

adjective
relating to involuntary bodily functions
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒ.ə.teɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒ.ɪ.tə.tɪv / (BrE)
Relating to involuntary bodily functions such as growth, digestion, or circulation, rather than to conscious activity.

Examples

Vegetative functions
Damage to the brainstem can disrupt vegetative activities essential for survival.
During a coma, only vegetative processes like circulation and digestion continue to operate.
The doctor explained that breathing and heartbeat are controlled by vegetative functions of the nervous system.
