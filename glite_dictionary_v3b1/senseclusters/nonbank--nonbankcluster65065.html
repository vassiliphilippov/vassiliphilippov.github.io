nonbank

adjective
not a bank
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑnˌbæŋk / (AmE), / ˈnɒnˌbæŋk / (BrE)
not relating to or provided by a bank; used to describe financial institutions or services that are not licensed banks

Examples

Nonbank financial services
The company offers nonbank financial services such as digital payments and money transfers.
Many nonbank lenders provide loans to people who cannot get them from traditional banks.
Regulators are increasing oversight of nonbank institutions in the financial sector.

Related Terms

  • antonym: banking - relating to banks or their services
  • synonym: financial - relating to money or finance
