undress

noun
state of partial or complete nakedness
3.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdrɛs / (AmE), / ʌnˈdrɛs / (BrE)
the state of wearing no or only partial clothing; being partly or completely naked

Examples

In undress at the pool
Guests are not permitted in the pool area while in undress.
He quickly covered himself, embarrassed to be seen in such undress.
The children were caught in a state of undress when the fire alarm sounded.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dress - the state of wearing clothes; opposite of undress
  • synonym: nakedness - the state of being without clothes
