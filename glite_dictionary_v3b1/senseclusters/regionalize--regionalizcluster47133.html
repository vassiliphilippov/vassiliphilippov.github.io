regionalize

Synonyms

    8.0 0.00/m
    sectionalize breaks an area into sections along geographic or administrative lines, while regionalize groups places into broader regions for administration.
  • territorialize
    9.8 0.01/m
    territorialize turns land into defined territories for political or administrative control, while regionalize arranges areas into regions for governance or function.
  • district
    5.8 0.00/m
    district divides an area into official districts with set boundaries for management, while regionalize groups those districts into larger regions.
  • zone
    4.2 0.58/m
    zone marks or divides land for a specific use like business or housing, while regionalize arranges whole areas into broad regions.
