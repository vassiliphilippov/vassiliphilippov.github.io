embrace

verb
to include as part of something
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbreɪs / (AmE), / ɪmˈbreɪs / (BrE)
to include or hold something as part of a larger whole; to encompass as part of a broader group or idea

Examples

Embraces many topics
Her research embraces both modern theories and traditional practices.
The category of endangered species embraces a large number of animals and plants.
The new law embraces all employees, regardless of their length of service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encompass - to include or surround within a scope
  • antonym: exclude - to leave out or not include
