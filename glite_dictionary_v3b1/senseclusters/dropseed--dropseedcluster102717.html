dropseed

noun
a grass of the genus Sporobolus
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑpˌsiːd / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpˌsiːd / (BrE)
any grass of the genus Sporobolus, characterized by small seeds that drop easily when mature

Examples

A dropseed grass
Several species of dropseed can be found growing in the prairies of North America.
The garden includes prairie dropseed, a grass prized for its fine texture and graceful appearance.
Wildlife often eat the seeds of dropseed during the late summer months.
