enormity

noun
great size or extent; vastness
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɔrməti / (AmE), / ɪˈnɔːmɪti / (BrE)
the great size or extent of something; vastness (usage sometimes considered incorrect, as 'enormity' more often refers to wickedness).

Examples

The enormity of the task
Historians debated the enormity of the challenge faced by early settlers.
She was overwhelmed by the enormity of the mountain range stretching before her.
The enormity of the project became clear when we saw the blueprints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immensity - the extremely large size, scale, or extent of something
  • antonym: wickedness - the quality of being morally wrong or evil (the original sense of 'enormity')
