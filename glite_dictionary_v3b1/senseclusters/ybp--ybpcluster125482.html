ybp

noun
years before 1950
Pronunciation: / waɪ bi pi / (AmE), / waɪ biː piː / (BrE)
a unit of time meaning 'years before present', used to indicate years before the reference year 1950 in scientific fields

Examples

10,000 ybp
The cave paintings were created about 15,000 ybp, according to recent studies.
Scientists estimate the glacier retreated around 10,000 ybp at the end of the last ice age.
This artifact dates to 5,000 ybp, making it one of the oldest found in the region.
