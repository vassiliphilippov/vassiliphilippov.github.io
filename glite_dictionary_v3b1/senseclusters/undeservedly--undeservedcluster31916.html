undeservedly

adverb
without having earned or merited
7.5 5.6
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dɪ.ˈzɜːrvd.liː/ /; / /ə.nə.ˈzɜːrvd.liː/ (AmE), /ʌn.dɪ.ˈzɜːvd.liː/ /; / /ə.nə.ˈzɜːvd.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that is not justified or merited; without being earned or warranted

Examples

Blamed undeservedly
