out-basket

noun
desk tray for outgoing mail
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌbæskɪt / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a tray or container on a desk for holding outgoing documents or mail

Examples

An out-basket tray
Her out-basket is overflowing with letters waiting to be mailed.
Please put the completed forms in my out-basket before you leave.
The secretary checked the out-basket for any documents that needed to be sent today.
