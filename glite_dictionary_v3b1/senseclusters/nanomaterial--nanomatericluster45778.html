nanomaterial

noun
material at nanoscale
Pronunciation: / ˌnænoʊməˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / ˌnanəʊməˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
a material made of extremely small (nanometer-scale) structures or particles, often with special properties and used in advanced technology
