lurky

noun
silent online observer
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈlɜːr.kiː/ /; / /ˈlɜːr.kɪ/ (AmE), /ˈlɜː.kiː/ /; / /ˈlɜː.kɪ/ (BrE)
a person who reads or observes online forums, chats, or social media without actively participating

Examples

Lurky forum users
In many online communities, a large number of users are lurky and rarely post comments.
She admitted to being lurky on the forum, reading every thread but never joining the conversation.
Although he was lurky at first, he eventually started sharing his thoughts with the group.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lurker - a person who reads online discussions but does not participate
  • antonym: participant - a person who actively takes part in online discussions
