chow

noun
a breed of medium-sized dog from China
7.5
Pronunciation: / tʃaʊ / (AmE), / tʃaʊ / (BrE)
a breed of medium-sized dog with a thick coat, fluffy curled tail, and distinctive blue-black tongue, believed to have originated in northern China

Examples

A chow dog
Her chow has a beautiful reddish coat and a distinctive blue-black tongue.
The family's chow greeted guests with a friendly wag of its fluffy tail.
They adopted two chows from a local animal shelter last summer.
