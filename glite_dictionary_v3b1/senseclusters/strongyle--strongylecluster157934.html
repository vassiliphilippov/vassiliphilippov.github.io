strongyle

noun
parasitic roundworm
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɑːndʒaɪl / (AmE), / ˈstrɒndʒaɪl / (BrE)
a parasitic roundworm, especially one that infects the intestines of animals

Examples

A strongyle worm
The veterinarian found a strongyle infection in the horse's intestines during the examination.
Farmers regularly deworm their animals to prevent strongyle infestations.
A microscope is needed to identify a strongyle in a stool sample.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nematode - a type of roundworm, many of which are parasitic
  • antonym: tapeworm - a parasitic flatworm that lives in the intestines
