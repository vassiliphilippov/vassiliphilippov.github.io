evacuate

verb
remove contents from; empty
6.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvækjueɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈvakjʊeɪt / (BrE)
to remove the contents of something; to empty a container or space

Examples

To evacuate the tank
Before refilling the tank, you need to evacuate it completely.
He evacuated the bottle and prepared it for the experiment.
Technicians evacuate the chamber by pumping out all the air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: empty - to remove all contents from something
  • antonym: fill - to put contents into something
