slimy substance

noun
viscous, slippery material
Pronunciation: / ˈslaɪmi ˈsʌbstəns / (AmE), / ˈslaɪmi ˈsʌbstəns / (BrE)
a viscous, slippery, and often unpleasant material, typically characterized by its moist or oily texture

Examples

A slimy substance
After peeling the old wallpaper, she found a slimy substance underneath.
Scientists collected a slimy substance from the rocks to study the local algae.
The floor near the pond was covered in a slimy substance that made walking hazardous.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slime - a thick, slippery liquid substance, often unpleasant
