zoophilic

adjective
animal-preferring
9.9
Pronunciation: /zuː.ə.ˈfɪl.ɪk/ /; / /zoʊ.ə.ˈfɪl.ɪk/ (AmE), /zuː.ə.ˈfɪl.ɪk/ /; / /zəʊ.ə.ˈfɪl.ɪk/ (BrE)
preferring or attracted to animals, especially as a host or habitat

Examples

Zoophilic mosquitoes
Certain species of mosquitoes are considered zoophilic because they prefer to feed on animals.
The zoophilic nature of the parasite means it rarely infects humans.
Researchers studied the zoophilic tendencies of ticks in rural environments.
