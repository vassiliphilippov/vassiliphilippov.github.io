stringless

adjective
without strings
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɪŋləs / (AmE), / ˈstrɪŋləs / (BrE)
having no strings or not equipped with strings.

Examples

Stringless guitar
She bought a stringless guitar for her child to play safely.
The new variety of stringless beans is easier to cook and eat.
He admired the stringless puppet, which moved with hidden mechanisms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stringed - having strings, especially referring to musical instruments
