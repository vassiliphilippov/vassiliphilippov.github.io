plasterer's float

noun
hand tool for smoothing plaster or cement
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈplæs.tɚ.ɚz floʊt / (AmE), / ˈplɑː.stər.əz fləʊt / (BrE)
a hand tool with a flat surface used for smoothing and finishing plaster, cement, or stucco.

Examples

After applying the plaster, she finished the surface with a plasterer's float for a perfectly even look.
The mason used a plasterer's float to smooth the final layer of cement on the wall.
