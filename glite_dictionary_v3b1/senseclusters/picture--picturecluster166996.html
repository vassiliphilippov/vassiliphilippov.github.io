picture

noun
film; movie (old-fashioned)
4.0 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪk.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈpɪk.tʃə/ (BrE)
(old-fashioned, especially in American English) A film or movie; a story enacted by sound and a sequence of images giving the illusion of continuous movement.

Examples

A silent picture
Many silent pictures from the early 20th century still survive today.
She dreams of making it big in pictures one day.
We watched a classic picture at the old theater last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: film - a series of moving pictures, usually telling a story
  • synonym: movie - a recorded story shown in cinemas or on television; film
