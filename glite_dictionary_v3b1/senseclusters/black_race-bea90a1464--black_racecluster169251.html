Black race

noun
people traditionally identified by dark skin color
Pronunciation: / blæk reɪs / (AmE), / blæk reɪs / (BrE)
A group of people traditionally identified by dark skin color, often referring to people of African ancestry; the term is now considered outdated and is rarely used in modern contexts except historically or anthropologically.
