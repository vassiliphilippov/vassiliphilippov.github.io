bryophytic

adjective
relating to bryophytes (mosses, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˌbraɪ.əˈfɪt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌbrʌɪ.əˈfɪt.ɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of bryophytes, the group of nonvascular plants that includes mosses, liverworts, and hornworts.

Examples

Bryophytic plants
Many bryophytic species are highly sensitive to changes in humidity.
Researchers studied the bryophytic communities to assess their role in ecosystem health.
The forest floor was covered with a bryophytic mat of mosses and liverworts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bryophyte - a member of the division Bryophyta, such as a moss or liverwort
