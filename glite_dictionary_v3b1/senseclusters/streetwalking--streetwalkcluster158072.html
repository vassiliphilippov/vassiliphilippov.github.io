streetwalking

noun
soliciting prostitution
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːtˌwɔkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstriːtˌwɔːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of soliciting customers for prostitution by walking the streets, especially at night

Examples

Nighttime streetwalking
The police increased patrols to reduce streetwalking in the downtown area.
She was arrested for streetwalking after being seen soliciting on several corners.
Laws against streetwalking are enforced more strictly in some cities than others.
