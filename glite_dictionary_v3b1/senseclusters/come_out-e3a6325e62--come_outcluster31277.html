Synonyms

    2.3 33/m
    appear is used when a book or film is issued to the public, while come out means it becomes available.
  • release
    1.8 59/m
    release means to make a product or information available to people, while come out focuses on becoming publicly available.
  • publish
    3.3 144/m
    publish means to prepare and make a book or article available, while come out highlights the moment it becomes public.
  • roll out
    3.9 2.57/m
    roll out means to introduce something officially in stages for users, while come out simply means it becomes available.
