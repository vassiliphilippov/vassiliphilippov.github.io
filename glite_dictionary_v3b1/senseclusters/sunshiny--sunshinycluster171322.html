sunshiny

adjective
bright and cheerful
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌʃaɪni / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌʃaɪni / (BrE)
bright with sunlight or having a cheerful, sunny quality

Examples

A sunshiny day
The children played outside on a sunshiny morning, laughing and running through the grass.
Her sunshiny personality made everyone around her feel happier.
We sat on the porch, enjoying the sunshiny weather and a cool breeze.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gloomy - dark or lacking cheerfulness
  • synonym: sunny - bright with sunlight
