armlock

noun
joint control hold
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrmlɑk / (AmE), / ˈɑːmlɒk / (BrE)
a wrestling or martial arts hold that immobilizes or controls an opponent's arm

Examples

A tight armlock
The wrestler applied a strong armlock to prevent his opponent from escaping.
She learned how to use an armlock during her judo training sessions.
The match ended quickly after he secured an armlock on his rival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grip - a firm hold or grasp, especially in wrestling
  • synonym: headlock - a hold that secures an opponent's head under the arm
