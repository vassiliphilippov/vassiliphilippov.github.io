Monoceros

noun
mythical one-horned horse
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈnɑsəˌrɑs / (AmE), / məˈnɒsərɒs / (BrE)
a mythical creature resembling a horse with a single horn, also known as a unicorn

Examples

A Monoceros in legends
Ancient stories describe the Monoceros as a symbol of purity and grace.
In medieval art, the Monoceros often appears alongside maidens and kings.
Some believed that the horn of a Monoceros could cure any poison.
