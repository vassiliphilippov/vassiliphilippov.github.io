collectivistic

adjective
supporting collective ownership or action
7.3 6.4
Pronunciation: /kə.lek.tɪ.ˈvɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /kə.lek.tɪ.ˈvɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by the principle of ownership or control by the group rather than individuals; supporting or embodying collective rather than individual action or values.

Examples

A collectivistic approach
A collectivistic approach to problem-solving can lead to greater cooperation within teams.
Her collectivistic views influenced the company's policies on shared resources.
Some societies are more collectivistic, valuing group harmony over individual achievement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collective - done by people acting as a group; relating to a group as a whole
  • antonym: individualistic - relating to or characterized by individualism; opposite of collectivistic
