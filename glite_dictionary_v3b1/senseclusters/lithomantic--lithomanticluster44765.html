lithomantic

adjective
relating to stone-based divination
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪθ.əˈmæn.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌlɪθ.əˈmæn.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of lithomancy, a form of divination using stones or pebbles.

Examples

Lithomantic stone reading
During the journey, the seer offered a lithomantic reading to predict favorable outcomes.
Her interest in ancient lithomantic practices led her to study classical occult texts.
The ritual was conducted in a lithomantic tradition, interpreting the arrangement of seven stones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lithomancy - divination by interpreting stones or pebbles
