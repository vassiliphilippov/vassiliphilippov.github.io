stout

adjective
brave and dependable
5.1 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / staÊŠt / (AmE), / staÊŠt / (BrE)
(of a person or their actions) brave, determined, and dependable, especially in the face of difficulty.

Examples

A stout defender
Despite the danger, she remained a stout defender of justice.
His stout heart carried him through the darkest times.
The organization needs a few stout supporters to keep it going.

Synonyms

    8.2 0.06/m
    stouthearted shows clear courage and steady resolve in danger, while stout adds a sense of dependable strength in hard situations.
  • stalwart
    7.1 0.77/m

  • staunch
    6.3 2.29/m

  • hardy
    4.9 0.39/m
    hardy endures harsh conditions and keeps going, while stout also stresses determined bravery and being dependable for others.
