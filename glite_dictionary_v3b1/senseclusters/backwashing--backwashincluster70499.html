backwashing

noun
filter cleaning process
Pronunciation: / ˈbækwɑːʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbækwɒʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a process of cleaning a filter by reversing the flow of water through it to remove trapped particles

Examples

Pool filter backwashing
Regular backwashing is necessary to maintain the efficiency of a swimming pool filter.
The technician performed backwashing to clear debris from the water treatment system.
Without proper backwashing, the filter can become clogged and stop working effectively.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filtration - the process of removing particles from a liquid or gas by passing it through a filter
  • synonym: flushing - cleaning something by forcing water or another liquid through it
