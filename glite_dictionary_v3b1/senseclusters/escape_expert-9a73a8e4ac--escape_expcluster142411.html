escape expert

noun
entertainer skilled in escaping acts
Pronunciation: / ɪˈskeɪp ˈɛkspɚt / (AmE), / ɪˈskeɪp ˈɛkspɜːt / (BrE)
an entertainer who specializes in escaping from restraints, traps, or confined spaces as a performance
