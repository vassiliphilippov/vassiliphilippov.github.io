reevaluation

noun
the act of evaluating again
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌɛvəˈlueɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌiːvəˈlueɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of evaluating something again, especially in order to reconsider its value, importance, or condition

Examples

A reevaluation of strategy
A sudden drop in sales prompted a reevaluation of their marketing strategy.
After receiving new information, the committee began a reevaluation of the proposed policy.
Her performance led to a reevaluation of her role within the company.
