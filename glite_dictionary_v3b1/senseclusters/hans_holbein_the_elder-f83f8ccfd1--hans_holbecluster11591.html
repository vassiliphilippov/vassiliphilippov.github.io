Hans Holbein the Elder

proper noun
German painter of religious works (1465–1524)
Pronunciation: / hɑnz ˈhoʊlbaɪn ði ˈɛldər / (AmE), / hans ˈhɒlbaɪn ði ˈɛldə / (BrE)
(1465–1524) A German painter known for religious works and as the father of Hans Holbein the Younger.
