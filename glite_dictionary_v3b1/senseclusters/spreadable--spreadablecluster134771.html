spreadable

adjective
easy to spread
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɛdəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsprɛdəbəl / (BrE)
able to be easily spread or applied over a surface, especially as a soft substance like butter, cheese, paint, or cream

Examples

Spreadable cheese
This cheese is so spreadable that you can easily put it on crackers.
The lotion is highly spreadable, making it simple to cover large areas of skin.
She prefers spreadable butter for her morning toast.
The butter is so spreadable that it glides smoothly over fresh bread.
She prefers spreadable cheese for making quick snacks for her children.
This new margarine remains spreadable even when taken straight from the fridge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creamy - smooth and easy to spread in texture
  • antonym: hard - difficult or impossible to spread
  • synonym: pliable - easily bent or shaped
  • antonym: sticky - tending to adhere to surfaces
