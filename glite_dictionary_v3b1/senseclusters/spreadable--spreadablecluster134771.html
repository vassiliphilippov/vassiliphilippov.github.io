spreadable

adjective
easy to spread
2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈspred.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈspred.ə.bəl/ (BrE)
able to be easily spread or applied over a surface, especially as a soft substance like butter, cheese, paint, or cream

Examples

Spreadable cheese
This cheese is so spreadable that you can easily put it on crackers.
The lotion is highly spreadable, making it simple to cover large areas of skin.
She prefers spreadable butter for her morning toast.

This new margarine remains spreadable even when taken straight from the fridge.
