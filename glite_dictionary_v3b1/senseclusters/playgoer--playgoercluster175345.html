playgoer

noun
person who attends theatrical performances
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪˌɡoʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌɡəʊ.ə / (BrE)
a person who regularly attends theatrical performances

Examples

A playgoer at theatre
As a dedicated playgoer, she keeps a journal of every performance she attends.
Seasoned playgoers often compare current performances with classics they have seen in the past.
The playgoer eagerly waited for the curtain to rise on the new production.
