outreach

verb
to reach farther
Pronunciation: / ˌaʊtˈritʃ / (AmE), / ˌaʊtˈriːtʃ / (BrE)

To outreach a rival

The plant's roots outreach those of its neighbors, allowing it to absorb more water.
