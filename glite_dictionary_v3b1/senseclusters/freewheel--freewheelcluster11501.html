freewheel

verb
live freely or irresponsibly
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfriˌwil / (AmE), / ˈfriːwiːl / (BrE)
to live in a relaxed, unhurried, or sometimes irresponsible way, without worrying about rules or consequences

Examples

To freewheel through life
After college, she decided to freewheel for a while before starting her career.
He spent his twenties freewheeling across Europe, taking odd jobs and enjoying life.
Some worry that if they freewheel too long, it will be hard to settle down later.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drift - to move or be carried along without purpose; to live aimlessly
