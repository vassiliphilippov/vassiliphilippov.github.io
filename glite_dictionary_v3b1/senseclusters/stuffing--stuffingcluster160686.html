stuffing

noun
excessive keyword insertion (SEO)
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈstʌf.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈstʌf.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
The practice of inserting an excessive number of keywords or phrases into web content, meta tags, or emails to try to manipulate search-engine rankings.

Examples

Keyword stuffing
Some sites engage in stuffing to boost search results, though Google frowns on it.
The SEO audit warned that excessive stuffing of keywords could lead to penalties and loss of traffic.
She removed hidden keyword stuffing from the page to comply with search engine guidelines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keyword stuffing - the specific practice of overusing keywords in content to influence search rankings
  • synonym: over-optimization - using excessive or manipulative SEO techniques that can harm search rankings
