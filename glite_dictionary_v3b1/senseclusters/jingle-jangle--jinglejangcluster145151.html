jingle-jangle

verb
make a metallic ringing sound
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪŋɡəl ˌdʒæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪŋɡəl ˌdʒæŋɡəl / (BrE)
to make a repeated light, metallic, ringing sound, often as small metal objects touch each other

Examples

To jingle-jangle softly
A wind chime would slowly jingle-jangle in the evening breeze.
He could hear the dog’s collar jingle-jangle as it ran around the yard.
The coins in her purse began to jingle-jangle with every step she took.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jingle - to make a light, ringing sound like small metal objects striking together
