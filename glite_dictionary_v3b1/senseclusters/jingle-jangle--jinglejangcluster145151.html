jingle-jangle

verb
make a metallic ringing sound
7.7 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒɪŋ.ɡəl ˈdʒæŋ.ɡəl/ (AmE), /ˈdʒɪŋ.ɡəl ˈdʒæŋ.ɡəl/ (BrE)
to make a repeated light, metallic, ringing sound, often as small metal objects touch each other
