Random Number God

noun
humorous term for controller of random outcomes
Pronunciation: / ˈrændəm ˈnʌmbɚ ɡɑd / (AmE), / ˈrændəm ˈnʌmbə ɡɒd / (BrE)
a humorous or informal term for a supposed force or entity that controls or influences random outcomes, especially in games or gambling

Examples

The Random Number God controls luck
After a streak of bad luck in the game, he jokingly blamed the Random Number God.
Every time you roll the dice, you invoke the Random Number God for a favorable outcome.
Online gamers often refer to the Random Number God when unpredictable things happen during play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fate - the development of events beyond a person's control, regarded as predetermined
  • synonym: luck - success or failure apparently brought by chance
