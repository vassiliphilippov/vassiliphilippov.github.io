Daricon

noun
anticholinergic drug for peptic ulcers
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛrɪˌkɒn / (AmE), / ˈdærɪˌkɒn / (BrE)
A trademarked anticholinergic drug used in the treatment of peptic ulcers.

Examples

Daricon drug
Daricon is less commonly used today due to the development of newer medications for ulcers.
Patients taking Daricon should be aware of possible side effects such as dry mouth or blurred vision.
The doctor prescribed Daricon to help relieve her peptic ulcer symptoms.
