upflung

adjective
edges turned upward
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌflʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌflʌŋ / (BrE)
having edges, rims, or parts that are turned or bent upward, especially of clothing or objects

Examples

Upflung brim
The hat's upflung brim gave her a stylish and confident appearance.
He admired the upflung collar of his new coat in the mirror.
The architect designed the roof with upflung edges to create a dramatic effect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downturned - having been turned or directed downward
  • synonym: upturned - having been turned or directed upward
