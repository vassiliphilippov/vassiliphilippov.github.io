batea

noun
shellfish farming raft
Pronunciation: / bəˈteɪə / (AmE), / bəˈteɪə / (BrE)
a large floating platform or raft used in coastal aquaculture for cultivating mussels or other shellfish

Examples

Mussels on a batea
The workers checked the mussels growing on the ropes beneath the batea every morning.
A new batea was installed in the bay to increase shellfish production.
Tourists watched as fishermen harvested oysters from the floating batea near the shore.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raft - a flat structure for floating on water
