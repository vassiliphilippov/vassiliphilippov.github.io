common bearberry

noun
a mat-forming evergreen shrub with red berries
8.1 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.mən ˈber.bər.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈkɒm.ən ˈbeə.bə.ri/ (BrE)
