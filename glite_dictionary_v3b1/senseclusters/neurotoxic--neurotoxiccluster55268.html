neurotoxic

adjective
poisonous to nerves or nerve cells
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈtɑksɪk / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈtɒksɪk / (BrE)
Poisonous or harmful to nerves or nerve cells.

Examples

Neurotoxic chemicals
Certain pesticides are classified as neurotoxic because they damage the nervous system.
Lead exposure can have neurotoxic effects, especially in young children.
The study examined the neurotoxic properties of various industrial chemicals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neuroprotective - serving to protect nerve cells from damage
  • synonym: toxic - poisonous; containing poison
