rankness

noun
strong, offensive smell (quality)
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈræŋknəs / (AmE), / ˈræŋknəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having a strong, offensive, or unpleasant smell

Examples

Rankness of the smell
After weeks of neglect, the rankness of the locker room became obvious to everyone.
The rankness of rotting garbage filled the alley with an unbearable odor.
The rankness of the swamp air made breathing deeply almost impossible.
