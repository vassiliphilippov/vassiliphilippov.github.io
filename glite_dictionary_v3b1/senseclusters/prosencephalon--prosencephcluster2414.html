prosencephalon

noun
front region of the developing brain
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊ.sɛnˈsɛf.əˌlɒn / (AmE), / ˌprəʊ.sɛnˈsɛf.əl.ɒn / (BrE)
the front part of the brain that develops from the anterior part of the neural tube in embryonic development

Examples

The prosencephalon part
Damage to the prosencephalon during early development can lead to serious neurological disorders.
Neuroscientists study the prosencephalon to better understand human brain evolution.
The prosencephalon eventually develops into major brain structures such as the cerebrum and thalamus.
