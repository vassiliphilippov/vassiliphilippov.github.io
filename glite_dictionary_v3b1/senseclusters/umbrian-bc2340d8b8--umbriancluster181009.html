Umbrian

noun
extinct Italic language of ancient Italy
Pronunciation: / ˈʌmbriən / (AmE), / ˈʌmbriən / (BrE)
an extinct Italic language once spoken by the Umbri people of ancient southern Italy

Examples

Umbrian language
Few people today can read or speak Umbrian since it became extinct centuries ago.
Linguists have compared Umbrian with other Italic languages such as Oscan and Latin.
The ancient inscription was written in Umbrian and required a specialist to translate it.
