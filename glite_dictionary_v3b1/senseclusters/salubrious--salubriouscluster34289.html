Synonyms

  • healthful
    3.2 3.42/m

  • healthy
    1.0 33/m
    healthy often describes people or choices that support good health, while salubrious sounds more formal and suits places or climate.
  • salutary
    7.6 1.17/m
    salutary means having a beneficial effect, often after something bad, while salubrious specifically means good for health or climate.
