parcel

verb
to cover with strips of canvas (nautical)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑr.səl / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.səl / (BrE)
to cover something, especially rope, with strips of canvas, typically as a protective wrapping

Examples

To parcel a rope
After cleaning the lines, he began to parcel each section to extend its lifespan.
Before storing the anchor rope for winter, the sailors carefully parceled it with fresh strips of canvas.
Traditionally, riggers parcel ropes to protect them from moisture and wear at sea.
