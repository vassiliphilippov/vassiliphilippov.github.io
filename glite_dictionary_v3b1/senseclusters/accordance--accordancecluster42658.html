accordance

noun
agreement; conformity
3.9 13/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàk…îrd…ôns / (AmE), / …ôÀàk…îÀêd…ôns / (BrE)
agreement or conformity with a rule, opinion, or request; concurrence or harmony.

Examples

Accordance with instructions
He completed the task in accordance with the manager's instructions.
The policy was changed in accordance with recent government regulations.
The teams acted in full accordance to ensure a successful outcome.

Synonyms

  • conformance
    7.0 0.82/m
    conformance means matching a set of rules or standards in form, while accordance means being in general agreement or harmony.
  • conformity
    4.8 4.26/m
    conformity describes behaving or thinking to match standards or people, while accordance focuses on agreement or harmony with a rule.
  • in harmony with
    3.7 1.31/m
    in harmony with expresses being consistent or in tune with something, while accordance is used formally to show agreement.
  • congruity
    7.4 0.18/m
    congruity means a good fit or suitability between things, while accordance stresses following a rule or being in agreement.
  • congruence
    6.7 1.69/m
    congruence means harmony or agreement of parts or ideas, while accordance leans toward following rules or formal agreement.
  • agreement
    1.0 26/m
    agreement is people sharing the same opinion or giving consent, while accordance often names formal or rule-based conformity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agreement - a situation in which people have the same opinion; harmony
  • antonym: disagreement - a lack of consensus or harmony; difference of opinion
