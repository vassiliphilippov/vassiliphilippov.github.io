Synonyms

  • insert
    3.5 20/m
    insert is a more formal word for putting something carefully into an object or text, while put in is more casual.
  • place
    1.0 157/m
    place is used for arranging or setting something deliberately in a position, while put in is more informal and general.
  • stick in
    3.0 1.55/m
    stick in suggests pushing something into a gap quickly or roughly, while put in is neutral and general in tone.
  • deposit
    2.5 5.59/m
    deposit means leaving something in a safe place or for storage, while put in simply means to place or insert it.
