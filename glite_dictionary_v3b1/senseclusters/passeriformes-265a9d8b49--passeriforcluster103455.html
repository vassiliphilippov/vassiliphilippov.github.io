Passeriformes

noun
order of perching birds
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpæs.ə.rɪˈfɔr.miːz /; / ˌpæsərɪˈfɔrmiːz / (AmE), / ˌpæs.ər.ɪˈfɔː.miːz /; / ˌpæsərɪˈfɔːmiːz / (BrE)
the largest order of birds, including perching birds such as sparrows, finches, robins, swallows, and many others
