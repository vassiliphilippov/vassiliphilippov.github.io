east by south

noun
compass point between east and east-southeast
Pronunciation: / ist baɪ saʊθ / (AmE), / iːst baɪ saʊθ / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point south of due east, corresponding to 101°15′.

Examples

East by south compass point
Sailors relied on the east by south heading for their navigation across the bay.
The map indicated the town was located east by south of the mountain range.
The ship changed course to east by south to avoid the storm.
