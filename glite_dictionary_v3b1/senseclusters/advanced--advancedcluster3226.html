advanced

adjective
at a late stage in life or time
6.2
Pronunciation: / ədˈvænst / (AmE), / ədˈvɑːnst / (BrE)
far on in life or time; having reached a late stage of existence or development, especially with regard to age

Examples

Advanced age
My grandfather is now in his advanced years, but he still maintains an active lifestyle.
She retired at the advanced age of seventy, after a long career.
The program is designed for people of advanced age who require special services.
