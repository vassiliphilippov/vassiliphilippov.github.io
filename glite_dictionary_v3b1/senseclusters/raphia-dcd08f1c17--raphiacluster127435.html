Raphia

noun
a tropical feather palm genus producing raffia fiber
Pronunciation: / ˈræfiə / (AmE), / ˈræfiə /; / ˈreɪfiə / (BrE)
a genus of feather palms found in tropical Africa, Madagascar, and Central and South America, cultivated for their commercial uses such as strong leaf fibers, palm wine, and building materials
