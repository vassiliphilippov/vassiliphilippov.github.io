methyl bromide

noun
poisonous fumigant gas or liquid (CH3Br)
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːθəl ˈbroʊmaɪd / (AmE), / ˈmiːθəl ˈbrəʊmaɪd / (BrE)
a poisonous gas or liquid (CH3Br) used as a fumigant to control pests such as rodents and worms

Examples

Methyl bromide fumigant gas
Methyl bromide was once widely used to fumigate soil before planting crops.
The use of methyl bromide has decreased because of environmental concerns.
Workers must wear protective gear when handling methyl bromide due to its toxicity.

  • synonym: bromomethane - another name for methyl bromide
