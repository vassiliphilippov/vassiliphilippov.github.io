finish

noun
the act of completing or ending
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
the act of completing something or the final stage of a process, especially as achieved in a competition, race, or performance.

Examples

Second-place finish
His best finish in the national marathon was second.
She crossed the line with a spectacular finish and won first place.
The audience applauded loudly at the finish of the performance.

  • antonym: start - the act of beginning something
