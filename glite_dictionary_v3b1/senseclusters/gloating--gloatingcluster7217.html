gloating

noun
malicious satisfaction or pleasure
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡloʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
A feeling or expression of malicious satisfaction or pleasure at someone else's misfortune.

Examples

Gloating over defeat
Her gloating knew no bounds after the other team's defeat.
The social media comments were filled with gloating about the failed project.
There was an unmistakable gloating in his eyes when his rival lost the debate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: schadenfreude - pleasure in another's misfortune
  • antonym: sympathy - feelings of pity and sorrow for someone else's misfortune
  • synonym: triumph - great victory or achievement (often similar satisfaction)
