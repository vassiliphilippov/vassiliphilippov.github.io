simmer

noun
temperature just below boiling point
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪmə / (BrE)
A state or temperature just below the boiling point, often used in cooking to allow gentle heating.

Examples

A gentle simmer
After bringing the water to a boil, reduce the heat to maintain a simmer.
Keep the soup at a gentle simmer so the flavors can meld.
Let the sauce cook at a simmer until it thickens.
