lak

noun
resin for shellac and dyes
Pronunciation: / læk / (AmE), / læk / (BrE)
a resinous substance secreted by certain insects, used in making shellac and dyes

Examples

Lak resin finish
The craftsman used lak to create a glossy finish on the wooden box.
Traditional red dye is often made from the resin of lak insects.
Collectors value antique furniture coated with natural lak for its rich color.

  • synonym: shellac - a resin made from processed lac, used as a varnish
