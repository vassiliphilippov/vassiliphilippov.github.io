sfumato

noun
soft color blending
9.9
Pronunciation: /sfuː.ˈmɑː.toʊ/ /; / /sfjuː.ˈmɑː.toʊ/ (AmE), /sfuː.ˈmɑː.tə/ /; / /sfjuː.ˈmɑː.tə/ (BrE)
a painting technique or effect that produces soft, gradual transitions between colors and tones, often creating a smoky or hazy look
