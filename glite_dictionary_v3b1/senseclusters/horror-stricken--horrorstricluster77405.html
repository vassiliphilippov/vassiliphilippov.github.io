horror-stricken

adjective
overcome with horror or fear
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrɚ ˌstrɪkən / (AmE), / ˈhɒrə ˌstrɪkən / (BrE)
Extremely shocked or terrified by something very unpleasant or frightening.

Examples

A horror-stricken face
He became horror-stricken as he realized the danger he was in.
She stood horror-stricken at the sight of the accident, unable to move or speak.
The villagers were horror-stricken when they discovered what had happened in the old house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horrified - feeling horror or shock
  • antonym: unafraid - not feeling fear or horror
