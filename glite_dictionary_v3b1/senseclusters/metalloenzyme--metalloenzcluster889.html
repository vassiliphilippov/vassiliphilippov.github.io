metalloenzyme

noun
enzyme with metal ion
8.2
Pronunciation: / məˌtæloʊˈɛnzaɪm / (AmE), / mɪˌtæləʊˈɛnzaɪm / (BrE)
an enzyme that contains a metal ion as an essential part of its structure and function

Examples

A metalloenzyme with metal
A metalloenzyme often requires a specific metal ion, such as zinc or iron, to function properly.
Researchers discovered that the metalloenzyme lost activity when the metal ion was removed.
Copper is an essential component of this metalloenzyme involved in cellular respiration.
