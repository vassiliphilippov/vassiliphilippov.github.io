sadistically

adverb
with cruel pleasure
Pronunciation: / səˈdɪstɪkli / (AmE), / səˈdɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a cruel way that shows enjoyment in causing pain or suffering to others

Examples

To sadistically cause pain
The villain sadistically enjoyed the fear he caused in his victims.
She smiled sadistically as she revealed the bad news to her rival.
He teased his little brother sadistically, knowing it would make him cry.
He laughed sadistically as his opponent struggled to get up from the ground.
The villain sadistically enjoyed making others feel helpless in his presence.
She described how the group sadistically mocked anyone who made a mistake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruelly - in a way that causes pain or suffering to others
  • antonym: kindly - in a way that shows kindness or compassion
  • antonym: mercifully - in a way that shows kindness or forgiveness
