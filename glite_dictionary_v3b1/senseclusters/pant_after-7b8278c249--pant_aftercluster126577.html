pant after

verb
breathe heavily after exertion
Pronunciation: / pænt ˈæf.tɚ / (AmE), / pænt ˈɑːf.tə / (BrE)
to breathe heavily and quickly because of recent physical exertion or excitement

Examples

To pant after running
He tends to pant after even a short climb up the stairs.
My dog would pant after playing fetch in the park.
The runners began to pant after finishing the marathon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pant - to breathe quickly and loudly with your mouth open, usually after physical effort
