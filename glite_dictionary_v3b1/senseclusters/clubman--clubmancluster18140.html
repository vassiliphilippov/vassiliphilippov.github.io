clubman

noun
male club member
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌbmən / (AmE), / ˈklʌbmən / (BrE)
a man who is a member of a club, especially a social or sports club

Examples

A regular clubman
The clubman greeted his friends warmly as he entered the lounge.
As a respected clubman, he often organized charity events for the community.
Every Friday, the clubman played cards with his fellow members at the club.

As a dedicated clubman, he attended every event organized by the society.
In the early 1900s, being a clubman was a mark of social status.
