manna

noun
sweet exudate from trees or shrubs
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmænə / (AmE), / ˈmænə / (BrE)
A sweet, hardened substance exuded by certain trees and shrubs, often used as food or in medicine.

Examples

Tree manna sweetener
Some cuisines use manna as a natural sweetener in traditional desserts.
The manna produced by the tamarisk tree was once valued for its medicinal properties.
The Bedouins collected manna that had hardened on the desert shrubs overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sap - the watery fluid circulating through a plant
