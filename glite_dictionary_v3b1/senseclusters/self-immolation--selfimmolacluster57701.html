self-immolation

noun
suicide by setting oneself on fire
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf ɪˈmoʊleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ɪˈməʊleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of deliberately setting oneself on fire, usually as a form of protest or suicide

Examples

Many were shocked by the student's act of self-immolation in the city square.
Reports of self-immolation as protest have occurred throughout history.
The monk's self-immolation drew international attention to the cause.
