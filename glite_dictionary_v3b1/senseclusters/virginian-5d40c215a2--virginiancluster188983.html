Virginian

noun
a native or resident of Virginia
Pronunciation: / vɚˈdʒɪnjən / (AmE), / vəˈdʒɪnjən / (BrE)
a person who was born in or lives in the state of Virginia, USA

Examples

A proud Virginian
As a lifelong Virginian, he knows the Blue Ridge Mountains well.
Many Virginians celebrate their state's history with annual festivals.
She is a proud Virginian who often talks about her hometown.
