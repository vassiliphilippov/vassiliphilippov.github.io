inactive

adjective
not participating in a chemical reaction
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈæktɪv / (BrE)
(chemistry) Not participating in a chemical reaction; chemically inert.

Examples

Inactive substance
Hydrogen is usually inactive at room temperature unless a catalyst is present.
The chemical solution contains both active and inactive compounds.
The laboratory required inactive gases for the experiment to prevent side reactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inert - chemically unreactive
