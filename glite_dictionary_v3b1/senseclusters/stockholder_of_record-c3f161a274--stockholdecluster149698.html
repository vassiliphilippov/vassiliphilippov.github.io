stockholder of record

noun
officially registered shareholder
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːkˌhoʊldər əv ˈrɛkərd / (AmE), / ˈstɒkˌhəʊldə əv ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
a person or entity whose name is officially listed as the owner of shares at a specific date in a corporation's records

Examples

The stockholder of record
As the stockholder of record, she must approve the transfer of her shares.
Only the stockholder of record is entitled to receive dividend payments.
The company's annual report was sent to every stockholder of record as of December 31.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beneficial owner - person who enjoys the benefits of ownership though not on record
  • synonym: registered owner - person officially recorded as owning something
