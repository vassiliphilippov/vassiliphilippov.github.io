citable

adjective
liable for citation
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsaɪtəbəl / (BrE)
liable to be given a formal citation or ticket, especially for a legal, traffic, or regulatory offense

Examples

Citable offense
Parking in a fire lane is a citable offense in most cities.
The officer explained that using a mobile phone while driving is citable under local law.
Leaving trash on the sidewalk can be considered a citable violation by city authorities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: punishable - deserving or liable to be punished
