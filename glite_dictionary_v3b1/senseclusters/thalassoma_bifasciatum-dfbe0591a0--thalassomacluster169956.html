Thalassoma bifasciatum

noun
species of small Atlantic wrasse
Pronunciation: / θəˌlæs.əˈmə baɪˌfæsiˈeɪtəm / (AmE), / θəˌlæs.əmə bɪˌfæsiˈɑːtəm / (BrE)
A small species of Atlantic wrasse, notable for the male's brilliant blue head, found in coral reefs of the western Atlantic Ocean.
