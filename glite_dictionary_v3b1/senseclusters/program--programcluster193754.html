program

verb
arrange or schedule events
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊˌgræm /; / ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
to arrange or plan the schedule of events or activities for something

Examples

To program an event
She expertly programmed the festival to ensure a diverse range of performances.
The committee will program a series of lectures for the conference.
We need to program every part of the evening, including dinner and entertainment.
He programmed the conference so that all speakers had enough time.
She was asked to programme the annual music festival.
The committee will programme the events to ensure a smooth schedule.

Related Terms

  • synonym: schedule - to arrange for something to take place at a particular time
