plunk

verb
put down heavily with a noise
Pronunciation: / plʌŋk / (AmE), / plʌŋk / (BrE)
to put or drop something or oneself down heavily and often with a loud sound

Examples

To plunk a bag down
He plunked his suitcase on the floor and let out a sigh.
Sarah plunked herself down in the nearest chair after the long walk.
The kid plunked a handful of coins onto the counter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plonk - to put something down heavily and noisily
