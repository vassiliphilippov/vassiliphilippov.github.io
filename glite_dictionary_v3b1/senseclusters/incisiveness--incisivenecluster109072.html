incisiveness

noun
clarity and sharpness of thought or expression
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsaɪsɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈsaɪsɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of expressing ideas clearly, sharply, and with force; keenness and clarity of thought or expression

Examples

Clear incisiveness of thought
He answered each question with remarkable incisiveness and confidence.
Journalists praised the incisiveness of his writing on political issues.
The incisiveness of her analysis impressed everyone at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acuity - sharpness or keenness, especially of thought, vision, or hearing
