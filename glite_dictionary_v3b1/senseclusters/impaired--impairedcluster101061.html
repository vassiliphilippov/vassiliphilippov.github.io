Synonyms

  • weakened
    2.7 0.00/m

  • damaged
    1.1 0.04/m
    damaged is harmed or spoiled so it is less useful, while impaired emphasizes loss of function or ability.
  • disabled
    2.3 20/m
    disabled has a lasting condition that limits activity, while impaired focuses on a loss or damage to normal function.
  • debilitated
    7.0 0.07/m
    debilitated is very weak from illness or injury, while impaired means reduced ability or function without implying recovery time.
  • infirm
    8.1 0.03/m
    infirm describes someone weak in health or strength, while impaired highlights damage that reduces normal function or ability.
