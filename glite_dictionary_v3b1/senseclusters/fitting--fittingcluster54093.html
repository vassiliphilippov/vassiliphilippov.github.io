  • appropriate
    3.5 40/m
    appropriate is more formal and used in official contexts like rules or reports, while fitting is everyday and neutral.
  • suited
    2.8 0.8/m
    suited is often used with 'to' to show a match or purpose, while fitting describes a general sense of being proper.
  • fit
