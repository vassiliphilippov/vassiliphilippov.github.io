cabalist

noun
student of Jewish Kabbalah
Pronunciation: / ˈkæbəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈkabəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or practices the Jewish mystical tradition known as the Kabbalah

Examples

Jewish cabalist
As a respected cabalist, she often taught lessons on Jewish mysticism.

The cabalist spent years deciphering the hidden meanings in ancient texts.
