winningly

adverb
charmingly
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪnɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈwɪnɪŋli / (BrE)
in a charming, attractive, or pleasing manner

Examples

To smile winningly
He greeted the guests winningly, making everyone feel welcome at the party.
The child looked up winningly at her parents, hoping for another piece of cake.
She answered the difficult question winningly, impressing both the teacher and her classmates.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awkwardly - in a clumsy or uncomfortable way
  • synonym: charmingly - in a delightful or attractive way
