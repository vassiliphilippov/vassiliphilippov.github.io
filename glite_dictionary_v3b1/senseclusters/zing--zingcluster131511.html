zing

verb
to move swiftly
5.1
Pronunciation: / zɪŋ / (AmE), / zɪŋ / (BrE)
to move quickly and energetically, often making a sharp or buzzing sound

Examples

To zing past
The bee zinged past my ear as I walked through the garden.
A paper airplane zinged across the classroom, surprising the teacher.
He watched the tennis ball zing over the net during the fast-paced match.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crawl - to move very slowly
  • synonym: whiz - to move quickly with a buzzing or hissing sound
  • synonym: zoom - to move or travel very quickly
