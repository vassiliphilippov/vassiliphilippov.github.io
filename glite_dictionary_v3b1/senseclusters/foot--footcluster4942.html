foot

noun
lower part of leg below the ankle
Pronunciation: / fʊt / (AmE), / fʊt / (BrE)
the lower part of the leg below the ankle, on which a person or animal stands and walks

Examples

A sore foot
After running the marathon, his foot was sore and swollen.
She dipped her foot into the cool water to test the temperature.
The doctor examined the injury on his left foot.
Birds have a unique structure in their foot that helps them perch on branches.
The horse lifted its foot carefully to avoid the sharp stones.
When the veterinarian examined the dog's injured foot, she found a small thorn.
An injury to his left foot kept him off the field for weeks.
She removed her shoes and felt the soft grass under her foot.
When standing for long periods, your feet may become tired.
