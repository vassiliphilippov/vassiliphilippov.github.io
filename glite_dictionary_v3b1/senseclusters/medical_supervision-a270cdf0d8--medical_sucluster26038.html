medical supervision

noun
oversight by healthcare professionals
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˌsuːpərˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˌsjuːpəˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
the monitoring or oversight of a person's health or medical treatment by qualified healthcare professionals

Examples

Medical supervision by doctors
Due to potential side effects, starting this medication needs medical supervision.
Patients with chronic illnesses often require continuous medical supervision.
This treatment should only be administered under medical supervision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medical oversight - the act of overseeing medical care
  • antonym: self-medication - taking medication without professional supervision
