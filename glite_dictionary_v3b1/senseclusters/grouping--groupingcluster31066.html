Synonyms

  • categorization
    4.4 2.14/m
    categorization is the process of putting items into named groups by shared traits, while grouping covers many organizing methods.
  • classification
    3.7 8.17/m
    classification arranges things into formal classes or categories for study or order, while grouping is a broader act of making groups.
  • sorting
    2.0 2.23/m
    sorting arranges items by a chosen rule, often to order them or separate types, while grouping simply gathers similar items.
  • clustering
    4.1 1.75/m
    clustering collects similar items into nearby groups, often by similarity in data or space, while grouping is any way of forming groups.
  • aggregation
    6.1 2.35/m
    aggregation brings things together into one whole or mass, often ignoring internal types, while grouping separates items into clear groups.
