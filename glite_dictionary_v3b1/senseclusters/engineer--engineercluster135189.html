engineer

verb
plan and direct a complex undertaking
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.dʒəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
to design, create, or arrange something complex using skill or technical knowledge

Examples

To engineer a solution
She skillfully engineered the company's expansion into new markets.
The government engineered a solution to the economic crisis through careful negotiation.
They engineered the whole event to appear spontaneous, though it was meticulously planned.
He skillfully engineered the merger between the two companies.
She engineered a new irrigation system for the entire city.
The team engineered the bridge to withstand strong earthquakes.
After months of planning, the team finally engineered a system capable of handling millions of users.
The company hired experts to engineer a new security system for their offices.
We need to engineer a system that minimizes downtime and maximizes efficiency.
