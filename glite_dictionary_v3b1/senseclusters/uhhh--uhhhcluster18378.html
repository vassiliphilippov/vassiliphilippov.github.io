uhhh

interjection
hesitation sound
Pronunciation: / ʌh / (AmE), / ʌh / (BrE)
used to express hesitation, uncertainty, or a pause while thinking or speaking

Examples

Uhhh I hesitate
"Uhhh, I think the answer is five, but I'm not completely sure," she replied.
He paused and said, "uhhh, let me check my notes before I answer."
During the interview, he kept saying uhhh whenever he was unsure of what to say next.
