wrong number

noun
an unintended telephone number
Pronunciation: / rɔŋ ˈnʌmbɚ / (AmE), / rɒŋ ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
a telephone number that is not the one intended or desired, especially when the person reached is not the one sought

Examples

Dial the wrong number
I think you have dialed the wrong number; there's nobody named Sarah here.
She apologized when she realized she had called the wrong number twice in one night.
Whenever I get the wrong number, I politely tell the caller they made a mistake.

Related Terms

  • antonym: right number - the telephone number of the intended recipient
