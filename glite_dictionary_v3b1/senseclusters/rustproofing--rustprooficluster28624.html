rustproofing

noun
metal protection treatment
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌstˌpruːfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrʌstˌpruːfɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or treatment of applying a protective coating to metal to prevent rust and corrosion

Examples

Rustproofing a car
The mechanic recommended rustproofing the car to extend its lifespan in a humid climate.
Annual rustproofing can help prevent damage to metal structures exposed to rain and snow.
Many manufacturers now include rustproofing as a standard feature on new vehicles.
Regular rustproofing can extend the life of your car, especially in wet climates.
The mechanic recommended rustproofing the undercarriage to protect it from salt damage.
Many new vehicles come with factory-applied rustproofing to prevent corrosion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coating - a layer of a substance covering a surface
  • antonym: corrosion - the gradual destruction of metal by chemical reaction
