Filipino

adjective
relating to the Philippines or its people
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪlɪˈpinoʊ / (AmE), / ˌfɪlɪˈpiːnəʊ / (BrE)
of or relating to the Philippines or its people, language, or culture

Examples

Filipino culture
He speaks both English and Filipino languages fluently.
She cooked a traditional Filipino dish for dinner last night.
The team studied Filipino customs and traditions for their report.

Related Terms
