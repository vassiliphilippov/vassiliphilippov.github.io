mistype

noun
typing error
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstaɪp / (AmE), / ˈmɪstaɪp / (BrE)
an instance of typing something incorrectly, usually by accident

Examples

A mistype in text
She noticed a mistype in the email and quickly fixed it before sending.
The document contained only one mistype, which was easy to correct.
He apologized for the mistype in the report and sent a revised version.

Related Terms

  • antonym: correction - the act of making something right after a mistake
  • synonym: error - a mistake or inaccuracy
  • synonym: typo - a small mistake made in typed or printed text
