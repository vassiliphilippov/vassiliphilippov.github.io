Arab

noun
member of an Arabic-speaking Semitic people
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈær.əb / (AmE), / ˈær.əb / (BrE)
a member of a Semitic people originally from the Arabian Peninsula and surrounding regions, who speaks Arabic and inhabits much of the Middle East and North Africa

Examples

An Arab person
Many Arabs live in countries across the Middle East and North Africa.
She is an Arab who grew up in Egypt and speaks several dialects of Arabic.
The Arabs have a long history of scholarship, trade, and cultural exchange.
