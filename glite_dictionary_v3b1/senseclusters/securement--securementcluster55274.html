securement

noun
fastening device or method
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊrmənt / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊəmənt / (BrE)
a device, fastener, or method used to hold something firmly in place

Examples

Securement strap
The movers used extra securement to keep the furniture from sliding in the truck.
Proper securement of cargo is essential for safe transportation on the highway.
She checked the securement of the straps before lifting the heavy box.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fastener - a device that attaches or secures things together
  • antonym: release - the act of letting something go or setting it free
  • synonym: restraint - a means of holding something back or in place
