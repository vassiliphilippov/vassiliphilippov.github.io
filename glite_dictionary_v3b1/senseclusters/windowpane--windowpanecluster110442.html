windowpane

noun
pane of glass in a window
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndoʊˌpeɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊˌpeɪn / (BrE)
a single pane of glass set in a window frame

Examples

Broken windowpane
A crack ran across the whole windowpane after the storm.
She cleaned each windowpane to let more light into the room.
The children watched snowflakes settle softly on the frosty windowpane.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pane - a sheet of glass in a window or door
