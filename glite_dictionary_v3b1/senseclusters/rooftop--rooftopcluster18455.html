rooftop

noun
the top surface of a roof
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈruːf.tɑːp / (AmE), / ˈruːf.tɒp / (BrE)
the flat or sloping outer surface at the very top of a building

Examples

The rooftop party
The party was held on the rooftop to take advantage of the cool evening breeze.
We enjoyed the city skyline from the rooftop of our hotel.
Workers were repairing the rooftop after the heavy storm damaged it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roof - the structure forming the upper covering of a building
