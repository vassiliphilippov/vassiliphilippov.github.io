Yunnan

proper noun
province of southern China
Pronunciation: / juˈnɑn / (AmE), / juːˈnæn / (BrE)
A province in southern China known for its diverse culture, scenic landscapes, and ethnic minorities.

Examples

Yunnan province
Many ethnic groups live in Yunnan, making it a culturally rich province.
She traveled across Yunnan to photograph its famous rice terraces.
The tea from Yunnan is highly prized for its unique flavor.
