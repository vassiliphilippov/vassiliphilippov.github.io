anti-vaccine

noun
vaccine opponent
Pronunciation: / ˌænti vækˈsiːn / (AmE), / ˌænti vækˈsiːn / (BrE)
a person who opposes vaccination or refuses to be vaccinated

Examples

An anti-vaccine protester
The anti-vaccine argued against mandatory immunization policies at the public meeting.
Many health officials are concerned about the influence of anti-vaccine groups on social media.
An anti-vaccine may refuse to allow their children to receive standard childhood vaccinations.
