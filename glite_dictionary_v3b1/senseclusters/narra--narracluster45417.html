narra

noun
Southeast Asian tree
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːrə / (AmE), / ˈnærə / (BrE)
a tropical tree of Southeast Asia known for its hard, reddish wood used in furniture and construction

Examples

narra wood
The craftsman carved a beautiful table from narra for the family dining room.
In the Philippines, narra is a popular choice for making durable furniture.
The reddish hue of narra wood gives cabinets a warm and elegant appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mahogany - a tropical hardwood tree valued for its reddish-brown timber
