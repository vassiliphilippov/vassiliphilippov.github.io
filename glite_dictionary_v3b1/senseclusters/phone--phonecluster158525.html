(Phonetics) An individual speech sound considered without regard to its status as a phoneme in any language.

Examples

A speech phone
In phonetic analysis, each phone is represented by a unique symbol in the IPA.
The [p] in 'spin' and 'pin' are different phones because they are pronounced differently.
The difference between a phone and a phoneme is important in linguistics.
