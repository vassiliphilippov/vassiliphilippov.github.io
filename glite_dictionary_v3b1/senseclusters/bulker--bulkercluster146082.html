bulker

noun
bulk cargo ship
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌlkər / (AmE), / ˈbʌlkə / (BrE)
a large cargo ship designed to transport unpackaged bulk goods such as grain, coal, or ore

Examples

A cargo bulker
The massive bulker arrived at the port to load grain for export.
A bulker can carry thousands of tons of coal across the ocean.
Crews worked quickly to unload the iron ore from the bulker docked at the terminal.
