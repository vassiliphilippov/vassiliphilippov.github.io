woman of the cloth

noun
female clergy member
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊmən əv ðə klɔθ / (AmE), / ˈwʊmən əv ðə klɒθ / (BrE)
a female member of the clergy or a spiritual leader, especially in the Christian Church

Examples

A woman of the cloth preaches
As a woman of the cloth, her sermons inspired many in her community.
Growing up, she never imagined she would become a woman of the cloth.
The new pastor is the first woman of the cloth to lead our congregation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: man of the cloth - a male clergy member or spiritual leader
