lava-like

adjective
resembling molten lava
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːvəˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɑːvəˌlaɪk / (BrE)

Examples

Thick lava-like texture

The dessert had a warm, lava-like center that flowed out when sliced.
After the eruption, a lava-like substance covered the ground, cooling slowly into rock.
