naos

noun
church central area
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈneɪ.ɑːs/ /; / /naʊz/ (AmE), /ˈneɪ.ɒs/ /; / /naʊz/ (BrE)
the central area or main body of an early Christian or Byzantine church where worshippers gather, equivalent to the nave

Examples

Church naos
The ancient church's naos was filled with light streaming through stained glass windows.
Tourists admired the mosaics decorating the walls of the naos during their visit.
Only clergy were allowed beyond the naos into the sanctuary in some traditions.
