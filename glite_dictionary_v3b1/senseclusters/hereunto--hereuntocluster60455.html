hereunto

adverb
to this document
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɪrˈʌntu / (AmE), / hɪəˈʌntuː / (BrE)
to this document or matter; used in formal or legal writing to refer to something mentioned above

Examples

Attached hereunto
The parties have set their hands and seals hereunto on the date above.
All terms and conditions hereunto agreed shall remain in effect until further notice.
The undersigned affirms the statements made hereunto are true and correct.
