neuropteran

noun
insect of the order Neuroptera
7.9
Pronunciation: / nuˈrɑːptərən / (AmE), / njʊəˈrɒptərən / (BrE)
an insect of the order Neuroptera, characterized by biting mouthparts and four large membranous wings with a network of veins

Examples

A neuropteran insect
Many garden insects preyed upon by the neuropteran are harmful to plants.
Scientists discovered a rare neuropteran species in the tropical rainforest.
The neuropteran is easily identified by its delicate, net-veined wings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lacewing - a neuropteran insect with delicate, lacy wings
