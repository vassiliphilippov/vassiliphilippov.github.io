bottom

noun
lower body clothing
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑtəm / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəm / (BrE)
clothing worn on the lower part of the body, such as pants, shorts, or skirts

Examples

Gym bottoms
She bought new bottoms for her gym workouts, including leggings and shorts.
During the sale, all bottoms were half price at the clothing store.
He prefers loose bottoms when relaxing at home after work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pants - clothing covering each leg separately, worn from the waist to the ankles
  • antonym: top - clothing worn on the upper part of the body
