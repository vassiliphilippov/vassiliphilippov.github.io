tentpole

noun
main support event
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛntˌpoʊl / (AmE), / ˈtɛntˌpəʊl / (BrE)
a main event, product, or project that supports or attracts significant attention or revenue for an organization

Examples

Summer tentpole
The studio relied on its summer tentpole to boost annual profits.
That festival has become the city's cultural tentpole each year.
The company launched a new product as its tentpole for the holiday season.
