Gekkonidae

noun
family of lizards known as geckos
9.9
Pronunciation: / gɛkˈɑnɪˌdi / (AmE), / gɛkˈɒnɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of small to medium-sized lizards commonly known as geckos, found in warm climates worldwide.
