Diapensia

noun
a genus of small evergreen plants
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəˈpɛnsiə / (AmE), / ˌdaɪ.əˈpɛn.si.ə / (BrE)
A small genus of perennial evergreen plants constituting the type genus of the family Diapensiaceae, found in arctic and alpine regions.

Examples

The genus Diapensia
Botanists discovered a rare species within the genus Diapensia during their expedition.
Some gardeners cultivate Diapensia in rock gardens for its resilience and evergreen foliage.
The Diapensia is well adapted to cold, harsh environments in alpine and arctic regions.
