Percocet

noun
opioid pain medication
8.5
Pronunciation: /pɜːr.ˈkoʊ.set/ /; / /pər.ˈkoʊ.set/ (AmE), /pə.ˈkəʊ.set/ /; / /pɜː.ˈkəʊ.set/ (BrE)
