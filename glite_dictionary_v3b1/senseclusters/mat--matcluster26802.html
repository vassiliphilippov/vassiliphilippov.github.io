mat

noun
dull, non-glossy quality or finish
6.7
Pronunciation: / mæt / (AmE), / mæt / (BrE)
A state or quality of being dull and lacking gloss or shine, especially in a surface or finish.

Examples

Mat finish
The painter chose a mat finish to reduce glare on the canvas.
The walls were coated with a mat paint that gives a subtle, elegant look.
This powder creates a mat effect on your face, minimizing shine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dull - lacking brightness, vividness, or sheen
  • antonym: glossy - having a shiny or lustrous surface
