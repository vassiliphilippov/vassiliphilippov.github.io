housing

noun
protective casing for a mechanical part
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
a protective or decorative covering or case for something, such as a machine part or a horse

Examples

Engine housing
A plastic housing was fitted over the wiring to prevent accidents.
He removed the metal housing to access the gears inside the machine.
The engine's housing protects its internal components from dust and moisture.
During the parade, each steed was draped in a brightly colored housing.
Historical paintings often depict warhorses adorned in ornate housings for battle.
The knight’s horse wore an elaborate housing embroidered with his family crest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: case - a container or cover for protecting something mechanical
  • synonym: chassis - the frame or casing for machinery or electronics
