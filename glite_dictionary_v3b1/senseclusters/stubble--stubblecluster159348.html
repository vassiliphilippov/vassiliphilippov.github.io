stubble

noun
short facial hair growth
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌb.əl / (AmE), / ˈstʌb.əl / (BrE)
short, stiff hairs growing on a man's face when he has not shaved for a few days

Examples

Facial stubble grows
A few days of stubble gave him a rugged look.
He hadn't shaved for a week, so thick stubble covered his chin.
She brushed her fingers against the rough stubble on his cheek.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: beard - hair growing on the lower part of a man's face
  • antonym: clean-shaven - having no facial hair due to shaving
