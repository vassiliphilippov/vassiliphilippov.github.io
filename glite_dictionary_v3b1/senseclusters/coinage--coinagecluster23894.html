coinage

noun
coins collectively
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔɪnɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɔɪnɪdʒ / (BrE)
Coins collectively; the coins produced by a government or authority.

Examples

Ancient coinage collection
Ancient coinage can reveal much about a civilization's history.

The government introduced new coinage after gaining independence.
