distant

adjective
emotionally or socially remote
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪstənt / (AmE), / ˈdɪstənt / (BrE)
not friendly or forthcoming; remote in manner or attitude

Examples

Distant attitude
After his promotion, Robert became increasingly distant with his old colleagues.
Her distant behavior made it hard for others to get to know her.
She greeted everyone with a distant smile and spoke very little throughout the evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aloof - not friendly or forthcoming; cool and distant
  • antonym: friendly - pleasant and supportive in manner; approachable
