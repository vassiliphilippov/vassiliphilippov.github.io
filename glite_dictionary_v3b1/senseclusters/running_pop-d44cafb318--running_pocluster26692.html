running pop

noun
tropical American passion flower plant
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪŋ pɑp / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ pɒp / (BrE)
A tropical American passion flower with finely dissected bracts, whose stems emit a disagreeable odor when crushed.

Examples

The running pop flower
Gardeners appreciate the delicate bracts of the running pop, despite its malodorous odor.
In tropical America, the running pop grows wild along the forest edges.
The running pop is known for its distinctive, unpleasant-smelling stems when broken.
