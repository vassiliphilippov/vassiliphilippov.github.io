flag

noun
list of newspaper/magazine staff and publication info
Pronunciation: / flæɡ / (AmE), / flæɡ / (BrE)
a list printed in each issue of a newspaper or magazine, usually on the editorial page, giving the name of the publication and the names of the editorial staff and other key information

Examples

The newspaper flag
After the new editor was hired, his name appeared in the flag of the publication.
If you want to know who runs the magazine, check the flag in the current issue.
The flag on page two lists the editor-in-chief and contact information for the newspaper.
