seasonless

adjective
timeless in style
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːzənləs / (AmE), / ˈsiːzənləs / (BrE)
suitable for use or wear at any time of year; not limited to a particular season

Examples

Seasonless clothing
She preferred seasonless designs that could be worn year after year without feeling outdated.
A seasonless coat is a smart investment because it works in both spring and autumn.
The brand focuses on seasonless clothing that remains stylish regardless of current trends.
This seasonless dress can be worn comfortably in both summer and winter.
Designers are creating more seasonless collections to meet changing consumer needs.
A seasonless wardrobe helps you save space and money throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: seasonal - suitable for or relating to a particular season
  • synonym: timeless - not affected by the passage of time or changing trends
  • antonym: trendy - very fashionable or up to date
  • synonym: versatile - able to be used in many different ways or situations
