truncus

noun
main body part
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌŋkəs / (AmE), / ˈtrʌŋkəs / (BrE)
the main part or trunk of a structure, especially in anatomy such as the body or a blood vessel

Examples

The truncus of artery
The surgeon carefully examined the truncus of the artery before proceeding with the operation.
Damage to the truncus can affect the function of several connected branches.
In anatomy class, we learned that the truncus refers to the main part of a vessel or organ.

Related Terms

  • antonym: branch - a part that extends from the main structure
  • synonym: trunk - the main part of the body or a structure
