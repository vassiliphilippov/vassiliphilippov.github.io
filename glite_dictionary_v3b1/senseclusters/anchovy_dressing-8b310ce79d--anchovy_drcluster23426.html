anchovy dressing

noun
salad dressing with anchovies and vinaigrette
7.5 4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈænˌtʃoʊvi ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈæn.tʃəʊ.vi ˈdrɛs.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a salad dressing made by combining vinaigrette with mashed anchovies, giving it a salty, umami flavor

Examples

Anchovy dressing on salad
Many people enjoy the salty, savory taste of anchovy dressing on their greens.
She tossed the salad with a generous amount of anchovy dressing for extra flavor.
The Caesar salad was made from scratch, including a homemade anchovy dressing.
