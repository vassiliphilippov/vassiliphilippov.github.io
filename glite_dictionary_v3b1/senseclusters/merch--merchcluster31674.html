merch

noun
branded products
Pronunciation: / mɜrtʃ / (AmE), / mɜːtʃ / (BrE)
informal term for products or goods, especially those sold by a brand, band, or group to fans

Examples

Band merch table
The band released new merch for their upcoming tour, including T-shirts and hats.
She bought some merch from her favorite streamer to support his channel.
After the show, fans crowded the booth to check out the latest merch.

  • synonym: merchandise - goods that are bought and sold
