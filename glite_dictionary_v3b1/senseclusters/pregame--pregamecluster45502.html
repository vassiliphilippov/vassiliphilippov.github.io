pregame

noun
gathering before event
Pronunciation: / ˈpriˌɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈpriːɡeɪm / (BrE)
a social gathering held before a main event, often involving drinking or socializing with friends.

Examples

Pregame party
We decided to have a small pregame at my apartment before heading to the concert.
The group organized a pregame to get everyone excited for the big match.
She brought snacks and drinks to the pregame before the wedding reception.

Related Terms

  • antonym: afterparty - a party held after a main event
  • synonym: tailgate - a social event held around a vehicle before a sports game, often with food and drinks
