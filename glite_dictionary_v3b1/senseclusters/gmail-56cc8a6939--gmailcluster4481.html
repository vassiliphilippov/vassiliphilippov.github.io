Gmail

proper noun
Google email service
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːˌmeɪl / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːˌmeɪl / (BrE)
a popular web-based email service provided by Google for sending and receiving electronic messages

Examples

A Gmail account
I created a new Gmail account to keep my work and personal emails separate.
She checks her Gmail every morning for important messages from her team.
Many people use Gmail because it offers a large amount of free storage.
