cannon ball

noun
solid projectile fired from a cannon
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkænən bɔl / (AmE), / ˈkænən bɔːl / (BrE)
A solid, heavy, round projectile formerly fired from a cannon, especially before the use of explosive shells.

Examples

A cannon ball fired
During the reenactment, the cannon fired a replica cannon ball across the field.
He found a rusty cannon ball while exploring the ancient fort.
The museum displayed an old cannon ball that was found on the battlefield.

Related Terms

  • synonym: projectile - an object that is thrown or fired as a weapon
  • antonym: shell - an explosive projectile fired from artillery
