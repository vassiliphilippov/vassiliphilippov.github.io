oscillating

adjective
moving or vibrating back and forth
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.səˌleɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɒs.ɪ.leɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
moving back and forth rhythmically or having periodic vibrations.

Examples

The oscillating needle
He watched the oscillating needle of the old-fashioned meter for any signs of electrical current.
Scientists measured the oscillating frequency of the suspended pendulum during the experiment.
The oscillating fan cooled the entire room evenly as it moved from side to side.

Related Terms

  • antonym: steady - not changing or moving; unchanging
  • synonym: vibrating - moving quickly back and forth
