allergy diet

noun
diet avoiding allergens
Pronunciation: / ˈælərdʒi ˌdaɪət / (AmE), / ˈalədʒi ˌdʌɪət / (BrE)
a diet that excludes foods to which a person is allergic

Examples

An allergy diet plan
Her doctor recommended an allergy diet to help identify which foods triggered her symptoms.
Many parents seek guidance before putting their child on an allergy diet.
Planning an allergy diet can be challenging if you have multiple food allergies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elimination diet - a diet removing specific foods to identify allergies or sensitivities
