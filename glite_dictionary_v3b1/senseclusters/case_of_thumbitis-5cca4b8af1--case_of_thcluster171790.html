case of thumbitis

noun
pain in thumbs from texting
9.9
Pronunciation: / keɪs əv θʌmˈaɪtɪs / (AmE), / keɪs əv θʌmˈaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A condition involving pain or discomfort in the thumbs resulting from excessive texting or use of handheld devices.

Examples

A case of thumbitis

Sarah visited the doctor when her case of thumbitis became too painful to ignore.
