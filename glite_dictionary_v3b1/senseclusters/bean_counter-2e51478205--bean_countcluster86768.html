bean counter

noun
person overly concerned with financial details
7.7 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbin ˌkaʊnər /; / ˈbin ˌkaʊntɚ / (AmE), / ˈbiːn ˌkaʊntə / (BrE)
an accountant or official who pays too much attention to financial details, often ignoring the bigger picture (informal, sometimes negative)

Examples
