viscosimeter

noun
instrument for measuring viscosity
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪskoʊˈsɪmətər / (AmE), / ˌvɪskəʊˈsɪmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument used for measuring the viscosity of a liquid

Examples

A viscosimeter device
After calibration, the viscosimeter produced accurate viscosity measurements for the experiment.
Quality control in the lab involved testing several samples with a viscosimeter.
The chemist used a viscosimeter to determine the fluid's resistance to flow.
