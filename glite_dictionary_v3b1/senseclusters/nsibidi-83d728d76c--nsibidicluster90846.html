Nsibidi

noun
Nigerian symbol script
9.9
Pronunciation: / nsiˈbidi / (AmE), / nsiˈbidi / (BrE)
an ancient system of symbols used for writing and communication by several ethnic groups in southeastern Nigeria

Examples

The Nsibidi script
The ancient Nsibidi script was used to record important messages and stories.
Researchers are studying Nsibidi symbols to learn more about early Nigerian cultures.
Some traditional ceremonies still include the use of Nsibidi signs for communication.

  • synonym: hieroglyph - a symbol used in ancient writing systems
