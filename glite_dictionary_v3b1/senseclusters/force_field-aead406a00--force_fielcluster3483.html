force field

noun
protective energy barrier
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrs fiːld / (AmE), / ˈfɔːs fiːld / (BrE)
an invisible barrier of energy that protects an area or object from harm, often in science fiction

Examples

Protective force field
The spaceship was protected by a powerful force field during the attack.
In the movie, the villain could not break through the hero's force field.
Scientists in the story created a force field to keep the laboratory safe from intruders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrier - something that blocks or separates
  • synonym: shield - a protective barrier against danger or attack
