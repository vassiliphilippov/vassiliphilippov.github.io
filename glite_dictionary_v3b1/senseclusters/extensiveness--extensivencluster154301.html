extensiveness

noun
great scope or comprehensiveness
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛnsɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnsɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being very large in scope, range, or degree; great breadth or comprehensiveness

Examples

The extensiveness of research
Scientists were impressed by the extensiveness of the research conducted for the project.
She was overwhelmed by the extensiveness of the museum's art collection.
The extensiveness of the new law affected millions of people nationwide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breadth - the extent or scope of something
  • antonym: narrowness - the quality of having little width or extent
  • synonym: scope - the range covered by something
