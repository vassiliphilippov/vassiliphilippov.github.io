Sotho language

noun
a group of southern Bantu languages of the Sotho people
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊθoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊθuː ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of a group of closely related southern Bantu languages spoken in Botswana, South Africa, and Lesotho, chiefly by the Sotho people

Examples

Sotho language speakers
He wrote his thesis on the structure of the Sotho language.
She is fluent in both English and Sotho language.
The Sotho language is widely spoken in Lesotho and parts of South Africa.
