Hubble

proper noun
space telescope
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌbəl / (AmE), / ˈhʌbəl / (BrE)
a large space telescope orbiting Earth, used to observe stars, galaxies, and other celestial objects

Examples

Hubble space telescope
The Hubble has provided scientists with detailed images of distant galaxies.
Astronomers use the Hubble to study the formation of stars and planets.
Many discoveries about the universe have been made thanks to the Hubble.

Related Terms

  • synonym: James Webb Space Telescope - a newer space telescope designed to observe the universe in infrared
  • synonym: telescope - an instrument used to observe distant objects by collecting electromagnetic radiation
