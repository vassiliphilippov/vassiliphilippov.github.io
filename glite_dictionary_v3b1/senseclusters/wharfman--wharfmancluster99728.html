wharfman

noun
dock manager
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈwɑːrf.mən/ /; / /ˈwɔːrf.mən/ (AmE), /ˈwɔːf.mən/ (BrE)
a person who owns, manages, or is responsible for the operation and fees of a wharf or dock

Examples

Wharfman manages the dock
The wharfman greeted the arriving ships and directed them to their berths.
After a long day, the wharfman closed the gates to the dock for the night.
Merchants paid the wharfman to store their goods safely by the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dockmaster - a person in charge of a dock or harbor
  • synonym: stevedore - a person employed at a dock to load and unload ships
