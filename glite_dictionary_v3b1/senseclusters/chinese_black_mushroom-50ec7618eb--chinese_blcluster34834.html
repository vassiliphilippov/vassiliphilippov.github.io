Chinese black mushroom

noun
edible Asian mushroom with dark cap
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃaɪˈniz blæk ˈmʌʃˌrum / (AmE), / ˌtʃaɪˈniːz blæk ˈmʌʃˌrʊm / (BrE)
an edible East Asian mushroom with a golden to blackish cap, commonly used in cooking, and an inedible stipe

Examples

Fresh Chinese black mushrooms
Fresh Chinese black mushrooms can often be found in Asian grocery stores.
She rehydrated the Chinese black mushrooms before adding them to the stir-fry.
The recipe calls for dried Chinese black mushrooms, which add a rich, earthy flavor to the dish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shiitake - the Japanese name for the same mushroom species
