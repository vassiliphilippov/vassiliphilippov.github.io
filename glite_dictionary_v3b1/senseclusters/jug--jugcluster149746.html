jug

noun
large container for liquids, narrow mouth
Pronunciation: / dʒʌɡ / (AmE), / dʒʌɡ / (BrE)
a large container with a narrow mouth, typically used for holding and pouring liquids

Examples

A jug of water
He filled the plastic jug with water before heading out on the hike.
She poured fresh lemonade from the jug into our glasses.
The old ceramic jug sat on the kitchen shelf as decoration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pitcher - a container for holding and pouring liquids, typically with a handle and spout
