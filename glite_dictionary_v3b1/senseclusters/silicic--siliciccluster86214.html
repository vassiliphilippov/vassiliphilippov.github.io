silicic

adjective
containing silica
8.1
Pronunciation: / sɪˈlɪsɪk / (AmE), / sɪˈlɪsɪk / (BrE)
containing, relating to, or derived from silica or silicon, especially in rocks or minerals

Examples

Silicic rocks
The geologist studied the silicic rocks found in the volcanic region.
Volcanic eruptions often produce silicic magma, which is rich in silica.
The laboratory analyzed the silicic composition of the mineral samples.

Related Terms

  • synonym: siliceous - containing or resembling silica
