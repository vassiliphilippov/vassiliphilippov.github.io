caprylic acid

noun
a fatty acid found in butter and oils
8.3 7.5
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈprɪl.ɪk ˈæs.əd/ /; / /kə.ˈprɪl.ɪk ˈæs.ɪd/ (AmE), /kə.ˈprɪl.ɪk ˈæs.ɪd/ (BrE)
a fatty acid with a rancid taste, found in butter, coconut oil, and other fats and oils, used in the manufacture of esters, perfumes, and as a food additive
