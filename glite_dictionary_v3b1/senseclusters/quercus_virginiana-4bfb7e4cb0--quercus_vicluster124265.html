Quercus virginiana

proper noun
species of oak tree (live oak) native to southeastern US
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkwərkəs ˌvɜrdʒɪˈneɪnə / (AmE), / ˈkwɜːkəs ˌvɜːdʒɪˈneɪnɪə / (BrE)
A medium-sized evergreen oak tree native to the southeastern United States and eastern Mexico, commonly known as the live oak, valued for its broad, spreading crown and very hard, durable wood.
