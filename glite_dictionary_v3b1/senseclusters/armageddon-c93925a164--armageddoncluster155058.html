Armageddon

noun
catastrophically destructive battle
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrməˈɡɛd(ə)n / (AmE), / ˌɑːməˈɡɛd(ə)n / (BrE)
a conflict or battle of catastrophic destruction, especially one thought to end or decide something significant

Examples

Nuclear Armageddon
Some fear that the next global conflict could escalate into an Armageddon.
The politicians warned that a nuclear war would be an Armageddon for humanity.
World War I was often referred to as an Armageddon by those who lived through it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apocalypse - the complete final destruction of the world
  • antonym: peace - freedom from war or violence
