buck

noun
framework for holding wood while sawing
Pronunciation: / bʌk / (AmE), / bʌk / (BrE)
a sturdy frame or stand used to hold wood in place while it is being sawed

Examples

A wood buck
He built a simple buck out of scrap lumber to help with his firewood project.
Make sure the buck is steady to avoid accidents while sawing the wood.
The carpenter placed the log on the buck before cutting it into smaller pieces.

Related Terms
