Hausa

noun
a member of a West African ethnic group
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsə /; / ˈhaʊ.sə / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsə /; / ˈhaʊ.sə / (BrE)
a member of the Hausa ethnic group in West Africa, especially in northern Nigeria and nearby regions
