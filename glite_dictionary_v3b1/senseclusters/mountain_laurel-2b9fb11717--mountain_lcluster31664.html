mountain laurel

noun
Pacific coast tree or shrub with aromatic foliage and tough wood
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən ˈlɔrəl / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈlɔːrəl / (BrE)
A tree or shrub of the Pacific coast with aromatic leaves and small cluster-forming flowers, followed by olive-like fruit; its wood is hard and tough.

Examples

Pacific mountain laurel
On our hike, we spotted a mountain laurel in full bloom near the stream.
The aromatic leaves of the mountain laurel released a pleasant scent when crushed.
The wood of the mountain laurel is prized for making tool handles and furniture.
