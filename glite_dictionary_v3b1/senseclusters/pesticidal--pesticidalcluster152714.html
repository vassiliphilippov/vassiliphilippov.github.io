pesticidal

adjective
kills pests
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛstəˈsaɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌpɛstɪˈsaɪdəl / (BrE)
relating to or having the ability to kill or control pests

Examples

Pesticidal solution
The farmer used a pesticidal solution to protect his crops from harmful insects.
Researchers are developing more environmentally friendly pesticidal products.
Wearing gloves is important when handling pesticidal chemicals in the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herbicidal - having the ability to kill unwanted plants or weeds
  • synonym: insecticidal - having the ability to kill insects
