subtribe

noun
taxonomic rank below tribe
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌtraɪb / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌtraɪb / (BrE)
a taxonomic group in biology that ranks below a tribe and above a genus

Examples

Plant subtribe
The botanist classified the plant into a subtribe based on its unique characteristics.
Within the animal kingdom, a subtribe helps organize species more precisely than a tribe.
Researchers discovered a new subtribe of insects in the tropical rainforest.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: genus - a taxonomic group ranking below a subtribe and above a species
  • degree higher: tribe - a taxonomic group ranking above a subtribe and below a family
