bandy-legged

adjective
having legs curving outward at the knees
8.0 8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbændi ˌlɛɡd / (AmE), / ˈbændi ˌlɛɡd / (BrE)
Having legs that curve outward at the knees, resulting in a bow-legged appearance.

Examples

Bandy-legged person
Despite being bandy-legged, he was an agile and swift runner on the field.
She was teased as a child for being slightly bandy-legged, but it never bothered her.
The old man was bandy-legged from years of hard physical labor.
