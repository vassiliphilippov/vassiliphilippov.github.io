colon cancer

noun
cancer of the colon
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊlən ˌkænsər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlən ˌkænsə / (BrE)
A malignant tumor that develops in the colon, often presenting early symptoms such as bloody stools.

Examples

Early colon cancer symptoms
A family history of colon cancer can increase your risk of developing the disease.
Doctors recommend regular screenings to detect colon cancer at an early stage.
Researchers are working on new treatments for advanced colon cancer cases.
