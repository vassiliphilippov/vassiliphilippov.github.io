differentiator

noun
something or someone that distinguishes
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃiˌeɪtər / (AmE), / ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃieɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that distinguishes or differentiates between things or people

Examples

The main differentiator
Attention to detail was the main differentiator between the two job applicants.
Product quality is a key differentiator in a competitive market.
The teacher is a true differentiator, helping each student reach their full potential.

Related Terms

  • antonym: commonality - a shared feature or attribute
  • synonym: distinction - a difference or contrast between similar things or people
