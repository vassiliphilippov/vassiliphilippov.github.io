Boran

proper noun
East African ethnic group
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɔːˈrɑːn / (AmE), / bɔːˈrɑːn / (BrE)
a member of an ethnic group in East Africa, especially in Kenya and Ethiopia, or a breed of cattle from that region

Examples

Boran people in Kenya
The Boran have a rich tradition of cattle herding in northern Kenya.
Researchers studied the social structure of the Boran community in Ethiopia.
The Boran breed is valued for its resilience in arid climates.
