phantasmata

noun
ghostly apparitions
9.9
Pronunciation: / fænˈtæzmətə / (AmE), / fænˈtæzmətə / (BrE)
archaic plural for ghostly apparitions or imagined figures, especially those seen in dreams or hallucinations
