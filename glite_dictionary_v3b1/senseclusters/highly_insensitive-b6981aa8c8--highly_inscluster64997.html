highly insensitive

adjective
extremely lacking in sensitivity
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪli ɪnˈsɛnsətɪv / (AmE), / ˈhaɪli ɪnˈsɛnsətɪv / (BrE)
Extremely lacking in sensitivity or consideration for others' feelings or circumstances.

Examples

Highly insensitive comment

It would be highly insensitive to joke about such a serious topic.
Posting that meme was highly insensitive given the current situation.
