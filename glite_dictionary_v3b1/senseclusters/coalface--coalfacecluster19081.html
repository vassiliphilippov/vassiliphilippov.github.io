coalface

noun
surface of a coal seam being mined
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊlˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlˌfeɪs / (BrE)
the exposed surface of a coal seam where mining is actively taking place.

Examples

At the coalface mine
Dust levels at the coalface can pose serious health risks to workers.
Safety measures at the coalface have greatly improved in recent years.
The miners spent hours working at the coalface deep underground.
