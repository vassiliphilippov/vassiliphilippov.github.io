turn off

verb
deactivate a device/system
Pronunciation: / tɝn ɔf /; / tɜrn ɒf / (AmE), / tɜːn ɒf / (BrE)
to stop a device or machine from working by using a switch or control

Examples

To turn off the lights
He forgot to turn off the oven after baking the cake.
Please turn off the television before you leave the room.
She quickly turned off the alarm when it started ringing.
Don't forget to turn off your computer before going home.
Please turn off the lights when you leave the room to save energy.
She quickly turned off the alarm clock and went back to sleep.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shut off - to stop something from operating, especially by turning a switch
  • synonym: switch off - to stop a machine or light from working by turning a switch
  • antonym: turn on - to make a device or system start working
