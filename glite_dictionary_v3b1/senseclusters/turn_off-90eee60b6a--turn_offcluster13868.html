Synonyms

  • switch off
    1.1 3.13/m
    switch off is to use a switch to stop a machine or light quickly, while turn off is the general term for stopping devices.
  • turn out
    2.7 1.20/m
    turn out is used to switch off lights or machines by flipping a switch, while turn off is the neutral phrase for stopping devices.
  • shut off
    2.6 3.79/m
    shut off stops a flow or machine, often by a valve or control, while turn off focuses on using a switch.
  • shut down
    2.9 18/m
    shut down stops a machine or whole system, sometimes permanently or in business, while turn off suits simple switches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shut off - to stop something from operating, especially by turning a switch
  • synonym: switch off - to stop a machine or light from working by turning a switch
  • antonym: turn on - to make a device or system start working
