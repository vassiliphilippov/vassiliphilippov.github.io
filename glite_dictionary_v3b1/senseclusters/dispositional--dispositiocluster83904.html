dispositional

adjective
relating to disposal
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪspəˈzɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌdɪspəˈzɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the disposal, transfer, or final settlement of property, assets, or a legal case

Examples

Dispositional order
The judge issued a dispositional order to finalize the transfer of the property.
All dispositional documents must be signed before the assets can be distributed.
The lawyer explained the dispositional process to the family after the will was read.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disposal - the act of getting rid of or transferring something
  • antonym: retentive - having the ability to retain or keep something
