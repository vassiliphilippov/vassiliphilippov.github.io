sizer

noun
device for sorting sizes
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪzə / (BrE)
a mechanical device that sorts or grades items according to their size for processing or packaging

Examples

Apple sizer sorter
The factory uses a sizer to separate apples into different categories before packaging.
After harvesting, the carrots are passed through a sizer to ensure uniformity for sale.
A new automatic sizer increased the efficiency of the potato processing line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grader - a device that classifies items by quality or size
  • synonym: sorter - a machine that separates items into groups
