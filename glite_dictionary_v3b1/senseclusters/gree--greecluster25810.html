gree

noun
prize or victory
Pronunciation: / ɡriː / (AmE), / ɡriː / (BrE)
an archaic term for prize, victory, or superiority in a contest or competition

Examples

Win the gree
In the old ballad, the knight won the gree at the tournament.
The warriors fought bravely, each hoping to claim the gree for his clan.
To take the gree was considered the highest honor in medieval contests.
