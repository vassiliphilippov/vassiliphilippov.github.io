untrustworthiness

noun
lack of trustworthiness
5.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtrʌstwɚðinɪs / (AmE), / ʌnˈtrʌstwəːðinɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of not being reliable or deserving of trust or confidence

Examples

His untrustworthiness showed
He lost many friends as a result of his untrustworthiness in both personal and business matters.
Her untrustworthiness became evident when she repeatedly broke her promises.
The company suffered due to the untrustworthiness of its former financial director.

Related Terms

  • antonym: trustworthiness - the quality of being reliable and deserving of trust
