time frame

noun
period when something occurs
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪm ˌfreɪm / (AmE), / ˈtaɪm ˌfreɪm / (BrE)
a period during which something happens or is expected to happen

Examples

A time frame for repairs
Can you give me a specific time frame for when the repairs will be finished?
The delivery time frame depends on your location and the shipping method you choose.
We need to complete the project within the agreed time frame.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deadline - a time by which something must be finished
  • synonym: duration - length of time something lasts
