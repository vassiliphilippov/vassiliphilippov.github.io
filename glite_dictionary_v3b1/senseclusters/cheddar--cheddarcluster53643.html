cheddar

noun
a type of hard cheese
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛdər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛdə / (BrE)
a hard, smooth-textured cheese, originally made in the village of Cheddar in southwestern England, now produced worldwide
