black-capped chickadee

noun
North American songbird with a black cap
Pronunciation: / blæk kæpt ˈtʃɪkədi / (AmE), / blak kæpt ˈtʃɪkədi / (BrE)
a small North American songbird with a black cap and bib, white cheeks, and grayish body, known for its distinctive call

Examples

A black-capped chickadee bird
A black-capped chickadee visited our bird feeder every morning last winter.
The black-capped chickadee is well known for its cheerful song all year round.
We spotted a black-capped chickadee flitting among the branches in the snowy forest.
