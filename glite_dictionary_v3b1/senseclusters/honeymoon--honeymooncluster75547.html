honeymoon

noun
initial harmonious period of a relationship
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌniˌmun / (AmE), / ˈhʌn.i.muːn / (BrE)
a period at the beginning of a relationship, business, or political arrangement when relations are especially good and harmonious

Examples

Honeymoon period
At the start of the project, everyone was optimistic during the honeymoon period.
The new CEO is enjoying a honeymoon phase, but tough decisions will come soon.
Their political alliance experienced a brief honeymoon before disagreements emerged.

  • antonym: trial - a period of difficulty or testing
