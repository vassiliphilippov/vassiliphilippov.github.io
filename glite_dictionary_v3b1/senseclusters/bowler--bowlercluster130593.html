bowler

noun
a person who bowls in bowling
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlər / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlə / (BrE)
a person who plays the game of bowling by rolling balls down an alley at pins

Examples

Skilled bowler at bowling
Many bowlers wear special shoes for better grip on the lanes.
She is an experienced bowler and competes in tournaments every weekend.
The team needed a new bowler after their star player was injured.
