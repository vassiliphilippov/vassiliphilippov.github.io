material

noun
things needed for making or doing something
1.0 1.9
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈtɪ.r.iː.əl/ /; / /mə.ˈtɪ.r.ɪ.əl/ (AmE), /mə.ˈtɪə.rɪ.əl/ (BrE)
a substance, fabric, or set of properties used to make or construct things, or to define the appearance of objects (including in computer graphics)

Examples

Material for clothes
All training materials are available for download on the website.

This jacket is made from a waterproof material that protects against the rain.

  • synonym: substance - a particular kind of matter with uniform properties
