to send or pass something from one person, place, or thing to another (such as information, signals, or diseases)

Examples

To transmit a disease
Doctors are working to ensure the disease does not transmit from person to person.
Please transmit the message to the rest of your team as soon as possible.
The radio tower can transmit signals over great distances.
Some diseases are transmitted by contaminated water.
The virus can transmit from person to person through coughing and sneezing.
They took measures to prevent mosquitoes from transmitting malaria in the area.

Synonyms

  • communicate
    3.4 0.08/m
    communicate often means sharing information or feelings directly between people, while transmit focuses on sending through a channel or medium.
  • convey
    3.4 23/m
    convey is used to express or pass on ideas or feelings clearly, while transmit stresses sending signals, data, or disease between places.
  • relay
    3.5 4.99/m
    relay means receive then pass on messages or signals along a chain, while transmit simply means send from one place to another.
  • send
    1.0 238/m
    send causes an object, letter, or message to go to someone else, while transmit emphasizes passing information or signals between sources.
  • send out
    1.8 14/m
    send out causes messages or goods to be sent from an office or system in quantity, while transmit means to pass between points.
  • pass on
    2.7 6.49/m
    pass on means give or tell something to another person directly, while transmit focuses on sending through a medium or channel.
  • pass along
    2.5 3.06/m
    pass along means pass information or objects from one person to the next, while transmit highlights sending across spaces or systems.
  • pass
    1.0 30/m
    pass means hand or move something to someone nearby, while transmit implies sending across a distance or through a medium.
  • carry
    2.0 19/m
    carry can mean spread disease or signals through a medium or person, while transmit is the act of sending between sources.
  • propagate
    7.0 1.12/m
    propagate describes waves or signals moving through a medium or area, while transmit is the general act of sending or passing.
