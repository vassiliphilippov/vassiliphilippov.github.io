privatistic

adjective
focused on private interests
Pronunciation: / ˌpraɪvəˈtɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌpraɪvəˈtɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or emphasizing private interests, life, or ownership rather than public or collective matters

Examples
