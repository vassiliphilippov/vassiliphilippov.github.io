toast

noun
someone doomed or in deep trouble (informal)
Pronunciation: / toʊst / (AmE), / təʊst / (BrE)
a person who is certain to fail or be ruined; someone doomed or in very serious trouble (informal)

Examples

You're toast now
If the boss finds out about this mistake, you're toast.
If we lose this last customer, we're toast.
Without my notes for the exam, I'm definitely toast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goner - a person who is certain to fail or die
