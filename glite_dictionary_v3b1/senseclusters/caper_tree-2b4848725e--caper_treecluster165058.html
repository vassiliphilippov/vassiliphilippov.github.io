caper tree

noun
a shrub or small tree with edible buds and fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪ.pɚ tri /; / ˈkeɪpər tri / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.pə tɹiː /; / ˈkeɪpə triː / (BrE)
