validate

verb
check data or input meets required rules (IT)
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvælɪdeɪt / (AmE), / ˈvælɪdeɪt / (BrE)
(in information technology) to check that data, input, or credentials meet required formats, constraints, or rules before they are accepted or processed.

Examples

To validate data
The signup form will validate your password strength before you can create an account.
Before processing payments, the system validated every card number and expiration date automatically.
Developers added a function for validating user input to prevent incorrect or dangerous data submissions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authenticate - to confirm that something (especially a credential or identity) is genuine
  • synonym: verify - to check or test whether something is true, accurate, or working correctly
