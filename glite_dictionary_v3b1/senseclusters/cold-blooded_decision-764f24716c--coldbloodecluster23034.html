cold-blooded decision

noun
ruthless, emotionless decision
1.0
Pronunciation: / koʊld ˈblʌdɪd dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / kəʊld ˈblʌdɪd dɪˈsɪʒən / (BrE)
a decision made without emotion, remorse, or pity; often implying ruthlessness

Examples

A cold-blooded decision
It was a cold-blooded decision to cancel the program without warning.
She faced criticism for her cold-blooded decision to end the partnership so abruptly.
The CEO's cold-blooded decision to lay off half the staff shocked everyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compassionate decision - a decision made with care and empathy for others
  • synonym: ruthless decision - a decision made without compassion or pity
