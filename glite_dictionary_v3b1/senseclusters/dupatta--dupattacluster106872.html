dupatta

noun
South Asian scarf
Pronunciation: / duːˈpʌtə / (AmE), / duːˈpʌtə / (BrE)
a long, lightweight scarf worn by women, especially in South Asia, over the shoulders or head as part of traditional dress

Examples

A dupatta scarf
She chose a colorful dupatta to match her traditional outfit for the festival.
The wind caught her dupatta as she walked through the busy market.
Many women wear a dupatta draped over their shoulders for both style and modesty.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scarf - a long piece of cloth worn around the neck or head
  • synonym: shawl - a piece of fabric worn over the shoulders or head
