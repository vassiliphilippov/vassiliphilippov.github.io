uplifter

noun
mood booster
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈlɪftər / (AmE), / ʌpˈlɪftə / (BrE)
a person or thing that raises spirits or improves mood

Examples

A real uplifter
Her positive attitude was a real uplifter during tough times.
Listening to that song is always an uplifter when I feel down.
He became the team's uplifter with his constant encouragement and energy.
Her cheerful attitude was a real uplifter for the whole team.
Listening to that song is always an uplifter when I am feeling down.
He became known as an uplifter in the community for his positive outlook.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downer - something that makes people feel sad or less happy
  • synonym: encourager - someone who gives support or confidence
  • synonym: inspirer - someone or something that inspires others
