stilbesterol

noun
synthetic nonsteroidal estrogen hormone
9.9
Pronunciation: /stɪl.ˈbest.rəl/ (AmE), /stɪl.ˈbest.rɒl/ (BrE)
A synthetic, potent nonsteroidal estrogen that was used medically (for conditions such as menstrual disorders and to prevent miscarriages) and was also formerly added to livestock and poultry feed; later found to cause cancer in daughters of treated women.
