wild morning-glory

noun
a wild climbing plant with pink flowers
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˌɡlɔri / (AmE), / waɪld ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˌɡlɒri / (BrE)
a climbing plant with pink or white funnel-shaped flowers, often considered a weed

Examples

Wild morning-glory flowers
Wild morning-glory can quickly cover fences and other plants in the garden.
The pink flowers of the wild morning-glory brighten up the roadside in early summer.
We pulled up several wild morning-glories that were choking the vegetable patch.
Gardeners often struggle to control wild morning-glory because it spreads so rapidly.
I spotted delicate pink blooms of wild morning-glory along the roadside.
The wild morning-glory quickly covered the fence with its twisting vines.
