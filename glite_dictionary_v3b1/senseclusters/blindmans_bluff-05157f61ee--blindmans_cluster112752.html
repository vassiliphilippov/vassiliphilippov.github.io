blindman's bluff

noun
children's game with a blindfolded chaser
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈblaɪndmænz ˌblʌf / (AmE), / ˈblaɪndmənz ˌblʌf / (BrE)
A children's game in which one player is blindfolded and tries to catch and identify the other players.

Examples

Play blindman's bluff
During the party, we decided to play a quick game of blindman's bluff.
He caught his sister while playing blindman's bluff, but couldn't guess who she was.
The children played blindman's bluff in the garden, laughing as they dodged the seeker.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blindfold - a piece of cloth used to cover someone's eyes
