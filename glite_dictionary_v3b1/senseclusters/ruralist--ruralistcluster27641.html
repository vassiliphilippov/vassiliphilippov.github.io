ruralist

noun
advocate of rural living
Pronunciation: / ˈrʊrəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈrʊərəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports, prefers, or lives in rural areas and values rural ways of life

Examples

A ruralist supporter
As a committed ruralist, she champions sustainable farming and local community projects.
Many ruralists believe that city expansion threatens natural habitats and countryside traditions.
The magazine featured several prominent ruralists discussing the benefits of living outside urban areas.
As a proud ruralist, he cherished the peace and community spirit of village life.
Many ruralists advocate for preserving open spaces and agricultural practices.
The author's ruralist perspective is evident in her writings about farm traditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: urbanist - a person who supports city life or urban development
  • antonym: urbanite - a person who lives in a city or prefers urban life
