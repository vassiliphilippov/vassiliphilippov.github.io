diag

noun
informal diagnostic test
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪæɡ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪæɡ / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for a diagnostic test, tool, or procedure used to identify faults in a machine, system, or device

Examples

Run a diag test
The engineer ran a quick diag to check for hardware issues before starting repairs.
If your phone is acting up, try running a diag to see what is wrong.
The mechanic used a diag to pinpoint the problem with the car's engine.

  • synonym: test - an examination to check the condition or performance of something
