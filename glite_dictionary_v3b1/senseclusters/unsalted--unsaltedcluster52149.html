unsalted

adjective
not containing added salt
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɔltɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɔːltɪd / (BrE)
(of food) not having had salt added; without salt or seasoning.

Examples

Unsalted butter
I prefer to buy unsalted butter for baking purposes.
She served a bowl of unsalted almonds as a healthy snack.
The recipe calls for unsalted chicken broth to better control the seasoning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: salted - having had salt added
