public square

noun
virtual forum for public discussion
Pronunciation: / c8p8cbl6ak skw5br / (AmE), / c8p8cbl6ak skwe59 / (BrE)
(figurative) an online or virtual space where people gather to discuss, debate, or share information about public issues; a digital forum for public discourse.

Examples

Online public square
During the election many debates moved into the public square of social media platforms.
Lawmakers argued the internet should remain a free public square for political discussion.
Local activists used the platform as a public square to organize a global protest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: public forum - a place or platform for public discussion or debate
  • synonym: town square - a central public space used for gatherings and events; a near metaphorical equivalent
