noneconomic

adjective
not financial
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɛkəˈnɑmɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌiːkəˈnɒmɪk / (BrE)
not related to money, trade, or financial matters; not producing or resulting in economic benefit or profit
