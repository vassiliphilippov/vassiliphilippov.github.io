bake

verb
become very hot in sunlight or heat
4.7
Pronunciation: / beɪk / (AmE), / beɪk / (BrE)
to feel very hot or to become overheated because of strong heat or sunlight

Examples

To bake in sun
After an hour on the beach, we were all baking in the midday sun.
If you don't find some shade soon, you'll be baking out here.
The city baked under a relentless heatwave for days.
After hours without shade, we were baked by the relentless heat.
His skin was baking as he worked in the midday sun.
The desert sun can bake the earth until cracks appear.

  • antonym: cool - to become or make less hot
