fireman

noun
baseball relief pitcher
2.1 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈfaɪr.mən/ (AmE), /ˈfaɪ.ə.mən/ (BrE)
(Baseball) A relief pitcher, especially one called into a game in a difficult situation to protect a lead.

Examples

Baseball fireman
Many teams rely on a trusted fireman to rescue them from late-inning trouble.
The team's veteran fireman was called in during the ninth inning to close out the game.
When the bases were loaded, the coach signaled for his best fireman to take the mound.
