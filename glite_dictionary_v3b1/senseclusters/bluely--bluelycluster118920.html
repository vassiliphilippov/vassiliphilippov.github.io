bluely

adverb
in a blue manner
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːli / (AmE), / ˈbluːli / (BrE)
in a way that is blue in color or has a blue appearance

Examples

Bluely shining water
The lake reflected the morning sky bluely across its calm surface.
Her eyes sparkled bluely in the sunlight, catching everyone's attention.
The mountains appeared bluely in the distance as the sun began to set.

Related Terms

  • synonym: azure - bright blue in color
  • antonym: redly - in a way that is red in color
