modularity

noun
network community measure
7.6
Pronunciation: /mɑːd.jə.ˈlær.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /mɑːd.jʊ.ˈlær.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /mɒd.jʊ.ˈlær.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /mɒd.jə.ˈlær.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
a measure in network analysis that shows how strongly a network is divided into separate communities or modules

Examples

Modularity in networks
The scientists used modularity to detect hidden communities within the complex network.
High modularity indicates that the network is clearly divided into separate groups.
By analyzing modularity, researchers can better understand the structure of social or biological networks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cohesion - the degree to which elements of a network are closely connected
  • synonym: community structure - the organization of a network into groups with dense internal connections
