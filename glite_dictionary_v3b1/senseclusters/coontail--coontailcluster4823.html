coontail

noun
raccoon tail decoration
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːnˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈkuːnˌteɪl / (BrE)
the tail of a raccoon, especially a strip of raccoon fur used as a decoration or part of clothing

Examples

Raccoon coontail fur
He attached a coontail to his backpack as a rustic decoration from the hunting trip.
The old hat was decorated with a coontail hanging from the brim.
Collectors sometimes seek out vintage jackets with an original coontail sewn onto them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raccoon tail - the tail of a raccoon, often used as a decorative item
