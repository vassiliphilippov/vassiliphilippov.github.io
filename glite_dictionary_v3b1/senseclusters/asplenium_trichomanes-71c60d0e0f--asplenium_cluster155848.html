Asplenium trichomanes

noun
a small rock-inhabiting fern
Pronunciation: / æsˈpliːniəm ˌtrɪkəˈmeɪniːz / (AmE), / æsˈpliːniəm ˌtrɪkəˈmeɪniːz / (BrE)
A small fern of the northern temperate zone and Hawaii, often found on rocks, with pinnate fronds; commonly known as maidenhair spleenwort.
