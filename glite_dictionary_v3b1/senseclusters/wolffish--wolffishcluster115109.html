wolffish

noun
large elongate oceanic fish with sharp teeth
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊlˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈwʊlfɪʃ / (BrE)
a large northern oceanic fish with strong teeth, found in deep or cold waters
