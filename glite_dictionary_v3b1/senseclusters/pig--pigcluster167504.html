pig

noun
greedy or selfish person
Pronunciation: / pɪɡ / (AmE), / pɪɡ / (BrE)
an insulting term for a person who is greedy, selfish, rude, or behaves badly

Examples

He is a pig at dinner
Don't be such a pig; leave some cake for the others.
He acted like a pig at the buffet, piling his plate sky-high.
She called him a pig for taking more than his share.
Don't be such a pig—clean up after yourself.
He acted like a real pig during dinner, talking loudly and making a mess.
She called her brother a pig when he refused to share his snacks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentleman - a polite or honorable man
  • synonym: glutton - person who eats or wants too much; greedy person
  • synonym: lout - an uncouth or aggressive man or boy
