chain

noun
series of connected things or events
4.7
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪn / (AmE), / tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a series of connected things or events, each depending on or linked to the next, forming a sequence

Examples

Chain of events
Breaking one link can interrupt the entire chain of communication.
The chain of events led to a surprising outcome.
There is a chain of hotels along the coast, each affiliated with the next.
A long chain of cars lined the road.
She wore a chain of tiny silver hearts around her neck.
They made a chain of paper clips for their art project.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: collection - a group of objects or people gathered together
  • synonym: sequence - a set of related things that follow each other in a particular order
  • synonym: series - a number of things or events of a similar kind coming one after another
