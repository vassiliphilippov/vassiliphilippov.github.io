meaningly

adverb
with intention
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːnɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈmiːnɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows intention, significance, or conveys a particular meaning

Examples

Smiled meaningly at friend
She smiled meaningly at her friend, hinting at a secret they shared.
He nodded meaningly to show he understood the hidden message.
The teacher glanced meaningly at the clock, signaling that time was almost up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pointedly - in a way that is clearly directed or intended
  • synonym: significantly - in a way that has a particular meaning or importance
