venturesome

adjective
willing to take risks; adventurous
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛntʃərsəm / (AmE), / ˈvɛntʃəs(ə)m / (BrE)
willing to take risks or embark on difficult or unusual undertakings; adventurous or daring.

Examples

A venturesome person
Her venturesome approach to business led to several innovative but risky projects.
The venturesome mountaineer attempted a new route up the cliff face despite the dangers.
They admired his venturesome spirit in setting out to travel the world alone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adventurous - willing to take risks or try out new methods
  • antonym: cautious - avoiding unnecessary risks or danger
