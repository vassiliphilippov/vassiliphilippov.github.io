Transcaucasian

adjective
of the South Caucasus
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænzkɔːˈkeɪʒən / (AmE), / ˌtrænzkɔːˈkeɪʒən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the region south of the Caucasus Mountains, especially in Eurasia

Examples

Transcaucasian region
The Transcaucasian landscape is known for its mountains and rich biodiversity.
Several Transcaucasian countries gained independence after the fall of the Soviet Union.
Traditional Transcaucasian cuisine features a variety of spices and fresh herbs.
