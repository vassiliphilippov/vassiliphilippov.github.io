baling wire

noun
wire used for binding bales
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪlɪŋ ˌwaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪlɪŋ ˌwaɪə / (BrE)
strong wire used for binding bales of material, such as hay or cotton, especially in agriculture or industry.

Examples

A piece of baling wire
A piece of baling wire was all she needed to fix the broken fence temporarily.
Old machinery is sometimes held together with nothing but baling wire and hope.
The farmer used baling wire to secure the load of hay on the trailer.
