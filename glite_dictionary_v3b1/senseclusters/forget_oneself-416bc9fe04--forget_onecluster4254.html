forget oneself

verb
lose self-control or act out of character
Pronunciation: / fərˈɡɛt wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / fəˈɡɛt wʌnˈself / (BrE)
to lose self-control or act in an uncharacteristic way, often due to strong emotion or excitement

Examples

To forget oneself at a party
At the party, many people forgot themselves and danced all night without care.
He tends to forget oneself when he gets excited and says things he regrets.
In the heat of the argument, she completely forgot herself and shouted at her boss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lose control - to lose the ability to control one's actions or emotions
