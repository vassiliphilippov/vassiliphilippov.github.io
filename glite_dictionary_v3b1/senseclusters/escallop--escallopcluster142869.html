escallop

verb
bake food with sauce and breadcrumbs
8.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˈskɑl.əp / (AmE), / ɪˈskæl.əp / (BrE)
To bake (food) in a sauce, often with milk, and usually with breadcrumbs on top.

Examples
