ambulance-chase tactic

noun
aggressive client-soliciting legal practice
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈæm.bjə.ləns tʃeɪs ˈtæk.tɪk / (AmE), / ˈæm.bjʊ.ləns tʃeɪs ˈtæk.tɪk / (BrE)
a method used by lawyers to aggressively seek out clients, often immediately after accidents or disasters, typically regarded as unethical

Examples

Ambulance-chase tactic by lawyers
He argued that ambulance-chase tactics give lawyers a bad name.
The firm was criticized for its ambulance-chase tactics after the accident.
To maintain their reputation, they refuse to use ambulance-chase tactics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ambulance chaser - a lawyer who seeks clients at accident or disaster sites; derogatory term
  • antonym: ethical practice - conduct in accordance with professional ethics, contrasting with exploitative client solicitation
