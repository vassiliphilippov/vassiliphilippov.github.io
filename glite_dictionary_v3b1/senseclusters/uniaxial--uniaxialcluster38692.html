uniaxial

adjective
single axis
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːniˈæksiəl / (AmE), / ˌjuːniˈæksiəl / (BrE)
having or relating to a single axis or direction

Examples

Uniaxial crystal structure
The mineral is classified as uniaxial due to its optical properties.
A uniaxial joint allows movement in only one plane.
Engineers tested the material's strength using a uniaxial stress test.

Related Terms

  • antonym: biaxial - having or relating to two axes
  • synonym: monaxial - having a single axis
