eased

adjective
made less severe or easier to bear
Pronunciation: / izd / (AmE), / iːzd / (BrE)
(of pain or sorrow) made less severe or more bearable.

Examples

Pain eased quickly
After the apology, some of the tension in the room was eased.
Her sadness was eased by the support of her friends and family.
The medication greatly eased his pain after the operation.
