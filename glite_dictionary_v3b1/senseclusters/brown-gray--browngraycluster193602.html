brown-gray

adjective
grey tinged with brown
Pronunciation: / braʊn ˈɡreɪ /; / ˌbraʊnˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / braʊn ˈɡreɪ /; / ˌbraʊnˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
of a grey color tinged with brown; having the appearance of grey mixed with brown

Examples

The bird's feathers were mostly brown-grey, helping it blend into the branches.
The sofa was upholstered in a soft brown-grey fabric.
