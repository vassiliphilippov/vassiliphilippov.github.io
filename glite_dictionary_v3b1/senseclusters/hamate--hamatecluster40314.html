hamate

noun
a bone in the wrist, outer side
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈheɪmeɪt / (BrE)
a wedge-shaped carpal bone on the outer side of the wrist, in line with the fourth and fifth fingers

Examples

The hamate bone
A fracture of the hamate can affect grip strength in athletes.
Symptoms of a hamate injury include pain on the ulnar side of the wrist.
The surgeon carefully examined the hamate before proceeding with the operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpal - any of the bones in the wrist
