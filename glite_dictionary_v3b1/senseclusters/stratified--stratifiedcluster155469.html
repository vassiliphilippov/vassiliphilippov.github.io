stratified

adjective
arranged in horizontal layers
Pronunciation: / ˈstræt̬.əˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈstræt.ɪ.faɪd / (BrE)
(of a substance or structure) deposited or arranged in horizontal layers, especially as seen in geology or sedimentary rocks.

Examples

Stratified rock layers
Scientists studied the stratified deposits to understand the region's geological history.
The stratified structure of the rock indicates that it was formed over millions of years.
The canyon walls revealed beautifully stratified layers of sandstone and shale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: layered - having or arranged in layers
