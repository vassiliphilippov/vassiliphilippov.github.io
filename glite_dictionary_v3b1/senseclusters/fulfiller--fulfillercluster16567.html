fulfiller

noun
order processing agent
Pronunciation: / fʊlˈfɪlər / (AmE), / fʊlˈfɪlə / (BrE)
a company or person that receives, stores, packs, and ships products on behalf of retailers or online sellers

Examples

Online fulfiller service
The company hired a fulfiller to handle all of its online orders efficiently.
A reliable fulfiller can help small businesses deliver products to customers quickly.
Many e-commerce platforms partner with a third-party fulfiller to manage shipping and storage.

  • synonym: supplier - a person or organization that provides something needed
