patrioteer

noun
aggressive or bellicose nationalist
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪtriəˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˌpatri.əˈtɪə / (BrE)
A person who shows extreme or aggressive patriotism, especially in a warlike or belligerent manner.

Examples

Aggressive patrioteers
Critics accused the party of attracting patrioteers rather than thoughtful citizens.
Some historians warn against the influence of patrioteers in shaping public opinion.
The fiery speech turned him into a well-known patrioteer during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chauvinist - A person displaying aggressive or exaggerated patriotism
  • degree lower: patriot - A person who loves and supports their country
