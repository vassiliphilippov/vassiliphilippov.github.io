Pantotheria

noun
extinct ancestral mammals of the Jurassic
Pronunciation: / ˌpæn.təˈθɪr.i.ə / (AmE), / ˌpæn.təˈθɪə.ri.ə /; / ˌpan.təˈθɪə.ri.ə / (BrE)
an extinct group of primitive mammals from the Jurassic period, believed to be ancestors of marsupials and placental mammals

The rapid diversification of Pantotheria marked a key evolutionary step during the Jurassic period.
