Gambia

proper noun
country in West Africa, surrounded by Senegal
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæmbiə / (AmE), / ˈɡæmbiə / (BrE)
a West African country, a narrow republic almost entirely surrounded by Senegal, with a short Atlantic coastline

Examples
