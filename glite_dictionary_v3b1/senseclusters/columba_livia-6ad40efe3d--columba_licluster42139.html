Columba livia

noun
the rock dove; Eurasian pigeon species
8.3
Pronunciation: / koʊˈlʌmbə ˈlɪviə / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbə ˈlɪvɪə / (BrE)
the scientific name for the rock dove, a pale grey Eurasian pigeon with black-striped wings, from which most domestic pigeons are descended

Examples

Columba livia bird
Many of our domestic pigeon breeds trace their ancestry back to Columba livia.
Ornithologists often refer to the common city pigeon by its scientific name, Columba livia.
The Columba livia is commonly found in urban environments around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rock dove - common name for Columba livia, the wild Eurasian pigeon
