to move or force something, especially a bone in a joint, out of its normal position

Examples

To dislocate a shoulder

Synonyms

  • luxate
    9.9 0.01/m
    luxate is the medical term for moving a joint out of place, while dislocate is the common verb used for that injury.
  • slip
    7.2 1.35/m
    slip often means a joint or bone moves out of position on its own, while dislocate describes a forced movement causing injury.
  • dislodge
    6.1 1.37/m
    dislodge usually means forcing or removing something fixed from a place, while dislocate refers to moving a bone from its joint.

Related Terms
