polyspermic

adjective
fertilized by multiple sperm
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈspɜrmɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈspɜːmɪk / (BrE)
describing an egg that has been fertilized by more than one sperm

Examples

A polyspermic egg
A polyspermic egg often does not develop normally.
Researchers observed that polyspermic fertilization can lead to genetic abnormalities in embryos.
The laboratory experiment resulted in several polyspermic eggs, which were studied for developmental issues.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monospermic - fertilized by a single sperm
