climacteric

noun
male period of midlife change
9.9 8.6
Pronunciation: /klaɪ.ˈmæk.tər.ɪk/ (AmE), /klaɪ.ˈmæk.tə.rɪk/ /; / /klaɪ.ˈmæk.te.rɪk/ (BrE)
A period in a man's life, typically in middle age, marked by physical and emotional changes, often considered as the male counterpart to menopause.

Examples

Male climacteric
He went through a climacteric in his late forties, struggling with fatigue and irritability.
Some doctors believe that men also experience a climacteric marked by hormonal shifts and mood changes.
The concept of a male climacteric is still debated among medical professionals.
