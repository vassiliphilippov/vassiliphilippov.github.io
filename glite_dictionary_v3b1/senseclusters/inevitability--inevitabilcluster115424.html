Synonyms

  • ineluctability
    9.9 0.00/m
    ineluctability is a formal, literary word for something impossible to escape, while inevitability is the general, common idea of certain happening.
  • inexorability
    9.9 0.02/m
    inexorability describes a relentless, unstoppable process, while inevitability names a certain outcome that will happen.
  • inexorableness
