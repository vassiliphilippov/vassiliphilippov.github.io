inevitability

noun
quality of being inevitable
Pronunciation: / ɪˌnɛvɪtəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ɪˌnɛvɪtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being certain to happen and impossible to avoid

Examples

The inevitability of defeat
Despite their best efforts, defeat seemed an inevitability by the final minutes of the match.
Many see the collapse of the regime as an inevitability rather than a remote possibility.
The inevitability of technological change means businesses must keep adapting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inevitable - certain to happen; unavoidable
  • antonym: uncertainty - the state of being uncertain
