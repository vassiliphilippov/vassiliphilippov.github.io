that

determiner
specific reference
1.0
Pronunciation: / ðæt / (AmE), / ðæt / (BrE)
used to refer to a specific person, thing, or idea that has already been mentioned or is known

Examples

That book
Could you hand me that pen on the table, please?
She wore that dress to the party last weekend.
I remember that movie we watched together last year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: this - used to refer to something close to the speaker
