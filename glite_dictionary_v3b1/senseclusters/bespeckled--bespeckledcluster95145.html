bespeckled

adjective
spotted or marked
7.1
Pronunciation: / bɪˈspɛkəld / (AmE), / bɪˈspɛkəld / (BrE)
covered with small spots or marks on the surface

Examples

A bespeckled apron
The artist wore a bespeckled apron after a long day of painting.
Her notebook was bespeckled with ink stains from her pen.
After the rain, the window was bespeckled with tiny droplets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plain - without any marks, spots, or patterns
  • synonym: speckled - covered or marked with many small spots
  • synonym: spotted - having small round marks or dots on the surface
