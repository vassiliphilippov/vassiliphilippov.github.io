gonococcal

adjective
caused by gonorrhea
Pronunciation: / ˌɡɑnəˈkɑkəl / (AmE), / ˌɡɒnəˈkɒkəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by the bacterium Neisseria gonorrhoeae, which causes gonorrhea

Examples

Gonococcal infection
The doctor prescribed antibiotics for the gonococcal infection.
A gonococcal urethritis can cause pain and discharge in patients.
Laboratory tests confirmed the presence of gonococcal bacteria in the sample.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gonorrheal - relating to or caused by gonorrhea
