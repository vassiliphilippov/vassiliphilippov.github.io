hydrolize

verb
cause to undergo hydrolysis
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪ.drəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈhɪ.drəˌlaɪz / (BrE)
to cause a chemical compound to undergo hydrolysis by reaction with water

Examples

To hydrolize sugars
Enzymes can hydrolize certain sugars into simpler compounds.
In the laboratory, they attempted to hydrolize the ester under controlled conditions.
The detergent is designed to hydrolize stubborn fat molecules during washing.
