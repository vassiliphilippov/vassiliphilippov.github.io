gentle stroke

noun
soft touch for comfort
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛn.tl̩ ˈstroʊk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.tl ˈstrəʊk / (BrE)
a soft, light touch made to offer comfort or reassurance

Examples

A gentle stroke on fur
A gentle stroke along the pet's fur helped it relax.
He offered her a gentle stroke as she wept quietly on the sofa.
She calmed the baby with a gentle stroke on his tiny hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caress - a gentle or loving touch
  • synonym: pat - a light touch or tap, often to comfort
