parm

noun
informal Italian dish
Pronunciation: / pɑrm / (AmE), / pɑːm / (BrE)
an informal term for a dish, especially chicken or eggplant, topped with tomato sauce and parmesan cheese

Examples

Chicken parm
I ordered a chicken parm for dinner at the new Italian place.
Their eggplant parm is famous for its rich tomato sauce and melted cheese.
We shared a huge parm sandwich during our lunch break.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chicken parmesan - a dish of breaded chicken topped with tomato sauce and cheese
  • synonym: eggplant parmesan - a dish of breaded eggplant with tomato sauce and cheese
