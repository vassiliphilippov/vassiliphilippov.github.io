Getae

proper noun
ancient Danube people
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːti / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːti / (BrE)
an ancient people who lived near the lower Danube in southeastern Europe

Examples

The Getae people
The Getae played a significant role in the history of the Balkans.
Several ancient writers described the customs of the Getae in their works.
Archaeologists have discovered artifacts belonging to the Getae near the Danube River.
