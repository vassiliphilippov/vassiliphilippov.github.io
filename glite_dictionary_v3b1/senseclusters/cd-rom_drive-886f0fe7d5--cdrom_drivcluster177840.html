CD-ROM drive

noun
computer device for reading CD-ROMs
1.2 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsiː.diː rɑːm draɪv/ /; / /ˈsiː.diː roʊm draɪv/ (AmE), /ˈsiː.diː rɒm draɪv/ /; / /ˈsiː.diː rəʊm draɪv/ (BrE)
