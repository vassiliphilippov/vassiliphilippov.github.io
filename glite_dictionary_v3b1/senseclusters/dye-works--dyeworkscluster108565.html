dye-works

noun
workshop for dyeing materials
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪˌwɝks / (AmE), / ˈdaɪˌwɜːks / (BrE)
a factory or workshop where dyes are used to color textiles, yarns, or other materials

Examples

Old dye-works factory
After the factory closed, the old dye-works were converted into artist studios.
Many workers were employed at the dye-works along the riverbank.
The town was known for its large dye-works during the 19th century.
