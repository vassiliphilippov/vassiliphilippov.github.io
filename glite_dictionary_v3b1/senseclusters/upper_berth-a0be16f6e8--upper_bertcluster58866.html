upper berth

noun
higher bunk bed (train/ship)
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpɚ bɝθ / (AmE), / ˈʌpə bɜːθ / (BrE)
the higher of two sleeping bunks, especially on a train or ship

Examples

The upper berth
She climbed into the upper berth while her brother took the lower one.
The ticket assigned him an upper berth in the overnight compartment.
When traveling by train, I usually prefer the upper berth for more privacy.
