stovetop

adjective
for use on stove
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈstoʊv.tɑːp/ (AmE), /ˈstəʊv.tɒp/ (BrE)
designed to be used on the surface of a stove or cooked using a stove rather than an oven

Examples

Stovetop recipe
This stovetop method is perfect for making creamy oatmeal in the morning.
She prefers stovetop popcorn because it tastes fresher than the microwave kind.
The recipe calls for a stovetop pan to simmer the sauce slowly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pan-fried - cooked in a pan on a stove
