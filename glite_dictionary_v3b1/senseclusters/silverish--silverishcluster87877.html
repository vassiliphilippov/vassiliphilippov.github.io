silverish

adjective
having a silvery or metallic grey color
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvərɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling or tinged with the color of silver; having a lustrous greyish appearance.

Examples

Silverish color
He bought a silverish paint to match the old car's faded finish.
Her grandmother's silverish hair glistened in the morning sunlight.
The fish had silverish scales that shimmered under the water.
