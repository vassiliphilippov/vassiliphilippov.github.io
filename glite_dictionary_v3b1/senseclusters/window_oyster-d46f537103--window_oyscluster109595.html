window oyster

noun
marine bivalve with a translucent shell
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndoʊ ˈɔɪstər / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ ˈɔɪstə / (BrE)
a tropical marine bivalve found in the Philippines, noted for its large, thin, flat, translucent shell

Examples

The window oyster shell
Because of its thin, translucent shell, the window oyster is valued both for food and for crafts.
Fishermen in the Philippines harvest the window oyster from shallow coastal waters.
The shells of the window oyster are often used to make decorative lamps and panels.
