gimp

noun
disability affecting walking; limp
7.9 8.4
Pronunciation: /gɪmp/ /; / /dʒɪmp/ (AmE), /gɪmp/ /; / /dʒɪmp/ (BrE)
A difficulty or disability in walking, typically due to injury or impairment of the legs or feet; a limp.

Examples

A gimp in his walk
After the accident, he walked with a noticeable gimp that never fully went away.
Her gimp made long walks difficult, but she refused to let it slow her down.
You could see the gimp in his step as he crossed the crowded street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: limp - a tendency to walk with difficulty, typically due to injury
  • degree higher: stagger - to walk or move unsteadily, as if about to fall
