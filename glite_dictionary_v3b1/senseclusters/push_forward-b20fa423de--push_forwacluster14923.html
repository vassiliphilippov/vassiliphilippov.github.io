push forward

verb
move ahead by pushing through
3.6 0.31/m
Pronunciation: / p ä É Ààf…îrw…ôrd / (AmE), / p ä É Ààf…îÀêw…ôd / (BrE)
to move ahead forcefully by making way through a crowd or obstacles, often by physically pushing others aside

Examples

To push forward through a crowd
Despite the heavy resistance, the soldiers continued to push forward towards their objective.
He had to push forward through the throng to catch his train.
The crowd made it difficult for her to push forward to the front of the stage.
