compass plant

noun
North American plant with colorful flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmpəs plænt / (AmE), / ˈkʌmpəs plɑːnt / (BrE)
a North American plant (especially of the genus Silphium) with red or rose-colored flowers, whose leaves align north-south and are traditionally believed to indicate direction
