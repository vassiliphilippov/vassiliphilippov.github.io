bunchy

adjective
occurring in clusters or bunches
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌntʃi / (AmE), / ˈbʌntʃi / (BrE)
happening or growing close together in bunches or clusters; forming or resembling a bunch

Examples

A bunchy hairstyle
Her hair became more bunchy after she tied it up in several small ponytails.
The bunchy clusters of flowers added color and texture to the garden.
The grapes on the vine were especially bunchy this year, making them easy to harvest.
