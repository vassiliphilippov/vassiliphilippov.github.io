amused

adjective
showing enjoyment or finding something funny
Pronunciation: / əˈmjuzd / (AmE), / əˈmjuːzd / (BrE)
finding something funny, entertaining, or pleasant; showing enjoyment or interest in something

Examples

An amused smile
Even though he tried to stay serious, a slightly amused smile appeared on his face.
She looked amused when she heard the story.
The children were amused by the playful kitten's antics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bored - feeling weary or uninterested
  • synonym: entertained - finding something enjoyable or amusing
