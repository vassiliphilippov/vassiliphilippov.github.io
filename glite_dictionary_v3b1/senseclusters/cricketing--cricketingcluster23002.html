cricketing

noun
playing cricket
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪkɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪkɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or practice of playing cricket, or participation in cricket matches

Examples

Cricketing on weekends
He devoted his weekends to cricketing with friends at the local park.
Her passion for cricketing began when she was a child watching matches on television.
After moving to England, he quickly became involved in the local cricketing community.
