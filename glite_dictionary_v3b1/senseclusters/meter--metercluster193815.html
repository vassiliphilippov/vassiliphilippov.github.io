meter

verb
stamp with a postage meter
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈmiːtə / (BrE)
to stamp mail with an imprint from a postage meter indicating the amount of postage paid

Examples

To meter mail
All outgoing letters are metered by the office staff before collection.
Please ensure you meter the mail accurately to avoid postage underpayment.
The company decided to meter its bulk mail instead of using stamps.
