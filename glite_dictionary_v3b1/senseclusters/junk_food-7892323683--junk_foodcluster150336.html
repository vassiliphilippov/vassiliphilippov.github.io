junk food

noun
unhealthy, low-nutrition food
Pronunciation: / dʒʌŋk fuːd /; / ˈdʒʌŋk fuːd / (AmE), / dʒʌŋk fuːd /; / ˈdʒʌŋk fuːd / (BrE)
food that is tasty but unhealthy, usually high in calories and low in nutritional value

Examples

Junk food snacks
