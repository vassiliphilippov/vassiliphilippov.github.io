limited

adjective
mediocre; not very good or impressive
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of ability, quality, or resources) not very good or impressive; mediocre or inadequate.

Examples

Limited experience
Due to her limited experience, she found the job quite challenging.
His skills as a pianist are rather limited compared to others in the competition.
The team's limited performance last season disappointed many fans.

Related Terms

  • antonym: excellent - of the highest quality; extremely good
  • synonym: mediocre - of only average quality; not very good
