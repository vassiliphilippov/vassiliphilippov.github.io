nitch

noun
wall recess
8.9
Pronunciation: / nɪtʃ / (AmE), / nɪtʃ / (BrE)
a small recessed space in a wall, often used to display a statue or ornament

Examples

Wall nitch
The artist placed her favorite sculpture in the nitch above the fireplace.
Each nitch in the hallway held a different piece of antique pottery.
They designed a special nitch in the wall to display family photos.

  • synonym: niche - a shallow recess in a wall for a statue or ornament
