unfought

adjective
not engaged in battle
7.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɔt / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɔːt / (BrE)
not having been fought or engaged in conflict or battle

Examples

An unfought battle
The border dispute remained unfought after both sides agreed to negotiate.
Many issues in the agreement were left unfought and unresolved.
The war ended with several battles still unfought due to the sudden peace treaty.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fought - having been engaged in battle or conflict
