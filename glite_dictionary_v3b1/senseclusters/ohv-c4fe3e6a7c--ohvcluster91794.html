OHV

noun
overhead-valve engine
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊ eɪtʃ ˈvi / (AmE), / ˌəʊ eɪtʃ ˈviː / (BrE)
an internal combustion engine with its valves located in the cylinder head and operated by pushrods

Examples

A classic OHV

The mechanic explained how an OHV differs from a modern overhead camshaft engine.
