metatarsophalangeal

noun
toe joint
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˌtɑrsoʊfəˈlændʒiəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛtətɑːsəʊfəˈlændʒɪəl / (BrE)
the joint between a metatarsal bone and the proximal phalanx of a toe, often called the MTP joint

Examples

Metatarsophalangeal joint
The doctor examined the swollen metatarsophalangeal after the patient complained of pain in her foot.
Arthritis can often affect the metatarsophalangeal, making it difficult to walk comfortably.
An X-ray revealed a fracture near the first metatarsophalangeal following the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interphalangeal - relating to the joints between the phalanges of the fingers or toes
  • synonym: toe joint - the joint connecting the bones of the toe
