Darwinical mindset

noun
perspective focused on survival, adaptation, and evolution
8.3
Pronunciation: / dɑːrˈwɪnɪkəl ˈmaɪndˌsɛt / (AmE), / dɑːˈwɪnɪkəl ˈmaɪndsɛt / (BrE)
an approach or perspective that emphasizes survival of the fittest, adaptation, and evolutionary strategies in thinking or decision-making

Examples

A Darwinical mindset approach
Her Darwinical mindset helped her succeed in a highly competitive environment.
In the business world, a Darwinical mindset often means prioritizing innovation and adaptability to stay ahead.
Organizations with a Darwinical mindset tend to embrace change rather than fear it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Darwinian - relating to Darwin's theory of evolution and natural selection
