Nan Ling

proper noun
mountain range in southeastern China
Pronunciation: / nɑn lɪŋ / (AmE), / næn lɪŋ / (BrE)
a mountain range in southeastern China, extending generally from east to west, known as a geographic barrier between northern and southern China

Examples

The Nan Ling mountains
Heavy rainfall is common in the mountains of the Nan Ling.
The Nan Ling range forms a natural boundary between different climatic regions in China.
The biodiversity of the Nan Ling region attracts many scientists and tourists each year.
