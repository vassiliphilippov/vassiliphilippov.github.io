Lens

noun
genus of plants including the lentil
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛnz / (AmE), / ˈlɛnz / (BrE)
A genus of small, erect or climbing herbs in the legume family, including the lentil plant, characterized by pinnate leaves, small white flowers, and flattened pods.

Examples

Lens genus herbs
Botanists classify lentil plants in the genus Lens, which is native to the Mediterranean region.
Several wild species within Lens have been studied for their resistance to disease.
The species Lens culinaris is widely cultivated for its edible seeds.
