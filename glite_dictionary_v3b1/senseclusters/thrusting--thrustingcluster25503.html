thrusting

noun
sharp hand gesture; jab-like motion
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈθrʌstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθrʌstɪŋ / (BrE)
a sharp, forceful movement of the hand or an object, resembling a blow or jab

Examples

A thrusting movement
He silenced the interrupter with a sudden thrusting of his hand.
Her angry thrusting startled the crowd during the heated debate.
The politician emphasized his point with a thrusting of his forefinger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jab - a quick, sharp push or punch
