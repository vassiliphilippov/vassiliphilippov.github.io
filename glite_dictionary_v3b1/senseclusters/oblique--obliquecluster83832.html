oblique

noun
a diagonally arranged abdominal muscle
7.6 0.3/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàbliÀêk / (AmE), / …ôÀàbliÀêk / (BrE)
a muscle situated on either side of the abdomen, running diagonally from the lower ribs to the pelvis; involved in movements such as twisting and bending the torso

Examples

Right oblique muscle
She felt a sharp pain in her oblique after an intense workout session.
Strengthening your obliques can help improve your core stability.
The trainer showed me an exercise that targets the oblique muscles on each side of my torso.

Synonyms

  • external oblique muscle
    5.1 0.00/m
    external oblique muscle is the outer diagonal abdominal muscle that helps twist and compress the trunk, while oblique is a name for side abdominal muscles.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: transversus abdominis - the horizontal abdominal muscle beneath the obliques
