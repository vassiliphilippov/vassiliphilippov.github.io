fearmongering

adjective
spreading fear deliberately
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪr.mʌŋ.gər.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈfɪə.mʌŋ.gər.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfɪə.mʌŋ.gə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
