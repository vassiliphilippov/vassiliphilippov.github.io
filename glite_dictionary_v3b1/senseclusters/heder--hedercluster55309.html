heder

noun
private room
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪdər / (AmE), / ˈheɪdə / (BrE)
a private room or chamber in a house, especially in Yiddish-speaking contexts

Examples

A quiet heder
She opened the door to a small heder where children were studying by candlelight.
The old house had a cozy heder at the back, used for quiet reading.
He invited his friend into the heder to discuss the family’s history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chamber - a private room, typically a bedroom
  • antonym: hall - a large room or passage in a building
