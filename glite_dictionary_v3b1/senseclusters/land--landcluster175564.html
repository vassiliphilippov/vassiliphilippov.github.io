land

noun
domain where something dominates (metaphorical)
1.9
Pronunciation: / lænd / (AmE), / lænd / (BrE)
a realm or domain where a particular quality, activity, or condition is dominant or prevalent, often used metaphorically

Examples

A land of dreams

She felt like she was living in a land where time stood still.
