snarkiness

noun
sarcastic attitude
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɑːrkɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈsnɑːkɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being sarcastic, mocking, or sharply critical in tone or manner

Examples

Snarkiness in comments
His snarkiness often made people laugh, but sometimes it hurt their feelings.
The comedian's snarkiness was a big part of her unique style on stage.
She tried to hide her snarkiness during the interview to appear more professional.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mockery - teasing or contemptuous language or behavior
  • synonym: sarcasm - the use of irony to mock or convey contempt
  • antonym: sincerity - the quality of being honest and genuine
