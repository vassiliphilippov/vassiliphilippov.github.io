personnel carrier

noun
military vehicle for troops
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrsənɛl ˈkæriər / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsəˈnel ˈkæriə / (BrE)
a military vehicle, typically armored, used for transporting soldiers and their equipment

Examples

Armored personnel carrier
A damaged personnel carrier was towed back to base for repairs.
The convoy included several tanks and a heavily armed personnel carrier.
The soldiers climbed into the personnel carrier before heading to the front lines.
