midtempo

noun
moderate-speed song
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌtɛmpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌtɛmpəʊ / (BrE)
a song or musical piece played at a moderate or medium speed, neither fast nor slow

Examples

A midtempo song
The band performed a catchy midtempo that got everyone moving on the dance floor.
Her new album features several midtempo tracks perfect for a relaxed afternoon.
He prefers to write songs in a midtempo style rather than fast or slow rhythms.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: ballad - a slow, sentimental song
  • degree higher: uptempo - a song played at a fast speed
