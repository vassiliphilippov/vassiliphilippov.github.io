revanchist

noun
one seeking restoration
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɑːntʃɪst / (AmE), / rɪˈvɑːnʃɪst / (BrE)
a person who seeks to regain lost territory or status, especially in a political context

Examples

A revanchist leader

Historians debated whether the movement was truly revanchist or simply nationalist in nature.
Many feared that revanchist attitudes could lead to renewed conflict in the region.
