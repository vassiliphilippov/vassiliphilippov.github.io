sea cucumber

noun
marine animal related to starfish and urchins
5.4
Pronunciation: / si ˈkjuːkʌmbɚ / (AmE), / siː ˈkjuːkʌmbə / (BrE)
A marine echinoderm with a soft, elongated body, leathery skin, and tentacles around the mouth, related to starfish and sea urchins.

Examples

A sea cucumber animal
Many species of sea cucumber play an important role in marine ecosystems by recycling nutrients.
The chef prepared a traditional Asian dish using dried sea cucumber as a delicacy.
While snorkeling, we spotted a large sea cucumber resting on the ocean floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: echinoderm - marine invertebrate phylum including starfish, sea urchins, and sea cucumbers
  • synonym: sea urchin - spiny marine echinoderm related to sea cucumbers and starfish
  • synonym: starfish - marine echinoderm with arms radiating from a central body
