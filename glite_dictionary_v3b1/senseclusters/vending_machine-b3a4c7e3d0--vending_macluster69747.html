vending machine

noun
machine selling goods automatically
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛndɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈvɛndɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine that automatically sells goods such as snacks or drinks when money is inserted

Examples

A vending machine snack
She bought a chocolate bar from the vending machine in the lobby.
The school installed a new vending machine for drinks and snacks.
When I was thirsty, I used the vending machine to get a bottle of water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slot machine - a machine that operates by inserting coins, usually for gambling or amusement
