scuffler

noun
surface scraper
Pronunciation: / ˈskʌflər / (AmE), / ˈskʌflə / (BrE)
a person or thing that scuffs surfaces by scraping or dragging, leaving marks or slight damage

Examples

A scuffler leaves marks
The heavy boots acted as a scuffler, leaving black marks on the wooden floor.
After dragging the chair across the room, he realized it was a real scuffler on the tiles.
Be careful with that old suitcase; it's a known scuffler and can damage delicate surfaces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: polisher - a person or thing that makes surfaces smooth and shiny
  • synonym: scraper - a tool or person that scrapes surfaces
