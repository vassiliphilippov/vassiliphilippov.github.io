allamanda

noun
tropical plant with large funnel-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌæl.əˈmæn.də / (AmE), / ˌal.əˈman.də / (BrE)
A tropical plant of the genus Allamanda, known for its large, showy, funnel-shaped flowers that grow in clusters.

Examples

The allamanda flower
During the summer, the allamanda produces clusters of beautiful funnel-shaped flowers.
The allamanda is prized in gardens for its vibrant yellow blooms.
We planted an allamanda along the fence to add a tropical touch to our yard.
