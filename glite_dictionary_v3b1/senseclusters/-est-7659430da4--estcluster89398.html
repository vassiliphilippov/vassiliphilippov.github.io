-est

x
superlative suffix
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪst / (AmE), / ɪst / (BrE)
a suffix added to adjectives and adverbs to form the superlative, indicating the highest degree of a quality

Examples

The biggest house
He is the fastest runner on the team.
This is the oldest building in the city.
She solved the puzzle the quickest of all the students.
