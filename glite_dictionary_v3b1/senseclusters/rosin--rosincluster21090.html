rosin

verb
apply rosin to something, esp. a bow
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑː.zən / (AmE), / ˈrɒz.ɪn / (BrE)

Examples

To rosin a bow

Make sure to rosin your bow regularly to get a clear sound.
