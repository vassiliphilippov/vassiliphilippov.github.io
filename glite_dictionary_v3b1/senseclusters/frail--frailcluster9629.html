frail

adjective
easily broken or delicate
2.9 3.3
Pronunciation: / freɪl / (AmE), / freɪl / (BrE)
physically weak, delicate, or easily broken

Examples

A frail old woman
After her illness, her body felt frail and easily fatigued.
The antique vase was so frail that even a gentle touch could have shattered it.
They tried to repair the frail bridge before anyone else crossed it.
After her long illness, she was still too frail to get out of bed unaided.
He helped his frail grandfather walk across the room.
The frail structure of the old bridge made it dangerous to cross.
