white-telephone comedy

noun
Italian film genre with elegant, comic themes
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪt təˈlɛfəˌnoʊ ˈkɑːmədi / (AmE), / ˈwaɪt tɪˈlɛfəˌni ˈkɒmədi / (BrE)
