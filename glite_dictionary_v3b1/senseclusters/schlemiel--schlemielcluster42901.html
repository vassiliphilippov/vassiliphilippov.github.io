schlemiel

noun
habitually clumsy or inept person
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʃləˈmil / (AmE), / ʃləˈmiːl / (BrE)
a person who is habitually clumsy or inept, especially one who bungles things repeatedly

Examples

A clumsy schlemiel
Everyone laughed when the schlemiel tripped over his own shoelaces during the presentation.
In sitcoms, the schlemiel character often gets caught up in comic misadventures.
No matter what he tries, the schlemiel always manages to spill coffee on himself.
