carelessness

noun
lack of attention; negligence
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛr.lɪs.nəs /; / ˈkɛr.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈkeə.lɪs.nəs /; / ˈkeə.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of not being careful, which can lead to mistakes or accidents

Examples

His carelessness caused mistakes
His carelessness led to a costly mistake in the report.
Repeated carelessness can harm your reputation at work.
The accident was due to carelessness rather than bad luck.
A moment of carelessness can sometimes lead to lifelong regrets.
Her carelessness while driving almost caused an accident.
The report blamed the spill on the carelessness of the workers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attentiveness - the quality of paying close attention
  • synonym: negligence - failure to take proper care in doing something
