loggerhead turtle

noun
large sea turtle with broad head, found in warm seas
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑgərˌhɛd ˌtɜrtəl / (AmE), / ˈlɒɡəˌhɛd ˈtɜːtl̩ / (BrE)
A very large, wide-ranging sea turtle (Caretta caretta) with a broad head, found in warm open seas, and mainly carnivorous.

Examples

A loggerhead turtle
A young loggerhead turtle was rescued after being found entangled in old fishing nets.
Conservationists are tagging loggerhead turtles to track their migration routes across the Atlantic Ocean.
The loggerhead turtle nests on beaches in the southeastern United States each summer.

  • synonym: sea turtle - large marine turtle of the family Cheloniidae
