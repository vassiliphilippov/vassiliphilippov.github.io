quadrennially

adverb
every four years
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈdrɛniəli / (AmE), / kwɒˈdrɛniəli / (BrE)
every four years; occurring or done once every four years

Examples

Held quadrennially worldwide
The World Cup is organized quadrennially, attracting teams from all over the globe.
Elections for the presidency are held quadrennially in several countries.
The committee reviews its policies quadrennially to ensure they remain effective.
