saint's life

noun
biography of a saint
Pronunciation: / seɪnts laɪf / (AmE), / seɪnts laɪf / (BrE)
a written account or biography detailing the life, virtues, and miracles of a saint, often intended to inspire devotion

Examples

A saint's life story
Many saint's lives include tales of martyrdom and piety.
She studied the saint's life to learn more about early Christian miracles.
The medieval manuscript contained a saint's life written in Latin.

  • synonym: hagiography - the writing of the lives of saints; the branch of literature dealing with saints' lives
