leaderless

adjective
without 5' leader sequence
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈliː.dər.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈliː.də.ləs/ (BrE)
in biology, describing an mRNA molecule that lacks the usual 5' untranslated leader sequence before the start codon

Examples

Leaderless mRNA

Some bacteria produce leaderless transcripts that start directly at the coding region.
A mutation resulted in a leaderless mRNA, affecting the efficiency of protein synthesis.
