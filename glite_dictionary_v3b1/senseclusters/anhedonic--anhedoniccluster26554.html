anhedonic

noun
person unable to feel pleasure
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.hɪˈdɑː.nɪk / (AmE), / ˌæn.hɪˈdɒn.ɪk / (BrE)
a person who is unable to experience pleasure from normally enjoyable activities

Examples

An anhedonic person
The psychiatrist described her patient as an anhedonic who found no joy in hobbies or social events.
As an anhedonic, he struggled to find motivation for activities he once loved.
Support groups can help an anhedonic reconnect with sources of pleasure in daily life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depressive - a person suffering from depression
  • antonym: hedonist - a person who seeks pleasure as the highest good
