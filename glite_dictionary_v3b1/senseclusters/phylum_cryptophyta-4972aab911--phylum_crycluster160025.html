phylum Cryptophyta

noun
group of aquatic single-celled organisms (cryptophytes)
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪləm ˌkrɪptoʊˈfaɪtə / (AmE), / ˈfʌɪləm ˌkrɪptəˈfʌɪtə / (BrE)
