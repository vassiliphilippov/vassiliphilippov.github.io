kilt

noun
traditional Scottish pleated skirt
Pronunciation: / kɪlt / (AmE), / kɪlt / (BrE)
A knee-length, pleated skirt made of tartan cloth, traditionally worn by men in the Scottish Highlands.

Examples

A kilt skirt
He wore a traditional kilt at the wedding in Edinburgh.
The soldier's kilt was decorated with his clan's tartan.
Tourists often buy a kilt as a souvenir when visiting Scotland.
