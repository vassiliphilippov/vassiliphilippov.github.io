polish

verb
make smooth and shiny by rubbing
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪʃ / (BrE)
to make the surface of something smooth and shiny by rubbing it

Examples

To polish the table
He used a soft cloth to polish the wooden table to a brilliant shine.
If you polish your shoes regularly, they will last much longer.
She spent an hour trying to polish her old silverware until it shone.

Related Terms
