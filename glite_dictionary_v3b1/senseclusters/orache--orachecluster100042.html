orache

noun
herbaceous plant of genus Atriplex
9.4 9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrətʃ / (AmE), / ˈɒrətʃ / (BrE)
A herbaceous plant of the genus Atriplex, found especially in deserts and salt marshes, with edible leaves resembling spinach.

Examples

Green orache leaves
Gardeners sometimes grow orache as a nutritious alternative to leafy greens.
Several species of orache thrive in salty marsh environments.
The leaves of orache can be cooked and eaten like spinach.
