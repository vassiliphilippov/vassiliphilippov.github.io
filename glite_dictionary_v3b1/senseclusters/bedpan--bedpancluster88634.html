bedpan

noun
shallow vessel for bedridden patients' toilet needs
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈbɛd.pan / (BrE)
a shallow container used by people who are confined to bed, especially for urination or defecation

Examples

A bedpan for patients
After her surgery, she had to use a bedpan until she was mobile again.
Hospitals keep bedpans ready for patients with limited mobility.
The nurse brought a bedpan to the elderly patient who couldn't get out of bed.
