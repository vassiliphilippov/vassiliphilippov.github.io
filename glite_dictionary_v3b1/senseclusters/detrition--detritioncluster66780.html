Synonyms

  • wearing away
    5.1 0.03/m
    wearing away names the simple process of surfaces being slowly eroded by force, while detrition is the technical term.
  • abrasion
    6.0 0.17/m
    abrasion is the wearing down of surfaces by rubbing or grit, while detrition emphasizes gradual frictional erosion.
  • corrasion
    9.9 0.00/m
    corrasion is the erosion caused by particles in wind or water, while detrition covers general frictional wearing in machines or nature.
  • attrition
    7.4 0.31/m
    attrition is the gradual wearing down often used for people or rocks, while detrition focuses on physical erosion by friction.
