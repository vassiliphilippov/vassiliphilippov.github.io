Synonyms

  • topic
    1.0 100/m
    topic is the specific thing someone talks or writes about, while subject is the broader area considered or studied.
  • matter
    2.0 149/m
    matter refers to a topic or situation being dealt with, while subject is the general theme people discuss or think about.
  • subject matter
    3.8 10/m

  • issue
    3.0 517/m
