colonnade

noun
a row of evenly spaced columns
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑː.ləˈneɪd /; / ˌkoʊləˈneɪd / (AmE), / ˌkɒl.əˈneɪd /; / ˌkɒləˈneɪd / (BrE)
a row of columns supporting a roof, entablature, or arches, especially in classical architecture
