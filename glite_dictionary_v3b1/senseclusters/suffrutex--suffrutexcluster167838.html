suffrutex

noun
low-growing woody perennial
9.9
Pronunciation: / səˈfruːtɛks / (AmE), / sʌˈfruːtɛks / (BrE)
A perennial plant with a woody base and low-growing, somewhat herbaceous stems, resembling a small shrub.

Examples

A suffrutex plant
Many Mediterranean suffrutex species thrive in rocky, well-drained soils.
The botanist identified the unusual plant as a suffrutex rather than a typical shrub.
This suffrutex regenerates each spring from its woody base after freezing winter temps.
