bunkhouse

adjective
plain and utilitarian
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌŋkˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋkˌhaʊs / (BrE)
simple, sturdy, and practical in style, like something used in a bunkhouse, often describing clothing or furnishings

Examples

Bunkhouse style jacket
He wore a bunkhouse jacket that was perfect for working outdoors in the early morning chill.
The cabin was decorated in a bunkhouse style, with sturdy wooden furniture and simple fabrics.
They preferred bunkhouse furnishings for their practicality and durability on the ranch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: luxurious - very comfortable and expensive in style
  • synonym: rustic - simple and rough in style, typical of the countryside
