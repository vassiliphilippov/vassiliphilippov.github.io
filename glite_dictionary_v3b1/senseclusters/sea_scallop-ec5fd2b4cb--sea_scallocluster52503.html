sea scallop

noun
large edible marine scallop
4.9
Pronunciation: / si ˈskæləp / (AmE), / si ˈskæləp /; / si ˈskælɒp / (BrE)
a large edible scallop from the Atlantic Ocean, valued for its meat and often served as food

Fishermen in New England harvest sea scallops from offshore beds every season.
