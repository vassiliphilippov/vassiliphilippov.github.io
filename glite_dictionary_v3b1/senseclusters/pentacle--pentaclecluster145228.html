pentacle

noun
five-pointed star figure, often symbolic
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛn.tə.kəl / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.tə.kəl / (BrE)
A five-pointed star formed by drawing five straight lines between the vertices of a pentagon, often enclosed within another pentagon; used as a symbol, especially in magic or occult traditions.

Examples

A pentacle symbol
A pentacle is often used as a protective symbol in various spiritual traditions.
She wore a silver necklace shaped like a pentacle for good luck.
The magician drew a pentacle on the floor as part of the ritual.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pentagram - a five-pointed star, especially one formed by continuous lines
