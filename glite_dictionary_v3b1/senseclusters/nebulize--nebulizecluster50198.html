nebulize

verb
to make unclear
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈneb.jə.laɪz/ /; / /ˈneb.jʊ.laɪz/ (AmE), /ˈneb.jʊ.laɪz/ /; / /ˈneb.jə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to make something vague, unclear, or indistinct

Examples

To nebulize an idea
The professor's complicated explanation only served to nebulize the concept for the students.
Too many technical terms can nebulize the main point of your presentation.
Instead of answering directly, the politician tried to nebulize the issue with vague statements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarify - to make something clear or easier to understand
  • synonym: obscure - to make something difficult to see or understand
