abashed

adjective
feeling embarrassed or ashamed
Pronunciation: / əˈbæʃt / (AmE), / əˈbæʃt / (BrE)
feeling embarrassed, disconcerted, or ashamed, often as a result of being caught in a socially awkward or uncomfortable situation

Examples

Abashed and silent
He felt abashed by the unexpected praise from his colleagues.
She looked abashed when she realized she had made a mistake during her speech.
The child was abashed after being gently corrected by the teacher.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embarrassed - feeling awkward, self-conscious, or ashamed
  • antonym: unabashed - not embarrassed or ashamed
