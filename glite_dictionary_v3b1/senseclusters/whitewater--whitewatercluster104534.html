whitewater

noun
frothy, turbulent river water
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
fast-moving, frothy water, typically found in rivers with rapids or near waterfalls

Examples

Fast whitewater rapids
Photographers gathered to capture the stunning whitewater at the base of the waterfall.
She signed up for a whitewater rafting trip on the Colorado River.
The canoeists navigated the whitewater with skill and caution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rapids - part of a river where the water moves very quickly over rocks
