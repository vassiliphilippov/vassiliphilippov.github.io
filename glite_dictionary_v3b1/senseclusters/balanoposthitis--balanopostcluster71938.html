balanoposthitis

noun
inflammation of penis head and foreskin
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbæl.ə.noʊ.pɑsˈθaɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˌbal.ə.nəʊ.pɒsˈθaɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of both the glans (head) of the penis and the foreskin.

Examples

Balanoposthitis inflammation
Poor hygiene can increase the risk of developing balanoposthitis, especially in uncircumcised males.
The patient was diagnosed with balanoposthitis after presenting symptoms of redness and swelling.
Topical antibiotics are often prescribed to treat cases of balanoposthitis.
