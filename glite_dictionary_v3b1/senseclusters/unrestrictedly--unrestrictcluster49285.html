unrestrictedly

adverb
without limits
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈstrɪktɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈstrɪktɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is not limited, controlled, or restricted

Examples

Speak unrestrictedly
The organization allows its members to speak unrestrictedly during meetings.
She was able to travel unrestrictedly across the country after receiving her new passport.
The new policy lets students use the library unrestrictedly throughout the week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freely - without restriction or limitation
  • antonym: limitedly - in a way that is restricted or controlled
