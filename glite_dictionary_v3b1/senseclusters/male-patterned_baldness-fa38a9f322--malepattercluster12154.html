male-patterned baldness

noun
hair loss on top/crown in men
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /meɪl ˈpæt.ərnd ˈbɔːld.nəs/ /; / /meɪl ˈpæt.ərnd ˈbɑːld.nəs/ (AmE), /meɪl ˈpæt.ənd ˈbɔːld.nəs/ (BrE)
A common type of hair loss in men, characterized by thinning or loss of hair on the crown and temples, often progressing to partial or complete baldness.

Examples

Male-patterned baldness symptoms
Genetics play a major role in the development of male-patterned baldness.
Some men begin to notice male-patterned baldness as early as their twenties.
Treatments for male-patterned baldness may help slow or reverse hair loss.
