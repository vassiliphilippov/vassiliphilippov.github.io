red-breasted merganser

noun
a species of diving duck
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛdˈbrɛstɪd mɚˈɡænzɚ / (AmE), / ˌrɛdˈbrɛstɪd mɜːˈɡænzə / (BrE)
a species of diving duck (Mergus serrator) found in North America and Europe, distinguished by its slim build, shaggy crest, and reddish-brown breast

Examples

A red-breasted merganser duck
During migration, flocks of red-breasted mergansers are commonly seen along coastal waters.
The male red-breasted merganser is easily recognized by its striking crest and reddish chest.
We spotted a red-breasted merganser gliding across the lake early this morning.
