character

noun
genetic attribute determined by genes
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛr.ɪk.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkær.ɪk.tə / (BrE)
(Genetics) a structural or functional attribute of an organism, such as eye color or blood type, determined by a gene or group of genes.

Examples

Genetic character
Each genetic character may be influenced by multiple genes.
Eye color is a character that is inherited from your parents.
Scientists studied the characters that distinguish different breeds of dogs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phenotype - observable characteristics of an organism resulting from the interaction of its genotype with the environment
  • synonym: trait - a genetically determined characteristic
