conk

verb
stop functioning suddenly (machine, informal)
7.0 6.3
Pronunciation: /kɑːŋk/ (AmE), /kɒŋk/ (BrE)
(informal) Of a machine or engine: to suddenly stop working; to break down unexpectedly.

Examples

The car conked out
Just as we reached the highway, the engine conked and wouldn't start again.
My old computer conked out in the middle of writing my essay.
The refrigerator conked last night, so all the food spoiled.

Related Terms

  • antonym: function - to operate or work properly
  • synonym: stall - (of an engine) to stop working suddenly
