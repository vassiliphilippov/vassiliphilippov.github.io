auto-changer

noun
record player device that changes records automatically
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːtoʊ ˌtʃeɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ ˌtʃeɪndʒə / (BrE)
an automatic device on a record player that changes records so that new ones are played without manual intervention

Examples

The auto-changer device
He repaired the auto-changer on his gramophone to make it fully functional again.
Many early record players featured an auto-changer as a convenient way to enjoy prolonged listening.
The vintage turntable is equipped with an auto-changer so it can play several records in sequence.
