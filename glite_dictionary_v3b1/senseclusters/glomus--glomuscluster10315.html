glomus

noun
benign vascular tumor
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgloʊ.məs/ (AmE), /ˈgləʊ.məs/ /; / /ˈglɒ.məs/ (BrE)
a usually benign, painful vascular tumor that arises from glomus cells, often found beneath fingernails or in the ear

Examples
