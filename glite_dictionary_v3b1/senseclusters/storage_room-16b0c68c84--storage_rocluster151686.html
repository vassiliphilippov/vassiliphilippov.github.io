storage room

noun
room for storing items
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔrɪdʒ ruːm / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrɪdʒ ruːm / (BrE)
a room used for storing items, especially those not in regular use

Examples

A storage room for supplies
She turned the empty closet into a small storage room for cleaning supplies.
The storage room was packed with old books and furniture.
We keep all the extra chairs in the storage room downstairs.

Related Terms
