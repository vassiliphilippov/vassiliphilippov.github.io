ungratified

adjective
not satisfied
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡrætɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡrætɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not having a wish, need, or desire satisfied or fulfilled; not feeling pleasure or satisfaction from something wanted

Examples

An ungratified ambition
His ambition remained ungratified after years of hard work and dedication.
Many people feel ungratified when their efforts go unnoticed by others.
The team's desire for recognition was left ungratified after the project ended.
Despite his efforts, she remained ungratified by the outcome of the project.
Many employees felt ungratified after their hard work went unnoticed.
He left the meeting ungratified, wishing his ideas had been appreciated.

  • antonym: gratified - having one's wishes or needs fulfilled
  • synonym: unsatisfied - not having one's needs or desires fulfilled
