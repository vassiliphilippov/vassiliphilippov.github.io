triskelion

noun
three-limbed radiating symbol
9.9
Pronunciation: / trɪˈskɛliən / (AmE), / trɪˈskɛlɪən / (BrE)
a figure composed of three stylized human legs, arms, or curved lines radiating from a central point, used as a symbol in ancient and modern designs

Examples

A triskelion symbol
Ancient coins sometimes feature a triskelion as a symbol of movement and progress.
The artist incorporated a modern triskelion motif into the design of the sculpture.
The flag of the Isle of Man famously displays a triskelion made of three armored legs.
