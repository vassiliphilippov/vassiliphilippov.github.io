loop knot

noun
knot forming a fixed loop in a rope
4.7
Pronunciation: / luːp nɑt / (AmE), / luːp nɒt / (BrE)
a knot made in a rope or cord to form a fixed loop that will not slip or change size

Examples

A loop knot in rope

To secure the tent, tie a loop knot at the end of the rope.
