revoicing

noun
giving new voice or sound
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈvɔɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈvɔɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving a new voice, sound, or arrangement, especially in music, linguistics, or audio production

Examples

Audio revoicing

The choir director suggested a revoicing of the harmonies to better suit the singers.
