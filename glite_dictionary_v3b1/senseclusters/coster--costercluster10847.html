coster

noun
street vendor
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑstər / (AmE), / ˈkɒstə / (BrE)
a person who sells goods, especially fruit and vegetables, from a street stall

Examples

The coster stall
The coster set up her stall early every morning at the busy market.
Many Londoners bought their daily fruit from a friendly coster on the street corner.
The old photograph showed a coster selling vegetables from a wooden cart.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hawker - a person who sells goods in the street or at public places
  • synonym: vendor - a person or company offering something for sale
