maple sugar

noun
sugar made from maple tree sap
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪpəl ˈʃuɡər / (AmE), / ˈmeɪpəl ˈʃʊɡə / (BrE)
Sugar obtained by boiling down the sap of the sugar maple tree, commonly used as a sweetener or in confections.

Examples

Maple sugar candy
Artisans in Vermont produce maple sugar every spring using traditional methods.
She sprinkled maple sugar on her pancakes for a rich, natural sweetness.
The process of making maple sugar requires boiling sap collected from maple trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maple syrup - sweet syrup made by boiling maple sap
