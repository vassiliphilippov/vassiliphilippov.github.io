caf

noun
informal small restaurant
7.7
Pronunciation: / kæf / (AmE), / kæf / (BrE)
an informal term for a small restaurant or café, often serving simple meals and drinks

Examples

Local caf
After the movie, we stopped by the caf for a quick snack.
She works at a busy caf near the train station every weekend.
Let's meet at the caf on the corner for breakfast tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diner - a small, informal restaurant, especially in the US
  • synonym: restaurant - a place where people pay to sit and eat meals
