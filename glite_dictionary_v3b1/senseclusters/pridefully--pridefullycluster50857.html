pridefully

adverb
with pride
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪdfəli / (AmE), / ˈpraɪdfəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows pride, often too much or arrogantly

Examples

To pridefully accept
He pridefully accepted the award in front of his colleagues.
The artist pridefully displayed her latest painting at the gallery opening.
They pridefully recounted their team's achievements during the meeting.
She pridefully accepted the award, barely acknowledging her team's efforts.
He pridefully recounted his accomplishments to anyone who would listen.
The artist pridefully displayed her latest painting at the gallery opening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogantly - in a way that shows an attitude of superiority
  • antonym: modestly - in a way that does not show pride or boastfulness
  • synonym: proudly - in a way that shows pride or satisfaction
