argh

noun
cry of frustration
9.8
Pronunciation: / ɑːr / (AmE), / ɑː / (BrE)
a short exclamation or sound expressing frustration, annoyance, or anger

Examples

An angry argh
With an exasperated argh, he realized he had missed the last bus home.
She let out a loud argh after spilling coffee on her shirt before the meeting.
The repeated errors in the document made him mutter a quiet argh under his breath.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sigh - a long, deep breath expressing sadness or relief
