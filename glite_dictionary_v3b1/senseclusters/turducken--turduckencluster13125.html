turducken

noun
stuffed poultry dish
Pronunciation: / tɜrˈdʌkən / (AmE), / tɜːˈdʌkən / (BrE)
a dish made by stuffing a deboned chicken into a deboned duck, which is then stuffed into a deboned turkey

Examples

A holiday turducken
For the holiday feast, they prepared a massive turducken that impressed all the guests.
I had never tasted a turducken before, but it was surprisingly delicious and rich.
The chef demonstrated how to assemble a turducken on the cooking show.
