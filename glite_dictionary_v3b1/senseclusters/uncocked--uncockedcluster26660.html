uncocked

adjective
not ready to fire
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɑkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɒkt / (BrE)
not set in a ready-to-fire position, especially referring to a firearm or mechanical device

Examples

Uncocked gun
For safety reasons, the hunter always kept his rifle uncocked until he was ready to shoot.
The old revolver was found uncocked in the drawer, making it less dangerous.
Before cleaning the gun, make sure it is uncocked and unloaded.
