stickman

noun
figure made of sticks
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈstɪkˌman / (BrE)
a simple figure of a person made by joining sticks or twigs, often used as a toy or decoration

Examples

Twig stickman
The children made a stickman from twigs and decorated it with leaves.
She placed a handmade stickman on the windowsill as a cheerful ornament.
After the storm, only the stickman in the garden remained standing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doll - a small model of a human figure used as a child's toy
  • synonym: stick figure - a simple drawing of a person using lines for limbs and a circle for the head
