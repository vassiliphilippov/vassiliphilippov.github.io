slop

noun
waste water or liquid refuse
3.3 5.5
Pronunciation: /slɑːp/ (AmE), /slɒp/ (BrE)
(usually plural) liquid waste or dirty water, especially that from kitchens, bathrooms, or chamber pots, which must be disposed of by hand.

Examples

Kitchen slops
After washing the dishes, she poured the slops into the yard.
He accidentally spilled the slop from the chamber pot on the floor.
In old country houses, slops had to be carried out every morning.
