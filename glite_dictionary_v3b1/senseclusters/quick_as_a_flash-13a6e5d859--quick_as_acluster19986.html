quick as a flash

adverb
very quickly
Pronunciation: / kwɪk əz ə flæʃ / (AmE), / kwɪk əz ə flæʃ / (BrE)
very quickly; with great speed.

Examples

Move quick as a flash
She tidied up her desk quick as a flash before the meeting started.
The rabbit disappeared into the bushes quick as a flash.
Whenever the teacher asked a question, Maria answered quick as a flash.

Related Terms

  • synonym: in a flash - very quickly; instantly
  • antonym: slow as molasses - extremely slowly
  • synonym: swift - moving or capable of moving with great speed
