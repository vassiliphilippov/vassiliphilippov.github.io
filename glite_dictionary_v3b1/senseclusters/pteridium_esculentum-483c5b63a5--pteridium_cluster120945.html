Pteridium esculentum

noun
fern native to southeastern Asia
Pronunciation: / tɛˈrɪdiəm ˌɛskjuˈlɛntəm / (AmE), / tɛˈrɪdɪəm ˌɛskjʊˈlɛntəm / (BrE)
A species of fern native to southeastern Asia, not hardy in cold temperate regions.

Examples
