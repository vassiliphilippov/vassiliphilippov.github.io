series

noun
set of similar things in order
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriːz / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriːz / (BrE)
A number of similar or related things, events, or people arranged or happening one after another.

Examples

A series of events
Her lecture was part of a series on modern art.
The author released a new book to add to his popular fantasy series.
The police are investigating a series of burglaries in the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: random - Lacking any definite plan or order or purpose
  • synonym: sequence - A particular order in which events or things follow each other
  • synonym: succession - A number of people or things of a similar kind following one after another
