punchless

adjective
without force
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌntʃləs / (AmE), / ˈpʌntʃləs / (BrE)
lacking power, force, or effectiveness

Examples

Punchless performance
The team gave a punchless performance and lost the match.
Her argument was punchless and failed to convince anyone in the meeting.
The movie's ending felt punchless compared to its exciting beginning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forceful - full of force or strength
  • synonym: powerless - without power or ability to act
  • synonym: weak - lacking strength or power
