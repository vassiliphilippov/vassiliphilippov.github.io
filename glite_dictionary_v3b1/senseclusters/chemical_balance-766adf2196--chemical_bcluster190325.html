chemical balance

noun
precise beam balance for chemical analysis
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈbæl.əns / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈbæl.əns / (BrE)
a highly precise beam balance used in quantitative chemical analysis to measure small masses accurately

Examples

A chemical balance scale
Before digital scales became common, chemists regularly used the chemical balance in their analyses.
She carefully placed the sample on the chemical balance to ensure accuracy in her experiment.
The laboratory is equipped with a chemical balance for precise measurements of reagents.
