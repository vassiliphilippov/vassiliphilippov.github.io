untreated

adjective
not treated with a reagent or dye
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtriːtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtriːtɪd / (BrE)
(of a specimen) not treated with a reagent or dye, especially for study under a microscope.

Examples

Untreated sample
It was important to analyze both untreated and stained tissue sections.
Researchers compared the effects of the drug on treated and untreated cells.
The untreated sample showed no color under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stained - (of a specimen) treated with a dye or reagent to enhance visibility under a microscope
