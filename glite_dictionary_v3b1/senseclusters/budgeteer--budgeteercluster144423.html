budgeteer

noun
budget manager
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌbʌdʒəˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˌbʌdʒɪˈtɪə / (BrE)

Examples

Company budgeteer
The budgeteer presented the annual financial plan to the board.
As a skilled budgeteer, she helped the company save thousands of dollars each year.
Every department relies on its budgeteer to allocate resources efficiently.
