foster care

noun
temporary animal care before adoption
3.0
Pronunciation: / c8ffst51st59r k5br / (AmE), / c8f52st59 ke59 / (BrE)
temporary supervised care given to animals (for example, cats or dogs) by volunteer families or individuals, usually arranged by a shelter or rescue to prepare the animals for adoption.

Examples

Cat in foster care
The shelter expanded its foster care program to place kittens with volunteer families.
After surgery, the abandoned puppy received temporary foster care from a volunteer until adoption.
Local organizations rely on networks of homes offering foster care when shelters are overcrowded.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foster home - a private home where an animal is temporarily cared for before being adopted
  • synonym: foster program - an organized scheme by a shelter or rescue to place animals with volunteer foster carers
