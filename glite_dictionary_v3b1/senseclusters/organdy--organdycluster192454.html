organdy

noun
sheer, stiff muslin fabric
7.3 9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrɡəndi / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡəndi / (BrE)
a fine, sheer, and stiff muslin fabric, typically used for women's dresses and curtains

Examples

Elegant curtains made of white organdie hung in the living room windows.

The bride chose a delicate organdie gown for her wedding ceremony.
