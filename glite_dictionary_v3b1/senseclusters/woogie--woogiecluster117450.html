woogie

verb
to dance energetically
to dance or move in a joyful and energetic way, often to lively music

Examples

To woogie happily
The children started to woogie as soon as the music began.
Whenever his favorite song plays, he can't help but woogie around the living room.
At the party, everyone was invited to woogie on the dance floor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sit still - to remain motionless without dancing or moving
