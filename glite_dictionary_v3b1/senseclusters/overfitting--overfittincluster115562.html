overfitting

noun
excessively tight fit
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the action or result of fitting clothing or equipment too tightly to the body, causing discomfort or restricted movement

Examples

Overfitting of shoes
The overfitting of the boots made it difficult for him to walk comfortably.
She returned the dress because overfitting around the waist made it hard to breathe.
Athletes should avoid overfitting their gear to prevent restricted movement during competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: looseness - the state of being loose or not tight
  • synonym: tightness - the state of being too tight or close-fitting
