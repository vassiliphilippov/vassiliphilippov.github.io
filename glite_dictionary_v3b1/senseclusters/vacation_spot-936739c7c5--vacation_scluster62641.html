vacation spot

noun
place popular for holidays or recreation
Pronunciation: / veɪˈkeɪʃən spɑt / (AmE), / veɪˈkeɪʃən spɒt / (BrE)
a place that is popular for holidays, recreation, or relaxation and is often visited by tourists

Examples

A vacation spot for families
Hawaii is a popular vacation spot for families from all over the world.
The magazine featured the top ten vacation spots to visit this summer.
They spent two weeks at a quiet vacation spot on the coast.
