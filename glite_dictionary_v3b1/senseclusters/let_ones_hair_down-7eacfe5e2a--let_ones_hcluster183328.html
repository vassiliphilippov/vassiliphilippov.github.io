let one's hair down

verb
relax and behave freely
Pronunciation: / lɛt wʌnz hɛr daʊn / (AmE), / lɛt wʌnz heə daʊn / (BrE)
to relax and behave in a free or uninhibited way, especially after a period of restraint or stress

Examples

To let one's hair down
After the big project was finished, everyone decided to let their hair down and celebrate.
At the festival, she finally felt free enough to let her hair down and have some fun.
It's important to let your hair down once in a while to relieve stress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loosen up - to become more relaxed or less tense
  • synonym: unwind - to relax after a period of work or tension
