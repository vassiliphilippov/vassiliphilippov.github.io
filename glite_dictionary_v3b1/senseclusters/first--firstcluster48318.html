first

noun
lowest forward gear in a vehicle
Pronunciation: / fɝst / (AmE), / fɜːst / (BrE)
the lowest forward gear in the gearbox of a motor vehicle, used for starting the car moving

Examples

First gear
Beginners are taught to start driving in first before shifting to higher gears.
He accidentally stalled the engine by trying to start in third instead of first.
I put the car into first and eased out of the driveway.
