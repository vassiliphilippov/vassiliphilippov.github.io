datedness

noun
state of being dated
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having a specific date or being marked by a particular date.

Examples

Datedness of a document
The datedness of the contract made it clear it was signed several years ago.
Some people appreciate the datedness of vintage furniture for its historical charm.
The datedness of the information in the report was a concern for the research team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obsolescence - the process of becoming outdated or no longer used
  • antonym: timeliness - the quality of being done or occurring at a suitable time
