brachytherapy

noun
internal cancer treatment
Pronunciation: / ˌbrækiˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌbrækiˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
a cancer treatment in which radioactive material is placed inside or close to a tumor

Examples

Brachytherapy cancer treatment
The doctor recommended brachytherapy as a targeted treatment for prostate cancer.
After her diagnosis, she learned that brachytherapy could reduce side effects compared to other treatments.
Many hospitals now offer brachytherapy for patients with certain types of tumors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chemotherapy - the use of drugs to treat cancer
  • synonym: radiotherapy - the use of radiation to treat diseases, especially cancer
