ragingly

adverb
extremely; very
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdʒɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈreɪdʒɪŋli / (BrE)
used informally to mean extremely or very, especially before adjectives to intensify their meaning

Examples

Ragingly popular movie
The movie was ragingly successful, breaking box office records in its first week.
She became ragingly angry when she heard the unfair decision.
His new book is ragingly popular among teenagers this year.

  • synonym: very - to a high degree
