tule

noun
marsh with reeds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːli / (AmE), / ˈtuːli / (BrE)
a marsh or swamp area overgrown with tule plants, especially in western North America

Examples

A tule marsh
The ducks built their nests in the thick tule along the lake's edge.
Frogs croaked loudly every night from the nearby tule after the rains.
Hikers avoided the tule because the ground was muddy and difficult to cross.

Related Terms

  • antonym: desert - a dry, barren area with little vegetation
  • synonym: marsh - an area of low, wet land with grasses and reeds
  • synonym: swamp - wetland with standing water and vegetation
