necro

verb
to revive old thread
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈnek.roʊ/ /; / /ˈniː.kroʊ/ (AmE), /ˈnek.rəʊ/ /; / /ˈniː.krəʊ/ (BrE)
to post in or revive an old online discussion or message thread, bringing it back into activity

Examples

To necro an old thread
If you necro a thread from five years ago, some users might get annoyed.
She accidentally necroed an old discussion by replying to it last night.
Forum rules often warn against trying to necro outdated topics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bump - to post in a thread to move it to the top of a forum
