table rapping

noun
spiritualist method using raps on a table
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪ.bəl ˌræp.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈteɪ.bəl ˌræp.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of alleged communication with spirits of the dead, in which messages are conveyed by rapping sounds made on a table, especially during séances

Examples

Table rapping during seances
During the nineteenth century, table rapping was a popular activity at spiritualist gatherings.
Some skeptics explained table rapping as a trick performed by the medium and not genuine communication with spirits.
The mysterious sounds produced during table rapping fascinated many people seeking contact with the afterlife.

  • synonym: séance - a meeting for communicating with spirits
