Northern Baptist Convention

proper noun
former U.S. Baptist church association
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrðərn ˈbæptɪst kənˈvɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈbæptɪst kənˈvɛnʃən / (BrE)
a former association of Baptist churches in the northern United States, established in 1907 and later renamed American Baptist Churches USA
