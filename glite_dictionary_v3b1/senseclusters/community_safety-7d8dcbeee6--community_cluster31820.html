community safety

noun
security and well-being of a community
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunəti ˈseɪfti / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪti ˈseɪfti / (BrE)
The state of being protected from crime, harm, or danger within a particular community; the well-being and security of a group of people living together.

Examples

Community safety measures
Better street lighting can make a significant difference to overall community safety.
Concerns about community safety increased after several incidents in the neighborhood.
The local council has launched new initiatives to improve community safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: public safety - the welfare and protection of the general public
