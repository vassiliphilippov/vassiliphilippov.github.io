flare

verb
shine or burn with sudden bright light
3.0
Pronunciation: / flɛr / (AmE), / fleə / (BrE)
to shine or burn with a sudden, bright, or unsteady light

Examples

Fireworks flare brightly
As the fireworks exploded, the sky flared with color and light.
Her temper flared when she heard the news, like a match struck in the dark.
The lighthouse beam flared briefly before fading into the fog.
A match flares brightly for just a moment before settling into a steady glow.
His temper flared when he heard the insult.
The embers flared briefly as she poked at the fire.
