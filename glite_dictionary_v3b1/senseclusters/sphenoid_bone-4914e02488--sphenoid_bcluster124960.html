sphenoid bone

noun
butterfly-shaped skull bone at the base
9.3 7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsfiː.nɔɪd boʊn/ /; / /ˈsfɪ.nɔɪd boʊn/ (AmE), /ˈsfiː.nɔɪd bəʊn/ /; / /ˈsfɪ.nɔɪd bəʊn/ (BrE)
