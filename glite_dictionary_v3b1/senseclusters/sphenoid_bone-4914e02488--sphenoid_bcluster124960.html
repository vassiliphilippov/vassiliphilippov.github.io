sphenoid bone

noun
butterfly-shaped skull bone at the base
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsfiːˌnɔɪd ˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈsfiːnɔɪd ˌbəʊn / (BrE)
a butterfly-shaped bone situated at the base of the skull, forming part of the eye socket and several cranial cavities

Examples

The sphenoid bone fracture
A fracture to the sphenoid bone can lead to complications affecting vision or brain function.
The sphenoid bone plays a crucial role in supporting the brain and forming the eye socket.
The pituitary gland sits in a depression of the sphenoid bone called the sella turcica.
