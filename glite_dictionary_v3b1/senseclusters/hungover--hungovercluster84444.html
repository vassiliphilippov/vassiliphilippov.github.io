hungover

adjective
feeling ill after drinking alcohol
4.2 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈhʌŋ.oʊ.vər/ (AmE), /ˈhʌŋ.əʊ.və/ (BrE)

Examples

To feel hungover badly

She called in sick because she felt hungover after too much wine.
You shouldn't drive if you're hungover the next morning.
