racetracker

noun
race track regular
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪsˌtrækər / (AmE), / ˈreɪsˌtrækə / (BrE)
a person who regularly attends or works at a race track, especially for horse racing

Examples

As a dedicated racetracker, she never missed a weekend at the local horse races.
Many racetrackers form close friendships with jockeys and trainers over the years.
