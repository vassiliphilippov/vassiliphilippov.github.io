fair deal

noun
just or reasonable treatment
Pronunciation: / fɛr dil / (AmE), / feə diːl / (BrE)
treatment that is just, reasonable, or impartial; conditions or actions that are equitable to all concerned.

Examples

A fair deal
After the negotiations, both sides felt they had reached a fair deal.
Everyone deserves a fair deal when applying for a job.
She fought hard to get a fair deal for the workers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: justice - the quality of being just or fair
  • antonym: unfair treatment - treatment that is unjust or biased
