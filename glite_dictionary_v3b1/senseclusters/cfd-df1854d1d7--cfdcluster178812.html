CFD

noun
fluid flow simulation
8.1
Pronunciation: / si ɛf di / (AmE), / si ɛf di / (BrE)
the scientific field and computational methods for simulating and analyzing the flow of liquids and gases

Examples

CFD simulation
The team used CFD to model how water would flow through the new pipe system.
Advances in CFD have made it easier to design more efficient car bodies.
Learning CFD is essential for engineers working in aerospace and automotive industries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aerodynamics - the study of the movement of air and its interaction with solid objects
  • synonym: fluid dynamics - the study of the movement of liquids and gases
