Yucatecan

noun
Mayan language branch
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːkəˈtɛkən / (AmE), / ˌjuːkəˈtɛkən / (BrE)
a language belonging to the Yucatecan branch of the Mayan language family, spoken in the Yucatán Peninsula

Examples

Yucatecan language
Linguists are documenting the grammar of a Yucatecan language spoken in rural villages.
The Yucatecan languages are important for understanding the history of the Maya civilization.
She learned a Yucatecan language to communicate with local communities in the Yucatán Peninsula.
