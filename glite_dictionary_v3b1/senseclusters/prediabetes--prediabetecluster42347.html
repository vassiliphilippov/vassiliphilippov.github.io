prediabetes

noun
early stage high blood sugar
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːdaɪəˈbiːtiːz / (AmE), / ˌpriːdaɪəˈbiːtiːz / (BrE)
a health condition in which blood sugar levels are higher than normal but not high enough to be diagnosed as diabetes

Examples

Prediabetes condition
If you have prediabetes, making lifestyle changes can help prevent type 2 diabetes.
Doctors recommend regular exercise and a healthy diet for people diagnosed with prediabetes.
Many people are unaware they have prediabetes because it often has no clear symptoms.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: diabetes - a disease where the body cannot properly control blood sugar levels
  • degree lower: normoglycemia - the state of having normal blood sugar levels
