line worker

noun
assembly line employee
Pronunciation: / laɪn ˈwɝːkər / (AmE), / laɪn ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
an employee who works on an assembly line, performing repetitive tasks as part of a manufacturing process

Examples

Line worker on assembly
After ten years as a line worker, she was promoted to supervisor.
Many line workers specialize in installing specific parts on the assembly line.
The factory hired dozens of new line workers to boost car production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assembly line - a manufacturing process in which parts are added in a sequence to create a finished product
