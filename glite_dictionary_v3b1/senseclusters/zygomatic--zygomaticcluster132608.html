zygomatic

noun
cheekbone; bone below the eye
Pronunciation: / ˌzaɪɡəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌzaɪɡəˈmatɪk / (BrE)
the bone that forms the prominence of the cheek below and to the side of the eye, also known as the cheekbone
