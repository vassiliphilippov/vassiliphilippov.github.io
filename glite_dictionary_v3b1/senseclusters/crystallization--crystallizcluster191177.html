crystallization

noun
making something definite or clear in the mind
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪstəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkrɪstəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which an idea or plan becomes clear, fixed, and definite, as if taking a solid form

Examples

Crystallization of ideas
After weeks of discussion, there was finally a crystallization of the project's main objectives.
Her thoughts went through a gradual crystallization until she knew exactly what she wanted to say.
The novel shows the crystallization of his political ideas during those turbulent years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarification - the act of making something clearer or easier to understand
  • synonym: formulation - the action of devising or creating something in a definite form
