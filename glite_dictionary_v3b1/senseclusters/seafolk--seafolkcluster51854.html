seafolk

noun
people of the sea
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːˌfoʊk / (AmE), / ˈsiːfəʊk / (BrE)
people who live by, work on, or are closely connected to the sea

Examples

Coastal seafolk
The seafolk have many traditions tied to the ocean.
Generations of seafolk have relied on fishing for their livelihood.
Legends about the seafolk are common in coastal villages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: landlubber - a person unfamiliar with the sea or sailing
  • synonym: seafarer - a person who works or travels on the sea
