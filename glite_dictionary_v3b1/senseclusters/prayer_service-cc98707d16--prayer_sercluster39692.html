prayer service

noun
religious gathering for prayer and hymns
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛr ˌsɜrvəs / (AmE), / ˈpreə ˌsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a religious gathering at which people come together to pray and often sing hymns

Examples

Sunday prayer service
Every Sunday morning, the church holds a prayer service for the community.
She found comfort in attending the daily prayer service at her local chapel.
The prayer service included readings, hymns, and moments of silence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: worship service - a religious gathering focused on worship, prayer, and sermon
