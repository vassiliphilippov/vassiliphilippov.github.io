cyanobacterial

adjective
relating to cyanobacteria (photosynthetic bacteria)
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪ.ə.noʊ.bækˈtɪr.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌsaɪ.ən.əʊ.bækˈtɪəri.əl / (BrE)
relating to, or caused by cyanobacteria, a group of photosynthetic bacteria

Examples

Cyanobacterial blooms
Researchers observed a significant increase in cyanobacterial blooms in the lake over the summer.
Some cyanobacterial species can fix atmospheric nitrogen, which benefits agricultural soils.
The water supply was closed due to cyanobacterial contamination, which poses risks to human health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cyanobacteria - photosynthetic bacteria of the phylum Cyanobacteria
