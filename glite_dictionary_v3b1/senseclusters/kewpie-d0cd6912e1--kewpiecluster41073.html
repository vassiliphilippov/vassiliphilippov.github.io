Kewpie

proper noun
chubby doll character
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːpi / (AmE), / ˈkjuːpi / (BrE)
a small, chubby doll with a topknot, based on early 20th-century illustrations by Rose O'Neill

Examples

A Kewpie doll
She found a rare Kewpie at the flea market and added it to her collection.
The Kewpie dolls were popular gifts for children in the early 1900s.
A vintage Kewpie with its signature topknot was displayed in the museum.
