kurta

noun
loose collarless shirt from South Asia
8.1 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…úrt…ô / (AmE), / Ààk…úÀêt…ô / (BrE)
A loose, collarless shirt, typically knee-length, worn by men and women on the Indian subcontinent, often paired with a salwar, churidar, or pyjama.

Examples
