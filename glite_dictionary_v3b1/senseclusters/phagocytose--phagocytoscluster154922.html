phagocytose

verb
to engulf cells
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfæɡəˈsaɪtoʊz / (AmE), / ˌfæɡəˈsaɪtəʊz / (BrE)
to engulf and digest particles, bacteria, or other cells, as performed by certain immune cells

Examples

Some immune cells can phagocytose dead tissue after an injury to promote healing.
