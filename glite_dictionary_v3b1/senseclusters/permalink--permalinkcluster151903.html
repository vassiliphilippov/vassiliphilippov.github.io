permalink

verb
to create permanent link
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrməˌlɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈpɜːməˌlɪŋk / (BrE)
to provide or create a permanent web address for a specific webpage or post so it can be linked to directly

Examples

To permalink an article
The blog automatically permalinks each new article for easy sharing.
After updating the post, she permalinked it so readers could always find the latest version.
Web developers often permalink important resources to ensure they remain accessible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: link - to create a connection to a webpage or resource
  • antonym: unlink - to remove a connection to a webpage or resource
