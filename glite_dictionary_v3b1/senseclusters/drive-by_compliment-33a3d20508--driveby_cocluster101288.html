drive-by compliment

noun
quick, passing compliment
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪv baɪ ˈkɑmpləˌmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈdraɪv baɪ ˈkɒmplɪmənt / (BrE)
A casual or unexpected compliment delivered briefly or in passing, often with little further interaction.

Examples

A drive-by compliment given
He gave his colleague a drive-by compliment about her presentation before rushing off to a meeting.
She felt uplifted after receiving a drive-by compliment from a stranger on the street.
Sometimes a drive-by compliment can make someone's entire day better.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration
