moated

adjective
surrounded by a moat
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈməʊtɪd / (BrE)
(of a building or place) surrounded or protected by a moat (a deep, wide ditch usually filled with water).

Examples

A moated castle
The moated garden created a peaceful and secluded retreat.
The castle was moated to protect it from invaders.
They visited a moated manor house on their holiday.
