Pteropogon

noun
genus of Australian and South African herbs
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrəˈpoʊɡɒn / (AmE), / ˌtɛrəˈpəʊɡɒn / (BrE)
A genus of Australian and South African herbs, some species of which were formerly included in the genus Helipterum.

Examples

The Pteropogon genus
Botanists recently reclassified some Helipterum species under the Pteropogon genus.
Several species in the Pteropogon genus are notable for their attractive seed heads.
The Pteropogon can be found in arid regions of both Australia and South Africa.
