meatless

adjective
not containing meat
Pronunciation: / ˈmit.ləs / (AmE), / ˈmiːt.ləs / (BrE)
(of food or a meal) not containing meat.

Examples

Meatless meal
Many people choose meatless options for health or environmental reasons.
The school cafeteria offers a meatless menu every Monday.
We prepared a meatless lasagna for the dinner party.
