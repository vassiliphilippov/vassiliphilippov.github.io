uniovulate

adjective
having a single ovule (botany)
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːniˈɑːvjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˌjuːniˈɒvjʊleɪt / (BrE)
(of a plant ovary or ovule) having or producing a single ovule.

Examples

Uniovulate plant ovary
Botanists often distinguish between uniovulate and multiovulate ovaries when describing plant families.
Certain plant species are classified as uniovulate due to their reproductive structures.
The uniovulate ovary produces only one seed per flowering period.
