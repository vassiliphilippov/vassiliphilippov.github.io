mildew

verb
become affected by mildew
5.9 0.13/m

(of organic matter) to become covered with or affected by mildew, especially as a result of humidity or dampness.

Examples

Books mildew quickly
Books stored in the basement will quickly mildew if the air is too humid.
If you leave bread out in a damp kitchen, it may mildew within days.
The old curtains in the attic began to mildew after several rainy seasons.
