sharptail

noun
North American grouse
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːrptaɪl / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːptaɪl / (BrE)
a North American grouse with a pointed tail, known for its strong flight and found in grasslands

Examples

A sharptail grouse
We spotted a sharptail in the grasslands during our hike.
The sharptail is known for its quick, powerful flight across open fields.
Photographers often visit the prairies to capture images of the elusive sharptail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prairie chicken - a North American grassland bird related to the grouse
