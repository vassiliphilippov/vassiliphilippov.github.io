inky cap

noun
a type of mushroom with cap that liquefies
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋki kæp / (AmE), / ˈɪŋki kap / (BrE)
a mushroom whose cap dissolves into a black, inky liquid after releasing its spores, especially of the genera Coprinus or Coprinellus

Examples

The inky cap mushroom
Because the inky cap liquefies quickly, it must be harvested and eaten soon after picking.
The inky cap appears delicate, but soon its cap will melt into a black liquid.
We found several inky caps growing in the damp grass after the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Coprinus - a genus of mushrooms known as inky caps
