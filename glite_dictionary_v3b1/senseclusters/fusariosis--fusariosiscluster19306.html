fusariosis

noun
plant fungal disease
Pronunciation: / fjuːˌzɛəriˈoʊsɪs / (AmE), / fjuːˌzɛəriˈəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a disease in plants caused by fungi of the genus Fusarium, often affecting crops

Examples

Fusariosis in wheat
The wheat crop was severely damaged by fusariosis this year.
Farmers are learning new methods to prevent fusariosis in their tomato fields.
Early detection of fusariosis can help save valuable crops from destruction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blight - a general term for plant diseases causing damage or death
  • synonym: fusarium wilt - a specific plant disease caused by Fusarium fungi
