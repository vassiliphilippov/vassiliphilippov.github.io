Kemalist

noun
supporter of Atatürk
9.1
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɑːlɪst / (AmE), / kəˈmælɪst / (BrE)
a supporter of the principles and reforms of Mustafa Kemal Atatürk in Turkey

Examples

A proud Kemalist
As a proud Kemalist, she advocated for secularism and modernization in Turkish society.
The political party was founded by a group of dedicated Kemalists in the early twentieth century.
He studied the history of Kemalist reforms and their impact on education in Turkey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Atatürkist - a follower of Atatürk's ideas and reforms
