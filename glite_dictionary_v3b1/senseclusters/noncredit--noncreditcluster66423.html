noncredit

adjective
not for academic credit
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈkrɛdɪt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkrɛdɪt / (BrE)
describing a course or class that does not count toward a degree or academic credit requirements

Examples

Noncredit course
She enrolled in a noncredit photography course to learn new skills.
Many community centers offer noncredit classes in art, music, and languages.
The university's noncredit workshops are open to the public and do not require enrollment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: credit - a unit that counts toward a degree or qualification
  • synonym: elective - a course chosen by the student, not required for the main program
