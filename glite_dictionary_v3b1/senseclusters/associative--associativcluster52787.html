associative

adjective
pertaining to connecting ideas or memories
1.0 4.7
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈsoʊ.sɪ.ə.tɪv/ /; / /ə.ˈsoʊ.ʃɪ.ə.tɪv/ (AmE), /ə.ˈsəʊ.sɪ.ə.tɪv/ /; / /ə.ˈsəʊ.ʃɪ.ə.tɪv/ (BrE)

Examples

Associative thinking
