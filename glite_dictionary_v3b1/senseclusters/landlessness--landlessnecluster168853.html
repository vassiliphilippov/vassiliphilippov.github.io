landlessness

noun
lack of land ownership
Pronunciation: / ˈlændləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈlændləsnəs / (BrE)
the condition of not owning or having access to land, especially for farming or living

Examples

Landlessness problem
Landlessness remains a serious problem for many families in developing countries.
The government introduced reforms to address widespread landlessness among rural workers.
Poverty and landlessness often go hand in hand in agricultural regions.
