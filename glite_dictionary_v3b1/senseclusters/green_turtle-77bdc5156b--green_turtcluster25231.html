green turtle

noun
large tropical sea turtle with greenish flesh
Pronunciation: / ɡrin ˈtɝt̬əl / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈtɜːtl̩ / (BrE)
a large tropical sea turtle with a smooth, greenish shell and flesh, found in warm seas and often used (historically) for turtle soup

Examples

The green turtle shell
Many conservation efforts focus on protecting the green turtle and its nesting sites.
The chef prepared a traditional soup using the meat of the green turtle.
We saw a green turtle swimming gracefully near the coral reef.
