green turtle soup

noun
soup made from green turtle meat
Pronunciation: / ɡrin ˈtɜrtl suːp / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈtɜːtl suːp / (BrE)
A soup traditionally made with the flesh of green turtles, often regarded as a delicacy.

Examples

A bowl of green turtle soup
At the banquet, the guests were served green turtle soup as a starter.
Historically, green turtle soup was a popular dish in British cuisine.
Some chefs recreate green turtle soup using alternative ingredients due to conservation concerns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mock turtle soup - imitation version of green turtle soup made from other meats
