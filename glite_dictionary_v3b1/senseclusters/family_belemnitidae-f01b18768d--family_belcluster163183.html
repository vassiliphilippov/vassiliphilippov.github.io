family Belemnitidae

noun
extinct family of Mesozoic cephalopods
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli bəˌlɛmˈnɪtɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli bɛˌlɛmˈnɪtɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of extinct cephalopod mollusks from the Mesozoic era, known for their bullet-shaped internal shells.
