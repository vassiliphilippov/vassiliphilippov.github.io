pay packet

noun
envelope with wages
2.8 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪ ˌpækət /; / ˈpeɪ ˌpæk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ ˌpakɪt /; / ˈpeɪ ˌpæk.ɪt / (BrE)
the money that a worker receives as wages, often in an envelope, at the end of a pay period
