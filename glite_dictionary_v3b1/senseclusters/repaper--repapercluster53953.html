repaper

verb
to cover again with paper
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˌriːˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
to cover a surface again with new paper, especially wallpaper

Examples

To repaper a wall
After years of wear, we decided to repaper the hallway to brighten it up.
The decorator will repaper the bedroom with a modern floral design next week.
It took all weekend to repaper the dining room, but the result was worth it.

  • synonym: wallpaper - to cover walls with wallpaper
