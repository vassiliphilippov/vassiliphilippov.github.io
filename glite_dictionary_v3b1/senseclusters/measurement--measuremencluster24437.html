measurement

noun
the process of quantifying size, amount, or degree
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛʒɚmənt / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒəmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of finding the size, amount, or degree of something, typically by assigning numbers according to a rule or standard

Examples

Accurate measurement of temperature
Accurate measurement of temperature is crucial in scientific experiments.
Before sewing, she took the measurement of his waist and chest.
The measurement of the room showed it was larger than expected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quantification - the process of expressing or measuring the quantity of something
