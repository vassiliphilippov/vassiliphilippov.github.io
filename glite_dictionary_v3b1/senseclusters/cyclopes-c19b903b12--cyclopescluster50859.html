Cyclopes

noun
genus of silky anteaters
Pronunciation: / saɪˈkloʊpiːz / (AmE), / sɪˈkləʊpiːz / (BrE)
a genus of small tree-dwelling anteaters found in Central and South America, known as the silky anteater
