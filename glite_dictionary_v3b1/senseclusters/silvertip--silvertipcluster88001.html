silvertip

noun
a large North American brown bear
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvərˌtɪp / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəˌtɪp / (BrE)
a large subspecies of brown bear, with brownish-yellow fur tipped with silver, found in the uplands of western North America; also known as the grizzly bear.

The silvertip bear
Due to habitat loss, sightings of the silvertip have become less frequent in some regions.
