pro-life faction

noun
group opposing abortion on ethical grounds
Pronunciation: / proʊˈlaɪf ˈfækʃən / (AmE), / prəʊˈlaɪf ˈfækʃən / (BrE)
A group of people who oppose abortion, believing that induced abortion is equivalent to killing and should be prohibited.

Examples

The pro-life faction
Debates between the pro-life faction and their opponents are often highly charged and emotional.
Policy changes are frequently influenced by pressure from the pro-life faction within the government.
The pro-life faction organized a march in front of the courthouse to protest recent legislation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pro-choice faction - group supporting the right to choose abortion
