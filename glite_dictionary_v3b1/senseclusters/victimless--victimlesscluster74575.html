victimless

adjective
causing no harm
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪktɪmləs / (AmE), / ˈvɪktɪmləs / (BrE)
not causing harm or injury to any person

Examples

Victimless crime
Some argue that drug use is a victimless crime because it does not directly harm others.
The debate continues over whether certain online activities are truly victimless.
He insisted that his actions were victimless and did not hurt anyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harmful - causing or likely to cause harm
  • synonym: harmless - not able or likely to cause harm
  • synonym: innocuous - not harmful or offensive
