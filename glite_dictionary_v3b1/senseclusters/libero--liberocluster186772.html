libero

noun
volleyball defensive player
Pronunciation: / ˈliːbəˌroʊ / (AmE), / ˈliːbərəʊ / (BrE)
a defensive specialist in volleyball who wears a different colored jersey and cannot attack the ball above net height

Examples

Volleyball libero
The libero wore a different colored jersey during the match.
As a skilled libero, she made several impressive digs to keep the ball in play.
Coaches often rely on their libero for quick reflexes and strong defensive skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defender - a player whose main role is to prevent the opposing team from scoring
