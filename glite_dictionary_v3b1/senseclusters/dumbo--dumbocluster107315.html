dumbo

noun
informal insult
8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈdʌm.boʊ/ (AmE), /ˈdʌm.bəʊ/ (BrE)
an informal and mildly insulting term for a person who is considered foolish or not smart

Examples

He is a dumbo
He felt like a dumbo after forgetting his keys at home again.
Don't call your brother a dumbo just because he made a mistake.
She laughed and admitted she was a bit of a dumbo when it comes to technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or lacks good sense
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence or ability
  • synonym: idiot - a person of low intelligence or who acts in a very stupid way
