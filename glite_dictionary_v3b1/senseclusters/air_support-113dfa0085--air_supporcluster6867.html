air support

noun
military assistance from aircraft
1.9
Pronunciation: / ɛr səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / eə səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
assistance provided by aircraft to military ground forces during combat operations

Examples

Effective air support
Effective air support can often turn the tide of ground combat operations.
Helicopters arrived to provide much-needed air support during the battle.
The troops requested air support when they encountered heavy resistance.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: close air support - air support provided in close proximity to friendly ground forces
