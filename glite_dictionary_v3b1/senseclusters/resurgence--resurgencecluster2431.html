resurgence

noun
renewed activity or revival
6.6 3.94/m
Pronunciation: / r…™Ààs…ùrd í…ôns / (AmE), / r…™Ààs…úÀêd í…ôns / (BrE)
A new increase of activity or interest in something after a period of little or no activity.

Examples

The resurgence of disease
Experts attribute the resurgence of the disease to declining vaccination rates.
The city experienced a resurgence in tourism after the historic district was restored.
There has been a resurgence of interest in vinyl records over the past decade.

    6.7 0.00/m

  • reinvigoration
    5.5 0.11/m
    reinvigoration gives people or groups new energy and strength, while resurgence refers to a renewed rise in activity or interest.
  • revitalization
    5.8 1.85/m

  • revivification
    9.9 0.03/m

  • reemergence
    5.5 0.51/m

  • rebirth
    6.1 0.80/m
