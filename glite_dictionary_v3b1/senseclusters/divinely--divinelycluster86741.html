divinely

adverb
in a manner relating to God or the divine
Pronunciation: / dəˈvaɪnli / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪnli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or by the power of God or a deity; by divine means

Examples

To be divinely guided
Many believers saw the event as divinely ordained.
She felt divinely inspired to compose the music.
The rescue was described as divinely guided by the villagers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heavenly - in a way relating to or coming from heaven
  • antonym: humanly - in a manner relating to human abilities rather than divine
