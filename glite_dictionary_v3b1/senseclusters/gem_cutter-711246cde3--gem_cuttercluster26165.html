gem cutter

noun
person who cuts precious stones
Pronunciation: / dʒɛm ˈkʌtər / (AmE), / dʒɛm ˈkʌtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to cut and shape precious stones for use in jewelry or ornamentation

Examples

A gem cutter shapes stones
Every gem cutter requires patience and a keen eye for detail.
She apprenticed with a renowned gem cutter to learn the art of faceting stones.
The gem cutter skillfully shaped the rough diamond into a brilliant gem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lapidary - a person who cuts, polishes, or engraves precious stones
