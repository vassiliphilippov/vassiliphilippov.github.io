mythicist

noun
denier of historicity
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪ.θɪ.sɪst/ (AmE), /ˈmɪ.θɪ.sɪst/ (BrE)
a person who believes or argues that certain historical figures or events are mythical rather than real

Examples

As a mythicist, she wrote articles questioning the historicity of legendary heroes.
