untrue

adjective
not true; not factual
7.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtru / (AmE), / ʌnˈtruː / (BrE)
not in accordance with the facts; not true or correct.

Examples

An untrue statement

The report was later found to be untrue by independent investigators.
