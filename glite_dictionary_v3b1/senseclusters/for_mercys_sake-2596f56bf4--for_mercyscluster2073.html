for mercy's sake

interjection
expression of exasperation or plea
6.4
Pronunciation: / fɔr ˈmɜrsiːz seɪk / (AmE), / fə ˈmɜːsiːz seɪk / (BrE)
used to express exasperation, impatience, or an urgent plea

Examples

For mercy's sake keep quiet
For mercy's sake, could you please keep the noise down while I'm working?
For mercy's sake, don't forget to lock the door when you leave.
Just tell me the truth, for mercy's sake, so we can move on.
