uncurrent

adjective
without electrical flow
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɜːrənt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌrənt / (BrE)
not conducting or carrying an electric current; lacking electrical flow

Examples

Uncurrent wire
The engineer confirmed that the wire was uncurrent before handling it for repairs.
Always check if a device is uncurrent to avoid the risk of electric shock.
The old lamp remained uncurrent even after replacing the bulb.

Related Terms

  • antonym: live - carrying an electric current
  • synonym: nonconductive - not allowing electricity to pass through
