bittering

noun
making bitter
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪtərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of making something taste bitter or causing bitterness in feeling

Examples

Bittering of taste
The bittering of the soup was unintentional, caused by too many herbs.
Some brewers use hops for the bittering of beer to balance its sweetness.
The bittering of their relationship was clear after the argument.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flavoring - the act of adding flavor to something
  • antonym: sweetening - the act of making something taste sweet
