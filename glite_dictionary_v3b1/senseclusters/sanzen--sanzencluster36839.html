sanzen

noun
Zen student meeting
Pronunciation: / sɑnˈzɛn / (AmE), / sænˈzɛn / (BrE)
a private meeting between a Zen student and a master for instruction, guidance, or kōan practice

Examples

Sanzen with the master
The student attended sanzen every morning to discuss her progress with the Zen master.
During sanzen, he asked for guidance on a difficult kōan he was working on.
Many practitioners find sanzen to be a crucial part of their spiritual development.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dokusan - a private interview between a Zen student and a master
  • synonym: meditation - the practice of focusing the mind for spiritual purposes
