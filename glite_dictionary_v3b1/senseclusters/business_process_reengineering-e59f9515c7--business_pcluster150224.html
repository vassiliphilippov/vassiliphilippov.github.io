business process reengineering

noun
redesign of business workflows for improvement
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs ˈprɑːsɛs ˌriːɪnˈdʒɪnɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˈprəʊsɛs ˌriːɪnˈdʒɪnɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the examination and redesign of business workflows and processes to achieve significant improvements in performance, efficiency, and quality

Examples

Business process reengineering initiatives
He led several business process reengineering initiatives during his time as operations manager.
Many companies undertake business process reengineering to boost efficiency and remain competitive.
The firm's success was largely attributed to strategic business process reengineering.
