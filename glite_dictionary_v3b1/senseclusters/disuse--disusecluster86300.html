disuse

verb
to stop using
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈjuːz / (AmE), / dɪsˈjuːz / (BrE)
to stop using something or to cease making use of it

Examples

To disuse old tools
Many old traditions disuse as new generations adopt different customs.
If you disuse a skill for too long, you may forget how to do it.
Some words gradually disuse and disappear from the language over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abandon - to give up or leave something behind
  • antonym: use - to put something into action or service
