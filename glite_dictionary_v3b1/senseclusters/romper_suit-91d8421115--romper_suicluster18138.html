romper suit

noun
one-piece garment for young children
6.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈrɑːm.pər suːt/ /; / /ˈrɑːm.pər sjuːt/ (AmE), /ˈrɒm.pə sjuːt/ /; / /ˈrɒm.pə suːt/ (BrE)
A one-piece garment for young children, usually with short legs and sometimes sleeves, designed for play and ease of movement; the lower part is shaped like bloomers.

Examples

A romper suit for children
He dressed his toddler in a colorful romper suit before taking her to the park.
She received a handmade romper suit as a gift at the baby shower.
This romper suit is perfect for warm weather, allowing babies to move freely.
