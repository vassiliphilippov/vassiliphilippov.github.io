mids

noun
midfield players
Pronunciation: / mɪdz / (AmE), / mɪdz / (BrE)
informal plural term for midfield players in team sports, especially football (soccer) or rugby

Examples

Football mids
The mids worked tirelessly to control the center of the pitch throughout the match.
Our team's mids are known for their excellent passing and vision.
After halftime, the coach substituted two of the mids to bring fresh energy to the game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forwards - players who play in attacking positions in team sports
  • synonym: midfielders - players who play in the midfield position in team sports
