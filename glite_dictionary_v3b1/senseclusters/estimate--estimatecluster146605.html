estimate

noun
approximate calculation or judgment
Pronunciation: / ˈɛstəˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛstɪmət / (BrE)
an approximate calculation or judgement of the value, number, quantity, or extent of something

Examples

A rough estimate
My estimate was much lower than the actual number of people who attended.
The contractor provided an estimate for the total cost of the repairs.
We need a more accurate estimate before starting the project.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: calculation - the process of using mathematics to find an answer
  • synonym: guess - an opinion or estimate without sufficient information
