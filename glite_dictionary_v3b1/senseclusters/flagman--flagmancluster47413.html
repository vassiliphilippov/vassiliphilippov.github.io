flagman

noun
person signaling with flag
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈflæɡˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈflæɡmən /; / ˈflæɡˌmæn / (BrE)
a person whose job is to signal with a flag to direct traffic or trains at a worksite, such as construction or railway sites

Examples

Railroad flagman
The flagman stood at the crossing to warn cars of the approaching train.
During road construction, a flagman directed traffic safely around the workers.
The engineer slowed the train when he saw the flagman waving a red flag.
The flagman stood at the crossing to ensure cars stopped for the passing train.
During the road repairs, a flagman directed vehicles safely through the construction zone.
The flagman waved his flag to alert the workers of the approaching train.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conductor - a person in charge of a train or its operation
  • synonym: flagger - a person who signals with a flag to control traffic
  • synonym: signalman - a person who gives signals, especially on a railway
