abidance

noun
the act of dwelling or residing in a place
9.7
Pronunciation: / əˈbaɪdəns / (AmE), / əˈbaɪdəns / (BrE)
the act of residing or staying in a particular place; habitation or residence.

Examples

Abidance at the house
During their abidance at the retreat, they found time to reflect and recharge.
His abidance in the quiet village lasted for many peaceful years.
The law required a minimum period of abidance to qualify for citizenship.
