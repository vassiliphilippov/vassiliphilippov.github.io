broad-mindedly

adverb
in a tolerant or open-minded way
3.5
Pronunciation: / brɔd ˈmaɪndɪdli / (AmE), / brɔːd ˈmaɪndɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a willingness to accept different opinions or behaviors; in a tolerant or open-minded manner.

Examples

To broad-mindedly accept ideas
She broad-mindedly considered proposals that challenged her own views.
The organization broad-mindedly welcomed people from various backgrounds and beliefs.
The school broad-mindedly allowed students to express themselves through diverse art forms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: narrow-mindedly - in an intolerant or prejudiced manner
  • synonym: tolerantly - in a tolerant manner
