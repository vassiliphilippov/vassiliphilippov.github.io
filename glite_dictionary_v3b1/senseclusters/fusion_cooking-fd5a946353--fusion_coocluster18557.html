fusion cooking

noun
cooking combining elements of different cuisines
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːʒən ˈkʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfjuːʒən ˈkʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
a style of cooking that combines ingredients, techniques, or seasonings from different cuisines to create unique dishes

Examples

Many modern restaurants feature fusion cooking to appeal to adventurous diners.
The popularity of fusion cooking has brought innovative flavors to traditional dishes around the world.
