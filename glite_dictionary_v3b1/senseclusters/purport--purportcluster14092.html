purport

noun
intended meaning or sense
8.4 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / p…ôrÀàp…îrt / (AmE), / p…ôÀàp…îÀêt / (BrE)
the general or intended meaning or substance of something, such as a document or speech

Examples

Purport of the message

The purport of her remarks was that changes needed to be made.
