valetudinarianism

noun
excessive concern about health
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /væl.ɪ.tuː.ˈdɪn.ə.rɪ.ə.nɪ.zəm/ /; / /væl.ɪ.tjuː.ˈdɪn.ə.rɪ.ə.nɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /væl.ɪ.tjuː.ˈdɪn.ə.ri.ə.nɪ.zəm/ /; / /væl.ɪ.tuː.ˈdɪn.ə.ri.ə.nɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the state of being overly concerned with one's health, especially involving a chronic preoccupation with illness or poor health
