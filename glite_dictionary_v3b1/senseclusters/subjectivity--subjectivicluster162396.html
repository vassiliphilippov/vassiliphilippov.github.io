subjectivity

noun
personal bias or perspective
1.8 5.7
Pronunciation: /səb.dʒek.ˈtɪv.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /sʌb.dʒek.ˈtɪv.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /səb.dʒek.ˈtɪv.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /sʌb.dʒek.ˈtɪv.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
The quality of being based on or influenced by personal feelings, tastes, or opinions rather than external facts.

Examples

Personal subjectivity
Her review was influenced by her own subjectivity rather than an impartial assessment.
In art criticism, a degree of subjectivity is inevitable when interpreting meaning.
The scientists aimed to eliminate subjectivity from their observations as much as possible.
