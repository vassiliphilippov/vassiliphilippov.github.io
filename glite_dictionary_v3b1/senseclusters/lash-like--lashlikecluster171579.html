lash-like

adjective
resembling a whip or lash
Pronunciation: / ˈlæʃˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlæʃˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having or resembling the shape or motion of a lash or whip; especially used to describe threadlike, flexible structures such as flagella.

Examples

lash-like tendrils
Her hair fell in lash-like tendrils across her forehead.
The bacterium propels itself with a lash-like flagellum.
Under the microscope, the algae displayed lash-like appendages that allowed movement.
