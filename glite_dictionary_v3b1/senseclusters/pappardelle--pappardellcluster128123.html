pappardelle

noun
wide flat pasta
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːpɑːrˈdɛli / (AmE), / ˌpæpɑːˈdɛleɪ / (BrE)
a type of wide, flat Italian pasta, usually served with rich sauces

Examples

Wide pappardelle pasta
The chef prepared homemade pappardelle with a rich mushroom sauce for the guests.
I ordered pappardelle because I love how the wide noodles hold the sauce.
Fresh pappardelle pairs perfectly with hearty meat ragùs in traditional Italian cooking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fettuccine - a type of flat Italian pasta, narrower than pappardelle
  • synonym: tagliatelle - a type of ribbon-shaped Italian pasta, similar but thinner than pappardelle
