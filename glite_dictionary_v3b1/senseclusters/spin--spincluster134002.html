spin

verb
revolve rapidly around own axis
4.6 1.0
Pronunciation: /spɪn/ (AmE), /spɪn/ (BrE)
to turn around quickly or cause something to turn rapidly (used for objects, people, or aircraft)

Examples

To spin around
Dancers would spin across the stage in perfect synchronization.
The little girl loves to spin in circles until she gets dizzy.
The top continued to spin for more than a minute before falling over.

He decided to spin the globe to pick his next travel destination.

The strong gusts of wind made the empty bottle spin across the street.
