Synonyms

  • spin around
    2.1 3.16/m
    spin around is to revolve quickly and repeatedly around one's own axis, while spin is a general verb for rapid turning.
  • whirl
    2.8 6.28/m
    whirl moves or spin rapidly in a circle, while spin is the neutral verb for turning quickly in many contexts.
  • twirl
    2.0 4.62/m
    twirl means to spin lightly and often for show or fun, while spin is a broader, neutral word for quick turning.
  • gyrate
    6.9 0.80/m
    gyrate means to move in circles or spirals and often sounds formal, while spin is a simple common word for turning quickly.
