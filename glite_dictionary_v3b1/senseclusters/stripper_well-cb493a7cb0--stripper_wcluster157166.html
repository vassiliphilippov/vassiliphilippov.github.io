stripper well

noun
an oil well producing under ten barrels a day
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɪpər wɛl / (AmE), / ˈstrɪpə wɛl / (BrE)
an oil well that produces less than ten barrels of oil per day, typically near the end of its productive life

Examples

Old stripper wells
After years of output decline, the old field now consists mainly of stripper wells.
Regulations regarding stripper wells often differ from those applied to higher-yield oil wells.
The company specializes in the operation and management of stripper wells across rural regions.
