midtown

adjective
central city area
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌtaʊn / (BrE)
located in or relating to the central part of a city, especially the main business or shopping area

Examples

Midtown hotel location
They chose a midtown hotel to be close to the theaters and restaurants.
The new office is in a midtown building near the main shopping street.
Living in a midtown neighborhood makes commuting much easier for many people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: downtown - located in or relating to the main business district of a city
  • antonym: uptown - located in or relating to the residential or northern part of a city
