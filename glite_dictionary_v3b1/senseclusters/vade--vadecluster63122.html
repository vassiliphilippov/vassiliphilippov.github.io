vade

verb
to go or walk
Pronunciation: / veɪd / (AmE), / veɪd / (BrE)
to go or walk, especially used in old or poetic language

Examples

To vade forth
The knight did vade forth into the misty morning.
In ancient tales, heroes often vade into unknown lands seeking adventure.
With heavy heart, she vaded away from her childhood home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depart - to leave, especially to start a journey
  • synonym: proceed - to move forward or continue
  • antonym: remain - to stay in the same place
