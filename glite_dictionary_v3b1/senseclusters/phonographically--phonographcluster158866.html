phonographically

adverb
by sound representation
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊnəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌfəʊnəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that represents the sounds of spoken language, especially through phonetic transcription or spelling reflecting pronunciation

Examples

Phonographically transcribed words
The linguist transcribed the word phonographically to help students with pronunciation.
Some dictionaries list words phonographically to show how they are spoken.
She prefers to write foreign names phonographically so readers can say them correctly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phonetically - in a way that relates to the sounds of speech
