unthematically

adverb
without a main theme
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnθɪˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌʌnθɪˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is not related to or organized around a main theme or subject

Examples

Arranged unthematically
The chapters are arranged unthematically, making the book difficult to navigate.
He discussed the issues unthematically, jumping from one topic to another.
The exhibition was organized unthematically, so the artworks seemed unrelated.

Related Terms

  • antonym: thematically - in a way that relates to a main theme or subject
