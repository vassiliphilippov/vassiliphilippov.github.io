billiard room

noun
room for playing billiards
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪljɚd ˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈbɪlɪəd ˌruːm / (BrE)
a room furnished and arranged for playing billiards or similar cue sports

Examples

A billiard room table
He installed a pool table in his billiard room to entertain guests.
The old mansion has a beautifully decorated billiard room on the ground floor.
We spent the evening in the billiard room playing a few friendly matches.
