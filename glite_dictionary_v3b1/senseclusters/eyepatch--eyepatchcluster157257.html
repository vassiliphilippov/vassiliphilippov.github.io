eyepatch

noun
cloth covering worn over one eye
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌpætʃ / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌpatʃ / (BrE)
A piece of cloth or other material worn over one eye, typically to protect an injured or diseased eye or for medical treatment.

Examples

A eyepatch for healing
After her surgery, Emma had to wear an eyepatch for several weeks.
He bought a black eyepatch to protect his injured eye.
The pirate wore an eyepatch after losing his eye in battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bandage - a piece of material used to cover and protect a wound
