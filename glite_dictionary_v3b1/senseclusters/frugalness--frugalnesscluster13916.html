frugalness

noun
carefulness in avoiding waste
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfruːɡəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈfruːɡəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being careful with money or resources to avoid waste

Examples

A frugalness habit
Despite their wealth, they maintained a sense of frugalness in their daily lives.
Her frugalness allowed her to save money for unexpected expenses.
The company's frugalness helped it survive during tough economic times.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extravagance - lack of restraint in spending resources
  • synonym: frugality - the quality of being economical with resources
