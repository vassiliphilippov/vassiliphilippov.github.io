drawer

noun
person who writes a check or draft
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔːr / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːə / (BrE)
the person who writes and signs a check or draft, instructing a bank or another party to pay money to someone else

Examples

Check drawer
If the drawer does not have sufficient funds, the check may bounce.
Legally, the drawer is responsible for the validity of the payment instrument.
The drawer must endorse the check before it can be deposited.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drawee - person or bank ordered to pay on a check or draft
  • antonym: payee - person to whom a check or draft is made payable
