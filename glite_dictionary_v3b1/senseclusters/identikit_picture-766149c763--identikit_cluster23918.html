Identikit picture

noun
police-made face composite from witness descriptions
7.3 6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪdəntɪˌkɪt ˈpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈaɪdəntɪˌkɪt ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
A picture of a person's face made by combining images of facial features according to descriptions, typically used by the police to identify suspects.

Examples

An Identikit picture of suspect

The Identikit picture matched the description given by several people at the scene.
The police released an Identikit picture of the suspect based on the witness’s account.
