sanctimommy attitude

noun
self-righteous parenting manner
9.9 8.9
Pronunciation: /sæŋk.tɪ.ˈmɑː.miː ˈæt.ə.tuːd/ (AmE), /sæŋk.tɪ.ˈmɒm.iː ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd/ (BrE)
A self-righteous or condescending manner exhibited by a mother (or parent) who proclaims her way of parenting as superior to others'.

Examples

Sanctimommy attitude at school
Her sanctimommy attitude made everyone else feel inadequate during the school meeting.
Many parents are put off by the sanctimommy attitude that sometimes dominates online groups.
There's no need for your sanctimommy attitude—parenting is not one size fits all.
