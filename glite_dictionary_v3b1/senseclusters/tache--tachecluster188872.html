tache

noun
mark of disgrace
9.9
Pronunciation: / tæʃ / (AmE), / tæʃ / (BrE)
a stain or blemish on a person's reputation, causing disgrace or dishonor

Examples

A tache on reputation
The scandal left a lasting tache on the politician's otherwise respectable career.
Despite his achievements, a single mistake became a tache on his reputation.
She worked hard to remove the tache of dishonor from her family's name.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blemish - a flaw or imperfection, especially on reputation
  • antonym: honor - high respect or esteem
  • synonym: stigma - a mark of shame or discredit
