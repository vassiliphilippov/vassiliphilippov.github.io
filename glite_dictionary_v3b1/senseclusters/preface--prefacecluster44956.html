preface

noun
introductory essay before a book's main text
2.2 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈpre.fəs/ /; / /ˈpre.fɪs/ (AmE), /ˈpre.fəs/ /; / /ˈpre.fɪs/ (BrE)
