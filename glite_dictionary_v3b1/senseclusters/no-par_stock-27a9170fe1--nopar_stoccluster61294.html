no-par stock

noun
stock with no stated par value
8.3 7.5
Pronunciation: /noʊ pɑːr stɑːk/ (AmE), /nəʊ pɑː stɒk/ (BrE)

Examples

Unlike traditional shares, no-par stock does not state a minimum selling price.
