Bokmal

noun
standard form of Norwegian; official language
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊkˌmɔl / (AmE), / ˈbɒkmɑːl / (BrE)
one of the two official written standards of the Norwegian language, closely related to Danish

Examples

Bokmal language
Many Norwegian books are written in Bokmal, but some use Nynorsk.
She studied Bokmal in school before moving to Oslo.
The government allows all official documents to be published in Bokmal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Nynorsk - the other official written standard of Norwegian
