industrial arts

noun
school subject on tools and machinery
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʌstriəl ɑrts / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəl ɑːts / (BrE)
A school subject focused on the study and practice of using tools and machinery, especially as taught in secondary or technical schools.

Examples

Industrial arts class
He credits his success as an engineer to skills learned in industrial arts during high school.
Many students enjoy industrial arts because they get to build projects with their hands.
The school expanded its industrial arts program to include advanced courses in robotics and woodworking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shop class - a practical educational class in which students learn to work with tools and machinery
