deworm

verb
to remove worms
4.2 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / diÀàw…úrm / (AmE), / diÀêÀàw…úÀêm / (BrE)
to give medicine or treatment to a person or animal to remove parasitic worms from their body

Examples
