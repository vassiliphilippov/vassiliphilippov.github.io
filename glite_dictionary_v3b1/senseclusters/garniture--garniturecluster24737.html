garniture

noun
decorative embellishment
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrnɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːnɪtjʊə / (BrE)
a decorative item or embellishment, especially one used to adorn food or a dish

Examples

Food garniture
The chef added a colorful garniture of edible flowers to the dessert plate.
A silver garniture adorned the center of the dining table during the banquet.
He carefully arranged the garniture to make the dish look more appealing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decoration - something that adds beauty or adornment
  • synonym: garnish - a small decorative item added to food
