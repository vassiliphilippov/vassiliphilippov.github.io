bole

verb
to form a trunk
Pronunciation: / boʊl / (AmE), / bəʊl / (BrE)
to provide with or form a trunk or main stem, especially in trees (rare, technical)

Examples

To bole a tree
The botanist explained how certain species bole rapidly in their early years.
In the ancient grove, many trees had beautifully boled forms that towered above the undergrowth.
Selective breeding can encourage saplings to bole more robustly as they mature.
