hansom

noun
two-wheeled horse-drawn carriage
Pronunciation: / ˈhæn.səm / (AmE), / ˈhæn.səm / (BrE)
a two-wheeled horse-drawn carriage with a covered passenger compartment and the driver's seat raised behind the passengers

Examples

A hansom carriage
In Victorian novels, characters often travel by hansom late at night.
The hansom driver tipped his hat as he helped the passengers aboard.
The detective hailed a hansom to take him through the foggy streets of London.
