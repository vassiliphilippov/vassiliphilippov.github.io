parasitosis

noun
disease from parasites
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəsaɪˈtoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌpærəsaɪˈtəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a disease or condition caused by the presence of parasites in the body

Examples

Parasitosis symptoms
The patient was treated for parasitosis after symptoms of itching and discomfort appeared.
Doctors often check for parasitosis in people returning from tropical regions.
Proper hygiene can help prevent parasitosis caused by contaminated food or water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infection - the invasion of the body by harmful microorganisms
  • synonym: infestation - the state of being invaded by parasites or pests
