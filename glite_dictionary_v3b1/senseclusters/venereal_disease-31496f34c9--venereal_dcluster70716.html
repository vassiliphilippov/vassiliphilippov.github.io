venereal disease

noun
sexually transmitted infection
Pronunciation: / vəˈnɪriəl dɪˈziz /; / vəˈnɪri.əl dɪˈziz / (AmE), / vɪˈnɪəriəl dɪˈziːz /; / vɪˈnɪəri.əl dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
a disease that is spread through sexual intercourse or genital contact (also called a sexually transmitted infection, STI)
