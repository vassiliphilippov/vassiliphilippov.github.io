rustling

adjective
making a soft, gentle sound
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌs.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈrʌs.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
making a soft, gentle, or whispering sound, like leaves or paper moving gently.

Examples

Rustling paper

She fell asleep to the rustling sound of the wind outside her window.
The rustling leaves created a peaceful atmosphere in the garden.
