prophesy

verb
predict by divine inspiration
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːfəˌsaɪ / (AmE), / ˈprɒf.ɪ.saɪ / (BrE)
to predict or reveal something, especially as if inspired by a divine source

Examples

To prophesy the future
He prophesied that the city would fall unless the people changed their ways.
Many believed she had the power to prophesy the future through her visions.
The ancient priest claimed he could prophesy great events yet to come.

Related Terms

  • synonym: predict - to say that something will happen in the future
