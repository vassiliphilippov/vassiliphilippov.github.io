Jose Julian Marti

proper noun
Cuban poet and independence leader
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈseɪ huːˈliːən mɑːrˈtiː / (AmE), / həʊˈseɪ huːˈliːən mɑːˈtiː / (BrE)
(1853–1895) Cuban poet and revolutionary leader, known for his role in the fight for Cuban independence from Spain.
