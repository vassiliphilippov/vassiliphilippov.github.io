geisha girl

noun
Japanese female entertainer
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪʃə ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪʃə ɡɜːl / (BrE)
A Japanese woman trained to entertain men with conversation, singing, and dancing, typically as part of traditional cultural practices.

Examples

A geisha girl performance
During his trip to Japan, he attended a dinner hosted by a famous geisha girl who performed traditional songs.
Novels set in Kyoto often describe the mysterious lives of geisha girls in the old districts.
The American sailor was fascinated by the geisha girl's graceful dance and elegant kimono.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geisha - Japanese woman trained to entertain, without the word 'girl'
