foot lever

noun
lever operated by the foot
2.9
Pronunciation: / fʊt ˈlɛvɚ / (AmE), / fʊt ˈliːvə / (BrE)
A lever designed to be operated by the foot, often used to control machinery or devices.

Examples

Press the foot lever
He pressed the foot lever to raise the platform.
The old printing press required the operator to use a foot lever to start the process.
The sewing machine's foot lever allows for hands-free operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pedal - A foot-operated lever or control, especially in vehicles or musical instruments
