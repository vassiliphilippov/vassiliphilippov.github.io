scarring

adjective
causing lasting harm
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑːrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskɑːrɪŋ / (BrE)
causing or resulting in lasting damage or harm, either physically or emotionally

Examples

Scarring experience
The accident had a scarring impact on his confidence for many years.
Some childhood experiences can be deeply scarring and hard to forget.
The war left a scarring legacy on the entire community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: damaging - causing harm or injury
  • antonym: healing - making healthy or whole again
