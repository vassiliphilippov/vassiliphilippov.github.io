unfulfilling

adjective
not satisfying
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnfʊlˈfɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnfʊlˈfɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
not giving satisfaction or a sense of achievement

Examples

An unfulfilling job
He left his unfulfilling job to pursue a career he was passionate about.
Many people feel that routine tasks can be unfulfilling over time.
She realized her relationship had become unfulfilling and decided to make a change.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fulfilling - giving satisfaction or a sense of achievement
  • synonym: unsatisfying - not providing satisfaction or pleasure
