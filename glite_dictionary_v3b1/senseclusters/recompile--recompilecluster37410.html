recompile

verb
to assemble again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːkəmˈpaɪl / (AmE), / ˌriːkəmˈpaɪl / (BrE)
to assemble or produce a collection, list, or document again, often after adding or updating material

Examples

To recompile a report
After receiving new data, the team had to recompile the entire report before the deadline.
She decided to recompile the list of participants to include the latest registrations.
The editor asked him to recompile the document with the updated figures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compile - to collect and assemble information or material
  • synonym: update - to make something more current by adding new information
