lunette

noun
crescent sand dune
9.9
Pronunciation: /luː.ˈnet/ (AmE), /ljuː.ˈnet/ /; / /luː.ˈnet/ (BrE)
a crescent-shaped sand dune formed on the downwind side of a dry lake or playa, especially in arid regions

Examples

A sand lunette
The desert landscape was dotted with several lunettes near the edge of the dry lake.
Scientists studied the formation of lunettes to understand wind patterns in arid environments.
Satellite images revealed a series of lunettes curving along the ancient playa.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dune - a hill or ridge of sand formed by wind
