X-radiation

noun
radiation in the form of X-rays
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɛks ˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən /; / ˌɛks ˌɹeɪdiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛks ˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
