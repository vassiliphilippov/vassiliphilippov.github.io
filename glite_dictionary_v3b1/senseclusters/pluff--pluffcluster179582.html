pluff

verb
to fluff up
9.9
Pronunciation: / plʌf / (AmE), / plʌf / (BrE)
to make something soft and fluffy by shaking, patting, or teasing its filling, especially a cushion or pillow

Examples

To pluff a pillow
Every morning, she would pluff the pillows to keep them looking fresh.
He pluffed the sofa cushions before the guests arrived to make the room more inviting.
After washing the comforter, you should pluff it to restore its shape.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flatten - to make something flat or less full
  • synonym: fluff - to make something soft and full by shaking or patting
