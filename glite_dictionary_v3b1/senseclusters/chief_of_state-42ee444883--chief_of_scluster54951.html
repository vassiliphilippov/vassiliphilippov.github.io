chief of state

noun
country's chief public representative
3.1 4.4
Pronunciation: /tʃiːf steɪt/ (AmE), /tʃiːf steɪt/ (BrE)
The chief public representative of a country, who may also be the head of government.

Examples

The chief of state
As chief of state, the monarch performs ceremonial duties at important national events.
In some nations, the chief of state holds significant power, while in others the role is mostly symbolic.
The president acts as both the chief of state and the head of government in many countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: head of state - The official leader of a country, especially in a ceremonial role
