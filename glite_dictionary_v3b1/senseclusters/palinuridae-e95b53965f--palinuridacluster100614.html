Palinuridae

noun
family of spiny lobsters
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪ.ləˈnʊr.ɪˌdi /; / ˌpælɪˈnʊrɪdiː / (AmE), / ˌpal.ɪˈnjuː.rɪ.diː /; / ˌpalɪˈnjʊərɪdiː / (BrE)
