mindshare

noun
brand awareness
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪnd.ʃər/ /; / /ˈmaɪnd.ʃeɪr/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪnd.ʃeə/ (BrE)
the amount of attention or awareness that a brand, product, idea, or topic has in people's minds or in public

Examples

Brand mindshare
The tech giant has gained significant mindshare among young consumers through creative marketing.
Startups often struggle to achieve enough mindshare to compete with established brands.
Effective advertising campaigns can quickly boost a company's mindshare in a crowded market.
The new technology quickly gained mindshare among young consumers around the world.
Activists worked hard to increase mindshare for environmental issues in the media.
Despite its quality, the product struggled to achieve significant mindshare in a crowded market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awareness - knowledge or perception of a situation or fact
  • synonym: brand recognition - how easily a brand is identified by consumers
  • antonym: market share - the portion of sales a company has in a market
  • antonym: obscurity - the state of being unknown or not noticed
  • synonym: visibility - the degree to which something is seen or noticed by the public
