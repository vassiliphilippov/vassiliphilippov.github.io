doorstep

noun
step or sill at the base of a doorway
1.0 4.94/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd…îrÀåst…õp / (AmE), / Ààd…îÀêÀåst…õp / (BrE)
the step or sill at the base of a doorway, forming the threshold you cross to enter or exit

Examples

The doorstep outside
After the rainstorm, muddy footprints were all over the doorstep.
He paused on the doorstep before entering his childhood home.
She left the package on the doorstep for her neighbor to find.

Synonyms

  • doorsill
    3.7 0.07/m
    doorsill is the horizontal bottom piece that supports the doorway frame, while doorstep is the step you stand on to enter.
  • threshold
    3.3 3.73/m
    threshold is the strip or entrance at the base of a doorway you cross to enter, while doorstep is the step you stand on.
