chief

adjective
most important; main
3.2 64/m
Pronunciation: / t Éif / (AmE), / t ÉiÀêf / (BrE)
most important; main; having the highest priority or rank among a group or set

Examples

Chief reason
Cost is the chief reason why they decided not to attend the event.
Her chief responsibility is to oversee the entire project.
The chief concern of the committee was the safety of the children.
