mustache

noun
hair grown on the upper lip
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌs.tæʃ /; / məˈstæʃ / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.tɑːʃ /; / ˈmʌstæʃ / (BrE)
A growth of hair on the upper lip, especially when deliberately grown and groomed.

Examples

A thick mustache
After he shaved off his mustache, many people barely recognized him.
He decided to grow a mustache for Movember and raised awareness for men's health.
Her father always kept his mustache neatly trimmed and well-groomed.
After accidentally trimming his moustache too short, he considered shaving it off completely.
He decided to grow a moustache for Movember to support men's health awareness.
Her grandfather's thick white moustache made him look very distinguished.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beard - hair grown on the chin, cheeks, and jaw
  • antonym: clean-shaven - having no facial hair
