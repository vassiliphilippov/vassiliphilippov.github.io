unburdened

adjective
free from burdens or worries
2.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɝːdnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɜːdnd / (BrE)
Not having or being weighed down by difficulties, responsibilities, or worries.

Examples

Unburdened by worries
After paying off her debts, she felt completely unburdened for the first time in years.
He entered the new job unburdened by prior experience or preconceived notions.
Their laughter was light and unburdened as they walked along the beach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: burdened - weighed down with a load, duty, or difficulty
