numerology

noun
study of mystical significance of numbers
Pronunciation: / ˌnuːməˈrɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌnjuːməˈrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the belief in or study of the mystical significance and influence of numbers on human life and events
