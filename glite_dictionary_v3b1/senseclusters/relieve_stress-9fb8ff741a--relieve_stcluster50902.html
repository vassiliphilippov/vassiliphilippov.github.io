relieve stress

verb
reduce internal mechanical stress in materials

(in materials science and engineering) to reduce internal or residual mechanical stress in a solid material (for example metal or glass), typically by heat treatment or controlled processing.

Examples

To relieve stress in metal
After welding, the technician heat-treated the frame to relieve stress and prevent future cracks.
Engineers often use vibration aging to relieve stress in assembled components before final testing.
The manufacturer reported that annealing successfully relieved stress in the metal shafts after forming.
