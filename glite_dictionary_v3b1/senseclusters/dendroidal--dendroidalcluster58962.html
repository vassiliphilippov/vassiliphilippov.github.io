dendroidal

adjective
relating to dendroidal sets (mathematics)
9.9
Pronunciation: /den.ˈdrɔɪ.dəl/ (AmE), /den.ˈdrɔɪ.dəl/ (BrE)
(in mathematics, especially category theory) relating to dendroidal sets or structures: tree-like combinatorial objects used to define or model operads and higher-categorical algebra.

Examples

Dendroidal set theory
Recent papers show that dendroidal sets generalize simplicial sets for modelling operads.
She studied the homotopy theory of dendroidal objects during her PhD at Cambridge.
A seminar introduced students to dendroidal categories and their role in higher algebra.
