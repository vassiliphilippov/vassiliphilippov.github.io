clarifying

adjective
making something clear
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈklær.əˌfaɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɑː.rɪˌfaɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

Clarifying purpose
A good diagram often serves a clarifying purpose.
