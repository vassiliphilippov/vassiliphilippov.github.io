protohuman

noun
early human ancestor
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtoʊˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəʊˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
an early ancestor of modern humans, not yet fully evolved into Homo sapiens

Examples

A protohuman fossil
Scientists believe that the protohuman species walked upright but still had many ape-like features.
The museum displayed a reconstructed skull of a protohuman found in East Africa.
Researchers study protohuman fossils to understand the evolution of modern humans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hominid - a member of the biological family that includes humans and their ancestors
  • degree higher: human - a member of the species Homo sapiens
