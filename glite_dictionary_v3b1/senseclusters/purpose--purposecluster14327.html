purpose

noun
reason for doing something; intended goal
1.6 152/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ùÀê.p…ôs /; / Ààp…ùÀêp…ôs / (AmE), / Ààp…úÀê.p…ôs /; / Ààp…úÀêp…ôs / (BrE)
the reason why something is done, made, or exists; the intended use or aim

Examples

The purpose of a tool
Each tool in the kit has a specific purpose and should be used accordingly.
