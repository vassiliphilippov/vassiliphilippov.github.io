purpose

noun
reason for doing something; intended goal
1.0 1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝː.pəs /; / ˈpɝːpəs / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.pəs /; / ˈpɜːpəs / (BrE)
the reason why something is done, made, or exists; the intended use or aim

Examples
