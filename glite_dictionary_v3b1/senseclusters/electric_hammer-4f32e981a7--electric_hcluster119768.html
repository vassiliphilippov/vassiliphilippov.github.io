electric hammer

noun
power tool hammer with electric motor
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈhæmɚ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈhamə / (BrE)
A hammer operated by an electric motor, used for driving nails or breaking surfaces.

Examples

An electric hammer tool
