fuel system

noun
system supplying fuel in vehicles or aircraft
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːl ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈfjʊːl ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
The system in a motor vehicle or aircraft that stores and supplies fuel to the engine.

Examples

The fuel system in cars
A problem in the fuel system can lead to decreased performance or even engine failure.
Modern cars rely on an electronic fuel system to maximize efficiency.
The mechanic inspected the fuel system to find the cause of the engine trouble.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fuel injection - the process or system of introducing fuel into an engine
