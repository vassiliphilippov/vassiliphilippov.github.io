immunocompetence

noun
ability to mount an immune response
Pronunciation: / ɪˌmjunəˈkɑmpətəns / (AmE), / ɪˌmjuːnəʊˈkɒmpɪtəns / (BrE)
the ability of the body to produce a normal immune response following exposure to an antigen

Examples

Body immunocompetence level
Declining immunocompetence in the elderly increases their susceptibility to infections.
Genetic disorders may result in a lack of immunocompetence in affected individuals.
The vaccine was used to assess the immunocompetence of the study participants.

Related Terms

  • antonym: immunodeficiency - an impaired ability to mount an immune response
