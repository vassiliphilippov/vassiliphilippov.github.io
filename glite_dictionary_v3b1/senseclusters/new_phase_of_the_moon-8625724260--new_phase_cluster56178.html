new phase of the moon

noun
first appearance of waxing crescent Moon
Pronunciation: / nuː feɪz əv ðə muːn / (AmE), / njuː feɪz əv ðə muːn / (BrE)
the time when the Moon appears as a narrow waxing crescent, marking the beginning of a new lunar cycle

Examples

The new phase of the moon begins

Many cultures celebrate the new phase of the moon with special rituals and traditions.
The new phase of the moon is an important marker in the lunar calendar.
