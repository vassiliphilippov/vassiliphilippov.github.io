appreciative

adjective
feeling or showing gratitude
Pronunciation: / əˈpriʃətɪv /; / əˈpriːʃətɪv / (AmE), / əˈpriːʃətɪv / (BrE)
feeling or showing gratitude or recognizing the value of something

Examples

An appreciative audience
An appreciative audience applauded the performers warmly.
She was very appreciative of their help during her illness.
The manager sent an appreciative email thanking the team for their hard work.
He wrote an appreciative letter to his mentor, thanking her for her support.
She smiled with an appreciative nod as she accepted the gift.
The audience gave an appreciative round of applause after the performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grateful - feeling or showing thanks for something received
  • antonym: unappreciative - not showing appreciation or gratitude
