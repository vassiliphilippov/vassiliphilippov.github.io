disjointed speech

noun
incoherent speech; hard to follow
7.7 6.2
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ˈdʒɔɪn.tɪd spiːtʃ/ (AmE), /dɪs.ˈdʒɔɪn.tɪd spiːtʃ/ (BrE)
speech or verbal expression that is lacking in coherence or logical connection, making it difficult to follow

Examples

Disjointed speech after accident
After the accident, he displayed disjointed speech and could not finish his sentences.
Her disjointed speech made it clear she was under a lot of stress.
The defendant's disjointed speech confused the jury during the trial.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incoherent speech - speech that cannot be understood easily due to lack of logical flow
