bilious

adjective
irritable or bad-tempered
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪliəs / (AmE), / ˈbɪliəs / (BrE)
Feeling or showing a bad temper or irritability, especially as if affected by indigestion.

Examples

Bilious mood at dinner
His bilious remarks made the dinner conversation tense and uncomfortable.
She grew bilious after a long day of petty annoyances and disappointments.
The critic's bilious review insulted both the actors and their audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amiable - friendly and pleasant in manner
  • synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or angered
