mushroom

noun
edible fleshy fungus body
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌʃˌruːm /; / ˈmʌʃˌruːm, ˈmʌʃˌrʊm / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃ.rʊm /; / ˈmʌʃ.ruːm /; / ˈmʌʃruːm /; / ˈmʌʃrʊm, ˈmʌʃruːm / (BrE)
a type of fleshy fungus, usually with a cap and stalk, that grows in soil or decaying matter; some are edible, some are not

Related Terms

  • synonym: fungus - any of a group of spore-producing organisms feeding on organic matter
  • antonym: toadstool - a poisonous or inedible fungus with a stalk and cap
