scorper

noun
engraving tool for scooping broad areas
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔrpər / (AmE), / ˈskɔːpə / (BrE)
A tool with a curved blade used by engravers to scoop out broad areas of wood or metal.

Examples

A scorper tool
A sharp scorper is essential for smoothing broad surfaces in metal engraving.
She carefully selected the right scorper for the delicate work on her print.
The engraver used a scorper to remove large sections of wood from the block.
