affectedly

adverb
in an artificial or pretentious way
Pronunciation: / əˈfɛk.tɪd.li / (AmE), / əˈfɛk.tɪd.li / (BrE)
In an artificial or pretentious manner, not naturally or genuinely.

Examples

Speak affectedly
He spoke affectedly, using words that felt unnatural in the conversation.
She smiled affectedly at her guests, trying to hide her discomfort.
The critic noted that the actor moved affectedly across the stage.

Related Terms
