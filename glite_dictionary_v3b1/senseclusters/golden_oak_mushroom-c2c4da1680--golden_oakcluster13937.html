golden oak mushroom

noun
edible East Asian mushroom with golden or dark cap
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən oʊk ˈmʌʃˌrum / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən əʊk ˈmʌʃruːm / (BrE)
an edible East Asian mushroom with a golden to dark brown cap and an inedible stipe, often used in cooking
