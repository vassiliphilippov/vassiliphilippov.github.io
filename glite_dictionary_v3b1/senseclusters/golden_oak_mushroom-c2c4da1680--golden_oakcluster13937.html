golden oak mushroom

noun
edible East Asian mushroom with golden or dark cap
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən oʊk ˈmʌʃˌrum / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən əʊk ˈmʌʃruːm / (BrE)
an edible East Asian mushroom with a golden to dark brown cap and an inedible stipe, often used in cooking

Examples

Dried golden oak mushroom
Dried golden oak mushroom is often used in East Asian soups and stews.
She bought a pack of golden oak mushrooms from the Asian grocery store.
The chef added fresh golden oak mushroom to the stir-fry for a rich, savory flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shiitake - an edible mushroom (Lentinula edodes), commonly used in East Asian cuisine
