denigratory

adjective
expressing disparagement or criticism
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛnɪɡəˌtɔri / (AmE), / dɪˈnɪɡrəˌtəri / (BrE)
(of a comment, statement, or attitude) expressing criticism or disparagement; tending to unfairly discredit or malign someone or something.

Examples

Denigratory comments
His denigratory comments damaged the reputation of the company.
She was hurt by the denigratory tone of his feedback.
The article contained several denigratory remarks about her professional abilities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: complimentary - expressing praise or admiration
  • synonym: disparaging - expressing the opinion that something is of little worth
