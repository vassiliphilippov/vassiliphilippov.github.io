family Ginkgoaceae

noun
plant family including Ginkgo and extinct relatives
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfæmɪli ˌɡɪŋkoʊˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ˌfæmɪli ˌɡɪŋkəʊˈeɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
A family of gymnosperm plants constituting the order Ginkgoales, which includes the living genus Ginkgo and related extinct forms.
