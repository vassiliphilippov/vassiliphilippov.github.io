hit man

noun
a male professional assassin for hire
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪt mæn / (AmE), / ˈhɪt mæn / (BrE)
a man who is hired as a professional killer, typically using a gun, especially by a criminal organization

Examples

A hit man with a gun
In police dramas, the character of the hit man often lurks in the shadows.
She was shocked to learn that her neighbor was a hit man for the mob.
The notorious gangster hired a hit man to eliminate his rival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assassin - a person who murders someone for political or religious reasons, especially for payment
  • synonym: killer - a person who kills, especially someone who kills in a deliberate and unlawful manner
