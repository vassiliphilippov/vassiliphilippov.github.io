hit man

noun
a male professional assassin for hire
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪt mæn / (AmE), / ˈhɪt mæn / (BrE)
a man who is hired as a professional killer, typically using a gun, especially by a criminal organization

The notorious gangster hired a hit man to eliminate his rival.
