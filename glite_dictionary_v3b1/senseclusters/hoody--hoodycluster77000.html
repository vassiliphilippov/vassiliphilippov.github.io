hoody

noun
hooded sweatshirt
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊdi / (AmE), / ˈhʊdi / (BrE)
a casual sweatshirt with a hood attached, often worn for warmth or comfort

Examples

A warm hoody
He pulled his hoody over his head when it started to rain.
Many students wear a hoody to stay comfortable during class.
She bought a new hoody with her favorite team's logo on it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoodie - a sweatshirt with a hood
  • antonym: jacket - a short coat worn on the upper body
  • synonym: sweatshirt - a long-sleeved, pullover shirt made of thick cotton
