lipa

noun
Croatian currency unit
Pronunciation: / ˈliːpə / (AmE), / ˈliːpə / (BrE)
a unit of currency in Croatia, equal to one hundredth of a kuna

Examples

A lipa coin
The price of a loaf of bread was fifty lipa in the local market.
He found a few lipa coins at the bottom of his pocket after his trip to Croatia.
Tourists often collect lipa as souvenirs from their visit to Croatia.

Related Terms
