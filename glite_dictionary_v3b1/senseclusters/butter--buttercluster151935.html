butter

noun
edible fat made from cream or milk
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
a pale yellow edible fatty substance made by churning cream or milk, used in cooking and as a spread

Examples

A slice of butter
Melt the butter in a pan and sauté the onions until golden.
She spread butter on her toast before adding jam.
The chef uses fresh butter in all his pastry recipes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: margarine - a butter substitute made from vegetable oils
