digitalis glycoside

noun
compound from foxglove used as a heart stimulant
6.5
Pronunciation: / dɪdʒɪˈtælɪs ˈɡlaɪkəˌsaɪd / (AmE), / dɪdʒɪˈtælɪs ˈɡlɪkəsiːd / (BrE)
any of a group of compounds derived from the foxglove plant, used as powerful stimulants for the heart in medical treatment
