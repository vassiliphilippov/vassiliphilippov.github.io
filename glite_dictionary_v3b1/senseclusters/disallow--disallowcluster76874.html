Synonyms

    3.3 14/m
    prohibit is an official rule or law that stops actions, while disallow is a general refusal to permit or accept something.
  • forbid
    3.0 10/m
    forbid is a direct order someone gives to stop an action, while disallow is often an official rule refusing permission.
  • ban
    1.4 20/m
    ban is an official public law or rule that bars something, while disallow is a formal refusal to permit or accept it.
  • proscribe
    8.6 0.4/m
    proscribe is a formal legal ban often naming forbidden acts, while disallow is a broader refusal to permit or accept something.
  • interdict
    9.4 0.2/m

  • reject
    1.9 40/m

  • deny
    1.6 20/m
