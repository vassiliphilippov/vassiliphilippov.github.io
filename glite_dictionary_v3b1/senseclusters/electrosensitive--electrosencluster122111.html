electrosensitive

adjective
sensitive to electricity
8.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈsɛnsətɪv / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈsɛnsətɪv / (BrE)
sensitive or responsive to electric fields or electromagnetic radiation

Examples

Electrosensitive animals
Certain animals are electrosensitive and can detect electric fields in their environment.
Some people believe they are electrosensitive and experience discomfort near electronic devices.
The new sensor is highly electrosensitive, allowing it to detect even weak electrical signals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electromagnetic - relating to electric and magnetic fields
  • antonym: insensitive - not affected by or responsive to something
