topcoat

noun
final protective layer
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑpˌkoʊt / (AmE), / ˈtɒpˌkəʊt / (BrE)
a final layer of paint or finish applied to a surface to protect it or improve its appearance

Examples

Apply a topcoat
After sanding, she applied a topcoat to the table for a glossy finish.
The car's topcoat protects the paint from scratches and weather damage.
Be sure to let the topcoat dry completely before using the furniture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: finish - a final coating or surface treatment
  • antonym: undercoat - a preparatory layer of paint applied before the topcoat
  • synonym: varnish - a clear protective finish applied to surfaces
