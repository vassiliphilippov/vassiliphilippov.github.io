affective

adjective
relating to emotions or feelings
Pronunciation: / əˈfɛktɪv / (AmE), / əˈfɛktɪv / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by emotions or feelings, especially as distinguished from thought or action.

Examples

Affective feelings
His poetry is known for its affective power, evoking deep feelings in the reader.
The film had a strong affective impact, moving many viewers to tears.
Therapists often address both affective and behavioral symptoms in their patients.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cognitive - relating to mental processes of knowing, opposite of affective
  • synonym: emotional - relating to emotion
