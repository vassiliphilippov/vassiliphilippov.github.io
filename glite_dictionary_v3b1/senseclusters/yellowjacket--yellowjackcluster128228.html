yellowjacket

noun
striped stinging wasp
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
a small, aggressive wasp with yellow and black stripes, known for its painful sting

Examples

A yellowjacket sting
A yellowjacket stung him while he was eating outside.
We found a yellowjacket nest under the porch and called pest control.
Be careful when drinking soda outdoors, as yellowjackets are attracted to sweet smells.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bee - a flying insect that makes honey and can sting
  • synonym: hornet - a large, stinging wasp with a painful sting
  • synonym: wasp - a stinging insect related to bees and ants
