cooling

noun
process of becoming cooler
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkuːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of becoming cooler or causing something to become cooler; a decrease in temperature

Examples

Air cooling process
Modern buildings use advanced systems for air cooling during summer.
Rapid cooling of the metal strengthens its structure.
The cooling of the engine is crucial to prevent overheating.
