too clever by half

adjective
excessively clever, to a fault
7.5
Pronunciation: / tuː ˈklɛvər baɪ hæf / (AmE), / tuː ˈklɛvə baɪ hɑːf / (BrE)
displaying excessive cleverness or ingenuity, which often leads to negative consequences or annoyance

Examples

Too clever by half argument
His argument was too clever by half and ended up undermining his own position.
Many found her too clever by half, constantly pointing out mistakes in others' work.
She devised a too clever by half solution that confused everyone and failed to solve the problem.
