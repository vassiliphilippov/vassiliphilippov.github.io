lol

x
internet laughter
Pronunciation: / ɛl oʊ ɛl / (AmE), / ɛl əʊ ɛl / (BrE)
an informal written abbreviation for 'laughing out loud', used online to show amusement or laughter

Examples

Type lol online
He sent me a meme that made me lol at my desk.
Whenever something is really funny in the group chat, everyone just types lol.
She replied with lol to show she found the comment amusing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rofl - internet slang for 'rolling on the floor laughing'
