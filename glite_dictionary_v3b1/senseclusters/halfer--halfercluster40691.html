halfer

noun
half marathon runner
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhælfər / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfə / (BrE)
a person who runs or competes in a half marathon, a race of about 21.1 kilometers or 13.1 miles

Examples

Halfer finishes strong
After months of training, he proudly called himself a halfer when he finished his first race.
Many halfers gather at the starting line early in the morning, ready for the challenge.
She became a halfer after completing the 13.1-mile event in her hometown.
