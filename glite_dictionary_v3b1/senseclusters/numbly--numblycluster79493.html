numbly

adverb
without feeling or emotion
5.9 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈnʌm.lɪ/ /; / /ˈnʌm.blɪ/ (AmE), /ˈnʌm.lɪ/ /; / /ˈnʌm.blɪ/ (BrE)
In a way that shows or suggests lack of physical or emotional feeling.

Examples

Sit numbly still
After hearing the news, he sat numbly at his desk, unable to react.
She walked numbly through the empty house, barely noticing her surroundings.
The crowd listened numbly as the announcement was made, too shocked to respond.
