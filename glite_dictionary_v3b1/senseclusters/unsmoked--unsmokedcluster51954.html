unsmoked

adjective
not smoked (food)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsmoʊkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈsməʊkt / (BrE)
not treated, preserved, or flavored by exposure to smoke, especially in food preparation

Examples

Unsmoked cheese
She always buys unsmoked cheese because she prefers a milder flavor.
The recipe calls for unsmoked bacon to avoid an overpowering taste.
Many people choose unsmoked fish for a lighter, more natural taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fresh - not preserved or processed
  • antonym: smoked - treated or flavored by exposure to smoke
