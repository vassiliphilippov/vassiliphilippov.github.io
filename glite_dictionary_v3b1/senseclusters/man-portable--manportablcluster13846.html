man-portable

adjective
able to be carried by one person
9.9
Pronunciation: / mæn ˈpɔrtəbəl / (AmE), / mæn ˈpɔːtəbəl / (BrE)
(of equipment) light or compact enough to be carried by a single person.

Examples

A man-portable generator
Engineers designed a man-portable generator for remote expeditions.
Soldiers carried man-portable radios during the mission to maintain communication.
The new missile system is man-portable and can be deployed quickly on the battlefield.
