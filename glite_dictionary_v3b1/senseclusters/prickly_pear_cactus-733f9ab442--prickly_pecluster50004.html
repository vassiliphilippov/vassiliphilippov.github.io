prickly pear cactus

noun
a cactus with flat, spiny joints and edible fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪkli pɛr ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈprɪkli peə ˈkæktəs / (BrE)
A cactus with flat, spiny segments and oval, often edible fruit, especially of the genus Opuntia, found in warm regions.

Examples

The prickly pear cactus thrives in arid regions and is commonly found in the deserts of the Americas.
