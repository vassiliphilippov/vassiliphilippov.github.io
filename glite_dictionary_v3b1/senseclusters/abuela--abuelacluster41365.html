abuela

noun
respectful address for elder
Pronunciation: / aˈbweɪlə / (AmE), / aˈbweɪlə / (BrE)
a respectful term used to address an older woman, especially in Spanish-speaking cultures, regardless of family relation

Examples

Hello, abuela
The children greeted their neighbor with a warm 'Good morning, abuela,' showing their respect.
In her community, everyone called the kind elderly woman abuela even though she was not related to them.
When visiting the market, Maria always addressed the vendor as abuela out of courtesy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abuelo - a respectful term for an older man or grandfather in Spanish
  • synonym: grandmother - the mother of one's parent
