Parkia javanica

noun
a Southeast Asian ornamental rainforest tree
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrki.ə dʒɑːˈvænɪkə / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɪə dʒɑːˈvɑːnɪkə / (BrE)
a tall evergreen rainforest tree native to Southeast Asia, with wide-spreading crown and yellow-white flowers, often grown as an ornamental in parks and large gardens
