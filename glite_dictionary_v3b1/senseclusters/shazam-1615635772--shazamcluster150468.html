Shazam

proper noun
music identification app
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈzæm / (AmE), / ʃəˈzæm / (BrE)
a mobile app and service that identifies songs by listening to short audio samples

Examples

Open Shazam app
She opened Shazam to find out the name of the song playing in the store.
Whenever he hears a new track on the radio, he quickly uses Shazam to identify it.
Many people rely on Shazam to discover music they like but do not know.
