nonbeliever

noun

Examples

Synonyms

  • unbeliever
    5.4 1.59/m

  • disbeliever
    5.5 0.41/m

  • skeptic
    4.1 4.28/m
    a skeptic questions and doubts claims and seeks proof, while nonbeliever simply means someone who does not accept a belief.
