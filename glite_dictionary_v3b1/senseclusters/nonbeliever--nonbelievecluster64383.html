nonbeliever

noun
person who does not believe
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.bɪˈliː.vɚ / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.bɪˈliː.və / (BrE)
a person who does not believe in a particular idea, religion, or divinity; an unbeliever or skeptic

Examples

A nonbeliever in religion
As a nonbeliever, she often questioned traditional customs and rituals.
Despite their differences, the nonbeliever and the religious leader found common ground in community service.
The nonbeliever quietly listened to the sermon without participating in the prayers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: believer - a person who believes, especially in a religious sense
  • synonym: skeptic - a person inclined to doubt or question accepted opinions
