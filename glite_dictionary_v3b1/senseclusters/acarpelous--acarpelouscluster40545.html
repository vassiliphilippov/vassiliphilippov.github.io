acarpelous

adjective
having no carpels (botany)
Pronunciation: / əˈkɑr.pə.ləs / (AmE), / əˈkɑː.pɪ.ləs / (BrE)
(of a plant or flower) lacking carpels (the female reproductive organs of a flower).

Examples

Acarpelous flower
Researchers discovered an acarpelous variant during the field survey.
The flowers of this species are acarpelous, producing no fruit after blooming.
Unlike most members of its family, the plant is completely acarpelous.
