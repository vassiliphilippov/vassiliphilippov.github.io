proliferant

adjective
increasing rapidly
Pronunciation: / prəˈlɪfərənt / (AmE), / prəˈlɪfərənt / (BrE)
tending to increase rapidly in number or to spread quickly; causing or characterized by proliferation

Examples

Proliferant plants
The proliferant weeds took over the entire garden within a few weeks.
Scientists are concerned about the proliferant nature of certain bacteria in hospital settings.
After the policy change, proliferant rumors spread quickly throughout the office.
