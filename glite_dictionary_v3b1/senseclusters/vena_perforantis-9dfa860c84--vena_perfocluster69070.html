vena perforantis

noun
vein accompanying a perforating artery in the leg
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə pɜːr.fə.ˈræn.tɪs/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə pə.ˈfɔː.rən.tɪs/ (BrE)
A vein that accompanies a perforating artery, draining muscles of the leg and emptying into the deep femoral vein.

Examples

A vena perforantis vein
Damage to a vena perforantis can lead to complications such as leg swelling and venous insufficiency.
Surgeons carefully identify the vena perforantis during procedures involving the deep femoral vein.
The vena perforantis plays a crucial role in the circulation within the lower limbs.
