husbandwoman

noun
female farmer or farm manager
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌzbəndˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈhʌzbəndˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who manages or works on a farm; a female farmer.

Examples

A husbandwoman on farm
Although rare today, the term husbandwoman was once used for female farmers in England.
In the old village records, a husbandwoman is listed as the owner of the property.
The play depicted a hardworking husbandwoman tending her crops through every season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: husbandman - a person (typically a man) who operates a farm
