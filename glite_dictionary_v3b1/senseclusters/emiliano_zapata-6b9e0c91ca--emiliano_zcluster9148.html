Emiliano Zapata

proper noun
Mexican revolutionary leader (1879–1919)
Pronunciation: / eɪˌmiːliˈɑːnoʊ zəˈpɑːtə / (AmE), / eɪˌmiːliˈɑːnə zəˈpɑːtə / (BrE)
