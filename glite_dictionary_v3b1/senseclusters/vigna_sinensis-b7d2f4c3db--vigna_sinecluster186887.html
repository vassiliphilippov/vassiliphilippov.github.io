Vigna sinensis

noun
annual legume; cowpea plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪɡnə saɪˈnɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˈvɪɡnə sɪˈnɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A sprawling annual plant of the Old World, cultivated especially in the southern United States for its edible seeds and use as forage and green manure.
