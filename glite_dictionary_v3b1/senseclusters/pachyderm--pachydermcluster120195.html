pachyderm

noun
large, thick-skinned mammal
Pronunciation: / ˈpækɪˌdɝm / (AmE), / ˈpækɪˌdɜːm / (BrE)
a large, thick-skinned mammal such as an elephant, rhinoceros, or hippopotamus

Examples

Large pachyderm animal
