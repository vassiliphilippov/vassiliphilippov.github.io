matrilateral

noun
maternal relative
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmætrɪˈlætərəl / (AmE), / ˌmætrɪˈlætərəl / (BrE)
a person who is related to someone through their mother or the maternal line

Examples

A matrilateral cousin
At the family gathering, she met a matrilateral cousin for the first time.
Genealogists often trace both patrilateral and matrilateral relatives to build a complete family tree.
He discovered that his matrilateral ancestors came from a different region than his father's family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maternal - related to the mother
  • antonym: patrilateral - a person related through the father or paternal line
