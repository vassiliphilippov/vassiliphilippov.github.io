psychic phenomena

noun
supernatural mental events
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪkɪk fəˈnɑmɪnə / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkɪk fəˈnɒmɪnə / (BrE)
Events or occurrences that seem to defy the known laws of physics and may be attributed to mental or supernatural processes.

Examples

Psychic phenomena events
Scientists remain skeptical about the existence of psychic phenomena due to the lack of empirical evidence.
Some people claim to have witnessed psychic phenomena such as telepathy and clairvoyance.
The study of psychic phenomena includes topics like precognition and psychokinesis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paranormal - beyond the range of normal experience or scientific explanation
  • synonym: supernatural - attributed to forces beyond scientific understanding or the laws of nature
