shadowlike

adjective
resembling a shadow
Pronunciation: / ˈʃædoʊˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈʃædəʊˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of a shadow, such as being dark, faint, or indistinct

Examples

Shadowlike figure
A shadowlike figure moved quietly across the room.
The mist gave the trees a shadowlike appearance in the early morning light.
He noticed a shadowlike shape flicker at the edge of his vision.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bright - full of light; shining strongly
  • synonym: shadowy - dim, indistinct, or full of shadows
