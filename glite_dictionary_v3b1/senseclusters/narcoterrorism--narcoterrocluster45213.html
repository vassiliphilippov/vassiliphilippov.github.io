narcoterrorism

noun
terrorism funded by drug trafficking
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːrkoʊˈtɛrərɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌnɑːkəʊˈtɛrərɪzəm / (BrE)

Examples

Narcoterrorism funds terrorism
Experts warn that narcoterrorism poses a significant threat to national security.
Recent reports link narcoterrorism to several violent incidents in the region.
The government launched a special task force to combat narcoterrorism along the border.
