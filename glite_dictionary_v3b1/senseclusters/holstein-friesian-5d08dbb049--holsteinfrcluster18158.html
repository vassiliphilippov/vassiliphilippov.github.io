Holstein-Friesian

noun
breed of dairy cattle from Holland
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊl.stiːn ˈfriː.ʒən/ /; / /ˈhoʊl.stiːn ˈfriː.zi.ən/ /; / /ˈhoʊl.staɪn ˈfriː.ʒən/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊl.stiːn ˈfriː.ʒən/ /; / /ˈhəʊl.stiːn ˈfriː.zi.ən/ (BrE)
