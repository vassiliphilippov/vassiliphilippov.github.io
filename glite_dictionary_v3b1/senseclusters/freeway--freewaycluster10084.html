freeway

noun
broad, high-speed highway
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈfriːweɪ / (BrE)
A wide road designed for fast-moving traffic, usually with no intersections or traffic signals.

Examples

A freeway road
Heavy rain caused an accident on the freeway this morning.
The city built a new freeway to reduce traffic in residential areas.
We took the freeway and arrived much faster than usual.

Related Terms

  • synonym: motorway - A similar broad highway in British English
