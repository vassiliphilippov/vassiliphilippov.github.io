mortifyingly

adverb
embarrassingly
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔːrtɪˌfaɪɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈmɔːtɪˌfaɪɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes great embarrassment or shame

Examples

Mortifyingly embarrassed
She realized mortifyingly late that her shirt was inside out during the meeting.
He was mortifyingly unaware of the mistake he had made in front of everyone.
The video of her fall was mortifyingly shared across social media.
