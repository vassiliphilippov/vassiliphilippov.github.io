countertenor

noun
male singer with a high voice (above tenor)
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.tɚˌtɛn.ɚ /; / ˈkaʊntɚˌtɛnɚ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.təˌtɛn.ə /; / ˈkaʊntətɛnə / (BrE)
a male singer with a singing voice higher than a tenor, usually similar to an alto or contralto
