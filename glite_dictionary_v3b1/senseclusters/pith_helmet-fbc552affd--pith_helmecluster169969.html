pith helmet

noun
lightweight sun hat worn in hot climates
Pronunciation: / pɪθ ˈhɛlmət / (AmE), / pɪθ ˈhɛlmɪt / (BrE)
a lightweight, hard sun hat made from pith or similar material, traditionally worn in hot climates for protection from the sun
