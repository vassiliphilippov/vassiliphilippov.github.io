larch

noun
deciduous coniferous tree (Larix genus)
7.8
Pronunciation: / lɑrtʃ / (AmE), / lɑːtʃ / (BrE)
a deciduous coniferous tree of the genus Larix, with needle-like leaves that are shed annually

Examples

A larch tree
In autumn, the needles of the larch turn golden before shedding.
Many cabins are built using tough, durable larch wood.
The old larch by the lake has grown steadily for decades.

  • synonym: fir - an evergreen conifer of the genus Abies
