flagger

noun
person reporting content
Pronunciation: / ˈflæɡər / (AmE), / ˈflæɡə / (BrE)
a person who marks online content as inappropriate or problematic so that moderators can review it

Examples

Online flagger
The flagger quickly reported the spam message to keep the forum safe.
Each flagger helps moderators by identifying posts that break the rules.
Without a dedicated flagger, inappropriate content might go unnoticed for days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moderator - a person who oversees and manages online discussions
  • synonym: reporter - a person who reports inappropriate content online
