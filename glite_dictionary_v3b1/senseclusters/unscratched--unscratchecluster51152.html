unscratched

adjective
without scratches
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskrætʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈskrætʃt / (BrE)
not having any scratches or marks on the surface; completely unmarked

Examples

Unscratched surface

He was relieved to find his car unscratched after the storm.
She carefully packed her laptop to keep it unscratched during travel.
