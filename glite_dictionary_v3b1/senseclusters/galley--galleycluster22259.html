galley

noun
kitchen area on an aircraft or ship
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæli / (AmE), / ˈɡæli /; / ˈɡali / (BrE)
the kitchen area on a ship, aircraft, or train where food is prepared

Examples

Ship galley kitchen
Access to the galley is restricted to crew members only.
The galley was fully stocked with snacks and drinks for the long-haul journey.
The flight attendant prepared the meals in the galley during the flight.
After the storm, the galley was in disarray but the crew needed hot food.
On the yacht, the compact galley was equipped with a small stove and sink.
The chef prepared several meals each day in the galley below deck.
