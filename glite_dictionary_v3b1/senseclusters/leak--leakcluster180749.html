leak

noun
hole or crack for escape of substance
2.7
Pronunciation: / lik / (AmE), / liːk / (BrE)
a hole or crack that lets liquid, gas, or light escape or enter; or the escape of liquid or gas through such an opening

Examples

A gas leak
After the accident, there was a dangerous gas leak in the building.
Light was streaming in through a leak in the tent flap during the night.
The plumber found a leak under the kitchen sink and fixed it quickly.
A small leak in the boat caused water to seep in slowly overnight.
If you smell gas, there could be a dangerous leak in the pipeline.
The plumber arrived quickly to fix the leak under the kitchen sink.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hole - an opening through something
  • synonym: spill - an amount of liquid that has accidentally flowed out of its container
