Euskera

noun
Basque language
Pronunciation: / eusˈkeɾa / (AmE), / eusˈkeɾa / (BrE)
the Basque language, spoken in the Basque Country region of Spain and France

Examples

Euskera language
Many schools in the Basque Country offer classes in Euskera alongside Spanish.
He is learning Euskera to better connect with his Basque heritage.
Official documents in the region are often available in both Euskera and Spanish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Basque - the language spoken by the Basque people
  • antonym: French - the Romance language spoken in France and other countries
  • antonym: Spanish - the Romance language spoken in Spain and Latin America
