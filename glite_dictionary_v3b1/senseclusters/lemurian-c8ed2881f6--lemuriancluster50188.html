Lemurian

adjective
of Lemuria or its culture
Pronunciation: / lɪˈmjʊriən / (AmE), / lɪˈmjʊəriən / (BrE)
relating to Lemuria, its supposed people, culture, or the beliefs and traditions associated with the lost continent, especially in occult or New Age contexts

Lemurian civilization

Artifacts labeled as Lemurian are often displayed in exhibitions about lost continents.
