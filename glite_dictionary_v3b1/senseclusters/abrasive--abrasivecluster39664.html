abrasive

adjective
harsh or insensitive (of manner)
3.7 5.2
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈbreɪ.sɪv/ /; / /ə.ˈbreɪ.zɪv/ (AmE), /ə.ˈbreɪ.sɪv/ /; / /ə.ˈbreɪ.zɪv/ (BrE)
