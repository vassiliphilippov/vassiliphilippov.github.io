chapfallen

adjective
dejected; dispirited
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæpˌfɔlən / (AmE), / ˈtʃæpˌfɔːlən / (BrE)
Dejected, dispirited, or brought low in spirit; downcast.

Examples

Look chapfallen
After hearing the disappointing news, she looked utterly chapfallen.
He sat chapfallen in the corner, barely responding to our encouragement.
The team walked off the field, chapfallen and silent after their unexpected defeat.

  • synonym: dejected - feeling sad and dispirited
  • synonym: downcast - looking or feeling sad or discouraged
