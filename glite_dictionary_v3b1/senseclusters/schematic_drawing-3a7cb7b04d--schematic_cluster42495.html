schematic drawing

noun
technical diagram of a system
Pronunciation: / skɪˈmæt̬ɪk ˈdrɔɪŋ / (AmE), / skɪˈmætɪk ˈdrɔːɪŋ / (BrE)
a diagram showing the elements of a system using abstract, graphic symbols rather than realistic pictures, especially for electrical or mechanical systems

Examples

A schematic drawing diagram
Students learned to read a schematic drawing of the new mechanical system in class.
The manual includes a detailed schematic drawing to help with installation.
The technician referred to the schematic drawing before repairing the circuit board.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diagram - a simplified drawing showing the appearance, structure, or workings of something
