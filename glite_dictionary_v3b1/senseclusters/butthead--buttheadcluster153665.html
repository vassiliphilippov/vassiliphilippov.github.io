butthead

noun
slang insult
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈbʌtˌhɛd / (BrE)
an insulting or playful term for a person who is being rude, silly, or annoying

Examples

Calling someone a butthead
Stop being such a butthead and help me with this project.
She laughed and called her brother a butthead after he made a silly joke.
During the game, he jokingly shouted, 'You butthead!' when his friend missed the shot.

Related Terms

  • antonym: friend - a person you like and trust
  • synonym: idiot - a person who acts in a stupid or silly way
  • synonym: jerk - an annoying or unpleasant person
