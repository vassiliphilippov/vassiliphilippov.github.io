Britannia

noun
white metal alloy
8.7
Pronunciation: / brɪˈtæniə / (AmE), / brɪˈtæniə / (BrE)
a white metal alloy similar to pewter, often used for making household items and tableware

Examples

Britannia metal teapot
The antique teapot was made of Britannia, giving it a silvery finish that never rusted.
Collectors often seek out old candlesticks crafted from Britannia for their unique appearance.
She inherited a set of Britannia tableware from her grandmother.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: silver - a shiny, precious metal used for coins and tableware
