clematis

noun
a climbing plant with showy flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈklɛmətɪs, klɪˈmætɪs / (AmE), / ˈklɛmətɪs, klɪˈmætɪs / (BrE)
a climbing plant of the genus Clematis, grown for its showy flowers, often used as an ornamental garden plant

Examples

A clematis plant
She planted a white clematis to add some height and color to her flower bed.
The old arbor is covered with fragrant clematis blossoms every spring.
The purple clematis climbed gracefully over the garden fence, blooming throughout the summer.
