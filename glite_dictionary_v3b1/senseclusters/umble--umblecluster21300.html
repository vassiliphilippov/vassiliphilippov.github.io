umble

noun
animal internal organs
Pronunciation: / ˈʌmbəl / (AmE), / ˈʌmbəl / (BrE)
the edible internal organs of an animal, especially those used in pies in the past

Examples

Umble pie
In medieval times, people often made pies filled with umble for special occasions.
The old recipe called for a mixture of umble and spices baked in a flaky crust.
Few modern cooks are familiar with the use of umble in traditional dishes.
