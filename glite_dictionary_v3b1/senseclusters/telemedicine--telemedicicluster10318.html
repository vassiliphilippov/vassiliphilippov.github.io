telemedicine

noun
remote medical care
4.4
Pronunciation: /tel.ə.ˈme.dɪ.sən/ /; / /tel.ɪ.ˈme.dɪ.sən/ (AmE), /tel.ə.ˈme.dɪ.sən/ /; / /tel.ə.ˈme.dɪ.sɪn/ (BrE)
the practice of providing medical care or consultation to patients remotely using technology such as video calls or online messaging

Related Terms

  • antonym: in-person care - medical care provided face-to-face at a physical location
  • antonym: in-person consultation - a medical appointment where the patient and doctor meet face-to-face
  • synonym: telehealth - the use of digital technology to deliver health-related services and information remotely
