floorplanning

noun
chip layout design
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈflɔːr.plæn.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈflɑːr.plæn.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈflɔː.plæn.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of arranging functional units or modules on an integrated circuit or chip to optimize performance, area, and wiring

Examples

Chip floorplanning
Effective floorplanning can significantly reduce the power consumption of a microchip.
The design team spent weeks on floorplanning to ensure the chip met all performance targets.
Modern software tools assist engineers with automated floorplanning for complex integrated circuits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: placement - the process of positioning components on a chip during design
  • synonym: routing - the process of determining wiring paths between components on a chip
