enterprisingness

noun
quality of being enterprising or resourceful
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.tɚˈpraɪ.zɪŋ.nəs / (AmE), / ˌɛn.təˈpraɪ.zɪŋ.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of showing initiative and willingness to undertake new or challenging projects

Examples

Her enterprisingness showed
Her enterprisingness impressed the board and won her the position of project leader.
The company's growth can be attributed to the enterprisingness of its founder.
Without enterprisingness, many new markets would remain unexplored.

Related Terms

  • antonym: timidity - lack of courage or confidence; shyness
