muted

adjective
(of sound) softened or muffled
4.0 2.13/m
Pronunciation: / Ààmjut…™d / (AmE), / ÀàmjuÀêt…™d / (BrE)
(of sound) made softer, quieter, or less intense than usual; subdued in tone

Examples

Muted voice
She spoke in a muted tone, barely above a whisper.
The music in the restaurant was muted so customers could talk comfortably.
Through the window, we heard the muted sounds of traffic outside.
Her voice was muted, barely louder than a whisper over the phone.
The orchestra played with muted trumpets to create a gentler mood.
Their conversation took on a muted tone when the children were asleep nearby.
