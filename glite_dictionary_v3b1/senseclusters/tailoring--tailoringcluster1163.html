tailoring

noun
personalized digital content
4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈteɪ.lə.rɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈteɪ.lə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
the practice of customizing or personalizing digital content, services, or experiences for individual users, typically using data analysis, algorithms, or AI.

Examples

Digital tailoring
The streaming service emphasizes tailoring recommendations to each viewer's tastes and viewing history.
Effective tailoring of news feeds reduced churn by showing users stories matching their interests.
They invested in AI tools for tailoring product pages based on customers' past purchases and clicks.
