Synonyms

  • elastic
    2.6 1/m
    elastic returns quickly to its original shape after stretching or compression, while springy emphasises a bouncy, spring-like rebound.
  • resilient
    5.0 0.4/m
    resilient recovers its shape after pressure or bending, while springy implies a quicker, bouncier return.
  • springlike
    4.2 0.00/m
    springlike describes something similar to a spring, while springy sounds more common and shows quick, bouncy return.
  • bouncy
    2.0 0.4/m
    bouncy rebounds with lively hops and often describes balls, while springy stresses elastic return after pressure or stretch.
  • stretchy
    1.9 0.5/m
    stretchy can stretch far and then return, often used for cloth, while springy focuses on quick elastic rebound.
  • flexible
    3.4 1/m
    flexible bends or changes shape without breaking, often slowly, while springy implies a quick, spring-like bounce back.
  • lively
    5.4 0.1/m
    lively usually means full of energy for people or events, while springy describes a material's elastic, quick return.
