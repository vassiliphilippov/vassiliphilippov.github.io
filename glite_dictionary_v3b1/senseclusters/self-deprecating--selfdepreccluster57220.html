self-deprecating

adjective
modest or critical about oneself
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf ˈdɛprɪˌkeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌself ˈdɛprɪˌkeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
modest or critical about one’s own abilities or worth; tending to undervalue oneself, often in a humorous or humble way

Examples

Self-deprecating humor
Despite his success, he always remains self-deprecating about his achievements.
He told a self-deprecating joke about his inability to cook.
Her self-deprecating humor made everyone feel at ease during the tense meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low view of one's importance
  • antonym: self-aggrandizing - promoting oneself as powerful or important
