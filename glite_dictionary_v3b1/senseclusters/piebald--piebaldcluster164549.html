piebald

noun
animal with two colors
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpaɪ.bɔːld/ /; / /ˈpaɪ.bɑːld/ (AmE), /ˈpaɪ.bɔːld/ (BrE)
an animal, especially a horse, that has irregular patches of two colors, usually black and white

Examples

A piebald pony
The circus featured a beautiful piebald pony with striking black and white markings.
She adopted a piebald cat whose fur was a mix of white and black patches.
Farmers often admire the unique appearance of a piebald among their herd.
