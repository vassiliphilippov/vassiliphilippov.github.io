macadamia nut

noun
edible seed of the macadamia tree
Pronunciation: / məkəˈdeɪmiə nʌt /; / məˌkædəˈmiːə nʌt / (AmE), / məkəˈdeɪmɪə nʌt /; / məˌkadəˈmiːə nʌt / (BrE)
the edible nut or seed of the macadamia tree, with a sweet, creamy flavor, often used in cooking and baking
