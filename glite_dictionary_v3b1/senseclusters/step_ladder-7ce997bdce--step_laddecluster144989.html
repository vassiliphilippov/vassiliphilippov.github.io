step ladder

noun
portable folding ladder with steps
6.8 2.5
Pronunciation: /step ˈlæd.ər/ (AmE), /step ˈlæd.ə/ (BrE)
a portable ladder consisting of two hinged sections that form an inverted V, typically with flat steps and a platform at the top

Examples

A small step ladder
A small step ladder can be folded and stored easily in a closet.
She used a step ladder to reach the top shelf in the kitchen.
The electrician carefully climbed the step ladder to fix the ceiling light.
