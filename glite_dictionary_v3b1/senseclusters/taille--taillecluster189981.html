taille

noun
waist measurement
Pronunciation: / taɪ / (AmE), / taɪ / (BrE)
a measurement of size, especially the waist measurement used for clothing or tailoring

Examples

Taille measurement

Different brands may use slightly different measurements for the same taille label.
