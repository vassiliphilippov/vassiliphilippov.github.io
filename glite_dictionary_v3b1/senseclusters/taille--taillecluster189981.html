taille

noun
waist measurement
9.9
Pronunciation: / taɪ / (AmE), / taɪ / (BrE)
a measurement of size, especially the waist measurement used for clothing or tailoring

Examples

Taille measurement
She ordered the dress in her usual taille to ensure a perfect fit around her waist.
The tailor asked for his taille before starting to make the suit.
Different brands may use slightly different measurements for the same taille label.

Related Terms

  • synonym: size - the measurement or dimensions of something
  • synonym: waist - the part of the body between the ribs and hips
