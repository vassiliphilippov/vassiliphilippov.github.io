vane

noun
stabilizing fin on a projectile
Pronunciation: / veɪn / (AmE), / veɪn / (BrE)
a fin or blade attached to the tail of an arrow, bomb, or missile to stabilize or guide its flight

Examples

Arrow vane
Each missile was equipped with a small vane at its tail to ensure accuracy.
The arrow's vane helps to keep it stable during flight.
The technician repaired the damaged vane before loading the bomb onto the aircraft.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fin - a thin, flat part that sticks out from a body or object to help stabilize or guide it
