trapping

noun
decorative covering for a horse
Pronunciation: / ˈtræpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtræpɪŋ / (BrE)
an ornamental covering or decoration for a horse, especially in former times for a warhorse

Examples

Medieval trapping decoration
Medieval trapping was an important part of a warhorse's ceremonial appearance.
Silken trappings adorned the steeds of the noblemen at the tournament.
The knight's horse was covered in elaborate trappings during the royal parade.
