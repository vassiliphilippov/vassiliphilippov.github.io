conflictless

adjective
without conflict
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːnflɪktləs / (AmE), / ˈkɒnflɪktləs / (BrE)
without any disagreement, opposition, or clash; free from conflict

Examples

A conflictless situation
The negotiations were surprisingly conflictless and ended with both sides satisfied.
They shared a conflictless friendship that lasted for decades.
A conflictless environment at work helps everyone feel more comfortable and productive.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contentious - causing or likely to cause disagreement or conflict
  • synonym: harmonious - friendly and free from disagreement
  • synonym: peaceful - calm and free from conflict or violence
