violet-purple

adjective
shade between violet and purple
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪələt ˈpɜːrpəl / (AmE), / ˈvaɪələt ˈpɜːpəl / (BrE)
A color that is intermediate between light violet and dark purple.

Examples

A violet-purple dress
She chose a dress in a beautiful violet-purple shade for the wedding.
The sky took on a violet-purple hue as the sun began to set.
The walls of the nursery were painted a soft violet-purple that felt calming and welcoming.

  • synonym: violet - A bluish-purple color
