optimism

noun
expectation of the best outcome
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɑp.təˌmɪz.əm /; / ˈɑptɪˌmɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈɒp.tɪ.mɪz.əm /; / ˈɒptɪmɪzəm / (BrE)
the tendency to expect the best possible outcome or to focus on the positive side of a situation
