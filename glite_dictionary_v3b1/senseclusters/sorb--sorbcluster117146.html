sorb

noun
acid fruit of the service tree
Pronunciation: / sɔrb / (AmE), / sɔːb / (BrE)
The acid, gritty-textured fruit of the service tree or related trees of the genus Sorbus.

Examples

A ripe sorb fruit
The sorb was once a popular wild fruit in parts of Europe.
The villagers harvested the sorb for its tart taste and medicinal uses.
You can make jelly from ripe sorbs collected in autumn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: service tree - a deciduous tree of the genus Sorbus, especially S. domestica
