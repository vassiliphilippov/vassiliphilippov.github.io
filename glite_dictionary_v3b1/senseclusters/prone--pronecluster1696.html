prone

adjective
likely to experience or do something
8.3 3.3
Pronunciation: /proʊn/ (AmE), /prəʊn/ (BrE)
having a tendency to do something, especially something undesirable; likely to experience or suffer from something

Examples

Prone to mistakes

He is prone to forgetting important meetings if he doesn't set a reminder.
Older buildings are more prone to damage during earthquakes.
