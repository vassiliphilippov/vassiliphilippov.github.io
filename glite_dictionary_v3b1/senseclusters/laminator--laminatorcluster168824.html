laminator

noun
person or machine making laminates
Pronunciation: / ˈlæm.əˌneɪ.tɚ /; / ˈlæmɪˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ˈlæm.ɪ.neɪ.tə /; / ˈlæmɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a machine or person that covers something with a protective or decorative layer by bonding materials together

Examples

A laminator machine
After training, he became a skilled laminator in the furniture manufacturing industry.
Each laminator must follow strict safety guidelines when operating the machinery.
The factory hired an experienced laminator to oversee the production of plastic sheets.
After printing the posters, she ran them through the laminator for a glossy finish.
The school purchased a new laminator for the teachers to use in the resource room.
We used the laminator to protect the important documents from getting damaged.
