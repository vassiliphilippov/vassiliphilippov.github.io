tinkle of glasses

noun
the sound of glassware striking lightly
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋkəl əv ˈɡlæsɪz / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋkl əv ˈɡlɑːsɪz / (BrE)
a light, clear sound made when glassware gently strikes together

Examples

The tinkle of glasses sound
She smiled at the familiar tinkle of glasses coming from the kitchen.
The tinkle of glasses could be heard as the guests toasted each other.
The gentle tinkle of glasses filled the room as servers cleared the tables.
