assembler

noun
genomics software that assembles DNA reads
Pronunciation: / 59c835bmb59l59r / (AmE), / 59c835bmbl59 / (BrE)
(in genomics and bioinformatics) software that reconstructs longer DNA sequences (contigs) or whole genomes by assembling many short sequencing reads.

Examples

Genome assembler
The assembler reconstructed the bacterial genome from thousands of short reads.
They ran the assembler overnight and reviewed the contigs for coverage and errors.
A cloud-based assembler made it easy for the lab to process terabytes of sequencing data.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genome assembler - a program specifically designed to assemble whole genomes from sequencing data
  • synonym: sequence assembler - software that reconstructs longer nucleotide sequences from short sequencing reads
