security

noun
the state of being safe from danger
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊr.ə.ti /; / sɪˈkjʊrəti / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊə.rɪ.ti /; / sɪˈkjʊərɪti / (BrE)
the state or feeling of being safe and free from danger, threat, or worry

Examples

A sense of security
Improvements in neighborhood policing have increased the sense of security among residents.
National security is a top priority for the government.
They moved to a gated community for greater security.
Financial stability can provide people with emotional security.
Having a trusted friend nearby gave her a sense of security during the storm.
The child clung to his blanket for security at night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry or unease
  • antonym: danger - the possibility of suffering harm or injury
  • antonym: fear - an unpleasant emotion caused by the threat of danger, pain, or harm
  • synonym: safety - the condition of being protected from harm or danger
