settleable

adjective
able to be paid
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛt̬ələbəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛtələbəl / (BrE)
capable of being paid, cleared, or resolved, especially referring to a debt, bill, or account

Examples

Settleable invoice
Only invoices marked as settleable can be paid automatically through the system.
The company reviewed which outstanding debts were settleable before closing the accounts.
If the claim is settleable, the insurance will process the payment within a week.
