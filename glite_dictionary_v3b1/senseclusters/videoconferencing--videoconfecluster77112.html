videoconferencing

noun
online video meetings
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪdioʊˌkɑːnfərɛnsɪŋ /; / ˈvɪdioʊˌkɑːn.fɚ.ənsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɪdiəʊˌkɒnfərənsɪŋ /; / ˈvɪdiəʊˌkɒn.fər.ənsɪŋ / (BrE)
a way of holding meetings or conversations with video and audio communication over the internet between people in different places

  • synonym: teleconferencing - holding meetings by telephone or audio connection
  • synonym: webinar - an online seminar or presentation
