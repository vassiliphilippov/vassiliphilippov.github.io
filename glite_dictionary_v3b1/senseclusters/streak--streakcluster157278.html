streak

noun
a continuous series of events
4.3 5.34/m
Pronunciation: / strik / (AmE), / striÀêk / (BrE)
a continuous series of similar events, especially of successes or failures, occurring without interruption

Examples

A winning streak

She broke her losing streak with a decisive victory in the finals.
The team is hoping to extend their winning streak this weekend.
