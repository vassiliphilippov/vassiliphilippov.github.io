undatable

adjective
not able to be given a date
6.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdeɪtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈdeɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible to assign a specific date to; not capable of being dated

Examples

Undatable manuscript
Many undated manuscripts in the collection remain undatable due to the lack of contextual clues.
The ancient artifact was considered undatable because there was no reliable evidence of its origin.
Without carbon dating results, the fossil is currently undatable by modern scientific methods.
