leafiness

noun
abundance of leaves
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈliːfinəs / (AmE), / ˈliːfinəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having many leaves or being covered with leaves

Examples

The leafiness of trees
The leafiness of the garden provided plenty of shade on hot summer days.
She admired the leafiness of the old oak trees lining the street.
After the rain, the leafiness of the forest seemed even more vibrant and lush.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barrenness - the state of having no vegetation or leaves
  • synonym: foliage - plant leaves collectively
