bad business

noun
unethical or harmful business practices
Pronunciation: / bæd ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / bæd ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
unethical or harmful conduct or practices in a business setting, typically indicating poor management or lack of integrity

Examples

Bad business practices
Failing to deliver products on time reflects bad business practices.
It's bad business to ignore the concerns of your employees.
Overcharging customers is considered bad business and can damage a company's reputation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: good business - ethical or effective practices in business
