unproductive

adjective
not producing desired results
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.prəˈdʌk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prəˈdʌk.tɪv / (BrE)
not producing the desired, useful, or expected results; not effective or fruitful

Examples

An unproductive meeting
After hours of discussion, their meeting proved unproductive and no solutions were found.
She found the research to be unproductive, yielding no new insights into the problem.
Spending so much time arguing was ultimately unproductive for the whole team.
His attempts to repair the car proved unproductive and frustrating.
Many hours were spent in unproductive meetings that led nowhere.
The soil in this region has become unproductive due to over-farming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fruitful - producing good or helpful results; productive
  • synonym: fruitless - failing to achieve the desired result
  • antonym: productive - achieving or producing useful results
  • synonym: sterile - incapable of producing offspring or results
