Examples

An unproductive meeting

Synonyms

  • ineffective
    3.4 9.06/m
    ineffective fails to produce the intended result in real situations, while unproductive means not giving useful or expected results.
  • fruitless
    4.8 2.18/m
    fruitless fails to bring desired results after effort, while unproductive describes things that do not produce useful outcomes.
  • futile
    6.0 6.54/m
    futile has no chance of success and often seems pointless, while unproductive simply means not producing useful results.
  • ineffectual
    6.8 1.79/m
    ineffectual shows a lack of power or skill to work well, while unproductive focuses on results not being achieved.
  • inefficacious
    9.4 0.03/m
    inefficacious sounds formal and means not producing the intended effect, while unproductive is more general and commonly used.
  • unavailing
    8.2 0.10/m
    unavailing is formal and suggests effort that fails, while unproductive is common to say something gives no useful results.
