twitchy

adjective
nervous or jerky
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪtʃi / (AmE), / ˈtwɪtʃi / (BrE)
showing small, sudden, and often involuntary movements, especially of muscles or body parts

Examples

Twitchy hands
He became twitchy after drinking too much coffee in the morning.
The horse grew twitchy as the thunderstorm approached.
Her twitchy fingers tapped nervously on the table during the interview.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing nervousness or movement
  • synonym: restless - unable to remain still or at rest
