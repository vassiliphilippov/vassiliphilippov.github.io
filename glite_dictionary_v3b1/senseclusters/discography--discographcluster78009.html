discography

noun
catalogue of musical recordings
6.5 5.7
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈskɔː.grə.fiː/ /; / /dɪ.ˈskɑː.grə.fiː/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈskɒ.grə.fiː/ (BrE)
a complete list of the musical recordings made by a particular performer, group, or composer, often with details about each recording

Examples

An artist's discography
Fans often collect every item in an artist's discography, from studio albums to rare live recordings.
The band's discography spans more than twenty years and includes several chart-topping albums.
The magazine featured a complete discography of the legendary jazz musician.
