gluten

noun
protein mixture in grains, gives dough elasticity
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡluːtən / (AmE), / ˈɡluːtən / (BrE)
a mixture of proteins found in wheat and other cereal grains that gives dough its elasticity and chewiness

Examples

Gluten in wheat
Removing gluten can change the texture of baked goods.
Some people avoid gluten because of allergies or sensitivities.
This bread is made from wheat, which contains gluten.
