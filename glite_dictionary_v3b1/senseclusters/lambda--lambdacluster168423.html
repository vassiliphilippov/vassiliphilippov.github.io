lambda

noun
craniometric point on the skull
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlæm.də / (AmE), / ˈlæm.də / (BrE)
the point on the skull where the sagittal and lambdoid sutures meet, used as a reference in craniometry

Examples

Skull lambda point
The lambda is an important landmark for neurosurgeons during certain procedures.
The position of the lambda is measured to assess cranial development in infants.
The technician identified the lambda while examining the patient's skull X-ray.
