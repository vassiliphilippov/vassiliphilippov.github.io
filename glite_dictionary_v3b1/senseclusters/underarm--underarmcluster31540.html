underarm

adjective, adverb
with the arm below shoulder level
5.3 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈʌn.dər.ɑːrm/ (AmE), /ˈʌn.də.rɑːm/ (BrE)
performed or done with the arm or hand moving forward and up from below shoulder level (especially a throw or stroke)
