overcompensatory

adjective
excessive corrective
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərkəmˈpɛnsəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəkəmˈpɛnsətəri / (BrE)
describing a response or action that exceeds what is necessary to correct a problem or imbalance

Examples

An overcompensatory action
Her overcompensatory efforts to fix the mistake ended up creating new problems.
The manager's overcompensatory response made the team feel overwhelmed.
Sometimes an overcompensatory reaction can be just as harmful as the original issue.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adequate - enough or sufficient for a particular purpose
  • synonym: excessive - more than is necessary or reasonable
