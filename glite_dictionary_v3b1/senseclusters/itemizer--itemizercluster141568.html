itemizer

noun
person claiming deductions
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪtəˌmaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈaɪtəˌmaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who makes an itemized list or claims individual deductions, especially on a tax return

Examples

Tax return itemizer
As an experienced itemizer, he kept receipts for every business expense throughout the year.
The accountant explained the benefits of being an itemizer rather than taking the standard deduction.
Many itemizers use software to track their deductible expenses efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deduction - an amount subtracted from taxable income
  • synonym: taxpayer - a person who pays taxes
