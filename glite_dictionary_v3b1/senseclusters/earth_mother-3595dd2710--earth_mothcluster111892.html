earth mother

noun
mythological female figure representing the Earth
3.3
Pronunciation: / ɜrθ ˈmʌðɚ / (AmE), / ɜːθ ˈmʌðə / (BrE)
(in mythology and religion) a female deity or personification thought to be the source of all life and fertility, representing the Earth.

Examples

Ancient earth mother
Ancient cultures often worshipped an earth mother as the source of fertility and life.
In mythology, the earth mother is revered for her nurturing and creative powers.
The festival was held in honor of the earth mother, asking for a bountiful harvest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mother Earth - the personification of the Earth as a nurturing mother
