Yenisey

proper noun
major river in Siberia, Russia
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛnɪˌseɪ / (AmE), / ˈjɛnɪˌseɪ / (BrE)
a major river in Siberia, Russia, flowing from the mountains near the Mongolian border northward into the Kara Sea

Examples

The Yenisey River
Many native communities rely on the Yenisey for transportation and fishing.
Spring floods can dramatically raise water levels along the Yenisey.
The Yenisey River is one of the longest river systems flowing into the Arctic Ocean.
