Examples

Synonyms

  • blooded
    8.5 2.46/m
    blooded is used for animals or sometimes people with unmixed ancestry, while full-blooded stresses wholly pure descent.
  • purebred
    4.2 0.20/m
    purebred describes animals bred within a recognized breed with known parents, while full-blooded covers pure ancestry in people or animals.
  • pedigreed
    6.2 0.13/m
    pedigreed means an animal has a recorded family history or certificate, while full-blooded means unmixed ancestry regardless of records.
