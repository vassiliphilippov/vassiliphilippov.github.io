waspishly

adverb
irritably or sharply
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːspɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈwɒspɪʃli / (BrE)
in a way that is irritable, sharp, or easily annoyed

Examples

Answered waspishly
He answered the reporter's question waspishly, making everyone in the room uncomfortable.
After a long day, she spoke waspishly to her colleagues about the unfinished work.
The critic waspishly dismissed the new play as unoriginal and dull.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful or relaxed manner
  • synonym: irritably - in an annoyed or easily angered manner
  • synonym: sharply - in a harsh or biting manner
