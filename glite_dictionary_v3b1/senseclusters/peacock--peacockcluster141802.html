peacock

noun
European butterfly with eyespot markings
Pronunciation: / ˈpiˌkɑk / (AmE), / ˈpiːkɒk / (BrE)
A European butterfly (Aglais io) with reddish-brown wings, each marked with a purple eyespot.

Examples

Peacock butterfly lands
Many gardeners welcome the peacock for its beauty and pest control abilities.
The peacock settled on a flower, displaying the brilliant eyespots on its wings.
The wings of the peacock resemble the plumage of its namesake bird.
