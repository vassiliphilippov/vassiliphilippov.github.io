beef

noun
informal complaint or objection
6.4 6.4
Pronunciation: /biːf/ (AmE), /biːf/ (BrE)
(informal) a complaint, argument, or dispute, especially between people

Examples

Have a beef with someone
Do you have a beef about the new policy, or are you just joking?
He always has a beef with the way the manager runs meetings.
If you have a beef with your neighbor, you should talk it out.
The two teams have had a long-standing beef that started years ago.
She tried to avoid any beef with her coworkers by staying neutral.
After talking things out, they realized their beef was just a misunderstanding.
