whitetip shark

noun
shark with white-tipped fins
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtˌtɪp ʃɑrk / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtˌtɪp ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a species of shark with white-tipped fins, found in the Pacific and Indian Oceans and the Red Sea, known for living in deep water and sometimes being dangerous
