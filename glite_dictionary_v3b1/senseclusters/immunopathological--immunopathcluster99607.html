immunopathological

adjective
immune-related disease
Pronunciation: / ɪˌmjunəˌpæθəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ɪˌmjuːnəˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to disease or damage caused by the immune system's response

Examples

Immunopathological reaction
The doctor explained that the rash was an immunopathological reaction to the medication.
Researchers are studying the immunopathological effects of certain viral infections.
Some chronic illnesses have a strong immunopathological component that complicates treatment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autoimmune - relating to the immune system attacking the body's own tissues
  • antonym: infectious - caused by infection with microorganisms
