Ficus elastica

noun
tropical Asian tree; popular houseplant
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪkəs ɪˈlæstɪkə / (AmE), / ˈfaɪkəs ɪˈlæstɪkə / (BrE)
a large tropical Asian tree with glossy, leathery leaves, often grown indoors as a houseplant and known as the source of Assam rubber

Examples

The Ficus elastica tree
Latex harvested from Ficus elastica was historically used in the production of rubber.
Many homeowners choose Ficus elastica for its attractive, glossy leaves and easy maintenance.
The Ficus elastica can grow impressively tall in its natural habitat, but is often kept small indoors.
