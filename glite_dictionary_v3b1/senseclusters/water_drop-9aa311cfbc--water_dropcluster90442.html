water drop

noun
small water particle
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər drɑp / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə drɒp / (BrE)
a small, round droplet of water

Examples

A water drop on leaf

The morning sun made each water drop on the grass sparkle brightly.
She watched a water drop slowly slide down the window during the storm.
A single water drop rolled down the window after the rainstorm.
The scientist placed a water drop on the microscope slide for examination.
She watched a water drop fall from the leaf onto the ground.
