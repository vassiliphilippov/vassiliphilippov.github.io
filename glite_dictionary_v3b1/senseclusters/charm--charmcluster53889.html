charm

noun
the quality of being attractive or pleasing
1.7 12/m
Pronunciation: / t É…ërm / (AmE), / t É…ëÀêm / (BrE)
the quality of being attractive or pleasing in a way that delights or fascinates others

Examples

Natural charm
His charm made it easy for him to make new friends wherever he went.
She won everyone over with her natural charm and sense of humor.
The old village has a unique charm that attracts many visitors each year.
