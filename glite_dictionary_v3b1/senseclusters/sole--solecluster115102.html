sole

noun
a type of edible flatfish
7.6
Pronunciation: / soʊl / (AmE), / səʊl / (BrE)
a flatfish with both eyes usually on the right side, widely found in warm seas and valued as food, especially in Europe

Examples

Grilled sole fish
Fresh sole is considered a delicacy in many European restaurants.
He ordered grilled sole with a lemon butter sauce for dinner.
The fisherman caught several sole off the coast this morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flounder - a general term for several similar flatfish
  • synonym: halibut - a large flatfish with both eyes on the right side, edible
