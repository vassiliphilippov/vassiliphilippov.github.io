clog dancing

noun
dance with wooden-soled shoes
4.3 4.6
Pronunciation: / klɑɡ ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / klɒɡ ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
A traditional dance performed while wearing shoes with wooden soles, characterized by heavy stamping steps.
