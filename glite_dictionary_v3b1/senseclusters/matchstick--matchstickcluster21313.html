matchstick

noun
stick used to make matches
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmætʃˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈmatʃ.stɪk / (BrE)
a short, thin stick of wood or cardboard used to make matches for lighting a fire

Examples

A matchstick box
All that was left after the fire were a few burnt matchsticks.
He struck a matchstick against the box to light the candle.
She carefully balanced a matchstick on the edge of the table.
