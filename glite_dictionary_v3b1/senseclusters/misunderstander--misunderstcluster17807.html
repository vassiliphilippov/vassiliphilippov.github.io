misunderstander

noun
person not understood
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˌʌndərˈstændər / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˌʌndəˈstændə / (BrE)
a person who is not understood correctly by others or whose words or actions are misinterpreted.

Examples

A lonely misunderstander
As a frequent misunderstander, she often felt isolated from her classmates.
The novel's main character is a misunderstander whose intentions are always questioned.
He became known as a misunderstander after his ideas were repeatedly misinterpreted.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misfit - a person who does not fit in with others
  • synonym: outcast - a person who is rejected or not accepted by others
