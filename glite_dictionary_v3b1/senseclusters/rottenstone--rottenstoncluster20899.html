rottenstone

noun
powdered porous limestone used for polishing
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːtənˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈrɒtənstəʊn / (BrE)
a soft, porous, siliceous limestone that has weathered and decomposed, commonly used in powdered form as an abrasive for polishing surfaces

Examples

Powdered rottenstone
After sanding the wood, she applied rottenstone to achieve a glass-smooth finish.
Cabinetmakers often rely on rottenstone for the final buffing of fine furniture.
You can polish metal surfaces to a high sheen using rottenstone mixed with oil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pumice - a light, porous volcanic rock used as an abrasive
