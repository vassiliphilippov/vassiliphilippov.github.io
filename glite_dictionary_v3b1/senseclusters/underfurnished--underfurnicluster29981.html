underfurnished

adjective
lacking furniture
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈfɜrnɪʃt / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈfɜːnɪʃt / (BrE)
having less furniture than is usual or needed

Examples

An underfurnished room
The living room looked underfurnished with only a single chair and a small table.
Guests noticed the house was underfurnished compared to others in the neighborhood.
After moving in, they found the apartment was underfurnished and needed more seating.

Related Terms

  • antonym: furnished - provided with furniture
  • degree lower: unfurnished - having no furniture at all
