moneychanger

noun
currency exchange machine
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌniˌtʃeɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈmʌniˌtʃeɪndʒə / (BrE)
a machine or person that exchanges banknotes for coins or different denominations of money

Examples

Laundromat moneychanger
The laundromat had a moneychanger near the entrance for customers needing coins.
She used the moneychanger to get quarters for the parking meter.
A broken moneychanger can be frustrating when you need small change quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ATM - a machine that dispenses cash and performs banking transactions
  • synonym: currency exchange - a place or service where money is exchanged for different currencies or denominations
