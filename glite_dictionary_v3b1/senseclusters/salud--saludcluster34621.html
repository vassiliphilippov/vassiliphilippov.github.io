salud

toast for good health
Pronunciation: / sɑːˈluːd / (AmE), / sæˈluːd / (BrE)
an exclamation used to wish good health, especially when making a toast, borrowed from Spanish

Examples

Say salud at a toast
He raised his glass and exclaimed salud before everyone took a sip.
At the party, Maria smiled and said salud as she toasted her friends.
They clinked their glasses and shouted salud to celebrate the good news.
