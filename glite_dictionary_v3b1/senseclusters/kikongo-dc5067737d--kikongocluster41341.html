Kikongo

proper noun
Central African language
9.9
Pronunciation: / kiˈkɔŋgoʊ / (AmE), / kiˈkɒŋgəʊ / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken in parts of Central Africa, especially in the Congo region

Examples

Kikongo language
Many people in western Congo speak Kikongo as their first language.
The translation of the document into Kikongo helped local communities understand the information.
She is learning Kikongo to communicate better with her relatives in Central Africa.
