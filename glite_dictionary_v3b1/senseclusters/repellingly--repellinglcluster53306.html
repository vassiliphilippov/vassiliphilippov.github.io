repellingly

adverb
in a way that repels or disgusts
6.9 8.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɛlɪŋli / (AmE), / rɪˈpɛlɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that repels or disgusts; in a way that causes distaste or aversion.
