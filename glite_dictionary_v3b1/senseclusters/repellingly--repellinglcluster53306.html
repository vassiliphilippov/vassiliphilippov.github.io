repellingly

adverb
in a way that repels or disgusts
6.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɛlɪŋli / (AmE), / rɪˈpɛlɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that repels or disgusts; in a way that causes distaste or aversion.

Examples

To speak repellingly
She spoke to him repellingly, with a tone that made everyone uncomfortable.
The artwork was repellingly grotesque, drawing attention even as it unsettled the viewers.
The spoiled food looked repellingly slimy, making me lose my appetite.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repellently - in a way that is repellent or causes aversion
