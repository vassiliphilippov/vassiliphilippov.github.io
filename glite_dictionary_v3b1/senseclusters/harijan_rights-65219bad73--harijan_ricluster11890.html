Harijan rights

noun
rights of marginalized communities in India
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑriˌdʒɑn raɪts / (AmE), / ˈhɑːrɪˌdʒʌn raɪts / (BrE)
the rights and entitlements claimed or sought by the marginalized community in India historically referred to as Harijans (Dalits), especially in the context of social and political reform

Examples

Harijan rights movement
Harijan rights were a significant part of Gandhi's movement for social equality.
Many activists campaigned for Harijan rights throughout the 20th century in India.
The constitution of India includes provisions that safeguard Harijan rights.
