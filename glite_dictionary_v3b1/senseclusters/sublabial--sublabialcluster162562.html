sublabial

adjective
beneath the lip
9.9
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈleɪbiəl / (AmE), / sʌbˈleɪbiəl / (BrE)
located or occurring beneath the lip, especially the lower lip

Examples

A sublabial incision
The dentist made a small sublabial incision to access the affected area.
A sublabial approach is often used in certain oral surgeries.
Swelling in the sublabial region can indicate an underlying dental issue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sublingual - situated or occurring under the tongue
