Battle of Saratoga

noun
major 1777 battle in the American Revolution
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbæt.əl əv sæ.rə.ˈtoʊ.gə/ (AmE), /ˈbæt.əl əv sæ.rə.ˈtəʊ.gə/ (BrE)
the decisive 1777 battle of the American Revolutionary War in which British forces under General Burgoyne were defeated by American troops, marking a turning point in the conflict

Examples

The Battle of Saratoga victory
In history class, we learned how the American victory at the Battle of Saratoga influenced France to support the colonies.
The Battle of Saratoga is often considered the turning point of the American Revolution.
The British defeat at the Battle of Saratoga had far-reaching consequences for the outcome of the war.
