professional victim

noun
person who habitually claims victimhood
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɛʃənəl ˈvɪktəm / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənl ˈvɪktɪm / (BrE)
a person who consistently presents themselves as a victim, often exaggerating or fabricating grievances for attention or advantage

Examples

A professional victim complains
Despite receiving help, he continued to act like a professional victim in every situation.
She was tired of her friend's constant complaints and refusal to take responsibility, claiming he was a professional victim.
The columnist accused several public figures of adopting the professional victim stance to deflect criticism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: victim mentality - an outlook where a person sees themselves as a victim of the negative actions of others
