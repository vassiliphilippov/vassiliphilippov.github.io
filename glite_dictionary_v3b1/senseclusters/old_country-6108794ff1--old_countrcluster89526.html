old country

noun
country of origin for immigrants
5.3
Pronunciation: / oʊld ˈkʌntri / (AmE), / əʊld ˈkʌntri / (BrE)
the country from which a person or their family originally comes, especially the country that immigrants left to move to a new country

Examples

The old country
He still longs for the food he ate as a child in the old country.
Many immigrants kept traditions from the old country alive in their new homes.
Stories passed down from the old country are cherished by the whole family.
