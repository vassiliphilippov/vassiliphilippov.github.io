medicinally

adverb
in a way relating to medicine
6.5
Pronunciation: / məˈdɪsənəli / (AmE), / məˈdɪs.ɪ.nəl.i / (BrE)
In a manner relating to medicine or having healing properties; for medicinal purposes.

Examples

Used medicinally for healing
Certain herbs are used medicinally in traditional remedies across many cultures.
Some oils can be applied medicinally to treat skin irritations.
The plant is not eaten as food but valued medicinally for its soothing properties.
