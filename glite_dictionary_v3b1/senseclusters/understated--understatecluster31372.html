understated

adjective
subtly or elegantly restrained
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈsteɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈsteɪtɪd / (BrE)
presented or expressed in a subtle and restrained way, especially so as to be elegant or in good taste

Examples

An understated style
Her style is always understated but undeniably elegant.
I appreciate artwork that takes a more understated approach rather than relying on bright colors.
The understated lighting in the restaurant created a calm atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extravagant - lacking restraint in spending or style
  • synonym: subtle - not obvious or blatant; delicately complex
