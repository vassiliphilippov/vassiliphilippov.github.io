clownishly

adverb
in a silly way
Pronunciation: / ˈklaʊnɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈklaʊnɪʃli / (BrE)
in a silly, foolish, or exaggeratedly playful manner, like a clown

Examples

Act clownishly at meeting
He acted clownishly at the meeting, making everyone laugh with his silly jokes.
The children ran clownishly around the playground, pretending to be circus performers.
She dressed clownishly for the costume party, wearing oversized shoes and a red nose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foolishly - in a silly or unwise manner
  • antonym: seriously - in a thoughtful or earnest manner
