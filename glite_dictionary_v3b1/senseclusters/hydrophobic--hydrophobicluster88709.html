hydrophobic

adjective
abnormally afraid of water
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdrəˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdrəˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
having an abnormal or extreme fear of water

Examples

Hydrophobic fear of water
Because of her hydrophobic tendencies, she becomes anxious even near small puddles.
Some children with autism can be hydrophobic and refuse to participate in swimming lessons.
The patient was diagnosed as hydrophobic following a traumatic water-related incident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aquaphobic - having a fear of water
