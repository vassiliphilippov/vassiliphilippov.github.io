verdantly

adverb
lushly, with greenery
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrdəntli / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is green with vegetation or lush and covered with plants

Examples

Verdantly covered hills
The garden was verdantly alive after the heavy spring rains.
They picnicked verdantly beneath the thick canopy of trees in the park.
The valley stretched out verdantly before them, full of wildflowers and tall grass.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barrenness - in a way that is dry or lacking vegetation
  • synonym: lushly - in a rich, abundant, and green manner
