anergic

adjective
lacking immune response
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈnɜrdʒɪk / (AmE), / əˈnɜːdʒɪk / (BrE)
lacking a normal immune response to specific antigens or stimuli

Examples

Anergic immune system
The doctor noted that the patient was anergic after several tests showed no immune reaction.
Some diseases can cause people to become anergic and unable to fight infections effectively.
During the allergy screening, the child remained anergic to all tested substances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immunodeficient - having a weakened or impaired immune system
  • antonym: responsive - showing a normal or strong reaction to stimuli
