lowlight

noun
darker hair streak
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a darker streak or section of hair colored to create contrast or add depth to the overall hairstyle

Examples

Hair lowlight
The stylist suggested adding a lowlight to give her hair more dimension.
After getting several lowlights, his hair looked richer and more natural.
She chose a chocolate brown lowlight to complement her blonde highlights.

Related Terms
