helianthus

noun
genus of plants including sunflowers
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːliˈænθəs / (AmE), / ˌhiːliˈænθəs / (BrE)
a genus of tall plants in the daisy family, notable for large flower heads with yellow petals and dark central disks, including the sunflower
