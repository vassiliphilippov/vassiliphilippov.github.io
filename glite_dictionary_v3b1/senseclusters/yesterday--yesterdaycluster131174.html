yesterday

adverb
on the day before today
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛstɚˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈjɛstədeɪ / (BrE)
on the day before today; during the day preceding today

Examples

Yesterday it rained
Yesterday, it rained all afternoon, so we stayed indoors.
I called her yesterday but she didn't answer the phone.
We finished the project yesterday after working late into the night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: today - on or during the present day
  • antonym: tomorrow - on the day after today
