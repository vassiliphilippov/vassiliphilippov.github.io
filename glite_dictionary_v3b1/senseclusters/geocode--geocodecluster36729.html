geocode

noun
location coordinates
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːoʊˌkoʊd / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːəʊˌkəʊd / (BrE)
a numerical code or set of coordinates that identifies a specific geographic location, often used in mapping or location services

Examples

A geocode number
Each property in the database has a unique geocode for mapping purposes.
The app uses your phone's geocode to show nearby restaurants.
By entering the geocode, you can pinpoint the exact location on the map.

Related Terms

  • synonym: address - the details of where a place is located
  • synonym: coordinates - numbers that specify a location on a map or grid
