shin bone

noun
cut of meat from an animal's lower leg
Pronunciation: / ʃɪn boʊn / (AmE), / ʃɪn bəʊn / (BrE)
A cut of meat taken from the lower part of the leg of an animal, especially used for stews and braised dishes.

Examples

Beef shin bone
Lamb shin bone becomes tender and flavorful after slow cooking.
She ordered oxtail and shin bone at the butcher's for the weekend soup.
The recipe calls for beef shin bone to give the stew a rich flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shank - a similar cut of meat from the leg
