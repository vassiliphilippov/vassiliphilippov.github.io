lizard

noun
a type of reptile with legs and a tail
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪzərd / (AmE), / ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
A reptile with a long body, usually two pairs of legs, and a tapering tail, belonging to the order Squamata.

Examples

A green lizard
A green lizard basked in the sun on the garden wall.
She kept a small lizard as a pet in a glass terrarium.
The lizard darted across the rock to catch an insect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reptile - a cold-blooded, scaly vertebrate animal including snakes, lizards, and crocodiles
