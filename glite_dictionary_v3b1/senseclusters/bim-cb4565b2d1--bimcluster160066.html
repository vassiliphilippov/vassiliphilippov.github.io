BIM

noun
digital building modeling
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbiː.aɪ.em/ /; / /bɪm/ (AmE), /ˈbiː.aɪ.em/ /; / /bɪm/ (BrE)
the process and technology of creating and managing digital representations of a building's physical and functional characteristics, known as Building Information Modeling

Examples

BIM technology
The construction team relied on BIM to coordinate the design and building process efficiently.
Using BIM helped the engineers detect potential issues before construction began.
Many modern architects consider BIM essential for managing complex building projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: CAD - computer-aided design, the use of computers to create technical drawings
  • degree lower: blueprint - a detailed technical drawing of a building or object
