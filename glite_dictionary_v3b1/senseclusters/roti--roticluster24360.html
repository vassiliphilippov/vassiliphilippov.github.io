roti

noun
unleavened flatbread
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊti / (AmE), / ˈrəʊti / (BrE)
a round, unleavened flatbread made from whole wheat flour, commonly eaten in South Asian and Caribbean cuisines

Examples

Fresh roti bread
She made fresh roti every morning to serve with breakfast.
The restaurant offers a variety of curries served with hot roti.
He learned how to cook roti from his grandmother in India.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chapati - a type of unleavened flatbread from South Asia
  • antonym: naan - a leavened, oven-baked flatbread from South Asia
