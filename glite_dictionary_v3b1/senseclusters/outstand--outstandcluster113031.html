outstand

verb
to surpass others
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈstænd / (AmE), / aʊtˈstænd / (BrE)
to be superior to or surpass someone or something in ability, quality, or achievement

Examples

Outstand the competition
She worked hard to outstand her peers in both academics and sports.
The new product managed to outstand all competitors in terms of quality and design.
He hoped his unique skills would help him outstand during the job interview.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excel - to be exceptionally good at something
  • antonym: lag - to fall behind in progress or achievement
  • synonym: outperform - to do better than someone or something
