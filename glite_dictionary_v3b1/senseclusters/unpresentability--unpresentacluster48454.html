unpresentability

noun
unsuitability for display
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprɪˌzɛntəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌʌnprɪˌzɛntəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being unsuitable or unfit to be shown, introduced, or presented to others

Examples

Unpresentability at work
His unpresentability made him nervous before the interview.
The unpresentability of the old furniture was obvious to everyone who visited.
She worried about the unpresentability of her report after noticing several errors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: presentability - the quality of being suitable to be shown or introduced
  • synonym: unacceptability - the quality of not being acceptable
