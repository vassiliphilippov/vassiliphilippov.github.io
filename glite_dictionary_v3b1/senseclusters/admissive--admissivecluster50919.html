admissive

adjective
allowing admission or entry
Pronunciation: / ədˈmɪsɪv / (AmE), / ədˈmɪsɪv / (BrE)
characterized by or allowing admission; receptive or permitting entry or inclusion

Examples

Admissive attitude
Her admissive attitude made collaboration easy for everyone involved.
The council adopted an admissive policy toward new members.
The museum became more admissive to contemporary art in recent years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exclusive - not admitting something; restricting entry or participation
  • synonym: receptive - willing to accept new ideas or suggestions
