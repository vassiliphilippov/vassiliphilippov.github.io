Plotinian

noun
follower of Plotinus
Pronunciation: / ploʊˈtɪniən / (AmE), / plɒˈtɪniən / (BrE)
a follower or supporter of Plotinus or his philosophical ideas, especially Neoplatonism

Examples

A Plotinian thinker
The Plotinian argued that reality is ultimately derived from a single, transcendent source.
As a dedicated Plotinian, he often referenced the teachings of Plotinus in his lectures.
Many Plotinians contributed to the spread of Neoplatonic philosophy throughout the ancient world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Neoplatonist - a follower of Neoplatonism, a philosophical system based on Plato's ideas
