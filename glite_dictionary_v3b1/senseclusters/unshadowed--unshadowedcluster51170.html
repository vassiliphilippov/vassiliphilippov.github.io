unshadowed

adjective
not darkened by shadow
5.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃædoʊd / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃadəʊd / (BrE)
not darkened or obscured by shadow; fully illuminated or clear.

Examples

An unshadowed face
Her unshadowed expression revealed a sense of pure happiness.
The painting captured the landscape in unshadowed afternoon light.
The path was unshadowed, making it easy to spot small animals along the way.
