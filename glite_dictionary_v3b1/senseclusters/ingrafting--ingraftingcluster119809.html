ingrafting

noun
joining by grafting
8.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈgræf.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈgræf.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of joining or inserting a part (such as plant tissue or skin) into another so that it becomes part of the host

Examples

Ingrafting plant tissue
