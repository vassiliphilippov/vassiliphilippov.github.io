fin whale

noun
large baleen whale species
Pronunciation: / fɪn weɪl / (AmE), / fɪn weɪl / (BrE)
A large species of baleen whale with a flat head and deep throat furrows, found in the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans.

Examples

A fin whale species
A fin whale can be recognized by its long, slender body and distinctive dorsal fin.
Researchers tracked the migration patterns of several fin whales across the Atlantic.
The fin whale is the second largest animal on Earth, after the blue whale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blue whale - the largest known animal, a closely related baleen whale
  • synonym: humpback whale - another species of large baleen whale
