bout

noun
brief period of excessive activity
4.7 1.27/m
Pronunciation: / baÊŠt / (AmE), / baÊŠt / (BrE)
A short period during which a particular activity, especially excessive eating or drinking, takes place.

Examples

Bout of eating
After his exam, he went on a bout of overeating to celebrate.
Following a stressful week, she indulged in a bout of comfort food.
The group gathered for a bout of drinking that lasted late into the night.

Synonyms

  • binge
    5.6 3.86/m
    binge is a short, intense period of too much eating or drinking, while bout is any brief spell of such activity.
  • bust
    7.4 1.85/m
    bust is an occasion of indulgent eating or drinking tied to celebration, while bout is a general short spell of excess.
  • tear
    9.7 1.30/m
    tear is a period of revelry with long, heavy eating or drinking, while bout is a shorter, simple spell of excess.
