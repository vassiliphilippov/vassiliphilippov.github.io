Cryptobranchus

noun
genus of large aquatic salamanders
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪptoʊˈbræŋkəs / (AmE), / ˌkrɪptəˈbraŋkəs /; / ˌkrɪptəʊˈbraŋkəs / (BrE)
a genus of large aquatic salamanders (giant salamanders or hellbenders) in the family Cryptobranchidae, found in eastern North America
