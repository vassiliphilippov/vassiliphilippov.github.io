Proteus

proper noun
mythological sea god capable of changing shape
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊtiəs / (AmE), / ˈprəʊtiəs / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) a prophetic sea god who served Poseidon and had the ability to change his shape at will.
