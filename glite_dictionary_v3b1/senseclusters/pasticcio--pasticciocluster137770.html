pasticcio

noun
artistic mixture
Pronunciation: / pɑːˈstiːtʃoʊ / (AmE), / paˈstiːtʃəʊ / (BrE)
a work of art, music, or literature created by combining pieces from different sources or styles

Musical pasticcio

Her latest novel is a pasticcio of different literary styles and genres.
The exhibition featured a pasticcio of paintings inspired by various art movements.
