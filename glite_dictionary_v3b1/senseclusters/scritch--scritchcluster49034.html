scritch

noun
light scratch mark
8.8
Pronunciation: /skrɪtʃ/ (AmE), /skrɪtʃ/ (BrE)
a light scratch or small mark made by rubbing or scraping a surface, skin, or fur

Examples

A small scritch mark
She found a tiny scritch on her hand after playing with the cat.
The old table had a scritch where someone had dragged a plate across it.
He heard a faint scritch on the door as the dog tried to get in.
