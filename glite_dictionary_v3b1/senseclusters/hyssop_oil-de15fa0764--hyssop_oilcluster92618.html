hyssop oil

noun
essential oil from hyssop plant
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪsəp ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈhɪsəp ɔɪl / (BrE)
an essential oil obtained from the hyssop plant, used chiefly in liqueurs and sometimes in perfumery

Examples

Hyssop oil flavor
Many artisan liqueurs are flavored with hyssop oil for its distinctive aroma.
Perfume makers sometimes use hyssop oil as a natural ingredient in their blends.
The recipe calls for a drop of hyssop oil to enhance the herbal notes.
