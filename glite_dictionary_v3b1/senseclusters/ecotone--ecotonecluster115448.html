ecotone

noun
boundary zone
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàiÀêko äÀåto än / (AmE), / ÀàiÀêk…ô äÀåt…ô än / (BrE)
a transitional area where two different social, cultural, or conceptual systems meet and blend features of both

Examples

Cultural ecotone
The city park became an ecotone where artists and business professionals mingled freely.
Her research focused on the ecotone between traditional and digital media in modern communication.
The festival served as an ecotone for different musical genres, attracting a diverse audience.

  • synonym: interface - a point where two systems or subjects meet and interact
