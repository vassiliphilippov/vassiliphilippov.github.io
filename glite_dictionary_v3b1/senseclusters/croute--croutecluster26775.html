croute

noun
dish on toasted bread
Pronunciation: / kruːt / (AmE), / kruːt / (BrE)
a slice of toasted bread or a pastry crust used as a base or case for serving or baking food

Examples

A mushroom croute
The chef served a delicious mushroom croute as an appetizer.
For brunch, we enjoyed smoked salmon on a crispy croute with fresh herbs.
Each guest received a small croute topped with melted cheese and tomatoes.
The chef presented the salmon baked in a flaky croute with a dill sauce.
For the holiday dinner, she made beef wrapped in a golden croute to impress her guests.
A crisp croute adds both texture and flavor to many classic French dishes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crust - the hard outer surface of bread
  • antonym: filling - the food placed inside a pastry or crust
  • synonym: toast - sliced bread browned by heat
