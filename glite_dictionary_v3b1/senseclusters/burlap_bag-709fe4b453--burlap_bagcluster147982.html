burlap bag

noun
bag made of coarse burlap fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝː.læp bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈbɜː.læp bæɡ / (BrE)
A bag made from strong, coarse fabric typically used for storing or transporting goods.

Examples

A burlap bag of rice
After the festival, volunteers collected rubbish in burlap bags for recycling.
She carried a large burlap bag filled with garden tools.
The farmer filled the burlap bag with potatoes fresh from the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gunny sack - a sack made of burlap or a similar material
