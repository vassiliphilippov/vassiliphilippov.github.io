olla

noun
talipot palm leaf used as writing material in India
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.lə / (AmE), / ˈɒl.ə / (BrE)
A leaf or strip from the talipot palm, used in India as writing paper, especially for ancient manuscripts.

Examples

Collectors consider an olla a valuable artifact from early Indian cultures.
