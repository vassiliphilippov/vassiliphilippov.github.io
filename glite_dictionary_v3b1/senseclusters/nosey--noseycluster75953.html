nosey

adjective
intrusively curious; prying
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊzi / (AmE), / ˈnəʊzi / (BrE)
Excessively interested in other people's affairs in a way that is considered intrusive or offending.

Examples

A nosey neighbor
My nosey neighbor always seems to know what time I get home.
She found his nosey questions rude and intrusive.
Stop being so nosey and mind your own business for once.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: curious - eager to know or learn something
  • antonym: discreet - careful and not likely to be noticed; not nosey
  • synonym: prying - excessively interested in others' private affairs
