nosey

adjective
intrusively curious; prying
3.6 4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈnoʊ.ziː/ /; / /ˈnoʊ.zɪ/ (AmE), /ˈnəʊ.ziː/ /; / /ˈnəʊ.zɪ/ (BrE)
