snaggle

noun
irregular tooth
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæɡəl / (AmE), / ˈsnæɡəl / (BrE)
an irregular, protruding, or broken tooth, especially one that sticks out noticeably

Examples

A snaggle tooth
The toddler's grin revealed a tiny snaggle that made everyone laugh at his charm.
She was self-conscious about the snaggle in her smile but learned to embrace it.
After chipping his tooth, he was left with a noticeable snaggle in the front.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gap tooth - a tooth with a noticeable space between it and another
  • synonym: snaggletooth - a tooth that is broken or sticks out unevenly
