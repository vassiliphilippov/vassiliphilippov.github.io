eliminate

verb
expel or remove from the body
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɪməˌneɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪmɪneɪt / (BrE)
(of a substance or waste) to expel or remove from the body, especially as part of a physiological process.

Examples

To eliminate waste
A healthy digestive system efficiently eliminates toxins from the body.
Some medications are eliminated by the liver and excreted in the bile.
The kidneys eliminate excess waste products through urine.
