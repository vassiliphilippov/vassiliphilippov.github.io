kraut

noun
fermented cabbage
Pronunciation: / kraʊt / (AmE), / kraʊt / (BrE)
fermented cabbage, especially as used in dishes like sauerkraut

Examples

A bowl of kraut
He topped his sausage with a generous helping of kraut.
The recipe calls for fresh kraut to add a tangy flavor to the dish.
At the festival, they served homemade kraut alongside grilled meats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sauerkraut - finely cut fermented cabbage
