transfer

verb
change to another vehicle or line
Pronunciation: / ˈtræns.fər / (AmE), / trænsˈfəː / (BrE)
to change from one vehicle, aircraft, or transportation line to another during a journey

Examples

To transfer to another bus
Passengers must transfer to the airport shuttle at the main terminal.
She transferred from the subway to the downtown bus to get to work.
We had to transfer trains in Philadelphia to reach our final destination.
