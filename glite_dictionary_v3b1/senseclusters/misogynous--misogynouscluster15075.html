misogynous

adjective
hating women
9.6
Pronunciation: / mɪˈsɑdʒənəs / (AmE), / mɪˈsɒdʒənəs / (BrE)
having or showing a hatred of women.

Examples

Misogynous attitude
Critics accused the author of portraying female characters in a misogynous manner.
His misogynous views became evident during the heated debate.
Some traditional policies were influenced by misogynous attitudes within society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misogynist - a person who hates or distrusts women
  • synonym: misogynistic - having or showing hatred, dislike, or mistrust of women
