unbridle passion

verb
express passion freely
6.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbraɪdld ˈpæʃən / (AmE), / ʌnˈbraɪdld ˈpæʃən / (BrE)
to express one's strong enthusiasm or emotion openly and without restraint

Examples

After years of holding back, he finally decided to unbridle passion for teaching in his classroom.
It was at the festival that she chose to unbridle passion for music, captivating everyone present.
The artist's latest exhibition is a bold attempt to unbridle passion on canvas.
