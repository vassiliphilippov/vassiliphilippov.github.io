one-night stand

noun
brief sexual encounter or relationship
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌnˌnaɪt ˈstænd / (AmE), / ˈwʌn naɪt ˈstænd /; / ˈwʌn.naɪt ˈstænd / (BrE)
a brief sexual encounter or relationship that lasts for only one night and is not repeated

Examples

A one-night stand with someone
He regretted the one-night stand almost as soon as it was over.
Some people see a one-night stand as purely physical, without emotional involvement.
They met at a party, and what she thought was a one-night stand turned into a long friendship.
After her one-night stand, she decided she wanted something more meaningful.
He admitted that the one-night stand meant nothing to him emotionally.
They both understood the one-night stand would not lead to a relationship.
