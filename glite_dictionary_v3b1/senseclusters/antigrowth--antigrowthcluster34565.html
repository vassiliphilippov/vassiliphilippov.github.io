antigrowth

adjective
opposing growth
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈɡroʊθ / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
opposing, preventing, or inhibiting growth or expansion

Examples

Antigrowth policies
The government introduced antigrowth regulations to protect the environment from overdevelopment.
Critics argue that antigrowth policies can slow down economic progress.
Some communities adopt antigrowth measures to preserve their rural character.

Related Terms

  • antonym: progrowth - supporting or encouraging growth
  • synonym: restrictive - limiting or controlling something
