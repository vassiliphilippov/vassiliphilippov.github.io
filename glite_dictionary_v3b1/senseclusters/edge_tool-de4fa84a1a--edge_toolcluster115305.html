edge tool

noun
cutting tool with a sharp edge
Pronunciation: / ɛdʒ tuːl / (AmE), / ɛdʒ tʃuːl / (BrE)
a tool with a sharp cutting edge, such as a chisel, knife, plane, or gouge, used for cutting, shaping, or carving material

Examples

A edge tool for carving
Many traditional woodworkers rely on hand-held edge tools for precise carving.
Sharpening an edge tool regularly extends its useful life and improves safety.
The carpenter selected an edge tool appropriate for shaping the oak board.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blunt instrument - a tool without a sharp edge, used for striking
  • synonym: cutting tool - a tool designed for cutting materials
