last mile

noun
final stage of delivery to customer
4.1
Pronunciation: / læst maɪl / (AmE), / lɑːst maɪl / (BrE)
the final stage of delivering goods, services, or data from a distribution point to the end user or customer.

Examples

Last mile delivery
Logistics firms are investing heavily in the last mile to meet customer expectations for fast delivery.
Urban planners examine the last mile when deciding where to place parcel lockers and bike lanes.
Retailers open micro-fulfillment centers to shorten the last mile and lower their shipping costs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: final mile - the concluding segment of a delivery route or service chain to the customer.
  • synonym: last-mile delivery - the process of transporting goods from a local distribution center to the final consumer's address.
