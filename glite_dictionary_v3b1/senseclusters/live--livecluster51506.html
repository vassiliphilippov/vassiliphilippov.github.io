live

adjective
highly reverberant (of a space)
Pronunciation: / laɪv / (AmE), / laɪv / (BrE)
(of a room or space) having highly reverberant acoustics, causing sounds to echo and persist.

Examples

Live room acoustics
A live acoustic environment can make speeches harder to understand due to excessive reverberation.
Some studios are designed to be live, enhancing certain musical styles with echoes.
The orchestra preferred performing in the live auditorium for its rich sound.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dead - (of a room) having little to no reverberation, acoustically absorbent
