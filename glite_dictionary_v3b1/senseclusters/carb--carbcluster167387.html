carb

noun
carbohydrate food
2.8
Pronunciation: / kɑrb / (AmE), / kɑːb / (BrE)
an informal term for a food that contains carbohydrates, such as bread, rice, or pasta, or for carbohydrates themselves

Examples

Low carb diet
She tries to avoid eating too many carbs when she is on a diet.
Pasta and bread are common carbs that people eat for energy.
Many athletes eat extra carbs before a big game to boost their performance.
Many athletes eat extra carbs before a big competition to boost their energy.
She tries to limit carbs in her diet to lose weight.
Bread, pasta, and potatoes are all considered carbs by nutritionists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carbohydrate - a type of nutrient found in foods like bread and pasta
  • antonym: fat - a nutrient found in foods like oil and butter
  • antonym: protein - a nutrient found in foods like meat and beans
