mascara

noun
cosmetic for eyelashes
6.5
Pronunciation: / məˈskɛrə / (AmE), / mæˈskɑː.rə / (BrE)
A cosmetic used to darken, thicken, and define the eyelashes.

Examples

Black mascara brush
After removing her makeup, she noticed traces of mascara on her cotton pad.
She applied a new waterproof mascara before going to the pool.
The mascara made her eyelashes appear longer and fuller.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lipstick - cosmetic for coloring the lips
