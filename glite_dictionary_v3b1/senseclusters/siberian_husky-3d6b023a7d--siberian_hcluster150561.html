Siberian husky

noun
breed of sled dog from Siberia
6.1 2.0
Pronunciation: /saɪ.ˈbɪr.iː.ən ˈhʌs.kɪ/ (AmE), /saɪ.ˈbɪə.rɪ.ən ˈhʌs.kɪ/ (BrE)
A medium-sized breed of dog developed in northeastern Siberia, known for its thick coat, erect ears, and use as a sled dog.

Examples

A Siberian husky dog
Many Siberian huskies participate in long-distance sled dog races in Alaska.
She adopted a Siberian husky to join her on winter hikes in the mountains.
The Siberian husky is known for its striking blue eyes and friendly temperament.
