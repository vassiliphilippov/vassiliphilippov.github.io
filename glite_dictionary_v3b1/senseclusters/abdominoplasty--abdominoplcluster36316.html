abdominoplasty

noun
cosmetic surgery on the abdomen
7.8
Pronunciation: / æbˈdɑːmɪnəˌplæsti / (AmE), / æbˌdɒmɪnəʊˈplæsti / (BrE)
a cosmetic surgical procedure to remove excess skin and fat from the abdomen and tighten the abdominal wall

Examples

An abdominoplasty surgery
After losing significant weight, she decided to have an abdominoplasty to remove the excess skin.
Recovery from abdominoplasty can take several weeks, depending on the extent of the surgery.
The surgeon explained the risks and benefits of abdominoplasty before the procedure.
