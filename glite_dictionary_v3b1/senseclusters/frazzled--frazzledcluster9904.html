frazzled

adjective
exhausted or stressed
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfræzəld / (AmE), / ˈfræzəld / (BrE)
extremely tired, stressed, or worn out, especially from overwork or worry

Examples

Frazzled after work
By the end of the busy week, he looked completely frazzled and needed a break.
After dealing with the noisy children all afternoon, she felt utterly frazzled.
The frazzled teacher tried to stay calm despite the chaos in the classroom.
