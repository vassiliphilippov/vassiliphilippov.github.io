cross-eyed

adjective
having eyes that turn inward; strabismus
Pronunciation: / krɔsˈaɪd / (AmE), / krɒsˈaɪd / (BrE)
Having eyes that turn inward towards the nose, resulting in a squint or a condition known as convergent strabismus.

Examples

Cross-eyed person
People used to tease him for being cross-eyed as a child.
She was born cross-eyed and had surgery to correct her vision.
The puppy looked adorably cross-eyed when it tried to focus on its nose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strabismic - having strabismus (eye misalignment)
  • antonym: wall-eyed - having divergent strabismus (eyes that turn outward)
