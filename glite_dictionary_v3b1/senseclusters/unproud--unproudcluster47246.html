unproud

adjective
lacking pride
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpraʊd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpraʊd / (BrE)
not feeling or showing pride; lacking a sense of pride in oneself or something.

Examples

Feeling unproud of self
Despite his success, he remained unproud and always credited his team.
She was unproud of her accomplishments, preferring to stay out of the spotlight.
Even after winning the award, he felt unproud because he believed others deserved it more.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low view of one's importance
  • antonym: proud - feeling deep pleasure or satisfaction as a result of achievements
