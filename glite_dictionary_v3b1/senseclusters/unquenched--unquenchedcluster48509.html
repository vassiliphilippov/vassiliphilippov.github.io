unquenched

adjective
not satisfied
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkwɛntʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwɛntʃt / (BrE)
not satisfied, extinguished, or put out; still active or present.

Examples

Unquenched thirst
Her unquenched thirst for knowledge drove her to read every book she could find.
The fire burned with unquenched intensity throughout the night.
Despite many setbacks, his unquenched ambition kept him moving forward.

Related Terms

  • antonym: quenched - satisfied, extinguished, or put out
  • synonym: unsatisfied - not fulfilled or content
