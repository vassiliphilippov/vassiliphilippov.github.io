camp-made

adjective
made at a summer camp
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmp ˌmeɪd / (AmE), / ˈkæmp ˌmeɪd / (BrE)
Made at a summer camp, especially as part of arts-and-crafts activities.

Examples

Camp-made crafts
He proudly showed off his camp-made birdhouse at the family picnic.
Her camp-made bracelet brings back fond memories of childhood summers.
The arts room was filled with camp-made crafts before the end-of-session exhibition.
