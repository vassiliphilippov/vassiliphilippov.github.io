censored

adjective
suppressed by censorship
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsərd / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsəd / (BrE)
suppressed, altered, or removed in accordance with the requirements of a censor; subject to censorship

Examples

A censored version
She watched the censored version of the film on television.
Some books remain censored in certain countries due to their content.
The report was heavily censored before its public release.

Related Terms

  • synonym: redacted - edited or altered, often with parts removed
  • antonym: uncensored - not suppressed or altered by censorship
