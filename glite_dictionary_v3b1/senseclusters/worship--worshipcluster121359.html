worship

verb
admire or love greatly, often excessively
3.4 4.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…úÀêr É…™p / (AmE), / Ààw…úÀê É…™p / (BrE)
to love, admire, or respect someone or something greatly, often to an excessive or uncritical degree

Examples

To worship a hero
As a child, she used to worship her older brother and wanted to be just like him.
He worships the ground his favorite teacher walks on.
Some people worship celebrities as if they were gods.

Synonyms

  • idolize
    4.9 1.77/m
    idolize means to admire someone very much and ignore their faults, while worship can mean deep respect often with religious feeling.
  • deify
    8.4 0.60/m
    deify means treating someone as a god or perfect being, while worship means giving great respect or love, sometimes religiously.
  • worship the ground someone walks on
    7.4 0.00/m
    worship the ground someone walks on means adoring someone in an overly eager, obvious way, while worship is the general word for deep respect.
  • hero-worship
    6.5 0.09/m
    hero-worship means seeing someone as perfect and praising them publicly, while worship is broader and can be religious or personal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adore - to love and respect someone deeply
  • synonym: idolize - to admire or love someone very much or too much
