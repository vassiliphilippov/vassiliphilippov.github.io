cabriole

noun
dressage horse leap
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæbriˌoʊl / (AmE), / ˈkæbriˌəʊl / (BrE)
a leap performed by a horse in dressage, where the horse jumps into the air and kicks out with its hind legs

Examples

Horse cabriole jump
The stallion performed a flawless cabriole during the dressage competition.
Spectators applauded as the horse executed a high cabriole in the arena.
Mastering the cabriole requires years of training for both horse and rider.

Related Terms

  • synonym: levade - a dressage movement where the horse raises its forelegs and balances on its hind legs
  • synonym: piaffe - a dressage movement where the horse trots in place
