buzz

noun
continuous, low humming sound
1.0 3.95/m

a continuous, low, humming sound, like that produced by a bee or a vibrating object

Examples

Synonyms

  • hum
    1.0 6.25/m
    hum is a low, steady sound from machines or bees, while buzz is a sharper, more insect like vibration.
  • thrum
    7.2 0.23/m
    thrum is a steady, rhythmic vibration you feel as well as hear, while buzz is usually a higher, sharper hum.
  • whirr
    6.7 1.11/m
    whirr is a fast, continuous vibrating noise from moving parts, while buzz usually means a steady insect like hum.
