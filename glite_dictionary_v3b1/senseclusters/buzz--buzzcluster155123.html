buzz

noun
continuous, low humming sound
1.0
Pronunciation: / bʌz / (AmE), / bʌz / (BrE)
a continuous, low, humming sound, like that produced by a bee or a vibrating object

Examples

The buzz of bees
The buzz of conversation faded as the speaker took the stage.
The buzz of the bees filled the summer garden.
We could hear the constant buzz of the machinery from the next room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hum - a continuous, low, droning sound
  • synonym: whirr - a low, regular sound like repeated rapid movement
