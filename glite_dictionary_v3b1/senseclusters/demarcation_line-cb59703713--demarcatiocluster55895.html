demarcation line

noun
boundary marking limits of area
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌdiːmɑːrˈkeɪʃən laɪn / (AmE), / ˌdiːˌmɑːˈkeɪʃən laɪn / (BrE)
a boundary that marks the limits of a specific area or zone

Examples

A demarcation line boundary
After the treaty, a new demarcation line was drawn separating the territories.
Crossing the demarcation line without permission was strictly forbidden by both countries.
The fence was erected along the demarcation line between the two properties.
