Teflon

noun
non-stick synthetic resin for cookware and industry
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛf.lɑn / (AmE), / ˈtɛf.lɒn / (BrE)
a synthetic resin (polytetrafluoroethylene) used especially as a non-stick coating for cookware and in industrial applications requiring surfaces to resist sticking or chemical reaction

Examples

Teflon coating
Industrial machinery often relies on Teflon parts for their non-stick properties.
She prefers Teflon-lined bakeware for easier clean-up after cooking.
The frying pan has a Teflon coating that prevents food from sticking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polytetrafluoroethylene - the chemical name for the polymer known as Teflon
