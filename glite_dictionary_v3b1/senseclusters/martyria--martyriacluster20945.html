martyria

noun
shrine for martyr
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɑːrˈtɪriə / (AmE), / mɑːˈtɪərɪə / (BrE)
a shrine or church built to commemorate a martyr or an event of martyrdom

Examples

Ancient martyria church
Pilgrims traveled long distances to visit the ancient martyria in the Holy Land.
The church was built as a martyria to honor the saint who died there.
Archaeologists discovered the remains of several early Christian martyria beneath the city.

  • synonym: shrine - a place regarded as holy because of its associations
