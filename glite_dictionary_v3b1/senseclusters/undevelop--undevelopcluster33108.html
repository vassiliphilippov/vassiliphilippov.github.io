undevelop

verb
to restore land
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈvɛləp / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈvɛləp / (BrE)
to remove buildings, roads, or other development from land and return it to a more natural or less developed state

Examples

To undevelop land
The city began undeveloping the old industrial waterfront to restore wetlands and public green space.
Environmental groups support undeveloping certain areas to protect wildlife habitats.
After the factory closed, the town considered undeveloping the site to create a nature reserve.

Related Terms

  • antonym: develop - to build on or improve land for human use
  • synonym: restore - to bring something back to its original condition
