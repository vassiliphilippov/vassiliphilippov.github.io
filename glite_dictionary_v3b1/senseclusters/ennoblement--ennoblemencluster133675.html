ennoblement

noun
the act or state of becoming noble
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnoʊbəlmənt / (AmE), / ɪˈnəʊblmənt / (BrE)
the act of making someone a noble or raising them to a higher social or moral status

Examples

The ennoblement of a person
Her actions led to the ennoblement of her entire family in the community.
The ennoblement of the scientist was recognized by granting him a title.
The novel explores themes of ennoblement through self-sacrifice and courage.
Following her ennoblement, she took part in many royal ceremonies.
He viewed his ennoblement as both an honor and a responsibility.
The king's ennoblement of loyal subjects was a common practice in the 18th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ennoble - to make someone a noble or to elevate in dignity or honor
