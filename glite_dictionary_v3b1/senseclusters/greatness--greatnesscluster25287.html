greatness

noun
the state of being important or eminent
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being very important, distinguished, or eminent

Examples

Her greatness in history
Her contributions to science secured her place in history for her greatness.
Many strive for greatness, but few achieve lasting recognition.
The team's greatness was evident throughout the championship season.

  • antonym: mediocrity - the quality of being average or unremarkable
