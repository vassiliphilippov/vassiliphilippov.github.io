Synonyms

  • well-fixed
    6.6 0.01/m
    well-fixed sounds more formal and British, describing steady money for life, while comfortable means worry-free finances.
  • well-to-do
    4.2 1.89/m
    well-to-do describes having plenty of money for a good life, while comfortable means enough money without extra luxury.
  • prosperous
    4.1 7.64/m
    prosperous shows success and growing wealth in business or life, while comfortable means financial ease without being rich.
  • well-heeled
    7.2 0.81/m
    well-heeled refers to people with wealth and fine things, while comfortable means having enough money but not excess luxury.
  • well-situated
    4.8 0.09/m
    well-situated often means having an advantageous position or resources, while comfortable refers specifically to steady financial security.
