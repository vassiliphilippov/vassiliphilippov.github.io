reallot

verb
allocate again after initial allotment
7.5 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / riÀê…ôÀàl…ët / (AmE), / ÀåriÀê…ôÀàl…ít / (BrE)
to allot or assign (something) again, especially after an initial distribution has changed

Examples

To reallot tickets
After the deadline passed, they had to reallot the remaining tickets among the applicants.
The committee decided to reallot the funds to prioritize community projects.
The housing authority plans to reallot the apartments to new residents next year.

Synonyms
