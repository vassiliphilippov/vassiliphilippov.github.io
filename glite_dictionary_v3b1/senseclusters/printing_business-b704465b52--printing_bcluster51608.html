Synonyms

    printing company is a firm that provides commercial printing for books and brochures, while printing business is the general term for such firms.
  • printing concern
    4.1 0.00/m
    printing concern sounds formal and businesslike and refers to a company that makes printed materials, while printing business is a neutral everyday term.
  • printworks
    3.9 0.00/m
    printworks is a large factory or building that produces books or packaging in volume, while printing business is any company offering printing services.
  • printery
    5.7 0.01/m

  • print shop
    2.8 0.44/m

  • printing shop
