genderist attitude

noun
view or behavior discriminating by gender
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛndərɪst əˈtɪtjud / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛndərɪst əˈtɪtjuːd / (BrE)
a view or behavior that discriminates against people based on their gender

Examples

Genderist attitude in workplace
Her report highlights how a genderist attitude can affect career advancement in the workplace.
Many activists are challenging the genderist attitudes that persist in politics and media.
The manager was criticized for his genderist attitude toward female employees.

Related Terms
