mal de mer

noun
seasickness; illness from motion at sea
Pronunciation: / mæl də ˈmɛr / (AmE), / mal də ˈmɛə / (BrE)
illness caused by motion while traveling by sea; seasickness

Examples

Mal de mer sickness
After just an hour on the ferry, she began to suffer from mal de mer.
For some people, even calm waters can trigger a severe case of mal de mer.
He took medication to prevent mal de mer during the long boat trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seasickness - illness caused by motion of a ship or boat at sea
