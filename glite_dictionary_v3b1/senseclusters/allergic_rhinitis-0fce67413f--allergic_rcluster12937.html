allergic rhinitis

noun
inflammation of the nose caused by allergy
Pronunciation: / əˈlɝdʒɪk raɪˈnaɪtɪs / (AmE), / əˈlɜːdʒɪk raɪˈnaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A condition in which the nose becomes inflamed due to an allergic reaction, resulting in symptoms such as sneezing, itching, and a runny nose.

Examples

Chronic allergic rhinitis
Chronic allergic rhinitis can impact concentration and quality of life.
His doctor prescribed antihistamines to help control his allergic rhinitis.
Many people develop allergic rhinitis during spring when pollen counts are high.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hay fever - inflammation of the nose due to pollen allergy
