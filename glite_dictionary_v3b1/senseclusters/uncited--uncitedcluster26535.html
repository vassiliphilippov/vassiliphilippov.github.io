uncited

adjective
not officially recognized
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsaɪtɪd / (BrE)
not formally praised, recognized, or given an official citation or award.

Examples

Uncited hero
Despite his bravery, the soldier's actions went uncited in the official after-action report.
Her contributions to the project remained uncited in the final acknowledgments.
Many acts of kindness often go uncited and unnoticed by the public.
