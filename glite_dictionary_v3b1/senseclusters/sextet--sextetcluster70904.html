sextet

noun
group of six; set of six things
8.2
Pronunciation: / sɛkˈstɛt / (AmE), / sɛkˈstɛt / (BrE)
a group or set of six people or things considered as a unit

She joined a vocal sextet that performs Renaissance music.
The jazz sextet played an energetic set at the club last night.
