GI

noun
abbreviation for 'geographical indication' (legal product origin marking)
8.7
Pronunciation: / dʒi aɪ / (AmE), / dʒiː aɪ / (BrE)
(law) An abbreviation for 'geographical indication', a name or sign used on certain products which indicates their specific geographical origin and attributes or reputation due to that origin.

Examples

GI status
Champagne has a GI, protecting its name under international trade law.
Many Indian teas have obtained GI status, helping to preserve their unique identities.
Only cheese made in certain regions can use the GI of 'Parmesan'.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trademark - a symbol, word, or words legally registered for use by a single company
