draconic

adjective
like a dragon
Pronunciation: / drəˈkɑnɪk / (AmE), / drəˈkɒnɪk / (BrE)
resembling or relating to a dragon, especially in appearance or character

Examples

Draconic statue
The ancient temple was guarded by a draconic statue with fierce eyes.
Her costume featured draconic scales and large, leathery wings.
The villain's draconic roar echoed through the dark cave.
