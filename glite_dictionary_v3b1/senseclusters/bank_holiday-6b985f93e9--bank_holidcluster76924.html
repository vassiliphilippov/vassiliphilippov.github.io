bank holiday

noun
public holiday when banks are closed
6.2
Pronunciation: / bæŋk ˈhɑːlɪˌdeɪ /; / bæŋk ˈhɑlɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / bæŋk ˈhɒlɪdeɪ /; / baŋk ˈhɒlɪdeɪ / (BrE)
