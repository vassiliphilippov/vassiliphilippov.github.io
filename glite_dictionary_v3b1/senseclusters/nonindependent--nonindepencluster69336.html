nonindependent

adjective
showing dependence
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɪndɪˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɪndɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
not statistically independent; showing dependence or a relationship with another variable or event.

Examples

Nonindependent samples
The results of the experiment were nonindependent because the samples influenced each other.
In this study, the variables are nonindependent due to a shared underlying factor.
Statistical tests assume data points are not nonindependent unless otherwise specified.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dependent - relying on or influenced by something else
  • antonym: independent - not influenced or affected by others
