heat ray

noun
ray or beam that generates heat
Pronunciation: / hit ɹeɪ / (AmE), / hiːt ɹeɪ / (BrE)
a beam of energy that produces heat, often imagined as a weapon or scientific device

Examples

A heat ray weapon
Scientists once dreamed of developing a heat ray to melt enemy tanks from a distance.
The illustration showed a spaceship firing a heat ray at the alien creature.
The villain threatened the city with his powerful heat ray weapon.
