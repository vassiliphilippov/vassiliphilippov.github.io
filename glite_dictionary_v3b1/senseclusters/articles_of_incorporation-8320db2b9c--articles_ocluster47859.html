articles of incorporation

noun
legal document forming a corporation
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːr.tɪ.kəlz əv ɪnˌkɔːr.pəˈreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈɑː.tɪ.klz əv ɪnˌkɔː.pəˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)

Examples
