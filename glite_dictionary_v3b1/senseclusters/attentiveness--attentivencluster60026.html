Synonyms

  • consideration
    3.2 4/m

  • thoughtfulness
    2.9 0.6/m
    thoughtfulness is kind, caring behavior that shows you think of others, while attentiveness is mainly about closely noticing their needs.
  • mindfulness
    4.2 5/m
