black-fronted bush shrike

noun
African bird species with a black front
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈfrʌn.tɪd bʊʃ ʃraɪk / (AmE), / blæk ˈfrɒn.tɪd bʊʃ ʃraɪk / (BrE)
a species of bush shrike (Chlorophoneus nigrifrons), a brightly colored African bird known for its prominent black face markings and musical calls

Examples

The black-fronted bush shrike bird
Birdwatchers traveled to the reserve hoping to spot a black-fronted bush shrike among the trees.
The black-fronted bush shrike is admired for its vivid plumage and melodic song throughout the African woodlands.
Unlike other shrikes, the black-fronted bush shrike prefers dense vegetation for nesting and feeding.
