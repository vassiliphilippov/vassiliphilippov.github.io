Achatina

noun
large African snail genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌækəˈtaɪnə / (AmE), / ˌækəˈtiːnə / (BrE)
a genus of large land snails native to Africa, some species of which are kept as pets or studied for their size

Examples

The Achatina snail
The Achatina can grow much larger than most other land snails.
Many people keep Achatina as exotic pets because of their impressive size.
Researchers study Achatina to learn more about the biology of giant snails.
