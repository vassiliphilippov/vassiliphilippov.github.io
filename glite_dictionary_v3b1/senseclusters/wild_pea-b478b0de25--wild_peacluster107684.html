wild pea

noun
wild-growing climbing legume plant
Pronunciation: / waɪld pi / (AmE), / waɪld piː / (BrE)
any of several climbing or vining plants of the legume family that resemble or are related to the cultivated pea and grow in the wild

Examples

Wild pea plant
Researchers study the wild pea to learn about drought resistance in plants.
Some species of wild pea are valued for their beautiful flowers as well as their hardy nature.
The wild pea thrives in rocky, open fields where few other legumes grow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pea - a round, green, edible seed or the plant it grows on, in the legume family
