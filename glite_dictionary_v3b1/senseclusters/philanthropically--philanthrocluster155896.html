philanthropically

adverb
in a charitable or generous manner
Pronunciation: / fɪˌlænθrəˈpɪkli / (AmE), / fɪˌlanθrəˈpɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a desire to promote the welfare of others, especially by donating money or resources to good causes.

Examples

Give philanthropically
Many wealthy individuals choose to give philanthropically as part of their legacy.
She donated philanthropically to several hospitals in her community.
The organization has always acted philanthropically by supporting educational initiatives.
