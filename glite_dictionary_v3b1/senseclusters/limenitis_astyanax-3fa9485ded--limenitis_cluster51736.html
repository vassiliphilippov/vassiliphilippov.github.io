Limenitis astyanax

noun
a North American butterfly species
Pronunciation: / lɪˈmɛnɪtɪs ˌæstiˈænæks / (AmE), / lɪˈmɛnɪtɪs ˌæstiˈanæks / (BrE)
a species of butterfly, commonly called the red-spotted purple, found in North America, with blue and black coloring and distinctive red spots on the underwing surfaces

Examples

Limenitis astyanax butterfly
Many nature enthusiasts photograph Limnitis astyanax during butterfly migrations in the summer.
The Limnitis astyanax is easily recognized by its iridescent blue wings and reddish markings.
The caterpillar stage of Limnitis astyanax feeds primarily on the leaves of wild cherry trees.
