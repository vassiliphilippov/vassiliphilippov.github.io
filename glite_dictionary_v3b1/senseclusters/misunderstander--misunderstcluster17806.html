misunderstander

noun
person who misinterprets
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˌʌndərˈstændər / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˌʌndəˈstændə / (BrE)
a person who fails to understand something correctly or misinterprets information

Examples

A confused misunderstander
The teacher patiently explained the concept again to the misunderstander in the class.
During the meeting, one misunderstander kept asking questions that had already been answered.
She realized she had been a misunderstander after rereading the instructions more carefully.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confuser - a person who causes confusion
  • antonym: interpreter - a person who explains or translates meaning
