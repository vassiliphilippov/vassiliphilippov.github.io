Ask Jeeves

proper noun
former search engine accepting English questions
7.3
Pronunciation: / æsk ˈdʒivz / (AmE), / ɑːsk ˈdʒiːvz / (BrE)
the name of a search engine, popular in the late 1990s and early 2000s, that allowed users to enter questions in plain English to find answers on the internet

Examples

Ask Jeeves search engine
Before Google became dominant, Ask Jeeves was famous for understanding natural language questions.
He recalls typing his homework questions into Ask Jeeves hoping for quick answers.
In the early days of the internet, my parents always used Ask Jeeves to look up information.
