tin man

noun
tinsmith or metalworker
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪn mæn / (AmE), / ˈtɪn mæn / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs items made of tin or light metal, such as a tinsmith

Examples

Tin man repairs pots
The tin man crafted a new watering can for the garden.
Villagers often brought their broken pots to the tin man for repair.
As a skilled tin man, he could fix almost any household item made of metal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blacksmith - a person who makes or repairs things made of iron
  • synonym: tinsmith - a person who makes or repairs things made of tin
