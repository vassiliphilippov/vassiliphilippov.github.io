bulltoad

noun
large toad species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌtoʊd / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌtəʊd / (BrE)
a large species of toad, sometimes used regionally to refer to the cane toad

Examples

A giant bulltoad
The children were fascinated by the huge bulltoad in the garden.
A bulltoad can often be found near ponds during the rainy season.
Local farmers warned us about the poisonous skin of the bulltoad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cane toad - a large, invasive toad species native to Central and South America
  • antonym: frog - a tailless amphibian with smooth skin and long legs for jumping
