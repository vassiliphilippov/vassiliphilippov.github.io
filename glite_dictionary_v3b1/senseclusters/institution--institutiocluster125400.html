institution

noun
long-established custom or practice
4.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.stɪ.ˈtuː.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪn.stɪ.ˈtjuː.ʃən/ /; / /ɪn.stɪ.ˈtʃuː.ʃən/ (BrE)
