maiden name

noun
a woman's surname before marriage
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdən neɪm / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdn neɪm / (BrE)
a woman's surname before she is married and changes her name by marriage

Examples

Her maiden name
Her maiden name was Johnson before she married and became Mrs. Smith.
On the marriage certificate, her maiden name is listed next to her married name.
The bank requested her maiden name for security verification.
