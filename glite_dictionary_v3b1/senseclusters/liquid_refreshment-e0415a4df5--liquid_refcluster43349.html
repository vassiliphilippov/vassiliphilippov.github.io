liquid refreshment

noun
a drink that refreshes
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪd rɪˈfrɛʃmənt / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd rɪˈfrɛʃmənt / (BrE)
a drink, especially one that is cool or refreshing, sometimes implying an alcoholic beverage

Examples

A glass of liquid refreshment
After the long meeting, we paused for some liquid refreshment in the lounge.
Nothing beats an ice-cold lemonade as a liquid refreshment on a hot afternoon.
They served a variety of liquid refreshments at the summer party, including juices and cocktails.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beverage - a drink, especially one other than water
  • antonym: food - solid nourishment, as opposed to drinks
