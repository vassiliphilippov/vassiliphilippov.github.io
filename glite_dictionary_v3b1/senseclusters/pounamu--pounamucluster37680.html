pounamu

noun
New Zealand greenstone
9.9
Pronunciation: / poʊˈnɑːmuː / (AmE), / paʊˈnɑːmuː / (BrE)
a type of greenstone or jade found in New Zealand, highly valued in Māori culture for making ornaments, jewelry, tools, or weapons
