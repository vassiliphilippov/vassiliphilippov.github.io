comm

noun
informal message
7.4 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / kÉ‘m / (AmE), / kÉ’m / (BrE)
an informal message or piece of information sent to someone, especially by radio or electronic means

Examples

A comm message
She received a comm from headquarters with the latest updates.
Before the meeting, he sent out a quick comm to the entire team.
The pilot waited for a comm from air traffic control before taking off.

Synonyms

  • com
    8.0 0.33/m
    com is a short electronic message sent by phone or computer, while comm is an informal message often sent by radio or email.
  • communication
    2.3 17/m
    communication is a general transfer of information between people or groups, while comm is a short informal message used over radio or electronic means.

Related Terms
