twirly dress

noun
dress with a flared, spinning skirt
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɝli drɛs / (AmE), / ˈtwɜːli drɛs / (BrE)
a dress with a skirt that flares or spins outward when the wearer turns or twirls

Examples

A bright twirly dress
For her birthday party, she picked out a bright pink twirly dress.
The twirly dress was perfect for dancing at the wedding reception.
The little girl loved her twirly dress and spun around to make the skirt fly.
