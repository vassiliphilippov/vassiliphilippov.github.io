chevalier

noun
French knight or honoree
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈvæl.jɪr / (AmE), / ˌʃɛvəˈlɪə / (BrE)
a knight or a member of certain orders of honor, especially in France

Examples

French chevalier
He was named a chevalier of the French Legion of Honor for his achievements.
The title of chevalier was bestowed upon her grandfather by the French government.
As a chevalier, he attended many official ceremonies and events.
