Burkean

noun
follower of Burke
9.9 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…úÀêrki…ôn / (AmE), / Ààb…úÀêki…ôn / (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the political ideas or philosophy of Edmund Burke, especially regarding conservatism and gradual change

Examples

A Burkean thinker
The senator, a well-known Burkean, argued for preserving long-standing traditions in government.
Many Burkeans believe that change should happen slowly and with respect for history.
Her Burkean views influenced her cautious approach to political reform.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conservative - a person who favors traditional views and values
  • antonym: radical - a person who advocates for thorough or complete political or social reform
