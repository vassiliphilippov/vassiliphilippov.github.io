embryo

noun
early-stage developing organism
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛm.bri.oʊ /; / ˈɛmbrioʊ / (AmE), / ˈɛm.bri.əʊ /; / ˈɛmbrɪəʊ / (BrE)
an animal or human organism in the early stages of development before birth or hatching
