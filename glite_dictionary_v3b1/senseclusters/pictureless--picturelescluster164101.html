pictureless

adjective
lacking visual charm
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪktʃərləs / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃələs / (BrE)
plain, unattractive, or lacking visual charm; not picturesque.

Examples

Pictureless street
The industrial suburb was oddly pictureless beneath the gray sky and rows of concrete warehouses.
Despite its history, the town center is rather pictureless and lacks any notable landmarks.
The hotel room was clean but completely pictureless, with bare walls and no decorations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: picturesque - visually attractive, especially in a charming way
  • synonym: plain - simple or lacking decoration
  • synonym: unattractive - not pleasing to look at
