servingman

noun
male servant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrvɪŋˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪŋˌmæn / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a male servant or attendant, especially in a household or at events

Examples

The servingman brought water
The servingman brought water to the guests at the banquet.
In the old play, a loyal servingman assists his master through many troubles.
A servingman waited patiently by the door, ready to help the household.
