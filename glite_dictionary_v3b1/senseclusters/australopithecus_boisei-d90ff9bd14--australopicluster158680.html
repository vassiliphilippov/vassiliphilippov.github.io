Australopithecus boisei

noun
extinct East African hominid species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɔstrələˌpɪθəkəs ˈbɔɪzaɪ / (AmE), / ˌɒstrələˌpɪθəkəs ˈbɔɪzaɪ / (BrE)
an extinct species of large-toothed early hominid that lived in eastern Africa about 1 to 2 million years ago

Examples
