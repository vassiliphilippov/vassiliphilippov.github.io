  • rostrum
    7.1 0.1/m
    General raised platform giving prominence to whoever stands or speaks
    vs soapbox: A platform raised above the surrounding level, used to give prominence to the person standing or speaking on it.
  • podium
    3.8 5/m
    Small raised platform used for formal speeches or award presentations
