confidant

noun
trusted person for private matters
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.fɪ.dænt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.fɪ.dænt / (BrE)
A person in whom one confides one's private matters, trusting them not to disclose the information to others.

Examples

A trusted confidant
Emma valued her sister as both a friend and a confidant.
Over the years, he became my most trusted confidant and advisor.
She needed a confidant to share her worries about the future with.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: acquaintance - a person one knows, but not a close friend or confidant
  • synonym: confidante - female form of confidant
