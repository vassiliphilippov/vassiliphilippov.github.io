fusillade

noun
simultaneous burst of gunfire
9.0 7.6
Pronunciation: /fjuː.zɪ.ˈlɑːd/ /; / /fjuː.sɪ.ˈleɪd/ (AmE), /fjuː.zɪ.ˈlɑːd/ /; / /fjuː.zə.ˈlɑːd/ (BrE)
a rapid and continuous series of shots, attacks, or similar actions happening all at once

Examples

A fusillade of bullets
A sudden fusillade shattered the silence, scattering everyone in panic.
During the parade, a fusillade of blanks was fired into the air as a salute.
The soldiers responded with a fusillade that forced the enemy to retreat.
The soldiers endured a fusillade of bullets as they advanced across the field.
A sudden fusillade of questions overwhelmed the speaker after his controversial statement.
The windows shattered under a fusillade of stones thrown by the angry crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrage - a concentrated artillery bombardment over a wide area
  • synonym: volley - a number of bullets, rocks, or other objects thrown or fired at once
