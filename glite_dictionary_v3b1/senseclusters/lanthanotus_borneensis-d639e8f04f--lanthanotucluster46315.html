Lanthanotus borneensis

noun
a lizard species native to Borneo
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌlænθəˈnoʊtəs bɔrˈneɪənˌsɪs / (AmE), / ˌlanθəˈnəʊtəs bɔːˈniːənˌsɪs / (BrE)
A species of stout-bodied lizard native to Borneo, also known as the Borneo earless monitor lizard.
