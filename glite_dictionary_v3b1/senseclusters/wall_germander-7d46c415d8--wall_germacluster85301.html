wall germander

noun
a European perennial subshrub with showy flowers
Pronunciation: / wɔl dʒərˈmændɚ / (AmE), / wɔːl dʒəˈmændə / (BrE)
A European perennial subshrub with red-purple or bright rose flowers marked with red and white spots; grown as an ornamental plant.

Examples

A wall germander plant

Gardeners admire the wall germander for its bright, long-lasting flowers.
