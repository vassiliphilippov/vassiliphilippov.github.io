sleep

noun
euphemism for death
1.0 9/m
Pronunciation: / slip /; / sliÀêp / (AmE), / sliÀêp / (BrE)
a state of inactivity or rest that resembles sleep, such as hibernation in animals or (figuratively) death

Examples

Eternal sleep
He hoped that when his time came, he would find peaceful sleep.
The phrase "put to sleep" is often used when discussing euthanasia for pets.
The poem spoke of eternal sleep as a release from pain.
