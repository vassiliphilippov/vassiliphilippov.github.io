Sherpa

noun
member of a Himalayan mountaineering people
6.8 0.62/m

Examples

A Sherpa guide

Many climbers hire a Sherpa to guide them to the summit of Mount Everest.
The Sherpas carried heavy equipment and set up camps for the expedition.
