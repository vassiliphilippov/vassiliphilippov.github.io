heelpiece

noun
ski binding part
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːlˌpiːs / (AmE), / ˈhiːlˌpiːs / (BrE)
the rear part of a ski binding that holds and releases the heel of a ski boot

Examples

Ski heelpiece
Before hitting the slopes, she checked that the heelpiece was properly adjusted on her skis.
A damaged heelpiece can make it unsafe to use your ski equipment.
The ski technician replaced the worn heelpiece to improve the binding's performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: toe piece - the front part of a ski binding that holds the toe of a ski boot
