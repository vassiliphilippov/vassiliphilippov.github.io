temperamental car

noun
unreliable or unpredictable car
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛmpɚəˈmɛntəl kɑr / (AmE), / ˌtɛmpərəˈmɛntl kɑː / (BrE)
a car that behaves unpredictably or is unreliable, often failing to work as expected

Examples

An old temperamental car
Our old temperamental car broke down again on the way to work.
She refuses to drive her temperamental car long distances because it's so unpredictable.
You never know if his temperamental car will start in the morning.

Related Terms
