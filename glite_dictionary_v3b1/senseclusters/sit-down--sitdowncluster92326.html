sit-down

noun
formal meal or meeting
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪtˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈsɪtˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a formal meal or meeting where people sit together, often for discussion or celebration

Examples

A sit-down dinner
The company organized a sit-down to celebrate the team's achievements.
We had a productive sit-down with the new manager to discuss our ideas.
The annual sit-down dinner brought together employees from all departments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banquet - a large formal meal for many people
  • synonym: gathering - a meeting or assembly of people
