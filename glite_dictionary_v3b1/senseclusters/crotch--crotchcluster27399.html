crotch

noun
area where legs join and surround genitals
Pronunciation: / krɑtʃ / (AmE), / krɒtʃ / (BrE)
the part of the body where the legs join the lower trunk, including the area around the genitals (also, the area at the front of the body where the inner thighs meet the trunk, especially in clothing)
