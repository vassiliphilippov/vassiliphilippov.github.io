percher

noun
person seated on a perch
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrtʃər / (AmE), / ˈpɜːtʃə / (BrE)
a person who is seated or positioned on a perch, especially in a high or precarious place.

Examples

A daring percher
Acrobats and performers such as the tightrope percher amazed audiences with their skill.
The daring percher balanced on the narrow ledge high above the crowd.
The statue depicted a small percher resting atop the old city gate.
