spiegeleisen

noun
pig iron containing manganese, used in steelmaking
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːɡl̩ˌaɪzən / (AmE), / ˈspiːɡl̩ˌaɪzən / (BrE)
Pig iron that contains a high percentage of manganese, used as a deoxidizing agent and to increase the manganese content in steelmaking.

Examples

High-manganese spiegeleisen
Early steelmakers relied on spiegeleisen to introduce manganese into their alloys.
The addition of spiegeleisen was crucial for improving the quality of the final steel product.
Without spiegeleisen, it would have been difficult to control the chemical composition of steel.
