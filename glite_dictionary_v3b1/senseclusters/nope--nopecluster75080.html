nope

interjection
informal refusal
7.7
Pronunciation: / noʊp / (AmE), / nəʊp / (BrE)
an informal way to say no or to refuse something

Examples

Nope, not today
"Did you see the movie last night?" "Nope, I was too busy with work."
When asked if he wanted more dessert, he simply replied, "nope."
She checked her phone for messages but found nope from her friends.
