taper

noun
slender candle with a wick
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪpər / (AmE), / ˈteɪpə / (BrE)
a slender candle, typically consisting of a stick of wax with a wick in the middle, used for lighting

Examples

A wax taper
He used a long taper to ignite the fire in the old fireplace.
In the dim library, only a single taper provided light for reading.
She lit a taper and placed it carefully on the windowsill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candle - a block of wax with a central wick for burning
