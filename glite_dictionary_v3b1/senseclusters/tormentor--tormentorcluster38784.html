tormentor

noun
a person who causes suffering
7.9 0.74/m
Pronunciation: / t…îrÀàm…õnt…ôr / (AmE), / t…îÀêÀàment…ô / (BrE)
a person who causes someone to suffer mental or physical pain, often deliberately and repeatedly

Examples

A cruel tormentor
She could not escape her tormentor, who followed her everywhere with cruel remarks.
The bully became the tormentor of many students in the school.
Years later, he finally confronted his childhood tormentor and found closure.

Synonyms

  • tormenter
    7.4 0.07/m
    tormenter is a less common spelling for someone who causes pain, while tormentor is the standard modern form.
  • abuser
    5.3 4.77/m
    abuser is someone who treats others cruelly or violently over time, while tormentor focuses on causing repeated pain.
  • maltreater
    9.9 0.00/m
    maltreater treats people cruelly or roughly, often over time, while tormentor stresses causing deliberate pain repeatedly.
  • harasser
    6.3 0.63/m
    harasser repeatedly annoys, threatens, or bothers someone, while tormentor implies causing deeper physical or mental pain.
  • victimizer
    8.3 0.26/m
    victimizer makes someone a victim by harming or exploiting them, while tormentor focuses on causing ongoing pain or suffering.
  • inflicter
    8.4 0.04/m
    inflicter causes an unpleasant thing or harm, but tormentor is specifically a person who causes repeated deliberate pain.
