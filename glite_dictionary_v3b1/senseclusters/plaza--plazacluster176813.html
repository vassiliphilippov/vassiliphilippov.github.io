plaza

noun
open public square or area
2.7 8.12/m
Pronunciation: / Ààpl…ëÀê.z…ô / (AmE), / Ààpl…ëÀê.z…ô / (BrE)
A large open public area in a city or town, often used for gatherings, pedestrian activity, or community events.

Examples

City plaza
Many tourists gathered in the plaza to watch the street performers.
The mayor held a rally at the central plaza to address the community.
The new shopping center was built around an open-air plaza with benches and fountains.

Synonyms

  • public square
    2.5 0.52/m
    a public square is where streets meet and people gather for events, while a plaza is a larger open area used for many community activities.
  • square
    1.0 27/m
    a square is a city space often formed where streets meet and used for gatherings, while a plaza is often larger and more for pedestrians.
  • piazza
    7.4 2.96/m
    a piazza is an Italian-style public area often framed by shops and cafes, while a plaza is a general large public space.
