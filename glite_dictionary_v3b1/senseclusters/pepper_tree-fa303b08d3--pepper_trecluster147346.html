pepper tree

noun
African deciduous tree with creamy flower clusters
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpər triː / (AmE), / ˈpɛpə triː / (BrE)
a type of tree with spreading branches and clusters of small flowers, called 'pepper tree' in different regions (can refer to different species)

Examples

The pepper tree flowers

Many birds gather among the branches of the old pepper tree near the village well.
The pepper tree in the garden provides ample shade during the hot afternoons.
Children enjoy playing beneath the drooping branches of the old pepper tree at the park.
Gardeners often plant pepper trees for their ornamental appearance and drought resistance.
The pepper tree in our yard provides shade and beautiful clusters of pink fruit in summer.
