horn-rimmed

adjective
having eyeglass frames made of horn or similar material
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrn ˌrɪmd / (AmE), / ˈhɔːn ˌrɪmd / (BrE)
(of eyeglasses) having frames made of horn, tortoiseshell, or plastic imitating these materials.

Examples

Horn-rimmed glasses
He always preferred horn-rimmed frames over metal ones for his spectacles.
She wore a pair of horn-rimmed glasses that gave her a scholarly appearance.
The detective adjusted his horn-rimmed glasses before examining the evidence.
