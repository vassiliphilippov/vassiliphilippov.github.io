glacial

adjective
relating to glaciers
Pronunciation: / ˈɡleɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˈɡleɪ.si.əl / (BrE)
of, relating to, or derived from a glacier; formed by or resulting from the action of glaciers.

Examples

Glacial valley
Many glacial features can be seen in the landscape of Norway.
Scientists study glacial movement to understand climate change.
These rocks are part of a glacial deposit left thousands of years ago.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glacier - a large mass of ice moving slowly over land
