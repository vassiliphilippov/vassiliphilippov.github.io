excoriation

noun
severe criticism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛkˌskɔriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˌskɔːrɪˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
severe or harsh criticism; strong censure

Examples

Public excoriation
After his speech, the politician faced excoriation from the media for his controversial remarks.
Her decision was met with public excoriation, leading to widespread debate about her actions.
The critic's excoriation of the author's latest novel was both detailed and relentless.
