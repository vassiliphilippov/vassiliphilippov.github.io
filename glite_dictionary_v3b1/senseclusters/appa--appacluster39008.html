appa

noun
South Asian term for father
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːpɑː / (AmE), / ˈʌpə / (BrE)
a term for father, especially used in South Asian families and communities

Examples

My appa smiles
When he returned home, he greeted his appa with a big smile.
Her appa always helped her with her homework in the evenings.
During the festival, the children made a special card for their appa.
