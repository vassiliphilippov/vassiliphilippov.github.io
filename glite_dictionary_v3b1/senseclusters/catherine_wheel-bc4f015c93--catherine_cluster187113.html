Catherine wheel

noun
spinning circular firework
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæθərɪn wiːl / (AmE), / ˈkæθərɪn wiːl / (BrE)
A type of circular firework that spins rapidly when ignited, sending out a display of colored sparks.

Examples

A Catherine wheel firework
The children watched in awe as the Catherine wheel spun and showered sparks across the garden.
The display was impressive, with several Catherine wheels lighting up the night sky.
We bought a large Catherine wheel for the Bonfire Night celebration.
