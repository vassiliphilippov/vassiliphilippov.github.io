electricity

noun
a form of energy from moving electric charge
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ɪ.lek.ˈtrɪ.sə.tiː/ /; / /ə.lek.ˈtrɪ.sə.tiː/ (AmE), /ɪ.lek.ˈtrɪ.sɪ.tiː/ /; / /e.lek.ˈtrɪ.sɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
a form of energy produced by the movement of electrons or charged particles, used to power devices, machines, and lighting

Examples
