Sempach

proper noun
Swiss town; site of 1386 Swiss victory
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmˌpɑk / (AmE), / ˈsɛmpak / (BrE)
A town in Switzerland, known chiefly as the site of a pivotal battle in 1386 where the Swiss Confederation defeated Hapsburg forces, leading to Swiss independence.
