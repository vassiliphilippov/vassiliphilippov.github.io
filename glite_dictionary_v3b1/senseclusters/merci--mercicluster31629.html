merci

proper noun
chocolate brand
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛrsi / (AmE), / ˈmɛːsi / (BrE)
a brand of boxed assorted chocolates produced by the German company August Storck, often given as a gift

Examples

A box of merci chocolates
She brought a box of merci chocolates to thank her hosts for dinner.
He received merci as a birthday present from his colleagues at work.
A box of merci is a popular gift during the holiday season in many countries.
