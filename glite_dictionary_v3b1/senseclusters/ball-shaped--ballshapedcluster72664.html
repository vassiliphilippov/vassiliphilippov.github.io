ball-shaped

adjective
having a spherical shape
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔl ˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈbɔːl ˌʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of a ball; spherical or nearly spherical in form

Examples

Ball-shaped seeds
Some seeds are ball-shaped, making them easy for animals to roll away.
The artist created a ball-shaped sculpture out of metal wires.
The lanterns hanging from the ceiling were all ball-shaped and glowed softly.
