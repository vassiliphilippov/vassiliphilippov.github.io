tactus

noun
light touch
Pronunciation: / ˈtæktəs / (AmE), / ˈtæktəs / (BrE)
the sense of touch or the act of touching something lightly

Examples

A gentle tactus
With a gentle tactus, she checked if the surface was still warm.
The artist used only a slight tactus to avoid damaging the delicate sculpture.
He recognized the fabric by the tactus of its smooth texture.
Newborns rely heavily on tactus to explore objects and bond with caregivers.
The experiment measured the sensitivity of tactus in different parts of the hand.
Artists who sculpt often have a highly developed sense of tactus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contact - the state or act of touching or being in touch
  • synonym: tactile - relating to or involving the sense of touch
  • synonym: touch - the act of coming into contact with something
