Tolstoyan

adjective
like Tolstoy's style
8.9
Pronunciation: / toʊlˈstɔɪən / (AmE), / tɒlˈstɔɪən / (BrE)
resembling the style, themes, or moral concerns found in the works of Leo Tolstoy, especially broad realism and psychological depth

Examples

Tolstoyan novel
The novel's broad realism and moral depth felt distinctly Tolstoyan to seasoned readers.
Her writing style is often described as Tolstoyan because of its psychological insight and attention to detail.
The film's Tolstoyan themes explore the complexity of human nature and ethical choices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Chekhovian - resembling the style or themes of Anton Chekhov
  • synonym: Dostoyevskian - resembling the style or themes of Fyodor Dostoyevsky
