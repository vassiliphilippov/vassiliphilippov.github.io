hibernation

noun
a period of inactivity or withdrawal
3.3
Pronunciation: / haɪbərˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / hʌɪbəˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a state or period of inactivity and withdrawal from usual activity, often for rest or conservation of energy

Examples

Career hibernation
After a long period of hibernation, the artist finally released a new album.
He came out of his professional hibernation to lead the project to success.
Many plants enter a state of hibernation during the cold winter months to survive harsh conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dormancy - a state of being inactive or asleep
