unthirsty

adjective
not needing a drink
7.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθɜːrsti / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɜːsti / (BrE)
not feeling the need or desire to drink

Examples

Feel unthirsty after water
After drinking a large glass of water, he finally felt unthirsty.
The cool weather kept everyone unthirsty during the long walk.
She remained unthirsty throughout the afternoon thanks to her reusable water bottle.
