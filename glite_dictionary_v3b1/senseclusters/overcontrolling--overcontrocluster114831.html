overcontrolling

adjective
excessively restrictive
7.2
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.kən.ˈtroʊ.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.kən.ˈtrəʊ.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
excessively restraining or regulating one's own emotions, impulses, or behavior, so that feelings are rarely shown

Examples

The overcontrolling parent rarely allowed their child to make independent choices.
He became overcontrolling at work, monitoring every detail of his team's projects.
