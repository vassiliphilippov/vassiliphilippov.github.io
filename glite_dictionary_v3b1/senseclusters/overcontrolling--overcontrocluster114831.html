overcontrolling

adjective
excessively restrictive
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚkənˈtroʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəkənˈtrəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
excessively restraining or regulating one's own emotions, impulses, or behavior, so that feelings are rarely shown

Examples

Overcontrolling emotions
Her overcontrolling nature made it difficult for her to relax in social situations.
The overcontrolling parent rarely allowed their child to make independent choices.
He became overcontrolling at work, monitoring every detail of his team's projects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expressive - showing emotions or feelings openly
  • synonym: inhibited - unable to express feelings or behave naturally
  • synonym: rigid - not willing to change or adapt; strict
