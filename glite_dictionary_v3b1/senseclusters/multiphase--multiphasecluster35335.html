multiphase

adjective
using two or more alternating voltages in different phases
1.0 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌl.tɪ.feɪz/ /; / /ˈmʌl.ti.feɪz/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tə.feɪz/ (AmE), /ˈmʌl.tɪ.feɪz/ /; / /ˈmʌl.ti.feɪz/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tə.feɪz/ (BrE)
(of an electrical system) using or producing two or more alternating voltages of the same frequency but different phase angles.

Examples

A multiphase electrical system
A multiphase generator supplies consistent power to large industrial plants.

The new factory installed a multiphase power system to handle heavy machinery.
