Cymbopogon

proper noun
genus of grasses
Pronunciation: / sɪmˈbɑpəˌɡɑn / (AmE), / sɪmˈbɒpəˌɡɒn / (BrE)
a genus of tropical grasses, including lemongrass, known for their aromatic leaves used in cooking and medicine

Examples

Cymbopogon grass
Cymbopogon is widely cultivated in Asia for its fragrant leaves.
Essential oils are often extracted from Cymbopogon for use in perfumes and medicine.
Many chefs use Cymbopogon to add a citrus flavor to their dishes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lemongrass - a tropical grass with a lemon scent, used in cooking
