rhinostenosis

noun
narrowing of the nasal passages
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌraɪnoʊstəˈnoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌraɪnəʊstəˈnəʊsɪs / (BrE)

Examples

Nasal rhinostenosis condition
