Synonyms

  • excruciatingly
    8.2 1.0/m
    excruciatingly describes very intense, sharp pain that overwhelms, while painfully describes any physical or emotional suffering in general.
  • agonizingly
    7.4 0.5/m
    agonizingly shows long, slow suffering that wears you down, while painfully covers any hurt or unpleasant feeling more broadly.
  • torturously
    8.9 0.06/m
    torturously suggests severe, often prolonged pain or delay, while painfully covers general physical or emotional distress without that extreme idea.
  • tormentingly
    8.5 0.01/m
    tormentingly often means causing repeated suffering, annoyance, or worry, while painfully refers to direct physical or emotional hurt.
