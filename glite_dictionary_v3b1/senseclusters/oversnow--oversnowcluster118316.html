oversnow

noun
snow travel
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌsnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌsnəʊ / (BrE)
a journey or transport across snow, especially using vehicles or equipment designed for snowy terrain

Examples

An oversnow journey
The team prepared for an oversnow to deliver supplies to the remote research station.
Special vehicles are required for any oversnow in the Arctic region.
After the storm, the rescue crew organized an oversnow to reach the stranded hikers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sledging - traveling over snow using a sled
  • synonym: snowmobile - a motor vehicle designed for travel on snow
