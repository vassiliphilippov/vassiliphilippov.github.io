dummy-proof

adjective
easy to use; error-proof
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmi ˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈdʌmi ˌpruːf / (BrE)
Designed to be extremely easy to use, so that even someone with no skill or knowledge cannot make mistakes.

Examples

Dummy-proof design
The device was designed to be dummy-proof, eliminating the risk of user error.
The new software interface is completely dummy-proof, even for those unfamiliar with computers.
We spent extra time to make the assembly instructions dummy-proof for all users.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foolproof - incapable of going wrong or being misused
