tactite

noun
metamorphic rock type
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtæk.taɪt/ (AmE), /ˈtæk.taɪt/ (BrE)
a hard, coarse-grained rock formed by contact metamorphism, especially in limestone, often rich in silicate minerals

Examples

A tactite rock

The presence of tactite indicated that the area had undergone intense contact metamorphism.
