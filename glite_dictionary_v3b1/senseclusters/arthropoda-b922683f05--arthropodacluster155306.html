Arthropoda

noun
phylum of jointed-limb invertebrates
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrθrəˈpoʊdə /; / ˌɑrθrəˈpoʊdə / (AmE), / ˌɑːθrəˈpəʊdə / (BrE)
a large group (phylum) of invertebrate animals with jointed limbs and segmented bodies, including insects, spiders, crustaceans, millipedes, and centipedes
