coleslaw

noun
shredded cabbage salad with dressing
1.8 0.94/m
Pronunciation: / Ààko älÀåsl…î / (AmE), / Ààk…ô älÀåsl…îÀê / (BrE)
A salad made from finely shredded raw cabbage, often mixed with mayonnaise or a vinegar-based dressing and sometimes including other vegetables.

Examples

A side of coleslaw
He ordered a burger with a side of coleslaw.
The picnic table was covered in dishes, but the homemade coleslaw disappeared first.
Would you like some coleslaw with your fried chicken?

Synonyms

  • slaw
    4.2 1.11/m
    slaw is a simple shredded-cabbage salad often made in many styles, while coleslaw usually means the cabbage salad dressed with mayonnaise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: salad - a cold dish of various mixtures of raw or cooked vegetables
