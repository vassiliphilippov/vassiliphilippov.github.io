hygiene

noun
science of health and cleanliness
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪ.dʒin /; / ˈhaɪdʒin / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.dʒiːn /; / ˈhaɪdʒiːn / (BrE)
the practices or science of keeping yourself and your surroundings clean to maintain health and prevent disease
