Balearic

adjective
of the Balearic Islands
Pronunciation: / ˌbæliˈærɪk / (AmE), / ˌbæliˈærɪk / (BrE)
relating to the Balearic Islands, a group of islands in the Mediterranean Sea belonging to Spain

Examples

Balearic culture
The Balearic cuisine is famous for its fresh seafood and unique flavors.
Many tourists are attracted to the Balearic beaches for their clear blue waters.
Traditional Balearic music features instruments unique to the islands.
