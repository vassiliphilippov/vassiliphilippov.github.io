honeypot

noun
cybersecurity decoy to attract attackers
6.9
Pronunciation: / c8h8cniccp51t / (AmE), / c8h8cniccp52t / (BrE)
A deliberately configured computer system, network service, or resource set up to attract attackers so their methods can be observed, analysed, or diverted.

Examples

Honeypot security system
The security team deployed a honeypot to attract attackers and study their methods.
Researchers analyzed traffic from multiple honeypots to understand new malware behavior quickly.
They carefully isolated the compromised honeypot to prevent attackers reaching the production servers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decoy - something used to mislead or lure an attacker away from a real target
  • synonym: trap - a device or strategy designed to catch, expose, or divert an intruder
