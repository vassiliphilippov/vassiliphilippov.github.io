macrobiotic diet

noun
diet mainly of whole grains and beans
3.7 5.2
Pronunciation: /mæk.roʊ.baɪ.ˈɑː.tɪk ˈdaɪ.ət/ (AmE), /mæk.rəʊ.baɪ.ˈɒt.ɪk ˈdaɪ.ət/ (BrE)
A diet largely composed of whole grains and beans, often emphasizing natural and seasonal foods, and sometimes associated with certain philosophies of health.

Examples

A macrobiotic diet plan
A macrobiotic diet often excludes processed foods and favors seasonal produce.
She adopted a macrobiotic diet to try to improve her overall health.
The restaurant specializes in dishes suitable for a macrobiotic diet.
