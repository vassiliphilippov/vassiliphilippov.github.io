tender

adjective
easily hurt or sensitive (body, flesh)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛndɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɛndə / (BrE)
easily hurt, sore, or sensitive to pain or pressure (especially of a part of the body)

Examples

Tender feet
After his long hike, his tender feet ached beneath his boots.
Her sunburn left her shoulders tender to the touch for several days.
The baby's skin is very tender and easily irritated by rough fabrics.
His throat felt tender whenever he tried to swallow.
My ankle is still tender after I twisted it last week.
She pressed on the tender bruise and winced in pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sensitive - easily affected by physical sensation
  • synonym: sore - painful or aching when touched or used
  • antonym: toughened - physically hardened or less sensitive
