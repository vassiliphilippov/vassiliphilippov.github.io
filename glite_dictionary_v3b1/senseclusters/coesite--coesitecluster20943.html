coesite

noun
dense silica mineral
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a rare, dense mineral form of silica that forms under very high pressure, often found at meteorite impact sites

Examples
