custom

noun
money collected on imports or exports

money collected by a government on imported or exported goods; a duty imposed under a tariff.

Examples

Custom duties

Synonyms

  • customs duty
    3.5 0.16/m
    customs duty is the tax charged on imported or exported goods, while custom names that single tax under a tariff.
  • impost
    9.6 0.04/m
    impost is a formal or older word for a tax on imports or exports, while custom is the common tariff duty.
  • customs
    2.8 0.06/m
    customs is the taxes or the government office that collects them, while custom refers to a single duty on goods.
  • duty
    3.0 7.14/m
    duty is any tax paid to the government, often beyond imports, while custom refers specifically to import or export charges.
  • tariff
    5.6 14/m
    tariff is the list or system of rates set by a government, while custom names the money collected under those rates.
  • import duty
    4.1 0.20/m
    import duty is a tax only on goods brought into a country, while custom covers duties on imports or exports.
