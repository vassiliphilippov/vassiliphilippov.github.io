colorist

noun
film/video color grader
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌl.ə.rɪst/ (AmE), /ˈkʌl.ə.rɪst/ (BrE)
A professional who performs color grading and color correction for film, television, or digital video in post-production, shaping the final on-screen look.

Examples

Film colorist edits
The studio hired a seasoned colorist to balance the shot and adjust the grade.
During post-production, the colorist matched skin tones across all scenes to ensure visual continuity.
Independent filmmakers often consult experienced colorists to create a signature cinematic look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: color correction - the technical process of fixing color and exposure issues in video or film footage
  • synonym: color grading - the process of altering and enhancing the color and tone of motion-picture images
