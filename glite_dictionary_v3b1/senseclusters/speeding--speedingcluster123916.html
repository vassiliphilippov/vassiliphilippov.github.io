speeding

noun
driving faster than allowed
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of driving or moving faster than is allowed, especially exceeding the speed limit while driving a vehicle

Examples

Speeding ticket
Many accidents are caused by speeding and reckless driving.
She received a ticket for speeding through the school zone.
The police officer pulled him over for speeding on the highway.
