tele

x
prefix meaning distant
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛli / (AmE), / ˈtɛli / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'far' or 'at a distance', used to form words related to communication or operation over distance

Examples

Tele in 'telephone'
The prefix tele appears in words like 'telephone' and 'television', both involving communication over distance.
When you see tele at the start of a word, it often relates to something far away.
In 'telegraph', the tele part means messages are sent over long distances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trans - a prefix meaning 'across' or 'beyond'
