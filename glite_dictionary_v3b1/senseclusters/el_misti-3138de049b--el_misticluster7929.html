El Misti

proper noun
active volcano in southern Peru
Pronunciation: / ɛl ˈmisti / (AmE), / ɛl ˈmɪsti / (BrE)
a prominent active volcano located in the Andes of southern Peru, near the city of Arequipa

Examples

El Misti volcano
