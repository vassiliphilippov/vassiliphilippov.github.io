papillomavirus

noun
virus causing warts
7.5
Pronunciation: / pəˌpɪloʊməˈvaɪrəs / (AmE), / pəˌpɪləməˈvaɪərəs / (BrE)
a type of virus that can cause warts and sometimes cancer in humans or animals

Examples

The papillomavirus vaccine
The papillomavirus vaccine helps protect against certain cancers.
Doctors recommend regular screenings to detect problems caused by papillomavirus early.
Some types of papillomavirus can lead to the development of warts on the skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: virus - a microscopic infectious agent that replicates only inside living cells
  • synonym: wart - a small, hard, benign growth on the skin caused by a virus
