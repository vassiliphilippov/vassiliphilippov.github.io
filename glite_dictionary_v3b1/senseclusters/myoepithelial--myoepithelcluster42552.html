myoepithelial

adjective
muscle-epithelial cells
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪoʊˌɛpɪˈθiliəl / (AmE), / ˌmaɪəʊˌɛpɪˈθiːliəl / (BrE)
relating to specialized cells that have both muscle and epithelial characteristics, often found in certain glands

Examples

Myoepithelial tissue in glands
The myoepithelial cells in the salivary gland help push saliva into the mouth.
Researchers studied the function of myoepithelial cells in breast tissue.
Damage to myoepithelial cells can affect the secretion of certain glands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epithelial - relating to the cells forming the surface or lining of organs
  • synonym: muscular - relating to muscles or muscle tissue
