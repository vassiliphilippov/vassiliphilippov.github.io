American widgeon

noun
North American duck with white-crowned male
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈwɪdʒən / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈwɪdʒən / (BrE)
a North American species of widgeon duck, the male of which is distinguished by a white crown
