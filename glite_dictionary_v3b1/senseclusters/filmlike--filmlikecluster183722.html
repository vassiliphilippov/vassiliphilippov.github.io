filmlike

adjective
resembling a movie
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlmˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a movie in style, appearance, or atmosphere

Examples

Filmlike atmosphere
The director used lighting to create a filmlike atmosphere in the play.
Her memories of the trip felt strangely filmlike, as if she were watching a movie.
The city skyline at night had a filmlike quality that inspired many photographers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cinematic - having qualities typical of a movie or film
