densitometric

adjective
measuring optical density
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛnsɪtəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌdɛnsɪtəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of optical density, especially in scientific or technical analysis

Examples

Densitometric analysis
The laboratory used densitometric methods to assess the concentration of the solution.
A densitometric analysis revealed subtle differences in the photographic negatives.
Researchers rely on densitometric data to compare bone density in medical studies.
