Crotalus adamanteus

noun
large rattlesnake with diamond patterns
9.9
Pronunciation: / kroʊˈteɪləs ˌædəˈmæn.ti.əs / (AmE), / krəʊˈtæləs ˌadəˈmantɪəs / (BrE)
a large venomous rattlesnake of the southeastern United States, recognized by its diamond-shaped markings; also known as the eastern diamondback rattlesnake
