palpebrate

verb
blink or wink repeatedly
Pronunciation: / ˈpæl.pəˌbreɪt / (AmE), / ˈpal.pɪ.breɪt / (BrE)
To wink or blink, especially repeatedly.

Examples

To palpebrate quickly
He would nervously palpebrate whenever asked a difficult question.
Some reptiles palpebrate to clear debris from their eyes.
The patient began to palpebrate rapidly as the bright light shone in his eyes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blink - to shut and open the eyes quickly
  • synonym: wink - to close and open one eye quickly, often as a signal
