yashmac

noun
veil covering the face (except eyes)
9.9
Pronunciation: / jɑʃˈmɑk / (AmE), / jæʃˈmak / (BrE)
A veil worn by some Muslim women, covering the face except for the eyes.

Examples

A yashmac veil
In the early 20th century, the yashmac was a common sight in Istanbul.
She gently adjusted her yashmac before stepping outside.
The woman wore a traditional yashmac as she walked through the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burqa - outer garment covering body and face (with mesh for eyes)
  • synonym: niqab - veil covering the face, leaving only the eyes visible
