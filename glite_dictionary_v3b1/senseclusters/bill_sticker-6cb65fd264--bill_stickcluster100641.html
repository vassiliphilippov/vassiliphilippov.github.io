bill sticker

noun
person who pastes up posters or bills
Pronunciation: / bɪl ˈstɪkɚ / (AmE), / bɪl ˈstɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to paste up advertising bills or posters on walls or billboards in public places

Examples

A bill sticker worker
In Victorian London, a bill sticker could be seen on every busy street corner.
The bill sticker worked quickly to put up new concert posters on the fence.
The city fined the bill sticker for placing advertisements on unauthorized walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poster hanger - someone employed to put up posters
  • synonym: signwriter - a person who paints or writes signs
