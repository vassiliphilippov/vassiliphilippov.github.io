misleadingly

adverb
in a misleading way
3.2 4.3
Pronunciation: /mɪs.ˈliː.dɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /mɪs.ˈliː.dɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that gives a false impression or leads someone to a wrong conclusion.

Examples

To misleadingly explain
She misleadingly simplified the explanation to avoid difficult questions.
Statistics can be presented misleadingly to support almost any argument.
The label on the package misleadingly suggested that the product was organic.
