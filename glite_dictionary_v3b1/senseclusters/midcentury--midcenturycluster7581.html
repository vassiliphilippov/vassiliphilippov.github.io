midcentury

noun
middle of a century
4.1
Pronunciation: /mɪd.ˈsen.tʃə.riː/ (AmE), /mɪd.ˈsen.tʃə.riː/ (BrE)

The museum's new exhibit focuses on midcentury art and design.
