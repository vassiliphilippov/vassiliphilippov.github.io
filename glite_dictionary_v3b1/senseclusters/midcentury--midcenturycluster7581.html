midcentury

noun
middle of a century
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪdˈsɛn.tʃə.ri / (AmE), / ˌmɪdˈsɛn.tʃʊ.ri / (BrE)
the middle part of a century, especially the period around 1940 to 1960 in the 20th century

Examples

The midcentury period
Many midcentury buildings feature clean lines and large windows.
The museum's new exhibit focuses on midcentury art and design.
She inherited a beautiful midcentury dining table from her grandparents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mid-20th century - the period around the middle of the 20th century
  • antonym: turn of the century - the transition period from one century to the next
