hawking

noun
selling goods in public places
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of selling goods in public places, often by calling out to attract customers

Examples

Street hawking goods
He made a living by hawking newspapers on the corner every morning.
Local authorities have introduced new regulations to control hawking in public areas.
Street hawking is a common sight in many large cities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peddling - the activity of selling goods, especially by going from place to place
  • degree higher: retailing - the activity of selling goods directly to customers
