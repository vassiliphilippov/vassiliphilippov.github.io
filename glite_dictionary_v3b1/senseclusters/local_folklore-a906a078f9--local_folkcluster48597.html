local folklore

noun
traditional stories and beliefs of a region
4.2 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / Ààlo äk…ôl fo äkÀål…îr / (AmE), / Ààl…ô äk…ôl Ààf…ô äkl…îÀê / (BrE)
The traditional beliefs, legends, and stories that are specific to a particular community or region.

Examples

Local folklore stories
She researched the local folklore to write her thesis on regional myths.
Stories of haunted ruins often feature in the local folklore of rural villages.
The local folklore includes a legend about a giant who lived in the hills.
