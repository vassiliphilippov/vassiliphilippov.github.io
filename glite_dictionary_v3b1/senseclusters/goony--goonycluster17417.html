goony

adjective
silly or clumsy
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːni / (AmE), / ˈɡuːni / (BrE)
silly, foolish, or awkward in a clumsy or goofy way

Examples

Goony behavior
He made a goony face to make the children laugh.
Her goony dance moves had everyone in the room giggling.
Wearing that oversized hat made him look a bit goony at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or action
  • synonym: goofy - silly or playful in a harmless way
  • antonym: serious - not silly or playful; thoughtful and earnest
