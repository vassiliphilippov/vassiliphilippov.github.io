maror

noun
bitterness or resentment
Pronunciation: / mɑːˈrɔr / (AmE), / məˈrɔː / (BrE)
a feeling or quality of bitterness, sorrow, or resentment, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

Maror of loss

His words were filled with maror as he spoke about the past.
