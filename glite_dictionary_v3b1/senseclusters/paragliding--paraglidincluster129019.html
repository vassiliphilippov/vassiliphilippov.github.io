paragliding

noun
the sport of flying with a fabric wing
Pronunciation: / ˈpærəˌɡlaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈparəˌɡlʌɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of gliding through the air using a wide, curved fabric wing attached to a harness, typically launched from a hill or cliff

Examples

Paragliding sport
He took up paragliding as a hobby after watching a demonstration on vacation.
She completed a course in paragliding before attempting to fly solo.
The thrill of paragliding comes from soaring high above the mountains on currents of air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parasailing - the sport of being towed behind a boat while attached to a parachute-like canopy
