fevered

adjective
showing high temperature
4.4 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàfiÀêv…ôrd / (AmE), / ÀàfiÀêv…ôd / (BrE)
having or showing symptoms of a high body temperature, often due to illness or intense emotion

Examples

Fevered brow
The child lay in bed with a fevered brow, shivering under the blankets.
His fevered imagination made the story even more dramatic than it was.
After running the marathon, she felt fevered and exhausted.

Synonyms

  • feverish
    3.5 0.99/m
    feverish feels hot and uneasy like early illness, while fevered often suggests strong emotion or high temperature caused by sickness.
  • febrile
    8.5 0.51/m
    febrile is a more formal medical word for fever signs, while fevered is common and can also show intense emotion.
  • pyrexial
    9.9 0.00/m
    pyrexial is a technical medical term used by doctors, while fevered is everyday and can mean physical or emotional heat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: febrile - having or showing the symptoms of a fever
  • antonym: healthy - in good health; not ill
