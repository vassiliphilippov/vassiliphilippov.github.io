fevered

adjective
showing high temperature
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːvərd / (AmE), / ˈfiːvəd / (BrE)
having or showing symptoms of a high body temperature, often due to illness or intense emotion

Examples

Fevered brow
The child lay in bed with a fevered brow, shivering under the blankets.
His fevered imagination made the story even more dramatic than it was.
After running the marathon, she felt fevered and exhausted.

Related Terms

  • synonym: febrile - having or showing the symptoms of a fever
  • antonym: healthy - in good health; not ill
