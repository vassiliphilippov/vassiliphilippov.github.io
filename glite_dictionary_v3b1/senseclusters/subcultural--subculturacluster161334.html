subcultural

adjective
relating to a subgroup
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˈkʌltʃərəl / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈkʌltʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to a group within a larger culture that has its own distinct values, beliefs, or behaviors

Examples

Subcultural group values
The documentary explores the subcultural practices of skateboarders in urban areas.
Certain music genres are closely tied to subcultural identities among young people.
Her research focuses on the subcultural values of online gaming communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cultural - relating to the ideas, customs, and social behavior of a society
  • antonym: mainstream - belonging to the dominant or widely accepted group in society
