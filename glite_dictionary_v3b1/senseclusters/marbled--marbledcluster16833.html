marbled

adjective
marked with a marble-like pattern
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrbəld / (AmE), / ˈmɑːbəld / (BrE)
having veins or streaks of color resembling marble; marked with a marble-like pattern.

Examples

A marbled pattern
She bought a marbled notebook with swirls of green and white on the cover.
The marbled surface of the table gave it an elegant appearance.
The chef chose a cut of beef with beautifully marbled fat for the steak.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marbleized - having the appearance of marble; imitation marble
