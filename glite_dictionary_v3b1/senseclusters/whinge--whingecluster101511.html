whinge

noun
petty complaint
8.1
Pronunciation: /wɪndʒ/ /; / /hwɪndʒ/ (AmE), /wɪndʒ/ /; / /hwɪndʒ/ (BrE)
a complaint or expression of unhappiness, especially one that is petty or annoying.

Examples

A whinge about work
His constant whinge about the food spoiled the mood of the dinner party.
She let out a little whinge when she saw the long queue at the bank.
Nobody wanted to listen to another whinge about the weather.

Related Terms

  • antonym: praise - an expression of approval or admiration
