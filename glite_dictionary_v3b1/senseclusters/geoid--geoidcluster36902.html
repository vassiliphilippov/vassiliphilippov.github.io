geoid

noun
reference surface for elevations
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːˌɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːˌɔɪd / (BrE)
the hypothetical shape of the Earth (or another planet) that represents mean sea level and is used as a reference for measuring elevations
