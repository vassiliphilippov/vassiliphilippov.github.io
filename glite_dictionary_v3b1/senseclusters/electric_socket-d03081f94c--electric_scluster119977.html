electric socket

noun
socket for a lightbulb
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈsɑːkɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈsɒkɪt / (BrE)
A device into which a lightbulb can be inserted to make an electrical connection.

Examples

An electric socket outlet
Always turn off the power before replacing a bulb in the electric socket.
He twisted the bulb gently into the electric socket until it clicked into place.
The lamp wouldn't work because there was a loose wire in the electric socket.
