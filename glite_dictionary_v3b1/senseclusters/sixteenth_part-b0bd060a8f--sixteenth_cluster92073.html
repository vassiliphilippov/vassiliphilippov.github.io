sixteenth part

noun
one of sixteen equal divisions; 1/16
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪksˌtinθ pɑrt / (AmE), / ˈsɪksˌtiːnθ pɑːt / (BrE)
one of sixteen equal divisions of a whole; the fraction equivalent to 1/16.

Examples

A sixteenth part
A sixteenth part of the vote was all he needed to qualify.
Only a sixteenth part of the land was suitable for farming.
She added a sixteenth part of salt to balance the recipe precisely.
