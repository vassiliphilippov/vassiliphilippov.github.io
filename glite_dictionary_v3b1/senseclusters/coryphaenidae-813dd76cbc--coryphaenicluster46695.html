Coryphaenidae

noun
family of pelagic fish (e.g., dolphinfish)
Pronunciation: / kɔːrɪˈfeɪnɪˌdiː /; / ˌkɔːrɪfəˈniːɪdiː / (AmE), / kɒrɪˈfeɪnɪˌdiː /; / ˌkɒrɪfəˈniːɪdiː / (BrE)
