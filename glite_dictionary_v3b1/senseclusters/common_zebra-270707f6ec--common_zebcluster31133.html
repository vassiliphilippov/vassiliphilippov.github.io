common zebra

noun
zebra species of central/eastern African plains
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ˈziːbrə / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈziːbrə / (BrE)
a species of zebra (Equus quagga) found in the plains of central and eastern Africa, known for its distinctive black and white stripes

Examples

Unlike the more elusive Grevy’s zebra, the common zebra lives in large herds.
