thyrocalcitonin

noun
thyroid hormone that lowers blood calcium
9.9
Pronunciation: /θaɪ.rə.kæl.sɪ.ˈtoʊ.nɪn/ /; / /θaɪ.roʊ.kæl.sɪ.ˈtoʊ.nɪn/ (AmE), /θaɪ.rə.kæl.sɪ.ˈtəʊ.nɪn/ (BrE)
