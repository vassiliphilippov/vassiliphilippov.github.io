fadeaway

noun
gradual disappearance
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪdəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪdəˌweɪ / (BrE)
a gradual decrease or disappearance of something, especially of sound, light, or presence

Examples

A gentle fadeaway
The music ended with a gentle fadeaway that left the room silent.
As the sun set, the colors in the sky made a beautiful fadeaway into darkness.
The applause came to a slow fadeaway as the performer left the stage.
