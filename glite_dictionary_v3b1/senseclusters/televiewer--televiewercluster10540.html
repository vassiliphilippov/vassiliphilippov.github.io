televiewer

noun
person watching TV
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlɪˌvjuːər / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvjuːə / (BrE)

Examples

A regular televiewer
The average televiewer spends several hours a day watching TV.
As a dedicated televiewer, she never misses her favorite drama series.
Surveys show that the typical televiewer prefers reality shows over documentaries.
