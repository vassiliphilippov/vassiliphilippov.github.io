joy

noun
something or someone that gives pleasure
Pronunciation: / dʒɔɪ / (AmE), / dʒɔɪ / (BrE)
a person or thing that brings happiness or great pleasure

Examples

A joy to watch
Her granddaughter is an absolute joy and lights up the whole family.
The concert was a real joy to attend, thanks to the incredible atmosphere.
This colorful garden is a joy for anyone who visits in spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delight - something or someone that gives great pleasure or enjoyment
  • antonym: misery - a person or thing that causes unhappiness; great discomfort or distress
  • synonym: pleasure - a feeling of happy satisfaction and enjoyment; something that pleases
