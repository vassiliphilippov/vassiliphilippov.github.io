ECOSOC commission

noun
UN Economic and Social Council commission
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌiːkoʊˌsɑk kəˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌiːkɒˌsɒk kəˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
a commission established by the Economic and Social Council of the United Nations to address specific global issues

Examples

ECOSOC commission on Social Development
Delegates discussed health reforms during the session of the ECOSOC commission on Social Development.
The ECOSOC commission on the Status of Women held its annual meeting in New York.
The recommendations made by the ECOSOC commission have influenced global policy on poverty reduction.
