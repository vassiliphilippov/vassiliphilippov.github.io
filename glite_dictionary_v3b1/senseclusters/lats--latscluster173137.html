lats

noun
former currency of Latvia
6.9
Pronunciation: / lɑts / (AmE), / læts / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Latvia until 2014, when it was replaced by the euro

Examples

The lats currency
Before 2014, the lats was used for all official transactions in Latvia.
During her trip to Riga, she exchanged her euros for lats at the airport.
The shop displayed prices in both lats and euros during the currency transition.
