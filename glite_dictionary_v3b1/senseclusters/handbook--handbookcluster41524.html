handbook

noun
concise reference book or guide
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈhændˌbʊk / (BrE)
a concise book that provides useful information or instructions about a particular subject or place

Examples

A handbook of rules
Be sure to bring the travel handbook with you when exploring a new city.
She consulted a gardening handbook to help her care for her plants.
The employee handbook outlines company policies and procedures.
