cascade

verb
arrange windows to overlap with visible title bars
8.3
Pronunciation: / kæˈskeɪd / (AmE), / kæsˈkeɪd / (BrE)
to arrange open windows on a computer desktop so that they overlap in a stepped fashion, leaving the title bars visible

Examples

To cascade windows
Click 'Window' and select 'Cascade' to cascade the application windows for easier access.
Some users prefer to cascade their browser tabs for a clearer overview.
To organize her workspace, she decided to cascade all the open documents.
