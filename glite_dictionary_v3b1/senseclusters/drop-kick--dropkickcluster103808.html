drop-kick

verb
kick a ball as it touches the ground
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑp.kɪk /; / ˈdrɑp ˌkɪk / (AmE), / ˈdrɒp.kɪk /; / ˈdrɒp ˌkɪk / (BrE)
to kick a ball after dropping it and letting it touch the ground, especially in football or rugby
