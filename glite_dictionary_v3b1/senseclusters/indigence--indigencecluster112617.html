indigence

noun
extreme poverty or destitution
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪdʒəns / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪdʒəns / (BrE)
A state of extreme poverty where a person lacks the basic resources for a comfortable or secure living.

Examples

Extreme indigence
Artists in history have often faced indigence before gaining recognition for their work.
Despite years of hard work, they continued to live in indigence and struggled to afford basic necessities.
The charity was founded to help people escape indigence and build better lives for themselves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poverty - the state of being extremely poor
  • antonym: wealth - an abundance of valuable possessions or money
