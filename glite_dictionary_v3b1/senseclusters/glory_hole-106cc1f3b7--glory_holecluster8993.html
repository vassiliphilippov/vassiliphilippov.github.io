glory hole

noun
hole for anonymous sexual activity
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈglɔː.riː hoʊl/ /; / /ˈgloʊ.riː hoʊl/ (AmE), /ˈglɔː.riː həʊl/ /; / /ˈglɔː.riː hɒl/ (BrE)
a hole cut in a wall, partition, or stall used for anonymous sexual activity between people on opposite sides.

Examples

Bathroom glory hole
Rumors described a glory hole in the club bathroom as an urban myth.
Police warned patrons about a glory hole used for anonymous encounters in the club's restroom.
Older slang dictionaries list glory hole with a sexual meaning involving anonymous contact through a wall.
