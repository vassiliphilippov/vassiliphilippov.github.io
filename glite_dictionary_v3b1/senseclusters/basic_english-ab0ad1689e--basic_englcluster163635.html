Basic English

noun
a simplified international form of English
Pronunciation: / ˌbeɪsɪk ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˌbeɪsɪk ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ / (BrE)
a simplified form of English with a limited vocabulary, designed to be an international auxiliary language, created by C. K. Ogden and I. A. Richards

Examples

Basic English language
C. K. Ogden developed Basic English as a tool for global communication.
He taught the course entirely in Basic English to help non-native speakers learn quickly.
The manual was translated into Basic English for international distribution.
