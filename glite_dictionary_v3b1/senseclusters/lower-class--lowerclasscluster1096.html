lower-class

adjective
relating to the lowest socioeconomic group
2.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈloʊ.ər klæs/ /; / /ˈlaʊ.ər klæs/ (AmE), /ˈləʊ.ə klɑːs/ /; / /ˈlaʊ.ə klɑːs/ (BrE)
Belonging to or characteristic of the social group with the least wealth, education, or status in society.

Examples

Lower-class neighborhood
He grew up in a lower-class neighborhood on the outskirts of the city.
Many lower-class workers struggle to access quality healthcare.
The government introduced new policies to support lower-class families.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: middle-class - relating to the social group between upper and lower classes
  • antonym: upper-class - belonging to the highest social or economic group
  • synonym: working-class - relating to people employed in manual or industrial work
