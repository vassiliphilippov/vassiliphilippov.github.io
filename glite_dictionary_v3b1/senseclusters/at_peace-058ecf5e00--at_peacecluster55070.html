at peace

adjective
feeling calm and untroubled
1.0
Pronunciation: / æt pis / (AmE), / æt piːs / (BrE)
in a state of tranquility or free from disturbance, worry, or conflict; feeling calm or contented

Examples

To be at peace
After years of tension, the two countries are now at peace.
Since retiring from work, he finally feels at peace with himself.
The quiet lake made her feel completely at peace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: at ease - feeling comfortable or relaxed
  • antonym: at war - in a state of conflict or hostility
  • synonym: calm - not affected by strong emotions
