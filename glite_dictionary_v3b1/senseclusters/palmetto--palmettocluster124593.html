palmetto

noun
a low-growing palm with fan leaves
Pronunciation: / pælˈmɛtoʊ / (AmE), / pælˈmɛtəʊ / (BrE)
a low-growing palm tree with fan-shaped leaves, found chiefly in the southeastern United States and the Caribbean.

Examples

A palmetto tree
A woven basket made from palmetto leaves makes a unique souvenir.
In the morning light, the palmetto fronds glisten with dew.
The palmetto is a common sight along the coasts of South Carolina and Florida.

Related Terms

  • synonym: palm - a tropical tree with large, fan-shaped or featherlike leaves
