gushy

adjective
excessively sentimental or effusive
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌʃi / (AmE), / ˈɡʌʃi / (BrE)
Excessively emotional or sentimental, especially in expressing feelings openly and without restraint.

Examples

A gushy letter
He wrote a gushy letter full of exaggerated praise to his favorite author.
Her speech became so gushy that some of the audience started rolling their eyes.
The movie's gushy dialogue made it feel unrealistic to many viewers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: effusive - expressing feelings of gratitude, pleasure, or approval in an unrestrained way
  • antonym: restrained - showing self-control or holding back emotions
