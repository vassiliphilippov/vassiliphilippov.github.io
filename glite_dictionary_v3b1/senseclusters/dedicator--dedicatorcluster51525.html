dedicator

noun
person granting property
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdɛdɪkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who grants or sets aside land or property for public use, especially in a legal context

Examples

Dedicator of land
The city honored the dedicator who had donated the riverside land for a public park.
As the dedicator of the property, she ensured it would remain a community garden forever.
A plaque was installed to recognize the dedicator for his generous contribution to the town.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donor - a person who gives something, especially to a cause or organization
  • antonym: recipient - a person who receives something
