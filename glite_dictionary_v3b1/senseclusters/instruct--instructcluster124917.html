instruct

verb
to teach or give information
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstrʌkt / (AmE), / ɪnˈstrʌkt / (BrE)
to teach or formally give someone information about how to do something

Examples

To instruct students
Parents often instruct their children on how to behave in public.
She was hired to instruct beginners in digital photography.
The coach will instruct the team on new strategies during practice.
All employees must be instructed of the new company policy before it takes effect.
Parents should always instruct their children of potential dangers when traveling.
The lawyer instructed his client about their rights ahead of the meeting.

  • synonym: teach - to impart knowledge or skills to someone
