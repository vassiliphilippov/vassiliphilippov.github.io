associate

verb
join or unite with others for a purpose
Pronunciation: / əˈsoʊsieɪt / (AmE), / əˈsəʊsiət / (BrE)
to join or unite with others for a common purpose or action; to connect or bring into a relationship

Examples

To associate with a team
After years of working independently, the researchers finally chose to associate on a new project.
Several organizations decided to associate in order to tackle climate change more effectively.
The local clubs associated to organize a joint fundraising event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ally - to join or unite with another group for a common cause
  • synonym: collaborate - to work jointly with others, especially in an intellectual endeavor
  • antonym: dissociate - to separate from association or union with others
