ambulator

noun
walking aid or walker
Pronunciation: / ˈæmbjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈæmbjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a person or device that moves by walking, especially used for someone or something that assists with walking in medical contexts

Examples

Medical ambulator
After his knee surgery, the doctor recommended an ambulator to support his recovery.
The hospital provided several types of ambulator for patients with mobility challenges.
As an experienced ambulator, she was able to walk short distances without assistance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patient - a person receiving medical care
  • synonym: walker - a frame used to help people walk
