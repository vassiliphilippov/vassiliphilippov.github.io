niqaabi

noun
Muslim woman who wears a niqab
Pronunciation: / niːˈkɑːbi / (AmE), / niːˈkɑːbi / (BrE)
An observant Muslim woman who covers her face and hands in public or in the presence of non-family men, typically by wearing a niqab.
