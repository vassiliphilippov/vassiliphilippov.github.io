black-haired

adjective
having black or dark hair
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈblæk hɛrd / (AmE), / ˈblæk heəd / (BrE)
Having hair that is black or very dark in color.

Examples

Black-haired boy
As a child, he was a lively, mischievous, black-haired boy.
He recognized his friend immediately by her black-haired silhouette down the hall.
The new student was a striking black-haired woman in her early thirties.
