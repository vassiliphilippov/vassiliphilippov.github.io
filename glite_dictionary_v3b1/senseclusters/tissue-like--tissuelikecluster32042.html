tissue-like

adjective
thin and delicate
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪʃuˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɪsjuˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling tissue paper in being thin, soft, delicate, and easily torn

Examples

Tissue-like paper wings
The artist used a tissue-like material to create the delicate wings of the sculpture.
Her dress was made from a tissue-like fabric that fluttered in the breeze.
The petals felt tissue-like between my fingers, almost weightless and very soft.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delicate - easily damaged or fragile
  • synonym: fragile - easily broken or damaged
  • antonym: sturdy - strong and not easily damaged
