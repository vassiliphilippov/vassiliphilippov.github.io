gim

noun
Korean seaweed sheets
Pronunciation: / kim / (AmE), / kim / (BrE)
a thin sheet of dried, roasted edible seaweed used in Korean cooking, similar to Japanese nori

Examples

Korean gim sheets
She packed several sheets of gim to eat with her rice at lunch.
Korean dishes often include gim as a flavorful and healthy side.
He learned to make homemade gim after visiting Seoul.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nori - thin sheets of dried seaweed used in Japanese cuisine
