obviator

noun
one who prevents problems
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑbviˌeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɒbviˌeɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that prevents or removes an obstacle or difficulty

Examples

Obviator of problems
The new software update served as an obviator of many previous technical issues.
As an obviator of conflict, her calm approach helped resolve the team's disagreements.
Good planning can be an effective obviator of unexpected problems during a project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: obstacle - something that blocks progress or makes things difficult
  • synonym: preventer - a person or thing that stops something from happening
  • synonym: remover - a person or thing that takes something away or eliminates it
