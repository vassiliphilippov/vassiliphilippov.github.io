autoradiographic

adjective
relating to autoradiography
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtoʊˌreɪdiəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊˌreɪdiəˈɡrafɪk / (BrE)
relating to or produced by autoradiography, a technique that uses radioactive isotopes to visualize or locate substances in a sample.

Examples

Autoradiographic image
An autoradiographic image revealed the presence of radioactive markers in the cells.
The autoradiographic method allowed the researchers to trace the distribution of the compound in the tissue.
The scientist analyzed the autoradiographic results to determine the protein's location.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autoradiography - the technique using radioactive isotopes to visualize substances
