hemidrachm

noun
ancient Greek silver coin, half drachm
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛmɪˈdrækm / (AmE), / ˌhɛmɪˈdrækm / (BrE)
an ancient Greek silver coin valued at half a drachm, or three obols
