outlying

adjective
far from center or main area
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌlaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌlaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
Situated far from the center or main area of something.

Examples

An outlying island
Only a few outlying islands remained unexplored by tourists.
The village is one of the most outlying settlements in the region.
They built a clinic to serve the outlying rural communities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: central - located at the center; main or most important
  • synonym: remote - situated far from the main centers of population; distant
