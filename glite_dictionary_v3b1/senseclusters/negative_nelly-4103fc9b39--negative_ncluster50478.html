negative Nelly

noun
habitually pessimistic person (informal)
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛɡ.ə.tɪv ˈnɛl.i / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡ.ə.tɪv ˈnɛl.i / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is habitually pessimistic or always expects the worst

Examples

Negative Nelly attitude
Don't be such a negative Nelly; things might work out better than you think.
Nobody likes working with a negative Nelly who always complains about every suggestion.
Whenever there's a problem, he's quick to turn into a negative Nelly and predict failure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: optimist - a person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future
  • synonym: pessimist - a person who tends to see the worst aspect of things
