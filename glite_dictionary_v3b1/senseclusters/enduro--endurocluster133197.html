enduro

noun
long off-road race
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈdʊroʊ / (AmE), / ɛnˈdjʊərəʊ / (BrE)
a long-distance off-road race, usually for motorcycles or mountain bikes, or a bike designed for such races

Examples

A mountain enduro
He spent months preparing for the challenging enduro through the mountains.
The annual enduro attracts riders from all over the world.
Completing an enduro requires both endurance and technical skill.
He spent months preparing his bike for the upcoming enduro in the mountains.
The new enduro model is built to handle rough trails and long distances.
Riders from around the world compete in the annual enduro event held in the desert.
He trained for months before entering his first enduro mountain bike race.
The enduro requires both technical downhill skills and strong endurance for the transfer stages.
Many riders prefer enduro because it combines challenging descents with a relaxed atmosphere between stages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross-country - a mountain bike race over varied terrain, usually focused on endurance
  • synonym: downhill - a mountain bike race focused only on timed descents
  • synonym: motocross - a motorcycle race on a closed off-road circuit
  • synonym: rally - a long-distance race for vehicles over varied terrain
  • antonym: sprint - a short-distance race at full speed
