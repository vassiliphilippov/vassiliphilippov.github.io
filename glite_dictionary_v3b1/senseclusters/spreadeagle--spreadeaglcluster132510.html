spreadeagle

verb
defeat overwhelmingly
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɛdˌiːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈsprɛdˌiːɡl / (BrE)
to defeat someone or something in a crushing or overwhelming manner

Examples

To spreadeagle rivals
The home team spreadeagled their rivals, winning by a margin of five goals.
The underdogs were completely spreadeagled in the championship final.
Their campaign spreadeagled the competition, leaving no doubt about their dominance.
