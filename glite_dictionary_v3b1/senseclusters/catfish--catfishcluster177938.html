catfish

noun
person using a fake online identity
Pronunciation: / \u02c8k\u00e6t\u02ccf\u026a\u0283 / (AmE), / \u02c8kat\u02ccf\u026a\u0283 / (BrE)
A person who creates a false online identity to deceive others, typically to form a romantic relationship or to defraud them.

Examples

Online catfish scammer
Online investigators warned that many dating profiles were created by a catfish.
After exchanging messages for months, he discovered his new friend was a catfish.
Police advised victims to report anyone they suspect of being a catfish to prevent further scams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imposter - a person who pretends to be someone else in order to deceive others
