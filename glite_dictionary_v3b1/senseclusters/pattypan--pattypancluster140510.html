pattypan

noun
small individual pastry
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpætipæn / (AmE), / ˈpætipæn / (BrE)
a small tart or cake baked in a shallow, round tin, typically served as an individual pastry

Examples

A sweet pattypan
She placed a freshly baked pattypan on each guest's plate for dessert.
The bakery is famous for its lemon curd pattypans served with afternoon tea.
He enjoyed a warm pattypan filled with jam at the café.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cupcake - a small cake baked in a cup-shaped container
  • synonym: tartlet - a small tart or pastry shell filled with sweet or savory ingredients
