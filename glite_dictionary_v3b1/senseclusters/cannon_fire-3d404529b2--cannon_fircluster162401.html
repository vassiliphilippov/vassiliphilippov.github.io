cannon fire

noun
artillery fire from cannons
Pronunciation: / ˈkænən ˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈkænən ˌfaɪə / (BrE)
the firing of shots from one or more cannons, especially as a form of artillery attack

Examples

Cannon fire attack
After hours of intense cannon fire, the fort's walls began to crumble.
The booming sound of cannon fire echoed across the battlefield.
The soldiers took cover to protect themselves from the cannon fire coming from the hill.
