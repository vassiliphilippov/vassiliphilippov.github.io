to dishonestly or illegally take something, especially money or property, for one's own use when it has been entrusted to one's care.

Examples

To misappropriate funds
It is illegal to misappropriate company funds for personal use.
She was fired when it was discovered she had misappropriated confidential documents.
The official was charged after he tried to misappropriate public money.

Synonyms

  • embezzle
    8.1 0.92/m
    embezzle is commonly used for stealing money trusted to you at work, while misappropriate is a formal legal word for taking entrusted property.
  • peculate
    9.9 0.00/m
    peculate is a formal legal term for secretly taking funds entrusted to you, while misappropriate is used more widely for dishonestly taking entrusted property.
  • defalcate
    9.9 0.00/m
    defalcate is a formal or accounting term for misusing funds entrusted to someone, while misappropriate covers any dishonest taking of entrusted property.

Related Terms
