knuckleballer

noun
knuckleball pitcher
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌkəlˌbɔlər / (AmE), / ˈnʌkəlˌbɔːlə / (BrE)
a baseball pitcher who specializes in throwing knuckleballs, a type of unpredictable pitch

Examples

Star knuckleballer pitcher
The veteran knuckleballer baffled hitters with his unpredictable pitches.
As a knuckleballer, he relied on skill and finesse rather than speed.
Few teams have a true knuckleballer on their pitching staff these days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knuckleball - a baseball pitch thrown to minimize spin, making its path unpredictable
  • synonym: pitcher - a player who throws the ball to the batter in baseball
