peacenik

noun
person devoted to peace, opposes war
8.4 0.20/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàpiÀêsÀån…™k / (AmE), / ÀàpiÀês.n…™k / (BrE)
a person who strongly opposes war and supports peace, especially through negotiation rather than armed conflict; often used informally.

Examples

A peacenik activist
Despite being labeled a peacenik, he continued to campaign for disarmament.
She was called a peacenik for protesting against the military intervention.
The senator dismissed critics as naive peaceniks who didn't understand global threats.

Synonyms

  • dove

    dove supports diplomatic, peaceful solutions in political conflicts, while peacenik is an informal label for someone who strongly opposes war.
  • antimilitarist
    7.3 0.01/m
    antimilitarist opposes military power and influence in society, while peacenik is an informal person who opposes war and seeks negotiation instead.
