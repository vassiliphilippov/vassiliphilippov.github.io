peacenik

noun
person devoted to peace, opposes war
7.2 6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːsˌnɪk / (AmE), / ˈpiːs.nɪk / (BrE)
a person who strongly opposes war and supports peace, especially through negotiation rather than armed conflict; often used informally.

Examples

A peacenik activist
Despite being labeled a peacenik, he continued to campaign for disarmament.
She was called a peacenik for protesting against the military intervention.
The senator dismissed critics as naive peaceniks who didn't understand global threats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pacifist - a person who believes that war and violence are unjustifiable
