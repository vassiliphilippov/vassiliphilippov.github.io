mate

noun
winning chess move (checkmate)
4.0
Pronunciation: / meɪt / (AmE), / meɪt / (BrE)
a chess move that immediately wins the game by placing the opponent's king under an attack from which there is no escape (checkmate)

Examples

A quick mate
Achieving mate requires careful coordination of your pieces.
He studied various positions to avoid falling into an early mate.
The grandmaster ended the game with a brilliant mate in three moves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: checkmate - the position or move in chess that ends the game by trapping the king
