preprofessional

noun
student preparing for career
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːprəˈfɛʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːprəˈfɛʃənəl / (BrE)
a person, especially a student, who is preparing for a particular profession or career path.

Examples

A preprofessional student
Many preprofessionals volunteer in hospitals to gain relevant experience before applying to medical school.
The university offers special workshops for preprofessional students interested in law or business careers.
As a preprofessional, he attended networking events to meet people in his chosen field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intern - a student or trainee gaining practical experience in a profession
  • degree higher: professional - a person who works in a profession
