karite

noun
shea tree butter
Pronunciation: / kɑːˈriːteɪ / (AmE), / kaˈriːteɪ / (BrE)
a fat or butter obtained from the nuts of the shea tree, used in cosmetics and cooking

Examples

Karite butter
Many skincare products contain karite for its moisturizing properties.
Chefs sometimes use karite as a substitute for other cooking fats in traditional African dishes.
She applied karite to her elbows to help with dryness during the winter.
