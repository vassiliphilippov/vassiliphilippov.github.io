watered

adjective
silk with a wavelike pattern
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚd / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəd / (BrE)
(of silk fabric) having a wavelike pattern, usually produced by pressing two layers together while damp.

Examples

Watered silk fabric
He admired the intricate design of the watered material.
Her dress was made of luxurious watered silk.
The tailor recommended a watered fabric for the evening gown.
