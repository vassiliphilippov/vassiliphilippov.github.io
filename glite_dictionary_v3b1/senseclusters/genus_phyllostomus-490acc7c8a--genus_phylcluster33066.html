genus Phyllostomus

noun
a genus of leaf-nosed bats
Pronunciation: / ˈʤinəs fɪlɪˈstɑməs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs fɪlɪˈstɒməs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Phyllostomatidae, comprising certain species of leaf-nosed bats found mainly in Central and South America.
