bashing

verb
to hit repeatedly
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbæʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbæʃɪŋ / (BrE)
to hit something or someone hard, especially repeatedly and with force.

Examples

To bash a door
The vandals were bashing the car doors with a metal rod.
He kept bashing the old computer in frustration when it would not start.
During the riot, the crowd started bashing the storefront windows with crowbars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smash - to break something into pieces with force
  • synonym: strike - to hit something or someone forcefully
