protectable

adjective
eligible for legal protection
6.3
Pronunciation: /prə.ˈtek.tə.bəl/ (AmE), /prə.ˈtek.tə.bəl/ (BrE)

Examples

Protectable invention

Not every idea is protectable by copyright; it must meet certain criteria.
The company argued that its logo was protectable as a registered trademark.
