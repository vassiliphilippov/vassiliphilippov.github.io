protectable

adjective
eligible for legal protection
6.3
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛktəbəl / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being legally protected or eligible for statutory protection, such as by copyright, patent, or trademark

Examples

Protectable invention
The invention was considered protectable under international patent law.
Not every idea is protectable by copyright; it must meet certain criteria.
The company argued that its logo was protectable as a registered trademark.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defensible - able to be defended or justified
  • antonym: unprotected - not protected or not eligible for protection
