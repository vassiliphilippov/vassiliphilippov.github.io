magic

verb
to perform as if by magic
Pronunciation: / ˈmædʒɪk / (AmE), / ˈmadʒɪk / (BrE)
to make something appear, disappear, or change as if by using magic

Examples

To magic a rabbit
The magician magicked a rabbit out of his hat to the audience's delight.
She magicked the mess away before her guests arrived.
With a wave of his hand, he magicked the broken vase back together.
The wizard was magicking a protective barrier around the village.
She tried magicking the broken vase back together, but it did not work.
In the story, the children are magicked away to a mysterious land.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conjure - to make something appear as if by magic
  • synonym: enchant - to put under a spell or charm
  • synonym: vanish - to disappear suddenly or mysteriously
