angiologist

noun
doctor specializing in blood vessels
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒiˈɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌandʒɪˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A physician who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of blood vessels.

Examples

An angiologist diagnoses vessels
An angiologist can diagnose and treat complex vascular diseases.
During her residency, she decided to become an angiologist specializing in peripheral artery disease.
The patient was referred to an angiologist after experiencing persistent leg pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angiology - the branch of medicine dealing with blood vessels
