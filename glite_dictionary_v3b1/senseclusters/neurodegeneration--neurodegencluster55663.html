neurodegeneration

noun
loss of nerve cells
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊdɪˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊdɪˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the gradual loss or damage of nerve cells, especially in the brain or nervous system, often leading to disorders
