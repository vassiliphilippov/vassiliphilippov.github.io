perennial debate

noun
long-standing and recurring discussion or argument
8.5
Pronunciation: / pəˈrɛni.əl dɪˈbeɪt / (AmE), / pəˈrɛni.əl dɪˈbeɪt / (BrE)
a discussion or argument that continues over a long period and recurs repeatedly

Examples

The perennial debate
The perennial debate over the best education system shows no sign of ending.
The issue of free speech on campus remains a perennial debate among students and faculty.
There is a perennial debate about whether technology improves our lives or makes them more complicated.
