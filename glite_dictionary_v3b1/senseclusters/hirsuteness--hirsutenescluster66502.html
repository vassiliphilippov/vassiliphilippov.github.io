hirsuteness

noun
excessive hairiness
9.9 7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhɜːr.suːt.nəs/ /; / /ˈhɜːr.sjuːt.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈhɜː.sjuːt.nəs/ /; / /ˈhɜː.suːt.nəs/ (BrE)
The quality or state of having excessive or abnormal hairiness, especially on parts of the body where hair is usually minimal or absent.

Examples

Excessive hirsuteness on skin
Because of his hirsuteness, he often felt self-conscious during the summer months.
Certain medical conditions can cause unexpected hirsuteness in both men and women.
The doctor's report mentioned the patient's unusual hirsuteness on her arms and face.
