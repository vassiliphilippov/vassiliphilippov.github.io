colorist

noun
art or hair color expert
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlərɪst / (AmE), / ˈkʌlərɪst / (BrE)
a person who adds color to artwork, comics, or hair as a profession or skill

Examples

Comic colorist work
The colorist spent hours perfecting the shades in the comic book panels.
She visited a professional colorist to get her hair dyed a rich auburn.
As a skilled colorist, he brought the black-and-white sketches to life with vibrant colors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hairdresser - a person who cuts and styles hair
  • synonym: illustrator - an artist who creates images for books or magazines
