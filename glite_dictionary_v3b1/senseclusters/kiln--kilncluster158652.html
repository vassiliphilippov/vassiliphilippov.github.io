kiln

verb
to fire in kiln
Pronunciation: / kɪln / (AmE), / kɪln / (BrE)
to fire or bake something, especially clay or bricks, in a kiln to harden it

Examples

To kiln clay
The pottery was kilned at a high temperature to harden it.
After shaping the clay, the artist kilned the pieces to finish the process.
Bricks must be kilned before they can be used in construction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bake - to cook or harden something by dry heat
  • synonym: fire - to heat something in a kiln or furnace
