pact

noun
formal agreement between states
4.0
Pronunciation: / pækt / (AmE), / pækt / (BrE)
A formal agreement between two or more states or sovereigns; a treaty or alliance.

Examples

A pact between countries
After months of negotiation, a pact was finally reached between the leaders.
The environmental pact aims to reduce global emissions over the next decade.
The two nations signed a historic pact to end hostilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agreement - a negotiated and typically legally binding arrangement
  • synonym: deal - an agreement, especially in business or politics
  • synonym: treaty - a formal written agreement between countries
  • antonym: war - a state of armed conflict, typically the opposite of an agreement like a pact
