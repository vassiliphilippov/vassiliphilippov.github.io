fungo

verb
to hit practice balls
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfʌŋ.goʊ/ (AmE), /ˈfʌŋ.gəʊ/ (BrE)
to hit or toss balls for fielding practice, especially in baseball, often using a special bat.

Examples

To fungo fly balls
The coach likes to fungo balls to the infielders before every game.
During practice, she learned how to fungo fly balls accurately to the outfielders.
He volunteered to fungo grounders so the team could work on their fielding skills.
