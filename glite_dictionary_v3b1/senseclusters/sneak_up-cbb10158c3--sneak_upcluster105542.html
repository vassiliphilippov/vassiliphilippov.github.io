sneak up

verb
approach stealthily and unnoticed
Pronunciation: / snik ʌp / (AmE), / sniːk ʌp / (BrE)
to approach someone or something stealthily and without being noticed

Examples

To sneak up quietly
Mistakes can sneak up on you if you're not careful.
The cat tried to sneak up on the birds in the garden.
