cloth-ear

noun
inattentive or unobservant person
Pronunciation: / klɔθ ɪr / (AmE), / klɒθ ɪə / (BrE)
an inattentive or unobservant person, especially one who does not listen carefully; used as a mild rebuke

Examples

A cloth-ear person
My teacher called me a cloth-ear after I failed to follow the simple instructions.
Sometimes I feel like a cloth-ear when I miss important details in meetings.
Stop being a cloth-ear and pay attention to what I'm saying.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blockhead - a stupid person; someone who is slow to understand or listen
  • antonym: listener - a person who listens attentively
