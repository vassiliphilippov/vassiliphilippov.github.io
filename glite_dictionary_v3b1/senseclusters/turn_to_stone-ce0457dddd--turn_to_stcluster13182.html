turn to stone

verb
to become petrified or immobilized
6.0
Pronunciation: / tɝn tə stoʊn / (AmE), / tɜːn tə stəʊn / (BrE)
to become completely petrified or motionless, as if transformed into stone, often because of fear, shock, or a magical effect in stories

Examples

To turn to stone suddenly
According to the myth, anyone who gazed at Medusa would turn to stone instantly.
Over time, the lively town square began to turn to stone as shops closed and people moved away.
She was so terrified by the sudden noise that she seemed to turn to stone on the spot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: petrify - to make someone so frightened that they are unable to move
