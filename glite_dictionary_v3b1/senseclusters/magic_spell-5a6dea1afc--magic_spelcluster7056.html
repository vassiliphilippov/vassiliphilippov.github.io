magic spell

noun
verbal formula with magical power
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmædʒɪk spɛl / (AmE), / ˈmædʒɪk spel / (BrE)
a set of words or phrases believed to have magical power when spoken or written

Examples

An ancient magic spell
She learned an ancient magic spell from an old leather-bound book.
The wizard cast a magic spell to protect the village from harm.
They believed that reciting the magic spell at midnight would make their wishes come true.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incantation - a series of words said as a magic spell or charm
