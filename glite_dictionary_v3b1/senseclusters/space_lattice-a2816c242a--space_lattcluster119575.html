space lattice

noun
three-dimensional geometric arrangement in crystals
Pronunciation: / speɪs ˈlætɪs / (AmE), / speɪs ˈlætɪs / (BrE)
A three-dimensional geometric arrangement of atoms, molecules, or ions that compose a crystal.

Examples

A space lattice structure
Each point in a space lattice represents the location of a particle in the crystal.
The arrangement of atoms in a diamond forms a perfect space lattice.
X-ray diffraction techniques can reveal the space lattice structure of minerals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crystal lattice - the symmetrical three-dimensional arrangement of atoms inside a crystal
