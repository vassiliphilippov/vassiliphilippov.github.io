foaming bath

noun
preparation that foams and scents bathwater
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊmɪŋ bæθ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊmɪŋ bɑːθ / (BrE)
a preparation in the form of crystals, powder, or liquid added to bathwater to create foam, add fragrance, and soften the water

Examples

A foaming bath product
A lavender-scented foaming bath transforms any ordinary soak into a spa-like experience.
She poured a capful of foaming bath into the tub to help her relax after work.
The hotel provided complimentary bottles of foaming bath for their guests.
