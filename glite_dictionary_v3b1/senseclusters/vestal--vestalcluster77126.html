vestal

noun
a chaste or pure woman
8.2 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛstəl / (AmE), / ˈvɛstəl / (BrE)
a woman who is chaste or pure, especially in an idealized or literary sense; sometimes used with reference to the Vestal Virgins of ancient Rome

Examples

A vestal virgin
In the ancient ceremony, a vestal was charged with tending the sacred flame.
She prided herself on living like a vestal, devoted to purity and spiritual pursuits.
Some poets have described her as a modern vestal, untouched by worldly concerns.
