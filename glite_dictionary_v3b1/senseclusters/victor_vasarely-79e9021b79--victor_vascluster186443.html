Victor Vasarely

proper noun
French op art painter (1908–1997)
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪktər vəˈsɑːrəli / (AmE), / ˈvɪktə vəˈsɑːrəli / (BrE)
A French painter of Hungarian origin (1908–1997) who was a pioneer of op art, known for his geometric abstract works.
