cash cow

noun
source of steady income
9.3
Pronunciation: / kæʃ kaʊ / (AmE), / kaʃ kaʊ / (BrE)
a business, product, or asset that generates a steady and significant flow of income, often with minimal ongoing effort

Examples

A cash cow business
Once established, the subscription service quickly became a reliable cash cow for the business.
The company's new software proved to be a real cash cow for the shareholders.
Tourism has long been the cash cow of the local economy.
