embed

verb
assign (journalist) to a military unit
7.8
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbɛd / (AmE), / ɪmˈbɛd / (BrE)
to assign a journalist to accompany and report on a military unit during a conflict or operation.

Examples

To embed a reporter
During the war, several journalists were embedded with front-line troops to provide real-time coverage.
She was embedded with the peacekeeping battalion for six months to document their day-to-day operations.
The government allowed select reporters to embed with the army during the conflict.
