unsuitably

adverb
in an inappropriate or unsuitable way
2.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsuːɾəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsuːtəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is not appropriate or suitable for a particular purpose, situation, or person

Examples

Speak unsuitably loudly
He spoke unsuitably loudly during the quiet ceremony, drawing unwanted attention.
The children were unsuitably dressed for the cold weather and soon felt chilly.
Wearing jeans to the formal dinner was unsuitably casual for the occasion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inappropriately - in a manner not suitable or proper for the circumstances
  • antonym: suitably - in a suitable or appropriate manner
