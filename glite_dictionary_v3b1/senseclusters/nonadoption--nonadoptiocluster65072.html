nonadoption

noun
state of not being adopted
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˈdɑpʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəˈdɒpʃən / (BrE)
the state or condition of not being legally adopted, especially referring to a child who has not been adopted

Examples

Child's nonadoption status
The child's nonadoption status affected her eligibility for certain social services.

The report highlighted the emotional impact of nonadoption on children in the welfare system.
