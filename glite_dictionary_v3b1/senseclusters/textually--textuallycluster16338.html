textually

adverb
by text message
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛkstʃuəli / (AmE), / ˈtɛkstjʊəli / (BrE)
by means of a text message or through written electronic communication such as SMS.

Examples

Textually confirm plans
He confirmed the meeting textually instead of making a phone call.
If you have any questions, please contact me textually and I will respond as soon as possible.
She prefers to communicate textually rather than through voice messages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electronically - by means of electronic communication
  • antonym: orally - by spoken word
