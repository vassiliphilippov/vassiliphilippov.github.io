clubber

noun
club member
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌbər / (AmE), / ˈklʌbə / (BrE)
a person who is a member of a club or organized group, especially a sports or social club

Examples

A sports clubber
As the oldest clubber in the rowing crew, he mentored all the younger members each season.
Every Friday, the local chess clubber would organize tournaments for new players.
The clubber proudly wore her team jacket to every event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: member - a person who belongs to a group or organization
  • antonym: outsider - a person who does not belong to a particular group
  • synonym: participant - someone who takes part in an activity or event
