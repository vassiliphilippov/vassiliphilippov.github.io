strandline

noun
shoreline debris mark
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈstrændˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈstrændˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a line of debris or mark left on a shore by the highest reach of the tide or water

Examples

Beach strandline
After the storm, a thick strandline of seaweed and driftwood stretched along the beach.
Children searched the strandline for interesting shells and small crabs.
The strandline showed how far the water had reached during the high tide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: high-water mark - the highest point reached by a body of water
  • synonym: tideline - the line left on the shore by the highest tide
