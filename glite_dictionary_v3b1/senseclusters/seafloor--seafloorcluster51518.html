seafloor

noun
bottom of the ocean

Examples

The seafloor surface
Scientists use submarines to explore the seafloor and discover new species.
Volcanic activity can create mountains on the seafloor over millions of years.
Cables laid on the seafloor connect continents for global internet communication.

Many strange creatures live on the deep seafloor where sunlight never reaches.
The shipwreck was discovered resting on the seafloor after hundreds of years.

Synonyms

  • ocean floor
    2.9 1.67/m
    ocean floor is the bottom of the sea with plains, ridges, and trenches, while seafloor names that surface more generally.
  • seabed
    2.4 0.90/m
    seabed is the ground at the sea's bottom and common in everyday speech, while seafloor is a neutral, general name for that surface.
  • sea bottom
    2.5 0.25/m
    sea bottom is a plain way to say the base of the sea, while seafloor is the standard single-word term used in writing.
