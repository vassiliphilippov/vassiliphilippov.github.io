frog kick

noun
swimming kick resembling a frog's motion
8.2 2.9
Pronunciation: /frɑːg kɪk/ /; / /frɔːg kɪk/ (AmE), /frɒg kɪk/ (BrE)
A swimming kick in which the knees are drawn upward and outward, then the legs are extended and brought together, mimicking the motion of a frog.

Examples

The frog kick stroke
Learning the frog kick is essential for mastering the breaststroke.
Many scuba divers prefer the frog kick because it stirs up less silt underwater.
She used the frog kick to propel herself across the pool efficiently.
