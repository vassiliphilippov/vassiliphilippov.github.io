speedwalk

verb
to walk quickly
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːdˌwɔk / (AmE), / ˈspiːdˌwɔːk / (BrE)
to walk at a pace faster than normal walking but slower than running, often for exercise

Examples

To speedwalk in the park
Every morning, she likes to speedwalk around the park for half an hour.
He decided to speedwalk to the train station to make sure he arrived on time.
Many people speedwalk as a low-impact alternative to jogging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: power walk - to walk briskly for exercise
  • degree higher: run - to move at a speed faster than walking by lifting both feet off the ground
  • degree lower: stroll - to walk in a slow, relaxed way
