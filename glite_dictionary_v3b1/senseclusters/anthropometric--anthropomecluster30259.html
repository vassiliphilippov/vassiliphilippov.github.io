anthropometric

adjective
relating to body measurement
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌænθrəpəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌanθrəʊpəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the measurement of the human body and its proportions.

Examples

Anthropometric data
Athletic coaches often rely on anthropometric data to tailor training programs for their athletes.
Ergonomists consider anthropometric measurements when designing comfortable office furniture.
The scientist used anthropometric methods to assess the body composition of the participants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropometry - the scientific study of measurements of the human body
