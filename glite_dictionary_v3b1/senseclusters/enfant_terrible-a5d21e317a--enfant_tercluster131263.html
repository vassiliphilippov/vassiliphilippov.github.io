enfant terrible

noun
person whose behavior embarrasses others
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɑ̃ˌfɑ̃ teɪˈriːbəl / (AmE), / ɑ̃ˌfɑ̃ tɛˈriːblə / (BrE)
a person, often young and successful, whose unconventional or shocking behavior embarrasses or annoys others

Examples

An enfant terrible personality
His outspoken remarks at the meeting made him something of an enfant terrible among his colleagues.
Once an enfant terrible of the theater, she is now respected for her innovative productions.
The young artist became known as the enfant terrible of the fashion world, constantly challenging accepted norms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iconoclast - a person who attacks or criticizes cherished beliefs or institutions
