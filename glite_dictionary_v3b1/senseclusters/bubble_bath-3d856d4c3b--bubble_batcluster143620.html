bubble bath

noun
foam-filled scented bath
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌbəl bæθ / (AmE), / ˈbʌbəl bɑːθ / (BrE)
A bath in which a special liquid or powder is added to the water to produce foam and scent.

Examples

A relaxing bubble bath
After a long day, I love to relax in a bubble bath with candles and music.
Children often enjoy a bubble bath because of all the colorful bubbles it creates.
She poured the lavender-scented bubble bath into the hot water and watched the foam grow.
