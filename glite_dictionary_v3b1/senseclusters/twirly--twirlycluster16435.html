twirly

adjective
dizzy or giddy
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈtwɜːr.liː/ /; / /ˈtwɜːr.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈtwɜː.liː/ /; / /ˈtwɜː.lɪ/ (BrE)

Examples

Feeling twirly after spinning
After spinning in circles, she felt twirly and had to hold onto the wall.
The children were so excited that they became twirly after running around the playground.
He laughed and admitted he was a bit twirly after the fast ride.
