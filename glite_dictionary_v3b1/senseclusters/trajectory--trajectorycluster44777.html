trajectory

noun
the path of a moving object in space
4.5 5.6
Pronunciation: /trə.ˈdʒe.k.tər.iː/ (AmE), /trə.ˈdʒe.k.tə.riː/ (BrE)

Examples

The trajectory of a ball
A baseball's trajectory can be affected by the wind.
She tracked the trajectory of the comet across the night sky.
The rocket's trajectory was carefully calculated to ensure a safe landing on Mars.
