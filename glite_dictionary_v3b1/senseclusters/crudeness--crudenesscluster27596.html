crudeness

noun
state of being unrefined or rough
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkruːdnəs / (AmE), / ˈkruːdnəs /; / ˈkruːdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being rough, unrefined, or lacking sophistication
