long legs

noun
legs longer than average
2.0
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ lɛɡz / (AmE), / lɒŋ lɛɡz / (BrE)
legs that are longer than average, often giving an advantage in running, jumping, or appearance

Examples

She has long legs
Her long legs make her a fast runner on the track team.
Models with long legs are often chosen for fashion shows.
The spider's long legs help it move quickly across the floor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: short legs - legs that are shorter than average
  • synonym: stilt-like legs - very long, thin legs resembling stilts
