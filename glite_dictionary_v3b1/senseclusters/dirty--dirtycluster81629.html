dirty

adjective
stormy or unpleasant weather
Pronunciation: / ˈdɝːti / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti / (BrE)
(of weather) characterized by stormy, wet, windy, or generally unpleasant conditions.

Examples

Dirty weather
Forecasts warned of dirty weather coming in from the Atlantic.
The sailors were prepared for dirty weather as the storm approached.
We had to postpone the picnic due to the dirty conditions outside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fair - of weather, fine and clear
  • synonym: stormy - of weather, with strong winds and heavy rain
