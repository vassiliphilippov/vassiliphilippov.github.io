police squad

noun
specialized police unit
Pronunciation: / pəˈlis skwɑd / (AmE), / pəˈliːs skwɒd / (BrE)
a small group of police officers assigned to deal with a particular type of crime or special duty

Examples

A police squad team
A police squad was dispatched immediately after the bank alarm went off.
The city formed a new police squad to combat drug trafficking in the downtown area.
The specialized police squad investigates cases of organized crime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: squad - a small organized group of people, especially soldiers or police officers
