angiosarcoma

noun
rare malignant tumor of blood vessels
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒioʊsɑːrˈkoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌandʒiəʊsɑːˈkəʊmə / (BrE)
A rare malignant tumor originating from the endothelial cells of blood vessels, most often occurring in the skin or breast.

Examples

Rare angiosarcoma tumor
Researchers are studying the genetic factors that contribute to angiosarcoma development.
The patient was diagnosed with angiosarcoma in her left breast after a routine screening.
Treatment options for angiosarcoma are limited due to its aggressive nature.
