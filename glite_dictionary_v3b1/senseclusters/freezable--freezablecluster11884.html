freezable

adjective
can be frozen
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfriːzəbəl / (BrE)
able to be frozen without being damaged or losing quality

Examples

Freezable food
The leftover pasta is freezable, so you can save it for another meal.
Not all fruits are freezable without changing their texture or taste.
Check the packaging to see if the product is freezable before putting it in the freezer.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: frozen - preserved or hardened by freezing
  • antonym: perishable - likely to spoil or decay quickly
