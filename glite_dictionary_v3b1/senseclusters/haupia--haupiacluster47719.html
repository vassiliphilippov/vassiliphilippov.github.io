haupia

noun
Hawaiian coconut dessert
9.7
Pronunciation: / haʊˈpiːə / (AmE), / haʊˈpiːə / (BrE)
a Hawaiian dessert made from coconut milk and thickened with starch, usually served as a pudding or in squares

Examples

A piece of haupia
At the luau, we finished our meal with a delicious piece of haupia.
She learned to make traditional haupia using fresh coconut milk from the market.
The restaurant offered haupia as a sweet treat after the main course.
