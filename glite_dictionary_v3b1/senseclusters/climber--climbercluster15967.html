climber

noun
a plant that climbs by clinging or twining
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪmər / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmə / (BrE)
A plant that grows upward by clinging to or twining around a support or other plants.

Examples

A climber plant
Morning glories are popular climbers because of their vibrant blue flowers.
The wisteria is a beautiful climber that drapes itself over garden arches.
We planted a fast-growing climber along the fence for added privacy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vine - a plant with a long, thin stem that grows along the ground or climbs by attaching itself to something
