lower

verb
look angry or sullen; scowl or frown
Pronunciation: / ˈlaʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈlaʊ.ə / (BrE)
(of a person) to look angry or sullen; to wrinkle one's forehead or draw down one's brows, typically as a sign of disapproval.

Examples

He lowers his brows
He tends to lower when he's unhappy with the decision.
The teacher's face began to lower as the noise in the classroom increased.
Without a word, she lowered at him, making her disapproval clear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scowl - to frown in an angry or bad-tempered way
  • antonym: smile - to make a happy or pleased facial expression
