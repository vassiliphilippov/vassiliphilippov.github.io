Synonyms

  • motion-picture photography
    5.0 0.00/m

  • camerawork
    4.0 0.09/m
    camerawork focuses on how the camera moves and is operated in a scene, while cinematography covers the whole visual making of a film.
  • filming
    2.1 3.40/m
