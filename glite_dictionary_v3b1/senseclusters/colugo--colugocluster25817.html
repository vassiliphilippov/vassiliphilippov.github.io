colugo

noun
gliding mammal of Southeast Asia/Philippines
9.2 9.6
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈluː.goʊ/ (AmE), /kə.ˈluː.gəʊ/ (BrE)
An arboreal nocturnal mammal native to Southeast Asia and the Philippines, resembling a lemur and equipped with skin folds that allow it to glide between trees.

Examples

The colugo glides
Despite its nickname 'flying lemur,' the colugo is not actually related to true lemurs.
Researchers studied the nocturnal habits of the colugo in the forests of Borneo.
The colugo can glide more than 70 meters between trees in the rainforest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flying lemur - another common name for the colugo, though not a true lemur
