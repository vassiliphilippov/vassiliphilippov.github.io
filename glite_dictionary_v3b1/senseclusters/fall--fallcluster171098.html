Synonyms

  • drop
    1.0 2/m
    drop is a sudden downward movement, often quick or caused by something, while fall is any coming down from higher places.
  • tumble
    2.9 2/m
    tumble is a sudden fall that often involves rolling or turning over, while fall is a general movement down from higher position.
  • spill
    1.7 0.6/m
    spill is an informal word for falling from a standing position, often when moving, while fall is a neutral term for coming down.
  • descent
    3.7 4/m
    descent is a slow or planned movement down or a downward slope, while fall is often sudden and unplanned coming down.
