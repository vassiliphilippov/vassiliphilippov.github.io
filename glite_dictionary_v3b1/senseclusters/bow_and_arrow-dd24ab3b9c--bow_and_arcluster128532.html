bow and arrow

noun
weapon system: bow with arrows
Pronunciation: / boʊ ənd ˈɛroʊ / (AmE), / bəʊ ənd ˈærəʊ / (BrE)
A weapon made up of a bow for shooting arrows and the arrows themselves, used in hunting or as a weapon of war.

Examples

A bow and arrow set
During the demonstration, the instructor showed how to use a bow and arrow safely.
Early hunters relied on the bow and arrow to catch animals for food.
Robin Hood was famous for his skill with the bow and arrow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: archery - the sport or skill of shooting with a bow and arrows
