enterobacterial

adjective
of Enterobacteriaceae
Pronunciation: / ˌɛntəroʊbəˈtɪəriəl / (AmE), / ˌɛntərəʊbəˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
relating to bacteria of the family Enterobacteriaceae, which includes many intestinal bacteria

Examples

Enterobacterial infection
The doctor explained that the infection was caused by an enterobacterial organism.
Researchers are studying the resistance patterns of enterobacterial pathogens in hospitals.
Proper hygiene can help prevent the spread of enterobacterial diseases in the community.
