Pentium

proper noun
Intel processor brand
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛntiəm / (AmE), / ˈpɛntiəm / (BrE)

Examples

Pentium processor
My first computer had a Pentium processor and ran Windows 98.
The Pentium series was popular among home computer users in the 1990s.
He upgraded his old desktop to a newer Pentium model for better performance.
