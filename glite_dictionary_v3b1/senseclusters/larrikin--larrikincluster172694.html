larrikin

adjective
mischievous and rebellious
Pronunciation: / ˈlærɪkɪn / (AmE), / ˈlærɪkɪn / (BrE)
behaving in a noisy, mischievous, or cheekily rebellious way, often ignoring rules or conventions

Examples

Larrikin behavior
His larrikin attitude often got him into trouble at school.
The comedian was known for her larrikin sense of humor and playful pranks.
Despite his larrikin behavior, everyone admired his honesty and spirit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheeky - boldly rude or disrespectful
  • antonym: obedient - willing to follow rules or authority
  • synonym: rowdy - noisy and disorderly
