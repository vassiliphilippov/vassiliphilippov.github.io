interlocus

noun
DNA region between loci
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈloʊkəs / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈləʊkəs / (BrE)
the region or distance of DNA located between two genetic loci on a chromosome

Examples

Interlocus distance
The scientists analyzed the interlocus distance to understand genetic recombination rates.
A mutation was found within the interlocus region separating two important genes.
Researchers compared the interlocus sequences across different species to study evolutionary changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interval - a space or distance between two points
  • antonym: locus - a specific position on a chromosome
