mock polo neck collar

noun
collar resembling a polo neck but sewn at both ends
3.7 6.1
Pronunciation: /mɑːk ˈpoʊ.loʊ nɛk ˈkɑːr.lər/ (AmE), /mɒk ˈpəʊ.ləʊ nɛk ˈkɒ.lə/ (BrE)
