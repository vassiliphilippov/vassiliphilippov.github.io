glancingly

adverb
lightly or indirectly
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈglæn.sɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈglɑːn.sɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that touches or affects something only lightly or at an angle, not directly.

Examples

Hit glancingly
The stone hit the window glancingly, causing only a small scratch.
She mentioned the topic glancingly during her speech, without going into detail.
The car was struck glancingly by another vehicle but was not seriously damaged.

  • synonym: obliquely - in a slanting or indirect way
