entanglement

noun
state of being tangled
6.3
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtæŋɡəlmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈtæŋɡəlmənt / (BrE)
a state of being twisted together or closely connected, making separation difficult

Examples

Cable entanglement
The entanglement of cables behind the desk made cleaning very difficult.
After hours of work, she finally managed to resolve the entanglement in her necklace chain.
Political entanglement between the two countries led to a complicated alliance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knot - an interlacing, looping, or tying together of something
  • antonym: order - a state in which everything is arranged neatly
  • synonym: tangle - a confused mass of things twisted together
