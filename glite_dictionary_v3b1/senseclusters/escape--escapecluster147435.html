escape

noun
act of getting away or breaking free
Pronunciation: / ɪˈskeɪp / (AmE), / ɪˈskeɪp / (BrE)
the act of getting away from a place, person, or situation where one is held or in danger

Examples

A daring escape
The movie tells the story of a dramatic escape from captivity.
The prisoner's daring escape made headlines nationwide.
Their dog managed an escape through a hole in the fence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flight - the act of fleeing or running away to avoid danger or capture
