Fairbanks

proper noun
Alaskan city
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrˌbæŋks / (AmE), / ˈfɛəˌbæŋks / (BrE)
a city in central Alaska, United States, known for its cold climate and proximity to the Arctic Circle

Examples

Fairbanks Alaska
