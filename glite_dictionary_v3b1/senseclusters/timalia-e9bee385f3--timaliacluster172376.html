Timalia

noun
genus of Asian babbler birds
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɪˈmɑːliə / (AmE), / tɪˈmeɪlɪə / (BrE)
The type genus of the bird family Timaliidae, which includes certain species of babblers native to Asia.
