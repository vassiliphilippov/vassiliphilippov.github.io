cult

noun
intensely followed interest or fad
2.0
Pronunciation: / kʌlt / (AmE), / kʌlt / (BrE)
An interest, hobby, or activity that is followed with intense or exaggerated enthusiasm by a particular group of people.

Examples

Cult following
That obscure band has developed a cult following among indie music fans.
The television show achieved cult status thanks to its passionate and loyal viewers.
Yoga quickly became a cult in the city, drawing dedicated practitioners from all walks of life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craze - an activity or object that is very popular for a short time
  • synonym: fad - a fashion or interest taken up with great enthusiasm for a short time
  • antonym: tradition - a custom or belief passed down over generations
