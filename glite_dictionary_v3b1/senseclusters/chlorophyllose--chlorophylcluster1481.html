chlorophyllose

adjective
relating to chlorophyll
7.9
Pronunciation: / klɔːˈrɑːfɪˌloʊs / (AmE), / klɔːˈrɪfɪˌləʊs / (BrE)
Relating to or containing chlorophyll; having the nature of or being like chlorophyll.

Examples
