simultaneousness

noun
the state of being simultaneous
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪməlˈteɪniəsnəs / (AmE), / ˌsɪməlˈteɪniəsnəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of occurring, existing, or being done at the same time.

Examples

The simultaneousness of events
Modern technology allows for the simultaneousness of communication across the globe.
The simultaneousness of the two events made it hard to believe they were unrelated.
The composer experimented with the simultaneousness of different musical themes in her symphony.
