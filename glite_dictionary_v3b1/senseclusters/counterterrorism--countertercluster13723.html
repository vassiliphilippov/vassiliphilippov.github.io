counterterrorism

noun
measures taken to prevent terrorism
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərˈtɛrərɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəˈtɛrərɪzəm / (BrE)
Action, policies, or strategies intended to prevent, deter, or respond to acts of terrorism.

Examples

Counterterrorism efforts
New laws were enacted to improve counterterrorism efforts nationwide.
She specializes in international security and counterterrorism policy.
The government has increased funding for counterterrorism operations in recent years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterinsurgency - military or political action taken to defeat insurgency
  • antonym: terrorism - the use of violence and intimidation, especially against civilians, for political aims
