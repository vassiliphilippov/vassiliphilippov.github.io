East India kino

noun
reddish resin from Pterocarpus trees
9.9
Pronunciation: / ist ˈɪndiə ˈkiːnoʊ / (AmE), / iːst ˈɪndiə ˈkiːnəʊ / (BrE)
a reddish or black resinous juice obtained from certain Pterocarpus trees, used in medicine and tanning

Examples

East India kino resin
A reddish stain marked the bark, evidence of East India kino oozing from the tree.
The extract from East India kino was prescribed in herbal medicine.
Traditional tanners used East India kino for its strong astringent properties.
