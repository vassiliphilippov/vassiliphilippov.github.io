cummerbund

noun
broad pleated sash for formal dress
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌm.ər.bʌnd / (AmE), / ˈkʌm.ə.bənd / (BrE)
A broad, pleated sash, usually worn around the waist as part of formal evening dress, especially with a tuxedo.

Examples

A cummerbund sash
At the formal banquet, all the men wore cummerbunds with their evening suits.
He adjusted his cummerbund before stepping onto the stage for the awards ceremony.
The classic tuxedo is often complemented by a black silk cummerbund.
