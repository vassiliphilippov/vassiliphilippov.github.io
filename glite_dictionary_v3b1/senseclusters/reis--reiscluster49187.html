reis

noun
old Portuguese currency
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪs / (AmE), / ˈreɪs / (BrE)
the former currency unit of Portugal and Brazil, plural of real

Examples

Old reis coins
The museum has a rare collection of nineteenth-century reis coins from Brazil.
He inherited a box of old reis from his Portuguese grandfather.
Before the introduction of the escudo, people in Portugal used reis for everyday purchases.

Related Terms

  • antonym: escudo - the currency that replaced the real in Portugal
  • synonym: real - the basic monetary unit of Portugal and Brazil before the escudo and cruzeiro
