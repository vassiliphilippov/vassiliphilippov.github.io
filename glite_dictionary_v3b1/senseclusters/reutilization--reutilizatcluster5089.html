reutilization

noun
using again
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌjuːtɪləˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌjuːtɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of using something again for a new or similar purpose

Examples

The reutilization of materials
The reutilization of old furniture can save money and reduce waste.
Many cities encourage the reutilization of materials to promote sustainability.
Through reutilization, companies can lower their environmental impact and operating costs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disposal - the act of getting rid of something
  • synonym: recycle - to process used materials so they can be used again
  • synonym: reuse - the act of using something again instead of throwing it away
