pop feminism

noun
mainstream, popular-culture feminism
6.1 5.2
Pronunciation: /pɑːp ˈfem.ɪ.nɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /pɒp ˈfem.ɪ.nɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
A contemporary form of feminism that is present in popular culture, often emphasizing mainstream ideas and accessible messaging about gender equality.

Examples

Pop feminism in culture
Pop feminism has made feminist ideas more visible in fashion, music, and film.
Pop feminism is often discussed in relation to celebrity activism and social media movements.
Some critics argue that pop feminism lacks depth, but it brings important issues to a wider audience.
