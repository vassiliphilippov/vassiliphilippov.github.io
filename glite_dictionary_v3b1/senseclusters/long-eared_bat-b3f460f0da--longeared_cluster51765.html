long-eared bat

noun
bat species with long ears
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ ˈɪrd bæt / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈɪəd bæt / (BrE)
any of various bats found in the Old or New World characterized by very long ears, including species in the genera Plecotus and others.

Examples

Long-eared bat species
