Examples

Bulging muscles
He had bulging muscles after years of lifting weights.
The box was so full that its sides were bulging.
The sack sat in the corner, its seams almost bulging with grain.
He noticed her bulging eyes when she was surprised.
The bulging wall suggested there was water damage behind the plaster.

Synonyms

  • protuberant
    8.4 0.10/m
    protuberant is used for parts that stick out sharply from a surface, while bulging implies a rounded swell from inside.
  • bulbous
    5.5 1.21/m
    bulbous describes a round, swollen shape like a plant bulb, while bulging focuses on swelling from pressure.
  • bulgy
    4.3 0.03/m
    bulgy is an informal word for something sticking out in a rounded way, while bulging is more neutral and common.
  • bellied
    5.6 0.53/m
    bellied describes something shaped like a belly or having a rounded front, while bulging emphasizes swelling from inside.
  • convex
    5.8 0.18/m
    convex means having an outward curved surface like a dome, while bulging suggests a swollen, rounded push caused by pressure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protruding - sticking out or extending beyond a surface
  • antonym: sunken - curving or moving inward rather than outward
  • synonym: swollen - abnormally expanded or puffed up, especially from internal pressure
