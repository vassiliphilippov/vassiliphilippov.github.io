bulging

adjective
swelling or curving outward
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌldʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʌldʒɪŋ / (BrE)
swelling or curving outward in a rounded shape, usually because of pressure inside

Examples

Bulging muscles
He had bulging muscles after years of lifting weights.
The box was so full that its sides were bulging.
The sack sat in the corner, its seams almost bulging with grain.
He noticed her bulging eyes when she was surprised.
The bulging wall suggested there was water damage behind the plaster.
The suitcase was so full that its sides were bulging with clothes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protruding - sticking out or extending beyond a surface
  • antonym: sunken - curving or moving inward rather than outward
  • synonym: swollen - abnormally expanded or puffed up, especially from internal pressure
