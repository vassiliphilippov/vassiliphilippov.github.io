mutual admiration society

noun
group with excessive mutual praise
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːtʃuəl ædˌmaɪrəˈʃən səˌsaɪəti / (AmE), / ˈmjuːtʃuəl ædˌmaɪrəˈʃən səˌsaɪəti / (BrE)
a group of people who express great admiration for each other, often in an excessive or self-congratulatory way

Examples

A mutual admiration society group
Critics accused the awards committee of being a mutual admiration society rather than recognizing true talent.
It's easy to mistake their book club for a mutual admiration society when everyone keeps complimenting each other's opinions.
The panel discussion turned into a bit of a mutual admiration society as the speakers praised each other's work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circle jerk - (slang) a group that praises itself or its members excessively
  • synonym: echo chamber - an environment where people only hear opinions similar to their own
