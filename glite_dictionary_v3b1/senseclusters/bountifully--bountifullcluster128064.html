bountifully

adverb
generously or abundantly
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊntɪfəli / (AmE), / ˈbaʊntɪfəli / (BrE)
In a way that is generous, plentiful, or abundant.

Examples

To bountifully give
She donated bountifully to several charities every year.
The garden was bountifully supplied with fresh vegetables throughout the summer.
The rain fell bountifully, ending the months-long drought.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generously - in a generous manner
  • antonym: sparingly - in a restricted or frugal manner
