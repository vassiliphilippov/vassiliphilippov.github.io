In a way that is generous, plentiful, or abundant.

Examples

To bountifully give
She donated bountifully to several charities every year.
The garden was bountifully supplied with fresh vegetables throughout the summer.
The rain fell bountifully, ending the months-long drought.

  • copiously
    6.1 0.40/m

  • aplenty
    4.2 0.00/m

  • profusely
    4.8 1.88/m

  • generously
    2.0 4.28/m
    generously means giving freely to others, often people, while bountifully can mean both giving and abundant quantity.
  • liberally
    3.9 1.18/m

  • lavishly
    5.6 1.41/m
