jade

noun
a green semiprecious gemstone
Pronunciation: / dʒeɪd / (AmE), / dʒeɪd / (BrE)
a hard, typically green semiprecious stone that can be highly polished, used for ornaments and jewelry; composed chiefly of jadeite or nephrite

Examples

A jade necklace
She wore a beautiful necklace made of jade and gold.
The museum displayed ancient Chinese carvings in jade.
The ring’s stone was a deep green jade that shimmered in the light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jadeite - a variety of jade, a sodium aluminum silicate
  • synonym: nephrite - a variety of jade, mainly composed of a calcium magnesium silicate
