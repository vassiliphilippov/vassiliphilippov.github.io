Urtica

noun
genus of nettle plants yielding fiber
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɚˈtaɪkə /; / ɜːrˈtiːkə / (AmE), / ɜːˈtiːkə /; / ɜːˈtɪkə / (BrE)
a genus of nettle plants, some species of which have fibers that can be used for making textiles or rope
