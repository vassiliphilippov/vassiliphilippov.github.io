escape

noun
safety valve in pressurized containers
2.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈskeɪp / (AmE), / ɪˈskeɪp / (BrE)
when a substance (like gas or liquid) leaks or is released from a container, or a device that allows this to happen

Examples

A gas escape
Safety regulations require that every pressure vessel have a functioning escape.
The escape released steam when the boiler pressure rose too high.
The engineer checked the escape to ensure it was operating correctly.
A slight escape of coolant was observed during the inspection.
Detecting any escape of natural gas is crucial for safety in the laboratory.
The engineers quickly sealed the pipe to stop the escape of toxic chemicals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leak - an unintended hole or crack that allows the escape of fluid
  • synonym: safety valve - a device for automatically releasing pressure from a boiler or other vessel
  • synonym: spill - an act of something, especially liquid, flowing out accidentally
