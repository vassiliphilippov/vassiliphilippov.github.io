escape

noun
safety valve in pressurized containers
2.5 3.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˈskeɪp / (AmE), / ɪˈskeɪp / (BrE)
when a substance (like gas or liquid) leaks or is released from a container, or a device that allows this to happen

Examples

A gas escape
