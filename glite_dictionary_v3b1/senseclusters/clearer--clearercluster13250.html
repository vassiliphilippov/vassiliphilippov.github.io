clearer

noun
financial transaction processor
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrər / (AmE), / ˈklɪərə / (BrE)
a person or organization that settles or processes financial transactions, such as payments or checks, between parties

Examples

Bank clearer
The central bank served as the clearer for all major interbank transactions that day.
A reliable clearer ensures that payments between companies are processed smoothly and efficiently.
Many financial institutions rely on a third-party clearer to handle the settlement of checks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intermediary - a person or organization acting as a link between others
  • synonym: settlement agent - an entity that settles financial transactions between parties
