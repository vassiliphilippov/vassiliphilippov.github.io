nonindustrial

adjective
not having developed industry
5.8
Pronunciation: / nɑn.ɪnˈdʌs.tri.əl / (AmE), / nɒn.ɪnˈdʌs.tri.əl / (BrE)
not having or involving highly developed manufacturing or industrial activity.

Examples

Nonindustrial society
He preferred to study nonindustrial societies to understand traditional ways of life.
Many remote areas remain largely nonindustrial and depend on agriculture for their livelihood.
The region is famous for its nonindustrial landscapes and unspoiled countryside.
