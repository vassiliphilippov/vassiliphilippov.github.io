gallery

noun
covered corridor with arches or columns
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæləri /; / ˈɡæləɹi / (AmE), / ˈɡæləri /; / ˈɡaləri / (BrE)
a long, narrow room, corridor, balcony, or porch in a building, often used for walking, viewing, or providing access
