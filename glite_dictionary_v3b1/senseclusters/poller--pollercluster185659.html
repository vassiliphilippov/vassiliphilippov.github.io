poller

noun
vote collector
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊlər / (AmE), / ˈpɒlə / (BrE)
a person or device that conducts, collects, or records votes in an election

Examples

Election poller
The poller checked each voter's identification before handing out ballots.
A new electronic poller was installed to speed up the voting process.
During the election, the poller ensured that all votes were properly recorded.

Related Terms

  • synonym: election official - a person responsible for managing voting at a polling place
  • antonym: voter - a person who casts a vote in an election
