swirly

adjective
dizzy or lightheaded
6.0 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Ààsw…úÀêrli / (AmE), / Ààsw…úÀêli / (BrE)
feeling dizzy or lightheaded, as if things are spinning or turning around you

Examples

Feeling swirly after spinning
After spinning on the ride, I felt swirly and had to sit down.
The medicine made her feel a bit swirly for a few minutes.
He stood up too quickly and suddenly everything seemed swirly around him.
