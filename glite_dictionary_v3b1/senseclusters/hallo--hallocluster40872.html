hallo

verb
to shout to attract attention
4.9
Pronunciation: / həˈloʊ / (AmE), / həˈləʊ / (BrE)
to call out or shout loudly to attract someone's attention, especially from a distance

Examples

To hallo across fields
He halloed across the yard when he saw the bus approaching.
The hikers halloed to their friends on the other side of the river.
She halloed loudly, hoping someone would hear her in the fog.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hail - to call out to attract attention
  • synonym: shout - to speak very loudly
  • antonym: whisper - to speak very quietly
