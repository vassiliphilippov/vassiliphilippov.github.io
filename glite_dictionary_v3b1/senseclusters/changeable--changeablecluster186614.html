changeable

adjective
varying in color when viewed from different angles
5.3 4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃeɪn.dʒə.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈtʃeɪn.dʒə.bəl/ (BrE)
(of a material or surface) showing different colors when viewed from different angles or in different lights; iridescent.

Examples

Changeable colors
Dragonfly wings appear changeable, reflecting different colors with every shift of the light.
The changeable silk dress shimmered in hues of blue and green as she moved.
We admired the changeable surface of the gemstone, which sparkled in many shades.
