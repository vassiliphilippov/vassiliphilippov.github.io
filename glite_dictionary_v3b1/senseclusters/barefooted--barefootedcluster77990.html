Synonyms

  • barefoot
    1.1 4.65/m
    barefoot is the common word for having no shoes on the feet, while barefooted is a less common alternative form.
  • shoeless
    1.7 0.27/m
    shoeless means without shoes and is common in plain speech, while barefooted stresses truly bare feet with no covering.
  • unshod
    8.6 0.06/m
    unshod appears in older or more formal texts and can refer to animals or people, while barefooted focuses on bare human feet.
  • unshoed
    9.1 0.00/m
    unshoed is mainly used for animals like horses or technical descriptions, while barefooted describes a person with bare feet.
