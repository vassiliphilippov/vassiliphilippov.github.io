barefooted

adjective, adverb
without shoes
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrˌfʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈbeəˌfʊtɪd / (BrE)
not wearing any shoes or other covering on the feet; with bare feet

Examples

The children ran barefooted across the cool grass in the morning.

She prefers to walk barefooted in the grass on warm summer days.
