interagency

adjective
between agencies
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈreɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
involving or conducted between two or more government or organizational agencies

Examples

Interagency cooperation
The interagency task force worked together to address the environmental crisis.
An interagency agreement was signed to improve cooperation between departments.
Regular interagency meetings help ensure all organizations are aligned on the project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intra-agency - within a single agency
