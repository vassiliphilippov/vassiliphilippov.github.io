indiscreetly

adverb
without discretion or self-restraint

In a way that shows a lack of discretion, wisdom, or self-restraint; without being careful about revealing private or sensitive information.

Examples

To indiscreetly ask questions
He indiscreetly asked about their financial problems at the dinner table.
She often speaks indiscreetly, not realizing the impact her words can have.
The journalist indiscreetly revealed confidential information during the interview.

Synonyms

    8.4 0.03/m

  • unwisely
    4.1 0.40/m
    unwisely describes doing something without sense or care in general life, while indiscreetly points to exposing secrets by careless words.
