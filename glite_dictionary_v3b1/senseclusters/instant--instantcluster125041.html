instant

adjective
of the present month (archaic, in dates)
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnstənt / (AmE), / ˈɪnstənt / (BrE)
(archaic) referring to the present month; used after the day in correspondence to specify it belongs to this month.

Examples

7th instant
Please reply to my letter of the 7th instant at your earliest convenience.
The meeting held on the 14th instant has been rescheduled to next week.
Your payment of the 3rd instant has been received and processed.
