responsibly

adverb
in a responsible manner
3.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɑnsəbli / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒnsəbli / (BrE)
in a way that shows you can be trusted to do what is right or expected; in a responsible manner

Examples

To act responsibly
Drivers must act responsibly to ensure everyone's safety on the road.
Please dispose of your waste responsibly to protect the environment.
She handled the project responsibly and met every deadline.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irresponsibly - in a way that is not responsible; without care or thought for consequences
  • synonym: responsible - having an obligation to do something or having control over or care for someone, as part of one's job or role
