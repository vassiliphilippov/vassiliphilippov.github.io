Nostradamus

proper noun
French astrologer and author of prophecies
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːstrəˈdɑːməs / (AmE), / ˌnɒstrəˈdɑːməs / (BrE)
(1503–1566) A French astrologer and physician famous for his book of prophecies, containing cryptic predictions that are widely interpreted and debated.

Examples

Nostradamus predictions
Many people believe that Nostradamus predicted significant world events centuries before they happened.
Some scholars spend years interpreting the cryptic verses left by Nostradamus in his famous book.
The writings of Nostradamus have sparked endless debates among historians and conspiracy theorists alike.
