pillar

noun
main supporter; important member
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlər / (AmE), / ˈpɪlə / (BrE)
A person who is a main supporter or an important member of a group, organization, or community.

Examples

Pillar of the community
Many regarded Dr. Khan as a pillar of the local medical community.
She was a pillar of strength for her family during difficult times.
Without its financial pillar, the organization would struggle to survive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cornerstone - an important quality or feature on which a particular thing depends or is based
  • synonym: mainstay - a person or thing on which something else is based or depends
