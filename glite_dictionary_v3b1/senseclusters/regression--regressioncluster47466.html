regression

noun
statistical prediction method
Pronunciation: / rɪˈɡrɛʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈɡrɛʃən / (BrE)
a statistical method for estimating the relationship between variables, used to predict one value from another

Examples

Linear regression analysis
A linear regression model can help forecast future sales based on historical data.
She explored multiple types of regression to find the best fit for the data.
The scientist used regression analysis to determine how temperature affects plant growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: correlation - a mutual relationship or connection between two variables
