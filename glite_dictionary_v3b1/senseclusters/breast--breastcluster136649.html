breast

verb
to reach the summit of something
Pronunciation: / brɛst / (AmE), / brɛst / (BrE)
to reach the summit of (a hill or mountain), or to move over the top of something

Examples

To breast the hill
Once we breast the final hill, we will see the valley spread out below.
The climbers breasted the ridge just before dawn, exhausted but exhilarated by the view.
The hikers paused to catch their breath after breasting the steep incline.
