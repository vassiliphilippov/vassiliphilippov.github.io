cotton ball

noun
fungus disease of cranberries
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːtən bɔl / (AmE), / ˈkɒtən bɔːl / (BrE)
A fungal disease that affects cranberries, characterized by the appearance of cottony masses on the berries.
