total darkness

noun
complete absence of light
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊt̬l ˈdɑrk.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.tl ˈdɑːk.nəs / (BrE)
a state or condition in which there is a complete absence of light

Examples

Complete total darkness
She woke up suddenly, disoriented by the total darkness in her room.
The explorers waited in total darkness for their eyes to adjust.
The power outage left the entire neighborhood in total darkness.
