wari

proper noun
ancient Andean civilization
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːri / (AmE), / ˈwɑːri / (BrE)
an ancient Andean civilization that flourished in central Peru from about 600 to 1000 AD, also known as Huari

Examples

The wari civilization
The wari built impressive cities and road networks in the highlands of Peru.
Artifacts from the wari reveal their advanced skills in pottery and weaving.
Many scholars study the influence of the wari on later Andean cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inca - a later major civilization in the Andes of South America
  • synonym: moche - an earlier ancient civilization in northern Peru
