Schizanthus

noun
genus of Chilean herbs with showy flowers
Pronunciation: / skɪˈzænθəs / (AmE), / skɪˈzanθəs / (BrE)
A genus of Chilean herbs in the nightshade family, known for their colorful, orchid-like flowers.
