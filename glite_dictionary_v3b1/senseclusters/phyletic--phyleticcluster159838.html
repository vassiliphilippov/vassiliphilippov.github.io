phyletic

adjective
relating to evolutionary development
Pronunciation: / faɪˈlɛtɪk / (AmE), / faɪˈlɛtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the evolutionary development and diversification of a species or other group of organisms.

Examples

The phyletic lineage
He discussed the phyletic lineage that connects all primates.
The concept of phyletic evolution contrasts with the idea of rapid speciation.
The researchers analyzed the phyletic relationships among different bird species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phylogenetic - relating to the evolutionary relationships among species
