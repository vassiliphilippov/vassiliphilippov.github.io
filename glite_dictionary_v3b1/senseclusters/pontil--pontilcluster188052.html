pontil

noun
glassblowing rod
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑːn.təl/ /; / /ˈpɑːn.tɪl/ (AmE), /ˈpɒn.tɪl/ /; / /ˈpɒn.təl/ (BrE)
a metal rod used in glassblowing to hold, shape, or transfer hot glass during the forming process

Examples
