pontil

noun
glassblowing rod
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːntɪl / (AmE), / ˈpɒntɪl / (BrE)
a metal rod used in glassblowing to hold, shape, or transfer hot glass during the forming process

Examples

Glassblower's pontil rod
The artist carefully attached the vase to a pontil to shape its base.
After removing the pontil, a small mark was left on the bottom of the glass.
Each piece of hand-blown glass is finished by breaking it off the pontil.
