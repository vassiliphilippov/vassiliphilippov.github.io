sudden rain

noun
brief, unexpected rainfall
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌd.ən reɪn / (AmE), / ˈsʌd.ən reɪn / (BrE)
A brief and unexpected period of rainfall that occurs without warning.

Examples

A sudden rain shower
A sudden rain can catch hikers unprepared if they don't check the weather.
The sudden rain soaked the streets in just a few minutes.
We had to run for cover when a sudden rain started during our picnic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: showers - short periods of rain, especially light or moderate
