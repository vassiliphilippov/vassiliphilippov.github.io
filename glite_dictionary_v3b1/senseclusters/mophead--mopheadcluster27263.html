mophead

noun
thick tuft of hair
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑpˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈmɒpˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person or animal with thick, bushy, or messy hair, fur, or wool (often used playfully)

Examples

A sheep's mophead
The sheep's thick mophead of fleece kept it warm through the cold night.
Her unruly mophead of curls made her easy to spot in a crowd.
The puppy's mophead of fur covered its eyes as it played in the yard.
After the rain, his hair looked like a real mophead.
She called her little brother a mophead because his curls stuck out in every direction.
The wind turned everyone into a mophead by the time we reached the park.
