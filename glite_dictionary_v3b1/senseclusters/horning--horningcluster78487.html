horning

noun
use of horns
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of using, producing, or dealing with horns, especially in animals or musical instruments

Examples

Cattle horning
The horning of cattle is a traditional farm practice in some regions.
During the ceremony, the horning of the instrument signaled the start of the event.
Farmers discussed the benefits and drawbacks of horning in their livestock management.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dehorning - the removal of horns from animals
