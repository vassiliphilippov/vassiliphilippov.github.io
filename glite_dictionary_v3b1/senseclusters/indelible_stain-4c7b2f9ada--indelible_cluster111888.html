indelible stain

noun
a permanent, irremovable mark
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɛləbəl steɪn / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛlɪbəl steɪn / (BrE)
a mark or discoloration that cannot be removed or erased

Examples

An indelible stain
Graffiti can often become an indelible stain on public monuments.
The spilled ink left an indelible stain on Mary’s favorite shirt.
Years later, the indelible stain on the carpet was still visible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: permanent mark - a mark that persists and does not fade or get removed
  • antonym: removable stain - a stain that can be removed or cleaned
