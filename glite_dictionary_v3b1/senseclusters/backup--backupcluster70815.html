backup

noun
accumulation from obstruction or clogging
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈbækʌp / (BrE)
An accumulation or obstruction caused by clogging or a stoppage, often in traffic or plumbing.

Examples

Traffic backup
The plumber fixed the backup in the basement drain quickly.
There was a backup in the sink that needed urgent repair.
We got stuck in a backup on the highway during rush hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blockage - something that blocks a passage or outlet
  • synonym: jam - a blockage or crowding, especially of traffic
