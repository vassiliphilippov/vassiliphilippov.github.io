mesosoma

noun
insect middle section
9.9
Pronunciation: /miː.sə.ˈsoʊ.mə/ /; / /miː.zə.ˈsoʊ.mə/ (AmE), /miː.sə.ˈsəʊ.mə/ /; / /miː.zə.ˈsəʊ.mə/ (BrE)
the middle section of the body in certain arthropods, such as insects (ants, wasps) and arachnids (scorpions), between the head and abdomen
