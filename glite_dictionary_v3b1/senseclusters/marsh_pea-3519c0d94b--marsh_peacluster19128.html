marsh pea

noun
scrambling marshland plant with purplish flowers
6.0
Pronunciation: / mɑrʃ pi / (AmE), / mɑːʃ piː / (BrE)
a scrambling perennial plant found in damp or marshy areas of Eurasia and North America, with purplish flowers

Examples

Marsh pea plant
Botanists conducted a survey of marsh pea populations in the flooded meadow.
The marsh pea produces beautiful purplish flowers during the spring and summer.
We spotted a marsh pea growing near the edge of the wetland trail.
