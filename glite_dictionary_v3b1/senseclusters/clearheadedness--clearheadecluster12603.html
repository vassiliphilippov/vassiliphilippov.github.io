clearheadedness

noun
mental clarity
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrˌhɛdɪdnəs /; / ˌklɪrˈhɛdɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈklɪəˌhɛdɪdnəs /; / ˌklɪəˈhɛdɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being able to think clearly, sensibly, and logically, especially in difficult situations
