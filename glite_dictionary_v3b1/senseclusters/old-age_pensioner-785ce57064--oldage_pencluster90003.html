old-age pensioner

noun
person receiving an old-age pension
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: /oʊld eɪdʒ ˈpen.ʃən.ər/ (AmE), /əʊld eɪdʒ ˈpen.ʃənə/ (BrE)
a person who receives a state pension because they have reached the official retirement age; especially in British English.

The government increased the winter fuel allowance for every old-age pensioner this year.
