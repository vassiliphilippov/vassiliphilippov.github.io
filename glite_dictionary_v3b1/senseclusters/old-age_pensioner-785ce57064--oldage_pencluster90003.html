old-age pensioner

noun
person receiving an old-age pension
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊld ˈeɪdʒ ˈpɛnʃənər / (AmE), / ˌəʊld ˈeɪdʒ ˈpɛnʃənə / (BrE)
a person who receives a state pension because they have reached the official retirement age; especially in British English.

Examples

An old-age pensioner benefits
As an old-age pensioner, she enjoys discounted travel on public transport.
Many old-age pensioners rely on the state pension as their main source of income.
The government increased the winter fuel allowance for every old-age pensioner this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pensioner - a person who receives a pension
  • synonym: retiree - a person who has retired from work
  • antonym: working-age - of age to be in employment, not retired
