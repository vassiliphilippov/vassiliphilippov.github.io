darndest

adjective
greatest effort
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑrndɪst / (AmE), / ˈdɑːndɪst / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is done with the greatest effort or to the highest degree possible

Examples

Do your darndest
He did his darndest to make everyone feel welcome at the party.
They tried their darndest to solve the difficult puzzle before the timer ran out.
She gave her darndest performance in the final round of the competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: least - the smallest amount or degree
  • synonym: maximum - the highest amount or degree possible
  • synonym: utmost - the greatest or most extreme degree
