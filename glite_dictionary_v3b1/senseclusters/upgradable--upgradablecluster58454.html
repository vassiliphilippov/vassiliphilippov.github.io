upgradable

adjective
can be improved
4.5
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈɡreɪdəbəl / (AmE), / ʌpˈɡreɪdəbəl / (BrE)
able to be improved or updated to a better or newer version.

Examples

An upgradable system
The software is easily upgradable to include new features as they are released.
Many modern smartphones are not upgradable in terms of storage or battery.
Before buying a computer, check if its components are upgradable for future needs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixed - not able to be changed or improved
  • synonym: modifiable - able to be changed or adjusted
