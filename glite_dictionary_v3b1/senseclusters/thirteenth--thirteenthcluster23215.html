thirteenth

noun
one of 13 parts
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜrˈtinθ / (AmE), / ˌθɜːˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
one of thirteen equal parts into which something is divided

Examples

A thirteenth share
Each student received one thirteenth of the prize money.
The cake was cut into thirteen slices, and I took the last thirteenth.
A thirteenth of the total votes was enough to influence the outcome.

  • degree lower: twelfth - one of twelve equal parts
