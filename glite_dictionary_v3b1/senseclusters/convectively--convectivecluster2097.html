convectively

adverb
by convection
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɛktɪvli / (AmE), / kənˈvɛktɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or is caused by convection, especially the movement of heat or fluids.

Examples

Heated convectively by air
The room was convectively heated as warm air rose from the radiator.
Clouds often form convectively when the ground warms the air above it.
The soup cooled convectively as air circulated over its surface.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convection - the transfer of heat by the movement of a fluid
