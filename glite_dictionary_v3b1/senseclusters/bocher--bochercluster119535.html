bocher

noun
Jewish religious student
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑxər / (AmE), / ˈbɒxə / (BrE)
a young man or boy, especially one who is a student in a Jewish religious school

Examples

A bocher studying
The bocher spent hours each day studying religious texts at the yeshiva.
As a bocher, he was respected for his dedication to learning.
Many families were proud when their son became a bocher in the community.
