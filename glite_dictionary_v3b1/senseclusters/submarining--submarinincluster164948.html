submarining

noun
operating a submarine
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbməˈriːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsʌbməˈriːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of operating, traveling in, or moving beneath the water's surface in a submarine or similar vessel

Examples

Submarining in deep water
The crew received special training in submarining before their first mission.
Advances in technology have made submarining safer and more efficient for naval forces.
He wrote a book about his experiences with submarining during the war.

  • antonym: surfacing - the act of coming up to the surface of water
