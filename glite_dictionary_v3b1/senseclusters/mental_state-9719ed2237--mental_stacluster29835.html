Examples

Synonyms

  • mental condition
    4.1 0.34/m
    mental condition refers to a person's overall emotional health often used in clinical contexts, while mental state is a general mental and emotional condition.
  • psychological state
    6.3 0.42/m
    psychological state describes a person's thoughts and feelings over time in scientific or everyday use, while mental state covers general psychological condition.
  • psychological condition
    5.6 0.17/m
    psychological condition often names a diagnosed disorder or pattern of mental functioning, while mental state is a more general psychological condition.
  • state of mind
    3.4 4.91/m
    state of mind means a person's mood or feeling at a particular moment, while mental state refers to a more stable condition over time.
