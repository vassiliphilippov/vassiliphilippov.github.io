ringingly

adverb
loud and clear
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋɪŋli / (BrE)
in a very clear, emphatic, or unmistakable way (like a ringing sound)

Examples

To speak ringingly clear
The judge declared the verdict ringingly, leaving no doubt in anyone's mind.
His name was ringingly announced over the loudspeakers at the ceremony.
The anthem was sung ringingly by the choir, filling the entire stadium.
The proposal was ringingly endorsed by the entire committee.
She ringingly declared her support for the new policy during the meeting.
His performance was ringingly praised by critics across the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decisively - in a way that settles an issue convincingly
  • synonym: emphatically - in a forceful or definite way
  • synonym: resoundingly - in a very loud or emphatic way
  • antonym: softly - in a quiet or gentle way
  • antonym: unclearly - in a way that is not clear or definite
