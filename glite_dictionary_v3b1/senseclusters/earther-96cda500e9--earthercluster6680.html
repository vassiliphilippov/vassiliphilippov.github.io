Earther

noun
Earth inhabitant
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθər / (AmE), / ˈɜːθə / (BrE)
a person who lives on or comes from Earth, especially as distinguished from people from other planets in science fiction

Examples

Alien meets an Earther
In the distant future, Earther colonies will exist on many planets across the galaxy.
The alien ambassador was curious about Earther customs and traditions.
Some Martians viewed every Earther with suspicion after the failed negotiations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Martian - a person or being from Mars
  • synonym: Terran - a person from Earth, especially in science fiction
