tender

adjective
easy to cut or chew (food)
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛn.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈten.də / (BrE)
(of food) easy to cut or chew; not tough.

Examples

Tender meat
Cook the chicken until the meat is tender and comes off the bone easily.
She prefers fish because it is light and tender when cooked properly.
The steak was so tender that it practically melted in my mouth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tough - (of food) difficult to cut or chew
