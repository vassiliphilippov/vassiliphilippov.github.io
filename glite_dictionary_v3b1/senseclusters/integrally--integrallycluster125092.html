integrally

adverb
in an essential or fundamental way
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntəɡrəli / (AmE), / ˈɪntɪɡrəli / (BrE)
In a way that is essential or fundamental to the whole; in an integral manner.

Examples

Is integrally connected
Art is integrally connected with culture and history.
Biology is integrally linked to the study of the environment.
Safety procedures are integrally important to the success of the operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fundamentally - in a basic or essential way
  • antonym: peripherally - in a way that is not essential or central
