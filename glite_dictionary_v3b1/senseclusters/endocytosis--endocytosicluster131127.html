endocytosis

noun
cellular intake process
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɛndoʊsaɪˈtoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊsaɪˈtəʊsɪs / (BrE)
the process by which a cell takes in substances by surrounding and engulfing them with its membrane

Related Terms

  • synonym: phagocytosis - the process by which a cell engulfs large particles or other cells
