endocytosis

noun
cellular intake process
7.4
Pronunciation: /en.doʊ.saɪ.ˈtoʊ.sɪs/ /; / /ɪn.doʊ.saɪ.ˈtoʊ.sɪs/ (AmE), /en.dəʊ.saɪ.ˈtəʊ.sɪs/ /; / /ɪn.dəʊ.saɪ.ˈtəʊ.sɪs/ (BrE)
the process by which a cell takes in substances by surrounding and engulfing them with its membrane
