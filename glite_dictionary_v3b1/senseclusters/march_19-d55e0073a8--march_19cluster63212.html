March 19

proper noun
Christian feast day of St. Joseph
Pronunciation: / mɑrtʃ nɪnˈtin / (AmE), / mɑːtʃ naɪnˈtiːn / (BrE)
a Christian feast day honoring St. Joseph, the husband of the Virgin Mary, observed annually on this date

Examples

March 19 feast day
Churches around the world hold processions and festivals on March 19 each year.
In Italy, March 19 is also celebrated as Father's Day, honoring St. Joseph.
Many Catholics attend special services on March 19 to celebrate the Feast of St. Joseph.
