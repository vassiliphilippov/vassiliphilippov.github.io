Con-Dem

noun
UK Conservative–Liberal Democrat coalition
8.5
Pronunciation: / kɑnˈdɛm / (AmE), / kɒnˈdɛm / (BrE)
a coalition made up of the Conservative and Liberal Democrat parties in the UK, especially referring to their joint government from 2010 to 2015

Examples

The Con-Dem government
After the election, the Con-Dem government introduced several controversial policies.
Many people criticized the Con-Dem coalition's approach to public spending.
Some voters felt let down by the promises made by the Con-Dem alliance.
