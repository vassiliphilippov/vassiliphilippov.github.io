decking

noun
outdoor platform
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɛkɪŋ / (BrE)
a flat outdoor surface or the boards used to make it, usually for walking or sitting on

Examples

Garden decking
They installed new decking in their backyard for summer parties.
The wooden decking around the pool needs to be cleaned every spring.
We enjoyed breakfast on the decking overlooking the garden.
The carpenter installed new decking to replace the old, weathered boards in the backyard.
Composite decking is popular because it requires less maintenance than traditional wood.
They chose a light-colored decking to keep the patio cool during summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flooring - material used to cover the floor of a room or structure
  • synonym: patio - a paved outdoor area adjoining a house
  • synonym: terrace - a level paved area next to a building
