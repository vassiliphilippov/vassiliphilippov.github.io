rawly

adverb
in an unrefined way
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔli / (AmE), / ˈrɔːli / (BrE)
in a raw, unrefined, or unfiltered manner; without preparation or covering

Examples

Speak rawly
He described his childhood rawly, sharing painful memories without hesitation.
The artist expressed her emotions rawly through her unpolished sketches.
During the interview, she spoke rawly about her struggles with anxiety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bluntly - in a direct and straightforward manner
  • synonym: openly - in a way that is honest and not secretive
  • antonym: smoothly - in a refined or polished manner
