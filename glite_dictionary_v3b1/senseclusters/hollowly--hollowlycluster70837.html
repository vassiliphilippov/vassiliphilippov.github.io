hollowly

adverb
in an empty or insincere manner
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑː.loʊ.li / (AmE), / ˈhɒl.əʊ.li / (BrE)
In a manner that sounds or feels empty, without warmth or sincerity.

Examples

To laugh hollowly
He laughed hollowly, betraying his lack of real joy.
Her voice echoed hollowly through the deserted corridor.
The apology sounded hollowly unconvincing to everyone present.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sincerely - in a genuine or heartfelt manner
