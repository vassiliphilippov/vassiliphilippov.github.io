dipterocarp

noun
tropical Asian tree of Dipterocarpaceae family
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪptəroʊˌkɑrp / (AmE), / dɪpˈtɛrəkɑːp / (BrE)
A tree belonging to the family Dipterocarpaceae, found mainly in tropical Asia and valued for its timber.

Examples

A dipterocarp tree
Scientists discovered a rare dipterocarp sapling in the protected reserve.
Selective logging has greatly reduced the number of wild dipterocarps in the region.
The rainforests of Borneo are dominated by towering dipterocarp species.
