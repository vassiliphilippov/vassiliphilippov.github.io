curate

noun
assistant clergyman in the Anglican Church
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkjʊr.eɪt / (AmE), / ˈkjʊər.ət / (BrE)
a member of the clergy, especially in the Anglican Church, who assists a vicar or rector in the performance of religious duties

Examples

Anglican curate
As a curate, she assisted the vicar with pastoral duties in the parish.
The curate organized a charity event to support local families in need.
The new curate was introduced to the congregation during Sunday service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rector - a clergyman in charge of a parish in the Anglican Church
  • synonym: vicar - a representative or deputy of a bishop or parish priest
