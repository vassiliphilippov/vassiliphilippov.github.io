quality

noun
distinctive character of a musical sound
1.0 4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkwɑː.lə.tiː/ /; / /ˈkwɑː.lɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈkwɒ.lɪ.tɪ/ /; / /ˈkwɒ.lə.tɪ/ (BrE)
(music) The characteristic that distinguishes a particular sound, voice, or musical note from others of the same pitch and loudness; timbre.

Examples

Quality of sound
Different instruments have a distinct quality even when playing the same note.
The quality of her voice made her performance unforgettable.
The bell's muffled quality added a somber mood to the scene.
