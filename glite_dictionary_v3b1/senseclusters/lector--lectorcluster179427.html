lector

noun
university public lecturer
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛktər / (AmE), / ˈlɛktə / (BrE)
a person who gives public lectures at a university, especially in certain European countries; a public lecturer.

Examples

University lector
As a lector, he regularly delivers lectures to large groups of students.
She was appointed lector in French literature at the university.
The faculty invited a visiting lector to speak about modern European history.
