junk sport

noun
trivial or low-quality sport/activity
2.5 6.7
Pronunciation: /dʒʌŋk spɔːrt/ (AmE), /dʒʌŋk spɔːt/ (BrE)
A sport or activity regarded as trivial, lacking seriousness, or considered to be of low quality or value.

Examples

Junk sport on TV

The rise of televised wrestling led some traditionalists to complain about the popularity of junk sports.
