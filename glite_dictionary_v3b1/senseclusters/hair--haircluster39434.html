hair

noun
fine filament on plants, like peach fuzz
Pronunciation: / hɛr / (AmE), / heə / (BrE)
a fine, filamentous outgrowth on the surface of a plant, resembling human or animal hair, as seen on the skin of some fruits or leaves

Examples

Plant hair
Botanists often examine the hair on stems to help identify plant species.
Many leaves have tiny hairs that help protect them from insects.
The peach's skin is covered in fine hair that gives it a fuzzy texture.
