varnish tree

noun
large Asian tree; source of varnish oil
2.7 8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈvɑːr.nɪʃ triː/ /; / /ˈvɑːr.nɪʃ tʃriː/ (AmE), /ˈvɑː.nɪʃ triː/ /; / /ˈvɑː.nɪʃ tʃriː/ (BrE)
