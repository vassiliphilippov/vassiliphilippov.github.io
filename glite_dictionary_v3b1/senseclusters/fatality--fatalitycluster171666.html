fatality

noun
quality of causing death
5.4
Pronunciation: / fəˈtæləti / (AmE), / fəˈtælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or power of causing death or fatal outcomes

Examples

Fatality of the poison

The drug's hidden fatality was only discovered after several patients died unexpectedly.
