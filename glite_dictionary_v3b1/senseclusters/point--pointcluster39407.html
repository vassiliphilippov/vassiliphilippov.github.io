point

verb
indicate game (hunting dog sense)
4.0
Pronunciation: / pɔɪnt / (AmE), / pɔɪnt / (BrE)
(of a hunting dog) to indicate the presence of game by standing rigidly and directing its muzzle toward it.

Examples

Dog points at game
As soon as the hound points, be ready to move in quietly.
The setter was trained to point quail in the open fields.
When the dog pointed, the hunters knew the pheasant was close.
