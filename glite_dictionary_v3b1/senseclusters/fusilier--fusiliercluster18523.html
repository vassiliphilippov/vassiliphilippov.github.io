fusilier

noun
a historical British infantry soldier
5.2 8.5
Pronunciation: /fjuː.zɪ.ˈlɪr/ /; / /fjuː.zə.ˈlɪr/ (AmE), /fjuː.zɪ.ˈlɪə/ (BrE)
(historically) a British infantry soldier armed with a light flintlock musket, especially one belonging to a regiment originally equipped in this way.
