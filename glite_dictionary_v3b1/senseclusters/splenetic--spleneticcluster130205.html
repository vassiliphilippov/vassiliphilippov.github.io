splenetic

adjective
very irritable; bad-tempered
Pronunciation: / spləˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / splɪˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
easily angered or irritable; bad-tempered; or (medical) relating to the spleen

Examples

A splenetic person
After a long, stressful day, he became increasingly splenetic at the slightest provocation.
His splenetic remarks alienated many of his colleagues at work.
The critic was known for her splenetic reviews of mediocre plays.
A splenetic crisis can occur if the spleen is suddenly injured.
She was diagnosed with a splenetic disorder after persistent abdominal pain.
The doctor explained that splenetic tissue showed signs of inflammation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: even-tempered - calm and not easily angered
  • synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or angered
