ineducable

adjective
cannot be taught
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˈɛdʒəkəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnˈɛdjuːkəbəl / (BrE)
unable to be educated or taught

Examples

An ineducable child
The school once considered him ineducable, but he proved them wrong with his determination.
Some children labeled as ineducable can thrive with the right support and resources.
Decades ago, students with disabilities were often unfairly called ineducable and denied an education.

Related Terms

  • antonym: teachable - able to be taught or educated
  • synonym: untrainable - impossible to train or teach
