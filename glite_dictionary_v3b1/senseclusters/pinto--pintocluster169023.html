pinto

noun
a horse with a white and colored patchy coat
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪn.toʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɪn.təʊ / (BrE)
a horse or pony with a coat marked by large patches of white and another color

Examples

A pinto horse
She always dreamed of riding a pinto across the open plains.
The ranch was home to a beautiful pinto with striking brown and white patches.
Their pinto stood out in the herd because of its unique coat pattern.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paint - an American horse breed with a similar patchy coat
