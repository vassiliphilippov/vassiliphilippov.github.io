fishiness

noun
suspicious quality
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃinəs / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of smelling or tasting like fish, or the quality of being suspicious or questionable

Examples

The fishiness of smell
There was a definite fishiness to his explanation that made everyone doubt his story.
The fishiness of the kitchen lingered long after the seafood dinner was over.
She noticed the fishiness in the deal and decided not to sign the contract.

Related Terms

  • synonym: odor - a particular smell, especially an unpleasant one
  • synonym: suspiciousness - the state of arousing doubt or mistrust
  • antonym: trustworthiness - the quality of being reliable and deserving of trust
