ferryboat

noun
boat for transporting people or vehicles across water
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛriˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈfɛrɪbəʊt / (BrE)
a boat used to carry people or vehicles across a body of water, usually at regular intervals on a set route

Examples

The ferryboat departs
The ferryboat departed from the dock every hour to take commuters to the city.
Tourists enjoyed the scenic views from the upper deck of the ferryboat as it sailed to the island.
We drove our car onto the ferryboat to cross the wide river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ferry - a boat or ship for conveying passengers and goods, especially over a relatively short distance and as a regular service
