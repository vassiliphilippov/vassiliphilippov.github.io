cob

noun
nut of a hazel tree
Pronunciation: / kɑb / (AmE), / kɒb / (BrE)
the edible nut of a hazel tree, especially in reference to certain varieties of Corylus

Examples

A hazel cob nut

In some regions, people roast cobs over an open fire as a seasonal treat.
