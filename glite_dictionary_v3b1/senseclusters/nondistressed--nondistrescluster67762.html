nondistressed

adjective
not damaged or worn
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndɪˈstrɛst / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˈstrɛst / (BrE)
not damaged, worn, or artificially aged; in normal or undamaged physical condition

Examples

Nondistressed jeans
He preferred nondistressed jeans because they looked cleaner and more professional for work.
The furniture in the showroom was all nondistressed, with smooth surfaces and no signs of wear.
Collectors often seek nondistressed items to ensure the highest possible value.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distressed - damaged, worn, or artificially aged in appearance
  • synonym: pristine - in perfect, original condition; unspoiled
