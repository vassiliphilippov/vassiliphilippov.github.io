surge

verb
rise or increase suddenly
4.8
Pronunciation: / sɝdʒ / (AmE), / sɜːdʒ / (BrE)
to rise or increase suddenly and powerfully

Examples

Prices surge up
Attendance at the concert surged once the headline act was revealed.
Energy prices began to surge after the announcement of new regulations.
The crowd surged forward as the gates opened.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plunge - to fall or drop suddenly and dramatically
  • synonym: soar - to rise quickly and smoothly to a high level
