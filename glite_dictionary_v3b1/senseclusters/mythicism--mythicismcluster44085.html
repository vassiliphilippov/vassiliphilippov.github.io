mythicism

noun
mythical quality
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪθɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
the quality or state of being mythical or having the characteristics of myth or legend

Examples

A story's mythicism
The novel is filled with mythicism, blending ancient legends with modern storytelling.
Artists often use mythicism to create a sense of wonder and mystery in their work.
The landscape's beauty gave the entire region an air of mythicism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mythicalness - the state of being mythical
  • antonym: realism - the quality of representing things as they are
