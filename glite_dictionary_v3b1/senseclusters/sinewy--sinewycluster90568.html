sinewy

adjective
full of sinews; tough meat
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnju.i / (AmE), / ˈsɪn.ju.i / (BrE)
(of meat) containing many sinews; tough and difficult to chew.

Examples

Sinewy meat
After hours of simmering, the once sinewy beef became soft and flavorful.
She pushed aside the sinewy pieces of meat on her plate, preferring the tender parts.
The sinewy steak was difficult to cut and even harder to chew.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tough - difficult to cut or chew (especially of meat)
