icefish

noun
small translucent fish
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈaɪsˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a small, translucent edible fish of the family Salangidae found in East Asian coastal and freshwater waters

Examples

Edible icefish
Fishermen caught a large net full of icefish early in the morning.
The chef prepared a delicate soup using fresh icefish from the local market.
During the festival, people enjoy dishes made with icefish as a seasonal treat.
