Afro

noun
rounded, curly hairstyle
6.8 4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈæ.froʊ/ /; / /ˈæ.frəʊ/ (AmE), /ˈæ.frəʊ/ (BrE)
A hairstyle in which the hair is naturally curly or tightly coiled and is styled outward from the scalp in a rounded shape.

Examples

A Afro hairstyle
After months of growing it out, his Afro looked big and beautiful.
She wore her hair in a perfectly shaped Afro at the party last night.
The Afro became a symbol of pride and cultural identity during the 1970s.
