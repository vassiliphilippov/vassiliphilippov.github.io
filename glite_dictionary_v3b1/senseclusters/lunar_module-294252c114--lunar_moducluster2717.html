lunar module

noun
spacecraft for moon landing and return
Pronunciation: / ˈluːnɚ ˈmɑːdʒuːl / (AmE), / ˈluːnə ˈmɒdjuːl / (BrE)
a spacecraft designed to transport astronauts from a command module to the surface of the moon and back

Examples

The lunar module landing
Astronauts transferred from the command module to the lunar module before beginning their descent to the moon.
The Apollo 11 lunar module landed safely on the Sea of Tranquility in 1969.
The damaged lunar module of Apollo 13 was used as a 'lifeboat' to keep the crew alive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lander - a spacecraft designed to land on the surface of a planet or moon
