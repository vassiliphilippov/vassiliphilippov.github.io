touchscreen

noun
interactive display you touch
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌtʃˌskrin / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃˌskriːn / (BrE)
A display device that allows the user to interact with a computer or other electronic device by touching areas on the screen.

Examples

A touchscreen panel
Many modern cars now feature large touchscreen panels for controlling navigation and music.
My touchscreen stopped working after I dropped the tablet on the floor.
The smartphone's touchscreen responded quickly to my gestures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: display - an electronic device that visually presents information
