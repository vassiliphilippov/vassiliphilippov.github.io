perfidy

noun
deliberate betrayal or treachery
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrfədi /; / ˈpɜrfɪdi / (AmE), / ˈpɜːfɪdi / (BrE)
the act of betraying someone's trust; treachery or deceitfulness
