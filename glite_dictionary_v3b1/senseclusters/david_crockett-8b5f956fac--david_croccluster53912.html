David Crockett

proper noun
American frontiersman and politician (1786–1836)
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪvɪd ˈkrɑːkɪt / (AmE), / ˈdeɪvɪd ˈkrɒkɪt / (BrE)
(1786–1836) An American frontiersman, soldier, and politician from Tennessee, known for his exploits as a folk hero and for dying at the siege of the Alamo.

Examples

David Crockett folk hero
David Crockett became a legendary figure in American folklore for his achievements on the frontier.
Many history books recount the bravery of David Crockett during the Battle of the Alamo.
The life of David Crockett has inspired numerous stories, songs, and movies in American culture.
