uncork

verb
remove the cork from a bottle
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɔrk / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɔːk / (BrE)
to remove the cork from a bottle, especially a wine bottle

Examples

To uncork a bottle
He decided to uncork a bottle of champagne to celebrate the occasion.
She uncorked the bottle and poured everyone a glass.
The waiter expertly uncorked the vintage red wine at our table.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cork - to close with a cork; to stop up a bottle with a cork
