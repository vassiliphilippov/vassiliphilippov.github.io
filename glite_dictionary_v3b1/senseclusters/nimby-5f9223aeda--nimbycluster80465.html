NIMBY

noun
person objecting to local developments

A person who objects to the presence of something perceived as undesirable (such as infrastructure or housing) in their own neighborhood, while not objecting to it being located elsewhere.

Examples

A NIMBY neighbor
Accusations of being a NIMBY often arise during debates over new housing developments.
As a classic NIMBY, she opposed the construction of a homeless shelter near her house but supported it elsewhere.
The project was delayed due to protests led by local NIMBYs.
