mini

adjective
very small size
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪni / (AmE), / ˈmɪni / (BrE)
very small or compact in size compared to others of the same type

Examples

Mini fridge
She bought a mini fridge for her dorm room because space was limited.
The bakery sells mini cupcakes that are perfect for parties.
He carried a mini umbrella in his bag in case it rained.
