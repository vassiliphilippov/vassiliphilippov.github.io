rust

noun
plant disease causing brown leaf spots
Pronunciation: / rʌst / (AmE), / rʌst / (BrE)
a plant disease that causes reddish-brown spots or streaks on leaves and stems, caused by fungi

Examples

Rust on leaves
Certain varieties of beans are more resistant to rust than others.
Farmers regularly inspect their fields for signs of rust on the leaves.
The wheat crop was devastated by a severe outbreak of rust this year.
Farmers use resistant plant varieties to minimize rust infections.
Scientists study different types of rust to improve agricultural yields.
The wheat crop was severely affected by rust this season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blight - a plant disease resulting in withering and death
  • synonym: mildew - a plant disease caused by fungi resulting in a whitish coating on the leaves
  • synonym: smut - a fungal disease affecting plants, producing black powdery spores
