Ainu

proper noun
indigenous people of Japan
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪnu / (AmE), / ˈaɪnuː / (BrE)
an indigenous people of northern Japan, especially Hokkaido, and their language

Examples

The Ainu people
The Ainu have preserved many unique traditions and customs in Hokkaido.
Researchers are working to document the Ainu language before it disappears.
The museum features artifacts and stories from the Ainu people.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Japanese - the main ethnic group of Japan
