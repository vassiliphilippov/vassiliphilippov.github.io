unbitten

adjective
not bitten
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɪtən / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɪtən / (BrE)
not having been bitten or marked by a bite

Examples

The bone unbitten
The dog left the bone completely unbitten in the yard.
She was relieved to find her sandwich still unbitten after the meeting.
The fruit remained unbitten on the kitchen counter all day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bitten - having been cut or marked by the teeth
  • synonym: untouched - not affected or changed; not handled or used
