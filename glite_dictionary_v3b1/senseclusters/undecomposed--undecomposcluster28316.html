undecomposed

adjective
not decayed or broken down
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndikoʊmˈpoʊzd / (AmE), / ˌʌndiːkəmˈpəʊzd / (BrE)
not having decayed or broken down; remaining in its original state.

Examples

An undecomposed body
In the cold climate, animal remains can stay undecomposed for many years.
Researchers discovered undecomposed organic matter buried deep in the bog.
The leaves remained undecomposed even after several months on the forest floor.
