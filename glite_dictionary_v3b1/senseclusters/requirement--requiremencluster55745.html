requirement

noun
thing that is needed or demanded
1.0 1.7
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈkwaɪr.mənt/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈkwaɪ.ə.mənt/ (BrE)
something that is needed or demanded for a particular purpose, situation, or as a condition
