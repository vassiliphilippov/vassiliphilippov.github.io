centrifugation

noun
separation process using a centrifuge
1.0 6.1
Pronunciation: /sen.trɪ.fjuː.ˈɡeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /sen.trə.fjuː.ˈɡeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /sen.trə.fjuː.ˈɡeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /sen.trɪ.fjuː.ˈɡeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of separating substances of different densities by spinning them in a centrifuge

Examples

Centrifugation process

Effective centrifugation is essential in biochemistry labs to isolate cellular components.
