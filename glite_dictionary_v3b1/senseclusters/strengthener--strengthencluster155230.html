strengthener

noun
a device or material that adds strength
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɛŋθənər / (AmE), / ˈstrɛŋθənə / (BrE)
a device or material used to provide additional strength or support to something

Examples

A strengthener device
Rubber pads serve as a strengthener for the old machine's vibrating parts.

The new metal beam acted as a strengthener in the building's frame.
