high five

noun
celebratory hand gesture
Pronunciation: / haɪ faɪv / (AmE), / haɪ faɪv / (BrE)
a gesture of celebration or greeting in which two people slap their raised open hands together

Examples

A high five celebration
After winning the match, the players gave each other a high five to celebrate.
She reached out for a high five when her friend solved the puzzle.
The coach offered a high five to every team member after the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fist bump - a gesture in which two people lightly bump their fists together
  • synonym: handshake - a greeting or agreement gesture involving grasping hands
