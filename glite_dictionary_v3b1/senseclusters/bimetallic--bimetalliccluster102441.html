bimetallic

adjective
made of two different metals or alloys
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.məˈtæl.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.məˈtæl.ɪk / (BrE)
Consisting of or formed from two different metals or alloys, especially sheets bonded together.

Examples

Bimetallic strip
A bimetallic coin is harder to counterfeit than coins made from a single metal.
Many thermostats use a bimetallic strip to control temperature changes.
The engineer explained how the bimetallic sheet responded to heat by bending.
