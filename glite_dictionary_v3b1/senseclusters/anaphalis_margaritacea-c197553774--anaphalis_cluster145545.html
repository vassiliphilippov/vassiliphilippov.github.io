Anaphalis margaritacea

noun
North American plant with white flower heads
Pronunciation: / əˌnæf.əˈlaɪs ˌmɑr.ɡə.rɪˈteɪ.si.ə / (AmE), / əˌnaf.əˈlaɪs ˌmɑː.ɡər.ɪˈteɪ.si.ə / (BrE)
A North American plant of the daisy family, known as pearly everlasting, with woolly leaves and small white flower heads with pearly bracts.
