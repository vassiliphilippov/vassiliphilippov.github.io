myoelectric

adjective
muscle electrical signals
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪoʊɪˈlɛktrɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪəʊɪˈlɛktrɪk / (BrE)
relating to electrical signals generated by muscle activity

Examples

Myoelectric signals
The myoelectric signals from his arm muscles control the movement of the prosthetic hand.
Researchers studied the myoelectric activity in athletes during intense exercise.
A myoelectric device can help restore some movement for people with limb loss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electromyographic - relating to the recording of electrical activity in muscles
