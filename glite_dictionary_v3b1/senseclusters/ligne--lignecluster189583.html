ligne

noun
unit of length for button diameter
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / liːn / (AmE), / liːn / (BrE)
a unit of linear measurement equal to 1/40 of an inch, used especially to measure the diameter of buttons and similar small items

Examples

A ligne size
Manufacturers commonly refer to the size of shirt buttons in lignes rather than millimeters.
The button was measured at 20 lignes in diameter, making it suitable for coats.
Vintage watches often describe their case size using the term ligne.
