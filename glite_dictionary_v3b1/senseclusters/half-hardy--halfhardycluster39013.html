half-hardy

adjective
plant: needs protection from frost
8.5 6.3
Pronunciation: /hæf.ˈhɑːr.dɪ/ /; / /hæf ˈhɑːr.dɪ/ (AmE), /hɑːf.ˈhɑː.dɪ/ /; / /hɑːf ˈhɑː.dɪ/ (BrE)
(of plants) able to survive outdoors except in severe frost, requiring some protection from freezing temperatures.

Examples

Half-hardy plants
Gardeners often place half-hardy plants in greenhouses during winter.
Many half-hardy annuals should be started indoors before the last frost.
Petunias are considered half-hardy and can be damaged by late spring frosts.

Related Terms
