scraggle

noun
tangled mass
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈskræ.gəl/ (AmE), /ˈskræ.gəl/ (BrE)
a rough, tangled, or uneven mass of things such as hair, stems, or vegetation

Examples

A scraggle of weeds
After months of neglect, the backyard was just a scraggle of weeds and overgrown grass.
She tried to comb through the scraggle of her hair after a windy day at the beach.
A scraggle of branches blocked the narrow path through the forest.

  • antonym: order - a state in which everything is arranged neatly
  • synonym: tangle - a confused or twisted mass of something
