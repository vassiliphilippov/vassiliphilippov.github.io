bloodthirst

noun
desire for violence
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdˌθɜrst / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌθɜːst / (BrE)
a strong desire for violence, especially to kill or harm others

Examples

Strong bloodthirst
The dictator's bloodthirst was feared by everyone in the country.
Stories of ancient warriors often highlight their bloodthirst in battle.
The movie villain's bloodthirst made him a truly terrifying character.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruelty - a willingness to cause pain or suffering
  • antonym: mercifulness - the quality of showing compassion or forgiveness
