Henry IV

proper noun
King of France (1589–1610), Bourbon founder
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛnri ðə fɔrθ / (AmE), / ˈhɛnri ðə fɔːθ / (BrE)
King of France from 1589 to 1610, founder of the Bourbon dynasty, notable for granting religious freedom to Protestants.

Examples

Henry IV of France
