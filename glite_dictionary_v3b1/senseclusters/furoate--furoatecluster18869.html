furoate

noun
salt of furoic acid
Pronunciation: / ˈfjʊroʊˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈfjʊərəʊˌeɪt / (BrE)
a salt or ester formed from furoic acid, commonly used in the names of certain medications

Examples

Furoate salt
The doctor prescribed a nasal spray containing furoate for allergies.
Some topical creams list furoate as an active ingredient to reduce inflammation.
Patients with asthma may use inhalers that include furoate compounds for better symptom control.

Related Terms
