foodservice

noun
industry serving food
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːdˌsɜːrvɪs / (AmE), / ˈfuːdˌsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
the industry or business of preparing and serving food to people, especially in restaurants, cafeterias, or catering

Examples

Foodservice industry
The university hired a new foodservice company to provide meals for students.
Many hospitals rely on foodservice providers to ensure patients receive nutritious meals.
Working in foodservice can be fast-paced, especially during busy lunch hours.
She has worked in foodservice for over a decade.
Many students find part-time jobs in foodservice while attending college.
The foodservice industry has adapted quickly to new health and safety regulations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catering - the business of providing food and drink for events or organizations
  • synonym: hospitality - the industry of providing services such as food, drink, and accommodation
  • synonym: restaurant - a place where people pay to sit and eat meals
