sinus coronarius

noun
venous channel in the heart
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪnəs kɔːˈroʊnəriəs / (AmE), / ˌsaɪnəs kɒˈrəʊnəriəs / (BrE)
a short blood vessel in the heart that collects blood from most of the heart's veins and empties into the right atrium

Examples

The sinus coronarius vein
Blockage of the sinus coronarius can lead to serious cardiac complications.
During the procedure, the surgeon carefully exposed the sinus coronarius.
The sinus coronarius collects blood from the cardiac veins before it enters the right atrium.
