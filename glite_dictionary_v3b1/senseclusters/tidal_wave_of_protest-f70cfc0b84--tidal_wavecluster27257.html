tidal wave of protest

noun
large surge of protests
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪ.dl weɪv əv proʊˈtɛst / (AmE), / ˈtaɪ.dl weɪv əv prəˈtɛst / (BrE)
A sudden, large, and overwhelming outpouring of protests or expressions of dissent.

Examples

A tidal wave of protest rises
A tidal wave of protest forced the company to reverse its decision.
The announcement triggered a tidal wave of protest across the nation.
There was a tidal wave of protest after the new policy was introduced.
