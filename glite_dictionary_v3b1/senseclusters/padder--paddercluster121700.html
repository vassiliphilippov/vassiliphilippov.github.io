padder

noun
one who adds extra material
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpædər / (AmE), / ˈpædə / (BrE)
a person or thing that adds unnecessary material, especially to make something longer or fuller

Examples

Essay padder
The teacher warned students not to become a padder by repeating the same ideas in their essays.
As an editor, she could easily spot a padder who tried to meet the word count with filler.
A good report is clear and direct, not the work of a padder.
