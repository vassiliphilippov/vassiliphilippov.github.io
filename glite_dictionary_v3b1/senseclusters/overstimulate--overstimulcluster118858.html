overstimulate

verb
to excite too much
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈstɪmjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈstɪmjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to give too much stimulation to someone or something, causing negative effects such as overexcitement, stress, or harm

Examples

Too much sugar can overstimulate children and make it hard for them to concentrate.
The constant notifications from her phone began to overstimulate her, making her feel anxious.
Bright colors and loud music may overstimulate some people at parties.
