clubhouse

noun
building used by a club
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌbˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈklʌbhaʊs / (BrE)
a building used by a club or organization for meetings and social activities

Examples

The clubhouse building
Members of the sailing club often gather at the clubhouse after a regatta.
Renovations to the old clubhouse included a new lounge area and updated kitchen.
The golf course clubhouse offers locker rooms, a restaurant, and a pro shop for players.
