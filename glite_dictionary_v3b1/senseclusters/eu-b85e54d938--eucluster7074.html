EU

noun
tech/market label for the Europe region
3.6 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / iÀê juÀê / (AmE), / iÀê juÀê / (BrE)
(in technology, online services, and commerce) a label for the Europe content/market or server region used to route users, price goods, or restrict content; may refer to the geographic region broadly rather than the political European Union.

Examples

EU server region
The game's European servers will open in the EU region next Tuesday at noon.
Change your account country to view prices in the EU store before you check out.
For compliance we keep backups on servers physically located inside the EU, not elsewhere.

Synonyms

  • Europe
    1.0 107/m
    Europe refers to the whole continent and its countries in politics and culture, while EU is a tech market label for routing, pricing, or content rules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Europe - the continent or geographic region of Europe; often the broader area intended by this regional label
