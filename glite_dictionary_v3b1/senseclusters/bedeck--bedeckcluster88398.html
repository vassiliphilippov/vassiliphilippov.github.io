bedeck

verb
decorate or adorn elaborately
8.4
Pronunciation: / bəˈdɛk / (AmE), / bɪˈdɛk / (BrE)
to decorate or adorn something, especially in a showy or elaborate way

Examples

To bedeck with flowers
During the holiday season, the streets are often bedecked with colorful lights and ornaments.
She bedecked herself with sparkling jewelry for the festival.
The grand ballroom was bedecked with countless flowers for the wedding.
