garmentless

adjective
not wearing any clothing
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrməntləs / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːməntləs / (BrE)
not wearing any clothing; lacking garments.

Examples

Garmentless and bare
After falling into the river, he emerged garmentless and embarrassed.
The model posed garmentless for the life drawing class, much to the students' fascination.
The story described a garmentless statue standing in the city square.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clothed - wearing clothes; covered with clothing
  • synonym: naked - without any clothes, exposed
