Istrian

noun
person from Istria
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪstriən / (AmE), / ˈɪstriən / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from Istria, a peninsula in the northern Adriatic Sea

Examples

An Istrian person

Many Istrians speak both Croatian and Italian due to the region's history.
An Istrian festival celebrates the unique culture and cuisine of the peninsula.
