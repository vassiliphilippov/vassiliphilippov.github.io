sarcomere

noun
unit of muscle fiber structure
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑr.kəˌmɪɹ / (AmE), / ˈsɑː.kəˌmɪə / (BrE)
the smallest structural and functional unit of a muscle fiber, consisting of a segment within a myofibril

Examples

Muscle sarcomere unit
Damage to the sarcomere can impair the muscle's ability to function efficiently.
Researchers studied how the structure of each sarcomere affected overall muscle strength.
The sarcomere shortens during muscle contraction, leading to movement.
