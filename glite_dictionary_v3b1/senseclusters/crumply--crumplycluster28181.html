Synonyms

    crumpled describes material pressed into clear folds and creases, while crumply means wrinkled or slightly crumpled in texture.
  • wrinkled
    1.5 3.08/m
    wrinkled shows small lines or folds from age or creasing, while crumply suggests a more broadly crumpled or uneven texture.
  • crinkled
    3.7 0.03/m
    crinkled has small, wavy folds across a surface, while crumply means generally wrinkled or crumpled with a rough look.
  • crinkly
    4.0 0.29/m
    crinkly looks covered in many small twists or waves, while crumply implies larger creases or a crumpled texture overall.
  • wrinkly
    2.4 0.34/m
    wrinkly often describes skin or soft fabric with many fine lines, while crumply refers to a crumpled, creased surface more generally.
  • crepey
    7.5 0.01/m
    crepey means thin, finely wrinkled like crepe fabric, while crumply means rougher, crumpled or more strongly creased texture.
