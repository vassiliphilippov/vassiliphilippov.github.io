continuer

noun
one who continues
6.7
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɪn.ju.ər / (AmE), / kənˈtɪn.ju.ə / (BrE)
a person or thing that continues something, or in mathematics and logic, an entity that extends a process or function.

Examples

The continuer of business
As the continuer of the family business, she introduced many new ideas.
In mathematics, a continuer can extend a function beyond its original domain.
He saw himself as a continuer of the tradition started by his mentor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: originator - a person who creates or starts something
  • synonym: successor - a person or thing that follows and continues the work of another
