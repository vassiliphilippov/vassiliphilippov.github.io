nelson

noun
type of wrestling hold
9.6 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈnel.sən/ (AmE), /ˈnel.sən/ (BrE)
A wrestling hold in which one arm is passed under the opponent's arm from behind and pressure is applied to the back of the neck.

Examples

Nelson hold
After escaping the nelson, she quickly reversed the move.
He practiced the nelson in preparation for the big match.
The wrestler applied a nelson to pin his opponent to the mat.
