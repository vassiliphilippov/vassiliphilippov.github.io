giant schnauzer

noun
large breed of schnauzer dog
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈʃnaʊzər / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈʃnaʊzə / (BrE)
a large, robust breed of schnauzer originally bred for working purposes, known for its thick, wiry coat and distinctive beard and eyebrows.

Examples

A giant schnauzer dog
At the dog show, her giant schnauzer impressed the judges with its obedience.
The giant schnauzer is known for its intelligence and protective nature.
They chose a giant schnauzer because of its reputation as a loyal family companion.
