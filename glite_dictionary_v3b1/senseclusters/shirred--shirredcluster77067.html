shirred

adjective
gathered with stitching
Pronunciation: / ʃɜrd / (AmE), / ʃɜːd / (BrE)
gathered or sewn with parallel rows of stitches to create a decorative, elastic effect

Examples

Shirred fabric dress
The blouse featured a shirred neckline that added a touch of elegance.
She admired the shirred sleeves on the vintage dress in the shop window.
The designer used a shirred fabric to create a comfortable and stylish waistband.
