culturalist

noun
culture-focused person
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltʃərəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies, values, or emphasizes the importance of culture and its influence on society

Examples

A culturalist scholar
The culturalist gave a lecture on how art reflects social values.
As a culturalist, she believes that language shapes the way we think.
Many culturalists argue that customs are essential to community identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropologist - a person who studies human societies and cultures
  • synonym: traditionalist - a person who supports or upholds tradition
