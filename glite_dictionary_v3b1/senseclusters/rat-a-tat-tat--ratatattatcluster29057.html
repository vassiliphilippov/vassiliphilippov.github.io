rat-a-tat-tat

noun
series of rapid sharp taps
Pronunciation: / ˌræt ə ˌtæt ˈtæt / (AmE), / ˌræt ə ˌtæt ˈtæt / (BrE)
a series of short, sharp tapping sounds, like those made by knocking on a door or striking a drum repeatedly

Examples

A rat-a-tat-tat sound
A rat-a-tat-tat on the roof signaled that the hailstorm had begun.
The sudden rat-a-tat-tat on the door made everyone jump.
You could hear the rat-a-tat-tat of machine guns in the distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tap - a light hit or knock
