multisport

noun
event with multiple sports
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌspɔrt / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌspɔːt / (BrE)
a competition or event in which participants take part in two or more different sports, often one after another

Examples

Multisport event
The school hosted a multisport event featuring basketball, swimming, and tennis competitions.
Training for a multisport requires athletes to develop skills in several different disciplines.
He enjoys participating in multisport races that challenge both his endurance and versatility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decathlon - an athletic contest with ten different track and field events
  • antonym: single-sport - an event involving only one sport
  • synonym: triathlon - a race combining swimming, cycling, and running
