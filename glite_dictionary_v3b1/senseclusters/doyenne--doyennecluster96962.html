doyenne

noun
senior or respected woman in a group
9.4
Pronunciation: / dɔɪˈɛn / (AmE), / dwɑːˈjɛn / (BrE)
the most respected or prominent woman in a particular field or group, often due to her age or experience

Examples

The doyenne of a group
As the doyenne of their book club, her opinion carried extra weight.
She is considered the doyenne of American fashion journalism.
The doyenne of the science faculty was honored at the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doyen - a man who is the senior or most respected member of a group
