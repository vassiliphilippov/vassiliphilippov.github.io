paper chain

noun
decorative chain made of paper loops
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpər tʃeɪn / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə tʃeɪn / (BrE)
A decorative chain made from loops of colored paper linked together, often used to decorate a room for celebrations.

Examples

A paper chain decoration
She taught her younger brother how to create a paper chain using strips of construction paper.
The children spent the afternoon making a paper chain to decorate the classroom for the holiday party.
We hung a colorful paper chain around the windows for the birthday celebration.
