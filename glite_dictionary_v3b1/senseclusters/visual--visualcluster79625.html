visual

noun
illustrative image
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒuəl / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒuəl / (BrE)
an image, picture, or graphic used to illustrate or explain something, especially in presentations or media

Examples

A visual aid
The teacher used several visuals to make the lesson more engaging for the students.
Modern news articles often include visuals such as charts and photographs to support the story.
She designed the visuals for the marketing campaign to attract more attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graphic - a visual element such as a drawing or diagram
  • synonym: illustration - a picture or diagram that helps explain or decorate text
  • antonym: text - written words as opposed to images or graphics
