off-kilter

adjective
not straight or unconventional
Pronunciation: / ˌɔfˈkɪltɚ / (AmE), / ˌɒfˈkɪltə / (BrE)
not aligned or balanced; slightly strange, eccentric, or unconventional

Examples

An off-kilter picture
After the earthquake, the picture frames on the wall looked off-kilter.
She has an off-kilter sense of humor that makes her stand out in the group.
The dialogue in the film was deliberately off-kilter to give it a surreal feeling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: askew - not in a straight or level position
  • synonym: unconventional - not based on or conforming to what is generally done or believed
