falling out

noun
disagreement causing separation
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑlɪŋ aʊt / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlɪŋ aʊt / (BrE)
a disagreement or quarrel that results in an end or weakening of a relationship

Examples

A falling out with friends
A minor disagreement quickly turned into a major falling out between the team members.
After a family falling out, they stopped speaking for several months.
Their falling out over money ended a fifteen-year friendship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quarrel - an angry argument or disagreement
  • antonym: reconciliation - restoration of friendly relations
