twizzle

noun
small twist or curl
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈtwɪzəl / (BrE)
a small twist or curl, especially of hair, fabric, or a slight twisting movement

Examples

A hair twizzle
She tucked a loose twizzle of hair behind her ear before leaving for work.
A playful twizzle of ribbon decorated the top of the gift box.
The dancer added a quick twizzle to her routine, impressing the judges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spiral - a shape or object that winds in a continuous curve
