bateau bridge

noun
temporary bridge over pontoons
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈtoʊ brɪdʒ / (AmE), / bəˈtəʊ brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a temporary bridge constructed over a series of pontoons or flat-bottomed boats, used especially for military or emergency purposes

Examples

A bateau bridge over water
During the flood, engineers quickly assembled a bateau bridge to allow supplies to reach the cut-off town.
Historically, a bateau bridge was often used in military campaigns to move troops across waterways.
The army crossed the river using a bateau bridge constructed by the combat engineers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pontoon bridge - a floating bridge supported by pontoons
