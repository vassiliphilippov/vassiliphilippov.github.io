unhaltingly

adverb
without stopping
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɔltɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɔːltɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that continues steadily without pausing, stopping, or hesitation

Examples

To speak unhaltingly
The speaker talked unhaltingly for over an hour, barely pausing to take a breath.
Rain fell unhaltingly throughout the night, soaking the fields by morning.
She worked unhaltingly on her project until it was finished.
He spoke unhaltingly about his experiences, impressing everyone with his confidence.
The river flowed unhaltingly through the valley, never slowing or stopping.
She worked unhaltingly toward her goal, never letting obstacles discourage her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: continuously - without stopping or interruption
  • antonym: hesitantly - in a way that shows uncertainty or pause
  • antonym: intermittently - in a way that starts and stops repeatedly
  • synonym: steadily - in a regular and even way, without stopping
