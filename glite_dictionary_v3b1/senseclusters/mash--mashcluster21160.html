mash

verb
crush or squash out of shape
2.2
Pronunciation: / mæʃ / (AmE), / mæʃ / (BrE)
to crush or press something until it becomes soft or loses its shape

Examples

To mash potatoes
He accidentally mashed his sandwich by sitting on it.
If you mash the berries too hard, they'll lose their shape.
She used a fork to mash the bananas until they were smooth.
He used a stone to mash the berries for the recipe.
Please mash the potatoes until they are smooth and creamy.
You need to mash the avocado before spreading it on toast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crush - to press something so hard that it is damaged or loses its shape
  • synonym: grind - to reduce to small particles by crushing
  • synonym: squash - to press something so that it becomes flat or out of shape
