drum

noun
a type of fish that makes a drumming noise
9.6
Pronunciation: / drʌm / (AmE), / drʌm / (BrE)
a small to medium-sized bottom-dwelling fish found in shallow coastal and fresh waters, known for making a drumming noise

Examples

Catch a drum
Anglers often try to catch drum along the muddy riverbanks in the spring.
Grilled drum is a popular dish in many coastal communities.
The drum gets its name from the sounds it produces during spawning season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: croaker - a fish that produces croaking noises, similar to the drum
