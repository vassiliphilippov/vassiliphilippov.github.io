gazillion

noun
an extremely large, indefinite number
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈzɪljən / (AmE), / ɡəˈzɪljən / (BrE)
an extremely large but unspecified number, used for emphasis or exaggeration

Examples

A gazillion stars
A gazillion stars lit up the night sky, making it look almost like daylight.
She said she had a gazillion things to do before the party started.
There are a gazillion reasons why I love visiting the beach in summer.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: billion - the number 1,000,000,000 (one thousand million)
  • synonym: zillion - an extremely large but unspecified number
