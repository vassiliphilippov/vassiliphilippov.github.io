preinjury

adjective
before an injury
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈɪndʒəri / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈɪndʒəri / (BrE)
describing the state or condition existing before an injury occurred

Examples

Preinjury condition
Doctors compared his current mobility to his preinjury abilities to assess recovery.
The team reviewed her preinjury statistics to set realistic rehabilitation goals.
He hoped to regain his preinjury strength after months of physical therapy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postinjury - relating to the period after an injury
