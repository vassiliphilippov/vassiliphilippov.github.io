saint

verb
declare someone a saint
3.5
Pronunciation: / seɪnt / (AmE), / seɪnt / (BrE)
to officially declare (a dead person) to be a saint, especially by canonization in the Christian Church

Examples

To saint someone officially
He was sainted centuries after his death by the Vatican.
It took years of debate before the cleric was finally sainted.
The Church decided to saint her after investigating her miracles.
