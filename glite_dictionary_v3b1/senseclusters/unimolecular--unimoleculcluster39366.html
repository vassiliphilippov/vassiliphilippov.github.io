unimolecular

adjective
single-molecule process
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪmoʊˈlɛkjələr / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪməˈlɛkjʊlə / (BrE)
involving or consisting of a single molecule, especially in a chemical reaction

Examples

A unimolecular reaction
A unimolecular reaction depends on the transformation of one molecule.
The scientist explained that the process was unimolecular and did not require a second reactant.
In some cases, a unimolecular step is the slowest part of the chemical reaction.

Related Terms
