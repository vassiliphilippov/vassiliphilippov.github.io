while

verb
to spend time leisurely
6.0
Pronunciation: / waɪl / (AmE), / waɪl / (BrE)
to spend time in a relaxed or pleasant way, often to make time pass (usually used as 'while away')

Examples

To while away time
They spent the afternoon whiling away the hours in the garden.
She likes whiling her weekends reading novels by the window.
We were whiling the time before dinner by playing cards.
They whiled away the long summer evenings talking on the porch.
She whiled away the hours by painting landscapes in her studio.
During the delay, he whiled away the time by listening to music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: idle - to spend time doing nothing or very little
  • synonym: kill time - to do something to make time seem to go by faster
  • synonym: pass - to spend or let time go by
  • antonym: work - to engage in activity or labor
