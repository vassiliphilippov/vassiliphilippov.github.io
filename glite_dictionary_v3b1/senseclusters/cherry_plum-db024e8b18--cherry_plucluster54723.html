cherry plum

noun
a tree with edible red or yellow fruit
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛri ˌplʌm /; / ˈtʃɛri plʌm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri ˌplʌm /; / ˈtʃɛri plʌm / (BrE)
a small tree native to Asia (Prunus cerasifera) or its small, round, edible fruit that looks like a cherry or plum

Examples

A cherry plum tree
Ripe cherry plums can be red or yellow, depending on the variety.
The cherry plum produces fruit early in the season, making it popular among gardeners.
They planted a cherry plum tree at the edge of the orchard.
A single cherry plum can fit easily in the palm of your hand.
She made delicious jam from a basket of fresh cherry plums.
The garden was full of ripe cherry plums ready to be picked.
