word of farewell

noun
goodbye or parting expression
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɝd əv fɛrˈwɛl / (AmE), / wɜːd əv feəˈwɛl / (BrE)
an expression spoken or written to bid someone goodbye and convey good wishes when parting

Examples

A word of farewell

His final word of farewell lingered in her memory long after he had gone.
They exchanged a quick word of farewell before boarding their separate trains.
