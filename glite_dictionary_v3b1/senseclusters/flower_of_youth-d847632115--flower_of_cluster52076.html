flower of youth

noun
the prime of one's youth
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊ.ɚ əv juθ / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə əv juːθ / (BrE)
the best, most vigorous period of a person's youth; the time when someone is most full of energy, hope, and potential

Examples

The flower of youth
He wrote poetry filled with hope and dreams during the flower of youth.
Many great athletes reach their peak performance in the flower of youth.
She cherished those days spent abroad in the flower of youth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prime of life - the period when someone is in their physical or mental prime
