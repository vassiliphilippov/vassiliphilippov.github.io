hyperlocal

adjective
focused on small area
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈloʊkəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈləʊkəl / (BrE)
relating to or focused on a very small, specific community, area, or neighborhood

Examples

Hyperlocal news coverage
The hyperlocal website reports on events happening only within our neighborhood.
Many businesses use hyperlocal marketing to reach customers in a specific area.
A hyperlocal approach helps address the unique needs of each small community.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: local - relating to a particular area or neighborhood
  • degree higher: regional - relating to a larger area or region
