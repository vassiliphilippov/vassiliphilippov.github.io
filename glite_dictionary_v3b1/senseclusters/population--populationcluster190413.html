population

noun
the act of populating a place
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːpjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɒpjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of populating a place; the process of causing people or other living beings to settle in an area

Examples

Population growth
Policies of population through immigration shaped the region’s development.
Rapid population of the island led to concerns about sustainability.
The population of the territory was engineered by the colonial powers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colonization - the act of settling among and establishing control over the indigenous people of an area
  • antonym: depopulation - the reduction in the number of people living in a place
