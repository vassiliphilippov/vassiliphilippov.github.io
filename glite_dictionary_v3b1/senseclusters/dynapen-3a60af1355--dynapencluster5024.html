Dynapen

noun
trade name for an antibacterial drug
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈdaɪ.nə.pen/ /; / /ˈdaɪ.nə.pən/ (AmE), /ˈdaɪ.nə.pen/ /; / /ˈdaɪ.nə.pən/ (BrE)
A trademark for an antibacterial drug (dicloxacillin) used to treat staphylococcal infections resistant to penicillin.

Examples

Dynapen antibiotic drug
After her infection was confirmed as penicillin-resistant, the doctor prescribed Dynapen.
Patients allergic to Dynapen should inform their healthcare provider before starting treatment.
Some strains of staphylococcus are effectively treated with Dynapen when other antibiotics fail.
