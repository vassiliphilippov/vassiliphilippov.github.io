have it large

verb
celebrate or party extravagantly
Pronunciation: / hæv ɪt lɑrdʒ / (AmE), / hæv ɪt lɑːdʒ / (BrE)
to celebrate or enjoy oneself in an extravagant or uninhibited way, especially at a party or social event
