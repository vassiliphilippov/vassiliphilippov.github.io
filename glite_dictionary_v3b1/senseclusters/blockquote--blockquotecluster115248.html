blockquote

verb
to format as block quote
6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈblɑː.kwoʊt/ (AmE), /ˈblɒ.kwəʊt/ (BrE)
to format or present text as a block quotation, often by indenting or using a special tag to set it apart from the main text

Examples

To blockquote a paragraph
Please blockquote the relevant section so it stands out from the rest of the text.
Writers often blockquote long passages to highlight important quotations in their articles.
If you blockquote the quote, it will be easier for readers to identify the source.

Related Terms

  • antonym: paraphrase - to express the meaning of something using different words
  • synonym: quote - to repeat or copy out words from a source
