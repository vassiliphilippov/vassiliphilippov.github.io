Taxophytina

noun
a botanical subdivision including yews
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtæksəˌfaɪˈtaɪnə /; / ˌtæksəˌfaɪˈtiːnə / (AmE), / ˌtaksəˌfʌɪˈtʌɪnə /; / ˌtaksəˌfɪˈtiːnə / (BrE)
a group in plant classification used for the yews, sometimes treated as a class (Taxopsida) or as a subdivision of Gymnospermophyta
