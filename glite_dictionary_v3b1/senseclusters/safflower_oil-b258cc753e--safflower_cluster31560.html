safflower oil

noun
oil from safflower seeds
4.2 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsæf.laʊ.ər ɔɪl/ /; / /ˈsæf.laʊ.ər ˈɔɪ.əl/ (AmE), /ˈsæf.laʊ.ə ɔɪl/ /; / /ˈsæf.laʊ.ə ˈɔɪ.əl/ (BrE)
a pale, flavorless oil made from safflower seeds, used mainly in cooking and sometimes in medicines or paints
