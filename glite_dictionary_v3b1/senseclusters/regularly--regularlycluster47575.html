regularly

adverb
in a consistent or uniform way
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjələrli / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊləli / (BrE)
in a consistent or repeated way, often at fixed or usual times

Examples

To exercise regularly daily
It is important to exercise regularly to maintain good health.
She checks her email regularly throughout the day to stay updated.
The trains run regularly every fifteen minutes from this station.
I try to go jogging regularly to maintain my health.
New issues of the magazine are published regularly throughout the year.
The teacher reminds us to check our homework regularly for mistakes.
