nightclub

noun
a late-night entertainment venue
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtˌklʌb / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtˌklʌb / (BrE)
a place open late at night that offers music, dancing, entertainment, and serves food and drinks

Examples

A nightclub with music
He started his career as a DJ at a popular nightclub.
Many celebrities are often seen at the trendiest nightclubs in the city.
They went to a nightclub downtown to dance and listen to live music.
He met his friends at the nightclub for an evening of music and dancing.
She performed as a singer at a popular nightclub downtown.
The city is famous for its vibrant nightclub scene and late-night parties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar - a place where alcoholic drinks are served
  • synonym: cabaret - a restaurant or nightclub where entertainment is performed while patrons eat or drink
