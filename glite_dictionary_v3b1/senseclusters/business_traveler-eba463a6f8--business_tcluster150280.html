business traveler

noun
a person who travels for work
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs ˈtrævələr / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˈtrævələ / (BrE)
a person who travels for work and whose travel expenses are paid by their employer

Examples

A business traveler books flights
As a frequent business traveler, she values efficiency and comfort in her accommodations.
Companies are beginning to reduce expenses by limiting how often a business traveler can fly internationally.
The hotel offers special amenities for the business traveler, including free Wi-Fi and meeting rooms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tourist - someone who travels for pleasure, not for work
