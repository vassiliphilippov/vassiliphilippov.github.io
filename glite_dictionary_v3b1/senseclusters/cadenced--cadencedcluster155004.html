cadenced

adjective
having a rhythmic cadence
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪdənst / (AmE), / ˈkeɪdənst / (BrE)
having a regular, rhythmic flow or beat; marked by cadence.

Examples

A cadenced rhythm
She marched forward, her footsteps falling in a steady, cadenced beat on the pavement.
The cadenced chants of the crowd grew louder as the protest continued.
The poet read his work in a cadenced voice, emphasizing every syllable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monotonous - dull, tedious, and repetitious; lacking in variety and interest
  • synonym: rhythmic - having or relating to rhythm; marked by a regular beat
