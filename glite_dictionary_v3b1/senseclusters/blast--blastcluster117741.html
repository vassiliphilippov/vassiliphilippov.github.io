blast

noun
a highly enjoyable experience
Pronunciation: / blæst / (AmE), / blɑːst / (BrE)
an event or experience that is extremely enjoyable or exciting; a lot of fun

Examples

A blast at the party
The concert last night was a total blast—everyone danced and sang along.
We had a real blast at the amusement park this weekend.
You should come to our barbecue; it's always a blast when everyone gets together.
Everyone agreed that the theme park trip was a blast for the kids.
Last night's concert was a blast—we danced all evening.
She said her birthday party was a blast and she can't wait to do it again.
