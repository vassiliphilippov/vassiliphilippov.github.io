wrongdoer

noun
person who commits a wrong or offense
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔŋˌduːər / (AmE), / ˈrɒŋˌduːə / (BrE)
a person who does something morally or legally wrong; an offender or culprit

Examples

A wrongdoer punished
He was seen not as a wrongdoer but as a person who made a mistake.
Society must find ways to rehabilitate the wrongdoer rather than just punish them.
The police worked diligently to bring the wrongdoer to justice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: culprit - a person responsible for a fault or crime
  • synonym: lawbreaker - a person who violates the law
  • synonym: offender - a person who commits an illegal act
