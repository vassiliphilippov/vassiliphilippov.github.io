demodulation

noun
signal extraction from a carrier wave
5.7 6.9
Pronunciation: /diː.mɑː.dʒə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /diː.moʊ.dʒə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /diː.mɒd.jʊ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /dɪ.mɒd.jʊ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
