webshot

noun
web page screenshot
4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈweb.ʃɑːt/ /; / /ˈweb.ʃɔːt/ (AmE), /ˈweb.ʃɒt/ (BrE)

Examples

Before making edits, he saved a webshot of the original homepage layout.
