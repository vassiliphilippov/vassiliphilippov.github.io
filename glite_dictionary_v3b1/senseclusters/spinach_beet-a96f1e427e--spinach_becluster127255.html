spinach beet

noun
beet grown for edible leaves and stalks
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnɪtʃ bit /; / ˈspɪnətʃ biːt / (AmE), / ˈspɪnɪtʃ biːt / (BrE)
a variety of beet grown for its edible leaves and stalks, eaten as a vegetable (not for its root)
