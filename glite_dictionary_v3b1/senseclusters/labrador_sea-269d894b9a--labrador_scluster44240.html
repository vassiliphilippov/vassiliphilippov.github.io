Labrador Sea

proper noun
sea between Labrador and southern Greenland
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlæbrədɔr si / (AmE), / ˈlæbrədɔː siː / (BrE)
A sea in the North Atlantic Ocean, located between the Labrador Peninsula in Canada and southern Greenland.

Examples

The Labrador Sea
Icebergs drift south in the Labrador Sea each spring, posing hazards to ships.
Many species of whales migrate through the Labrador Sea during the summer months.
The Labrador Sea is known for its cold temperature and strong ocean currents.
