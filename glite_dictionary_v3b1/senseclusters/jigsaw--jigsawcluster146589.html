jigsaw

noun
picture puzzle
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪɡˌsɔ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪɡˌsɔː / (BrE)
a puzzle made of many small, irregularly shaped pieces that fit together to form a complete picture

Examples

A jigsaw puzzle

She bought a challenging 2000-piece jigsaw for her vacation.

He spent hours putting together a 1,000-piece jigsaw on the kitchen table.
Completing a difficult jigsaw can be a relaxing way to spend an evening.
The children worked together to finish the animal jigsaw before dinner.
