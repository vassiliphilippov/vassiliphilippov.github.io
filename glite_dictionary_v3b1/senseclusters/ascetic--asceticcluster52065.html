ascetic

noun
a person practicing self-denial for spiritual discipline
9.1 6.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈse.tɪk/ (AmE), /ə.ˈse.tɪk/ (BrE)
a person who practices severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious or spiritual reasons

Examples

A devoted ascetic
Her ascetic lifestyle was marked by strict fasting and silence.
Some monks embrace an ascetic existence, rejecting worldly comforts.
The ascetic lived alone in the mountains, dedicating his life to meditation and prayer.
