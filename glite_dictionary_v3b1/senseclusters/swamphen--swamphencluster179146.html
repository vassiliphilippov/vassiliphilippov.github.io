swamphen

noun
a water bird of the genus Porphyrio
9.9 7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈswæm.fən/ /; / /ˈswɒm.fən/ (AmE), /ˈswɒm.fən/ /; / /ˈswæm.fən/ (BrE)
a medium-sized aquatic bird of the genus Porphyrio, recognized by its large feet, frontal shield, and resemblance to a domestic hen

Examples

The purple swamphen is commonly found in freshwater wetlands across Europe and Asia.
