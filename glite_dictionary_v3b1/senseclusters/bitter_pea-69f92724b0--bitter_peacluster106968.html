bitter pea

noun
Australian spiny shrub of genus Daviesia
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtər pi / (AmE), / ˈbɪtə piː / (BrE)
any of several spiny Australian shrubs of the genus Daviesia, bearing yellow flowers and triangular seeds

Examples

The bitter pea shrub
Many gardeners appreciate the bitter pea for its drought resistance in Australian landscapes.
Seeds from the bitter pea are triangular and distinctive among native shrubs.
The bitter pea is well known for its striking yellow flowers and sharp spines.
