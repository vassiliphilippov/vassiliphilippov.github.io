Sonora lac

noun
resinous substance from Mexican shrubs
9.9
Pronunciation: / səˈnɔrə læk / (AmE), / səˈnɔːrə læk / (BrE)
A resinous substance resembling lac, secreted by a scale insect living on twigs of certain Mexican shrubs and used locally as medicine.

Examples

Sonora lac resin
Locals harvest Sonora lac from the twigs of native Mexican shrubs.
Researchers are studying the properties of Sonora lac for potential medical applications.
The traditional healers use Sonora lac in their medicinal preparations.
