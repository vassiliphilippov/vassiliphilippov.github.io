humanly

adverb
in a human manner; as only a human can
4.1 4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuːmənli / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmənli / (BrE)
in a way that is characteristic of or possible for human beings; in a manner typical of humans.

Examples

Not humanly possible
It is not humanly possible to lift such a heavy object without machinery.
The nurses did everything humanly possible to save the patient.
We should treat others as humanly as we can, with empathy and respect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inhumanly - in a way that lacks human qualities or kindness
