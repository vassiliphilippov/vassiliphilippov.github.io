knock it off

verb
stop doing something annoying
8.1 5.31/m
Pronunciation: / nÉ‘k Éªt É”f / (AmE), / nÉ’k Éªt É’f / (BrE)
(informal) used to tell someone to stop doing something that is annoying or disruptive.

Examples

Please knock it off
Hey, knock it off! You're making too much noise.
If you don't knock it off, you'll have to leave the room.
The teacher told the students to knock it off when they started shouting.

Synonyms
