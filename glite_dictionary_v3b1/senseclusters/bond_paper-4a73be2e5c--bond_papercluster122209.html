bond paper

noun
high-quality white writing paper
Pronunciation: / bɑnd ˈpeɪ.pɚ / (AmE), / bɒnd ˈpeɪ.pə / (BrE)
A high-quality, strong, durable white writing paper, typically used for business and official documents.

Examples

A sheet of bond paper
Most official letters are typed on bond paper because of its quality and durability.
She printed the contract on bond paper for a more professional presentation.
We need to purchase more bond paper for the office printer.
