intercultural

adjective
between cultures
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈkʌltʃərəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈkʌltʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving interaction between two or more different cultures

Examples

Intercultural exchange programs
The university encourages intercultural exchanges to help students learn about global perspectives.
Effective intercultural communication is essential in international business negotiations.
The city hosts an annual intercultural fair featuring food and music from around the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monocultural - relating to or involving only one culture
  • synonym: multicultural - relating to or including people from several different cultures
