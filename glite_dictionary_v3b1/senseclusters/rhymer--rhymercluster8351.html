rhymer

noun
one who writes rhymes; minor poet
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪmər / (AmE), / ˈraɪmə / (BrE)
a person who writes rhymes, especially someone considered a minor or inferior poet

Examples

A rhymer writes poems
It's easy to be a rhymer, but much harder to write poetry that moves people.
The critic dismissed him as a mere rhymer rather than a true poet.
Though she fancied herself a poet, her friends considered her more of a rhymer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poet - a person who writes poems
  • synonym: versifier - someone who writes verses, often considered mechanical or inferior
