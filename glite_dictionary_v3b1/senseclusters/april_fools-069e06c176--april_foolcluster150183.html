April Fools'

noun
1 April, day for practical jokes
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪprəl ˈfuːlz / (AmE), / ˌeɪprɪl ˈfuːlz / (BrE)
The first day of April, known for playing practical jokes and hoaxes.

Examples

April Fools' Day
Every year on April Fools', my friends try to trick me with silly pranks.
She forgot it was April Fools' and believed every joke all day.
The company announced a fake product on April Fools' to amuse its customers.
