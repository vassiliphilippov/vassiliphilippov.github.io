fruit drink

noun
sweetened beverage with diluted fruit juice
7.4
Pronunciation: / fruːt drɪŋk / (AmE), / fruːt drɪŋk / (BrE)
a sweetened beverage made from diluted fruit juice, often containing added sugar or flavoring

Examples
