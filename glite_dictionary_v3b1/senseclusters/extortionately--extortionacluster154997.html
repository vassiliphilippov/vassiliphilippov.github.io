extortionately

adverb
to an exorbitant or excessive degree
3.2
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɔrʃənətli / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɔːʃənətli / (BrE)
to an exorbitant or excessively high degree, especially with regard to prices or demands

Examples

Extortionately high
He complained that the hotel charged extortionately for Wi-Fi access.
Only the wealthiest tourists can afford the extortionately priced souvenirs.
The rent in the city center has become extortionately expensive in recent years.
