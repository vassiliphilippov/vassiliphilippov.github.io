nonpresence

noun
absence; not being present
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈprɛzəns / (AmE), / nɒnˈprɛzəns / (BrE)
the state or condition of not being present in a place or situation

Examples

Nonpresence of staff
The nonpresence of key witnesses delayed the start of the trial.
Her nonpresence at the ceremony was surprising to everyone who expected her.
The manager commented on the nonpresence of several employees during the important meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absence - the state of being away or not present
  • antonym: presence - the state of being present in a place
