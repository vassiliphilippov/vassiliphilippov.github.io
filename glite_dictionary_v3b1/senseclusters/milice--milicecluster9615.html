milice

noun
civilian armed group
Pronunciation: / miˈlis / (AmE), / miˈliːs / (BrE)
an armed group of civilians organized for military service, especially to defend a local area or engage in irregular warfare

Examples

Local milice group
During the unrest, local farmers formed a milice to defend their village against looters.
The government accused the milice of operating outside the law.
A milice was quickly organized to protect the town from bandit attacks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: army - a large organized military force
  • synonym: militia - a military force made up of ordinary citizens
