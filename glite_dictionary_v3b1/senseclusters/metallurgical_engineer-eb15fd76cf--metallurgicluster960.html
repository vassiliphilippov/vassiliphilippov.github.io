metallurgical engineer

noun
engineer specializing in metals
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtl̩ˈɜrdʒɪkəl ɪnˈdʒɪnɪr / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəlˈɜːdʒɪkəl ɪnˈdʒɪnɪə / (BrE)
an engineer who specializes in the extraction, processing, and production of metals and their alloys

Examples

Metallurgical engineer at company
After earning her degree, she worked as a metallurgical engineer at an aerospace company.
Many mining companies hire a metallurgical engineer to oversee the refinement of raw ore into usable metal.
The role of a metallurgical engineer involves developing new alloys and improving existing manufacturing processes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: metallurgist - a person who studies or works in metallurgy
