locust tree

noun
a type of hardwood tree (Leguminosae)
5.5 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / Ààlo äk…ôst triÀê / (AmE), / Ààl…ô äk…ôst triÀê / (BrE)
A hardwood tree of the family Leguminosae, especially the black locust or honey locust, known for its tough wood and compound leaves.
