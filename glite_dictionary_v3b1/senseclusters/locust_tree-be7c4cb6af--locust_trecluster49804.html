locust tree

noun
a type of hardwood tree (Leguminosae)
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊkəst triː / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəst triː / (BrE)
A hardwood tree of the family Leguminosae, especially the black locust or honey locust, known for its tough wood and compound leaves.

Examples

The locust tree leaves
In autumn, the yellow leaves of the locust tree create a beautiful display.
Many furniture makers value the hard wood of the locust tree.
The locust tree in our backyard provides excellent shade during the summer.

Related Terms
