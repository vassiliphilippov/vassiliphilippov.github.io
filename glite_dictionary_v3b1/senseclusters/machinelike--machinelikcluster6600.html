machinelike

adjective
automatic and unfeeling, like a machine
Pronunciation: / məˈʃinˌlaɪk /; / məˈʃiːnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / məˈʃiːnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
acting or functioning in a way that is automatic, precise, or lacking emotion, like a machine

Examples

A machinelike routine
Despite many changes, his machinelike routine never varied from day to day.
Her machinelike responses seemed rehearsed and devoid of genuine feeling.
The team operated with machinelike precision throughout the tournament.
His machinelike efficiency made him the top performer in the department.
The dancers moved in a perfectly machinelike sequence throughout the performance.
She answered every question in a machinelike manner, without showing any emotion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatic - working by itself with little or no direct human control
  • antonym: human - showing qualities of emotion, warmth, or thought
  • synonym: mechanical - operating like a machine, automatic and unfeeling
