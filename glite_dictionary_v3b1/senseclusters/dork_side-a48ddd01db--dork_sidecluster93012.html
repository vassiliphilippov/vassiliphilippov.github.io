dork side

noun
one's nerdy or awkward side
6.6
Pronunciation: / dɔrk saɪd / (AmE), / dɔːk saɪd / (BrE)
The aspect of a person's personality that involves nerdy, awkward, or unfashionable interests, often embraced playfully.

Examples

Embrace your dork side
Don't be afraid to embrace your dork side around friends who understand you.
He finally let his dork side show by quoting lines from Star Trek all night.
Joining the comic book club was her way of celebrating her dork side.
