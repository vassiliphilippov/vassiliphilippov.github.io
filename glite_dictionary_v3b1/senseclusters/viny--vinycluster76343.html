viny

adjective
like a vine
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪni / (AmE), / ˈvaɪni / (BrE)
having the qualities, appearance, or characteristics of a vine, especially in form or growth

Examples

The viny branches
The viny branches climbed up the old stone wall.
Her garden was filled with viny plants that twisted around the trellis.
We admired the viny growth covering the fence at the back of the yard.

Related Terms

  • antonym: woody - having the characteristics of wood
