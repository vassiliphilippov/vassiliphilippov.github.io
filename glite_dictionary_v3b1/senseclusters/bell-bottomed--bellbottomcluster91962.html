bell-bottomed

adjective
flared at the bottom (of trousers)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛl ˌbɑːtəm(d) / (AmE), / ˈbɛl ˌbɒtəm(d) / (BrE)
(of trousers) having legs that become wider from the knee downward, flaring out at the bottom.

Examples

Bell-bottomed pants
His bell-bottomed pants swept the floor as he walked.
She wore her favorite bell-bottomed jeans to the party.
The fashion from the 1970s made bell-bottomed trousers popular again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bell-bottoms - trousers with legs that flare at the bottom
