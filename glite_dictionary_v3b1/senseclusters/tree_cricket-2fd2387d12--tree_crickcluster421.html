tree cricket

noun
pale arboreal American cricket
Pronunciation: / triː ˈkrɪkɪt / (AmE), / triː ˈkrɪkɪt / (BrE)
A pale, arboreal American cricket known for producing loud, distinctive stridulating sounds.

Examples

A tree cricket chirping
A tree cricket landed on the window, its transparent wings shimmering in the light.
At night, the loud chorus of the tree cricket fills the summer air.
Scientists studied the calling patterns of the tree cricket in the forest canopy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cricket - an insect known for producing chirping sounds by rubbing its wings
