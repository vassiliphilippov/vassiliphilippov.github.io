clappers

noun
percussion instrument clicked together by dancers
7.0 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈklæp.ərz/ (AmE), /ˈklæp.əz/ (BrE)
A percussion instrument made of a pair of hollow pieces of wood or bone, which are clicked together in rhythm, typically used by dancers.
