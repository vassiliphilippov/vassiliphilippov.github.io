necrophiliac

adjective
morbid interest in death
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɛkrəˈfɪliæk / (AmE), / ˌnɛkrəˈfɪliæk / (BrE)
having an abnormal or morbid interest in death, corpses, or dead things, not necessarily sexual in nature

Examples

Necrophiliac art style
The artist's necrophiliac themes shocked many visitors at the gallery.
Her necrophiliac fascination was evident in her collection of antique mourning jewelry.
Some horror films appeal to viewers with a necrophiliac curiosity about death.

Related Terms

  • synonym: morbid - showing an unhealthy interest in disturbing subjects, especially death
