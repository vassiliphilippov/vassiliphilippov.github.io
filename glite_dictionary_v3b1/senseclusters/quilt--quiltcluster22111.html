quilt

verb
sew layers of fabric together
6.3 2.7
Pronunciation: /kwɪlt/ (AmE), /kwɪlt/ (BrE)
to sew together layers of fabric, usually with padding, to make a warm bed covering or similar item

Examples

To quilt a blanket
He spent the weekend quilting the layers together to create a warm bedspread.
She decided to quilt a blanket for her friend's newborn baby.
The artisan will quilt the jacket with a special decorative pattern.
My grandmother loves to quilt in her spare time, creating beautiful patterns by hand.
She quilted a cover for her baby using scraps of colorful fabric.
The club meets every Tuesday to quilt blankets for the local hospital.
