understanding

noun
informal agreement or arrangement
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dər.ˈstæn.dɪŋ/ /; / /ə.n.dər.ˈstæn.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.ˈstæn.dɪŋ/ /; / /ə.n.də.ˈstæn.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
an informal or unspoken agreement between two or more parties; a mutual arrangement or promise, often not written down

Examples

A business understanding
After a long discussion, the two companies reached an understanding on pricing policies.
There is an understanding between the neighbors about using the shared driveway.
We came to an understanding that both sides would benefit from the project.

Related Terms
