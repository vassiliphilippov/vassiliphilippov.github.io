undeluded

adjective
not deceived
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈluːdɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈluːdɪd / (BrE)
not deceived or misled; seeing things as they truly are

Examples

An undeluded view
Despite the rumors, she stayed undeluded and focused on the facts.
He remained undeluded by the flashy advertisements and made a careful decision.
It is important to be undeluded when evaluating new opportunities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deluded - believing something that is not true
