cajeta

noun
Mexican caramel sauce
Pronunciation: / kaˈheɪtə / (AmE), / kaˈheɪtə / (BrE)
a sweet Mexican caramel made from slowly cooked goat's milk and sugar, used as a sauce, spread, or candy
