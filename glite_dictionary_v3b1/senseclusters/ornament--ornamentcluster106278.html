ornament

noun
an object used to decorate
1.6 7.77/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…îÀêrn…ôm…ônt / (AmE), / Àà…îÀên…ôm…ônt / (BrE)
an object or decoration that is used to make something more attractive or beautiful

Examples

A Christmas ornament
Each shelf was lined with a different kind of ornament.
She hung a shiny ornament on the Christmas tree.
The vase on the table was used mainly as an ornament.
