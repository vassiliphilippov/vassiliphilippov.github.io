gasket coil

noun
method of coiling rope
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæskət kɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡæskɪt kɔɪl / (BrE)
A simple method of coiling rope to prevent tangling, commonly used in maritime contexts.

Examples

A gasket coil of rope
After washing the lines, she made a neat gasket coil to store them for the season.
He showed the new sailors how a gasket coil keeps the deck tidy and safe.
It's important to practice making a gasket coil so the rope won't snag when deployed.
