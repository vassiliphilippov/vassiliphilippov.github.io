Samburu

proper noun
Kenyan ethnic group
9.9
Pronunciation: / sæmˈbuːruː / (AmE), / sæmˈbuːruː / (BrE)
a member of a semi-nomadic ethnic group living in northern Kenya, known for their distinctive culture and traditions

Examples

Samburu people
The Samburu have preserved many of their traditional customs despite modern influences.
A group of Samburu women performed a dance at the cultural festival.
Researchers visited the Samburu to learn about their way of life in northern Kenya.
