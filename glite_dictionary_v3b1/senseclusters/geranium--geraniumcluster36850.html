geranium

noun
a flowering plant of the Geranium family
9.0
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / dʒəˈreɪniəm / (BrE)
a herbaceous plant of the genus Geranium or the family Geraniaceae, widely cultivated for its rounded, often brightly colored flowers

Examples

A geranium flower
A pot of geraniums sat on the kitchen table, brightening up the whole room.
Gardeners favor geranium for its resilience and long flowering season.
She planted pink geraniums in the window box to add color to the apartment.

  • synonym: pelargonium - a similar-looking plant often called 'geranium' in horticulture
