gossipy

adjective
talking eagerly about others' private affairs

Pronunciation: /ˈgɑː.sə.piː/ /; / /ˈgɑː.sɪ.piː/ (AmE), /ˈgɒs.ɪ.piː/ /; / /ˈgɒs.ə.piː/ (BrE)
Enjoying or characterized by talking about the private affairs of others, especially in a casual or unconstrained way.

Examples

The gossipy conversation at the cafe was filled with rumors and speculation.
