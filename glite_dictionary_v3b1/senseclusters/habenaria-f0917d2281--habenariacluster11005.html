Habenaria

noun
a genus of terrestrial orchids
Pronunciation: / hɑːbəˈnɛriə /; / ˌhæbəˈnɛriə / (AmE), / habɪˈnɑːriə /; / ˌhabəˈnɛːrɪə / (BrE)
