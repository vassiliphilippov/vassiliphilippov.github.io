demand for identification

noun
request to prove identity
1.0 6.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmænd fər aɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈmɑːnd fə aɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
A request, especially by someone in authority such as a sentry or guard, for a person to present documents or other proof verifying their identity.

Examples

A demand for identification
At the entrance to the base, the guard issued a demand for identification before allowing anyone inside.
In emergency situations, a demand for identification helps ensure only authorized personnel are admitted.
The sentry's demand for identification caught the visitor by surprise late at night.
