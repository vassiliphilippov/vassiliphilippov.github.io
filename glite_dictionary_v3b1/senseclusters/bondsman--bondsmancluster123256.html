bondsman

noun
man acting as surety; bail guarantor
9.6 7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑːndz.mən/ (AmE), /ˈbɒndz.mən/ (BrE)
a man who promises to pay money if someone does not fulfill an obligation, especially one who acts as surety for a defendant's bail

Examples

Bail bondsman
She hired a bondsman to fulfill the financial requirements of her bail.
The bondsman appeared in court to guarantee the defendant’s release on bail.
Without a bondsman, the accused would have remained in custody until trial.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surety - a person who takes responsibility for another's performance of an undertaking, e.g. appearing in court
