Pistacia terebinthus

noun
Mediterranean tree yielding turpentine
Pronunciation: / pɪˈsteɪʃiə tɛrəˈbɪnθəs / (AmE), / pɪˈsteɪsɪə tɛrəˈbɪnθəs / (BrE)
a small Mediterranean tree of the cashew family that produces Chian turpentine, known for its aromatic resin and compound leaves

Examples
