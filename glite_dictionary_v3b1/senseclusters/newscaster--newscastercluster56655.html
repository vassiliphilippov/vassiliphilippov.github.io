newscaster

noun
news presenter on radio or TV
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzˌkæstər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌkɑːstə / (BrE)
A person who reads and presents news on radio or television.

Examples

A newscaster on TV
As a newscaster, she strives to present information accurately and objectively.
The newscaster delivered the evening headlines with a calm and steady voice.
The audience recognized the newscaster from his popular morning show.

Related Terms

  • synonym: newsreader - a person who presents the news on television, especially in British English
