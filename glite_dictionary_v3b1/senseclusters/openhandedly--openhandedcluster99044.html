openhandedly

adverb
frankly or generously
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊpənˈhændɪdli / (AmE), / ˌəʊpənˈhændɪdli / (BrE)
in a generous or giving way, without holding back or being reserved

Examples

Gives openhandedly
She always donates openhandedly to charities, never hesitating to help those in need.
The manager praised his team openhandedly for their hard work and dedication.
He spoke openhandedly about his past mistakes, hoping others could learn from them.
The family gave openhandedly to those in need during the holiday season.
He always shares his time and resources openhandedly with his friends.
The community responded openhandedly to the call for donations after the disaster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frankly - in an open, honest, and direct manner
  • synonym: freely - without restriction or holding back
  • synonym: generously - in a way that shows willingness to give more than expected
  • antonym: secretively - in a way that hides information or feelings
  • antonym: stingily - in a way that is unwilling to give or spend
