tight budget

noun
having little money to spend
1.0
Pronunciation: / taɪt ˈbʌdʒɪt / (AmE), / taɪt ˈbʌdʒɪt / (BrE)
A situation in which only a small amount of money is available to spend.

Examples

A tight budget plan
Families on a tight budget often look for discounts and special offers at grocery stores.
Living on a tight budget means you have to make careful choices every day.
The company managed to complete the project despite a tight budget.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ample budget - a large or generous amount of money available for spending
  • synonym: limited budget - only a small amount of money available for spending
  • synonym: shoestring budget - an extremely small budget
