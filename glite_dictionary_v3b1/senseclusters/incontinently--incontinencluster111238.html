incontinently

adverb
without restraint
9.3
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈkɑːn.tɪ.nənt.liː/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈkɒn.tɪ.nənt.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that shows a lack of control or restraint, especially over feelings or actions

Examples

To laugh incontinently
He laughed incontinently at the joke, unable to stop himself.
Tears streamed incontinently down her face as she heard the news.
The children ran incontinently into the playground as soon as the bell rang.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recklessly - in a way that shows a lack of care about danger or consequences
  • antonym: restrainedly - in a controlled or limited manner
  • synonym: uncontrollably - in a manner that cannot be controlled
