signifier

noun
symbol or representation
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡnɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a symbol, word, or sign that represents or stands for something else, especially in language or communication

Examples

A signifier of peace
In literature, a white dove is often used as a signifier of peace.
The company logo acts as a signifier of its brand identity.
In linguistics, the word itself is the signifier, while the concept it refers to is the signified.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sign - an object, quality, or event whose presence indicates something
  • synonym: symbol - a mark or character representing something else
