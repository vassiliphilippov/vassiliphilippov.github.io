chicken-heartedly

adverb
in a cowardly or timid way
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪk.ɪn ˌhɑːr.tɪd.li / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ɪn ˌhɑː.tɪd.li / (BrE)
In a cowardly or timid manner; showing lack of courage.

Examples

To chicken-heartedly refuse
He chicken-heartedly refused to confront his boss about the mistake.
She chicken-heartedly backed out of giving her speech at the last moment.
The team chicken-heartedly avoided any tough questions during the interview.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bravely - in a courageous or bold way
  • synonym: timidly - in a way that shows a lack of courage or confidence
