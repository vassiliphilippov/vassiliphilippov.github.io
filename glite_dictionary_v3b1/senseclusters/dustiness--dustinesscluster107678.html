dustiness

noun
state of being dusty
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌst.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdʌs.ti.nɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being dusty or covered with dust

Examples

The dustiness of air
Despite the dustiness of the road, the children played outside all afternoon.
Spring cleaning reduced the dustiness in the house significantly.
The dustiness of the old attic made it difficult to breathe without sneezing.
