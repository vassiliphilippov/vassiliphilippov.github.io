seafood buffet

noun
buffet meal with various seafood dishes
Pronunciation: / ˈsiˌfʊd bəˈfeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌfuːd ˈbʊfeɪ / (BrE)
A meal or dining event where a wide variety of seafood dishes are served for self-service.

Examples

A seafood buffet meal
She tried oysters for the first time at the local seafood buffet.
The hotel restaurant is famous for its lavish seafood buffet every Friday night.
We treated ourselves to a seafood buffet while vacationing at the beach resort.
