Indian shot

noun
tropical plant used for arrowroot starch
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ʃɒt / (BrE)
A tropical plant (canna) grown especially for its edible rootstock, from which arrowroot starch is obtained.

Examples

Indian shot plant
Starch extracted from Indian shot roots is sometimes used as a thickener in cooking.
The farmer cultivated Indian shot for its nutritious rootstock and vibrant flowers.
The seeds of the Indian shot are hard and can be used for jewelry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canna - a tropical herbaceous plant of the genus Canna
