get a rise out of

verb
provoke a reaction (annoyance, etc.) in someone
6.5
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ə raɪz aʊt ʌv / (AmE), / ɡɛt ə raɪz aʊt ɒv / (BrE)

The comedian kept trying to get a rise out of the audience with his controversial jokes.
