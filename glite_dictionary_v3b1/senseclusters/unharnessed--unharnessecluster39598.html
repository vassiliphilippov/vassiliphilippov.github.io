unharnessed

adjective
not wearing a harness
5.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɑrnəst / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɑːnɪst / (BrE)
not wearing or fitted with a harness, especially referring to animals or equipment not attached for pulling or control

Examples

Unharnessed horses
After a long day of work, the horses were left unharnessed to graze in the meadow.
The sled dogs waited unharnessed while their owner prepared their food.
It is safer to keep the equipment unharnessed when not in use.
