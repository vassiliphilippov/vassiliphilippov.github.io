piratically

adverb
in a pirate-like or illegal manner
6.4 9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈrætɪkli /; / paɪˈrætɪkli / (AmE), / pɪˈratɪkəli /; / paɪˈrætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is characteristic of pirates, especially being bold, lawless, or illegal

Examples
