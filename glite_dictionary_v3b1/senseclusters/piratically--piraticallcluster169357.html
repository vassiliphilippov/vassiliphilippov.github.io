piratically

adverb
in a pirate-like or illegal manner
6.4
Pronunciation: / pəˈrætɪkli /; / paɪˈrætɪkli / (AmE), / pɪˈratɪkəli /; / paɪˈrætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is characteristic of pirates, especially being bold, lawless, or illegal

Examples

Act piratically
She acted piratically when she took credit for someone else's work.
The novel was published piratically without the author's knowledge or consent.
The unauthorized copies were distributed piratically across several countries.
The treasure was taken piratically from the merchant ship during the night.
They acted piratically, ignoring all rules and seizing what they wanted.
The novel describes a crew that sails piratically across the open seas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boldly - in a brave or daring way
  • synonym: illegally - in an illegal manner
  • antonym: lawfully - in a lawful or legal manner
  • synonym: lawlessly - in a way that disregards the law
  • antonym: legally - in a way that follows the law
