noncannibalistic

adjective
not reducing own sales
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkænɪbəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkænɪbəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
not taking sales, customers, or resources away from another product or service of the same company.

Examples

Noncannibalistic product launch
The new smartphone was designed to be noncannibalistic and attract a different market segment.
Launching a noncannibalistic service helped the company grow without hurting its existing products.
They introduced a noncannibalistic product line to expand their customer base.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cannibalistic - taking sales or resources from another product of the same company
