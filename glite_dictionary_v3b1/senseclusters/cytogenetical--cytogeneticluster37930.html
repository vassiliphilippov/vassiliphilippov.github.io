cytogenetical

adjective
relating to cytogenetics
6.7
Pronunciation: / saɪtoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪkəl / (AmE), / saɪtəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪkəl / (BrE)
of or relating to cytogenetics, the branch of genetics concerned with the structure and function of the cell, especially the chromosomes.

Examples

Cytogenetical analysis
Recent advances in cytogenetical techniques have improved the diagnosis of chromosomal disorders.
She published several papers on the cytogenetical aspects of plant hybrids.
The laboratory specializes in cytogenetical analysis for genetic counseling.
