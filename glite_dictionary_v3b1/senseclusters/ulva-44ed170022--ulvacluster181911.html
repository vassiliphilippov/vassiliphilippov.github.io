Ulva

noun
genus of green seaweed (sea lettuce)
Pronunciation: / ˈʌlvə / (AmE), / ˈʌlvə / (BrE)
a genus of green seaweed with thin, flat, sheet-like leaves, commonly known as sea lettuce
