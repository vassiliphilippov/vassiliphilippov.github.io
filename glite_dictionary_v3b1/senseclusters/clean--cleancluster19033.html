clean

adjective
complete; thorough; total
Pronunciation: / klin / (AmE), / kliːn / (BrE)
complete and without anything left out; thorough and total.

Examples

A clean break
After years of conflict, she finally made a clean break and started a new life.
The party won a clean sweep in the elections, taking all the seats.
The thieves made a clean getaway from the scene, leaving no evidence behind.

  • antonym: partial - not complete; only part of the whole
