Apatosaurus

noun
large herbivorous Jurassic dinosaur genus
Pronunciation: / əˌpæt̬.əˈsɔr.əs / (AmE), / əˌpæt.əˈsɔː.rəs / (BrE)
A large, long-necked, quadrupedal herbivorous dinosaur of the late Jurassic period, belonging to the genus Apatosaurus.

Examples

A large Apatosaurus
A full-grown Apatosaurus could reach lengths of up to 25 meters.
Scientists believe that Apatosaurus lived during the late Jurassic period.
The skeleton of a Apatosaurus dominates the center of the dinosaur exhibit.
