petite marmite

noun
rich soup of meat and vegetables
6.7
Pronunciation: / pəˈtit mɑrˈmit / (AmE), / pəˈtiːt mɑːˈmiːt / (BrE)
A rich soup made with a variety of meats and vegetables, traditionally served in a small individual pot.

Examples

A petite marmite soup
For lunch, they ordered petite marmite, brimming with vegetables and tender pieces of beef.
She enjoyed a bowl of petite marmite before the main course arrived.
The restaurant's specialty is petite marmite, served piping hot in individual bowls.
