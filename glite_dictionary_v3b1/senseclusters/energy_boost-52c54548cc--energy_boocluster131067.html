energy boost

noun
a sudden increase in energy or vitality
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnɚdʒi buːst / (AmE), / ˈɛnədʒi buːst / (BrE)
a sudden increase in physical or mental energy or vitality, often as a result of food, drink, or activity

Examples

An energy boost from food
Athletes often eat bananas before a game for an energy boost.
Drinking a cup of coffee in the afternoon gives me an energy boost when I start to feel tired.
Many people look for an energy boost from vitamins or supplements.
