leftist

adjective
supporting the political left
5.7 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈlef.tɪst/ (AmE), /ˈlef.tɪst/ (BrE)
Supporting or relating to the principles or policies of the political left, especially those advocating social equality and government involvement in economic affairs.

Examples

Leftist ideas
Although her parents are conservatives, she holds strongly leftist views.
Many young voters are increasingly drawn to leftist ideas and policies.
The government's new tax plan received heavy criticism from leftist groups.

Related Terms
