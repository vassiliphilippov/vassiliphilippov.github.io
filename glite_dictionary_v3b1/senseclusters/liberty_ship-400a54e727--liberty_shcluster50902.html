Liberty ship

noun
type of WWII cargo ship
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪbɚti ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈlɪbəti ʃɪp / (BrE)
a type of large, slow cargo ship mass-produced in the United States during World War II to transport supplies and troops

Examples

A Liberty ship cargo
During the war, the United States built thousands of Liberty ships to support the Allied forces.
