dumpling

noun
fruit dessert in pastry
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmplɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʌmplɪŋ / (BrE)
A dessert made by baking fruit, such as apples, wrapped in a layer of pastry.

Examples

Apple dumpling dessert
My grandmother makes a delicious peach dumpling every autumn.
She served warm dumpling with vanilla ice cream for dessert.
The menu featured a cherry dumpling as a house specialty.
