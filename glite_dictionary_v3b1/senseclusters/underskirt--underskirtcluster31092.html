underskirt

noun
undergarment worn under a skirt
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌskɝt / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌskɜːt / (BrE)
A piece of underwear worn underneath a skirt to give fullness or for modesty.

Examples

A underskirt garment
During the era, every woman wore a underskirt beneath her dress for added modesty.
She wore a underskirt to make the dress flare out more elegantly.
The chiffon underskirt was just visible beneath the hem of her gown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: petticoat - a skirt-like undergarment worn under a skirt or dress
