cholecalciferol

noun
vitamin D3; prevents rickets
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊ.lɪˌkæl.səˈfɛr.ɔl / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.lɪˌkæl.sɪˈfɪə.rɒl / (BrE)
a type of vitamin D (vitamin D3), a fat-soluble compound essential for healthy bones and prevention of rickets

Examples

Cholecalciferol vitamin D3
Cholecalciferol is especially important in the prevention and treatment of rickets in children.
Doctors often prescribe cholecalciferol for patients with a vitamin D deficiency.
Many supplements contain cholecalciferol to help maintain healthy vitamin D levels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vitamin D - a group of fat-soluble vitamins including D2 and D3
