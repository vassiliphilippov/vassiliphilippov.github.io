chorale

noun
Protestant hymn tune, esp. Lutheran
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɑl / (AmE), / kəˈrɑːl / (BrE)
a stately hymn tune, especially associated with Protestant (particularly Lutheran) worship.

Examples

A chorale hymn
During the service, the congregation sang a traditional Lutheran chorale.
Many composers have written elaborate arrangements based on the melody of a chorale.
The choir performed a Bach chorale with remarkable precision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hymn - a religious song or poem of praise
