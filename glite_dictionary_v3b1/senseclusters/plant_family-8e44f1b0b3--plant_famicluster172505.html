plant family

noun
group of related plants in taxonomy
Pronunciation: / plænt ˈfæməli / (AmE), / plɑːnt ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a group of related plants classified together as a taxonomic rank, containing one or more genera that share common characteristics

Examples

A plant family group
Cacti are all classified within the plant family Cactaceae.
Scientists discovered a new plant family growing in the remote valley.
The rose is a member of the plant family Rosaceae.

Related Terms

  • synonym: family - a taxonomic group of related organisms ranking above genus and below order
