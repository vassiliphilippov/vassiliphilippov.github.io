autocross

verb
to race in timed course
Pronunciation: / ˈɔtoʊˌkrɔs / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊˌkrɒs / (BrE)
to compete in a timed driving event on a course marked by cones, usually in a parking lot or open area

Examples

To autocross on weekends
Many car enthusiasts autocross on weekends to test their driving skills.
She decided to autocross her new sports car at the local event.
After learning the basics, he was eager to autocross for the first time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: race - to compete in speed against others
  • synonym: rally - to compete in a motorsport event on public or private roads
