iconographic

adjective
relating to pictorial symbols
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪkənəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪkənəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or using pictorial symbols or images to represent information or ideas.

Examples

Iconographic chart symbols
The museum displayed an iconographic chart showing the evolution of ancient writing systems.
Many apps use iconographic buttons to help users navigate the interface easily.
The artist's work is known for its strong iconographic elements that convey deep meaning.
