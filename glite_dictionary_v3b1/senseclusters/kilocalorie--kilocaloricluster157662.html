kilocalorie

noun
unit of energy, used in nutrition
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkɪloʊˈkæləri / (AmE), / ˌkɪləˈkæləri / (BrE)
a unit of heat equal to the amount of energy needed to raise the temperature of one kilogram of water by one degree Celsius, often used to measure the energy content in food

Examples

A kilocalorie unit
A kilocalorie is commonly referred to as a 'Calorie' in nutritional contexts.
Athletes often track their kilocalorie consumption to optimize performance.
Most food packaging lists the kilocalorie content to help consumers manage their energy intake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calorie - unit of energy, often referring to a small calorie or large calorie
