Examples

A kilocalorie unit
A kilocalorie is commonly referred to as a 'Calorie' in nutritional contexts.
Athletes often track their kilocalorie consumption to optimize performance.
Most food packaging lists the kilocalorie content to help consumers manage their energy intake.
