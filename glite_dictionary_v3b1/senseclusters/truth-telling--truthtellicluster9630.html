truth-telling

adjective
honest and revealing
Pronunciation: / ˈtruːθˌtɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtruːθˌtɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
honest or openly revealing the truth, especially by disclosing facts that may be hidden or uncomfortable

Examples

Truth-telling journalist
The journalist's truth-telling report exposed corruption in the local government.
Her truth-telling style made some people uncomfortable, but others admired her honesty.
The film is praised for its truth-telling portrayal of life in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candid - open and honest in expression
  • antonym: deceptive - misleading or dishonest
