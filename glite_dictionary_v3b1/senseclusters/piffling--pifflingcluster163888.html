piffling

adjective
trivial; of little importance
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪf.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪf.lɪŋ / (BrE)
Unimportant; trivial or insignificant, especially to a degree considered not worth mentioning.

Examples

A piffling amount
Compared to their other achievements, this award seemed rather piffling.
The manager dismissed our concerns as piffling and not worth discussing any further.
They spent hours arguing over a piffling detail that barely affected the outcome.

  • synonym: petty - of secondary or minor importance
  • synonym: trivial - of little value or importance
