Delilah

noun
dangerously seductive woman
Pronunciation: / dəˈlaɪlə / (AmE), / dɪˈlaɪlə / (BrE)
a woman who is dangerously seductive and likely to lead men into compromising or damaging situations; a femme fatale.

Examples

A modern Delilah
His downfall was inevitable once he became captivated by a modern Delilah.
In literature, the character is portrayed as a classic Delilah, manipulating those around her with ease.
They warned him not to trust her, calling her a real Delilah.

Related Terms

  • synonym: femme fatale - an alluring and mysterious woman who seduces and brings disaster to men
