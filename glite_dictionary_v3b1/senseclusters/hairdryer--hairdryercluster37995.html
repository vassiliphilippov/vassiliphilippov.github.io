hairdryer

noun
device for drying hair
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌdraɪər / (AmE), / ˈheəˌdraɪə / (BrE)
an electrical device that blows warm air to dry hair quickly after washing

Examples

A hairdryer device
She used a hairdryer to style her hair before leaving for work.
The hotel room was equipped with a hairdryer in the bathroom.
After swimming, he borrowed his sister's hairdryer to dry his hair quickly.
