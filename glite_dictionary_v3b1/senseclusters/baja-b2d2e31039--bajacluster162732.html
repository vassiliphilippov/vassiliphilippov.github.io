Baja

proper noun
Mexican peninsula
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːhɑː / (AmE), / ˈbɑːhɑː / (BrE)
a region and peninsula in northwestern Mexico, commonly known as Baja California

Examples

Baja California peninsula
Many tourists visit Baja for its beautiful beaches and landscapes.
The drive along the Baja peninsula offers stunning views of the Pacific Ocean.
Marine life in Baja attracts divers from around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Baja California - the full name of the peninsula and region in northwestern Mexico
  • synonym: California - a region in the western United States and Mexico, adjacent to Baja
