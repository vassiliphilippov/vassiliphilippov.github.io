steel arch bridge

noun
bridge with an arched steel structure
Pronunciation: / stiːl ɑrtʃ brɪdʒ / (AmE), / stiːl ɑːtʃ brɪdʒ / (BrE)
A bridge made of steel with its main supporting structure shaped in the form of an arch.

Examples

A steel arch bridge
A new steel arch bridge will help ease traffic congestion downtown.
The city is famous for its historic steel arch bridge built in the early 20th century.
The engineers decided to construct a steel arch bridge across the wide river.
