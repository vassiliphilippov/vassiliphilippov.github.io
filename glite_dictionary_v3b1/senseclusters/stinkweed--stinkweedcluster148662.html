stinkweed

noun
a foul-smelling Eurasian weed
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋkˌwid / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkˌwiːd / (BrE)
A foul-smelling Eurasian weed with round, flat pods, now naturalized across North America.

Examples

A stinkweed plant
Even a small patch of stinkweed can fill the air with its unpleasant odor.
Farmers often work hard to remove stinkweed from their fields.
The garden was overrun with stinkweed by mid-summer.
