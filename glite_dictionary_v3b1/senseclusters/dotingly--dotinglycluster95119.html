dotingly

adverb
feebly, senilely
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdoʊ.tɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈdəʊ.tɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)
in a manner that shows mental decline, feebleness, or senility; weakly or foolishly (archaic or literary)

Examples

Gaze dotingly at memories
The old man gazed dotingly at his childhood home, lost in distant memories.
She spoke dotingly, her words trailing off as if distracted by confusion.
He wandered dotingly through the garden, forgetting where he had intended to go.

Related Terms

  • synonym: senilely - in a manner characteristic of old age or senility
  • antonym: vigorously - in a strong and energetic manner
