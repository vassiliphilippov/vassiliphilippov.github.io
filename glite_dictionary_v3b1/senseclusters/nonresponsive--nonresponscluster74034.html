nonresponsive

adjective
not meeting requirements
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈspɑnsɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈspɒnsɪv / (BrE)
failing to meet the essential requirements of a formal request, bid, or application, making it subject to rejection
