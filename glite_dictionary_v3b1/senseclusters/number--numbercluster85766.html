number

noun
grammatical category of singular/plural etc.
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
the grammatical category indicating how many persons, things, or groups are being referred to, such as singular, plural, or dual forms of nouns, pronouns, and verbs

Examples

Singular number
In English, the verb must agree in number with its subject.
Some languages have a dual number used for precisely two items.
The number of the noun determines the correct pronoun to use.
