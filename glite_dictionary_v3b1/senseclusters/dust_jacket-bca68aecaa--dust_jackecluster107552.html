dust jacket

noun
removable printed book cover
Pronunciation: / dʌst ˈdʒækɪt / (AmE), / dʌst ˈdʒakɪt / (BrE)
A removable paper cover for a book, often printed with promotional information and designed to protect the binding.

Examples

A dust jacket cover
Be sure not to tear the dust jacket while handling the new book.
The library removed all the dust jackets to preserve them from damage.
The novel's dust jacket featured an eye-catching illustration.
