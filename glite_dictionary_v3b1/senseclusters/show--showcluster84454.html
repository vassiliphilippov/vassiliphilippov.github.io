Examples

To show a picture
In the photograph, the artist shows the mountain landscape bathed in morning light.
The mural shows historical scenes from the town's past.
The painting shows a group of villagers celebrating the harvest festival.

Synonyms

  • depict
    3.5 35/m
    depict is to draw or paint someone or something in art, while show means to present it visually.
  • portray
    3.7 3.54/m
    portray presents a person or character with mood or style in art, while show simply displays the visual image.
  • picture
    2.5 7.81/m
    picture means to form or make a visual image of someone or scene, while show means to present that image.
  • render
    6.3 9.18/m
    render often means to create or give an artistic or technical image, while show refers to any visual presentation.
  • illustrate
    3.5 49/m
    illustrate uses pictures or diagrams to explain or decorate ideas, while show focuses on simply presenting a visual.
  • represent
    2.6 13/m
    represent describes or stands for something and can be abstract, while show is about directly presenting a clear visual image.
