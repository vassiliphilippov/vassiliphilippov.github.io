lossless

adjective
without any loss of energy or data
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔsləs / (AmE), / ˈlɒsləs / (BrE)
Not producing or involving any loss, especially of energy, data, or information.

Examples

Lossless data transfer
Fiber optic cables are valued for their lossless data transfer over long distances.
Many people prefer lossless audio formats for the best possible sound experience.
The engineer ensured the signal transmission was lossless to prevent any quality degradation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lossy - involving some loss, especially of data or energy
