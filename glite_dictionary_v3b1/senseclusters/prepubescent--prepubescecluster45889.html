prepubescent

adjective
before the onset of puberty
6.0 5.9
Pronunciation: /priː.pjuː.ˈbes.ənt/ /; / /priː.puː.ˈbes.ənt/ (AmE), /priː.pjuː.ˈbes.ənt/ (BrE)
(especially of a person) at or relating to the stage immediately before puberty, often marked by accelerated growth and physical development.

Examples

Prepubescent stage
Her voice was still high and clear, typical of a prepubescent girl.
Many prepubescent boys experience a sudden growth spurt before their voices change.
The study focused on prepubescent children and their patterns of physical activity.
