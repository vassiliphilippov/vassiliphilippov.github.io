slinky

adjective
sleek and seductive
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈslɪŋki / (BrE)
attractively sleek, smooth, and sinuous in movement or appearance, often in a way that is suggestive or seductive.

Examples

A slinky black dress
She wore a slinky black dress that caught everyone's attention at the party.
The cat moved in a slinky manner as it crept across the room.
He admired the slinky design of the new sports car.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsy - awkward or lacking grace in movement
  • synonym: seductive - attractive in a way that suggests a tempting allure
  • synonym: sleek - smooth and glossy in appearance
