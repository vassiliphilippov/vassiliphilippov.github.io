imitatrix

noun
female imitator
Pronunciation: / ˌɪmɪˈteɪtrɪks / (AmE), / ˌɪmɪˈteɪtrɪks / (BrE)
an archaic term for a woman or girl who imitates others

Examples

An imitatrix woman
In Victorian novels, an imitatrix might be admired for her ability to mimic fashionable manners.

Rarely used today, the word imitatrix appears in some classical literature.
