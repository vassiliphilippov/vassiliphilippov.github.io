waterproof jacket

noun
jacket that keeps water out
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔt̬ɚˌpruf ˈdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌpruːf ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
a jacket made of material that does not allow water to pass through, keeping the wearer dry in wet conditions

Examples

A waterproof jacket
A good waterproof jacket is essential for staying dry while hiking in bad weather.
He forgot his waterproof jacket and got soaked during the storm.
She put on her waterproof jacket before heading out into the rain.
