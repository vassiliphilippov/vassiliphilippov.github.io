Synonyms

    5.6 1.58/m

  • get even
    5.4 1.64/m

  • retaliate
    7.3 4.31/m
    retaliate is a formal word for striking back against harm, while get back is more casual and common in speech.
  • avenge
    4.9 2.90/m
    avenge means punish someone for a wrong, often as justice, while get back suggests personal revenge rather than formal justice.
  • pay back
    3.2 0.33/m
    pay back means do harm or repay someone for a wrong in personal terms, while get back is a general phrase for revenge.
  • strike back
    4.9 1.16/m
    strike back means make a strong counterattack after being attacked, while get back is used more broadly for revenge acts.
  • fight back
    3.3 7.42/m

  • settle
