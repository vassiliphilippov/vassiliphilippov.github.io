smoothing plane

noun
hand tool for finishing wood
Pronunciation: / ˈsmuðɪŋ pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈsmuːðɪŋ pleɪn / (BrE)
a small hand tool used in woodworking for finishing and smoothing the surface of wood

Examples

A smoothing plane tool
Every woodworker should have a good smoothing plane in their collection of tools.
He carefully ran the smoothing plane along the grain of the wood to remove fine imperfections.
The carpenter selected a smoothing plane to give the tabletop a perfectly even finish.
