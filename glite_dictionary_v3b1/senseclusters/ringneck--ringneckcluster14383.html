ringneck

noun
animal with neck ring
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋˌnɛk / (BrE)
a bird or animal with a ring-shaped marking around its neck, especially certain pheasants or parrots
