ape

noun
person who imitates others
8.9 8.3
Pronunciation: / eɪp / (AmE), / eɪp / (BrE)
a person who imitates the words or behavior of another, often in an unoriginal or slavish way

Examples

An ape of style
He was dismissed as little more than an ape of greater artists.
She was tired of being called an ape just because she admired her mentor's style.
They accused the journalist of being an ape, simply repeating official statements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imitator - a person who copies the actions or words of another
  • antonym: original - a person who does not copy others; the opposite of an imitator
