black knot

noun
fungal disease of plum and cherry trees
9.5
Pronunciation: / blæk nɑt / (AmE), / blæk nɒt / (BrE)
a fungal disease affecting plum and cherry trees, characterized by black, knot-like swellings on branches and twigs

Examples

Black knot disease
