dipperful

noun
quantity held by dipper
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪpərˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈdɪpəfʊl / (BrE)
the amount that can be held by a dipper

Examples

A dipperful of water
She poured a dipperful of water into the bucket.
The cook added a dipperful of soup to each bowl.
He scooped a dipperful of grain from the barrel for the chickens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cupful - the amount that can be held by a cup
  • synonym: spoonful - the amount that can be held by a spoon
