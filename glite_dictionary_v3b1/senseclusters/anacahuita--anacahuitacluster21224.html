anacahuita

noun
Mexican olive tree
Pronunciation: / ˌænəkəˈwiːtə / (AmE), / ˌænəkəˈwiːtə / (BrE)
a tree native to the Americas, also called the Mexican olive, known for its white flowers and medicinal uses

Examples

The anacahuita tree
The anacahuita is often planted for its attractive white flowers.
Local communities use the leaves of the anacahuita for traditional remedies.
Birds are attracted to the fruit of the anacahuita in the spring.
