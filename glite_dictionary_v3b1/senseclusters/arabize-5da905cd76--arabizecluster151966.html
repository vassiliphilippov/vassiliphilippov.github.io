Arabize

verb
to make Arabic
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈærəˌbaɪz / (AmE), / ˈærəbaɪz / (BrE)
to translate text or adapt something to the Arabic language or culture

Examples

To Arabize a book
The publisher decided to Arabize the popular novel for readers in the Middle East.
Many companies Arabize their websites to reach Arabic-speaking customers.
The museum worked to Arabize its exhibits for the local community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anglicize - to make something English in form or character
  • synonym: translate - to express words or text in another language
