olla podrida

noun
rich Spanish mixed-meat and vegetable stew
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊjə poʊˈdɾidə / (AmE), / ˌɒlə pɒˈdɾiːdə / (BrE)
A rich Spanish stew or casserole made from a mixture of meats, vegetables, and often sausages and spices.

Examples

Traditional olla podrida
I tasted olla podrida on my trip to Spain and loved its hearty flavors.
Many Spanish cookbooks include a recipe for olla podrida as a classic dish.
The chef prepared a traditional olla podrida with pork, chorizo, and beans for the guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burgoo - a thick stew, especially one made with many kinds of meat and vegetables
