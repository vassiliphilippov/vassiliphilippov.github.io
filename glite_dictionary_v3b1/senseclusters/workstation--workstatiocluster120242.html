workstation

noun
cloud-hosted virtual desktop
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈwɜːrk.steɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈwɜːk.steɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a cloud-hosted or remotely accessed virtual desktop environment that provides a workstation-like interface and computing resources without requiring the user to own the physical hardware.

Examples

Cloud workstation access
The design team accessed their cloud workstation from any device while traveling.
Our company rents high-performance workstations in the cloud for remote video editing.
After the laptop failed, she streamed a powerful workstation hosted by the IT department.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remote desktop - a technology or service that allows a user to connect to and control a desktop environment on another machine over a network
  • synonym: virtual desktop - a software-based desktop environment hosted on remote servers and delivered to users over a network
