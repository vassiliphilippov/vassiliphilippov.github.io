irreducible

adjective
impossible to reduce or simplify
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˈduːsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈdjuːsəbl / (BrE)
impossible to make smaller, simpler, or less complex; not able to be reduced further.

Examples

An irreducible problem
Some mathematical expressions are irreducible and cannot be factored any further.
The equation was considered irreducible since it could not be simplified further.
Their demands represented an irreducible minimum in the negotiations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reducible - able to be reduced or simplified
