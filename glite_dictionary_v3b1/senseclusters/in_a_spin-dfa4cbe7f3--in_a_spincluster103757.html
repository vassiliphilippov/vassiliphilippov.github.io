in a spin

adjective
confused or agitated state
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪn ə spɪn / (AmE), / ɪn ə spɪn / (BrE)
feeling confused, disoriented, or agitated, typically due to unexpected events or overwhelming emotions.

Examples

To be in a spin
After reading the complicated instructions, I felt completely in a spin.
He was in a spin after the hectic events of the day.
The sudden announcement left everyone in a spin during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing confusion or agitation
  • synonym: disoriented - unable to think clearly or make sense of things
