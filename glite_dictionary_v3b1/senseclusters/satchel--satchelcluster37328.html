satchel

noun
a small shoulder bag, often for books
Pronunciation: / ˈsætʃəl / (AmE), / ˈsætʃəl / (BrE)
a small bag with a flat bottom and a shoulder strap, used especially for carrying books or personal items

Examples

A satchel bag
He slung his satchel over his shoulder before leaving the house.
She carried her books to school in a brown satchel.
The old leather satchel was filled with letters and photographs.
