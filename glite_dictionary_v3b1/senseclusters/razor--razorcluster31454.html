razor

noun
tool for shaving hair
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪzər / (AmE), / ˈreɪzə / (BrE)
a sharp-edged tool used for shaving hair from the skin, especially the face or body

Examples

A razor blade
Disposable razors are popular for travel and convenience.
He used a razor to carefully shave his beard in the morning.
She bought a new razor with multiple blades for a closer shave.
