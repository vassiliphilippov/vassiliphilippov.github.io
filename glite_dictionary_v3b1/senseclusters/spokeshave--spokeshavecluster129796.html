spokeshave

noun
tool for shaping curved wood surfaces
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈspoʊkˌʃeɪv / (AmE), / ˈspəʊkʃeɪv / (BrE)
a small woodworking tool with a blade and a handle on each side, used for shaping or smoothing rounded or curved wooden surfaces

Examples

A spokeshave tool

The craftsman used a spokeshave to smooth the handle of the chair.
