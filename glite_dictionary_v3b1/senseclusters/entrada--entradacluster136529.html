entrada

noun
starter dish
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈtrɑːdə / (AmE), / ɛnˈtrɑːdə / (BrE)
a small dish served before the main course, especially as a starter in some Spanish-speaking regions

Examples

Vegetable entrada
We shared a delicious entrada of grilled vegetables before the main meal arrived.
The menu offered a variety of entradas, including soups and salads.
He always looks forward to the entrada when dining at Spanish restaurants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appetizer - a small dish served before the main meal
  • antonym: main course - the principal dish in a meal
