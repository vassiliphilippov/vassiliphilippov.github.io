phantasmically

adverb
like a phantom
9.9
Pronunciation: / fænˈtæzmɪkli / (AmE), / fænˈtæzmɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that resembles a phantom or illusion; as if unreal or dreamlike

Examples

Move phantasmically through fog
The figures moved phantasmically through the fog, barely visible to the naked eye.
Colors shifted phantasmically across the walls, creating an otherworldly atmosphere.
The memory returned phantasmically, as if it were only a dream.

Related Terms

  • antonym: realistically - in a way that is true to life or reality
  • synonym: spectrally - in a ghostly or spectral manner
