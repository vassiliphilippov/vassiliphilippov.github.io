inspiratory

adjective
relating to inhalation phase of breathing
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈspɪrəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ɪnˈspɪrəˌtəri / (BrE)
relating to or involving the act of inhaling, particularly the phase of breathing in during respiration.

Examples

Inspiratory phase
A sudden inspiratory gasp can sometimes indicate an underlying respiratory condition.
Researchers measured the inspiratory capacity of each participant during the lung function test.
The doctor listened carefully for inspiratory sounds in the patient's lungs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expiratory - relating to the exhalation phase of breathing
