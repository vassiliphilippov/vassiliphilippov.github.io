anonymize

verb
to remove identifying details
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑːnɪˌmaɪz / (AmE), / əˈnɒnɪˌmaɪz / (BrE)
to remove or hide information from something so that the person involved cannot be recognized or identified

Examples

To anonymize data
The company must anonymize all customer data before sharing it with researchers.
To protect privacy, the report was carefully anonymized before publication.
She used software to anonymize the images by blurring faces and removing names.
Before publishing the research, the team anonymized all patient records to protect their privacy.
The app automatically anonymizes your location data before sharing it with third parties.
To comply with regulations, companies must anonymize sensitive information in their databases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depersonalize - to remove personal characteristics or identity
  • antonym: identify - to recognize or name someone or something
