klop

noun
sharp knocking sound
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / klÉ‘p / (AmE), / klÉ’p / (BrE)
a short, sharp knocking sound, often used to imitate the noise of a knock or tap

Examples

A klop sound
A sudden klop on the window made everyone jump during the quiet afternoon.
He heard a faint klop at the door and went to see who was there.
The horse's hooves made a steady klop as it walked down the street.

Synonyms

  • rap
    2.1 0.90/m
    rap is a quick light knock you make on a surface, while klop imitates a short, sharp tapping sound.
  • knock
    1.3 5.76/m
    knock is the usual sound when you hit a door or machine, while klop is the short, sharp imitation used in writing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knock - a sharp sound made by striking a surface
  • synonym: tap - a light touch or knock producing a sound
