imminently

adverb
very soon; at any moment
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɪm.ə.nənt.li / (AmE), / ˈɪm.ɪ.nənt.li / (BrE)
In a way that is likely to happen very soon; about to occur at any moment.

Examples

To imminently happen
Rescue teams are standing by, as an eruption is imminently possible.
The decision was imminently awaited by all members of the board.
The storm is expected to make landfall imminently along the coast.
