yere

adverb
formerly; before now
Pronunciation: / jiːr / (AmE), / jiːə / (BrE)
at an earlier time; before now (archaic)

Examples

Yere sunrise
He had departed yere the sun had risen.
The castle stood tall yere the city was built around it.
Many traditions were established yere our time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: formerly - at a previous time; in the past
  • antonym: now - at the present time
