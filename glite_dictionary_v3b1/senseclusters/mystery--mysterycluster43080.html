mystery

noun
something hard to explain or understand
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstəri / (AmE), / ˈmɪstəri / (BrE)
something that is difficult or impossible to understand or explain

Examples

A mystery remains
For years, the disappearance of the ship was shrouded in mystery.
It’s a mystery to me how he managed to escape unnoticed.
The cause of the sudden illness remains a mystery to the doctors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enigma - a person or thing that is mysterious or difficult to understand
  • synonym: secret - something kept hidden or unexplained
  • antonym: solution - an answer that explains or makes something clear
