Wintera colorata

noun
New Zealand evergreen shrub or small tree
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪntərə koʊˈlɒrə.tə / (AmE), / ˈwɪntərə kəˈlɒrətə / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub or small tree with foliage blotched red and yellow, producing small black fruits; native to New Zealand, also known as horopito
