clearinghouse

verb
to coordinate distribution
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrɪŋˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈklɪərɪŋˌhaʊs / (BrE)
to act as a central agency that collects, organizes, and redistributes information, payments, or requests for others

Examples

To clearinghouse requests
The organization will clearinghouse all incoming donations and distribute them to local shelters.
Our office is set up to clearinghouse requests from different departments and ensure everyone gets what they need.
The website aims to clearinghouse information about scholarships for students across the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distribute - to give or deliver something to people
  • antonym: hoard - to collect and keep a large amount of something, often secretly
