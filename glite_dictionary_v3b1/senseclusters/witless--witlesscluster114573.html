witless

adjective
lacking sense or intelligence
4.7 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪt.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈwɪt.ləs/ (BrE)
(of a person) lacking sense, intelligence, or good judgment; foolish or stupid.

Examples

Only a witless person would leave their keys in the car with the engine running.
