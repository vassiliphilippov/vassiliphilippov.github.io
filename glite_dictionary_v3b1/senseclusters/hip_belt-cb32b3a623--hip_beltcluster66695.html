hip belt

noun
supportive band for hips
3.3
Pronunciation: / hɪp bɛlt / (AmE), / hɪp bɛlt / (BrE)
a wide supportive band worn around the hips to relieve pain, support pregnancy, or stabilize the lower back and pelvis

Examples

Medical hip belt
The doctor recommended wearing a hip belt to help with lower back pain.
Pregnant women sometimes use a hip belt for extra support during the later months.
After his injury, he found that a hip belt made walking much more comfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brace - a device that supports or holds a body part in place
  • synonym: lumbar support - a device or cushion that supports the lower back
