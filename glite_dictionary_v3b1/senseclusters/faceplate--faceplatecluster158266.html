faceplate

noun
protective covering on front of device
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪsˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈfeɪsˌpleɪt / (BrE)
A protective covering or panel fitted to the front of a device, machine, or component to shield or conceal it.

Examples

The faceplate panel
Dust had accumulated on the computer's faceplate, making it look old.
He removed the faceplate to access the wiring behind the socket.
The new switch comes with a stainless steel faceplate for added durability.

  • synonym: panel - flat piece forming part of a surface
