crazy weed

noun
plant causing locoism in livestock
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪzi wid / (AmE), / ˈkreɪzi wiːd / (BrE)
Any of several leguminous plants of western North America that can cause locoism in livestock when eaten.

Examples

Crazy weed poisoning
Cattle that graze on crazy weed often exhibit symptoms of poisoning.
Several species known as crazy weed are a concern for ranchers in the western United States.
