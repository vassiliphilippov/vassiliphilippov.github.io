water dog

noun
dog trained for retrieving in water
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔt̬ɚ dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə dɒɡ / (BrE)
a dog trained to work in or around water, especially for retrieving waterfowl during hunting

Examples

Water dog retrieves ducks
After the shot, the water dog swiftly dove in to retrieve the bird.
Portuguese water dogs are known for their swimming skills and intelligence.
The hunter relied on his water dog to fetch ducks from the lake.
