undateable

adjective
cannot be dated
7.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈdeɪ.tə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈdeɪ.tə.bəl/ (BrE)
impossible to determine the date or age of something

Examples

Undateable artifact
The ancient manuscript was so damaged that it was considered undateable by the experts.
Without any inscriptions or artifacts, the cave paintings remained undateable for decades.
The origin of the stone tool is undateable due to the lack of organic material.
