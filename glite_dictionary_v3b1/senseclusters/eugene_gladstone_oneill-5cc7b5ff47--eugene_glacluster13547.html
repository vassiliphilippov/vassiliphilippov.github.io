Eugene Gladstone O'Neill

proper noun
American playwright (1888–1953)
Pronunciation: / juːˈdʒin ˈɡlædstoʊn oʊˈniːl / (AmE), / juːˈdʒiːn ˈɡlædstəʊn əʊˈniːl / (BrE)
