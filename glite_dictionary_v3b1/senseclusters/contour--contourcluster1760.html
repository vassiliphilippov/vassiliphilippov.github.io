contour

noun
line joining points of equal height on a map
5.3 11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀên.t är / (AmE), / Ààk…ín.t ä…ô / (BrE)
A line on a map that joins points of equal elevation above a given level, such as sea level.

Examples

Contour lines on a map
A contour can show where the slope of a hill becomes steeper.
Atlantic City is only a few meters above the 10-meter contour along the coast.
The hikers followed the contour lines on the map to avoid steep climbs.

Synonyms

  • contour line
    4.2 0.17/m
    contour line is the full mapping term used on maps and in textbooks to show elevation, while contour is a shorter general term.

Related Terms

  • synonym: isoline - a line on a map connecting points having the same value of a given quantity
