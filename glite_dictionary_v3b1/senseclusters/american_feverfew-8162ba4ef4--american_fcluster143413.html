American feverfew

noun
a North American perennial herb with medicinal use
8.5
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈfiːvərˌfjuː / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈfiːvəˌfjuː / (BrE)
a stout perennial herb of eastern North America, bearing whitish flowers, whose leaves have been traditionally used by Catawba Indians to treat burns
