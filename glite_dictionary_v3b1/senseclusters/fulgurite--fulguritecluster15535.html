fulgurite

noun
glassy lightning tube
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌlɡjəˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈfʌlɡjʊəˌraɪt / (BrE)
a glassy, tube-like formation created when lightning strikes sand or soil and fuses it together

Examples

A fulgurite formation
The museum displayed a rare fulgurite that had formed after a powerful thunderstorm.
Scientists studied the structure of the fulgurite to learn more about the effects of lightning on minerals.
Walking along the beach, she discovered a small fulgurite hidden among the sand dunes.
