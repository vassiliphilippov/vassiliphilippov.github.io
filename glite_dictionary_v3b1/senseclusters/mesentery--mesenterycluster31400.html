mesentery

noun
membrane supporting the intestines
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛzənˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈmɛzəntri / (BrE)
a fold of the peritoneum that attaches the intestine to the posterior wall of the abdomen and supports the small intestines

Examples

The mesentery fold
Inflammation of the mesentery can lead to serious digestive complications.
The mesentery contains blood vessels that supply the small intestine.
The surgeon carefully avoided damaging the mesentery during the abdominal operation.
