water butt

noun
container for storing rainwater outdoors
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər bʌt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə bʌt / (BrE)
a large container placed outside to collect and store rainwater, typically from a roof.

Examples

A water butt outside
