dewberry bush

noun
north American trailing bramble plant
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈduːˌbɛri bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈdjuːˌbɛri bʊʃ / (BrE)
A trailing bramble plant, especially in North America, that produces dewberries, similar to blackberries.

Examples

The dewberry bush fruit
Birds love to eat the ripe fruit from the dewberry bush in our backyard.
Every spring, the dewberry bush near our fence bursts into white blossoms.
We found a dewberry bush growing at the edge of the forest trail.
