metagenic

adjective
alternation of generations
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
involving or relating to alternation of generations, especially in the life cycle of certain organisms

Examples

Metagenic life cycle
The metagenic cycle of some algae includes both haploid and diploid stages.
Scientists studied the metagenic development of jellyfish in the laboratory.
Certain ferns display a metagenic pattern, alternating between two distinct generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alternation of generations - a life cycle with both sexual and asexual phases
