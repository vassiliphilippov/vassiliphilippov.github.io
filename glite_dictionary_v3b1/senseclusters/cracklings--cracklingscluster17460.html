cracklings

noun
crisp residue from rendered fat
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkræk.lɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈkræk.lɪŋz / (BrE)
the crisp, browned bits of pork or other meat left after rendering fat, especially lard.

Examples

Pork cracklings
After rendering the lard, the cooks scooped out the golden cracklings and served them hot.
She saved the cracklings to add crunch to her salads and soups.
The homemade cornbread was topped with crispy cracklings for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pork rinds - fried or roasted skin of pork, often eaten as a snack
