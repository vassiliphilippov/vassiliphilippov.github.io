middle school

noun
school for grades between elementary and high school
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪd.əl skuːl / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.əl skuːl / (BrE)
A school for children between elementary school and high school, typically including grades 6 to 8.

Examples

A middle school student
Many students start learning a second language in middle school.
She transferred to a new middle school after her family moved to the city.
The basketball team won the middle school championship last year.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: elementary school - a school for the first years of formal education, typically grades 1–5 or 1–6
  • degree higher: high school - a school for students in the final grades before college, typically grades 9–12
  • synonym: junior high school - a school for students in grades below high school, often grades 7–9
