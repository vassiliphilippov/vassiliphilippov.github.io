overhead

adjective
situated above; from above
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈhɛd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈhɛd / (BrE)
located or coming from above, especially in relation to a person or object beneath

Examples

Overhead sign
A large sign hung overhead to direct traffic through the station.
The helicopter hovered overhead during the parade.
The lights were installed overhead to brighten the entire room.
