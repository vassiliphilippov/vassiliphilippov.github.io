vampirish

adjective
seductively predatory
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈvæmpɪrɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈvæmpɪrɪʃ / (BrE)
seductively dangerous or predatory in a way that exploits or drains others, like a vampire or femme fatale

Examples

Vampirish charm
Her vampirish charm made it hard for anyone to resist her requests.
The character in the novel had a vampirish allure that both fascinated and frightened the other guests.
He gave her a vampirish smile that hinted at hidden motives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: innocent - not harmful or exploitative
  • synonym: predatory - seeking to exploit or harm others for personal gain
  • synonym: vampy - seductively attractive in a mysterious or dangerous way
