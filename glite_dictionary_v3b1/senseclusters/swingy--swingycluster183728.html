swingy

adjective
lively or buoyant rhythm
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪŋi / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋi / (BrE)
having a lively, rhythmic, or flowing swinging motion (especially in music, movement, or clothing)

Examples

The jazz band's performance had a swingy feel that kept everyone tapping their feet.
She wore a swingy skirt that moved with every step.
The curtains had a swingy look, swaying gently in the breeze.
He liked the swingy rhythm of the new jazz song.
