rhodora

noun
flowering shrub
9.9
Pronunciation: / roʊˈdɔːrə / (AmE), / rəʊˈdɔːrə / (BrE)
a North American shrub with pink or purple flowers, closely related to the rhododendron

Many poets have admired the delicate beauty of the rhodora in their works.
