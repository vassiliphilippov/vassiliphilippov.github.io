cypher

noun
person of no importance or influence
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪfər / (AmE), / ˈsʌɪfə / (BrE)
a person of no importance or influence; a nonentity

Examples

A social cypher
At the meeting, she realized that her opinions were ignored, making her feel like a cypher.
Despite his position, many viewed him as a mere cypher in the company, with no real authority.
The king was a cypher, controlled by powerful advisors behind the throne.
