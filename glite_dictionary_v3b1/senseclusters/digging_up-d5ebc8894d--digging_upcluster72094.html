digging up

noun
the act of unearthing something, esp. a body
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪɡɪŋ ʌp / (AmE), / ˈdɪɡɪŋ ʌp / (BrE)
the act of uncovering something by digging it out of the ground, especially a buried object or corpse

Examples

The digging up of shells
Farmers reported the digging up of unexploded shells from the fields during plowing season.
Police faced criticism for the digging up of the old cemetery without notifying the families.
The digging up of ancient graves revealed new artifacts about the early settlers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhumation - the act of digging up something buried, especially a corpse
