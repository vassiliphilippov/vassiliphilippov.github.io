traineeship

noun
period or program of job training, often with support
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪniˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈtreɪniːʃɪp / (BrE)
a period of training or a program, often with financial support, that prepares someone for a specific job or profession

Examples

Complete a traineeship
After completing his traineeship, he was offered a permanent position at the company.
She applied for a traineeship in medical research at the university hospital.
The government offers traineeships to help young people gain job skills and experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apprenticeship - a position as an apprentice, involving formal training for a trade
  • synonym: internship - a period of work experience offered by an organization, often for students or trainees
