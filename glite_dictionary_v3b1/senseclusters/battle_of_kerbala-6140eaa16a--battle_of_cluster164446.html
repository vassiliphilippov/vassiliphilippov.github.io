Battle of Kerbala

proper noun
battle of 680 CE in Karbala, Iraq
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbætəl əv kərˈbɑːlə / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv kɜːˈbɑːlə / (BrE)
the battle fought in 680 CE near Karbala, Iraq, in which Husayn ibn Ali, the grandson of Muhammad, and his followers were killed by the forces of the Umayyad caliph Yazid I

Examples
