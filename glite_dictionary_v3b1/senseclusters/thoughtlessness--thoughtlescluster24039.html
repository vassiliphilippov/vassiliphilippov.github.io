thoughtlessness

noun
lack of consideration for others
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈθɔt.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of not thinking about other people or the results of your actions; lack of care or consideration

Examples

Thoughtlessness causes problems
Her thoughtlessness led to several misunderstandings among her friends.
His thoughtlessness hurt her feelings more than he realized.
The manager apologized for his thoughtlessness in not thanking the team.
His thoughtlessness often causes problems for those around him.
She regretted her thoughtlessness after realizing how her words had hurt her friend.
The manager was frustrated by the thoughtlessness of employees who ignored basic safety protocols.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carelessness - lack of attention or consideration
  • antonym: thoughtful - showing consideration for others
  • antonym: thoughtfulness - the quality of being considerate and careful in thought
