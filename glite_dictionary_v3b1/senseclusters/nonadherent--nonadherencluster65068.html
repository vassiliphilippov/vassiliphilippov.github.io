nonadherent

adjective
not following rules
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnədˈhɪrənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnədˈhɪərənt / (BrE)
not following or sticking to a prescribed treatment, rule, or required standard

Examples

Nonadherent patients
The doctor was concerned that the patient was nonadherent to the prescribed medication schedule.
Schools may take action if students are repeatedly nonadherent to the dress code.
A nonadherent coating prevents dust from sticking to the surface.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliant - willing to follow rules or instructions
