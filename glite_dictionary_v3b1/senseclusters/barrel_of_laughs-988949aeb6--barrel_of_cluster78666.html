barrel of laughs

noun
very amusing or entertaining person or thing
Pronunciation: / ˈbærəl əv læfs / (AmE), / ˈbarəl əv lɑːfs / (BrE)
someone or something that is very amusing or entertaining; a source of great fun or enjoyment.

Examples

A barrel of laughs
Although the training session was long, the instructor made it a barrel of laughs.
The party last night was a barrel of laughs thanks to all the fun games.
You can always count on Sam to turn any meeting into a barrel of laughs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bore - a person or thing that is dull or uninteresting
  • synonym: riot - someone or something very amusing
