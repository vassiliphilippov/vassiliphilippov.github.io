quercetin

noun
plant antioxidant compound
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɜrsətɪn / (AmE), / ˈkwɜːsɪtɪn / (BrE)
a natural compound found in many fruits and vegetables, often used as a dietary supplement for its antioxidant properties

Examples

Quercetin supplement
Many health supplements contain quercetin because of its potential benefits.
Onions and apples are especially rich in quercetin.
Researchers are studying how quercetin might help reduce inflammation in the body.
