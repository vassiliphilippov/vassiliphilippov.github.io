sparrowhawk

noun
small woodland raptor
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈspæroʊˌhɑk / (AmE), / ˈspærəʊˌhɔːk / (BrE)
a small bird of prey with short wings and a long tail, commonly found in woodlands and known for hunting small birds

Examples

A sparrowhawk hunting
A sparrowhawk soared above the trees, searching for its next meal.
The sparrowhawk is often seen darting quickly through woodland areas.
We spotted a sparrowhawk perched quietly on a branch, watching the smaller birds below.

Related Terms

  • synonym: falcon - a fast-flying bird of prey with long pointed wings
  • synonym: hawk - a bird of prey with sharp talons and keen eyesight
  • antonym: pigeon - a common bird often hunted by birds of prey
