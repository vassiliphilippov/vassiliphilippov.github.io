abjectly

adverb
in a hopeless or resigned way
Pronunciation: / ˈæb.dʒɛkt.li / (AmE), / ˈab.dʒɛkt.li / (BrE)
in a hopeless, resigned, or extremely humble manner

Examples

To abjectly give up
After losing the match, he abjectly slumped into his chair, unable to cheer up.
He abjectly apologized for his mistake, barely able to meet her gaze.
She abjectly accepted the unjust decision without protest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defiantly - in a boldly resistant or challenging manner (opposite nuance)
  • synonym: hopelessly - in a way that shows no hope
