porringer

noun
shallow handled bowl for porridge
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔːrɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈpɒrɪndʒə / (BrE)
a small, shallow bowl, typically with a handle, used for serving porridge or soup

Examples
