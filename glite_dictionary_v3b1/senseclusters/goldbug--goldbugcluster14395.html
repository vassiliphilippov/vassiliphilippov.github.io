goldbug

noun
gold investment supporter
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
a person who strongly supports gold as an investment or as the basis for a country's currency

Examples

Many goldbugs believe that gold is the safest investment in times of crisis.
The financial advisor was known as a goldbug for his strong preference for precious metals.
The economist was known as a goldbug for his advocacy of returning to the gold standard.
Many goldbugs believe that gold is the safest store of value in uncertain times.
During the debate, she was called a goldbug because of her views on monetary policy.
Many goldbugs believe that gold is the best protection against inflation.
During times of economic uncertainty, goldbugs often recommend buying gold.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bear - an investor who believes prices will fall
  • synonym: bull - an investor who believes prices will rise
  • antonym: fiat supporter - a person who supports government-issued currency not backed by a physical commodity
  • synonym: gold investor - a person who invests in gold
  • synonym: gold standard - a monetary system where currency value is based on gold
  • synonym: silverbug - a person who strongly supports the use or investment in silver
  • antonym: silverite - a person who supports the use of silver as currency
