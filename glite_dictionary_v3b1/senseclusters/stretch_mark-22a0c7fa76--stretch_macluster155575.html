stretch mark

noun
line on skin from stretching
2.7
Pronunciation: / stɹɛtʃ mɑɹk / (AmE), / stɹɛtʃ mɑːk / (BrE)
a narrow line or streak on the skin that appears when it has been stretched, often due to rapid growth, weight gain, or pregnancy

Examples

Visible stretch marks
After her pregnancy, she noticed several stretch marks on her abdomen.
Many lotions claim to reduce the appearance of stretch marks, but results may vary.
Rapid weight gain can sometimes cause stretch marks to appear on the thighs or hips.
