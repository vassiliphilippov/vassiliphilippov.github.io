fastener

noun
device that holds or attaches things
Pronunciation: / ˈfæsnər / (AmE), / ˈfɑːsnə / (BrE)
A device or mechanism used to attach or secure two or more objects together or to hold something in place.

Examples

A metal fastener
Many children's shoes have Velcro fasteners instead of laces.
She used a fastener to keep the bag closed during her flight.
The jacket's fastener broke, so he couldn't zip it up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clasp - a device with interlocking parts used for fastening things together
  • synonym: fastening - something that attaches or holds things together
