shine

verb
direct light onto something
5.1
Pronunciation: / ʃaɪn / (AmE), / ʃaɪn / (BrE)
to direct or throw the light from a lamp or other source onto something

Examples

To shine a light
Could you shine a light on this document for me?
She asked him to shine the flashlight under the bed so she could see.
The security guard shone his torch around the dark alley.
