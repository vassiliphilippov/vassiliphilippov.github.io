Synonyms

  • ask
    1.0 939/m
    ask is the everyday verb people use to request information in speech, while inquire sounds more formal and fits written or polite questions.
  • query
    3.7 2.54/m
    query is often a formal or technical question used to express doubt or check data, while inquire is more general information-seeking.
  • question
    1.0 22/m
    question can mean asking or challenging someone, often testing or doubting, while inquire focuses on politely seeking information.
