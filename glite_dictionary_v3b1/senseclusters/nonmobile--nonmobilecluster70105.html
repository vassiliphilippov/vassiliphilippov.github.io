nonmobile

adjective
not movable
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈmoʊbaɪl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈməʊbaɪl / (BrE)
not able to move or be moved easily; stationary or fixed in place

Examples

Nonmobile equipment
The nonmobile machinery was installed in a permanent location in the factory.
Because the safe is nonmobile, it provides extra security against theft.
Some patients become nonmobile after surgery and require special care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immobile - unable to move or be moved
  • antonym: mobile - able to move or be moved easily
  • synonym: stationary - not moving; not intended to be moved
