seahorse

noun
small fish with a horse-shaped head
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsiˌhɔrs / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌhɔːs / (BrE)
a small marine fish with a horse-like head bent downward and a curled tail, swimming upright; belonging to the genus Hippocampus.

Examples

A seahorse fish
A seahorse can grasp objects with its prehensile tail.
The aquarium features a display tank filled with brightly colored seahorses.
Unlike most fish, the seahorse swims in an upright position.
