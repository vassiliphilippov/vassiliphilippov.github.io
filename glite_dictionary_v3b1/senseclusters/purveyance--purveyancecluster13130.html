purveyance

noun
the act of supplying or providing
Pronunciation: / pərˈveɪ.əns / (AmE), / pəˈveɪ.əns / (BrE)
the act of supplying or providing something, especially provisions or necessities

Examples

The purveyance of food
Medieval monarchs often relied on the purveyance of grain and meat for their armies.
The purveyance of fresh water to the soldiers was essential for the success of the mission.
Their company specializes in the purveyance of fine wines and delicacies to high-end hotels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: provision - the act of supplying or providing something, especially food and other necessities
