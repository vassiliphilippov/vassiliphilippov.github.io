neoencephalon

noun
recently evolved part of the brain (cerebral cortex)
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌniː.oʊɛnˈsɛf.əˌlɒn / (AmE), / ˌniː.əʊɛnˈsɛf.əˌlɒn / (BrE)
the part of the brain with the most recent evolutionary development, comprising the cerebral cortex and related structures

Examples

The neoencephalon part
Damage to the neoencephalon can result in significant impairment of reasoning and perception.
Researchers study the neoencephalon to better understand the evolution of complex brain functions.
The neoencephalon is responsible for higher cognitive functions in humans.
