baselessness

noun
lack of foundation
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having no foundation, justification, or support

Examples

The baselessness of claims
The baselessness of his accusations was revealed during the trial.
She was frustrated by the baselessness of the rumors spreading about her.
Critics pointed out the baselessness of the claims made in the article.

Related Terms

  • synonym: groundlessness - the state of lacking a basis or justification
  • antonym: validity - the quality of being logically or factually sound
