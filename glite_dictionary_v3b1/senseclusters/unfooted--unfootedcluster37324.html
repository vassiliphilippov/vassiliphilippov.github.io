unfooted

adjective
without feet or base
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfʊtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfʊtɪd / (BrE)
not having feet or not provided with a base or support

Examples

Unfooted vase
The ancient vase was unfooted and could not stand upright on the shelf.
Without a proper base, the sculpture remained unfooted and unstable.
Some plants are unfooted, lacking any visible roots or anchoring structures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: footed - having feet or a base for support
  • synonym: unsupported - not having support or a base
