lactamase

noun
antibiotic-destroying enzyme
Pronunciation: / ˈlæktəˌmeɪs /; / ˈlæk.təˌmeɪs / (AmE), / ˈlæktəˌmeɪz /; / ˈlæk.təˌmeɪz / (BrE)
an enzyme (usually made by bacteria) that breaks down lactam antibiotics, making them ineffective
