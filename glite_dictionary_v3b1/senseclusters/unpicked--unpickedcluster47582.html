unpicked

verb
past of unpick
5.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɪkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɪkt / (BrE)
past tense and past participle of unpick: to remove stitches from a garment or fabric, undoing sewing.

Examples

She unpicked the hem
She carefully unpicked the seams to alter the dress.
The tailor unpicked the stitching to repair the torn pocket.
After realizing the mistake, he unpicked the entire row of stitches.
