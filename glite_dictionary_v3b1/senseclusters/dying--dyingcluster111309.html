dying

verb
to stop living
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
present participle of 'die', meaning to stop living or cease to exist

Examples

The plant is dying
The old tree in the backyard is dying after years of neglect.
Many people are dying to see the new movie when it comes out.
The tradition is slowly dying as fewer people practice it each year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: living - to be alive or continue to exist
  • synonym: perishing - to die or be destroyed, especially suddenly
