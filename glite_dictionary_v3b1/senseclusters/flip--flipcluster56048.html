flip

verb
toss or turn over quickly in air
1.0 1.6
Pronunciation: /flɪp/ (AmE), /flɪp/ (BrE)
to move, turn, toss, or throw something quickly and lightly, often with a flick of the hand or finger; to activate or change something with a quick motion

He accidentally flipped his phone onto the floor while reaching for it.
Using a spatula, she flipped the pancake to cook the other side.
You should flip the burgers after five minutes on the grill.
