teras

noun
malformed, nonviable fetus (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛr.əs / (AmE), / ˈtɛː.rəs / (BrE)
(medicine) A grossly malformed and usually nonviable fetus.

Examples

Historical medical literature often described cases of teras in the context of early embryological studies.
