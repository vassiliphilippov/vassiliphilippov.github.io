unscalable

adjective
not able to expand
7.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskeɪləbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈskeɪləbəl / (BrE)
not able to be expanded or increased efficiently to handle greater demand or growth

Examples

Unscalable system
The current database is unscalable and cannot support more users as the company grows.
They realized their business model was unscalable after struggling to meet increased demand.
An unscalable solution may work for now, but it will cause problems in the future.

Related Terms

  • antonym: scalable - able to be expanded or increased efficiently
