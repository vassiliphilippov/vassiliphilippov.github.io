boules

noun
French ball game
Pronunciation: / buːlz / (AmE), / buːlz / (BrE)
an outdoor game in which players throw heavy balls as close as possible to a small target ball

Examples

Play boules outdoors
Every Sunday, the locals gather in the park to play boules together.
She learned the rules of boules while visiting southern France last summer.
A friendly match of boules is a common sight in many French villages.
