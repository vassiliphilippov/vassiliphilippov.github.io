businesswoman

noun
a woman engaged in business
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who works in business, especially one with an important position in a company or who owns her own business.

Examples

A businesswoman manages companies
As a successful businesswoman, she manages several companies across different industries.
Many young women look up to her as a pioneering businesswoman in the tech sector.
The keynote speaker was an influential businesswoman known for her innovative leadership.

Related Terms

  • antonym: businessman - a man engaged in commercial or industrial business
