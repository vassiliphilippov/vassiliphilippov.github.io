ergotamine

noun
alkaloid drug for migraines
7.9
Pronunciation: / ərˈɡɑːtəˌmiːn / (AmE), / ɜːˈɡɒtəmiːn / (BrE)
an alkaloid drug derived from ergot, used to constrict blood vessels and treat migraine headaches

Examples

Ergotamine drug treatment
Due to its effectiveness in constricting blood vessels, ergotamine must be used with caution.
Patients are advised to avoid overuse of ergotamine due to potential side effects.
The doctor prescribed ergotamine for her severe migraine attacks.
