nutball

noun
nut-based sweet
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtbɔl / (AmE), / ˈnʌtbɔːl / (BrE)
a small, round sweet or confection made with nuts and usually held together with sugar, chocolate, or syrup

Examples

Homemade nutballs
She made a batch of nutballs for the holiday party, using almonds and honey.
The bakery displayed several varieties of nutball in the front window.
After dinner, he enjoyed a chocolate-covered nutball with his coffee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bonbon - a small sweet or candy, often with a filling
  • synonym: truffle - a small, round chocolate confection, sometimes with nuts
