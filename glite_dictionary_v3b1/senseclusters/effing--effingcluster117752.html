effing

adverb
slang intensifier
Pronunciation: / ˈɛfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛfɪŋ / (BrE)
used as a slang intensifier to emphasize a statement, often as a mild substitute for a strong swear word

Examples

An effing mess
That was an effing good movie, and I would watch it again.
I can't believe how effing cold it is outside this morning.
She was effing tired after working a double shift at the restaurant.
