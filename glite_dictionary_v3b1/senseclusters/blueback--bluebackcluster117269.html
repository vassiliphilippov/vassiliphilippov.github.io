blueback

noun
fish with blue back
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌbæk / (BrE)
a fish with a blue-colored back, or less commonly, a book with a blue cover

Examples

A large blueback fish

Researchers studied the migration patterns of the blueback in the river.
She placed the old blueback on the shelf with her other favorite books.
