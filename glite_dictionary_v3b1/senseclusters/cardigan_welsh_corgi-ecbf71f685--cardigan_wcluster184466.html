Cardigan Welsh corgi

noun
a breed of Welsh herding dog with long tail
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrdɪɡən wɛlʃ ˈkɔːrɡi / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdɪɡən wɛlʃ ˈkɔːɡi / (BrE)
a breed of small herding dog from Wales, characterized by rounded ears, a long tail, and a sturdy, slightly bowlegged build

Examples

A Cardigan Welsh corgi dog
She adopted a Cardigan Welsh corgi because she loved its long fluffy tail.
The Cardigan Welsh corgi is known for its intelligence and affectionate nature.
Unlike its cousin, the Cardigan Welsh corgi has rounded ears and a more robust frame.
