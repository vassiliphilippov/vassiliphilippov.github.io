airbrushing

noun
spray painting technique
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌbrʌʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈeəˌbrʌʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the technique of applying paint or pigment to a surface by spraying it from an airbrush, often for artistic or decorative purposes

Examples

Artistic airbrushing
The artist used airbrushing to create smooth color transitions on the canvas.
Model car enthusiasts often rely on airbrushing for detailed and realistic finishes.
Magazine covers sometimes use airbrushing to enhance the appearance of photographs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hand painting - applying paint to a surface using a brush by hand
  • synonym: spray painting - applying paint by spraying it onto a surface
