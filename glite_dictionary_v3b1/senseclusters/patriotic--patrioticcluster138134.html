patriotic

adjective
showing love and loyalty to one's country
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪtriˈɑtɪk / (AmE), / ˌpatriˈɒtɪk / (BrE)
having or expressing strong support for and loyalty to one's country

Examples

Patriotic spirit
Her patriotic spirit inspired others to volunteer for community service.
Many display their country's flag on holidays as a patriotic gesture.
The crowd erupted in cheers when the patriotic anthem began to play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nationalistic - having strong identification with one's own nation and support for its interests
  • antonym: unpatriotic - not showing love or loyalty to one's country
