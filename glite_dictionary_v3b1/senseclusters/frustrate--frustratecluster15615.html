frustrate

verb
to cause annoyance
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌstreɪt / (AmE), / ˈfrʌstreɪt / (BrE)
to make someone feel upset or annoyed because they cannot do or achieve something

Examples

To frustrate people
It can frustrate students when homework is unclear.
Long delays often frustrate travelers at the airport.
She was frustrated by her inability to solve the puzzle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annoy - to make someone slightly angry or irritated
  • antonym: satisfy - to make someone pleased by giving them what they want
