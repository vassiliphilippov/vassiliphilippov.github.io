prevent erosion

verb
stop soil or land from being worn away
5.2
Pronunciation: / priˈvɛnt ɪˈroʊʒən / (AmE), / prɪˈvɛnt ɪˈrəʊʒən / (BrE)
to stop soil or land from being worn away by natural forces such as wind or water

Examples

To prevent erosion naturally
Farmers plant cover crops to prevent erosion on their fields during the winter months.
