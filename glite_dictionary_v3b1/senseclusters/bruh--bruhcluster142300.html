bruh

noun
informal address
9.7
Pronunciation: / brʌ / (AmE), / brʌ / (BrE)
an informal term used to address or refer to a friend, peer, or acquaintance, similar to 'dude' or 'bro'

Examples

Hey bruh
Hey bruh, are you coming to the game tonight?
I can't believe you forgot your keys again, bruh.
When bruh walked in, everyone started laughing at his joke.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bro - an informal term for a male friend or brother
  • synonym: dude - an informal term for a man or friend
