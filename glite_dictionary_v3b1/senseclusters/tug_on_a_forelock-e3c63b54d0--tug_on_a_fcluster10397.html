tug (on) a forelock

verb
gesture or act of showing respect or submission
9.7 8.7
Pronunciation: /tʌg ə ˈfɔːr.lɑːk/ /; / /tʌg ɑːn ə ˈfɔːr.lɑːk/ (AmE), /tʌg ə ˈfɔː.lɒk/ /; / /tʌg ɒn ə ˈfɔː.lɒk/ (BrE)
to show respect or submission, originally by making a gesture of tugging the forelock of one's hair as a sign of deference

Examples

To tug a forelock respectfully
He seemed reluctant to tug his forelock to the new manager, preferring to speak his mind.
People in the village were expected to tug their forelock when the squire passed by.
She refused to tug on her forelock just to please those in authority.
