hindcast

noun
past weather simulation
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhaɪnd.kæst/ (AmE), /ˈhaɪnd.kɑːst/ (BrE)

Examples

A detailed hindcast can reveal how ocean currents behaved during past storms.
