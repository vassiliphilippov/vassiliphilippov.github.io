woah

interjection
command to stop
8.3
Pronunciation: / woʊ / (AmE), / wəʊ / (BrE)
used to tell someone or something to stop, slow down, or pause suddenly

Examples

Woah, stop the horse
He pulled on the reins and shouted woah to stop the horse before the fence.
When the car started rolling downhill, she yelled woah to get everyone's attention.
The coach called out woah as the players rushed too quickly onto the field.

Related Terms

  • antonym: go - to move or proceed
  • synonym: whoa - used to command someone or something to stop or slow down
