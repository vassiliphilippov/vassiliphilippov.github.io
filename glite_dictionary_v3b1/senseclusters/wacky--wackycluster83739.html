wacky

adjective
amusingly odd or eccentric
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwæki / (AmE), / ˈwæki / (BrE)
amusingly odd, silly, or eccentric in a way that is funny or strange

Examples

A wacky idea
She's always coming up with wacky ideas for our office parties.
That science fiction movie had a really wacky plot twist near the end.
The comedian is known for his wacky sense of humor and wild costumes.
Everyone enjoyed his wacky sense of humor at the party.
She came up with a wacky plan to celebrate her birthday in space.
The film is known for its wacky characters and bizarre plot twists.

  • antonym: serious - solemn or thoughtful in character or manner; not silly or wacky
  • synonym: silly - showing lack of good sense or judgment; absurd or foolish
