prep

noun
preparatory school
5.2
Pronunciation: / prɛp / (AmE), / prɛp / (BrE)
a preparatory school, especially a private secondary school, or a student who attends such a school

Examples

A top prep school
He attended a prestigious prep before going to university.
Many students at the prep participate in sports and academic clubs.
The prep offers advanced courses to help students prepare for college.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preparatory school - a school that prepares students for higher education
  • antonym: public school - a school that is funded and run by the government
