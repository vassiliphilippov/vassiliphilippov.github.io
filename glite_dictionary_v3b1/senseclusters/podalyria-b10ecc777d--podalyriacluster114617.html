Podalyria

noun
genus of South African shrubs
9.9
Pronunciation: / poʊˈdælərɪə / (AmE), / pəʊˈdalɪrɪə / (BrE)
A genus of South African leguminous shrubs, often included in the genus Sophora.

Examples

Botanists debated whether Podalyria should remain a distinct genus or be merged with Sophora.
