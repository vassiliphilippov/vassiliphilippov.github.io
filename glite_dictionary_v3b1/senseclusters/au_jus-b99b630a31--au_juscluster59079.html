au jus

adjective
served with natural juices (culinary)
7.5 6.8
Pronunciation: / oʊ ˈʒu / (AmE), / əʊ ˈʒuː / (BrE)
(of cooked meat) served with its natural juices or gravy.

Examples

Beef au jus
For a classic French dip sandwich, serve the beef au jus for dipping.
She prefers her roast pork au jus rather than with thick gravy.
The prime rib was brought out au jus, adding a rich flavor to the meal.
