in arrears

adverb
late with payments; money owed
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɪn əˈrɪrz / (AmE), / ɪn əˈrɪəz / (BrE)
owing money that should have been paid earlier; late in making regular payments

Examples

Rent in arrears
If your rent is in arrears, you may risk eviction.
The company was in arrears on its utility bills for several months.
Their mortgage is in arrears and the bank has sent them a warning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: behind - late in payments or progress
  • synonym: in debt - owing money to someone
  • antonym: solvent - able to pay debts; not owing money
