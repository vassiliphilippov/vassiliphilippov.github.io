viscose rayon

noun
rayon fabric made from viscose fibers
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪsˌkoʊs ˈreɪˌɑn / (AmE), / ˈvɪskəʊs ˈreɪɒn / (BrE)
a type of rayon fabric made from viscose (cellulose xanthate) fibers, known for its silk-like feel and drape

Examples

Viscose rayon fabric
Manufacturers often use viscose rayon for lightweight, breathable summer clothing.
She prefers viscose rayon garments for their comfort and elegant drape.
The dress was made of viscose rayon, giving it a smooth and luxurious feel.
