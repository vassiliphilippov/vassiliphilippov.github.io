theater of operations

noun
military area of active operations
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈθiːətər əv ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃənz /; / ˈθiət̬ɚ əv ˌɑpəˈreɪʃənz / (AmE), / ˈθɪətə əv ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənz / (BrE)
a region or area where active military action takes place during a conflict
