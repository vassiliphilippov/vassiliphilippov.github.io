Synonyms

    1.0 96/m

  • play the role of

  • portray
    3.7 9.34/m
    portray means to show a character's nature or feelings in a performance or art, while act focuses on performing actions.
  • represent
    3.9 20/m
    represent means to play or stand for a character in a performance, while act is the basic verb for performing roles.
  • enact
    4.8 3.21/m
    enact means to act out a scene or idea, often with clear movement or ritual, while act is the actor's basic performance.
  • roleplay
    4.7 0.14/m
    roleplay means to practice or pretend a character in training or games, while act usually means performing in a real production.
  • playact
    6.7 0.15/m
    playact means to act in an exaggerated or showy way on stage, while act is the neutral word for performing a role.
  • appear
    2.1 16/m
    appear means to take part or show up in a play or film, while act specifically means to perform a dramatic role.
  • play a part
    2.5 2.50/m
    play a part means to take on a role or help achieve something, while act refers specifically to performing as an actor.
