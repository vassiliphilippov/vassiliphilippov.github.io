rivet

verb
to completely hold attention
8.3 5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvɪt /; / ˈrɪvət / (AmE), / ˈrɪvɪt / (BrE)
to attract and hold someone's complete attention; to captivate

Examples

Rivet attention
Children sat in a circle, their eyes riveted on the magician's every move.
Her performance was so powerful, it riveted everyone to their seats until the final bow.
The speaker's compelling story riveted the entire audience for over an hour.
As soon as the movie began, all the children's eyes were riveted to the screen.
Her explanation riveted everyone's attention and no one dared to interrupt.
The speaker's powerful words riveted the audience throughout the entire presentation.
