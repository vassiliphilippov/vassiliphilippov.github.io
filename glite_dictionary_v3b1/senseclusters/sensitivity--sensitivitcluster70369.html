Synonyms

  • sensitiveness
    6.8 0.02/m
    sensitiveness detects very small physical changes in people or instruments, while sensitivity names the general bodily ability to respond to stimuli.
  • sensibility
    6.6 1.07/m
    sensibility is a slightly old-fashioned or literary word for perception and feeling, while sensitivity is the everyday bodily response to stimuli.
  • acuteness
    6.5 0.01/m
    acuteness describes a very sharp, finely tuned sense, while sensitivity is the general ability to notice external stimuli.
