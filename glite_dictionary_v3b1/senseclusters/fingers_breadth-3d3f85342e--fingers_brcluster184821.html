finger's breadth

noun
width of a finger (as a measure)
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡərz brɛdθ / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəz brɛdθ / (BrE)
a unit of linear measurement equal to the width of a finger, used informally or historically

Examples

A finger's breadth forward
He moved the chess piece a finger's breadth forward on the board.
She pulled the curtain aside by a finger's breadth to peek outside.
The river had risen only a finger's breadth overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inch - a standard unit of length equal to 2.54 centimeters
