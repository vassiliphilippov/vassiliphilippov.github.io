tax bill

noun
statement of money owed for taxes
Pronunciation: / tæks bɪl / (AmE), / tæks bɪl / (BrE)
a statement showing the amount of money owed in taxes by an individual or organization

Examples

Pay the tax bill
If you do not pay your tax bill on time, you may incur penalties.
She was surprised by the high amount listed on her annual tax bill.
The accountant helped me understand the details of my tax bill this year.
