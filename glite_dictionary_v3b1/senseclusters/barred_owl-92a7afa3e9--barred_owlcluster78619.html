barred owl

noun
large North American owl with brown streaks
5.2
Pronunciation: / bɑrd ˈaʊl / (AmE), / bɑːd ˈaʊl / (BrE)
a large owl of eastern North America with brown streaks on its breast and abdomen

Examples

A barred owl flying
A barred owl glided silently between the trees at dusk.
The call of the barred owl is often described as sounding like "Who cooks for you?"
We saw a barred owl perched on a low branch in the forest.
