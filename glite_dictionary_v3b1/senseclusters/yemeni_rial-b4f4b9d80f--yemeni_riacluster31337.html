Yemeni rial

noun
currency unit of Yemen
Pronunciation: / jəˈmɛni riˈɑl / (AmE), / jɛˈmiːni riˈɑːl / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Yemen, equal to 100 fils

Examples

500 Yemeni rials
He paid 500 Yemeni rials for the souvenirs at the market.
The exchange rate for the Yemeni rial fluctuated sharply after the economic crisis.
Tourists found it difficult to convert dollars directly to the Yemeni rial.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: fils - a monetary subunit of various Arab countries, including Yemen (one hundred of which make up a rial)
