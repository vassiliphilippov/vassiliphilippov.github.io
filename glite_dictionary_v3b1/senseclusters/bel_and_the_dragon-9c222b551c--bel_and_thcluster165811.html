Bel and the Dragon

proper noun
Apocryphal addition to Book of Daniel
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɛl ənd ðə ˈdræɡən / (AmE), / bɛl ənd ðə ˈdræɡən / (BrE)
An Apocryphal book added to the Book of Daniel, consisting of three separate narratives: that of Bel, the dragon, and Daniel in the lion's den.

Bel and the Dragon story
In reading Bel and the Dragon, one encounters legendary tales of Daniel's wisdom.

The story of Bel and the Dragon explores themes of faith and divine protection.
