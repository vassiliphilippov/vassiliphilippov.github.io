small-capitalization

adjective
stocks of companies with small market cap
4.2
Pronunciation: / smɔl ˌkæpɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / smɔːl ˌkæpɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
(of stocks) relating to companies with a market capitalization of less than one billion dollars.
