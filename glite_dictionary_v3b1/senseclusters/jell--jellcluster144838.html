jell

verb
to become definite
Pronunciation: / dʒɛl / (AmE), / dʒɛl / (BrE)
to become clear, definite, or to take shape; to set or solidify, especially in ideas or plans

Examples

Plans jell together
After weeks of discussion, their ideas finally began to jell into a workable plan.
It took some time for the new team to jell, but now they work well together.
The details of the project will jell as we gather more information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crystallize - to become clear or take a definite form
  • antonym: dissolve - to disappear or become less clear
  • synonym: solidify - to make or become solid or definite
