jell

verb
to become definite
5.1
Pronunciation: /dʒel/ (AmE), /dʒel/ (BrE)
to become clear, definite, or to take shape; to set or solidify, especially in ideas or plans

Examples

It took some time for the new team to jell, but now they work well together.
The details of the project will jell as we gather more information.
