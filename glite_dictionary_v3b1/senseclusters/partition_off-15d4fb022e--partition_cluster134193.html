partition off

verb
divide an area into parts
Pronunciation: / pɑrˈtɪʃən ɔf / (AmE), / pɑːˈtɪʃən ɒf / (BrE)
to divide an area or space into separate parts using partitions or barriers

Examples

To partition off a room
Heavy curtains were used to partition off the dining area from the rest of the hall.
They decided to partition off the large office to create two meeting rooms.
To save on heating costs, the warehouse was partitioned off into smaller spaces.

  • synonym: section - to divide something into sections
