glamorization

noun
making something appear more attractive
Pronunciation: / ˌɡlæmɚəˈzeɪʃən /; / ɡlæmɚaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɡlæmərʌɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌɡlæməraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something or someone appear more attractive or exciting, often in a superficial or unrealistic way
