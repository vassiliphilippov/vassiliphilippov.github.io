parasol

noun
light handheld sunshade
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpærəˌsɔl / (AmE), / ˈpærəsɒl / (BrE)
A light, usually small, handheld umbrella used to provide shade from the sun.

Examples

A parasol on beach
On the beach, each chair was shaded by a colorful parasol.
She carried a delicate parasol to protect herself from the midday heat.
Victorian ladies were often seen strolling with a lace parasol in hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: umbrella - a device for protection against rain or sun, typically collapsible
