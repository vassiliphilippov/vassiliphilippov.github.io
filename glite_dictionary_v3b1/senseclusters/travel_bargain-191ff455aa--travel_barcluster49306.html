travel bargain

noun
discounted deal for travelers
Pronunciation: / ˈtrævəl ˈbɑrɡən / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl ˈbɑːɡɪn / (BrE)
a special low price or deal offered to travelers, often on commercial transportation such as airlines

Examples

Cheap travel bargain
Many students wait for travel bargains before planning their vacations abroad.
The airline advertised a new travel bargain for off-season flights to Europe.
We found a fantastic travel bargain online and booked our trip immediately.
