intercity

noun
city-to-city transport
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈsɪti / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈsɪti / (BrE)
a long-distance train or bus service that travels between two or more cities

Examples

The intercity arrives
She caught the early morning intercity to visit her family in the capital.
The intercity offers comfortable seating and free Wi-Fi for long journeys.
Many commuters prefer the intercity for its speed and convenience between major cities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: express - a fast train or bus that makes few stops
  • antonym: local - a train or bus that stops at all or most stations
