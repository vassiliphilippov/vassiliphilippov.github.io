Physostigma venenosum

noun
African vine producing Calabar beans
Pronunciation: / faɪ.səˈstɪɡ.mə vəˈniː.nə.səm / (AmE), / fɪ.səˈstɪɡ.mə vɛˈnɛn.ə.səm / (BrE)
A tropical African woody vine producing Calabar beans, which contain the toxic compound physostigmine.

Examples

Physostigma venenosum plant
Physostigma venenosum is native to the tropical regions of West Africa.
Scientists extract physostigmine from the seeds of Physostigma venenosum for use in medicine.
