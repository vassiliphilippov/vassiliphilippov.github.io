lineless

adjective
without a lining
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪnləs / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnləs / (BrE)
not having a lining; without an inner layer of fabric or material inside a garment, container, or other object

Examples

Lineless dress
She preferred a lineless dress for the hot summer days.
The artist used a lineless notebook to sketch freely without distractions.
He bought a lineless bag because it was lighter and easier to fold.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lined - having an inner layer of fabric or material
  • synonym: unlined - not having a lining inside
