atherosclerotic

adjective
relating to atherosclerosis
Pronunciation: / ˌæθəroʊskləˈrɑːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌæθərəʊskləˈrɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by atherosclerosis, a disease in which plaque builds up inside arteries.

Examples

Atherosclerotic plaque
An atherosclerotic plaque can restrict blood flow and increase the risk of a heart attack.
Researchers are developing new treatments to slow atherosclerotic progression in at-risk populations.
The doctor explained that the patient's heart problems were caused by atherosclerotic changes in the arteries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atherosclerosis - disease involving plaque buildup in arteries
