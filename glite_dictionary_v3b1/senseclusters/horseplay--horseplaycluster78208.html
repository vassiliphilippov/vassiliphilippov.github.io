horseplay

noun
rough or boisterous play
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌpleɪ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsˌpleɪ / (BrE)
rough, energetic, or boisterous play, usually intended for fun but lacking restraint

Examples

Stop horseplay now
Please stop the horseplay before someone gets hurt.
The teachers warned the students that horseplay in the hallway could lead to injuries.
There was a lot of laughter and horseplay at the summer camp.
