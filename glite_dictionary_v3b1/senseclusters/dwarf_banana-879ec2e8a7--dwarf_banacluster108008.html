dwarf banana

noun
short banana tree with edible fruit
7.5
Pronunciation: / dwɔrf bəˈnænə / (AmE), / dwɔːf bəˈnænə / (BrE)
A low-growing variety of banana tree, especially cultivated in the West Indies for its clusters of edible yellow fruit.

Examples

A dwarf banana plant
Farmers in the Caribbean often grow dwarf banana plants for their abundant harvests.
The dwarf banana is popular in gardens because it remains compact yet produces sweet fruit.
Unlike taller varieties, the dwarf banana can thrive in smaller spaces and home gardens.
