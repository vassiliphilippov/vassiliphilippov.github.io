struggles of immigrants

noun
challenges experienced by immigrants
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstrʌɡəlz əv ˈɪmɪɡrənts / (AmE), / ˈstrʌɡlz əv ˈɪmɪɡrənts / (BrE)
