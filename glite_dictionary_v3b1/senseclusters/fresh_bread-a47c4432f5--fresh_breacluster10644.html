fresh bread

noun
recently baked bread
1.0
Pronunciation: / frɛʃ brɛd / (AmE), / frɛʃ brɛd / (BrE)
Bread that has been recently baked and has not become stale.

Examples

A loaf of fresh bread
She bought fresh bread from the bakery every morning before work.
The aroma of fresh bread filled the kitchen as it cooled on the counter.
We enjoyed fresh bread with olive oil and herbs at the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stale bread - bread that is old and no longer fresh
