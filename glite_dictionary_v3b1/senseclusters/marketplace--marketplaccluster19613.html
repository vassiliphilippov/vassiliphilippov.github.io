marketplace

noun
sphere of buying and selling activities
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkɪtˌpleɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪtˌpleɪs / (BrE)
the world or sphere of commercial activity where goods and services are bought and sold, often involving competition between businesses
