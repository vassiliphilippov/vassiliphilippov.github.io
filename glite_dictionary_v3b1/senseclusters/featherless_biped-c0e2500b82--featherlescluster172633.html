featherless biped

noun
humorous term for a human
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛðərləs ˈbaɪpɛd / (AmE), / ˈfɛðələs ˈbaɪpɛd / (BrE)
a humorous or philosophical term for a human being, referencing the fact that humans walk on two legs and lack feathers

Examples

A featherless biped
He jokingly described himself as a featherless biped during the debate on human nature.
In the cartoon, aliens labeled the Earthlings as curious featherless bipeds.
The philosopher cleverly reduced the human to a featherless biped in his argument.
