pitcherful

noun
the amount that fills a pitcher
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtʃɚ.fʊl / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃə.fʊl / (BrE)
An amount of liquid or other substance that fills a pitcher.

Examples

A pitcherful of water
After the game, the team shared a pitcherful of cold water.
She poured a pitcherful of iced tea for the picnic.
The recipe calls for one pitcherful of lemonade to serve the guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cupful - the amount that fills a cup
  • synonym: jugful - the amount that fills a jug
