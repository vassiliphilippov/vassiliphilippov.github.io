flamboyance

noun
extravagant or showy style or behavior
7.1
Pronunciation: / flæmˈbɔɪəns / (AmE), / flamˈbɔɪəns / (BrE)
the quality of being strikingly elaborate, showy, or extravagant in style, manner, or appearance

Examples

Typical flamboyance style
He delivered his speech with typical flamboyance, captivating everyone in the audience.
Her dress was the epitome of flamboyance, with feathers and sparkling jewels.
The restaurant's décor oozes flamboyance, from golden chandeliers to velvet curtains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extravagance - lack of restraint in spending money or use of resources; excessive elaborateness
  • antonym: modesty - the quality of being unassuming or moderate in estimation of one's abilities; opposite of showiness
