Tartar

noun
member of Mongolian people of central Asia
9.9 0.39/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…ërt…ôr / (AmE), / Ààt…ëÀêt…ô / (BrE)
a member of a group of Mongolian peoples of central Asia, especially those who invaded parts of Russia and Eastern Europe in the 13th century

Examples

Tartar invaders

Some Tartar descendants still live in regions of Central Asia today.
The Tartars swept across the Russian steppes during the 13th century.
