AirPlay

verb
to stream wirelessly
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌpleɪ / (AmE), / ˈeəˌpleɪ / (BrE)
to wirelessly stream audio or video from one device to another using Apple's AirPlay technology

Examples

To AirPlay a video
You can AirPlay your favorite movie from your iPhone to the TV for everyone to watch.
He often AirPlays music to the wireless speakers in his kitchen while cooking.
They AirPlayed the presentation slides to the conference room projector during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cast - to send media from one device to another wirelessly
  • synonym: stream - to transmit audio or video over a network in real time
