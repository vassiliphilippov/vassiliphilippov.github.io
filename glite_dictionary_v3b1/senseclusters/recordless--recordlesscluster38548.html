recordless

adjective
without documentation
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkərdləs / (AmE), / ˈrɛkɔːdləs / (BrE)
having no written, official, or documented record

Examples

Recordless history
The ancient ceremony remained recordless, passed down only through oral tradition.
Many minor incidents in history are recordless and impossible to verify.
Because the meeting was recordless, no one could confirm what was discussed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: documented - having written or official records
  • synonym: unrecorded - not written down or officially documented
