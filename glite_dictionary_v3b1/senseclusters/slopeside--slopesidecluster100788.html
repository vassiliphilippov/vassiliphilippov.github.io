slopeside

adjective
beside ski slope
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsloʊpˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsləʊpˌsaɪd / (BrE)
located directly beside or on a ski slope, offering immediate access to skiing

Examples

Slopeside hotel
We enjoyed our slopeside hotel because we could ski right out the door each morning.
A slopeside cabin is perfect for families who want quick access to the lifts.
The resort offers several slopeside restaurants with beautiful mountain views.
