imperialization

noun
process of empire-building
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌpɪriəlɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪmˌpɪəriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of extending a country's power and influence through colonization or empire-building

Examples

Imperialization of a country
The imperialization of Africa in the nineteenth century had lasting effects on its societies.
Many historians debate the motives behind the imperialization of distant territories.
Resistance movements often arose in response to the imperialization of local lands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colonization - the act of establishing control over foreign lands and peoples
  • antonym: decolonization - the process of a colony gaining independence from a colonial power
