spheroid

noun
three-dimensional cell culture model
Pronunciation: / c8sf6arcc546ad / (AmE), / c8sf6a59.r546ad / (BrE)
(biology) a three-dimensional cluster of cells grown in vitro that resembles a sphere and is used as a model of tissue or tumor microenvironments.

Examples

A cell spheroid
Researchers cultured a cancer cell line as a spheroid to study drug penetration.
The lab technician placed several spheroids into separate wells to compare growth under different conditions.
Using a 3D model, students observed how cancer cells in a spheroid respond differently than flat cell layers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: 3D cell culture - a method of growing cells in three dimensions rather than flat monolayers, used to model tissues
