rock sea bass

noun
a species of sea bass inhabiting rocky coasts
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑk si ˌbæs / (AmE), / ˈrɒk si ˌbæs / (BrE)
a small species of sea bass found in rocky coastal waters of the western Atlantic, known for its robust body and dark coloration

Examples

The diver spotted a rock sea bass hiding among the stones on the ocean floor.
