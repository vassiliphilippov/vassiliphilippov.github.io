chunkiness

noun
thickness or bulkiness
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌŋkinəs / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌŋkinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being thick, bulky, or containing large pieces

Examples

Soup chunkiness
The chunkiness of the soup made it very filling.
She liked the chunkiness of the knit sweater because it felt warm and cozy.
The chunkiness of the salsa added a satisfying texture to the dish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of being even or without lumps
  • synonym: thickness - the quality of being thick
