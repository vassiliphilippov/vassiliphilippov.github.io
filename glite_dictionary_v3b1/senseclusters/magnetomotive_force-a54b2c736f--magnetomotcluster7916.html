magnetomotive force

noun
force that creates magnetic flux
7.4
Pronunciation: /mæɡ.nə.toʊ.ˈmoʊ.tɪv fɔːrs/ (AmE), /mæɡ.nə.tə.ˈməʊ.tɪv fɔːs/ (BrE)
A force that produces a magnetic flux in a magnetic circuit, analogous to electromotive force in an electric circuit.

Examples

The magnetomotive force value
Calculating the required magnetomotive force is essential in transformer design.
The magnetomotive force generated by the coil drives the magnetic flux through the core.
The efficiency of the machine depends on the magnetomotive force applied to the magnetic circuit.
