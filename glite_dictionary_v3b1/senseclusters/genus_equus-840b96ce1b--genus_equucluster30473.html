genus Equus

noun
biological genus of horses, zebras, and donkeys
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈiːkwəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈiːkwəs / (BrE)
A genus of mammals in the family Equidae, including horses, zebras, and donkeys, and the only surviving genus of the family.
