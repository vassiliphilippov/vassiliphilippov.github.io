aneurysmal

adjective
pertaining to an aneurysm
6.1
Pronunciation: / æn.jəˈrɪz.məl / (AmE), / æn.jʊˈrɪz.məl / (BrE)
relating to or affected by an aneurysm.

Examples

Aneurysmal blood vessels
Radiology revealed multiple aneurysmal segments in the cerebral vessels.
Surgical intervention was required due to the aneurysmal changes in the artery wall.
The patient was diagnosed with an aneurysmal dilation of the aorta.
