Chesapeake

proper noun
Michener novel
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛsəˌpiːk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛsəpiːk / (BrE)
a 1978 historical novel by James A. Michener that explores the people and history of the Chesapeake Bay region in the United States

She read Chesapeake over the summer and was fascinated by its depiction of American history.
