unresolved

adjective
not settled or solved
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈzɑlvd /; / ˌʌn.rɪˈzɑlvd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈzɒlvd /; / ˌʌn.rɪˈzɒlvd / (BrE)
not settled, solved, or concluded; still open or undecided (used for problems, conflicts, questions, or situations)

Examples

Unresolved problem
The issue of funding for the new building remains unresolved.
The mystery surrounding her disappearance is still unresolved.
There were several unresolved questions after the meeting ended.
The cause of the mysterious disappearance is still unresolved by the investigators.
The dispute between the two companies remains unresolved after months of negotiation.
There are still unresolved issues that need to be discussed at tomorrow's meeting.
