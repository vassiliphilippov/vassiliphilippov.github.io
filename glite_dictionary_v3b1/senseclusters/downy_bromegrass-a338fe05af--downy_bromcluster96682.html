downy bromegrass

noun
a Mediterranean annual grass with soft, hairy leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊni ˈbroʊmˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈdaʊni ˈbrəʊmˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
an annual or winter annual grass of Mediterranean origin, with softly hairy leaves, widely considered a weed in many regions
