micro-level

adjective
detailed or individual scale
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌlɛvəl / (BrE)
focusing on the smallest, most detailed, or individual aspects of something

Examples

Micro-level analysis
The study analyzed micro-level changes in the behavior of individual students.
At the micro-level, small adjustments can have a big impact on the final result.
Sociologists often examine micro-level interactions to understand group dynamics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detailed - showing or involving many small parts or facts
  • antonym: macro-level - focusing on the large-scale or overall aspects of something
