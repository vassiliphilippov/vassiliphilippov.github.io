pignolia

noun
edible seed of pine tree; pine nut
Pronunciation: / piˈnjoʊliə / (AmE), / piˈnjəʊliə / (BrE)
The edible seed of certain pine trees, especially species found in southwestern North America, often used in cooking.

Examples

A pignolia seed
Authentic Italian biscotti often contains pignolia as a key ingredient.
She sprinkled toasted pignolias over the salad for extra crunch.
The pesto was made with fresh basil and pignolia for a rich, nutty flavor.
