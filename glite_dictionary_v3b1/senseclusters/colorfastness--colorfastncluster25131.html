colorfastness

noun
resistance to fading
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlərˌfæstnəs / (AmE), / ˈkʌləˌfɑːstnəs / (BrE)
the quality of a material to retain its color without fading or running when washed or exposed to light

Examples

The colorfastness quality
The colorfastness of these curtains means they will look new even after years of sunlight.
Before buying a shirt, she checked its colorfastness to make sure it would not bleed in the wash.
Manufacturers often test the colorfastness of fabrics to ensure quality and customer satisfaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: durability - the ability to withstand wear, pressure, or damage
  • antonym: fading - the process of losing color or brightness
