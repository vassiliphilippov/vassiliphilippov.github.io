Afro-Eurasia

noun
Africa, Europe, and Asia as one landmass
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌæfroʊ jʊˈreɪʒə / (AmE), / ˌafrəʊ jʊəˈreɪʒə / (BrE)
The combined landmass of Africa, Europe, and Asia, considered as a single continent or supercontinent.

Examples

Afro-Eurasia landmass
Climate patterns in Afro-Eurasia have significantly influenced the development of agriculture and population centers.
Many major empires in history once dominated parts of Afro-Eurasia.
The Silk Road spanned across Afro-Eurasia, linking distant civilizations through trade and culture.
