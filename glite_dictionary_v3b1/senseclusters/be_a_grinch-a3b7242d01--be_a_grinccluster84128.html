be a grinch

verb
act miserly or spoil others' enjoyment
6.6
Pronunciation: / bi ə ɡrɪntʃ / (AmE), / biː ə ɡrɪntʃ / (BrE)
to act in a miserly, unfriendly, or joy-spoiling manner, especially during celebrations or festive times

Examples

To be a grinch at Christmas
It's hard not to be a grinch when you're feeling under the weather.
Please don't be a grinch and let the kids enjoy the party.
Whenever the holidays come around, my brother tends to be a grinch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: killjoy - a person who spoils the enjoyment of others
  • synonym: party pooper - someone who dampens the fun at social gatherings
