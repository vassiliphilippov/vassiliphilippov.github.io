geo

x
prefix meaning earth
Pronunciation: / dʒiːoʊ / (AmE), / dʒiːəʊ / (BrE)
a combining form (prefix) meaning 'earth' or 'ground', used to form words related to the Earth

Examples

Geo prefix in geology
The word geography comes from the prefix meaning earth and the word for writing.
Scientists use geothermal energy, which is heat from within the earth, to generate electricity.
Many English words, like geology and geometry, begin with this prefix.
