ventrolateral

adjective
front and side position
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛntroʊˈlætərəl / (AmE), / ˌvɛntrəʊˈlætərəl / (BrE)
located toward both the front and the side of the body or an organ

Examples

Ventrolateral body side
The tumor was found on the ventrolateral surface of the kidney during the scan.
Muscles in the ventrolateral region help support the abdominal wall.
The ventrolateral approach is often used in certain types of brain surgery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dorsal - relating to the back side
  • synonym: lateral - relating to the side
  • synonym: ventral - relating to the front or belly side
