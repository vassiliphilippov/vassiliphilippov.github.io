ionium

noun
thorium-230 isotope
Pronunciation: / aɪˈoʊniəm / (AmE), / aɪˈəʊniəm / (BrE)
a former name for the radioactive isotope thorium-230, used especially in geological dating

Examples

Radioactive ionium isotope
Scientists measured the amount of ionium in the sediment to estimate its age.
The discovery of ionium helped improve methods for dating ancient marine deposits.
Although ionium is no longer a common term, it appears in older scientific literature.
