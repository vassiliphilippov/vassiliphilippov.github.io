bloom

noun
flower or flowering part of a plant
4.1
Pronunciation: / bluːm / (AmE), / bluːm / (BrE)
the flower of a plant, or the process or period when a plant produces flowers

Examples

A rose bloom

The cherry trees are covered in fragrant bloom each spring.
During the spring, the garden comes alive with the bloom of many flowers.
If you prune the plant too early, it may not bloom this season.
The rosebush was in full bloom by the end of May.
