dermatophytic

adjective
caused by skin fungi
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɜːrmətəˈfaɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌdɜːmətəˈfaɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or caused by dermatophytes, a group of fungi that infect the skin, hair, or nails

Examples

Dermatophytic skin infection
The doctor diagnosed a dermatophytic infection after examining the patient's skin rash.
Athlete's foot is a common dermatophytic condition affecting the feet.
Proper hygiene can help prevent dermatophytic diseases in communal showers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fungal - relating to or caused by fungi
