mountain viscacha

noun
South American mountain rodent
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən vɪˈskɑːtʃə / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn vɪˈskɑːtʃə / (BrE)
a rodent native to the mountainous regions of Chile and Peru, sometimes bred in captivity

Examples

Mountain viscachas in Andes
