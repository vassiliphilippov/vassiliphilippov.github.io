fluorescent dye

noun
dye that glows under UV light
3.5 3.4
Pronunciation: /flʊ.ˈres.ənt daɪ/ /; / /flɔː.ˈres.ənt daɪ/ (AmE), /flʊə.ˈres.ənt daɪ/ (BrE)
a dye that emits visible light when exposed to ultraviolet or other forms of light, often used in scientific and medical applications for tracking or detection

Examples

A fluorescent dye solution
By adding a fluorescent dye to the solution, researchers could easily track the chemical reaction.
The scientist used a fluorescent dye to visualize the cells under a microscope.
This fluorescent dye highlights areas where the protein is present in the sample.
