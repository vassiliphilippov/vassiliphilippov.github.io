waterskier

noun
person on water skis
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌskiːər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌskiːə / (BrE)
a person who is pulled behind a boat while standing on water skis for sport or recreation

Examples

An experienced waterskier
The experienced waterskier glided smoothly across the lake behind the speeding boat.
As a beginner waterskier, she practiced balancing on the skis before attempting any tricks.
Spectators cheered when the waterskier completed a perfect turn around the buoy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skier - a person who moves over snow or water on skis
  • synonym: wakeboarder - a person who rides a wakeboard behind a boat
