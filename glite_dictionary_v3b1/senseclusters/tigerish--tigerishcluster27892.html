tigerish

adjective
fierce or merciless like a tiger
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪɡərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡərɪʃ / (BrE)
having qualities resembling those of a tiger, especially in fierceness or lack of mercy

Examples

Tigerish determination
He showed a tigerish determination to achieve his goals, refusing to give up.
She glared at him with a tigerish intensity that made him step back.
The lawyer's tigerish cross-examination unsettled the witness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fierce - showing intense aggressiveness or violence
  • antonym: merciful - showing compassion or forgiveness
