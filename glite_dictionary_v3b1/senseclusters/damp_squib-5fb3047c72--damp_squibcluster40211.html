damp squib

noun
disappointing or anticlimactic event
8.5 9.2
Pronunciation: / dæmp skwɪb / (AmE), / damp skwɪb / (BrE)
An event or situation that is much less impressive or exciting than expected; a disappointment.

Examples

A damp squib event
Despite the buildup, the supposed scandal was a complete damp squib in the media.
Many hoped for a dramatic finale, but the show fizzled out as a damp squib.
The highly advertised concert ended up being a damp squib due to technical problems.
