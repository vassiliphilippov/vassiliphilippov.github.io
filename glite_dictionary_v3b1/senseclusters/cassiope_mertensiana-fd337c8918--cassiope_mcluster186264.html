Cassiope mertensiana

noun
mountain heath with bell-shaped white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌkæsiˈoʊpi mɜːrˈtɛnsiənə / (AmE), / ˌkæsiˈəʊpiː mɜːˈtɛnsɪənə / (BrE)
A species of heath found in the mountains of western North America, known for its bell-shaped white flowers.
