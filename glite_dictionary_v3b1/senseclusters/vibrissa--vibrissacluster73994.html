vibrissa

noun
whisker-like sensory hair of mammals
9.9
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈbrɪsə / (AmE), / vɪˈbrɪsə / (BrE)
A long, stiff, sensitive hair growing from the snout or brow of certain mammals, such as cats, commonly functioning as a tactile organ.
