dress uniform

noun
formal military ceremonial uniform
Pronunciation: / drɛs ˈjunəˌfɔrm / (AmE), / drɛs ˈjuːnɪfɔːm / (BrE)
a formal military uniform worn by members of the armed forces on ceremonial or official occasions

Examples

A dress uniform ceremony
Cadets are required to wear their dress uniform during graduation ceremonies.
Each officer wore his dress uniform for the annual military ball.
The general's dress uniform was adorned with medals and ribbons.

Related Terms

  • antonym: service uniform - a standard military uniform for everyday wear
