inseminator

noun
male causing pregnancy
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛmɪneɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛmɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a male animal or person that causes pregnancy in another by natural mating

Examples

A bull inseminator
The farmer selected the healthiest inseminator to improve the quality of the herd.
In some breeding programs, only one inseminator is chosen for the entire group of females.
The role of the inseminator is crucial in ensuring successful reproduction among livestock.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breeder - an animal or person that produces offspring
  • antonym: sterile - unable to produce offspring
