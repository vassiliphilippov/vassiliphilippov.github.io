whammy

noun
an evil spell or curse
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwæmi / (AmE), / ˈwæmi / (BrE)
an evil spell or curse that brings bad luck or misfortune

Examples

Put a whammy on
He feared the team might lose if the fans put a whammy on them.
The magician joked about removing the whammy from the unlucky ticket.
The old woman was convinced that someone had put a whammy on her house.

  • synonym: curse - a solemn utterance intended to invoke supernatural power to inflict harm
