circle of Willis

noun
arterial ring at the base of the brain
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.kəl əv ˈwɪl.ɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.kəl əv ˈwɪl.ɪs / (BrE)
a circular arrangement of arteries located at the base of the brain that supplies blood to the brain and surrounding structures

Examples
