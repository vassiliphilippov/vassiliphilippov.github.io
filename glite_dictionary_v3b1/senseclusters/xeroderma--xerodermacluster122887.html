xeroderma

noun
abnormal dryness of the skin
7.2
Pronunciation: / zɛrəˈdɜrmə / (AmE), / zɪərəˈdɜːmə / (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by abnormal dryness and roughness of the skin.

Examples

Severe xeroderma symptoms
Long-term exposure to harsh weather conditions can aggravate xeroderma in susceptible individuals.
Patients with xeroderma often experience discomfort due to persistent dryness and flaking of the skin.
The doctor recommended a moisturizing regimen to manage the symptoms of xeroderma.
