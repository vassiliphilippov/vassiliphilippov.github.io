Tamerlane

proper noun
Mongol conqueror, 14th–15th century
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtæmərˌleɪn / (AmE), / ˈtaməˌleɪn / (BrE)
A Mongol conqueror (1336–1405) who ruled Samarkand and created a vast empire from Turkey to Mongolia.

Examples

Tamerlane the conqueror
Many legends and stories have been told about the conquests of Tamerlane.
The armies of Tamerlane swept across Central Asia, leaving a lasting impact on history.
The tomb of Tamerlane in Samarkand attracts visitors from around the world.
