sockeye salmon

noun
fatty red flesh of a Pacific salmon
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːkaɪ ˈsæmən / (AmE), / ˈsɒkaɪ ˈsæmən / (BrE)
the fatty red flesh of a salmon species native to the Pacific coast and rivers, valued as food

Examples

Grilled sockeye salmon
Grilled sockeye salmon is a popular dish at many seafood restaurants along the Pacific Northwest.
The rich, red color of sockeye salmon makes it a favorite among sushi chefs.
We bought fresh sockeye salmon at the market to prepare for dinner.

Related Terms
