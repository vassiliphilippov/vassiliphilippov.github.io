bighearted

adjective
generous; kind and giving
Pronunciation: / ˌbɪɡˈhɑːrtɪd / (AmE), / ˌbɪɡˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
Kind and generous; willing to give freely and help others.

Examples

Bighearted person
He acted in a bighearted way, always ready to help those in need.
Her bighearted nature made her popular among her friends and neighbors.
The company was praised for its bighearted donations to local charities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generous - willing to give or share unselfishly
  • antonym: stingy - unwilling to give or spend; ungenerous
