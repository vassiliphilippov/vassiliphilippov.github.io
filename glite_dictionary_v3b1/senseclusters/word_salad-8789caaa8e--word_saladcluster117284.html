word salad

noun
disorganized, incoherent speech or writing
9.6
Pronunciation: / wɝːd ˈsæl.əd / (AmE), / wɜːd ˈsæl.əd / (BrE)
incoherent or nonsensical speech or writing, especially as a symptom of mental illness such as schizophrenia

Examples

Confusing word salad
After the accident, his sentences turned into a confusing word salad.
The patient began speaking in a word salad that was impossible to understand.
The psychiatrist recognized the symptoms when she heard the word salad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gibberish - nonsensical or unintelligible speech or writing
