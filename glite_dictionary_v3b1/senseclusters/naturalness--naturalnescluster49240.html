naturalness

noun
quality of being natural or genuine
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃrəl.nəs /; / ˈnætʃrəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈnatʃrəl.nəs /; / ˈnatʃrəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being natural, genuine, or free from artificiality or affectation
