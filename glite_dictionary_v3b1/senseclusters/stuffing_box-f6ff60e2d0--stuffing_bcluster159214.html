stuffing box

noun
chamber for sealing a moving shaft or piston
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌfɪŋ ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈstʌfɪŋ ˌbɒks / (BrE)
a small chamber around a moving shaft or piston, filled with packing material that is compressed to form a seal and prevent leakage
