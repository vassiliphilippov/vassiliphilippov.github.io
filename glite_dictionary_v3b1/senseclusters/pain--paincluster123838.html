pain

verb
cause emotional distress
Pronunciation: / peɪn / (AmE), / peɪn / (BrE)

She was pained by his harsh criticism.
His harsh criticism pained her more than he realized.
It pains me to see you so upset about the situation.
The memory of the accident still pains him after many years.
