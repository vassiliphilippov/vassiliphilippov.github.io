dauphine

noun
native of Dauphiné
9.9
Pronunciation: / doʊˈfiːn / (AmE), / dəʊˈfiːn / (BrE)
a person who comes from or is a native of the historical Dauphiné region of France

Examples

A proud dauphine

The museum featured artwork by a famous dauphine from the eighteenth century.
As a dauphine, he often spoke about the unique culture of his homeland.
