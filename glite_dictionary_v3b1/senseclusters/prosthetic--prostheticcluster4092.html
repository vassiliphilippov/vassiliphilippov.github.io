prosthetic

adjective
relating to artificial body parts
1.6
Pronunciation: / prɑˈsθɛtɪk /; / prɑːsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / prɒsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to an artificial body part, such as a limb, heart, or tooth, that replaces a missing or defective one

Examples

Prosthetic leg
After the accident, he learned to walk again with a prosthetic leg.
Recent advances in prosthetic technology have improved mobility for many patients.
The clinic specializes in prosthetic limbs for children and adults.
Advancements in prosthetic technology have greatly improved patients' quality of life.
Her doctor recommended a prosthetic limb after her accident.
The prosthetic device fit so well that it was barely noticeable.
