blaze

verb
move rapidly with intensity
3.3
Pronunciation: / bleɪz / (AmE), / bleɪz / (BrE)
to move rapidly, often with intensity or brightness, as if emitting flames or light

Examples

To blaze ahead
As the alarm sounded, she blazed down the hallway to safety.
The train blazed through the countryside under a sky full of stars.
Thousands cheered as the runners blazed past the finish line.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crawl - to move very slowly
  • synonym: race - to move at great speed
