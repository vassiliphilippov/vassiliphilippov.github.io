bursal

adjective
relating to a bursa (body sac)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːsəl / (AmE), / ˈbɜːsəl / (BrE)
relating to or affecting a bursa, a fluid-filled sac in the body that reduces friction between tissues such as bone and muscle.

Examples

Bursal infection
A bursal infection can occur after repeated trauma or pressure on the joint.
Doctors may prescribe anti-inflammatory medication for bursal swelling.
The patient experienced pain due to bursal inflammation in his shoulder.
