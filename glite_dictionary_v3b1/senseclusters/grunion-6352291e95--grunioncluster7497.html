Grunion

proper noun
US Navy submarine
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrʌniən / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌniən / (BrE)
the name of a United States Navy Gato-class submarine (USS Grunion, SS-216) commissioned in 1942 and lost during World War II
