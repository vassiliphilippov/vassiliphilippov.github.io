broletto

noun
Italian town hall
Pronunciation: / broʊˈlɛtoʊ / (AmE), / brəʊˈlɛtəʊ / (BrE)
a medieval Italian town hall or public building used for assemblies and civic meetings

Examples

Medieval broletto building
The medieval broletto stands at the center of the old city square.
Many important decisions were made in the broletto during the Middle Ages.
Tourists admired the architecture of the ancient broletto in Brescia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: palazzo - an Italian palace or large public building
  • synonym: town hall - a building used for local government meetings
