obsessor

noun
person with obsession
8.1
Pronunciation: /əb.ˈses.ər/ (AmE), /əb.ˈses.ə/ (BrE)
a person who is obsessed with something or someone, often to an excessive degree

Examples

A music obsessor
As an obsessor of mystery novels, she read every new release as soon as it came out.
The obsessor spent hours each day researching rare stamps for his collection.
His friends worried that being an obsessor of social media was affecting his real-life relationships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enthusiast - a person who is very interested in a particular activity or subject
  • synonym: fanatic - a person filled with excessive enthusiasm for something
