genus Santalum

noun
genus of parasitic Indonesian/Malaysian trees
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs sænˈteɪləm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈsæntələm / (BrE)
A genus of parasitic trees found in Indonesia and Malaysia, including sandalwood species.

Ecologists have studied the unique parasitic nature of trees in the genus Santalum.
