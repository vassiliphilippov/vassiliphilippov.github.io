arboriculture

noun
cultivation and care of trees
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrbərɪˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˌɑːbərɪˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
The cultivation, management, and study of trees, especially for ornamental or landscape purposes.

Examples

Arboriculture practices
Advances in arboriculture have helped cities maintain healthier, longer-living trees.
Proper arboriculture can greatly improve the appearance and safety of public parks.
She studied arboriculture to pursue a career in urban forestry.
