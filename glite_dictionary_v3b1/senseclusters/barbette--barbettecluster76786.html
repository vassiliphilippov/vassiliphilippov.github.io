barbette

noun
historical gun platform in a fort
9.7 9.9
Pronunciation: / bɑrˈbɛt / (AmE), / bɑːˈbɛt / (BrE)
(historical) A mound or platform inside a fort or battery from which guns can be fired over the parapet.

Examples

A barbette platform
During the siege, soldiers took cover behind the barbette as they reloaded their artillery.
Engineers built a new barbette inside the battery to support heavier guns.
The cannons were mounted on a barbette to allow a wider field of fire over the fort walls.
