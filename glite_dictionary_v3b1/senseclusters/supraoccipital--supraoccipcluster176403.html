supraoccipital

noun
upper rear skull bone
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəɑkˈsɪpɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəɒkˈsɪpɪtəl / (BrE)
a bone forming the upper rear part of the skull in many vertebrates

Examples

Supraoccipital bone in reptiles
The paleontologist identified the supraoccipital in the fossilized skull of the ancient reptile.
Damage to the supraoccipital can affect the overall shape of the vertebrate skull.
In some species, the supraoccipital fuses with other cranial bones during development.

Related Terms

  • synonym: occipital bone - the bone forming the back and base of the skull
