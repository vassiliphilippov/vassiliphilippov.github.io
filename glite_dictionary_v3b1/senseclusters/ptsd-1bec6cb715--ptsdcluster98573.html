PTSD

noun
anxiety disorder after trauma
6.1 6.20/m
Pronunciation: / Àåpi ti …õs Ààdi / (AmE), / ÀåpiÀê tiÀê …õs ÀàdiÀê / (BrE)
An anxiety disorder caused by exposure to traumatic events, resulting in symptoms such as reliving the trauma, emotional numbness, and survivor guilt.
