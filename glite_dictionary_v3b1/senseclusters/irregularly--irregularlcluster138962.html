irregularly

adverb
not regularly or evenly
1.4 3.5
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈre.gjə.rə.lɪ/ /; / /ɪ.ˈre.gjʊ.rə.lɪ/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈre.gjʊ.lə.lɪ/ /; / /ɪ.ˈre.gjə.lə.lɪ/ (BrE)
in a way that does not follow a regular pattern, schedule, or arrangement; inconsistently or unevenly

Examples

The artist painted the walls irregularly to give the room a rustic feel.
The coastline is irregularly shaped, with many small coves and inlets.
