indulto

noun
official pardon
9.9
Pronunciation: / inˈdultoʊ / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʊltəʊ / (BrE)
an official pardon or act of clemency, especially in Spanish law or awarded in bullfighting for exceptional performance

Examples

Royal indulto
The king granted an indulto to several prisoners during the national holiday.
In the bullring, the matador received an indulto for his outstanding bravery and skill.
The government announced an indulto as a gesture of reconciliation after the conflict.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clemency - mercy or lenience shown by someone in authority
  • synonym: pardon - an official forgiveness for an offense
  • antonym: punishment - a penalty imposed for wrongdoing
