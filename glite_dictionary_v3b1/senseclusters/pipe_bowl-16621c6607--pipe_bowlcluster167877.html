pipe bowl

noun
part of a tobacco pipe holding tobacco
Pronunciation: / paɪp boʊl / (AmE), / paɪp bəʊl / (BrE)
the round, open part of a tobacco pipe that holds the tobacco

Examples

The pipe bowl holds tobacco
After smoking, she cleaned the ashes from the pipe bowl.
He carefully packed the pipe bowl with fresh tobacco before lighting it.
The antique pipe bowl was carved from briar wood and had intricate designs.
