non-dramatic

adjective
not exciting or emotional
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn drəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn drəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
not exciting, emotional, or involving drama; lacking dramatic qualities

Examples

Non-dramatic speech
Her explanation was clear and non-dramatic, making it easy for everyone to understand.
The meeting ended in a non-dramatic way, with everyone agreeing on the next steps.
He prefers a non-dramatic lifestyle, avoiding unnecessary excitement or conflict.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dramatic - exciting or full of emotion
  • synonym: undramatic - not dramatic or exciting
