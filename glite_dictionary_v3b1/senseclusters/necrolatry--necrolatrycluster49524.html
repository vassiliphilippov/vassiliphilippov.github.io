necrolatry

noun
worship of the dead
Pronunciation: / ˌnɛkɹəˈlætɹi / (AmE), / ˌnɛkɹəˈlatɹi / (BrE)
superstitious worship or veneration of the dead, especially as a ritual or religious practice

Examples

Necrolatry worship
In her research, she found evidence of necrolatry among several indigenous tribes.
Scholars debate whether the rituals amounted to true necrolatry or merely symbolic respect for the departed.
The ancient civilization practiced necrolatry in elaborate rites to honor their ancestors.
