rheumatologist

noun
doctor specializing in rheumatic diseases
2.7 6.1
Pronunciation: /ruː.mə.ˈtɑː.lə.dʒɪst/ /; / /ruː.mə.ˈtɑː.lə.dʒəst/ (AmE), /ruː.mə.ˈtɒl.ə.dʒɪst/ /; / /ruː.mə.ˈtɒl.ə.dʒəst/ (BrE)
A doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of rheumatic diseases, such as arthritis and related disorders affecting joints, muscles, and bones.

Examples

Rheumatologist treats arthritis
After months of unexplained symptoms, he finally saw a rheumatologist who diagnosed him with lupus.
Many rheumatologists collaborate with physical therapists to develop treatment plans for their patients.
The patient was referred to a rheumatologist for further evaluation of her joint pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orthopedist - a doctor specializing in the musculoskeletal system
  • synonym: rheumatology - the branch of medicine dealing with rheumatic diseases
