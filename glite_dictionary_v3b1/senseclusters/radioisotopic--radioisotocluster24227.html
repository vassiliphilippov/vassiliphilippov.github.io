radioisotopic

adjective
involving radioisotopes
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdioʊaɪsəˈtɑpɪk / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəʊaɪsəˈtɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving radioisotopes, which are radioactive forms of chemical elements

Examples

The radioisotopic dating method
The radioisotopic dating method helped determine the age of the rock sample.
Scientists use radioisotopic tracers to study chemical processes in the body.
The team analyzed the radioisotopic composition of the soil to assess contamination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: radioactive - emitting radiation as a result of nuclear decay
  • antonym: stable - not radioactive; not undergoing nuclear decay
