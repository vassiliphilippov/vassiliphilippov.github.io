broadness

noun
the state of being wide
2.5 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / Ààbr…îd.n…ôs / (AmE), / Ààbr…îÀêd.n…ôs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being wide; great width

Examples

The broadness of view
She admired the broadness of the avenue lined with ancient trees.
The broadness of his shoulders was noticeable even in a crowd.
The broadness of the river made it difficult to build a bridge across.

Synonyms

  • wideness
    2.2 0.04/m
    wideness describes the simple state of being wide, while broadness often suggests general size or range in feeling or use.
  • breadth
    3.9 2.11/m
    breadth means the distance or extent across something and can feel more formal or measured, while broadness is more general.
  • width
    1.0 18/m
    width is a concrete measurement across an object used in math and design, while broadness names a general state of being wide.
