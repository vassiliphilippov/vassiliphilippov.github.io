keep the conversation lively

verb
make conversation interesting and engaging
Pronunciation: / kip ðə ˌkɑnvərˈseɪʃən ˈlaɪvli / (AmE), / kiːp ðə ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃən ˈlaɪvli / (BrE)
to make sure that a conversation remains interesting, engaging, and active

Examples

To keep the conversation lively
His insightful questions helped keep the conversation lively during the panel discussion.
It can be difficult to keep the conversation lively when people are tired after a long day.
She made jokes throughout dinner to keep the conversation lively and everyone entertained.

Related Terms

  • synonym: break the ice - to do or say something to relieve tension or get conversation started
  • synonym: keep the conversation going - to sustain or maintain a conversation so it does not stop
