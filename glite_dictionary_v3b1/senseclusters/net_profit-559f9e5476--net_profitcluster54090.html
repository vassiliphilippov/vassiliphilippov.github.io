Synonyms

  • net income
    3.0 3/m
    net income is the total profit shown in financial statements for a period, while net profit names the remaining profit after all deductions.
  • profits
    2.3 0.1/m
    profits refers to money left after costs and taxes in general business talk, while net profit is the precise accounting result.
  • net
    5.6 20/m
    net names the final amount after all charges, often including depreciation, while net profit focuses on actual leftover profit.
