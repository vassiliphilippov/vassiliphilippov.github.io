chassis

noun
frame of a motor vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæsi / (AmE), / ˈʃæsi / (BrE)
the base frame of a motor vehicle, onto which the body and other components are mounted

Examples

Car chassis
Before assembly, every chassis undergoes a thorough inspection for structural integrity.
The car's chassis was damaged in the accident, requiring extensive repairs.
The engineer designed a lighter chassis to improve the vehicle's performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frame - the supporting structure of a vehicle or other object
