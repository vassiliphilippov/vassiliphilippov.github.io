Lebistes

noun
former genus name for guppies
Pronunciation: / ləˈbɪstiːz / (AmE), / lɛˈbɪstiːz / (BrE)
a former genus name for guppies, now classified under Poecilia

Examples

The Lebistes fish
Many early scientific papers refer to the guppy as Lebistes rather than Poecilia.
Specimens labelled as Lebistes can still be found in museum collections.
The genus Lebistes is now considered obsolete in modern fish classification.
