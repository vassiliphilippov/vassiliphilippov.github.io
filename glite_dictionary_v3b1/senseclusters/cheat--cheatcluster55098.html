cheat

noun
person who deceives; swindler
Pronunciation: / tʃit / (AmE), / tʃiːt / (BrE)
a person who deceives others by breaking rules or acting dishonestly, especially to gain an unfair advantage

Examples

A card cheat
After the poker game, everyone realized he was a cheat who had marked the cards.
It's disappointing to find out that your business partner is a cheat.
She refused to associate with a cheat, no matter how charming he seemed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fraud - a person who pretends to be something they are not, or deceives others for personal gain
  • antonym: honest person - someone who does not cheat or deceive others
  • synonym: swindler - a person who uses deception to deprive someone of money or possessions
