Captain James Cook

proper noun
English navigator and explorer (1728–1779)
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.tən ˈdʒeɪmz kʊk / (AmE), / ˈkæp.tɪn ˈdʒeɪmz kʊk / (BrE)
(1728–1779) English navigator and explorer who charted the eastern coast of Australia, claimed it for Britain, and discovered several Pacific islands.
