zebra finch

noun
small Australian songbird with zebra-like stripes
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈziː.brə fɪntʃ / (AmE), / ˈziː.brə fɪntʃ / (BrE)
a small Australian songbird (Taeniopygia guttata) with black-and-white markings resembling those of a zebra, often kept as a pet or aviary bird.

Examples

A zebra finch bird
I watched a pair of zebra finches build their nest in the aviary.
The zebra finch is a popular choice for pet bird enthusiasts due to its lively nature.
The male zebra finch has bright orange cheeks and distinctive black barring.
