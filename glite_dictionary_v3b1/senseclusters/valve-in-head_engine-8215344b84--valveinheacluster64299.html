valve-in-head engine

noun
engine with valves in cylinder head
7.6 8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvælv ɪn ˈhɛd ˈɛn.dʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈvælv ɪn ˈhɛd ˈɛn.dʒɪn / (BrE)
