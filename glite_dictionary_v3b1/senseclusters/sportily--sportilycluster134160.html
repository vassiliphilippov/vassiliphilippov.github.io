sportily

adverb
in a fair manner
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔːrtɪli / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows fairness, good manners, or grace in competition or play

Examples

To lose sportily
She congratulated her rival sportily after losing the tennis match.
The team accepted their defeat sportily and applauded the winners.
He always plays sportily, respecting both the rules and his opponents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fairly - in a just or impartial way
  • antonym: unsportingly - in a manner lacking fairness or good sportsmanship
