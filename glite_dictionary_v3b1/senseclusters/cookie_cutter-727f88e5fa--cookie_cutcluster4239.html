cookie cutter

adjective

Pronunciation: /ˈkʊk.iː ˈkʌt.ər/ /; / /ˈkʊk.ɪ ˈkʌt.ər/ (AmE), /ˈkʊk.iː ˈkʌt.ə/ /; / /ˈkʊk.ɪ ˈkʌt.ə/ (BrE)
