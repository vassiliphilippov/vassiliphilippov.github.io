brahminic

adjective
relating to Brahmins or their traditions
5.0
Pronunciation: / brɑːˈmɪnɪk / (AmE), / brɑːˈmɪnɪk / (BrE)
relating to, characteristic of, or associated with Brahmins (the priestly caste in Hinduism) or their customs, traditions, or philosophy

Examples

Brahminic traditions
He wrote a dissertation on the influence of brahminic codes in ancient India.
Some festivals are deeply rooted in brahminic traditions and rituals.
The brahminic philosophy has played a significant role in shaping Hindu thought.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brahminical - relating to Brahmins or Brahminism
