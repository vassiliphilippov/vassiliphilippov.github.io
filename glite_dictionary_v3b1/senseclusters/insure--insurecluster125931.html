insure

verb

to make certain or guarantee that something will happen

Examples

To insure success
Please insure that all forms are filled out correctly before submitting them.
Proper training will insure everyone knows what to do in an emergency.
She double-checked the doors to insure they were locked before leaving.
Her determination will insure her success in the competition.
Regular maintenance helps insure the longevity of your car.
We must insure that all safety measures are in place before starting the project.

Synonyms

  • ensure
    2.8 110/m
    ensure means to take steps so something definitely happens, while insure expresses guaranteeing that outcome.
  • guarantee
    2.8 53/m
    guarantee is a firm promise that something will happen or be fixed, while insure focuses on making sure it occurs.
  • assure
    3.5 15/m
    assure is often used to comfort a person that things will happen, while insure means to make the event certain.
  • warrant
    6.5 2.19/m
    warrant formally promises quality or truth in documents, while insure means to make certain an outcome will happen.
  • see to it
    3.5 3.64/m
    see to it means taking care to make something happen in practice, while insure means guaranteeing the result.
  • see
    1.0 136/m
    see can mean check or make sure something is done, while insure means guarantee that it happens.
