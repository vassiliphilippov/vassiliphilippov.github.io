erosion

noun
gradual decline or reduction
4.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈroʊʒən / (AmE), / ɪˈrəʊʒən / (BrE)
the gradual decline or reduction of something, especially in strength or value

Examples

Erosion of trust
Many investors are concerned about the erosion of their savings due to inflation.
The constant criticism led to an erosion of the team's morale over time.
There has been a noticeable erosion of trust between the public and the government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deterioration - the process of becoming progressively worse
  • antonym: improvement - the process of something becoming better
