Turkism

noun
Turkish nationalism
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrkɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈtɜːkɪzəm / (BrE)
a political ideology or movement that emphasizes Turkish national identity, unity, or interests

Examples

Turkism in politics
The party openly promoted Turkism as a core principle of its platform during the election campaign.
Many intellectuals in the early 20th century supported Turkism to strengthen national unity.
Debates about Turkism often arise in discussions of cultural and political identity in Turkey.

Related Terms

  • antonym: internationalism - the principle of cooperation among nations
  • synonym: nationalism - strong support for the interests and culture of one's nation
  • synonym: pan-Turkism - the idea of uniting all Turkic peoples
