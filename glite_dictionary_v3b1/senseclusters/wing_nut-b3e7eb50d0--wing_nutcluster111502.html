wing nut

noun
extreme partisan (pejorative)
Pronunciation: / wɪŋ nʌt / (AmE), / wɪŋ nʌt / (BrE)
an informal, insulting term for a person who holds extreme or irrational political views, especially at one wing of a political party.

Examples

A political wing nut
The columnist called the activist a wing nut for denying established science.
Many voters see his proposals as extreme, calling him a wing nut who will not compromise.
She refused to debate with the wing nut who spread rumors about her record.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extremist - a person who holds extreme political or religious views
  • synonym: fanatic - a person filled with excessive enthusiasm for a cause or belief
