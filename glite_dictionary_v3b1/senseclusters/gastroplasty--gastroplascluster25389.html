gastroplasty

noun
stomach surgery
6.7
Pronunciation: /gæs.trə.ˈplæs.tiː/ /; / /gæs.troʊ.ˈplæs.tiː/ (AmE), /gæs.trə.ˈplæs.tiː/ (BrE)
a surgical operation to repair or reconstruct the stomach, often to restore its function after injury, disease, or defect

Examples

Stomach gastroplasty repair
The patient underwent a gastroplasty to repair damage caused by a severe ulcer.
Advances in surgical techniques have made gastroplasty safer and more effective for stomach reconstruction.
After the accident, doctors recommended a gastroplasty to restore normal stomach function.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bypass surgery - surgery to create a new pathway for food in the digestive system
  • synonym: gastrectomy - surgical removal of part or all of the stomach
