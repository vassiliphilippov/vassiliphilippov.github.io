noddy

noun
tropical seabird; simple person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑdi / (AmE), / ˈnɒdi / (BrE)
a small tropical seabird of the tern family, or informally, a simple or foolish person

Examples

A noddy bird
We spotted several noddies nesting on the island during our boat trip.
The noddy flew low over the water, searching for fish to eat.
He acted like a real noddy when he forgot his own birthday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or lacks good sense
  • synonym: tern - a type of seabird with long wings and a forked tail
