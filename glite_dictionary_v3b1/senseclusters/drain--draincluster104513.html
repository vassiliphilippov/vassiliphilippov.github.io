drain

noun
act of letting liquid drain out
Pronunciation: / dreɪn / (AmE), / dreɪn / (BrE)
The act or process of emptying something by letting liquid flow out of it.

Examples

A full drain
After the storm, a complete drain of the flooded basement was necessary.
He supervised the drain of water from the swimming pool before cleaning it.
The drain of the oil tank took longer than expected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drainage - the process or means of draining liquid from something
