raccoon

noun
fur of the raccoon (used in clothing)
3.6
Pronunciation: / rəˈkun / (AmE), / rəˈkuːn / (BrE)
The fur of the North American raccoon, used especially for making garments.

Examples

Raccoon fur coat
She wore a luxurious coat made of raccoon for the winter gala.
The designer's new line features collars lined with soft raccoon.
Traditional trappers once supplied raccoon to hat makers across North America.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mink - the fur of the mink, used in high-quality garments
