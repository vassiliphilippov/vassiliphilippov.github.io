Norway lobster

noun
a slender edible European lobster
5.1 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈnɔːr.weɪ ˈlɑːb.stər/ (AmE), /ˈnɔː.weɪ ˈlɒb.stə/ (BrE)
A slender European lobster (Nephrops norvegicus) valued for its edible flesh, similar in appearance to the American lobster but smaller and narrower.

Examples

The Norway lobster
Fishermen along the coasts of Scotland catch Norway lobster during the summer months.
Many chefs consider Norway lobster a delicacy, especially in Mediterranean cuisine.
The restaurant's special was grilled Norway lobster served with fresh herbs and lemon.
