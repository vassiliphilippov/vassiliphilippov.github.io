lunchtime

noun
customary time for lunch
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌntʃˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈlʌntʃtaɪm / (BrE)
the usual or scheduled time of day when lunch is eaten

Examples

At lunchtime
He scheduled the meeting just before lunchtime to ensure everyone would attend.
The cafeteria gets very crowded around lunchtime every day.
We usually take a short walk at lunchtime to get some fresh air.
