husky

adjective
muscular and heavily built (person)
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌski / (AmE), / ˈhʌski / (BrE)
(of a person, especially a man or boy) muscular and heavily built.

Examples

Husky man
As a teenager, he was already quite husky and strong.
The coach looked for husky players to join the team.
The store has a special section for husky boys' clothing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burly - large and strong; heavily built
  • antonym: slight - (of a person) small and slender
  • synonym: strapping - (especially of a young person) tall and strong
