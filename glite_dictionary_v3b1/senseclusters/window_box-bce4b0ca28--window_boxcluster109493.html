window box

noun
container for plants on a windowsill
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndoʊ ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ ˌbɒks / (BrE)
A long narrow container for growing plants, typically placed on a windowsill or fixed to the outside of a window.

Examples

A window box planter
Every morning, sunlight streamed through the kitchen window box lined with fresh herbs.
She filled the window box with colorful petunias for the summer.
They installed a new window box to brighten up the front of their house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flower box - a container for growing plants, placed outside a window
