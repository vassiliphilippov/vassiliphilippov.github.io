radio aerial

noun
antenna for receiving radio signals
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdioʊ ɛrˈiəl / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ ˈeəriəl / (BrE)
An antenna that receives radio signals for a radio receiver, typically omnidirectional.

Examples

A radio aerial on car
Many older cars still have a visible radio aerial on the roof.
The technician adjusted the radio aerial to get a clearer signal.
You need to extend the radio aerial fully for the best reception.
