perfumy

adjective
smelling like perfume
Pronunciation: / pərˈfjuːmi / (AmE), / pəˈfjuːmi / (BrE)
having a strong or noticeable scent similar to perfume

Examples

Perfumy soap scent
The perfumy scent of the soap lingered in the bathroom after she left.
He found the air in the shop too perfumy for his taste.
Some people prefer a more subtle fragrance instead of a perfumy one.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aromatic - having a noticeable and pleasant smell
  • synonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell
  • antonym: odorless - having no smell
