visual acuity

noun
sharpness of machine vision
Pronunciation: / \u02c8v\u026a\u0292u\u0259l \u0259\u02c8kju\u02d0\u026ati / (AmE), / \u02c8v\u026a\u0292u\u0259l \u0259\u02c8kju\u02d0\u026ati / (BrE)
(in computing and AI) the ability of a machine vision system or algorithm to resolve fine visual detail in images or scenes, often evaluated by recognition or detection performance under varying conditions.

Examples

AI visual acuity
The research team measured the model's visual acuity using blurred and low-light test images.
Engineers improved the camera's visual acuity by increasing sensor resolution and sharpening algorithms.
Benchmarks reported the autonomous vehicle's visual acuity before and after the software update.
