permalink

noun
permanent web address
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrməˌlɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈpɜːməˌlɪŋk / (BrE)
a permanent web address that links directly to a specific webpage or post

Examples

A permalink to a post
You can share the permalink to let others find the article easily.
Each blog post has its own permalink for easy reference and sharing.
Click the permalink below the comment to get a direct link to it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: URL - the address of a web page on the internet
  • synonym: hyperlink - a clickable link to another webpage or document
