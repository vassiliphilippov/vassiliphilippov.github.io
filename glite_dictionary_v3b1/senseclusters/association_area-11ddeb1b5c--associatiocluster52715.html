association area

noun
brain region for higher processing
Pronunciation: / əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən ˈɛriə / (AmE), / əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən ˈeəriə / (BrE)
an area of the cerebral cortex that is not primarily involved in motor or sensory functions but is responsible for higher-order processing and integration of information

Examples

The association area of brain
Damage to the association area can affect reasoning and problem-solving skills.
Researchers used MRI scans to study the function of a specific association area in the brain.
The parietal lobe contains an association area crucial for integrating sensory information.
