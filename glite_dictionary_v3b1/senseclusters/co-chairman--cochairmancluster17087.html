co-chairman

noun
joint committee leader
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊˈtʃɛrmən / (AmE), / ˌkəʊˈtʃeəmən / (BrE)
a person who shares the position of chairman with one or more others in a group or committee

Examples

The co-chairman of board
She was elected co-chairman of the board alongside her colleague.
The co-chairman worked together to organize the annual conference.
As co-chairman, he shared responsibilities for leading the committee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chairman - the leader or head of a committee or organization
  • synonym: co-chair - a person who shares the role of chair with another
