Theobroma

proper noun
genus of cacao trees
9.9
Pronunciation: / θiˈoʊbroʊmə / (AmE), / θiːˈɒbrəmə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical trees, including the cacao tree, known for producing seeds used to make chocolate

Examples

Theobroma cacao tree
The Theobroma genus includes several species, but the most famous is Theobroma cacao.
Botanists study Theobroma to understand the origins of chocolate production.
Farmers in tropical regions often cultivate Theobroma trees for their valuable seeds.
