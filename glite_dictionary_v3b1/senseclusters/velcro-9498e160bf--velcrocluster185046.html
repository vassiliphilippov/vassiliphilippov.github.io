Velcro

noun
nylon fastening fabric with hooks and loops
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛlˌkroʊ / (AmE), / ˈvɛl.krəʊ / (BrE)
a strong nylon fabric with tiny hooks and loops that stick together, used as a fastening for clothes, shoes, and other items

Examples

The Velcro fastener
After many uses, the Velcro on his jacket started to wear out.
She opened her bag by pulling apart the Velcro strip.
The child's shoes fasten with Velcro instead of traditional laces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hook-and-loop fastener - a fastening system using hooks and loops, often called Velcro
