search

noun
boarding and inspecting a ship at sea
Pronunciation: / sɝːtʃ / (AmE), / sɜːtʃ / (BrE)
the act of boarding and inspecting a ship on the high seas, usually for enforcement or regulatory purposes

Examples

A search of a ship
International law governs when a search at sea is permitted.
The captain protested the search but ultimately allowed the inspectors on board.
The navy conducted a search of the vessel suspected of illegal activities.
