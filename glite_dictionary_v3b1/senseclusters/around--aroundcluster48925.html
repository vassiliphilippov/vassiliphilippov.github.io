around

adverb
to various places; without fixed direction
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to many different places or with movement in no particular direction; here and there.

Examples

Rumors spread around
Rumors quickly spread around after the announcement was made.
She likes to travel around during the summer, visiting new countries each year.
We spent the day wandering around the city, exploring different neighborhoods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: about - to or in many different places or directions; approximately
