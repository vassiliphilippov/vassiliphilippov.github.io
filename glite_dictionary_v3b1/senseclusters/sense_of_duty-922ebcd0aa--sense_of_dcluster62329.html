sense of duty

noun
feeling of moral responsibility
Pronunciation: / sɛns əv ˈduːti / (AmE), / sɛns əv ˈdjuːti / (BrE)
A feeling or awareness that one has a moral or ethical responsibility to act in a certain way.

Examples

A sense of duty
He accepted the position out of a sense of duty, not personal ambition.
Her strong sense of duty compelled her to help those in need.
Many soldiers are driven by a sense of duty to serve their country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conscientiousness - the quality of wishing to do one's work or duty well and thoroughly
  • synonym: sense of responsibility - an awareness that one is accountable for something
