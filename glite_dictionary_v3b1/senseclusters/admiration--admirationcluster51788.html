admiration

noun
feeling of approval and pleasure
Pronunciation: / ˌædməˈreɪʃən /; / ˌæd.məˈreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌadmɪˈreɪʃən /; / ˌæd.mɪˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a feeling of respect, approval, or pleasure towards someone or something

Examples

His achievements earned him the admiration of his colleagues and friends.
She looked at her teacher with admiration after the inspiring lesson.
The artist's work attracted widespread admiration at the exhibition.

Her admiration for her mentor was evident in the heartfelt speech she gave.
The artist's work was met with widespread admiration from critics and collectors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: respect - a feeling of deep admiration for someone due to their abilities, qualities, or achievements
