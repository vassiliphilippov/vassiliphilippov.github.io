Saussurea

noun
genus of Eurasian herbs
Pronunciation: / soʊˈsjʊəriə / (AmE), / sɔːˈsjʊəriə / (BrE)
a genus of herbs native to temperate and cool regions of Eurasia, known for their woolly leaves and often purple or blue flowers; some species are used in traditional medicine
