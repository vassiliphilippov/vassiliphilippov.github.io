miscasting

noun
defective casting
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈkæstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈkɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
a casting that is defective or flawed due to errors in the moulding or pouring process.

Examples

Defective miscasting
