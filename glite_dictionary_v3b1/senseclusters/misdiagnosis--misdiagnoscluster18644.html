misdiagnosis

noun
incorrect analysis
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsdaɪˈɡnoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌmɪsdaɪɡˈnəʊsɪs / (BrE)
an incorrect analysis or explanation of the cause, nature, or source of a problem or situation, especially outside of medicine

Examples

Economic misdiagnosis
The committee's misdiagnosis of the factory's low productivity delayed effective reforms for months.
A misdiagnosis of the company's financial troubles led to the wrong solutions being implemented.
Their misdiagnosis of the root cause resulted in wasted time and resources.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diagnosis - the identification of the cause or nature of a problem
  • synonym: misinterpretation - an incorrect understanding or explanation of something
