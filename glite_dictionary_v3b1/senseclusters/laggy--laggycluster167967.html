laggy

adjective
sluggish or tired
Pronunciation: / ˈlæɡi / (AmE), / ˈlæɡi / (BrE)
feeling slow, sluggish, or lacking energy, especially after tiredness, travel, or illness

Examples

Feeling laggy after travel
After staying up all night, I felt really laggy the next morning.
The team was laggy after their long journey and struggled to keep up during practice.
She always feels laggy for a few days after recovering from a cold.

Related Terms

  • antonym: energetic - having or showing a lot of energy
  • synonym: sluggish - moving or reacting more slowly than usual
