webcast

verb
to broadcast online
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛbˌkæst / (AmE), / ˈwɛbˌkɑːst / (BrE)
to broadcast video or audio content over the internet, either live or as a recorded stream

Examples

To webcast a ceremony
The university will webcast the graduation ceremony so families can watch from home.
They plan to webcast the product launch to reach a global audience.
Many conferences now webcast their sessions for people who cannot attend in person.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broadcast - to transmit a program or information by radio or television
  • synonym: stream - to transmit audio or video content over the internet in real time
