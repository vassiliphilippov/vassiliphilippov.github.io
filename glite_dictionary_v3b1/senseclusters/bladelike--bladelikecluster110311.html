bladelike

adjective
shaped like a blade
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbleɪdˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbleɪdˌlaɪk / (BrE)
narrow, flat, and tapering like the blade of a sword or knife.

Examples

Bladelike shape
Her haircut was styled in bladelike layers that framed her face.
Several species of grass have thin, bladelike foliage.
The leaves of the yucca plant are bladelike and stiff.
