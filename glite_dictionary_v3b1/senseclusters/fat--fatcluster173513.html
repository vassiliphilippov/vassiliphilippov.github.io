fat

adjective
rich or fertile (of land)
5.4
Pronunciation: / fæt / (AmE), / fæt / (BrE)
(of land or soil) marked by great fertility or fruitfulness; rich and productive.

Examples

Fat soil
After years of careful farming, the once-barren fields became fat and full of life.
Legends were told of the fat valley that never failed to yield a harvest.
The settlers sought out the fat lands along the river for their crops.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barren - (of land) too poor to produce much or any vegetation
  • synonym: fertile - (of land) capable of producing abundant vegetation or crops
  • synonym: rich - (of land) containing plenty of nutrients and productive
