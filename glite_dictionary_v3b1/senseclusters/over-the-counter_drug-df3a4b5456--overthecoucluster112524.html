over-the-counter drug

noun
drug available without prescription
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚ ðə ˈkaʊntɚ ˌdrʌɡ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvə ðə ˈkaʊntə ˌdrʌɡ / (BrE)
A drug that can be purchased without a doctor's prescription.

Examples

An over-the-counter drug
He prefers to try over-the-counter drugs before visiting the doctor for his allergies.
Some cold medications are only available as over-the-counter drugs, while others require a prescription.
You can buy many painkillers as over-the-counter drugs at the pharmacy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prescription drug - a drug that can only be obtained with a doctor's prescription
