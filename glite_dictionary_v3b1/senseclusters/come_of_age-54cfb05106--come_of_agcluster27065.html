come of age

verb
become legally or socially an adult
Pronunciation: / kʌm əv eɪdʒ / (AmE), / kʌm əv eɪdʒ / (BrE)
to reach the age when a person legally becomes an adult or achieves full maturity

Examples

To come of age legally
He finally came of age and inherited his family's estate.
In many cultures, a young person is said to come of age at eighteen.
The tradition celebrates the day when boys and girls come of age.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mature - to become fully developed physically or mentally
  • antonym: minor - a person who has not reached legal adulthood
