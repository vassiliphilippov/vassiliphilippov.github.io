Senegalese franc

noun
former currency unit of Senegal
8.1
Pronunciation: /se.nə.gə.ˈliːz fræŋk/ (AmE), /se.nə.gə.ˈliːz fræŋk/ (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in Senegal, used before the country adopted the West African CFA franc.

Examples

The Senegalese franc currency
Before 1945, the Senegalese franc was used as the national currency.
Collectors sometimes seek rare coins of the Senegalese franc for their historical value.
The transition from the Senegalese franc to the CFA franc marked a significant change in the country's economic history.
