misrepresented

adjective
presented falsely or inaccurately
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˌrɛprɪˈzɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˌrɛprɪˈzɛntɪd / (BrE)
presented in a false or distorted way; having its true meaning or character incorrectly described

Examples

Misrepresented facts
Several statements in the article were misrepresented and gave readers a false impression.
She felt her intentions had been completely misrepresented in the press.
The lawyer argued that the evidence was misrepresented by the prosecution during the trial.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accurate - free from error; correct or true
  • synonym: distorted - altered from the true or natural shape; twisted or misrepresented
