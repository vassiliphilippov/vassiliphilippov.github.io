congenator

noun
organism of the same genus or shared ancestry
9.9 8.5
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈdʒen.ə.tər/ /; / /kən.dʒen.ˈeɪ.tər/ (AmE), /kən.ˈdʒen.ə.tə/ /; / /kən.dʒen.ˈeɪ.tə/ (BrE)
an animal or plant belonging to the same genus or having a shared common descent with another organism

Examples

A congenator species
Biologists often compare genetic traits among congenators to study evolutionary relationships.
The two frog species are congenators found in different parts of the rainforest.
Unlike its congenator, this plant thrives in much drier climates.
