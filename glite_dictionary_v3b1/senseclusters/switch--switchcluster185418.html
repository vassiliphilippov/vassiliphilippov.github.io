Examples

To switch sides

Synonyms

  • change over
    3.9 1.55/m
    change over is used for moving from one system or method to another, while switch is a general verb for changing between things.
  • shift
    2.0 65/m
    shift describes moving or changing position or focus, while switch emphasizes changing from one thing or person to another.
  • swap
    2.1 5.26/m
    swap means giving one thing for another in a direct trade, while switch covers general changes or substitutions.
  • exchange
    1.8 20/m
    exchange is to give and receive something in return, while switch highlights changing or shifting between options.
  • interchange
    4.1 0.26/m
    interchange suggests mutual replacement or use in both directions, while switch refers to changing from one thing to another.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alternate - to repeatedly change from one thing to another
  • synonym: change - to make or become different; to alter
  • synonym: exchange - to give something and receive something of the same kind in return
  • synonym: reverse - to change to the opposite direction, order, or position
  • antonym: stay - to remain with the same thing, person, or situation
  • synonym: swap - to give something and receive something in return
