egalitarian

noun
person who believes in equality for all
4.7
Pronunciation: / ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛriən / (AmE), / ɪˌɡælɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
a person who believes that all people should be equal and have the same rights and opportunities

Examples

An egalitarian thinker
As an egalitarian, he opposes any form of discrimination.
Many egalitarians advocate for changes in education and healthcare policies.
She has always considered herself an egalitarian and supports equal rights movements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egalitarian - believing in or based on the principle that all people are equal
