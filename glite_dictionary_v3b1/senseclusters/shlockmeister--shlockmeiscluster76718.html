shlockmeister

noun
someone who sells shoddy goods (slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃlɑkˌmaɪstər / (AmE), / ˈʃlɒkˌmaɪstə / (BrE)
(slang) A merchant who sells shoddy or inferior merchandise, especially in a flashy or unscrupulous manner.

Examples

A shlockmeister seller
Despite his flashy advertisements, everyone knew he was just a shlockmeister with nothing good to offer.
He earned a reputation as a shlockmeister after years of peddling fake designer watches at the market.
The neighborhood was full of shlockmeisters selling low-quality souvenirs to unsuspecting tourists.
