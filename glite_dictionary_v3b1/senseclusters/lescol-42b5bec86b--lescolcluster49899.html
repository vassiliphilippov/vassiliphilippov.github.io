Lescol

noun
brand name statin drug (fluvastatin)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛskoʊl / (AmE), / ˈlɛskɒl / (BrE)
A trademarked name for a statin drug (fluvastatin) used to lower cholesterol, typically taken orally at bedtime.
