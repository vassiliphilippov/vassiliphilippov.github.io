baul

adjective
of Baul tradition
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊl / (AmE), / ˈbaʊl / (BrE)
relating to the Baul musicians, their music, or their religious tradition of Bengal.

Examples

Baul tradition
The festival featured several baul performances that captivated the audience with their unique melodies.
She wore a traditional outfit inspired by baul culture for the cultural event.
Many visitors were fascinated by the baul songs and their spiritual themes.
