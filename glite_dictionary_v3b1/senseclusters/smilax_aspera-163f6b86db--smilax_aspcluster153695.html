Smilax aspera

noun
species of spiny climbing evergreen plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsmaɪlæks æsˈpɪrə / (AmE), / ˈsmaɪlæks æsˈpɪərə / (BrE)
A species of climbing or creeping evergreen plant with spiny, zigzag stems, shiny leaves, and racemes of pale-green flowers, native to regions from the Canary Islands and southern Europe to Ethiopia and India.
