Synonyms

  • unusual
    2.2 54/m
    unusual is not common or rare in appearance, while peculiar is different from what people expect and feels odd.
  • out of the ordinary
    4.1 1.53/m
    out of the ordinary means unexpected or not typical in everyday life, while peculiar sounds more personal and slightly strange.
  • odd
    1.0 41/m
    odd feels slightly strange or unexpected in casual talk, while peculiar is more marked and can seem a bit stranger.
  • strange
    2.3 84/m
    strange feels hard to understand or surprising to people, while peculiar is unusual but often a little less mysterious.
  • curious
    4.0 9.88/m
    curious can mean interesting or odd and invite questions, while peculiar mainly means unusual without being interesting.
  • singular
    7.0 1.72/m
    singular is strikingly unusual and often formal or positive in tone, while peculiar is simply odd or unexpected in everyday use.
