peculiar

adjective
unusual; strange
5.0
Pronunciation: / pɪˈkjuljɚ /; / pɪˈkjuljər / (AmE), / pɪˈkjuːliə / (BrE)
different from what is normal or expected; unusual or strange

Examples

Peculiar smell

The room had a peculiar smell that I couldn't quite identify.
There is something peculiar about the way those birds move.
His accent sounded peculiar to the local residents.
She has a peculiar sense of humor that not everyone appreciates.
There was a peculiar smell coming from the kitchen.
