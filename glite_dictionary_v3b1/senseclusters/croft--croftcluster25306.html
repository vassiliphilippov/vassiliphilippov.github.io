noun
small Scottish farm worked by a crofter
8.6 0.2/m
Pronunciation: / krÉ‘ft / (AmE), / krÉ’ft / (BrE)
a small farm, especially in Scotland, typically worked by a tenant farmer (crofter) and often including a house and land

Examples

A Scottish croft
For generations, the family has lived and worked their croft in northern Scotland.
He inherited a small croft on the Isle of Skye from his grandparents.
They spent the summer restoring the old stone house on the croft.
