spoiler alert

noun
warning before revealing plot details
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔɪlər əˌlɝt / (AmE), / ˈspɔɪlə əˌlɜːt / (BrE)
a warning that information about a book, film, or other narrative may reveal important plot details and potentially spoil the experience for those who have not yet read or seen it

Examples

Spoiler alert warning
Spoiler alert: The main character turns out to be the villain.
Before I discuss the ending, let me just say spoiler alert for anyone who hasn't read the book.
When writing reviews, he always includes a spoiler alert before discussing major plot twists.
