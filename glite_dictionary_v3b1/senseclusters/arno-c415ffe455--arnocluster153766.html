Arno

proper noun
major river in central Italy
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
a river in central Italy that rises in the Apennines and flows through Florence and Pisa to the Ligurian Sea

Examples

The Arno river
The Arno flooded parts of Florence in 1966, causing severe damage to the city.
The famous Ponte Vecchio bridges the Arno in Florence.
Tourists often enjoy strolling along the banks of the Arno in Pisa.
