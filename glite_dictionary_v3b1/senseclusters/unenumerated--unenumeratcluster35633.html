unenumerated

adjective
not listed individually
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪˈnuːmɚeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈnjuːməreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not individually listed or specified, especially in a formal document or list

Examples

Unenumerated rights
The court recognized certain unenumerated rights that are not explicitly mentioned in the constitution.
Some benefits remain unenumerated in the official policy document but are still protected by law.
The agreement covers both listed and unenumerated items relevant to the contract.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enumerated - individually listed or specified
  • synonym: unspecified - not stated clearly or in detail
