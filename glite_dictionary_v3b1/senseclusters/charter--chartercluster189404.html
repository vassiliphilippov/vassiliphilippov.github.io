charter

verb
hire or lease a vehicle or service
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːrtər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːtə / (BrE)
to hire or lease a vehicle, vessel, or aircraft for private use for a certain period

Examples

To charter a plane
The company charters private jets for business executives.
They chartered a yacht to explore the Caribbean islands.
We decided to charter a bus for the weekend trip to the mountains.
The company decided to charter a private jet for the executives' trip to Europe.
They chartered a boat for a week to explore the islands in comfort.
We are considering chartering a bus for the staff outing next month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lease - to grant or take the temporary possession of property or services for payment
  • synonym: rent - to pay for the temporary use of something
