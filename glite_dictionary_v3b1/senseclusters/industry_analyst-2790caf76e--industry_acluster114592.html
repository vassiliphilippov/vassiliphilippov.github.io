industry analyst

noun
expert evaluating industry trends
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndəstri ˈænəˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈɪndəstri ˈænəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies and evaluates the conditions, trends, and prospects of a particular industry, often for investment, advisory, or research purposes

Examples

Industry analyst reports
As an industry analyst, she closely monitors both established competitors and emerging startups.
Many investors rely on reports from an industry analyst to make informed decisions.
The industry analyst predicted significant growth for renewable energy companies over the next decade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: market analyst - a person who studies market trends and predicts their impact
