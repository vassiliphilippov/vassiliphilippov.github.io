ringing

noun
sound made by a bell or similar object
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the clear, resonant sound produced by a bell or similar object when struck

Examples

Ringing of a bell
She was startled by the sudden ringing of the phone late at night.
The ringing of the church bells could be heard all over the town.
The ringing outside signaled the start of the celebration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chime - a musical sound made by a bell or a set of bells
  • synonym: peal - a loud, prolonged ringing of bells
