field wormwood

noun
a species of European wormwood plant
8.1 8.8
Pronunciation: /fiːld ˈwɜːr.mʊd/ (AmE), /fiːld ˈwɜː.mʊd/ (BrE)
A European species of wormwood (Artemisia campestris) similar in properties to common wormwood, often found in dry fields and waste grounds.

Examples

Field wormwood plant
Field wormwood is often found in dry, sandy soils across much of Europe.
Herb gatherers prized field wormwood for its medicinal uses.
The silvery foliage of field wormwood makes it easy to recognize among wild plants.
