warm up

verb
become warm or warmer
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɔrm ʌp / (AmE), / wɔːm ʌp / (BrE)
to become warm or warmer, or to make something warmer

Examples

To warm up your hands
She warmed up her hands by holding them near the fire.
Stir the soup occasionally as it warms up on the stove.
The room began to warm up as the sun rose.
