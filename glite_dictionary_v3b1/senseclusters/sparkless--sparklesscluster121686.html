sparkless

adjective
without energy
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːrkləs / (AmE), / ˈspɑːkləs / (BrE)
lacking energy, excitement, or literal sparks

Examples

Sparkless performance
The movie was so sparkless that many people left before it ended.
Her speech felt sparkless and failed to inspire the audience.
After a long day, his conversation was unusually sparkless and quiet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dull - not lively or interesting
  • antonym: exciting - causing great enthusiasm and eagerness
  • synonym: lifeless - without energy or vitality
