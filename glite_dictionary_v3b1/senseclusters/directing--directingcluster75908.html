directing

adjective
giving guidance or showing the way
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛktɪŋ, daɪˈrɛktɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛktɪŋ, daɪˈrɛktɪŋ / (BrE)
giving guidance, supervision, or direction to someone or something; leading or showing the way

Examples

The committee plays a significant directing role in the organization's activities.
Under his directing influence, the team achieved remarkable success.
