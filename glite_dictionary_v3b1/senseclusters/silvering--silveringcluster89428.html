silvering

noun
metallic surface sheen
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvərɪŋ / (BrE)
a bright or metallic sheen that appears on a surface, especially as a result of aging or a photographic effect

Examples

Mirror silvering effect
The antique mirror showed signs of silvering around its edges.
Photographers sometimes notice silvering on old prints as they age.
A faint silvering gave the painting a unique, shimmering effect under the light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sheen - a soft shine or brightness on a surface
  • antonym: tarnish - a dullness or discoloration on a metal surface
