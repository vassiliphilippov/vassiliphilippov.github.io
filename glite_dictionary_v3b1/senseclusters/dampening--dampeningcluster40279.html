dampening

noun
making something slightly wet
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdæmpənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdæmpənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making something slightly wet

Examples

Light dampening of soil
A light dampening of the soil helps the seeds to germinate properly.
He applied a quick dampening to the cloth before wiping the dusty table.
The dampening of the shirt made it easier to iron out wrinkles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drying - the act of removing moisture or making dry
  • synonym: wetting - the act of making something wet
