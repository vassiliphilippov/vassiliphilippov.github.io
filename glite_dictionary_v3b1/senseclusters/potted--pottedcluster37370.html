potted

adjective
preserved or cooked in a pot or jar
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑt̬ɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɒtɪd / (BrE)
(of food) preserved or cooked and stored in a pot, jar, or can, often covered with fat or jelly.

Examples

Potted meat
She enjoyed potted meats as part of a traditional English breakfast.
The picnic basket included potted salmon, which was spread on fresh bread.
You can buy potted shrimp at many specialty food shops.
