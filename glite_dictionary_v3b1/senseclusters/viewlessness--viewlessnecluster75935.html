viewlessness

noun
absence of view
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvjuːləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈvjuːləsnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of having no view or being invisible to sight

Examples

Foggy viewlessness
The thick mist created a sense of viewlessness across the entire valley.
At night, the darkness brought an eerie viewlessness to the deserted street.
The artist captured the feeling of viewlessness in her painting of the foggy morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invisibility - the state of being impossible to see
  • synonym: opacity - the quality of not allowing light to pass through; not transparent
  • antonym: visibility - the state of being able to be seen
