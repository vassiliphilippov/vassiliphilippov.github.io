pearling

noun
formation of droplets
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the formation of small, rounded drops or beads on a surface, especially of moisture.

Examples

Pearling of water drops
After the rain, pearling appeared on the leaves, making them sparkle in the sunlight.
The pearling on the glass was a sign of high humidity inside the room.
Tiny pearling formed on the cold bottle as soon as it was taken out of the fridge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beading - the formation of small drops or beads on a surface
  • synonym: condensation - the process by which water vapor becomes liquid droplets
