interindividual

adjective
between individuals
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˌɪndəˈvɪdʒuəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntərˌɪndɪˈvɪdjʊəl / (BrE)
occurring or existing between different individuals

Examples

Interindividual differences
The study focused on interindividual variation in learning abilities among students.
Doctors noticed interindividual differences in how patients responded to the medication.
Understanding interindividual factors can help personalize treatment plans for each person.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intraindividual - occurring within a single individual
