diglossia

noun
two language varieties
Pronunciation: / daɪˈɡlɑsiə /; / daɪˈɡlɑːsiə / (AmE), / daɪˈɡlɒsiə / (BrE)
a situation where a community uses two varieties of a language or two different languages for different social or functional purposes
