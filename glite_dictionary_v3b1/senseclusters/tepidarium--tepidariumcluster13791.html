tepidarium

noun
lukewarm atmosphere
9.9
Pronunciation: /te.pɪ.ˈde.r.ɪ.əm/ (AmE), /te.pɪ.ˈde.r.ɪ.əm/ (BrE)
a situation or atmosphere characterized by lukewarmness or lack of enthusiasm, especially in discussions or meetings

Examples

Tepidarium mood
The annual review was held in a tepidarium of polite but unenthusiastic conversation.
Instead of lively debate, the classroom discussion became a tepidarium with little real engagement.
Her proposal was met with a tepidarium of mild interest and no strong opinions.
