app slave

noun
person overly dependent on apps
4.6
Pronunciation: / æp sleɪv / (AmE), / æp sleɪv / (BrE)
a person who is excessively dependent on mobile applications, especially to the point of constantly checking or using them

Examples

App slave behavior
He realized he was an app slave when he couldn't resist checking every notification instantly.
Many teens today risk turning into app slaves if they're not careful with their phone use.
She tries not to become an app slave by limiting her screen time each day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: digital detox - temporary period of refraining from use of digital devices
  • synonym: phone addict - someone addicted to their mobile phone
