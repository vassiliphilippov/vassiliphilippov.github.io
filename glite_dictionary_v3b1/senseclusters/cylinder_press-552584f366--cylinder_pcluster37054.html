cylinder press

noun
type of printing press using a rolling cylinder
8.1 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪl.ən.dər pres/ /; / /ˈsɪl.ɪn.dər pres/ (AmE), /ˈsɪl.ɪn.də pres/ /; / /ˈsɪl.ən.də pres/ (BrE)
a printing press in which the type is placed on a flat bed and a cylinder holding the paper rolls over it to transfer the image

Examples

A cylinder press machine
Early books were often printed using a cylinder press, which allowed for higher printing speeds.
The old newspaper office still has a working cylinder press from the nineteenth century.
With the introduction of the cylinder press, mass printing became much more efficient.
