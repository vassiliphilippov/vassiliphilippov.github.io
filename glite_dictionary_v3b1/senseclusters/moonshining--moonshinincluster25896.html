moonshining

noun
illegal liquor making
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌʃaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌʃaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making alcoholic liquor illegally, especially in secret and without government permission

Examples

Illegal moonshining
The old cabin was once used for moonshining during the days of Prohibition.
He learned the art of moonshining from his grandfather in the mountains.
Authorities discovered a hidden still used for moonshining deep in the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bootlegging - the illegal production or distribution of alcohol
  • degree lower: distilling - the process of making alcoholic spirits by distillation
  • antonym: legal brewing - the lawful production of alcoholic beverages
