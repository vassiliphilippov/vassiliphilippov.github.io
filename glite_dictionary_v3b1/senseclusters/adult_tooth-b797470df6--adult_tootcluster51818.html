adult tooth

noun
permanent tooth replacing a baby tooth
3.6
Pronunciation: / əˈdʌlt tuθ / (AmE), / əˈdʌlt tuːθ / (BrE)
a tooth that replaces a baby tooth in childhood and is meant to last through adulthood; one of the permanent teeth in humans

Examples

A adult tooth
After her last baby tooth fell out, all of her remaining teeth were adult teeth.
Dentists recommend taking good care of every adult tooth to avoid problems in later life.
Most children start losing their baby teeth around age six as each adult tooth begins to emerge.

Related Terms

  • antonym: baby tooth - a temporary tooth in children that is later replaced by a permanent tooth
  • synonym: permanent tooth - a tooth in humans that replaces a baby tooth and is meant to last through adulthood
