igloo

noun
dome-shaped snow shelter
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪgˌlu / (AmE), / ˈɪɡluː / (BrE)
A dome-shaped shelter built from blocks of snow, traditionally constructed by Inuit people.

Examples

A igloo shelter
Children learned how to build a small igloo during the winter camp.
The explorers took refuge from the storm in a sturdy igloo.
Tourists often visit the Arctic to experience sleeping in an authentic igloo.
