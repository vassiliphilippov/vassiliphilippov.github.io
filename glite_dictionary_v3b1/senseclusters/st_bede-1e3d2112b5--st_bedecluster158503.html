St. Bede

proper noun
English monk and scholar (672–735)
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈbiːd / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈbiːd / (BrE)
(also known as the Venerable Bede) An English monk and scholar (672–735) noted for his historical and theological writings.

Examples

St. Bede feast day
Every year, scholars commemorate the feast day of St. Bede.
Many historians rely on St. Bede for knowledge of early English history.
The writings of St. Bede have influenced scholars for centuries.
