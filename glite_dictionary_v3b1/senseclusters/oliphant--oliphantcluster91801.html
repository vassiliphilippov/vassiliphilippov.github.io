oliphant

noun
archaic word for elephant
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊlɪˌfænt / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪˌfɑːnt / (BrE)
an archaic word for an elephant, especially used in medieval literature

Examples

A mighty oliphant

Scholars studied the use of the word oliphant in medieval poetry and stories.
