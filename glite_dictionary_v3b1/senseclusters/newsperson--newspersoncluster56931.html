newsperson

noun
reporter or editor of news stories
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzˌpɜːrsən / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who investigates, reports, or edits news stories for newspapers, television, radio, or other media

Examples

A newsperson reports news
Each newsperson in the newsroom had a unique approach to gathering information.
It takes dedication and curiosity to succeed as a newsperson in today's media landscape.
The experienced newsperson covered the story with accuracy and integrity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: journalist - a person who writes for newspapers or magazines or prepares news to be broadcast
  • synonym: reporter - a person who reports news or conducts interviews for newspapers or broadcasts
