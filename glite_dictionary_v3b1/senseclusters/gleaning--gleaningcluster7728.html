gleaning

noun
collected bits
Pronunciation: / ˈɡliːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡliːnɪŋ / (BrE)
things that have been collected gradually, especially bits of information or leftover crops after harvesting

Examples

Historical gleanings
The historian's book was based on gleanings from old letters and diaries.
After the harvest, villagers gathered the gleanings left in the fields.
She shared her gleanings from months of research at the conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collection - a group of things gathered together
  • synonym: harvest - the process or period of gathering crops
