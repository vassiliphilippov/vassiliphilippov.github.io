monofuel

noun
single rocket propellant
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑnoʊˌfjuːəl /; / ˈmɑnoʊˌfjuːl / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəʊˌfjuːəl /; / ˈmɒnəʊˌfjuːl / (BrE)
a single fuel used as the only energy source for a vehicle, machine, or system
