hodoscope

noun
particle-tracing scientific instrument
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊdəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈhɒdəˌskəʊp / (BrE)
a device used in physics to detect and trace the path of a charged particle

Examples

A hodoscope device
Modern versions of the hodoscope can trace multiple particles simultaneously for advanced research.
Physicists used a hodoscope to analyze the patterns of cosmic rays entering the laboratory.
The hodoscope displayed a clear record of each particle's trajectory during the experiment.

  • synonym: detector - device that detects and measures physical quantities or particles
