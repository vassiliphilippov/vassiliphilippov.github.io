waste baler

noun
machine for compacting recyclable waste
7.6
Pronunciation: / weɪst ˈbeɪlər / (AmE), / weɪst ˈbeɪlə / (BrE)
a machine used to compact waste materials such as cardboard, plastic, and other recyclables into bales for easier handling and transportation

Examples

A waste baler machine
By using a waste baler, businesses can significantly reduce the volume of their discarded cardboard.
Operators must be trained to use the waste baler safely to avoid workplace injuries.
The company purchased a new waste baler to streamline their recycling process.
