polygrapher

noun
lie detector operator
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɑɪɡrəfər / (AmE), / pɒˈlɪɡrəfə / (BrE)
a person trained to operate a polygraph, or lie detector, machine

Examples

Polygrapher gives a test
The polygrapher carefully monitored the subject's responses during the test.
After the interview, the polygrapher reviewed the data with the police officers.
Becoming a certified polygrapher requires specialized training and experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: examiner - a person who conducts tests or examinations
  • synonym: interrogator - a person who questions someone closely, often in an official context
