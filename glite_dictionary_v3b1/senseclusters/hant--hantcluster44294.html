hant

noun
ghost or spirit
9.9
Pronunciation: / hænt / (AmE), / hænt / (BrE)
an old or dialect word for a ghost or spirit, especially one that haunts a place

Examples

A hant at night
The villagers believed a hant wandered the fields at night, searching for peace.
Old stories tell of a hant that guards the abandoned mill on the riverbank.
Some say the eerie noises in the attic are made by a restless hant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ghost - the spirit of a dead person, often believed to appear to the living
  • synonym: spirit - a supernatural being or essence, often invisible
