Synonyms

  • cathode-ray oscilloscope
    8.2 0.00/m
    a cathode-ray oscilloscope uses a bright CRT beam to paint waveforms on a screen, while an oscilloscope can use various display types.
  • CRO
    8.4 0.01/m
    CRO is the common lab name for a CRT oscilloscope used by technicians, while an oscilloscope is the full technical term used in documents.
  • oscillograph
    9.9 0.01/m
    an oscillograph records and prints waveforms often on paper or film, while an oscilloscope usually shows signals live on a screen.
  • scope
    7.9 2/m
    scope is an informal short name used in labs and workshops, while an oscilloscope is the full, precise instrument name.
