lag

verb
insulate pipes or structures
Pronunciation: / læɡ / (AmE), / læɡ / (BrE)
to cover pipes or other structures with insulating material to prevent heat loss.

Examples

To lag pipes
After the renovation, all the exposed ducts were carefully lagged to meet safety standards.
It is essential to lag any external pipes to prevent them from freezing in winter.
The engineers decided to lag the hot water pipes to improve energy efficiency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insulate - to cover or protect something to prevent heat, sound, or electricity from escape or entry
