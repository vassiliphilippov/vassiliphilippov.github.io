kapo

noun
camp prisoner supervisor
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.poʊ/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.pəʊ/ (BrE)
a prisoner in a concentration camp who was assigned by guards to supervise other prisoners or perform administrative duties

Examples

Camp kapo
The kapo enforced strict rules among the prisoners in the barracks.
Many survivors recalled the cruelty of a particular kapo during their time in the camp.
Becoming a kapo sometimes meant betraying fellow prisoners to survive.
