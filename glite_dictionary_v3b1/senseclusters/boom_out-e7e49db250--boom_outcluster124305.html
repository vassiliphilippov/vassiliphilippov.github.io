boom out

verb
emit a deep, loud sound
5.7 0.24/m
Pronunciation: / buÀêm a ät / (AmE), / buÀêm a ät / (BrE)
to make a deep, loud, and resonant sound, especially so as to be clearly heard at a distance

Examples

Name boomed out loudly

The announcer's voice boomed out over the loudspeakers, grabbing everyone's attention.
Thunder boomed out across the valley, shaking the windows of the old house.
