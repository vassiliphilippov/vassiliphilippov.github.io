welt

noun
raised mark on the skin
Pronunciation: / wɛlt / (AmE), / wɛlt / (BrE)
a raised, red mark on the skin, usually caused by a blow, pressure, or an allergic reaction

Examples

A red welt
A red welt formed where the insect had bitten him.
She broke out in a welt after touching the plant.
The leather strap left a long welt across his arm.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bruise - an injury appearing as a purple or dark mark on the skin
  • synonym: weal - a red, swollen mark left on flesh by a blow or pressure
