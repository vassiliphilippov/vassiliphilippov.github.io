Irish whiskey

noun
whiskey made in Ireland, mainly from barley
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrɪʃ ˈwɪski / (AmE), / ˈaɪərɪʃ ˈwɪski / (BrE)
A type of whiskey distilled and matured in Ireland, typically made chiefly from barley, and known for its smooth flavor profile.
