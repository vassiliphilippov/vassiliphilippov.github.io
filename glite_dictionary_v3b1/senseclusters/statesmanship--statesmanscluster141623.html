statesmanship

noun
skillful management of public affairs
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪts.mənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈsteɪts.mən.ʃɪp / (BrE)
The ability to manage public affairs with wisdom, skill, and integrity, especially in government or politics.

Examples

Good statesmanship skills
Good statesmanship requires vision, patience, and the ability to unite people.
Her statesmanship was evident in the way she handled the crisis calmly and wisely.
The nation benefited from his years of experience and statesmanship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diplomacy - the skill of managing international relations
  • synonym: politics - activities associated with the governance of a country or area
