carabineros

proper noun
Chilean police force
Pronunciation: / ˌkærəbiˈneɪroʊs / (AmE), / ˌkærəbiˈnɛːrɒs / (BrE)
the national uniformed police force of Chile, responsible for maintaining public order and safety

Examples

Chilean carabineros police
The carabineros patrolled the Plaza de Armas during the national holiday to maintain public order.
After the protest, the carabineros helped restore calm in the city center.
Tourists often see carabineros directing traffic and assisting people in Santiago.

Related Terms

  • synonym: police - an official organization that enforces the law and maintains public order
