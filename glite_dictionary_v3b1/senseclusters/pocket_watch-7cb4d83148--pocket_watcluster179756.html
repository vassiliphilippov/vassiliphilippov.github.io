pocket watch

noun
watch designed to be carried in a pocket
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːkɪt wɑːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt wɒtʃ / (BrE)
a watch designed to be carried in a pocket, typically attached to a chain

Examples

A pocket watch chain
A silver pocket watch dangled from the chain in his jacket pocket.
He took his pocket watch from his waistcoat to check the time.
The antique pocket watch was passed down through generations in her family.

Related Terms

  • antonym: wristwatch - a watch designed to be worn on the wrist
