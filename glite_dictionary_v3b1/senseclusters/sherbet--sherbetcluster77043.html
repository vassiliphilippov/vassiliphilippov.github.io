sherbet

noun
frozen dessert with fruit juice and dairy
8.2 1.8
Pronunciation: /ˈʃɜːr.bət/ /; / /ˈʃɜːr.bɪt/ /; / /ˈʃɜːr.bərt/ (AmE), /ˈʃɜː.bət/ /; / /ˈʃɜː.bɪt/ (BrE)
