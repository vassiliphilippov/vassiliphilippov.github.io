mutant

adjective
undergoing or resulting from mutation
6.5 1.46/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmjuÀêt…ônt / (AmE), / ÀàmjuÀêt…ônt / (BrE)
tending to undergo or resulting from mutation, especially used in genetics or biology.

Examples

Synonyms

  • mutational
    8.1 0.11/m

  • mutable
    7.9 0.01/m
    mutable is able to change or be changed genetically, while mutant is already altered as a result of a mutation.
