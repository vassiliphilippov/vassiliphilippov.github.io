gelatinousness

noun
state of being gelatinous or jelly-like
7.3
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈlætənuːsnəs / (AmE), / dʒəˈlatɪnəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being gelatinous; having a jelly-like consistency or viscosity

Examples

The gelatinousness of stew
After prolonged cooking, the stew developed a noticeable gelatinousness due to the dissolved collagen.
Scientists measured the gelatinousness of the sample to understand its potential uses in medical implants.
The gelatinousness of the dessert made it both fun and challenging to eat with chopsticks.

  • synonym: viscous - having a thick, sticky consistency between solid and liquid
