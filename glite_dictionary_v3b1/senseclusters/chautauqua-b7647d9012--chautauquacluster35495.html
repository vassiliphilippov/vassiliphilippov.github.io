Chautauqua

proper noun
educational center in New York
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈtɔkwə / (AmE), / ʃəˈtɔːkwə / (BrE)
the Chautauqua Institution, an educational and cultural center on Chautauqua Lake, New York, known for summer programs of lectures and performances

Examples

Chautauqua Institution
