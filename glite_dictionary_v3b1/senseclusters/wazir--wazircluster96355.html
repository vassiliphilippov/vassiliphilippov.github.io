wazir

noun
Pashtun tribe member
9.9
Pronunciation: / wəˈzɪr / (AmE), / wəˈzɪə / (BrE)
a member of the Wazir, a Pashtun tribe living in southeastern Afghanistan and northwestern Pakistan, or the tribe as a whole

Examples
