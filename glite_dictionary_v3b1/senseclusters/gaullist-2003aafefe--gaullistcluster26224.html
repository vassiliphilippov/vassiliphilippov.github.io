Gaullist

noun
supporter of de Gaulle
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊlɪst / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːlɪst / (BrE)
a supporter of the policies or ideas of Charles de Gaulle, especially in French politics

Examples

A Gaullist leader
The Gaullist movement influenced many aspects of modern French government.
He identified himself as a proud Gaullist during the political debate.
Several Gaullists were elected to parliament after the reforms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Bonapartist - a supporter of the policies or ideas of Napoleon Bonaparte
  • synonym: de Gaulle - French general and statesman, leader of Free France during World War II
  • antonym: opponent - someone who opposes or is against a particular policy or leader
