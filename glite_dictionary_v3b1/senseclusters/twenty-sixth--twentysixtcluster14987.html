twenty-sixth

adjective
ordinal number, 26th in a sequence
Pronunciation: / ˌtwɛntiˈsɪksθ / (AmE), / ˌtwentiˈsɪksθ / (BrE)
Coming next after the twenty-fifth in a sequence; being number 26 in order.

Examples

Twenty-sixth place

Her birthday falls on the twenty-sixth of June each year.
This is the twenty-sixth time he has visited the museum.
