Synonyms

  • stop
    1.0 16/m
    stop is a full ending of movement or action, while halt often suggests a short, temporary pause.
  • pause
    1.0 16/m
    pause is a brief break in activity or speech, while halt can mean a firmer or more complete stopping.
  • stoppage
    4.1 0.79/m
    stoppage is a period when activity is forced to stop, while halt is a general pause or interruption.
  • interruption
    3.0 7.79/m
    interruption is an event that breaks continuity suddenly, while halt is any stop or pause in movement or progress.
  • standstill
    3.5 1.99/m
    standstill is a complete stop with no movement or progress, while halt can be a shorter, partial pause.
  • suspension
    4.3 4.20/m
    suspension is an official or planned temporary stopping, while halt is a plain stop or pause in action.
  • hiatus
    6.3 2.18/m
    hiatus is a gap in a series or process, often longer and formal, while halt is a simple stop or pause.
