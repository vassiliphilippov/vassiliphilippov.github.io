sati

proper noun
Hindu goddess
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːti / (AmE), / ˈsɑːti / (BrE)
a Hindu goddess, daughter of Daksha and first wife of Shiva, who self-immolated and is venerated in some traditions

Examples

Sati goddess worship
Many temples in India are dedicated to Sati and her story of devotion.
The legend of Sati is an important part of Hindu mythology and religious texts.
Artists often depict Sati alongside Shiva in traditional paintings and sculptures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Parvati - the Hindu goddess, reincarnation of Sati and wife of Shiva
