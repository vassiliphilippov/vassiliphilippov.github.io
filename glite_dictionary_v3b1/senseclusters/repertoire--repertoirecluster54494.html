repertoire

noun
the works an artist or group can perform
7.2 2.69/m
Pronunciation: / Ààr…õp…ôrÀåtw…ër / (AmE), / Ààr…õp…ôtw…ëÀê / (BrE)
the complete list of works such as plays, songs, operas, or pieces that an artist or company can perform and regularly does perform

Examples

A singer's repertoire
Jazz musicians often expand their repertoire by learning new standards.
She has a vast repertoire of classical piano pieces for every occasion.
The theatre company added a new comedy to its repertoire this season.
