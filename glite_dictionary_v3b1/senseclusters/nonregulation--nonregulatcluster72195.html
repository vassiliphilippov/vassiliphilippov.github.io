nonregulation

noun
lack of official controls
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌrɛɡjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌrɛɡjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the absence of official rules, controls, or supervision over an activity or industry

Examples

Banking nonregulation
The financial crisis was partly caused by years of nonregulation in the banking sector.
Critics argue that nonregulation can lead to unsafe products reaching consumers.
Due to nonregulation, some companies operated without any oversight or accountability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deregulation - the removal or reduction of government rules or controls
  • antonym: regulation - the act of controlling or governing by rules
