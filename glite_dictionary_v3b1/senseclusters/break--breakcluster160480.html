Synonyms

  • fracture
    4.7 2.91/m
    fracture is the medical word for a clean crack in hard things like bones, while break is the general everyday verb.
  • crack
    1.0 18/m
    crack is a line or small split that may not separate an object fully, while break is the broader verb for damaging.
  • rupture
    6.8 3.66/m
    rupture is a sudden violent bursting or tearing of material like pipes or tissue, while break is the more common word.
  • smash
    2.1 14/m
    smash means hit or strike something until it shatters and is ruined, while break can be milder or accidental.
