toasting fork

noun
long-handled fork for toasting food
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊstɪŋ fɔrk / (AmE), / ˈtəʊstɪŋ fɔːk / (BrE)
A long-handled fork used for cooking or toasting food such as bread or frankfurters, especially over an open fire.

Examples

A toasting fork over fire
After years of use, the old toasting fork was still hanging by the hearth.
She used a toasting fork to brown the bread over the fireplace.
The children enjoyed roasting marshmallows with a toasting fork during their camping trip.
