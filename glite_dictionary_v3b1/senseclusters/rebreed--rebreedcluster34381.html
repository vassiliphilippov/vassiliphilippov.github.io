rebreed

verb
to breed again
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbriːd / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbriːd / (BrE)
to breed an animal or plant again, especially after a previous breeding attempt or cycle

Examples

To rebreed animals
After the first litter was adopted, the farmer decided to rebreed the dog in the spring.
The horticulturist planned to rebreed the rare orchid to improve its color.
If the initial attempt fails, you may need to rebreed the animals next season.
