Examples

To charbroil meat

Synonyms

  • grill
    1.0 9.30/m
    grill is cooking food over direct heat on a grate, while charbroil means using hot coals or open flame to char food.
  • barbecue
    1.3 1.46/m
    barbecue is cooking meat outdoors often slowly over smoke and flame, while charbroil focuses on quick charring over hot coals.
  • broil
    2.8 2.04/m
    broil is cooking under very high heat from above in an oven or grill, while charbroil means grilling over open coals.
