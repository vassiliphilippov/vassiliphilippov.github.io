charbroil

verb
to grill over flame
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɑːr.brɔɪl/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɑː.brɔɪl/ (BrE)
to cook food over an open flame or hot coals, usually on a grill.

Examples

To charbroil meat
They love to charbroil burgers at summer barbecues for that smoky flavor.
The chef decided to charbroil the chicken to give it a crisp, charred exterior.
You can charbroil vegetables for a delicious, slightly smoky taste.

  • synonym: roast - to cook food using dry heat, often in an oven or over a fire
