slab

noun
thick, flat piece or block
7.8
Pronunciation: / slæb / (AmE), / slæb / (BrE)
A thick, flat piece or slice of solid material, such as stone, concrete, or food, usually with broad and even surfaces.

Examples

A slab of stone
He cut a thick slab of cheese to serve with the bread.
She placed a heavy slab of marble on the table as a centerpiece.
The workers poured concrete into molds to make a slab for the driveway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: block - a solid piece of hard material, similar shape or purpose
