cyst

noun
an abnormal fluid-filled sac in the body
6.2
Pronunciation: / sɪst / (AmE), / sɪst / (BrE)
an abnormal, sac-like structure in the body, usually filled with fluid or other material

Examples

A cyst on wrist
A small cyst developed on his wrist after the injury.
The doctor removed a cyst from her ovary during surgery.
Ultrasound imaging confirmed that the lump was a harmless cyst.
A sebaceous cyst can sometimes become infected and require medical treatment.
After surgery, the painful cyst was removed and sent for analysis.
The doctor discovered a small cyst on her ovary during the examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abscess - a swollen area within body tissue, containing pus
  • degree higher: tumor - an abnormal mass of tissue in the body
