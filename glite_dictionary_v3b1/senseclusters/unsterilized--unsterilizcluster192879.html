unsterilized

adjective
not sterilized; not made free from germs
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɛrəˌlaɪzd /; / ˌʌnˈstɛrəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɛrɪlaɪzd /; / ˌʌnˈstɛr.ɪ.laɪzd / (BrE)
not made free from living microorganisms; not sterilized.

Examples

An unsterilized instrument
All instruments used in the procedure must be sterilized, not unsterilized.
The water was considered unsafe because it was unsterilized.
Using unsterilized needles can lead to serious infections.
It is unsafe to drink water from an unsterilised bottle.
The kittens were found in an unsterilised shelter, increasing the risk of infection.
The surgeon refused to use the unsterilised instruments during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sterilised - made free from bacteria or other living microorganisms
  • antonym: sterilized - made free from living microorganisms; rendered sterile
