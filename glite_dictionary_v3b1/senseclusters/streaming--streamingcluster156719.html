streaming

noun
movement of cytoplasm in a cell
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstriːmɪŋ / (BrE)
the continuous movement of cytoplasm within a living cell, distributing nutrients and organelles

Examples

Cytoplasmic streaming
Cytoplasmic streaming helps distribute materials efficiently inside plant cells.
Disruption of streaming can interfere with cellular functions.
The microscope allowed us to observe streaming in the cell's cytoplasm.
