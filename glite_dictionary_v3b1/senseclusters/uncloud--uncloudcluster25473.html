uncloud

verb
to make clear
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈklaʊd / (AmE), / ʌnˈklaʊd / (BrE)
to make something clear or free from clouds, confusion, or obscurity

Examples

To uncloud the mind
A good night's sleep helped to uncloud her mind.
The morning sun began to uncloud the sky after the storm.
Talking with a friend can sometimes uncloud your thoughts when you feel confused.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarify - to make something easier to understand or less confused
  • antonym: obscure - to make something unclear or difficult to understand
