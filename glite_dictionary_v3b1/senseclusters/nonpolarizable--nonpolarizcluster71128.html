nonpolarizable

adjective
not able to be magnetized
Pronunciation: / nɑnˌpoʊləˈraɪzəbəl / (AmE), / nɒnˌpəʊləˈraɪzəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be polarized or to develop a dipole moment when exposed to a magnetic or electric field
