fat metabolism

noun
the body's process of breaking down fats
6.7 3.1
Pronunciation: /fæt mə.ˈtæb.ə.lɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /fæt mɪ.ˈtæb.ə.lɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the process by which the body breaks down fats into fatty acids and glycerol, and further into simpler compounds to be used by cells for energy and other functions

Examples

Fat metabolism process
Disorders in fat metabolism can lead to health problems such as fatty liver disease.
Regular exercise can improve fat metabolism and help with weight management.
Some diets claim to boost your fat metabolism for faster weight loss.

  • synonym: lipid metabolism - the processes involved in the synthesis and degradation of lipids in cells
