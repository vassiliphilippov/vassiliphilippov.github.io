First Crusade

proper noun
Christian military campaign (1096-1099) to capture Jerusalem
9.9
Pronunciation: /fɜːrst kruː.ˈseɪd/ (AmE), /fɜːst kruː.ˈseɪd/ (BrE)
a military expedition by Western European Christians from 1096 to 1099, aimed at recapturing Jerusalem from Muslim control, resulting in the establishment of several Crusader states
