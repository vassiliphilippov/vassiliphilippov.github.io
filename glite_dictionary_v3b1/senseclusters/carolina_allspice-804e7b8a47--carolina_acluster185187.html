Carolina allspice

noun
a fragrant shrub of the southeastern U.S.
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌkæɹəˈlaɪnə ˈɔlˌspaɪs / (AmE), / ˌkarəˈlʌɪnə ˈɔːlˌspʌɪs / (BrE)
