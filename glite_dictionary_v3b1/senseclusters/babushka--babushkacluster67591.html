babushka

noun
elderly woman, grandmother
8.5
Pronunciation: / bəˈbuʃkə / (AmE), / bəˈbʊʃkə / (BrE)
an elderly woman, especially a grandmother, typically of Russian or Eastern European origin

Examples

A kind babushka
My babushka used to bake delicious bread every Sunday for the whole family.
The children listened closely as their babushka shared stories from her village in Russia.
At the festival, a friendly babushka offered us homemade pastries and tea.

Related Terms

  • antonym: grandfather - the father of one's parent
  • synonym: grandmother - the mother of one's parent
