sequencer

noun
music arrangement device
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsiː.kwən.sər/ (AmE), /ˈsiː.kwən.sə/ (BrE)
a device or software that arranges sounds, musical notes, or events in a specific order, often for music production

Examples

Modern electronic bands often rely on a sequencer to arrange their backing tracks.
She learned how to use a sequencer to compose her own digital music at home.
