sequencer

noun
music arrangement device
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːkwənsər / (AmE), / ˈsiːkwənsə / (BrE)
a device or software that arranges sounds, musical notes, or events in a specific order, often for music production

Examples

Music sequencer
The music producer programmed the sequencer to play a complex rhythm automatically.
Modern electronic bands often rely on a sequencer to arrange their backing tracks.
She learned how to use a sequencer to compose her own digital music at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drum machine - an electronic device that produces drum sounds and patterns
  • synonym: synthesizer - an electronic instrument that generates and modifies sounds
