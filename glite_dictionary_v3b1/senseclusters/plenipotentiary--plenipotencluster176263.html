plenipotentiary

noun
diplomat with full powers
9.9 8.3
Pronunciation: /plen.ɪ.poʊ.ˈten.ʃə.riː/ /; / /plen.ɪ.pə.ˈten.ʃə.riː/ (AmE), /plen.ɪ.pəʊ.ˈten.ʃə.riː/ /; / /plen.ɪ.pəʊ.ˈten.ʃər.iː/ (BrE)
a diplomat who has full authority to represent their government, especially in negotiating and signing treaties

Examples

A plenipotentiary diplomat
As a plenipotentiary, she had the authority to sign binding agreements on behalf of her country.
The conference was attended by several plenipotentiaries from different nations.
The government appointed a new plenipotentiary to negotiate the peace treaty.
