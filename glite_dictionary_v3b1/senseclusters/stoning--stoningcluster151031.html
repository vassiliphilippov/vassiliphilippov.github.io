stoning

noun
punishment by throwing stones at someone
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊ.nɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstəʊ.nɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of throwing stones at a person as a form of punishment, sometimes resulting in death; historically used as a judicial or extrajudicial form of capital punishment

Examples

Stoning punishment
Historical records document the use of stoning as a method of execution in some societies.
The criminal was sentenced to stoning for his actions under ancient laws.
They protested against stoning, calling it a cruel and inhumane punishment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lapidation - the legal or formal term for stoning as punishment
