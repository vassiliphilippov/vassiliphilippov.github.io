home equity credit

noun
loan secured by home equity
Pronunciation: / hoʊm ˈɛk.wɪ.ti ˈkrɛd.ɪt / (AmE), / həʊm ˈɛk.wɪ.ti ˈkrɛd.ɪt / (BrE)
a loan in which the borrower's equity in their home serves as collateral, often used for major expenses

Examples

Apply for home equity credit
Applying for home equity credit requires a thorough assessment of your property's value and equity.
Many homeowners use home equity credit to finance renovations or pay off high-interest debt.
She decided to consolidate her loans by taking out home equity credit from her bank.
