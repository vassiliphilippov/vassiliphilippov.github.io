mythbuster

noun
debunker of myths
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪθˌbʌstər / (AmE), / ˈmɪθˌbʌstə / (BrE)
a person or group who investigates and tests the truth of popular beliefs, stories, or myths

Examples

The mythbusters team
The mythbusters designed a series of tests to challenge the old urban legend.
As a self-proclaimed mythbuster, she enjoys researching the facts behind common misconceptions.
The documentary featured a team of mythbusters examining famous historical claims.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skeptic - a person who questions accepted beliefs
