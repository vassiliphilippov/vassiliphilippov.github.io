reclasp

verb
to fasten again
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈklæsp / (AmE), / ˌriːˈklɑːsp / (BrE)
to fasten or close again with a clasp or similar fastening device

Examples

To reclasp a bracelet

He had to reclasp his watch because the strap came loose during the meeting.
The jeweler reclasped the necklace to ensure it was secure before returning it to the customer.
