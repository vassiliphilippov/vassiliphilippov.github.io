Mandara

noun
Chadic language of Cameroon
Pronunciation: / mænˈdɑːrə / (AmE), / mænˈdɑːrə / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in the Mandara mountains of Cameroon, notable for having only two vowels

Examples

Mandara language
Linguists study Mandara because of its unique vowel system.
She is fluent in both French and Mandara, facilitating communication in her village.
The Mandara language is primarily used by communities in the mountainous regions of northern Cameroon.
