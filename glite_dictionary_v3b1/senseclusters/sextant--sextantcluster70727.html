sextant

noun
an angular distance of sixty degrees
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkstənt / (AmE), / ˈsɛkstənt / (BrE)
a unit of angular distance equal to sixty degrees, especially in geometry or astronomy

Examples

A sextant angle
A sextant represents exactly one-sixth of a full circle, or sixty degrees.
In geometry, each interior angle of a regular hexagon is a sextant.
The ancient astronomers divided the sky into sextants to measure positions of stars.
