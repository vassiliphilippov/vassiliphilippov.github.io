unflappably

adverb
calmly, without panic
7.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈflæpəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈflæpəbli / (BrE)
in a calm and unshakable manner, not easily upset or disturbed

Examples

To answer unflappably
Despite the unexpected questions, she answered unflappably and impressed the interviewers.
He unflappably managed the emergency, keeping everyone calm and focused.
The pilot reacted unflappably to the turbulence, reassuring the passengers with his steady voice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calmly - in a peaceful and relaxed way
  • antonym: nervously - in an anxious or worried way
