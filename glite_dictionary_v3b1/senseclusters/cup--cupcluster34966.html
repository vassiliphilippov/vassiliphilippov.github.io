cup

verb
form something into a cup shape
5.8
Pronunciation: / kʌp / (AmE), / kʌp / (BrE)
to shape the hands or another part of the body so that they form a curved, hollow form like a cup

Examples

To cup your hands
He gently cupped the bird in his palms before releasing it.
She cupped her hands to drink from the stream.
The nurse cupped the child's face to comfort her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hollow - make a space inside by removing substance
