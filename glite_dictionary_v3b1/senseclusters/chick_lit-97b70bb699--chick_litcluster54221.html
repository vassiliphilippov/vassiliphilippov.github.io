chick lit

noun
light fiction genre for young women
Pronunciation: / tʃɪk lɪt / (AmE), / tʃɪk lɪt / (BrE)
a genre of light, often humorous fiction primarily aimed at young women, focusing on their experiences and relationships

Examples

Popular chick lit books
Her favorite author writes chick lit that never fails to make her laugh.
Many readers enjoy chick lit because of its relatable characters and witty dialogue.
The new bestseller dominated the chick lit section of the bookstore for months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: romance novel - a novel centered around a romantic relationship, typically with a happy ending
