Hibiscus heterophyllus

noun
Australian tree with acid leaves
9.9
Pronunciation: / haɪˈbɪskəs ˌhɛt̬əroʊˈfɪləs / (AmE), / hɪˈbɪskəs ˌhɛtərəʊˈfɪləs / (BrE)
An Australian tree or shrub in the mallow family (Malvaceae), known for its acid-tasting leaves and large flowers.
