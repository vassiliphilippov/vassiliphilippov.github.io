trawl

verb
fish using a trawl net from a boat
4.2
Pronunciation: / trɔl / (AmE), / trɔːl / (BrE)
to fish by dragging a large net or line with hooks through the water

Examples

To trawl for fish

Fishermen trawl the ocean floor to catch shrimp and other seafood.
The police trawled through hours of video footage to find clues.
She trawled the internet for the best deals on flights.
