articular muscle

noun
muscle attaching to a joint capsule
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈtɪkjələr ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪkjʊlə ˈmʌsl / (BrE)
a muscle that attaches directly to the capsule of a joint

Examples

Articular muscles attach joint
Damage to the articular muscles can contribute to joint pain and dysfunction.
During surgery, the articular muscle was carefully separated from the joint capsule.
The articular muscle plays a crucial role in maintaining joint stability.
