primordial dwarf

noun
person with a rare genetic form of dwarfism
8.0
Pronunciation: / praɪˈmɔr.di.əl dwɔrf / (AmE), / praɪˈmɔː.di.əl dwɔːf / (BrE)
a person with a rare genetic form of dwarfism characterized by extremely small stature, with body proportions, mental, and sexual development that are otherwise generally normal.

Examples

A primordial dwarf person
A primordial dwarf usually has normal mental and sexual development, unlike some other forms of dwarfism.
Doctors diagnosed him as a primordial dwarf after specialized genetic testing.
The family attended a conference to learn more about raising a primordial dwarf.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: achondroplasia - the most common type of genetic dwarfism
  • synonym: dwarfism - condition of unusually short stature resulting from genetic or medical causes
