dime

verb
to give small money
8.9
Pronunciation: / daɪm / (AmE), / daɪm / (BrE)
to give someone a small amount of money, especially a dime or a couple of dollars

Examples

To dime a friend
I dimed my friend for lunch when he forgot his wallet.
She dimed her coworker a couple of dollars for the vending machine.
Whenever someone is short on change, Mark is quick to dime them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donate - to give money or goods to help someone
  • synonym: lend - to give someone money temporarily
