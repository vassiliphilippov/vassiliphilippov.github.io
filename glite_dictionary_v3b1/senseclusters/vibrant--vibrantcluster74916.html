vibrant

adjective
strong and resonant (of sound)
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪ.brənt / (AmE), / ˈvaɪ.brənt / (BrE)
(of a sound) strong, resonant, and full of energy.

Examples

Vibrant sound
A vibrant note from the cello set the tone for the entire orchestra.
He recalled the vibrant laughter echoing throughout his childhood home.
The singer's vibrant voice filled the concert hall and moved the audience.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: booming - (of sound) loud, deep, and resonant
  • degree lower: mellow - (of sound) pleasantly smooth or soft; free from harshness
  • synonym: resonant - (of sound) deep, clear, and continuing to sound or ring
