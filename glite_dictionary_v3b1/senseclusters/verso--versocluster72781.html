verso

noun
left-hand page of an open book
8.3 0.22/m
Pronunciation: / Ààv…úrso ä / (AmE), / Ààv…úÀês…ô ä / (BrE)
the left-hand page of an open book, or the page that appears on the left when a book is open, typically bearing even page numbers.
