halo

verb
to surround with light
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈheɪləʊ / (BrE)
to surround or encircle something with or as if with a ring of light

Examples

To halo a mountain
The mountain peak was haloed by the rising sun.
A soft glow haloed her silhouette as she stood by the window.
The streetlights haloed the wet pavement after the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encircle - to form a circle around something
  • synonym: envelop - to surround or cover completely
