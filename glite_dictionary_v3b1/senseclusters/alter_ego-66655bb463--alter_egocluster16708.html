alter ego

noun
a very close and trusted friend
Pronunciation: / ˌɔltər ˈiːɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɔːltə ˈiːɡəʊ / (BrE)
a very close and trusted friend who seems almost a part of yourself; a person’s inseparable companion or kindred spirit

Examples

His alter ego friend
Everyone knew that Mark and Leo were inseparable, with Leo almost acting as Mark’s alter ego.
She considered her childhood friend her alter ego—someone who always understood her without words.
The novelist’s main character is widely seen as his alter ego in the story.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enemy - a person who is actively opposed or hostile
  • synonym: soulmate - a person ideally suited to another as a close friend or romantic partner
