colorway

noun
color combination
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlərˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈkʌləˌweɪ / (BrE)
a specific combination or arrangement of colors used in a product, design, or artwork

Examples

A colorway design
The designer released the jacket in a new colorway featuring blue and gold accents.
Collectors often seek out rare colorways of popular sneakers.
This rug is available in several colorways to match different room styles.
The artist released a new print in a vibrant colorway featuring blues and oranges.
Each season, the brand introduces sneakers in a different colorway to attract collectors.
She preferred the classic black-and-white colorway for her living room decor.

  • synonym: palette - a range of colors used in a particular design or artwork
