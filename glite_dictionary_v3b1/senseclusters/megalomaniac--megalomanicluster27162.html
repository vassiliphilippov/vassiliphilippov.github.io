megalomaniac

noun
person obsessed with power or self-importance
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛɡəloʊˈmeɪniæk / (AmE), / ˌmɛɡələˈmeɪnɪak / (BrE)
a person who has an obsession with power and grandiose ideas about their own importance, often to a pathological degree

Examples

A megalomaniac leader
Her colleagues joked that a megalomaniac must have designed the enormous new office.
Many saw the dictator as a megalomaniac who put his own interests above all else.
The megalomaniac believed he was destined to rule the entire country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egotist - a person who is excessively conceited or self-absorbed
