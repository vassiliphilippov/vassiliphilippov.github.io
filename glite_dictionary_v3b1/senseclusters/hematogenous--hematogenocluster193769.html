hematogenous

adjective
carried by blood
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːməˈtɑdʒənəs / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈtɒdʒənəs / (BrE)
originating in or carried by the blood

Examples

Hematogenous infection
The bacteria caused a hematogenous infection that affected several organs.
Doctors suspected a hematogenous spread of the disease after finding it in the bloodstream.
Certain cancers can metastasize through hematogenous routes to distant parts of the body.
The bacteria caused a haematogenous infection that spread throughout the body.
Doctors suspected a haematogenous route for the cancer's metastasis.
Some diseases are haematogenous and can be detected through blood tests.
