mouch

noun
beggar or scrounger
Pronunciation: / maʊtʃ / (AmE), / maʊtʃ / (BrE)
a person who obtains food, money, or goods by asking for them or taking them without paying; a beggar or scrounger

Examples

A mouch at lunch
The group grew tired of the constant mouch who never contributed to the meal.
At every party, there is always a mouch hoping to get free drinks and snacks.
He was known as a mouch because he always asked for spare change outside the store.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beggar - a person who asks others for money or food
  • antonym: donor - a person who gives something, especially money or goods
  • synonym: scrounger - a person who gets things by asking or taking without paying
