ward

verb
protect from harm or danger
4.8 3.93/m
Pronunciation: / w…îrd / (AmE), / w…îÀêd / (BrE)
to guard or protect someone or something from danger or harm

Examples

To ward off danger
Good health habits can help ward diseases away.
He lit incense to ward evil spirits from the house.
She used an umbrella to ward off the heavy rain.
