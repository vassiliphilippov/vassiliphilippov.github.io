Methedrine

noun
trade name for methamphetamine (stimulant drug)

A trade name for methamphetamine, a drug used as a stimulant to the nervous system and as an appetite suppressant, usually in the form of its crystalline hydrochloride.

Examples

Methedrine drug
After being prescribed Methedrine, the patient experienced increased alertness and decreased appetite.
In the 1960s, Methedrine was sometimes used to treat obesity under close medical supervision.
The abuse of Methedrine led to significant changes in prescription practices and drug control laws.

Synonyms

  • methamphetamine hydrochloride

  • deoxyephedrine
