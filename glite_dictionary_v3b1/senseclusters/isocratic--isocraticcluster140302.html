isocratic

adjective
equal political power
9.6
Pronunciation: /aɪ.sə.ˈkræt.ɪk/ /; / /ɪ.sə.ˈkræt.ɪk/ (AmE), /aɪ.sə.ˈkræt.ɪk/ /; / /ɪ.sə.ˈkræt.ɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or supporting a system in which all people or groups have equal political power or rule.

Examples

Isocratic society

An isocratic approach ensures that no single group dominates the political process.
Some activists called for an isocratic system to replace the current government structure.
