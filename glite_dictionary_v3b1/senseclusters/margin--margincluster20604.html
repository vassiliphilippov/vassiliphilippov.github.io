Synonyms

    2/m3.6
    Measures how much selling price exceeds production or purchase cost
    vsmargin: the difference between the cost to produce or buy something and its selling price, usually shown as a percentage and used to measure how profitable a product or business is
  • gross margin
    0.3/m4.9
    Calculates company sales minus cost of goods sold as a percentage of net revenue
    vsmargin: the difference between a company's sales and the cost of goods sold, expressed as a percentage of net sales
  • gross profit margin
    0.03/m4.1
    Focuses on net sales minus direct cost of goods and services sold
    vsmargin: the difference between net sales and the cost of goods and services sold, often expressed as a percentage of net sales
  • margin of profit
    0.01/m3.9
    Shows how much sales revenue exceeds total costs, expressed as a sales percentage
    vsmargin: The amount by which revenue from sales exceeds costs, expressed as a percentage of net sales.
