primary color

noun
basic color in pigment mixing
1.0 1.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpraɪ.mə.riː ˈkʌ.lər/ /; / /ˈpraɪ.mə.riː ˈkɔː.lər/ (AmE), /ˈpraɪ.mə.riː ˈkʌ.lə/ (BrE)
