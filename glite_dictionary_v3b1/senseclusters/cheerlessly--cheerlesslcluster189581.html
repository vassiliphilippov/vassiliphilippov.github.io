cheerlessly

adverb
in an unhappy or gloomy manner
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪrləsli / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪələsli / (BrE)
In an unhappy, gloomy, or disheartened manner; lacking cheer or optimism.

Examples

To look cheerlessly
He stared cheerlessly out of the rain-soaked window, lost in thought.
Jane cheerlessly agreed to attend the meeting, knowing it would bring no joy.
The workers cheerlessly completed their assignments, dragging through the long afternoon shift.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gloomily - in a way that is dark or depressed in mood
  • antonym: happily - in a happy or cheerful manner
