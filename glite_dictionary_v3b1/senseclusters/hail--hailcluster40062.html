hail

verb
rain as small ice balls
Pronunciation: / heɪl / (AmE), / heɪl / (BrE)
(of rain) to fall as small balls or lumps of ice from the sky.

Examples

It hails hard
If it hails tomorrow, the picnic will be postponed.
It started to hail as we were driving home from the mountains.
Last night, it hailed so heavily that some car windows cracked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rain - water that falls from clouds in drops
  • degree lower: snow - precipitation in the form of flakes of crystalline water ice
