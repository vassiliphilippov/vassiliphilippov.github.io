revolutionary

introducing radical change

Examples

Revolutionary idea

Synonyms

  • transformative
    5.6 3.44/m
    transformative brings big, lasting change to people or systems, while revolutionary often means a sudden, dramatic break with the past.
  • radical
    6.0 37/m
    radical is extreme or basic change to ideas or systems, while revolutionary suggests a wide, often sudden overhaul.
  • groundbreaking
    5.1 3.85/m
    groundbreaking introduces new methods or ideas that lead the field, while revolutionary changes many parts of society or a system.
  • innovative
    5.5 23/m
    innovative uses new, creative ideas or tools in work or products, while revolutionary changes how many people live or think.
