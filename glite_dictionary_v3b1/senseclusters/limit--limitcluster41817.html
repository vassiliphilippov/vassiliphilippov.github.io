limit

noun
boundary of a specific area
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪt / (BrE)
the boundary or edge of a specific area beyond which something does not extend or is not allowed to go

Examples

Limit of the park
Do not go beyond the limit of the park after dark.
The city expanded to the limit of its original boundaries.
The lake marks the southern limit of the nature reserve.

  • synonym: boundary - the edge or border of something
