noxious

adjective
harmful to health or well-being
7.8 2.02/m
Pronunciation: / Ààn…ëÀêk É…ôs / (AmE), / Ààn…ík É…ôs / (BrE)
harmful or injurious to health, either physical or mental; poisonous or very unpleasant

Examples

Noxious substances
Exposure to certain noxious substances can lead to serious health problems.
Factory fumes released a noxious odor that affected the entire neighborhood.
She argued that the noxious influence of violent media should not be underestimated.

Synonyms

  • toxic
    4.0 22/m
    toxic describes things that cause physical or mental harm, often chemical or emotional, while noxious means harmful or unpleasant.
  • poisonous
    2.8 3.97/m
    poisonous contains or produces poison that can cause illness or death if eaten or touched, while noxious means harmful or unpleasant.
