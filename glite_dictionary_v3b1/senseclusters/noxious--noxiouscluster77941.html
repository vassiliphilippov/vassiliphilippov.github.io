noxious

adjective
harmful to health or well-being
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːkʃəs / (AmE), / ˈnɒkʃəs / (BrE)
harmful or injurious to health, either physical or mental; poisonous or very unpleasant

Examples

Noxious substances
Exposure to certain noxious substances can lead to serious health problems.
Factory fumes released a noxious odor that affected the entire neighborhood.
She argued that the noxious influence of violent media should not be underestimated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmful - causing or likely to cause harm
  • antonym: innocuous - not harmful or offensive
