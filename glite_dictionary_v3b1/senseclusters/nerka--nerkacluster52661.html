nerka

noun
Pacific sockeye salmon
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnɜrkə / (AmE), / ˈnɜːkə / (BrE)
a type of Pacific salmon, also known as sockeye salmon, found in North American and Asian rivers

Examples

The nerka salmon
Fishermen eagerly await the arrival of the nerka each summer in Alaska.
The river is famous for its annual nerka migration.
Grizzly bears often feed on nerka during the spawning season.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: chum salmon - a species of Pacific salmon also known as dog salmon
  • synonym: sockeye salmon - a species of Pacific salmon with red flesh
