tally

verb
agree, be consistent or correspond
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtæli / (AmE), / ˈtæli / (BrE)
(of facts, statements, or accounts) to agree or be consistent; to correspond or match.

Examples

Facts tally exactly
All the expenses tally perfectly with the receipts provided.
Her story didn't tally with the facts we discovered.
The numbers in the report do not tally with those in the financial statement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agree - be in harmony or accord
  • antonym: conflict - be in opposition or disagreement
  • synonym: match - be compatible or correspond
