revenue enhancement

noun
government-imposed charge for public funds
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛvəˌnu ɪnˈhæns·mənt / (AmE), / ˈrɛv.juː ɪnˈhɑːns.mənt / (BrE)
a charge, such as a tax or fee, imposed by a government on individuals, property, or activities to raise funds for public expenditure
