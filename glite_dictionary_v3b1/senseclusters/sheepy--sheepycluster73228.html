sheepy

adjective
like a sheep
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːpi / (AmE), / ˈʃiːpi / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a sheep, especially in appearance or behavior

Examples

A sheepy face
She wore a sheepy coat that was soft and warm to the touch.
His sheepy expression made everyone smile during the meeting.
The blanket felt especially sheepy on cold winter nights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: goatlike - resembling or characteristic of a goat
  • synonym: ovine - relating to or resembling sheep
