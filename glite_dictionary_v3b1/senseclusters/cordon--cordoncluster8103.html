cordon

verb
to block off area
5.8 1.11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…îrd…ôn / (AmE), / Ààk…îÀêd…ôn / (BrE)
to block off or enclose an area using a line of people or barriers, usually for safety or security

Examples

To cordon off streets
Police had to cordon off the street after the accident.
The authorities cordoned the area to keep bystanders away from the scene.
Firefighters quickly cordoned off the building to ensure everyone's safety.
