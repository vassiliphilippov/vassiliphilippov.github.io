jubbah

noun
long outer garment
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌbə / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌbə / (BrE)
a long, loose outer garment traditionally worn by men in some Muslim countries

Examples

A white jubbah
He wore a white jubbah to the mosque for Friday prayers.
The tailor carefully stitched the decorative trim onto the new jubbah.
In some regions, a jubbah is worn during special religious ceremonies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: robe - a long, loose outer garment
  • synonym: thobe - a traditional ankle-length garment worn by men in Arab countries
