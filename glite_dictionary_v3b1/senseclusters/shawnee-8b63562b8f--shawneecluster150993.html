Shawnee

proper noun
Native American people
8.8
Pronunciation: / ʃɔːˈniː / (AmE), / ʃɔːˈniː / (BrE)
a member of a Native American people originally from the eastern United States

Examples

The Shawnee tribe
The Shawnee were known for their resistance during westward expansion in the 18th century.
A museum exhibit features artifacts from the Shawnee and their neighboring tribes.
Many descendants of the Shawnee still live in Oklahoma today.
