carry over

verb
keep goods for sale in next season
Pronunciation: / ˈkæri ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈkæri ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to keep goods unsold in one season for sale in the next season

Examples

To carry over stock
Due to weak sales, they had to carry over many items from last summer.
It is common for retailers to carry over surplus inventory into the following season.
The store decided to carry over its unsold winter coats to the next year.
