Brahma

In Hinduism, the Creator god, one of the three major deities in the Hindu pantheon, alongside Vishnu and Shiva.

Examples

Hindu god Brahma
Brahma is often depicted with four faces, each reciting one of the Vedas.
Many devotees worship Brahma as the creator of the universe.
Unlike Vishnu and Shiva, there are very few temples dedicated solely to Brahma.
