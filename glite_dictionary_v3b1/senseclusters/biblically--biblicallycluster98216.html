biblically

adverb
in a biblical manner
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪblɪkli / (AmE), / ˈbɪblɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the Bible or is based on its teachings or language

Examples

Biblically inspired ceremony
The ceremony was conducted biblically, following traditions described in the Old Testament.
He tried to solve the dispute biblically, seeking guidance from scripture.
The painting depicts the scene biblically, with attention to historical and religious details.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scripturally - in a way that relates to religious scriptures
  • antonym: secularly - in a way that is not connected to religion
