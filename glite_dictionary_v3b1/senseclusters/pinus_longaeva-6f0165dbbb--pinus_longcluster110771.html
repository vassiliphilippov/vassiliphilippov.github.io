Pinus longaeva

noun
a very long-lived pine tree species
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpaɪ.nəs lɑːŋ.ˈgeɪ.və/ /; / /ˈpaɪ.nəs lɔːŋ.ˈgeɪ.və/ (AmE), /ˈpaɪ.nəs lɒŋ.ˈgeɪ.və/ (BrE)
