jade green

noun
a light green color (like jade)
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒeɪd ˈɡrin / (AmE), / ˌdʒeɪd ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
a light green color with shades ranging from bluish green to yellowish green, resembling the color of jade.

Examples

Jade green color
Artists often use jade green to evoke a sense of freshness and tranquility in their paintings.
She wore a dress in jade green that perfectly matched her eyes.
The walls were painted a soft jade green, giving the room a calming atmosphere.
