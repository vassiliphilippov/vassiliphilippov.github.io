appealing

adjective
attractive or interesting
Pronunciation: / əˈpiːlɪŋ /; / əˈpilɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈpiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
attractive, interesting, or able to inspire positive feelings such as sympathy or affection

Examples

An appealing smile
Her friendly smile made her instantly appealing to everyone she met.
I find the idea of working from home very appealing these days.
The new design is much more appealing to younger customers.
Her appealing nature made it easy for the audience to empathize with her character's struggles.
The author created an appealing protagonist who quickly won over the readers' hearts.
The film's success was largely due to its appealing cast of characters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attractive - pleasing or appealing to the senses
  • synonym: sympathetic - (of a character) evoking sympathy or compassion
  • antonym: unappealing - not attractive or interesting
