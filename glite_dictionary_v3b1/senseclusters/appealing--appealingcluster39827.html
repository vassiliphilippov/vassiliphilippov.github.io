appealing

adjective
attractive or interesting
1.0 2.0
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈpiː.lɪŋ/ /; / /ʌ.ˈpɪə.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ə.ˈpiː.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
