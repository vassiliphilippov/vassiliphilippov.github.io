Tamiflu

proper noun
antiviral flu medicine
Pronunciation: / ˈtæmɪfluː / (AmE), / ˈtæmɪfluː / (BrE)
a brand name for an antiviral medication used to treat and prevent influenza (the flu)

Examples

Tamiflu brand
The doctor prescribed Tamiflu to help with her flu symptoms.
Many pharmacies ran out of Tamiflu during the severe flu season.
You should start taking Tamiflu within two days of noticing flu symptoms for best results.
Doctors prescribed Tamiflu to patients who showed early symptoms of the flu.
During the flu season, many pharmacies quickly ran out of Tamiflu.
Some people take Tamiflu as a preventive measure after being exposed to influenza.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Relenza - another antiviral drug used to treat influenza
  • synonym: antiviral - a drug used to treat viral infections
  • synonym: oseltamivir - the generic name for the active ingredient in Tamiflu
