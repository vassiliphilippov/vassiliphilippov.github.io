master

noun
a key that opens multiple locks
Pronunciation: / ˈmæs.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.stə / (BrE)
a key that opens several different locks, especially the main locks in a building or set of buildings

Examples

Master key
After losing his key, he had to call someone with the master to let him in.
Only the building manager has access to the master that can open every apartment door.
The locksmith made a master for the building's security system.
