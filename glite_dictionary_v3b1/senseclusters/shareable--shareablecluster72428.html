shareable

adjective
suitable for sharing
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛrəbəl / (AmE), / ˈʃeərəbəl / (BrE)
intended or suitable to be divided and used or enjoyed by several people together

Examples

Shareable dish for friends
The restaurant offers many shareable dishes that are perfect for groups of friends.
She brought a shareable dessert to the party so everyone could have a taste.
This app makes your photos easily shareable with family and friends online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divisible - able to be separated into parts
  • antonym: individual - intended for one person only
