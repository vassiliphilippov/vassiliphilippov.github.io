prechordal

adjective
in front of notochord
Pronunciation: / priːˈkɔrdl̩ / (AmE), / priːˈkɔːdəl / (BrE)
located in front of the notochord, especially during embryonic development

Examples

The prechordal plate
The prechordal plate plays a crucial role in the early development of the brain.
Researchers studied the prechordal region to understand facial formation in embryos.
Abnormalities in the prechordal mesoderm can lead to developmental disorders.
