densify

verb
to make data dense
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛnsɪfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈdɛnsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to convert a sparse or incomplete data structure or dataset into a dense form by filling in missing entries or values

Examples

To densify data
The software can densify a sparse matrix to optimize certain calculations.
Researchers often densify incomplete datasets before running statistical analyses.
To improve performance, the engineer decided to densify the data structure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: populate - to fill in missing values or entries in a dataset
  • antonym: sparsify - to make a data structure more sparse by removing or omitting entries
