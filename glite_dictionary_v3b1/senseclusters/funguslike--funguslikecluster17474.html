funguslike

adjective
resembling fungi
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋɡəsˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋɡəsˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having characteristics or an appearance similar to that of fungi.

Examples

Funguslike organism
Scientists discovered a funguslike organism infecting the crops.
Some protists are described as funguslike because of their appearance and behavior.
The funguslike growth spread rapidly across the damp wall.
