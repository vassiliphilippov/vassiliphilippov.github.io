encore

noun
additional performance given after applause
3.7 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːn.kɔːr/ /; / /ˈɑːŋ.kɔːr/ (AmE), /ˈɒŋ.kɔː/ /; / /ˈɒ.kɔː/ (BrE)
an additional or repeated performance given at the end of a concert or show, typically in response to sustained applause from the audience

Examples

A concert encore
After delivering a stunning speech, she was called back for an encore.
The crowd demanded an encore after the band finished their set.
The pianist played an unexpected encore that delighted the audience.
