toothlessly

adverb
without force
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːθləsli / (AmE), / ˈtuːθləsli / (BrE)
in a way that lacks teeth or force; without power or effectiveness

Examples

Toothlessly bark
The old dog barked toothlessly at the passing cars.
The committee criticized the policy toothlessly, making no real impact.
He protested toothlessly, knowing his words would not change the outcome.

Related Terms

  • antonym: powerfully - in a way that shows strength or force
  • synonym: weakly - in a way that lacks strength or power
