butt-weld

noun
weld joining two edges together
9.9 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌt.weld/ (AmE), /ˈbʌt.weld/ (BrE)
A type of weld in which the ends of two pieces of metal are joined by placing them edge to edge and welding along the joint.

Examples

A butt-weld joint
A butt-weld is commonly used when constructing steel frames and pipelines.
The pipes were joined using a strong butt-weld to ensure a leak-proof seal.
The technician inspected each butt-weld for quality and durability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: weld - join pieces of metal or plastic by heating and melting the edges
