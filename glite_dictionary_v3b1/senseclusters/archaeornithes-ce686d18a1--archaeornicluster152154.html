Archaeornithes

noun
primitive fossil birds of early avian evolution
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.kiː.oʊ.ˈnɪ.θiːz/ /; / /ɑːr.kiː.ə.ˈnɪ.θiːz/ (AmE), /ɑː.kiː.əʊ.ˈnɪ.θiːz/ /; / /ɑː.kiː.ə.ˈnɪ.θiːz/ (BrE)
