Archaeornithes

noun
primitive fossil birds of early avian evolution
Pronunciation: / ˌɑrki.oʊˈnɪθiz /; / ˌɑːrki.oʊˈnɪθiːz / (AmE), / ˌɑːkɪəʊˈnɪθiːz /; / ˌɑːki.əʊˈnɪθiːz / (BrE)
an extinct group of primitive, reptile-like birds from the Jurassic or early Cretaceous periods, considered an early stage in bird evolution
