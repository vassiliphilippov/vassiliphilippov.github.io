transumption

noun
act of being taken up
9.9
Pronunciation: / trænˈsʌmpʃən / (AmE), / trænˈsʌmpʃən / (BrE)
the act of taking up, consuming, or being carried away, especially in a figurative or religious context

Examples

Transumption into heaven
The ancient manuscript described the transumption of the prophet into the afterlife.
In some traditions, transumption is seen as a spiritual elevation of the soul.
The poet used the word transumption to express the soul's journey beyond the earthly realm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assumption - the act of taking up or receiving, especially into heaven
  • synonym: consumption - the act of using up or eating
