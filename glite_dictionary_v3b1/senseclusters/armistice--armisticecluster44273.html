armistice

noun
temporary agreement to stop fighting
2.3 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrmɪstɪs / (AmE), / ˈɑːmɪstɪs / (BrE)
An agreement between opposing sides in a conflict to stop fighting while peace terms are discussed; a truce.

Examples

An armistice agreement
After years of conflict, an armistice was finally signed by both parties.
The armistice ended active hostilities but did not resolve the underlying issues.
The two countries agreed to an armistice in hopes of negotiating a lasting peace.
