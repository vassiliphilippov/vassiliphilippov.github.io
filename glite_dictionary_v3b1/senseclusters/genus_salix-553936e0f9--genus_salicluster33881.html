genus Salix

noun
botanical genus including willows
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈseɪ.lɪks / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈsæ.lɪks / (BrE)
the genus of plants comprising the willows, a large and widespread group of shrubs and trees
