gobo

noun
burdock root vegetable
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊboʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊbəʊ / (BrE)
the edible root of the burdock plant, commonly used as a vegetable in Japanese and other Asian cuisines

Examples

Sliced gobo vegetable
She thinly sliced gobo and added it to the stir-fry for an earthy flavor.
In Japanese cuisine, gobo is often simmered with carrots and soy sauce.
The market sells fresh gobo alongside other Asian vegetables every spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burdock - a plant with edible roots used in cooking and herbal medicine
