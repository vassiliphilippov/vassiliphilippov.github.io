tamanu

noun
seed for oil
9.9
Pronunciation: / təˈmɑːnuː / (AmE), / təˈmɑːnuː / (BrE)
the seed or nut of the tamanu tree, used to produce tamanu oil for cosmetic and medicinal purposes

Examples

Tamanu nut oil
The villagers harvested tamanu to make a traditional healing oil.
Cosmetic companies value tamanu for its use in natural skincare products.
After drying the tamanu, they pressed it to extract the rich oil.

  • synonym: seed - the part of a plant from which a new plant can grow
