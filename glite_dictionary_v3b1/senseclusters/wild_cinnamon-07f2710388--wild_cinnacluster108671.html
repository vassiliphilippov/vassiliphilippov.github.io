wild cinnamon

noun
West Indian tree; source of bay rum
5.6 6.2
Pronunciation: /waɪld ˈsɪn.ə.mən/ /; / /waɪld ˈsɪn.ə.mɪn/ (AmE), /waɪld ˈsɪn.ə.mən/ (BrE)
