relleno

noun
cosmetic tissue filler
Pronunciation: / reˈjeɪnoʊ / (AmE), / reˈjeɪnəʊ / (BrE)
a substance injected or inserted to restore volume, smooth wrinkles, or enhance tissues, especially in cosmetic or medical procedures

Examples

Facial relleno
The doctor recommended a relleno to reduce the appearance of deep facial lines.
After the procedure, the relleno made her cheeks look fuller and more youthful.
Some patients choose a relleno to enhance the shape of their lips.
