horror-struck

adjective
overcome by intense horror or fear
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrər ˌstrʌk / (AmE), / ˈhɒrə ˌstrʌk / (BrE)
Extremely shocked and filled with horror; overcome by intense fear or shock.

Examples

Horror-struck face
He stood horror-struck, unable to move as the events unfolded before him.
She was horror-struck when she discovered the truth about the accident.
The horror-struck witness could barely speak after seeing the scene.
