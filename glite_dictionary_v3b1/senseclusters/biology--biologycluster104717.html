biology

noun
study of living organisms
1.0
Pronunciation: / baɪˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / baɪˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The science that studies living organisms, including their structure, function, growth, evolution, and distribution.

Examples

Biology class
Advances in biology have led to new medical treatments and discoveries.
During the biology class, we learned about cell structure and genetics.
