everbearing

adjective
producing fruit repeatedly
Pronunciation: / ˈɛvərˌbɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌbɛrɪŋ / (BrE)
producing fruit continuously or repeatedly throughout a growing season

Examples

Everbearing strawberries
The everbearing raspberry bushes provide fresh fruit from early summer until the first frost.
Gardeners often prefer everbearing varieties for a longer harvest period.
Unlike traditional types, everbearing strawberries can be picked several times each season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remontant - producing flowers or fruit more than once in a season
  • antonym: seasonal - producing fruit or flowers only during a specific season
