nonpsychotic

noun
person without psychosis
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnsaɪˈkɑtɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnsaɪˈkɒtɪk / (BrE)
a person who does not have psychosis or show psychotic symptoms

Examples

A nonpsychotic patient
The study compared the behavior of nonpsychotic patients with those diagnosed with schizophrenia.
Support groups were available for both nonpsychotic and psychotic individuals at the clinic.
Doctors must distinguish between nonpsychotic and psychotic symptoms when making a diagnosis.
