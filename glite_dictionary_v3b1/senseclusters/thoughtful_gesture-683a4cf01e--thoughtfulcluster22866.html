thoughtful gesture

noun
considerate action for others
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈθɔt.fəl ˈdʒɛs.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt.fəl ˈdʒɛs.tʃə / (BrE)
an action performed with consideration and care for others

Examples

A thoughtful gesture of kindness
A handwritten note is often a thoughtful gesture that brightens someone's day.
Bringing her soup when she was sick was a thoughtful gesture that meant a lot to her.
He surprised his colleague with a thoughtful gesture on her birthday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate
  • antonym: rude gesture - an action showing a lack of respect or consideration
