Aga

noun
cast-iron stove
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːɡə / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡə / (BrE)
a large, heavy cast-iron stove that stays warm and is used for cooking and heating, especially in kitchens

Examples

An Aga stove
The family gathered around the warm Aga on cold winter mornings.
She baked fresh bread every day using her old Aga in the kitchen.
Many traditional British homes feature an Aga as the centerpiece of the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oven - an enclosed compartment for cooking food by baking or roasting
  • synonym: range - a large cooking stove with burners and an oven
