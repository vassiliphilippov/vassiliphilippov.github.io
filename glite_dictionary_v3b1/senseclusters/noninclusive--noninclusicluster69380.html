noninclusive

adjective
not including endpoints
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪnˈkluːsɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪnˈkluːsɪv / (BrE)
not including a stated or implied endpoint, boundary, or limit, especially in intervals, ranges, or counts

Examples

Noninclusive range
In mathematics, a noninclusive range does not contain its endpoints.
The sale runs from Monday to Friday, noninclusive of the weekend.
Please enter a noninclusive count, so the final number is not included.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exclusive - not including something; leaving out
  • antonym: inclusive - including the endpoints or limits
