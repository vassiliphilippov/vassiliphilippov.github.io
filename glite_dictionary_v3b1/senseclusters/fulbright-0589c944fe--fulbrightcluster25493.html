Fulbright

noun
scholarship recipient
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊlbraɪt / (AmE), / ˈfʊlbraɪt / (BrE)
a person who has received a Fulbright scholarship or grant, often to study, teach, or conduct research abroad

Examples

A Fulbright scholar
She became a Fulbright and spent a year teaching English in South Korea.
As a Fulbright, he conducted research on renewable energy in Germany.
The university celebrated its new Fulbright scholars at a special ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fellow - a person awarded a fellowship for study or research
  • synonym: scholar - a person who receives a scholarship for academic study
