veggie garden

noun
garden for growing vegetables
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒi ˌɡɑrdən / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒi ˌɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)
An area of ground where vegetables are grown, especially for personal use.

Examples
