canted

adjective
sloping, tilted, or not level
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkæntəd / (AmE), / ˈkæntɪd / (BrE)
not positioned exactly upright or level; sloping or tilted away from the true vertical or horizontal.

Examples

A canted roof
A canted roof allows rain to run off more easily.
The old fence posts were canted after years of weather exposure.
The painting was slightly canted, making the whole room look off-kilter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: level - perfectly horizontal or vertical; not sloped
  • synonym: tilted - sloping or inclined from the horizontal or vertical position
