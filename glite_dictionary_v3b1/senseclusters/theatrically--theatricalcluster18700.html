theatrically

adverb
in a dramatic or stage-like manner
6.5 5.0
Pronunciation: /θiː.ˈæ.trɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /θɪ.ˈæ.trɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /θiː.ˈæ.trɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /θɪ.ˈæ.trɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in an exaggerated or overly dramatic way, as if performing in a theater

Examples
