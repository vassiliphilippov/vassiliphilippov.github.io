theatrically

adverb
in a dramatic or stage-like manner
6.5
Pronunciation: / θiˈætrɪkli / (AmE), / θɪˈætrɪkli / (BrE)
in an exaggerated or overly dramatic way, as if performing in a theater

Examples

To act theatrically
He sighed theatrically and threw his hands in the air for effect.
She entered the room theatrically, as if stepping onto a stage.
The children theatrically recited their lines during the school play.
He sighed theatrically to show his annoyance at the situation.
She entered the room theatrically, drawing everyone's attention with her grand gestures.
The actor theatrically removed his hat and bowed to the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dramatically - in a way that is exaggerated or theatrical
