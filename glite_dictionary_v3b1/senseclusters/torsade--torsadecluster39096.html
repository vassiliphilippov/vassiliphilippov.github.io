torsade

noun
twisted decorative cord
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɔrˈsɑd / (AmE), / tɔːˈsɑːd / (BrE)
a decorative twisted cord or ribbon used as trimming on clothing or furnishings

Examples

Torsade trim on curtains
The designer added a silver torsade to the hem of the evening gown for extra elegance.
Antique chairs were often decorated with a thick torsade around the cushions.
She admired the intricate torsade that bordered the curtains in the old mansion.
