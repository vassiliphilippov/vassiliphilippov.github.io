squirreltail

noun
bushy grass species
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɝːrəlteɪl / (AmE), / ˈskwɪərəltɛɪl / (BrE)
a type of grass with a bushy, tail-like seed head resembling a squirrel's tail

Examples

Wild squirreltail grass
The hillside was dotted with squirreltail waving gently in the summer wind.
Farmers often notice squirreltail growing along the edges of their fields.
The distinctive seed heads of squirreltail make it easy to identify among other grasses.
