dorsally

adverb
toward/on the back (dorsal) side
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔrsəli / (AmE), / ˈdɔːsəli / (BrE)
in a direction toward or situated on the back or upper side (dorsal side) of an organism or body part.

Examples

Fins positioned dorsally
In many fish, the fins are positioned dorsally.
The muscles attach dorsally along the spine.
The reptile can be identified by the ridges that run dorsally on its body.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ventrally - in a ventral location or direction
