heavy meal

noun
large, filling meal
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvi miːl / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi miːl / (BrE)
a meal that is large and filling, often containing rich or substantial foods.

Examples

A heavy meal after dinner

The restaurant is famous for its heavy meals that keep you full all day.
