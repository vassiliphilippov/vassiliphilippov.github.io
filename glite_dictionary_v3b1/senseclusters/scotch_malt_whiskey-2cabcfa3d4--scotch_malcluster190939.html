Scotch malt whiskey

noun
whiskey made from malted barley in Scotland
5.1
Pronunciation: / skɑtʃ mɔlt ˈwɪski / (AmE), / skɒtʃ mɔːlt ˈwɪski / (BrE)
Whiskey distilled in Scotland, especially one made from malted barley in a pot still.
