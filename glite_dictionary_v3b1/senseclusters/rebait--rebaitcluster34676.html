rebait

verb
to provoke again
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbeɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbeɪt / (BrE)
to provoke, tempt, or try to elicit a reaction from someone again, especially after a previous attempt

Examples

To rebait an argument
After the heated debate, he tried to rebait her with another controversial comment.
Online trolls often rebait users to keep arguments going in the comments section.
She refused to let him rebait her into another pointless discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bait - to provoke or annoy someone to get a reaction
  • antonym: ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to someone
  • synonym: taunt - to provoke or challenge someone with insulting remarks
