slither

verb
move smoothly or sneakily
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪðɚ / (AmE), / ˈslɪðə / (BrE)
to move smoothly, quietly, and often secretly, typically by sliding along a surface

Examples

To slither quietly away
He tried to slither out of the room before anyone could ask him a question.
The snake managed to slither silently across the crowded path without being noticed.
The spy slithered into the shadows, blending in with the darkness.

Related Terms
