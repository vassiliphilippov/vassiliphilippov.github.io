unrooted

adjective
lacking stability
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈruːtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈruːtɪd / (BrE)
not fixed in place or lacking a stable base, support, or connection to a particular place or situation

Examples

Feeling unrooted
After moving so often, she felt unrooted in the new city.
The unrooted plant struggled to survive after being pulled from the soil.
Many people feel unrooted when they start a new life in a foreign country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rootless - having no permanent home or stable base
  • antonym: settled - having a stable or permanent home or situation
