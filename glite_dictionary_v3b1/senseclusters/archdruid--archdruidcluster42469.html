archdruid

noun
leading authority
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrtʃˌdruːɪd / (AmE), / ˈɑːtʃˌdruːɪd / (BrE)
the most prominent leader or authority in a particular field, movement, or group, often used figuratively or playfully.

Examples

Archdruid of science
He was considered the archdruid of modern architecture for his innovative designs.
As the archdruid of the local poetry scene, she inspired many young writers.
Many people saw her as the archdruid of environmental activism in her country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chief - the person with the highest rank or authority
  • antonym: follower - a person who supports and is guided by another
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group or organization
