In an excessively flattering or insincere manner, often to an unctuous or overdone degree.

Examples

To fulsomely praise
Critics often respond fulsomely to popular artists, sometimes to the point of exaggeration.
She praised the new director fulsomely, though her sincerity was questioned by many.
The ambassador thanked his hosts fulsomely for their hospitality during the reception.
