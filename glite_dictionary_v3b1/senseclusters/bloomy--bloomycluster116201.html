bloomy

adjective
rosy and youthful
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːmi / (AmE), / ˈbluːmi / (BrE)
having a healthy, rosy, or youthful appearance, especially of the face or cheeks

Examples

Bloomy cheeks
Her bloomy cheeks made her look full of life and energy.
After a long walk in the fresh air, his face appeared especially bloomy.
The child's bloomy complexion was a sign of good health.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pale - lacking color or brightness in the face
  • synonym: rosy - having a pink or healthy color
