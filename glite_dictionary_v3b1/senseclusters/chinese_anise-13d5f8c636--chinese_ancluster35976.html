Chinese anise

noun
tree with aromatic star-shaped fruit, used as spice
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪˈniz ˈænɪs / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz ˈænɪs / (BrE)
A small tree native to China and Vietnam, known for its star-shaped, anise-scented fruit used as a spice and in traditional medicine.

Examples

Chinese anise tree
Adding Chinese anise to the stew gives it a distinct spicy aroma.
Her recipe calls for one Chinese anise pod to enhance the flavor of the broth.
Traditional medicine often uses Chinese anise for its digestive benefits.
