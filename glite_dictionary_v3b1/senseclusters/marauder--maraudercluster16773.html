marauder

noun
person who raids or plunders
4.3 7.0
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈrɔː.dər/ (AmE), /mə.ˈrɔː.də/ (BrE)
a person who attacks people or places to steal valuable things; a raider or plunderer

Examples

Armed marauders
Armed marauders roamed the countryside in search of riches to steal.
The village was burned by a group of marauders during the night.
They fortified their homes to protect themselves from marauders.
