pleather

noun
artificial leather
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛðər / (AmE), / ˈplɛðə / (BrE)
a synthetic material designed to look and feel like leather

Examples

A pleather jacket
She bought a stylish pleather handbag because it was more affordable than real leather.
The sofa was covered in pleather, making it easy to clean and maintain.
Many people choose pleather jackets as a cruelty-free alternative to animal leather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faux leather - artificial material resembling leather
  • antonym: leather - material made from animal skin
  • synonym: vinyl - a synthetic plastic material often used as a leather substitute
