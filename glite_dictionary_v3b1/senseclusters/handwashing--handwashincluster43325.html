handwashing

noun
washing by hand
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌwɑʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌwɒʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of washing clothes, dishes, or other items by hand instead of using a machine

Examples

Handwashing delicate clothes
Delicate fabrics often require handwashing to prevent damage from harsh machine cycles.
He spent the afternoon handwashing the dishes after the family dinner.
Some people prefer handwashing their clothes to make them last longer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laundering - the process of washing clothes and linens
  • antonym: machine washing - washing items using a washing machine
