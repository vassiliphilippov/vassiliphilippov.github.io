crossing guard

noun
person who helps people cross streets safely
4.7 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɔːs.ɪŋ gɑːrd/ /; / /ˈkrɑːs.ɪŋ gɑːrd/ (AmE), /ˈkrɒs.ɪŋ gɑːd/ /; / /ˈkrɔːs.ɪŋ gɑːd/ (BrE)
