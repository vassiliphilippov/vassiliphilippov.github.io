(music) A group of notes connected by a slur, indicating that they are to be played or sung smoothly together as a phrase.

Examples

Musical ligature
Older musical manuscripts often feature complex ligatures unfamiliar to modern performers.
The ligature implies that these notes are to be sung as a single musical phrase.
The medieval chant was written using a ligature to indicate the smooth connection between the notes.
