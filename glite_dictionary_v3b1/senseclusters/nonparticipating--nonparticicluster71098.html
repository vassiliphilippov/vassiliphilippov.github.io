nonparticipating

adjective
not taking part
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.pɑrˈtɪs.əˌpeɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.pɑːˈtɪs.ɪ.peɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
not taking part in an activity, event, or process

Examples

Nonparticipating members
The nonparticipating members observed the meeting without contributing any comments.
Only a few nonparticipating students remained seated while the rest joined the group activity.
During the survey, nonparticipating respondents were not included in the final results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inactive - not participating or involved in activity
  • antonym: participating - taking part in an activity or event
