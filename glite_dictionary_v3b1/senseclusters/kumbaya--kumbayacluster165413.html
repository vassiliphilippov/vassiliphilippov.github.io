kumbaya

noun
moment of harmony
Pronunciation: / ˌkʌmbɑːˈjɑː / (AmE), / ˌkʌmbɑːˈjɑː / (BrE)
a situation or feeling of peace, harmony, or togetherness, often used ironically to suggest superficial or insincere agreement

Examples

Kumbaya moment
After the heated debate, there was a surprising kumbaya moment when everyone agreed on a solution.
The manager dismissed the proposal as too kumbaya and unrealistic for the current situation.
They hoped the retreat would create a genuine kumbaya atmosphere among the team members.
The meeting ended with a forced sense of kumbaya that nobody really believed.
Critics dismissed the proposal as just more political kumbaya without real solutions.
After hours of arguing, they finally reached a fake kumbaya moment for the cameras.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discord - disagreement or lack of harmony
  • synonym: harmony - agreement or peaceful coexistence
  • synonym: unity - the state of being joined together as a whole
