Examples

Synonyms

  • make
    1.0 1151/m
    make is to create or produce something by putting parts or effort together, while form focuses on shaping or creating appearance.
  • shape
    2.0 35/m
    shape means to give a specific form or figure for a purpose, while form is a wider word for making or creating.
  • mold
    3.2 3.88/m
    mold means to shape something by using a mold or hands, while form is the more general act of making or creating.
  • construct
    3.5 23/m
    construct means to build something by joining parts, while form covers making or shaping both physical and abstract things.
