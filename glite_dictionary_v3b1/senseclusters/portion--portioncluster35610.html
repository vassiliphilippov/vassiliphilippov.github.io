portion

noun
an individual serving of food or drink
1.3 5.51/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…îr É…ôn / (AmE), / Ààp…îÀê É…ôn / (BrE)
an amount of food or drink served to one person at a meal

Examples

A portion of food
Each portion of soup was carefully measured and served piping hot.
He requested a larger portion of mashed potatoes than anyone else at the table.
The restaurant offers a generous portion for a reasonable price.

Synonyms

  • serving
    1.0 17/m
    serving is the amount given to one person at a meal, while portion is the usual share served on a plate.
  • helping
    1.0 0.56/m
    helping is a casual amount you serve yourself or are given at a meal, while portion sounds more neutral and formal.
  • plateful
    2.3 0.21/m
    plateful is the amount that exactly fills a plate, while portion means the usual single person's share at a meal.
