psoriatic

adjective
relating to psoriasis
Pronunciation: / ˌsɔːriˈætɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɒriˈætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by psoriasis, a chronic skin disease that causes red, scaly patches

Examples

Psoriatic skin patches
The doctor prescribed a new cream for her psoriatic skin lesions.
He joined a support group for people with psoriatic conditions.
Early treatment can help manage psoriatic symptoms and improve quality of life.
