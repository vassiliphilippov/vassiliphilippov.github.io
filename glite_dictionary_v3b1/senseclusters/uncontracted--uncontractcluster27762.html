uncontracted

adjective
not shortened form
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈtræktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈtræktɪd /; / ˌʌnkənˈtraktɪd / (BrE)
not shortened or reduced; especially not written or spoken in a contracted form

Examples

Uncontracted verb form
In formal writing, it is better to use the uncontracted forms of verbs.
The teacher asked the students to rewrite the sentences using uncontracted language.

In formal writing, the uncontracted forms of verbs are often preferred.
