rememberer

noun
person who commemorates
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɛmbərər / (AmE), / rɪˈmɛmbərə / (BrE)
a person who remembers, recalls, or keeps memories alive, especially for a group or community

Examples

Family rememberer
As the family's rememberer, he shared stories about their ancestors at every reunion.
The village appointed a rememberer to keep the traditions and legends alive for future generations.
She became the unofficial rememberer after collecting old photographs and letters from relatives.
As the family rememberer, he keeps track of all our important dates and stories.
The village rememberer shared tales from generations past with the children.
Every group needs a rememberer to preserve its history and traditions.
As the group's rememberer, she kept track of all their shared stories.
The village relied on its oldest rememberer to pass down traditions and history.
He became the unofficial rememberer of family birthdays and important dates.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgetter - a person who fails to remember or disregards the past
  • synonym: recollector - a person who remembers or recalls things
