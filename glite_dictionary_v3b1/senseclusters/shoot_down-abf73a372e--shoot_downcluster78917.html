shoot down

verb
bring down aircraft by shooting
4.4 6.59/m
Pronunciation: /  ÉuÀêt da än / (AmE), /  ÉuÀêt da än / (BrE)
to fire at an aircraft or missile and force it to fall to the ground

Examples

To shoot down a plane
The fighter pilot managed to shoot down two enemy planes during the battle.
The news reported that the military had shot down an unidentified aircraft near the border.
They used surface-to-air missiles to shoot down the incoming drone.

Synonyms

  • down
    5.5 3.18/m
    down means causing something to fall or be defeated by many methods, while shoot down means firing at aircraft to force them down.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bring down - to cause something, especially aircraft, to fall by force
  • synonym: take out - to destroy or disable, especially in a military context
