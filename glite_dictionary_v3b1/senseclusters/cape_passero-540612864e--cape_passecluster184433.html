Cape Passero

proper noun
cape at southeast tip of Sicily
Pronunciation: / keɪp pəˈsɛroʊ / (AmE), / keɪp pəˈsɛrəʊ / (BrE)
A cape that forms the southeastern corner of the island of Sicily, Italy.
