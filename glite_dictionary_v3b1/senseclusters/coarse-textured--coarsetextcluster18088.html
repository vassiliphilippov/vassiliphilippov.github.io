coarse-textured

adjective
having a rough or uneven surface
7.0
Pronunciation: / kɔrs ˈtɛkstʃɚd / (AmE), / kɔːs ˈtɛkstʃəd / (BrE)
having a rough or uneven surface; not smooth to the touch

Examples

A coarse-textured fabric
He bought a coarse-textured sweater that was warm but slightly scratchy.
The artist deliberately chose a coarse-textured canvas to enhance the painting's tactile quality.
The pottery was made from a coarse-textured clay that felt gritty in her hands.
