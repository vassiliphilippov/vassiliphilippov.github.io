jeté

noun
ballet leap
9.5
Pronunciation: / ʒəˈteɪ / (AmE), / ʒɛˈteɪ / (BrE)
a ballet leap in which a dancer jumps from one foot to the other, often with a split in the air

Examples

A high jeté
The dancer executed a beautiful jeté that impressed the entire audience.
During the performance, her jeté seemed to float effortlessly across the stage.
He practiced his jeté every day to perfect his technique for the recital.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leap - a jump into the air
  • antonym: pirouette - a ballet turn on one foot
