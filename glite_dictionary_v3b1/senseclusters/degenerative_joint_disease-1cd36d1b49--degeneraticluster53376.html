degenerative joint disease

noun
chronic cartilage breakdown in joints; osteoarthritis
6.8 4.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˈdʒɛnərətɪv dʒɔɪnt dɪˈziz / (AmE), / dɪˈdʒɛnərətɪv dʒɔɪnt dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
a chronic condition characterized by the progressive breakdown of cartilage in the joints, commonly known as osteoarthritis

Examples

Chronic degenerative joint disease
Physical therapy can help manage the symptoms of degenerative joint disease.
