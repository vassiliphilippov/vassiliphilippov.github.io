racerunner

noun
a fast, North American lizard
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪsˌrʌnər / (AmE), / ˈreɪsˌrʌnə / (BrE)
a very swift lizard native to eastern and central parts of the United States, known for its speed

Examples

The racerunner lizard
Biologists in the region have been studying the habitat of the six-lined racerunner.
The racerunner darted across the sand before I could take a photo.
Unlike some lizards, the racerunner relies on speed to escape predators.
