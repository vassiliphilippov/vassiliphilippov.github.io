cordoba

noun
Nicaraguan unit of currency
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːrdəbə / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdəbə / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Nicaragua, equal to 100 centavos

Examples

Nicaraguan cordobas
She exchanged her dollars for Nicaraguan cordobas at the airport.
The price of lunch was about fifty cordobas in the local cafe.
Tourists are advised to check the current exchange rate for the cordoba before traveling.
