limpidity

noun
clearness of expression or thought
9.3
Pronunciation: / lɪmˈpɪdəti / (AmE), / lɪmˈpɪdəti / (BrE)
the quality of being clear and easy to understand; clearness of expression or thought

Examples

Limpidity of explanation

The journalist's limpidity ensured that complex topics became accessible to a general audience.
