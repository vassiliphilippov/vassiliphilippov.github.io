brawniness

noun
muscular strength; physical power
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔːni.nɪs / (AmE), / ˈbrɔː.ni.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being physically strong and muscular

Examples

Strong brawniness
Despite his brawniness, he was gentle and kind-hearted.
His brawniness made him a valuable player on the football team.
The statue is admired for both its grace and its brawniness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strength - the quality or state of being strong
  • antonym: weakness - the state or condition of lacking strength
