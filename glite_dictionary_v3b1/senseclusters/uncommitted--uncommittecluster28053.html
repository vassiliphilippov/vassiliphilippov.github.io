uncommitted

adjective
not pledged or bound
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈmɪtɪd / (BrE)
not bound or pledged to a particular course of action, cause, or person; not having made a firm decision or commitment.

Examples

Uncommitted voters
Many voters remained uncommitted until the final days of the election campaign.
She decided to keep her schedule uncommitted so she could accept new opportunities.
The funds are currently uncommitted and may be allocated as needed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: committed - pledged or bound to a cause, course, or person
