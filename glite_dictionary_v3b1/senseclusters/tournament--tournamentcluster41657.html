tournament

noun
sports competition with multiple games
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːnəmənt / (AmE), / ˈtʊənəmənt / (BrE)
a sports competition in which players or teams compete in a series of games to determine the overall winner

Examples

A football tournament
Our team entered an international tournament and made it to the semi-finals.
She won the tennis tournament after defeating the defending champion.
The chess tournament will be held over three days next month.
