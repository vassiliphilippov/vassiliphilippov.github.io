thrall

noun
state of complete control or domination
8.0
Pronunciation: / θrɔl / (AmE), / θrɑːl /; / θrɔːl / (BrE)
the state of being completely controlled or dominated by someone or something, or a person who is completely controlled or dominated

Examples

In the thrall of power
Many people have fallen into the thrall of social media, unable to disconnect.
She escaped the thrall of her controlling partner after many years.
The novel explores a world in the thrall of a tyrant king.
He was in the thrall of his powerful boss and could not make decisions for himself.
The country remained in the thrall of a dictator for decades.
She fell into the thrall of the mysterious cult leader.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bondage - the state of being a slave or under control of someone else
  • antonym: freedom - the state of being free, not under the control of another
  • antonym: master - a person who has control or power over others
  • synonym: slave - a person who is legally owned by someone else and forced to work for them
