horsewoman

noun
a woman involved with horses
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈhɔːswʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who rides horses, especially one skilled in horsemanship or who works with horses

Examples

A skilled horsewoman
As a young girl, she dreamed of becoming a famous horsewoman and caring for her own stables.
She is an accomplished horsewoman known for her skill in dressage.
The champion horsewoman spent years breeding and training thoroughbreds on her farm.
After years of training, she became a renowned horsewoman in her community.
As a young horsewoman, she loved spending hours at the stables.
The event celebrated the achievements of every accomplished horsewoman present.

Related Terms

  • synonym: equestrian - a person who rides horses, especially professionally
  • antonym: horseman - a man who breeds, cares for, or rides horses
