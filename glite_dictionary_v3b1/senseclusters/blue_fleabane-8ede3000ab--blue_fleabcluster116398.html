blue fleabane

noun
a weed with pale blue flowers
5.4
Pronunciation: / bluː ˈfliːˌbeɪn / (AmE), / bluː ˈfliːbeɪn / (BrE)
a widespread weed with pale purple-blue flowers, belonging to the genus Erigeron, often found in dry, grassy places

Examples

The blue fleabane flower
Gardeners often remove blue fleabane because it spreads quickly and can overtake other plants.
In late summer, the blue fleabane blooms with clusters of pale blue flowers.
The field was dotted with blue fleabane, adding a subtle splash of color to the dry grass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fleabane - any of several plants believed to repel fleas, especially of the genus Erigeron
