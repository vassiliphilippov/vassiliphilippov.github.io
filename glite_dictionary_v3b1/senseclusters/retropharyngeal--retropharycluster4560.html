retropharyngeal

adjective
behind the pharynx
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛtroʊfəˈrɪndʒiəl / (AmE), / ˌriːtrəʊfəˈrɪndʒiəl / (BrE)
located or occurring behind the pharynx (throat area)

Examples

Retropharyngeal abscess
A retropharyngeal abscess can cause difficulty in swallowing and breathing.
The MRI revealed swelling in the retropharyngeal space of the patient's neck.
Doctors are concerned about infections spreading to the retropharyngeal area in young children.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prepharyngeal - located in front of the pharynx
