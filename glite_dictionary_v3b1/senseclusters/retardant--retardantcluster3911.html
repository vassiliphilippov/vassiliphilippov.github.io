retardant

noun
substance that slows or prevents an action/process

Examples

A retardant chemical

Many fabrics contain a flame retardant to improve safety in the home.

Synonyms

  • fire-retardant
    4.4 0.21/m
    fire-retardant material is treated to resist catching fire and heat, while retardant is any substance that slows unwanted processes.
  • flame-retardant
    4.9 0.78/m
    flame-retardant fabric resists spreading flames and keeps small fires down, while retardant is a general substance that slows harmful reactions.
  • retarder
    7.6 0.14/m
    retarder can be a device, person, or additive used to slow motion, while retardant usually means a chemical that slows processes like burning.
