socket

verb
to insert into socket
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːkɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɒkɪt / (BrE)
to put or fix something into a socket, or to join by using a socket.

Examples

To socket a bulb
He socketed the replacement bulb into the lamp and then tested the switch.
The technician carefully socketed the processor onto the motherboard.
She socketed the handle into the tool to make it ready for use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insert - to put something into something else
  • antonym: remove - to take something out of something else
