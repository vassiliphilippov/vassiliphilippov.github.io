see

verb
understand or interpret meaning
Pronunciation: / siː /; / si / (AmE), / siː / (BrE)
to understand, realize, or become aware of something in your mind

Examples

I didn't see any humor in his remarks.
She tried to see the situation from their perspective.
What message do you see in this poem?

Many see this policy as a step forward for the community.
She doesn't see the project as a waste of time, but as a learning opportunity.
I didn't see the mistake until it was too late.
Now I see what you meant in your earlier message.
She sees why the changes are necessary after reading the report.
