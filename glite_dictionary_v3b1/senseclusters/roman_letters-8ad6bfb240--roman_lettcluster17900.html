roman letters

noun
typeface based on ancient Roman inscriptions
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊ.mən ˈlɛt.ərz / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.mən ˈlɛt.əz / (BrE)
the style of letters or typeface based on inscriptions used by the ancient Romans, characterized by straight lines and simple forms

Examples

Classic roman letters
