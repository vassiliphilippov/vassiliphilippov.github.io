nondealer

adjective
for end-users
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈdiːlər / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈdiːlə / (BrE)
intended for customers or end-users rather than for dealers or authorized distributors

Examples

Nondealer warranty

Special nondealer pricing was available only to individual buyers during the promotion.
This offer is valid for nondealer purchases made through our official website.
