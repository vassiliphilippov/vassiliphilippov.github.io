remediation

noun
the act of correcting a fault or error
2.5
Pronunciation: / rɪˌmiːdiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˌmiːdiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the action of correcting or remedying a fault, error, or undesirable situation

Examples

Educational remediation programs
Educational remediation programs help students overcome learning gaps and succeed in school.
Effective remediation requires careful assessment of both the problem and its underlying causes.
The company initiated remediation of the contaminated soil after the spill was discovered.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neglect - the act of not giving enough care or attention to something
  • synonym: rectification - the act of putting something right; correction
