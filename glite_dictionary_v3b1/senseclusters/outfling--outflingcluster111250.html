outfling

noun
protruding part
a part or object that sticks out or projects from a surface; a protruding piece or extension

Examples

A wooden outfling
The old ship's hull had a wooden outfling that served as a makeshift figurehead.
A small outfling on the wall made it difficult to place the bookshelf flush against it.
The architect designed a unique outfling above the entrance to create visual interest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: projection - a part that extends outward from a surface
  • synonym: protuberance - a thing that protrudes from something else
  • antonym: recess - a hollow or indentation in a surface
