Roger that

x
acknowledgment of understanding or agreement
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑdʒər ðæt / (AmE), / ˈrɒdʒə ðæt / (BrE)
used to acknowledge that a message has been received and understood; understood or agreed

Examples

Roger that message
'Do you read me?' ' Roger that.'
I'll bring the documents to the meeting—Roger that.
She smiled and said, 'Roger that,' before leaving the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affirmative - confirming yes; expressing agreement, especially in radio communication
  • synonym: copy that - used to acknowledge receipt of a message, especially in radio communication
  • antonym: negative - no; denial or refusal, especially in radio communication
