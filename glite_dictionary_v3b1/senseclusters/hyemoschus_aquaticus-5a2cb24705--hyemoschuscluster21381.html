Hyemoschus aquaticus

noun
species of large West African chevrotain
9.9
Pronunciation: / haɪˌɛmɒskəs əˈkwætɪkəs / (AmE), / haɪˌɛmɒskəs ɔːˈkwætɪkəs / (BrE)
a species of chevrotain, also known as the water chevrotain, native to marshy areas of west Africa and noted as the largest of the chevrotains.

Examples

Hyemoschus aquaticus species
Hyemoschus aquaticus is rarely seen because it lives in dense, marshy forests.
Researchers observed a group of Hyemoschus aquaticus feeding near a riverbank at dusk.
Unlike other chevrotains, Hyemoschus aquaticus is well adapted to aquatic environments.
