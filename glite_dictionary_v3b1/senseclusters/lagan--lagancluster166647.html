lagan

noun
goods at sea attached to a buoy for recovery
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.ɡən / (AmE), / ˈlæɡ.ən / (BrE)
goods or wreckage cast into the sea, attached to a buoy to enable later recovery

Examples

lagan goods at sea
After the storm, several items of lagan were reported off the coast by fishermen.
Maritime law distinguishes lagan from other types of sea wreckage based on attachment to a buoy.
The sunken treasure was classified as lagan because a buoy marked its location for recovery.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: derelict - property abandoned at sea without hope of recovery
  • synonym: flotsam - wreckage of a ship or its cargo floating on the sea
  • synonym: jetsam - goods thrown overboard to lighten a ship and washed ashore
