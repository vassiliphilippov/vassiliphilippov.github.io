pull off

verb
remove by pulling sharply
1.0
Pronunciation: / pʊl ɑf /; / pʊl ɔf / (AmE), / pʊl ɒf / (BrE)
to remove something by pulling it away from where it is attached

Examples

She tried to pull off the stubborn lid, but it wouldn't budge.
With a quick motion, she pulled off the bandage from her knee.
