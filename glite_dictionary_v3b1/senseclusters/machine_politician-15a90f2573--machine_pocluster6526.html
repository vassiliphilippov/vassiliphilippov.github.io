machine politician

noun
politician directed by algorithms
8.3
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈʃiːn pɑː.lə.ˈtɪʃ.ən/ (AmE), /mə.ˈʃiːn pɒ.lɪ.ˈtɪʃ.ən/ (BrE)
(in politics) a politician whose strategy, messaging, or decisions are produced, controlled, or heavily directed by automated systems, algorithms, or artificial intelligence rather than by direct human judgment.

Examples

AI-driven machine politician
Observers argued the campaign treated the candidate as a machine politician, driven by automated messaging and data.
Critics warned that a machine politician might follow algorithmic advice even when it harms local communities.
After the election, many felt the party had selected a machine politician whose posts were clearly managed by bots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bot - an automated software agent that can post messages or take actions online
  • synonym: puppet - a person who is controlled by others rather than acting independently
