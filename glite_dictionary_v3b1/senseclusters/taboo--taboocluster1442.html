taboo

adjective
forbidden by custom or tradition

Synonyms

  • tabu
    9.9 0.00/m
    tabu is used for things that are forbidden by religious or social custom in some cultures, while taboo is the general English term.
  • forbidden
    3.2 4.49/m
    forbidden describes things not allowed by law, rules, or custom, while taboo focuses on social or cultural bans.
  • verboten
    9.2 0.41/m
    verboten means strongly prohibited by authority or social rule and sounds formal, while taboo refers to culturally forbidden actions.
