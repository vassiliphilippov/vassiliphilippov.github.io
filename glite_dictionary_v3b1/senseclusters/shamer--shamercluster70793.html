shamer

noun
person who shames others
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪmər / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪmə / (BrE)
a person who criticizes or makes others feel ashamed, often by pointing out their faults or shortcomings

Examples

A shamer of faults
No one likes a shamer who constantly points out others' faults.
She refused to let the online shamer affect her confidence.
Instead of being a shamer, try offering constructive feedback.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bully - a person who habitually seeks to harm or intimidate others
  • synonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or finds fault
  • antonym: supporter - a person who encourages or helps others
