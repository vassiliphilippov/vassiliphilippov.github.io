awkwardly

adverb
in a clumsy or uncomfortable manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɔ.kwɚd.li / (AmE), / ˈɔː.kwəd.li / (BrE)
In a manner that is clumsy, uncomfortable, or lacking grace or social ease.

Examples

To move awkwardly
He answered the question awkwardly, clearly nervous in the interview.
She tripped awkwardly over the curb in front of everyone.
The silence stretched awkwardly between them after the argument.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gracefully - in an elegant or smooth manner
