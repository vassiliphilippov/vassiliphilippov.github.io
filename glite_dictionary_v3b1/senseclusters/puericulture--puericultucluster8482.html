puericulture

noun
childcare science
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpjʊrɪˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˌpjʊərɪˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)

Examples

Puericulture science

Modern puericulture emphasizes both nutrition and emotional care for young children.
Hospitals often offer puericulture classes for new parents to learn about infant care.
