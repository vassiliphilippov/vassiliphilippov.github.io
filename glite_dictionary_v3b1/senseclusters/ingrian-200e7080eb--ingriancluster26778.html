Ingrian

noun
member of a western Finnish ethnic group
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋɡriən / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡriən / (BrE)
a member of a western Finnish people formerly inhabiting the Baltic region where Saint Petersburg now stands

Examples

An Ingrian person
An Ingrian community settled near the coast in the 17th century.
Historians have documented the traditions of the Ingrian people in the Baltic area.
Today, very few Ingrian descendants remain in the region around Saint Petersburg.
