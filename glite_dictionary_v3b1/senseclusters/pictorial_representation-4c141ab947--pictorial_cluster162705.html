pictorial representation

noun
visual depiction, e.g., painting or photo
1.0
Pronunciation: / pɪkˈtɔriəl ˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / pɪkˈtɔːriəl ˌrɛprɪzɛnˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a visual depiction of something, such as a scene or object, typically through painting, drawing, or photography

Examples

A pictorial representation of a scene
A pictorial representation can often convey complex emotions more effectively than words.
The museum features a pictorial representation of the city as it looked in the 18th century.
The textbook uses a pictorial representation to explain the structure of the cell.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illustration - a picture or diagram that helps make something clear or attractive
  • synonym: portrayal - the act or art of representing or depicting something visually
