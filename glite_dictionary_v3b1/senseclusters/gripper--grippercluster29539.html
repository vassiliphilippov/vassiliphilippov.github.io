gripper

noun
hand exercise device
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪpər / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪpə / (BrE)
a small handheld device used for strengthening the fingers, hand, and forearm by repeated squeezing

Examples

Hand gripper
After his injury, he used a gripper daily to regain hand strength.
The physical therapist recommended a gripper to improve her grip and finger flexibility.
Many athletes use a gripper as part of their regular training routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dumbbell - a short bar with weights used for exercise
  • synonym: hand exerciser - a device for exercising the hand and fingers
