ductor

noun
flow-directing device
a device or part that guides or directs the flow of air, water, or other substances

Examples

Air ductor system
The ductor channels cool air throughout the building.
Engineers installed a new ductor to improve water circulation in the system.
A malfunction in the ductor caused uneven airflow in the ventilation unit.
