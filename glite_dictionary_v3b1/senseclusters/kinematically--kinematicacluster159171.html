kinematically

adverb
in terms of motion
Pronunciation: / ˌkɪnəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌkɪnɪˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the motion or movement of objects, without considering the forces that cause the motion
