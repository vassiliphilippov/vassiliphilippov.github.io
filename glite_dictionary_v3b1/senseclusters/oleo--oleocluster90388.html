oleo

noun
butter substitute spread
9.9 7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊlioʊ / (AmE), / ˈəʊliəʊ / (BrE)
A spread made mainly from vegetable oils, used as a substitute for butter.

Examples

A oleo spread

You can melt oleo to use in baking if you don't have real butter.
