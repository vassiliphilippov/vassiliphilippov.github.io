brink

noun
point just before something happens
Pronunciation: / brɪŋk / (AmE), / brɪŋk / (BrE)
the point at which something, typically something important or disastrous, is about to happen; the edge or border of a steep place or critical situation

Examples

On the brink of disaster
Scientists warned that the species is on the brink of extinction due to habitat loss.
Standing on the brink of the cliff, she looked out over the valley below.
The company was on the brink of collapse before the new investors stepped in.

Related Terms

  • synonym: verge - the point at which something is likely to begin
