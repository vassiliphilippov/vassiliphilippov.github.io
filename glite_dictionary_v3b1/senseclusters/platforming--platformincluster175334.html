platforming

noun
giving public exposure
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈplætfɔːrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈplætfɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving someone an opportunity to share their views, work, or opinions publicly

Examples

Platforming new voices
The conference is platforming young activists who are making a difference in their communities.
Some critics argue that platforming controversial speakers can legitimize harmful ideas.
The magazine is committed to platforming underrepresented artists from around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: promoting - supporting or encouraging the public recognition of someone or something
  • antonym: silencing - preventing someone from expressing their views publicly
