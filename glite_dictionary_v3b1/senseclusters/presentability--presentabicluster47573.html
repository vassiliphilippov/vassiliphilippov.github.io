presentability

noun
suitability for presentation
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛzəntəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌprɛzəntəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being suitable or appropriate for being shown, especially because something is clear, organized, or professional

Examples

Presentability of a report
The manager praised the presentability of the proposal during the meeting.
Her attention to detail improved the presentability of the final product.
Good presentability is important when submitting documents to clients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appearance - the way that someone or something looks
  • synonym: professionalism - the quality of being professional and competent
  • antonym: unsuitability - the quality of not being appropriate or fit for a purpose
