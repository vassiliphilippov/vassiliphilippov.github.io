propagation

noun
movement of a wave through a medium

the process by which a wave, such as sound or light, travels through a medium from one place to another

Examples

Propagation of sound waves
Atmospheric conditions can affect the propagation of radio waves.
Fiber optic cables allow the efficient propagation of light signals over long distances.
The propagation of sound waves requires a material medium such as air or water.
