intraperitoneally

adverb
by peritoneal cavity
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˌpɛrəˌtoʊniˈæli / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˌpɛrəˌtiːniˈæli / (BrE)
by or within the peritoneal cavity, especially by means of injection into it

Examples

Injected intraperitoneally
The medication was administered intraperitoneally to ensure rapid absorption in the mice.
Researchers injected the solution intraperitoneally as part of the experimental procedure.
Pain relief was achieved more quickly when the drug was given intraperitoneally rather than orally.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intravenously - by means of injection into a vein
  • antonym: subcutaneously - by means of injection under the skin
