atonic

adjective
unstressed (in linguistics)
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈtɑnɪk / (AmE), / əˈtɒnɪk / (BrE)
(of a syllable) not carrying stress; unstressed.

Examples

Atonic syllable
In the word 'banana', the middle syllable is atonic.
The linguist noted that the final syllable of the word was atonic.
Unstressed, or atonic, syllables are common in English polysyllabic words.
