moving van

noun
vehicle for moving furniture and belongings
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːvɪŋ væn / (AmE), / ˈmuːvɪŋ væn / (BrE)
A large vehicle designed for transporting furniture and personal belongings when moving to a new home or office.

Examples

A moving van truck
After everything was packed into the moving van, we said goodbye to our old apartment.
The moving van was parked outside, ready to be loaded with boxes and appliances.
We hired a moving van to transport all our furniture to the new house.
