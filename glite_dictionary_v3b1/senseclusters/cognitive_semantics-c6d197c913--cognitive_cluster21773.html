cognitive semantics

noun
branch of semantics focused on cognitive meaning
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑɡnəˌtɪv səˈmæntɪks / (AmE), / ˈkɒɡnɪtɪv sɪˈmæntɪks / (BrE)
the branch of semantics that studies how meaning is influenced by cognitive processes such as perception, memory, and conceptualization

Examples

Cognitive semantics theory
Cognitive semantics explores how human conceptualization shapes the interpretation of words and sentences.
Her research in cognitive semantics examines how metaphors reflect mental structures.
Many linguists today prefer cognitive semantics over more formal approaches to meaning.
