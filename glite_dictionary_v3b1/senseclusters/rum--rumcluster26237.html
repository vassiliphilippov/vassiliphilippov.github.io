rum

noun
card game forming sets and runs
5.4 6.6
Pronunciation: /rʌm/ (AmE), /rʌm/ (BrE)
A card game in which players try to form sets and runs of cards; the winner is the first to meld all their cards.

Examples

Rum card game
After dinner, they dealt the cards and started a game of rum.
His favorite game at family gatherings is rum.
We spent the afternoon playing rum with a deck of old cards.
