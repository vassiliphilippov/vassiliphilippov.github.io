colliculus

noun

Pronunciation: /kə.ˈlɪk.jʊ.ləs/ /; / /kə.ˈlɪk.juː.ləs/ (AmE), /kə.ˈlɪk.jʊ.ləs/ /; / /kə.ˈlɪk.juː.ləs/ /; / /kɒ.ˈlɪk.jʊ.ləs/ (BrE)
a small rounded elevation or prominence on the surface of an organ or tissue, especially in anatomy.

Examples
