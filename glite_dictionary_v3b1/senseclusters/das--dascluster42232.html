das

noun
small African/Asian mammal, hyrax
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɑs / (AmE), / dæs / (BrE)
A small herbivorous mammal found in Africa and Asia, resembling a rodent but more closely related to elephants; also called a hyrax or rock rabbit.

Examples

The das animal
Although the das is small, its closest relatives are surprisingly elephants.
Researchers observed a group of dasses sunbathing on the rocky outcrops.
The das is known for its loud, distinctive calls heard at dawn and dusk.
