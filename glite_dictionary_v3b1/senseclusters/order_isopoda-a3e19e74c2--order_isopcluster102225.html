order Isopoda

noun
order of crustaceans including woodlice
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdɚ aɪˈsɑpədə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə aɪˈsɒpədə / (BrE)
a large order of crustaceans that includes woodlice and their relatives, characterized by a flattened body and seven pairs of legs

Examples

The order Isopoda crustaceans
Common woodlice in gardens are familiar representatives of the order Isopoda.
Scientists discovered a new species within the order Isopoda living in the deep sea.
The order Isopoda includes both terrestrial and aquatic species with diverse adaptations.
