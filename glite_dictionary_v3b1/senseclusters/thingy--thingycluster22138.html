thingy

noun
childish body part term
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪŋi / (AmE), / ˈθɪŋi / (BrE)
an informal or childish word used to refer to a body part, especially when the exact name is avoided or unknown

Examples

A body thingy
The little boy pointed to his thingy and giggled, not knowing the proper word.
Parents sometimes use the word thingy when talking to young children about their bodies.
She asked her son if his thingy hurt after he fell off the bike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: part - a portion or piece of something, including the body
  • synonym: thingamajig - an informal word for an object whose name is unknown or forgotten
