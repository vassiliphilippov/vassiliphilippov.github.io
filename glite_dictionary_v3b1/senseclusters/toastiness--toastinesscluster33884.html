toastiness

noun
pleasant warmth
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊstinəs / (AmE), / ˈtəʊstinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being pleasantly warm, especially in a way that is comforting like toast

Examples

Winter toastiness
I love the toastiness of my sweater on a cold winter morning.
The toastiness of the cabin made it a perfect place to relax after skiing.
She enjoyed the toastiness of the sun as she sat by the window.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chilliness - the quality of being cold or cool
  • synonym: warmth - the state or quality of being warm
