Deuteromycota

noun
group of fungi without known sexual reproduction
Pronunciation: / duːˌtɛrəˈmaɪkoʊtə /; / ˌduːtərəˌmaɪˈkoʊtə / (AmE), / djuːˌtɛərəˈmaɪkətə /; / ˌdjuːtərəˌmaɪˈkəʊtə / (BrE)
a large group of fungi for which no sexual reproductive stage has been observed or identified
