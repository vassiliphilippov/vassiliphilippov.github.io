horizontal tail

noun
horizontal stabilizer and elevator on an aircraft
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌhɔːrɪˈzɑːntəl teɪl / (AmE), / ˌhɒrɪˈzɒntəl teɪl / (BrE)
the horizontal stabilizing surface at the tail of an aircraft, typically including the stabilizer and elevator, used to control pitch and stability

Examples

The horizontal tail surface
Damage to the horizontal tail can affect an airplane's pitch control.
Modern jets often have advanced designs for the horizontal tail to improve performance.
The horizontal tail helps stabilize the aircraft during flight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stabilizer - a structure on an aircraft that provides stability
  • antonym: vertical tail - the vertical stabilizer and rudder at the tail of an aircraft
