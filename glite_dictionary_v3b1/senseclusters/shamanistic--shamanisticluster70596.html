shamanistic

adjective
relating to shamanism or shamans
Pronunciation: / ʃɑːməˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ʃæməˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of shamanism or shamans.

Examples

Shamanistic traditions
Her art is inspired by shamanistic traditions from Central Asia.
Researchers studied the influence of shamanistic practices on early medicine.
The tribe still follows many shamanistic rituals and ceremonies.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: animistic - relating to the belief that natural objects have spirits
  • synonym: shamanism - the beliefs and practices of shamans
