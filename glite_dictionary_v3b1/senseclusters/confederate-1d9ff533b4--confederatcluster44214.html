Confederate

adjective
of the southern Confederacy in the American Civil War
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɛdərət / (AmE), / kənˈfɛdərət / (BrE)
relating to the southern states that seceded to form the Confederacy during the American Civil War (1861–1865)
