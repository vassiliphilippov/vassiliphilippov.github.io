marketer

noun
person who promotes or sells goods or services
3.5 6.94/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ërk…ôt…ôr / (AmE), / Ààm…ëÀêk…™t…ô / (BrE)
a person whose job is to promote or sell goods or services

Examples

A marketer at work
As a marketer, Sarah developed creative campaigns to boost product awareness.
Social media has become an essential tool for every modern marketer.
The company hired an experienced marketer to increase its online presence.

Synonyms

  • marketeer
    4.6 0.31/m
    marketeer promotes and sells professionally in business settings, while marketer covers broader promotion or selling roles across companies and products.
  • seller
    1.0 18/m
    seller offers goods directly for money in shops or online, while marketer plans and promotes products to attract buyers.
  • vendor
    2.0 21/m
    vendor supplies goods, often at markets or to shops, while marketer focuses on promoting or selling those products to customers.
