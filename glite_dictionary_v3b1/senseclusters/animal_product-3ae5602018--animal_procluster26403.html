animal product

noun
product made from animals
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪməl ˈprɑːdʌkt / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl ˈprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
a product made from any part of an animal, such as meat, milk, leather, or wool

Examples

Animal product leather
Leather is an animal product commonly used in the fashion industry.
Milk and cheese are both animal products derived from cows.
Some people avoid all animal products in their diet for ethical reasons.
