surgery

noun
hospital room for surgical operations
3.7 1.97/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…ùÀêd í…ôri / (AmE), / Ààs…úÀêd í…ôri / (BrE)
a room in a hospital equipped for performing surgical operations

Examples

Hospital surgery
Access to the surgery is restricted to authorized medical staff only.
Fresh towels and sterile equipment are always prepared in the surgery before each procedure.
The patient was wheeled into the surgery for her operation.
