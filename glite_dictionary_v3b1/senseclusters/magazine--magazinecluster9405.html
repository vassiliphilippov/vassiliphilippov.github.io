magazine

noun
periodic paperback publication
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmæɡəˈziːn /; / ˌmæɡəˈzin / (AmE), / ˌmaɡəˈziːn / (BrE)
a periodic publication with articles, pictures, and stories, usually about a particular subject

Examples

A fashion magazine
He subscribes to a science magazine that comes out every month.

The dentist's waiting room was filled with stacks of old magazines.
Many people subscribe to a magazine to stay updated on current events.
She bought her favorite fashion magazine at the corner bookstore.
The article was featured in a popular science magazine last month.
