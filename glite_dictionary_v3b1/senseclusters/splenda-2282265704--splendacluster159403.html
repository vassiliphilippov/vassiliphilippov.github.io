Splenda

proper noun
artificial sweetener brand
Pronunciation: / ˈsplɛndə / (AmE), / ˈsplɛndə / (BrE)
a brand name for a widely used artificial sweetener added to food and drinks as a sugar substitute

Examples

Splenda packets
Many people choose Splenda as a low-calorie alternative to sugar in their tea.
The recipe suggests using Splenda instead of regular sugar for fewer calories.
He bought a box of Splenda to use in his morning oatmeal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aspartame - another artificial sweetener used as a sugar substitute
  • synonym: sucralose - a type of artificial sweetener used in many products
  • antonym: sugar - a natural sweetener commonly used in food and drinks
