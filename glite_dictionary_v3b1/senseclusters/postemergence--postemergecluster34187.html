postemergence

adjective
after plant emergence
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstɪˈmɜrdʒəns / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstɪˈmɜːdʒəns / (BrE)
applied or occurring after a plant or crop has emerged from the soil

Examples

Postemergence herbicide
The farmer used a postemergence herbicide to control weeds after the corn had sprouted.
Proper timing of postemergence treatments can improve crop yields significantly.
Researchers compared the effectiveness of preemergence and postemergence weed control methods.
