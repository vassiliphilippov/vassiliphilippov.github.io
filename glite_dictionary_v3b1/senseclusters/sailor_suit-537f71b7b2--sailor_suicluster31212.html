sailor suit

noun
child's outfit styled after sailor's uniform
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlər suːt / (AmE), / ˈseɪlə suːt / (BrE)
a child's or sometimes an adult's outfit styled after a traditional sailor's uniform, typically with a middy blouse and broad collar

Examples

A sailor suit outfit
As a child, he often wore a sailor suit for family portraits.
Her son demanded a sailor suit after seeing a picture of one in a storybook.
The museum displayed a Victorian-era sailor suit belonging to a young prince.
