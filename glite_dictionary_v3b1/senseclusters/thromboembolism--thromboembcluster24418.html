A condition in which a blood vessel is blocked by an embolus that has broken loose from a blood clot (thrombus) elsewhere in the body.

Examples

Thromboembolism in vessels
Doctors monitor high-risk patients for signs of thromboembolism during prolonged bed rest.
Pulmonary thromboembolism can be life-threatening if not treated promptly.
The patient suffered a thromboembolism after surgery, requiring immediate intervention.
