equestrian sport

noun
competitive horse riding sport
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkwɛstriən spɔrt / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɛstriən spɔːt / (BrE)
A sport or competitive activity involving horsemanship and skill in riding horses.

Examples

Popular equestrian sport
Jumping and dressage are both popular equestrian sports in international competitions.

The Olympics feature several equestrian sports, including eventing and dressage.
