consoler

noun
person who comforts
Pronunciation: / kənˈsoʊlər / (AmE), / kənˈsəʊlə / (BrE)
a person who comforts someone who is sad or upset

Examples

A caring consoler
After the loss, her brother became her main consoler and source of strength.
The teacher acted as a consoler to students struggling with difficult emotions.
He found comfort in the words of a kind consoler at the support group.
After the funeral, her aunt became her main consoler during the difficult days.
He acted as a consoler to his friend who had just lost his job.
Children often look for a consoler when they are feeling sad or scared.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comforter - a person who provides comfort or support
  • antonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or finds fault
  • antonym: disturber - a person who causes distress or trouble
  • synonym: supporter - a person who gives encouragement or help
