Mastigoproctus giganteus

noun
large whip-scorpion species emitting vinegar-like odor
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmæstɪgoʊˈproʊktəs dʒɪˈgænˌtiəs / (AmE), / ˌmæstɪgəʊˈprɒktəs dʒɪˈgæntiəs / (BrE)
a large species of whip-scorpion native to Mexico and the southern United States, known for emitting a vinegary odor when alarmed
