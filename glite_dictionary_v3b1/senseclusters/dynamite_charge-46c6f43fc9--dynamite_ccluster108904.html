dynamite charge

noun
an explosive charge of dynamite
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪnəˌmaɪt tʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnəmaɪt tʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
an amount of dynamite prepared and set as an explosive for demolition or blasting purposes

Examples

A dynamite charge explosion
A single dynamite charge was enough to split the rock in half.
Safety protocols require strict procedures before setting a dynamite charge.
The workers placed a dynamite charge at the base of the old bridge for controlled demolition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: explosive charge - a charge of any explosive prepared for detonation
