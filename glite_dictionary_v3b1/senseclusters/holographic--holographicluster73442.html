holographic

adjective
written in the author's own hand
6.2 6.7
Pronunciation: /hɔː.ləʊ.ˈgræf.ɪk/ /; / /hoʊ.lə.ˈgræf.ɪk/ (AmE), /hɒ.lə.ˈgræf.ɪk/ /; / /hɔː.ləʊ.ˈgræf.ɪk/ (BrE)
(of a document) written entirely by hand by its author

Examples
