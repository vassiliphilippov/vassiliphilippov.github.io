dispersion

noun
finance: volatility difference/strategy
9.3 0.17/m
Pronunciation: / d6ac8sp5dd09259n / (AmE), / d6ac8sp5cd08359n / (BrE)
In finance, the difference between the implied volatility of an index and the implied volatilities of its component stocks; also a trading strategy that seeks to profit from that difference.

Examples

Dispersion trading strategy
Major funds implemented dispersion trades to exploit volatility gaps between the index and its constituents.
Traders monitor dispersion between stock volatilities and the index before entering a spread trade.
Her team reduced risk by selling options when implied dispersion signaled unusually high component volatility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dispersion trade - a strategy aiming to profit from gaps between index and component volatilities
  • synonym: volatility arbitrage - trading strategies that exploit relative mispricings of volatility
