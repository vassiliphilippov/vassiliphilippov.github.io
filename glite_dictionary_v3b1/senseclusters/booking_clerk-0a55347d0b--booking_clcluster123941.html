booking clerk

noun
person who sells tickets
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkɪŋ klɜrk / (AmE), / ˈbʊkɪŋ klɑːk / (BrE)
a person whose job is to sell tickets for theater seats, travel accommodations, or other events

Examples

The booking clerk sells tickets
If you have any questions about reservations, ask the booking clerk at the front desk.
She worked as a booking clerk at the theater for several years.
The booking clerk handed me my train ticket and wished me a pleasant journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ticket agent - a person who sells tickets for transportation or events
