coralbells

noun
perennial plant with red feathery flowers
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəlˌbɛlz / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəlˌbɛlz / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to the western United States, known for its bright red feathery flower spikes, often grown as an ornamental.
