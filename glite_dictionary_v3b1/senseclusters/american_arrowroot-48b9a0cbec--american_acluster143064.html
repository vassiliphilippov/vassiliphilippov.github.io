American arrowroot

noun
West Indian plant yielding arrowroot starch
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈæroʊˌruːt / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈarəʊˌruːt / (BrE)
a white-flowered West Indian plant whose root yields arrowroot starch, commonly used as a food thickener

Examples

American arrowroot plant
Farmers in the Caribbean cultivate American arrowroot for both local use and export.
The starch extracted from American arrowroot is prized for its purity and fine texture.
You can use American arrowroot as a gluten-free thickener in soups and sauces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrowroot - starch obtained from certain plant roots, or the plant itself
