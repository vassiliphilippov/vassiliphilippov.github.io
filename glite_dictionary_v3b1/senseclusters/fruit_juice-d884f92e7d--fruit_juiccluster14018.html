fruit juice

noun
liquid extracted from fruit for drinking
Pronunciation: / fruːt dʒuːs / (AmE), / fruːt dʒuːs / (BrE)
the liquid that is squeezed or pressed from fresh fruit, used as a drink

Examples

A glass of fruit juice
She poured herself a glass of cold fruit juice after her run.
The fridge is stocked with different types of fruit juice, including orange and apple.
Would you like some fruit juice with your breakfast?
