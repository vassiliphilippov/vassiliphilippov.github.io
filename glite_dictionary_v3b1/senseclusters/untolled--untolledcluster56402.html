untolled

adjective
without a toll
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtoʊld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtəʊld / (BrE)
not subject to a toll; free from a fee charged for passage, use, or access, especially of roads or bridges

Examples

Untolled road
They chose the untolled highway to save money on their trip.
The city recently opened an untolled bridge to improve access for commuters.
Many drivers prefer untolled routes even if they take a little longer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toll-free - not requiring payment of a toll
  • antonym: tolled - requiring payment of a toll
