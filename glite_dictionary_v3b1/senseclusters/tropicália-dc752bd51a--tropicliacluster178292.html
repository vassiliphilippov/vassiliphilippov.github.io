Tropicália

noun
Brazilian art movement
Pronunciation: / tɾoʊpiˈkɑːliə / (AmE), / tɾɒpɪˈkɑːliə / (BrE)
a Brazilian artistic movement of the late 1960s that blended traditional Brazilian culture with modern and international influences, especially in music and visual arts

Examples

Tropicália movement
The album is considered a classic of the Tropicália movement in Brazilian music.
Many artists in the 1960s embraced Tropicália to challenge cultural and political norms.
Her paintings reflect the colorful and experimental spirit of Tropicália.

Related Terms

  • synonym: avant-garde - new and experimental ideas in art and music
  • synonym: bossa nova - a style of Brazilian music combining samba and jazz
