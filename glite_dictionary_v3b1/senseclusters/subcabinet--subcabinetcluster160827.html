subcabinet

noun
lower-level government group
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˈkæbɪnət / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈkæbɪnət / (BrE)
a group or committee in government that ranks below the main cabinet and handles specific issues or policies

Examples

The subcabinet group
The new environmental policy was first discussed by the subcabinet before being presented to the main cabinet.
She was appointed to lead a subcabinet focused on healthcare reform.
Decisions made by the subcabinet often influence the final recommendations to the government.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: cabinet - the main group of government ministers or advisors
  • synonym: committee - a group of people appointed for a specific function
