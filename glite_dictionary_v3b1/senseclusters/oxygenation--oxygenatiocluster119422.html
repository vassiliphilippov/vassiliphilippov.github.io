oxygenation

noun
process of adding oxygen
Pronunciation: / ˌɑksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of supplying, combining with, or treating something with oxygen, especially in a biological or medical context.

Examples

Blood oxygenation
Doctors monitored the patient's blood oxygenation during surgery to ensure safety.
During exercise, increased heart rate leads to greater oxygenation of the muscles.
The process of oxygenation is crucial for fish survival in aquariums.
