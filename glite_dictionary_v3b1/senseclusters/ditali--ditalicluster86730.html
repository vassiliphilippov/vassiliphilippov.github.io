ditali

noun
small tube pasta
Pronunciation: / diˈtɑli / (AmE), / diˈtɑːli / (BrE)
a small, short tube-shaped pasta commonly used in soups and salads

Examples

Ditali pasta soup
The chef added ditali to the vegetable soup for extra texture.
For a quick lunch, she tossed cooked ditali with olive oil and fresh herbs.
Many Italian salads feature ditali because of its small, easy-to-eat shape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ditalini - a similar but slightly smaller tube-shaped pasta
  • synonym: macaroni - a type of short, curved tube-shaped pasta
