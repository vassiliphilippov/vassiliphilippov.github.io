draft

noun
written order to pay money
Pronunciation: / dræft / (AmE), / drɑːft / (BrE)
a written order for the payment of money, drawn by one person or bank on another

Examples

Bank draft payment
He deposited the draft at his local bank for processing.
The company sent a draft to cover the outstanding balance.
Upon receiving the goods, they issued a draft payable in thirty days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bill of exchange - a written order to pay a set sum to a named person or entity
  • synonym: check - a written order directing a bank to pay money
