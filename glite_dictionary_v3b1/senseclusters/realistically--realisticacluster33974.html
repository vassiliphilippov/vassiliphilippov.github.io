realistically

adverb
in a lifelike or true-to-life manner
Pronunciation: / ˌriəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌrɪəˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that closely resembles real life or the original object; in a lifelike or true-to-life manner

Examples

Realistically painted animals
In the film, the dinosaurs were realistically animated to make them look alive.
She described the economic situation realistically, without exaggeration or denial.
The portrait was realistically painted, capturing every detail perfectly.
