scrub plane

noun
woodworking tool for rough shaping
7.2 6.7
Pronunciation: /skrʌb pleɪn/ (AmE), /skrʌb pleɪn/ (BrE)
a narrow, heavy woodworking plane used to quickly remove excess material from rough lumber

Examples

A scrub plane tool
A scrub plane is essential for working with uneven, rough-sawn lumber.
After setting the scrub plane, she quickly reduced the thickness of the wood.
The carpenter used a scrub plane to remove the rough outer layer of the board.
