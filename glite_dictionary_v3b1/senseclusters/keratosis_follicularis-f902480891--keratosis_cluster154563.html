keratosis follicularis

noun
rare inherited skin disorder with crusted patches
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɛrəˈtoʊsɪs fɑːˌlɪkjʊˈlɛrɪs / (AmE), / ˌkɛrəˈtəʊsɪs fɒlɪkjʊˈlɛərɪs / (BrE)
a rare hereditary skin disorder characterized by dark, crusted patches that may sometimes contain pus

Examples

Keratosis follicularis skin disorder
Because keratosis follicularis usually appears in childhood, early intervention is important for preventing complications.

Treatment options for keratosis follicularis are limited, but retinoids may help manage the symptoms.
