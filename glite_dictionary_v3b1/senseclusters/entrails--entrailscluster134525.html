entrails

noun
internal organs, especially intestines
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnˌtreɪlz / (AmE), / ˈɛn.trɛɪlz / (BrE)
the internal organs of a person or animal, especially those in the abdominal cavity; intestines or guts

Examples

Animal entrails
Archaeologists discovered the entrails of ancient animals preserved in the caves.
In some cultures, the entrails of animals are used in traditional dishes.
The hunters cleaned the deer and removed its entrails before taking it back to camp.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guts - the intestines or stomach, especially of animals
  • synonym: viscera - internal organs, especially of the abdomen
