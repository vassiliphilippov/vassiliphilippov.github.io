orthopedist

noun
specialist in bone and joint correction
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈθopədɪst /; / ˌɔːrθəˈpiːdɪst / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɒpɪdɪst /; / ˌɔː.θəˈpiː.dɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in correcting deformities or problems of the bones, joints, or muscles, especially in children

Examples

Orthopedist doctor
After breaking her leg, she visited an orthopedist for specialized care.
Many athletes consult an orthopedist to treat joint injuries.
The orthopedist recommended physical therapy to help the child recover from scoliosis.
After the accident, she was referred to an orthopaedist for treatment of her broken leg.
As a leading orthopaedist, he has published numerous papers on pediatric bone disorders.
The orthopaedist recommended surgery to correct the spinal curvature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orthopedic surgeon - a doctor qualified to perform surgery on bones and joints
  • synonym: orthopedist - specialist in skeletal disorders (American spelling)
