steamed

adjective
angered; annoyed
Pronunciation: / stiːmd / (AmE), / stiːmd / (BrE)
feeling angry or annoyed; aroused to impatience or anger

Examples

He got steamed
Don't get steamed over a little misunderstanding; just talk it through.
He was so steamed that he could barely speak when he found out the news.
She got really steamed after waiting in line for over an hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angry - feeling strong displeasure or annoyance
  • antonym: calm - not showing or feeling anger or annoyance
  • synonym: irritated - showing slight anger or impatience
