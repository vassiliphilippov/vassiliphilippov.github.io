interstitial cell-stimulating hormone

noun
pituitary hormone affecting ovulation and androgen release
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚˈstɪʃəl sɛl stɪˈmjuleɪtɪŋ ˈhɔrmoʊn / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈstɪʃəl sɛl stɪˈmjuːleɪtɪŋ ˈhɔːməʊn / (BrE)
a hormone secreted by the anterior pituitary gland that stimulates ovulation in females and the release of androgens in males
