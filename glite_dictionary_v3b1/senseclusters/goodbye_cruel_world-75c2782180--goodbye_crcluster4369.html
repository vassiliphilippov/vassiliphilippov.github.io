Pronunciation: / ɡʊdˈbaɪ kruːəl wɝːld / (AmE), / ɡʊdˈbaɪ kruːəl wɜːld / (BrE)
A dramatic phrase used to express despair or resignation from difficult or disappointing circumstances, sometimes humorously or exaggerating for effect.

Examples

Goodbye, cruel world phrase
After losing the competition, she texted her friends, 'Goodbye, cruel world,' though she was only joking.
He shouted 'Goodbye, cruel world!' before dramatically pretending to leave the party.
Whenever something went wrong, he would sigh and say, 'Goodbye, cruel world,' to make his friends laugh.
