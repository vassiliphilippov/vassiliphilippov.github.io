bowels

noun
the innermost part; the depths (esp. of the Earth)
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊəlz / (AmE), / ˈbaʊəlz / (BrE)
the innermost or deepest part of something, especially used figuratively to refer to the center or depths of the Earth

Examples

The bowels of the earth
Ancient legends speak of treasures hidden in the bowels of the mountain.
Rescuers searched the bowels of the earth for survivors after the mine collapse.
The workers drilled deep into the bowels of the Earth to reach the oil.
