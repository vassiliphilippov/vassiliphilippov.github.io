beaker

noun
laboratory glass container
3.7 3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːkər / (AmE), / ˈbiːkə / (BrE)
A wide cylindrical container with a flat bottom, usually made of glass or plastic, used in laboratories for mixing, stirring, and heating liquids.

Examples

Glass beaker in lab
After heating the solution in the beaker, she observed a color change.
Make sure the beaker is clean before starting your experiment.
The scientist poured the substance carefully into the beaker to avoid spilling it.
