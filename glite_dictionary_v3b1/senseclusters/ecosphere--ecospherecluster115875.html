ecosphere

noun
sealed mini ecosystem
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈiːkoʊˌsfɪr / (AmE), / ˈiːkəˌsfɪə / (BrE)
a small, sealed, self-sustaining environment containing living organisms, often used for decoration or education
