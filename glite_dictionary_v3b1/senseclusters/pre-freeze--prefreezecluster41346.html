pre-freeze

verb
to freeze in advance
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈfriːz / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈfriːz / (BrE)
to freeze something in advance or before a main process or use

Examples

To pre-freeze food
You should pre-freeze the dough to make it easier to slice later.
Some recipes recommend you pre-freeze the fruit before blending it into smoothies.
To save time, they pre-freeze the meat before packaging it for sale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freeze - to make something become solid because of cold
  • antonym: thaw - to become unfrozen or to make something unfrozen
