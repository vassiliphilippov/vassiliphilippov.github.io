portraiture

noun
verbal description of a person
7.5 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…îrtr…ôt É…ôr / (AmE), / Ààp…îÀêtr…ôt É…ô / (BrE)
A description in words of a person's appearance and character; a verbal or written portrait.

Examples

Biographical portraiture
His biography excels in its portraiture of the artist as a young man.
Literary portraiture often captures both appearance and inner life.
The novelist's skillful portraiture revealed the complexities of her main character.

Synonyms

  • portrait
    4.0 10/m
    portrait is a single detailed written or spoken description of a person, while portraiture is the practice of making such verbal portraits.
  • portrayal
    4.4 6.22/m
    portrayal is a depiction of someone in art, writing, or acting, while portraiture focuses on a written or spoken description of their appearance and character.

Related Terms

  • synonym: portrait - a painting, drawing, or photograph of a person
