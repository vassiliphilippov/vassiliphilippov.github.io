natural resources

noun
materials or substances from nature for use
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃrəl rɪˈsɔrsɪz / (AmE), / ˈnatʃrəl rɪˈsɔːsɪz / (BrE)
materials or substances occurring in nature which can be used for economic gain, energy, or sustenance, such as minerals, forests, water, and fertile land

Examples

Natural resources management
Conservation efforts aim to manage natural resources more sustainably for future generations.
The country's economy depends heavily on its abundant natural resources.
Water, coal, and timber are all considered important natural resources.
