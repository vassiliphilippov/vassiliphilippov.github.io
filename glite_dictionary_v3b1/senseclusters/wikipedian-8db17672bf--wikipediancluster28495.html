Wikipedian

adjective
of Wikipedia or its community
Pronunciation: / ˌwɪkɪˈpiːdiən / (AmE), / ˌwɪkɪˈpiːdiən / (BrE)
relating to Wikipedia, its community, or its style, policies, or practices

Examples

Wikipedian style article
The article was written in a Wikipedian style, emphasizing neutrality and reliable sources.
She admired the Wikipedian approach to collaborative editing and fact-checking.
Following Wikipedian guidelines, he avoided adding personal opinions to the entry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encyclopedic - relating to or characteristic of an encyclopedia
