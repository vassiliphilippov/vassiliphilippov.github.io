safety lock

noun
locking device on a weapon for safety
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti lɑk / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti lɒk / (BrE)
a device fitted to a weapon to prevent it from being fired accidentally or unintentionally

Examples

Safety lock on a gun
Always engage the safety lock when you are not using your firearm.
He inspected the safety lock to ensure the gun was secure before handling it.
The revolver's safety lock prevented the weapon from discharging by accident.
