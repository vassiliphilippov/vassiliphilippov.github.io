jellyfish

noun
a gelatinous, tentacled marine animal
5.9 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒe.lɪ.fɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈdʒe.lɪ.fɪʃ/ (BrE)
a soft-bodied sea animal with a jelly-like, often bell-shaped body and long, stinging tentacles, found in oceans

Examples
