Mao

proper noun
Chinese communist leader (1893–1976)
5.3
Pronunciation: / maʊ / (AmE), / maʊ / (BrE)
(1893–1976) The Chinese communist leader who was founding father of the People's Republic of China and ruled the country from 1949 until his death.

Examples

Mao Zedong
Mao played a central role in the founding of the People's Republic of China in 1949.
Many historians debate the legacy of Mao and his impact on modern China.
The Cultural Revolution was one of the major political campaigns led by Mao.
