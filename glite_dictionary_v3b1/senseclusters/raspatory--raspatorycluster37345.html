raspatory

noun
bone-scraping tool
Pronunciation: / ˈræspəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈraspətəri / (BrE)
a surgical instrument used for scraping bone during medical procedures

Examples

Surgical raspatory
The surgeon used a raspatory to carefully scrape bone during the procedure.
A raspatory is essential for certain orthopedic surgeries involving bone tissue.
After sterilizing the raspatory, the doctor prepared to remove the damaged bone.
