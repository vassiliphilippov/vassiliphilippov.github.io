Tanacetum ptarmiciflorum

noun
plant of the Canary Islands with white flowers and hairy stems
Pronunciation: / təˈnæs ɪtəm tɑːrˌmɪs əˈflɔːr əm / (AmE), / təˈnæs ɪtəm tɑːˌmɪs ɪˈflɔːr əm / (BrE)
A shrubby perennial plant native to the Canary Islands, characterized by white flowers, hairy stems, and dustlike down; sometimes classified in the genus Chrysanthemum.
