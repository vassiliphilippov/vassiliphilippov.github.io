subterminal

adjective
near but not at the end
6.7
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈtɝːmɪnəl / (AmE), / sʌbˈtɜːmɪnəl / (BrE)
situated near but not exactly at the end or tip of something, especially of a body part or structure.

Examples

A subterminal bud
A subterminal bud forms just below the tip of the branch.
The butterfly's wings display a subterminal band of bright orange.
The eggs have a subterminal opening that allows the larva to emerge easily.
