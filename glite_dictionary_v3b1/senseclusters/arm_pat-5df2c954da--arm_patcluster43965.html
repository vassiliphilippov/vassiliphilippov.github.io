arm pat

noun
gentle pat on the arm for comfort
Pronunciation: / ɑrm pæt / (AmE), / ɑːm pæt / (BrE)
a gentle touch or pat on the arm given to offer comfort or reassurance

Examples

A arm pat of comfort
He offered a comforting arm pat before leaving the room.
She gave her friend a gentle arm pat to show support.
Sometimes a simple arm pat can mean more than words.
