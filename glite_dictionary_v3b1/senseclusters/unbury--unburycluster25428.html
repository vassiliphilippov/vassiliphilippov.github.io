unbury

verb
to reveal hidden things
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɛri / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɛri / (BrE)
to reveal or make known something that was hidden, secret, or forgotten, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

To unbury the truth
The journalist worked hard to unbury the truth behind the mysterious disappearance.
It took years to unbury the family secrets that had been kept for generations.
Historians often unbury forgotten stories from old letters and documents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expose - to make something visible or known that was hidden
  • antonym: hide - to keep something secret or out of sight
  • synonym: uncover - to reveal something that was hidden or secret
