Aotus

noun
a genus of nocturnal South American monkeys
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.əˌtʌs /; / ˈeɪ.oʊ.tʌs / (AmE), / ˈaʊ.təs /; / ˈeɪ.əʊ.təs / (BrE)
a genus of small nocturnal monkeys, known as night monkeys or douroucoulis, native to Central and South America
