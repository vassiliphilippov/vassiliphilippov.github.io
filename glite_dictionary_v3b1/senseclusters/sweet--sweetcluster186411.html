sweet

adjective
pleasing or gratifying (figurative sense)
1.0
Pronunciation: / swit / (AmE), / swiːt / (BrE)
pleasing, delightful, or charming to the senses, mind, or feelings

Examples

Sweet victory moment
He took sweet revenge on his former rival in the finals.
The team enjoyed a sweet victory after years of hard work.
There is nothing quite like the sweet taste of success.
A sweet melody drifted through the open window, filling the room with calm.
Everyone admired the sweet smile she gave to the children.
The movie had a sweet ending that left the audience feeling happy.

  • synonym: charming - delightful or pleasing in a gentle way
  • synonym: pleasant - giving a sense of happy satisfaction or enjoyment
