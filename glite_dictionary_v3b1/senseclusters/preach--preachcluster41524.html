Synonyms

    3.7 16/m
    advocate publicly supports a cause or policy, while preach often speaks with moral fervor and a lecturing, sometimes religious, tone.
  • exhort
    7.7 0.8/m
    exhort strongly urges people to act, while preach often gives moral arguments and can be more about belief than direct commands.
  • sermonize
    8.0 0.08/m
