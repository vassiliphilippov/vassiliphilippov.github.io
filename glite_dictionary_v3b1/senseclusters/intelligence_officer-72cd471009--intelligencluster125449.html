intelligence officer

noun
government agent involved in espionage
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns ˈɑːfɪsər / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a person employed by a government to gather, analyze, and report secret information, especially relating to national security or espionage

Examples

Intelligence officer abroad
As an intelligence officer, she often traveled abroad under a false identity.
The intelligence officer was tasked with uncovering a foreign plot against the country.
The testimony of the former intelligence officer revealed new details about covert operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spy - a person who secretly collects and reports information on the activities of others
