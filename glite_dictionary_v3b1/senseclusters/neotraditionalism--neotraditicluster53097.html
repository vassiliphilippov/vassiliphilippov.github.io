neotraditionalism

noun
return to tradition
9.5
Pronunciation: /niː.oʊ.trə.ˈdɪ.ʃə.nəl/ /; / /niː.oʊ.trə.ˈdɪ.ʃən.əl/ (AmE), /niː.əʊ.trə.ˈdɪ.ʃə.nəl/ /; / /niː.əʊ.trə.ˈdɪ.ʃən.əl/ (BrE)
a movement or belief in reviving or intentionally using traditional values, styles, or techniques, especially in culture, art, design, or music
