shirt off someone's back

noun
extreme generosity or selflessness
8.4
Pronunciation: / ʃɝt ɑf ˈsʌmwʌnz bæk / (AmE), / ʃɜːt ɒf ˈsʌmwəns bæk / (BrE)
used to describe someone who would give or does give anything they have, even their most valued possessions, to help others

Examples

Give the shirt off someone's back
I know Tom would give you the shirt off his back if you needed it.
People say he gave charities the shirt off his back during hard times.
She's so generous, she would gladly lend the shirt off her back to a friend in trouble.

Related Terms

  • synonym: altruistic - showing a selfless concern for the well-being of others
  • synonym: generous - willing to give money, help, kindness, etc., especially more than is usual or expected
  • synonym: selfless - putting other people's needs before your own
