squib

noun
small firework that fizzes
6.9
Pronunciation: / skwɪb / (AmE), / skwɪb / (BrE)
A small firework consisting of a tube filled with powder that burns with a fizzing noise.

Examples

A squib firework
After lighting the squib, a trail of sparks shot out into the night.
She was disappointed when the squib failed to explode as expected.
The children watched as the squib fizzed and popped on the pavement.
