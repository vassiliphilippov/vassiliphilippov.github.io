Iraqi Intelligence Service

proper noun
Iraq's principal intelligence agency
5.8
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrɑki ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns ˈsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / ɪˈrɑːki ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
the principal intelligence agency of Iraq, known for its involvement in national security and covert operations, especially during the regime of Saddam Hussein

Examples

The Iraqi Intelligence Service agency
