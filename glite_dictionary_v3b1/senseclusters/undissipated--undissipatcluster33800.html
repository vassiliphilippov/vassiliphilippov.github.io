undissipated

adjective
sober or restrained
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈdɪsɪˌpeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈdɪsɪpeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not morally dissipated; sober, restrained, or not given to excessive indulgence or excess.

Examples

Undissipated character
Despite the temptations of the city, his character remained undissipated and disciplined.
Her undissipated lifestyle set her apart from her more reckless friends.
He was admired for his undissipated habits and strong moral values.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dissipated - overindulging in sensual pleasures; lacking restraint
  • synonym: temperate - showing moderation or self-restraint
