chutney

noun
spicy condiment from fruits or vegetables
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌtni / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌtni / (BrE)
A spicy condiment made from chopped fruits or vegetables cooked with vinegar, sugar, and spices, often served with Indian dishes.

Examples

A jar of chutney
I like to spread a little chutney on my sandwich for a spicy kick.
She served the curry with a side of mango chutney to add extra flavor.
The homemade chutney was sweet, tangy, and full of spices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pickle - vegetables or fruits preserved in vinegar or brine; sometimes similar to chutney
  • synonym: relish - a condiment eaten with food to add flavor; similar to chutney
