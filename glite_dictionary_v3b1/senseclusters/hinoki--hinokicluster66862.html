hinoki

noun
Japanese cypress tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / hiˈnoʊki / (AmE), / hɪˈnəʊki / (BrE)
a Japanese cypress tree valued for its fragrant, durable wood, often used in construction and crafts

Examples
