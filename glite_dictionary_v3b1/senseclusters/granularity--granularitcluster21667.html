granularity

noun
quality of having large particles
Pronunciation: / ˌɡrænjəˈlærəti / (AmE), / ˌɡræn.jʊˈlær.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality or condition of being composed of relatively large or coarse particles.

Examples

Coarse granularity
Different filters affect the granularity of the coffee grounds produced by the grinder.
The granularity of the salt was too coarse for baking delicate pastries.
The granularity of the sand made it perfect for use in concrete mixtures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coarseness - the quality of being rough or composed of large particles
  • antonym: fineness - the quality of being composed of very small or fine particles
