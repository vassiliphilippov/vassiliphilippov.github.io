jitters

noun
extreme nervousness or anxiety
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪt̬ɚz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪtəz / (BrE)
a feeling of extreme nervousness or anxiety, especially before an important event

Examples

The jitters before a test
A strong cup of coffee in the morning sometimes gives me the jitters.
He tried to calm his jitters before the big exam.
She always gets the jitters before speaking in public.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nerves - a feeling of nervousness or anxiety
