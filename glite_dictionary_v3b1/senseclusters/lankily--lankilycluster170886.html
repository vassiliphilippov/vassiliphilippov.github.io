lankily

adverb
in a limp way
Pronunciation: / ˈlæŋkɪli / (AmE), / ˈlæŋkɪli / (BrE)
in a limp, loose, or drooping manner, lacking firmness or stiffness

Examples

Curtains hang lankily
The curtains hung lankily in the humid air, barely moving with the breeze.
After the rain, his hair clung lankily to his forehead.
The old flag draped lankily from the pole, showing its age.

Related Terms

  • antonym: firmly - in a strong or solid way
  • synonym: limply - in a way that lacks firmness or stiffness
