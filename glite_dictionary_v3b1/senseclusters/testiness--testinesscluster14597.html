testiness

noun
state of being easily irritated
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛstɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈtɛstɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being easily annoyed or irritated; irritability

Examples

His testiness showed
He apologized for his testiness after realizing he had snapped at his friend without reason.
Her testiness was evident whenever someone disagreed with her opinion during the meeting.
Prolonged lack of sleep can increase testiness and reduce your ability to cope with stress.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmness - the quality of being free from agitation or strong emotion
  • synonym: irritability - the quality of being easily annoyed; irritableness
