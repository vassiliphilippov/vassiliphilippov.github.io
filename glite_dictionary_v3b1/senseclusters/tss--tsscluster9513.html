tss

interjection
hissing disapproval
Pronunciation: / t͡s / (AmE), / t͡s / (BrE)
used to express annoyance, disapproval, or to get someone's attention with a sharp hissing sound

Examples

A sharp tss sound
He made a loud tss when he saw the mess in the kitchen.
"Tss, you should know better than to do that," the teacher said quietly.
With a quick tss, she signaled her friend to stop talking.
