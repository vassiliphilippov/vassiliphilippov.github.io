glass eye

noun
artificial eye made of glass
3.9
Pronunciation: / ɡlæs aɪ / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs aɪ / (BrE)
an artificial eye made of glass used as a prosthetic replacement for a real eye

Examples

A glass eye
After his accident, he was fitted with a glass eye that matched his real one closely.
He often jokes about his glass eye when meeting new people.
The artist painted a remarkably lifelike glass eye for the sculpture's face.
