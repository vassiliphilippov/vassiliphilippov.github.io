daylily

noun
flowering perennial plant; blooms last one day
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪˌlɪli / (AmE), / ˈdeɪˌlɪli / (BrE)
A perennial plant with long, narrow leaves and lily-like flowers, each of which typically blooms for only one day.

Examples

A daylily flower
Each daylily flower opens at dawn and withers by nightfall, making every sunrise special.
Gardeners appreciate daylily varieties for their hardiness and brief, spectacular blossoms.
The vibrant orange daylily blooms brought color to the garden every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lily - a plant with large, often fragrant flowers in the genus Lilium
