nonsequential

adjective
not in order
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.sɪˈkwɛn.tʃu.əl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.sɪˈkwɛn.tʃu.əl / (BrE)
not arranged or happening in a specific or logical order or sequence

Examples

A nonsequential order
The chapters in this book are nonsequential, so you can read them in any order.
He completed the tasks in a nonsequential way, jumping from one to another.
The files were saved in a nonsequential order, making them harder to find.

Related Terms

  • synonym: random - happening or chosen without a specific pattern or order
  • antonym: sequential - arranged or happening in a specific order or sequence
