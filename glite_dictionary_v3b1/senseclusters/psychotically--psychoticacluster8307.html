psychotically

adverb
in a disordered way
Pronunciation: / saɪˈkɑːtɪkli / (AmE), / saɪˈkɒtɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows symptoms of severe mental disorder or instability

Examples

Psychotically violent
The villain stared psychotically at his captives, making everyone in the room uneasy.
She began to laugh psychotically after hearing the disturbing news.
He acted psychotically when confronted with the stressful situation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insanely - in a way that suggests madness or extreme irrationality
  • antonym: rationally - in a logical or reasonable way
