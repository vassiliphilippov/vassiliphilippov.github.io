knot

noun
hard round part in wood where branch grew
2.5
Pronunciation: / nɑt / (AmE), / nɒt / (BrE)
a hard lump or mass, such as in wood, muscles, or yarn

Examples

The weaver noticed a small knot in the middle of the cotton yarn.
