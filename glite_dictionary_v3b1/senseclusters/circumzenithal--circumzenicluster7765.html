circumzenithal

adjective
around the zenith
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜrkəmˈzɛnɪθəl / (AmE), / ˌsɜːkəmˈzɛnɪθəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated around the zenith, especially describing atmospheric phenomena occurring near the point directly overhead

Examples

A circumzenithal arc
The rare circumzenithal arc was visible for several minutes after the rain stopped.
Scientists observed a circumzenithal halo forming high above the clouds.
Photographers waited patiently to capture the circumzenithal phenomenon in the clear afternoon sky.
