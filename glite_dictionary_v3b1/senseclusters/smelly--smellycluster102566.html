smelly

adjective
having an unpleasant smell
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɛli / (AmE), / ˈsmɛli / (BrE)
having a strong or unpleasant odor

Examples

A smelly sock
After a long run, his socks became unbearably smelly.
She opened the fridge and noticed a smelly piece of cheese hiding in the back.
The garbage bin was so smelly that we could hardly stand to be near it.
