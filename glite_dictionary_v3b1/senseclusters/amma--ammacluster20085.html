amma

noun
female spiritual leader
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈʌmə / (BrE)
a respectful title or form of address for a female spiritual leader, holy woman, or revered elder, especially in South Asian religious contexts

Examples

Blessings from amma
Pilgrims waited for hours to receive a blessing from the revered amma.
The amma led the meditation session, offering guidance and wisdom to her followers.
Many people traveled across the country to seek advice from the respected amma in her ashram.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swami - a Hindu religious teacher or holy man
