wetter

noun
person who urinates in bed while asleep
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛtər / (AmE), / ˈwɛtə / (BrE)
a person, especially a child, who urinates involuntarily while asleep in bed; a bed-wetter.

Examples

A bed wetter
