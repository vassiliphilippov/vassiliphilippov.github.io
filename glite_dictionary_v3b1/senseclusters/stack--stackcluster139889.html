stack

noun
neat pile of objects
Pronunciation: / stæk / (AmE), / stak /; / stæk / (BrE)
a pile or heap of things, often arranged one on top of another

Examples

A stack of books
He built a stack of firewood near the back door.
She arranged the books in a stack on the table.
There was a stack of plates in the kitchen waiting to be washed.
She had a stack of unread magazines on her desk.
There's a stack of paperwork waiting for you when you get back.
We made a stack of pancakes for breakfast this morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pile - a number of things lying on top of each other
