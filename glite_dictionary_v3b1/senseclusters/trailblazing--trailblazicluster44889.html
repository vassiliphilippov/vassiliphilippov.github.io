trailblazing

noun
making new paths
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtreɪl.bleɪ.zɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈtreɪl.bleɪ.zɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of creating a new path or route through wild or undeveloped land by clearing and marking it

Examples

Trailblazing a path
The rangers' trailblazing through the dense forest allowed hikers to reach the lake.
Early settlers relied on trailblazing to open up new routes across the mountains.
Without careful trailblazing, it would have been impossible to navigate the thick jungle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exploration - the act of traveling through an area to learn about it
  • synonym: pathfinding - the act of finding or making a way through unknown areas
