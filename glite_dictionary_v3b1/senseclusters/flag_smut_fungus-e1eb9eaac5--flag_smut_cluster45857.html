flag smut fungus

noun
fungus causing cereal disease with black spores
9.0
Pronunciation: / flæɡ smʌt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / flæɡ smʌt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a fungal disease chiefly affecting cereals and grasses, in which sori form in the plant tissue and release black masses of spores
