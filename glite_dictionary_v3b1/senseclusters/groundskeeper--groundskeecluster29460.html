groundskeeper

noun
person who maintains grounds
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊndzˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊndzˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to maintain and care for the grounds of an estate, park, or sports field

Examples

The groundskeeper cleans
After the storm, the groundskeeper worked overtime to clear fallen branches.
As a groundskeeper, she takes pride in keeping the gardens immaculate.
The groundskeeper arrived early to mow the lawns before the park opened.
