coloratura soprano

noun
high soprano skilled in ornate vocal music
Pronunciation: / ˌkʌl.ə.rəˈtu.rə səˈprɑː.noʊ / (AmE), / ˌkɒl.ə.rəˈtjʊə səˈprɑː.nəʊ / (BrE)
A soprano singer whose voice is agile and capable of performing elaborate ornamentation and rapid passages, particularly in coloratura music.

Examples

Coloratura soprano singer
Maria auditioned for the role, showcasing her talent as a coloratura soprano.
Only a skilled coloratura soprano can execute the challenging runs in this aria.
The lead in the opera was performed by an acclaimed coloratura soprano known for her agility and range.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lyric soprano - a soprano with a warm and melodious tone, lighter than dramatic soprano
  • degree lower: mezzo-soprano - female singing voice between soprano and contralto
  • degree lower: soprano - the highest female singing voice; also a singer with such a voice
