muggle

noun
non-magical person
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌɡəl / (AmE), / ˈmʌɡl̩ / (BrE)
a person who does not have magical powers, especially in a fantasy setting

Examples

A muggle in magic
In the wizarding world, a muggle cannot perform magic.
The secret must be kept hidden from every muggle in the city.
She grew up as a muggle before discovering her magical heritage.
