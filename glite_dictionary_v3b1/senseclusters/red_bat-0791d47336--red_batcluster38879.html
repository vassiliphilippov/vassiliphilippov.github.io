red bat

noun
a North American bat with reddish fur
Pronunciation: / rɛd bæt / (AmE), / rɛd bæt / (BrE)
A North American bat with brick-red or rusty red fur, often with white-tipped hairs.

Examples

The red bat animal
Scientists study the migration patterns of the red bat across North America.
The red bat is often seen roosting among the leaves in summer.
The distinctive coloring of the red bat helps it blend into autumn foliage.
