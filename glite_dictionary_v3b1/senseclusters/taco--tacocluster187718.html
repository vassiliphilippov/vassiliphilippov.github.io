taco

noun
Mexican dish of a filled tortilla
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑː.koʊ / (AmE), / ˈtæk.əʊ / (BrE)
A Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various mixtures, such as seasoned meat, beans, lettuce, and cheese.

Examples

A taco with beef
I ordered a beef taco with extra cheese at the restaurant.
She made fresh fish tacos for dinner last night.
The food truck sells the best chicken taco in town.
