utterer

noun
one who passes counterfeit money
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʌtɚɚ / (AmE), / ˈʌtərə / (BrE)
a person who circulates or attempts to pass off forged banknotes or counterfeit coins as genuine

Examples

Counterfeit utterer
Authorities believe the utterer had been working with a larger forgery ring.
In court, the accused utterer denied knowing the coins were counterfeit.
The police arrested the utterer after he tried to spend several fake twenty-dollar bills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterfeiter - a person who makes fraudulent copies of money or documents
