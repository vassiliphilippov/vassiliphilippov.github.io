dyssodia

noun
abnormal body odor
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsoʊdiə / (AmE), / dɪˈsəʊdiə / (BrE)
a medical condition marked by an unpleasant or abnormal body odor

Examples

Dyssodia causes odor

Certain metabolic disorders can lead to dyssodia in affected individuals.
Treatment for dyssodia often involves addressing the underlying cause of the odor.
