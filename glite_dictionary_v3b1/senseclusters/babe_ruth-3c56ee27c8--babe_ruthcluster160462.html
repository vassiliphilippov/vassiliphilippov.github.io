Babe Ruth

proper noun
legendary American baseball player
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌbeɪb ˈruːθ / (AmE), / ˌbeɪb ˈruːθ / (BrE)
(1895–1948) United States professional baseball player, widely regarded as one of the greatest hitters in the history of the sport, famous for his home run record.
