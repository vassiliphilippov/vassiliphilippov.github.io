voivode

noun
regional governor
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔɪˌvoʊd / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪˌvəʊd / (BrE)
a governor or military leader of a province or region, especially in Eastern European countries

Examples

The voivode leader
The voivode issued new laws to strengthen the region's defenses.
In medieval times, a voivode often led both the army and the administration.
The king appointed a trusted voivode to govern the border province.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commander - a person in authority, especially over a military force
  • synonym: governor - the official leader of a region or province
