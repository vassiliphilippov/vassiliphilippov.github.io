swirly

adjective
curved or spiral patterns
Pronunciation: / ˈswɜːrli / (AmE), / ˈswɜːli / (BrE)
having shapes, lines, or patterns that curve or spiral in a twisting way

Examples

Swirly patterns on walls
The artist painted swirly designs all over the walls of the café.
She chose a swirly font for the invitation to make it look more playful.
Clouds formed swirly patterns in the sky before the storm arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curly - having curves or twists
  • synonym: spiral - winding in a continuous curve around a central point
  • antonym: straight - not curved or bent
