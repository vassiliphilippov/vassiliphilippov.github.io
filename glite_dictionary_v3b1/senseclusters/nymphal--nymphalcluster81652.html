nymphal

adjective
like a nymph
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪmfəl / (AmE), / ˈnɪmfəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a nymph, especially suggesting delicate beauty or youthful charm

Examples

Nymphal beauty
Her nymphal appearance made her seem almost otherworldly in the soft morning light.
The dancer moved with a nymphal grace that captivated the entire audience.
Artists often depict spring as a season with nymphal figures among blooming flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angelic - having a sweet, delicate, or innocent quality
  • synonym: nymphlike - resembling or characteristic of a nymph
