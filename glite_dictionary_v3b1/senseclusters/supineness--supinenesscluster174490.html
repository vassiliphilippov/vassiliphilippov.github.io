supineness

noun
passive or lazy state
Pronunciation: / suːˈpaɪnəs / (AmE), / suːˈpaɪnɪs / (BrE)
a state of lying on the back or showing passive, lazy, or inactive behavior

Examples

His supineness in action
His supineness in the face of injustice frustrated his friends.
The manager criticized the team's supineness during the crucial project phase.
She overcame her supineness and started taking initiative at work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: activity - the state of being active or energetic
  • synonym: laziness - the quality of being unwilling to work or use energy
  • synonym: passivity - the quality of accepting or allowing things to happen without active response
