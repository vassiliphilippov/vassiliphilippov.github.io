noun
UK sick pay benefit

Statutory Sick Pay; a legally required payment in the UK that employers give to employees who are absent from work due to illness.

Examples

UK SSP payment
If you are too ill to work, you may be eligible for SSP from your employer.
Many employees rely on SSP to help cover their expenses during periods of sickness.
The rules for claiming SSP are set by the UK government and apply to most workers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benefit - a payment or advantage provided by the government or employer
  • synonym: sick pay - money paid to an employee when they are off work due to illness
