devil's advocate

noun
someone who deliberately argues an opposing view
5.6 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdev.əlz ˈæd.və.kət/ (AmE), /ˈdev.əlz ˈæd.və.kət/ (BrE)
a person who expresses a contrary or unpopular opinion in order to provoke debate or test the strength of the opposing arguments

Examples

Play devil's advocate
Although he disagreed, he took the devil's advocate role to strengthen our proposal.
Let me play devil's advocate for a moment and question that assumption.
She often acted as devil's advocate in meetings to ensure every idea was challenged.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contrarian - a person who opposes or rejects popular opinion, especially in stock market dealings
