excessive

adjective
more than is reasonable or necessary
6.5 3.0
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈses.ɪv/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈses.ɪv/ (BrE)
more than is reasonable, necessary, or acceptable; too much in amount, degree, feeling, or action

Examples

An excessive amount
He used an excessive amount of salt in the recipe, ruining the flavor.
Parents are worried about their children's excessive screen time on phones and tablets.
The company was criticized for its excessive price increases this year.
His excessive enthusiasm sometimes makes others uncomfortable.
She found his praise a bit excessive and insincere.
The report criticized the company for its excessive use of resources.
