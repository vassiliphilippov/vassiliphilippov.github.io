Synonyms

  • burial mound
    7.1 0.21/m
    burial mound is an artificial heap of earth built over graves in archaeology, while barrow is the common older word.
  • tumulus
    9.9 0.05/m
    tumulus is the technical archaeological term for a stone or earth burial, while barrow is a more general, everyday word.
  • grave mound
    8.8 0.03/m
    grave mound is a simple phrase for an earth heap over a grave, while barrow is the historic name used for such burial mounds.
