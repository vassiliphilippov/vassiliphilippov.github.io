nap

noun
a short daytime sleep
1.0 12/m
Pronunciation: / nÃ¦p / (AmE), / nÃ¦p / (BrE)
a short period of sleep, usually during the day

Examples

A quick nap
He took a quick nap on the sofa before dinner.
Sometimes I feel much better after a short afternoon nap.
The baby fell asleep for a nap in her stroller.
I often take a nap after lunch to recharge my energy.
She woke from her nap feeling refreshed and ready to work.
The baby is quiet now because he's down for his afternoon nap.
