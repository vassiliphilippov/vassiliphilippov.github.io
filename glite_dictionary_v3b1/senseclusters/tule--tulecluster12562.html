tule

noun
plant material for weaving
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆtuËli / (AmE), / ËˆtuËli / (BrE)
the dried stalks or stems of the tule plant, used as material for weaving mats, baskets, or boats

Examples

Tule mats
The villagers braided dried tule into strong mats to cover the boat.
Artisans skillfully weave tule into baskets and hats for local markets.
Traditional houses in the region often use tule for roofing and insulation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rush - a grass-like plant growing in wet places, used for mats and baskets
