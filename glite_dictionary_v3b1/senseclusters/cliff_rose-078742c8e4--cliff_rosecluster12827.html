cliff rose

noun
a tufted plant of temperate seacoasts and mountains
6.5
Pronunciation: / klɪf roʊz / (AmE), / klɪf rəʊz / (BrE)
a tufted plant of seacoasts and mountains in the north temperate zone, sometimes used as a ground cover

Examples

The cliff rose plant
Gardeners sometimes use cliff rose as an attractive ground cover for steep banks.
The cliff rose thrives in rocky coastal landscapes with little soil.
While hiking in the mountains, we spotted several blooming cliff roses.
