earlap

noun
flap on a cap to cover the ears
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrˌlæp / (AmE), / ˈɪəˌlæp / (BrE)
one of two fabric flaps attached to a cap or hat, designed to cover and protect the ears from cold.

Examples

A earlap on a hat
During cold weather, hats with a earlap are especially useful for children.
She pulled down the earlap of her winter hat before stepping outside.
The old hunter's cap had a leather earlap on each side to keep him warm.
