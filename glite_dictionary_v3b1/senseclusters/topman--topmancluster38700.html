topman

noun
sailor on upper rigging
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑpmən / (AmE), / ˈtɒpmən / (BrE)
a sailor who works on the highest sails and rigging of a ship, especially on the masts

Examples

The topman climbs mast
The topman climbed swiftly up the mast to adjust the sails before the storm hit.
As a skilled topman, he was trusted to handle the most dangerous tasks aloft.
During training, each new recruit watched the topman demonstrate how to secure the rigging high above the deck.
The experienced topman quickly climbed the mast to secure the loose rope.
During the storm, every topman was needed to adjust the sails high above the deck.
He dreamed of becoming a topman and working among the sails and rigging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boatswain - a ship's officer in charge of equipment and crew
  • synonym: deckhand - a sailor who works on the deck of a ship
  • synonym: sailor - a person who works on a ship
  • synonym: seaman - a sailor or crew member on a ship
