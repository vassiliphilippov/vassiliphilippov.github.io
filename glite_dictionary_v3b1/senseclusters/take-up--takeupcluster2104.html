take-up

noun
act of tightening, absorbing, or reeling in
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪk ʌp / (AmE), / ˈteɪk ʌp / (BrE)
the action or a device for tightening, absorbing, or drawing in something, such as slack in a rope, thread, or film
