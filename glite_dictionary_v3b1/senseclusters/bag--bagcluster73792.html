bag

noun
amount contained in a single bag
1.9
Pronunciation: / bæɡ / (AmE), / bæɡ / (BrE)

Examples

A bag of chips

I bought a bag of rice at the supermarket.
She handed me a bag of apples picked fresh from the orchard.
