hemopneumothorax

noun
blood and air in chest
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːmoʊˌnuːmoʊˈθɔːræks / (AmE), / ˌhiːməʊˌnjuːməʊˈθɔːræks / (BrE)
a medical condition in which both blood and air are present in the chest cavity, often due to trauma

Examples

A hemopneumothorax injury
The doctor identified a hemopneumothorax on the X-ray after the car accident.
Emergency surgery was required to treat the patient's hemopneumothorax and restore normal breathing.
A hemopneumothorax can be life-threatening if not treated quickly in trauma cases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hemothorax - blood in the chest cavity
  • synonym: pneumothorax - air in the chest cavity causing lung collapse
