unplug the device

verb
disconnect a device from power
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈplʌɡ ðə dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ʌnˈplʌɡ ði dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
to disconnect an electrical device from its power supply by removing its plug from the socket

Examples

To unplug the device
If the screen freezes, try to unplug the device and plug it back in.
Remember to unplug the device overnight to save energy.
You should always unplug the device before cleaning it to ensure safety.
