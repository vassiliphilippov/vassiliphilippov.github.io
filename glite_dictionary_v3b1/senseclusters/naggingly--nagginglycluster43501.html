naggingly

adverb
persistently annoying
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnæɡɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈnæɡɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that repeatedly annoys or worries someone

Examples

To naggingly annoy
The thought of the unfinished project naggingly stayed in her mind all weekend.
He was naggingly reminded of his promise every time he saw the note.
The pain in his shoulder returned naggingly whenever he tried to lift something heavy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: constantly - happening all the time or very often
  • synonym: persistently - in a way that continues for a long time
