concessioner

noun
person or company running a concession
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɛʃənər / (AmE), / kənˈsɛʃənə / (BrE)
a person or company that is granted or operates a concession, especially for selling goods or services in a particular place

Examples

A concessioner sells goods
As a concessioner, she is responsible for maintaining high standards of customer service.
The park awarded a new contract to a concessioner to manage the souvenir shop.
The stadium's concessioner sold drinks and snacks to the spectators during the game.
