Having or showing a strong, unreasonable attachment to a particular belief or opinion, and an intolerance toward those who hold different views.

Examples

Bigoted remarks
Many people found his bigoted remarks deeply offensive and divisive.
She refused to engage in bigoted conversations about other cultures.
The school promotes respect and understanding instead of bigoted behavior.

Synonyms

  • intolerant
    4.6 3.83/m
    intolerant is unwilling to accept different beliefs or behaviours, while bigoted shows a strong, unreasonable attachment and active rejection of others' views.
  • prejudiced
    5.0 1.25/m
    prejudiced shows unfair dislike of people or groups for reasons like race or religion, while bigoted holds rigid beliefs and rejects different people.
  • narrow-minded
    5.1 1.68/m
    narrow-minded has a limited, closed view and resists new ideas, while bigoted actively defends beliefs and refuses to accept others.
  • illiberal
    8.2 0.33/m
    illiberal opposes free or different opinions in a formal or political way, while bigoted is personally intolerant and strongly attached to beliefs.
