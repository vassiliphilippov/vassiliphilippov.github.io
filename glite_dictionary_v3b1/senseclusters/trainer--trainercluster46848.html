trainer

noun
software component that trains ML models

(in computing, especially machine learning) a software component or system that trains machine learning models by running training loops, adjusting parameters, and managing data and optimization.

Examples

ML trainer software
The ML framework includes a trainer that optimizes neural networks for image classification.
During experiments, the team adjusted the trainer's hyperparameters to reduce overfitting on the validation set.
Cloud-based trainers let startups train large models without investing in expensive local GPUs.
