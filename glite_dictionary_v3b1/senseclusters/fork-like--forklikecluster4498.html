fork-like

adjective
resembling a fork; branching
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɔːkˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the shape or characteristics of a fork; divided or separated into two or more branches.

Examples

A fork-like shape
The geologist pointed to a fork-like crack running through the rock.
The insect's fork-like antennae help it navigate its environment.
The river split into two fork-like branches just before it reached the sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forked - divided into two or more branches; like a fork
