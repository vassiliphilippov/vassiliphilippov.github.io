scissortail

noun
a long-tailed gray flycatcher bird
9.2 8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪz.ər.teɪl/ (AmE), /ˈsɪz.ə.teɪl/ (BrE)
a slender gray flycatcher native to the southwestern United States, Mexico, and Central America, distinguished by its long, deeply forked tail and white breast with salmon and scarlet markings
