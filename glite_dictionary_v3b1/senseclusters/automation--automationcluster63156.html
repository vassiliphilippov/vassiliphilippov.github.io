automation

noun
automatic control equipment; self-operating technology
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˈmeɪʃən /; / ˌɔːtəˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
the use of machines, computers, or technology to do work automatically without human help
