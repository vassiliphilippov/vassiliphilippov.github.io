asserting

adjective
relating to stating or declaring
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈsɝː.t̬ɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈsɜː.tɪŋ / (BrE)
expressing something as a declaration or statement of fact; relating to the act of asserting

Examples

An asserting tone
Academic writing often employs an asserting tone to establish authority.
Her asserting manner left no room for doubt in the meeting.
The lawyer's asserting arguments convinced the jury of his client's innocence.
