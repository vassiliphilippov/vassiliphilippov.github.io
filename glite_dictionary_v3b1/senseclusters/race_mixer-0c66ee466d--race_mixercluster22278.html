race mixer

noun
event fostering racial integration
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪs ˌmɪksər / (AmE), / ˈreɪs ˌmɪksə / (BrE)
An event organized to promote social interaction and integration among people of different races.

Examples

A race mixer event
Dozens attended the race mixer to network and celebrate diversity in the city.
Organizers hope the race mixer will lead to lasting friendships across communities.
The university held a race mixer to encourage understanding between students of different backgrounds.
