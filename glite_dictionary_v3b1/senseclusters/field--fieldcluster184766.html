field

noun
visible area through an optical instrument
6.7 6.01/m
Pronunciation: / fiÀêld / (AmE), / fiÀêld / (BrE)
the area that can be seen through an optical instrument, such as a microscope or telescope

Examples

Microscope field
A larger field provides a broader view of celestial objects.
Observers expanded the field by adjusting the telescope's lens.
The field of the microscope allows scientists to examine tiny organisms in great detail.

Synonyms

  • field of view
    4.2 2.06/m
    field of view shows how much you can see through an instrument at once, while field means the area you see through it.
  • microscopic field
    5.1 0.02/m
    microscopic field is the small area seen through a microscope at one magnification, while field is the general visible area through an instrument.

Related Terms

  • synonym: view - the range or extent of what can be seen
