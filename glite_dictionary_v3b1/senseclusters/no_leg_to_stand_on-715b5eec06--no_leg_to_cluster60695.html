no leg to stand on

x
without justification or valid support
Pronunciation: / noʊ lɛɡ tə stænd ɑn / (AmE), / nəʊ lɛɡ tə stænd ɒn / (BrE)
to have no valid reason, support, or justification for one's actions or opinions

Examples

No leg to stand on in argument
After forgetting the deadline, he had no leg to stand on in his defense.
If witnesses contradict her story, she will have no leg to stand on in court.
Without evidence, the theory really has no leg to stand on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: no grounds - without reasonable or justifiable basis
