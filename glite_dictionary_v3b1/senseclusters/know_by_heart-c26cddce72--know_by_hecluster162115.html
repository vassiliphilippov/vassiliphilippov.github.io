know by heart

verb
memorize exactly; recall without reading
3.7
Pronunciation: / noʊ baɪ hɑrt / (AmE), / nəʊ baɪ hɑːt / (BrE)
to have memorized something so thoroughly that it can be recalled or recited without needing to read it

Examples

To know by heart a poem
After listening to the song so many times, I knew by heart every word.
She can recite every poem in the book because she knows by heart each one.
You have to know by heart all the important phone numbers in case you lose your phone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forget - to be unable to remember something
  • synonym: memorize - to learn something so well that you can remember it exactly
