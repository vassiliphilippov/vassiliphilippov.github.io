biddie

noun
gossipy old woman
9.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪd.iː/ /; / /ˈbɪd.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈbɪd.iː/ /; / /ˈbɪd.ɪ/ (BrE)
an old woman who is gossipy, meddlesome, or quarrelsome, often used as an insulting term

Examples

A nosy biddie
The neighbors called her a biddie because she always watched from her window and reported everything.
No one wanted to sit near the biddie who constantly criticized everyone at the meeting.
He complained that the biddies on the street were always spreading rumors about his family.
