an abbreviation for Deep Packet Inspection, a network technique that examines the contents of data packets to monitor, filter, or manage internet traffic

Examples

DPI network filtering
Many internet service providers use DPI to detect and block certain types of online content.
The government implemented DPI to monitor suspicious network activity for security purposes.
Some privacy advocates are concerned about the widespread use of DPI in public networks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firewall - a security system that controls incoming and outgoing network traffic
  • synonym: packet filtering - a method of controlling network access by monitoring outgoing and incoming packets
