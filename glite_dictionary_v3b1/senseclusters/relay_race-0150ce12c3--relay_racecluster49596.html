relay race

noun
team race with members running in sequence
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈriːleɪ reɪs / (AmE), / ˈriːleɪ reɪs / (BrE)
A race between teams in which each member completes a part of the distance before passing on to the next participant.

Examples

A relay race team
Each runner handed the baton to the next during the relay race.
Our school team trained hard for the relay race at the regional meet.
She anchored her team to victory in the 4x100 meter relay race.
