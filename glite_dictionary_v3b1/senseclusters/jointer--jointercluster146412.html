jointer

noun
carpenter's plane for board edges
7.7 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒɔɪn.tər/ (AmE), /ˈdʒɔɪn.tə/ (BrE)
a long carpenter's plane used to shape and straighten the edges of boards so that they fit together accurately
