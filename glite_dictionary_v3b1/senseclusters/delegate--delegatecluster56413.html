delegate

noun
person chosen to represent others
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛlɪˌɡeɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɛlɪɡət / (BrE)
a person who is chosen or elected to represent a group, especially at a meeting or conference

Examples

Conference delegate
As a union delegate, she attended all major negotiations on behalf of her coworkers.
Each country sent a delegate to the international climate conference.
The delegate from Ohio addressed the assembly with new proposals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: representative - a person chosen or appointed to act or speak for others
