Kleinian

adjective
of Felix Klein's mathematics
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪniən / (AmE), / ˈklaɪniən / (BrE)
relating to the mathematical theories or work of Felix Klein, especially in geometry or group theory.

Examples

Kleinian group theory
The mathematician gave a lecture on Kleinian groups and their applications in modern geometry.
Many advanced topics in hyperbolic geometry are based on Kleinian concepts.
Her research focuses on Kleinian structures and their role in group theory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Kleinian geometry - a branch of geometry developed by Felix Klein
  • synonym: Kleinian group - a type of group in mathematics studied by Felix Klein
