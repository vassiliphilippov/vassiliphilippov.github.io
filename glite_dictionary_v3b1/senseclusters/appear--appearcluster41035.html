appear

verb
be issued or published
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈpɪr / (AmE), / əˈpɪə / (BrE)
(of a book, film, etc.) to be issued, published, or made available to the public.

Examples

Appear in print
Her debut novel will appear in bookstores next month.
The magazine appears every Friday and is available in both print and online editions.
The new regulations first appeared in the government bulletin last week.
