swisher

noun
one making swishing sound
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈswɪʃə / (BrE)
a person or thing that makes a soft swishing or rustling sound as it moves

Examples

A swisher dress
The dancer's costume was a true swisher, brushing softly against the stage with every move.
As the wind picked up, the tall grass became a gentle swisher in the quiet field.
He moved through the hallway like a swisher, his coat making a faint sound with each step.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clatterer - something that makes a loud, clattering sound
  • synonym: rustler - something that makes a soft, rustling sound
