torsional

adjective
twisting forces
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrʒənəl / (AmE), / ˈtɔːʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by twisting or turning forces

Examples

Torsional force
The engineer calculated the torsional strength of the metal rod before construction began.
A torsional force can cause a shaft to twist and eventually break if it is too strong.
The bridge was designed to withstand torsional stress during strong winds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bending - relating to forces that cause something to curve
  • synonym: rotational - relating to rotation or turning around an axis
