eudaemonic

adjective
producing happiness and well-being
9.9
Pronunciation: / juːˌdæˈmɑːnɪk / (AmE), / juːˌdiːˈmɒnɪk / (BrE)

Examples

Policies that are eudaemonic aim to enhance societal well-being and promote public contentment.
The retreat promised a eudaemonic experience, focused on personal growth and positive emotions.
