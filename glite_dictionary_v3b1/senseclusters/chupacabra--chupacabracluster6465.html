chupacabra

noun
mythical blood-sucking creature
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃuːpəˈkɑːbrə / (AmE), / ˌtʃuːpəˈkɑːbrə / (BrE)
a legendary or mythical creature said to attack and drink the blood of livestock, especially goats
