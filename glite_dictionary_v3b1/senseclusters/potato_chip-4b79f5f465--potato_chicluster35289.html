potato chip

noun
thin, crisp fried potato slice
Pronunciation: / pəˈtoʊdoʊ tʃɪp / (AmE), / pəˈtəʊtəʊ tʃɪp / (BrE)
A thin, crisp slice of potato that is deep-fried or baked and eaten as a snack.

Examples

A bag of potato chips
He prefers baked potato chips as a healthier alternative.
She grabbed a handful of potato chips from the bowl during the party.
Would you like a sandwich and some potato chips for lunch?
