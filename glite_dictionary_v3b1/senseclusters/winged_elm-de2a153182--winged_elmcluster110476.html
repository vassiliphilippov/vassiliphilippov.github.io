winged elm

noun
North American elm with corky-winged branches
9.9
Pronunciation: / wɪŋd ɛlm / (AmE), / wɪŋd ɛlm / (BrE)
A North American elm tree (Ulmus alata) with twigs and young branches bearing prominent corky ridges or projections.

Examples

The winged elm tree
The winged elm is easily recognized by the corky ridges on its branches.
The forest ranger pointed out a mature winged elm growing near the stream.
We planted a winged elm in our backyard to attract more birds.
