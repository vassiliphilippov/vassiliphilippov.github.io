batteries-included

adjective
ready to use; fully equipped
Pronunciation: / ˈbætəriːz ɪnˈkluːdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbætəriːz ɪnˈkluːdɪd / (BrE)
(of a product or system) supplied with all necessary components or features for immediate use, without requiring additional parts or installation.

Examples

batteries-included product
I appreciate when gadgets are sold batteries-included, saving time and hassle during setup.
The children's camera comes batteries-included, so you can start taking photos immediately.
We opted for the batteries-included version of the software to avoid installing extra plugins.
