sandwichman

noun
man carrying advertising boards
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsænd.wɪtʃˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈsæn.wɪdʒˌmæn / (BrE)
a man employed to carry advertising boards, typically hanging from the shoulders, usually on the front and back of the body

Examples

A sandwichman carries boards
A sandwichman walked up and down the high street, advertising a new restaurant opening nearby.
Dressed as a sandwichman, he braved the cold to hand out flyers for the event.
The company hired a sandwichman to promote their summer sale in the busy shopping district.
