teether

noun
child growing teeth
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːðər / (AmE), / ˈtiːðə / (BrE)
a baby or young child who is in the process of growing their first teeth

Examples

A baby teether
The teether cried throughout the night because her gums were sore.
Parents often give a teether a special toy to chew on for comfort.
It can be challenging to soothe a teether during this stage of development.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infant - a very young child or baby
  • synonym: toddler - a young child who is just beginning to walk
