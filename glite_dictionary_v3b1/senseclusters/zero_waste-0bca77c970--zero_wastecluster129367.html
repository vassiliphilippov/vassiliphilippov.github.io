zero waste

noun
philosophy of eliminating waste
Pronunciation: / ˌzɪroʊ ˈweɪst / (AmE), / ˌzɪərəʊ ˈweɪst / (BrE)
A philosophy or approach aimed at eliminating the generation of waste by redesigning systems and promoting resource reuse, recycling, and reduction.

Examples

Zero waste lifestyle
Her commitment to zero waste living inspired others to reduce their use of disposable products.
Many communities are striving to become zero waste cities within the next decade.
The company adopted a zero waste policy to minimize its environmental impact.
