bolter

noun
flour sifting device
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊltər / (AmE), / ˈbəʊltə / (BrE)
a machine or cloth used to sift flour and separate fine meal from bran in milling

Examples

Flour bolter
The old mill still uses a bolter to produce high-quality flour.
After grinding the wheat, the grain passes through a bolter to remove the bran.
A skilled miller knows how to adjust the bolter for the finest results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mill - a machine for grinding grain into flour
  • synonym: sieve - a device with a mesh or perforated bottom used for separating coarse from fine parts
