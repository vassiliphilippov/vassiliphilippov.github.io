Hungarian goulash

noun
Hungarian paprika-seasoned meat stew
Pronunciation: / hʌŋˈɡɛriən ɡuˈlɑʃ / (AmE), / hʌŋˈɡɛriən ˈɡuːlæʃ / (BrE)
a rich, hearty stew of meat and vegetables, highly seasoned with paprika, originating from Hungary
