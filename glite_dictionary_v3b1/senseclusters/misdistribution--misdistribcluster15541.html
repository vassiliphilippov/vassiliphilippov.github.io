misdistribution

noun
incorrect allocation
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən / (BrE)
an abnormal or incorrect spreading or allocation of a substance within an organism or part of the body

Examples

Misdistribution of blood
The misdistribution of blood flow can lead to tissue damage in certain organs.
Scientists are studying the misdistribution of fat in patients with metabolic disorders.
Improper injection technique may result in the misdistribution of medication within the body.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distribution - the act of spreading or allocating something
  • synonym: maldistribution - an uneven or faulty distribution of something
