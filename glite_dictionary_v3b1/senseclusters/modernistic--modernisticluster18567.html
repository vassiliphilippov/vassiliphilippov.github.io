modernistic

adjective
influenced by a recent style or modernism
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑdərˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɒdəˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a recent or contemporary trend, especially in art, architecture, or design, often influenced by modernism.

Examples

Modernistic furniture design
She prefers modernistic furniture with clean lines and minimal ornamentation in her living room.
The building's modernistic design stands out among the older, traditional structures in the city.
The museum features an impressive collection of modernistic paintings and sculptures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times as opposed to the remote past
