handshake

noun
act of shaking hands as greeting or agreement
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌʃeɪk / (AmE), / ˈhændˌʃeɪk / (BrE)
the act of grasping and shaking someone’s hand, typically as a greeting, farewell, or to seal an agreement

Examples

A friendly handshake
A simple handshake can convey respect and trust between two people.
After their long argument, a friendly handshake marked their reconciliation.
They sealed the deal with a firm handshake and a smile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: greeting - an act of welcoming, such as a word or gesture
