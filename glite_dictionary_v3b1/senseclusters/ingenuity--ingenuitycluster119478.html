ingenuity

noun
cleverness or inventiveness
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndʒəˈnuːəti /; / ˌɪn.dʒəˈnuː.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌɪndʒɪˈnjuːɪti /; / ˌɪn.dʒəˈnjuː.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality of being clever, original, and inventive, especially in solving problems or creating things

Examples

Show ingenuity in solving
Her ingenuity allowed her to find creative solutions to difficult problems.
The ingenuity of the new device impressed the entire engineering team.
We admired the architect's ingenuity in using recycled materials for the building.
His ingenuity allowed him to fix the engine with only a few basic tools.
The architect's ingenuity is evident in the unique design of the building.
They admired the ingenuity of her solution to the difficult problem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleverness - the quality of being clever or quick-witted
  • synonym: creativity - the use of imagination or original ideas to create something
  • antonym: dullness - the lack of inventiveness or original thought; being uninteresting or unimaginative
  • synonym: inventiveness - the ability to create or design new things or ideas
  • synonym: resourcefulness - the ability to find quick and clever ways to overcome difficulties
  • antonym: stupidity - lack of intelligence or cleverness
