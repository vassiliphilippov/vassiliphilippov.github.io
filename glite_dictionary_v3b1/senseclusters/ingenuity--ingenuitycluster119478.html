  • creativity
    3.4 24/m
    creativity is the ability to imagine new ideas in art or everyday life, while ingenuity stresses clever practical solutions.
  • innovativeness
    6.7 0.12/m
    innovativeness is the habit of introducing new methods or products in organizations, while ingenuity is personal cleverness in solving problems.
  • originality
    4.5 4.57/m

  • cleverness
