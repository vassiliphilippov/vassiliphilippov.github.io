aventurine

noun
translucent, sparkly quartz mineral
9.8
Pronunciation: / əˈvɛntʃəˌrin / (AmE), / əˈvɛntjʊəriːn / (BrE)
a translucent variety of quartz containing small, reflective mineral inclusions, usually mica, giving it a sparkly or glistening appearance

Examples

Green aventurine
Green aventurine is often believed to bring good luck and prosperity.
The artisan used aventurine to make a dazzling pendant for the necklace.
The bowl sparkled in the sunlight, thanks to the flecks of mica in the aventurine.
