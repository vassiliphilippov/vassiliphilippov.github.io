audience

noun
group interested in or receiving information/entertainment
Pronunciation: / ˈɔdiəns / (AmE), / ˈɔːdiəns / (BrE)
a group of people who are interested in or consume information, entertainment, or products, such as from a performance, broadcast, or advertisement

Examples

Target audience
Advertisers are always looking for ways to reach their target audience.
The book was written to appeal to a young adult audience.
The new television series attracted a huge audience on its opening night.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: crowd - a large number of people gathered together, typically in a disorganized or unruly way
  • synonym: spectator - a person who watches an event, especially a sports event
  • synonym: viewer - a person watching television or a broadcast
