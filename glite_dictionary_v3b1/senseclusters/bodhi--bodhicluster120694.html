bodhi

noun
sacred fig tree
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊdi / (AmE), / ˈbəʊdi / (BrE)
the sacred fig tree (Ficus religiosa), especially the tree under which the Buddha is believed to have attained enlightenment

Examples

The bodhi tree
Pilgrims placed flowers beneath the bodhi that shaded the ancient shrine.
The bodhi is an important symbol in Buddhist tradition and is often visited by devotees.
Legends say the Buddha meditated under a bodhi for many days before reaching enlightenment.
