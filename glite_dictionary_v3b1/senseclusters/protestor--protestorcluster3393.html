protestor

noun
person expressing objection
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊˌtɛstər / (AmE), / ˈprəʊˌtɛstə / (BrE)
a person who publicly expresses strong objection or disapproval, especially by taking part in a demonstration

Examples

A protestor holds a sign
The protestor held a sign demanding justice outside the courthouse.
Police tried to keep the protestors away from the main entrance during the rally.
Each protestor shared their reasons for joining the demonstration in front of the city hall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demonstrator - a person who takes part in a public protest or demonstration
  • antonym: supporter - a person who approves of and encourages someone or something
