Colchis

proper noun
ancient region of Greek myth, Jason's quest
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːl.kɪs / (AmE), / ˈkɒl.kɪs / (BrE)
An ancient region located on the eastern coast of the Black Sea, south of the Caucasus, noted in Greek mythology as the destination where Jason sought the Golden Fleece.

Examples

Colchis ancient region
According to myth, Jason and the Argonauts sailed to Colchis to find the Golden Fleece.
Historians believe that the real Colchis was situated in what is now western Georgia.
The legend of Colchis has inspired countless works of art and literature.
