cleanse

noun
regimen or period to purify
4.8 8.40/m
Pronunciation: / klÉ›nz / (AmE), / klÉ›nz / (BrE)
A regimen or period intended to purify or rid the body, mind, or environment of unwanted substances or influences (for example, a dietary detox or a break from social media).

Examples

A juice cleanse
She completed a seven-day cleanse of juices and smoothies to try to improve her digestion.
After months online, he decided on a weekend cleanse by deleting social apps and turning off notifications.
The company underwent a management cleanse, replacing several executives to improve public trust.

Synonyms

  • detoxification
    4.9 0.59/m
    detoxification is a medical process to remove poisons from the body, while cleanse is a broader regimen to purify body, mind, or space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digital detox - a period of time when someone voluntarily reduces or stops using digital devices or social media
