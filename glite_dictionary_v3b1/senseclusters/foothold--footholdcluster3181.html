foothold

noun
place to support the foot
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌhoʊld / (AmE), / ˈfʊthəʊld / (BrE)
A place where your foot can be securely placed, used for support in standing or climbing.

Examples

A secure foothold
I lost my foothold and nearly slipped during the hike.
She carefully tested each foothold before moving higher up the ladder.
The climber searched for a secure foothold on the steep rock face.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handhold - a thing to hold with the hand when climbing or moving
