guard duty

noun
task of acting as a guard or sentry
Pronunciation: / ɡɑrd ˈduːti / (AmE), / ɡɑːd ˈdjuːti / (BrE)
the task or period of acting as a guard or sentry, often as part of a military or security assignment

Examples

Guard duty shift
During guard duty, you must remain alert at all times.
He was assigned guard duty at the entrance to the camp.
She complained about having to pull guard duty on consecutive nights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sentry duty - the function or period of serving as a sentry
