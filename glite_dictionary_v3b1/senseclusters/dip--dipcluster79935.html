dip

noun
a candle made by repeated dipping
4.8
Pronunciation: / dɪp / (AmE), / dɪp / (BrE)
a candle made by repeatedly dipping a wick into molten wax or tallow to build up layers

Examples

A wax dip candle
In colonial times, every household needed a supply of dips for lighting after dark.
She learned how to make a dip using traditional candle-making techniques.
The old farmhouse was lit by a single dip on the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: taper - a slender candle, especially one used ceremonially
