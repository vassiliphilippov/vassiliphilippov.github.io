patriarchy

noun
male-dominated social system
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪ.triˌɑːr.ki /; / ˈpeɪtriˌɑːrki / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ.triˌɑː.ki /; / ˈpætriˌɑːki / (BrE)
a social system or organization in which men hold the main power and control, especially in leadership, authority, and inheritance
