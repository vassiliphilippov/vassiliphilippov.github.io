needleful

noun
amount in a needle
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdlfʊl / (AmE), / ˈniːdlfʊl / (BrE)
the amount of liquid that fills a needle, especially for an injection

Examples

A needleful of medicine
The doctor prepared a needleful of anesthetic before starting the procedure.
She carefully measured a needleful of insulin for her morning injection.
After drawing a needleful of blood, the nurse labeled the sample for testing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dose - a measured amount of medicine taken at one time
