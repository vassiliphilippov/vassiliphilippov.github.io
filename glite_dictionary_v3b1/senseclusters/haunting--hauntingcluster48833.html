haunting

noun
ghostly presence
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːntɪŋ / (BrE)
a repeated appearance or presence of a ghost or supernatural being in a place

Examples

A ghostly haunting
The abandoned hotel is rumored to have frequent hauntings that scare away visitors.
Locals share stories about the haunting of the old church late at night.
Many believe the strange noises are evidence of a haunting in the house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apparition - a ghost or ghostly figure appearing unexpectedly
  • antonym: possession - the state of being controlled by a spirit or supernatural force
