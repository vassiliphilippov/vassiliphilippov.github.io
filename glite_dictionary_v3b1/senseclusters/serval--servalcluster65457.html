serval

noun
African wildcat with spotted coat
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrvəl / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvəl / (BrE)
a slender, long-legged African wildcat with large, untufted ears and a tawny coat covered in black spots

Examples

A serval cat
At the wildlife reserve, visitors had the chance to observe a serval hunting at dusk.
She watched a documentary about how the serval adapts to life in the African savanna.
The serval relies on its keen hearing to locate prey in tall grass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leopard - a large, spotted big cat found in Africa and Asia
