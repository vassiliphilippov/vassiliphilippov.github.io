unmilled

adjective
without milled edge
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɪld / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɪld / (BrE)
having a smooth edge without milling or reeding, especially referring to coins or metal rims

Examples

Unmilled coin edge
The rare coin was unmilled, making it especially valuable to collectors.
He noticed the edge of the medal was unmilled and completely smooth.
Some older coins are unmilled, lacking the ridges found on modern currency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: milled - having a grooved or reeded edge, especially on coins
  • synonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
