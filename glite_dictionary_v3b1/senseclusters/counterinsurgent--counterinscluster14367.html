counterinsurgent

noun
opponent of rebellion
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərɪnˈsɜrdʒənt / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntərɪnˈsɜːdʒənt / (BrE)
a person or group that acts to oppose or suppress an uprising or rebellion

Examples

A counterinsurgent force
The counterinsurgents launched a campaign to regain control of the occupied towns.
Local counterinsurgents worked closely with the army to restore peace in the region.
Training for counterinsurgents often includes both military and community engagement skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: government forces - official military or police acting against rebels
  • antonym: insurgent - a person who rises in active revolt
