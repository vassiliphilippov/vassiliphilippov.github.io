unbearableness

noun
intolerable quality
7.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɛrəbəlnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɛərəbəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being impossible or extremely difficult to endure or tolerate

Examples

The unbearableness of pain
The unbearableness of the pain forced him to seek medical help immediately.
She could not stand the unbearableness of the constant noise in the city.
During the long, hot summer, the unbearableness of the heat became a daily struggle.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bearableness - the quality of being able to be endured
  • synonym: intolerability - the state of being impossible to tolerate
