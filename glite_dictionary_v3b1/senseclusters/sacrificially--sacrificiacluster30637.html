sacrificially

adverb
in a sacrificial manner
Pronunciation: / ˌsækrəˈfɪʃəli / (AmE), / ˌsækrɪˈfɪʃəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or involves making a sacrifice, especially as part of a religious ceremony or offering.

Examples

Offer sacrificially at altar
The priest placed the offerings sacrificially on the altar during the ancient ritual.
She gave her time sacrificially to help those in need, expecting nothing in return.
The animals were prepared sacrificially according to the traditions of the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: devotedly - in a way that shows strong loyalty or dedication
  • antonym: selfishly - in a way that is concerned only with oneself
