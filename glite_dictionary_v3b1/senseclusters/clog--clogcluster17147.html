clog

verb
to perform a clog dance
Pronunciation: / klɑɡ / (AmE), / klɒɡ / (BrE)
to perform a traditional dance in which the performer wears clogs and makes rhythmic tapping sounds with their feet

Examples

To clog on stage
In the festival, she will clog on stage with the other dancers.
The children learned to clog as part of their school project on folk traditions.
When they clog in unison, the tapping sounds fill the entire hall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clog dance - a traditional dance performed wearing clogs, making rhythmic sounds
