charrette

noun
cart for student work
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈrɛt / (AmE), / ʃəˈrɛt / (BrE)
a cart or wagon, especially one historically used to collect and transport students' drawings and models at art and architecture schools

Examples

Old charrette cart
The old charrette stood in the corner of the art school as a reminder of tradition.
Students hurried to place their final models on the charrette before the deadline.
The museum's exhibit included a restored charrette once used by architecture students.
