cunning

noun
skill in deception or trickery
6.6 3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌ.nɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkʌ.nɪŋ/ (BrE)
the ability to achieve things by being clever, especially by tricking or deceiving people

Examples

The fox is often a symbol of cunning in fables and folklore.
