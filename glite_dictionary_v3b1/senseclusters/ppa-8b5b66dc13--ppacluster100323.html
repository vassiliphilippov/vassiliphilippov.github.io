PPA

noun
finance account type
7.5
Pronunciation: / pi pi eɪ / (AmE), / piː piː eɪ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'pre-payment account' or 'personal payment account', used in finance to refer to a type of account for managing payments

Examples

Bank PPA account
You can transfer money to your PPA using the mobile app.
The company requires all employees to have a PPA for salary payments.
She checked her PPA balance before making the online purchase.
