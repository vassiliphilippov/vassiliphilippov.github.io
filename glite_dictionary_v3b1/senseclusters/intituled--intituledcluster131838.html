intituled

verb
to give a title
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɪtʃuːld / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɪtjuːld / (BrE)
to give a title to something; to name or entitle (archaic).

Examples

The document intituled a book
The document was intituled 'A History of the Kings of England' by its author.
Several poems in the collection are intituled with the names of famous cities.
In the preface, the work is intituled 'Reflections on Human Nature'.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entitle - to give a title to a book, work, or person
