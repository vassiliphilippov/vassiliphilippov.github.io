wonderless

adjective
without wonder
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈwʌn.dər.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈwʌn.də.ləs/ (BrE)
not having or not causing a feeling of wonder, amazement, or curiosity; dull or unremarkable

Examples

A wonderless life
After years of routine, his life felt wonderless and dull.
The children stared at the exhibit with wonderless faces, showing no excitement.
Traveling to new places can help break a wonderless routine.
Despite the hype, the exhibition was rather wonderless and failed to impress the visitors.
Her speech was clear but ultimately wonderless, leaving the audience unmoved.
The landscape outside the city was flat and wonderless, stretching for miles without anything interesting to see.
