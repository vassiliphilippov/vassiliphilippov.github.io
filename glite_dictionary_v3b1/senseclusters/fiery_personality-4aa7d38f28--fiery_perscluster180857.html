fiery personality

noun
intense and passionate temperament
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚi pɚˌsɑːnˈæləti / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəri pəˌsɒnˈaləti / (BrE)
A character or temperament that is intense, passionate, and sometimes quick-tempered.

Examples

A fiery personality
A fiery personality can be both a strength and a challenge in leadership roles.
Despite his fiery personality, he was surprisingly patient with young children.
Her fiery personality inspired her team to achieve great things.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm personality - a character that is composed and not easily disturbed
  • synonym: hot-tempered - easily becoming angry; having a quick temper
