phrasal idiom

noun
type of idiom; meaning not literal
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪzəl ˈɪdiəm / (AmE), / ˈfreɪzəl ˈɪdiəm / (BrE)
an idiomatic expression whose meaning cannot be deduced from the meanings of its individual words

Common phrasal idioms
