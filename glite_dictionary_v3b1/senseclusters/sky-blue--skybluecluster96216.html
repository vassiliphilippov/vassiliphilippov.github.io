sky-blue

noun
light shade of blue
Pronunciation: / skaɪ bluː / (AmE), / skaɪ bluː / (BrE)
A light shade of blue, resembling the color of a clear daytime sky.

Examples

A sky-blue paint
He looked up at the endless sky-blue and felt calm.
She chose a dress in a delicate sky-blue for the summer party.
The artist mixed white and blue paint to get the perfect sky-blue for her canvas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: azure - a bright blue color, often likened to the sky
