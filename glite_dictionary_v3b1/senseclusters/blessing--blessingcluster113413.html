blessing

noun
the act of praying for divine help or protection
Pronunciation: / ˈblɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
a prayer or act of asking God or a higher power for help, protection, or favor

Examples

Say a blessing
Many families say a blessing at the start of their evening meal.
She knelt in silence, offering a blessing for her daughter's safety.
The priest gave his blessing before the meal began.
Before dinner, the family paused while their grandmother said the blessing.
He waited until the blessing was finished before starting to eat.
Would you like to say the blessing tonight?
Before the meal, the priest offered a blessing to the gathered family.
The bishop's blessing was sought before beginning the project.
They received a special blessing at their wedding ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benediction - a prayer asking for divine blessing, especially at the end of a religious service
  • antonym: curse - an appeal to a supernatural power for harm to come to someone
  • synonym: grace - a short prayer before a meal
