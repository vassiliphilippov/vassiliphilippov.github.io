downland

adjective
of chalk hills
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈdaʊn.lənd / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of areas of chalk hills, especially in southern England, or found growing in such regions

Examples

Downland scenery
The downland landscape is famous for its rolling green hills and wildflowers.
Many rare butterflies thrive in the unique downland habitats of southern England.
She enjoyed hiking across the downland paths during the spring.
