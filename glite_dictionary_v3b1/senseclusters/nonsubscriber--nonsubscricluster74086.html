nonsubscriber

noun
person not signing
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.səbˈskraɪbər / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.səbˈskraɪbə / (BrE)
a person who does not sign or formally agree to a document, agreement, or pledge

Examples

Nonsubscriber to the agreement
The nonsubscriber was not entitled to any benefits under the agreement.
Only those who signed the petition could vote, so each nonsubscriber was excluded from the process.
In historical contracts, a nonsubscriber often had no legal standing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: signatory - a person who has signed a document or agreement
  • antonym: subscriber - a person who signs or formally agrees to a document or agreement
