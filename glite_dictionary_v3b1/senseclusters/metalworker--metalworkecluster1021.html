metalworker

noun
person who shapes or makes things from metal
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtlˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəlˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to shape or make things from metal, often by hammering or welding

Examples

A metalworker shapes metal
As a metalworker, she creates custom railings and sculptures for local businesses.
Many traditional villages still have a metalworker who makes tools and kitchenware by hand.
The metalworker skillfully hammered the red-hot iron into a horseshoe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blacksmith - a person who makes and repairs things made of iron by hand
