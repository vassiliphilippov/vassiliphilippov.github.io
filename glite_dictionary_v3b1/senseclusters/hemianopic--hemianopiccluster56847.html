hemianopic

adjective
partial vision loss
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːmiəˈnɑpɪk / (AmE), / ˌhiːmiəˈnɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by hemianopia, a condition where half of the visual field is lost in one or both eyes

Examples

Hemianopic visual field
After the accident, the doctor explained that the patient was now hemianopic on the left side.
Special glasses were designed to help people with hemianopic vision navigate safely.
The neurologist recognized the hemianopic symptoms during the initial examination.
