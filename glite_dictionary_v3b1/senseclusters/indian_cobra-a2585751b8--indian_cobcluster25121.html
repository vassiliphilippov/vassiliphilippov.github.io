Indian cobra

noun
venomous snake of tropical Asia, especially India
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈkoʊbrə / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈkəʊbrə / (BrE)
a highly venomous snake native to tropical Asia, especially India, known for its hood and role in traditional culture

Examples

The Indian cobra snake
A snake charmer skillfully handled the Indian cobra during the street performance.
The Indian cobra is often depicted in Indian mythology and folklore.
The bite of an Indian cobra can be fatal if not treated promptly.
