false mistletoe

noun
American plant resembling mistletoe
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɔls ˈmɪsəlˌtoʊ / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈmɪsəlˌtəʊ / (BrE)
Any of several American plants that closely resemble the true (Old World) mistletoe but belong to different families.

Examples

False mistletoe plant
False mistletoe can be mistaken for true mistletoe because of its similar appearance.
Many birds are attracted to the false mistletoe for its berries.
The forest was dotted with clusters of false mistletoe growing on tree branches.
