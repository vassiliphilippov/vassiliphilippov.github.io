metaproterenol

noun
a bronchodilator drug for asthma and lung diseases
7.4
Pronunciation: /met.ə.proʊ.ˈtɜːr.ə.nəl/ (AmE), /met.ə.prəʊ.ˈtɜː.ə.nəl/ (BrE)
