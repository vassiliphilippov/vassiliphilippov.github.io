metaproterenol

noun
a bronchodilator drug for asthma and lung diseases
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəproʊˈtɛrənɔl / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəprəʊˈtɛrənɒl / (BrE)
A bronchodilator drug used to treat asthma, emphysema, and other lung conditions by relaxing airway muscles and improving breathing.

Examples

Inhaled metaproterenol medicine
Patients with chronic bronchitis may benefit from inhaling metaproterenol regularly.
Side effects of metaproterenol can include increased heart rate and trembling.
The doctor prescribed metaproterenol to help control her asthma symptoms.
