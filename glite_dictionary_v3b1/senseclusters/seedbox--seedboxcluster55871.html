seedbox

noun
container for seedlings
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsiːd.bɑːks/ /; / /ˈsiːd.bɔːks/ (AmE), /ˈsiːd.bɒks/ (BrE)
a container or box used for holding, sowing, or storing seeds

Examples

A seedbox for planting
She filled the seedbox with soil and planted tomato seeds.
After a week in the seedbox, the tiny sprouts began to appear.

She kept her collection of rare seeds in a wooden seedbox on the windowsill.
Before planting, he carefully labeled each seedbox to avoid mixing up the varieties.
The gardener opened the old seedbox and found packets from previous years.
