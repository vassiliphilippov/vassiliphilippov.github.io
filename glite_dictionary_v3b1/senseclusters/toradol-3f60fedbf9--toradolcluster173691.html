Toradol

noun
a brand of injectable NSAID pain reliever
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrəˌdɔl / (AmE), / ˈtɔːrəˌdɒl / (BrE)
A brand name for ketorolac, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used for short-term management of moderate to severe pain, typically administered by injection.

Examples

Toradol injection
Athletes sometimes receive Toradol injections to manage inflammation after injuries.
Because of its potency, Toradol is usually given only in a hospital setting.
The doctor administered Toradol to treat her severe postoperative pain.
