blockbusting

noun
unethical real estate tactic
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈblɑːk.bʌs.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈblɒk.bʌs.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
an unethical real estate practice where agents profit by encouraging homeowners to sell quickly by instilling fear of neighborhood decline

Examples

Real estate blockbusting
Laws were enacted to stop blockbusting from destabilizing urban neighborhoods.
The real estate agent was accused of blockbusting after spreading rumors about the area.
Community leaders worked together to educate residents about the dangers of blockbusting.
