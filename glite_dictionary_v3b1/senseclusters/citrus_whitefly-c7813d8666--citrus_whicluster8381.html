citrus whitefly

noun
pest insect of citrus trees
6.6 7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪtrəs ˈwaɪtˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪtrəs ˈwaɪtˌflʌɪ / (BrE)
a small whitefly (Dialeurodes citri) that is a common pest of citrus trees, feeding on their leaves and weakening the plant
