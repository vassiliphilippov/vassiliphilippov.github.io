cowpoke

noun
cattle worker on horseback (esp. US)
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊˌpoʊk / (AmE), / ˈkaʊˌpəʊk / (BrE)
a person, especially in the western United States, who is employed to tend cattle and perform ranch duties, often on horseback

Examples

A cowpoke on horseback
At the rodeo, a skilled cowpoke demonstrated his lasso technique.
The cowpoke rode across the open plain, checking on the herd.
The old cowpoke shared stories of life on the ranch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cowboy - a person who herds and tends cattle, especially in the western US
