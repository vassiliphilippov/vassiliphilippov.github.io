octagon

noun
eight-sided polygon
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɑktəˌɡɑn / (AmE), / ˈɒktəɡən / (BrE)
A flat shape with eight straight sides and eight angles; an eight-sided polygon.

Examples

A octagon shape
In geometry class, we learned how to calculate the area of a regular octagon.
The mixed martial arts fights are held in a cage called the octagon.
The stop sign is shaped like an octagon to help drivers recognize it quickly.
