dagoba

noun
Buddhist shrine
Pronunciation: / dəˈɡoʊbə / (AmE), / dəˈɡəʊbə / (BrE)
a dome-shaped Buddhist shrine or stupa, especially in Sri Lanka, used for worship, meditation, or to house relics
