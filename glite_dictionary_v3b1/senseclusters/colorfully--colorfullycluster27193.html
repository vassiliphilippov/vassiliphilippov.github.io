colorfully

adverb
vividly or expressively
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlɚfəli / (AmE), / ˈkʌləfəli / (BrE)
in a lively, expressive, or vividly descriptive manner, especially in speech or writing

Examples

Colorfully tell a story
She described her childhood adventures colorfully, making everyone laugh with her stories.
The author writes colorfully, bringing each scene to life with rich details.
He spoke colorfully about his travels, using expressive language and gestures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dully - in a boring or uninteresting way
  • synonym: vividly - in a way that is very clear, bright, or detailed
