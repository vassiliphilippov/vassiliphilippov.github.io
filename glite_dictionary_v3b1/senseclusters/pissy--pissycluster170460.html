pissy

adjective
irritable or annoyed
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪsi / (AmE), / ˈpɪsi / (BrE)
easily annoyed, irritable, or in a bad mood, often in a petty or unpleasant way

Examples

A pissy mood
She gets a bit pissy when she hasn't had her morning coffee.
After waiting in line for an hour, he became noticeably pissy with the staff.
Try not to be so pissy about small mistakes; everyone makes them sometimes.
