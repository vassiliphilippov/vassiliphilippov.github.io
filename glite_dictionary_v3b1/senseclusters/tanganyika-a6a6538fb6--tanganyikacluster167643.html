Tanganyika

proper noun
large lake in central Africa
Pronunciation: / ˌtæŋɡəˈniːjə / (AmE), / ˌtæŋɡəˈniːjə / (BrE)
a large lake in central Africa, located in the Great Rift Valley between Tanzania and the Democratic Republic of the Congo; the longest freshwater lake in the world

Examples

Tanganyika lake
Fishermen along the shores of Tanganyika rely on the lake for their livelihoods.
Lake Tanganyika is known for its incredible biodiversity and depth.
The expedition studied the unique species found only in Tanganyika.
