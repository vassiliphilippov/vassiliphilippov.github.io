Spiranthes cernua

noun
a North American vanilla-scented orchid
Pronunciation: / spaɪˈrænθiːz ˈsɜrnjuə / (AmE), / spaɪˈrænθiːz ˈsɜːnjuə / (BrE)
A North American orchid with tall, erect, densely flowered spiraling clusters of creamy white, vanilla-scented flowers, commonly found in damp areas in the east and central regions.
