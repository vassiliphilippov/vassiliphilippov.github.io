photo opportunity

noun
occasion arranged for publicity photos
5.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.toʊ ɑː.pər.ˈtuː.nɪ.tɪ/ /; / /ˈfoʊ.toʊ ɑː.pər.ˈtjuː.nɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.təʊ ɒp.ə.ˈtjuː.nɪ.ti/ /; / /ˈfəʊ.təʊ ɒp.ə.ˈtuː.nɪ.ti/ (BrE)
