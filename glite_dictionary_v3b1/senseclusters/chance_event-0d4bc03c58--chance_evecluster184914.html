chance event

noun
unexpected or accidental event
Pronunciation: / tʃæns ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / tʃɑːns ɪˈvent / (BrE)
an event that happens unexpectedly or by accident, without an obvious cause

Examples

A chance event happens
Sometimes a chance event can completely change the direction of your life.
The discovery of penicillin was a chance event that led to major advances in medicine.
The meeting was not planned; it was a chance event that surprised both of us.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accident - an unfortunate event that happens unexpectedly
  • synonym: fortuity - a chance occurrence or accident
