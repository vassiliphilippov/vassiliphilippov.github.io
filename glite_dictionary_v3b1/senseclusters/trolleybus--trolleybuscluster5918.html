trolleybus

noun
electric bus drawing power from overhead wires
3.2 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtrɑː.lɪ.bʌs/ (AmE), /ˈtrɒ.lɪ.bʌs/ (BrE)
a road vehicle for carrying passengers, powered by electricity from overhead wires rather than an onboard engine

Examples

A trolleybus vehicle
A trolleybus glided silently down the street, powered by its network of overhead wires.
The city has invested in a new fleet of trolleybuses to reduce air pollution.
Unlike diesel buses, a trolleybus produces no exhaust emissions at the point of use.
