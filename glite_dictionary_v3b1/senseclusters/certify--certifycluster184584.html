certify

verb
guarantee as meeting a standard
4.6 3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.tə.faɪ /; / ˈsɝː.t̬əˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.tɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
to officially state or guarantee that something or someone meets certain standards or is authorized

Examples

To certify a building
Only a licensed inspector can certify the building as earthquake-resistant.
The company can certify that all their electronics meet safety standards.
This product is certified organic by an independent agency.
Only a trained inspector can certify that the building is safe for occupation.
She was certified to teach in three different states.
The agency will certify your documents for official use abroad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authorize - to give official permission for something
  • synonym: license - to give permission officially, especially by regulation
  • antonym: revoke - to officially cancel the validity or permission previously granted
  • synonym: verify - to make sure or demonstrate that something is true or accurate
