ergonomically

adverb
for comfort and efficiency
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɜrɡəˈnɑmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɜːɡəˈnɒmɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner designed to maximize comfort, efficiency, and safety for people using equipment or working in a space

Examples

Designed ergonomically
The new keyboard is designed ergonomically to reduce strain on your wrists.
Factory workers benefit when their tools are arranged ergonomically for easier access.
She set up her home office ergonomically to prevent back and neck pain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awkwardly - in a way that is uncomfortable or difficult to use
  • synonym: comfortably - in a way that provides physical ease or relaxation
  • synonym: efficiently - in a way that achieves maximum productivity with minimum wasted effort
