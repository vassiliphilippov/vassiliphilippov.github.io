antiseptically

adverb
in a cold manner
Pronunciation: / ˌæntɪˈsɛptɪkli / (AmE), / ˌæntɪˈsɛptɪkli / (BrE)
in a detached, clinical, or emotionally cold manner, without warmth or human feeling

Examples

Speak antiseptically
She spoke antiseptically about the accident, avoiding any mention of the victims' emotions.
The report was written antiseptically, listing facts without any personal opinions.
He described the events antiseptically, focusing only on statistics and procedures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clinically - in a way that is unemotional or detached
  • antonym: emotionally - in a way that shows strong feelings
