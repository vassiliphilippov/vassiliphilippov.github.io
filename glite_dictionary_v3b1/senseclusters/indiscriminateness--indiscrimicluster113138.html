indiscriminateness

noun
lack of distinction
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnətnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnətnəs / (BrE)

The indiscriminateness of the selection process made it seem unfair to many applicants.
