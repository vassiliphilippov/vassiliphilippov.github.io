screecher

noun
person who screams or shouts loudly
Pronunciation: / skriːtʃər / (AmE), / skriːtʃə / (BrE)
a person who speaks or cries out in a very loud, high-pitched voice

Examples

Loud screecher voice
Children sometimes become screechers when they're playing outside.
No one wanted to sit near the screecher during the concert.
The angry screecher could be heard from the other side of the building.
