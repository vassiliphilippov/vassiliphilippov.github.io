circuital

adjective
about a circuit
Pronunciation: / sɚˈkjuːɾəl / (AmE), / səˈkjuːɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving a circuit, especially in the context of electricity or magnetism

Examples

Circuital current
The engineer explained the circuital path of the electric current in the device.
A circuital magnetic field is produced when current flows through the wire loop.
Understanding circuital systems is essential for students studying electronics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circuitous - having a roundabout or indirect path
  • antonym: linear - arranged in or extending along a straight line
