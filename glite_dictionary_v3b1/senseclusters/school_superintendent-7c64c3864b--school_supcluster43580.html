school superintendent

noun
head of a school system or district
4.0
Pronunciation: / skuːl ˌsuːpərɪnˈtɛndənt / (AmE), / skuːl ˌsuːpərɪnˈtɛndənt / (BrE)
the person who oversees and manages the operations of a school system or district

Examples

School superintendent manages district
After twenty years as a teacher, she was promoted to school superintendent.
The school superintendent announced new policies to improve classroom safety.
The school superintendent is responsible for managing all schools in the district.
