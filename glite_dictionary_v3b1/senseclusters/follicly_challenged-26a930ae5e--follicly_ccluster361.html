follicly challenged

adjective
humorous term for bald or balding
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑː.lɪ.kli ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (AmE), / ˈfɒ.lɪ.kli ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (BrE)
