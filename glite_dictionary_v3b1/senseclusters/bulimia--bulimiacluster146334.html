bulimia

noun
an eating disorder involving binge eating and compensatory behaviors

a serious eating disorder characterized by episodes of excessive eating (binges) followed by attempts to avoid weight gain, such as vomiting, fasting, or excessive exercise

Examples

Eating disorder bulimia
Her struggle with bulimia began in her late teens, affecting her physical and emotional health.

Synonyms

  • bulimia nervosa
    5.8 0.22/m
    bulimia nervosa is the formal medical name doctors use in reports and clinics, while bulimia is the general everyday term.
  • binge-purge syndrome
    7.4 0.00/m
    binge-purge syndrome describes repeated bingeing followed by purging in clinical terms, while bulimia is the common disorder name.
  • binge-vomit syndrome
    9.9 0.00/m
    binge-vomit syndrome stresses cycles of eating then self-induced vomiting, whereas bulimia covers that behavior and other purge methods.
  • bulimarexia
    9.9 0.00/m
    bulimarexia describes alternating overeating and food aversion like anorexia, while bulimia focuses on bingeing followed by purging to avoid weight gain.
