bullishly

adverb
forcefully or aggressively
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈbʊlɪʃli / (BrE)
in a forceful, aggressive, or domineering manner, like a bull

Examples

To act bullishly in meetings
He bullishly insisted on his plan despite everyone else's concerns.
The CEO spoke bullishly about the company's future during the press conference.
She bullishly pushed her ideas forward, not allowing others to interrupt.
