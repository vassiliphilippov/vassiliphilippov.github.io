cosplay as

verb
dress up as a fictional character
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑsˌpleɪ æz / (AmE), / ˈkɒsˌpleɪ æz / (BrE)
to dress up in costume as a specific fictional character, especially from a film, book, video game, or comic, often as part of fan culture or at conventions

Examples

To cosplay as a hero

Hundreds of participants cosplayed as characters from the popular anime series at the expo.
They decided to cosplay as classic video game icons for the themed party.
