nonmagnetic

adjective
not able to be magnetized
Pronunciation: / nɑn.mæɡˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / nɒn.mæɡˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
Not capable of being magnetized or attracted by a magnet.

Examples

A nonmagnetic material
Because the device was nonmagnetic, it could safely be used near MRI machines.
Many coins are made from nonmagnetic metals such as copper or aluminum.
Stainless steel is often nonmagnetic, making it useful in medical instruments.
