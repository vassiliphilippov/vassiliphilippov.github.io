Synonyms

  • death penalty
    4.1 14/m
    death penalty is the legal sentence of death for serious crimes, while execution is the actual act of carrying that sentence out.
  • capital punishment
    4.2 2/m
    capital punishment is the formal legal term for death ordered by the state, while execution is the physical act of carrying out that sentence.
