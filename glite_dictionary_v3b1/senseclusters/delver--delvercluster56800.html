delver

noun
deep investigator
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdel.vər/ (AmE), /ˈdel.və/ (BrE)
a person who investigates or explores a subject deeply and thoroughly, especially in research or study

Examples

A delver into books
As a literary delver, she spent years tracing obscure sources for her biography.
The journalist was known as a persistent delver into political scandals.
Every great scientist is a delver who seeks answers beyond the obvious.

Related Terms
