delver

noun
deep investigator
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛlvər / (AmE), / ˈdɛlvə / (BrE)
a person who investigates or explores a subject deeply and thoroughly, especially in research or study

Examples

A delver into books
As a literary delver, she spent years tracing obscure sources for her biography.
The journalist was known as a persistent delver into political scandals.
Every great scientist is a delver who seeks answers beyond the obvious.

Related Terms

  • synonym: explorer - a person who investigates unknown areas or subjects
  • synonym: researcher - a person who studies a subject in detail to discover new information
  • antonym: superficialist - a person who only examines things on the surface
