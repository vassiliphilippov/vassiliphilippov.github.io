musculoskeletal

adjective
of muscles and skeleton
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌs.koʊˈskɛl.ɪ.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˌmʌs.kjʊ.ləʊˈskɛl.ɪ.tl̩ / (BrE)
Relating to or affecting both the muscles and the skeleton.

Examples

Musculoskeletal system
Injuries to the musculoskeletal system are common among athletes.
The patient was referred to a specialist for musculoskeletal pain affecting his back and legs.
Yoga can improve musculoskeletal health by strengthening both bones and muscles.
