transactionally

adverb
in an exchange-focused way
8.4
Pronunciation: / trænˈzækʃənəli / (AmE), / trænˈzækʃənəli / (BrE)
in a manner that treats interactions or relationships as exchanges for practical or material benefit rather than emotional connection

Examples

Transactionally approach deals
He approached every business deal transactionally, focusing only on what he could gain.
Some people interact transactionally, always expecting something in return for their help.
The manager handled employee requests transactionally, rarely considering personal circumstances.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotionally - in a way that is based on feelings or emotions
  • synonym: pragmatically - in a practical or realistic way
