Loch Ness monster

noun
legendary creature of Loch Ness, Scotland
8.0
Pronunciation: / lɑk nɛs ˈmɑnstɚ / (AmE), / lɒx nɛs ˈmɒnstə / (BrE)
a large, legendary aquatic creature said to inhabit Loch Ness in Scotland, often depicted as resembling a plesiosaur or serpent
