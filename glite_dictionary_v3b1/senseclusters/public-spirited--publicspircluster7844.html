Showing an unselfish concern for the well-being and interests of the community or society as a whole.

Examples

A public-spirited volunteer
A public-spirited group of volunteers organized the community clean-up event.
His public-spirited actions inspired others to get involved in local issues.
The mayor is known for her public-spirited leadership and commitment to city improvements.

Synonyms

  • altruistic
    6.7 2.16/m
    altruistic shows a moral, often strong concern for other people's welfare, while public-spirited focuses on helping the wider community.
  • selfless
    3.2 3.01/m
    selfless describes giving up personal needs to help individuals, while public-spirited describes caring for the community or public interest.
  • unselfish
    2.0 0.91/m
    unselfish means willing to put others first in small or everyday situations, while public-spirited means acting for society's good.

Related Terms
