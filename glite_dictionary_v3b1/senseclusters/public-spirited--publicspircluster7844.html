Synonyms

  • altruistic
    6.7 2/m
    altruistic shows a moral, often strong concern for other people's welfare, while public-spirited focuses on helping the wider community.
  • selfless
    3.2 3/m
    selfless describes giving up personal needs to help individuals, while public-spirited describes caring for the community or public interest.
  • unselfish
    2.0 1/m
    unselfish means willing to put others first in small or everyday situations, while public-spirited means acting for society's good.
