public-spirited

adjective
concerned with public welfare
4.8 3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpʌb.lɪk ˈspɪr.ɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ˈpʌb.lɪk ˈspɪr.ɪ.təd/ (AmE), /ˈpʌb.lɪk ˈspɪr.ɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ˈpʌb.lɪk ˈspɪr.ɪ.təd/ (BrE)
