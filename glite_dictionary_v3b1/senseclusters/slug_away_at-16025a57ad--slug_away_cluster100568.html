slug away at

verb
work hard and persistently at something
Pronunciation: / slʌɡ əˈweɪ æt / (AmE), / slʌɡ əˈweɪ at / (BrE)
to work hard and persistently at a task, especially one that is difficult or lengthy

Examples

To slug away at work
Despite the pouring rain, the team kept slugging away at the construction site until sunset.
I've been slugging away at this report all weekend, but it's still not done.
She slugged away at her thesis for months before finally finishing it.
