act of kindness

noun
a generous or helpful deed
1.0
Pronunciation: / ækt əv ˈkaɪndnəs / (AmE), / ækt əv ˈkaɪndnəs / (BrE)
a generous or considerate action intended to help or benefit someone

Examples

An act of kindness
She performed an act of kindness by helping her elderly neighbor carry groceries.
Sometimes a simple act of kindness can brighten someone’s entire day.
The world would be a better place if everyone did at least one act of kindness daily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charity - the voluntary giving of help to those in need
  • synonym: good deed - an action that is morally good or beneficial
