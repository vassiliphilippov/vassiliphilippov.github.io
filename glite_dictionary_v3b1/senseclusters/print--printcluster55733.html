print

noun
photograph made from a negative
Pronunciation: / prɪnt / (AmE), / prɪnt / (BrE)
A photograph produced on paper from a photographic negative or digital image.

Examples

A photo print
He collects vintage prints from the early days of photography.
She carefully placed the print of her grandfather on the mantelpiece.
The prints from last weekend's photo shoot arrived today.
