Charcot-Marie-Tooth disease

noun
hereditary nerve disorder causing muscle weakness
7.0
Pronunciation: /ʃɑːr.ˈkoʊ mə.ˈriː tuːθ dɪ.ˈziːz/ /; / /ʃɑːr.ˈkoʊ mə.ˈriː tʊθ dɪ.ˈziːz/ (AmE), /ʃɑː.ˈkəʊ mə.ˈriː tuːθ dɪ.ˈziːz/ /; / /ʃɑː.ˈkəʊ mə.ˈriː tʊθ dɪ.ˈziːz/ (BrE)
a hereditary neurological disorder that causes progressive weakness and wasting of the muscles in the hands and lower legs, often starting in childhood or young adulthood
