Carcharhinus plumbeus

noun
a species of gray shark (sandbar shark)
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑrkəˈraɪnəs ˈplʌmbiəs / (AmE), / ˌkɑːkəˈraɪnəs ˈplʌmbiːəs / (BrE)
A species of large, gray shark commonly found along the coasts of the middle Atlantic states of the US, known as the sandbar shark.
