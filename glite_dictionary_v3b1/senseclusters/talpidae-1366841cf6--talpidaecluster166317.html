Talpidae

noun
family of burrowing mammals (moles)
9.9
Pronunciation: / tælˈpɪdiː / (AmE), / talˈpɪdɪi / (BrE)
A family of small, burrowing mammals known as moles, characterized by their cylindrical bodies, velvety fur, and spade-like forelimbs.
