negative

adjective
not showing disease or condition (medicine, science)
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛɡətɪv / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡətɪv / (BrE)
(of a medical test or scientific result) not showing the presence of microorganisms, disease, or a specific condition.

Examples

Negative test result
His COVID-19 test result was negative, much to his relief.
Several screenings were performed, all yielding negative results.
The blood culture came back negative for any bacterial infection.

Related Terms

  • antonym: positive - showing the presence of disease or a specific condition (medicine, science)
