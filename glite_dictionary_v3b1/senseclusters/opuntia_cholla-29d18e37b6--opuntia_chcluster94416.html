Opuntia cholla

noun
a spiny cactus species
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈpʌn.ti.ə ˈtʃoʊ.jə / (AmE), / əˈpʌn.tɪ.ə ˈtʃɒl.ə / (BrE)
A species of arborescent cactus with very spiny, cylindrical stem segments, native to the southwestern United States and Mexico.

Examples

The Opuntia cholla cactus
Many hikers avoid Opuntia cholla due to its extremely sharp spines.
Several desert animals use the Opuntia cholla for both shade and protection.
The Opuntia cholla is well adapted to the arid climate of the desert southwest.
