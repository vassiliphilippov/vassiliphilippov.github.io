no-life attitude

noun
lifestyle lacking social activities or interests
7.4 7.2
Pronunciation: / noʊ laɪf ˈætɪtjud / (AmE), / nəʊ laɪf ˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
an outlook or way of living characterized by little or no engagement in social activities or interests

Examples

A no-life attitude problem
His no-life attitude made it hard for him to connect with new people at college.
She was often criticized for her no-life attitude and reluctance to join group events.
You won't make many friends if you keep this no-life attitude year after year.
