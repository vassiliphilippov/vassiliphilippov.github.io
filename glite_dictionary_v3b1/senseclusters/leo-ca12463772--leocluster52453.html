Leo

noun
zodiac constellation between Cancer and Virgo
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈliː.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈliː.əʊ / (BrE)
a prominent zodiac constellation in the Northern Hemisphere, situated between Cancer and Virgo; also the astrological sign associated with people born between July 23 and August 22.

Examples

Leo constellation
Many amateur astronomers enjoy viewing Leo in the night sky during spring.
People born under Leo are said to be confident and outgoing.
The star Regulus marks the heart of the constellation Leo.
