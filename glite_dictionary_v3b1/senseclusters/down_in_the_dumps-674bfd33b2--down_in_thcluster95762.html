down in the dumps

adjective
feeling sad or depressed
Pronunciation: / daʊn ɪn ðə dʌmps / (AmE), / daʊn ɪn ðə dʌmps / (BrE)
feeling sad or depressed; in a low mood

Examples

Down in the dumps feeling
After hearing the bad news, he was down in the dumps for days.
She tried to cheer her friend up when she seemed down in the dumps.
Whenever I'm down in the dumps, I listen to upbeat music to lift my mood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - feeling happy and positive
  • synonym: depressed - feeling very unhappy and without hope
