chuckler

noun
person who laughs softly
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌklər / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌklə / (BrE)
a person who laughs quietly or softly, often in response to something amusing

Examples

A chuckler laughs softly
The chuckler in the back row found every joke amusing.
At the movie, a quiet chuckler could be heard during the funny scenes.
Even in serious meetings, he was known as the office chuckler.

Related Terms

  • synonym: giggler - a person who laughs in a light, silly way
  • synonym: laugher - a person who laughs
