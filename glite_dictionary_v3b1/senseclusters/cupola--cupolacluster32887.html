cupola

noun
vertical furnace for melting iron
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːpələ / (AmE), / ˈkjuːpələ / (BrE)
a vertical cylindrical furnace used for melting iron in foundries, typically for casting

Examples

Iron-melting cupola
