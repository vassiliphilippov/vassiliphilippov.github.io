Ornithorhynchus anatinus

noun
scientific name for the platypus
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrnɪθoʊˈrɪŋkəs ˌænəˈtaɪnəs / (AmE), / ˌɔːnɪθəˈrɪŋkəs ˌanəˈtʌɪnəs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the platypus, a small aquatic monotreme native to Australia and Tasmania, known for its broad bill, webbed feet, and dense fur.
