tarnishment

noun
loss of respect or shine
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑrnɪʃmənt / (AmE), / ˈtɑːnɪʃmənt / (BrE)
the process or result of becoming less respected or losing brightness or value

Examples

Tarnishment of reputation
The scandal led to the tarnishment of the company's image.
Years of neglect resulted in the tarnishment of the silverware.
False rumors can cause the tarnishment of a person's reputation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: damage - harm that reduces value or reputation
  • synonym: disgrace - loss of respect or honor
  • antonym: enhancement - the process of improving or increasing value
