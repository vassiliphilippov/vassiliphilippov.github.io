perverseness

noun
deliberate stubborn resistance
7.5
Pronunciation: / pərˈvɜrsnəs / (AmE), / pəˈvɜːsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of deliberately acting in a way that is unreasonable, wrong, or against what is expected or proper

Examples

His perverseness behavior
His perverseness made it nearly impossible for his teachers to discipline him effectively.
She admired his perverseness in refusing to follow the crowd, even when it cost him dearly.
The perverseness of the child's behavior surprised even the most patient of caregivers.
His perverseness was clear when he insisted on breaking every rule just for the sake of it.
Some leaders act out of pure perverseness, ignoring the advice that would help their communities.
The perverseness of his attitude shocked even his closest friends.

  • synonym: perversity - the state or quality of being perverse
