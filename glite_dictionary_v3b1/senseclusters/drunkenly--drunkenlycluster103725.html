drunkenly

adverb
in a drunken manner
1.4 4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌŋkənli / (AmE), / ˈdrʌŋkənli / (BrE)
In a way that shows the effects of having consumed too much alcohol; in a drunken manner.

Examples

To drunkenly stagger
He staggered drunkenly down the street after the party.
She laughed drunkenly, oblivious to the stares of others around her.
The man drunkenly tried to find his keys but dropped them twice.
