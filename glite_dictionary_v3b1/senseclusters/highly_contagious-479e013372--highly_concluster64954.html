highly contagious

adjective
very easily spread (disease)
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪli kənˈteɪdʒəs / (AmE), / ˈhaɪli kənˈteɪdʒəs / (BrE)
(of a disease or infection) very easily spread from one person to another.

Examples

Highly contagious disease
During the outbreak, several highly contagious illnesses spread rapidly through the community.
Measles is considered a highly contagious virus, especially in unvaccinated populations.
The hospital implemented strict protocols to contain the highly contagious infection.
