staple

adjective
regularly used or essential, especially for food or goods
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈsteɪpəl / (BrE)
(of food or commodities) regularly used, important, or forming a principal part of something.

Examples

Staple crops
Corn has been a staple crop for centuries in that region.
Milk is considered a staple food in many households around the world.
Rice is a staple ingredient in many Asian cuisines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: essential - absolutely necessary; extremely important
  • synonym: main - most important; principal
  • antonym: minor - less important or not essential
