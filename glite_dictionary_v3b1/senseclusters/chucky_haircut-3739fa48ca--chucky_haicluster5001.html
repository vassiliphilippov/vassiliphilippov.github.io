chucky haircut

noun
uneven or messy haircut
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌki ˈhɛrˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌki ˈheəkʌt / (BrE)
A haircut that appears uneven, messy, or roughly cut, often lacking professional finish.
