microgreen

noun
young edible plant
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌɡrin / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌɡriːn / (BrE)
a very young, edible plant harvested just after the first leaves appear, often used as a garnish or in salads

Examples

Fresh microgreens salad
The chef decorated the plate with colorful microgreens for a fresh look.
You can grow microgreens at home on a sunny windowsill in just a few days.
Salads made with microgreens are packed with flavor and nutrients.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mature plant - a fully grown plant
  • synonym: seedling - a young plant grown from a seed
  • synonym: sprout - a young shoot from a germinating seed
