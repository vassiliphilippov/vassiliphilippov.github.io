to a lower place

adverb
to a place that is lower
1.0
Pronunciation: / tu ə ˈloʊɚ pleɪs / (AmE), / tuː ə ˈləʊə pleɪs / (BrE)
in or to a place that is lower in position or level.

Examples

Carry to a lower place
She carried the box to a lower place so it would be easier to reach.
The books fell to a lower place on the shelf during the earthquake.
We decided to move the picnic to a lower place near the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: below - at a lower level or position
  • synonym: downstairs - at or to a lower floor of a building
  • antonym: upstairs - at or to a higher floor of a building
