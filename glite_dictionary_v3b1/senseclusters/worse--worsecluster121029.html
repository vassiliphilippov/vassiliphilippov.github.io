worse

adverb
less effectively or successfully
4.1
Pronunciation: / wɝs / (AmE), / wɜːs / (BrE)
In a less effective, successful, or desirable manner; to a greater extent of severity or badness.

Examples

To do worse
After skipping practice, she performed worse in the final match than before.
His cold got worse after he went out in the rain.
The new version of the app works even worse than the previous one.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: badly - in a bad or unsatisfactory manner
  • antonym: better - in a more effective or desirable manner
