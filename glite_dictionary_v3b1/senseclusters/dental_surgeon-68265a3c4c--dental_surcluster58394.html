dental surgeon

noun
dentist qualified for oral surgery
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛntəl ˈsɝːdʒən / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl ˈsɜːdʒən / (BrE)
A dentist who is specially qualified to perform surgical procedures on the mouth, teeth, jaws, and related structures.

Examples

Dental surgeon performs surgery
If you need a complex extraction, a dental surgeon will perform the procedure.
She became a dental surgeon after several years of specialized training.
The dental surgeon recommended removing my impacted wisdom tooth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dentist - a person qualified to treat diseases and conditions that affect the teeth and gums
