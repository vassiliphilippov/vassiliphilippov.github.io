uncrusted

adjective
without a crust
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrʌstɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrʌstɪd / (BrE)
not having a hard outer layer or crust

Examples

Uncrusted bread slice
She preferred her bread uncrusted for a softer texture.
The chef served an uncrusted cheesecake that was creamy and smooth.
Children often like their sandwiches uncrusted for easier eating.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crusted - having a hard outer layer or crust
  • synonym: crustless - without a crust or hard outer layer
