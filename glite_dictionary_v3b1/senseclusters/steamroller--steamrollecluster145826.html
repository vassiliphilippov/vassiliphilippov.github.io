steamroller

verb
flatten with a steamroller
5.2 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàstiÀêmÀåro äl…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàstiÀêmÀår…ô äl…ô / (BrE)
to level or flatten (a surface) using a steamroller; to make smooth by passing a heavy, rolling machine over it

Examples
