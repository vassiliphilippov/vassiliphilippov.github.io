steamroller

verb
flatten with a steamroller
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːmˌroʊlər / (AmE), / ˈstiːmˌrəʊlə / (BrE)
to level or flatten (a surface) using a steamroller; to make smooth by passing a heavy, rolling machine over it

Examples

To steamroller the ground
The construction crew steamrollered the site until it was perfectly flat.
The workers steamrollered the gravel to create a smooth road surface.
They will steamroller the new playground area before laying the asphalt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flatten - to make or become flat or flatter
  • synonym: pave - to cover (a surface) with a hard material
