savory

noun
aromatic herb used in cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvəri /; / ˈseɪ.vəri /; / ˈseɪ.vɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈseɪvəri /; / ˈseɪ.vəri /; / ˈseɪ.vər.i / (BrE)
Either of two aromatic herbs of the genus Satureja (mint family), used in cooking for their spicy flavor.
