savory

noun
aromatic herb used in cooking
7.5 6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvəri /; / ˈseɪ.vəri /; / ˈseɪ.vɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈseɪvəri /; / ˈseɪ.vəri /; / ˈseɪ.vər.i / (BrE)
Either of two aromatic herbs of the genus Satureja (mint family), used in cooking for their spicy flavor.

Examples

Fresh savory herb
Gardeners often grow savory alongside other herbs in their kitchen gardens.

Winter savory has a stronger taste than its summer counterpart.
Savory is often added to bean dishes to enhance their flavor.
She planted savory alongside thyme and rosemary in her herb garden.

Savory is a key ingredient in many Mediterranean dishes.
She grows both summer and winter savory in her herb garden.
The chef added fresh savory to the stew for extra flavor.
