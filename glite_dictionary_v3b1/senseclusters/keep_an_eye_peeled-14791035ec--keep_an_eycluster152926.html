keep an eye peeled

verb
be alert; watch carefully
Pronunciation: / kip ən aɪ pild / (AmE), / kiːp ən aɪ piːld / (BrE)
to be alert and watchful, especially for something specific

Examples

To keep an eye peeled for friends
I'll keep an eye peeled for your friend at the entrance.
Tourists are advised to keep an eye peeled for pickpockets in crowded areas.
When you're walking alone at night, make sure to keep an eye peeled for any unusual activity.
