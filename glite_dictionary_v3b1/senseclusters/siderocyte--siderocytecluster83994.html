siderocyte

noun
abnormal red blood cell with iron granules
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪd.ər.oʊ.saɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪd.ərəʊ.saɪt / (BrE)
an abnormal red blood cell that contains granules of iron not bound in hemoglobin

Examples

Abnormal siderocytes in blood
A blood smear revealed numerous siderocytes in the patient's sample.
Hematologists study siderocytes to diagnose certain types of anemia.
The presence of siderocytes can indicate a disturbance in iron metabolism.
