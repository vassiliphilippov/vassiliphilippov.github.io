fantail

noun
fan-shaped tail
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈfæn.teɪl/ (AmE), /ˈfæn.teɪl/ (BrE)

Examples

Peacock's fantail

The goldfish with a fantail is popular in home aquariums.
