fantail

noun
fan-shaped tail
Pronunciation: / ˈfænˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈfænˌteɪl / (BrE)
a bird, animal, or object with a tail or part that spreads out like a fan

Examples

Peacock's fantail
The bird's fantail spread wide as it tried to attract a mate.
Some old trains had a fantail at the back for improved airflow.
The goldfish with a fantail is popular in home aquariums.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peacock - a large bird known for its colorful, fan-shaped tail
  • synonym: swallow - a small bird, some species of which have a tail that spreads like a fan
