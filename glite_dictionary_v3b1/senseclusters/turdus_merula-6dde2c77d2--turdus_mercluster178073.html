Turdus merula

noun
scientific name for the common blackbird
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.dəs məˈruː.lə / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.dəs mɛˈruː.lə / (BrE)
The scientific name for the common blackbird, a widespread European thrush with all-black plumage in males and brownish tones in females.
