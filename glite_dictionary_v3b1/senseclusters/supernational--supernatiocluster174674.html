supernational

adjective
beyond national authority
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈnæʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈnæʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or extending beyond the authority or interests of individual nations

Examples

Supernational organization
The United Nations is considered a supernational body that addresses global issues.
A supernational agreement was reached to regulate trade across several continents.
Some critics argue that supernational organizations can undermine national sovereignty.

Related Terms

  • synonym: international - involving two or more countries
  • antonym: national - relating to a single nation
