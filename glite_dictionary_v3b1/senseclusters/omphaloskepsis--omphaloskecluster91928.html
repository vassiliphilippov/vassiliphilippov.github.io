omphaloskepsis

noun
contemplation of one's navel; self-absorption
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɑmfəloʊˈskɛpsɪs / (AmE), / ˌɒmfələʊˈskɛpsɪs / (BrE)
The act of contemplating one's navel, especially as an aid to meditation; also used figuratively to mean excessive self-absorption or self-reflection.
