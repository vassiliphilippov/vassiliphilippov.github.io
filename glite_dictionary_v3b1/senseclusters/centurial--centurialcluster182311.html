centurial

adjective
of Roman centuria
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈtjʊəriəl / (AmE), / sɛnˈtjʊəriəl / (BrE)
relating to a centurion or to a centuria, a Roman military unit of about one hundred soldiers

Examples

Centurial shield
The archaeologist discovered a centurial shield during the excavation of the Roman camp.
A centurial standard was carried at the front of the formation by the Roman soldiers.
The historian gave a lecture on the centurial structure of the ancient Roman army.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centurion - an officer in the Roman army commanding a centuria
  • synonym: military - relating to soldiers or armed forces
