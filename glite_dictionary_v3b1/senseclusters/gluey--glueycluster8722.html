gluey

adjective
sticky or adhesive
Pronunciation: / ˈɡluː.i / (AmE), / ˈɡluː.i / (BrE)
having a sticky or adhesive quality, like glue

Examples

A gluey surface
After the rain, the path became gluey and hard to walk on.
I don't like gluey sauces on my pasta.
The mixture turned gluey after simmering for too long.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sticky - having the property of adhering or sticking to a surface
