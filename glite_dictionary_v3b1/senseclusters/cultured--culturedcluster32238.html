cultured

adjective
refined in taste and manners
1.0 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌl.tʃərd/ (AmE), /ˈkʌl.tʃəd/ (BrE)
having or showing good taste, manners, and education; refined and polite in behavior or interests

Examples

Cultured taste in music
A cultured person will appreciate art, literature, and music from around the world.
Her cultured accent revealed years of careful language study and travel abroad.
They hosted the dinner party in a cultured atmosphere, complete with classical music and fine china.

  • synonym: genteel - polite and refined in an affected or exaggerated way
  • antonym: uncultured - lacking good taste, manners, or education; unrefined
