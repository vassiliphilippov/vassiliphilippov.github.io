overreliant

adjective
excessively dependent
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪˈlaɪənt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪˈlaɪənt / (BrE)
depending too much on someone or something, often to an unhealthy or excessive degree

Examples

Be overreliant on help
Many students become overreliant on online resources instead of developing their own study skills.
The company was overreliant on a single supplier, which caused problems when deliveries were delayed.
Parents worry that children are overreliant on technology for entertainment and learning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dependent - relying on someone or something for support
  • antonym: self-sufficient - able to provide for oneself without help from others
