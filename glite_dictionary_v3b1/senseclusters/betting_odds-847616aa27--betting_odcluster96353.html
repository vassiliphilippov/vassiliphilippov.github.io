betting odds

noun
ratio expressing returns in gambling
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛtɪŋ ɑdz / (AmE), / ˈbɛtɪŋ ɒdz / (BrE)
the ratio representing the likelihood of a particular outcome used in gambling, indicating how much a bettor stands to win relative to their stake

Examples

Betting odds ratio
Bookmakers posted betting odds of 3 to 1 on the underdog team.
She checked the betting odds before placing her wager on the horse race.
The betting odds for the favorite shifted dramatically after news of the injury.
