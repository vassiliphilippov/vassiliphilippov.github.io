Venus's flytrap

noun
a carnivorous plant with snapping leaves
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈviːnəs ɪz ˈflaɪˌtræp /; / ˈviːnəsɪz ˈflaɪˌtræp / (AmE), / ˈviːnəs ɪz ˈflaɪˌtræp /; / ˈviːnəsɪz ˈflaɪˌtræp / (BrE)
a small carnivorous plant native to the Carolinas, with hinged leaves that snap shut to trap insects for food
