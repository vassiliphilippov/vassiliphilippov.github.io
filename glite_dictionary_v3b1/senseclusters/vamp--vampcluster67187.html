vamp

verb
provide a shoe with a new vamp
Pronunciation: / væmp / (AmE), / væmp / (BrE)
to provide a shoe with a new vamp (the upper part covering the instep and toes)

Examples

To vamp a shoe
If you want to restore these boots, you'll need to vamp them rather than just clean them.
Some old-fashioned shoe styles are best preserved by vamping instead of replacing the whole shoe.
The cobbler offered to vamp my worn-out shoes with new leather fronts.
