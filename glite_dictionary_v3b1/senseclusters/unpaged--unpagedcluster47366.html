unpaged

adjective
not swapped to disk
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpeɪdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpeɪdʒd / (BrE)
not subject to virtual-memory paging; kept in physical memory and not swapped to disk.

Examples

Unpaged memory
The system reserved an unpaged area of memory for critical operations.
Device drivers often require unpaged buffers to ensure data is always accessible.
Because the data was unpaged, it remained in RAM and was not written to disk.

Related Terms

  • antonym: paged - subject to virtual-memory paging; can be swapped to disk
