veracious

adjective
habitually truthful and honest
4.8
Pronunciation: / vəˈreɪʃəs / (AmE), / vəˈreɪʃəs / (BrE)
(of a person) habitually speaking the truth; truthful and honest.

Examples

A veracious person

Everyone respected her as a veracious reporter who never distorted the facts.
The committee needed a veracious account of the incident to guide their decision.
