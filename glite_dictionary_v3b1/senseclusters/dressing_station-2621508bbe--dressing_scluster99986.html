dressing station

noun
military first-aid station near combat
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛsɪŋ ˌsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsɪŋ ˌsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A station near a combat area where wounded individuals receive first aid.

Examples

A dressing station near combat
After the battle, the medics quickly transported the wounded soldiers to the nearest dressing station.
He was stabilized at a dressing station before being moved to a field hospital.
The dressing station was set up just behind the frontline to provide urgent care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aid station - A place where emergency medical aid is provided, especially in military contexts
