unstill

verb
to make restless
9.9 0.01/m
to make someone or something restless, agitated, or disturbed; to unsettle.

Examples

To unstill the mood
The sudden noise seemed to unstill the peaceful atmosphere in the library.
News of the upcoming changes began to unstill the employees, making them anxious.
A single shout was enough to unstill the calm crowd gathered in the square.
