rutin

noun
plant antioxidant
Pronunciation: / ˈruːtɪn / (AmE), / ˈruːtɪn / (BrE)
a natural compound found in some plants, valued for its antioxidant properties and often used as a supplement to support blood vessel health

Examples

Rutin supplement
Buckwheat is a good source of rutin and is often included in healthy diets.
Scientists study rutin for its potential benefits in reducing inflammation.
Some supplements contain rutin to support blood vessel health.
Many people take rutin supplements to help with circulation and reduce bruising.
The doctor recommended rutin for its potential benefits in strengthening capillaries.
Some fruits and vegetables naturally contain rutin, which may support vascular health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flavonoid - a group of plant compounds with antioxidant effects
  • synonym: quercetin - a related plant flavonoid with antioxidant effects
  • synonym: vitamin P - another name for certain flavonoids, including rutin
