phraseless

adjective
unable to speak
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪsləs / (AmE), / ˈfreɪsləs / (BrE)
unable to express oneself in words or temporarily at a loss for words

Examples

Left phraseless by shock
He was left phraseless by the unexpected news and could not respond for several minutes.
The beauty of the landscape rendered her completely phraseless as she tried to take it all in.
After the performance, the audience sat phraseless, struggling to find words to describe what they had seen.

Related Terms

  • antonym: articulate - able to express oneself clearly in words
  • synonym: speechless - unable to speak, often due to shock or surprise
