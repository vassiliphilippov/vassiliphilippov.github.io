parsimoniousness

noun
extreme reluctance to spend money
9.9 9.2
Pronunciation: /pɑːr.sə.ˈmoʊ.niː.əs.nəs/ /; / /pɑːr.sɪ.ˈmoʊ.niː.əs.nəs/ (AmE), /pɑː.sə.ˈməʊ.niː.əs.nəs/ /; / /pɑː.sɪ.ˈməʊ.niː.əs.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being very unwilling to spend money or use resources; extreme stinginess or frugality
