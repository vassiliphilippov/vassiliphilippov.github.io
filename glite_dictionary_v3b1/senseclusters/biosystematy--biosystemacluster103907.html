biosystematy

noun
taxonomic classification using modern biological data
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊ.sɪˈstɛ.mə.ti / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊ.sɪˈstɛm.əti / (BrE)
the use of data, such as cytogenetic or biochemical information, to determine taxonomic relationships, especially within an evolutionary framework
