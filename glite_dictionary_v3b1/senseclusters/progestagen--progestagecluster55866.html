progestagen

noun
pregnancy hormone
Pronunciation: / proʊˈdʒɛstədʒən / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛstədʒən / (BrE)
a natural or synthetic hormone with effects similar to progesterone, used to prepare the uterus for pregnancy, support gestation, or in contraception and hormone therapy
