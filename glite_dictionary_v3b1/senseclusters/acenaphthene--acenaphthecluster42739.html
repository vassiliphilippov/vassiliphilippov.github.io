acenaphthene

noun
hydrocarbon for dyes
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪsəˈnæfθiːn / (AmE), / ˌeɪsɪˈnæfθiːn / (BrE)
a colorless solid hydrocarbon used in the manufacture of dyes and other chemicals

Examples
