maneless

adjective
without a mane
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪnləs / (AmE), / ˈmeɪnləs / (BrE)
lacking a mane, especially referring to animals such as lions or horses

Examples

A maneless lion
The maneless lioness watched over her cubs in the grass.
Some horse breeds are naturally maneless and have smooth necks.
Researchers observed a rare maneless male lion in the wild.
