reimportation

noun
bringing goods back
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌɪmpɔːrˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌɪmpɔːˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of bringing goods back into a country after they have previously been exported

Examples

The reimportation of goods
The government imposed strict rules on the reimportation of certain electronic products.
Pharmaceutical companies monitor the reimportation of medicines to control pricing and safety.
After being exported for processing, the reimportation of raw materials is carefully tracked.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exportation - the act of sending goods to another country for sale
  • synonym: importation - the act of bringing goods into a country from abroad
