flintlock

noun
old firearm mechanism using flint for ignition
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪntˌlɑk /; / ˈflɪnt.lɑk / (AmE), / ˈflɪntˌlɒk /; / ˈflɪnt.lɒk / (BrE)
