floaty

adjective
light; tending to float or be airy
5.5 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfloʊ.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈfləʊ.tiː/ (BrE)
light and likely to float on water or rise in air; having a soft, airy quality.

Examples

A floaty dress
She wore a floaty dress that seemed to drift in the breeze.
The artist used a floaty fabric to create the illusion of movement.
Those floaty curtains let sunlight filter beautifully into the room.
