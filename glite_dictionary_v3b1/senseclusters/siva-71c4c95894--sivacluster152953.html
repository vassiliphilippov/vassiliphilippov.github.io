Siva

proper noun
major Hindu god, the destroyer and restorer
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːvə / (AmE), / ˈʃiːvə / (BrE)
A principal god in Hinduism, known as 'the auspicious one', worshipped as the destroyer and restorer of worlds.

Examples

God Siva
Ancient temples dedicated to Siva can be found throughout India.
Many devotees pray to Siva during Maha Shivaratri to seek his blessings.
The mythology of Siva includes stories of both destruction and renewal.
