marinate

verb
soak food in a flavored liquid before cooking
1.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmæ.rɪ.neɪt/ /; / /ˈmæ.rə.neɪt/ (AmE), /ˈmæ.rɪ.neɪt/ /; / /ˈmæ.rə.neɪt/ (BrE)
to soak food in a seasoned liquid before cooking to add flavor or make it more tender

Examples

To marinate vegetables
