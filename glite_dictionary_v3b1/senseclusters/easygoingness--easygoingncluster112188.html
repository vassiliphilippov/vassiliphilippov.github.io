easygoingness

noun
the state of being relaxed and unconcerned
Pronunciation: / ˌiziˈɡoʊɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˌiːziˈɡəʊɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being calm, relaxed, and not easily worried or upset

Examples

Calm easygoingness
He handled the sudden changes with remarkable easygoingness, never once losing his cool.
Her easygoingness made her popular among her coworkers, who appreciated her stress-free attitude.
Traveling with someone who has a sense of easygoingness makes any trip more enjoyable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease
  • synonym: calmness - the quality of being calm; tranquility
  • synonym: relaxation - the state of being free from tension or anxiety
