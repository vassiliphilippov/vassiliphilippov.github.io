womanist

noun
advocate for women
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊmənɪst / (AmE), / ˈwʊmənɪst / (BrE)
a person who advocates for the rights, experiences, and empowerment of women, especially Black women

Examples

A womanist leader
As a proud womanist, she works to uplift the voices of Black women in her community.
The conference featured several womanist speakers discussing intersectionality and social justice.
He became a womanist after learning about the unique challenges faced by women of color.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feminist - a person who supports equality for women
  • antonym: sexist - a person who discriminates based on gender
