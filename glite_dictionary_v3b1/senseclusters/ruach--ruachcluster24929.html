ruach

noun
soul's middle level
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈruːɑx / (AmE), / ˈruːɑx / (BrE)
in Jewish mysticism, the intermediate aspect of the soul that connects emotion and intellect, positioned between lower and higher soul levels

Examples

Kabbalistic ruach
In Kabbalistic teachings, the ruach is considered the seat of moral virtues and emotions.
The rabbi explained how the ruach bridges the physical and spiritual aspects of a person.
Meditation practices in Jewish mysticism often focus on elevating the ruach toward higher understanding.
