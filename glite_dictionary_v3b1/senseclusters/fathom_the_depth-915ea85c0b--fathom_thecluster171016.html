fathom the depth

verb
to fully understand the extent of something
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfæθəm ðə dɛpθ / (AmE), / ˈfaðəm ðə dɛpθ / (BrE)

Scientists are still trying to fathom the depth of the ocean’s mysteries.
