three-on-the-tree

noun
three-speed manual transmission on the steering column
Pronunciation: / θɹi ɑn ðə tɹi / (AmE), / θɹi ɒn ðə tɹi / (BrE)
a three-speed manual transmission in a vehicle with the gear shift mounted on the steering column

Examples

A three-on-the-tree transmission
He fondly recalled learning to drive on a three-on-the-tree when he was a teenager.
It's rare to find a car with a three-on-the-tree these days.
My grandfather's old truck had a three-on-the-tree that was tricky to shift at first.
