outthrust

noun
protruding rock or land formation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌθrʌst / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌθrʌst / (BrE)
a part of rock or land that juts or protrudes outward from a surface

Examples

Rocky outthrust
The rocky outthrust was clearly visible above the treetops.
Waves crashed against the outthrust at the edge of the cliff.
We rested on a granite outthrust overlooking the valley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: projection - something that juts out from a surface
  • antonym: recess - an inward curve or hollow in a surface
