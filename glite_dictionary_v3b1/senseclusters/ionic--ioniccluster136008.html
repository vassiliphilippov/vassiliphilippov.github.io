ionic

adjective
relating to ions or ionic compounds
Pronunciation: / aɪˈɑnɪk / (AmE), / aɪˈɒnɪk / (BrE)
containing, involving, or occurring in the form of ions; relating to or denoting chemical compounds consisting of ions held together by electrostatic forces.

Examples

Ionic compound
Calcium ions play a crucial role in ionic transport in biological cells.
Sodium chloride is an ionic compound composed of sodium and chloride ions.
The ionic strength of a solution can affect biochemical reactions.
