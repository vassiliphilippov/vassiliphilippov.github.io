Lophodytes cucullatus

noun
species of duck (hooded merganser)
Pronunciation: / loʊˈfɒdəˌtiːz kjuːˈkʌlətəs / (AmE), / ləˈfɒdɪtiːz kjuːˈkʌlətəs / (BrE)
A small North American duck species, commonly known as the hooded merganser, notable for the male's distinctive high circular crest.
