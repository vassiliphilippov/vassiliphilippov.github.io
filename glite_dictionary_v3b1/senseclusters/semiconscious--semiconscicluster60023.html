semiconscious

adjective
partly conscious
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈkɑnʃəs / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈkɒnʃəs / (BrE)
partly conscious but not fully aware of sensations or surroundings

Examples

Be semiconscious after injury
After the blow to his head, she lay semiconscious on the floor.
He was semiconscious when the paramedics arrived at the scene.
The patient remained semiconscious throughout most of the procedure.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: conscious - aware of and responding to one's surroundings
  • antonym: unconscious - not awake and aware of and responding to one's environment
