thirstily

adverb
in an eager or intense manner
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈθɝːstɪli / (AmE), / ˈθɜːstɪli / (BrE)
in an eager or enthusiastic manner; with intense desire or longing

Examples

Listen thirstily
He listened thirstily to every word, eager to learn more.
She thirstily read every book she could find on the subject.
The children drank the lemonade thirstily after playing in the sun all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eagerly - in a way that shows keen or enthusiastic desire
  • synonym: hungrily - in a manner showing strong desire, especially for food or something needed
