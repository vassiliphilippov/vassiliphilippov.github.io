berretta

noun
stiff square clerical cap
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈrɛtə / (AmE), / bəˈretə / (BrE)
a stiff square cap with ridges across the crown, worn especially by Roman Catholic clergy

Examples

A black berretta
During the ceremony, each priest wore a black berretta as part of his traditional attire.

The museum displayed an ornate berretta from the 18th century.
