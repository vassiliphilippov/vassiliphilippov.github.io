cyberphobia

noun
irrational fear of technology or computers
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪbərˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌsʌɪbəˈfəʊbɪə / (BrE)
An irrational fear or strong dislike of computers or advanced technology.

Examples

Strong cyberphobia
Despite her initial cyberphobia, she gradually became comfortable with using digital technology.
His cyberphobia made it difficult for him to adapt to the new workplace systems.
People suffering from cyberphobia may avoid computers and electronic devices altogether.

Related Terms

  • synonym: technophobia - irrational fear or dislike of advanced technology
