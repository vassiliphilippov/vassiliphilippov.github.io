powder

noun
fine cosmetic or medical preparation
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdɚ /; / ˈpaʊdər / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə / (BrE)
a fine, dry substance made by crushing or grinding something, often used for cosmetics, medicine, or cooking

Examples

Face powder
After shaving, he sprinkled some talcum powder on his neck.
She applied a light layer of powder to her face before leaving the house.
The pharmacist recommended a medicated powder to treat the rash.
After the hike, his clothes were covered in fine white powder.
She sprinkled powder over the cake before serving it.
The pharmacist measured the powder carefully before mixing it into the solution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dust - fine, dry particles of matter
  • synonym: ointment - a smooth oily substance used on the skin for medical purposes
