unfeelingness

noun
lack of emotion or compassion
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfiːlɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈfiːlɪŋnɪs / (BrE)
lack of emotion, sympathy, or compassion; the quality of being hardhearted or insensitive.

Examples

Cold unfeelingness
He spoke with such unfeelingness that it left a chilling silence in the room.
Her unfeelingness during the tragedy shocked everyone present.
The movie captured the unfeelingness of bureaucratic systems perfectly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: callousness - disregard for others' feelings
  • antonym: compassion - sympathetic concern for others
  • synonym: insensitivity - lack of concern for others' feelings
