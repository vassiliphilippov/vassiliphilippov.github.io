immunoblot

noun
protein detection film
8.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmjunəˌblɑt / (AmE), / ɪˈmjuːnəˌblɒt / (BrE)
a membrane or film showing bands that indicate the presence of specific proteins, produced by an immunoblot assay

Examples

An immunoblot membrane
The scientist analyzed the immunoblot to determine which proteins were present in the sample.
After running the assay, clear bands appeared on the immunoblot indicating successful detection.
A high-quality immunoblot is essential for accurate protein identification in research.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrophoresis - a technique for separating molecules by electric charge
  • synonym: western blot - a laboratory method to detect specific proteins in a sample
