micron

noun
one millionth of a meter
7.7 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.krɑːn/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.krən/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krɒn/ (BrE)
a unit of length equal to one millionth of a meter (also called a micrometer), used to measure very small things

Examples

The width of a human hair is typically about 50 microns.
Modern filters can remove contaminants as small as a single micron.
Particles smaller than one micron are invisible to the naked eye.
The thickness of the coating was measured in microns to ensure precision.
