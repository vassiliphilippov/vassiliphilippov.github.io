rouille

noun
French garlic sauce
9.2
Pronunciation: / ruːˈjiː / (AmE), / ruːˈjiː / (BrE)
a French sauce made with garlic, olive oil, and often saffron, traditionally served with fish soup

Examples

Rouille sauce with fish
The chef served the bouillabaisse with a generous spoonful of rouille on the side.
She learned to make authentic rouille to accompany her homemade fish stew.
A dollop of rouille adds a rich, garlicky flavor to traditional French seafood dishes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aioli - a Mediterranean sauce made of garlic and olive oil
