ungoverned

adjective
not controlled or restrained
7.3 5.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡʌvɚnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡʌvənd / (BrE)
not controlled, restrained, or regulated; lacking discipline or constraint

Examples
