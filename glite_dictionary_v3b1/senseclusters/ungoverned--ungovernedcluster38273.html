ungoverned

adjective
not controlled or restrained
7.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡʌvɚnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡʌvənd / (BrE)
not controlled, restrained, or regulated; lacking discipline or constraint

Examples

An ungoverned temper
His ungoverned temper often got him into trouble at work.
The protest quickly turned into an ungoverned display of anger and frustration.
Years of ungoverned expansion led to a chaotic organization.
Her ungoverned curiosity often led her into trouble.
In the absence of strong leadership, the crowd became increasingly ungoverned and chaotic.
Without clear rules, the class turned ungoverned and noisy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: controlled - restrained or regulated, not wild or impulsive
  • antonym: governed - controlled or regulated
  • synonym: unbridled - not controlled or restrained
  • synonym: unchecked - not held in check or restrained
  • synonym: undisciplined - lacking in discipline or self-control
