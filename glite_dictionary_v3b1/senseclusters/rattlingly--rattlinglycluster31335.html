rattlingly

adverb
very fast or noisy
Pronunciation: / ˈræt̬lɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈræt.lɪŋ.li / (BrE)
in a way that is very fast, energetic, or noisy, often producing a rattling sound

Examples

Rattlingly fast ride
The old truck drove rattlingly over the gravel road, making everyone bounce in their seats.
She spoke rattlingly about her adventures, barely pausing to take a breath.
The wind blew rattlingly through the loose windowpanes during the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noisily - in a loud or noisy manner
  • antonym: quietly - in a quiet or silent manner
