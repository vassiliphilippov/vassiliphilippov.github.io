lose one's shit

verb
become very angry or upset (informal, vulgar)
3.3 9.1
Pronunciation: /luːz wʌnz ʃɪt/ /; / /luːz wəns ʃɪt/ (AmE), /luːz wʌnz ʃɪt/ /; / /luːz wəns ʃɪt/ (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) to suddenly become extremely angry, upset, or emotionally overwhelmed in a way that is hard to control

Examples

To lose his shit suddenly

Don't lose your shit over a minor mistake—it's not the end of the world.
