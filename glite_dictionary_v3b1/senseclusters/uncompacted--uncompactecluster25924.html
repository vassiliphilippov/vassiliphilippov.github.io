uncompacted

adjective
loose; not packed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəmˈpæktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəmˈpæktɪd / (BrE)
not pressed or packed together; loose or not made compact

Examples

Loose uncompacted soil

After the excavation, the ground was left uncompacted and needed to be leveled.
Seeds grow better in uncompacted earth where their roots can spread easily.
