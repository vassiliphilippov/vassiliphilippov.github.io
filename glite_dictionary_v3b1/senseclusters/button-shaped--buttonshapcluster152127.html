button-shaped

adjective
shaped like a button
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬n ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈbʌtn ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Shaped like a button; having a round, flat, or disk-like form resembling a button.

Examples

Button-shaped pendant
She wore a necklace with a delicate button-shaped pendant.
The cactus had small, button-shaped clusters all over its surface.
The pastry was topped with button-shaped pieces of chocolate.
