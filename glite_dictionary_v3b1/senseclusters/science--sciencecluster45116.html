science

noun
study of nature
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
the systematic study of the natural world through observation, experimentation, and analysis

Examples

Science discovers facts
Advances in science have led to new treatments for many diseases.
She has always been fascinated by science and hopes to become a researcher.
The teacher encouraged students to ask questions and explore science through experiments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: art - creative expression or skill, often contrasted with science
  • synonym: scientific - relating to or based on science
