pellucid

adjective
very clear in expression or style
8.6 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / p…ôÀàluÀês…™d / (AmE), / p…ôÀàluÀês…™d / (BrE)
(of language or style) clear and easy to understand; transparently lucid.

Examples

Pellucid writing style
Her pellucid explanations made complex theories accessible to everyone in the audience.
In academic writing, a pellucid style is often more effective than flowery language.
The author was praised for her pellucid prose, which left little room for confusion.
