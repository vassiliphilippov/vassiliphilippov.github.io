corpsman

noun
military medical assistant
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːrsmən /; / ˈkɔːrzmən / (AmE), / ˈkɔːsmən /; / ˈkɔːzmən / (BrE)
a medical assistant in the military who provides first aid and medical care to service members

Examples

Military corpsman aid
The wounded marine was treated by a skilled corpsman before being evacuated.
During the training exercise, a corpsman demonstrated how to apply a tourniquet.
Many corpsmen serve on ships, providing medical care to sailors and marines.
The corpsman quickly responded to the call for help during the training exercise.
After the explosion, a corpsman rushed to treat the injured soldiers.
Every Marine unit relies on a skilled corpsman to provide medical support in the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nurse - a person trained to care for the sick or injured
