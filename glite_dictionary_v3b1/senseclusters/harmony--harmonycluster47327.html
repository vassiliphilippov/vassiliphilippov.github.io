harmony

noun
musical combination of chords
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːr.mə.ni /; / ˈhɑr.mə.ni / (AmE), / ˈhɑː.mə.ni / (BrE)
the combination of musical notes that sound pleasant together, or the study of such combinations in music

Examples

Musical harmony
Jazz musicians are known for their innovative use of harmony to create unique sounds.
She studied the harmony of Beethoven's late string quartets in her music theory class.
The beauty of the song comes from its rich harmony and intricate chord progressions.
The choir's harmony was so beautiful that the audience was moved to tears.
The composer's use of unexpected harmony created a unique and memorable sound.
Their voices blended in perfect harmony during the duet.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discord - lack of agreement or harmony in musical sound
  • antonym: melody - musical term for a sequence of single notes that is musically satisfying
