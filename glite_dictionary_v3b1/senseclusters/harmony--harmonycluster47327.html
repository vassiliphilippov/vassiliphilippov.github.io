harmony

noun
musical combination of chords

Examples

Musical harmony
Jazz musicians are known for their innovative use of harmony to create unique sounds.
She studied the harmony of Beethoven's late string quartets in her music theory class.
The beauty of the song comes from its rich harmony and intricate chord progressions.
The choir's harmony was so beautiful that the audience was moved to tears.

Their voices blended in perfect harmony during the duet.

Synonyms

  • musical harmony
    3.7 0.01/m
    musical harmony focuses on chord progressions and how chords fit in songs, while harmony names the combination and study of pleasing notes.
  • consonance
    8.0 0.27/m
    consonance describes a single pleasing or stable sound or interval, while harmony refers to the broader combination and study of such notes.
