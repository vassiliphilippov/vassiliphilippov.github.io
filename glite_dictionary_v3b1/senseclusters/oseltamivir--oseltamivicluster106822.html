oseltamivir

noun
antiviral flu medicine
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊsɛlˈtæməˌvɪr / (AmE), / ˌəʊsɛlˈtæmɪvɪə / (BrE)
a medicine used to treat and prevent influenza (the flu)

Examples

Oseltamivir medicine
The doctor prescribed oseltamivir to help reduce her flu symptoms.
Many patients start taking oseltamivir within two days of noticing flu symptoms.
Pharmacies often run out of oseltamivir during severe flu seasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: influenza - a common viral infection known as the flu
