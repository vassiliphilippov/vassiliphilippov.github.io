overtime

noun
work beyond regular hours
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈəʊvətaɪm / (BrE)
time worked in addition to one’s regular working hours, especially when paid at a higher rate

Examples

Paid overtime hours
She was paid extra for all the overtime she worked this month.
The manager asked everyone to put in some overtime to meet the deadline.
Working regular overtime can affect your health over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compensatory time - paid time off given instead of payment for overtime hours
  • antonym: regular hours - standard period for work as defined by a contract
