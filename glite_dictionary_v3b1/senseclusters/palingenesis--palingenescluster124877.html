palingenesis

noun
rebirth or regeneration
9.9
Pronunciation: /pæl.ɪn.ˈdʒen.ə.sɪs/ /; / /pæl.ɪn.ˈdʒen.ə.səs/ (AmE), /pæl.ɪn.ˈdʒen.ɪ.siːz/ /; / /pæl.ɪn.ˈdʒen.ɪ.sɪs/ (BrE)
the process of rebirth, regeneration, or repeated emergence, especially in biological or philosophical contexts

Examples

World palingenesis
The ancient myth described the palingenesis of the world after a great flood.
Some biologists study palingenesis to understand how certain organisms regenerate lost parts.
In philosophy, palingenesis can refer to the spiritual renewal of an individual or society.
