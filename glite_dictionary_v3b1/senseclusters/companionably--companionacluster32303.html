companionably

adverb
in a friendly way
3.5
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpænjənəbli / (AmE), / kəmˈpænjənəbli / (BrE)
in a friendly and sociable way, showing a sense of companionship

Examples

Walk companionably together
They walked companionably through the park, enjoying each other's company.
The two colleagues worked companionably on the project, sharing ideas and laughter.
After dinner, the family sat companionably in the living room, talking about their day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amiably - in a pleasant and friendly manner
  • antonym: coldly - in an unfriendly or unemotional way
  • synonym: cordially - in a warm and friendly way
