hardwired

adjective
physically connected
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌwaɪrd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌwaɪəd / (BrE)
connected by physical electrical wiring or cabling rather than using wireless technology

Examples

Hardwired connection
The security cameras are hardwired to ensure a stable connection at all times.
Each desk in the computer lab is hardwired for high-speed internet access.
Unlike wireless devices, hardwired systems are less likely to experience interference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wired - connected by wires or cables
  • antonym: wireless - using radio signals instead of wires or cables
