bodied

adjective
having physical form; incarnate
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːdid / (AmE), / ˈbɒdid / (BrE)
having a physical or bodily form; embodied or incarnate.

Examples

Bodied form appears
Her bodied presence in the room was both comforting and reassuring to everyone.
Philosophers often debate whether souls are bodied or disembodied after death.
The story described a bodied apparition walking through the ancient halls.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disembodied - lacking a physical or bodily form
  • synonym: incarnate - embodied in flesh; given a bodily form
