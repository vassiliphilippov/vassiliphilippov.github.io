bodied

adjective
having physical form; incarnate
6.8 2.23/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ëÀêdid / (AmE), / Ààb…ídid / (BrE)
having a physical or bodily form; embodied or incarnate.

Examples

Bodied form appears

The story described a bodied apparition walking through the ancient halls.

Synonyms

  • embodied
    5.5 0.00/m

  • incarnate
    7.8 1.51/m

  • corporeal
    8.4 1.18/m
    corporeal describes what is physical rather than spiritual, whereas bodied focuses on having an actual bodily form.
