Chickasaw language

noun
Muskogean language of the Chickasaw people
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪkəˌsɔː ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkəsɔː ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The Muskogean language traditionally spoken by the Chickasaw people of the southeastern United States.

Examples

Chickasaw language spoken
Efforts are underway to preserve and revitalize the Chickasaw language among younger generations.
She attended a workshop to improve her fluency in the Chickasaw language.
The Chickasaw language is classified as critically endangered by UNESCO.
