jacket potato

noun
baked potato with its skin on
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒækɪt pəˈteɪtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒækɪt pəˈteɪtəʊ / (BrE)
A baked potato served with its skin left on, especially as commonly eaten in Britain.

Examples

A jacket potato meal
In the UK, a jacket potato is a popular lunchtime dish often sold in cafés.
The recipe calls for a large jacket potato, cooked until the skin is crisp and golden.
Tonight, I made a jacket potato filled with cheese and beans for dinner.
