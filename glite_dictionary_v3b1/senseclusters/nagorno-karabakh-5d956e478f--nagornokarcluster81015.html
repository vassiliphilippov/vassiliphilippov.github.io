Nagorno-Karabakh

proper noun

Pronunciation: /nə.ˈgɔːr.noʊ ˈkær.ə.bæk/ /; / /nə.ˈgɔːr.noʊ kɑː.rə.ˈbɑːk/ (AmE), /nə.ˈgɔː.nəʊ kɑː.rə.ˈbɑːk/ /; / /næ.ˈgɔː.nəʊ kɑː.rə.ˈbɑːk/ (BrE)
A mountainous region in the South Caucasus, formerly an unrecognized de facto state, internationally recognized as part of Azerbaijan but predominantly inhabited by ethnic Armenians and the subject of long-standing conflict.
