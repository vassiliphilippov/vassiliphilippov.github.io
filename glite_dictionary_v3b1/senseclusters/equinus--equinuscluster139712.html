equinus

noun
foot deformity
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkwaɪnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈkwaɪnəs / (BrE)
a medical condition in which the foot is fixed in a downward-pointing position, limiting heel contact with the ground

Examples

Equinus foot
The doctor diagnosed the child with equinus after observing the way he walked.
Physical therapy can help improve mobility in patients with equinus.
Surgery may be considered if equinus does not respond to non-invasive treatments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clubfoot - a congenital deformity in which the foot is twisted out of shape or position
