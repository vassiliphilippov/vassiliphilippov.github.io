legume family

noun
family of plants bearing bean pods (Fabaceae)

Pronunciation: /lə.ˈgjuːm ˈfæm.ə.li/ /; / /ˈle.ɡjuːm ˈfæm.ə.li/ (AmE), /ˈle.ɡjuːm ˈfæm.ɪ.li/ /; / /lə.ˈgjuːm ˈfæm.ɪ.li/ (BrE)
