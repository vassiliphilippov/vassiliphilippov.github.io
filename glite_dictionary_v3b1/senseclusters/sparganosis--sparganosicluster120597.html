sparganosis

noun
tapeworm larva infection
Pronunciation: / ˌspɑːrɡəˈnoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌspɑːɡəˈnəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a disease caused by infection with the larvae of tapeworms from the genus Spirometra

Examples

sparganosis infection
The patient developed sparganosis after drinking untreated water in a rural area.
Doctors confirmed sparganosis by identifying the tapeworm larvae in tissue samples.
Symptoms of sparganosis can include swelling and pain where the larvae migrate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cysticercosis - a disease caused by the larvae of pork tapeworms
  • synonym: tapeworm infection - an infection caused by tapeworms in the body
