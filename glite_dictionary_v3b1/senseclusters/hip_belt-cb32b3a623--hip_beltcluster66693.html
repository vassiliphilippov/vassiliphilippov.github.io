hip belt

noun
belt worn on hips
Pronunciation: / hɪp bɛlt / (AmE), / hɪp bɛlt / (BrE)
a belt or narrow band worn around the hips, often as a fashion accessory or to hold clothing in place

Examples

A stylish hip belt
She chose a colorful hip belt to add style to her summer dress.
The hip belt helped keep his pants in place during the hike.
Many fashion trends feature a wide hip belt worn over long shirts or tunics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waistband - a band of material around the waist of clothing
