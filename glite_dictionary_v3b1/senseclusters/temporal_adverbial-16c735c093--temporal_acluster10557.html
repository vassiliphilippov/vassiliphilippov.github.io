temporal adverbial

noun
adverbial indicating time
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpɚəl ædˈvɝbiəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərəl ədˈvɜːbɪəl / (BrE)
an adverbial phrase or word that indicates when an action occurs

Examples

A temporal adverbial phrase
Adding a temporal adverbial helps clarify when the event takes place.
In the sentence 'She left yesterday,' the word yesterday is a temporal adverbial.
Teachers often ask students to identify the temporal adverbial in a sentence.
