late-night

adjective
happening late at night
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪtˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈleɪtˌnaɪt / (BrE)
happening or done late at night, especially after the usual or expected time

Examples

Late-night study session
Many students turn to late-night study sessions during exam season.
She often watches late-night television shows before going to bed.
The city’s subway system shuts down during the late-night hours for maintenance.
