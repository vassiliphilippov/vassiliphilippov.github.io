marine

adjective
relating to ships or shipping
Pronunciation: / məˈrin / (AmE), / məˈriːn / (BrE)
relating to ships, shipping, navigation, or seamen, especially as distinct from the sea itself

Examples

Marine insurance
He specialized in marine insurance, dealing with policies for ships and cargo.
Regulations about marine safety have become stricter in recent years.
The museum features a collection of marine navigation instruments from the eighteenth century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maritime - relating to the sea, ships, or navigation, often used interchangeably with 'marine'
  • antonym: terrestrial - relating to the land, as opposed to the sea or ships
