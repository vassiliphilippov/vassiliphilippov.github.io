abider

noun
one who endures
Pronunciation: / əˈbaɪdər / (AmE), / əˈbaɪdə / (BrE)
a person who endures, tolerates, or patiently accepts something difficult or unpleasant

Examples

Patient abider
Despite the harsh conditions, he remained an abider and never gave up hope.
As an abider of strict rules, she rarely questioned authority.
The team respected him as an abider who could handle any challenge with patience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: complainer - a person who expresses dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • synonym: endurer - a person who continues despite hardship
  • synonym: tolerator - someone who tolerates or puts up with something
