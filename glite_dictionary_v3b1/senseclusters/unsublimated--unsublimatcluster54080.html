unsublimated

adjective
not vaporized by sublimation
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsʌblɪˌmeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsʌblɪmeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not converted from a solid directly into a vapor by the process of sublimation; remaining in the solid state
