bioplasmic

adjective
life energy (pseudo)
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˈplæzmɪk / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊˈplæzmɪk / (BrE)
relating to a supposed life energy or subtle vital force, especially in pseudoscientific or spiritual contexts

Examples

Bioplasmic energy field
Some alternative healers believe in the existence of a bioplasmic field around the human body.
The book describes bioplasmic energy as a force that connects all living things.
She claimed the room still held a faint bioplasmic energy from the ritual the night before.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spiritual - relating to the spirit or non-physical energy
  • synonym: vital - relating to life or essential energy
