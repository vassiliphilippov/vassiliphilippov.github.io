calligraph

verb
write in a decorative style
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪɡræf / (AmE), / kəˈlɪɡrɑːf / (BrE)
to write or inscribe in a beautiful or decorative style, especially by hand

Examples

To calligraph poetry
Artists often calligraph poetry on handmade paper for a personal touch.
He carefully calligraphed his name at the bottom of the certificate.
She learned to calligraph invitations for weddings and special occasions.
