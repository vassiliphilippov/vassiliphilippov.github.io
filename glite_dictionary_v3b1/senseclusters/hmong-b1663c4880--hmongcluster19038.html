Hmong

noun
an ethnic group from Southeast Asia
Pronunciation: / mɔŋ / (AmE), / mɒŋ / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous people traditionally living in the mountainous regions of southern China, Vietnam, Laos, and Thailand, many of whom have emigrated to the United States and other countries

Examples

A Hmong person
Many Hmong settled in Minnesota after the Vietnam War.
She is studying the history of the Hmong people in Southeast Asia.
The Hmong have a rich tradition of textile arts and storytelling.
