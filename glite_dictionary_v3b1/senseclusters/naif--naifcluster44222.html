naif

noun
a naive or inexperienced person
Pronunciation: / naɪˈiːf, ˈneɪf / (AmE), / naˈiːf, ˈneɪf / (BrE)
A person who is naive or inexperienced, especially in matters of the world or society.

Examples

A young naif
Despite his education, John remained a naif in the ways of business.
She played the role of a naif discovering the world for the first time.
The shrewd politician easily manipulated the naif newcomer in parliament.

Related Terms

  • synonym: innocent - lacking worldly experience or guile
  • synonym: naive - lacking experience or sophistication
