microsatellite

noun
short DNA sequence
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌsætəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌsætəlaɪt / (BrE)
a short, repeating sequence of DNA used as a genetic marker in research and analysis

Examples

Microsatellite DNA marker
Scientists use microsatellites to study genetic differences between populations.
The presence of a specific microsatellite can help identify inherited diseases.
Researchers compared microsatellite patterns to track the migration of animal species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genetic marker - a gene or DNA sequence used to identify individuals or species
  • degree higher: minisatellite - a longer, repeating DNA sequence used in genetic studies
