pullout

noun
military withdrawal or disengagement
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊlˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈpʊl.aʊt /; / ˈpʊlˌaʊt / (BrE)
the act of withdrawing or removing from a place, situation, or activity (especially suddenly or completely, such as military withdrawal)

Examples

Military pullout
After a hasty pullout, tensions in the region quickly escalated.
Many soldiers felt conflicted about the abrupt pullout from the operation.
The government announced a complete pullout of troops from the conflict zone.
The military pullout from the region was completed ahead of schedule.
After the sudden pullout of investors, the project was canceled.
The newspaper included a special pullout section on the upcoming election.

Related Terms

  • antonym: advance - the act of moving forward in a military action
  • synonym: retreat - the act of moving back or withdrawing
  • synonym: withdrawal - the act of removing forces from an area
