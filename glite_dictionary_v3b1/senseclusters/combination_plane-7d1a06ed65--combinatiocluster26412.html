combination plane

noun
woodworking tool with interchangeable cutters
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːmbɪˈneɪʃən pleɪn / (AmE), / ˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən pleɪn / (BrE)
a woodworking tool with interchangeable cutters of various shapes, used to perform multiple planing operations

Examples

A combination plane tool
A well-maintained combination plane can replace several single-purpose tools in a workshop.
He collected vintage combination planes to display in his woodworking studio.
The carpenter used a combination plane to create both grooves and rabbets on the window frame.
