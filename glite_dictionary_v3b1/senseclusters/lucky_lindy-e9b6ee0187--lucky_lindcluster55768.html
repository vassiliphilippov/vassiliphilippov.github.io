Lucky Lindy

proper noun
nickname of aviator Charles Lindbergh
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌki ˈlɪndi / (AmE), / ˈlʌki ˈlɪndi / (BrE)
a nickname for Charles Lindbergh, the American aviator who made the first solo nonstop flight across the Atlantic Ocean in 1927
