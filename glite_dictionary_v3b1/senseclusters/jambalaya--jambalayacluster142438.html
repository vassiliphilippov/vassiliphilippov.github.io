jambalaya

noun
spicy Creole rice dish with meat or seafood
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒæmbəˈlaɪə / (AmE), / ˌdʒæmbəˈlaɪə / (BrE)
a spicy Creole dish consisting of rice cooked with ham, sausage, chicken, or shellfish, and usually seasoned with tomatoes, peppers, onions, and celery

Examples

Spicy jambalaya dish
He loves to experiment with different ingredients when making jambalaya at home.
Her homemade jambalaya was packed with sausage, shrimp, and vegetables.
We ordered a large bowl of jambalaya at the New Orleans restaurant.
