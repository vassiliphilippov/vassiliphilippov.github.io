airmail letter

noun
letter sent by airmail
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌmeɪl ˈlɛtər / (AmE), / ˈeəˌmeɪl ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
a letter that is sent using airmail service, typically for faster international delivery

Examples

An airmail letter
He wrote his grandmother an airmail letter from France so it would arrive quickly.
In the past, sending an airmail letter overseas was much faster than regular mail.
She preserved every airmail letter sent to her during the war.
