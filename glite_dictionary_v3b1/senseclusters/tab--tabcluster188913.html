tab

verb
to move to next field
3.1
Pronunciation: /tæb/ (AmE), /tæb/ (BrE)
to move the cursor to the next tab stop or field in a document or program, usually by pressing the Tab key

Examples

To tab through fields
You can tab through the form fields to fill them out more quickly.
If you press Shift and tab, you will move to the previous field.
Many users find it faster to tab between input boxes instead of using the mouse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: navigate - to move through fields or options in a program
  • synonym: select - to choose a field or option in a program
