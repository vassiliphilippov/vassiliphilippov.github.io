dental plate

noun
removable dental appliance for missing teeth
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛntəl pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl pleɪt / (BrE)
a removable dental appliance that replaces missing teeth; a prosthesis fitted to the mouth to restore dental function and appearance

Examples

A dental plate fits
After losing several teeth, she was fitted with a dental plate to help her chew properly.
My grandfather takes out his dental plate every night before going to bed.
The dentist explained how to care for the new dental plate to prevent damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: denture - an artificial replacement for one or more teeth
