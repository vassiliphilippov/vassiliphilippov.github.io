carrousel

noun
airport luggage conveyor belt
6.6 3.2
Pronunciation: / kæɹəˌsɛl / (AmE), / kærəˈsɛl / (BrE)
a rotating conveyor belt at an airport from which arriving passengers collect their luggage

Examples

Airport carrousel
After landing, he hurried to the carrousel to retrieve his luggage.
Her bag was the last one to arrive on the carrousel.
We waited by the carrousel for our suitcases to appear.
