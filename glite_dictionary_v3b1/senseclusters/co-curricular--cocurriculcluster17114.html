co-curricular

adjective
outside academic curriculum
4.3
Pronunciation: /koʊ kə.ˈrɪk.jə.lər/ (AmE), /kəʊ kə.ˈrɪk.jʊ.lə/ (BrE)
relating to activities that complement but are not part of the regular academic curriculum

Examples

Co-curricular activities
Participation in co-curricular activities can help students develop leadership and teamwork skills.
The school offers a wide range of co-curricular programs, including music, debate, and community service.
Many universities value co-curricular involvement when considering applicants for scholarships.
