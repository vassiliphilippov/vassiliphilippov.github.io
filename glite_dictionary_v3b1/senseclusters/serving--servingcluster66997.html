serving

noun
the delivery of a legal writ or summons
4.1 8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪŋ / (BrE)
the formal act of delivering a legal writ, summons, or other court document to the person to whom it is addressed
