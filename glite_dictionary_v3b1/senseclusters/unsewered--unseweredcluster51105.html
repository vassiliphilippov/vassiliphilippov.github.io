unsewered

adjective
without sewer connection
8.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsuːərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsjʊəd / (BrE)
not connected to a public sewer system for waste disposal

Examples

An unsewered house
Many rural homes remain unsewered and rely on septic tanks.
The new housing development is still unsewered, so residents must use private waste systems.
Living in an unsewered area can present challenges for sanitation and public health.
