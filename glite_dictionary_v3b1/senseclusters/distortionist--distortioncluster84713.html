distortionist

noun
an artist who distorts forms in painting
7.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɔrʃənɪst / (AmE), / dɪˈstɔːʃənɪst / (BrE)
a painter who deliberately distorts forms or shapes in their artwork for expressive or stylistic effect.

Examples

A distortionist painter
As a distortionist, he reshaped familiar scenes into surreal and unsettling images.
Critics praised the distortionist for redefining traditional artistic boundaries through her unique perspective.
The gallery featured several works by a renowned distortionist known for her abstract portraits.
