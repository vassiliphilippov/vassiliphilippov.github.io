ultramarathon

noun
race longer than marathon
4.7
Pronunciation: /ʌl.trə.ˈmær.ə.θən/ (AmE), /ʌl.trə.ˈmæ.rə.θən/ (BrE)
a running race that is longer than the standard marathon distance of 42.195 kilometers (26.2 miles)

Examples

An ultramarathon race
He completed his first ultramarathon after months of intense training and preparation.
Many athletes consider running an ultramarathon to be the ultimate test of endurance.
The ultramarathon route passed through mountains, forests, and remote villages.
He trained for months to complete his first ultramarathon in the mountains.
Many athletes find the mental challenge of an ultramarathon even greater than the physical one.
The ultramarathon took runners through forests, across rivers, and up steep hills.
