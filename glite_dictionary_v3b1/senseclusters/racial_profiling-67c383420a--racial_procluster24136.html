racial profiling

noun
the targeting of individuals by race by police
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪʃəl ˈproʊfaɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈreɪʃəl ˈprəʊfaɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice by law enforcement officials of targeting or suspecting people of crimes based on their race or ethnicity

Examples

Police racial profiling
Some argue that racial profiling undermines trust between law enforcement and minority communities.
The community protested against racial profiling by local police.
The new policy was intended to eliminate racial profiling at traffic stops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discrimination - unjust or prejudicial treatment based on group characteristics
  • synonym: profiling - the recording and analysis of a person's psychological and behavioral characteristics
