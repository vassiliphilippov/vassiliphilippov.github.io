understanding

adjective
sympathetic and empathetic
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɚˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
showing sympathy and insight into other people's feelings and situations; empathetic and tolerant.

Examples

An understanding teacher
It's important to have at least one understanding friend in your life.
My boss was very understanding when I explained the problem.
She hoped her parents would be understanding about her decision.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insensitive - showing lack of concern for others' feelings
  • synonym: sympathetic - feeling, showing, or expressing sympathy
  • synonym: tolerant - showing willingness to allow or accept differing views or behaviors
