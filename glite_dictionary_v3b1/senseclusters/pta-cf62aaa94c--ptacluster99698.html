PTA

noun
artery-widening procedure
5.0 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / pi ti e…™ / (AmE), / piÀê tiÀê e…™ / (BrE)
a medical procedure in which a small balloon is inserted to widen a narrowed blood vessel, especially an artery

Examples

PTA procedure
The cardiologist recommended a PTA to improve blood flow in the patient's leg.
After the PTA, the patient noticed a significant reduction in pain while walking.
A PTA is often performed to treat blocked arteries without the need for open surgery.

Synonyms

  • angioplasty
    5.3 0.94/m
    angioplasty uses a balloon or device to repair or unblock blood vessels, while PTA inserts a small balloon to widen narrowed arteries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angioplasty - a procedure to restore blood flow through a narrowed or blocked artery
  • antonym: bypass surgery - surgical procedure to redirect blood flow around a blocked artery
