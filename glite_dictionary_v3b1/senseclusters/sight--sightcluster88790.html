sight

noun
mental view; perception or judgement
Pronunciation: / saɪt / (AmE), / saɪt / (BrE)
a person's mental perception or judgement; the way in which someone views or considers something

Examples

In his sight
In his sight, the project was a complete success.
In my father's sight, honesty was the most important virtue.
To her sight, the dispute had no possible solution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perspective - a particular attitude toward or way of regarding something; a point of view
  • synonym: view - an opinion or attitude about something
