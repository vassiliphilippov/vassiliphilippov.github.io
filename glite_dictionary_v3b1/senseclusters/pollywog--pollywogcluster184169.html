pollywog

noun
larval frog or toad (tadpole)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑliˌwɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈpɒliˌwɒɡ / (BrE)
a tadpole; the larval stage of a frog or toad, typically with a tail and no legs

Examples

A pollywog tadpole
In biology class, we observed the transformation of a pollywog into a young frog.
Over time, the pollywog gradually developed legs and lost its tail.
The children watched a pollywog swim in the clear pond.
