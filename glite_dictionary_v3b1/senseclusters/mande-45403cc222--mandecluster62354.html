Mande

noun
West African language group
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːn.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈmæn.deɪ / (BrE)
a group of closely related African languages of the Niger-Congo family, spoken chiefly from Senegal to the Ivory Coast
