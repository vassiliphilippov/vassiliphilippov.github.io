unmuscled

adjective
without developed muscles
6.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmʌsəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈmʌsəld / (BrE)
lacking muscles or a muscular build (physically weak or not muscular)

Examples

An unmuscled body
Despite his height, he remained unmuscled throughout his teenage years.
The actor transformed his unmuscled frame for the demanding action role.
Her unmuscled arms made lifting the heavy box a real challenge.
The jellyfish is an unmuscled creature that drifts with the ocean currents.
Some simple organisms remain unmuscled throughout their entire life cycle.
Unlike insects, worms are often described as unmuscled due to their lack of muscle tissue.
