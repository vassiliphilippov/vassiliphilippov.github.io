hemp family

noun
a plant family including hemp and hops
9.8
Pronunciation: / hɛmp ˈfæməli / (AmE), / hɛmp ˈfamɪli / (BrE)
a family of herbaceous plants, including hemp (Cannabis) and hops (Humulus), sometimes classified in Cannabaceae

Examples

The hemp family plants
Cannabis and Humulus, both part of the hemp family, are wind-pollinated herbaceous plants.
Researchers are studying the evolution of the hemp family to understand similarities between cannabis and hops.
The hemp family includes both the economically important hemp and the plant used for brewing hops.
