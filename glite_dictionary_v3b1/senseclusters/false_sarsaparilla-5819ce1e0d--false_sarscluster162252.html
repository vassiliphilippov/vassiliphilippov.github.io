false sarsaparilla

noun
herb with aromatic roots, sarsaparilla substitute
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔls ˌsɑːrspəˈrɪlə / (AmE), / ˈfɔːls ˌsæspəˈrɪlə / (BrE)
a common perennial herb with aromatic roots, used as a substitute for sarsaparilla, native to central and eastern North America

Examples

false sarsaparilla plant
Many herbalists recommend false sarsaparilla as an alternative to imported sarsaparilla.
Native Americans traditionally used false sarsaparilla in teas as a natural remedy.
The roots of false sarsaparilla have a pleasant aroma, often compared to true sarsaparilla.
