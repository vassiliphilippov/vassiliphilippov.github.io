academization

noun
making academic
Pronunciation: / əˌkædəmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌkædəmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something more academic or connected to academic standards and institutions

Examples

Academization of training
The academization of vocational training has led to more theoretical courses.
Some educators debate whether the academization of early childhood education is beneficial.
Recent reforms have accelerated the academization of teacher preparation programs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deacademization - the process of making something less academic
  • synonym: professionalization - the process of making something more professional
