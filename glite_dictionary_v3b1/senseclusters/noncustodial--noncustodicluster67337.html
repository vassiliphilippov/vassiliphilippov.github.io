noncustodial

adjective
not managed by third party
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkʌˈstoʊdiəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkʌˈstəʊdiəl / (BrE)
not held or managed by a third-party custodian, but directly controlled by the owner

Examples

Noncustodial wallet control
A noncustodial wallet allows users to control their own digital assets without relying on a bank.
Many people prefer noncustodial accounts for greater privacy and security.
With a noncustodial system, only the owner has access to the funds.
