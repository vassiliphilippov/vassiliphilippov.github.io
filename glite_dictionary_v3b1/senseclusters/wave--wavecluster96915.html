wave

noun
hand gesture for signaling
Pronunciation: / weɪv / (AmE), / weɪv / (BrE)
a gesture of moving the hand to signal, greet, or call attention

Examples

A friendly wave
A friendly wave can break the ice when meeting new neighbors.
His wave was meant to catch the attention of the passing taxi.
She gave a cheerful wave as the train pulled away from the station.
