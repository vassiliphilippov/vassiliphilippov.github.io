propitious

adjective
favorable; likely to bring success
4.5
Pronunciation: / prəˈpɪʃəs / (AmE), / prəˈpɪʃəs / (BrE)
giving or indicating a good chance of success; favorable or auspicious.

Examples

A propitious start
His career advanced quickly after a propitious start at the new company.
The weather was propitious for their long hike through the mountains.
They considered the peaceful negotiations a propitious sign for lasting cooperation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auspicious - indicating a positive or successful outcome
  • synonym: favorable - giving an advantage or showing approval
  • antonym: unfavorable - not giving or indicating a good chance of success
