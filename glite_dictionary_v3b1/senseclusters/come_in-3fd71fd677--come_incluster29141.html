come in

verb
to place or rank in a competition
Pronunciation: / kʌm ɪn / (AmE), / kʌm ɪn / (BrE)
to achieve a particular position or rank in a competition, race, or contest, usually stated with an ordinal number

Examples

Come in second place
Despite her injury, she came in fourth in the tournament.
He was disappointed to come in second after leading the entire race.
Our team managed to come in fifth at the national championships.
