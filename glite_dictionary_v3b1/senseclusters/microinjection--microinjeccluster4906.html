microinjection

noun
cell injection technique
6.7
Pronunciation: /maɪ.kroʊ.ɪn.ˈdʒek.ʃən/ (AmE), /maɪ.krəʊ.ɪn.ˈdʒek.ʃən/ (BrE)

Examples

Microinjection technique
The scientist performed microinjection to introduce new genetic material into the cell.
Successful microinjection requires steady hands and specialized equipment in the laboratory.
Researchers use microinjection to study the effects of specific molecules inside living cells.
