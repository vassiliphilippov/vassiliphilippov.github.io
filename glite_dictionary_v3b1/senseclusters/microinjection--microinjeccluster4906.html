microinjection

noun
cell injection technique
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊɪnˈdʒɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊɪnˈdʒɛkʃən / (BrE)
a technique for injecting substances directly into a cell using a very fine needle or micropipette

Examples

Microinjection technique
The scientist performed microinjection to introduce new genetic material into the cell.
Successful microinjection requires steady hands and specialized equipment in the laboratory.
Researchers use microinjection to study the effects of specific molecules inside living cells.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electroporation - a technique for introducing substances into cells using an electric field
  • synonym: injection - the act of inserting a substance into something, especially with a needle
