innocent

adjective
lacking worldly experience; naïve
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈɪn.ə.sənt/ (AmE), /ˈɪn.ə.sənt/ (BrE)
lacking experience, knowledge, or sophistication; naive or unaware

Examples

She was innocent of the workings of local government.
Tourists often remain innocent of cultural customs when visiting new countries.
