nonrepressive

adjective
not controlling or suppressive
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈprɛsɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈprɛsɪv / (BrE)
not tending to control, restrain, or suppress actions, feelings, or expression

Examples

A nonrepressive approach
The school adopted a nonrepressive approach to student creativity and self-expression.
A nonrepressive environment encourages people to share their honest opinions without fear.
The new manager's nonrepressive style led to more open communication among employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: permissive - allowing or characterized by great freedom of behavior
  • antonym: repressive - tending to control, restrain, or suppress
