The act or process of using something so much that it becomes less effective, productive, or valuable.

Examples

The overutilization of resources
Healthcare experts warn that overutilization of antibiotics can lead to resistant bacteria.
Many analysts believe that overutilization of public resources results in higher costs and lower quality.
The overutilization of groundwater threatens the long-term sustainability of local agriculture.
Due to overutilisation of hospital facilities, patients may experience longer wait times.
Regulators are concerned about the overutilisation of antibiotics in farming.
The overutilisation of natural resources can lead to environmental degradation.

Synonyms

  • overuse
    3.8 1.68/m
    overuse is the common word for using something too much and causing harm, while overutilization is more formal and technical.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overuse - excessive use of something
  • antonym: underutilisation - failure to use something to its full potential
  • antonym: underutilization - failure to use something fully or adequately
