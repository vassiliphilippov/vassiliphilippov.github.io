bok choy

noun
Chinese cabbage with white stalks
Pronunciation: / bɑk tʃɔɪ /; / ˌbɑk ˈtʃɔɪ /; / ˌbɑːk ˈtʃɔɪ / (AmE), / bɒk tʃɔɪ /; / ˌbɒk ˈtʃɔɪ / (BrE)
