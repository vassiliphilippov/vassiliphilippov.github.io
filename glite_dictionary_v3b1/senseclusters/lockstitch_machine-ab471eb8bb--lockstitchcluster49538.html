lockstitch machine

noun
type of sewing machine using lockstitch
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑkˌstɪtʃ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈlɒkˌstɪtʃ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a sewing machine that joins fabric using the lockstitch method, intertwining upper and lower threads to form secure stitches

Examples

A lockstitch machine for sewing
A modern garment factory often uses a mix of lockstitch machines and specialty machines.
She learned to thread the lockstitch machine during her first sewing class.
The tailor preferred his vintage lockstitch machine for precise, durable seams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lockstitch - a stitch formed by two threads interlocking in fabric
  • antonym: overlock machine - a sewing machine that trims and overcasts fabric edges
