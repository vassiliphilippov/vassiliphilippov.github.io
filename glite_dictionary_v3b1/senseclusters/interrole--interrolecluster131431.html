interrole

noun
conflict between roles
9.3
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tə.ˈroʊl/ /; / /ˈɪn.tər.roʊl/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈrəʊl/ /; / /ˈɪn.tə.rəʊl/ (BrE)
