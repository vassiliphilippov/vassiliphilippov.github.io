interrole

noun
conflict between roles
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌroʊl / (AmE), / ˈɪntəˌrəʊl / (BrE)
a conflict or interaction between two or more social roles held by the same person, often leading to stress or difficulty in fulfilling each role

Examples

An interrole struggle
Many working parents experience interrole when job responsibilities interfere with family commitments.
The study examined how interrole affects employee satisfaction and stress levels.
She struggled with interrole as her volunteer work conflicted with her professional obligations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: role conflict - a situation where a person faces competing demands from different roles
  • synonym: work-life balance - the ability to manage work and personal life roles effectively
