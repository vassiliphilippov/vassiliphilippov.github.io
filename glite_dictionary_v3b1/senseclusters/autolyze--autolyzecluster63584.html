autolyze

verb
to rest dough for enzymes
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɔtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəˌlaɪz / (BrE)
to let dough rest so that natural enzymes break down the flour, improving gluten development and dough texture

Examples

To autolyze bread dough
Many artisan bakers autolyze their dough to achieve a better crumb structure in the final bread.
If you autolyze the flour and water before kneading, the dough will be easier to handle.
The recipe suggests you autolyze the mixture for at least twenty minutes for best results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rest - to allow dough to sit without working it
