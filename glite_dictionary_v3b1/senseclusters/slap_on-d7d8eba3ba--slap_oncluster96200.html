slap on

verb
apply something quickly or carelessly
5.7
Pronunciation: / slæp ɑn / (AmE), / slæp ɒn / (BrE)
to apply something, especially a substance such as paint or cream, quickly or carelessly

Examples

To slap on paint
Don't just slap on makeup; take your time for a better result.
He slapped on a fresh coat of paint without smoothing the surface.
She slapped on some sunscreen before heading outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apply - to put or spread a substance onto a surface
  • degree lower: dab on - to apply something gently and lightly
