roller

noun
long heavy sea wave
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə / (BrE)
a long, heavy sea wave that rolls steadily towards the shore

Examples

A big sea roller
After the storm, rollers continued to pound the rocky coastline.
Strong winds sent huge rollers crashing onto the beach.
Surfers waited patiently for the next roller to arrive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breaker - a wave that breaks into foam as it approaches the shore
