Seleucus

proper noun
founder of the Seleucid dynasty, Macedonian general
9.9
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈluː.kəs/ /; / /ˈse.ljuː.kəs/ (AmE), /sə.ˈluː.kəs/ /; / /ˈse.ljuː.kəs/ (BrE)
