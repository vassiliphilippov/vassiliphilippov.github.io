unyoked

adjective
not joined together
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈjoʊkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈjəʊkt / (BrE)
no longer joined or fastened together, especially by a yoke

Examples

Unyoked oxen
After the plowing was finished, the oxen were unyoked and allowed to rest.
The two carts remained unyoked in the barn overnight.
He felt unyoked from his responsibilities after retiring from his job.

Related Terms

  • synonym: separated - not joined or connected
  • antonym: yoked - joined or fastened together, especially by a yoke
