mentalis

noun
chin muscle
9.8
Pronunciation: /men.ˈteɪ.lɪs/ (AmE), /men.ˈteɪ.lɪs/ (BrE)
a muscle located in the chin that raises and pushes up the lower lip

Examples

The mentalis muscle
The mentalis muscle helps you pout by pushing up your lower lip.
Damage to the mentalis can affect facial expressions and speech.
Surgeons must be careful not to injure the mentalis during chin procedures.
