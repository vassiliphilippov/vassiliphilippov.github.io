hubbed

adjective
having a central hub
Pronunciation: / hʌbd / (AmE), / hʌbd / (BrE)
having a hub or equipped with a central part for connecting other components

Examples

Hubbed wheel
The mechanic recommended a hubbed wheel for better stability and easier installation.
This bicycle uses a hubbed design to allow for quick attachment of the spokes.
Many modern devices feature a hubbed connector to simplify assembly and maintenance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hubless - lacking a central hub or connecting part
