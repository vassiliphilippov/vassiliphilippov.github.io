scaler

noun
device for removing scale
Pronunciation: / ˈskeɪlər / (AmE), / ˈskeɪlə / (BrE)
a device or person that removes scale or deposits from a surface, especially in dentistry or industry

Examples

Dental scaler removes plaque
The dental hygienist used a scaler to remove plaque from my teeth.
Industrial workers rely on a scaler to clean rust from metal pipes.
A scaler is essential for keeping machinery free of mineral buildup.
