bag lady

noun
homeless woman carrying belongings in bags
5.9
Pronunciation: / bæɡ ˈleɪdi / (AmE), / bæɡ ˈleɪdi / (BrE)
a homeless woman who carries her belongings with her in shopping bags, often seen in public places

Examples

Bag lady on street
Neighbors organized a charity drive after hearing about the plight of the elderly bag lady living downtown.
She offered a cup of coffee to the bag lady she passed on her way to work.
The city’s park is a common place to see a bag lady resting on a bench.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bagman - a homeless man who carries his belongings in bags
