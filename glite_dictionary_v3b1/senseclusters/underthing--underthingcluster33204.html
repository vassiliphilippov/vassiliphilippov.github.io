underthing

noun
protective layer underneath
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌθɪŋ / (BrE)
a layer, lining, or piece of padding placed beneath another object to protect or support it

Examples

Soft underthing padding
They placed a soft underthing beneath the vase to prevent scratches on the table.
A thin felt underthing was used to protect the floor from the heavy furniture.
Before laying the carpet, he added an underthing for extra comfort and insulation.

  • synonym: padding - material used to protect or cushion something
