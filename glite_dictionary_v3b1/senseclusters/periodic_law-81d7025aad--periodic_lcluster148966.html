periodic law

noun
principle that element properties repeat periodically
3.3 5.2
Pronunciation: /pɪ.riː.ˈɑː.dɪk lɑː/ /; / /pɪ.riː.ˈɑː.dɪk lɔː/ (AmE), /pɪ.ri.ˈɒ.dɪk lɔː/ /; / /pɜː.ˈraɪ.ɒ.dɪk lɔː/ (BrE)
