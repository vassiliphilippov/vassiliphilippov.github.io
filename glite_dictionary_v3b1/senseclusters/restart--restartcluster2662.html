Synonyms

  • recommence
    5.6 0.1/m
    recommence is used in formal or written contexts to begin again after a pause, while restart is common and everyday.
  • reinitiate
    5.9 0.08/m
    reinitiate is used for processes or systems in technical settings to start them again, while restart is more general and common.
  • resume
    3.1 14/m
    resume is to continue an activity from where it stopped after a pause, while restart may mean begin again from the beginning.
  • reactivate
    4.3 2/m
    reactivate is to make something active again after being inactive, while restart focuses on starting the same process again.
