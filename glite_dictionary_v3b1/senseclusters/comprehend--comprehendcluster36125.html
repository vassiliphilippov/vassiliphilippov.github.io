comprehend

verb
to include or encompass
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːm.prəˈhɛnd / (AmE), / ˌkɒm.prɪˈhɛnd / (BrE)
to include or contain something as part of a larger whole; to encompass within limits or scope

Examples

Comprehend many parts
His responsibilities comprehend several different departments within the company.
The new legislation comprehends all previous amendments under one law.
The syllabus comprehends both theoretical and practical skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encompass - to include comprehensively; cover or surround
  • antonym: exclude - to leave out; not include
  • synonym: include - to contain as part of the whole; comprise
