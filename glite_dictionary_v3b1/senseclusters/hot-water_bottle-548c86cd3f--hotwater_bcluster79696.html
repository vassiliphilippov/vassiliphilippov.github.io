hot-water bottle

noun
rubber container for hot water to provide warmth
4.5 2.3
Pronunciation: /hɑːt ˈwɔː.tər ˈbɑːt.əl/ /; / /hɑːt ˈwɑː.tər ˈbɑːt.əl/ (AmE), /hɒt ˈwɔː.tə ˈbɒt.əl/ (BrE)
