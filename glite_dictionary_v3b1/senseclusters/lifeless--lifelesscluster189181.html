lifeless

adjective
unable to support life
1.1 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪ.fləs/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪ.fləs/ (BrE)
(of a place or thing) not having the ability to support or sustain life.

Examples

Lifeless planet
Scientists discovered that the surface of Mars is lifeless due to extreme cold and lack of atmosphere.
The robotic probe sent back data confirming the moon remains a lifeless wasteland.
Unlike Earth, the distant exoplanet appeared completely lifeless in the telescope images.
