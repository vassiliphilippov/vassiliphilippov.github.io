interworking

noun
systems working together
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of different systems, devices, or networks working together effectively

Examples

Interworking of networks
The interworking of various computer networks allows for seamless communication between offices.
Successful interworking between different software applications can save companies time and money.
Engineers focused on improving the interworking of the new devices with existing systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: integration - the process of combining parts into a whole
  • synonym: interoperability - the ability of systems to work together and exchange information
  • antonym: isolation - the state of being separate and not working together
