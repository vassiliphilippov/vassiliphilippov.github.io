food waste

noun
discarded or unused food
Pronunciation: / fuːd weɪst / (AmE), / fuːd weɪst / (BrE)
food that is discarded, especially unused or leftover food from kitchens, shops, or production processes

Examples

Food waste disposal
Composting food waste can help decrease landfill use and create nutrient-rich soil.
Many households throw away large amounts of food waste each week without realizing the environmental impact.
The restaurant implemented new policies to reduce food waste in its kitchen.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: food loss - food lost before it reaches the consumer, e.g. during production or processing
  • synonym: garbage - waste material, especially household waste
