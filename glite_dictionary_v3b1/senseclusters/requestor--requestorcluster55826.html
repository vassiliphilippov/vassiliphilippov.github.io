requestor

noun
person making request
3.7
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkwɛstər / (AmE), / rɪˈkwɛstə / (BrE)
a person who asks for something or makes a request

Examples

A requestor of help
All requestors must submit their applications by Friday.
The library requires requestors to provide identification before accessing special collections.
Each requestor will receive a confirmation email once their form is processed.
The requestor must fill out all sections of the application before submission.
If the requestor needs additional information, they should contact the support team.
Each requestor is assigned a unique identification number for tracking purposes.
