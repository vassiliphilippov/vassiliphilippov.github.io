sux

interjection
slang disappointment
Pronunciation: / sʌks / (AmE), / sʌks / (BrE)
slang exclamation used to express disappointment, sympathy, or annoyance about something bad or unfortunate

Examples

Sux, that's awful
When I found out the concert was canceled, all I could say was sux.
He texted me 'that sux' after hearing about my lost wallet.
It really sux when your plans fall through at the last minute.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awesome - an exclamation expressing something is very good or impressive
  • synonym: bummer - an informal exclamation expressing disappointment or frustration
