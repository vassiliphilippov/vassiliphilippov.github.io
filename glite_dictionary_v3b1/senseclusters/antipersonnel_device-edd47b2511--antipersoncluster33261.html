antipersonnel device

noun
weapon targeting people
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.ti.pɚˈsɒn.əl dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈpɜː.sən.əl dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
A weapon designed to injure or kill people rather than damage equipment or vehicles.

Examples

An antipersonnel device
Engineers are working to develop better detection methods for antipersonnel devices.
Many humanitarian groups have called for a global ban on antipersonnel devices.
The country banned the deployment of antipersonnel devices under international law.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antimaterial device - a weapon designed to target equipment or vehicles rather than people
  • synonym: antipersonnel mine - a land mine designed to injure or kill people
