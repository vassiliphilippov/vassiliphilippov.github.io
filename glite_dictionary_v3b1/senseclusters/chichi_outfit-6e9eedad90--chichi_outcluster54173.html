chichi outfit

noun
fancy or showy clothing
Pronunciation: / ʃiːˈʃiː ˈaʊtfɪt / (AmE), / ʃiːˈʃiː ˈaʊtfɪt / (BrE)
A set of clothes designed to be very fashionable, stylish, or ostentatiously elegant.

Examples

A chichi outfit
He bought a new chichi outfit for the gallery opening downtown.
Many fashion bloggers favor a chichi outfit for high-profile events.
She arrived in a chichi outfit that everyone admired instantly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fancy dress - clothing worn to impress or for special occasions
  • antonym: understated outfit - clothing that is simple and not showy
