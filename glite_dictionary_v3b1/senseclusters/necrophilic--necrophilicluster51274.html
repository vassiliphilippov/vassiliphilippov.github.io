necrophilic

adjective
morbid interest in death
9.9
Pronunciation: /ne.krə.ˈfɪl.ɪk/ (AmE), /ne.krə.ˈfɪl.ɪk/ (BrE)
having a strong, often morbid, interest in or attraction to death, corpses, or funerary practices (not sexual).
