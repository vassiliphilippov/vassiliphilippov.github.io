diverter

noun
traffic control device
6.3
Pronunciation: / daɪˈvɜrtər / (AmE), / daɪˈvɜːtə / (BrE)
a structure or device placed to change the direction of traffic or prevent vehicles from passing through an area

Examples

Traffic diverter
The city installed a diverter to stop cars from using the residential street as a shortcut.
A concrete diverter was placed at the intersection to improve pedestrian safety.
Local residents requested a diverter to reduce heavy traffic in their neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrier - a structure that blocks or limits movement
  • synonym: obstruction - something that blocks a path or makes movement difficult
