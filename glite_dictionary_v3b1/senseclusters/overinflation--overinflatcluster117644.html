overinflation

noun
excessive price rise
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪnˈfleɪʃən / (BrE)
a situation in which prices or values increase excessively or beyond reasonable levels

Examples

Economic overinflation
The government took measures to prevent overinflation from damaging the economy.
Experts warned that overinflation could erode people's savings rapidly.
During the crisis, overinflation made basic goods unaffordable for many citizens.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deflation - a decrease in the general price level of goods and services
  • synonym: hyperinflation - extremely rapid or out of control inflation
