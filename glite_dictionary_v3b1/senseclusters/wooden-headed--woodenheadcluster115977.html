wooden-headed

adjective
stupid; slow-witted
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdən ˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈwʊdən ˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
(informal) slow to understand; stupid or unintelligent.

Examples

A wooden-headed person
Calling someone wooden-headed can come across as quite rude, so be careful when you use it.
Despite her teacher's explanation, he remained wooden-headed and didn't grasp the main idea.
His wooden-headed approach to the problem only made things worse.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clever - quick to understand; intelligent
  • synonym: dull-witted - slow to understand; lacking intelligence
