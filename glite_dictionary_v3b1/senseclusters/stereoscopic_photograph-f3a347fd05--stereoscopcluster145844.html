stereoscopic photograph

noun
3D image pair viewed for depth
5.0 5.9
Pronunciation: /ste.ri.ə.ˈskɑː.pɪk ˈfoʊ.tə.græf/ (AmE), /ste.ri.ə.ˈskɒp.ɪk ˈfəʊ.tə.grɑːf/ (BrE)
