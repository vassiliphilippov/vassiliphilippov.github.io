cheapie

noun
inexpensive item
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːpi / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːpi / (BrE)
an informal word for something that is inexpensive or cheaply made, often of low quality

Examples

A cheapie watch
He picked up a cheapie watch at the market, but it stopped working after a week.
Sometimes a cheapie can be just as useful as a more expensive brand.
They bought a cheapie tent for the festival, knowing it might not last long.
He bought a cheapie umbrella, but it broke in the first rainstorm.
You can find plenty of cheapies at the market, but they may not last long.
Instead of spending a lot, she chose a cheapie phone for her travels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bargain - something bought for less than the usual price
  • synonym: cheap - low in price or quality
  • antonym: luxury - something expensive and of high quality
