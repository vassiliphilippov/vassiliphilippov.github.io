splinter group

noun
group that has split from a larger organization
7.8 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsplɪn.tər gruːp/ (AmE), /ˈsplɪn.tə gruːp/ (BrE)
a small organization that has separated from a larger one, especially because of differences in opinions or beliefs

Examples

A splinter group forms

Negotiators tried to bring the splinter group back into the main organization.
The radical members formed a splinter group after disagreeing with the party leadership.
