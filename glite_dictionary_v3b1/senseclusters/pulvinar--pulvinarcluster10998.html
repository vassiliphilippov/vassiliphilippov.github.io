pulvinar

noun
thalamic brain region
9.9
Pronunciation: / pʌlˈvaɪnɑr / (AmE), / pʊlˈvɪnɑː / (BrE)
a region of the thalamus in the brain that plays a role in processing sensory information, especially vision

Examples

Brain pulvinar region
The pulvinar is important for integrating visual and sensory information in the brain.
Researchers studied how the pulvinar responds to different types of visual stimuli.
Damage to the pulvinar can lead to problems with attention and perception.
