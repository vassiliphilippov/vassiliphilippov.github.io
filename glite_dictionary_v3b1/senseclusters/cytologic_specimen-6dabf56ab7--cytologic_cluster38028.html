cytologic specimen

noun
sample of cells for examination
Pronunciation: / saɪtəˈlɑdʒɪk ˈspɛsɪmən / (AmE), / saɪtəˈlɒdʒɪk ˈspɛsɪmən / (BrE)
A sample of cells collected from a body tissue or fluid, used for examination and diagnosis under a microscope.

Examples

A cytologic specimen sample
A cytologic specimen can help detect cancerous changes at an early stage.
Proper handling of the cytologic specimen is crucial for accurate diagnosis.
The doctor sent the cytologic specimen to the pathology lab for further analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biopsy - sample of tissue taken from the body for examination
