cembalo

noun
a keyboard instrument with plucked strings
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛm.bəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛm.bə.ləʊ / (BrE)
A keyboard musical instrument with strings that are plucked by plectra mounted on pivots, also known as a harpsichord.

Examples

A cembalo instrument
During the concert, the distinct sound of the cembalo filled the hall.
She specializes in early music and often plays the cembalo in her ensembles.
The musician performed a Baroque piece on the cembalo with impressive skill.
