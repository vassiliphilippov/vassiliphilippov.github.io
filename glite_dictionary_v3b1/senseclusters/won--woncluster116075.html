won

adjective
certain of victory; cannot be lost
3.3
Pronunciation: / wʌn / (AmE), / wʌn / (BrE)
(of a contest, game, or situation) certain of victory; secured and no longer subject to loss or defeat.

Examples

A won game
After scoring that goal, it was a won match for their team.
She relaxed once she realized it was a won battle.
With all the key moves played, it’s essentially a won game for us now.
