sham

noun
something fake or false
8.2
Pronunciation: / ʃæm / (AmE), / ʃæm / (BrE)
a thing or person that is not genuine; a fake or someone pretending to be something they are not

Examples

A sham document
He exposed the document as a complete sham after a careful review.
Many believed the charity was a sham set up to steal donations.
The entire contest turned out to be a sham and no prize was ever awarded.
He was exposed as a sham after his lies came to light.
Many people were taken in by the sham posing as a charity worker.
The crowd soon realized the speaker was a sham with no real qualifications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fraud - an act of deceiving or misrepresenting; something intended to deceive
  • antonym: genuine - something real or authentic
  • synonym: imposter - a person who pretends to be someone else in order to deceive
