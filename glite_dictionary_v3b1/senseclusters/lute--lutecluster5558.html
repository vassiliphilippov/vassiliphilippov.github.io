lute

noun
a pear-shaped, plucked string instrument
8.6 0.8/m
Pronunciation: / lut / (AmE), / luÀêt / (BrE)
A musical instrument with a pear-shaped body, a bent neck, and a fretted fingerboard, typically played by plucking the strings.

Examples

Play the lute
During the concert, the musician played the lute with exceptional delicacy.
Medieval paintings often depict angels holding a lute among other musical instruments.
She spent years learning to play the lute as it was popular in Renaissance music.
