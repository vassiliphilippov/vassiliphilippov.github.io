glasslike

adjective
dull or blank appearance
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a dull, expressionless, or emotionless appearance, especially referring to the eyes or gaze.

Examples

Glasslike eyes
Her glasslike eyes stared ahead, giving no hint of what she was feeling.
After hearing the shocking news, his expression became glasslike and distant.
The patient’s glasslike gaze worried the nurse, who called for the doctor immediately.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lively - full of life, energy, or emotion
  • synonym: vacant - showing no sign of thought or emotion
