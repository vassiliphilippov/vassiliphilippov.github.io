lurchingly

adverb
with unsteady movement
Pronunciation: / ˈlɝːtʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈlɜːtʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner characterized by sudden, unsteady, or jerky movements

Examples

Lurchingly down the road
The old truck moved lurchingly over the rocky path, making everyone bounce in their seats.
He walked lurchingly after twisting his ankle during the hike.
The boat advanced lurchingly through the rough waves during the storm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smoothly - in a steady and even manner
  • synonym: unsteadily - in a way that lacks stability or balance
