lurchingly

adverb
with unsteady movement
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈlɜːr.tʃɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈlɜː.tʃɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)

Examples

He walked lurchingly after twisting his ankle during the hike.
The boat advanced lurchingly through the rough waves during the storm.
