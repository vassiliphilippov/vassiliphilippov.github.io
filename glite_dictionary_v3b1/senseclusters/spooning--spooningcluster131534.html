spooning

noun
moving food with spoon
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspuːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspuːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of moving or serving food using a spoon, often involving repeated spoonfuls

Examples

Spooning soup
The child's noisy spooning of cereal woke everyone in the kitchen.
She finished the spooning of soup into the bowls before calling everyone to the table.
Careful spooning of the sauce ensured each plate looked perfect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: serving - the act of giving out food or drink
