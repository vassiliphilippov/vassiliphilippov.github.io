sea bathing

noun
bathing or swimming in the sea
Pronunciation: / si ˈbeɪ.ðɪŋ / (AmE), / siː ˈbeɪ.ðɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of swimming or bathing in the sea, especially for pleasure or health reasons

Examples

Sea bathing in summer
Children enjoy sea bathing during their family holidays at the coast.
Many doctors in the 1800s recommended sea bathing to improve health.
Victorian resorts became popular for their sea bathing and fresh air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bathing - the act of immersing the body in water for cleansing or recreation
  • synonym: swimming - the sport or activity of moving through water by using the limbs
