myasthenia

noun
muscular weakness disorder
Pronunciation: / maɪ.əsˈθi.ni.ə /; / ˌmaɪ.əsˈθiː.ni.ə / (AmE), / maɪ.æsˈθiː.nɪ.ə /; / ˌmaɪ.asˈθiː.nɪ.ə / (BrE)
a medical condition that causes muscular weakness and fatigue, especially in the face and neck
