experiential

adjective
based on personal experience
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌspɪriˈɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ɪkˌspɪəriˈɛnʃəl / (BrE)
relating to or based on experience, especially as opposed to theory

Examples

An experiential learning
Her experiential knowledge of the culture came from years of traveling abroad.
The course takes an experiential approach to learning by involving students in real-world projects.
They designed an experiential workshop that encouraged participants to learn by doing.
Her experiential knowledge of the culture made her insights unique.
Some people prefer theoretical study, while others learn best through experiential methods.
The workshop offers an experiential approach to learning new skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: empirical - based on observation or experiment rather than theory
