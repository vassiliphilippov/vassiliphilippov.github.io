headmastership

noun
the position of headmaster
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌmæstərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌmɑːstəʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or tenure of being a headmaster, especially in a school

Examples

The headmastership of a school
He was proud to accept the headmastership of the prestigious grammar school.
Her headmastership was marked by significant reforms and higher academic standards.
The committee is searching for someone suitable for the headmastership after the current principal retires.

Related Terms

  • synonym: headmaster - the male principal of a school, especially in the UK
