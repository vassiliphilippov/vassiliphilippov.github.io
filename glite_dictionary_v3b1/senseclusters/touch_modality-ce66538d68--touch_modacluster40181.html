touch modality

noun
faculty of perceiving touch
Pronunciation: / tʌtʃ məˈdæl.ə.ti / (AmE), / tʌtʃ məˈdæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the ability to perceive external objects or forces through physical contact, especially with the hands; the sense of touch

Examples

The touch modality sense
In some neurological disorders, the touch modality may be impaired or lost entirely.
Researchers study how the brain processes information from the touch modality to understand sensory integration.
The touch modality enables us to identify textures and shapes without using our eyes.

  • synonym: touch - the sense by which objects are perceived through contact
