obtusely

adverb
in a stupid or insensitive way
8.0 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / …ôbÀàtusli / (AmE), / …ôbÀàtjuÀêsli / (BrE)
In a manner that is slow to understand, insensitive, or lacking sharpness of intellect; stupidly or insensitively.

Examples

To obtusely misunderstand
He obtusely misunderstood the instructions, causing unnecessary confusion.
She obtusely ignored the obvious hints that something was wrong.
The manager responded obtusely to the team's concerns, missing the real issue.

Synonyms

  • uncomprehendingly
    8.1 0.1/m
    uncomprehendingly shows someone fails to understand what is said or happening, while obtusely often adds dullness or social insensitivity.
  • dumbly
    7.5 0.04/m
    dumbly acts or looks stupid and unthinking, while obtusely also suggests slow thought and a lack of social sensitivity.
  • unintelligently
    7.0 0.02/m
    unintelligently means doing something with poor judgment or low skill, while obtusely stresses slowness of understanding and insensitivity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bluntly - in a direct and uncompromising way, sometimes insensitively
  • antonym: sensitively - in a manner showing awareness and consideration for others' feelings
