leadenly

adverb
in a dull, heavy way
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛdənli / (AmE), / ˈlɛdənli / (BrE)
in a dull, heavy, or gray manner, especially describing the appearance of light, sky, or color.

Examples

Clouds hang leadenly
The sky hung leadenly over the city, threatening rain all afternoon.
She spoke leadenly, her voice heavy with disappointment.
Mist drifted leadenly through the quiet streets at dawn.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brightly - in a way that is full of light or color
  • synonym: dully - in a way that lacks brightness or liveliness
