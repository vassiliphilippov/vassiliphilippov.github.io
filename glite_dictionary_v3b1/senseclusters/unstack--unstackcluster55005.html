unstack

verb
to widen data format
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstæk / (AmE), / ʌnˈstæk / (BrE)
to convert data from a long (stacked) format into a wide format by turning rows into columns, especially in data analysis.

Examples

To unstack data columns
You can unstack the data frame to make each category appear as a separate column.
After importing the dataset, she decided to unstack it for easier analysis.
The analyst used a script to unstack the results and present them in a wide format.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pivot - to reorganize data by turning unique values into columns
  • antonym: stack - to convert data from a wide format to a long (stacked) format
