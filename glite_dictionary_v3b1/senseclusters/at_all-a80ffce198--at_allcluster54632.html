Synonyms

  • in the least
    4.4 3.08/m
    in the least is used mainly in negative sentences to mean not even a bit, while at all appears in many negative or question forms.
  • in the slightest
    3.8 1.06/m
    in the slightest sounds stronger and more emphatic in speech, while at all is a neutral way to stress lack or degree.
  • the least bit
    3.7 2.22/m
    the least bit refers to a very small amount in speech, while at all is the standard idiom for any amount in negatives.
  • even a little
    1.3 2.60/m
    even a little is a plain literal phrase for a small amount, while at all is the fixed idiom used in negatives and questions.
