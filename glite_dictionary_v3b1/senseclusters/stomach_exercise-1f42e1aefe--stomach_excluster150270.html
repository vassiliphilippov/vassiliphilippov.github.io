stomach exercise

noun
exercise for strengthening abdominal muscles
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌmək ˌɛksərˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈstʌmək ˌɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
A physical exercise intended to strengthen the muscles of the abdomen.

Examples

A stomach exercise routine
Leg raises are a popular type of stomach exercise for toning the abs.
She does a series of stomach exercises every morning before breakfast.
The fitness instructor recommended adding more stomach exercises to my routine to improve core strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crunch - an abdominal exercise involving lifting the upper body toward the knees
  • synonym: sit-up - an exercise in which a person lies on the back and lifts the upper body
