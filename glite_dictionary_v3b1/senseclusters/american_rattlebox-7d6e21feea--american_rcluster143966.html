American rattlebox

noun
tropical plant with rattling seed pods
9.0
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈrætlˌbɑks / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈræt(ə)lbɒks / (BrE)
A tropical American annual herb with inflated pods containing ripe seeds that rattle when shaken.
