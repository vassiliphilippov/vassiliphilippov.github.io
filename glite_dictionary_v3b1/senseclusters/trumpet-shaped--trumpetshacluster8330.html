trumpet-shaped

adjective
shaped like a trumpet
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌmpɪt ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪt ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of a trumpet, typically flaring outward from a narrow base to a wide opening.

The morning glories bloomed in clusters of vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers.
