hotkey

verb
to assign shortcut key
4.2
Pronunciation: /hɑːt.ˈkiː/ (AmE), /hɒt.ˈkiː/ (BrE)
to assign a function or command to a specific key or key combination, or to activate a function by pressing such a key.

Examples

To hotkey a command
You can hotkey your favorite tools for faster access in the software.
She hotkeyed the save function to F2 so she could work more efficiently.
Many gamers hotkey important actions to make gameplay smoother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shortcut - a key or combination of keys that performs a command quickly
  • antonym: unbind - to remove a function from a key or key combination
