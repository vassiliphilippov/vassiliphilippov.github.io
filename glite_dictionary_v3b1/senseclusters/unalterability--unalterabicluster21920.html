unalterability

noun
state of being unchangeable
6.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˌɔltɚəˈbɪlɪti /; / ˌʌnˌɔltərəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ʌnˌɔːltərəˈbɪlɪti /; / ˌʌnˌɔːltərəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being impossible to change or alter
