ikat

noun
patterned dyed fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈiːkɑt / (AmE), / ˈiːkæt / (BrE)
a fabric featuring patterns created by resist-dyeing the threads before weaving

Examples

An ikat scarf
The artisan displayed a beautiful ikat scarf with intricate geometric designs.
Many traditional markets in Indonesia sell handmade ikat textiles.
She decorated her living room with colorful ikat cushions from Southeast Asia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: batik - a fabric dyed using wax-resist techniques
  • synonym: tie-dye - a method of dyeing fabric with patterns by tying sections
