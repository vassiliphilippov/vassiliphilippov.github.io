phlegmatically

adverb
in a calm or unemotional manner
8.3
Pronunciation: / flɛɡˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / flɛɡˈmatɪkli / (BrE)
In a calm, unemotional, or indifferent manner; showing little emotion or reaction.

Examples

To act phlegmatically
Despite the chaos, he continued his work phlegmatically and without any sign of stress.
She handled the criticism phlegmatically, showing no visible reaction.
The announcement was received phlegmatically by the team, who showed little surprise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calmly - in a calm manner
  • antonym: emotionally - in an emotional manner
