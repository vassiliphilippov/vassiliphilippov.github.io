life-giving

adjective
giving or sustaining life
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪfˌɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪfˌɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
having the power to give or sustain life; vitalizing or nourishing.

Examples

Life-giving rain
Farmers depend on the life-giving rain each season to nourish their crops.
Sunlight is often described as a life-giving force for all living creatures.
The life-giving waters of the river made the valley bloom with vegetation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vital - essential to life or necessary for existence
