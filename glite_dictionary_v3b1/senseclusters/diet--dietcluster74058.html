diet

noun
prescribed selection of foods
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪət /; / ˈdaɪ.ət / (AmE), / ˈdaɪət /; / ˈdaɪ.ət / (BrE)
a special or restricted way of eating, often for health or weight loss

Examples

A strict diet
After his surgery, Mark followed a strict diet to help with his recovery.
Many people start a new diet in January to improve their health.
The doctor prescribed a low-sodium diet to control her blood pressure.
After her doctor’s advice, she decided to go on a strict diet.
He lost weight by reducing sugar and following a low-carb diet.
Many people find it hard to stick to a diet during the holidays.
