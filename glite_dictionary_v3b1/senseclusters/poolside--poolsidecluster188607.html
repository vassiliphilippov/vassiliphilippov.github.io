poolside

noun
area beside pool
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpuːlˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈpuːlˌsaɪd / (BrE)
the area immediately next to a swimming pool, often used for sitting or relaxing

Examples

Breakfast at the poolside
We enjoyed breakfast by the poolside before going for a swim.
The hotel offers comfortable chairs and umbrellas at the poolside for guests.
Children played games on the poolside while their parents watched nearby.
