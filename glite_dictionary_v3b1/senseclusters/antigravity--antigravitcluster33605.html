antigravity

adjective
counteracting gravity
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈɡrævəti / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈɡrævɪti / (BrE)
describing a device, system, or technology that counteracts the force of gravity or enables levitation

Examples

Antigravity boots
The engineers designed an antigravity vehicle for rapid transportation across rough terrain.
Science fiction often features antigravity boots that allow characters to walk on walls or ceilings.
Researchers are exploring antigravity technologies to make lifting heavy objects easier in the future.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gravitational - relating to or caused by gravity
  • synonym: levitating - rising or floating in the air without support
