inspectability

noun
ability to be inspected
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌspɛktəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ɪnˌspɛktəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being able to be inspected or examined

Examples

The inspectability of software
The inspectability of the new software makes it easier for auditors to review its processes.
Engineers improved the inspectability of the machine by adding clear panels and access points.
High inspectability is essential for products that require regular safety checks.
