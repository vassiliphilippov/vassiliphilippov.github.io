traffic

noun
social or verbal interchange
Pronunciation: / ˈtræf.ɪk / (AmE), / ˈtræf.ɪk / (BrE)
communication or exchange, especially of ideas or messages, between people (usually followed by 'with').

Examples

Traffic with strangers
He avoided traffic with those whose morals he distrusted.
The university was a place of lively intellectual traffic between scholars from around the world.
There was a constant traffic of ideas between the two writers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: communication - the act of conveying information or ideas
