scorpionfish

noun
marine fish with armored head and venomous spines
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔːr.pi.ənˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈskɔː.pi.ənˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a marine fish with a tapering body, spiny armored head, and venomous spines, found especially in warm seas
