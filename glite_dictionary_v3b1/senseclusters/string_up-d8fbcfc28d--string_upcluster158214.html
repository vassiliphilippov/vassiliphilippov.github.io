string up

verb
kill by hanging
6.4
Pronunciation: / strɪŋ ʌp / (AmE), / strɪŋ ʌp / (BrE)
to kill (someone) by hanging them, especially as a form of punishment or execution

Examples

To string up a criminal

The vigilante mob threatened to string up the horse thief outside the courthouse.
