piney

adjective
from Piney Woods region
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪni / (AmE), / ˈpaɪni / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the Piney Woods region in the southern United States, or coming from that region

Examples

Piney woods region

Local dishes often feature ingredients unique to the piney region.
