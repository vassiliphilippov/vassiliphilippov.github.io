Newtonian

noun
follower of Isaac Newton
Pronunciation: / nuːˈtoʊniən / (AmE), / njuːˈtəʊniən / (BrE)
A person who follows the ideas or principles of Isaac Newton, especially in science or philosophy.

Examples

A Newtonian scientist

Many philosophers in the 18th century identified themselves as Newtonians.
The Newtonians revolutionized physics by emphasizing empirical evidence and mathematics.
