trapezium bone

noun
wrist bone near the base of the thumb
4.7 8.3
Pronunciation: / trəˈpiziəm boʊn / (AmE), / trəˈpiːziəm bəʊn / (BrE)
the carpal bone on the thumb side of the wrist that articulates with the first and second metacarpal bones

Examples

The trapezium bone in wrist
An injury to the trapezium bone can severely impact your ability to grip objects.
Arthritis in the trapezium bone is a common cause of thumb pain in older adults.
The trapezium bone is crucial in allowing the thumb its wide range of motion.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: carpal bone - any of the small bones forming the wrist
  • synonym: trapezoid bone - a neighboring carpal bone in the wrist near the trapezium bone
