autoimmunity

noun
immune response against one's own tissues
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtəʊɪˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊɪˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
a condition in which the immune system produces antibodies against the body's own tissues, leading to autoimmune disease or hypersensitivity reactions

Examples

Autoimmunity causes disease
Researchers are studying the genetic factors that contribute to autoimmunity in different populations.
Rheumatoid arthritis is a disorder caused by autoimmunity, where the body's own immune system attacks the joints.
Symptoms caused by autoimmunity can vary widely depending on which organs are affected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autoimmune disease - a disease caused by autoimmunity
