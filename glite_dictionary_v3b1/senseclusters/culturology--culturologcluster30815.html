culturology

noun
study of cultures
Pronunciation: / ˌkʌltʃəˈrɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌkʌltʃəˈrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the academic study of cultures, their development, and their influence on societies

Examples

Culturology study
She decided to major in culturology at university.
The conference brought together experts in culturology from around the world.
His research in culturology focuses on how traditions shape modern societies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropology - the study of human societies and cultures and their development
  • synonym: sociology - the study of society, social relationships, and institutions
