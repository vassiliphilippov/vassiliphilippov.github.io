unplanned

adjective
not done intentionally; not planned
3.3 3.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈplænd / (AmE), / ʌnˈplænd / (BrE)
not planned, arranged, or intended; happening without preparation or by chance

Examples

An unplanned event
An unplanned detour led us to a charming little cafe we never would have found otherwise.
The meeting was entirely unplanned and took everyone by surprise.
Their child was unplanned, but they welcomed the new addition to their family with joy.
An unplanned event forced the organizers to change their schedule.
She was surprised by her unplanned visit to the city.
The unplanned meeting led to some interesting new ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accidental - happening by chance, not planned
  • antonym: intentional - done on purpose or deliberately
  • antonym: planned - arranged or decided in advance
  • synonym: spontaneous - performed or occurring as a result of a sudden impulse or inclination
