unexceptionally

adverb
in an ordinary way
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪkˈsɛpʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈsɛpʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that is not unusual, remarkable, or out of the ordinary

Examples

Ended unexceptionally
The ceremony ended unexceptionally, with no surprises or unexpected events.
He performed unexceptionally on the test, neither excelling nor failing.
Most days pass unexceptionally, following the same routine as always.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exceptionally - in a way that is unusual or remarkable
  • synonym: ordinarily - in a usual or normal way
