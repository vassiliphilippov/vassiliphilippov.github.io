tonneau

noun
truck bed cover
9.9
Pronunciation: / təˈnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɒnəʊ / (BrE)
a cover for the open cargo area of a pickup truck or similar vehicle, used to protect contents from weather or theft

Examples

Tonneau cover for truck
He bought a new tonneau to keep his camping gear dry in the back of his truck.
The mechanic recommended installing a tonneau for better security and improved fuel efficiency.
After adding a tonneau, the tools in the pickup stayed safe from rain and sun.
