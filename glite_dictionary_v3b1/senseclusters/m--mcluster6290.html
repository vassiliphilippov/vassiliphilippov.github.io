m

noun
symbol for meter (SI unit of length)
Pronunciation: / ɛm / (AmE), / ɛm / (BrE)
the symbol for the meter, the basic unit of length in the International System of Units (SI), equal to approximately 1.094 yards

Examples

m of length
One m is approximately equal to 3.281 feet.
She ran a 100 m race in under 12 seconds.
The swimming pool is 50 m long and perfect for competitions.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cm - symbol for centimeter, a unit equal to one hundredth of a meter
  • degree higher: km - symbol for kilometer, a unit equal to 1000 meters
  • synonym: meter - the basic SI unit of length, equal to 100 centimeters
