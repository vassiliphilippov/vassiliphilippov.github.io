broodiness

noun
desire to hatch eggs
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːdinəs / (AmE), / ˈbruːdinəs / (BrE)
the state of wanting to sit on eggs to hatch them, or a strong desire to have children

Examples

A hen's broodiness
The hen's broodiness made her stay on the nest for days.
Some people experience a sudden broodiness when they see babies or young animals.
Farmers sometimes try to reduce broodiness in hens to increase egg production.

Related Terms

  • antonym: infertility - the inability to have children
  • synonym: maternal instinct - a natural desire to care for and nurture offspring
