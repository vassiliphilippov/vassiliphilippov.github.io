angiomatosis

noun
vascular tissue overgrowth
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒioʊməˈtoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌandʒiəʊməˈtəʊsɪs /; / ˌændʒiəʊməˈtəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a medical condition where many abnormal blood or lymphatic vessels grow in an organ or tissue
