theological

adjective
relating to theology or religious doctrine
Pronunciation: / ˌθi.əˈlɑ.dʒɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌθiː.əˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of religion and the nature of God; concerning theology or religious doctrine.

Examples

A theological study
His theological writings influenced generations of scholars.
She attended a theological seminary to prepare for ministry work.
The debate focused on theological differences between the two denominations.
