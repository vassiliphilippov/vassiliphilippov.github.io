pingback

verb
to notify linked site
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪŋ.bæk/ (AmE), /ˈpɪŋ.bæk/ (BrE)
to send an automatic notification to another website or server indicating that you have linked to its content

Examples

To pingback a blog
If you link to another blog post, your site will automatically pingback the original author.
The system is set up to pingback any referenced articles as soon as they are published.
After updating her website, she made sure it would pingback all the sources she cited.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alert - to warn or inform about something
  • synonym: notify - to inform someone officially
