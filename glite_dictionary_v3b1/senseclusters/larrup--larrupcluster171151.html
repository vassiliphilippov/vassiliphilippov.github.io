larrup

verb
strike or spank as punishment
Pronunciation: / ˈlæɹəp / (AmE), / ˈlarəp / (BrE)
to hit or beat someone, especially as a punishment; to spank

Examples

To larrup a child
In the old days, teachers would sometimes larrup unruly students as discipline.
She jokingly warned that she would larrup her brother if he stole her book again.
When the boys misbehaved, their father threatened to larrup them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spank - to strike someone, especially a child, on the buttocks with an open hand as punishment
