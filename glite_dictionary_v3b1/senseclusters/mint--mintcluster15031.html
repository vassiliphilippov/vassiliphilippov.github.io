mint

noun
a candy flavored with mint oil
Pronunciation: / mɪnt / (AmE), / mɪnt / (BrE)
a small, flavored candy made with mint oil, often eaten to freshen the breath.

Examples

A mint candy
He offered me a mint after lunch to freshen my breath.
She keeps a tin of mints in her purse for long meetings.
Would you like a mint with your coffee?

Related Terms

  • synonym: lozenge - a small medicinal tablet, often dissolving in the mouth like a mint
  • synonym: peppermint - a type of mint-flavored candy or the flavoring itself
