malnourish

verb
to provide with inadequate nourishment
Pronunciation: / mælˈnɜrɪʃ / (AmE), / mælˈnʌrɪʃ / (BrE)
to provide (a person or animal) with insufficient or inadequate nourishment, leading to poor health or development

Examples

To malnourish a child
If you malnourish a pet, it may develop severe health problems.
Poor diet and illness can malnourish children, affecting their growth and development.
The drought continued to malnourish the entire population of the village.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: starve - to suffer or die from lack of food
  • synonym: undernourish - to provide with too little nourishment
