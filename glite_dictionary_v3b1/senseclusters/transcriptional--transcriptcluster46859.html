transcriptional

adjective
related to gene copying
7.4
Pronunciation: / trænˈskrɪpʃənl̩ / (AmE), / trænˈskrɪpʃənl̩ / (BrE)
relating to the process of copying genetic information from DNA to RNA

Examples

Transcriptional activity in genes
The transcriptional regulation of genes is crucial for cell development.
Scientists measured the transcriptional response to the new drug in the laboratory.
Changes in transcriptional activity can indicate how a cell reacts to stress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genetic - relating to genes or heredity
  • antonym: translational - relating to the process of translating RNA into protein
