lymphangiography

noun
X-ray examination of lymphatic system with contrast medium
9.5
Pronunciation: / lɪmˌfændʒiˈɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / lɪmˌfan.dʒiˈɒɡ.rə.fi / (BrE)
an X-ray examination of lymph nodes and lymph vessels following the injection of a radiopaque contrast medium

Examples

Lymphangiography X-ray test
Lymphangiography can detect blockages or abnormalities in the lymphatic system.
Advances in imaging technology have made lymphangiography safer and more precise.
The doctor ordered a lymphangiography to help diagnose the patient's swollen lymph nodes.
