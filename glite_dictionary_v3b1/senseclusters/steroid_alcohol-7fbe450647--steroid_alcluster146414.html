steroid alcohol

noun
waxy steroid compound with an alcohol group
7.8
Pronunciation: / stəˈrɔɪd ˈæl.kəˌhɔl / (AmE), / stɪəˈrɔɪd ˈæl.kəˌhɒl / (BrE)
any of a class of waxy, insoluble organic compounds found in plants or animals, with a structure based on steroids and containing an alcohol group (such as cholesterol).

Examples

Common steroid alcohol
Cholesterol is a common steroid alcohol essential for cell membrane structure.
Plants produce a variety of steroid alcohols that help regulate growth and development.
Researchers studied the role of steroid alcohol derivatives in metabolic disorders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sterol - any of a subgroup of steroids with a hydroxyl group, including cholesterol
