distractive

adjective
causing distraction
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstræktɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈstræktɪv / (BrE)
causing someone to lose focus or attention

Examples

Distractive phone alerts
The constant notifications on her phone were extremely distractive while she was studying.
He found the bright lights in the office to be distractive during meetings.
Wearing headphones helped block out the distractive noises from the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distracting - taking attention away from something
  • antonym: focusing - helping to concentrate attention
