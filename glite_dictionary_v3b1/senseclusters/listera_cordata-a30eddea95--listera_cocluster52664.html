Listera cordata

noun
a species of small orchid
Pronunciation: / lɪˈstɛrə kɔrˈdeɪtə / (AmE), / lɪˈstɪərə kɔːˈdeɪtə / (BrE)
A species of small orchid with two heart-shaped leaves and a loose cluster of green to rust-colored flowers, found in Europe, Asia, North America, and Greenland.
