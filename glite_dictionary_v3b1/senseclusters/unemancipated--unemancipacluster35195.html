unemancipated

adjective
not liberated
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪˈmænsɪˌpeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈmænsɪpeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not socially or personally liberated, often holding traditional or restrictive views about behavior or rights

Examples

Unemancipated views
His unemancipated beliefs about gender roles often led to disagreements with his friends.
The community remained largely unemancipated, resisting changes in social norms.
She was criticized for her unemancipated opinions on education and women's rights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conservative - favoring traditional views and values
  • antonym: emancipated - socially or personally liberated; free from traditional restrictions
