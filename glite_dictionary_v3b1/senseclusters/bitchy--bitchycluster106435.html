bitchy

adjective
spiteful or malicious (especially remark)
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtʃi / (AmE), / ˈbɪtʃi / (BrE)
(of a remark or behavior) spiteful, malicious, or mean-spirited, especially in a petty or snide way.

Examples

Bitchy remark
His bitchy attitude turned a casual conversation into an argument.
She made a bitchy comment about her coworker's dress during the meeting.
Sometimes people send bitchy messages online without considering how hurtful they are.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catty - spiteful in a sly or petty way
  • antonym: kind - having a friendly, generous, and considerate nature
