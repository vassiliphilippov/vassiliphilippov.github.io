convectional

adjective
caused by convection
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɛkʃənəl / (AmE), / kənˈvɛkʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by convection, especially the movement of heat or air

Examples

Convectional rainfall
The area is known for its convectional rainfall, which occurs almost every afternoon in summer.
Warm air rising from the ground leads to convectional currents that can form clouds.
Meteorologists study convectional processes to better predict weather patterns in tropical regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convective - relating to the transfer of heat by convection
