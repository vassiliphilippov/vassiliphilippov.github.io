tabulator

noun
device that counts occurrences
Pronunciation: / təˈbjuːleɪtər /; / təˈbjuː.leɪ.tər / (AmE), / təˈbjuːleɪtə /; / təˈbjuː.leɪ.tə / (BrE)
a device or software program that counts, processes, or arranges data into tables or columns
