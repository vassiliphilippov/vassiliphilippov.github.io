laparoscope

noun
surgical instrument for abdominal examination
5.7 7.2
Pronunciation: / ləˈpɛrəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ləˈparəskəʊp / (BrE)
a slender instrument inserted through the abdominal wall for examining abdominal organs or performing minor surgery

Examples

A laparoscope instrument
Advances in laparoscope technology have made minimally invasive surgery safer and more effective.
After inserting the laparoscope, the medical team carefully examined the liver and intestines.
The surgeon used a laparoscope to view the patient's abdominal cavity during the procedure.
