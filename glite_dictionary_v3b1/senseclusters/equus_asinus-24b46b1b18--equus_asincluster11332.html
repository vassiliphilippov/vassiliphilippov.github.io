Equus asinus

noun
scientific name for the donkey
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkwəs ˈæsɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈɛkwəs ˈæsɪnəs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the donkey, a domesticated animal used as a beast of burden, noted for its patience and stubbornness.
