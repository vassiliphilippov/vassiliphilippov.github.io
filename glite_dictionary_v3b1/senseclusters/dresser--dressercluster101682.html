dresser

noun
cabinet with shelves for storage/display
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛsər / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsə / (BrE)
a piece of furniture with shelves, often with drawers or cupboards below, used for storing and displaying items such as dishes or kitchenware, especially in a kitchen or dining room

Examples

Kitchen dresser
In many traditional homes, a dresser serves as both storage and a place to show off family heirlooms.
She displayed her collection of china plates on the oak dresser in the dining room.
The antique dresser had beautifully carved shelves and drawers for storing utensils.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cabinet - a cupboard with shelves or drawers for storage or display
  • synonym: sideboard - a piece of dining room furniture for storing dishes and serving food
