rash

adjective
reckless; lacking caution
1.9 3.5
Pronunciation: /ræʃ/ (AmE), /ræʃ/ (BrE)
acting or done without careful consideration of the possible consequences; reckless or impulsive

Examples

Rash decision
Climbing without proper equipment would be incredibly rash.
It was rash of him to invest all his money in one company.
She made a rash decision and regretted it later.
He regretted making such a rash decision without considering the consequences.
Her rash promise left her with no way to back out later.
It would be rash to invest all your savings in a single company.

Related Terms

  • antonym: careful - giving thought to the possible consequences of an action; exercising caution
  • antonym: cautious - careful to avoid potential problems or danger
  • synonym: reckless - without thinking or caring about the consequences of an action
