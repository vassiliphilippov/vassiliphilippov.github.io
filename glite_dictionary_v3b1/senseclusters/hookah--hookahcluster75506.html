hookah

noun
water pipe for smoking (esp. tobacco)
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊkə / (AmE), / ˈhʊkɑː / (BrE)
an oriental water pipe for smoking, in which smoke passes through water before being inhaled

Examples

A hookah pipe
Many cafes in the Middle East offer guests the chance to smoke a hookah after dinner.
She bought a beautifully decorated hookah as a souvenir from her trip to Istanbul.
They gathered in the lounge, sharing stories while passing around a hookah filled with flavored tobacco.
