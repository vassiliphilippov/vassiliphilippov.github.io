Battle of Granicus River

noun
first major victory of Alexander over Persians (334 BC)
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbæt.əl əv grə.ˈnaɪ.kəs ˈrɪv.ər/ (AmE), /ˈbæt.əl əv grə.ˈnaɪ.kəs ˈrɪv.ə/ (BrE)
the first major battle won by Alexander the Great against the Persian Empire in 334 BC, marking the beginning of his conquest of Asia Minor
