architecture

noun
structure of computer hardware or software
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃər / (AmE), / ˈɑːkɪtɛktʃə / (BrE)
(computing) The design and organization of a computer's hardware or system software.

Examples

Computer architecture
The company's new processor uses a completely different architecture from previous models.
Their engineers are developing an innovative architecture for high-performance servers.
Understanding the software architecture is essential for debugging complex systems.
