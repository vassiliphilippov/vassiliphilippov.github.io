citable

adjective
able to be referenced
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsaɪtəbəl / (BrE)
able to be cited or referenced, especially in academic or formal writing

Examples

Citable sources only
Only citable sources should be used in your research paper.
The article was not considered citable by the academic journal due to lack of evidence.
Before including a fact, check if it comes from a citable publication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quotable - suitable for being quoted or cited
  • antonym: uncitable - not able to be cited or referenced
