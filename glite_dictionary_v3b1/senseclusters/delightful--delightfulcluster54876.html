delightful

adjective
greatly pleasing or entertaining
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlaɪtfəl / (AmE), / dɪˈlaɪtfəl / (BrE)
Causing great pleasure and enjoyment; very pleasing or entertaining.

Examples

A delightful laugh
Her delightful laugh made everyone in the room smile.
The children's performance was absolutely delightful and entertained the whole audience.
We spent a delightful afternoon walking through the gardens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charming - very pleasant or attractive
  • antonym: unpleasant - not enjoyable or giving pleasure; disagreeable
