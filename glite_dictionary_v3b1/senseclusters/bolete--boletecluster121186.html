bolete

noun
a type of mushroom in the Boletaceae family
9.9
Pronunciation: / boʊˈliːt / (AmE), / bəˈliːt / (BrE)
a mushroom of the family Boletaceae, typically having a thick stem and sponge-like pores beneath the cap instead of gills
