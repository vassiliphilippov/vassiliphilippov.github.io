under attack

adjective
being targeted by assault or criticism
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚ əˈtæk / (AmE), / ˈʌndə əˈtæk / (BrE)
being the target of physical assault, criticism, or censure; being assaulted or criticized

Examples

Under attack by critics
She felt under attack when the panel questioned her decisions so aggressively.
The ancient city came under attack at dawn, with enemy troops approaching from all sides.
The company was under attack from environmental groups for its pollution record.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defended - protected from attack or criticism
  • synonym: under fire - being subject to criticism or attack
