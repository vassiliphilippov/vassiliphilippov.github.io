culinarian

noun
skilled cook
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkʌlɪˈnɛriən / (AmE), / ˌkʌlɪˈneəriən / (BrE)
a person who is skilled in preparing and cooking food

Examples

A skilled culinarian
The culinarian impressed everyone with her creative use of spices and fresh ingredients.
After years of training, he became a respected culinarian in the city's top restaurant.
Aspiring to be a culinarian, she enrolled in a prestigious cooking school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chef - a professional cook, especially the chief cook in a restaurant or hotel
  • synonym: cook - a person who prepares food as a job or hobby
