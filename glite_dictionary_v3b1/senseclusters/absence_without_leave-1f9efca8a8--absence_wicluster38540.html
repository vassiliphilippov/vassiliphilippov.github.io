absence without leave

noun
unapproved absence from duty
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæbsəns wɪðˌaʊt ˈliv / (AmE), / ˈæbsəns wɪðˌaʊt ˈliːv / (BrE)
the act of being away from one's post, especially in the armed forces, without official permission; unauthorized absence from duty

Examples
