MIPS

noun
processor architecture
Pronunciation: / mɪps / (AmE), / mɪps / (BrE)

Examples

MIPS processor chip
Many routers and printers use a MIPS processor for efficient performance.

Older game consoles often relied on MIPS chips for their main processing tasks.
