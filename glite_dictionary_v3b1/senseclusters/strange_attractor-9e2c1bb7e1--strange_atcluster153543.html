strange attractor

noun
complex pattern in chaotic systems
Pronunciation: / streɪndʒ əˈtræktər / (AmE), / streɪndʒ əˈtræktə / (BrE)
In chaos theory, a complex pattern in a dynamical system toward which the system tends to evolve, where the approach is chaotic rather than predictable.
