funnel

noun
ship's smokestack or chimney
6.1 5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌnəl / (AmE), / ˈfʌnəl / (BrE)
(Nautical) The tall chimney on a ship, used to expel smoke and gases from boilers or engines.

Examples

Ship funnel
