stopover

noun
temporary stop during a journey
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑpˌoʊvər /; / ˈstɑːpˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈstɒpˌəʊvə / (BrE)
a short stay at a place between parts of a journey, usually when traveling long distances

Examples

A stopover in Paris
Due to delays, our stopover in Paris was much longer than expected.
The journey included a two-day stopover in Rome for sightseeing.
We had a brief stopover in Singapore before continuing our flight to Sydney.
During their European trip, they arranged a brief stopover in Paris.
Our journey included a stopover in Tokyo to break up the long flight.
We had a six-hour stopover in Dubai before our connecting flight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: layover - a period of waiting between parts of a journey, especially in air travel
