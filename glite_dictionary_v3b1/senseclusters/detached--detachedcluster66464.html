detached

adjective
not fixed or connected; separated
4.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtætʃt / (AmE), / dɪˈtætʃt / (BrE)
separated or disconnected from something to which it is usually attached; not fixed in position

Examples

Detached wires
After the storm, several detached wires lay across the road.
He found a detached piece of the model on the floor.
The detached shutter dangled precariously from the window frame.
A detached wire caused the device to stop working.
She found a detached button on the floor and sewed it back on.
The guesthouse is completely detached from the main building.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attached - joined or fastened to something
  • synonym: disconnected - not joined or attached; separate
