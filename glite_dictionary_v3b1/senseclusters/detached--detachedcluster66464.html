Synonyms

  • unattached
    4.2 0.08/m
    unattached is loose and not fastened to anything, while detached describes something separated from what it is usually joined to.
  • unaffixed
    7.2 0.00/m
    unaffixed is a formal way to say not fastened or fixed, while detached is more common in everyday speech.
  • unanchored
    6.4 0.00/m
    unanchored lacks a steady anchor and can mean unstable, while detached simply means separated and not fixed in place.
  • uncoupled
    4.9 0.01/m
    uncoupled means parts that were joined are now separated, while detached does not always imply a previous direct joining.
  • separate
    2.1 40/m
    separate stands apart or is different from others, while detached focuses on being physically disconnected from what held it.
  • disengaged
    4.4 0.00/m
    disengaged is released or no longer connected, often in machines or roles, while detached means simply not fixed in position.
