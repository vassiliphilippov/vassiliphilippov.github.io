nonmajority

noun
group less than half
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.məˈdʒɔr.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.məˈdʒɒr.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
a group of people or votes that does not make up more than half of the total; a minority

Examples

The nonmajority group
The nonmajority voiced their concerns during the community meeting.
In the election, the nonmajority parties formed a coalition to influence policy.
The committee ensured that the nonmajority had a chance to present their views.

Related Terms

  • antonym: majority - a group that is more than half of a larger group
  • synonym: minority - a group that is less than half of a larger group
