hard drive

noun
computer data storage device
1.9
Pronunciation: / hɑrd draɪv / (AmE), / hɑːd draɪv / (BrE)
a device in a computer used for storing and retrieving digital information on one or more rigid disks

Examples

A hard drive device
Before selling the computer, he wiped all the data from the hard drive.
My computer crashed because the hard drive failed unexpectedly.
She upgraded her laptop with a larger hard drive to store more files and photos.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disk drive - a device that reads from and writes to a disk
  • antonym: solid-state drive - a data storage device that uses flash memory instead of spinning disks
