networking

noun
connecting computers
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛtˌwɝːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of connecting computers, devices, or systems so they can communicate and share data

Examples

Computer networking setup

Proper networking allows employees to access shared files from any computer in the building.
