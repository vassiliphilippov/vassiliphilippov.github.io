cabbie

noun
taxi driver
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæbi / (AmE), / ˈkæbi / (BrE)
a person whose job is to drive a taxi

Examples

The cabbie drives
The cabbie chatted with his passengers as he drove through the city.
After a long shift, the tired cabbie parked his taxi near the station.
Many cabbies know the best shortcuts to avoid traffic jams.
After years as a cabby, he knew every shortcut in the city.
The cabby waited patiently outside the hotel as his passenger collected her luggage.
The friendly cabby shared stories about life in New York during the drive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chauffeur - a person employed to drive a private or hired car
  • antonym: passenger - a person who is traveling in a vehicle but is not driving it
  • synonym: taxi driver - a person who drives a taxi for passengers
