red sprite

noun
bright reddish atmospheric electrical phenomenon
Pronunciation: / rɛd spraɪt / (AmE), / rɛd spraɪt / (BrE)
a brief, glowing red flash of light high in the atmosphere, typically caused by electrical discharges above thunderstorms

Examples

A red sprite flash
Curious about unusual weather phenomena, she read an article about red sprites and elves.
Scientists captured rare footage of red sprites using high-speed cameras.
Several pilots have reported seeing red sprites above intense thunderstorms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blue jet - another type of upper-atmosphere lightning, blue-colored and shaped like a jet
  • synonym: elves - very brief disk-shaped flashes of light above thunderstorms
