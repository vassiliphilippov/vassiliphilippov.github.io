nonessential

noun
something not necessary
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːnɪˈsɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪˈsɛnʃəl / (BrE)

Examples

Cut nonessentials

During a crisis, it’s important to distinguish between what is critical and what are merely nonessentials.
She packed only the essentials for her trip, leaving behind all nonessentials.
