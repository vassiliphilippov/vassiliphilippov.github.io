poke

noun
a North American plant with edible shoots, poisonous berries

A tall, coarse North American perennial herb (Phytolacca americana) with small white flowers and blackish-red berries, whose young stems are edible but berries and roots are poisonous.

Examples

Poke plant berries
Although the berries of poke are attractive, they are highly poisonous to humans.
The fields behind the house were overgrown with wild poke during the summer.
Young poke shoots are cooked and eaten in some parts of the southern United States.
