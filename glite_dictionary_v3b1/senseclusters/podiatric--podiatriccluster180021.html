podiatric

adjective
foot medical care
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊdiˈætrɪk / (AmE), / ˌpəʊdiˈætrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the medical care, treatment, or study of feet

Examples

Podiatric medical care
She scheduled a podiatric examination after experiencing persistent heel pain.
The hospital recently opened a new podiatric department for specialized foot care.
A podiatric specialist recommended custom insoles to improve his walking comfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orthopedic - relating to the correction of deformities of bones or muscles
  • synonym: podiatry - the medical specialty concerned with the feet
