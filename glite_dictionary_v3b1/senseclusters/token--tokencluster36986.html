token

noun
disk used in place of money in certain machines or systems
3.6 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊkən / (AmE), / ˈtəʊkən / (BrE)
a metal or plastic disk used as a substitute for money, often for use in slot machines, public transportation, or games.

Examples

A subway token
He found an old token from a long-closed amusement park.
She inserted a token into the subway turnstile to pay her fare.
The arcade only accepts tokens instead of cash in its slot machines.
