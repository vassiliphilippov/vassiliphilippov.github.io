atmosphere

noun
weather or climate in a place
Pronunciation: / ˈæt.məsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˈæt.məs.fɪə / (BrE)
the physical conditions such as weather or climate in a particular place at a particular time

Examples

Humid atmosphere
During the summer, the coastal atmosphere becomes warm and humid.
The atmosphere in the valley was damp and chilly that morning.
The hikers prepared for the changeable atmosphere of the mountain region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: climate - the general weather conditions of a region
  • synonym: weather - the state of the atmosphere at a particular place and time
