uninvested

adjective
lacking commitment
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈvɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈvɛstɪd / (BrE)
not having money, effort, or emotional commitment put into something

Examples

Uninvested in work
She remained uninvested in the outcome of the meeting and barely paid attention.
Many students felt uninvested in the group project and did not participate actively.
The company struggled because its employees were largely uninvested in its success.

Related Terms

  • antonym: committed - dedicated or invested in something
  • synonym: disengaged - not involved or interested
