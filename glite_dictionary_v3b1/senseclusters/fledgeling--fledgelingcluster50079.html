fledgeling

adjective
young bird with developed flight feathers
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛdʒ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflɛdʒ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a young bird) having just developed wing feathers large enough for flight.

Examples

Fledgeling chick
A fledgeling owl peered curiously from the nest's edge.
The fledgeling sparrows hopped awkwardly along the garden wall.
We watched as the fledgeling robins took their first flights.

Related Terms
