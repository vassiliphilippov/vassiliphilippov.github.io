(of a machine or system) not performing its regular or intended function; not working properly.

Examples

A malfunctioning machine
A malfunctioning air conditioner made the summer unbearably hot in the office.
Engineers rushed to repair the malfunctioning circuit before it caused further problems.
The malfunctioning elevator left the residents trapped between floors for several hours.

Synonyms

  • faulty
    3.5 2.78/m
    faulty describes an item with a defect that causes wrong operation, while malfunctioning means not working properly.
  • defective
    3.9 2.87/m
    defective refers to a product with a manufacturing flaw, while malfunctioning focuses on a device not working as intended.
  • dysfunctional
    6.1 5.98/m
    dysfunctional describes a system that does not operate normally, often for groups or people, while malfunctioning means a machine is not working properly.
  • nonfunctional
    4.4 0.18/m
    nonfunctional is completely not working or useless, while malfunctioning suggests parts may work but the system fails to work properly.
  • out of order
    2.1 0.65/m
    out of order is a public sign meaning a machine is broken or unavailable, while malfunctioning describes internal problems causing incorrect operation.
  • down
    2.9 14/m
    down is an informal word for a service or system being temporarily unavailable, while malfunctioning means it is not working properly.
