sanguine

adjective
cheerfully optimistic or positive
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsæŋ.ɡwɪn / (AmE), / ˈsæŋ.ɡwɪn / (BrE)
cheerfully optimistic or positive, especially in a difficult situation

Examples

Sanguine attitude
Despite the company's setbacks, the CEO remained sanguine about its future.
Even in the face of adversity, she had a sanguine outlook on life.
His sanguine attitude kept the team motivated during difficult times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: optimistic - expecting the best in all things
  • antonym: pessimistic - having a negative or gloomy outlook
