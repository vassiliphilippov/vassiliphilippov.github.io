crisscross

adjective
marked with crossing lines
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪsˌkrɔs / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsˌkrɒs / (BrE)
marked with a pattern of crossing lines or intersecting paths

Examples

Crisscross pattern
His shirt had a distinctive crisscross design woven into the fabric.
She admired the crisscross pattern of shadows on the floor created by the blinds.
The old map was crisscross with faded pencil lines showing possible routes.
