breathability

noun
air and moisture passage
Pronunciation: / ˌbriːθəˈbɪlɪti /; / ˌbriːðəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌbriːθəˈbɪlɪti /; / ˌbriːðəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of a material or fabric that allows air (and sometimes moisture) to pass through, making it comfortable to wear

Examples

The breathability of fabric
The breathability of this jacket makes it perfect for hiking in warm weather.
Athletes often choose clothing with high breathability to stay comfortable during intense workouts.
The breathability of cotton sheets helps people sleep better on hot nights.
The breathability of this fabric makes it ideal for summer clothing.
Athletes often choose shoes with high breathability to keep their feet cool and dry.
When buying a tent, consider its breathability to prevent condensation inside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: permeability - the ability of a material to allow liquids or gases to pass through it
  • synonym: ventilation - the provision of fresh air to a room, building, or material
  • antonym: waterproofness - the quality of not allowing water to pass through
