overtest

verb
to test excessively
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈtɛst / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈtɛst / (BrE)
to test someone or something more than is necessary or reasonable

Examples

To overtest students
Some schools overtest students, leaving them stressed and exhausted by constant exams.
It is important not to overtest new products, as it can delay their release unnecessarily.
Parents worry that teachers may overtest children instead of focusing on real learning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overexamine - to examine too much or too often
  • antonym: under test - to test less than is necessary
