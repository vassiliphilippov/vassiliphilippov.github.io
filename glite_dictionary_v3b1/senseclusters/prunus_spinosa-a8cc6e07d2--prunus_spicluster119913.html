Prunus spinosa

noun

A thorny Eurasian shrub with small, plumlike blue-black fruits, commonly known as blackthorn or sloe.

Examples

Prunus spinosa shrub

The hedgerows were thick with Prunus spinosa, their branches heavy with ripe sloes.
