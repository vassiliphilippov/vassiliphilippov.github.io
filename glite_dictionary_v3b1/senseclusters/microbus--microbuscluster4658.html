microbus

noun
small passenger bus
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌbʌs / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌbʌs / (BrE)
a small bus designed to carry a limited number of passengers, usually fewer than a standard bus

Examples

A microbus for students
The school uses a microbus to transport students on field trips.
We rented a microbus for our group tour around the city.
A microbus is ideal for airport shuttles with small groups of passengers.
The school uses a microbus to transport students from nearby neighborhoods.
We hired a microbus for our group trip to the countryside.
A microbus is often more convenient than a full-sized bus in crowded cities.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bus - a large vehicle for carrying many passengers
  • synonym: minibus - a small bus for carrying a few passengers
  • degree lower: van - a medium-sized vehicle for transporting goods or people
