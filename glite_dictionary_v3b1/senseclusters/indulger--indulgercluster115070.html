indulger

noun
person who enjoys pleasures
5.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈdʌl.dʒər/ /; / /ən.ˈdʌl.dʒər/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈdʌl.dʒə/ /; / /ən.ˈdʌl.dʒə/ (BrE)
a person who often allows themselves to enjoy pleasures, luxuries, or treats, sometimes to excess

Examples

A dessert indulger
As an indulger, she never missed an opportunity to try new desserts on vacation.
He was known as an indulger in fine wines and gourmet foods among his friends.
Being an indulger sometimes made it hard for him to stick to a strict diet.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ascetic - a person who avoids pleasures and lives a simple life
  • synonym: pleasure-seeker - a person who actively looks for enjoyment and pleasure
