potion

noun
medicinal or magical drink
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈpəʊʃən / (BrE)
a liquid with medicinal, magical, or poisonous properties, prepared to be drunk

Examples

A potion of medicine
He drank the potion and immediately began to feel better.
Legends tell of a potion that grants eternal youth.
The witch brewed a mysterious potion in her cauldron.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antidote - a medicine that counteracts a poison
  • synonym: elixir - a magical or medicinal potion
