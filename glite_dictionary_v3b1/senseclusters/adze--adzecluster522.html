adze

noun
wood-shaping tool
Pronunciation: / ædz / (AmE), / ædz / (BrE)
A tool with a curved blade at a right angle to the handle, used for shaping and smoothing wood.

Examples

A adze tool
After sharpening the adze, she was able to carve the bowl more efficiently.
The carpenter used an adze to smooth the surface of the wooden beam.
Traditional canoe makers still rely on an adze for shaping large logs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: axe - a tool with a heavy blade used for chopping wood
