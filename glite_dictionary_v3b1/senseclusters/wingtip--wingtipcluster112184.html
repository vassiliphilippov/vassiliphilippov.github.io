wingtip

noun
outer edge of wing
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋˌtɪp / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋˌtɪp / (BrE)
the outermost part or end of a bird's wing, especially the feathers at the far edge

Examples

A bird's wingtip
The eagle's wingtip brushed the surface of the water as it swooped down.
You can see the distinctive markings on the wingtip of this butterfly species.
The bird injured its wingtip during the storm but was able to recover quickly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: base - the part of the wing closest to the body
  • synonym: wing - the limb or appendage used for flying in birds and some other animals
