hierophant

noun
tarot card of tradition
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhaɪ.ə.rə.fænt/ /; / /ˈhaɪ.rə.fænt/ (AmE), /ˈhaɪ.ə.rə.fænt/ /; / /ˈhaɪ.rə.fænt/ (BrE)
a tarot card symbolizing religious authority, tradition, and moral guidance, often representing conformity to established values

Examples

The hierophant card often points to the importance of community and shared beliefs.
When the hierophant is drawn, it can mean seeking guidance from a trusted mentor or institution.
