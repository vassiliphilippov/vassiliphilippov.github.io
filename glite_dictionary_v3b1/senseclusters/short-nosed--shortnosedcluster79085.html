short-nosed

adjective
having a short or blunt nose
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrt ˌnoʊzd / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt ˌnəʊzd / (BrE)
having a nose that is short or blunt in shape

Examples

A short-nosed revolver

She preferred to draw short-nosed cartoon animals because they looked cuter.
The bulldog is a short-nosed breed that often has breathing difficulties.
