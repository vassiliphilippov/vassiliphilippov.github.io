dip

noun
a brief swim
Pronunciation: / dɪp / (AmE), / dɪp / (BrE)
a brief swim or immersion in water for enjoyment or refreshment

Examples

We went for a quick dip in the lake before lunch.
After a refreshing dip in the lake, we sat on the shore to dry off.
He gave the cloth a quick dip in the soapy water before wiping the table.
She took a dip of her brush in the paint before starting her artwork.
