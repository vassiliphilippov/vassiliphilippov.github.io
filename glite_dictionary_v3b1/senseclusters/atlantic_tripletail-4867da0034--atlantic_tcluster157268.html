Atlantic tripletail

noun
a large Atlantic fish with three rounded fins
Pronunciation: / əˈtlæntɪk ˈtrɪplˌteɪl / (AmE), / ətˈlantɪk ˈtrɪplteɪl / (BrE)
a large marine fish (Lobotes surinamensis) with three rounded fins, found in warm Atlantic waters from Cape Cod to northern South America

Examples

The Atlantic tripletail fish
Fishermen along the Gulf frequently catch Atlantic tripletails during the summer months.
The Atlantic tripletail is known for floating on its side, mimicking debris.
We spotted a Atlantic tripletail swimming near the boat just off the Florida coast.
