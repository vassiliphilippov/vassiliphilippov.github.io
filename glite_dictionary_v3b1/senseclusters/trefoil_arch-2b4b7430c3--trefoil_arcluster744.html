trefoil arch

noun
pointed arch with three rounded cusps
9.9 8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈtriː.fɔɪl ɑːrtʃ/ /; / /ˈtre.fɔɪl ɑːrtʃ/ (AmE), /ˈtriː.fɔɪl ɑːtʃ/ /; / /ˈtre.fɔɪl ɑːtʃ/ (BrE)
