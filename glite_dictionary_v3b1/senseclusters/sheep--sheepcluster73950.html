sheep

noun
domesticated woolly mammal
Pronunciation: / ʃip / (AmE), / ʃiːp / (BrE)
A domesticated, typically woolly ruminant mammal (Ovis aries) kept for its wool, meat, and milk, often with horns.

Examples

A flock of sheep
A flock of sheep grazed peacefully in the green meadow.
She spun yarn from the sheep's wool she collected last spring.
The farmer led the sheep into the barn before the storm began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goat - a hardy domesticated ruminant mammal related to the sheep
