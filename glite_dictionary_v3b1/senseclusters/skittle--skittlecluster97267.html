skittle

noun
wooden pin used in skittles or ninepins
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈskɪt.əl / (BrE)
a small wooden pin used as a target in the game of ninepins or skittles, especially in England

Examples

A wooden skittle
He knocked down every skittle on his first try and cheered loudly.
In traditional pubs, customers sometimes play a game of skittle in the alley.
The wooden skittle tumbled over as the ball struck it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bowling pin - a bottle-shaped object toppled by a bowling ball
