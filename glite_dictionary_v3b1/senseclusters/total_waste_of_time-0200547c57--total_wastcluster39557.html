total waste of time

noun
something completely unproductive
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊ.t̬əl weɪst əv taɪm / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl weɪst əv taɪm / (BrE)
an activity or event that is completely unproductive or pointless

Examples

A total waste of time activity
He thinks social media is a total waste of time, but others find it enjoyable.
Many people felt that the meeting was a total waste of time and accomplished nothing important.
Watching that movie turned out to be a total waste of time—I wish I'd done something else instead.

Related Terms

  • antonym: productive - yielding positive results or benefits
  • synonym: waste of time - something that is not worth the time spent on it
