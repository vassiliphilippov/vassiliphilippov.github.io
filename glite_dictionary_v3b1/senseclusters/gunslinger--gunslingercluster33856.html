gunslinger

noun
skilled gun user, often a killer
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnˌslɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌslɪŋə / (BrE)
a person, especially in the American Old West, noted for being skilled and ready to use guns, often as a professional killer or law enforcer

Examples

A gunslinger in the West
In the movie, Clint Eastwood plays a mysterious gunslinger seeking justice.
Many legends tell of a gunslinger who could outdraw any opponent.
The sheriff faced the notorious gunslinger in a showdown at high noon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gunfighter - a person who is skilled in the use of guns, especially in the Old West
