home birth

noun
childbirth at home
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊm bɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm bɜːθ / (BrE)
the act of giving birth to a baby at home instead of in a hospital or clinic

Examples

A home birth plan
After much research, they chose a home birth for their first child.
Some parents prefer a home birth because it feels more comfortable and private.
A trained midwife assisted during the home birth to ensure everything went smoothly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hospital birth - childbirth that takes place in a hospital
  • synonym: midwife - a person trained to assist women in childbirth
