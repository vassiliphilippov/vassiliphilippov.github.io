measuring cup

noun
graduated cup for measuring ingredients
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ˌkʌp / (BrE)
a cup with markings used to measure specific amounts of liquid or granular ingredients, typically in cooking or baking

Examples

A measuring cup with sugar
He filled a measuring cup with sugar before adding it to the mixture.
Pour the milk into the measuring cup to ensure you add the right amount.
She used a measuring cup to portion out the flour for the cake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: measuring spoon - a spoon with markings for measuring small amounts of ingredients
