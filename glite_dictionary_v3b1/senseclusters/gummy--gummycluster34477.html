gummy

adjective
soft and chewy
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmi / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmi / (BrE)
soft and chewy in texture, like certain candies or jellies

Examples

Gummy candy
The gummy texture of the candy made it fun to eat.
She prefers gummy vitamins because they are easier to chew than pills.
After sitting in the sun, the chocolate became soft and gummy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chewy - having a texture that requires a lot of chewing
  • antonym: hard - firm and difficult to bite or chew
