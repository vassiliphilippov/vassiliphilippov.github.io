ranter

noun
a person who rants loudly or angrily
Pronunciation: / ˈræntər / (AmE), / ˈrɑːntə / (BrE)
a person who speaks in a loud, angry, or forceful way, often complaining or criticizing at length

Examples

A loud ranter
During the public meeting, a persistent ranter disrupted the proceedings with loud complaints.
He doesn't listen to criticism and simply dismisses his opponent as just another ranter.
The talk show host was dismissed as a ranter who never let others speak.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rant - to speak or shout at length in an angry, impassioned way
