gelada

noun
Ethiopian monkey species
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈlɑːdə / (AmE), / dʒəˈlɑːdə / (BrE)
a large Ethiopian monkey with a short tail and a patch of bare skin on its chest

Examples

The gelada monkey
The gelada lives in the high grasslands of Ethiopia and feeds mainly on grass.
Researchers observed a group of geladas communicating with complex vocal sounds.
Tourists were excited to spot a wild gelada during their trek in the Simien Mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baboon - a large Old World monkey with a long snout and large canine teeth
