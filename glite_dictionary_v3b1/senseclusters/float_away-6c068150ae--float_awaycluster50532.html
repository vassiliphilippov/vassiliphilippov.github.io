float away

verb
to drift off or disappear gradually
Pronunciation: / floʊt əˈweɪ / (AmE), / fləʊt əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to move slowly through the air or over water and gradually disappear from sight; to drift away or vanish slowly

Examples

To float away slowly
A tiny feather began to float away on the breeze.
She watched her worries float away as she listened to music.
The clouds seemed to float away and reveal a clear blue sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drift away - to move slowly away from a place, often gradually out of sight or awareness
