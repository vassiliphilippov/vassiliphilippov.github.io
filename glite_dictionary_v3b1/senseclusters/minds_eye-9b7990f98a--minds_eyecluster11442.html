mind's eye

noun
visualization in imagination or memory
5.5
Pronunciation: / maɪndz aɪ / (AmE), / maɪndz aɪ / (BrE)
the ability to imagine or see images and scenes in your mind

Examples

The mind's eye imagines
Even after all these years, she appears vividly in his mind's eye.

Writers often use their mind's eye to create imaginary worlds.
She could see every detail of the house in her mind's eye.
The author used her mind's eye to create a vivid world for her story.
With his mind's eye, he pictured how the garden would look in spring.
