soc

noun
student club abbreviation
Pronunciation: / sɑk / (AmE), / sɒk / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for 'society', especially referring to a student club or organization at a school or university

Examples

Drama soc
She joined the photography soc to meet other students interested in cameras and editing.
The drama soc is putting on a new play this semester at the university theater.
He became president of the chess soc after showing great enthusiasm for the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: club - an organization for people with a shared interest or activity
  • synonym: society - an organized group of people with a common interest
