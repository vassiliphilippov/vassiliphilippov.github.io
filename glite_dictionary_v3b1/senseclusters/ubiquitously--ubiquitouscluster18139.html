ubiquitously

adverb
everywhere, commonly
8.2
Pronunciation: / juːˈbɪkwɪtəsli / (AmE), / juːˈbɪkwɪtəsli / (BrE)
in a way that is present or found everywhere or very commonly

Examples

Found ubiquitously everywhere
Mobile internet access is now available ubiquitously in most developed countries.
Fast food restaurants can be found ubiquitously throughout the city.
The company's logo appeared ubiquitously on billboards, buses, and television ads.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rarely - not often; in few places or cases
  • synonym: universally - in a way that applies everywhere or to everyone
