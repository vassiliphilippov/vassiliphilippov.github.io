unpeel

verb
to reveal gradually
6.8 0.04/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàpiÀêl / (AmE), /  ånÀàpiÀêl / (BrE)
to reveal or uncover something gradually, as if by removing layers one by one (often used figuratively)

Examples

The journalist began unpeeling the layers of the story to find the truth beneath.
As we kept unpeeling the mystery, more surprising facts came to light.

Synonyms

  • unspool
