negotiate

verb
succeed in passing through or over something
6.0 3.3
Pronunciation: / nɪˈɡoʊʃiˌeɪt /; / nəˈɡoʊʃiˌeɪt /; / nəˈɡoʊʃieɪt / (AmE), / nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪt / (BrE)
to successfully get through, around, or past an obstacle or something difficult

Examples

To negotiate a steep slope
He had to negotiate a steep and slippery slope to reach the campsite.
