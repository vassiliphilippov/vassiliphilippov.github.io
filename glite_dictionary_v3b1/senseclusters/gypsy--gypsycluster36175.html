gypsy

verb
to wander freely
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪpsi / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪpsi / (BrE)

Examples

After college, she enjoyed gypsying through different countries before starting her career.
He dreamed of gypsying across the continent with just a backpack and a map.
