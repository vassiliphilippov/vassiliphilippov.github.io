carbonless

adjective
without carbon paper
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrbənləs / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbənləs / (BrE)
not requiring carbon paper to make a copy of a document, especially referring to special paper that creates duplicates automatically

Examples

Carbonless copy paper
The company now uses carbonless forms to simplify paperwork for employees.
Please fill out the carbonless receipt so you can keep a copy for your records.
Switching to carbonless invoices helped reduce waste in the office.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carbon paper - thin paper used to make copies by placing it between sheets
  • synonym: self-copying - able to make a copy automatically
