homemaker

noun
person who manages a household
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊm.meɪ.kər/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊm.meɪ.kə/ (BrE)
A person, typically a woman, who manages the household and takes care of the home, especially as a full-time occupation.

Examples

A homemaker at home
After her second child was born, she decided to become a homemaker and focus on raising her family.
He appreciates all the hard work his mother puts in as a homemaker every day.
Many homemakers manage not only the cleaning and cooking but also the family's finances.
