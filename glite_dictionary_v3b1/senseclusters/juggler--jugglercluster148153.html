juggler

noun
person who juggles objects for entertainment
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌɡlər / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌɡlə / (BrE)
a person who entertains by continuously tossing and catching or manipulating objects such as balls, clubs, or rings, often performing tricks of manual dexterity

Examples

A juggler at circus
At the circus, a skilled juggler balanced on a unicycle while performing tricks.
She hired a street juggler for her son's birthday party.
The juggler amazed the crowd by tossing five flaming torches into the air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: juggling - the act or art of keeping several objects in motion in the air
