fatwood

noun
resinous pine kindling
Pronunciation: / ˈfætˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈfætˌwʊd / (BrE)
pine wood that is rich in resin and used as kindling to start fires easily

Examples

A piece of fatwood
The campers gathered fatwood to help get their fire started quickly in the damp forest.
You only need a small piece of fatwood to ignite a pile of logs.
Old pine stumps are often a good source of fatwood for outdoor cooking.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: firewood - wood used as fuel for a fire
  • synonym: kindling - small pieces of wood used to start a fire
