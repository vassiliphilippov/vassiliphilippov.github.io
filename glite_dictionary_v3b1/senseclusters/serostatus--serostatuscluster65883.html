serostatus

noun
antibody status
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪroʊˌsteɪtəs /; / ˌsɪroʊˈsteɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪərəʊˌsteɪtəs /; / ˌsɪərəʊˈsteɪtəs / (BrE)
a person's status showing whether specific antibodies are present in their blood, indicating exposure to a disease or infection
