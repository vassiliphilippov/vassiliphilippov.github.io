greening

noun
process of becoming environmentally sustainable
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈgriː.nɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈgriː.nɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process or movement of making an economy, industry, organisation, or system more environmentally sustainable by adopting eco‑friendly policies, practices, and technologies.

Examples

Greening the economy
The greening of the economy requires investment in renewable energy and green infrastructure.
Local businesses welcomed the greening incentives that offered tax breaks for reducing emissions and waste.
Policymakers debated whether the rapid greening of industry would harm jobs without retraining programs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decarbonization - the process of reducing carbon dioxide emissions across an economy or sector
  • synonym: sustainability - the quality of causing little or no damage to the environment and being able to be maintained over the long term
