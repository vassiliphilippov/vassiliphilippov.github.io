unidentified flying object

noun
unknown object seen in the sky
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.aɪˈdɛntɪˌfaɪd ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈɑbdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪˈdɛn.tɪ.faɪd ˈflaɪ.ɪŋ ˈɒb.dʒɪkt / (BrE)
an object seen in the sky whose origin or nature cannot be immediately identified, often thought to be of extraterrestrial origin

Examples

An unidentified flying object sighting
Scientists are investigating the video footage of an unidentified flying object captured over the ocean.
Several people claimed to have witnessed an unidentified flying object near the city last night.
The pilot reported seeing an unidentified flying object hovering above the mountains.
