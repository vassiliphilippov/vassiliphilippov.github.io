Synonyms

  • approach
    2.1 32/m
    approach is used in formal or planned situations and can mean coming closer in time or place, while near is simple and direct.
  • come near
    1.8 1.72/m
    come near is a common, natural phrase for moving closer in space or time, while near is shorter and can sound more formal.
  • draw near
    2.6 1.40/m
    draw near sounds slightly old-fashioned or literary for things coming closer, while near is more neutral and modern in speech.
  • draw close
    2.9 1.38/m
    draw close often feels poetic and shows gentle movement together, while near is a plain verb used in everyday speech.
  • close
    1.5 30/m
    close as a verb can mean moving toward or shutting something, while near means to move closer in distance or time.
