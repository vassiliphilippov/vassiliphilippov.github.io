Mimosoideae

noun
subfamily of flowering plants in the legume family

Examples

Mimosoideae plant family
Acacia and acacias are often classified under Mimosoideae in modern taxonomy.
Some botanists consider Mimosoideae to be a distinct family known as Mimosaceae.
