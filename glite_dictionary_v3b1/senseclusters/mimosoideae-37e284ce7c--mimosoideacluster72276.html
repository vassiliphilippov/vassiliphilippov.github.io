Mimosoideae

noun
subfamily of flowering plants in the legume family
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɪˌmoʊsoʊˈaɪdi.i / (AmE), / mɪˌməʊsəʊˈiːdiː / (BrE)
a subfamily of flowering plants in the legume family, sometimes treated as a separate family (Mimosaceae) in some classification systems

Examples

Mimosoideae plant family
Acacia and acacias are often classified under Mimosoideae in modern taxonomy.
Some botanists consider Mimosoideae to be a distinct family known as Mimosaceae.
The Mimosoideae subfamily includes plants with compound leaves, such as the mimosa.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mimosa - a genus of plants known for sensitive leaves
