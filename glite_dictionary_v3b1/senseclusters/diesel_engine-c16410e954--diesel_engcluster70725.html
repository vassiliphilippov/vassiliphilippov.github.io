diesel engine

noun
internal combustion engine burning heavy oil
2.8 1.10/m

Examples

Synonyms

  • diesel motor
    3.5 0.01/m
    diesel motor is a common, informal name for the same engine used in vehicles and generators, while diesel engine is the full technical term.
  • diesel
    3.4 4.79/m
    diesel is often the short word people use for the fuel or the engine, while diesel engine names the specific compression-ignition machine.
