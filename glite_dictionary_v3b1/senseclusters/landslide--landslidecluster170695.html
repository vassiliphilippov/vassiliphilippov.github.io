landslide

verb
to win overwhelmingly
4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈlænd.slaɪd/ /; / /ˈlæn.slaɪd/ (AmE), /ˈlænd.slaɪd/ /; / /ˈlæn.slaɪd/ (BrE)
to win an election, contest, or competition by a very large margin or majority

Examples

To landslide an election
The candidate is landsliding in the polls, leading by more than twenty points.
After months of campaigning, her party landslided to victory in the national election.
Analysts predict that the team will landslide their rivals in the championship game.
