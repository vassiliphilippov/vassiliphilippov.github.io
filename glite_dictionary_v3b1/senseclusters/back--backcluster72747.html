back

noun
part of the body along the spine
1.0
Pronunciation: / bæk / (AmE), / bak /; / bæk / (BrE)
the rear part of a person, animal, object, or place; the side or surface opposite the front

Examples

A sore back

A label was sewn into the back of his jacket.

The shirt tore along the back seam when he bent over.

She rested against the back of the chair, enjoying its comfort.
The sofa has a high back to provide better support.

Related Terms

  • antonym: front - the opposite side of the body from the back, usually the chest or face
  • synonym: rear - the back part of something, especially a building or vehicle
  • antonym: seat - the part of a chair on which you sit
  • synonym: spine - the series of vertebrae extending from the skull to the lower back
