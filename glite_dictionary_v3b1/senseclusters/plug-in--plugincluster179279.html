plug-in

adjective
powered or charged by plugging in
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈplʌ.gɪn/ /; / /ˈplʌ.gən/ (AmE), /ˈplʌ.gɪn/ /; / /ˈplʌ.gən/ (BrE)
(of a device or vehicle) designed to be connected to an external electrical supply for charging or operation; able to be plugged in.

Examples

Plug-in car

The company introduced a plug-in version of its popular SUV to meet emissions regulations.
He rented a plug-in electric car for the weekend to avoid using petrol.
