burner

noun
part of stove for heating pots and pans
5.4 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbɜːr.nər/ (AmE), /ˈbɜː.nə/ (BrE)
A part of a stove or range that produces heat for cooking, on which pots and pans are placed.

Examples

Stove burner
Always make sure the burner is turned off after you finish cooking.
She placed the kettle on the back burner to bring the water to a boil.
The stove has four burners, so you can cook several dishes at once.
