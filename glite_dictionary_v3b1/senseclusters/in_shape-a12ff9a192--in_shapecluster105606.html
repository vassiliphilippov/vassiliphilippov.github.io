in shape

adjective
physically fit and healthy
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪn ʃeɪp / (AmE), / ɪn ʃeɪp / (BrE)
(of a person) physically fit and healthy, especially as a result of exercise.

Examples

In shape for running
After months of training, she was finally in shape for the marathon.
Even during the winter, he manages to keep in shape by working out indoors.
It's important to stay in shape as you get older.

Related Terms

  • antonym: out of shape - not physically fit or in poor physical condition
