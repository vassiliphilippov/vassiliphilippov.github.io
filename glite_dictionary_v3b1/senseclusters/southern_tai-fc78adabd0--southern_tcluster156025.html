Southern Tai

noun
branch of the Tai languages in southern Thailand
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌðɚn taɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən taɪ / (BrE)
a branch of the Tai languages spoken mainly in southern Thailand, especially in Surat Thani, Chumphon, and surrounding provinces

Examples

Southern Tai language group
Linguists have identified Southern Tai as a distinct subgroup within the Tai languages.
Much research has focused on documenting the grammar and vocabulary of Southern Tai.
The Southern Tai dialects are primarily spoken in the lower provinces of Thailand.
