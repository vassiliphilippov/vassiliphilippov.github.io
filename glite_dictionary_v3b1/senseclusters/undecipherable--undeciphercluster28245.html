undecipherable

adjective
impossible to understand or interpret
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈsaɪfərəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈsaɪfərəbəl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to understand or interpret, especially in the context of written text or code

Examples

An undecipherable message
After so many edits, the original message became completely undecipherable.
Her doctor’s handwriting on the prescription was practically undecipherable.
The ancient manuscript was so damaged that most of its text was undecipherable.
