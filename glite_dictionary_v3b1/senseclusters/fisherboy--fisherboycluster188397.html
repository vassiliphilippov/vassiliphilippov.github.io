fisherboy

noun
boy who fishes
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃərˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃəbɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy who catches fish, especially as a job or pastime

Examples

Young fisherboy by lake
The young fisherboy spent every morning by the lake with his net.
Villagers often saw the fisherboy returning home with a basket full of fish.
As a child, he dreamed of becoming a fisherboy like his older brother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angler - a person who fishes with a rod and line
  • synonym: fisherman - a person who catches fish for a living or as a hobby
  • antonym: fishmonger - a person who sells fish
