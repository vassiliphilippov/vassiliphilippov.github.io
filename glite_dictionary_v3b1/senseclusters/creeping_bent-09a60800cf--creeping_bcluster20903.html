creeping bent

noun
a spreading lawn grass
Pronunciation: / ˈkripɪŋ bɛnt / (AmE), / ˈkriːpɪŋ bɛnt / (BrE)
a common grass with long runners, often used for lawns and golf courses

Examples

creeping bent grass
Gardeners often select creeping bent for its ability to create a lush, dense turf.
Many parks use creeping bent because it quickly covers bare patches in the lawn.
The creeping bent on the golf course requires regular maintenance to stay healthy.
