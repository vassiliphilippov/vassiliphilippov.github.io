overstressed

adjective
excessively stressed
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈstrɛst / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈstrɛst / (BrE)
feeling or showing too much stress or pressure, often to the point of being unable to cope

Examples

An overstressed worker
After working long hours for weeks, he felt completely overstressed and needed a break.
Many students become overstressed during final exams and struggle to sleep well.
If you are feeling overstressed, it is important to talk to someone and seek support.

Related Terms

  • antonym: relaxed - free from tension or anxiety
  • degree lower: stressed - feeling mental or emotional strain
