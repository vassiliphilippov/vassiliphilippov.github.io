sprocket

noun
toothed wheel for engaging a chain
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɑk.ɪt /; / ˈsprɑːkɪt / (AmE), / ˈsprɒk.ɪt /; / ˈsprɒkɪt / (BrE)
a wheel or part with teeth that fits into a chain or track to move or drive something in a machine, bicycle, or similar device
