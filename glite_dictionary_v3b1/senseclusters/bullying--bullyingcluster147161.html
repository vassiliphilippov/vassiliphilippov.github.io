bullying

noun
intimidation or mistreatment of a weaker person
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbʊl.ɪ.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈbʊl.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbʊl.ɪ.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈbʊl.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
