pickpocketing

noun
stealing from pockets
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkˌpɑkɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪkˌpɒkɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of stealing money or valuables from someone's pocket or bag without them noticing

Examples

Street pickpocketing
The city has taken steps to reduce pickpocketing in crowded tourist areas.
He was arrested for pickpocketing during the busy festival.
Travelers should be aware of pickpocketing when using public transportation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: robbery - the act of taking property unlawfully from a person or place by force or threat
  • synonym: theft - the act of stealing something
