figuratively

adverb
metaphorically, not literally
4.3 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪg.jə.rə.tɪv.lɪ/ /; / /ˈfɪg.jɜːr.ə.tɪv.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈfɪg.jə.rə.tɪv.lɪ/ /; / /ˈfɪg.jʊə.rə.tɪv.lɪ/ (BrE)
In a way that uses words or expressions with a meaning that is different from the literal interpretation; metaphorically.

Examples
