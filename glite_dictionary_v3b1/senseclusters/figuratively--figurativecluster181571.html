figuratively

adverb
metaphorically, not literally
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪɡjərətɪvli / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡjʊrətɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that uses words or expressions with a meaning that is different from the literal interpretation; metaphorically.

Examples

To figuratively speak
Figuratively speaking, the news hit me like a ton of bricks.
The mountain of laundry in her room was only a figuratively description of the mess.
When he said he was drowning in paperwork, he meant it figuratively rather than literally.

Related Terms

  • antonym: literally - In a literal manner; exactly as stated
  • synonym: metaphorically - In a metaphorical manner; not literally
