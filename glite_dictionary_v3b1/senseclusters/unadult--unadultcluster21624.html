unadult

verb
to treat as not adult
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnəˈdʌlt / (AmE), / ʌnəˈdʌlt / (BrE)
to treat someone as if they are not an adult, often making them feel less mature or capable.

Examples

To unadult a teenager
It is important not to unadult teenagers when discussing their responsibilities.
He felt frustrated when his colleagues would unadult him during meetings.
Parents sometimes unadult their grown children without realizing it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infantilize - to treat someone as if they are a child
  • antonym: respect - to treat someone with consideration and maturity
