precollegiate

adjective
before college
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːkəˈlɪdʒiət / (AmE), / ˌpriːkəˈlɪdʒiət / (BrE)
relating to the period or activities before entering college

Examples

Precollegiate summer courses
The university offers precollegiate summer courses for high school students.
Her precollegiate achievements helped her get accepted into a top college.
Many precollegiate programs focus on preparing students for academic success in higher education.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postsecondary - relating to education after high school, especially college or university
  • synonym: secondary - relating to high school education
