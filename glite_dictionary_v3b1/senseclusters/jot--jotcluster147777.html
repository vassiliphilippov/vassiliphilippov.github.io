jot

verb
write briefly or quickly
3.9 0.67/m
Pronunciation: / dÊ’É‘t / (AmE), / dÊ’É’t / (BrE)
to write something quickly and briefly, often as a reminder or note

Examples

To jot a note
He jotted something on a scrap of paper and handed it to me.
Let me jot down your phone number before I forget it.
She always jots a quick note in her planner after meetings.

Synonyms

  • jot down
    2.9 1.75/m
    jot down is the common spoken phrase for making a quick note, while jot is a shorter verb used in writing.
  • write down
    1.1 8.42/m
    write down is to record information by writing it to remember later, while jot means write quickly and briefly as a short reminder.
  • dash down
    4.4 0.01/m
    dash down means to write something quickly and without much care, while jot implies a quick, neat note kept as a reminder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: note - to write down a short message or reminder
  • synonym: scribble - to write something quickly and carelessly
