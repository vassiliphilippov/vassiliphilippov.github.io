cholo

noun
Filipino young man
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊləʊ / (BrE)
in the Philippines, an informal term for a young man or boy, often used in a friendly or affectionate way

Examples

Filipino cholo boy
The vendor called his nephew a cholo and smiled as the boy ran to help him.
In the neighborhood, everyone knew the friendly cholo who played basketball every afternoon.
She affectionately referred to her younger brother as cholo when introducing him to her friends.
