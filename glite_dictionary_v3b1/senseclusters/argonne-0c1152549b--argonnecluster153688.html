Argonne

proper noun
French region; lab name
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈɡɑn / (AmE), / ɑːˈɡɒn / (BrE)
a region in northeastern France known for World War I battles and the name of a U.S. national laboratory

Examples

Argonne Forest battle
The Argonne Forest was the site of a major World War I offensive.
Scientists at Argonne National Laboratory are working on advanced energy research.
Many soldiers remember the difficult terrain of the Argonne during the war.
