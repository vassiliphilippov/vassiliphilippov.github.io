embrocation

noun
medicinal liquid for sore muscles
Pronunciation: / ˌɛm.broʊˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛm.brəʊˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a medicinal liquid applied to the skin to relieve muscular stiffness or pain

Examples

A embrocation lotion
After a long run, he used an embrocation on his aching legs.
Cyclists often apply embrocation before races to keep their legs warm.
The pharmacist recommended an embrocation for her muscle pain.
