family Echeneidae

noun
a fish family with a head suction disk (remoras)
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ɛˌkiːnɪˈaɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli iːˌkiːnɪˈiːdaɪ / (BrE)
a family of fishes known as remoras or suckerfish, characterized by a sucking disk on the head for attaching to other fishes or ships
