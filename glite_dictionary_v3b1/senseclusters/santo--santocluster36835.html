santo

noun
saint or holy person
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːntoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsæntəʊ / (BrE)

Examples

A santo festival

Each santo in the procession was decorated with flowers and candles.
Many people pray to a santo for protection and guidance in their daily lives.
