post hoc

noun
logical fallacy of assuming causation from sequence
9.9 0.12/m

the logical fallacy of assuming that because one event follows another, the first caused the second

Examples

Post hoc fallacy
It's important to avoid post hoc reasoning when evaluating scientific studies.
Many political arguments rely on a post hoc assumption that one event caused another simply because it came first.
The article commits the post hoc fallacy by suggesting the new policy directly caused the crime rate to drop.
