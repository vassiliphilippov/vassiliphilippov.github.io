epibenthic

adjective
on sea or lake bottom
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈbɛnθɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈbɛnθɪk / (BrE)
living on or just above the bottom surface of a sea or lake

Examples

Epibenthic animals
Researchers collected samples of epibenthic organisms from the shallow coastal waters.
The epibenthic community plays a vital role in recycling nutrients on the ocean floor.
Some fish species are classified as epibenthic because they live just above the seabed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benthic - living on the bottom of a sea or lake
  • antonym: pelagic - living in the open water away from the bottom
