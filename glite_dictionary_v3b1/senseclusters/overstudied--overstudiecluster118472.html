overstudied

adjective
excessively studied
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈstʌdid / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈstʌdid / (BrE)
having studied too much, often to the point of tiredness or reduced effectiveness

Examples

Overstudied student
After weeks of preparation, he felt completely overstudied before the big test.
Some students become overstudied and find it hard to concentrate during exams.
If you are feeling overstudied, it might help to take a short break.

Related Terms

  • antonym: refreshed - feeling rested and energetic
