alderperson

noun
member of a city council (gender-neutral)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔldɚˌpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈɔːldəˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
A member of a municipal legislative body, such as a city council, especially used as a gender-neutral alternative to 'alderman'.

Examples

An alderperson votes
As an alderperson, she worked to improve public transportation in her district.
Several alderpersons attended the community meeting to hear residents' concerns.
The city council appointed a new alderperson to represent the downtown ward.
