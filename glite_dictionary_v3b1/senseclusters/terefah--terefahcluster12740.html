terefah

adjective
not conforming to Jewish dietary laws
9.9
Pronunciation: / təˈreɪfə / (AmE), / təˈreɪfə / (BrE)
(of food) not conforming to Jewish dietary laws; not kosher.

Examples

terefah meat
According to Jewish law, consuming terefah meat is strictly prohibited.
He asked whether the ingredients were terefah or kosher before ordering.
The restaurant offers kosher options but also serves some terefah dishes for other guests.

Related Terms
