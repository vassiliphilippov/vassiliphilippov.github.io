messieurs

noun
plural male title
8.7
Pronunciation: / meɪˈsjɜːrz / (AmE), / meˈsjɜː /; / ˈmesjɜː / (BrE)
a formal or polite way to address or refer to two or more men (used as a title or to mean 'gentlemen')

Examples

Dear messieurs
The invitation was addressed to messieurs Smith and Johnson.
At the meeting, messieurs Brown, Lee, and Patel presented their proposal.
The letter began with 'Dear messieurs Carter and Evans,'.
The speaker addressed the assembled messieurs before beginning his presentation.
At the formal dinner, the host welcomed the messieurs and their guests.
Please, messieurs, take your seats so we can start the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Messrs. - the written abbreviation for 'messieurs', used before men's names
  • synonym: gentlemen - polite or formal way to refer to men
  • synonym: misters - the plural of 'mister', a title for men
