liwan

noun
Middle Eastern porch
9.9
Pronunciation: / liˈwɑːn / (AmE), / liˈwɑːn / (BrE)
a covered entrance hall or porch in traditional Middle Eastern houses, often open on one side

Examples

The liwan porch
The family gathered in the liwan to enjoy the evening breeze after dinner.
Guests were welcomed into the spacious liwan before being shown into the main rooms.
The architect designed a modern house inspired by the traditional liwan layout.

Related Terms

  • synonym: veranda - a roofed platform along the outside of a house
