smoothhound shark

noun
a small shark of European coastal waters
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsmuːðˌhaʊnd ʃɑrk / (AmE), / ˈsmuːðˌhaʊnd ʃɑːk / (BrE)
A small, slender shark found in European coastal waters, also known as the smooth dogfish.

Examples

A smoothhound shark
A smoothhound shark swims gracefully along the rocky shore in search of crustaceans.
Local fishermen sometimes catch smoothhound sharks while fishing for cod off the coast.
Researchers tagged several smoothhound sharks to study their migration patterns in the North Sea.

  • synonym: dogfish - a small shark with a blunt snout
