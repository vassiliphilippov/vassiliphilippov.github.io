Urban V

proper noun
French pope (1362–1370), canonized in 1870
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɜːr.bən ðə fɪfθ/ /; / /ˈɜːr.bən fɪfθ/ (AmE), /ˈɜː.bən ðə fɪfθ/ /; / /ˈɜː.bən fɪfθ/ (BrE)
(1310–1370) The French pope from 1362 to 1370, known for his attempts to restore the papacy to Rome and for his later return to Avignon; canonized in 1870.

Examples

Urban V pope
Urban V moved the papal court briefly back to Rome in an effort to restore its prestige.
During the pontificate of Urban V, efforts were made to reconcile France and England.
Many churches commemorate Urban V as a saint due to his canonization in 1870.
