burb

noun
informal suburb
8.6
Pronunciation: /bɜːrb/ (AmE), /bɜːb/ (BrE)
an informal term for a suburb or suburban area, especially a residential community outside a city

Examples

A quiet burb
After years in the city, they moved to a quiet burb to raise their children.
Many people commute from the burb to work downtown every day.
She enjoys the parks and schools in her burb more than those in the city center.
