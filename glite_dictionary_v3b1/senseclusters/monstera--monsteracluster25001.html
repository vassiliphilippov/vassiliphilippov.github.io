monstera

noun
tropical fruit resembling a pinecone
4.9
Pronunciation: / mɑnˈstɛrə / (AmE), / mɒnˈstɪərə / (BrE)
A tropical fruit with a cylindrical shape, similar to a pinecone, known for its pineapple-banana flavor; also refers to the plant producing it.

Examples

A monstera fruit
Before eating a monstera, you must ensure it is completely ripe to avoid irritation.
She displayed a ripe monstera beside the more familiar tropical fruits at the farmers' market.
The monstera fruit is prized for its sweet, pineapple-banana flavor when fully ripe.
