guaiacum wood

noun
aromatic wood from Guaiacum trees
6.7 8.7
Pronunciation: /ɡwaɪ.ˈæk.əm wʊd/ (AmE), /ɡwaɪ.ˈæk.əm wʊd/ (BrE)
The dense, aromatic heartwood of trees in the genus Guaiacum, often used to yield oils for perfumes and traditional medicines.

Examples

The essential oil extracted from guaiacum wood is a prized ingredient in many perfumes.
Traditional medicine sometimes uses guaiacum wood for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
