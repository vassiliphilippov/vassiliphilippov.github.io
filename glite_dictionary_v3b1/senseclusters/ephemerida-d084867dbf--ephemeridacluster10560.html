Ephemerida

noun
an order of mayflies
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˌfɛmɚˈɪdə / (AmE), / ɪˌfɛmɪˈrɪdə / (BrE)
An order of insects comprising the mayflies, known for their aquatic larvae and short-lived adult stages; also called Ephemeroptera.

Examples

Ephemerida larvae
Researchers collected samples of Ephemerida larvae from the riverbed.
The Ephemerida are notable for their brief adult lifespan.
The delicate wings of the Ephemerida distinguish them from other aquatic insects.
