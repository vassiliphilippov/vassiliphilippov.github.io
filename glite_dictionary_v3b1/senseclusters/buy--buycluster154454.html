buy

noun
an advantageous purchase; a bargain
1.5
Pronunciation: / baɪ / (AmE), / baɪ / (BrE)
a purchase considered to be especially good value or a bargain

Examples

A great buy
He told me this car was the best buy on the lot.
That antique vase was quite a buy at only twenty dollars.
The end-of-season sale offered some fantastic buys for savvy shoppers.
