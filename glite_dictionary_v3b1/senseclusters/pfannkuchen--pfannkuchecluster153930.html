pfannkuchen

noun
German-style sweet pancake
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑnˌkuːkən / (AmE), / ˈfanˌkuːkən / (BrE)
A puffy, mildly sweet German pancake made with eggs and lemon flavoring, typically sprinkled with confectioners' sugar and served with jam or fruit sauce.

Examples

Traditional pfannkuchen with jam
For breakfast, she made traditional pfannkuchen and served them with raspberry jam.
The cookbook features a recipe for pfannkuchen, a classic German pancake that's light and fluffy.
We enjoyed homemade pfannkuchen sprinkled with powdered sugar at the family brunch.
