pfannkuchen

noun
German-style sweet pancake
9.9 9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑːn.kuː.kən/ /; / /ˈpæn.kʊ.kən/ (AmE), /ˈfɑːn.kuː.kən/ /; / /ˈpɑːn.kuː.kən/ (BrE)
A puffy, mildly sweet German pancake made with eggs and lemon flavoring, typically sprinkled with confectioners' sugar and served with jam or fruit sauce.

Examples
