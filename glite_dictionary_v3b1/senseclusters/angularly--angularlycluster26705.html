angularly

adverb
with sharp angles
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡjələrli / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡjʊləli / (BrE)
in a way that has sharp angles or corners, or relates to angles

Examples

Built angularly for style
The modern building was constructed angularly to create a striking appearance.
She moved angularly across the stage, emphasizing the sharpness of her dance.
The artist arranged the furniture angularly to give the room a unique look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sharply - in a way that is abrupt or has a clear edge
  • antonym: smoothly - in a way that is even and without sharp angles
