transgendered

adjective
having undergone a change of gender (outdated)
Pronunciation: / trænzˈdʒɛndɚd / (AmE), / tranzˈdʒɛndəd / (BrE)
(of a person) having undergone a partial or complete change of gender, especially by medical or surgical means. Now considered outdated and often replaced by 'transgender'.

Examples

Transgendered woman
Several people who identified as transgendered shared their stories at the conference.
She described herself as transgendered in the early 2000s, but now prefers the term 'transgender'.
The term transgendered is now considered outdated in most communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transgender - relating to or being a person whose gender identity differs from the sex assigned at birth
