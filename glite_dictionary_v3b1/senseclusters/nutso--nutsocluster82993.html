nutso

noun
informal insult
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈnʌt.soʊ/ (AmE), /ˈnʌt.səʊ/ /; / /ˈnʌt.soʊ/ (BrE)
an informal and often pejorative term for a person who is mentally unstable or behaves in a wild, unpredictable way

Examples

He is a nutso
Everyone thought the new neighbor was a bit of a nutso after his wild outburst at the meeting.
She called her ex-boyfriend a total nutso because of his unpredictable behavior.
The kids joked that their teacher was a nutso for assigning so much homework.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crazy - a person who is mentally unstable or acts irrationally
  • synonym: lunatic - a person who is mentally ill or behaves in a very foolish way
  • antonym: sane - a person who is mentally healthy and reasonable
