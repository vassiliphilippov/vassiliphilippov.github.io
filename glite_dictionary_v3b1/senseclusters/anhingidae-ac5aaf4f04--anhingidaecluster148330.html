Anhingidae

noun
family of aquatic birds (snakebirds)
Pronunciation: / ænˈhɪndʒɪˌdi /; / ænˈhɪŋɡɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ænˈhɪndɪˌdiː /; / ænˈhɪŋɡɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of large aquatic birds, called snakebirds or darters, with long necks, found in warm regions
