live

verb
to survive or remain alive
1.0
Pronunciation: / lɪv / (AmE), / lɪv / (BrE)
to be alive, survive, or continue to exist

Examples

To live after an accident
After the shipwreck, only a few managed to live until they were rescued.
Many animals cannot live without water for more than a few days.
The doctors told him he might not live through the night, but he pulled through.
As long as the plant receives sunlight and water, it will live and grow.
Doctors were surprised that she continued to live after the operation.
Turtles can live for over a hundred years in the wild.

  • synonym: exist - to have existence; to be
  • synonym: survive - to remain alive after a dangerous event or situation
