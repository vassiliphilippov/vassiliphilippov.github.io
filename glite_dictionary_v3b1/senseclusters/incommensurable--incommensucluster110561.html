incommensurable

adjective
not sharing a common measure or factor
8.5 8.6
Pronunciation: /ɪn.kə.ˈmen.sə.rə.bəl/ /; / /ɪn.kə.ˈmen.ʃə.rə.bəl/ (AmE), /ɪn.kə.ˈmen.sjʊə.rə.bəl/ /; / /ɪn.kə.ˈmen.ʃə.rə.bəl/ (BrE)
not able to be measured or compared because there is no common standard or way to do so

Examples
