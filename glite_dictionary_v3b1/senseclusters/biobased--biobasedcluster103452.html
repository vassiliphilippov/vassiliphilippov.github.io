biobased

adjective
from living organisms
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪoʊˌbeɪst / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəʊˌbeɪst / (BrE)
made from living or renewable biological sources (such as plants or animals), not from synthetic or petroleum materials

Examples

Many companies are investing in biobased materials to reduce reliance on fossil fuels.
Switching to biobased products can lower a company's carbon footprint.

Related Terms

  • antonym: petroleum-based - made from petroleum or oil-derived materials
  • synonym: renewable - able to be replaced naturally and used again
  • antonym: synthetic - made by chemical synthesis, not from natural sources
