forehand stroke

noun
sports stroke with palm facing forward
6.1 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈfɔːr.hænd stroʊk/ /; / /ˈfɑːr.hænd stroʊk/ (AmE), /ˈfɔː.hænd strəʊk/ (BrE)
a stroke in sports such as tennis or badminton made with the palm of the hand facing in the direction of the stroke

Examples

A forehand stroke in tennis
After a quick rally, Maria ended the point with a precise forehand stroke down the line.
His forehand stroke is known to be the strongest part of his game.
She hit a powerful forehand stroke that landed just inside the baseline.
