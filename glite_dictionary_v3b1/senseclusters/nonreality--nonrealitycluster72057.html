nonreality

noun
sense of unreality
9.2
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.rɪ.ˈæl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /nɑːn.riː.ˈæl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /nɒn.rɪ.ˈæl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /nɒn.riː.ˈæl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
a feeling or state in which oneself or the world seems unreal or disconnected, often linked to anxiety or trauma

Examples

A sense of nonreality
After the accident, she experienced a persistent feeling of nonreality that made everything seem distant.
Periods of intense anxiety sometimes brought on a sense of nonreality for him.
Therapy helped him cope with episodes of nonreality following the traumatic event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: derealization - a feeling that the external world is unreal or strange
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
