intermix

verb
to mix or blend together
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmɪks / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmɪks / (BrE)
to combine or mix together different substances or elements so that they become one or are difficult to separate

Examples

To intermix colors
Different colors began to intermix, creating a beautiful pattern.
During the festival, cultures intermix in vibrant celebrations.
The two solutions intermix to form a uniform liquid.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blend - to mix two or more substances so they combine smoothly
  • antonym: separate - to keep apart or divide into distinct parts
