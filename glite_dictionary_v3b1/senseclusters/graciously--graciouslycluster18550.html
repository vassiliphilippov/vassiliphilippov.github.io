graciously

adverb
in a kind or courteous manner
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪʃəsli / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a gracious, courteous, or kind manner.

Examples

To graciously accept
He always responds graciously to criticism, no matter how harsh it is.
She graciously accepted the award and thanked everyone involved in the project.
The host graciously welcomed all the guests into her home despite the short notice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courteously - in a polite and respectful manner
  • antonym: rudely - in an impolite or offensive manner
