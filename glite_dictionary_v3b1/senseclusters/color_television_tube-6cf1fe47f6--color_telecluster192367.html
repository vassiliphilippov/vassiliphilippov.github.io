color television tube

noun
cathode-ray tube for color TV display
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlər ˌtɛlɪˌvɪʒən tuːb / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ˌtɛlɪˌvɪʒən tjuːb / (BrE)
a cathode-ray tube used in televisions to display images in full color
