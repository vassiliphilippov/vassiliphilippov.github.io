lil

proper noun
name prefix in music
Pronunciation: / lɪl / (AmE), / lɪl / (BrE)
a name prefix used before a personal or stage name, especially in rap and pop music, meaning 'little' or indicating youth

Examples

Lil Wayne
The rapper chose lil Ace as his stage name to highlight his youthful energy.
Many artists in hip-hop use lil before their names to create a unique identity.
She released her first single under the name lil Star when she was just sixteen.
