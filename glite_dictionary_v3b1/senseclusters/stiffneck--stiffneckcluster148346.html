stiffneck

noun
stubborn person
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪfˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈstɪfˌnɛk / (BrE)
a person who is stubborn and refuses to change their opinions or behavior

Examples

A stubborn stiffneck
Despite everyone's advice, the stiffneck refused to consider any other options.
Being a stiffneck can make it difficult to work in a team environment.
His reputation as a stiffneck made negotiations challenging for the whole group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flexible - willing to change or adapt
  • synonym: obstinate - stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion
