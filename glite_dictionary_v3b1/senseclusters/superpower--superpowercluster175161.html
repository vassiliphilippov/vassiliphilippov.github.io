superpower

noun
extraordinary ability
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌpaʊər / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌpaʊə / (BrE)
an extraordinary or magical ability that gives someone special powers, especially in stories about superheroes

Examples

A flying superpower
The ability to fly is a common superpower in many superhero stories.
If you could choose any superpower, what would it be and why?
Her unique superpower allowed her to read people's thoughts instantly.
The comic book character discovered his superpower after being exposed to a mysterious energy.
If you could have any superpower, which one would you choose and why?
Her unique superpower allowed her to read people's thoughts and help them solve problems.

  • synonym: power - the ability to do something or act in a particular way
  • antonym: weakness - a lack of strength or ability
