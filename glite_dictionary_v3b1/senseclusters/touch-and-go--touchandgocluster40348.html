touch-and-go

adjective

Pronunciation: /tʌtʃ ənd goʊ/ /; / /tʌtʃ ænd goʊ/ /; / /tʌtʃ ən goʊ/ (AmE), /tʌtʃ ənd gəʊ/ /; / /tʌtʃ ænd gəʊ/ /; / /tʌtʃ ən gəʊ/ (BrE)
(of a situation) dangerous or uncertain, with the outcome in doubt until the last moment.

Examples

A touch-and-go situation
After her accident, her recovery was touch-and-go for several weeks.
It was touch-and-go whether the patient would survive the night.
The rescue mission was touch-and-go due to the bad weather conditions.
