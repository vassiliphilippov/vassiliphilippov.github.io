rusty

adjective
covered with rust
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌsti / (AmE), / ˈrʌsti / (BrE)
covered with or affected by rust (a reddish-brown substance that forms on iron or steel when it gets wet)

Examples

Rusty metal surface
After years at sea, the anchor became extremely rusty.
She found a rusty nail while gardening in the backyard.
The old bicycle was so rusty it wouldn't move without a lot of oil.
The old gate was so rusty that it squeaked every time we opened it.
After not playing the piano for years, my skills are a bit rusty.
She found a rusty key hidden under the floorboard in the attic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corroded - damaged by a chemical process, especially rust in metals
  • antonym: polished - smooth and shiny, not affected by rust or corrosion
