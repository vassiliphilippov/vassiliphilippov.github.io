rusty

adjective
covered with rust
2.7 1.9
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌ.stiː/ /; / /ˈrʌ.stɪ/ (AmE), /ˈrʌ.stiː/ /; / /ˈrʌ.stɪ/ (BrE)
