grill

noun
metal bars used as a grate or partition
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪl / (AmE), / ɡrɪl / (BrE)
A framework of metal bars used as a partition, grate, or for cooking food over a fire.

Examples

Metal grill bars
He replaced the old fireplace grill with a sturdier one.
She liked the decorative iron grill on the front door.
The prisoners could only see each other through the metal grill between their cells.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grate - a framework of iron bars used to hold fuel in a fireplace
