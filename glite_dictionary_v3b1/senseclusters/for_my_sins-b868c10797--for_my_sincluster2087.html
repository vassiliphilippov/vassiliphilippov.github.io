for my sins

x
as a guilty pleasure; indulgently
Pronunciation: / fər maɪ sɪnz / (AmE), / fə maɪ sɪnz / (BrE)
used humorously to indicate that something is done as a guilty pleasure or as an indulgence, often implying one shouldn't but does anyway

Examples

For my sins again
For my sins, I agreed to organize the event again this year.
I watch cheesy romantic comedies for my sins when I'm having a bad day.
She's still a fan of boy bands, for my sins.
