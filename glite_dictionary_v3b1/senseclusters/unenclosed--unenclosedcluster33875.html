unenclosed

adjective
not closed in or surrounded
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈkloʊzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈkləʊzd / (BrE)
not closed in, surrounded, or contained; open or lacking a barrier or enclosure

Examples

An unenclosed porch
The unenclosed porch was a perfect place to enjoy the evening breeze.
The picnic area was completely unenclosed, allowing for beautiful views of the mountains.
They kept their horses on an unenclosed field near the river.
