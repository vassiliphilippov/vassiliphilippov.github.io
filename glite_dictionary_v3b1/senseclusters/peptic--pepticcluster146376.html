peptic

adjective
relating to digestion
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛptɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɛptɪk / (BrE)
relating to or promoting the process of digestion, especially by means of digestive enzymes or juices.

Examples

Peptic digestion process

The stomach secretes peptic juices to help break down food.
