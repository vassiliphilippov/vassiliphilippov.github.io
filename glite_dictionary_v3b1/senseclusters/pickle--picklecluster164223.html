pickle

verb
preserve food in vinegar or brine
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈpɪkəl / (BrE)
to preserve food in a solution of vinegar or brine, often with spices

Examples

To pickle cucumbers
Many people pickle cucumbers to make them last through the winter.
My grandmother taught me how to pickle vegetables using a traditional family recipe.
You can pickle onions in a mixture of vinegar and spices for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ferment - to undergo or cause a process of fermentation in food
  • synonym: preserve - to treat food so that it will not spoil
