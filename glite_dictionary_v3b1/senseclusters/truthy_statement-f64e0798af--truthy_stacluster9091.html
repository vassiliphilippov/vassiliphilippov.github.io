truthy statement

noun
statement that seems true or believable
Pronunciation: / ˈtruːθi ˈsteɪtmənt / (AmE), / ˈtruːθi ˈsteɪtmənt / (BrE)
an assertion that appears to be true or believable, even if it is not necessarily factual

Examples

Truthy statement on social media
On social media, truthy statements often gain more traction than actual facts.
She recognized that many advertisements rely on truthy statements to persuade consumers.
The politician's speech was full of truthy statements that glossed over real issues.

Related Terms

  • antonym: truthful statement - an assertion that is accurate and factual
  • synonym: truthiness - the quality of seeming or being felt to be true, even if not necessarily true
