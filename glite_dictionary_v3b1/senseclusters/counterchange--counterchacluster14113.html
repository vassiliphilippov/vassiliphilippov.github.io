counterchange

noun
reversed color pattern
9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkaʊn.tər.tʃeɪndʒ/ (AmE), /ˈkaʊn.tə.tʃeɪndʒ/ (BrE)
an arrangement in which colors or patterns are reversed or alternated, especially in heraldry or design

Examples

Counterchange of colors
The artist used a counterchange of black and white to create a striking visual effect.
In heraldry, a counterchange often involves swapping the colors of a shield's background and symbol.
The floor tiles were laid out in a counterchange pattern, alternating light and dark squares.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alternation - the act of alternating or changing between two things
  • antonym: uniformity - the quality of being the same or consistent throughout
