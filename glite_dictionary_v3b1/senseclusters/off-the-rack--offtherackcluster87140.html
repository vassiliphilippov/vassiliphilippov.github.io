Synonyms

  • ready-to-wear
    3.9 0.02/m
    ready-to-wear is clothing made in standard sizes and sold from stock in shops, while off-the-rack is a common informal term for this.
  • off-the-peg
    6.0 0.02/m
    off-the-peg is the British phrase for clothes made in standard sizes ready to buy, while off-the-rack is the more general term.
  • off-the-shelf
    4.1 0.31/m
    off-the-shelf describes products made in standard forms and sold immediately from stock, while off-the-rack refers specifically to ready-made clothing.
