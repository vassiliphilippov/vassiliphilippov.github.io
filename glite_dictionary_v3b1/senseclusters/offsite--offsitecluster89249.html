offsite

adverb
away from main site
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌsaɪt / (BrE)
at or to a location away from the usual workplace or main site

Examples

Work offsite
The training session was held offsite to give employees a change of environment.
Some employees prefer to work offsite for better concentration and flexibility.
The company organized an offsite meeting to discuss the new project strategy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: onsite - at the main location or workplace
  • synonym: remotely - at a distance from the usual place, especially by using technology
