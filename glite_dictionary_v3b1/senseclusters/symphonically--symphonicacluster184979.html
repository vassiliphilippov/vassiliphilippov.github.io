symphonically

adverb
in a symphonic manner
Pronunciation: / sɪmˈfɑnɪkli / (AmE), / sɪmˈfɒnɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or resembles a symphony or symphonic music

Examples

Music arranged symphonically
The composer structured the piece symphonically to create a rich, layered sound.
Her music is arranged symphonically, giving it a grand and complex feel.
The film score builds symphonically to a powerful climax with the full orchestra.
