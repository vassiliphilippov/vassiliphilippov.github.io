dark

adjective
having a deep or brownish-black color (hair/skin/eyes)
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɑrk / (AmE), / dɑːk / (BrE)
(of a color, hair, skin, or eyes) having a deep or brownish-black color; closer to black than to white

Examples

Dark hair color
He inherited his father's dark hair and olive skin.
Many people admire the striking look of dark features.
She has beautiful dark eyes that stand out in the sunlight.
He wore a pair of dark glasses to the meeting.
Men often wear dark colors for formal occasions.
She chose a dark green for the living room walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brunet - having dark brown or black hair
  • antonym: fair - having pale skin or light hair
  • antonym: light - (of a color) pale or having high lightness, closer to white than to black
