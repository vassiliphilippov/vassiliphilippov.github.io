round-nose

noun
pliers for bending wire
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊnd ˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈraʊnd ˌnəʊz / (BrE)
a type of pliers with smooth, rounded jaws used for bending wire and forming loops, especially in jewelry and electrical work

Examples

Round-nose pliers
The jeweler used round-nose to create perfect loops in the silver wire.
Electricians often carry round-nose for shaping and bending wires during installations.
She reached for her round-nose when making earrings in her workshop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flat-nose pliers - pliers with flat jaws for gripping and bending wire
  • synonym: needle-nose pliers - pliers with long, thin jaws for gripping small objects
