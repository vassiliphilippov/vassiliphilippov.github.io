virgin birth

noun
conception without sexual intercourse, esp. in Christianity
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrdʒɪn bɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdʒɪn bɜːθ / (BrE)
The conception of a child by a woman who has not had sexual intercourse, especially ascribed to the birth of Jesus Christ.

Examples

The virgin birth story
His lecture explored cultural myths surrounding the idea of virgin birth throughout history.
Some scientists have studied reports of animals exhibiting a form of virgin birth called parthenogenesis.
The doctrine of the virgin birth is central to Christian theology.
