boke

noun
photographic blur quality
9.9
Pronunciation: /boʊk/ (AmE), /bəʊk/ (BrE)
the aesthetic quality of the out-of-focus or blurred areas in a photograph, especially as produced by a particular lens

Examples

Boke effect in photos
The portrait's beautiful boke made the subject stand out against a soft background.
Many photographers choose lenses specifically for the quality of their boke.
He admired the creamy boke in the evening light behind the flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blur - an indistinct or hazy area in an image
  • antonym: sharpness - the clarity or distinctness of detail in a photograph
