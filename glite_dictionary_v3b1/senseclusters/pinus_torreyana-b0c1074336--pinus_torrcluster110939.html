Pinus torreyana

noun
a pine tree species native to California
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪnəs tɔːˈreɪ.ənə / (AmE), / ˈpɪn.əs tɒˈreɪ.ənə / (BrE)
A medium-sized pine tree native to southwestern California, having five needles per cluster and long cylindrical cones; also known as Torrey pine.
