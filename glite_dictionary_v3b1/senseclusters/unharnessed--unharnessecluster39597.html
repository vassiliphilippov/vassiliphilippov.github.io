unharnessed

adjective
not controlled or used
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɑrnəst / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɑːnɪst / (BrE)
not controlled, restrained, or put to use; free or unused.

Examples

Unharnessed energy
The river's unharnessed power could provide electricity for the entire region.
Many creative ideas remain unharnessed due to lack of resources.
Scientists are searching for ways to use unharnessed solar energy more efficiently.
