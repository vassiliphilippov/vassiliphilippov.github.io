artillerist

noun
military gun operator
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈtɪlərɪst / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪlərɪst /; / ɑːˈtɪlɪərɪst / (BrE)
a person (usually a soldier) who operates or specializes in large military guns or artillery weapons

Examples

An artillerist fires cannon
The experienced artillerist adjusted the angle of the cannon before the battle began.
During the training exercise, each artillerist learned how to load and fire the heavy guns.
The army relied on the skill of every artillerist to support the advancing troops.
The experienced artillerist quickly calculated the distance to the enemy position.
During the battle, each artillerist played a crucial role in supporting the infantry.
After years of training, she became a skilled artillerist in the army.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gunner - a person who operates a gun or artillery piece
  • antonym: infantryman - a soldier who fights on foot
