Dipteronia

noun
genus of deciduous Chinese shrubs
Pronunciation: / dɪp.təˈroʊ.ni.ə / (AmE), / dɪp.təˈrəʊ.ni.ə / (BrE)
a genus of large deciduous shrubs native to central and southern China, known for clusters of winged seeds that turn red as they mature
