chump

noun
gullible or foolish person
7.2
Pronunciation: / tʃʌmp / (AmE), / tʃʌmp / (BrE)
A person who is easily deceived or taken advantage of; a fool.

Examples

A chump falls for tricks
Don't be a chump; read the fine print before you sign anything.
He was such a chump for believing that scam email was real.
They made me feel like a chump when I agreed to do all the work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dupe - a person who is easily deceived
  • synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • antonym: wise - showing experience, knowledge, and good judgment
