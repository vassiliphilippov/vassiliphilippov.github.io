unpremeditated

adjective
not preplanned; spontaneous
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌpriməˈdeɪtɪd /; / ˌʌn.priːˈmɛd.ɪ.teɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌpriːməˈdeɪtɪd /; / ˌʌn.priːˈmɛd.ɪ.teɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
done without being planned or thought about beforehand; spontaneous

Examples

An unpremeditated action
An unpremeditated joke eased the tension in the meeting.
Her apology was completely unpremeditated and came straight from the heart.
The actor delivered an unpremeditated speech that surprised the audience.
His apology seemed entirely unpremeditated and sincere.
The artist's unpremeditated brushstrokes gave the painting a sense of life and spontaneity.
The judge ruled that it was an unpremeditated act rather than a planned crime.

  • synonym: spontaneous - done without being planned or forced
