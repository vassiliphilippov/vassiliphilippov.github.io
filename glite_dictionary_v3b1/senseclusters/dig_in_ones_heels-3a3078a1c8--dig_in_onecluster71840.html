dig in one's heels

verb
refuse to change position or opinion
9.9 5.4
Pronunciation: /dɪg ɪn wʌnz hiːlz/ /; / /dɪg ɪn wʌns hiːlz/ /; / /dɪg ən wʌnz hiːlz/ (AmE), /dɪg ɪn wʌnz hiːlz/ /; / /dɪg ɪn wʌns hiːlz/ /; / /dɪg ən wʌnz hiːlz/ (BrE)
