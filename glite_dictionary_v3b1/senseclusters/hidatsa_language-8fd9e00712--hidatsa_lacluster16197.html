Hidatsa language

noun
the traditional language of the Hidatsa people
Pronunciation: / hɪˈdɑːtsə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / hɪˈdɑːtsə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Siouan language traditionally spoken by the Hidatsa people of North Dakota

Examples

The Hidatsa language
