firefighting

noun
putting out fires
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌfaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌfaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or job of putting out fires and preventing them from spreading

Examples

Firefighting as a job
Modern firefighting relies on advanced equipment and teamwork to control dangerous blazes.
He has been interested in firefighting since he was a child watching fire trucks race by.
Training for firefighting includes learning how to use hoses, ladders, and breathing apparatus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fire prevention - measures taken to avoid the outbreak of fire
  • synonym: fireman - a person whose job is to put out fires
