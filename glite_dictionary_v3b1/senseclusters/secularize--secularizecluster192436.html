secularize

verb
transfer from religious to civil control
5.3 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsek.jə.lə.raɪz/ /; / /ˈsek.jʊ.lə.raɪz/ (AmE), /ˈsek.jə.lə.raɪz/ /; / /ˈsek.jʊ.lə.raɪz/ (BrE)
