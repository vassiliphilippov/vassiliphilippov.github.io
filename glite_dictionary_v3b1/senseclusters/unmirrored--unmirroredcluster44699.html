unmirrored

adjective
not reflected
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɪrɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɪrəd / (BrE)
not reflected or not having a mirror image

Examples

Unmirrored image
The painting was intentionally left unmirrored to create a sense of asymmetry.
In the calm water, only the sky was unmirrored while the trees reflected perfectly.
Some designs appear distorted when unmirrored compared to their symmetrical versions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reflected - shown as a mirror image
