Peronospora

noun
genus of downy mildew fungi
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛrəˈnɑspəɹə /; / pɛrəˈnɒspərə / (AmE), / ˌpɛrəˈnɒspərə /; / pɛrəˈnɒspərə / (BrE)
a genus of destructive downy mildew fungi that infect plants and cause significant agricultural damage

Examples

Peronospora fungus infection
