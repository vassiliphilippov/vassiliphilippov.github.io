handle

verb
deal with or discuss a subject
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhændl̩ / (AmE), / ˈhændl / (BrE)
to deal with or discuss a subject, issue, or theme, especially in speech, writing, or artistic work

Examples

To handle a topic
Her speech handled several controversial issues that many others avoided.
In his latest film, the director handles social inequality through powerful storytelling.
The author chose to handle the topic of grief with sensitivity and depth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: address - to speak about or deal with a particular topic
  • synonym: cover - to deal with a subject in detail
  • antonym: ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to something
