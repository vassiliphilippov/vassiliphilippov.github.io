bohemian

noun
unconventional artist or writer
7.1 0.37/m
Pronunciation: / bo äÀàhiÀêmi…ôn / (AmE), / b…ô äÀàhiÀêmi…ôn / (BrE)
a person, especially an artist or writer, who leads an unconventional, nonconformist lifestyle, often in pursuit of artistic or literary goals

Examples

A bohemian artist
His bohemian lifestyle attracted both admiration and criticism from his more conventional peers.
She has always considered herself a bohemian, preferring art galleries and poetry readings to more traditional pursuits.
The city was once home to a lively community of bohemians known for their creative spirit and disregard for convention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonconformist - a person whose behavior or views do not conform to prevailing ideas or practices
