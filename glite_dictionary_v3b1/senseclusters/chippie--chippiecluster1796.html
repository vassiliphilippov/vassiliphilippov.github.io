chippie

noun
fish and chip shop
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪpi / (BrE)
an informal term for a fish and chip shop or, in some regions, a carpenter

Examples

A chippie dinner
After the movie, we grabbed dinner at the local chippie on the corner.
He has worked as a chippie for over twenty years, building houses across the city.
Many people in the UK call a fish and chip shop a chippie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpenter - a person who makes or repairs wooden objects
  • synonym: chip shop - a shop selling fish and chips
