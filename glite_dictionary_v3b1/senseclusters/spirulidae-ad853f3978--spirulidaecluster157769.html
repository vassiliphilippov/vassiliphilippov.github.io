Spirulidae

noun
family of cephalopod mollusks (ram's horn squid)
Pronunciation: / spaɪˈruːlɪˌdiː / (AmE), / spaɪˈruːlɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of cephalopod mollusks comprising the ram's horn squid, coextensive with the genus Spirula

Examples

The Spirulidae family
Only one living species of Spirulidae, Spirula spirula, is recognized today.
Scientists have studied Spirulidae fossils to understand cephalopod evolution.
The Spirulidae are known for their internal coiled shells, often called ram's horns.
