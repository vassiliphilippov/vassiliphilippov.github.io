tether

verb
share mobile internet connection
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈte.ðər/ (AmE), /ˈte.ðə/ (BrE)
to connect one device to another so it can use the latter's internet or mobile data connection, typically via USB, Bluetooth, or Wi‑Fi.

Examples

To tether a phone
At the café, she decided to tether her laptop to her phone because the Wi-Fi was slow.
He tethered his tablet via Bluetooth so the family could stream videos on the road.
During the outage, the team kept tethering devices to a single phone to maintain communication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connect - to link devices or systems so they can communicate or share data
  • synonym: hotspot - a device or service that shares its internet connection with other devices
