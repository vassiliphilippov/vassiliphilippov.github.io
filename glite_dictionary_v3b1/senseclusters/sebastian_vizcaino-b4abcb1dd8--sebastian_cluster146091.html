Sebastian Vizcaino

proper noun
Spanish explorer of California coast
9.9
Pronunciation: / səˈbæstʃən vɪsˈkaɪnoʊ / (AmE), / sɪˈbæstɪən vɪsˈkaɪnəʊ / (BrE)
(1550–1615) A Spanish explorer who was the first European to extensively explore and map the California coast.

Examples

Sebastian Vizcaino explorer
Sebastian Vizcaino mapped much of the California coastline during his expedition in the early 17th century.
Historians credit Sebastian Vizcaino with documenting the region’s geography and Native American settlements.
Many of California’s place names were given by Sebastian Vizcaino on his voyages.
