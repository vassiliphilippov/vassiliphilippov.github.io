ingathering

noun
return of exiles
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌɡæðərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌɡæðərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of bringing together people or things that have been dispersed, especially the return of exiles to their homeland

Examples

The ingathering of exiles was a significant event in the nation's history.
