rough horsetail

noun
a species of horsetail plant with rough stems
Pronunciation: / rʌf ˈhɔrˌsteɪl / (AmE), / rʌf ˈhɔːsteɪl / (BrE)
an evergreen plant with rough, erect stems, formerly used for scouring utensils; a type of horsetail (Equisetum hyemale)

Examples

The rough horsetail plant
Centuries ago, people used rough horsetail to scrub and polish pans.
Gardeners appreciate rough horsetail for its ability to thrive in wet soil.
The rough horsetail has distinctive jointed stems that feel gritty to the touch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horsetail - a non-flowering plant of the genus Equisetum with hollow, jointed stems
