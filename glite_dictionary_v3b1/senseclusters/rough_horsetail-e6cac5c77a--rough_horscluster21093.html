rough horsetail

noun
a species of horsetail plant with rough stems
Pronunciation: / rʌf ˈhɔrˌsteɪl / (AmE), / rʌf ˈhɔːsteɪl / (BrE)
an evergreen plant with rough, erect stems, formerly used for scouring utensils; a type of horsetail (Equisetum hyemale)
