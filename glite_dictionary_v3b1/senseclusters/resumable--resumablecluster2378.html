resumable

adjective
can be continued
4.4
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzuːməbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈzjuːməbəl / (BrE)
able to be continued after being paused or interrupted

Examples

A resumable download
The software update is resumable, so you can pause and continue it later.
Many modern downloads are resumable if your internet connection drops.
The online course is resumable, allowing students to study at their own pace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interruptible - able to be stopped or paused
  • antonym: irreversible - unable to be changed or continued once stopped
