mudslinging

noun
throwing mud
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌdˌslɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmʌdˌslɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of throwing mud at someone or something, especially as a playful or physical activity

Examples

Playful mudslinging
The children enjoyed an afternoon of mudslinging after the heavy rain turned the field into a mess.
During the festival, a mudslinging contest was held for anyone brave enough to get dirty.
After the mudslinging ended, everyone was covered from head to toe in mud.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mud fight - a playful fight where people throw mud at each other
