meniscus

noun
lens curved concave on one side, convex on the other
9.3 8.9
Pronunciation: / məˈnɪskəs / (AmE), / mɪˈnɪskəs / (BrE)
a lens that is concave on one side and convex on the other, used to focus or disperse light in optical instruments
