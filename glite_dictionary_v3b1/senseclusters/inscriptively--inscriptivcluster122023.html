inscriptively

adverb
by means of an inscription
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈskrɪptɪvli / (AmE), / ɪnˈskrɪptɪvli / (BrE)
by means of an inscription; in a way that involves writing or recording something as an inscription

Examples

Written inscriptively on stone
Ancient laws were often recorded inscriptively in stone or metal for all to see.
Her generosity was honored inscriptively with a plaque at the hospital.
The king's achievements were commemorated inscriptively on temple walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inscripted - written as or on an inscription
