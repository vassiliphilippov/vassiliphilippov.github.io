conversational partner

noun
AI or virtual dialogue partner
4.4
Pronunciation: /kɑːn.vər.ˈseɪ.ʃən.əl ˈpɑːr.nər/ /; / /kən.vər.ˈseɪ.ʃən.əl ˈpɑːr.nər/ (AmE), /kɒn.və.ˈseɪ.ʃən.əl ˈpɑːt.nə/ /; / /kən.və.ˈseɪ.ʃən.əl ˈpɑːt.nə/ (BrE)
A non-human interlocutor (for example, an AI chatbot or virtual agent) designed to engage in spoken or written dialogue with a user.

Examples

AI conversational partner
Language apps now pair users with a conversational partner to practice speaking with an AI assistant.
Schools are experimenting with conversational partners that give immediate feedback to language learners.
The museum's kiosk offers a friendly conversational partner that answers questions about exhibits.
