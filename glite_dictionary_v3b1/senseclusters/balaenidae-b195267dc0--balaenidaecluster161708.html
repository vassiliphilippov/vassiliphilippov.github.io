Balaenidae

noun
a family of large baleen whales
Pronunciation: / bəˈliːnəˌdaɪ / (AmE), / bəˈliːnɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of large baleen whales, commonly known as right whales, found in various oceans.

Examples

Balaenidae whale family
Conservation efforts for Balaenidae focus on protecting them from ship strikes and entanglement in fishing gear.
Members of the Balaenidae are easily recognizable by their large heads and arched mouths.
Scientists have studied the migration patterns of the Balaenidae in the North Atlantic Ocean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: right whale - a large baleen whale of the genus Eubalaena, member of Balaenidae
