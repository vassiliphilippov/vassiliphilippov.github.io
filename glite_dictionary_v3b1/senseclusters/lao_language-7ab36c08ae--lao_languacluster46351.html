Lao language

noun
official language of Laos
Pronunciation: / laʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / laʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language of the Lao people, chiefly spoken in Laos and some regions of Thailand; the official language of Laos

Examples

Lao language in Laos
Many people in the Isan region of Thailand use the Lao language at home.
She is learning to speak Lao language to communicate with her relatives in Vientiane.
The Lao language uses its own unique script closely related to Thai.
