pard

noun
mythical spotted beast
9.9
Pronunciation: /pɑːrd/ (AmE), /pɑːd/ (BrE)
a mythical or heraldic spotted wild animal from medieval bestiaries, often depicted as a leopard-like beast in coats of arms

Examples

Heraldic pard

In medieval heraldry, a pard was often used to symbolize courage and ferocity.
