Union

adjective
relating to the northern side in the US Civil War
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːniən / (AmE), / ˈjuːniən / (BrE)
of or relating to the northern states of the United States and those loyal to the federal government during the American Civil War (1861–65).

Examples

Union soldier
His grandfather served as a Union officer during the conflict.
Many Union soldiers marched south in the early years of the Civil War.
The Union army won a significant victory at Gettysburg.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Confederate - of or relating to the southern states that seceded from the US during the Civil War
