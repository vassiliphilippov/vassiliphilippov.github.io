trafficking

noun
illegal trade activity
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtræfɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtræfɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of buying, selling, or exchanging illegal or secret goods, people, or information, often in an improper or unlawful way

Examples

Trafficking illegal drugs
Authorities are working to stop trafficking of illegal drugs across the border.
International organizations are fighting against human trafficking to protect vulnerable people.
The new law aims to reduce trafficking in endangered animal species.
Authorities are investigating the trafficking of sensitive information between the two companies.
He was accused of trafficking with criminal organizations to gain influence.
The journalist uncovered a network involved in trafficking rare artifacts across borders.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: commerce - the activity of buying and selling, especially on a large scale
  • synonym: dealing - the activity of doing business or having transactions with others
  • synonym: smuggling - the act of moving goods or people illegally into or out of a country
  • degree lower: trade - the activity of buying, selling, or exchanging goods and services
