washcloth

noun
small cloth for washing body or face
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃˌklɔθ / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃˌklɒθ / (BrE)
A small square cloth used for washing the face and body.

Examples

A washcloth for face
After playing outside, the child wiped his face with a damp washcloth.
He folded the washcloth and placed it next to the soap on the bathroom shelf.
She grabbed a clean washcloth from the stack before stepping into the shower.
