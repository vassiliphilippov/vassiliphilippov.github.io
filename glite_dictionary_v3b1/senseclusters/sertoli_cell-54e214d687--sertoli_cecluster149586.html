Sertoli cell

noun
supporting cell in the testis
7.4
Pronunciation: / sərˈtoʊli sɛl / (AmE), / səˈtəʊli sɛl / (BrE)
an elongated cell in the seminiferous tubules of the testis that supports and nourishes developing sperm cells
