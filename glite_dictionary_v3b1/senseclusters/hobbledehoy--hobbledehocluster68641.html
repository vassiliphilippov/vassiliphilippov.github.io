hobbledehoy

noun
awkward adolescent boy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑːbəldəˈhɔɪ / (AmE), / ˌhɒbəldəˈhɔɪ / (BrE)
an awkward or clumsy adolescent boy, especially one who is bad-mannered or ungainly

Examples

A hobbledehoy boy
At family gatherings, his aunt always teased him for being a tall, thin hobbledehoy.
Once a shy hobbledehoy, he grew into a confident and refined young man.
The hobbledehoy tripped over his own feet as he entered the room, blushing furiously.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lout - an uncouth or aggressive young man
