tropological

adjective
figurative language
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtroʊpəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌtrɒpəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the use of figurative language or literary tropes

Examples

Tropological use of metaphor
The professor discussed the tropological techniques used by poets to convey deeper meanings.
Her analysis focused on the tropological structure of the novel's narrative.
Students learned to identify tropological devices in classic literature during the course.

Related Terms

  • synonym: figurative - using words or expressions with a meaning different from the literal
  • antonym: literal - using words in their most basic sense without metaphor or exaggeration
