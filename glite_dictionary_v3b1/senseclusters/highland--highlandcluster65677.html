highland

noun
elevated or mountainous land
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
an area of land that is at a higher elevation, especially mountainous or hilly terrain

Examples

A highland region
Forests and streams are common features of the Scottish highland.
The village sits at the edge of the highland, overlooking the river below.
These sheep are well-suited to the highland climate with its cooler temperatures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lowland - land that is at a lower elevation, especially flat or gently sloping terrain
