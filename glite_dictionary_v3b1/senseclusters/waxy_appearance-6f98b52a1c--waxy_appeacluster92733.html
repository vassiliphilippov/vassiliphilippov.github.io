waxy appearance

noun
smooth, glossy look like wax
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwæksi əˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / ˈwaksi əˈpɪərəns / (BrE)
a glossy, smooth look that resembles the surface of wax, often used especially to describe skin or other surfaces

Examples

A waxy appearance of skin
A waxy appearance of the skin can sometimes indicate an underlying health condition.
After the illness, his face took on a waxy appearance that worried the doctors.
The candle's surface had the same waxy appearance as polished marble.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - lacking brightness; not shiny
  • synonym: glossy - having a shiny or lustrous surface
