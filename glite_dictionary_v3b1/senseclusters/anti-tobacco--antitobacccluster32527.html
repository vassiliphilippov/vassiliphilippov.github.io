anti-tobacco

adjective
opposed to tobacco
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌænti təˈbækoʊ / (AmE), / ˌænti təˈbækəʊ / (BrE)
opposed to the use, sale, or promotion of tobacco products

Examples

Anti-tobacco laws
The city passed several anti-tobacco laws to protect public health.
Many schools have launched anti-tobacco programs to educate students about the dangers of smoking.
He supports anti-tobacco campaigns to reduce smoking among teenagers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anti-smoking - opposed to smoking or the promotion of smoking
  • antonym: pro-tobacco - supporting the use or promotion of tobacco products
