local post office

noun
nearest postal service branch
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ.kəl poʊst ˈɑː.fɪs / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl pəʊst ˈɒf.ɪs / (BrE)
a branch of the postal service in a particular area where people can send and receive mail and packages

Examples

The local post office
I went to the local post office to mail a birthday card to my sister.
Our local post office recently changed its opening hours.
The local post office is just a five-minute walk from my house.
