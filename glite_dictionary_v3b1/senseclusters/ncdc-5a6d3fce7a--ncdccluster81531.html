NCDC

noun
Nigerian public-health agency
6.0
Pronunciation: / ən si di si / (AmE), / ən siː diː siː / (BrE)
the Nigeria Centre for Disease Control, the Nigerian government agency responsible for disease surveillance, prevention, and outbreak response.

Examples

Nigeria NCDC alert
The NCDC issued an urgent travel advisory after several new cases were confirmed in the state.
Local hospitals coordinated with the NCDC to distribute vaccines and share testing data this morning.
During the outbreak, the minister praised the NCDC for its timely epidemiological reports and guidance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: CDC (United States) - the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, a similar national public-health agency
  • synonym: public health agency - a government body responsible for protecting and improving the health of the population
