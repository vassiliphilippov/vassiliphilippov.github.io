pitahaya

noun
edible fruit from cactus plant
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪtəˈhaɪə / (AmE), / ˌpɪtəˈhaɪə / (BrE)
A brightly colored edible fruit of certain cacti, typically with red or yellow skin and sweet flesh, also known as dragon fruit.

Examples

A ripe pitahaya fruit
Farmers markets often sell fresh pitahayas during the summer season.
I added slices of pitahaya to the fruit salad for extra color and taste.
The pitahaya has a vibrant pink skin and a sweet, refreshing flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dragon fruit - another common name for pitahaya
