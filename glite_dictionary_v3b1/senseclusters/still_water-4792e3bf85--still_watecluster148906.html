still water

noun
calm, unmoving water
1.4
Pronunciation: /stɪl ˈwɔː.tər/ /; / /stɪl ˈwɑː.tər/ (AmE), /stɪl ˈwɔː.tə/ (BrE)
water that is calm and not moving, typically found in lakes, ponds, or reservoirs

Examples

Calm still water

The canoe glided silently across the still water, leaving barely a ripple behind.
