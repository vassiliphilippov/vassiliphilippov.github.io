sinking feeling

noun
sudden onset of dread or anxiety
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋkɪŋ ˈfiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋkɪŋ ˈfiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
A sudden feeling of dread or apprehension, often associated with anxiety or a sense that something bad is about to happen.

Examples

A sinking feeling hits
A sinking feeling hit him when he heard the bad news.
As the lights went out, a sinking feeling settled in her stomach.
She had a sinking feeling as she realized she had left her passport at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foreboding - a feeling that something bad is going to happen
