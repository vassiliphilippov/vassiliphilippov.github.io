roll tape

verb
to begin recording (audio or video)
3.9
Pronunciation: / roʊl teɪp / (AmE), / rəʊl teɪp / (BrE)
to begin recording audio or video, especially on a tape recorder or video camera

Examples

To roll tape quickly

The director shouted, "Roll tape!" and the actors got into their positions.
They rolled tape as soon as the band started their set.
