gloxinia

noun
tropical plant with bell-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / ɡlɑkˈsɪniə / (AmE), / ɡlɒkˈsɪniə / (BrE)
A tropical plant of the genus Gloxinia or Sinningia, grown for its showy, bell-shaped flowers, often cultivated as a greenhouse plant.

Examples

A gloxinia flower
Greenhouse enthusiasts prize the gloxinia for its large, velvety petals.
She specialized in cultivating rare species, including the beautiful gloxinia.
The gloxinia on the windowsill bloomed in vibrant shades of purple and pink.
