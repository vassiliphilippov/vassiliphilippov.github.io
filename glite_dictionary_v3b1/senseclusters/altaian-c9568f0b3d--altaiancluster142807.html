Altaian

noun
person from Altai
Pronunciation: / ælˈtaɪən / (AmE), / ælˈtaɪən / (BrE)
a person who is a member of the Altaian ethnic group or comes from the Altai region of Siberia

Examples

An Altaian person
An Altaian elder explained the region's traditional stories to the visiting students.
The Altaian people have a rich cultural heritage rooted in the Altai Mountains.
Many Altaians continue to practice traditional crafts and music in their communities.
