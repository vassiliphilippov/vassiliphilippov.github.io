snapper

noun
a large, biting freshwater turtle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæpər / (AmE), / ˈsnæpə / (BrE)
a large freshwater turtle with a broad head and strong, hooked jaws, known for its tendency to bite; especially found in North America

Examples

A snapper turtle
