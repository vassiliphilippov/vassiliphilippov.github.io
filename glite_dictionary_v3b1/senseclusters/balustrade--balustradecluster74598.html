balustrade

verb
to add a railing
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbæl.ə.streɪd/ (AmE), /ˈbæl.ə.streɪd/ (BrE)
to provide or enclose a structure such as a balcony, staircase, or terrace with a balustrade.

Examples

To balustrade a balcony
They balustraded the new terrace to make it safer for children.
The architect decided to balustrade the balcony for both style and security.
After the renovation, the staircase was beautifully balustraded with carved wooden posts.
