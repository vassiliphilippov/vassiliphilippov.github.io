sluttishness

noun
the quality of being sluttish or slatternly
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌtɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈslʌtɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being sluttish or slatternly, especially in appearance or habits; untidiness or lack of cleanliness.

Examples

The sluttishness of home
He was appalled by the sluttishness of the once-immaculate home.
Some novels portray urban decay through the sluttishness of their characters' surroundings.
The landlady scolded her tenant for the sluttishness of his room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neatness - the quality of being clean and orderly
  • synonym: slovenliness - the quality of being untidy or careless in appearance or habits
