meted out

verb
distributed or given out, especially as punishment
7.1 0.35/m
Pronunciation: / mit a ät / (AmE), / miÀêt a ät / (BrE)
distributed or given out to people, especially in fixed portions or as punishment.

Examples

Justice meted out
Justice was meted out swiftly after the incident.
Punishments were meted out according to the severity of the offenses.
The judge meted out a harsh sentence to the defendant.
