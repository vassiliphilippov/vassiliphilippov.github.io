clothes horse

noun
frame for displaying clothes
3.1
Pronunciation: /kloʊðz hɔːrs/ /; / /kloʊz hɔːrs/ (AmE), /kləʊðz hɔːs/ /; / /kləʊz hɔːs/ (BrE)
a frame or stand used for hanging clothes, either to display them or to dry them indoors

Examples

A clothes horse for drying
The shop window featured a vintage clothes horse dressed in the latest fashion.
She hung her wet laundry on the clothes horse to dry overnight.
A wooden clothes horse stood in the corner, displaying colorful scarves and hats.
After doing the laundry, she hung the shirts on the clothes horse in the living room.
During rainy weather, a clothes horse is very useful for drying clothes indoors.
He folded the clothes horse and put it away once the clothes were dry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dress form - a model of the human torso used for fitting or displaying clothes
  • synonym: drying rack - a rack for drying clothes indoors
  • synonym: mannequin - a life-sized model of a human used to display clothes
  • antonym: washing line - a cord or wire for hanging clothes to dry outdoors
