pilling

noun
fabric fiber balls
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪl.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpɪl.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the formation of small balls of fiber on the surface of fabric due to wear or friction

Examples

Pilling on sweaters
Frequent washing can cause pilling on sweaters.
The new fabric is designed to resist pilling even after many washes.
She was disappointed to see pilling appear on her favorite shirt after a few months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lint - small fibers that come off fabric surfaces
  • synonym: snag - a pulled or broken thread in fabric
