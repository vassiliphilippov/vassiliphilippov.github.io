Indian rupee

noun
the currency of India
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ruːˈpiː / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ruːˈpiː / (BrE)
the official currency and basic unit of money in India, equal to 100 paise

Examples

The Indian rupee value
The Indian rupee gained value against the US dollar last week.
The price of the meal was 500 Indian rupees at a local restaurant.
Tourists often exchange dollars for Indian rupees when they arrive in Delhi.
