Synonyms

  • organization
    2.7 3/m
    organization arranges parts or people into a working system, while structure describes the pattern and relations among those parts.
  • configuration
    4.8 5/m
    configuration shows how parts are set up, often in a system or machine, while structure is the overall pattern they form.
  • arrangement
    2.7 6/m
    arrangement puts items in a chosen order or layout for use, while structure focuses on the lasting relations between those parts.
  • composition
    4.1 5/m
    composition names the parts and materials that make a whole, while structure shows how those parts fit and work together.
  • order
    1.0 80/m
    order puts things in a specific sequence or pattern, while structure describes the wider layout and relations of all parts.
