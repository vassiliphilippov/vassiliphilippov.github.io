neonatologist

noun
newborn specialist doctor
8.3
Pronunciation: /niː.ə.nə.ˈtɑː.lə.dʒɪst/ /; / /niː.oʊ.nə.ˈtɑː.lə.dʒɪst/ (AmE), /niː.ə.nə.ˈtɒl.ə.dʒɪst/ (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in the medical care of newborn babies, especially those who are ill or premature

Examples

The neonatologist checks babies
The neonatologist visited the intensive care unit to check on the premature twins.
After the complicated delivery, a neonatologist was called to examine the newborn.
Parents often meet with a neonatologist if their baby needs special medical attention after birth.
