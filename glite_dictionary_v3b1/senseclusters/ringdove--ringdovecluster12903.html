ringdove

noun
a Eurasian dove with a black neck ring
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋˌdʌv / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ.dʌv /; / ˈrɪŋˌdʌv / (BrE)
a large Eurasian dove or pigeon (Columba palumbus) with a black ring or white patches on its neck, commonly found in woodlands
