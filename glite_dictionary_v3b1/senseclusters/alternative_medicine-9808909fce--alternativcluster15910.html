alternative medicine

noun
non-conventional medical practices
1.8
Pronunciation: / ɔlˈtɝːnətɪv ˈmɛdəsən / (AmE), / ɔːlˈtɜːnətɪv ˈmɛdɪsɪn / (BrE)
medical treatments and practices not typically part of conventional Western medicine, often using natural remedies or non-drug methods

Examples

Alternative medicine treatments
Acupuncture and herbal remedies are popular forms of alternative medicine in many cultures.
Many people turn to alternative medicine to treat chronic pain when conventional therapies fail.
Some doctors have begun to integrate alternative medicine approaches alongside traditional treatments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complementary medicine - medical treatments used together with conventional medicine
  • antonym: conventional medicine - medical practices widely accepted and used by the mainstream healthcare system
