Dunkard

noun
member of a Christian sect practicing immersion baptism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌŋ.kərd / (AmE), / ˈdʌŋ.kəd / (BrE)
A member of a Christian sect, particularly one of the German Baptist Brethren, known for practicing baptism by immersion.

Examples

Dunkard Christian member
Many Dunkard traditions have been preserved through centuries of careful observance.

The Dunkard community is known for its commitment to peaceful living and simplicity.
