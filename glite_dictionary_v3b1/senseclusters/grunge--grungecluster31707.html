grunge

noun
1990s music style
4.8
Pronunciation: / ɡrʌndʒ / (AmE), / ɡrʌndʒ / (BrE)
a style of music and fashion characterized by a messy, unpolished look and sound, popular in the 1990s

Examples

Grunge music style
The band's raw sound and casual clothes made them icons of grunge in the early 1990s.
She decorated her room with posters of famous grunge musicians from Seattle.
Many teenagers adopted the grunge look, wearing flannel shirts and ripped jeans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alternative rock - a genre of rock music that differs from mainstream rock
  • antonym: pop - a genre of popular music with catchy melodies and simple lyrics
  • synonym: punk - a loud, fast-moving, and aggressive style of rock music
