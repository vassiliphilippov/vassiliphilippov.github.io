Wilsonia pusilla

noun
a small yellow songbird with a black crown
Pronunciation: / wɪlˈsoʊniə pjuˈsɪlə / (AmE), / wɪlˈsəʊniə pjuˈsɪlə / (BrE)
a small North American wood warbler with yellow plumage and a distinctive black crown, commonly known as Wilson's warbler
