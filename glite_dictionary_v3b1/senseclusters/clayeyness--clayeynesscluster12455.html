clayeyness

noun
quality of resembling clay
Pronunciation: / ˈkleɪ.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈkleɪ.i.nɪs /; / ˈkleɪ.i.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being like or containing clay, especially in texture or composition
