three-way calling

noun
phone feature for three-person conversation
Pronunciation: / ˈθriˌweɪ ˈkɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθriːweɪ ˈkɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
A telephone feature that allows three people to participate in the same conversation simultaneously without operator assistance.

Examples

With three-way calling, you can easily add a friend to your existing phone conversation.
