le sigh

interjection
dramatic interjection expressing disappointment
Pronunciation: / lə ˈsaɪ / (AmE), / lə ˈsiː / (BrE)
used as a humorous or dramatic way to express disappointment, frustration, or resignation, often imitating a theatrical sigh

Examples

Le sigh again
Le sigh, I can't believe the train is late again.
Le sigh, my favorite café is closed for renovations this week.
Every time the printer jams, I let out a dramatic le sigh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sigh - an audible exhalation expressing emotion or relief
