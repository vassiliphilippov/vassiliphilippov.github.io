family Lampridae

noun
a family of opah fish
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli læmˈpraɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli læmˈprɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of large, deep-bodied marine fishes known as opahs, found in warm and temperate oceans.

The family Lampridae fishes
