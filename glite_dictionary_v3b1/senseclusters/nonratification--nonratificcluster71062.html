nonratification

noun
failure to approve
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of not formally approving or confirming something, especially an agreement or treaty

Examples

The nonratification of treaty
The nonratification of the treaty caused uncertainty among the involved countries.
Due to the parliament's nonratification, the proposed changes could not take effect.
International organizations expressed concern over the nonratification of the climate agreement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ratification - the formal approval or confirmation of an agreement
  • synonym: rejection - the act of refusing to accept or approve something
