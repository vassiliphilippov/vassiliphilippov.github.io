coalhole

noun
container or cellar for coal storage
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊlˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlˌhəʊl / (BrE)
a compartment, bin, or small cellar in a building used for storing coal, often with an external cover at street level

Examples
