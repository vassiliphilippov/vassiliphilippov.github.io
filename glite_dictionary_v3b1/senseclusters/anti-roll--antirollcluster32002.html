anti-roll

noun
roll-preventing device
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈroʊl / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈrəʊl / (BrE)
a device or component installed on a vehicle, vessel, or load to prevent or reduce rolling motion from side to side

Examples

Install an anti-roll
The engineer recommended installing an anti-roll to improve the truck's stability on sharp turns.
Sailboats often use an anti-roll system to keep the vessel steady in rough seas.
Adding an anti-roll to the trailer helped prevent the cargo from shifting during transport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anti-sway bar - a bar fitted to vehicles to reduce side-to-side movement
  • synonym: stabilizer - a device that helps keep something steady or balanced
