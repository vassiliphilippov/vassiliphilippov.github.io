bleary

adjective
tired or unfocused in appearance
6.4 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈblɪ.riː/ /; / /ˈblɪr.iː/ (AmE), /ˈblɪə.riː/ (BrE)
(of eyes or vision) blurred, unfocused, or tired, usually because of lack of sleep or exhaustion

Examples

Bleary tired eyes
After staying up all night to finish the project, she arrived at work with bleary eyes.
He rubbed his bleary face, struggling to wake up on Monday morning.
The students looked bleary after cramming for their exams until sunrise.
After staring at the screen for hours, everything looked a bit bleary to her.
Through the rain-soaked window, the city lights appeared bleary and distant.
When he woke up, his bleary eyes struggled to focus on the clock.
