twit

noun
a foolish or annoying person
Pronunciation: / twɪt / (AmE), / twɪt / (BrE)
a silly or annoying person; someone regarded with mild contempt or derision

Examples

A silly twit
He made a complete twit of himself at the meeting by forgetting everyone's name.
Only a real twit would wear sandals in the snow.
Stop being such a twit and pay attention to what you're doing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgement
  • antonym: genius - a person of great intelligence
  • synonym: idiot - a very foolish or stupid person
