quartette

noun
group of four performers or singers
Pronunciation: / kwɔrˈtɛt / (AmE), / kwɔːˈtɛt / (BrE)
a group of four performers or singers who perform together, especially in music

Examples

Jazz quartette
She joined a jazz quartette that plays at local clubs every weekend.
The string quartette gave a stunning performance at the concert hall last night.
The vocal quartette harmonized beautifully during the festival.
Each quartette in the competition brought its own unique style to the stage.
He joined a vocal quartette that specializes in a cappella performances.
The jazz quartette played an energetic set at the club last night.

  • degree higher: quintet - a group of five people, especially musicians or singers
  • degree lower: trio - a group of three people, especially musicians or singers
