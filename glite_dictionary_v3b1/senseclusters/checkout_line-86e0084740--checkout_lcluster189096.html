checkout line

noun
line for paying at a store
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛkaʊt laɪn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛkaʊt laɪn / (BrE)
a line of people waiting to pay for their purchases at a store or supermarket

Examples

The checkout line
She realized she had forgotten her wallet when she was already in the checkout line.
The checkout line at the grocery store was so long that I almost gave up shopping.
We waited in the checkout line for almost twenty minutes before finally reaching the register.

Related Terms

  • synonym: checkout counter - the place where customers pay for their purchases in a store
  • synonym: queue - a line of people or vehicles waiting for something
