halberdier

noun
soldier armed with a halberd
Pronunciation: / ˌhælbərˈdɪr / (AmE), / ˌhæl.bəˈdɪə / (BrE)
a guard or soldier armed with a halberd, especially in European armies from the 15th to 17th centuries
