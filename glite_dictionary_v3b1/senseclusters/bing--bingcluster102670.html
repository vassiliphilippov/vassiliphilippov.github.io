bing

noun
coal mining waste heap
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪŋ / (BrE)
a large heap or pile of waste material left over from coal mining, especially in Scotland

Examples

Coal bing
The children climbed the old coal bing to watch the sunset over the valley.
A massive bing dominated the landscape near the abandoned mine.
Wildflowers have started to grow on the surface of the bing outside the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slag heap - a hill of waste material from mining or smelting
  • synonym: spoil heap - a pile of waste earth or rock removed during mining
