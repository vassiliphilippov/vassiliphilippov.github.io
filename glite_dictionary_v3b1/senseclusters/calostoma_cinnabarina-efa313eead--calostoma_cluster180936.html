Calostoma cinnabarina

noun
a type of gasteromycete fungus
Pronunciation: / ˌkæl.əˈstoʊ.mə ˌsɪn.ə.bəˈraɪ.nə / (AmE), / ˌkæl.əˈstɒ.mə ˌsɪn.ə.bəˈraɪ.nə / (BrE)
A species of gasteromycete fungus with a leathery stalk and a globose fruiting body containing a red spore case.
