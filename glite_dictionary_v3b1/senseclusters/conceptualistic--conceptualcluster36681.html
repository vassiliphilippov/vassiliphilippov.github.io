conceptualistic

adjective
relating to conceptualism
2.6
Pronunciation: / kənˌsɛp.tʃu.əˈlɪs.tɪk / (AmE), / kənˌsɛp.tjʊ.əˈlɪs.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of conceptualism, especially in philosophy or the arts.

Examples

The conceptualistic art
Critics praised the conceptualistic elements of the performance, highlighting its intellectual depth and innovation.
Her writing style became increasingly conceptualistic as she explored philosophical themes in her novels.
The artist's latest exhibition showcases a distinctly conceptualistic approach, focusing on ideas rather than visual realism.

Related Terms

  • antonym: realistic - relating to realism, often contrasted with conceptualistic in art or philosophy
