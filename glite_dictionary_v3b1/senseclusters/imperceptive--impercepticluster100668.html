imperceptive

adjective
lacking awareness
Pronunciation: / ˌɪmpərˈsɛptɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪmpəˈsɛptɪv / (BrE)
not noticing or not quick to notice things; lacking awareness or perception

Examples

An imperceptive person
He was so imperceptive that he failed to notice the obvious clues in the room.
Her imperceptive nature made it difficult for her to understand her friends' feelings.
The teacher found it challenging to work with students who were imperceptive to subtle hints.

Related Terms

  • antonym: perceptive - quick to notice or understand things
  • synonym: unobservant - not noticing details or changes
