spooner

noun
person who mixes sounds
Pronunciation: / ˈspuːnər / (AmE), / ˈspuːnə / (BrE)
a person who accidentally swaps the initial sounds or letters of words when speaking

Examples

A spooner slips
Everyone laughed when the spooner called the 'light rain' a 'right lane' during his speech.
As a child, she was a bit of a spooner, often mixing up her words in funny ways.
The teacher, a known spooner, amused the class with his accidental word swaps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spoonerism - an accidental swapping of sounds or letters in speech
