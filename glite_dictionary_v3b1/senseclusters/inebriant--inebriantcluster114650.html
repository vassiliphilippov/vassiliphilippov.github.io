inebriant

noun
alcoholic substance causing intoxication
9.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˈniːbriənt / (AmE), / ɪˈniːbrɪənt / (BrE)
A substance, especially a liquor or brew, that contains alcohol and causes intoxication.

Examples

An inebriant drink
Scientists studied the effects of different inebriants on the central nervous system.
She avoided all inebriant drinks at the party, choosing juice instead.
The law strictly limits the sale of inebriants to minors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alcoholic beverage - a drink that contains alcohol
  • synonym: intoxicant - a substance, especially alcohol, that intoxicates
