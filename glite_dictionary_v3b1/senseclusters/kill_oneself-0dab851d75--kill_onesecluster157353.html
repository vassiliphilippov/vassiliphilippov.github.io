kill oneself

verb
to overexert oneself; to work too hard
Pronunciation: / kɪl wʌnˌsɛlf / (AmE), / kɪl wʌnˌsɛlf / (BrE)
to work or strain oneself more than is healthy or necessary; to make an excessive effort

Examples

Don't kill yourself at work
Don't kill yourself trying to please everyone at work.
He was killing himself studying for the final exams every night.
She doesn't have to kill herself to get the job done on time.
