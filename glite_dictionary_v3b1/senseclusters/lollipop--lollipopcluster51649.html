lollipop

noun
hard candy on a stick
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːliˌpɑp / (AmE), / ˈlɒliˌpɒp / (BrE)
a hard, flavored candy mounted on a small stick and eaten by licking or sucking

Examples

A cherry lollipop
He unwrapped his favorite grape lollipop and started to suck on it slowly.
She gave her son a cherry lollipop as a reward for good behavior.
The children lined up to get a free lollipop from the booth at the fair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candy - sweet, confectionery treat made mainly from sugar
