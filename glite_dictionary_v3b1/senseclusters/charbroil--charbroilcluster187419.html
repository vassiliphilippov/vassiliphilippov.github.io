charbroil

noun
grill for charring
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːrˌbrɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːˌbrɔɪl / (BrE)
a grill or broiler that cooks food over direct high heat, giving it a slightly charred flavor

Examples

A charbroil grill
The chef placed the steaks on the charbroil to give them a smoky flavor.
They bought a new charbroil for their backyard barbecue parties.
The restaurant's signature burgers are cooked on a charbroil for extra taste.
