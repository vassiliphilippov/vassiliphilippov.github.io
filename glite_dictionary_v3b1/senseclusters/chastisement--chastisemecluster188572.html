chastisement

noun
a reprimand or rebuke
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæstɪzˌmɛnt /; / ˈtʃæstaɪzmənt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːstɪzmənt /; / ˈtʃæstɪzmənt / (BrE)
the act of criticizing, scolding, or rebuking someone for a mistake or wrongdoing
