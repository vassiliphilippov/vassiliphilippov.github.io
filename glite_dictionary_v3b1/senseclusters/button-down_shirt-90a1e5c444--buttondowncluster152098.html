button-down shirt

noun
shirt with buttons on collar points
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌbʌt̬.ənˌdaʊn ˈʃɝːt / (AmE), / ˌbʌt.ənˌdaʊn ˈʃɜːt / (BrE)
a shirt with a collar whose points fasten down with buttons

Examples

A blue button-down shirt
A blue button-down shirt can be both casual and professional.
He chose a crisp button-down shirt for his job interview.
Her favorite button-down shirt was missing a collar button.
