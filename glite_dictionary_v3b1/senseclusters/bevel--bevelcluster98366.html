bevel

verb
to cut an edge at an angle
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈbɛvəl / (BrE)
to cut or shape (an edge or surface) so that it is not perpendicular to the adjacent surfaces, but instead forms a sloping edge

Examples

To bevel a surface
The carpenter carefully beveled the edges of the table for a smoother finish.
The glass was beveled to create an elegant, decorative appearance.
You need to bevel the metal before welding the pieces together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chamfer - to cut an edge or corner to make a symmetrical sloping edge
