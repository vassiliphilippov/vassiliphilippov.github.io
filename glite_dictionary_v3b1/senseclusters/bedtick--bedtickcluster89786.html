bedtick

noun
mattress cover
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌtɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌtɪk / (BrE)
a mattress or large cloth bag filled with straw, feathers, or similar material, used for sleeping

Examples

A straw-filled bedtick
The old farmhouse still had a bedtick stuffed with straw in the guest room.
She repaired the torn bedtick before refilling it with fresh feathers.
During the camping trip, he slept on a simple bedtick laid out on the floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mattress - a large pad for supporting the body in bed
  • degree lower: pillowcase - a cloth cover for a pillow
