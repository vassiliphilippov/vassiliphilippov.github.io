rope of sand

noun
something unreliable or insubstantial
Pronunciation: / roʊp əv sænd / (AmE), / rəʊp əv sand / (BrE)
something that is insubstantial, unreliable, or cannot be depended on

Examples

A rope of sand breaks
All their assurances turned out to be a rope of sand when trouble came.
He realized his plans were built on a rope of sand and quickly fell apart.
Their alliance proved to be a rope of sand, dissolving under the slightest pressure.
