intellectualism

noun
emphasis on intellect
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌɪntəlɛkˈtjuːəlɪzəm /; / ˌɪntəlɛkˈtʃuːəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief in or emphasis on the importance of intellectual activity, reason, and knowledge
