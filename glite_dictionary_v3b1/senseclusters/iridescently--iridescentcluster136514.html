iridescently

adverb
with rainbow colors
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˈdɛsəntli / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈdɛsəntli / (BrE)
in a way that displays changing, rainbow-like colors that shimmer or shine.

Examples

Iridescently shining colors
The soap bubble floated iridescently in the sunlight, reflecting many colors.
Her dress sparkled iridescently under the stage lights during the performance.
The fish scales glimmered iridescently as they moved through the clear water.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dully - in a way that lacks brightness or shine
  • synonym: shimmeringly - in a way that shines with a flickering or wavering light
