gown

noun
protective garment for surgeons
Pronunciation: / ɡaʊn / (AmE), / ɡaʊn / (BrE)
A protective garment worn by surgeons or other medical staff during operations to maintain a sterile environment.

Examples

Surgical gown
Disposable gowns are commonly used to reduce the risk of infection during surgery.
Each member of the medical team changed into a clean gown for the procedure.
The surgeon donned a sterile gown before entering the operating room.
