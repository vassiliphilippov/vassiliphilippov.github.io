picture frame

noun
structure for holding a picture
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪktʃɚ freɪm / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə freɪm / (BrE)
A structure made to surround and support a picture, especially for display or protection.

Examples

Wooden picture frame
I accidentally knocked the picture frame off the shelf, and the glass shattered.
She placed the photograph in a beautiful picture frame on her desk.
The picture frame was made of carved wood and decorated with gold leaf.
