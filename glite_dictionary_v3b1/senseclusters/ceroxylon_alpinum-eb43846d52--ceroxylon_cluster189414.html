Ceroxylon alpinum

noun
Andean palm species yielding wax
Pronunciation: / sɛˌrɑksɪˌlɑn ælˈpaɪnəm / (AmE), / sɛˌrɒksɪˌlɒn ælˈpiːnəm / (BrE)
A species of palm native to the Andes, known for producing a resinous wax used in candle-making.

Examples
