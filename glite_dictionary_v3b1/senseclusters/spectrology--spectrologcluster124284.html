spectrology

noun
study of spectra
Pronunciation: / spɛkˈtrɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / spɛkˈtrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of spectra, especially those of light or radiation

Examples

Spectrology of light
She decided to pursue spectrology to better understand the composition of distant galaxies.
Advances in spectrology have helped scientists identify elements in stars.
The laboratory offers courses in spectrology for students interested in light analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: optics - the scientific study of light and vision
  • synonym: spectroscopy - the study of the interaction between matter and electromagnetic radiation
