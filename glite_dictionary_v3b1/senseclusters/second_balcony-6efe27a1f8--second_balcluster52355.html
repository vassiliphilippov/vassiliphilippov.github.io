second balcony

noun
uppermost balcony in a theatre
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛk.ənd ˈbæl.kəni / (AmE), / ˈsek.ənd ˈbæl.kəni / (BrE)
the rearmost or highest balcony area in a theatre, typically containing the least expensive seats

Examples

The second balcony seats
Her view from the second balcony was distant, but she could still enjoy the music.
The ushers directed us up several flights of stairs to reach the second balcony seats.
We could only afford tickets in the second balcony for the opening night performance.
