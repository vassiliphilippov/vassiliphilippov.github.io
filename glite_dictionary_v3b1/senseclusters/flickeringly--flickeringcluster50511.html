flickeringly

adverb
intermittently; briefly
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈflɪk.ər.ɪŋ.liː/ /; / /ˈflɪk.rɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈflɪk.ə.rɪŋ.liː/ /; / /ˈflɪk.rɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that is unsteady, brief, or with quick, repeated changes (especially of light or movement)

Examples

Light shines flickeringly
The candlelight danced flickeringly on the walls during the power outage.
Her hope returned flickeringly as she listened to the encouraging news.
The old television screen glowed flickeringly before finally turning off.
The old television screen glowed flickeringly in the dark room.
Fireflies moved flickeringly through the summer night, creating a magical scene.
The neon sign outside the diner shone flickeringly as the storm approached.
