Salmonella typhimurium

noun
species of Salmonella causing food poisoning
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsælməˈnɛlə taɪˌfɪmjʊˈriəm / (AmE), / ˌsælməˈniːlə taɪˌfɪmjʊˈriːəm / (BrE)
a species of Salmonella bacterium that commonly causes food poisoning in humans, leading to symptoms such as diarrhea and abdominal pain
