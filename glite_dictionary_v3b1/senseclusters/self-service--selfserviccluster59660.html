self-service

noun
practice of serving oneself
3.4 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈself.sɜːr.vɪs/ /; / /ˈself.sɜːr.vəs/ (AmE), /ˈself.sɜː.vɪs/ /; / /ˈself.sɜː.vəs/ (BrE)
A system or practice in which customers serve themselves, especially in shops or restaurants, rather than being served by an employee.

Examples

Self-service restaurant
At the self-service gas station, customers pump their own fuel.
Many supermarkets now use a self-service checkout system to speed up shopping.
The hotel offers a self-service breakfast buffet every morning.
