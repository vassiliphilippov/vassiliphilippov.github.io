drug dealer

noun
person illegally selling drugs
4.8 7.39/m
Pronunciation: / dr å…° Ààdil…ôr / (AmE), / dr å…° ÀàdiÀêl…ô / (BrE)
a person who sells illegal drugs, typically without a license

Examples

A drug dealer sells drugs
She was determined to help her brother avoid the influence of a local drug dealer.
The documentary explores the daily life of a young drug dealer in the city.
The police arrested a suspected drug dealer near the school yesterday.

Synonyms

  • drug peddler
    7.4 0.01/m
    a drug peddler sells illegal drugs in small, secret street deals without a license, while a drug dealer is the general term for such sellers.
  • pusher
    8.4 0.66/m
    a pusher sells drugs at street level in small amounts, while a drug dealer can refer to sellers at any scale.
  • peddler
    8.1 0.17/m
    a peddler moves from place to place selling goods or drugs, while a drug dealer specifically sells illegal drugs.
  • dealer
    6.5 11/m
    a dealer buys and sells illegal goods, often including stolen items, while a drug dealer focuses on selling illegal drugs.
