flash mob

noun
spontaneous group public performance
9.5 4.6
Pronunciation: / flæʃ mɑb / (AmE), / flæʃ mɒb / (BrE)
a group of people who assemble suddenly in a public place to perform an unusual or entertaining act, typically organized via social media, and then disperse quickly

Examples

A flash mob dance
A flash mob surprised shoppers by performing a choreographed dance in the middle of the mall.
She participated in a flash mob that performed a pillow fight in the park.
The video of the flash mob singing in the train station quickly went viral online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: performance art - art presented in a live performance, often in public settings
  • synonym: spontaneous - done or occurring as a result of a sudden impulse or inclination
