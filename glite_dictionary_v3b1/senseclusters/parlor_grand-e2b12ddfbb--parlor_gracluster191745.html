parlor grand

noun
a small grand piano
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrlɚ ˌɡrænd / (AmE), / ˈpɑːlə ˌɡrænd / (BrE)
a small grand piano, typically smaller than a concert grand, designed for use in homes or small venues

Examples

A parlor grand piano
A restored parlor grand can be a beautiful centerpiece in any home.
She practiced every day on her family's cherished parlor grand.
The parlor grand added elegance and rich sound to the living room.
The music teacher recommended purchasing a parlour grand for the recital space.
They bought a parlour grand to fit perfectly in their living room.
Unlike a concert grand, a parlour grand is better suited for smaller interiors.
