stuffed egg

noun
hard-boiled egg with seasoned yolk filling
3.7 2.0
Pronunciation: /stʌft eg/ (AmE), /stʌft eg/ (BrE)
A hard-boiled egg cut in half, with the yolk mixed with mayonnaise and seasonings, then placed back into the white.

Examples

I love adding a bit of paprika to my stuffed eggs for extra flavor.
