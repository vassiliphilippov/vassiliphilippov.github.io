koi

noun
ornamental carp
4.2
Pronunciation: / kɔɪ / (AmE), / kɔɪ / (BrE)
a brightly colored variety of carp often kept in outdoor ponds for decoration

Examples

Colorful koi fish
The tranquil pond was filled with colorful koi swimming gracefully beneath the surface.
Many people enjoy watching koi because of their vibrant patterns and calm movements.
She added a few new koi to her garden pond to create a more lively atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goldfish - a small, orange ornamental fish often kept in ponds or tanks
