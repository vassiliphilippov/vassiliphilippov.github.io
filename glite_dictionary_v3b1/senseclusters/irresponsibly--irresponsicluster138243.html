irresponsibly

adverb
without responsibility or care
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrəˈspɑːnsəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪrəˈspɒnsəbli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of responsibility or care for the consequences of one's actions.

Examples

To speak irresponsibly
He spoke irresponsibly about topics he knew nothing about.
She spent her savings irresponsibly and soon found herself in debt.
The chemicals were disposed of irresponsibly, harming the environment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: responsibly - in a responsible manner
