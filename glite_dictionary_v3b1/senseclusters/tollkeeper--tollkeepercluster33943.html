tollkeeper

noun
one who collects tolls
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊlˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈtəʊlˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to collect tolls at a gate, bridge, or road

Examples

The tollkeeper booth
As a tollkeeper, she worked long hours in a small booth by the roadside.
Every traveler had to stop and pay the tollkeeper before entering the city.
The old bridge still had a tollkeeper collecting coins from travelers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toll collector - a person who collects tolls, especially in modern contexts
