rib

verb
to form ribs in knitted fabric
Pronunciation: / rɪb / (AmE), / rɪb / (BrE)
to form raised, vertical lines in knitted fabric, typically as a decorative or structural feature

Examples

To rib a sweater
She decided to rib the cuffs of the sweater for extra elasticity.
Some scarves are ribbed to create a textured effect.
When you rib the fabric, it stretches more easily and fits snugly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ribbed - having a pattern or texture of raised lines, especially in fabric
