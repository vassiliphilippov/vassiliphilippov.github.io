vasovasostomy

noun
surgical reconnection of vas deferens after vasectomy
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌveɪsoʊˌvæsoʊˈstɑːmi / (AmE), / ˌveɪsəʊˌvəsəʊˈstɒmi / (BrE)
a surgical procedure to reconnect the vas deferens in order to restore fertility after a vasectomy

Examples

A vasovasostomy surgery
After his vasectomy, he opted for a vasovasostomy in hopes of conceiving another child.
The chances of success with a vasovasostomy depend on how long ago the vasectomy was performed.
The surgeon explained the risks and benefits of a vasovasostomy to the couple.
