posting

noun
record in financial accounts
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstɪŋ / (BrE)
an entry or record made in a company's financial accounts

Examples

Account posting
All incoming payments are recorded as a posting in the accounts ledger.
Late postings can lead to errors in the cash flow statement.
The auditor reviewed each posting for accuracy during the inspection.
