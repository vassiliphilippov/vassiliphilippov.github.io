cohost

noun
assistant property manager
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌhoʊst / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌhəʊst / (BrE)
a person who assists the main host in managing a short-term rental or online property listing, handling tasks like bookings and guest communication

Examples

Airbnb cohost
The property owner relied on a cohost to handle guest check-ins and answer questions.
As a cohost, she managed the cleaning schedule and responded to booking requests.
He became a cohost to help his friend manage the busy holiday rental season.
