family Ptilonorhynchidae

noun
the bowerbird family of birds
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪlənɔːˈrɪnkaɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˌpɪlənɔːˈrɪŋkɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of passerine birds known as bowerbirds, native to Australia and New Guinea, notable for the males' elaborate courtship bowers.
