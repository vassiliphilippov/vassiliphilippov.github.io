angiographic

adjective
relating to angiograms
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒiəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌandʒiəˈɡrafɪk / (BrE)
relating to the imaging of blood vessels or to images (angiograms) produced by angiography

Examples

Angiographic images
The angiographic findings confirmed the presence of a blocked artery.
Doctors reviewed the angiographic images before deciding on the best treatment.
An angiographic study can help detect abnormalities in blood vessels.
The angiographic procedure revealed a blockage in the patient's coronary artery.
Doctors rely on angiographic evidence to diagnose certain heart conditions.
Recent advances have improved the clarity of angiographic images in medical diagnostics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angiogram - an image of blood vessels produced by angiography
  • synonym: radiographic - relating to images produced by X-rays or other radiation
