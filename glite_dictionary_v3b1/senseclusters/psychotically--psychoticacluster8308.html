psychotically

adverb
extremely; intensely
8.9
Pronunciation: / saɪˈkɑtɪkli / (AmE), / saɪˈkɒtɪkli / (BrE)
in an extremely or unreasonably intense way; used informally as an intensifier.

Examples

Psychotically focused
He was psychotically focused on winning, barely stopping to eat or sleep.
The weather turned psychotically cold overnight, surprising everyone in the city.
She cleaned the house psychotically before her guests arrived, leaving every surface spotless.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extremely - to a very great degree
  • synonym: insanely - to an extreme or unreasonable degree
  • antonym: mildly - to a small or moderate degree
