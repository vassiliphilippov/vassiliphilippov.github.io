service woman

noun
female repair worker
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːvɪs ˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs ˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman whose job is to maintain or repair equipment, appliances, or systems.

Examples

A service woman technician
After training, she became the first service woman in her company.
If you need help with your plumbing, a qualified service woman can come to your house.
The washing machine was fixed by an experienced service woman within an hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repairwoman - a woman whose occupation is to repair things
  • antonym: serviceman - a man whose job is to repair things
