snowball

noun
ball of crushed ice with fruit syrup
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a sweet, round treat made of ice or ice cream, often with syrup or coconut, named for its resemblance to a ball of snow
