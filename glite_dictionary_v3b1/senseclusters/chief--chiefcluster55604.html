chief

noun
leader of a tribe or group
Pronunciation: / tʃif / (AmE), / tʃiːf / (BrE)
the leader or person with the highest authority in a group, organization, or community

Examples

He quickly rose through the ranks to become the chief of his department.
The chief addressed the company during the morning meeting.
After years of hard work, she was promoted to chief of operations.
Before making any big decisions, you should consult the chief.
The chief called a meeting to discuss workplace safety protocols.
