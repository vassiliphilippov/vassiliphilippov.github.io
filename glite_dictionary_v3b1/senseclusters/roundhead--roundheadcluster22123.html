roundhead

noun
person with a broad, rounded head
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊndˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈraʊndˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person with a head that is short and broad, having a rounded shape (brachycephalic)

Examples
