thereto

adverb
to that (formal use)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ðɛrˈtu / (AmE), / ðəˈtuː / (BrE)
to that; to the thing just mentioned or referred to, especially in formal or legal contexts.

Examples

Attached thereto
Access will be granted to the property and any buildings attached thereto.
All required documents and amendments thereto must be submitted by the end of the month.
The contract and all rights appertaining thereto shall be governed by this agreement.
