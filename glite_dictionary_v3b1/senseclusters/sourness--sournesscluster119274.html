sourness

noun
the quality of being sour or acidic
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊrnəs / (AmE), / ˈsaʊənəs /; / ˈsaʊənɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having a sharp, acidic taste, like lemon juice, vinegar, or yogurt

Examples

Lemon sourness
A hint of sourness in the wine indicated it had gone bad.
He prefers yogurt with just the right amount of sourness for a refreshing taste.
The sourness of the lemonade made her lips pucker.
She likes the sourness of yogurt more than its sweetness.
The sourness in the sauce balanced the richness of the meat.
The sourness of the lemon made my mouth pucker immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acidity - the quality of being acid; sourness
  • degree lower: bitterness - the quality of having a harsh, sharp taste, less acidic than sourness
  • antonym: sweetness - the quality of being sweet in taste or character
