sourness

noun
the quality of being sour or acidic
2.9 1.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsaʊr.nəs/ /; / /ˈsaʊ.ər.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈsaʊə.nəs/ /; / /ˈsaʊ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality or state of having a sharp, acidic taste, like lemon juice, vinegar, or yogurt

Examples
