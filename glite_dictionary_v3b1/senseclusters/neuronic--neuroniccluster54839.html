neuronic

adjective
relating to neurons
Pronunciation: / nʊˈrɑː.nɪk / (AmE), / njʊəˈrɒn.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to neurons, the nerve cells that transmit information in the nervous system.

Examples

neuronic degeneration
Certain disorders are linked to neuronic degeneration in the brain.
Modern treatments aim to restore neuronic functions lost after injury.
The researchers observed changes in neuronic activity during the test.
