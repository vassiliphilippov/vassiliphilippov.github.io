pop tent

noun
quickly erected small tent
6.4
Pronunciation: / pɑp tɛnt / (AmE), / pɒp tɛnt / (BrE)
a small, lightweight tent that can be easily carried and quickly set up, typically by unfolding or 'popping' into shape

Examples

A pop tent
After hiking all day, she appreciated how quickly the pop tent sprang into shape.
Many festival-goers prefer a pop tent since it saves time and effort when camping.
We brought a pop tent for our camping trip because it was so easy to set up.
