reopener

noun
one who reopens
5.6
Pronunciation: / riˈoʊpənər / (AmE), / riˈəʊpənə / (BrE)
a person or thing that reopens something that was previously closed

Examples

Reopener of the store
After renovations, the reopener of the library was celebrated by the whole community.
The reopener of the negotiations brought hope for a new agreement.
As the reopener of the case, the detective found new evidence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: closer - a person or thing that closes something
  • synonym: restorer - a person who brings something back to its former condition
