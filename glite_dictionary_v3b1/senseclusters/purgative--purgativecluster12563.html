purgative

adjective
strongly laxative (adj.)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːɡətɪv / (AmE), / ˈpɜːɡətɪv / (BrE)
(of a medicine or substance) having a strong laxative effect; tending to cause evacuation of the bowels.
