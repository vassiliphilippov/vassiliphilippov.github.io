cell nucleus

noun
membrane-bound control center of a cell
2.5 2.7
Pronunciation: /sel ˈnuː.kliː.əs/ /; / /sel ˈnjuː.kliː.əs/ (AmE), /sel ˈnjuː.kliː.əs/ /; / /sel ˈnuː.kliː.əs/ (BrE)
