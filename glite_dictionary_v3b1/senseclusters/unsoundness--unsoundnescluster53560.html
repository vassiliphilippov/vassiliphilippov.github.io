unsoundness

noun
the condition of being damaged or decayed
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsaʊnd.nəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈsaʊnd.nəs / (BrE)
The state or condition of being damaged, decayed, or not structurally sound.

Examples

Structural unsoundness
Doctors found unsoundness in the horse's leg, making it unfit for racing.
