precious

adjective
affectedly cute or charming
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛʃəs / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃ.əs / (BrE)
(of a performance, manner, etc.) affectedly or excessively delicate, cute, or charming, often in a way that seems contrived or insincere.

Examples

Precious behavior
Her precious gestures seemed more irritating than endearing.
The actor's precious delivery made the audience cringe rather than laugh.
The critic described the film as charming at times but occasionally too precious for its own good.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affected - artificial or insincere in manner
  • antonym: natural - genuine, not affected or contrived in manner
