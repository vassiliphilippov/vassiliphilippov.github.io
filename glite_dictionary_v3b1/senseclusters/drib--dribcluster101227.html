drib

verb
to move ball by touches
7.0
Pronunciation: /drɪb/ (AmE), /drɪb/ (BrE)
to move a ball forward by making repeated small touches or controlled bounces, especially in sports like football or basketball

Examples

To drib a ball

If you drib too slowly, the defenders will catch up to you.
She tried to drib past her opponent but lost control of the ball.
