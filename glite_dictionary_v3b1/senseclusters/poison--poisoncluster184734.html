poison

verb
kill or harm with poison
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪzən / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzən / (BrE)
to harm or kill a person, animal, or plant by giving them a poisonous substance

Examples

To poison the king

The villain planned to poison the king to seize the throne.
Polluted water may poison an entire fish population.
Some wild mushrooms can poison animals that eat them.
The villain tried to poison the king by adding cyanide to his wine.
