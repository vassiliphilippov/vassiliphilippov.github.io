minute hand

noun
hand on a clock showing minutes
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnət hænd / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪt hand / (BrE)
the long hand on a clock or watch that shows the minutes

Examples

The minute hand moves
He adjusted his watch so that the minute hand pointed exactly at twelve.
I glanced at the minute hand to see how much time was left.
The minute hand moved steadily around the clock face.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hour hand - the hand on a clock or watch that shows the hours
