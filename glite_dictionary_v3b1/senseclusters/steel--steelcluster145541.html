steel

verb
prepare oneself mentally for difficulty
9.1
Pronunciation: / stil / (AmE), / stiːl / (BrE)
to prepare yourself mentally to face something difficult or unpleasant with determination or courage

Examples

To steel yourself
He steeled himself for the criticism he knew was coming.
She tried to steel herself before walking into the interview.
You have to steel your nerves when delivering bad news.
