Australorp

noun
Australian chicken breed
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɑstrəˌlɔrp / (AmE), / ˈɒstrəlɔːp / (BrE)
a breed of chicken developed in Australia, known for its excellent egg-laying ability and hardiness

Examples

The Australorp chicken
The Australorp is prized by farmers for its consistent egg production throughout the year.
She chose an Australorp because of its calm temperament and glossy black feathers.
Many backyard chicken keepers recommend the Australorp for beginners due to its hardiness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Leghorn - a breed of chicken known for high egg production
  • synonym: Rhode Island Red - a popular American chicken breed valued for eggs and meat
