counterrevolutionist

noun
person seeking to reverse a revolution
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊn.tər.rɛ.vəˈluː.ʃə.nɪst / (AmE), / ˌkaʊn.tə.rɛ.vəˈluː.ʃə.nɪst / (BrE)
A person who seeks to reverse the changes or overthrow the regime established by a previous revolution.

Examples

A counterrevolutionist group
Historians debate the motives of the counterrevolutionist factions during the civil war.
Many counterrevolutionists were exiled after trying to restore the previous monarchy.
The new regime quickly labeled its opponents as counterrevolutionists and suppressed their activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterrevolutionary - a person or action opposing a revolution or revolutionary government
