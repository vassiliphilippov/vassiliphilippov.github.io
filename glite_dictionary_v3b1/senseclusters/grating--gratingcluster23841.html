grating

noun
frame of iron bars for a fire
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a frame or cover made of parallel or crossed bars, used as a barrier or to hold something (such as in a fireplace or over an opening)

Examples

Metal grating cover
Ash often falls through the grating into the tray below.
She set the logs carefully onto the grating before lighting the fire.
The old cottage still had an iron grating in its fireplace.
He peered through the grating to see what was happening in the next room.
Sunlight filtered through the grating and cast patterns on the floor.
The metal grating over the drain prevents debris from clogging the pipes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grate - framework of iron bars for holding fuel in a fireplace
  • synonym: grid - a framework of spaced bars that are parallel to or cross each other; a grating
