snowscape

noun
snowy landscape art
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌskeɪp / (BrE)
a view or picture of a landscape covered with snow

Examples

A snowscape painting
The gallery displayed a beautiful snowscape painted by a local artist.
She photographed the mountain snowscape just as the sun was rising.
A large snowscape hung in the living room, making the space feel calm and cool.
After the heavy snowfall, the park was transformed into a magical snowscape.
The artist painted a beautiful snowscape showing trees blanketed in white.
We enjoyed hiking through the quiet snowscape early in the morning.

  • synonym: winterscape - a scene or view typical of winter
