grass-of-Parnassus

noun
bog plant with pale, buttercup-like flower

Pronunciation: / ˌgræs əv pɑːrˈnæsəs / (AmE), / ˌɡrɑːs əv pɑːˈnasəs / (BrE)
A perennial bog plant of the genus Parnassia, with broad smooth basal leaves and solitary pale, buttercup-like flowers, found in wet habitats.
