doggy bag

noun
bag for restaurant leftovers
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːɡi bæg / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡi bæɡ / (BrE)
a bag or container provided by a restaurant for taking home leftover food

Examples

A doggy bag from restaurant
After dinner, she asked the waiter for a doggy bag to take her leftovers home.
He put the uneaten steak in a doggy bag for lunch the next day.
Restaurants often provide a doggy bag if you can't finish your meal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: takeout - food purchased from a restaurant for eating elsewhere
