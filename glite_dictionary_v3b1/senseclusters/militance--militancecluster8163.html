militance

noun
militant or combative attitude
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪtəns / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəns / (BrE)
the quality of being militant, especially showing aggression or a combative attitude in support of a cause

Examples

Her militance on issues
Her militance on environmental issues earned her both admirers and critics.
Some activists questioned whether such militance would achieve the desired results.
The group's militance unnerved local authorities, who feared violent protests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: passivity - lack of resistance or action; the opposite of aggressiveness
