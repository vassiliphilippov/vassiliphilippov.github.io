north by west

noun
compass point west of due north
8.4
Pronunciation: / nɔrθ baɪ wɛst / (AmE), / nɔːθ baɪ west / (BrE)
The compass point one point west of due north, corresponding to 348°45′.

Examples

North by west compass point
He adjusted his compass to point north by west before starting the hike.
The ship set a course of north by west to avoid the reef.
The wind was blowing from north by west, bringing cooler air across the hills.
