horrifyingly

adverb
in a horrifying manner
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔːrɪˌfaɪɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈhɒrɪˌfaɪɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that causes horror; in a way that is extremely shocking or frightening.

Examples

To laugh horrifyingly
He laughed horrifyingly, sending chills down everyone's spine.
The building collapsed horrifyingly quickly after the explosion.
The news was horrifyingly graphic, leaving viewers deeply unsettled.
