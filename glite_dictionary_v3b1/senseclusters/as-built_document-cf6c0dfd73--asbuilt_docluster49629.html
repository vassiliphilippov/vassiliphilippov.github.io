as-built document

noun
record of completed construction project
6.3
Pronunciation: / æz bɪlt ˈdɑːkjəmənt / (AmE), / æz bɪlt ˈdɒkjʊmənt / (BrE)
an official record detailing the final specifications and changes of a construction project as it was actually completed

Examples

The as-built document record
The architect reviewed the as-built document to ensure all specifications were followed.
The contractor submitted the as-built document at the end of the project for verification.
Without an as-built document, future renovations could encounter unexpected challenges.
