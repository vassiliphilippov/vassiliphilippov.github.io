aurist

noun
ear specialist doctor
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrɪst / (AmE), / ˈɔːrɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in the treatment of ear diseases and disorders

Examples

An aurist doctor
In the 19th century, an aurist would often treat patients with hearing loss.
She visited an aurist to seek advice about her persistent ear pain.
The aurist recommended a new treatment for his chronic ear infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: otologist - a medical specialist in ear diseases
