Pilates

noun
exercise system
3.7
Pronunciation: / pɪˈlɑːtiz / (AmE), / pɪˈlɑːtiːz / (BrE)
a system of physical exercises designed to improve strength, flexibility, and posture, often performed on a mat or with special equipment

Examples

Pilates class

Many people use Pilates to strengthen their core muscles and improve flexibility.
