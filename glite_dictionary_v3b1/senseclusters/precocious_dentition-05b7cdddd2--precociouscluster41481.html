precocious dentition

noun
early eruption of teeth
8.4
Pronunciation: / prɪˈkoʊʃəs dɛnˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / prɪˈkəʊʃəs dɛnˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
the eruption of teeth at an unusually early age, earlier than is typical for development

Examples

Precocious dentition in babies
Cases of precocious dentition can indicate underlying medical conditions in newborns.
Parents were surprised when their child showed signs of precocious dentition at only three months old.
The pediatrician noted precocious dentition in the infant during the examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: teething - the process by which a baby's teeth emerge through the gums
