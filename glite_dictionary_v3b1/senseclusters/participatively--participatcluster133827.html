participatively

adverb
with active involvement
Pronunciation: / pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪtɪvli / (AmE), / pɑːˈtɪsɪpətɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves active participation by people

Examples

To act participatively
The committee made decisions participatively, ensuring everyone had a voice in the process.
Students learned more effectively when they engaged participatively in classroom discussions.
The project was managed participatively, with input from all team members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaboratively - in a way that involves working together with others
  • antonym: independently - in a way that does not involve others
