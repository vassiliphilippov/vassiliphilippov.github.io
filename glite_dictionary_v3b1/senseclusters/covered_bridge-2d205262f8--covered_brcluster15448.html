covered bridge

noun
bridge with a roof and sides
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvərd brɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkʌvəd brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a bridge with a roof and enclosed sides protecting the passageway

Examples

A covered bridge
Many covered bridges were originally built to protect the wooden structure from the elements.
The old covered bridge was a popular spot for tourists taking photographs.
We crossed a charming covered bridge on our drive through the countryside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bridge - a structure carrying a road, path, or railway across a river, road, or other obstacle
