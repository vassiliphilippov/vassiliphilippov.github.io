tramway

noun
tracks for trams or streetcars
Pronunciation: / ˈtræmˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈtræm.weɪ / (BrE)
a set of rails or tracks on which trams or streetcars travel

Examples

City tramway tracks
A narrow tramway runs through the city center, connecting several districts.
Construction workers repaired the damaged tramway after last night's storm.
The old tramway was removed to make space for a new road.
