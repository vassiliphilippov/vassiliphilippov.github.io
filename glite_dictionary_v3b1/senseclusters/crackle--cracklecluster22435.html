crackle

noun
series of sharp snapping sounds
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrækəl / (AmE), / ˈkræk.əl / (BrE)
a series of short, sharp, snapping or popping sounds

Examples

A crackle on radio
She listened to the crackle on the radio as the signal faded in and out.
The crackle of the burning logs filled the room with a cozy atmosphere.
There was a crackle of static before the announcer started speaking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pop - a short, sharp sound like a small explosion
  • synonym: snap - a sudden, sharp cracking noise
