agria

noun
yellow-fleshed potato
Pronunciation: / əˈɡriːə / (AmE), / əˈɡriːə / (BrE)
a variety of potato with yellow flesh, commonly used for frying and cooking

Examples

The agria potato
The chef prefers to use agria for making golden, crispy French fries.
We bought a bag of agria at the market for tonight's dinner.
Because of its texture, agria is excellent for roasting and mashing.
