staunch supporter

noun
loyal and firm advocate
7.0
Pronunciation: / stɔntʃ səˈpɔrtər / (AmE), / stɔːntʃ səˈpɔːtə / (BrE)
a person who is very firm and loyal in supporting a cause, organization, or individual

Examples

A staunch supporter of team
Even in difficult times, he remained a staunch supporter of the team.
She has always been a staunch supporter of equal rights.
The mayor thanked every staunch supporter for their unwavering commitment to the campaign.

Related Terms

  • synonym: steadfast supporter - a person who remains consistently loyal while supporting something
