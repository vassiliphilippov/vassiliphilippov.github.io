gun shop

noun
firearms store
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌn ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈɡʌn ʃɒp / (BrE)
a store where firearms and related equipment are sold

Examples

The gun shop entrance
He visited the gun shop to purchase ammunition for his hunting trip.
The gun shop offers safety courses for new firearm owners.
Local regulations require background checks at every gun shop in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armory - a place where weapons and military equipment are stored or sold
  • synonym: pawnshop - a shop where personal items are exchanged for loans and sometimes firearms are sold
