cracker

noun
a paper party favor that pops when pulled
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrækər / (AmE), / ˈkrækə / (BrE)
a decorated paper tube that makes a sharp noise when pulled apart and typically contains a small toy or gift, used as a party favor, especially at Christmas

Examples

Pull a Christmas cracker
Children squealed with delight as the cracker exploded, revealing a tiny toy inside.
Each guest was given a cracker to pull at the Christmas table.
He bought a box of festive crackers for the holiday celebration.

Related Terms
