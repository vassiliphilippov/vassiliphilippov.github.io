cow's head

noun
a kind of morel mushroom
Pronunciation: / kaʊz hɛd / (AmE), / kaʊz hɛd / (BrE)
a morel mushroom (Gyromitra gigas) with a ridged and pitted cap attached to the stem for about half its length

Examples

A cow's head mushroom
During their hike, they found a cow's head growing at the base of a pine tree.
He showed us how to identify a cow's head by its distinct ridged and pitted cap.
The chef added sliced cow's head to the mushroom risotto for an earthy flavor.
