Synonyms

  • propose
    3.0 84/m
    propose is more formal and often used for serious plans or official offers, while suggest is more general and casual.
  • put forward
    3.2 4.21/m
    put forward is a neutral phrasal verb used in discussion or meetings, while suggest is more everyday and flexible.
  • float
    5.3 6.49/m
    float is to offer an idea tentatively for others to react to, while suggest covers offering ideas more openly or casually.
  • advance
    3.9 3.52/m
    advance is to present a plan formally or argue it as new, while suggest is simply to offer an idea for consideration.
