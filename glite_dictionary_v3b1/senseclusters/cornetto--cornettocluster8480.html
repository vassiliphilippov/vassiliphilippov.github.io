cornetto

noun
historic wind instrument
Pronunciation: / kɔrˈnɛtoʊ / (AmE), / kɔːˈnɛtəʊ / (BrE)
a curved wooden wind instrument from the Renaissance and Baroque periods, with finger holes and a small mouthpiece

Examples

Baroque cornetto solo
The musician played a beautiful solo on the cornetto during the Renaissance concert.
A skilled cornetto player is essential for authentic Baroque ensemble performances.
The museum displayed a rare cornetto alongside other historical instruments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recorder - a woodwind instrument with finger holes and a whistle mouthpiece
  • synonym: trumpet - a brass wind instrument with a bright, powerful sound
