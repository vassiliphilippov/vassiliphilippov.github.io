epistemically

adverb
relating to knowledge
9.6
Pronunciation: /e.pɪ.ˈstem.ɪ.kəl.i/ /; / /ɪ.pɪ.ˈstem.ɪ.kəl.i/ (AmE), /e.pɪ.ˈstem.ɪ.kəl.i/ /; / /ɪ.pɪ.ˈstem.ɪ.kəl.i/ (BrE)

Examples

The argument is epistemically sound because it is based on reliable evidence.
