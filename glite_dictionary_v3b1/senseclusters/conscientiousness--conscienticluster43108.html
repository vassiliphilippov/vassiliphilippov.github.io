conscientiousness

noun
quality of being thorough and responsible
1.5 7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.ʃiˈen.ʃəs.nəs / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.ʃiˈen.ʃəs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being careful, thorough, and responsible in doing work or duties, often because of a strong sense of right and wrong

Examples

High conscientiousness level

Employers value conscientiousness in workers who reliably complete their tasks with great care.
Her conscientiousness made her an exemplary student who always submitted perfect assignments.
