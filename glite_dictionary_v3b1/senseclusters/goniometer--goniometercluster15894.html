goniometer

noun
angle-measuring instrument
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɡoʊniˈɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / ˌɡəʊniˈɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure angles, especially in joints or crystals

Examples

A goniometer measures angles
The physical therapist used a goniometer to assess the patient's range of motion.
A goniometer is essential for measuring the precise angles in crystal structures.
Students learned how to use a goniometer during their laboratory session on joint flexibility.
