sacerdotal

adjective
relating to priests or the priesthood
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsæsərˈdoʊtəl /; / ˌsæsɚˈdoʊtəl / (AmE), / ˌsasəˈdəʊtəl /; / ˌsæsəˈdəʊtəl / (BrE)
relating to priests or the priesthood; priestly

Examples

Sacerdotal traditions
Her writings critiqued the sacerdotal traditions within the religious community.

The ancient text describes sacerdotal duties in elaborate detail.
The bishop donned his sacerdotal robes for the ceremony.
Their chanting had a distinctly sacerdotal quality, as if invoking sanctity.
