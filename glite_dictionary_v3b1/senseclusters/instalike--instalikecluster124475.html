instalike

noun
thing deserving instant like (social media)
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnstəˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɪnstəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
something viewed as so good or appealing that it deserves an instant 'like' on social media platforms.

Examples

A real instalike
Her creative cake design was a total instalike among her friends on Instagram.
That travel photo is an instalike—the composition and colors are just perfect.
The puppy video was so adorable, it was an obvious instalike for everyone who watched it.
