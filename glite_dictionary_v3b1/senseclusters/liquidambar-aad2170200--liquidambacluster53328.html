Liquidambar

noun
a genus of trees; sweet gum
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪkwɪdˈæmbɑr / (AmE), / ˌlɪkwɪdˈæmbɑː / (BrE)
a genus of deciduous trees known as sweet gum, with star-shaped leaves and spiky or spiny fruit, native to warm regions of the Americas and Asia
