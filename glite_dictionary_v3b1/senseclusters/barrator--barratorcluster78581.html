barrator

noun
person guilty of barratry
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbærətər / (AmE), / ˈbærətə / (BrE)
A person who is guilty of barratry, especially one who persistently instigates lawsuits.

Examples

As a notorious barrator, he had a reputation for constantly engaging in vexatious litigation.

The lawyer was accused of being a barrator for filing frivolous lawsuits.
