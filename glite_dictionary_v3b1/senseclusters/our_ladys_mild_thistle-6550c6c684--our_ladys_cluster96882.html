Our Lady's mild thistle

noun
a thistle with blotched leaves; Silybum marianum
9.9
Pronunciation: / aʊr ˈleɪdiz ˈmaɪld ˈθɪsəl / (AmE), / aʊə ˈleɪdiz ˈmaɪld ˈθɪsəl / (BrE)
A tall biennial thistle of the Old World with large, white-blotched leaves and purple flower heads, also known as milk thistle (Silybum marianum), often naturalized in California and South America.
