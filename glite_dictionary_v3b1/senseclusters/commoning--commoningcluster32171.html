commoning

verb
to share resources collectively
8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.mən.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈkɑː.mɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkɒm.ən.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈkɒm.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
to manage, use, or share land or resources collectively, especially according to traditional or communal rights

Examples

To common the fields
For centuries, villagers have been commoning the meadows to graze their animals.
The community is commoning the riverbank, allowing everyone to fish and gather water.
By commoning the land, the group ensures fair access to resources for all members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: communalize - to make something shared by a community
  • antonym: privatize - to transfer from public or communal to private ownership
