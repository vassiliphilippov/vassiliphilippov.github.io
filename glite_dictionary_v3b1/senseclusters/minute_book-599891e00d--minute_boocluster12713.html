minute book

noun
book for recording meeting minutes
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnɪt bʊk / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪt bʊk / (BrE)
A book in which the written records (minutes) of meetings are officially kept.

Examples

The minute book records
Every organization's minute book must be kept up to date and stored safely.
The auditors checked the minute book to verify the board's decisions over the past year.
The company secretary placed the signed minutes in the minute book for future reference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minutes - written records of what was said and decided at a meeting
