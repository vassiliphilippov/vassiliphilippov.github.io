Synonyms

  • engraved
    4.3 0.02/m
    engraved is cut or pressed into a surface for precise letters, while inscribed means words or designs written, carved, or engraved.
  • graven
    9.8 0.20/m
    graven is an older word for images cut into stone, while inscribed is modern and used for writing on surfaces.
  • incised
    7.0 0.00/m
    incised is cut into a surface with a sharp tool leaving fine lines, while inscribed covers any writing or carving.
  • etched
    5.1 0.01/m
    etched is made by cutting or chemical biting for artistic detail, while inscribed is a general term for writing on surfaces.
  • carved
    3.1 0.00/m
    carved is shaped by cutting and may mean whole objects or decoration, while inscribed focuses on written or engraved marks.
