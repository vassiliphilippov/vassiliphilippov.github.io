Synonyms

  • drawing pin
    2.3 0.04/m
    a drawing pin is the British name for a short flat-headed pin used to fix papers on boards, while thumbtack is the US term.
  • pushpin
    1.6 0.15/m
    a pushpin has a rounded or plastic head and sticks into soft bulletin boards, while a thumbtack has a flat metal head.
  • tack
    2.2 0.81/m
    a tack is a small nail used for many fastening tasks, while a thumbtack is mainly for holding papers on boards.
