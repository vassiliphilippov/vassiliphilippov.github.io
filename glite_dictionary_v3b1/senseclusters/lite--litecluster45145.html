lite

adjective
reduced-feature, lightweight product version
4.2 0.01/m

(of a product or software) having reduced features, smaller size, or lower resource requirements so it runs on older or limited devices or uses less data.

Examples

Lite app version
The lite version of the app runs smoothly on older phones.
