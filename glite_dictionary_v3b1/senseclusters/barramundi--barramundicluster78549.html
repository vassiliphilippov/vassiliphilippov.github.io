barramundi

noun
large edible Australian fish
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌbær.əˈmʌn.di / (AmE), / ˌbær.əˈmʊn.di / (BrE)
A large edible Australian fish (Lates calcarifer) found in marine, estuarine, and freshwater habitats, valued for its firm white flesh and sporting qualities.

Examples

Grilled barramundi fish
The chef served grilled barramundi with lemon and herbs for a delicious dinner.
The river is home to both saltwater crocodiles and barramundi.
Tourists travel to northern Australia to catch the famous barramundi during the fishing season.
