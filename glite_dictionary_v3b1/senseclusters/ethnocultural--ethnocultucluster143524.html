ethnocultural

adjective
relating to ethnicity and culture
Pronunciation: / ˌɛθnoʊˈkʌltʃərəl / (AmE), / ˌɛθnəʊˈkʌltʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to both ethnicity and culture, or to the cultural characteristics of an ethnic group

Examples

Ethnocultural diversity
The museum features exhibits that highlight the ethnocultural diversity of the region.
Many schools celebrate ethnocultural events to promote understanding among students.
Her research focuses on ethnocultural influences in modern literature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cultural - relating to the ideas, customs, and social behavior of a society
  • synonym: ethnic - relating to a particular race or group of people
