layby

noun
roadside stopping area for vehicles
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪˌbaɪ / (AmE), / ˈleɪbʌɪ / (BrE)
A designated paved area at the side of a main road where vehicles can stop temporarily.

Examples

A layby on road
The police asked drivers to wait in the layby while the accident was cleared.
There was a food truck parked in the layby off the highway.
We pulled into a layby to check the map and stretch our legs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rest area - an area beside a road where travelers can stop and rest
  • degree lower: shoulder - the edge of a road suitable for emergency stopping
