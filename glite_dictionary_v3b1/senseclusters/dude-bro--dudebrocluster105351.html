dude-bro

noun
boisterous, fitness-focused young man
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈduːdˌbroʊ / (AmE), / ˈdjuːdˌbrəʊ / (BrE)
A young man, typically college-aged, who is stereotypically focused on fitness, partying, and behavior considered boisterously masculine and superficial.

Examples

A dude-bro party
Despite his dude-bro image, Tyler is actually very considerate and hardworking.
Jake's a total dude-bro—he spends every weekend partying with his fraternity.
The gym was full of dude-bros lifting heavy weights and bragging about protein shakes.

  • synonym: bro - a man, often used to address a male friend; also used for similar subcultures
