limper

noun
person who limps or hobbles
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmpɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪmpə / (BrE)
a person who limps or walks with a hobbling gait

Examples

A limper with injury
As soon as she injured her ankle, she became a noticeable limper around the office.
Every marathon has at least one determined limper who refuses to give up.
The coach noticed a limper struggling at the back of the group during the drill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: limp - to walk with difficulty, typically because of a damaged or stiff leg or foot
