tenseness

noun
mental or emotional strain
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛn.sə.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.sə.nɪs / (BrE)
A state of mental or emotional strain, anxiety, or suspense.

Examples

Office tenseness
After days of uncertainty, the tenseness in the office was almost palpable.
Deep breathing exercises can help reduce tenseness caused by stress.
The tenseness in his voice made it clear he was worried about the interview.
