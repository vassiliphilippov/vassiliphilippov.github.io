grindable

adjective
can be ground
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪndəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪndəbəl / (BrE)
able to be reduced to small particles or powder by grinding

Examples

Grindable coffee beans
These coffee beans are grindable in any standard grinder.
The mineral is not easily grindable due to its hardness.
Make sure the spices are grindable before adding them to the recipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crushable - able to be crushed into smaller pieces
  • antonym: ungrindable - not able to be ground into smaller particles
