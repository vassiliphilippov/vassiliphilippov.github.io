crabbing

noun
persistent complaining
Pronunciation: / ˈkræbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkræbɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of complaining or grumbling, especially in a persistent or petty way

Examples

Constant crabbing
His constant crabbing about the food made dinner less enjoyable for everyone.
Instead of crabbing about the weather, she decided to enjoy the day.
The manager grew tired of the team's crabbing over minor issues.
