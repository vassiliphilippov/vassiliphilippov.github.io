truckling

noun
servile or submissive obeisance
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌk.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrʌk.lɪŋ / (BrE)
Obsequiously submissive behavior, especially a mean or servile act of yielding to another's will.

Examples

Truckling to power
Critics accused the government of truckling to foreign interests at the expense of national welfare.
His constant truckling to the new manager made him unpopular with his colleagues.
She refused to engage in truckling, choosing instead to voice her honest opinions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defiance - open resistance; bold disobedience
  • synonym: subservience - willingness to obey others unquestioningly
