flaky

adjective
eccentric or unconventional
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪki / (AmE), / ˈfleɪki / (BrE)
conspicuously unconventional, eccentric, or odd in behavior or appearance.

Examples

Flaky personality
Her flaky ideas made her stand out at the otherwise conservative conference.
Some people found his flaky style hard to take seriously, while others admired his originality.
The artist is known for his flaky performances that often surprise the audience.
