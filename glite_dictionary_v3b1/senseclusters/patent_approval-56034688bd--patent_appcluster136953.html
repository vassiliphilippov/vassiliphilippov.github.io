patent approval

noun
official permission to use an invention
Pronunciation: / ˈpætənt əˈpruːvəl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪtənt əˈpruːvəl / (BrE)
official permission granted by a governing authority to use or exploit an invention, typically after an application process

Examples

Patent approval granted
After years of research, the company finally received patent approval for its new vaccine.
Delays in patent approval can significantly impact a business's market strategy.
Many startups struggle to achieve patent approval before launching their products.
