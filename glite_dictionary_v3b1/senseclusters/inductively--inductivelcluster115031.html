inductively

adverb
by electromagnetic induction
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʌktɪvli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌktɪvli / (BrE)
by means of electromagnetic induction, using a changing magnetic field to produce electric current, heat, or motion

Examples

Inductively heated metal
The motor is powered inductively, eliminating the need for direct electrical contacts.
Some modern cooktops heat pots inductively for faster and more efficient cooking.
Wireless chargers transfer energy inductively to compatible devices placed on their surface.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electromagnetically - by means of electromagnetic processes
