Gastrolobium

Pronunciation: / ˌɡæstroʊˈloʊbiəm / (AmE), / ˌɡæstrəˈləʊbiəm / (BrE)
A genus of Australian evergreen shrubs that are poisonous to livestock, commonly known as poison bush.
