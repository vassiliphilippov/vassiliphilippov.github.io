madcap

noun
reckless or impulsive person
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmædˌkæp / (AmE), / ˈmadˌkap / (BrE)
a person who acts in a reckless, impulsive, or irresponsible way

Examples

A madcap friend
Everyone warned him not to trust that madcap with anything important.
She has always been a bit of a madcap, trying wild ideas without thinking them through.
The film follows a madcap who constantly gets his friends into trouble.

Related Terms

  • antonym: careful person - someone who behaves cautiously and avoids risks
  • synonym: daredevil - a person who enjoys taking dangerous risks
