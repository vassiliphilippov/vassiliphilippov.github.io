ghost story

noun
a story featuring ghosts or the supernatural
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊst ˈstɔri / (AmE), / ɡəʊst ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
a story about ghosts or supernatural events, often intended to frighten or entertain

Examples

A ghost story at night
He wrote a ghost story set in an old, abandoned mansion.
The movie is based on a famous Victorian ghost story.
We stayed up late telling ghost stories by candlelight during the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horror story - a story intended to scare or horrify, not necessarily about ghosts
