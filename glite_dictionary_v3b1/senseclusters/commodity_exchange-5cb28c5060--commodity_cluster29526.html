commodity exchange

noun
marketplace for trading commodities
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɑːdədi ɪksˌtʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / kəˈmɒdəti ɪksˌtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
an organized marketplace where commodities are bought and sold, especially for future delivery

Examples

A commodity exchange market
A commodity exchange facilitates the trading of agricultural products and raw materials.
The Chicago Board of Trade is a notable commodity exchange in the United States.
Traders watch price fluctuations closely at the commodity exchange every day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stock exchange - an organized market for buying and selling stocks and shares
