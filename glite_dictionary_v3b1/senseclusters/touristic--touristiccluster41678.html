touristic

adjective
for tourists
Pronunciation: / tʊˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / tʊəˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to tourists or tourism, especially describing places or activities designed for visitors

Examples

A touristic place
The city center has become very touristic, with many souvenir shops and fast food restaurants.
Some travelers avoid touristic areas to experience more authentic local culture.
The guide recommended a less touristic village for a quieter holiday experience.
The city has many touristic sites that attract visitors from around the world.
Some locals avoid the touristic areas during the busy summer months.
They prefer authentic experiences over touristic activities when they travel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: authentic - genuine and true to local culture or tradition
  • synonym: tourist - a person who travels for pleasure
  • synonym: touristy - attractive to tourists, often in a commercial or inauthentic way
  • synonym: travel-related - connected with travel or travelers
