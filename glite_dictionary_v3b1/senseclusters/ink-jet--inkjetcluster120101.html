ink-jet

adjective
relating to ink-jet printing technology
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋkˌdʒɛt / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkˌdʒɛt / (BrE)
of or relating to a printer or printing technology that sprays small jets of ink onto paper or other surfaces

Examples

Ink-jet printer
Her report included several pages of color ink-jet photos.
The ink-jet technology allows faster and more affordable color printing.
We just bought a new ink-jet printer for the office.

Related Terms

  • antonym: laser - relating to a printer that uses a laser to form images
