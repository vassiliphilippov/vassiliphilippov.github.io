ruggedly

adverb
in a strong or rough manner
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌɡɪdli / (AmE), / ˈrʌɡɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that is strong, rough, or has a sturdy and robust quality.

Examples

Ruggedly handsome man
He is ruggedly handsome, with strong features and a weathered look.
The mountain rose ruggedly from the valley, its cliffs jagged and imposing.
The trail winds ruggedly through the dense forest, challenging even the experienced hikers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rugged - having a strong, rough, or sturdy appearance or quality
