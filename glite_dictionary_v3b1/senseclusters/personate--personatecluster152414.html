personate

verb
pretend to be another person
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrsəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsəneɪt / (BrE)
to pretend to be another person, especially in order to deceive or to perform in a role

Examples

To personate a stranger
During the play, she would personate several characters, each with a distinct voice.
He attempted to personate a police officer to gain entry into the building.
The actor managed to personate the famous painter with remarkable accuracy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impersonate - to pretend to be another person
  • synonym: masquerade - to pretend to be someone you are not
