camphorated

adjective
containing or treated with camphor
Pronunciation: / ˈkæm.fəˌreɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈkam.fər.eɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
(of a substance) impregnated with camphor, typically to provide medicinal or aromatic properties.

Examples

Camphorated liniment
Some traditional treatments call for camphorated liniments to soothe minor skin irritations.
The pharmacist prepared a solution of camphorated oil for the patient's chest rub.
This ointment is camphorated to help relieve muscle aches and pains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: camphor - a white, volatile, aromatic substance used in medicine and as a flavoring
