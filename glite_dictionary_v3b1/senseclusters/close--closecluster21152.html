Synonyms

  • approach
    2.1 32/m
    approach means to move nearer in neutral or formal speech, while close often implies coming very near or touching something.
  • near
    1.0 9.37/m
    near means to come nearer in space or time in everyday speech, while close also suggests a short distance or strong contact.
  • draw close
    2.9 1.38/m
    draw close feels a little old-fashioned and shows slow movement toward someone, while close is more common in everyday speech.
  • draw near
    2.6 1.40/m
    draw near often sounds literary and suggests slowly moving toward a point, while close is neutral and used in speech or writing.
  • come near
    1.8 1.72/m
    come near is a plain phrase for moving closer in space or time, while close often also implies strong physical or emotional closeness.
  • get closer to
    1.9 3.74/m
    get closer to usually means making progress toward a goal or result, while close focuses on moving nearer in space or meeting.
