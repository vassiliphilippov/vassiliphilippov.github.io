meat cutter

noun
person who cuts meat
2.4 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / Ààmit Àåk åt…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàmiÀêt Àåk åt…ô / (BrE)
a person whose job is to cut, trim, and prepare meat for sale in a store or market

Examples

Skilled meat cutter
The meat cutter carefully sliced the beef into thin strips for the customer.
After years of training, he became a skilled meat cutter at the supermarket.
Every morning, the meat cutter prepares fresh cuts for the display case.

Synonyms

  • butcher
    1.0 4.41/m
    butcher is someone who prepares, cuts, and sells meat in shops, while meat cutter focuses on cutting and trimming meat for sale.
  • meatman
    6.8 0.02/m
    meatman is a male seller of meat and an older term, while meat cutter is a neutral name for someone who trims and prepares meat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butcher - a person who sells and sometimes prepares meat
