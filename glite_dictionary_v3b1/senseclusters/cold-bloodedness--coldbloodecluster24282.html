cold-bloodedness

noun
lack of warmth or emotion
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊldˈblʌdɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌkəʊldˈblʌdɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having a body temperature that changes with the environment, or of being emotionally unfeeling or cruel

Examples

The cold-bloodedness of a killer
The cold-bloodedness of the murderer shocked the entire community.
Reptiles rely on their cold-bloodedness to adapt to different temperatures in their environment.
Her cold-bloodedness during negotiations made her a tough opponent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruelty - deliberate infliction of pain or suffering
  • synonym: insensitivity - lack of emotional response or concern
  • antonym: warmth - friendliness or kindness in manner
