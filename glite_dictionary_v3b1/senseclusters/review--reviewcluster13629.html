review

noun
an act of revising material or practice
1.7
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvju / (AmE), / rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)
a period or act of going over material previously studied, especially to improve performance or refresh one's memory

Examples

Exam review
She set aside an hour each night for review before her final exams.
The music students had a short review of the scale patterns before the concert.
We will hold a review session next week to help you prepare for the test.

Related Terms

  • synonym: revision - the process of rereading or studying material again
