faded

adjective
diminished in brightness or strength
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˈfeɪdɪd / (BrE)
having lost brightness, strength, or freshness; reduced in intensity or vividness, especially due to age or exposure.

Examples

Faded memories
Her memories of childhood have faded over time.
The denim jacket looked stylishly faded around the collar and sleeves.
The old photograph showed faded colors after years of sunlight exposure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bright - full of light or vivid in color
