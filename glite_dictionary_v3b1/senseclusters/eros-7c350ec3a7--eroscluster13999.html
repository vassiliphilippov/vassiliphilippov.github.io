Eros

proper noun
Greek god of love; son of Aphrodite
Pronunciation: / ˈiːroʊs / (AmE), / ˈɪərɒs / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, the god of love, son of Aphrodite, often identified with the Roman Cupid.
