soother

noun
relief medicine
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːðər / (AmE), / ˈsuːðə / (BrE)
a medicine, lozenge, or similar product that relieves irritation or discomfort, especially in the throat.

Examples

Throat soother
She popped a throat soother to relieve the irritation before the long speech.
The pharmacist recommended a honey-based soother for his sore throat.
Many singers use a soother before performing to keep their voices clear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lozenge - a small medicated tablet for soothing the throat
  • synonym: remedy - a medicine or treatment for relieving pain or discomfort
