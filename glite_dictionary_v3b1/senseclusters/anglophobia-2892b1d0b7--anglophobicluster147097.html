Anglophobia

noun
dislike or fear of Britain
Pronunciation: / ˌæŋɡləˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌæŋɡləˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
A strong dislike or fear of Britain, British people, or British customs.

Examples

Strong Anglophobia
Academic studies have examined the roots of Anglophobia in various former colonies.
His Anglophobia became evident when he refused to attend the celebration of British culture.
The political leader was often accused of promoting Anglophobia in his speeches.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Anglophilia - love or admiration of England or England's culture
  • synonym: xenophobia - dislike of or prejudice against people from other countries
