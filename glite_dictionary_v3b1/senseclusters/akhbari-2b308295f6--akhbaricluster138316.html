Akhbari

noun
member of a Shiite Muslim movement in 17th-century Iraq
9.9 0.00/m

Examples

Modern Shiite jurisprudence distinguishes between Akhbari and Usuli doctrines.
The Akhbari school emphasizes strict adherence to traditions rather than independent reasoning.
