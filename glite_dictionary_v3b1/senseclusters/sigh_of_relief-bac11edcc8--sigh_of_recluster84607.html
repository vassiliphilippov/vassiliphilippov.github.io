sigh of relief

noun
exhalation showing comfort after stress
Pronunciation: / saɪ əv rɪˈlif / (AmE), / saɪ əv rɪˈliːf / (BrE)
an audible exhalation expressing feelings of comfort or reassurance after anxiety or worry

Examples

A sigh of relief
After receiving the test results, she gave a loud sigh of relief.
He let out a sigh of relief when he found his missing wallet.
There was an audible sigh of relief from the audience when the storm passed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relief - a feeling of reassurance and relaxation following release from anxiety or distress
