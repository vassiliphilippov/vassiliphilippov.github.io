bedevil

verb
treat cruelly or torment
6.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈdɛv.əl / (AmE), / bɪˈdɛv.əl / (BrE)
to treat someone with persistent cruelty or torment

Examples

Bullies bedevil a student
She felt bedeviled by the constant teasing from her classmates.
The captors continued to bedevil their prisoner for days on end.
The cruel kids would often bedevil their new teacher with endless pranks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harass - to annoy or trouble someone repeatedly
  • synonym: torment - to cause severe suffering to; to torture
