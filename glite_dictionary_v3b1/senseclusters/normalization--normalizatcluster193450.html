Examples

Normalization of standards
Education reform included the normalization of curricula to ensure all students received similar instruction.
The normalization of technical terms helps facilitate international collaboration in science.
The government introduced new regulations aimed at normalization of safety procedures across all industries.
International normalisation helps ensure products are safe and compatible worldwide.

Synonyms

  • standardization
    4.8 2.85/m
    standardization develops and applies formal technical rules, while normalization is the wider process of setting standards for uniformity.
  • uniformization
    7.2 0.00/m
    uniformization makes things consistent and alike, while normalization focuses on applying standards or regulations for wider uniformity.
  • norming
    7.0 0.07/m
    norming sets shared rules and expected behavior within a group, while normalization is a formal process of applying standards.
  • harmonization
    5.5 0.86/m
    harmonization aligns rules across organizations or countries so they work together, while normalization establishes uniform standards more generally.
  • regularization
    6.7 0.03/m
    regularization makes something regular or orderly, while normalization specifically creates and applies standards to ensure consistency.
