reflex

adjective
done automatically; involuntary action
1.2 3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.fleks/ /; / /rɪ.ˈfleks/ (AmE), /ˈriː.fleks/ /; / /rɪ.ˈfleks/ (BrE)
(of an action) performed automatically and without conscious thought, especially as a response to a stimulus.

Examples

Reflex action
Blinking when something approaches your eyes is a reflex response.
The baby's grip on a finger is a natural reflex action.
The doctor noticed a reflex jerk in the patient's leg when he tapped the knee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatic - done or occurring without conscious thought
  • antonym: voluntary - done by choice or intention
