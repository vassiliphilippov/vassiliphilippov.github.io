undedicated

adjective
not officially dedicated
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɛdəˈkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɛdɪˈkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not officially set aside or assigned for a particular purpose; not dedicated.

Examples

An undedicated area
Although the fountain has been built, it is still undedicated and not open to the public.
Several rooms in the new library are currently undedicated, awaiting assignment to specific uses.
The community center remains undedicated while they finalize the opening ceremony plans.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dedicated - having been officially assigned or set aside for a particular purpose
