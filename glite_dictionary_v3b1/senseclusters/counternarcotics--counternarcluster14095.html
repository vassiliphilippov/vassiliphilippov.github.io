counternarcotics

noun
anti-drug operations
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərnɑːrˈkɑːtɪks / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntənɑːˈkɒtɪks / (BrE)
the activities or operations aimed at preventing and disrupting illegal drug production, distribution, or use

Examples

Counternarcotics efforts
The government increased funding for counternarcotics efforts along the border.
She works in a counternarcotics division that targets major drug trafficking organizations.
International cooperation is essential for effective counternarcotics operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drug enforcement - the enforcement of laws against illegal drugs
  • antonym: narcotics trafficking - the illegal trade or distribution of drugs
