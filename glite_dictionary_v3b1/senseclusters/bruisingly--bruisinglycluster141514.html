bruisingly

adverb
harshly or painfully
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːzɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbruːzɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes physical or emotional pain or damage, often harshly or forcefully

Examples

Compete bruisingly hard
The two teams competed bruisingly, leaving several players exhausted and sore.
She spoke bruisingly about his mistakes, not holding back her criticism.
The negotiations were bruisingly tough, with both sides refusing to compromise.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gently - in a mild or kind manner
  • synonym: harshly - in a severe or unkind manner
