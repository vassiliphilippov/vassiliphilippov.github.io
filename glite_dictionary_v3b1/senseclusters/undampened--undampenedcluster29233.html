undampened

adjective
not made wet
5.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdæmpənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdæmpənd / (BrE)
not made wet or moist; remaining dry and unaffected by moisture.

Examples

Undampened tent interior
Despite the heavy rain, the inside of the tent remained undampened all night.
The documents were stored in a sealed box and stayed undampened during the flood.
She was relieved to find her clothes undampened after the leak was fixed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: damp - slightly wet
  • synonym: dry - not wet or moist
