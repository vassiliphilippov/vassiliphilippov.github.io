Republic of Artsakh

proper noun
unrecognized state in the South Caucasus
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ɑrˈsɑk / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ɑːˈsɑːk / (BrE)
an unrecognized de facto state in the South Caucasus, located within the borders of Azerbaijan and primarily inhabited by ethnic Armenians; also known as Nagorno-Karabakh Republic.

Examples

The Republic of Artsakh conflict
Hundreds fled their homes during the conflict in the Republic of Artsakh.
Talks continue regarding the status of the Republic of Artsakh and its relationship with neighboring countries.
The Republic of Artsakh declared independence in the early 1990s, though it remains unrecognized internationally.
