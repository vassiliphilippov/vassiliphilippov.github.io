calean

noun
oriental water-cooled tobacco pipe
8.1
Pronunciation: / kəˈliː.ən / (AmE), / kəˈliː.ən / (BrE)
an oriental tobacco pipe with a long, flexible tube, in which smoke is cooled by passing through water before inhalation

Examples

A calean pipe
In the old café, travelers gathered around a calean to share stories and relax.
She watched locals prepare the calean, carefully filling the bowl with sweet-scented tobacco.
The calean was placed in the center, its long tube winding across the rug to each guest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hookah - an oriental tobacco pipe with water for cooling smoke; also called a water pipe
