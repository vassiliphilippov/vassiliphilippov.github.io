calean

noun
oriental water-cooled tobacco pipe
8.1 9.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈliː.ən/ /; / /kæl.ˈjæn/ (AmE), /kə.ˈliː.ən/ /; / /kæl.ˈjæn/ (BrE)
an oriental tobacco pipe with a long, flexible tube, in which smoke is cooled by passing through water before inhalation
