air-to-air

adjective
between aircraft in flight
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɛr tə ˈɛr / (AmE), / ˌɛə tə ˈɛə / (BrE)
(of missiles, combat, or communication) operating between aircraft in flight or launched or directed from one aircraft to another.

Examples

Air-to-air missiles
During the operation, the pilots maintained air-to-air communication to coordinate their maneuvers.
Modern military technology includes advanced air-to-air radar systems for target acquisition.
The fighter jet was armed with air-to-air missiles for the mission.

Related Terms

  • antonym: surface-to-air - operating or launched from the ground towards aircraft
