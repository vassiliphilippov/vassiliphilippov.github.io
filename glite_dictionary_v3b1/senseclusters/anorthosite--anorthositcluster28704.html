anorthosite

noun
igneous rock type
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈnɔrθəˌsaɪt / (AmE), / əˈnɔːθəsaɪt / (BrE)
a type of igneous rock made mainly of plagioclase feldspar, often found in the Earth's crust and on the Moon

Examples

A anorthosite rock
Scientists discovered a large deposit of anorthosite deep beneath the mountain range.
The pale color of the lunar highlands is due to the presence of anorthosite.
Geologists study anorthosite to learn about the formation of the Earth's crust.

  • synonym: granite - a common coarse-grained igneous rock
