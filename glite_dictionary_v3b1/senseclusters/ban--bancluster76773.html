ban

noun
Moldovan/Romanian currency unit
9.5
Pronunciation: / bɑn / (AmE), / ban / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Romania and Moldova, equal to one hundredth of a leu

Examples

A ban coin
A single ban is rarely used, as most prices are given in lei.
She exchanged her dollars for lei and received several bani in coins.
The shopkeeper handed him his change in the form of ten bani.
A single ban has little value on its own, but they add up when saving coins.
I received three ban as change after buying a newspaper in Bucharest.
The price of a stamp increased by ten bani last year.
