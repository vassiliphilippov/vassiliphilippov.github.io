Branta

noun
genus of wild geese
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbræn.tə / (AmE), / ˈbræn.tə / (BrE)
A genus of wild geese that includes several species found in the Northern Hemisphere, known for their dark plumage and migratory habits.
