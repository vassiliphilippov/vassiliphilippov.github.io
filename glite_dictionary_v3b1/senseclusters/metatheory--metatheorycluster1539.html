metatheory

noun
theory about theories
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmet.ə.θiː.ə.ri/ /; / /ˈmet.ə.θɪə.ri/ (AmE), /ˈmet.ə.θɪə.ri/ /; / /ˈmet.ə.θiː.ə.ri/ (BrE)

Examples
