tramcar

noun
four-wheeled mining wagon on tracks
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtræm.kɑr / (AmE), / ˈtræm.kɑː / (BrE)
A four-wheeled wagon that runs on tracks in a mine, used especially for transporting materials such as coal.

Examples

Mine tramcar
Each tramcar could carry several tons of ore at a time.
The coal was loaded into a tramcar and transported to the surface.
Workers pushed the heavy tramcar along the narrow track deep inside the tunnel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minecart - a cart used for transporting ore or coal in mines
