dingo

noun
Australian wild dog
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪŋgoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋgəʊ / (BrE)
a wild dog with yellowish-brown fur, native to Australia

Examples

The dingo is native to Australia and plays an important role in its ecosystem.

Researchers have studied the impact of dingoes on local wildlife and livestock.
The dingo is native to Australia and is rarely seen near human settlements.
