hall

noun
passage in a building
Pronunciation: / hɔl / (AmE), / hɔːl / (BrE)
an interior passage or corridor in a building, onto which rooms open

Examples

A long hall
Her office is just down the hall from the conference room.
I heard footsteps echoing in the empty hall late at night.
The kids left their shoes in the hall after coming home from school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corridor - a long passage in a building from which doors lead into rooms
