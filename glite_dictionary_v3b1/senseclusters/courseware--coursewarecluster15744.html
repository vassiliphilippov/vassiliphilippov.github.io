courseware

noun
digital learning materials
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrsˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈkɔːsˌweə /; / ˈkɔːswɛə / (BrE)
computer-based materials or software used for teaching and learning, especially in online courses

Examples

Courseware for learning
The university provides courseware for all its online courses.
Students can access the courseware from any device with an internet connection.
Modern courseware often includes interactive quizzes and video lectures.
The university adopted new courseware to support its online degree programs.
Teachers can use courseware to organize lessons and track student progress.
Many students find interactive courseware helpful for practicing language skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: e-learning - learning conducted via electronic media, typically on the internet
  • synonym: educational software - software designed for teaching or learning purposes
  • synonym: learning management system - a platform for managing and delivering educational courses
  • antonym: textbook - a book used as a standard source of information for a course
