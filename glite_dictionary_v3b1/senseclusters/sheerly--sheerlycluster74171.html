sheerly

adverb
in a delicate way
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪrli / (AmE), / ˈʃɪəli / (BrE)
in a thin, delicate, or almost transparent manner, especially referring to fabric or material.

Examples

Curtains hang sheerly
The curtains hung sheerly in the sunlight, letting the room fill with a soft glow.
Her dress was made so sheerly that you could see the outline of her silhouette.
The scarf draped sheerly over her shoulders, adding a touch of elegance to her outfit.

Related Terms

  • antonym: thickly - in a dense or heavy manner
  • synonym: transparently - in a way that allows light to pass through; see-through
