subliminally

adverb
without conscious awareness
Pronunciation: / səˈblɪmənəli / (AmE), / sʌbˈlɪmɪnəli / (BrE)
in a way that influences the mind without conscious awareness

Examples

Music played subliminally
The music played subliminally in the background, creating a calm atmosphere without anyone noticing.
Some believe that images can subliminally affect our decisions even if we do not see them clearly.
The message was delivered subliminally, so most people were not aware of its influence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consciously - with full awareness or intention
  • synonym: unconsciously - without being aware or conscious
