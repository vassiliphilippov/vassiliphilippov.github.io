regional

noun
area-based competition
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdʒənəl / (AmE), / ˈriːdʒənəl / (BrE)
a competition or event held within a specific geographic region, often as a qualifying round for a larger contest.

Examples

A regional tournament
After winning their district, the team advanced to the regional to compete against other top schools.
She was thrilled to qualify for the regional in the science fair this year.
The regional attracts talented athletes from across the state each spring.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: final - the last and deciding round of a competition
  • synonym: qualifier - a preliminary competition to decide who advances
  • synonym: semifinal - a round that precedes the final in a competition
