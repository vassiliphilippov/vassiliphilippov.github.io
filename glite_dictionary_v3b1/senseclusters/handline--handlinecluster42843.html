handline

noun
rope for support in climbing
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈhændˌlaɪn / (BrE)
A rope or line used to hold onto for support while climbing up or down a steep or slippery section.

Examples

Climbing handline
A handline was installed along the steep section to aid hikers.
Always check the handline for wear before trusting your weight to it.
The climbers relied on a handline to safely descend the slippery slope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rope - a strong thick line made of long twisted threads
