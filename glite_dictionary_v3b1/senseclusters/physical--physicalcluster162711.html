physical

adjective
using bodily force or violence
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl / (BrE)
involving the use of bodily force, especially with violence or aggression

Examples

Physical fight
If you keep threatening me, I’ll have to get physical to defend myself.
The protesters became physical when police tried to remove them from the building.
The referee warned both players not to get too physical during the match.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forcible - done by force, especially physical force
  • antonym: gentle - not using force or violence; mild
