lunge

noun
sudden forward movement
6.1
Pronunciation: / lʌndʒ / (AmE), / lʌndʒ / (BrE)
a sudden forward movement, especially to attack someone or reach for something

Examples

A sudden lunge
He made a quick lunge to catch the falling glass.
The cat's lunge caught the mouse by surprise.
With a sudden lunge, the fencer attacked his opponent.

Related Terms

  • antonym: retreat - the act of withdrawing or moving back
  • synonym: thrust - a sudden forward movement; to push or drive with force
