yaqona

noun
Fijian kava ceremony
9.9
Pronunciation: /jə.ˈŋgoʊ.nə/ /; / /jə.ˈgoʊ.nə/ (AmE), /jə.ˈŋgəʊ.nə/ /; / /jə.ˈgəʊ.nə/ (BrE)
a traditional Fijian ceremony in which a special drink made from the kava root is prepared and shared among participants

Examples

Yaqona ceremony
The chief welcomed the guests with a traditional yaqona to mark the beginning of the celebration.
Visitors to Fiji are often invited to participate in a yaqona as a sign of respect.
During the yaqona, everyone sat in a circle and shared the drink in silence.
