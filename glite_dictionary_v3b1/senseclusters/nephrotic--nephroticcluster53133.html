nephrotic

adjective
kidney disease-related
Pronunciation: / nɛˈfrɑtɪk / (AmE), / nɛˈfrɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by kidney disease, especially nephrosis

Examples

Nephrotic syndrome
The patient was diagnosed with a nephrotic condition after several tests.
Doctors monitored the child's nephrotic symptoms closely during treatment.
A nephrotic syndrome can cause swelling and protein loss in the urine.
