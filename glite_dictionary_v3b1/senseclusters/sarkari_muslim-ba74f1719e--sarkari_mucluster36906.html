sarkari Muslim

noun
Muslim employed by or identified with the government
Pronunciation: / sɑɹˈkɑri ˈmʊslɪm / (AmE), / sɑːˈkɑːri ˈmʊslɪm / (BrE)
A Muslim who is employed by the government, or who is perceived as being aligned with or favored by government authorities, especially in South Asian contexts.

Examples

sarkari Muslim worker
Despite being a sarkari Muslim, he always advocated for minority rights.
She was often labeled a sarkari Muslim by critics who questioned her independence.
The new policy attracted support from many sarkari Muslims within the bureaucracy.
