aspen

noun
tree with fluttering leaves (Populus genus)
5.7 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈæs.pən/ (AmE), /ˈæs.pən/ (BrE)
A type of tree of the poplar genus, with rounded leaves attached to flattened stalks that cause them to flutter in even the slightest breeze.

Examples

A aspen tree
A grove of aspen stood near the lake, their slender trunks gleaming in the sunlight.
In autumn, the aspen trees turn a brilliant golden yellow, attracting many tourists to the mountains.
The leaves of the aspen tremble with the slightest breeze, creating a shimmering sound in the forest.
