dextrous

adjective
skillful in physical movements
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛkstrəs / (AmE), / ˈdɛkstrəs / (BrE)
skillful and quick in physical movements, especially with the hands

Examples

A dextrous juggler
A dextrous juggler entertained the audience with an amazing display of skill.
Her dextrous handling of the tools impressed everyone in the workshop.
The surgeon's dextrous hands made the complex procedure seem effortless.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adroit - clever or skillful in using the hands or mind
  • antonym: clumsy - awkward or lacking skill in movement
  • synonym: deft - skillful and quick in one's movements
