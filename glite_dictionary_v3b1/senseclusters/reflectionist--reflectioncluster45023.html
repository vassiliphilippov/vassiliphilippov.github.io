reflectionist

noun
expert in reflected light
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈflɛkʃənɪst / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛkʃənɪst / (BrE)
a scientist or technician who studies or works with the physical properties of reflected light, mirror images, or related optical phenomena

Examples

Reflectionist studies light
The reflectionist carefully measured the angles to analyze how the light bounced off the surface.
As a reflectionist, she developed new techniques for studying mirror images in optical experiments.
The museum invited a reflectionist to explain the science behind their interactive light displays.
