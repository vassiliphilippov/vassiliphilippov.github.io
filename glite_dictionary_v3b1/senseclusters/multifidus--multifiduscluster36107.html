multifidus

adjective
divided into segments
Pronunciation: / mʌlˈtɪfɪdəs / (AmE), / mʌlˈtɪfɪdəs / (BrE)
divided into many segments or parts, especially referring to a muscle with multiple divisions

Examples

Multifidus muscle fibers
The multifidus muscle plays a key role in supporting the lower back.
In anatomy class, we studied the structure of the multifidus muscle along the spine.
A healthy multifidus is important for maintaining good posture and spinal stability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: segmented - divided into separate parts or sections
  • antonym: undivided - not separated into parts or segments
