lawned

adjective
made of fine fabric
7.6
Pronunciation: / lɔnd / (AmE), / lɔːnd / (BrE)
made of lawn, a fine lightweight cotton or linen fabric

Examples

He admired the lawned curtains that let in soft, filtered sunlight.
