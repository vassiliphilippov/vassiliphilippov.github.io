moai

noun
Easter Island statue
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.aɪ / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.aɪ / (BrE)
a large stone statue with a human figure, carved by the Rapa Nui people on Easter Island

Examples

Easter Island moai
Tourists travel from around the world to see the famous moai on Easter Island.
Each moai was carved from volcanic rock and transported across the island.
Researchers study the moai to learn more about the ancient Rapa Nui civilization.
