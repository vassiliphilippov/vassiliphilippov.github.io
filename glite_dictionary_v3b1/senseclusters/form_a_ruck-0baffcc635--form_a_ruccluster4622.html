form a ruck

verb
gather in a rugby formation
Pronunciation: / fɔrm ə rʌk / (AmE), / fɔːm ə rʌk / (BrE)
to gather in a tight formation during a game of rugby, usually to contest possession after a tackle

Examples

To form a ruck quickly
After the whistle, the players formed a ruck right in front of the posts.
It is crucial for teammates to form a ruck to secure possession in rugby.
The forwards form a ruck quickly to protect the ball after the tackle.
