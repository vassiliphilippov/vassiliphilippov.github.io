order Bennettitales

noun
extinct order of fossil gymnosperm plants
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər bəˌnɛtɪˈteɪliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə bɛˌnɛtɪˈteɪliːz / (BrE)
