wagon tire

noun
metal hoop on a wagon wheel
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwæɡən ˌtaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈwæɡən ˌtaɪə / (BrE)
a metal hoop fitted around a wagon wheel to form its tread and protect the wheel
