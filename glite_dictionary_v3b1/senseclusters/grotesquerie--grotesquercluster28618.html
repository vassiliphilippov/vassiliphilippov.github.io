grotesquerie

noun
quality of being grotesque or bizarre
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊˈtɛskəri / (AmE), / ɡrəʊˈtɛskəri / (BrE)
the quality of being grotesque; something characterized by ludicrous, bizarre, or unnatural distortion

Examples

The grotesquerie of costumes
He was captivated by the grotesquerie of the circus performers' costumes.
The artist's paintings often bordered on grotesquerie, blending the beautiful and the bizarre.
The novel was filled with grotesquerie that unsettled and fascinated readers alike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grotesque - strange or unnatural in a way that is often disturbing or funny
