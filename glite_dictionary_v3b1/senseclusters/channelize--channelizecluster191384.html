(in signal processing and telecommunications) to separate a wideband signal or continuous data stream into multiple distinct channels (for example, frequency or logical channels) for parallel transmission or processing.

Examples

To channelize data streams
The software will channelize the incoming data into separate frequency channels for analysis.
Engineers channelized the radio spectrum so multiple signals could be monitored simultaneously.
During processing, the system is channelizing each stream to improve throughput and reduce latency.
