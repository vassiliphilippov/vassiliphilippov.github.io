unsmudged

adjective
not stained or blurred
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsmʌdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsmʌdʒd / (BrE)
not marked, stained, or blurred by a smudge; clean or clear in appearance

Examples

An unsmudged drawing
The artist was careful so the charcoal drawing remained unsmudged after hours of work.
Despite the rain, the address on the envelope stayed unsmudged and easy to read.
She admired her unsmudged makeup in the mirror before leaving for the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clean - free from dirt, marks, or stains
  • antonym: smudged - marked or blurred by a smear or stain
