radialis

adjective
of the radius bone
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdiˈælɪs / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiˈælɪs / (BrE)
relating to the radius bone or the radial (thumb) side of the forearm or wrist

Examples

Radialis artery
The doctor examined the radialis artery to check his pulse.
Pain on the radialis side of the wrist can indicate a sports injury.
Physical therapy focused on strengthening the radialis muscles after the fracture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ulnar - relating to the ulna bone or the little finger side of the forearm
