Ruskinian

adjective
of John Ruskin
9.9
Pronunciation: / rʌsˈkɪniən / (AmE), / rʌsˈkɪniən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of John Ruskin, his ideas, or his artistic and social style

Examples

Ruskinian art style
The museum features a Ruskinian approach to art, emphasizing nature and craftsmanship.
Her essay explores the Ruskinian influence on 19th-century architecture.
Many critics admire the Ruskinian ideals present in his social writings.
