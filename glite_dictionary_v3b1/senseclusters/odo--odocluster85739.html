odo

noun
odometer abbreviation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈəʊdəʊ / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for an odometer, a device in a vehicle that measures the distance traveled

Examples

Car odo reading
The odo on my car just hit 50,000 miles after our road trip.
Check the odo reading before buying a used vehicle to know how far it has traveled.
He reset the odo after servicing his motorcycle to track the next maintenance interval.
