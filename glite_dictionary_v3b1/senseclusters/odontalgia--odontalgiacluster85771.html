odontalgia

noun
pain in or around a tooth
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊ.dɑnˈtæl.dʒi.ə / (AmE), / ˌɒd.ænˈtæl.dʒi.ə / (BrE)
Pain or an ache located in or around a tooth; toothache.

Examples

Severe odontalgia pain
Chronic odontalgia can interfere with eating and sleeping.
