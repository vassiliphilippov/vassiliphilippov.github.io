Mary Stuart

proper noun
Queen of Scotland (1542–1587), executed for sedition
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmeə.riː ˈstjuː.ərt/ /; / /ˈmeə.riː ˈstuː.ərt/ (AmE), /ˈmeə.riː ˈstjuː.ət/ /; / /ˈmeə.riː ˈstʃuː.ət/ (BrE)
(1542–1587) Queen of Scotland from 1542 to 1567, who was forced to abdicate, fled to England, and was later executed for her involvement in plots against Elizabeth I.
