yellow-blue color blindness

noun
inability to distinguish yellow and blue
8.8 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈje.loʊ bluː ˈkʌ.lər ˈblaɪnd.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈje.ləʊ bluː ˈkʌ.lə ˈblaɪnd.nəs/ (BrE)
a condition in which a person has difficulty distinguishing between yellow and blue hues, often confusing the two colors
