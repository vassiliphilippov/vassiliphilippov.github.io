particular

adjective
special; exceptional; greater than usual
5.1
Pronunciation: / pərˈtɪkjələr / (AmE), / pəˈtɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
special, exceptional, or more important than usual

Examples

Particular attention
She gave particular attention to every detail in the project.
The teacher praised him for his particular kindness toward others.
This matter is of particular importance to our clients.
He gave particular attention to the safety instructions before boarding the plane.
Of particular importance are the results from last year’s financial audit.
She takes particular pride in her garden, more than anything else in her home.

  • synonym: special - better or more important than usual
