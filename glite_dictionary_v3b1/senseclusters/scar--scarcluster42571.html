scar

verb
leave a permanent mark on skin or surface
2.4 3.4
Pronunciation: / skɑr / (AmE), / skɑː / (BrE)
to mark the skin or a surface permanently with a scar, often as a result of injury, disease, or surgery

Examples

To scar the skin

The accident scarred her for life, both physically and emotionally.
Years of conflict have scarred the landscape, leaving visible traces of destruction.
