lede

noun
introductory section of a news story
9.8
Pronunciation: / lid / (AmE), / liːd / (BrE)
the introductory section of a news story or article, intended to entice the reader's interest.

Examples

The lede paragraph
His editor asked him to rewrite the lede to make it more engaging.
Many readers decide whether to continue based on the strength of the lede.
The reporter struggled to craft a compelling lede for her article on the election.
