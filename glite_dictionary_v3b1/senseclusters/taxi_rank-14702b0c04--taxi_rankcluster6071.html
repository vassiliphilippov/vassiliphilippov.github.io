taxi rank

noun
place where taxis wait for passengers
4.1 2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtæksi ræŋk / (AmE), / ˈtæksi ræŋk / (BrE)
a place where taxis park and wait for passengers to hire them, especially in a designated area

Examples
