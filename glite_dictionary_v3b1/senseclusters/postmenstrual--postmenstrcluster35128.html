postmenstrual

adjective
after last menstruation
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstmɛnˈstruːəl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstmɛnˈstruːəl / (BrE)
relating to or measured from the first day of a mother's last menstrual period, especially in reference to fetal or neonatal age

Examples

Postmenstrual age
The baby's postmenstrual age was carefully tracked in the neonatal intensive care unit.
Doctors use postmenstrual age to assess the development of premature infants.
The study included only infants with a postmenstrual age of at least thirty-four weeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gestational - relating to the period of development in the womb
