matchmake

verb
to arrange romance
Pronunciation: / ˈmætʃˌmeɪk / (AmE), / ˈmætʃˌmeɪk / (BrE)
to arrange or try to arrange a romantic relationship between other people

Examples

To matchmake a couple
She loves to matchmake her friends at parties.
He tried to matchmake his coworkers, but they were not interested.
Some people enjoy matchmaking for their single friends.
