Tet

proper noun
Vietnamese New Year festival
6.6
Pronunciation: / tɛt / (AmE), / tɛt / (BrE)
the Vietnamese New Year festival, celebrated for several days starting on the first day of the lunar calendar, usually in late January or early February

Examples

Tet New Year
During Tet, people visit relatives and offer best wishes for the new year.
Many Vietnamese families gather together to celebrate Tet with traditional foods and customs.
The streets are decorated with vibrant flowers and lanterns during Tet.
