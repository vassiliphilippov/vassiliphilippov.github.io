go past

verb
surpass; exceed a standard or expectation
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ pæst / (AmE), / ɡəʊ pɑːst / (BrE)
to be superior or better than a particular standard or expectation; to surpass or exceed

Examples

To go past expectations
She managed to go past the limitations of her training.
The movie's success went past what anyone had predicted.
This year's profits go past all previous records.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exceed - to go beyond a particular limit or standard
  • antonym: fall short - to fail to reach a required or expected standard
  • synonym: surpass - to be better or greater than someone or something else
