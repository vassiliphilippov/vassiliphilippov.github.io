overworn

adjective
extremely exhausted
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈwɔrn / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈwɔːn / (BrE)
extremely tired or exhausted, especially as a result of too much work, travel, or stress

Examples

An overworn worker
After a week of nonstop travel, he appeared completely overworn and needed a break.
The nurses looked overworn after working several double shifts in a row.
She felt overworn by the end of the semester and desperately needed some rest.
