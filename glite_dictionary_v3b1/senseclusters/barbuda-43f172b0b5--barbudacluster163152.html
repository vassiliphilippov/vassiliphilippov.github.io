Barbuda

proper noun
Caribbean island, part of Antigua and Barbuda
Pronunciation: / bɑːrˈbuːdə / (AmE), / bɑːˈbjuːdə / (BrE)
an island in the Caribbean, forming part of the country Antigua and Barbuda, with its largest city being Codrington.

Examples

Barbuda island
After the hurricane, many homes on Barbuda were damaged.
She plans to visit Codrington, the largest town on Barbuda, next year.
The beautiful beaches of Barbuda attract visitors from around the world.
