froglet

noun
young frog with tail
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɑːɡlət / (AmE), / ˈfrɒɡlət / (BrE)
a young frog that has just changed from a tadpole and is not yet a fully grown frog

Examples

A froglet on lily pad
The children watched a tiny froglet crawl out of the water onto a lily pad.
After a few weeks, the tadpoles in the aquarium turned into froglets with small tails.
A froglet is easy to spot because it still has a short tail from its tadpole stage.
The children watched a tiny froglet hop out of the water onto the grass.
After a few weeks, the tadpoles turned into froglets with small legs.
A froglet still needs to stay near water as it continues to grow.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: frog - an adult amphibian with smooth skin and long legs for jumping
  • degree lower: tadpole - the larval stage of a frog before it develops legs
