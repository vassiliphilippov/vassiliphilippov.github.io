possession

noun
mania for one thing or idea
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɛʃ.ən / (AmE), / pəˈzɛʃ.ən / (BrE)
an intense or abnormal fixation on a single thing or idea; a mania limited to one object or concept

Examples

Possession with an idea
Her possession with cleanliness led her to wash her hands dozens of times a day.
Some believed that his possession with the occult bordered on madness.
The artist's possession with a single theme produced hundreds of identical paintings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compulsion - an irresistible urge to behave in a certain way, especially against one's wishes
  • synonym: obsession - an idea or thought that continually preoccupies the mind
