underripe

adjective
not fully mature
3.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dər.ˈraɪp/ /; / /ˈʌn.dər.raɪp/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.ˈraɪp/ /; / /ˈʌn.də.raɪp/ (BrE)
not fully developed or mature; not ready to eat or use (especially for fruit, vegetables, or other things)

Examples

An underripe fruit
