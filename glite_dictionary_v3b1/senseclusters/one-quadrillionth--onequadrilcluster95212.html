one-quadrillionth

noun
one part in a quadrillion equal parts

Pronunciation: / wʌn kwɑˈdrɪliənθ / (AmE), / wʌn kwɒˈdrɪliənθ / (BrE)
One part of a quadrillion equal parts; the ordinal number corresponding to one divided by a quadrillion (1/1,000,000,000,000,000).
