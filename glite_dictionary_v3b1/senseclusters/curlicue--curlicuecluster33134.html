curlicue

noun
a decorative spiral or curved line
8.7 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkɜːr.lɪ.kjuː/ (AmE), /ˈkɜː.lɪ.kjuː/ (BrE)
a decorative spiral or twisting line or shape, often used in writing, design, or as a flourish

Examples

A curlicue design

The old letter was adorned with elegant curlicues on every capital letter.
