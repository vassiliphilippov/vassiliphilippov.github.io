high-top

noun
ankle-high shoe
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪ tɑp / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ tɒp / (BrE)
a shoe, especially a sneaker, with a top that extends above the ankle for extra support

Examples

A pair of high-tops
He bought a new pair of high-tops for basketball practice.
The high-top design provides extra ankle support during intense activities.
Many people prefer high-tops for their stylish look and comfort.

Related Terms

  • antonym: low-top - a shoe that does not cover the ankle
  • synonym: sneaker - a soft, comfortable shoe designed for sports or casual wear
