death list

noun
list of targeted victims
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛθ lɪst / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ lɪst / (BrE)
a list of people targeted to be killed or harmed, especially by an individual, group, or organization.

Examples

A death list of targets
After receiving threats, the activist feared she might appear on a death list.
Authorities warned that the hackers had published a new death list online.
The police uncovered a death list with several prominent names during their investigation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hit list - a list of potential victims to be harmed or killed
