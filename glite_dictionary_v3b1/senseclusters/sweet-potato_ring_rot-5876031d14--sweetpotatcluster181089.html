sweet-potato ring rot

noun
disease causing rings of dry rot in sweet potatoes
Pronunciation: / ˌswit pəˈteɪtoʊ rɪŋ rɑt / (AmE), / ˌswiːt pəˈteɪtəʊ rɪŋ rɒt / (BrE)
a disease of sweet potatoes in which the roots are surrounded by rings of dry rot

Examples

Sweet-potato ring rot disease
Farmers noticed sweet-potato ring rot affecting several rows in the field last season.
Proper storage helps prevent sweet-potato ring rot from spreading through harvested crops.
Symptoms of sweet-potato ring rot include concentric rings of dry, decayed tissue on the roots.
