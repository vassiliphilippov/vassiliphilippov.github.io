bronchoalveolar

adjective
of bronchi and alveoli
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌbrɑːŋkoʊælˈviːələr / (AmE), / ˌbrɒŋkəʊælˈviːələ / (BrE)
relating to both the bronchi and alveoli in the lungs

Examples

Bronchoalveolar lavage
The patient underwent a bronchoalveolar lavage to help diagnose the lung infection.
Researchers studied bronchoalveolar cells to understand the effects of air pollution.
A bronchoalveolar biopsy can provide important information about certain lung diseases.
