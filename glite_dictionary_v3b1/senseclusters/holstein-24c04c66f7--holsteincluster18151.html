Holstein

noun
breed of dairy cattle
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊlstaɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɒlstaɪn / (BrE)
a large breed of dairy cattle originally from northern Holland, known for its black-and-white markings and high milk production

Examples

A Holstein cow
A prize-winning Holstein took first place at the county fair.
Many modern dairies choose Holsteins for their exceptional yield.
The farm invested in a new Holstein herd to increase milk production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Jersey - a breed of small dairy cattle from Jersey, known for rich milk
