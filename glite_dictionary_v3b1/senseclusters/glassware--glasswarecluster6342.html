glassware

noun
objects or containers made of glass
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːswɛə / (BrE)
Drinking vessels, dishes, or other utensils made of glass, especially those used for serving food and beverages.

Examples

Delicate glassware items
Please be careful when washing the delicate glassware in the sink.
She bought a beautiful set of glassware for her new apartment.
The restaurant uses only high-quality glassware for serving drinks.

  • synonym: tableware - articles for use at the table (e.g., plates, cutlery)
