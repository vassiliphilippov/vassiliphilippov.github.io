ungrafted

adjective
not joined by grafting
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡræftɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡrɑːftɪd / (BrE)
not joined to another plant or tree by the process of grafting

Examples

An ungrafted tree
The orchard contained both ungrafted and grafted apple trees.
Some gardeners prefer to grow ungrafted roses for their natural characteristics.
The ungrafted vine produced a unique variety of grapes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: grafted - joined to another plant by grafting
