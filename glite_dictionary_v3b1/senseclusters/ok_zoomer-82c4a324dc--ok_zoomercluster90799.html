OK, zoomer

x
humorous term for a young person
8.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈkeɪ ˈzuːmər / (AmE), / əʊˈkeɪ ˈzuːmə / (BrE)
Used, often humorously or dismissively, to refer to a young person, especially one belonging to Generation Z.

Examples

OK, zoomer phrase
After her enthusiastic talk about TikTok, her older coworker said, 'OK, zoomer.'
He shrugged off the criticism with a playful 'OK, zoomer.'
Whenever someone struggles with new technology, she jokes, 'OK, zoomer.'

Related Terms

  • antonym: OK, boomer - phrase used to dismiss an older person's views
