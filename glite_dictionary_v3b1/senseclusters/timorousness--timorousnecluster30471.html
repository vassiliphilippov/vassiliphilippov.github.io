timorousness

noun
state of being timid or fearful
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪmərəsnəs /; / ˈtɪmərəsnɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɪmərəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being timid, fearful, or lacking confidence, especially in new or unfamiliar situations

Examples

His timorousness showed
His timorousness prevented him from taking the bold steps needed to advance his career.
She overcame her timorousness and finally spoke up during the meeting.
The timorousness of the explorer vanished as he grew accustomed to the unfamiliar jungle.
He attributed his timorousness to a lack of experience with travel.
Her timorousness kept her from speaking up at the meeting, despite having good ideas.
The child's timorousness faded as he grew more comfortable in the new school environment.

  • synonym: timidity - the quality of being easily frightened or lacking confidence
