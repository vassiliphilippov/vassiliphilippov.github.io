massy

adjective
large and solid
Pronunciation: / ˈmæsi / (AmE), / ˈmæsi / (BrE)
large, heavy, and solid in form or appearance

Examples

A massy stone wall
The ancient fortress had massy stone walls that stood for centuries.
A massy oak table dominated the center of the dining room.
Clouds gathered in massy shapes above the mountain peaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bulky - large and difficult to move or handle
  • antonym: light - not heavy or solid
  • synonym: massive - large and heavy in size or amount
