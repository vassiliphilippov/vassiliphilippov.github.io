hechsher

noun
kosher certification mark
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛkʃər / (AmE), / ˈhɛkʃə / (BrE)
an official certification mark indicating that a food or product complies with Jewish dietary laws

Examples

A hechsher mark
The cereal box displayed a hechsher to show it was suitable for a kosher diet.
Before buying snacks for the event, she checked for a hechsher on each package.
Some products have more than one hechsher to indicate different levels of kosher supervision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: halal - food permitted under Islamic law
  • synonym: kosher - food that meets Jewish dietary laws
