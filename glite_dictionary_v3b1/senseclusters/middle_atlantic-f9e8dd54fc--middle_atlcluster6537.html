middle Atlantic

adjective
relating to a U.S. region (Delaware, Maryland, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪd.əl ətˈlæn.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɪd.əl ətˈlæn.tɪk / (BrE)
of or relating to a region of the eastern United States that generally includes Delaware, Maryland, Virginia, and usually New York, Pennsylvania, and New Jersey.

Examples

The middle Atlantic region
Many famous universities are located in the middle Atlantic region.
The middle Atlantic states often experience varied weather patterns throughout the year.
Tourists often visit the middle Atlantic for its historical landmarks and beautiful coastlines.
