metrician

noun
expert in poetic meter
Pronunciation: / məˈtrɪʃən / (AmE), / mɪˈtrɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who studies, composes, or analyzes poetic meter and versification

Examples

A metrician studies poetry
The metrician explained the differences between iambic and trochaic meter to the students.
As a respected metrician, he published several books on the structure of classical poetry.
Many poets consult a metrician to refine the rhythm of their verses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poet - a person who writes poems
  • synonym: prosodist - a person skilled in the study of poetic meter and versification
