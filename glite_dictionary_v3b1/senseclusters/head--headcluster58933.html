head

verb
(of a river) to originate from a place
5.3
Pronunciation: / hɛd / (AmE), / hɛd / (BrE)
to have its source or rise (of a river or plant); to originate or form a head

Examples

A river heads here
Several small streams head from the glacier high in the mountains.
The Mississippi River heads in northern Minnesota and flows south to the Gulf of Mexico.
The Nile heads in the African Great Lakes region.
Once the barley begins to head, it needs less irrigation.
The lettuce will head in about two months after planting.
The wheat headed early this year due to the warm spring.
