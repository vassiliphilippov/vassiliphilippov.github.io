barometer

noun
instrument for measuring atmospheric pressure
9.4
Pronunciation: / bəˈrɑː.mə.tɚ / (AmE), / bəˈrɒm.ɪ.tə / (BrE)
an instrument for measuring atmospheric pressure, used especially in weather forecasting

Examples

A barometer instrument
She checked the barometer every morning before heading out to sea.
Sudden drops in the barometer often indicate an approaching change in the weather.
The weather forecast relies on readings from a barometer to predict storms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manometer - instrument for measuring pressure of gases or liquids
