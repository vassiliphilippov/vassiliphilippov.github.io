ignitability

noun
ability to catch fire
8.1
Pronunciation: /ɪg.nɪ.tə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɪg.naɪ.tə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ɪg.nɪ.tə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɪg.naɪ.tə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)

Examples

The ignitability of fabrics
