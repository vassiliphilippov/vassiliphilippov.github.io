blue wall of silence

noun
unwritten police code of secrecy
9.9
Pronunciation: / blu wɔl əv səˈlaɪnɪs /; / blu wɑl əv saɪləns / (AmE), / bluː wɔːl əv sɪˈlaɪnɪs /; / bluː wɔːl əv saɪləns / (BrE)
an unwritten code among police officers to remain silent or not report a colleague's wrongdoing, in order to protect them from investigation or punishment
