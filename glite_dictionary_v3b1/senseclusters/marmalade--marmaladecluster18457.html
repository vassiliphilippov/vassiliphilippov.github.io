marmalade

noun
citrus fruit preserve with rind
9.9 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑːr.mə.leɪd/ (AmE), /ˈmɑː.mə.leɪd/ (BrE)
a sweet preserve made from the pulp and peel of citrus fruits, especially oranges, boiled with sugar

We bought a jar of homemade marmalade at the farmers' market last weekend.
