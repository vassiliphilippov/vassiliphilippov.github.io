loup

noun
archaic word for wolf
9.9
Pronunciation: / luːp / (AmE), / luːp / (BrE)
an archaic or dialect word for a wolf, the wild carnivorous mammal related to the dog

Examples

A wild loup
The shepherd warned the children about a hungry loup seen near the village at dusk.
In old tales, a fierce loup would prowl the forests at night.
The villagers believed that a loup was responsible for the missing sheep.
