nonrepresentation

noun
lack of representation
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the state or condition of not being represented or not having representation in a group, organization, or decision-making process

Examples

Nonrepresentation in government
The community voiced concerns about their nonrepresentation in local government decisions.
Many activists fight against nonrepresentation of minority groups in the media.
The issue of nonrepresentation was raised during the conference on equal rights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exclusion - the act of leaving someone or something out
  • antonym: representation - the act of having someone speak or act for you
