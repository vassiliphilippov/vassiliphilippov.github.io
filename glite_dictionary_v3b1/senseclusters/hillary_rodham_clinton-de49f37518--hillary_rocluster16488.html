Hillary Rodham Clinton

proper noun
American politician and former U.S. Secretary of State
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪləri ˈrɑːdəm ˈklɪntən / (AmE), / ˈhɪləri ˈrɒdəm ˈklɪntən / (BrE)
(born 1947) American politician, diplomat, and former First Lady; served as U.S. Secretary of State (2009–2013), U.S. Senator (2001–2009), and was the Democratic nominee for President in 2016.
