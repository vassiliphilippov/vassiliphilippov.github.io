tree swallow

noun
North American swallow species
5.2
Pronunciation: / tri ˈswɑloʊ /; / ˈtri ˌswɑːloʊ / (AmE), / tri ˈswɒləʊ /; / ˈtriː ˌswɒləʊ / (BrE)
