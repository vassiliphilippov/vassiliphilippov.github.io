mesonotum

noun
middle thorax segment
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛsəˈnoʊtəm / (AmE), / ˌmiːsəˈnəʊtəm / (BrE)
the middle segment of the upper (dorsal) part of an insect's thorax

Examples

The mesonotum segment
The wings of many insects are attached to the mesonotum for support during flight.
Scientists examined the mesonotum to identify differences between the two beetle species.
Damage to the mesonotum can affect an insect's ability to move its wings properly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: metanotum - the rear segment of the dorsal part of an insect's thorax
  • antonym: pronotum - the front segment of the dorsal part of an insect's thorax
