uplink

verb
to send data upward
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌlɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌlɪŋk / (BrE)
to send data or signals from a device to a satellite or network, especially in telecommunications.

Examples

To uplink data
The engineers will uplink the latest software update to the satellite.
You need a strong connection to uplink large files to the remote server.
They uplinked the video feed to the broadcast network in real time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: download - to receive data from a network or server
  • synonym: transmit - to send out signals or data electronically
