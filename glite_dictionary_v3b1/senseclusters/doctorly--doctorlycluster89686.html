doctorly

adjective
like a doctor
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑktərli / (AmE), / ˈdɒktəli / (BrE)
behaving in a way that is typical of a doctor, especially showing authority, professionalism, or expert knowledge

Examples

Doctorly tone
He spoke in a doctorly tone, reassuring everyone that the situation was under control.
Her doctorly manner made the patients feel safe and cared for.
Even outside the hospital, she often gave doctorly advice to friends and family.
She gave a doctorly explanation of the symptoms to reassure the patient.
His doctorly manner made everyone in the clinic feel at ease.
The report was written in a very doctorly style, full of medical terminology.
She gave a doctorly explanation of the complex medical procedure to the students.
His doctorly tone made everyone listen carefully during the conference.
The committee was impressed by her doctorly approach to solving the research problem.
