answer the door

verb
open the door in response to a knock or ring
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.sɚ ðə dɔːr / (AmE), / ˈɑːn.sə ðə dɔː / (BrE)
to open the door in response to a knock or ring, usually to see who is there

Examples

To answer the door quickly
Could you answer the door while I finish cooking dinner?
I heard the bell and quickly answered the door before it rang again.
She was nervous but still answered the door when strangers knocked.
