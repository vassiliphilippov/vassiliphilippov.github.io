watercress

noun
edible aquatic plant used in salads
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚˌkrɛs /; / ˈwɔt̬ɚˌkrɛs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌkrɛs /; / ˈwɔːtəkrɛs / (BrE)
a small, edible aquatic plant with pungent leaves, often used in salads or as a garnish
