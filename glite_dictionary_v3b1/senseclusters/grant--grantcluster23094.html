grant

verb
transfer property or rights by deed
Pronunciation: / ɡrænt / (AmE), / ɡrɑːnt / (BrE)
(law) To transfer the ownership or rights to property, especially land, by an official deed or legal instrument.

Examples

To grant land
The attorney helped grant the land to the new owner.
The estate was legally granted to her after the settlement.
The government may grant mineral rights to private companies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convey - to transfer the title to property by deed or other legal means
