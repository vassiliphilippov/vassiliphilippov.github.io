jet plane

noun
aircraft powered by jet engines
Pronunciation: / dʒɛt pleɪn / (AmE), / dʒɛt pleɪn / (BrE)
an aircraft powered by one or more jet engines, allowing for high speeds and altitudes

Examples

A jet plane takes off
Children stood at the airport, watching a jet plane take off for the first time.
Many people travel abroad quickly thanks to the speed of the jet plane.
The jet plane soared high above the clouds on its way to New York.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jet - an aircraft powered by a jet engine
  • antonym: propeller plane - an airplane powered by propellers rather than jet engines
