thine

pronoun
archaic 'yours'
Pronunciation: / ðaɪn / (AmE), / ðaɪn / (BrE)
an archaic word meaning 'yours', used to indicate that something belongs to the person being spoken to

Examples

This is thine
This land is thine by right of inheritance.
If this book pleases thee, let it be thine.
The victory is thine, for thou hast earned it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mine - belonging to me
  • synonym: yours - belonging to you
