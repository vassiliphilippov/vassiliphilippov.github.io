invitation

noun
spoken or written request to attend or join
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.vɪˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.vɪˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A spoken or written request to someone to attend an event or participate in an activity.

Examples

A party invitation
I'm considering accepting his invitation to visit Paris in the spring.
She sent out dozens of invitations to her birthday party.
We received a formal invitation to the company's annual dinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: refusal - the act of declining an offer or request
  • synonym: request - an act of asking politely or formally for something
  • synonym: summons - an official request to be present, usually legal
