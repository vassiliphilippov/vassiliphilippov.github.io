rincon

noun
sheltered coastal cove
Pronunciation: / riŋˈkoʊn / (AmE), / rɪŋˈkɒn / (BrE)
a small sheltered bay or cove along a coastline, often used as a safe anchorage

Examples

Sheltered rincon bay
The fishermen found shelter in a quiet rincon when the weather turned rough.
We spent the afternoon swimming in a beautiful rincon hidden from the main beach.
Sailors often look for a rincon to anchor safely during storms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bay - a broad inlet of the sea where the land curves inward
  • synonym: cove - a small, sheltered bay on a coast
