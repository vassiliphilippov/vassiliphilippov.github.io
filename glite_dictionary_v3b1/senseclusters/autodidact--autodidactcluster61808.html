autodidact

noun
self-taught person
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtəˈdaɪdækt / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊˈdaɪdakt / (BrE)
a person who has learned a subject or skill by themselves rather than through formal instruction

Examples

An autodidact learner
His lifelong passion for astronomy developed because he was a dedicated autodidact from an early age.
Many successful entrepreneurs are proud to be autodidacts who learned through experience and self-study.
She is an autodidact who mastered several languages without ever attending a class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-taught - having learned something by oneself, without formal instruction
