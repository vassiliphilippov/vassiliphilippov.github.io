(Linguistics) The entity that is affected by or undergoes the action of a verb in a clause, rather than performing the action.

Examples

Verb patient (linguistics)
In the sentence 'The window was broken by the ball,' 'the window' is the patient.
Linguists often distinguish between the patient and the agent in a sentence.
The verb usually marks the patient differently from the agent in some languages.
