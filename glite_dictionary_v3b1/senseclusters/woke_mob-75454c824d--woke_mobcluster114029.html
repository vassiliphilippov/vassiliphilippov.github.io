woke mob

noun
socially conscious group, seen as overzealous
Pronunciation: / woʊk mɑb / (AmE), / wəʊk mɒb / (BrE)
a group of people who advocate for social justice and political correctness, often seen as quick to criticize or demand change, especially in a way perceived as excessive or intolerant

Examples

A woke mob protests
She was worried that a woke mob would target her for her unpopular opinion.
Some people believe the woke mob stifles open discussion and debate.
The woke mob rallied online, calling for the company to apologize for its marketing campaign.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cancel culture - practice of withdrawing support from public figures after objectionable behavior
  • synonym: social justice warrior - person expressing progressive views, sometimes pejoratively
