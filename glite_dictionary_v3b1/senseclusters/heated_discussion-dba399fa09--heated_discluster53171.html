heated discussion

noun
intense or passionate debate
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːtɪd dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / ˈhiːtɪd dɪˈskʌʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
an argument or debate marked by strong emotion or intensity

Examples

A heated discussion today
After a heated discussion, the team finally agreed on the best course of action.
Political issues often lead to heated discussions among family members during holidays.
The meeting turned into a heated discussion when the topic of budget cuts came up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: argument - a conversation or discussion characterized by disagreement
  • antonym: calm discussion - a discussion without strong emotion or intensity
  • synonym: debate - a formal argument or discussion on a particular topic
