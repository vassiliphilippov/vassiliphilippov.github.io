awkward moment

noun
socially uncomfortable or embarrassing situation
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɑkwərd ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈɔːkwəd ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a situation in which a person feels socially uncomfortable or embarrassed

Examples

An awkward moment in talk
She often laughs to diffuse an awkward moment in conversation.
There was an awkward moment of silence after his inappropriate joke.
We shared an awkward moment when we both reached for the same drink.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comfortable situation - a situation where one feels at ease socially
  • synonym: embarrassing moment - a situation causing embarrassment or awkwardness
