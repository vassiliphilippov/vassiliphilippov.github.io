Synonyms

  • dedicated
    3.9 9.31/m
    dedicated describes steady hard work for a task or cause, while devoted often shows strong personal loyalty or affection.
  • committed
    4.0 4.18/m
    committed means having made a clear promise to a cause or plan, while devoted is more about loyal feeling and care.
  • loyal
    1.0 20/m
    loyal means giving steady support or allegiance to someone or something, while devoted adds deep affection and personal dedication.
  • faithful
    1.8 5.96/m
    faithful means staying true and steady in duty or love over time, while devoted often sounds more warmly affectionate and personal.
  • allegiant
    8.2 0.01/m
    allegiant describes firm loyalty to a ruler, government, or cause in a formal way, while devoted covers personal dedication and affection.
