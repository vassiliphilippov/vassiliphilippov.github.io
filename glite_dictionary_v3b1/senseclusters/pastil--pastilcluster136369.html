pastil

noun
medicated throat lozenge
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpæstɪl / (AmE), / ˈpæstɪl / (BrE)
A small, medicated lozenge, often sweetened, used to soothe sore throats or coughs.

Examples

A pastil for throat
Always keep a packet of pastils handy when you have a cold.
He sucked on a honey-flavored pastil during the lecture to keep from coughing.
The pharmacist recommended a pastil to ease my sore throat.
