caoutchouc tree

noun
tree yielding commercial rubber
9.9 9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkaʊ.tʃʊk triː/ /; / /ˈkaʊ.tʃuːk triː/ (AmE), /ˈkaʊ.tʃuːk triː/ /; / /ˈkaʊ.tʃʊk triː/ (BrE)
A deciduous tree native to the Amazon and Orinoco rivers, known for its leathery leaves, fragrant yellow-white flowers, and milky sap, which is the main source of commercial rubber.
