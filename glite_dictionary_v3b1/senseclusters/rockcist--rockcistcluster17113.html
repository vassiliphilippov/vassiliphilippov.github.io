rockcist

adjective
music genre prejudice
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑkˌsɪst / (AmE), / ˈrɒkˌsɪst / (BrE)
showing or expressing prejudice or discrimination against certain music genres or artists, especially in favor of rock music.

Examples

Rockcist attitude
He was called rockcist for refusing to listen to any music that was not classic rock.
Her rockcist comments about pop and hip-hop artists upset her friends.
Some fans accused the magazine of being rockcist in its coverage of new music releases.
