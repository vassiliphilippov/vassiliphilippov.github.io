akaryocyte

noun
cell without a nucleus
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈkɛri.oʊˌsaɪt / (AmE), / əˈkærɪ.əˌsaɪt / (BrE)
A cell that lacks a nucleus, such as a red blood cell (erythrocyte).

Examples

An akaryocyte cell
Scientists studied the functions of an akaryocyte in circulating blood.
Some platelets are classified as akaryocytes due to the absence of a nucleus.
Unlike most cells, a mature red blood cell is an akaryocyte and therefore cannot divide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: erythrocyte - red blood cell
  • antonym: nucleated cell - a cell that possesses a nucleus
