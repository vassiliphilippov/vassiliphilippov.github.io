cream cleanser

noun
moisturizing facial cleanser in cream form
2.0
Pronunciation: / krim ˈklɛnzɚ / (AmE), / kriːm ˈklɛnzə / (BrE)
A facial or skin cleanser in the form of a thick, creamy lotion, typically used to remove dirt and makeup while moisturizing the skin.

Examples

A cream cleanser lotion
For sensitive skin, a cream cleanser may be less irritating than a gel formula.
She applies a cream cleanser every night to keep her skin soft and clean.
The dermatologist recommended switching to a hydrating cream cleanser during the winter months.
