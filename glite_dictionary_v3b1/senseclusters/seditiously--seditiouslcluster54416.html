seditiously

adverb
rebelliously
Pronunciation: / sɪˈdɪʃəsli / (AmE), / sɪˈdɪʃəsli / (BrE)
in a manner that encourages rebellion or resistance against authority or government

Examples

Speak seditiously at protest
The activist spoke seditiously at the protest, urging people to challenge the new law.
Pamphlets were distributed seditiously to encourage workers to strike against the company.
He wrote seditiously about the government's actions in his underground newspaper.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loyally - in a way that shows support for authority or government
  • synonym: rebelliously - in a way that resists or opposes authority
