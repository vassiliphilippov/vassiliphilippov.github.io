seeding

noun
planting or ranking
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of planting seeds or assigning ranks to participants in a competition based on their ability

Examples

Tournament seeding
The seeding of the tennis tournament ensured that the top players would not meet in the early rounds.
Farmers completed the seeding of their fields just before the heavy rains began.
Proper seeding is important for both successful crops and fair sports competitions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: planting - the act of putting seeds or plants in the ground
  • synonym: ranking - the process of arranging people or things in order of importance or quality
