Mack truck

noun
brand of heavy-duty truck
3.5
Pronunciation: / mæk trʌk / (AmE), / mæk trʌk / (BrE)
a large, heavy-duty truck manufactured by the Mack Trucks company, often used for long-distance hauling and freight transport

Examples

A Mack truck driver

The company uses a fleet of Mack trucks to transport goods across the country.
