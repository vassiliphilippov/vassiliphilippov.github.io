hard work

noun
persistent serious effort or labor
Pronunciation: / hɑrd wɝk / (AmE), / hɑːd wɜːk / (BrE)
persistent and serious effort or labor required to achieve something valuable or difficult

Examples

Her hard work pays
Her hard work on the project earned her a promotion at the company.
Success is rarely achieved without a lot of hard work and determination.
They recognized the hard work of the volunteers at the award ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diligence - careful and persistent effort
  • antonym: laziness - the quality of being unwilling to work or use energy
