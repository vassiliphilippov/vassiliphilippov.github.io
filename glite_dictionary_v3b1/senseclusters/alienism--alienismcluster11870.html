alienism

noun
state of being foreign or strange
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.li.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.li.ə.nɪ.zəm / (BrE)
The state or quality of being foreign, strange, or different; the condition of being alien.

Examples

Feeling of alienism
In his poetry, the sense of alienism pervades each line, highlighting the author's feelings of exclusion.
Some critics attribute the novel's unsettling tone to its subtle exploration of alienism.
The immigrant struggled with alienism in a society so different from his own.
