multidrop

adjective
multiple connection points
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌdrɑp / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌdrɒp / (BrE)
involving or allowing multiple points of connection or delivery, especially in communication or transport systems

Examples

Multidrop network setup
The new network uses a multidrop configuration to connect all the sensors to a single cable.
A multidrop delivery route allows the driver to make several stops in one trip.
Engineers chose a multidrop bus system to reduce wiring complexity in the building.
