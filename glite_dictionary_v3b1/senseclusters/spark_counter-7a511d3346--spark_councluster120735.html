spark counter

noun
device detecting ionizing radiation by sparks
Pronunciation: / spɑrk ˈkaʊntər / (AmE), / spɑːk ˈkaʊntə / (BrE)
a device that detects ionizing radiation from elementary particles by producing sparks when radiation passes through it

Examples

A spark counter device
Early versions of the spark counter were crucial in the discovery of cosmic rays.
The laboratory used a spark counter to detect radioactive particles.
When the spark counter registered a signal, the scientists recorded the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Geiger counter - an instrument for detecting and measuring ionizing radiation
