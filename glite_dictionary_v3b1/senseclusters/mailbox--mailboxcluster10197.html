mailbox

noun
private box for delivery of mail
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪlˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈmeɪlˌbɒks / (BrE)
a box where mail is delivered, collected, or deposited, such as outside a home or on the street

She dropped her letter into the mailbox on the corner before heading to work.
