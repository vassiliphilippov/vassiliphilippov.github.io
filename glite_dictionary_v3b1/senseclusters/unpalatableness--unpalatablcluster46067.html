unpalatableness

noun
quality of being unpleasant to the taste
7.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpæləˌtəblnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈpalətəblnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being unpleasant or unacceptable to the taste.

Examples

Unpalatableness of food
He complained about the unpalatableness of the overcooked fish at the restaurant.
Some vegetables are avoided by children because of their perceived unpalatableness.
The unpalatableness of the medicine made it difficult for the child to swallow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unpalatable - not pleasant to taste; difficult to accept
