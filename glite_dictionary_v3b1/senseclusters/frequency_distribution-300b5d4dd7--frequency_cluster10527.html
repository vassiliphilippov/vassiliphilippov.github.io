frequency distribution

noun
statistical table of value occurrences
1.0 4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːkwənsi ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən / (AmE), / ˈfriːkwənsi ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən / (BrE)
A statistical representation showing how often each value of a variable occurs within a dataset.

Examples

A frequency distribution table
Constructing a frequency distribution helps to summarize the data and identify patterns.
The frequency distribution showed that most students scored between 70 and 80 percent on the exam.
The left-skewed frequency distribution indicated that a majority of observations were high values.
