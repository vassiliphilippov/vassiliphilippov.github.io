basque

noun
fitted women's garment
Pronunciation: / bæsk / (AmE), / bɑːsk / (BrE)
a close-fitting garment for women that has a fitted waist and flares out over the hips

Examples

A basque garment
She wore a beautiful basque that accentuated her waist at the party.
The designer added a lace basque to the evening gown for a vintage look.
Many Victorian dresses featured a basque to create an elegant silhouette.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bodice - the upper part of a woman's dress, covering the chest and back
  • synonym: corset - a tightly fitting undergarment worn to shape the waist
