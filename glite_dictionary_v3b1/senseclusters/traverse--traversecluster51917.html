traverse

verb
formally deny an allegation in law
9.3 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / tr…ôÀàv…úrs / (AmE), / tr…ôÀàv…úÀês / (BrE)
(Law) To formally deny an allegation of fact made by the opposing party in a legal suit.

Examples

To traverse a claim
If you wish to traverse this claim, you must file a formal denial with the court.
The defendant chose to traverse the plaintiff's allegations in his statement of defense.
Their lawyer traversed every material fact alleged by the prosecution during the proceedings.

Synonyms

  • deny
    2.0 64/m
