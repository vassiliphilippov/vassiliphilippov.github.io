urgent attention

noun
immediate focus or care due to urgency
3.6 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…úrd í…ônt …ôÀàt…õn É…ôn / (AmE), / Àà…úÀêd í…ônt …ôÀàt…õn É…ôn / (BrE)
immediate focus, action, or care due to the seriousness or importance of a situation

Examples

Urgent attention needed
Broken equipment often goes unrepaired unless it receives urgent attention.
The matter requires urgent attention to prevent further damage.
We need to give urgent attention to the safety issues raised in the report.

Synonyms

  • urgency
    3.3 9.32/m
    urgency is the general state that something needs quick action or care, while urgent attention is the specific focus or response given immediately.
  • exigency
    9.1 1.11/m
    exigency is a formal word for an urgent need or emergency that forces action, while urgent attention describes the immediate care or focus given.
  • exigence
    9.2 0.11/m
    exigence is a rare or academic term for an urgent demand in texts, while urgent attention means the real, immediate focus or care people give.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immediate attention - attention or action given at once, without delay
  • antonym: routine attention - regular care or maintenance without urgency
