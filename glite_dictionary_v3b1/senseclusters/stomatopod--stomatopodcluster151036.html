stomatopod

noun
a predatory marine crustacean (mantis shrimp)
Pronunciation: / stoʊˈmætəˌpɒd /; / stoʊˈmætəˌpɑd / (AmE), / stəʊˈmatəpɒd /; / stəˈmatəˌpɒd / (BrE)
a marine crustacean of the order Stomatopoda, known for its powerful claws and complex eyes; also called mantis shrimp
