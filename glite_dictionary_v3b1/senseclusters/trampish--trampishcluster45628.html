trampish

adjective
sexually provocative
Pronunciation: / ˈtræmpɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈtræmpɪʃ / (BrE)
behaving or appearing in a way that suggests sexual promiscuity or resembles that of a prostitute

Examples

Trampish behavior
Some people unfairly judged her as trampish because of her bold fashion choices.
The character in the novel was described as having a trampish attitude that shocked her conservative family.
He made a rude comment about her supposedly trampish behavior at the party.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modest - behaving in a way that does not attract sexual attention
  • synonym: promiscuous - having or showing many casual sexual relationships
