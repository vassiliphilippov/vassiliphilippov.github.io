Tyrian purple

noun
ancient purple dye from sea snails
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪriən ˈpɝːpəl /; / ˈtɪriən ˈpɜːrpl̩ / (AmE), / ˈtɪriən ˈpɜːpl /; / ˈtɪəriən ˈpɜːpl / (BrE)
a vivid purple dye and color, historically rare and valuable, often associated with royalty and luxury
