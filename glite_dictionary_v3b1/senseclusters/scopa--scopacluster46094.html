scopa

noun
insect pollen brush
Pronunciation: / ˈskoʊpə / (AmE), / ˈskəʊpə / (BrE)
a brush-like structure on certain insects, especially bees, used for carrying pollen

Examples

Bee scopa
The bee used its scopa to collect pollen from the flower.
Scientists observed pollen grains sticking to the scopa of the insect.
Without a well-developed scopa, some bees cannot efficiently transport pollen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pollen basket - a structure on bees' legs for carrying pollen
