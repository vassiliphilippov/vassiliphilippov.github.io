Chinese jujube

noun
sweet, dark red fruit of a buckthorn tree
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪniz ˈdʒuːdʒuːb / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪniːz ˈdʒuːdʒuːb / (BrE)
A dark red, plumlike fruit from Old World buckthorn trees, commonly cultivated for its edible, sweet flesh.

Examples

Ripe Chinese jujube fruit
Farmers cultivate Chinese jujube trees for both their fruit and ornamental value.
She bought a bag of dried Chinese jujube at the Asian grocery.
The Chinese jujube is popular in traditional Chinese medicine for its nutritional value.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jujube - the fruit of any tree of the genus Ziziphus, especially Ziziphus jujuba
