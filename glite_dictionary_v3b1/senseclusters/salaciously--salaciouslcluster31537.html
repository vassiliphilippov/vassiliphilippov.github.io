salaciously

adverb
in a lascivious or lecherous manner
Pronunciation: / səˈleɪʃəsli / (AmE), / səˈleɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows an excessive or inappropriate interest in sexual matters; lasciviously.

Examples

To salaciously smile
He grinned salaciously when he overheard their conversation.
The play was criticized for being salaciously provocative rather than artistically daring.
The tabloids reported on the scandal salaciously, focusing on every prurient detail.
