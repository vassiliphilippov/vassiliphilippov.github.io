flirt

noun
a person who behaves playfully to attract others
Pronunciation: / flɝt / (AmE), / flɜːt / (BrE)
a person who behaves playfully with others, often to attract sexual interest or affection

Examples

A playful flirt
He was known as a flirt at every party he attended.
Some people thought she was just friendly, but others called her a flirt.
The office flirt always had a witty comment and a charming smile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tease - a person who makes fun of or provokes someone, sometimes playfully
