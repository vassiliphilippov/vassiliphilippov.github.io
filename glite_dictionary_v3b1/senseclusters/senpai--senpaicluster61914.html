senpai

noun
a senior or mentor, especially in Japanese contexts
7.4 8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsen.paɪ/ /; / /ˈsem.paɪ/ (AmE), /ˈsen.paɪ/ /; / /ˈsem.paɪ/ (BrE)
