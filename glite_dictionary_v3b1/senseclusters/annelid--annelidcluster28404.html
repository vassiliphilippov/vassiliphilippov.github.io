annelid

noun
segmented worm of the phylum Annelida
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.ə.lɪd / (AmE), / ˈæn.əl.ɪd / (BrE)
a worm with a long, cylindrical body that is divided into segments, belonging to the phylum Annelida
