combat boot

noun
military-style lace-up boot
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmˌbæt buːt /; / ˈkɑm.bæt buːt / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.bæt buːt / (BrE)
a sturdy, lace-up boot originally designed for soldiers, now also worn in everyday life or as a fashion item
