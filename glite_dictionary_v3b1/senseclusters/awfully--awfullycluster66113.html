awfully

adverb
extremely or dreadfully bad
3.0 2.62/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…îfli / (AmE), / Àà…îÀêfli / (BrE)
in a very bad or unpleasant way; extremely badly or dreadfully

Examples

Awfully cold outside
It was awfully cold last night, and the pipes froze.
She felt awfully sorry for upsetting her friend.
The movie was awfully boring, and I left before it was over.
He behaved awfully toward his guests, showing no manners at all.
She played the violin awfully at the recital, making everyone cringe.
The cake turned out awfully, so we couldn't serve it to anyone.
