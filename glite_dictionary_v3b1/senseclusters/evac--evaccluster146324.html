evac

verb
to leave for safety
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈiːvæk / (AmE), / ˈiːvæk / (BrE)
to leave a place quickly for safety, especially in an emergency situation

Examples

To evac the building
The coach told the team to evac the gym when the fire alarm sounded.
During the drill, students practiced how to evac the building quickly and calmly.
If you hear the siren, evac the area immediately and wait for further instructions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evacuate - to remove people from a dangerous place for safety
  • antonym: remain - to stay in the same place
