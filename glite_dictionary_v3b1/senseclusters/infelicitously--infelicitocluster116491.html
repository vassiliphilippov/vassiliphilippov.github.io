infelicitously

adverb
in an awkward or inappropriate manner
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.fəˈlɪs.ə.təs.li / (AmE), / ˌɪn.fɛˈlɪs.ɪ.təs.li / (BrE)
In a manner that is awkward, inappropriate, or unfortunate.

Examples

To speak infelicitously
His attempt at humor fell flat because he delivered the joke infelicitously.
She phrased her criticism rather infelicitously, offending some of her colleagues.
The politician responded infelicitously, causing further confusion among the audience.
