BOGO

verb
to offer buy-one-get-one
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊɡəʊ / (BrE)
to sell or promote goods by offering a second item free or at a reduced price when one is purchased

Examples

To BOGO snacks
The supermarket decided to BOGO all snacks to attract more customers this weekend.
Many stores BOGO seasonal items at the end of each season to clear out inventory.
If you want to save money, wait until they BOGO your favorite brand of coffee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discount - to reduce the price of something
  • antonym: full price - to sell at the regular, non-discounted price
