x
in the manner of a wealthy or privileged person
Pronunciation: / æz ə pluːt / (AmE), / æz ə pluːt / (BrE)
(informal) As someone who is wealthy or privileged; in the manner of a plutocrat.

Examples

Acting as a plute
Ever since the promotion, he started acting as a plute and lost touch with his old friends.
Politicians who live as a plute often struggle to relate to ordinary voters.
She spoke as a plute when discussing luxury vacations most people couldn't afford.
