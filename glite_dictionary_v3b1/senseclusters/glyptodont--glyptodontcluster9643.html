glyptodont

noun
extinct armored mammal
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɪptəˌdɑnt / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪptədɒnt / (BrE)
a large, extinct, armored mammal related to armadillos that lived during the prehistoric era

Examples

A glyptodont shell
The paleontologist carefully reconstructed the shell of a glyptodont for the museum display.
Scientists believe the glyptodont used its armored body for protection against predators.
During the Ice Age, the glyptodont roamed the grasslands of South America.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mammoth - a large, extinct, hairy elephant-like mammal
