fissure of Rolando

noun
brain groove between frontal and parietal lobes
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪʃ.ər əv roʊl.ˈɑːn.doʊ/ /; / /ˈfɪʒ.ər əv roʊl.ˈɑːn.doʊ/ (AmE), /ˈfɪʃ.ə əv rəʊl.ˈɑːn.dəʊ/ /; / /ˈfɪʒ.ə əv rəʊl.ˈɑːn.dəʊ/ (BrE)
