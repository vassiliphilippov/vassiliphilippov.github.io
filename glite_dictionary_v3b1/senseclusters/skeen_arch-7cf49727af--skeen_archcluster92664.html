skeen arch

noun
arch with height less than half its width
Pronunciation: / skiːn ɑrtʃ / (AmE), / skiːn ɑːtʃ / (BrE)
An arch in which the height is less than half the width.

Examples

A skeen arch shape
A skeen arch is ideal for architectural styles that require broad, low arches.
Engineers chose a skeen arch because it provided the needed stability with less vertical height.
The bridge uses a skeen arch design to support the wide span with a low rise.
