semi-precious stone

noun
gemstone of lesser value
1.6 3.0
Pronunciation: /se.mɪ.ˈpreʃ.əs stoʊn/ (AmE), /se.mɪ.ˈpreʃ.əs stəʊn/ (BrE)
A gemstone that is less rare or valuable than precious stones, such as quartz, amethyst, or turquoise.

Examples

A semi-precious stone collection
He became interested in collecting rare semi-precious stones from around the world.
Many people prefer affordable jewelry made with semi-precious stones like amethyst and turquoise.
The necklace was set with several colorful semi-precious stones rather than diamonds.

Related Terms
