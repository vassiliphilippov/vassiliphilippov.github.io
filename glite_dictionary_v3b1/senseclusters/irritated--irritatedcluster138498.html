irritated

adjective
annoyed or slightly angry
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrəˌteɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɪrɪteɪtɪd / (BrE)
feeling annoyed or slightly angry, especially as a result of something repetitive or bothersome

Examples

An irritated tone
His irritated tone made it clear he was not happy with the decision.
I always feel irritated when people interrupt me during meetings.
She became irritated with the constant noise outside her window.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: angry - having strong feelings of displeasure
  • synonym: annoyed - slightly angry; bothered
  • degree lower: bothered - mildly worried or upset
  • antonym: calm - not feeling angry or annoyed
