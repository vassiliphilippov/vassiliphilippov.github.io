deliberative

adjective
involving careful discussion or debate
Pronunciation: / dəˈlɪb.ɚ.ə.tɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪb.ər.ətɪv / (BrE)
involving or characterized by careful consideration, discussion, or debate before making decisions

Examples

A deliberative process
A deliberative approach ensures all viewpoints are considered before reaching a decision.
The committee held a deliberative session to discuss the proposed changes.
The council functions as a deliberative body, debating issues openly and thoroughly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consultative - offering advice or consultation, especially officially or professionally
