cliffside

adjective
beside a cliff
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪfsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈklɪfsaɪd / (BrE)
located on or next to the side of a cliff.

Examples

Cliffside hotel
They built a cliffside restaurant with panoramic ocean views.
The cliffside path was narrow and offered breathtaking scenery.
We admired the cliffside village perched high above the waves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mountainside - located on or next to the side of a mountain
  • synonym: seaside - located by the sea
