maintain the high ground

verb
keep a position of advantage or superiority
2.4
Pronunciation: / meɪnˈteɪn ðə haɪ ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / meɪnˈteɪn ðə haɪ ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
