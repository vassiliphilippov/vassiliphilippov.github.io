Synonyms

  • sporadic
    7.0 2/m
    sporadic refers to things that appear scattered and rare in places, while intermittent means they happen at irregular times.
  • noncontinuous
    5.8 0.00/m
    noncontinuous describes something that does not run without breaks, while intermittent means it stops and starts at odd intervals.
  • off-and-on
    3.8 0.1/m
    off-and-on is a casual phrase for things that stop and begin again often, while intermittent is more neutral and formal.
  • occasional
    2.8 13/m

  • irregular
