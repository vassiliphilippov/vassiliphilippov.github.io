deposit

noun
facility for storage or safekeeping
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɑzɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɒzɪt / (BrE)
a place where items, valuables, or funds can be stored or left for safekeeping

Examples

A safe deposit
All artworks are kept in a special deposit until the exhibition opens.
She left her suitcase in the luggage deposit at the train station.
The hotel offers a secure deposit for guests' valuables at the front desk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repository - a place where things are stored or can be found
  • synonym: vault - a secure room or chamber for storing valuables
