Tianjin

proper noun
major port city in northern China
Pronunciation: / tiɛnˈdʒɪn / (AmE), / tjanˈdʒɪn / (BrE)
a major port and industrial city in northern China, located near the Bohai Sea, east-southeast of Beijing

Examples

Tianjin city
In 2008, Tianjin hosted several major Olympic events.
Many international companies have offices in Tianjin due to its strategic location.
The high-speed train from Beijing to Tianjin takes less than an hour.
