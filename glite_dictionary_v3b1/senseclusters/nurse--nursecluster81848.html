nurse

verb
maintain a feeling or idea persistently
2.4 5.4
Pronunciation: /nɜːrs/ (AmE), /nɜːs/ (BrE)
to carefully maintain or hold onto a feeling, idea, or belief, especially for a long time

Examples

To nurse a dream
He has nursed ambitions of becoming a writer for many years.
It's unhealthy to nurse old grudges instead of letting them go.
She continued to nurse her resentment long after the disagreement was over.
