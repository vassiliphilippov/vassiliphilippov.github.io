unendorsed

adjective
without endorsement
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈdɔrst / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈdɔːst / (BrE)
not having an endorsement or signature, especially on the back of a cheque or negotiable document, and therefore not authorized for transfer

Examples

Unendorsed cheque
The bank rejected the unendorsed cheque because it lacked the necessary signature.
You cannot deposit an unendorsed money order until the payee signs it.
Always check that a document is not unendorsed before attempting to transfer it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: endorsed - having an official signature or approval, especially on a document
