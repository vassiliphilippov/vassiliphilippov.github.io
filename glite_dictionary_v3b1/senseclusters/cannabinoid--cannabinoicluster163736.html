cannabinoid

noun
cannabis compound
7.2
Pronunciation: / kəˈnæbəˌnɔɪd / (AmE), / kəˈnæbɪnɔɪd / (BrE)
a chemical compound (from cannabis, the body, or made synthetically) that acts on cannabinoid receptors in the body
