assize

noun
historic legal writ for property recovery
7.4
Pronunciation: / əˈsaɪz / (AmE), / əˈsaɪz / (BrE)
a writ formerly issued by a court to the sheriff for the recovery of property

Examples

Court assize order
Medieval sheriffs were often tasked with enforcing an assize after disputes over land ownership.
The concept of the assize became obsolete as modern legal systems developed.
The court issued an assize to help the landowner reclaim his property.
