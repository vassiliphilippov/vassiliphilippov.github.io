huckleberry

noun
a small dark-purple berry, similar to a blueberry
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌkəlˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈhʌkəlbəri /; / ˈhʌkəlˌbɛri / (BrE)
a small, dark-purple or blue-black berry, similar to a blueberry, found in North America

Examples

A huckleberry pie
She made a delicious pie filled with fresh huckleberries.
The huckleberry harvest is an eagerly anticipated event in the region.
We picked huckleberries from the bushes along the trail.
Bears are known to forage for huckleberry bushes in the summer.
She picked a handful of ripe huckleberries on her morning hike.
The pie was filled with fresh huckleberry jam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bilberry - A small, dark blue edible berry from northern Europe and western Asia
  • synonym: blueberry - a small blue edible berry from plants of the genus Vaccinium
