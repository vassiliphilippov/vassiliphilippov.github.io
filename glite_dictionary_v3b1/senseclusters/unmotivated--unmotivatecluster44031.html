unmotivated

adjective
without motivation or enthusiasm
1.0 3.6
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈmoʊ.tɪ.veɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ʌn.ˈmoʊ.tə.veɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈməʊ.tɪ.veɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ʌn.ˈməʊ.tə.veɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
Not having or showing any motivation or enthusiasm to act or achieve something.

Examples

An unmotivated person
