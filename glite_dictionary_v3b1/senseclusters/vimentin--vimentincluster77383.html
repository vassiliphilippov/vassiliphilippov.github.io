vimentin

noun
cell marker protein
9.9
Pronunciation: /vɪ.ˈmen.tɪn/ /; / /vaɪ.ˈmen.tɪn/ (AmE), /vɪ.ˈmen.tɪn/ /; / /vaɪ.ˈmen.tɪn/ (BrE)

Examples

Vimentin marker test
