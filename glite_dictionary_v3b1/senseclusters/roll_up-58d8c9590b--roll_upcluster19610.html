roll up

verb
to close a car window by moving it upward
Pronunciation: / roʊl ʌp / (AmE), / rəʊl ʌp / (BrE)
to close a car window by turning a handle or pressing a button to move it upward

Examples

To roll up the window
As we entered the city, she quickly rolled up her window.
Please roll up your window so the rain doesn't get in.
You have to roll up the windows before going through the car wash.
