skin-tight

adjective
very tight, clinging to the skin
3.1
Pronunciation: / skɪnˈtaɪt / (AmE), / skɪnˈtaɪt / (BrE)
(of clothing) fitting very closely to the skin; extremely tight.

Examples

Skin-tight clothing
Athletes often wear skin-tight suits to reduce air resistance during races.
She wore a skin-tight dress to the party, turning heads as she entered the room.
The skin-tight jeans were uncomfortable after a big meal.
