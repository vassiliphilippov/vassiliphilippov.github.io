captioning

noun
adding text to video
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkæpʃənɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of showing written words on a screen or image to explain, transcribe, or translate spoken content or sounds

Examples

Captioning on videos
Many educational videos use captioning to help viewers understand the content.
The movie theater offers captioning for people who are deaf or hard of hearing.
Advances in technology have made automatic captioning more accurate and accessible.
The movie's captioning helped viewers who are deaf or hard of hearing understand the dialogue.
Live captioning during the conference made the presentations accessible to everyone.
She turned on captioning to follow along with the foreign film more easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subtitling - the process of displaying translated or transcribed text at the bottom of a video
  • synonym: transcription - the act of converting speech into written text
