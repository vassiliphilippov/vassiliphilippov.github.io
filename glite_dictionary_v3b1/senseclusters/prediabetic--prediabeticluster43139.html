prediabetic

noun
person at risk for diabetes
5.1
Pronunciation: /priː.daɪ.ə.ˈbet.ɪk/ /; / /prɪ.daɪ.ə.ˈbet.ɪk/ (AmE), /priː.daɪ.ə.ˈbet.ɪk/ /; / /prɪ.daɪ.ə.ˈbet.ɪk/ (BrE)
a person who has higher than normal blood sugar levels but does not yet have diabetes

Examples

A prediabetic person
After a routine checkup, the doctor told him he was a prediabetic and needed to change his lifestyle.
Many prediabetics can prevent diabetes by eating healthier and exercising regularly.
The clinic offers special programs for prediabetic patients to help them manage their blood sugar.
