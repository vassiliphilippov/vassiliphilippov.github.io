whacking

noun
repeated blows as corporal punishment
Pronunciation: / ˈwækɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwækɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of striking someone repeatedly as a form of corporal punishment

Examples

A whacking with a stick
After his misbehavior at school, he received a proper whacking from his father.
Stories of strict discipline often included the occasional whacking with a ruler.
The teacher threatened a whacking for anyone caught cheating on the test.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beating - the act of striking repeatedly; often used interchangeably for corporal punishment
  • synonym: spanking - striking the buttocks as a form of corporal punishment
