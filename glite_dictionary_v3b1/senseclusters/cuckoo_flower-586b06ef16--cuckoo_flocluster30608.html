cuckoo flower

noun
European perennial with pink flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊku flaʊər /; / ˈkʊku ˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʊku ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a wild perennial plant with pink or white flowers, commonly found in damp meadows in Europe and western Asia; also called lady's smock (Cardamine pratensis)
