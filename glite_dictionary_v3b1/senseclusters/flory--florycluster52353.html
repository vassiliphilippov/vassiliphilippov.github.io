flory

adjective
heraldic floral design
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔːri / (AmE), / ˈflɔːri / (BrE)
decorated with or ending in flowers, especially with designs resembling fleurs-de-lis, in heraldry

Examples

Flory cross design
The knight's shield was adorned with a cross flory at each end.
In the coat of arms, the border was described as flory to indicate its floral decoration.
The artist included a flory pattern to give the emblem a traditional heraldic look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fleury - decorated with fleurs-de-lis in heraldry
