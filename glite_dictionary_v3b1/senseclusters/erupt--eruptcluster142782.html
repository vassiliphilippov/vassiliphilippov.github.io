erupt

verb
to suddenly appear on the skin
3.5 3.4
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈrʌpt/ /; / /ˈiː.rəpt/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈrʌpt/ /; / /ˈiː.rəpt/ (BrE)
(of a rash, spots, or sores) to suddenly appear or break out on the skin

Examples

A rash erupts
A painful rash erupted on his back overnight, causing him to worry.
Blisters can erupt suddenly as a reaction to an allergen.
Red spots often erupt on children's skin during chickenpox.
After eating shellfish, a red rash began to erupt across her arms and face.
His skin tends to erupt with itchy bumps whenever he gets too stressed.
Pimples sometimes erupt suddenly before important events.
