belt

noun
loop to transmit motion in machines
Pronunciation: / bɛlt / (AmE), / bɛlt / (BrE)
A continuous loop of flexible material used to transmit motion or power between two or more rotating shafts or pulleys.

Examples

Machine belt
A loose belt can cause machines to run inefficiently or stop working.
The mechanic replaced the worn-out belt on the conveyor system.
The vacuum cleaner stopped working because the belt snapped during use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: belt drive - a mechanical system using a belt to transmit motion
  • synonym: chain - loop of linked metal used to transmit motion in machines
