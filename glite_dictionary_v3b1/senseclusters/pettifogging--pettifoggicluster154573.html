pettifogging

noun
petty disputing
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpet.ɪ.fɑːg.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈpet.ɪ.fɔːg.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpet.ɪ.fɒg.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of arguing or disputing over unimportant or trivial details, often in a petty or unfair way

Examples

Endless pettifogging
The meeting was delayed by constant pettifogging over minor points in the agenda.
She grew frustrated with his pettifogging about every small error in the report.
Legal cases can drag on due to endless pettifogging between the lawyers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fairness - the quality of treating people equally or without bias
  • synonym: nitpicking - finding small or unimportant faults
  • synonym: quibbling - arguing about minor or unimportant details
