Amiidae

noun
family of freshwater fishes (bowfins)
Pronunciation: / ˌæmiˈaɪɪdi /; / ˌeɪ.miˈɪ.di.i / (AmE), / ˌæmɪˈaɪɪdiː /; / ˌæm.iˈɪ.diː.iː / (BrE)
a family of freshwater fishes known as bowfins, native to North America
