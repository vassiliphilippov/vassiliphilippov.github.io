jodhpurs

noun
riding trousers with flared hips
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑd.pɚz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒd.pəz / (BrE)
Trousers that are flared at the hips and tight from the knee to the ankle, traditionally worn for horse riding.

Examples

A pair of jodhpurs
He wore a pair of jodhpurs and tall leather boots for the riding competition.
Her grandfather kept his old jodhpurs as a reminder of his days as a cavalry officer.
The shop specializes in equestrian gear, including jodhpurs, jackets, and helmets.
