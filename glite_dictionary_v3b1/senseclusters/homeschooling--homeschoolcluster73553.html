homeschooling

adjective
for home education
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌskuːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌskuːlɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to materials, programs, or approaches intended for educating children at home rather than in a traditional school

Examples

Homeschooling materials
They purchased a homeschooling curriculum to better suit their children's learning styles.
The library offers many homeschooling resources for parents educating at home.
She attended a workshop on homeschooling methods to improve her teaching approach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: educational - relating to the process of teaching or learning
  • antonym: school-based - relating to education provided in a traditional school setting
