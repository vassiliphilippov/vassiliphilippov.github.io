baby face

noun
a youthful or innocent-looking face
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.bi feɪs / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi feɪs / (BrE)
a face with youthful or innocent features, often making the person appear younger than their actual age

Examples

Baby face features
Actors with a baby face are frequently cast as younger characters.
Despite being in his thirties, his baby face makes him look much younger.
She is often underestimated because of her baby face and gentle demeanor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: innocent-looking - having an appearance that suggests innocence
  • antonym: mature-looking - having an appearance that suggests older age or maturity
