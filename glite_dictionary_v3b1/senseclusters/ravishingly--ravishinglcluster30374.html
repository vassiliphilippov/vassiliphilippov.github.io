ravishingly

adverb
in an enchanting or captivating way
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrævɪʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈravɪʃɪŋli / (BrE)
In a delightful, enchanting, or captivating manner; to an exceptionally attractive or charming degree.

Examples

A ravishingly beautiful dress
He described the painting as ravishingly vibrant, full of life and color.
The actress looked ravishingly elegant in her red gown at the awards ceremony.
The garden was ravishingly beautiful in the early morning light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beautifully - in a beautiful manner
  • antonym: plainly - in a plain or unremarkable manner
