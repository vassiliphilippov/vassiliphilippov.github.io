turn a hair

verb
show no sign of worry or surprise
5.5
Pronunciation: / tɝn ə hɛr / (AmE), / tɜːn ə heə / (BrE)
to show any sign of worry, fear, or surprise; to remain completely calm and unaffected

Examples

Not turn a hair
Even when the whole plan fell apart, she didn't turn a hair.
He faced the angry crowd and didn't turn a hair.
Most people would be shocked by such news, but he didn't turn a hair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flinch - to make a quick, nervous movement as a reaction to fear or pain
