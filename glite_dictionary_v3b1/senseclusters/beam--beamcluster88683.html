beam

noun
gymnastics balancing apparatus
Pronunciation: / biːm / (AmE), / biːm / (BrE)
a long, narrow bar raised off the ground, used chiefly by female gymnasts to perform balancing exercises

Examples

Gymnastics beam
After slipping on the beam, she quickly regained her poise and finished her routine.
She performed a flawless routine on the beam at the gymnastics competition.
The beam requires great balance and concentration from gymnasts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balance beam - the specific gymnastics event or apparatus involving a narrow beam in women's gymnastics
