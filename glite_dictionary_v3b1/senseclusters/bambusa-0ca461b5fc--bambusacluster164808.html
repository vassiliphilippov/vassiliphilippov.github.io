Bambusa

noun
a genus of tall, clumping bamboos
9.4
Pronunciation: / bæmˈbuːsə / (AmE), / bæmˈbuːsə /; / bamˈbuːsə / (BrE)
a genus of tall, clumping bamboos from tropical and subtropical regions, known for their strong stems and use in construction and gardening
