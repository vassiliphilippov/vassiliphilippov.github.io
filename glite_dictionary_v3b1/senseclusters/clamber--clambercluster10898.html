clamber

verb
climb awkwardly using hands and feet
Pronunciation: / ˈklæmbər / (AmE), / ˈklæmbə / (BrE)
to climb or move in an awkward and laborious way, using hands and feet

Examples

To clamber up rocks
Several hikers clambered down the steep hillside to reach the trail.
She clambered up the ladder as quickly as she could.
The children clambered over the large rocks at the beach.
