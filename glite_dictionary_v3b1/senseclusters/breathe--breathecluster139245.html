breathe

verb
say or reveal (esp. a secret)
Pronunciation: / briːð / (AmE), / briːð / (BrE)
to utter, say, or reveal something, especially a secret

Examples

Breathe a word
He would rather die than breathe a hint of their plan.
She never breathed the secret, even under pressure.
You must not breathe a word of this to anyone.
