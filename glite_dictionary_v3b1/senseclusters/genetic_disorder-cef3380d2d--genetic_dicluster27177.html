Synonyms

  • genetic abnormality
    4.6 0.13/m
    genetic abnormality is any unusual genetic change that may cause harm, while genetic disorder names the resulting illness.
  • genetic defect
    4.4 0.31/m
    genetic defect is a faulty gene or change often seen as harmful, while genetic disorder refers to the resulting disease.
  • inherited disease
    3.0 0.16/m
    inherited disease is an illness passed from parents through genes, while genetic disorder highlights the genetic cause regardless of symptoms.
  • inherited disorder
    4.2 0.07/m

  • hereditary disease
    3.9 0.13/m
    hereditary disease is a condition families pass down through genes, while genetic disorder stresses the genetic change causing the illness.
  • hereditary condition
    4.0 0.05/m
    hereditary condition is any family-passed health issue through genes, while genetic disorder names specifically conditions caused by genetic abnormalities.
