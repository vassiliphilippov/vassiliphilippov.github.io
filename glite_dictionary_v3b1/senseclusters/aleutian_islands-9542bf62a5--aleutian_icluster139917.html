Aleutian Islands

proper noun
archipelago southwest of Alaska
Pronunciation: / əˈluːʃən ˈaɪˌlændz / (AmE), / əˈljuːʃən ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
a chain of islands in the North Pacific Ocean, extending southwest from the coast of Alaska.

Examples

The Aleutian Islands chain
