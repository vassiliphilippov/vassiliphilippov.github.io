landlubber

noun
inexperienced sailor or person unfamiliar with the sea
7.5 6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈlænd.lʌ.bər/ (AmE), /ˈlænd.lʌ.bə/ (BrE)
a person who is not used to being at sea; someone inexperienced with life on a ship
