Niihau Island

proper noun
westernmost main Hawaiian island
6.1
Pronunciation: / niːˈhaʊ ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / niːˈhaʊ ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
The westernmost of the main Hawaiian Islands, located northwest of Kauai.

Examples

Niihau Island beaches
A rare species of seal can be found on the beaches of Niihau Island.
Few tourists are allowed to visit Niihau Island because it is privately owned.
The native Hawaiian language is still spoken by residents on Niihau Island.
