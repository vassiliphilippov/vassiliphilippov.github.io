beef

noun
meat from a cow, bull, or steer
Pronunciation: / biːf / (AmE), / biːf / (BrE)
the flesh of a cow, bull, or steer, used as food

Examples

Roast beef dinner
Many people enjoy slow-cooked beef in stews and soups.
She ordered a beef steak with mashed potatoes for dinner.
The chef prefers to use fresh beef for making hamburgers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mutton - meat from an adult sheep
  • antonym: pork - meat from a pig
  • degree lower: veal - meat from a young calf
