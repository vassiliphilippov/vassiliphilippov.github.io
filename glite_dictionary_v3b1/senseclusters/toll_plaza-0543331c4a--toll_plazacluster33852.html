toll plaza

noun
area with tollbooths on a road
1.7 2.7
Pronunciation: / toʊl ˈplɑzə / (AmE), / təʊl ˈplɑːzə / (BrE)
A designated area on a road or highway where tollbooths are situated for the collection of tolls from passing vehicles.

Examples

A toll plaza booth
A new toll plaza was constructed to handle increased traffic on the expressway.
Drivers are advised to have exact change ready before entering the toll plaza.
Traffic slowed to a crawl as we approached the toll plaza during rush hour.
