auditable

adjective
able to be heard
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɔdətəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪtəbəl / (BrE)
able to be heard or perceived by the ear; capable of being heard.

Examples

Auditable sound
The music was barely auditable over the sound of the traffic outside.
She spoke in such a low voice that her words were hardly auditable.
From the hallway, an auditable whisper suggested someone was still in the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audible - able to be heard
  • antonym: inaudible - impossible to hear
