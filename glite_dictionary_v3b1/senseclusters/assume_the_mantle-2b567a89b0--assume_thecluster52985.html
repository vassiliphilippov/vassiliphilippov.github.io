assume the mantle

verb
take on a leadership role or responsibility
6.4
Pronunciation: / əˈsum ðə ˈmæntəl / (AmE), / əˈsjuːm ðə ˈmæntl̩ / (BrE)
to take on an important role or responsibility, especially one involving leadership

Examples

To assume the mantle of leader
After the CEO resigned, Sarah was chosen to assume the mantle and lead the company.
As the old king stepped down, his young son prepared to assume the mantle of leadership.
Many believed it was her destiny to assume the mantle of her family's philanthropic work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: take up the cudgels - to take on the cause or defense of something or someone
