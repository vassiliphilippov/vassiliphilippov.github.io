syndicate

noun
news agency syndicating content
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪndɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪndɪkət / (BrE)
a news agency that supplies articles, features, or photographs to multiple newspapers or media outlets for simultaneous publication

Examples

A news syndicate
Many small newspapers rely on a syndicate for national news stories and editorials.
She sold her column to a syndicate, ensuring its appearance in dozens of publications.
The article was distributed by a syndicate to newspapers across the country.
