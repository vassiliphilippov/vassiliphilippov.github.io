drawbench

noun
metalworking machine
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdrɑː.bentʃ/ /; / /ˈdrɔː.bentʃ/ (AmE), /ˈdrɔː.bentʃ/ (BrE)

Examples

A drawbench machine

A drawbench is essential in the process of making fine wire for electronics.
After setting up the drawbench, the workers began pulling the steel through the die.
