Constantinian

adjective
of Constantine's era
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnstənˈtɪniən / (AmE), / ˌkɒnstənˈtɪniən / (BrE)
relating to the Roman emperor Constantine or the period of his reign

Examples

Constantinian reforms
The Constantinian reforms changed the structure of the Roman Empire.
Many churches built during the Constantinian period reflect new architectural styles.
Historians debate the impact of Constantinian policies on early Christianity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Augustan - relating to the reign of the Roman emperor Augustus
  • synonym: Byzantine - relating to the Eastern Roman Empire or Byzantium
