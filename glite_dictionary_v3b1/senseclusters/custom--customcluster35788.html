custom

noun
regular patronage of a business
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstəm / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəm / (BrE)
the habit or practice of regularly buying goods or services from a particular business

Examples

Repeat custom
Shops rely on repeat custom to stay in business.
The bakery appreciated my custom and often gave me a discount.
Your continued custom is greatly valued by our company.
