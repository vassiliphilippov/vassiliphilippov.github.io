sleeping hibiscus

noun
plant with drooping hibiscus-like flowers
7.2 6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈslipɪŋ haɪˈbɪskəs / (AmE), / ˈsliːpɪŋ hɪˈbɪskəs / (BrE)
any of several plants of the genus Malvaviscus, known for their drooping, bell-shaped flowers that resemble unopened hibiscus blooms.

Examples

A sleeping hibiscus flower
She planted a sleeping hibiscus near the window for its bright color and unusual shape.
The garden was filled with blooming sleeping hibiscus plants, their flowers elegantly drooping in the afternoon sun.
Unlike open hibiscus flowers, the sleeping hibiscus keeps its petals less spread, giving it a unique look.
