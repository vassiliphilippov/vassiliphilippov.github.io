ticket window

noun
window where tickets are sold
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkɪt ˌwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt ˌwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
A window at a venue or station where tickets are sold to customers.

Examples

The ticket window counter
Please present your ID at the ticket window to collect your tickets.
She noticed the long queue at the ticket window before deciding to purchase her ticket online.
We waited in line at the ticket window to buy seats for the Saturday show.

Related Terms

  • synonym: box office - a place where tickets are sold, especially at a theater or cinema
