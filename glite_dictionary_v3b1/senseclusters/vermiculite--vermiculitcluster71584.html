vermiculite

noun
a mineral used for insulation and gardening

Pronunciation: /vɜːr.ˈmɪk.jə.laɪt/ /; / /vər.ˈmɪk.jə.laɪt/ (AmE), /vɜː.ˈmɪk.jʊ.laɪt/ /; / /vɜː.ˈmɪk.jə.laɪt/ (BrE)
