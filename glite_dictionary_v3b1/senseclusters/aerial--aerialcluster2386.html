aerial

noun
radio or TV signal receiver/sender
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɛriəl / (AmE), / ˈeəriəl / (BrE)
a device for sending or receiving radio or television signals, typically consisting of a wire or rod

Examples

TV aerial
I climbed onto the roof to adjust the aerial during the storm.
Portable radios often come with a built-in aerial for better reception.
The old television needed a new aerial to get a good signal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antenna - a device for transmitting or receiving radio waves; common in American English
