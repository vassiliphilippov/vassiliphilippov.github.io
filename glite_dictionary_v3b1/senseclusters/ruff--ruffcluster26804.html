ruff

verb
play a trump card in cards
9.9 8.3
Pronunciation: / rʌf / (AmE), / rʌf / (BrE)
to play a trump card in a card game when you cannot follow suit

Examples

To ruff a trick
If you have no hearts left, you may ruff with a spade.
She expertly ruffed her partner's lead to win the trick.
You should avoid ruffing too early in the game.
She ruffed the trick when clubs were led and she had no clubs left.
If you cannot follow suit, you may need to ruff to win the hand.
He ruffed with a spade, surprising the other players at the table.
