explode

verb
disprove or refute decisively
6.1
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsploʊd, ɛkˈsploʊd / (AmE), / ɪkˈspləʊd, ɛkˈspləʊd / (BrE)
To show that a theory or claim is baseless or false, especially by providing evidence; to refute something and render it obsolete.

Examples

To explode a theory
Modern genetics has effectively exploded the old theories about inheritance.
Several historians have exploded the claim that the battle changed the course of the war.
The new research exploded the longstanding myth about the effectiveness of the treatment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debunk - to expose the falseness or hollowness of an idea or belief
  • synonym: disprove - to show that something is false or incorrect
