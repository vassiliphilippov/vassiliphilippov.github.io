chinny

adjective
easily offended
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪni / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪni / (BrE)
easily upset or offended, especially about small or harmless things

Examples

A chinny person
He can be quite chinny if you tease him, so be careful with your jokes.
Some people are chinny about criticism, even when it is meant to help.
Try not to be so chinny about harmless jokes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sensitive - easily affected by the feelings or actions of others
  • antonym: thick-skinned - not easily upset or offended
  • synonym: touchy - easily upset or offended
