theater director

noun
supervisor of stage production and actors
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈθiː.ə.tər də.ˈrek.tər/ /; / /ˈθɪə.tər dɪ.ˈrek.tər/ (AmE), /ˈθiː.ə.tə dɪ.ˈrek.tə/ /; / /ˈθɪə.tə dɪ.ˈrek.tə/ (BrE)
A person responsible for supervising the actors and guiding the artistic and dramatic aspects of a stage production.
