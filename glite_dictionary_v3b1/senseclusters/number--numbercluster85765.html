number

noun
performance or act within a program
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
a short performance, musical piece, or act that is part of a larger entertainment program

Examples

Dance number
Each comedian had their own unique number in the variety performance.
She performed a solo number in the middle of the show, receiving loud applause.
The opening number was an energetic dance that thrilled the audience.
