Synonyms

  • farm worker
    1.1 1.11/m
    a farm worker does general paid physical work on farms, while a farmhand is the traditional informal term for such workers.
  • field hand
    4.0 0.26/m
    a field hand works mainly in crop fields doing manual tasks, while a farmhand can work across the whole farm doing varied chores.
  • hired hand
    3.6 0.41/m
    a hired hand is any paid manual worker, often seasonal or temporary, while a farmhand is specifically a farm-based worker.
  • agricultural laborer
    3.2 0.15/m
    an agricultural laborer is a formal term for people who till and do farm work, while a farmhand sounds more everyday and informal.
