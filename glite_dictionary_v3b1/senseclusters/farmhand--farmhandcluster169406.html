farmhand

noun
person hired to work on a farm
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːrmˌhænd / (AmE), / ˈfɑːmhænd / (BrE)
a person employed to work on a farm, especially doing physical labor

Examples

A farmhand worker
He worked as a farmhand every summer during college.
Several farmhands gathered in the barn to discuss the day's tasks.
The farmhand woke up before sunrise to tend to the animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laborer - a person doing unskilled manual work
