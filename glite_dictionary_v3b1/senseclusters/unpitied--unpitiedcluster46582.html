unpitied

adjective
without pity
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɪtid / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɪtid / (BrE)
not receiving pity, sympathy, or compassion from others

Examples

An unpitied man
After his betrayal, he was left unpitied by everyone he once called a friend.
The villain's cruel actions made her unpitied in the eyes of the public.
He died alone and unpitied, remembered only for his harshness.
He suffered alone and unpitied by those around him.
The stray dog wandered the streets, unpitied and ignored by everyone.
Her struggles went unpitied, as no one seemed to notice her pain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pitied - regarded with pity or compassion
  • synonym: pitiless - showing no pity or compassion
  • synonym: unforgiven - not forgiven or excused
  • synonym: unloved - not loved or cared for by others
