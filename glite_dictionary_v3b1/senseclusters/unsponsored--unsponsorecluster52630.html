unsponsored

adjective
without sponsorship
5.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspɑnsərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈspɒnsəd / (BrE)

Examples

An unsponsored trip

The team entered the competition as an unsponsored group, relying on their own resources.
Many artists prefer to remain unsponsored to maintain creative freedom.
