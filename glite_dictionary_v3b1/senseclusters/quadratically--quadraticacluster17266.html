quadratically

adverb
involving a square
8.6
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈdrætɪkli / (AmE), / kwɒˈdrætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or changes as the square of another quantity

Examples

Increase quadratically
The cost of the project increased quadratically as the number of participants grew.
In physics, some forces depend quadratically on the distance between objects.
The brightness of the light drops quadratically as you move farther away.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: exponentially - in a way that increases very rapidly by powers
  • degree lower: linearly - in a way that changes at a constant rate
