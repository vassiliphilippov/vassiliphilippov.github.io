transgressively

adverb
beyond accepted limits
8.8
Pronunciation: / trænzˈɡrɛsɪvli / (AmE), / trænzˈɡrɛsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that goes beyond accepted rules, limits, or boundaries, often deliberately.

Examples

To act transgressively
The filmmaker approached the subject transgressively, breaking many traditional cinematic rules.
She spoke transgressively about topics that others avoided in polite conversation.
The novel is written transgressively, challenging the boundaries of genre and style.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conformingly - in a way that follows accepted rules or standards
  • synonym: rebelliously - in a way that resists authority or control
