oligophagy

noun
extreme picky eating
9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.lɪ.ˈɡɑː.fə.dʒiː/ /; / /ɑː.lɪ.ˈɡɑː.fə.dʒiː/ (AmE), /ɒ.lɪ.ˈɡɒf.ə.dʒiː/ /; / /ə.lɪ.ˈɡɒf.ə.dʒiː/ (BrE)
a condition or behavior in which a person eats only a very limited variety of foods
