screwtop

noun
container cap that screws on and off
Pronunciation: / ˈskruː.tɑp / (AmE), / ˈskruː.tɒp / (BrE)
a cap or lid for a container that must be twisted to open or close

Examples

A screwtop bottle
Remember to tightly close the screwtop after pouring out the medicine.
She struggled to open the screwtop on the bottle but it was stuck.
Wine with a screwtop is often more convenient than one with a cork.
