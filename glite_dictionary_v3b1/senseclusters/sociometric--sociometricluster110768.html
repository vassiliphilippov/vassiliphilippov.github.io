sociometric

adjective
measuring social relations
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsi.oʊˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsi.əˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement and analysis of social relationships and interactions within a group

Examples

Sociometric analysis
The teacher used sociometric methods to understand the friendships in her classroom.
A sociometric survey revealed the most influential members of the team.
Researchers collected sociometric data to map the group's communication patterns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychometric - relating to the measurement of psychological traits and abilities
  • synonym: social - relating to society or its organization
