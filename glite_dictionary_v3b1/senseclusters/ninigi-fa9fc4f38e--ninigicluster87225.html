Ninigi

proper noun
mythical first ruler of Japan, grandson of Amaterasu
9.9
Pronunciation: / niːˈniːɡi / (AmE), / niːˈniːɡi / (BrE)
In Japanese mythology, the grandson of the sun goddess Amaterasu, regarded as the first ruler of Japan and the ancestor of its emperors.

Examples

Ninigi in mythology
According to Shinto beliefs, Ninigi received the sacred regalia from Amaterasu.
Many legends describe how Ninigi descended from heaven to rule over Japan.
The imperial family of Japan is traditionally said to be descended from Ninigi.
