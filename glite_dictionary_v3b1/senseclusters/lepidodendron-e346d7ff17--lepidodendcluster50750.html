Lepidodendron

noun
extinct fossil tree
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛpɪdoʊˈdɛndrən / (AmE), / ˌlɛpɪdəˈdɛndrən / (BrE)
an extinct genus of large, tree-like plants from the Carboniferous period, known mainly from fossilized trunks and stems found in coal-bearing rocks
