lookdown

noun
a silvery, deep-bodied marine fish
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊkˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈlʊkˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a silvery, deep-bodied marine fish (Selene vomer) of the western Atlantic Ocean, with a steeply sloped forehead and eyes set high on its head.

Examples

A lookdown fish
Fishermen sometimes catch lookdowns near the coast during the summer months.
The lookdown swims gracefully, its flattened body reflecting light like a mirror.
The aquarium has a beautiful lookdown on display in its marine gallery.
