hydromorphone

noun
opioid pain medication
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdroʊˈmɔrfoʊn / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪdrəˈmɔːfəʊn / (BrE)
a powerful opioid pain medication used to treat moderate to severe pain

Examples

Hydromorphone pain medication
Because of its potency, hydromorphone is used only when less powerful analgesics are not effective.
She received a dose of hydromorphone in the hospital after her surgery.
The doctor prescribed hydromorphone to manage the patient's severe post-surgical pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: morphine - an opioid pain medication from which hydromorphone is derived
  • synonym: oxycodone - another opioid pain medication used for moderate to severe pain
