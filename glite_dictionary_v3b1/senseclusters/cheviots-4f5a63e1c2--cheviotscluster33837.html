Cheviots

proper noun
hills on the England-Scotland border
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiː.vi.əts / (AmE), / ˈʃiː.vi.əts / (BrE)
a range of hills located on the border between England and Scotland, known for their scenic views and as the source of the River Tweed

Examples

The Cheviots hills
