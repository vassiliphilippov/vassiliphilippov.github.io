metafictional

adjective
about self-aware fiction
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈfɪkʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈfɪkʃənəl / (BrE)
describing a literary work that draws attention to its own fictional status or the act of storytelling

Examples

Related Terms

  • antonym: realistic - representing things as they are, without drawing attention to the fiction
  • synonym: self-referential - referring to itself or its own creation
