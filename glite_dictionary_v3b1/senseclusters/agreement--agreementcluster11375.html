Synonyms

  • consensus
    4.1 14/m
    consensus is a general shared view among a group, while agreement covers any situation of same opinion, harmony, or approval.
  • accord
    4.0 2/m
    accord is a formal state of harmony between parties, while agreement is a general term for same opinion or approval.
  • meeting of minds
    4.2 0.08/m
    meeting of minds is when people fully understand and agree to act together, while agreement can be looser or less complete.
  • concurrence
    7.2 0.2/m
    concurrence is agreeing at the same time or sharing the same view, while agreement is the general state of consent or harmony.
  • consent
    3.2 16/m
    consent is giving permission or approval for something to happen, while agreement also includes shared opinion or harmony between people.
  • assent
    6.8 0.6/m
    assent is a formal or official saying yes to a proposal, while agreement can describe a general shared opinion or harmony.
  • concord
    6.7 0.06/m
    concord is peaceful agreement or accord between people or groups, while agreement is broader and used in many everyday situations.
  • concordance
    7.7 0.05/m
    concordance is an orderly match or agreement among things or texts, while agreement is a general shared opinion or approval.
  • harmony
    2.5 7/m
    harmony is smooth, pleasant working together of people or ideas, while agreement means having the same opinion or formal approval.
