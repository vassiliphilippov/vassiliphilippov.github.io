background

noun
essential information for understanding a situation
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈbækɡraʊnd / (BrE)
the information, context, or conditions that help explain or set the scene for something

Examples

Background information
Before we go any further, let me provide some background on the topic.
She researched the background of the company before her interview.
Understanding the background of the dispute is crucial to finding a solution.
Economic uncertainty formed the background to many of this year's political debates.
The negotiations took place against the background of rising tensions in the region.
There was constant noise in the background during our meeting at the café.

Related Terms

  • synonym: context - the circumstances forming the setting for an event or statement
  • antonym: foreground - the part of a situation or image nearest to the viewer or given more prominence
  • synonym: setting - the place or surroundings where something is positioned or an event takes place
