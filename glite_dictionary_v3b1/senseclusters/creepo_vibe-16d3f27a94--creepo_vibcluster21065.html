creepo vibe

noun
an unsettling or creepy atmosphere
Pronunciation: / ˈkripoʊ vaɪb / (AmE), / ˈkriːpəʊ vaɪb / (BrE)
an unsettling or disturbing atmosphere or feeling, often caused by someone or something that seems creepy or suspicious

Examples

A creepo vibe at bar

That message gave me a creepo vibe, so I decided not to respond.
