expression

noun
process of a gene producing its product
2.4
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
(Genetics) The process by which information from a gene is used to synthesize a functional gene product, such as a protein.

Examples

Gene expression
Gene expression can be influenced by both genetic and environmental factors.
Scientists measured the expression levels of several important genes in the sample.
The expression of the gene increased dramatically after treatment.
