uncapitalized

adjective
not treated as asset
7.5
Pronunciation: /ən.ˈkæp.ɪ.təl.aɪzd/ (AmE), /ən.ˈkæp.ɪ.təl.aɪzd/ (BrE)
in accounting, recorded as an expense rather than as a capital asset on the balance sheet

Examples

Uncapitalized expense
The company considered the purchase of laptops as uncapitalized and included the cost in operating expenses.
Small repairs are usually uncapitalized and do not appear as assets on the balance sheet.
Office supplies are often uncapitalized because they are used up within a year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: capitalized - recorded as a capital asset on the balance sheet
  • synonym: expensed - charged as an expense in the current period
