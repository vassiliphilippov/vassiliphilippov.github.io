Synonyms

  • mass-produce
    3.5 0.66/m
    mass-produce means making large quantities with machines and fixed processes, while manufacture is a general term for producing goods.
  • turn out
    3.5 1.23/m
    turn out means producing things quickly or in large numbers, often by machines, while manufacture focuses on production, often industrial.
  • fabricate
    5.5 3.01/m
    fabricate means build or make parts from prepared components, often by skilled workers, while manufacture implies mass production.
