musketry

noun
collective term for musketeers and muskets
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌskətri / (AmE), / ˈmʌskɪtri / (BrE)
Musketeers and their muskets considered as a collective group, especially in a military context.

Examples

Loud musketry
During the battle, the sound of musketry filled the air as musketeers fired volley after volley.
Old manuscripts describe the discipline and precision of 17th-century musketry.
The advancing army was supported by a line of musketry that echoed across the field.
