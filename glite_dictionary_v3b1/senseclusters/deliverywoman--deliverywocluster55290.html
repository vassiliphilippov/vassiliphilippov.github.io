deliverywoman

noun
woman who delivers goods
1.1
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪvəriˌwʊmən / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvəriˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to deliver goods to customers or businesses

Examples

A deliverywoman brings packages
A friendly deliverywoman handed me the bouquet with a big smile.
The deliverywoman dropped off the package at my doorstep just before noon.
We thanked the deliverywoman for bringing our groceries during the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courier - a person employed to deliver messages, packages, or mail
  • antonym: deliveryman - a man employed to make deliveries
