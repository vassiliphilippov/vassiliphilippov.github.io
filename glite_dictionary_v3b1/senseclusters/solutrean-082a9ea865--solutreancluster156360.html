Solutrean

noun
prehistoric European people
9.9
Pronunciation: / səˈluːtriən / (AmE), / səˈluːtriən / (BrE)
a member of the prehistoric people associated with the Solutrean culture of Upper Paleolithic Europe

Examples

A Solutrean person

The museum displayed artifacts created by the Solutrean people thousands of years ago.
Researchers believe the Solutrean had advanced techniques for making stone blades.
