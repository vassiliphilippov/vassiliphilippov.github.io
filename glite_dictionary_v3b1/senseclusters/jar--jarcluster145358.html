jar

noun
sudden jarring impact
Pronunciation: / dʒɑɹ / (AmE), / dʒɑː / (BrE)
a sudden harsh or jarring impact or shock

Examples

A jar from impact
Every jar of the rough road rattled our teeth.
She felt a sudden jar as the elevator stopped abruptly.
The train came to a halt with a jar that made everyone stumble.
