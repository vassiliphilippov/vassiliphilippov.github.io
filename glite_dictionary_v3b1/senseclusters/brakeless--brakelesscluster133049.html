brakeless

adjective
without brakes
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkləs / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkləs / (BrE)
not equipped with brakes or lacking a braking system

Examples

Brakeless bike
He rode a brakeless bike through the busy city streets.
Driving a brakeless vehicle is extremely dangerous and illegal in most places.
The old cart was completely brakeless, making it hard to control on hills.
