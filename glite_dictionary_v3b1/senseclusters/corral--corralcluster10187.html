corral

noun
enclosure for cattle or horses
Pronunciation: / kəˈræl / (AmE), / kəˈrɑːl / (BrE)
an enclosure or pen for keeping livestock, especially cattle or horses

Examples

A wooden corral
After the roundup, the mustangs were kept in the corral overnight.
The ranchers led the cattle into the corral before dusk.
The sturdy wooden corral can hold up to fifty animals at a time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pen - an enclosure for confining livestock
