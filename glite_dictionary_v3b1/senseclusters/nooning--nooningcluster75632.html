nooning

noun
midday rest or meal
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnuːnɪŋ / (BrE)
a rest or meal taken at noon, especially by workers or travelers in the past

Examples

A nooning break
The farmers gathered in the field for their nooning before returning to work.
During the long journey, the travelers enjoyed a brief nooning by the river.
In older times, a nooning was a welcome pause from the day's labor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: break - a short period of rest from work
  • synonym: lunch - a meal eaten in the middle of the day
