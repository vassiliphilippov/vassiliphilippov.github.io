interiorization

noun
designing interiors
8.3
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tɪr.ɪə.raɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪn.tɪə.rɪə.raɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪn.tɪə.rɪə.raɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of designing, adapting, or fitting out the interior of a building or space for use

Examples

Interiorization of a building
The interiorization of the old factory transformed it into a stylish office space.
Architects paid special attention to the interiorization of the new community center.
Successful interiorization can make even small apartments feel spacious and comfortable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exteriorization - the process of focusing on or adapting the exterior of a building
  • synonym: interior design - the art or process of designing the interior decoration of a room or building
  • synonym: renovation - the process of improving or modernizing a building
