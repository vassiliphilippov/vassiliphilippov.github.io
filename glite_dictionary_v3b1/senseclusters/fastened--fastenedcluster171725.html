fastened

adjective
secured or closed with fasteners
Pronunciation: / ˈfæsənd /; / ˈfæsn̩d / (AmE), / ˈfɑːsənd / (BrE)
closed or secured so that it does not open or move, usually with buttons, clasps, or similar fastenings

Examples

A fastened shirt
His shirt remained neatly fastened during the entire meeting.
She made sure her coat was fully fastened before going outside.
The diary was fastened with a small brass lock and key.
Please make sure your seatbelt is fastened before the car starts moving.
The door was tightly fastened to keep out the cold wind.
With all the buttons fastened, his coat looked neat and tidy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: secured - firmly fixed in place
  • antonym: unfastened - not secured, open or undone
