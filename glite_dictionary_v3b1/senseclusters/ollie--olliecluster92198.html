ollie

verb
to jump on skateboard
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑli / (AmE), / ˈɒli / (BrE)
to jump into the air with a skateboard or similar board by snapping the tail and leveling the board while riding.

Examples

To ollie a curb
He learned how to ollie over the curb after weeks of practice.
She tried to ollie higher to clear the small set of stairs.
Many beginners struggle to ollie smoothly when they first start skateboarding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jump - to push oneself off a surface and into the air
  • degree higher: kickflip - to flip a skateboard in the air while performing an ollie
