belle de nuit

noun
plant with nocturnal fragrant flowers
9.0
Pronunciation: / bɛl də ˈnwi / (AmE), / bɛl də ˈnwiː / (BrE)
A pantropical climbing plant with white, fragrant flowers that bloom at night, commonly called 'belle de nuit.'

Examples

The belle de nuit flower
Gardeners often plant belle de nuit near patios to enjoy their sweet fragrance in the evening.
In tropical regions, the belle de nuit climbs along fences and trellises, creating a striking nighttime display.
The belle de nuit is admired for its beautiful white blossoms that only open after sunset.
