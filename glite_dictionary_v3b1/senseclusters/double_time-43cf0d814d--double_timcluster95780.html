double time

adverb
at twice the normal speed
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl taɪm / (AmE), / ˈdʌb(ə)l taɪm / (BrE)
at a speed that is twice as fast as normal; very quickly

Examples

Play in double time
Let's rehearse the piece again, but this time play it in double time to pick up the energy.
The soldiers were ordered to march in double time to reach the camp before sunset.
When the deadline approached, the team worked at double time to finish the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: double-quick - at very fast speed; quickly
  • antonym: half time - at half the usual speed (especially in music)
