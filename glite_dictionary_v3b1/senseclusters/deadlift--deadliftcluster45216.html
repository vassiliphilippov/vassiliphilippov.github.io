deadlift

noun
a weightlifting exercise
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdˌlɪft / (AmE), / ˈdɛd.lɪft / (BrE)
a weightlifting exercise in which a loaded barbell is lifted off the ground to the hips and then lowered back to the ground

Examples

Heavy deadlift exercise
His heaviest deadlift last year was over 200 kilograms.
Proper form is crucial when performing the deadlift to prevent injury.
She added the deadlift to her workout routine to build strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bench press - a weightlifting exercise in which a person presses a weight upwards while lying on a bench
  • synonym: squat - a strength exercise in which the trainee lowers their hips from a standing position and then stands back up
