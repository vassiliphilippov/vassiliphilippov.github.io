mushroom sauce

noun
sauce containing mushrooms
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌʃˌruːm sɔs / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃrəm sɔːs / (BrE)
A sauce made with or flavored by mushrooms, often served as an accompaniment to meat, pasta, or vegetables.

Examples

A spoonful of mushroom sauce
Add a spoonful of mushroom sauce to your chicken for extra taste.
She made a delicious mushroom sauce to go with the pasta.
The steak was topped with a creamy mushroom sauce that was rich in flavor.
