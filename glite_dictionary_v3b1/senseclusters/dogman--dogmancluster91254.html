dogman

noun
professional dog handler
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡˌmæn / (BrE)
a person who works with or handles dogs, especially as a professional trainer or handler

Examples

Professional dogman
The dogman demonstrated advanced obedience techniques with the police dogs.
Every dogman on the team is responsible for the care and training of their assigned animals.
After years as a dogman, he opened his own dog training school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handler - a person who trains or manages animals
  • synonym: trainer - a person who teaches skills to animals or people
