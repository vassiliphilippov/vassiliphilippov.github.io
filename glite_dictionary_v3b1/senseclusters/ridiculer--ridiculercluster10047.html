ridiculer

noun
one who mocks using ridicule or sarcasm
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪd.ɪ.kju.lər / (AmE), / ˈrɪd.ɪ.kjuː.lə / (BrE)

Though he called himself a ridiculer, his sarcasm sometimes went too far.
