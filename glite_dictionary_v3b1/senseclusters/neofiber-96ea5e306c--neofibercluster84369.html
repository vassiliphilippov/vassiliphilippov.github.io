Neofiber

noun
genus of round-tailed muskrats
Pronunciation: / ˈnioʊˌfaɪbər / (AmE), / ˈniːəʊˌfaɪbə / (BrE)
A genus of North American rodents commonly known as the round-tailed muskrat.
