wanker

noun
insulting slang term for a contemptible person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwæŋkər / (AmE), / ˈwaŋ.kə / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) an insulting term for a person considered contemptible or foolish, originally meaning someone who masturbates.

Examples

A wanker insult
Don't be such a wanker—listen to what people are saying for once.
He called the arrogant manager a total wanker after the meeting.
She stormed out, muttering that her colleague was a complete wanker.
