trailing arbutus

noun
low-growing North American evergreen shrub
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪlɪŋ ɑːrˈbjuːtəs / (AmE), / ˈtreɪlɪŋ ɑːˈbjuːtəs / (BrE)
a low-growing evergreen shrub of eastern North America with leathery leaves and clusters of fragrant pink or white flowers
