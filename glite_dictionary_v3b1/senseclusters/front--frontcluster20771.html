front

noun
outward appearance to conceal feelings
Pronunciation: / frʌnt / (AmE), / frʌnt / (BrE)
a false or outward appearance, especially used to conceal true feelings or intentions

Examples

A brave front
Despite her worries, she put on a brave front for her friends.
He maintained a cheerful front, even though he was struggling inside.
Underneath his confident front, he was actually quite nervous.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facade - a deceptive outward appearance
  • synonym: mask - a covering or disguise to hide true feelings
