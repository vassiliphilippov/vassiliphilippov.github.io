in the loop

adjective
informed or kept up to date
Pronunciation: / ɪn ðə luːp / (AmE), / ɪn ðə luːp / (BrE)
aware of or informed about a particular matter or ongoing events in an organization, group, or situation.

Examples

Keep in the loop
Her manager kept her in the loop regarding all major decisions.
It's important that everyone stays in the loop during the entire project.
Let me know if you want to be in the loop about future developments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: out of the loop - not informed or kept up to date
