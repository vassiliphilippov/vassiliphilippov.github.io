different clowns, same circus

x
same problems with different people
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪfɚənt klaʊnz seɪm ˈsɜrkəs / (AmE), / ˈdɪfrənt klaʊnz seɪm ˈsɜːkəs / (BrE)
used to describe a situation where the people involved have changed but the underlying problems or patterns remain the same

Examples

Different clowns, same circus
I changed departments hoping for less drama, but it’s just different clowns, same circus.
It's a new management team, but it's still different clowns, same circus at that company.
Politics never seems to change—every election is different clowns, same circus.
