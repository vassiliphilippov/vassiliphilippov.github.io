non-commercialism

noun
rejection of commercial activity
Pronunciation: / nɑn kəˈmɜr.ʃəˌlɪz.əm / (AmE), / nɒn kəˈmɜː.ʃəl.ɪ.zəm / (BrE)
The principle or practice of not being involved in commercial activities or enterprises.

Examples

Spirit of non-commercialism
Despite increasing corporate sponsorships, the festival has maintained a spirit of non-commercialism.
Many artists value non-commercialism as it allows for greater creative freedom.
The museum prides itself on its non-commercialism and commitment to public education.

Related Terms

  • antonym: commercialism - emphasis on or orientation toward commercial interests
