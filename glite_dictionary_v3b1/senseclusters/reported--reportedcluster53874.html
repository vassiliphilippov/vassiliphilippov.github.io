reported

adjective
presented in an official or formal account
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɔrtɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈpɔːtɪd / (BrE)
presented or made known through an official or formal account, rather than directly observed or experienced

Examples

The reported incident
After the reported incident, the school implemented stricter safety measures.
The reported symptoms were documented by the nurse in the patient's chart.
There is no evidence supporting the reported decline in sales.
