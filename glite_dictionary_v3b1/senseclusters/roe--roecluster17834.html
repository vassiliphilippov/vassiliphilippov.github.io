roe

noun
egg mass of certain crustaceans
9.1
Pronunciation: / roʊ / (AmE), / rəʊ / (BrE)
the mass of eggs or spawn of certain crustaceans, such as lobsters or shrimp, often used as food

Examples

Crab roe
Fishermen collected the roe from crabs to sell at the market.
Lobster roe turns bright red when cooked and is considered a delicacy by some.
The chef garnished the dish with a spoonful of shrimp roe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caviar - salted roe of sturgeon or other large fish, delicacy
