supermax

noun
high-security prison
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌmæks / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəmæks / (BrE)
a prison designed with the highest level of security to house the most dangerous or high-risk inmates

Examples

A supermax prison
After the violent incident, the inmate was moved to a supermax facility for increased security.
Only the most dangerous criminals are sent to a supermax prison.
The new supermax was built to prevent any possibility of escape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maximum security prison - a prison with very strict security measures
  • antonym: minimum security prison - a prison with the lowest level of security
