vowelize

verb
pronounce or treat as a vowel
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvaʊəlaɪz /; / ˈvaʊ.əˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈvaʊəlaɪz /; / ˈvaʊ.ə.laɪz / (BrE)
to pronounce or treat (a sound or letter) as a vowel, especially by inserting a vowel sound or quality
