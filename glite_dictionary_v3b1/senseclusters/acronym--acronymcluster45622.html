acronym

noun
word formed from initials and pronounced as one word
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈækrəˌnɪm / (AmE), / ˈækrənɪm / (BrE)
A word formed from the initial letters of a phrase or group of words and pronounced as a single word.

Examples

New acronyms in technology
Many new acronyms are created in the world of technology.
NASA is an acronym for National Aeronautics and Space Administration.
The word 'radar' is an acronym that has become part of everyday language.
