supplementation

noun
the act of adding to fill a need or deficiency
7.1 4.0
Pronunciation: /sʌp.lə.mən.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /sʌp.lɪ.men.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /sʌp.lɪ.men.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /sʌp.lə.mən.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of adding something extra to make up for a lack or to improve something, especially in diet or nutrition
