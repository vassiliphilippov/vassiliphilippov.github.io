political donation

noun
contribution to a political cause or campaign
3.6
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪt̬ɪkəl doʊˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl dəʊˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a sum of money or other contribution given to a politician, political campaign, or political party to support their activities

Examples

A political donation
She made a generous political donation to support environmental policy reform.
Strict laws regulate the size of any political donation in that country.
The senator refused to accept a political donation from corporate entities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bribe - money given to influence decisions, often illegally
  • synonym: campaign contribution - a donation made specifically to a political campaign
