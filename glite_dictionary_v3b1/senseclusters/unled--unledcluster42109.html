unled

adjective
without guidance
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɛd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɛd / (BrE)
not guided, directed, or led by anyone

Examples

An unled group
The expedition remained unled after their guide became ill.
An unled team can struggle to make important decisions in difficult situations.
They entered the abandoned building unled and unsure of what they would find.
