truculency

noun
aggressive or defiant attitude
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌk.jə.lən.si / (AmE), / ˈtrʌk.jʊ.lən.si / (BrE)
an aggressive or defiant attitude; the quality of being quick to argue or fight

Examples

His truculency attitude
His truculency made negotiations difficult for everyone involved.
She confronted her critics with a noticeable truculency in her voice.
The debate was marked by truculency rather than constructive discussion.
