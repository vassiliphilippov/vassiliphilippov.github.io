Synonyms

  • lean
    5.0 4.45/m
    lean describes a deviation toward one side from vertical, often of a person or object, while tilt means a general slant away from level.
  • list
    9.1 1.54/m
    list refers to a ship or structure leaning to one side, usually unexpectedly, while tilt is any slant or inclination from straight or level.
  • inclination
    6.3 0.93/m
    inclination is the measured angle showing how far something tilts from a reference line, while tilt is the general idea of a slant or lean.
  • slope
    3.2 9.62/m
