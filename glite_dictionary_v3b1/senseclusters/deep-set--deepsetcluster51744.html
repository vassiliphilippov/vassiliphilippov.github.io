deep-set

adjective
set far back; sunken appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈdipˌsɛt /; / ˌdipˈsɛt / (AmE), / ˈdiːpˌsɛt /; / ˌdiːpˈsɛt / (BrE)
(of eyes or facial features) positioned far back from the surface, giving a sunken or recessed appearance
