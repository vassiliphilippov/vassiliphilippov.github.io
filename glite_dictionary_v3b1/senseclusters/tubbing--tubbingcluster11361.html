tubbing

noun
relaxing in a tub
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʌbɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of sitting or relaxing in a tub, especially a hot tub, for enjoyment or relaxation

Examples

Hot tubbing
After a long hike, they enjoyed some tubbing in the hot springs.
Many resorts offer outdoor tubbing as a way to unwind and enjoy nature.
We spent the evening tubbing and watching the stars overhead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soaking - sitting in water for relaxation
