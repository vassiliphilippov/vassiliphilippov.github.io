intriguer

noun
a person who plots or schemes
8.0 7.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈtriː.ɡər/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈtriː.ɡə/ (BrE)
a person who schemes or devises secret plots, often for personal gain or to manipulate situations

Examples

An intriguer in court
During court politics, an intriguer often gained power by spreading rumors.
Some saw him as a visionary, others as a mere intriguer seeking personal advantage.
The novel’s main villain is an intriguer manipulating events from behind the scenes.
