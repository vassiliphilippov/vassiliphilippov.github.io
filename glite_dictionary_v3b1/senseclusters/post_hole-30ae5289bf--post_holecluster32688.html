post hole

noun
hole dug for a fence post
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊst hoʊl / (AmE), / ˈpəʊst həʊl / (BrE)
a hole dug in the ground to hold a fence post or similar upright support

Examples

A post hole in ground
Before installing the new fence, we had to dig each post hole by hand.
The builder measured the distance carefully before digging the first post hole.
They filled each post hole with concrete to secure the wooden posts.
