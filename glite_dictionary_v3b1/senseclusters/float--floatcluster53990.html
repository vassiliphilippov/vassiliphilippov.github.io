float

verb
make a surface level or smooth with a tool
1.7
Pronunciation: / floʊt / (AmE), / fləʊt / (BrE)
to make a surface level or smooth, especially using a float tool in plastering or masonry

Examples

To float a wall

It's important to float the render while it's still wet so it sets evenly.
The mason carefully floated the concrete to achieve a perfectly smooth finish.
