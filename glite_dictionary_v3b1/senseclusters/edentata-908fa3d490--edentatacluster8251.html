Edentata

noun
historical mammal order with few or no teeth
Pronunciation: / iːˌdɛnˈteɪtə /; / ˌiː.dɛnˈteɪ.tə / (AmE), / iːˌdɛnˈtɑːtə /; / ˌiː.dɛnˈtɑː.tə / (BrE)
a former order of mammals with few or no teeth, including anteaters, sloths, and armadillos (now mostly replaced in scientific classification)

Examples

The classification of Edentata is now largely considered outdated in modern taxonomy.
