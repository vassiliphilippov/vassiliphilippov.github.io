capital gains tax

noun
tax on profits from asset sales
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpɪtl ɡeɪnz tæks / (AmE), / ˈkapɪtl ɡeɪnz taks / (BrE)
a tax levied on profits earned from the sale of assets such as stocks, bonds, or property

Examples

Capital gains tax rate
Careful tax planning can help minimize your capital gains tax liability.
Investors must pay capital gains tax on the profit they make from selling shares.
Some countries do not impose a capital gains tax on residential property sales.

Related Terms

  • synonym: income tax - tax on income earned by individuals or businesses
  • synonym: sales tax - tax imposed on sales of goods and services
