schmear

noun
spread of soft food
Pronunciation: / ʃmɪr / (AmE), / ʃmɪə / (BrE)
a spread or layer of a soft food, especially cream cheese, placed on bread or a bagel

Examples

Bagel with schmear
He ordered a bagel with a schmear of cream cheese for breakfast.
Would you like a little schmear on your toast, or do you prefer it plain?
The deli offers a variety of schmears, including chive and smoked salmon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spread - a soft food that is spread on bread or crackers
  • synonym: topping - a food placed on top of another food
