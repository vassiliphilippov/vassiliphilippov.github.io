scissors

noun
wrestling hold using the legs
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪzərz / (AmE), / ˈsɪzəz / (BrE)
A wrestling hold in which the legs are wrapped around an opponent's body or head and squeezed together.

Examples

Scissors hold
He escaped the scissors and countered with his own maneuver.
She won the match by locking in a powerful scissors hold.
The wrestler applied a tight scissors around his opponent's waist.
