muzzleloading

noun
loading firearms at muzzle
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌzəlˌloʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmʌzəlˌləʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice or activity of using firearms that are loaded from the open end of the barrel (the muzzle)

Examples

Muzzleloading hobby
He became interested in muzzleloading after reading about early American history.
The club organizes muzzleloading competitions for enthusiasts of antique firearms.
Safety is especially important when practicing muzzleloading at the shooting range.

Related Terms

  • synonym: black powder shooting - the sport of shooting with traditional black powder firearms
  • antonym: breechloading - the practice of loading firearms from the rear (breech) of the barrel
