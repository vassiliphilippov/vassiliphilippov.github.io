More than two or three but not many; an indefinite small number, used with countable nouns.

Examples

Several books were stacked on the table in the corner of the room.
Several students stayed after class to ask questions about the assignment.
The museum displayed several paintings from the 19th century.

Synonyms

  • few
    1.0 815/m
