trouper

noun
touring actor or performer
4.4 6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtruːpər / (AmE), / ˈtruːpə / (BrE)
An actor or performer, especially one who is part of a touring company and travels to perform in different places.

Examples

Seasoned trouper
As a seasoned trouper, she was used to life on the road with the theater company.
He spent most of his youth as a trouper, performing Shakespeare in small towns.
The old trouper shared stories of performing in makeshift venues across the country.
