family Laricariidae

noun
a family of armored catfish
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ˌlərɪˌkɛəriˈaɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˌlarɪˌkɛːrɪˈaɪdiː / (BrE)
