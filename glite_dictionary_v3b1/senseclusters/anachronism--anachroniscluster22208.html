anachronism

noun
something out of its proper time
5.9 7.8
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈnæ.krə.nɪz.əm/ /; / /ə.ˈnæk.rə.nɪz.əm/ (AmE), /ə.ˈnæ.krə.nɪz.əm/ /; / /ə.ˈnæk.rə.nɪz.əm/ (BrE)
a person or thing that is out of place in time, especially something old-fashioned or from a different period

A wristwatch in a movie set in ancient Rome is a clear anachronism.
