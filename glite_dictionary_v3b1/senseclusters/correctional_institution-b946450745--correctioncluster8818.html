correctional institution

noun
government-run place for confining offenders
4.2
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɛkʃənəl ˌɪnstəˈtuʃən / (AmE), / kəˈrɛkʃənəl ˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃən / (BrE)
a place where people are confined as punishment for a crime or while awaiting trial, especially one operated by the government

Examples

A correctional institution
After his conviction, he was sent to a correctional institution to serve his sentence.
She works as a counselor at a local correctional institution for young offenders.
The government is investing in programs to reform inmates within the correctional institution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jail - a place for the confinement of people accused or convicted of a crime
  • synonym: prison - a building where people are legally held as punishment for a crime
