nondisruptive

adjective
not causing disturbance
4.8
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.dɪs.ˈrʌp.tɪv/ /; / /nə n.dɪs.ˈrʌp.tɪv/ (AmE), /nɒn.dɪs.ˈrʌp.tɪv/ /; / /nə n.dɪs.ˈrʌp.tɪv/ (BrE)
not causing interruption, disturbance, or significant change to a process or situation

Examples

A nondisruptive system

We appreciated the nondisruptive nature of the maintenance work during business hours.
A nondisruptive approach is often preferred when updating critical software systems.
