motion

noun
formal proposal in meetings
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈməʊʃən / (BrE)
a formal proposal put forward in a meeting or deliberative assembly for discussion and decision by vote

Examples

A motion to vote
A new motion was introduced at the start of the meeting.
She seconded the motion to delay the vote until next week.
The committee passed the motion unanimously after a brief discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amendment - formal change proposed to a motion or document
  • synonym: proposal - suggestion or plan put forward for consideration
