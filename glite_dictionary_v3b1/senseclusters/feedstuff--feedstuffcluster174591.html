feedstuff

noun
animal food material
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːdstʌf / (AmE), / ˈfiːdstʌf / (BrE)
any material used as food for animals, especially livestock

Examples

The quality of the feedstuff can greatly affect the health of the animals.
