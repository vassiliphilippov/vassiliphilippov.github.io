agglutinate

verb
to string together morphemes
8.4 7.7
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈgluː.tɪ.neɪt/ (AmE), /ə.ˈgluː.tɪ.neɪt/ (BrE)
(of a language or its structure) to join or string together morphemes in a sequence so that each adds a distinct meaning.

Examples

To agglutinate words
In Turkish, nouns often agglutinate several suffixes.
Linguists study how these languages agglutinate elements to convey nuances of meaning.
Some languages agglutinate morphemes instead of using word order to express grammatical relations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concatenate - to link things together in a series
