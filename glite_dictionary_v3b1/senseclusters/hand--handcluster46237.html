hand

verb
to guide or assist physically by hand
Pronunciation: / hænd / (AmE), / hænd / (BrE)
to guide or assist (someone) physically, especially by offering a hand to help them move or enter a place

Examples

To hand her down
He gently handed his grandmother down the steps.
Let me hand you through the crowd to your seat.
The attendant handed the guest into the carriage carefully.
