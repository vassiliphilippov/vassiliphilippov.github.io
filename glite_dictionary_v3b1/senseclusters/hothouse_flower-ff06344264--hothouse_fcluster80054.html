hothouse flower

noun
overly delicate or sheltered person
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtˌhaʊs ˈflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɒtˌhaʊs ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
a person who is overly delicate or sheltered, and struggles to cope with harsh or challenging circumstances

Examples

A hothouse flower person
Because he had always been protected by his family, Mark became something of a hothouse flower when he went to college.
Some critics dismissed the writer as a hothouse flower who could not handle real-world adversity.
The military was a harsh environment for a hothouse flower unused to discipline and stress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shrinking violet - a very shy or timid person
  • antonym: tough cookie - a person who is strong, resilient, or able to face difficulties
