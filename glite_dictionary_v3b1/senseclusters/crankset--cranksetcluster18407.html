crankset

noun
bicycle pedal mechanism
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkræŋk.set/ (AmE), /ˈkræŋk.set/ (BrE)
the set of bicycle components that connects the pedals to the chain and gears, allowing the rider to turn the wheels

Examples

Bicycle crankset parts
He replaced the old crankset to improve his bike's performance.
A lightweight crankset can make cycling up hills much easier.
The mechanic inspected the crankset for any signs of wear or damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chainring - the toothed wheel attached to the crankset that drives the chain
  • synonym: pedal - the part of a bicycle pushed by the rider's foot to move the bike
