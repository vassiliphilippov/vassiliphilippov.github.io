content

noun
proportion of a substance in a mixture
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.tɛnt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tɛnt / (BrE)
The amount or proportion of a particular substance that is present in a mixture, material, or object.

Examples

Sugar content
Air quality reports often mention the ozone content in the atmosphere.
The label shows that the fat content of this cheese is 30%.
We measured the alcohol content of the solution in the laboratory.
