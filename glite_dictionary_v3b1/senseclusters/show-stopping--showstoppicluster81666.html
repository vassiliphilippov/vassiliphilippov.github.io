show-stopping

adjective
extremely impressive
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊˌstɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌstɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
extremely impressive or attention-grabbing, especially in a performance, appearance, or event

Examples

Show-stopping performance
The actress received a standing ovation for her show-stopping performance in the musical.
He entered the room in a show-stopping suit that caught everyone's attention.
The chef prepared a show-stopping dessert that wowed all the guests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ordinary - not special or impressive
  • synonym: spectacular - very impressive or dramatic
