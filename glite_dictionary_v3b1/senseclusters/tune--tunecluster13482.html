tune

verb
adjust the pitch of an instrument
5.3
Pronunciation: / tun / (AmE), / tjuːn / (BrE)
to adjust the pitch of a musical instrument so that it produces the correct notes

Examples

To tune a guitar
He called a professional to tune his violin before the concert.
She patiently tuned the piano until every note was in harmony.
You should tune your guitar regularly to keep it sounding good.
