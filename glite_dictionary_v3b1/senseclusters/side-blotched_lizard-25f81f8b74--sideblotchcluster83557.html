side-blotched lizard

noun
species of small lizard native to western US deserts
Pronunciation: / saɪd blɑtʃt ˈlɪzɚd / (AmE), / saɪd blɒtʃt ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
a small, spotted lizard (Uta stansburiana) native to arid regions of the western United States, known for a dark blotch on each side behind the front limbs
