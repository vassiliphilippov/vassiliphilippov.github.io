yuke

noun
large mining truck
Pronunciation: / juːk / (AmE), / juːk / (BrE)
a large truck used for transporting heavy materials, especially in mining or construction

Examples

A yuke truck
The massive yuke rumbled down the dirt road, loaded with rocks from the quarry.
Workers waited as the yuke dumped its heavy load at the construction site.
Each yuke can carry several tons of material in a single trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dump truck - a truck for carrying loose material such as sand or gravel
  • synonym: haul truck - a heavy-duty truck used in mining and construction to transport materials
