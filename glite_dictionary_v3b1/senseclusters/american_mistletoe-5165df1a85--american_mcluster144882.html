American mistletoe

noun
a North American parasitic plant used at Christmas
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈmɪsəlˌtoʊ /; / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈmɪs.əlˌtoʊ / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈmɪsl̩təʊ /; / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈmɪs.əl.təʊ / (BrE)
a parasitic plant native to North America, often used as a Christmas decoration and known for growing on trees and weakening them
