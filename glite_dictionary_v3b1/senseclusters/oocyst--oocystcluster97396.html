oocyst

noun
protozoan cyst stage
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.əˌsɪst /; / ˈoʊəˌsɪst / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.əˌsɪst /; / ˈəʊəsɪst / (BrE)
a thick-walled, resistant structure formed by certain protozoa or parasites, containing developing or infective stages
