rangeman

noun
rangeland manager
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪndʒˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈreɪndʒˌmæn / (BrE)
a person whose job is to manage, protect, and oversee open rangeland, often caring for livestock and land resources

Examples

Rangeman manages cattle
The rangeman checked the cattle and fences every morning.
A skilled rangeman knows how to balance the needs of livestock and the environment.
The new rangeman introduced better grazing practices to protect the land.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cowboy - a person who tends cattle on horseback, especially in the western US
  • synonym: rancher - a person who owns or manages a ranch
