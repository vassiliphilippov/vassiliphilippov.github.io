cardinal

noun
a crested North American songbird
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrdənl / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdɪnl / (BrE)
a North American songbird with a distinctive crest and a thick bill, the male of which has bright red plumage

Examples

A red cardinal
A bright red cardinal perched on the fence, singing cheerfully in the morning sun.
It's easy to spot a male cardinal in winter against the snowy landscape.
Several cardinals visit our backyard bird feeder every day.
