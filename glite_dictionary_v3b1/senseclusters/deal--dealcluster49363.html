deal

noun
plank of softwood (fir or pine)
7.8 0.53/m
Pronunciation: / diÀêl / (AmE), / diÀêl / (BrE)
a plank or board of softwood, especially fir or pine, used in building and carpentry

Examples

A deal board
The builder used a long deal for the new shelves in the workshop.
The carpenter stacked several deals against the wall, ready to be cut to size.
These tables are made from seasoned deal and finished with a protective varnish.
He ordered several sheets of deal to repair the shed roof.
Much of the house was constructed using local deal instead of more expensive hardwood.
The carpenter selected a plank of deal for the window frame.
