uncried

adjective
not shed as tears
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkraɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkraɪd / (BrE)
not expressed by crying or not shed as tears

Examples

Uncried tears
He felt the weight of uncried tears after hearing the sad news.
Her pain remained uncried, hidden behind a calm expression.
There were many uncried emotions she could not put into words.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cried - expressed by crying or shed as tears
  • synonym: unshed - not released or not let out, especially tears
