mineralize

verb
convert to a mineral substance
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnɚəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪnərəlaɪz / (BrE)
to convert or be converted into a mineral substance, especially by the infiltration of minerals

Examples

To mineralize bones
Bones begin to mineralize as minerals are deposited in their structure.
Certain chemicals can mineralize organic matter during the fossilization process.
The company developed a technique to mineralize wastewater, making it cleaner and safer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fossilize - to convert into a fossil
