aureola

noun
halo in art
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈriːələ / (AmE), / ɔːˈriːələ / (BrE)
a halo or radiant circle of light shown around the head or body, especially in religious art

Examples

A glowing aureola
The saint in the painting was depicted with a glowing aureola above her head.
Artists often use an aureola to show holiness or divinity in religious figures.
A golden aureola surrounded the angel, making it stand out in the artwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: halo - a circle of light appearing to surround a person, often in art
  • synonym: nimbus - a luminous cloud or a halo surrounding a supernatural being
