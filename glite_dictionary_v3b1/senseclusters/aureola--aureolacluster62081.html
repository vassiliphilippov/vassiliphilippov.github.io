aureola

noun
halo in art
9.4
Pronunciation: /ɔːr.iː.ˈoʊ.lə/ /; / /ɑːr.iː.ˈoʊ.lə/ (AmE), /ɔː.riː.ˈəʊ.lə/ (BrE)

Examples
