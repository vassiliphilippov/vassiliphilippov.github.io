station

noun
broadcasting frequency allocation
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
the frequency assigned to a radio or television broadcasting station on which it transmits its signal

Examples

Radio station frequency
Each station operates on its own unique frequency.
