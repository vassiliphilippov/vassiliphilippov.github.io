crab-eating opossum

noun
South American opossum species
Pronunciation: / ˈkræb ˌitɪŋ əˈpɑsəm / (AmE), / ˈkræb ˌiːtɪŋ əˈpɒsəm / (BrE)
a South American opossum (Philander opossum) found in forests and wetlands, known for its varied diet that includes crustaceans

Examples

The crab-eating opossum animal
Researchers observed a crab-eating opossum foraging for crabs along the riverbank.
The crab-eating opossum is commonly found near streams and wetlands in South America.
Unlike other opossums, the crab-eating opossum often incorporates aquatic animals into its diet.
