guide

noun
model or standard for comparison
Pronunciation: / ɡaɪd / (AmE), / ɡaɪd / (BrE)
something that serves as a model or standard for making comparisons or judgments.

Examples

A guide for success
His achievements serve as a guide to what is possible in this field.
The company sets a strict guide for evaluating all new products.
The professor used last year’s project as a guide for students to follow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benchmark - a standard or point of reference for comparison
  • synonym: standard - a level of quality or attainment used as a measure or model
