preconstruction

adjective
before building work
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːkənˈstrʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːkənˈstrʌkʃən / (BrE)
occurring or existing before construction begins, especially referring to meetings, services, or activities carried out prior to building work.

Examples

Preconstruction meeting
A preconstruction survey was conducted to document the condition of nearby buildings.
The team held a preconstruction meeting to discuss safety protocols and project timelines.
Detailed planning during the preconstruction phase can help prevent costly mistakes later.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postconstruction - occurring after construction is completed
  • synonym: preliminary - happening before the main event or activity
