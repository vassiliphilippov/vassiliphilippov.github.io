incandescently

adverb
brightly or passionately
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkænˈdɛsəntli / (AmE), / ˌɪnkænˈdɛsəntli / (BrE)
in a way that shines brightly or shows intense emotion or passion

Examples

To shine incandescently
The stage was incandescently lit, making the performance even more dramatic.
He argued incandescently for the rights of the workers during the meeting.
Her eyes shone incandescently with excitement as she described her new project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brilliantly - in a very bright or impressive way
  • antonym: dully - in a way that is not bright or lively
  • synonym: passionately - in a way that shows strong emotion
