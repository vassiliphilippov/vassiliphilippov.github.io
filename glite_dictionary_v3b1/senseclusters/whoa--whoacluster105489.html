whoa

verb
to command to stop
8.3
Pronunciation: / woʊ / (AmE), / wəʊ / (BrE)
to command a horse or animal to stop, or to cause someone or something to stop suddenly

Examples

To whoa a horse
The trainer shouted whoa to get the horse to stop at the gate.
She quickly said whoa when her dog started running toward the road.
He pulled the reins and yelled whoa as the carriage approached the intersection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: halt - to stop or cause to stop
