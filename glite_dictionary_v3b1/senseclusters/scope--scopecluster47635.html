scope

noun
electronic instrument for visualizing electrical signals
3.8 7.9
Pronunciation: / skoʊp / (AmE), / skəʊp / (BrE)
an electronic instrument that displays visual representations of electrical signals, typically for measurement or analysis

Examples

Digital scope display
Modern laboratories often have at least one digital scope for troubleshooting electronic equipment.

The technician used a scope to check the signal output from the circuit.
