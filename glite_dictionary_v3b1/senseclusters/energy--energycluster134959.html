energy

noun
lively quality in writing or art
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnɚdʒi / (AmE), / ˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
enthusiasm, drive, or liveliness, especially in people or creative work

Examples

Critics praised the energy of his prose, noting its imaginative flair.
Her novel is full of energy and keeps the reader engaged from start to finish.

Her energy and enthusiasm inspired the whole team to work harder.
The job requires someone with a lot of energy and initiative.
