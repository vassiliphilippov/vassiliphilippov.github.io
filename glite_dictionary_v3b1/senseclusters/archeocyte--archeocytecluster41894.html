archeocyte

noun
sponge stem cell
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrkioʊˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɑːkɪəˌsaɪt / (BrE)
an undifferentiated cell in sponges that can develop into other cell types and is important for regeneration

Examples

The archeocyte cell
The archeocyte is essential for the sponge's ability to regenerate lost parts.
Scientists study archeocytes to understand how sponges repair themselves after injury.
Without archeocytes, sponges would not be able to replace damaged or aging cells.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stem cell - an undifferentiated cell that can develop into specialized cells
