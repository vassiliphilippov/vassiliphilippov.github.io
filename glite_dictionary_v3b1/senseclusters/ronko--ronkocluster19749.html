ronko

noun
woven carrying basket
9.9 0.00/m
a coarse woven basket or container used to carry fish, produce, or other goods

Examples

Woven ronko basket
She carried the day's catch home in a woven ronko from the market.
Farmers often use a ronko to transport fresh vegetables to the village.
The fisherman loaded his ronko with fish before heading back to shore.
