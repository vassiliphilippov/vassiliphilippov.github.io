Aggeus

proper noun
biblical minor prophet, also called Haggai
Pronunciation: / əgˈdʒiː.əs / (AmE), / agˈdʒiː.əs / (BrE)
A Hebrew minor prophet, considered the first of three post-exile prophets after the Neo-Babylonian Exile of Judah; more commonly known as Haggai.

Examples

Prophet Aggeus
Few people recognize Aggeus by this name; he is often called Haggai.
Scholars discuss how Aggeus encouraged the rebuilding of the Temple in Jerusalem.
The book of Aggeus is found among the Minor Prophets in some versions of the Bible.
