obliquely

adverb
at an oblique angle
4.6
Pronunciation: / oʊˈbliːkli /; / əˈbliːkli / (AmE), / əˈbliːkli /; / ɒˈbliːkli / (BrE)
in a slanting, sideways, or indirect way; not straight or direct

Examples

Look obliquely sideways
She glanced obliquely at her supervisor, unsure how to respond.
The building's shadow stretched obliquely over the lawn as the sun set.
The late afternoon light fell obliquely across the dining room table.
She glanced obliquely at her colleague, unsure how to respond.
The road crossed the river obliquely, not at a right angle.
The sun shone obliquely through the window, casting long shadows across the floor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: perpendicularly - at a right angle; straight up or down
