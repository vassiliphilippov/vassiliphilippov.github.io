dictate

noun
guiding principle or command
3.5 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈdɪk.teɪt/ (AmE), /ˈdɪk.teɪt/ (BrE)

Examples

Dictates of reason

The company followed the dictates of the new policy without question.
