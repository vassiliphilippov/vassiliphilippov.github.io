deck

noun
outdoor platform attached to a building
8.2 1.2
Pronunciation: /dek/ (AmE), /dek/ (BrE)
a flat surface or platform, such as a floor on a ship or a wooden platform attached to a house for outdoor living

Examples

Wooden deck outside
He built a new wooden deck outside the living room to enjoy the morning sun.
The house features a spacious deck ideal for summer barbecues and gatherings.
We spent the evening relaxing on the deck overlooking the backyard.
Heavy rain made the main deck slippery during the storm.
Passengers enjoyed the fresh air on the upper deck of the ferry.
The crew gathered on the deck to watch the sunrise over the ocean.
