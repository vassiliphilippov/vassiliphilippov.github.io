Ichneumonidae

noun
family of parasitic wasps (ichneumon flies)
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌnuːˈmɒnɪˌdiː /; / ɪkˌnuːməˈnɪdiː / (AmE), / ɪkˌnjuːˈmɒnɪˌdiː /; / ɪkˌnjuːməˈnɪdiː / (BrE)
