unintellectual

adjective
not interested in ideas
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˌtɛlɪkˈtʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˌtɛlɪkˈtjuːəl / (BrE)
not interested in, showing little ability in, or not respecting intellectual activities, ideas, or learning

Examples

The conversation was pleasant but rather unintellectual and lighthearted.

Her unintellectual approach made it difficult for her to engage in academic conversations.
