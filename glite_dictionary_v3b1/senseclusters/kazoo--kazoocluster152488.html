kazoo

noun
small buzzing wind instrument
Pronunciation: / kəˈzu / (AmE), / kəˈzuː / (BrE)
A small musical instrument, usually made of metal or plastic, that produces a buzzing sound when the player hums into it.

Examples

A kazoo instrument
Children laughed as they made music with their brightly colored kazoos.
He pulled a kazoo from his pocket and started humming a cheerful tune.
The band included a playful kazoo solo in their final song.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmonica - a small rectangular wind instrument played by blowing into holes
