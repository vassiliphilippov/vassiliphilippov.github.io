thrill

verb
tremble with excitement or fear
6.0 2.2
Pronunciation: /θrɪl/ (AmE), /θrɪl/ (BrE)

Examples

To thrill with fear

He would thrill at the sound of thunder rumbling through the mountains.
The audience seemed to thrill at every twist in the suspenseful story.
