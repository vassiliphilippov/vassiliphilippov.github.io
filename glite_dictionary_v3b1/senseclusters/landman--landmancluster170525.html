landman

noun
countryman or farmer
Pronunciation: / ˈlændmən / (AmE), / ˈlændmən / (BrE)
a person who lives on or works the land, especially a farmer or countryman (chiefly historical or dialectal)

Examples

Old landman
The old landman shared stories of harvests and village life with the children.
Unlike the sailors, he was a true landman who knew every field and harvest season.
In the past, a landman would rarely travel far from his home village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: countryman - a person who lives in the countryside
  • synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
  • antonym: sailor - a person who works on a ship at sea
