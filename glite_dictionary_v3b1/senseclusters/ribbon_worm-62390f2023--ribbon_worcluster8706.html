ribbon worm

noun
soft unsegmented marine worm
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪbən wɜrm / (AmE), / ˈrɪbən wɜːm / (BrE)
a soft, unsegmented marine worm that has a long, threadlike proboscis and can stretch and contract its body

Examples

A ribbon worm species
Marine biologists discovered a new type of ribbon worm off the coast.
Some species of ribbon worm are among the longest animals in the world.
The ribbon worm can extend its proboscis to catch prey.
