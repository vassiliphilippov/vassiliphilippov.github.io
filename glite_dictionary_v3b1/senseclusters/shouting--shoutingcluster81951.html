shouting

noun
writing in all caps online
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
Writing or typing text in all capital letters in digital communication, used to convey emphasis, anger, or strong excitement.

Examples

Shouting in all caps
Some online posts use shouting in all caps to express strong emotion.
She complained that the company's emails felt like shouting because of the frequent ALL CAPS subject lines.
To avoid shouting online, editors recommend using italics or bold instead of typing everything in capitals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ALL CAPS - text written entirely in capital letters, often perceived as shouting in online communication
