Pholis

noun
genus of marine gunnel fishes
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊlɪs / (AmE), / ˈfəʊlɪs / (BrE)
A genus of elongated, marine fishes known as gunnels, which are the type genus of the family Pholidae.
