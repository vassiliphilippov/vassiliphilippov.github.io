packing box

noun
chamber for sealing around a shaft or piston
Pronunciation: / ˈpækɪŋ bɑks / (AmE), / ˈpækɪŋ bɒks / (BrE)
a small chamber in which packing material is compressed around a moving shaft or piston to create a seal and prevent leakage

Examples

Packing box in a pump
Routine maintenance of the packing box extends the lifespan of the pump.
The engineer inspected the packing box for signs of leakage before starting the machine.
You must replace the worn packing in the packing box to maintain an effective seal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stuffing box - a housing containing packing to seal a shaft from leakage
