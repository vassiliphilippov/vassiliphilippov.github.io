grille

noun
metal framework in front of a car radiator
5.3 4.3
Pronunciation: /grɪl/ (AmE), /grɪl/ (BrE)
a framework of metal bars or mesh placed at the front of a vehicle to allow air into the radiator while protecting it from debris
