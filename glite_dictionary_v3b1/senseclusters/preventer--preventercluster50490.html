preventer

noun
something that stops events
3.8
Pronunciation: / prɪˈvɛntər / (AmE), / prɪˈvɛntə / (BrE)
a person or thing that stops something from happening or reduces the risk of it occurring

Examples

A preventer of accidents
A smoke alarm is an important preventer of fire accidents in the home.
Vaccines act as a preventer of many serious diseases.
Wearing a helmet is a simple preventer of head injuries while cycling.
