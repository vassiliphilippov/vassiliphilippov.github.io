tonk

verb
to knock lightly
Pronunciation: / tɑŋk / (AmE), / tɒŋk / (BrE)
to hit or knock something lightly, often producing a sound

Examples

To tonk the door
He tonked the door shut before leaving the room.
She tonked the glass with her spoon to get everyone's attention.
The child tonked the toy car against the table, making a soft sound.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bang - to hit something loudly or forcefully
  • synonym: tap - to hit something lightly and quickly
