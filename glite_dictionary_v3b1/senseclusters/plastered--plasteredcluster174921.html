plastered

adjective
hair smoothed down with substance
4.7 4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈplæstɚd / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstəd / (BrE)
(of hair) made smooth and flat by applying a sticky or glossy substance, such as pomade or gel.

Examples

Plastered hair look
After walking in the rain, her hair became plastered to her forehead.
He preferred the classic look, with every strand plastered into place.
His dark hair was plastered back with gel for the party.
