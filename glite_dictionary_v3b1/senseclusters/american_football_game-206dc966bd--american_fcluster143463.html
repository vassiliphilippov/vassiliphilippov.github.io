American football game

noun
team sport played with an oval ball
1.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈme.rɪ.kən ˈfʊt.bɔːl geɪm/ (AmE), /ə.ˈme.rɪ.kən ˈfʊt.bɔːl geɪm/ (BrE)
A team sport played on a rectangular field between two teams of 11 players, where the objective is to advance the ball into the opposing team's end zone by running or passing.

Examples

An exciting American football game
He played his first American football game when he was only ten years old.
The stadium was packed for the championship American football game last night.
Watching an American football game on Thanksgiving has become a family tradition.

Related Terms
