headrace

noun
water channel feeding a mill or turbine
9.1 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌreɪs / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌreɪs / (BrE)
a channel or waterway that directs water to a mill, water wheel, or turbine for power generation

Examples

The headrace channel
Debris blocking the headrace reduced the efficiency of the hydroelectric turbine.
Engineers inspected the headrace to ensure a steady water supply for the plant.
The old mill was powered by water from the headrace that ran alongside the building.
