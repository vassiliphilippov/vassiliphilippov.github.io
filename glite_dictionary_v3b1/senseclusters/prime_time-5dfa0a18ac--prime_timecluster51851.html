prime time

noun
peak TV viewing hours in the evening
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪm taɪm / (AmE), / ˈpraɪm taɪm / (BrE)
the time in the evening when the most people are watching television and advertising is most expensive

Examples

The show airs during prime time when most viewers are at home.
