kopje

noun
small isolated hill in southern Africa
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊpi, ˈkɒpi /; / ˈkɑːpi / (AmE), / ˈkɒpi, ˈkəʊpi /; / ˈkɒpi / (BrE)
A small, isolated hill rising abruptly from the surrounding flat land, especially in southern Africa.

Examples

A kopje hill
A lone kopje stood in the middle of the wide, grassy plain.
From the top of the kopje, we could see herds of wildebeest stretching to the horizon.
Leopards are often found resting on a kopje to keep watch over the savanna.
A lone koppie stood out against the flat expanse of the veld.
The expedition camped near a koppie for shelter from the wind.
We climbed to the top of the koppie to watch the sunset over the savannah.
