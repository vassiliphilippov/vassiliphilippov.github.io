downness

noun
feeling low
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnnəs / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnnəs / (BrE)
a state or feeling of being sad, low, or depressed

Examples

A feeling of downness
He felt a wave of downness after hearing the disappointing news.
During the dark winter days, her sense of downness seemed to grow.
Talking to friends helped him overcome his downness and feel better.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depression - a prolonged feeling of deep sadness
  • antonym: happiness - the state of being happy or joyful
  • synonym: sadness - the state of being unhappy or sorrowful
