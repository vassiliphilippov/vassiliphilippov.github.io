hold my beer

x
humorous phrase before a bold or risky act
Pronunciation: / hoʊld maɪ bɪr / (AmE), / həʊld maɪ bɪə / (BrE)
used humorously to suggest that one is about to attempt or demonstrate something daring, foolish, or impressive, often after someone's previous achievement or challenge.

Examples

Hold my beer phrase
'You call that a challenge?' she laughed, 'hold my beer.'
Before trying the difficult skateboard trick, he shouted, 'hold my beer!'
Whenever someone claims a task is impossible, Dave is the first to say, 'hold my beer.'
