shipper

noun
fan supporting a fictional couple
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈʃɪp.ər/ (AmE), /ˈʃɪp.ə/ (BrE)
informal (in fandom) a fan who supports, promotes, or roots for a romantic relationship between two (often fictional) characters.

Examples

A shipper in fandom
She proudly announced on Twitter that she was a devoted shipper of the new show's main couple.
Even though he criticized the plot, he remained a secret shipper of the childhood friends' romance.
The fan forum filled with art and theories created by enthusiastic shippers supporting different pairings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ship (v.) - to support or wish for a romantic relationship between two characters or people (short for 'relationship').
