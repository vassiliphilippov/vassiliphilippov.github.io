ave

noun
abbreviation for avenue
3.4
Pronunciation: / æv / (AmE), / æv / (BrE)
an abbreviation for avenue, used to refer to a broad street or thoroughfare, especially in addresses.

Examples

Main ave
The new restaurant is located at 500 Main ave, just across from the library.
Please send the package to 123 Oak ave by Friday.
Their office moved from Pine Street to Maple ave last month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: avenue - a wide street or main thoroughfare, especially in a city
  • synonym: street - a public road in a city or town
