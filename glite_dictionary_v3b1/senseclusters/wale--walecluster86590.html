wale

noun
raised mark on the skin
Pronunciation: / weɪl / (AmE), / weɪl / (BrE)
a raised mark or ridge on the skin, especially one caused by a blow or allergic reaction

Examples

Red wale on skin
After the allergic reaction, large red wales appeared on her back.
The child's arm had a wale where the branch had struck him.
The old photograph showed a wale left by the lash.

Related Terms

  • synonym: weal - a raised red mark on the skin
  • synonym: welt - a red, swollen mark on the skin
