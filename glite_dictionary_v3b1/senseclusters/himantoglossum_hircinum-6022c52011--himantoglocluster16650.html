Himantoglossum hircinum

noun
lizard orchid species
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɪˌmæntəˈɡlɑsəm hɜrˈsaɪnəm / (AmE), / hɪˌmantəˈɡlɒsəm hɜːˈsɪnəm / (BrE)
A species of orchid commonly known as the lizard orchid, native to Europe and North Africa, recognized for its long, twisted labellum and strong scent.
