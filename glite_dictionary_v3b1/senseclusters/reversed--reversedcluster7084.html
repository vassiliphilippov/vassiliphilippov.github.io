reversed

adjective
turned inside out and resewn
4.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɜrst / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːst / (BrE)
(of fabric or clothing) turned inside out and resewn, especially so that the worn parts are concealed and the item appears new or improved.

Examples

Reversed jacket
After years of wear, she had the jacket reversed by a skilled seamstress.
The reversed collar looked as good as new.
The tailor suggested a reversed cuff to give the shirt a fresh look.
