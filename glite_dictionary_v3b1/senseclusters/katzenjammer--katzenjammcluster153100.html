katzenjammer

noun
loud, confused noise; uproar
Pronunciation: / ˈkætsənˌdʒæmər / (AmE), / ˈkatsənˌdʒamə / (BrE)
loud, confused noise; uproar or clamor from many sources.

Examples

Street katzenjammer
After the parade, the city square was filled with a katzenjammer that lasted for hours.
As she entered the busy market, she was overwhelmed by the katzenjammer of bargaining voices and clattering carts.
The classroom erupted into a katzenjammer when the substitute teacher arrived late.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clamor - a loud, sustained noise
  • synonym: din - a loud, unpleasant, confused noise
  • synonym: uproar - a state of noisy confusion
