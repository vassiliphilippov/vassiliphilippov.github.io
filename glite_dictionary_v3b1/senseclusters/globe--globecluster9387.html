globe

verb
to make round
Pronunciation: / ɡloʊb / (AmE), / ɡləʊb / (BrE)
to form something into a round shape or to cover it with a globe-like object

Examples

To globe the clay
The artist globed the clay into a perfect sphere for her sculpture.
Tiny lights were globed in frosted glass, creating a magical effect in the garden.
Raindrops globed on the surface of the leaf, reflecting the morning sunlight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flatten - to make something flat or less round
  • synonym: round - to make or become round in shape
