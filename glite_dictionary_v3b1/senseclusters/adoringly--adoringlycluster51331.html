adoringly

adverb
with deep love or admiration
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈdɔrɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈdɔːrɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows deep love or admiration.

Examples

To adoringly look
Fans cheered adoringly for their favorite singer at the concert.
He always speaks adoringly about his childhood dog.
She gazed adoringly at her newborn daughter as she slept.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contemptuously - in a contemptuous manner, showing disdain
  • synonym: lovingly - in a loving manner
