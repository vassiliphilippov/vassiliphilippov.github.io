Passion

noun
the suffering of Jesus before and during the Crucifixion
Pronunciation: / ˈpæʃən / (AmE), / ˈpæʃən / (BrE)
The sufferings of Jesus Christ from the Last Supper to his death on the cross.

Examples

The Passion of Christ
He composed a choral work based on the events of the Passion.
Many churches reenact the Passion each year during Holy Week.
The gospel recounts the details of Christ's Passion and crucifixion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crucifixion - the execution of Jesus by being nailed or bound to a cross
