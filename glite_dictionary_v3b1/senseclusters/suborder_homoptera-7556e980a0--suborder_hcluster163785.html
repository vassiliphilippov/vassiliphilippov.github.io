suborder Homoptera

noun
group of plant-sucking insects
9.9
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈmɑptərə / (AmE), / hɒˈmɒptərə / (BrE)
A former division of insects that includes aphids, cicadas, leafhoppers, and similar plant-sucking species.

Examples

Suborder Homoptera insects
Many agricultural scientists study the suborder Homoptera because its members can damage crops.
Taxonomists have debated the classification of insects within the suborder Homoptera for decades.
The suborder Homoptera includes familiar pests such as aphids and whiteflies.
