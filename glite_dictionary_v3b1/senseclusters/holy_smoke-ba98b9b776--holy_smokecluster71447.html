holy smoke

interjection
exclamation of surprise or astonishment
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊli ˈsmoʊk / (AmE), / ˌhəʊli ˈsməʊk / (BrE)
used to express surprise, astonishment, or admiration, often in response to something unexpected or impressive

Examples

Holy smoke!
Holy smoke, that startup just raised a million dollars in its first week!
Holy smoke, we're almost out of gas already!
I opened the door and, holy smoke, the room was filled with balloons!
