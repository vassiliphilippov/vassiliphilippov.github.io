Gulf of Tehuantepec

proper noun
Pacific Ocean gulf in southern Mexico
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɡʌlf əv təˌwɑːn.təˈpɛk / (AmE), / ɡʌlf əv tɛˌwæn.tɪˈpɛk / (BrE)
an arm of the Pacific Ocean along the southern coast of Mexico, noted for strong wind currents and significant shipping transit

Examples

The Gulf of Tehuantepec coast
A major shipping route passes through the Gulf of Tehuantepec due to its strategic location.
Many migratory birds cross the Gulf of Tehuantepec on their way to Central America.
The Gulf of Tehuantepec is known for its frequent and strong gales, called Tehuantepecers.
