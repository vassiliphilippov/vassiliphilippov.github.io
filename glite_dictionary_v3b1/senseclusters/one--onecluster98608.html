one

adverb
informal intensifier (emphatic)
1.6
Pronunciation: / wʌn / (AmE), / wʌn / (BrE)
(informal) Used as an intensifier before adjectives or nouns to emphasize their degree or quality.

Examples

One big mistake
It was one crazy idea, but it actually worked.
That was one incredible performance last night!
