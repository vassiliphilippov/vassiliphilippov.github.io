unglued

adjective
very upset
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡluːd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡluːd / (BrE)
extremely upset or unable to remain calm, especially in a stressful situation

Examples

Go unglued under stress
He became completely unglued when he realized he had lost his passport at the airport.
After hearing the bad news, she went unglued and started to cry uncontrollably.
The team came unglued under pressure and lost the game in the final minutes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or anger
  • synonym: distressed - feeling very upset or anxious
