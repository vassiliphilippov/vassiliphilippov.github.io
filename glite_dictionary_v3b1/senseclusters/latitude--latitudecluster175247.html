latitude

noun
imaginary line parallel to the equator
5.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈlæt.ə.tuːd/ /; / /ˈlæt.ɪ.tuːd/ (AmE), /ˈlæt.ɪ.tjuːd/ /; / /ˈlæt.ə.tjuːd/ (BrE)
the distance north or south of the equator, measured in degrees, or an imaginary line around the Earth parallel to the equator
