Examples

Synonyms

  • line of latitude
    3.4 0.03/m
    line of latitude is an imaginary circle around Earth that marks north or south position, while latitude measures that position in degrees.
  • parallel of latitude
    5.1 0.02/m
    parallel of latitude is an imaginary circle parallel to the equator that marks north or south, while latitude measures that angle in degrees.
  • parallel
    5.1 1.14/m
    parallel is an imaginary line on maps that runs east to west and marks position, while latitude is the numeric angle showing north or south.
  • lat
    8.4 0.09/m
    lat is an abbreviation used on maps and charts for latitude, while latitude is the full word meaning distance north or south in degrees.
