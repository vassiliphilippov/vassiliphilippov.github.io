faultfinder

noun
someone who habitually criticizes others
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔltˌfaɪndər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːltˌfaɪndə / (BrE)
a person who regularly criticizes or finds fault with others, especially their motives or actions

Examples

A faultfinder criticizes others
Despite her accomplishments, there was always a faultfinder ready to downplay her achievements.
Don't let a faultfinder discourage you from pursuing your goals.
He quickly gained a reputation as a faultfinder in the office due to his constant complaints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or finds fault
  • antonym: praiser - a person who expresses approval or admiration
