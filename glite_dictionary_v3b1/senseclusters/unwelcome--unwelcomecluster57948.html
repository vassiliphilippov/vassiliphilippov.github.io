unwelcome

adjective
not wanted or not welcome
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɛlkəm / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛlkəm / (BrE)
not wanted or received with pleasure; causing displeasure

Examples

Unwelcome attention
His comments brought unwelcome attention to the sensitive issue.

The news of the delay was completely unwelcome to the project team.
Rising energy prices bring an unwelcome burden to many families.
She found the criticism from her colleague to be particularly unwelcome.
The sudden storm was an unwelcome surprise for beachgoers.
