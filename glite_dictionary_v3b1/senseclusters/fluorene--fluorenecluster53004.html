fluorene

noun
hydrocarbon for dyes
Pronunciation: / ˈflʊəˌriːn / (AmE), / ˈflʊəriːn / (BrE)
a colorless crystalline hydrocarbon used in making dyes, plastics, and other chemicals

Examples
