blast

verb
send a strong stream of air at
4.0
Pronunciation: / blæst / (AmE), / blɑːst / (BrE)
to direct a strong stream of air or wind at someone or something

Examples

To blast hair dry
She blasted her hair with the dryer to style it quickly before leaving.
The air conditioning was blasting cold air at us all afternoon.
The bus driver blasts warm air through the vents on chilly mornings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blow - to send air out or move air toward something
  • synonym: gust - to blow strongly and suddenly, often used of wind
