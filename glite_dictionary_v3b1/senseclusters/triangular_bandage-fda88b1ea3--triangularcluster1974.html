triangular bandage

noun
wide triangle-shaped bandage used to support an arm
Pronunciation: / traɪˈæŋɡjələr ˈbændɪdʒ / (AmE), / traɪˈaŋɡjʊlə ˈbændɪdʒ / (BrE)
a wide triangular piece of cloth used as a bandage, typically to support an injured forearm by hanging it from around the neck

Examples

A triangular bandage for arm
After her fall, the nurse placed her arm in a triangular bandage to keep it stable.
He demonstrated how to tie a triangular bandage to support a broken arm.
The first aid kit contained a triangular bandage for emergencies.
