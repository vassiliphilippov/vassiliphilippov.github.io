natural order

noun
the universe as governed by natural laws
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃ(ə)rəl ˈɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
the physical universe regarded as an orderly system governed by natural laws, as opposed to human or supernatural influence

Examples

The natural order of universe
Many philosophers have debated how much of human behavior is dictated by the natural order of the universe.
Science seeks to understand the rules and patterns that govern the natural order.
Some people believe everything has its place within the natural order of things.
