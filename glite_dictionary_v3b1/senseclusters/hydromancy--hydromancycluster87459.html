hydromancy

noun
divination using water
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdrəˌmænsi / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪdrə(ʊ)ˌmansi / (BrE)
divination by means of water, especially by interpreting patterns observed in water or its movements.

Examples

Hydromancy by water
Ancient priests often practiced hydromancy to predict the outcome of wars.
Some believed that hydromancy could reveal messages from the gods through the ripple patterns.
The ritual of hydromancy required complete silence as water was poured into a silver bowl.
