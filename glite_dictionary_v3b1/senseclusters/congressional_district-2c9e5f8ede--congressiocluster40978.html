congressional district

noun
U.S. voting area for House of Representatives
9.9 3.3
Pronunciation: / kənˈɡrɛʃənəl ˈdɪstrɪkt / (AmE), / kənˈɡrɛʃənəl ˈdɪstrɪkt / (BrE)
