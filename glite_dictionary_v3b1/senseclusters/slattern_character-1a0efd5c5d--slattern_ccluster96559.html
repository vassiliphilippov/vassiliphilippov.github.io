slattern character

noun
a promiscuous/slovenly female character
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈslæt̬ɚn ˈkɛrɪktɚ / (AmE), / ˈslætən ˈkærɪktə / (BrE)
A literary or dramatic character who is depicted as a promiscuous or slovenly woman.
