Battle of Agincourt

proper noun
decisive 1415 English victory over French army
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbætl əv ˈædʒɪnˌkɔrt / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˈædʒɪnˌkɔːt / (BrE)
a battle fought in northern France in 1415, where English forces under Henry V decisively defeated a larger French army during the Hundred Years' War
