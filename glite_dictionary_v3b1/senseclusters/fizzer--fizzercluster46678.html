fizzer

noun
hissing firework
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzər / (AmE), / ˈfɪzə / (BrE)
a small firework that produces a hissing or fizzing sound as it burns

Examples

A small fizzer firework
The children cheered as the fizzer sent out a stream of sparks and a loud hiss.
At the festival, each guest received a small fizzer to light and enjoy safely.
He bought a box of fizzers for the backyard celebration on New Year's Eve.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firecracker - a small explosive firework
  • synonym: sparkler - a handheld firework that emits sparks
