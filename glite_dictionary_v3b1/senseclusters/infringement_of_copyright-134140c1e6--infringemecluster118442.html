infringement of copyright

noun
violation of copyright law
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfrɪndʒmənt əv ˈkɑːpiraɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈfrɪndʒmənt əv ˈkɒpiraɪt / (BrE)
the act of violating the exclusive rights granted to a copyright holder, such as reproducing or distributing copyrighted material without permission
