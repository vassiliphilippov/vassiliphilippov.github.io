ferally

adverb
wildly or savagely
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrəli / (AmE), / ˈfɛrəli / (BrE)
in a wild, untamed, or savage manner

Examples

To bark ferally
The stray dog barked ferally at anyone who came near its food.
She glared ferally at the intruder, ready to defend her territory.
The children ran ferally through the fields, laughing and shouting with excitement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tame - not wild or savage; domesticated
  • synonym: wildly - in a wild or uncontrolled way
