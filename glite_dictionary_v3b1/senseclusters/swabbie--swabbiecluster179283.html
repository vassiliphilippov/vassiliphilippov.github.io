swabbie

noun
informal insult
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑbi / (AmE), / ˈswɒbi / (BrE)
an inexperienced or unimportant person, often used as an informal insult for someone seen as foolish or low-ranking

Examples

A swabbie at work
He was treated like a swabbie on his first day at the company.
Stop acting like a swabbie and take your responsibilities seriously.
They called him a swabbie after he made a silly mistake during the meeting.
