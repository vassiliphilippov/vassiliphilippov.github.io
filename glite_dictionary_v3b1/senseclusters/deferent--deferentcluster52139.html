Examples

A deferent manner

Synonyms

  • deferential
    6.6 1.11/m
    deferential shows polite submission to someone because of their rank or age, while deferent means showing respect or yielding.
  • obeisant
    9.1 0.02/m
    obeisant bows to authority and shows eager obedience, while deferent simply shows respect or yields to others' wishes.
  • respectful
    1.1 9.49/m
    respectful is polite and shows regard in normal situations, while deferent often means yielding or acting submissively.
  • regardful
    7.7 0.01/m
    regardful pays attention to others' feelings and needs, while deferent focuses more on yielding or showing deference.
