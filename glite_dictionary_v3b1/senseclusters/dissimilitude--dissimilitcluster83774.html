dissimilitude

noun
lack of similarity; dissimilarity

Pronunciation: /dɪ.sɪ.ˈmɪl.ɪ.tuːd/ /; / /dɪ.sə.ˈmɪl.ɪ.tuːd/ (AmE), /dɪ.sɪ.ˈmɪl.ɪ.tjuːd/ /; / /dɪ.sə.ˈmɪl.ɪ.tjuːd/ (BrE)
