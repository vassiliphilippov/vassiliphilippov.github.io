Ugric

noun
Finno-Ugric language branch
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːɡrɪk / (AmE), / ˈjuːɡrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or denoting a branch of the Finno-Ugric language family, including Hungarian and certain languages spoken in northwestern Siberia

Examples

Ugric language family
Hungarian is the most widely spoken Ugric language.
Linguists study the common features of Ugric languages and their relationships to other Finno-Ugric tongues.
Some Ugric languages are endangered due to declining numbers of speakers in Siberia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Finno-Ugric - relating to the language family containing Finnish, Hungarian, and others
