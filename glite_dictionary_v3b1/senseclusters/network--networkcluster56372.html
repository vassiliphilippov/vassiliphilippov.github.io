network

noun
system of interconnected electronic circuits
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛtˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌwɜːk / (BrE)
(electronics) A system of interconnected electronic components or circuits that function together.

Examples

An electronic network
Audio signals pass through a complex network of amplifiers and filters on the board.
The engineer designed a network of sensors to monitor temperature throughout the building.
This radio uses a network of resistors and capacitors to control frequency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circuit - a closed path or loop through which an electric current flows
