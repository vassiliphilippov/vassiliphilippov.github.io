arabica

adjective
of Coffea arabica
4.3
Pronunciation: / əˈræbɪkə / (AmE), / əˈræbɪkə / (BrE)
relating to or made from the coffee species Coffea arabica, especially describing coffee or beans from that species

Examples

Arabica coffee blend
The café only serves arabica coffee because of its smooth and mild flavor.
Many people prefer arabica beans for their lower caffeine content and delicate taste.
She bought a bag of freshly roasted arabica beans from a local market.

Related Terms

  • antonym: robusta - relating to the coffee species Coffea canephora, known for stronger flavor and higher caffeine
