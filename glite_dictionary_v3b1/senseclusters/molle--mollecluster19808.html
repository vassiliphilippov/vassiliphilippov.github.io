molle

noun
Peruvian evergreen tree with pink fruits
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.leɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɒl.i / (BrE)
A small evergreen tree native to Peru, with a broad, rounded crown, slender drooping branches, clusters of greenish flowers, and rose-pink fruits; also known as the Peruvian pepper tree.

Examples

The molle tree
Clusters of pink fruits from the molle can be used as a spice similar to pepper.
Historic plazas in Peru are often shaded by ancient molle trees.
The molle is commonly planted as an ornamental tree in warm climates around the world.
