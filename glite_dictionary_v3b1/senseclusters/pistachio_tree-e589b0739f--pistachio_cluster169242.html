pistachio tree

noun
tree that yields pistachio nuts
Pronunciation: / pɪˈstæʃioʊ tri / (AmE), / pɪˈstæʃiəʊ triː / (BrE)
a small tree native to southern Europe and Asia Minor that produces hard-shelled pistachio nuts

Examples

The pistachio tree nut
Farmers in Iran cultivate the pistachio tree for its valuable crop of nuts.
The pistachio tree thrives in dry, sunny climates and can live for hundreds of years.
We planted a young pistachio tree in our backyard last spring.
