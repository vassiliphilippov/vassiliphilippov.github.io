acrylic

noun
synthetic fabric made from polyacrylonitrile
5.3 3.3
Pronunciation: / əˈkrɪlɪk / (AmE), / əˈkrɪlɪk / (BrE)
a synthetic fiber or fabric used in clothing and textiles, often as a lightweight, warm alternative to wool
