acrylic

noun
synthetic fabric made from polyacrylonitrile
Pronunciation: / əˈkrɪlɪk / (AmE), / əˈkrɪlɪk / (BrE)
a synthetic fiber or fabric used in clothing and textiles, often as a lightweight, warm alternative to wool

Examples

Acrylic sweater
Many artists prefer wool over acrylic for winter wear, but the latter is more affordable.
The blanket's acrylic fibers make it lightweight and warm.
This sweater is made from acrylic, so it's both soft and easy to wash.
Many modern paints use acrylic as a binder for their pigments.
She prefers acrylic because it is lightweight and dries quickly.
The sweater is made from soft acrylic that feels like wool.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nylon - a type of synthetic polymer fiber
  • synonym: polyester - a synthetic fabric made from polyester fibers
