noncolonial

adjective
not colonial
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəˈloʊniəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəˈləʊniəl / (BrE)
not relating to, characteristic of, or resulting from a colony or colonial system

Examples

Noncolonial government
The new government promoted a noncolonial identity in its national policies.
Historians studied the region's noncolonial traditions that existed before foreign rule.
Their approach to trade was distinctly noncolonial, focusing on equal partnerships.
