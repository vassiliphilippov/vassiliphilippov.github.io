coco plum

noun
tropical tree with edible fruit
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊkoʊ ˌplʌm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkəʊ ˌplʌm / (BrE)
A small tropical American tree (Chrysobalanus icaco) that bears edible, plumlike fruit.

Examples

Coco plum tree
She made a delicious jam from fresh coco plums picked this morning.
The coco plum is often found along coastal areas in the Caribbean.
The leaves and fruit of the coco plum are used in local herbal remedies.
