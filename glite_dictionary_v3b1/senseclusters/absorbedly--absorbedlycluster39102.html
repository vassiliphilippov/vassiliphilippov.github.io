absorbedly

adverb
with full attention
6.0
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɔrbdli / (AmE), / əbˈzɔːbdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows complete attention or deep focus on something

Examples

Listen absorbedly to speaker
He listened absorbedly to the speaker, not noticing the time passing.
The children watched the magician absorbedly, their eyes wide with wonder.
She painted absorbedly, lost in her creative process for hours.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distractedly - in a way that shows a lack of focus
  • synonym: intently - with close attention or concentration
