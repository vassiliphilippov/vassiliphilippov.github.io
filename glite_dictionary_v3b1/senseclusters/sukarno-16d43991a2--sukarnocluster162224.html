Sukarno

proper noun
first president of Indonesia (1901–1970)
7.2
Pronunciation: /suː.ˈkɑːr.noʊ/ (AmE), /suː.ˈkɑː.nəʊ/ (BrE)
(1901–1970) Indonesian statesman who led his country's independence movement from the Netherlands and served as its first president from 1945 to 1967.

Examples

Sukarno's speeches inspired millions during the country's struggle for freedom.
Many Indonesians regard Sukarno as the founding father of their nation.
