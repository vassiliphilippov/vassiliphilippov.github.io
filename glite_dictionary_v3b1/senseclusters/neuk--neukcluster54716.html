neuk

proper noun
Scottish place name
Pronunciation: / nuːk / (AmE), / njuːk / (BrE)
a district or coastal area in Scotland, especially used in place names such as 'Neuk of Fife'

Examples

Neuk of Fife

The Neuk is known for its beautiful scenery and historic fishing towns.
