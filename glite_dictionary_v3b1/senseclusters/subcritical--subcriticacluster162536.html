subcritical

adjective
below critical point
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈkrɪtɪkəl / (AmE), / sʌbˈkrɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
below the critical value, temperature, or pressure at which a substance changes phase or exhibits critical behavior.

Examples

Subcritical temperature
The reactor was kept in a subcritical state to ensure safety during the experiment.
At subcritical pressures, water does not turn into steam even when heated.
Scientists observed the material's properties at subcritical temperatures to study its phase changes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: supercritical - above the critical temperature or pressure
