banana boat

noun
inflatable recreational boat towed by a speedboat
Pronunciation: / bəˈnænə ˌboʊt / (AmE), / bəˈnænə bəʊt / (BrE)
a long, inflatable recreational boat shaped like a banana, usually pulled by a speedboat and ridden by several people for fun

Examples

Ride a banana boat
The children squealed with delight as the banana boat bounced over the waves.
We booked a banana boat ride during our trip to the seaside resort.
Wearing life jackets, the group climbed onto the banana boat for an exciting adventure.
