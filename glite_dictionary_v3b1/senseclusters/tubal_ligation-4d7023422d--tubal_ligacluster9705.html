tubal ligation

noun
surgical sterilization for women
9.7 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtuː.bəl laɪ.ˈgeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈtjuː.bəl laɪ.ˈgeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a surgical procedure for female sterilization in which the Fallopian tubes are tied, cut, or sealed to prevent pregnancy

Examples

Tubal ligation surgery
A tubal ligation is considered a permanent form of birth control.
She decided to have a tubal ligation after her third child.
The doctor explained the risks and benefits of tubal ligation before scheduling the surgery.
