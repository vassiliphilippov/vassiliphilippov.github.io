jog trot

noun
easy horse gait, between walk and trot
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒɑɡ trɑt / (AmE), / dʒɒɡ trɒt / (BrE)
A gentle, moderate gait of a horse, intermediate between a walk and a trot.

Examples

A jog trot gait
After a few minutes at a walk, the instructor asked them to switch to a jog trot.
She trained her horse to move smoothly at a jog trot before increasing the speed.
The horse maintained a relaxed jog trot along the trail for most of the ride.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: trot - a moderately fast gait of a horse
  • degree lower: walk - a slow gait of a horse where at least two feet are on the ground
