jog trot

noun
easy horse gait, between walk and trot
9.9 6.7
Pronunciation: /dʒɑːg trɑt/ /; / /dʒɔːg trɔːt/ (AmE), /dʒɒg trɒt/ (BrE)
A gentle, moderate gait of a horse, intermediate between a walk and a trot.

Examples

The horse maintained a relaxed jog trot along the trail for most of the ride.
