mosaic

verb
to decorate with small pieces
Pronunciation: / moʊˈzeɪɪk / (AmE), / məʊˈzeɪɪk / (BrE)
to cover or decorate a surface with a pattern made from small pieces of colored material such as glass, stone, or tile

Examples

To mosaic a wall
The artist mosaicked the garden wall with colorful glass pieces.
They mosaic the entrance floor to create a welcoming pattern for visitors.
After weeks of work, she finally mosaicked the tabletop with tiny ceramic tiles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inlay - to decorate a surface by setting pieces of material into it
  • antonym: paint - to cover a surface with color using a brush or similar tool
