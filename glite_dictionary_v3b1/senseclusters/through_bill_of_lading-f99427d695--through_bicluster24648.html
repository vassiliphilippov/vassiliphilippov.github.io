through bill of lading

noun
bill of lading for multimodal transport
9.9 7.2
Pronunciation: /θruː bɪl ˈleɪ.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /θruː bɪl ˈleɪ.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
a bill of lading that covers a shipment transported using more than one mode of transport or passing through different distribution centers

Examples

A through bill of lading document
A through bill of lading streamlines documentation for shipments crossing international borders and switching carriers.
The shipping company issued a through bill of lading to cover the entire journey, including rail and sea transport.
With a through bill of lading, the consignee can track the goods through all stages of transit.
