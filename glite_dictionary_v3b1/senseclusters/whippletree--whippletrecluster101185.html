whippletree

noun
crossbar in draft animal harnesses
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪpəlˌtri / (AmE), / ˈwɪpəlˌtri / (BrE)
a horizontal crossbar pivoted in the middle, used to distribute the force from a draft animal's traces to the vehicle or implement being pulled.

Examples

A whippletree crossbar
A broken whippletree can make it hard for horses to pull a heavy load evenly.
Each whippletree was carefully balanced to ensure equal effort from all the horses.
The blacksmith made a new whippletree for the farmer's wagon.
