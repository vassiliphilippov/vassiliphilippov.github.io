metabolic process

noun
chemical processes in living organisms
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈbɑlɪk ˈprɑsɛs / (AmE), / ˌmetəˈbɒlɪk ˈprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
any of the chemical and physical processes occurring within a living cell or organism that are necessary to maintain life

Examples

A metabolic process in cells
Enzymes play a vital role in every metabolic process inside the cell.
Photosynthesis is an example of a metabolic process in plants.
Regular exercise can boost your body's metabolic processes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: metabolism - the set of life-sustaining chemical reactions in organisms
