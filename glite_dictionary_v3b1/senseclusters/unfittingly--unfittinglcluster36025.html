unfittingly

adverb
inappropriately
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is not suitable or appropriate for the situation

Examples

Spoke unfittingly at ceremony
He spoke unfittingly at the ceremony, making jokes during a serious moment.
The painting was unfittingly displayed in a dark corner of the gallery.
She reacted unfittingly to the news, laughing when everyone else was silent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inappropriately - in a way that is not suitable or proper
  • antonym: suitably - in a way that is appropriate or fitting
