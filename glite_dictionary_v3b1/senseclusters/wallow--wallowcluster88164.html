wallow

noun
act of rolling about lazily
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːloʊ / (AmE), / ˈwɒləʊ / (BrE)
an act of rolling about or lying in water, mud, or another surface, often in a lazy or relaxed way

Examples

A relaxing wallow
A good wallow in the bath helped him relax after a stressful day.
After a long hike, the pigs enjoyed a wallow in the cool muddy pond.
The children found a shallow pool and had a playful wallow before dinner.
