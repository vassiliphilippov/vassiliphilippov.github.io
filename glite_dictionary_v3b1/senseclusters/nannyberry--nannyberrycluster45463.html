nannyberry

noun
edible berry shrub
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnæniˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈnæniˌbɛri / (BrE)
a North American shrub (Viburnum lentago) with clusters of edible blue-black berries

Examples

Nannyberry shrub
In autumn, the nannyberry bushes are covered with dark, sweet fruit.
Birds and wildlife are attracted to the nannyberry for its nutritious berries.
We made jam from the ripe nannyberry we gathered in the woods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elderberry - a shrub with small dark berries used in food and drink
  • synonym: viburnum - a genus of shrubs or small trees with clusters of white or pink flowers
