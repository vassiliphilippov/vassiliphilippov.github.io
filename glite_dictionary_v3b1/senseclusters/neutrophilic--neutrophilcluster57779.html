neutrophilic

adjective
relating to neutrophils
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnuːtrəˈfɪlɪk / (AmE), / ˌnjuːtrəˈfɪlɪk / (BrE)
relating to or showing an affinity for neutrophils or neutral dyes, especially in biological or medical contexts

Examples

Neutrophilic cells
The doctor noted a neutrophilic infiltration in the patient's blood sample.
A neutrophilic response is often seen in bacterial infections.
Certain stains highlight neutrophilic cells under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • antonym: basophilic - relating to or showing an affinity for basophils or basic dyes
  • antonym: eosinophilic - relating to or showing an affinity for eosinophils or eosin dye
