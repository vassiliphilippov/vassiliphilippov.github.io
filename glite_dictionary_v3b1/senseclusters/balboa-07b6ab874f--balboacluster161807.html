Balboa

proper noun
Spanish explorer, first European to see Pacific from Americas
8.9
Pronunciation: /bɑːl.ˈboʊ.ə/ /; / /bæl.ˈboʊ.ə/ (AmE), /bæl.ˈbəʊ.ə/ (BrE)
(1475–1519) Spanish explorer who was the first European to see the eastern shore of the Pacific Ocean after crossing the Isthmus of Darien in 1513.

Examples

Balboa the explorer
Balboa is credited with the European discovery of the Pacific Ocean.
Many places in Panama are named after Balboa in honor of his achievements.
Vasco Núñez de Balboa led an expedition across Panama in 1513.
