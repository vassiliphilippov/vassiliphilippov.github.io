windsurfing

verb
to ride a sailboard
3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪnd.sɜːr.fɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈwɪnd.sɜː.fɪŋ/ (BrE)
to ride on water using a board with an attached sail, propelled by the wind.

Examples

To windsurf on water
They spent the afternoon windsurfing across the calm lake under a clear blue sky.
Many tourists enjoy windsurfing when they visit the Mediterranean coast in summer.
He learned windsurfing as a teenager and now teaches others how to balance on the board.
