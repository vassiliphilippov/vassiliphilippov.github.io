mirror

noun
reflective glass surface for images
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪrɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɪrə / (BrE)
a smooth, highly polished surface, typically made of glass, that reflects images by bouncing light

Examples

A bathroom mirror
A full-length mirror hangs on the bedroom door.
She checked her hair in the mirror before leaving the house.
The sunlight reflected off the mirror and brightened the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: looking glass - an old-fashioned term for a mirror
