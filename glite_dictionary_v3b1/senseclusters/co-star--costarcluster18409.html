co-star

verb
appear as one of the leading performers
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊ ˌstɑːr /; / ˈkoʊ stɑr / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ ˌstɑː /; / ˈkəʊ stɑː / (BrE)
