copper rockfish

noun
a Pacific North American species of rockfish
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːpər ˈrɑkˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɒpə ˈrɒkˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a species of rockfish with a coppery coloration found in the Pacific coastal waters of North America
