Garifuna

proper noun
Caribbean ethnic group
Pronunciation: / ˌɡɑːrɪˈfuːnə / (AmE), / ˌɡærɪˈfuːnə / (BrE)
a member of a Caribbean people of mixed African and Indigenous descent, or their language and culture

Examples

A Garifuna person
The Garifuna have preserved many of their traditional customs and dances.
She is proud of her Garifuna heritage and often speaks the language at home.
Many Garifuna communities can be found along the coast of Central America.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Arawak - an Indigenous people of the Caribbean and South America
  • synonym: Carib - an Indigenous people of the Caribbean region
