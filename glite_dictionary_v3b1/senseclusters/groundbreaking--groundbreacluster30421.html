groundbreaking

noun
ceremonial start of construction
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊndˌbreɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊn(d)ˌbreɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
a ceremony to mark the beginning of a construction project, usually by turning the first sod of earth

Examples

Groundbreaking ceremony
A traditional groundbreaking ceremony was held before construction began on the hospital.
The mayor attended the groundbreaking for the new library downtown.
Yesterday's groundbreaking marked the start of the city's largest infrastructure project.
