pivoting window

noun
window opening by central pivot
5.2 5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪvətɪŋ ˈwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɪvətɪŋ ˈwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
A window that opens by turning on a central pivot, either horizontally or vertically.

Examples

A pivoting window
Modern homes often feature a pivoting window as an alternative to traditional sash designs.
She opened the pivoting window, letting the cool autumn breeze into the kitchen.
The architect suggested installing a pivoting window for better air circulation in the living room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: casement window - a window that is attached to its frame by one or more hinges at the side
  • synonym: sash window - a window made of one or more movable panels that form a frame to hold panes of glass
