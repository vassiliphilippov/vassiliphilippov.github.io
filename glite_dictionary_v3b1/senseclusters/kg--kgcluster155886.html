kg

noun
symbol for kilogram (unit of mass)
1.0
Pronunciation: / keɪ dʒiː / (AmE), / keɪ dʒiː / (BrE)
The symbol for kilogram, the basic unit of mass in the International System of Units (SI), equal to 1,000 grams.

Examples

kg unit
The newborn weighed exactly 3.2 kg at birth.
The package must not exceed 10 kg in total weight.
This recipe calls for one kg of flour.
