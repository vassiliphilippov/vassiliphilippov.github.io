autographer

noun
handwritten author
8.7
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈtɑːɡrəfər / (AmE), / ɔːˈtɒɡrəfə / (BrE)
a person who writes a text in their own handwriting, especially an author or scribe who composes manuscripts by hand

Examples

Famous autographer letter
The historian discovered a letter written by a famous autographer from the 18th century.
Before the invention of the typewriter, every novelist was an autographer by necessity.
She preferred to be an autographer, filling journals with her own handwriting instead of using a computer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: author - a person who writes books or other texts
  • synonym: scribe - a person who copies or writes documents by hand
  • antonym: typist - a person who types documents
