insouciant

adjective
unconcerned; carefree
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈsuː.sjənt/ /; / /ɪn.ˈsuː.sɪ.ənt/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈsuː.sɪ.ənt/ /; / /ɪn.ˈsuː.sjənt/ (BrE)
