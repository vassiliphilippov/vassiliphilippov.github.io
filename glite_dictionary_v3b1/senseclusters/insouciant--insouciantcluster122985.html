insouciant

adjective
unconcerned; carefree
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsuːsiənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈsuːsiənt / (BrE)
showing a casual lack of concern; indifferent or carefree.

Examples

An insouciant attitude
His insouciant answer made it clear that he was unbothered by the criticism.
She felt remarkably insouciant about the looming deadline, choosing to read a novel instead of working.
The cat lounged on the windowsill, utterly insouciant to the chaos around her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carefree - free from anxiety or responsibility
  • antonym: concerned - worried or troubled about something
  • synonym: nonchalant - calm and relaxed; not displaying anxiety or interest
