guarantee

noun
written assurance for a product or service
Pronunciation: / ˌgærənˈti /; / ˌɡɛrənˈti / (AmE), / ˌɡærənˈtiː / (BrE)
a formal or written promise that something will meet certain standards, is true, or will happen

Examples

Product guarantee
Always check if the product has a guarantee for quality and performance.
She requested a written guarantee before making the purchase.
The refrigerator comes with a one-year guarantee against mechanical defects.
The product comes with a two-year guarantee against defects.
There is no guarantee that your application will be accepted.
We need a guarantee that this will never happen again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assurance - a positive declaration intended to give confidence
  • synonym: promise - a declaration that something will or will not be done
  • synonym: warranty - a written guarantee, often for repairs or replacement
