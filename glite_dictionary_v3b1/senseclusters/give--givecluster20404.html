Synonyms

  • deliver
    2.6 41/m
    deliver is used in formal contexts to present or perform something to an audience, while give is more general and less informal.
  • deliver a speech
    3.3 0.45/m
    deliver a speech is a phrase for formally presenting words to an audience, while give is a broader verb for presenting many things.
  • make a speech
    2.8 1.19/m
    make a speech is used for giving a formal talk at events or ceremonies, while give is more general and appears in many contexts.
  • speak
    3.2 80/m
    speak means talking or giving a formal talk to an audience, while give is a more general verb covering gestures, signals, or performances.
  • talk
    3.4 61/m
    talk is often less formal and can mean an informal lecture or chat, while give is neutral and can mean present or perform.
