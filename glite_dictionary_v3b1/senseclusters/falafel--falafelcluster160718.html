falafel

noun
deep-fried ball of chickpeas or beans
6.3 3.4
Pronunciation: / fəˈlɑːfəl / (AmE), / fəˈlæfəl / (BrE)
A small, deep-fried ball or patty made from ground chickpeas or fava beans, usually seasoned with herbs and spices.

Examples

A falafel ball
She makes her own falafel using a blend of chickpeas and fresh herbs.
The restaurant is famous for its crispy falafel sandwiches.
We ordered a plate of falafel with tahini sauce for lunch.
