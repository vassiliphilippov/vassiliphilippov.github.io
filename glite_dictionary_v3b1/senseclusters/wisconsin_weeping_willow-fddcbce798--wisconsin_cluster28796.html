Wisconsin weeping willow

noun
a hybrid willow tree with upright habit
7.7
Pronunciation: / wɪˈskɑnsɪn ˈwiːpɪŋ ˈwɪloʊ / (AmE), / wɪsˈkɒnsɪn ˈwiːpɪŋ ˈwɪləʊ / (BrE)
a hybrid variety of willow tree, usually not displaying a strongly drooping or 'weeping' growth habit

Examples

The Wisconsin weeping willow tree
Landscapers often choose the Wisconsin weeping willow for its resilience and unique appearance.
The Wisconsin weeping willow in our garden grew upright rather than drooping down.
Unlike the classic weeping willow, the Wisconsin weeping willow does not have strongly hanging branches.

  • synonym: weeping willow - a type of willow tree with long, drooping branches
