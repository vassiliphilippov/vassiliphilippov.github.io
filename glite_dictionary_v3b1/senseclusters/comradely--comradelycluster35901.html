comradely

adjective
friendly and supportive; showing camaraderie
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmrədli / (AmE), / ˈkɒmrədli / (BrE)
friendly and supportive in a way that shows camaraderie or solidarity

Examples

A comradely attitude
Despite their political differences, they maintained a comradely respect for one another.
The veteran shared a comradely handshake with his old friend from the army.
There was a comradely atmosphere at the meeting, with everyone working together toward a common goal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: friendly - kind and pleasant; acting like a friend
  • antonym: hostile - unfriendly or antagonistic
