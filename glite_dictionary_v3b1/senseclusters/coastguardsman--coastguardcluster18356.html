coastguardsman

noun
male member of a coastguard
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊstˌɡɑrdzmən / (AmE), / ˈkəʊstˌɡɑːdzmən / (BrE)
a male member of a coastguard, responsible for protecting coastal areas and performing rescue operations at sea

Examples

A coastguardsman on duty
During his career, he served as a coastguardsman along the eastern shore.
Every coastguardsman must be trained in both navigation and emergency response.
The coastguardsman bravely rescued the fishermen from the sinking boat during the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coastguard - a person who protects coastal areas and assists in marine rescues
  • antonym: coastguardswoman - a female member of a coastguard
