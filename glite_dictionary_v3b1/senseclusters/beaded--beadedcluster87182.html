beaded

adjective
covered with drops, like beads
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbiːdɪd / (BrE)
(of a surface) covered with small drops of liquid, resembling beads.

Examples

Beaded with sweat
After running for an hour, his forehead was beaded with sweat.
Her arms were beaded with raindrops as she entered the house.
The glass was beaded with condensation from the cold drink.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: damp - slightly wet
  • degree higher: dripping - very wet, with liquid falling in drops
