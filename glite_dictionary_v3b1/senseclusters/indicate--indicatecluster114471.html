indicate

verb
be medically recommended or necessary
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.dɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.dɪ.keɪt / (BrE)
(of a medicine, treatment, or procedure) to be recommended as advisable or necessary, especially for a particular condition or case.

Examples

Treatment indicated for illness
A low-sodium diet is indicated in cases of high blood pressure.
Surgery may be indicated if the patient's symptoms worsen despite medication.
The use of antibiotics is indicated only when bacterial infection is confirmed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contraindicate - suggest that a particular treatment or procedure should not be used
