Chichimec

noun

Pronunciation: /tʃɪ.tʃɪ.ˈmek/ /; / /tʃiː.tʃiː.ˈmek/ (AmE), /tʃɪ.tʃɪ.ˈmek/ /; / /tʃiː.tʃiː.ˈmek/ (BrE)
(historical) a member of a group of indigenous peoples in northern Mexico, often regarded as uncivilized by settled Mesoamerican societies

Examples

A Chichimec tribe
The Aztecs often described their northern neighbors as Chichimec tribes.
Archaeologists have found artifacts linked to the ancient Chichimec people in central Mexico.
In historical texts, the term Chichimec was sometimes used to label outsiders.
