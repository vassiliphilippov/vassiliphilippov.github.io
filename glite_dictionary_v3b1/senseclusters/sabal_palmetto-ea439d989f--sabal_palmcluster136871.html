Sabal palmetto

noun
a palm tree native to the southern US
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪbəl pælˈmiːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈseɪbəl pælˈmiːtəʊ / (BrE)
a species of palm tree native to the coastal southern United States, with fan-shaped leaves and edible leaf buds, also known as cabbage palmetto

Examples

The Sabal palmetto tree
Local cuisines sometimes use the heart of the Sabal palmetto in salads.
Many coastal landscapes are dotted with tall Sabal palmetto trees.
The Sabal palmetto is the state tree of South Carolina and Florida.
