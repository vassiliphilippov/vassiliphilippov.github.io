planisher

noun
metal smoothing tool
Pronunciation: / ˈplænɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈplænɪʃə / (BrE)
a tool used to smooth and flatten metal surfaces by hammering or pressing

Examples

A metal planisher
The craftsman used a planisher to achieve a perfectly smooth surface on the metal plate.
After shaping the bowl, she finished it with a planisher for a polished look.
A planisher is essential for jewelers who want to create flawless metalwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anvil - a heavy iron block used in metalworking
  • synonym: hammer - a tool with a heavy head used for striking
