Windward Isles

proper noun
group of islands in the southeastern West Indies
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndˌwɚd aɪlz / (AmE), / ˈwɪndwəd aɪlz / (BrE)
a group of islands in the southeastern West Indies, forming the southern part of the Lesser Antilles.

Examples

The Windward Isles
Hurricanes frequently pass near the Windward Isles during the Atlantic storm season.
The Windward Isles include countries such as Grenada, Saint Lucia, and Martinique.
Tourists flock to the Windward Isles for their beautiful beaches and vibrant cultures.
