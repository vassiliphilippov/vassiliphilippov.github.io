steam heat

noun
heating system using steam and radiators
2.6
Pronunciation: / stiːm hiːt / (AmE), / stiːm hiːt / (BrE)
A system of heating a building in which steam produced in a boiler is circulated to radiators.

Examples

Steam heat system
During repairs, the steam heat made the boiler room uncomfortably hot.
Many historic buildings rely on steam heat rather than more modern systems.
The old apartment building still uses steam heat to keep residents warm in winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: central heating - a system for heating a building from a central source
