addlebrained

adjective
stupid and confused
Pronunciation: / ˈædəlˌbreɪnd / (AmE), / ˈædəlˌbreɪnd / (BrE)
Stupid and confused; having a muddled or disordered mind.

Examples

An addlebrained person
Her addlebrained response left everyone more confused than before.
I wouldn't trust his advice—he always sounds so addlebrained in meetings.
Only an addlebrained scheme like that could possibly fail so quickly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearheaded - mentally alert and clear
  • synonym: muddleheaded - mentally confused or muddled
