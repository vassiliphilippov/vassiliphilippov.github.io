hypersexuality

noun
excessive sexual urges
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˌsɛkʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˌsɛkʃuˈælɪti / (BrE)
a condition of experiencing extremely frequent or intense sexual urges or behaviors

Examples

Extreme hypersexuality
The psychiatrist explained that hypersexuality can sometimes be a symptom of certain mental health conditions.
Some medications may cause hypersexuality as an unexpected side effect in patients.
Support groups can help individuals struggling with hypersexuality manage their behaviors.
