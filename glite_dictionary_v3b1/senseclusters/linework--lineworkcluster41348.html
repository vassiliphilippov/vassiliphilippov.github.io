linework

noun
drawing with lines
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪnwɜːrk / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnwɜːk / (BrE)
the technique or style of using lines in drawing or illustration to create images or effects

Examples

Linework in drawing
The comic book artist is known for his detailed linework and expressive characters.
Good linework can make even a simple sketch look dynamic and interesting.
She practiced her linework every day to improve her drawing skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hatching - a drawing technique using closely spaced lines
  • antonym: shading - the technique of representing light and shadow in drawing
