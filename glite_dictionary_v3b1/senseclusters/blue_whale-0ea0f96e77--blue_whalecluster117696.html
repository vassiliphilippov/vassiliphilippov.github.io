blue whale

noun
the largest known whale species
Pronunciation: / blu weɪl / (AmE), / bluː weɪl / (BrE)
a very large migratory marine mammal (Balaenoptera musculus), bluish-grey in color, and the largest animal known to have ever existed

Examples

A blue whale swims
The blue whale is considered the largest animal ever to have lived on Earth.
The tongue of a blue whale can weigh as much as an elephant.
We saw a blue whale breaching the surface during our boat tour.
