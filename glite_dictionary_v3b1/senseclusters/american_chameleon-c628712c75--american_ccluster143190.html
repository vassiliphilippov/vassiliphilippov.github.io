American chameleon

noun
color-changing tropical American lizard
3.0
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən kəˈmiː.li.ən / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən kəˈmiː.li.ən / (BrE)
a small tropical lizard native to the Americas, known for its ability to change skin color

Examples

The American chameleon changes color
Gardeners sometimes spot an American chameleon hunting insects among the leaves.
The American chameleon is often seen sunning itself on tree branches.
Unlike true chameleons, the American chameleon is actually a type of anole.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anole - a small lizard of the Americas, also able to change color
