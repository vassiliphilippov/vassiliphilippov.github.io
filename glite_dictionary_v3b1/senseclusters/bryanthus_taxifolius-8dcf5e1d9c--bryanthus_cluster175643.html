Bryanthus taxifolius

noun
alpine shrub with pink or purple flowers
Pronunciation: / braɪˈænθəs ˌtæksɪˈfoʊliəs / (AmE), / braɪˈænθəs ˌtæksɪˈfəʊlɪəs / (BrE)
A small evergreen shrub with tiny leaves and pink or purple flowers, found on alpine summits and high elevations in Asia, Europe, and the United States.
