interfile

verb
to file among others
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈfaɪl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈfaɪl / (BrE)
to file documents or items among or between others in a sequence or order

Examples

To interfile documents
The librarian carefully interfiled the new books with the existing collection on the shelf.
Please interfile these documents alphabetically with the rest of the client records.
After reviewing the papers, she interfiled them in the appropriate folders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insert - to put something into something else
  • antonym: remove - to take something away from a place
