spoofer

noun
deceiver or disguiser
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspuːfər / (AmE), / ˈspuːfə / (BrE)
a person or device that pretends to be something else, often to deceive or hide identity

Examples

Network spoofer
The security team detected a spoofer trying to access the network with a fake IP address.
A spoofer can make emails appear as if they come from a trusted source.
He was caught using a spoofer to bypass the website's location restrictions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: authenticator - a person or device that verifies identity
  • synonym: faker - someone who pretends to be something they are not
  • synonym: impostor - a person who pretends to be someone else
