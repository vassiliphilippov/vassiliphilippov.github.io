reappreciation

noun
renewed gratitude
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːəˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)

Examples

Her time away brought a new reappreciation for the support of her friends.
