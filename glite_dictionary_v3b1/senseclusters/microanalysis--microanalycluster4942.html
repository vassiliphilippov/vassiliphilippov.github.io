microanalysis

noun
detailed small-scale study
7.3
Pronunciation: /maɪ.krə.ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ /; / /maɪ.kroʊ.ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ (AmE), /maɪ.krə.ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ /; / /maɪ.krəʊ.ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ (BrE)
the detailed study or analysis of phenomena at a very small or individual scale, such as specific behaviors or interactions

Examples

Behavior microanalysis
The researcher performed a microanalysis of classroom conversations to understand student participation.
Through microanalysis, the linguist identified subtle changes in tone during the interview.
A detailed microanalysis can reveal patterns that are invisible in broader studies.
