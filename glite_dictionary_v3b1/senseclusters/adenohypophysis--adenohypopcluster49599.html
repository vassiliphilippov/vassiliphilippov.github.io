adenohypophysis

noun
anterior lobe of the pituitary gland
Pronunciation: / ˌædɪnoʊhaɪˈpɑfəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌædɪnəʊhaɪˈpɒfəsɪs / (BrE)
the anterior lobe of the pituitary gland, which is primarily glandular and secretes several hormones

Examples

The adenohypophysis gland
Damage to the adenohypophysis can result in various endocrine disorders.

The adenohypophysis releases hormones that regulate growth and metabolism.
