cozener

noun
cheater or deceiver
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌzənər / (AmE), / ˈkɒzənə / (BrE)
a person who cheats or deceives others, especially by trickery or fraud

Examples

A cozener cheats people
The villagers warned newcomers about the notorious cozener who preyed on travelers.
He was exposed as a cozener after several people lost their savings to his schemes.
In old stories, a clever cozener often tricked the wealthy out of their fortunes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fraudster - someone who commits fraud
  • antonym: honest person - someone who tells the truth and does not cheat
  • synonym: swindler - a person who cheats others out of money or assets
