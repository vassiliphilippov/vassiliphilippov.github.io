gray-haired

adjective
having gray or white hair
1.0 1.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɡreɪˈhɛrd /; / ˈɡreɪˌhɛrd /; / ɡreɪ hɛrd / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪˈheəd /; / ˈɡreɪˌheəd /; / ɡreɪ hɛəd / (BrE)
(of a person) having hair that is gray or white, typically as a sign of aging.
