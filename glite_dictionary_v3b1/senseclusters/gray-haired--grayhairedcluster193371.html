Synonyms

  • white-haired
    1.7 1.25/m
    white-haired is hair that is fully white and often seen in older people, while gray-haired describes hair with gray tones from aging.
  • gray-headed
    4.4 0.04/m
    gray-headed describes having gray or white hair on the head specifically, while gray-haired refers to a person's overall gray hair.
  • silver-haired
    2.7 0.44/m
    silver-haired sounds more poetic and flattering, while gray-haired is plain descriptive and commonly used for aging people's hair.
  • gray
    1.0 12/m
    gray can describe a person's age or look broadly, while gray-haired specifically names having gray or white hair.
