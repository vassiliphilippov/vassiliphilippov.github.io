paint a picture

verb
describe in detail to create an impression
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪnt ə ˈpɪktʃər / (AmE), / ˈpeɪnt ə ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
to describe someone or something in detail, often in a way that creates a vivid impression in the listener's or reader's mind

Examples

To paint a picture clearly
She painted a picture of her childhood that was both nostalgic and bittersweet.
The author paints a picture of resilience in the face of adversity.
The report paints a picture of a thriving but troubled city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depict - to describe or show someone or something in a picture or words
  • synonym: portray - to describe or represent someone or something in a particular way
