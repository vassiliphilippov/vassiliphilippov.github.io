turbit

noun
pigeon breed
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrbɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɜːbɪt / (BrE)
a breed of domestic pigeon recognized for its short beak and frilled feathers

Examples

The turbit pigeon
The turbit is often displayed at bird shows because of its distinctive frilled feathers.
Many breeders admire the turbit for its compact body and short beak.
He keeps several turbits in his aviary alongside other fancy pigeon breeds.
