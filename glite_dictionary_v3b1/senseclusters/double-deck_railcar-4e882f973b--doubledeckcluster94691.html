double-deck railcar

noun
railcar with two levels for passengers
5.5 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈdʌb.əl dek ˈreɪl.kɑːr/ (AmE), /ˈdʌb.əl dek ˈreɪl.kɑː/ (BrE)
