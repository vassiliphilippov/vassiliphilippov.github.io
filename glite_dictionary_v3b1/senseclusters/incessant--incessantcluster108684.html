incessant

adjective
continuing without interruption
8.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛsənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛsənt / (BrE)
continuing without pause or interruption; never-ending.

Examples

The incessant noise
I couldn't sleep because of the incessant barking of the neighbor's dog.
The incessant rain flooded the streets for days.
Their incessant chatter made it hard to concentrate on my work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceaseless - without stop or pause; unending
  • antonym: intermittent - occurring at irregular intervals; not continuous
  • synonym: unremitting - never relaxing or slackening; persistent
