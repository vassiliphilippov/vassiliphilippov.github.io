preppy

noun
prep school student
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛpi / (AmE), / ˈprɛpi / (BrE)
a young person, especially a student, who dresses or behaves in a neat, traditional, and often expensive style associated with private schools

Examples

A preppy student
He was known as a preppy because of his neat clothes and private school background.
Many preppies join exclusive clubs during their time at prep school.
She never considered herself a preppy, even though she attended a prestigious academy.
The group of preppies gathered near the tennis courts after class.
At the party, the preppies stood out in their blazers and khakis.
Many preppies choose to attend prestigious universities after high school.
The new student looked like a typical preppy with his blazer and polished shoes.
At the party, several preppies gathered in a corner, laughing about their summer vacations.
She was labeled a preppy because of her neat appearance and expensive clothes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prepster - a person who dresses or behaves in the style typical of prep schools
  • synonym: snob - a person who believes they are superior to others
