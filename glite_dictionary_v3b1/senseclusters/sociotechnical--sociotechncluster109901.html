sociotechnical

adjective
social and technical
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsi.oʊˈtɛknɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsi.əʊˈtɛknɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the interaction between social and technical aspects of an organization, system, or process

Examples

A sociotechnical system
The new software was developed using a sociotechnical approach to ensure both user needs and technical requirements were met.
A sociotechnical system considers how people and technology work together in the workplace.
Researchers studied the sociotechnical factors influencing the success of the hospital's digital transformation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: systemic - relating to or affecting an entire system
  • degree lower: technical - relating to practical skills or technology
