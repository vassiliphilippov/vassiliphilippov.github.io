bitchiness

noun
malicious or spiteful behavior
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtʃ.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈbɪtʃ.i.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being spiteful, malicious, or nastily critical, especially in a petty or mean-spirited way

Examples

The bitchiness of remarks
Despite his success, he was criticized for the bitchiness of his remarks during interviews.
Her bitchiness was apparent in the way she gossiped about her colleagues.
They were tired of the constant bitchiness in the office and wished for a friendlier atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate
  • synonym: maliciousness - the state of being deliberately harmful or spiteful
  • synonym: meanness - unkindness or spitefulness
