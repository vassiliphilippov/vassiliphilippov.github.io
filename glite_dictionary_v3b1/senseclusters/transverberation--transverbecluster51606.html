transverberation

noun
piercing through
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænzvərbəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtrænzvɜːbəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or instance of piercing through or transfixing something, especially by a sharp object

Examples

Transverberation by arrow
The transverberation of the arrow left a clean wound through the target.
During the accident, the transverberation of glass shards caused serious injuries.
Historical accounts describe the transverberation of swords in medieval battles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: penetration - the act of entering or passing through something
  • synonym: perforation - the act of making a hole through something
