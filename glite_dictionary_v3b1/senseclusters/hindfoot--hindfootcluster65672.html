hindfoot

noun
rear foot of a quadruped
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪndˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈhaɪndˌfʊt / (BrE)
the rear foot or part of the foot in a four-legged animal

Examples

The hindfoot of animals
The deer injured its hindfoot while jumping over the fence.
The veterinarian wrapped the hindfoot carefully to protect the wound.
Tracks in the mud showed the distinct shape of the animal’s hindfoot.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forefoot - the front foot of a quadruped
