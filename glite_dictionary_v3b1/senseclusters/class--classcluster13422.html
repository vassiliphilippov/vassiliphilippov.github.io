class

noun
group of students taught together
1.5 1.0
Pronunciation: /klæs/ (AmE), /klɑːs/ (BrE)
a group of students who are taught together, the lessons they attend, or the group graduating in the same year

Examples

A class of students
Our math class meets every Monday and Wednesday at 10 a.m.
She enjoys being part of a small class because it's easier to ask questions.
The whole class went on a field trip to the science museum.
After missing several classes, he found it difficult to catch up with the material.
She signed up for a class in creative writing this semester.
The class meets every Monday and Wednesday afternoon.
Every five years, the class reunites to celebrate their graduation anniversary.
Our class of 2020 will always remember the virtual graduation ceremony.
She was voted president of her class in high school.
