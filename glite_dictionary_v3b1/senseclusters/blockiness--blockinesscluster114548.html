blockiness

noun
chunky or angular quality
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈblɑː.kɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈblɒ.kɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being block-like in shape or lacking fine detail, often appearing chunky or angular

Examples

Blockiness of a sculpture
The blockiness of the sculpture gave it a bold, modern appearance.
Low-resolution images often suffer from blockiness that makes details hard to see.
The architect intentionally used blockiness in the building's design to create a sense of strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chunkiness - the quality of being thick or bulky in form
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of having an even or fine surface without roughness
