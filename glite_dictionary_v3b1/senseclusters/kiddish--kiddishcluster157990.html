kiddish

adjective
childlike or for children
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪdɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɪdɪʃ / (BrE)
suitable for, intended for, or characteristic of children; designed to appeal to or entertain children

Examples

Kiddish games
The decorations at the party were bright and kiddish, perfect for a group of young children.
Some people found the movie too kiddish for adult audiences.
She chose a kiddish theme for her little brother's birthday celebration.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adult - suitable for grown-ups, not for children
  • synonym: childish - typical of a child or children
  • synonym: juvenile - relating to young people or children
