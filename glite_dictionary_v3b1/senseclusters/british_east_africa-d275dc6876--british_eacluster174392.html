British East Africa

proper noun
former British territories in eastern Africa
Pronunciation: / ˌbrɪtɪʃ iːst ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (AmE), / ˌbrɪtɪʃ iːst ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (BrE)
