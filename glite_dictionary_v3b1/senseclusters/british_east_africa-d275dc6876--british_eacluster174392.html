British East Africa

proper noun
former British territories in eastern Africa
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌbrɪtɪʃ iːst ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (AmE), / ˌbrɪtɪʃ iːst ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (BrE)
a former group of British territories in eastern Africa, including Kenya, Tanganyika, Uganda, and Zanzibar, existing mainly from the late 19th to the mid-20th century
