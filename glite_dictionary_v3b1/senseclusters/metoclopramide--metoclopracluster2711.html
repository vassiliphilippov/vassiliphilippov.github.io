metoclopramide

noun
anti-nausea medication
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈkloʊprəˌmaɪd / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈklɒprəˌmaɪd / (BrE)
a medication used to treat nausea, vomiting, and certain stomach or digestive problems

Examples

Metoclopramide medicine
The doctor prescribed metoclopramide to help with her nausea.
After surgery, he was given metoclopramide to prevent vomiting.
Some patients take metoclopramide before meals to improve digestion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domperidone - a drug used to relieve nausea and vomiting
  • synonym: ondansetron - a medication used to prevent nausea and vomiting
