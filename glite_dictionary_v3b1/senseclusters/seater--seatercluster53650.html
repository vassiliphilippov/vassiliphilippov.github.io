seater

noun
person in a position
5.3 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsiÀêt…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàsiÀêt…ô / (BrE)
a person who occupies a particular seat or position, especially an elected or official post

Examples

City council seater
The new seater of the city council promised to fight for public transport improvements.
Each seater on the committee is responsible for a different area of policy.
After the election, the previous seater congratulated her successor on winning the post.
