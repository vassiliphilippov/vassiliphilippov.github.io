Synonyms

  • noncombustible
    5.3 0.04/m
    noncombustible describes materials that cannot burn in normal conditions, while incombustible stresses absolute inability to catch fire.
  • nonflammable
    4.1 0.09/m
    nonflammable means it does not catch fire easily in normal use, while incombustible says it cannot burn at all.
  • unburnable
    4.7 0.02/m
    unburnable is used for things not suitable or safe to burn, while incombustible is a strict term meaning cannot catch fire.
