shudderingly

adverb
with a shudder or shiver
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌdɚɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈʃʌdərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner marked by or expressing a shudder; with a shiver of fear, disgust, or revulsion.

Examples

He shudderingly recalled
He shudderingly recalled the unpleasant memory from his childhood.
She shudderingly turned away from the gruesome sight.
The idea of walking through the haunted house appealed to her, though she agreed shudderingly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shudder - to tremble convulsively, typically from fear or revulsion
