double cream

noun
cream with high fat content (48%+)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl ˈkrim / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl ˈkriːm / (BrE)
A type of cream with a fat content of 48% or more, used in cooking and as a topping for desserts.

Examples

Double cream topping
In the UK, double cream is often poured over pies and puddings as a dessert topping.
Strawberries taste delicious with a generous serving of double cream on top.
The recipe called for double cream to give the sauce a rich, velvety texture.
