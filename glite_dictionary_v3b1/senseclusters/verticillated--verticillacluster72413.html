verticillated

adjective
arranged in a whorl (botany)
Pronunciation: / vərˈtɪsɪˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / vəˈtɪsɪleɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of plant parts) arranged in one or more whorls, especially referring to leaves or branches in a circular formation around a stem.
