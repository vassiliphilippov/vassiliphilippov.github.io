tease

verb
separate or comb fibers
Pronunciation: / tiz / (AmE), / tiːz / (BrE)
to separate, raise, or fluff up fibers or material by combing, pulling, or brushing

Examples

To tease wool fibers
Before spinning the yarn, you need to tease the wool to separate the fibers.
She carefully teased the cotton to prepare it for stuffing the pillows.
The felting process often begins by teasing the fibers to ensure even texture.
She used a special comb to tease the fibers apart carefully.
The spinner began to tease the wool before spinning it into yarn.
To make felt, you must first tease the raw wool to loosen and clean it.
After you tease the sections, use hairspray to help them stay in place.
She carefully teased her bangs before pinning them back.
The stylist began to tease her hair at the crown for extra volume.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nap - the soft or fuzzy surface on fabric
