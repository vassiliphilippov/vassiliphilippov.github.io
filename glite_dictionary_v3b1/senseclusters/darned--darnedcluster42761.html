darned

adjective
mild informal intensifier
Pronunciation: / dɑrnd / (AmE), / dɑːnd / (BrE)
Used informally as a mild expletive to emphasize or intensify meaning, often as a substitute for stronger language.

Examples

So darned hard
I was so darned tired after the hike that I fell asleep instantly.
If you can't find your keys, check the darned pockets of your coat again.
That is the darned cutest puppy I have ever seen!

Related Terms

  • degree lower: blessed - very mild intensifier, sometimes used ironically
  • degree higher: damned - offensive intensifier, stronger than 'darned'
