snow thrower

noun
machine for removing snow
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊ ˌθroʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊ ˌθrəʊ.ə / (BrE)
a machine designed to remove snow by scooping it up and forcefully ejecting it through a chute

Examples

A snow thrower machine
After the heavy storm, I used the snow thrower to clear the driveway.
He bought a new snow thrower before winter started.
The snow thrower makes removing deep snow much faster than shoveling by hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snow blower - a machine that removes snow, often used synonymously with snow thrower
