cagelike

adjective
resembling a cage
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪdʒˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈkeɪdʒˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of a cage, especially in being enclosed or barred

Examples

Cagelike enclosure
The animal was kept in a cagelike enclosure for observation.
Sunlight filtered through the cagelike bars of the old window.
She felt trapped in the cagelike structure of her small apartment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enclosed - surrounded or shut in on all sides
  • antonym: open - not enclosed or covered
