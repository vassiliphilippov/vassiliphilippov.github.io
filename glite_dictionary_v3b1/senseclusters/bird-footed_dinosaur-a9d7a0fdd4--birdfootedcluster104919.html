bird-footed dinosaur

noun
carnivorous dinosaurs with bird-like feet
8.0 8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈbɜːrd.fʊt.ɪd ˈdaɪ.nə.sɔːr/ /; / /ˈbɜːrd.fʊt.əd ˈdaɪ.nə.sɔːr/ (AmE), /ˈbɜːd.fʊt.ɪd ˈdaɪ.nə.sɔː/ /; / /ˈbɜːd.fʊt.əd ˈdaɪ.nə.sɔː/ (BrE)
any of various carnivorous dinosaurs from the Triassic to Cretaceous periods, characterized by short forelimbs and strong hind legs used for walking or running
