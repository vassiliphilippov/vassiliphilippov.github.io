transculturation

noun
cultural blending process
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænzˌkʌltʃəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtrænzˌkʌltʃəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which two or more cultures interact, exchange, and blend to create new, hybrid cultural forms or practices

  • antonym: isolation - the state of being separated from others, especially culturally
