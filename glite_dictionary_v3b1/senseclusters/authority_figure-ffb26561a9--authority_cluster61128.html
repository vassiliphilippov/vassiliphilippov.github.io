authority figure

noun
person with recognized authority or power
Pronunciation: / əˈθɔrəti ˌfɪɡjər / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɒrəti ˌfɪɡə / (BrE)
a person who is regarded as having power or control, especially because of their position, expertise, or influence

Examples

An authority figure in school
As a teacher, she quickly became an authority figure to her students.
He played the role of an authority figure in the community, earning respect from everyone.
Many children rebel against any authority figure during their teenage years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authority - the power or right to give orders and make decisions
