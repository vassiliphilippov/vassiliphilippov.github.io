impudent

adjective
rude and disrespectful
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmpjədənt / (AmE), / ˈɪmpjʊdənt / (BrE)
rude and showing a lack of respect, especially in a bold or shameless way

Examples

An impudent child
It was impudent of him to challenge the manager in front of everyone.
She gave an impudent grin when asked about her tardiness.
The boy's impudent remarks shocked the teacher.
Despite being warned, he gave an impudent grin to the teacher.
Her impudent remark shocked everyone at the meeting.
The impudent child responded to every adult with a cheeky comment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impertinent - rude and not showing respect
  • antonym: respectful - showing politeness and respect
