Synonyms

    4.8 0.2/m
    depressive disorder is the formal clinical name doctors use in diagnoses, while depression is the common everyday label.
  • clinical depression
    3.9 0.4/m
    clinical depression is the medically diagnosed condition needing treatment, while depression is the broader term people use for lasting sadness.
  • major depressive episode
    6.1 0.02/m
