bastnaesite

noun
a rare earth mineral
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbæstnəˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈbæsnəˌsaɪt / (BrE)
A yellow to brown mineral, consisting mainly of carbonate and fluoride of cerium and other rare earth elements, and serving as a major source of rare earths.
