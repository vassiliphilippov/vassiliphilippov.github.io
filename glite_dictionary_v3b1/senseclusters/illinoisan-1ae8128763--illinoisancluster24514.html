Illinoisan

noun
native or resident of Illinois, USA
Pronunciation: / ˌɪləˈnɔɪ.ən / (AmE), / ˌɪləˈnɔɪ.ən / (BrE)
a person who was born in, or is a resident of, the U.S. state of Illinois.

Examples

An Illinoisan person
As an Illinoisan, she was proud of her state's rich history and diverse culture.
Many Illinoisans enjoy spending their weekends exploring the beautiful parks along Lake Michigan.
The company hired an Illinoisan to manage its new offices in Chicago.
