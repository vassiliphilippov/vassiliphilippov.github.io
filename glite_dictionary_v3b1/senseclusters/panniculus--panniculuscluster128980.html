panniculus

noun
small cloth covering
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈnɪkjələs / (AmE), / pəˈnɪkjʊləs / (BrE)
an old term for a small cloth or covering, especially used to wrap something or someone

Examples

A cloth panniculus
She wrapped the infant in a soft panniculus before placing him in the cradle.
In ancient times, a panniculus was often used to protect valuables from dust.
The historian described how a panniculus served as a simple garment or covering.
