gimcrack

noun
cheap decorative trinket
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪm.kræk / (AmE), / ˈɡɪm.kræk / (BrE)
A showy but worthless or cheap ornament; a trinket or bauble of little value.

Examples

A gimcrack trinket
He dismissed the figurines as mere gimcracks not worth keeping.
She filled her shelves with gimcracks collected from flea markets.
The mantel was cluttered with old gimcracks and dusty photographs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bauble - a small, inexpensive decorative item
  • synonym: trinket - a small decorative object of little value
