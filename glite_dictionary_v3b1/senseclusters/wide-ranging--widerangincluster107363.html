wide-ranging

adjective
covering a large area or variety
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪdˌreɪndʒɪŋ /; / waɪd ˈreɪndʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪdˌreɪndʒɪŋ /; / waɪd ˈreɪndʒɪŋ / (BrE)
covering or including a large variety of subjects, ideas, or areas; extensive or comprehensive in scope

Examples

Her interests are wide-ranging, from classical literature to quantum physics.

The government announced a wide-ranging series of reforms affecting many sectors.
Her interests are wide-ranging, from classical music to mountain climbing.
The government is introducing wide-ranging reforms affecting nearly every sector.
The report offered wide-ranging solutions to the company’s financial problems.
