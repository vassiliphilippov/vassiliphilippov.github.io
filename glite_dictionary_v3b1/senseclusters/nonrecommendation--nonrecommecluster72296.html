nonrecommendation

noun
refusal to recommend
8.2
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.rek.ə.mən.ˈdeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /nə.rek.ə.mən.ˈdeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /nɒn.rek.ə.mən.ˈdeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /nə.rek.ə.mən.ˈdeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a formal statement or decision not to recommend, support, or endorse someone or something

Examples

A nonrecommendation letter
The manager issued a nonrecommendation after considering the employee's performance.
Receiving a nonrecommendation can make it difficult to find a new job.
She was disappointed when her professor sent a nonrecommendation instead of a reference letter.
The committee issued a nonrecommendation regarding the new policy, suggesting it should not be implemented.
After reviewing the evidence, the board's nonrecommendation influenced the final decision to reject the proposal.
A nonrecommendation from the expert panel led the company to reconsider its investment plans.
The committee's nonrecommendation left the final decision to the board.
Due to a lack of evidence, the panel issued a nonrecommendation on the proposed policy.
A nonrecommendation does not mean disapproval; it simply means no advice is given.
