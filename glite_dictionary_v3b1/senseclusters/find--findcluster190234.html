find

verb
declare a legal verdict
Pronunciation: / faɪnd / (AmE), / faɪnd / (BrE)
(of a court, judge, or jury) to decide on and declare a verdict after consideration of the case.

Examples

Court finds him guilty
He was found guilty on all charges following a three-week trial.
If the evidence is insufficient, the court may find the accused not guilty.
The jury found the defendant guilty of fraud after only two hours of deliberation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acquit - to declare not guilty
  • synonym: convict - to declare guilty of a crime
