interstage

adjective
between two stages
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌsteɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɪntəˌsteɪdʒ / (BrE)
situated or occurring between two stages, especially as an intermediate part in a multi-stage system

Examples

Interstage connection
The engineer checked the interstage connections to ensure smooth operation between the rocket's phases.
An interstage filter helps reduce noise between the two amplifier stages in the circuit.
Proper interstage design is crucial for the efficiency of multi-stage chemical processes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intermediate - occurring between two points, stages, or things
