sickie

noun
person faking illness
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪki / (AmE), / ˈsɪki / (BrE)
a person who pretends to be ill in order to avoid work or responsibilities

Examples

He is a sickie
After missing work again, his colleagues started calling him a sickie behind his back.
The manager warned that anyone caught being a sickie would face disciplinary action.
She was unfairly labeled a sickie even though she had a genuine medical condition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hardworker - a person who works diligently and does not avoid duties
  • synonym: malingerer - a person who pretends to be sick to avoid work
  • synonym: shirker - a person who avoids work or duty
