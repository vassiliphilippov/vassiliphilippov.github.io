sparrowlike

adjective
resembling a sparrow
Pronunciation: / ˈspæroʊˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈspærəʊˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a sparrow, especially in appearance or behavior

Examples
