traveler's tree

noun
tropical plant with fan-shaped leaves, also called traveler's palm
8.5 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈtræv.ə.lərz triː/ /; / /ˈtræv.ə.lərz tʃriː/ (AmE), /ˈtræv.ə.ləz triː/ /; / /ˈtræv.ə.ləz tʃriː/ (BrE)
a tall tropical, palm-like plant (Ravenala madagascariensis) native to Madagascar, with fan-shaped leaves, known for its edible nuts and for leafstalks that store clear sap which can provide drinkable water in emergencies; reputedly used by travelers as an emergency water source
