contributorily

adverb
in a causing way
7.8
Pronunciation: / kənˈtrɪbjətərɪli / (AmE), / kənˈtrɪbjʊtərɪli / (BrE)
in a way that helps to cause or add to something

Examples

Acts contributorily to damage
The faulty wiring acted contributorily to the fire that damaged the building.
The judge ruled that the patient's actions were contributorily responsible for the outcome.
Several factors worked contributorily to cause the unexpected failure of the system.
She participated contributorily in the group discussion, offering several useful ideas.
Each member worked contributorily to ensure the project's success.
He acted contributorily by sharing his expertise with the team.

  • synonym: helpfully - in a way that provides help or assistance
