glisten

verb
shine with a soft, wet-looking light
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɪsən / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪsən / (BrE)
to shine with a soft reflected light, as if wet or polished

Examples

To glisten with light
Freshly washed cars glisten in the showroom, attracting potential buyers.
Her lips glistened after she applied a shiny lip gloss.
The morning dew made the grass glisten under the rising sun.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - not shiny; lacking brightness or sheen
  • synonym: gleam - shine brightly, especially with reflected light
  • synonym: glitter - shine with a bright, shimmering, reflected light
