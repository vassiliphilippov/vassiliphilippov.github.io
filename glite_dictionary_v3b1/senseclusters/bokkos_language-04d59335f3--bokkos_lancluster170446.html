Bokkos language

noun
a Chadic language of northern Nigeria
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊkɒs ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbɒkɒs ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken primarily in Plateau State, Nigeria, particularly in Bokkos, Barakin-Ladi, and Mangu areas

Examples

Bokkos language in Nigeria
A dictionary of the Bokkos language was recently published to support language preservation efforts.
Linguists have been studying the Bokkos language to better understand its Chadic roots and structure.
Many people in Plateau State continue to use the Bokkos language in daily conversation.
