unprimed

adjective
without primer
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpraɪmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpraɪmd / (BrE)
not coated with a primer or preparatory base layer before painting or finishing

Examples

Unprimed canvas surface
The artist preferred to work on unprimed canvas for a more natural texture.
Leaving the wood unprimed can result in uneven paint absorption and a rough finish.
Builders warned that unprimed surfaces might not hold paint as well over time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: primed - coated with a primer or preparatory base layer
