stick in the mud

noun
a person who resists change or excitement
3.8 6.4
Pronunciation: /stɪk ɪn ðə mʌd/ /; / /stɪk ɪn ðiː mʌd/ (AmE), /stɪk ɪn ðə mʌd/ /; / /stɪk ɪn ðiː mʌd/ (BrE)
