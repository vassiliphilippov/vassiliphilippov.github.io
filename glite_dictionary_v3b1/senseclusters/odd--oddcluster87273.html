odd

adjective
approximately more than (number)
Pronunciation: / ɑd / (AmE), / ɒd / (BrE)
(of a number) about or more than the specified number; in addition to the number stated, but not exactly known.

Examples

Fifty-odd years
He lived in Paris for ten-odd years before coming back home.
They collected thirty-odd signatures on the petition.
We had fifty-odd people show up for the meeting.
