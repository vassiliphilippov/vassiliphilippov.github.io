rainout

noun
removal by rain
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪnˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈreɪnˌaʊt / (BrE)
the process by which rain removes airborne particles, pollutants, or gases from the atmosphere and deposits them on the ground

Examples

Pollutant rainout
Scientists measured the rainout of pollutants after the heavy storm to understand its impact on air quality.
The rainout process helps clear harmful particles from the atmosphere during periods of frequent rainfall.
After the volcanic eruption, rainout contributed to the removal of ash from the air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deposition - the process of substances being laid down or deposited
  • synonym: washout - the removal of substances from the air or soil by water
