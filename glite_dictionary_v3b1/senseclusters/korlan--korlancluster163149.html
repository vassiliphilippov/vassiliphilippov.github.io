korlan

noun
sour fruit from the korlan tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔr.læn / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.læn / (BrE)
A small, sour, red or purple fruit from the korlan tree (Nephelium hypoleucum), native to Southeast Asia.

Examples

A korlan fruit
Farmers harvest korlan during the rainy season in northern Thailand.
She tasted the korlan and was surprised by its tangy flavor.
The market stall was selling fresh korlans alongside rambutans and lychees.
