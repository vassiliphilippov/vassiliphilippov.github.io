guessable

adjective
easy to figure out
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɛsəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɛsəbəl / (BrE)
able to be figured out, predicted, or discovered from available information or clues

Examples

A guessable answer
The answer to the riddle was so guessable that everyone got it right away.
Make sure your PIN is not easily guessable to protect your account.
The plot twist was too guessable and did not surprise the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obvious - easily seen or understood
  • synonym: predictable - able to be foreseen or anticipated
  • antonym: unpredictable - not able to be foreseen or anticipated
