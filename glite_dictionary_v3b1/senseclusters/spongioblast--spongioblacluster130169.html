spongioblast

noun
embryonic neuroglia-forming cell
Pronunciation: / ˈspʌndʒiəˌblæst / (AmE), / ˈspʌndʒɪəˌblɑːst / (BrE)
a columnar epithelial cell in the developing central nervous system that matures into neuroglial cells
