feminize

verb
induce female flowers / produce feminized seeds
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfe.mɪ.naɪz/ (AmE), /ˈfe.mɪ.naɪz/ (BrE)
(in horticulture/plant breeding) to induce plants or flowers to develop female reproductive organs or to treat plants so that the seeds they produce are female (feminized seeds).

Examples

To feminize plants
Commercial growers often feminise cannabis crops by applying hormones to produce seeds that yield female plants.
To ensure a high percentage of bud-producing plants, the lab feminised selected specimens before seed collection.
Home gardeners avoid accidental pollination by feminising mother plants to obtain reliable feminized seeds for propagation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feminize - American spelling; used interchangeably, especially in technical horticultural contexts.
