knight of the square flag

noun
a knight entitled to display a square banner
2.6
Pronunciation: / naɪt əv ðə skwɛr flæɡ / (AmE), / naɪt əv ðə skweə flæɡ / (BrE)
A knight recognized for exceptional valor, permitted to display a square banner and hold a higher command in battle.

Examples

A knight of the square flag honor
Earning the title of knight of the square flag was a rare honor in medieval chivalry.
He proudly rode into battle as a knight of the square flag, his banner waving high.
The knight of the square flag led his troops with distinction during the siege.
