Archean

adjective
pertaining to the earliest geological eon or its rocks
6.2 9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈkiː.ən / (AmE), / ɑːˈkiː.ən / (BrE)
of or relating to the earliest geological eon (the Archean), or to the oldest known rocks formed during this period, roughly 4,000 to 2,500 million years ago.

Examples

Archean rocks
Geologists study Archean formations to understand early continental crust.
Many important mineral deposits are found in Archean greenstone belts.
The Archean rocks contain some of Earth's oldest fossils.
