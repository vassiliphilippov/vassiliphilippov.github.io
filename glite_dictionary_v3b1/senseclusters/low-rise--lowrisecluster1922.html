low-rise

adjective
building with few floors
5.9 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈloʊ.raɪz/ /; / /ˈlaʊ.raɪz/ (AmE), /ˈləʊ.raɪz/ /; / /ˈlaʊ.raɪz/ (BrE)
(of a building) having only one or a few floors and usually no elevator.

Examples

Low-rise building
The city has strict regulations against constructing anything taller than a low-rise building in this area.
They moved into a low-rise apartment complex with a view of the park.
Unlike the skyscrapers downtown, most schools in the suburbs are low-rise structures.
