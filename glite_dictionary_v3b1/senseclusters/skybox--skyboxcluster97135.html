skybox

noun
enclosed premium viewing area at a stadium
Pronunciation: / ˈskaɪˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌbɒks / (BrE)
an enclosed, elevated private area in a sports stadium offering premium seating and amenities for watching events

Examples

A skybox suite
Corporations often rent a skybox to entertain their clients during sporting events.
The skybox offered comfortable seating and a great view of the concert.
We watched the football game from a skybox overlooking the field.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bleachers - uncovered bench seating in a stadium, typically less expensive
  • synonym: luxury box - another term for an elevated, private viewing area at a stadium
