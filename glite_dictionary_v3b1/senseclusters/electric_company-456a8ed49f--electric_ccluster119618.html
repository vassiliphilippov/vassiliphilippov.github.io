electric company

noun
company supplying electricity
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈlek.trɪk ˈkʌm.pə.ni/ /; / /ə.ˈlek.trɪk ˈkʌm.pə.ni/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈlek.trɪk ˈkʌm.pə.ni/ /; / /ə.ˈlek.trɪk ˈkʌm.pə.ni/ (BrE)
A company or public utility that generates, transmits, or supplies electricity to consumers.

Examples

The electric company supplies power
She called the electric company to report a blackout in her neighborhood.
The electric company sent out a bill for last month's energy usage.
When there was a storm, the electric company worked quickly to restore power to homes.
