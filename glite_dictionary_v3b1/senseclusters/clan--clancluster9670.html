clan

noun
group of related families or people
Pronunciation: / klæn / (AmE), / klæn / (BrE)
A group of families or people who are related to each other, especially in Scotland, or a large family with a strong sense of shared identity.

Examples

A clan of families
In some cultures, the clan system plays a significant role in community structure.
Leslie discovered that her family belonged to the MacGregor clan in Scotland.
The clan gathered every summer for a large family reunion in the Highlands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tribe - a social group consisting of people with shared ancestry, language, culture, and name
