themeless

adjective
without a theme
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈθiːmləs / (AmE), / ˈθiːmləs / (BrE)
lacking a central theme, subject, or unifying idea; not using a predefined theme or template (especially in design, websites, or software)

Examples

Themeless design
The crossword was themeless, so each clue stood on its own.
She found the story themeless and difficult to follow.
Many modern art pieces are intentionally themeless to encourage open interpretation.
The designer created a themeless website to give it a unique appearance.
Many developers prefer a themeless approach for greater flexibility in their projects.
A themeless template allows users to build their own style from scratch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: custom - made or done to order; not using a standard template
  • antonym: thematic - having or relating to a central theme
  • antonym: themed - having a specific theme or design applied
  • synonym: unfocused - not concentrated on a single subject or idea
