skinfold

noun
fold of skin
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈskɪn.foʊld/ (AmE), /ˈskɪn.fəʊld/ (BrE)
a fold of skin (sometimes with underlying fat) on the body, often measured to estimate body fat
