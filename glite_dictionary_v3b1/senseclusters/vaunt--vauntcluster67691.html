vaunt

verb
to boast or show off
Pronunciation: / vɔnt / (AmE), / vɔːnt / (BrE)
to boast about or praise something, especially excessively or ostentatiously; to show off

Examples

To vaunt achievements
He tends to vaunt his academic credentials at every opportunity.
The company did not hesitate to vaunt its latest achievements in the press release.
Their vaunting of past victories irritated many in the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boast - talk with excessive pride and self-satisfaction
  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's importance
