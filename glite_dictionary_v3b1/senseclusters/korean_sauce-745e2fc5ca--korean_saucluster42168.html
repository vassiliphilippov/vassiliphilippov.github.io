Korean sauce

noun
sauce typical of Korean cuisine
1.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈriː.ən sɔs / (AmE), / kəˈriː.ən sɔːs / (BrE)
a sauce that is characteristic of or commonly used in Korean cuisine, often made with ingredients like soy sauce, garlic, and chili

Examples

Korean sauce with garlic
She added a touch of Korean sauce to the stir-fry for a spicy kick.
The chef recommends marinating beef in Korean sauce for extra flavor.
You can buy Korean sauce at most Asian grocery stores these days.
