glaucomatous

adjective
bluish-green color
Pronunciation: / ɡlɔːˈkɑːmətəs / (AmE), / ɡlɔːˈkəʊmətəs / (BrE)
having a dull bluish-green or grayish color; glaucous, especially in rare or literary use.
