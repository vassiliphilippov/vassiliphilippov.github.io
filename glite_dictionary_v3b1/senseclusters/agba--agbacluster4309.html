agba

noun
respected elder
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːɡbɑː / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡbɑː / (BrE)
a respected elder or senior member of a community, especially in Yoruba or Nigerian culture

Examples

Wise agba leader
The village gathered to hear the advice of the agba before making a decision.
As an agba, she was often asked to mediate family disagreements.
Respect for the agba is an important value in many Nigerian communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elder - an older, respected member of a community
  • antonym: youth - a young person, especially one not yet an adult
