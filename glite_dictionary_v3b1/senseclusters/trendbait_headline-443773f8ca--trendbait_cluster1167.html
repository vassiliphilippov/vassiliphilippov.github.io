trendbait headline

noun
headline designed to attract attention using trends
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛndˌbeɪt ˈhɛdˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈtrɛndˌbeɪt ˈhɛd.laɪn / (BrE)
a headline created to catch attention by referencing popular trends or topics

Examples

Trendbait headline attracts clicks
He criticized the magazine for its reliance on trendbait headlines that mislead readers.
Journalists are encouraged to avoid trendbait headlines in favor of accurate reporting.
Many online news sites use trendbait headlines to increase traffic to their articles.
