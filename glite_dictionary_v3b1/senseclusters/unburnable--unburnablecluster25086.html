unburnable

adjective
cannot be burned
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɜrnəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɜːnəbl / (BrE)
not able or suitable to be burned or set on fire

Examples

Batteries are unburnable and should never be thrown into a fire or incinerator.
