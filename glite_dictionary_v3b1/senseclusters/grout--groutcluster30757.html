grout

verb
to fill with grout
Pronunciation: / ɡraʊt / (AmE), / ɡraʊt / (BrE)
to fill the spaces between tiles or seal joins with grout, a fluid cement or mortar

Examples

To grout tiles
After setting the tiles, you should grout the gaps to finish the floor.
Remember to thoroughly grout all corners to prevent water leaks.
The contractor will grout around the bathtub this afternoon.
