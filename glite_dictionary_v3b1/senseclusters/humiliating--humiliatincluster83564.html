humiliating

adjective
causing embarrassment or shame
Pronunciation: / hjuˈmɪliˌeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / hjʊˈmɪlieɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
making you feel embarrassed or ashamed by causing you to realize your faults or limitations

Examples

A humiliating mistake
It was humiliating to admit that he had made such a basic mistake.
Losing to such an inexperienced team was a truly humiliating defeat for the players.
She found the public apology deeply humiliating and could barely hold back her tears.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exalting - making you feel very proud or honored
  • synonym: humbling - making you feel less important or proud
