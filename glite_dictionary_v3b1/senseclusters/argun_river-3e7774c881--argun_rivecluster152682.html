Argun River

proper noun
river on China-Russia border
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrɡən ˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡʊn ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in eastern Asia that originates in China, forms part of the border between China and Russia, and flows into the Amur River

Examples

The Argun River border
