insurance company

noun
company that sells insurance
1.6 2.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈʃʊrəns ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃʊərəns ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
A company or financial institution that sells insurance policies and provides coverage to policyholders in exchange for premiums.

Examples

An insurance company sells policies
After her car accident, she filed a claim with her insurance company.
Many people compare rates from different insurance companies before purchasing a policy.
The insurance company sent an adjuster to assess the damage to our home.
