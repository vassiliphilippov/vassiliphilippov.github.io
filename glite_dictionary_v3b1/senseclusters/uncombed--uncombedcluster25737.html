uncombed

adjective
not combed (of hair)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkoʊmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkəʊmd / (BrE)
(of hair) not made neat or straightened by combing; not combed.

Examples

Uncombed hair
His uncombed appearance made him look as if he had just woken up.
She rushed out of the house with her uncombed hair flying in all directions.
The child's uncombed curls gave her a wild, carefree look.

Related Terms

  • antonym: combed - (of hair) made neat or arranged with a comb
