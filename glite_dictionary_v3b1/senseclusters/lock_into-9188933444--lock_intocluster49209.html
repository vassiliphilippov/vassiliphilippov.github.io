lock into

verb
be trapped or bound by a situation or contract
1.6 4.7
Pronunciation: /lɑːk ˈɪn.tuː/ /; / /lɑːk ˈɪn.tə/ (AmE), /lɒk ˈɪn.tuː/ /; / /lɒk ˈɪn.tə/ (BrE)
