egg en cocotte

noun
egg baked in cream or butter in a ramekin
Pronunciation: / ɛɡ ɑ̃ koʊˈkɔt / (AmE), / ɛɡ ɑ̃ kɒˈkɒt / (BrE)
An egg individually baked or steamed with cream or butter in a small dish such as a ramekin.

Examples

An egg en cocotte dish
For a special breakfast, she prepared an egg en cocotte topped with fresh herbs.
He impressed his guests by serving classic eggs en cocotte as a starter.
The brunch menu featured an egg en cocotte with wild mushrooms and truffle oil.
