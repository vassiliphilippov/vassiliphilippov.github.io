ultrasensitive

adjective
requiring special care
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrəˈsɛnsətɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈsɛnsətɪv / (BrE)
requiring special protection or very careful handling because exposure or disclosure could cause harm or risk.

Examples

Ultrasensitive materials
The laboratory keeps all ultrasensitive materials in a locked, climate-controlled vault.
Access to ultrasensitive information is limited to a few trusted employees.
The government classified the report as ultrasensitive due to national security concerns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confidential - meant to be kept secret or private
  • synonym: secure - protected against danger or threat
  • degree lower: sensitive - easily affected or requiring careful handling
