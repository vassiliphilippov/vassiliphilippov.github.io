presentably

adverb
in a suitable or acceptable manner
8.0
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɛntəbli / (AmE), / prɪˈzɛntəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is suitable or acceptable to be seen; in a presentable manner

Examples

To appear presentably
Despite the chaos, he managed to appear presentably at the meeting.
She made sure to dress presentably for the important job interview.
The room was presentably arranged before the guests arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: presentable - acceptable in appearance; fit to be seen
  • antonym: unpresentably - in a way not suitable to be seen
