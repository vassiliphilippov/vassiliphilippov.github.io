water witch

noun
female who finds water with a divining rod
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ˌwɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌwɪtʃ / (BrE)
a woman who claims to find underground water using a divining rod or similar method

Examples

A water witch locates water
Local farmers hired a water witch to locate a good spot for their new well.
Many regarded the water witch with a mix of skepticism and fascination.
The water witch walked slowly across the field, her divining rod twitching in her hands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: water diviner - a person who locates water using divining methods
