pajama

verb
to put on pajamas
7.7
Pronunciation: / pəˈdʒɑːmə / (AmE), / pəˈdʒɑːmə / (BrE)
to dress someone in pajamas or to put on pajamas oneself

Examples

To pajama a child
She quickly pajamaed the toddler so he would be ready before the visitors arrived.
After his bath, he pajamaed himself and got into bed.
The children were already pajamaed when their grandparents arrived for a visit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dress - to put clothes on oneself or someone else
  • antonym: undress - to remove clothes from oneself or someone else
