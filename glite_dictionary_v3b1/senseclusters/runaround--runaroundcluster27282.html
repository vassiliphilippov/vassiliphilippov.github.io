runaround

noun
hectic activity
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnəˌraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈrʌnəˌraʊnd / (BrE)
a period of busy or hectic activity, often involving a lot of moving from place to place to get things done

Examples

A busy runaround
The last-minute changes caused a stressful runaround for the entire team.
After a morning full of runaround, she finally managed to finish all her errands.
Preparing for the festival involved a lot of runaround and coordination among volunteers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bustle - energetic and noisy activity
  • antonym: calm - a state of peace and quiet
  • synonym: commotion - a state of confused and noisy movement
