abstemious

adjective
moderate, self-restrained (food/drink)
6.3 8.3
Pronunciation: / əbˈstiːmiəs /; / æbˈstiːmiəs / (AmE), / əbˈstiːmɪəs /; / æbˈstiːmiəs / (BrE)
not allowing yourself much food or drink; showing moderation or self-restraint, especially in eating and drinking

Examples

An abstemious person
Despite the feast laid before him, John remained abstemious and ate only a small portion.
Her abstemious habits contributed greatly to her good health and longevity.
The monks led abstemious lives, avoiding excess in every aspect of their daily routine.
Doctors often recommend an abstemious diet to patients with certain health conditions.
Even during celebrations, she remained abstemious and declined a second glass of wine.
He led an abstemious lifestyle, rarely indulging in rich foods or alcohol.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indulgent - allowing more than is necessary or healthy
  • synonym: temperate - showing moderation or self-restraint, especially with alcohol or food
