cub

noun
inexperienced youth or beginner
9.2
Pronunciation: / kʌb / (AmE), / kʌb / (BrE)
an awkward or inexperienced young person, especially a boy or young man

Examples

Cub reporter
As a cub reporter, she made several mistakes on her first assignment.
He behaved like a cub during his internship, unsure of what to do next.
The older members were patient with the cub, remembering their own first days.

  • synonym: novice - a person new to or inexperienced in a field or situation
