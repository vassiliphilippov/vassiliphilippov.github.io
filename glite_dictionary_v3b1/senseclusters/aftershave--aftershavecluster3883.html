aftershave

noun
post-shaving lotion
Pronunciation: / ˈæftərˌʃeɪv / (AmE), / ˈɑːftəˌʃeɪv / (BrE)
a scented liquid or lotion applied to the skin after shaving to soothe, refresh, and add fragrance

Examples

Bottle of aftershave

The fresh scent of his new aftershave lingered in the air.
Many people choose an aftershave that soothes sensitive skin.
He splashed aftershave on his cheeks after finishing his morning shave.
The fresh scent of aftershave lingered in the bathroom long after he left.
Some people prefer an alcohol-free aftershave to avoid skin irritation.

  • synonym: lotion - a liquid preparation applied to the skin for soothing or cosmetic purposes
  • synonym: moisturizer - a cream or lotion used to keep the skin soft and hydrated
