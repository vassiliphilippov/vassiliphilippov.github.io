slabwood

noun
outer log wood
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈslæb.wʊd/ (AmE), /ˈslæb.wʊd/ (BrE)
wood cut from the outer part of a log, typically used for fuel or rough construction

Examples

Slabwood for fuel
They burned slabwood in the stove to heat the cabin.
The fence was built from rough slabwood left over from the sawmill.
Farmers often use slabwood for simple outdoor structures and sheds.
