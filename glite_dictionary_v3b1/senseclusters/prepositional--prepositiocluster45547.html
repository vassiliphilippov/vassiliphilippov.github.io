prepositional

adjective
relating to a preposition
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛpəˈzɪʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ˌprɛpəˈzɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or formed with a preposition, especially in grammar

Examples

A prepositional phrase
A prepositional phrase usually consists of a preposition and its object.
In the sentence 'on the table,' 'on the table' is a prepositional phrase.
The teacher asked the students to underline all the prepositional phrases in the paragraph.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preposition - a word used before a noun or pronoun to show direction, time, place, etc.
