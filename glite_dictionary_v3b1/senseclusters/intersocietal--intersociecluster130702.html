intersocietal

adjective
between societies
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərsəˈsaɪətəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəsəˈsaɪətəl / (BrE)
happening or existing between different societies

Examples

Intersocietal differences
The study examined intersocietal differences in attitudes toward education.
Promoting intersocietal dialogue can help reduce misunderstandings between cultures.
The organization supports intersocietal projects that encourage cooperation across borders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: international - involving two or more nations
  • antonym: intrasocietal - occurring within a single society
