patchwork

noun
mixture of disparate or incongruous ideas

A theory, argument, or system composed of miscellaneous or incongruous elements gathered from various sources.

Examples

A patchwork system
Critics argue that the policy is a patchwork of old ideas lacking unity.
Her proposal felt like a patchwork stitched from unrelated concepts.
The report presented a patchwork of theories rather than a coherent explanation.
