mother hen

noun
overprotective or caring person
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌðər hɛn /; / ˈmʌðɚ hɛn / (AmE), / ˈmʌðə hɛn / (BrE)
a person who is very caring and protective towards others, often fussing over their well-being

Examples

She is a mother hen
Ever since the new intern started, Carla has acted like a mother hen, checking on her constantly.
He tends to be a mother hen to his younger siblings, making sure they have everything they need.
Sometimes, being a mother hen can make people feel smothered rather than supported.
As the team's mother hen, she made sure everyone had lunch and enough rest.
At the office, Mark is a real mother hen, always checking that his colleagues are okay.
Try not to be such a mother hen—your kids need to learn independence.
