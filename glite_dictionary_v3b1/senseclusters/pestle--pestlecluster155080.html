pestle

noun
club-shaped grinding tool for use in a mortar
5.3 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpe.səl/ (AmE), /ˈpe.səl/ (BrE)
a heavy, club-shaped tool with a rounded end, used for crushing and grinding substances in a mortar

Examples

A pestle and mortar
After adding garlic to the bowl, he worked it with the pestle until smooth.
She used a pestle to crush the spices into a fine powder.
The pharmacist ground the pills using a mortar and pestle.
In traditional medicine, herbs are often ground using a pestle.
She ground the spices with a pestle and mortar before adding them to the curry.
The chef used a pestle to crush the garlic into a fine paste.

Related Terms
