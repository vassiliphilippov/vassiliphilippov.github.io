pericardial

adjective
relating to the pericardium around the heart
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛrɪˈkɑrdiəl / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪˈkɑːdiəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated around the pericardium, the membrane enclosing the heart.

Examples

Pericardial effusion
A pericardial effusion was detected during the echocardiogram.
Excess fluid in the pericardial space can cause cardiac tamponade.
The surgeon examined the pericardial sac for signs of inflammation.
