political hack

noun
unscrupulous or self-serving politician
5.6 6.9
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈlɪ.tɪ.kəl hæk/ /; / /pə.ˈlɪ.tə.kəl hæk/ (AmE), /pə.ˈlɪ.tɪ.kəl hæk/ /; / /pə.ˈlɪ.tə.kəl hæk/ (BrE)
