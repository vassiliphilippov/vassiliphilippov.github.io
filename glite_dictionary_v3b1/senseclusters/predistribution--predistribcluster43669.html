predistribution

noun
advance distribution
8.2
Pronunciation: /priː.dɪs.trɪ.ˈbjuː.ʃən/ /; / /prə.dɪs.trɪ.ˈbjuː.ʃən/ (AmE), /priː.dɪs.trɪ.ˈbjuː.ʃən/ /; / /prə.dɪs.trɪ.ˈbjuː.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or process of distributing goods, resources, or materials in advance of when they are needed or before a main distribution

Examples

Predistribution of supplies
The government planned a predistribution of emergency supplies to remote areas before the hurricane season.
By arranging the predistribution of textbooks, the school ensured students had materials on the first day.
A predistribution of food packages helped families prepare for the upcoming winter storm.

  • synonym: distribution - the act of giving or delivering something to people
  • antonym: withholding - the act of holding something back or delaying its release
