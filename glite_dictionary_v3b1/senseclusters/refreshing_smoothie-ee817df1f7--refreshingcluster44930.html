refreshing smoothie

noun
cold fruit drink; thirst-quenching
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfrɛʃɪŋ ˈsmuːði / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɛʃɪŋ ˈsmuːði / (BrE)
A cold, blended beverage made from fruits that is particularly thirst-quenching and invigorating.

Examples

A refreshing smoothie drink
After her run, she cooled down with a refreshing smoothie bursting with tropical flavors.
On a hot afternoon, nothing beats a refreshing smoothie made from fresh berries and ice.
The café menu offered a signature refreshing smoothie that quickly became a customer favorite.
