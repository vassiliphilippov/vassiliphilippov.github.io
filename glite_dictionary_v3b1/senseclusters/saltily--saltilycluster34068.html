saltily

adverb
with irritation or salt
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːltɪli / (AmE), / ˈsɒltɪli / (BrE)
in a salty manner, either literally with salt or figuratively with irritation or sharpness

Examples

Reply saltily to questions
He answered saltily when questioned about his late arrival.
The soup was seasoned saltily, making it too strong for some guests.
She looked at him saltily after his sarcastic comment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blandly - in a mild or unexciting way
  • synonym: irritably - in an annoyed or easily angered way
