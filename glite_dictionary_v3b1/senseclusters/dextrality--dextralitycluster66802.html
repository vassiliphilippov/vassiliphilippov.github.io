dextrality

noun
preference for using right hand
8.5
Pronunciation: / dɛkˈstræləti / (AmE), / dɛkˈstralɪti / (BrE)
the preference for using the right hand over the left

Examples

Strong dextrality
Her strong dextrality made her favor the right hand in almost every task.
Studies have shown that dextrality is more common in the general population than left-handedness.
The artist's dextrality was evident in the precise brushstrokes she created with her right hand.

Related Terms

  • antonym: left-handedness - preference for using the left hand
  • synonym: right-handedness - use of the right hand more skillfully or easily
