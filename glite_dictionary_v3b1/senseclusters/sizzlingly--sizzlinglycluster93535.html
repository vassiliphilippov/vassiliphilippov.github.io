sizzlingly

adverb
with a sizzling sound
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪzəlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsɪzəlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that produces or is accompanied by a sizzling, hissing, or crackling sound or sensation.

Examples

Sizzlingly loud sound
The steak was sizzlingly hot when it arrived at the table.
The summer air was sizzlingly warm, making it hard to stay outside for long.
The wires sparked sizzlingly as they touched the metal surface.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noisily - in a loud or noticeable way
  • antonym: quietly - in a way that makes little or no noise
