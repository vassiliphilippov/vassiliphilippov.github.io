intend

verb
to have as a plan or purpose
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛnd / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛnd / (BrE)
to have something in mind as a plan or purpose; to plan or mean to do something

Examples

To intend to call
I intended to call you yesterday, but I got busy with work.
She intends to study medicine at university next year.
They intend this program to benefit the entire community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forget - to fail to remember or intend to do something
  • synonym: mean - to have as a purpose or intention
  • synonym: plan - to intend to do something; to have a scheme for
