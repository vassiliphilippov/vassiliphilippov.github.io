nonlocalized

adjective
not limited to one area
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈloʊkəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈləʊkəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not restricted to or occurring in a specific place or area

Examples

Nonlocalized pain
The doctor explained that the pain was nonlocalized and could not be traced to a single organ.
A nonlocalized rash appeared on several parts of her body at once.
Unlike a tumor in one spot, a nonlocalized infection can be harder to treat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generalized - affecting all or most parts rather than a specific area
  • antonym: localized - restricted to a particular place or area
