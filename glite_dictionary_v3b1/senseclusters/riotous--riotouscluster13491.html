(of plants or growth) produced or growing in extreme abundance; luxuriant or exuberant.

Examples

Riotous growth
After the spring rain, the countryside erupted in riotous greenery.
The garden was filled with riotous blossoms of every color imaginable.
Vines grew in riotous profusion along the cottage walls.

Synonyms

  • luxuriant
    6.0 0.39/m
    luxuriant is thick and richly growing, while riotous describes growth that is extremely abundant and exuberant.
  • exuberant
    5.8 0.62/m
    exuberant shows lively, energetic growth, while riotous stresses large, luxuriant abundance in plants or growth.
  • lush
    3.7 6.12/m
    lush looks rich, green, and full of life, while riotous means growing in extreme abundance and wild richness.
  • floriferous
    8.2 0.04/m
    floriferous bears many flowers in showy display, while riotous means general, extreme abundance of growth not only flowers.
  • rampant
    6.4 0.35/m
    rampant spreads quickly and often out of control, while riotous focuses on lush, abundant growth without that sense of control.
  • profuse
    6.5 0.38/m
    profuse refers to large or excessive amounts in many contexts, while riotous specifically describes luxuriant plant growth.
