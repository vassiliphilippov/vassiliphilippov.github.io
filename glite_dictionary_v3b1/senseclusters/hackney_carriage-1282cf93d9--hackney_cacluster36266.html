hackney carriage

noun
carriage or taxi for public hire
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhækni ˈkærɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhakni ˈkarɪdʒ / (BrE)
A carriage, originally horse-drawn and now often referring to a taxi, that is available for hire by the public.

Examples

A hackney carriage taxi
Many Londoners rely on the traditional hackney carriage for safe, regulated transport.
She hailed a hackney carriage to take her across the city late at night.
The museum displayed an original hackney carriage from the Victorian era.
