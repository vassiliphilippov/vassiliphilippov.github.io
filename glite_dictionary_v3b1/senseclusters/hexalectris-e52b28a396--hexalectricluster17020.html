Hexalectris

noun
a genus of myco-heterotrophic orchids
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛksəˈlɛktrɪs /; / hɛksəˈlɛktrɪs / (AmE), / ˌhɛksəˈlɛktrɪs /; / hɛksəˈlɛktrɪs / (BrE)
a genus of orchids native to North America that are fully myco-heterotrophic (they lack chlorophyll and depend on fungi for nutrition)
