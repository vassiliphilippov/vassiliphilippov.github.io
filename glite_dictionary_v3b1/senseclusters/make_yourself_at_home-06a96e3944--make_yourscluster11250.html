make yourself at home

verb
feel comfortable and relaxed in someone else's home
Pronunciation: / meɪk jɔɹˈsɛlf æt hoʊm / (AmE), / meɪk jɔːˈsɛlf æt həʊm / (BrE)
to behave in a relaxed and comfortable way as if you were in your own home, especially when you are in someone else's house

Examples

To make yourself at home
Guests are encouraged to make yourself at home during their stay.
Please make yourself at home while I finish setting the table.
When you arrive, just make yourself at home and help yourself to anything you need.
