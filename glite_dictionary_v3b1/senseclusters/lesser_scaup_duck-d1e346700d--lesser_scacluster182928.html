lesser scaup duck

noun
North American diving duck species
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛsɚ skaʊp dʌk / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə skaʊp dʌk / (BrE)
a common North American diving duck (Aythya affinis), the male of which has a purplish head and a blue bill

The male lesser scaup duck is easily recognized by its purplish-black head and blue bill.
