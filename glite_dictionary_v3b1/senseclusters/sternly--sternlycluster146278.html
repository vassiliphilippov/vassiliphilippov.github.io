sternly

adverb
in a severe or strict manner
3.9 2/m
Pronunciation: / Ààst…ùnli / (AmE), / Ààst…úÀênli / (BrE)
In a severe, strict, or serious manner; showing sternness.

Examples

Look sternly
He looked at the students sternly and demanded absolute silence.
She sternly reminded her children to clean their rooms before going outside.
The judge spoke sternly to the defendant, warning against further misbehavior.
