Play-Doh

proper noun
children's modeling compound
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪˌdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌdəʊ / (BrE)
a brand of soft, colorful modeling compound for children to shape and mold

Examples

Play-Doh brand
The children spent the afternoon making animals out of Play-Doh.
She received a set of colorful Play-Doh for her birthday and played for hours.
Teachers often use Play-Doh in art class to encourage creativity.
