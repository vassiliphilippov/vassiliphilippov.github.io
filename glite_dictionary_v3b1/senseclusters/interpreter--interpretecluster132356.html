interpreter

noun
person who creatively expresses or represents something
3.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɜrprətər / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːprɪtə / (BrE)
a person who expresses or represents something, especially a work or subject, through art, performance, or another creative medium

Examples

Artistic interpreter
As an interpreter of ancient myths, her choreography was both original and insightful.
He was a talented interpreter of classic piano works, bringing new emotion to familiar pieces.
The artist became known as an interpreter of everyday life in her sculptures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: performer - someone who acts, sings, or otherwise presents a creative work
