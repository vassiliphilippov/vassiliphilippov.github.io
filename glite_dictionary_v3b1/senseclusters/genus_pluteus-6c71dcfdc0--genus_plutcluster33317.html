genus Pluteus

noun
genus of fungi with roof-shaped caps
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs pluːˈtiː.əs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈpluː.tjuː.əs / (BrE)
A large genus of fungi in the family Pluteaceae, characterized by cap shapes that resemble a roof and often found in abundance early in summer.
