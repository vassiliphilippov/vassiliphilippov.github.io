Not allowed to be considered or accepted, especially in a court or formal context.

Examples

Inadmissible evidence
Certain types of hearsay evidence are inadmissible in most courts.
His excuse was deemed inadmissible by the committee.
The judge ruled that the documents were inadmissible in the trial.

Synonyms

  • impermissible
    7.7 0.60/m
    impermissible is not allowed by rules or law, while inadmissible is specifically not accepted as evidence in court.
  • unallowable
    6.4 0.01/m
    unallowable is forbidden by rules or practice, while inadmissible is mainly used to say something cannot be considered in court.
  • excludable
    7.1 0.12/m
    excludable can be removed or left out under rules, while inadmissible means it cannot be accepted in court or formal review.
