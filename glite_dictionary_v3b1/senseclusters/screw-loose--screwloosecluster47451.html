screw-loose

adjective
not behaving normally; eccentric
Pronunciation: / ˈskruː luːs / (AmE), / ˈskruː luːs / (BrE)
(of a person) not behaving in a normal or sensible way; somewhat crazy or eccentric.

Examples

Has a screw-loose
Her neighbors suspect she has a screw-loose after seeing her talk to the plants every morning.
If you think that plan will work, you must have a screw-loose.
People say he’s got a screw-loose because of his wild ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or extremely eccentric
  • antonym: sane - of sound mind; not mentally ill or abnormal
