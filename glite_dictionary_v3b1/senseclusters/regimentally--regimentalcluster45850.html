regimentally

adverb
in a regimental manner; by regiments
5.8 7.2
Pronunciation: /re.dʒɪ.ˈmen.təl.ɪ/ (AmE), /re.dʒɪ.ˈmen.təl.ɪ/ (BrE)
In a way that relates to or is characteristic of a regiment or regiments.

Examples

Act regimentally strict
His authority was asserted regimentally, following military tradition to the letter.
The awards were distributed regimentally, in accordance with the established hierarchy.
The troops advanced regimentally across the field, maintaining strict order.
