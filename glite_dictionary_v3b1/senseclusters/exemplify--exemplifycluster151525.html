exemplify

verb
illustrate by an example
3.7
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɛmpləˌfaɪ /; / ɪɡˈzɛmplɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɛmplɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to be or give a clear example of something; to illustrate or show something by example

Examples

To exemplify good behavior
Her actions exemplify the company's commitment to customer service.
Several case studies exemplify the benefits of adopting new technology.
The teacher used a real-world scenario to exemplify the complex theory.
Her achievements exemplify the dedication required to succeed in this field.
The building exemplifies the best of modern architecture.
This painting perfectly exemplifies the style of the Impressionist movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarify - to make something clear or easier to understand
  • synonym: illustrate - to explain or decorate something by using pictures or examples
  • synonym: typify - to be a typical example of
