Arabism

noun
Arab unity ideology
Pronunciation: / ˈærəˌbɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈærəbɪzəm / (BrE)
a political ideology or movement that promotes unity, independence, or solidarity among Arab peoples and nations

Examples

Arabism movement
The rise of Arabism in the twentieth century influenced many political movements in the Middle East.
Some leaders promoted Arabism to encourage cooperation among different Arab countries.
Debates about Arabism often focus on its impact on regional identity and politics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nationalism - the belief in the interests and culture of one's nation
  • synonym: pan-Arabism - the advocacy of political and cultural unity among Arab countries
  • antonym: separatism - the advocacy of separation from a larger group or country
