funk track

noun
music recording in the funk genre
Pronunciation: / fʌŋk træk / (AmE), / fʌŋk træk / (BrE)
A music recording that belongs to the funk genre, typically characterized by a strong rhythmic groove and prominent bass line.

Examples

A funk track groove
He added a new funk track to his party playlist.
She enjoys dancing to any funk track with a catchy groove.
The radio station featured a classic funk track from the 1970s.
