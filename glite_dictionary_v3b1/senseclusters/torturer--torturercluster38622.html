torturer

noun
person who inflicts severe pain
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrtʃɚər / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtʃərə / (BrE)
a person who deliberately inflicts severe physical pain, usually as a form of punishment or to force information from someone

Examples

The torturer inflicts pain
During the regime, many prisoners testified about the brutal methods used by the torturer.
He confronted his former torturer in court, demanding justice for the pain inflicted.
The film depicted the torturer as a cold and ruthless character.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tormentor - someone who causes someone to suffer, especially intentionally
