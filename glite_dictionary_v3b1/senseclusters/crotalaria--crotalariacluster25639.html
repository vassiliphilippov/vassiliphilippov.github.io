crotalaria

noun
genus of plants with rattling pods
Pronunciation: / ˌkroʊtəˈlɛriə / (AmE), / ˌkrɒtəˈlɑːriə / (BrE)
Any of various plants of the genus Crotalaria, often having inflated seed pods that rattle, and used for pasture or green manure.

Examples

Crotalaria plants
Farmers often grow crotalaria to enrich the soil with vital nutrients.
Many species of crotalaria are valued as pasture plants in tropical agriculture.
The distinctive pods of crotalaria make a rattling sound when shaken by the wind.
