gauge

verb
measure capacity or volume
7.9 3.0
Pronunciation: / ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
to measure, estimate, or judge the amount, size, or quality of something

Examples

To gauge the distance
Experts gauge the volume of a container to ensure accurate billing.
