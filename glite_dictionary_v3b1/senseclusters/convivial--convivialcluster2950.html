convivial

adjective
cheerful and sociable
7.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɪviəl / (AmE), / kənˈvɪviəl / (BrE)
cheerful and sociable; enjoying friendly company and lively atmosphere.

Examples

A convivial party
She has such a convivial personality that strangers quickly become friends.
The dinner party had a convivial vibe that made everyone feel at ease.
The pub was filled with laughter and convivial conversation well into the night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gregarious - fond of company; sociable
  • synonym: sociable - enjoying the company of others
  • antonym: unsociable - not enjoying the company of others
