May blob

noun
swamp plant with yellow flowers, marsh marigold
Pronunciation: / meɪ blɑb / (AmE), / meɪ blɒb / (BrE)
A swamp plant found in Europe and North America, with bright yellow flowers resembling buttercups; commonly known as marsh marigold (Caltha palustris).

Examples

Bright May blobs
In spring, the wetlands come alive with May blobs blooming along the stream banks.
She identified the bright yellow flowers as May blobs during her nature walk.
The pond's edge was dotted with clusters of May blob flowers after the heavy rain.
