sustainable

adjective
able to be maintained or continued
1.0 3.7
Pronunciation: / səˈsteɪnəbl̩ / (AmE), / səˈsteɪnəbl̩ / (BrE)
Able to be maintained or continued over the long term without depleting resources or causing severe damage.

Examples

A sustainable lifestyle
Her lifestyle is not sustainable in the long term due to excessive spending.
The government is searching for sustainable solutions to the energy crisis.
We need to develop sustainable farming practices to protect the environment.
