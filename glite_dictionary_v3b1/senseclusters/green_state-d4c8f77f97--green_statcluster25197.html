green state

noun
environmentally sustainable condition
4.0
Pronunciation: / ɡrin steɪt / (AmE), / ɡriːn steɪt / (BrE)
a condition or status of being environmentally friendly or sustainable, especially at the level of a country, city, or organization

Examples

A green state policy
Achieving a green state requires cooperation between government, industry, and citizens.
Many businesses strive for a green state to appeal to environmentally conscious customers.
The country is aiming to reach a green state by investing in clean technology and reducing emissions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sustainable - able to be maintained without depleting resources or harming the environment
