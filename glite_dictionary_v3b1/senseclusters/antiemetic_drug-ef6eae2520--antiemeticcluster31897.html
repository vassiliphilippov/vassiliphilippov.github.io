antiemetic drug

noun
drug preventing nausea or vomiting
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.ti.ɪˈmɛt.ɪk drʌɡ / (AmE), / ˌæn.ti.ɪˈmɛt.ɪk drʌɡ / (BrE)

Examples
