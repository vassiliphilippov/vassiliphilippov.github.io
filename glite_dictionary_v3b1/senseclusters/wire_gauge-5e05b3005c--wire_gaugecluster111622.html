wire gauge

noun
tool/system for measuring wire diameter
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪɚ ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪə ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
a tool or system for measuring the diameter or thickness of wire

Examples

A wire gauge tool
A wire gauge is essential when working with different types of cables.
He checked the thickness of the wire with a wire gauge before starting the project.
The electrician used a wire gauge to determine the correct size for the wiring.
