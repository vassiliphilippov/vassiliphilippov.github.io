scrub-bird

noun
small Australian bird found in scrub
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈskrʌb bɜrd / (AmE), / ˈskrʌb bɜːd / (BrE)
a small, fast-running Australian bird resembling a wren, usually found in brush or scrub habitats

Examples

A scrub-bird in brush
Birdwatchers traveled to the national park in hopes of spotting a scrub-bird in its natural habitat.
Many conservation efforts focus on protecting the scrub-bird and its diminishing environment.
The scrub-bird is rarely seen due to its shy nature and preference for dense undergrowth.
