trove

noun
collection of valuable or discovered items
Pronunciation: / troʊv / (AmE), / trəʊv / (BrE)
a collection of valuable objects, resources, or information, especially one that is discovered or hidden

Examples

A trove of treasures
She inherited a trove of family letters dating back to the nineteenth century.
The ancient coins were part of a remarkable trove discovered by archaeologists.
The library's archives are a trove of historical documents and rare books.

Related Terms

  • synonym: treasure trove - valuable items or artifacts found hidden
