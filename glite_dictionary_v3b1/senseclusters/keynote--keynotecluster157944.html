keynote

noun
central theme or idea
4.2 0.28/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkiÀåno ät /; / ÀàkiÀêÀåno ät / (AmE), / ÀàkiÀên…ô ät / (BrE)
the central or main idea or theme of a speech, discussion, or work

Examples

The keynote of the story

The keynote of her argument was the importance of education for all.
