overvote

verb
to cast too many votes
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈvoʊt /; / ˈoʊvɚˌvoʊt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈvəʊt /; / ˈəʊvəˌvəʊt / (BrE)
to select more choices than allowed on a ballot, making the vote invalid
