camphor ice

noun
medicated camphor-based skin balm
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkæm.fər aɪs / (AmE), / ˈkæm.fə aɪs / (BrE)
A topical medicinal preparation made from camphor, wax, spermaceti, and castor oil, used for soothing the skin.
