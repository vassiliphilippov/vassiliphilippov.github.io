rarebit

noun
cheese dish served over toast
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛrbɪt / (AmE), / ˈreəbɪt / (BrE)
A dish consisting of melted cheese, often mixed with ale or beer, served over toasted bread.

Examples

Welsh rarebit dish
For lunch, she made rarebit using sharp cheddar and a splash of ale.
The cookbook includes a traditional recipe for Welsh rarebit.
We ordered a plate of rarebit at the pub, topped with spicy mustard.
