black buck

noun
Indian antelope with spiral horns
5.3 8.7
Pronunciation: / blæk bʌk / (AmE), / blæk bʌk / (BrE)
a species of antelope (Antilope cervicapra) native to the Indian subcontinent, characterized by a dark back and spiral horns, especially in the male

Examples

A black buck herd
A herd of black buck grazed peacefully in the grasslands of central India.
The male black buck is easily recognized by his striking dark coat and long spiral horns.
Wildlife photographers often visit reserves in Rajasthan to capture images of the elusive black buck.
