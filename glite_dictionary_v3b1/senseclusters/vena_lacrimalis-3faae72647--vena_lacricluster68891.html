vena lacrimalis

noun
vein draining the lacrimal gland
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə læ.krɪ.ˈmeɪ.lɪs/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə læ.krɪ.ˈmeɪ.lɪs/ (BrE)
A vein that drains blood from the lacrimal gland and empties into the superior ophthalmic vein.

Examples

The vena lacrimalis vein
Inflammation near the vena lacrimalis can lead to complications affecting the eye.
Surgeons must be careful not to damage the vena lacrimalis during eye procedures.
The vena lacrimalis is responsible for draining blood from the lacrimal gland.
