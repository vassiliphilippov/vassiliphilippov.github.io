Republican candidate

noun
candidate representing the Republican Party
Pronunciation: / rəˈpʌblɪkən ˈkændəˌdeɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪkən ˈkændɪdeɪt / (BrE)
A candidate who is representing the Republican Party in an election.

Examples

Republican candidate in election
She volunteered for the Republican candidate in the local mayoral race.
The Republican candidate promised to lower taxes if elected.
There was fierce debate between the Democratic and Republican candidate during the televised forum.
