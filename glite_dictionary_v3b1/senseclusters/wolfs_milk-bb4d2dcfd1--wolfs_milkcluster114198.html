wolf's milk

noun
a European perennial weed (leafy spurge)
Pronunciation: / wʊlfs mɪlk / (AmE), / wʊlfs mɪlk / (BrE)
A tall perennial plant native to Europe, now naturalized and considered a troublesome weed in eastern North America; also known as leafy spurge (Euphorbia esula).

Examples

Wolf's milk plant
Wolf's milk is recognized as an invasive species in many parts of North America.
Farmers struggle to control the spread of wolf's milk in their pastures.
The field was overrun with wolf's milk, making it hard for crops to grow.
