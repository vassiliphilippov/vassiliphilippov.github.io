biostatistics

noun
statistical analysis in biology
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊstəˈtɪstɪks / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊstəˈtɪstɪks / (BrE)
The branch of biology that applies statistical methods to the analysis and interpretation of biological data.

Examples

Study of biostatistics
She is studying biostatistics to prepare for a career in medical research.
The conference featured new advances in biostatistics for genetics and epidemiology.
Understanding biostatistics is essential for analyzing clinical trial results.
