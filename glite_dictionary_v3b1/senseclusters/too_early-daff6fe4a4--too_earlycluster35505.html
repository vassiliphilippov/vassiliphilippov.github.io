too early

adverb
before the expected or proper time
1.0 8.73/m
Pronunciation: / tu Àà…ùÀêli / (AmE), / tuÀê Àà…úÀêli / (BrE)

Examples

It is too early

We arrived too early for the concert, so the doors were still closed.

Synonyms

  • ahead of time
    2.3 4.70/m
    ahead of time means before a planned or usual moment, while too early implies it happens sooner than wanted or expected.
