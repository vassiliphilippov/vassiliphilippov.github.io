classifier

noun
person who organizes into categories
Pronunciation: / ˈklæs.ɪ.faɪ.ər / (AmE), / ˈklɑː.sɪ.faɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who arranges or organizes things into classes or categories

Examples

A skilled classifier
As a classifier, she developed a new system for organizing the museum's artifacts.
Every scientific field needs a skilled classifier to make sense of complex data.
The team hired an expert classifier to sort the biological samples into different species.
