Picasso

proper noun
Spanish modern artist
Pronunciation: / pɪˈkɑːsoʊ / (AmE), / pɪˈkæsəʊ / (BrE)
a renowned Spanish painter and sculptor, famous for co-founding the Cubist movement and influencing modern art

Examples

Picasso exhibition
The museum displayed several original paintings by Picasso in its modern art section.
Art students often study the unique techniques developed by Picasso throughout his career.
Many people admire the bold style of Picasso and his influence on twentieth-century art.
