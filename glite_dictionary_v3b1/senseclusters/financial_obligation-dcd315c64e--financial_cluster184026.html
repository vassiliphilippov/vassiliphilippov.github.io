financial obligation

noun
requirement to pay money owed
Pronunciation: / fəˈnænʃəl ˌɑːbləˈɡeɪʃən / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃəl ˌɒblɪˈɡeɪʃən / (BrE)
an amount of money that a person or organization is legally required to pay to another
