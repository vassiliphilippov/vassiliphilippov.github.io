copperplate

noun
engraved copper printing plate
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.pɚˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒp.əˌpleɪt / (BrE)
A smooth plate of copper that has been etched or engraved, used for printing images or text.

Examples

An engraved copperplate
Restoring the fragile copperplate revealed intricate details of the artist's original work.
She studied the technique of making a copperplate during her printmaking course.
The museum displays a nineteenth-century copperplate used for printing old maps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: etching - The process of using acid to engrave a design on a metal plate
