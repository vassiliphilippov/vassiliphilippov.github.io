primatology

noun
study of primates
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpraɪməˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌprɪməˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of primates, especially in relation to their behavior, biology, and evolution

Examples

Primatology courses
Courses in primatology often include topics such as primate evolution and conservation.
Many breakthroughs in primatology have come from long-term field studies in Africa.
She pursued a PhD in primatology to better understand the social behavior of chimpanzees.
