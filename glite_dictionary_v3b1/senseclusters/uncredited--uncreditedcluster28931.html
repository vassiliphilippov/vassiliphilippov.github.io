uncredited

adjective
not acknowledged as credit
5.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrɛdɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrɛdɪtɪd / (BrE)
not recorded, acknowledged, or applied as a credit, especially in a financial account or record

Examples

Uncredited bank payment

He was upset that his work went uncredited in the final report.
Several refunds were still uncredited by the end of the month.
