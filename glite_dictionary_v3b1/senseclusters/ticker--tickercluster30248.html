ticker

noun
on-screen scrolling display
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈtɪk.ər/ (AmE), /ˈtɪk.ə/ (BrE)
a horizontal on-screen display that continuously scrolls short items of information such as headlines, stock prices, or brief notices.

Examples

News ticker bar
The finance app shows a continuous ticker at the bottom with real-time stock prices and headlines.
Producers added a red ticker across the screen to display breaking news during the live broadcast.
She paused the video so she could read each headline on the scrolling ticker more easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: news crawl - a continuously moving line of text on a television screen showing news or other information
  • synonym: news ticker - a scrolling display used by broadcasters or apps to show news headlines or brief updates
