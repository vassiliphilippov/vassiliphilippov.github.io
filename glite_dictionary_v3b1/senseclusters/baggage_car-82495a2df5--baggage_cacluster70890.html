baggage car

noun
railway car for passengers' luggage
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɡɪdʒ kɑr / (AmE), / ˈbæɡɪdʒ kɑː / (BrE)
a railway car used for carrying passengers' luggage

Examples

A baggage car on train
Older railways often had a separate baggage car attached to the end of the train.
Porters carefully loaded trunks onto the baggage car before passengers boarded.
The train's baggage car was filled with suitcases and crates for the cross-country journey.
