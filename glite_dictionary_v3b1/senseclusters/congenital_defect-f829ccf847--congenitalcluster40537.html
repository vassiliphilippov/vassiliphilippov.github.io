congenital defect

noun
abnormality present at birth
3.0
Pronunciation: / kənˈdʒɛnɪtəl dɪˈfɛkt / (AmE), / kənˈdʒɛnɪtəl dɪˈfɛkt / (BrE)
a physical or functional abnormality that is present at birth

Examples

A congenital defect at birth
Doctors discussed the possible causes of her congenital defect with the family.
Early diagnosis of a congenital defect can improve the chances of successful treatment.
