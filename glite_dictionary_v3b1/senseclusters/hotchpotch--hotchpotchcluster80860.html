hotchpotch

noun
a stew with meat and vegetables
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtʃ.pɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɒtʃ.pɒtʃ / (BrE)
A stew or thick soup made with meat and vegetables.

Examples

A bowl of hotchpotch
After a long hike, nothing was more comforting than a bowl of steaming hotchpotch.
My grandmother used to make a delicious hotchpotch every winter using fresh garden vegetables.
We served the thick hotchpotch with crusty bread at the family gathering.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stew - a dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid
