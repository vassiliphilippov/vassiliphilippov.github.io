pettily

adverb
in a petty or trivial manner
7.8 7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛtɪli / (AmE), / ˈpɛtɪli / (BrE)

Examples

To pettily complain
He pettily insisted on following every minor rule, even when it inconvenienced others.
Sarah pettily complained about her colleague's small mistakes, rather than focusing on the bigger issues.
They argued pettily over who would sit near the window during the meeting.
