psychographic

adjective
relating to psychography
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to psychography, especially the practice of automatic writing or alleged spirit communication.

Examples

Psychographic writing session
The medium claimed her psychographic abilities allowed her to write messages from the spirit world.
During the séance, a psychographic letter was produced, supposedly written by a deceased relative.
Researchers studied the psychographic writings to understand more about the phenomenon of automatic writing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: skeptical - doubting or questioning the truth of something
  • synonym: spiritualistic - relating to belief in communication with spirits
