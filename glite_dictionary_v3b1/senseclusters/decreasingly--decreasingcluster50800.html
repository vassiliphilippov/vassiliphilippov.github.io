decreasingly

adverb
in a lessening way
3.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkriːsɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪˈkriːsɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that becomes less or smaller over time or in amount

Examples

Hiring decreasingly
The company has been hiring decreasingly as the market slows down.
Interest in the old technology is decreasingly common among younger generations.
The river flows decreasingly during the dry season each year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: increasingly - in a way that becomes greater or larger over time
