tai chi

noun
Chinese meditative physical exercise
2.4
Pronunciation: / taɪ ˈtʃi / (AmE), / taɪ ˈtʃiː / (BrE)
A Chinese system of slow, meditative physical exercise designed to promote relaxation, balance, and good health.

Examples

Tai chi exercise
Her doctor recommended tai chi as a gentle form of exercise after her surgery.
Many people practice tai chi in the mornings to improve their flexibility and reduce stress.
The community center offers tai chi classes for seniors every Tuesday and Thursday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: qigong - A Chinese system of physical exercises and breathing control related to tai chi
