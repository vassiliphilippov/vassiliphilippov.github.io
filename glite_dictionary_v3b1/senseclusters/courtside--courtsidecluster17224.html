courtside

noun
area beside court
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrtˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːtˌsaɪd / (BrE)
the area or seats located directly beside a sports court, often reserved for spectators, officials, or photographers

Examples

A courtside view
She was thrilled to have courtside seats for the basketball game.
Reporters gathered courtside to interview the players after the final whistle.
From his courtside position, the referee had a clear view of the action.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sideline - the line or area marking the side boundary of a sports field or court
  • degree higher: stands - the seating area for spectators at a sports event
