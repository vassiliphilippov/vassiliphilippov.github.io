ocean

noun
major saltwater body on Earth
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈəʊʃən / (BrE)
A very large area of salt water that covers most of the Earth's surface, separating continents.

Examples

The ocean waves
After months at sea, the sailors were overjoyed to see the vast ocean stretching before them.
She dreams of studying marine life in the ocean one day.
The Pacific ocean is the largest and deepest of the world's oceans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sea - a large body of salt water smaller than an ocean, often partly enclosed by land
