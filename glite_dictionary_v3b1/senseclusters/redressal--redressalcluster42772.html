redressal

noun
compensation or remedy
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdrɛsəl / (AmE), / rɪˈdrɛsəl / (BrE)
the act of correcting or compensating for a wrong, grievance, or complaint

Examples

Complaint redressal
The government set up a committee to ensure redressal of citizens' complaints.
Victims of the accident demanded redressal for their losses.
The company offered redressal to customers affected by the service disruption.
The government set up a new system for the redressal of public grievances.
Quick redressal of customer complaints is important for maintaining trust.
She filed a petition seeking redressal for the unfair treatment she received.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compensation - something, typically money, awarded to someone as a recompense for loss or injury
  • antonym: injustice - an unfair act or treatment
  • synonym: remedy - a means of legal reparation or correction for a wrong
