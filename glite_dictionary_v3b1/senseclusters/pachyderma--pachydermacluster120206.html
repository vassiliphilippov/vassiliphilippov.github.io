pachyderma

noun
abnormal thickening of the skin
Pronunciation: / ˌpæk.ɪˈdɝː.mə / (AmE), / ˌpæk.ɪˈdɜː.mə / (BrE)
abnormal thickening of the skin, especially due to congenital enlargement or obstruction of lymph vessels
