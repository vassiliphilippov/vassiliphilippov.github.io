sun pitcher

noun
tropical insectivorous pitcher plant
6.5 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌn ˌpɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈsʌn ˌpɪtʃə / (BrE)
any of several tropical South American pitcher plants with racemes of nodding white or pink flowers, which trap and digest insects in pitcher-shaped leaves with spoon-shaped caps.

Examples

The sun pitcher plant
Botanists study the sun pitcher to better understand the evolution of insectivorous plants.
During our hike in the Guiana highlands, we spotted a rare sun pitcher among the rocks.
The sun pitcher is known for its remarkable ability to trap and digest insects.
