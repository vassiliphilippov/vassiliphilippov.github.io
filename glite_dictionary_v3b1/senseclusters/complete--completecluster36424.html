complete

adjective
finished; brought to an end
1.0 42/m
Pronunciation: / k…ômÀàplit / (AmE), / k…ômÀàpliÀêt / (BrE)
having come to an end; finished or concluded

Examples

A complete project
After years of work, the project is finally complete.
The complete report was submitted ahead of the deadline.
The artist declared the painting complete after adding the final touches.
