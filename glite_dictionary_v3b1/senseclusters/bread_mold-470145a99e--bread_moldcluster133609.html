bread mold

noun
a fungus (Rhizopus) that grows on bread
6.3
Pronunciation: / brɛd moʊld / (AmE), / brɛd məʊld / (BrE)
a common fungus of the genus Rhizopus that grows on bread and similar foods, often appearing as a fuzzy, grayish growth

Examples

Fuzzy bread mold
Scientists often use bread mold to study basic biological processes.

The slice of bread was covered in bread mold after sitting out for a week.
