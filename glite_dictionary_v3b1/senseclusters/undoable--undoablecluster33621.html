undoable

adjective
cannot be reversed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈduːəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈduːəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to reverse, cancel, or undo once completed

Examples

Undoable change
Be careful with this action, as it is undoable and cannot be changed later.
Some mistakes are undoable, so it is important to think before acting.
Deleting your account is an undoable process, so make sure you really want to proceed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irreversible - impossible to change back to a previous state
  • antonym: reversible - able to be changed back to a previous state
