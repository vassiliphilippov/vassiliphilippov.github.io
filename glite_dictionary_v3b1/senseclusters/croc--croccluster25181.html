croc

noun
plastic casual shoe
5.1
Pronunciation: / krɑk / (AmE), / krɒk / (BrE)
an informal name for a lightweight, molded plastic shoe made by the company Crocs, often worn for comfort or casual use

Examples

A pair of crocs
She slipped on her favorite pink crocs before heading out to the garden.
Many people wear crocs because they are comfortable and easy to clean.
He found a single croc under the couch after cleaning the living room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boot - a type of shoe that covers the ankle or higher
  • synonym: sandal - an open type of shoe with straps
  • synonym: slipper - a comfortable, casual shoe for indoor use
