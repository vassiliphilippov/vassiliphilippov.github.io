avouch

verb
affirm or assert openly
Pronunciation: / əˈvaʊtʃ / (AmE), / əˈvaʊtʃ / (BrE)
to affirm or assert openly and bluntly; to declare or admit without hesitation

Examples

To avouch innocence
He did not hesitate to avouch his innocence before the entire court.
She will readily avouch the facts as she witnessed them.
They avouched that they had completed the task honestly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affirm - to state something as true with confidence
  • antonym: deny - to declare something is not true
