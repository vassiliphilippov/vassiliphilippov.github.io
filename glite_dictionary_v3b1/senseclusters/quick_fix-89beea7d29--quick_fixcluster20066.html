quick fix

noun
hasty or temporary solution
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /kwɪk fɪks/ (AmE), /kwɪk fɪks/ (BrE)
A solution or repair done hastily and temporarily, often to address a problem quickly rather than thoroughly.

Examples

A quick fix solution
Politicians often look for a quick fix instead of addressing the root causes of economic problems.
That patch is just a quick fix; it won't hold up for long in heavy rain.
The mechanic offered a quick fix for the leaking radiator, but advised a more permanent repair later.

Related Terms

  • antonym: permanent solution - a solution intended to last and fully resolve a problem
  • synonym: stopgap - a temporary way of dealing with a problem
