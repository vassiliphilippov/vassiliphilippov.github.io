kinesis

noun
biological movement in response to stimulus
7.2 7.6
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈniː.sɪs/ /; / /kɪ.ˈniː.sɪs/ (AmE), /kə.ˈniː.sɪs/ /; / /kɪ.ˈniː.sɪs/ (BrE)
movement of an organism or cell in response to a stimulus, without a specific direction toward or away from the source

Examples
