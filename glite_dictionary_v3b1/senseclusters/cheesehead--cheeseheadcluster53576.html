cheesehead

noun
foam cheese hat
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːzˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzˌhɛd / (BrE)
a novelty foam hat shaped like a wedge of cheese, often worn by sports fans to show support for their team, especially in Wisconsin

Examples

Foam cheesehead hat
He proudly wore his cheesehead to every Green Bay Packers game.
Tourists often buy a cheesehead as a souvenir when visiting Wisconsin.
The crowd was filled with fans wearing a bright yellow cheesehead on their heads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mascot - an object or figure used as a symbol for a group or team
