last-minute

adjective
done just before a deadline
Pronunciation: / læst ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / lɑːst ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
happening or done at the latest possible time, especially just before a deadline or event

Examples

A last-minute decision
Her last-minute decision to join the trip surprised everyone.
The store was crowded with last-minute shoppers on Christmas Eve.
We had to make some last-minute changes to the presentation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eleventh-hour - happening at the very last possible moment
