beech marten

noun
Eurasian marten species
Pronunciation: / bitʃ ˈmɑrtən / (AmE), / biːtʃ ˈmɑːtən / (BrE)
A Eurasian marten with a brown coat and a pale breast and throat, found in wooded and rocky areas.

Examples

The beech marten animal
Researchers spotted a beech marten near the old stone bridge last night.
The beech marten is known for its agility when climbing trees and rocks.
Unlike the pine marten, the beech marten often lives close to human settlements.
