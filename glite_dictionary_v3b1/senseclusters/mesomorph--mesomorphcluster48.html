mesomorph

noun
person with a muscular, athletic build
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛzəˌmɔɹf / (AmE), / ˈmɛzəməʊf / (BrE)
a person whose body type is characterized by a well-developed musculature and a sturdy, athletic build

Examples

A mesomorph body
Because of his natural build, the coach described him as a mesomorph ideal for football.
Many bodybuilders fall into the mesomorph category due to their muscular physiques.
The training program was specially designed for mesomorphs to maximize strength and definition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ectomorph - a person with a lean and delicate body build
  • antonym: endomorph - a person with a rounder, softer body build
