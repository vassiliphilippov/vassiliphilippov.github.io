aspirin

noun
painkiller and anti-inflammatory drug
1.4 1.7
Pronunciation: /ˈæ.sprɪn/ /; / /ˈæ.spə.rɪn/ (AmE), /ˈæ.spə.rɪn/ /; / /ˈæ.sprɪn/ (BrE)
A drug used to reduce pain, fever, and inflammation, also used to reduce the risk of blood clots.

Examples

Take aspirin daily
Doctors often recommend aspirin as a first-line treatment for mild pain and inflammation.
She took two aspirin tablets to relieve her headache.
Taking aspirin daily can help reduce the risk of heart attacks in some individuals.
