Synonyms

  • atmosphere
    3.2 23/m
    atmosphere is the general mood or character you sense in a place, while flavor points to the place's distinctive overall impression.
  • ambiance
    5.3 1.30/m
    ambiance describes the mood or style people notice in a place, while flavor means the place's overall distinctive quality or impression.
  • feel
    3.4 12/m
    feel is a casual impression people get from a place or event, while flavor shows its particular, notable character.
  • feeling
    1.4 15/m
    feeling refers to people's emotions or mood in a place, while flavor describes the place's overall characteristic atmosphere or style.
