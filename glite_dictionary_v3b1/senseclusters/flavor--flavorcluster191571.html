flavor

noun
distinctive atmosphere or quality
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvə / (BrE)
the distinctive atmosphere or quality of a place, situation, or event; the general impression it gives

Examples

A local flavor
The celebration had an international flavor thanks to guests from many countries.
The old theater gave the performance an authentic flavor of the past.
Visiting a local market is the best way to experience the real flavor of the city.
The concert hall retained the historic flavour of the original theater.
The festival had a unique flavour that attracted visitors from across the country.
The new leadership gave the company a completely different flavour.

  • synonym: tone - the general character or attitude of a place, situation, or piece of writing
