nonviral

adjective
not widely shared
5.1
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈvaɪ.rəl/ /; / /nə.ˈvaɪ.rəl/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈvaɪ.rəl/ /; / /nə.ˈvaɪ.rəl/ (BrE)

Examples

Nonviral campaign
The company chose a nonviral approach, relying on local events instead of social media campaigns.
Unlike viral content, nonviral advertisements often target a specific audience rather than the general public.
Their marketing strategy was intentionally nonviral, focusing on personal recommendations and word of mouth.
