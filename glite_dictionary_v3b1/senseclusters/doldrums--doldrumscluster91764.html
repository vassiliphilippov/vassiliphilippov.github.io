doldrums

noun
region of calm winds near the equator
7.2 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈdoʊl.drəmz/ /; / /ˈdɑːl.drəmz/ (AmE), /ˈdɒl.drəmz/ (BrE)
a region near the equator with calm and light winds, especially between the northern and southern trade winds in the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans

Examples
