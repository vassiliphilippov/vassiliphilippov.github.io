swiper

noun
card reading device
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈswaɪ.pər/ (AmE), /ˈswaɪ.pə/ (BrE)
a machine or device that reads the magnetic stripe on a credit or bank card when the card is passed through it

Examples

Card swiper machine
The cashier slid the card through the swiper to process the payment.
If the swiper is not working, you may need to enter your card details manually.
Many stores now use a chip reader instead of a traditional swiper for credit cards.

  • synonym: scanner - a device that scans and reads information from objects or documents
