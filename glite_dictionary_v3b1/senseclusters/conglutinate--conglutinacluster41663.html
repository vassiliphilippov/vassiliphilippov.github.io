conglutinate

verb
to cause to stick together
6.3 8.4
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈgluː.tɪ.neɪt/ /; / /kən.ˈgluː.tə.neɪt/ (AmE), /kən.ˈgluː.tɪ.neɪt/ /; / /kən.ˈgluː.tə.neɪt/ (BrE)
to stick things together or to become stuck together, especially by something sticky

Examples
