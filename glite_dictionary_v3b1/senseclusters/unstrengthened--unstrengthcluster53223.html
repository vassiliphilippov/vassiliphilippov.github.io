unstrengthened

adjective
not reinforced
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstrɛŋθənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈstrɛŋθənd / (BrE)
not made stronger or reinforced; lacking additional support or fortification

Examples

An unstrengthened wall
The old wall remained unstrengthened despite warnings about its stability.
Because the structure was unstrengthened, it suffered severe damage during the storm.
Engineers advised against using unstrengthened materials in the construction project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reinforced - made stronger or more secure
  • synonym: weakened - made less strong or less effective
