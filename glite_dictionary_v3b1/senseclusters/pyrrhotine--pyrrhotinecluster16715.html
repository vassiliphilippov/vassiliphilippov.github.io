pyrrhotine

noun
brownish iron sulfide mineral
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…™r …ô Àåta…™n / (AmE), / Ààp…™r …ô ÀåtiÀên / (BrE)
A brownish iron sulfide mineral (FeS) with weak magnetic properties, found in metamorphic and igneous rocks.

Examples

Brown pyrrhotine mineral
Due to its weak magnetic properties, pyrrhotine can be mistaken for other iron minerals.
The geologist discovered a vein of pyrrhotine embedded within the rock sample.
The presence of pyrrhotine can indicate specific geological processes in metamorphic rocks.
