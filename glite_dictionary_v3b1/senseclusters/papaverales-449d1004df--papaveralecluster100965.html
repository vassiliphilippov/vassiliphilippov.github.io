Papaverales

noun
order of flowering plants including poppies
Pronunciation: / pəˌpævəˈreɪliz / (AmE), / pəˌpævəˈreɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of dicotyledonous flowering plants that includes the poppy family (Papaveraceae) and several related families
