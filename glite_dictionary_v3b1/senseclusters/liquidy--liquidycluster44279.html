liquidy

adjective
watery or thin
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪdi / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪdi / (BrE)
having a thin, watery, or liquid-like quality

Examples

Liquidy sauce
The sauce turned out too liquidy and did not stick to the pasta.
If the paint is liquidy, add more powder to thicken it.
She prefers her yogurt thick, not liquidy like a drink.

Related Terms

  • antonym: thick - having a dense or heavy consistency
  • synonym: watery - containing or resembling water; thin or diluted
