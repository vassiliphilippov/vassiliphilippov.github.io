Taiwan dollar

noun
currency of Taiwan
Pronunciation: / taɪˈwɑn ˈdɑlər / (AmE), / taɪˈwɑːn ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
the basic unit of money in Taiwan

Examples

The Taiwan dollar
She exchanged her US dollars for Taiwan dollars at the airport.
The Taiwan dollar gained strength compared to other Asian currencies this year.
The price was listed as 300 Taiwan dollars on the menu.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dollar - the basic monetary unit used in several countries, including the US, Canada, and Australia
