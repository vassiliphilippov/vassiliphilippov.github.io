lax

adjective
not strict or careful
9.9
Pronunciation: / læks / (AmE), / læks / (BrE)
not sufficiently strict, severe, or careful

Examples

Lax rules
Company policies have become increasingly lax in recent years.
He was reprimanded for his lax attitude toward safety regulations.
The teacher was criticized for being too lax with her students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slack - careless, negligent, or not strict
  • antonym: strict - demanding that rules or guidelines be followed precisely
