filmstrip

noun
row of image thumbnails
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlmˌstrɪp / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmˌstrɪp / (BrE)
a row or strip of small images, often shown on a screen, for browsing or selecting photos or video frames

Examples

Photo filmstrip on screen
You can scroll through the filmstrip at the bottom to choose your favorite photo.
The video editor displays a filmstrip of frames so you can easily find the right moment.
She clicked on the filmstrip to preview each image before making her selection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gallery - a collection or display of images or photos
  • synonym: thumbnail - a small version of an image used for previewing
