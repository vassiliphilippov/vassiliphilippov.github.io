yogi

noun
a practitioner of yoga with spiritual attainment
Pronunciation: / ˈjoʊɡi / (AmE), / ˈjəʊɡi / (BrE)
a person who practices yoga, especially one who has achieved a high level of spiritual insight

Examples

A yogi practices yoga
Many people seek guidance from a yogi to improve their physical and spiritual well-being.
She studied with a renowned yogi during her travels in India.
The yogi meditated for hours in search of enlightenment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yogini - a female practitioner of yoga
