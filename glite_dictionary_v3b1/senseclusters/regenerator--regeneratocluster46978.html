regenerator

noun
organism that regrows tissue
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdʒɛnəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒɛnəreɪtə / (BrE)
an organism or body part that is able to regrow lost or damaged tissue

Examples

Animal regenerator
The salamander is a well-known regenerator because it can regrow entire limbs after injury.
Scientists study the regenerator abilities of certain animals to improve human medicine.
A starfish is a natural regenerator, able to replace lost arms over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: healer - an organism or thing that heals or repairs
  • synonym: regenerate - to regrow lost or damaged tissue
