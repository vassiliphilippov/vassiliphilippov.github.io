stylebook

noun
fashion photo collection
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪlˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlˌbʊk / (BrE)
a book or magazine containing photographs of clothing collections or suggested fashion combinations, often used to present a designer's work

Examples

Fashion stylebook
The fashion house released a new stylebook to showcase its spring collection.
She browsed the stylebook for inspiration before updating her wardrobe.
Buyers studied the stylebook to decide which designs to order for their stores.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catalogue - a book listing items, often with pictures, for sale or display
  • synonym: lookbook - a collection of photographs showing fashion styles or outfits
