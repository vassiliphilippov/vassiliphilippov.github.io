pageboy

noun
boy wedding attendant
5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpeɪdʒ.bɔɪ/ (AmE), /ˈpeɪdʒ.bɔɪ/ (BrE)
a young boy who assists at a wedding ceremony, often carrying the rings or flowers

Examples

Wedding pageboy
The pageboy walked carefully down the aisle with the wedding rings.
At the ceremony, their nephew served as the pageboy and wore a small suit.
Traditionally, a pageboy helps the bride by carrying her train or flowers.
