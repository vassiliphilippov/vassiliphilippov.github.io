minute

noun
unit of time (60 seconds)
1.0
Pronunciation: / mɪˈnut /; / ˈmɪnɪt /; / ˈmɪnət / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
a unit of time equal to 60 seconds, or a very short period of time; also, the time it takes to travel a certain distance

Examples

A five minute break
He finished the test in just under sixty minutes.
It takes about ten minutes to boil an egg.
The movie will start in five minutes.
Don't waste a single minute if you want to catch your train.
She called me the minute she heard the news.
The party started the minute he walked through the door.
Could you spare a minute to help me with this?
I'll be there in a minute, so please wait for me.
She finished the entire task in just a minute.
