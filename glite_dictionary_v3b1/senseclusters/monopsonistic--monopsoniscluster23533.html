monopsonistic

adjective
single-buyer market
Pronunciation: / məˌnɑːpsəˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / məˌnɒpsəˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a market in which there is only one buyer

Examples

A monopsonistic market
In a monopsonistic labor market, workers have fewer options for employment.
The government investigated the monopsonistic practices of the large corporation.
Farmers often face monopsonistic conditions when selling their crops to a single buyer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monopolistic - relating to a market with only one seller
