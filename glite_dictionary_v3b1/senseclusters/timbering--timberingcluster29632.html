timbering

noun
cutting trees for timber
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪmbərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪmbərɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or business of cutting, preparing, or harvesting trees for use as timber

Examples

Commercial timbering
The timbering of the northern woods provided jobs for many families in the region.
Strict regulations now control timbering to protect endangered species and habitats.
After years of timbering, the once dense forest had become much thinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deforestation - the removal of a forest or stand of trees
  • synonym: logging - the process of cutting down trees for timber
