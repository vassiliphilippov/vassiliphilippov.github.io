diaper changer

noun
changing table or station
Pronunciation: / da6ap59r cct83e6and9259r / (AmE), / da6a59p59 cct83e6and9259 / (BrE)
a flat surface, table, or fold-down station (often found in homes or public restrooms) used to place a baby on while changing a diaper; sometimes a device designed to make diaper changing easier.

Examples

A nursery diaper changer
The nursery installed a high-tech diaper changer to automate diapering in the ward.

They bought a portable diaper changer for weekend trips so changing on the go was easier.
