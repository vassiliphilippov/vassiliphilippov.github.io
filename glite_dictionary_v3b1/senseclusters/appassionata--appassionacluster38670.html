appassionata

proper noun
Beethoven piano sonata
Pronunciation: / əˌpɑːʃəˈnɑːtə / (AmE), / əˌpæʃəˈnɑːtə / (BrE)
the popular name for Beethoven's Piano Sonata No. 23 in F minor, Op. 57, known for its passionate character

Examples

Beethoven's Appassionata

The appassionata is admired for its emotional depth and technical challenges.
She listened to the appassionata to feel inspired before her own recital.
