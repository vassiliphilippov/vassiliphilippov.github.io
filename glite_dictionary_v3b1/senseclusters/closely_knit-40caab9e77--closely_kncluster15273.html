closely knit

adjective
united by strong ties
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊsli nɪt / (AmE), / ˈkləʊsli nɪt / (BrE)
(of a group of people) united or held together by strong social or cultural ties.

Examples

A closely knit family
Despite moving to different cities, the siblings remained a closely knit family.
She valued the support of her closely knit group of friends during tough times.
The town's closely knit community welcomed newcomers with open arms.

Related Terms
