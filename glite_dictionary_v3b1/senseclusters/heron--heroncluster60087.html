heron

noun
large wading bird with long neck and bill
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛr.ən / (AmE), / ˈhɛr.ən / (BrE)
A large wading bird with a long neck, long legs, and a long, pointed bill, typically grey or white in colour.

Examples

A heron by river
A heron stood motionless by the river, waiting for a fish to swim by.
The grey heron spread its wings and took off silently from the lake shore.
We saw a heron wading gracefully through the shallow water at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egret - a type of wading bird similar to a heron, usually with white plumage
