nanoparticle

noun
tiny particle
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈnæn.oʊ.pɑːr.tɪ.kəl/ /; / /ˈnæn.ə.pɑːr.tɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /ˈnæn.əʊ.pɑː.tɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
an extremely small particle, usually between 1 and 100 nanometers in size, used in science and technology

Examples
