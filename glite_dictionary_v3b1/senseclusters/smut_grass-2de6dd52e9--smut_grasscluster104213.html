smut grass

noun
a grass species often infested with dark fungus
4.1 7.9
Pronunciation: /smʌt græs/ (AmE), /smʌt grɑːs/ /; / /smʌt græs/ (BrE)
A grass native to the West Indies but commonly found in the southern United States, characterized by tufted wiry stems and often infested with a dark fungus.
