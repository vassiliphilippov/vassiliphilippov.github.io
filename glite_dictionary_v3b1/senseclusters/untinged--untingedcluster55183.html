untinged

adjective
without any trace
7.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɪndʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɪndʒd / (BrE)
not having any trace, hint, or slight amount of a particular quality, feeling, or color

Examples

Untinged by sarcasm
His apology was untinged by sarcasm, making it seem completely sincere.
The report was untinged by bias, presenting only the facts as they were.
Her excitement was untinged by any fear or hesitation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pure - not mixed with anything else; untainted
  • antonym: tinged - having a slight amount of a color or quality
  • synonym: unaffected - not influenced or changed by something
