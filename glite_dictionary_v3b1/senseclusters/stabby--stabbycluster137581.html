stabby

adjective
sharp, stabbing pain
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈstæbi / (AmE), / ˈstæbi / (BrE)
causing or resembling a sudden, sharp, stabbing pain.

Examples

Stabby pain
After running, she felt a stabby pain beneath her ribs that made breathing difficult.
The cold wind gave him a stabby sensation in his ears as he walked outside.
Sometimes I get a stabby feeling in my knee when I climb stairs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - not sharp or intense in sensation
  • synonym: piercing - very sharp or intense, like being pierced
