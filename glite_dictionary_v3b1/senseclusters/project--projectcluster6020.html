project

verb
to extend outward, stick out
6.8
Pronunciation: / prəˈdʒɛkt / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛkt / (BrE)
to extend outward or beyond a surface; to stick out

Examples

Project from the wall
From the wall, a wooden beam projects several feet.
Sharp rocks project from the surface of the frozen lake, making it dangerous to skate.
The balcony projected over the bustling street below, offering a perfect view.

Related Terms
