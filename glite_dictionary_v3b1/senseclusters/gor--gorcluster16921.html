gor

interjection
old exclamation
Pronunciation: / ɡɔːr / (AmE), / ɡɔː / (BrE)

Gor!

‘Gor, I can’t believe how much you’ve grown since I last saw you!’
‘Gor! What a mess you’ve made in here,’ she exclaimed.
