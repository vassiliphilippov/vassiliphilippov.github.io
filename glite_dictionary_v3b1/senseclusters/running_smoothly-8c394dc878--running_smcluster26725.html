running smoothly

adverb
functioning efficiently and without problems
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪŋ ˈsmuðli / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ ˈsmuːðli / (BrE)
operating without problems, interruptions, or difficulties; functioning efficiently

Examples

Running smoothly all day
After the software update, the whole system was running smoothly for weeks.
Thanks to proper planning, the event was running smoothly from start to finish.
The teacher works hard to keep the classroom running smoothly throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: break down - to stop functioning (as a machine, system, etc.), often suddenly
  • synonym: go off without a hitch - to happen or progress without any problems
