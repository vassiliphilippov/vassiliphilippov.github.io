unsecured

adjective
not secured by collateral (finance)
2.9
Pronunciation: / ʌn.sɪˈkjɝd / (AmE), / ʌn.sɪˈkjʊəd / (BrE)
not protected by collateral or financial security, especially relating to loans or debt instruments.

Examples

Unsecured loan
Because the loan was unsecured, the interest rate was significantly higher than for secured loans.
He invested in several unsecured promissory notes, despite the risks involved.
The company issued an unsecured bond to raise capital for expansion.
