felt-tip pen

noun
pen with a felt tip for writing
7.1 1.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfelt.tɪp pen/ (AmE), /ˈfelt.tɪp pen/ (BrE)
A pen with a tip made of felt or similar porous material, used for writing or drawing.

Examples

A felt-tip pen
I prefer using a felt-tip pen for taking notes because of its smooth ink flow.
Please do not write on the whiteboard with a felt-tip pen.
She used a felt-tip pen to illustrate the poster in bright colors.

  • synonym: marker pen - a pen with a broad felt tip, often for marking surfaces
