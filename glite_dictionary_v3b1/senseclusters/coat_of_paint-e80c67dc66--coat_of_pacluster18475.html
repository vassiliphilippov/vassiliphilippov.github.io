coat of paint

noun
a layer of paint applied to a surface
3.2
Pronunciation: / koʊt əv peɪnt / (AmE), / kəʊt əv peɪnt / (BrE)
a single layer of paint covering a surface

Examples

Two coats of paint
After two coats of paint, the room finally looked bright and clean.
She applied a thick coat of paint to hide the old stains.
The door needs another coat of paint to look fresh and new.

Related Terms

  • synonym: layer - a sheet, quantity, or thickness of material covering a surface
  • degree lower: varnish - a layer of liquid that dries to form a hard, shiny surface, often used on wood
