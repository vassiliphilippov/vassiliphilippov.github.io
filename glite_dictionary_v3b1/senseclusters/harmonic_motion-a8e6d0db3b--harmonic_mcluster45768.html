harmonic motion

noun
periodic movement around an equilibrium point
1.0 5.1
Pronunciation: /hɑːr.ˈmɑː.nɪk ˈmoʊ.ʃən/ (AmE), /hɑː.ˈmɒ.nɪk ˈməʊ.ʃən/ (BrE)
