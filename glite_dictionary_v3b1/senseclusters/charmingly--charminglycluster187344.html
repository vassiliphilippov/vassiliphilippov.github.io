charmingly

adverb
in a delightful or pleasant manner
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːrmɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːmɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is pleasant and delightful, often attracting and pleasing others.

Examples

To charmingly apologize
He charmingly apologized for being late, making it difficult to stay annoyed.
She smiled charmingly at everyone she met, winning over the entire group.
The room was charmingly decorated with fresh flowers and delicate lights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: offensively - in a manner that gives offence
  • synonym: pleasantly - in a pleasing or enjoyable manner
