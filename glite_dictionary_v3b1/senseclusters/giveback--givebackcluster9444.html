giveback

noun
return of assets
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪvˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvˌbæk / (BrE)
the act of returning money, property, or rights to their previous owner, especially as restitution.

Examples

Court-ordered giveback
The court ordered a giveback of the stolen property to its rightful owner.
After negotiations, the company agreed to a giveback of employee benefits.
The charity organized a giveback event to return unused supplies to the community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confiscation - the act of taking something away, especially by authority
  • synonym: restitution - the act of restoring or giving back something to its rightful owner
