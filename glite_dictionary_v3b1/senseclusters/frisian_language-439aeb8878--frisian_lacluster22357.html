Frisian language

noun
West Germanic language of Friesland, Netherlands
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪʒiən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈfrɪzɪən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a West Germanic language spoken mainly in Friesland in the northwestern Netherlands, closely related to English

Examples

Frisian language in Friesland
Many linguists consider the Frisian language to be the closest living relative of English.
She decided to learn the Frisian language during her stay in the Netherlands.
The Frisian language is still spoken by people in the province of Friesland.
