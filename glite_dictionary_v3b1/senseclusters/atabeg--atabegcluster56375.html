atabeg

noun
historical Muslim official
Pronunciation: / ˈætəˌbɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈætəˌbɛɡ / (BrE)
a historical title for a governor or high official in some Muslim countries, especially during the Middle Ages

Examples

A powerful atabeg
The powerful atabeg governed the province with both authority and wisdom.
During the Seljuk period, an atabeg often acted as a guardian for young princes.
Historians study the influence of each atabeg on the region's political development.

Related Terms

  • synonym: governor - an official in charge of a region or province
  • synonym: vizier - a high-ranking political advisor or minister in Muslim countries
