Bonapartist

noun
supporter of Napoleon
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌboʊnəˈpɑːrtɪst / (AmE), / ˌbəʊnəˈpɑːtɪst / (BrE)
a supporter of Napoleon Bonaparte or his political principles and policies

Examples

Bonapartist supporter
The Bonapartist movement sought to restore the empire after Napoleon's exile.
Many Bonapartists remained loyal to the ideals of strong centralized leadership.
During the political upheaval, he was accused of being a secret Bonapartist.
