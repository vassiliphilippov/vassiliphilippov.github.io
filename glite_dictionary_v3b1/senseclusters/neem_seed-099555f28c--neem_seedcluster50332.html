neem seed

noun
seed of the neem tree, used in agriculture and medicine
Pronunciation: / nim sid / (AmE), / niːm siːd / (BrE)
the seed of the neem tree, used as a source of pesticides, fertilizers, and medicinal products

Examples

Crushed neem seed
Farmers often add crushed neem seed to compost to improve soil quality.
The oil extracted from neem seed is widely used as a natural pesticide.
Traditional medicine sometimes includes preparations made from neem seed to treat skin conditions.
