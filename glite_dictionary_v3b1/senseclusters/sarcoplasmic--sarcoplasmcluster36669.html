sarcoplasmic

adjective
of muscle cell cytoplasm
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑːrkoʊˈplæzmɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɑːkəʊˈplæzmɪk / (BrE)
relating to the sarcoplasm, which is the cytoplasm of muscle cells

Examples

Sarcoplasmic reticulum
The sarcoplasmic reticulum stores calcium ions needed for muscle contraction.
Researchers studied the sarcoplasmic changes in athletes after intense exercise.
A defect in the sarcoplasmic proteins can lead to muscle disorders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: muscular - relating to muscles
