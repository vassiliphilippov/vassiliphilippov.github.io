stuffed mushroom

noun
mushroom caps filled with a savory stuffing
2.5
Pronunciation: / stʌft məˈʃruːm / (AmE), / stʌft ˈmʌʃruːm / (BrE)
a dish consisting of mushroom caps filled with a savory mixture such as meats, seafood, cheese, or vegetables, and often baked or grilled

Examples

Baked stuffed mushrooms
At the party, the trays of stuffed mushrooms disappeared within minutes.
She ordered the stuffed mushroom appetizer filled with crab and cream cheese.
The chef demonstrated how to make stuffed mushrooms using spinach and breadcrumbs.
