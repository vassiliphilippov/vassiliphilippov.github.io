viewer

noun
optical device for viewing transparencies
4.8 1.81/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàvjuÀê…ö / (AmE), / ÀàvjuÀê…ô / (BrE)
a device with a lens or other optical element used to look at photographic transparencies, such as slides or film strips

Examples

Slide viewer

She found an antique viewer at the photography shop.
The old viewer allows you to look at each frame of the film strip.
