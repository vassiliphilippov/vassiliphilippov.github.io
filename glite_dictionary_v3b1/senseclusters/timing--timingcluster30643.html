timing

noun
the time when something happens
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the choice, control, or quality of when something happens, especially to achieve a good result

Examples

Good timing
Good timing is essential for a successful performance.

The timing of the announcement surprised everyone.
Good timing is essential in comedy to deliver a punchline effectively.
Her timing during the race allowed her to overtake her competitor at just the right moment.
The engine's performance depends on the precise timing of the spark plugs.
