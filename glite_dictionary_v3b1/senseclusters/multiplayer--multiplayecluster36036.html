multiplayer

noun
game mode with many players
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌpleɪə / (BrE)
a mode or session in a game where several players participate together or compete against each other

Examples

An online multiplayer
The new racing game features an exciting multiplayer that lets you compete with friends online.
After finishing the story mode, they spent hours playing multiplayer together every weekend.
Many players prefer multiplayer because it offers more unpredictable and challenging matches.

Related Terms
