plugger

noun
steady worker
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌɡər / (AmE), / ˈplʌɡə / (BrE)
a person who works steadily and persistently, especially without seeking attention or reward

Examples

A steady plugger
The company valued him as a reliable plugger who always finished his tasks.
As a plugger, she never gave up, even when the work was difficult.
The plumber used a plugger to seal the hole in the pipe.
Despite facing many setbacks, he remained a true plugger and never gave up.
The team valued her as a plugger who always put in extra effort.
You can count on a plugger to finish the job, no matter how tough it gets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grinder - someone who works hard, especially at repetitive tasks
  • synonym: hard worker - a person who puts in a lot of effort and diligence
  • antonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
  • synonym: worker - a person who works, especially one who does manual labor
