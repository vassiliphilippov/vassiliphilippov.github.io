call a spade a spade

verb
speak plainly and directly
Pronunciation: / kɔl ə speɪd ə speɪd / (AmE), / kɔːl ə speɪd ə speɪd / (BrE)
to speak plainly and directly about something, even if it is unpleasant or controversial

She always prefers to call a spade a spade, even if the truth is uncomfortable.
