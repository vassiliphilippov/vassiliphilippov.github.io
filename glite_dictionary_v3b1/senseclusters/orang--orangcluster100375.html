orang

noun
large ape of Borneo and Sumatra
Pronunciation: / əˈræŋ / (AmE), / əˈræŋ / (BrE)
A large, long-armed ape native to Borneo and Sumatra, known for its arboreal lifestyle.

Examples

An orang in trees
At the sanctuary, visitors can observe a young orang learning to climb trees.
Conservationists are working to protect the endangered orang populations in Sumatra.
The orang moved gracefully among the high branches searching for fruit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orangutan - large, long-armed arboreal ape native to Southeast Asia
