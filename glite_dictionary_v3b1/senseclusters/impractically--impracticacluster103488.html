impractically

adverb
not in a practical way
6.1
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpræktɪkli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpræktɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is not practical, useful, or sensible

Examples

To impractically stack boxes
He tried to carry all the boxes impractically stacked on top of each other.
The plan was impractically complicated for such a simple problem.
She dressed impractically for the cold weather, wearing only a thin jacket.
The sofa was impractically large for the small living room.
He dressed impractically for the hike, wearing formal shoes instead of boots.
The plan was impractically complicated and difficult to carry out.

Related Terms

  • antonym: practically - in a way that is practical or realistic
  • antonym: sensibly - in a reasonable or practical way
  • synonym: unrealistically - in a way that is not realistic or practical
  • synonym: unreasonably - to a degree that is not fair or sensible
