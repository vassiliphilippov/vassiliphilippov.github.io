sticktight flea

noun
parasitic flea on poultry
4.5 8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈstɪk.taɪt fliː/ (AmE), /ˈstɪk.taɪt fliː/ (BrE)
a small parasitic flea (mainly Echidnophaga gallinacea) that attaches itself tightly to the skin of birds and sometimes mammals, particularly on the heads of chickens

Examples

sticktight flea on chickens
After noticing strange spots on her hens' combs, she discovered they were caused by sticktight fleas.
Infestation by sticktight fleas can cause severe irritation and infection in poultry.
The veterinarian recommended a special treatment to eliminate sticktight fleas from the chicken coop.
