coachmaster

noun
driver of horse-drawn coach
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊtʃˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtʃˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who drives or is responsible for a horse-drawn coach

Examples

Coachmaster drives horses
The coachmaster checked the harnesses before setting off at dawn.
Travelers relied on the coachmaster to guide them safely through the countryside.
In old novels, the coachmaster is often described as a skilled and trusted servant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coachman - a person who drives a horse-drawn coach
  • antonym: passenger - a person who travels in a vehicle but is not the driver
