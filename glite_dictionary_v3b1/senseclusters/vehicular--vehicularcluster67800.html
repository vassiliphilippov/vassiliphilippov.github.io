vehicular

adjective
relating to vehicles or motor vehicles
Pronunciation: / vəˈhɪkjələr / (AmE), / vɪˈhɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to, or intended for vehicles, especially motor vehicles

Examples

Vehicular traffic
Pedestrian zones are created to limit vehicular access in busy city centers.
The accident caused a vehicular traffic jam that lasted for hours.
The city implemented new vehicular restrictions to reduce air pollution.
