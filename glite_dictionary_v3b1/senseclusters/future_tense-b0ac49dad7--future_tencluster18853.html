future tense

noun
verb tense for future actions
1.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈfjuː.tʃər tens/ (AmE), /ˈfjuː.tʃə tens/ (BrE)
A verb tense used to express actions or states that will occur after the present moment.

Examples

The future tense form
In English, the future tense is often formed using the auxiliary verb 'will'.
She explained how to use the future tense when talking about plans.
Some languages have more than one future tense to indicate different shades of meaning.
