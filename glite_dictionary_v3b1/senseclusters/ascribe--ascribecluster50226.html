to regard something as belonging to, caused by, or produced by a particular person, source, or cause

Examples

To ascribe meaning to
Many people ascribe magical powers to the ancient artifact.
She tends to ascribe her success to a supportive family environment.
Some historians ascribe the painting to a student of Leonardo da Vinci.

Synonyms

  • attribute
    3.8 28/m
    attribute says something is caused by or belongs to a person or thing, while ascribe is a more formal verb used for the same idea.
  • assign
    3.2 5.65/m
    assign gives or credits something to a person or source, while ascribe focuses on regarding something as caused or produced by someone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attribute - to regard as resulting from a specified cause
  • synonym: impute - to attribute a quality or action to someone, often in a negative sense
