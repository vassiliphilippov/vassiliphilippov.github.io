hyperintellectual

noun
overly intellectual person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpərˌɪntəˈlɛktjʊəl / (BrE)
a person who is extremely or excessively intellectual in manner, attitude, or conversation

Examples

A hyperintellectual person
Some people found the professor to be a hyperintellectual who made every discussion overly complex.
At parties, he was often labeled a hyperintellectual because of his constant references to philosophy.
Her hyperintellectual approach sometimes alienated colleagues who preferred practical solutions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anti-intellectual - a person who opposes or distrusts intellectuals or intellectual pursuits
  • synonym: intellectual - a person who uses the mind creatively or critically
