paranoid

noun
person with paranoia
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpær.ə.nɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈpær.ə.nɔɪd / (BrE)
a person who suffers from paranoia, having irrational fears or suspicions of others

Examples

A paranoid person
As a paranoid, she found it hard to trust even her closest friends.
Doctors tried to help the paranoid overcome his constant suspicions.
The paranoid kept checking the locks on his doors, convinced someone was watching him.
