aqua

noun
bluish-green color
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːkwə, ˈækwə / (AmE), / ˈakwə, ˈɑːkwə / (BrE)
A light bluish-green color, resembling that of water.

Examples

Light aqua color
She wore a dress in shimmering aqua that caught everyone's attention.
The pale aqua walls made the room feel cool and inviting.
They chose aqua for their wedding theme because it reminded them of the ocean.
