exclusivist

adjective
limiting to select group
8.8
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈskluːsɪvɪst / (AmE), / ɪkˈskluːsɪvɪst / (BrE)
intentionally limiting membership, access, or participation to a select group and excluding others

Examples

Exclusivist club policy
The club has an exclusivist policy that only allows members from certain backgrounds.
His exclusivist views made it difficult for new employees to feel welcome in the team.
Critics argue that the organization is too exclusivist and should be more open to diversity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exclusive - limiting to only certain people or groups
  • antonym: inclusive - open to all; not excluding any group
