mellow

adjective
slightly intoxicated and relaxed
6.7 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmel.oʊ/ /; / /ˈmel.əʊ/ (AmE), /ˈmel.əʊ/ /; / /ˈmel.oʊ/ (BrE)
Slightly and pleasantly intoxicated, typically from alcohol or drugs (especially marijuana), often resulting in a relaxed or contented mood.

Examples

He likes to smoke a little to get mellow before listening to his favorite albums.
The party had a mellow vibe, with everyone relaxed and enjoying themselves.
