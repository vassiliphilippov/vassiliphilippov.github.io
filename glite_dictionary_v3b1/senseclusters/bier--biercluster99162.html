bier

noun
stand for a coffin before burial
Pronunciation: / bɪr / (AmE), / bɪə / (BrE)
a stand on which a coffin or a corpse is placed before burial or cremation

Examples

A bier stand
As the procession moved slowly, everyone walked behind the bier to the cemetery.
Mourners gathered around the bier to pay their final respects.
The body was placed on a bier draped with flowers and wreaths.
A wooden bier stood in the center of the chapel awaiting the mourners.
Pallbearers lifted the bier and began the solemn procession to the grave.
The coffin rested on a simple bier adorned with flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catafalque - a decorated platform supporting a coffin during a funeral
  • synonym: coffin - box in which a dead body is buried or cremated
