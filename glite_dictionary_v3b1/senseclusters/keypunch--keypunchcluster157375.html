keypunch

noun
data entry worker
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːˌpʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈkiːˌpʌntʃ / (BrE)
a person whose job is to enter data into a system using a keypunch machine or keyboard

Examples

A keypunch operator
In the 1970s, a keypunch was essential for processing large amounts of business data.
She found work as a keypunch at the local office, entering information from paper forms.
Before computers became common, every payroll department needed at least one keypunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: data entry clerk - a person who inputs data into a computer system
  • synonym: typist - a person who types documents as a job
