Trichechus

noun
genus of manatees (aquatic mammals)
Pronunciation: / trɪˈkiːkəs / (AmE), / trɪˈkiːkəs / (BrE)
A genus of aquatic mammals in the family Trichechidae, commonly known as manatees.

Examples

The Trichechus manatee
Fossils from the Trichechus lineage have been found in several parts of the world.
Scientists studied the diet of Trichechus in their natural habitat.
The genus Trichechus includes all three living species of manatees.
