blastoporic

adjective
relating to a blastopore in embryos
Pronunciation: / ˌblæstəˈpɔrɪk / (AmE), / ˌblæstəˈpɔːrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or associated with a blastopore, the opening that forms during the early embryonic development of some animals
