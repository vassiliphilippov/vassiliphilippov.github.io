sleepless

adjective
always watchful; vigilant
7.8 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsliÀêpl…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàsliÀêpl…ôs / (BrE)
(literary) Always watchful or vigilant; never inattentive.

Examples

Sleepless guard
Her sleepless attention to her children's safety brought her peace of mind.
The sleepless guards remained alert throughout the long night.
With a sleepless sense of duty, the lighthouse keeper watched for ships in distress.
