twinkler

noun
object emitting flickering light
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪŋk.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈtwɪŋk.lə / (BrE)
an object that emits or reflects light in an intermittent, flickering manner

Examples

A twinkler light
As the night fell, the only light came from a distant twinkler on the horizon.
She bought a set of solar twinklers to decorate the garden paths.
The Christmas tree was covered in twinklers that lit up the room with a magical glow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: twinkle - to shine with a flickering or sparkling light
