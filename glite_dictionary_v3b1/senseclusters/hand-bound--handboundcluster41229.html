hand-bound

adjective
made by hand
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈhændˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
made or fastened together by hand, especially referring to books or similar items

Examples

Hand-bound book
The collector prized the hand-bound volume for its unique craftsmanship.
She received a beautiful hand-bound journal as a birthday gift.
Many rare manuscripts are preserved in hand-bound editions at the museum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handmade - made by hand rather than by machine
  • antonym: machine-bound - bound or fastened together by machine
