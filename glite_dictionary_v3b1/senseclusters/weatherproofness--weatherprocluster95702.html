weatherproofness

noun
resistance to weather
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛðərˌprufnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɛðəˌpruːfnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being able to prevent or resist damage, penetration, or malfunction caused by weather (such as rain, wind, or snow)
