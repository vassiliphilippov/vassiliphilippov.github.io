Atlantean

adjective
of Atlantis
Pronunciation: / ətˈlæntiən / (AmE), / ətˈlæntiən / (BrE)
relating to the mythical island of Atlantis or its supposed people and culture

Examples

Atlantean ruins
The ruins were said to be of Atlantean origin, hidden beneath the ocean for centuries.
She wore jewelry inspired by Atlantean designs in the ancient legend.
Many stories describe advanced Atlantean technology lost to history.

  • synonym: mythical - existing only in myths or legends
