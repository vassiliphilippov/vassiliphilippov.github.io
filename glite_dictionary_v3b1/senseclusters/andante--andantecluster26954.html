andante

adjective
moderately slow tempo (music)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ænˈdɑnteɪ / (AmE), / ænˈdɑːnteɪ / (BrE)
(in music) at a moderately slow tempo, typically considered to be a walking pace.

Examples

Andante movement
He instructed the orchestra to keep an andante pace throughout this section.
The piece should be played andante to reflect its gentle character.
The second movement of the symphony is marked andante, creating a calm and peaceful mood.

Related Terms
