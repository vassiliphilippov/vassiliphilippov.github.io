public speaking

noun
the act of addressing an audience
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk ˈspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of speaking to an audience, usually to inform, persuade, or entertain

Examples

Good public speaking skills
Good public speaking skills are valuable in many careers.

The workshop focused on techniques for effective public speaking.
His confidence in public speaking grew with every presentation.
Many people find public speaking to be a daunting experience at first.
She enrolled in a class to improve her public speaking skills.
