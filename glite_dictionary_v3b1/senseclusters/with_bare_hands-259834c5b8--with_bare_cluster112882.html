with bare hands

preposition
using only one's hands, no tools
5.5
Pronunciation: / wɪð ˈbɛr ˈhændz / (AmE), / wɪð ˈbeə ˈhændz / (BrE)
without using any tools, weapons, or other aids; using only one's hands

Examples

To break with bare hands
He tore the old wallpaper off the walls with bare hands.
She dug the hole in the garden with bare hands when she couldn't find a shovel.
The wrestler broke the wooden board with bare hands during the demonstration.
