wheezy

adjective
having a breathy or coarse tone, like a reed instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːzi / (AmE), / ˈwiːzi / (BrE)
having a tone or sound characterized by a high-pitched, breathy, or coarse quality, like a wheeze; reminiscent of the sound of a reed instrument.

Examples

Wheezy voice
After years of use, the organ's wheezy notes had become its most distinctive feature.
His wheezy voice gave the song a haunting, nostalgic quality.
The old accordion produced a wheezy melody that filled the room with charm.
