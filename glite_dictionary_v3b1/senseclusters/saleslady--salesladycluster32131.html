saleslady

noun
woman who sells goods in a store
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlzˌleɪdi / (AmE), / ˈseɪlzˌleɪdi / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to sell goods to customers, especially in a shop or store

Examples

A saleslady in store
A friendly saleslady greeted us as we entered the store.
The saleslady answered all my questions about the different phone models.
The saleslady helped me find the perfect dress for the wedding.

Related Terms

  • antonym: salesman - a man whose job is to sell goods to customers
  • synonym: salesperson - a person whose job is to sell goods to customers, gender-neutral term
