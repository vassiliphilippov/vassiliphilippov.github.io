pedunculated

adjective
with a stalk
9.1
Pronunciation: / pɪˈdʌŋkjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / pɪˈdʌŋkjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
having or attached by a stalk or stem, especially in medical or botanical contexts

Examples

A pedunculated tumor
The surgeon removed a pedunculated tumor from the patient's colon.
In botany, a pedunculated flower is one that grows on a distinct stalk.
The scan revealed several pedunculated polyps in the intestinal lining.

Related Terms
