kingpin

noun
most important person; leader
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋˌpɪn / (BrE)
the most important or influential person in a group, organization, or activity

Examples

Crime kingpin
As the kingpin of the operation, she controlled every important decision.
He always wanted to be the kingpin in his field, commanding respect from all his peers.
Police arrested the alleged kingpin of the drug cartel last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boss - a person in charge; the leader of a group
  • antonym: underling - a person of lower rank or authority; subordinate
