  • incompetent

  • untrained
    3.4 1.87/m

  • inexperienced

  • unskillful
    4.4 0.03/m
    unskillful often describes an action done clumsily, while unskilled describes a person who lacks the needed ability or training.
  • unaccomplished
    4.8 0.02/m
    unaccomplished suggests lack of achievement or polish, while unskilled focuses on lacking practical ability or training for a task.
