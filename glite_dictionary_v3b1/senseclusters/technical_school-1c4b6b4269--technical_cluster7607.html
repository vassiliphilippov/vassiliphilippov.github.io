technical school

noun
a school for technical or vocational training
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛk.nɪ.kəl skuːl / (AmE), / ˈtɛk.nɪ.kəl skuːl / (BrE)
a school that provides instruction in the mechanical and industrial arts or applied sciences, especially for occupational training

Examples

A technical school
After graduating high school, she enrolled in a technical school to become an automotive technician.
Many students choose a technical school for practical training and faster entry into the workforce.
The city opened a new technical school specializing in information technology and engineering.
