sex drive

noun
desire for sexual activity
Pronunciation: / sɛks draɪv / (AmE), / sɛks draɪv / (BrE)
a person's natural desire or urge for sexual activity.

Examples

High sex drive
After the treatment, she noticed her sex drive had returned to normal.
Some medications can cause a decrease in a person's sex drive.
Testosterone plays a significant role in regulating the sex drive in men.
