kitchen sink regression

noun
regression affecting many aspects or variables
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtʃən sɪŋk rɪˈɡrɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃɪn sɪŋk rɪˈɡrɛʃən / (BrE)
a software or statistical regression that affects many aspects or incorporates all possible variables, often leading to widespread problems or overcomplicated models

Examples

Kitchen sink regression problem
Developers are working to fix a kitchen sink regression that affects the user interface and database.
Our QA process caught a kitchen sink regression that broke several modules.
The team struggled to track down the kitchen sink regression introduced in the new update.
