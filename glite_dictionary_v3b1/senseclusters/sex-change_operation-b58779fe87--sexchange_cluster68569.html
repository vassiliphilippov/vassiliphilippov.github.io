sex-change operation

noun
surgical procedure to change sexual characteristics
7.6 4.6
Pronunciation: /seks tʃeɪndʒ ɑː.pər.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /seks tʃeɪndʒ ɒp.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a medical procedure that alters a person's sexual characteristics to resemble those of the opposite sex, often involving surgery and hormone treatment

Examples

A sex-change operation
After years of consideration, she decided to undergo a sex-change operation to live as her true self.
He traveled abroad to have a sex-change operation due to restrictions in his home country.
Medical advances have made the sex-change operation safer and more effective than ever before.
