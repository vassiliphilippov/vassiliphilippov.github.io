upslope

noun
higher part of slope
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌsloʊp / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌsləʊp / (BrE)
the higher or uphill part of a slope or an area of ground that rises upward.

Examples

The upslope area
The cabin was built on the upslope to avoid flooding during heavy rains.
Hikers rested on the upslope before continuing to the mountain peak.
Snow tends to melt faster on the upslope facing the morning sun.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downhill - the lower part or side of a slope
  • degree lower: foothill - a low hill at the base of a mountain or slope
  • degree higher: summit - the highest point of a hill or mountain
