committer

noun
person placing in custody
9.1
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈmɪt.ər/ (AmE), /kə.ˈmɪt.ə/ (BrE)
an official or person who legally places someone in custody, care, or a mental institution

Examples

Hospital committer
The committer signed the documents authorizing the patient's admission to the hospital.
As the committer, the judge had to ensure all legal procedures were followed.
Only an authorized committer can order someone to be placed in protective custody.
