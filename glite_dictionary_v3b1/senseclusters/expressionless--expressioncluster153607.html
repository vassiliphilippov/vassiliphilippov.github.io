Examples

Expressionless face
His expressionless gaze made it impossible to know what he was thinking.
She maintained an expressionless face throughout the entire interview.
The actor delivered his lines in an expressionless manner, adding to the character's mystery.

Synonyms

  • unexpressive
    5.5 0.12/m
    unexpressive describes a face that shows little or no feeling in everyday speech, while expressionless names no visible emotion.
  • inexpressive
    5.5 0.20/m
    inexpressive appears more in formal writing to describe a dull face with little expression, while expressionless means not showing feelings.
  • impassive
    7.1 1.50/m
    impassive suggests calm detachment and no visible feeling, while expressionless simply describes a face that shows no emotion.
  • emotionless
    5.5 0.81/m
    emotionless often implies a lack of feeling inside, while expressionless only refers to an outward unreadable face.
  • unemotional
    4.4 0.52/m
    unemotional describes a calm person not moved by strong feelings, while expressionless points to a face that hides emotions.
  • affectless
    9.3 0.14/m
    affectless is a rare, formal term for showing no outward feeling, while expressionless is common and used in everyday speech.
  • unspeaking
    6.4 0.00/m
    unspeaking often means silent and without visible feeling, while expressionless focuses on a face that shows no emotion.
  • blank
    4.2 6.00/m
    blank can mean both not understanding and having no face movement, while expressionless only means showing no emotion.
