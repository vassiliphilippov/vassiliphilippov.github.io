Synonyms

  • unexpressive
    5.5 0.00/m
    unexpressive describes a face that shows little or no feeling in everyday speech, while expressionless names no visible emotion.
  • inexpressive
    5.5 0.04/m
    inexpressive appears more in formal writing to describe a dull face with little expression, while expressionless means not showing feelings.
  • impassive
    7.1 0.5/m
    impassive suggests calm detachment and no visible feeling, while expressionless simply describes a face that shows no emotion.
  • emotionless
    5.5 0.5/m
    emotionless often implies a lack of feeling inside, while expressionless only refers to an outward unreadable face.
  • unemotional
    4.4 0.5/m
    unemotional describes a calm person not moved by strong feelings, while expressionless points to a face that hides emotions.
  • affectless
    9.3 0.04/m

  • unspeaking
