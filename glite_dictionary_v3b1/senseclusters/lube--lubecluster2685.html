lube

noun
substance reducing friction; lubricant
5.9 1.57/m
Pronunciation: / luÀêb / (AmE), / luÀêb / (BrE)
A substance, such as oil or grease, that reduces friction by making surfaces smooth or slippery; lubricant.

Examples

A drop of lube
Add some lube to the chain to keep your bike running smoothly.
Some lube can help stop those annoying squeaks in door hinges.
The mechanic always checks the lube levels during a car service.

Synonyms

  • lubricant
    3.7 2.90/m
    lubricant is the formal, technical name for oil or grease that reduces friction, while lube is a short informal word.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lubricant - A substance used to reduce friction between surfaces in contact
