truck farm

noun
farm growing produce for market
Pronunciation: / trʌk fɑrm / (AmE), / trʌk fɑːm / (BrE)
A farm where vegetables and other produce are grown to be sold at market rather than for personal use.

Examples

A truck farm market
Historically, truck farms played a vital role in feeding urban populations.
Many truck farms surround the city, supplying it with seasonal vegetables.
They bought fresh tomatoes and cucumbers from the local truck farm.
