Spartacus

proper noun
leader of slave revolt
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːrtəkəs / (AmE), / ˈspɑːtəkəs / (BrE)
the famous leader of a major slave revolt against ancient Rome in the 1st century BCE

Examples

Spartacus leads a revolt
Spartacus is remembered for leading a rebellion against Roman slavery.
Many movies and books have told the story of Spartacus and his fight for freedom.
Historians still study how Spartacus challenged the power of ancient Rome.
