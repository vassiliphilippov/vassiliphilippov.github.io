adopter

noun
early user
3.9
Pronunciation: / əˈdɑptər / (AmE), / əˈdɒptə / (BrE)
a person or organization that is among the first to start using or accepting something new, such as an idea, product, or technology

Examples

Early adopter of technology
Tech companies often target adopters who are eager to try the latest gadgets.
The success of a new app depends on how quickly adopters embrace it.
Early adopters can influence the popularity of emerging technologies.
As an early adopter, he always tries the latest gadgets before anyone else.
The company rewarded each adopter of its new software with a special discount.
Many adopters of electric cars are motivated by environmental concerns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: follower - a person who accepts or adopts something after others have done so
  • synonym: innovator - a person who introduces new ideas or methods
  • antonym: laggard - a person who is slow to adopt new ideas or technologies
