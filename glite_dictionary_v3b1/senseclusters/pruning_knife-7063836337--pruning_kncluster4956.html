pruning knife

noun
knife for trimming plants
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːnɪŋ naɪf / (AmE), / ˈpruːnɪŋ naɪf / (BrE)
a knife with a curved or hooked blade, used for cutting or trimming plants, especially in gardening or horticulture

Examples

A pruning knife blade
A small pruning knife fits easily in a pocket for quick access during gardening.
She carefully used a pruning knife to remove dead branches from the fruit trees.
The gardener sharpened his pruning knife before working on the rose bushes.
