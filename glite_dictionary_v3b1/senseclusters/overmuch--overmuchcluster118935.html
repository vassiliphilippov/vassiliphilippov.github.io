overmuch

adjective
too much; excessive
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈmʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈmʌtʃ / (BrE)

Examples

Overmuch attention

There isn't overmuch food left after the party.
