emergently

adverb
unexpectedly arising
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɜrdʒəntli / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒəntli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to something arising or coming into existence unexpectedly or spontaneously

Examples

Emergently appear
The team noticed that new behaviors appeared emergently as the project progressed.
Patterns can develop emergently in complex systems without any central control.
The solution was found emergently when the group combined their ideas unexpectedly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: predictably - in a way that can be expected or foreseen
  • synonym: spontaneously - in a way that happens without planning or external cause
