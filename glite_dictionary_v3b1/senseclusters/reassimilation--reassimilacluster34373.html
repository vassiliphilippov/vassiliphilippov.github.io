reassimilation

noun
being assimilated again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˌsɪməˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːəˌsɪmɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of being assimilated again, especially after a period of separation or change

Examples

Cultural reassimilation
After years abroad, his reassimilation into his home culture was more difficult than he expected.
The reassimilation of nutrients occurs in some plant tissues after stress.
The government created programs to support the reassimilation of refugees into society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assimilation - the process of becoming part of a group or system
  • antonym: separation - the act of moving apart or being apart
