unmalleable

adjective
not able to be shaped or worked
5.1 7.2
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈmæl.ɪ.ə.bəl/ /; / /ən.ˈmæl.ɪ.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈmæl.ɪ.ə.bəl/ /; / /ən.ˈmæl.ɪ.ə.bəl/ (BrE)
not able to be shaped, bent, or worked easily; difficult or impossible to alter in form.

Examples

An unmalleable metal
Gold is valued for being soft and not unmalleable, unlike some harder metals.
Most plastics are pliable, but this particular formula is surprisingly unmalleable.
The material was too unmalleable to be shaped into jewelry by hand.
