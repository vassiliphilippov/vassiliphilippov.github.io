mantle

noun
short, sleeveless cloak-like garment
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˈmæn.tl̩ / (BrE)
a sleeveless garment resembling a cloak but typically shorter, worn over the shoulders, especially in historical contexts

Examples

Velvet mantle
Her velvet mantle fluttered as she entered the hall.
In portraits from the Middle Ages, nobles are often depicted with a mantle draped over their shoulders.
The king wore a richly embroidered mantle during the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cape - a sleeveless outer garment that fastens at the neck and hangs over the back
  • synonym: cloak - a loose outer garment, usually sleeveless, that hangs from the shoulders
