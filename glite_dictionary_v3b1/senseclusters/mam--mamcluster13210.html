mam

noun
term for mother
Pronunciation: / mam / (BrE)
a polite or affectionate term for mother, especially used in some regions of the UK and Ireland

Examples

My mam
He gave his mam a hug before leaving for school.
My mam always makes the best Sunday dinners.
When I was sick, my mam stayed up all night to take care of me.
