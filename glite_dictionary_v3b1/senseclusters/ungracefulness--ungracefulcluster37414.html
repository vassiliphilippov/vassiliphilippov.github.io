ungracefulness

noun
lack of grace or elegance
5.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡreɪsfəlnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡreɪsfəlnəs / (BrE)
a lack of grace or elegance in movement, appearance, or expression; awkwardness or clumsiness

Examples

His ungracefulness in movement
He was embarrassed by his ungracefulness when he tripped during the formal event.
Her ungracefulness was evident on the dance floor, where her movements appeared stiff and awkward.
The sculpture captured a deliberate ungracefulness that reflected the artist's unique style.
