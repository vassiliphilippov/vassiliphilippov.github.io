stern warning

noun
serious warning or reprimand
Pronunciation: / stɝn ˈwɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / stɜːn ˈwɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
a firm and serious warning, often given as a strong caution or reprimand

Examples

A stern warning
After forgetting her homework again, Maria received a stern warning from her teacher.
He was let off with a stern warning, but threatened with suspension if he was late again.
The judge gave the defendant a stern warning before announcing the sentence.
