nkisi

noun
spirit in ritual object
9.9
Pronunciation: / ənˈkiːsi / (AmE), / ənˈkiːsi / (BrE)
a spiritual object or the spirit believed to inhabit it in Central African traditions, thought to have healing or protective powers
