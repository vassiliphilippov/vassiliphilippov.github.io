czarist

adjective
relating to a czar or czarist government
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈzɑrɪst /; / ˈzɑːrɪst / (AmE), / ˈzɑːrɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a czar or the system of government ruled by czars, especially in Russia before 1917.
