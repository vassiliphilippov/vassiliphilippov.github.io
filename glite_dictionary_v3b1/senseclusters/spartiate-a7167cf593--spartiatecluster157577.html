Spartiate

noun
Spartan ruling citizen
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːrtiˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈspɑːtɪeɪt / (BrE)
a full citizen of ancient Sparta, especially one belonging to its ruling class

Examples

A Spartiate leader
Only a Spartiate could participate in the Spartan assembly and vote on important matters.
The strict training of a Spartiate began in childhood and continued throughout life.
Unlike helots, a Spartiate enjoyed full rights and privileges in Spartan society.
