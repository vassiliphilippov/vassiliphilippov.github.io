Stegosaurus stenops

noun
species of plated herbivorous dinosaur
9.1
Pronunciation: /steg.ə.ˈsɔːr.əs ˈste.nɑːps/ (AmE), /steg.ə.ˈsɔːr.əs ˈste.nɒps/ (BrE)
