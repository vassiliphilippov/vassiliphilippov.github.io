krennerite

noun
a gold telluride mineral
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛnərˌaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkrɛnəraɪt / (BrE)
An orthorhombic gold telluride mineral that may contain a small amount of silver.

Examples

Krennerite mineral sample
Some specimens of krennerite contain traces of silver in addition to gold and tellurium.
The geologist discovered a rare sample of krennerite in the old mining district.
Unlike calaverite, krennerite crystallizes in the orthorhombic system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sylvanite - a monoclinic silver-gold telluride mineral
