imaginably

adverb
possibly; conceivably
4.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmædʒɪnəbli / (AmE), / ɪˈmædʒɪnəbli / (BrE)
in a way that can be imagined or conceived; possibly or conceivably.

Examples

It is imaginably possible
It is imaginably possible that life exists elsewhere in the universe.
The project could imaginably be completed ahead of schedule if everyone works together.
She faced every imaginably difficult challenge during her journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conceivably - possibly or believably
  • synonym: possibly - in a way that might happen or be true
  • antonym: unimaginably - in a way that cannot be imagined
