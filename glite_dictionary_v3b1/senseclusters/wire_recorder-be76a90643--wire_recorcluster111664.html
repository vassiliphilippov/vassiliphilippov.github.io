wire recorder

noun
early magnetic audio recorder using wire
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪɚ rɪˌkoʊrdɚ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪə rɪˌkɔːdə / (BrE)
an early type of magnetic audio recorder that used thin steel wire to record and play back sound
