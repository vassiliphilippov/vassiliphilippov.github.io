Acinonyx jubatus

noun
scientific name of the cheetah
Pronunciation: / ˌæsɪˈnoʊnɪks dʒuːˈbeɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌæsɪˈnɒnɪks dʒuːˈbɑːtəs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the cheetah, a large, slender, spotted cat native to Africa and southwestern Asia, known as the fastest land mammal.
