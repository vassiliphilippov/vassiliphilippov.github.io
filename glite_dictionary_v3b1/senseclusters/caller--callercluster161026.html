caller

noun
a social or business visitor
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔlər / (AmE), / ˈkɔːlə / (BrE)
a person who visits someone socially or for business, usually without prior arrangement

Examples

A friendly caller
She was delighted to receive a caller from her hometown yesterday.
The receptionist informed me that a caller was waiting in the lobby.
We had an unexpected caller at the door just after dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: visitor - a person visiting a person or place
