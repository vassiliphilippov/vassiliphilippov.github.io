red lauan

noun
a type of reddish tropical hardwood
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛd luˈɑːn /; / ˌrɛd lɑˈwɑn / (AmE), / ˌrɛd lɑːˈɑːn /; / ˌrɛd lɑːˈwɑːn / (BrE)
a large Philippine tree (Shorea negrosensis) and its hard, reddish wood, used in construction and furniture

Examples

Red lauan wood
Because of its durability, red lauan is popular in furniture making.
Many exporters list red lauan under the name Philippine mahogany.
The cabinets were made from red lauan, giving them a rich, deep color.
Furniture made from red lauan has a distinctive, attractive color.
Many forests in the Philippines are home to the red lauan tree.
The durable wood of the red lauan is highly prized for building houses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Philippine mahogany - generic commercial name for several tropical hardwoods from the Philippines
  • synonym: lauan - any tree of the genus Shorea found in the Philippines, source of commercial timber
