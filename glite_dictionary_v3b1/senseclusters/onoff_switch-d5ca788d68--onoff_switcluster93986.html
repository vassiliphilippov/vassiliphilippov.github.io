on/off switch

noun
a switch with on and off positions
1.1 1.9
Pronunciation: /ɑːn ɑːf swɪtʃ/ /; / /ɑːn ɔːf swɪtʃ/ (AmE), /ɒn ɒf swɪtʃ/ /; / /ən ɒf swɪtʃ/ (BrE)
a switch designed to control the flow of electricity by toggling between the 'on' and 'off' positions

Examples

An on/off switch
Most power tools have an easy-to-reach on/off switch for safety.
She pressed the on/off switch to turn the lamp on.
The computer wouldn't start because the on/off switch was broken.
