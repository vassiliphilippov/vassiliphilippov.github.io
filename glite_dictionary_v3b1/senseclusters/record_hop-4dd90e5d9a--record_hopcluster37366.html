record hop

noun
teen dance party with recorded music
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkərd hɑp / (AmE), / ˈrɛkɔːd hɒp / (BrE)
an informal dance party, especially for teenagers, where popular music is played on records rather than by a live band

Examples

A record hop party
She met her best friend at a record hop in 1961.
Teenagers flocked to the record hop to dance to the latest hits.
The high school gym was decorated for the record hop on Friday night.
