perfectivity

noun
completed action aspect
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜːrfɛkˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌpɜːfɪkˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
the grammatical aspect of a verb that expresses a completed or finished action

Examples

Perfectivity in verbs
In Slavic languages, perfectivity is marked by different verb forms to show completed actions.
The concept of perfectivity helps learners understand when an action is finished in a sentence.
Some languages use prefixes or suffixes to indicate perfectivity in their verbs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aspect - a grammatical category expressing how an action extends over time
  • antonym: imperfectivity - the grammatical aspect expressing ongoing or repeated actions
