Nemean Games

noun
ancient Greek athletic festival at Nemea
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈniːmiən ɡeɪmz / (AmE), / ˈniːmiən ɡeɪmz / (BrE)
the ancient Panhellenic athletic games held every two years at Nemea during the second and fourth years of each Olympiad in ancient Greece

Examples

The Nemean Games festival
Many athletes traveled long distances to compete in the Nemean Games every other year.
The ancient Nemean Games were considered one of the four great Panhellenic festivals of Greece.
Victors in the Nemean Games were awarded crowns made of wild celery.
