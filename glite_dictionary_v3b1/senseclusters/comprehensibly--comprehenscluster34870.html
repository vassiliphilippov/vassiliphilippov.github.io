comprehensibly

adverb
clearly; understandably
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnsəbli / (AmE), / ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnsəbli / (BrE)

Examples

The guide explained the rules comprehensibly, making the process much easier for newcomers.
