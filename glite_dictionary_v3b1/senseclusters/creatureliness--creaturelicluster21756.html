creatureliness

noun
state of being created
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːtʃərlinəs / (AmE), / ˈkriːtʃəlɪnɪs / (BrE)
the state of being a living creature, especially with human limitations, needs, or dependence (often in a philosophical or spiritual sense)

In moments of illness, our creatureliness becomes impossible to ignore.
The film highlights the creatureliness of its characters as they struggle to survive.
