Pronunciation: / ad proʊsɛˈkwɛndəm / (AmE), / ad prəʊsɛˈkwɛndəm / (BrE)
a Latin legal phrase meaning 'for the purpose of prosecuting', used to indicate legal action on someone's behalf

Examples

Ad prosequendum appointment
The judge appointed a guardian ad prosequendum to represent the child's interests in court.
A special administrator ad prosequendum was named to pursue the lawsuit on behalf of the estate.
Legal documents often use the term ad prosequendum when referring to actions taken for prosecution.
