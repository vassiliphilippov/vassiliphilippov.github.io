Cub Scout

noun
junior member of Boy Scouts
Pronunciation: / kʌb skaʊt / (AmE), / kʌb skaʊt / (BrE)
a boy, usually aged between 7 and 10, who is a member of the junior section of the Boy Scouts organization

Examples

Cub Scout badge
As a Cub Scout, he earned badges for helping in his community.
My son became a Cub Scout when he turned eight years old.
The Cub Scouts met every Tuesday night to learn new skills and play games.
