superagency

noun
large multi-service agency
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˈsuːpərəɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
a very large commercial agency, especially in advertising or marketing, that provides a wide range of services under one organization

Examples

Advertising superagency
The new superagency quickly dominated the advertising industry by offering a full range of services.
Many smaller firms merged to form a superagency capable of handling global marketing campaigns.
Clients prefer working with a superagency because they can manage everything from PR to digital media.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: agency - a business that provides a particular service
  • degree higher: conglomerate - a large corporation made up of several different companies
