finger plate

noun
protective plate on a door to prevent finger marks
8.3 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪŋ.gər pleɪt/ (AmE), /ˈfɪŋ.gə pleɪt/ (BrE)
a flat protective covering, usually fitted to a door, to prevent it from being soiled by dirty fingers

Examples

A finger plate on door
Many Victorian houses feature ornate finger plates as part of their original fittings.
The brass finger plate on the old wooden door had grown dull over time.
We decided to install a finger plate to keep fingerprints off the paintwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: door plate - a plate or plaque fixed to a door, sometimes decorative or functional
