parataxis

noun
side-by-side clauses
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈtæksɪs / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈtæksɪs / (BrE)
a style of writing or speaking where clauses, phrases, or ideas are placed side by side without using conjunctions or clear logical connections
