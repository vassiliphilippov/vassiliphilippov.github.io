left

noun
left side of the baseball outfield
Pronunciation: / lɛft / (AmE), / lɛft / (BrE)
(baseball) the part of the outfield to the left of the catcher, as viewed from home plate.

Examples

Left field
A line drive to left brought in the winning run.
He hit a fly ball to left in the seventh inning.
The coach moved the fastest outfielder to left for the final play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: left field - the part of the outfield in baseball behind third base
