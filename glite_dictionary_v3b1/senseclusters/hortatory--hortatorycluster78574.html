hortatory

adjective
giving strong encouragement; urging action
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔːr.təˌtɔːr.i / (AmE), / ˈhɔː.tə.təri / (BrE)
strongly urging someone to take a particular action or adopt a certain course; giving strong encouragement

Examples

Strong hortatory remarks
Her hortatory remarks encouraged students to participate actively in the program.
The coach delivered a hortatory speech to inspire the team before the final match.
The letter contained a hortatory message urging citizens to volunteer their help.
