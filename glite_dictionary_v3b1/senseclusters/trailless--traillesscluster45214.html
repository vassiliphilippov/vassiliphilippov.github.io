trailless

adjective
leaving no trace
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪlləs / (AmE), / ˈtreɪlləs / (BrE)
leaving no tracks, marks, or visible signs behind; not showing any trace of passage

Examples

Trailless movement
The animal moved through the forest in a trailless manner, leaving no sign of its presence.
After the snowfall, the field appeared completely trailless and untouched.
The spy vanished into the night, his escape route trailless and impossible to follow.

  • synonym: trackless - having no tracks or paths; leaving no trace
