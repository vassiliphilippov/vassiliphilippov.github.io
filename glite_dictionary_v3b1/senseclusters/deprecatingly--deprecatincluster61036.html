deprecatingly

adverb
with disapproval
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛprəˌkeɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈdɛprɪˌkeɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows disapproval, criticism, or a lack of value for something or someone

Examples

Look deprecatingly at mistakes
He glanced deprecatingly at the messy room, clearly unhappy with its condition.
The manager spoke deprecatingly about the team's recent performance in the meeting.
She laughed deprecatingly when someone complimented her artwork, downplaying her talent.

Related Terms

  • antonym: approvingly - in a way that shows approval or praise
  • synonym: critically - in a way that expresses disapproval or judgment
