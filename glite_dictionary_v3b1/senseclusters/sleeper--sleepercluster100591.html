sleeper

noun
convertible sofa or furniture-bed
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːpər / (AmE), / ˈsliːpə / (BrE)
A piece of furniture, such as a sofa, that can be converted into a bed.

Examples

Sleeper sofa bed
She unfolded the sleeper each night before going to sleep.
The small apartment was furnished with a compact sleeper instead of a traditional bed.
We bought a sleeper for the guest room so visitors can stay overnight.
