dog sled

noun
sled pulled by dogs
Pronunciation: / dɔɡ slɛd / (AmE), / dɒɡ slɛd / (BrE)
A sled pulled by one or more dogs, typically used for transportation over snow or ice.

Examples

A dog sled ride
During the festival, visitors can take a ride on a traditional dog sled through the snowy forest.
Racing a dog sled requires skill, stamina, and excellent communication with the dogs.
The team loaded their supplies onto the dog sled before setting out across the tundra.
