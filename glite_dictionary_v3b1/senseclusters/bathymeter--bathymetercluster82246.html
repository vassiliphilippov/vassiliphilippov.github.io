bathymeter

noun
instrument for measuring water depth
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈθɪm.ə.tər / (AmE), / bəˈθɪm.ɪ.tə / (BrE)
an instrument used for measuring the depth of water, especially in oceans or lakes

Examples

A bathymeter instrument
A modern bathymeter can take automated readings during underwater surveys.
The oceanographers used a bathymeter to chart the depth of the trench.
Their research vessel was equipped with a highly precise bathymeter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depth gauge - an instrument for measuring the depth of something, especially water
