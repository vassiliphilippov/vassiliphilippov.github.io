beau ideal

noun
the perfect example of something
Pronunciation: / boʊ iːˈdɑːl / (AmE), / bəʊ iːˈdɑːl / (BrE)
The perfect or most admired example of something; an ideal embodiment of a concept.

Examples

The beau ideal example
For many writers, Shakespeare remains the beau ideal of literary genius.
She was considered the beau ideal of grace and beauty in her time.
The ancient statue was the beau ideal of classical sculpture.
