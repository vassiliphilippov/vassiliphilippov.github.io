toward

preposition
contributing to amount
Pronunciation: / təˈwɔrd / (AmE), / təˈwɔːd / (BrE)
used to indicate a contribution or payment as part of the total amount needed for something

Examples

Money toward a gift
She donated five dollars toward the cost of the new library books.
Every student contributed a small amount toward the class trip expenses.
He put his birthday money toward buying a new bicycle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: for - used to indicate the purpose or recipient of something
