In a way that shows great care, dedication, or regularity; with extreme conscientiousness.

Examples

Religiously follows a routine
He exercises religiously every morning before breakfast.
She religiously checks her email throughout the day.
They religiously follow all the safety procedures at work.

  • assiduously

  • faithfully
