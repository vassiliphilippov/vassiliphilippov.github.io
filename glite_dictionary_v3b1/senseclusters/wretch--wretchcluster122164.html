wretch

noun
wicked or despicable person
Pronunciation: / rɛtʃ / (AmE), / rɛtʃ / (BrE)
a person who behaves in a wicked, despicable, or unpleasant way

Examples

A wicked wretch
After his crimes were revealed, everyone in the town shunned the wretch.
Only a true wretch would betray his closest friend for money.
The cruel wretch stole food from the hungry children.

Related Terms

  • antonym: saint - a person of great virtue, regarded as good or holy
  • synonym: scoundrel - a dishonest or unscrupulous person
  • synonym: villain - a person who is evil or wicked
