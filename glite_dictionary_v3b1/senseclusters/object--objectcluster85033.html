object

verb
express disapproval; oppose
7.3 4.2
Pronunciation: / əˈbdʒɛkt /; / əbˈdʒɛkt / (AmE), / əbˈdʒɛkt / (BrE)
to express or feel disapproval of something; to be opposed to something

Examples

To object strongly

Several members of the audience objected to the speaker's remarks.
She objected that the proposal was unfair to the employees.
