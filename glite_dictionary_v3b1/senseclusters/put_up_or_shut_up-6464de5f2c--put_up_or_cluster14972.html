put up or shut up

verb
take action or stop talking
2.7
Pronunciation: / pʊt ʌp ɔr ʃʌt ʌp / (AmE), / pʊt ʌp ɔː ʃʌt ʌp / (BrE)
used to tell someone to take action on what they are saying or stop talking about it
