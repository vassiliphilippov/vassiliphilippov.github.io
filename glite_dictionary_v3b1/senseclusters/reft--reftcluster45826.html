reft

adjective
deprived; bereft
Pronunciation: / rɛft / (AmE), / rɛft / (BrE)
deprived of or lacking something, especially as a result of loss or removal

Examples

Reft of hope
He wandered the fields, reft of hope and comfort.
The castle stood reft of its former glory after the long siege.
She felt utterly reft when her closest friend moved away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deprived - lacking something that is needed or wanted
