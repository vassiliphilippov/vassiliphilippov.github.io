espouser

noun
supporter or advocate
Pronunciation: / ɪˈspaʊzər / (AmE), / ɪˈspaʊzə / (BrE)
a person who supports, adopts, or advocates a particular idea, cause, or belief

Examples

Active espouser of change
He became a leading espouser of renewable energy in his community.
As an espouser of equal rights, she often spoke at public events.
The organization is seeking an espouser to help promote its mission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocate - a person who publicly supports or recommends a cause or policy
  • antonym: opponent - a person who disagrees with or fights against an idea or cause
  • synonym: proponent - a person who argues in favor of something
