triple

verb
to make or become three times as much
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpəl / (BrE)

Examples

To triple sales

The factory managed to triple its production in just two years.
Within a decade, the city's population could triple if current growth continues.
