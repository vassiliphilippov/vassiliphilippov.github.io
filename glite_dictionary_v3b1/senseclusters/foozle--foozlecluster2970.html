foozle

noun
clumsy mistake
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːzəl / (AmE), / ˈfuːzəl / (BrE)
a clumsy or bungled attempt, especially a mistake made in a game or sport.

Examples

A golf foozle
His final foozle on the golf course cost him the match.
The chef's foozle with the sauce ruined the entire meal.
Despite an early foozle, the team managed to recover and win the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunder - a careless or stupid mistake
  • synonym: fumble - an awkward or clumsy mistake, especially in sports
  • antonym: success - the achievement of a desired result
