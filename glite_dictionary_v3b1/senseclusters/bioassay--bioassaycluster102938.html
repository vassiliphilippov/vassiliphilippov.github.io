bioassay

verb
test substance's effect on living organisms
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˈæ.seɪ / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊˈæ.seɪ / (BrE)
to determine the biological activity or concentration of a substance by testing its effects on living organisms or cells.

Examples

To bioassay a sample
Each sample was bioassayed for its toxic effects on the bacterial culture.
The laboratory will bioassay the new drug to determine its potency.
They are currently bioassaying the compounds for antiviral properties.
