swaggerer

noun
person who acts arrogantly
Pronunciation: / ˈswæɡərər / (AmE), / ˈswæɡərə / (BrE)
a person who walks or behaves in a very confident and typically arrogant or boastful way

Examples

A swaggerer walks proudly
Despite his reputation as a swaggerer, few people took his boasts seriously.
He was known as a swaggerer in the office, always bragging about his successes.
The swaggerer strutted down the street, drawing annoyed glances from passersby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braggart - someone who boasts about achievements or possessions
  • antonym: modest person - someone who does not show off or behave arrogantly
