workflow management

noun
organizing and overseeing work processes
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkˌfloʊ ˌmænɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌfləʊ ˌmænɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the process of planning, organizing, and overseeing the stages of work activities within an organization to improve efficiency and productivity

Examples

Effective workflow management
Effective workflow management can dramatically reduce project completion times in large organizations.
Good workflow management requires clear communication and careful planning at every stage.
The company adopted new software to streamline workflow management across departments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: process management - the administration of business processes to achieve organizational goals
