polished

adjective
refined and elegant, socially assured
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːlɪʃt / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪʃt / (BrE)
showing refinement, elegance, and confidence, especially as a result of wide social experience

Examples

Polished manners
He greeted his guests with a polished charm that made them feel at ease.
Her polished presentation impressed everyone at the meeting.
The diplomat's polished manners were evident at every event he attended.
