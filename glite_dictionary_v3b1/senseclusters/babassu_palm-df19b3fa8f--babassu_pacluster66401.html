babassu palm

noun
Brazilian palm tree yielding edible oil and vegetable ivory
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈbɑːsu ˌpɑːm / (AmE), / bəˈbasuː ˌpɑːm / (BrE)
a tall feather palm (Attalea speciosa) native to northern Brazil, bearing hard-shelled nuts that yield valuable oil and a type of vegetable ivory

Examples

The babassu palm tree
Artisans carve small sculptures from the vegetable ivory found inside babassu palm nuts.
Oil extracted from the babassu palm is used in cooking and cosmetics.
The babassu palm is a crucial source of income for many communities in northern Brazil.
