babassu palm

noun
Brazilian palm tree yielding edible oil and vegetable ivory
9.9 7.9
Pronunciation: /bə.ˈbɑː.suː pɑːm/ (AmE), /bə.ˈbɑː.suː pɑːm/ (BrE)
a tall feather palm (Attalea speciosa) native to northern Brazil, bearing hard-shelled nuts that yield valuable oil and a type of vegetable ivory
