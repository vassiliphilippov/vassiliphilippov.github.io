twig blight

noun
disease of woody plant twig tips
8.7 7.3
Pronunciation: /twɪg blaɪt/ (AmE), /twɪg blaɪt/ (BrE)
a disease affecting the tips or ends of twigs on woody plants, often causing them to die back

Examples

Twig blight disease
Gardeners must prune affected branches promptly to control twig blight.
Several species of fungi can cause twig blight in ornamental shrubs.
The apple orchard suffered from severe twig blight after a wet spring.
