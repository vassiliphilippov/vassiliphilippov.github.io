pseudo

noun
a person making false pretenses
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsjuːdəʊ / (BrE)
A person who pretends to be something they are not; an impostor or fake.

Examples

A pseudo friend
Beware of the pseudo in the group—he is always pretending to be someone he's not.
Everyone thought he was a real expert, but he turned out to be a pseudo.
The club quickly exposed the pseudo who claimed to be an Olympic athlete.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fake - a person who is not what they claim to be
  • antonym: genuine - being truly what something is said to be
  • synonym: impostor - a person who pretends to be someone else
