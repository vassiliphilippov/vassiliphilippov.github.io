defervescence

noun
reduction of a fever
8.2 9.5
Pronunciation: /de.fər.ˈves.əns/ /; / /də.fər.ˈves.əns/ (AmE), /de.fə.ˈves.əns/ /; / /də.fə.ˈves.əns/ (BrE)
the reduction or subsiding of a fever, as indicated by a drop in body temperature

Examples

The defervescence period
A period of defervescence usually follows the administration of antipyretic medication.
Rapid defervescence often indicates a good response to treatment in infectious diseases.
The patient's defervescence was noted by the nursing staff after three days of high fever.
