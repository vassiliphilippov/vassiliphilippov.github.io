tapis

noun
decorative heavy textile
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpɪs / (AmE), / ˈtapɪs / (BrE)
A heavy, decorated woven textile used for curtains or upholstery.

Examples

A tapis curtain
A beautiful tapis covered the old oak table in the drawing room.
She admired the intricate design of the tapis used for the settee.
The colonial mansion boasted windows draped with an ornate tapis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tapestry - a piece of thick textile fabric with pictures or designs, used as a wall hanging or furniture covering
