amenability

noun
willingness to cooperate
Pronunciation: / əˌmɛnəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / əˌmiːnəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being willing to cooperate or be influenced; openness to suggestion or influence

Examples

Amenability to feedback
Her amenability to feedback made her a valued member of the team.
His amenability to new ideas encouraged creative discussion among colleagues.
The amenability of the witnesses ensured the investigation progressed smoothly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cooperativeness - the quality of being cooperative
  • antonym: stubbornness - the quality of being unwilling to cooperate or change one's mind
