western

adjective
of or relating to the West (Europe and the Americas)
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstərn / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the western parts of the world, especially Europe and the Americas, as distinguished from Asia or the Orient.

Examples

Western culture
Globalization has influenced western economies in both positive and negative ways.
Many western countries celebrate Christmas as a major holiday.
The philosopher is famous for his critical analysis of western thought.

Related Terms

  • antonym: eastern - relating to the eastern parts of the world, especially Asia; opposite of western
