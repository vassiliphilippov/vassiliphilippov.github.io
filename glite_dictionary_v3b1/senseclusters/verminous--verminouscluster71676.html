verminous

adjective
of or relating to vermin; repulsive
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜːrmɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈvɜːmɪnəs / (BrE)
of the nature of vermin; extremely offensive or repulsive; infested with or resembling vermin.

Examples

Verminous behavior
His verminous behavior disgusted everyone in the community.
The old mattress was so verminous that no one dared touch it.
They found themselves in a verminous alley, crawling with rats and insects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - free from dirt, marks, or mess; not repulsive
  • synonym: loathsome - extremely unpleasant or repulsive
