Extraneous sounds that interfere with or are present during the recording, transmission, or perception of a desired signal.

Examples

Loud background noise
During the meeting, the background noise from the construction site made it difficult to hear the speaker.
He used special filters to remove background noise from the audio track.
Sensitive microphones often pick up background noise that can distort the recording quality.
