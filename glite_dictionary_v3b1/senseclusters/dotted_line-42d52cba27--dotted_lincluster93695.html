dotted line

noun
a line made of dots or dashes, esp. for signatures
4.1 2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑt̬ɪd laɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɒtɪd laɪn / (BrE)
a line consisting of a series of dots or short dashes, used especially to indicate where something should be written or signed on a document
