Synonyms

  • stabilize
    4.3 3/m
    stabilize is used when something becomes more steady or stops changing gradually, while level off describes a trend that becomes flat after rising or falling.
  • plateau
    5.1 0.07/m
    plateau means stop increasing and stay the same for a time, while level off can mean becoming steady after rising or falling.
  • flatline
    6.1 0.1/m
    flatline means stop changing and stay at an unchanging level, while level off is a neutral term for becoming steady.
