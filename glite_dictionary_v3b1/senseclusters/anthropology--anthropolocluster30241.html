anthropology

noun
the study of human origins and societies
Pronunciation: / ˌænθrəˈpɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌænθrəˈpɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the origins, development, and social relationships of human beings

Examples

Cultural anthropology
His research in anthropology focuses on ancient civilizations.
Many universities offer courses in cultural anthropology.
She decided to study anthropology because she was fascinated by different cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sociology - the study of the development, structure, and functioning of human society
