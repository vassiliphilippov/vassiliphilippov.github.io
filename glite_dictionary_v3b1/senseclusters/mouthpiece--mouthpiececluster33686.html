mouthpiece

noun
protective device for the mouth (sports)
1.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmaʊθ.piːs/ (AmE), /ˈmaʊθ.piːs/ (BrE)
A protective device worn inside the mouth, especially by athletes, to shield the teeth and mouth from injury.
