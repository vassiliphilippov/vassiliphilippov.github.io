urgh

interjection
disgust or frustration
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɜːr / (AmE), / ɜː / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express disgust, frustration, or annoyance

Examples

Say urgh loudly
When she saw the spoiled milk, she exclaimed, 'urgh, that smells terrible!'
He stepped in something sticky and muttered 'urgh' under his breath.
After hearing the bad news, she just sighed and said 'urgh'.
