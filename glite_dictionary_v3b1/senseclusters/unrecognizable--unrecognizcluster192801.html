unrecognizable

adjective
impossible to recognize
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈrɛkəɡˌnaɪzəbəl /; / ʌnˌrɛkəɡˈnaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈɡnʌɪzəbl /; / ʌnˌrɛkəɡˈnaɪzəbəl / (BrE)
Impossible to recognize, especially due to damage, change, or alteration.

Examples

Completely unrecognizable face
After the fire, the old house was completely unrecognizable to its former residents.
His handwriting had become so messy it was nearly unrecognizable.
Without her glasses, the actor was unrecognizable to most fans.
After her dramatic makeover, she was almost unrecognisable from her previous self.
The city skyline became unrecognisable after years of rapid development.
The old factory was so badly burned it was completely unrecognisable to former workers.
