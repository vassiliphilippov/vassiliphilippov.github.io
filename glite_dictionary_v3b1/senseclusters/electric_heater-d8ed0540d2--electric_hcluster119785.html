electric heater

noun
device that heats by electricity
2.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈhiːtər / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛk.trɪk ˈhiː.tə / (BrE)
a device that uses electricity to produce heat, typically for warming a room or small space

Examples

An electric heater device
She turned off the electric heater before leaving for work.
The old cottage was cold, so they relied on a small electric heater for comfort.
We bought an electric heater to keep the living room warm during winter.
