inaptly

adverb
awkwardly; unsuitably
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈæptli / (AmE), / ɪnˈæptli / (BrE)
in a way that is not suitable, appropriate, or fitting for the situation (including clumsy or awkward manner)

Examples

To inaptly describe
He inaptly described the complex issue with a simple joke that confused everyone.
The manager inaptly handled the complaint, making the customer even more upset.
She inaptly chose words that did not fit the seriousness of the situation.
He inaptly compared the simple problem to a major crisis.
The joke was inaptly timed during the serious meeting.
Her comments were inaptly directed at the wrong audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aptly - in a suitable or appropriate way
  • synonym: awkwardly - in a clumsy or uncomfortable way
  • synonym: inappropriately - in a manner not suitable for the context
  • synonym: unsuitably - in a way that is not appropriate
