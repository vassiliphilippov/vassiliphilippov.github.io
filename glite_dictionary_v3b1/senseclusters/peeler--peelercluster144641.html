peeler

noun
tool for removing skin of vegetables or fruits
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːlər / (AmE), / ˈpiːlə / (BrE)
A small hand-held device with a sharp blade used to remove the outer skin from vegetables or fruits.

Examples

A peeler tool
Every kitchen should have a good quality peeler for fruits and vegetables.
I used a peeler to quickly remove the skin from the apples.
This peeler makes preparing potatoes much easier and faster.
