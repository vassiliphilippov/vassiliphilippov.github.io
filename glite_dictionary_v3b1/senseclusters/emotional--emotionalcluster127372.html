Synonyms

    6.0 1/m
    emotive often aims to stir strong feelings in writing or speech, while emotional describes a person who shows strong feelings.
  • impassioned
    5.8 2/m
    impassioned expresses intense feeling in speech or writing, while emotional is a broader word for showing strong feelings.
  • passionate
    2.8 20/m
    passionate shows very strong desire or love in actions and words, while emotional covers many kinds of strong feelings.
  • excitable
    4.1 0.8/m
    excitable reacts quickly and strongly to events, while emotional more generally means someone who shows or feels strong emotions.
  • sensitive
    4.4 0.01/m
    sensitive easily notices and is hurt by others' feelings or moods, while emotional refers to showing strong personal feelings.
  • worked up
    6.5 0.8/m
    worked up describes being upset or agitated in the moment, while emotional is a general trait of showing strong feelings.
  • excited
    1.0 40/m

  • aroused
    5.6 0.01/m
