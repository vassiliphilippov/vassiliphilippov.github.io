pyrethrin

noun
natural insecticide
Pronunciation: / paɪˈriːθrɪn / (AmE), / paɪˈriːθrɪn / (BrE)
a natural chemical compound derived from certain chrysanthemum flowers, used as an insecticide to kill insects

Examples

Pyrethrin insecticide spray
Many organic farmers prefer pyrethrin because it breaks down quickly in the environment.
The spray contains pyrethrin to help control mosquitoes in the garden.
Always wear gloves when handling pyrethrin products to avoid skin irritation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insecticide - a substance used to kill insects
  • synonym: pesticide - a chemical used to kill pests, including insects
