Synonyms

    3.7 1/m
    patchy is uneven in parts and often shows good and bad areas, while spotty means low or mixed quality overall.
  • uneven
    2.3 5/m
    uneven has varying surface or quality that changes without pattern, while spotty describes things with irregular, inconsistent performance.
  • flaky
    7.0 0.2/m
    flaky is informal and suggests something or someone fails or acts unpredictably, while spotty describes uneven quality or performance.
