total twathead

noun
insult for a very foolish or annoying person
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊtl ˈtwæthɛd / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtl ˈtwæthɛd / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) A person considered extremely foolish or annoying, used as an insulting term.

Only a total twathead would forget to lock the door when leaving.
