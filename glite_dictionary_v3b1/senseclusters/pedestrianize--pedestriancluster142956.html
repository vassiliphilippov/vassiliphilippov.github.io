pedestrianize

verb
to make dull
7.3
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈde.strɪ.ə.naɪz/ /; / /pə.ˈde.strɪ.ən.aɪz/ (AmE), /pə.ˈde.strɪ.ə.naɪz/ /; / /pə.ˈde.strɪ.ən.aɪz/ (BrE)

Examples

To pedestrianize a story

Critics argued that the new translation would pedestrianize the poetry's original beauty.
Some edits can unintentionally pedestrianize a story, stripping it of its unique charm.
