rigger

noun
equipment operator
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈrɪɡə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to set up, operate, or maintain lifting or rigging equipment, especially for moving heavy objects

Examples

Construction rigger
The experienced rigger checked all the safety harnesses before the team began work.
A rigger is essential on construction sites where heavy loads must be moved safely.
During the event setup, the rigger installed lighting equipment high above the stage.
The rigger carefully inspected the crane before starting the operation.
A skilled rigger is essential for safely moving large machinery at the construction site.
Before the concert, the rigger set up the lighting equipment above the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: operator - a person who controls machinery or equipment
  • synonym: technician - a worker skilled in the technical details of a job
