minaret

noun
slender tower of a mosque
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪnəˈrɛt / (AmE), / ˌmɪnəˈrɛt / (BrE)
a tall, slender tower, typically part of a mosque, with one or more balconies from which the call to prayer is made

Examples

A minaret tower
A muezzin climbed the minaret each day to make the call to prayer.
The mosque's minaret rose high above the city skyline, visible from miles away.
Tourists admired the intricate details on the ancient minaret during their guided tour.
