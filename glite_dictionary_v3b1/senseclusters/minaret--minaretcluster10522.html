minaret

noun
slender tower of a mosque
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪnəˈrɛt / (AmE), / ˌmɪnəˈrɛt / (BrE)
a tall, slender tower, typically part of a mosque, with one or more balconies from which the call to prayer is made
