refabrication

noun
the act of assembling again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌfæb.rɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌfæb.rɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of assembling or constructing something again, especially after disassembly or initial fabrication

Examples

Complete refabrication process
After inspection, the team decided a complete refabrication of the machine was necessary.
Modern techniques have made refabrication of complex components more efficient and precise.
The refabrication of the damaged bridge took several months to complete.
