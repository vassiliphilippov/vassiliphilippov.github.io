Synonyms

  • unconversant
    8.4 0.01/m
    unconversant means not familiar or knowledgeable about a subject, while illiterate stresses lacking formal education or training in that subject.
  • uninitiated
    8.0 0.83/m
    uninitiated has had no prior experience and is new to a field, while illiterate implies lacking education or knowledge there.
  • unknowledgeable
    7.1 0.06/m
    unknowledgeable simply lacks facts or information about a topic, while illiterate suggests broader lack of education or subject skill.
  • uninformed
    3.7 3.41/m

  • ignorant
    3.7 23/m
