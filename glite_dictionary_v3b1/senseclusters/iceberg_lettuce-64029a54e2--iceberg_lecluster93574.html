iceberg lettuce

noun
a type of crisp, firm lettuce
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsbɝɡ ˈlɛtɪs / (AmE), / ˈaɪsbɜːɡ ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
a variety of lettuce with crisp, tightly packed pale green leaves forming a firm, round head

Examples

A leaf of iceberg lettuce
Many fast-food burgers use a leaf of iceberg lettuce for its texture and freshness.
She added chopped iceberg lettuce to the salad for extra crunch.
The grocery store was out of iceberg lettuce, so I bought romaine instead.
