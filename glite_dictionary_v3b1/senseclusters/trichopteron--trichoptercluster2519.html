trichopteron

noun
caddis fly; insect of the order Trichoptera
Pronunciation: / traɪˈkɑptərɒn / (AmE), / trɪˈkɒptərɒn / (BrE)
A caddis fly, an insect of the order Trichoptera, characterized by aquatic larvae and adults with hairy wings.
