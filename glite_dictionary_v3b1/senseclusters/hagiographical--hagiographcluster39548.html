hagiographical

adjective
about saints' lives
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhæɡiəˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌhæɡiəˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the writing or study of the lives of saints or religious biographies.

Examples

Hagiographical biography
The author took a hagiographical approach, focusing on the saint's miracles and virtues.
Many medieval texts are hagiographical, describing the lives of Christian saints in great detail.
Her research includes several hagiographical accounts from the early church period.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biographical - relating to the story of a person's life
  • antonym: secular - not connected with religious or spiritual matters
