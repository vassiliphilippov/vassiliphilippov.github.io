dear

noun
sweet or gentle person (esp. a child)
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪr / (AmE), / dɪə / (BrE)
a person who is loved, sweet, or used as a term of endearment

Examples

My dear

He's a real dear, always polite and gentle with others.
The little dear brought me flowers from the garden.
Come here, dear, and let me help you with your coat.
Good night, my dear; sweet dreams!
She was always a dear to everyone she met.
