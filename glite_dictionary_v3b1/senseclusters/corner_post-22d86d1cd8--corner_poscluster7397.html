corner post

noun
vertical support at building corner
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnɚ ˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnə ˌpəʊst / (BrE)
A vertical support placed at the angle where two walls meet, supporting the structure at the corner of a building.

Examples

A corner post support
A damaged corner post can compromise the whole structure of the shed.
The new barn was stabilized by securing each corner post into a concrete base.
They checked the alignment of every corner post before starting construction of the walls.
