meningocele

noun
congenital neural defect; sac with cerebrospinal fluid
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈnɪŋɡəˌsil / (AmE), / məˈnɪŋɡəˌsiːl / (BrE)
A congenital medical condition in which a sac containing cerebrospinal fluid, but no nerve tissue, protrudes from the brain or spinal meninges.

Examples

A meningocele sac
Prenatal ultrasounds sometimes reveal a meningocele so that treatment can be planned early.
Surgical intervention is often required to repair a meningocele and prevent future complications.
The doctors diagnosed the newborn with a meningocele after observing the protruding sac on his lower back.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spina bifida - a birth defect where the spine and spinal cord don't form properly
