steeler

noun
steel factory worker
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːlər / (AmE), / ˈstiːlə / (BrE)
a person who works in the production or processing of steel, especially in a steelworks

Examples

Experienced steelers in factory
The experienced steelers operated the heavy machinery in the factory with great skill.
Many steelers lost their jobs when the old steel plant closed down.
The union negotiated better working conditions for the steelers at the mill.

Related Terms

  • antonym: miner - a person who works in a mine extracting minerals
  • synonym: steelworker - a person who works in a steel mill or steel industry
