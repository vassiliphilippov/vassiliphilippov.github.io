bloodless

adjective
without blood or bloodshed
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌd.ləs / (AmE), / ˈblʌd.ləs / (BrE)
Without blood or not involving bloodshed; lacking violence, especially in reference to events such as surgeries or coups.

Examples

Bloodless revolution
A bloodless operation reduces the risk of infection for patients.
The company underwent a bloodless transition of leadership after the founder retired.
The revolution was remarkable because it was entirely bloodless.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peaceful - free from disturbance; tranquil, not involving conflict or violence
  • antonym: violent - involving the use of physical force intended to hurt, damage, or kill
