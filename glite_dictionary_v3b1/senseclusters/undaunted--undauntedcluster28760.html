undaunted

adjective
not discouraged or disheartened
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdɔntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdɔːntɪd / (BrE)
not discouraged or intimidated; remaining confident and determined in the face of difficulty or danger

Examples

Despite several setbacks, his undaunted spirit inspired everyone around him.

Despite the storm, the hikers pressed on, undaunted by the worsening weather.
She remained undaunted by the harsh criticism and continued her research.
The team was undaunted by the early setbacks and played on with determination.
