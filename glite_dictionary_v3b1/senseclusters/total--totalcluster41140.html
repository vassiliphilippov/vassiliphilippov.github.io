total

noun
sum of several numbers
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊ.t̬əl /; / ˈtoʊtəl / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl /; / ˈtəʊtəl / (BrE)
the sum or whole amount you get when you add numbers or quantities together

Examples

A total amount
She calculated the total of her monthly expenses.
The total for your groceries comes to $45.80.
Their fundraising efforts resulted in a total of over a thousand dollars.
She calculated the total of all her monthly expenses.
The total of your bill comes to $45.50.
The total rose significantly after including the last item.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggregate - the total obtained by adding together different amounts
  • antonym: partial - not complete; not the whole amount
  • synonym: sum - the total amount resulting from the addition of two or more numbers
