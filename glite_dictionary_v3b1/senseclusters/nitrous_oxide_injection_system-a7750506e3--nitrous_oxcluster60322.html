nitrous oxide injection system

noun
mechanical system injecting nitrous oxide for engine power
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtrəs ɑkˌsaɪd ɪnˈdʒɛkʃən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtrəs ɒkˌsaɪd ɪnˈdʒɛkʃən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
