Chrysophyllum oliviforme

noun
tropical American timber tree with purple fruit
Pronunciation: / kraɪsoʊˈfɪləm ɒˌlɪvəˈfɔːrmiː / (AmE), / krɪsəˈfɪləm ɒˌlɪvɪˈfɔːmiː / (BrE)
A tropical American tree with dark, hard, heavy wood and small purple fruits resembling plums; valued for its timber.

Examples

Chrysophyllum oliviforme tree
Many parks in southern Florida feature Chrysophyllum oliviforme among their native plant species.
The wood of the Chrysophyllum oliviforme is prized by local carpenters for its durability.
We tasted the small, purple fruit of a Chrysophyllum oliviforme tree while visiting the botanical garden.
