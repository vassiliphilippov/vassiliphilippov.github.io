gastronome

noun
person keen on fine food and drink
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæstrəˌnoʊm / (AmE), / ˈɡæstrənəʊm / (BrE)
a person who enjoys and appreciates fine food and drink; a gourmet

Examples

A gastronome enjoys food

The magazine featured an interview with a renowned gastronome from Paris.
