clear-sighted

adjective
mentally acute; able to judge clearly
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrˌsaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈklɪəˌsaɪtɪd / (BrE)
having the ability to think clearly, understand things quickly, and make accurate judgments

Examples

Clear-sighted thinking
Despite the pressure, she remained clear-sighted and made the right decision.
Her clear-sighted analysis helped the company avoid serious mistakes.
We need a clear-sighted leader who can handle this crisis calmly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farsighted - able to understand what will be needed in the future
  • synonym: insightful - showing a deep understanding of a situation or problem
  • synonym: perceptive - having a good understanding of things; quick to notice or realize
  • antonym: shortsighted - lacking foresight; not considering the future
