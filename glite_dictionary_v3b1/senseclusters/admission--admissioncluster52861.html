Synonyms

  • acknowledgment
    4.2 2/m
    acknowledgment is a general recognition of truth or fact, while admission often implies accepting something previously denied or hidden.
  • avowal
    8.2 0.03/m
    avowal is a formal public declaration of belief or feeling, while admission names the act of admitting a truth once denied.
  • confession
    3.4 16/m
    confession is a statement admitting wrongdoing or a crime, while admission is a broader acknowledgment of any truth, not only guilt.
