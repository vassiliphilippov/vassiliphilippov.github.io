a mile away

adverb
one mile distant; obvious or easy to notice
2.2
Pronunciation: / ə ˈmaɪl əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ə ˈmaɪl əˈweɪ / (BrE)
at a distance of one mile from a place; (figurative) very obvious or easy to detect.

Examples

Obvious a mile away
Her excitement was obvious a mile away.
It's so quiet here that you can hear a train coming a mile away.
The bakery is only a mile away from my apartment, so I walk there every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: far - at a great distance in space or time
  • antonym: near - close to someone or something in distance
