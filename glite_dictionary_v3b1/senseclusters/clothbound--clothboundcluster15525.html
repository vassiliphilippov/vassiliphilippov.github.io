clothbound

adjective
covered in cloth
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈklɔθˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈklɒθˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
having a cover made of cloth, especially used to describe books with cloth covers

Examples

Clothbound book cover
The library displayed a rare clothbound edition of the novel in its special collection.
He enjoys the feel of clothbound books more than those with glossy covers.
Many collectors seek out clothbound versions for their durability and classic appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hardcover - a book with a rigid protective cover
  • antonym: paperback - a book with a flexible paper cover
