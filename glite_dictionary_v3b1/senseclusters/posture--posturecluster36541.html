posture

noun
the position of the body or limbs
1.0 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑːs.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈpɒs.tʃə/ (BrE)
the way in which someone holds and positions their body when sitting or standing

Examples

Good posture
Good posture can help prevent back pain and other health problems.
He adjusted his posture before speaking to the audience.
The doctor observed her posture while she was sitting in the chair.
He was praised for his confident posture during the interview.
Her posture improved noticeably after she started yoga classes.
Sitting with poor posture can cause back pain over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pose - a particular position in which someone stands or sits
  • antonym: slouch - a drooping or careless way of standing or sitting
  • synonym: stance - the way in which someone stands, especially when deliberately adopted
