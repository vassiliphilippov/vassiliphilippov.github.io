electric motor

noun
machine converting electricity to motion
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈməʊtə / (BrE)
A machine that converts electrical energy into mechanical motion.

Examples

An electric motor repair
He repaired the electric motor in his garage door opener last weekend.
Most modern washing machines use an electric motor to spin the drum.
The factory relies on a powerful electric motor to keep its conveyor belts running.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engine - a machine with moving parts that converts power into motion
  • synonym: motor - a machine that produces motion or power for doing work
