transpacific

adjective
across the Pacific
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænspəˈsɪfɪk / (AmE), / ˌtrænspəˈsɪfɪk / (BrE)
crossing, spanning, or relating to travel or communication across the Pacific Ocean

Examples

Transpacific flight
The airline launched a new transpacific route between Los Angeles and Tokyo.
A transpacific cable allows for faster internet connections between Asia and North America.
Many goods are shipped on transpacific vessels from China to the United States.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transatlantic - crossing or relating to travel across the Atlantic Ocean
