boggling

adjective
astonishing or confusing
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑɡlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɒɡlɪŋ / (BrE)
very surprising or difficult to understand because it is so unusual or amazing

Examples

A boggling puzzle
The complexity of the puzzle was truly boggling for everyone in the room.
She found the amount of information in the report absolutely boggling.
The special effects in the movie were so boggling that the audience gasped in amazement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astonishing - very surprising or impressive
  • synonym: confusing - difficult to understand or unclear
  • antonym: ordinary - not unusual or special
