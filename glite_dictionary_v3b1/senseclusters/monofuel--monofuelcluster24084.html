monofuel

adjective
single-fuel powered
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnoʊˌfjuːl / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəʊˌfjuːl / (BrE)
designed to operate using only one type of fuel

Examples

Monofuel engine
The new generator is monofuel and runs only on diesel.
A monofuel vehicle cannot switch between gasoline and alternative fuels.
Some rocket engines are designed as monofuel systems for simplicity and safety.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bifuel - able to operate using two types of fuel
  • synonym: single-fuel - using only one type of fuel
