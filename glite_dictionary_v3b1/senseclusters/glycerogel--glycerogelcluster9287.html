glycerogel

noun
medicated skin preparation with glycerin and gelatin
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɪsəˌroʊˌdʒɛl / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪsərəʊˌdʒɛl / (BrE)
A medicated preparation for the skin made from glycerin and glycerinated gelatin, used as a base for topical medications.

Examples

A glycerogel base
A glycerogel base ensures that the ointment is absorbed efficiently without leaving a greasy residue.
Many pharmacies can prepare custom glycerogels for patients with sensitive skin.
The doctor prescribed a glycerogel to treat her dry, irritated skin.
