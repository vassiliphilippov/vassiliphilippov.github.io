cheering

noun
encouragement from a crowd
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
encouragement or support expressed by shouts or applause from a crowd of spectators

Examples

Loud cheering crowd
Despite the rain, there was plenty of cheering at the parade.
Her speech was interrupted several times by enthusiastic cheering.
The team's victory was met with loud cheering from their fans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: applause - approval or praise expressed by clapping or cheering
  • antonym: booing - expressing disapproval by shouting 'boo' or similar sounds
