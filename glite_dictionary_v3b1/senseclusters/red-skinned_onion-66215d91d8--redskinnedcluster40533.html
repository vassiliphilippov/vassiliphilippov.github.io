red-skinned onion

noun
onion with red-colored outer skin
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛd skɪnd ˈʌnjən / (AmE), / ˈrɛd skɪnd ˈʌnjən / (BrE)
a variety of onion with a white to deep red outer skin, commonly used in salads and cooking

Examples

A red-skinned onion bulb
At the market, the red-skinned onion stood out among the yellow and white varieties.
She prefers to use red-skinned onion in her salads for extra color and flavor.
The recipe calls for one finely chopped red-skinned onion and two cloves of garlic.
