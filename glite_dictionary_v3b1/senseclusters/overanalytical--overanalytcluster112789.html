overanalytical

adjective
excessively analytical
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˌænəˈlɪtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərˌænəˈlɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
tending to analyze things too much or in excessive detail, often making situations more complicated

Examples

An overanalytical person
His overanalytical nature made it hard for him to make quick decisions.
The meeting dragged on because of her overanalytical questions about every detail.
Sometimes being overanalytical can prevent you from enjoying the moment.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: analytical - skilled at breaking things down into parts to understand them
  • antonym: impulsive - acting without thinking or analyzing
