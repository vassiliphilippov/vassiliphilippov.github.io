lie down

verb
recline; put body flat to rest
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /laɪ daʊn/ (AmE), /laɪ daʊn/ (BrE)
to put your body in a flat or horizontal position, usually to rest or sleep

Examples

To lie down on sofa
After feeling dizzy, she decided to lie down on the sofa.
I'm going to lie down for a few minutes before dinner.
You should lie down if you feel unwell.
After a long day at work, I like to lie down and relax for a while.
If you don't feel well, you should try lying down for a few minutes.
She felt dizzy and decided to lie down on the couch.
