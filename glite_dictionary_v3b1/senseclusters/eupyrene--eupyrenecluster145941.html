eupyrene

noun
fertile insect sperm
Pronunciation: / juːˈpaɪriːn / (AmE), / juːˈpaɪriːn / (BrE)
a normal, fertile sperm cell found in certain insects, as opposed to nonfertile sperm cells

Examples

A eupyrene under microscope
The scientist observed several eupyrene under the microscope during the insect's reproductive study.
Unlike apyrene sperm, only eupyrene are capable of fertilizing eggs in these moth species.
Researchers compared the structure of eupyrene and apyrene sperm to understand their different functions.
