derogation

noun
act of belittling or disparaging
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛrəˈɡeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛrəˈɡeɪʃən / (BrE)
an expression or act that belittles or disparages someone or something

Examples

Personal derogation
His speech was filled with subtle derogation rather than open criticism.
Such constant derogation can damage a person's self-esteem over time.
The article's derogation of her achievements was both unfair and unfounded.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disparagement - the act of speaking about someone in a derogatory or belittling manner
  • antonym: praise - expression of approval or admiration
