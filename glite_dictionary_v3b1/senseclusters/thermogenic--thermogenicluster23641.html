thermogenic

adjective
producing heat
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜrmoʊˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
causing or relating to the production of heat, especially within the body

Examples

Thermogenic effect
Certain spices are known for their thermogenic properties, which can help boost metabolism.
The thermogenic response in the body increases after intense exercise.
Some dietary supplements claim to have a thermogenic effect that aids in weight loss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endothermic - absorbing heat
  • antonym: exothermic - releasing heat
