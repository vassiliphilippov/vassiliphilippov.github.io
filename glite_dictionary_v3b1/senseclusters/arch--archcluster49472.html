arch

verb
form a curved, arch-like shape
Pronunciation: / ɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ɑːtʃ / (BrE)
to form or make something form a curved shape like an arch

Examples

To arch your back
She gently arches her eyebrows in surprise whenever she hears unexpected news.
The cat's back arched when it was startled by the dog.
The willow tree arched over the stream, creating a shaded pathway.
