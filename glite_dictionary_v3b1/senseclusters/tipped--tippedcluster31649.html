tipped

adjective
having a tip; e.g., 'rubber-tipped'
Pronunciation: / tɪpt / (AmE), / tɪpt / (BrE)
having a tip, or having a tip as specified, often used in combination (e.g., 'rubber-tipped')

Examples

Metal-tipped dart
Many darts are tipped with metal to help them stick to the board.
She prefers a tipped brush for precision when painting fine details.
The old man walked steadily with his rubber-tipped cane.

Related Terms

  • antonym: untipped - not having a tip or specified tip
