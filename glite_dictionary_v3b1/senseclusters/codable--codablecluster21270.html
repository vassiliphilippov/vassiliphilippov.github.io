codable

adjective
able to be coded
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊdəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊdəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be assigned a code or category for classification, analysis, or billing purposes

Examples

Codable data fields

Researchers found that most responses were codable into clear categories for their study.
