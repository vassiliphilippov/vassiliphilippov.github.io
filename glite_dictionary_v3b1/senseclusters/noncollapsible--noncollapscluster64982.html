noncollapsible

adjective
not able to be collapsed
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.kəˈlæp.sə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.kəˈlæp.sɪ.bəl / (BrE)
Not able to be collapsed or made to fall in or fold together.

Examples

A noncollapsible tube
Make sure to use a noncollapsible tube for this laboratory experiment.
The hospital specified noncollapsible chairs to ensure patient support.
Unlike traditional tents, this shelter uses a noncollapsible frame for added safety.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collapsible - able to be collapsed or folded together
