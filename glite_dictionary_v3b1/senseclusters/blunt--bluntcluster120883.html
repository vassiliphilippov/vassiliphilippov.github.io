blunt

noun
marijuana-filled cigar
Pronunciation: / blʌnt / (AmE), / blʌnt / (BrE)
a cigar that has been hollowed out and filled with marijuana for smoking

Examples

Smoke a blunt
He rolled a blunt before the party and shared it with his friends.
Some people prefer a blunt because it burns more slowly than a joint.
The smell of a freshly lit blunt filled the room.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cigar - a roll of tobacco leaves for smoking
  • synonym: joint - a rolled cigarette containing marijuana
