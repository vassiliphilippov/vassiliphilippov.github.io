rattlesnake fern

noun
a fern with rattle-like sporangia
Pronunciation: / ˈrætlˌsneɪk fɝn / (AmE), / ˈrætlˌsneɪk fɜːn / (BrE)
An American fern (Botrychium virginianum) whose clusters of sporangia resemble the rattle of a snake.
