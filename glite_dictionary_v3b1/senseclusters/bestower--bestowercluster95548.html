bestower

noun
a person who gives a gift
Pronunciation: / bɪˈstoʊər / (AmE), / bɪˈstəʊə / (BrE)
a person who gives something as a gift, especially property or honors

Examples

The bestower of awards
As the bestower of the award, she delivered a heartfelt speech.
The bestower of the scholarship was present at the ceremony.
Tradition honored the bestower of gifts with gratitude and celebration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: giver - a person who gives something
  • antonym: recipient - a person who receives something
