canner

noun
person collecting cans
Pronunciation: / ˈkænər / (AmE), / ˈkænə / (BrE)
a person who collects discarded metal or aluminum cans to sell or recycle

Examples

Street canner
The canner pushed a shopping cart full of empty cans down the street early in the morning.
Many cities have regular canners who collect recyclables from public bins.
After the festival, a canner quickly gathered cans left behind by attendees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recycler - a person who collects materials to be processed and reused
  • synonym: scavenger - a person who searches for and collects discarded items
