mobilizer

noun
device that moves objects
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈməʊbɪˌlaɪzə / (BrE)
a device or machine that causes movement or sets objects in motion

Examples

Heavy mobilizer machine
The engineers designed a new mobilizer to transport heavy equipment across the warehouse.
After the upgrade, the mobilizer could move larger loads more efficiently.
A malfunction in the mobilizer caused delays on the assembly line.

Related Terms

  • antonym: immobilizer - a device that prevents movement
  • synonym: mover - a machine or person that moves something
