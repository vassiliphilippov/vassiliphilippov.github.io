Amazonian

adjective
of the Amazon region
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌæməˈzoʊniən / (AmE), / ˌæməˈzəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to the Amazon region, its rainforest, or its people

Examples

Amazonian rainforest
The Amazonian rainforest is home to thousands of plant and animal species.
Researchers studied the Amazonian tribes to learn about their traditional medicine.
The Amazonian climate is hot and humid throughout most of the year.
