pollotarianism

noun
diet including poultry but not mammalian meat
9.9 8.2
Pronunciation: /pɑː.lə.ˈter.i.ən.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /poʊ.loʊ.ˈter.i.ən.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /pɒ.lə.ˈteə.ri.ən.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /pəʊ.lə.ˈteə.ri.ən.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the practice of eating poultry but not meat from mammals as part of one's diet
