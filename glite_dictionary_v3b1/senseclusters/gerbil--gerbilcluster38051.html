gerbil

noun
small desert rodent; common pet
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɝː.bəl /; / ˈdʒɝːbəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜː.bɪl /; / ˈdʒɜːbɪl / (BrE)
a small rodent with soft fur and long hind legs, native to desert regions (often kept as a pet)

Examples

Scientists study the gerbil because it thrives in dry conditions similar to deserts.
The gerbil quickly dug a tunnel in its new sand-filled habitat.
