pasta sauce

noun
a sauce for serving with pasta
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpæstə sɔs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːstə sɔːs / (BrE)
a sauce, usually tomato-based, cream-based, or oil-based, served with pasta such as spaghetti, penne, or fusilli

Examples

Spicy pasta sauce
He prefers a spicy pasta sauce made with fresh tomatoes and herbs.
She poured homemade pasta sauce over the steaming spaghetti.
You can find many varieties of pasta sauce at the grocery store.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alfredo sauce - a creamy pasta sauce made with butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese
  • synonym: marinara sauce - a type of pasta sauce made from tomatoes, garlic, herbs, and onions
  • synonym: pesto - an Italian pasta sauce made from basil, pine nuts, garlic, Parmesan, and olive oil
