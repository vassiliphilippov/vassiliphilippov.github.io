square capitals

noun
ancient Roman uppercase writing style
Pronunciation: / skwɛr ˈkæp.ɪ.tl̩z / (AmE), / skwɛə ˈkæp.ɪ.tlz / (BrE)
an ancient Roman style of writing with upright, block-like capital letters, which served as the basis for modern capital letters
