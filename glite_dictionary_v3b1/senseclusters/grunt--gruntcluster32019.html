grunt

noun
unskilled soldier or worker
8.0
Pronunciation: / ɡrʌnt / (AmE), / ɡrʌnt / (BrE)
an unskilled or low-ranking soldier, especially in the infantry, or a worker who does basic or routine tasks

Examples

An army grunt
After the merger, he started as a grunt in the mailroom before working his way up.
Being a grunt meant long hours and little recognition, but he took pride in his work.
The army relies on grunts to carry out its most dangerous missions.
