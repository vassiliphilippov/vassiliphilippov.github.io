sixth sense

noun
intuitive perception beyond the five senses
1.0 7.6
Pronunciation: / sɪksθ sɛns / (AmE), / sɪksθ sɛns / (BrE)
a supposed intuitive ability to perceive or understand things without using the five physical senses

Examples

A sixth sense feeling
His sixth sense told him that something was wrong, even though he couldn't explain it.
She navigated the crowded market as if guided by a sixth sense.
Some people claim to have a sixth sense that warns them of danger.

Related Terms
