buckwheat

noun
grain-like seed used for flour
7.0 4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkˌwit / (AmE), / ˈbʌkwiːt / (BrE)
A grain-like seed that is ground into flour and used in making foods such as pancakes, noodles, and porridge.

Examples

Buckwheat flour
Gluten-free bread can be made using buckwheat flour instead of wheat flour.
She bought some buckwheat to make pancakes for breakfast.
The recipe called for a cup of ground buckwheat mixed with eggs and milk.

Related Terms
