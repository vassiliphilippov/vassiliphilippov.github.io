squelchy

adjective
wet, resonant sound
6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈskwel.tʃiː/ /; / /ˈskwel.tʃɪ/ (AmE), /ˈskwel.tʃiː/ /; / /ˈskwel.tʃɪ/ (BrE)
