paper-thin

adjective
extremely thin
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪpər ˈθɪn /; / ˌpeɪ.pɚ ˈθɪn / (AmE), / ˌpeɪpə ˈθɪn /; / ˌpeɪ.pə ˈθɪn / (BrE)
extremely thin or delicate, like a sheet of paper (can be used literally or figuratively)

Examples

The artist created a sculpture from paper-thin sheets of metal.
The old book's paper-thin pages tore easily when handled.
