Synonyms

  • scarcity
    4.4 4.99/m
    scarcity is a common word for things in short supply, while dearth is more formal and literary.
  • paucity
    8.6 1.57/m
    paucity describes a very small amount in formal writing, while dearth is a general word for scarcity.
  • shortage
    2.7 21/m
    shortage names a practical, often temporary lack of supplies, while dearth sounds more literary and general.
  • deficiency
    4.5 15/m
    deficiency refers to a lack that causes a problem, often for health or function, while dearth means a general scarcity.
  • insufficiency
    6.5 0.36/m
    insufficiency often focuses on not having enough skill or power, while dearth simply means a general lack.
