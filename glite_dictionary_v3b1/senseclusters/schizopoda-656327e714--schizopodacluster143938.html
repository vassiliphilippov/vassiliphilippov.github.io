Schizopoda

noun
a former group of small marine crustaceans
9.9
Pronunciation: /skɪz.ə.ˈpoʊ.də/ (AmE), /skɪz.ə.ˈpəʊ.də/ (BrE)
a former taxonomic division of Malacostraca, now replaced by the orders Mysidacea and Euphausiacea, comprising certain small, transparent marine crustaceans

Examples

Schizopoda marine crustaceans
Early marine biologists often referred to certain shrimplike creatures as Schizopoda.
Many scientific papers from the 19th century discuss the classification of Schizopoda among crustaceans.
The order Schizopoda was divided into Mysidacea and Euphausiacea in modern taxonomy.
