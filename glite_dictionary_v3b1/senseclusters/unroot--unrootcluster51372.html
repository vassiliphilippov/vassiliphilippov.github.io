unroot

verb
to remove by roots
5.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàruÀêt / (AmE), /  ånÀàruÀêt / (BrE)

Examples

To unroot a plant
The gardener tried to unroot the stubborn weed from the flower bed.
Strong winds can unroot even the largest trees during a storm.
It took several people to unroot the old post from the ground.
