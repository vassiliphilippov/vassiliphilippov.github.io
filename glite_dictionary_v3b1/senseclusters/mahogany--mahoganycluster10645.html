mahogany

noun
reddish-brown hardwood used in furniture
6.0 4.3
Pronunciation: / məˈhɑɡəni / (AmE), / məˈhɒɡəni / (BrE)
a tropical tree or its strong, reddish-brown wood, used for high-quality furniture and woodworking

Examples

Mahogany furniture
Antique dealers often prize furniture made of mahogany for its durability and rich color.
