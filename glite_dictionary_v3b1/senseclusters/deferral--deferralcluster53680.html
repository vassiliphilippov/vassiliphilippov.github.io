Examples

A deferral of payment

The committee agreed to a deferral of the final decision until more data could be gathered.

Synonyms

  • deferment
    6.2 1.19/m
    deferment is a more formal term for delaying something in official or legal situations, while deferral names the same general postponing.
  • postponement
    3.4 1.09/m
    postponement often means delaying a scheduled event until later, while deferral describes the act or state of delaying something.
