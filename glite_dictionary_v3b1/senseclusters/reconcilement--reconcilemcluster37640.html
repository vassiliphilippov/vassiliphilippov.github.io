reconcilement

noun
process of making consistent
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkənˈsaɪlmənt / (AmE), / ˌrɛkənˈsaɪlmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of making two or more things consistent or compatible, especially accounts, statements, or ideas

Examples

Reconcilement of accounts
The reconcilement of the two financial statements revealed several discrepancies.
After much discussion, the reconcilement of their differing opinions was finally achieved.
The accountant is responsible for the monthly reconcilement of all company accounts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conflict - a serious disagreement or argument
  • synonym: reconciliation - the process of making things compatible or bringing them into agreement
