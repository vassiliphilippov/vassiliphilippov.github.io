sword knot

noun
ornamental tassel on a sword hilt
6.7
Pronunciation: / sɔrd nɑt / (AmE), / sɔːd nɒt / (BrE)
an ornamental tassel or cord attached to the hilt of a sword, often used both for decoration and to secure the sword to the wrist

Examples

A sword knot tassel
A gold sword knot dangled from the officer's ornate saber hilt.
He replaced the frayed sword knot with a new silk one before the parade.
The museum displayed an array of antique swords, each with a finely crafted sword knot.
