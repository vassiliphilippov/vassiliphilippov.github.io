taste tester

noun
person who tests food or drink quality
5.9
Pronunciation: / teɪst ˈtɛstər / (AmE), / teɪst ˈtɛstə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to sample food or drink to assess its quality or flavor

Examples

Professional taste tester
Becoming a professional taste tester requires a refined palate and attention to detail.
She works as a taste tester for a large chocolate company.
The chef relied on his taste tester to ensure each dish met high standards.
