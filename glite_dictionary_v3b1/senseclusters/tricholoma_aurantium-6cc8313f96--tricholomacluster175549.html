Tricholoma aurantium

noun
orange-tan mushroom species
Pronunciation: / trɪkəˌloʊmə ɔːˈræntiəm / (AmE), / trɪkəˌləʊmə ɔːˈrantɪəm / (BrE)
A species of orange-tan mushroom whose gills turn brown with maturity, notable for its strong odor and taste.

The Tricholoma aurantium mushroom
