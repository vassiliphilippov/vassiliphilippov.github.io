coin collector

noun
person who collects coins
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔɪn kəˌlɛktər / (AmE), / ˈkɔɪn kəˌlɛktə / (BrE)
a person who collects coins, either as a hobby or professionally, and may study their history and characteristics

Examples

Coin collector hobby
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a coin collector after finding an old penny in her backyard.
My uncle is an avid coin collector who often attends international coin fairs.
The museum hired a renowned coin collector to evaluate their ancient currency collection.
