synth

noun
artificial human
Pronunciation: / sɪnθ / (AmE), / sɪnθ / (BrE)
a synthetic person, robot, or artificial human, especially as depicted in science fiction stories

Examples

A synth with feelings
The film explores whether a synth can develop real emotions like a human.
In the futuristic city, synths work alongside people in many different jobs.
She was surprised to learn her new coworker was actually a synth designed to look completely human.

Related Terms

  • synonym: android - a robot with a human appearance
  • antonym: human - a person; a member of the species Homo sapiens
  • synonym: robot - a machine capable of carrying out complex actions automatically
