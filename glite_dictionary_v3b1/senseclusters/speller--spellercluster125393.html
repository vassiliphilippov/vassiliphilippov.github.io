speller

noun
spell-checking software
3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈspel.ər/ (AmE), /ˈspel.ə/ (BrE)
a software tool or program that checks the spelling in text and often suggests corrections or flags possible errors.

Examples

Computer speller
The word processor's built-in speller flagged several typos in the draft before submission.
Many email clients include spellers that suggest corrections as you type to avoid embarrassing mistakes.
During the proofreading session, she relied on the online speller to check unfamiliar technical terms.
