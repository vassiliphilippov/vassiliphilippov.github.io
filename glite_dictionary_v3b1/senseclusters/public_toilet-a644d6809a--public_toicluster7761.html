public toilet

noun
a toilet open for use by the public
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk ˈtɔɪlət / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈtɔɪlət / (BrE)
a room or building with toilets and washing facilities that is available for use by anyone, usually in a public place

Examples

A public toilet building
She searched the park for a public toilet when she arrived with her children.
The city council plans to renovate several old public toilets this year.
There was a public toilet near the museum entrance, which was very convenient for tourists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lavatory - a room or compartment with a toilet and a sink
  • antonym: private toilet - a toilet not available to the general public, typically in a private residence
  • synonym: restroom - a room or suite of rooms that contains toilets, commonly used in American English
