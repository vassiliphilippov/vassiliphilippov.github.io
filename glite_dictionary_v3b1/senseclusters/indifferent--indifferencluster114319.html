indifferent

adjective
chemically unreactive or inactive
2.7 5.4
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈdɪ.frənt/ /; / /ɪn.ˈdɪf.ə.rənt/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈdɪ.fə.rənt/ /; / /ɪn.ˈdɪ.frənt/ (BrE)
(of a substance) having little or no ability to react chemically; inactive or inert.

Examples

Indifferent gas
Noble gases are often described as indifferent due to their lack of chemical activity.
The catalyst remained indifferent throughout the entire experiment, showing no signs of reaction.
This gas is considered indifferent because it does not participate in the reaction.
