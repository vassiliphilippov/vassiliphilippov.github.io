malicious gossip

noun
harmful, reputation-damaging gossip
4.5 5.8
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈlɪʃ.əs ˈgɑː.sɪp/ (AmE), /mə.ˈlɪʃ.əs ˈgɒs.ɪp/ (BrE)
disgraceful or harmful gossip intended to damage the reputation of others by revealing or spreading private information about them

Examples

Malicious gossip spreads
It is important to avoid listening to malicious gossip that can ruin someone's reputation.
She was very hurt when she discovered the malicious gossip being spread about her at work.
The tabloid was known for publishing malicious gossip about celebrities' personal lives.
