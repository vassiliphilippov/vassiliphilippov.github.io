conversion

noun
successful extra point/score after touchdown
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɝːʒən / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːʃən / (BrE)
(in American football, rugby, or basketball) a successful free throw or attempt to score points after a touchdown or try.

Examples

Rugby conversion
He kicked the conversion after the touchdown, bringing the score to 21-14.
Her free throw conversion rate this season has improved significantly.
The team celebrated after a successful conversion extended their lead.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: touchdown - a scoring play in American football
  • synonym: try - an attempt to score additional points after a touchdown in rugby
