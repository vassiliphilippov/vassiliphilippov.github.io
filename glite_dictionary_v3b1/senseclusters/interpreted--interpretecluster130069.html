interpreted

adjective
understood or explained in a particular way
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɚprətɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːprɪtɪd / (BrE)
understood or explained in a particular way; having had a meaning assigned to it

Examples

Is interpreted as
Her silence was interpreted by many as a sign of agreement.
His gestures were interpreted as a form of encouragement.
The law can be interpreted in several different ways.

Related Terms

  • antonym: misinterpreted - understood or explained incorrectly
  • synonym: understood - comprehended or grasped meaning
