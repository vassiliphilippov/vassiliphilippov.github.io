pipage

noun
fee for use of pipes
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪpɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪpɪdʒ / (BrE)
a fee paid for the use of pipes, especially for conveying water, gas, or similar services

Examples

The pipage fee
Some industrial clients claim the pipage is too high compared to other municipalities.
The city council voted to reduce the pipage for residential users next year.
Your monthly bill includes a pipage based on the volume of water delivered.
