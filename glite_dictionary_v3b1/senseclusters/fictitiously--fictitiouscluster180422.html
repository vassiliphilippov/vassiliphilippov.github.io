fictitiously

adverb
in an invented or imaginary manner
6.2 5.8
Pronunciation: /fɪk.ˈtɪʃ.əs.liː/ /; / /fə.ˈtɪʃ.əs.liː/ (AmE), /fɪk.ˈtɪʃ.əs.liː/ /; / /fə.ˈtɪʃ.əs.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that is invented, imaginary, or not real; in a fictional manner

Examples
