overscheduled

adjective
too many commitments
5.2 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / Àåo äv…ôrÀàsk…õd íuÀêld / (AmE), / Àå…ô äv…ôÀà É…õdjuÀêld / (BrE)
having too many activities, appointments, or commitments planned, often causing stress or lack of free time

Examples

An overscheduled day
After accepting every invitation, his calendar quickly became overscheduled and he felt overwhelmed.
Many children today are overscheduled with extracurricular activities and have little time to relax.
She realized she was overscheduled when she had meetings back-to-back all week.

Synonyms

    6.1 0.01/m

  • overburdened
    5.3 0.03/m
    overburdened feels weighed down by duties or stress, while overscheduled has too many scheduled appointments and no free time.
