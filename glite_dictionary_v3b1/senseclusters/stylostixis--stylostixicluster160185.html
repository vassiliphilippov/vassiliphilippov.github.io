stylostixis

noun
insertion of needles for medical treatment
Pronunciation: / staɪloʊˈstɪksɪs / (AmE), / staɪləʊˈstɪksɪs / (BrE)
A method of treating pain or disease by inserting needles at specific points on the skin; acupuncture.
