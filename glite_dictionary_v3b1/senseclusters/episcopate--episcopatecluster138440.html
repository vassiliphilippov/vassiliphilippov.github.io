episcopate

noun
district under a bishop's authority
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈpɪskəˌpeɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪskəpət / (BrE)
the area or district under the authority of a bishop in the Christian Church

Examples

Large episcopate
He was sent to oversee a large episcopate following the retirement of his predecessor.
The boundaries of the episcopate were redrawn to reflect changes in the population.
The new reforms reshaped the episcopate in several rural areas.
