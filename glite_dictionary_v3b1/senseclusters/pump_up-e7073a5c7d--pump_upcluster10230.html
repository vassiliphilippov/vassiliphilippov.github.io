pump up

verb
increase energy or excitement
Pronunciation: / pʌmp ʌp / (AmE), / pʌmp ʌp / (BrE)
to increase or enhance the energy, excitement, or enthusiasm of someone or something

Examples

To pump up energy
Before the game, the coach tried to pump up the players with an inspiring speech.
Her infectious enthusiasm really pumped up the whole office.
The DJ knows how to pump up the atmosphere and get everyone dancing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm down - to become or make less excited or energetic
  • synonym: energize - to give energy or enthusiasm to someone or something
