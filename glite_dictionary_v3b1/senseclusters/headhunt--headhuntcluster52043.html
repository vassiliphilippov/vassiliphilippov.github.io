headhunt

verb
to recruit executives
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈhed.hʌnt/ (AmE), /ˈhed.hʌnt/ (BrE)
to actively search for and recruit highly qualified people, especially for senior or specialized positions

Examples

The company decided to headhunt a new marketing director from a rival firm.
