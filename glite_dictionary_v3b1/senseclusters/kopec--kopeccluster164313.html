kopec

noun
small hill or mound
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊpɛts / (AmE), / ˈkɒpɛts / (BrE)
a small hill or mound, especially as found in Central Europe

Examples

A grassy kopec
The village church stood on a grassy kopec overlooking the valley.
From the top of the kopec, you could see the entire countryside spread out below.
Children loved to roll down the steep sides of the kopec after school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hill - a naturally raised area of land, smaller than a mountain
  • synonym: mound - a small raised area of earth or stones
  • degree higher: mountain - a large natural elevation of the earth's surface
