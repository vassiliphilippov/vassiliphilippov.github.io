lumberwoman

noun
female lumberjack; woman who fells trees
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌmbɚˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈlʌmbəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose occupation is felling trees and preparing timber

Examples

A lumberwoman felling trees
As a seasoned lumberwoman, she spent her days felling trees in the dense forest.
Despite the challenges, the lumberwoman handled heavy equipment with remarkable skill.
The magazine featured an inspiring story about a pioneering lumberwoman in the Pacific Northwest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lumberjack - a person whose job is cutting down trees and logging
  • synonym: woodsman - a person skilled at working or living in the woods
