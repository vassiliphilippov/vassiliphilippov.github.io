dissimilarity

noun
lack of similarity; difference
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪs.əˈmɪl.əˌrɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.ɪ.mɪˈlær.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The state or quality of being different from something or someone else; lack of resemblance or similarity.

Examples

Marked dissimilarity
Despite their shared background, there was marked dissimilarity in their approaches to the problem.
Genetic dissimilarity can help scientists track the origins of different species.
The dissimilarity between the two paintings became apparent upon closer inspection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: difference - a way in which two or more things are not the same
  • antonym: similarity - the state of being similar or alike
