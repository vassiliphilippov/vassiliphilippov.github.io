legs

noun
ability to endure; staying power
Pronunciation: / lɛɡz / (AmE), / lɛɡz / (BrE)
the ability of an idea, show, product, etc., to continue being successful or popular for a long time

Examples

The legs of an idea
That technology had legs and remained relevant long after its introduction.
The classic novel still has legs after decades in print.
This advertising campaign really had legs and ran for several years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: staying power - the ability to last or endure successfully
