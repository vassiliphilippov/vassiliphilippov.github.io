giver

noun
a person who gives something
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪvər /; / ˈɡɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvə / (BrE)
a person who gives something to others, especially generously or selflessly

Examples

A giver of gifts
Every good relationship relies on both partners being a giver at times.
She is known as a generous giver to many local charities.
The anonymous giver donated enough money to fund the new library.
As a true giver, he volunteered countless hours at the community center.
She has always been a giver, putting her friends' needs ahead of her own.
The world needs more givers who are willing to devote themselves to helping others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donor - a person who gives something, especially to charity
  • antonym: recipient - a person who receives something
