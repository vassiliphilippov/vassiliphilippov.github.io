envisionment

noun
mental vision or plan
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɪʒənmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɪʒənmənt / (BrE)
the act or result of imagining or forming a mental picture of something, especially a possible plan or future outcome

Examples

An envisionment of future
Her envisionment of a green campus became the university's long-term master plan.
The architect's envisionment included open spaces and sustainable buildings.
A clear envisionment of the project's goals helped the team stay focused.
Her envisionment of the new building inspired the entire design team.
The artist's envisionment of the landscape was both creative and detailed.
Effective planning often begins with a clear envisionment of the desired outcome.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imagination - the ability to form ideas or images in the mind
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
  • synonym: vision - an idea or mental image of what the future could be like
  • synonym: visualization - the act of forming a mental image of something
