marine iguana

noun
Galapagos shore-dwelling seaweed-eating lizard
9.2 5.7
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈriːn ɪ.ˈɡwɑː.nə/ /; / /mə.ˈriːn ə.ˈɡwɑː.nə/ (AmE), /mə.ˈriːn ɪ.ˈɡwɑː.nə/ /; / /mə.ˈriːn ə.ˈɡwɑː.nə/ (BrE)
a large, dark-colored lizard native to the Galapagos Islands, known for living on the shore and feeding mainly on seaweed

Examples
