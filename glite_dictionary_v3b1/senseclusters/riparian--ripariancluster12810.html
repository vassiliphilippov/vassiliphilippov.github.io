riparian

adjective
on the banks of a river or stream
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɛriən / (AmE), / rɪˈpɛːrɪən / (BrE)
relating to or situated on the banks of a river or stream.

Examples

Riparian land
Local laws restrict development in riparian areas to protect wildlife habitats.
The riparian vegetation helps prevent soil erosion along the riverbank.
They bought a house on riparian land, just a few meters from the edge of the stream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: riverine - relating to or situated on a river or riverbanks
