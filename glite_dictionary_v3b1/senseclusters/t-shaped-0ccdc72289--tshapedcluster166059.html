T-shaped

adjective
deep specialist plus broad general knowledge
6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈtiː.ʃeɪpt/ /; / /tiː.ˈʃeɪpt/ (AmE), /ˈtiː.ʃeɪpt/ /; / /tiː.ˈʃeɪpt/ (BrE)
(figurative, business) having deep expertise in one area and a broad working knowledge of many other areas; used to describe a desirable skill profile in people or teams.

Examples

T-shaped skills
Hiring managers value T-shaped candidates who combine deep coding skills with broad product knowledge.
Our team needs a T-shaped designer who understands interaction design and can also collaborate across disciplines.
To grow, she became T-shaped, deepening her research abilities while learning basic programming and marketing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generalist - a person with broad knowledge across a range of subjects or skills
  • antonym: specialist - a person with deep expertise in a single area
