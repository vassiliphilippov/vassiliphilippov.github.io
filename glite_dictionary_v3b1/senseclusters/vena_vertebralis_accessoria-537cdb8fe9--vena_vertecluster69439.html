vena vertebralis accessoria

noun
a specific accessory vertebral vein in the neck
Pronunciation: / ˌveɪ.nə vərˌtiː.brəˈlɪs əkˈsɛs.əˌraɪ.ə / (AmE), / ˌveɪ.nə vəːˌtiː.bræˈlɪs əkˈsɛs.əˌraɪ.ə / (BrE)
a vein that accompanies the vertebral vein, passing through the foramen of the transverse process of the seventh cervical vertebra and draining into the brachiocephalic vein.

Examples

The vena vertebralis accessoria vein
Damage to the vena vertebralis accessoria during surgery can cause significant bleeding in the neck.
In rare cases, the vena vertebralis accessoria can serve as an additional pathway for venous drainage from the cervical spine.
The vena vertebralis accessoria may be seen during detailed anatomical dissection of the lower cervical region.
