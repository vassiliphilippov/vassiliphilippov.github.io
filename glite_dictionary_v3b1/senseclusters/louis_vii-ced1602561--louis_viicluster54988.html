Louis VII

proper noun
French king, led Second Crusade
9.1
Pronunciation: / luːˈiː səˈvɛnθ / (AmE), / luːˈiː səˈvɛnθ / (BrE)
(1120–1180) King of France who led the unsuccessful Second Crusade and fought frequent wars with Henry II of England.
