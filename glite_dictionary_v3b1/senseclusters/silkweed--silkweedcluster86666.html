silkweed

noun
plant of the genus Asclepias
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlkwid / (AmE), / ˈsɪlkwiːd / (BrE)
any of various plants of the genus Asclepias, known for their milky juice and seed pods with silky tufts

Examples

Silkweed seed pods
In late summer, the silkweed pods split open and release seeds carried by the wind.
Monarch butterflies often lay their eggs on silkweed because it serves as food for their larvae.
The field was dotted with silkweed plants bursting with fluffy seeds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: milkweed - another common name for plants of the genus Asclepias
