jee

particle
respectful honorific suffix
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒiː / (AmE), / dʒiː / (BrE)
a respectful honorific suffix used in South Asian languages, added after a person's name to show respect

Examples

Sharma jee
The students respectfully called their teacher Sharma jee during the ceremony.
In many Indian families, children address elders with the suffix jee to show respect.
He greeted his neighbor as Gupta jee every morning on his way to work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: madam - a respectful form of address for a woman
  • synonym: sir - a respectful form of address for a man
