Asclepias albicans

noun
a southwestern US milkweed plant species
9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈskliː.piː.əs æl.ˈbɪ.kəns/ (AmE), /ə.ˈskliː.piː.əs æl.ˈbɪ.kəns/ (BrE)
A tall perennial herb native to the southwestern United States, characterized by mostly leafless, white, waxy stems and whitish, star-shaped flowers.
