overgod

noun
supreme authority
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌɡɑd / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌɡɒd / (BrE)
a person, organization, or thing regarded as supremely powerful or completely dominant in a particular field or area

Examples

Industry overgod
In the tech world, some see him as the overgod who shapes the industry's future.
The company acted like an overgod, controlling every aspect of the market.
Critics warned against giving any one leader the status of overgod in the organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overlord - a person with great power or authority over others
  • synonym: ruler - a person who governs or controls a country or group
  • antonym: subordinate - a person or thing of lower rank or importance
