anti-life

adjective
opposed to life
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈæntiˌlaɪf / (AmE), / ˈæntiˌlaɪf / (BrE)
opposed to or destructive of life or living things

Examples

Anti-life attitude
The villain's anti-life philosophy threatened the survival of the entire planet.
Many activists argued that the new law was fundamentally anti-life in its effects.
He criticized the movement for promoting anti-life attitudes toward the environment.

Related Terms
