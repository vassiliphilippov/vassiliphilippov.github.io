nonlife

noun
insurance except life
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑnˌlaɪf / (AmE), / ˈnɒnˌlaɪf / (BrE)
insurance that covers risks other than life insurance, such as property or casualty insurance

Examples

Nonlife coverage
The company expanded its nonlife portfolio to include home and auto insurance.
Premiums for nonlife policies can vary depending on the type of coverage.
Many insurers offer both life and nonlife products to their customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: casualty insurance - insurance covering loss from accidents or liability
  • antonym: life insurance - insurance that pays out on the death of the insured person
  • synonym: property insurance - insurance covering damage to property
