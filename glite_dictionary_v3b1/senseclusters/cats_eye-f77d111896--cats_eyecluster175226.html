cat's eye

noun
gemstone showing a cat's eye effect
7.0
Pronunciation: / kæts aɪ / (AmE), / kæts aɪ / (BrE)
A gemstone, such as chrysoberyl or chalcedony, that exhibits a luminous band of reflected light resembling a cat’s eye when cut en cabochon.

Examples

A cat's eye gem
A fine cat's eye can display a sharp, bright band of light across its surface.
Collectors prize cat's eye gemstones for their unique reflective quality.
The jeweler showed me a beautiful cat's eye ring set in gold.
