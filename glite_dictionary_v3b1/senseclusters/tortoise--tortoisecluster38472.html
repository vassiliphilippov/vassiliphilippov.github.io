tortoise

noun
land-dwelling turtle with a domed shell
1.0 1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔr.təs / (AmE), / ˈtɔː.təs / (BrE)
A slow-moving, plant-eating land reptile with a domed shell and thick legs, found in warm regions.

Examples

A giant tortoise
A giant tortoise can live for more than a hundred years in the wild.
The children watched as the tortoise slowly crossed the garden path.
Unlike sea turtles, the tortoise is adapted to life entirely on land.
