motorsailer

noun
sail and motor boat
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtərˌseɪlər / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəˌseɪlə / (BrE)

A motorsailer offers flexibility in both calm and windy conditions.
The family upgraded from a sailboat to a motorsailer for longer journeys.
