uplink

noun
transmission from Earth to spacecraft or satellite
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌlɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌlɪŋk / (BrE)
a transmission from Earth to a spacecraft or satellite, or the path by which such a signal is sent

Examples

Satellite uplink
A strong uplink is crucial for sending commands to remote satellites.
Engineers monitored the uplink to ensure consistent communication with the spacecraft.
The satellite receives the uplink signal before transmitting data back to Earth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downlink - a transmission from a spacecraft or satellite to Earth, or its signal path
