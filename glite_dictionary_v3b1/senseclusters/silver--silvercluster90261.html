silver

noun
a light gray color
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə / (BrE)
a light gray color with a metallic sheen, resembling the metal silver

Examples

Silver wall color
He chose silver for the walls to give the room a modern look.
Her dress was a beautiful silver that shimmered under the lights.
The car is available in red, black, and silver.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: charcoal - a dark gray color
  • synonym: gray - a color between black and white
  • degree higher: white - the lightest color; the color of milk or fresh snow
