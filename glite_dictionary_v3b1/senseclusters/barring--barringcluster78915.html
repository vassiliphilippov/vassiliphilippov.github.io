barring

preposition
except for
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːrɪŋ / (BrE)
except for or unless a particular event or condition happens

Examples

Barring any changes
Barring any last-minute changes, the concert will begin at eight o'clock.
Barring a major storm, the flight should arrive on schedule.
Barring unforeseen circumstances, she will finish the project by Friday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: except - not including; other than
  • synonym: unless - except if; only if not
