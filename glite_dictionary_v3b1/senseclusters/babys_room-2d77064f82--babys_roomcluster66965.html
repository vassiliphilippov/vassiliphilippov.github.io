baby's room

noun
room for a baby
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪbiz ruːm / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbɪz ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house that is specially furnished and decorated for a baby

Examples

The baby's room
The baby's room has soft colors and a crib near the window.
They set up a monitor in the baby's room so they could hear the baby at night.
We spent weeks decorating the baby's room before our daughter was born.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nursery - a room in a house for the special use of young children or babies
