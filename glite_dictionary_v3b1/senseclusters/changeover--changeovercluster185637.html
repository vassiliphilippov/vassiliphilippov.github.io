changeover

noun
a transition from one system or state to another
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪndʒˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒˌəʊvə / (BrE)
the process or result of replacing or transitioning from one system, activity, or state to another

Examples

The changeover process
The changeover from analog to digital television broadcasting happened nationwide in 2009.
The factory's changeover to automated production took several months to complete.
There was a smooth changeover between the night and day shift at the hospital.
