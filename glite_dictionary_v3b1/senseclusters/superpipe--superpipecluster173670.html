superpipe

noun
large U-shaped ramp
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌpaɪp / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌpaɪp / (BrE)
a very large, U-shaped ramp used for performing tricks in snowboarding or skateboarding competitions

Examples

A superpipe ramp
The snowboarder performed a backflip in the superpipe during the competition.
Olympic athletes train for hours each day to master tricks in the superpipe.
The new skate park features a massive superpipe that attracts professionals from around the world.
