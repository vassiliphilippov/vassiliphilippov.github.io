fartette

noun
a playful term for someone who farts
Pronunciation: / fɑrˈtɛt / (AmE), / fɑːˈtɛt / (BrE)
A humorous or playful term for a female or someone, especially in a family, who farts frequently or is jokingly singled out for farting.

Examples

The fartette of family
At every family gathering, Uncle Bob is jokingly called the fartette of the group.
Her brothers laughed and dubbed her the family's official fartette.
You don't want to sit next to the fartette during movie night!
