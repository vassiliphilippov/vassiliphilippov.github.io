hyperconscious

adjective
extremely aware
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈkɑnʃəs / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəkˈɒnʃəs / (BrE)
extremely or excessively aware, especially of oneself or one's surroundings

Examples

To be hyperconscious of feelings
During the presentation, she was hyperconscious of how her audience was reacting to every word.
He became hyperconscious of his appearance after seeing himself on video.
Some people are hyperconscious of social norms and worry about making mistakes in public.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-aware - conscious of oneself and one's actions
  • antonym: unaware - not conscious or aware
