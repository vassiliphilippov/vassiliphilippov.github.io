chauffeur

noun
a person paid to drive a private car
Pronunciation: / ʃoʊˈfɜr / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊfə / (BrE)
a person employed to drive a private car for someone else

Examples

A private chauffeur
As a chauffeur, he is always punctual and keeps the car spotless.
During the gala event, their chauffeur waited patiently outside the venue.
The wealthy family hired a chauffeur to drive them everywhere in their new limousine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: driver - a person who drives a vehicle
  • antonym: passenger - a person who is transported in a vehicle but not operating it
