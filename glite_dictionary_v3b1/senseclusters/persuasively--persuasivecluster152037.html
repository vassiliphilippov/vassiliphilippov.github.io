persuasively

adverb
in a convincing manner
2.3
Pronunciation: / pərˈsweɪsɪvli / (AmE), / pəˈsweɪsɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner that is able to convince someone to do or believe something.

Examples

To speak persuasively
He argued persuasively in favor of the new policy.
She spoke so persuasively that everyone agreed to her proposal.
The lawyer persuasively presented the evidence to the jury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convincingly - in a way that makes someone believe something is true
