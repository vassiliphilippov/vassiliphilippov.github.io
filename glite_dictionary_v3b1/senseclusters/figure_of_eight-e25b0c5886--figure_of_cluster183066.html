figure of eight

noun
knot shaped like the number 8
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪɡjɚ əv eɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡə əv eɪt / (BrE)
a knot shaped like the number 8, tied in a rope to prevent it from slipping through a hole or pulley
