unfondly

adverb
without affection
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɑndli / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɒndli / (BrE)
in a way that shows dislike or lack of affection

Examples

To unfondly recall memories
She unfondly recalled the arguments she had with her old roommate.
Many people unfondly remember the long, cold winters of their childhood.
He spoke unfondly of his time working at the factory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coldly - in an unemotional or unfriendly way
  • antonym: fondly - in a loving or affectionate way
