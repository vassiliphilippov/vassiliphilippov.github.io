sublimation

noun
printing process using dye turned to gas
6.7
Pronunciation: /sʌb.lɪ.ˈmeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /sʌb.lə.ˈmeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /sʌb.lɪ.ˈmeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /sʌb.lə.ˈmeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a printing process (often called dye-sublimation) in which solid dye is heated to become a gas that transfers and bonds to a substrate, commonly polyester or specially coated materials.

Examples

Sublimation printing
They adopted sublimation printing to transfer vibrant images onto polyester sports jerseys.
Our workshop uses sublimation to print custom mugs because the colors remain bright after washing.
Before production they tested sublimation on sample fabric to check color accuracy and durability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dye-sublimation printing - a printing technique that uses heat to turn dye into gas and transfer images to polyester or coated surfaces
  • synonym: transfer printing - a group of printing methods for moving designs from one surface to another; often used as a general term related to sublimation printing
