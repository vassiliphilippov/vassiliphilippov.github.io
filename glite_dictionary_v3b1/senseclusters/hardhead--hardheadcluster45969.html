hardhead

noun
stubborn or practical person
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person who is practical, stubborn, or tough-minded and does not easily change their mind

Examples

A stubborn hardhead
The new coach is a real hardhead who insists on strict discipline from the team.
You need to be a hardhead to succeed in such a competitive business environment.
Despite everyone's advice, the hardhead refused to change his decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pragmatist - a person who deals with things sensibly and realistically
  • antonym: pushover - a person who is easy to influence or persuade
  • synonym: stubborn - unwilling to change one's mind or attitude
