shellfire

noun
artillery bombardment with shells
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛlˌfaɪr / (AmE), / ˈʃɛlˌfaɪə / (BrE)
artillery fire in which explosive shells are launched at a target; the act or effect of being bombarded by shells.

Examples

Intense shellfire attack
Many buildings were destroyed by the intense shellfire during the battle.
The soldiers endured heavy shellfire throughout the night.
Under shellfire, troops took cover in the trenches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bombardment - a continuous attack with bombs, shells, or other missiles
