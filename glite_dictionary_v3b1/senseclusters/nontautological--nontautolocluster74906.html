nontautological

adjective
not redundant
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌtɔtəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌtɔːtəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
not redundant or needlessly repetitive in expression; not stating the same thing twice

Examples

Nontautological writing
The professor praised her nontautological writing style for its clarity and directness.
Please make your argument nontautological so that it is easier to follow.
His speech was refreshingly nontautological, avoiding unnecessary repetition of ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concise - expressing much in few words; brief and clear
  • antonym: tautological - needlessly repetitive; stating the same thing twice
