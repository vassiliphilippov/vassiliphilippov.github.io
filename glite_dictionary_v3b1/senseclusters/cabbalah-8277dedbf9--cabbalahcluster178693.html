Cabbalah

noun
Jewish mystical philosophy
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈbɑː.lə/ /; / /kə.ˈbæl.ə/ (AmE), /kə.ˈbæl.ə/ /; / /kə.ˈbɑː.lə/ (BrE)
