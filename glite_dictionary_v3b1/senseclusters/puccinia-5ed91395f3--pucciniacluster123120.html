Puccinia

noun
parasitic fungus genus affecting plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / pʊkˈsɪniə / (AmE), / pʊkˈsɪniə / (BrE)
a large group (genus) of parasitic fungi that cause diseases in crops and other important plants
