embodied

adjective
existing in bodily or physical form
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbɑdiːd / (AmE), / ɪmˈbɒdɪd / (BrE)
possessing or existing in bodily or physical form; made concrete or personified in a physical entity.

Examples

Embodied in actions
Her generosity was embodied in every action she took.
The artist's vision was embodied in a striking bronze sculpture.
The spirit was believed to be embodied in a human form.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incarnate - embodied in flesh; given a bodily form
