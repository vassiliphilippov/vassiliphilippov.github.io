nonsymbolic

adjective
literal or direct
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.sɪmˈbɑlɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.sɪmˈbɒlɪk / (BrE)
not involving or using symbols; literal, direct, or concrete rather than abstract or representational

Examples

Nonsymbolic activities
Early math education often begins with nonsymbolic activities like counting real objects.
The artist preferred a nonsymbolic approach, focusing on realistic depictions rather than abstract forms.
Researchers studied how children solve nonsymbolic problems before learning to use numbers.
