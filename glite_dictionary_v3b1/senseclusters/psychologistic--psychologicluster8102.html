psychologistic

adjective
overly psychological
9.9
Pronunciation: /saɪ.kə.lə.ˈdʒɪs.tɪk/ /; / /saɪ.kə.lɑː.ˈdʒɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /saɪ.kə.lɒ.ˈdʒɪs.tɪk/ /; / /saɪ.kə.lə.ˈdʒɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
explaining or interpreting things (such as social, moral, cultural, or logical issues) mainly in terms of psychology or psychological processes, often ignoring other important factors

Related Terms

  • antonym: anti-psychologistic - opposing the idea that logic is grounded in psychology
  • antonym: holistic - considering the whole rather than just parts
  • synonym: psychological - relating to the mind or mental processes
  • synonym: psychologism - the theory that logic or epistemology is based on psychology
  • synonym: reductionist - explaining complex things in overly simple terms
