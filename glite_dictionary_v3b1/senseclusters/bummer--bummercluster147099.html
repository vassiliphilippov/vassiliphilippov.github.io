bummer

noun
bad drug reaction (slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌmɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌmə / (BrE)
A bad experience or unpleasant reaction resulting from the use of a hallucinogenic drug.

Examples

A drug bummer
He described his acid trip as a total bummer that left him anxious and paranoid for hours.
I'm glad I never had a bummer while experimenting in college.
Some users fear taking LSD because they might experience a bummer instead of the euphoria they expect.
