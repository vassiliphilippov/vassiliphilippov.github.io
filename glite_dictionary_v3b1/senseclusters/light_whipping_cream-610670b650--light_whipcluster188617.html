light whipping cream

noun
cream with 30–36% butterfat for whipping
4.8 2.4
Pronunciation: / laɪt ˈwɪpɪŋ kriːm / (AmE), / laɪt ˈwɪpɪŋ kriːm / (BrE)
cream containing 30% to 36% butterfat, suitable for whipping but lighter than heavy cream

Examples

Light whipping cream over strawberries
She poured light whipping cream over fresh strawberries for dessert.
The recipe calls for one cup of light whipping cream to be whipped until soft peaks form.
You can use light whipping cream instead of heavy cream for a slightly lighter texture.
