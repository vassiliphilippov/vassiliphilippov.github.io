Porkulus project

noun
wasteful government spending project
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrk.jʊ.ləs ˈprɑː.dʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˈpɔː.kjʊ.ləs ˈprɒdʒ.ekt / (BrE)
A government project considered to involve excessive or unnecessary spending, especially in the context of economic stimulus legislation.

Examples

A Porkulus project bill
Editorials often criticize any large infrastructure bill as a potential Porkulus project.
Many citizens were outraged when the highway renovation was dubbed a Porkulus project by the opposition.
Some senators defend funding, arguing their initiative is essential and not a Porkulus project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boondoggle - A wasteful or pointless project, especially one funded by government
