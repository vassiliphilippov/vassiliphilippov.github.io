tideway

noun
a channel with tidal currents
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪdˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪdˌweɪ / (BrE)
a channel or waterway in which a tidal current flows.

Examples

The tideway channel
Navigating the narrow tideway requires skill, especially at low tide.
Salmon can be seen swimming up the tideway during spawning season.
The boat drifted slowly down the tideway as the water level changed with the tide.
