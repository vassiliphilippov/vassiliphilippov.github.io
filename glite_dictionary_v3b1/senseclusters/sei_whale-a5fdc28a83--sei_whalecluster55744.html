sei whale

noun
a large species of baleen whale
8.6 6.3
Pronunciation: /seɪ weɪl/ /; / /seɪ hweɪl/ (AmE), /seɪ weɪl/ (BrE)
A large baleen whale of the species Balaenoptera borealis, similar to but smaller than the finback whale, found in most oceans.

Examples

A sei whale
A sei whale can reach speeds up to 50 kilometers per hour when swimming.
Scientists are concerned about the population decline of the sei whale due to commercial whaling.
The sei whale is frequently seen near the surface during its long migrations.
