goopy

adjective
thick and sticky
5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈguː.pi/ (AmE), /ˈguː.pi/ (BrE)
thick, sticky, and wet in texture, often unpleasantly so

Examples

Goopy glue
The glue turned goopy after sitting open on the table all night.
She wiped the goopy sunscreen off her hands before touching her phone.
After mixing the ingredients, the dough was still too goopy to knead properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gooey - soft and sticky in texture
