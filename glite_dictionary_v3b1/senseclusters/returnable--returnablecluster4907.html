returnable

adjective
able to be returned
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtɜrnəbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈtɜːnəbl / (BrE)
able to be returned, especially to a place of purchase or to the supplier for reuse or refund.

Examples

Returnable glass bottles
Many stores sell beverages in returnable glass bottles to promote recycling.
Please check if the item is returnable before making a purchase online.
Some cans are returnable for a small deposit when you bring them back to the supermarket.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonreturnable - not able to be returned
  • synonym: refundable - able to be refunded or paid back
