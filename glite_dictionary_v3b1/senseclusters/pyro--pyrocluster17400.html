pyro

noun
fire-obsessed person
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpaɪ.roʊ/ (AmE), /ˈpaɪ.rəʊ/ (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is fascinated with or obsessed by fire or setting things on fire

Examples

School pyro nickname
Everyone at school knew him as the pyro because he was always talking about fire.
The group joked that their friend was a pyro after he volunteered to light the barbecue again.
She was called a pyro for her fascination with fireworks and bonfires.
