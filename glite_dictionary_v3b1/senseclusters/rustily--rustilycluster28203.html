rustily

adverb
with reduced skill
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌstɪli / (AmE), / ˈrʌstɪli / (BrE)
in a way that shows reduced skill or fluency due to lack of recent practice; awkwardly or imperfectly.

Examples

Speak rustily
After years without speaking French, he greeted his friends rustily and struggled to remember words.
The old machine started rustily, making strange noises before running smoothly.
She danced rustily at first, but soon regained her confidence on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in a clumsy or unskilled manner
  • antonym: fluently - in a smooth and skillful way
