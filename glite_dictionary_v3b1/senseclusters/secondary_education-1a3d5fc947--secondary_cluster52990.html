secondary education

noun
education after primary school
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛkəndɛri ɛˈdjuːkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛkəndəri ɪˈdjuːkeɪʃən / (BrE)
education that follows primary or elementary education, typically provided by a high school or similar institution and preparing students for higher education or employment

Examples

Secondary education level
After finishing secondary education, she decided to pursue a degree at university.

Many countries make secondary education compulsory for students up to the age of sixteen.
