flowy

adjective
smooth and flowing
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfloʊ.iː/ (AmE), /ˈfləʊ.iː/ (BrE)
having a smooth, continuous, and free-flowing style or movement, often used to describe music, writing, or dance

Examples

Flowy dance moves
The dancer's flowy movements captivated everyone in the audience.
She wore a flowy dress that moved beautifully as she walked along the beach.
His writing style is so flowy that the story feels like a gentle stream.
