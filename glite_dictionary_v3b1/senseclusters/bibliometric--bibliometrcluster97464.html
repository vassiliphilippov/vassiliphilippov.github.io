bibliometric

adjective
statistical analysis of publications
Pronunciation: / ˌbɪbliəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌbɪbliəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the statistical analysis of books, articles, or other publications

Examples

Bibliometric study
The researchers conducted a bibliometric study to evaluate trends in scientific publishing.
A bibliometric analysis can reveal which topics are most frequently discussed in academic journals.
She used bibliometric methods to measure the impact of various authors in her field.

Related Terms

  • antonym: qualitative - relating to the quality or characteristics of something, not measured by numbers
  • synonym: statistical - relating to the use of statistics
