homeschool

verb
to teach at home
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌskuːl / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌskuːl / (BrE)
to teach a child at home instead of sending them to a traditional school

Examples

To homeschool children
Many parents choose to homeschool their children for greater flexibility in learning.
After moving to a remote area, they decided to homeschool their two sons.
Some families homeschool because they want to provide a personalized education.

Related Terms

  • synonym: educate - to teach or instruct someone
  • antonym: school - to educate in a traditional school setting
