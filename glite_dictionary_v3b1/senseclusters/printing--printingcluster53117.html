printing

noun
set of copies produced at one time
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɪn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
a set of copies of a book, newspaper, or similar printed work produced at one time

Examples

First printing
After the first printing sold out, collectors began looking for early copies.
The publisher is planning a second printing of the popular novel due to high demand.
