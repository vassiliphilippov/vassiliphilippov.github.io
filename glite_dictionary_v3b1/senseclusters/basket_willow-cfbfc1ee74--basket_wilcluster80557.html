basket willow

noun
Eurasian willow used for basket making
7.2 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbæs.kət ˈwɪ.loʊ/ /; / /ˈbæs.kɪt ˈwɪ.loʊ/ (AmE), /ˈbɑː.skɪt ˈwɪ.ləʊ/ (BrE)
A Eurasian willow (Salix viminalis) with flexible, reddish or purple twigs, used especially for making baskets.

Examples

Basket willow twigs
Farmers grow basket willow along riverbanks because its flexible twigs are ideal for weaving.
In traditional workshops, the bark of basket willow is stripped for use in natural dyes.
The local artisans use basket willow to craft beautiful, sturdy baskets for the market.
