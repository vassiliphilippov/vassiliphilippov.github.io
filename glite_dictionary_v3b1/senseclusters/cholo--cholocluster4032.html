cholo

noun
Latino gang member
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊləʊ / (BrE)
a slang term for a Latino person, especially a young man associated with urban street culture or gangs in the United States

Examples

Urban cholo style
The film explores the struggles faced by a young cholo growing up in East Los Angeles.
He dressed in the style typical of a cholo, with baggy pants and a bandana.
Some people use the word cholo in a negative way to stereotype Latino youth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gangster - a member of a gang, especially involved in criminal activity
