Kiowa

proper noun
Native American people
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkaɪ.oʊ.wə / (AmE), / ˈkaɪ.ə.wə / (BrE)
a member of a Native American people originally from the Great Plains region of the United States

Examples

The Kiowa tribe
The Kiowa are known for their intricate beadwork and vibrant cultural festivals.
Many Kiowa traditions have been passed down through generations of storytellers.
A Kiowa elder spoke about the tribe's history at the community gathering.
