leucaemia

noun
cancer of blood-forming tissues
3.7 5.6
Pronunciation: / luːˈkiː.mi.ə / (AmE), / luːˈkiː.mi.ə / (BrE)
A type of cancer affecting the blood-forming tissues, characterized by an abnormal increase in white blood cells.
