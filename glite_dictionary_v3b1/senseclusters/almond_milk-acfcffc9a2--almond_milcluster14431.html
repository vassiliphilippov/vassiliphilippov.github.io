almond milk

noun
plant-based milk from ground almonds
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːmənd mɪlk / (AmE), / ˈɑːmənd mɪlk / (BrE)
a plant-based milk made by grinding almonds and mixing them with water, often used as a substitute for dairy milk

Examples

A carton of almond milk
He bought a carton of almond milk for his morning smoothies.
Many people use almond milk in their coffee as a dairy-free option.
Vegans often choose almond milk because it contains no animal products.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dairy milk - milk produced by mammals, especially cows
  • synonym: soy milk - milk made from soybeans, a non-dairy alternative
