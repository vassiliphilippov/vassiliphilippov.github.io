undeservingly

adverb
not deserved
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈzɜːrvɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈzɜːvɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is not deserved or not earned

Examples

Blamed undeservingly
She was undeservingly blamed for the mistake, even though she had nothing to do with it.
The team was undeservingly criticized despite their hard work and dedication.
He received the award undeservingly, as others had contributed much more to the project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deservedly - in a way that is rightfully earned or merited
  • synonym: unjustly - in a way that is not fair or right
