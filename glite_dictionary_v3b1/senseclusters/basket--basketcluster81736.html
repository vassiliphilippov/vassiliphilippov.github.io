basket

noun
woven container with handles
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæskət / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a container, typically made by weaving together thin strips of material and often with handles, used for carrying or holding things

Examples

Wicker basket
A picnic basket is perfect for carrying food to the park.
Please put your dirty clothes in the laundry basket.
She filled the basket with fresh apples from the orchard.
