take a dip

verb
briefly swim or immerse in water
2.1
Pronunciation: / teɪk ə dɪp / (AmE), / teɪk ə dɪp / (BrE)
to briefly enter water for swimming or to immerse oneself, usually for a short period

Examples

To take a dip in ocean
After a long walk on the beach, we decided to take a dip in the ocean.
It's so hot today—do you want to take a dip in the lake to cool off?
She likes to take a dip every morning before breakfast.
