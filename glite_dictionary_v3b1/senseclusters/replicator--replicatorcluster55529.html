replicator

noun
device that copies objects
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈre.plɪ.keɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈre.plɪ.keɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a machine or device that creates exact copies of objects or materials, sometimes even making copies of itself

Examples

A replicator machine
The crew used the replicator to create spare parts for the damaged engine.
With a replicator, astronauts could produce fresh meals during long space missions.
Science fiction often features a replicator that can instantly make any object needed.
The scientist designed a replicator that could produce food from basic materials.
In science fiction stories, a replicator can create almost any object on demand.
Some viruses act as natural replicators, making copies of themselves inside host cells.

  • synonym: generator - a machine that produces something, especially electricity or other outputs
  • degree lower: printer - a machine that produces copies of text or images on paper
