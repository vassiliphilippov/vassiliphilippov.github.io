longboard

noun
large surfboard
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋbɔːd / (BrE)
a large surfboard, usually longer and wider than a shortboard, used for riding waves and making smooth turns

Examples

A surf longboard
He learned to surf on a longboard because it was easier to balance.
Many beginners prefer a longboard for its stability and smooth ride.
She carried her longboard down to the beach for an early morning surf session.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shortboard - a smaller, more maneuverable surfboard
  • synonym: surfboard - a board used for riding waves in surfing
