pose

verb
present a question or problem
Pronunciation: / poʊz / (AmE), / pəʊz / (BrE)
to present or offer (a question, problem, or possibility) for people to think about or consider

Examples

To pose a question
Her argument posed new challenges for the research team to consider.
The incident poses serious concerns regarding security at the event.
The report poses several important questions about the future of the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raise - to bring up (a question or issue) for discussion
  • synonym: suggest - to put forward (an idea, question, etc.) for consideration
