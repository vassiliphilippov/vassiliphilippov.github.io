landholding

noun
piece of land owned or held
3.0 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈlænd.hoʊl.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈlænd.həʊl.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
an area of land that someone owns or possesses, or the act of owning land (especially for farming, real estate, or as an asset)

Examples

A large landholding
