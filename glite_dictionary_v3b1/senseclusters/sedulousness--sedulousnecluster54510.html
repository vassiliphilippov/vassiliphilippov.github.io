sedulousness

noun
persistent diligence and attention
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛdʒ.əl.əs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɛd.jʊ.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being constantly diligent and attentive to detail

Examples

Constant sedulousness
Her sedulousness in her research was admired by all her colleagues.
Maintaining a garden requires sedulousness and patience throughout the year.
The team completed the project ahead of schedule thanks to their sedulousness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diligence - persistent and careful effort
  • synonym: industriousness - quality of working energetically and devotedly
  • antonym: laziness - lack of willingness to work or use energy
