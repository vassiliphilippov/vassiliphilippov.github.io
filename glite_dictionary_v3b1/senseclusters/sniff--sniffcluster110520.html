sniff

noun
an act of inhaling through the nose
5.1
Pronunciation: / snɪf / (AmE), / snɪf / (BrE)
an act of breathing in through the nose, especially to detect a smell

Examples

A quick sniff
He took a quick sniff of the milk to check if it was sour.
The dog gave the stranger a cautious sniff before wagging its tail.
With a loud sniff, she tried to clear her blocked nose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sniffle - an act or sound of sniffing repeatedly, usually because of a runny nose
