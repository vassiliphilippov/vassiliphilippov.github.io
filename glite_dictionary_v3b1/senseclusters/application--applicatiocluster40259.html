application

noun
diligent effort or attention
Pronunciation: / ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
serious or diligent effort, especially in carrying out a task or duty

Examples

Hard application
His success was a result of hard work and application.
She showed great application to her studies throughout the semester.
This job requires real application and focus to complete successfully.
