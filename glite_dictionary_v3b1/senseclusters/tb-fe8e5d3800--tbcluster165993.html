TB

noun
tuberculosis; infectious lung disease
Pronunciation: / tiː biː / (AmE), / tiː biː / (BrE)
An infectious disease, also known as tuberculosis, caused by the tubercle bacillus, typically affecting the lungs but sometimes other parts of the body.

Examples

TB test
Routine screening for TB is recommended for people in high-risk occupations.
She was hospitalized after being diagnosed with TB.
The doctor explained that TB is still a major health problem in some countries.

  • synonym: tuberculosis - infectious disease mainly affecting the lungs
