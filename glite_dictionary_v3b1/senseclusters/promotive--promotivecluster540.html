promotive

adjective
encouraging development or progress
1.0
Pronunciation: / prəˈmoʊtɪv / (AmE), / prəˈməʊtɪv / (BrE)
encouraging or supporting the development or progress of something

Examples

A promotive effect
Regular feedback and mentorship have a promotive effect on students' learning outcomes.
The manager created a promotive environment to help employees reach their full potential.
Their policies are promotive of innovation and technological advancement.
