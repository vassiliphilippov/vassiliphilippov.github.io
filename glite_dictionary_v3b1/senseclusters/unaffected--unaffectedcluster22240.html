unaffected

adjective
natural and sincere; not artificial
1.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ə.ˈfek.tɪd/ /; / /ʌn.ə.ˈfek.təd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ə.ˈfek.tɪd/ /; / /ʌn.ə.ˈfek.təd/ (BrE)
