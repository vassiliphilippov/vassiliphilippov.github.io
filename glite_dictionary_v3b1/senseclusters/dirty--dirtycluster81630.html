dirty

adjective
dull or discolored (of color)
2.1 1.32/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd…ùÀêti / (AmE), / Ààd…úÀêti / (BrE)
(of a color or shade) discolored by impurities; not bright or clear; often used to describe a hue mixed with gray, brown, or another dulling tone.

Examples

Dirty-blonde hair
His dirty-blonde hair made him stand out among his dark-haired siblings.
She painted the room a dirty white to match the old furniture.
The artist chose a dirty green to give the landscape a gloomy atmosphere.
