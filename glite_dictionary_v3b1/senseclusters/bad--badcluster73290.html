bad

adjective
feeling regret or sorrow
1.6
Pronunciation: / bæd / (AmE), / bæd / (BrE)
feeling regret, guilt, or sorrow about something one has done or failed to do

Examples

She felt bad about missing her friend's birthday party.
Whenever I forget to call my parents, I feel bad for days.
