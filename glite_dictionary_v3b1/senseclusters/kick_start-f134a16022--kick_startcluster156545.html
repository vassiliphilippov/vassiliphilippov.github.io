kick start

noun
foot-operated starter on a motorcycle
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪk stɑrt / (AmE), / ˈkɪk stɑːt / (BrE)
a lever on a motorcycle or similar vehicle that is operated with the foot to start the engine

Examples

The kick start lever
He tried to get the bike going by pushing down on the kick start.
She learned how to use the kick start after buying her first scooter.
The motorcycle's kick start jammed, so it wouldn't start.
