marchman

noun
border guard
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrtʃmən / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtʃmən / (BrE)
a person who lives near or guards a border region, especially in medieval times

Examples

Medieval marchman
The marchman was responsible for warning the village of any approaching danger.
In medieval England, a marchman often lived in isolated outposts along the frontier.
Legends tell of a brave marchman who defended the border against invaders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: border guard - a person who protects a country's border
  • synonym: sentinel - a guard who keeps watch
