incognito

adjective, adverb
with identity concealed
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌkɑːɡˈniːtoʊ / (AmE), / ɪnˌkɒɡˈniːtəʊ / (BrE)
concealing your identity or doing something in a way that hides who you are
