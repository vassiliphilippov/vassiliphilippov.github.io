homolog

noun
corresponding part by ancestry
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːməlɔɡ /; / ˈhoʊməˌlɔɡ /; / ˈhɑːməˌloʊɡ / (AmE), / ˈhɒməlɒɡ / (BrE)
a gene, organ, or structure in different species that is similar because of shared ancestry (biology)
