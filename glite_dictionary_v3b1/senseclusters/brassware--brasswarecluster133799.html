brassware

noun
objects made of brass
Pronunciation: / ˈbræsˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːswɛə / (BrE)
objects made from brass, such as utensils, ornaments, vessels, or fittings used in homes or construction

Examples

Antique brassware
The antique shop displayed a variety of brassware on its shelves.
She polished the old brassware to restore its original shine before the guests arrived.
Many traditional homes feature brassware as part of their kitchen or living room decor.
The antique shop displayed a collection of polished brassware from the early twentieth century.
He replaced the old bathroom taps with modern brassware to give the space a fresh look.
Many hotels choose high-quality brassware for their bathrooms to ensure durability and style.

  • synonym: fixture - a piece of equipment or furniture fixed in place, especially in a building
  • synonym: hardware - metal tools, fittings, or equipment used in construction or homes
  • synonym: silverware - objects made of silver, especially cutlery and tableware
