sufferingly

adverb
with visible suffering
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌfərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsʌfərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows suffering or patient endurance of pain or hardship

Examples

To look sufferingly
He gazed sufferingly at the long line, knowing he had no choice but to wait.
She listened sufferingly to the endless complaints, trying not to lose her patience.
The dog looked sufferingly at its empty food bowl, hoping someone would notice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patiently - in a way that shows tolerance of delays or problems
  • synonym: stoically - in a way that endures pain or hardship without showing feelings
