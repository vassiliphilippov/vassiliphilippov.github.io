written word

noun
language in written form
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtən wɝd / (AmE), / ˈrɪtən wɜːd / (BrE)
language or information communicated by means of writing, rather than speech.

Examples

The written word
He built his career as a craftsman of the written word, publishing novels and essays.
In the digital age, the power of the written word remains as strong as ever.
She has a deep respect for the written word and always chooses her books carefully.

Related Terms

  • antonym: spoken word - language as expressed in speech rather than writing
