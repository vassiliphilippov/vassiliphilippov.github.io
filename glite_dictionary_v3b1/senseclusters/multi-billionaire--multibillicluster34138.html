multi-billionaire

noun
person with wealth of several billions
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈbɪljəˌnɛr / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈbɪljəˌneə / (BrE)
a person whose material wealth totals several billions of dollars or equivalent currency units

Examples

A multi-billionaire investor
Her investments made her a multi-billionaire in just a decade.
Several multi-billionaires pledged large donations to fight climate change.
The tech company's founder became a multi-billionaire before he turned forty.
