fishbowl

noun
glass bowl for keeping pet fish
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃˌboʊl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃbəʊl / (BrE)
a glass bowl, typically round and transparent, used for keeping small fish as pets

Examples

A glass fishbowl
He tapped on the fishbowl and watched the fish dart around.
She kept her goldfish in a large fishbowl on the living room table.
The fishbowl needed to be cleaned after a week of use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aquarium - a tank, typically larger than a fishbowl, for keeping aquatic animals and plants
