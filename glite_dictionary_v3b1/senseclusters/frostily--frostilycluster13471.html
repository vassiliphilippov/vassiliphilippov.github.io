frostily

adverb
in a cold or unfriendly manner
7.0 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfrɔː.stə.liː/ /; / /ˈfrɔː.stɪ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈfrɒs.tɪ.liː/ /; / /ˈfrɒs.tə.liː/ (BrE)
In a manner lacking warmth or enthusiasm; in a cold or unfriendly way.

Examples

To speak frostily
His suggestions were frostily received by the committee.
She greeted him frostily, barely nodding in his direction.
The manager spoke frostily when asked about the team's performance.
