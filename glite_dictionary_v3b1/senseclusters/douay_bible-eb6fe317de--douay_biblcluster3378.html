Douay Bible

proper noun
English translation of the Vulgate by Catholic scholars
9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈduː.eɪ ˈbaɪ.bəl/ /; / /ˈdjuː.eɪ ˈbaɪ.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈdjuː.eɪ ˈbaɪ.bəl/ /; / /ˈduː.eɪ ˈbaɪ.bəl/ (BrE)
An English translation of the Latin Vulgate Bible prepared by Roman Catholic scholars at Douay and Rheims in the late 16th and early 17th centuries.

Examples

The Douay Bible translation

The Douay Bible differs from the King James Bible in several passages.
