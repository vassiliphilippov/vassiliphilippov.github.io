innocuously

adverb
harmlessly
5.5
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈnɑː.kjʊ.əs.lɪ/ /; / /ɪ.ˈnɑː.kjuː.əs.lɪ/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈnɒ.kjʊ.əs.lɪ/ /; / /ɪ.ˈnɒ.kjuː.əs.lɪ/ (BrE)
in a way that does not cause harm, offense, or danger

Examples

To act innocuously
The dog wagged its tail innocuously as the children approached.
She made a comment innocuously, not realizing it could be misunderstood.
The chemicals mixed innocuously without causing any reaction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dangerously - in a way that is likely to cause harm or danger
  • synonym: harmlessly - in a way that does not cause harm
