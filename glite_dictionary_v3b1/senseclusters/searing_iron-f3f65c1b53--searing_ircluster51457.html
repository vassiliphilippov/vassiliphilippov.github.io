searing iron

noun
hot instrument for tissue destruction
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪrɪŋ ˈaɪərn / (AmE), / ˈsɪərɪŋ ˈaɪən / (BrE)
a heated metal instrument used to destroy tissue, especially to stop bleeding or remove growths

Examples

A searing iron tool
In the past, a searing iron was commonly used to stop severe bleeding.
The patient flinched as the heated searing iron came into contact with his skin.
The surgeon used a searing iron to cauterize the wound after the operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cautery - an instrument or substance used to burn tissue, especially for medical purposes
