unanimated

adjective
not lively; dull
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈænɪˌmeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈænɪmeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not animated or lively; dull or lacking in life or spirit.

Examples

An unanimated room
Although the room was full, the atmosphere remained unanimated and dull.
Her unanimated expression made it hard to tell how she felt about the news.
The speaker's unanimated delivery caused the audience to lose interest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: animated - full of life or excitement; lively
  • synonym: lively - full of life and energy
