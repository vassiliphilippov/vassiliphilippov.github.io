Synonyms

  • unharmed
    2.1 2/m
    unharmed often means safe and without damage or hurt in general situations, while uninjured stresses no physical or mental injury.
  • unhurt
    1.0 0.3/m
    unhurt describes a person as physically not injured after an accident, while uninjured can also cover mental harm.
  • unwounded
    4.8 0.02/m
    unwounded refers specifically to having no wounds or cuts on the body, while uninjured can mean no harm at all.
  • unscathed
    6.3 2/m
