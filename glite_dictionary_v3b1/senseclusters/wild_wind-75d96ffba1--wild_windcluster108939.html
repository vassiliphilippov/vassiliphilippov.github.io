wild wind

noun
strong, forceful wind
Pronunciation: / waɪld wɪnd / (AmE), / waɪld wɪnd / (BrE)
a wind that blows with great force or without restraint

Examples

A strong wild wind
The wild wind knocked over several trees during the storm last night.
She wrapped her coat tightly as the wild wind swept across the open field.
Sailors feared the sudden arrival of a wild wind while out at sea.

Related Terms

  • antonym: breeze - a gentle wind
  • synonym: gale - a very strong wind
