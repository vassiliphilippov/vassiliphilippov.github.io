leavings

noun
leftover remains
Pronunciation: / ˈlivɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈliːvɪŋz / (BrE)
the parts or pieces that remain after the rest has been used, eaten, or removed

Examples

The leavings of food
After the party, only a few leavings of cake and chips were left on the table.
The birds picked at the leavings scattered on the ground after the picnic.
He swept the kitchen floor, collecting the leavings from last night's dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leftovers - food remaining after a meal
  • synonym: remnants - small parts left after the main part is gone
  • antonym: whole - something complete, with nothing removed
