exonerated

adjective
freed from blame or guilt
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɑːnərˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɒnəˌreɪtɪd / (BrE)

The court exonerated her of all charges related to the incident.
