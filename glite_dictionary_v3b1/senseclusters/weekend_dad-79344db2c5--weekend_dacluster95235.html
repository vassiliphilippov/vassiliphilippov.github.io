weekend dad

noun
father who sees his children only on weekends
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːkˌɛnd dæd / (AmE), / ˈwiːkˌɛnd dad / (BrE)
a father who only sees or takes care of his children on weekends, often due to divorce or separation

Examples

Many weekend dads struggle to stay involved in their children's daily lives.
