Viola reichenbachiana

noun
common European violet species
9.5
Pronunciation: / viˈoʊlə ˌraɪkənˈbɑːkiənə / (AmE), / viˈəʊlə ˌraɪkənˈbækɪənə / (BrE)
A common European violet species that grows in woods and hedgerows, known for its heart-shaped leaves and purple flowers.
