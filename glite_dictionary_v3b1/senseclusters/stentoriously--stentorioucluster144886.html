stentoriously

adverb
very loudly
Pronunciation: / stɛnˈtɔːriəsli / (AmE), / stɛnˈtɔːriəsli / (BrE)
in a very loud and powerful manner, especially of a voice or sound

Examples

To stentoriously shout
The coach shouted stentoriously across the field to get the players' attention.
She read the announcement stentoriously so that even those in the back could hear.
The actor delivered his lines stentoriously, filling the entire theater with his voice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loudly - in a manner that produces a lot of noise
  • antonym: softly - in a quiet or gentle manner
