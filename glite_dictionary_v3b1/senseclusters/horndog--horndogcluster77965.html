horndog

noun
slang for sexually eager person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrnˌdɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːndɒɡ / (BrE)
a slang term for a person, usually a man, who is very eager for sexual activity

Examples

A real horndog
Everyone knows Jake is a horndog who flirts with everyone at the bar.
She laughed and called him a horndog after he made another suggestive joke.
Despite his reputation as a horndog, he is actually quite shy around new people.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prude - a person who is very modest or easily shocked by sexual matters
  • synonym: womanizer - a man who pursues many women for sexual relationships
