matka

noun
Indian raw silk fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌtkə / (AmE), / ˈmʌtkə / (BrE)
a coarse, handwoven silk fabric made from raw silk, traditionally produced in parts of India

Examples

Matka silk fabric
She wore a beautiful sari made of matka for the traditional ceremony.
The artisan explained how matka is woven from raw silk using simple handlooms.
Many designers appreciate the unique texture and natural sheen of matka fabric.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mulberry silk - a fine silk fabric made from mulberry silkworms
  • synonym: tussar - a type of wild silk fabric from India
