Laburnum

noun
genus of poisonous flowering trees or shrubs
9.6
Pronunciation: / ləˈbɝnəm / (AmE), / ləˈbɜːnəm / (BrE)
A genus of small trees or shrubs with bright yellow flowers, all parts of which are poisonous.

Examples

Bright Laburnum flowers
Children should be warned not to touch the Laburnum as it is highly poisonous.
In spring, the golden flowers of the Laburnum are a striking sight.
The garden was dominated by a large Laburnum tree in full bloom.
