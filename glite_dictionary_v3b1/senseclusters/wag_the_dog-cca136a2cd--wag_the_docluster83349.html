wag the dog

verb
divert attention from an important issue
9.1 7.0
Pronunciation: /wæɡ ðə dɔːg/ /; / /wæɡ ðə dɑːg/ (AmE), /wæɡ ðə dɒɡ/ (BrE)
to intentionally divert attention from an important matter to something less significant, especially in politics or the media
