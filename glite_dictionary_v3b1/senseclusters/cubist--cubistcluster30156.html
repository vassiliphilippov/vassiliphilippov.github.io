cubist

noun
artist belonging to cubism movement
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːbɪst / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbɪst / (BrE)
an artist who works according to the principles of cubism, an early 20th-century art movement characterized by abstracted and geometric forms

Examples

Famous cubist artist
Many art collectors are eager to acquire works by a renowned cubist.
Pablo Picasso is often regarded as a leading cubist of the twentieth century.
The exhibition features several masterpieces by the famous French cubist, Georges Braque.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cubism - an early 20th-century art movement characterized by use of geometric shapes and abstraction
