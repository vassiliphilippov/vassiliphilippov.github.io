ayapana

noun
tropical shrub used for tea-like drink
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪ.əˈpɑː.nə / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.əˈpɑː.nə / (BrE)
A low, spreading tropical American shrub with long slender leaves, used to make a mildly stimulating drink similar to tea; sometimes classified in the genus Eupatorium.

Examples

The ayapana plant
Some traditional medicines in South America use ayapana leaves for their mild stimulating effect.
The herbalist brewed a pot of ayapana to help her relax in the evening.
We found ayapana growing wild along the riverbank during our hike.
