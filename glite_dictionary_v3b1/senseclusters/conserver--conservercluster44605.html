conserver

noun
food-preserving container
6.1
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɜːrvər / (AmE), / kənˈsɜːvə / (BrE)
a container or device used to preserve food for future use

Examples

A conserver jar
She poured the homemade jam into a conserver to keep it fresh for months.
The old kitchen still had a large conserver used for storing pickled vegetables.
After canning the tomatoes, he placed each jar in a conserver for the winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jar - a glass container used for storing food
  • synonym: preserver - a container or device that keeps food from spoiling
