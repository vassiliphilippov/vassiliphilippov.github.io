nutbag

noun
bag for nuts
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈnʌtbæɡ / (BrE)
a small bag or packet used for carrying, storing, or selling edible nuts

Examples

A nutbag of almonds
She bought a crunchy nutbag of mixed almonds and cashews from the market.
He always keeps a nutbag in his backpack for a healthy snack during the day.
The store offered a discount if you purchased more than one nutbag at a time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pouch - a small bag for carrying items
  • synonym: sack - a large bag made of strong material
