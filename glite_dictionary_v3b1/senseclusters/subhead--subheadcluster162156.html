subhead

noun
heading for a section within a text
2.6 2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌhɛd / (BrE)
a heading given to a subsection of a text, often used to break up sections within a chapter, article, or document

Examples

A subhead in text

Each chapter in the manual is organized under a subhead for easy reference.
The magazine article included a subhead beneath the main title to clarify its topic.
