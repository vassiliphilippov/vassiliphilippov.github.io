readoption

noun
adopting again
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.əˈdɑːp.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌriː.ədˈɒp.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of adopting something again, especially a policy, practice, or idea

Examples

The readoption of guidelines
The readoption of the previous guidelines improved the team's performance.
After much debate, the council voted for the readoption of the original plan.
Many welcomed the readoption of traditional methods in the classroom.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abandonment - the act of giving up or leaving something behind
  • synonym: adoption - the act of beginning to use or accept something
  • synonym: reinstatement - the act of restoring something to its previous position or condition
