yeah

interjection
sarcastic disbelief
Pronunciation: / jæ / (AmE), / jæ / (BrE)
used to express doubt or disbelief, often in a sarcastic or dismissive way.

Examples

Yeah, right
He claimed he could run a marathon in two hours, and she replied, "yeah, right."
"You finished all your homework already? Yeah, I bet," his friend said with a smirk.
When he said he would never be late again, everyone just said, "yeah, sure."

Related Terms

  • antonym: definitely - used sincerely to show agreement or certainty
  • synonym: really - used to question the truth of something, often sarcastically
  • synonym: sure - used sarcastically to show disbelief or doubt
