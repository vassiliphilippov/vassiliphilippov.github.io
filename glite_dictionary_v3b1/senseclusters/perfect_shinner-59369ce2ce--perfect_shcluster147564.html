perfect shinner

noun
impressive skateboarding trick hitting the shin
Pronunciation: / pərˈfɛkt ˈʃɪnər / (AmE), / pəˈfɛkt ˈʃɪnə / (BrE)
A skateboarding trick in which the board strikes or lands on the rider's shin, performed with remarkable accuracy or impact.
