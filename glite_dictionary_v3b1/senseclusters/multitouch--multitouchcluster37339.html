multitouch

adjective
detects multiple touches
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌtʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌtʌtʃ / (BrE)
able to detect or respond to more than one point of contact at the same time, especially on a screen or device

Examples

Multitouch screen
The new tablet features a multitouch display that allows you to use several fingers at once.
Many modern smartphones support multitouch gestures for zooming and rotating images.
The interactive map uses multitouch technology to let multiple users explore it together.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single-touch - able to detect only one point of contact at a time
  • synonym: touchscreen - a display that responds to touch
