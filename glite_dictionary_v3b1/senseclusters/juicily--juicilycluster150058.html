juicily

adverb
in a juicy way
3.8 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd íuÀês…™li / (AmE), / Ààd íuÀês…™li / (BrE)
in a way that is full of juice or in a lively, interesting manner

Examples

Peach drips juicily
The peach was so ripe that it dripped juicily down her chin.
He recounted the story juicily, making everyone laugh at the details.
The burger was cooked perfectly and tasted juicily delicious.
