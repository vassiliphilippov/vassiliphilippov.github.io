hornet

noun
a large stinging paper wasp
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrnɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɔːnɪt / (BrE)
a large wasp known for its powerful sting, typically larger than a common wasp and living in paper nests

Examples

A hornet sting
A hornet flew into the room, causing everyone to panic.
He was stung by a hornet while hiking in the woods.
The nest was swarming with angry hornets when they disturbed it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wasp - a stinging winged insect related to bees and ants
