gripsack

noun
small suitcase
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪpˌsæk / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪpˌsak / (BrE)
A small, portable suitcase used for carrying clothes and personal items, especially in the past.

Examples

A gripsack suitcase
He packed his clothes into a worn leather gripsack before leaving for the city.
She always traveled light, carrying only a single gripsack on her journeys.
The detective hurriedly grabbed his gripsack and rushed out to catch the train.

Related Terms

  • synonym: valise - a small piece of hand luggage
