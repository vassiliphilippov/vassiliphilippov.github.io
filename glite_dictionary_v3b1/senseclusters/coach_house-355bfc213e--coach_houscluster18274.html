coach house

noun
building for housing coaches and vehicles
4.2
Pronunciation: / koʊtʃ haʊs / (AmE), / kəʊtʃ haʊs / (BrE)
a small building, typically near a large house or estate, used for storing coaches, carriages, or other vehicles

Examples

Old coach house
The coach house was once filled with elegant carriages and horses.
The old estate still has a beautiful coach house next to the main building.
They converted the original coach house into a cozy guest cottage.
