riflewoman

noun
female rifle-bearing soldier
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪfəlˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈraɪflˌwʊmən /; / ˈraɪf(ə)lˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is trained or skilled in using a rifle, especially as a soldier or in shooting sports
