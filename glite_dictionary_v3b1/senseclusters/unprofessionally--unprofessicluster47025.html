unprofessionally

adverb
without proper skill
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprəˈfɛʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌʌnprəˈfɛʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that lacks the skill, care, or standards expected in a profession

Examples

Behave unprofessionally
The lawyer behaved unprofessionally by arriving late and unprepared for the trial.
If you respond unprofessionally to criticism, it can damage your reputation at work.
The report was written unprofessionally, with many errors and missing information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carelessly - in a way that shows lack of attention or care
  • antonym: professionally - in a way that shows skill and proper conduct for a profession
