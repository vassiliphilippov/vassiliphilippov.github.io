anemone

noun
marine animal with ring of tentacles
Pronunciation: / əˈnɛməni / (AmE), / əˈnɛməni / (BrE)
A sea-dwelling animal that resembles a flower, with a ring of tentacles around its mouth, related to corals but having no hard skeleton.

Examples

Sea anemone tentacles
An anemone attaches itself to rocks or coral reefs in shallow seas.
The clownfish darted among the swaying tentacles of the anemone.
Unlike corals, an anemone does not form a hard skeleton.
