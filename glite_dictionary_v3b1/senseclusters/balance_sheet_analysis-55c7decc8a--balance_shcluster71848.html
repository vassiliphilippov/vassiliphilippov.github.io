balance sheet analysis

noun
evaluation of a company's balance sheet
1.0 4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæləns ʃiːt əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˈbæl.əns ʃiːt əˈnæl.ə.sɪs / (BrE)
The examination and evaluation of a company's assets, liabilities, and equity, usually performed at regular intervals to assess financial health.

Examples

A balance sheet analysis
A thorough balance sheet analysis is essential before considering any merger or acquisition.
After conducting a balance sheet analysis, the finance team identified areas to reduce liabilities.
Regular balance sheet analysis helps companies maintain strong financial health and transparency.
