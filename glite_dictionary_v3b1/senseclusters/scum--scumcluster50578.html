scum

verb
to form surface layer
6.6
Pronunciation: / skʌm / (AmE), / skʌm / (BrE)
to form or become covered with a layer of scum on the surface of a liquid

Examples

Pond scums over
After several hot days, the pond scummed over with green algae.
If you leave the soup uncovered, it may scum on the surface.
The water in the bucket scummed quickly after being left outside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - to remove or become free from a covering layer
  • synonym: film - to form a thin layer on a surface
