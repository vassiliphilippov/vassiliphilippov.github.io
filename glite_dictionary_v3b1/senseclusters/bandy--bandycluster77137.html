bandy

verb
mention or discuss casually
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbændi / (AmE), / ˈbandi / (BrE)
to mention or discuss (an idea, rumor, or topic) casually or repeatedly, often without serious consideration

Examples

Bandy rumors around
Rumors of his resignation were bandied around the office.
The committee bandied several ideas about before making a decision.
We shouldn't bandy accusations without any evidence.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: debate - discuss a subject formally; argue about (a subject)
  • degree lower: suggest - to mention or propose as an idea without much discussion
