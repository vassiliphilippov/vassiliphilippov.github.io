trabeculate

adjective
having or relating to trabeculae
Pronunciation: / trəˈbɛkjʊlət / (AmE), / trəˈbiːkjʊlət / (BrE)
Composed of or relating to trabeculae; having a structure characterized by small, beam-like or lattice-like bars.

Examples

Trabeculate bone structure
Scientists noted the trabeculate pattern in the newly described species' cartilage.
The trabeculate bone allows for greater lightweight strength in the skeletal structure.
Under the microscope, the tissue appeared highly trabeculate and porous.
