protection

noun
state of being kept safe
1.0
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛkʃən / (AmE), / prəˈtɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act or state of keeping someone or something safe from harm, danger, or injury; something that prevents damage or injury

Examples

Protection from harm
Insurance provides financial protection against accidents and disasters.
She wore a hat for protection from the sun.
The forest offered the animals some protection from predators.
Plastic wrapping provides protection for electronic devices during shipping.
The lotion forms a barrier on your skin that offers protection from the sun.
The workers wore helmets as protection against falling debris.
Many homeowners install alarm systems as a form of protection against burglary.
She wears sunglasses for protection from the sun's ultraviolet rays.
The government offered protection to endangered wildlife species in the national park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defense - the act of protecting from attack or harm
  • antonym: exposure - the state of being unprotected or vulnerable to harm
  • antonym: harm - physical or mental damage or injury
  • synonym: shelter - something that provides cover or safety, especially from danger or bad weather
