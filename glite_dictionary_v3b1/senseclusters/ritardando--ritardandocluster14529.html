ritardando

adverb
gradually slowing down in music
9.9 7.1
Pronunciation: /rɪ.tɑːr.ˈdɑːn.doʊ/ /; / /rɪ.tər.ˈdæn.doʊ/ (AmE), /rɪ.tɑː.ˈdɑːn.dəʊ/ /; / /rɪ.tə.ˈdæn.dəʊ/ (BrE)
(music) Gradually slowing down the tempo of a piece or passage.

Examples
