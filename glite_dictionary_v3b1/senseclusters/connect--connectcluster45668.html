connect

verb
establish communication with someone
1.0 1.6
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈnekt/ (AmE), /kə.ˈnekt/ (BrE)
to establish contact or a relationship with someone, especially by communicating or understanding them

Examples

To connect with people

She found it easy to connect with people wherever she traveled.
The charismatic teacher was able to connect with students from diverse backgrounds.
