clubable

adjective
sociable; suited to club membership
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌb.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈklʌb.ə.bəl / (BrE)
(of a person) sociable and suitable for membership of a club; enjoying the company of others in social clubs.

Examples

A clubable person
Despite his fame, she found him surprisingly clubable and down-to-earth at dinner parties.
He was a clubable fellow, always eager to make new acquaintances at the local pub.
Many politicians of the 19th century prided themselves on being clubable gentlemen.
