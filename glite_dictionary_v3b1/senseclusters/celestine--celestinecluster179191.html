celestine

noun
strontium sulfate mineral
Pronunciation: / səˈlɛstiːn / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛstiːn / (BrE)
a mineral consisting of strontium sulfate, typically found as blue or colorless crystals in sedimentary rocks
