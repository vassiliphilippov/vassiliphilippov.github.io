in the shade

preposition
in a cool, sun-sheltered place
4.2
Pronunciation: / ɪn ðə ʃeɪd / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ʃeɪd / (BrE)
in a place that is sheltered from direct sunlight, usually making it cooler or more comfortable

Examples

In the shade of a tree
On hot afternoons, children often play in the shade to avoid the heat.
The cat was sleeping quietly in the shade of the garden wall.
We spread our picnic blanket in the shade to enjoy a cooler lunch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in the sun - in a place exposed to direct sunlight
