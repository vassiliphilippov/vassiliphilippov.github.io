popple

noun
light bubbling sound
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑpəl / (AmE), / ˈpɒpəl / (BrE)
a light bubbling or popping sound, especially made by water or liquid in gentle motion

Examples

A popple of water
A gentle popple came from the kettle as the water began to warm on the stove.
The popple of the stream was soothing as we sat by the riverbank.
She listened to the soft popple of bubbles rising in her glass of soda.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gurgle - a low, bubbling sound made by water
  • synonym: ripple - a small wave or undulating motion on the surface of water
