-lessness

x
noun-forming suffix
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛsnəs / (AmE), / ˈlɛsnəs / (BrE)
a suffix forming nouns that mean the state or quality of being without something

Examples

The -lessness suffix
The -lessness in 'hopelessness' shows the absence of hope.
Words like 'fearlessness' and 'carelessness' use the suffix -lessness to express a lack of something.
Adding -lessness to 'end' creates 'endlessness', meaning the state of having no end.

Related Terms

  • antonym: -ful - suffix forming adjectives meaning 'full of'
  • synonym: -ness - suffix forming nouns indicating a state or quality
