islands of Langerhans

noun
pancreatic cell clusters producing insulin
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪləndz əv ˈlæŋɡɚˌhænz / (AmE), / ˈaɪləndz əv ˈlæŋɡəhænz / (BrE)
groups of specialized cells in the pancreas that produce insulin and other hormones regulating blood sugar
