artless

adjective
sincere; without deception
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrtləs / (AmE), / ˈɑːtləs /; / ˈɑːtlɪs / (BrE)
simple, sincere, and natural; without deception or artificiality (of a person or their behavior)
