slimily

adverb
in an insincere way
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈslaɪmɪli / (AmE), / ˈslaɪmɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is unpleasantly insincere, untrustworthy, or overly flattering

Examples

He speaks slimily
He slimily complimented his boss, hoping for a promotion.
The politician slimily avoided answering the reporter's direct question.
She slimily agreed with everything, making everyone uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obsequiously - in an excessively eager or flattering manner
  • antonym: sincerely - in a genuine and honest way
