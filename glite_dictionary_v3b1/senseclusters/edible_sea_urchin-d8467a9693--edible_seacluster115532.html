edible sea urchin

noun
sea urchin species used as food
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdəbəl si ˈɝːtʃɪn / (AmE), / ˈɛdəbl̩ si ˈɜːtʃɪn / (BrE)
A type of sea urchin that is suitable for eating and is often harvested for culinary use.

Examples

Fresh edible sea urchin
Divers harvest edible sea urchin along rocky coastlines to supply high-end restaurants.
In Japan, edible sea urchin roe, known as uni, is considered a delicacy.
The chef prepared a delicate dish using fresh edible sea urchin from the local market.
