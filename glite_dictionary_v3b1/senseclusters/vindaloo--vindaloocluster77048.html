vindaloo

noun
spicy Indian curry
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪndəˈlu / (AmE), / ˌvɪndəˈluː / (BrE)
a very spicy Indian curry dish, typically made with meat, potatoes, and a hot sauce

Examples

Spicy vindaloo curry
He loves ordering vindaloo at his favorite Indian restaurant because of its intense heat.
The chef prepared a lamb vindaloo with extra spices for the adventurous diners.
If you enjoy spicy food, you should definitely try a vindaloo next time.
