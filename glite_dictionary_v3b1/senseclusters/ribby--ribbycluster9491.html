ribby

adjective
with visible ribs
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪbi / (AmE), / ˈrɪbi / (BrE)
having prominent or visible ribs, usually because of thinness or lack of flesh

Examples

Ribby stray dog
The stray dog looked very ribby after weeks without food.
After a long illness, the horse became noticeably ribby and weak.
You could see the ribby outline of the animal as it searched for food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emaciated - extremely thin, often due to illness or lack of food
  • antonym: plump - having a full, rounded shape
  • synonym: skinny - very thin, often in an unhealthy way
