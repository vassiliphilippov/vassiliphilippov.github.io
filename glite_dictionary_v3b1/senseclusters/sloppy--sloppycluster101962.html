sloppy

adjective
overly sentimental or emotional
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑpi / (AmE), / ˈslɒpi / (BrE)
excessively or abnormally sentimental or emotional, especially in a way that seems insincere or lacking subtlety

Examples

Sloppy love story
Her sloppy letter made it hard to take her feelings seriously.
Some critics dismissed the novel as sloppy and overly dramatic.
The movie was so sloppy that I found myself rolling my eyes at every emotional scene.

Related Terms

  • antonym: restrained - characterized by moderation and self-control, the opposite of excessive emotion
  • synonym: sentimental - showing or causing excessive emotions, especially tenderness or sadness
