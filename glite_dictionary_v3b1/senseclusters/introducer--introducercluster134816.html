introducer

noun
person who presents
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈdusər / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈdjuːsə / (BrE)
a person or thing that presents or introduces someone or something to others, especially at an event or for the first time

Examples

Event introducer
The introducer welcomed the guest speaker to the stage with a brief biography.
A special introducer is used in medical procedures to help insert catheters safely.
At the awards ceremony, the introducer made the audience laugh with a funny story.
The introducer gave a brief speech before inviting the award winner to the stage.
At the conference, the introducer made sure each speaker felt welcome.
A good introducer helps the audience feel connected to the guest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: host - a person who receives or entertains guests
  • synonym: presenter - a person who introduces or hosts an event or show
