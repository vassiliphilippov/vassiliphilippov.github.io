harakiri

noun
ritual suicide by self-disembowelment (samurai)
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑːrəˈkɪri / (AmE), / ˌhærəˈkɪri / (BrE)
a ritual suicide by self-disembowelment with a sword, traditionally practiced by samurai in Japan

Examples

Traditional harakiri ritual

Stories of harakiri illustrate the importance of honor in samurai culture.
The disgraced samurai committed harakiri rather than face execution.
