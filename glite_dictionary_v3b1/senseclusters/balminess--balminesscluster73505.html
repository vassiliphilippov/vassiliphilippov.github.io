balminess

noun
pleasant mildness of weather
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːlminəs / (AmE), / ˈbɑːlmɪnəs / (BrE)
the pleasantly mild and soothing quality of the air or weather

Examples

The balminess of spring
After the long winter, the balminess of spring was a welcome change.
The balminess of the climate attracted tourists to the island.
We enjoyed the balminess of the evening as we dined outdoors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mildness - the quality of being moderate or gentle, especially of weather
