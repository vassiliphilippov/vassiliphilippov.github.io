A period when a service, such as electricity or computer systems, is temporarily unavailable or stopped.

Examples

Power outage
A severe storm caused a power outage across the whole city last night.
Due to maintenance, the website will experience a brief outage this weekend.
The company apologized to customers for the unexpected outage that lasted several hours.

Synonyms
