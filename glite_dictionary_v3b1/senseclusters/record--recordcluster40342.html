record

noun
team's wins, losses, and ties
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkərd /; / ˈrekɚd / (AmE), / ˈrɛkɔːd /; / ˈrekɔːd / (BrE)
the achievements, performance history, or statistics of a person, team, or organization, especially in sports or law

Examples

She broke the world record for the fastest marathon.
The team is determined to set a new record next season.
