sustainably

adverb
in a lasting way
4.2
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈsteɪ.nə.blɪ/ /; / /sə.ˈsteɪ.nə.bliː/ (AmE), /sə.ˈsteɪ.nə.blɪ/ /; / /sə.ˈsteɪ.nə.bliː/ (BrE)
in a way that does not harm the environment or use up resources, so it can continue for a long time

Examples

To sustainably use resources
The company sources its materials sustainably to protect the environment for future generations.
Farmers are learning to grow crops more sustainably by rotating fields and reducing chemical use.
We need to manage our finances sustainably to avoid debt and ensure long-term stability.
The company now produces all its goods sustainably to reduce its environmental impact.
Farmers are learning to grow crops sustainably by rotating fields and using fewer chemicals.
If we want to protect natural resources, we must live sustainably in our daily lives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: responsibly - in a way that shows care for the consequences of actions
  • antonym: unsustainably - in a way that cannot be continued without causing harm or running out of resources
  • antonym: wastefully - in a way that uses resources carelessly or excessively
