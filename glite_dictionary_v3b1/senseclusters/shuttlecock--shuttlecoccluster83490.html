shuttlecock

verb
toss back and forth repeatedly
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌt̬lˌkɑk / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtlˌkɒk / (BrE)
to send or toss to and fro rapidly between people or places, especially in a repetitive or back-and-forth manner, like a shuttlecock is hit in badminton.

Examples

To shuttlecock ideas
During the meeting, ideas were shuttlecocking back and forth across the table.
The proposal was shuttlecocked between different departments for months before a decision was made.
They shuttlecocked the blame, each refusing to accept responsibility for the error.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bandy - to pass or exchange (words) rapidly and back and forth
