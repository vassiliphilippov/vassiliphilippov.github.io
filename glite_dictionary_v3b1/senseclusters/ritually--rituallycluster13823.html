ritually

adverb
in a ceremonial manner
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃuəli / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃuəli / (BrE)
In a way that involves or relates to a ritual or ceremony.

Examples

To ritually observe festivals
Ancient festivals were ritually observed every year by the entire community.
He was ritually anointed by the high priest during the coronation.
The team ritually gathers before every game to recite their oath.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceremonially - in a way that is related to ceremony
