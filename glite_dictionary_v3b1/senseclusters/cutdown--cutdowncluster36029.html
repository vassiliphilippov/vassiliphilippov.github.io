cutdown

noun
shortened version
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌtˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈkʌtˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a shortened or edited version of a film, commercial, song, or other recorded material.

Examples

Cutdown version
The director released a special cutdown of the movie for television broadcast.
We watched a cutdown of the documentary that was only twenty minutes long.
The radio station played a cutdown of the song to fit their schedule.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abridgment - a shortened form of a written work
  • synonym: edit - a version of material that has been changed or shortened
  • antonym: full version - the complete, unedited form of material
