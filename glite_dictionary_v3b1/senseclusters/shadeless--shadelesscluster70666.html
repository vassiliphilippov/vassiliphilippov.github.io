shadeless

adjective
without shade
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪdləs / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪdləs / (BrE)
having no shade or protection from direct sunlight

Examples

Shadeless field
The children played in the shadeless field and quickly became hot and tired.
We walked for miles along a shadeless road under the blazing sun.
The picnic area was completely shadeless, so we brought umbrellas for cover.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shady - protected from sunlight by shade
  • synonym: sunny - bright with sunlight and little or no shade
