whorled milkweed

noun
a North American milkweed with whorled leaves
8.6
Pronunciation: / wɜrld ˈmɪlkwid / (AmE), / wɜːld ˈmɪlkwiːd / (BrE)
A species of milkweed native to eastern North America, characterized by narrow leaves arranged in whorls and greenish-white flowers.
