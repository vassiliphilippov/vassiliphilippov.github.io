anomaly

noun
deviation from the norm
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑːməli /; / əˈnɑː.mə.li / (AmE), / əˈnɒməli /; / əˈnɒm.ə.li / (BrE)
something or someone that is not normal, expected, or typical

Examples

A strange anomaly
Astronomers detected a gravitational anomaly that suggested the presence of a hidden planet.
Her calm reaction was an anomaly in an otherwise tense situation.
Scientists discovered an unexpected anomaly in the experiment's data.
Being left-handed in a family of right-handed people made him feel like an anomaly.
She was considered an anomaly among her peers because of her unique perspective.
The new student proved to be an anomaly in a school known for its strict traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abnormality - something abnormal; a deviation from the norm
  • antonym: normality - the state of being normal; conforming to the standard
  • synonym: oddity - a person or thing that is strange or unusual
