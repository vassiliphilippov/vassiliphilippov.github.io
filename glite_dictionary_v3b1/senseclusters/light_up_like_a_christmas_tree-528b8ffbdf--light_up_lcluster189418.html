light up like a Christmas tree

verb
become brightly illuminated
3.7
Pronunciation: / laɪt ʌp laɪk ə ˈkrɪs.məs ˈtri / (AmE), / laɪt ʌp laɪk ə ˈkrɪs.məs ˈtriː / (BrE)
(of a place or object) to be decorated or made brightly visible by many decorative lights; to become dramatically illuminated.

Examples

The building lit up like a Christmas tree
The town hall lit up like a Christmas tree for the festival, covered in colorful bulbs.
Every December the main street lights up like a Christmas tree, attracting visitors from the whole county.
When the city council approved the display, the bridge lit up like a Christmas tree that evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illuminate - to light something up or make it brighter
