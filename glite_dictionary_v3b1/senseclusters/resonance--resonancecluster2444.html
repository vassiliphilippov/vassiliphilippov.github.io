resonance

noun
speech sound quality from vocal chamber resonance
1.3 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈre.zə.nəns/ (AmE), /ˈre.zə.nəns/ (BrE)
the quality of a voiced speech sound determined by the vibration and amplification in the resonating chambers of the throat, mouth, and nasal cavities

Examples

Nasal resonance
Nasal resonance can affect how certain words are pronounced.
Proper resonance is crucial for clear and pleasant speech.
The rich resonance of her voice made her an excellent singer.
