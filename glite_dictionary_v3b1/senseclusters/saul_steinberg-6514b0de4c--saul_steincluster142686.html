Saul Steinberg

proper noun
Romanian-born American cartoonist and illustrator
7.5
Pronunciation: / sɔl ˈstaɪnˌbɜːrɡ / (AmE), / sɔːl ˈstaɪnbɜːɡ / (BrE)
(1914–1999) a United States cartoonist and illustrator, born in Romania, known for his satirical caricatures and contributions to The New Yorker magazine.
