meese

noun
joking plural of moose
9.9
Pronunciation: / miːs / (AmE), / miːs / (BrE)
a humorous or incorrect plural form of 'moose', used jokingly instead of the correct 'moose'.

Examples

A herd of meese
He laughed and said he saw a herd of meese by the lake.
Some people jokingly call several moose meese when telling funny stories.
The children giggled at the idea of a group of meese crossing the road.
