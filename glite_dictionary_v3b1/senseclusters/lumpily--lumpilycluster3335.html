lumpily

adverb
unevenly, with lumps
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌmpɪli / (AmE), / ˈlʌmpɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is full of lumps or uneven parts

Examples

Mashed potatoes lumpily made
The mashed potatoes were prepared lumpily, leaving small chunks throughout the dish.
Paint was applied lumpily to the wall, creating an uneven texture.
The batter mixed lumpily and did not bake evenly in the oven.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smoothly - in a way that is even and without lumps
  • synonym: unevenly - in a way that is not even or regular
