cummock

noun
large wooden tub
an old or dialect word for a large wooden vessel or tub, often used for storing liquids or food

Examples

A large cummock
The farmer poured fresh milk into the cummock after the morning milking.
In the old kitchen, a cummock stood by the door, filled with water from the well.
They used a cummock to store salted fish during the winter months.
