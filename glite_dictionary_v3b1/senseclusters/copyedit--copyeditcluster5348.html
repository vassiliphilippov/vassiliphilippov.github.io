copyedit

verb
edit to correct errors in written text
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑpiˌɛdɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒpiˌɛdɪt / (BrE)
to edit written or printed material to correct errors in grammar, spelling, style, or consistency

Examples

To copyedit articles
He spent several hours copyediting articles for the magazine.
It is important to copyedit your essay to catch any grammar mistakes.
She was hired to copyedit the final draft of the manuscript before publication.
