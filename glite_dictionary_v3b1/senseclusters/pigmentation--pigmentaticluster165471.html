pigmentation

noun
cosmetic tattooing procedure
5.5
Pronunciation: / ccp6a61m59nc8te6a8359n / (AmE), / ccp6a61m5bnc8te6a8359n / (BrE)
a cosmetic or paramedical procedure in which pigment is implanted into the skin to mimic makeup, restore features, or conceal scars.

Examples

Cosmetic pigmentation
After chemotherapy, she chose microblading and cosmetic pigmentation to restore her lost eyebrows.
The clinic specializes in paramedical pigmentation to camouflage surgical scars and skin irregularities.
He trained in permanent makeup and offers natural-looking pigmentation for clients with thinning brows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: micropigmentation - a technique of depositing pigment into the dermal layer of the skin for cosmetic or medical purposes.
  • synonym: permanent makeup - a cosmetic technique that applies semi-permanent pigment to the skin to simulate makeup (eyebrows, eyeliner, lips).
