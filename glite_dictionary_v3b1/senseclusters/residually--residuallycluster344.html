residually

adverb
in a remaining way
7.5
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈzɪ.djuː.ə.li/ /; / /rɪ.ˈzɪ.duː.ə.li/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈzɪ.djuː.ə.li/ (BrE)
in a manner relating to what remains after the main part has been removed or used.

Examples

Pain remains residually
The pain from his injury lingered residually even after the treatment ended.
Some chemicals may remain residually in the soil after cleaning.
She felt residually tired despite getting a full night's sleep.
