continent-wide

adjective
affecting the entire continent
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑn.tɪ.nəntˈwaɪd / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.tɪ.nəntˈwaɪd / (BrE)

Examples

A continent-wide drought
