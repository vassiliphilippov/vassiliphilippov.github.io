awkward

adjective
socially uncomfortable; lacking ease
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːkwərd / (AmE), / ˈɔːkwəd / (BrE)
feeling or showing embarrassment or discomfort in social situations; lacking ease or confidence in manner

Examples

An awkward smile
He gave an awkward smile when he was introduced to the group.
She always feels awkward at large social gatherings where she knows no one.
The conversation turned awkward after he mentioned his recent breakup.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confident - showing certainty and self-assurance
  • synonym: shy - nervous or timid in the company of others
