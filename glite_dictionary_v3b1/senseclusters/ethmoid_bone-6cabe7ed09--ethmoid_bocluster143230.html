ethmoid bone

noun
bone in cranium, part of eye sockets and nasal cavity
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθˌmɔɪd boʊn / (AmE), / ˈɛθ.mɔɪd bəʊn / (BrE)
a light, spongy bone at the base of the cranium, forming part of the eye sockets and the nasal cavity

Examples

The ethmoid bone base
During the operation, the surgeon carefully avoided damaging the ethmoid bone.
Fracture of the ethmoid bone may cause leakage of cerebrospinal fluid.
The ethmoid bone helps support the structure of the nasal cavity.
