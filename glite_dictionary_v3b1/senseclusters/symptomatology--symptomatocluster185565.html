symptomatology

noun
set of symptoms
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪmptəməˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌsɪmptəməˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the set or collection of symptoms typical of a particular disease or medical condition

Examples

Symptomatology of flu
The doctor carefully reviewed the patient's symptomatology before making a diagnosis.
Understanding the symptomatology of the illness helped the team develop an effective treatment plan.
Researchers are studying the symptomatology of the new virus to identify patterns in its progression.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diagnosis - the identification of a disease from its symptoms
  • synonym: symptoms - signs or indications of a disease or condition
