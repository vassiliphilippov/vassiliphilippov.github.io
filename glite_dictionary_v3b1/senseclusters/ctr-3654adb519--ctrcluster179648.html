CTR

sym
click-through rate
Pronunciation: / si ti ɑr / (AmE), / siː tiː ɑː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for click-through rate, the percentage of people who click on a link or advertisement

Examples

Ad CTR
The marketing team was pleased with the high CTR of their new online advertisement.
If your CTR is low, you may need to improve your ad design.
Advertisers often track CTR to measure the effectiveness of their campaigns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: click-through rate - the percentage of users who click on a specific link or ad
  • antonym: impression - an instance of an ad being displayed to a user
