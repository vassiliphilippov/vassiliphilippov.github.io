soft-coated wheaten terrier

noun
a medium-sized Irish terrier breed with a soft wheaten coat
Pronunciation: / sɑft ˈkoʊtɪd ˈwiːtn ˈtɛriər / (AmE), / sɒft ˈkəʊtɪd ˈwiːtn ˈtɛriə / (BrE)
a breed of medium-sized terrier from Ireland, known for its abundant, soft, wheaten-colored coat and very hairy head and muzzle

Examples

A soft-coated wheaten terrier dog
After years of research, she decided to adopt a soft-coated wheaten terrier from a rescue center.
Grooming a soft-coated wheaten terrier requires regular brushing to prevent matting.
The soft-coated wheaten terrier is known for its friendly temperament and silky coat.
