constative

adjective
factual statement
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnstətɪv / (AmE), / ˈkɒnstətɪv / (BrE)
describing a statement that conveys information and can be judged as true or false

Examples

Constative statement
The sentence 'Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius' is a constative statement.
In linguistics, a constative utterance can be evaluated as true or false.
He distinguished between constative and performative sentences in his lecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: declarative - making a statement or declaration
  • antonym: performative - describing a statement that performs an action rather than stating a fact
