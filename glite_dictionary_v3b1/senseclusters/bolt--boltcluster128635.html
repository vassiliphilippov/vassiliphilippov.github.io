bolt

noun
sudden abandonment of a group
Pronunciation: / boʊlt / (AmE), / bəʊlt / (BrE)
a sudden and unexpected act of leaving or abandoning a group or organization, especially a political party

Examples

A political bolt
His bolt left the leadership scrambling to maintain stability.
The political crisis deepened after the dramatic bolt by several key members.
The senator's bolt from the party surprised many of her colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defection - the act of abandoning a cause or party for another
