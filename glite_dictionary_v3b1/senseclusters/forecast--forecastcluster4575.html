Synonyms

  • prediction
    2.6 31/m
    prediction is a general statement about what will happen based on belief or evidence, while forecast often uses formal data for weather or business.
  • projection
    4.4 13/m
    projection is an estimate based on past and present data used for planning, while forecast focuses on likely outcomes like weather or market trends.
  • prognostication
    9.3 0.59/m
    prognostication is often a speculative or superstitious prediction based on signs, whereas forecast relies on data and analysis.
