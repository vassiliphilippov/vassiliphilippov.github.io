kart

noun
small racing vehicle
3.5
Pronunciation: / kɑrt / (AmE), / kɑːt / (BrE)
a small, low vehicle, usually with a simple frame and often an engine, used for racing or recreation on special tracks

Examples

A kart race
He spent the afternoon fixing his kart before the big race.
Children lined up to take turns driving the electric kart around the indoor track.
Professional drivers often start their careers in kart racing.
He raced his kart around the track, trying to beat his best time.
Children lined up to take turns driving the electric karts at the amusement park.
Wearing a helmet is required when operating a kart for safety reasons.
