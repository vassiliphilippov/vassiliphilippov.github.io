at loose ends

adjective
feeling uncertain or directionless
Pronunciation: / ət lus ɛndz / (AmE), / ət luːs endz / (BrE)
feeling uncertain, directionless, or without anything definite to do

Examples

To be at loose ends
Many retirees initially find themselves at loose ends before adjusting to their new routine.
She often feels at loose ends after finishing a big project.
With his friends out of town, Jake was at loose ends all weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: directionless - without direction or purpose
  • antonym: settled - having found stability or purpose
