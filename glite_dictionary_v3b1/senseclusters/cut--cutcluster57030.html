cut

noun
level of quality or rank
Pronunciation: / kʌt / (AmE), / kʌt / (BrE)
a level or degree of quality or rank, especially as used in comparisons

Examples

A cut above
His performance was a cut above what we expected from a newcomer.
Jess is a cut above the other candidates for the job.
These luxury hotels are a cut above the competition in both comfort and service.

  • synonym: notch - a degree or step in a scale
