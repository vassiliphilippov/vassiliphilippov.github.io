psychogalvanic response

noun
change in skin's electrical properties due to stress or anxiety
8.6
Pronunciation: /saɪ.kə.gæl.ˈvæn.ɪk rɪ.ˈspɑːns/ (AmE), /saɪ.kə.gæl.ˈvæn.ɪk rɪ.ˈspɒns/ (BrE)
a measurable change in the skin's electrical properties, such as resistance or current, in response to psychological stress or anxiety

Examples
