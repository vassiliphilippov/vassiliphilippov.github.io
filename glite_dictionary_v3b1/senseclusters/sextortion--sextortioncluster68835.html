sextortion

noun
sexual extortion via blackmail
Pronunciation: / sɛkˈstɔr.ʃən / (AmE), / sɛkˈstɔː.ʃən / (BrE)
the practice of obtaining sexual favors, money, or other benefits from someone by threatening to reveal sexually explicit images or information about them

Examples

Sextortion cases
Law enforcement agencies are working to combat sextortion cases around the world.
Many teenagers have become sextortion victims after trusting strangers online.
She warned her friends about the risk of sextortion after her own experience.
