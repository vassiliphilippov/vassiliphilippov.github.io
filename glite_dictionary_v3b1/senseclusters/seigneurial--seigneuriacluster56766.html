seigneurial

adjective
of feudal lords
Pronunciation: / seɪnˈjʊəriəl / (AmE), / seɪnˈjʊəriəl / (BrE)
relating to a feudal lord or the system of lords and their lands

Examples

Seigneurial system
The seigneurial system shaped the rural landscape of New France for centuries.
Many old estates still display seigneurial architecture and traditions.
After the revolution, seigneurial privileges were gradually eliminated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feudal - relating to the social system of feudalism
  • synonym: lordly - characteristic of a lord; grand or noble
