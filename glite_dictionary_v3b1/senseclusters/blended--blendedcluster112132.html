blended

adjective
mixed so parts are indistinguishable
Pronunciation: / ˈblɛndɪd / (AmE), / ˈblɛndɪd / (BrE)
combined or mixed so thoroughly that the constituent parts are indistinguishable.

Examples

A blended mixture
Her voice had a blended quality, merging effortlessly with the other singers in the choir.
The chef served a blended soup where no single vegetable was identifiable.
They use a blended fabric made from cotton and polyester for added comfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mixed - combined or put together to form a single substance or mass
