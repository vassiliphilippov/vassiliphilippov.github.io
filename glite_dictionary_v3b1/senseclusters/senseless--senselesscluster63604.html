senseless

adjective
lacking sense or good judgment
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsləs / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsləs /; / ˈsɛnslɪs / (BrE)
lacking sense, reason, or purpose; foolish, unreasonable, or pointless
