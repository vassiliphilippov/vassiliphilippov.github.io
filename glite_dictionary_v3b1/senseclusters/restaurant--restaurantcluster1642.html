Synonyms

  • eatery
    3.1 1.77/m
    eatery is an informal word for a place to eat, while restaurant often suggests a sit-down place with table service.
  • eating place
    1.0 0.17/m
    eating place is a broad term for any spot where people eat, while restaurant means a paid business that prepares and serves meals.
  • eating house
    1.6 0.03/m
    eating house refers to an older or less common building to eat in, while restaurant is the modern common word for such businesses.
