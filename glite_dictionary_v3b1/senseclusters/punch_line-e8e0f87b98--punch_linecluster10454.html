punch line

noun
ending of a joke or story with an effect
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌntʃ ˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈpʌntʃ ˌlaɪn / (BrE)
the final phrase or sentence of a joke, story, or anecdote that delivers the intended humorous or dramatic effect

Examples

The punch line of a joke
He paused for effect before delivering the punch line of his joke.
If you forget the punch line, the whole joke falls flat.
The comedian's punch line had everyone in the audience laughing out loud.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tagline - a memorable phrase or sentence associated with a product or campaign
