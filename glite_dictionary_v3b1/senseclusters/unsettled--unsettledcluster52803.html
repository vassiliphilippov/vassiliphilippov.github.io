unsettled

adjective
(of weather) likely to change; unstable
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛt.əld / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛt.əld / (BrE)
(of weather or conditions) likely to change; not stable or firmly fixed.

Examples

Unsettled weather
Autumn often brings unsettled conditions with frequent showers and gusty winds.
Her plans for the summer remain unsettled due to changing circumstances.
The forecast says the unsettled weather will last all week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: changeable - liable to change; variable
  • antonym: stable - not likely to change; steady
