cytogenetics

noun
study of chromosomes and heredity
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪtəˌdʒəˈnɛtɪks / (AmE), / ˌsaɪtəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪks / (BrE)
The branch of biology that studies the structure and function of chromosomes and their role in heredity.

Examples

Branch of cytogenetics
Advances in cytogenetics have improved the diagnosis of genetic disorders.
She decided to specialize in cytogenetics after taking an advanced biology course.
The cytogenetics lab analyzes patients' chromosomes for abnormalities.
