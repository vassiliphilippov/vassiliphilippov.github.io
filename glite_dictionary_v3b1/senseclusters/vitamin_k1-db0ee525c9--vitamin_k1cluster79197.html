vitamin K1

noun
a form of vitamin K important for blood clotting
3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈvaɪ.tə.mɪn keɪ wʌn/ /; / /ˈvɪt.ə.mɪn keɪ wʌn/ (AmE), /ˈvɪt.ə.mɪn keɪ wʌn/ /; / /ˈvaɪ.tə.mɪn keɪ wʌn/ (BrE)
