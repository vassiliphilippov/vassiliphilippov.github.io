blustering

adjective
blowing violently in gusts (wind/weather)
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌstərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblʌstərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of wind or weather) blowing violently and in abrupt bursts.

Examples

Blustering wind
The blustering winds rattled the windows all night long.
The weather forecast predicted a blustering afternoon, so sailing was cancelled.
We struggled to walk against the blustering gusts on the cliffs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blustery - (of wind or weather) characterized by strong, gusty winds
