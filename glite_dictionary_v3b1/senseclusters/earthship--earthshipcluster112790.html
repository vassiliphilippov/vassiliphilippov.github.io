earthship

noun
spacecraft from Earth
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈɜːθˌʃɪp / (BrE)
a spacecraft originating from Earth or designed to carry people from Earth, especially in science fiction

Examples

Earthship to Mars
The aging earthship prepared for its long journey to the distant colony.
In the novel, humanity's last hope rested on a single earthship leaving the solar system.
The crew spent months training before boarding the massive earthship bound for Mars.
