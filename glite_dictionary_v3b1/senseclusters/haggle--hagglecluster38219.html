haggle

noun
intense bargaining or argument
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhæɡəl / (AmE), / ˈhæɡəl / (BrE)
an intense argument or negotiation, especially about the price or terms of a purchase or agreement

Examples

A long haggle
After much haggle, both sides agreed on the contract terms.
The negotiations turned into a prolonged haggle over the final price.
They had a fierce haggle with the vendor at the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bargain - an agreement between two or more parties as to what each will do for the other
  • degree higher: quarrel - an angry argument or disagreement
