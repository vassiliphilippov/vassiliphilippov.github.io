prompt

noun
instruction for an AI model
3.8
Pronunciation: / prɑmpt / (AmE), / prɒmpt / (BrE)
A textual instruction, example, or cue given to an AI or language model to elicit a specific response, behavior, or task output.

Examples

An AI prompt
The researcher wrote a precise prompt asking the model to summarize the report in three sentences.
During the workshop, participants tested different prompts to see which produced the clearest AI responses.
Before publishing, she refined the prompt to reduce hallucinations and improve factual accuracy.

  • synonym: instruction - a direction or order telling someone or something what to do
