ace

noun
playing card with a single pip
Pronunciation: / eɪs / (AmE), / eɪs / (BrE)
a playing card in a standard deck marked with a single pip, typically considered the highest or lowest in rank

Examples

An ace of spades
An ace of spades is often considered a lucky card.
He collected all four aces to win the game.
She drew an ace from the deck to start the round.
