Synonyms

  • humanoid

  • anthropoid
    7.9 0.04/m

  • anthropomorphous
    9.9 0.01/m
    anthropomorphous gives animals or objects a human form or look, while humanlike simply means resembling human characteristics.
  • anthropomorphic
    8.4 1.04/m

  • humaniform
    9.0 0.00/m
