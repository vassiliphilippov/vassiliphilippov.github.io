elemental

adjective
relating to a pure chemical element
5.1 5.4
Pronunciation: /el.ə.ˈmen.təl/ /; / /el.ɪ.ˈmen.təl/ (AmE), /el.ə.ˈmen.təl/ /; / /el.ɪ.ˈmen.təl/ (BrE)
Relating to or being a chemical element in its pure form, rather than combined with others.

Examples

Elemental oxygen
Chemists isolated elemental oxygen from air in the eighteenth century.
Pure elemental mercury is liquid at room temperature.
The mineral contains elemental iron rather than an iron compound.
