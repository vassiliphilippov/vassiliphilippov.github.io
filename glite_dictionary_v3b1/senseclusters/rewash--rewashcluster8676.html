rewash

verb
to wash again
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈwɒʃ / (BrE)
to wash something again, usually because it was not cleaned properly the first time

The chef decided to rewash the vegetables to make sure they were clean.
