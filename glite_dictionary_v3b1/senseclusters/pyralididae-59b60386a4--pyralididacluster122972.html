Pyralididae

noun
family of moths including pests and stored product moths
Pronunciation: / pɪˌræləˈdɪdiː /; / pɪˌrælɪˈdɪdi / (AmE), / pɪˌralɪˈdɪːdiː / (BrE)
a family of moths that includes bee moths, corn borers, and flour moths, many of which are pests of crops and stored products
