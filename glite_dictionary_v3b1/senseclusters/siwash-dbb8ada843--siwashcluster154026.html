Siwash

noun
offensive slur
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪwɒʃ / (BrE)
an offensive slur for a Native American or First Nations person; strongly discouraged and considered derogatory

Examples

A Siwash slur
The word Siwash is now widely recognized as an offensive and outdated term.
You should never refer to anyone as a Siwash because it is deeply disrespectful.
Many organizations have removed the term Siwash from their names due to its offensive nature.

Related Terms

  • antonym: First Nations - indigenous peoples in Canada who are neither Inuit nor Métis
  • antonym: Native American - a member of the indigenous peoples of the United States
