response

noun
manner of reacting or receiving
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
the way in which someone reacts to a situation, event, or another person's action, especially as a form of greeting or reception

Examples

Hostile response to remarks
He was disappointed by the hostile response to his honest remarks.
Her proposal received a surprisingly enthusiastic response from the committee.
The celebrity did not expect the lukewarm response from fans at the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reaction - an action performed or a feeling experienced in response to a situation or event
  • synonym: reception - the way in which people react to or receive someone or something
