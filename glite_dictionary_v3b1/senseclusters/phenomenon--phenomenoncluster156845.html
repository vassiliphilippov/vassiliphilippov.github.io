phenomenon

noun
remarkable or exceptional occurrence
1.0
Pronunciation: / fəˈnɑːməˌnɑn / (AmE), / fəˈnɒmɪnən / (BrE)
a remarkable or exceptional person, thing, or event; something impressive or outstanding that attracts attention

Examples

A global phenomenon
Her rise to fame was a social media phenomenon that captured worldwide attention.
The rapid growth of the company became a business phenomenon studied by many economists.
The scientist became a phenomenon for her groundbreaking discoveries in genetics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marvel - something that causes wonder or astonishment
  • synonym: miracle - an extraordinary and astonishing happening
  • antonym: ordinary - not remarkable, lacking special features
