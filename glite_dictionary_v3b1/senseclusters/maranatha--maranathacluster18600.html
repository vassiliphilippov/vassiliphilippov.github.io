maranatha

x
Christian prayer phrase
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmærəˈnɑːθə / (AmE), / ˌmærəˈnɑːθə / (BrE)
an Aramaic expression meaning 'O Lord, come', used as a prayer or invocation in Christian worship

Examples

Pray maranatha
At the end of the prayer, the congregation softly whispered maranatha together.
The pastor explained the meaning of maranatha during the Sunday service.
Many early Christians would greet each other with the word maranatha as a sign of hope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amen - a word used at the end of a prayer meaning 'so be it'
