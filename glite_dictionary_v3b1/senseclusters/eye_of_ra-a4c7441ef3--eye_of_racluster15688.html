Eye of Ra

noun
Egyptian mythological goddess, Ra's destructive aspect
9.9

In ancient Egyptian mythology, a goddess personifying the sun's destructive power, often depicted as a lioness and regarded as an extension of the sun god Ra.

Legends describe the Eye of Ra as both a protector and destroyer among the gods.
