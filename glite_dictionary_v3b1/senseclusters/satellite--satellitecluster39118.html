satellite

adjective
dependent on a central power
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsæt̬.əl.aɪt / (AmE), / ˈsæt.əl.aɪt / (BrE)
(of a town, community, or country) dependent on and dominated by a more powerful central authority.

Examples

Satellite town
Many people commute from satellite towns to work in the city center.
The Soviet Union had several satellite states in Eastern Europe during the Cold War.
Urban planners often study the growth of satellite communities around major metropolitan areas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: autonomous - independent; self-governing
  • synonym: dependent - relying on or controlled by something else
