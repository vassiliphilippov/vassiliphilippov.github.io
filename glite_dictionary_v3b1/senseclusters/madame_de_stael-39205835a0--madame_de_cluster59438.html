Madame de Stael

proper noun
French Romantic writer and intellectual
9.6
Pronunciation: / mæˌdæm də stɑːˈɛl / (AmE), / mæˌdæm də stɑːˈɛl / (BrE)
(1766–1817) a French writer and intellectual known for her influence on Romanticism and her works on literature and politics.

Examples

Madame de Stael writings
In her writings, Madame de Stael advocated for political liberty and the importance of individual expression.
Many scholars regard Madame de Stael as a pioneering figure in the development of European Romanticism.
The literary salons hosted by Madame de Stael attracted leading intellectuals of the era.
