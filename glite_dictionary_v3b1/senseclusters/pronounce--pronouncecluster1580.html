pronounce

verb
formally declare a judgment
1.9
Pronunciation: / prəˈnaʊns / (AmE), / prəˈnaʊns / (BrE)
to declare or state a formal judgment or opinion about someone or something, especially officially or authoritatively

Examples

To pronounce someone guilty
He was pronounced guilty by the court after a lengthy trial.
The committee will pronounce judgment on the allegations next week.
The doctor refused to pronounce on the patient's chances of recovery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: declare - to make known formally or officially
  • synonym: judge - to form an opinion or conclusion about
