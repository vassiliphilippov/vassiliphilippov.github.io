I.W.W.

proper noun
former radical U.S. labor union (Industrial Workers of the World)
9.9
Pronunciation: /aɪ ˈdʌ.bəl.juː ˈdʌ.bəl.juː/ (AmE), /aɪ ˈdʌ.bəl.juː ˈdʌ.bəl.juː/ (BrE)

Examples
