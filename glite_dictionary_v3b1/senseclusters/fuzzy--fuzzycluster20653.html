fuzzy

adjective
indistinct or blurry in outline
5.1 3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌzi / (AmE), / ˈfʌzi / (BrE)
not clear or sharp; indistinct or confused (can refer to images, memories, ideas, or information)

Examples
