particle

noun
a tiny piece or amount
1.1 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑːr.tɪ.kəl/ /; / /ˈpɑːr.tə.kəl/ (AmE), /ˈpɑː.tɪ.kəl/ /; / /ˈpɑː.tə.kəl/ (BrE)
a very small piece or amount of something; a tiny part, especially in science or physics

Examples
