slasher

noun
horror film with killer
Pronunciation: / ˈslæʃər / (AmE), / ˈslæʃə / (BrE)
a horror film that features a violent killer who attacks and murders victims, often with bladed weapons

Examples

A slasher movie
The new slasher terrified audiences with its intense scenes and shocking plot twists.
He prefers a classic slasher over other types of horror movies.
Many critics argue that the slasher genre became popular in the 1980s.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horror film - a movie designed to frighten or disturb viewers
  • degree lower: thriller - a suspenseful film with excitement and tension
