puff up

verb
make larger or swollen; inflate
1.8 3.6
Pronunciation: /pʌf ʌp/ /; / /pʌf əp/ (AmE), /pʌf ʌp/ /; / /pʌf əp/ (BrE)
to make something larger, inflated, or swollen, or to become swollen or expanded, often due to air, liquid, or emotion

Examples

To puff up a pillow
She used a pump to puff up the air mattress for the guests.
Some companies puff up their sales figures to attract investors.
The frog will puff up its body to appear larger when threatened.
He tends to puff up his chest when he's feeling proud.
Her face began to puff up after she got stung by a bee.
The bread will puff up in the oven as it bakes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deflate - to let air or gas out of something, making it smaller
  • synonym: inflate - to fill something with air or gas so that it expands
  • synonym: swell up - to become larger and rounder, usually due to fluid or air
