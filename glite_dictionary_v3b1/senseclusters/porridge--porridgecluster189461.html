porridge

noun
soft food made from boiled grains or legumes
3.4 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpɔːr.ɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈpɑːr.ɪdʒ/ (AmE), /ˈpɒr.ɪdʒ/ (BrE)
