ketotic

adjective
relating to ketosis
8.4
Pronunciation: / kiˈtoʊtɪk / (AmE), / kiˈtəʊtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by ketosis, a metabolic state with increased ketone bodies in the body

Examples

A ketotic state
After several days of fasting, the patient entered a ketotic state.
A ketotic breath often has a distinctive fruity odor due to high ketone levels.
Doctors monitor for ketotic symptoms in people following strict low-carbohydrate diets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ketosis - a metabolic state with elevated levels of ketone bodies
