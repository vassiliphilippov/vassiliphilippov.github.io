noseband

noun
strap on animal's bridle over nose
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊzˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈnəʊzˌbænd / (BrE)
A strap on a bridle that passes over the nose of a horse or other animal to help control it.

Examples

A noseband on a horse
A well-fitted noseband can improve communication between the rider and the horse.
She replaced the old leather noseband before the next competition.
The horse's noseband was adjusted to ensure a snug but comfortable fit.
