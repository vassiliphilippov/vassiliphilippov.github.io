Copernicanism

noun
radical shift in thinking
9.9
Pronunciation: /kɑː.pər.ˈnɪk.ən.ɪz.əm/ /; / /kɑː.pər.ˈnɪk.ə.nɪz.əm/ (AmE), /kɒp.ə.ˈnɪk.ən.ɪz.əm/ /; / /kɒp.ə.ˈnɪk.ə.nɪz.əm/ (BrE)
a fundamental shift in perspective or a radical change in thinking, especially one that challenges established views
