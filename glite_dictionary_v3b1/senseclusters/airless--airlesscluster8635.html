airless

adjective
without air
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrləs / (AmE), / ˈeələs / (BrE)
lacking air or not containing any air, often making breathing difficult or impossible

Examples

Airless space
The astronauts trained in an airless chamber to simulate space.
After hours in the airless basement, they were desperate for fresh air.
The moon is an airless world, so astronauts must wear special suits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stuffy - unpleasantly warm and lacking fresh air
  • antonym: ventilated - supplied with fresh air
