Greenwich Mean Time

noun
global time standard at Greenwich meridian
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɛnɪtʃ min taɪm / (AmE), / ˈɡrɛnɪtʃ miːn taɪm / (BrE)
the mean solar time at the Prime Meridian in Greenwich, England, used as a global time standard

Examples

Greenwich Mean Time standard
All timestamps are recorded in Greenwich Mean Time to avoid confusion.
International telecasts are often scheduled according to Greenwich Mean Time.
The conference will begin at 10:00 a.m. Greenwich Mean Time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Coordinated Universal Time - the primary world time standard, often replacing GMT
