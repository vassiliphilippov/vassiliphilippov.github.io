twenty-two pistol

noun
.22-caliber pistol
Pronunciation: / ˌtwɛnti ˈtu ˈpɪstl̩ / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti ˈtjuː ˈpɪstl̩ / (BrE)
A pistol that uses .22-caliber ammunition, known for its low recoil and common use in target shooting or training.

The twenty-two pistol is often recommended for beginner shooters because of its light recoil.
