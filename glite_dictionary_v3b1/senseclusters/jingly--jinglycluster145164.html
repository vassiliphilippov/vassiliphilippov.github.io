jingly

adjective
having a ringing sound like small bells
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪŋli / (BrE)
having a series of high-pitched ringing sounds, like those made by small bells

Examples

A jingly sound
As the dog ran, its collar made a jingly sound throughout the house.
The jingly bells on her costume made everyone turn to watch her dance.
We enjoyed the jingly melody of the music box as we fell asleep.

Related Terms
