statically

adverb
by static electricity
Pronunciation: / ˈstætɪkli / (AmE), / ˈstætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or is caused by static electricity, making objects stick or produce sparks.

Examples

Statically charged hair
The plastic wrap clung statically to the bowl after being removed from the package.
Her hair stood up statically after she took off her wool hat.
The confetti stuck statically to the television screen because of the dry air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrically - in a way that involves electricity
