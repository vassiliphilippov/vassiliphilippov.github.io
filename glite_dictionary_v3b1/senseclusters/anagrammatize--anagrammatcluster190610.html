anagrammatize

verb
form or interpret anagrams
Pronunciation: / əˈnæɡrəˌmætaɪz /; / əˈnæɡrəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / əˈnæɡ.rə.mə.taɪz /; / əˈnaɡ.rə.mə.taɪz / (BrE)
To arrange the letters of a word or phrase so as to form an anagram, or to read them out of order to discover a hidden meaning.

He loves to anagrammatise famous names for fun.
Some cryptic crossword clues require you to anagrammatise certain words to find the solution.
The puzzle asked us to anagrammatise the letters in 'listen' to uncover a hidden word.
