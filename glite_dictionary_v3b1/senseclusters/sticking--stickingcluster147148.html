sticking

adjective
extending out beyond a surface
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
extending out above or beyond a surface or boundary; protruding.

Examples

Sticking out nails
A few nails were sticking out from the wooden board.
She noticed a slip of paper sticking out of his pocket.
The dog's ears were sticking up above the tall grass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protruding - extending outward beyond the surrounding surface
  • antonym: receding - moving or sloping backward; not sticking out
