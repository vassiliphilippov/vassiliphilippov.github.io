Toontown theme

noun
cartoon-inspired decoration style
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːntaʊn θiːm / (AmE), / ˈtuːntaʊn θiːm / (BrE)
A style of decoration that is inspired by cartoons, featuring bright colors, whimsical shapes, and playful elements resembling those from animated worlds.

Examples

A Toontown theme party
She chose a Toontown theme for her son's birthday, complete with balloon animals and cartoon murals.
The children's party had a Toontown theme with colorful characters on every wall.
Their playroom was decorated in a lively Toontown theme reminiscent of classic animated shows.
