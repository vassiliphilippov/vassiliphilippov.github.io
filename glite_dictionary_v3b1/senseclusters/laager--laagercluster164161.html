laager

noun
defensive camp of circled wagons
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːɡər / (AmE), / ˈlɑːɡə / (BrE)
A camp fortified by arranging wagons in a circle, used especially by settlers in Southern Africa for defense.

Examples

A laager camp
The museum displays a reconstructed laager to show visitors how people lived in the past.
The pioneers formed a laager each night to protect themselves against attack.
We read about the settlers' journey and how they would defend their laager.
