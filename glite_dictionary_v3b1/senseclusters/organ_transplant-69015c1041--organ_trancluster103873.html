organ transplant

noun
surgical procedure to transfer an organ
1.5 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrɡən ˈtrænˌsplænt / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡən ˈtrænsplɑːnt / (BrE)
a medical operation in which an organ is moved from a donor to a recipient to replace a damaged or missing organ

Examples

An organ transplant operation
After the accident, she required an organ transplant to survive.
The hospital specializes in organ transplant procedures, particularly kidney and liver transplants.
Waiting lists for an organ transplant can often be very long.
