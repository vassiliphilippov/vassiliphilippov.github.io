Examples

To hemorrhage badly
After the accident, he began to hemorrhage from a deep wound in his leg.
Doctors rushed to control the artery before she could hemorrhage further.
Without immediate surgery, the patient might hemorrhage to death internally.
The doctor worked quickly to stop the patient from haemorrhaging after the surgery.
If you haemorrhage internally, it can be very dangerous without immediate treatment.
The injured athlete began to haemorrhage on the field before the medics arrived.
