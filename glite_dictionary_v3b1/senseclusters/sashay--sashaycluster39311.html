sashay

noun
square dance figure involving sideways steps
Pronunciation: / sæˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / sæˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
A square dance figure in which partners circle each other, taking sideways steps.

Examples

Square dance sashay
During the final sashay, the crowd cheered for their smooth moves.
Each couple performed a sashay as the caller gave instructions.
The dancers moved gracefully through the sashay in perfect unison.
