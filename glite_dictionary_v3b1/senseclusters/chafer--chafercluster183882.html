chafer

noun
food-warming container
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪfər / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪfə / (BrE)
a metal container used to keep cooked food warm, especially at buffets or catered events

Examples

Buffet chafer
The caterers arranged the hot dishes in a chafer to keep them warm throughout the evening.
Please refill the water in the chafer so the food stays at the right temperature.
At the breakfast buffet, scrambled eggs were served from a stainless steel chafer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chafing dish - a dish used to keep food warm over a heat source
  • degree lower: serving tray - a flat tray used to serve or display food
