coronal

adjective
of a crown or outer layer
Pronunciation: / kəˈroʊnəl / (AmE), / kəˈrəʊnəl / (BrE)
relating to a crown, the head, or the outer layer of the sun or another celestial body

Examples

Coronal plane of the head
The surgeon made an incision along the coronal plane of the skull.
Scientists observed a massive coronal mass ejection from the sun last week.
The dentist explained the importance of the coronal part of the tooth in oral health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cranial - relating to the skull or cranium
  • synonym: solar - relating to the sun
