sod

noun
offensive term for a male engaging in anal sex
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɑd / (AmE), / sɒd / (BrE)
(offensive, vulgar, slang) A person, especially a man, who engages in anal intercourse, especially a male who engages in anal sex with another man.

Examples

Called a sod (insult)
He was called a sod by his peers, though the label was meant to insult.
Some old laws targeted so-called sods with harsh punishments.
Using the word sod today is considered highly offensive and inappropriate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sodomite - a person who practices anal or oral sex, especially a man who has anal sex with another man
