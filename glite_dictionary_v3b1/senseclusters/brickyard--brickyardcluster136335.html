brickyard

noun
place for making and selling bricks
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪkˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbrɪkˌjɑːd / (BrE)
a place where bricks are manufactured and sold

Examples

The brickyard factory
She found work at the local brickyard after finishing school.
The old brickyard has been converted into a community park.
Trucks arrived daily to load fresh bricks from the bustling brickyard.
