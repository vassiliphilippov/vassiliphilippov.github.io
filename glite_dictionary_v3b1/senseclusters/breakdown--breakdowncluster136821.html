breakdown

noun
failure of normal operation
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkdaʊn / (BrE)
an instance of something stopping to function or operate, especially a machine or system

Examples

Car breakdown
A sudden breakdown in the computer system delayed the project.
Engineers worked through the night to repair the electrical breakdown.
The factory was forced to shut down due to a breakdown in the main generator.

  • synonym: failure - a lack of success in functioning or operation
