hoorah

interjection
expression of joy
7.9
Pronunciation: / hʊˈrɑ / (AmE), / hʊˈrɑː / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express joy, approval, or encouragement

Examples

Hoorah for the team
When the team scored the winning goal, the crowd shouted hoorah in celebration.
Everyone gave a loud hoorah as the parade passed by.
She raised her glass and exclaimed hoorah for their success.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boo - an exclamation expressing disapproval or contempt
  • synonym: hooray - an exclamation used to show excitement or approval
  • synonym: hurrah - an exclamation of joy or approval
