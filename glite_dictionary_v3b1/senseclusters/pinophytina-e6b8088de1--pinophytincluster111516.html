Pinophytina

noun
taxonomic group of conifers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪnoʊfaɪˈtaɪnə /; / ˌpaɪ.nəˈfaɪ.tɪ.nə / (AmE), / ˌpɪnəʊfaɪˈtiːnə /; / ˌpɪn.əˈfaɪ.tɪ.nə / (BrE)
a scientific group of plants that includes most conifers (such as pines, firs, and spruces), sometimes considered a class or a subdivision in plant classification
