calamus oil

noun
aromatic oil from calamus root
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkæləˌməs ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈkæləməs ɔɪl / (BrE)
an aromatic oil extracted from the root of the calamus plant, used in perfumes and sometimes considered carcinogenic
