Amazon

proper noun
the second longest river in the world, in South America
Pronunciation: / ˈæm.əˌzɑn / (AmE), / ˈam.ə.zən / (BrE)
The second longest river in the world, flowing from the Andes in western South America eastward into the South Atlantic Ocean.
