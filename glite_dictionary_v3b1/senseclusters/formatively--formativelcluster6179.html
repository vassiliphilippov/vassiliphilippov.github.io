formatively

adverb
with ongoing feedback
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrmətɪvli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmətɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that aims to improve learning or performance by providing feedback during the process.

Examples

Formatively assess students
Teachers often assess students formatively to help them improve before the final exam.
The manager provided feedback formatively throughout the training session to support employee growth.
By reviewing drafts formatively, the editor helped the writer strengthen the final article.

Related Terms

  • synonym: constructively - in a way that is helpful and intended to improve
  • antonym: summatively - in a way that evaluates learning at the end of a process
