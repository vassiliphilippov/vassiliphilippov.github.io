exhortatory

adjective
strongly encouraging; urging
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɔːr.təˌtɔːr.i / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔː.tə.təri / (BrE)
giving strong encouragement to someone to do something; urging earnestly or strongly

Examples

An exhortatory speech
He adopted an exhortatory tone, urging the audience to take immediate action.
Her exhortatory speech inspired the volunteers to work even harder for the cause.
The coach's exhortatory remarks lifted the team's spirits before the final match.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discouraging - causing someone to lose confidence or enthusiasm
  • synonym: encouraging - giving support or confidence, inspiring someone to do something
