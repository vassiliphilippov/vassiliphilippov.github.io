international terrorism

noun
terrorism by foreigners in another country
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚˈnæʃənəl ˈtɛrəˌrɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl ˈtɛrərɪzəm / (BrE)
The use of violence or threats by individuals or groups to instill fear or achieve political aims, where the acts are committed in a country by persons who are not its natives.

Examples

International terrorism threat
International terrorism poses a significant threat to global security and stability.
Many countries collaborate on intelligence sharing to prevent acts of international terrorism.
The government introduced new policies to combat international terrorism after several attacks abroad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: terrorism - the unlawful use of violence and intimidation, especially against civilians
