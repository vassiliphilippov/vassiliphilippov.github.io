limited

adjective
restricted in amount or scope
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtɪd / (BrE)
restricted in amount, size, number, or scope; not large or unlimited

Examples

Limited access
Access to the database is limited to authorized users only.
His ability to help was limited by the resources available.
The company offers a limited selection of products this season.
Our time to visit the museum was limited by the opening hours.
She has only a limited knowledge of French, but she can manage basic conversations.
They released a limited edition of the book to celebrate its anniversary.
Her limited experience made the new job a challenge.
The guests had a limited selection of drinks to choose from.
The offer is available for a limited time only, so act quickly.
