bug out

verb
(of eyes) bulge outward
3.5 6.4
Pronunciation: /bʌg aʊt/ (AmE), /bʌg aʊt/ (BrE)
(of a person's eyes) to bulge or protrude outward, often in surprise or shock.

Examples

His eyes bugged out
His eyes bugged out when he saw the size of the cake.
I was so startled that my eyes seemed to bug out of my head.
If you keep staring at the screen, your eyes will bug out eventually.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pop - (of eyes) to protrude or stick out suddenly
  • antonym: recede - (of eyes, features) move back or become less prominent; the opposite physical direction
