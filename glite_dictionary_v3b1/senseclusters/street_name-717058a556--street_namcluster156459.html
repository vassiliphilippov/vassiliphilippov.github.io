street name

noun
brokerage firm's name holding securities
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstrit ˌneɪm / (AmE), / ˈstriːt ˌneɪm / (BrE)
the name of a brokerage firm in which securities are held on behalf of a customer, rather than in the customer's own name

Examples
