abort

noun
an act of terminating a process or project early
Pronunciation: / əˈbɔrt / (AmE), / əˈbɔːt / (BrE)
an act of stopping a project or process before it is finished, especially deliberately or due to unforeseen problems

Examples

Mission abort
After reviewing the risks, the team initiated an abort to avoid potential disasters.
Engineers discussed the possibility of an abort in case of complications during launch.
The mission had to face an unexpected abort due to technical failure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cancellation - the act of deciding that an organized event will not happen
  • synonym: termination - the act of ending something
