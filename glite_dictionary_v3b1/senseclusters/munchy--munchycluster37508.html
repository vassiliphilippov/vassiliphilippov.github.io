munchy

adjective
good for snacking
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌntʃi / (AmE), / ˈmʌntʃi / (BrE)
describing food that is good for snacking or easy to chew

Examples

Munchy snacks
She brought a bag of munchy chips to share at the picnic.
After school, he always looks for something munchy to eat while doing homework.
The party table was filled with munchy treats like popcorn and pretzels.
