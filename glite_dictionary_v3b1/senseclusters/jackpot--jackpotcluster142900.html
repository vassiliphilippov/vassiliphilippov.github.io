jackpot

noun
large cash prize; outstanding reward
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒæk.pɑːt/ (AmE), /ˈdʒæk.pɒt/ (BrE)
a large cash prize or reward, especially one won in a lottery or gambling game; also, any especially impressive or valued success

Examples

After months of searching for the perfect job, Tom finally hit the career jackpot with an amazing offer.
