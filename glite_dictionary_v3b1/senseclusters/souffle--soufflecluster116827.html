souffle

noun
light, fluffy baked egg-based dish
Pronunciation: / suːˈfleɪ / (AmE), / suːˈfleɪ / (BrE)
A light, fluffy baked dish made with egg yolks, beaten egg whites, and various flavorings such as cheese, vegetables, or fruit.

Examples

A souffle dish
A cheese souffle makes an impressive and delicious appetizer.
She carefully folded the egg whites to keep the souffle light and airy.
The chocolate souffle was the highlight of our meal at the restaurant.
