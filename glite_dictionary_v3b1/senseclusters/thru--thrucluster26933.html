thru

preposition
informal 'through'
Pronunciation: / θru / (AmE), / θru / (BrE)
an informal or nonstandard spelling of 'through', used to indicate movement from one side to the other

Examples

Drive thru the city
We drove thru the city late at night to avoid traffic.
The sign said 'Drive thru open 24 hours' at the fast food restaurant.
She walked thru the park on her way to school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: through - from one side or end to the other
