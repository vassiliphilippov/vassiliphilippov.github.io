bat

noun
club for hitting ball in sports
3.2 1.0
Pronunciation: /bæt/ (AmE), /bæt/ (BrE)
a club or racket used to hit a ball in sports such as baseball, cricket, table tennis, or squash

Examples

A baseball bat
He picked up the bat and prepared to take his turn at the plate.
Please bring your bat to practice tomorrow.

The coach showed me how to hold a cricket bat properly.
