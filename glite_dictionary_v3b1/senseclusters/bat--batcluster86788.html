bat

noun
club for hitting ball in sports
Pronunciation: / bæt / (AmE), / bæt / (BrE)
a club or racket used to hit a ball in sports such as baseball, cricket, table tennis, or squash

Examples

A baseball bat
He picked up the bat and prepared to take his turn at the plate.
Please bring your bat to practice tomorrow.
The cricket player swung his bat with impressive skill.
He gripped the bat tightly as he prepared to serve.
Sarah bought a new bat before joining the squash club.
The players were required to bring their own bat to the squash match.
He gripped the bat firmly before facing the bowler.
She bought a new bat before the start of the cricket season.
The coach showed me how to hold a cricket bat properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bat - a club used in baseball (baseball bat)
  • degree lower: paddle - a small bat with a flat surface, used in table tennis or similar games
  • antonym: racket - equipment with a net used in tennis and similar sports
  • synonym: racquet - a bat with a round or oval frame strung with catgut, used in sports such as tennis and badminton
