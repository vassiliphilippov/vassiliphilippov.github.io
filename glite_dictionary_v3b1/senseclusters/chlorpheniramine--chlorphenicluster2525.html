chlorpheniramine

noun
antihistamine drug
Pronunciation: / klɔːrˌfɛnɪˈræmɪn / (AmE), / klɔːˌfɛnɪˈræmɪn / (BrE)
a medicine used to relieve symptoms of allergies such as sneezing, runny nose, and itching
