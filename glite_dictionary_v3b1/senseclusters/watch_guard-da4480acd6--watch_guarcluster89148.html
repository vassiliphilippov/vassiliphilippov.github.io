watch guard

noun
chain or ribbon for securing a pocket watch
Pronunciation: / wɑtʃ ɡɑrd / (AmE), / wɒtʃ ɡɑːd / (BrE)
a short chain or ribbon for attaching a pocket watch to a man's vest, ensuring it is secure and easily accessible

Examples

A velvet watch guard
A velvet watch guard hung elegantly from the gentleman’s waistcoat.
He wore a vintage vest complete with a silver watch guard clipped to his pocket watch.
The antique shop displayed several ornate watch guards alongside gold pocket watches.
