almendro

noun
hardwood for construction
9.9
Pronunciation: / alˈmɛndroʊ / (AmE), / alˈmɛndrəʊ / (BrE)
the strong, durable hardwood from the almendro tree, used in construction, carving, or furniture

Examples

Almendro wood furniture
The craftsman selected almendro for the table because of its strength and beautiful grain.
Builders in Central America often use almendro for beams due to its durability.
The carved bowl made from almendro has lasted in the family for generations.
