weekender

noun
person taking weekend trips
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːkˌɛndər / (AmE), / ˈwiːkˌɛndə / (BrE)
a person who spends weekends away from home, typically on short vacations or traveling to a holiday home

Examples

A city weekender
Many city dwellers become weekenders, escaping to the countryside every Friday.
She bought a cottage upstate and now joins the other weekenders every summer.
The small coastal town was crowded with weekenders looking for relaxation.

  • synonym: vacationer - a person who is on vacation
