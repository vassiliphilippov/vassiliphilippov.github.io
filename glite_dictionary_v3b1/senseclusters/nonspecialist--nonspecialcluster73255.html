nonspecialist

noun
person without expertise
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈspɛʃəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈspɛʃəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who does not have expert knowledge or training in a particular subject or field

Examples

A nonspecialist reader
The museum offers tours that even a nonspecialist can enjoy and understand.
This article explains the science in terms that are accessible to the nonspecialist.
As a nonspecialist, she appreciated the clear and simple language of the report.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expert - a person with special knowledge or skill in a particular area
  • synonym: layperson - someone who is not an expert or professional in a field
