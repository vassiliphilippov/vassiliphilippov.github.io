revenuer

noun
government agent enforcing tax and bootlegging laws
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛv.əˌnuː.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɛv.ɪˌnjuː.ə / (BrE)
a government agent whose job is to collect taxes or enforce laws against illegal alcohol production or distribution

Examples

The revenuer agent

He disguised his operations to keep the revenuer from shutting him down.
The old distillers hid their whiskey to avoid being caught by a revenuer.
