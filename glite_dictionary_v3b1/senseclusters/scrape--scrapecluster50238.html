scrape

noun
mark or scratch indicating damage
Pronunciation: / skreɪp / (AmE), / skreɪp / (BrE)
a scratch, mark, or small injury caused by rubbing or scraping against something hard or rough

Examples

A scrape on skin
She noticed a fresh scrape on her car door after parking downtown.
The floor showed several scrapes from dragging heavy furniture across it.
The wooden table had a scrape on its surface from the moving boxes.
He fell off his bike and got a painful scrape on his knee.
Nurse cleaned the scrape and put a bandage over it.
The child cried after getting a scrape from tripping on the sidewalk.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cut - an injury where the skin is sliced or split
  • synonym: graze - a slight injury where the skin is rubbed off
  • synonym: scratch - a mark made by scraping or tearing a surface
