consequentiality

noun
importance or significance
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnsɪˌkwɛnʃiˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌkɒnsɪˌkwɛnʃiˈæləti / (BrE)
the quality of having significant results, importance, or consequences

Examples

Consequentiality of a decision
The consequentiality of his decision became clear only after the results were announced.
Many people underestimate the consequentiality of small daily choices in their lives.
The historian emphasized the consequentiality of the treaty in shaping modern Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: importance - the state of being of great value or influence
  • antonym: insignificance - the quality of being unimportant or trivial
  • synonym: significance - the quality of being important or meaningful
