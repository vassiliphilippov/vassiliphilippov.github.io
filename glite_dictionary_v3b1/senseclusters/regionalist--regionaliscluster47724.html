regionalist

noun
localist artist or writer
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdʒənəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈriːdʒənəlɪst / (BrE)
an artist, writer, or supporter who focuses on depicting or celebrating local landscapes, traditions, or rural life, especially as part of the Regionalism movement

Examples

Regionalist painter
The famous regionalist captured the spirit of rural America in his paintings.
As a regionalist, she wrote stories that highlighted the unique customs of her hometown.
Many critics praised the regionalist for his authentic portrayal of local landscapes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cosmopolitan - a person who is at ease in many different cultures or places
  • synonym: regionalism - an artistic or literary movement emphasizing local characteristics
