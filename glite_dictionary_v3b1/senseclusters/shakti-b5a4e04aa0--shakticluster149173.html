Shakti

noun
divine female energy in Hinduism
9.9 0.18/m
Pronunciation: / Àà É…ëÀêkti / (AmE), / Àà Éakti / (BrE)
In Hinduism, the divine female energy or generative force, personified as the wife of Shiva and a benevolent form of Devi.

Examples

Shakti divine energy
In mythology, Shakti is often depicted alongside Shiva as his consort and equal.
Many Hindu devotees worship Shakti as the supreme creative energy in the universe.
The temple is dedicated to Shakti, representing the power and compassion of the divine feminine.
