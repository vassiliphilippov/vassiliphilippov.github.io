effervesce

verb
to bubble, fizz, or foam

(of a liquid) to produce small bubbles of gas and become fizzy or foamy; to show lively or excited behavior.

Examples

To effervesce with bubbles
Mountain streams often effervesce as they surge over rocks and boulders.
The champagne began to effervesce as soon as it was poured into the glass.
When you add baking soda to vinegar, the mixture will effervesce rapidly.
