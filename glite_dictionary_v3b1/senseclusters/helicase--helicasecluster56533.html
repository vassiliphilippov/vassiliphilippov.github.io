helicase

noun
DNA-unwinding enzyme
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlɪˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈhɛlɪˌkeɪz /; / ˈhiːlɪˌkeɪz / (BrE)
an enzyme that unwinds or separates strands of DNA or RNA during cellular processes such as replication or repair

Examples

A helicase enzyme
A helicase is essential for separating DNA strands during cell division.
