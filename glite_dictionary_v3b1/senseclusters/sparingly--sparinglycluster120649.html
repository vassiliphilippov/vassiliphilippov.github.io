sparingly

adverb
with restraint or in small amounts
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛrɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈspɛːrɪŋli / (BrE)
in limited amounts; using or doing something with restraint or moderation

Examples

Use sparingly water
Because of the drought, water was used sparingly throughout the summer.
He spends money sparingly, always careful not to exceed his budget.
Salt should be added sparingly to this recipe to avoid overpowering the flavors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generously - in a generous or liberal manner
  • synonym: moderately - to a moderate or limited degree
