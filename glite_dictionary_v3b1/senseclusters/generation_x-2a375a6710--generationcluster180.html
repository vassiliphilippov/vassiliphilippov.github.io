Generation X

noun
generation born in 1960s–1970s
3.6
Pronunciation: /dʒen.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən eks/ (AmE), /dʒen.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən eks/ (BrE)
the generation of people born roughly between the early 1960s and late 1970s, following the baby boomers

Examples

Generation X people
Authors often discuss how Generation X differs from older and younger generations.
Many Generation X members grew up during times of economic uncertainty.
Technology adoption among Generation X accelerated rapidly in the 1990s.
