Hanging Gardens of Babylon

proper noun
ancient terraced garden in Babylon
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋɪŋ ˈɡɑrdənz əv ˈbæbɪˌlɑn / (AmE), / ˈhæŋɪŋ ˈɡɑːdnz əv ˈbæbɪlən / (BrE)
an ancient terraced garden in Babylon, reputedly built by Nebuchadnezzar II around 600 BC and considered one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.
