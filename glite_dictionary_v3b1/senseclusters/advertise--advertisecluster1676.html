advertise

verb
announce availability on a network
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.vər.taɪz / (AmE), / ˈæd.və.taɪz / (BrE)
(in computing and networking) to broadcast or announce the availability, address, or capabilities of a device, service, or route on a network so that other devices or clients can discover it.

Examples

To advertise a service
The printer advertises its service on the local network using mDNS for easy discovery.
Our IoT camera advertises streams and capabilities so the mobile app can connect automatically.
During startup the router advertises routing information to neighbors to update their forwarding tables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: announce - to make something known or tell people about it
  • synonym: broadcast - to send information widely over a network so many devices can receive it
