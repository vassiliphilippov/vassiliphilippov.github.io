touristy

adjective
frequented by tourists; commercialized
2.9 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊr.ɪsti / (AmE), / ˈtʊə.rɪsti / (BrE)
(of a place) visited by large numbers of tourists, often in a way that suggests it is commercialized or lacking local character.

Examples

A touristy place
Although it can be touristy in the summer, the old town still retains its charm.
The city center is very touristy, with souvenir shops on every corner.
We usually try to avoid the most touristy beaches and find somewhere quieter.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: picturesque - (of a place) visually attractive, especially in a quaint or charming way
  • antonym: unspoiled - (of a place) not affected by tourism or development
