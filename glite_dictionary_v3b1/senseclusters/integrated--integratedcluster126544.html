integrated

adjective
combined into a unified whole
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.təˌɡreɪ.tɪd /; / ˈɪn.tɪˌɡreɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tɪ.ɡreɪ.tɪd /; / ˈɪn.tɪˌɡreɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
combined or unified; having different parts working together as a single whole
