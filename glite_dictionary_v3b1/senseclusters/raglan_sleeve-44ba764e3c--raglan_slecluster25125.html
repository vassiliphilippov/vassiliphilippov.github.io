raglan sleeve

noun
sleeve extending in one piece to neckline
Pronunciation: / ˈræɡlən sliːv / (AmE), / ˈræɡlən sliːv / (BrE)
a sleeve that extends in one piece fully to the collar, leaving a diagonal seam from the underarm to the neckline

Examples

A raglan sleeve shirt
Many baseball shirts feature a raglan sleeve to allow more freedom of movement.
She wore a sweatshirt with a grey raglan sleeve design for extra comfort.
The raglan sleeve gives the jacket a sporty, relaxed fit.
