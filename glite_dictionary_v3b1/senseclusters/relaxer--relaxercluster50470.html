relaxer

noun
hair straightening product
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlæksər / (AmE), / rɪˈlæksə / (BrE)
a chemical product used to straighten curly or textured hair by breaking down its natural structure

Examples

Hair relaxer
She used a relaxer to make her hair straight and smooth for the event.
Many salons offer relaxer treatments for clients with curly or textured hair.
After applying the relaxer, he noticed his hair was much easier to comb.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hair straightener - a product or device used to make hair straight
  • antonym: perm - a chemical treatment to make hair curly or wavy
