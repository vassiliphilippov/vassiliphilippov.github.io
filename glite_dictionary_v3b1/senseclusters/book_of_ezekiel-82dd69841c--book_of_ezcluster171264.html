Book of Ezekiel

noun
Old Testament book of Ezekiel's prophecies
8.9
Pronunciation: / bʊk əv ɪˈziːkiəl / (AmE), / bʊk əv ɪˈziːkɪəl / (BrE)
An Old Testament book containing the prophecies of Ezekiel concerning the downfall and subsequent restoration of Jerusalem and Judah.

Examples

The Book of Ezekiel prophecies
Many scholars have studied the visions described in the Book of Ezekiel.
She quoted a passage from the Book of Ezekiel during her lecture on prophecy.
The Book of Ezekiel contains dramatic imagery and complex symbolism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Book of Isaiah - another Old Testament prophetic book
  • synonym: Ezekiel - the prophet Ezekiel, author of the Book of Ezekiel
  • synonym: Old Testament - the first part of the Christian Bible, which includes the Book of Ezekiel
