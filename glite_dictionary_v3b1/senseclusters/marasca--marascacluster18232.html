marasca

noun
Dalmatian sour cherry for maraschino liqueur
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈrɑːskə /; / məˈræskə / (AmE), / məˈrɑːskə / (BrE)
a variety of sour cherry tree (Prunus cerasus marasca) from the Dalmatian region, valued for its bitter fruit used in making maraschino liqueur
