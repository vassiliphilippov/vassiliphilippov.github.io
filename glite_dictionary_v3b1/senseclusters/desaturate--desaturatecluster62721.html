desaturate

verb
to reduce color
7.1
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈsæ.tʃə.reɪt/ /; / /diː.ˈsæ.tʃə.reɪt/ (AmE), /diː.ˈsæ.tʃə.reɪt/ /; / /dɪ.ˈsæ.tʃə.reɪt/ (BrE)
to reduce or remove the intensity of color in an image or object

Examples

To desaturate an image
If you desaturate the image, it will appear in shades of gray instead of vibrant colors.
Graphic designers often desaturate backgrounds to make text stand out more clearly.
You can desaturate a photo in most editing software with just a few clicks.

Related Terms
