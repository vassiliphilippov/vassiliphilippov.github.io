squirt

noun
sudden spurt of liquid
6.2 1.7
Pronunciation: /skwɜːrt/ (AmE), /skwɜːt/ (BrE)
A sudden, forceful discharge of liquid in a small stream.

Examples

There was a sudden squirt of water from the hose when I turned it on.
With a quick squirt, the juice shot out of the orange and onto the table.
