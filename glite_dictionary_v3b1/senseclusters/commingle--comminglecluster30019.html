Examples

To commingle different things

Synonyms

  • mix
    1.0 32/m
    mix is a common verb for joining different things or substances, while commingle feels more formal or legal in some contexts.
  • combine
    2.7 71/m
    combine means to bring two or more things together simply, while commingle implies blending them so they become mixed or hard to separate.
  • blend
    2.3 20/m
    blend suggests two things mix smoothly into one, while commingle focuses on different items mixing together, sometimes in a formal way.
  • intermingle
    5.2 0.74/m
    intermingle means elements mix so their parts are mixed and hard to tell apart, while commingle is a more general term.
  • intermix
    5.6 0.09/m
    intermix means different substances mix together and become hard to separate, while commingle is used more broadly and formally.
  • mingle
    3.3 4.10/m
    mingle often describes people or things mixing in a casual way, while commingle suggests blending items more fully or formally.
  • meld
    5.3 2.01/m
    meld means two things fuse smoothly into a single new whole, while commingle means simply to mix or combine different elements.
  • merge
    3.2 16/m
    merge describes joining parts into one unit or company, while commingle focuses on mixing different items rather than structured joining.
  • fuse
    3.9 3.48/m
    fuse often implies a strong, permanent joining of materials, while commingle means mixing or blending without that strong bond.
  • admix
    8.8 0.11/m
    admix is a formal or technical term for adding one substance into another, while commingle is more common and general in use.
