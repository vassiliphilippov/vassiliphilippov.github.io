proliferator

noun
one who spreads rapidly
Pronunciation: / prəˈlɪfəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / prəˈlɪfəreɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes something to increase or spread rapidly

Examples

A proliferator of ideas
The internet has become a powerful proliferator of news and opinions worldwide.
Certain plants act as proliferators in new environments, quickly spreading and dominating the area.
He was known as a proliferator of innovative business ideas within the company.
International agencies monitor proliferators of nuclear weapons to prevent global threats.
The internet can act as a powerful proliferator of misinformation if not carefully regulated.
Some invasive species are known proliferators that disrupt local ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disseminator - a person or thing that spreads information or ideas widely
  • antonym: inhibitor - a person or thing that slows down or prevents a process
  • synonym: multiplier - someone or something that increases the number or amount of something
  • antonym: restrainer - someone or something that limits or prevents the spread of something
  • synonym: spreader - someone or something that causes something to spread
