lexical

adjective
relating to words or vocabulary
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛksɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈlɛksɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the words, vocabulary, or word meanings of a language (not its grammar)

Examples

Lexical meaning
Her lexical skills improved dramatically after studying several foreign languages.
Researchers conducted a lexical analysis of the text to study word usage.
The dictionary includes detailed lexical information for every entry.
A lexical approach focuses on vocabulary rather than grammar.
She specializes in lexical research and the study of word meanings.
The lexical entries in the new dictionary are remarkably thorough.

Related Terms

  • antonym: grammatical - relating to grammar rather than vocabulary
  • synonym: semantic - relating to meaning in language
