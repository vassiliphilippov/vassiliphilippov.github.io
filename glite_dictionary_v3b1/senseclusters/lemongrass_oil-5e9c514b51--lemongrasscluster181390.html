lemongrass oil

noun
aromatic oil from lemongrass
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛmənˌɡræs ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈlɛmənˌɡrɑːs ɔɪl / (BrE)
an aromatic oil with a lemon-like scent, extracted from lemongrass, used in cooking, perfumes, and medicines

Examples

Lemongrass oil drops
Many Asian recipes call for a few drops of lemongrass oil to enhance the dish's flavor.
She diffused lemongrass oil in her room for its fresh, citrusy aroma.
Some herbal remedies include lemongrass oil as a natural ingredient to ease headaches.
