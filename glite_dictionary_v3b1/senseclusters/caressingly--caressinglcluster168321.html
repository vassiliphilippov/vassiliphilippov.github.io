caressingly

adverb
gently and lovingly
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɛsɪŋli / (AmE), / kəˈrɛsɪŋli / (BrE)
in a gentle and loving manner, as if giving a caress

Examples

To caressingly touch
She stroked the cat caressingly as it purred in her lap.
He spoke caressingly to calm his partner after the long day.
The mother brushed her child's hair caressingly before bedtime.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harshly - in a rough or unkind way
  • synonym: tenderly - in a gentle and caring way
