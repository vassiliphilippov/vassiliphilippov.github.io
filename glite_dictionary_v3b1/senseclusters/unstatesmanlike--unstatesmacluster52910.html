unstatesmanlike

adjective
lacking statesmanlike qualities
9.7 7.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈsteɪts.mən.laɪk/ /; / /ə.ˈsteɪts.mən.laɪk/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈsteɪts.mən.laɪk/ /; / /ə.ˈsteɪts.mən.laɪk/ (BrE)
Not displaying the qualities or conduct expected of a statesman; lacking dignity, prudence, or statesmanship.

Examples

An unstatesmanlike act
His impulsive reaction was viewed as unstatesmanlike by both colleagues and the media.
Many critics found the senator's comments to be unstatesmanlike and inappropriate for the occasion.
The committee avoided any unstatesmanlike tactics during the negotiations.
