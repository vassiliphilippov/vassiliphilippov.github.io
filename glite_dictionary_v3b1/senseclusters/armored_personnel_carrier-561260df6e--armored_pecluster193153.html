armored personnel carrier

noun
military vehicle for transporting troops
3.8 5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrmɚd pɚˈsɑnəl ˈkæriɚ /; / ˈɑːrmɚd ˌpɚˈsɑːnəl ˈkæriɚ / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd pəˈsɒnəl ˈkæriə /; / ˈɑːməd ˌpɜːˈsɒnəl ˈkæriə / (BrE)
a military vehicle with armor protection, designed to transport soldiers or infantry safely in combat zones, often equipped with caterpillar tracks or wheels
