untack

verb
to remove tacks
8.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtæk / (AmE), / ʌnˈtæk / (BrE)
to remove tacks or fastenings from something, especially by pulling them out

Examples

To untack a poster
She carefully untacked the notice from the bulletin board without tearing the paper.
After the event, volunteers untacked all the posters from the walls.
He untacked the fabric from the old chair to replace it with a new cover.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detach - to separate something from what it is attached to
  • antonym: fasten - to attach or secure something firmly
