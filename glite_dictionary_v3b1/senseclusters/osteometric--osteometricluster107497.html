osteometric

adjective
bone measurement
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstiəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒstiəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of bones, especially in scientific or medical contexts

Examples

Osteometric measurements
The scientist recorded osteometric measurements to analyze the growth patterns of the skeleton.
Accurate osteometric data are essential for identifying differences between species in paleontology.
The team used osteometric techniques to estimate the age and sex of the ancient remains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropometric - relating to the measurement of the human body
  • synonym: osteology - the scientific study of bones
