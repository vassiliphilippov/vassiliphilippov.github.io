editor in chief

noun
chief editorial leader of a publication
5.0 3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɛdɪtɚ ɪn ˈtʃif /; / ˌɛdɪtər ɪn ˈtʃiːf / (AmE), / ˌɛdɪtə ɪn ˈtʃiːf / (BrE)
the person with the highest editorial authority and responsibility for the content and direction of a publication (such as a newspaper or magazine)
