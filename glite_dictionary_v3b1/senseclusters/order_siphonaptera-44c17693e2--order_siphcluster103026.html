order Siphonaptera

noun
taxonomic order of fleas
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.dər sɪ.fə.ˈnæp.tər.ə/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.də sɪ.fə.ˈnæp.tə.rə/ (BrE)
The taxonomic order of small, wingless insects known as fleas, which are external parasites of mammals and birds.

Examples

Order Siphonaptera insects
All members of the order Siphonaptera are adapted for a parasitic lifestyle on warm-blooded hosts.
Scientists study the order Siphonaptera to understand the transmission of diseases like plague.
The order Siphonaptera includes more than 2,500 species of fleas worldwide.
