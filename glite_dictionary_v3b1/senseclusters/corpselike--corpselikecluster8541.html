corpselike

adjective
like a dead body
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrpsˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɔːpsˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a dead body, especially in appearance or color

Examples

A corpselike face
After staying up all night, he appeared almost corpselike at breakfast.
The abandoned house had a corpselike silence that made everyone uneasy.
Her skin was so pale and cold that she seemed corpselike in the dim light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deathly - suggesting death or a dead body
  • synonym: lifeless - without life or energy
  • antonym: lively - full of life and energy
