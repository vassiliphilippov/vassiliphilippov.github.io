corpselike

adjective
like a dead body

resembling or characteristic of a dead body, especially in appearance or color

Examples

A corpselike face

The abandoned house had a corpselike silence that made everyone uneasy.
Her skin was so pale and cold that she seemed corpselike in the dim light.

Synonyms

  • cadaveric
    9.9 0.36/m
    cadaveric is a technical adjective used for things relating to a corpse, while corpselike describes something that looks like a dead body.
  • cadaverous
    9.1 0.21/m
    cadaverous is very pale and thin, like someone sick or starving, while corpselike simply means resembling a dead body.
  • deathly
    6.5 0.56/m
    deathly often describes pale, still, or silent qualities, while corpselike focuses on actual dead body appearance or color.
  • deathlike
    8.6 0.06/m
    deathlike looks or feels like death in mood or scene, whereas corpselike refers to the look or color of a corpse.
