zarking around

verb
act in a silly or playful way
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈzɑrkɪŋ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈzɑːkɪŋ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to behave in a silly or playful way, often wasting time or not being serious

Examples

To zark around playfully
She got annoyed when her colleagues kept zarking around during the important conference call.
Stop zarking around and help me clean up this mess!
The kids were zarking around in the backyard instead of doing their homework.
