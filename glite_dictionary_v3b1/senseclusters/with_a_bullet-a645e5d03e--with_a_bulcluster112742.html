with a bullet

adverb
by a large margin (on a chart)
4.8
Pronunciation: / wɪð ə ˈbʊlɪt / (AmE), / wɪð ə ˈbʊlɪt / (BrE)
(of a chart ranking) by a large margin or with rapidly increasing success or popularity, especially in music or entertainment rankings.

Examples

Ranked with a bullet
Her new single entered the charts at number one with a bullet.
The film shot to the top of the box office with a bullet.
They won the contest with a bullet, far ahead of the second-place team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: narrowly - by a small margin
