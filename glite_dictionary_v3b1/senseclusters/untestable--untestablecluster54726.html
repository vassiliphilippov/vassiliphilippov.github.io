untestable

adjective
cannot be tested
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɛstəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɛstəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be tested, examined, or proven by experiment or observation

Examples

An untestable theory
The theory was considered untestable because no experiment could confirm or deny it.
Many philosophical questions are inherently untestable by scientific methods.
With current technology, some diseases remain untestable in their early stages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: testable - able to be tested or examined
  • synonym: unprovable - not able to be proven true or false
