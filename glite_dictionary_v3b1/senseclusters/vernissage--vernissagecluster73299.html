vernissage

noun
varnishing a painting
Pronunciation: / vɛrnɪˈsɑʒ / (AmE), / vɛːnɪˈsɑːʒ / (BrE)
the act or process of applying varnish to a painting, especially as a final protective layer (archaic)

Examples

Final vernissage
The artist performed a careful vernissage to protect the painting's vibrant colors.
In the 19th century, a vernissage was often the final step before an artwork was displayed.
Restorers sometimes use a modern vernissage technique to preserve old masterpieces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: varnishing - the act of applying varnish to a surface
