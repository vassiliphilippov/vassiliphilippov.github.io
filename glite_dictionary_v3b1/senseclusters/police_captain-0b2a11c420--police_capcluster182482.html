police captain

noun
senior police officer in charge of a precinct
2.5 2.8
Pronunciation: / pəˈlis ˈkæp.tɪn / (AmE), / pəˈliːs ˈkæp.tɪn / (BrE)
a police officer of a high rank who is in charge of a precinct or a major division within a police department

Examples

Police captain in charge
After years of service, he was finally promoted to police captain in the city police department.
As a police captain, she was responsible for supervising several detectives and patrol officers.
The police captain addressed the media about the recent rise in crime in the precinct.
