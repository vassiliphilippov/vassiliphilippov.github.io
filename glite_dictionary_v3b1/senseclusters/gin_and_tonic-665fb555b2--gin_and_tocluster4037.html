gin and tonic

noun
a mixed drink of gin and tonic water
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɪn ən ˈtɑnɪk /; / dʒɪn ənd ˈtɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʒɪn ən ˈtɒnɪk /; / dʒɪn ənd ˈtɒnɪk / (BrE)
a mixed alcoholic drink made with gin and tonic water, usually served with ice and a slice of lemon or lime
