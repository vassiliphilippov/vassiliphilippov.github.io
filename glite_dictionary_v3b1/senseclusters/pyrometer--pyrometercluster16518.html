pyrometer

noun
instrument for measuring high temperatures
Pronunciation: / paɪˈrɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / paɪˈrɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument for measuring very high temperatures, especially in furnaces or kilns

Examples

A pyrometer device
A pyrometer provides accurate readings even at temperatures too high for ordinary thermometers.
Modern steel production relies on the precise measurements of a pyrometer.
The engineer used a pyrometer to monitor the temperature inside the blast furnace.
