itemize

verb
to list items one by one
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪtəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈaɪtəmaɪz / (BrE)
to list things separately and in detail

Examples

To itemize expenses
Please itemize all your expenses for the trip in this report.
The accountant asked her to itemize her tax deductions before submitting the form.
We need to itemize each component of the budget for transparency.
He carefully itemized his deductions on the tax form.
Please itemize all the expenses in your report.
The company asked her to itemize every task completed during the project.
Please itemise all your expenses for the trip in the report.
The accountant will itemise each deduction on your tax return this year.
They asked the contractor to itemise every cost in the proposal for transparency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enumerate - to mention things one by one, to count
  • synonym: itemize - to list or mention items separately (American spelling)
  • synonym: list - to make a list of
  • antonym: summarise - to present the main points without detailed listing
