graciousness

noun
kindness and gentle courtesy
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪʃəsnəs /; / ˈɡreɪʃəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being kind, polite, gentle, and generous towards others

Examples

Show graciousness to others
Even in defeat, he showed remarkable graciousness toward his opponents.
Her graciousness made everyone at the party feel welcome and appreciated.
The graciousness of the host was evident in every thoughtful detail.
He accepted the award with genuine graciousness and humility.
Her graciousness made everyone feel at ease during the evening party.
The ambassador was praised for his graciousness towards guests from all backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courtesy - polite behavior that shows respect for other people
  • synonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate
  • antonym: rudeness - the quality of being impolite or disrespectful
