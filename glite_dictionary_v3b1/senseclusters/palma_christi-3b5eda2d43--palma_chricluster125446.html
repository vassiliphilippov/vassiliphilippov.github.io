palma christi

noun
tropical shrub; source of castor oil
Pronunciation: / ˌpælmə ˈkrɪsti / (AmE), / ˌpɑːlmə ˈkrɪsti / (BrE)
a large tropical shrub (Ricinus communis) with palmate leaves, grown for its seeds that produce castor oil and ricin
