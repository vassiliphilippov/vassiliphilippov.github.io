Milesian

adjective
of legendary Irish people
9.9
Pronunciation: / maɪˈliːʒən / (AmE), / maɪˈliːʒən / (BrE)
relating to the Milesians, a legendary people in Irish mythology said to be ancestors of the Irish.

Examples

Milesian legend
The ancient tale describes a Milesian arrival that changed the course of Irish legend.
Many Irish myths feature Milesian heroes who journeyed from distant lands.
The Milesian tradition is central to the island's mythological history.
