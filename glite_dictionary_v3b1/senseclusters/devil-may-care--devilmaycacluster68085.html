devil-may-care

adjective
cheerfully irresponsible; carefree
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛvəl meɪ ˈkɛr / (AmE), / ˌdɛvəl meɪ ˈkeə /; / ˌdɛvəl meɪ ˈkɛə / (BrE)
carefree and unconcerned about risks or consequences; showing a reckless or irresponsible attitude

Examples

Devil-may-care attitude
His devil-may-care approach to life often gets him into trouble, but he never seems concerned.
She has a devil-may-care attitude towards her finances, spending freely without worry.
With a devil-may-care grin, he signed up for the most dangerous challenge at the festival.
Her devil-may-care attitude made her popular at parties but sometimes got her into trouble at work.
They admired his devil-may-care style, refusing to play by anyone else's rules.
With his devil-may-care grin, he tackled every challenge as if nothing could go wrong.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carefree - untroubled and unconcerned
  • antonym: cautious - showing care and attention, especially to avoid danger
  • degree higher: reckless - without regard for danger or consequences
