swingman

noun
replica sports jersey
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪŋˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋˌmæn / (BrE)
a mid-level licensed sports jersey designed to look like a professional player's shirt but sold as a more affordable replica

Examples

Swingman jersey
He bought a swingman jersey to support his favorite basketball team at the game.
The store offers both authentic and swingman versions of the new football shirts.
Many fans choose a swingman because it looks professional but costs less than the official jersey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: replica jersey - an imitation sports jersey made to resemble the original
