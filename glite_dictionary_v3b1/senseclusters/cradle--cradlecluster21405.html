cradle

noun
a rocking baby bed
4.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkreɪ.dəl/ (AmE), /ˈkreɪ.dəl/ (BrE)
a small bed for a baby, typically mounted on rockers so that it can be swung or rocked gently

Examples

A cradle for babies
He rocked the cradle gently to soothe the crying baby.
Her grandmother passed down an antique cradle that had been in the family for generations.
The newborn slept peacefully in her cradle beside her parents' bed.
