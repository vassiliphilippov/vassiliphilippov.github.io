axis

noun
center line of rotation
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈæksɪs / (AmE), / ˈæksɪs /; / ˈaksɪs / (BrE)
an imaginary or real straight line around which an object rotates or is symmetrically arranged

Examples

Earth's axis of rotation
A spinning top wobbles as its axis tilts.
The Earth rotates around its axis once every twenty-four hours.
The wheel was turning smoothly on its central axis.
A planet spins on its axis as it orbits the sun.
The graph has an x-axis and a y-axis to plot data points.
The sculpture balances perfectly on its central axis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: axle - rod or spindle for rotating wheels or gears
  • antonym: center - the middle point or part of something
