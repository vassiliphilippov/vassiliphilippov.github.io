gasteromycete

noun
a fungus of the class Gasteromycetes
Pronunciation: / ˌɡæstəroʊˈmaɪsiːt / (AmE), / ˌɡastərəʊˈmʌɪsiːt / (BrE)
Any fungus belonging to the class Gasteromycetes, characterized by spores that develop inside the fruiting body and are released only when mature.
