parvenu

noun
person newly risen to wealth or status
9.9 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ër.v…ôÀånu / (AmE), / Ààp…ëÀê.v…ô.njuÀê / (BrE)
a person who has suddenly gained wealth or influence but has not yet been accepted by others of that social class

Examples

A wealthy parvenu
Despite his financial success, he was often dismissed as merely a parvenu among the established elite.
Many socialites considered her a parvenu who did not understand their customs.
The self-made millionaire was treated as a parvenu by the old aristocratic families.

Synonyms

  • arriviste
    9.9 0.10/m
    an arriviste shows off new status to join high society, while a parvenu is someone recently wealthy and not accepted.
  • nouveau-riche
    9.2 0.01/m
    a nouveau-riche has newly gained wealth and shows less refinement, while a parvenu may be wealthy but not yet socially accepted.
  • upstart
    7.4 0.59/m
    an upstart rises quickly and often seems arrogant, while a parvenu is newly rich or powerful but not socially accepted.
