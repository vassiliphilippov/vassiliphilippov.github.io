bacopa

noun
herb for memory
Pronunciation: / bəˈkoʊpə / (AmE), / bəˈkəʊpə / (BrE)
a small flowering herb, especially Bacopa monnieri, used in traditional medicine and as a supplement believed to support memory and cognition

Examples

Bacopa supplement capsules
Many students take bacopa supplements hoping to improve memory and concentration during exams.
Traditional healers have used bacopa for centuries to support mental clarity.
The herbalist recommended bacopa as a natural way to boost cognitive function.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brahmi - another name for Bacopa monnieri, used in Ayurvedic medicine
  • synonym: ginkgo - a plant supplement believed to improve memory
