photostream

noun
online photo sequence
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊtoʊstriːm / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊstriːm / (BrE)
a sequence of photos shared online, typically displayed in chronological order on a website or app

Examples

Online photostream
She uploaded her vacation pictures to her photostream so friends could see them.
His photostream is filled with beautiful landscapes from his travels around the world.
You can scroll through the photostream to view all the recent photos he has shared.

Related Terms

  • synonym: album - a set of photos grouped together, often by theme or event
  • synonym: gallery - a collection of photos or images displayed together
