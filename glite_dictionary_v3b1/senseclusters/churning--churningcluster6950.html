churning

noun
agitating to make butter
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of agitating or stirring a liquid, especially to make butter by shaking cream.

Examples

Churning of cream
The churning of cream in the old wooden barrel took nearly an hour.
Modern factories use machines for the churning of butter on a large scale.
She watched the churning of the milk with fascination during the farm tour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agitation - the action of shaking or moving something forcefully
  • synonym: stirring - the act of mixing a substance by moving it around
