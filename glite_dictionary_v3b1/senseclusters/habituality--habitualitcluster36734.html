habituality

noun
state of being habitual
7.6
Pronunciation: / həˌbɪtʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / həˌbɪtjuˈælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being habitual or regularly done by habit

Examples

Habituality of routines
The habituality of her daily walk helped her maintain a healthy lifestyle.
He admired the habituality with which she completed her assignments on time.
Breaking the habituality of old patterns can be challenging but rewarding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: habitualness - the state of being habitual
  • antonym: irregularity - the state of not being regular or habitual
  • synonym: routine - a regular way of doing things
