militarist

noun
person advocating military solutions
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlətəˌrɪst / (AmE), / mɪˈlɪtərɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports the use of military force or the maintenance of a strong military, often advocating war or military solutions to problems

Examples

A militarist supports war
Many militarists believe that armed force is the best solution to international conflicts.
She was a vocal militarist who encouraged increased defense spending.
The politician’s militarist views alienated some members of the peace movement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pacifist - a person who opposes war or violence as a means of settling disputes
