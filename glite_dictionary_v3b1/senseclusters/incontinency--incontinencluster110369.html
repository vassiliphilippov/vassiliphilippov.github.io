incontinency

noun
involuntary urination or defecation
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑntɪnənsi / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒntɪnənsi / (BrE)
the inability to control urination or defecation; lack of voluntary control over the bladder or bowels

Examples

Chronic incontinency
Chronic incontinency can significantly impact a person's daily life.
The patient suffered from incontinency as a result of the surgery.
Treatment options are available for those dealing with urinary incontinency.
