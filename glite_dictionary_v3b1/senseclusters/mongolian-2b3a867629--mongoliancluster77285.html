Mongolian

proper noun
person from Mongolia
Pronunciation: / mɑŋˈɡoʊliən / (AmE), / mɒŋˈɡəʊliən / (BrE)
a person from Mongolia or of Mongolian descent

Examples

A Mongolian tourist
A Mongolian greeted us warmly when we arrived in Ulaanbaatar.
The Mongolian community celebrates its traditional festivals every year.
Many Mongolians are skilled in horseback riding and archery.
