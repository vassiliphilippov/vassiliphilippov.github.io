guarana

noun
caffeinated plant seed
Pronunciation: / ˌɡwɑːrəˈnɑː / (AmE), / ˌɡwɑːrəˈnɑː / (BrE)
a South American plant whose seeds are used as a natural source of caffeine, especially in drinks and supplements

Examples

Guarana plant seeds
Many energy drinks include guarana as a natural source of caffeine.
The seeds of guarana are ground and added to soft drinks in Brazil.
Some people prefer guarana supplements to help them stay alert during long study sessions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caffeine - a natural stimulant found in coffee, tea, and some plants
  • synonym: coffee - a popular caffeinated drink made from roasted coffee beans
