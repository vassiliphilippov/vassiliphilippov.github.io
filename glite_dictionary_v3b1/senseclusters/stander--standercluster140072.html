stander

noun
upright support device
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstændər / (AmE), / ˈstændə / (BrE)
a medical device or frame that supports a person in an upright standing position for therapy or rehabilitation

Examples

A child in a stander
The therapist placed the child in a stander to help her practice standing during rehabilitation.
Using a stander can improve posture and circulation for people with limited mobility.
Doctors recommended a stander to support his therapy after the spinal injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: walker - a frame used to help people walk
  • antonym: wheelchair - a chair with wheels used by people who cannot walk
