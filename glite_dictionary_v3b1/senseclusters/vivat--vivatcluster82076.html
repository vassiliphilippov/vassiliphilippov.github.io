vivat

interjection
long live exclamation
Pronunciation: / ˈviːvæt / (AmE), / ˈviːvæt / (BrE)
an exclamation meaning 'long live', used to express good wishes for someone's continued success or life

Examples

To shout vivat loudly
As the new leader entered the hall, the crowd cried out vivat in celebration.
During the ceremony, students shouted vivat to honor their retiring teacher.
The tradition was to exclaim vivat at the end of the coronation.
