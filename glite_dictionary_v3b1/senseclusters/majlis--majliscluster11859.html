majlis

noun
legislative assembly
Pronunciation: / ˈmædʒlɪs / (AmE), / ˈmædʒlɪs / (BrE)
the official legislative or parliamentary assembly that makes laws in some countries, especially in the Middle East and South Asia

Examples

Iranian majlis parliament
The Iranian majlis passed several new laws regarding education reform this year.
Debates in the majlis often last for hours before a decision is reached.
Elections for the national majlis are held every four years in that country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: congress - a formal meeting or assembly, especially a legislative body
  • synonym: parliament - the highest legislative body in a country
