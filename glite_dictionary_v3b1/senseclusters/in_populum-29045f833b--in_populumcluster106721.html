in populum

noun
appeal to popularity
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn ˈpɑːp.jə.ləm/ /; / /ɪn ˈpɑːp.juː.ləm/ (AmE), /ɪn ˈpɒp.jʊ.ləm/ /; / /ɪn ˈpɒp.jə.ləm/ (BrE)
a rhetorical appeal or fallacy that seeks to persuade by appealing to popular opinion or emotion rather than logical reasoning

Examples

In populum argument

Many advertisements use in populum tactics to make their products seem more desirable.
