kanji

noun
Japanese character
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːndʒi / (AmE), / ˈkændʒi / (BrE)
a Japanese character of Chinese origin, or the set of such characters, used in the Japanese writing system

Examples

Japanese kanji characters
She practiced writing each kanji carefully in her notebook.
Learning to read kanji can be challenging for beginners of Japanese.
The word for 'mountain' in Japanese is written with a single kanji.
She practiced writing kanji every day to improve her Japanese skills.
Many street signs in Japan are written in kanji as well as in hiragana.
Learning the meaning of each kanji can be challenging for beginners.
