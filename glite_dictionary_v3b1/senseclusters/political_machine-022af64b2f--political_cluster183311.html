political machine

noun
group controlling a political party
7.5
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪdɪkəl məˈʃin / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
an organized group that controls and influences the activities and policies of a political party, often through patronage and a hierarchical structure

Examples

A political machine controls
He rose through the ranks with the support of the powerful political machine.
Many reformers tried to weaken the influence of the political machine in the 20th century.
The city's political machine was infamous for its control over local elections.

Related Terms

  • synonym: political party - an organized group of people with similar political aims and opinions
