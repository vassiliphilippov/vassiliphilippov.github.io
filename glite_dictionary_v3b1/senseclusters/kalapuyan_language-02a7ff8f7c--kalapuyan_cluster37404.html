Kalapuyan language

noun
indigenous language of the Kalapuya
Pronunciation: / ˌkæləˈpuːjən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌkæləˈpuːjən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an indigenous Penutian language formerly spoken by the Kalapuya people of western Oregon, USA

Examples

Kalapuyan language of Oregon
Linguists have worked to document the Kalapuyan language before it disappeared.
Only a few elders remembered words and phrases from the Kalapuyan language.
The revival of the Kalapuyan language is important for preserving cultural heritage.
