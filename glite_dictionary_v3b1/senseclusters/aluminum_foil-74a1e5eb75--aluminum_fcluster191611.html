aluminum foil

noun
thin sheet of aluminum for wrapping or cooking
Pronunciation: / əˈluː.mə.nəm ˌfɔɪl /; / əˌluːməˈnɪəm fɔɪl / (AmE), / ˌal.jʊˈmɪn.i.əm ˌfɔɪl /; / ˌaljʊˈmɪnɪəm fɔɪl / (BrE)
Thin sheets of aluminium/aluminum used for wrapping food and covering containers for cooking, storage, and insulation.
