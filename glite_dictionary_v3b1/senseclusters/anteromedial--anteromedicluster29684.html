anteromedial

adjective
front and middle position
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌæntəroʊˈmiːdiəl / (AmE), / ˌæntərəʊˈmiːdiəl / (BrE)
located toward the front and middle of a body or structure

Examples

The anteromedial surface
The anteromedial surface of the tibia is easily felt just below the knee.
Doctors examined the anteromedial region of the brain for signs of injury.
The ligament provides support to the anteromedial part of the joint.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anterolateral - located toward the front and side
  • antonym: posteromedial - located toward the back and middle
