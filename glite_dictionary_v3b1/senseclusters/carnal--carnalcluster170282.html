carnal

adjective
relating to bodily or sexual desires
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrnəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑːnəl / (BrE)
relating to physical or bodily desires, especially sexual, as opposed to spiritual or intellectual

Examples

Carnal desires
He tried to resist his carnal desires and focus on prayer.
The book was banned for its explicit depiction of carnal pleasures.
Their relationship was based on more than just carnal attraction.
Archaeologists examined the carnal remains discovered at the site.
Many ancient rituals were steeped in carnal symbolism.
The play explores the tension between carnal desires and moral duty.

  • antonym: spiritual - relating to the soul or non-physical aspects; opposite of bodily
