S wrench

noun
wrench with an S-shaped handle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛs rɛntʃ / (AmE), / ɛs rɛntʃ / (BrE)
a wrench with an S-shaped handle, used for reaching bolts in confined or hard-to-access spaces

Examples

An S wrench tool
An S wrench is ideal for loosening nuts in tight spaces where a straight handle won't fit.
He bought a new set of S wrenches for his workshop.
The mechanic used an S wrench to reach the bolt hidden behind the engine.
