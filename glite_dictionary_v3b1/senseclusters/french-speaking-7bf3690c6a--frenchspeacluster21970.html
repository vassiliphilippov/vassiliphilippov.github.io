French-speaking

adjective
able to speak French
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛntʃ ˌspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfrɛntʃ ˌspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
able to communicate in French, especially as a native or habitual language.

Examples

French-speaking people
Canada has a large French-speaking population in the province of Quebec.
Many African countries have both English- and French-speaking communities.
She attended a French-speaking school while living in Switzerland.
