Synonyms

    5.6 0.02/m
    noncooperative can mean unwilling or unable to work with others, while uncooperative usually stresses a plain refusal to help.
  • unaccommodating
    7.0 0.07/m
    unaccommodating is not willing to help or change plans for others, while uncooperative simply means refusing to work together.
  • unobliging
    7.8 0.00/m
    unobliging refuses to do small favors or help people personally, while uncooperative refers to a broader refusal to work together.
  • disobliging
    8.1 0.01/m
    disobliging acts deliberately unhelpful and may offend others, while uncooperative simply means not willing to work with people.
  • balky
    8.8 0.35/m
    balky refuses to proceed or cooperate in a stubborn, sudden way, while uncooperative is a general unwillingness to help or work.
