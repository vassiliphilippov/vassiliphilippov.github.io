bowling shoe

noun
shoe worn for bowling
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlɪŋ ʃu / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlɪŋ ʃuː / (BrE)
a special shoe with a smooth sole worn while bowling, typically to protect the alley and allow proper movement

Examples

A pair of bowling shoes
He forgot to bring his bowling shoes to the tournament.
The alley rents bowling shoes to players who need them.
Wearing bowling shoes is required to protect the polished lanes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bowling - the sport of rolling a ball to knock down pins
