touchdown

noun
score in American football
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌtʃˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃ.daʊn / (BrE)
(in American football) an act of scoring points by carrying or catching the ball in the opponent's end zone.

Examples

Touchdown celebration
He ran fifty yards to secure a touchdown for his team.
That was his third touchdown of the season.
The team celebrated after scoring a touchdown in the final seconds of the game.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: field goal - a score in American football by kicking the ball through the uprights
  • synonym: goal - a score in various sports achieved by sending the ball or puck into the opponent's net or goal area
