rowanberry

noun
red fruit of a rowan tree
7.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊ.ən.be.riː/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊ.ən.be.rɪ/ (BrE)
The small, round, typically bright red fruit of the rowan tree, often found in clusters and used decoratively or as food for wildlife.

Examples

Bright rowanberries clusters
Birds flocked to the garden to feed on the ripe rowanberries.
She decorated the wreath with sprigs of holly and rowanberries.
The branches were heavy with bright rowanberries in late autumn.
