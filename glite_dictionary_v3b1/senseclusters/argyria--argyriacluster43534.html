argyria

noun
skin discoloration from silver
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈdʒɪriə / (AmE), / ɑːˈdʒɪəriə / (BrE)
a medical condition in which the skin turns blue or gray due to excessive exposure to silver

Examples

Argyria skin condition
Prolonged exposure to silver compounds can result in argyria.
The patient developed argyria after years of using silver-based medications.
Doctors diagnosed her with argyria when her skin began to turn a bluish-gray color.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cyanosis - bluish discoloration of the skin due to lack of oxygen
