treeing

noun
driving animal up tree
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtriːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtriːɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of driving an animal up a tree, especially by a hunting dog during a hunt

Examples

Treeing a raccoon
The hounds are trained for treeing raccoons during the hunt.
Successful treeing requires dogs to work together and follow the animal's scent.
Hunters praised the dog's skill in treeing a fox quickly and safely.
