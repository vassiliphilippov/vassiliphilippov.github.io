oolite

noun
sedimentary rock grains
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈuː.ə.laɪt/ /; / /ˈuː.laɪt/ (AmE), /ˈuː.ə.laɪt/ /; / /ˈuː.laɪt/ (BrE)
a sedimentary rock made of many small, rounded grains of calcium carbonate (the grains themselves are also called oolites)

Examples

  • synonym: oolitic limestone - a sedimentary rock composed mainly of oolites
