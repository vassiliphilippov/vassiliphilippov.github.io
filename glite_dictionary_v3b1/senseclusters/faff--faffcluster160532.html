faff

noun
unnecessary fuss
Pronunciation: / fæf / (AmE), / fæf / (BrE)
a fuss, unnecessary activity, or annoying delay that wastes time or complicates something.

Examples

A big faff
Booking the tickets online turned out to be a real faff because the website kept crashing.
I wish there wasn't so much faff involved in filling out these forms.
Moving house is always a huge faff, no matter how well you plan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bother - trouble or inconvenience
  • synonym: hassle - a situation causing difficulty or trouble
  • antonym: simplicity - the quality of being easy or uncomplicated
