iconically

adverb
by direct resemblance
6.6
Pronunciation: / aɪˈkɑnɪkli / (AmE), / aɪˈkɒnɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that directly resembles or imitates the thing represented, especially visually or symbolically.

Examples

Iconically shown objects
The artist iconically depicted the sun as a bright yellow circle in the sky.
Traffic signs often use images iconically to communicate their meaning quickly and clearly.
In the logo, the company is iconically represented by a simple apple shape.
