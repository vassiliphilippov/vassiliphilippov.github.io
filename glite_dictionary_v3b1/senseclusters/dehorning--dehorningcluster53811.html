dehorning

noun
removal of animal horns
5.6
Pronunciation: / diˈhɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / diˈhɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of removing the horns from livestock, especially cattle, to prevent injury or for management purposes

Examples

Dehorning cattle
The veterinarian explained the benefits of dehorning to the new cattle owners.
Proper equipment is essential for safe and humane dehorning procedures.
Many farms schedule dehorning when calves are still young to reduce stress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disbudding - removal of horn buds from young animals
