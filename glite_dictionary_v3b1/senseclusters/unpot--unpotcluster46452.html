unpot

verb
to remove from pot
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɑt / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɒt / (BrE)
to remove a plant from its pot, usually in preparation for transplanting

Examples

To unpot a plant
Before moving the flowers to the garden bed, gently unpot each one.
It is important to unpot the plant carefully to avoid damaging its roots.
She unpotted the orchid to inspect the health of its roots.

Related Terms

  • antonym: repot - to put a plant into a new pot
  • synonym: transplant - to move a plant from one place or container to another
