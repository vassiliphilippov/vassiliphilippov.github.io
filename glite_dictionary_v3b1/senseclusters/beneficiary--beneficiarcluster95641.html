beneficiary

noun
person receiving benefits or funds
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛnəˈfɪʃiˌɛri / (AmE), / ˌbɛnɪˈfɪʃəri / (BrE)
A person or organization that receives money or other benefits from something such as a will, trust, insurance policy, or financial arrangement.

Examples

Insurance beneficiary
Each policyholder must name at least one beneficiary when signing up for insurance.
The orphanage is the main beneficiary of the charity's annual fundraiser.
The primary beneficiary of the trust will receive monthly payments for life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: donor - a person who gives something
  • synonym: recipient - a person or thing that receives something
