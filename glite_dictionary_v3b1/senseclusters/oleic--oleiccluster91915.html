oleic

adjective
containing oleic acid
7.2
Pronunciation: / oʊˈliːɪk / (AmE), / əʊˈliːɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing oleic acid, a common fatty acid found in many animal and vegetable oils

Examples

Oleic acid content
Avocado is rich in oleic acid, which is considered beneficial for heart health.
Some cooking oils are labeled as high oleic to indicate their healthy fat content.
Scientists analyzed the oleic composition of different vegetable oils in the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fatty - containing or composed of fat
  • antonym: saturated - containing the maximum amount of hydrogen and no double bonds (in chemistry)
