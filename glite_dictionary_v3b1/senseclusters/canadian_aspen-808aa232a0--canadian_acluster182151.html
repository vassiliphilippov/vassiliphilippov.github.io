Canadian aspen

noun
a North American species of aspen tree
5.7
Pronunciation: / kəˈneɪdiən ˈæspən / (AmE), / kəˈneɪdiən ˈaspən / (BrE)
A species of aspen tree native to eastern North America, known for its narrow crown.

Examples

A Canadian aspen tree
A Canadian aspen can be recognized by its narrow, graceful crown.
In autumn, the leaves of the Canadian aspen turn a brilliant yellow.
The forest was dotted with Canadian aspen trees, their leaves shimmering in the breeze.
