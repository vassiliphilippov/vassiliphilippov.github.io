splitsaw

noun
handsaw for cutting with the grain
Pronunciation: / ˈsplɪtˌsɔ / (AmE), / ˈsplɪtˌsɔː / (BrE)
a handsaw designed for cutting wood along the grain

Examples

A splitsaw tool
Proper use of a splitsaw ensures a smoother split when working with hardwoods.
The carpenter reached for his splitsaw to cut the plank along the grain.
Traditional woodworkers often prefer a splitsaw for making long, straight cuts in lumber.
