sickler

noun
maker of sickles
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪklər / (AmE), / ˈsɪklə / (BrE)
a person who makes or sells sickles, the curved agricultural tool used for cutting crops

Examples

Village sickler
The village sickler crafted new tools for the upcoming harvest season.
As a skilled sickler, he supplied farmers with sharp, reliable sickles every year.
They visited the local sickler to have their old sickles repaired before the wheat was ready.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blacksmith - a person who makes and repairs things in iron by hand
  • antonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
