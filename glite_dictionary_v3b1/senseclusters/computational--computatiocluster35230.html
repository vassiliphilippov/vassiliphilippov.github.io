computational

adjective
relating to computation or computers
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːmpjuˈteɪʃənl / (AmE), / ˌkɒmpjuˈteɪʃənl / (BrE)
relating to the process of calculation or to the use of computers for solving problems.

Examples

Computational power
Advances in computational power have revolutionized scientific research.
She specializes in computational biology, using software to analyze genetic data.
The team used computational models to simulate the chemical reaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: computerized - controlled or operated by a computer
  • antonym: manual - done by hand, not by machine or computer
