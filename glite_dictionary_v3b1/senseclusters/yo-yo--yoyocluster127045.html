yo-yo

noun
toy with a spool on a string
Pronunciation: / ˈjoʊˌjoʊ / (AmE), / ˈjəʊ.jəʊ / (BrE)
a toy consisting of a round spool attached to a string, which can be spun up and down by hand

Examples

A yo-yo toy
As a child, Ben collected every color of yo-yo he could find.
He watched the yo-yo go up and down the string again and again.
Maria showed off her new yo-yo tricks at the school talent show.
