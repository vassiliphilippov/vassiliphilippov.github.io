tactical maneuver

noun
action for tactical advantage
Pronunciation: / ˈtæk.tɪ.kəl məˈnuː.vɚ /; / ˈtæk.tɪ.kəl məˈnuːvər / (AmE), / ˈtæk.tɪ.kəl məˈnjuː.və /; / ˈtæktɪkəl məˈnjuːvə / (BrE)
an action or movement made to achieve a specific advantage in a tactical situation, often in military or strategic contexts
