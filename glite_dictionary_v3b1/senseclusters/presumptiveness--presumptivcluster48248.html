presumptiveness

noun
overconfident attitude
8.5
Pronunciation: /prɪ.ˈzʌmp.tɪv.nəs/ (AmE), /prɪ.ˈzʌmp.tɪv.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being too confident or forward, often without proper justification or respect

Examples

Her presumptiveness in speech
Her presumptiveness in interrupting the meeting annoyed her colleagues.
The presumptiveness of his request surprised everyone in the room.
Some people mistake presumptiveness for confidence, but it can be seen as disrespectful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: presumption - behavior that is too bold or forward
