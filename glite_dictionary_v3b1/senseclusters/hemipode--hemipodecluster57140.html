hemipode

noun
small quail-like bird of Eurasia and Africa
6.2 9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛmɪˌpoʊd / (AmE), / ˈhɛmɪpəʊd / (BrE)
a small, quail-like bird found in southern Eurasia and northern Africa, lacking a hind toe and typically living in grassy plains

Examples

A hemipode bird

The hemipode hides in tall grasses, making it difficult for predators to find.
Unlike true quail, the hemipode does not have a hind toe.
