noodle over

verb
think about; reflect on
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːdəl ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈnuːdl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to think about or consider something carefully, especially in a casual or informal way

Examples

To noodle over ideas
He noodled over the offer for days before finally saying yes.
She likes to noodle over new designs during her morning walks.
We need to noodle over the budget proposal before submitting it to the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consider - to think about carefully
  • antonym: dismiss - to decide not to think about something any longer
  • synonym: ponder - to think about something carefully, especially before making a decision
