Dictamnus alba

noun
Eurasian herb with flammable white flowers
Pronunciation: / dɪkˈtæmnəs ˈælbə / (AmE), / dɪkˈtamnəs ˈalbə / (BrE)
A Eurasian perennial herb with white flowers that emit a flammable vapor, especially in hot weather.

Examples

Dictamnus alba white flowers
Gardeners are fascinated by the strange property of Dictamnus alba to produce flammable gas.
The flowers of Dictamnus alba can emit a faint vapor that sometimes bursts into flame on hot days.
We planted Dictamnus alba in a sunny spot to enjoy its aromatic scent and striking white flowers.
