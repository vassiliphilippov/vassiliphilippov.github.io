intellective

noun
intellectual thinker
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntəˈlɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪntɪˈlɛktɪv / (BrE)
a person who is intellectual or known for deep thinking (rare, archaic)

Examples

An intellective thinker
In the old university, he was respected as an intellective among his peers.
The salon was filled with artists and at least one intellective who led the discussion.
Though rarely used today, the term intellective once described a person of great thought.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intellectual - a person who uses the mind creatively or critically
  • synonym: thinker - someone who thinks deeply and seriously
