prove

verb
test or try out experimentally
1.5
Pronunciation: / pruv / (AmE), / pruːv / (BrE)
to put something to the test in order to determine its quality, performance, or reliability; to try out experimentally

Examples

To prove a product
Bakers often prove a new recipe before adding it to the menu.
Before you buy the tool, prove its durability by using it in the workshop.
The scientists decided to prove the theory with a series of experiments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: experiment - to try out new ideas or methods to discover their effects
  • synonym: test - to try something out in order to see how effective, reliable, or successful it is
