tie clip

noun
jewelry to hold a tie in place
Pronunciation: / taɪ klɪp / (AmE), / taɪ klɪp / (BrE)
A small piece of jewelry worn to fasten a man's tie to the underlying shirt front, keeping it in place.

Examples

A tie clip fastener
He wore a silver tie clip to keep his tie neat during the meeting.
I gave my father a gold tie clip for his birthday.
The tie clip matched his cufflinks perfectly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tie bar - piece of jewelry that holds a tie in place
  • synonym: tie pin - a pin used to fasten a tie to a shirt
