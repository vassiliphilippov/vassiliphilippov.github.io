resolve

noun
firm determination; resoluteness
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɑlv / (AmE), / rɪˈzɒlv / (BrE)
firm determination to achieve something; the quality of being resolute or steadfast

Examples

Strong resolve
Even in the face of hardship, she never lost her resolve to succeed.
His resolve to change his life inspired those around him.
She faced every obstacle with unwavering resolve and determination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indecision - the inability to make decisions quickly or firmly
  • synonym: resolution - the quality of being determined or resolute; also a firm decision
