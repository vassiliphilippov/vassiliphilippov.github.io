entry word

noun
main word heading a dictionary entry
Pronunciation: / ˈɛntri wɝd / (AmE), / ˈɛntri wɜːd / (BrE)
the main word or form that appears as the heading of a dictionary entry and is alphabetized in the dictionary

Examples

The entry word in a dictionary
In bilingual dictionaries, the entry word is usually printed in bold for easy reference.
Teachers often ask students to look up the entry word and read its various meanings.
The entry word is always listed at the top of the dictionary definition.
