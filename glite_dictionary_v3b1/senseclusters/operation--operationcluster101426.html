Synonyms

  • surgery
    2.7 37/m

  • surgical operation
    3.0 0.19/m
    surgical operation names a doctor-made incision to fix or remove tissue, while operation is the general medical word for that procedure.
  • surgical procedure
    4.3 1.29/m

  • surgical process
    4.6 0.00/m
