gunship

noun
armed military vessel
5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈgʌn.ʃɪp/ (AmE), /ˈgʌn.ʃɪp/ (BrE)
a small, heavily armed military boat or aircraft used for patrol, combat, or support missions, especially near coasts or rivers

Examples

River gunship
The navy sent a gunship to patrol the river and prevent illegal crossings.
During the operation, a gunship provided cover fire for the ground troops.
The old gunship was retired after years of service along the coastline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patrol boat - a small naval vessel used for patrol duties
  • antonym: transport - a vehicle used to carry people or goods, not for combat
  • synonym: warship - a ship built and equipped for fighting
