expensively

adverb
in a costly or luxurious manner
2.3
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛnsɪvli / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛnsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that costs a lot of money or requires a great deal of expense.

Examples

Dressed expensively
She was dressed expensively for the gala event, wearing designer clothes and jewelry.
The house was furnished expensively, with antiques and artwork in every room.
They dined expensively at a five-star restaurant overlooking the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheaply - inexpensively; at a low cost
