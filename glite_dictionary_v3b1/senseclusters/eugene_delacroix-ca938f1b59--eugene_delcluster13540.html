Eugene Delacroix

proper noun
French Romantic painter (1798–1863)

Pronunciation: /juː.ˈdʒiːn də.lə.ˈkrɔɪ/ /; / /juː.ˈdʒiːn də.lə.ˈkrwɑː/ (AmE), /juː.ˈdʒiːn də.lə.ˈkrɔɪ/ /; / /juː.ˈdʒiːn də.lə.ˈkrwɑː/ (BrE)
(1798–1863) A French Romantic painter noted for his expressive brushstrokes and vivid use of color, regarded as a leading figure in 19th-century European art.
