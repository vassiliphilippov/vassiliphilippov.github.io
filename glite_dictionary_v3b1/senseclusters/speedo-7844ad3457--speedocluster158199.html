Speedo

noun
vehicle speedometer
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈspiːdəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a vehicle's speedometer, the instrument that shows how fast the vehicle is moving

Examples

Car Speedo
She glanced at the Speedo and slowed down when the needle climbed past sixty.
The Speedo was broken, so he had no idea how fast he was driving.
Make sure to check the Speedo regularly to avoid speeding tickets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: speedometer - an instrument in a vehicle that shows its speed
