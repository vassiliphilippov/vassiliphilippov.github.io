Brassica oleracea gongylodes

noun
cultivated plant with edible swollen stem
Pronunciation: / brəˌsɪkə oʊˌlɛriˈeɪʃə ˌgɒŋɡɪˈloʊdiːz / (AmE), / brəˌsɪkə əˌlɛəriˈeɪʃə ˌgɒŋɡɪˈləʊdiːz / (BrE)
A cultivated variety of cabbage grown for its enlarged, turnip-shaped edible stem, commonly known as kohlrabi.
