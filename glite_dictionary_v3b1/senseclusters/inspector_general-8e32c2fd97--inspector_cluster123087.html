inspector general

noun
senior official for investigations and oversight
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌspɛktər ˈdʒɛnərəl / (AmE), / ɪnˌspɛktə ˈdʒɛnərəl / (BrE)
a senior official in the military or government responsible for overseeing investigations and ensuring compliance with regulations and procedures.

Examples

The inspector general report
An official report from the inspector general revealed several cases of misconduct within the department.
Each branch of the armed forces has its own inspector general to oversee internal investigations.
The inspector general conducted a thorough review of the military's safety procedures.
