plug

noun
electrical device for making a connection
Pronunciation: / plʌɡ / (AmE), / plʌɡ / (BrE)
an electrical device with two or three pins that is inserted into a socket to make an electrical connection

Examples

An electric plug
He replaced the old plug because its cord was frayed.
Make sure the plug is fully inserted into the socket before switching on the lamp.
This appliance has a three-pin plug suitable for use in many countries.
