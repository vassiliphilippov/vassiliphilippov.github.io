catadioptric

adjective
using lenses and mirrors
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkætədaɪˈɑːptrɪk / (AmE), / ˌkætədaɪˈɒptrɪk / (BrE)

Many modern cameras use catadioptric lenses for compact and powerful zoom capabilities.
