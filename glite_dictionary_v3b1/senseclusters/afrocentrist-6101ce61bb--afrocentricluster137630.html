Afrocentrist

noun
supporter of Afrocentrism
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæfroʊˈsɛntrɪst / (AmE), / ˌafrəʊˈsɛntrɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or promotes Afrocentrism, emphasizing the importance of African culture and history

Examples

An Afrocentrist scholar
The Afrocentrist argued for a curriculum that highlights African achievements throughout history.
Many Afrocentrists believe it is important to challenge stereotypes about Africa in the media.
As an Afrocentrist, she organized events celebrating African heritage in her community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Afrocentrism - an approach that centers African culture and history
  • antonym: Eurocentrist - a person who supports or promotes Eurocentrism
