semi-skimmed milk

noun
milk with some cream removed
3.4 1.7
Pronunciation: /ˈse.mɪ.skɪmd mɪlk/ /; / /ˈsiː.mi.skɪmd mɪlk/ (AmE), /ˈse.mɪ.skɪmd mɪlk/ /; / /ˈsiː.mi.skɪmd mɪlk/ (BrE)
