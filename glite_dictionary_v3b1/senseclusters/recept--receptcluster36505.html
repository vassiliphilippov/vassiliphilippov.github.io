recept

noun
archaic recipe or prescription
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɛpt / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛpt / (BrE)
an archaic term for a recipe, receipt, or medical prescription, especially a formula for preparing a remedy

Examples

A recept for medicine
The physician wrote a recept for a herbal remedy to treat the illness.
In the old manuscript, a recept for cough syrup was carefully recorded.
Apothecaries once relied on a recept to prepare medicines for their patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prescription - a written order for medicine or treatment
  • synonym: receipt - an old term for a recipe or medical formula
  • synonym: recipe - a set of instructions for preparing a particular dish
