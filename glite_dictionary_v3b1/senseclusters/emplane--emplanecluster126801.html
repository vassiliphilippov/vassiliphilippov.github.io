emplane

verb
to board an airplane
Pronunciation: / ɛmˈpleɪn / (AmE), / ɛmˈpleɪn / (BrE)
to board an airplane; to go onto or into an aircraft for a journey

Examples

To emplane a plane
After the announcement, everyone lined up eagerly to emplane for their holiday journey.
Passengers are asked to emplane as soon as their flight is called.
The troops began to emplane for the overseas mission at dawn.
