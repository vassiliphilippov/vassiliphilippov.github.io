eruditely

adverb
in an erudite manner
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr.ə.dɪt.li / (AmE), / ˈɛr.jʊ.dɪt.li / (BrE)
In an erudite manner; with deep and extensive knowledge.

Examples

To speak eruditely
He eruditely referenced historical events to support his argument.
She spoke eruditely about classical literature during the panel discussion.
The professor eruditely explained complex scientific theories to the audience.
