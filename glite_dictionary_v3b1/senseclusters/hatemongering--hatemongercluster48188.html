hatemongering

noun
spreading hatred
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪtˌmʌŋɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈheɪtˌmʌŋɡərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of encouraging or promoting hatred toward individuals or groups, especially in public speech or writing

Examples

Online hatemongering
The organization condemned all forms of hatemongering in the media.
He was criticized for his hatemongering speeches that targeted minority groups.
Social media platforms are working to reduce hatemongering online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incitement - the act of encouraging others to behave violently or illegally
  • antonym: tolerance - the willingness to accept or respect different opinions or people
