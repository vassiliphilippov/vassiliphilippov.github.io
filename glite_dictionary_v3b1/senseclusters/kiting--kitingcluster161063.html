kiting

noun
fraudulent banking practice
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the illegal act of writing checks or transferring funds between accounts to create false balances and access money temporarily

Examples

Bank kiting scheme
The company discovered a case of kiting during its annual financial audit.
He was charged with kiting after authorities found he had written checks with insufficient funds.
Banks have strict policies to detect and prevent kiting schemes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: check fraud - the crime of using checks to illegally obtain money
  • synonym: embezzlement - theft or misappropriation of funds placed in one's trust
