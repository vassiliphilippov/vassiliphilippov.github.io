propagandize

verb
promote propaganda or biased ideas
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑː.pəˈɡæn.daɪz /; / ˌprɑːpəˈɡændaɪz /; / prɑːˈpæɡənˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˌprɒ.pəˈɡæn.daɪz /; / ˌprɒpəˈɡændaɪz /; / prɒpəˈɡændaɪz / (BrE)
to spread or promote ideas or information, especially in a biased or misleading way, in order to influence people
