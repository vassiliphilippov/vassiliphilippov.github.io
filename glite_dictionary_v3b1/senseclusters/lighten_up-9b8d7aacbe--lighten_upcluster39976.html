lighten up

verb
make more cheerful by brightening or adding color
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪ.tən ʌp/ /; / /ˈlaɪ.tən əp/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪ.tən ʌp/ /; / /ˈlaɪ.tən əp/ (BrE)
to make a place or thing brighter, lighter in color, or more attractive

Examples
