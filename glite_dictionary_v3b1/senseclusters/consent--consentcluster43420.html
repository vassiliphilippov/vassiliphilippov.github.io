consent

noun
permission or agreement
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɛnt / (AmE), / kənˈsɛnt / (BrE)
permission for something to happen or agreement to do something, especially given formally or explicitly

Examples

Written consent
Parental consent is required for students under 18 to participate in the trip.
The patient gave her consent before the operation could proceed.
Without written consent, the data cannot be shared with third parties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approval - agreement or official permission
  • antonym: refusal - denial or rejection of permission or agreement
