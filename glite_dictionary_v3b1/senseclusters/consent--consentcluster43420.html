Examples

Written consent

Without written consent, the data cannot be shared with third parties.

Synonyms

  • permission
    1.0 43/m
    permission is the act of being allowed by someone in charge, while consent is a person's clear, voluntary agreement to something.
  • approval
    1.6 26/m
    approval is a formal yes from an authority or group, while consent is an individual's explicit agreement to what is proposed.
  • authorization
    4.1 2.44/m
    authorization is an official paper or order that gives legal power, while consent is a person's voluntary agreement.
  • agreement
    1.0 26/m
    agreement is a shared decision or contract made between people, while consent is one person's clear permission or approval.
