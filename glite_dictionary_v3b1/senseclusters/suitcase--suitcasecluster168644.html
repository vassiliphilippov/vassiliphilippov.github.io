suitcase

noun
portable travel case for clothes
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːtˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈsuːtkeɪs / (BrE)
A portable rectangular container with a handle, used for carrying clothes and personal belongings while traveling.

Examples

A suitcase handle
He packed his favorite shirts into the suitcase before leaving for his trip.
She bought a new suitcase for her trip to Europe next month.
The old suitcase was battered from years of travel and adventure.

  • synonym: luggage - bags and suitcases used when traveling
  • degree higher: trunk - a large strong box used for storage or transport
