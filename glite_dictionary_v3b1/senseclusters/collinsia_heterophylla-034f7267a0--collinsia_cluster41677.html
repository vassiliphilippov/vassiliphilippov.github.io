Collinsia heterophylla

noun
a California wildflower with ringed pale flowers
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪnziə ˌhɛtəroʊˈfɪlə / (AmE), / kɒˈlɪnziə ˌhɛtərəʊˈfɪlə / (BrE)
A species of annual flowering plant native to California, noted for its rings of white, lavender, or pale-blue flowers arranged around its stems, resembling a pagoda.
