grocery boy

noun
boy delivering groceries
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊsəri bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊsəri bɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy or young man employed to deliver groceries to customers

Examples

Grocery boy delivers orders
In the 1950s, it was common for a grocery boy to deliver orders by bicycle.
She remembered the friendly grocery boy who whistled as he made his deliveries every Saturday morning.
The grocery boy left a basket of fruit and vegetables at our front door.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delivery boy - boy or young man employed to deliver goods
  • degree higher: grocery clerk - person who works in a grocery store
