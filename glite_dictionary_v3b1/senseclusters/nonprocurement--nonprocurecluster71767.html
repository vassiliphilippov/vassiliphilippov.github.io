nonprocurement

noun
not acquiring goods
9.6
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.prə.ˈkjʊr.mənt/ (AmE), /nɒn.prə.ˈkjʊə.mənt/ (BrE)
an agreement, activity, or the process of not acquiring goods or services, especially in official or institutional settings

Examples

Nonprocurement agreement
The organization received funding through a nonprocurement agreement rather than a traditional contract.
Certain grants are classified as nonprocurement to distinguish them from purchases of goods or services.
The university handled the partnership as a nonprocurement to comply with institutional policies.
The organization has a clear policy regarding nonprocurement of certain restricted items.
Budget cuts led to the nonprocurement of new equipment this year.
Government regulations require documentation for both procurement and nonprocurement activities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: procurement - the act of obtaining goods or services, especially for business or government
