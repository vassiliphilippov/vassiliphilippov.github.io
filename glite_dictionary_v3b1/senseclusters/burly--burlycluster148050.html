burly

adjective
large, muscular in build
4.6 2.47/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ùÀêli / (AmE), / Ààb…úÀêli / (BrE)
(of a person) large and strong, with a heavy, muscular build.

Examples

A burly man
Despite his burly appearance, he had a gentle and kind personality.
Several burly firefighters carried heavy equipment up the stairs during the emergency.
The burly security guard easily restrained the unruly fan at the concert.

Synonyms

  • brawny
    4.7 0.67/m
    brawny is very muscular with clear muscle shape, while burly describes a large, heavy person with strong build.
  • husky
    3.3 0.71/m
    husky is heavily built and often slightly stocky, while burly describes a large, heavy person with a strong muscular frame.
  • beefy
    4.0 1.56/m
    beefy looks solid and thick-set in a casual way, while burly feels more formal and stresses heavy muscle.
  • strapping
    4.4 0.02/m
    strapping is tall and powerfully built, while burly focuses on being heavy and muscular without implying tall height.

Related Terms

  • synonym: husky - strong and heavily built (of a person)
  • antonym: slender - thin, slim in build
  • synonym: strapping - tall and strong; robust
