elastica

noun
former plant genus
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlæstɪkə / (AmE), / ɪˈlæstɪkə / (BrE)
a former botanical genus name for certain tropical rubber trees, now classified under the genus Ficus

Examples

Elastica tree label
The old herbarium label still referred to the specimen as elastica instead of Ficus.
Botanists once classified the rubber tree under the genus elastica before updating its taxonomy.
You may find references to elastica in historical botanical literature about tropical plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ficus - a genus of tropical trees and shrubs including the rubber tree
