no

determiner
zero quantity; not any
1.6 1.0
Pronunciation: /noʊ/ (AmE), /nəʊ/ (BrE)
Used with mass nouns or plural count nouns to indicate a complete or almost complete lack or zero quantity of something.

Examples

No money left
He has no interest in watching football matches.
There were no tickets left for the concert.
With no warning, the storm arrived suddenly.
