frostiness

noun
coldness like frost
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɑːstinəs / (AmE), / ˈfrɒstinɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being cold like frost; chilly temperature or atmosphere.

Examples

The frostiness of winter
The frostiness of the landscape was visible in the sparkling ice on the trees.
The frostiness of the morning air made everyone shiver as they stepped outside.
There was a certain frostiness in the air after the sudden cold front passed through.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chilliness - the quality or state of being noticeably cold
  • antonym: warmth - the quality or state of being warm
