purple clematis

noun
climbing plant with purplish-blue flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpəl kləˈmætɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl̩ klɪˈmætɪs / (BrE)
A climbing plant native to northeastern North America, known for its waxy purplish-blue flowers.

Examples

The purple clematis flower
In the spring, their garden is filled with the blooms of purple clematis.
She chose a purple clematis to brighten the entrance to her home.
The purple clematis climbed gracefully along the old wooden fence.
