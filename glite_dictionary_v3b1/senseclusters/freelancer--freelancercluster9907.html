freelancer

noun
self-employed worker offering services
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːˌlænsər / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌlɑːnsə / (BrE)
a person who works independently, selling their work or services by the hour, day, or job, rather than working on a regular salary basis for one employer

Examples

A freelancer works independently
As a freelancer, she enjoys the flexibility to choose her own projects.
He worked as a freelancer after leaving his full-time job.
Many companies are hiring freelancers to meet temporary business needs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contractor - a person or company that undertakes a contract to provide materials or labor
