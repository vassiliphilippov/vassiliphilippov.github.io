expressionistically

adverb
in an expressionist style
9.6
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌsprɛʃəˈnɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ɪkˌsprɛʃəˈnɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or is characteristic of expressionism, often using bold or exaggerated style to convey emotion

Examples

Filmed expressionistically style
The director chose to film the scene expressionistically to highlight the character's inner turmoil.
The play was staged expressionistically, with distorted sets and intense lighting effects.
Her paintings are often created expressionistically, using vivid colors and dramatic brushstrokes.
