Joe Schmoe

proper noun
average or ordinary person (esp. man)
7.9
Pronunciation: / dʒoʊ ʃmoʊ / (AmE), / dʒəʊ ʃməʊ / (BrE)
a name used to refer to an average or ordinary person, especially a typical, unremarkable man

Examples

A regular Joe Schmoe
He's just a regular Joe Schmoe trying to make ends meet like everyone else.
That movie wasn't made for critics; it was made for the typical Joe Schmoe.
The company wants to create a product that appeals to the average Joe Schmoe on the street.
