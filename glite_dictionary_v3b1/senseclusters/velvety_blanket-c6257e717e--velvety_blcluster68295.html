velvety blanket

noun
smooth, soft covering (often figurative)
7.4 3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛl.və.ti ˈblæŋ.kət / (AmE), / ˈvɛl.vəti ˈblæŋ.kɪt / (BrE)
something that covers an area in a way that is very smooth and soft, like velvet; often used figuratively to describe an enveloping appearance or sensation.

Examples

A velvety blanket of mist
A thick velvety blanket of mist drifted across the hills at dawn.
She wrapped herself in a velvety blanket of comfort after the long day.
Snow fell silently, covering the street in a velvety blanket of white.
