cream-colored

adjective
pale yellowish-white in color
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːm ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈkriːm ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a pale yellowish-white color similar to that of fresh cream

Examples
