bridgeable

adjective
able to be bridged or spanned
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪdʒəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈbrɪdʒəbl̩ / (BrE)
Able to be connected by a bridge, either physically or figuratively.

Examples

Bridgeable gap
The canyon is bridgeable using modern engineering techniques.
The river was only bridgeable at its narrowest point.
There are cultural differences, but they are bridgeable with understanding and communication.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unbridgeable - not able to be bridged or spanned
