wheelman

noun
vehicle driver
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlmæn / (AmE), / ˈwiːlmæn / (BrE)
a person who drives or steers a vehicle, especially as the main driver or helmsman

Examples

Rally wheelman
The wheelman skillfully navigated the winding mountain roads during the rally.
As the designated wheelman, she made sure everyone arrived safely at the event.
During the parade, the wheelman wore a vintage cap to match the classic car.

Related Terms

  • synonym: driver - a person who operates a vehicle
  • synonym: helmsman - a person who steers a ship or boat
  • antonym: passenger - a person who is traveling in a vehicle but is not operating it
