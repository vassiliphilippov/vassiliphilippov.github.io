freeskiing

noun
trick-based skiing
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːˌskiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌskiːɪŋ / (BrE)
a style of skiing that focuses on performing tricks, jumps, and navigating terrain features rather than traditional racing

Examples

Freeskiing tricks
He has been practicing freeskiing for years and can perform impressive aerial tricks.
The resort built a new park specifically for freeskiing enthusiasts to try jumps and rails.
Many young athletes are drawn to freeskiing because of its creativity and excitement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alpine skiing - downhill skiing on marked pistes, usually focused on speed
  • synonym: freestyle skiing - a type of skiing involving tricks, jumps, and stunts
