firestone

noun
piece of flint for lighting fires
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəstəʊn / (BrE)
a piece of flint used to produce sparks for lighting a fire

Examples

A firestone for sparks
Early travelers always carried a firestone to help them make campfires.
The blacksmith struck the firestone against steel to create a shower of sparks.
With only a firestone and some dry leaves, she managed to light a small fire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flint - a hard stone used to produce sparks
