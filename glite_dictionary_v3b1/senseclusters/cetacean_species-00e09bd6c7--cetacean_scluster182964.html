cetacean species

noun
species of whale, dolphin, or porpoise
Pronunciation: / sɪˈteɪʃən ˈspiːʃiz / (AmE), / sɪˈteɪʃən ˈspiːsiːz / (BrE)
Any species of marine mammal belonging to the order Cetacea, including whales, dolphins, and porpoises.

Examples

Cetacean species worldwide
Climate change poses new threats to all cetacean species worldwide.
Many cetacean species migrate thousands of miles to find food or breeding grounds.
Recent surveys have identified several endangered cetacean species in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cetacean - any marine mammal of the order Cetacea
  • synonym: dolphin - a small, toothed marine mammal of the family Delphinidae
  • synonym: whale - large marine mammal of the order Cetacea
