cetacean species

noun
species of whale, dolphin, or porpoise
Pronunciation: / sɪˈteɪʃən ˈspiːʃiz / (AmE), / sɪˈteɪʃən ˈspiːsiːz / (BrE)
Any species of marine mammal belonging to the order Cetacea, including whales, dolphins, and porpoises.
