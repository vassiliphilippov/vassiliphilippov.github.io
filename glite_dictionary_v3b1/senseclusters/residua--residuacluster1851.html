residua

noun
remaining substances
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɪdʒuə / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪdjʊə / (BrE)
the plural form of residuum, meaning the remaining parts or substances left after a process or event

Examples

Chemical residua
After the filtration process, several residua were found at the bottom of the container.
The archaeologists carefully examined the residua left behind in the ancient pottery.
Chemical analysis revealed that the residua contained traces of rare minerals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remnants - small remaining parts of something
  • synonym: residuum - the part that remains after the main part has been removed
