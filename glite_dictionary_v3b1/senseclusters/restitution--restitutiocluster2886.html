Synonyms

  • reparation
    6.8 1.08/m
    reparation is compensation given to make amends for a wrong, while restitution focuses on returning or replacing what was lost.
  • redress
    7.6 1.60/m
    redress is the act of correcting a wrong or giving compensation, while restitution specifically aims to return or restore what was lost.
  • restoration
    3.4 14/m
    restoration is the process of returning something to its former state, while restitution can also mean legal return or compensation for loss.
  • return
    1.0 13/m
    return is the act of giving something back after it is taken or borrowed, while restitution can include compensation or full restoration.
  • amends
    3.5 0.00/m
    amends are actions or payments done to make up for harm, while restitution refers to returning, restoring, or legally compensating for loss.
