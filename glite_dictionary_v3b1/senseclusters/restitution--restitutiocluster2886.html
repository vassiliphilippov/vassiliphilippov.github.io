restitution

noun
restoring to original state
9.9 5.5
Pronunciation: /re.stɪ.ˈtuː.ʃən/ /; / /rə.stɪ.ˈtuː.ʃən/ (AmE), /re.stɪ.ˈtjuː.ʃən/ /; / /re.stɪ.ˈtuː.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of returning something lost or stolen, restoring something to its original state, or compensating for loss or damage
