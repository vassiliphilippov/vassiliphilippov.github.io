immobilizer

noun
medical support device
4.6
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈmoʊ.bə.laɪ.zər/ /; / /ɪ.ˈmoʊ.bɪ.laɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈməʊ.bɪ.laɪ.zə/ /; / /ɪ.ˈməʊ.bə.laɪ.zə/ (BrE)
a medical device such as a splint or brace used to keep a limb or joint from moving to aid healing

Examples

Arm immobilizer brace
The doctor placed an immobilizer on her arm to prevent further injury after the accident.
Wearing an immobilizer helped his knee heal properly after the surgery.
The nurse adjusted the immobilizer to ensure the patient's leg stayed completely still.

Related Terms

  • synonym: splint - a rigid device used to support and immobilize a broken bone
