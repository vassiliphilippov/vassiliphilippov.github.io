fatigue fracture

noun
break caused by repeated stress
5.0
Pronunciation: / fəˈtiɡ ˈfræk.tʃɚ / (AmE), / fəˈtiːɡ ˈfræk.tʃə / (BrE)
a crack or break in a material or bone caused by repeated or prolonged stress rather than a single injury

Examples

A fatigue fracture crack
Regular inspections help detect a fatigue fracture before it leads to catastrophic failure.
The engineer discovered a fatigue fracture in the bridge support beam after years of heavy traffic.
The runner suffered a fatigue fracture in her foot due to continuous long-distance training.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stress fracture - a small crack in a bone caused by repeated force or overuse
