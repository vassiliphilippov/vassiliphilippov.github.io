multisection

noun
division into sections
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈsɛkʃən / (BrE)
the process of dividing or preparing something into several sections, especially for analysis or examination

Examples

Multisection of samples
The laboratory used multisection to analyze different layers of the rock sample.
A careful multisection of the document revealed hidden details in each part.
In medical research, multisection allows scientists to examine tissues from several angles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dissection - the act of cutting something apart for study
  • synonym: segmentation - the division of something into separate parts or sections
