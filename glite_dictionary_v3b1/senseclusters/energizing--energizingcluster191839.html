energizing

adjective
providing energy or motive force
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnərˌdʒaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛnədʒʌɪzɪŋ /; / ˈɛnəˌdʒaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
providing or supplying energy, power, or motive force.

Examples

Energizing music
The coach gave an energizing speech before the game to motivate the team.
The music had an energizing effect on everyone at the party.
This beverage contains energizing ingredients intended to boost alertness and stamina.
A brisk morning walk can have an energising effect on your day.
Her speech was energising and inspired everyone to take action.
The new CEO's energising vision brought fresh motivation to the whole company.
