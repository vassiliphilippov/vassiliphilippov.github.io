conspiracy of silence

noun
deliberate agreement not to discuss something
6.7 6.4
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈspɪr.ə.siː əv ˈsaɪ.ləns/ (AmE), /kən.ˈspɪr.ə.siː əv ˈsaɪ.ləns/ (BrE)
an agreement among people to deliberately avoid speaking about a particular subject, often to protect themselves or others or to hide wrongdoing

Examples

A conspiracy of silence
The community's conspiracy of silence allowed the abuse to continue unchecked.
The government maintained a conspiracy of silence about the incident for years.
There seemed to be a conspiracy of silence among the employees regarding the manager's misconduct.
