frivolously

adverb
in a carefree or superficial way
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪvələsli / (AmE), / ˈfrɪvələsli / (BrE)
In a way that is carefree, superficial, or lacking in seriousness or purpose.

Examples

Spend money frivolously
He spent his money frivolously on expensive clothes and gadgets he didn't need.
She laughed frivolously at jokes that were not particularly funny.
The committee warned against making decisions frivolously without considering the consequences.

Related Terms

  • antonym: seriously - in a thoughtful or earnest manner
