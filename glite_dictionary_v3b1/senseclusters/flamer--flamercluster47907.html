flamer

noun
online provocateur
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪmər / (AmE), / ˈfleɪmə / (BrE)
a person who posts angry, insulting, or inflammatory messages online or in electronic communication

Examples

Internet flamer
The forum moderator warned the persistent flamer to stop posting offensive comments.
After several heated arguments, the flamer was banned from the chat room.
Ignoring a flamer is often the best way to prevent further disruption in online discussions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: peacemaker - a person who tries to prevent or stop conflict
  • synonym: troll - a person who deliberately provokes others online
