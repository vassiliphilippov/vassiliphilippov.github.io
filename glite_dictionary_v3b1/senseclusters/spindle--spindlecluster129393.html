spindle

noun
stick used in spinning yarn
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪn.dl̩ / (AmE), / ˈspɪn.dl̩ / (BrE)
a slender stick or pin used for winding and twisting yarn during spinning

Examples

Wooden spindle for yarn
A wooden spindle lay beside the basket of freshly carded fibers.
Ancient spinners commonly used a drop spindle to turn wool into yarn.
The thread was carefully wound onto the spindle as she worked the spinning wheel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distaff - a staff for holding wool or flax in spinning
