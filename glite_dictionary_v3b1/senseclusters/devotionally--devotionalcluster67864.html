devotionally

adverb
with strong dedication
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvoʊʃənəli / (AmE), / dɪˈvəʊʃənəli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows strong love, loyalty, or dedicated commitment to someone or something

Examples

To care devotionally
She cared for her elderly parents devotionally, always putting their needs before her own.
The volunteers worked devotionally to rebuild the community center after the storm.
He practiced the piano devotionally every morning, hoping to become a concert pianist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faithfully - in a loyal and reliable way
  • antonym: indifferently - without interest or concern
  • synonym: loyally - in a way that shows firm and constant support
