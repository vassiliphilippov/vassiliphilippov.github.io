dental assistant

noun
a person who assists a dentist
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈden.təl ə.ˈsɪs.tənt/ (AmE), /ˈden.təl ə.ˈsɪs.tənt/ (BrE)
a person who assists a dentist by preparing patients, sterilizing instruments, and handling other support tasks in a dental office

Examples

The dental assistant takes X-rays
During her appointment, the dental assistant took X-rays of her teeth.
He trained as a dental assistant before starting his dental studies.
The dental assistant prepared the instruments before the patient arrived.
