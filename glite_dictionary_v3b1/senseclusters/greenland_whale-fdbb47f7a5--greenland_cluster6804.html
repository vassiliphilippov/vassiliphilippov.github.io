Greenland whale

noun
a large Arctic whale (bowhead whale)

Pronunciation: /ˈgriːn.lənd weɪl/ /; / /ˈgriːn.lænd weɪl/ /; / /ˈgriːn.lənd hweɪl/ (AmE), /ˈgriːn.lənd weɪl/ /; / /ˈgriːn.lænd weɪl/ (BrE)
