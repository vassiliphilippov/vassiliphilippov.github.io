doozy of a challenge

noun
an unusually difficult task
Pronunciation: / ˈduzi ʌv ə ˈtʃælɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈduːzi ɒv ə ˈtʃælɪndʒ / (BrE)
an extremely difficult or exceptional challenge or task

Examples

A doozy of a challenge to finish
Completing the marathon in such hot weather was a doozy of a challenge for all the runners.
Rebuilding the old car was a doozy of a challenge, but she managed to finish it.
The final exam turned out to be a real doozy of a challenge, stumping even the best students.
