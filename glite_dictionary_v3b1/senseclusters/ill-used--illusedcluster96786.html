ill-used

adjective
treated unfairly; taken advantage of
9.0
Pronunciation: / ɪlˈjuːzd / (AmE), / ɪlˈjuːzd / (BrE)
(of a person) treated unfairly; taken advantage of or subjected to abuse or neglect.

Examples

An ill-used person

She felt ill-used after her hard work was ignored by her manager.
The ill-used employees finally decided to speak out against their boss.
