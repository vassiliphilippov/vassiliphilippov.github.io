premenstrually

adverb
before menstruation
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈmɛnstruəli / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈmɛnstruəli / (BrE)
in the time or manner relating to the days before menstruation

Examples

Feel premenstrually tired
She often feels more tired premenstrually than at other times of the month.
Some people notice increased irritability premenstrually due to hormonal changes.
Headaches can occur premenstrually as part of premenstrual syndrome.

Related Terms

  • antonym: menstrually - during menstruation
  • synonym: premenstrual - relating to the days before menstruation
