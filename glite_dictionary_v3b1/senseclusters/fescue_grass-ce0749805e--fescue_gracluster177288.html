fescue grass

noun
type of pasture and lawn grass
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛskju ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈfɛskju ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A type of grass with wide, flat leaves, cultivated in Europe and America for lawns, pasture, and hay.

Examples

Fescue grass lawn
Because of its drought resistance, fescue grass is popular in home gardens.
Cattle often graze on fescue grass in the summer pastures.
The landscaper recommended planting fescue grass for a lush and durable lawn.
