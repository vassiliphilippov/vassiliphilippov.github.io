hieroglyphics

noun
ancient picture writing
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪks / (AmE), / ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪks / (BrE)
a system of writing using pictures or symbols, especially that of ancient Egypt

Examples

Ancient Egyptian hieroglyphics
The ancient Egyptians used hieroglyphics to record important events on temple walls.
Scholars spent years trying to decode the meaning of hieroglyphics found in the pyramids.
The museum displayed a stone tablet covered in mysterious hieroglyphics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alphabet - a set of letters used in a language's writing system
  • synonym: pictograph - a symbol representing a word or idea in picture form
