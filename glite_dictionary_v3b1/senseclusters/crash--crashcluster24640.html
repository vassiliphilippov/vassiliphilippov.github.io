crash

noun
computer/system failure
1.7 3.5
Pronunciation: /kræʃ/ (AmE), /kræʃ/ (BrE)
an unexpected event that causes a computer or system to stop working suddenly and become inoperative

Examples

A computer crash
A sudden power outage can trigger a crash of the database.
After the crash, the IT team worked quickly to restore the servers.
The system suffered a crash just as I was about to save my work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: failure - state of not working or performing properly
  • synonym: malfunction - failure to function properly
  • antonym: restart - to start something again after stopping
