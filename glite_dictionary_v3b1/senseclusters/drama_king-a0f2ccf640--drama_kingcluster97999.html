drama king

noun
man who exaggerates emotions for effect
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑː.mə kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrɑː.mə kɪŋ / (BrE)
a man or boy who exaggerates emotions or reactions, especially to attract attention

Examples

A drama king complains
His friends told him not to act like a drama king after the minor disagreement.
Stop being such a drama king every time something goes wrong at work.
Whenever he loses at chess, he turns into a real drama king.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drama queen - woman or girl who exaggerates emotions for attention
