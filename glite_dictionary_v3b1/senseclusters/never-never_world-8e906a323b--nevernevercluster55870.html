never-never world

noun
imaginary or unrealistic place or state
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɛvɚ ˈnɛvɚ wɝld / (AmE), / ˌnɛvə ˈnɛvə wɜːld / (BrE)
An unrealistic or imaginary place or state, often detached from practical reality.

Examples

Living in a never-never world
He accused the government of living in a never-never world rather than facing hard economic facts.
Some people escape their problems by retreating into a never-never world of daydreams.
The author creates a never-never world where anything is possible and nothing is real.
