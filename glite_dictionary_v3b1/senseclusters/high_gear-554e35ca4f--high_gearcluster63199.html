high gear

noun
vehicle gear for fastest speed
6.3
Pronunciation: / haɪ ɡɪr / (AmE), / haɪ ɡɪə / (BrE)
a forward gear in a vehicle's transmission that allows for the highest speed relative to engine revolutions

Examples

High gear in car
Climbing a steep hill is difficult if you stay in high gear.
Once the car reached the highway, he shifted into high gear for better fuel efficiency.
The cyclist switched to high gear as he sped down the slope.

Related Terms

  • antonym: low gear - a gear with the lowest speed and greatest torque in a vehicle
