matchet

noun
large knife for cutting vegetation
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmætʃɪt / (AmE), / ˈmatʃɪt / (BrE)
A large, heavy knife used as a weapon or for cutting through vegetation, similar to a machete.

Examples

A sharp matchet
He kept a sharp matchet handy while working in the fields for protection and clearing brush.
The farmer used a matchet to clear the thick undergrowth along the path.
With a single swing of the matchet, she cut through the tough vines blocking the entrance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: machete - A broad, heavy knife used for cutting vegetation; very similar tool
