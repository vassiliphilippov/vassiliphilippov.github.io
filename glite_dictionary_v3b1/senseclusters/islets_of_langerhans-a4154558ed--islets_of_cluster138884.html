islets of Langerhans

noun
hormone-secreting pancreatic cell clusters
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪlɪts əv ˈlæŋərˌhænz / (AmE), / ˈaɪlɪts əv ˈlæŋəhænz / (BrE)
Groups of cells in the pancreas that produce and secrete hormones such as insulin.

Examples

Islets of Langerhans cells

Researchers studied the islets of Langerhans to better understand hormone secretion in the pancreas.
The islets of Langerhans regulate blood sugar by releasing insulin into the bloodstream.
