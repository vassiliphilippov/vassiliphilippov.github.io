conterminous

adjective
sharing a boundary; adjacent
9.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɜr.mə.nəs /; / kənˈtɜrmənəs / (AmE), / kɒnˈtɜː.mɪ.nəs /; / kənˈtɜːmɪnəs / (BrE)
sharing a common boundary or being directly next to something; adjacent or touching

Examples

Conterminous properties
Her property is conterminous with the city park, making her backyard especially scenic.
The two countries are conterminous along their western borders.
These districts are conterminous with the river, following its twists and turns.
Most European Union states are conterminous with one or more neighboring countries.
The 48 conterminous states in the US exclude Alaska and Hawaii.
The conterminous countries maintained peace along their common border.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contiguous - sharing a common border; touching
  • antonym: separate - not touching or remaining apart
