bouquet

noun
arrangement of flowers given as a gift
1.5
Pronunciation: / boʊˈkeɪ / (AmE), / buːˈkeɪ / (BrE)
A carefully arranged collection of flowers, typically given as a gift or carried on special occasions.

Examples

A bouquet of roses
He handed her a bouquet to celebrate her promotion.
She received a beautiful bouquet of roses for her birthday.
The bride held a white bouquet as she walked down the aisle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: posy - a small bunch of flowers
