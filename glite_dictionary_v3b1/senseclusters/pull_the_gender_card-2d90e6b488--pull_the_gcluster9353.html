pull the gender card

verb
invoke gender for advantage or sympathy
4.1
Pronunciation: / pʊl ðə ˈdʒɛndər kɑrd / (AmE), / pʊl ðə ˈdʒɛndə kɑːd / (BrE)
to mention or emphasize gender, often to gain advantage, sympathy, or to deflect criticism in an argument or situation
