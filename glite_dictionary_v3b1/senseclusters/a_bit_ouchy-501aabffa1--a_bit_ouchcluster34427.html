a bit ouchy

adjective
slightly painful or uncomfortable
Pronunciation: / ə bɪt ˈaʊtʃi / (AmE), / ə bɪt ˈaʊtʃi / (BrE)
Slightly painful or uncomfortable; feeling a mild sensation of pain.

Examples

A bit ouchy tooth
After the dentist visit, my tooth felt a bit ouchy all afternoon.
Getting your ears pierced can be a bit ouchy at first.
That scrape on my knee is a bit ouchy, but I’ll be fine.
