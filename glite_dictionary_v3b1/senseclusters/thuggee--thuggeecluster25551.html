thuggee

noun
organized robbery and murder (historical, India)
8.6
Pronunciation: / θʌˈgi / (AmE), / θʌˈgiː / (BrE)
A system of organized robbery and murder carried out by criminal bands, especially in India during the 18th and 19th centuries.

Examples

The thuggee system
During the British colonial period, authorities tried to suppress thuggee throughout India.
Scholars have studied the history of thuggee and its impact on Indian society.
The word thuggee describes ritualistic murder and theft performed by organized gangs.
