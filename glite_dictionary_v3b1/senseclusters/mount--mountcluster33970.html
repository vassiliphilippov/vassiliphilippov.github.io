mount

noun
large hill or mountain
Pronunciation: / maʊnt / (AmE), / maʊnt / (BrE)
A large hill or mountain; a land mass that rises well above its surroundings.

Examples

Snowy mount
From her window, she could see the snow-capped mount in the distance.
Many sacred temples are built on the summit of a mount.
The trek up the mount was challenging, but the view from the top was breathtaking.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: hill - a naturally raised area of land, not as high or craggy as a mountain
  • synonym: mountain - a large natural elevation of the earth's surface
