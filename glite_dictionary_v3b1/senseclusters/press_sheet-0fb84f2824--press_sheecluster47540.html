press sheet

noun
printed sheet before folding or cutting
4.9
Pronunciation: / prɛs ʃit / (AmE), / prɛs ʃiːt / (BrE)
a sheet of paper printed during a press run and before it has been folded or cut

Examples

A press sheet page
A single press sheet contains multiple pages of a publication arranged for later assembly.
Collectors sometimes seek out rare stamps on uncut press sheets from the post office.
The printer inspected each press sheet for quality before sending it along for folding.
