hop-up

noun
airsoft device that gives backspin
Pronunciation: / hɑp ʌp / (AmE), / hɒp ʌp / (BrE)
(in airsoft) a device or adjustable mechanism in an airsoft gun that imparts backspin to plastic BBs to stabilise their flight and increase range and accuracy.

Examples

An upgraded hop-up unit
An upgraded hop-up unit in the airsoft rifle increased long-range accuracy.
She adjusted the hop-up carefully to give the BBs backspin and improve range.
New players often forget to tune the hop-up, which makes shooting inconsistent at distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hop-up unit - the same component or assembly in an airsoft gun that applies backspin to BBs
