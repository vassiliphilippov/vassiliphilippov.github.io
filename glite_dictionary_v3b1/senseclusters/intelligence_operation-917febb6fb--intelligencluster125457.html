intelligence operation

noun
covert mission to gather information
3.0 4.0
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈtel.ə.dʒəns ɑː.pər.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈtel.ə.dʒəns ɒp.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
