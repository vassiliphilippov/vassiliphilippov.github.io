confirmer

noun
person who verifies
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɜrmər / (AmE), / kənˈfɜːmə / (BrE)
a person or thing that confirms or verifies something as true or correct

Examples

The confirmer checks details
The confirmer must check all the details before approval.
Each transaction requires a confirmer to ensure accuracy and prevent errors.
As the official confirmer, she signed off on the final report.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approver - a person who gives official agreement or permission
  • antonym: denier - a person who refuses to accept or admit something
  • synonym: verifier - a person who checks or proves something is true
