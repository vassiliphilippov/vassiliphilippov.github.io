leadwort family

noun
family of salt-tolerant herbs and shrubs (Plumbaginaceae)
Pronunciation: / ˈliːdˌwɔrt ˈfæm.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈliːdwɜːt ˈfæm.ɪ.li / (BrE)
a family of perennial herbs, shrubs, and vines (Plumbaginaceae), found worldwide and especially common in saltwater habitats
