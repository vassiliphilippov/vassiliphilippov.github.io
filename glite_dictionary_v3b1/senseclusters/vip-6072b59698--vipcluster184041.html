VIP

noun
very important or influential person
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˌviːˌaɪˈpiː / (AmE), / ˌviːˌaɪˈpiː / (BrE)

Examples

A VIP guest

Only a few VIPs were allowed behind the scenes at the concert.
They created a separate lounge for VIPs attending the conference.
