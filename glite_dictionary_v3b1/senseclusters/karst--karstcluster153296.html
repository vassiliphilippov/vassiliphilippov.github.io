karst

adjective
relating to limestone terrain
8.6
Pronunciation: /kɑːrst/ (AmE), /kɑːst/ (BrE)
relating to landscapes or features formed by the dissolution of soluble rocks such as limestone, including caves and sinkholes

Examples

Karst caves
The region is famous for its karst caves and underground rivers.
Researchers mapped the karst terrain to study how water moves through the rock.
Many unique plants grow in the karst landscape due to its special soil and drainage.
