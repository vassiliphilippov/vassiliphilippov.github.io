subsistence

noun
means of maintaining life or survival
4.7
Pronunciation: / səbˈsɪstəns / (AmE), / səbˈsɪstəns / (BrE)
the means or resources needed to support life, especially at a basic or minimal level

Examples

Subsistence farming
After the war, food shortages made basic subsistence a daily struggle.
Many rural families depend on fishing for their subsistence.
The villagers relied on agriculture as their primary source of subsistence.
After losing his job, he struggled to maintain even basic subsistence for his family.
In the remote village, farming was mainly for subsistence rather than profit.
With such a low wage, many families are forced to live at a level of subsistence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: livelihood - means of securing the necessities of life
  • degree lower: poverty - the state of being extremely poor
  • degree higher: prosperity - the state of being successful and having plentiful resources
  • synonym: survival - the state or fact of continuing to live
