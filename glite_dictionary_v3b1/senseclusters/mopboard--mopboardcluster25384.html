mopboard

noun
molding along base of interior wall
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑpˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈmɒpˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a narrow strip of molding that covers the joint between a wall and the floor, protecting the wall from knocks and dirt

Examples

White mopboards
After painting the walls, she installed new white mopboards for a cleaner finish.
Dirt often collects where the mopboard meets the floor, so be sure to clean thoroughly.
The old house still had its original mopboards along every wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baseboard - a board covering the lowest part of an interior wall
  • synonym: skirting board - British term for baseboard
