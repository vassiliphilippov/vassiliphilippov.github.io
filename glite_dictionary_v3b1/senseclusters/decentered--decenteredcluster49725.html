decentered

adjective
not centered
6.5
Pronunciation: / diˈsɛntərd / (AmE), / diˈsɛntəd / (BrE)
physically not aligned with the geometric center or axis; positioned off-center or misaligned

Examples

Decentered lens
The lens was slightly decentered, causing the image to appear blurry on one side.
Engineers corrected the decentered shaft to ensure smooth operation of the machine.
A decentered wheel can lead to uneven tire wear and vibration while driving.

Related Terms

  • antonym: centered - positioned at or aligned with the center
  • synonym: misaligned - not correctly aligned or positioned
