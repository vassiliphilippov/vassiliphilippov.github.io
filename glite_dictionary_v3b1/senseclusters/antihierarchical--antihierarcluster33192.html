antihierarchical

noun
opponent of hierarchy
9.9
Pronunciation: /æn.tɪ.haɪ.ə.ˈrɑː.kɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.haɪ.ə.ˈrɑː.kɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
a person who opposes hierarchical authority or systems and supports non-hierarchical organization

Examples

An antihierarchical leader
As an antihierarchical, he advocated for equal participation in all group decisions.
The organization attracted many antihierarchical thinkers who challenged traditional power structures.
She was known as an antihierarchical in her efforts to promote flat management in the company.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hierarchist - a person who supports hierarchical systems
