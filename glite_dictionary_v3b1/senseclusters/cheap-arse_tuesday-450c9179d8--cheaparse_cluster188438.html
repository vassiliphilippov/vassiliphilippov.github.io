cheap-arse Tuesday

noun
discounted day (often cinema)
Pronunciation: / tʃip ɑrs ˈtuːz.deɪ / (AmE), / tʃiːp ɑːs ˈtjuːz.deɪ / (BrE)
An informal term for a day, usually Tuesday, when entertainment venues such as cinemas offer discounted prices.

Examples

Cheap-arse Tuesday discounts
Some restaurants also offer half-price meals on cheap-arse Tuesday.
The cinema is packed on cheap-arse Tuesday because everyone wants a deal.
We planned our outing specifically for cheap-arse Tuesday to save on tickets.
