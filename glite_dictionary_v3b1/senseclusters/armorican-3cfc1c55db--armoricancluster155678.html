Armorican

noun
person from Armorica
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈmɔːrɪkən / (AmE), / ɑːˈmɒrɪkən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Armorica, an ancient region in northwestern France, especially a member of its Celtic people

Examples

An Armorican tribe
The Armorican tribes played a significant role in resisting Roman expansion.
Artifacts from an Armorican settlement were discovered near the coast of Brittany.
Historians believe that the Armorican people had strong cultural ties with the Celts of Britain.
