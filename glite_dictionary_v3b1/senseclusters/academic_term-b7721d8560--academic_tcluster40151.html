academic term

noun
school or university session period
2.5
Pronunciation: / əˈkædəmɪk tɝm / (AmE), / əˈkadəmɪk tɜːm / (BrE)
A period during which a school, college, or university holds classes.

Examples

A academic term period
Many universities operate on a three academic term schedule each year.
Students must register for courses before the new academic term starts.
The academic term usually begins in early September and ends in December.

  • synonym: term - period during which instruction is given in a school or university
