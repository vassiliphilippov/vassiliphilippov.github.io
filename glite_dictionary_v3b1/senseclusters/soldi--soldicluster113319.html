soldi

noun
old Italian coin
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊldi / (AmE), / ˈsɒldi / (BrE)
an old Italian coin of small value, used in Italy in past centuries

Examples

Ancient soldi coins
In the old village market, a loaf of bread cost only a few soldi.
Collectors sometimes find rare soldi among ancient Italian coins.
He saved his soldi to buy a small gift for his family.

Related Terms
