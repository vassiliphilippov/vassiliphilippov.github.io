weld

noun
a fused metal joint
4.9
Pronunciation: / wɛld / (AmE), / wɛld / (BrE)
A joint between pieces of metal formed by heating them until they fuse or by hammering them together.

Examples

A strong weld
A high-quality weld is essential for structural safety.
After cooling, the weld held the two steel plates firmly together.
The weld was inspected for any cracks or weak points.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joint - the place where two things are joined
