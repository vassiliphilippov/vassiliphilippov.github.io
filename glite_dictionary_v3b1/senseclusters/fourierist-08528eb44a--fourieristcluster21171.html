Fourierist

noun
follower of Fourier
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊriərɪst / (AmE), / ˈfʊəriərɪst / (BrE)
a supporter or follower of the social theories of Charles Fourier, a French utopian socialist

Examples

A Fourierist thinker
The nineteenth-century community was established by a group of dedicated Fourierists.
As a Fourierist, she believed in reorganizing society into cooperative communities.
Many Fourierists gathered to discuss their vision for a better world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: socialist - a person who supports or advocates socialism
  • synonym: utopian - a person who believes in or pursues idealistic social reforms
