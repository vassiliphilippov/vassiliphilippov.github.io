statutorily

adverb
in accordance with statute
6.6
Pronunciation: / stəˈtjuːt̬əˌrɪli / (AmE), / stəˈtjuːtərɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is required or allowed by statute (a written law)

Examples

To statutorily require
Certain responsibilities are statutorily delegated to the local council.
The board acted statutorily when making that decision.
The company was statutorily required to submit annual reports to the authorities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: legally - in a way that is allowed by law
