blitzkrieg strategy

noun
fast and intense campaign or effort
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪtsˌkriɡ ˈstræt̬ədʒi / (AmE), / ˈblɪtskriːɡ ˈstrætədʒi / (BrE)
a fast and intense campaign or effort, often intended to achieve rapid results

Examples

A blitzkrieg strategy campaign
The company used a blitzkrieg strategy to outpace competitors during the holiday season.
The marketing team adopted a blitzkrieg strategy to capture market share quickly.
Their blitzkrieg strategy left little time for rivals to respond to the new product launch.
