bill

noun
draft law presented to a legislature
2.6 3.4
Pronunciation: /bɪl/ (AmE), /bɪl/ (BrE)
A written proposal for a new law that is presented for debate and approval before a legislature.

Examples

A new bill in Congress
After several debates, the bill finally passed in the House of Representatives.
If the bill is approved by both chambers, it becomes a law.
The senator introduced a bill to improve environmental protection.
