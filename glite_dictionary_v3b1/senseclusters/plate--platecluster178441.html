plate

verb
to arrange food
Pronunciation: / pleɪt / (AmE), / pleɪt / (BrE)
to arrange and present food attractively on a plate for serving

Examples

To plate a meal
The chef plated the fish carefully, adding a lemon wedge and microgreens for garnish.
She plates each dessert with artistic precision before serving it to the guests.
After cooking the steak, he plated it alongside roasted vegetables and mashed potatoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dish up - to put food onto plates for eating
  • synonym: serve - to present food to someone at a meal
