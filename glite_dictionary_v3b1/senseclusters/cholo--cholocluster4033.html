cholo

noun
offensive ethnic slur
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊləʊ / (BrE)
an offensive slang term for a person of Mexican descent, especially in the United States; considered derogatory and should not be used

Examples

Offensive cholo slur
The word cholo is considered highly offensive and should not be used in conversation.
