(British, informal) Describing a room or atmosphere that is warm, stuffy, and lacking fresh air.

Examples

A fuggy room
After the meeting, the boardroom became unbearably fuggy and oppressive.
She opened the windows to get rid of the fuggy atmosphere in the living room.
The small kitchen felt fuggy after hours of cooking with the windows closed.
