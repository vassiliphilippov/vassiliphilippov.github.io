hearty laugh

noun
loud and cheerful laugh
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrti læf / (AmE), / ˈhɑːti lɑːf / (BrE)
a loud, cheerful, and sincere laugh

Examples

A hearty laugh echoed
A hearty laugh echoed down the hallway after he heard the good news.
His hearty laugh was contagious and soon everyone joined in.
She greeted us with a hearty laugh and a warm smile.
