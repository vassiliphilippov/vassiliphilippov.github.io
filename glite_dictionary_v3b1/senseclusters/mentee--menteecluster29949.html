mentee

noun
person being mentored
Pronunciation: / mɛnˈti / (AmE), / mɛnˈtiː / (BrE)
a person who is advised, trained, or counseled by a mentor

Examples

A mentee meets mentor
As a mentee, she met with her mentor once a month for guidance.
Each mentee is assigned a different advisor based on their interests.
The company launched a program that pairs every new employee as a mentee with a senior staff member.
