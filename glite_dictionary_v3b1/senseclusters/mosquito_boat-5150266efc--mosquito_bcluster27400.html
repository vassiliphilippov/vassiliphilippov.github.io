mosquito boat

noun
small fast torpedo boat (military)
Pronunciation: / məˈskoʊˌtoʊ ˌboʊt / (AmE), / məˈskiːtəʊ bəʊt / (BrE)
a small, fast, unarmored and lightly armed torpedo boat, especially used for patrol and attack purposes during wartime

Examples

A mosquito boat patrol
A mosquito boat could outmaneuver larger ships thanks to its size and agility.
During World War II, the navy relied on mosquito boats for high-speed coastal attacks.
The harbor base kept several mosquito boats ready for night patrols.

Related Terms

  • synonym: PT boat - patrol torpedo boat, a fast attack craft used by the US Navy
