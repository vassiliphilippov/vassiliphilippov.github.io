religious mysticism

noun
mystical approach to religion
5.3 7.4
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈmɪstɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈmɪstɪsɪzm / (BrE)
A form of religion or spiritual practice centered on achieving a direct, personal communion with an ultimate reality or divine presence through mystical experiences.
