probabilistic classifier

noun
classifier outputting class probabilities
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊbəˈbɪlɪstɪk klæˈsɪfaɪər / (AmE), / ˌprɒbəˈbɪlɪstɪk klɑːˈsɪfʌɪə / (BrE)
a statistical or machine learning model that assigns probabilities to different classes for a given input, rather than making a single definitive classification

Examples

A probabilistic classifier model
A probabilistic classifier can tell you not only the predicted class but also the likelihood of each possibility.
Training a probabilistic classifier requires models that can output probability distributions, such as logistic regression.
When dealing with uncertain data, a probabilistic classifier is often preferred over a deterministic one.

Related Terms

  • synonym: classifier - a model or algorithm that assigns items to categories or classes
