rise and shine

verb
wake up and be cheerful
1.0
Pronunciation: / raɪz ənd ʃaɪn / (AmE), / raɪz ənd ʃaɪn / (BrE)
used to tell someone to wake up and start the day in a cheerful or energetic way

Examples

To rise and shine early
No matter how tired he felt, he would still rise and shine every day for work.
Parents often say rise and shine to their children in the morning.
When my alarm goes off, I tell myself to rise and shine and start the day positively.
