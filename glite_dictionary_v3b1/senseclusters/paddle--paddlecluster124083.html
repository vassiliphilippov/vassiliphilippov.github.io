paddle

noun
flat board used for punishment
Pronunciation: / ˈpæd.əl / (AmE), / ˈpæd.əl / (BrE)
a flat wooden board used as an instrument for corporal punishment, especially in schools or institutions

Examples

School paddle
Corporal punishment with a paddle is now banned in most schools.
He was punished with a paddle for breaking the school rules.
The principal kept a paddle in his office for disciplining students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cane - slender stick used as an instrument of corporal punishment
  • synonym: switch - thin flexible rod used for corporal punishment
