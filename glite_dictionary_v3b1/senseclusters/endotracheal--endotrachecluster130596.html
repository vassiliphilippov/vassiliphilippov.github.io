endotracheal

adjective
inside the trachea
Pronunciation: / ˌɛndoʊˈtreɪkiəl / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊˈtreɪkɪəl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring within the trachea (windpipe)

Examples

Endotracheal tube
The doctor inserted an endotracheal tube to help the patient breathe during surgery.
Proper endotracheal suctioning is essential to prevent infection in intensive care units.
An endotracheal approach is often used for administering anesthesia in major operations.
