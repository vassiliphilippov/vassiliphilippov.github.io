autostylic

adjective
jaw fused to skull
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɔː.tə.ˈstaɪ.lɪk/ /; / /ɔː.toʊ.ˈstaɪ.lɪk/ (AmE), /ɔː.tə.ˈstaɪ.lɪk/ (BrE)
describing a jaw structure in which the upper jaw is fused directly to the skull

Examples

An autostylic jaw
In some fish, the autostylic jaw provides a rigid connection to the skull.
The evolution of autostylic jaws marked a significant change in vertebrate anatomy.
Unlike sharks, lungfish possess an autostylic jaw structure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hyostylic - having a jaw structure supported mainly by the hyomandibular bone
