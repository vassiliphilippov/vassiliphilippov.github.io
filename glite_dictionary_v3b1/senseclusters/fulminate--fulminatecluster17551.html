fulminate

verb
to cause to explode loudly
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊl.məˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈfʌl.mɪ.neɪt / (BrE)
to cause to explode suddenly and with a loud noise

Examples

To fulminate an explosive
Some compounds fulminate when subjected to extreme heat or pressure.
The alchemist warned that the chemical mixture could fulminate if mishandled.
Tiny crystals may fulminate with a deafening blast during experiments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: explode - to burst or shatter violently as a result of rapid combustion or decomposition
