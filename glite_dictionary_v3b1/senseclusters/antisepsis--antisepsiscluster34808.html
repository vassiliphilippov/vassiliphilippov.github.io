antisepsis

noun
prevention of infection by eliminating microbes
5.8 5.6
Pronunciation: /æn.tɪ.ˈsep.sɪs/ /; / /æn.tə.ˈsep.sɪs/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.ˈsep.sɪs/ /; / /æn.tə.ˈsep.sɪs/ (BrE)
the process or practice of preventing infection by eliminating or stopping the growth of harmful microorganisms
