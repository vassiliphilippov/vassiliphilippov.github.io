in a way that does not attempt to impress others or show off; simply and modestly.

Examples

To speak unpretentiously
She spoke unpretentiously about her achievements, never boasting despite her success.
The restaurant is unpretentiously decorated, creating a welcoming atmosphere for everyone.
Their home was unpretentiously furnished, focusing on comfort rather than style.
