pashmina

noun
fine Himalayan wool
7.5
Pronunciation: / pæʃˈmiːnə / (AmE), / pæʃˈmiːnə / (BrE)
a fine, soft wool from Himalayan goats, often used to make luxurious shawls and scarves

Examples

Pashmina wool
She wrapped a delicate pashmina around her shoulders to keep warm on the cool evening.
The market was famous for its colorful pashmina scarves and shawls.
Artisans carefully hand-weave each pashmina to create luxurious, lightweight fabric.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cashmere - a soft wool from Kashmir goats, similar to pashmina
  • synonym: wool - the soft, curly hair from sheep or similar animals used for textiles
