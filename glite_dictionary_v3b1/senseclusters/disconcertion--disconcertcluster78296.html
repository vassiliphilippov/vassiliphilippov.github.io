disconcertion

noun
anxious embarrassment or confusion
7.1 7.1
Pronunciation: /dɪs.kən.ˈsɜːr.ʃən/ (AmE), /dɪs.kən.ˈsɜː.ʃən/ (BrE)
A feeling of anxious embarrassment or confusion caused by unexpected events or circumstances.

Examples

Feel disconcertion suddenly
He could not hide his disconcertion when his name was unexpectedly called.
His sudden outburst caused a moment of disconcertion among the guests.
The awkward silence in the room added to her disconcertion.
