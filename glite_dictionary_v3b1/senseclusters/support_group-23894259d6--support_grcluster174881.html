support group

noun
group for mutual help and encouragement
1.0
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrt ɡruːp / (AmE), / səˈpɔːt ɡruːp / (BrE)
a group of people who meet regularly to give each other help and encouragement, especially concerning a shared problem or difficult experience

Examples

A support group meeting
After her diagnosis, Maria joined a support group for cancer survivors.
Many parents of autistic children find comfort in attending a local support group.
The online support group offers a safe space for sharing experiences and advice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-help group - a group organized to provide mutual assistance for members facing similar issues
