queer

adjective
homosexual; related to sexual/gender minority
Pronunciation: / kwɪr / (AmE), / kwɪə / (BrE)
(of a person) homosexual, or related to same-sex attraction; sometimes used to describe anything differing from heterosexual or cisgender norms, often reclaimed as a positive or neutral term within LGBTQ+ communities.

Examples

Queer identity
Historically, queer was used as a slur, but many now use it with pride to express their identity.
Some people identify as queer rather than using more specific labels like gay or lesbian.
The film was praised for its positive portrayal of queer relationships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gay - homosexual (especially of a man)
  • antonym: straight - heterosexual person
