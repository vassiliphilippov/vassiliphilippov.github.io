civic responsibility

noun
duties expected of a citizen
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvɪk rɪˌspɑnsəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈsɪvɪk rɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the duties and obligations expected of a citizen in society, such as obeying laws and participating in civic activities

Examples

Civic responsibility duties
Educating oneself about current events is part of being aware of one's civic responsibility.
Jury duty is one example of a civic responsibility in many countries.
Voting in elections is often considered a key civic responsibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: civil duty - the responsibilities expected of individuals as members of society
