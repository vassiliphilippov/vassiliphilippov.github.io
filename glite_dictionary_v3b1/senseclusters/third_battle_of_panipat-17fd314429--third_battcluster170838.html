Third Battle of Panipat

proper noun
major 1761 battle in India between Marathas and Afghans
Pronunciation: / θɜrd ˈbætəl əv ˈpanɪˌpɑt / (AmE), / θɜːd ˈbætl əv ˈpænɪˌpɑːt / (BrE)
the major battle fought in 1761 near Panipat, India, in which the Afghan ruler Ahmad Shah Abdali defeated the Maratha Empire, significantly changing the power balance in the Indian subcontinent

Examples

The Third Battle of Panipat
During a history lecture, the professor explained the causes and consequences of the Third Battle of Panipat.

The defeat of the Maratha army in the Third Battle of Panipat led to a significant decline in their power.
