organicity

noun
natural coherence
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrɡəˈnɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡəˈnɪsəti / (BrE)
the quality of being unified and coherent, or of developing naturally rather than being artificially imposed

Examples

Organicity of a group
The organicity of the team's collaboration led to innovative solutions.
Critics praised the organicity of the film's plot and character development.
She admired the organicity of the garden, where every plant seemed to belong.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artificiality - the quality of being made or produced by humans rather than occurring naturally
  • synonym: coherence - the quality of being logical and consistent
  • synonym: unity - the state of being joined as a whole
