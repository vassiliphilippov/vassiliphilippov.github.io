wee dog

noun
a small dog (esp. in Scottish/Irish English)
Pronunciation: / wi dɔg / (AmE), / wi dɒg / (BrE)
A small dog; often used in Scottish or Irish English to refer affectionately to a little dog.

Examples

A wee dog
A wee dog barked excitedly at the children passing by.
He rescued a lost wee dog from the side of the road.
She always travels with her wee dog curled up on her lap.

Related Terms

  • synonym: small dog - a dog that is small in size
