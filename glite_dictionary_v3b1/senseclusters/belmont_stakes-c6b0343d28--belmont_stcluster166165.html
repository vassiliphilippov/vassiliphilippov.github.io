Belmont Stakes

noun
annual prestigious American horse race
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛl.mɑnt steɪks / (AmE), / ˈbɛl.mɒnt steɪks / (BrE)
An annual horse race for three-year-old Thoroughbreds held in Elmont, New York, and the third and final leg of the U.S. Triple Crown series.
