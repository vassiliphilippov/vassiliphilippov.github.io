Satanism

noun
worship of Satan or devils
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪ.tənˌɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈseɪ.tən.ɪz.əm / (BrE)
the worship of Satan or devils, or beliefs and practices associated with such veneration

Examples

Satanism worship
Books exploring the history of Satanism often discuss its cultural impact in the twentieth century.

Some modern forms of Satanism do not involve literal worship of Satan but rather focus on individualism.
