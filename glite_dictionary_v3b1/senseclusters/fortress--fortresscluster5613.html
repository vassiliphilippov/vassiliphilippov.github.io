fortress

noun
strong defensive structure
4.4 5.41/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf…îrtr…ôs / (AmE), / Ààf…îÀêtr…ôs / (BrE)
A large, strong building or group of buildings designed to be defended from attack.

Examples

A fortress wall
During the siege, soldiers took refuge within the walls of the fortress.
The ancient city was protected by a massive fortress overlooking the valley.
They explored the abandoned fortress on the hill, imagining its former glory.

Synonyms

  • fort
    2.3 7.10/m
    a fort is a military building or place for defense, while a fortress is larger and often a group of defended buildings.
  • citadel
    7.7 2.26/m
    a citadel is a strong fortress built in or near a city for shelter, while a fortress is any large defended building.
  • stronghold
    5.5 3.33/m
    a stronghold is any fortified place or center of power, while a fortress is specifically a large defended building.

Related Terms
