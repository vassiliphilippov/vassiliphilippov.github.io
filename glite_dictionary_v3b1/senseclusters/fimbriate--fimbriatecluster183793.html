fimbriate

verb
to add a fringe
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪmbriˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪmbriˌeɪt / (BrE)
to form or add a fringe or narrow border to something; to edge with a fringe.

Examples

To fimbriate the fabric
The artisan fimbriated the fabric's edge to create a soft fringe for the ceremonial sash.
The designer fimbriated the curtains to give them a more decorative appearance.
Ancient garments were often fimbriated to signify status or rank.

  • synonym: trim - to decorate the edge of something
