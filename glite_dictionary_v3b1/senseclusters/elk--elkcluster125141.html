elk

noun
large European/Asian deer
6.6
Pronunciation: / ɛlk / (AmE), / ɛlk / (BrE)
a large deer with broad, flat antlers, found in Europe and Asia (called 'moose' in North America)

Examples

European elk
During the autumn, male elk can be heard calling across the valleys.
The dense forests are home to the majestic elk.
We spotted a herd of elk grazing near the river at dawn.
In North America, the animal known as a moose is called an elk in Europe.
The male elk's massive antlers are shed and regrown each year.
We saw an elk crossing the forest road during our vacation in Sweden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moose - large North American deer, called 'elk' in Europe
