Clement VII

proper noun
pope from 1523 to 1534; opposed Henry VIII's divorce
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkle.mənt ðə ˈse.vənθ/ /; / /ˈkle.mənt ˈse.vən/ (AmE), /ˈkle.mənt ðə ˈse.vənθ/ /; / /ˈkle.mənt ˈse.vən/ (BrE)
the pope of the Roman Catholic Church from 1523 to 1534, known for his conflict with Henry VIII over the king's divorce and subsequent marriage to Anne Boleyn.
