March

noun
the third month of the year
1.0
Pronunciation: / mɑrtʃ / (AmE), / mɑːtʃ / (BrE)
The third month of the year, following February and preceding April.

Examples

March month
Her birthday is in March, when the weather starts to get warmer.
Many flowers begin to bloom during the month of March.
The school year usually ends in late March in some countries.
