Colored People Time

noun
(offensive) stereotype about Black people and tardiness
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌl.ərd ˈpiː.pəl taɪm/ (AmE), /ˈkʌl.əd ˈpiː.pəl taɪm/ (BrE)
(offensive) A humorous or stereotypical term referring to the perception that Black people are habitually late or have a relaxed attitude toward punctuality, sometimes used within the Black community, but considered offensive.
