nondirectional

adjective
not aimed one way
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.dəˈrɛk.ʃən.əl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dɪˈrɛk.ʃən.əl / (BrE)
not limited to or aimed in any particular direction

Examples

A nondirectional beacon
The airport uses a nondirectional beacon to help guide pilots.
This microphone is nondirectional, so it picks up sound from all around.
A nondirectional antenna can receive signals from any direction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: directional - limited to or aimed in a particular direction
  • synonym: omnidirectional - able to send or receive signals in all directions
