truculence

noun
aggressive defiance; hostility
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌk.jə.ləns / (AmE), / ˈtrʌk.jʊ.ləns / (BrE)
aggressive defiance or hostility; a disposition to be truculent or combative

Examples

Surprising truculence
Despite his small stature, the dog showed surprising truculence toward strangers.
Her truculence made it difficult for her to get along with the team.
The politician's truculence in debates often alienates his colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggressiveness - readiness or tendency to attack or confront
  • antonym: meekness - submissiveness; lack of aggression
