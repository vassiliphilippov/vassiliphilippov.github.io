on a losing wicket

adverb
in a disadvantaged or difficult position
6.0
Pronunciation: / ɑn ə ˈluːzɪŋ ˈwɪkɪt / (AmE), / ɒn ə ˈluːzɪŋ ˈwɪkɪt / (BrE)
in a situation where success is unlikely or one is at a disadvantage

Examples

To be on a losing wicket
After his business partner left, he realized he was on a losing wicket.
She knew she was on a losing wicket when everyone disagreed with her plan.
Trying to convince them now is like being on a losing wicket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: on a sticky wicket - in a difficult or tricky situation (orig. cricket)
