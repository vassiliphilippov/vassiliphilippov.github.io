revalorization

noun
restoring value
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌvælərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌvæləraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of restoring or increasing the cultural, social, or symbolic value of a tradition, practice, or object

Examples

Revalorization of traditions
The revalorization of traditional crafts has helped preserve local culture in the region.
Government initiatives focused on the revalorization of indigenous languages in schools.
Many artists support the revalorization of forgotten art forms through exhibitions and workshops.

Related Terms

  • antonym: devaluation - the process of reducing the value or importance of something
  • synonym: revitalization - the process of bringing new life or energy to something
