primatological

adjective
about primate study
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpraɪmətəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌprɪmətəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the scientific study of primates such as monkeys, apes, and lemurs

Examples

Primatological research
Her primatological research has helped us understand how gorillas communicate in the wild.
The conference featured several primatological presentations on the social behavior of lemurs.
He is known for his primatological work focusing on the diet of orangutans.
