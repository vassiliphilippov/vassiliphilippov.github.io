unconceptualized

adjective
not clearly conceptualized
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈsɛptʃuəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈsɛptjʊəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not formed into a clear concept or not thought out in a definite way

Examples

An unconceptualized idea
The project proposal was still unconceptualized when the deadline approached.
Many of his early thoughts remained unconceptualized until he started writing them down.
Without a clear plan, their goals stayed unconceptualized for months.
