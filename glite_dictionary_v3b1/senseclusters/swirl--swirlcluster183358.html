swirl

verb
move in a twisting spinning motion
Pronunciation: / swɝl /; / swɜrl / (AmE), / swɜːl / (BrE)
to move in a twisting, spinning, or circular motion

Examples

Leaves swirl in wind
A sudden breeze made the fallen leaves swirl along the pavement.
She watched the cream swirl into her coffee.
Snowflakes swirled around them as they hurried home.
Leaves swirled in the wind after the rainstorm.
She watched the cream swirl into the coffee, creating delicate patterns.
The water began to swirl around the drain, forming a miniature whirlpool.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eddy - to move in a circular motion, often referring to water
  • degree lower: flow - to move steadily and continuously in a current or stream
  • synonym: spin - to turn around quickly or cause to turn around
  • synonym: whirl - to move or cause to move rapidly around and around
