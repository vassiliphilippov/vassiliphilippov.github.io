Synonyms

  • whirl
    2.6 2.58/m
    whirl is to turn quickly in a tight circle, while swirl is to move in a softer twisting, circular motion.
  • spin
    4.4 0.42/m
    spin means turning around an axis, often fast and steady, while swirl implies a looser twisting, circular motion.
  • gyrate
    6.9 0.80/m
    gyrate means to move in wide circles or spirals, often formally described, while swirl feels more natural and fluid.
  • twirl
    2.0 4.62/m
    twirl is a quick, playful turning of a person or object, while swirl is a flowing, twisting circular motion.
  • eddy
    5.8 0.13/m
    eddy is a small circular current in water or air, while swirl describes general twisting, circular movement of things or liquids.

Related Terms
