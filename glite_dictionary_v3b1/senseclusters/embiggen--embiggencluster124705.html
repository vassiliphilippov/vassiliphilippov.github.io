embiggen

verb
make or become bigger (informal)
5.5
Pronunciation: / ɛmˈbɪɡən / (AmE), / ɛmˈbɪɡən / (BrE)
to make or become bigger, especially in an informal or humorous context; to enlarge or increase in amount, size, or scope

Examples

To embiggen the team
Leaders discussed ways to embiggen the organization’s reach.
Social media campaigns can embiggen your chances of being noticed.
The new project aims to embiggen our influence in the community.
