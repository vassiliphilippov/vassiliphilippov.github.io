mal-

x
prefix for bad or wrong
Pronunciation: / mæl / (AmE), / mæl / (BrE)
a prefix meaning bad, wrong, or ill, used to form words indicating something negative or faulty

Examples

The prefix mal- meaning bad
The prefix mal- in 'malfunction' shows that something is not working correctly.
Words like 'malnutrition' and 'malpractice' use the mal- prefix to indicate something negative.
Adding mal- to 'adjusted' creates 'maladjusted', meaning not properly adjusted.

  • synonym: mis- - prefix meaning wrong or badly
