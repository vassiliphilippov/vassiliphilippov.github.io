States' Rights Democratic Party

noun
segregationist U.S. political party (1948)
9.9
Pronunciation: /steɪts raɪts de.mə.ˈkræt.ɪk ˈpɑːr.tiː/ (AmE), /steɪts raɪts de.mə.ˈkræt.ɪk ˈpɑː.tiː/ (BrE)
