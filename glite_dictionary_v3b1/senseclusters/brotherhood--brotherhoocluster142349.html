brotherhood

noun
employees' labor organization
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌðərˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈbrʌðəhʊd / (BrE)
an organization of employees, especially one formed to represent and negotiate for workers' rights and interests

Examples

Workers' brotherhood
After John joined the brotherhood, he gained access to better benefits at work.
Many workers relied on the brotherhood to help resolve disputes with their employer.
The brotherhood met weekly to discuss contract negotiations with management.

Related Terms

  • synonym: union - an organized association of workers formed to protect and further their rights
