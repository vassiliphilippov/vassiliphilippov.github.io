overheight

noun
excess in height
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌhaɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌhaɪt / (BrE)
the amount by which something exceeds the allowed or expected height

Examples

Bridge overheight limit
The truck was stopped at the bridge due to its overheight compared to the posted limit.
Engineers measured the overheight of the new building to ensure it met city regulations.
A warning sign indicated the overheight of vehicles that could safely pass through the tunnel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deficiency - the state of being lower than the required amount
  • synonym: excess - an amount that is more than necessary or allowed
