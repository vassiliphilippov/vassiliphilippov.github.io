clubroot

noun
plant root disease
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈklʌb.ruːt/ (AmE), /ˈklʌb.ruːt/ (BrE)
a plant disease that causes swollen, deformed roots, especially in cabbages and related vegetables

Examples

Clubroot disease
Farmers noticed that their cabbages were suffering from clubroot after a wet spring.
The spread of clubroot can devastate entire fields of broccoli and cauliflower.
Soil testing revealed the presence of clubroot in the vegetable garden.
The farmer lost many cabbages to clubroot last season.
Proper crop rotation can help prevent clubroot in vegetable gardens.
Symptoms of clubroot include yellowing leaves and swollen, misshapen roots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Plasmodiophora brassicae - the microscopic parasite that causes clubroot disease
  • antonym: healthy - free from disease or abnormality
  • synonym: root knot - a plant disease causing root swelling, often by nematodes
  • synonym: root rot - a disease causing decay of plant roots
