anise

noun
aromatic plant used as flavoring
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪs / (AmE), / ˈænɪs / (BrE)
a plant whose seeds are used to flavor food and drinks with a sweet, liquorice-like taste, and sometimes for medicine

Examples
