giant crab

noun
very large Japanese deep-water crab
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt kræb / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt kræb / (BrE)
a very large species of deep-water Japanese crab, known as the Japanese spider crab, with extremely long legs

Examples

A giant crab species
Fishermen caught a giant crab off the coast of Japan.
The giant crab is famous for its impressive size and long lifespan.
The aquarium has a giant crab on display, astonishing visitors with its massive legs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Japanese spider crab - the world's largest known crab species, native to Japanese waters
