manhole

noun
covered access opening to underground utilities
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmænˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈmæn.həʊl / (BrE)
A covered opening in a street or sidewalk, allowing access to underground utilities such as sewers or electrical systems.

Examples

A manhole cover
A manhole is often found in the middle of city roads for easy access to utilities.
Be careful not to step on a loose manhole cover when walking at night.
The workers lifted the manhole cover to repair the sewer pipes beneath the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: access hatch - A similar covered opening for entry, often in buildings or vehicles
