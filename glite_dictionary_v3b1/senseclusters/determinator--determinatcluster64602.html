determinator

noun
deciding factor
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɜːrmɪˌneɪtər / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that determines or decides something

Examples

The determinator of success
Persistence is often the key determinator of long-term success in any field.
In this experiment, temperature was the main determinator of the reaction rate.
The judge acted as the final determinator in the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decider - a person who makes a decision
  • synonym: factor - something that contributes to a result
  • synonym: influencer - someone or something that has an effect on an outcome
