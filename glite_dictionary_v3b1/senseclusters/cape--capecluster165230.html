cape

noun
a short, sleeveless outer garment
2.1
Pronunciation: / keɪp / (AmE), / keɪp / (BrE)
A sleeveless outer garment that fastens at the neck and hangs loosely from the shoulders, shorter than a cloak.

Examples

A superhero cape
For the costume, all you need is a simple cape and a mask.
She wore a red cape over her dress to the party.
The superhero's cape fluttered dramatically in the wind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cloak - a long, loose outer garment with sleeves, usually worn over the shoulders
