squawroot

noun
herb with knotty rootstock, blue berries
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɔˌrut / (AmE), / ˈskwɔːruːt / (BrE)
A tall herb native to eastern North America and Asia, producing blue, berrylike fruit and a thick, knotty rootstock formerly used medicinally.
