silverleaf

adjective
silvery leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvərˌliːf / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəliːf / (BrE)
having leaves that are silvery in color or covered with a silvery coating

Examples

Silverleaf plant
The silverleaf plants sparkled in the morning sunlight after the rain.
Gardeners often choose silverleaf varieties to add contrast to flower beds.
The silverleaf foliage stood out beautifully against the darker evergreens.

Related Terms

  • antonym: green - having the color of healthy leaves or grass
  • synonym: silvery - having the color or shine of silver
