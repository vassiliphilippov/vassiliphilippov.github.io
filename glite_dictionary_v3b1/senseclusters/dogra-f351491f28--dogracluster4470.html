Dogra

adjective
of the Dogra people
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊɡrə / (AmE), / ˈdəʊɡrə / (BrE)
relating to the Dogra people, their language, culture, or the Jammu region of northern India

Examples

Dogra culture
The Dogra architecture in Jammu is known for its unique blend of styles.
She wore a beautiful Dogra shawl to the cultural festival.
Many Dogra traditions are celebrated during local festivals in the region.
