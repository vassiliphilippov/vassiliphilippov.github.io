common ginger

noun
tropical plant; source of gingerroot
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmən ˈdʒɪndʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈdʒɪndʒə / (BrE)
A tropical Asian plant (Zingiber officinale) widely cultivated for its pungent root, which is used as a spice and flavoring.

Examples

Common ginger root
Common ginger is used in many Asian dishes for its strong, spicy flavor.
Farmers in India grow large quantities of common ginger for both local use and export.
The root of the common ginger plant can be used fresh, dried, or powdered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ginger - spice made from the root of the ginger plant
  • synonym: gingerroot - the root of the ginger plant, used as a spice
