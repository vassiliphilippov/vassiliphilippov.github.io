authoritarian

noun
person enforcing strict obedience to authority
4.6 6.6
Pronunciation: /ə.θɔːr.ə.ˈter.iː.ən/ /; / /ɔː.θɔːr.ə.ˈter.iː.ən/ (AmE), /ɔː.θɒr.ɪ.ˈteə.riː.ən/ (BrE)
a person who favors or enforces strict obedience to authority, especially that of the government, at the expense of personal freedom; a tyrannical or dictatorial person

Examples

An authoritarian ruler
Many people resisted the authoritarian who tried to suppress free speech in the town.
She accused her father of being an authoritarian whenever he laid down strict house rules.
The company's new manager is an authoritarian who expects total compliance from all employees.

Related Terms

  • antonym: democrat - a person who supports democracy or democratic forms of government
  • synonym: dictator - a ruler with total power, typically one who has obtained power by force
