hairline

noun
edge where hair grows on head
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈheə.laɪn / (BrE)
the edge or boundary where the hair grows on a person's head.

Examples

Receding hairline
Genetic factors often influence a person's hairline and its shape.
He noticed his hairline was receding as he got older.
The barber suggested a new style to suit his widow's peak hairline.
