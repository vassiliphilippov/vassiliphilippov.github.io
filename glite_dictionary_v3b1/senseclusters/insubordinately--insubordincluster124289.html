insubordinately

adverb
disobediently to authority
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnsəˈbɔːrdənətli / (AmE), / ˌɪnsəˈbɔːdɪnətli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows refusal to obey authority or follow orders

Examples

Answer insubordinately
The student answered insubordinately when the teacher asked him to sit down.
She acted insubordinately by ignoring her manager's instructions during the project.
Speaking insubordinately to a commanding officer can have serious consequences in the military.
