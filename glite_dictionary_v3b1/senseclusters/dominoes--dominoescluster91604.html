dominoes

noun
game played with rectangular tiles
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːməˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪnəʊz / (BrE)
a game, usually played by two or more people, in which small rectangular tiles marked with spots are matched according to rules

Examples

To play dominoes
Every Friday night, the family gathers to play dominoes around the kitchen table.
In the park, a group of friends played dominoes and laughed together until sunset.
She taught her grandchildren the rules of dominoes during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domino - the rectangular tile used in the game
