in utero

adverb
existing or occurring in the uterus before birth
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈjutəroʊ / (AmE), / ɪn ˈjuːtərəʊ / (BrE)
While still inside the uterus; occurring or existing before birth.

Examples

In utero development
Many genetic conditions can be detected in utero using modern screening techniques.
Some birth defects can develop in utero before the mother is even aware of the pregnancy.
The virus was transmitted to the fetus in utero.
