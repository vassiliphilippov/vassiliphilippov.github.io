ropemaker

noun
person who makes ropes
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊpˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊpˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose occupation is making ropes by hand or with machinery

Examples

A ropemaker at work
As a skilled ropemaker, she supplied the entire fleet with strong lines.
Generations of ropemakers have worked in this old workshop.
The ropemaker demonstrated traditional techniques at the historical festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craftsman - a skilled manual worker, especially one who makes things by hand
  • degree lower: rope - a strong, thick line made by twisting together strands of hemp, nylon, or similar material
