American organ

noun
free-reed keyboard instrument with suction bellows

Examples

Synonyms

  • reed organ
    8.1 0.04/m
    reed organ is a free-reed keyboard usually played with bellows that push air to the reeds, while American organ uses suction bellows.
  • harmonium
    8.0 0.18/m
    harmonium has free reeds and a keyboard with bellows that push air, while American organ uses suction bellows for a softer sound.
