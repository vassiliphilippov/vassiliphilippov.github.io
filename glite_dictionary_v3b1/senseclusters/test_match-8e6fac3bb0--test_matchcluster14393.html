test match

noun
international cricket match between national teams
3.8
Pronunciation: / tɛst mætʃ / (AmE), / tɛst mætʃ / (BrE)
A cricket match played between national representative teams, regarded as the highest standard of the game, especially as part of an international series.

Examples

A test match series
Fans eagerly anticipate the annual test match series between India and Pakistan.
The first test match between England and Australia was played in 1877.
Tickets for the test match sold out within hours of going on sale.
