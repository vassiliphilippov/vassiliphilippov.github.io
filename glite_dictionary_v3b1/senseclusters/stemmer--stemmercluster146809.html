stemmer

noun
miner's tool for ramming packing over explosives
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛmər / (AmE), / ˈstɛmə / (BrE)
A miner's tool, typically a bar, used for packing material tightly over an explosive charge before blasting.

Examples

Miner's stemmer
Always check your stemmer for any damage before beginning to tamp down the charge.
The miner used a stemmer to ensure the explosives were securely packed.
Without a proper stemmer, the blasting process can be both ineffective and dangerous.
