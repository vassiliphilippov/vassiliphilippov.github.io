flunk

noun
failure in an exam or course
9.9
Pronunciation: / flʌŋk / (AmE), / flʌŋk / (BrE)
A failure, especially in a test or course; the act of failing to achieve a passing grade.

Examples

A flunk in math
He was upset about getting a flunk in mathematics last semester.
If you get another flunk, you might have to repeat the year.
The college officially notified him of his flunk in chemistry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fail - To be unsuccessful in achieving a passing grade
  • antonym: pass - To succeed in meeting the required standard in an exam or course
