counterchange

verb
to swap colors in design
9.3
Pronunciation: /kaʊn.tər.ˈtʃeɪndʒ/ /; / /ˈkaʊn.tər.tʃeɪndʒ/ (AmE), /kaʊn.tə.ˈtʃeɪndʒ/ /; / /ˈkaʊn.tə.tʃeɪndʒ/ (BrE)
to reverse or exchange the colors or tones in a design so that opposing areas swap their appearance

Examples

To counterchange patterns
The designer decided to counterchange the background and foreground colors for a striking effect.
In heraldry, artists often counterchange patterns to create a balanced and mirrored look.
To make the logo stand out, they counterchanged the two main colors in the design.
