dominium

noun
territory or domain
9.9
Pronunciation: / doʊˈmɪniəm / (AmE), / dəˈmɪnɪəm / (BrE)
a territory or domain under the control of a ruler or state; a dominion or realm

Examples

A vast dominium
The ancient city was once the heart of a vast dominium ruled by a powerful king.
Explorers mapped the boundaries of the new dominium claimed by their country.
Laws in the dominium differed greatly from those in neighboring regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dominion - a territory or area controlled by a ruler or government
  • synonym: empire - a group of countries or regions controlled by one ruler or government
  • synonym: realm - a kingdom or domain ruled by a monarch
