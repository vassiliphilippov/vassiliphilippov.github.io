predominance

noun
state of being dominant or most important
Pronunciation: / prɪˈdɑːmɪnəns / (AmE), / prɪˈdɒmɪnəns / (BrE)
the state of being greater, more important, or more common than others

Examples

The predominance of men
In the past, there was a predominance of men in scientific professions.
Recent studies highlight the predominance of online learning among university students.
The predominance of one political party in the region has lasted for decades.
Recent years have seen the predominance of electronic devices in daily life.
The predominance of female voices in the choir gave the music a delicate tone.
There was a clear predominance of blue in the artist’s latest series of paintings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dominance - the state of being dominant
  • antonym: inferiority - the state of being lower in importance or quality
  • synonym: prevalence - the fact or condition of being widespread or common
  • antonym: subordination - the act of being lower in rank or position
