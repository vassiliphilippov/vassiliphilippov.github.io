rusty blackbird

noun
a North American blackbird with rusty-edged plumage
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌsti ˈblækˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈrʌsti ˈblækˌbɜːd / (BrE)
A North American blackbird (Euphagus carolinus) whose bluish-black plumage is edged with rust in the fall.

Examples

The rusty blackbird bird
I spotted a rusty blackbird foraging along the edge of the pond.
In autumn, the male rusty blackbird displays a distinct rusty tinge on its feathers.
The rusty blackbird has experienced a dramatic decline in population over recent decades.
