red sanders

noun
tree valued for red dyewood and timber
7.6 8.6
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈsændərz / (AmE), / rɛd ˈsandəz / (BrE)
a tree native to India and the East Indies (Pterocarpus santalinus), valued for its hard, fragrant timber used in cabinetwork and its dark red heartwood used for dyeing

Examples

Red sanders tree
Illegal logging of red sanders is a major environmental concern in parts of southern India.
The dark red heartwood of red sanders has been used for centuries as a natural dye.
The furniture was crafted from red sanders, giving it a rich hue and a distinctive fragrance.
