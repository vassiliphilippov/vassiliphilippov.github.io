driving belt

noun
belt transmitting motor power
8.5 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdraɪ.vɪŋ belt/ (AmE), /ˈdraɪ.vɪŋ belt/ (BrE)
a belt that transmits motion and power from a motor to other parts of a machine or machinery

Examples

A loose driving belt
A loose driving belt can cause the motor to slip and lose efficiency.
Many household appliances use a driving belt to transfer power from the motor to moving parts.
The mechanic replaced the worn driving belt to restore the machine's operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conveyor belt - belt that carries materials or objects from one place to another
