rectally

adverb
via the rectum
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛktəli / (AmE), / ˈrɛktəli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or through the rectum, especially for medical procedures or medication administration

Examples

Administer rectally medication
The nurse administered the medication rectally to ensure faster absorption.
Some treatments are given rectally when patients cannot swallow pills.
Suppositories are often inserted rectally for certain medical conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: orally - by way of the mouth
