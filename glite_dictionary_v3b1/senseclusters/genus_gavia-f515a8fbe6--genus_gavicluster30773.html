genus Gavia

noun
genus of loons (aquatic birds)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈɡeɪviə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈɡeɪvɪə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Gavidae, comprising the loons, a group of aquatic birds known for their distinctive calls and excellent diving ability.
