road pizza

noun
flattened object or wreck on the road
Pronunciation: / roʊd ˈpiːtsə / (AmE), / rəʊd ˈpiːtsə / (BrE)
an informal term for a tire, vehicle, or object that has been flattened or destroyed on the road, often after an accident

Examples

A road pizza tire
After the truck's blowout, the shredded tire became just another road pizza on the shoulder.
Driving down the highway, she swerved to avoid a piece of road pizza in her lane.
The mechanic laughed and said my car was one step away from turning into road pizza.
