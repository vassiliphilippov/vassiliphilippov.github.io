physics department

noun
academic division for physics
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzɪks dɪˌpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪks dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the division of an institution, such as a university, that is responsible for teaching and research in physics

Examples

The physics department
Many groundbreaking discoveries have come from this renowned physics department.
She decided to major in physics after visiting the university's physics department.
The physics department organized a seminar on quantum mechanics last Friday.
