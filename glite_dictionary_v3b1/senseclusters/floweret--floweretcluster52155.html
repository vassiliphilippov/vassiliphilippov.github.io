floweret

noun
small or diminutive flower
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊərˌɛt / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪt / (BrE)
a small or diminutive flower, especially one that forms part of a larger composite flower head

Examples

A small floweret
As we examined the daisy, we noticed that every floweret was perfectly formed.
Each floweret on the sunflower contributes to the formation of seeds.
The broccoli head consists of hundreds of tiny green flowerets.
