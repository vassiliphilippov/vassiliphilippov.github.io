emarginate

adjective
with a notched or indented tip
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɑrdʒɪnət / (AmE), / ɪˈmɑːdʒɪnət / (BrE)
having a shallow notch at the tip of a leaf, petal, or other structure

Examples

An emarginate leaf tip
Some butterfly wings are slightly emarginate along the outer edge.
The leaves are emarginate, with a small notch at the apex.
The petal's emarginate tip distinguishes it from similar species.
