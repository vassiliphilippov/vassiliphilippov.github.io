firm

noun
business organization; company
Pronunciation: / fɝm / (AmE), / fɜːm / (BrE)
a business organization or company, especially one involved in professional, commercial, or industrial activities

Examples

Law firm

She joined a prestigious law firm after finishing university.
The accounting firm moved its headquarters to New York City last year.
