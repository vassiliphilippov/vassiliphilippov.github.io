Gentianaceae

noun
family of plants including gentians
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛnʃiəˈneɪsiˌi /; / ˌdʒɛn.ʃəˈneɪ.si.i / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛnʃiəˈniːsiː /; / ˌdʒɛn.tʃi.əˈneɪ.si.i / (BrE)
