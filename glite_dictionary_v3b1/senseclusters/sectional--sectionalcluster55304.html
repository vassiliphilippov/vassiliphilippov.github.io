Relating to or limited to a particular region, subdivision, or group within a larger community or territory.

Examples

Sectional interests
Sectional differences sometimes make it difficult to reach a national consensus.
She criticized the legislation for serving sectional interests rather than the whole country.
The debate quickly took on a sectional character, dividing north and south.

Synonyms

  • regional
    3.5 58/m
    regional describes a larger geographic area covering several localities, while sectional means a limited part or group within a bigger community.
  • subnational
    6.4 0.48/m
    subnational relates to parts of a country like states or provinces, while sectional covers any limited subdivision or group within a larger territory.

Related Terms

  • antonym: national - relating to an entire nation rather than a section
