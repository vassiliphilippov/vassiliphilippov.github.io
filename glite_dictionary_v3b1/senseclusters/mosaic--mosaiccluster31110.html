mosaic

noun
composite of aerial photographs
Pronunciation: / moʊˈzeɪɪk / (AmE), / məʊˈzeɪɪk / (BrE)
an arrangement of aerial photographs combined to form a single composite image, often used in surveying or mapping

Examples

Aerial mosaic map
A detailed mosaic was produced to assist with the environmental study of the region.
Careful alignment is crucial when constructing a mosaic for accurate mapping.
The survey team created a mosaic from hundreds of overlapping aerial images.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photomosaic - a mosaic-like picture created from many smaller photographs
