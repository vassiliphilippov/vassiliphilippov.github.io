unrisen

adjective
not yet risen
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɪzən / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪzən / (BrE)
not yet risen or appeared above the horizon, especially referring to the sun, moon, or similar objects

Examples

Unrisen sun
They set off before the unrisen sun to avoid the midday heat on the trail.
The sky was still dark with the unrisen moon hidden behind the hills.
Farmers often begin their work under the light of an unrisen dawn.

Related Terms

  • antonym: risen - having appeared above the horizon
