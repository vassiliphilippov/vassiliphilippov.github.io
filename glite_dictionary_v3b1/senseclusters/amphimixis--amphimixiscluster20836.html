amphimixis

noun
sexual reproduction via gamete fusion
Pronunciation: / ˌæmfɪˈmɪksɪs /; / ˌæm.fəˈmɪk.sɪs / (AmE), / ˌamfɪˈmɪksɪs /; / ˌam.fɪˈmɪk.sɪs / (BrE)
