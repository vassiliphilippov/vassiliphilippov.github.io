premodern

adjective
before modern era
5.5
Pronunciation: / priˈmɑdərn / (AmE), / priˈmɒdən / (BrE)
relating to a time or society before the development of modern technology or ideas

Examples

Premodern societies
Many premodern societies relied on agriculture and simple tools.
The museum displays artifacts from premodern civilizations around the world.
In premodern times, communication was much slower and less reliable than today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ancient - belonging to a very old period in history
  • antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times
