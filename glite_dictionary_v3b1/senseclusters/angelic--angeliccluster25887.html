angelic

adjective
like an angel; exceptionally kind or pure
Pronunciation: / ænˈdʒɛlɪk / (AmE), / ænˈdʒelɪk /; / ænˈdʒɛlɪk / (BrE)
having the qualities of an angel, such as being very kind, pure, innocent, or beautiful, or relating to angels
