dogfighting

noun
close-range air combat
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔgˌfaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɒgˌfaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
aerial combat between fighter aircraft at close range, involving rapid and skillful maneuvers

Examples

Aerial dogfighting
The pilots trained for intense dogfighting scenarios to prepare for real combat situations.
Modern fighter jets are equipped with technology to give them an edge in dogfighting.
During the war, dogfighting skills often determined which side gained air superiority.
