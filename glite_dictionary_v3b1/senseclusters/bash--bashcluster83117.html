bash

noun
informal party or celebration
Pronunciation: / bæʃ / (AmE), / bæʃ / (BrE)
An informal party or celebration, especially one that is lively or boisterous.

Examples

A birthday bash
He was invited to an exclusive bash at the downtown club.
The office bash lasted well into the night.
We're throwing a bash for her birthday this weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: party - a social gathering of invited guests, typically involving eating, drinking, and entertainment
