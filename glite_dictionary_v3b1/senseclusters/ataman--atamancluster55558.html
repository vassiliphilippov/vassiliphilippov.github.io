ataman

noun
Cossack leader
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈætəmæn / (AmE), / ˈætəmæn / (BrE)
the leader or chief of Cossacks or certain Slavic military groups, especially in Russia or Ukraine

Examples

The ataman leader

During the uprising, the ataman organized the defense of the village.
Each regiment chose its own ataman to lead them into battle.
