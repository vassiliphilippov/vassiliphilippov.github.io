tonal system

noun
system of tones in a language
5.0 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈtoʊ.nəl ˈsɪs.təm/ (AmE), /ˈtəʊ.nəl ˈsɪs.təm/ (BrE)
the set of pitch patterns or tones used in a particular language or dialect to distinguish meaning between words or syllables

Examples
