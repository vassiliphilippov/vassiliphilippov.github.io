acentric

adjective
not centered; having no center
Pronunciation: / eɪˈsɛntrɪk / (AmE), / eɪˈsɛntrɪk / (BrE)
not centered or lacking a center

Examples

Acentric design
Because the figure was acentric, it seemed to float irregularly on the page.
In genetics, an acentric chromosome fragment is one that lacks a centromere.
The design was deliberately acentric, drawing attention away from a central focus.

Related Terms

  • antonym: centric - centered; having or situated at a center
