silk

verb
to cover with silk
9.3
Pronunciation: / sɪlk / (AmE), / sɪlk / (BrE)
to cover, line, or coat something with silk, or to give it a smooth, silky finish

Examples

To silk a lining
She spent the afternoon silking the dress linings with delicate silk fabric.
The craftsman is silking the cushions to give them a luxurious feel.
After silking the lampshade, it looked elegant and smooth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coat - to cover a surface with a layer of something
  • synonym: line - to cover the inside surface of something
