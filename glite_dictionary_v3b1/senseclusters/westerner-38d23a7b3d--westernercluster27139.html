Westerner

noun
person from the West
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstərnər / (AmE), / ˈwɛstənə / (BrE)
a person from a country in the Western world, especially Europe or North America

Examples

Many Westerners travel
Many Westerners travel to Asia to experience different cultures and traditions.
The film was made by a Westerner who spent years living in France.
Some Westerners find it challenging to adapt to local customs when moving abroad.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Easterner - a person from the Eastern world, such as Asia
