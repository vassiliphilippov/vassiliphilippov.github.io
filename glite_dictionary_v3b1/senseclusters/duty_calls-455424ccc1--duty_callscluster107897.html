duty calls

x
obligation requires immediate attention
Pronunciation: / ˈduːti kɔlz / (AmE), / ˈdjuːti kɔːlz / (BrE)
used to say that a responsibility or obligation requires one's attention or action, often requiring one to leave or stop what one is doing

Examples

Duty calls suddenly
As a doctor, when duty calls, she never hesitates to help her patients.
I would love to stay longer, but duty calls and I have to get back to work.
They were about to leave for vacation, but duty calls so he had to postpone their trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obligation - something which a person is bound to do, especially legally or morally
