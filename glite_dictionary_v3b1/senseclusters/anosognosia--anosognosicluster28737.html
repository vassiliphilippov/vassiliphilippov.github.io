anosognosia

noun
unawareness of illness
9.9
Pronunciation: /æn.ə.soʊg.ˈnoʊ.ziː.ə/ /; / /æn.ə.soʊg.ˈnoʊ.ʒə/ (AmE), /æn.ə.sɒg.ˈnəʊ.ziː.ə/ /; / /æn.ə.sɒg.ˈnəʊ.ʒə/ (BrE)
a medical condition in which a person is unaware of their own illness or disability

Examples

anosognosia after stroke
After his stroke, he showed signs of anosognosia and insisted he could move his paralyzed arm.
Doctors explained that anosognosia made it difficult for her to recognize her memory problems.
Family members were frustrated by his anosognosia, as he denied needing any help with daily tasks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: denial - refusal to accept reality or fact
  • antonym: insight - awareness and understanding of one's own condition
