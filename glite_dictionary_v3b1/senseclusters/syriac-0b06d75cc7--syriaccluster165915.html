Syriac

noun
member of Syriac community
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriˌæk / (AmE), / ˈsɪriˌæk / (BrE)
a member of an ethnic and religious community historically associated with the Syriac language, especially among Eastern Christian groups

Examples

Syriac community
The Syriac community has preserved its ancient traditions for centuries.
Many Syriacs still speak their ancestral language at home and in church.
As a Syriac, he celebrates important religious festivals with his extended family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Assyrian - a member of an ethnic group from the ancient region of Assyria
