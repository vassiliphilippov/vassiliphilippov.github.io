Seri

proper noun
Malay honorific title
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛri / (AmE), / ˈsɛri / (BrE)
a title or honorific used in Malay and Indonesian names to show rank or respect

Examples

Seri Paduka
The governor was formally addressed as Seri during the state ceremony.
In some royal titles, the word Seri is placed before the person's name.
The honorific Seri signifies high respect in Malay culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Sri - a similar honorific title used in South and Southeast Asian cultures
