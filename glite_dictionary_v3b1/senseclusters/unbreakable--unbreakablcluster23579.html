unbreakable

adjective
impossible to break; very strong
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbreɪkəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈbreɪkəbl / (BrE)
impossible to break or damage, especially under normal conditions; very strong or durable.

Examples

An unbreakable glass
She wrapped the vase carefully, even though it was advertised as unbreakable.
The company claims that their new phone is virtually unbreakable.
These safety glasses are made with unbreakable lenses to protect your eyes.
