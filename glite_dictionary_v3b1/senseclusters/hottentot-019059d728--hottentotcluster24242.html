Hottentot

proper noun
offensive colonial term
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtənˌtɑt / (AmE), / ˈhɒt.ən.tɒt / (BrE)
an offensive, outdated term formerly used to refer to a member of the Khoikhoi people of southern Africa

Examples

A Hottentot in history
The term Hottentot is now considered highly offensive and should not be used.
Historical texts sometimes refer to the Khoikhoi people as Hottentot, but this is outdated and disrespectful.
Modern scholars avoid the word Hottentot due to its colonial and derogatory origins.
