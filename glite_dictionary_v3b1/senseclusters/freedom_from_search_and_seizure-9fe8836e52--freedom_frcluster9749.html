freedom from search and seizure

noun
legal right against unreasonable searches
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfriː.dəm frəm ˈsɝːtʃ ənd ˈsiː.ʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈfriː.dəm frəm ˈsɜːtʃ ənd ˈsiː.ʒə / (BrE)
The legal right to protection against unreasonable search and seizure by authorities, as guaranteed by the Fourth Amendment to the US Constitution.
