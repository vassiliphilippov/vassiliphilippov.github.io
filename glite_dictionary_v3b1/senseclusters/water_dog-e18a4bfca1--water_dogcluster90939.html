water dog

noun
person fond of water activities
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ ˌdɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌdɒɡ / (BrE)
a person who greatly enjoys spending time in or on the water, especially swimming or boating

Examples

A real water dog

My brother is such a water dog that he learned how to surf before he could ride a bike.
You can always spot a water dog by their constant enthusiasm for the beach or swimming pool.
