up in flames

x
suddenly destroyed or lost
3.9
Pronunciation: / ʌp ɪn fleɪmz / (AmE), / ʌp ɪn fleɪmz / (BrE)
Completely destroyed or lost suddenly, often in dramatic or disastrous circumstances.

Examples

Up in flames suddenly
All his hard work went up in flames when the computer crashed.
Her dreams of becoming a singer seemed to go up in flames after her voice was injured.
The old theatre went up in flames after a faulty wire sparked a fire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: go up in smoke - to be destroyed or come to nothing suddenly, especially after great effort
