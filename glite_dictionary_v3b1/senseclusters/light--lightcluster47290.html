light

adjective
weighing or measuring less than it should
3.9
Pronunciation: / laɪt / (AmE), / laɪt / (BrE)
(of a weight or measure) less than the correct, legal, or full amount, especially deliberately so.

Examples

Light weight
Authorities discovered that the bags of rice were consistently light at this market.
Please check the scales to ensure you don't get a light pound of apples.
The shopkeeper was fined for selling light loaves to his customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scant - barely sufficient or adequate in quantity
  • synonym: short - less than the full, correct amount
