vena pulmonalis inferior

noun
pulmonary vein returning blood from lower lung lobes
Pronunciation: / ˈviː.nə ˌpʊl.məˈneɪ.lɪs ɪnˈfɪr.i.ər / (AmE), / ˈveɪ.nə ˌpʊl.məˈneɪ.lɪs ɪnˈfɪə.ri.ə / (BrE)
either of two pulmonary veins (left and right) returning oxygenated blood from the lower lobes of the lungs to the left atrium of the heart

Examples

The vena pulmonalis inferior
A blockage in the vena pulmonalis inferior can result in serious cardiac complications.
During surgery, care must be taken not to damage the vena pulmonalis inferior.
The vena pulmonalis inferior returns blood from the lower lobes of each lung to the heart.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pulmonary vein - vein carrying oxygenated blood from lungs to heart
  • antonym: vena pulmonalis superior - pulmonary vein returning blood from upper lung lobes
