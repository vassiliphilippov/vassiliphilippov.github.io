uterine cavity

noun
space within the uterus
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːt̬ərɪn ˈkævəti / (AmE), / ˈjuːtəriːn ˈkævəti / (BrE)
the space inside the uterus between the cervical canal and the Fallopian tubes, where implantation and pregnancy occur

Examples

The uterine cavity space
Abnormal bleeding can sometimes be caused by polyps in the uterine cavity.
An ultrasound was performed to assess the size of the uterine cavity.
The embryo implants in the uterine cavity during early pregnancy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: uterus - the organ in female mammals where offspring develop before birth
