jungle

verb
to travel in jungle
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋɡl / (BrE)
to move through or operate within dense, jungle-like vegetation, especially in a challenging or adventurous way

Examples

To jungle through vines
The expedition jungled for days before finding a safe place to camp.
They jungled through thick vines and muddy ground to reach the ancient ruins.
During the rainy season, soldiers often jungled to navigate the remote border areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: explore - to travel through an area to learn about it
  • synonym: trek - to make a long and difficult journey, especially on foot
