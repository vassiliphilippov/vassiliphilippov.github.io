fronton

noun
decorative building pediment
9.9
Pronunciation: / frɒnˈtɒn / (AmE), / frɒnˈtɒn / (BrE)
a pediment or decorative triangular section above the entrance of a classical building, often containing sculpture or relief.

Examples

Temple fronton
The ancient temple's fronton was decorated with scenes from mythology.
Restorers carefully cleaned the marble fronton to reveal its original details.
Visitors admired the intricate sculptures carved into the building's fronton.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frieze - a horizontal band of sculpted or painted decoration on a building
  • synonym: pediment - a triangular upper part of the front of a building in classical style
