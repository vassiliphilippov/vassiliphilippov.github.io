kinesiology

noun
science of human movement
Pronunciation: / kɪˌniːsiˈɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / kɪˌniːsiˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of human movement, particularly the mechanics and anatomy involved

Examples
