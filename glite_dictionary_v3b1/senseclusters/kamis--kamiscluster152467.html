kamis

noun
Shinto deities (plural)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmiz / (AmE), / ˈkɑːmiz / (BrE)
the plural form of 'kami', referring to multiple spirits or deities in the Shinto religion of Japan

Examples

Shinto kamis
The villagers prayed to the kamis for a good harvest each year.
Many shrines in Japan are dedicated to different kamis who protect the local area.
Legends tell of powerful kamis living in the mountains and forests.
