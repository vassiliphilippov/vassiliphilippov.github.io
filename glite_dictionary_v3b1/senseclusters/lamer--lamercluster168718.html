lamer

noun
unskilled person
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪmər / (AmE), / ˈleɪmə / (BrE)
a person who is considered unskilled, uncool, or lacking ability, especially in online communities

Examples

Online lamer
In the online forum, he was quickly labeled a lamer for asking basic questions.
Some gamers use the term lamer to describe players who do not follow the rules.
She did not want to seem like a lamer when joining the new coding group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expert - a person with a high level of skill or knowledge
  • synonym: noob - a newcomer or someone inexperienced, especially in gaming or online contexts
