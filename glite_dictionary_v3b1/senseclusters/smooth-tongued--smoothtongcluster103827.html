smooth-tongued

adjective
persuasively or slickly spoken
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌsmuðˈtʌŋd / (AmE), / ˌsmuːðˈtʌŋd / (BrE)
Good at persuading people with clever or insincere talk.

Examples

A smooth-tongued speaker
Be careful of smooth-tongued salespeople making promises they can't keep.
His smooth-tongued explanations made it hard for anyone to argue with him.
The politician was known as a smooth-tongued speaker who could charm any audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glib - (of words or a speaker) fluent but insincere and shallow
