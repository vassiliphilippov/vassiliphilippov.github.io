succuss

verb
to shake vigorously
Pronunciation: / səˈkʌs / (AmE), / səˈkʌs / (BrE)
to shake something, especially in a repeated or vigorous way

Examples

To succuss a bottle
The pharmacist will succuss the solution before preparing the medication.
To ensure proper mixing, you must succuss the bottle several times.
The instructions say to succuss the vial vigorously before each use.
