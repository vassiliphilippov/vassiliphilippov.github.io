E-LASIK

noun
type of eye surgery for vision correction
8.1 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / iÀê Ààle…™s…™k / (AmE), / iÀê Ààle…™s…™k / (BrE)
A refractive eye surgery that reshapes the cornea by creating a hinged flap in the epithelial layer to correct vision.

Examples

E-LASIK eye surgery
After researching various procedures, she decided that E-LASIK was the best option for correcting her nearsightedness.
Many patients recover quickly from E-LASIK surgery and experience improved vision within days.
The ophthalmologist explained how E-LASIK differs from standard LASIK during the consultation.

Synonyms

  • laser-assisted subepithelial keratomileusis
    8.4 0.00/m
    laser-assisted subepithelial keratomileusis is the formal technical name for the procedure, while E-LASIK is the shorter clinical name.
  • LASEK
    6.6 0.00/m
    LASEK reshapes the cornea by making a flap in the outer eye surface, while E-LASIK specifically creates a hinged epithelial flap.
