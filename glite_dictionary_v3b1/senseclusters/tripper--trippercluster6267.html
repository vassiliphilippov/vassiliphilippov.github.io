tripper

noun
automatic catch mechanism; switch device
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪpər / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpə / (BrE)
a catch mechanism or device that acts as a switch, especially one that automatically releases or activates a system when actuated

Examples

Safety tripper
Inspect the safety tripper regularly to ensure it operates properly.
The machine will not run until the tripper is reset.
When the tripper engages, it automatically shuts off the conveyor belt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: switch - a device for making and breaking the connection in an electric circuit
