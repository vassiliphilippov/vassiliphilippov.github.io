free

adjective
not fixed; able to move
1.0 14/m
Pronunciation: / friÀê / (AmE), / friÀê / (BrE)
able to move; not attached, fastened, or fixed in position.

Examples

Came free
He worked until the jammed drawer finally came free.
She tried to pull her foot free from the muddy ground.
The latch broke, and the gate swung free in the wind.
