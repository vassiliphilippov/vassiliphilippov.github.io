hors d'oeuvre

noun
small appetizer before a meal
3.9 6.7
Pronunciation: /ɔːr də.ˈɜːr/ /; / /hɔːr də.ˈɜːr/ (AmE), /ɔː də.ˈɜː/ /; / /hɔː də.ˈɜː/ (BrE)

Examples
