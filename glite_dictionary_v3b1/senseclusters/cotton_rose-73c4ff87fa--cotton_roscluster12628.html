cotton rose

noun
a plant of the genus Filago
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːtn ˌroʊz / (AmE), / ˈkɒtn ˌrəʊz / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Filago, characterized by compact clusters of small, woolly flower heads.

Examples

Woolly cotton rose
Gardeners often plant cotton rose for its distinctive woolly flowers.
She pressed a dried cotton rose flower between the pages of her journal.
The fields were dotted with clusters of cotton rose swaying gently in the breeze.
