bleared

adjective
blurred vision
Pronunciation: / blɪrd / (AmE), / blɪəd / (BrE)
having blurred or dimmed vision, especially due to tiredness, tears, or strain

Examples

Bleared tired eyes
After crying for hours, her bleared eyes could barely make out the words on the page.
He rubbed his bleared vision, trying to focus on the road ahead.
The long night left him with bleared eyes and a heavy head.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blurry - not clear or distinct; blurred
  • antonym: clear - easy to see or understand; not blurred
