bean curd

noun
cheeselike food made from soybeans
7.3 2.4
Pronunciation: /biːn kɜːrd/ (AmE), /biːn kɜːd/ (BrE)
A soft, white, cheeselike food made by coagulating soybean milk and pressing the resulting curds, commonly used as a protein-rich meat substitute in Asian cuisines.

Examples

Soft bean curd
Many vegetarians use bean curd as a substitute for meat in their recipes.
She bought fresh bean curd from the Asian market to make stir-fry.
The soup was garnished with cubes of bean curd and fresh cilantro.
