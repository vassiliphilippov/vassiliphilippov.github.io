lagging

noun
insulation wrapping for pipes or boilers
7.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈlæg.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈlæg.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
material used to cover pipes or boilers, or to lay in attics, to prevent heat loss by providing insulation.

Examples

Lagging material
Old houses often have little or no lagging in their attics, leading to high energy bills.
The plumber recommended adding lagging to the pipes to prevent them from freezing.
You should check that the boiler's lagging is intact and free from damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insulation - material that reduces the rate of heat transfer
