OMS

noun
order management software
Pronunciation: / oʊ ɛm ɛs / (AmE), / əʊ ɛm ɛs / (BrE)
a software system used by businesses to track, manage, and process customer orders

Examples

OMS order tracking
The retailer implemented a new OMS to handle online and in-store orders more efficiently.
Training staff on the updated OMS helped reduce errors in order fulfillment.
A reliable OMS is essential for companies with high volumes of daily transactions.
