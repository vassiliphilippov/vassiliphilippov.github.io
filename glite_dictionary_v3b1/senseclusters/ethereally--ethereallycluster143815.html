ethereally

adverb
in a delicate way
Pronunciation: / ɪˈθɪriəli / (AmE), / ɪˈθɪəriəli / (BrE)
in a way that is light, delicate, or otherworldly

Examples

To move ethereally
The dancer moved ethereally across the stage, captivating the entire audience.
Mist floated ethereally over the quiet lake at dawn.
Her voice sounded ethereally beautiful in the silent cathedral.
