reduce

verb

1.0 1.4
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈduːs/ /; / /rɪ.ˈdjuːs/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈdjuːs/ /; / /rɪ.ˈdʒuːs/ (BrE)
