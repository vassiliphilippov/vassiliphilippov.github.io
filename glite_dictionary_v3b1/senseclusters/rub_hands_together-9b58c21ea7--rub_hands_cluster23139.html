rub hands together

verb
warm hands by moving them against each other
Pronunciation: / rʌb hændz təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / rʌb hændz təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to move one's hands swiftly against each other, especially to warm them by friction

Examples

To rub hands together quickly
During the campfire, they all rubbed their hands together to fight off the cold.
He often rubs his hands together when he enters a warm room from outside.
She rubbed her hands together briskly as she stepped into the chilly air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: warm up - to make oneself or something warmer, often by physical action
