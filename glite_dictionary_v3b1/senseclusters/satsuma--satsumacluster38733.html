satsuma

noun
a type of mandarin orange
6.2
Pronunciation: / sætˈsuːmə / (AmE), / sætˈsuːmə / (BrE)
a type of mandarin orange that is seedless, easy to peel, and sweet, often eaten fresh

Examples

She packed a satsuma in her lunch for a healthy snack.
The satsuma is easy to peel and contains very few seeds.
