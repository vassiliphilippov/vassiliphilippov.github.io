catchable

adjective
able to be contracted
Pronunciation: / ˈkætʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkætʃəbəl / (BrE)
able to be contracted or transmitted, especially referring to a disease or infection

Examples

A catchable disease
The flu is especially catchable in schools during the winter months.
Doctors warned that the virus was highly catchable in crowded places.
Washing your hands often can help prevent catchable illnesses from spreading.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contagious - able to be spread from one person to another
  • synonym: infectious - capable of causing infection and spreading to others
  • antonym: noncommunicable - not able to be transmitted from person to person
