jig

noun
device for guiding tools in machine work
Pronunciation: / dʒɪɡ / (AmE), / dʒɪɡ / (BrE)
a device used to hold a piece of work and guide a tool or machine working on it, especially in engineering or manufacturing processes

Examples

Metal jig tool
A specialized jig ensures each component is cut to exactly the right shape.
The machinist set the metal piece carefully in the jig before starting to drill holes.
They designed a new jig to improve efficiency in the assembly process.
