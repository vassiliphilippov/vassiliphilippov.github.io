nonviolently

adverb
in a nonviolent manner
4.2
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈvaɪələntli / (AmE), / nɒnˈvaɪələntli / (BrE)
in a way that avoids the use of physical force or violence

Examples

Act nonviolently
He encouraged his followers to act nonviolently, even when faced with aggression.
The dispute was resolved nonviolently, avoiding any physical confrontation.
The protesters marched nonviolently despite provocation from the authorities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peacefully - in a calm and non-violent way
  • antonym: violently - using or involving physical force or violence
