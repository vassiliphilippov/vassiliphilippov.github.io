irising

verb
to show rainbow colors
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaɪrɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
to display or produce rainbow-like colors that change with the angle of view or light.

Examples

To irise with colors
The soap bubble was irising in the sunlight, shimmering with every color.
As the oil spread on the water, it began irising beautifully under the afternoon sun.
The surface of the beetle's shell was irising as it moved through the grass.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - lacking brightness or color
  • synonym: iridesce - to show changing rainbow-like colors
