crapola

noun
slang for nonsense
Pronunciation: / kræˈpoʊlə / (AmE), / kræˈpəʊlə / (BrE)
slang for something that is nonsense, worthless, or of very poor quality

Examples

Total crapola story
He dismissed the story as complete crapola and refused to believe it.
Most of what you read in that magazine is just crapola with no real facts.
She called the movie crapola because the plot made no sense at all.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - ideas or statements that are silly or have no meaning
  • synonym: rubbish - something that is worthless or of poor quality
  • antonym: truth - something that is true or factual
