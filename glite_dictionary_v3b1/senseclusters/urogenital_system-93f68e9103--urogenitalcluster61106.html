urogenital system

noun
system of reproductive and urinary organs
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌjʊroʊˈdʒɛnətl̩ ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəʊˈdʒɛnɪtəl ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the organ system in the body comprising both the reproductive and urinary organs, responsible for reproduction and the production and elimination of urine
