humped

adjective
having a humped or curved back
1.0
Pronunciation: / hʌmpt / (AmE), / hʌmpt / (BrE)
having a hump; having an abnormally curved or rounded back, especially due to a spinal deformity such as kyphosis.

Examples

A humped back
Certain breeds of cattle are naturally humped along their backs.
She moved slowly, her humped shoulders barely clearing the top of the counter.
The old man was humped from years of hard labor in the fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kyphotic - relating to or affected by kyphosis
  • antonym: straight-backed - having a straight back; not curved or humped
