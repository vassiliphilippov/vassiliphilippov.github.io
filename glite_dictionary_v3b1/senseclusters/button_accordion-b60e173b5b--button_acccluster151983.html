button accordion

noun
accordion with buttons instead of keys
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ən əˈkɔːrdiən / (AmE), / ˈbʌtən əˈkɔːdiən / (BrE)
A type of accordion that uses buttons to produce notes instead of piano-like keys.

Examples

A button accordion instrument
Learning to play the button accordion can be challenging but rewarding.
She prefers the sound of a button accordion over the piano accordion.
The musician played a lively tune on his button accordion during the festival.
