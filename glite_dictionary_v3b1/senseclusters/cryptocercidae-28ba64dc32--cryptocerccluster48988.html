Cryptocercidae

noun
family of wood cockroaches
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪptoʊˈsɜːrsɪdiː /; / krɪp.toʊ.sɜrˈsɪd.i.aɪ / (AmE), / ˌkrɪptəʊˈsɜːsɪdiː /; / krɪp.təʊ.sɜːˈsɪd.i.aɪ / (BrE)
a family of wood cockroaches (insects) in the order Blattodea, found in decaying wood and closely related to termites
