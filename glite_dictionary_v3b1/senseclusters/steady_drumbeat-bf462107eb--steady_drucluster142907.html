steady drumbeat

noun
continuous, regular rhythmic sound (like drums)
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛdi ˈdrʌmˌbit / (AmE), / ˈstɛdi ˈdrʌmˌbiːt / (BrE)
a continuous and regular rhythmic sound, often produced by drums or resembling the sound of drums, marking time or creating a mood

Examples

A steady drumbeat of news
A steady drumbeat of news stories kept the topic in the public eye.
Protesters marched with a steady drumbeat driving their chants forward.
The steady drumbeat echoed through the hall, filling the space with energy.
