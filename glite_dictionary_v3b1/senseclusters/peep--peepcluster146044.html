peep

noun
a secret or brief look
Pronunciation: / pip / (AmE), / piːp / (BrE)
a quick or secret look at something, especially when not supposed to be seen

Examples

A secret peep
He took a quick peep through the curtains to see if anyone was outside.
She caught a peep of the answers during the test, but felt guilty immediately.
The children tried to get a peep at their birthday presents before the party started.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glance - a quick look
  • synonym: peek - a quick or furtive look
  • antonym: stare - a long fixed look
