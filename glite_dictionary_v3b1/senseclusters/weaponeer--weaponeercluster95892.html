weaponeer

noun
weapon maker or repairer
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛpəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌwiːpəˈnɪə / (BrE)
a person who manufactures, fabricates, or repairs weapons or weapon parts

Examples

Weaponeer makes swords
The skilled weaponeer spent hours crafting a new sword for the village guard.
As a weaponeer, she was responsible for maintaining all the firearms in the armory.
The army hired a weaponeer to upgrade their outdated equipment before the mission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: weaponsmith - a person who makes or repairs weapons
