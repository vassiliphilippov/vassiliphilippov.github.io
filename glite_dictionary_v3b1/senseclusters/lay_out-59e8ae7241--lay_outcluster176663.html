Synonyms

  • set out
    3.2 4.40/m
    set out presents information or objects in a clear, logical order, while lay out means arranging or displaying items visibly or preparing them.
  • arrange
    1.7 19/m
    arrange puts things in order and neat positions for display, while lay out adds the idea of preparing and showing items clearly.
  • array
    3.9 2.02/m
    array arranges items in an ordered, often attractive way, while lay out is a general action of arranging or preparing things for view.
  • set up
    2.7 48/m
