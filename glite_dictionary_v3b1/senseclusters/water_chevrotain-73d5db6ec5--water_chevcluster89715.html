water chevrotain

noun
African hoofed mammal; largest chevrotain
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ ˈʃɛv.rəˌteɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈʃɛv.rə.tæn / (BrE)
A small, nocturnal hoofed mammal native to marshy areas of West Africa, and the largest species of chevrotain.

Examples

The water chevrotain swims
Researchers recently observed a water chevrotain swimming to escape a predator.
The water chevrotain is a shy creature that occupies the swamps and riversides of West Africa.
Unlike many other antelope-like animals, the water chevrotain prefers thick vegetation near water.
