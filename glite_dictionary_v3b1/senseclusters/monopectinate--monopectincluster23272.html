monopectinate

adjective
one-sided comb-like
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnoʊˈpɛktɪneɪt / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəʊˈpɛktɪneɪt / (BrE)
having comb-like structures arranged on only one side, especially in biological contexts

Examples

Monopectinate antennae
The moth's monopectinate antennae help it detect pheromones in the air.
Scientists noted that the beetle's legs were monopectinate, with spines only on one side.
In some species, only the males have monopectinate structures on their appendages.
