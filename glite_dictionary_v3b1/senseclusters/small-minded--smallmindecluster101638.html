small-minded

adjective
having a narrow or petty outlook
6.5 0.77/m
Pronunciation: / sm…îlÀàma…™nd…™d / (AmE), / sm…îÀêlÀàma…™nd…™d / (BrE)
Having a narrow, petty, or intolerant outlook, often showing a lack of generosity or openness.

Examples

A small-minded person
Critics accused the committee of being small-minded in their rejection of the proposal.
She dismissed his small-minded comments as unimportant and focused on the bigger picture.
Their refusal to accept new ideas revealed just how small-minded they truly were.

Synonyms

  • narrow-minded
    5.1 1.68/m
    narrow-minded is unwilling to accept different ideas and views in many situations, while small-minded also implies pettiness and meanness.
  • closed-minded
    5.2 0.39/m
    closed-minded is unwilling to consider new opinions or change, while small-minded also suggests mean, narrow, or petty behavior.
  • petty
    3.9 3.90/m
    petty is mean and focused on small unimportant matters, while small-minded is more broadly narrow and intolerant.

Related Terms
