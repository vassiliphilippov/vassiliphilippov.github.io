primly

adverb
in a proper or formal manner
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪmli / (AmE), / ˈprɪmli / (BrE)
in a formally precise or proper manner, often showing disapproval of anything improper or unrefined

Examples

Dresses primly always
He always dresses primly, even for casual events, never loosening his tie.
She sat primly on the edge of her chair, hands folded neatly in her lap.
The children walked primly in a straight line behind their teacher.

Related Terms

  • antonym: improperly - in a way that is not proper or suitable
  • synonym: properly - in a way that is considered correct or appropriate
