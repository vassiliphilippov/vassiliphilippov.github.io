topknot

noun
decorative knot or bow in the hair
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑpˌnɑt / (AmE), / ˈtɒp.nɒt / (BrE)
a knot, tuft, or decorative item (such as a ribbon, bow, or hair) worn or arranged on top of the head

Examples

A topknot hairstyle
She tied a bright red topknot into her hair for the celebration.
The little girl wore a blue ribbon topknot that matched her dress.
Victorian portraits often show women with elaborate topknots as part of their hairstyle.
He pulled his long hair into a topknot before heading to practice.
Some birds have a distinctive topknot of feathers on their heads.
The dancer wore a bright red ribbon around her topknot.
