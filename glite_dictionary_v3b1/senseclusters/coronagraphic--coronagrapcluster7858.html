coronagraphic

adjective
using a coronagraph
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔːrənoʊˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɒrənəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or using a coronagraph, an instrument designed to observe the solar corona by blocking direct light from the sun

Examples

Coronagraphic images
The coronagraphic images revealed new details about the sun's outer atmosphere.
Scientists used a coronagraphic filter to block the sun's glare during the eclipse.
A coronagraphic observation can help detect faint objects near bright stars.
