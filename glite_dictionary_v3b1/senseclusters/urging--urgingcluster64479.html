  • prompting
    3.8 0.03/m
    prompting is a gentle suggestion or cue that pushes someone to act, while urging is stronger pressure to get action.
  • spur
    4.1 0.7/m
    spur is a quick cause or push that motivates someone to try something, while urging is a sustained, stronger pressing.
  • goad
