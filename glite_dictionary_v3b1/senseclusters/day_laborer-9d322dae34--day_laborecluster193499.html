day laborer

noun
worker paid by the day
3.9 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪ ˌleɪbərɚ /; / ˈdeɪ ˌleɪbərər / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ ˌleɪb(ə)rə /; / ˈdeɪ ˌleɪbərə / (BrE)
a person who is employed and paid on a daily basis, typically for manual or unskilled work

Examples

A day laborer works
After losing his job, he worked as a day laborer to support his family.
Many day laborers gather at the marketplace each morning, hoping for work.

Many day labourers gather at the market each morning, hoping to find construction work.
The government is planning support programs for day labourers affected by the lockdown.
