Fiji dollar

noun
official currency of Fiji
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːdʒi ˈdɑlər / (AmE), / ˈfiːdʒi ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
the official currency and monetary unit of Fiji, subdivided into 100 cents

Examples

The Fiji dollar
She exchanged her currency for Fiji dollars before arriving in Nadi.
The Fiji dollar has been relatively stable against the US dollar this year.
The price for a meal in Suva is about twenty Fiji dollars.
