tap house

noun
building licensed to sell alcohol
5.0
Pronunciation: / tæp haʊs / (AmE), / tæp haʊs / (BrE)
A building or establishment with a bar that is licensed to sell alcoholic drinks, especially beer.

Examples

A tap house bar
Many breweries now operate their own tap houses, serving fresh beer directly from the source.
The new tap house in town offers over twenty varieties of craft beer.
We spent Saturday evening at the local tap house catching up with old friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar - a place where alcoholic drinks are served
  • synonym: pub - a place where people go to drink alcohol, especially beer, and talk to friends
