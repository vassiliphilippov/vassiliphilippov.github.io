grasshopper mouse

noun
insectivorous North American mouse
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræsˌhɑpər maʊs / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːsˌhɒpə maʊs / (BrE)
a small, carnivorous mouse of western North America that feeds on insects and is known for its aggressive behavior
