tip

verb
walk quietly on one's toes
Pronunciation: / tɪp / (AmE), / tɪp / (BrE)
to walk lightly and quietly on the tips of one's toes

Examples

To tip into a room
He tipped into the dark room, careful not to wake anyone.
She tried to tip past the sleeping dog without making a sound.
The child tipped softly across the cold kitchen floor to find her slippers.
