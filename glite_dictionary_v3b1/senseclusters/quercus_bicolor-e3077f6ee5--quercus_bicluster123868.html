Quercus bicolor

noun
species of oak tree native to eastern North America
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkwɜːr.kəs ˈbaɪ.kə.lər/ (AmE), /ˈkwɜː.kəs ˈbaɪ.kə.lə/ (BrE)
