cooter

noun
large freshwater river turtle of the US/Mexico
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːtər / (AmE), / ˈkuːtə / (BrE)
A large freshwater turtle found in rivers and streams of the southern United States and northern Mexico, especially of the genus Pseudemys.

Examples

A cooter turtle
During our canoe trip, we saw several cooters sunning themselves on logs.
The biologist explained that the cooter is an important part of the local ecosystem.
We spotted a cooter basking on a rock by the riverbank.
