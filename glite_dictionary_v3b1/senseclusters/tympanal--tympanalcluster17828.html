tympanal

adjective
of the eardrum
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɪmˈpænəl / (AmE), / tɪmˈpænəl / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a tympanum or eardrum, especially in insects

Examples

Tympanal organ in insects
The moth's tympanal organs allow it to detect the ultrasonic calls of bats.
Scientists studied the tympanal membrane in grasshoppers to understand their hearing abilities.
Damage to the tympanal area can affect an insect's ability to sense vibrations.
