migrator

noun
software tool for moving data
8.1
Pronunciation: / \u02c8ma\u026a\u02cc\u0261re\u026at\u0259r / (AmE), / m\u028c\u026a\u02c8\u0261re\u026at\u0259 / (BrE)
(computing) a software component or tool that transfers, converts, or moves data, records, or configurations from one system, format, or environment to another.

Examples

Database migrator
The data migration project included a migrator that converted records to the new schema overnight.
Developers built custom migrators to move user accounts between the legacy and cloud systems safely.
Before deployment, the team tested the migrator on a small dataset to ensure no data loss occurred.

Related Terms

  • synonym: migrate - to move data, functions, or services from one system or environment to another
  • synonym: migration - the process of moving data, applications, or systems from one environment to another
