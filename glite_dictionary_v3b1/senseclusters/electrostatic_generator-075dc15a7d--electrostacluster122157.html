electrostatic generator

noun
device generating static electric charge
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈstætɪk ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈstætɪk ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪtə / (BrE)
