one-spot

noun
domino/die showing one pip
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌn spɑt / (AmE), / ˈwʌn spɒt / (BrE)
A domino or die whose upper face shows a single pip or dot.

Examples

A one-spot domino
A one-spot is often called an ace in many dice games.
She rolled a one-spot and lost her turn in the game.
You need to match the one-spot domino to continue the sequence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ace - playing card or die with a single pip
