squeakily

adverb
with a squeaky sound
Pronunciation: / ˈskwiːkɪli / (AmE), / ˈskwiːkɪli / (BrE)
in a manner that produces a high-pitched, squeaky sound

Examples

Move squeakily
The old bicycle moved squeakily down the street as he rode to school.
She laughed squeakily when she heard the surprising news.
The mouse ran squeakily across the kitchen floor, startling everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noisily - in a way that makes a lot of noise
  • antonym: quietly - in a way that makes little or no noise
