home equity loan

noun
loan secured by home equity
Pronunciation: / hoʊm ˈɛkwɪti loʊn / (AmE), / həʊm ˈɛkwɪti ləʊn / (BrE)
A loan secured against the equity value of the borrower's home, typically used for large expenses like renovations or debt consolidation.

Examples

A home equity loan application
After years of making payments, they qualified for a home equity loan to renovate their kitchen.
Before applying for a home equity loan, it's important to understand the risks if you can't make payments.
Many homeowners use a home equity loan to pay for major repairs or unexpected medical expenses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mortgage - a loan in which property is used as collateral
