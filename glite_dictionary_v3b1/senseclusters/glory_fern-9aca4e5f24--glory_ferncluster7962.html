glory fern

noun
a tropical fern species
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɔri fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːri fɜːn / (BrE)
A tropical fern, named after a country house in Barbados where it was first discovered.

Examples

The glory fern plant
Botanists were excited to study the glory fern after its discovery in Barbados.
She placed the glory fern in a shady spot to mimic its natural habitat.
The conservatory featured an impressive collection, including several rare glory ferns.
