overextension

noun
exceeding limits
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪkˈstɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪkˈstɛnʃən / (BrE)
the act of exceeding reasonable limits, boundaries, or taking on more than can be managed (such as authority, responsibilities, or resources)

Examples

Business overextension
