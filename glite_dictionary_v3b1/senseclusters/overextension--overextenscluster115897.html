overextension

noun
exceeding limits
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪkˈstɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪkˈstɛnʃən / (BrE)
the act of exceeding reasonable limits, boundaries, or taking on more than can be managed (such as authority, responsibilities, or resources)

Examples

Business overextension
The company's overextension into new markets led to financial difficulties.
His overextension of authority caused tension among the team members.
After years of overextension, the organization finally set clearer boundaries for its projects.
The company's rapid growth led to overextension and financial difficulties.
He suffered from overextension after accepting too many projects at once.
Careful planning can help prevent overextension of your resources and time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overcommitment - the act of committing to more than one can handle
  • antonym: restraint - the act of limiting or controlling something
