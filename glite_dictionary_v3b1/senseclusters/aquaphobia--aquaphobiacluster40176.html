aquaphobia

noun
extreme fear of water or drowning
Pronunciation: / ˌækwəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌakwəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
An extreme or irrational fear of water, especially of drowning.

Examples

Aquaphobia fear
Despite her aquaphobia, she agreed to try swimming lessons with her friends.
His severe aquaphobia made vacations by the ocean very stressful.
Therapy helped her gradually overcome her aquaphobia and enjoy water activities again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hydrophobia - fear of water, often used for rabies
