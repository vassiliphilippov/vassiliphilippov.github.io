impaler

noun
one who pierces
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpeɪlər / (AmE), / ɪmˈpeɪlə / (BrE)
a person or thing that pierces or kills by driving a sharp object through something or someone

Examples

The impaler legend
The legend spoke of an impaler feared by all.
In the story, the villain was known as the impaler for his cruel methods.
Some insects are natural impalers, using sharp mouthparts to catch their prey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: piercer - a person or thing that makes holes by piercing
