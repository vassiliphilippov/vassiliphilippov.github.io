frizzante

noun
lightly sparkling wine
8.2
Pronunciation: / frɪˈzɑːnteɪ / (AmE), / frɪˈzænteɪ / (BrE)
a lightly sparkling wine or other drink with gentle carbonation, especially in Italian cuisine

Examples

A frizzante drink
They ordered a bottle of frizzante to enjoy with their summer picnic.
The gentle bubbles in the frizzante made it perfect for a light lunch.
If you prefer less fizz, try a frizzante instead of a fully sparkling wine.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: spumante - an Italian sparkling wine with full carbonation
  • degree lower: still wine - wine without any carbonation or bubbles
