giant anteater

noun
large South American anteater species
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈænˌtiːtər / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈænˌtiːtə / (BrE)
a large, shaggy-haired, toothless mammal from South America with a long tongue and powerful claws, known for feeding on ants and termites

Examples

The giant anteater animal
The giant anteater uses its long tongue to extract ants and termites from nests.
Unlike other anteaters, the giant anteater spends most of its time on the ground.
We saw a giant anteater slowly crossing the dirt road in the rainforest.
