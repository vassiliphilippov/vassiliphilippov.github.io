aloofness

noun
emotional or social distance
Pronunciation: / əˈluːfnəs /; / əˈlufnəs / (AmE), / əˈluːfnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being emotionally or socially distant, reserved, or uninvolved

Examples

Her aloofness in meetings
Despite her aloofness, she was secretly interested in the outcome of the discussion.
His aloofness at family gatherings made him seem unapproachable.
The manager's aloofness created tension among the employees.
Despite her aloofness, people are often drawn to her calm confidence.
Her aloofness during the meeting made it difficult to know what she was thinking.
His aloofness is often mistaken for arrogance, but he is simply shy around strangers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coldness - lack of emotional warmth or affection
  • synonym: detachment - lack of emotional involvement or connection
  • synonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern
  • antonym: warmth - showing friendliness, affection, or enthusiasm
