aloofness

noun
emotional or social distance

the quality or state of being emotionally or socially distant, reserved, or uninvolved

Examples

Her aloofness in meetings

His aloofness at family gatherings made him seem unapproachable.

Her aloofness during the meeting made it difficult to know what she was thinking.
His aloofness is often mistaken for arrogance, but he is simply shy around strangers.
