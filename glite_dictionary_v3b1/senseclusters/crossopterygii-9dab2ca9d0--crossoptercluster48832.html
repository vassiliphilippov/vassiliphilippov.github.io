Crossopterygii

noun
group of extinct lobe-finned fishes
9.9
Pronunciation: /krɑːs.ɑːp.tə.ˈrɪ.dʒiː/ (AmE), /krɒs.ɒp.tə.ˈrɪ.dʒiː/ (BrE)
