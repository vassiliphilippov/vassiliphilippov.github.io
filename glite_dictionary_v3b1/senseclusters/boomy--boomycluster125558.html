Examples

Synonyms

  • booming
    3.4 0.09/m
    booming is very active and growing quickly in markets, while boomy feels similar but often highlights noisy, strong expansion.
  • roaring
    4.1 0.03/m
    roaring is loud, very successful and profitable in business, while boomy means rapid growth but sounds less formal.
  • burgeoning
    6.5 0.7/m
    burgeoning means starting to grow quickly and often describes new businesses, while boomy implies broader, wide expansion.
  • prosperous
    4.1 8/m
    prosperous means wealthy or doing well over time, while boomy stresses fast, wide growth and sudden business expansion.
