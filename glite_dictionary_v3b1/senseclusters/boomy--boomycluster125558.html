boomy

adjective
rapidly growing
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːmi / (AmE), / ˈbuːmi / (BrE)
experiencing rapid growth, expansion, or prosperity, especially in business or the economy

Examples

Boomy economy
The boomy tech industry attracted many new startups to the city.
During the boomy years, unemployment rates dropped and wages increased.
Investors were eager to take advantage of the boomy real estate market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: booming - growing or developing quickly and successfully
  • antonym: declining - becoming smaller, weaker, or less successful
  • synonym: prosperous - successful and thriving, especially financially
