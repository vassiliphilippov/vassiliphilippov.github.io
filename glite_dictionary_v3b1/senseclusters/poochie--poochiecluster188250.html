poochie

noun
pet name for dog
Pronunciation: / ˈpuːtʃi / (AmE), / ˈpuːtʃi / (BrE)
an affectionate or playful term for a small dog

Examples

A little poochie
She dressed her poochie in a tiny sweater for the winter walk.
Every morning, the little poochie waits by the door for his treat.
The children laughed as the poochie chased its tail in circles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dog - a domesticated animal kept as a pet or for work
  • synonym: pooch - an informal term for a dog
