maple-leaf

noun
the national emblem of Canada
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪpəl liːf / (AmE), / ˈmeɪpəl liːf / (BrE)
the stylized leaf of a maple tree, used as the national emblem of Canada

Examples

A maple-leaf pin
As a proud Canadian, she wore a maple-leaf pin on her jacket.
The maple-leaf symbolizes unity, tolerance, and peace throughout Canada.
The red maple-leaf on the Canadian flag is recognized worldwide.
