scribal

adjective
of scribes
Pronunciation: / ˈskraɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈskraɪbəl / (BrE)
relating to scribes or the act of copying written texts by hand

Examples

scribal tradition
The scribal tradition preserved many ancient texts for future generations.
Researchers analyzed the scribal handwriting to date the manuscript.
Some scribal errors in the document changed the meaning of several passages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scriptorial - relating to writing or script, especially in manuscripts
