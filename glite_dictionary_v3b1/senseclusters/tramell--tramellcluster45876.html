tramell

noun
layered fishing net
9.9
Pronunciation: / trəˈmɛl / (AmE), / trəˈmɛl / (BrE)
a fishing net made of several layers of mesh, designed to entangle and catch fish

Examples

A tramell net
The crew carefully lowered the tramell into the water before sunrise.
After hours of fishing, the tramell was full of silver fish.
Local fishermen prefer using a tramell because it is effective for catching different species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gillnet - a fishing net that catches fish by their gills
  • synonym: trawl - a large net dragged along the sea bottom to catch fish
