profit

verb
gain an advantage or benefit
3.2 8.93/m
Pronunciation: / Ààpr…ëÀêf…™t / (AmE), / Ààpr…íf…™t / (BrE)
to get an advantage or benefit from something (including making money)

Examples

To profit from experience
Both countries profited from increased trade and cooperation.
He profited by listening to experienced colleagues in his field.
The team profited greatly from her leadership skills.
Many small businesses barely profit during economic downturns.
The company failed to profit from the early adoption of new technology.
You can profit greatly from investing in your education.

Synonyms

  • benefit
    2.3 64/m
