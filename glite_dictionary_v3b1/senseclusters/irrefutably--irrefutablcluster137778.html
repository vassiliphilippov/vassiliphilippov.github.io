irrefutably

adverb
undeniably
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˈfjuːtəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈfjuːtəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be disproved or denied

Examples

Is irrefutably true
The evidence was irrefutably clear, leaving no room for doubt in the courtroom.
She irrefutably proved her point with a series of well-researched facts.
The results irrefutably demonstrate the effectiveness of the new treatment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disputably - in a way that can be disputed or questioned
  • synonym: undeniably - in a way that cannot be denied
