raggie

noun
cleaning cloth
9.6
a small piece of cloth or rag used for cleaning or wiping surfaces

Examples

A cleaning raggie
She wiped the counter with a damp raggie after cooking dinner.
He keeps a raggie in his car to clean the windshield when it gets dirty.
After spilling juice, the child grabbed a raggie from the drawer to clean it up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rag - a piece of old cloth used for cleaning
  • synonym: towel - a piece of absorbent cloth for drying or wiping
