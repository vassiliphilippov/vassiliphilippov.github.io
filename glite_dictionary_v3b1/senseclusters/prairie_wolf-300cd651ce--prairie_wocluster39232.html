prairie wolf

noun
another name for the coyote
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛri wʊlf / (AmE), / ˈprɛəri wʊlf / (BrE)
a small wild wolf native to western North America; another name for the coyote

Examples

The prairie wolf howls
Despite its name, the prairie wolf inhabits forests and deserts as well as prairies.
Early settlers often mistook the prairie wolf for a small dog from a distance.
The distinctive howls of the prairie wolf can be heard at night across the plains.
