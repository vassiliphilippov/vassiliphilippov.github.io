kipuka

noun
island in lava flow
Pronunciation: / kiˈpuːkə / (AmE), / kiˈpuːkə / (BrE)
an area of land that remains untouched and surrounded by lava flows after a volcanic eruption

Examples

A kipuka area
Rare species of plants can survive in a kipuka after a volcanic eruption.
Scientists studied the unique ecosystem within the kipuka surrounded by hardened lava.
The hikers rested in a kipuka that was untouched by the recent lava flow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oasis - a fertile area in a desert where water is found
