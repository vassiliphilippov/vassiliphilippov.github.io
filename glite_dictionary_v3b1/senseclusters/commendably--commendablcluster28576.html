commendably

adverb
deserving praise
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɛndəbli / (AmE), / kəˈmɛndəbli / (BrE)
in a way that deserves praise or approval

Examples

To commendably admit mistake
He commendably admitted his mistake and worked hard to fix it.
The team commendably completed the project ahead of schedule despite many challenges.
She commendably stood up for what she believed was right.

Related Terms

  • synonym: admirably - in a way that arouses respect or approval
  • antonym: blameworthy - in a way that deserves blame or criticism
