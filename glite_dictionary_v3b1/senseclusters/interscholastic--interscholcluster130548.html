interscholastic

adjective
between secondary schools; especially competitions
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.tɚ.skəˈlæs.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪn.tə.skəˈlæs.tɪk / (BrE)
existing or taking place between secondary schools, especially in connection with competitions or activities involving students from different schools.

Examples

An interscholastic debate
Our school is hosting the annual interscholastic debate tournament next month.
Participation in interscholastic sports can help students build teamwork and discipline.
The new policy encourages more interscholastic cooperation in science and math programs.
