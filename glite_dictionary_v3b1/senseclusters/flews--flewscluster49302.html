flews

noun
hanging upper lips of some dogs
Pronunciation: / fluːz / (AmE), / fluːz / (BrE)
The thick, hanging upper lips of certain dogs, especially bloodhounds and similar breeds.

Examples

Dog flews
After drinking water, the beagle's flews were left wet and messy.
Some breeds are known for their pronounced flews, which give them a distinctive look.
The bloodhound's flews drooped noticeably as it sniffed along the trail.
