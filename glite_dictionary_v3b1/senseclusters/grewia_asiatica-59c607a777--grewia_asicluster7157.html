Grewia asiatica

noun
drought-resistant shrub with edible red fruits
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːwiə eɪˈʃiːətɪkə / (AmE), / ˈɡriːwiə æˈsiːatɪkə / (BrE)
A drought-resistant Asiatic shrub or small tree bearing small, pleasantly acidic, red edible fruits, often used in making sherbets.
