last-minute gift

noun
a present bought at the last moment
2.3 2.0
Pronunciation: /læst ˈmɪn.ət gɪft/ (AmE), /lɑːst ˈmɪn.ɪt gɪft/ (BrE)
A gift bought or arranged just before it is needed, typically due to lack of preparation.

Examples
