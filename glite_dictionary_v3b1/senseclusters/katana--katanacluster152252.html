katana

noun
Japanese samurai sword
Pronunciation: / kəˈtɑːnə / (AmE), / kəˈtɑːnə / (BrE)
a long, curved Japanese sword with a single edge, traditionally used by samurai

Examples

A katana sword
The skilled craftsman spent months forging a beautiful katana for the collector.
During the demonstration, the martial artist expertly wielded his katana with precision.
Legends tell of a katana so sharp it could cut through silk in midair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sabre - a sword with a curved blade, used especially by cavalry
  • synonym: sword - a weapon with a long metal blade and a handle
