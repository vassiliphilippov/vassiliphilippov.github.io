fancier

noun
a person with a strong interest in something
Pronunciation: / ˈfænsiər / (AmE), / ˈfænsɪə / (BrE)
a person who has a strong interest in or enthusiasm for a particular activity or thing, especially as a hobby

Examples

A fancier of pigeons
As a pigeon fancier, he spends hours caring for his birds in the backyard.
She's a well-known fancier of exotic orchids and often travels to find rare species.
The club is open to every dog fancier who wants to share training tips and advice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collector - a person who collects things as a hobby
  • synonym: enthusiast - a person who is very interested in a particular activity or subject
