passageway

noun
narrow connecting corridor or hall
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪdʒˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈpæsɪdʒweɪ /; / ˈpæsɪdʒˌweɪ / (BrE)
a long, narrow space that connects rooms, parts of a building, or places (such as a corridor or hallway)

Examples

A secret passageway
A secret passageway was discovered behind the library wall.
She hurried down the passageway to answer the doorbell.
The two offices are connected by a short passageway.
A secret passageway behind the bookshelf led to a hidden chamber.
Air flows through the nasal passageways when we breathe.
The nurse led us quietly down a narrow passageway to the examination room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corridor - a long passage in a building from which doors lead into rooms
  • synonym: duct - a tube or channel that carries a liquid, air, etc.
  • synonym: hallway - a corridor in a building
  • antonym: obstruction - something that blocks a passageway
  • antonym: room - an enclosed space within a building
