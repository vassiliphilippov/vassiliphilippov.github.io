disqualifier

noun
reason for ineligibility
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈkwɑːlɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / dɪsˈkwɒlɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
something that makes a person or thing ineligible or unsuitable for a particular activity, position, or benefit

Examples

A criminal disqualifier
A criminal record can be a serious disqualifier for certain jobs.
Not meeting the age requirement was the main disqualifier for the competition.
The presence of allergens in the product acted as a disqualifier for people with allergies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obstacle - something that blocks progress or prevents success
  • antonym: qualification - a condition that makes someone suitable for something
