agreeableness

noun
pleasant or enjoyable quality
7.3
Pronunciation: / əˈɡriː.ə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / əˈɡriː.ə.bəl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being pleasant or enjoyable, often due to agreeable conditions or surroundings

Examples

Agreeableness of the place
Her agreeableness contributed greatly to the friendly atmosphere in the office.
Many guests commented on the agreeableness of the hotel's atmosphere and staff.
The agreeableness of the weather made their picnic especially enjoyable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disagreeableness - the quality of being unpleasant, opposite of agreeableness
  • synonym: pleasantness - the state of being pleasant
