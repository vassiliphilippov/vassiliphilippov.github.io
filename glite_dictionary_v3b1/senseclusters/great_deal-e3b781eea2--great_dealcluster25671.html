great deal

noun
an excellent offer or discount
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt diːl / (AmE), / ɡreɪt diːl / (BrE)
an especially advantageous offer or discount, typically resulting in significant savings or value

Examples

A great deal on shoes
I got a great deal on this laptop during the holiday sale.
She was excited to find a great deal on her favorite brand of shoes.
This website is known for offering great deals on travel packages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bargain - something bought or offered at a price favorable to the buyer
  • antonym: rip-off - a transaction in which someone is overcharged or receives poor value
