Tsuga canadensis

noun
eastern hemlock tree (scientific name)
Pronunciation: / ˈtsuːɡə kəˈnædɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˈtsuːɡə kəˈnædɛnsɪs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the eastern hemlock, a large forest tree native to the eastern United States and Canada, valued for its wood and use as pulpwood.
