spiritual being

noun
incorporeal entity with supernatural powers
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪtʃuəl ˈbiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtjʊəl ˈbiːɪŋ / (BrE)
A non-physical entity believed to have powers to influence human affairs, such as a spirit, angel, or demon.

Examples

A spiritual being
Many cultures believe in the existence of a spiritual being guiding their destinies.
She claimed to have seen a spiritual being during her meditation.
The story describes a spiritual being interfering in the affairs of mortals.
