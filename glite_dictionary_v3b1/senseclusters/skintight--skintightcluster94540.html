skintight

adjective
very closely fitting to the body
4.1 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈskɪn.taɪt/ (AmE), /ˈskɪn.taɪt/ (BrE)
(of clothing) fitting very closely to the body so that it clings to the skin.

Examples

Skintight leggings
She wore a pair of skintight leggings to the gym.
Skaters often prefer skintight outfits for better aerodynamics.
The actor was dressed in a skintight superhero costume for the role.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loose - not fitting closely to the body
  • degree lower: tight - fitting closely, but not necessarily clinging to the skin
