seismological

adjective
relating to seismology (study of earthquakes)
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪzməˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsaɪzməˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with seismology, the scientific study of earthquakes and the movement of the Earth's crust

Examples

Recent seismological equipment
Recent advances in seismological equipment have improved early warning systems for tsunamis.
Researchers in a seismological laboratory monitor tremors around the clock.
The seismological report detailed the magnitude and location of the earthquake.
