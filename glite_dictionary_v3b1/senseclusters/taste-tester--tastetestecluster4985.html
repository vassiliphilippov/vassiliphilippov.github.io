taste-tester

noun
person who judges food or drink quality
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪst ˌtɛstər / (AmE), / ˈteɪst ˌtɛstə / (BrE)
a person who samples food or drink in order to judge its quality, flavor, or safety

Examples

Professional taste-tester
As a professional taste-tester, she evaluates dozens of products each week.
The company hired a taste-tester to ensure their new recipe met quality standards.
The role of a taste-tester requires a highly sensitive palate and attention to detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: food taster - a person employed to sample food to check for poison or quality
