tuck away

verb
eat a lot of food eagerly
7.3
Pronunciation: / tʌk əˈweɪ / (AmE), / tʌk əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to eat a large amount of food, often quickly or with enthusiasm

Examples

To tuck away food
After skipping lunch, he tucked away an enormous steak for dinner.
She tucked away a full plate of pasta before anyone else had finished.
The children tucked away the birthday cake in no time.
