Ornithomimus

proper noun
bird-like dinosaur genus
Pronunciation: / ɔːrˌnɪθəˈmaɪməs / (AmE), / ɔːˌnɪθəˈmaɪməs / (BrE)
a genus of fast, bird-like dinosaur that lived during the Late Cretaceous period
