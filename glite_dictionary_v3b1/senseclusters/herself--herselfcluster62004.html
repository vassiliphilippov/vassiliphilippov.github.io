herself

pronoun
female reflexive pronoun
Pronunciation: / hərˈsɛlf / (AmE), / həˈsɛlf / (BrE)
used to emphasize that the woman or girl mentioned did something alone or was directly involved

Examples

All by herself
She made the cake all by herself for her birthday party.
After the meeting, Maria decided to handle the project herself.
The artist painted the entire mural herself without any assistance.
She completed the entire project herself without any help from her colleagues.
The author herself attended the book signing event and greeted her fans.
After the accident, she drove herself to the hospital for treatment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: himself - reflexive pronoun for a man or boy
  • synonym: themselves - reflexive pronoun for more than one person
