transversely

adverb
across or at right angles
5.3
Pronunciation: / trænzˈvɝːsli / (AmE), / trɑːnzˈvɜːsli / (BrE)
In a direction or manner that is across or at right angles to the main direction or axis.

Examples

Placed transversely across
A beam placed transversely can help distribute the weight more evenly.
The logs were sliced transversely to show the growth rings clearly.
The muscle fibers run transversely across the abdomen.

Related Terms

  • antonym: longitudinally - in a longitudinal manner; along the length
