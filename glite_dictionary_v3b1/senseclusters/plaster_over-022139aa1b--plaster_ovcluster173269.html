plaster over

verb
cover with thick layer of plaster
1.0 3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈplæ.stər ˈoʊ.vər/ (AmE), /ˈplɑː.stə ˈəʊ.və/ (BrE)
to cover something with a thick or heavy layer of plaster, or a similar material, often to hide or repair imperfections

Examples

To plaster over cracks
After fixing the crack in the wall, they plastered over the repairs for a smooth finish.
Old posters were plastered over by the new ones advertising the latest movie.
She tried to plaster over the graffiti with a fresh coat of paint.

  • synonym: cover up - to conceal or hide completely
