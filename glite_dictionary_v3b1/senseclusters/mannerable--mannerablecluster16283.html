mannerable

adjective
polite; well-mannered
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmænərəbəl / (AmE), / ˈmænərəbəl / (BrE)
behaving politely and showing good manners

Examples

Mannerable children
The teacher was pleased to see such mannerable students in her classroom.
At the party, everyone noticed how mannerable the children were with the guests.
His parents taught him to be mannerable in all situations, especially when meeting new people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polite - showing good manners and respect for others
  • antonym: rude - not polite; showing bad manners
  • synonym: well-mannered - behaving in a socially acceptable and polite way
