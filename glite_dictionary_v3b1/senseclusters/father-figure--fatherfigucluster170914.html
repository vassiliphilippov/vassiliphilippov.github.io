father-figure

noun
a man acting as a paternal guide or influence
2.4 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈfɑː.ðər ˈfɪɡ.jər/ /; / /ˈfɑː.ðər ˈfɪɡ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈfɑː.ðə ˈfɪɡ.ə/ /; / /ˈfɑː.ðə ˈfɪɡ.jə/ (BrE)
a man who is regarded as a guiding, protective, or influential paternal presence, especially one whom someone identifies with psychologically
