father-figure

noun
a man acting as a paternal guide or influence
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑðɚ ˌfɪgjɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðə ˌfɪɡə / (BrE)
a man who is regarded as a guiding, protective, or influential paternal presence, especially one whom someone identifies with psychologically

Examples

A father-figure man
After his own father died, the coach became a father-figure to many of the children on the team.
Many people find a father-figure in a mentor or teacher who guides them through difficult times.
She always looked up to her uncle as a father-figure throughout her childhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: role model - a person looked to by others as an example to be imitated
