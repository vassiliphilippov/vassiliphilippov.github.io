dissimilarly

adverb
in a different way
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɪmələrli / (AmE), / dɪˈsɪmɪləli / (BrE)
in a way that is not similar; differently or in an unlike manner

Examples

Dress dissimilarly
The two sisters dressed dissimilarly for the party, each showing her own style.
Plants may react dissimilarly to changes in temperature depending on their species.
Although the houses look alike from outside, they are decorated dissimilarly inside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: differently - in a different or distinct way
  • antonym: similarly - in a similar or same way
