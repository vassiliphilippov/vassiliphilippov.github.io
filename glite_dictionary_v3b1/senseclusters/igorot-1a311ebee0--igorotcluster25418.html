Igorot

proper noun
Philippine indigenous group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌiːɡəˈroʊt / (AmE), / ˌiːɡəˈrəʊt / (BrE)
a member of any of several indigenous peoples native to the mountainous regions of northern Luzon in the Philippines

Examples

An Igorot person
The Igorot have preserved many of their traditional customs and crafts.
Tourists visit the region to see the famous rice terraces built by the Igorot people.
During the festival, several Igorot performed a traditional dance in colorful attire.
