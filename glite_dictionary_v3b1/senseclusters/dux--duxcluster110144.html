dux

noun
top student
9.1
Pronunciation: / dʌks / (AmE), / dʌks / (BrE)
the student with the highest academic achievement in a class or school, especially in Australia or New Zealand

Examples

School dux
At the end of the year, she was awarded the title of dux for her outstanding grades.
He worked hard all year to become the dux of his school.
The dux gave a speech at the graduation ceremony, inspiring the other students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: valedictorian - the top student in a graduating class, especially in the US
