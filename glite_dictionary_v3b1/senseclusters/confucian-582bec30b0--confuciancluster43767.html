Confucian

adjective
of or relating to Confucianism
Pronunciation: / kənˈfjuːʃən / (AmE), / kənˈfjuːʃən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Confucianism, the philosophical and ethical system based on the teachings of Confucius.

Examples

Confucian philosophy
Her research focuses on Confucian teachings and their impact on East Asian societies.
Many schools in China still teach Confucian principles as part of their curriculum.
The government's policies were influenced by Confucian values of respect for elders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Confucianism - the philosophical and ethical system of Confucius
