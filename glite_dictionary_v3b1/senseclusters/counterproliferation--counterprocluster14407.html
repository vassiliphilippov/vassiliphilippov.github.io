counterproliferation

adjective
preventing weapon spread
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərprəˌlɪfəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəprəˌlɪfəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
relating to actions or policies designed to prevent the spread or acquisition of weapons, especially weapons of mass destruction

Examples

Counterproliferation policy
The government announced new counterproliferation measures to stop the illegal trade of nuclear materials.
International cooperation is essential for effective counterproliferation efforts against chemical weapons.
The agency specializes in counterproliferation strategies to prevent the spread of dangerous technologies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonproliferation - preventing the increase or spread of weapons, especially nuclear weapons
