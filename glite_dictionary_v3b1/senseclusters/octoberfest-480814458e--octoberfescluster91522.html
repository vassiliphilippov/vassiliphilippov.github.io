Octoberfest

noun
a type of German lager beer
Pronunciation: / oʊˈtoʊbɚˌfɛst / (AmE), / ɒkˈtoʊbəˌfɛst / (BrE)
a strong, sweet, copper-colored lager originally brewed in Germany for the Oktoberfest celebration

Examples

A Octoberfest lager
Every autumn, local breweries produce a special Octoberfest that quickly sells out.
He enjoyed sampling the rich, malty flavor of the Octoberfest at the festival.
Imported Octoberfest lagers are popular at German-themed beer gardens.
