stepdown

noun
resignation from position

Examples

CEO stepdown
The company's shares fell after the sudden stepdown of its chief financial officer.
Her stepdown from the board surprised many of her colleagues.
After his stepdown, the organization began searching for a new leader.

Synonyms

  • stepping down
    3.7 1.15/m
    stepping down is a formal act of leaving a position of authority, while stepdown names that act in general usage.
  • resignation
    3.4 9.16/m
    resignation is the official act or written notice of leaving work, while stepdown describes leaving a post or role.
