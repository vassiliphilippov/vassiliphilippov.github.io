stepdown

noun
resignation from position
4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈstep.daʊn/ (AmE), /ˈstep.daʊn/ (BrE)
the act of leaving a position of authority or resigning from a job or role.

Examples

CEO stepdown
The company's shares fell after the sudden stepdown of its chief financial officer.
Her stepdown from the board surprised many of her colleagues.
After his stepdown, the organization began searching for a new leader.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resignation - the act of formally leaving a job or position
  • synonym: retirement - the act of leaving one's job and stopping work, usually due to age
