establisher

noun
person who founds
Pronunciation: / ɪˈstæblɪʃər / (AmE), / ɪˈstæblɪʃə / (BrE)
a person or thing that sets up, creates, or founds something such as an organization, institution, system, or set of guidelines

Examples

Establisher of a company
As the establisher of the charity, he dedicated years to helping the local community.
The establisher of the company was known for her innovative ideas and leadership.
Historians often debate who was the true establisher of the ancient city.
The establisher of the new guidelines worked closely with all departments to ensure success.
As the establisher of the charity, she was responsible for its mission and growth.
A clear process can be an effective establisher of good habits in the workplace.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abolisher - a person who puts an end to something
  • synonym: creator - a person who brings something into existence
  • synonym: founder - a person who establishes an institution or organization
  • antonym: successor - a person who follows or replaces another
