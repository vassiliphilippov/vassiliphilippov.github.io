intracapsular

adjective
within a capsule
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈkæpsjələr / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈkæpsjʊlə / (BrE)
located or occurring within a capsule, especially the capsule of a joint or organ

Examples

Intracapsular fracture
The doctor explained that the fracture was intracapsular and would require special treatment.
An intracapsular cataract extraction involves removing the entire lens and its capsule.
Damage to intracapsular ligaments can seriously affect joint stability.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extracapsular - located or occurring outside a capsule
