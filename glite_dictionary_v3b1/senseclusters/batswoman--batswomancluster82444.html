batswoman

noun
female cricket batter
Pronunciation: / ˈbætsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈbætswʊmən / (BrE)
A female player who bats, especially in the sport of cricket.

Examples

A batswoman in cricket
Each batswoman on the team has her own unique batting style.
Mithali Raj is considered one of the greatest batswomen in the history of cricket.
The young batswoman scored a century in her debut match.

Related Terms

  • synonym: batsman - a person (traditionally male or gender-neutral) who bats in cricket
  • synonym: batter - a person who is batting in sports (gender-neutral)
