phlegmatical

adjective
unemotional; calm and unexcitable
Pronunciation: / flɛɡˈmætɪkəl / (AmE), / flɛɡˈmatɪkəl / (BrE)
having or showing little emotion; calm and unexcitable.

Examples

A phlegmatical person

Her phlegmatical response to the shocking news surprised everyone in the room.
The phlegmatical expression on his face made it hard to tell what he was really feeling.
