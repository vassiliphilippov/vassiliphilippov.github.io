dense forest

noun
forest with closely packed trees
Pronunciation: / dɛns ˈfɔrɪst / (AmE), / dɛns ˈfɒrɪst / (BrE)
a forest with trees growing closely together, making movement and visibility difficult

Examples

A dense forest
It was almost impossible to see the sky through the dense forest overhead.
The hikers lost their way in the dense forest that covered the mountainside.
Wildlife thrives in the dense forest because it provides plenty of shelter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jungle - a dense, tropical forest
