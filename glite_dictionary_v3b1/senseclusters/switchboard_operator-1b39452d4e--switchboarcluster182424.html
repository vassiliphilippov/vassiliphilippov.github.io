switchboard operator

noun
person who connects telephone calls
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪtʃˌbɔrd ˌɑpəˈreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈswɪtʃ.bɔːd ˌɒp.ərˈeɪ.tər / (BrE)
a person whose job is to connect and direct telephone calls at a switchboard

Examples

The switchboard operator connects calls
Decades ago, every large company employed a switchboard operator to handle incoming calls.
The switchboard operator connected me to the manager's office within seconds.
When the line was busy, the switchboard operator politely asked me to wait.

Related Terms

  • synonym: receptionist - person who greets visitors and answers calls in an office
  • synonym: telephonist - person operating a telephone switchboard (mainly British)
