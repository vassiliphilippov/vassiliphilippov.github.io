footy

noun
baby sleepwear
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfʊ.tiː/ /; / /ˈfʊ.tɪ/ (AmE), /ˈfʊ.tiː/ /; / /ˈfʊ.tɪ/ (BrE)
a one-piece garment for babies or young children that covers the body and feet, like footed pajamas or rompers

Examples

A baby footy
The baby looked adorable in a soft footy decorated with stars and moons.
She packed an extra footy in the diaper bag for the daycare visit.
During winter, parents often dress their infants in a warm footy to keep them cozy at night.
The baby looked adorable in a striped footie on her first night home.
Parents often choose a warm footie to keep their infants cozy during winter.
He outgrew his favorite blue footie much faster than expected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: onesie - a one-piece garment for babies covering the torso and sometimes the legs and arms
  • synonym: pajamas - loose-fitting clothes worn for sleeping
  • synonym: romper - a one-piece garment for babies or young children, usually with short legs
