Rogerian

noun
follower of Carl Rogers
9.0
Pronunciation: /rɑː.ˈdʒɪr.iː.ən/ /; / /rɑː.ˈdʒɪə.riː.ən/ (AmE), /rɒ.ˈdʒɪə.ən/ /; / /rə.ˈdʒɪə.ən/ (BrE)
a person who supports or practices the theories or methods of Carl Rogers, especially in client-centered therapy

Examples

A Rogerian therapist
As a Rogerian, she focused on empathy and non-directive listening during therapy sessions.
The Rogerian believed that clients could find their own solutions through self-exploration.
Many Rogerians emphasize the importance of unconditional positive regard in counseling.
