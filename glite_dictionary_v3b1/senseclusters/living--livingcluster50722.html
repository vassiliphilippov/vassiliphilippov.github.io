living

adjective
lifelike; true to life
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a likeness, description, etc.) so realistic as to appear alive; lifelike.

Examples

Living image
She is the living image of her grandmother, both in appearance and mannerisms.
The artist created a living portrait that seemed to watch everyone in the room.
The novel offers a living depiction of 19th-century London.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dead - lacking animation or realism; not lifelike
  • synonym: lifelike - very realistic; closely resembling real life
