Avignon

proper noun
city in southeastern France, former seat of papacy
6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈæv.ɪ.njən/ /; / /ˈæv.ɪ.njɔːn/ (AmE), /ˈæv.ɪ.njɒn/ /; / /ˈæv.ɪ.njɔːn/ (BrE)
A city in southeastern France on the Rhone River, historically notable as the seat of the papacy from 1309 to 1377 and the residence of antipopes during the Great Schism.
