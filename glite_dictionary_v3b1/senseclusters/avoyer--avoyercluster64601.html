avoyer

noun
Swiss medieval magistrate
Pronunciation: / əˈvwɑɪər / (AmE), / əˈvwɑːjeɪ / (BrE)
a medieval official or magistrate in certain Swiss towns, especially responsible for presiding over councils

Examples

The avoyer leads council
In medieval Bern, the avoyer was responsible for leading the city council.
The avoyer played a key role in the administration of Swiss towns during the Middle Ages.
Historical records describe the avoyer as a respected figure in local government.
