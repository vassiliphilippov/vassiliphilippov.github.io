Hylocichla mustelina

noun
North American woodland thrush
Pronunciation: / haɪ.loʊˈsɪk.lə ˌmʌs.təˈlaɪ.nə / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪ.lə(ʊ)ˈkɪk.lə ˌmʌs.tɪˈliː.nə / (BrE)
a large North American thrush with a reddish-brown back, spotted breast, and a distinctive, melodious song, found in eastern American woodlands
