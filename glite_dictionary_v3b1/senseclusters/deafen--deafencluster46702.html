deafen

verb
make soundproof
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛfən / (AmE), / ˈdɛfən / (BrE)
to make (a room, building, etc.) soundproof, or protected from outside noise

Examples

To deafen a room
She hired a contractor to deafen her apartment and minimize sound from the street.
The studio engineers worked hard to deafen the recording booth before the session began.
They plan to deafen the new conference room so outside traffic noise won't disturb meetings.
