gendered

adjective
having grammatical gender
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛndərd / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛndəd / (BrE)
classified or distinguished according to grammatical gender, such as masculine or feminine forms in language.

Examples

Gendered nouns
In French, every noun is gendered as either masculine or feminine.
Some languages, like English, are less gendered than others in their grammar.
The word for 'sun' is gendered differently in German and Spanish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genderless - not having grammatical gender distinctions
