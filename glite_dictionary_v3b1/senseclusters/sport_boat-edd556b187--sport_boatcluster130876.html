sport boat

noun
recreational watercraft
3.1
Pronunciation: / spɔrt boʊt / (AmE), / spɔːt bəʊt / (BrE)
a small boat designed for recreational activities such as racing, water skiing, or other water sports

Examples

A sport boat race
They spent the afternoon racing their sport boat across the lake.
A sport boat is perfect for water skiing and wakeboarding with friends.
He stored his sport boat at the marina during the winter months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: speedboat - a fast boat designed for racing or quick travel on water
  • degree higher: yacht - a larger boat used for pleasure or racing
