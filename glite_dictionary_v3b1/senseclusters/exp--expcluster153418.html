exp

noun
expiration abbreviation
Pronunciation: / ɛks pi / (AmE), / ɛks pi / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'expiration' or 'expiry', used to indicate the date when something becomes invalid or unusable

Examples

Exp date label
Always check the exp date on food packaging before eating.
The coupon is no longer valid after the exp date printed on it.
She wrote the exp date clearly on the label for easy reference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expiration date - the date when something is no longer valid or safe to use
  • synonym: expiry - the end of a period after which something is no longer valid
