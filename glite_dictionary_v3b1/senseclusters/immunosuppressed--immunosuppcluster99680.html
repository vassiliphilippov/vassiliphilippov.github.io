immunosuppressed

adjective
having a weakened immune system
3.4 7.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˌmjunəsoʊˈprɛst / (AmE), / ɪˌmjuːnəʊsəˈprɛst / (BrE)
having a weakened or deficient immune response, making one more susceptible to infections or disease

Examples

Certain medications can leave patients immunosuppressed after organ transplants.
People who are immunosuppressed must take extra precautions to avoid infections.
The doctor explained that the flu poses greater risks for the immunosuppressed population.
