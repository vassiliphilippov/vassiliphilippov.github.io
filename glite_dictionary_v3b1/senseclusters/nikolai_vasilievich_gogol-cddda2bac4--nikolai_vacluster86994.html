Nikolai Vasilievich Gogol

proper noun
Russian author, pioneer of realism
Pronunciation: / nɪˈkoʊlaɪ vəˈsɪliəvɪtʃ ˈɡoʊɡəl / (AmE), / nɪˈkɒlaɪ vəˈsɪliəvɪtʃ ˈɡəʊɡɒl / (BrE)
