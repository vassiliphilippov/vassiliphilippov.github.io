Nikolai Vasilievich Gogol

proper noun
Russian author, pioneer of realism
7.9
Pronunciation: / nɪˈkoʊlaɪ vəˈsɪliəvɪtʃ ˈɡoʊɡəl / (AmE), / nɪˈkɒlaɪ vəˈsɪliəvɪtʃ ˈɡəʊɡɒl / (BrE)
(1809–1852) A Russian writer noted for introducing realism into Russian literature and for works such as 'Dead Souls' and 'The Overcoat'.
