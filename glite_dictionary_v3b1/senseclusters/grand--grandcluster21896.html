grand

noun
a type of large horizontal piano
Pronunciation: / ɡrænd / (AmE), / ɡrænd / (BrE)
a grand piano; a large, wing-shaped piano with horizontal strings, usually supported by three legs

Examples

A grand piano
She spent years saving up to buy herself a grand for her living room.
The grand was carefully tuned before the piano recital.
The concert hall featured a beautiful Steinway grand on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grand piano - a full form of a horizontal piano
  • antonym: upright - a vertically strung type of piano
