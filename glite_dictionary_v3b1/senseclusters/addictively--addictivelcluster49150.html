addictively

adverb
in a compelling way
Pronunciation: / əˈdɪktɪvli / (AmE), / əˈdɪktɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that makes you want to keep doing something again and again

Examples

To addictively check phone
She found herself addictively checking her phone every few minutes throughout the day.
The series is addictively entertaining, making it hard to watch just one episode.
He played the puzzle game addictively until he finally beat the highest score.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compulsively - in a way that is hard to control or stop
  • synonym: habitually - in a way that is done regularly or as a habit
