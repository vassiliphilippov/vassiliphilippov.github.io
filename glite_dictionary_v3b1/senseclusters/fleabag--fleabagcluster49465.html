fleabag

noun
dirty or shabby person
Pronunciation: / ˈfliˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈfliːˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a person or animal that is dirty, shabby, or unkempt, often in a humorous or mildly insulting way

Examples

A scruffy fleabag
After a week of camping without a shower, I felt like a total fleabag.
The stray cat was a real fleabag, but she was friendly and sweet.
He joked that his old coat made him look like a fleabag on the street.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentleman - a man who is clean and well-dressed
  • synonym: mutt - a dog of mixed breed, often scruffy
  • synonym: tramp - a person who is homeless and dirty
