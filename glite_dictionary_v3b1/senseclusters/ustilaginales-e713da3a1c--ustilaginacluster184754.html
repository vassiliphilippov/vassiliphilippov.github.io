Ustilaginales

noun
order of parasitic smut fungi
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːstɪˌlædʒəˈneɪliːz /; / ˌʌstɪˌlædʒɪˈneɪliːz / (AmE), / ˌjuːstɪˌlædʒəˈneɪliːz /; / ˌʌstɪˌladʒɪˈneɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of parasitic fungi that cause smut diseases in plants; sometimes classified within the class Tiliomycetes
