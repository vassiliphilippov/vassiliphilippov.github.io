artistic style

noun
characteristic way of creating art
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪstɪk staɪl / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪstɪk staɪl / (BrE)
the distinctive manner or approach in which an artist, school, or movement creates art, reflecting characteristic techniques, forms, and expressions

Examples

Unique artistic style
Every painter has a unique artistic style that sets them apart from others.
Her artistic style evolved significantly over the course of her career.
The exhibition showcased the artistic style of the Impressionist movement.
