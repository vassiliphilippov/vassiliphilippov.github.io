buzzy

adjective
humming or buzzing
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌzi / (AmE), / ˈbʌzi / (BrE)
making or producing a low, continuous humming or buzzing sound

Examples

Buzzy sound
The old refrigerator made a constant buzzy noise in the kitchen.
We could hear a buzzy sound coming from the streetlights at night.
The classroom was filled with a buzzy atmosphere as students whispered before the exam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humming - making a low, steady sound like a hum
  • antonym: silent - making no sound at all
