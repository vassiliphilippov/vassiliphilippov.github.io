nondiffusion

noun
lack of spreading
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.dɪˈfjuːʒən / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dɪˈfjuːʒən / (BrE)
the absence or prevention of spreading, scattering, or dispersing of something such as heat, particles, or substances

Examples

The nondiffusion of heat
The special coating ensures the nondiffusion of moisture through the walls.
Scientists studied the nondiffusion of particles in the new composite material.
Effective insulation relies on the nondiffusion of heat to maintain temperature.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diffusion - the process of spreading or dispersing something
