sterileness

noun
state of being free from germs
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛrəlˌnəs / (AmE), / ˈstɛrɪlnəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being free from pathogenic organisms, especially in relation to non-living objects.

Examples

Complete sterileness of instruments
After autoclaving, the instruments achieved complete sterileness.
Laboratory tests verified the sterileness of all the equipment before use.
The sterileness of the operating room is crucial to preventing infections.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sterility - the condition of being sterile, free from living microorganisms
