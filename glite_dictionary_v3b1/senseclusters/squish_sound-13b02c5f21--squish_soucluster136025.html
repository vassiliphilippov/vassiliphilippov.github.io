squish sound

noun
soft, wet noise
Pronunciation: / skwɪʃ saʊnd / (AmE), / skwɪʃ saʊnd / (BrE)
a soft, wet noise, typically made when something is compressed or stepped on

Examples

A squish sound when stepped
He made a squish sound when he stepped in the puddle.
Her boots left a squish sound on the wet grass after the rain.
The squish sound of the rotten fruit was unpleasant as she picked it up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: squelch - a squelching sound; similar wet, squishy noise
