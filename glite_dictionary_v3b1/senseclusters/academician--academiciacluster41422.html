academician

noun
university scholar or academy member
Pronunciation: / əˌkædəˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌkædəmɪˈsɪən /; / əˌkadɪˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
a member of an academy or a recognized scholar, especially in a college, university, or artistic institution

Examples

A respected academician
Dr. Lee is a respected academician who has published several influential books on physics.
Many academicians attended the international conference to present their latest research.
The award was given to an academician with decades of service in higher education.
Being named an academician is considered a great honor in the art community.
She became an academician after years of significant contributions to literature.
The renowned scientist was made an academician of the National Academy of Sciences.
As an academician, she was frequently invited to argue her theories at conferences.
Several academicians contributed to the new guidelines for doctoral research.
The panel included an academician renowned for his rigorous approach to philosophical debates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: academic - a person who teaches or conducts research at a college or university
  • synonym: fellow - an honorary member of a learned society or academy
  • synonym: scholar - a learned person, especially one who has profound knowledge of a particular subject
