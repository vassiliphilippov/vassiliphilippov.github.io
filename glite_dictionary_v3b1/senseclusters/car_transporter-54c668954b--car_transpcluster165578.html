car transporter

noun
truck for transporting cars
3.6
Pronunciation: / kɑr trænˈspɔrtɚ / (AmE), / kɑː trænˈspɔːtə / (BrE)
a large truck designed to carry multiple motor vehicles at once, typically for delivery to dealerships or customers

Examples

A car transporter truck
After the race, the damaged cars were loaded onto a car transporter for removal.
The dealership received its new vehicles on a car transporter yesterday.
We saw a car transporter loaded with shiny new sedans on the highway.
