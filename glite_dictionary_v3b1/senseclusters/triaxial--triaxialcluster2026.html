triaxial

adjective
with three axes
8.4
Pronunciation: / traɪˈæksiəl / (AmE), / traɪˈæksiəl / (BrE)
having or involving three axes or directions, especially at right angles to each other

Examples

Triaxial sensor
The engineer used a triaxial sensor to record vibrations in all three dimensions.
A triaxial test is commonly performed in soil mechanics to measure strength properties.
Modern smartphones often include a triaxial accelerometer for motion detection.
