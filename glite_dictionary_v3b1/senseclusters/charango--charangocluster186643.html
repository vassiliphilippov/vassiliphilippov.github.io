charango

noun
Andean string instrument
Pronunciation: / tʃəˈrɑːŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / tʃəˈræŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a small stringed musical instrument from South America, similar to a lute, often used in Andean music

Examples

A charango instrument
The musician played a lively tune on his charango during the festival.
She learned to play the charango while traveling in Bolivia.
The unique sound of the charango added a special touch to the folk song.

  • synonym: lute - a plucked string instrument with a rounded body
