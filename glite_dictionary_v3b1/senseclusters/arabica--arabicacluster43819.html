arabica

noun
coffee plant species
Pronunciation: / əˈræbɪkə / (AmE), / əˈræbɪkə / (BrE)
a species of coffee plant, Coffea arabica, widely grown for its high-quality coffee beans

Examples

Arabica plant variety
Many specialty cafes serve coffee made exclusively from arabica beans for their smooth flavor.
The high-altitude farms in Ethiopia are famous for growing wild arabica plants.
She prefers the taste of arabica over other types of coffee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coffee - a beverage made from roasted coffee beans
  • antonym: robusta - a species of coffee plant known for stronger, more bitter beans
