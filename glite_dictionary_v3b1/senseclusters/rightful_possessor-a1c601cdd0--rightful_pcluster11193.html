rightful possessor

noun
person legally entitled to property
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtfəl pəˈzɛsər / (AmE), / ˈraɪtfʊl pəˈzɛsə / (BrE)
a person who is legally entitled to possess or own property

Examples

The rightful possessor of property
Only the rightful possessor has the authority to sell this land.
The court ruled that she was the rightful possessor of the disputed property.
The necklace was returned to its rightful possessor after decades of legal battles.
