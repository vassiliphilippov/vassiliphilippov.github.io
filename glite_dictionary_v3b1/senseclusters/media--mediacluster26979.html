media

noun
storage devices
3.4 14/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmiÀêdi…ô / (AmE), / ÀàmiÀêdi…ô / (BrE)
physical devices or materials used to store, record, or transmit information, such as CDs, hard drives, or USB drives

Examples

Digital media
Always back up important files on external media to prevent data loss.
The IT department replaced all the old media with new solid-state drives.
Different types of media are used to store photos, music, and documents.

Synonyms

  • storage medium
    4.1 0.17/m
    a storage medium is a device or material for holding information, while media refers to physical items that store, record, or transmit data.
  • data-storage medium
    4.1 0.00/m
    a data-storage medium holds digital or analog files for later use, while media covers physical items that store, record, or transmit information.
  • medium
    4.1 14/m
    a medium is a channel for communicating or transmitting messages like TV or radio, while media means physical devices that store or record information.
