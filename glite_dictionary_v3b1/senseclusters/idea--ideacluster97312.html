idea

noun
a personal opinion or belief
1.0
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
a thought, suggestion, plan, intention, or belief formed in the mind

Examples

A new idea
He got the idea that they were avoiding him after the last meeting.
It's just my idea, but I think we should try a different approach.
She has the idea that he might be interested in her artwork.
His idea was to finish the book before the end of the week.
She went to the store with the idea of buying fresh vegetables.
The main idea of the meeting is to discuss future projects.
It was your idea to start the project early, wasn’t it?
She has an interesting idea for improving the school.
The idea of traveling alone both excited and frightened him.
