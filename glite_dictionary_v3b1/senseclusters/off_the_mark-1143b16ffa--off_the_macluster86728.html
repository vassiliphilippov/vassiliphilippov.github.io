off the mark

adjective
inaccurate; incorrect
1.8
Pronunciation: / ɑf ðə mɑrk / (AmE), / ɒf ðə mɑːk / (BrE)
not accurate or correct; not hitting the intended target or result

Examples

Off the mark answer
Her answer was off the mark and didn't address the main issue.
If your estimate is off the mark, it could affect the whole project.
The weather forecast was off the mark again this week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: on the mark - completely accurate or correct
  • synonym: wide of the mark - not accurate or not relevant; incorrect
