indefensibility

noun
impossibility of defense
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˌfɛnsəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˌfɛnsəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being impossible to defend, justify, or protect from attack or criticism

Examples

The lawyer argued about the indefensibility of the proposed law in court.
Many critics pointed out the indefensibility of the company's environmental practices.
He was forced to admit the indefensibility of his earlier statements.
The lawyer argued the indefensibility of the company's actions in court.
Due to its location, the castle's indefensibility became clear during the siege.
Critics pointed out the indefensibility of the policy from both ethical and practical perspectives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: untenability - the quality of being unable to be maintained or defended
  • synonym: vulnerability - the quality of being easily harmed or attacked
