Synonyms

    8.6 0.2/m
    protuberance is a rounded bulge or bump on a surface, while protrusion is a general term for anything that sticks out.
  • projection
    4.6 0.9/m
    projection is a part that extends outward from a surface, while protrusion focuses simply on sticking out without implying length.
  • extrusion
    8.1 0.1/m
    extrusion is often a body pushed out or formed by force, while protrusion is any part that sticks out.
  • bulge
    3.2 4/m
    bulge is a rounded swelling that pushes outward from a surface, while protrusion covers any form that sticks out.
  • prominence
    5.5 0.7/m
    prominence is a part that is especially high or noticeable in its surroundings, while protrusion just means sticking out.
  • jut
    6.5 0.2/m
    jut is a sharp point or short extension from an edge, while protrusion is any part that projects from a surface.
