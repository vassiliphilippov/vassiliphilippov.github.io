intramural

adjective
within the same institution
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈmjʊrəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈmjʊərəl / (BrE)
carried on within the limits of a particular institution, especially a school or college

Examples

Intramural sports games
Participation in intramural activities is encouraged as a way to foster school spirit.
She joined the college's intramural basketball league to stay active and meet new friends.
The university offers a variety of intramural sports for students each semester.

Related Terms
