annexure

noun
attached appendix
8.1
Pronunciation: / əˈnɛkʃər / (AmE), / əˈnɛkʃə / (BrE)
a document or appendix attached to a main document for reference or additional information

Examples

Contract annexure
The contract includes an annexure listing all the technical specifications.
Please review annexure B for the detailed financial statements.
All supporting documents are provided in the annexure at the end of this report.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attachment - a file or document added to another document or email
