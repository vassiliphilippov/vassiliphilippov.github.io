saskatoon

noun
edible North American berry
Pronunciation: / ˌsæskəˈtun / (AmE), / ˌsæskəˈtuːn / (BrE)
a small edible purple or red berry native to North America, produced by the saskatoon shrub (Amelanchier alnifolia)

Examples

The saskatoon berry
The saskatoon is prized for its sweet, nutty flavor in Indigenous cuisine.
We stopped by the roadside to pick fresh saskatoons during our summer road trip.
Wild saskatoons are used in pies, jams, and jellies throughout Canada.
