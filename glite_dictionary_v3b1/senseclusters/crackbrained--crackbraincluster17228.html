crackbrained

adjective
foolishly impractical or crazy
Pronunciation: / ˈkrækˌbreɪnd / (AmE), / ˈkrækˌbreɪnd / (BrE)
Foolish or wildly impractical; showing a lack of sense or responsibility.

Examples

Crackbrained idea
Despite being warned, they went ahead with their crackbrained scheme to travel across the desert without enough water.
His crackbrained idea to invest all their savings in one stock worried his friends.
Sarah thought the plan to build a treehouse on the edge of a cliff was completely crackbrained.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insane - extremely foolish or mentally unsound
  • antonym: sensible - showing good sense or judgment
