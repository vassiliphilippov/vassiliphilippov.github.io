nibbler

noun
person or animal that nibbles
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪb.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈnɪb.lə / (BrE)
a person or animal that takes small, repeated bites of food or other items

Examples

A slow nibbler
He was such a slow nibbler that his dessert lasted an hour.
My pet rabbit is a real nibbler, always working on her carrots.
The nibbler at the party preferred small snacks to full meals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nibble - to take small, quick bites
