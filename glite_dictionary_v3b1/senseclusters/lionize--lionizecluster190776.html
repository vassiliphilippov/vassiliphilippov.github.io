Synonyms

  • celebrate
    2.1 14/m
    celebrate calls for public praise or events to honor someone, while lionize means give them celebrity attention and fame.
  • glorify
    5.2 5.48/m
    glorify offers strong praise and may exaggerate someone's virtues, while lionize focuses on making them a public celebrity.
  • iconize
    7.9 0.00/m
    iconize turns a person into a lasting symbol admired by many, while lionize focuses on public celebrity and attention.
  • heroize
    7.9 0.02/m

  • idolize
    4.9 1.77/m
    idolize means admire or worship someone intensely and personally, while lionize means treat them publicly as a celebrity.
