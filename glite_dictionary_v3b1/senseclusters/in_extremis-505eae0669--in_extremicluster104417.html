in extremis

adverb
at the point of death
Pronunciation: / ɪn ɪkˈstriːmɪs / (AmE), / ɪn ɪkˈstriːmɪs / (BrE)
at the point of death; in a very critical or desperate situation, especially just before death

Examples

Dictate in extremis
He dictated his last will in extremis, moments before he passed away.
The patient was in extremis when the ambulance finally arrived.
The priest administered the rites to the wounded soldier who was in extremis.
