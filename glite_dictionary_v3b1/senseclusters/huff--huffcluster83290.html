huff

verb
blow air forcefully and loudly
4.3 3.8
Pronunciation: /hʌf/ (AmE), /hʌf/ (BrE)
to blow out air forcefully and loudly, often in effort or annoyance

Examples

To huff and puff
She huffed and wiped her brow after blowing at the dust on the old books.
The runner huffed as he neared the top of the steep hill.
The train huffed noisily as it pulled out of the station.
