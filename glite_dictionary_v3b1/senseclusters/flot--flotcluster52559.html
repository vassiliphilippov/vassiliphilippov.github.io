flot

noun
group of ships
9.9
Pronunciation: / flɒt / (AmE), / flɒt / (BrE)
an old term for a group or fleet of ships, especially a small fleet

Examples

A flot of ships
The enemy's flot approached the harbor at dawn.
A flot of ships sailed together across the misty sea.
In the old chronicles, a flot was often sent to protect the coast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fleet - a large group of ships under one command
  • synonym: flotilla - a small fleet of ships
