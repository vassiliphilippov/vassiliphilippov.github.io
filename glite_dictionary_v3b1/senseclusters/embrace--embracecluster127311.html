embrace

noun
an act of holding someone closely in your arms
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbreɪs / (AmE), / ɪmˈbreɪs / (BrE)
an act of holding someone or something closely in your arms, either literally (as a hug) or figuratively (being surrounded or enclosed)

Examples

A warm embrace
As the movie ended, the couple shared a tender embrace.
She greeted him with a warm embrace after his long journey.
Their embrace was full of comfort and reassurance.
She found comfort in the embrace of warm blankets on a cold night.
The cottage lay hidden in the embrace of the dense forest.
The valley was held in the gentle embrace of mist and morning light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enclosure - the state of being enclosed
  • degree lower: grasp - the act of holding something tightly
  • synonym: hug - an act of holding someone tightly in your arms for comfort or affection
