embrace

noun
an act of holding someone closely in your arms
4.4 2.8
Pronunciation: /ɪm.ˈbreɪs/ /; / /em.ˈbreɪs/ (AmE), /ɪm.ˈbreɪs/ /; / /em.ˈbreɪs/ (BrE)
an act of holding someone or something closely in your arms, either literally (as a hug) or figuratively (being surrounded or enclosed)

Examples

The cottage lay hidden in the embrace of the dense forest.
The valley was held in the gentle embrace of mist and morning light.
