etcetera

noun
additional unspecified items; and so forth
Pronunciation: / ɛtˈsɛtərə / (AmE), / ɛtˈsɛtərə / (BrE)
an additional unspecified item or items; things of a similar kind already mentioned or implied

Examples

Books, papers, etcetera
His room was cluttered with books, papers, clothes, and various etcetera scattered everywhere.
She brought snacks, drinks, napkins, and all the usual etcetera to the picnic.
The summary just listed dates, names, and other etcetera without much detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: et cetera - used at the end of a list to indicate more of the same kind; etc.
