yellowface

verb
to impersonate Asians
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊˌfeɪs / (BrE)
to portray an Asian person in performance by a non-Asian, often using makeup, costume, or stereotypes.

Examples

To yellowface a role
The film was criticized for choosing to yellowface instead of hiring an Asian actor.
Many people now recognize that to yellowface is disrespectful and offensive.
The director apologized after being accused of trying to yellowface a major character in the play.
