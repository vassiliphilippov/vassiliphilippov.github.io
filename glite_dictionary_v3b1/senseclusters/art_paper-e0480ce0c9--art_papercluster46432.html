art paper

noun
high-quality, smooth paper for art printing
2.3 3.6
Pronunciation: /ɑːrt ˈpeɪ.pər/ (AmE), /ɑːt ˈpeɪ.pə/ (BrE)
a high-quality, smooth paper often containing a filler such as china clay, used especially for printing art reproductions and high-end publications

Examples

High-quality art paper
She prefers to use art paper for her watercolor paintings because of its smooth texture.
The exhibition catalog stood out due to its thick art paper pages.
The magazine was printed on glossy art paper to enhance the photo quality.
