glowing

adjective
highly enthusiastic or complimentary
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊɪŋ / (BrE)
expressing strong approval or enthusiasm; highly complimentary

Examples

Glowing praise
He spoke in glowing terms about his former teacher.
Her boss gave her a glowing recommendation after the project was finished.
The movie received glowing reviews from critics nationwide.
