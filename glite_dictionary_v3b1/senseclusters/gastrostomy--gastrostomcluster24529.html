gastrostomy

noun
surgical opening into the stomach
6.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /gæs.ˈtrɑːs.tə.miː/ (AmE), /gæs.ˈtrɒs.tə.miː/ (BrE)
a surgical procedure to create an opening through the abdominal wall into the stomach, typically for feeding or drainage

Examples

A gastrostomy tube
A gastrostomy tube was inserted to allow for long-term enteral feeding.
Recovery from the gastrostomy procedure was uneventful and the site healed well.
The patient required a gastrostomy to receive nutrition due to swallowing difficulties.

  • synonym: colostomy - a surgical opening from the colon to the surface of the body
