hey presto

interjection
expression for a sudden, magical result
9.9
Pronunciation: / heɪ ˈprestoʊ / (AmE), / heɪ ˈprestəʊ / (BrE)
Used to indicate that something has happened suddenly or as if by magic.

Examples

Hey presto moment
He pulled the cloth and, hey presto, the table was perfectly set.
Just add hot water and, hey presto, your meal is ready!
She waved her hand over the box and, hey presto, a pigeon flew out.
