Ascaridae

noun
family of large parasitic roundworms
Pronunciation: / əˈskær.ɪˌdi /; / əˈskɛrɪˌdiː / (AmE), / æsˈkær.ɪ.diː /; / əˈskærɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of large roundworms that are parasites in the intestines of vertebrates
