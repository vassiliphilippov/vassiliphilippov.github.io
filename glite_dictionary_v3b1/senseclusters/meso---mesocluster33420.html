a prefix meaning 'middle' or 'intermediate' in size, position, or degree

Examples

Meso-america region
The prefix meso- is used in 'mesosphere' to refer to the middle layer of the atmosphere.
In biology, meso- can indicate something located between two other structures.
The term 'mesomorph' uses meso- to describe a body type that is intermediate between thin and heavy.
