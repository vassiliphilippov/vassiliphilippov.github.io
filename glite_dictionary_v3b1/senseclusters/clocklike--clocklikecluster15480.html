clocklike

adjective
resembling a clock
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈklɒkˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a clock in shape, appearance, or features, especially having a round face or visible hands and numbers

Examples

Clocklike face
The building's clocklike windows gave it a unique and recognizable appearance.
She wore a pendant with a clocklike design, complete with tiny hands and numbers.
The robot's face was oddly clocklike, with dials where eyes should be.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circular - having the shape of a circle
  • synonym: clockwork - operating with mechanical regularity like a clock
