shower

noun
party for giving gifts to someone
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊər / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊə / (BrE)
a party at which guests present gifts to someone, typically to celebrate a forthcoming event or milestone, such as a wedding or the birth of a baby

Examples

A baby shower
Her coworkers organized an office shower before she went on maternity leave.
Many friends brought thoughtful gifts to the baby shower.
They hosted a surprise shower for the bride-to-be last Saturday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: party - a social gathering of invited guests
