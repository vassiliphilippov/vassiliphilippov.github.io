psychoacoustics

noun
study of sound perception
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊəˈkuːstɪks / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊəˈkuːstɪks / (BrE)
the scientific study of how humans perceive and interpret sounds

Examples

Psychoacoustics research
Researchers in psychoacoustics investigate how people distinguish between different musical notes.
Advances in psychoacoustics have improved the design of hearing aids and audio technology.
The field of psychoacoustics explores why certain sounds are perceived as pleasant or unpleasant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acoustics - the science of sound and its properties
  • synonym: psychophysics - the study of relationships between physical stimuli and mental responses
