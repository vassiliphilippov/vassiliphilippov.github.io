maypop

noun
North American passionflower with egg-shaped fruit
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪˌpɑp / (AmE), / ˈmeɪˌpɒp / (BrE)
A North American passionflower (Passiflora incarnata) native to the southeastern United States, known for its large, egg-shaped fruit and showy flowers.

Examples

The maypop fruit
Children sometimes use maypop fruit for games, enjoying the loud pop when stepped on.

The maypop produces fruit in late summer that is prized by local wildlife.
