healthfully

adverb
in a healthy way
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlθfəli / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθfəli / (BrE)
in a way that promotes or maintains good health

Examples

To eat healthfully
She always tries to eat healthfully by choosing fresh fruits and vegetables.
After his illness, he began living more healthfully and exercising regularly.
It can be challenging to cook healthfully when you have a busy schedule.

Related Terms

  • synonym: healthily - in a way that is good for your health
  • antonym: unhealthily - in a way that is bad for your health
