Caitra

noun
first month of the Hindu calendar
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪtrə / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪtrə / (BrE)
The first month of the Hindu calendar, typically corresponding to March or April in the Gregorian calendar.

Examples

Month of Caitra
Holi is celebrated in the month of Caitra according to the Hindu calendar.
Many Hindu festivals are observed during Caitra, making it an important time of the year.
The weather in Caitra often marks the beginning of spring in many parts of India.
