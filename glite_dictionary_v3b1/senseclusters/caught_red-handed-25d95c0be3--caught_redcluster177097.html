caught red-handed

adjective
discovered while committing a wrongdoing
Pronunciation: / kɔt ˌrɛd ˈhændɪd / (AmE), / kɔːt ˌrɛd ˈhændɪd / (BrE)
discovered in the act of doing something wrong or illegal

Examples

Caught red-handed stealing money
Several employees were caught red-handed taking money from the cash register.
She was caught red-handed copying answers during the exam.
The burglar was caught red-handed as he tried to climb out of the window.
