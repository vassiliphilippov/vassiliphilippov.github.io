Bibb lettuce

noun
type of tender, crisp lettuce
5.7
Pronunciation: / bɪb ˈlɛtɪs / (AmE), / bɪb ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
A variety of lettuce with tender, relatively crisp leaves, often used in salads.

Examples

Fresh Bibb lettuce
He prefers to use Bibb lettuce in his salads for its soft texture and slightly sweet flavor.
The restaurant served the grilled chicken on a bed of fresh Bibb lettuce.
You can substitute romaine if you can't find Bibb lettuce at the store.

Related Terms
