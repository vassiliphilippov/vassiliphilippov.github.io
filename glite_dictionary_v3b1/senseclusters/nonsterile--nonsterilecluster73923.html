nonsterile

adjective
able to reproduce
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈstɛrəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈstɪəraɪl / (BrE)
able to produce offspring; not sterile or infertile

Examples

Nonsterile animals breed
The veterinarian confirmed that the animal was nonsterile and could have puppies.
After the treatment, the patient remained nonsterile and was able to conceive.
Scientists studied the nonsterile plants to improve crop yields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fertile - able to produce offspring or support growth
  • antonym: sterile - unable to produce offspring
