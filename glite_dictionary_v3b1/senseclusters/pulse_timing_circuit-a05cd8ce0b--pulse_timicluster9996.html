pulse timing circuit

noun
electronic circuit that controls pulse timing
2.3
Pronunciation: / pʌls ˈtaɪmɪŋ ˈsɝː.kɪt / (AmE), / pʌls ˈtaɪmɪŋ ˈsɜː.kɪt / (BrE)

Examples
