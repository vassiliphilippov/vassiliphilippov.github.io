hyaloid membrane

noun
transparent membrane in the eye
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪ.ə.lɔɪd ˈmɛm.breɪn / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.əlɔɪd ˈmɛm.breɪn / (BrE)
the transparent membrane that encloses the vitreous humor in the eye and separates it from the retina

Examples

The hyaloid membrane in eye
Damage to the hyaloid membrane can lead to complications in the vitreous body.
In some conditions, the hyaloid membrane may become opaque and affect vision.
The surgeon carefully separated the hyaloid membrane from the retina during the eye operation.
