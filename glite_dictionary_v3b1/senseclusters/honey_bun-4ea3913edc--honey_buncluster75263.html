honey bun

noun
sweet pastry with honey or sugar and nuts
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌni bʌn / (AmE), / ˈhʌni bʌn / (BrE)
a sweet, sticky pastry, often spiral-shaped and flavored with honey, sugar, cinnamon, and sometimes nuts

Examples

A honey bun pastry

The bakery sells honey buns fresh out of the oven every day.
Every morning, he buys a honey bun from the corner bakery.
She warmed her honey bun in the microwave before eating it.
The smell of fresh honey buns filled the kitchen.
