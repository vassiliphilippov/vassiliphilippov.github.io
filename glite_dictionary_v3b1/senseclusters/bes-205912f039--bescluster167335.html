Bes

proper noun
Egyptian household god
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɛs / (AmE), / bɛs / (BrE)
an ancient Egyptian dwarf-god who protected households, mothers, and children, and was associated with music and dance

Examples

Bes god statue
Families in ancient Egypt often placed images of Bes in their homes for protection.
The goddess Hathor and Bes were both linked to music and joy in Egyptian mythology.
Amulets of Bes were worn to safeguard mothers and children during childbirth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Anubis - an ancient Egyptian god associated with mummification and the afterlife
  • synonym: Isis - an ancient Egyptian goddess of magic and motherhood
