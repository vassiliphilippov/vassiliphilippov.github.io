sense of déjà vu

noun
feeling of having experienced something before
1.9 5.3
Pronunciation: /sens əv ˈdeɪ.ʒɑː vuː/ /; / /sens əv ˈdeɪ.ʒə vuː/ (AmE), /sens əv ˈdeɪ.ʒɑː vuː/ /; / /sens əv ˈdeɪ.ʒə vuː/ (BrE)
the feeling that one has already experienced the present situation, although it is actually happening for the first time

Examples

A sense of déjà vu
Every time I visit this town, I get a sense of déjà vu that I can't quite explain.
The movie gave me a sense of déjà vu, as if I had watched it before.
Upon hearing the story, she was overtaken by a sense of déjà vu that lingered for days.
