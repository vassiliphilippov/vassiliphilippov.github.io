butterhead

noun
soft lettuce variety
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtərˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌhɛd / (BrE)
a type of lettuce with soft, round leaves and a mild, buttery flavor and texture

Examples

A butterhead lettuce
She prefers butterhead in her salads because of its tender leaves.
The market had fresh butterhead available alongside other types of lettuce.
A sandwich with butterhead adds a soft and mild flavor to the meal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butter lettuce - another name for butterhead lettuce
  • antonym: romaine - a crisp variety of lettuce with long, sturdy leaves
