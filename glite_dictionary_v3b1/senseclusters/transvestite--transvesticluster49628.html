transvestite

noun
person who cross-dresses for sexual pleasure
Pronunciation: / trænzˈvɛstaɪt / (AmE), / tranzˈvɛstʌɪt /; / tranzˈvɛstaɪt / (BrE)
a person who wears clothes traditionally associated with the opposite sex, often for personal expression or sexual reasons (now considered outdated and sometimes offensive)

Some people mistakenly confuse the terms transvestite and transgender, though they refer to different experiences.
The documentary followed a transvestite as they navigated daily challenges in their community.
