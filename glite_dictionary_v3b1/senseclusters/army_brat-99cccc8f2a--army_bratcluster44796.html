army brat

noun
child of a U.S. Army officer
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrmi bræt / (AmE), / ˈɑːmi bræt / (BrE)
a child of a career officer in the United States Army, often raised on military bases and familiar with military life

Examples

As an army brat, he attended schools in five different countries.

Many army brats find it easy to adapt to new environments.
