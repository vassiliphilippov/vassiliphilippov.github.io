count off

verb
to call out numbers in order (in a group)
Pronunciation: / kaʊnt ɔf / (AmE), / kaʊnt ɒf / (BrE)
(of a group) to call out numbers in turn, usually to assign positions or organize sequence.

Examples

To count off numbers
