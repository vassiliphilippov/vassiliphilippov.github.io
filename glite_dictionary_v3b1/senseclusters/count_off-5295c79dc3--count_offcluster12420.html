count off

verb
to call out numbers in order (in a group)
5.0
Pronunciation: / kaʊnt ɔf / (AmE), / kaʊnt ɒf / (BrE)
(of a group) to call out numbers in turn, usually to assign positions or organize sequence.

Examples

To count off numbers
As part of the drill, the soldiers had to count off from left to right.
Each student counted off to ensure everyone was present and in order.
The coach asked the players to count off so he could divide them into two teams.
