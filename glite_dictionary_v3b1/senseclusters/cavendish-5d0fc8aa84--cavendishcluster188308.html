Cavendish

noun
processed pipe tobacco
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkævəndɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkævəndɪʃ / (BrE)
a type of tobacco that is pressed, heated, and often sweetened, mainly used for pipe or chewing tobacco

Examples

Cavendish tobacco blend
He preferred the smooth flavor of Cavendish in his evening pipe.
The shop offered several blends, but Cavendish was the most popular among regular customers.
She bought a pouch of Cavendish to try in her new pipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Burley - a type of light air-cured tobacco often used in blends
  • synonym: Virginia - a popular type of tobacco leaf used in cigarettes and pipe tobacco
