Shintoist

adjective
of or relating to Shintoism
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪn.toʊ.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈʃɪn.təʊ.ɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Shintoism, the indigenous religion of Japan that emphasizes ritual practices and belief in kami (spirits)

Examples

Shintoist shrine
Many Shintoist shrines are located throughout Japan, each with its own unique history.
The ceremony included several Shintoist customs, such as offering food and sake to the kami.
The festival was celebrated in a traditional Shintoist manner, with rituals honoring the local spirits.
