bluewing

noun
small duck with blue wing patches
8.1 6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈbluː.wɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbluː.wɪŋ/ (BrE)
A type of small North American duck, also called the blue-winged teal, known for its distinctive bluish wing patches.

Examples

The bluewing duck

During migration, bluewing flocks can often be seen rising from wetlands.
The bluewing is prized among birdwatchers for its colorful plumage.
