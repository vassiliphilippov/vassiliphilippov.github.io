birdfeeder

noun
outdoor device for feeding wild birds
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝdˌfiːdər / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌfiːdə / (BrE)
A container or structure placed outdoors to supply food for wild birds.

Examples

A birdfeeder with seeds
Every morning, she refills the birdfeeder with fresh seeds for the sparrows.
The squirrels keep trying to get food from our birdfeeder.
We hung a birdfeeder outside the kitchen window to attract more finches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bird table - outdoor structure for feeding wild birds, especially in the UK
