outstand

verb
to stand out
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈstænd / (AmE), / aʊtˈstænd / (BrE)
to be prominent or noticeable compared to others; to stand out distinctly

Examples

Outstand in a crowd
Her creative ideas always outstand in group discussions.
The bright red building outstands among the gray offices on the street.
His kindness and generosity truly outstand in the community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blend in - to not be easily noticed; to be similar to others
  • synonym: stand out - to be easily seen or noticed
