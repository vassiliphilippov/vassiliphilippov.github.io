hot air

noun
heated air that rises
Pronunciation: / hɑt ɛr / (AmE), / hɒt eə / (BrE)
air that has been heated and tends to rise, especially as used in scientific or everyday contexts.

Examples

Hot air rises
In summer, hot air rising from the pavement can create shimmering effects.
The balloon was lifted by the hot air inside its envelope.
The heater pumped hot air throughout the entire house.
