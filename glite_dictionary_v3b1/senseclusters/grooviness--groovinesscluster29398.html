grooviness

noun
having grooves
Pronunciation: / ˈɡruːvinəs / (AmE), / ˈɡruːvinəs / (BrE)
the condition of having grooves, channels, or ridges on a surface

Examples

The grooviness of a record
The grooviness of the vinyl record helps the needle produce clear sound.
Engineers measured the grooviness of the metal parts to ensure a perfect fit.
The grooviness on the tire's surface improves traction on wet roads.
