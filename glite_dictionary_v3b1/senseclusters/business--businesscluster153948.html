Synonyms

  • business activity
    3.4 0.60/m
    business activity refers to actions taken to run a commercial enterprise, while business covers the whole trade or company running those actions.
  • commercial activity
    3.2 0.61/m

  • commerce
    2.7 11/m
    commerce means buying and selling, often on a larger or international scale, while business covers all commercial activity and enterprises.
