nephew

noun
son of your sibling
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛfju / (AmE), / ˈnɛf.juː / (BrE)
the son of your brother or sister, or of your spouse's brother or sister

Examples

My nephew visits
Although I have three nephews, I don't get to see them very often.
My nephew came to visit us during his school vacation.
She bought a birthday present for her youngest nephew.
