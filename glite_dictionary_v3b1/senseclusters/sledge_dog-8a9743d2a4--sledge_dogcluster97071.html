sledge dog

noun
dog that pulls a sled
Pronunciation: / slɛdʒ dɔɡ / (AmE), / slɛdʒ dɒɡ / (BrE)
a dog trained to pull a sled, typically working in teams in snowy regions

Examples

A sledge dog team
He adopted a retired sledge dog that used to compete in long-distance races.
In Alaska, the life of a sledge dog can be both challenging and rewarding.
The sledge dog teams raced across the icy tundra, pulling heavy supplies behind them.
