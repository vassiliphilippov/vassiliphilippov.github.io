bioenergetics

noun
body-based psychotherapy
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪks / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊˌɛnəˈdʒetɪks / (BrE)
a form of body-oriented psychotherapy that uses physical exercises and breathing techniques to release emotional tension

Examples

Bioenergetics therapy session
After months of bioenergetics, he felt more relaxed and aware of his body.
The therapist recommended bioenergetics to help her manage stress and emotional blockages.
Many people find bioenergetics helpful for releasing tension stored in the body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: body psychotherapy - psychotherapy focusing on the connection between mind and body
  • antonym: talk therapy - psychotherapy based mainly on verbal communication
