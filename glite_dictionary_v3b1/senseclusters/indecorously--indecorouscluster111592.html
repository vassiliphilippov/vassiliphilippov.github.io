indecorously

adverb
in an improper or unsuitable way
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˈdɛkərəsli / (AmE), / ˌɪnˈdɛkərəsli / (BrE)
In a manner lacking propriety or decorum; in an improper or inappropriate way.

Examples

Behave indecorously at dinner
She behaved indecorously at the formal dinner, drawing disapproving looks from those around her.
The guests laughed indecorously during the solemn ceremony, shocking the other attendees.
The students indecorously interrupted their teacher, disregarding all classroom etiquette.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decorously - in a manner that shows good taste or propriety
  • synonym: indecorous - lacking propriety or good taste; improper
