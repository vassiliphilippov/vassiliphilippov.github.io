mortiser

noun
wood joint craftsperson
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrtɪsər / (AmE), / ˈmɔːtɪsə / (BrE)
a person who cuts mortises in wood, especially as part of making joints in carpentry or woodworking

Examples

Skilled mortiser worker
The workshop hired a skilled mortiser to create precise joints for the new furniture.
As a professional mortiser, she specialized in restoring antique wooden doors and frames.
The mortiser carefully measured each piece of wood before cutting the mortises.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpenter - a person who makes and repairs wooden objects and structures
  • synonym: joiner - a craftsperson who joins pieces of wood, especially for furniture or fittings
