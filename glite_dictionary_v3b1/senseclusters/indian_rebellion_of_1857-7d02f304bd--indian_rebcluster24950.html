Indian Rebellion of 1857

noun
major Indian uprising against British rule (1857–58)
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.di.ən rɪˈbɛl.jən əv ˌeɪtˈtiːn ˈfɪf.ti ˈsɛv.ən / (AmE), / ˈɪn.dɪ.ən rɪˈbɛl.jən əv ˌeɪtˈiːn ˈfɪf.ti ˈsɛv.ən / (BrE)
a major uprising against British rule in India in 1857–1858, involving widespread revolts and battles, especially by Indian soldiers.

Examples

Indian Rebellion of 1857 uprising
