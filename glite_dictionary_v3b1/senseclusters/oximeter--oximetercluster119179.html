oximeter

noun
device for measuring blood oxygen

Pronunciation: /ɑːk.ˈsɪm.ə.tər/ /; / /ɑːk.ˈsɪ.mɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ɒk.ˈsɪ.mɪ.tə/ /; / /ɒk.ˈsɪm.ə.tə/ (BrE)
A medical device used to measure the level of oxygen in arterial blood.

Examples

A oximeter device
Because her oximeter readings were low, the doctor ordered supplemental oxygen.
Portable oximeters are commonly used to monitor patients with lung conditions at home.
The nurse used an oximeter to check the patient's oxygen levels during the procedure.
