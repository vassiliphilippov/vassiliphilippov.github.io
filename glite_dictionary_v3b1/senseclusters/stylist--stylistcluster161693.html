stylist

noun
professional who plans outfits and looks
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪlɪst / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlɪst / (BrE)
a professional who selects and coordinates clothing, accessories, and the overall appearance of people for clients, photo shoots, performances, or media productions.

Examples

Fashion stylist
The magazine's stylist pulled a sleek, monochrome wardrobe for the shoot.
She hired a stylist to coordinate outfits for the awards ceremony and private fittings.
On set, the director asked the stylist to change the actor's jacket to something brighter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fashion stylist - a professional who plans and selects clothing and accessories for photoshoots, celebrities, or clients
  • synonym: image consultant - a professional who advises clients on appearance, dress, and personal style
