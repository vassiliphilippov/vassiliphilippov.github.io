alliaceous plant

noun
bulbous plant with onion-like odor
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌæl.iˈeɪ.ʃəs plænt / (AmE), / ˌal.iˈeɪ.ʃəs plɑːnt / (BrE)
A bulbous plant of the onion family, typically having a characteristic pungent odor.

Examples

An alliaceous plant bulb
Some people are allergic to the distinctive chemicals found in alliaceous plants.
The garden included several alliaceous plants such as onions, garlic, and chives.
The pungent smell of alliaceous plants is unmistakable, especially during harvest season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: garlic - a strong-smelling pungent-tasting bulb, used as food
  • synonym: onion - a widely cultivated edible bulb
