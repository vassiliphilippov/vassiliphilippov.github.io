rotisserie

noun
restaurant specializing in roasted meats
Pronunciation: / roʊˈtɪsəri / (AmE), / rəʊˈtɪsəri / (BrE)
a restaurant that specializes in roasted or barbecued meats, often cooked on a spit

Examples

Rotisserie restaurant
The rotisserie offers a wide selection of spit-roasted chicken and pork.
They dined at a famous rotisserie on the edge of town.
We reserved a table at the new rotisserie for our family dinner.
