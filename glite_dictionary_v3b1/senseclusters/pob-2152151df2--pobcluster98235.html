POB

noun
abbreviation for 'Post Office Box'
Pronunciation: / pi oʊ bi / (AmE), / piː əʊ biː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for 'Post Office Box,' a numbered compartment in a post office used for receiving mail to be collected by the addressee.

Examples

A POB number
He rents a POB for all his business correspondence.
Please send your documents to my POB for faster delivery.
The notice was addressed to a POB instead of a street address.
