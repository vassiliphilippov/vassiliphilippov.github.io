cutaneous sensation

noun
a sensation felt through the skin
4.4 4.8
Pronunciation: /kjuː.ˈteɪ.niː.əs sɛn.ˈseɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kjuː.ˈteɪ.niː.əs sɛn.ˈseɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
