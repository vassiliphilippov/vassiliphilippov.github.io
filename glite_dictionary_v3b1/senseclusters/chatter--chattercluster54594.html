chatter

noun
animal high-pitched rapid noise
3.4 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃæ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈtʃæ.tə/ (BrE)
a series of rapid, high-pitched, repeated noises, such as those made by animals or machines

Examples

Monkey chatter
Monkey chatter echoed through the rainforest canopy as the sun rose.
The chatter of sparrows filled the early morning air.
The constant chatter from the parakeets made it hard to concentrate.
Factory workers wore ear protection to guard against the constant chatter of the machines.
The engine's chatter grew louder as the car accelerated down the road.
There was an odd chatter coming from the washing machine during the spin cycle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chirp - a short, sharp sound made by small birds or insects
  • synonym: rattle - a series of short, sharp noises made by machine parts
