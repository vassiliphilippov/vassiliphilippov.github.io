undetached

adjective
emotionally involved
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈtætʃt / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈtætʃt / (BrE)
feeling or showing emotional involvement, concern, or attachment; not emotionally detached.

Examples

Undetached feelings
Even after hearing the bad news, she remained undetached and offered comfort to her colleagues.
His undetached attitude made him a loyal and supportive friend during difficult times.
Teachers who are undetached often build stronger relationships with their students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attached - emotionally connected or involved
  • antonym: detached - emotionally uninvolved or distant
