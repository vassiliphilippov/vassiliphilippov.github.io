rubric

noun
instructions for church services (often printed in red)
Pronunciation: / ˈruː.brɪk / (AmE), / ˈruː.brɪk / (BrE)
an established rule, guideline, or direction, especially for conduct or procedure

Please follow the rubric when filling out your application forms.
The department issued a new rubric for evaluating student performance.
