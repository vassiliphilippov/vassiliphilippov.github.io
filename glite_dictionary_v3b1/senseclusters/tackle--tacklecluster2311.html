tackle

verb
to stop an opponent by knocking them down (sports)
2.3 2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtækəl / (AmE), / ˈtækəl / (BrE)
to seize and throw down an opponent, especially the player carrying the ball, in sports such as football or rugby

Examples

To tackle a player
In rugby, it's legal to tackle a player as long as you avoid dangerous contact.
She sprinted across the field to tackle her opponent and stop a goal.
The defender managed to tackle the running back just before he reached the end zone.
