embroidery needle

noun
needle with large eye for embroidery
6.1
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbroʊdəri ˈniːdəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈbrəʊdəri ˈniːdl / (BrE)
a long needle with a large eye, designed for threading heavy darning or embroidery thread
