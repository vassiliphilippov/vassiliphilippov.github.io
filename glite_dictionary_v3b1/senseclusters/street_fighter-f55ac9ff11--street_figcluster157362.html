street fighter

noun
aggressive, unscrupulous competitor
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstrit ˌfaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈstriːt ˌfaɪtə / (BrE)
a person who is very aggressive and willing to use underhanded or unscrupulous methods, especially in competition or conflict

Examples

A political street fighter
His reputation as a street fighter helped him win elections in tough neighborhoods.
The politician was known as a street fighter who never shied away from confrontation.
You need to be more than just tough to beat a seasoned street fighter in business.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brawler - a person who fights in a rough and noisy way, especially in public
  • antonym: gentleman - a man who behaves in a very polite and honorable way (antonym in the context of fair and restrained conduct)
