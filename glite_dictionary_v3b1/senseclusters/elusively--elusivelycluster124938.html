elusively

adverb
difficult to grasp
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈluːsɪvli / (AmE), / ɪˈluːsɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that is difficult to find, catch, or understand

Examples

Elusively hidden
The solution to the puzzle remained elusively hidden despite hours of searching.
She smiled elusively, leaving everyone wondering what she really meant.
The meaning of the poem danced elusively just beyond his understanding.
