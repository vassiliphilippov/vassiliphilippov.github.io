cellophane

noun
transparent moisture-proof wrapping material
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛl.əˌfeɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.ə.feɪn / (BrE)
a thin, transparent, paperlike material that is impervious to moisture and used for wrapping food, candy, and other items

Examples

A cellophane wrap
Cigarettes are often sold in packs covered with cellophane.
She wrapped the cookies in cellophane to keep them fresh.
The bouquet was neatly packaged in colorful cellophane.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plastic wrap - a thin, transparent plastic film used for sealing food items
