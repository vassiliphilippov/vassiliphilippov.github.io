abjectness

noun
extreme humility
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈæbdʒɛktnəs / (AmE), / ˈæbdʒɛktnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely humble, submissive, or lacking in pride or dignity

Examples

The abjectness of his apology
The abjectness of his apology made everyone in the room uncomfortable.
She could not bear the abjectness of begging for help after losing her job.
His abjectness in the face of criticism surprised his colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humiliation - the state of being made to feel ashamed or foolish
  • antonym: pride - a feeling of self-respect and personal worth
