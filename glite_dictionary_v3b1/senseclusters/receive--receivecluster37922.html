Examples

To receive praise
Her performance received widespread acclaim from critics.

The proposal received very little attention from the media.
Her latest novel was received with great enthusiasm by the critics.
His speech received a mixed reaction from the audience.

Synonyms

  • get
    3.5 609/m
    get is an informal verb that often shows becoming or suffering from something, while receive is more formal and means being given or affected.
  • experience
    2.1 140/m
    experience means going through an event or feeling and noticing it, while receive focuses on being given or acted upon by others.
  • have
    1.0 1188/m
    have often shows a state or possession like illness or feeling, while receive implies being given something or treated by someone.
