channelization

noun
management through specified communication channels
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃænəlɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌtʃæn.ə.ləˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌtʃænəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌtʃæn.ə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of managing or directing communication or activities through specified channels, especially to improve organization or efficiency

Examples

Channelization of information
Effective channelization of information can significantly reduce misunderstandings within large organizations.
The new policy emphasized channelization of all feedback through the official online portal.
They designed a system for the channelization of customer support queries to improve response times.
Efficient channelisation of information is critical to avoiding misunderstandings in large organizations.
Proper channelisation of complaints ensures that issues are addressed quickly by the right department.
The channelisation of funds helped streamline the charity's operations.
