hazel

noun
light brown color, often of eyes
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈheɪzəl / (BrE)
A light brown color with a yellowish or reddish tint; particularly a greenish-brown shade when describing eyes.

Examples

Hazel eyes
He wore a shirt in a soft hazel shade that matched the autumn leaves.
Her eyes were a beautiful hazel, sparkling in the sunlight.
The artist mixed yellow and brown to create the perfect hazel color for the portrait's eyes.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: brown - a color between red and yellow on the spectrum of visible light
  • degree higher: green - the color between blue and yellow in the spectrum
