antique

adjective
old-fashioned or out of fashion
Pronunciation: / ænˈtik / (AmE), / ænˈtiːk / (BrE)
(of style, appearance, or ideas) old-fashioned or out of fashion; not in contemporary use.

Examples

Antique style clothes

The furniture in the lobby has a rather antique appearance, reminiscent of the early 20th century.
