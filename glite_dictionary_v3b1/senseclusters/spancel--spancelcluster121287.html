spancel

noun
animal leg restraint
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspænsəl / (AmE), / ˈspænsəl / (BrE)
a rope or band used to tie the legs of an animal, especially cattle, to prevent movement

Examples

A spancel for cattle
The farmer tied a spancel around the cow's legs before milking.
Without a spancel, the young calf kept wandering off from the herd.
He made a simple spancel from a piece of rope to restrain the goat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hobble - a device used to restrict the movement of an animal's legs
  • synonym: shackle - a fastening for securing a limb
