domelike

adjective
shaped like a dome
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊmlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdəʊmlaɪk / (BrE)
having the shape or appearance of a dome

Examples

A domelike roof
The ancient temple was topped with a domelike roof that could be seen from miles away.
Clouds gathered in a domelike formation above the mountain peak.
The jellyfish had a translucent, domelike body floating gently in the water.
