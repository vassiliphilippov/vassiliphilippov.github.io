tipper lorry

noun
type of truck with tilting bed
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪpər ˈlɔri / (AmE), / ˈtɪpə ˈlɒri / (BrE)
A large vehicle with an open platform that can be raised at one end so that its contents are discharged by gravity, used to transport loose materials such as sand or gravel.

Examples

A tipper lorry unloads
A tipper lorry can empty its cargo quickly by raising its front platform.
After unloading the sand, the tipper lorry drove back to collect another load.
The construction company used a tipper lorry to deliver gravel to the building site.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dump truck - truck with a tilting bed for unloading contents, common American term
