jiujitsu

noun
Japanese unarmed martial art
5.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /dʒuː.ˈdʒɪt.suː/ /; / /dʒiː.ˈdʒɪt.suː/ (AmE), /dʒuː.ˈdʒɪt.suː/ /; / /dʒiː.ˈdʒɪt.suː/ (BrE)
A method of self-defense originating in China and Japan, involving unarmed techniques that use an attacker's force against them.

Examples

Traditional jiujitsu techniques
