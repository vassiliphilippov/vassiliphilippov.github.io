jiujitsu

noun
Japanese unarmed martial art
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒuː.dʒɪt.su / (AmE), / ˌdʒuːˈdʒɪt.suː / (BrE)
A method of self-defense originating in China and Japan, involving unarmed techniques that use an attacker's force against them.

Examples

Traditional jiujitsu techniques
She has been practicing jiujitsu for five years and has earned a black belt.
The self-defense class teaches basic jiujitsu techniques suitable for beginners.
Using principles of jiujitsu, he was able to subdue the attacker without any weapons.
