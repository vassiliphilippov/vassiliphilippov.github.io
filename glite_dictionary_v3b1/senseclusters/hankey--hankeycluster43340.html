hankey

noun
square cloth for wiping nose or eyes
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋki / (AmE), / ˈhæŋki / (BrE)
A square piece of cloth used for wiping the eyes or nose, or as a costume accessory; variant of handkerchief or hanky.
