incinerator

noun
furnace for burning waste
4.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɪnəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɪnərətə / (BrE)
A furnace or apparatus for burning waste materials to ashes, especially refuse.

Examples

A incinerator for waste
Hazardous medical waste must be destroyed in a specialized incinerator.
The city built a new incinerator to manage increasing amounts of garbage.
The old incinerator was shut down due to environmental concerns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crematorium - a place where corpses are burned to ashes
