intercut

verb
to alternate scenes
7.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈkʌt/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈkʌt/ (BrE)
to alternate or insert different scenes, shots, or elements within a sequence, especially in film or video editing
