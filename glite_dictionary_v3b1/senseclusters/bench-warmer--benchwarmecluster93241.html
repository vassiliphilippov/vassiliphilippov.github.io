bench-warmer

noun
sports player who rarely plays in games
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛntʃˌwɔrmər / (AmE), / ˈbɛntʃˌwɔːmə / (BrE)
a sports player who rarely or never takes part in games, usually remaining seated on the bench as a substitute

Examples

A bench-warmer player
After months as a bench-warmer, he was finally given a chance to start in the match.
Even though he was a bench-warmer, he kept encouraging his teammates from the sidelines.
She was tired of being a bench-warmer and worked hard to impress the coach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: starter - player who is in the main lineup and regularly plays in games
