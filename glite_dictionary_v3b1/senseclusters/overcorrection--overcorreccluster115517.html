overcorrection

noun
excessive adjustment
7.2
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.kə.ˈrek.ʃən/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.kə.ˈrek.ʃən/ (BrE)
an excessive correction or adjustment that causes a new problem, often by going too far in the opposite direction
