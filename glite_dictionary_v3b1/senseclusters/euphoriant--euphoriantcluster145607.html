euphoriant

adjective
causing intense happiness or euphoria
6.2
Pronunciation: / juˈfɔriənt / (AmE), / juːˈfɔːrɪənt / (BrE)
Tending to produce a feeling of intense happiness or excitement (euphoria), especially used to describe certain drugs or substances.

Examples

Euphoriant effect
Researchers studied the euphoriant properties of the new medication.
Some recreational drugs are classified as euphoriant due to their impact on mood.
The treatment involved a euphoriant effect that helped ease the patient's anxiety.
