milk thistle

noun
a tall white-blotched thistle plant
Pronunciation: / mɪlk ˈθɪsəl / (AmE), / mɪlk ˈθɪsəl /; / mɪlk ˈθɪsl / (BrE)
a tall, prickly plant with white-blotched leaves and purple flowers, often used in herbal medicine to support liver health
