lotebush

noun
thorny desert shrub
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊtˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈləʊtˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
a thorny shrub native to the southwestern United States, also known as wolfberry, that provides shelter and food for wildlife

Examples

The lotebush shrub
The lotebush provides shelter for many desert animals.
Birds often build their nests in the dense branches of the lotebush.
Hikers in the Southwest may spot a lotebush growing among the rocks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mesquite - a thorny shrub or small tree common in deserts of the southwestern US
  • synonym: wolfberry - a shrub with edible berries, also called lotebush
