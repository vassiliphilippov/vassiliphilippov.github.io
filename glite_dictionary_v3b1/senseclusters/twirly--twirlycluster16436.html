twirly

adjective
spiral or spinning
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɜːrli / (AmE), / ˈtwɜːli / (BrE)
having twists, curls, or a tendency to spin or move in circles

Examples

Twirly dress spins
She wore a twirly dress that spun out when she danced.
The child drew a twirly pattern with her finger in the sand.
His twirly mustache made him look like an old-fashioned movie villain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curly - having curls or a curved shape
  • antonym: straight - not curved, twisted, or bent
