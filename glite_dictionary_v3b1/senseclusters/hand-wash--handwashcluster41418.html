hand-wash

verb
wash by hand, not machine
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhænd ˌwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈhænd ˌwɒʃ / (BrE)
to wash something by hand rather than using a machine

Examples

To hand-wash clothes
Please hand-wash all silk garments in cold water.
She always hand-washes her delicate lingerie to avoid damaging the fabric.
The label says you should hand-wash this shirt to keep it in good condition.
