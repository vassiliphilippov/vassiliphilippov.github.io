otter

noun
semi-aquatic carnivorous mammal
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːtər / (AmE), / ˈɒtə / (BrE)
a semi-aquatic carnivorous mammal with webbed and clawed feet and usually dark brown fur, found in rivers, lakes, and coastal areas.

Examples

River otter
Conservation efforts have helped increase the local otter population in recent years.
The otter built its den along the muddy riverbank.
We watched an otter swimming gracefully in the river near our cabin.
