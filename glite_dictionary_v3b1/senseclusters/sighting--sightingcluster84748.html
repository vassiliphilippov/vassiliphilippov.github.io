sighting

noun
an instance of seeing something unusual
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
an occasion when someone sees something, especially something rare or unexpected

Examples

A sighting of a bird
The police are investigating after a sighting of the missing child in the city center.
There were several sightings of the rare bird reported last month.
UFO sightings have increased in this area over the past year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: observation - the action or process of closely monitoring or watching something
