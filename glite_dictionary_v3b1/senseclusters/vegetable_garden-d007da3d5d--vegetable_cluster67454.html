vegetable garden

noun
a garden for growing vegetables
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒtəbl ˌɡɑrdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒtəbl ˌɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)
A small area of land where vegetables are grown for personal use or consumption.

Examples

A vegetable garden
She spends every weekend tending to her vegetable garden behind the house.
Starting a vegetable garden can be a rewarding and healthy hobby.
This year, the vegetable garden produced an abundance of tomatoes and cucumbers.
