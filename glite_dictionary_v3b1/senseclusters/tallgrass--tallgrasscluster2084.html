tallgrass

noun
tall, moisture-loving grass
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔlˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈtɔːlˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
Any of various grasses that are characteristically tall and flourish in areas with abundant moisture.

Examples

The tallgrass prairie
Big bluestem is a prominent species among the tallgrass found in these fields.
Prairie conservationists are working to restore tallgrass ecosystems.
The native tallgrass once covered large portions of the Midwest.
