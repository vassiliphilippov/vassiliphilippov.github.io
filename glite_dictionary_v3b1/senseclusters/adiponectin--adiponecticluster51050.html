adiponectin

noun
metabolic protein hormone
9.9
Pronunciation: /æ.dɪ.pə.ˈnek.tɪn/ /; / /ə.dɪ.pə.ˈnek.tɪn/ (AmE), /æ.dɪ.pə.ˈnek.tɪn/ /; / /ə.dɪ.pə.ˈnek.tɪn/ (BrE)
a protein hormone made by fat cells that helps control the amount of fat and sugar in the blood
