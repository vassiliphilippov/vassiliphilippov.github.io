natural

adjective
innately gifted or talented
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃ(ə)rəl / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl / (BrE)
having a talent, ability, or quality that appears to be innate or inherited rather than learned or acquired; instinctively skilled.

Examples

Natural talent
Although he'd never painted before, it was clear he was a natural with colors and composition.
He's a natural when it comes to playing the piano, needing little practice to excel.
The coach called her a natural leader after she motivated the whole team at practice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acquired - developed or learned, not innate
  • synonym: innate - existing from birth; inherent
  • synonym: talented - having a natural aptitude or skill
