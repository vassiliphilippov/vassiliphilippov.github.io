equalizing dividend

noun
dividend paid to offset changes in dividend schedule
5.6 9.4

a dividend paid to shareholders to compensate for losses caused by a change in the dividend schedule

Examples

An equalizing dividend payment
An equalizing dividend may be declared to make up for changes in a company's dividend timetable.

The board decided to issue an equalizing dividend after switching to annual payments.
