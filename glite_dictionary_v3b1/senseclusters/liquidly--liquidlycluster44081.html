liquidly

adverb
smoothly or fluidly
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪdli / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪdli / (BrE)
in a smooth, flowing, or fluid manner, like a liquid

Examples

Moved liquidly across
The dancer moved liquidly across the stage, captivating the audience with her grace.
His voice flowed liquidly through the poem, making each word sound musical.
Raindrops slid liquidly down the window, blurring the view outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fluidly - in a way that flows easily and smoothly
  • antonym: roughly - in a way that is not smooth or gentle
  • synonym: smoothly - in a way that is even and without interruption
