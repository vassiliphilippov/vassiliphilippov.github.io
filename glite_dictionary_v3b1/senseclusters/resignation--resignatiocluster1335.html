resignation

noun
accepting something unpleasant without protest
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzɪɡˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛzɪɡˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the acceptance of an unpleasant situation or fate without protest or resistance

Examples

Accept with resignation
He listened in silent resignation, realizing there was nothing he could do to change the outcome.
There was a look of resignation on her face as she heard the disappointing news.
With a sigh of resignation, she accepted her fate and returned to her desk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acceptance - the process of accepting something
  • antonym: defiance - open resistance or bold disobedience
