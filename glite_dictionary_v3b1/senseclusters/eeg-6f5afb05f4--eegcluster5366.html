EEG

noun
record of brain's electrical activity
5.3
Pronunciation: / i i dʒi / (AmE), / iː iː dʒiː / (BrE)
a graphical record of the brain's electrical activity, produced by an electroencephalograph

Examples

An EEG test
After the seizure, the hospital conducted an EEG to monitor his brain waves.
Researchers analyzed the EEG results to study sleep patterns in children.
The doctor reviewed the patient's EEG to check for abnormal brain activity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electroencephalogram - a recording of the electrical activity of the brain
