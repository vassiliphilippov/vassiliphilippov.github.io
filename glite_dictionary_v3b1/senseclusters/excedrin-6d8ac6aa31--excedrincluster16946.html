Excedrin

noun
pain relief brand
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɛdrɪn / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛdrɪn / (BrE)
a trademarked brand of over-the-counter pain reliever, often used informally to refer to any headache medicine

Examples

Take Excedrin for headache
She took some Excedrin to help with her headache before the meeting.
Do you have any Excedrin or another painkiller for my migraine?
Even though it was not the exact brand, he called the medicine Excedrin out of habit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aspirin - a common over-the-counter pain reliever
  • synonym: ibuprofen - a non-prescription medicine used to reduce pain and inflammation
