electronic fetal monitor

noun
device monitoring fetal heartbeat and contractions
2.7
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˈfiːtəl ˈmɑnɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˈfiːtəl ˈmɒnɪtə / (BrE)
a medical device that uses electronic sensors to track a fetus's heartbeat and the mother's uterine contractions during childbirth
