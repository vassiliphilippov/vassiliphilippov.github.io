inflationist

adjective
favoring inflation policies
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfleɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ɪnˈfleɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
supporting or relating to policies that encourage inflation in an economy

Examples

Inflationist policies
The new finance minister is known for his inflationist approach to economic growth.
Critics argue that inflationist policies can lead to higher prices and reduced purchasing power.
Some economists believe an inflationist stance can help reduce unemployment during a recession.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deflationary - relating to or favoring policies that reduce inflation
