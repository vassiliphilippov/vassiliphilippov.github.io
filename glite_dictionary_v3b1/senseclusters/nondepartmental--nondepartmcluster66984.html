nondepartmental

adjective
not linked to department
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndɪˌpɑrtˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˌpɑːtˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
not belonging to or associated with a specific department within an organization or government

Examples

Nondepartmental agency
The government created a nondepartmental agency to oversee the new regulations.
She works for a nondepartmental organization that collaborates with several ministries.
Funding was provided through a nondepartmental grant program open to all applicants.

Related Terms

  • antonym: departmental - belonging to or associated with a specific department
  • synonym: independent - not controlled by or connected to another organization
