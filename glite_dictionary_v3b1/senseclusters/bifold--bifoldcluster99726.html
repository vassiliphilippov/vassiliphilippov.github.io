bifold

noun
two-section wallet
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈbaɪfəʊld / (BrE)
a wallet that folds once in the middle, usually with two sections for holding cards and cash

Examples

A bifold wallet
He slipped his new bifold into his back pocket before leaving the house.
The store offers both bifold and trifold wallets in various colors and materials.
She bought her father a leather bifold for his birthday.

Related Terms

  • antonym: trifold - a wallet that folds twice into three sections
  • synonym: wallet - a small, flat case for carrying money and cards
