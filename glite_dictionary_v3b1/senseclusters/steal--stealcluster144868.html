steal

verb
move quietly and secretly
4.3 8.24/m
Pronunciation: / stil / (AmE), / stiÀêl / (BrE)
to move somewhere quietly and secretly so as not to be seen or heard

Examples

To steal away quietly
A cat steals across the garden at night, hunting for mice.
He stole down the hallway, careful not to make a sound.
She tried to steal out of the room without waking anyone.
