bridal-wreath

noun
a flowering shrub with white spring flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪdəl riθ /; / ˈbraɪdəl ˌriθ / (AmE), / ˈbraɪdl riːθ /; / ˈbraɪdl ˌriːθ / (BrE)
an ornamental shrub with many small white flowers, often grown in gardens (especially Spiraea species or similar plants)
