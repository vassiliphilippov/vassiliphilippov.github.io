dewily

adverb
sentimentally, tearfully
Pronunciation: / ˈduːɪli / (AmE), / ˈdjuːɪli / (BrE)
in a way that appears teary-eyed, sentimental, or emotionally moved, as if with eyes moist with tears

Examples

She smiles dewily
He gazed dewily at the old photographs, remembering happier times.
The actress looked dewily at her co-star during the emotional scene.
Her eyes shone dewily as she listened to the moving speech.
