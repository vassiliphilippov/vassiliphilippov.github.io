sale

noun
state of being available to buy
1.0 23/m
Pronunciation: / seÉªl / (AmE), / seÉªl / (BrE)
the state of being available to be bought; offered or displayed for purchase

Examples

For sale sign
Fresh strawberries are now for sale at the farmers' market.
Many rare books are on sale at the downtown shop.
The old school building is being put up for sale next month.

Synonyms

  • for sale
    1.0 10/m
    for sale is written on goods or signs to show they can be bought, while sale refers to the state of being offered for purchase.
  • on the market
    2.3 7.96/m
    on the market means available to the public to buy, while sale simply names the state of being offered for purchase.
  • on the block
    4.1 0.15/m
    on the block often means being offered at an auction or public sale, while sale refers to general availability to buy.
