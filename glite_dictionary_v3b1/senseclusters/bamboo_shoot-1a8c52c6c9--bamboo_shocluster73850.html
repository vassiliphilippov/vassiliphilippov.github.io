bamboo shoot

noun
young, edible bamboo plant shoot
Pronunciation: / bæmˈbu ˈʃuːt / (AmE), / bamˈbuː ˈʃuːt / (BrE)
the young, edible shoot of a bamboo plant, often used as a vegetable in Asian cuisine

Examples

Bamboo shoots in cooking
Bamboo shoots are a common ingredient in many Southeast Asian dishes.
Fresh bamboo shoots must be boiled before eating to remove bitterness.
She added bamboo shoots to the stir-fry for extra crunch and flavor.
