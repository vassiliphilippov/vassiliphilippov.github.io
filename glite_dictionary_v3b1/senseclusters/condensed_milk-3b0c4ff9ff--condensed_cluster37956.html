condensed milk

noun
sweetened, thickened milk product
1.3 2.3
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈdenst mɪlk/ (AmE), /kən.ˈdenst mɪlk/ (BrE)
Milk from which water has been removed and sugar added, creating a thick, sweet product used in cooking and baking.

Examples

Condensed milk with sugar
Condensed milk is often used to make caramel and fudge.
She poured condensed milk over her strawberries for a quick treat.
The recipe calls for one cup of condensed milk to give the dessert its rich sweetness.
