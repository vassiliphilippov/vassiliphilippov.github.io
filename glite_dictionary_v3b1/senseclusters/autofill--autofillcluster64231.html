autofill

noun
automatic form entry
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːtoʊˌfɪl / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊˌfɪl / (BrE)
a software feature that automatically enters saved information into form fields for the user

Examples

Browser autofill feature
The autofill saved me time by entering my address automatically on the checkout page.
If you clear your browser data, the autofill suggestions will disappear.
Many people rely on autofill to remember their passwords and contact information.
The autofill on my phone saves me time when entering my email address.
If you enable autofill, your browser can remember your shipping information for future orders.
She cleared her autofill data to protect her privacy on the shared computer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manual entry - entering information by hand without automatic help
