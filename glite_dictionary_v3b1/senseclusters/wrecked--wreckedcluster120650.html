wrecked

adjective
badly damaged or destroyed
2.3
Pronunciation: / rɛkt / (AmE), / rɛkt / (BrE)
Badly damaged or destroyed, especially as a result of an accident or disaster.

Examples

A wrecked car
After the hurricane, we saw several wrecked boats along the shore.
He bought a wrecked car to use for spare parts.
The wrecked building was declared unsafe and had to be demolished.

Related Terms

  • synonym: damaged - harmed physically or functionally
  • antonym: intact - remaining whole, not damaged
