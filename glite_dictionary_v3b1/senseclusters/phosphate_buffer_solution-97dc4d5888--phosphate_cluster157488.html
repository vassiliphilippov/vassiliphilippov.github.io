phosphate buffer solution

noun
solution containing a phosphate buffer
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊsfeɪt ˈbʌfər səˈluːʃən / (AmE), / ˈfəʊsfeɪt ˈbʌfə səˈluːʃən / (BrE)
A solution that contains a phosphate buffer, used to maintain a stable pH in chemical and biological systems.

Examples

A phosphate buffer solution
