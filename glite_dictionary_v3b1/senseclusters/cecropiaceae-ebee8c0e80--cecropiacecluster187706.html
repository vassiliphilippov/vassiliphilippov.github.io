Cecropiaceae

noun
former family of tropical flowering plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɛˌkroʊpiˈeɪsiːˌiː / (AmE), / siˌkrɒpiˈeɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
a former family of tropical flowering plants, now usually included in the family Urticaceae or Moraceae in some classifications.

Examples

The Cecropiaceae family
Botanists debate whether Cecropiaceae deserves recognition as a separate family.
Recent taxonomic revisions have merged the Cecropiaceae with the Urticaceae family.
The genus Cecropia was once classified under the Cecropiaceae family.
