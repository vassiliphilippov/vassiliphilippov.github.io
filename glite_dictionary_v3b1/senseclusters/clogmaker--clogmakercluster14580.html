clogmaker

noun
maker of clogs
Pronunciation: / ˈklɔɡˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈklɒɡˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes wooden shoes known as clogs

Examples

The clogmaker carves shoes
The village clogmaker carved each pair of shoes by hand using traditional tools.
As a skilled clogmaker, he passed his craft down to his children.
Tourists watched the clogmaker demonstrate how to shape wood into sturdy footwear.
