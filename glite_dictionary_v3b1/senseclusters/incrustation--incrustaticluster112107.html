incrustation

noun
decorative coating or inlay
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.krəˈsteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.krʌsˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a decorative layer or coating of contrasting material applied to a surface as an inlay or overlay

Examples

Decorative incrustation design
Decorative incrustation can add value and beauty to otherwise plain objects.
Experts spent weeks restoring the ancient vase to reveal the original gold incrustation.
The marble table featured an intricate incrustation of mother-of-pearl along its edges.
