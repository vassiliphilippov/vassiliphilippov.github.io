self-contained

adjective
composed and self-possessed (of a person)
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf kənˈteɪnd / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf kənˈteɪnd / (BrE)
(of a person) calm, composed, and in control of one's emotions or faculties; self-possessed.

Examples

Self-contained person
Despite the chaotic situation, she remained self-contained and handled every challenge calmly.
He is known for being self-contained, rarely letting his feelings show in public.
The manager's self-contained manner reassured everyone during the emergency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composed - calm and in control of emotions
  • synonym: self-possessed - in control of one's feelings and actions
