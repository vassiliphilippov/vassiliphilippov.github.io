clearcut

adjective
obvious and definite
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈklɪəˌkʌt / (BrE)
easy to recognize, understand, or decide because it is obvious and definite

Examples

Clearcut answer
There was a clearcut winner in the competition, leaving no room for debate.
The instructions were clearcut, so everyone understood what to do.
Her answer was clearcut and left no doubt about her intentions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ambiguous - having more than one possible meaning; not clear
  • synonym: definite - clearly decided or certain
  • synonym: obvious - easy to see or understand
