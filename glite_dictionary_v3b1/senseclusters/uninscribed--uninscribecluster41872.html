uninscribed

adjective
not inside another figure
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈskraɪbd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈskraɪbd / (BrE)
not drawn or fitted inside another figure so that its points or vertices lie on the boundary, especially in geometry

Examples

Uninscribed polygon in circle
The pentagon was uninscribed because none of its corners touched the circle.
In the diagram, only the blue triangle is uninscribed while the others are inscribed.
A shape is uninscribed if it does not fit perfectly within another figure's boundaries.
