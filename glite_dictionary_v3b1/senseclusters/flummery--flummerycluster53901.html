flummery

noun
sweet, bland oatmeal pudding
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌməri / (AmE), / ˈflʌməri / (BrE)
A bland, sweet dessert made from stewed oatmeal or other ingredients, often formed into a custard or pudding.

Examples

A bowl of flummery
Although flummery was once popular, few people make it from scratch today.
Her grandmother always served a pink strawberry flummery at holiday dinners.
Traditional Welsh flummery is made by soaking oatmeal overnight and then straining and boiling it.
