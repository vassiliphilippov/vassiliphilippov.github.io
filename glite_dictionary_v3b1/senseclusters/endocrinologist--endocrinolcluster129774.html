endocrinologist

noun
medical specialist in hormones
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.də.krəˈnɑ.lə.dʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛn.də.krɪˈnɒl.ə.dʒɪst / (BrE)
A doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the endocrine system.

Examples

Endocrinologist specialist
After months of unexplained fatigue, I was referred to an endocrinologist for further testing.
Pediatric patients with diabetes often work closely with an endocrinologist to manage their condition.
The endocrinologist explained how hormonal imbalances could affect metabolism.
