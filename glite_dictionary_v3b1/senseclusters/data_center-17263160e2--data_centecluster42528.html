data center

noun
facility for computers
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪtə ˌsɛntər / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtə ˌsɛntə / (BrE)
a facility where computer systems and related equipment are housed, managed, and maintained

Examples

A data center facility
The company built a new data center to handle increased internet traffic.
Security is a top priority in every data center to protect sensitive information.
Many businesses rely on a data center to store their digital records safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cloud computing - the use of remote servers to store and process data
  • synonym: server farm - a large group of networked computer servers
