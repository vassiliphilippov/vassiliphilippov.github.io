cell

noun
small room for a monk or nun
Pronunciation: / sɛl / (AmE), / sɛl / (BrE)
A small room in which a monk or nun lives, usually in a monastery or convent.

Examples

Monk's cell
Each nun decorated her cell with simple religious symbols.
The ancient monastery still preserves the stone cells of its earliest inhabitants.
The monk retreated to his cell for evening prayers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chamber - a private room, often in religious or official contexts
