aberration

noun
something unusual or abnormal
Pronunciation: / ˌæbəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌabəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a state or condition that is very different from what is normal or expected

Examples

A rare aberration
His angry outburst was considered an aberration and not typical of his usual calm demeanor.
The scientist explained that the data point was an aberration and did not fit the established pattern.
The sudden drop in temperature was an aberration for the region at this time of year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anomaly - something that deviates from what is standard, normal, or expected
  • antonym: normality - the state of being usual, typical, or expected
