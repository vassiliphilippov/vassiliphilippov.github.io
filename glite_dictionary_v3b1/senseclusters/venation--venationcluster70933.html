venation

noun
arrangement of veins in animals (zoology)
Pronunciation: / veɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / vɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
(zoology) The arrangement or system of veins or venous blood vessels in an animal's body or organ.

Examples

Animal venation system
Differences in venation can help identify species in comparative anatomy.
Medical students must learn about the venation in different organs for accurate diagnosis.
The scientist studied the venation of the frog's limbs to understand its circulatory system.
