eustress

noun
positive motivating stress
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːstrɛs / (AmE), / ˈjuːstrɛs / (BrE)
a positive form of stress that motivates or energizes a person to perform well

Examples

Positive eustress effect
The excitement before a big performance can create eustress that helps you do your best.
Many people experience eustress when starting a new project they are passionate about.
A challenging workout often brings eustress that boosts motivation and energy.
