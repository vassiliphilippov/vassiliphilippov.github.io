hen

noun
flesh of an older female chicken for cooking
Pronunciation: / hɛn / (AmE), / hɛn / (BrE)
The meat of an older female chicken, typically used for stewing rather than roasting or frying due to its tougher texture.

Examples

Hen for stew
Farmers often sell older hens for stewing because their meat is tougher than that of young chickens.
My grandmother always preferred using a hen for her famous chicken and dumplings.
The recipe calls for one whole hen to make a rich chicken stew.
