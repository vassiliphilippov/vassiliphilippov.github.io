nattily

adverb
in a neat or stylish way
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnæ.tɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈnat.ɪ.li / (BrE)
In a neat, smart, or stylish manner.

Examples

Dressed nattily
Even in casual clothes, he managed to look nattily put together.
She arranged the flowers nattily in the vase, making sure each one was perfectly placed.
The young man was nattily dressed in a sharp suit and polished shoes.
