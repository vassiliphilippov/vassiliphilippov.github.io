land agent

noun
person arranging land sales or management
Pronunciation: / ˈlænd ˌeɪdʒənt /; / lænd ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈlænd ˌeɪdʒənt /; / lænd ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to manage, sell, or administer real land or estates for the owner

Examples

Estate land agent
After inheriting the farm, she hired a land agent to manage the estate.
In rural Scotland, a good land agent is essential for handling tenancy agreements.
The land agent showed us several properties that suited our requirements.
She worked as a land agent for several rural estates across the region.
The land agent was responsible for overseeing repairs and collecting rents from the tenants.
When the estate changed ownership, a new land agent was appointed to manage the property.

Related Terms

  • synonym: estate agent - person whose business is selling, renting, or managing buildings and land
  • synonym: estate manager - a person in charge of managing a large property or estate
