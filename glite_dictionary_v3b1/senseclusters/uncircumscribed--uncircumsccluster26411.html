uncircumscribed

adjective
without clear borders
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈsɜːrkəmskraɪbd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈsɜːkəmskraɪbd / (BrE)
not having clearly defined or sharp boundaries; spreading beyond a distinct margin, especially in medical contexts

Examples

Uncircumscribed lesion
The doctor noted an uncircumscribed lesion that required further investigation.
An uncircumscribed tumor can be more difficult to remove surgically.
Radiologists look for uncircumscribed masses as a possible sign of malignancy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: circumscribed - having clear and well-defined boundaries
