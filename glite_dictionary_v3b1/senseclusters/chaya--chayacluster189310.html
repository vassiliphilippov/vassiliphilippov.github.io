chaya

noun
edible tropical shrub
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪə / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪə / (BrE)
a tropical leafy shrub, native to Central America, whose leaves are cooked and eaten as a vegetable

Examples

Cooked chaya leaves
The chef added steamed chaya leaves to the traditional stew.
Farmers in Mexico often grow chaya for its nutritious leaves.
Before eating, you must cook chaya leaves to remove toxins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kale - a leafy green vegetable with curly or flat leaves
  • synonym: spinach - a leafy green vegetable often cooked or eaten raw
