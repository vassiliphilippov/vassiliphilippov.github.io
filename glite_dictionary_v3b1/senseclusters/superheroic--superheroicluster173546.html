superheroic

adjective
like a superhero
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərhɪˈroʊɪk / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəhɪəˈrəʊɪk / (BrE)
having qualities like a superhero (such as great bravery or strength), or relating to superheroes or the superhero genre

Examples

Superheroic strength
The rescue team made a superheroic effort to reach the stranded hikers before nightfall.
Her superheroic determination inspired everyone in the community to help rebuild after the storm.
He showed superheroic strength when he lifted the heavy beam off his friend.
The movie's superheroic battles thrilled audiences of all ages.
She wore a superheroic costume to the comic convention last weekend.
His superheroic deeds inspired the children in his neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heroic - showing great bravery or courage
  • antonym: ordinary - not special or remarkable; normal
  • antonym: villainous - having the qualities of a villain
