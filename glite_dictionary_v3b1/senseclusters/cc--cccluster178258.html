cc

noun
unit of volume; cubic centimeter
3.4 3.14/m
Pronunciation: / si si / (AmE), / siÀê siÀê / (BrE)
a unit of volume equal to one cubic centimeter, or one thousandth of a liter, often used in scientific and medical contexts

Examples

One cc of liquid
The beaker contains 250 cc of water for the experiment.
The doctor injected 5 cc of the vaccine into the patient's arm.
This syringe can hold up to 10 cc of liquid.

Synonyms

  • cubic centimeter
    3.1 0.45/m
    a cubic centimeter is the full unit name used in writing and science, while cc is its short, everyday abbreviation used in notes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ml - a metric unit of volume equal to one thousandth of a liter (milliliter)
