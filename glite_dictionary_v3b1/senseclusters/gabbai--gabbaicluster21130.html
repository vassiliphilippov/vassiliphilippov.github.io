gabbai

noun
synagogue caretaker
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɡə.ˈbaɪ/ /; / /ˈɡæ.baɪ/ (AmE), /ɡə.ˈbaɪ/ /; / /ˈɡæ.baɪ/ (BrE)
an official or caretaker in a synagogue, responsible for maintenance, organizing services, or managing funds

Examples

After the festival, the gabbai organized volunteers to help clean the prayer hall.
When repairs were needed, the gabbai arranged for contractors to fix the roof.
The gabbai made sure the Torah scrolls were ready for the morning service.

Each year, the gabbai presents a detailed financial report to the congregation.
