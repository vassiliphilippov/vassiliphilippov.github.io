liminary

adjective
at a threshold
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪməˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪnəri / (BrE)
relating to a threshold or transitional state, especially at or near the border between two conditions or phases

Examples

Liminary stage
The ceremony marked a liminary stage between childhood and adulthood.
Artists often explore liminary spaces where reality and imagination meet.
The discussion remained in a liminary phase, not reaching any final decisions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: definite - clearly defined or determined
  • synonym: liminal - relating to a transitional or initial stage of a process
  • synonym: transitional - relating to or characteristic of a process or period of transition
