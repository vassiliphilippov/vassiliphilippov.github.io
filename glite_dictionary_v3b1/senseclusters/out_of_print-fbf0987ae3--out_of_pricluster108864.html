out of print

adjective
no longer published or available for sale
Pronunciation: / aʊt əv prɪnt / (AmE), / aʊt əv prɪnt / (BrE)
(of books) no longer available from the publisher for purchase.

Examples

Out of print books
Collectors often pay high prices for works that are out of print.
Many classic novels are currently out of print and difficult to find in stores.
The first edition of the book has been out of print for years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in print - (of books) currently available from the publisher
