fud

noun
spreadable sweet topping
Pronunciation: / fʌd / (AmE), / fʌd / (BrE)
a soft, sweet, and easily spreadable food, often used as a topping or filling for cakes and desserts

Examples

Chocolate fud topping

The bakery offers cupcakes topped with a rich layer of fud.
He made a simple dessert by layering fruit and fud in a glass.
