seeded player

noun
top-ranked competitor in a tournament
8.4 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsidɪd ˈpleɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈsiːdɪd ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
A player ranked among the best in a tournament, often placed in a bracket to avoid meeting other top players in early rounds.

Examples

A seeded player in tournament

Being a seeded player often helps avoid tough early matchups.
The top seeded player advanced easily through the quarterfinals.
