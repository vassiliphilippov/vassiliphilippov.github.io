Synonyms

  • toroidal
    9.9 0.08/m
    toroidal is a technical term for a three-dimensional doughnut form used in science, while doughnut-shaped is everyday and simple.
  • annular
    7.9 0.65/m
    annular describes a ring that often looks flat or hollow like an eclipse, while doughnut-shaped suggests a full three-dimensional doughnut form.
  • ring-shaped
    3.4 0.17/m

  • ringlike
