prophet

noun
person who predicts or proclaims the future
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːfɪt / (AmE), / ˈprɒfɪt / (BrE)
a person regarded as an inspired teacher or proclaimer of the future, often considered divinely inspired or authoritative

Examples

A prophet predicts
Many people considered her a prophet after her warnings proved true.
The prophet predicted years of peace and prosperity for the kingdom.
Throughout history, prophets have played crucial roles in guiding communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fortune-teller - a person who predicts future events
  • synonym: oracle - a person through whom advice or prophecy is sought
  • synonym: seer - a person who is supposed to be able to see what the future holds
