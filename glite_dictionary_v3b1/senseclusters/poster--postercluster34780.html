poster

noun
person who posts online
3.6
Pronunciation: / \u02c8po\u028ast\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8p\u0259\u028ast\u0259 / (BrE)
a person who publishes messages, comments, or other content on an online platform such as a forum, social network, or news site.

Examples

Forum poster
The first poster on the forum linked several studies to support her claim.
A frequent poster on the site usually replies to news articles within minutes.
After the controversy, the anonymous poster deleted their account and left no trace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commenter - a person who writes comments on an online article, post, or discussion
  • antonym: lurker - someone who reads online discussions but does not post or participate
