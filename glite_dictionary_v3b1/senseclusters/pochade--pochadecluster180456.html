pochade

noun
portable artist's box
Pronunciation: / poʊˈʃɑːd / (AmE), / pɒˈʃɑːd / (BrE)
a small portable box or easel used by artists to carry paints and tools for making quick outdoor sketches or studies

With his pochade packed, he was ready for a day of sketching in the park.
