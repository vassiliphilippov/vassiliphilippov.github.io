sarong

noun
loose cloth wrap worn as a skirt
Pronunciation: / səˈrɔŋ / (AmE), / səˈrɒŋ / (BrE)
a loose skirt made from a brightly colored piece of cloth wrapped around the body, commonly worn by men and women in the South Pacific and Southeast Asia

Examples

A sarong skirt
He wrapped a sarong around his waist after swimming.
Many traditional dancers in Bali perform in elaborate sarongs.
She wore a colorful sarong to the beach over her swimsuit.
