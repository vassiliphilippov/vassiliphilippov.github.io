last

noun
shoemaker's foot-shaped form
Pronunciation: / læst / (AmE), / lɑːst / (BrE)
a block or form shaped like a human foot, used by shoemakers in making or repairing shoes to ensure the correct shape and fit

Examples

A shoe last
Different sizes of lasts are needed to make shoes for children and adults.
She learned how to stretch the shoe over the last to ensure a perfect fit.
The cobbler placed the leather over the last as he crafted a new pair of shoes.
