absurdist

adjective
relating to absurdism
8.6
Pronunciation: / əbˈsɝːdɪst / (AmE), / əbˈsɜːdɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the belief that human existence is irrational, meaningless, or illogical

Examples

Absurdist theater style
The director is known for his absurdist approach to storytelling in film.
Her paintings often feature absurdist elements that question the meaning of reality.
Many absurdist plays highlight the confusion and unpredictability of life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: existentialist - relating to the philosophy that emphasizes individual existence and freedom
  • antonym: rationalist - relating to the belief that reason is the main source of knowledge
