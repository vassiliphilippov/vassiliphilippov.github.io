sine die

adverb
without a fixed date; indefinitely
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪni ˈdaɪi, ˌsiːni ˈdiːeɪ / (AmE), / ˌsaɪni ˈdaɪi, ˌsiːni ˈdiːeɪ / (BrE)
Without fixing a date for a future meeting or resumption; indefinitely.

Examples

sine die adjournment
The committee decided to suspend the discussions sine die until further notice.
The court postponed the hearing sine die due to administrative delays.
The meeting was adjourned sine die, with no date set for reconvening.
