MS

noun
abbreviation for multiple sclerosis, a nervous system disease
Pronunciation: / ɛm ɛs / (AmE), / ɛm ɛs / (BrE)
a chronic, progressive disease of the nervous system characterized by loss of the myelin sheath surrounding certain nerve fibers, leading to varied neurological symptoms; abbreviation of multiple sclerosis.

Examples

MS diagnosis
After years of unexplained symptoms, she was finally diagnosed with MS.
Many patients with MS experience periods of remission and relapse.
The support group meets weekly to assist people living with MS.
