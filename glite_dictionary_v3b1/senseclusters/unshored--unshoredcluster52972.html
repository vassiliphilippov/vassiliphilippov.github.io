unshored

adjective
not reinforced
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃɔrd / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɔːd / (BrE)
not supported or reinforced with a shore, prop, or brace

Examples

Unshored wall collapses
The unshored structure was declared unsafe by the building inspector.
Heavy winds caused the unshored scaffolding to collapse overnight.
Engineers warned that leaving the wall unshored could lead to serious accidents.
