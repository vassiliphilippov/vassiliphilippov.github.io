Zionist

noun
supporter of Zionism
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈzaɪ.ə.nɪst / (AmE), / ˈzaɪ.ə.nɪst / (BrE)
A person who supports Zionism, especially a Jewish supporter of the movement for the re-establishment and development of a Jewish nation in what is now Israel.

Examples

A proud Zionist
He has always identified himself as a proud Zionist and supports the state of Israel.
She studied the history of the Zionist movement for her thesis.
The conference brought together Zionist leaders from around the world.
