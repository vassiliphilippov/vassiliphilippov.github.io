flaxseed oil

noun
oil from flax seeds, used in food and industry
Pronunciation: / ˈflækˌsid ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈflæk.siːd ɔɪl / (BrE)
an oil extracted from flax seeds, used as a food supplement and in products such as paints and varnishes
