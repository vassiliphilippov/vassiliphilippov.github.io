milepost

noun
distance marker by road or railway
5.9 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪl.poʊst/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪl.pəʊst/ (BrE)
A post set up beside a road or railway to mark the distance in miles to or from a given place.

Examples

A milepost marker
Every milepost along the railway is numbered for maintenance purposes.
The old milepost at the crossroads still shows the distances to nearby towns.
We stopped at a milepost to check how far we had come.

Related Terms

  • synonym: milestone - a stone or marker indicating the distance in miles along a road
