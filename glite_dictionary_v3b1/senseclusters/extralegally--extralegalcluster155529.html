extralegally

adverb
outside the law
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəˈliːɡəli / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈliːɡəli / (BrE)
in a manner that is outside or beyond the limits of the law

Examples

Act extralegally
The organization operated extralegally to provide aid where official help was not available.
Some activists acted extralegally to protest the new regulations.
The committee condemned any attempt to resolve disputes extralegally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illegally - in a way that is against the law
  • antonym: legally - in a way that is allowed by law
