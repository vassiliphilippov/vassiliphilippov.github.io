VSS

noun
vehicle speed sensor
9.0
Pronunciation: / vi ɛs ɛs / (AmE), / viː ɛs ɛs / (BrE)
a device in a vehicle that measures its speed and sends this information to electronic systems

Examples

VSS sensor failure
The mechanic diagnosed a faulty VSS after the car's speed readings became inaccurate.
A malfunctioning VSS can affect the performance of automatic transmission systems.
Replacing the VSS restored the vehicle's cruise control and speedometer functions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: speedometer - an instrument in a vehicle that shows the speed it is traveling
