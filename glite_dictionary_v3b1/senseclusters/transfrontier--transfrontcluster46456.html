transfrontier

adjective
across borders
Pronunciation: / trænsfrʌnˈtɪr / (AmE), / trænsfrɒnˈtɪə / (BrE)
relating to or crossing the borders between countries or regions

Examples

A transfrontier conservation area
The creation of a transfrontier conservation area helped protect endangered species in both countries.
A transfrontier agreement was signed to improve trade and cooperation between the neighboring nations.
Scientists are studying the impact of transfrontier pollution on river ecosystems that flow across borders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross-border - involving or extending across national borders
  • antonym: domestic - relating to one country or internal affairs
