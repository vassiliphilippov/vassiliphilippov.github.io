minute steak

noun
thin steak cooked quickly
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnɪt steɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪt steɪk / (BrE)
A thin slice of steak that can be cooked quickly, usually by frying or grilling.

Examples

A minute steak slice
At the diner, I ordered a minute steak with eggs for breakfast.
She cooked a minute steak for lunch because she was short on time.
The minute steak was so thin it was ready in less than two minutes.
