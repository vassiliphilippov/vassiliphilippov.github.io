surfbird

noun
Pacific shorebird, similar to a sandpiper
8.1 7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsɜːrf.bɜːrd/ (AmE), /ˈsɜːf.bɜːd/ (BrE)
a sandpiper-like shorebird (Aphriza virgata) found on the Pacific coasts of North and South America, known for its association with rocky shores and surf zones
