humanitarianism

noun
the doctrine of promoting human welfare
2.7
Pronunciation: / hjuˌmænɪˈtɛriənɪzəm / (AmE), / hjuːˌmanɪˈteəriənɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief or doctrine that people have a duty to promote human welfare and alleviate suffering

Examples

Humanitarianism promotes welfare
His lifelong commitment to humanitarianism earned him international recognition.
Many volunteers are motivated by humanitarianism when helping communities in need.
The organization is guided by a strong sense of humanitarianism in its relief efforts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: altruism - selfless concern for the well-being of others
  • synonym: philanthropy - the desire to promote the welfare of others, often through charitable acts
