Suba

noun
East African language
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːbə / (AmE), / ˈsuːbə / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken by the Suba people, mainly in parts of East Africa, and now considered endangered

Examples

Suba language
Linguists are working to document the Suba language before it disappears completely.
The children learned traditional stories in Suba from their grandparents.
Efforts to revitalize Suba include teaching it in local schools.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Luo - a Nilotic language spoken in East Africa
  • synonym: Swahili - a widely spoken Bantu language in East Africa
