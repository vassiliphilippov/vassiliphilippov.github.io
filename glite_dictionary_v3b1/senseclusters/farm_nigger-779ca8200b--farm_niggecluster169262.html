farm nigger

noun
offensive racial slur (historical)
Pronunciation: / fɑrm ˈnɪɡər / (AmE), / fɑːm ˈnɪɡə / (BrE)
OFFENSIVE, VULGAR. An extremely derogatory and insulting term for a Black person, with historical connotations relating to agricultural labor. Now considered highly offensive and inappropriate in all contexts.

Examples

farm nigger term
He was horrified by old records that included the term farm nigger.
Historians warn against repeating language like farm nigger because of its racist legacy.
The use of the phrase farm nigger is universally condemned as hate speech.
