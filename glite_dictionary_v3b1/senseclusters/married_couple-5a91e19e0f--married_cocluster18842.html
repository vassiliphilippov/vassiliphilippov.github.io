married couple

noun
two people joined in marriage
Pronunciation: / ˈmærid ˈkʌpəl / (AmE), / ˈmærid ˈkʌpl / (BrE)
Two people who are married to each other; a husband and wife or two spouses joined by marriage.

Examples

A married couple
A young married couple moved into the house next door last month.
The married couple celebrated their tenth wedding anniversary with a romantic trip to Paris.
The hotel offers special packages for every married couple on their honeymoon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: divorced couple - two people formerly married who are now divorced
  • synonym: spouses - persons who are married to each other; partners in marriage
