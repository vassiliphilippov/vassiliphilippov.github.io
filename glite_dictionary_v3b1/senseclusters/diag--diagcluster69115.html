diag

verb
to run diagnostics
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪæɡ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪæɡ / (BrE)
to run diagnostic tests on a machine, system, or device to identify problems or faults

Examples

To diag a computer
The technician will diag the computer to find out why it keeps crashing.
Before replacing any parts, she decided to diag the printer for possible errors.
He asked his colleague to diag the network after noticing frequent disconnects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diagnose - to identify the cause of a problem, especially in a machine or system
  • antonym: repair - to fix something that is broken or not working
