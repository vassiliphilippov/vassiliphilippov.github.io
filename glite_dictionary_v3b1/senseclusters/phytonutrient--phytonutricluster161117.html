phytonutrient

noun
plant health compound
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪtoʊˈnuːtriənt / (AmE), / ˌfʌɪtəʊˈnjuːtrɪənt / (BrE)
a natural compound found in plants that is believed to provide health benefits when consumed

Examples

Plant phytonutrients
Many fruits and vegetables contain phytonutrients that may help reduce the risk of disease.
Nutritionists recommend eating a variety of foods rich in phytonutrients for better health.
Scientists are studying how different phytonutrients affect the human body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antioxidant - a substance that helps protect cells from damage
  • synonym: vitamin - an essential nutrient needed in small amounts for health
