decreasingly

adverb
in descending order
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkriːsɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪˈkriːsɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that arranges or orders things from largest or highest to smallest or lowest

Examples

Sorted decreasingly
The list was sorted decreasingly so the highest scores appeared at the top.
Arrange the numbers decreasingly to make it easier to compare the results.
The data should be displayed decreasingly by value for clarity.
