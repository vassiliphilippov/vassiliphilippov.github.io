far left

adjective
the most radical left-wing political views/groups
2.2
Pronunciation: / fɑr lɛft / (AmE), / fɑː lɛft / (BrE)
(in politics) the section of a group or party whose views are the most radical or extreme, particularly on the left-wing spectrum.

Examples

The far left group
Members of the far left often advocate for radical reforms in economic and social policies.
Some voters are drawn to the far left because of its promises of sweeping social change.
The candidate’s platform was criticized for being too far left for mainstream constituents.

Related Terms

  • antonym: far right - the most extreme right-wing views/groups
  • synonym: left-wing - supporting or relating to political views on the left
