herringbone

noun
fabric with a V-shaped weaving pattern
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛr.ɪŋˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈhɛr.ɪŋˌbəʊn /; / ˈhɛr.ɪŋ.bəʊn / (BrE)
a pattern of V-shaped lines, often used in fabric, flooring, or other materials
