multilingual

adjective
using or knowing several languages
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈlɪŋɡwəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈlɪŋɡwəl / (BrE)
able to use or expressed in several languages; speaking, written, or containing more than one language.

Examples

Multilingual staff
Many international organizations employ multilingual staff to communicate with people from around the world.
Switzerland is famous for being a multilingual country with four official languages.
The conference offered multilingual interpretation services to accommodate attendees from different regions.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bilingual - able to use two languages
  • degree lower: monolingual - knowing or using only one language
