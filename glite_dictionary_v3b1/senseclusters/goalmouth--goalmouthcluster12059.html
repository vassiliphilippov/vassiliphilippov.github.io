goalmouth

noun
area directly in front of the goal (sports)
7.4 4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈgoʊl.maʊθ/ (AmE), /ˈgəʊl.maʊθ/ (BrE)
(in sports) the area directly in front of the goal, where much of the attacking and defending action takes place.

Examples

The goalmouth area
A scramble in the goalmouth nearly resulted in a goal for the home team.
The striker was quick to pounce on the loose ball in the goalmouth.
There was chaos in the goalmouth as defenders tried to clear the ball.
