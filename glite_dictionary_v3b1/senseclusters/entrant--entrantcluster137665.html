entrant

noun
new product entering a market
Pronunciation: / ˈɛntrənt / (AmE), / ˈɛntrənt / (BrE)
a new product or commodity that enters a market or competition, especially one competing with those already established

Examples

Market entrant
A new entrant in the smartphone market quickly gained popularity among young professionals.
Many established brands felt threatened by the unique features of the entrant.
The company struggled to survive as an entrant against well-known household names.

Related Terms

  • antonym: incumbent - an established product already in the market
