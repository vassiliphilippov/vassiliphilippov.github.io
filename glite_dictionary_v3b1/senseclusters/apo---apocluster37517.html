prefix: away from
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈæpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈapəʊ / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'away from', 'separate', or indicating the absence of a component, especially in scientific or technical terms

Examples

apo- prefix meaning away
The prefix apo- in 'apogee' means 'away from the Earth'.
In biology, apo- is used in words like 'apoptosis', meaning a process of cells separating or dying.
The term 'apocenter' uses apo- to indicate the point farthest away in an orbit.
In biochemistry, apo- is used to describe a protein without its required cofactor.
The term apo-enzyme refers to the inactive form of an enzyme before it binds to its cofactor.
Scientists use apo- to indicate the absence of a specific group in molecular structures.
