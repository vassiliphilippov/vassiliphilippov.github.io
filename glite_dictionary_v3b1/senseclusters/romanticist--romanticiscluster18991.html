romanticist

noun
person indulging in sentimentality or idealism
2.7 6.6
Pronunciation: /roʊ.ˈmæn.tɪ.sɪst/ /; / /rə.ˈmæn.tɪ.sɪst/ (AmE), /rəʊ.ˈmæn.tɪ.sɪst/ (BrE)
a person who is inclined to indulge in excessive sentimentality or idealism, especially regarding love, beauty, or art.

Examples

A true romanticist
As a true romanticist, she cherished handwritten letters and long walks under the stars.
Some critics dismissed him as a mere romanticist, unable to face the harsher aspects of reality.
The novel's protagonist is a romanticist who views the world through rose-colored glasses.
