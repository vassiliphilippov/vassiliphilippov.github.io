tenth part

noun
one of ten equal parts; a tenth
6.9
Pronunciation: / tɛnθ pɑrt / (AmE), / tɛnθ pɑːt / (BrE)
one of ten equal parts into which something is or may be divided; a tenth.

Examples

A tenth part of team

She divided the cake so that each child received a tenth part of it.
You only need to pay a tenth part of the total amount now.
