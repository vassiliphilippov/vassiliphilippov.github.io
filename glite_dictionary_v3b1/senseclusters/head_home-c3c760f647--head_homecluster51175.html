head home

verb
to go or return home
1.0 1.4
Pronunciation: /hɛd hoʊm/ (AmE), /hɛd həʊm/ (BrE)
to go or return to your home, especially after being somewhere else or after an activity

Examples

She usually heads home around six o'clock every evening.
