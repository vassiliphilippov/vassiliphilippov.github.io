responsively

adverb
in a reactive way
3.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɑːnsɪvli / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒnsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that reacts quickly and positively to something or someone

Examples

Team replies responsively
The support team replied responsively to every customer inquiry within an hour.
She listened responsively to her friend's concerns, offering advice and support.
The website adapts responsively to different screen sizes for a better user experience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: passively - without active response or resistance
  • synonym: promptly - without delay; quickly
  • synonym: reactively - in a way that responds to something
