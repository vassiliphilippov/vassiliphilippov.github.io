indiscernibly

adverb
unclearly or imperceptibly
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈsɜrnəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈsɜːnəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be seen, noticed, or distinguished clearly

Examples

Merged indiscernibly close
The two shades of blue merged indiscernibly on the artist's canvas.
Her expression changed indiscernibly, so no one noticed her discomfort.
The seasons shifted indiscernibly from summer to autumn in the quiet countryside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to see or understand
  • synonym: imperceptibly - in a way that is impossible to perceive
