flight

noun
an instance of imagination or extravagance
Pronunciation: / flaɪt / (AmE), / flaɪt / (BrE)
an act or instance of moving beyond what is ordinary or realistic, especially in imagination or in ideas

Examples

A flight of imagination
Her explanation became a flight of fancy, far removed from the facts.
The poet's flight of imagination inspired his audience.
The speech soared into a flight of rhetoric, impressing the crowd with its inventiveness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fancy - imagination or a fantastic notion
  • synonym: imagination - the faculty or action of forming new ideas
