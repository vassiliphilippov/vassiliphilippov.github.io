carnation

noun
a brightly colored, fragrant garden flower
8.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /kɑːr.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kɑː.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a garden plant with sweetly scented, usually double flowers in a variety of colors, especially pink, red, or white; widely cultivated as an ornamental.

Examples

A pink carnation

The bouquet was filled with pink and white carnations.
