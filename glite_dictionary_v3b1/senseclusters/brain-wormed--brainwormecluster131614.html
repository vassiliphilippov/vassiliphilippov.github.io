brain-wormed

adjective
obsessed or fixated on a thought or song
5.7 8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbreɪn.wɜːrmd/ (AmE), /ˈbreɪn.wɜːmd/ (BrE)
Obsessed or fixated on a thought, idea, or song, especially to the point of distraction.

Examples

Brain-wormed by a song

She felt brain-wormed by the problem, thinking about it constantly.
That catchy song got stuck in my head; I'm brain-wormed.
