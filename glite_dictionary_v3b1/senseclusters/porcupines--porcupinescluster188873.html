porcupines

noun
meatballs with rice in tomato sauce
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔr.kjuˌpaɪnz / (AmE), / ˈpɔː.kjʊˌpaɪnz / (BrE)
Meatballs made of ground beef and rice, simmered in tomato sauce, so called because the rice grains stick out like porcupine quills.

Examples

Porcupines meatballs
Porcupines are easy to prepare and make a comforting family meal.
He requested a second helping of porcupines, impressed by the tender meatballs and flavorful tomato sauce.
My grandmother used to make porcupines for Sunday dinner when I was a child.
