activate

verb
make radioactive by exposure to radiation
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈæktɪˌveɪt / (AmE), / ˈæktɪveɪt / (BrE)

Examples

To activate a metal

Scientists used a neutron source to activate the sample for further analysis.
The laboratory is careful not to activate any unnecessary materials during the experiment.
