gatekeeping

noun
controlling access
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtˌkiːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtˌkiːpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of controlling or limiting access to something, especially information, opportunities, or communities

Examples

Strict gatekeeping rules
The club's strict gatekeeping made it difficult for new members to join.
Online communities sometimes face criticism for excessive gatekeeping of information.
Some people see gatekeeping as necessary to maintain high standards in a group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: allowing - permitting or giving access
  • synonym: restricting - limiting or controlling something
