establish

verb
to create or build something abstract
Pronunciation: / ɪˈstæblɪʃ /; / əˈstæblɪʃ / (AmE), / ɪˈstæblɪʃ / (BrE)
to make something exist, be accepted, or recognized, such as a reputation, relationship, or condition

Examples

To establish trust
It can take years to establish a sense of trust within a community.
She worked hard to establish a reputation as a trustworthy expert in her field.
The organization aims to establish strong partnerships with local businesses.
Her research established a direct connection between stress and illness.
The artist's use of perspective establishes a sense of depth in the painting.
The committee hopes to establish clear guidelines for all members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: create - to cause something to exist
  • antonym: destroy - to put an end to the existence of something
  • synonym: found - to establish or set up something, especially an organization or institution
