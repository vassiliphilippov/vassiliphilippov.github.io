Ptolemy

noun
ancient dynasty of Macedonian kings in Egypt
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…ëÀê.l…ô.mi / (AmE), / Ààt…íl.…ô.mi / (BrE)
the dynasty of Macedonian rulers who governed Egypt from 323 BC to 30 BC, founded by Ptolemy I and concluding with Cleopatra VII

Examples

The Ptolemy dynasty
Cleopatra was the last monarch of the Ptolemy line before Rome annexed Egypt.
Many famous artifacts from ancient Egypt date to the era of the Ptolemy dynasty.
The Ptolemy dynasty ruled Egypt for nearly three centuries following the death of Alexander the Great.
