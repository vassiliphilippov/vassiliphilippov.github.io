physically challenged

adjective
having a physical disability
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪzɪkli ˈtʃæləndʒd / (AmE), / ˌfɪzɪkli ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (BrE)
describing a person who has a physical disability or impairment, often used as a polite or euphemistic alternative to 'disabled'

Examples

The physically challenged person
Programs for the physically challenged can greatly improve quality of life.
Special parking spaces are reserved for physically challenged individuals.
The building was renovated to make it accessible for the physically challenged.

Related Terms

  • antonym: able-bodied - not physically disabled; having no physical impairments
  • synonym: disabled - having a physical or mental condition that limits movements, senses, or activities
