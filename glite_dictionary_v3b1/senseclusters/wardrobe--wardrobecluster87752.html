wardrobe

noun
collection of a person's clothing
1.2 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔːr.droʊb/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.drəʊb/ (BrE)
the collection of clothes that someone owns or that are used for a particular purpose, such as in a theater

Examples

A summer wardrobe
After her promotion, she updated her entire wardrobe with new business attire.
His summer wardrobe consists mainly of shorts and t-shirts.
She has a separate wardrobe for formal events and special occasions.
After years of performances, the company's wardrobe contained outfits from dozens of productions.
She was responsible for organizing the wardrobe before each rehearsal.
The theater's wardrobe was filled with elaborate period costumes for the upcoming play.

  • synonym: costume - clothing worn by an actor or performer for a particular role
