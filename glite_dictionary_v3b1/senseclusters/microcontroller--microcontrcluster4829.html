microcontroller

noun
small control computer
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊkənˈtroʊlər / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊkənˈtrəʊlə / (BrE)
a small computer on a single integrated circuit used to control electronic devices

Examples

A microcontroller chip
The robot's microcontroller processes sensor data and controls its movements.
Many home appliances now include a microcontroller to automate their functions.
Students learned to program a microcontroller to operate lights and motors in their project.
The microcontroller in the washing machine manages the different wash cycles automatically.
Engineers programmed the microcontroller to control the lights and sensors in the smart home system.
A single microcontroller can operate multiple devices in a modern car.

Related Terms

  • synonym: controller - a device that manages or regulates the operation of another device
  • synonym: microprocessor - a central processing unit on a single chip, used in computers
