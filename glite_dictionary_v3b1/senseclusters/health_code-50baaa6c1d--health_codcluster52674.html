health code

noun
digital health-status QR/badge
Pronunciation: / hɛlθ koʊd / (AmE), / hɛlθ kəʊd / (BrE)
a digital indicator (for example, a QR code or app badge) that shows a person's health status — such as vaccination, recent test results, or exposure clearance — used to permit entry, travel, or services.

Examples

Show a health code
Many venues now require a health code scanned at the door before allowing patrons inside.
She opened the app and showed her health code, proving a recent negative test to the guard.
During the outbreak, international travelers needed an approved digital health code to board connecting flights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: health pass - a digital or physical credential indicating a person's health clearance for entry or travel
  • synonym: vaccine passport - an official document or digital record showing vaccination status, used to permit travel or access
