Elohim

proper noun
spiritual beings (New Age)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛˈloʊhim / (AmE), / ɛˈləʊhiːm / (BrE)
a group of divine or highly spiritual beings believed in some New Age and theosophical traditions to guide or influence humanity

Examples

Spiritual Elohim beings
Some spiritual groups believe the Elohim have been guiding humanity for thousands of years.
During the meditation session, participants tried to connect with the Elohim for wisdom and insight.
Books on New Age spirituality often mention the Elohim as powerful cosmic beings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angels - spiritual beings believed to act as messengers or agents of the divine
  • synonym: ascended masters - enlightened spiritual beings in theosophy and New Age beliefs
