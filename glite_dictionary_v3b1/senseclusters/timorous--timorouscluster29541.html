timorous

adjective
showing nervousness or fear
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪmərəs / (AmE), / ˈtɪmərəs / (BrE)
Showing or suffering from nervousness, fear, or a lack of confidence.

Examples

A timorous person
Even a timorous mouse can find courage in desperate times.
He gave a timorous speech, barely raising his voice above a whisper.
The timorous child hid behind her mother when she met new people.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bold - showing willingness to take risks; confident and courageous
  • synonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence; easily frightened
