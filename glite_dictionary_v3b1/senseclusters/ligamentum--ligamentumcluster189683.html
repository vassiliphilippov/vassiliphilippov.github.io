ligamentum

noun
figurative bond
Pronunciation: / lɪˈɡæməntəm / (AmE), / lɪˈɡamɛntəm / (BrE)
an archaic term for a bond or tie that connects people or things, used figuratively.

Examples

A ligamentum of friendship
The shared history served as a strong ligamentum between the neighboring communities.
Tradition acts as a ligamentum holding generations together through time.
For centuries, language has been the ligamentum uniting diverse peoples.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bond - a connection or tie between people or things
  • antonym: division - the act of separating or being separated
  • synonym: link - something that connects or joins things together
