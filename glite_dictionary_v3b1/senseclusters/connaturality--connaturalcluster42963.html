connaturality

noun
natural affinity
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnˌnætʃəˈrælɪti / (AmE), / ˌkɒnˌnætʃəˈrælɪti / (BrE)
a natural connection or similarity between people or things because they share the same nature or qualities

The connaturality between friends

Some artists feel a deep connaturality with the natural world, inspiring their work.
In biology, connaturality can refer to traits shared by related species.
