implication

noun
involvement, especially suggesting guilt
5.6 3.8
Pronunciation: /ɪm.plə.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪm.plɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪm.plɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪm.plə.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the fact or act of being involved or connected to something, especially in a way that suggests guilt or wrongdoing
