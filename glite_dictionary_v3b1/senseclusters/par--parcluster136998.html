par

verb
to trim outer layer
Pronunciation: / pɑr / (AmE), / pɑː / (BrE)
to trim or cut away the outer layer of something, especially fruit or vegetables

Examples

To par an apple
She began to par the apples before baking the pie.
He used a small knife to par the skin from the potatoes.
Before cooking, you should par the carrots to remove any dirt or rough spots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peel - to remove the outer covering of fruit or vegetables
  • synonym: slice - to cut something into thin pieces
