Synonyms

  • abundant
    3.0 12/m
    abundant describes things available in very large amounts, while rich suggests something full and productive and often valuable.
  • plentiful
    2.5 4.90/m
    plentiful means more than enough for many people or uses, while rich focuses on being full and producing a lot.
  • productive
    2.7 5.33/m
    productive means able to produce large amounts or results, while rich describes being full or supplying plenty of something.
  • copious
    6.8 1.70/m
    copious describes large amounts produced or written down, while rich suggests a general full supply in many contexts.
  • ample
    3.7 10/m
    ample means more than needed for a particular purpose or space, while rich means broadly full or highly productive.
  • bountiful
    4.9 1.25/m
    bountiful paints a generous, often natural image like a large harvest, while rich is a more general idea of plenty.
  • well-endowed
    6.8 0.05/m
    well-endowed often describes having large resources or funds in an organization, while rich is a broader word for plenty or abundance.
