baleen whale

noun
whale with baleen plates for filter-feeding
5.6 0.10/m

A large whale that has baleen plates for filtering plankton and small fish from the water, rather than teeth.

Examples

Large baleen whales
Many baleen whales migrate thousands of miles each year to find food.
The blue whale is the largest baleen whale and the largest animal on earth.
Unlike toothed whales, baleen whales filter krill and plankton from the ocean.
