old

adjective
having lived for a long time
Pronunciation: / oʊld / (AmE), / əʊld / (BrE)
(of a person or living thing) having lived for a long time; advanced in years.

Examples

An old man
He became wise as he grew old.
My grandmother is ninety years old and still very active.
Their old dog sleeps most of the day now.
