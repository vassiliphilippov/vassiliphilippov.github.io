sally

noun
military sortie from besieged position
Pronunciation: / ˈsæli / (AmE), / ˈsæli / (BrE)
a sortie made by troops in a besieged position in order to attack the besiegers

Examples

A military sally
A sudden sally surprised the attackers and pushed them back from the fortress walls.
During the siege, several sallies were attempted to break the enemy's lines.
The defenders made a bold sally against the besieging army at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sortie - an attack made by troops coming out from a position of defense
