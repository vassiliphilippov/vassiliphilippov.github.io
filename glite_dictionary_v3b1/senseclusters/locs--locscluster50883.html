locs

noun
rope-like hairstyle
Pronunciation: / lɑks / (AmE), / lɒks / (BrE)
a hairstyle in which sections of hair are twisted, matted, or braided into rope-like strands

Examples

Long locs hairstyle
She wore her locs in a neat bun for the interview.
After years of growing his locs, he was proud of their length and strength.
Many people choose to style their locs with beads and colorful wraps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braids - hair woven together in three or more strands
  • synonym: dreadlocks - hair styled into long, matted, rope-like strands
