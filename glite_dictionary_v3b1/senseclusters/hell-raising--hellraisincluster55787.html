hell-raising

noun
rowdy or mischievous behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlˌreɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɛlˌreɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
rowdy or mischievous behavior, often intended to cause trouble or excitement for fun

Examples

Wild hell-raising
In his youth, he was famous for hell-raising and wild parties.
The film tells the story of a hell-raising rock band on tour.
Their hell-raising antics got them kicked out of the club last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mischief - playful misbehavior or troublemaking
  • antonym: orderliness - the quality of being neat and well-behaved
  • synonym: rowdiness - noisy and disorderly behavior
