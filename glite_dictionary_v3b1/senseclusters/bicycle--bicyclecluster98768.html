bicycle

noun
two-wheeled pedal-powered vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪ.sɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ.sɪ.kəl / (BrE)
a vehicle with two wheels that is powered by a person pushing pedals with their feet

Examples

Ride a bicycle
Every morning, he commutes to work on his bicycle.
She learned to ride a bicycle when she was five years old.
They parked their bicycle outside the café while they had lunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bike - a shortened, informal term for bicycle
  • degree higher: tricycle - a vehicle with three wheels, especially for children
  • degree lower: unicycle - a vehicle with one wheel, usually ridden for performance or skill
