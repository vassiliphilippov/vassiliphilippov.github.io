oversubscription

noun
excess demand
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərsəbˈskrɪpʃən / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəsəbˈskrɪpʃən / (BrE)
a situation in which the demand for something is greater than the available supply or capacity

Examples

Course oversubscription
The popular course experienced oversubscription, so not all students could enroll.
Due to oversubscription, the company had to hold a lottery for the limited tickets.
The housing project faced oversubscription as more people applied than there were available units.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shortage - a lack of something needed or desired
  • antonym: surplus - an amount of something left over when requirements have been met
