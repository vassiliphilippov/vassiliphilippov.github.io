Energy Secretary

noun
head of the Department of Energy
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnərdʒi ˈsɛkrəˌtɛri /; / ˈɛnɚdʒi səˌkrɛtɛri / (AmE), / ˈɛnədʒi ˈsɛkrətəri /; / ˈɛnədʒi ˈsɛkrətri / (BrE)
the government official who leads the Department of Energy and is responsible for national energy policy
