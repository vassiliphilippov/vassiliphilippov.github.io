leprosis

noun
coffee plant disease
9.9
Pronunciation: / lɛˈproʊsɪs / (AmE), / lɛˈprəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a viral disease of plants (such as coffee or citrus) that causes lesions, dead spots, and tissue damage
