earthily

adverb
coarsely or vulgarly
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθɪli / (AmE), / ˈɜːθɪli / (BrE)
in a coarse, direct, or sometimes sexual and vulgar manner

Examples

To joke earthily
He joked earthily about the topic, making everyone at the table laugh and blush.
The comedian spoke earthily, using language that some found a bit too direct.
She described the scene earthily, not shying away from the crude details.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bawdily - in a humorously indecent or lewd way
  • synonym: coarsely - in a rough or crude manner
  • antonym: refinedly - in a polished or elegant manner
