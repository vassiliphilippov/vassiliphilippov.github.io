lobscouse

noun
sailors' meat and vegetable stew
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːbˌskaʊs / (AmE), / ˈlɒbˌskaʊs / (BrE)
a sailor's stew made from meat, vegetables, and hardtack, traditionally eaten at sea

Examples

A lobscouse stew
On the old ship, lobscouse was a staple dish made with whatever ingredients were available.
She recreated a traditional lobscouse recipe for the maritime festival.
The sailors enjoyed a hearty meal of lobscouse after a long day at sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scouse - a similar stew and also a nickname for people from Liverpool
