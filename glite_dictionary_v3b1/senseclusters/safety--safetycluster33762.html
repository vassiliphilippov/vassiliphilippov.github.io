safety

noun
safe place or condition
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti /; / ˈseɪf.ti / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti /; / ˈseɪf.ti / (BrE)
the state or condition of being protected from danger, risk, or harm; a place or situation where you are safe

Examples

Personal safety
She found safety behind the locked doors of her house.
The hikers finally reached the safety of their campsite.
They ran to safety when the building caught fire.
The company takes every possible measure to ensure the safety of its employees.
They implemented new procedures to improve food safety in the factory.
Wearing a helmet adds an extra layer of safety while cycling on busy roads.

Related Terms

  • antonym: danger - the possibility of suffering harm or injury
  • synonym: refuge - a place that provides shelter or protection from danger
  • antonym: risk - the possibility of loss or injury
  • synonym: security - the state of being free from danger or threat
