feely

noun
emotional person
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfiː.li/ (AmE), /ˈfiː.li/ (BrE)
a person who tends to make decisions or react mainly based on emotions rather than logic

Examples

A feely person
My brother is such a feely that he cries during every sad movie.
In group discussions, the feely often focuses on how decisions will affect people's feelings.
She was called a feely because she always trusted her heart over her head.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sentimentalist - a person who is guided more by emotion than by reason
