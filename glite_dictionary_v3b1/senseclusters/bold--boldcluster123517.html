bold

adjective
fearless and daring
1.0
Pronunciation: / boʊld / (AmE), / bəʊld / (BrE)
Willing to take risks; showing a willingness to face danger or act confidently without fear.

Examples

Bold move
His bold remarks inspired others to speak up as well.
She made a bold decision to start her own business in a new country.
The bold explorers set out to cross the icy wilderness despite the risks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brave - ready to face and endure danger or pain; showing courage
  • synonym: courageous - not deterred by danger or pain; brave
  • antonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence; easily frightened
