crossways

adverb
transversely; across
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌweɪz / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌweɪz / (BrE)
In a position or direction that is across something; transversely.

Examples

Crossways over the package
She placed the ribbon crossways over the package to make a neat design.
The fallen tree lay crossways on the narrow road, blocking all traffic.
The street was closed where the truck had stopped crossways at the intersection.
