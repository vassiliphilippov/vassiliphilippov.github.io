as cool as a cucumber

adjective
very calm and composed
4.1 0.04/m

Examples

As cool as a cucumber in crisis
Despite the chaos in the office, Marcus stayed as cool as a cucumber and solved the problem swiftly.

She walked up to the stage as cool as a cucumber, showing no trace of nerves.
