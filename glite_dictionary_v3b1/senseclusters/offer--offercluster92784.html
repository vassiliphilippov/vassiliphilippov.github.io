offer

noun
proposal, bid, or thing presented for acceptance
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfɚ /; / ˈɔːfər / (AmE), / ˈɒfə / (BrE)
a proposal, bid, or something that is presented for acceptance or rejection

Examples

A job offer
He considered the highest offer before accepting the deal.
She received a job offer from a prestigious law firm.
Their company made an offer to purchase the building.
His offer to pay for dinner was much appreciated by everyone.
She accepted his generous offer of help without hesitation.
The job offer came with an excellent benefits package.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bid - an offer of a price, especially at an auction
  • antonym: counteroffer - an offer made in response to another offer
  • synonym: proposal - a suggestion or plan put forward for consideration
  • antonym: refusal - an act of declining or rejecting an offer or proposal
