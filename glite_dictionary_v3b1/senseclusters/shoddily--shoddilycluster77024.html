shoddily

adverb
in a careless or poor-quality way
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːdəli / (AmE), / ˈʃɒdɪli / (BrE)
In a careless or poor-quality manner; using cheap or inferior materials or effort.

Examples

To work shoddily
He handled the project shoddily, missing several important details.
The new bridge was constructed shoddily, raising concerns about its safety.
The repairs were done shoddily, so the problem soon returned.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carelessly - without care or attention; without regard for detail
  • antonym: meticulously - in a way that shows great attention to detail
