gradeless

adjective
without grades
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪdləs / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪdləs / (BrE)
not using or not having grades or marks for evaluation, especially in an educational context

Examples

Gradeless assessment
The university introduced a gradeless assessment system to encourage deeper learning.
Some students prefer gradeless classes because they feel less pressure to compete.
A gradeless report card focuses on feedback rather than letter grades.

Related Terms

  • antonym: graded - given a grade or mark
  • synonym: ungraded - not given a grade or mark
