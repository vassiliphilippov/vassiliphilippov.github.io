greenspace

noun
urban natural area
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːnˌspeɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnˌspeɪs / (BrE)
an area in a city or town with grass, trees, or other plants, set aside for recreation, nature, or environmental benefit
