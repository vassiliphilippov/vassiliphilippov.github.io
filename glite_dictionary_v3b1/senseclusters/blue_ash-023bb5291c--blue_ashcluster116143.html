blue ash

noun
a type of ash tree with bluish-green foliage
Pronunciation: / bluː ˈæʃ / (AmE), / bluː ˈaʃ / (BrE)
a North American ash tree (Fraxinus quadrangulata) with bluish-green leaves and hard, brown wood

Examples

Blue ash tree
Carpenters prize blue ash for its hard, durable lumber.
In spring, the leaves of the blue ash appear a distinct bluish-green among the other trees.
The woods behind their house are full of blue ash and hickory trees.
