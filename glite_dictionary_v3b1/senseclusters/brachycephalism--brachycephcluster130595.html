brachycephalism

noun
condition of having a short, broad head
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbrækiˈsɛfəlˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌbrækɪˈsɛfəlɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
The condition or quality of having a short, broad head; the state of being brachycephalic.

Examples

Brachycephalism in humans
Human populations may naturally vary in skull shape, including cases of brachycephalism.
Researchers studied the genetic factors contributing to brachycephalism in several animal species.
The veterinarian explained that brachycephalism can affect the breathing of certain dog breeds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brachycephalic - having a short, broad head
  • antonym: dolichocephalism - condition of having a long, narrow head
