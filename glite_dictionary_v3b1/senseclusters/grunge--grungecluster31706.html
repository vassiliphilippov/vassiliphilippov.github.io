grunge

noun
dirt; something that makes unclean
Pronunciation: / ɡrʌndʒ / (AmE), / ɡrʌndʒ / (BrE)
something dirty or regarded as making something unclean; grime or filth.

Examples

Remove grunge
She scrubbed the grunge from her shoes after hiking through the mud.
The sink was covered in a thick layer of grunge after months of use.
Years of smoke and grunge had stained the walls yellow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cleanliness - the state of being clean
  • synonym: filth - disgusting dirt; foul matter
  • synonym: grime - dirt ingrained on a surface
