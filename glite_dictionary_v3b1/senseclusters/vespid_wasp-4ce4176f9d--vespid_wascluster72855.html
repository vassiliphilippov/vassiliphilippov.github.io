vespid wasp

noun
a social, nest-building wasp (family Vespidae)
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛspɪd wɒsp / (AmE), / ˈvɛspɪd wɒsp / (BrE)
A wasp belonging to the family Vespidae, which includes mostly social, nest-building species such as yellowjackets and hornets.

Examples

A vespid wasp colony
A vespid wasp colony can contain thousands of individuals working together to build nests.
Many people mistake a vespid wasp for a bee due to its similar size and coloration.
The scientist collected samples of a vespid wasp to study its highly organized social structure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hornet - a large wasp in the family Vespidae
  • synonym: yellowjacket - a type of vespid wasp known for its yellow and black coloring
