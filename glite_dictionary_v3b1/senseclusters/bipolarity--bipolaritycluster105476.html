bipolarity

noun
having two opposites
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪpoʊˈlærəti / (AmE), / ˌbaɪpəʊˈlærəti / (BrE)
the state or quality of having two opposite or contrasting characteristics, conditions, or poles

Examples

System bipolarity
The bipolarity of the political system led to frequent disagreements between the two main parties.
Scientists studied the bipolarity of the molecule, which had both positive and negative ends.
Her personality showed a certain bipolarity, shifting between extreme optimism and deep pessimism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duality - the quality of having two parts or aspects
  • synonym: polarity - the presence of two opposite or contrasting principles or tendencies
  • antonym: unity - the state of being one or united
