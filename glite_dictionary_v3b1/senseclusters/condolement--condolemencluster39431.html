condolement

noun
expression of sympathy
Pronunciation: / kənˈdoʊlmənt /; / kənˈdoʊləmənt / (AmE), / kənˈdəʊlmənt /; / kənˈdəʊləmənt / (BrE)
the act or words of expressing sympathy or sorrow to someone who is grieving or has suffered a loss

Related Terms

  • antonym: congratulation - an expression of pleasure at another's success or good fortune
