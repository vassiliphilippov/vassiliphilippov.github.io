C.E.

adverb
abbreviation for 'Common Era' (dating years)
3.9
Pronunciation: /siː iː/ (AmE), /siː iː/ (BrE)
abbreviation for 'Common Era', used in dating years in the same way as A.D. but preferred in secular contexts

Examples

C.E. dates
