Located or applied under the skin; relating to the area beneath the epidermis.

Examples

Subcutaneous fat layer
Fat is stored in the subcutaneous layer beneath the skin.
Some medications are designed for subcutaneous delivery to ensure slow absorption.
The doctor administered the vaccine using a subcutaneous injection.

Synonyms

  • subdermal
    6.1 0.07/m
    subdermal is placed under the skin in a small area, while subcutaneous describes tissue or injections beneath the skin.
  • subepidermal
    7.9 0.00/m
    subepidermal lies just below the outer skin layer, while subcutaneous refers more broadly to tissue placed under the skin.
  • hypodermic
    7.0 0.47/m
    hypodermic relates to the area under the skin or is used for injections, while subcutaneous simply means under the skin.
