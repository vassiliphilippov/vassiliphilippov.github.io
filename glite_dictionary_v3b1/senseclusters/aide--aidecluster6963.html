aide

noun
assistant or helper, often in a professional role
5.8
Pronunciation: / eɪd / (AmE), / eɪd / (BrE)
a person who assists another, especially in a professional, official, or military capacity

Examples

Personal aide
His aide handed him the documents he needed for the meeting.

As an aide to the colonel, she often coordinated communications between units.
The general's aide handed him the confidential report before the meeting.
The young captain served as an aide during the overseas mission.
