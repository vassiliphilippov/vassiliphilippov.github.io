streaky

adjective
marked with stripes or lines
4.4 0.27/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆstriËki / (AmE), / ËˆstriËki / (BrE)
Marked with stripes or irregular lines of color, or having uneven areas of color or texture.

Examples

Streaky marks
After running in the rain, her mascara left streaky marks down her cheeks.
He bought a streaky piece of bacon with visible layers of fat and meat.
The sky appeared streaky with clouds stretched across the horizon.

Synonyms

  • streaked
    2.7 0.00/m

  • striped
    1.0 1.23/m

  • stripy
    1.0 0.05/m
    stripy has informal, often narrow stripes used in casual speech, while streaky suggests uneven color or texture.
