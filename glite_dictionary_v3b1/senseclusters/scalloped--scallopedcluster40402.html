scalloped

adjective
having an edge with rounded projections
6.3 3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈskæl.əpt/ (AmE), /ˈskɒl.əpt/ (BrE)
having an edge or margin marked by a series of rounded projections or curves resembling the edge of a scallop shell

Examples

Scalloped edge design
She admired the scalloped border on the ceramic plate.
The edges of the tablecloth were scalloped, giving it an elegant and decorative look.
The leaf had a scalloped margin that made it easy to identify.

Related Terms

  • antonym: serrated - having a jagged or saw-toothed edge
