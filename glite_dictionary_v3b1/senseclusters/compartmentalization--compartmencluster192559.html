compartmentalization

noun
the act of dividing into sections or categories
Pronunciation: / kəmˌpɑːrtˌmɛntələˈzeɪʃən /; / kəmˌpɑːrtˌmentl̩əˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / kəmˌpɑːtmɛntəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / kəmˌpɑːtməntəlʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of dividing something into separate sections or categories, especially to organize or isolate parts
