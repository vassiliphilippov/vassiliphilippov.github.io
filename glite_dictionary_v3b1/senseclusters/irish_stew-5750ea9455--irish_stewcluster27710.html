Irish stew

noun
traditional Irish meat and potato stew
1.0 2.1
Pronunciation: /ˈaɪ.rɪʃ stuː/ /; / /ˈaɪ.ər.ɪʃ stuː/ /; / /ˈaɪ.rɪʃ stjuː/ (AmE), /ˈaɪ.rɪʃ stjuː/ /; / /ˈaɪ.ə.rɪʃ stjuː/ /; / /ˈaɪ.rɪʃ stʃuː/ (BrE)
A traditional Irish dish consisting of meat, usually mutton or lamb, slow-cooked with potatoes, onions, and sometimes other vegetables.
