lesser hemlock

noun
poisonous plant resembling parsley
9.9 8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈles.ər ˈhem.lɑːk/ /; / /ˈlɛs.ər ˈhɛm.lɑːk/ (AmE), /ˈlɛs.ə ˈhɛm.lɒk/ /; / /ˈles.ə ˈhem.lɒk/ (BrE)
