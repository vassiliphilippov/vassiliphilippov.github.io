beatbox

verb
to make vocal rhythms
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtbɑks / (AmE), / ˈbiːtbɒks / (BrE)
to create music or rhythm sounds using only the mouth, lips, tongue, and voice

Examples

To beatbox on stage
He learned to beatbox by watching online tutorials and practicing every day.
During the performance, she started to beatbox and amazed the entire audience.
Some musicians beatbox to add rhythm to their a cappella groups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vocal percussion - the art of producing drum sounds with the voice
