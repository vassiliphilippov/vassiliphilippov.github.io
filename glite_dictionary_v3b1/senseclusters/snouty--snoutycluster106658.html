snouty

adjective
prominent nose
Pronunciation: / ˈsnaʊti / (AmE), / ˈsnaʊti / (BrE)
having a prominent or noticeable snout or nose

Examples

Snouty pig faces
The piglets had snouty faces that made them look adorable.
Her drawing of the dog emphasized its snouty features.
Some reptiles have especially snouty heads to help them find food.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flat-nosed - having a flat or less prominent nose
  • synonym: nosy - having a large or noticeable nose
