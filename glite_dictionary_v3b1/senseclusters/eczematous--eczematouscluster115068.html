eczematous

adjective
relating to eczema
Pronunciation: / ɛkˈsiːmətəs / (AmE), / ɛkˈziːmətəs / (BrE)
having the characteristics of or relating to eczema, especially in appearance or symptoms

Examples

Eczematous skin rash
The doctor noted several eczematous lesions on the patient's hands.
Her skin became eczematous after using a new detergent.
Treatment helped reduce the eczematous inflammation on his legs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eczema - a medical condition causing inflamed, itchy skin
  • synonym: psoriatic - relating to or affected by psoriasis
