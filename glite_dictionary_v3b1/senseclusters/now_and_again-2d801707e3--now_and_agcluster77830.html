now and again

adverb
occasionally; from time to time
6.1
Pronunciation: / naʊ ənd əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / naʊ ənd əˈɡɛn / (BrE)
from time to time; occasionally; not very often.

Examples

Now and again I eat cake
Now and again, I treat myself to a slice of cake after dinner.
Now and again, the town holds a festival that brings everyone together.
He checks his old email account now and again, just in case he’s missed something important.

  • synonym: occasionally - at some times; not regularly
