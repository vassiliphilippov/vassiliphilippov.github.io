hydronic

adjective
using liquid for heating
8.1
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdrɑnɪk / (AmE), / haɪˈdrɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to heating or cooling systems that use water or another liquid to transfer heat

Examples

Hydronic heating system
The new house features a hydronic floor heating system for even warmth throughout the rooms.
Many modern buildings use hydronic systems to improve energy efficiency and comfort.
A hydronic radiator uses hot water to heat the space quietly and effectively.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forced-air - relating to heating systems that use air to transfer heat
  • synonym: radiant - relating to the transfer of heat by radiation
