sympatrically

adverb
in the same area
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪmˈpætrɪkli / (AmE), / sɪmˈpætrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that occurs within the same geographic area, especially referring to species living or evolving together without physical separation

Examples

Evolve sympatrically together
The two plant species evolved sympatrically in the same forest without being separated by barriers.
Researchers observed that the animals lived sympatrically and interacted frequently in their shared habitat.
Some fish species can speciate sympatrically in a single lake, without geographic isolation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: allopatrically - in a way that occurs in separate geographic areas
