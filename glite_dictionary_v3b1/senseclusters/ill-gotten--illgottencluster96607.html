ill-gotten

adjective
obtained illegally or dishonestly
6.3
Pronunciation: / ɪl ˈɡɑtən / (AmE), / ɪl ˈɡɒtən / (BrE)
(of money, goods, etc.) obtained by illegal or dishonest means.

Examples

Ill-gotten money

The authorities seized his ill-gotten gains after a lengthy investigation.
