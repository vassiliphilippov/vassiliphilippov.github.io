starving artist

noun
artist struggling financially
3.8 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈstɑːr.vɪŋ ˈɑːr.tɪst/ /; / /ˈstɑːr.vɪŋ ˈɑːr.təst/ (AmE), /ˈstɑː.vɪŋ ˈɑː.tɪst/ /; / /ˈstɑː.vɪŋ ˈɑː.təst/ (BrE)
