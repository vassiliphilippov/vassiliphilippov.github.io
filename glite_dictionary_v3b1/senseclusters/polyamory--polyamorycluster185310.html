polyamory

noun
multiple romantic relationships
7.9
Pronunciation: /pɑː.li.ˈæm.ə.riː/ (AmE), /pɒl.ɪ.ˈæm.ə.riː/ (BrE)
the practice or belief of having romantic or sexual relationships with more than one person at the same time, with the consent of everyone involved
