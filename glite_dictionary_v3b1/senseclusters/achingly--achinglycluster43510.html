achingly

adverb
with deep intensity
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈeɪkɪŋli / (BrE)

Examples

An achingly beautiful song

He looked achingly at the old photographs, remembering happier times.
The story is achingly honest about the struggles of growing up.
