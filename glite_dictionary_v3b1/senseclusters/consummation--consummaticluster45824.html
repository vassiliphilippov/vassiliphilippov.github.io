consummation

noun
the act of completion or fulfilment
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnsəˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒnsjʊˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of bringing something to completion or fulfilment

Examples

Final consummation
She waited patiently for the consummation of her years of hard work and dedication.
The consummation of the long-awaited peace treaty was celebrated by both nations.
The project reached its consummation after many months of planning and effort.

  • synonym: completion - the act of completing something
