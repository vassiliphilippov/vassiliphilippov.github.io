euronithopod

noun
group of herbivorous dinosaurs including hadrosaurs and iguanodon
9.9
Pronunciation: / jʊroʊˈnɪθəˌpɑd / (AmE), / jʊərəʊˈnɪθəpɒd / (BrE)
A member of a widespread group of herbivorous dinosaurs that includes duck-billed dinosaurs and their early relatives such as hadrosaurs, trachodon, and iguanodon.

Examples

A euronithopod dinosaur
Scientists believe that euronithopods were among the most successful herbivorous dinosaurs of the Cretaceous period.
The discovery of a euronithopod fossil shed new light on their evolutionary history.
The hadrosaur, a type of euronithopod, is known for its distinctive duck-billed snout.
