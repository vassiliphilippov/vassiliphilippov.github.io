dystopic

adjective
like a miserable society
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtoʊpɪk / (AmE), / dɪsˈtəʊpɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling an imagined society where there is great suffering, oppression, or injustice

Examples

A dystopic society
The novel describes a dystopic world where freedom is completely suppressed.
Many critics called the city’s future plans dystopic and deeply troubling.
Her artwork often features dystopic landscapes filled with ruined buildings and dark skies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nightmarish - frightening or extremely unpleasant, like a nightmare
  • antonym: utopian - relating to an ideal or perfect society
