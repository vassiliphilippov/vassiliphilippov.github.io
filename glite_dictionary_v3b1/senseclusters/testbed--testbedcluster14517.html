testbed

noun
testing environment
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛstˌbɛd / (AmE), / ˈtɛstˌbɛd / (BrE)
a controlled environment or setup used for testing new ideas, products, or technologies before wider use

Examples

A testbed environment
The engineers used a testbed to evaluate the performance of the new engine design.
Before launching the app, the team ran it on a secure testbed to find any bugs.
This laboratory serves as a testbed for innovative medical devices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laboratory - a place equipped for scientific experiments and research
  • synonym: prototype - an early sample or model used to test a concept
