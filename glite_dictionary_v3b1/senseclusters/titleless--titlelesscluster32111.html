titleless

adjective
without legal title
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪtələs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtələs / (BrE)
lacking legal title or official documentation proving ownership of property or goods

Examples

Titleless land
The land was titleless, making it difficult for the family to prove their ownership.
Buyers should be cautious when purchasing titleless vehicles, as legal issues may arise.
He discovered the antique was titleless, so he could not sell it at auction.
