token

noun
digital asset on a blockchain
6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtoʊ.kən/ (AmE), /ˈtəʊ.kən/ (BrE)
a digital asset created and recorded on a blockchain that represents value, rights, access, or ownership within a decentralized network or application.

Examples

A crypto token
The project issued a new token on the blockchain to grant holders voting rights.
Investors traded several tokens on decentralized exchanges after the startup announced its tokenomics.
She used the platform's native token to pay for transaction fees and access premium features.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryptocurrency - a digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography and decentralized networks to secure transactions.
  • synonym: digital asset - any item of value that exists in digital form and may be traded or used within digital systems.
