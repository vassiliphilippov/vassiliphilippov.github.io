click

noun
catch in a ratchet mechanism
6.1
Pronunciation: / klɪk / (AmE), / klɪk / (BrE)
A hinged catch in a ratchet mechanism that fits into a notch to move a wheel forward or prevent it from moving backward.

Examples

A ratchet click
After cleaning the ratchet, the click worked smoothly again.
If the click is worn, the mechanism may fail to operate properly.
The click engaged, stopping the wheel from turning in reverse.
