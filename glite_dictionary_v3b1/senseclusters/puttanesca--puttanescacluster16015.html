puttanesca

noun
Italian pasta sauce
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌputəˈnɛskə / (AmE), / ˌpʊtəˈnɛskə / (BrE)
a spicy Italian pasta sauce made with tomatoes, olives, capers, garlic, and sometimes anchovies

Examples

Puttanesca sauce recipe
He cooked a delicious puttanesca for dinner, filling the kitchen with the aroma of olives and garlic.
The restaurant's signature dish is spaghetti puttanesca, known for its bold and tangy flavors.
She learned to make puttanesca from her Italian grandmother, using fresh tomatoes and capers.
