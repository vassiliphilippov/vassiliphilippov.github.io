knicker

noun
knee-length trousers
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪkər / (AmE), / ˈnɪkə / (BrE)
a loose-fitting knee-length trouser gathered at the knee, worn for sports or as historical dress

Examples

Old-fashioned knicker pants
The actor's costume included a period knicker ending just below the knee.
He wore brown knickers and long socks for the vintage cycling event.
Golfers in the early 1900s often played in knickers and flat caps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knickerbocker - a type of loose-fitting knee-length trouser
  • degree lower: shorts - short trousers ending above the knee
  • synonym: trousers - an outer garment covering the body from the waist to the ankles, with a separate part for each leg
