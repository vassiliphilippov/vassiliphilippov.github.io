closable

adjective
can be made closed
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkləʊzəbəl / (BrE)
in mathematics, describing an operator that can be extended to a closed operator by taking its closure

Examples

Closable operator
The operator defined on smooth functions is closable in the Hilbert space setting.
If an operator is closable, its closure is unique and well-defined.
Mathematicians often check whether a given unbounded operator is closable before proceeding with further analysis.
