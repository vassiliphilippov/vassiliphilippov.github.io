A nut with two flat projections that allow it to be tightened and loosened by hand without tools.

Examples

Tighten a wing-nut by hand
He replaced the regular nut with a wing-nut so he could adjust it without a wrench.
Make sure the wing-nut is securely fastened before using the machine.
The shelf came with wing-nuts for easy assembly by hand.

Synonyms

  • butterfly nut
    6.1 0.01/m
    a butterfly nut has two flat winglike projections for easy hand turning, while a wing-nut is the general name for this hand-tightened fastener.
  • thumbnut
    9.2 0.01/m
    a thumbnut has smaller or ridged projections for turning by fingers, while a wing-nut normally has larger flat wings for hand tightening.
