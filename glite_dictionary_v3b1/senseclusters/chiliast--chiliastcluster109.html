chiliast

noun
believer in the coming millennium
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪliˌæst / (AmE), / ˈkɪlɪæst / (BrE)
a person who believes in the coming of a future thousand-year age of peace and prosperity (the millennium), especially in a religious or doctrinal context

Examples

A chiliast believer
As a devoted chiliast, he wrote extensively on his vision of a future millennium.
Many early Protestant reformers were known as chiliasts because of their belief in a coming golden age.
The doctrine promoted by the chiliast gained popularity among the congregation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: millenarian - person believing in the coming of a millennium
