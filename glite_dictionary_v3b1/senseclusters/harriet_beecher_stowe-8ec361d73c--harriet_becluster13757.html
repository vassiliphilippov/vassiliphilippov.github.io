Harriet Beecher Stowe

proper noun
American author of 'Uncle Tom's Cabin'
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhæriət ˈbiːtʃər stoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhæriət ˈbiːtʃə stəʊ / (BrE)
(1811–1896) An American author best known for her novel 'Uncle Tom's Cabin', which powerfully depicted the realities of slavery and helped fuel the abolitionist movement in the United States.
