bonelessly

adverb
without bones
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊnlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnlɪsli / (BrE)
in a way that lacks bones or with the bones removed, especially referring to food or body parts.

Examples

Cooked bonelessly
The chicken was prepared bonelessly so that children could eat it easily.
She moved bonelessly across the stage, her body appearing to flow without structure.
The fish was served bonelessly, making it perfect for a quick meal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deboned - having had the bones removed
