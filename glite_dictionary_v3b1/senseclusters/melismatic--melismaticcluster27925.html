melismatic

adjective
singing multiple notes per syllable
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛlɪzˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛlɪzˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by singing a single syllable of text while moving between several notes

Examples

Melismatic singing style
The choir performed a melismatic passage that showcased their vocal agility.
Her melismatic singing style is common in traditional Middle Eastern music.
Listeners admired the melismatic ornamentation in the soloist's rendition of the hymn.
