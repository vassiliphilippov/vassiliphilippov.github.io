airwaves

noun
radio or TV frequencies
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌweɪvz / (AmE), / ˈeəˌweɪvz / (BrE)
the radio or television frequencies used for broadcasting programs and signals

Examples

Across the airwaves
The president's speech was transmitted across the airwaves to millions of listeners.
Many people still get their news from the airwaves rather than the internet.
Pirate radio stations sometimes illegally use the airwaves to broadcast music.
The president addressed the nation over the airwaves last night.
After years off the radio, her voice returned to the airwaves with a new show.
Controversial opinions often spark debate when they hit the airwaves.

  • synonym: frequency - a specific channel for radio or TV transmission
  • synonym: transmission - the act of sending out radio or television signals
