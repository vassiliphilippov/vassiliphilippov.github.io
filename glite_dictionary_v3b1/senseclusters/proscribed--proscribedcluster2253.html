proscribed

adjective
forbidden; banned by authority
8.8
Pronunciation: / proʊˈskraɪbd / (AmE), / prəˈskraɪbd / (BrE)
forbidden or not allowed, especially by law or official authority.

Examples

A proscribed substance
Certain substances remain proscribed by international law.
Discussion of the controversial policy was proscribed in the meeting.
The film contains scenes that were once proscribed by local censors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forbidden - not allowed; banned
  • antonym: permitted - allowed; not forbidden
