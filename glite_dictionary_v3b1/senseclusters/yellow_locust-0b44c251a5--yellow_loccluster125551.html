yellow locust

noun
thorny tree with white flowers, native to U.S.
7.9 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈje.loʊ ˈloʊ.kəst/ (AmE), /ˈje.ləʊ ˈləʊ.kəst/ (BrE)
