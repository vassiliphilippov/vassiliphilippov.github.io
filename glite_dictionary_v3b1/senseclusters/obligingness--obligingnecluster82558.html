obligingness

noun
willingness to help others
Pronunciation: / əˈblaɪdʒɪŋnɪs / (AmE), / əˈblaɪdʒɪŋnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being willing to do favors or help others; readiness to oblige

Examples

Obligingness to others
Her obligingness made her a favorite among her colleagues, who often depended on her assistance.
I appreciate his obligingness whenever I ask him for a favor.
The obligingness of the hotel staff made our stay very pleasant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: helpfulness - the quality of being useful or willing to help
  • antonym: unhelpfulness - the quality of being unwilling to help
