disarmer

noun
person opposed to violence; supports disarmament
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɑrmər / (AmE), / dɪˈzɑːmə / (BrE)
a person who opposes the use of violence and supports disarmament, especially as a means of resolving disputes

Examples

Peaceful disarmer
As a lifelong disarmer, she has participated in many protests against military intervention.
Some critics argue that a true disarmer must reject all forms of violent resistance.
The peace talks included several prominent disarmers who advocated for the removal of all nuclear weapons.

Related Terms

  • antonym: militarist - a person who believes in maintaining a strong military capability
  • synonym: pacifist - a person who believes that war and violence are unjustifiable
