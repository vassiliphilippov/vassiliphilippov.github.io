sundae

noun
ice cream dessert with toppings
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌn.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.deɪ / (BrE)
a dessert of ice cream served with a sweet sauce or topping, such as syrup, fruit, or nuts

Examples

A sundae dessert
I'd like a hot fudge sundae for dessert.
She topped her sundae with sprinkles and cherries.
The restaurant offers a build-your-own sundae bar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ice cream - a sweet frozen food made from cream or milk and sugar
