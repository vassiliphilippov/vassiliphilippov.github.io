ahead of the curve

adjective
more advanced than others
Pronunciation: / əˈhɛd əv ðə kɝv / (AmE), / əˈhɛd əv ðə kɜːv / (BrE)
more advanced, informed, or innovative than others in a particular field or activity

Examples

Ahead of the curve in technology
Investing in renewable energy now helps nations remain ahead of the curve as technology evolves.
She takes extra training to stay ahead of the curve when new regulations come out.
The company's commitment to innovation keeps it ahead of the curve in its industry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: behind the curve - less advanced or up-to-date than others
  • synonym: cutting edge - the most advanced or innovative stage
