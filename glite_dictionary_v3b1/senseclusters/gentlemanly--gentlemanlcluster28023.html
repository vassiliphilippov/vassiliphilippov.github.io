gentlemanly

adjective
characteristic of a gentleman; courteous
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛnt(ə)lˌmænli / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnt(ə)lˌmænli / (BrE)
polite, honorable, or considerate in a way thought to be characteristic of a gentleman

Examples

A gentlemanly manner
Despite the heated argument, her opponent remained calm and gentlemanly.
He responded to their criticisms in a gentlemanly manner, never raising his voice.
His gentlemanly conduct impressed everyone at the formal dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chivalrous - showing courage, honor, and readiness to help others
  • antonym: ladylike - appropriate to or characteristic of a lady
  • antonym: uncouth - lacking good manners or refinement
