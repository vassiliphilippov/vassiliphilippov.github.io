unlevel

adjective
uneven or irregular
3.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɛvəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɛvəl / (BrE)
not even, flat, or regular; having an uneven or irregular surface or slope

Examples

Unlevel surface
The table was placed on an unlevel floor, causing the dishes to slide to one side.
His performance was unlevel throughout the season, with some games much better than others.
Walking on the unlevel ground made it difficult to keep her balance.
The old floorboards made the entire room feel unlevel when you walked across it.
We had to place a wedge under the chair because the ground was unlevel.
Painting on an unlevel surface can be very challenging for beginners.
