beaded

adjective
decorated with beads
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbiːdɪd / (BrE)
decorated or covered with small round objects called beads, often sewn onto fabric or string

Examples

Beaded dress
She wore a beautiful beaded dress to the party.
The artist created a beaded necklace using colorful glass beads.
The traditional costume was heavily beaded along the sleeves and collar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embroidered - decorated with patterns sewn using thread
  • antonym: plain - not decorated or ornamented
