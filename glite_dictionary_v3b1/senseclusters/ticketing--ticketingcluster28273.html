ticketing

noun
selling or issuing tickets
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of selling, issuing, or managing tickets for events, travel, or services

Examples

Online ticketing
The new ticketing system allows customers to buy train tickets online quickly and easily.
Many theaters have switched to digital ticketing to reduce paper waste and speed up entry.
Problems with ticketing caused long lines at the entrance to the football match.
