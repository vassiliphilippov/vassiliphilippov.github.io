internationalist

noun
advocate of international cooperation
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.tɚˈnæʃ.ə.nə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈnæʃ.ə.nə.lɪst / (BrE)
A person who supports or advocates international cooperation or internationalism.

Examples

An internationalist thinker
As an internationalist, he believes global cooperation is essential for addressing climate change.
Many internationalists attended the conference to discuss cross-border environmental challenges.
She is a prominent internationalist who has worked tirelessly for global peace initiatives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nationalist - a person who advocates for national independence or interests
