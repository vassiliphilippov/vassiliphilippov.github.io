preadoption

noun
before official adoption
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːəˈdɑːpʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːəˈdɒpʃən / (BrE)
the period or process before something, especially a child or policy, is officially adopted

Examples

Preadoption period
The agency offers preadoption counseling to prospective parents.
During the preadoption phase, families undergo background checks and interviews.
Many questions arise in the preadoption process regarding legal and emotional readiness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postadoption - the period or process after something is officially adopted
  • synonym: preliminary - coming before the main event or action
