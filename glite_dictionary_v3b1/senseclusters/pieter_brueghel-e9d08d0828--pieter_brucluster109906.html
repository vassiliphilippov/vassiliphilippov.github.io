Pieter Brueghel

proper noun
Flemish painter (1525–1569) noted for landscapes
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːtər ˈbrɔɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈpiːtə ˈbrɔɪɡəl / (BrE)
(1525–1569) A renowned Flemish painter known for his detailed landscapes and scenes of peasant life.
