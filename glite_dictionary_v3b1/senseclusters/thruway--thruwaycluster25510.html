A wide road built for high-speed vehicular traffic, typically with limited access points.

Examples

A thruway road
The new thruway has greatly reduced travel times between towns.
There was an accident on the thruway this morning, causing major delays.
We took the thruway to get to the city faster.

Synonyms

  • freeway
    2.2 12/m
    freeway is a wide road with no intersections and fast traffic, while thruway refers to a limited-access high-speed highway.
  • expressway
    2.8 1.73/m
    expressway is a wide road built for fast travel with few access points, while thruway is a similar limited-access route.
  • motorway
    1.8 0.57/m
    motorway is a multi-lane road for fast traffic in British English, while thruway is the American term for limited-access highways.
  • superhighway
    3.5 0.36/m
    superhighway is a very wide, high-capacity road often described informally, while thruway is a standard term for limited-access highways.
