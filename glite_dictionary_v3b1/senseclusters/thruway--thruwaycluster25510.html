thruway

noun
high-speed highway with limited access
Pronunciation: / ˈθruˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈθruːweɪ / (BrE)
A wide road built for high-speed vehicular traffic, typically with limited access points.

Examples

A thruway road
The new thruway has greatly reduced travel times between towns.
There was an accident on the thruway this morning, causing major delays.
We took the thruway to get to the city faster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expressway - a major highway designed for fast traffic with controlled entry and exit
  • synonym: freeway - an express highway with no intersections, mainly American English
  • synonym: motorway - a road for fast traffic, especially in Britain
