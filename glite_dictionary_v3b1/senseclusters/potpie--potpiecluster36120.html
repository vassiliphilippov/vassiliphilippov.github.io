potpie

noun
deep-dish savory pie or stew
4.6 2.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑːt.paɪ/ (AmE), /ˈpɒt.paɪ/ (BrE)
A savory dish made by baking meat and vegetables, often under a pastry crust, or a meat stew with dumplings.

Examples

A potpie dish

She made a delicious chicken potpie for dinner last night.
The restaurant's beef potpie is a customer favorite during the winter months.
