all-night

adjective
lasting or operating all night
Pronunciation: / ˈɔlˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɔːlˌnaɪt / (BrE)
lasting, open, or operating through the whole night; continuing for the entire night.

Examples

An all-night session

The city is famous for its all-night bus service.
