dehydrated

adjective
lacking body water or fluids
2.6
Pronunciation: / diˈhaɪ.dɹeɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / diˈhaɪ.dɹeɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
suffering from an excessive loss of water from the body; lacking sufficient fluids to function normally

Examples

Severely dehydrated patient
After hiking for hours without enough water, she became dehydrated and dizzy.
Athletes are at risk of becoming dehydrated if they don't drink during long workouts.
The nurse checked whether the patient was dehydrated after the illness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hydrated - having enough water in the body
  • synonym: parched - extremely dry or thirsty due to lack of water
