apothecary

noun
person who prepared and sold medicines
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑː.θəˌkɛr.i / (AmE), / əˈpɒθ.ɪ.kər.i / (BrE)
a person, especially in the past, who prepared and sold medicines and drugs

Examples

Village apothecary
In the 18th century, a visit to the apothecary was common when someone fell ill.
She inherited her skills as an apothecary from her father, who ran the local shop.
The town's apothecary carefully measured herbs and powders for each remedy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pharmacist - a person trained to prepare and dispense medicinal drugs
