barite

noun
white mineral; barium source
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrˌaɪt /; / ˈbɛrˌaɪtiːz / (AmE), / ˈbɑːr.aɪt /; / ˈbærʌɪtiːz / (BrE)
A white or colorless mineral consisting of barium sulfate, used as the main source of barium.
