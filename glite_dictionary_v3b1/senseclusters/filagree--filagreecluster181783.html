filagree

noun
delicate wire ornamental work
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪləˌɡri / (AmE), / ˈfɪlɪɡriː / (BrE)
Delicate and intricate ornamental work, often made from fine twisted gold or silver wire.

Examples

Delicate filagree work
She admired the delicate filagree on the silver jewelry box.
Skilled artisans crafted the crown's filagree by hand.
The antique brooch was adorned with gold filagree that sparkled in the light.
