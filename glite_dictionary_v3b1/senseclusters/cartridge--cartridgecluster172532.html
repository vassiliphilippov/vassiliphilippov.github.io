cartridge

noun
ammunition case for firearms
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrtrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtrɪdʒ / (BrE)
A metal or plastic case containing a charge and a bullet or shot, used as ammunition in firearms.

Examples

Rifle cartridge
Always use the correct type of cartridge for your handgun to ensure safety.
She found a spent cartridge on the forest floor after the shooting practice.
The hunter loaded a new cartridge into his rifle before aiming at the target.
