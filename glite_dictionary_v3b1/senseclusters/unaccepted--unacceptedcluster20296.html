unaccepted

adjective
not approved or not accepted
Pronunciation: / ʌnəkˈsɛptɪd / (AmE), / ʌnəkˈsɛptɪd / (BrE)
not approved or regarded as conforming to standards; not widely accepted or admitted.

Examples

An unaccepted idea
Her views were considered unaccepted by the mainstream scientific community at the time.
The proposal was unaccepted by the committee, so they had to revise it.
Using technical jargon in a friendly letter may be seen as unaccepted usage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unacceptable - not satisfactory or allowable; intolerable
