pipe dream

noun
unrealistic or impractical hope
Pronunciation: / paɪp driːm / (AmE), / paɪp driːm / (BrE)
an unrealistic or impossible hope, plan, or fantasy that is very unlikely to happen

Owning a mansion by the sea is just a pipe dream for most people.
