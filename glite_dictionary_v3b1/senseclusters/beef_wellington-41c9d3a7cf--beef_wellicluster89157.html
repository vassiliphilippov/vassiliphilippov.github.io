beef Wellington

noun
beef dish in pastry
3.6
Pronunciation: / bif ˈwɛlɪŋtən / (AmE), / biːf ˈwɛlɪŋtən / (BrE)
A dish consisting of rare-roasted beef tenderloin coated with a layer of mushroom paste and wrapped in puff pastry, then baked.

Examples

A beef Wellington dish
For our anniversary dinner, she prepared a delicious beef Wellington served with roasted potatoes.
His attempt at making beef Wellington was surprisingly successful for a first try.
The restaurant is famous for its perfectly cooked beef Wellington and creamy sauces.
