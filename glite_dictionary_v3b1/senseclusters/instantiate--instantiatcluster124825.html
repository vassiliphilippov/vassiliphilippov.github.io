instantiate

verb
to give or find an example of
8.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstænʃieɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈstænʃieɪt / (BrE)
to provide or find an example of something, especially a word or usage, in actual use

Examples

Instantiate a word
Can you instantiate this grammatical pattern with a few examples from modern literature?
It can be difficult to instantiate rare meanings of common words in contemporary speech.
The linguist tried to instantiate the new word by quoting a passage from local newspapers.
