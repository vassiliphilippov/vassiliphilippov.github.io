earthly reason

noun
rational or plausible explanation
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθli ˈriːzən / (AmE), / ˈɜːθli ˈriːzn / (BrE)
a rational or plausible explanation for something, especially when none seems apparent

Examples

Any earthly reason
Is there any earthly reason why you would skip such an important meeting?
She couldn't imagine an earthly reason why the letter took so long to arrive.
There seemed to be no earthly reason for the power outage during the clear afternoon.
