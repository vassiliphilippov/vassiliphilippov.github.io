stitching

noun
joining pieces by sewing stitches
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɪtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of joining or attaching parts by making stitches with needle and thread

Examples

Strong stitching on clothes
If the stitching comes undone, the seam will split.
She admired the careful stitching along the edge of the fabric.
The stitching on this quilt is both strong and decorative.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sewing - the activity or craft of making or mending clothes or other items using a needle and thread
