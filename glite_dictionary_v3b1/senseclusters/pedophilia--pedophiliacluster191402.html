pedophilia

noun
sexual attraction to children
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛdəˈfɪliə /; / ˌpiːdəˈfɪliə / (AmE), / ˌpiːdəˈfɪliə / (BrE)
A psychiatric disorder characterized by sexual attraction to children, typically prepubescent.

Examples
