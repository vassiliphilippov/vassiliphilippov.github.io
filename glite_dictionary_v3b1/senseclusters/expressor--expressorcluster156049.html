expressor

noun
juice-extracting device
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛsər / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛsə / (BrE)
a machine or device that squeezes or presses material, such as fruit, to extract juice or liquid

Examples

Fruit expressor
The factory installed a new expressor to improve the efficiency of juice production.
After loading the grapes, the expressor quickly separated the juice from the pulp.
She bought a compact expressor for making fresh orange juice every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: juicer - a device for extracting juice from fruit or vegetables
  • synonym: press - a machine that applies pressure to extract liquid
