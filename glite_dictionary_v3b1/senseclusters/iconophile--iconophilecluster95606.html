iconophile

noun
admirer of icons
9.9
Pronunciation: /aɪ.ˈkɑːn.ə.faɪl/ /; / /ˈaɪ.kə.nə.faɪl/ (AmE), /aɪ.ˈkɒn.ə.faɪl/ /; / /ˈaɪ.kə.nə.faɪl/ (BrE)
a person who greatly admires or is devoted to famous people or cultural icons

Examples

Famous iconophile
As an iconophile, she collected autographs and memorabilia from every major pop star.
The museum's curator was a true iconophile, passionate about preserving celebrity history.
His friends teased him for being an iconophile because of his obsession with movie legends.
