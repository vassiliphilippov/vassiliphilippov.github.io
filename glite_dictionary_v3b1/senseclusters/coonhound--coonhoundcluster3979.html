coonhound

noun
breed of hound for hunting raccoons
8.9 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkuÀênÀåha änd / (AmE), / ÀàkuÀênÀåha änd / (BrE)
Any of several breeds of hound developed for hunting raccoons, known for their tracking ability and endurance.

Examples
