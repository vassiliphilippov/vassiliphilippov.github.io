side

adjective
located on a side
Pronunciation: / saɪd / (AmE), / saɪd / (BrE)
located on or relating to the side of something rather than the front, back, top, or bottom

Examples

Side street
A side street offered a quieter route away from the main road.
They built a side entrance for easier access to the garden.
We entered the house through the side door to avoid the construction in the front.
