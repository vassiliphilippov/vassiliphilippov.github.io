atoxic

adjective
not poisonous; not producing poison
4.7
Pronunciation: / eɪˈtɑksɪk / (AmE), / əˈtɒksɪk / (BrE)
Not poisonous; not producing or resulting from a toxin or poison.

Examples

An atoxic substance
Doctors prefer to use atoxic dyes during medical imaging procedures to avoid harmful reactions.
Researchers discovered that the new compound was completely atoxic in animal trials.
The laboratory is equipped to test whether a substance is atoxic before further use.
