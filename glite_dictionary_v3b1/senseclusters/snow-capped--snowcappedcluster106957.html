snow-capped

adjective
having a top covered with snow
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌkæpt / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌkæpt / (BrE)
(of a mountain or hill) having the top covered with snow.

Examples

Snow-capped mountain
From her window, she could see a snow-capped peak rising above the valley.
The snow-capped summit was a breathtaking sight against the clear blue sky.
The hikers admired the distant snow-capped mountains glowing in the evening light.
