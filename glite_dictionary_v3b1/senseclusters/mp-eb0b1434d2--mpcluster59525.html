MP

noun
military police officer
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛmˈpi /; / ɛm pi / (AmE), / ˌɛmˈpiː /; / ɛm pi / (BrE)
a member of the military police, or the military police as a group, responsible for enforcing discipline and guarding prisoners

Examples

MP patrol
The MP escorted the soldiers back to their barracks after curfew.
The MP questioned the detainee about his involvement in the incident.
Two MPs were stationed at the entrance to the facility.
After the disturbance, several MPs arrived to restore order among the troops.
Each camp had its own MP to enforce curfew and military discipline.
The MP escorted the prisoner back to the holding cell.

Related Terms

  • antonym: civilian police officer - a member of the regular police force, not military
  • synonym: military police - the police organization attached to a military force
  • synonym: military policeman - a soldier who is a member of the military police
