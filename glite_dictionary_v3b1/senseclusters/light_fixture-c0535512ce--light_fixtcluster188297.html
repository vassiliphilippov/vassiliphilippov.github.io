light fixture

noun
electrical fitting for holding a light source
4.4 3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪt ˌfɪkstʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt ˌfɪkstʃə / (BrE)
An electrical device that holds and supplies power to a light source, typically designed to allow for the replacement of the lamp.

Examples

A light fixture unit
Please turn off the main light fixture before you leave the office.
She replaced the old ceiling light fixture with a modern design.
The new light fixture in the dining room gives off a warm, inviting glow.
