insupportably

adverb
without justification
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnsəˈpɔːrtəbli /; / ɪn.səˈpɔːr.təbli / (AmE), / ˌɪnsəˈpɔːtəbli /; / ɪn.səˈpɔː.təbli / (BrE)
in a way that is impossible to support, justify, or endure

Examples

An insupportably high cost
The cost of living in the city has become insupportably high for many families.
His claims were insupportably exaggerated and quickly dismissed by the experts.
The noise from the construction site was insupportably loud throughout the night.
The pain became insupportably intense as the night went on.
She found the noise in the city insupportably loud after living in the countryside.
The humidity was insupportably high during the summer heatwave.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defensibly - in a way that can be defended or justified
  • synonym: indefensibly - in a way that cannot be defended or justified
  • synonym: intolerably - in a way that cannot be accepted or endured
  • antonym: tolerably - in a way that can be endured or accepted
  • synonym: unbearably - in a way that is impossible to endure
  • synonym: unjustifiably - in a way that cannot be justified
