culturist

noun
biological culture worker
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌl.tʃə.rɪst/ (AmE), /ˈkʌl.tʃə.rɪst/ (BrE)
a laboratory worker who grows and maintains biological cultures such as cells, bacteria, or tissues for study or testing

Examples

Lab culturist
The culturist carefully monitored the growth of cells in the laboratory incubator.
A skilled culturist prepared sterile plates to grow bacterial colonies for the hospital laboratory tests.
Every culturist must follow strict procedures to prevent contamination of the samples.
