nonexpert

adjective
without special knowledge
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈɛkspɚt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈɛkspɜːt / (BrE)
not having specialized knowledge or skill in a particular subject or activity

Examples

Nonexpert opinion
The nonexpert audience found the technical presentation difficult to follow.
As a nonexpert in finance, she asked for help understanding the report.
The article was written in simple language for nonexpert readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amateur - a person lacking professional skill or expertise
  • antonym: expert - having or showing special skill or knowledge
