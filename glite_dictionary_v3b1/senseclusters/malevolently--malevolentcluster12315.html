malevolently

adverb
in an evil or harmful-intentioned way
9.6
Pronunciation: / məˈlɛvələntli / (AmE), / məˈlɛvələntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a wish to do evil or cause harm to others.

Examples

To smile malevolently
He smiled malevolently as he revealed his cruel intentions.
Rumors spread malevolently through the small town, damaging many reputations.
She looked at him malevolently, her eyes narrowing with suspicion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: benevolently - in a kind and well-meaning way
