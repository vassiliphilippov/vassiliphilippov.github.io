Arduino

proper noun
open-source electronics platform
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈduːinoʊ / (AmE), / ɑːˈduːiːnəʊ / (BrE)
an open-source electronic board and software platform used to build and program digital devices and interactive projects

Examples

Arduino board project
He used an Arduino to build a simple weather station for his science project.
The robotics club taught students how to program an Arduino to control motors and sensors.
With an Arduino, you can easily create interactive electronic devices at home.
He learned to program an Arduino to control the lights in his room.
The robotics club used an Arduino to build their automated car project.
With an Arduino, beginners can easily experiment with sensors and motors.
