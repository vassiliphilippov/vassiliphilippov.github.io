weighing machine

noun
device for measuring weight or mass
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈweɪɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
an instrument or device used to measure weight or mass, typically showing the amount on a dial or display.

Examples

A weighing machine scale
He stepped onto the weighing machine to check his weight before the doctor's appointment.
Most gyms have a weighing machine available for members to use.
The old-fashioned weighing machine in the market still works perfectly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balance - a specific kind of weighing device using a beam and two pans
  • synonym: scale - an instrument for weighing, often a synonym for weighing machine
