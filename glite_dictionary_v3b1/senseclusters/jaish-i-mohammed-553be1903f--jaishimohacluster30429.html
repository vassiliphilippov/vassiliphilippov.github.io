Jaish-i-Mohammed

proper noun
militant Islamist group active in Kashmir
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒaɪʃ i məˈhɑːməd / (AmE), / dʒaɪʃ i məˈhɒməd / (BrE)
A militant Islamist group founded in 2000, active mainly in Kashmir, known for terrorist activities and involvement in kidnappings to secure the release of imprisoned militants.

Examples

Jaish-i-Mohammed group
Authorities blamed Jaish-i-Mohammed for several high-profile terrorist attacks in Kashmir.
International sanctions have been imposed on Jaish-i-Mohammed due to its links with extremist activities.
The Indian government designated Jaish-i-Mohammed as a terrorist organization in 2001.
