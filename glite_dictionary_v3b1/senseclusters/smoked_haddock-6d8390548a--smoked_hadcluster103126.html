smoked haddock

noun
haddock preserved by smoking
Pronunciation: / smoʊkt ˈhædək / (AmE), / sməʊkt ˈhædək / (BrE)
Haddock that has been preserved and flavoured by smoking, typically served cooked, often with butter.

Examples

A serving of smoked haddock
For breakfast, she enjoyed a serving of smoked haddock with scrambled eggs.
The recipe calls for smoked haddock, potatoes, and cream to make a traditional chowder.
You can poach smoked haddock in milk for a classic British dish.
