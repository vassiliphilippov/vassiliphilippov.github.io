kula

noun
Pacific exchange system
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkuː.lə/ (AmE), /ˈkuː.lə/ (BrE)
a ceremonial exchange system involving the trading of valuable items among Pacific island communities, especially in the Trobriand Islands

Examples

Kula exchange ceremony
The kula tradition helps build social ties among different island communities.
Anthropologists have long been fascinated by the kula exchange system in the Pacific.
During the kula, participants travel great distances to trade valuable shell necklaces and armbands.
