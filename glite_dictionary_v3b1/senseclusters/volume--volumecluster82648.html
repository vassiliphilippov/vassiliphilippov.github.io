volume

noun
quantity or amount of something
3.9 25/m
Pronunciation: / Ààv…ëljum / (AmE), / Ààv…íljuÀêm / (BrE)
a large amount or quantity of something; the extent or size of something in bulk or magnitude

Examples

High volume of traffic
Due to the high volume of applications, processing times may be longer than usual.
The volume of traffic on the roads increases during holiday weekends.
The company reported a record volume of sales this quarter.

Synonyms

  • bulk
    2.5 5.90/m
    bulk refers to the large physical mass or size of something, while volume means the overall amount or three-dimensional space it occupies.
  • quantity
    1.6 23/m
    quantity means a measurable amount or number of items, while volume focuses on overall size, mass, or the space something fills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bulk - the mass or magnitude of something large
  • synonym: mass - a large amount of something without a definite shape or form
  • synonym: quantity - an amount or number of something
