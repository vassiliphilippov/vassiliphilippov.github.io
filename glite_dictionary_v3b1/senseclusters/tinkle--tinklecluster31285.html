tinkle

noun
light, clear ringing sound
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋkəl / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋkəl / (BrE)
a light, clear, metallic sound, like that produced by a small bell

Examples

A bell tinkle
A faint tinkle echoed down the hallway, catching her attention.
The tinkle of wind chimes was soothing as the breeze picked up.
We heard the tinkle of tiny bells as the cat entered the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chime - a clear ringing sound, often from a bell
  • synonym: clink - a short, sharp ringing sound
