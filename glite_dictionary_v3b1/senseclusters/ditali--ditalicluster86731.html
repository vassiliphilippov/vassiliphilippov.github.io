ditali

noun
sewing finger caps
Pronunciation: / diˈtɑːli / (AmE), / diˈtɑːli / (BrE)
small protective caps worn on the finger while sewing, used to push the needle through fabric; plural of ditale

Examples

Sewing ditali
She searched the sewing kit and found three ditali tucked into a velvet pouch.

Vintage ditali made of silver are popular among sewing enthusiasts and collectors.
