slimeball

noun
lump of slime
Pronunciation: / ˈslaɪmˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈslaɪmˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a small lump or blob of slime or a sticky, viscous substance

Examples

A sticky slimeball
The child made a slimeball out of the green goo and threw it at the wall.
After the rain, a slimeball of mud stuck to the bottom of my shoe.
She found a slimeball under the couch while cleaning the living room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blob - a small drop or lump of a thick liquid or sticky substance
  • synonym: glob - a small, round mass of a thick substance
