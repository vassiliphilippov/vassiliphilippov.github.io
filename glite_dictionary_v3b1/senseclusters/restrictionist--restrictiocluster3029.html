restrictionist

noun
supporter of limits
8.8
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈstrɪk.ʃən.ɪst/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈstrɪk.ʃən.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who supports limiting or restricting something, especially immigration or trade

Examples

A restrictionist politician
The new law was praised by every restrictionist in the parliament.
As a known restrictionist, she argued for tighter controls on imports.
Debates between restrictionists and advocates of open borders are common in politics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: liberalizer - a person who supports removing restrictions, especially in trade or immigration
  • synonym: protectionist - a person who supports protecting domestic industries by restricting imports
