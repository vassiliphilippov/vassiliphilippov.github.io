nitrogenize

verb
treat with nitrogen
7.8 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈnaɪ.trə.dʒə.naɪz/ (AmE), /ˈnaɪ.trə.dʒə.naɪz/ (BrE)
to treat or combine something with nitrogen or a nitrogen compound, typically in a chemical or industrial process

Examples

To nitrogenize the soil
Modern fertilizers often nitrogenize the soil to promote better crop growth.
The researchers nitrogenized the samples before beginning their experiments.
To improve fuel quality, the refinery plans to nitrogenize the hydrocarbon mixture.
Some fertilizers are produced by first nitrogenising basic feedstock chemicals.
The company plans to nitrogenise the tanks before filling them with gas.
To preserve the freshness of coffee beans, manufacturers often nitrogenise the storage containers.
