corrector

noun
cosmetic concealer
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɛktər / (AmE), / kəˈrɛktə / (BrE)
a cosmetic product used to neutralize or conceal skin blemishes or uneven skin tone before applying foundation

Examples

Green corrector makeup
She applied a green corrector before foundation to hide the redness around her nose.
A peach-toned corrector can help cover dark circles under the eyes.
Makeup artists often use a corrector to even out skin tone before adding other products.

  • synonym: foundation - a cosmetic base applied to the face
