intricately

adverb
in a detailed or complex manner
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.trɪ.kət.li / (AmE), / ˈɪn.trɪ.kət.li / (BrE)
In a way that is very detailed or complicated, with many interconnected parts.

Examples

To intricately design patterns
Her dress was intricately embroidered with patterns from her homeland.
The intricately carved wooden doors attracted visitors to the ancient temple.
The movie's plot was intricately woven, requiring viewers to pay close attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: simply - in a simple or plain manner
