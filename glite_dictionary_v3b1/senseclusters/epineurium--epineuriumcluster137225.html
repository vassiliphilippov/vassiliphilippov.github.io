epineurium

noun
nerve outer covering
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈnʊriəm / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈnjʊəriəm / (BrE)
the outer layer of connective tissue that surrounds a peripheral nerve

Examples

The epineurium layer
The epineurium protects the nerve from physical damage during movement.
During surgery, the doctor made an incision through the epineurium to access the nerve.
Inflammation of the epineurium can lead to nerve pain and swelling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endoneurium - the delicate connective tissue around individual nerve fibers
  • synonym: perineurium - the connective tissue surrounding bundles of nerve fibers within a nerve
