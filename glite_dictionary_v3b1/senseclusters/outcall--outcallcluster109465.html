outcall

verb
make a higher bid in a card game
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌkɔl / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌkɔːl / (BrE)
(in card games) to make a higher bid than the previous bid or another player.

Examples

To outcall a bid
He wasn't sure whether to outcall or pass in the final round.
If you want to win, you must outcall your opponents at the right moment.
She managed to outcall everyone at the table and took the lead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outbid - to offer more money or a higher bid than someone else
