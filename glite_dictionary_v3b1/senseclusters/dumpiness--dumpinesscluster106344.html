dumpiness

noun
short and stout physique
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmpi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdʌmpi.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being short and somewhat thick or stout in appearance

Examples

Dumpiness in body
Despite his dumpiness, he moved with surprising agility on the dance floor.
He was sensitive about his dumpiness and often avoided group photos.
Her dumpiness made it difficult for her to find clothes that fit well.

Related Terms

  • antonym: slenderness - the quality of being thin or gracefully slim
  • synonym: stoutness - the quality of being somewhat fat or heavily built
