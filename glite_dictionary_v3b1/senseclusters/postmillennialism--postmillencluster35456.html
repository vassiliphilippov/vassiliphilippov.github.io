postmillennialism

noun
Christian millennium belief
9.9
Pronunciation: /poʊst.mɪ.ˈlen.ɪ.ə.lɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /pəʊst.mɪ.ˈlen.ɪ.ə.lɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the belief that the world will experience a period of peace and goodness before the Second Coming of Christ or the end of the world, often brought about by human effort or gradual improvement

Related Terms

  • synonym: millenarianism - belief in a coming transformation of society, often a thousand-year reign
  • synonym: millennialism - the belief in a coming transformation of society, often involving a thousand-year period of peace
  • antonym: premillennialism - the belief that Christ will return before the millennium
