eyewall

noun
storm ring in cyclone
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌwɔl / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌwɔːl / (BrE)
the ring of intense thunderstorms that surrounds the calm center, or eye, of a tropical cyclone

Examples

The eyewall of hurricane
The eyewall of the hurricane produced extremely strong winds and heavy rain as it passed over the island.
Meteorologists warned that the eyewall would bring the most dangerous conditions to the coastal region.
As the storm intensified, the eyewall became more defined and powerful.
