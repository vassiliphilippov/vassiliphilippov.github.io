Mickey Mouse job

noun
trivial or badly executed task
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪki maʊs dʒɑb / (AmE), / ˈmɪki maʊs dʒɒb / (BrE)
an unimportant, trivial, or badly executed task

Examples

A Mickey Mouse job
He complained that filing those old papers was just a Mickey Mouse job.
My boss gave me another Mickey Mouse job to keep me busy while the real project was delayed.
She left the company because she was tired of doing Mickey Mouse jobs instead of meaningful work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: menial job - a job requiring little skill or prestige
  • synonym: trivial task - a task of little importance or value
