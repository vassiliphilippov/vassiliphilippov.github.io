flabbergasted

adjective
extremely surprised or astonished
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflæb.ɚˌɡæs.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈflæb.əˌɡɑː.stɪd / (BrE)
extremely surprised or shocked; astonished to the point of being speechless.

Examples

To be flabbergasted
She was flabbergasted by the generosity of her friends at her surprise birthday party.
The audience sat flabbergasted as the magician revealed his final trick.
When I checked my exam results, I was completely flabbergasted by my high score.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astonished - very surprised or amazed
  • synonym: stunned - startled or overwhelmed, often with surprise or shock
  • antonym: unimpressed - not feeling admiration or interest
