ninthly

adverb
in the ninth place
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪnθli / (AmE), / ˈnaɪnθli / (BrE)
used to introduce the ninth point or item in a list or sequence.

Examples

Ninthly we note
Ninthly, we must consider the impact on local communities.
The report lists several recommendations; ninthly, it suggests increasing funding for education.
He addressed each concern in order, and ninthly, he discussed the timeline for completion.
