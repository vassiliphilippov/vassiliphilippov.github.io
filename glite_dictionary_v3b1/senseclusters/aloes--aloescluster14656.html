aloes

noun
purgative from aloe leaves
4.2 9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈæloʊz / (AmE), / ˈaləʊz / (BrE)
A bitter purgative substance made from the juice of the leaves of various aloe plants, formerly used as a medicine to stimulate bowel movements.

Examples

Bitter aloes juice
In ancient times, aloes were commonly used as a natural laxative remedy.
She found a small tin labeled aloes among her grandmother's old medicinal herbs.
The doctor prescribed aloes to treat chronic constipation in the patient.
