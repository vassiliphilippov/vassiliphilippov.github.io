decaffeinate

verb
remove caffeine from (a drink)
Pronunciation: / diˈkæfəˌneɪt / (AmE), / diˈkæfɪneɪt / (BrE)
to remove caffeine from coffee, tea, or another drink or substance

Examples

To decaffeinate coffee beans
Many manufacturers decaffeinate coffee beans before roasting them.

This plant can decaffeinate beverages using a natural water process.
The company uses a special process to decaffeinate its coffee beans without chemicals.
Some people prefer to decaffeinate their tea so they can drink it in the evening.
After learning about the effects of caffeine, she decided to decaffeinate her morning routine.
