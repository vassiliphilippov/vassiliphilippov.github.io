United Self-Defense Force of Colombia

proper noun
Colombian paramilitary group (1997–2006)
Pronunciation: / juːˈnaɪtɪd ˈsɛlf dɪˈfɛns ˈfɔrs əv kəˈlʌmbiə / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪtɪd ˈsɛlf dɪˈfɛns ˈfɔːs ɒv kəˈlʌmbiə / (BrE)
a right-wing paramilitary organization in Colombia, formed in 1997 as an alliance of regional groups, involved in armed conflict and drug trafficking
