scent

noun
a trail smell for tracking
Pronunciation: / sɛnt / (AmE), / sɛnt / (BrE)
a smell left by a person or animal that can be followed to track their movement

Examples

Animal scent trail
A wolf can follow the scent of its prey for miles over rough terrain.
Rain can sometimes wash away the scent, making it harder for hunters to track animals.
The police dogs picked up the scent and quickly tracked the suspect through the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: odor - a distinctive smell, especially an unpleasant one
