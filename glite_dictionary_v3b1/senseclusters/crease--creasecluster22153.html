crease

noun
line or mark made by folding
Pronunciation: / kris / (AmE), / kriːs / (BrE)
a line, mark, or fold made on a surface by folding, pressing, or wrinkling (such as on fabric, paper, or skin)

Examples

A crease in trousers
A sharp crease ran down the center of each trouser leg.
He made a crease in the paper to help cut it straight.
She ironed the crease out of her skirt before leaving for work.
A deep crease ran across the old man's forehead.
She ironed her shirt carefully to remove every crease.
Try not to put a crease in the paper while folding it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fold - a line or shape formed by bending one part over another
  • synonym: wrinkle - a small line or fold in something, usually skin or fabric
