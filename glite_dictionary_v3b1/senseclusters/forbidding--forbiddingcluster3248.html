forbidding

adjective
threatening or unfriendly in appearance
Pronunciation: / fərˈbɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / fəˈbɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
unfriendly or threatening in appearance; making people feel danger or unwilling to approach

Examples

A forbidding look
A forbidding silence fell over the abandoned street as night approached.
His forbidding expression made everyone hesitate before speaking.
The castle's forbidding walls rose sharply against the darkening sky.
A forbidding mountain range loomed darkly against the evening sky.
Her forbidding glare silenced the noisy classroom instantly.
The castle's forbidding walls made visitors hesitant to approach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: daunting - seeming difficult to deal with, intimidating
  • antonym: inviting - pleasant and attractive
  • synonym: menacing - threatening or suggesting danger
  • synonym: ominous - suggesting that something bad will happen
  • antonym: welcoming - friendly or inviting in appearance
