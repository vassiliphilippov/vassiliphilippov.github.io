pot

noun
total money or chips bet in a game
6.1
Pronunciation: / pɑt / (AmE), / pɒt / (BrE)
the total amount of money or chips that players bet and compete for in a game such as poker.

Examples

Poker pot
At the end of the round, the winner took the entire pot.
He pushed all his chips into the pot and waited for his opponent's move.
The pot grew quickly as more players made their bets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jackpot - a large prize or the accumulated stakes in a game or contest
