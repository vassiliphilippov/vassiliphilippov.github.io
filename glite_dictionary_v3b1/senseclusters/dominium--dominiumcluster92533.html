dominium

noun
legal ownership right
Pronunciation: / doʊˈmɪniəm / (AmE), / dəˈmɪnɪəm / (BrE)
the right of ownership or control over property, especially as recognized in law

Examples

Dominium of the land
The court recognized his dominium over the disputed land.
Under Roman law, dominium was the highest form of property ownership.
The transfer of dominium requires proper legal documentation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ownership - the state or fact of owning something
  • synonym: possession - the state of having or holding property
  • antonym: tenancy - the right to use or occupy property owned by another
