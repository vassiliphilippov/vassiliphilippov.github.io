grande

adjective
large drink size
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɑːndeɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡrændeɪ / (BrE)
used to describe a large size, especially for drinks in some coffee shops

Examples

Grande coffee size
She ordered a grande latte at the café to start her morning.
If you want more coffee, ask for the grande size instead of the regular.
The menu lists small, grande, and extra-large options for every drink.

Related Terms

  • synonym: large - of greater than average size
  • antonym: small - of less than average size
