breathlessly

adverb
without breath; with excitement or exertion
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛθləsli / (AmE), / ˈbrɛθləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows or causes a lack of breath, typically due to excitement, fear, or exertion.

Examples

Wait breathlessly
He waited breathlessly for the results of the competition.
She ran breathlessly up the stairs, eager to deliver the news.
The audience listened breathlessly to the thrilling conclusion of the story.
