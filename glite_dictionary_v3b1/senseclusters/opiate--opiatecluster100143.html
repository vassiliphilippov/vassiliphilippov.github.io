opiate

verb
to give opiates
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpiˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊpiˌeɪt / (BrE)
to give or administer an opiate drug to someone, especially for pain relief.

Examples

To opiate a patient
The doctor opiated the patient to manage severe post-surgical pain.
Paramedics opiated the accident victim before transporting him to the hospital.
In some cases, nurses have opiated patients to provide comfort during end-of-life care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medicate - to treat with medicine
  • synonym: sedate - to calm or put to sleep with a drug
