chink in the armor

noun
a vulnerability in someone or something strong
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃɪŋk ɪn ði ˈɑrmɚ / (AmE), / tʃɪŋk ɪn ði ˈɑːmə / (BrE)
a weakness or vulnerable point in an otherwise strong person, organization, or system

Examples

A chink in the armor
After years of success, the scandal exposed a chink in the armor of the company.
Her lack of confidence was a chink in the armor that competitors quickly exploited.
The new tax laws revealed a chink in the armor of the financial system.
