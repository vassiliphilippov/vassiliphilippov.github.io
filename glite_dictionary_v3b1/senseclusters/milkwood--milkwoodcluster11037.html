milkwood

noun
tree with milky sap
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkwʊd / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkwʊd / (BrE)
a tree or shrub that produces a milky sap, found in various regions such as South Africa and Australia

Examples

A milkwood tree
The milkwood is protected in some coastal areas due to its ecological importance.
Birds often build their nests in the branches of the milkwood tree.
Hikers admired the thick trunk and glossy leaves of the ancient milkwood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sapwood - the softer, younger outer wood of a tree
