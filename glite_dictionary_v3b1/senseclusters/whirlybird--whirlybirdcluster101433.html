whirlybird

noun
informal term for helicopter
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːliˌbɝːd / (AmE), / ˈwɜːliˌbɜːd / (BrE)
(informal) A helicopter; an aircraft without fixed wings that obtains lift from the rotation of overhead blades.

Examples

A whirlybird helicopter
He took his first ride in a whirlybird during his military training.
Kids watched in awe as a police whirlybird hovered above the crowd.
The whirlybird landed gracefully on the hospital's helipad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: helicopter - an aircraft that is lifted and propelled by one or more horizontal rotors
