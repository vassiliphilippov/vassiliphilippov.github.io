choppy

adjective
having abrupt or uneven transitions
7.6 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɑː.piː/ /; / /ˈtʃɑː.pɪ/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɒ.piː/ /; / /ˈtʃɒ.pɪ/ (BrE)
having abrupt or uneven transitions, often lacking smoothness or flow, especially in writing, speech, or movement.

Examples

Choppy writing
Editing can help turn a choppy draft into a more polished and coherent piece.
His presentation felt choppy, jumping from one topic to another without transitions.
The author's choppy prose made the story difficult to follow.

Related Terms
