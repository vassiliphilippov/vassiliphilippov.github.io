Irving Berlin

proper noun
Russian-born American songwriter and composer
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrvɪŋ bɚˈlɪn / (AmE), / ˈɜːvɪŋ bəˈlɪn / (BrE)
(1888–1989) Russian-born American songwriter known for composing more than 1,500 songs and several celebrated musical comedies.

Examples

Irving Berlin songs
Irving Berlin wrote many of America's most beloved songs, including "White Christmas."
