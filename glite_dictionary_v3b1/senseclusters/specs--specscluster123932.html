specs

noun
informal term for glasses (eyeglasses)
Pronunciation: / spɛks / (AmE), / spɛks / (BrE)
informal term for a pair of glasses (eyeglasses) worn to correct defective vision.

Examples

A pair of specs
Don't forget your specs when you go out, or you won't see a thing!
He reached for his specs before reading the newspaper.
Her new specs have bright red frames and suit her perfectly.
