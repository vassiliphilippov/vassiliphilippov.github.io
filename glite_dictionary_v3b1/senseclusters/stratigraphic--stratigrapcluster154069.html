stratigraphic

adjective
relating to rock layers
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌstrætəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌstrætɪˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the layers of rock, soil, or sediment and their arrangement in the earth

Examples

The stratigraphic record
The stratigraphic record provides important clues about the earth's history.
Scientists use stratigraphic methods to determine the age of fossils.
A stratigraphic map shows the different layers of rock beneath the surface.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geological - relating to the science of the earth's physical structure
