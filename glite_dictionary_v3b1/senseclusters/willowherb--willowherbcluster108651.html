willowherb

noun
plant of the genus Epilobium

Pronunciation: / ˈwɪloʊˌhɜrb / (AmE), / ˈwɪləʊˌhɜːb / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Epilobium, typically having pink or yellow flowers with seeds covered in silky hairs.
