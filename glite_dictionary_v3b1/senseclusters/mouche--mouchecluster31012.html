mouche

noun
artificial beauty spot
Pronunciation: / muːʃ / (AmE), / muːʃ / (BrE)
a small artificial beauty spot worn on the face, especially fashionable in the 18th century

Examples

A mouche on the cheek
In the 18th century, it was fashionable to wear a mouche on one's cheek.
She added a small mouche to complete her historical costume for the play.
The portrait showed a noblewoman with a delicate mouche near her mouth.

Related Terms
