culminant

noun
climax; highest point
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlmɪnənt / (AmE), / ˈkʌlmɪnənt / (BrE)
the climax or highest point of something; the culmination (archaic or rare)

Examples

The culminant of the story
The story reached its culminant with a dramatic revelation.
Historians consider the treaty to be the culminant of the peace negotiations.
The festival's culminant was a spectacular fireworks display over the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: culmination - the highest or climactic point of something
  • antonym: nadir - the lowest point
  • synonym: peak - the highest point or maximum
