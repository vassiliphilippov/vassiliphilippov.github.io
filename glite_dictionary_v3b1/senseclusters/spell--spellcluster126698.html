spell

verb
to place under a magic spell
Pronunciation: / spɛl / (AmE), / spɛl / (BrE)
to put someone or something under the influence of magic; to cast a spell on

Examples

To spell a person
It is said that the witch spelled the harvest to fail.
She claimed her uncle could spell bad luck upon his enemies.
The villain tried to spell the entire kingdom asleep.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bewitch - to affect by witchcraft or magic; to cast a spell on
  • synonym: enchant - to put someone under a magical spell or charm
