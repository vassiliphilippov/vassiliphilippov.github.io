ethnology

noun
study of human cultures and ethnic groups
5.2
Pronunciation: / ɛθˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ɛθˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of the characteristics, origins, and relationships of different human cultures and ethnic groups

Examples

Ethnology study
His research in ethnology took him to remote villages in South America.
She decided to specialize in ethnology to better understand the diversity of world cultures.
The museum's new exhibit is based on years of ethnology conducted in Central Asia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropology - the study of humans, past and present
