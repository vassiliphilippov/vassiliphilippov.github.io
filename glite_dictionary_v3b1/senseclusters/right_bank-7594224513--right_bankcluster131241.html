Right Bank

noun
north bank of the Seine in Paris
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪt ˌbæŋk / (AmE), / ˈraɪt ˌbaŋk / (BrE)
the part of Paris situated on the north bank of the River Seine, known for its cultural, commercial, and historical significance

Examples
