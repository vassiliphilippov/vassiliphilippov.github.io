isethionate

noun
mild surfactant salt
Pronunciation: / aɪˌsiːθiˈoʊneɪt / (AmE), / aɪˌsiːθiˈəʊneɪt / (BrE)
a mild anionic surfactant salt used in soaps and cleansers to help remove dirt and oil from the skin

Examples

Isethionate in soap
Many gentle cleansers use isethionate to avoid irritating sensitive skin.
The shampoo contains isethionate for effective but mild cleaning.
Dermatologists often recommend products with isethionate for people with dry or delicate skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detergent - a cleaning agent that removes dirt and oil
  • synonym: surfactant - a substance that reduces surface tension, helping to clean surfaces
