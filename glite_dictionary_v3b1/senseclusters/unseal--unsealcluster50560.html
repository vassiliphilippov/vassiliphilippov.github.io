unseal

verb
open by removing a seal
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsiːl / (AmE), / ʌnˈsiːl / (BrE)
to open something by breaking or removing its seal

Examples

To unseal a letter
Once the box was unsealed, the contents were revealed to everyone present.
She carefully unsealed the old envelope to avoid tearing the paper.
The lawyer officially unsealed the documents in court.
