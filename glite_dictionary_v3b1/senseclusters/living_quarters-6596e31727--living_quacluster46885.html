living quarters

noun
place where people live
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌkwɔrtərz / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌkwɔːtəz / (BrE)
rooms or buildings where people live, especially as a specific set of accommodations within a larger establishment

Examples

Spacious living quarters
Her new job offered spacious living quarters on the university campus.
The cruise ship provides comfortable living quarters for its crew members.
The soldiers were assigned to cramped living quarters during their deployment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: residence - a place where someone lives
