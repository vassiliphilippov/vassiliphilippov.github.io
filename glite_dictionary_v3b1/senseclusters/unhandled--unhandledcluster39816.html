unhandled

adjective
not touched by people
4.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhændəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈhændəld / (BrE)
not touched, held, or physically dealt with by people, often to prevent damage or contamination

Examples

Unhandled objects
The ancient vase remained unhandled in the museum display case to preserve its condition.
Please leave the documents unhandled until the archivist arrives to examine them.
Food items should be kept unhandled to maintain hygiene before serving.

Related Terms

  • antonym: handled - touched or dealt with by people
  • synonym: untouched - not touched or affected by anything
