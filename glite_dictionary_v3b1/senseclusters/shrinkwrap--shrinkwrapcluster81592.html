shrinkwrap

verb
wrap with heat-shrinking plastic
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrɪŋkˌræp / (AmE), / ˈʃrɪŋkˌræp / (BrE)
to wrap an object tightly in plastic film that shrinks when heated to form a secure covering

Examples

To shrinkwrap products
The company decided to shrinkwrap the manuals with the DVDs as a single unit.
They shrinkwrap each package before shipping to protect it from moisture.
Workers shrinkwrapped pallets of goods before loading them onto the truck.
