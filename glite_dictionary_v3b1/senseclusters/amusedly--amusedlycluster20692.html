amusedly

adverb
with amusement
Pronunciation: / əˈmjuzɪdli / (AmE), / əˈmjuːzɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows you find something funny or entertaining

Examples

Looked amusedly at children
He looked amusedly at the children as they performed their silly dance.
She listened amusedly to his exaggerated story about the weekend.
The teacher watched amusedly as her students tried to solve the tricky puzzle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: playfully - in a fun or lighthearted way
  • antonym: seriously - in a way that shows you are not joking or laughing
