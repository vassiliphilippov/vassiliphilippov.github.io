flea bite

noun
a bite on the skin from a flea
9.7 2.1
Pronunciation: / fliː baɪt / (AmE), / fliː baɪt / (BrE)
A small swelling or irritation of the skin caused by the bite of a flea.

Examples

Itchy flea bite
It's important to clean a flea bite to prevent infection.
She noticed a flea bite on her ankle after walking the dog.
The child kept scratching the flea bite on his arm.
