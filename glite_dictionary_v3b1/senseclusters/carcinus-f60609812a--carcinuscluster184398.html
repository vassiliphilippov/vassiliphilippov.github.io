Carcinus

proper noun
crab genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɑːrˈsaɪnəs / (AmE), / kɑːˈsɪnəs / (BrE)
a genus of crabs that includes the common shore crab, especially the green crab

Examples

The Carcinus crab
The invasive Carcinus species has spread rapidly along the coastline.
Scientists are studying the impact of Carcinus on native marine life.
The green crab, a member of the Carcinus genus, is found in many parts of the world.
