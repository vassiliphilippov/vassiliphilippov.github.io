reproduction

noun
the act of making a copy
3.9 4.30/m
Pronunciation: / Àåripr…ôÀàd åk É…ôn / (AmE), / ÀåriÀêpr…ôÀàd åk É…ôn / (BrE)
the act or process of making a copy of something, especially in art, printing, or technology

Examples

Reproduction of photos
Modern printers allow for quick reproduction of photographs at home.
She requested a reproduction of the historic document for her research.
The museum displayed a reproduction of the famous painting alongside the original.

Synonyms

  • replication
    4.0 1.17/m
    replication makes an exact copy in labs or computers, while reproduction means copying used in art, printing, or technology.
  • duplication
    3.5 2.03/m
    duplication makes an exact copy, often for machines or files, while reproduction refers to copying in art, printing, or technology.
  • copying
    1.0 2.44/m
    copying is the everyday act of making a copy by hand or machine, while reproduction often refers to formal copies in art, printing, or technology.
