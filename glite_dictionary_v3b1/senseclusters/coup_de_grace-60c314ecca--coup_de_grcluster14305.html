coup de grace

noun
final or merciful finishing blow
6.7
Pronunciation: / ku də ˈɡrɑs / (AmE), / kuː də ˈɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a decisive or final blow or action that brings a situation to a close, especially one that ends suffering or finishes someone off, usually mercifully

Examples

The coup de grace blow
After years of declining sales, the recession dealt the coup de grace to the business.
Her resignation was the coup de grace for the struggling company.
The executioner delivered the coup de grace to end the suffering of the wounded soldier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deathblow - a blow that causes death
  • antonym: reprieve - a delay or cancellation of punishment or death
