wind gauge

noun
instrument measuring wind speed and direction
Pronunciation: / wɪnd ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / wɪnd ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the speed and direction of the wind

Examples

A wind gauge device
A portable wind gauge is useful for golfers and sailors alike.
After the storm, the wind gauge showed gusts exceeding 50 miles per hour.
The meteorologist checked the wind gauge before launching the weather balloon.

  • synonym: anemometer - device for measuring wind speed
