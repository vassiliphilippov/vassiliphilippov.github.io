platen

noun
flat plate in a press or printer
9.9 7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈplæ.tən/ (AmE), /ˈplæ.tən/ (BrE)
a flat or rounded part in a machine that presses, holds, or supports something, especially in printing or typing
