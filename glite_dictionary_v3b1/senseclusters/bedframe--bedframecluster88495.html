bedframe

noun
framework supporting a bed
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌfreɪm / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌfreɪm / (BrE)
the structural framework that supports and holds the mattress and sometimes the base of a bed

Examples

A metal bedframe
He bought a new metal bedframe to match his modern decor.
The old wooden bedframe creaked every time I moved.
We assembled the bedframe before putting the mattress on top.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bedstead - the framework of a bed, especially one with a headboard and footboard
