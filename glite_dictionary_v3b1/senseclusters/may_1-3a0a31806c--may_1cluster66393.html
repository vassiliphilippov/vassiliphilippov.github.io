May 1

noun
first day of May, May Day festival or labor holiday
1.0
Pronunciation: / meɪ fɜrst / (AmE), / meɪ fɜːst / (BrE)
The first day of May, traditionally celebrated as May Day in many countries, marked by festivals celebrating spring or, in some countries, as a holiday honoring labor and workers.

Examples

May 1 holiday
May 1 is recognized as International Workers' Day in several nations.
In some cultures, May 1 marks the beginning of summer festivals.
Many countries celebrate May 1 with parades and public holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: May Day - an annual festival or workers' holiday celebrated on May 1
