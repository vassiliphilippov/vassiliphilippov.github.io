self-collected

adjective
collected by the participant/patient
5.1 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ccs5blf.k59c8l5bk.t6ad / (AmE), / ccs5blf.k59c8l5bk.t6ad / (BrE)
(in medicine and research) collected or provided by the participant or patient themself rather than by a clinician or researcher.

Examples

Self-collected samples
For the study, participants provided self-collected saliva samples at home each morning.
The clinic accepted self-collected swabs to reduce contact during the pandemic.
Researchers found that self-collected specimens were as reliable as clinician-collected ones in this trial.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-administered - done or taken by the person themself rather than by a professional
