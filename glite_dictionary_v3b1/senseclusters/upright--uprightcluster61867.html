upright

noun
a vertical piano
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈʌp.raɪt / (BrE)
a piano in which the strings and soundboard are positioned vertically, allowing it to take up less floor space than a grand piano

Examples

Upright piano
She learned to play the upright in her grandmother's living room.
The old upright took up little space against the wall.
They moved the heavy upright into the school auditorium.
