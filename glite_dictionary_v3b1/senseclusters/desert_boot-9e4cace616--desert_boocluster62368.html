desert boot

noun
lightweight ankle-high boot with crepe sole
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛzɚt buːt / (AmE), / ˈdezət buːt / (BrE)
A lightweight ankle-high boot made of suede or leather, typically with a crepe rubber sole, originally designed for desert conditions.

Examples

A pair of desert boots
He wore a pair of classic desert boots with his jeans.
My desert boots are comfortable for walking long distances.
The store sells desert boots in several colors and sizes.
