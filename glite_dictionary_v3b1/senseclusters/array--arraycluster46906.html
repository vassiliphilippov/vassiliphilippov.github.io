array

noun
impressive arrangement or display
Pronunciation: / əˈreɪ / (AmE), / əˈreɪ / (BrE)
an impressive or orderly arrangement or display of things or people

The general reviewed the array of soldiers standing at attention in the courtyard.
The museum showcased an array of ancient artifacts in its main exhibition hall.
