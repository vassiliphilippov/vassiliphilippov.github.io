desirelessness

noun
absence of desire
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪərləsnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪəlɪsnəs / (BrE)
a state of having no desire, especially a lack of sexual desire or interest

Examples

Sexual desirelessness
After the illness, she experienced persistent desirelessness that worried both her and her partner.
Some people seek desirelessness as a way to achieve inner peace and contentment.
The medication's side effects included fatigue and desirelessness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apathy - lack of interest or enthusiasm
  • antonym: libido - sexual desire or drive
