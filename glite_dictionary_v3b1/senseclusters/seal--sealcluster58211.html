seal

noun
fastener for tight closure
3.7 5.32/m
Pronunciation: / siÀêl / (AmE), / siÀêl / (BrE)
a material, device, or coating used to close something tightly or to protect a surface from air, liquid, or moisture

Examples

Jar seal
Make sure the jar's seal is intact before you open it.
The airtight seal keeps food fresh for longer.
The engineer checked the seal on the pipe to ensure there were no leaks.
Apply a seal to the deck to protect it from rain and moisture damage.
Before installing the tiles, a seal should be applied to the surface.
The painter recommended using a waterproof seal on the brick walls.

Synonyms

  • sealing material
    3.4 0.01/m
    sealing material is a substance used to fill joints and stop leaks, while a seal is a device or coating that closes or protects.
  • sealant
    4.2 0.75/m
    sealant is a substance used in joints to block liquids and protect surfaces, while a seal is any device or coating that closes things.
  • sealer
    5.0 0.61/m
    sealer is a coating applied to porous surfaces, often before painting, while a seal is any device or material that closes or protects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gasket - a mechanical seal which fills the space between two surfaces
  • synonym: plug - a device for closing an opening
  • synonym: sealant - a substance used to block the passage of fluids through surfaces
