purchase agreement

noun
legal contract for a purchase
3.6 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ùÀê.t É…ôs …ôÀå…°riÀê.m…ônt / (AmE), / Ààp…úÀê.t É…ôs …ôÀå…°riÀê.m…ônt / (BrE)
a legal contract stating the details and terms under which a purchase will take place, especially for property or goods

Examples

A purchase agreement contract
Before finalizing the deal, both parties signed a purchase agreement outlining the terms.

Synonyms

  • purchase contract
    3.3 0.07/m
    purchase contract is a formal legal document that sets out sale terms between buyer and seller, while purchase agreement often refers to similar deals.
  • sales agreement
    2.8 0.07/m
    sales agreement records the transfer of ownership and agreed price from seller to buyer, while purchase agreement focuses on the purchase terms and conditions.
