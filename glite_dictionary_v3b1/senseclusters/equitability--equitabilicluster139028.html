equitability

noun
fairness; impartiality
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkwɪtəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌɛkwɪtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being fair, impartial, and just to all parties involved

Examples

Fair equitability
The new policy aims to increase equitability in access to education for all students.
Many organizations are working to improve equitability in the workplace.
Voters expect a high level of equitability in the distribution of public resources.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bias - an unfair preference for or against something
  • synonym: fairness - the quality of treating people equally or without bias
  • synonym: impartiality - the state of not favoring one side over another
