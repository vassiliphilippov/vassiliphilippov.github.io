MPC

noun
music production device
Pronunciation: / ɛm pi si / (AmE), / ɛm pi si / (BrE)
a brand of electronic music production device used for creating, sequencing, and sampling digital beats and sounds

Examples

MPC beat machine
The producer made the entire beat on his MPC in just one afternoon.
Many hip-hop artists rely on the MPC for its unique sampling capabilities.
She learned to program complex rhythms using an MPC in her home studio.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drum machine - an electronic instrument that imitates drum sounds
  • synonym: sampler - an electronic device that records and plays back audio samples
