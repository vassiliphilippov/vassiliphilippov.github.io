spice

verb
to add spices or herbs for flavor
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: / spaɪs / (AmE), / spaɪs / (BrE)
to add spices or seasonings to food or drink to give it flavor

Examples

To spice the soup
He spiced the curry generously, making it deliciously aromatic.
She likes to spice her soup with a pinch of cumin.
You can spice the chicken before roasting it for extra flavor.
They spiced the stew with smoked paprika and a pinch of black pepper before dinner.
She spices her tea with cinnamon and cloves every morning.
The chef spiced the chicken to give it a unique and flavorful taste.
