pâtissier

noun
pastry chef
8.3
Pronunciation: / pɑːˈtiːsieɪ / (AmE), / paˈtisjeɪ / (BrE)
a chef who specializes in making pastries, cakes, and desserts

Examples

The patissier chef
The pâtissier spent hours perfecting the chocolate mousse for the dessert menu.
At the bakery, the pâtissier is known for his delicate fruit tarts and éclairs.
She trained as a pâtissier in Paris before opening her own pastry shop.
