recenter

verb
to move to center
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈsɛntər / (AmE), / ˌriːˈsɛntə / (BrE)
to move something back to the center or make it centered again

Examples

To recenter the image
If the image looks uneven, you can recenter it with this tool.
After zooming in, she had to recenter the map to find her location.
The instructor asked the students to recenter their attention on the main topic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: center - to place in or move to the center
  • antonym: off-center - to move away from the center
