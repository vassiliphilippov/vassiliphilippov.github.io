hysterics

noun
fits of uncontrollable emotion
5.2
Pronunciation: / hɪˈstɛrɪks / (AmE), / hɪˈstɛrɪks / (BrE)
a state of uncontrollable emotion, such as extreme laughter, crying, or panic, often regarded as excessive or irrational

Examples

To have hysterics
During the tense moment, she was on the verge of hysterics but managed to stay calm.
His ridiculous joke sent the entire class into hysterics.
She burst into hysterics when she saw the surprise party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hysteria - a state of uncontrollable emotion, especially among groups of people
