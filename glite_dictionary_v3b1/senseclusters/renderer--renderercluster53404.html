renderer

noun
performer or interpreter
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛndərər / (AmE), / ˈrɛndərə / (BrE)
a person who performs or interprets a musical, dramatic, or literary work for an audience

Examples

Poem renderer
The talented renderer captivated the audience with her moving performance of the poem.
As a skilled renderer of Shakespearean roles, he was invited to many theaters.
Critics praised the renderer for bringing new life to the classic novel on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interpreter - someone who explains or presents something in a particular way
  • synonym: performer - a person who entertains an audience
