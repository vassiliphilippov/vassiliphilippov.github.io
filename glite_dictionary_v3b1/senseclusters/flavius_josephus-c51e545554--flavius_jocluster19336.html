Flavius Josephus

proper noun
Jewish historian and leader (37–c. 100)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪviəs dʒoʊˈsiːfəs / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvɪəs dʒəʊˈsiːfəs / (BrE)
(37–c. 100), Jewish historian and military leader who took part in the Jewish revolt against Rome and authored influential works on Jewish history.
