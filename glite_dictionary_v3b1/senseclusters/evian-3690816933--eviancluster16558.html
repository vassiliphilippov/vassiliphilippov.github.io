Evian

proper noun
1938 refugee conference
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪviˌɑn / (AmE), / ˈeɪviːˌɑːn / (BrE)
the international conference held in July 1938 in Évian-les-Bains, France, to discuss the issue of Jewish refugees fleeing Nazi Germany

Examples

Evian Conference 1938
The Evian Conference brought together delegates from over thirty countries in 1938.
Many historians debate the impact of the Evian meeting on refugee policy before World War II.
Few countries agreed to accept more refugees after the Evian discussions concluded.
