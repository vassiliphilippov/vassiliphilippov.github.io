greensward

noun
ground covered with green grass
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrinˌswɑrd / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnswɑːd / (BrE)
an area of ground covered with green grass

Examples

A greensward area
Guests strolled across the lush greensward during the outdoor wedding.
The park was a vast expanse of greensward dotted with wildflowers.
They spread a picnic blanket on the greensward beneath the shade of an old oak tree.
