vegetable hummingbird

noun
tropical tree with edible flowers, Sesbania grandiflora
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒtəbl ˈhʌmɪŋˌbɜrd / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒtəbəl ˈhʌmɪŋbɜːd / (BrE)
a small tropical tree (Sesbania grandiflora) with red or pink flowers, native to Australia and Asia, and often cultivated for its edible flowers and leaves

Examples

The vegetable hummingbird tree
Many Southeast Asian dishes use the blossoms of the vegetable hummingbird as a key ingredient.

The vegetable hummingbird thrives in warm climates with plenty of sunlight and water.
