image breaker

noun
person who destroys religious images
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmɪdʒ ˌbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈɪmɪdʒ ˌbreɪkə / (BrE)
a person who destroys images, especially those used in religious worship; an iconoclast

Examples

An image breaker destroys
As an image breaker, he believed that sacred images distracted from true worship.
During the Reformation, many image breakers removed statues and paintings from churches.
The zeal of the image breakers shocked those who valued religious art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iconoclast - a person who attacks or destroys religious images or established beliefs
