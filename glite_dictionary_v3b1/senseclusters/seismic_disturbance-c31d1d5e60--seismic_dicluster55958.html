Synonyms

  • seismic event
    4.6 0.2/m
    a seismic event is a natural occurrence releasing energy in the Earth's crust, while a seismic disturbance names the resulting shaking or movement.
  • earthquake
    2.2 30/m
    an earthquake is a sudden strong shaking often causing damage, while a seismic disturbance is a broader term for earth movement.
  • seism
    9.5 0.00/m
    a seism is the scientific term for an earthquake or tremor used in reports, while a seismic disturbance sounds more general and plain.
  • quake
    5.1 8/m
    a quake is the informal, short word people use for an earthquake, while a seismic disturbance is more formal and general.
  • shock
    6.0 0.9/m
    a shock is a sudden violent earth movement often linked to an earthquake, while a seismic disturbance covers any sudden ground agitation.
  • tremor
