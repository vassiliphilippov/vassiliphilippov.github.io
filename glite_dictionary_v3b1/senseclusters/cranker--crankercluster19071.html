cranker

noun
person using hand crank
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkræŋ.kər/ (AmE), /ˈkræŋ.kə/ (BrE)
a person who turns a hand crank to start or operate a machine, especially older vehicles or equipment

Examples

Old car cranker
The old car would not start until the cranker gave the handle a strong turn.
In early factories, a cranker was needed to keep the machines running smoothly.
He worked as a cranker on the farm, starting engines every morning.
