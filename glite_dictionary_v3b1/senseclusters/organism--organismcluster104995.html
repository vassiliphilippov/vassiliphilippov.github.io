organism

noun
individual living thing
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrɡəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡənɪzəm / (BrE)
an individual living thing, such as an animal, plant, or single-celled life form

Examples

A living organism
A human is a complex organism made up of billions of cells.
Every organism requires energy to survive and grow.
Scientists discovered a new organism deep in the ocean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creature - a living being, especially an animal
