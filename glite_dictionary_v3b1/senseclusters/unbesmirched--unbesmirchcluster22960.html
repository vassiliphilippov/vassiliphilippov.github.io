unbesmirched

adjective
not stained or tainted
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnbɪˈsmɜːrtʃt / (AmE), / ˌʌnbɪˈsmɜːtʃt / (BrE)
not stained, tainted, or damaged, especially in reputation or character

Examples

An unbesmirched reputation
Despite the rumors, her reputation remained unbesmirched throughout her career.
The judge was known for his unbesmirched integrity and fairness in the courtroom.
He left the company with his record unbesmirched by any scandal or controversy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spotless - completely clean or without fault
  • antonym: tainted - damaged or spoiled, especially in reputation
  • synonym: untarnished - not damaged or spoiled; pure
