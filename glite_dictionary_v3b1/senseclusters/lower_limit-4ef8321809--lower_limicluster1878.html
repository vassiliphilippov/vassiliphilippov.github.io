lower limit

noun
minimum possible amount or value
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊər ˈlɪmɪt /; / ˈloʊ.ɚ ˈlɪm.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈləʊə ˈlɪmɪt /; / ˈləʊ.ə ˈlɪm.ɪt / (BrE)
the smallest possible or allowed amount, value, or boundary that something can reach

Examples
