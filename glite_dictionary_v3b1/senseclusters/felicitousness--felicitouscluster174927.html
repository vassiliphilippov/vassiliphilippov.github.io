felicitousness

noun
quality of being pleasing or appropriate
9.8
Pronunciation: / fəˈlɪsɪtəsnəs / (AmE), / fɪˈlɪsɪtəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being pleasing and suited to the occasion, especially in manner or style of expression

Examples

The felicitousness of expression
Critics praised the felicitousness of his word choices in the novel.
His writing lacks the felicitousness that makes a story truly memorable.
The felicitousness of her speech captivated the entire audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appropriateness - the quality of being suitable or proper in the circumstances
  • antonym: awkwardness - the quality of being clumsy or not appropriate
