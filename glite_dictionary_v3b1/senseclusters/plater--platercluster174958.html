plater

noun
worker who applies metal coating
5.3 7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpleɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈpleɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a skilled worker who coats articles with a thin layer of metal, especially silver or gold, by electroplating or another method.

Examples

Expert plater crafts silverware
After years of experience, she became the head plater at the workshop.
Our company employs a skilled plater to restore antique silverware.
The plater carefully coated the jewelry with a layer of gold.
