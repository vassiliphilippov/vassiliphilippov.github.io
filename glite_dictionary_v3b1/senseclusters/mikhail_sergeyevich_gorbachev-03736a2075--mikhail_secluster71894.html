Mikhail Sergeyevich Gorbachev

proper noun
Soviet leader who ended the Cold War
Pronunciation: / mɪˈkaɪl sərˈɡeɪəvɪtʃ ˈɡɔrbətʃɛf / (AmE), / mɪˈkaɪl sɜːˈɡeɪəvɪtʃ ˈɡɔːbətʃɒf / (BrE)
(1931–2022) Soviet statesman who served as the last leader of the Soviet Union, introducing major political and economic reforms and helping to end the Cold War.

Examples
