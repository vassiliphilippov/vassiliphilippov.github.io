Schopenhauerian

noun
follower of Schopenhauer
Pronunciation: / ˌʃoʊpənˈhaʊəriən / (AmE), / ˌʃəʊpənˈhaʊəriən / (BrE)
a person who follows, supports, or is strongly influenced by the philosophy of Arthur Schopenhauer

Examples

A Schopenhauerian thinker
As a dedicated Schopenhauerian, he often discussed the nature of suffering and desire.
The professor's lectures revealed her as a true Schopenhauerian in her outlook on life.
Many artists in the 19th century were influenced by Schopenhauerian ideas about will and pessimism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pessimist - a person who tends to see the worst aspect of things
  • synonym: philosopher - a person engaged in the study of philosophy
