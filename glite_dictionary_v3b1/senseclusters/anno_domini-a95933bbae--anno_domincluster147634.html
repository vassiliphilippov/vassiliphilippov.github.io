Anno Domini

noun
years after the birth of Christ
8.2 6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈæn.oʊ ˈdɑː.mɪ.niː/ (AmE), /ˈæn.əʊ ˈdɒm.ɪ.niː/ (BrE)
used to indicate that a year comes after the traditional date of the birth of Christ; often abbreviated as AD.

Examples

Anno Domini year
All historical records dated Anno Domini refer to years after Jesus's birth.
The year Anno Domini 1066 is famous for the Norman conquest of England.
This manuscript was written in the eleventh century Anno Domini.
