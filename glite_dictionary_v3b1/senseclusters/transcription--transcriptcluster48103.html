transcription

noun
musical arrangement/adaptation
1.5
Pronunciation: / trænˈskrɪpʃən / (AmE), / trænˈskrɪpʃən / (BrE)
the arrangement and adaptation of a piece of music for a different instrument, voice, or ensemble than originally intended

Examples

Piano transcription
She was praised for her brilliant transcription of the jazz standard.
The orchestra performed a transcription of a famous piano concerto.
This violin piece is actually a transcription of a well-known choral work.
