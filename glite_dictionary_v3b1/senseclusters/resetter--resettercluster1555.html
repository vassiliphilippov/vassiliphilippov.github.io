resetter

noun
jewelry stone placer
Pronunciation: / riˈsɛtər / (AmE), / riˈsɛtə / (BrE)
a person who places or secures a gemstone in a piece of jewelry, especially by moving it into a new setting

Examples

Jewelry resetter
The resetter carefully placed the sapphire into the new gold ring.
After the diamond fell out, she took her necklace to a skilled resetter for repair.
A professional resetter can give old jewelry a fresh and modern look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jeweler - a person who makes, repairs, or sells jewelry
  • synonym: setter - a person who sets gemstones in jewelry
