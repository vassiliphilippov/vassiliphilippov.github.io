roller

noun
Old World bird, tumbles in flight
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə / (BrE)
an Old World bird of the family Coraciidae, related to the kingfishers, known for its acrobatic tumbling or rolling flight.

Examples

A roller bird
A roller perched on the branch, ready to swoop and show off its unique flight patterns.
Birdwatchers traveled far to catch a glimpse of the rare blue-bellied roller in the wild.
The European roller is admired for its vibrant blue feathers and stunning aerial displays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kingfisher - a brightly colored bird related to rollers
