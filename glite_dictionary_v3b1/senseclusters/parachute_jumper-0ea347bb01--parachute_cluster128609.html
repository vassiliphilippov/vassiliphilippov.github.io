parachute jumper

noun
person who jumps from aircraft with a parachute
Pronunciation: / ˈpærəˌʃut ˈdʒʌmpər / (AmE), / ˈpærəʃuːt ˈdʒʌmpə / (BrE)
a person who jumps from an aircraft using a parachute, either for sport or in the course of their work

Examples

A parachute jumper lands
She trained for months to become a certified parachute jumper.
Spectators cheered as the parachute jumper opened her chute high above the stadium.
The parachute jumper landed safely in the field after his thrilling descent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parachutist - a person who descends by parachute
