fecit

x
Latin for 'made by'
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈfeɪkɪt / (BrE)
a Latin word meaning 'made (by)', used on artworks to indicate the creator

Examples

Artist's fecit inscription
The sculpture was inscribed with the artist's name followed by fecit.
On the bottom of the vase, you can see 'Maria fecit' written in small letters.
Many old paintings include the word fecit to show who created them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pinxit - a Latin word meaning 'painted (by)', used on paintings to indicate the artist
