nonvascular

adjective
without blood vessels
7.7
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈvæskjələr / (AmE), / nɒnˈvæskjʊlə / (BrE)
not relating to or involving blood vessels, or lacking vascular tissue or a significant blood supply

Examples

Nonvascular tumor
The tumor was classified as nonvascular because it did not contain any blood vessels.
Some plants are nonvascular, meaning they lack specialized tissues for transporting water and nutrients.
Doctors ruled out a nonvascular cause for the swelling after further tests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: vascular - relating to or containing blood vessels
