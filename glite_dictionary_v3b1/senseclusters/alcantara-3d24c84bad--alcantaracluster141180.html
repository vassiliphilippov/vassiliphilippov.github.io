Alcantara

noun
synthetic suede fabric
Pronunciation: / ælˈkæntərə / (AmE), / ælˈkæntərə / (BrE)
a synthetic suede-like fabric used especially for car interiors and upholstery

Examples

Alcantara car seats
The luxury car featured seats upholstered in Alcantara for a premium touch.
Many sports cars use Alcantara on their steering wheels for better grip and style.
The designer chose Alcantara fabric to give the furniture a modern, elegant look.
