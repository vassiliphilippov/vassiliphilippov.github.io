geodetic

adjective
relating to geodesy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːəˈdɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəˈdɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or determined by geodesy, the scientific measurement and representation of the Earth.

Examples

Accurate geodetic data
Accurate geodetic data is crucial for modern mapping and satellite navigation.
He specializes in geodetic surveys for infrastructure projects.
The team used geodetic instruments to measure subtle shifts in the Earth's crust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geodesic - relating to the shortest path between two points on a curved surface
