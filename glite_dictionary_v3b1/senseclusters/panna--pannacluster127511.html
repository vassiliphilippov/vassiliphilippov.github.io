panna

noun
soccer move through legs
Pronunciation: / ˈpænə / (AmE), / ˈpænə / (BrE)
a soccer move in which a player passes the ball between an opponent's legs, often to get past them

Examples

Soccer panna move
The crowd cheered when he performed a perfect panna against the defender.
She practiced the panna to improve her dribbling skills on the soccer field.
During the match, he managed to slip a quick panna past his opponent.
