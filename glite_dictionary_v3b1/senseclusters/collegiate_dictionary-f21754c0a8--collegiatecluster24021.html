collegiate dictionary

noun

4.1 3.3

A dictionary that is abridged and designed for convenient use, often suitable for students or general reference.

Examples

A collegiate dictionary for students
Most students prefer carrying a collegiate dictionary because of its portable size.
She bought a collegiate dictionary for her English class and found it very helpful.
The collegiate dictionary provides clear definitions without the extra detail found in unabridged versions.
