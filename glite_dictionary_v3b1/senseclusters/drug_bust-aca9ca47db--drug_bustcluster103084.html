drug bust

noun
police seizure of illegal drugs
Pronunciation: / drʌɡ bʌst / (AmE), / drʌɡ bʌst / (BrE)
an occasion when police seize illegal drugs, typically as part of a law enforcement operation

Examples

A drug bust operation
The drug bust led to the confiscation of large quantities of cocaine and heroin.
The police announced a major drug bust in the downtown area last night.
Three people were arrested following a drug bust at an abandoned warehouse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raid - a sudden attack by law enforcement, often to make arrests or seize evidence
