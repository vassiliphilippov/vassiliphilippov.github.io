Egyptological

adjective
about ancient Egypt studies
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌiːdʒɪptəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌiːdʒɪptəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of ancient Egypt, its history, language, or antiquities

Examples

Egyptological study
She attended an Egyptological conference to learn about the latest discoveries in the Valley of the Kings.
The museum's Egyptological collection includes rare artifacts from the Old Kingdom.
He wrote an Egyptological article about the decipherment of ancient hieroglyphs.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: Egyptian - relating to Egypt or its people
  • synonym: archaeological - relating to the study of ancient societies through their remains
