presentist

adjective
judging past by present
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛzəntɪst / (AmE), / ˈprɛzəntɪst / (BrE)
interpreting or judging past events solely by the standards and values of the present time

Examples

Presentist thinking
The article was criticized for its presentist view of medieval society.
Some scholars warn against a presentist interpretation of historical events.
Her analysis was considered too presentist by those who value historical context.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anachronistic - belonging to a period other than that being considered
  • antonym: historicist - interpreting events with attention to historical context
