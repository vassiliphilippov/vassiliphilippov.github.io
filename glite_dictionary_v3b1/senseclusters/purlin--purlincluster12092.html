purlin

noun
roof support beam
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrlɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɜːlɪn / (BrE)
a horizontal beam in a roof structure that supports the rafters

Examples

Roof purlins
The old barn needed new purlins to support the weight of the roof.
During the renovation, the workers replaced several damaged purlins above the rafters.
Steel purlins are often used in modern commercial buildings for added strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beam - a long, sturdy piece of wood or metal used in construction
  • synonym: rafter - a sloping beam that supports a roof
