student lodging

noun
inexpensive supervised accommodation for students
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːdənt ˈlɑːdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstjuːdənt ˈlɒdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
inexpensive supervised accommodation, especially for young people or students on trips, such as cycling tours

Examples

A student lodging place
During their cycling holiday, the group stayed at a student lodging near the lake.
Most student lodgings offer basic amenities and communal kitchens for travelers.
She booked a night in a student lodging recommended by the university during her study tour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hostel - an inexpensive accommodation for travelers, especially young people
