particleboard

noun
engineered wood board
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrtɪkəlˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtɪkəlˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a type of board made by compressing small wood particles and glue into flat sheets

Examples

A particleboard sheet
The new desk was constructed from durable particleboard to keep costs low.
Many affordable cabinets use particleboard instead of solid wood.
Moisture can damage particleboard furniture if it is not properly sealed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chipboard - a board made from compressed wood chips and resin
  • synonym: plywood - a board made from thin layers of wood glued together
