rap session

noun
informal group discussion for sharing feelings
Pronunciation: / ræp ˈsɛʃ.ən / (AmE), / ræp ˈseʃ.ən / (BrE)
an informal meeting or discussion where people openly express and share their feelings, ideas, or experiences, often in a supportive group setting

Examples

Group rap session
After the workshop, everyone stayed for a rap session to discuss their personal experiences.
The school counselor organized a rap session to help students talk about their stress.
We held a weekly rap session so everyone felt comfortable voicing their opinions.
