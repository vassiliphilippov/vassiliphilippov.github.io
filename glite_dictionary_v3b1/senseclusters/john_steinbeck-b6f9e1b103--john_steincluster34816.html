John Steinbeck

proper noun
American novelist (1902–1968)
5.3
Pronunciation: / dʒɑn ˈstaɪnˌbɛk / (AmE), / dʒɒn ˈstaɪnbɛk / (BrE)
(1902–1968) An American writer known for his novels depicting the lives of agricultural workers and the working class, such as 'The Grapes of Wrath' and 'Of Mice and Men'.

Examples

John Steinbeck novels
