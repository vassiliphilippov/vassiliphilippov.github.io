blamelessly

adverb
in a blameless, irreproachable manner
Pronunciation: / ˈbleɪmləsli / (AmE), / ˈbleɪmləsli / (BrE)
In a manner free from blame or fault; irreproachably.

Examples

To live blamelessly
He lived blamelessly, earning the respect of his community.
She conducted herself blamelessly throughout the proceedings.
Their actions were judged blamelessly by the board.

Related Terms
