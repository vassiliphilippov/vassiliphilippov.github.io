dawn on

verb
become clear to someone; realize suddenly
6.2 3.1
Pronunciation: /dɔːn ɑːn/ /; / /dɔːn ən/ /; / /dɑːn ɑːn/ (AmE), /dɔːn ɒn/ /; / /dɔːn ən/ (BrE)
(of an idea) to become clear or apparent to someone; to suddenly realize or understand.

Examples

It dawned on her

It didn't dawn on me until later that I had left my phone at home.
Only after watching the news did it finally dawn on them how serious the situation was.
