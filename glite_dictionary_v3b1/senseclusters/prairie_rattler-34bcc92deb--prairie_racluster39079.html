prairie rattler

noun
a venomous rattlesnake of central North America
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛri ˈræt̬lər / (AmE), / ˈpreəri ˈrætlə / (BrE)
a venomous rattlesnake, widely found between the Mississippi River and the Rocky Mountains, commonly known as the western rattlesnake

Examples

The prairie rattler snake

The prairie rattler is known for its distinctive buzzing warning sound.
Watch out for the prairie rattler while hiking through the grasslands.
