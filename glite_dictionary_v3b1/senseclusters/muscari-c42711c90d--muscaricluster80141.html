Muscari

noun
genus of plants; grape hyacinth
Pronunciation: / mʌˈskɑːri /; / mʌˈskɛəri / (AmE), / mjuːˈskɑːri /; / mʊsˈkɑːri / (BrE)
a genus of small, bulbous perennial plants with dense clusters of usually blue, urn- or bell-shaped flowers, commonly known as grape hyacinths
