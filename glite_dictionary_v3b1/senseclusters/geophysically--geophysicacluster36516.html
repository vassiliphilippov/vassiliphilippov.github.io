geophysically

adverb
relating to Earth's physics
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːoʊˈfɪzɪkli / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəʊˈfɪzɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the physical properties or processes of the Earth

Examples

The area is geophysically unstable
The area is geophysically unstable due to frequent earthquakes and volcanic activity.
Scientists studied the region geophysically to understand its underground water systems.
The island is geophysically unique, with rare rock formations and mineral deposits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: physically - in a manner relating to the body or material things
