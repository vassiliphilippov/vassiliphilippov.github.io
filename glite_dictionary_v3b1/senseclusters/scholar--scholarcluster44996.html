scholar

noun
a person who learns or studies
4.2 1.62/m
Pronunciation: / Ààsk…ëÀêl…ôr / (AmE), / Ààsk…íl…ô / (BrE)
a person who is learning, especially a child taught by a teacher; also, a person who studies a particular subject deeply

Examples

Young scholar in class
As a scholar of languages, she quickly mastered French and Spanish.
Each scholar was eager to demonstrate what they had learned during the year.
The young scholar listened carefully to every word the teacher said.
