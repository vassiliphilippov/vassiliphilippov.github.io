microdisk

noun
tiny optical resonator
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌdɪsk / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌdɪsk / (BrE)
a very small, circular optical component used to confine and control light in photonic devices

Examples

Optical microdisk
The engineer designed a microdisk to improve the efficiency of the optical circuit.
Researchers use a microdisk to trap and manipulate light on a tiny scale.
A single microdisk can serve as a key component in advanced photonic chips.

Related Terms

  • synonym: microring - a small ring-shaped optical resonator
  • synonym: resonator - a device that naturally oscillates at certain frequencies
