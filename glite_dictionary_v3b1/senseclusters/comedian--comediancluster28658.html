comedian

noun
professional performer of comedy
Pronunciation: / kəˈmiːdiən / (AmE), / kəˈmiːdiən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make people laugh, by telling jokes, performing funny acts, or acting in comedies

Examples

Famous comedians perform
Many famous comedians got their start in small comedy clubs.
She dreams of becoming a successful comedian and performing on stage.
The comedian had the audience in stitches with his clever jokes.
After years of acting in dramas, she reinvented herself as a comedian.
Many famous comedians started their careers in small clubs before becoming stars.
The comedian entertained the audience with a brilliant stand-up routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clown - a comic entertainer, especially in a circus
  • synonym: comic - a person who is funny or makes people laugh, often professionally
  • antonym: dramatist - a person who writes plays, contrasting with one who performs
  • antonym: tragedian - An actor who performs in tragedies
