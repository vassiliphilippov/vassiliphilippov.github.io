Masoretic

adjective
relating to the Masorah or Masoretes
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌmæsəˈrɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌmasəˈrɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the Masorah, especially the traditional Hebrew text of the Jewish Bible established by the Masoretes.

Examples

The Masoretic text
Differences between the Masoretic text and the Septuagint are frequently the subject of biblical studies.
Scholars often refer to the Masoretic tradition when discussing the preservation of the Hebrew Bible.
The Masoretic vowel system helped standardize pronunciation in Jewish communities.
