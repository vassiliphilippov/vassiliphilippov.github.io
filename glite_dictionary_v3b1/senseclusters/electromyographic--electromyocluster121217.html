electromyographic

adjective
muscle electrical recording
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˌmaɪəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˌmaɪəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the recording or measurement of electrical activity in muscles

Examples

Electromyographic study
The doctor ordered an electromyographic study to investigate the patient's muscle weakness.
Researchers analyzed the electromyographic signals to understand how the muscles responded to stimulation.
An electromyographic test can help diagnose certain nerve and muscle disorders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electromyogram - a recording of the electrical activity of muscle tissue
  • synonym: neuromuscular - relating to nerves and muscles
