generalized seizure

noun
epileptic seizure with whole-body convulsions

A type of epileptic seizure affecting the entire brain, during which the person becomes unconscious and experiences convulsions throughout the body.

Examples

A generalized seizure attack
Children with epilepsy may be at risk for generalized seizures during sleep.
If a patient experiences a generalized seizure, ensure their airway is clear and move objects away.
The doctor explained that her sudden collapse was caused by a generalized seizure.

Synonyms
