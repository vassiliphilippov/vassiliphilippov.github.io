phreatophyte

noun
groundwater-absorbing plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / friˈætəˌfaɪt / (AmE), / friˈætəˌfaɪt / (BrE)
a plant with deep roots that absorbs water from underground sources, often found in dry regions
