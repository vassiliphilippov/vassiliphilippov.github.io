plessor

noun
medical hammer for examining reflexes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛsər / (AmE), / ˈplɛsə / (BrE)
A small hammer with a rubber head used by doctors to perform percussive examinations, such as testing reflexes or examining the chest.
