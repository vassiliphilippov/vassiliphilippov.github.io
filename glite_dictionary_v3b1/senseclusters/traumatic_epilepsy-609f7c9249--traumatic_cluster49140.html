traumatic epilepsy

noun
epilepsy caused by head injury
1.9 5.5
Pronunciation: /trɔː.ˈmæt.ɪk ˈep.ɪ.lep.siː/ /; / /trə.ˈmæt.ɪk ˈep.ɪ.lep.siː/ (AmE), /trɔː.ˈmæt.ɪk ˈep.ɪ.lep.siː/ /; / /trə.ˈmæt.ɪk ˈep.ɪ.lep.siː/ (BrE)
Epilepsy characterized by convulsive seizures that result from a head injury.

Examples

Traumatic epilepsy after injury
After his accident, he developed traumatic epilepsy that required long-term treatment.
Cases of traumatic epilepsy often emerge months or years after the initial head injury.
Doctors diagnosed the patient with traumatic epilepsy following the severe concussion.
