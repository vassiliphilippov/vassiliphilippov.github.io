beeline

noun
the most direct route
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbiˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈbiːlaɪn / (BrE)
the most direct route between two places or points

Examples

Make a beeline
After the meeting ended, she made a beeline for the coffee machine.
He walked into the store and headed in a beeline for the electronics section.
Tourists often make a beeline to the city's famous landmarks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detour - a longer or indirect route taken to avoid something or visit somewhere along the way
  • synonym: shortcut - a quicker or more direct way than the usual route
