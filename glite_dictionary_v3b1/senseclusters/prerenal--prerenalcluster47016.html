prerenal

adjective
in front of kidney
7.8
Pronunciation: / priˈriːnəl / (AmE), / priˈriːnəl / (BrE)
situated in front of or anterior to a kidney in anatomical position.

Examples

Prerenal mass location
The doctor noted a prerenal mass during the abdominal ultrasound examination.
A prerenal artery supplies blood to the area just in front of the kidney.
Surgical access to the prerenal space is sometimes necessary for certain procedures.
