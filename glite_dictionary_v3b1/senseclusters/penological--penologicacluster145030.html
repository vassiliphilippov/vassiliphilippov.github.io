penological

adjective
prison management
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛnəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpiːnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study or practice of prison management and the punishment of crime

Examples

Penological theories
The new law was based on penological theories about rehabilitation and deterrence.
She published several articles on penological approaches to reducing recidivism.
Modern penological research often focuses on alternatives to incarceration.
