dillweed

noun
herb from dill plant
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪlˌwid / (AmE), / ˈdɪlˌwiːd / (BrE)
the feathery leaves of the dill plant, used as an herb in cooking, either fresh or dried

Examples

Fresh dillweed in salad
She sprinkled fresh dillweed over the salmon before baking it in the oven.
The recipe calls for a teaspoon of dried dillweed to enhance the flavor of the soup.
You can substitute dillweed for parsley if you want a different taste in your salad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dill - an aromatic plant whose leaves and seeds are used as herbs
  • synonym: parsley - a common herb with curly or flat leaves used in cooking
