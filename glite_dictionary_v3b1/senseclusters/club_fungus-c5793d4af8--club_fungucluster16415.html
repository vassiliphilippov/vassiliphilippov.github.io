club fungus

noun
fungus with a club-shaped body
Pronunciation: / klʌb ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / klʌb ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
A fungus with a club-shaped fruiting body, especially a member of the phylum Basidiomycota.

Examples

A club fungus species
Scientists are fascinated by the unique structure of the club fungus.
Some species of club fungus play an important role in breaking down dead plant material.
The club fungus can be found growing on decaying wood in temperate forests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coral fungus - a fungus with branching, coral-like fruiting body
