voluminous

adjective
large in volume or size
8.4
Pronunciation: / vəˈluːmɪnəs /; / vəˈluːmənəs / (AmE), / vəˈluːmɪnəs / (BrE)
very large or full in size, amount, or extent

Examples

Her voluminous hair
Her voluminous hair attracted a lot of attention at the party.
The artist wore a voluminous coat to protect himself from the cold studio.
The library contained voluminous records dating back centuries.
Her voluminous correspondence with friends filled several boxes over the years.
The library contained a voluminous collection of ancient manuscripts.
The senator was known for making voluminous speeches during debates.

  • antonym: compact - closely and neatly packed together; small in size
  • synonym: copious - large in quantity; abundant
  • antonym: scant - barely sufficient or adequate in amount
