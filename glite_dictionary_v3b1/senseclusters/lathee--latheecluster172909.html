lathee

noun
Indian police club or baton
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːti / (AmE), / ˈlɑːti / (BrE)
a heavy stick, often made of bamboo and bound with iron, used by police in India as a club or baton

Examples

A heavy lathee
Each officer carried a bamboo lathee as part of their standard equipment.
He was struck on the back with a lathee by the constable.
The police wielded a lathee to disperse the crowd during the protest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baton - a thin stick used by police or as a symbol of authority
  • synonym: club - a heavy stick used as a weapon
  • synonym: lathi - alternative spelling for the same Indian police club
