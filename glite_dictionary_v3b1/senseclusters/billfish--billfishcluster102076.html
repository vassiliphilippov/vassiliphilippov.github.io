billfish

noun
predatory fish with long jaw and hard scales
7.7 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪl.fɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈbɪl.fɪʃ/ (BrE)
a large, predatory fish with a long, pointed jaw or beak, such as marlin, sailfish, or swordfish
