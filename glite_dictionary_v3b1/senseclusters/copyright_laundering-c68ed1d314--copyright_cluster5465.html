copyright laundering

noun
concealing copyright infringement
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːpɪˌraɪt ˈlɔːndərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒpiraɪt ˈlɔːndərɪŋ / (BrE)
the illegal practice of concealing copyright infringement or disguising pirated content as original
