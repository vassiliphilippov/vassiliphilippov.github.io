slag

verb
to remove impurities
8.2
Pronunciation: /slæɡ/ (AmE), /slæɡ/ (BrE)
to remove slag or the layer of impurities from molten metal during a refining process

Examples

To slag the furnace
The workers spent hours slagging the furnace to ensure the steel was pure.
After slagging the molten metal, the impurities were discarded safely.
Proper slagging is essential in producing high-quality iron.
