upshift

verb
to change to higher gear
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpʃɪft / (AmE), / ˈʌpʃɪft / (BrE)
to change to a higher gear in a vehicle or machine to increase speed or efficiency

Examples

To upshift the car

Drivers should upshift smoothly to avoid damaging the transmission.
He learned to upshift quickly while driving on the highway.

You should upshift when the engine reaches a higher RPM to avoid overworking it.
Learning when to upshift is an important skill for driving a manual transmission vehicle.
