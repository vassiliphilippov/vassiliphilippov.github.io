contemplativeness

noun
deep, thoughtful reflection
9.3
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɛm.plə.tɪv.nəs / (AmE), / kənˈtɛm.plə.tɪv.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being deeply thoughtful or reflective; serious and prolonged consideration or meditation

Examples

Deep contemplativeness
Her contemplativeness was apparent as she gazed out the window, lost in thought.
Moments of contemplativeness help him recharge and gain perspective on decisions.
The poem's contemplativeness invites readers to ponder the deeper meaning of life.
