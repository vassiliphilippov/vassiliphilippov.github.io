on the pill

adjective
taking oral contraceptives
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə pɪl / (AmE), / ɒn ðə pɪl / (BrE)
(of a woman) taking an oral contraceptive medication on a regular basis.

Examples

To be on the pill
After her doctor’s advice, she decided to go on the pill to regulate her cycle.
Many women choose to be on the pill for reasons besides contraception.
She mentioned that she was on the pill during the conversation about family planning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: birth control pill - a tablet taken orally to prevent pregnancy
  • synonym: contraceptive - a device or drug used to prevent pregnancy
