care

verb
to provide care for; look after
Pronunciation: / kɛr / (AmE), / keə / (BrE)
to look after, provide for, or take responsibility for someone or something

Examples

To care for children
Doctors and nurses care for patients in hospitals around the world.
It is important to care for your pets every day.
She cared for her elderly parents when they became ill.
After their parents passed away, he began to care for the family business.
Nurses care for many patients each day in the hospital.
She stayed home to care for her younger brother after school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handle - to deal with or take care of
  • synonym: look after - to take care of someone or something
  • synonym: manage - to be in charge of, run, or look after
  • antonym: neglect - to fail to care for properly
