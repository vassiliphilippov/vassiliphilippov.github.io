pitchfork

verb
lift or move with a pitchfork
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtʃˌfɔrk / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃˌfɔːk / (BrE)
to lift or move (something) using a pitchfork

Examples

To pitchfork hay

The workers pitchforked the compost onto a large pile behind the barn.
With practiced ease, he pitchforks straw into the loft above.
