slushy

noun
icy sweet drink
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌʃi / (AmE), / ˈslʌʃi / (BrE)
a cold, sweet drink made with crushed ice and flavored syrup, often sold at fairs or convenience stores

Examples

A cherry slushy
On a hot summer day, nothing is more refreshing than a cold slushy from the corner store.
The children lined up to buy a cherry slushy after playing in the park.
She spilled her blue raspberry slushy all over her new shirt.
