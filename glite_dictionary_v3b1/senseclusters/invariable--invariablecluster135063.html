invariable

noun
quantity or value that does not change
2.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɛriəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪnˈveəriəbl̩ / (BrE)
a quantity or value that does not change in different circumstances or over time, especially in mathematics or science

Examples

A mathematical invariable
In certain mathematical formulas, the parameter acts as an invariable.
Some natural phenomena are governed by invariables that remain unchanged across conditions.
The speed of light is considered an invariable in physics equations.
