viral pneumonia

noun
pneumonia caused by a virus
3.7 0.03/m

Pneumonia caused by infection with a virus, as opposed to bacteria or fungi, often resulting in inflammation of the lungs.

Examples

Viral pneumonia infection

The patient was diagnosed with viral pneumonia after exhibiting symptoms of cough and high fever.
Unlike bacterial cases, viral pneumonia often does not respond to antibiotics.
