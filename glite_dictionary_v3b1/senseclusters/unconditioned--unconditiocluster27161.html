unconditioned

adjective
not limited by conditions
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.kənˈdɪʃ.ənd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kənˈdɪʃ.ənd / (BrE)
not limited or restricted by conditions; absolute

Examples

Unconditioned loyalty
His loyalty was unconditioned, unaffected by any circumstances or requirements.
Scientists studied the animal's unconditioned responses to various stimuli.
The monk pursued a state of unconditioned happiness, free from all external influences.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conditional - subject to one or more conditions or requirements
  • synonym: unconditional - not subject to any conditions; absolute
