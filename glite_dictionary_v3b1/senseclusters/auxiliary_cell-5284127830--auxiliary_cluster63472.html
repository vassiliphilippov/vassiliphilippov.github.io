auxiliary cell

noun
logistics-focused terrorist group
9.5
Pronunciation: / ɑɡˈzɪl.i.ɛr.i sɛl / (AmE), / ɔːɡˈzɪl.i.ə.ri sɛl / (BrE)
a terrorist group responsible for logistics and support tasks, typically larger and less compartmentalized than operational cells

Examples

An auxiliary cell group
Authorities broke up an auxiliary cell that had been funding the group's activities for years.
The organization relied heavily on an auxiliary cell to transport weapons across the border.
Unlike operational groups, an auxiliary cell typically handles recruitment, finances, and logistical planning.
