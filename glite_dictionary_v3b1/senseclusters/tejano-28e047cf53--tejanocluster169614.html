Tejano

noun
Texas-Mexican music style
8.0
Pronunciation: /tə.ˈhɑː.noʊ/ /; / /teɪ.ˈhɑː.noʊ/ (AmE), /tə.ˈhɑː.nəʊ/ /; / /teɪ.ˈhɑː.nəʊ/ (BrE)
a style of popular music from southern Texas that combines Mexican folk, European polkas, and American influences, often featuring accordion and dance rhythms

Examples

Tejano music band
The lively Tejano band played at the festival, getting everyone on their feet to dance.
She grew up listening to Tejano music at family gatherings in San Antonio.
Accordion melodies are a key feature of traditional Tejano songs.
