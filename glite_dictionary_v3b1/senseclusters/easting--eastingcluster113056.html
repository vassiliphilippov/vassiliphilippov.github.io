easting

noun
eastward movement
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈiːstɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of moving toward the east or an eastward journey or direction

Examples

A ship's easting
The ship's steady easting brought it closer to the sunrise each morning.
After days of easting, the explorers finally reached the mountain range.
Their easting was slowed by strong headwinds over the open plains.

Related Terms

  • antonym: westing - the act or process of moving toward the west
