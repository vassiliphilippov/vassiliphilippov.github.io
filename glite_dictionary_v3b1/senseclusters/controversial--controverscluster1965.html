controversial

adjective
causing public disagreement
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːntrəˈvɜːrʃəl / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃəl / (BrE)
Causing or likely to cause public disagreement or heated debate.

Examples

Controversial topic
Genetic engineering remains a controversial topic among scientists and the public alike.
His latest film was considered too controversial for mainstream audiences.
The politician gave a controversial speech that sparked debate across the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contentious - causing or likely to cause an argument; controversial
  • antonym: uncontroversial - not likely to cause disagreement or controversy
