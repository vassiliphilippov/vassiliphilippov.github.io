patrolman

noun
police officer who patrols an area
Pronunciation: / pəˈtroʊlmən / (AmE), / pəˈtrəʊlmən / (BrE)
a police officer, typically male, who patrols a particular area to maintain law and order

Examples

A patrolman on duty
After hearing a noise in the alley, a patrolman responded quickly to investigate.
The patrolman walked along the main street, checking the storefronts for signs of trouble.
The new patrolman was assigned to cover the downtown district during night shifts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: police officer - a member of a police force
