kagga

noun
Kannada philosophical poem
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːɡə / (AmE), / ˈkɑːɡə / (BrE)
a short, philosophical poem in the Kannada literary tradition, often reflecting on life and morality

Examples

A kagga poem
The teacher shared a famous kagga to inspire the students at the start of the day.
He enjoys reading a kagga every morning to reflect on its meaning.
Many Kannada poets have written at least one kagga in their lifetime.
