soja bean

noun
annual Asian plant, soybean
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊdʒə ˌbin / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdʒə ˌbiːn / (BrE)
An erect bushy annual plant native to Asia, cultivated for its edible seeds rich in oil and protein, commonly known as the soybean.
