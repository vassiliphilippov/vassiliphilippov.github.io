featherhead

noun
foolish person
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛðərˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈfɛðəhɛd / (BrE)
a person who is foolish, scatterbrained, or not serious in their thoughts or actions

Examples

A featherhead person
Don't be such a featherhead—you left your phone at home again.
Her friends called her a featherhead because she always forgot important dates.
He can act like a featherhead sometimes, but he means well.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or lacks good sense
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence or ability
  • synonym: scatterbrain - a person who is forgetful or disorganized
