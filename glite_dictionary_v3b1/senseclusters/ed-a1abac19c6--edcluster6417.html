ED

noun
eating disorder (abbreviation)
Pronunciation: / iː diː / (AmE), / iː diː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for eating disorder: a medical condition involving persistent disturbances in eating behavior and related thoughts and emotions.

Examples

ED diagnosis
Researchers discussed the rise of ED among adolescents in online communities.
She sought treatment after years of struggling with ED and body image issues.
Support groups offer hope and practical strategies for people living with ED.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eating disorder - a range of conditions (for example, anorexia nervosa, bulimia nervosa) characterised by abnormal eating habits and distress about body weight or shape
