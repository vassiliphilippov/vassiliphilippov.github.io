backfire

noun
gases and gunpowder escaping backward from a gun
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈbækˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a loud explosive noise, especially from a gun or engine, caused by fuel or gases igniting unexpectedly
