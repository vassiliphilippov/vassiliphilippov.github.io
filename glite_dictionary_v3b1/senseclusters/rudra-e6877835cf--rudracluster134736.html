Rudra

proper noun
Hindu deity, father of storm gods
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàruÀêdr…ô / (AmE), / ÀàruÀêdr…ô / (BrE)
A deity in Hindu mythology, regarded as the father of the storm gods (Maruts), a controller of nature, and sometimes identified with Shiva.

Examples

Rudra god of storms
In Vedic literature, Rudra is often invoked as a fierce god of storms and the hunt.
Many Hindus consider Rudra to be an early form of the god Shiva.
Prayers were offered to Rudra to appease his destructive power and seek his protection.
