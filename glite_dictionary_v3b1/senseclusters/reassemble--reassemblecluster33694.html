reassemble

verb
put together again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈsɛmbəl / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈsɛmbəl / (BrE)
to put (something) together again after it has been taken apart

Examples

To reassemble the engine
After cleaning all the parts, he began to reassemble the engine.
Once the meeting ended, the group agreed to reassemble next week to continue their discussion.
The technician will reassemble your computer after repairing the damaged components.
