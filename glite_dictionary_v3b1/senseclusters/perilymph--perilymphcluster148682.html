perilymph

noun
fluid in the space of the inner ear
8.2 9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpe.rə.lɪmf/ /; / /ˈpe.rɪ.lɪmf/ (AmE), /ˈpe.rə.lɪmf/ /; / /ˈpe.rɪ.lɪmf/ (BrE)
the clear bodily fluid that occupies the space between the bony labyrinth and the membranous labyrinth in the inner ear

Examples
