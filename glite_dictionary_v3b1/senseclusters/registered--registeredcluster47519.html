registered

adjective
officially listed as qualified
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒɪstɚd /; / ˈrɛdʒ.ɪ.stɚd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstəd /; / ˈrɛdʒ.ɪ.stəd / (BrE)
officially listed or recorded by an authority or organization (such as for a profession, service, or mail)

Examples

Registered nurse
Only registered electricians are allowed to sign off on the safety of this building.
She visited a registered dietitian for advice on her meal plans.
To work as a registered nurse in this country, you must pass specific exams.
He sent the documents by registered mail for extra security.
The company only hires registered nurses to ensure a high standard of care.
You must be a registered user to access premium features.

Related Terms

  • synonym: licensed - having official permission to do something, especially professionally
  • antonym: unregistered - not officially recorded or enlisted
