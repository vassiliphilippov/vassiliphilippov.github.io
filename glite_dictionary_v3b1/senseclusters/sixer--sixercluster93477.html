sixer

noun
cricket shot for six runs
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪksər / (AmE), / ˈsɪksə / (BrE)
a shot in cricket that scores six runs by clearing the boundary without touching the ground

Examples

Hit a sixer
The batsman thrilled the crowd with a powerful sixer over the long-on boundary.
She celebrated after hitting her first sixer in a competitive match.
Every fan cheered when their favorite player smashed a sixer in the final over.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boundary - a shot in cricket that reaches or crosses the boundary
  • degree lower: four - a cricket shot that scores four runs by reaching the boundary after touching the ground
