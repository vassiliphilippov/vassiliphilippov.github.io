supernormal

adjective
exceeding normal or average limits
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈnɔrməl / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈnɔːməl / (BrE)
greater than what is usual, normal, or average; exceeding a typical level or standard.

Examples

Supernormal profits
A supernormal influx of tourists overwhelmed the city's infrastructure.
Her reaction to the stimulus was considered supernormal by the research team.
The company recorded supernormal profits during the economic boom.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abnormal - deviating from what is normal, especially in a negative or strange way
  • synonym: exceptional - unusually good; outstanding or above the norm
  • antonym: subnormal - below normal or average
