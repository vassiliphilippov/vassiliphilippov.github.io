end

noun
leftover piece of cloth
6.4
Pronunciation: / ɛnd / (AmE), / ɛnd / (BrE)
a piece of cloth left over after the main part has been used or sold; a remnant or leftover piece

Examples

A fabric end
She made a patchwork pillow using several ends from the store.
The store sold ends of material for quilt making at lower prices.
The tailor offered a discount on a brightly colored end of fabric.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remnant - a small remaining quantity of something, especially of fabric after the rest has been used or sold
