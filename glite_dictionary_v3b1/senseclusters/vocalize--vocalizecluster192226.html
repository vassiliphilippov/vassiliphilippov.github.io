vocalize

verb
pronounce as a vowel

Pronunciation: /ˈvoʊ.kə.laɪz/ (AmE), /ˈvəʊ.kə.laɪz/ (BrE)
(of a consonant, usually a liquid) to pronounce or articulate as a vowel rather than as a consonant.

Examples
