brood

verb
hang or hover menacingly
5.4
Pronunciation: / brud / (AmE), / bruːd / (BrE)
(of something threatening, dark, or menacing) to hang or hover over; to loom persistently.

Examples

Clouds brood overhead
A sense of unease brooded above the meeting as tensions ran high.
A thick fog brooded over the city, making the morning feel eerie and still.
Storm clouds brooded on the horizon, warning of the coming rain.
