stair-rod

noun
rod for holding a stair carpet
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛr ˌrɑd / (AmE), / ˈsteə ˌrɒd / (BrE)
a thin metal rod placed in the angle between steps to hold a stair carpet in position

Examples

A stair-rod holder
After the carpet was replaced, each stair-rod had to be carefully put back in place.
He tripped over a loose stair-rod while rushing down the steps.
The brass stair-rod gleamed in the light at the foot of the stairs.
