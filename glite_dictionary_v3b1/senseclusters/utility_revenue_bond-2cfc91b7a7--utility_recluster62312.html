utility revenue bond

noun
bond for funding public utility construction
6.5 6.1
Pronunciation: /juː.ˈtɪl.ɪ.tiː ˈrev.ə.nuː bɑːnd/ /; / /juː.ˈtɪl.ə.tiː ˈrev.ən.juː bɑːnd/ /; / /jʊ.ˈtɪl.ɪ.tiː ˈrev.ə.nuː bɑːnd/ (AmE), /juː.ˈtɪl.ɪ.tiː ˈrev.ə.njuː bɒnd/ /; / /jə.ˈtɪl.ɪ.tiː ˈrev.ə.njuː bɒnd/ /; / /jʊ.ˈtɪl.ɪ.tiː ˈrev.ə.njuː bɒnd/ (BrE)
a bond issued by a government or other entity to fund the construction or improvement of public utility services, with repayment made from the revenue generated by those services
