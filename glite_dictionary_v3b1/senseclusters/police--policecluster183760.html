Synonyms

  • police force
    2.0 7/m
    a police force is the organized body of officers who work in an area, while police are the everyday word for that organization.
  • police department
    1.3 8/m
    a police department runs local officers and public safety services in a city, while police are the general name for those officers.
  • law enforcement agency
    3.1 2/m
    a law enforcement agency is a formal body that investigates and enforces laws at many levels, while police are officers who patrol local communities.
  • constabulary
    8.1 0.3/m
    a constabulary is a traditional British name for a local policing body, while police are the general modern word for that organization.
