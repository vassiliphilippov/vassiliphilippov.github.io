Phalangida

noun
order of arachnids (harvestmen)
Pronunciation: / fəˈlæŋɪdə / (AmE), / fəˈlæŋɪdə / (BrE)
A taxonomic order of arachnids commonly known as harvestmen, characterized by a compact body and long legs.
