recirculate

verb
to circulate again
4.7 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåriÀêÀàs…úÀêrkj…ôÀåle…™t / (AmE), / ÀåriÀêÀàs…úÀêkj äle…™t / (BrE)
to send or distribute something again, especially information, documents, or money, among people or places

Examples

To recirculate a memo
After the correction, she asked the secretary to recirculate the memo to all department heads.
The engineer needed to recirculate the coolant to prevent the machine from overheating.
Please recirculate the updated schedule to everyone on the team as soon as possible.
