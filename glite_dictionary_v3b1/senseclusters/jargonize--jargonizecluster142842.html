jargonize

verb
to use technical language
8.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd í…ëÀêr…°…ôÀåna…™z / (AmE), / Ààd í…ëÀê…°…ôna…™z / (BrE)
to fill with or use technical or specialized language, making something difficult for outsiders to understand

Examples

To jargonize language
The manual was so jargonized that new employees struggled to follow the instructions.
Try not to jargonize your presentation if you want everyone to understand your ideas.
The professor tended to jargonize his lectures, making them challenging for non-specialists.
