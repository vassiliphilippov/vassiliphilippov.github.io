dining room

noun
room for eating meals
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪnɪŋ ˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnɪŋ ˌruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house or building where people eat meals, especially dinner

Examples

The dining room
She set the table in the dining room before the guests arrived.
The house has a large dining room that overlooks the garden.
We gathered in the dining room to celebrate her birthday with a festive meal.
