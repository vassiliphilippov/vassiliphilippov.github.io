pregame

noun
preparation before event
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpriˌɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌɡeɪm / (BrE)
a period or session of preparation or warm-up before a sports match or performance

Examples

Pregame meeting
The coach gathered the team for a quick pregame meeting to discuss their strategy.
Fans arrived early to watch the players go through their pregame routines on the field.
A good pregame helps athletes focus and get ready for the competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postgame - the period or activities following a sports match or performance
  • synonym: warm-up - light exercise or activity to prepare for a sport or performance
