insane

adjective
slang: extremely impressive
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈseɪn / (AmE), / ɪnˈseɪn / (BrE)
Informal slang: extremely impressive, excellent, or remarkable; used as a strong positive intensifier.

Examples

An insane party
That concert was insane, the best show I've ever seen.
Her guitar solo was absolutely insane and left the crowd cheering loudly.
He pulled off an insane trick during the competition that no one expected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amazing - causing great surprise or wonder; remarkable
  • synonym: incredible - so extraordinary as to seem impossible; very good or impressive
