(in digital platforms and virtual economies) the act of withdrawing or abolishing a virtual currency's or token's status as money within a platform so it can no longer be used as a medium of exchange or store of value there.

Examples

Game demonetization event
The game's recent demonetisation of gold coins forced players to buy items with real money.
Community outcry followed the platform's demonetisation of the token, which wiped out user balances overnight.
After the update, the developer announced demonetisation that removed the auction house currency from circulation.
