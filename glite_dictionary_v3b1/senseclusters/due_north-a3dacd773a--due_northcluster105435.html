due north

noun
cardinal direction; exactly north
Pronunciation: / du nuɚθ / (AmE), / djuː nɔːθ / (BrE)
the direction on a compass that points exactly to the geographic North Pole; the cardinal point at 0 or 360 degrees.

Examples

Due north direction
From this point, walk due north for two miles to reach the cabin.
The hikers set their compass to due north before starting their journey.
The ship sailed due north to avoid the approaching storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: north - the cardinal compass point that is at 0 or 360 degrees
  • antonym: south - the cardinal compass point opposite north; at 180 degrees
