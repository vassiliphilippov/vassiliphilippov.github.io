debye

noun
unit of dipole moment
9.9
Pronunciation: /də.ˈbaɪ/ /; / /ˈdeɪ.biː/ (AmE), /də.ˈbaɪ/ /; / /ˈdeɪ.biː/ (BrE)
a unit used to measure the electric dipole moment of molecules

Examples

One debye unit
The water molecule has a dipole moment of about 1.85 debye.
Scientists often express molecular dipole moments in debye for convenience.
A value of 0 debye means the molecule has no dipole moment.
