Synonyms

  • stretch out
    3.4 1.0/m
    stretch out means to extend your arm or body to touch or grab something, while reach also covers metaphorical contact.
  • extend
    2.7 12/m
    extend means to stretch something longer or reach outward with an object, while reach focuses on touching or making contact.
  • extend to
    3.4 3/m
    extend to describes how something continues as far as a point or limit, while reach often means to touch or attain.
  • stretch
    1.0 13/m
    stretch means to pull or make something longer by force, while reach means to extend to touch or make contact.
