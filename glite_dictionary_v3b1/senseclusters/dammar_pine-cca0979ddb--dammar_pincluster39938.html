dammar pine

noun
tree of the genus Agathis yielding dammar resin
Pronunciation: / ˈdæmɑr paɪn / (AmE), / ˈdamɑː paɪn / (BrE)
any of several trees of the genus Agathis that produce dammar resin, used in varnishes and other products

Examples

dammar pine trees
Local industries rely on the resin extracted from dammar pine for making adhesives.
The dammar pine is native to Southeast Asia and valued for its clear, hard resin.
The forest was dominated by tall dammar pines whose resin was collected for varnish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dammar - the resin obtained from dammar pine
  • synonym: kauri pine - another Agathis tree yielding resin
