biologically

adverb
in terms of biology
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.əˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to biology or living organisms.

Examples

To be biologically related
Humans and chimpanzees are biologically similar in many ways.
The food is biologically grown without synthetic chemicals.
These twins are biologically identical, but their personalities are different.
