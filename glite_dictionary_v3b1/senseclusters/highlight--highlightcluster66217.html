highlight

noun
bright area in a picture or image
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.laɪt / (BrE)
a bright or light area on a picture or image, especially where light is reflected or directly shines

Examples

A bright highlight
In this photograph, the sun creates a strong highlight on the water's surface.
She used white paint to emphasize the highlight on the cheekbone.
The artist added a highlight on the apple to make it look shiny.
