unuseful

adjective
not usable
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈjuːsfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈjuːsfəl / (BrE)
not useful or not able to be used

Examples

An unuseful tool
The broken chair was completely unuseful after the leg snapped off.
Many of the old tools in the shed are now unuseful because they are rusted.
If the instructions are unclear, they become unuseful to anyone trying to assemble the product.
The instructions were so unclear that they were completely unuseful to me.
He found the old map unuseful because the roads had changed.
Most of the advice given in the meeting was unuseful for solving the actual problem.

Related Terms

  • antonym: useful - able to be used for a practical purpose
  • synonym: useless - not useful or effective
