biotype

noun
group of organisms with the same genotype
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪ.oʊ.taɪp / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ.əʊ.taɪp / (BrE)
A group of organisms having the same genotype, or the specific genetic characteristics they share.

Examples

A biotype group
Each biotype may react differently to environmental changes.
Some plants can resist the pest because they belong to a different biotype.
The researchers identified a new biotype of the malaria parasite in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genotype - the genetic constitution of an organism
