detransitivize

verb
make or become intransitive (linguistics)
8.6
Pronunciation: / diːˈtrænzɪˌtɪvaɪz / (AmE), / diːˈtrænsɪˌtɪvaɪz /; / diːˈtrænzɪvʌɪz / (BrE)
(Linguistics) To make a verb intransitive, or to become intransitive, usually by removing its direct object.
