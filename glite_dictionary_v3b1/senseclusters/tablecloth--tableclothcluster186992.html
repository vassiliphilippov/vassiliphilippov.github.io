tablecloth

noun
cloth covering for a table
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪbəlˌklɔθ / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəlˌklɒθ / (BrE)
A cloth used to cover a dining table, especially for a meal.

Examples

A tablecloth on table
Please remove the tablecloth after the meal is finished.
She spread a clean tablecloth over the table before dinner.
The white tablecloth was decorated with embroidered flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: table cover - a covering for a table, often synonym for tablecloth
