garrulously

adverb
in a talkative manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡær.ə.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈɡær.jʊ.ləs.li / (BrE)
in a talkative or excessively chatty manner

Examples

To speak garrulously
She spoke garrulously about her day, barely stopping to take a breath.
The old man garrulously recounted stories from his youth to anyone who would listen.
They laughed garrulously during dinner, enjoying each other's company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: garrulous - excessively talkative, especially on trivial matters (adjective)
  • synonym: talkatively - in a manner of talking a lot; chattily
