square

adjective
having four equal sides and right angles
1.1
Pronunciation: / skwɛr / (AmE), / skwɛə / (BrE)
having four equal sides and four right angles; forming a right angle.

Examples

Square corners
He drew a square corner to mark the edge.
Place the square tile in the center of the mosaic.
The sheet of paper has a perfectly square shape.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: cubic - having the shape or properties of a cube (three-dimensional square)
  • degree lower: rectangular - having four right angles but not necessarily equal sides
