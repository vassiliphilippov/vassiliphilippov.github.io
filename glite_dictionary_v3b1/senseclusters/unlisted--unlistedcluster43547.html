unlisted

adjective
not included in a published list
3.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɪstɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪstɪd / (BrE)
not included in a public or official list or directory (such as a phone book or registration list)

Examples

Several residents were unable to vote because they were unlisted on the electoral roll.
The committee could not find his name because he was an unlisted voter.
