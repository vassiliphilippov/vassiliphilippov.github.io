queer

adjective
strange; unusual; odd (dated)
3.1
Pronunciation: / kwɪr / (AmE), / kwɪə / (BrE)
strange or unusual; deviating from what is expected or normal (now dated or potentially offensive)

Examples

A queer accent
His queer accent made it hard to guess where he was from.
She gave me a queer look when I mentioned the story.
There was something queer about the way the lights flickered in the old house.
