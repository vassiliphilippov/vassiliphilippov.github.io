unbuttered

adjective
without butter
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbʌtərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbʌtəd / (BrE)
not having butter spread or added to it

Examples

Unbuttered popcorn
She prefers her popcorn unbuttered for a lighter snack.
The waiter brought an unbuttered baked potato as requested.
Some people enjoy eating unbuttered toast with jam in the morning.
