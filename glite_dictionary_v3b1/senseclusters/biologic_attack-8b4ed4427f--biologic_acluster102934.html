biologic attack

noun
attack using biological agents
3.0 6.2
Pronunciation: /baɪ.ə.ˈlɑː.dʒɪk ə.ˈtæk/ (AmE), /baɪ.ə.ˈlɒdʒ.ɪk ə.ˈtæk/ (BrE)
