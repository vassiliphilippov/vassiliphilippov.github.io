veau

noun
young cow or calf
Pronunciation: / voʊ / (AmE), / vəʊ / (BrE)
a young bovine animal, especially one that is still dependent on its mother; a calf

Examples

A young veau
The chef prepared a dish using tender veau for the special menu.
At the farm, visitors could feed the newborn veau with a bottle.
French cuisine often features veau in classic recipes like blanquette de veau.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adult cow - a fully grown female bovine animal
  • synonym: calf - a young bovine animal, especially a young cow or bull
