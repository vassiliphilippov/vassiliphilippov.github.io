(Anatomy) a long, narrow slit or groove that divides an organ, such as the brain or liver, into lobes or parts.

Examples

Brain fissure
A fissure in the liver divides it into distinct lobes.
During surgery, the doctor identified the fissure running along the surface of the lung.
The central fissure separates the brain into left and right hemispheres.
