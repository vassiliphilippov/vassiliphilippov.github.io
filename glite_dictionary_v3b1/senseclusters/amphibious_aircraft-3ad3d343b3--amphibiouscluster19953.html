amphibious aircraft

noun
airplane capable of landing on water and land
5.2
Pronunciation: / æmˈfɪbiəs ˈɛrˌkræft / (AmE), / amˈfɪbiəs ˈeəˌkrɑːft / (BrE)
An airplane designed to take off and land on both water and land.

Examples

An amphibious aircraft lands
As an aviation enthusiast, he dreams of piloting his own amphibious aircraft across the islands.
Because the lake had no runway, only an amphibious aircraft could reach the remote research station.
The navy deployed an amphibious aircraft to conduct search and rescue missions after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seaplane - an aircraft designed to take off and land on water only
