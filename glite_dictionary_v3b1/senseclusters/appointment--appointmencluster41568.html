appointment

noun
arranged meeting
1.2
Pronunciation: / əˈpɔɪntmənt / (AmE), / əˈpɔɪntmənt / (BrE)
an arrangement to meet someone at a particular time and place, especially for a formal reason

Examples

Doctor appointment
I have a dentist appointment at 3 o'clock this afternoon.
Please arrive ten minutes early for your appointment with the manager.
She called the doctor's office to schedule an appointment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cancellation - the action of cancelling something that was arranged
  • synonym: meeting - an assembly of people, especially for a formal purpose
