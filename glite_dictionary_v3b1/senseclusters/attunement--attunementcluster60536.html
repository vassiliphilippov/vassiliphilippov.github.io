attunement

noun
state of harmony
Pronunciation: / əˈtuːnmənt / (AmE), / əˈtjuːnmənt / (BrE)
a state of harmony, emotional connection, or close understanding with someone or something

Examples

Emotional attunement
Through careful listening, the therapist developed a deep attunement to her client's emotions.
Musicians rely on attunement with each other to create a seamless performance.
Practicing mindfulness can improve your attunement to your own thoughts and feelings.
The therapist encouraged attunement between parents and children to strengthen their relationship.
Musicians rely on attunement to play together smoothly and respond to each other's cues.
Effective leaders show attunement to the emotions and needs of their team members.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disconnect - a lack of connection or understanding
  • antonym: discord - lack of agreement or harmony
  • synonym: harmony - a pleasing arrangement or agreement
  • synonym: rapport - a close and harmonious relationship
