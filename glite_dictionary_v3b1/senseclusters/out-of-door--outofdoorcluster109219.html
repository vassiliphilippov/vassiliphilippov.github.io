out-of-door

adjective
in or suited for the open air
Pronunciation: / ˌaʊt əv ˈdɔr / (AmE), / ˌaʊt əv ˈdɔː / (BrE)
Situated, suitable for, or happening in the open air rather than indoors.

Examples

Out-of-door activities
He preferred out-of-door activities like hiking and birdwatching to spending time indoors.
Special out-of-door clothing is needed for camping in cold weather.
The wedding reception was held in a beautiful out-of-door setting surrounded by gardens.
