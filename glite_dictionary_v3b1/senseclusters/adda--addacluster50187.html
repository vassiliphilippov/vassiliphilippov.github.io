adda

noun
social meeting place
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːdə / (AmE), / ˈʌdə / (BrE)
a place where people regularly gather to talk, relax, or socialize, especially in South Asian contexts

Examples

Neighborhood adda
Every evening, the friends met at their favorite adda to share stories and laughter.
The old café became a popular adda for students to discuss ideas and relax after class.
He missed the lively adda sessions with his childhood friends after moving to another city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hangout - a place where people spend time together informally
  • synonym: meeting place - a location where people gather for a purpose
