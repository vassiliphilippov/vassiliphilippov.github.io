pengo

noun
former Hungarian currency unit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛŋˌɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɛŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Hungary from 1927 until it was replaced by the forint in 1946

Examples

The pengo currency
During the hyperinflation of 1946, people carried bags of pengos to buy groceries.
He found a collection of old pengo notes in his grandfather's attic.
The pengo was replaced by the forint after World War II.

Related Terms
