impresario

noun
organizer of public entertainments
7.6
Pronunciation: / ɪm.prəˈsɑːr.i.oʊ / (AmE), / ɪm.prəˈsɑː.ri.əʊ / (BrE)
a person who organizes and often finances public entertainments, especially concerts, plays, or operas

Examples

The impresario organizes shows
As an impresario, she brought new energy to the city’s theater scene.
The impresario secured a world-famous violinist for the opening night of the concert series.
The ballet would not have happened without the vision and commitment of its impresario.

  • synonym: organizer - person who arranges events or activities
