tease apart

verb
disentangle fibers or separate elements
Pronunciation: / tiz əˈpɑrt / (AmE), / tiːz əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
to disentangle and separate (fibers or similar elements), especially by raising and loosening them; to carefully separate the components of something, often using a tool or by hand.

Examples

To tease apart fibers
Scientists carefully teased apart the tissue samples to analyze the cells under a microscope.
She used a fine comb to tease apart the wool fibers before spinning.
The old man patiently teased apart the knotted threads with a needle.

  • synonym: untangle - to free from tangles or complications
