ranting

noun
loud, emotional tirade or speech
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈræntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɑːntɪŋ / (BrE)
loud, forceful speech or expression marked by strong emotion and little reasoned argument.

Examples

Loud ranting speech
Despite her ranting, nobody at the meeting took her arguments seriously.
His ranting about government corruption grew louder as the evening went on.
The mayor's ranting during the press conference shocked many viewers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harangue - a lengthy and aggressive speech
  • synonym: tirade - a long, angry speech of criticism or accusation
