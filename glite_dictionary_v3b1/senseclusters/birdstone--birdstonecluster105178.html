birdstone

noun
bird-shaped artifact
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːdstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdstəʊn / (BrE)
a small, prehistoric Native American stone artifact carved in the shape of a bird, often used for ceremonial or decorative purposes
