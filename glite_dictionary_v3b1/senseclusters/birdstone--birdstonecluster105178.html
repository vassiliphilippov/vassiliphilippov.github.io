birdstone

noun
bird-shaped artifact
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːdstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdstəʊn / (BrE)
a small, prehistoric Native American stone artifact carved in the shape of a bird, often used for ceremonial or decorative purposes

Examples

A birdstone artifact
The museum displayed a beautifully carved birdstone from the ancient Hopewell culture.
Archaeologists were excited to find a rare birdstone during the excavation of the burial mound.
Collectors value each birdstone for its unique design and historical significance.
