chamise

noun
evergreen shrub
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈmiz / (AmE), / ʃəˈmiːz / (BrE)
a tough, evergreen shrub native to western North America, especially common in chaparral regions

Examples

Dense chamise shrub
The hillside was covered with dense chamise and other chaparral plants.
Firefighters monitored the dry chamise because it can burn quickly during wildfires.
Many animals use chamise as shelter in the California foothills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chaparral - dense vegetation of shrubs and small trees found in dry areas
  • synonym: manzanita - a type of evergreen shrub found in western North America
