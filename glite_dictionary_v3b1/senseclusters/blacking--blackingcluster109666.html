blacking

noun
shoe-polishing substance
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈblækɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblækɪŋ / (BrE)
A substance, typically a paste or liquid, used to polish and produce a shiny protective coating on shoes or boots.

Examples

Blacking for shoes
He applied a thick layer of blacking to his boots before the inspection.
In Victorian times, children often worked in blacking factories.
She polished her shoes with blacking every morning to keep them shining.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shoe polish - a cream or liquid used to polish shoes
