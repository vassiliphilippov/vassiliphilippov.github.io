come in

verb
to be received (news, messages, etc.)
2.4 31/m
Pronunciation: / kÊŒm Éªn / (AmE), / kÊŒm Éªn / (BrE)
to arrive, be received, or become available (such as news, information, or something starting to be relevant)

Examples
