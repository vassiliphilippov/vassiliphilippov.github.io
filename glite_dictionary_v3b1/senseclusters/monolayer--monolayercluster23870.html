monolayer

noun
single layer of particles
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnəˌleɪər / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəˌleɪə / (BrE)
a single, closely packed layer of atoms, molecules, or cells arranged on a surface

Examples

A monolayer of cells
The scientists observed a monolayer of graphene under the microscope.
A monolayer of cells was used to test the effects of the new drug.
The surface was coated with a monolayer to prevent corrosion.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bilayer - a structure consisting of two layers of atoms, molecules, or cells
  • synonym: film - a thin layer or coating of material
