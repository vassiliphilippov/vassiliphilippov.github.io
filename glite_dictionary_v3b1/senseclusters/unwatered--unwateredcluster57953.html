unwatered

adjective
dry or anhydrous
4.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɔtɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɔːtəd / (BrE)
not given water; dry or lacking moisture because no water was supplied

Examples

Unwatered plants
The plants in the corner remained unwatered for several days and began to wilt.
The chemist stored the compound in its unwatered form to prevent unwanted reactions.
After the drought, many fields were left unwatered and barren.
The unwatered lawn turned brown after weeks of drought.
She noticed the unwatered flowers drooping in the midday sun.
If left unwatered for too long, the houseplants will not survive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anhydrous - containing no water
  • synonym: dry - not wet or containing no moisture
  • antonym: watered - supplied with or containing water
