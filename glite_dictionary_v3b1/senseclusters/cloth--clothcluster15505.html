cloth

noun
woven or knitted material; fabric
Pronunciation: / klɔθ / (AmE), / klɒθ / (BrE)
A material made by weaving, knitting, or felting fibers, used especially for making clothes and other items.

Examples

A clean cloth
After dinner, he wiped the table with a clean cloth.
She bought a piece of cloth to sew a costume for the play.
The artisan dyed the cloth a vibrant shade of blue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fabric - material made by weaving fibers together
  • synonym: material - substance used to make things, especially textiles
