3D

noun
three-dimensional movie presentation
Pronunciation: / ˌθriː diː / (AmE), / ˌθriː ˈdiː / (BrE)
a movie or visual presentation that gives the appearance of three dimensions to enhance realism

Examples

A 3D movie
Some people prefer traditional movies to watching them in 3D.
Tickets for the 3D screening sold out quickly.
We saw the new superhero film in 3D and the effects were amazing.
