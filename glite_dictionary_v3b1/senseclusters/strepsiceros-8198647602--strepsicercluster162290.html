Strepsiceros

noun
former genus of African antelopes
Pronunciation: / strepˈsɪsɪˌroʊs /; / ˌstrep.sɪˈsɪr.əs / (AmE), / strepˈsɪsɪˌrəʊs /; / ˌstrep.sɪˈsɪə.rəs / (BrE)
a former or historical genus of African antelopes that includes species such as kudu, bongo, nyala, and bushbuck
