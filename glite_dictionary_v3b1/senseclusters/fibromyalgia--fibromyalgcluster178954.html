fibromyalgia

noun
chronic pain disorder
7.9
Pronunciation: /faɪ.broʊ.maɪ.ˈæl.dʒə/ (AmE), /faɪ.brəʊ.maɪ.ˈæl.dʒə/ (BrE)
a medical condition characterized by widespread pain, fatigue, and tenderness in the muscles and soft tissues

Examples

Fibromyalgia symptoms
After years of unexplained symptoms, she was finally diagnosed with fibromyalgia.
People with fibromyalgia often experience both muscle pain and extreme tiredness.
Managing fibromyalgia can require a combination of medication, exercise, and stress reduction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arthritis - inflammation of the joints causing pain and stiffness
  • synonym: chronic fatigue syndrome - a disorder causing extreme tiredness and other symptoms
