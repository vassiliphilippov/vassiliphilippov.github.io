Manteodea

noun
order of insects containing mantises
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmæntiˈoʊdiə / (AmE), / ˌmantiˈəʊdiə / (BrE)
An order of insects comprising the mantises, previously considered a suborder of Orthoptera in older classifications.

Insect taxonomy has changed over time, but Manteodea remains a distinct order.
