Rupicapra rupicapra

noun

Pronunciation: / ˌruːpɪˈkæprə ˌruːpɪˈkæprə / (AmE), / ˌruːpɪˈkæprə ˌruːpɪˈkæprə / (BrE)
A species of goat-antelope, commonly known as the chamois, native to mountainous areas of Europe and western Asia, characterized by upright horns with backward-hooked tips.
