ungettable

adjective
impossible to obtain
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡɛtəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡɛtəbəl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to obtain or acquire

Examples

Ungettable tickets
Tickets for the concert became ungettable within minutes of going on sale.
For many collectors, the original edition is simply ungettable now.
Some luxury watches are so popular they are practically ungettable at retail price.

Related Terms

  • antonym: available - able to be obtained or used
  • degree lower: rare - not common or hard to find
  • synonym: unattainable - impossible to achieve or reach
