charred pancake cup

noun
a rare cup-shaped fungus (Sarcosoma globosum)
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃɑrd ˈpæn.keɪk kʌp / (AmE), / tʃɑːd ˈpæn.keɪk kʌp / (BrE)
A rare species of fungus (Sarcosoma globosum) in the family Sarcosomataceae, distinguished by its black, cup-shaped fruiting body that resembles a charred pancake.

Examples

The charred pancake cup fungus
