dictator

noun
ruler with absolute power
2.1
Pronunciation: / dɪkˈteɪtər / (AmE), / dɪkˈteɪtə / (BrE)
a ruler who has absolute power, usually one who takes control by force and is not restricted by law or constitution

Examples

Military dictator
After the revolution, the dictator was removed from power and democracy was restored.
Many people fled the nation to escape the dictator's oppressive regime.
The country suffered under the rule of a brutal dictator for over two decades.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autocrat - a ruler with unlimited power; a synonym for dictator
  • antonym: democrat - someone who supports democracy; an antonym of dictator in political sense
  • degree lower: president - the elected head of a republic, often with limited powers; lesser degree than dictator
  • synonym: tyrant - a cruel and oppressive ruler; another synonym for dictator
