waggle

noun
side-to-side or up-and-down movement
Pronunciation: / ˈwæɡəl / (AmE), / ˈwæɡl̩ / (BrE)
a movement in which something is moved repeatedly from side to side or up and down

Examples

A waggle of the tail
She performed a small waggle of her hips during the dance routine.
The golfer practiced his waggle before taking the first swing.
The puppy gave a quick waggle of its tail when it saw its owner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shake - to move quickly up and down or from side to side
  • synonym: wiggle - a small, quick movement up and down or from side to side
