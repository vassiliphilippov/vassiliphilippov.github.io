figurational

adjective
depicting real forms
8.9
Pronunciation: /fɪɡ.jə.ˈreɪ.ʃən.əl/ /; / /fɪɡ.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən.əl/ (AmE), /fɪɡ.jə.ˈreɪ.ʃən.əl/ /; / /fɪɡ.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən.əl/ (BrE)
relating to the depiction or representation of real objects, especially human figures, in visual art

Examples

Figurational art style
The artist's figurational style focuses on realistic portrayals of people and everyday scenes.
Many critics praised the figurational elements in her latest exhibition for their emotional depth.
He prefers figurational painting over abstract art because it tells a clearer story.
