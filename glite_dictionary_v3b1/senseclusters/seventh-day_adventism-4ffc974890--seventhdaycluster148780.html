Seventh-Day Adventism

noun
Protestant Christian denomination observing Saturday Sabbath
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛvənθ deɪ ædˈvɛntɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌsɛvənθ deɪ ədˈvɛntɪzəm / (BrE)
A Protestant Christian movement distinguished by its observance of Saturday as the Sabbath and its emphasis on the imminent Second Coming of Christ.

Examples

Seventh-Day Adventism church
Seventh-Day Adventism emphasizes healthy living and education as part of its religious teachings.
Many members of Seventh-Day Adventism follow a vegetarian diet as part of their faith.
She grew up in a family devoted to Seventh-Day Adventism and attended church every Saturday.

  • synonym: Adventism - The belief in the imminent Second Coming of Christ
