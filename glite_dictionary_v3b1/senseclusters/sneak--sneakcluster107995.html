sneak

adjective
secretive or stealthy
5.6 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / sniÀêk / (AmE), / sniÀêk / (BrE)
Done in a quiet and secretive way to avoid being noticed; characterized by stealth and caution.

Examples

Sneak attack
He heard the sneak footsteps of someone approaching in the dark hallway.
She gave her friend a sneak glance to signal it was time to leave.
The soldiers launched a sneak attack on the enemy base during the night.

Synonyms

  • furtive

  • surreptitious
    8.5 0.59/m
