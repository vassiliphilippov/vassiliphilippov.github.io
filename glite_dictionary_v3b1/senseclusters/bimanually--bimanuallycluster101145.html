bimanually

adverb
with both hands
7.3
Pronunciation: / baɪˈmænjuəli / (AmE), / baɪˈmænjuəli / (BrE)
using or involving both hands at the same time

Examples

To work bimanually
The pianist played the complex piece bimanually, impressing the entire audience.
Certain medical examinations must be performed bimanually for accuracy.
She kneaded the dough bimanually to ensure it was evenly mixed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ambidextrously - using both hands with equal skill
  • antonym: unimanually - using or involving one hand only
