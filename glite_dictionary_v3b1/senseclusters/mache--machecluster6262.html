mache

noun
paper modeling material
Pronunciation: / məˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / mæˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
a lightweight modeling material made from paper and adhesive, used for crafting and sculpture

Examples

Mache mask
The children built a mask from layers of mache and painted it with bright colors.
Artists often use mache to create lightweight sculptures and decorative objects.
You can make a bowl out of mache by layering paper and glue over a mold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: papier-mâché - a material made from paper and glue, used for modeling
