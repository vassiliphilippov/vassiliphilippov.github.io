mossing

noun
gathering or covering with moss
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɒsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of gathering moss or the process of becoming covered with moss

Examples

To moss the rocks
The gardener spent the afternoon mossing the rocks for a natural look.
Over the years, the statue in the garden began mossing and blending into the landscape.
Some people enjoy mossing in forests to collect soft green moss for crafts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moss - a small green plant that grows in moist places
