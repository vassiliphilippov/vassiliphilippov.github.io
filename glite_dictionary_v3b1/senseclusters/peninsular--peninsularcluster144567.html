peninsular

adjective
relating to a peninsula
7.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈnɪnsələr / (AmE), / pəˈnɪnsjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to, forming, or resembling a peninsula.

Examples

Peninsular region
Coastal communities often face challenges due to their peninsular geography.
The peninsular isolation of the area preserved its distinct culture.
The peninsular region is known for its unique biodiversity.
