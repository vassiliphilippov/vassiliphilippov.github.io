grid

noun
system for electrical power distribution
6.0 3.1
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪd / (AmE), / ɡrɪd / (BrE)
a system of interconnected high-voltage transmission lines through which electrical power is distributed across a large area or region

Upgrading the national grid is essential to support renewable energy sources like wind and solar.
