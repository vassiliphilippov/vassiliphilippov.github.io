uniformly

adverb
in a consistent or even way
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈjunɪˌfɔrmli / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪˌfɔːmli / (BrE)
in a way that is the same in all parts, times, or cases; evenly or consistently

Examples

To be uniformly dressed
All students must be uniformly dressed according to the school code.
The paint was applied uniformly across the entire wall for a smooth finish.
The temperature was not uniformly distributed throughout the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evenly - in a regular or equal way
  • antonym: randomly - in a way that happens without a pattern
