non-cash

noun
payment without cash
4.1
Pronunciation: / nɑn kæʃ / (AmE), / nɒn kæʃ / (BrE)
a payment or transaction that does not involve physical cash, such as by card, cheque, or bank transfer

Examples

Non-cash payment
The store encourages non-cash payments to reduce the risk of theft.
Many employees now receive their salaries through non-cash transactions like direct deposit.
Using non-cash methods can make it easier to track your spending.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cash - money in the form of coins or notes
  • synonym: cashless - not using or involving physical cash
