Muscogee

noun
Native American language
Pronunciation: / mʌsˈkoʊgi / (AmE), / mʌsˈkəʊgi / (BrE)
the Muskogean language spoken by the Muscogee (Creek) people of the southeastern United States

Examples

Muscogee language
She is learning Muscogee to help preserve her family's cultural heritage.
The university offers a course in Muscogee for students interested in indigenous languages.
Many traditional songs are still sung in Muscogee at community gatherings.
