gaumless

adjective
lacking intelligence or vitality
Pronunciation: / ɡɔmləs / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːmləs / (BrE)
(British informal) lacking intelligence or energy; slow-witted or sluggish.

Examples

A gaumless person
Don't be so gaumless; pay attention to what's happening around you.
She thought him gaumless for forgetting his way home yet again.
The workers seemed gaumless after the long night shift.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alert - quick to notice and understand things
  • synonym: dim - slow to understand; not intelligent
