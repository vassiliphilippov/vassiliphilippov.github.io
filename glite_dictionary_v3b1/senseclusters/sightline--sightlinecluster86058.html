sightline

noun
viewpoint or perspective
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪtˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsaɪtˌlaɪn / (BrE)
an unobstructed line of vision from a particular position to what is being viewed, especially in a building, theater, or design
