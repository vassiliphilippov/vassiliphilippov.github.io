etesian

adjective
Mediterranean summer winds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtiːziən / (AmE), / ɪˈtiːziən / (BrE)
relating to the annual summer winds that blow across the Mediterranean region, especially bringing cooler air

Examples

Etesian winds
The etesian winds help to cool the coastal cities of Greece during the hottest months.
Farmers rely on the regular arrival of the etesian breezes to protect their crops from extreme heat.
Sailors in the Mediterranean often plan their journeys around the predictable etesian winds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monsoon - a seasonal wind, especially in South Asia
