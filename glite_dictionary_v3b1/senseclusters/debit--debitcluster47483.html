debit

verb
record an amount as a debit
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛbɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɛbɪt / (BrE)
to record or charge an amount of money to the debit side of an account

Examples

To debit an account
Before processing the payment, please ensure you have sufficient funds to debit the account.
She debited the amount to the correct ledger as required.
The bank will debit your account for the monthly subscription fee.

Related Terms

  • antonym: credit - to record or enter as credit in an account
