desolate

adjective
bleak and uninhabited (of places)
6.8 2.73/m

(of a place) empty, bleak, and providing no shelter or sustenance; deserted and inhospitable.

Examples

Desolate landscape
After weeks without rain, the landscape became desolate and lifeless.
Standing on the desolate shore, she felt utterly alone.
The explorers struggled to cross the desolate desert, where nothing grew and there was no shelter.
