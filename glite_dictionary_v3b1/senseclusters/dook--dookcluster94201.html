dook

noun
ferret or quick dip
a playful term for a ferret or, in some dialects, a quick dip in water

Examples

A pet dook
The children laughed as the dook chased a ball around the living room.
After a long walk, he enjoyed a refreshing dook in the cool stream.
She heard the soft dook of her pet as it played in its cage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dip - a brief plunge into water
  • synonym: ferret - a small, domesticated mammal often kept as a pet
