animalize

verb
represent in the form of an animal
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.ə.məˌlaɪz /; / ˈænɪməˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈæn.ɪ.mə.laɪz /; / ˈænɪməlaɪz / (BrE)
to represent or portray something as an animal or in the form of an animal
