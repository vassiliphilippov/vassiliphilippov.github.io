nonsalaried

adjective
without regular salary
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsælərid / (AmE), / nɒnˈsælərid / (BrE)
not receiving a regular salary for work performed

Examples

Nonsalaried workers
Many nonsalaried workers rely on hourly wages or commissions instead of a fixed income.
The organization depends on nonsalaried volunteers to help run its community programs.
He accepted a nonsalaried position to gain experience in the field.

Related Terms

  • antonym: salaried - receiving a regular salary for work
  • synonym: unpaid - not receiving payment for work
