ambulance chasing

noun
unethical legal client solicitation
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæm.bjə.ləns ˌtʃeɪ.sɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈæm.bjʊ.ləns ˌtʃeɪ.sɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of soliciting legal clients at the scenes of accidents or disasters, often considered unethical

Examples

Ambulance chasing lawyers
Critics argue that ambulance chasing undermines public trust in the legal profession.
Many states have implemented rules to prevent lawyers from ambulance chasing.
The law firm was accused of ambulance chasing after repeatedly showing up at accident scenes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: solicitation - the act of seeking business actively, especially in law
