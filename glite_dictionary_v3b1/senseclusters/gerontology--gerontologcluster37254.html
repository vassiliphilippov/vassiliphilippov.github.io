gerontology

noun
study of aging and elderly-related issues
Pronunciation: / dʒɛr.ənˈtɑ.lə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛr.ɒnˈtɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
The scientific study of the aging process and of the diseases and problems that affect elderly people.

Examples

Study of gerontology
Recent advances in gerontology have led to better treatments for age-related diseases.
She decided to specialize in gerontology because she wanted to help improve the lives of older adults.
The university offers a master's program in gerontology for students interested in aging studies.
