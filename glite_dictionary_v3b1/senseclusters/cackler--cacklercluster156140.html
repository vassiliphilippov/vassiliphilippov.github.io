cackler

noun
a noisy, insectivorous Old World bird
Pronunciation: / ˈkæk.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæk.lə / (BrE)
any of several insect-eating Old World birds known for their loud, repetitive song, sometimes classified in the family Muscicapidae

Examples

Birdwatchers gathered early to catch a glimpse of the elusive cackler at dawn.
