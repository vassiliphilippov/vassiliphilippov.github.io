brise

noun
gentle wind
Pronunciation: / briːz / (AmE), / briːz / (BrE)
a gentle or light wind; a variant spelling of 'breeze'

Examples

A cool brise
A cool brise drifted through the open window, bringing relief from the heat.
They enjoyed their picnic under the trees, feeling the soft brise on their faces.
The curtains fluttered gently in the evening brise as the sun set.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breeze - a gentle wind
  • degree higher: gale - a very strong wind
  • degree higher: gust - a sudden strong rush of wind
