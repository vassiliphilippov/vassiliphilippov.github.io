umfundisi

noun
teacher or priest
Pronunciation: / umfunˈdiːsi / (AmE), / umfunˈdiːsi / (BrE)
a teacher or Christian minister, especially in southern African communities; also used as a respectful title for such a person

Children at the school greeted umfundisi with respect every morning.
