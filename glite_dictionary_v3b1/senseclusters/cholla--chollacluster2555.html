cholla

noun
spiny, jointed cactus from southwestern US and Mexico
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɔɪ.jə / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒj.ə / (BrE)
Any of several cacti with spiny, jointed, cylindrical stem segments, native to the southwestern United States and Mexico.

Examples

A cholla cactus
Hikers are warned to avoid getting too close to a cholla because of its sharp spines.
In his garden, he cultivates several varieties of cholla collected from the Sonoran Desert.
The desert landscape was dotted with cholla and towering saguaro.
