cuneal

adjective
wedge-shaped (especially anatomical)
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːniəl / (AmE), / ˈkjuːniəl / (BrE)
shaped like a wedge; wedge-shaped, especially in reference to anatomical structures.

Examples

cuneal shape
Certain rocks have a cuneal form that narrows to a thin edge.
The cuneal region of the brain is located in the occipital lobe.
The artist carved a cuneal design into the surface of the stone.
