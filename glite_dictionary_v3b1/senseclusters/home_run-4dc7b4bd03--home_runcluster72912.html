home run

noun
baseball hit allowing a run to score
Pronunciation: / hoʊm rʌn /; / ˈhoʊm ˌrʌn / (AmE), / həʊm rʌn /; / ˈhəʊm ˌrʌn / (BrE)
a hit in baseball that allows the batter to round all the bases and score a run

Examples

Hit a home run
He dreamed of hitting a home run at his first professional baseball game.
She broke the school's record with her fifth home run of the season.
The crowd erupted in cheers when he hit a home run in the final inning.
He hit a spectacular home run in the ninth inning to win the game.
Her innovative solution was considered a home run by the entire team.
Their new marketing strategy turned out to be a real home run for the company.
