buttercup squash

noun
a variety of winter squash
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ɚˌkʌp skwɑʃ /; / ˈbʌtərˌkʌp skwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəkʌp skwɒʃ / (BrE)
a variety of winter squash with a drum-shaped fruit, dark green rind with grey or silver markings, and sweet orange flesh
