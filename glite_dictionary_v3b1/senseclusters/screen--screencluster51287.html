screen

verb
show a film or video by projection
1.0
Pronunciation: / skrin / (AmE), / skriːn / (BrE)
to show a film, video, or television program by projecting it onto a screen for an audience to view

Examples

To screen a movie
The festival will screen several new documentaries this year.
The film was screened to a packed audience last night.
They plan to screen the classic movie at the local theater.
