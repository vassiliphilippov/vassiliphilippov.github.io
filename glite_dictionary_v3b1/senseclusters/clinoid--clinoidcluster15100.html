clinoid

noun
bony skull projection
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪnɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈklaɪnɔɪd / (BrE)
a bony projection, especially one of the clinoid processes on the sphenoid bone of the skull

Examples

Clinoid process
The surgeon carefully avoided the clinoid during the delicate brain operation.
Damage to the anterior clinoid can affect nearby nerves in the skull.
In anatomy class, we learned to identify each clinoid on the sphenoid bone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: process - a projection or outgrowth of bone
