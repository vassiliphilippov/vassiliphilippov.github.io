inflicter

noun
one who causes harm
8.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈflɪktər / (AmE), / ɪnˈflɪktə / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes something unpleasant, harmful, or painful to happen to someone or something

Examples

The inflicter of pain
The inflicter of the harsh punishment was never brought to justice.
She refused to be the inflicter of pain, even when provoked.
Historians debated who was the true inflicter of suffering during the conflict.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perpetrator - a person who carries out a harmful or illegal act
  • antonym: victim - a person who suffers harm or unpleasantness
