futile

adjective
pointless; producing no result
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːtəl /; / ˈfjuːt̬əl / (AmE), / ˈfjuːtaɪl / (BrE)
incapable of producing any useful result; pointless or useless

Examples

A futile effort
All their attempts to solve the problem proved futile in the end.
Despite their best efforts, the search for survivors seemed futile.
He realized it was futile to argue with her any further.
After hours of futile negotiations, neither party was willing to compromise.
Her efforts to convince the jury proved futile.
It would be futile to try to change his mind now.

Related Terms

  • antonym: effective - successful in producing a desired result
  • synonym: fruitless - failing to achieve the desired result
  • synonym: ineffectual - not producing any or the desired effect
  • synonym: vain - producing no result; unsuccessful
