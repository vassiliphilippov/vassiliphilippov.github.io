doormat

noun
submissive or weak person
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔːrˌmæt /; / ˈdoʊˌmæt / (AmE), / ˈdɔːˌmæt /; / ˈdɔː.mæt / (BrE)
a person who lets others treat them badly or take advantage of them by being too submissive

Examples

He is a doormat
He let his coworkers treat him like a doormat, never standing up for himself.
If you always say yes to everything, people may start seeing you as a doormat.
She refused to be a doormat any longer and started asserting her opinions.
He doesn't like being treated like a doormat by his coworkers anymore.
If you let people take advantage of you, they'll continue to see you as a doormat.
She realized she had been a doormat in her last relationship and decided to stand up for herself.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bully - a person who habitually seeks to harm or intimidate those whom they perceive as vulnerable
  • synonym: martyr - someone who suffers or allows themselves to be mistreated for others, often needlessly
  • synonym: pushover - a person who is easy to influence or defeat
  • antonym: tyrant - a person who uses power or authority cruelly or unjustly
