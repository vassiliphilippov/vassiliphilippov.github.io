leg-pull

noun
a playful attempt to deceive as a joke
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛɡ ˌpʊl / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡ ˌpʊl / (BrE)
an act of teasing or jokingly trying to make someone believe something that is not true

Examples

A leg-pull joke
Don't take his story seriously—he's having a leg-pull at your expense!
He told me he won the lottery, but I knew it was just a leg-pull.
She couldn't believe how long it took him to realize it was just a leg-pull.
