everyday

adjective
suitable for routine or casual use
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛvriˌdeɪ /; / ˈɛv.riˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛvriˌdeɪ /; / ˈɛv.ri.deɪ / (BrE)
