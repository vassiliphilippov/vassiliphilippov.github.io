water main

noun
large pipe for public water supply
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ ˌmeɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌmeɪn / (BrE)
A large pipe that carries water from a public water supply to buildings and homes.

Examples

A water main pipe
A burst water main caused flooding on several streets downtown.
A team of engineers inspected the water main for signs of leakage.
The city is replacing the old water mains to improve the supply system.
