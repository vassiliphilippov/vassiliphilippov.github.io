Müllerian

adjective
of embryonic ducts
9.9
Pronunciation: / mʊˈlɛriən / (AmE), / mʊˈlɜːriən / (BrE)
relating to the Müllerian ducts, which are embryonic structures in vertebrates that develop into parts of the female reproductive system

Examples

Müllerian duct development
The doctor discussed Müllerian anomalies that can affect female reproductive development.
A Müllerian duct abnormality may lead to certain congenital conditions in women.
Researchers study Müllerian structures to understand the origins of reproductive organs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Wolffian - relating to the Wolffian ducts, embryonic structures developing into male reproductive organs
