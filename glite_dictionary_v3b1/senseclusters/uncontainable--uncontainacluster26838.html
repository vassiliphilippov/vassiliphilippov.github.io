uncontainable

adjective
impossible to restrain
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈteɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈteɪnəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to control, restrain, or keep within limits

Examples

Uncontainable excitement
The crowd's uncontainable excitement filled the stadium during the final match.
His uncontainable laughter made everyone else start laughing too.
After hearing the good news, she felt an uncontainable sense of joy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: controllable - able to be controlled or restrained
  • synonym: irrepressible - impossible to control or restrain
