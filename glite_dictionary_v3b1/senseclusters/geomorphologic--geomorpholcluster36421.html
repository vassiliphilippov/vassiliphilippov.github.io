geomorphologic

adjective
relating to Earth's surface structure
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiː.oʊˌmɔːr.fəˈlɑː.dʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː.əʊˌmɔː.fəˈlɒ.dʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the form and processes of the Earth's surface or its geological structure, especially as studied in geomorphology.

Examples

Geomorphologic features
Coastal management strategies must consider the geomorphologic features of the shoreline.
Researchers published a study on the geomorphologic changes caused by river flooding.
The geomorphologic characteristics of the region reveal its glacial history.
