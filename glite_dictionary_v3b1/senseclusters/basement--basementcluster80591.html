basement

noun
lowest floor below ground level
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsmənt / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsmənt / (BrE)
The lowest floor of a building, typically partly or entirely below ground level, often used for storage or utility purposes.
