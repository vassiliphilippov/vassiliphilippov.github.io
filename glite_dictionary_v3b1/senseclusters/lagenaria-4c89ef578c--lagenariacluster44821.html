Lagenaria

noun
genus of vines including bottle gourds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌlæɡəˈnɛriə / (AmE), / ˌlæɡəˈnɛːriə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical and subtropical vines in the gourd family, including bottle gourds commonly grown for their fruit.

Examples

Lagenaria bottle gourd
Farmers in tropical regions often cultivate Lagenaria for both food and utensils.
The Lagenaria genus includes several types of gourds used for making containers and musical instruments.
The distinctive shape of the fruit makes Lagenaria popular in traditional crafts around the world.
