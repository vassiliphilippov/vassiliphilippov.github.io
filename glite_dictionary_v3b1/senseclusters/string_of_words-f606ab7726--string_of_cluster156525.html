string of words

noun
a sequence of words in a row
Pronunciation: / strɪŋ əv wɜrdz / (AmE), / strɪŋ əv wɜːdz / (BrE)
a linear sequence of words as spoken or written, with no implication of grammatical structure

Examples

A string of words
In computational linguistics, a string of words may lack grammatical structure.
The password must be a unique string of words that only you know.
The poet rearranged the string of words to create a more powerful image.
