terras

noun
plural of terra
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrəz / (AmE), / ˈtɛrəs / (BrE)
the plural form of 'terra', meaning lands or earths, especially in historical or scientific contexts

Examples

Ancient terras
The ancient map depicted several terras unknown to explorers.
Scientists studied the surfaces of distant terras on other planets.
Legends spoke of lost terras beyond the known world.
