splunge

noun
messy splash mark
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / splÊŒndÊ’ / (AmE), / splÊŒndÊ’ / (BrE)

Examples

A splunge of paint

After the juice spilled, a sticky splunge appeared on the kitchen counter.
The rain left a muddy splunge on his white shirt.
