splunge

noun
messy splash mark
Pronunciation: / splʌndʒ / (AmE), / splʌndʒ / (BrE)
a splash or splatter of liquid that creates a mess, often leaving a stain or blot

Examples

A splunge of paint
There was a brown splunge on the floor where the paint had dripped.
After the juice spilled, a sticky splunge appeared on the kitchen counter.
The rain left a muddy splunge on his white shirt.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cleanliness - the state of being clean and free from marks
  • synonym: smudge - a dirty mark or smear
  • synonym: splash - a mark or sound made by something falling into liquid
  • synonym: stain - a mark left on a surface, often by a liquid
