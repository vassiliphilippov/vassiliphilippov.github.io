durable

adjective
long-lasting; resistant to wear
Pronunciation: / ˈdʊrəbl /; / ˈdʊrəbəl /; / ˈdʊr.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈdjʊərəbl /; / ˈdjʊərəbəl /; / ˈdjʊə.rə.bəl / (BrE)
able to last a long time without being easily worn out or damaged; long-lasting

Examples

Durable shoes
