chippy

noun
fish and chips shop
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪpi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪpi / (BrE)
a small shop or restaurant that sells fried fish and chips, especially in Britain

Examples

Many people in the UK enjoy getting takeaway from a chippy on Friday nights.
The chippy near my house always has a long line at lunchtime.
