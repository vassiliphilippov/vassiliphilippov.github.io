longshorewoman

noun
female port laborer
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋˌʃɔrˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌʃɔːˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to load and unload cargo from ships at a port

Examples

A longshorewoman loads cargo
As a longshorewoman, she often works long hours in challenging weather conditions.
The port hired its first longshorewoman last year, breaking a long tradition of male-only crews.
The union celebrated the achievements of every longshorewoman on International Women's Day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: longshoreman - a person (typically male) who loads and unloads ships at a port
  • synonym: stevedore - a person employed at a dock to load and unload ships
