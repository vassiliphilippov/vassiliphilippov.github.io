purty

adjective
nonstandard 'pretty'
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrti / (AmE), / ˈpɜːti / (BrE)
nonstandard or dialectal form of 'pretty', meaning attractive or pleasing in appearance

Examples

A purty girl
She wore a purty dress to the dance and everyone noticed her.
That's a purty little house you have by the lake.
He gave her a purty smile when she walked into the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pretty - attractive in a delicate or charming way
  • antonym: ugly - unpleasant or unattractive in appearance
