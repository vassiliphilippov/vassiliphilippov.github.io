Synonyms

  • interchangeable
    4.8 2.96/m
    interchangeable describes two items that can be swapped with no loss of function, while substitutable means they can be replaced.
  • exchangeable
    4.0 0.18/m
    exchangeable describes goods that can be traded or swapped in value, while substitutable focuses on replacement without changing function.
  • replaceable
    3.3 0.97/m
    replaceable says something can be put in another's place when needed, while substitutable stresses equal function or meaning.
