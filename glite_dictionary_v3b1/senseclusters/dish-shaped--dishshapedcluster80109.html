dish-shaped

adjective
having a concave, dish-like shape
7.2
Pronunciation: / dɪʃ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / dɪʃ ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having a concave form or shape similar to that of a dish or shallow pan.

Examples

Dish-shaped flowers
She admired the dish-shaped flowers blooming in her garden.
The ancient bowl had a distinctively dish-shaped base.
The satellite dish was perfectly dish-shaped to receive signals from space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concave - curved inward like the interior of a circle or sphere
