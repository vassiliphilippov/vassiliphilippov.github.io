trickery

noun
the use of tricks to deceive
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪk.ə.ri /; / ˈtrɪkəri / (AmE), / ˈtrɪk.əri /; / ˈtrɪkəri / (BrE)
the use of tricks or dishonest methods to cheat or deceive someone

Examples

He warned me about their trickery before I agreed to the deal.
Political campaigns are often accused of trickery and manipulation.
The company used trickery to convince customers to buy unnecessary products.
