doot

verb
to make horn sound
Pronunciation: / duːt / (AmE), / duːt / (BrE)
to make a short, playful horn- or trumpet-like sound, often imitating a toy instrument or for fun

Examples

To doot a trumpet
The children loved to doot their toy trumpets during the party.
He picked up the plastic horn and gave it a cheerful doot to make his friends laugh.
Every time the cartoon character appeared, a silly doot sound played in the background.

Related Terms

  • synonym: honk - to make a loud sound with a horn, especially a car horn
  • synonym: toot - to make a short, sharp sound with a horn or whistle
