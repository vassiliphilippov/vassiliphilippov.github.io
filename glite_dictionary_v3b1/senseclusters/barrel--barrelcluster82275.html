barrel

noun
a bulging cylindrical shape, hollow with flat ends
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛr.əl / (AmE), / ˈbær.əl / (BrE)
a bulging cylindrical shape that is hollow and has flat ends

Examples

Barrel-shaped pillar
The bridge was supported by concrete pillars with a barrel-like shape.
The reactor's core was encased in a thick barrel to contain the pressure.
The sections of the tunnel were constructed using large steel barrels.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cylinder - a solid or hollow object with straight parallel sides and circular ends
