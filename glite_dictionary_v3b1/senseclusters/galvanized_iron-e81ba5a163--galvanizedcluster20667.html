galvanized iron

noun
iron coated with zinc
Pronunciation: / ˈɡælvəˌnaɪzd ˈaɪərn / (AmE), / ˈɡalv(ə)naɪzd ˈʌɪən / (BrE)
iron coated with zinc to prevent it from rusting

Examples

Galvanized iron sheet
Old water tanks are often constructed from galvanized iron to resist rust.
The roof was made of galvanized iron sheets to withstand the rain.
They used galvanized iron for the fence to ensure it would last longer.
