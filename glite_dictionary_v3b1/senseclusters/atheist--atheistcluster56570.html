atheist

adjective
relating to atheism
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪθiɪst / (AmE), / ˈeɪθɪɪst / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by atheism; pertaining to disbelief in the existence of deities.

Examples

Atheist views
During the debate, her atheist perspective was evident.
His atheist beliefs often influenced his approach to philosophical discussions.
The organization has a number of atheist members who advocate for secular policies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atheistic - Relating to or characteristic of atheism or atheists
  • antonym: theistic - Relating to belief in the existence of a god or gods
