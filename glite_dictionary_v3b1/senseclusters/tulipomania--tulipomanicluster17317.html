tulipomania

noun
obsession with tulips
Pronunciation: / ˌtuːlɪpoʊˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ˌtjuːlɪpəˈmeɪnɪə / (BrE)
an intense or obsessive enthusiasm for tulips, especially for collecting or cultivating many varieties

Examples

Tulipomania for rare tulips
During the 17th century, tulipomania swept through the Netherlands, causing tulip prices to soar.
His tulipomania led him to travel across Europe in search of rare bulbs.
The exhibition explores the history and cultural impact of tulipomania in art and society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collecting - the activity of gathering items as a hobby
  • synonym: mania - an excessive enthusiasm or desire
