salpingitis

noun
inflammation of a Fallopian or Eustachian tube
Pronunciation: / ˌsælpɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌsælpɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of a Fallopian tube or a Eustachian tube, usually caused by infection.

Examples
