chicha

noun
oriental water pipe for smoking tobacco
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃi.tʃə / (AmE), / ˈtʃi.tʃə / (BrE)
an oriental water pipe for smoking tobacco or flavored substances, in which the smoke is cooled by passing through water, typically via a long, flexible tube

Examples

Smoke a chicha pipe
The chicha was beautifully decorated with colored glass and intricate designs.
Tourists often try smoking a chicha at cafés across the Middle East.
We spent the evening relaxing and sharing a chicha in the courtyard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hookah - an oriental water pipe for smoking tobacco
  • synonym: shisha - another term for a water pipe used to smoke flavored tobacco
