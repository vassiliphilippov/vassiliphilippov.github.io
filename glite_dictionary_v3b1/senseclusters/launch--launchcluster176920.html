launch

verb
send a ship into water for the first time
1.5 3.4
Pronunciation: / lɔnʧ /; / lɔntʃ / (AmE), / lɔːntʃ / (BrE)
to send or propel something, such as a ship, boat, or vehicle, into motion or into the air
