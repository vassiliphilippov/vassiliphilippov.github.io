saloon keeper

noun
proprietor of a saloon or bar
8.0
Pronunciation: / səˈluːn ˌkiːpər / (AmE), / səˈluːn ˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person who owns or manages a saloon, especially a bar or tavern

Examples

Saloon keeper manages bar
After twenty years as a saloon keeper, he knew all the town's secrets.
Every Saturday night, the saloon keeper hired a local band to play lively music.
The saloon keeper welcomed weary travelers with a warm meal and a cold drink.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bartender - a person who serves drinks at a bar
  • synonym: publican - the landlord of a pub or bar
