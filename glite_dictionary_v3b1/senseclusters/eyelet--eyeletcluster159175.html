eyelet

verb
to add eyelets
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪlət / (AmE), / ˈaɪlət / (BrE)
to make or provide something with eyelets, usually by punching and reinforcing holes for lacing or hanging

Examples

To eyelet curtains
The workers eyeleted the curtains so they could be hung on the rod easily.
She carefully eyelets each invitation card to thread the ribbon through.
After the banners were eyeleted, they were ready to be displayed at the event.
