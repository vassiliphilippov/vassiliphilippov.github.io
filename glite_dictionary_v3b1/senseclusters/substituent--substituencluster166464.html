substituent

noun
replacement person or thing
7.9
Pronunciation: / səbˈstɪtʃuənt / (AmE), / səbˈstɪtjʊənt / (BrE)
a person or thing that takes the place of another; a substitute in a general, non-technical sense

Examples

A substituent member
The committee appointed a substituent to fill the vacant position temporarily.
When the main actor fell ill, a substituent performed in his place for the evening show.
A substituent was chosen to represent the company at the conference.

Related Terms

  • antonym: original - the first or primary person or thing being replaced
  • synonym: replacement - someone or something that takes the place of another
  • synonym: substitute - a person or thing acting in place of another
