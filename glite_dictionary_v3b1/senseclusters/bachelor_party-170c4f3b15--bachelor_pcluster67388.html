bachelor party

noun
party before a man's wedding
Pronunciation: / ˈbætʃələr ˌpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈbætʃələ ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
A party held for a man shortly before he is married, typically attended only by men and often involving celebrations with his male friends.

Examples

A bachelor party celebration
A classic bachelor party usually involves the groom's closest friends and plenty of celebration.
He had too much fun at his bachelor party and almost missed his wedding rehearsal.
We're planning a big bachelor party for Mark before his wedding next weekend.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hen party - a party for a woman about to be married
  • synonym: stag party - a party for a man about to be married
