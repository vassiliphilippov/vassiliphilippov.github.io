Passamaquoddy

proper noun
Native American people
Pronunciation: / ˌpæsəməˈkwɑːdi / (AmE), / ˌpæsəməˈkwɒdi / (BrE)
a Native American people of northeastern North America, especially in Maine and New Brunswick, or their Algonquian language
