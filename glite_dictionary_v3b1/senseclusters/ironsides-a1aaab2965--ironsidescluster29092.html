Ironsides

proper noun
US Navy ship name
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪərnˌsaɪdz / (AmE), / ˈaɪənˌsaɪdz / (BrE)
the name given to several United States Navy ships, especially a famous American Civil War ironclad warship

Examples

USS Ironsides warship
The historic Ironsides played a key role in several naval battles during the Civil War.
Tourists visited the restored Ironsides to learn about its significance in American history.
The legend of Ironsides continues to inspire naval traditions today.
