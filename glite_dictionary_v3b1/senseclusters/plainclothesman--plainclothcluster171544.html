plainclothesman

noun
male detective or officer in civilian clothing
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪnˌkloʊðzˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈpleɪnˌkləʊðzˌmæn / (BrE)
A male police officer or detective who works in ordinary civilian clothes rather than a uniform, especially while on duty.

Examples

A plainclothesman officer
A plainclothesman approached us and asked a few questions about the incident.
No one suspected the plainclothesman was actually a detective on a case.
The plainclothesman followed the suspect through the crowded market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plainclotheswoman - female detective or officer in civilian clothing
