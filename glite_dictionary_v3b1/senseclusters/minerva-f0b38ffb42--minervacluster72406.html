Minerva

proper noun
Roman goddess of wisdom
Pronunciation: / mɪˈnɝːvə / (AmE), / mɪˈnɜːvə / (BrE)
In Roman mythology, the goddess of wisdom, war, and the arts, equivalent to the Greek goddess Athena.

Examples

Minerva goddess wisdom
In Roman mythology, Minerva was revered as the goddess of wisdom and strategic warfare.
Sculptures of Minerva can be found in many European museums.
The university adopted Minerva as a symbol of knowledge and learning.

  • synonym: Athena - Greek goddess of wisdom and warfare
