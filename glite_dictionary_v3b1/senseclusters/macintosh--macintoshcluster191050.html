macintosh

noun
waterproof fabric used for raincoats
Pronunciation: / ˈmæk.ɪn.tɑʃ /; / ˈmæk.ɪnˌtɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈmæk.ɪn.tɒʃ / (BrE)
a lightweight waterproof fabric, typically rubberized, used especially for making raincoats.
