multiculturalist

noun
supporter of cultural diversity
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or values the coexistence and appreciation of multiple cultures within a society
