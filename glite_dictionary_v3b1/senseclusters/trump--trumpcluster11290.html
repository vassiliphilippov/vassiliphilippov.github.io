trump

noun
winning suit in card games
5.1 10/m
Pronunciation: / trÊŒmp / (AmE), / trÊŒmp / (BrE)
(in card games) the suit or card that ranks above the others and can win over other suits

Examples

Diamonds were declared as the trump suit for this round.
