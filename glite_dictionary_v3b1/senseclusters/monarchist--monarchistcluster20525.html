monarchist

noun
advocate of monarchy
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnərkɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəkɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates the principles of monarchy or the rule of a monarch

Examples

A monarchist supporter
As a committed monarchist, she believed the king should remain head of state.
Many monarchists gathered to celebrate the queen's anniversary.
The debate between the monarchist and republican factions dominated the election.

Related Terms

  • antonym: republican - a person who supports a government without a monarch
  • synonym: royalist - a person who supports a monarch or monarchy
