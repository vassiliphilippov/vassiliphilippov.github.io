Vaisakha

noun
second month of the Hindu calendar
9.9
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈsɑːkə / (AmE), / vaɪˈsɑːkə / (BrE)
the second month of the Hindu calendar, typically falling in April and May.

Examples

The month Vaisakha
According to the traditional calendar, the new year falls in Vaisakha.
Harvest season in Punjab often coincides with Vaisakha.
The festival of Buddha Purnima is celebrated in the month of Vaisakha across India.
