unspun

adjective
not made into yarn
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈspʌn / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈspʌn / (BrE)
not twisted into thread or yarn; not having been spun

Examples

Unspun cotton fibers
The unspun cotton was piled high in baskets at the factory.
She preferred to work with unspun fibers for her weaving projects.
Before being dyed, the wool remains unspun and soft to the touch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raw - in a natural, unprocessed state
  • antonym: spun - twisted into thread or yarn
