waterproof

noun
water-resistant coat or garment
1.4 0.40/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…ît…ôrÀåpruf / (AmE), / Ààw…îÀêt…ôÀåpruÀêf / (BrE)
A coat or garment made of material that does not let water pass through, worn to protect against rain.

Examples

A waterproof coat
Don't forget your waterproof if it's going to rain today.
He put on his waterproof before heading out into the storm.
The hikers carried lightweight waterproofs for the unexpected downpour.

Synonyms

  • waterproof jacket
    2.0 0.03/m
    a waterproof jacket is made of strong material with sealed seams to keep you dry in heavy rain, while waterproof is a coat worn to stop rain.
  • raincoat
    1.0 2.71/m
    a raincoat is made from water-resistant fabric and is often lighter for light rain, while waterproof means a garment that fully blocks water.

Related Terms
