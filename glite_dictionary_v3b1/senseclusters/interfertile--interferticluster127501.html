interfertile

adjective
able to breed together
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈfɜːrtəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈfɜːtaɪl / (BrE)
able to breed or produce offspring with another species, group, or population

Examples

Interfertile species
The two species are interfertile and can produce healthy hybrid offspring.
Researchers discovered that the populations remained interfertile despite being separated for centuries.
Some closely related animals are not interfertile and cannot produce viable young together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fertile - able to produce offspring or seeds
  • antonym: infertile - unable to produce offspring or seeds
