sexually transmitted disease

noun
disease spread by sexual contact
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkʃuəli ˌtrænzˈmɪtɪd dɪˈziz / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃʊəli ˌtrænzˈmɪtɪd dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
A disease that is passed from one person to another through sexual contact.

Examples

A sexually transmitted disease infection
Doctors recommend practicing safe sex to avoid contracting a sexually transmitted disease.
Education about sexually transmitted disease is crucial for teenagers and young adults.
Regular health checks can help prevent the spread of sexually transmitted diseases.
