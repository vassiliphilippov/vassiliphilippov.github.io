freight

noun
charge for transporting goods
Pronunciation: / freɪt / (AmE), / freɪt / (BrE)
the charge or cost for transporting goods by a common carrier, such as by ship, train, or truck

Examples

Freight charges
Because he chose air transport, the freight was significantly higher than usual.
Before agreeing to the shipment, check whether freight is included in the contract.
The company increased its prices to cover the rising freight costs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shipping - the process or business of transporting goods
