pasqueflower

noun
perennial plant with spring-blooming flowers
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpæskˌflaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpæskˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
a perennial plant of the genus Pulsatilla, noted for its showy, early-spring flowers; sometimes included in the genus Anemone

Examples

The pasqueflower plant
Botanists sometimes classify the pasqueflower within the genus Anemone due to its similar appearance.
She carefully planted the pasqueflower bulbs along the garden's border to enjoy their vibrant blossoms.
The delicate purple pasqueflower blooms early each spring in the alpine meadows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Pulsatilla - the genus of perennial herbaceous plants known as pasqueflowers
  • degree higher: anemone - another genus to which pasqueflower is sometimes assigned
