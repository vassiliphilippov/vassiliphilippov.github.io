footprint

noun
area occupied by an object
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌprɪnt / (AmE), / ˈfʊt.prɪnt / (BrE)
the surface area occupied by an object, especially in reference to equipment, buildings, or other items

Examples

Building footprint
Due to its large footprint, the new machine takes up most of the lab bench.
The architects tried to minimize the building's footprint to preserve green space.
The printer's footprint is small enough to fit on a corner of the desk.
