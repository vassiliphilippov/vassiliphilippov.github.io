abbreviation for 'Junior', used after a son's name to distinguish him from his father with the same name

Examples

Jnr after name
In official documents, the suffix Jnr helps avoid confusion between father and son.
The invitation was addressed to Mark Smith Jnr, not his father.
When his son was born, he decided to name him Thomas Evans Jnr.
