arteria ileocolica

noun
artery supplying ileum, cecum, appendix, and ascending colon
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.tɪ.ˈriː.ə aɪ.liː.oʊ.ˈkɑː.lɪ.kə/ /; / /ɑːr.tɪ.ˈriː.ə ɪ.liː.oʊ.ˈkɑː.lɪ.kə/ (AmE), /ɑː.tɪ.ˈriː.ə aɪ.liː.əʊ.ˈkɒ.lɪ.kə/ /; / /ɑː.tɪ.ˈriː.ə ɪ.liː.əʊ.ˈkɒ.lɪ.kə/ (BrE)
a branch of the superior mesenteric artery that supplies blood to the terminal ileum, cecum, appendix, and ascending colon

Examples
