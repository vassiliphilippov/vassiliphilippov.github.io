angiology

noun
study of blood and lymph vessels
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒiˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌandʒiˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The branch of medical science concerned with the study of blood vessels, lymph vessels, and their disorders.
