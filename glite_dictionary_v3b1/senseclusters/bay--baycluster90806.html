bay

adjective
reddish-brown animal color (horses)
Pronunciation: / beɪ / (AmE), / beɪ / (BrE)
(of an animal, especially a horse) having a moderate reddish-brown coat with a black mane, tail, and lower legs.

Examples

Bay colored fur
A bay stallion was featured in the parade among other colored horses.
Her favorite horse was a striking bay with a glossy coat.
The bay mare trotted gracefully across the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chestnut - (of an animal, especially a horse) of a reddish-brown color, usually with the mane and tail the same color as the coat
