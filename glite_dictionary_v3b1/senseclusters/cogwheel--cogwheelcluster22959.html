cogwheel

noun
gear-shaped settings icon
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.gwiːl/ /; / /ˈkɔː.gwiːl/ (AmE), /ˈkɒ.gwiːl/ (BrE)
A gear-shaped graphical icon used in software interfaces to open or indicate settings, preferences, or configuration options.

Examples

Cogwheel icon
On newer apps, you tap the cogwheel icon to open Settings and customize your preferences.
She noticed a small cogwheel in the corner that opened the app's privacy options immediately.
When you click the cogwheel, a menu appears offering language, account, and notification settings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gear - a toothed wheel; also used to refer to gear-shaped icons in interfaces
  • synonym: settings - the preferences or configuration options that the cogwheel icon typically opens
