fetishistic

adjective
excessively devoted
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛtɪˈʃɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌfɛtɪˈʃɪstɪk / (BrE)

Some collectors develop a fetishistic attachment to rare stamps or coins.
The film explores the fetishistic nature of modern consumer culture.
