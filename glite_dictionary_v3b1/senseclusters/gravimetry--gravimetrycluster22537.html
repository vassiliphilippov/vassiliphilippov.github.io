gravimetry

noun
measurement of specific gravity
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈvɪmətri / (AmE), / ɡrəˈvɪmətri / (BrE)
the measurement of specific gravity or gravitational force, especially for scientific analysis

Examples

Gravimetry measurement
Advances in gravimetry have improved the accuracy of geophysical surveys.
The laboratory specializes in gravimetry to analyze mineral composition in rock samples.
Through gravimetry, scientists can determine changes in Earth's gravitational field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: densitometry - the measurement of optical or material density
