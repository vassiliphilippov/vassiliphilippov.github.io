field

noun
area of land for sports or games
1.0 36/m
Pronunciation: / fild / (AmE), / fiÀêld / (BrE)
an area of land specially prepared and marked for playing sports or games

Examples

Soccer field
After heavy rain, the field was too muddy to play soccer.
The championship will be held on the new field next Saturday.
The players ran onto the field as the game was about to begin.

Synonyms

  • playing field
    1.5 0.28/m
    playing field is the common phrase for a marked sports area, while field is a broader word used in many contexts.
  • athletic field
    2.1 0.27/m
    athletic field is a maintained outdoor area for track and field sports, while field is a general term for any marked sports ground.
  • playing area
    1.8 0.11/m
    playing area means any prepared space for games, often indoors or small, while field usually means a larger outdoor marked sports area.
  • pitch
    2.6 1.93/m
    pitch is a British term for a marked ground used in sports like soccer or rugby, while field is more general and global.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pitch - an area of ground marked for playing a sport, especially British English
