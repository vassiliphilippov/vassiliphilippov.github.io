irksomely

adverb
annoyingly
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɜːrksəmli / (AmE), / ˈɜːksəmli / (BrE)
in an annoying or irritating manner

Examples

To irksomely tap pen
She irksomely tapped her pen on the desk during the entire lecture.
The child irksomely interrupted the conversation with constant questions.
He irksomely pointed out every small mistake I made.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annoyingly - in a way that causes annoyance
  • antonym: pleasantly - in a way that is enjoyable or pleasing
