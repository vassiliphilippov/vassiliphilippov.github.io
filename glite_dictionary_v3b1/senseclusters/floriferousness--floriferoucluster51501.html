floriferousness

noun
abundance of flowers
Pronunciation: / flɔːˈrɪfərəsnəs / (AmE), / flɒˈrɪfərəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of producing many flowers

Examples

The floriferousness of garden
The floriferousness of the garden impressed all the visitors during spring.
Breeders select plants for their floriferousness to create more colorful displays.
High floriferousness is a desirable trait in ornamental horticulture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barrenness - the state of not producing flowers or fruit
  • synonym: flowering - the process of producing flowers
