cowboy

noun
man who looks after cattle on horseback
5.2 1.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkaʊ.bɔɪ/ (AmE), /ˈkaʊ.bɔɪ/ (BrE)
a man whose job is to look after cattle and perform related tasks, especially on horseback, typically in the western US or Canada

Examples

A cowboy rides horses
He dreams of becoming a cowboy and riding horses every day.
Several cowboys gathered around the campfire after a long day herding cattle.
The life of a cowboy on the open range was full of hard work and adventure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cowgirl - a woman who works with cattle, performing similar duties to a cowboy
  • synonym: rancher - a person who owns or manages a ranch
