namby-pamby

adjective
weak or lacking in courage
Pronunciation: / ˌnæmbiˈpæmbi / (AmE), / ˌnambiˈpambi / (BrE)
lacking courage, strength, or conviction; weak and ineffective.

Examples

Namby-pamby attitude
Don't be so namby-pamby; stand up for yourself when you know you're right.
She dismissed his namby-pamby attitude and urged him to take action.
The critics called the film's plot too namby-pamby for their taste.

Related Terms

  • antonym: courageous - having or showing bravery
  • synonym: cowardly - lacking bravery; easily frightened
  • synonym: spineless - lacking courage or determination
