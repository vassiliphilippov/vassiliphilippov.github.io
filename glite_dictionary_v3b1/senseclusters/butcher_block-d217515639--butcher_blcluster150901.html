butcher block

noun
thick hardwood slab for food preparation
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊtʃɚ blɑk / (AmE), / ˈbʊtʃə blɒk / (BrE)
a thick slab of hardwood, typically formed by bonding together strips of wood, used especially for cutting and preparing food

Examples

A butcher block counter
