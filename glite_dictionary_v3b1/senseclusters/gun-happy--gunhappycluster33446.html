gun-happy

adjective
excessively prone to using guns or violence
8.1
Pronunciation: / ɡʌn ˈhæpi / (AmE), / ɡʌn ˈhæpi / (BrE)
Having or showing a tendency to use guns or violence excessively and often recklessly.

Examples

Gun-happy behavior
Parents were concerned about their child's fascination with gun-happy movies.
Some critics argue that certain video games are far too gun-happy for young audiences.
The detective was notorious for his gun-happy methods during confrontations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trigger-happy - inclined to use weapons or violence without much thought
