biodiesel

noun
plant-based fuel
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪoʊˌdiːzəl / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəʊˌdiːzəl / (BrE)
a fuel made from plant oils or animal fats, used as an alternative to regular diesel fuel

Examples

Biodiesel fuel
The city switched its public buses to biodiesel to help reduce air pollution.
Farmers often use biodiesel in their tractors as a more sustainable option.
Many people are interested in biodiesel because it is made from renewable resources.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biofuel - fuel made from renewable biological sources
  • antonym: diesel - a type of fuel made from petroleum, used in engines
