oxhorn

noun
horn of an ox
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɑksˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈɒksˌhɔːn / (BrE)
the horn of an ox, often used as a material for making combs, buttons, or other objects

Examples

Oxhorn comb
The craftsman shaped the oxhorn into a beautiful hair comb.
In the past, drinking vessels were sometimes made from oxhorn.
She admired the smooth texture of the oxhorn handle on the old knife.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ivory - a hard, white material from the tusks of elephants
