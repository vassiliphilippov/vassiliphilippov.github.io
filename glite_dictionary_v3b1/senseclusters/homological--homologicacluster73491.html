homological

adjective
having a similar evolutionary origin
4.4 8.2
Pronunciation: /hə.mə.ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl/ /; / /hoʊ.mə.ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /hə.mə.ˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl/ /; / /hɒ.mə.ˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
(Biology) Having a similar evolutionary origin, though not necessarily the same function.

Examples

A homological bone
Researchers compared several animal skeletons to identify homological bones across species.
The wings of bats and the arms of humans are homological structures, sharing an evolutionary origin.
Though they have different functions, the pectoral fins of fish and the forelimbs of mammals are homological.

  • synonym: homologous - having the same evolutionary origin; biologically corresponding or similar
