keyhole neck

noun
neckline with a cutout below the collarbones
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːˌhoʊl nɛk / (AmE), / ˈkiːhəʊl nɛk / (BrE)
A style of neckline on a garment, featuring a small, rounded or teardrop-shaped cutout below the collarbones.

Examples

A keyhole neck blouse
Her blouse with a keyhole neck drew compliments at the party.
Many summer tops now include a stylish keyhole neck for a modern look.
The dress features a keyhole neck that adds a touch of elegance to the design.
