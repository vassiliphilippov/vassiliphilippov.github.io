introspect

verb
examine one's own thoughts or feelings
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.trəˈspɛkt / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trəˈspɛkt / (BrE)
to examine or reflect on one's own thoughts and feelings

Examples

To introspect about feelings
After the meeting, she took some time to introspect on her reaction to the criticism.
He often introspects about his goals and values while walking in the park.
It's important to introspect before making major life decisions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignore - to pay no attention to something
  • synonym: reflect - to think deeply or carefully about something
  • synonym: ruminate - to think deeply about something
