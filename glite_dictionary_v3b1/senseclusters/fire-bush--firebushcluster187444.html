fire-bush

noun
shrub with autumn red foliage
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ ˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
A densely branched Eurasian shrub whose foliage turns purple-red in autumn.

Examples

Autumn fire-bush
A mature fire-bush can provide striking color in landscaping projects.
Gardeners often plant fire-bush for its vibrant foliage in the fall.
The fire-bush in their garden turned a deep red as autumn arrived.
