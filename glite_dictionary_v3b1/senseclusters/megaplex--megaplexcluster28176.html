megaplex

noun
large cinema complex
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmeg.ə.pleks/ (AmE), /ˈmeg.ə.pleks/ (BrE)
a very large movie theater complex with many screens showing different films at the same time

Examples

Megaplex movie theater
The new megaplex downtown has over twenty screens and a huge food court.
We spent the afternoon at the megaplex watching two different movies back to back.
Many families prefer going to a megaplex because of the wide variety of films available.
