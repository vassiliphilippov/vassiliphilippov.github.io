junkman

noun
slang drug dealer
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋkmæn / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋkmæn / (BrE)
slang: a person who sells illegal drugs, especially heroin

Examples

Police catch junkman
The police arrested the junkman after a long undercover investigation.
Neighbors suspected that the old warehouse was being used by a junkman to store illegal drugs.
The community worked together to report the activities of a known junkman in the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dealer - a person who buys and sells illegal drugs
  • synonym: pusher - a person who sells illegal drugs
  • antonym: user - a person who takes illegal drugs
