keratoderma

noun
thickened, horny skin disorder
8.3
Pronunciation: / kɛɹətoʊˈdɝmə / (AmE), / kɛɹətəˈdɜːmə / (BrE)
A skin disorder characterized by a thickened, horny layer of skin.

Examples

Thick keratoderma skin
Some forms of keratoderma are inherited and appear in childhood.
The patient was diagnosed with keratoderma after developing thick patches on his palms and soles.
Treatment for keratoderma may involve topical creams to soften the skin.
