field

noun
all competitors in a contest or event
1.0
Pronunciation: / fiːld / (AmE), / fiːld / (BrE)
all the competitors or participants in a contest, race, or sporting event

Examples

Field of runners
She finished first out of a field of fifteen competitors.
The field for the marathon includes runners from over twenty countries.
This year's field is especially strong, with many former champions returning.
A late scratch reduced the number of runners in the field to nine.
Betting on the field can offer higher odds if the favorites are not expected to win.
The field for the Kentucky Derby includes several strong contenders this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contestants - people taking part in a contest
  • synonym: entrant - a competitor in a race or contest
  • antonym: favorite - the competitor most likely to win a race
  • synonym: participants - those who are involved in an event or activity
