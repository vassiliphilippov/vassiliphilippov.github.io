openhearted

adjective
sincere, kind, and generous
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊpənˈhɑrtɪd /; / ˌoʊ.pənˈhɑːr.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊpənˈhɑːtɪd /; / ˌəʊ.pənˈhɑː.tɪd / (BrE)
showing genuine kindness, honesty, and willingness to share feelings openly; sincere and generous

Examples

An openhearted person
He spoke in an openhearted way, sharing his experiences honestly and without reservation.
Her openhearted welcome made the new team member feel immediately at ease.
The community’s openhearted support helped the family through difficult times.
He offered openhearted advice to his friends, never holding anything back.
Her openhearted response made everyone feel at ease during the meeting.
The team appreciated their manager's openhearted communication style.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coldhearted - lacking compassion or kindness
  • synonym: forthcoming - willing to share information freely; communicative
  • synonym: generous - willing to give more of something, especially money, than is strictly necessary or expected
  • antonym: guarded - cautious in sharing feelings; not open
  • synonym: kindhearted - having a kind and sympathetic nature
