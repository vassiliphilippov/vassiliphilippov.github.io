jump

verb
enter eagerly or suddenly into an activity
Pronunciation: / dʒʌmp / (AmE), / dʒʌmp / (BrE)
to enter into an activity, situation, or opportunity eagerly or suddenly

Examples

To jump at a chance
Many startups jump at the chance to work with big tech companies.
She decided to jump into the conversation despite her nervousness.
Whenever there's a new project, he likes to jump in and take the lead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plunge - to enter suddenly or energetically into something, often used figuratively
