smoke signal

noun
long-distance message sent with smoke
4.8 3.9
Pronunciation: /smoʊk ˈsɪg.nəl/ (AmE), /sməʊk ˈsɪg.nəl/ (BrE)
a method of sending messages over long distances by creating patterned puffs of smoke, traditionally used by some indigenous peoples and in emergencies

Examples

Send a smoke signal
Sending a smoke signal was a traditional way to convey news across great distances.
The stranded hikers produced a smoke signal to draw the attention of rescuers.
We spotted a smoke signal rising from the forest, indicating someone needed help.
