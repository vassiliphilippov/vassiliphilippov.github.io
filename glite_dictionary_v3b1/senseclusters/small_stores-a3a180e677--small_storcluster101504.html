small stores

noun
personal items sold aboard ship or base (naval)
6.7
Pronunciation: / smɔl stɔrz / (AmE), / smɔːl stɔːz / (BrE)
Personal items permitted by regulations, sold aboard ships or at naval bases and charged to a person's pay, especially in naval contexts.

Examples

Buy small stores aboard ship
He deducted the cost of his small stores purchases directly from his navy pay.
Sailors could purchase soap and shaving cream from the small stores on the ship.
The small stores office was open daily for crew members to buy personal necessities.
