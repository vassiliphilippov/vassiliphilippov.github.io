gummy

adjective
showing a lot of gum when smiling
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈgʌ.miː/ /; / /ˈgʌ.mɪ/ (AmE), /ˈgʌ.miː/ /; / /ˈgʌ.mɪ/ (BrE)
(of a smile) showing an unusually large amount of gum tissue above the teeth; relating to prominence of the gums when smiling.

Examples

Gummy smile
Her gummy smile made her self-conscious whenever someone took a close-up photograph.
After treatment, his once gummy grin revealed more teeth and much less gum.
Dentists sometimes recommend surgery to reduce a gummy smile and improve facial balance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gingival display - the amount of gum tissue visible when smiling (a dental term)
  • synonym: gummy smile - a smile that exposes an excessive amount of gum tissue above the teeth
