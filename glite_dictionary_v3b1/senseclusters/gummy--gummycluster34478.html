(of a smile) showing an unusually large amount of gum tissue above the teeth; relating to prominence of the gums when smiling.

Examples

Gummy smile

After treatment, his once gummy grin revealed more teeth and much less gum.
Dentists sometimes recommend surgery to reduce a gummy smile and improve facial balance.
