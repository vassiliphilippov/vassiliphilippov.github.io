unsell

verb
to withdraw from sale
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛl / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛl / (BrE)
to withdraw a product from sale or cancel a previous sale or offer.

Examples

To unsell a product
The company decided to unsell the product after receiving negative feedback from customers.
Due to safety concerns, the manufacturer had to unsell several batches from the market.
After reconsidering, she chose to unsell her artwork and keep it for herself.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sell - to offer something for purchase
  • synonym: withdraw - to remove something from availability or use
