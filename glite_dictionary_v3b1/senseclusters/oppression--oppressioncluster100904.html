Synonyms

  • tyranny
    6.7 5/m
    Describes cruel rule concentrated in one person or small ruling group
    vs oppression: cruel and oppressive government or rule where power is held by one person or a small group and used harshly
  • repression
    7.2 2/m
    Emphasizes forcing silence and blocking public expression or rights
    vs oppression: A state in which a group or individual is kept under control, often by force, preventing them from expressing themselves or exercising their rights.
  • despotism
    9.6 0.3/m
    Focuses on absolute centralized power held by one person or group
    vs oppression: a system of government where one person or group has absolute power, often ruling in a cruel or oppressive way
  • subjugation
    8.4 0.5/m
    Refers to bringing people under control, often by force or conquest
    vs oppression: the act of bringing someone or something under control or domination, especially by force or conquest
