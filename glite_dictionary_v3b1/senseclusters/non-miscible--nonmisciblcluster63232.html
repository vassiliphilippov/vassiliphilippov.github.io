non-miscible

adjective
not able to be mixed (liquids)
8.0 7.1
Pronunciation: /nɑːn ˈmɪs.ə.bəl/ /; / /nən ˈmɪs.ə.bəl/ /; / /nɑːn ˈmɪs.ɪ.bəl/ (AmE), /nɒn ˈmɪs.ə.bəl/ /; / /nən ˈmɪs.ə.bəl/ /; / /nɒn ˈmɪs.ɪ.bəl/ (BrE)
(of liquids) not able to be mixed to form a homogeneous solution.

Examples

Non-miscible liquids
Oil and water are non-miscible and will separate into layers when combined.
The chemist explained that certain solvents are non-miscible with water, so they must be mixed carefully.
The product instructions cautioned against using non-miscible liquids together.
