voltaic battery

noun
battery made of voltaic cells
7.5 5.1
Pronunciation: /voʊl.ˈteɪ.ɪk ˈbæt.ər.iː/ (AmE), /vɒl.ˈteɪ.ɪk ˈbæt.ə.rɪ/ (BrE)
A battery consisting of a series or parallel arrangement of voltaic cells that produce electricity through chemical reactions.

Examples

A voltaic battery cell
Each voltaic battery is composed of several individual cells working together to generate electrical power.
Early experiments with a voltaic battery demonstrated the principles of electrolysis and electric current.
The invention of the voltaic battery revolutionized the study of electricity in the nineteenth century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: battery - a device that produces electricity, typically from chemical reactions
  • synonym: voltaic cell - an individual cell generating electricity via chemical reactions
