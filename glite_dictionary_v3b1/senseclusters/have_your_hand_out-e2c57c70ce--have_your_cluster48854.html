have your hand out

verb
ask for money or help
1.0
Pronunciation: / hæv jʊr hænd aʊt / (AmE), / hæv jɔː hænd aʊt / (BrE)
to ask for or expect money or help, especially repeatedly or without making an effort yourself

Examples
