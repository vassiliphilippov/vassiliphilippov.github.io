rewarder

noun
giver of rewards
3.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈwɔrdər / (AmE), / rɪˈwɔːdə / (BrE)
a person or thing that gives a reward to someone for their actions or achievements

Examples

A generous rewarder
The company was known as a generous rewarder of employee achievements.
As a rewarder of good behavior, the teacher handed out small prizes to her students.
The foundation became a prominent rewarder of scientific innovation in the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benefactor - a person who gives help or money to others
  • antonym: punisher - a person or thing that gives punishment
