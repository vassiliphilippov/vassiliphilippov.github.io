Injun

noun
offensive term for Native Americans
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndʒən / (AmE), / ˈɪndʒən / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) An outdated and offensive term for a Native American.

Examples

Old Injuns stories
He told stories about cowboys and Injuns that he had heard from his grandfather.
Please avoid using the term Injun; it is hurtful and disrespectful to Native Americans.
The old Western movies often used the word Injun, but it is now considered deeply offensive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Indian - a member of the indigenous peoples of North or South America
