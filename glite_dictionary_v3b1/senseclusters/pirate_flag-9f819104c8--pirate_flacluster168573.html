pirate flag

noun
flag with skull and crossbones, used by pirates
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪrət flæɡ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪərət flaɡ / (BrE)
a flag, typically featuring a white skull and crossbones on a black background, used to identify a pirate ship

Examples

A pirate flag with skull
A tattered pirate flag was spotted flying above the old lighthouse.
Children dressed as pirates for Halloween, complete with eye patches and a pirate flag.
The ship hoisted a pirate flag as it approached the merchant vessel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Jolly Roger - traditional name for a pirate flag featuring a skull and crossbones
