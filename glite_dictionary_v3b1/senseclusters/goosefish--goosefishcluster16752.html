goosefish

noun
large-mouthed anglerfish of the Atlantic
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡusˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡuːsfɪʃ / (BrE)
A large-mouthed anglerfish found in the western Atlantic, noted for its broad flattened head and a wormlike filament used to lure prey.

Fishermen sometimes catch goosefish along the northeast coast of the United States.
