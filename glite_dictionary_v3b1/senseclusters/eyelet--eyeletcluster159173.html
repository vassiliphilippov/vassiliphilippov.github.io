eyelet

noun
ornamental fabric with small stitched holes
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪlət / (AmE), / ˈaɪlɪt / (BrE)
Fabric decorated with small holes that have neatly stitched edges, forming an ornamental pattern.

Examples

Eyelet fabric pattern
She chose curtains made from white eyelet for a light and airy look.
The dress was trimmed with delicate eyelet along the hem.
This pillowcase features intricate eyelet embroidery as its main decoration.

Related Terms
