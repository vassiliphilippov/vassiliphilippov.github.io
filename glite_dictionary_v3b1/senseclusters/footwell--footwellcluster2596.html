footwell

noun
recessed space for feet
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌwɛl / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌwɛl / (BrE)
a recessed space under a seat or similar structure where you put your feet, especially in a car or other vehicle

Examples

The footwell of a car
She placed her bag in the footwell of the car to keep it out of the way.
The heater warmed the footwell under his desk during the cold winter months.
He found his missing pen rolling around in the footwell beneath the passenger seat.
She found her missing phone in the car's footwell.
The passenger placed his backpack in the footwell to keep it out of the way.
After the hike, his muddy boots left dirt all over the footwell of the van.
