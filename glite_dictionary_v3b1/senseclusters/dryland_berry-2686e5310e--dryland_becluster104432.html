dryland berry

noun
shrub with dark blue sweet berries
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪˌlænd ˈbɛri / (AmE), / ˈdraɪˌlænd ˈbɛri / (BrE)
A low, deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States that bears dark blue sweet berries.
