brandy nose

noun
nose enlarged and reddened by alcohol use
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbrændi noʊz / (AmE), / ˈbrandi nəʊz / (BrE)
An enlargement of the nose with redness and prominent blood vessels, often caused by excessive alcohol consumption.

Examples

A brandy nose redness
She explained that a brandy nose can also be caused by underlying skin conditions, not just alcohol.
The old captain's brandy nose was a familiar sight in the local pub.
Years of heavy drinking had left him with a prominent brandy nose and reddened cheeks.

Related Terms
