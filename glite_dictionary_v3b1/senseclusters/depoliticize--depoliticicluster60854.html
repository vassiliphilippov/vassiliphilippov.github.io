depoliticize

verb
to remove politics
6.9
Pronunciation: / diːpəˈlɪtɪsaɪz /; / diːpəˈlɪtəˌsaɪz / (AmE), / diːpəˈlɪtɪsaɪz / (BrE)
to remove political influence, character, or aspects from something
