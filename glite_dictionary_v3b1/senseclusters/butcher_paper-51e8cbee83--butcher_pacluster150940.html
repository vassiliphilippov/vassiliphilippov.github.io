butcher paper

noun
strong paper for wrapping meat
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊtʃɚ ˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʊtʃə ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a strong, sturdy paper used for wrapping meat and other foods, designed to resist leakage of blood or fluids

Examples

Butcher paper for wrapping
Many artists enjoy using butcher paper for making large sketches and murals.
She asked the deli worker to cover the roast in butcher paper instead of plastic wrap.
The steaks were wrapped in butcher paper to keep them fresh during transport.
