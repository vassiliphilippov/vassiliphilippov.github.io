auctioneer

noun
person who conducts an auction
Pronunciation: / ˌɔkʃəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌɔːkʃəˈnɪə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to conduct an auction, selling goods or property to the highest bidder

Examples

A fast-talking auctioneer
As the auctioneer spoke rapidly, the bidders signaled their offers.
The auctioneer raised his hammer and called out the next item for sale.
The auctioneer sold the antique vase to the highest bidder in just a few minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auction - a public sale in which goods or property are sold to the highest bidder
  • antonym: bidder - a person who offers to pay a certain amount at an auction
