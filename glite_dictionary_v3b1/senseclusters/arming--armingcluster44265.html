arming

noun
equipping with weapons
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɑːmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of equipping people or forces with weapons, especially in preparation for conflict or war

Examples

The arming of soldiers
International talks focused on the arming and disarmament of the region.
The country's rapid arming has raised concerns among its neighbors.
The general ordered the arming of all available troops before the battle.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disarming - the act of taking away weapons or making someone less able to use weapons
