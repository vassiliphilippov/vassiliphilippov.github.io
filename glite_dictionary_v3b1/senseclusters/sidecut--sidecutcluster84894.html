sidecut

noun
haircut with short sides
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdkʌt / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdkʌt / (BrE)
a haircut or hairstyle where the sides of the head are cut shorter than the top or one side is cut shorter than the other

Examples

Trendy sidecut hairstyle
He decided to get a sidecut before starting his new job.
The sidecut has become a popular choice among young men in recent years.
She styled her sidecut with a bit of gel for a modern look.
She decided to get a bold sidecut before starting college.
His sidecut made him stand out at the music festival.
The stylist recommended a sidecut for a modern, edgy look.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bob - a short haircut for women, typically straight around the head at jaw-level
  • antonym: buzz cut - a very short haircut where all the hair is clipped close to the scalp
  • synonym: undercut - a hairstyle with the sides and back cut very short and the top left longer
