nothin

noun
informal 'nothing'
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌθɪn / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪn / (BrE)
an informal or nonstandard way of saying 'nothing', meaning not anything or zero.

Examples

Nothin to eat
He looked in the fridge and found nothin to eat.
After all that work, I got nothin in return.
She said there was nothin she could do to help.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nothing - not anything; zero
  • antonym: something - an unspecified thing; a thing of some kind
