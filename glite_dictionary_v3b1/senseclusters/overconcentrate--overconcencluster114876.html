overconcentrate

verb
to make too strong
7.6 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Àåo äv…ôrÀàk…ëÀêns…ônÀåtre…™t / (AmE), / Àå…ô äv…ôk…ínÀàs…õntre…™t / (BrE)
to make something too concentrated, especially by adding too much of a substance or reducing volume excessively

Examples

To overconcentrate a solution
If you overconcentrate the juice, it will taste too strong and may be unpleasant to drink.
Be careful not to overconcentrate the cleaning solution, as it could damage surfaces.
The chemist warned that overconcentrating the sample could affect the accuracy of the results.

Synonyms

  • oversaturate
    6.7 0.00/m
    oversaturate means adding so much substance that a mixture cannot take more, while overconcentrate also covers reducing volume to make something too strong.
  • supersaturate
    8.2 0.09/m
    supersaturate describes a liquid holding more dissolved substance than normal, while overconcentrate can mean any excess by adding or reducing volume.

Related Terms
