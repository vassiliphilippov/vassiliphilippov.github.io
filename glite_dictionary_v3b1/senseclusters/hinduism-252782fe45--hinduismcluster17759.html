Hinduism

noun
major religion of India; belief in reincarnation and caste
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪn.duˌɪ.zəm /; / ˈhɪn.duˌɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈhɪn.dʒuː.ɪ.zəm /; / ˈhɪn.djuːˌɪz.əm / (BrE)
a major religion and cultural tradition of South Asia, especially India, with beliefs in reincarnation, many gods, and spiritual practices

Examples

Hinduism religion beliefs
Hinduism teaches the concept of reincarnation and karma.
He studied the sacred texts of Hinduism during his time in India.
Many followers of Hinduism celebrate Diwali, the festival of lights.
Many festivals celebrated in India have their roots in Hinduism.
Rituals and traditions from Hinduism are observed in many South Asian countries.
She decided to study Hinduism as part of her comparative religion course.
