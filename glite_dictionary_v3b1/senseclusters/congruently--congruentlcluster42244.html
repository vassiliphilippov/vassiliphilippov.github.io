congruently

adverb
in agreement
5.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈɡruːəntli / (AmE), / kɒnˈɡruːəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is in agreement or matches in form, character, or function

Examples

Congruently written reports
The two reports were written congruently, making it easy to compare their findings.
Her actions and beliefs aligned congruently with the values of the organization.
The design elements fit together congruently to create a harmonious appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accordingly - in a way that is appropriate to the particular circumstances
  • synonym: consistently - in a way that always behaves or appears the same
  • antonym: incongruently - in a way that does not match or agree
