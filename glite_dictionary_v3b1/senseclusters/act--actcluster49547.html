act

noun
formal written law by a legislature
Pronunciation: / ækt / (AmE), / ækt / (BrE)
A statute or written law formally passed by a legislative body, especially a parliament or congress.

Examples

A new act of parliament
Parliament enacted a new act to improve data protection.
The Clean Air Act was passed to regulate air pollution nationwide.
The senator referred to the Act during her speech on legal reforms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: statute - a written law passed by a legislative body
