waste time

verb
spend time on unimportant things
1.0 1.4
Pronunciation: /weɪst taɪm/ (AmE), /weɪst taɪm/ (BrE)
to spend time doing things that are not useful or important, especially when you could be doing something more productive

Examples

To waste time watching TV

We can't afford to waste time if we want to meet the deadline.
