coxswain

noun
person who steers a boat or racing crew
9.9 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːks.ən/ /; / /ˈkɑːks.weɪn/ (AmE), /ˈkɒks.ən/ /; / /ˈkɒks.weɪn/ (BrE)
The person who steers and directs a ship's boat or a racing crew, especially in rowing.
