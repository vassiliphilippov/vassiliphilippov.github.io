dramatic work

noun
writing intended for performance
1.0
Pronunciation: / drəˈmæt̬ɪk wɝːk / (AmE), / drəˈmætɪk wɜːk / (BrE)
a piece of writing created for performance on stage, television, or in a movie, such as a play or screenplay

Examples

A dramatic work script
Many famous movies are based on classic dramatic works originally written for the stage.
She spent months writing her latest dramatic work for the university theater.
The festival featured a new dramatic work that explored themes of family and identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: musical - a play or movie in which singing and dancing play an essential part
  • synonym: play - a written work intended for theatrical performance
  • synonym: screenplay - a script written for a film or television program
