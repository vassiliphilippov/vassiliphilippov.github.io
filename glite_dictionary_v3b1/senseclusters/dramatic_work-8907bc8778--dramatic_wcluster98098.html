dramatic work

noun
writing intended for performance
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /drə.ˈmæt.ɪk wɜːrk/ (AmE), /drə.ˈmæt.ɪk wɜːk/ (BrE)
a piece of writing created for performance on stage, television, or in a movie, such as a play or screenplay

Examples

The festival featured a new dramatic work that explored themes of family and identity.
