baking

noun
cooking by dry oven heat
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or activity of cooking food (such as bread, cakes, or pastries) by dry heat, especially in an oven

Examples

Home baking
Modern baking techniques allow for healthier homemade treats.
She enjoys baking as a weekend hobby, especially making cakes and pastries.
The baking of fresh bread filled the house with a wonderful aroma.
His favorite hobby is baking, especially making sourdough bread.
She spent the afternoon baking for her family.
The kitchen was filled with the delicious smell of baking.
