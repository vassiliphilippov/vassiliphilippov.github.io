contraceptive method

noun
technique or device to prevent pregnancy
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.trəˈsɛp.tɪv ˈmɛθ.əd / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.trəˈsɛp.tɪv ˈmɛθ.əd / (BrE)
a technique or device used to prevent pregnancy, such as condoms, intrauterine devices, or birth control pills

Examples

Many contraceptive methods also help prevent the transmission of certain diseases.
