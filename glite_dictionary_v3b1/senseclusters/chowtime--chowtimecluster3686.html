chowtime

noun
mealtime (slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaʊtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈtʃaʊtaɪm / (BrE)
a slang term for the time when a meal is served or it is time to eat

Examples

Chowtime is announced
The soldiers lined up quickly when the sergeant announced it was chowtime.
After a long hike, everyone was excited for chowtime at the campsite.
Grandpa always calls out chowtime when dinner is ready.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dinnertime - the time when dinner is served or eaten
  • synonym: mealtime - the time at which a meal is usually eaten
