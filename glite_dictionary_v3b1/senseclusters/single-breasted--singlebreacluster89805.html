single-breasted

adjective
having one row of buttons (in clothing)
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪŋɡəlˈbrɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋɡlˈbrɛstɪd / (BrE)
(of a jacket, coat, or similar garment) having a narrow overlap and fastened with a single row of buttons.

Examples

A single-breasted jacket
He wore a classic single-breasted suit to the interview.
Her choice was a navy single-breasted coat, perfect for the winter.
This single-breasted blazer gives a modern and streamlined appearance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: double-breasted - (of a garment) having a wider overlap with two parallel rows of buttons
