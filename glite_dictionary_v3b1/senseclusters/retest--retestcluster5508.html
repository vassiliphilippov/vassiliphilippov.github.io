retest

noun
second test
2.9 1.80/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåriÀêÀàt…õst / (AmE), / ÀåriÀêÀàt…õst / (BrE)
a second or repeated test given after the first attempt, usually to improve a result or confirm findings

Examples

A retest opportunity
If you do not pass the first exam, you will have a chance to take a retest.
The laboratory performed a retest to confirm the accuracy of the initial results.
After failing the driving test, she scheduled a retest for the following month.
