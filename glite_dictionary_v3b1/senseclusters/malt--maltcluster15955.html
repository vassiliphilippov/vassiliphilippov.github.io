malt

noun
milkshake with malted milk powder
Pronunciation: / mɔlt / (AmE), / mɔːlt / (BrE)
A milkshake made with added malted milk powder, often giving it a richer flavor and a slightly grainy texture.

Examples

A chocolate malt
Every Saturday, we ordered a chocolate malt at the local diner.
He prefers a malt to a regular milkshake because of the richer taste.
The classic malt is made by blending ice cream, milk, and malt powder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: milkshake - a sweet drink made by blending milk, ice cream, and flavoring
