unmeltable

adjective
cannot be melted
5.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɛltəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɛltəbl / (BrE)
not able to be melted by heat or high temperature

Examples

Unmeltable metal
The new alloy is completely unmeltable under normal industrial conditions.
Scientists searched for an unmeltable substance to use in the spacecraft's heat shield.
Even after hours in the furnace, the material remained unmeltable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heat-resistant - able to resist damage from high temperatures
  • antonym: meltable - able to be melted by heat
