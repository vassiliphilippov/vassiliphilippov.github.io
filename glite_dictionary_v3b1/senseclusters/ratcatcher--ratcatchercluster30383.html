ratcatcher

noun
informal hunting attire
Pronunciation: / ˈrætˌkætʃər / (AmE), / ˈrætˌkætʃə / (BrE)
informal hunting clothes, typically a tweed jacket, breeches, and boots, worn for fox-hunting or beagling

Examples

Traditional ratcatcher outfit
He arrived at the meet wearing a traditional ratcatcher, complete with tweed jacket and leather boots.
The club requires all new members to wear a ratcatcher for their first hunt.
She admired the classic look of his ratcatcher as he prepared for the beagling event.
