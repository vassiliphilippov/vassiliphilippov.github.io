trenching spade

noun
narrow-bladed shovel for digging trenches
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛntʃɪŋ speɪd / (AmE), / ˈtrɛn.tʃɪŋ speɪd / (BrE)
a narrow-bladed hand shovel used by infantrymen for digging trenches

Examples

A trenching spade tool
He cleaned the mud from his trenching spade after a long day fortifying the camp.
Many World War I infantrymen carried a trenching spade as standard equipment.
The soldier used his trenching spade to dig a shallow foxhole during the night patrol.
