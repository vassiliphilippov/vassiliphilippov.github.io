intermural

adjective
between institutions (e.g. competitions)
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmjʊrəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmjʊərəl / (BrE)
Occurring between two or more institutions, especially educational or sporting competitions.

Examples

An intermural competition
An intermural chess competition was held between local colleges last month.
Our school participates in several intermural athletic leagues each year.
The universities organized an intermural debate tournament to foster academic exchange.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intramural - Occurring within a single institution, especially in sports or activities
