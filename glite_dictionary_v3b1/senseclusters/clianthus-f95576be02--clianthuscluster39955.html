Clianthus

noun
genus of Australasian flowering shrubs or vines
Pronunciation: / kliˈænθəs / (AmE), / klaɪˈænθəs / (BrE)
A genus of semi-prostrate shrubs or vines native to Australasia, known for their striking flowers.
