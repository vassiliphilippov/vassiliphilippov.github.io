shibori

noun
Japanese dyeing technique
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃɪˈbɔːri / (AmE), / ʃɪˈbɔːri / (BrE)
a Japanese technique of dyeing fabric by folding, twisting, or binding it to produce decorative patterns

Examples

Shibori dyeing technique
The artisan demonstrated the shibori process to a group of interested students.
She wore a beautiful scarf decorated with traditional shibori patterns.
Many modern designers incorporate shibori techniques into their textile collections.

Related Terms

  • synonym: batik - a technique of wax-resist dyeing applied to fabric
  • synonym: tie-dye - a method of dyeing fabric by tying it to create patterns
