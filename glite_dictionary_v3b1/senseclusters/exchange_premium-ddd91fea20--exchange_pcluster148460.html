exchange premium

noun
fee for exchanging currencies
6.3 5.9
Pronunciation: / ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ ˈpriːmiəm / (AmE), / ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ ˈpriːmiəm / (BrE)
