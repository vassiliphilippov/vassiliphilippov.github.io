medicalize

verb
to treat as medical issue
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪkəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəlaɪz / (BrE)
to treat or view something (such as a condition, behavior, or experience) as a medical issue that needs diagnosis or treatment
