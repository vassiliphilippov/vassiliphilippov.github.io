munchy

adjective
slightly hungry
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌntʃi / (AmE), / ˈmʌntʃi / (BrE)
feeling slightly hungry or having a strong desire to eat snacks, especially between meals

Examples

Feeling munchy
I always get munchy in the afternoon and look for something to snack on.
After the movie, everyone felt munchy and ordered a pizza to share.
She was a bit munchy before dinner, so she grabbed a handful of nuts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: full - having eaten enough food
  • degree higher: hungry - needing or wanting food
  • synonym: peckish - slightly hungry
