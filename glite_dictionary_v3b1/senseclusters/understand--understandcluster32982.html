understand

verb
perceive meaning or show sympathy
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɚˈstænd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈstænd / (BrE)
to perceive the meaning, significance, or nature of something; to be sympathetic or tolerant towards someone's feelings or actions

Examples

Understand other people
He always tries to understand other people's problems before judging them.
It's important to understand how she feels in this situation.
You don't need to explain — I understand!

Related Terms

  • synonym: comprehend - grasp mentally; fully understand
  • antonym: misunderstand - fail to understand correctly
