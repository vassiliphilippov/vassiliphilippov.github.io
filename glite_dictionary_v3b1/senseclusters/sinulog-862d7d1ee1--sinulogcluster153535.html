Sinulog

noun
Philippine festival dance
9.9
Pronunciation: / siˈnuːlɔg / (AmE), / siˈnuːlɒg / (BrE)
a traditional Philippine dance performed in honor of the Santo Niño, featuring a distinctive forward-and-back two-step movement

Examples

Traditional Sinulog dance
Every January, thousands of people gather in Cebu to watch the colorful Sinulog parade.
The students learned the steps of the Sinulog for their cultural presentation at school.
Tourists were amazed by the energy and rhythm of the Sinulog dancers during the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: festival - a day or period of celebration, often with cultural performances
