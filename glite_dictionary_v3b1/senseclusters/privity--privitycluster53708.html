privity

noun
privacy or secrecy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪvəti / (AmE), / ˈprɪvɪti / (BrE)
the state of being private or secret, especially regarding confidential matters or relationships

Examples

In strict privity
The negotiations were conducted in strict privity to protect sensitive information.
She valued privity when discussing personal matters with her closest friends.
The contract was signed in privity, with only the involved parties present.

Related Terms

  • synonym: privacy - the state of being free from public attention
  • antonym: publicity - the state of being public or widely known
  • synonym: secrecy - the action of keeping something secret or hidden
