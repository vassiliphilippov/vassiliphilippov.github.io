cringingly

adverb
embarrassingly
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪndʒɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkrɪndʒɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is embarrassing, awkward, or causes discomfort (either for oneself or others)

Examples

The comedian cringingly recalled his most embarrassing moment on stage.
