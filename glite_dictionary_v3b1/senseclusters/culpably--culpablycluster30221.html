culpably

adverb
in a way deserving blame
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlpəbli / (AmE), / ˈkʌlpəbli / (BrE)
In a manner deserving blame or censure; blameworthily.

Examples

To culpably overlook evidence
Officials admitted they had culpably overlooked crucial evidence in the case.
She acted culpably by ignoring repeated warnings about the risks.
The company was found culpably negligent in failing to provide safety equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blameworthily - in a way that is deserving of blame
  • antonym: innocently - in a way that is free from blame
