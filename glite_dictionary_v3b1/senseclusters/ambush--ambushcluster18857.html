Synonyms

  • ambuscade
    9.9 0.00/m
    ambuscade is a formal or old-fashioned military word for a hidden attack, while ambush is the common everyday term.
  • bushwhack
    9.0 0.08/m
    bushwhack is a sudden surprise attack often in rough woodland, while ambush covers any attack from a hidden position.
  • lie in wait
    6.8 0.32/m
    lie in wait means hiding and waiting to attack, while ambush names the sudden attack from a hidden place.
  • waylay
    8.6 0.13/m
    waylay means to stop or meet someone unexpectedly, often to rob or talk, while ambush means attack from a concealed place.
