piney

adjective
smelling of pine
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪni / (AmE), / ˈpaɪni / (BrE)
having the smell, taste, or qualities of pine trees

Examples

Piney scent
The soap left a pleasant piney scent in the bathroom after use.
We walked through a piney forest that smelled fresh and clean.
Her candle collection includes a piney fragrance perfect for winter evenings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resinous - having the smell or qualities of resin
  • synonym: woody - having the characteristics or smell of wood
