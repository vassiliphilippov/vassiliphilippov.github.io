dialect atlas

noun
map of dialect features
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəˌlɛkt ˈætləs / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəlɛkt ˈætləs / (BrE)
An atlas that maps the geographic distribution of distinctive linguistic features or dialects in a language.

Examples

A dialect atlas
A dialect atlas is an important resource for studying language change over time.
Researchers used a dialect atlas to compare vocabulary usage in rural areas.
The dialect atlas revealed striking differences in pronunciation across regions.
