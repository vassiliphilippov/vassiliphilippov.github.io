good-bye

noun
a farewell remark or act
Pronunciation: / ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (AmE), / ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (BrE)
an instance of saying farewell when leaving

Examples

Say good-bye
He didn't get the chance to say good-bye to his friends before moving away.
She waved and said a quick good-bye before leaving for the airport.
The party ended with hugs and tearful good-byes at the door.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farewell - an act of parting or of marking someone's departure
  • antonym: hello - a greeting used when meeting someone
