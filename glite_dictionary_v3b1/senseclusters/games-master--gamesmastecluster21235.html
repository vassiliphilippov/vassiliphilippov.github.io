games-master

noun
teacher in charge of school sports and games
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪmz ˌmæs.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmz ˌmɑː.stə / (BrE)
a teacher responsible for organizing and supervising sports and games at a school, especially in British schools.

Examples

The games-master supervises football
After school, the games-master supervised the boys during football practice on the field.
During assembly, the games-master announced the winners of the inter-house sports competition.
The games-master organized the annual athletics meet with great enthusiasm.

  • synonym: games-mistress - female teacher in charge of games at a school (British)
