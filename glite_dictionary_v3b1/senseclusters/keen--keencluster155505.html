keen

adjective
sharply perceptive or discerning
3.6
Pronunciation: / kin / (AmE), / kiːn / (BrE)
having or showing a sharp or quick perception; able to notice and distinguish subtle details

Examples

Keen eye
His keen observations during the meeting impressed his colleagues.
She has a keen understanding of human behavior, always sensing motives others miss.
The detective's keen mind quickly unraveled the complicated mystery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acute - having a perceptive intellect or sharpness of mind
  • antonym: dull - lacking sharpness of intellect or perception
  • synonym: incisive - showing clear and direct insight
