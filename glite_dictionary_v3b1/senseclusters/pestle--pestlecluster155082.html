pestle

verb
grind or crush in a mortar
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛsəl / (AmE), / ˈpɛsəl / (BrE)
to crush, grind, or pulverize (a substance) in a mortar, usually with a pestle tool

Examples

To pestle spices
She carefully pestled the spices in the mortar until they turned to powder.
The recipe calls for you to pestle the garlic before adding it to the sauce.
They spent the afternoon pestling herbs to make a medicinal paste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grind - to reduce to small particles by crushing
  • synonym: pulverize - to crush into a powder or dust
