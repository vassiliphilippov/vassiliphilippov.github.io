éclair

noun
flash of lightning
9.9
Pronunciation: / eɪˈklɛr / (AmE), / eɪˈklɛə / (BrE)
a flash of lightning or a sudden bright flash of light, used in literary or rare contexts

Examples

A bright éclair
For a moment a single éclair divided the sky and showed the distant hills clearly.
The poet described the storm as a night filled with éclairs and thunder.
An éclair illuminated the landscape, revealing the old castle on the hill.

Related Terms

  • antonym: darkness - the absence of light
  • synonym: lightning - a sudden electrical discharge in the atmosphere
