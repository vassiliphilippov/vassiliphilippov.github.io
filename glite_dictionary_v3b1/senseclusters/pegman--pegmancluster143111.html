pegman

noun
mapping software figure
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛɡˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈpɛɡˌmæn / (BrE)
a small human-shaped figure used in mapping software to represent the user, especially for entering Street View mode

Examples

The pegman icon
To explore the neighborhood, drag the pegman onto the street you want to view.
The pegman icon appears in the corner of the map, ready to be used for Street View.
She clicked on the pegman to see a real-world image of the location.

Related Terms

  • synonym: avatar - a graphical representation of a user in digital environments
