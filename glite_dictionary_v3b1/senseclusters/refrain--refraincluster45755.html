Synonyms

  • eschew
    8.9 3.33/m
    eschew is a formal choice to avoid something often for moral reasons, while refrain is a general act of holding back.
  • abstain
    5.0 1.98/m
    abstain means choosing not to do or consume something, often for health or rules, while refrain covers any holding back.
  • forbear
    8.1 0.31/m
    forbear shows patient self-control in not speaking or acting, while refrain is a general deliberate hold on action or speech.
  • avoid
    1.0 152/m
    avoid means keep away from something or stop it happening, while refrain focuses on stopping oneself from doing something.
