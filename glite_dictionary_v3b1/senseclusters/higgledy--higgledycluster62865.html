higgledy

adverb
messily or disorderly
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪɡəldi / (AmE), / ˈhɪɡəldi / (BrE)
in a messy, disorganized, or jumbled manner

Examples

Stacked higgledy on shelf
The books were stacked higgledy on the shelf, making it hard to find anything.
After the party, shoes and bags were left higgledy around the entrance.
She packed her suitcase higgledy, not caring about order or arrangement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chaotically - in a completely disordered or confused way
  • antonym: neatly - in an orderly or tidy way
