nonideological

adjective
not based on ideology
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːnaɪdiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnaɪdiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
not influenced by or based on any particular set of beliefs or ideology

Examples

Nonideological decisions
The judge was praised for her nonideological decisions in the courtroom.
They wanted a nonideological discussion focused only on the facts.
His nonideological approach helped bring people with different views together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impartial - not favoring one side or group over another
