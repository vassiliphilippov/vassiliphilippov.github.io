wha

interjection
informal exclamation
Pronunciation: / wɑ / (AmE), / wɒ / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to express surprise, confusion, or to ask someone to repeat what they said

Examples

Say wha again
He looked at the strange device and said, "wha? How does this even work?"
"Wha? I thought the meeting was tomorrow, not today!"
When she heard the unexpected news, she could only respond with a surprised "wha?"

Related Terms

  • synonym: huh - an exclamation expressing confusion or asking for repetition
  • synonym: what - used to ask for information or clarification
