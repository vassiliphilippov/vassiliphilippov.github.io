incursion

noun
sudden attack into enemy territory
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɝːʒən / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɜːʃən / (BrE)
a sudden or brief attack into enemy territory, especially to make a raid or foray

Examples

Military incursion
The border guards responded quickly to the incursion by enemy troops.
The general planned a surprise incursion deep into hostile land.
There was an incursion into the disputed territory at dawn yesterday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invasion - an instance of invading a country or region with armed force
  • synonym: raid - a sudden attack, especially one by police or soldiers
  • antonym: retreat - the act of withdrawing from enemy forces as a result of their superior power or after a defeat
