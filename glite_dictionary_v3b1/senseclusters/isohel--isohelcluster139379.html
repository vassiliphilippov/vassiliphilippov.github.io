isohel

noun
line connecting points of equal sunshine
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsoʊˌhɛl / (AmE), / ˈaɪsəʊˌhɛl / (BrE)
A line drawn on a map connecting points that receive equal amounts of sunshine during a given period.

Examples

An isohel line
Drawing an isohel helps scientists analyze sunlight patterns over large geographic areas.
The isohels showed that the southern coast had more sunshine than the northern inland regions.
The meteorological map displayed an isohel indicating regions that received similar hours of sunshine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: isobar - a line connecting points of equal atmospheric pressure
  • synonym: isotherm - a line connecting points of equal temperature
