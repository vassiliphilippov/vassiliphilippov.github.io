kyphoscoliosis

noun
spinal curvature disorder
9.9
Pronunciation: /kaɪ.foʊ.skoʊ.lɪ.ˈoʊ.sɪs/ (AmE), /kaɪ.fəʊ.skəʊ.lɪ.ˈəʊ.sɪs/ (BrE)
a medical condition in which the spine has abnormal curvature in both the side-to-side and front-to-back directions

Examples

Severe kyphoscoliosis curvature
The X-ray revealed that the patient had severe kyphoscoliosis affecting her posture.
Children with kyphoscoliosis may require special braces or surgery to correct their spine.
Physical therapy can help manage symptoms of kyphoscoliosis and improve mobility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kyphosis - excessive outward curvature of the spine
  • synonym: scoliosis - sideways curvature of the spine
