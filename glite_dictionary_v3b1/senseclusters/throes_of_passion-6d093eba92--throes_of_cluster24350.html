throes of passion

noun
intense feelings of love or desire
Pronunciation: / θroʊz əv ˈpæʃ.ən / (AmE), / θrəʊz əv ˈpæʃ.ən / (BrE)
a state of intense and overwhelming feelings of love or desire

Examples

In deep throes of passion
Caught in the throes of passion, they forgot about everyone else in the room.
He acted impulsively, driven by the throes of passion that overwhelmed him.
The novel describes a character lost in the throes of passion for her lover.
