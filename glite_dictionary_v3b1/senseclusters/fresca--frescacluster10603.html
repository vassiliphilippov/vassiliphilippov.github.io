fresca

noun
citrus soft drink
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛskə / (AmE), / ˈfrɛskə / (BrE)
a brand of citrus-flavored soft drink, especially popular in the United States

Examples

A cold fresca
On a hot summer afternoon, nothing is more refreshing than a cold fresca.
She brought a bottle of fresca to the picnic for everyone to share.
Many people enjoy drinking fresca as a low-calorie alternative to other sodas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soda - a sweet, carbonated soft drink
