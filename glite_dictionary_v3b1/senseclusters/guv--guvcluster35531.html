guv

noun
British respectful address
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡʌv / (AmE), / ɡʌv / (BrE)
an informal British term used to address a man, especially one in authority or as a sign of respect

Examples

Excuse me, guv
Excuse me, guv, could you tell me the way to the station?
The taxi driver greeted his passenger with a friendly 'Morning, guv!'
You dropped your wallet, guv, thought you might want it back.

Related Terms

  • synonym: governor - a person in authority, especially a boss or superior
  • synonym: sir - a polite or respectful way of addressing a man
