filament

noun
plastic feedstock for 3D printers
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪləmənt / (AmE), / ˈfɪləmənt / (BrE)
a length of thermoplastic material, usually wound on a spool, used as feedstock in consumer 3D printers (commonly PLA, ABS, PETG).

Examples

3D printer filament
The designer printed a prototype using PLA filament in a desktop 3D printer.
She replaced the empty spool and loaded new filament before starting the long print job.
Different materials require different temperatures, so choose filament suitable for your printer and model.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feedstock - material supplied to a machine or process for conversion (in this sense, used for 3D printing).
