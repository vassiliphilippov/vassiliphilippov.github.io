recursively

adverb
by repeated self-reference
7.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɜrsɪvli / (AmE), / rɪˈkɜːsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that involves repeating a process by referring back to itself, especially in mathematics or computing

Examples

To recursively call functions
The program sorts the list recursively by dividing it into smaller parts.
You can solve the puzzle recursively by breaking it down into simpler versions of itself.
The definition is applied recursively until the simplest case is reached.
