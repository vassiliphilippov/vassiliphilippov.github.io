sugarhouse

noun
sugar processing building
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʊɡɚˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡəˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a building or factory where sugar is processed or refined from sugarcane or sugar beets

Examples

Old sugarhouse factory
The old sugarhouse at the edge of town is now a museum about the sugar industry.
Workers loaded sacks of raw sugar into the sugarhouse for processing.
During the harvest season, the sugarhouse operates day and night to refine sugarcane.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mill - a building equipped for processing raw materials
  • synonym: refinery - a factory where substances are made pure
