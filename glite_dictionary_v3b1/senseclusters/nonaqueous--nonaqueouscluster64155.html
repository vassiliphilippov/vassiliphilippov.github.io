nonaqueous

adjective
without water
Pronunciation: / nɒnˈeɪkwiəs / (AmE), / nɒnˈeɪkwɪəs / (BrE)
not containing water or not using water as a solvent

Examples

Nonaqueous solution
The chemist prepared a nonaqueous solution to avoid any reaction with water.
Some batteries use nonaqueous electrolytes for better performance.
A nonaqueous solvent was chosen to dissolve the compound safely.
