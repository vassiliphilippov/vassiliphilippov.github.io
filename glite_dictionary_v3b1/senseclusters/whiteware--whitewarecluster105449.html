whiteware

noun
white ceramics or appliances
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtˌweə / (BrE)
ceramic ware that is undecorated and white, or large household appliances such as refrigerators and washing machines

Examples

Unpainted whiteware pottery
The pottery shop displayed beautiful whiteware before it was painted or glazed.
Many kitchens are equipped with whiteware like dishwashers and refrigerators.
She collects antique whiteware because of its simple and elegant appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appliance - a device or machine used in the home, such as a refrigerator or washing machine
  • synonym: ceramics - objects made from clay and hardened by heat
