demi-glace

noun
French brown sauce
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛmiˈɡlɑs / (AmE), / ˌdɛmiˈɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a rich brown sauce in French cuisine made by reducing stock and sauce until thick and flavorful

Examples

Rich demi-glace sauce
The chef finished the dish with a spoonful of demi-glace for extra depth of flavor.
A classic French steak is often served with demi-glace on the side.
He learned to make demi-glace during his culinary training in Paris.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gravy - a sauce made from meat juices, often served with meat
