amphictyony

noun
ancient Greek religious association of states
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /æm.ˈfɪk.tiː.ə.niː/ /; / /æm.ˈfɪk.tɪ.ə.niː/ (AmE), /æm.ˈfɪk.tiː.ə.niː/ /; / /æm.ˈfɪk.tɪ.ə.niː/ (BrE)
