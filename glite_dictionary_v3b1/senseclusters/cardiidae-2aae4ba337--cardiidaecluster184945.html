Cardiidae

noun
family of heart-shaped bivalve mollusks
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːrˈdiːɪˌdiː /; / ˌkɑr.diˈaɪ.di.i / (AmE), / ˌkɑːdɪˈɪːdiː /; / ˌkɑː.dɪˈaɪ.di.iː / (BrE)
a family of bivalve mollusks, commonly called cockles, with heart-shaped shells that burrow in sand
