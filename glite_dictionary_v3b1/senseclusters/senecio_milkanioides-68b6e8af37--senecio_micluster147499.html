Senecio milkanioides

noun
South African succulent climber; houseplant
Pronunciation: / səˈniːsi.oʊ mɪlˌkæniˈɔɪdiːz / (AmE), / sɪˈniːsi.əʊ mɪlˌkaniˈɔɪdiːz / (BrE)
A South African succulent evergreen climber with yellow flowers, often grown as a houseplant for its foliage; sometimes classified in the genus Senecio.
