hillbilly heroin

noun
slang term for prescription opioids
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪlˌbɪli ˈhɛroʊɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɪlˌbɪli ˈhɛrəʊɪn / (BrE)
A slang term for prescription opioids, especially oxycodone, used in reference to their high potential for abuse.

Examples

Hillbilly heroin abuse
Authorities seized a shipment of hillbilly heroin disguised as prescription medication.
Some communities have been devastated by the spread of hillbilly heroin addiction.
The doctor warned about the dangers of abusing hillbilly heroin after seeing a rise in overdose cases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heroin - an illegal and highly addictive opioid drug
  • synonym: oxycodone - a powerful prescription opioid painkiller
