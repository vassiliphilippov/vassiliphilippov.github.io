down

noun
(US football) a play to advance the ball
5.6 5.8
Pronunciation: /daʊn/ (AmE), /daʊn/ (BrE)
(in American football) a unit of play beginning with the snap of the ball and ending when the play is complete, used to attempt to advance the ball.

Examples

First down
He was tackled after just one down, losing yards for his side.
If they don't advance ten yards in four downs, they lose possession.
The team gained a first down on their third play.
