daidzein

noun
soy isoflavone compound
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪdzin /; / ˈdaɪdˌziːn / (AmE), / ˈdaɪdzeɪn /; / ˈdaɪdˌziːn / (BrE)
a natural compound (isoflavone) found in soybeans and other plants, often studied for its health effects
