Bob Marley

proper noun
Jamaican reggae singer and icon
2.1
Pronunciation: / bɑb ˈmɑrli / (AmE), / bɒb ˈmɑːli / (BrE)
(1945–1981) A Jamaican singer, songwriter, and musician who popularized reggae music worldwide and became an international symbol of Rastafarian culture and social change.
