owling

noun
nocturnal wakefulness
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
the habit of staying awake and active during the night, especially when others are asleep

Examples

Late-night owling
His weekend owling left him exhausted for morning lectures all week.
She blamed her constant owling for her lack of energy during the day.
After years of owling, he found it hard to adjust to a regular schedule.

Related Terms

  • antonym: early riser - a person who wakes up early in the morning
  • synonym: insomnia - difficulty falling or staying asleep
  • synonym: night owl - a person who prefers to be active late at night
