tantamount

adjective
equivalent in seriousness or effect
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtæn.təˌmaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈtan.tə.maʊnt / (BrE)
(predicative) Equivalent in seriousness or effect to; virtually the same as.

Examples

Tantamount to lying
Calling her a liar is tantamount to accusing her of fraud.
His refusal to answer was tantamount to an admission of guilt.
In many cultures, breaking a promise is tantamount to dishonor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: equivalent - equal in value, amount, function, meaning, etc.
