fatwa

noun
Islamic legal ruling
Pronunciation: / ˈfæt.wɑ / (AmE), / ˈfæt.wɑː / (BrE)
A legal ruling or formal pronouncement on a point of Islamic law given by a recognized religious authority.

Examples

A fatwa ruling
Many misconceptions surround the true nature of a fatwa in Islamic societies.
The controversial fatwa attracted international attention and debate.
The religious leader issued a fatwa prohibiting certain financial practices.
A controversial fatwah sparked international debate and protests.
Journalists explained the meaning of a fatwah to readers unfamiliar with Islamic law.
The cleric issued a fatwah regarding acceptable business practices for Muslims.
