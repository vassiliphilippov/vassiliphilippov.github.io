greenstick

adjective
partial bone fracture
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgriːn.stɪk/ (AmE), /ˈgriːn.stɪk/ (BrE)
describing a type of bone fracture in which the bone bends and cracks without breaking completely

Examples

Greenstick fracture in children
The doctor explained that a greenstick fracture is more common in children than in adults.
After the fall, X-rays showed a greenstick break in her forearm.
Treatment for a greenstick fracture usually involves immobilizing the bone with a cast.
