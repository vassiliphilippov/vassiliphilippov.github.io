gigger

noun
aquatic animal hunter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈɡɪɡə / (BrE)
a person who hunts aquatic animals, such as frogs or fish, using a gig, a multi-pronged spear

Examples

A gigger with a spear
The experienced gigger moved quietly through the shallow water, searching for frogs at dusk.
Each spring, local giggers gather at the river to hunt with their handmade spears.
He learned the skills of a gigger from his grandfather, who taught him how to use the gig.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fisher - a person who catches fish for a living or as a hobby
  • synonym: hunter - a person who hunts wild animals
