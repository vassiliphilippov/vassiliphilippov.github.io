lookee

verb
to look playfully
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊki / (AmE), / ˈlʊki / (BrE)
to look or see, used playfully or informally to draw attention to something.

Examples

To lookee at that
Lookee at all the stars in the sky tonight!
Grandpa pointed and said, 'lookee what I found in the garden!'
If you lookee over there, you can see the parade coming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: look - to direct your eyes in order to see something
  • synonym: see - to notice or become aware of something by using your eyes
