deskman

noun
office or hotel worker
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛskˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈdɛskˌmæn / (BrE)
a person who works at a desk, especially in an office or at a hotel reception

Examples

Hotel deskman
The deskman answered the phone and directed the call to the manager.
At the hotel, the deskman provided guests with maps and local information.
She asked the deskman for help with her reservation when she arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clerk - an employee who performs administrative or office tasks
  • synonym: receptionist - a person who greets and assists visitors at a front desk
