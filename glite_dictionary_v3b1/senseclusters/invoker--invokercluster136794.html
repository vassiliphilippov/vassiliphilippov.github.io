invoker

noun
one who calls upon
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvoʊkər / (AmE), / ɪnˈvəʊkə / (BrE)
a person or thing that calls upon or brings something into action, especially in a ritual, ceremonial, or general context

The software invoker triggers the process whenever a new file is detected.
