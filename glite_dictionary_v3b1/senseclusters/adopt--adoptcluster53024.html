adopt

verb
adapt a work for the stage or screen
Pronunciation: / əˈdɑpt / (AmE), / əˈdɒpt / (BrE)
to adapt a work, such as a book, for presentation in another dramatic form, such as a play or screenplay

Examples

To adopt a book
She was hired to adopt the memoir into a stage production for local theaters.
The director hopes to adopt several classic stories as short films in her next project.
The studio plans to adopt the bestselling novel for the big screen next year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adapt - to modify something for a new use, notably to turn a work into another form, such as a play or screenplay
