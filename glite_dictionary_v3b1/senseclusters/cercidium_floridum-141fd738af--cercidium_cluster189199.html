Cercidium floridum

noun
a spiny southwestern US tree with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / sərˈsɪdiəm flɔːˈrɪdəm / (AmE), / səˈsɪdɪəm flɒˈrɪdəm / (BrE)
a spiny, densely branched tree native to the southwestern United States, known for its showy yellow flowers and blue-green bark; also called blue palo verde
