clubbable

adjective
sociable; fit for club membership
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌb.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈklʌb.ə.bl̩ / (BrE)
(especially of a person) sociable and well-suited to membership in a social club; inclined to enjoy the company of others in clubs or groups.

Examples

A clubbable person
Being clubbable was almost a requirement for entering the city's elite circles.
He is such a clubbable fellow that everyone enjoys inviting him for dinner.
Her clubbable nature made her popular among members of the committee.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unsociable - not enjoying or seeking the company of others
