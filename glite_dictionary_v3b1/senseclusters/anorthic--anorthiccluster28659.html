anorthic

adjective
having three unequal crystal axes at oblique angles
5.7
Pronunciation: / əˈnɔrθɪk / (AmE), / əˈnɔːθɪk / (BrE)
(of a crystal system) having three unequal crystal axes that intersect at oblique angles; triclinic.

Examples

Anorthic crystal system
Scientists studied the anorthic form to understand the mineral's properties.
The mineral crystallized in an anorthic system, making its structure complex to analyze.
Unlike cubic crystals, anorthic crystals lack axes of equal length or right angles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: triclinic - having three unequal crystal axes all at oblique angles
