fortifier

noun
strengthening agent
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrtɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a person or substance that makes something stronger or more effective

Examples

A fortifier substance
Iron is a common fortifier added to breakfast cereals to improve their nutritional value.
The new coach acted as a fortifier for the team's confidence and morale.
Adding extra insulation serves as a fortifier against cold weather in older houses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reinforcer - something that increases strength or effectiveness
  • synonym: strengthener - something that makes something stronger
  • antonym: weaken - to make something less strong
