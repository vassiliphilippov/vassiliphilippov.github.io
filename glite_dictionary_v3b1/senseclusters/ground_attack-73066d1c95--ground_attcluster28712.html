ground attack

noun
military attack by ground troops
4.7 3.5
Pronunciation: /graʊnd ə.ˈtæk/ (AmE), /graʊnd ə.ˈtæk/ (BrE)
a military attack carried out by or involving ground troops, as opposed to forces operating in the air or at sea

Examples

A ground attack launched
After weeks of bombing, the army launched a coordinated ground attack on the enemy positions.
Military analysts anticipated a ground attack following the air strikes.
The ground attack began at dawn with thousands of troops advancing across the border.
