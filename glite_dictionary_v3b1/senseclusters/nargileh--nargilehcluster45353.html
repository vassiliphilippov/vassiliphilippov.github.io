nargileh

noun
oriental tobacco water pipe
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːrɡəleɪ / (AmE), / ˈnɑːɡɪleɪ / (BrE)
an oriental tobacco water pipe with a long flexible tube, used for smoking flavored tobacco, where the smoke passes through water before being inhaled

Examples

A nargileh pipe
He bought a beautifully decorated nargileh as a souvenir from his trip to Turkey.
In Istanbul, it's common to see people relaxing with a nargileh after dinner.
They spent the evening smoking nargileh and chatting at the café.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shisha - a water pipe for smoking, especially in Middle Eastern countries
