citron tree

noun
tree with large lemonlike fruits, thick rind
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪtrən tri / (AmE), / ˈsɪtrən triː / (BrE)
A small, thorny evergreen tree or shrub native to India, widely cultivated for its large lemonlike fruits with thick, warty rind.

Examples

The citron tree fruit
In ancient times, the citron tree was highly valued for its fragrant fruits.
The citron tree is known for its distinctive thick, warty fruit rind.
They planted a citron tree in the botanical garden to add to their citrus collection.
