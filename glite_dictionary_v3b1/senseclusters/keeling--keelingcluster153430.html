keeling

noun
capsizing of a boat
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the action or process of a boat or ship turning over or capsizing onto its side

Examples

Sudden keeling of a boat
The sudden keeling of the fishing boat sent gear and people sliding toward the rail.
Heavy winds caused the yacht's keeling during the storm, alarming everyone on board.
After the keeling of the canoe, the crew quickly worked to right it and continue their journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capsizing - the act of a boat overturning in the water
  • antonym: righting - the act of returning a boat to an upright position
