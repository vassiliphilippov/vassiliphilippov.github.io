ascendance

noun
state of dominance or control
3.6
Pronunciation: / əˈsændəns / (AmE), / əˈsændəns / (BrE)
the state of having power, influence, or control over others

Examples

Political ascendance
After years of political struggle, his ascendance within the party became clear to everyone.
Her ascendance to a leadership role was the result of hard work and determination.
The company's rapid ascendance in the market surprised its competitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dominance - the state of being dominant or in control
  • antonym: subservience - the state of being submissive or controlled by others
