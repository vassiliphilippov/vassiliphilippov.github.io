boil over

verb
overflow while boiling
Pronunciation: / bɔɪl ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / bɔɪl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
(of a liquid) to overflow or cause to overflow the container while boiling.

Examples

Soup boils over
Be careful or the soup will boil over and make a mess on the stove.
If you leave the kettle unattended, it might boil over.
The pot boiled over and the water spilled onto the counter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overflow - to flow over the brim or edge
