alternating

adjective
reversing direction (of electric current)
Pronunciation: / ˈɔltɚˌneɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɔːltəneɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an electric current) repeatedly reversing direction at regular intervals.

Examples

Alternating current
An inverter can convert direct current into alternating current for household use.
Power lines typically carry alternating current over long distances.
The device is designed to operate with an alternating current supply.

Related Terms

  • antonym: direct - (of electric current) flowing in one direction only
