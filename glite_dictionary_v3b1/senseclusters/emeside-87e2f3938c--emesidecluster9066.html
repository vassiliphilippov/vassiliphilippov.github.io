Emeside

noun
anticonvulsant drug for epilepsy
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmɪsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈɛmɪsaɪd / (BrE)
A trademarked anticonvulsant drug (also known as ethosuximide) used to treat petit mal epilepsy.

Examples

Emeside medicine
Children with absence epilepsy are often treated with Emeside.
Side effects of Emeside should be discussed with your healthcare provider.
The doctor prescribed Emeside to help control her seizures.
