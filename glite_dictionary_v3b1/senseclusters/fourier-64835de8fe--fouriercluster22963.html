Fourier

proper noun
French mathematician
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊrieɪ / (AmE), / ˈfʊərieɪ / (BrE)
Joseph Fourier (1768–1830), a French mathematician and physicist known for developing mathematical methods such as Fourier analysis, including Fourier series and transforms
