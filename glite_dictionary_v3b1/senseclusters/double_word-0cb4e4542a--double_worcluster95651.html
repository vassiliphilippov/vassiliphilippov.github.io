double word

noun
bonus game square
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl wɝd / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl wɜːd / (BrE)
a square or space in a word game (like Scrabble) that doubles the score of a word placed on it

Examples

Double word square
She scored extra points by placing her tiles on a double word square.
Landing on a double word space can change the outcome of the game.
He planned his move carefully to take advantage of the double word bonus.
If you place your tiles on a double word square in Scrabble, your score for that word is doubled.
In some computer systems, a double word refers to a data unit twice the size of a standard word.
Landing on a double word square can change the outcome of a board game.

  • degree higher: triple word - a square in a word game that triples the value of a word
