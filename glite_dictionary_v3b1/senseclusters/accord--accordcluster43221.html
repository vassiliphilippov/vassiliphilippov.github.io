accord

verb
to give or grant (formally)
Pronunciation: / əˈkɔrd / (AmE), / əˈkɔːd / (BrE)
to give or grant someone power, status, or recognition; to allow or bestow

Examples

To accord respect
Equal rights should be accorded to all citizens regardless of their background.
Her achievements were accorded much recognition by her colleagues.
The committee accorded him the honor of leading the ceremony.
