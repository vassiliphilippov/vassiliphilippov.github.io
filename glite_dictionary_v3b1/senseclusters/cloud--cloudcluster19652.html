cloud

noun
visible mass of particles in air
1.0 9.74/m
Pronunciation: / klaÊŠd / (AmE), / klaÊŠd / (BrE)
a large group or mass of small things, such as dust, smoke, insects, or blossoms, floating or moving together

Examples

Cloud of dust

The garden was filled with a cloud of white blossoms in the spring.
