trigger

noun
lever that fires a gun
4.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtrɪg.ər/ (AmE), /ˈtrɪg.ə/ (BrE)
a small lever or device that you press or pull to start a machine or make a gun fire

Examples

Gun trigger
Always keep your finger off the trigger until you are ready to shoot.
He squeezed the trigger and the gun fired.
The detective examined the trigger for fingerprints.
Smoke set off the alarm's trigger, causing the system to activate.
Squeezing the trigger releases the spring and fires the projectile.
The safety mechanism prevents the trigger from being pulled accidentally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lever - a rigid bar used to exert a force
  • antonym: safety catch - a device that prevents accidental firing
  • synonym: switch - a device for making and breaking the connection in an electrical circuit
