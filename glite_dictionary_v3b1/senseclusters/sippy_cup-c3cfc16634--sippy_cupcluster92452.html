sippy cup

noun
child's spill-proof cup
2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪp.i kʌp/ /; / /ˈsɪp.iː kʌp/ (AmE), /ˈsɪp.i kʌp/ /; / /ˈsɪp.iː kʌp/ (BrE)
a small, spill-proof cup with a lid and spout, usually for young children but sometimes used by adults (often humorously)

Examples

Toddler's sippy cup
The toddler carried her sippy cup everywhere she went.
He poured juice into the sippy cup to avoid any mess at breakfast.
After switching to a sippy cup, their son stopped spilling milk on the floor.
He carried his coffee in a sippy cup during the long commute to avoid spills.
At the office, she joked about needing a sippy cup for her morning tea.
The insulated sippy cup kept his drink hot and prevented any mess in the car.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bottle - a container with a narrow neck used for holding liquids, often for babies
  • degree higher: mug - a large cup with a handle, used for drinking hot liquids
  • synonym: training cup - a cup designed to help children transition from bottles to regular cups
  • synonym: travel mug - a portable insulated cup for hot drinks
  • synonym: tumbler - a drinking glass or cup without a handle
