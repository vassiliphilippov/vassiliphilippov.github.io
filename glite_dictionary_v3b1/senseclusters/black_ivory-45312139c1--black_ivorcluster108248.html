black ivory

noun
rare, luxury coffee processed by elephants
3.6
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈaɪvəri / (AmE), / blak ˈaɪvəri / (BrE)
a rare and expensive coffee made from beans eaten and excreted by elephants, noted for its unique flavor
