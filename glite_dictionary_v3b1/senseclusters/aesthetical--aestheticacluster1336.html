aesthetical

adjective
relating to beauty or good taste
Pronunciation: / ɛsˈθɛtɪkəl / (AmE), / iːsˈθɛtɪkəl / (BrE)
concerned with or relating to the appreciation of beauty or good taste.

Examples

An aesthetical sense
Her aesthetical sensibility influenced the way she arranged the flowers.
The new gallery embraces an aesthetical approach to interior design.
This building stands out due to its aesthetical harmony with the environment.
