cold as ice

adjective
emotionally unfeeling; showing no warmth
Pronunciation: / koʊld əz aɪs / (AmE), / kəʊld əz aɪs / (BrE)
emotionally unfeeling or lacking kindness; showing no warmth or compassion

Examples

As cold as ice
After the argument, her tone was as cold as ice when she spoke to me.
He stared at me with a cold as ice expression, betraying no emotion.
The manager's response to the complaint was cold as ice and utterly dismissive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold-hearted - lacking kindness or compassion
  • antonym: warm-hearted - kind and sympathetic
