noncompeting

adjective
not in competition
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəmˈpitɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəmˈpiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
not competing or not in competition with others

Examples

Noncompeting businesses
The two organizations are noncompeting and serve different customer bases.
He chose a noncompeting field to avoid direct rivalry with his friends.
The agreement ensures that both parties remain noncompeting in the same market.

Related Terms

  • antonym: competitive - involving or characterized by competition
  • synonym: cooperative - working together rather than competing
