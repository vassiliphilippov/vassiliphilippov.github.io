entertained

adjective
pleasantly occupied or amused
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɛntərˈteɪnd / (AmE), / ˌɛntəˈteɪnd / (BrE)
Feeling pleasantly occupied with amusement or enjoyment, often as a result of a performance, activity, or distraction.

Examples

To stay entertained
During the long flight, I stayed entertained by watching movies on my tablet.
She felt entertained throughout the entire concert and didn’t lose interest once.
The children were entertained by the magician’s tricks for over an hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amused - experiencing enjoyment, especially in a light or playful way
  • antonym: bored - feeling weary and uninterested
