grow

verb
to cultivate or raise (plants, crops)
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊ / (AmE), / ɡrəʊ / (BrE)
to cultivate or produce (plants, crops, etc.) by caring for them

Examples

To grow vegetables
She decided to grow her own food to eat healthier.
The farmer worked hard to grow enough wheat for the season.
We grow tomatoes and herbs in our garden every summer.
Farmers grow wheat and corn in this region every year.
That family has grown olives on their land for generations.
We grow organic vegetables in our backyard garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cultivate - to prepare and use land for growing plants or crops
  • synonym: raise - to look after and protect plants or animals so that they grow
