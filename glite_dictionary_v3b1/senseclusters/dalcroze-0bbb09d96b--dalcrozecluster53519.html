Dalcroze

proper noun
Swiss music educator
Pronunciation: / dalˈkroʊz / (AmE), / dalˈkrəʊz / (BrE)
Émile Jaques-Dalcroze, a Swiss composer and music educator known for developing eurhythmics, a method of teaching music through movement.

Examples

Dalcroze the educator
Many music teachers study the methods of Dalcroze to incorporate movement into their lessons.
The influence of Dalcroze can be seen in modern music education programs around the world.
Students enjoyed learning rhythm through the innovative techniques introduced by Dalcroze.
