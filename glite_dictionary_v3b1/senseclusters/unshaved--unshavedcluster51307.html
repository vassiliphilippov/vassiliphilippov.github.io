unshaved

adjective
not shaved; with hair left growing
1.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃeɪvd / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃeɪvd / (BrE)
not having been shaved; with hair, especially facial hair, left growing

Examples

An unshaved face
He looked in the mirror and saw his unshaved chin for the first time in years.
His unshaved face gave him a rugged appearance.
She noticed that everyone at the meeting was unshaved after the long weekend.
