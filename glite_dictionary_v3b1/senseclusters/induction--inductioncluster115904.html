induction

noun
formal entry into an organization or position
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʌk.ʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌk.ʃən / (BrE)
the formal process of admitting someone into an organization, position, or office

Examples

Induction ceremony
Her induction into the Sports Hall of Fame was a proud moment for her family.
Military induction requires recruits to attend an orientation and training session.
The company's annual induction ceremony welcomed several new employees to the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: initiation - formal admission or introduction, especially into an organization
  • synonym: installment - the act of installing someone in an office or position
