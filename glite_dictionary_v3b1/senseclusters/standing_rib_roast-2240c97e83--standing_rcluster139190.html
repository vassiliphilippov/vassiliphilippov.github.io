standing rib roast

noun
a cut of beef or venison with several ribs, roasted upright
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstændɪŋ rɪb roʊst / (AmE), / ˈstændɪŋ rɪb rəʊst / (BrE)
a cut of beef or venison comprising several ribs with the meat attached along the outside of the ribs, typically roasted upright for cooking

Examples

A standing rib roast dinner
A perfectly cooked standing rib roast makes an impressive centerpiece for any special occasion.
The butcher recommended roasting the standing rib roast with fresh herbs and garlic.
We served a delicious standing rib roast for our holiday dinner last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prime rib - a popular term for a standing rib roast, especially when referring to beef
