yellowish-orange

adjective
orange tinged with yellow
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊɪʃ ˈɔrɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊɪʃ ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
having an orange color with a tinge of yellow; resembling the color between yellow and orange.

Examples

A yellowish-orange scarf
She wore a yellowish-orange scarf that brightened her entire outfit.
The sunset cast a stunning yellowish-orange glow over the landscape.
The walls were painted a warm yellowish-orange to create a cozy atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: orange - a bright reddish-yellow color
  • synonym: yellow-orange - a color between yellow and orange
