dirty-faced

adjective
having a face that is dirty
Pronunciation: / ˈdɝːti feɪst / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti feɪst / (BrE)
having a face smeared with dirt or grime; with a dirty face

Examples

Dirty-faced children

She handed a handkerchief to the dirty-faced boy standing at the door.
The dirty-faced children played happily in the muddy yard.
