upload

verb
transfer a mind into a digital system
8.6
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u028cp\u02cclo\u028ad / (AmE), / \u02c8\u028cp\u02ccl\u0259\u028ad / (BrE)
(in science fiction and theoretical discussions) to transfer or copy a person's mind, consciousness, or mental content into a digital system or virtual environment.

Examples

To upload a mind
Some futurists imagine humans could upload their minds into a computer simulation.
After the accident she hoped scientists might one day have a way to upload her consciousness.
Researchers discuss the ethical issues raised by uploading a person's memories into a virtual environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mind uploading - the theoretical process of copying or emulating a human mind in a computer
  • synonym: whole brain emulation - a proposed technique for replicating the structure and function of a brain in software
