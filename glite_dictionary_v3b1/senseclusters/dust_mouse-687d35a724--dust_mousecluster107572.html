dust mouse

noun
small clump of dust and debris
7.6
Pronunciation: / dʌst maʊs / (AmE), / dʌst maʊs / (BrE)
a small ball or clump of dust, lint, hair, and debris that collects on floors, especially under furniture

Examples

A dust mouse on floor
A dust mouse drifted across the hardwood floor whenever the door slammed.
The cat chased a dust mouse under the coffee table, thinking it was real prey.
While cleaning, she discovered a dust mouse hiding behind the radiator.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dust bunny - a small ball of dust and lint found indoors
