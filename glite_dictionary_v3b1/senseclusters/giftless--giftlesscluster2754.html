giftless

adjective
without talent
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪftləs / (AmE), / ˈɡɪftləs / (BrE)
lacking natural talent or special ability

Examples

Giftless musician
Although he was giftless in sports, he always tried his best on the field.
The giftless student struggled with the advanced math problems but never gave up.
She felt giftless compared to her talented siblings, but she found joy in her own hobbies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gifted - having natural talent or special ability
  • synonym: talentless - having no natural talent or skill
