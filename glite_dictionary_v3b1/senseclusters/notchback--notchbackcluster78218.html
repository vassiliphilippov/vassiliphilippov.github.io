notchback

adjective
car with stepped trunk
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑtʃˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈnɒtʃˌbæk / (BrE)
having a car body style with a clearly defined step between the roof and the trunk, as opposed to a smooth slope.

Examples

Notchback car design
The new model features a notchback design that appeals to traditional car buyers.
She chose the notchback version for its classic appearance and spacious trunk.
Many drivers prefer a notchback because it offers a clear separation between the cabin and the trunk.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fastback - a car with a continuous slope from the roof to the rear
  • synonym: sedan - a car with a separate trunk and passenger compartment
