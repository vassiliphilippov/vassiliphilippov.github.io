nervus

noun
vein in leaf or wing
Pronunciation: / ˈnɜrvəs / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvəs / (BrE)
a vein or rib in a leaf or the wing of an insect, serving as a supporting or vascular ridge

Examples

Leaf nervus
The entomologist identified each nervus on the dragonfly's delicate wing.
A botanist studies the pattern of every nervus in the leaf to classify the plant.
Under the microscope, the nervus structure in the butterfly's wing became clear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nervure - a vein in the wing of an insect
  • synonym: vein - a thin line or rib in a leaf or wing
