disestablish

verb
deprive a church of official state status
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪs.ɪˈstæb.lɪʃ /; / ˌdɪsɪˈstæblɪʃ / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.ɪˈstæb.lɪʃ /; / ˌdɪsɪˈstæblɪʃ / (BrE)
to remove the official status or government support of a church or other organization

Examples
