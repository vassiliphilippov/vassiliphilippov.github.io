exhaustiveness

noun
thoroughness; completeness
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɔstɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːstɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being thorough and including all possible details or aspects

Examples

The exhaustiveness of report
The exhaustiveness of the report ensured that no important information was overlooked.
We appreciated the exhaustiveness of her answers during the interview.
His research is known for its exhaustiveness and attention to detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: completeness - the state of having all necessary parts or elements
  • antonym: superficiality - the quality of not being thorough or detailed
  • synonym: thoroughness - the quality of being very careful and complete
