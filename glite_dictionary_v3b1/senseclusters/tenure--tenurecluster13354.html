Examples

Land tenure
After centuries, the system of land tenure in Europe has fundamentally changed.

Synonyms

  • land tenure
    6.7 0.67/m
    land tenure is the customary legal system that decides who may use and occupy fields, farms, or houses, while tenure names that right.
  • landownership
    4.7 0.16/m
    landownership means full legal ownership and duties over a property, while tenure can mean different holding arrangements without full ownership.
  • property right
    4.6 7.33/m
    property right is a general legal claim to possess, use, or sell goods or land, while tenure focuses on how land is held or occupied.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: leasehold - the holding of property by lease
