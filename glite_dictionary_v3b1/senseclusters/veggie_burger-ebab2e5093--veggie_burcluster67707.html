veggie burger

noun
hamburger with a vegetable-based patty
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒi ˌbɝːɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒi ˌbɜːɡə / (BrE)
A hamburger-style sandwich with a patty made from vegetables or plant-based ingredients instead of meat.

Examples

A veggie burger sandwich
I usually order a veggie burger when I go out for lunch.
She cooked delicious veggie burgers on the grill for her friends.
The restaurant offers a veggie burger as a healthy alternative to beef.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hamburger - a sandwich with a cooked ground beef patty in a bun
