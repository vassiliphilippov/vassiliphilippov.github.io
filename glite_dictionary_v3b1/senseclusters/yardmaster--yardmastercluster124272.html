yardmaster

noun
person managing a railway yard
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑrdˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈjɑːdˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person responsible for managing the operations of a railway yard

Examples

The yardmaster manages trains
A yardmaster must stay alert to keep the switching operations running smoothly.
After years of experience, she was promoted to yardmaster at the central depot.
The yardmaster coordinated the movement of trains and cargo to prevent delays.
