cross bun

noun
sweet spiced bun with a cross on top
4.1
Pronunciation: / krɑs bʌn / (AmE), / krɒs bʌn / (BrE)
A moderately sweet, spiced raised roll typically containing raisins or currants, sometimes citron, and decorated with a cross of icing or glaze on top.

Examples

A cross bun with icing
My grandmother always bakes cross buns for Good Friday each year.
The scent of warm, spiced cross buns filled the kitchen.
You can buy cross buns fresh from the bakery every spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hot cross bun - a spiced sweet bun, usually eaten at Easter, marked with a cross on top
