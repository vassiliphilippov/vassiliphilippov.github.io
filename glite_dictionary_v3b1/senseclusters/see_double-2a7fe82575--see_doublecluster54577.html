see double

verb
perceive two images of an object
4.2
Pronunciation: / si ˈdʌbəl / (AmE), / siː ˈdʌbəl / (BrE)
to perceive two images of one object instead of one, usually due to a medical condition or temporary disturbance of vision

Examples

To see double images
After hitting his head, he began to see double and felt dizzy.
If you see double frequently, you should consult an eye specialist.
The medication made her see double for several minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diplopia - the medical term for seeing double; double vision
