take-up

noun
rate or level of adoption
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪk ʌp / (AmE), / ˈteɪk ʌp / (BrE)
the rate or extent to which people accept, adopt, or begin to use a product, service, scheme, or policy.

Examples

High take-up rate
Government reports show rising take-up of the renewable-energy scheme among households.
Early take-up of the new commuter pass was strongest among young professionals in the city.
Analysts measured customer take-up after the promotion to forecast future subscription revenue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adoption - the process of beginning to use or accept a new product, idea, or policy.
  • synonym: uptake - the act of taking up or using something; level of adoption or acceptance.
