principally

adverb
mainly; chiefly
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪnsəpəli / (AmE), / ˈprɪnsɪpəli / (BrE)
mainly; for the most part; chiefly.

Examples

Directed principally at education
Our efforts should be directed principally at improving education.
She is known principally for her work in environmental science.
The new park was created principally for children and families.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chiefly - primarily; mostly
  • synonym: mainly - mostly; for the most part
