complete and utter disaster

noun
total failure or catastrophe
Pronunciation: / kəmˈplit ənd ˈʌtər dɪˈzæstər / (AmE), / kəmˈpliːt ənd ˈʌtə dɪˈzɑːstə / (BrE)
A situation that is an absolute failure or catastrophe in every respect.

Examples

A complete and utter disaster
His attempt to fix the computer was a complete and utter disaster, and it won't turn on now.
The meeting became a complete and utter disaster after everyone started arguing.
The party they organized turned into a complete and utter disaster when the venue flooded.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catastrophe - a sudden and widespread disaster or failure
  • synonym: fiasco - a complete and embarrassing failure
