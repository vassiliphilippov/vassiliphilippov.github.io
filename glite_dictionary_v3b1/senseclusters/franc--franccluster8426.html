franc

noun
former currency of France and others
5.8 5.2
Pronunciation: /fræŋk/ (AmE), /fræŋk/ (BrE)
the former basic monetary unit of France and several other countries, equal to 100 centimes

Examples

Swiss franc
Before the euro, people in Switzerland paid in francs for most purchases.
She kept an old five franc coin as a souvenir from her trip to Paris.
The price of coffee in Cameroon is listed in Central African francs.
