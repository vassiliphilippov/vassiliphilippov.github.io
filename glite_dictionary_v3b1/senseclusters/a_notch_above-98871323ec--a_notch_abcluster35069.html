a notch above

adjective
slightly superior in quality or status
Pronunciation: / ə nɑtʃ əˈbʌv / (AmE), / ə nɒtʃ əˈbʌv / (BrE)
Slightly superior in quality, status, or rank compared to someone or something else.

Examples

A notch above others
Despite the competition, this restaurant is a notch above the rest in service and taste.
Her work ethic is a notch above what we usually expect.
This model is a notch above its predecessor in terms of performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: a cut above - one step or level better than others
