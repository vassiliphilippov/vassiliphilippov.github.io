literality

noun
quality of being literal
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪtəˈræləti / (AmE), / ˌlɪtəˈræləti / (BrE)
the quality of being literal, exact, or true to the actual meaning of words or facts

Examples

The literality of words
Her literality sometimes caused confusion when people used sarcasm around her.
The literality of the translation made the story difficult to enjoy.
Children often interpret instructions with strict literality and may miss implied meanings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: literalness - the state of being literal or exact
