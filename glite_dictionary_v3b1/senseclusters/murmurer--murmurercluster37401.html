murmurer

noun
person who speaks softly and indistinctly
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝː.mɚ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɜː.mər.ə / (BrE)
a person who speaks in a soft, indistinct voice, often making it difficult to understand what is being said

Examples

A murmurer speaks softly
As a habitual murmurer, he was often asked to repeat himself.
In the crowded library, a quiet murmurer disturbed the silence more than a loud talker would have.
She could barely hear the murmurer at the other end of the hallway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whisperer - a person who speaks in a quiet, hushed voice
