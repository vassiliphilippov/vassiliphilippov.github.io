sack

noun
strong dry Spanish white wine (historical)
8.8
Pronunciation: / sæk / (AmE), / sæk / (BrE)
a light, dry, strong white wine from Spain or the Canary Islands, especially popular in the 16th and 17th centuries; includes sherry
