efflorescent

adjective
budding or bursting into flower
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɛfləˈrɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌɛfləˈrɛsənt / (BrE)
(of a plant or bud) bursting into flower; in the process of blooming.

Examples

An efflorescent plant
Every year, the efflorescent cherry blossoms attract thousands of visitors to the park.
In the garden, scores of efflorescent roses filled the air with their scent.
The magnolia tree was efflorescent after the first warm days of spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blooming - in flower; blossoming
  • antonym: withered - dried up; no longer fresh or alive
