unsurpassable

adjective
impossible to be exceeded
7.5
Pronunciation: / ʌn.sɚˈpæs.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌn.səˈpɑː.sə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to be bettered or exceeded; unmatched or supreme in quality or achievement.

Examples

An unsurpassable standard
He set an unsurpassable standard of excellence that others strive to reach.
Her unsurpassable talent on the piano impressed everyone at the concert.
The team's record for most wins in a season remains unsurpassable to this day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incomparable - so good or impressive as to have no equal
  • antonym: surpassable - able to be exceeded or bettered
  • synonym: unbeatable - impossible to defeat or surpass
