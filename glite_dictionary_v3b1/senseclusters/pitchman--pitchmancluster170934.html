pitchman

noun
an aggressive salesman; showy product promoter
4.9 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪtʃ.mən/ /; / /ˈpɪtʃ.mæn/ (AmE), /ˈpɪtʃ.mən/ /; / /ˈpɪtʃ.mæn/ (BrE)
a man who sells products by giving lively, persuasive sales talks, often in public places like fairs or on television
