breadnut

noun
tropical food tree
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛdˌnʌt / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdˌnʌt / (BrE)
a tropical tree whose edible seeds are used as food in various regions

Examples

Breadnut tree
The breadnut is valued for its nutritious seeds in many tropical regions.
Farmers harvest breadnut seeds to use in traditional dishes and snacks.
In some countries, the breadnut tree is also planted to prevent soil erosion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breadfruit - a tropical tree with starchy edible fruit
  • synonym: chestnut - an edible nut from a deciduous tree
