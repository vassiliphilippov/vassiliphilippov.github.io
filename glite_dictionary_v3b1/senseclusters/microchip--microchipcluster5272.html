Synonyms

  • chip

    chip is the short, common word for a small semiconductor part used in devices, while microchip sounds more technical and specific.
  • integrated circuit
    4.2 0.7/m
    integrated circuit is the technical term for an electronic circuit built on a single chip, while microchip refers to the physical small silicon piece.
  • microcircuit
