microchip

noun
integrated circuit on a small silicon chip
4.9 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ.tʃɪp/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ.tʃɪp/ (BrE)
a very small piece of silicon containing an integrated circuit, used in computers, electronics, or for identification
