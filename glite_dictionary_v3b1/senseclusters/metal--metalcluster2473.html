metal

verb
to surface a road
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtəl / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəl / (BrE)
to cover or surface a road with broken stone or similar hard material to make it suitable for vehicles.

Examples

To metal a road
The rural road was metalled to allow cars to pass even during the rainy season.
Workers metal the paths in the park to make them easier to walk on.
After the repairs, the city council decided to metal the entire street for better durability.
