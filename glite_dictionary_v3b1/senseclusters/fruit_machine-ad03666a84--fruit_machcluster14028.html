fruit machine

noun
British slot machine; gambling device with fruit symbols
8.5
Pronunciation: / fruːt məˈʃin / (AmE), / fruːt məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A coin-operated gambling machine with rotating reels displaying fruit symbols, where certain combinations result in winning money; chiefly used in British English.

Examples

The fruit machine pays
He won twenty pounds after playing the fruit machine for just five minutes.
She put a coin into the fruit machine and hoped for a winning combination.
The pub had several old fruit machines lined up against the wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slot machine - American term for a similar gambling machine
