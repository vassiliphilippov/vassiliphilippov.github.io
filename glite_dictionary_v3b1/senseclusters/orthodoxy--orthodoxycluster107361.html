orthodoxy

noun
traditional or established beliefs
6.8 7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrθəˌdɑksi /; / ˈɔr.θəˌdɑk.si / (AmE), / ˈɔːθədɒksi /; / ˈɔː.θəˌdɒk.si / (BrE)
beliefs or practices that are traditional or generally accepted, especially in religion or other fields

Examples
