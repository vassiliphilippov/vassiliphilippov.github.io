Rasta

noun
follower of Rastafarianism
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈræstə / (AmE), / ˈrɑːstə / (BrE)
A person who follows Rastafarianism, a religious and social movement that originated in Jamaica in the 1930s.

Examples

A Rasta person
Many young people were inspired by the local Rasta community and their music.
She learned a lot about Jamaican culture from her friend, who is a devout Rasta.
The Rasta wore dreadlocks and spoke passionately about peace and unity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Rastafarian - A follower of Rastafarianism; sometimes used interchangeably with 'Rasta'
