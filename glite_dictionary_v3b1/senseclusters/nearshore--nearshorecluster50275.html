nearshore

adjective
close to shore
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪrˌʃɔr / (AmE), / ˈnɪəˌʃɔː / (BrE)
located or occurring close to the shore or coastline

Examples

Nearshore waters
Many fish species thrive in the nearshore environment where the water is shallow.
The nearshore area is often used for recreational activities like kayaking and paddleboarding.
Scientists study the nearshore ecosystem to understand how pollution affects marine life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coastal - relating to or near a coast
  • antonym: offshore - located or happening away from the shore
