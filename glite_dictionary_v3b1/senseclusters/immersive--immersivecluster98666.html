immersive

adjective
deeply engaging
5.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɜrsɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːsɪv / (BrE)
deeply engaging and making you feel as if you are part of an experience or environment

Examples

An immersive experience
The museum offers an immersive exhibit that lets visitors experience life in ancient Rome.
Virtual reality technology creates an immersive environment for learning and entertainment.
She found the novel so immersive that she finished it in one sitting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absorbing - completely holding your attention
  • antonym: distracting - taking attention away from something
  • synonym: engaging - holding your attention and interest
