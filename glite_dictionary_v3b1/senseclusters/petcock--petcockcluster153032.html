petcock

noun
small valve or drain faucet
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛtˌkɑk / (AmE), / ˈpɛt.kɒk / (BrE)
a small valve or faucet for draining liquids or releasing air from a vessel, pipe, or engine

Examples

The petcock valve
Before changing the coolant, make sure to open the petcock at the bottom of the radiator.
Please close the petcock tightly after draining the oil.
The mechanic released air from the tank by turning the petcock.
