royally

adverb
in a grand or royal manner
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔɪ.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪ.ə.li / (BrE)
In a manner befitting or characteristic of royalty; in a grand, splendid, or generous way.

Examples

To be royally annoyed
She was royally annoyed by the unexpected interruption during her speech.
The guests were royally entertained at the palace banquet.
They were royally welcomed by their friends after returning home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magnificently - in a splendid, impressive, or grand way
  • antonym: meanly - in a poor or ungenerous manner
