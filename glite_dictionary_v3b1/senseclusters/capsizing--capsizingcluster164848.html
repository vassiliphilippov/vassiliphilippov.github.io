capsizing

noun
the overturning of a boat or ship in water
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.saɪ.zɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkap.saɪ.zɪŋ / (BrE)
(nautical) the event of a boat or ship accidentally turning over in the water.

Examples

Boat capsizing accident
After the capsizing, all passengers were quickly rescued by the nearby crew.
Proper weight distribution can help prevent the capsizing of a vessel.
The sudden storm led to the capsizing of several small fishing boats.
