Amanita caesarea

noun
an edible mushroom species (Caesar's mushroom)
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌæməˈniːtə siˈzɛəriə / (AmE), / ˌaməˈniːtə siːˈzɛːrɪə / (BrE)
A widely distributed edible mushroom species, also known as Caesar's mushroom, resembling the fly agaric but safe to eat.

Examples

The Amanita caesarea mushroom
Chefs in Italy often prepare dishes featuring Amanita caesarea during mushroom season.
The Amanita caesarea is highly prized by mushroom hunters in Mediterranean regions.
Unlike its toxic relatives, Amanita caesarea is safe to eat when properly identified.

Related Terms
