dispatch

noun
promptness and efficiency
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspætʃ / (AmE), / dɪˈspatʃ /; / dɪˈspætʃ / (BrE)
promptness and efficiency in carrying out a task or duty

Examples

Work done with dispatch
Orders are processed and shipped out with speed and dispatch.
The project was completed with great dispatch and drew praise from the client.
They handled the emergency with remarkable dispatch and efficiency.
The officers moved with despatch to coordinate the emergency response.
The secretary handled all requests with remarkable despatch.
We expect all orders to be processed with despatch during the busy season.
