unstarched

adjective
not stiffened with starch
4.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɑrtʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɑːtʃt / (BrE)
not treated with starch, especially referring to fabric or clothing that remains soft and flexible

Examples

Unstarched cotton shirt
He preferred to wear unstarched shirts for comfort during the summer.
The tablecloth felt soft and unstarched after being washed.
Her unstarched dress gave her a relaxed and casual appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soft - having a smooth or gentle texture; not stiff
  • antonym: starched - treated with starch to make fabric stiff
