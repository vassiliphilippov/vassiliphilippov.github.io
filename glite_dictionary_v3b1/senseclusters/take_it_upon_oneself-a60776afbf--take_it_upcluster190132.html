take it upon oneself

verb
to assume responsibility voluntarily
Pronunciation: / teɪk ɪt əˈpɑn wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / teɪk ɪt əˈpɒn wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to decide to do something or take responsibility for it without being asked or told to do so

Examples

To take it upon oneself to act
After noticing the mess, she took it upon herself to clean the kitchen before anyone arrived.
No one asked him, but he took it upon himself to call every guest and confirm their attendance.
Realizing there was a problem, I took it upon myself to find a solution for the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: undertake - to accept responsibility for doing something
  • synonym: volunteer - to offer to do something by choice
