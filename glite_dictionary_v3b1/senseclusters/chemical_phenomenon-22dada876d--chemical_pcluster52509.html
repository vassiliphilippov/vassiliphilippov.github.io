chemical phenomenon

noun
natural event involving chemistry
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛmɪkəl fəˈnɑːmɪnən / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl fəˈnɒmɪnən / (BrE)
a natural event or process that involves changes in the composition or properties of substances due to chemistry, such as reactions or molecular transformations

Examples

A chemical phenomenon reaction
Combustion is a rapid chemical phenomenon that releases energy in the form of heat and light.
Photosynthesis is a complex chemical phenomenon occurring in green plants.
Rusting is a classic example of a chemical phenomenon resulting from the reaction of iron and oxygen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemical reaction - a process in which substances change into different substances
