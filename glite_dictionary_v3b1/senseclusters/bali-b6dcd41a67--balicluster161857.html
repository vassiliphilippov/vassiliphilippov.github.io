Bali

proper noun
Indonesian island known for culture and scenery
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːli / (AmE), / ˈbɑːli / (BrE)
an island province of Indonesia east of Java, known for its volcanic landscapes, rich culture, traditional dances, and handicrafts

Examples

Bali island province
