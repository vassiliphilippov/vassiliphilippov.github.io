Jirga

noun
Pashtun elders' decision-making assembly
9.9 9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪrɡə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪːɡə / (BrE)
A traditional assembly of male elders in Pashtun communities that makes decisions on local disputes and governance, especially in Afghanistan and Pakistan.

Examples

A Jirga assembly
In some rural regions, important community matters are still decided by a Jirga.
The tribal elders gathered for a Jirga to resolve the land dispute.
Women's participation in a Jirga is traditionally limited in many areas.
