farm building

noun
building on a farm for agriculture
7.0
Pronunciation: / fɑrm ˈbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / fɑːm ˈbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
a building situated on a farm and used for agricultural purposes, such as barns, stables, or sheds

Examples

A farm building on field
A red farm building stood at the edge of the wheat field.
The old farm building was converted into a cozy guesthouse.
They plan to restore several historic farm buildings on the property.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barn - a large farm building for storing crops or housing animals
  • synonym: stable - a building on a farm for housing horses or other livestock
