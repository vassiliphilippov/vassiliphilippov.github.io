black day

noun
South Korean singles' observance (April 14)
Pronunciation: / blæk deɪ / (AmE), / blæk deɪ / (BrE)
A South Korean informal observance held on April 14 when single people gather and eat jajangmyeon (black-bean-sauce noodles) to acknowledge their single status.

Examples

Korean black day noodles
In Seoul, many students observe black day by sharing jajangmyeon with friends.
