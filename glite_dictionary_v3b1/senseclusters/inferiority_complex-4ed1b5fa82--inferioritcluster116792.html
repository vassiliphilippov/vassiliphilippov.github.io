inferiority complex

noun
persistent feeling of inadequacy
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌfɪriˈɔrəti ˈkɑmplɛks / (AmE), / ɪnˌfɪəriˈɒrɪti ˈkɒmplɛks / (BrE)
a persistent feeling of inadequacy, often resulting from a belief that one is less worthy or capable than others

Examples

An inferiority complex feeling
His constant need for reassurance may stem from an inferiority complex developed in childhood.
Overcoming an inferiority complex often requires both self-reflection and support from others.
She struggled for years with an inferiority complex that affected her confidence at work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: superiority complex - an exaggerated feeling of being better than others
