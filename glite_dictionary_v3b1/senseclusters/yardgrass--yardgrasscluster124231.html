yardgrass

noun
a coarse annual grass species; weed
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑrdˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈjɑːdˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A coarse annual grass with fingerlike spikes of flowers, originally native to Old World tropics and now a widespread naturalized weed.

Examples

The yardgrass weed
Farmers often struggle to control yardgrass in their fields because it spreads so rapidly.
She pulled out clumps of yardgrass from the cracks in the sidewalk.
The yardgrass quickly took over the edges of the garden during the rainy season.
