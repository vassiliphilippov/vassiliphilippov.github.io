salute

verb
propose a toast to someone or something
3.9 3.9
Pronunciation: / səˈlut / (AmE), / səˈluːt / (BrE)
(of people at a gathering) to raise a glass and offer good wishes or honor to someone or something, typically by proposing a toast.

Examples

To salute with drinks
Everyone at the table stood to salute the retiring professor.
He raised his glass to salute the team's achievements.
Let us salute the newlyweds on their special day!

Related Terms

  • synonym: toast - to propose or drink to someone's health or honor
