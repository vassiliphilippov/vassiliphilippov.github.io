affective disorder

noun
mental illness involving mood disturbances
6.0 6.0
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈfek.tɪv dɪs.ˈɔːr.dər/ (AmE), /ə.ˈfek.tɪv dɪs.ˈɔː.də/ (BrE)
a mental disorder characterized by a significant disturbance in mood or emotions, not caused by detectable brain abnormalities

Examples

An affective disorder diagnosis
Bipolar disorder, formerly called manic depression, is classified as an affective disorder.
Depression is a common type of affective disorder that affects millions of people worldwide.
Treatment for affective disorder often involves both medication and psychotherapy.

Related Terms
