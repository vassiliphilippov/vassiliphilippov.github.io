starver

noun
one who causes starvation
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːrvər / (AmE), / ˈstɑːvə / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes someone to starve or go without food

Examples

A cruel starver
The cruel ruler was known as a starver of his people during the famine.
History remembers the dictator as a starver who denied food to entire regions.
No one wanted to be called a starver after the suffering caused by the food shortage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oppressor - a person who treats others cruelly or unfairly
  • antonym: provider - a person who supplies food or necessities
