mongrelization

noun
crossbreeding process
9.1
Pronunciation: /mʌŋ.grə.lɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /mʌŋ.grə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /mʌŋ.grə.lɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /mʌŋ.grə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of crossbreeding different breeds or varieties, resulting in hybrids or mixed-breed offspring

Examples

Dog mongrelization
The mongrelization of the local dog population led to a wide variety of appearances and temperaments.
Some farmers encourage mongrelization in plants to create more resilient crops.
Scientists debated the effects of mongrelization on the genetic diversity of the species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hybridization - the process of producing hybrids by combining different breeds or species
  • antonym: purebreeding - the process of breeding from parents of the same breed or variety
