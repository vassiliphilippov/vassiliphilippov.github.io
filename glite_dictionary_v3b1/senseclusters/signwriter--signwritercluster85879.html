signwriter

noun
person who paints signs
4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsaɪn.raɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈsaɪn.raɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to design and paint signs for businesses or public places

Examples

A signwriter paints signs
The company hired a skilled signwriter to design their new logo above the entrance.
As a signwriter, she carefully painted each letter by hand on the shop window.
Many traditional signwriters still use brushes and paint instead of digital tools.
