vestibule

noun
entrance hall or lobby
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛstɪˌbjuːl / (AmE), / ˈvɛstɪbjuːl / (BrE)
a large entrance hall or lobby next to the outer door of a building, often serving as a waiting area or reception room

Examples

Hotel vestibule
A cold draft swept through the vestibule each time the front doors opened.
Please wait for me in the vestibule before the meeting starts.
The hotel's vestibule was elegantly decorated with marble floors and chandeliers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foyer - an entrance hall or lobby, especially in a theater or hotel
  • synonym: lobby - a large entrance or reception area inside a building
