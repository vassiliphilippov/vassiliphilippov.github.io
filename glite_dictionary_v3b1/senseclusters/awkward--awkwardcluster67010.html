awkward

adjective
difficult to handle due to shape or size
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːkwərd / (AmE), / ˈɔːkwəd / (BrE)
difficult to handle or manage, especially because of shape, size, or design.

Examples

Awkward shape

She found the large, awkward box difficult to lift on her own.
The package was so awkward that I had trouble fitting it through the door.
