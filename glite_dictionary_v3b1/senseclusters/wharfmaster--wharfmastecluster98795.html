wharfmaster

noun
wharf operations manager
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrfˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈwɑːfˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person responsible for managing and overseeing the operations of a wharf

Examples

The wharfmaster manages cargo
The wharfmaster ensured that all cargo was loaded and unloaded safely at the busy port.
After a long day, the wharfmaster reviewed the schedule for the next shipment arrivals.
Every morning, the wharfmaster inspected the wharf to make sure it was ready for incoming vessels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dockmaster - a person who manages the operations of a dock
  • synonym: harbormaster - an official in charge of a harbor's operations
