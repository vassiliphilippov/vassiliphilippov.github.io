Gutenberg

proper noun
printing press inventor
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːtənˌbɜːrɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡuːtənˌbɜːɡ / (BrE)
Johannes Gutenberg, the German inventor of the printing press, or something named after him.

Examples

Gutenberg Bible
The Gutenberg Bible was one of the first books printed with movable type.
Many historians credit Gutenberg with revolutionizing the spread of knowledge in Europe.
The museum displayed a replica of Gutenberg's original printing press.
