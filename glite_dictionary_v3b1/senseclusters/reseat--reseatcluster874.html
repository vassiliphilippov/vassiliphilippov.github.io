reseat

verb
provide with new seats
5.2
Pronunciation: / riˈsit / (AmE), / riːˈsiːt / (BrE)
to provide (a place, such as a hall or theater) with new seats; to install new seating

Examples

To reseat a theater
After decades of use, it was necessary to reseat the entire stadium.
The city plans to reseat the old opera house next year.
The renovation project includes funds to reseat the main auditorium with modern chairs.
