beam down

verb
transmit data/signal downward
5.3 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / biÀêm da än / (AmE), / biÀêm da än / (BrE)
(in telecommunications) to transmit a signal, data, or broadcast downward from a satellite, aircraft, or other transmitter to a ground or lower-level receiver.

Examples

Satellite beams down data
The satellite will beam down high-resolution photographs to the ground station every hour.
Engineers asked the probe to beam down telemetry during each scheduled contact window.
During the test, the drone beamed down encrypted commands to the remote sensor array.

Synonyms

    6.3 0.04/m

Related Terms

  • synonym: transmit - to send information, signals, or power from one place or device to another
