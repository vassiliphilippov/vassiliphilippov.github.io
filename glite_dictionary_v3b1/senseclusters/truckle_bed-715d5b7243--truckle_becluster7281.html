truckle bed

noun
a low bed designed to fit under another bed
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌk.əl bɛd / (AmE), / ˈtrʌk.əl bɛd / (BrE)
A low bed on wheels or casters designed to be stored under a higher bed and pulled out for use.

Examples

A truckle bed on wheels
The truckle bed was pulled out at night for the youngest child to sleep on.
The guest room has a truckle bed that fits neatly beneath the main bed.
They bought a truckle bed to save space in their children's room.

Related Terms
