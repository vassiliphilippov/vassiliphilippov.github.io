requisite

adjective
necessary; required
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkwəzɪt / (AmE), / ˈrɛkwɪzɪt / (BrE)
necessary or required for a particular purpose; essential.

Examples

Requisite skills
Candidates must have the requisite experience for this job.
He lacked the requisite skills to complete the project successfully.
Please submit all requisite documents before the deadline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: necessary - needed or essential for a purpose
  • antonym: optional - not required or compulsory
