kidney-shaped

adjective
having the shape of a kidney
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪdni ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈkɪdni ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a shape resembling a kidney, typically rounded with an inward curve on one side; used to describe leaves, beans, or objects.

Examples

Kidney-shaped leaf
Many beans are naturally kidney-shaped, making them easy to identify.
The architect designed a kidney-shaped swimming pool for the backyard.
The leaf's kidney-shaped form helps distinguish the species from others.

  • degree lower: round - having a circular or spherical shape
