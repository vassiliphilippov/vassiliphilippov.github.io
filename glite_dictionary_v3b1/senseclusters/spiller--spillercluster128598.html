spiller

noun
one who spills
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪlər / (AmE), / ˈspɪlə / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes something to be spilled, especially accidentally

Examples

Juice spiller
The toddler was a frequent spiller of juice at the table.
He became known as the office spiller after knocking over his coffee twice in one week.
The faulty bottle cap was a real spiller, making a mess every time it was used.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cleaner - a person or thing that cleans up spills
  • synonym: tipper - a person or thing that tips something over
