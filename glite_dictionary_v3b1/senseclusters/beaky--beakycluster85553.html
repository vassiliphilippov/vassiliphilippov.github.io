beaky

adjective
prominent nose
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːki / (AmE), / ˈbiːki / (BrE)
having a prominent, pointed, or curved nose that resembles a bird's beak

Examples

A beaky nose
The actor's beaky profile made him stand out in every film.
She inherited her grandfather's beaky nose and sharp features.
Children sometimes teased him for his beaky appearance at school.
