Examples

The autonomic nervous system
Doctors study the autonomic nervous system to understand disorders that affect basic bodily functions.
Stress can trigger a response in the autonomic nervous system, leading to increased blood pressure.
The autonomic nervous system regulates the heartbeat and controls digestion without conscious effort.
