Synonyms

  • outturn
    8.5 0.01/m
    outturn is the specific amount of goods made in a set period, while output is the general amount a system produces.
  • production
    2.2 66/m
    production often refers to the process or total made in a factory, while output names the result or amount from a machine or system.
  • yield
    4.1 25/m
    yield often means the amount or profit from crops, investments, or a process, while output means the amount produced by a machine or system.
  • turnout
    4.1 0.09/m
    turnout can mean the number who attend or the amount made, while output refers specifically to what a person, machine, or system produces.
