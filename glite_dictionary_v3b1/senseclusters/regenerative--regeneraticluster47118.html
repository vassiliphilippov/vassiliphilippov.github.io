regenerative

adjective
restoring and improving
6.0
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈdʒen.ə.rə.tɪv/ /; / /rə.ˈdʒen.ə.rə.tɪv/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈdʒen.ə.rə.tɪv/ /; / /rə.ˈdʒen.ə.rə.tɪv/ (BrE)
relating to methods or practices that restore and improve the health and vitality of ecosystems, soil, or communities.

Examples

Regenerative agriculture
The farm switched to regenerative agriculture to improve soil health and support local wildlife.
Many environmentalists advocate for regenerative land management to combat climate change.
Using regenerative practices, the community restored the damaged forest over several years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restorative - having the ability to restore health or strength
