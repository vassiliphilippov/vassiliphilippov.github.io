panhysterectomy

noun
surgical removal of uterus, ovaries, and related tissues
9.1 9.7
Pronunciation: /pæn.hɪ.stə.ˈrek.tə.miː/ (AmE), /pæn.hɪ.stə.ˈrek.tə.miː/ (BrE)
a surgical operation to remove the uterus, ovaries, oviducts, cervix, and related lymph nodes

Examples

A panhysterectomy operation
A panhysterectomy is more extensive than a simple hysterectomy because it removes additional reproductive organs.

The patient underwent a panhysterectomy as part of her cancer treatment.
