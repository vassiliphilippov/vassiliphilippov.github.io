bam

noun
a loud, sudden noise
Pronunciation: / bæm / (AmE), / bæm / (BrE)
A sudden, very loud noise, often used to describe an explosive or striking sound.

Examples

A loud bam
I was drifting off to sleep when a bam from the street woke me up.
Suddenly, there was a bam as the fireworks exploded overhead.
The door slammed shut with a loud bam that startled everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bang - a sudden loud noise
  • synonym: crash - a loud sound of something breaking or hitting
