Ctenocephalus

noun
former genus name of fleas
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛnoʊˈsɛfələs / (AmE), / ˌtɛniːəʊˈsɛfæləs / (BrE)
a former genus name of fleas, now synonymous with Ctenocephalides

Examples

Ctenocephalus flea
In older entomological texts, the dog flea is sometimes classified under Ctenocephalus.
Researchers have traced the evolution of Ctenocephalus through its reclassification over time.
The genus Ctenocephalus is now considered a synonym of Ctenocephalides.
