Cypraeidae

noun
family of cowrie sea snails
Pronunciation: / saɪˈpriːɪˌdiː / (AmE), / sɪˈpriːɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of tropical marine gastropod mollusks known as cowries, characterized by their glossy, brightly patterned shells.

Examples

The Cypraeidae family
Collectors often seek rare cowries from the Cypraeidae family for their unique patterns.
Marine biologists discovered a new member of the Cypraeidae off the coast of Madagascar.
The Cypraeidae family includes many species prized for their colorful shells.
