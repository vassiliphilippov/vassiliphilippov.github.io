Cypraeidae

noun
family of cowrie sea snails
Pronunciation: / saɪˈpriːɪˌdiː / (AmE), / sɪˈpriːɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of tropical marine gastropod mollusks known as cowries, characterized by their glossy, brightly patterned shells.
