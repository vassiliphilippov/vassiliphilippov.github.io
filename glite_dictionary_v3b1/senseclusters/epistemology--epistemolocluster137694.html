epistemology

noun
theory of knowledge in philosophy
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˌpɪstəˈmɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ɪˌpɪstəˈmɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of philosophy concerned with the theory of knowledge, especially with regard to its methods, validity, and scope

Examples

Feminist epistemology study
His research focuses on feminist epistemology and its implications for science.
Questions of epistemology arise whenever we consider what counts as reliable evidence.
The professor introduced the key concepts of epistemology in her philosophy lecture.
