roadwork

noun
athletic endurance training
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊdwɜːrk / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdwɜːk / (BrE)
endurance training, such as running, performed on roads by athletes to improve fitness and stamina

Examples

Morning roadwork run
The coach insisted that every athlete complete their morning roadwork before breakfast.
Boxers often rely on roadwork to build the stamina needed for long matches.
Despite the rain, she never skipped her daily roadwork along the city streets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conditioning - physical training to improve fitness and endurance
  • antonym: sprinting - running at full speed over a short distance
