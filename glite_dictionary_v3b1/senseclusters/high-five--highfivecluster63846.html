high-five

noun
gesture of celebration or greeting
1.0 1.8
Pronunciation: /ˈhaɪ.faɪv/ (AmE), /ˈhaɪ.faɪv/ (BrE)
A gesture of greeting, celebration, or congratulations in which two people each raise one hand and slap their palms together.

Examples

Give a high-five
After winning the match, the teammates gave each other a high-five as they walked off the field.
She greeted her friend at the airport with a big smile and a high-five.
The teacher offered a high-five to each student who finished their assignment early.
