kerosene heater

noun
heater that burns kerosene for heat or cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛrəˌsin ˈhitər / (AmE), / ˈkɛrəˌsiːn ˈhiːtə / (BrE)
A portable heater that uses kerosene as fuel for producing heat, often used for indoor heating or cooking.

Examples

A kerosene heater indoors
During the winter, they relied on a kerosene heater to keep their cottage warm.
Make sure the room is ventilated when using a kerosene heater indoors.
The old kerosene heater was stored in the garage over the summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oil heater - heater that uses oil as a fuel source
