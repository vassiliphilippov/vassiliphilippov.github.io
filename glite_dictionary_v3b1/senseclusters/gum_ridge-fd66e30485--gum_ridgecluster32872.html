gum ridge

noun
ridge in the mouth holding the teeth
Pronunciation: / ɡʌm ˌrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡʌm ˌrɪdʒ / (BrE)
The bony ridge in the mouth that forms the borders of the upper and lower jaws and contains the sockets of the teeth.

Examples

The gum ridge in mouth
A prosthetic tooth must fit securely against the gum ridge to be effective.
Pain along the gum ridge may indicate underlying dental issues.
The dentist examined the patient's gum ridge for signs of swelling and infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alveolar ridge - the bony ridge in the mouth containing the tooth sockets
