undomesticated

adjective
not suited for home life
Pronunciation: / ʌn.dəˈmɛs.tɪ.keɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ʌn.dəˈmes.tɪ.keɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
Not accustomed to or suited for home life; lacking domestic habits or tendencies.

Examples

An undomesticated partner
Even after moving in together, he remained undomesticated and rarely helped with household chores.
Her undomesticated attitude clashed with her partner's desire for a tidy, orderly home.
Some wild animals remain undomesticated, even after years in captivity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domesticated - accustomed to or adapted for home life or tame environment
