best-laid plan

noun
carefully made plans that may fail
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛst leɪd ˈplæn / (AmE), / ˌbɛst leɪd ˈplæn / (BrE)
an expression meaning that even the most carefully prepared plans can go wrong

Examples

The best-laid plans fail
It’s a reminder that even the best-laid plans may not work out exactly as expected.
No matter how detailed your best-laid plans are, unexpected problems can still arise.
She was frustrated when her best-laid plans for the trip fell through at the last moment.
