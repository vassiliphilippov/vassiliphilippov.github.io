adscription

noun
inscription or dedication
Pronunciation: / ædˈskrɪpʃən / (AmE), / ædˈskrɪpʃən / (BrE)
an inscription, dedication, or short written note added to an object, especially as a label or explanation

Examples

An adscription on a vase
The ancient vase bore an adscription that revealed its origin and purpose.
A faded adscription on the painting named the artist and the year it was completed.
Collectors value items with a clear adscription because it adds historical context.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dedication - a note dedicating something to someone
  • synonym: inscription - words written or engraved on an object
