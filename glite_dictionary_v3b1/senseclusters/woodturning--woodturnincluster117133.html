woodturning

noun
shaping wood on lathe
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdˌtɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌtɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the craft or process of shaping wood on a lathe to create objects such as bowls or spindles

Examples

Woodturning class
He took a class in woodturning to learn how to make wooden bowls.
Many beautiful vases and candlesticks are created through woodturning.
Her passion for woodturning began when she inherited her grandfather's lathe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sculpture - the art of making figures or objects by carving or shaping materials
