heart muscle

noun
muscle tissue of the heart
1.9
Pronunciation: / hɑrt ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / hɑːt ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
the muscle tissue that forms the walls of the heart and is responsible for its rhythmic contractions

Examples

Heart muscle tissue
Damage to the heart muscle can lead to serious health problems.
Exercises like swimming can help strengthen the heart muscle.
The heart muscle requires a steady supply of oxygen to function properly.
