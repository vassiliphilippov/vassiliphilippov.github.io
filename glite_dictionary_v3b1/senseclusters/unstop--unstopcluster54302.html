unstop

verb
to become unblocked
4.1
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈstɑːp/ /; / /ən.ˈstɑːp/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈstɒp/ /; / /ən.ˈstɒp/ (BrE)
to remove a blockage from something so that it can flow or work again, especially a pipe, drain, or gutter
