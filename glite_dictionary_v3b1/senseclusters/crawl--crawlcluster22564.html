crawl

verb
have a tingling or repulsive skin sensation
Pronunciation: / krɔl / (AmE), / krɔːl / (BrE)
(of skin or flesh) to feel a tingling or repulsive sensation, as if insects were moving on it, often due to fear, disgust, or anxiety.

Examples

Skin crawls
Her flesh crawled at the eerie sound coming from the basement.
The idea of insects in my bed made my skin crawl.
Whenever I see spiders, my arms start crawling with an uneasy feeling.
