Ryukyuan

noun
a person from the Ryukyu Islands
Pronunciation: / riːˈjuːkiˌuːən / (AmE), / riːˈjuːkiːuən / (BrE)
a person belonging to the indigenous people of the Ryukyu Islands, southwest of Japan

Examples

A Ryukyuan person
A significant number of Ryukyuans now live in mainland Japan as well as on the islands.
Many Ryukyuans have preserved their traditional language and customs despite outside influences.
The museum featured artifacts from the ancient Ryukyuan culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Japanese - a person from Japan or of Japanese descent
