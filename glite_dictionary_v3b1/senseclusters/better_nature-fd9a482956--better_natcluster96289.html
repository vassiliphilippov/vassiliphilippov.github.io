better nature

noun
one's kind or virtuous side
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛt̬ɚ ˈneɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɛtə ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
the more virtuous, kind, or principled aspect of a person's character

Examples

His better nature
Despite his anger, his better nature urged him to forgive her.
She appealed to his better nature in hopes of changing his mind.
When challenged, let your better nature guide your response.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dark side - the less desirable or morally questionable aspect of a person's character
  • synonym: good side - the positive or commendable aspect of a person's character
