Salesian

adjective
of St. John Bosco's order
Pronunciation: / səˈliːʒən / (AmE), / səˈliːzɪən / (BrE)
relating to St. John Bosco or to the religious order he founded, the Salesians, especially in the context of education or charity

Examples

Salesian school values
The Salesian community is dedicated to helping disadvantaged youth through education.
She attended a Salesian school that emphasized kindness and service.
Many Salesian institutions operate in countries around the world, focusing on youth development.

Related Terms
