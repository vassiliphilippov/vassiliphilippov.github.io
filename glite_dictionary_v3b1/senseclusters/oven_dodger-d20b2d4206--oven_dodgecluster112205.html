oven dodger

noun
person who avoids cooking
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈʌvən ˈdɑdʒər / (AmE), / ˈʌvən ˈdɒdʒə / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who avoids cooking, especially at home

Examples

An oven dodger at parties
As an oven dodger, he never volunteers to cook at house parties.
He’s an oven dodger who always suggests eating out instead of preparing a meal.
She admits to being a bit of an oven dodger and relies heavily on ready meals.
