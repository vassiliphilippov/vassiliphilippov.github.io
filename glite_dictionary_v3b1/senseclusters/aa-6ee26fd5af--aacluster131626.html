AA

noun
associate degree in arts
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˈeɪ ˈɑː / (BrE)
An academic degree called Associate of Arts, typically awarded after two years of study at a college or community college.

Examples

AA degree
Many students complete an AA to save money before pursuing a bachelor's degree.
She earned her AA at a local community college before transferring to a university.
The requirements for an AA vary depending on the chosen major and institution.

Related Terms

  • antonym: AS - Associate of Science degree, similar but focused on science
