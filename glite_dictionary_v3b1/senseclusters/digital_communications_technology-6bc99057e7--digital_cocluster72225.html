digital communications technology

noun
technology for transmitting digital information
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒɪt̬l kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃənz tɛkˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtl kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənz tɛkˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the technology and engineering involved in transmitting information in digital form, such as via computers, mobile phones, or the internet

Examples

Modern digital communications technology
Advances in digital communications technology have revolutionized how we connect across the globe.
Many modern businesses depend heavily on digital communications technology to operate efficiently.
The new curriculum includes a course on digital communications technology for engineering students.

Related Terms

  • antonym: analog communications technology - communication technology using continuous signals rather than digital
  • synonym: digital technology - technology that processes information as digital signals
