lemme

verb
to allow me (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛmi / (AmE), / ˈlɛmi / (BrE)
an informal way of saying 'let me', used in casual speech or writing

Examples

Please lemme see
Lemme see that photo you just took.
If you have any questions, just lemme know.
Lemme help you carry those bags to your car.
