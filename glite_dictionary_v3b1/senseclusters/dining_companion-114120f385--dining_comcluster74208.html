dining companion

noun
someone you share a meal with
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪnɪŋ kəmˈpænjən / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnɪŋ kəmˈpanjən / (BrE)
a person with whom you share a meal, especially in a social setting

Examples

Dining companion at dinner
Good manners are important when you are someone's dining companion in a formal setting.
He was an excellent dining companion, always engaging everyone in interesting conversation.
She invited her colleague to be her dining companion at the corporate dinner.
