tapa bark

noun
fibrous bark used to make tapa cloth
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːpə bɑːrk / (AmE), / ˈtæpə bɑːk / (BrE)
the thin, fibrous bark of the paper mulberry or Pipturus albidus used in making tapa cloth

Examples

tapa bark cloth
She studied the method of turning tapa bark into decorative cloth patterns.
The artisans carefully stripped the tapa bark from the paper mulberry tree to begin their craft.
Traditional Hawaiian clothing often incorporated tapa bark processed into sheets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tapa cloth - traditional Polynesian cloth made from bark
