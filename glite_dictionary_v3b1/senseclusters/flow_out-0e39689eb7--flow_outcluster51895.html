Synonyms

  • flow off
    3.2 0.01/m
    flow off means a liquid moves away from a surface in a steady stream, while flow out means spill outward more generally.
  • flow away
    3.2 0.04/m
    flow away is a liquid carried away slowly by a current, while flow out means move or spill outward in a stream.
  • stream
    1.7 3/m
    stream means to move continuously in large amounts like water or people, while flow out means spill outward in a stream.
  • pour out
    2.9 1/m
    pour out means to send a large quantity out quickly, while flow out means to move or spill outward slowly in a stream.
  • run out
    1.3 1/m
    run out means a liquid leaves a container until empty, often by accident, while flow out means spill outward in a stream.
  • drain
    1.0 9/m
    drain means to remove liquid often by letting it flow away through a channel, while flow out means spill outward in a stream.
