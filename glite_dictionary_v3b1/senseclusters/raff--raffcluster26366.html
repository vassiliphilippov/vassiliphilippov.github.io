raff

verb
to collect hastily
9.9
Pronunciation: / ræf / (AmE), / ræf / (BrE)
to gather or collect things quickly and carelessly

Examples

To raff things up
She raffed her books off the table and stuffed them into her backpack.
In a hurry, he raffed his clothes from the floor and ran out the door.
They raff whatever supplies they can find before the store closes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assemble - to gather people or things together in one place
  • antonym: organize - to arrange or order things carefully
  • synonym: scrape - to collect or gather something with difficulty or effort
