jointless

adjective
without joints
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪntləs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪntləs / (BrE)
formed in one continuous piece, without joints or seams

Examples

A jointless concrete structure
The new bridge design uses a jointless concrete structure for greater durability.
She admired the jointless glass tabletop, which appeared to be a single flawless sheet.
Modern bathtubs are often made from jointless acrylic to prevent leaks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: jointed - having joints or connections between parts
  • synonym: seamless - having no seams or joints
