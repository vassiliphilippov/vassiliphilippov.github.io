nonmeaningful

adjective
lacking meaning or significance
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈmiːnɪŋˌfəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈmiːnɪŋfəl / (BrE)
having no meaning, significance, or purpose; lacking meaning or value.

Examples

A nonmeaningful answer
His answer was vague and nonmeaningful, failing to address the real issue.
Many people fear living a nonmeaningful existence without purpose or direction.
The data collected was ultimately nonmeaningful and did not advance the research.

  • synonym: meaningless - having no meaning or purpose
