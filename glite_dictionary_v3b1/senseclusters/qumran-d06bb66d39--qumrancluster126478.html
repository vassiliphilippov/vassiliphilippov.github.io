Qumran

proper noun
archaeological site
9.9
Pronunciation: / kuːmˈrɑːn / (AmE), / kuːmˈrɑːn / (BrE)
an ancient archaeological site near the Dead Sea, known for the discovery of the Dead Sea Scrolls and the ancient Jewish community that lived there

Examples

Archaeologists have uncovered many important artifacts at Qumran near the Dead Sea.
The community at Qumran is often linked to the discovery of the Dead Sea Scrolls.
Many historians debate the daily life of the people who lived in Qumran centuries ago.
