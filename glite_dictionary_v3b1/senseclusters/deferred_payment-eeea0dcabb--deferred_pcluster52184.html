deferred payment

noun
postponed payment arrangement
6.2 4.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɝrd ˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈfɜːd ˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
an arrangement in which payment for goods or services is postponed to a later date, rather than paid immediately

Examples

A deferred payment plan
The store offered a deferred payment option to help customers spread the cost over six months.
They signed a contract that included a deferred payment agreement.
With deferred payment, you can receive the product now and pay for it later.

Related Terms
