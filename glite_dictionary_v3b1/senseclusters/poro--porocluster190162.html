poro

proper noun
West African male society
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːrəʊ / (BrE)
a traditional secret male initiation society in parts of West Africa, responsible for rites of passage and community governance

Examples

Poro initiation ceremony
Young men in the region are initiated into the poro as part of their transition to adulthood.
The poro plays an important role in maintaining traditional laws and customs in the community.
Membership in the poro is considered a great honor among the local people.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sande - a traditional secret female society in West Africa
