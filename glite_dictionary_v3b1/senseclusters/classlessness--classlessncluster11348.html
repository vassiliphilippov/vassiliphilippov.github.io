classlessness

noun
absence of social classes
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈklæsləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsləsnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of having no social classes or distinctions between groups in society

Examples

Society of classlessness
The revolution aimed to create a society based on classlessness and equal opportunity.
Many utopian novels describe worlds where classlessness has been achieved.
Debates about classlessness often arise in discussions of social justice and reform.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egalitarianism - the belief in or practice of equal rights and opportunities for all people
  • antonym: inequality - the state of not being equal, especially in status or opportunities
