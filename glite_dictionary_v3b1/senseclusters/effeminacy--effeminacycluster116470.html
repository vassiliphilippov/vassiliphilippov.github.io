effeminacy

noun
quality of having womanly traits (esp. in men)
8.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛmənəsi / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛmɪnəsi / (BrE)
the quality or trait of having characteristics regarded as typical of women; the display of behaviors or manners considered unmanly in men, often viewed as undesirable

Examples

Effeminacy in men
Some societies have historically regarded effeminacy in men as a weakness.
The effeminacy of his gestures contrasted sharply with his rugged appearance.
The poet was criticized for the effeminacy of his style by his contemporaries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: effeminateness - the quality of resembling or displaying female characteristics (especially in men)
  • antonym: masculinity - possession of qualities traditionally associated with men
