flight attendant

noun
airline passenger service staff
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪt əˌtɛndənt / (AmE), / ˈflaɪt əˌtendənt / (BrE)
A person whose job is to look after passengers on an airplane and ensure their safety and comfort during flights.

Examples

The flight attendant helps
If you need anything, just ask the flight attendant for help.
The flight attendant demonstrated the safety procedures before the plane took off.
The flight attendants walked down the aisle serving drinks and snacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: steward - male attendant serving passengers, especially on ships or trains
  • synonym: stewardess - female flight attendant (historically used)
