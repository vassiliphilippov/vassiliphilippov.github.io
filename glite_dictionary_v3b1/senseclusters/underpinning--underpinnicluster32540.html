underpinning

noun
foundation support method
5.6
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dər.ˈpɪn.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.ˈpɪn.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
a method, process, or material used to strengthen or support the foundation of a building or structure

Examples

Building underpinning
The engineers recommended underpinning to prevent the old house from sinking further.
After discovering cracks in the walls, the building required immediate underpinning for safety.
Modern construction often uses steel beams as part of the underpinning process.

  • synonym: reinforcement - the act of strengthening or supporting something
