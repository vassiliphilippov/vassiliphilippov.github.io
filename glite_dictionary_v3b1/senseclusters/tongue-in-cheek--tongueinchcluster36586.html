tongue-in-cheek

adverb
ironically or humorously, not seriously
Pronunciation: / tʌŋ ɪn tʃik /; / ˌtʌŋ ɪn ˈtʃik / (AmE), / tʌŋ ɪn tʃiːk /; / ˌtʌŋ ɪn ˈtʃiːk / (BrE)
in an ironic, humorous, or insincere way; not meant to be taken seriously
