prism

noun
solid geometric figure with parallel bases
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈprɪzəm / (BrE)
a solid geometric figure with two parallel, congruent bases joined by parallelogram faces

Examples

Rectangular prism shape
In geometry class, we learned how to calculate the volume of a rectangular prism.
Light passing through a prism can be separated into a rainbow of colors.
The science museum had a display featuring a triangular glass prism to demonstrate light refraction.
