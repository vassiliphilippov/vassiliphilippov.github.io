lockage

noun
blockage or obstruction in plumbing
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːkɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈlɒkɪdʒ / (BrE)
a blockage or obstruction in plumbing or other closed passageways; also, the act or process of securing with a lock

Examples

Pipe lockage
Engineers tried several methods to clear the lockage before the water could flow freely again.
Shipping delays were caused by a lockage somewhere in the main supply pipe.
The plumber had to remove a stubborn lockage from the kitchen sink drain.
