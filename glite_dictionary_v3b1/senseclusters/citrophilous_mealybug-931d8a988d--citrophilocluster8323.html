citrophilous mealybug

noun
pest that attacks citrus plants
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪ.trə.fɪ.ləs ˈmiː.li.bʌg/ (AmE), /ˈsɪ.trə.fɪ.ləs ˈmiː.li.bʌg/ (BrE)
a type of mealybug that is particularly destructive to citrus plants, feeding on their juices and causing significant agricultural damage
