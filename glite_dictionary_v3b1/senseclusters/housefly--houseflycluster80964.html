housefly

noun
common fly found near humans
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌflʌɪ / (BrE)
A common species of fly (Musca domestica) that is frequently found in human dwellings and is known to spread various diseases.

Examples

A housefly on food
A housefly landed on my sandwich while I was eating lunch outside.
Because houseflies carry germs, it's important to keep food covered.
The buzzing of a single housefly kept me awake for hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fly - a small insect with two wings (order Diptera)
