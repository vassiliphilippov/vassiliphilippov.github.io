imperially

adverb
in an imperial manner
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɪriəli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪəriəli / (BrE)
In a way that is characteristic of an emperor or empire; in an imperial manner.

Examples

To act imperially
She carried herself imperially throughout the grand ceremony, commanding everyone's attention.
The new laws were imperially decreed by the emperor.
The palace was decorated imperially, with gold and velvet adorning each hall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imperial - relating to an empire or emperor
