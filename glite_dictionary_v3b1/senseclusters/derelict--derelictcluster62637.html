Synonyms

  • neglectful
    4.4 0.45/m
    neglectful describes not giving enough care or attention to people or tasks, while derelict stresses failing duties or responsibilities.
  • remiss
    7.6 1.13/m

  • delinquent
    6.8 0.85/m
    delinquent is often used for missed payments or legal failures, while derelict means neglectful in duty or care.
