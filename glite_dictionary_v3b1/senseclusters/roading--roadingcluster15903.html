roading

verb
to build roads
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊ.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊ.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
to build or improve a road or access track, especially to provide access to a remote area.

Examples

To road a hillside
The company is roading the hillside to make it accessible for construction vehicles.
Local authorities began roading the area to connect the village with the main highway.
After months of planning, they finally started roading the forest to reach the new campsite.
