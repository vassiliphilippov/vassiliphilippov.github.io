monopole

noun
single magnetic pole
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑː.nə.poʊl/ (AmE), /ˈmɒ.nə.pəʊl/ (BrE)
a hypothetical particle or object that has only one magnetic pole instead of both a north and south pole

Examples

Magnetic monopole
Scientists have theorized about the existence of a monopole for many years.
No monopole has ever been observed in nature despite extensive research.
The discovery of a magnetic monopole would revolutionize our understanding of physics.
