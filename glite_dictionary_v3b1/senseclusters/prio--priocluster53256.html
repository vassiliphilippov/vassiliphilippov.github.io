prio

noun
informal priority
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpraɪəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for something that is considered a priority or of high importance

Examples

Top prio
Finishing this project is my top prio right now.
Let me know your main prio for this week so I can help.
We need to decide which tasks are a prio before the meeting.
