louse

verb
to remove lice
8.8
Pronunciation: / laʊs / (AmE), / laʊs / (BrE)
to remove lice from a person, animal, or object by searching and cleaning thoroughly

Examples

To louse a child
The nurse had to louse the children's hair after an outbreak at school.
Farmers often louse their livestock to prevent the spread of parasites.
She carefully loused her daughter's hair with a special comb.
