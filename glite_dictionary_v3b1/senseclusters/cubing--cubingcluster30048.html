cubing

noun
cutting into cubes
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbɪŋ / (BrE)
the action or process of cutting food into small cube-shaped pieces, especially in cooking

Examples

Cubing vegetables
The recipe calls for cubing the potatoes before boiling them.
Proper cubing of vegetables ensures even cooking in stews and soups.
After cubing the cheese, she arranged the pieces on a platter for the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dicing - cutting food into small square pieces
  • antonym: slicing - cutting food into thin, flat pieces
