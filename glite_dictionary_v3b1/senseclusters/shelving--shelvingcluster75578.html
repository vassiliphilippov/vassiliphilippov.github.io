shelving

adjective
gently sloping
5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈʃel.vɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈʃel.vɪŋ/ (BrE)
sloping gradually or forming a gentle slope, especially referring to ground, shore, or seabed

Examples

Divers prefer the shelving seabed because it makes entry into the water easier.
