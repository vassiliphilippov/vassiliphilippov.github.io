powwowing

noun
Native American gathering
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊˌwaʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊˌwaʊɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of attending or participating in a Native American social gathering with dancing, singing, and cultural ceremonies

Examples

Native powwowing festival
Every summer, families travel long distances for a weekend of powwowing and reconnecting with their heritage.
The community center hosted powwowing to teach younger generations about traditional songs and dances.
Many visitors were welcomed to join in the powwowing and learn about Native American customs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: festival - a celebration or series of cultural events
  • synonym: powwow - a Native American social event with dancing and ceremonies
