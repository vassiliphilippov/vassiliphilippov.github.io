special

adjective
designed for a particular purpose
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈspe.ʃəl/ (AmE), /ˈspe.ʃəl/ (BrE)
different from what is usual; made or intended for a particular purpose, person, or occasion; unique or specific

Examples

A special chair
She designed a special chair for people with back pain.
The museum has a special exhibit running this month.
This special diet is required for patients with diabetes.
A special train was organized for concertgoers on Saturday night.
The airline offered a special flight to accommodate holiday travelers.
They scheduled a special meeting for urgent business.
Each member has a special function within the organization.
She was chosen for a special assignment that required unique skills.
The committee created a special fund for emergency situations.
