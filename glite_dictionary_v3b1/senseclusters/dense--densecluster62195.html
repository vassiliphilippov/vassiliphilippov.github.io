dense

adjective
closely packed together
Pronunciation: / dɛns / (AmE), / dɛns / (BrE)
Having parts that are packed closely together; crowded or compact.

Examples

Dense crowd
A dense crowd filled the stadium for the championship match.
She struggled to comb her dense hair every morning.
The jungle was so dense that sunlight barely reached the ground.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sparse - thinly dispersed or scattered
  • synonym: thick - having a large distance between opposite sides or surfaces; densely packed
