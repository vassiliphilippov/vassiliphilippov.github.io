surfboarder

noun
person who rides a surfboard
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːfˌbɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfˌbɔːdə / (BrE)
a person who rides waves on a surfboard, especially as a sport

Examples

A surfboarder rides waves
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a professional surfboarder.
Many surfboarders travel to Hawaii for its legendary surf spots.
The experienced surfboarder skillfully caught the biggest wave of the day.
