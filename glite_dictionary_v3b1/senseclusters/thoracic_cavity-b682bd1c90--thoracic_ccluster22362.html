thoracic cavity

noun
chest cavity containing lungs and heart
6.1 4.8
Pronunciation: /θə.ˈræs.ɪk ˈkæv.ə.ti/ /; / /θɔː.ˈræs.ɪk ˈkæv.ə.ti/ (AmE), /θə.ˈræs.ɪk ˈkæv.ɪ.ti/ /; / /θɔː.ˈræs.ɪk ˈkæv.ɪ.ti/ (BrE)
the space within the chest bounded by the ribs, diaphragm, and neck, containing the lungs, heart, and other organs
