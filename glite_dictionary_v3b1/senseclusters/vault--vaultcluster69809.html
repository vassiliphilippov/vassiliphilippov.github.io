vault

noun
arched ceiling or roof
6.7
Pronunciation: / vɔlt / (AmE), / vɔːlt / (BrE)
an arched structure of brick, stone, or concrete forming a ceiling or roof

Examples

Stone vault
Builders carefully restored the collapsed vault of the medieval chapel.
The ancient crypt was protected by a thick stone vault.
The cathedral's central vault was decorated with intricate frescoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arch - a curved symmetrical structure spanning an opening and typically supporting the weight of a bridge, roof, or wall above it
  • degree higher: dome - a rounded vault forming the roof of a building or structure, typically with a circular base
