illicitly

adverb
in an unlawful or forbidden manner
2.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɪsɪtli / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪsɪtli / (BrE)
in a way that is not allowed by law, rules, or custom; illegally or improperly

Examples

To act illicitly
He acquired the confidential documents illicitly, bypassing proper authorization channels.
She imported rare artifacts illicitly, violating international trade laws.
The company was fined after it was discovered they had acted illicitly in obtaining contracts.
He was arrested after he illicitly obtained confidential information from the database.
She had been illicitly selling prescription drugs online for months.
The group was accused of illicitly importing goods into the country.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lawfully - in a lawful or permitted manner
  • antonym: legally - in a way that is allowed by law
