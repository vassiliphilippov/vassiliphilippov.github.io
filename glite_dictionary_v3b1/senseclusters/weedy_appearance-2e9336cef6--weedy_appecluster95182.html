weedy appearance

noun
unkempt or overgrown look
Pronunciation: / ˈwidi əˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / ˈwiːdi əˈpɪərəns / (BrE)
an unkempt or untidy look, especially due to overgrowth of weeds or similar plants

Examples

A weedy appearance
After months without maintenance, the schoolyard took on a weedy appearance.
Her garden's weedy appearance frustrated her neighbors who preferred neatly trimmed lawns.
The abandoned house had a weedy appearance that made it seem neglected.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neat appearance - an appearance that is tidy and orderly
  • synonym: unkempt - having an untidy or disheveled appearance
