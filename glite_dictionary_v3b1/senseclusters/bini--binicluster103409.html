bini

noun
member of Edo people
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːni / (AmE), / ˈbiːni / (BrE)
a member of the Bini (Edo) people, an ethnic group from southern Nigeria

Examples

A bini leader
A respected bini leader spoke at the cultural festival in Benin City.
The bini have a rich tradition of bronze casting and sculpture.
Many bini families celebrate their heritage through music and dance.
