bolshie

adjective
rebellious or difficult
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɒlʃi / (BrE)
uncooperative, rebellious, or deliberately difficult to deal with, especially in response to authority

Examples

Bolshie attitude
She became quite bolshie when her manager tried to enforce new rules.
His bolshie attitude made group projects challenging for everyone involved.
Teenagers can sometimes act bolshie when they feel misunderstood by adults.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliant - willing to obey or cooperate
  • synonym: defiant - openly resisting authority or rules
