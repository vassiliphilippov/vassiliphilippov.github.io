grand mal epilepsy

noun
epilepsy with convulsive seizures
Pronunciation: / ɡrænd mɑl ɛpˈlɛp.si / (AmE), / ɡrænd mæl ɛpˈlɛp.si / (BrE)
A type of epilepsy characterized by seizures that involve loss of consciousness, muscle stiffening, and generalized convulsive movements.

Examples

Grand mal epilepsy seizure
During a grand mal epilepsy attack, the patient loses consciousness and experiences violent muscle contractions.
He was diagnosed with grand mal epilepsy after experiencing several severe seizures in one week.
The medication helped reduce the frequency of his grand mal epilepsy episodes.
