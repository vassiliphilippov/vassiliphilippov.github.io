greenhead

noun
biting marsh fly
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːnˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnˌhɛd / (BrE)
a large greenish biting fly, especially found in marshy areas of North America

Examples

A greenhead fly
We were bitten by a greenhead while walking near the salt marsh.
The picnic was interrupted by swarms of greenheads buzzing around us.
Fishermen often complain about the painful bites of the greenhead during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horsefly - a large biting fly often found near water or livestock
  • synonym: mosquito - a small flying insect that bites and sucks blood
