stink bell

noun
malodorous Californian weed with bell-shaped flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / stɪŋk bɛl / (AmE), / stɪŋk bɛl / (BrE)
A foul-smelling herb found in California, with bell-shaped flowers, commonly growing as a weed in grainfields.

Examples

The stink bell plant
