weed remover

noun
gardening tool for extracting weeds
6.4
Pronunciation: / wid rɪˈmuːvɚ / (AmE), / wiːd rɪˈmuːvə / (BrE)
a gardening tool, typically featuring steel claws, used to extract weeds by their roots for thorough removal

Examples

A weed remover tool
She bought a stainless steel weed remover to help tidy up her garden beds.
The new weed remover made pulling dandelions from the lawn quick and easy.
Using a weed remover prevents the roots from breaking and growing back.
