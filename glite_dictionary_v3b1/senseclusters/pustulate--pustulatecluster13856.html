pustulate

adjective
blemished with pustules or skin imperfections
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌstʃəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈpʌstjʊlət / (BrE)
(of skin or complexion) marked by the presence of pustules or blemished by skin imperfections.
