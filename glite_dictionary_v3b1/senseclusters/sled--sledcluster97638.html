sled

noun
vehicle for travel over snow
Pronunciation: / slɛd / (AmE), / slɛd / (BrE)
a vehicle with runners, usually pulled by animals or gravity, used for travel over snow

Examples

A wooden sled
He loaded the supplies onto the sled before setting off into the woods.
In Alaska, dog teams pull a sled across the frozen landscape.
The children rode their sled down the snowy hill all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sledge - a vehicle for traveling over snow, often larger and heavier than a sled
  • synonym: toboggan - a long, light, narrow sled without runners, used for sliding over snow or ice
