flat on one's back

adjective
lying down on one's back
5.4
Pronunciation: / flæt ɑn wʌnz bæk / (AmE), / flæt ɒn wʌnz bæk / (BrE)
lying down with one's back against a surface, often due to being at rest, injured, or unwell

Examples

Flat on his back after flu
After coming down with the flu, Tom was flat on his back for days.
He spent a week flat on his back after he hurt his spine.
She suddenly collapsed and was lying flat on her back in the grass.
