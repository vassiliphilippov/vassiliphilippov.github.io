commonplace book

noun
notebook for quotations or ideas
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmənˌpleɪs bʊk / (AmE), / ˈkɒmənˌpleɪs bʊk / (BrE)
a notebook used to record memorable quotations, ideas, or information for personal reference
