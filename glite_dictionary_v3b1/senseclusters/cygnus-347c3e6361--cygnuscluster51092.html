Cygnus

proper noun
ISS cargo spacecraft
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪg.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈsɪg.nəs/ (BrE)
A robotic cargo spacecraft (developed by Orbital ATK and launched by Northrop Grumman) used to carry supplies and experiments to the International Space Station.

Examples

Cygnus spacecraft mission
During a recent mission, the Cygnus spacecraft delivered supplies to the International Space Station.
Engineers inspected the damaged Cygnus module before approving it for another resupply flight next month.
Orbital ATK developed the first Cygnus cargo ship; Northrop Grumman later continued its production.
