chocolate-box surprise

noun
an unexpected delightful gift or event
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑk.lət bɑks səˈpraɪz / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒk.lət bɒks səˈpraɪz / (BrE)
an unexpected and delightful gift or occurrence, often charming or pleasing in a way reminiscent of receiving a box of chocolates

Examples

A chocolate-box surprise

The invitation to the gala arrived as a delightful chocolate-box surprise in the mail.
Winning the trip was a complete chocolate-box surprise for the whole family.
