regulator

noun
device for controlling flow, pressure, or temperature
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjəˌleɪtər /; / ˈrɛɡjəˌleɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a device that controls or maintains the level, flow, pressure, temperature, speed, or other aspect of a machine or system

Examples

Pressure regulator
An automatic temperature regulator adjusts the heat inside the incubator.
The pressure regulator keeps the gas flow within safe limits.
You should check that the water regulator is working properly before starting the machine.
A faulty regulator can cause the system to overheat.
The speed regulator in the car ensures the engine runs smoothly.
This machine uses a pressure regulator to maintain safe operation.
