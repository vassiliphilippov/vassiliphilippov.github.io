fragrant cliff fern

noun
a fern with fragrant fronds
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪɡrənt klɪf fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈfreɪɡrənt klɪf fɜːn / (BrE)
a fern native to northern Eurasia and North America, distinguished by its fragrant fronds
