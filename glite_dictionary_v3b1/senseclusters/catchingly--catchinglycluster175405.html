catchingly

adverb
attractively; infectiously
Pronunciation: / ˈkætʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkætʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that attracts attention or is likely to spread to others

Examples

To catchingly attract attention
Her laughter was so catchingly joyful that everyone joined in.
The song was catchingly upbeat, making it popular on the radio.
He smiled catchingly, and soon the whole room was smiling with him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attractively - in a way that draws attention or interest
  • antonym: dully - in a way that does not attract attention
  • synonym: infectiously - in a way that spreads easily to others
