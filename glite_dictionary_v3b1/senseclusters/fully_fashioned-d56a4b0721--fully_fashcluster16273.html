fully fashioned

adjective
knitted to fit body shape
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊli ˈfæʃənd / (AmE), / ˈfʊli ˈfæʃənd / (BrE)
(of knitted garments) made to fit the shape of the body by shaping the fabric during knitting, rather than cutting and sewing after knitting.

Examples

Fully fashioned knitwear
She prefers fully fashioned knitwear for its smooth lines and superior comfort.
The manufacturer specializes in fully fashioned sweaters that follow the contours of the body.
These fully fashioned stockings are known for their comfortable, tailored fit.
