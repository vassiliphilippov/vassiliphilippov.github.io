medicalese

noun
medical jargon
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛdɪkəˈliːz / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪkəˈliːz / (BrE)
the specialized language or jargon used by medical professionals, often difficult for non-experts to understand

Examples

The medicalese language
The patient's family struggled to understand the medicalese in the hospital report.
She asked the doctor to avoid medicalese and explain the diagnosis in simple terms.
Many medical websites are filled with medicalese that can confuse ordinary readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gobbledygook - language that is meaningless or hard to understand
  • synonym: jargon - specialized language used by a particular group
  • antonym: plain English - language that is clear and easy to understand
