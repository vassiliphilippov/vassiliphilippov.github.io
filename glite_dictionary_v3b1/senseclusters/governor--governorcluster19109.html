governor

noun
head of a state government
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌvərnər / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvənə / (BrE)
the elected official who is the head of the government of a state in the United States or other countries

Examples

State governor
After the hurricane, the governor declared a state of emergency and coordinated the relief efforts.
Each state in the U.S. has its own governor who leads the executive branch of the state government.
The newly elected governor promised to focus on improving education and healthcare in the state.

  • synonym: president - the elected head of a republican state
