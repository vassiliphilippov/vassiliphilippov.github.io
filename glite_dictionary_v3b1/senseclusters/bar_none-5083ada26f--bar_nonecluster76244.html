bar none

adverb
without exception; with no rivals
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɑr nʌn / (AmE), / bɑː nʌn / (BrE)
used to emphasize that there are no exceptions or rivals to what is being stated; without exception.

Examples

Bar none the best
He is, bar none, the most talented chef in the city.
That was the fastest race we've had this season, bar none.
This is the best book I have read, bar none.
