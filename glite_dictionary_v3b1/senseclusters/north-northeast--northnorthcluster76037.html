north-northeast

noun
compass point between north and northeast
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnɔrθ nɔrˈθist / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθ nɔːˈθiːst / (BrE)
the direction or compass point halfway between north and northeast, at an angle of 22.5° east of due north

Examples

North-northeast direction
The ship set a course for north-northeast to avoid the storm.
The wind was blowing steadily from the north-northeast all morning.
We hiked in the north-northeast direction for several miles until reaching the lake.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: north - the direction corresponding to 0° on the compass
  • degree higher: northeast - the direction between north and east, or 45° on the compass
