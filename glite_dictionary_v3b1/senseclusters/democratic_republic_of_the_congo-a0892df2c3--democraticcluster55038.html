Democratic Republic of the Congo

proper noun
country in central Africa
3.7
Pronunciation: / dəˈmɑːkrətɪk rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ðə ˈkɑːŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / dɪˌmɒkrətɪk rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ðə ˈkɒŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
A country in central Africa that gained independence from Belgium in 1960, known for its vast natural resources and river systems.

Examples
