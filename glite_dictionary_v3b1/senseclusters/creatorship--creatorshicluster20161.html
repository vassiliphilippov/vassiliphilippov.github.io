creatorship

noun
state of being creator
Pronunciation: / kriˈeɪtərʃɪp / (AmE), / kriˈeɪtəʃɪp / (BrE)
the state or quality of being a creator, especially of an idea, work, or project

Examples

The creatorship of a project
Her creatorship of the project was recognized by the entire team.
The artist's creatorship is evident in every detail of the sculpture.
Debates about creatorship often arise when multiple people contribute to a single invention.
