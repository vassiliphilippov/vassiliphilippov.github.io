fly ball

noun
baseball: a high-hit ball
7.9 3.1
Pronunciation: / flaɪ bɔl / (AmE), / flaɪ bɔːl / (BrE)
(in baseball) a ball that is hit high up into the air rather than along the ground.

Examples

A fly ball in baseball
It's harder to track a fly ball when the sun is in your eyes.
She hit a towering fly ball to left field in the seventh inning.
The outfielder caught the fly ball just before it reached the fence.
