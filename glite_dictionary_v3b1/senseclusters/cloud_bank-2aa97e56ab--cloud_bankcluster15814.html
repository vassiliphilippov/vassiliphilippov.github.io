cloud bank

noun
layer or mass of clouds visible from far away
Pronunciation: / klaʊd bæŋk / (AmE), / klaʊd baŋk / (BrE)
a large mass or layer of clouds seen from some distance, often as a horizontal formation

Examples

A dark cloud bank
As the storm approached, a dark cloud bank rolled over the mountains.
From the ship, we could see a thick cloud bank forming on the horizon.
The airplane emerged above a cloud bank into clear blue sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cloud - a visible mass of condensed water vapor floating in the atmosphere
