algorithmic

adjective
relating to algorithms; systematic or rule-based
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌælɡəˈrɪðmɪk / (AmE), / ˌælɡəˈrɪðmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or having the characteristics of an algorithm; using or involving a defined sequence of steps or rules, especially in computing or problem-solving.

Examples

Algorithmic process
Her approach to scheduling is highly algorithmic, following a set pattern of rules.
Modern encryption relies on algorithmic methods to secure data.
The algorithmic trading system can analyze thousands of stocks in real time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: algorithm - a process or set of rules to be followed in calculations or problem-solving
