Friedrich Anton Mesmer

proper noun
Austrian physician and founder of mesmerism
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈfriː.drɪk ˈæn.tɔːn ˈmez.mər/ /; / /ˈfrɪd.rɪk ˈæn.tən ˈmez.mər/ (AmE), /ˈfriː.drɪk ˈæn.tən ˈmez.mə/ /; / /ˈfriː.drɪk ˈæn.tɒn ˈmɛz.mə/ (BrE)
(1734–1815) An Austrian physician known for his theory of 'animal magnetism' and his efforts to treat diseases using a form of hypnotism.

Friedrich Anton Mesmer claimed he could cure diseases through animal magnetism.

The term 'mesmerize' was inspired by the work of Friedrich Anton Mesmer in the 18th century.
