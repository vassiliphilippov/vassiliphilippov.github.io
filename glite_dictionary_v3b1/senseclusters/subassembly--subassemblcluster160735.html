subassembly

noun
component for assembly
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbəˈsɛmbli / (AmE), / ˌsʌbəˈsɛmbli / (BrE)
a component or unit that is assembled separately and later incorporated into a larger product or system

Examples

A subassembly unit
The engine is delivered as a subassembly to the main factory for final installation.
Each subassembly must pass quality checks before being added to the finished product.
Technicians worked on the electronic subassembly before integrating it into the device.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: assembly - the process of putting together parts to make a complete product
  • synonym: module - a self-contained unit that can be combined with others to form a larger system
  • degree lower: part - a single piece or element of a larger whole
