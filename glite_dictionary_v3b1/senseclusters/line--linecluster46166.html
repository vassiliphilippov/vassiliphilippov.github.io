line

noun
railroad track and roadbed for trains
5.6 1.0
Pronunciation: /laɪn/ (AmE), /laɪn/ (BrE)
a long, thin, flexible object such as a rope, wire, pipe, or cable used for carrying, connecting, or transporting

Examples
