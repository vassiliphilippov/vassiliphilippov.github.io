picturephone

noun
video telephone device
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪktʃɚˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃəfəʊn / (BrE)
a telephone device that enables users to see each other while talking, combining video and audio communication

Examples

A picturephone call
In the 1960s, engineers introduced the picturephone as a futuristic way to communicate.
The picturephone allowed families to see distant relatives while talking to them.
Although the picturephone was innovative, it was too expensive for most people at the time.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: telephone - a device for transmitting speech over distances
  • synonym: videophone - a device that allows people to see and hear each other during a call
