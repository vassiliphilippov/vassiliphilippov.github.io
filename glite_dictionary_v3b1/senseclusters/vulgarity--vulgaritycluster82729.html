vulgarity

noun
crudeness; lack of refinement or taste
Pronunciation: / vʌlˈɡær.ə.ti / (AmE), / vʌlˈɡær.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being crude, coarse, or lacking in taste or refinement

Examples

The vulgarity of jokes
Critics complained about the vulgarity of the comedian's jokes.
He was offended by the vulgarity of their behavior at the formal dinner.
Some people associate bright neon colors with a sense of vulgarity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coarseness - quality of being rough or crude
  • antonym: refinement - the quality of being polished or cultured
