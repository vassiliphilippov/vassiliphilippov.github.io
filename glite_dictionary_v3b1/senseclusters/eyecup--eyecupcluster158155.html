eyecup

noun
vessel for washing or medicating the eye
1.4 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈaɪ.kʌp/ (AmE), /ˈaɪ.kʌp/ (BrE)
a small cup with a curved rim to fit the eye socket, used to apply medicated or cleansing solutions to the eyeball

Examples

A eyecup for washing
After splashing my eye with dust, I used an eyecup to wash it out.
The doctor filled the eyecup with saline solution and placed it gently over my eye.
You should rinse the eyecup thoroughly before each use to avoid infection.
