Septuagesima

noun
third Sunday before Lent
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛptjuəˈdʒɛsɪmə / (AmE), / ˌsɛptjuːəˈdʒɛsɪmə / (BrE)
The third Sunday before Lent in the Christian liturgical calendar, or the ninth Sunday before Easter.

Examples

Septuagesima Sunday
Hymns and liturgies specific to Septuagesima were sung at the morning service.
Many traditional churches still observe Septuagesima as the official start of pre-Lenten preparations.
The feast of Septuagesima has largely faded from modern calendars, but remains important for some denominations.
