cooling tower

noun
industrial structure for cooling water
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːlɪŋ ˌtaʊər / (AmE), / ˈkuːlɪŋ ˌtaʊə / (BrE)
A tall, typically cylindrical structure used in industry to lower the temperature of water by allowing it to partially evaporate before reuse.

Examples

A cooling tower structure
A thick plume of mist rose above the power plant’s cooling tower on the chilly morning.
Routine maintenance is critical to keep the cooling tower operating safely and effectively.
The factory built a new cooling tower to improve its energy efficiency.
