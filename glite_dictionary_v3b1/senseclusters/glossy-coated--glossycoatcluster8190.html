glossy-coated

adjective
having shiny, smooth hair or fur
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɑsi ˌkoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡlɒsi ˌkəʊtɪd / (BrE)
(of animals) having a coat or hair that is shiny and smooth looking.

Examples

A glossy-coated horse
A glossy-coated horse often indicates good health and a proper diet.
She brushed her cat daily to keep it glossy-coated and free of tangles.
The glossy-coated dog caught everyone's attention at the park.
