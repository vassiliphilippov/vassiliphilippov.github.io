sea urchin

noun
spiny-shelled marine animal
5.4
Pronunciation: / si ˈɝː.tʃɪn / (AmE), / si ˈɜː.tʃɪn / (BrE)
A small, round marine animal with a spiny shell, commonly found in shallow waters.

Examples

A sea urchin animal
Many species of sea urchin feed on algae growing on the ocean floor.
