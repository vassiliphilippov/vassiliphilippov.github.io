lower deck

noun
deck beneath the main deck on a ship
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊər dɛk / (AmE), / ˈləʊə dɛk / (BrE)
the deck on a ship located below the main deck, often used for crew accommodation or storage.

Examples

The lower deck cabin
Because he was a junior member of the crew, his cabin was on the lower deck.
Most of the cargo was stored on the lower deck to keep it away from seawater.
The sailors gathered on the lower deck during the evening meal.
