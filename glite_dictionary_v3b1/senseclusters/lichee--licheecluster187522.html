lichee

noun
tropical fruit tree with edible fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈliː.tʃi / (AmE), / ˈliː.tʃi / (BrE)
A tropical evergreen tree native to China, cultivated for its sweet, edible fruit with rough red skin and translucent flesh.

Examples

Lichee tree
I tried a lichee for the first time and loved its sweet, juicy taste.
The orchard was filled with lichee trees bearing clusters of ripe fruit.
They served chilled lichee with cream for dessert at the banquet.
