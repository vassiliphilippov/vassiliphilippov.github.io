suggestible

adjective
easily influenced by suggestions
Pronunciation: / səgˈdʒɛstəbəl / (AmE), / səgˈdʒɛstəbəl / (BrE)
easily influenced by the suggestions or opinions of others; susceptible to suggestion.

Examples

Easily suggestible person
Because she was in a vulnerable state, she was particularly suggestible at the time.
Children are especially suggestible and can be influenced by persuasive advertising.
The hypnotist claimed that only suggestible people would respond to his commands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gullible - easily fooled or deceived
  • synonym: influencable - capable of being influenced
  • antonym: resistant - not easily affected or influenced
