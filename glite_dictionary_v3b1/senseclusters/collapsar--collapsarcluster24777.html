collapsar

noun
collapsed star remnant
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈlæpsɑr / (AmE), / kəˈlæpsɑː / (BrE)
a massive star that collapses under its own gravity to form a black hole, often producing powerful gamma-ray bursts
