polyvinyl

noun
synthetic plastic material
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈvaɪnəl / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈvaɪnəl / (BrE)
a synthetic plastic material made from polymers of vinyl compounds, used in various products

Examples

A polyvinyl pipe
The pipes in the new building are made of polyvinyl for extra durability.
Many modern wallpapers use polyvinyl to make them washable and long-lasting.
The laboratory stored chemicals in containers made from polyvinyl to prevent leaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: PVC - polyvinyl chloride, a common type of polyvinyl plastic
  • synonym: plastic - a synthetic material made from polymers
