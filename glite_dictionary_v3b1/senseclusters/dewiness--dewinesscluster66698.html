dewiness

noun
quality of being covered with dew
6.0 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàduÀê.i.n…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàdjuÀê.i.n…ôs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being moist or covered with dew, often appearing fresh or slightly wet.

Examples

Natural dewiness of skin
Her skin had a natural dewiness that made her look radiant and refreshed.
The morning grass shimmered with a delicate dewiness that faded as the sun rose.
They admired the dewiness on the petals after the light rain.

Synonyms

  • moistness
    1.1 0.17/m
    moistness is a light, even wetness often on skin or soil, while dewiness is moisture from morning dew that looks fresh.
  • dampness
    1.0 1.22/m
    dampness is a cold, unwanted wetness in places like basements or clothing, while dewiness is fresh surface moisture seen in the morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dewy - covered with dew or resembling dew
  • antonym: dryness - the state of being dry, lacking moisture
