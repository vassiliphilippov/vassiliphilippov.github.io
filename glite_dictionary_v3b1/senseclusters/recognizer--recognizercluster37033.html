recognizer

noun
person or device that identifies
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkəɡˌnaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈrɛkəɡˌnaɪzə / (BrE)
a person or device that identifies or detects something, especially by analyzing input, features, or patterns

Examples

Facial recognizer

A speech recognizer can convert spoken words into text quickly and accurately.
As a skilled recognizer of rare plants, she was often asked for help by botanists.
