star-glory

noun
tropical climbing plant with red star-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / stɑr ˈɡlɔri / (AmE), / stɑː ˈɡlɔːri / (BrE)
A tropical American annual climbing plant (Ipomoea quamoclit) with red or occasionally white star-shaped flowers and finely divided leaves, widely naturalized outside its native range.

Examples

Red star-glory flowers
Gardeners often plant star-glory along fences so its vines can climb and create a colorful display.
In warm climates, star-glory can grow quickly and spread easily beyond cultivated areas.
The star-glory is prized for its bright red flowers that attract hummingbirds to the garden.
