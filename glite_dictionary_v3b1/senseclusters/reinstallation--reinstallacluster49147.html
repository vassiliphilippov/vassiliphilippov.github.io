reinstallation

noun
installing again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌɪnstəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌɪnstəˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of installing something again, especially software or equipment

Examples

Software reinstallation
After a system crash, the reinstallation of the operating system was necessary.
The technician completed the reinstallation of the printer drivers in just a few minutes.
Sometimes a fresh reinstallation can solve persistent software problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: installation - the act of putting something in place for use
  • antonym: removal - the act of taking something away or off
