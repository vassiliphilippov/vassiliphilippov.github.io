Synonyms

  • change
    1.1 377/m
    change is a general word for something becoming different in any way, while alteration often names a particular change to one thing.
  • modification
    3.9 18/m
    modification is a deliberate change made to improve or fit something, while alteration is a neutral word for any kind of change.
  • revision
    2.9 13/m
    revision is a careful reworking to correct or improve a text or plan, while alteration means a general change in form.
  • reworking
    3.9 0.61/m
    reworking is changing something by editing or reshaping its parts, while alteration simply names any change without showing process.
  • adjustment
    2.9 12/m
    adjustment is a small, careful change to make something fit or work better, while alteration can be small or large.
