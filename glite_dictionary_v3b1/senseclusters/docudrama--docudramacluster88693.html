docudrama

noun
film or TV program dramatizing real events
6.4 0.31/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd…ëk.juÀådr…ëÀê.m…ô / (AmE), / Ààd…ík.juÀådr…ëÀê.m…ô / (BrE)
