cranberry sauce

noun
sweet sauce made from cranberries
Pronunciation: / ˈkrænˌbɛri sɔs / (AmE), / ˈkranˌbɛri sɔːs / (BrE)
A sweet sauce made from cranberries and sugar, commonly served as a condiment with roast meats, especially turkey.

Examples

Homemade cranberry sauce
She made homemade cranberry sauce using fresh cranberries.
The tartness of cranberry sauce complements the richness of roast meats.
We had cranberry sauce with our Thanksgiving turkey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cranberry jelly - A gelatinous variant of cranberry sauce, often canned
