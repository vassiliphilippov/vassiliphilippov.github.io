longhand

noun
ordinary cursive handwriting
1.9 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈlɔːŋ.hænd/ (AmE), /ˈlɒŋ.hænd/ (BrE)
ordinary handwriting in which each letter is written out fully and is cursively connected, as opposed to typing or shorthand.

Examples

A longhand letter
He wrote the entire report in longhand before typing it up.

Some students prefer taking notes in longhand rather than on a laptop.
