eclosion

noun
sudden emergence
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkloʊʒən / (AmE), / ɪˈkləʊʒən / (BrE)
the sudden emergence or coming into prominence of something, such as an idea, movement, or talent

Examples

Eclosion of ideas
The eclosion of new technologies has transformed the way we communicate.
Historians studied the eclosion of modern art in the early twentieth century.
The festival celebrated the eclosion of young artists who challenged traditional forms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appearance - the act of becoming visible or noticeable
  • antonym: decline - a gradual decrease or loss of importance
  • synonym: emergence - the process of coming into view or becoming important
