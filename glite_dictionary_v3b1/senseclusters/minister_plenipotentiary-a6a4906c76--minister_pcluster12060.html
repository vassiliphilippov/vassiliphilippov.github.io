minister plenipotentiary

noun
diplomatic official below ambassador
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnɪstər plɛnɪˌpɑːtəntieri / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪstə ˌplɛnɪˌpɒtənˈtɪəri / (BrE)
A diplomatic official ranking below an ambassador and having full authority to represent their government, but with less status than an ambassador.

Examples

A minister plenipotentiary official
As a minister plenipotentiary, she was authorized to sign agreements on behalf of her country.
Historically, a minister plenipotentiary ranked just below an ambassador in diplomatic missions.
The president appointed a minister plenipotentiary to negotiate the terms of the treaty.
