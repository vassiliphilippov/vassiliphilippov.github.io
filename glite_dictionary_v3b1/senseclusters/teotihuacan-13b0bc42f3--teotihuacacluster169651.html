Teotihuacan

proper noun
ancient Mexican city
Pronunciation: / teɪˌoʊtiwɑːˈkɑːn / (AmE), / teɪˌɒtɪwɑːˈkæn / (BrE)
an ancient city and civilization in central Mexico, famous for its large pyramids and archaeological ruins
