narcoleptic

adjective
relating to narcolepsy
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑr.kəˈlɛp.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɑː.kəˈlep.tɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by narcolepsy, a disorder characterized by excessive daytime sleepiness and sudden sleep attacks.

Examples

Narcoleptic symptoms
People with a narcoleptic condition often struggle to stay awake during the day.
Some medications can help manage narcoleptic episodes and improve quality of life.
The patient's narcoleptic symptoms began in adolescence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: narcolepsy - A disorder characterized by uncontrollable episodes of deep sleep.
