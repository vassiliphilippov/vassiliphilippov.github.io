homebred

noun
locally bred individual
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊm.bred/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊm.bred/ (BrE)
a person or animal that has been bred, raised, or produced in one's own home or country rather than imported

Examples

A homebred dog
The winning dog at the show was a proud homebred from a local kennel.
Many farmers prefer to raise homebred livestock for better adaptation to the environment.
The athlete was a homebred, having trained in her hometown since childhood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: import - a person or animal brought in from another place or country
  • synonym: native - a person or animal born in a particular place
