AWD

noun
vehicle traction system
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ ˌdʌbəlˈjuː ˈdiː / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˌdʌbəlˈjuː ˈdiː / (BrE)
a system in vehicles that sends power to all four wheels to improve traction and handling

Examples

AWD system
Many modern crossovers come standard with AWD to help drivers in slippery conditions.
He chose a car with AWD because he often drives on rough mountain roads.
The dealership explained the benefits of AWD for families living in snowy areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: four-wheel drive - a vehicle system that powers all four wheels
  • antonym: two-wheel drive - a vehicle system that powers only two wheels
