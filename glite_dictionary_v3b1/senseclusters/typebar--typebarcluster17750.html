typebar

noun
typewriter metal arm
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪpˌbɑr / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpˌbɑː / (BrE)
a metal part in a typewriter that strikes the ribbon to print a character on paper

Examples

The typebar jammed
The typebar jammed, so the letter did not appear on the page.
Each typebar in the old typewriter had a different letter or symbol at its end.
He carefully cleaned the typebars to keep the typewriter working smoothly.
