shovel

noun
amount held by a shovel
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌvəl / (AmE), / ˈʃʌvəl / (BrE)
An amount of something that fills a shovel.

Examples

A shovel of sand
Add a shovel of gravel to the mix before stirring.
He tossed another shovel of coal onto the fire.
They moved the dirt one shovel at a time.
