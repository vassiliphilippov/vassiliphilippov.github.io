profiteering

adjective
seeking unfair profit
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑːfɪˈtɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌprɒfɪˈtɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
seeking to make unfair or excessive profits, especially by taking advantage of a situation

Examples

Profiteering companies
The government introduced new laws to stop profiteering practices during the crisis.
Many people criticized the profiteering companies for raising prices on essential goods.
Authorities are investigating several profiteering businesses accused of exploiting shortages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exploitative - taking unfair advantage of others for personal gain
  • antonym: fair - acting in a just and honest way
