idocrase

noun
a mineral gemstone found in limestone
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪdəˌkreɪs / (AmE), / ˈɪdəʊˌkreɪs / (BrE)
a green, yellow, or brown mineral consisting of a hydrated silicate, occurring as crystals in limestone and used as a gemstone
