St Patrick's Day

noun
Irish holiday on March 17; celebrates Ireland's patron saint
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈpætrɪks deɪ / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈpætrɪks deɪ / (BrE)
A public holiday celebrated on 17 March, commemorating St Patrick, the patron saint of Ireland, widely observed by Irish people and recognized around the world with festivities and parades.

Examples

St Patrick's Day parade
Every year, people all over the world celebrate St Patrick's Day with parades and green clothing.
On St Patrick's Day, many landmarks are illuminated in green to honor Irish heritage.
We planned a special dinner for St Patrick's Day and invited some friends to join us.
