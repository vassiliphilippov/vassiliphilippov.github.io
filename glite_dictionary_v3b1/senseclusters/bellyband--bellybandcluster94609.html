bellyband

noun
maternity support belt
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛliˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈbɛliˌbænd / (BrE)
a wide band, belt, or wrap worn around the abdomen during pregnancy or after childbirth to support the belly and lower back.

Examples

Maternity bellyband
She wore a maternity bellyband to support her growing belly during late pregnancy.
After the birth, the nurse recommended a soft postpartum bellyband to ease abdominal discomfort.
Many runners use a compression bellyband during exercise to support core muscles after surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maternity belt - a supportive belt worn during pregnancy to relieve back and abdominal strain
  • synonym: support belt - a band or belt that provides extra support to the abdomen or lower back
