remittal

noun
formal act of remission or forgiveness
6.1
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɪtəl / (AmE), / rɪˈmɪtəl / (BrE)
the act of absolving, remitting, or forgiving, especially as a formal pronouncement of pardon in a religious or legal context

Examples

Remittal of sentence
His legal team appealed for the remittal of his sentence, citing good behavior.
The ceremony ended with a formal remittal of the congregation's transgressions.
The priest granted remittal of sins after hearing her confession.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absolution - formal release from guilt, obligation, or punishment
  • synonym: remission - the cancellation of a debt, charge, or penalty
