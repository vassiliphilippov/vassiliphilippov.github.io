pout

noun
a freshwater catfish in eastern US
Pronunciation: / paʊt / (AmE), / paʊt / (BrE)
A type of freshwater catfish found in eastern United States, especially the brown bullhead (Ameiurus nebulosus).

Examples

A brown pout
Anglers often use worms as bait to attract pouts in these waters.
The children caught a small pout while fishing in the creek.
The local pond is known for its healthy population of pout and sunfish.
