northerly

adverb
toward the north
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrðərli / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðəli / (BrE)
towards the north; in a northern direction

Examples

Travel northerly
The birds began to migrate northerly as the weather warmed.
The path curves northerly along the riverbank.
We drove northerly until we reached the mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: northward - moving or facing toward the north
  • antonym: southerly - towards the south; in a southern direction
