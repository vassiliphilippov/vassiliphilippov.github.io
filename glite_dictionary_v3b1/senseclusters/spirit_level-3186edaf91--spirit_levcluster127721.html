spirit level

noun
device for checking level surfaces
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪt ˌlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪt ˌlɛvəl / (BrE)
a device consisting of a sealed tube containing liquid and an air bubble, used to check if a surface is exactly horizontal or vertical

Examples

A spirit level tool
A spirit level is an essential tool for any carpenter.
He placed the spirit level on the shelf to ensure it was perfectly flat.
She checked the surface with a spirit level before installing the countertop.
