poufed

adjective
puffed or bouffant style
7.0
Pronunciation: / puːft / (AmE), / puːft / (BrE)
styled or shaped in a puffed, rounded, or bouffant manner, especially referring to hair or fabric

Examples

Poufed hair style
She wore her hair poufed for the special occasion, adding extra volume on top.
The dress had poufed sleeves that made it look elegant and dramatic.
In the 1980s, many people preferred a poufed hairstyle for parties and events.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flat - not raised or puffed; lacking volume
