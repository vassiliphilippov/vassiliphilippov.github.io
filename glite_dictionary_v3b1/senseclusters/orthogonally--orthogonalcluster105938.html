orthogonally

adverb
at right angles
8.1
Pronunciation: /ɔːr.ˈθɑː.gə.nəl.iː/ (AmE), /ɔː.ˈθɒ.gə.nəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a manner at right angles or independently with respect to something else

Examples

Intersect orthogonally at right angles
The two streets intersect orthogonally at the city center.
In mathematics, vectors are said to be related orthogonally if they meet at a right angle.
The designer arranged the furniture orthogonally to maximize space in the room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: parallel - in a manner that is always the same distance apart and never meets
  • synonym: perpendicularly - in a manner forming a right angle
