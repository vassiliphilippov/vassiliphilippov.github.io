the bad place

noun
euphemism for hell or a place of punishment
Pronunciation: / ðə bæd pleɪs / (AmE), / ðə bæd pleɪs / (BrE)
a euphemistic term for hell or a place of punishment; also used metaphorically for any very negative or unpleasant place or situation

Examples

The bad place of punishment
After the scandal, the company was in the bad place with the public.
He tried hard to avoid ending up in the bad place because of his actions.
Some people believe that telling lies will send you to the bad place.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heaven - the place or state of eternal happiness after death
  • synonym: hell - the place or state of eternal punishment after death
