laburnum

noun
ornamental tree with yellow flowers
7.6
Pronunciation: / ləˈbɜrnəm / (AmE), / ləˈbɜːnəm / (BrE)
A small tree or shrub with bright yellow flowers, all parts of which are poisonous.

Examples

Bright laburnum flowers
Children should be warned not to eat any part of the laburnum as it is poisonous.
The laburnum in our garden is covered in golden blossoms every spring.
The avenue was lined with blooming laburnum trees, their yellow flowers bright against the green leaves.
