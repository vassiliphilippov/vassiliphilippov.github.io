gladly

adverb
with pleasure; willingly
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlædli / (AmE), / ˈɡladli / (BrE)
In a willing or happy manner; with pleasure.

Examples

To gladly help
He gladly volunteered to organize the event.
I would gladly help you move if you need an extra hand.
She gladly accepted the invitation to the celebration.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reluctantly - In an unwilling and hesitant way
  • synonym: willingly - In a way that shows readiness or consent; voluntarily
