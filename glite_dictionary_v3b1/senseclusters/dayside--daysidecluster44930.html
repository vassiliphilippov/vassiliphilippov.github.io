dayside

adjective
sunlit side
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈdeɪsaɪd / (BrE)
relating to or located on the side of a planet, moon, or other body that is facing the sun and is illuminated.

Examples

Dayside temperature
Scientists observed the dayside atmosphere of the planet to study its weather patterns.
Temperatures on the dayside can be much higher than on the nightside.
The probe sent back images of the dayside surface covered in bright sunlight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nightside - the side of a planet or moon facing away from the sun
  • synonym: sunlit - illuminated by sunlight
