pride

noun
group of lions
Pronunciation: / praɪd / (AmE), / praɪd / (BrE)
a group of lions forming a social unit living together

Examples

A pride of lions
A pride of lions lounged in the shade beneath the trees.
The safari guide pointed out a large pride resting near the waterhole.
Young males often leave their pride to start their own groups.
