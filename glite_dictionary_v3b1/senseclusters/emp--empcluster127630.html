emp

verb
to disable electronics
Pronunciation: / iː ɛm piː / (AmE), / iː ɛm piː / (BrE)
to disable or destroy electronic devices or systems by exposing them to an electromagnetic pulse

Examples

To emp the devices
The hackers planned to emp the security cameras before breaking into the building.
A powerful device was used to emp all the enemy's communication equipment during the operation.
If you emp a car, its electronic systems will stop working instantly.

  • synonym: disable - to make something unable to function
  • antonym: repair - to fix or restore something to working order
