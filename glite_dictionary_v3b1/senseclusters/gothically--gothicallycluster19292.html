gothically

adverb
in goth style
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːθɪkli / (AmE), / ˈɡɒθɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner characteristic of the goth subculture, especially in style, appearance, or attitude

Examples

Gothically dressed teens
She decorated her room gothically, with dark curtains and antique candle holders.
The band performed gothically, using dramatic lighting and somber costumes on stage.
He always dresses gothically, favoring black clothes and silver jewelry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gothic - relating to the goth subculture or style
