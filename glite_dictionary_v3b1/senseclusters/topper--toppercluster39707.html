topper

noun
worker who adds or makes tops
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːpər / (AmE), / ˈtɒpə / (BrE)
a worker who makes or adds the top to something, especially in manufacturing or production processes

Examples

Jar topper
Each topper was responsible for adding the lids to hundreds of jars per day.
He worked as a topper in the hat factory, carefully attaching brims to each hat.
The candy company needed an experienced topper to finish the chocolate boxes.
