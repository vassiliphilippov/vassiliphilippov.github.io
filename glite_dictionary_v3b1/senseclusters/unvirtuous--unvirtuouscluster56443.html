unvirtuous

adjective
lacking virtue
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvɜːrtʃuəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈvɜːtjuəs / (BrE)
lacking moral goodness or virtue; not behaving in a morally good way

Examples

An unvirtuous act
He regretted his unvirtuous actions after reflecting on his behavior.
The novel's main character is known for her unvirtuous choices and lack of remorse.
Society often criticizes unvirtuous conduct, especially when it harms others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immoral - not conforming to accepted standards of morality
  • antonym: virtuous - having or showing high moral standards
