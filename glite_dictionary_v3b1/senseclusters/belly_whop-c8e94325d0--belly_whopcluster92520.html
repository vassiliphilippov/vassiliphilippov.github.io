belly whop

noun
dive landing flat on the belly
8.8 7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛli wɑp / (AmE), / ˈbɛli wɒp / (BrE)
a dive in which the front of the body, especially the abdomen, strikes the water flatly and forcefully

Examples

A belly whop dive

Kids often challenge each other to do the biggest belly whop at the pool.
The crowd winced as she attempted a dive but ended up with a loud belly whop.
