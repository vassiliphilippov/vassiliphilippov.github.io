luminesce

verb
emit light without heat
Pronunciation: / ˌluːməˈnɛs / (AmE), / ˌluː.mɪˈnɛs / (BrE)
To emit light not caused by heat; to exhibit luminescence.

Examples

To luminesce softly
New paint technologies allow surfaces to luminesce without any electrical power.
Some deep-sea creatures luminesce to attract prey in complete darkness.
The chemical compounds will luminesce under ultraviolet light during the experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fluoresce - to emit light by fluorescence
  • synonym: glow - to emit a steady light, often soft or subdued
