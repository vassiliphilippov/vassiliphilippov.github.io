toast mistress

noun
woman acting as a toastmaster
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊst ˌmɪstrəs / (AmE), / ˈtəʊst ˌmɪstrɪs / (BrE)
A woman who presides over a banquet or formal event as a toastmaster, introducing speakers and ensuring the proceedings run smoothly.

Examples

The toast mistress speaks
At the awards dinner, the toast mistress ensured the program ran on time.
She was honored to serve as toast mistress for the alumni celebration.
The toast mistress introduced the keynote speaker with grace and humor.
