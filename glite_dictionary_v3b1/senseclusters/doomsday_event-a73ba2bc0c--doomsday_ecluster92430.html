doomsday event

noun
catastrophic or apocalyptic occurrence
4.0 8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈduːmz.deɪ ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈduːmz.deɪ ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
