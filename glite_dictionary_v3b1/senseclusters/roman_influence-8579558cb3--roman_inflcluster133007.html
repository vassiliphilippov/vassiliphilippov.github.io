Roman influence

noun
impact of ancient Roman design or culture
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊ.mən ˈɪn.flu.əns / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.mən ˈɪn.flu.əns / (BrE)
the impact or effect of ancient Roman culture, design, or style on later periods or creations

Examples

Roman influence on art
Her jewelry collection reveals the subtle Roman influence in the intricate metalwork.
Many Renaissance artists were deeply inspired by Roman influence when creating their masterpieces.
The new government building clearly shows a strong Roman influence in its columns and arches.
