weightlessness

noun
feeling of freedom
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪtləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈweɪtləsnɪs / (BrE)
a feeling of being light, free, or unburdened, especially from worries or stress

Examples

A feeling of weightlessness
After finishing the project, he felt a sense of weightlessness as his stress disappeared.
There was a surprising weightlessness in her voice after she received the good news.
The vacation brought him a rare weightlessness he had not felt in years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heaviness - a state of feeling weighed down or burdened
  • synonym: lightness - a state of being free from heaviness or burden
