tubenose

noun
seabird with tubular nostrils
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːbˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈtjuːbˌnəʊz / (BrE)
a seabird with tubular nostrils, such as an albatross or petrel, adapted for life at sea

Examples

A tubenose seabird
The tubenose glided effortlessly above the waves, searching for food far from land.
Scientists study the migration patterns of the tubenose to understand ocean currents.
Many species of tubenose are threatened by plastic pollution in the sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: albatross - a large seabird with long wings, part of the tubenose group
  • synonym: petrel - a small to medium-sized seabird with tubular nostrils
