shibuichi

noun
Japanese copper-silver alloy
Pronunciation: / ʃiːˈbuːiːtʃi / (AmE), / ʃɪˈbuːɪtʃi / (BrE)
a Japanese alloy of copper and silver, traditionally used in decorative metalwork

Examples

Shibuichi alloy
The sword's handle was decorated with shibuichi to create a unique, subtle color.
Collectors admire the soft gray tones of shibuichi in traditional Japanese ornaments.
The artisan chose shibuichi for its elegant appearance in the custom brooch.
