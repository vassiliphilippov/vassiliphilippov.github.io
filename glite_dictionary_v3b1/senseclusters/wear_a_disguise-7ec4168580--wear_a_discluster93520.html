wear a disguise

verb
dress to conceal identity
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɛr ə dɪsˈɡaɪz / (AmE), / wɛə ə dɪsˈɡaɪz / (BrE)
to dress in clothing or use a costume to conceal one's real identity

Examples

To wear a disguise for play
For the play, each actor was required to wear a disguise suited to their character.
She decided to wear a disguise when going out so the paparazzi wouldn't spot her.
The spy had to wear a disguise to avoid being recognized by his enemies.
