categorization

noun
the act of grouping into categories
3.1 3.6
Pronunciation: /kæt.ə.ɡə.rɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /kæt.ə.ɡə.rə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kæt.ə.ɡə.rɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /kæt.ə.ɡə.rə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or process of putting people, things, or ideas into groups based on shared qualities or characteristics
