hadrosaur

noun
duck-billed herbivorous dinosaur
9.1 8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈhæ.drə.sɑːr/ /; / /ˈhæ.drə.sɔːr/ (AmE), /ˈhæ.drə.sɔː/ (BrE)
