semiperiphery

noun
intermediate position
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈpɛrəˌfɪri / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈpɛrɪfəri / (BrE)
a group, region, or country that is between the most developed (core) and least developed (periphery) areas in a system, often used in world systems theory
