baking form

noun
container or mold for baking
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪkɪŋ fɔrm / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkɪŋ fɔːm / (BrE)
a pan, mold, or container used for shaping and holding dough or batter as it bakes; available in various shapes and sizes

Examples

A baking form pan
Different recipes require a specific baking form to achieve the best result.
She greased the baking form before pouring in the cake batter.
The pastry held its shape perfectly after being removed from the baking form.

  • synonym: cake tin - a metal container for baking cakes, esp. in British English
