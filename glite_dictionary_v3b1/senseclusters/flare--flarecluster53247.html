flare

noun
a sudden outburst of emotion
Pronunciation: / flɛr / (AmE), / fleə / (BrE)
a sudden intense outburst of emotion

Examples

Flare of anger
His temper erupted in a sudden flare during the heated debate.
She felt a flare of jealousy when she saw her friend with the new phone.
There was a visible flare of excitement in the crowd when the band took the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eruption - a violent release, often of emotion or activity
  • synonym: outburst - a sudden release of strong emotion
