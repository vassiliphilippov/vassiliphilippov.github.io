nuclearization

noun
acquiring nuclear capability
Pronunciation: / ˌnuːkliərɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnjuːkliərɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of developing, acquiring, or equipping with nuclear power or nuclear weapons

Examples

Nuclearization of a country
The nuclearization of the country changed the balance of power in the region.
International talks focused on preventing further nuclearization in unstable areas.
Concerns about nuclearization have led to new arms control agreements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: denuclearization - the process of removing or banning nuclear weapons or power
  • synonym: proliferation - the rapid increase or spread of something, especially weapons
