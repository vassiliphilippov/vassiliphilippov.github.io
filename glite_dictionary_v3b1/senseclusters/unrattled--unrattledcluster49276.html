unrattled

adjective
calm under pressure
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrætəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈrætəld / (BrE)
not upset, worried, or disturbed, especially in a difficult or stressful situation

Examples

Unrattled under pressure
Despite the sudden power outage, the manager stayed unrattled and calmly directed everyone to safety.
He remained unrattled during the interview, even when asked challenging questions.
The team was unrattled by the early setback and quickly regained their focus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or anger
  • synonym: composed - having control of one's emotions; not agitated
  • antonym: disturbed - feeling worried or troubled
  • antonym: nervous - easily upset or agitated
