spraying

noun
dispersion of substances on crops
Pronunciation: / ˈspreɪ.ɪŋ /; / ˈspreɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspreɪ.ɪŋ /; / ˈspreɪɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of applying a liquid in fine droplets, often using a sprayer (for example, spraying crops with chemicals or spraying water)

Examples
