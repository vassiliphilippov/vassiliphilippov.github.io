ever-blooming

adjective
continually flourishing
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɛvər ˌbluːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛvə ˌbluːmɪŋ / (BrE)
continually active, productive, or flourishing over a long period, often used figuratively

Examples

Ever-blooming energy
Her ever-blooming enthusiasm inspired everyone on the team to work harder.
The city is known for its ever-blooming cultural scene, with festivals throughout the year.
He admired her ever-blooming creativity, which brought fresh ideas to every project.

  • antonym: temporary - lasting for only a limited period of time
  • synonym: unceasing - never stopping; constant
