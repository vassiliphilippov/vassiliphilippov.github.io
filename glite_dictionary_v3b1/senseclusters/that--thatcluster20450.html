that

pronoun
demonstrative pronoun
Pronunciation: / ðæt / (AmE), / ðæt / (BrE)
a demonstrative pronoun used to refer to a specific person, thing, or idea previously mentioned or clearly identified

Examples

That is mine
I like the red car, but that one is too expensive for me.
She pointed to the painting and said that was her favorite.
If you want a snack, that is the best choice on the table.

Related Terms

  • antonym: this - a demonstrative pronoun referring to something near the speaker
  • degree higher: those - a demonstrative pronoun referring to multiple things farther from the speaker
