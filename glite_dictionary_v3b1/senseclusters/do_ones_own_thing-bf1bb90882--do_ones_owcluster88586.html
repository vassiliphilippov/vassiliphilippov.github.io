do one's own thing

verb
act independently; follow one's own interests
1.0
Pronunciation: / du wʌnz oʊn θɪŋ /; / duː wʌnz oʊn θɪŋ / (AmE), / duː wʌnz əʊn θɪŋ / (BrE)
to act or behave independently, following your own interests or preferences instead of conforming to others

Examples

He doesn't care about trends; he just likes to do his own thing.
While everyone else joined the tour, I chose to do my own thing for the afternoon.
