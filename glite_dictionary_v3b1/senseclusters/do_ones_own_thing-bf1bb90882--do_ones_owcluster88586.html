do one's own thing

verb
act independently; follow one's own interests
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: /duː wʌnz oʊn θɪŋ/ /; / /duː wʌns oʊn θɪŋ/ /; / /duː wʌnz əʊn θɪŋ/ (AmE), /duː wʌnz əʊn θɪŋ/ /; / /duː wʌns əʊn θɪŋ/ (BrE)
