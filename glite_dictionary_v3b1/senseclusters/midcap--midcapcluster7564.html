midcap

adjective
medium market value
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌkæp / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌkæp / (BrE)
describing a company with a medium-sized market capitalization, typically between small-cap and large-cap companies

Examples

Midcap company
Many investors diversify by including midcap stocks in their portfolios.
The fund focuses on midcap companies that show strong growth potential.
Analysts predict that midcap firms will outperform both small-cap and large-cap stocks this year.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: large-cap - describing a company with a large market capitalization
  • degree lower: small-cap - describing a company with a small market capitalization
