gloving

noun
performance with gloves
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlʌvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡlʌvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or practice of wearing gloves, especially as a form of performance art involving expressive hand movements

Examples

Gloving dance show
At the festival, his gloving performance amazed the crowd with colorful lights and quick hand movements.
She practices gloving every evening to improve her skills for the upcoming dance competition.
Many people enjoy watching gloving videos online to learn new tricks and routines.

  • synonym: mime - the theatrical technique of expressing action or emotion without words
