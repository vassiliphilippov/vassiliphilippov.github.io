news

noun
recent and important information or reports
Pronunciation: / nuz / (AmE), / njuːz / (BrE)
information about recent or important events, especially as reported by newspapers, radio, or television

Examples

Breaking news story
Good news arrived in the form of a job offer.
She called her family immediately after hearing the news about the accident.
We watched the evening news to learn more about the developing story.
Breaking news interrupted the television program with an urgent announcement.
I read the news in the local newspaper every morning.
The news of their engagement spread quickly through town.

Related Terms

  • synonym: announcement - a public or formal statement about a fact, occurrence, or intention
  • degree lower: gossip - casual or unconstrained conversation about others, often unconfirmed
  • synonym: report - an account or statement describing events or situations
