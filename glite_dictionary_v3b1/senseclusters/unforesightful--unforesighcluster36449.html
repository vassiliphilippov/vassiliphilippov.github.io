unforesightful

adjective
lacking foresight or anticipation
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.fɔrˈsaɪt.fəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.fɔːˈsaɪt.fəl / (BrE)
Lacking the ability to foresee or anticipate future developments; lacking foresight.

Examples

An unforesightful decision
He accused the management team of being unforesightful in their approach to the crisis.
Some consider cutting research funding an unforesightful move that could harm future innovation.
The board's unforesightful decision led to unexpected difficulties a year later.

Related Terms

  • antonym: farseeing - having foresight; able to anticipate future events
  • synonym: shortsighted - lacking imagination or foresight
