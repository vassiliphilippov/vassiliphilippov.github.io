plated

adjective
coated or arranged
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈpleɪtɪd / (BrE)

Examples

Gold plated cutlery

Each dish was carefully plated to impress the guests at the restaurant.
The antique mirror has a plated frame that shines in the sunlight.
