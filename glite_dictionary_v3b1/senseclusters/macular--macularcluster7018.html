macular

adjective
of the eye's macula
Pronunciation: / ˈmækjələr / (AmE), / ˈmakjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to the macula, the central area of the retina in the eye responsible for sharp vision

Examples

Macular degeneration
The doctor explained that macular degeneration can lead to vision loss in older adults.
She underwent a special test to check for macular abnormalities in her eyes.
Early detection of macular changes is important for effective treatment.

  • synonym: retinal - relating to the retina of the eye
