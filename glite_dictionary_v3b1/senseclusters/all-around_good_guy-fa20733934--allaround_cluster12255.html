all-around good guy

noun
a generally kind and helpful man
Pronunciation: / ˌɔl əˈraʊnd ɡʊd ɡaɪ / (AmE), / ˌɔːl əˈraʊnd ɡʊd ɡaɪ / (BrE)
A man who is generally kind, helpful, and well-liked by others.

Examples

An all-around good guy
Despite his success, Mark remains an all-around good guy who treats everyone with respect.
Everyone likes Tom because he’s an all-around good guy who always offers to help.
Jake proved he was an all-around good guy by volunteering at the animal shelter.
