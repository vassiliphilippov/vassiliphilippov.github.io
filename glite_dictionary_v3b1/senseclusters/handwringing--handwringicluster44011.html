handwringing

adjective
excessively anxious
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌrɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌrɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
showing or expressing excessive worry, anxiety, or concern, especially in a way that does not lead to action.

Examples

Handwringing response
The committee's handwringing approach delayed any real progress on the issue.
Many criticized the handwringing statements from officials as lacking concrete solutions.
Instead of handwringing debates, the team decided to take immediate action.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anxious - feeling or showing worry or unease
  • antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or worry
