mud dauber

noun
type of wasp that builds mud nests
7.9
Pronunciation: / mʌd ˈdɔbɚ / (AmE), / mʌd ˈdɔːbə / (BrE)
A type of wasp that builds nests out of mud and provisions them with paralyzed insects for its larvae.

Examples

A mud dauber wasp
A mud dauber built its nest on the side of our garage last summer.
The mud dauber captures spiders to feed its growing larvae.
Unlike hornets, the mud dauber is rarely aggressive towards humans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wasp - a stinging insect related to bees and ants
