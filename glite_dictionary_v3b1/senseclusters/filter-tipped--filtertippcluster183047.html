filter-tipped

adjective
having a filtering tip (of cigarettes/cigars)
4.5 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪl.tər.tɪpt/ /; / /ˈfɪl.tər tɪpt/ (AmE), /ˈfɪl.tə.tɪpt/ /; / /ˈfɪl.tə tɪpt/ (BrE)
(of a cigarette or cigar) having a tip that contains a filter to remove some substances from the smoke before it is inhaled.

Examples

Filter-tipped cigarettes
He only smokes filter-tipped cigarettes because he thinks they're less harsh.
Many modern cigarettes are filter-tipped to reduce tar and nicotine inhalation.
The new brand introduced a filter-tipped cigar for health-conscious smokers.
