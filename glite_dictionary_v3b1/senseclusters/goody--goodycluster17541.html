goody

noun
small treat or gift
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʊdi / (AmE), / ˈɡʊdi / (BrE)
a small treat, gift, or enjoyable item, especially one given to children or as a reward

Examples

A bag of goodies
The teacher brought a bag of goodies to share with the class after the test.
Children rushed to the table to grab their favorite goody from the party bags.
At Halloween, neighbors hand out goodies to trick-or-treaters in colorful wrappers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gift - something given to someone without payment
  • synonym: treat - something special and enjoyable, often a small gift or food
