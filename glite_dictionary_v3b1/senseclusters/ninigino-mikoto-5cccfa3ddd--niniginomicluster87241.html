Ninigino-Mikoto

proper noun
first ruler of Japan in mythology
9.9
Pronunciation: / niːniːgiːnoʊ mɪˈkoʊtoʊ / (AmE), / niːniːgiːnəʊ mɪˈkəʊtəʊ / (BrE)
In Japanese mythology, the grandson of the sun goddess Amaterasu and regarded as the first ruler of Japan.

Examples

Ninigino-Mikoto in mythology
According to legend, Ninigino-Mikoto descended from heaven to rule over Japan.
Many Shinto rituals honor Ninigino-Mikoto as a founding ancestor.
The story of Ninigino-Mikoto plays a central role in early Japanese mythology.
