cryptococcosis

noun
fungal infection affecting lungs and nervous system
9.5 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪp.toʊ.kɑːˈkoʊ.sɪs / (AmE), / ˌkrɪp.tə.kɒˈkəʊ.sɪs / (BrE)
A fungal infection primarily affecting the lungs, that can spread to the nervous system, caused by fungi of the genus Cryptococcus.
