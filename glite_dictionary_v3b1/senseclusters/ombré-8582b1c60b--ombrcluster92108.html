ombré

adjective
blended color gradient
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːmˌbreɪ / (AmE), / ˈɒmˌbreɪ / (BrE)
having colors or tones that gradually blend into each other, typically moving from light to dark

Examples

Ombré hair color
She wore an ombré dress that faded from deep blue at the top to pale blue at the bottom.
The artist painted the sky in an ombré effect, blending orange into pink and purple.
Many people love the ombré look for their hair, with darker roots and lighter ends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gradient - a gradual change in color or shade
  • antonym: solid - having a single, uniform color or shade
