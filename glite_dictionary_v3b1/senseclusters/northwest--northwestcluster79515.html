northwest

noun
direction halfway between north and west
1.0
Pronunciation: / nɔrθˈwɛst /; / ˌnɔrθˈwɛst / (AmE), / nɔːθˈwɛst /; / ˌnɔːθˈwɛst / (BrE)
the direction or compass point halfway between north and west (315 degrees), or the area towards this direction

Examples

The hikers set their course for the northwest to reach the mountain pass.
Winds were coming from the northwest all afternoon.
