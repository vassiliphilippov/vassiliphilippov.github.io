intensional

adjective
concerning meaning or properties, not individual examples
6.9 9.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛnʃənəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛnʃənəl / (BrE)
Relating to or involving the set of attributes or properties that determine the reference of a word, phrase, or concept, rather than the actual things it refers to.
