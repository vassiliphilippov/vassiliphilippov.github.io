intensional

adjective
concerning meaning or properties, not individual examples
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛnʃənəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛnʃənəl / (BrE)
Relating to or involving the set of attributes or properties that determine the reference of a word, phrase, or concept, rather than the actual things it refers to.

Examples

Intensional meaning
She distinguished between the intensional properties of a concept and its real-world examples.
Some debates in logic revolve around intensional versus extensional definitions.
The intensional definition of a triangle is a three-sided polygon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extensional - relating to actual things a word refers to, rather than attributes
