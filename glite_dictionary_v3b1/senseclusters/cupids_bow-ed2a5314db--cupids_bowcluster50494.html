Cupid's bow

noun
curved bow with outward-curving ends
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːpɪdz ˌboʊ / (AmE), / ˈkjuːpɪdz ˌbəʊ / (BrE)
A bow with a curved shape and ends that curve outward in the opposite direction, traditionally associated with representations of Cupid.

Examples

An old Cupid's bow display
He admired the ornate carvings on the ancient Cupid's bow displayed in the museum.

The design of the Cupid's bow is elegant and reminiscent of classical mythology.
