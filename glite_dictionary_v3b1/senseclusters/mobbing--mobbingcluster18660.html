mobbing

noun
crowd attack
7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑː.bɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈmɒb.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of a crowd surrounding, overwhelming, or attacking a person or place, often in an aggressive or chaotic way

Examples

Fan mobbing outside
The celebrity was the target of mobbing as fans rushed toward her outside the theater.
Security struggled to prevent mobbing at the entrance when the store opened for the big sale.
After the match, mobbing by excited supporters made it difficult for the players to leave the field.
