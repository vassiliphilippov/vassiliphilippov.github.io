self-absorbed

adjective
preoccupied with oneself
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf əbˈzɔrbd / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf əbˈzɔːbd / (BrE)
Only concerned with your own interests or thoughts and not aware of or interested in other people.

Examples

A self-absorbed person
His self-absorbed attitude made collaboration difficult for the whole team.
She became so self-absorbed that she barely noticed her friends needed help.
Too much time alone can make anyone a bit self-absorbed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egocentric - thinking only of oneself, without regard for the feelings or desires of others
  • antonym: selfless - concerned more with the needs and wishes of others than with one's own
