self-absorbed

adjective
preoccupied with oneself
6.3 1.63/m
Pronunciation: / Àås…õlf …ôbÀàz…îrbd / (AmE), / Àås…õlf …ôbÀàz…îÀêbd / (BrE)
Only concerned with your own interests or thoughts and not aware of or interested in other people.

Examples

A self-absorbed person
His self-absorbed attitude made collaboration difficult for the whole team.
She became so self-absorbed that she barely noticed her friends needed help.
Too much time alone can make anyone a bit self-absorbed.

Synonyms

  • self-involved
    7.0 0.39/m
    self-involved focuses on your own activities and thoughts and often ignores others, while self-absorbed is inwardly preoccupied and not aware of people.
  • self-centered
    3.9 2.69/m
    self-centered cares mostly about personal needs and goals, while self-absorbed is lost in private thoughts and not tuned to others.
  • egocentric
    6.8 0.00/m
    egocentric sees events only from their own view and cannot grasp others' feelings, while self-absorbed stays wrapped in personal thoughts.
  • selfish
    1.0 16/m
    selfish acts to gain personal benefit even if it hurts others, while self-absorbed is mainly occupied by inner thoughts and feelings.
  • egoistic
    7.4 0.47/m
    egoistic puts self-interest first as a guiding choice, while self-absorbed is unaware of others and wrapped in private thoughts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egocentric - thinking only of oneself, without regard for the feelings or desires of others
  • antonym: selfless - concerned more with the needs and wishes of others than with one's own
