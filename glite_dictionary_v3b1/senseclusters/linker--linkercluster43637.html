linker

noun
knitting seam machine
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋkə / (BrE)
a machine used in knitting and hosiery production to join the edges of knitted fabric by linking loops and finishing seams

Examples

Knitting linker machine
The technician operated the linker to create smooth, strong seams on the socks.
A modern linker can join hundreds of garments in a single day.
After knitting the pieces, they used a linker to finish the edges neatly.
