Mysticeti

noun
suborder of baleen whales
9.9 9.1
Pronunciation: / mɪˈstɪsətaɪ /; / mɪˈstɪsəti / (AmE), / mɪˈstɪsəti / (BrE)
a suborder of large marine mammals known as baleen whales, including right whales, rorquals, blue whales, and humpback whales, characterized by having baleen plates instead of teeth
