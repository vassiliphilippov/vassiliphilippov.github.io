ante

verb
to pay in advance
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈæn.tiː/ /; / /ˈæn.tɪ/ (AmE), /ˈæn.tiː/ /; / /ˈæn.tə/ (BrE)
to pay or provide money in advance as a required contribution, deposit, or guarantee

Examples

To ante a fee
Each member anted a small fee to join the club.
Before the auction began, bidders anteed a deposit to participate.
The company anteed a large sum to secure the contract.

Related Terms
