star fruit

noun
tropical fruit shaped like a star
2.3 3.2
Pronunciation: /stɑːr fruːt/ (AmE), /stɑː fruːt/ (BrE)
a tropical fruit with a yellow-brown color and deep ridges, whose cross-section resembles a star; eaten raw, in salads, or as a dessert when ripe

Examples

Ripe star fruit

The unique shape of the star fruit makes it popular as a garnish.
