star fruit

noun
tropical fruit shaped like a star
3.8 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / Ààst…ër Àåfrut / (AmE), / Ààst…ëÀê ÀåfruÀêt / (BrE)
a tropical fruit with a yellow-brown color and deep ridges, whose cross-section resembles a star; eaten raw, in salads, or as a dessert when ripe

Examples

Ripe star fruit
Have you ever tasted ripe star fruit on its own as a dessert?
She added sliced star fruit to the salad for a tropical twist.
The unique shape of the star fruit makes it popular as a garnish.

Synonyms

  • carambola
    9.2 0.05/m
