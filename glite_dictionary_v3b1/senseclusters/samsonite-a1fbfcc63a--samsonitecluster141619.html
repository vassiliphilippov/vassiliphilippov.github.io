Samsonite

adjective
of Samsonite brand
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmsəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsæmsəˌnaɪt / (BrE)
made by or relating to the Samsonite brand, especially used before nouns to describe luggage or travel products

Examples

Samsonite suitcase
She packed her clothes in a sturdy Samsonite suitcase for the long journey.
The store offers a wide selection of Samsonite luggage for frequent travelers.
He prefers Samsonite bags because they are known for their durability.
