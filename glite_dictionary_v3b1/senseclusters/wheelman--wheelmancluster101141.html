wheelman

noun
getaway driver
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlmæn / (AmE), / ˈwiːlmæn / (BrE)
a person who drives a vehicle during a crime, especially as the getaway driver

Examples

Getaway wheelman
The wheelman kept the engine running while the others robbed the store.
After the heist, the wheelman sped away before the police arrived.
Police arrested the suspected wheelman just a few blocks from the scene.
The wheelman kept the engine running while the others hurried out of the store.
Police arrested the suspected wheelman after a high-speed chase through the city.
In many heist movies, the wheelman is just as important as the mastermind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accomplice - a person who helps another commit a crime
  • synonym: driver - a person who operates a vehicle
  • synonym: getaway driver - a person who drives a vehicle to help criminals escape
  • antonym: passenger - a person who is traveling in a vehicle but is not driving
  • antonym: police officer - a member of the police force
