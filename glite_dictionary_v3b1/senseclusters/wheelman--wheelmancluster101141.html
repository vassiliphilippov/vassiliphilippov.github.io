wheelman

noun
getaway driver
8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈwiːl.mən/ /; / /ˈwiːl.mæn/ (AmE), /ˈwiːl.mən/ /; / /ˈwiːl.mæn/ (BrE)

Examples
