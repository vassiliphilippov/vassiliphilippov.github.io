madia oil plant

noun
South American oil-producing herb
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdiə ɔɪl plænt / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdɪə ɔɪl plɑːnt / (BrE)
A South American herb with sticky glandular foliage that is cultivated as a source of madia oil.
