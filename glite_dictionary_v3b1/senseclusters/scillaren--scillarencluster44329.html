scillaren

noun
a glycoside from squill used as medicine
Pronunciation: / sɪˈlɛrən / (AmE), / sɪˈlærən / (BrE)
a crystalline steroid glycoside obtained from squill (Drimia maritima), used in medicine as a cardiac stimulant.

Examples

Scillaren is used in some countries as a treatment for certain heart conditions.
