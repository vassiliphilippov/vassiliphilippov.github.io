cryocautery

noun
destruction of tissue by freezing
Pronunciation: / ˌkraɪ.oʊˈkɔː.tɚ.i / (AmE), / ˌkraɪ.əʊˈkɔː.tər.i / (BrE)
the application of extreme cold to destroy tissue, typically as a medical treatment

Examples

Cryocautery treatment
Cryocautery offers a minimally invasive alternative to surgical excision in some cases.
Doctors often use cryocautery in the treatment of warts and other benign growths.
The dermatologist recommended cryocautery to remove the skin lesion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryotherapy - treatment by applying cold to tissues
