phantasmal

adjective
resembling a phantom; ghostly
8.9
Pronunciation: / fænˈtæzməl / (AmE), / fanˈtazməl / (BrE)
resembling a phantom; ghostly or unreal in appearance or effect.

Examples

A phantasmal figure
In the moonlight, the fog gave the entire street a phantasmal quality.
She felt a phantasmal hand on her shoulder, sending chills down her spine.
The old mansion was said to be haunted by a phantasmal figure that vanished in daylight.
