Synonyms

  • freeing
    3.4 0.1/m
    freeing is a direct act of making people or things free, while liberation often means freeing people from oppression.
  • emancipation
    6.7 2/m
    emancipation is a formal legal freeing from slavery or social limits, while liberation focuses on ending oppression or control.
  • release
    1.8 13/m
    release is letting someone out of physical confinement or restraint, while liberation means freeing groups from long-term oppression or unfair treatment.
