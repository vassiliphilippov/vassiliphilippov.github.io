psychoactive substance

noun
a drug altering mood or perception
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊˈæktɪv ˈsʌbstəns / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊˈæktɪv ˈsʌbstəns / (BrE)
a drug or chemical that affects the mind, producing changes in mood, perception, or behavior
