uxorious

adjective
excessively devoted to one's wife
8.3
Pronunciation: / ʌgˈzɔriəs / (AmE), / ʌkˈsɔːrɪəs / (BrE)
having or showing an excessive or foolish fondness for one's wife

Examples

An uxorious husband
He was teased for being almost uxorious in the way he deferred to his wife's opinion.
In his letters, he appeared uxorious, mentioning his wife with great affection and pride.
Some see his uxorious devotion as endearing, while others view it as a weakness.
