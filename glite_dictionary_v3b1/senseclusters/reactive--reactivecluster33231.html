reactive

adjective
readily undergoing reactions
3.0
Pronunciation: / riˈæktɪv / (AmE), / riˈæktɪv / (BrE)
undergoing or participating readily in chemical or other types of reactions

Examples

Reactive chemicals
Chlorine is much more reactive than nitrogen under normal conditions.
Sodium is a highly reactive metal and must be stored carefully.
Some gases become more reactive at higher temperatures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inert - not tending to react chemically
