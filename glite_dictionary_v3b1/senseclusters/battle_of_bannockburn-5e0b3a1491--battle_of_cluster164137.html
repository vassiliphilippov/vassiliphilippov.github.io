Battle of Bannockburn

noun
decisive 1314 battle securing Scottish independence
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbænəkˌbɝn ˈbætəl / (AmE), / ˈbænəkˌbɜːn ˈbætəl / (BrE)
a decisive battle in 1314 near Stirling, Scotland, in which Scottish forces under Robert the Bruce defeated the English, securing Scottish independence
