towel rack

noun
bar or frame for hanging towels
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊəl ræk / (AmE), / ˈtaʊəl ræk / (BrE)
a bar or frame for hanging towels, usually found in a bathroom or kitchen

Examples

Bathroom towel rack
She hung her wet bath towel on the towel rack to dry.
The hotel bathroom had a heated towel rack for extra comfort.
We installed a new towel rack next to the shower.

Related Terms

  • synonym: towel rail - a bar fixed to a wall for hanging towels, chiefly British usage
