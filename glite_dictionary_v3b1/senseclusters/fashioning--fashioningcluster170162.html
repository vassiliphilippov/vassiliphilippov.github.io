fashioning

noun
the act of shaping or making
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfæʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfæʃənɪŋ / (BrE)
the action of shaping or making something, especially by hand or through a particular process

Examples

The fashioning of jewelry
Her interests include the fashioning of handmade jewelry.
The fashioning of new policies is a complex process.
The fashioning of the sculpture took several weeks of careful work.
