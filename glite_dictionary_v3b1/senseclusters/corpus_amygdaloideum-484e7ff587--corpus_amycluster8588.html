corpus amygdaloideum

noun
brain structure involved in emotion
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔr.pəs əˌmɪɡ.dəˌlɔɪˈdi.əm / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.pəs əˌmɪɡ.dəˌlɔɪˈdiː.əm / (BrE)
an almond-shaped mass of nuclei located in the anterior temporal lobe of the brain, forming part of the limbic system and playing a key role in motivation and emotional processing

Examples
