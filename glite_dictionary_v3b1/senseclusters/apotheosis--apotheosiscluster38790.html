apotheosis

noun
the perfect example; pinnacle of perfection
9.6 8.5
Pronunciation: /ə.pɑː.θiː.ˈəʊ.səs/ /; / /ə.pɑː.θiː.ˈoʊ.sɪs/ (AmE), /ə.pɒ.θiː.ˈəʊ.sɪs/ (BrE)
the highest point or most perfect example of something; the elevation of someone to divine status

Examples
