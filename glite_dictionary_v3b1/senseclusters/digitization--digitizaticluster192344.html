digitization

noun
conversion of analog data to digital format
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪdʒɪtəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌdɪdʒ.ɪ.t̬əˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪdʒɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌdɪdʒ.ɪ.taɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of converting information from an analog format into a digital format that can be processed by computers

Examples

The digitization of records
Many industries are investing heavily in the digitization of their records.
The digitization of old photographs makes them easier to store and share online.
The library is currently undergoing digitization to preserve its rare manuscripts.
Our library is undergoing digitisation to make its resources available online.
Proper digitisation ensures that the quality of the original image is maintained.
The digitisation of historical documents allows for easier access and preservation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digitalisation - the adoption or increase in use of digital technologies
  • synonym: digitalization - the process of adopting digital technology to change a business model or process
