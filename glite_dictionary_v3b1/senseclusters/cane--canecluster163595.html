cane

noun
a walking stick used for support
3.1
Pronunciation: / keɪn / (AmE), / keɪn / (BrE)
a stick used to help someone walk, especially one used by elderly or injured people for support

Examples

A walking cane
After his surgery, he relied on a cane to get around the house.
She gripped her cane as she crossed the busy street.
The old man set his cane beside the door before sitting down.
