unsettledness

noun
unresolved state
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛtəldnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛtəldnəs / (BrE)
the state of being not yet resolved, paid, or finalized, especially regarding accounts or debts

Examples

Unsettledness of accounts
The unsettledness of the company's finances worried the investors.
After months of unsettledness, the two parties finally reached an agreement.
The unsettledness in the market made it difficult to plan future investments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: resolution - the act of solving or settling something
  • synonym: uncertainty - the state of being unsure or not settled
