marsupial mole

noun
small burrowing Australian marsupial
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɑrˈsuːpiəl moʊl / (AmE), / mɑːˈsuːpiəl məʊl / (BrE)
a small, burrowing Australian marsupial that resembles a mole in appearance and habits

Examples

The marsupial mole burrows
Researchers were fascinated by the adaptations that allow the marsupial mole to thrive in such harsh environments.
The marsupial mole spends most of its life underground, rarely coming to the surface.
Unlike true moles, the marsupial mole is native only to the deserts of Australia.
