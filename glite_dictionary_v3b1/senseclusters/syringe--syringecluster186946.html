syringe

verb
spray or irrigate with a syringe
7.3
Pronunciation: / sɪˈrɪndʒ /; / səˈrɪndʒ / (AmE), / sɪˈrɪndʒ / (BrE)
to inject, spray, or irrigate liquid into or onto something using a syringe

Examples

To syringe a wound
It is important to syringe wounds carefully to prevent infection.
The nurse syringed the patient's ear to remove the blockage.
The veterinarian syringed the dog's wound with saline solution.
The doctor carefully syringed the solution into the patient's ear to remove the blockage.
After the wound was cleaned, the nurse syringed saline to rinse away any debris.
Veterinarians often syringe medicine into an animal's mouth when it cannot swallow pills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inject - to force a liquid into something, especially with a syringe
  • synonym: irrigate - to wash out a body part with a flow of liquid
