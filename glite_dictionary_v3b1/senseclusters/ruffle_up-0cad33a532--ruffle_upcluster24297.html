ruffle up

verb
make a surface uneven
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌfəl ʌp / (AmE), / ˈrʌfl ʌp / (BrE)
to disturb the smoothness of a surface, making it uneven or wrinkled

Examples

To ruffle up hair
His hand ruffled up her hair, making it look wild and tousled.
She ruffled up the bedspread before lying down for a nap.
The wind began to ruffle up the calm surface of the lake.
