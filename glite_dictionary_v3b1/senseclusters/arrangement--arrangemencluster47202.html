arrangement

noun
orderly grouping; organized placement
Pronunciation: / əˈreɪndʒmənt / (AmE), / əˈreɪndʒmənt / (BrE)
the way things are organized, ordered, or positioned; the result of arranging things

Examples

Neat arrangement of flowers
She admired the careful arrangement of books on the library shelf.
The florist created a beautiful arrangement of roses and lilies for the wedding.
The seating arrangement at the dinner party ensured that everyone felt included.
The arrangement of chapters in this book helps readers understand the topic progressively.
The arrangement of the seats made the room feel much more spacious.
We need a new arrangement for storing these files so they are easier to find.
She admired the careful arrangement of flowers in the vase.
The arrangement of tables allowed everyone to see the speaker clearly.
The arrangement of the paintings on the wall made the room feel larger.
