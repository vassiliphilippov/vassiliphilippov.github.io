questor

noun
person seeking something
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɛstər / (AmE), / ˈkwɛstə / (BrE)
a person who seeks or pursues something, especially a quest or goal

Examples

A questor seeks treasure
The questor traveled across deserts and mountains in search of the ancient artifact.
As a lifelong questor of knowledge, she never stopped learning new things.
In the story, the young questor faces many challenges on his journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: finder - a person who discovers or locates something
  • synonym: seeker - a person who looks for or tries to find something
