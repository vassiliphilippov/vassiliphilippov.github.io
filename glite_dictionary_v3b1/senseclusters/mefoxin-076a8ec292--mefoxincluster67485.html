Mefoxin

noun
brand name for antibiotic cefoxitin
Pronunciation: / ˈmiː.fɑk.sɪn / (AmE), / ˈmiː.fɒk.sɪn / (BrE)
A branded, broad-spectrum antibiotic used to treat bacterial infections; Mefoxin is the trade name for cefoxitin.

Examples

Mefoxin antibiotic
Pharmacists must monitor patients for allergic reactions when administering Mefoxin.
Some bacteria are resistant to penicillin but respond well to Mefoxin.
The doctor prescribed Mefoxin to treat the patient's postoperative infection.
