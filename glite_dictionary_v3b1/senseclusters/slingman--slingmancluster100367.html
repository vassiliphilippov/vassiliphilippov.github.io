slingman

noun
person carrying baby in sling
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪŋmæn / (AmE), / ˈslɪŋmæn / (BrE)
a person who carries or supports a baby or small child in a cloth sling worn across the body

Examples

Baby slingman
The slingman walked through the park with his baby comfortably resting against his chest.
At the community event, a slingman demonstrated different ways to tie a baby sling.
She admired the slingman who skillfully balanced shopping bags while carrying his child.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babywearer - a person who carries a baby using a sling or carrier
  • synonym: parent - a mother or father of a child
