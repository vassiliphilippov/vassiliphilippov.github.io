smilingly

adverb
in a smiling manner
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsmaɪlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsmaɪlɪŋli / (BrE)
In a smiling manner; with a smile.

Examples

To smilingly accept
He smilingly accepted the compliment, clearly pleased with himself.
She greeted the guests smilingly as they arrived at the party.
The teacher smilingly encouraged her students to keep trying, even when they made mistakes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheerfully - in a happy and optimistic way
  • antonym: grimly - in a serious, gloomy, or disapproving manner
