impression

noun
imitation of a person
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɛʃən / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛʃən / (BrE)
an imitation of a person's voice, mannerisms, or appearance, often made to entertain others

Examples

Funny impression of a celebrity
He did a hilarious impression of his teacher in class.
Her impression of the celebrity was so accurate that everyone laughed.
The comedian's impression of the president went viral online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imitation - the act of copying someone or something
