meshing

noun
generation of polygonal mesh for simulation or modelling

(in computing and engineering) the process of generating a polygonal or polyhedral mesh of a geometric model for numerical simulation or 3D rendering, dividing the domain into discrete elements.

Examples

3D model meshing
Before the simulation run, the team spent a day meshing the CAD model to avoid numerical errors.
Poor meshing produced distorted elements that invalidated the finite-element analysis results.
They refined the surface and improved meshing before running the aerodynamic simulation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: finite-element mesh - a discretized representation of a continuum used in finite-element analysis
  • synonym: mesh generation - the computational process of creating a mesh used for finite-element or finite-volume analysis
