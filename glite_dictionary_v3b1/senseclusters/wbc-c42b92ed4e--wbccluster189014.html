WBC

noun
abbreviation for 'white blood cell'
3.5 0.21/m
Pronunciation: / Àåd åb…ôl.juÀê.biÀêÀàsiÀê / (AmE), / Àåd åb…ôl.juÀê.biÀêÀàsiÀê / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'white blood cell'; a type of blood cell that engulfs and digests bacteria and fungi, playing a key role in the body's immune system

Examples

High WBC count
A high WBC level can indicate the presence of an infection.
During the test, a low WBC count was cause for medical concern.
The doctor checked the patient's WBC count to assess their immune system.
