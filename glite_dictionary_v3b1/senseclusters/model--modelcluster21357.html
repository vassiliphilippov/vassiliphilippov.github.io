model

noun
representation of something for study or demonstration
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑː.dəl /; / ˈmɑdəl /; / ˈmɑːdəl / (AmE), / ˈmɒd.əl /; / ˈmɒdəl / (BrE)
a representation or simulation of something, often on a smaller scale or in a simplified form, used to demonstrate, study, or predict its structure or function

  • synonym: replica - an exact copy of something, especially on a smaller scale
  • synonym: simulation - imitation of a real-world process or system over time
  • synonym: theory - a set of principles explaining a phenomenon
