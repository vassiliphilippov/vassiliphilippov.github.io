preborn

noun
unborn child
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpriˌbɔrn / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌbɔːn / (BrE)
a child or fetus that has not yet been born; an unborn baby

Examples

A preborn child
Doctors performed tests to assess the health of the preborn before the scheduled delivery.
Many laws are being debated regarding the rights of the preborn in different countries.
The nutrition of the mother can greatly affect the development of the preborn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fetus - an unborn offspring in the later stages of development
  • antonym: newborn - a baby that has just been born
  • synonym: unborn - not yet born; existing before birth
