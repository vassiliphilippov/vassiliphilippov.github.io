epilith

noun
organism growing on rock
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛpɪlɪθ / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪlɪθ / (BrE)
An organism, such as a plant or fungus, that grows upon the surface of a rock.

Examples

An epilith organism
Lichens are often studied as an epilith because of their ability to grow on bare stone.
Mosses are a common type of epilith found on shaded rock surfaces.
The scientist noted the presence of an unusual epilith thriving on the granite outcrop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epiphyte - a plant that grows on another plant
