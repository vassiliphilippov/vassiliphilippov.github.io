secondary school

noun
school between elementary and college
2.0 5.64/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…õk…ônd…õri skuÀêl / (AmE), / Ààs…õk…ônd…ôri skuÀêl / (BrE)
A school for students aged between about 11 and 18, intermediate between elementary school and college, typically including grades 9 to 12.

Examples

A secondary school
After graduating from secondary school, she decided to attend university.
Many students participate in sports teams during their years at secondary school.
Teachers at my secondary school encouraged students to think independently.

Synonyms

  • high school
    1.0 133/m
    high school is the common, everyday name for grades nine to twelve before college, while secondary school sounds more formal and broad.
  • senior high school
    1.8 0.72/m
    senior high school is a public school for grades nine to twelve after middle school, while secondary school is more general.
  • senior high
    2.1 0.77/m
    senior high refers to the upper grades after junior high, while secondary school covers the full secondary age range from eleven to eighteen.
