shrapnel

noun
explosion metal fragments
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʃræpnəl / (AmE), / ˈʃræpnəl / (BrE)
small pieces of metal that are scattered by an explosion, especially from a bomb or shell

Examples

Shrapnel wounds

Doctors removed several pieces of shrapnel from his leg after the accident.
