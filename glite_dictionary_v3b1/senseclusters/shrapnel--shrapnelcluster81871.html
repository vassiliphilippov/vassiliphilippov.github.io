shrapnel

noun
explosion metal fragments
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʃræpnəl / (AmE), / ˈʃræpnəl / (BrE)
small pieces of metal that are scattered by an explosion, especially from a bomb or shell

Examples

Shrapnel wounds
The explosion sent shrapnel flying in all directions, injuring several people nearby.
Doctors removed several pieces of shrapnel from his leg after the accident.
Soldiers wore protective gear to shield themselves from shrapnel during combat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debris - scattered pieces of waste or remains
  • synonym: fragment - a small piece broken off something
