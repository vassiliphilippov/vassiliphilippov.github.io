embalm

verb
preserve a dead body from decay
6.5 7.2
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbɑm / (AmE), / ɪmˈbɑːm / (BrE)
to preserve a dead body by treating it with special substances to prevent decay

Examples

To embalm a body
Ancient Egyptians would embalm their pharaohs for burial in the pyramids.
He learned how to embalm animals as part of his training as a taxidermist.
The funeral home will embalm the body before the service.
