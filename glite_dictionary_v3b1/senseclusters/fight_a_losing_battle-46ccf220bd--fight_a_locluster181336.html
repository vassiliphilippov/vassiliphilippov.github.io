fight a losing battle

verb
struggle unsuccessfully against the odds
Pronunciation: / faɪt ə ˈluːzɪŋ ˈbæt̬əl / (AmE), / faɪt ə ˈluːzɪŋ ˈbætəl / (BrE)
to try hard to achieve something when it is very unlikely that you will succeed

Examples

To fight a losing battle
Despite her hard work, she realized she was fighting a losing battle against the bureaucracy.
He kept fighting a losing battle to keep his business afloat during the recession.
They knew they were fighting a losing battle trying to convince him to change his mind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: struggle in vain - to try without success; to labor ineffectively
