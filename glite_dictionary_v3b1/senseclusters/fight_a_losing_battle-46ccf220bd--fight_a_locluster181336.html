fight a losing battle

verb
struggle unsuccessfully against the odds
4.5 5.2
Pronunciation: /faɪt ə ˈluː.zɪŋ ˈbæt.əl/ (AmE), /faɪt ə ˈluː.zɪŋ ˈbæt.əl/ (BrE)
