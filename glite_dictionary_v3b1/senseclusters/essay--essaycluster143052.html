essay

noun
short literary composition on a subject
Pronunciation: / ˈɛˌseɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛseɪ / (BrE)
a short piece of writing on a particular subject, often presenting the author's personal viewpoint or argument

Examples

A school essay
His essay challenges the common assumptions about technological progress.
I found her essay on modern art extremely insightful and well-argued.
She wrote an essay on the importance of environmental protection for her English class.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: article - a short piece of writing on a particular topic, often for a magazine or newspaper
  • degree lower: report - a detailed account or statement describing or analyzing an event or situation
  • degree higher: treatise - a formal and systematic written discourse on a subject
