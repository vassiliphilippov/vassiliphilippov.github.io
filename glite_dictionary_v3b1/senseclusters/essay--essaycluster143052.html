essay

noun
short literary composition on a subject
1.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈe.seɪ/ (AmE), /ˈe.seɪ/ (BrE)
a short piece of writing on a particular subject, often presenting the author's personal viewpoint or argument

Examples
