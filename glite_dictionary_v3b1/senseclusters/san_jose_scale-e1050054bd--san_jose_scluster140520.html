San Jose scale

noun
pest insect affecting fruit trees
9.3
Pronunciation: / sæn hoʊˈseɪ skeɪl / (AmE), / san həʊˈzeɪ skeɪl / (BrE)
a small insect native to East Asia, now established in the United States, that is a serious pest of fruit trees

Examples

San Jose scale insect
Orchard owners often inspect trees for signs of San Jose scale infestations in early spring.
Proper management is essential to control the spread of San Jose scale among fruit trees.
The San Jose scale can quickly infest and damage apple orchards if left untreated.
