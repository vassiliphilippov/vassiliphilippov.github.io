Isoetaceae

noun
family of aquatic plants (quillworts)
9.9
Pronunciation: / aɪˌsoʊɪˈteɪsiˌi / (AmE), / aɪˌsəʊɪˈtiːsiː / (BrE)
A family of aquatic or marsh plants known as quillworts, consisting of a single genus, Isoetes.

Examples

The Isoetaceae family
Members of the Isoetaceae are commonly called quillworts due to their grass-like appearance.
Scientists discovered a new species within the Isoetaceae near the lake's edge.
The Isoetaceae family is distributed worldwide in both temperate and tropical waters.
