oxygenator

noun
agent adding oxygen
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑksɪdʒəˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɒksɪdʒəˌneɪtə /; / ˈɒksɪdʒɪˌneɪtə / (BrE)
a device or substance that adds oxygen to something, especially in chemical, medical, or industrial settings
