uninspirable

adjective
not able to be inspired
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈspaɪrəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈspaɪrəbəl / (BrE)

The uninspirable atmosphere in the office made it hard for anyone to feel excited about their work.
