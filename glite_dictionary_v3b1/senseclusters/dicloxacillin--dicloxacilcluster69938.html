dicloxacillin

noun
antibiotic for staph infections
8.5
Pronunciation: /daɪ.klɑːk.sə.ˈsɪl.ɪn/ /; / /daɪ.klɔːk.sə.ˈsɪl.ɪn/ (AmE), /daɪ.klɒk.sə.ˈsɪl.ɪn/ (BrE)
