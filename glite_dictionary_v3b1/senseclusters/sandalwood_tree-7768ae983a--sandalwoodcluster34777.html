sandalwood tree

noun
tree with fragrant, yellowish heartwood
4.7 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsæn.dəl.wʊd triː/ /; / /ˈsæn.dəl.wʊd tʃriː/ (AmE), /ˈsæn.dəl.wʊd triː/ /; / /ˈsæn.dəl.wʊd tʃriː/ (BrE)
A tree native to Indonesia and Malaysia with fragrant, close-grained yellowish heartwood used in perfumery, woodworking, and as an insect repellent.

In Indonesia, the sandalwood tree is protected due to overharvesting for its valuable wood.
The sandalwood tree is highly valued for the aromatic oil extracted from its heartwood.
