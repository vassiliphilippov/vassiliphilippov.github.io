trading floor

noun
room in exchange where trading occurs
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪdɪŋ flɔr / (AmE), / ˈtreɪdɪŋ flɔː / (BrE)
the large central area in a stock exchange or commodity market where traders buy and sell financial securities or commodities, often in person

Examples

The trading floor area
She started her career working on the trading floor of the New York Stock Exchange.
The chaos on the trading floor intensified as the stock prices fluctuated wildly.
The energy on the trading floor is often overwhelming for newcomers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exchange - a central marketplace for buying and selling securities or commodities
