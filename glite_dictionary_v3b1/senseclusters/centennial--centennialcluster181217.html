centennial

noun
100th anniversary or its celebration
7.1
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈtɛniəl / (AmE), / sɛnˈtɛniəl / (BrE)
the 100th anniversary of a significant event, or the celebration of it

Examples

Centennial celebration
Many alumni returned to their university for the centennial celebration.
The city held a grand parade to mark the centennial of its founding.
This park was created in honor of the country's centennial in 1876.
