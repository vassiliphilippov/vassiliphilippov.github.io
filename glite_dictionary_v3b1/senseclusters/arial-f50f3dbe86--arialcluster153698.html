Arial

proper noun
sans-serif typeface
Pronunciation: / ˈɛəriəl / (AmE), / ˈɛːrɪəl / (BrE)
a widely used sans-serif typeface known for its clarity and readability in digital and printed text

Examples

Arial font style
The presentation looked professional because all the slides used Arial for the text.
Many websites choose Arial as their default font for easy reading.
She changed the document's font from Times New Roman to Arial for a more modern appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Helvetica - a popular sans-serif typeface similar to Arial
  • antonym: Times New Roman - a widely used serif typeface
