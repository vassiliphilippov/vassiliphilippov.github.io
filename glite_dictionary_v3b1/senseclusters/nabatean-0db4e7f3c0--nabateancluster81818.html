Nabatean

noun
ancient Semitic language
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnæbəˈtiːən / (AmE), / ˌnæbəˈtiːən / (BrE)
the ancient Semitic language spoken and written by the Nabateans, known from inscriptions and historical texts

Examples

Nabatean script
Archaeologists discovered a Nabatean inscription carved into the rock at Petra.
The Nabatean language provides valuable insight into ancient Middle Eastern trade routes.
Few people today can read the old Nabatean script found on these artifacts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Hebrew - a Semitic language of the ancient Hebrews
