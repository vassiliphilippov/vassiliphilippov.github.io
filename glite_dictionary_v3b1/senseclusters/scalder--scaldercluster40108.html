scalder

noun
person or machine for scalding
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔldər / (AmE), / ˈskɔːldə / (BrE)
a person or machine that heats or cleans something with boiling water or steam, especially to remove unwanted material

Examples

The poultry scalder
The poultry scalder is used to loosen feathers before plucking.
A modern scalder can process hundreds of chickens per hour in large factories.
The worker operated the scalder to clean the equipment with hot steam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boiler - a device that heats water or other fluids
  • synonym: washer - a machine or person that cleans something
