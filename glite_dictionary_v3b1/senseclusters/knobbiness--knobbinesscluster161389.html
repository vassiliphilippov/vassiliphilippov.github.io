knobbiness

noun
lumpy surface quality
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːbinəs / (AmE), / ˈnɒbinəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having small lumps or rounded bumps on the surface

Examples

The knobbiness of potato
The knobbiness of the potato made it difficult to peel quickly.
She ran her hand over the knobbiness of the old wooden table.
The knobbiness of the mountain path made walking a challenge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bumpiness - the quality of having uneven or raised areas
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of having an even, flat surface
