ELT

noun
data processing method
8.6
Pronunciation: / iː ɛl tiː / (AmE), / iː ɛl tiː / (BrE)
a data-processing method where data is extracted, loaded into a target system, and then transformed within that system

Examples

ELT process
Many modern data warehouses use ELT to handle large volumes of raw information efficiently.

Understanding ELT is important for anyone working in data engineering or analytics.
