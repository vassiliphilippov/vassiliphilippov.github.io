stunt man

noun
male performer of dangerous film stunts
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌnt mæn / (AmE), / ˈstʌnt man / (BrE)
A man employed to perform dangerous acts for actors in films or television, especially when the action requires special skills or involves risk.

Examples

A stunt man performs
A skilled stunt man is essential for shooting high-risk movie sequences safely.
After years as a stunt man, he transitioned into acting roles himself.
The stunt man jumped from the burning building to create a realistic action scene.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stuntwoman - A female stand-in for movie stars to perform dangerous stunts
