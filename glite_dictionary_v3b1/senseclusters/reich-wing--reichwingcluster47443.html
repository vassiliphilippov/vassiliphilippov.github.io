reich-wing

noun
pejorative for extreme right-wing views
Pronunciation: / raɪk wɪŋ / (AmE), / raɪk wɪŋ / (BrE)
A derogatory term for extreme or authoritarian right-wing political ideology or views, often with implied associations to fascism or Nazism.

Examples

The reich-wing views
His reich-wing comments on immigration sparked a strong backlash online.
Many criticized the reich-wing rhetoric for promoting authoritarian policies.
The newspaper accused the new government of having reich-wing tendencies.
