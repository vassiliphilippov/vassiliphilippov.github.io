typer

noun
person arranging type
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtaɪ.pər/ (AmE), /ˈtaɪ.pə/ (BrE)
a person who sets or arranges type for printing, especially by composing letters and spacing for printed pages

Examples

Newspaper typer
The typer spent hours arranging each letter by hand for the newspaper's front page.
In the old print shop, a skilled typer was essential for producing clear and accurate books.
Before computers, every magazine relied on a typer to prepare the layout for printing.
