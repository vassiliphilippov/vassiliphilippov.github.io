skyway

noun
elevated walkway
Pronunciation: / ˈskaɪˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌweɪ / (BrE)
an elevated or covered walkway or bridge that connects buildings above ground level

Examples

Downtown skyway
The downtown skyway allows people to walk between office buildings without going outside.
We admired the city view from the glass skyway connecting the two hotels.
During winter, many commuters use the skyway to avoid the cold streets below.
The two office towers are linked by a glass skyway that offers great city views.
During winter, people use the skyway to avoid walking outside in the cold.
A new skyway was built to connect the shopping mall to the hotel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: footbridge - a bridge designed for people to walk across
  • synonym: overpass - a bridge that carries one road or path over another
  • synonym: skybridge - an enclosed bridge connecting two buildings above ground
  • antonym: tunnel - an underground passage connecting places
  • synonym: walkway - a path or passage for walking
