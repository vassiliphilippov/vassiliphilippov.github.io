Magdalenian

noun
late Paleolithic culture
Pronunciation: / ˌmæɡdəˈleɪniən / (AmE), / ˌmæɡdəˈleɪniən / (BrE)
a prehistoric culture of the late Upper Paleolithic in western Europe, known for cave art and advanced bone and antler tools

Examples

The Magdalenian culture
The Magdalenian period is famous for its detailed cave paintings and carvings.
Researchers discovered new Magdalenian tools in a French archaeological site.
Many Magdalenian artifacts are displayed in museums across Europe.
