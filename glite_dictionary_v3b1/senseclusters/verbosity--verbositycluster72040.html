verbosity

noun
amount of detail in program logs
7.7
Pronunciation: /vɜːr.ˈbɑː.sə.tiː/ (AmE), /və.ˈbɒ.sə.tiː/ (BrE)
(computing) the degree of detail included in a program's messages or log output; an adjustable setting used to show more or less diagnostic information.
