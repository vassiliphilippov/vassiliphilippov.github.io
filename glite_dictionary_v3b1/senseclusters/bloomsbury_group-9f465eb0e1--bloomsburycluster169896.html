Bloomsbury Group

proper noun
group of early 20th-century London intellectuals
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbluːmz.bə.riː gruːp/ (AmE), /ˈbluːmz.bə.riː gruːp/ (BrE)
A group of English writers, thinkers, and artists who lived or worked in the Bloomsbury area of London in the early 20th century, known for their influence on literature and art and their unconventional attitudes.
