on-the-spot

adjective
at the scene of action
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɑn ðə ˈspɑt / (AmE), / ˌɒn ðə ˈspɒt / (BrE)

Examples

On-the-spot coverage

She made an on-the-spot decision to help the injured man.
The police conducted an on-the-spot investigation after the incident was reported.
