souse

verb
to soak or cook in a marinade
7.4
Pronunciation: / saʊs / (AmE), / saʊs / (BrE)
to soak or cook (food, especially fish or meat) in a marinade, typically with vinegar or brine

Examples

To souse meat
Before grilling the pork, she likes to souse it in her signature marinade.
The chef decided to souse the herring in a mixture of vinegar and spices overnight.
Traditional recipes often souse fish to preserve its flavor and texture.
