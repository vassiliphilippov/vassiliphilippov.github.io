immunoglobulin A

noun
an antibody class in mucosal tissues

Pronunciation: /ɪ.mjuː.nə.ˈg.lɑː.bjʊ.lɪn eɪ/ /; / /ɪ.mjuː.nə.ˈg.lɑː.bjə.lɪn eɪ/ (AmE), /ɪ.mjuː.nə.ˈg.lɒb.jʊ.lɪn eɪ/ /; / /ɪ.mjuː.nə.ˈg.lɒb.jə.lɪn eɪ/ (BrE)
