connective tissue

noun
linking or supporting element (literal or figurative)
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛk.tɪv ˈtɪʃ.uː / (AmE), / kəˈnɛk.tɪv ˈtɪs.juː / (BrE)
Something that serves to link, join, or support different parts or groups, either literally as a type of body tissue or figuratively as a unifying element.

Examples

Team connective tissue
Collaboration within teams often acts as the connective tissue holding organizations together.
Shared values can serve as the connective tissue among diverse community members.
Technology has become the connective tissue of modern communication networks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bond - a force or influence that unites people or groups
  • synonym: link - a relationship or connection between things
