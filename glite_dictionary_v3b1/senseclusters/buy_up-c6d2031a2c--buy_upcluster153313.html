buy up

Examples

To buy up farmland

The large corporation tried to buy up all the smaller competitors in the region.
When news of the takeover broke, investors started to buy up shares in the company.

Synonyms

  • buy out
    3.5 1.75/m
    buy out means buying a company's shares or a partner's stake to gain ownership, while buy up means buying most items to control supply.
  • take over
    3.8 9.27/m
    take over is gaining control of a company, often by acquisition or force, while buy up focuses on buying most stock to monopolize a market.
