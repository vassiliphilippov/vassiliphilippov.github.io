Synonyms

  • military headquarters
    3.5 0.2/m
    military headquarters is the main place where commanders oversee and direct armed forces, while headquarters can mean any directing unit.
  • general headquarters
    5.5 0.00/m
    general headquarters is the central command for large military operations, while headquarters can be any unit directing forces.
  • command post
    5.3 1/m
    command post is a nearby, often temporary field center for directing troops, while headquarters is a broader, permanent command base.
  • HQ
    3.4 2/m
    HQ is the short form used in writing and speech for a command location, while headquarters is the full formal term.
  • GHQ
    7.4 0.01/m
