headquarters

noun
military unit with commander and staff
2.5 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈhed.kwɔːr.tərz/ /; / /ˈhed.kɔːr.tərz/ (AmE), /ˈhed.kwɔː.təz/ /; / /ˈhed.kɔː.təz/ (BrE)

Examples
