aplenty

adverb
in abundance
Pronunciation: / əˈplɛnti / (AmE), / əˈplɛnti / (BrE)
in large amounts; in abundance; existing or available in great quantity

Examples

Desserts aplenty
There were desserts aplenty at the celebration.
Opportunities for adventure are aplenty in this vibrant city.
After the harvest, the market had fresh fruits aplenty for everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abundantly - in large quantities; plentifully
  • antonym: scarce - in short supply; not enough
