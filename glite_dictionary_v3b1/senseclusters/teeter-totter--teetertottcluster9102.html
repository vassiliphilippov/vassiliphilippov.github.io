teeter-totter

noun
playground equipment (seesaw)
Pronunciation: / ˈtitər ˌtɑtər / (AmE), / ˈtiːtə ˌtɒtə / (BrE)
A long board balanced on a central support, which children ride at either end to go up and down; a seesaw.

Examples

Playground teeter-totter
He fell off the teeter-totter and scraped his knee.
The children played for hours on the teeter-totter at the park.
There was a colorful teeter-totter in the school playground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seesaw - a similar playground apparatus, synonym in British English
