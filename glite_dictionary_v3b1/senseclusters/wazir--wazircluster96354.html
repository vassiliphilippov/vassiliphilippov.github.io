wazir

noun
Muslim minister; chess piece
9.9
Pronunciation: / wəˈzɪr / (AmE), / wəˈzɪə / (BrE)
a high-ranking official or minister in some Muslim countries, or a chess piece in certain chess variants

Examples

Wazir of the sultan
The wazir played a crucial role in advising the sultan on important decisions.
In some chess variants, the wazir moves one square in any direction.
The king trusted his wazir more than any other official in the court.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minister - a high-ranking government official
  • synonym: vizier - a high-ranking political advisor or minister in Muslim countries
