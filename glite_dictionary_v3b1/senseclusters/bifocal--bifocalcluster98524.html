bifocal

adjective
having two focal points; esp. for eyeglasses
3.3
Pronunciation: / baɪˈfoʊkəl / (AmE), / baɪˈfəʊkəl / (BrE)
having two focal points, especially describing eyeglass lenses with two different optical powers for near and distant vision

Examples

Bifocal glasses
He recently switched to bifocal glasses after struggling to read fine print.
My grandmother needs bifocal lenses to help her see both up close and at a distance.
The optician explained how bifocal lenses improve vision for people with presbyopia.
