longline

adjective
extended garment length
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌlaɪn / (BrE)
describing a garment that is longer than the usual style, often extending over the hips or thighs for extra coverage

Examples

Longline jacket style
The designer introduced a longline blazer that paired well with both jeans and skirts.
He prefers longline cardigans because they provide extra warmth and coverage.
A longline bra offers more support and a smoother silhouette under clothing.
