lossy

adjective
causing energy or information loss
9.9 6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈlɑː.siː/ /; / /ˈlɔː.siː/ (AmE), /ˈlɒ.siː/ (BrE)
(of a system or process) characterized by or causing the dissipation or irreversible loss of energy or information.

Examples

Lossy audio format
Audio files in lossy formats take up less space but sacrifice some sound quality.
Some image compression algorithms are lossy, meaning details are permanently discarded.
The circuit was found to be highly lossy, resulting in significant energy waste.
