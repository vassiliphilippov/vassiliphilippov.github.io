black book

noun
list of people out of favor
1.0
Pronunciation: / blæk bʊk / (AmE), / blæk bʊk / (BrE)
a list of people who are out of favor or in disgrace, often kept as a record of individuals to be avoided or punished

Examples

The black book list
After their argument, she joked that he was on her black book and shouldn't expect a call soon.
The detective discovered his name in the gangster's black book of enemies.
The manager kept a black book of employees who had broken company rules.
