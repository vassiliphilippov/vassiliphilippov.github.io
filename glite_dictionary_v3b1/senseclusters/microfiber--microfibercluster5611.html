microfiber

adjective
made of fine fibers
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌfaɪbər / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌfaɪbə / (BrE)
made from or relating to very fine synthetic fibers, especially used for fabrics or cleaning materials

Examples

Microfiber cleaning cloth
She bought a microfiber cloth to clean her glasses without scratching them.
The microfiber sheets felt soft and cool against his skin.
Many sports towels are microfiber because they absorb water quickly and dry fast.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cotton - made from the soft white fibers of the cotton plant
  • synonym: synthetic - made by chemical synthesis, especially to imitate a natural product
