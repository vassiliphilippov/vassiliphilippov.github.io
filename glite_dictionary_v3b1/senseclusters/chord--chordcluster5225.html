chord

noun
harmonious group of musical notes
Pronunciation: / kɔrd / (AmE), / kɔːd / (BrE)
a group of three or more musical notes played together, producing a harmonious sound

Examples

A piano chord
Learning how to form a basic chord is fundamental for any aspiring pianist.
The guitarist played a chord that filled the room with rich sound.
The song begins with a simple chord progression that repeats throughout.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmony - the combination of simultaneously sounded musical notes
  • antonym: melody - a sequence of single notes that is musically satisfying
