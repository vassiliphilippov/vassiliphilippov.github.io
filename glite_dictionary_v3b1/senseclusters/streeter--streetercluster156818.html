streeter

noun
person on the street
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːtər / (AmE), / ˈstriːtə / (BrE)
a person who spends a lot of time on the street, often socializing or interacting with others in public spaces

Examples

Friendly streeter
The friendly streeter greeted everyone as he walked down the avenue.
As a known streeter, she always had the latest news from the neighborhood.
Many streeters gather at the park to chat and watch the world go by.

Related Terms

  • antonym: homebody - a person who prefers staying at home
  • synonym: street person - someone who spends much of their time on the street
