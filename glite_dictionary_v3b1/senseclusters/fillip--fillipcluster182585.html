fillip

noun
stimulus; something that boosts
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪl.əp / (AmE), / ˈfɪl.ɪp / (BrE)
a thing that acts as a stimulus or boost to an activity or feeling; something that arouses or encourages

Examples

A fillip to energy
Her praise was just the fillip I needed to finish the project.
The company’s new marketing campaign gave a much-needed fillip to sales.
Winning the award provided a real fillip to his confidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incentive - a thing that motivates or encourages someone to do something
  • synonym: stimulus - something that encourages activity or feeling
