non-league

noun
amateur sports level
Pronunciation: / nɑn ˈliːɡ / (AmE), / nɒn ˈliːɡ / (BrE)
the system or level of amateur and semi-professional sports competitions outside the main professional leagues, especially in football (soccer).

Examples

The non-league system
After his injury, he continued playing in the non-league while working full-time.
Many talented players start their careers in non-league football before moving to professional teams.
The club was relegated from the main division and now competes in the non-league.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amateur - a person who takes part in a sport for pleasure, not as a job
  • antonym: league - an organized group of professional sports teams
