antitrypsin

noun
enzyme-inhibiting protein
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˈtrɪpsɪn /; / ˌæntiˈtrɪpsɪn / (AmE), / ˌæntɪˈtrɪpsɪn /; / ˌæntiˈtrɪpsɪn / (BrE)
a substance (especially a protein in the blood) that inhibits the enzyme trypsin
