unmercifully

adverb
without mercy or compassion
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɜrsɪfəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɜːsɪfəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no mercy or compassion; with harshness or cruelty.

Examples

To be unmercifully teased
He was unmercifully teased for his mistake in class.
The coach unmercifully pushed the team to practice for hours without a break.
The critics unmercifully attacked the new play in every major publication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mercilessly - in a merciless manner; without compassion
