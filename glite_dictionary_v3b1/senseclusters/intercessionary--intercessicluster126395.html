intercessionary

adjective
praying for others
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈsɛʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈsɛʃənəri / (BrE)
relating to the act of intervening or praying on behalf of others, especially in a religious context

Examples

Intercessionary prayer service
The church held an intercessionary service to pray for peace in the community.
She wrote an intercessionary letter on behalf of her friend, asking for leniency.
Many religious traditions include intercessionary prayers for those in need.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocative - acting in support of someone or something
  • synonym: mediatory - serving as a mediator or go-between
