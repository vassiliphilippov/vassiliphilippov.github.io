facecloth

noun
cloth for washing face
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪsˌklɔθ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪsˌklɒθ / (BrE)
a small, soft cloth used for washing the face

Examples

A soft facecloth
After a long day, he refreshed himself with a damp facecloth.
She packed a clean facecloth in her travel bag for the trip.
The hotel provided each guest with a fresh facecloth every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: washcloth - a small cloth for washing the body or face
