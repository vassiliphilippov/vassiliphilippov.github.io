cherrystone

noun
type of edible hard-shell clam
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛr.iˌstoʊn /; / ˈtʃɛriˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛr.iˌstəʊn /; / ˈtʃɛrɪstəʊn / (BrE)
a medium-sized hard-shell clam, larger than a littleneck and smaller than a chowder clam, often eaten raw or cooked
