juicehead

noun
juice enthusiast
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːsˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːsˌhɛd / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who drinks a lot of fruit juice or is very enthusiastic about fresh juices

Examples

A healthy juicehead
At the summer picnic, everyone teased him as a juicehead for drinking three glasses of orange juice.
My friend is such a juicehead that he owns his own juicer and tries new recipes every week.
The café is popular among juiceheads who enjoy fresh blends and healthy drinks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: juice lover - a person who loves drinking juice
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who never drinks alcohol
