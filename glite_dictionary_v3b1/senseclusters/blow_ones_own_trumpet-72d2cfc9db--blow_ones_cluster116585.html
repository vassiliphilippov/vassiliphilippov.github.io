blow one's own trumpet

verb
boast about oneself
3.8
Pronunciation: / bloʊ wʌnz oʊn ˈtrʌmpɪt / (AmE), / bləʊ wʌnz əʊn ˈtrʌmpɪt / (BrE)
to boast or talk proudly about your achievements or abilities

Examples

To blow one's own trumpet loudly
Although he rarely blows his own trumpet, his colleagues know how skilled he is.
It can be difficult for modest people to blow their own trumpet during job interviews.
She's always quick to blow her own trumpet whenever she completes a project.
It's better to be humble than to constantly blow your own trumpet.
She tends to blow her own trumpet whenever she talks about her career.
While he worked hard, he never liked to blow his own trumpet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blow one's own horn - to boast about oneself (alternate idiom, chiefly US)
  • synonym: boast - to talk with excessive pride about one's achievements
  • synonym: brag - to talk with excessive pride
  • antonym: modest - not talking about one's achievements
  • antonym: self-deprecate - to play down one's achievements or abilities
