to exercise authority over a country, people, or organization; to rule or control by law or power

Examples

To govern a country
After the revolution, a new party began to govern the country.
She hopes to govern in a fair and transparent manner if elected.
The committee was set up to govern the university's policies.

Synonyms

  • rule
    1.0 26/m
    rule is a formal term meaning to have authority over a country or people, while govern emphasizes official power and law.
  • rule over
    4.2 1.44/m
    rule over means to control people or a place directly, while govern suggests running a state with laws and institutions.
  • control
    2.4 93/m
    control is to direct or limit things and people in many situations, while govern refers to ruling a country or organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: control - to have power over or direct the actions of
  • antonym: obey - to follow commands or authority, opposite action to govern
  • synonym: rule - to exercise ultimate power or authority over a country or people
