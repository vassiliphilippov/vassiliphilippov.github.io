melt in the mouth

adjective
very tender, delicious food idiom
2.5 3.0
Pronunciation: /melt ɪn ðə maʊθ/ /; / /melt ɪn ðə maʊð/ (AmE), /melt ɪn ðə maʊθ/ /; / /melt ɪn ðə maʊð/ (BrE)
(of food) to be extremely tender, soft, or delicious, dissolving or becoming very easy to eat when put in the mouth.
