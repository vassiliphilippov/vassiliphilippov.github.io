ergonomist

noun
workplace design expert
Pronunciation: / ˌɜrɡəˈnɑːmɪst / (AmE), / ˌɜːɡəˈnɒmɪst / (BrE)
a specialist who studies and designs workplaces, products, or systems to improve comfort, safety, and efficiency for people

Examples

An ergonomist studies workplaces
The company hired an ergonomist to redesign the office for better employee comfort.
An ergonomist can help prevent workplace injuries by improving equipment and layout.
After consulting with an ergonomist, they replaced all the old chairs with more supportive ones.
The company consulted an ergonomist to redesign their office chairs for better back support.
An ergonomist studies how people use tools and equipment to reduce the risk of injury.
After the assessment, the ergonomist recommended several changes to improve workplace safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: designer - a person who plans the form or structure of something
  • synonym: human factors engineer - a professional who applies knowledge of human abilities to design systems and products
