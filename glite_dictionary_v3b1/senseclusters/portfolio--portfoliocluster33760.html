portfolio

noun
case for carrying papers or drawings
Pronunciation: / pɔrˈtfoʊlioʊ / (AmE), / pɔːtˈfəʊliəʊ / (BrE)
a large, flat, thin case for carrying loose papers, drawings, or maps, often made of leather

Examples

Leather portfolio case
After the meeting, she slipped the contracts carefully back into her portfolio.
He bought a leather portfolio to keep his important documents organized.
The artist carried her sketches in a black portfolio to the gallery.
