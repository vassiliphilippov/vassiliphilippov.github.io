sponge

noun
absorbent material or sea animal skeleton
Pronunciation: / spʌndʒ / (AmE), / spʌndʒ / (BrE)
A soft, porous material, either the absorbent internal skeleton of a sea animal or a synthetic product, used for cleaning or absorbing liquids.

Examples

A sponge for cleaning
I prefer using a natural sponge in the shower because it feels gentle on my skin.
She scrubbed the counter with a wet sponge until it was spotless.
The spilled juice was quickly soaked up with a sponge.
