baselessly

adverb
without evidence
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪslɪsli / (AmE), / ˈbeɪslɪsli / (BrE)
in a way that is not supported by facts, evidence, or justification

Examples

To baselessly accuse
The rumor spread baselessly through the office, causing unnecessary worry among employees.
She was baselessly accused of cheating on the exam, even though there was no proof.
Critics claimed baselessly that the project would fail, but it turned out to be a success.

Related Terms

  • synonym: groundlessly - without any good reason or basis
  • antonym: justifiably - in a way that can be justified or supported
  • synonym: unjustifiably - in a way that cannot be justified
