rubrail

noun
protective side strip
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbˌreɪl / (AmE), / ˈrʌbˌreɪl / (BrE)
a protective strip attached along the side of a boat, vehicle, or dock to prevent damage from scrapes or impacts
