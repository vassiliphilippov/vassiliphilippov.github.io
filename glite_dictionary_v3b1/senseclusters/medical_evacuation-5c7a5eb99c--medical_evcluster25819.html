medical evacuation

noun
transport of patients to medical care
9.1 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmed.ɪ.kəl ɪ.væk.juː.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈmed.ə.kəl ɪ.væk.juː.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈmed.ɪ.kəl iː.væk.juː.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈmed.ɪ.kəl ɪ.væk.juː.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈmed.ə.kəl ɪ.væk.juː.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the transportation of people who are injured or ill, usually by aircraft, to a place where they can receive medical treatment
