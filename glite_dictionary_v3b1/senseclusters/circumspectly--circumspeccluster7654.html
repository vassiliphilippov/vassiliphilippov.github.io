circumspectly

adverb
in a cautious or prudent manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrkəmˌspɛktli / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkəmˌspɛktli / (BrE)
In a careful and cautious way, taking all circumstances and possible consequences into account.

Examples

To circumspectly consider risks
He always invests circumspectly, considering all potential risks.
She moved circumspectly around the issue, never giving a direct answer.
The manager circumspectly reviewed all the documents before signing the contract.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cautiously - in a careful way to avoid danger or mistakes
  • antonym: recklessly - without thinking about the consequences or dangers
