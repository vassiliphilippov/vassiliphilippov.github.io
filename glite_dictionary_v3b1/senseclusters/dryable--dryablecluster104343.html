dryable

adjective
can be dried
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈdraɪəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be dried without being damaged or harmed

Examples

A dryable shirt
Check the label to see if the shirt is dryable before putting it in the dryer.
Some delicate fabrics are not dryable and should be air-dried instead.
The new towels are fully dryable and stay soft after each wash.

Related Terms

  • antonym: undryable - not able to be dried without damage
  • synonym: washable - able to be washed without damage
