immune

adjective
relating to immunity
4.5 3.3
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈmjuːn/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈmjuːn/ (BrE)
protected against a particular disease or infection; having immunity (medical/biological)

Examples

Immune system cells

After years of exposure, the scientists found themselves immune to the bacteria.
