guinea-hen flower

noun
checkered Eurasian ornamental flower
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪni hɛn ˈflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈɡɪni hɛn ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
A Eurasian flowering plant (Fritillaria meleagris) with nodding, bell-shaped flowers that are typically checkered with purple or maroon, often grown as an ornamental.

Examples

The guinea-hen flower plant
Gardeners prize the guinea-hen flower for both its delicate appearance and unusual coloration.
She planted several guinea-hen flowers along the path to create a pattern of purple and white.
The guinea-hen flower added a striking touch to the spring garden with its checkered blossoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fritillary - any plant of the genus Fritillaria, to which the guinea-hen flower belongs
  • synonym: snake's head - another common name for Fritillaria meleagris
