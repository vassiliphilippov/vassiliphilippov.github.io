false mildew

noun
parasitic fungus affecting plants
8.1 7.0
Pronunciation: /fɔːls ˈmɪl.duː/ /; / /fɑːls ˈmɪl.djuː/ (AmE), /fɒls ˈmɪl.djuː/ /; / /fɔːls ˈmɪl.djuː/ (BrE)

Examples
