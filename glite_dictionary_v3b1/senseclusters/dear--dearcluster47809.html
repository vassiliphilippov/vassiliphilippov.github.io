dear

adjective
deeply sincere or heartfelt
Pronunciation: / dɪr / (AmE), / dɪə / (BrE)
loved, cherished, or valued very much; held in close affection

Examples

A dear friend
He expressed his dearest hopes for a peaceful resolution.
Her dearest wish was to see her family together again.
We offer our dearest condolences on your loss.
He spent the holidays with his dear friends and family.
Many of my dear colleagues attended my retirement party.
She has always been very dear to me, especially during difficult times.
His dear friend supported him through the toughest times.
She held her dear grandmother's hand tightly.
The memories of their dear dog still bring them comfort.
