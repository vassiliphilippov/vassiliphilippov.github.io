Synonyms

  • loved
    1.0 0.00/m
    loved is being greatly cared for and wanted by someone, while dear means being held in close, warm affection.
  • beloved
    2.1 18/m
    beloved is an often formal or poetic word for someone dearly loved, while dear is more common in everyday speech.
  • cherished
    3.4 0.02/m
    cherished is loved and carefully protected over time, while dear focuses on close warmth and personal affection.
  • adored
    2.6 0.01/m
    adored shows strong, almost worshipping love and admiration, while dear describes gentle, everyday close affection.
  • darling
    1.8 1.01/m
    darling is often used as a direct, warm name for someone, while dear is more general and less intimate as address.
  • treasured
    4.1 0.03/m
    treasured means valued for memory or worth like a keepsake, while dear emphasizes close personal affection.
