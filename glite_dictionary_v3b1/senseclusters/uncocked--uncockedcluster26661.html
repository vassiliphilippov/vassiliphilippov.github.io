uncocked

adjective
not tilted
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɑkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɒkt / (BrE)
not tilted or set at an angle; positioned straight or level, especially referring to hats or objects.

Examples

Uncocked hat
He wore his cap uncocked, sitting perfectly straight on his head.
The picture frame remained uncocked after she carefully adjusted it on the wall.
Unlike his friends, he preferred his hat uncocked for a neater appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: straight - not bent or angled; level
  • antonym: tilted - set at an angle; not level
