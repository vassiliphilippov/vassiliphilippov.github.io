befouled

adjective
made dirty or contaminated
5.5
Pronunciation: / bɪˈfaʊld / (AmE), / bɪˈfaʊld / (BrE)
made dirty or contaminated; polluted or soiled.

Examples

Befouled reputation
His reputation was befouled by a series of false rumors and accusations.
The air in the old factory is befouled with chemical fumes and dust.
The once-clear lake was befouled by industrial waste and trash.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - free from dirt or impurities
  • synonym: dirty - covered or marked with an unclean substance; not clean
