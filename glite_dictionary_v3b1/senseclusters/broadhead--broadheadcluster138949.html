broadhead

noun
hunting arrowhead
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔːdhɛd / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdhɛd / (BrE)
a type of arrowhead with a wide, flat blade designed for hunting to cause large wounds

Examples

A broadhead arrow
The archer carefully screwed a broadhead onto his arrow before the hunt.
Many hunters prefer using a broadhead for its effectiveness in bringing down large game.
Always check that your broadhead is securely attached to avoid accidents during archery practice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrowhead - the pointed tip of an arrow
  • antonym: field point - a type of arrowhead with a pointed tip used for target practice
