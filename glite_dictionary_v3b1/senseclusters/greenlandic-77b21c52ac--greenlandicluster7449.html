Greenlandic

noun
language of Greenland
Pronunciation: / ˌɡriːnˈlændɪk / (AmE), / ˌɡriːnˈlændɪk / (BrE)
the Inuit language spoken in Greenland, also known as Kalaallisut

Examples

Greenlandic language
She is learning Greenlandic to communicate with her relatives in Nuuk.
The documentary was narrated in both Danish and Greenlandic for local audiences.
Many children in Greenland grow up speaking Greenlandic as their first language.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Inuit - the group of languages spoken by the Inuit people
  • synonym: Kalaallisut - the standard variety of the Greenlandic language
