baking-powder biscuit

noun
type of biscuit leavened with baking powder
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪkɪŋ ˌpaʊdɚ ˈbɪskɪt / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkɪŋ ˌpaʊdə ˈbɪskɪt / (BrE)
a light, quick bread leavened with baking powder rather than yeast, commonly served as a side dish or for breakfast

Examples

Freshly baked baking-powder biscuits are a staple side at Southern dinners.
She served warm baking-powder biscuits with butter for breakfast.
The recipe calls for buttermilk to make fluffy baking-powder biscuits.
