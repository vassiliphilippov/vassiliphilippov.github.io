cursory

adjective
done quickly, not thorough
7.0 2.11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…úÀêr.s…ô.ri / (AmE), / Ààk…úÀê.s…ôri / (BrE)
Done quickly and without giving enough attention to detail; not thorough.

Examples

A cursory inspection
After a cursory inspection, she approved the shipment.
He only gave the report a cursory glance before the meeting.
The editor's cursory review missed several important errors.
