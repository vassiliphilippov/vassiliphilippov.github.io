azalea

noun
flowering shrub with showy blooms
Pronunciation: / əˈzeɪliə / (AmE), / əˈzeɪliə / (BrE)
a deciduous or evergreen shrub with showy flowers, belonging to the genus Rhododendron, commonly grown for ornamental purposes

Examples

Pink azaleas bushes
A row of pink azaleas lined the walkway, creating a stunning floral display.
Every spring, her grandmother would plant a new azalea by the front porch.
The garden was filled with colorful azalea bushes in full bloom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rhododendron - a genus of shrubs, including azaleas, with large, often showy flowers
