aromaticity

noun
the quality of having a fragrance
Pronunciation: / ˌærəˌmæt̬ɪˈsɪti / (AmE), / ˌærəʊməˈtɪsɪti / (BrE)
the property or quality of having an aroma or fragrant smell.

Examples

Aromaticity of flowers
Researchers studied the aromaticity of various essential oils for their calming effects.
Some wines are known for the distinct aromaticity they develop as they age.
The chef valued the aromaticity of fresh herbs in his dishes.
