premade

noun
gaming team group
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌmeɪd / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌmeɪd / (BrE)
an organized group of players who join a multiplayer game together as a team, rather than individually

Examples

A premade team
We joined the match as a premade, which helped our team coordinate strategies quickly.
The tournament rules do not allow any premade groups to compete for fairness.
Playing with a premade can make online games more enjoyable and less stressful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: party - a group of players who play together in a game
  • antonym: solo queue - playing a multiplayer game alone, not as part of a group
