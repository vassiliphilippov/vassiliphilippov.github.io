non-romantic

adjective
not about romance
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn roʊˈmæntɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn rəʊˈmæntɪk / (BrE)
not involving, expressing, or relating to love or romance

Examples

Non-romantic friendship
They share a deep but non-romantic bond that has lasted for years.
The movie focuses on non-romantic relationships between coworkers.
He clarified that their partnership was strictly non-romantic and based on mutual respect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: platonic - describing a close relationship without romantic or sexual attraction
  • antonym: romantic - involving or relating to love or romance
