structureless

adjective
without internal order
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstrʌktʃərləs / (AmE), / ˈstrʌktʃələs / (BrE)
lacking a regular or organized internal structure; amorphous rather than crystalline.

Examples

Structureless material
The scientist described the new material as structureless because it lacked any visible pattern under the microscope.
Unlike crystals, glass is considered structureless due to its random atomic arrangement.
The artist used a structureless form to create a sense of chaos in the sculpture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amorphous - having no definite shape or structure
  • antonym: crystalline - having a regular, repeating internal structure
