messily

adverb
in a messy way
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛsɪli / (AmE), / ˈmɛsɪli / (BrE)
In a messy or untidy manner.

Examples

To messily wrap gifts
He wrapped the gift messily, with wrinkled paper and crooked tape.
She ate her ice cream messily, leaving chocolate all over her face.
The children painted messily, splattering colors across the table and floor.
