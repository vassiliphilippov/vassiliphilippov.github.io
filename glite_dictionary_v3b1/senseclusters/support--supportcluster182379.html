Synonyms

  • assistance
    1.0 65/m
    assistance provides practical help or resources for tasks, while support covers help, approval, or emotional backing for people or causes.
  • aid
    1.0 54/m
    aid gives practical or emergency help to someone in need, while support also includes long-term approval, encouragement, or financial help.
  • helping hand
    1.4 1.38/m
    a helping hand is a friendly, often quick action to assist someone, while support can be steady help or approval over time.
  • backing
    2.6 0.06/m
    backing is public or financial approval given to a person or plan, while support can be any kind of help or encouragement.
  • encouragement
    1.7 7.49/m
    encouragement is the words or actions that boost someone's confidence, while support also includes practical help and formal approval.
  • favor
    1.0 48/m
    a favor is a helpful act or preference offered to someone, while support is broader help, approval, or ongoing encouragement.
