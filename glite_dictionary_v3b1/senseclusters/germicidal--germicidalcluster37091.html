germicidal

adjective
killing or inhibiting germs/microorganisms
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɝːmɪˈsaɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒɜːmɪˈsaɪdəl / (BrE)
Capable of killing germs or preventing the growth of microorganisms.

Examples

A germicidal soap
Regular use of germicidal soap reduces the risk of spreading bacteria at home.
The hospital uses germicidal lamps in operating rooms to maintain a sterile environment.
This cleaning spray has germicidal properties that help protect against infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antibacterial - Active against bacteria; destroys bacteria
  • synonym: antimicrobial - Kills or inhibits the growth of microorganisms
