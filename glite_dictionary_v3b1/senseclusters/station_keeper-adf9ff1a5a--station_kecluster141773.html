station keeper

noun
police sergeant on duty in a station
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪʃən ˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈsteɪʃən ˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a police sergeant in charge of a police station during a particular shift or on duty

Examples

Station keeper on duty
As station keeper, she ensured all operations at the precinct ran smoothly throughout the night.
The station keeper recorded the details of every person brought into the station overnight.
When the inspector arrived, the station keeper briefed him on all incidents reported during the shift.
