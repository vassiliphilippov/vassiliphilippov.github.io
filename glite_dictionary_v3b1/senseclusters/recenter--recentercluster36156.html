recenter

verb
to regain calm or focus
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈsɛntər / (AmE), / ˌriːˈsɛntə / (BrE)
to restore one's calm, balance, or mental focus after being disturbed or distracted

Examples

To recenter yourself
She took a few deep breaths to recenter herself after the stressful phone call.
Meditation helps him recenter when he feels overwhelmed by work.
After the argument, he needed a moment alone to recenter before returning to the group.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compose - to calm oneself and regain control
  • antonym: unsettle - to disturb or make someone lose their calm
