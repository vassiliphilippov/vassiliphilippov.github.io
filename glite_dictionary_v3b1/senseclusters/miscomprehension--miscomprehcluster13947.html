miscomprehension

noun
failure to understand
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪskɑːmprɪˈhɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪskɒmprɪˈhɛnʃən / (BrE)
a failure to understand something correctly

Examples

A miscomprehension of rules
A simple miscomprehension of the rules caused the team to lose points.
Her miscomprehension of the question led to an incorrect answer on the test.
The project failed due to a miscomprehension between the manager and the staff.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comprehension - the ability to understand something
  • synonym: misunderstanding - a situation in which something is not understood correctly
