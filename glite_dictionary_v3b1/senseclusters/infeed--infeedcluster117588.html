infeed

noun
machine input section
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌfiːd / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌfiːd / (BrE)
the part of a machine or process where material is supplied or fed into the system

Examples

Infeed section
The technician adjusted the infeed to ensure the wood entered the saw smoothly.
If the infeed is blocked, the entire production line may stop.
Regular cleaning of the infeed helps prevent jams and maintains efficiency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: outfeed - the part of a machine where material exits after processing
