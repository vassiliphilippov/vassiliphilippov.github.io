Mennonitism

noun
Mennonite religious beliefs and practices
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛn.əˌnaɪ.tɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.əˌnaɪ.tɪz.əm / (BrE)
The doctrines, beliefs, and practices of the Mennonites, including scriptural authority, adult baptism, plain dress, foot washing, and restricting marriage to members of the group.
