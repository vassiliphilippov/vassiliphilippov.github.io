Sedum telephium

noun
a perennial plant with purplish-white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːdəm tɪˈlɛfiəm / (AmE), / ˈsiːdəm tɛˈliːfiəm / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to northern temperate regions, with toothed leaves and heads of small purplish-white flowers; commonly known as orpine.
