content word

noun
word that carries core meaning (linguistics)
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.tɛnt ˌwɝd / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tɛnt ˌwɜːd / (BrE)
A word, such as a noun, verb, adjective, or adverb, that carries significant meaning and can stand alone in conveying information, as opposed to functional words like prepositions or articles.

Examples

A content word meaning
In the sentence 'The cat sleeps', each content word adds specific information to the meaning.
Language learners often focus on learning content words to expand their vocabulary.
Unlike function words, a content word usually has a dictionary definition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: function word - a word with little lexical meaning but serving a grammatical purpose
