unprotectable

adjective
cannot be protected
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprəˈtɛktəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnprəˈtɛktəbl̩ / (BrE)
not able to be kept safe from harm, damage, or attack; impossible to defend or protect

Examples

Unprotectable old building
The ancient manuscript was considered unprotectable due to its fragile condition and exposure to the elements.
Some areas of the coastline are simply unprotectable against the force of major storms.
Without proper resources, the village remained unprotectable from potential threats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defenseless - without protection or defense; unable to resist attack
  • antonym: protectable - able to be kept safe from harm or attack
  • synonym: vulnerable - easily harmed or attacked; open to danger
