Chesapeake Bay

proper noun
large estuary between Virginia and Maryland
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃɛsəpiːk ˈbeɪ / (AmE), / ˌtʃɛsəpiːk ˈbeɪ / (BrE)
a large estuary of the North Atlantic Ocean between Virginia and Maryland in the eastern United States, fed mainly by the Susquehanna River

Examples
