taille

noun
vine pruning
Pronunciation: / taɪ / (AmE), / taɪ / (BrE)
the pruning or cutting back of vines or shrubs, especially in horticulture or viticulture

Examples

Vineyard taille
The vineyard manager scheduled the annual taille to ensure healthy grape production.
Proper taille helps control the growth and shape of the vines each season.
After the taille, the workers collected the cut branches from the ground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pruning - the act of trimming plants by cutting away dead or overgrown branches
  • synonym: trimming - the act of cutting away unwanted parts of a plant
