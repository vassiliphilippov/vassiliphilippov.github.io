tantalization

noun
teasing or tormenting act
Pronunciation: / ˌtæn.təl.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌtæn.tə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of teasing, tormenting, or provoking someone by keeping something desirable out of reach or by persistent annoyance

Examples

Constant tantalization
Her constant tantalization of her brother with his favorite toy drove him crazy.
His tantalization of his friends went too far and hurt their feelings.
The aroma of fresh bread was a cruel tantalization for those waiting in line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: teasing - the act of making fun of or provoking in a playful or unkind way
  • degree higher: torment - severe physical or mental suffering
