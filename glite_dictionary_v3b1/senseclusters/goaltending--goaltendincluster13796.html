goaltending

noun
illegal basketball block
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈgoʊl.ten.dɪŋ/ /; / /ˈgoʊl.tən.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈgəʊl.ten.dɪŋ/ /; / /ˈgəʊl.tən.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
an illegal action in basketball where a player touches the ball on its downward path toward the basket or after it has touched the backboard

Examples

Basketball goaltending violation
The team lost two points because of a goaltending violation in the final quarter.
He jumped too late and was called for goaltending after blocking the shot above the rim.
Fans argued with the referee about whether the play was really goaltending or a clean block.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basket interference - an illegal play involving touching the ball or basket while the ball is on or above the rim
