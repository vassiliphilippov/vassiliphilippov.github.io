try

verb
melt fat to purify (render)
Pronunciation: / traɪ / (AmE), / traɪ / (BrE)
(of fat or lard) to melt in order to separate out impurities; to render.

Examples

To try animal fat
Early settlers would try animal fat over a fire to make soap and candles.
It's important to try the fat slowly so it doesn't burn.
The recipe calls for you to try lard before adding it to the pastry dough.
