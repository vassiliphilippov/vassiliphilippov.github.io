assailable

adjective
capable of being attacked or criticized
9.2
Pronunciation: / əˈseɪ.lə.bəl / (AmE), / əˈseɪ.lə.bəl / (BrE)
capable of being attacked, criticized, or harmed; not protected or defended against attack.

Examples

An assailable castle
The castle was easily assailable because its walls were crumbling.
Their argument was weak and therefore highly assailable.
Without proper security, the system remains assailable to hackers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unassailable - impossible to attack, question, or defeat
