forgetfulness

noun
tendency to forget
Pronunciation: / fərˈɡɛtfəlnəs / (AmE), / fəˈɡɛtfəlnəs /; / fəˈɡɛtf(ʊ)lnɪs / (BrE)
the tendency or state of often forgetting things
