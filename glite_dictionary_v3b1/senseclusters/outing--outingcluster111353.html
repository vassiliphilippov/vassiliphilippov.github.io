outing

noun
social or recreational day trip
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtɪŋ /; / ˈaʊ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtɪŋ /; / ˈaʊ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
a short trip or social event taken for pleasure or recreation, usually as a group

Examples

A outing to park
Our company is organizing an outing to the national park this weekend.
The children eagerly packed their lunches for the school outing.
We enjoyed a wonderful family outing by the lake on Sunday.
The school's annual outing to the zoo is always popular with students.
They planned a group outing to the countryside for a picnic.
We organized a family outing to the beach last weekend.

  • synonym: picnic - an occasion when a packed meal is eaten outdoors, especially during a trip
