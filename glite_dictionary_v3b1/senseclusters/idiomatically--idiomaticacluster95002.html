idiomatically

adverb
in an idiomatic manner
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪdiəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɪdɪəˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that uses or relates to expressions that are natural to a native speaker or typical of a language.

Examples

Speak idiomatically
He spoke idiomatically, choosing phrases that only native speakers would use.
The instructions were not written idiomatically, so they sounded awkward to English speakers.
Translating poetry idiomatically can be very challenging.
