nuclear terrorism

noun
terrorism involving nuclear materials
3.0 6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnuː.kli.ɚ ˈter.əˌrɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.klɪ.ə ˈter.ər.ɪzm / (BrE)
