slimming

noun
weight loss process
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈslɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of losing body weight, especially through diet, exercise, or treatments to become thinner

Examples

Slimming program
She joined a slimming program to improve her health before the summer holidays.
Many people try different slimming methods, such as special diets or exercise routines.
Advertisements for slimming products often promise quick results, but not all are effective.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dieting - the practice of eating food in a regulated way to lose weight
  • antonym: weight gain - an increase in body weight
  • synonym: weight loss - the reduction of total body weight
