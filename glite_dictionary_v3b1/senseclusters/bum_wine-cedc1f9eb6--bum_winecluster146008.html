bum wine

noun
low-quality cheap alcoholic wine
7.6
Pronunciation: / bʌm waɪn / (AmE), / bʌm waɪn / (BrE)
A cheap or low-quality alcoholic wine, typically associated with being consumed by people with limited means.

Examples

Cheap bum wine
College students sometimes buy bum wine because it fits their budget.
He regretted drinking so much bum wine the next morning.
The shopkeeper kept the bum wine on the lowest shelf in the corner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fortified wine - a wine to which a distilled spirit has been added, often low-cost
  • degree lower: table wine - an ordinary wine with lower alcohol content and price
