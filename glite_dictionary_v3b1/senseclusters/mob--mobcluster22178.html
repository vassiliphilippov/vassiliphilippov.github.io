mob

noun
disorderly crowd
3.4 12/m
Pronunciation: / mÉ‘b / (AmE), / mÉ’b / (BrE)
a large crowd of people, especially one that is disorderly or unruly

Examples

An angry mob
A mob quickly gathered outside the store after rumors of a celebrity visit spread.
The angry mob surged towards the government building, shouting and waving banners.
The police struggled to control the mob as tempers flared during the protest.
