tape measure

noun
flexible measuring instrument
1.4 1.7
Pronunciation: / teɪp ˈmɛʒər / (AmE), / teɪp ˈmɛʒə / (BrE)
A flexible measuring instrument, usually a strip of cloth, plastic, or metal marked with units, used for measuring length or distance.

Examples

A tape measure tool
Before buying new curtains, she used a tape measure to check the window size.
He always keeps a tape measure in his tool belt for quick measurements.
The tailor wrapped the tape measure around the customer's waist.
