hurr durr

noun
mocking term for nonsensical chatter
Pronunciation: / hɚ dɚ / (AmE), / hɜː dɜː / (BrE)
used to represent meaningless, foolish, or nonsensical speech, often imitating or mocking someone perceived as saying something stupid

Examples

Silly hurr durr talk
Her comment was nothing but hurr durr, completely unrelated to the topic.
Instead of addressing the real issues, the debate turned into a series of hurr durr exchanges.
Whenever someone disagreed with him online, he dismissed their argument as just hurr durr.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - spoken or written words that have no meaning or make no sense
