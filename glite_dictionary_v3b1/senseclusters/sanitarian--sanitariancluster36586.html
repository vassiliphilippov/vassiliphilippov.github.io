sanitarian

noun
public health worker
7.4
Pronunciation: /sæn.ɪ.ˈter.iː.ən/ (AmE), /sæn.ɪ.ˈteə.ri.ən/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to promote and ensure public health and cleanliness, especially by managing sanitation standards and systems

Examples

The sanitarian inspects health

After the flood, the sanitarian checked the water supply for contamination.
