sanitarian

noun
public health worker
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsænɪˈtɛriən / (AmE), / ˌsænɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to promote and ensure public health and cleanliness, especially by managing sanitation standards and systems

Examples

The sanitarian inspects health
The sanitarian visited the school to check the cleanliness of the cafeteria.
A sanitarian is responsible for ensuring that public swimming pools meet health standards.
After the inspection, the sanitarian provided recommendations to improve hygiene in the restaurant.
The city hired a sanitarian to plan the new wastewater treatment facility.
A sanitarian inspects restaurants to make sure they follow health regulations.
After the flood, the sanitarian checked the water supply for contamination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epidemiologist - a specialist in the study of disease spread and control
  • synonym: health inspector - a person who checks places for health and safety compliance
  • synonym: sanitation engineer - a person who designs and manages systems for public health and sanitation
