Synonyms

  • employ
    2.2 35/m
    employ is slightly more formal and often means making practical use of something, while use is the general everyday verb.
  • apply
    2.8 54/m
    apply is often used for methods, rules, or devices put into action, while use is broader and more general.
  • utilize
    4.9 31/m
    utilize sounds formal and suggests making effective or practical use of available things, while use is common everyday language.
