heroization

noun
process of making someone a hero

the process of making someone into a hero, or treating them as a hero, often in a cultural or historical context

Examples

The heroization of leaders
His courageous actions during the crisis led to the heroization of his name in his hometown.
Recent debates question whether the heroization of tech founders is warranted by their actual contributions.
The heroization of military leaders can significantly influence national identity.

Synonyms

  • glorification
    6.8 0.33/m
    glorification portrays someone as ideal or admirable, while heroization means making someone into a celebrated public hero in culture or history.
  • valorization
    9.1 0.13/m
    valorization praises or gives honor to someone, while heroization specifically turns a person into a public or cultural hero.
  • mythologization
    9.8 0.01/m
    mythologization turns a person into a legend or symbolic figure, while heroization focuses on treating them as a hero in culture.
  • mythification
    9.1 0.03/m
    mythification makes someone seem legendary through stories and exaggeration, while heroization stresses making them a praised public hero.
  • mythmaking
    7.0 0.20/m
    mythmaking creates or promotes stories about a person, while heroization aims more narrowly to make them a cultural or historical hero.
