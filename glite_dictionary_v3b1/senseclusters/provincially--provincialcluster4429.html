provincially

adverb
by or through a province
7.2 5.4
Pronunciation: /prə.ˈvɪn.ʃə.liː/ (AmE), /prə.ˈvɪn.ʃə.liː/ (BrE)
by or through a province; in a way that relates to a province rather than the national or local level.

Examples

Regulated provincially
Education standards may differ when they are provincially regulated instead of nationally standardized.
Several programs are provincially administered rather than governed at the federal level.
The health system is provincially funded and managed in each region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: locally - in a way relating to a particular area or community
  • antonym: nationally - in a way relating to the nation as a whole
