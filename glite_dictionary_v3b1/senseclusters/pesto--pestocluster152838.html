pesto

noun
Italian sauce of basil, garlic, pine nuts, and cheese
3.3 1.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpe.stoʊ/ (AmE), /ˈpe.stəʊ/ (BrE)
a sauce originally from Italy, made with crushed basil, garlic, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil, often served with pasta

Examples

Homemade pesto sauce
Homemade pesto is easy to prepare and tastes much better than store-bought.
She tossed the pasta in a fresh pesto before serving it to her guests.
The sandwich was topped with sun-dried tomatoes and a layer of pesto.
