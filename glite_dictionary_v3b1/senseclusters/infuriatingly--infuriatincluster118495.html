infuriatingly

adverb
in an angering way
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfjʊriˌeɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈfjʊəriˌeɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes great anger or extreme frustration

Examples

Infuriatingly slow response
He infuriatingly ignored every suggestion I made during the meeting.
The website infuriatingly crashed just as I was about to submit my application.
She infuriatingly kept interrupting me while I was trying to explain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annoyingly - in a way that causes irritation or annoyance
  • antonym: calmly - in a way that is peaceful and not angry
