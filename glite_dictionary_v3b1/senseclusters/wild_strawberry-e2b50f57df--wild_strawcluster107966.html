wild strawberry

noun
small wild European strawberry plant or fruit
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈstrɔːˌbɛri / (AmE), / waɪld ˈstrɒˌbəri / (BrE)
A small, sweet red fruit of a European plant (Fragaria vesca), typically found growing wild, and the plant itself.

Examples

Fresh wild strawberry
She tossed a handful of fresh wild strawberry into the fruit salad.
The wild strawberry has a much more intense flavor than the cultivated variety.
We found a patch of wild strawberry plants at the edge of the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strawberry - a sweet soft red fruit with a seed-studded surface
