scrollwork

noun
decorative spirals
Pronunciation: / ˈskroʊlˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈskrəʊlˌwɜːk / (BrE)
a decorative design featuring spirals or curves, often carved, molded, or painted on objects or surfaces

Examples

Intricate scrollwork design
The antique desk was decorated with intricate scrollwork along its edges.
You can see beautiful scrollwork on the gates of the old mansion.
The artist added gold scrollwork to the frame to make it stand out.
The antique mirror was framed with intricate scrollwork that caught the light beautifully.
She admired the elegant scrollwork on the old iron gate as she entered the garden.
The craftsman spent hours perfecting the wooden scrollwork for the grand staircase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filigree - delicate ornamental work made from fine wire
  • synonym: ornamentation - the act or process of decorating or adorning
