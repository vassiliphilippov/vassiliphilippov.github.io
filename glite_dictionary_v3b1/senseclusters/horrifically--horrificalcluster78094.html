horrifically

adverb
shockingly or terribly
6.0
Pronunciation: / həˈrɪfɪkli / (AmE), / hɒˈrɪfɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely shocking, frightening, or horrible

Examples

Horrifically violent event
The accident was horrifically violent, leaving everyone in shock.
She was horrifically injured in the explosion but managed to survive.
The story was horrifically sad and moved many readers to tears.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horribly - in a way that causes horror or disgust
  • synonym: terribly - in a very bad or unpleasant way
  • antonym: wonderfully - in a way that causes delight or admiration
