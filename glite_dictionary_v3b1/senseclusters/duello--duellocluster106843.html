duello

noun
formal duel
9.9
Pronunciation: / duˈɛloʊ / (AmE), / djuˈɛləʊ / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a formal fight or duel between two people, usually to settle a dispute

Examples

A formal duello
The two gentlemen agreed to a duello at dawn to resolve their honor dispute.
In the novel, a tragic duello changes the fate of both families forever.
Historical records describe a famous duello fought with swords in the old city square.

Related Terms

  • synonym: battle - a fight between opposing groups or individuals
  • synonym: duel - a formal fight between two people, often with weapons
