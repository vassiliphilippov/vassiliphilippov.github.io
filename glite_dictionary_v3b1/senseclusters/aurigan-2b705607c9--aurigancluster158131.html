Aurigan

noun
copper alloy for bits
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrɪɡən / (AmE), / ˈɔːrɪɡən / (BrE)
a type of copper alloy used in making horse bits and other equestrian equipment, known for its durability and comfort for horses

Examples

Aurigan horse bits
Many riders prefer bits made from Aurigan because they are gentle on the horse's mouth.
The tack shop recommended an Aurigan bit for horses with sensitive mouths.
She noticed her horse responded better after switching to an Aurigan snaffle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: German silver - a nickel-copper-zinc alloy used for horse bits and other items
  • synonym: stainless steel - a corrosion-resistant steel alloy used for horse bits
