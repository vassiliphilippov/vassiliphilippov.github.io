dispirited

adjective
lacking enthusiasm or hope
3.5 4.4
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ˈpɪr.ɪ.tɪd/ /; / /dɪs.ˈpɪr.ə.tɪd/ (AmE), /dɪs.ˈpɪ.rɪ.tɪd/ /; / /dɪs.ˈpɪ.rɪ.təd/ (BrE)
feeling or showing a lack of enthusiasm, hope, or spirit; downhearted or discouraged

Examples

Look dispirited after loss
After the team's third consecutive loss, the players looked dispirited as they left the field.

She tried to cheer her dispirited friend with a kind message.
The team grew dispirited after losing three games in a row.
