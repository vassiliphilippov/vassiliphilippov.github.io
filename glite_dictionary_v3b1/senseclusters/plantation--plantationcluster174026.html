plantation

noun
area of planted trees without undergrowth
1.5 3.1
Pronunciation: /plæn.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /plænt.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /plæn.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /plænt.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
A garden or area where trees have been planted in a deliberate arrangement, typically forming a small wood without undergrowth.

Examples

Tree plantation
A small plantation was established near the school to provide shade and beauty.
On weekends, they walked through the plantation admiring the neat rows of birches.
The old estate featured a plantation of oak trees with grass between the trunks.
