orbitally

adverb
involving an orbit
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrbɪtəli / (AmE), / ˈɔːbɪtəli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or involving an orbit, especially the path of an object around another in space

Examples

Orbitally stable path
The spacecraft was orbitally aligned to ensure a stable path around the Earth.
Engineers calculated the trajectory orbitally to avoid collisions with other satellites.
The telescope is orbitally positioned to observe distant galaxies without atmospheric interference.
