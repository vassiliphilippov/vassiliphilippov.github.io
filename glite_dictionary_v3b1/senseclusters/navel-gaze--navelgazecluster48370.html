navel-gaze

verb
excessively introspect or self-reflect
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪvəl ˌɡeɪz / (AmE), / ˈneɪvəl ˌɡeɪz / (BrE)
to spend too much time thinking about oneself or one's own problems without taking action or considering others

Examples

To navel-gaze deeply
If you always navel-gaze, you'll miss opportunities for growth and change.
Sometimes it's easier to navel-gaze than to confront real problems head-on.
The team was accused of navel-gazing instead of delivering results for their clients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: introspect - to examine one's own thoughts or feelings
  • synonym: self-reflect - to think about one's own character or actions
