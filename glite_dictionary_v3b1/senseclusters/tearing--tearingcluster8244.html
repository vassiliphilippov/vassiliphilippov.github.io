tearing

noun
the act of shedding tears
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of shedding tears, typically as an expression of emotion

Examples

Eye tearing
Allergies often cause itching and unexpected tearing of the eyes.
Constant tearing made her eyes red and puffy by the end of the movie.
There was a sudden tearing when he received the sad news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crying - the act of making tears come from your eyes, usually because of emotion or pain
  • synonym: weeping - shedding tears, usually silently, as an expression of sorrow
