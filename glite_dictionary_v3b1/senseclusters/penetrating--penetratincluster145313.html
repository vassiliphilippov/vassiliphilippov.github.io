penetrating

adjective
showing keen or incisive insight
4.9 5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnəˌtreɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɛnɪˌtreɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Having or showing an ability to understand things quickly and clearly; keen or incisive.

Examples

A penetrating question
He has a penetrating mind that quickly grasps complex issues.
Her penetrating questions revealed the flaws in the argument.
The critic's penetrating analysis gave me a new perspective on the film.

  • antonym: dull - lacking sharpness or insight
