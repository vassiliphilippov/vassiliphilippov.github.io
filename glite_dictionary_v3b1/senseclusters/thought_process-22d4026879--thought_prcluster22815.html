thought process

noun
series of mental steps; reasoning
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈθɔt ˌproʊ.ses / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt ˌprəʊ.ses / (BrE)

Examples

Clear thought process

Meditation can help slow down your thought process and reduce stress.
