as a matter of course

x
routinely or automatically; as usual practice
Pronunciation: / æz ə ˈmætər əv kɔrs / (AmE), / əz ə ˈmætə əv kɔːs / (BrE)
used to describe something that is done routinely, automatically, or as a regular practice without special thought or question.

Examples

As a matter of course routine
Applicants are interviewed as a matter of course before being offered a job.
He apologizes as a matter of course, even when he's not at fault.
The company backs up its computers as a matter of course to prevent data loss.
