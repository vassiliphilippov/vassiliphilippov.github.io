undevelopable

adjective
cannot be processed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈvɛləpəbl / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈvɛləpəbl / (BrE)
not capable of being developed into visible images by photographic or chemical processing

Examples

Undevelopable film
The damaged film was completely undevelopable after being exposed to light.
If the chemicals are expired, your negatives might become undevelopable.
She was disappointed to learn that her old photographs were undevelopable due to improper storage.
