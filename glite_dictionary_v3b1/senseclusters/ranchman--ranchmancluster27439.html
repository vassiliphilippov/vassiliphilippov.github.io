ranchman

noun
male ranch worker
Pronunciation: / ˈræntʃˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈræntʃˌmæn / (BrE)

Examples

An experienced ranchman
The experienced ranchman taught his son how to care for the horses.
After years as a ranchman, he knew every part of the land by heart.
The ranchman repaired the fence before the cattle wandered too far.
