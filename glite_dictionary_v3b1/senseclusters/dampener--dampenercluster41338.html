dampener

noun
thing that lessens excitement
Pronunciation: / ˈdæmpənər / (AmE), / ˈdæmpənə / (BrE)
something that reduces excitement, enthusiasm, or the intensity of a situation or feeling

Examples

A dampener on fun
The bad news acted as a dampener on the team's celebration after their big win.
His negative attitude was a real dampener during the otherwise cheerful meeting.
The cancellation of the concert put a dampener on everyone's weekend plans.

Related Terms

  • antonym: booster - something that increases excitement or enthusiasm
  • synonym: downer - something that makes people feel less happy or excited
  • synonym: spoiler - something that ruins enjoyment or excitement
