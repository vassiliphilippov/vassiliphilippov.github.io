glassworking

noun
making glass objects
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsˌwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsˌwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the craft or process of shaping and forming objects from glass

Examples

Traditional glassworking
She became interested in glassworking after visiting a local art studio.
The museum features a demonstration of traditional glassworking techniques.
Learning glassworking requires patience and attention to detail.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ceramics - the art of making objects from clay and similar materials
  • synonym: glassblowing - the art of shaping glass by blowing air into it
