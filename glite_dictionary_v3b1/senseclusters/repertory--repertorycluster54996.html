A place or container where a stock of things is kept; a storehouse or repository.

Examples

Repertory of artifacts
A museum can act as a repertory for artifacts from lost civilizations.
Researchers accessed the repertory of geological samples kept in the university vaults.
The ancient library served as a repertory of valuable manuscripts and scrolls.

Synonyms
