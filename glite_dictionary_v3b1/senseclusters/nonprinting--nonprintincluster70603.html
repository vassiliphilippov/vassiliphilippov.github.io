nonprinting

adjective
not shown in print
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈprɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈprɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
not producing printed output or not appearing in printed form, especially in documents or on screens

Examples

Nonprinting characters
The software displays nonprinting characters to help users edit documents more precisely.
Page numbers are set as nonprinting elements in this template and will not appear on the final copy.
You can choose to show or hide nonprinting marks when working in your word processor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: printing - producing text or images on paper
