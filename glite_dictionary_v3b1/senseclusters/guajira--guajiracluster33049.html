guajira

noun
Cuban music style
Pronunciation: / ɡwaˈhiɾə / (AmE), / ɡwaˈhiːrə / (BrE)
a style of Cuban music and dance with rural origins, often featuring guitar and poetic lyrics

Examples

Traditional guajira music
The musicians performed a beautiful guajira that reminded the audience of the Cuban countryside.
She learned to dance the guajira while visiting Havana with her family.
A classic guajira played softly in the background as they enjoyed their meal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: son - a traditional Cuban music and dance style
