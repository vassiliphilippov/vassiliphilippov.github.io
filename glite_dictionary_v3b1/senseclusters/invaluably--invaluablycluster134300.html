invaluably

adverb
extremely usefully
5.1
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvæljuəbli / (AmE), / ɪnˈvæljuəbli / (BrE)

Examples

Invaluably helpful advice
Her guidance was invaluably important to the success of the team.
The volunteers contributed invaluably to the community project.
He invaluably assisted us by sharing his expertise during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: worthlessly - in a way that lacks value or usefulness
