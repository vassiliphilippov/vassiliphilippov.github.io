victualer

noun
innkeeper; supplier of food and drink
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪtələr /; / ˈvɪtʃuələr / (AmE), / ˈvɪtələ /; / ˈvɪtjʊələ / (BrE)
An innkeeper; a person who keeps an inn or supplies food and drink, especially in British usage.

Examples
