passage

noun
short musical section
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpæsɪdʒ / (BrE)
a section or part of a written text or a musical composition

Examples

A passage from a book
Her favorite passage in the symphony always gives her chills.
The pianist skillfully performed the difficult passage near the end of the concerto.
This passage requires careful attention to dynamics and phrasing.
She highlighted an inspiring passage from the novel for her essay.
The teacher asked us to read the passage aloud in class.
This passage explains the main idea of the article.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excerpt - a short extract from a text
  • synonym: phrase - a short musical segment typically forming a distinct unit
  • synonym: section - a distinct part of a written work
  • antonym: whole - the complete text, not a section of it
