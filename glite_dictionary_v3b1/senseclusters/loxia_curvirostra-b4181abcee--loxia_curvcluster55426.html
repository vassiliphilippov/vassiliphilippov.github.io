Loxia curvirostra

noun
species of finch with crossed bill
Pronunciation: / ˌloʊksiə ˌkɜrvɪˈrɑstrə / (AmE), / ˌlɒksɪə ˌkɜːvɪˈrɒstrə / (BrE)
