steely-eyed

adjective
having a firm, determined expression
8.1 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈstiː.liː.aɪd/ /; / /ˈstiː.li aɪd/ (AmE), /ˈstiː.lɪ.aɪd/ /; / /ˈstiː.li aɪd/ (BrE)
