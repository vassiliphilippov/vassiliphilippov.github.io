let fly

verb
suddenly shoot or throw something
8.2 5.8
Pronunciation: / lɛt flaɪ / (AmE), / lɛt flaɪ / (BrE)
to shoot, throw, or send something suddenly and forcefully (can be a physical object or words)

Examples
