broad-brush approach

noun
general or oversimplified method
6.5
Pronunciation: / brɔd brʌʃ əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / brɔːd brʌʃ əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a method or way of dealing with something that is general and ignores or oversimplifies details

Examples

A broad-brush approach ignores details
Critics argued that the broad-brush approach failed to address the unique needs of different communities.
The management adopted a broad-brush approach rather than taking time to study the individual problems in detail.
When dealing with diverse populations, a broad-brush approach can lead to significant misunderstandings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generalization - the act of making sweeping or broad statements
  • antonym: nuanced approach - a method that pays careful attention to details and subtleties
