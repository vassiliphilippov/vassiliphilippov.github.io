bane of one's existence

noun
source of persistent distress or annoyance
Pronunciation: / beɪn əv wʌnz ɪgˈzɪstəns / (AmE), / beɪn əv wʌnz ɪgˈzɪstəns / (BrE)
A person or thing that causes continuous misery, distress, or annoyance to someone.

Examples

The bane of one's existence
For many students, math homework can seem like the bane of their existence.
That leaky faucet has become the bane of his existence for months.
The early morning alarm is truly the bane of my existence during winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nuisance - a person, thing, or circumstance causing inconvenience or annoyance
  • synonym: torment - a cause of severe suffering or distress
