marbleization

noun
texture or appearance like marble
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑrbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌmɑːrbələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɑːbələɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌmɑːbɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or result of acquiring a texture or appearance resembling that of marble, typically involving veining or mottling.
