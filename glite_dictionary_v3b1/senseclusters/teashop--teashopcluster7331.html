teashop

noun
restaurant serving tea and snacks
Pronunciation: / ˈtiˌʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌʃɒp / (BrE)
A small restaurant or café where tea and light refreshments are served.

Examples

A teashop by river
After a long walk, they relaxed in a cozy teashop by the river.
The village teashop is famous for its homemade pastries.
We stopped at a little teashop for some scones and tea.

Related Terms
