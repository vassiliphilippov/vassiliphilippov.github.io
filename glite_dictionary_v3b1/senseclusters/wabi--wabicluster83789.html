wabi

noun
lonely melancholy
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːbi / (AmE), / ˈwɑːbi / (BrE)
a feeling of loneliness, desolation, or quiet melancholy, especially in the original Japanese sense

Examples

A deep wabi
After the war he felt a deep wabi, an empty loneliness that never fully left him.
The poem captures the sense of wabi that comes with autumn's fading light.
She sat in silence, experiencing a gentle wabi as she watched the rain fall.
