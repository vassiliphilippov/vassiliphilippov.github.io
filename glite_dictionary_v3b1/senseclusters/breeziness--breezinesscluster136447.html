breeziness

noun
the state of being mildly windy
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbriziˌnəs / (AmE), / ˈbriːziːnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being mildly windy or characterized by gentle breezes

Examples

Summer breeziness
She enjoyed the breeziness that drifted through the open window on summer evenings.
The breeziness of the beach made our walk especially refreshing.
There was a pleasant breeziness in the garden after the afternoon rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breezy - having a light, gentle wind
  • antonym: stillness - absence of wind or movement in the air
