drying up

noun
removal of moisture
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪɪŋ ʌp / (AmE), / ˈdraɪɪŋ ʌp / (BrE)
the process of removing or extracting moisture, especially from a substance or environment

Examples

The drying up process
Effective drying up of herbs preserves their flavor for months.
Frequent drying up of paintbrushes can ruin their bristles.
The drying up of the river left the surrounding farmland parched.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dehydration - the process of losing or removing water
