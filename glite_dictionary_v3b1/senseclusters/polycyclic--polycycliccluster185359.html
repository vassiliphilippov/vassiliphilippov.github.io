polycyclic

adjective
multiple ring structures
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈsaɪklɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈsɪklɪk / (BrE)
having two or more interconnected ring structures, especially in chemical compounds

Examples

Polycyclic compound
Many antibiotics are classified as polycyclic because of their complex ring structures.
Researchers studied the effects of polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons on air quality.
Some natural toxins are polycyclic compounds found in certain plants.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bicyclic - having two interconnected ring structures
  • antonym: monocyclic - having a single ring structure
