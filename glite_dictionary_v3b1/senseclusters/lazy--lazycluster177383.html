Synonyms

  • slothful
    7.2 0.3/m
    slothful is habitually slow and idle in action, while lazy is a general unwillingness to work or use energy.
  • indolent
    8.0 0.2/m
    indolent is a formal word for someone who avoids effort by habit, while lazy is the everyday term for unwillingness.
  • shiftless
    8.4 0.4/m
    shiftless shows lack of ambition and initiative, while lazy only says a person is unwilling to work.
  • work-shy
    8.5 0.01/m
    work-shy describes someone who avoids paid work or duty, while lazy means not wanting effort in general.
  • lackadaisical
    8.6 0.5/m
    lackadaisical shows low energy and careless attitude, while lazy focuses on unwillingness to do work or effort.
