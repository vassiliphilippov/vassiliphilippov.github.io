ravaged

adjective
severely damaged or destroyed
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrævɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˈravɪdʒd / (BrE)
having been severely damaged or destroyed by force, violence, or other destructive agency.

Examples

The ravaged coastline

The once-beautiful town was ravaged by war and natural disaster.
Years of drought and overgrazing had ravaged the landscape almost beyond recognition.
