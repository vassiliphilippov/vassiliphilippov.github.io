negative

noun
photographic film with reversed colors
3.7 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈneɡ.ə.tɪv/ /; / /ˈneɡ.tɪv/ (AmE), /ˈneɡ.ə.tɪv/ /; / /ˈneɡ.tɪv/ (BrE)
A piece of photographic film or image in which the colors and brightness values are reversed, used especially in making photographic prints.

Examples

Photo negative
He carefully stored the wedding negatives in a special envelope for safekeeping.
The old camera still used film negatives to develop pictures.
You can make extra prints from the negative if you ever lose the original photo.

Related Terms

  • antonym: positive - a photographic image with true colors and brightness, opposite of negative
  • degree higher: print - a photographic image made from a negative
