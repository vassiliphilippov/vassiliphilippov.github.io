miss out

verb
fail to benefit from or experience
Pronunciation: / mɪs aʊt / (AmE), / mɪs aʊt / (BrE)
to fail to benefit from or take advantage of something; to not participate in or experience something enjoyable or worthwhile

Examples

To miss out on fun
If you don't buy your tickets now, you might miss out on the concert.
She always feels like she misses out when her friends go on trips without her.
We missed out because we arrived after all the good seats were taken.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lose out - to be disadvantaged or to not obtain something desirable
