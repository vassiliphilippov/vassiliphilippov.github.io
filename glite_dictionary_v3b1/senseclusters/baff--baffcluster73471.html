baff

verb
to strike lightly
Pronunciation: / bæf / (AmE), / bæf / (BrE)
to strike something with a soft or light blow, or to baffle or frustrate someone

Examples

To baff a shield
In the old poem, the knight would baff his shield with a gentle touch.
The teacher's riddle seemed to baff even the brightest students in the class.
He tried to baff the dust from the table with a soft cloth.
