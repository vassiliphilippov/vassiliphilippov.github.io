considerately

adverb
in a thoughtful, considerate way
1.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɪd.ɚ.ət.li / (AmE), / kənˈsɪd.ər.ət.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows careful thought for the feelings or needs of others.

Examples

To considerately postpone
He considerately postponed the meeting so everyone could attend.
She considerately held the door open for the elderly man.
The guests considerately lowered their voices when they saw the baby sleeping.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inconsiderately - in an inconsiderate manner
  • synonym: thoughtfully - in a thoughtful manner
