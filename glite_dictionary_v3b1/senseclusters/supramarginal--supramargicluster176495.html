supramarginal

noun
brain region (informal)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəˈmɑːrdʒɪnəl / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˈmɑːdʒɪnəl / (BrE)
an informal term for the supramarginal gyrus, a part of the brain's parietal lobe involved in language and perception

Examples

Supramarginal gyrus
The neurologist explained that the supramarginal plays a key role in processing spoken language.
After the injury, scans showed damage to the left supramarginal in his brain.
Researchers are studying how the supramarginal contributes to reading and writing skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angular gyrus - a brain region near the supramarginal gyrus involved in language and cognition
  • synonym: supramarginal gyrus - a region of the parietal lobe involved in language and perception
