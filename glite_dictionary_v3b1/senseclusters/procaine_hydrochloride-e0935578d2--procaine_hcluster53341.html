procaine hydrochloride

noun
local anesthetic compound
Pronunciation: / proʊˈkeɪn haɪˌdrɑːkləˌraɪd / (AmE), / prəʊˈkeɪn haɪˌdrɒkləˌraɪd / (BrE)
A white crystalline powder used as a local anesthetic, commonly known by the trade name Novocain.

Examples

Procaine hydrochloride local anesthetic
