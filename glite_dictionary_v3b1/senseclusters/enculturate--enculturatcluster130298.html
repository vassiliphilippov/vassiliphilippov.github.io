enculturate

verb
to adapt culturally
9.2
Pronunciation: /en.ˈkʌl.tʃə.reɪt/ /; / /eŋ.ˈkʌl.tʃə.reɪt/ (AmE), /en.ˈkʌl.tʃə.reɪt/ /; / /eŋ.ˈkʌl.tʃə.reɪt/ (BrE)
to adapt or modify something so that it fits the customs, values, or expectations of a particular culture

Examples

To enculturate a product
The company worked hard to enculturate its products for the Asian market.
Teachers help students enculturate by introducing them to local customs and traditions.
The film was enculturated to appeal to audiences in different countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acculturate - to adapt to or adopt the cultural traits of another group
  • synonym: localize - to adapt something for use in a particular country or culture
