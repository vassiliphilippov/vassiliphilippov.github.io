lobelia

noun
a genus of flowering plants
7.6
Pronunciation: / loʊˈbiliə / (AmE), / ləʊˈbiːliə / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Lobelia, typically with blue, violet, or white tubular flowers, some species of which are grown as ornamentals or used in herbal medicine.

Examples

Blue lobelia flowers
She planted several lobelia around the edge of the garden for a splash of blue.
Some species of lobelia are valued for their medicinal properties.
The vibrant lobelia stood out among the more subdued-colored flowers in the flowerbed.
