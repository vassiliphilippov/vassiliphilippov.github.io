labial vein

noun
vein that drains the lips
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.bi.əl veɪn / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bi.əl veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the lips of the mouth

Examples

Labial vein of mouth
During the anatomy lesson, we observed the labial vein in a cross-section of the lip.
Swelling of the labial vein can occur following trauma to the mouth.
The surgeon carefully avoided the labial vein during the procedure to minimize bleeding.
