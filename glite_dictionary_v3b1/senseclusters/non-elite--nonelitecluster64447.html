non-elite

adjective
not elite
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn ɪˈlit / (AmE), / ˌnɒn ɪˈliːt / (BrE)
not belonging to or characteristic of the elite group; ordinary or not among the best or most privileged

Examples

Non-elite schools
The scholarship is available to non-elite students from all backgrounds.
Many non-elite athletes train just as hard as professionals.
The event was open to both elite and non-elite participants.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elite - belonging to the best or most privileged group
  • synonym: ordinary - not special or exceptional; common
