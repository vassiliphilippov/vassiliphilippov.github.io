absorbed

adjective
deeply engrossed or fully attentive
9.9
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɔrbd / (AmE), / əbˈzɔːbd / (BrE)
deeply interested or wholly engaged in something; giving all one's attention to something

Examples

Absorbed in thought
She was so absorbed in her book that she didn't hear her phone ring.
The children listened with absorbed attention to the storyteller.
While painting, he became completely absorbed in his work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distracted - unable to concentrate because one's mind is preoccupied
  • synonym: engrossed - completely occupied by or giving full attention to something
