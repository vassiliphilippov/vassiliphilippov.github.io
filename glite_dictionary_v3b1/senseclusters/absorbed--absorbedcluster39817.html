absorbed

adjective
deeply engrossed or fully attentive
3.6 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / …ôbÀàz…îrbd / (AmE), / …ôbÀàz…îÀêbd / (BrE)
deeply interested or wholly engaged in something; giving all one's attention to something

Examples

Absorbed in thought
She was so absorbed in her book that she didn't hear her phone ring.
The children listened with absorbed attention to the storyteller.
While painting, he became completely absorbed in his work.

  • rapt
    7.5 1.42/m

  • engaged

  • wrapped up
    4.8 2.37/m

  • captivated
    3.9 0.00/m
    captivated means being charmed or strongly attracted, while absorbed focuses on giving all your attention without charm.
  • enthralled
    6.9 0.15/m

  • fascinated
    2.1 0.07/m

  • entranced
    5.8 0.03/m

  • mesmerized

  • spellbound
