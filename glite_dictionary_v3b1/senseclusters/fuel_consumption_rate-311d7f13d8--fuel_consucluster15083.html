fuel consumption rate

noun
rate at which fuel is used
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːəl kənˈsʌmpʃən reɪt / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəl kənˈsʌmpʃən reɪt / (BrE)
the amount of fuel used by a vehicle or machine over a certain distance or time, typically expressed as miles per gallon or liters per 100 kilometers

Examples

The fuel consumption rate
He calculated the fuel consumption rate during the long road trip to monitor expenses.
Regular maintenance can help improve your car's fuel consumption rate.
The fuel consumption rate of hybrid cars is much lower than that of traditional gasoline vehicles.
