foodway

noun
cultural food traditions
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːdˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈfuːdˌweɪ / (BrE)
the traditional customs, practices, and beliefs about food in a particular culture or community

Examples

Traditional foodways study
Anthropologists study foodways to understand how different societies prepare and share meals.
The festival celebrates the unique foodways of the region, including traditional recipes and cooking methods.
Immigrants often bring their foodways with them, enriching the local culture with new flavors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cuisine - a style or method of cooking characteristic of a particular country or region
  • synonym: tradition - a custom or belief passed down within a group or society
