pocket change

noun
small amount of coins or money
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːkɪt tʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt tʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
a small amount of money, especially coins, that someone carries with them for minor expenses
