Nissen hut

noun
prefabricated semicircular hut, often military
6.3 7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈnɪs.ən hʌt/ (AmE), /ˈnɪs.ən hʌt/ (BrE)
a prefabricated hut made of corrugated iron with a semicircular cross-section, typically used for temporary military or storage purposes

Examples

A Nissen hut building
During World War II, soldiers were often housed in Nissen huts on military bases.
Engineers assembled the Nissen hut quickly, providing emergency shelter for the displaced villagers.
The old airfield still has several abandoned Nissen huts standing near the runway.
