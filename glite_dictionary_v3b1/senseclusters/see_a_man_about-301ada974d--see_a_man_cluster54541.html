see a man about

verb
leave for an undisclosed reason

Pronunciation: / si ə mæn əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / siː ə mæn əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
(informal) Used to indicate that one is leaving to undertake some private or undisclosed business, often as an excuse to avoid stating the real reason.

Examples

To see a man about something
