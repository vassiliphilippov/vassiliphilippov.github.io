regenerable

adjective
able to be renewed
6.5
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdʒɛnərəbəl /; / rɪˈdʒɛnərəbl / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒɛnərəbəl /; / rɪˈdʒɛnərəbl / (BrE)
able to be restored, renewed, or made usable again after use or damage (including by technical or chemical processes)

Examples

Using a regenerable catalyst reduces waste and lowers operating costs in industrial processes.
