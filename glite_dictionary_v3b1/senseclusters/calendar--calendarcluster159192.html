calendar

verb
to enter or schedule in a calendar
Pronunciation: / ˈkæl.ən.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæl.ən.də / (BrE)
to enter or schedule (an event, appointment, etc.) in a calendar for future reference or action.

Examples

To calendar a meeting
Don’t forget to calendar your upcoming appointments so you don’t miss them.
I immediately calendared the due date to ensure I wouldn't forget.
The assistant calendars every staff meeting as soon as she receives the notice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: schedule - to arrange or plan (an event) to take place at a particular time
