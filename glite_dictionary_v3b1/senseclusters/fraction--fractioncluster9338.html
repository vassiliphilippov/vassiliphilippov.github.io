fraction

noun
small part or portion
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfræk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈfræk.ʃən / (BrE)
a very small part or segment of something; a piece of a whole

Examples

A small fraction
He inherited just a fraction of the estate.
Only a fraction of the population attended the meeting.
You need to add even a tiny fraction of salt for the recipe to taste right.

Related Terms

  • synonym: portion - a part of a whole; a piece
  • antonym: whole - something complete, entire amount
