financial support

noun
money provided to help something happen
1.4
Pronunciation: / faɪˈnænʃəl səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃəl səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
money or other financial resources given to help make something possible, such as a project, activity, or organization

Examples

The charity relies on financial support from the public to continue its work.
Without financial support, many students would not be able to attend university.
