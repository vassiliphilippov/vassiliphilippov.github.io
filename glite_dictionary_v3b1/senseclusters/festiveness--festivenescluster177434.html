festiveness

noun
cheerful atmosphere
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛstɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɛstɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being cheerful, lively, and appropriate for a celebration or festival

Examples

The festiveness of celebration
The festiveness of the parade brought the whole community together in celebration.
Colorful lights and music added to the festiveness of the holiday party.
You could feel the festiveness in the air as people prepared for the festival.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gloom - a state of darkness or sadness
  • synonym: merriment - cheerful fun and enjoyment
