inviter

noun
electronic invitation tool
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvaɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈvaɪtə / (BrE)
a device, application, or feature that sends invitations, especially electronically, to people for events or meetings

Examples

Online inviter tool
The meeting inviter automatically sent calendar links to all participants.
Our new app includes an inviter that makes it easy to schedule group events.
She used the inviter feature to send digital invitations for her birthday party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: organizer - a person or tool that arranges and manages events
  • synonym: scheduler - a tool or person that arranges times for events or meetings
