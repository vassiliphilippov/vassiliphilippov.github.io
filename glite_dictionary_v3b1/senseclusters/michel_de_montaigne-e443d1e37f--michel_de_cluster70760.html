Michel de Montaigne

proper noun
French essayist and philosopher (1533–1592)
Pronunciation: / miːˈʃɛl də mɒnˈteɪn / (AmE), / miːˈʃɛl də mɒ̃ˈtɛɲ / (BrE)
(1533–1592) French writer considered the originator of the modern essay, known for his influential works on philosophy and human nature.
