regionalist

noun
supporter of a region
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdʒənəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈriːdʒənəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or emphasizes the interests and culture of a particular region over a larger area or nation

Examples

Regionalist leader
The regionalist campaigned for more funding for schools in their area.
As a proud regionalist, she promoted local traditions and festivals.
Debates between the regionalist and the central government became more frequent after the new law.

Related Terms

  • synonym: localist - a person who favors local control and interests
  • antonym: nationalist - a person who strongly supports their nation as a whole
