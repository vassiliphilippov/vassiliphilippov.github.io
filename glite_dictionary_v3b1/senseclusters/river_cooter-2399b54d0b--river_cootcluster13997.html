river cooter

noun
large freshwater river turtle of southern US and northern Mexico
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvɚ ˈkuːtɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɪvə ˈkuːtə / (BrE)
a large freshwater turtle native to rivers and streams of the southern United States and northern Mexico, known for its smooth shell and aquatic habits

Examples

The river cooter turtle
The river cooter spends much of its time basking on logs along the riverbank.
Unlike some other turtles, the river cooter is highly aquatic and rarely leaves its watery habitat.
We spotted a river cooter swimming gracefully in the clear water.
