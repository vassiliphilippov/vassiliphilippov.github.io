ruby-crowned kinglet

noun
species of North American songbird
Pronunciation: / ˌruːbiˌkraʊnd ˈkɪŋlɪt / (AmE), / ˌruːbiˌkraʊnd ˈkɪŋlɪt / (BrE)
A small American songbird of the kinglet family, the male of which has a distinctive red crown patch.

Examples

Male ruby-crowned kinglet
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a male ruby-crowned kinglet displaying its scarlet crown.
During the migration season, ruby-crowned kinglets can be heard singing their complex songs.
The ruby-crowned kinglet is often seen flitting through shrubs in search of insects.
