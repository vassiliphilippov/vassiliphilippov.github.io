huddle

verb
crowd together or curl up
2.3 2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌd.əl / (AmE), / ˈhʌd.əl / (BrE)
to crowd closely together or curl up, often for warmth, comfort, or protection

Examples

To huddle together
She huddled in a corner, trying to stay hidden from view.
The children huddled together for warmth during the cold night.
We huddled under the small shelter until the storm passed.
During the emergency, everyone huddled in the corner of the room, waiting for instructions.
The children huddled together under the tree to stay dry during the rainstorm.
We decided to huddle around the campfire for warmth as the night grew colder.
