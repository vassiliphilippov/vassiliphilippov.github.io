quotative

noun
reported speech marker

Pronunciation: /ˈkwoʊ.tə.tɪv/ (AmE), /ˈkwəʊ.tə.tɪv/ (BrE)
a word, particle, or grammatical marker used to introduce reported speech or a quotation

Examples

A quotative word
