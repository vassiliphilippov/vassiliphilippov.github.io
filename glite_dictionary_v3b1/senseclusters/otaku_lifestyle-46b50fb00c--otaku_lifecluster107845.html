otaku lifestyle

noun
life focused on anime and gaming culture
Pronunciation: / oʊˈtɑːku ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (AmE), / əʊˈtɑːku ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (BrE)
A way of life centered around a deep interest in anime, manga, and gaming culture, often characterized by passionate fandom and hobbyist activities.

Examples

The otaku lifestyle culture
He embraced the otaku lifestyle, spending his weekends attending anime conventions and playing video games.
Her room is decorated with figures and posters that reflect her otaku lifestyle.
Many blogs document the daily routines and favorite activities of people living an otaku lifestyle.
