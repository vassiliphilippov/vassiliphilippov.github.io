lase

verb
to use a laser
8.9
Pronunciation: / leɪz / (AmE), / leɪz / (BrE)
to treat, cut, or remove material, especially tissue, by applying laser radiation

Examples

To lase a scar
The doctor will lase the scar tissue to improve the patient's vision.
Modern dentists often lase cavities instead of using traditional drills.
Specialists can lase birthmarks to reduce their appearance with minimal discomfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irradiate - to expose to radiation
