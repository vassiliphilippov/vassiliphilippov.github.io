zopilote

noun
American black vulture
Pronunciation: / soʊpiˈloʊteɪ /; / ˌzoʊpiˈloʊteɪ / (AmE), / sɒpɪˈləʊteɪ /; / ˌzɒpɪˈləʊteɪ / (BrE)
a large black vulture native to the Americas, especially common in Mexico and Central America; also, any New World vulture (scavenging bird of the family Cathartidae)
