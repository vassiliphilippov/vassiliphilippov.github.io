supplement

noun
something added to make up a deficiency
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌpləmənt / (AmE), / ˈsʌplɪmənt / (BrE)
something extra that is added to improve, enhance, or make up for a lack (such as a health product or extra amount)

Examples

Vitamin supplement
Athletes often take a supplement to ensure they get enough vitamins and minerals.
She added a calcium supplement to her daily routine for stronger bones.
The doctor recommended an iron supplement to address her anemia.
She takes a daily supplement to improve her health.
The magazine includes a free supplement on travel tips.
The new software acts as a supplement to the existing system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addition - a thing that is added to something else
  • synonym: additive - a substance added to something to improve or preserve it
  • synonym: complement - something that completes or enhances something else
