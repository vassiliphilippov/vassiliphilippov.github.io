worsening

noun
process of becoming worse
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrsənɪŋ /; / ˈwɝːsənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːsənɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or act of becoming worse or declining in quality, value, or condition

Examples

His financial troubles led to a gradual worsening of his mental health.
Doctors monitored the patient's worsening over several days.
The worsening of diplomatic relations led to further tension.
There has been a noticeable worsening of air quality in the city.
