barber chair

noun
adjustable chair used in barbershops
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrbər tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbə tʃeə / (BrE)
a large, adjustable chair with a headrest and footrest, used for seating customers in a barbershop while receiving haircuts or shaves

Examples

A barber chair reclined
Every modern barber chair can be reclined to help the barber shave customers more easily.
He polished the old barber chair until the leather gleamed.
The customer settled comfortably into the barber chair before his haircut began.
