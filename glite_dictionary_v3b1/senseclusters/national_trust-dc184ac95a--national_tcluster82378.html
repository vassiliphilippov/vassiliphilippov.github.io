National Trust

proper noun
UK body preserving historic sites and landscapes
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃənl trʌst / (AmE), / ˈnæʃənəl trʌst / (BrE)
A British organization dedicated to preserving historic buildings, monuments, and natural areas of beauty, founded in 1895 and supported by endowments and private subscriptions.
