Mycoplasmatales

noun
order of bacteria including mycoplasmas
Pronunciation: / maɪˌkɑːplæzˈmeɪtəliːz / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkəʊplæzˈmeɪtəliːz / (BrE)
an order of bacteria within the class Mollicutes, comprising organisms of the family Mycoplasmataceae; includes mycoplasmas, which lack a cell wall

Examples

Order Mycoplasmatales bacteria
Bacteria in the order Mycoplasmatales are characterized by a lack of a cell wall.
Many species within Mycoplasmatales are pathogenic to humans and animals.
Research on Mycoplasmatales has advanced our understanding of antibiotic resistance in mycoplasmas.
