throat protector

noun
neck protection for athletes
7.9 3.3
Pronunciation: /θroʊt prə.ˈtek.tər/ (AmE), /θrəʊt prə.ˈtek.tə/ (BrE)
a piece of protective equipment worn around the neck by a hockey goalkeeper or baseball catcher to shield the throat from injury

Examples
