nudie

noun
rhinestone stage suit
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːdi / (AmE), / ˈnjuːdi / (BrE)
a decorative, rhinestone-covered stage suit, especially in the style created by designer Nudie Cohn and worn by country musicians and performers

Examples

A sparkling nudie suit
The singer dazzled the audience in a sparkling nudie covered with rhinestones and embroidery.
Collectors pay high prices for an original nudie once worn by a famous country star.
He wore a custom-made nudie to the music awards, attracting everyone's attention.
