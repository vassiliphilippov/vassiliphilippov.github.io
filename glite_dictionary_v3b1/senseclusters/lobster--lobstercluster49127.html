lobster

verb
to catch lobsters
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑbstər / (AmE), / ˈlɒbstə / (BrE)
to catch lobsters, usually as a job or regular activity, often using traps in the sea

Examples

To lobster for a living
He spent his mornings lobstering off the coast to supply the local restaurant.
Many families in the village have been lobstering for generations.
During the summer, she tried lobstering and learned how to set traps in the water.
