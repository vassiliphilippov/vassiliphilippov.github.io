Pouteria zapota

noun
tropical tree; source of mamey sapote fruit
Pronunciation: / poʊˈtɪəriə zəˈpoʊtə / (AmE), / paʊˈtɪəriə zəˈpəʊtə / (BrE)
A tropical American tree with mahogany-like wood and sweet, edible, egg-shaped fruit, also known as the mamey sapote.
