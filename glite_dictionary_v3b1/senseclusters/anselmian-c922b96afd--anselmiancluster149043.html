Anselmian

adjective
of Anselm's philosophy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ænˈsɛlmiən / (AmE), / ænˈsɛlmiən / (BrE)
relating to the ideas, philosophy, or theology of Anselm, especially his arguments about the existence of God

Examples

Anselmian philosophy
The Anselmian approach to theology emphasizes reason and faith working together.
Many philosophers have debated the validity of the Anselmian argument for the existence of God.
Her research focused on Anselmian concepts in medieval Christian thought.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scholastic - relating to medieval philosophy and theology
