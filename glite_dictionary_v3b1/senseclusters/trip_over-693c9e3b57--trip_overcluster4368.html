trip over

verb
stumble after catching one's foot
5.4
Pronunciation: / trɪp ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / trɪp ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to lose one's balance because one's foot strikes or catches on something while walking or running

Examples

To trip over shoelaces
Be careful not to trip over your shoelaces before the race starts.
I always seem to trip over that step when I come into the room.
She nearly tripped over the garden hose on the sidewalk.
