gaze

noun
a long fixed look
5.7
Pronunciation: / ɡeɪz / (AmE), / ɡeɪz / (BrE)
a long and steady look at someone or something

Examples

A steady gaze
He met her gaze with a reassuring smile.
She held his gaze for several moments before looking away.
The painting captured the somber gaze of the old man.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: glance - a quick look
  • synonym: stare - a fixed and often wide-eyed look
