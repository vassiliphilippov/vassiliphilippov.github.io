tickly

adjective
sensitive to tickling
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkli / (AmE), / ˈtɪkli / (BrE)
easily made to laugh or react by light touching, especially because of sensitivity to being tickled.

Examples

Tickly feet
My little brother is so tickly that he giggles whenever you touch his feet.
She avoided wearing wool sweaters because they made her neck feel tickly all day.
The grass felt tickly against his bare legs as he ran through the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ticklish - easily made to laugh or squirm by being tickled
