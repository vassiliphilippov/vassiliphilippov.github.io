see

verb
visit a professional for advice or treatment
Pronunciation: / siː /; / si / (AmE), / siː / (BrE)
to visit or consult a professional person, such as a doctor or official

Examples

To see a doctor
He decided to see a financial adviser about his investments.
If your symptoms continue, you should see a doctor immediately.
We were advised to see a specialist for further tests.
She prefers to see new patients in the morning.
The dentist will see you at 3 p.m. for your appointment.
The manager doesn't see clients without a scheduled meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meet - to come into the presence or company of someone
  • synonym: receive - to formally accept or admit someone as a visitor or guest
