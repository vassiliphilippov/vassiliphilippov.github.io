stan

verb
be an obsessive fan
Pronunciation: / stæn / (AmE), / stæn / (BrE)
to be an extremely devoted or obsessive fan of someone or something, especially expressed online; to strongly support or defend them.

Examples

To stan a celebrity
I stan that indie band; their lyrics really helped me through college.
She admits to stanning the actor and defends his performances in every online thread.
Many fans stanned the director after the film's unexpected announcement of a sequel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fan - a person who has a strong interest in or admiration for someone or something.
  • synonym: idolize - to admire or love someone excessively or uncritically.
