phut

verb
to make popping sound
Pronunciation: / fʌt / (AmE), / fʌt / (BrE)
to make a soft, short popping or explosive sound, often suddenly and quietly

Examples

The engine phuts
The old engine phut and stopped in the middle of the road.
As the candle burned down, it suddenly phut and went out.
The toy balloon phut when it touched the sharp corner of the table.
