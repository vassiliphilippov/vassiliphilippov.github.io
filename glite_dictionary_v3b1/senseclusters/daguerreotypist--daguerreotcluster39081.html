daguerreotypist

noun
early photographer
9.9
Pronunciation: / dəˈɡɛrəˌtaɪpɪst / (AmE), / dəˈɡɛərəˌtaɪpɪst / (BrE)
a person who makes photographs using the daguerreotype process, an early photographic technique

Examples

The daguerreotypist makes photos
The daguerreotypist carefully prepared the silver plate before taking the portrait.
In the 19th century, a skilled daguerreotypist was highly sought after for family photographs.
She studied the work of a famous daguerreotypist to learn about early photography techniques.
