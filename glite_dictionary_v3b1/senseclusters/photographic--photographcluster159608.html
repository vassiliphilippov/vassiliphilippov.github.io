photographic

adjective
relating to photography
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtəˈgræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəˈgræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or obtained by using photography; concerning the art or practice of taking and processing photographs.

Examples

Photographic equipment
His memory is so strong it's almost photographic.
She has a keen interest in photographic composition and lighting.
The museum featured an exhibition of early photographic equipment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonphotographic - not relating to or obtained by photography
  • synonym: photographic - obtained or produced by photography
