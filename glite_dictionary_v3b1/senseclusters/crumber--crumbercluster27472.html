crumber

noun
table cleaning tool
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌmbər / (AmE), / ˈkrʌmbə / (BrE)
a small tool used to remove crumbs from a table, especially in restaurants

Examples

Table crumber
The waiter used a crumber to clean the table between courses.
After the meal, she picked up the crumber to tidy up the crumbs.
A silver crumber was placed on the tablecloth for the formal dinner.
