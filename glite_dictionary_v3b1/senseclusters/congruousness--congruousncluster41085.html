congruousness

noun
quality of agreement or appropriateness
9.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈɡruːəs.nəs / (AmE), / kənˈɡruː.əs.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being in agreement, harmony, or appropriate with something else.

Examples

The congruousness of ideas
A lack of congruousness can make even a well-planned event feel awkward.
She appreciated the congruousness between his words and his actions.
The congruousness of the design with its surroundings impressed the judges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: congruity - the quality of being in agreement or harmony
  • antonym: incongruity - the state of being out of place or not in harmony
