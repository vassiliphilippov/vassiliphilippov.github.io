tree-frog

noun
an arboreal frog with toe pads
6.2 2.8
Pronunciation: / tri frɔg /; / triː frɑːɡ / (AmE), / triː frɒg /; / triː frɒɡ / (BrE)
a small frog that lives in trees and has sticky pads on its toes for climbing

Examples

A tree-frog in trees
The tree-frog uses its sticky pads to cling to leaves high in the rainforest canopy.
