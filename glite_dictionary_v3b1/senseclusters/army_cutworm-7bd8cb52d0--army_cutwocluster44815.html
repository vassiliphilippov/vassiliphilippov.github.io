army cutworm

noun
agricultural pest caterpillar in North America
9.0 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːr.miː ˈkʌ.twɜːrm/ /; / /ˈɑːr.miː ˈkʌt.wɜːrm/ (AmE), /ˈɑː.miː ˈkʌ.twɜːm/ /; / /ˈɑː.miː ˈkʌt.wɜːm/ (BrE)
the larva of a noctuid moth (Euxoa auxilliaris) that migrates in large groups and is destructive to grains and alfalfa crops, especially in the midwestern United States
