section

noun
a land unit of 1 square mile
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
A land unit used in the U.S. and Canada, equal to 1 square mile (2.59 square kilometers), especially as part of the rectangular survey system.

Examples

A land section
Each section is further divided into smaller parcels for sale or lease.
The farm covers an entire section of land west of the river.
The property consists of four sections, making it a total of four square miles.
