fly over

verb
pass above in flight
Pronunciation: / flaɪ ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / flaɪ ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to travel above something by flying

Examples

To fly over a city
Several helicopters flew over the city during the parade.
The pilot flies over the mountains every week as part of his route.
We watched the flock of birds fly over the lake at sunset.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fly past - to pass by something while flying
  • antonym: fly under - to pass beneath something while flying
