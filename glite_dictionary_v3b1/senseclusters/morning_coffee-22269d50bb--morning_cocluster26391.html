morning coffee

noun
coffee consumed in the morning
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
a cup of coffee typically consumed in the morning as part of a daily routine or to help wake up

Examples

A morning coffee cup
I can't function properly until I've had my morning coffee.
She always enjoys her morning coffee while reading the news.
The rich aroma of morning coffee filled the kitchen.
