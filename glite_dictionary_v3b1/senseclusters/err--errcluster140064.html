err

verb
to make a mistake
Pronunciation: / ɛr / (AmE), / ɜː / (BrE)
to make a mistake or be incorrect

Examples

To err in judgment
Even experts can err when the data is incomplete.
The doctor did not err in her diagnosis, as the results later confirmed.
To err is human, but to repeat mistakes is careless.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunder - to make a careless or serious mistake
  • antonym: correct - to make right; to remove mistakes
  • synonym: misjudge - to judge wrongly or incorrectly
