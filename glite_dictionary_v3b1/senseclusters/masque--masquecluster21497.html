masque

noun
facial beauty mask
5.0
Pronunciation: /mæsk/ (AmE), /mɑːsk/ /; / /mæsk/ (BrE)
a cosmetic product or treatment (a thick cream, gel, clay, or sheet) applied to the face to cleanse, hydrate, or treat the skin, usually removed or rinsed off after a set time.

Examples

A facial masque
She left the spa with a glowing complexion after a refreshing masque.
He bought a clay masque to clear the oiliness from his T-zone before the party.
Many beauty bloggers recommend overnight hydrating masques for dry skin during cold winter months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facial - a beauty treatment for the face that may include cleansing, masks, and massage
