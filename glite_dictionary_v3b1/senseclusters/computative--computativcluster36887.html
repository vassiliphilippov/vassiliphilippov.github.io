computative

adjective
calculating or practical
8.2
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpjuːtətɪv / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtətɪv / (BrE)
inclined to calculate, plan, or approach situations in a practical and often unemotional way

Examples

Computative mindset
Her computative approach to negotiations made colleagues view her as emotionally distant but effective.
He is known for his computative mindset when making important business decisions.
The manager's computative style helped the team achieve their goals efficiently.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotional - showing strong feelings
  • synonym: pragmatic - dealing with things sensibly and realistically
