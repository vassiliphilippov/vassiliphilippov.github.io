extinct

adjective
permanently inactive (of volcano)
7.6 4.3
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈstɪŋkt/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈstɪŋkt/ (BrE)
(of a volcano) permanently inactive, having stopped erupting and showing no signs of future activity.

Examples

Extinct volcano
Mount Slemish is classified as an extinct volcano by geologists.
The scientists declared the volcano extinct after centuries without any eruptions.
Unlike active or dormant volcanoes, an extinct volcano is not expected to erupt again.
