nonsensuous

adjective
not sensory
9.3
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɛnʃuəs / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɛnʃuəs / (BrE)
not appealing to or involving the senses or physical pleasure; intellectual rather than sensory

Examples

A nonsensuous idea
The philosopher's arguments were highly nonsensuous, focusing on logic rather than feeling.
Her artwork is deliberately nonsensuous, avoiding bright colors and rich textures.
He prefers nonsensuous music that stimulates the mind instead of the senses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intellectual - relating to the intellect rather than the senses
  • antonym: sensuous - appealing to or involving the senses or physical pleasure
