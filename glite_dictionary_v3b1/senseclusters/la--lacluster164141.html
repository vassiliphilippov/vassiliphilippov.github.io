la

noun
sixth note in solmization
Pronunciation: / lɑ / (AmE), / lɑː / (BrE)
the syllable used to name the sixth note of a major or minor scale in solmization (e.g., do-re-mi-fa-sol-la-ti-do)

Examples

The la note
In the song, the melody rises from la to ti and back to do.
She struggled to hold the la during her vocal warm-up.
When practicing scales, students often sing 'do re mi fa sol la ti do.'

  • degree lower: sol - the syllable naming the fifth note of a major or minor scale in solmization
  • degree higher: ti - the syllable naming the seventh note of a major or minor scale in solmization
