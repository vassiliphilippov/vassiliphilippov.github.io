pts

noun
plural of pint (abbr.)
4.1
Pronunciation: / pi ti ɛs / (AmE), / piː tiː ɛs / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'pints', used as the plural form in recipes and measurements to indicate liquid volume

Examples

3 pts of milk
The instructions say to add three pts of water to the mixture.
He bought two pts of milk from the grocery store.
The recipe requires one and a half pts of cream for the sauce.
