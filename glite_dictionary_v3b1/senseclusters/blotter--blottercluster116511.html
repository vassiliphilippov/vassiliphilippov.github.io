blotter

noun
police station event record
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈblɒtə / (BrE)
a daily written record maintained by a police station, listing events such as arrests or incidents

Examples

Police blotter
A review of the police blotter showed several incidents over the weekend.
Reporters often check the blotter for news about crimes and emergencies.
The officer entered the details of the arrest in the blotter before leaving his shift.

  • synonym: register - an official list or record of names or events
