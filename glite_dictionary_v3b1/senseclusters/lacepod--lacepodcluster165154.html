lacepod

noun
an annual herb with divided leaves and white flowers
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪsˌpɑd / (AmE), / ˈleɪsˌpɒd / (BrE)
An annual herb with deeply divided basal leaves and slender racemes of small white flowers, producing single-seeded, winged fruits.
