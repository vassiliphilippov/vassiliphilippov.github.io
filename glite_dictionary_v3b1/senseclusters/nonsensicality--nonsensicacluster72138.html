nonsensicality

noun
the state of being nonsensical
Pronunciation: / nɑnˌsɛnˈsɪkələti / (AmE), / nɒnˌsɛnˈsɪkələti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being nonsensical; the condition of lacking meaning or sense.

Examples

The nonsensicality of plot
Critics remarked on the nonsensicality of the film's plot.
She was frustrated by the nonsensicality of his arguments.
The nonsensicality of the instructions left everyone confused.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - something that has no meaning or makes no sense
