Ojibwa

noun
an Algonquian language of the Ojibwa
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈdʒɪbwɑː /; / oʊˈdʒɪb.weɪ / (AmE), / əʊˈdʒɪbwə /; / əʊˈdʒɪb.weɪ / (BrE)
the Algonquian language spoken by the Ojibwa people of North America
