beta orbiter

noun
man who supports woman without romantic success
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪtə ˈɔːrbɪtər / (AmE), / ˈbiːtə ˈɔːbɪtə / (BrE)
a man who regularly supports or admires a woman from the sidelines, usually without romantic or sexual reciprocation

Examples

A beta orbiter man
He spent months as her beta orbiter, always available but never more than a friend.
Jake realized he was just a beta orbiter after years of unrequited attention.
Some forums discuss how to avoid becoming a beta orbiter in online dating.
