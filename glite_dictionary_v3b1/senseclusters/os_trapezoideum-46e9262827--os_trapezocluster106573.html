os trapezoideum

noun
specific wrist (carpal) bone
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑs træpɛˈzoɪdiəm / (AmE), / ɒs trəˌpiːzəʊˈdiːəm / (BrE)
the small carpal bone in the wrist located between the trapezium and capitate bones

Examples

The os trapezoideum bone
An X-ray revealed a fracture in the patient's os trapezoideum.
The os trapezoideum is a small but important carpal bone in the human wrist.
The surgeon carefully examined the os trapezoideum during the procedure to assess the extent of the injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capitate - the largest carpal bone, adjacent to the os trapezoideum
  • synonym: trapezium - the wrist bone adjacent to the os trapezoideum
