victory lap

noun
celebratory circuit after a win
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪktəri læp / (AmE), / ˈvɪktəri læp / (BrE)
an act of celebrating a success or achievement, often by making a circuit or public display after winning a competition

Examples

Career victory lap
Her promotion at work felt like a victory lap after years of dedication.
The crowd cheered as the champion took his victory lap around the stadium.
The team took a victory lap to thank their supporters after the big win.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celebration - the act of marking an occasion with festivities
  • antonym: defeat - loss or failure in a competition or conflict
