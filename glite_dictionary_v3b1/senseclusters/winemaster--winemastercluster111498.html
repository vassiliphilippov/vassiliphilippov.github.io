winemaster

noun
wine expert
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪnˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈwaɪnˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person with expert knowledge in tasting, evaluating, and selecting wines, often serving as a judge or consultant

Examples

Winemaster judges wine
The winemaster selected the perfect bottle to complement the chef's special dish.
As a respected winemaster, she is often invited to judge international wine competitions.
The vineyard hired a new winemaster to improve the quality of their wines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oenologist - an expert in the science of wine and winemaking
  • synonym: sommelier - a trained wine steward in a restaurant
