Jiddah

proper noun
port city in Saudi Arabia, near Mecca
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪdə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪdə / (BrE)
A port city in western Saudi Arabia on the Red Sea, near Mecca; an important commercial center and gateway for pilgrims traveling to Mecca.

Examples

Jiddah port city
During the Hajj season, thousands of pilgrims pass through Jiddah on their way to Mecca.
Many international flights to Saudi Arabia land first in Jiddah.
The port of Jiddah is a hub for commerce in the region.
