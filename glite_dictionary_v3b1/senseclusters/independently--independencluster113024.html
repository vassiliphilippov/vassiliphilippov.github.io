independently

adverb
individually or separately
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈpɛndəntli / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈpɛndəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is done alone, without help or involvement from others

Examples

Work independently
Each student worked independently on the assignment to avoid influencing each other's results.
Each witness was interviewed independently to ensure unbiased testimony.
The twins prefer to dress independently, choosing their own unique styles.
After moving away to college, he learned to live independently.
She was able to solve the problem independently without any supervision.
The company operates independently from its parent organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autonomously - in a self-governing or self-directed way
  • antonym: dependently - in a way that relies on others
  • synonym: separately - as a distinct or individual entity
