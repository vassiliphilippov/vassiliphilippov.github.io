collective effort

noun
joint work by a group
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɛktɪv ˈɛfərt / (AmE), / kəˈlɛktɪv ˈefət / (BrE)
the work or action done together by a group of people to achieve a common goal

Examples

A collective effort
Bringing clean water to the village was a collective effort supported by everyone.
Solving climate change will require a global collective effort and cooperation.
The success of the campaign resulted from a collective effort across several departments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: teamwork - work done by a group acting together
