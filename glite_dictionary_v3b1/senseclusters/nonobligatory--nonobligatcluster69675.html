nonobligatory

adjective
not required; optional
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.əˈblɪɡ.əˌtɔr.i / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əˈblɪɡ.ə.tər.i / (BrE)
not required by rule, law, or obligation; optional.

Examples

A nonobligatory meeting
Attendance at the meeting is nonobligatory, so you may choose to participate or not.
Completing these survey questions is nonobligatory and will not affect your access to services.
The training session is nonobligatory, but it may be helpful for your professional development.

Related Terms

  • antonym: obligatory - required by a law or rule; compulsory
  • synonym: optional - not compulsory or required; left to one's choice
