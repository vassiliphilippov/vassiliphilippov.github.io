misopedist

noun
person who dislikes children
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsəˈpiːdɪst / (AmE), / ˌmɪsəˈpiːdɪst / (BrE)
a person who dislikes or has a hatred of children

Examples

A misopedist dislikes children
The main character was a notorious misopedist who refused to attend any children's parties.
Rumors spread that the old man was a misopedist because he always avoided the neighborhood playground.
Her reputation as a misopedist made her unpopular among parents in the community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: philanthropist - a person who loves and helps others, especially children
