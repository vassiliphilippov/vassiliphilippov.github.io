renunciation

noun
formal rejection of a belief or cause
Pronunciation: / rɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the formal rejection of a belief, cause, or way of life, especially religious beliefs, often in favor of an opposing cause or belief

Examples

Renunciation of faith
After a long inner struggle, she made a public renunciation of her political affiliation.
His renunciation of his former faith shocked the entire community.
The ceremony marks the renunciation of earthly desires for spiritual pursuit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abandonment - the act of giving up something completely
  • antonym: acceptance - the act of agreeing to or approving something
  • synonym: rejection - the dismissing or refusing of a proposal, idea, etc.
