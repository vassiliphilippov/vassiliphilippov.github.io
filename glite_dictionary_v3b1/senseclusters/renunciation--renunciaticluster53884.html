Synonyms

    abjuration is a formal, public renouncing of a belief, while renunciation is a broader formal rejection, often tied to personal change.
  • apostasy
    9.9 2.11/m
    apostasy is leaving or abandoning a religion, while renunciation is any formal rejection of a belief or way of life.
  • recantation
    9.0 0.14/m
    recantation is publicly withdrawing a specific statement or belief, while renunciation is a wider formal rejection of a belief or cause.
  • repudiation
    8.7 0.56/m

  • abnegation
    9.9 0.06/m
    abnegation is self-denying or giving up desires for moral reasons, while renunciation is a formal rejection of a belief or cause.
