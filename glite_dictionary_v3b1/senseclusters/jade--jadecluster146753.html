jade

adjective
bluish or yellowish green color
Pronunciation: / dʒeɪd / (AmE), / dʒeɪd / (BrE)
Of a color resembling jade; especially varying from bluish green to yellowish green.

Examples

Jade colored dress
She wore a jade dress that brought out the green in her eyes.
Soft jade tones painted the walls, evoking a tranquil feeling.
The room was decorated with jade accents to create a calming atmosphere.
