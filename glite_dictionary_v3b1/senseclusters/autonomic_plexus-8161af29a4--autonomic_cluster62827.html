autonomic plexus

noun
nerve network controlling involuntary functions
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˈnɑmɪk ˈplɛksəs / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈnɒmɪk ˈplɛksəs / (BrE)
a network of sympathetic and parasympathetic nerve fibers regulating involuntary bodily functions

Examples

The autonomic plexus network
Damage to an autonomic plexus can lead to serious disruptions in organ regulation.
Doctors studied the structure of the autonomic plexus to better understand nerve diseases.
The solar autonomic plexus plays a crucial role in controlling digestive functions.
