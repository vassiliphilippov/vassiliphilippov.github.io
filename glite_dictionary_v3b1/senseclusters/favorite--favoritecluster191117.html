favorite

verb
to mark as a favorite online
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪv(ə)rɪt /; / ˈfeɪvərɪt / (AmE), / ˈfeɪv(ə)rɪt /; / ˈfeɪvərɪt / (BrE)
To indicate approval or mark an item as a personal favorite, especially by clicking a designated icon on social media platforms.

Examples

To favorite a post

I often favorite tweets that I want to read again later.
She favorited my photo on the app last night.
I always favourite the posts that inspire me on this site.
Many users favourite tweets to bookmark them for future reference.
She favourited the recipe so she could find it easily later.
