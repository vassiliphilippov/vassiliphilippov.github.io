dominative

adjective
exercising authority
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːmɪˌneɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪnətɪv / (BrE)
having the quality of exercising control or authority over others

Examples

Dominative leader
Her dominative personality often made her the leader in group projects.
The dominative influence of the older sibling shaped the family's decisions.
A dominative approach can sometimes discourage teamwork and open discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dominant - having power and influence over others
  • antonym: submissive - willing to obey or yield to others
