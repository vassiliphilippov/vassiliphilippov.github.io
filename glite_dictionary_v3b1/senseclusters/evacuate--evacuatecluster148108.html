evacuate

verb
move people from danger to safety
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈvæk.juː.eɪt/ /; / /ɪ.ˈvæk.jə.eɪt/ /; / /iː.ˈvæk.juː.eɪt/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈvæk.jʊ.eɪt/ /; / /ɪ.ˈvæk.juː.eɪt/ /; / /iː.ˈvæk.jʊ.eɪt/ (BrE)
to move people or leave a place for safety, especially because of danger or an emergency

Examples

Residents were told to evacuate the city before the hurricane made landfall.
