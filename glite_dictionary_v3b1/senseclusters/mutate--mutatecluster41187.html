mutate

verb
undergo a genetic change
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːteɪt / (AmE), / ˈmjuːteɪt / (BrE)
to change or cause to change in genetic material, form, or nature, especially in living things

Examples

Scientists observed that the plant cells began to mutate after the experiment.
