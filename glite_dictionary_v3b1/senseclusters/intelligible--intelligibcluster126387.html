intelligible

adjective
clearly spoken or enunciated
3.1 3.6
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈtel.ɪ.dʒə.bəl/ /; / /ɪn.ˈtel.ə.dʒə.bəl/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈtel.ɪ.dʒə.bəl/ /; / /ɪn.ˈtel.ə.dʒə.bəl/ (BrE)
clear and easy to understand; able to be comprehended

Examples

Clear and intelligible speech
Clear and intelligible pronunciation is essential for language learners.
Despite the noise, his instructions were still intelligible to the crowd.
The lecturer's intelligible voice made it easy for everyone in the hall to follow along.
His explanation was clear and intelligible to everyone in the room.
She spoke slowly to make her words more intelligible to the audience.
The announcement was barely intelligible over the sound of the crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audible - able to be heard
  • synonym: comprehensible - able to be understood or grasped; understandable
  • antonym: incomprehensible - impossible to understand
  • antonym: unintelligible - impossible to understand; not clear
