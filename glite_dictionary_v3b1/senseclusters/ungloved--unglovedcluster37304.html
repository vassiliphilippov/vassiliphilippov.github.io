ungloved

adjective
without gloves
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡlʌvd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡlʌvd / (BrE)
not wearing gloves or having had gloves removed

Examples

Ungloved hands
The surgeon carefully washed his ungloved hands before leaving the operating room.
She reached out with her ungloved fingers to feel the texture of the fabric.
During the inspection, workers were reminded not to touch equipment with ungloved hands.
