beam

verb
emit or shine with light
3.0 0.9/m
Pronunciation: / biÀêm / (AmE), / biÀêm / (BrE)
to emit light or shine brightly, especially in a specified direction

Examples

To beam a light
A flashlight beamed across the dark room, revealing dust in the air.
Holiday lights beamed from every house on the street during the festival.
The sun beamed through the window, warming the kitchen floor.
