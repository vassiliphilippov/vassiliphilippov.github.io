evoke

verb
summon or bring into existence
1.8 6.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvoʊk / (AmE), / ɪˈvəʊk / (BrE)
to call forth, summon, or bring into existence, often as if by magic or supernatural means

Examples

To evoke a spirit
Her words seemed to evoke the spirit of the old poet in the quiet room.
Legends tell of musicians who could evoke wild animals from the forest with their melodies.
The ancient ritual was performed to evoke rain from the heavens.

  • synonym: summon - to call for the presence of, especially with authority or magic
