Synonyms

  • disease
    2.3 194/m
    disease is a specific medical problem with clear causes or signs, while condition often means a broader or chronic health issue.
  • illness
    1.1 58/m
    illness often refers to feeling unwell and symptoms people notice, while condition focuses more on a medical state, often long-term.
  • health problem
    1.4 8.15/m
    health problem is a common phrase for any physical or mental trouble that limits daily life, while condition often names a diagnosed medical state.
  • sickness
    1.0 5.38/m
