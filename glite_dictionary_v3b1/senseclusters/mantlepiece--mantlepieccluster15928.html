mantlepiece

noun
shelf above a fireplace
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmæntəlˌpis / (AmE), / ˈmæn(t)lpiːs / (BrE)
A shelf above a fireplace, usually projecting from the wall, often used for displaying ornaments or clocks.

Examples

A mantlepiece shelf
A beautiful antique clock sat proudly in the center of the mantlepiece.
During the holidays, they hung stockings along the mantlepiece for each member of the family.
She placed the family photographs carefully on the mantlepiece above the fireplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mantel - the structure framing the opening of a fireplace
