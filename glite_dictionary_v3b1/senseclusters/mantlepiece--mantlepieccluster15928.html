mantlepiece

noun
shelf above a fireplace
4.8 0.14/m

A shelf above a fireplace, usually projecting from the wall, often used for displaying ornaments or clocks.

Examples

A mantlepiece shelf
A beautiful antique clock sat proudly in the center of the mantlepiece.
During the holidays, they hung stockings along the mantlepiece for each member of the family.
She placed the family photographs carefully on the mantlepiece above the fireplace.

Synonyms

  • mantelpiece
    4.9 0.72/m
    mantelpiece is a widely used spelling for the fireplace shelf in houses and catalogs, while mantlepiece is an alternative spelling.
  • chimneypiece
    8.2 0.04/m
    chimneypiece is a slightly more formal or old-fashioned word for the decorative fireplace shelf, while mantlepiece is the everyday term.
  • mantle
    5.6 0.91/m
    mantle refers to the projecting shelf or frame and often has other meanings, while mantlepiece specifically means the fireplace shelf.
