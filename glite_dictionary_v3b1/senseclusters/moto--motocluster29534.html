moto

noun
motorcycle race heat
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəʊ / (BrE)
a single race or heat in a motorcycle competition, especially in motocross events

Examples

A motocross moto
He finished second in the first moto but won the overall event.
Riders prepared their bikes for the next moto under the hot afternoon sun.
After a tough moto, she celebrated her best result of the season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: final - the last and deciding race in a competition
  • synonym: heat - a single round or section of a race or competition
