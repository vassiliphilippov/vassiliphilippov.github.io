paternal quality

noun
qualities typical of a father
2.6
Pronunciation: / pəˈtɝːnəl ˈkwɑːləti / (AmE), / pəˈtɜːnəl ˈkwɒləti / (BrE)
the kindness, protectiveness, and other traits typical of or suitable for a father

The coach's paternal quality was evident in the way he cared for his young team.
