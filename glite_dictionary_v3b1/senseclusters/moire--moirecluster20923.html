moire

noun
silk fabric with a wavy pattern
Pronunciation: / mwɑːr / (AmE), / ˈmwɑːreɪ / (BrE)
A silk fabric that has a rippled or wavy appearance, often produced by passing the material through engraved rollers.

Examples

A moire fabric
Antique furniture is sometimes upholstered with richly colored moire fabric.
She selected a moire ribbon to add a touch of elegance to her hat.
The dress was made of shimmering moire that caught the light with every movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: taffeta - a crisp, smooth woven fabric, similar to moire
