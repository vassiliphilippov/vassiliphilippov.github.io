band

noun
strip of contrasting color or material (adornment)
Pronunciation: / bænd / (AmE), / bænd / (BrE)
a strip, line, or area of contrasting color or material, often as decoration or to form a pattern

Examples

A red band
A red band of fabric encircled the top of the hat, giving it an elegant touch.
The athlete’s uniform featured a gold band on each sleeve.
The bride wore a white dress with a blue band around the waist.
A yellow band marks the edge of the flag.
The caterpillar has a distinctive black band around its middle.
The socks are decorated with a red band near the top.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stripe - a long, narrow band differing in color or texture
