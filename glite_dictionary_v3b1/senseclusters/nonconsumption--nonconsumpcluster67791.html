nonconsumption

noun
not using or consuming
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkənˈsʌmpʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈsʌmpʃən / (BrE)
the state or act of not using, eating, or consuming something

Examples

Nonconsumption of alcohol
The nonconsumption of alcohol is encouraged in some cultures for religious reasons.
Environmental campaigns promote the nonconsumption of single-use plastics to reduce waste.
Doctors recommend the nonconsumption of certain foods for people with allergies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abstinence - the practice of not doing or consuming something, especially for health or moral reasons
  • antonym: consumption - the act of using, eating, or drinking something
