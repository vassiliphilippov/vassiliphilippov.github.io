distillment

noun
essence or summary
8.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɪlmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪlmənt / (BrE)
the purest or most essential part of something, often in a concentrated or summarized form

Examples

A distillment of ideas
The novel is a distilment of the author's personal experiences and observations.
His report was a careful distilment of the most relevant facts and figures.
The documentary offers a distilment of decades of scientific research into a single hour.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detail - an individual fact or item
  • synonym: essence - the most important or central part of something
  • synonym: summary - a brief statement of the main points
