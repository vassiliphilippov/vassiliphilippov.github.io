bawler

noun
person who shouts loudly
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔlɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlə / (BrE)
a person who shouts or speaks in a loud, harsh voice

Examples

A loud bawler
Don't be such a bawler; we're inside a library.
Even as a child, he was a notorious bawler at family gatherings.
The bawler at the back of the crowd was disturbing everyone with his constant yelling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shouter - someone who speaks or calls out loudly
  • antonym: whisperer - someone who speaks in a very soft or low voice
