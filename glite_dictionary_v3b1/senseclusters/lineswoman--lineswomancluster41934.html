lineswoman

noun
female sports official assisting referee
3.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪnz.wʊm.ən/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪnz.wʊm.ən/ (BrE)
a female official in sports such as tennis, soccer, or football, who assists the referee by making decisions about the boundaries of the playing area or offside.
