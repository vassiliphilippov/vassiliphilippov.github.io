bimah

noun
synagogue platform
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːmə / (AmE), / ˈbiːmə / (BrE)
a raised platform in a synagogue from which the Torah is read and services are conducted or led

Examples

The bimah platform
The Torah was carried to the bimah for the reading during the service.
Children watched as the cantor led prayers from the bimah in the center of the synagogue.
Visitors admired the ornate woodwork of the bimah during the synagogue tour.
The rabbi stood on the bimah to read from the Torah during the service.
Children gathered near the bimah to watch the ceremony in the synagogue.
The ornate woodwork of the bimah was a highlight of the historic building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pulpit - a raised platform in a church from which sermons are delivered
