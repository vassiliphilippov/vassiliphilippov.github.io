corn oil

noun
vegetable oil made from corn
1.5
Pronunciation: / kɔrn ɔɪl / (AmE), / kɔːn ɔɪl / (BrE)
a pale yellow vegetable oil obtained from the germ of corn kernels, used in cooking and food products

Examples

Corn oil for cooking
Manufacturers often choose corn oil for making margarine and salad dressings.
The chef prefers using corn oil for frying because of its high smoke point.
You can substitute corn oil for butter in many baking recipes for a lighter texture.
