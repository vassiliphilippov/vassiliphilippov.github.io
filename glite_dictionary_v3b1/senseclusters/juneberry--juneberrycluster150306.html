juneberry

noun
edible berry from Amelanchier plants
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːnˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːnˌbɛri / (BrE)
A small edible purple or red berry, produced by various species of the genus Amelanchier, commonly found in North America.

Examples

A juneberry fruit
Birds flock to the shrubs each summer to feast on the ripe juneberries.
She baked a pie using fresh juneberries picked from her garden.
The children climbed the hill to gather juneberries for their breakfast.
