filtrate

noun
liquid or gas passed through a filter
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪl.treɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪl.treɪt / (BrE)
a liquid or gas that has passed through a filter, especially as the product of a filtration process

Examples

A clear filtrate
After the solution was poured through the paper, the clear filtrate was collected in a beaker.
In wastewater treatment, the filtrate is often further purified before release.
The filtrate must be examined to ensure all impurities are removed.
