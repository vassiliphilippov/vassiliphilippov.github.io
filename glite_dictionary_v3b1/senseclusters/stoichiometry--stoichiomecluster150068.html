stoichiometry

noun
quantitative relationships in chemical reactions
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌstoʊɪkiˈɑmətri / (AmE), / ˌstɔɪkiˈɒmɪtri / (BrE)
the calculation of the relative quantities of reactants and products involved in a chemical reaction.
