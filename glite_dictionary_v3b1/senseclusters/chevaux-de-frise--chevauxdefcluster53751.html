chevaux-de-frise

noun
movable defensive barrier with spikes
Pronunciation: / ʃəˌvoʊ də ˈfriz / (AmE), / ʃəˌvəʊ də ˈfriːz / (BrE)
a defensive barrier consisting of a movable wooden frame studded with spikes or barbed wire, used to obstruct cavalry or enemy troops
