Examples

Short-handed team

They found themselves short-handed during the busy holiday season and had to hire temporary staff.

Synonyms

  • short-staffed
    3.9 0.19/m
    short-staffed describes an everyday workplace with too few workers for the tasks, while short-handed names that staff shortage.
  • understaffed
    3.4 0.53/m
    understaffed sounds slightly more formal and often appears in reports about a business, while short-handed is more neutral everyday speech.
  • undermanned
    5.6 0.08/m
    undermanned feels old-fashioned or military and may seem gendered to some, while short-handed is neutral in general use.
