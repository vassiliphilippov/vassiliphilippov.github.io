sales blitzkrieg

noun
sudden, intense sales campaign
Pronunciation: / seɪlz ˈblɪtskɹiːɡ / (AmE), / seɪlz ˈblɪtskriːɡ / (BrE)
a sudden and intense sales campaign designed to overwhelm competitors or quickly gain market share

Examples

A sales blitzkrieg campaign
After the sales blitzkrieg, the company's brand recognition soared.
The company's sales blitzkrieg last quarter resulted in a record number of new customers.
We weren't prepared for the sales blitzkrieg launched by the new competitor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marketing offensive - a coordinated campaign to promote products
  • synonym: sales push - an aggressive attempt to sell products
