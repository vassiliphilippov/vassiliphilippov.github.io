teacher

noun
person whose job is teaching
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːtʃər / (AmE), / ˈtiːtʃə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to teach, especially in a school or college.

Examples

A school teacher
He worked as a math teacher for over twenty years.
Her favorite teacher inspired her to become a scientist.
The teacher explained the lesson clearly to the class.

Related Terms

  • antonym: student - a person who is studying, especially formally at a school or university
