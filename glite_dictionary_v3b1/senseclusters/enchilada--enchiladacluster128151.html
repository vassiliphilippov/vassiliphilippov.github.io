enchilada

noun
Mexican tortilla dish with filling and sauce
7.2 3.2
Pronunciation: /e.ntʃɪ.ˈlɑː.də/ /; / /e.ntʃə.ˈlɑː.də/ (AmE), /e.ntʃɪ.ˈlɑː.də/ /; / /e.ntʃə.ˈlɑː.də/ (BrE)
A Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla rolled around a filling, typically of meat, and baked in a chili-seasoned tomato sauce.

Examples

A enchilada dish

She made a vegetarian enchilada using beans and roasted vegetables.
The recipe calls for chicken and cheese as the filling for the enchilada.
