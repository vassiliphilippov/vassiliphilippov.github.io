defaulter

noun
someone who does not appear in court as required
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɔltər / (AmE), / dɪˈfɔːltə / (BrE)
a person who fails to appear in court as required by law

Examples

Court defaulter
Authorities are actively seeking the defaulter after their unexplained absence from court.
Repeatedly being a defaulter may lead to additional legal penalties.
The court issued a warrant for the defaulter who failed to appear at the hearing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absconder - person who evades justice or leaves unlawfully to avoid prosecution
