seiche

noun
oscillating wave in a lake or bay
8.0 9.0
Pronunciation: /seɪʃ/ /; / /siːtʃ/ (AmE), /seɪʃ/ (BrE)
A wave oscillation on the surface of an enclosed or partially enclosed body of water, such as a lake or bay, caused by atmospheric or seismic disturbances.
