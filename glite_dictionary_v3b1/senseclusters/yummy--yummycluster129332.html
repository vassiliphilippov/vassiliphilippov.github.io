yummy

adjective
very tasty; delicious
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈjʌmi / (AmE), / ˈjʌmi / (BrE)

Examples

Yummy cake

The chocolate cake was so yummy that everyone asked for seconds.
Those strawberries look really yummy—I can't wait to try them.
