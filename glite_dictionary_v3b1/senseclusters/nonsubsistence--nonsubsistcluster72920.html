nonsubsistence

adjective
not for basic needs
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.səbˈsɪstəns / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.səbˈsɪstəns / (BrE)

Examples

nonsubsistence benefits
The government offers nonsubsistence benefits such as educational grants and childcare support.
Many nonsubsistence activities, like sports and entertainment, improve quality of life.
The charity focuses on both subsistence and nonsubsistence needs in the community.
