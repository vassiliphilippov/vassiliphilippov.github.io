Synonyms

  • exchange
    2.1 20/m
    exchange is giving and receiving items or information between people, while interchange focuses on sharing or swapping things between groups.
  • swap
    2.6 4/m
    swap is trading one thing directly for another, often of equal value, while interchange can mean broader sharing of items or information.
  • reciprocation
    6.3 0.03/m
    reciprocation is doing something in return as a social response, while interchange describes exchanging or sharing items, money, or information.
