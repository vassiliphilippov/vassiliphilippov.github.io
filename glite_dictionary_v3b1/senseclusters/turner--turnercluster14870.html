turner

noun
lathe operator (craftsperson)
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrnər / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to shape wood, metal, or other materials on a lathe

Examples

Wood turner
A skilled turner can produce intricate patterns on metal or wood using a lathe.
He apprenticed as a turner and quickly mastered working with precision tools.
The turner shaped the wooden legs for the new table with great skill.
