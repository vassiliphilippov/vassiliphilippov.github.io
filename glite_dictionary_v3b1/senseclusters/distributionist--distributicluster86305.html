distributionist

noun
supporter of distributionism
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstrɪbjuːʃənɪst / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɪbjʊʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates the economic theory of distributionism, which promotes widespread ownership of property and means of production

Examples

A distributionist thinker
The distributionist movement gained popularity in the early twentieth century.
As a distributionist, she believed that property should be owned by as many people as possible.
The debate featured a distributionist and a capitalist discussing economic fairness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: capitalist - a person who supports capitalism
  • synonym: distributist - a person who supports distributionism
  • antonym: socialist - a person who supports socialism
