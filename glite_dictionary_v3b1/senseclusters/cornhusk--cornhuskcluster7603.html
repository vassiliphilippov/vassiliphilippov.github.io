cornhusk

noun
outer covering of an ear of corn
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnˌhʌsk / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌhʌsk / (BrE)
the outer leafy covering that surrounds an ear of corn

Examples

A cornhusk doll
A cornhusk doll is a traditional Native American craft.
After shucking the corn, she composted the cornhusk.
The tamales were wrapped in cornhusk before being steamed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: husk - the dry outer covering of some fruits or seeds
