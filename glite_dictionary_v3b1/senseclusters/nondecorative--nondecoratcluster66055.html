nondecorative

adjective
not for decoration
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndɛˈkɔrətɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˈkɒrətɪv / (BrE)
not intended or used for decoration; serving only a practical or functional purpose

Examples

Nondecorative materials
The architect chose nondecorative materials to emphasize the building's industrial style.
These nondecorative shelves are meant only for storage, not for display.
He preferred a nondecorative approach, focusing on function over appearance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decorative - intended to make something look more attractive
  • synonym: functional - designed to be practical and useful rather than decorative
