paring knife

noun
small knife for peeling or trimming
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪrɪŋ naɪf / (AmE), / ˈpeərɪŋ naɪf / (BrE)
a small, sharp knife used for peeling or trimming fruits and vegetables

Examples

A sharp paring knife
A sharp paring knife makes it easy to remove the skin from potatoes.
She used a paring knife to peel the apples for the pie.
The recipe suggests dicing onions with a paring knife for better control.
