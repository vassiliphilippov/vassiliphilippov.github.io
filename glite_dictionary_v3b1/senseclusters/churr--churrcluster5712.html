churr

verb
make a low, vibrating sound (birds/insects)
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃɜr / (AmE), / tʃɜː / (BrE)
(of some birds or insects) to make a low, vibrant, trilling sound.

Examples

To churr softly
At dawn, the nightjars began to churr softly in the bushes.
The insect churrs incessantly throughout the warm summer evenings.
We paused on the path, listening as a distant bird churred in the shadows.
