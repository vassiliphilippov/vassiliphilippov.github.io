Synonyms

  • excess
    3.3 13/m
    excess is a simple, common word for having too much of something, while overabundance sounds more formal and emphatic.
  • superfluity
    8.7 0.12/m
    superfluity sounds formal and literary, while overabundance is more neutral and refers plainly to an excessive quantity.
  • superabundance
    7.9 0.16/m
    superabundance suggests a very large or overflowing amount, while overabundance means having more than is needed.
  • surfeit
    8.8 0.63/m
    surfeit often implies overindulgence or discomfort from too much, while overabundance simply means an excessive amount.
  • plethora
    7.7 3.30/m
    plethora feels slightly dramatic and shows many or unmanageable amounts, while overabundance is a plain term for excess.
  • surplus
    3.4 0.13/m
    surplus often names extra goods or stock that remain after use, while overabundance stresses having more than wanted.
