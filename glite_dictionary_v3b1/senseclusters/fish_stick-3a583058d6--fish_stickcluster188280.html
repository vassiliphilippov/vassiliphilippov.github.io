fish stick

noun
breaded and fried piece of fish
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ stɪk / (AmE), / fɪʃ stɪk / (BrE)
a long, thin piece of fish, usually covered in breadcrumbs or batter and fried or baked

Examples

Fried fish sticks
He didn't like seafood, but he would always make an exception for fish sticks.
Many children love eating fish sticks with ketchup for dinner.
She placed a tray of frozen fish sticks in the oven for a quick meal.
