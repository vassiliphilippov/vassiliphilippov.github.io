work stoppage

noun
employee protest by refusing to work
1.6 4.2
Pronunciation: / wɝːk ˈstɑːp.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / wɜːk ˈstɒp.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
an occasion when a group of employees refuse to work as a form of protest, often about pay or working conditions

After days of negotiation, the work stoppage finally came to an end.
The union called a work stoppage to demand better safety measures at the factory.
