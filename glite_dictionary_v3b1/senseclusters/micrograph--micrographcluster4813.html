micrograph

noun
photograph taken through a microscope
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪ.krəˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈmʌɪ.krəˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
a photograph or other image produced by a microscope to show a magnified view of a small object or organism

Examples

A micrograph image
Each micrograph in the report was labeled with the magnification and scale bar.
The scientist examined the micrograph to identify the structure of the cell.
This stunning micrograph highlights the intricate patterns on a butterfly's wing.
