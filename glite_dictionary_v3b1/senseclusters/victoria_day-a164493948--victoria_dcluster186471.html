Victoria Day

noun
Canadian public holiday in May
3.1
Pronunciation: / vɪkˈtɔɹiə deɪ / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːriə deɪ / (BrE)
A public holiday in Canada celebrated on the Monday on or before May 24th in honor of Queen Victoria's birthday.

Examples

Victoria Day holiday
Victoria Day marks the unofficial start of the summer season in Canada.
Many Canadians celebrate Victoria Day with fireworks and outdoor activities.
The stores will be closed on Victoria Day for the public holiday.
