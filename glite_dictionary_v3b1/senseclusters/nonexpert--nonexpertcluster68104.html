nonexpert

noun
person without expertise
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈɛkspɜrt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈekspɜːt / (BrE)
a person who does not have special knowledge or skill in a particular area

Examples

A nonexpert reader
The instructions were written so that a nonexpert could understand them.
As a nonexpert, she found the technical report difficult to follow.
The museum guide explained the exhibit in terms that any nonexpert could grasp.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amateur - a person who engages in an activity without professional skill
  • antonym: expert - a person with special knowledge or skill in a particular area
  • synonym: layperson - someone who is not an expert or professional in a field
