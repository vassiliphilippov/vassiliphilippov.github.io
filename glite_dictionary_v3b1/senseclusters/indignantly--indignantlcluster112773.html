indignantly

adverb
in an indignant manner; angrily due to injustice
5.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɪɡnəntli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪɡnəntli / (BrE)
In a manner showing anger or annoyance at something perceived as unfair or insulting.

Examples

To indignantly deny
He indignantly denied any involvement in the scandal.
She spoke indignantly when the accusation was made.
The teacher listened indignantly to the unfair criticism from the parents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angrily - in an angry manner
  • antonym: calmly - in a calm manner, without anger
