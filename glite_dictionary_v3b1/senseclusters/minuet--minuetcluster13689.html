minuet

noun
stately dance/music in triple time
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪn.juˈɛt /; / ˌmɪnjuˈɛt / (AmE), / ˌmɪn.juˈɛt /; / ˌmɪnjuˈɛt / (BrE)
a slow, elegant dance in triple time from the 17th and 18th centuries, or the music written for this dance
