Examples

Ultraviolet radiation from sun

Synonyms

  • ultraviolet light
    4.0 1.12/m
    ultraviolet light is the common everyday term people use for invisible sun rays, while ultraviolet radiation sounds more scientific.
  • ultraviolet
    5.1 1.55/m
    ultraviolet often appears as an adjective or short noun in speech, while ultraviolet radiation names the physical type of rays.
  • UV
    4.2 0.03/m
    UV is the short, common label seen on sunscreen and forecasts, while ultraviolet radiation is the full scientific term.
  • ultraviolet illumination
    6.5 0.01/m
    ultraviolet illumination refers to using UV for lighting effects or safety checks, while ultraviolet radiation is the general physical radiation.
