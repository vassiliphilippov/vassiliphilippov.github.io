housecleaner

noun
person who cleans homes
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌkliːnər / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌkliːnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to clean houses, usually for payment

Examples

The housecleaner cleans
The housecleaner arrives every Friday to tidy up the entire apartment.
After the party, we decided to hire a housecleaner to help restore order.
A reliable housecleaner can make a big difference in a busy household.
