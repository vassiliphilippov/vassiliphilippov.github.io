tiepin

noun
pin for fastening a necktie
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈtaɪˌpɪn / (BrE)
A decorative pin used to fasten a necktie to a shirt, keeping the tie in place.

Examples

A tiepin fastener
He wore a silver tiepin to keep his tie neatly in place during the meeting.
My grandfather gave me his vintage tiepin as a gift on my birthday.
The gold tiepin added a touch of elegance to his suit.

Related Terms
