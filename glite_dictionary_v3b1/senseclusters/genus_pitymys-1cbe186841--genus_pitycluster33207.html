genus Pitymys

noun
former genus of pine mice
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈpɪtɪˌmɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈpɪtɪˌmɪs / (BrE)
A formerly recognized genus of small rodents known as pine mice, now usually classified under the genus Microtus.
