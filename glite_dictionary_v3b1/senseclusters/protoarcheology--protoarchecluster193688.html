protoarcheology

noun
study of prehistoric artifacts and fossils
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtoʊˌɑrkiˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəʊˌɑːkiˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of prehistoric human artifacts and fossils, typically focusing on periods before the advent of written records

Examples

Protoarcheology study
