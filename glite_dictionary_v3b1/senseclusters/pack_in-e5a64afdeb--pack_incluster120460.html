pack in

verb
fit many things into a short time
Pronunciation: / pæk ɪn / (AmE), / pæk ɪn / (BrE)
to include or arrange a large number of activities or events within a short period of time

Examples

To pack in activities
Despite their busy schedule, they managed to pack in several museum visits before lunch.
I always try to pack in as much sightseeing as possible when I travel.
She packed in three appointments this afternoon, leaving hardly any free time.
