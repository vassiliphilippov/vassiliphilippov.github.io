unquantifiable

adjective
cannot be measured
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌkwɑːn.təˈfaɪ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌkwɒn.tɪˈfaɪ.ə.bəl / (BrE)

Examples

An unquantifiable amount
The impact of her kindness was truly unquantifiable in the lives of those she helped.
Some aspects of happiness are unquantifiable and cannot be captured by statistics.
The artist's influence on the community is unquantifiable and goes beyond numbers.
