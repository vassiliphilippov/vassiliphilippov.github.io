bigamist

noun
person who commits bigamy
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪɡəmɪst / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡəmɪst / (BrE)
a person who marries someone while already legally married to another person

Examples

A bigamist marries twice
A bigamist can face legal consequences in most countries.
She was shocked to learn her husband was a bigamist with another family in another city.
The court found him to be a bigamist after discovering his secret second marriage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bigamy - the act of marrying someone while already married to another person
