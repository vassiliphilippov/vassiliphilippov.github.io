plot

noun
small marked area of land for growing plants
1.9 8.69/m
Pronunciation: / plÉ‘t / (AmE), / plÉ’t / (BrE)
a small piece of land, often marked off, used for a particular purpose such as growing specific plants or vegetables

Examples

A garden plot
Each student was assigned a plot in the community garden.
He cultivated a small plot of land behind his house to grow tomatoes.
The weeds had taken over the abandoned vegetable plot.

Synonyms

  • plot of ground
    2.3 0.08/m
    plot of ground is a small marked area used for growing plants, while plot is the short, general word for that area.
  • plot of land
    3.0 0.97/m
    plot of land is a small area of ground used for a particular purpose like gardening, while plot is the general term.
  • piece of ground
    2.8 0.17/m
    piece of ground is any portion of land with clear boundaries, while plot usually implies a small area for growing plants.
  • allotment
    3.7 0.46/m
    allotment is a rented small garden plot often in community sites, while plot is a neutral term for any small garden area.
  • patch
    2.7 16/m
    patch is a small, irregular area of plants or color, while plot is a planned small piece used for growing crops.

Related Terms
