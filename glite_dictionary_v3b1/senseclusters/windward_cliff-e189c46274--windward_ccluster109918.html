windward cliff

noun
cliff facing the wind
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndˌwɝd klɪf / (AmE), / ˈwɪndwəd klɪf / (BrE)
a cliff that faces toward the direction from which the wind is blowing

Examples

Windward cliff face
Gulls circled above the windward cliff, searching for a place to nest.
The lighthouse was built atop the windward cliff, safeguarding ships from the hidden rocks below.
Vegetation is sparse on the windward cliff due to constant exposure to strong sea breezes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leeward cliff - a cliff sheltered from the wind, opposite of windward
