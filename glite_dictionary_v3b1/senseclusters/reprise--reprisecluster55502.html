Synonyms

  • repeat
    1.0 104/m
    repeat means doing or saying something again, while reprise usually refers to performing a song, role, or musical section again.
  • recapitulate
    8.6 0.08/m
    recapitulate restates a main theme in music or writing, while reprise means to perform or return to a musical section.
  • replay
    2.1 1.76/m
    replay plays a recording or broadcast again, while reprise usually means performing again live or returning to a musical part.
