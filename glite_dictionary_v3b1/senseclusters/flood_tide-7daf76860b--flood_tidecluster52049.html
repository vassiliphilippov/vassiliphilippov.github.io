flood tide

noun
peak or highest point of development
8.5
Pronunciation: / flʌd taɪd / (AmE), / flʌd tʌɪd / (BrE)
The highest or most successful point of something as it develops or unfolds.

Examples

Flood tide of success
At the flood tide of public interest, the campaign was more successful than ever.
She felt she was riding the flood tide of her career after the award.
The company reached the flood tide of its prosperity before the market crash.

Related Terms

  • synonym: climax - the most intense, exciting, or important point of something
  • synonym: culmination - the highest or climactic point of something
  • antonym: low tide - the lowest or least successful point of something
