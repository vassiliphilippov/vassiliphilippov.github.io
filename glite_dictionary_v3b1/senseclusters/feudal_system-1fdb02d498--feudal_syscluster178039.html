feudal system

noun
social structure of medieval Europe under feudalism
7.8 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàfjuÀêd…ôl Àås…™st…ôm / (AmE), / ÀàfjuÀêd…ôl Àås…™st…ôm / (BrE)

Examples
