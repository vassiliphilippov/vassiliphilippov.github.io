rebound relationship

noun
romantic relationship after a breakup
5.5 5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈriˌbaʊnd ˌrileɪˈʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈriːˌbaʊnd ˌriːleɪˈʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
a romantic relationship that someone enters soon after ending a previous one, often as a way to cope with emotional distress

Examples
