ramon

noun
edible tree seed
Pronunciation: / rəˈmoʊn / (AmE), / rəˈməʊn / (BrE)
the edible seed or fruit of the ramon tree, often used as food after roasting or grinding into flour

Examples

Ramon seeds for food
The villagers collected ramon to grind into flour for baking bread.
After roasting, ramon seeds can be eaten as a nutritious snack.
Some traditional dishes use ramon as a main ingredient instead of wheat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acorn - the nut of the oak tree, used as food in some cultures
  • synonym: nut - an edible seed, usually encased in a hard shell
