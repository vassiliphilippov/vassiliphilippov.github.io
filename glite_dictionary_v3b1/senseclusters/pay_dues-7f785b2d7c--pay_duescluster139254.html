pay dues

verb
endure hardships before success
4.4
Pronunciation: / peɪ 'duz / (AmE), / peɪ 'djuːz / (BrE)
to endure hardships or make necessary sacrifices, especially as a prerequisite for achieving success or recognition

Examples

She knew that to succeed in the industry, she would have to pay dues.
