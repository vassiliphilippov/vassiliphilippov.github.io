intently

adverb
with focused attention
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛntli / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛntli / (BrE)
with focused and concentrated attention

Examples

Look intently at screen
He stared intently at the computer screen, searching for an error.
She listened intently to every word of the announcement.
The children watched intently as the magician performed his tricks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: absentmindedly - in a way that shows lack of attention
  • synonym: attentively - in a way that shows attention or focus
