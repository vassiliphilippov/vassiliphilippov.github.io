Tussilago farfara

noun
a perennial herb known as coltsfoot
9.9
Pronunciation: /tʌs.ɪ.ˈleɪ.goʊ ˈfɑːr.fə.rə/ /; / /tʊs.si.ˈlɑː.goʊ fɑr.ˈfɑː.rə/ (AmE), /tʌs.ɪ.ˈleɪ.ɡəʊ ˈfɑː.fə.rə/ /; / /tʊs.si.ˈlɑː.ɡəʊ fɑː.ˈfɑː.rə/ (BrE)
A perennial herb, commonly known as coltsfoot, with large rounded leaves and yellow flowers that appear before the leaves; native to Europe but now widespread; historically used in herbal medicine.

Examples

Tussilago farfara plant
Tussilago farfara has been traditionally used to treat coughs and respiratory issues.
Many botanical gardens grow Tussilago farfara for its early yellow flowers.
You can often find Tussilago farfara growing along riverbanks in early spring.
