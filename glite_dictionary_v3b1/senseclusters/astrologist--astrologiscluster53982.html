astrologist

noun
person who interprets planetary positions
3.3 6.2
Pronunciation: / əˈstrɑː.lə.dʒɪst / (AmE), / əˈstrɒl.ə.dʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who claims to predict the future or interpret events by studying the positions and movements of the sun, moon, and planets

Examples

The astrologist reads stars

Many people consult an astrologist to learn about their romantic prospects and career paths.
The astrologist predicted that a significant change would occur in May based on the alignment of Jupiter and Mars.
