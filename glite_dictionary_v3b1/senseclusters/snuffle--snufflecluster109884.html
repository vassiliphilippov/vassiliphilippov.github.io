Examples

To snuffle with a cold
Allergy season makes her snuffle constantly at her desk.
He began to snuffle as the cold took hold of him.
The child snuffled noisily while looking for a tissue.
He snuffled through his words, tears running down his cheeks.
She couldn't help but snuffle as she explained what had upset her.
The child began to snuffle quietly after being told off.

Synonyms

  • sniffle
    2.3 1.14/m
    sniffle is breathing repeatedly and quietly through the nose because of a cold or tears, while snuffle sounds heavier and congested.
  • snivel
    8.1 0.01/m
    snivel is inhaling mucus up the nose quietly or sounding like a soft complaint, while snuffle is noisy breathing from a blocked nose.

Related Terms
