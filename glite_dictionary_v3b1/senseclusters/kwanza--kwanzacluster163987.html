kwanza

noun
the currency of Angola
Pronunciation: / kwɑn.zə / (AmE), / kwan.zə / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Angola, equal to 100 cêntimos

Examples

The kwanza currency
He exchanged his dollars for kwanza before traveling to Angola.
Most transactions in Angola are conducted using the kwanza as the official currency.
The price of the meal was 2,500 kwanzas at the local restaurant.
