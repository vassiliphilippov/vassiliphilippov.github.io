time vampire

noun
thing that wastes a lot of time
6.2
Pronunciation: / taɪm ˈvæmˌpaɪr / (AmE), / taɪm ˈvæm.paɪə / (BrE)
something that consumes a lot of time, often unnecessarily or without purpose

Examples

A time vampire at work
Email can be a real time vampire if you check it constantly throughout the day.
That reality TV show became a time vampire for him during the holidays.
Unplanned meetings often prove to be the biggest time vampires in the office.
