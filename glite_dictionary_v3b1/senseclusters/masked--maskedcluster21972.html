masked

adjective
concealed or disguised (intent or nature)
Pronunciation: / mæst / (AmE), / mɑːskt / (BrE)
having its true nature or intention concealed, especially to mislead or deceive

Examples

Masked intentions
His true feelings were masked by a calm and friendly demeanor.
The CEO issued a masked threat to the employees about consequences for poor performance.
The offer seemed generous, but there was a masked intention behind it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barefaced - not disguised or concealed; open
  • synonym: disguised - having concealed or altered appearance or character
