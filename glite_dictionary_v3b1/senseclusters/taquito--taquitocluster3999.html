taquito

noun
fried rolled tortilla
Pronunciation: / tɑːˈkitoʊ / (AmE), / tɑːˈkiːtəʊ / (BrE)
a small rolled tortilla filled with meat or cheese and fried until crisp, often served as a snack or appetizer

Examples

A plate of taquitos
We ordered a plate of taquitos to share as an appetizer at the restaurant.
She made homemade taquitos filled with shredded chicken and cheese for the party.
Crispy taquitos are often served with salsa, guacamole, or sour cream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flauta - a similar Mexican dish of a rolled, fried tortilla
  • synonym: taco - a Mexican dish of a folded or rolled tortilla with a filling
