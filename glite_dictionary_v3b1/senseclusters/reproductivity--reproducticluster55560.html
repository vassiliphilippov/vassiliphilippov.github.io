reproductivity

noun
ability to be repeated
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriːprəˌdʌkˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌriːprəˌdʌkˈtɪvəti / (BrE)
the quality of producing the same results when an experiment or procedure is repeated under the same conditions

Examples

Reproductivity of results
The reproductivity of the experiment was questioned after several teams failed to get the same results.
High reproductivity is essential for scientific findings to be considered reliable.
The researchers improved the reproductivity of their method by standardizing the testing conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repeatability - the ability to achieve the same results in repeated tests
  • synonym: reproducibility - the ability to obtain consistent results when repeating an experiment
