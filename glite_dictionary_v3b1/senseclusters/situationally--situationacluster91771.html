situationally

adverb
depending on context
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪtʃuˈeɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌsɪtjʊˈeɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that depends on or is determined by the specific situation or context

Examples

Applied situationally
The rules are applied situationally rather than being the same for everyone.
He responded situationally, adjusting his approach based on the team's needs.
Decisions are often made situationally when there is no clear policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contextually - in a way that relates to the context
  • antonym: universally - in a way that applies to all situations
