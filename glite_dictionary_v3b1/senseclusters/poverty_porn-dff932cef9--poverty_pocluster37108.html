poverty porn

noun
sensationalized exploitation of poverty in media
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑvɚti pɔrn / (AmE), / ˈpɒvəti pɔːn / (BrE)
media, images, or narratives that depict poverty in a sensationalized or exploitative way, often to provoke sympathy or shock.
