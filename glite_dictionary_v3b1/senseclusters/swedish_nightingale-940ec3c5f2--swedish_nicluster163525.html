Swedish Nightingale

proper noun
nickname of Swedish soprano Jenny Lind
Pronunciation: / ˈswiː.dɪʃ ˈnaɪtɡeɪl / (AmE), / ˈswiː.dɪʃ ˈnaɪtɡeɪl / (BrE)
a nickname for Jenny Lind (1820–1887), a Swedish operatic soprano renowned for her purity of voice, who toured the United States in the 19th century

Examples

Swedish Nightingale Jenny Lind
Jenny Lind, known as the Swedish Nightingale, captivated audiences across Europe and America.
Many biographies detail how the Swedish Nightingale became an international sensation in the 1800s.
The Swedish Nightingale performed to sold-out crowds during her U.S. tour managed by P. T. Barnum.
