Lamaze

noun
childbirth preparation method
Pronunciation: / ləˈmɑz / (AmE), / ləˈmɑːz / (BrE)
a method of childbirth preparation that emphasizes breathing and relaxation techniques to reduce pain during labor

Examples

Lamaze class
Many expectant parents enroll in Lamaze classes to learn techniques for managing labor pain.
The Lamaze method teaches controlled breathing and relaxation to help women during childbirth.
She credited her calm delivery to the Lamaze exercises she practiced before giving birth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: epidural - an injection to reduce pain during childbirth
  • synonym: natural childbirth - childbirth with minimal medical intervention
