polar zone

noun
region near Earth's poles with frigid climate
2.0 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpoʊ.lər zoʊn/ (AmE), /ˈpəʊ.lə zəʊn/ (BrE)
the region around the Earth's North or South Pole, characterized by extremely cold climate and icy conditions

Examples

The polar zone region
Few people inhabit the harsh conditions found within the polar zone of the Northern Hemisphere.
Penguins are typically found in the southern polar zone, where temperatures can drop extremely low.
Scientists conduct research in the polar zone to study climate change and its effects on the environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arctic - the region around the North Pole; very cold northern area
  • antonym: temperate zone - region between the polar and tropical zones; moderate climate
