bemusement

noun
state of confusion or puzzlement
Pronunciation: / bɪˈmjuzmənt / (AmE), / bɪˈmjuːzmənt / (BrE)
a state of confusion or puzzlement resulting from an inability to understand something

Examples

An expression of bemusement
His face wore an expression of bemusement as he tried to solve the puzzle.
She looked at the complicated instructions with obvious bemusement.
The students' bemusement was clear when the professor introduced the new theory.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and easy to understand
  • synonym: confusion - lack of understanding or clarity
