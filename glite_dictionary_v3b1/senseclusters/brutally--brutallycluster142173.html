brutally

adverb
in a cruel or violent way
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːtəli / (AmE), / ˈbruːtəli / (BrE)
In a cruel, harsh, or violent manner.

Examples

To be brutally honest
She was brutally honest about the project's chances of success.
The animal was brutally attacked by a predator.
The protesters were brutally dispersed by the security forces.
