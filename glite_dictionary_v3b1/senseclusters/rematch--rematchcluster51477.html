rematch

noun
a game or contest played again
Pronunciation: / ˈriˌmætʃ / (AmE), / ˈriːˌmatʃ / (BrE)
A second or subsequent contest or game played between the same opponents, usually to decide a winner after an earlier meeting.

Examples

A big rematch
Fans are already excited about the rematch between the rival teams next season.
She trained hard for the rematch, determined to win this time.
The two boxers agreed to a rematch after their first fight ended in a draw.

  • synonym: match - a contest or game between opponents
