lemon extract

noun
concentrated lemon flavoring
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛmən ɪkˌstrækt / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən ɪkˌstrakt / (BrE)
a concentrated flavoring made from lemons, used to add lemon flavor to foods and drinks

Examples

A teaspoon of lemon extract
Add a teaspoon of lemon extract to the batter for a fresh, citrusy flavor.
Bakers often keep lemon extract on hand to quickly enhance desserts without needing fresh lemons.
She used lemon extract instead of lemon zest in her cake recipe.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: lemon juice - juice pressed from lemons
  • synonym: vanilla extract - a concentrated flavoring extracted from vanilla beans
