wallboard

noun
a board for covering interior walls
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔlˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈwɔːlˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a large, flat board made from plaster, wood pulp, or other materials, used to cover interior walls and partitions in buildings

Examples

A wallboard panel
She painted the wallboard a pale blue to brighten up the living room.
The contractor installed wallboard throughout the new house to create smooth interior surfaces.
Water damage can cause wallboard to deteriorate quickly if not repaired promptly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drywall - a panel made of gypsum plaster pressed between thick sheets of paper, used to cover walls and ceilings
