Synonyms

  • drywall
    2.9 2/m
    drywall is the everyday US name for gypsum panels used on interior walls and ceilings, while wallboard is a broader covering board term.
  • plasterboard
    3.4 0.06/m
    plasterboard is a rigid gypsum panel used to make inner walls and ceilings in buildings, while wallboard covers various interior board types.
  • gypsum board
    3.5 0.04/m
    gypsum board is the technical name for panels with a gypsum core and paper faces, while wallboard can mean many interior board materials.
  • Sheetrock
    3.5 0.5/m
