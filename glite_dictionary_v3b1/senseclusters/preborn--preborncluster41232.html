preborn

adjective
before birth
Pronunciation: / ˈpriˌbɔrn / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌbɔːn / (BrE)
existing before birth or relating to an unborn baby or fetus

Examples

Preborn baby care
The new policy aims to improve healthcare for preborn children and their mothers.
Doctors monitor the development of the preborn baby during regular checkups.
Some laws are designed to protect the rights of preborn individuals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postnatal - relating to the period after birth
  • synonym: unborn - not yet born; existing before birth
