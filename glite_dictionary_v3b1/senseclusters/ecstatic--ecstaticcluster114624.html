ecstatic

adjective
overwhelmingly happy or excited
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstætɪk / (AmE), / ɪkˈstætɪk / (BrE)
feeling or expressing overwhelming happiness or joyful excitement.

Examples

Feeling ecstatic joy
He felt ecstatic after winning the championship match.
She was ecstatic when she heard the news of her promotion.
The crowd erupted into ecstatic cheers as the band took the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delighted - feeling or showing great pleasure
  • synonym: elated - marked by high spirits or excitement
  • synonym: euphoric - experiencing intense excitement and happiness
  • antonym: miserable - feeling extremely unhappy or uncomfortable
