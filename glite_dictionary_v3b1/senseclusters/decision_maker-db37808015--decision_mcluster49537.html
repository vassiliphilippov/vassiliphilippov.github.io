decision maker

noun
person with authority to make decisions
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɪʒən ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / dɪˈsɪʒ.ən ˌmeɪ.kə / (BrE)
a person who has the authority to make important decisions, especially in a business or organization

Examples

Company decision maker
As a decision maker, she is responsible for approving large financial investments.
Important changes require the approval of a decision maker within the organization.
The decision maker in the company approved the new marketing strategy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: executive - a person with senior managerial responsibility in a business
  • synonym: manager - a person responsible for controlling or administering an organization or group of staff
  • antonym: subordinate - a person under the authority of others
