shank the ball

verb
hit the ball poorly (in sports)
8.0 7.2
Pronunciation: /ʃæŋk ðə bɔːl/ /; / /ʃæŋk ðə bɑːl/ (AmE), /ʃæŋk ðə bɔːl/ /; / /ʃæŋk ðiː bɔːl/ (BrE)
to hit the ball incorrectly, especially causing it to veer sharply off course

Examples

To shank the ball badly
He tried to shank the ball but fortunately it still landed on the green.
If you shank the ball in soccer, you might miss an easy goal.
She accidentally shanked the ball, sending it far to the right of the target.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hook the ball - to hit the ball causing it to curve sharply in the opposite direction (usually the left for a right-handed player)
  • synonym: slice - to hit a ball so it curves sharply to one side unintentionally
