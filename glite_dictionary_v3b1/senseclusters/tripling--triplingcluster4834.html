tripling

noun
an increase by three times
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪplɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrɪplɪŋ / (BrE)
an increase or multiplication by three times; the act of making something three times as much or as many

Examples

A tripling in size
A tripling in enrollment put pressure on the university's resources.
Scientists reported a tripling of the reaction rate under the new conditions.
The tripling of prices caused widespread concern among consumers.
