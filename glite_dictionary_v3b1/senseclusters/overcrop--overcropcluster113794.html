overcrop

verb
exhaust soil by over-cultivation
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkrɑp / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkrɒp / (BrE)
to exhaust land or soil by cultivating it too intensively or too frequently

Examples

To overcrop land
Government policies were introduced to prevent farmers from overcropping their land.
If farmers continue to overcrop the same fields, the soil will become barren.
The land was overcropped for years, leading to a significant loss of nutrients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deplete - to reduce the amount or supply of something significantly
