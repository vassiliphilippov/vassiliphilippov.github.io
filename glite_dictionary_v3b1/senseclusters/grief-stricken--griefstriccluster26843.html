grief-stricken

adjective
overwhelmed by grief
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːf ˌstrɪkən / (AmE), / ˈɡriːf ˌstrɪk.ən / (BrE)
overwhelmed or deeply affected by grief, especially as a result of loss

Examples

Grief-stricken family
After the disaster, the entire town was left grief-stricken and in shock.
She looked grief-stricken at the news of her friend's passing.
The grief-stricken family gathered together to support each other after their loss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bereaved - deprived of a loved one through death
  • synonym: heartbroken - suffering severe emotional pain from loss or disappointment
  • antonym: jubilant - showing great joy or triumph
