survivable

adjective
able to be survived
4.1
Pronunciation: / sərˈvaɪvəbəl / (AmE), / səˈvaɪvəbəl / (BrE)
able to be survived or endured, even in difficult or dangerous situations

Examples

Survivable injuries
Doctors said the injuries from the accident were survivable with prompt medical attention.
The harsh winter was barely survivable for the explorers stranded in the mountains.
Thanks to emergency supplies, the conditions after the earthquake remained survivable for the residents.
The doctors assured us that the injuries were survivable with prompt medical attention.
Thanks to modern technology, many accidents that were once fatal are now survivable.
The harsh conditions on the mountain were barely survivable for the climbers.

  • antonym: fatal - causing death; not able to be survived
  • synonym: manageable - able to be dealt with or controlled
  • synonym: tolerable - able to be endured or put up with
