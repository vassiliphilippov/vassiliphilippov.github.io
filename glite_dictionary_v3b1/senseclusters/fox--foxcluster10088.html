fox

noun
small carnivorous mammal with bushy tail
1.0 5.96/m
Pronunciation: / fÉ‘ks / (AmE), / fÉ’ks / (BrE)
a small, carnivorous mammal with a pointed muzzle, upright ears, and a bushy tail, noted for its cleverness and solitary hunting habits

Examples

A wild fox
A red fox darted into the undergrowth as we approached.
Many farmers consider the fox a cunning and troublesome animal.
The fox uses its keen sense of hearing to locate prey beneath the snow.

Related Terms
