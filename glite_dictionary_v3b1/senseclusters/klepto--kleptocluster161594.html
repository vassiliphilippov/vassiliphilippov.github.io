klepto

adjective
tending to steal
Pronunciation: / ˈklɛptoʊ / (AmE), / ˈklɛptəʊ / (BrE)
informal: having a tendency to steal or suffering from kleptomania

Examples

A klepto coworker
Everyone knew he was a bit klepto after several things went missing from the office.
Her friends joked that she was klepto because she always took souvenirs from restaurants.
The teacher warned the class about being klepto after some supplies disappeared.

Related Terms

  • antonym: honest - not likely to steal or cheat
  • synonym: kleptomaniac - a person with an irresistible urge to steal
