Epilobium hirsutum

noun
species of flowering plant (great willowherb)
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpəˌloʊbiəm hɪrˈsuːtəm / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˌləʊbiəm hɪəˈsuːtəm / (BrE)
A species of plant native to Europe and Asia, with purplish-red flowers and hairy stems and leaves, commonly known as great willowherb; also found in North America.
