poopy

interjection
childish exclamation
Pronunciation: / ˈpuːpi / (AmE), / ˈpuːpi / (BrE)
an informal, childish exclamation used to express mild annoyance, frustration, or disappointment

Examples

Poopy! That happened
Poopy! I forgot to bring my lunch to school today.
When his drawing got smudged, he exclaimed, 'Poopy!' and started over.
She shouted 'Poopy!' after dropping her ice cream on the ground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: darn - an informal exclamation expressing annoyance or frustration
  • synonym: drat - an old-fashioned exclamation of mild annoyance
