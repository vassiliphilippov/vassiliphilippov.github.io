name day

noun
celebration on a saint's feast day of the same name
5.7 6.0
Pronunciation: /neɪm deɪ/ (AmE), /neɪm deɪ/ (BrE)
A day dedicated to celebrating a person who has the same name as a saint, usually observed on the feast day of that saint.

Examples

She received flowers and small gifts from her friends for her name day.
