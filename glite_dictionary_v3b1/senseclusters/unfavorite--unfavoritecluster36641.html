unfavorite

noun
least liked person or thing
7.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfeɪvərɪt / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪvərɪt / (BrE)
a person or thing that is not liked or is least preferred among a group.

Examples

The team's unfavorite
Despite his efforts, he remained the team's unfavorite throughout the tournament.
The movie quickly became the critics' unfavorite of the year.
She worried about being the teacher's unfavorite in class.

Related Terms

  • antonym: favorite - a person or thing that is especially liked or preferred
  • synonym: outcast - a person who is rejected or not accepted by a group
