flowchart

verb
to diagram a process
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈfloʊ.tʃɑːrt/ (AmE), /ˈfləʊ.tʃɑːt/ (BrE)
to create a diagram that shows the steps or decisions in a process using standard flowchart symbols

Examples

To flowchart a process

Before starting the project, the manager suggested flowcharting each step to identify possible issues.
They are flowcharting the customer service workflow to improve efficiency.
