cementitious

adjective
having the properties of cement
6.0
Pronunciation: / sɛmənˈtɪʃəs / (AmE), / sɛmɛnˈtɪʃəs / (BrE)
having the characteristics of cement, or relating to substances that act as or contain cement

Examples

cementitious materials
Researchers are developing more eco-friendly cementitious composites for sustainable construction.
The new building was constructed using cementitious materials to ensure durability.
This coating provides a cementitious layer that protects the metal from corrosion.

Related Terms
