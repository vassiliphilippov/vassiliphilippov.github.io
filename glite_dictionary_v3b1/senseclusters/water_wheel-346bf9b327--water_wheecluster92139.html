water wheel

noun
wheel driven by water to generate power
6.0 3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːtər wiːl /; / ˈwɔtəˌwiːl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə wiːl /; / ˈwɔːtəˌwiːl / (BrE)
a large wheel turned by flowing water, used to power machines or lift water (especially before electricity)

Examples
