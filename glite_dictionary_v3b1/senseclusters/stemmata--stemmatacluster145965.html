stemmata

noun
genealogical diagrams
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛm.ə.tə / (AmE), / ˈstɛm.ə.tə / (BrE)
diagrams or charts showing genealogical relationships, such as family trees, or the relationships among manuscripts in textual studies

Examples

Genealogical stemmata
The historian used several stemmata to trace the lineage of the royal family.
In the critical edition, the editor included stemmata to show how the manuscripts are related.
Researchers compared different stemmata to understand the evolution of the text over centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: family tree - a diagram showing the relationships between people in several generations of a family
  • synonym: genealogy - the study or tracing of lines of family descent
