surface assimilation

noun
accumulation of molecules on a surface
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrfɪs əˌsɪməˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfɪs əˌsɪmɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the accumulation of molecules of a gas or liquid onto the surface of a solid to form a thin film

Examples

Surface assimilation of gases
Catalysts are often activated by surface assimilation of reactant gases.
Researchers studied the surface assimilation of water vapor on various minerals.
The efficiency of the filter depends on rapid surface assimilation of impurities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adsorption - adhesion of molecules to a surface
