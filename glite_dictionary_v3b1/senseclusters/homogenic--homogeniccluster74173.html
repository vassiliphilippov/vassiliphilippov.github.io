homogenic

adjective
uniform in composition
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊməˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌhəʊməˈdʒɛnɪk /; / ˌhɒməˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
composed of parts that are all the same kind or come from the same source; uniform in structure or origin
