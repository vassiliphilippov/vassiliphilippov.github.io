Polyodontidae

noun
family of paddlefishes
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑlioʊˈdɒntɪdi /; / ˌpɑliˌoʊdɑnˈtɪdi / (AmE), / ˌpɒliəʊˈdɒntɪdiː /; / ˌpɒliˌəʊdanˈtɪdiː / (BrE)
