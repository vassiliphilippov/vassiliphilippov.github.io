couldn't resist

verb
unable to refrain due to temptation
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊd(ə)nt rɪˈzɪst / (AmE), / ˈkʊd(ə)nt rɪˈzɪst / (BrE)
was unable to stop oneself from doing something, especially due to strong temptation or desire.

Examples

She couldn't resist chocolate
Despite her diet, Maria couldn't resist the chocolate cake on the table.
He saw the freshly baked cookies and couldn't resist taking one.
She couldn't resist laughing at his silly joke.

Related Terms

  • antonym: give in - to yield or submit to something
  • synonym: resist - to withstand or refrain from something tempting or harmful
