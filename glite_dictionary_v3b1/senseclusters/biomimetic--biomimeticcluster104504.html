biomimetic

adjective
inspired by biology
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊmɪˈmɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊmɪˈmɛtɪk / (BrE)
imitating or inspired by the structures, functions, or processes of living organisms

Examples

Biomimetic design
The engineers developed a biomimetic surface that repels water just like a lotus leaf.
Researchers are studying biomimetic materials to create stronger and lighter building components.
The biomimetic approach in robotics helps machines move more naturally and efficiently.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artificial - made by humans rather than occurring naturally
  • synonym: bionic - having artificial body parts that imitate natural ones
