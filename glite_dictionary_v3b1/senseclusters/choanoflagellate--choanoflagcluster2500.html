choanoflagellate

noun
aquatic protozoan
Pronunciation: / koʊˌænəˈflædʒəˌleɪt / (AmE), / kəʊˌanəˈflædʒɪlət / (BrE)
a single-celled aquatic organism with a collar of flagella, considered closely related to animals

Examples

A choanoflagellate cell
Scientists believe that choanoflagellates are the closest living relatives of animals.
A choanoflagellate uses its collar of flagella to capture food particles from the water.
The study of choanoflagellates helps researchers understand the evolution of multicellular life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protozoan - a single-celled microscopic organism
  • synonym: sponge - a simple aquatic animal related to choanoflagellates
