adman

noun
man in the advertising business
Pronunciation: / ˈædˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈæd.man / (BrE)
a man who works in the advertising industry, especially as a creative professional or executive

Examples

An adman in advertising
After a decade as an adman, he knew all the tricks of the trade.
She often joked that marrying an adman meant always seeing commercials differently.
The talented adman came up with a clever campaign that went viral.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advertiser - a person or organization that advertises to promote products or services
  • synonym: adwoman - a woman who works in advertising
