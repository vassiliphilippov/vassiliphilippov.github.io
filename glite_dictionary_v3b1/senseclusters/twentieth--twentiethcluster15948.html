twentieth

noun
position number 20 in a sequence
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɛn.ti.əθ / (AmE), / ˈtwɛn.ti.ɪθ / (BrE)
The position of someone or something that is number 20 in a sequence; one of twenty equal parts.

Examples

Twentieth of June
On the twentieth of June, the company will announce its quarterly results.
Only a twentieth of the cake remained after the party was over.
She finished in twentieth place in the marathon, just ahead of her friend.
