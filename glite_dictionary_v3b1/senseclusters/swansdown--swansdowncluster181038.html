swansdown

noun
soft feather or fluff
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːnzˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈswɒnzˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a small, soft feather or tuft of down from a swan, or a tiny piece of soft fluff

Examples

A swansdown feather
A light swansdown landed on her sleeve and floated to the floor.
The pillow was filled with the softest swansdown for extra comfort.
She brushed a piece of swansdown from the baby's blanket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: down - soft fine feathers found under the tougher exterior feathers of birds
  • synonym: fluff - light, soft, and small pieces of fiber or feather
