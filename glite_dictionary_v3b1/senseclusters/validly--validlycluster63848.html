validly

adverb
in a valid manner
Pronunciation: / ˈvælɪdli / (AmE), / ˈvælɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is legally or logically sound; in a valid manner

Examples

To validly act
Legislation must be validly enacted to be binding.
The contract was not signed validly, so it is unenforceable.
The deed was validly executed in accordance with the law.

Related Terms

  • antonym: invalidly - in a manner that is not valid
