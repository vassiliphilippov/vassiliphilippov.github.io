underbody

noun
underside of a vehicle
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈʌn.dər.bɑː.dɪ/ (AmE), /ˈʌn.də.bɒd.ɪ/ (BrE)
the lower exterior part of a vehicle or machine, especially the protective or structural underside.

Examples

Car underbody
The mechanic inspected the underbody of the SUV for rust and damaged panels.
Before winter, she had a protective coating sprayed onto the car's underbody to prevent corrosion.
We drove the truck over a ramp so the service team could photograph its underbody for the report.
