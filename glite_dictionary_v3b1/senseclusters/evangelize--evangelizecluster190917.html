evangelize

verb
to promote beliefs
6.9
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈvæn.dʒə.laɪz/ /; / /iː.ˈvæn.dʒə.laɪz/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈvæn.dʒə.laɪz/ /; / /iː.ˈvæn.dʒə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to try to persuade people to accept a particular belief, especially a religious one

Examples

To evangelize new beliefs
She traveled to remote villages to evangelize and share her religious message.
He often evangelizes about the benefits of a healthy lifestyle to his friends.
The organization aims to evangelize its core values through community outreach programs.
Missionaries traveled to distant countries to evangelize and share their beliefs.
He spent years evangelizing in small towns across the region.
Some people feel uncomfortable when others try to evangelize about their personal beliefs.

  • antonym: discourage - to try to prevent someone from adopting a belief or action
  • synonym: preach - to publicly speak about and promote a belief or idea
  • synonym: proselytize - to try to convert someone to a religion or belief
