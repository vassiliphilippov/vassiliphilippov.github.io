guffaw

noun
loud, hearty laughter
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈfɔ / (AmE), / ɡəˈfɔː / (BrE)
a loud and boisterous burst of laughter

Examples

A loud guffaw
A sudden guffaw echoed across the quiet library, startling everyone.
His joke was met with a raucous guffaw from the crowd.
She tried to stifle a guffaw during the serious meeting.
