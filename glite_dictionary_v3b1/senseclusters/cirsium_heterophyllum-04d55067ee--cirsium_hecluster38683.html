Cirsium heterophyllum

noun
European thistle with reddish-purple flowers
Pronunciation: / sɜːrˈsaɪ.əm ˌhɛt.əˈrɑː.fɪ.ləm / (AmE), / sɜːˈsɪ.əm ˌhɛt.əˈrɒ.fɪ.ləm / (BrE)
