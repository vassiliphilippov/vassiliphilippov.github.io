nan

noun
Indian leavened flatbread
Pronunciation: / nɑn / (AmE), / nɑːn / (BrE)
A type of leavened, oven-baked flatbread from India, typically shaped like a teardrop.

Examples

Fresh nan bread
She learned to make nan in a traditional clay tandoor oven.
The chef brushed garlic on the freshly baked nan before serving it.
We ordered butter chicken and a basket of nan to share at the restaurant.
