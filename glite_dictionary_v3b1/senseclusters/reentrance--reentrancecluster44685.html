reentrance

noun
return or entering again
5.5
Pronunciation: / riˈɛntrəns / (AmE), / riˈɛntrəns / (BrE)
the act of entering a place, position, or state again after having left it.

Examples

Dramatic reentrance
The actor made a dramatic reentrance onto the stage after the intermission.
After years abroad, her reentrance into the local community was warmly welcomed.
Security protocols delayed the reentrance of staff into the building after the alarm.
