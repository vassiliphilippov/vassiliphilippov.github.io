forestlike

adjective
resembling a forest
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrɪstˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɒrɪstˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of a forest, especially in appearance or atmosphere

Examples

Forestlike appearance
The park's forestlike setting made it a peaceful place to walk and relax.
Her backyard became forestlike after she planted dozens of tall trees and shrubs.
The restaurant's forestlike decor featured wooden beams and hanging plants.
