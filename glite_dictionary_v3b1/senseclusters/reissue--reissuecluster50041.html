reissue

noun
a publication or recording issued again
7.5
Pronunciation: / riˈɪʃu / (AmE), / riˈɪʃuː / (BrE)
a publication or recording, such as a book or music album, that is released again without changes, usually after its initial release

Examples

A reissue album
Collectors eagerly awaited the reissue of the rare comic book.
Her favorite album was only available as a reissue with bonus tracks.
The publisher decided to reissue the classic novel due to renewed interest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: edition - a particular version or release of a publication
  • antonym: original - the first or initial publication or recording
