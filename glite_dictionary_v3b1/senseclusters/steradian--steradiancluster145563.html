steradian

noun
SI unit of solid angle
9.9
Pronunciation: / stɛˈreɪdiən / (AmE), / stɪˈreɪdiən / (BrE)
the SI unit of solid angle, equal to the solid angle subtended at the center of a sphere by an area of its surface equal to the square of its radius

Examples

One steradian unit
A cone with its vertex at the center of a sphere can subtend one steradian.
Luminous intensity is measured in candelas per steradian.
The solid angle of a complete sphere is 4π steradians.
