psyched

adjective
excited or eager
Pronunciation: / saɪkt / (AmE), / saɪkt / (BrE)
feeling very excited, enthusiastic, or eager about something

Examples

To be psyched up
He was really psyched to start his new job on Monday.
Everyone was psyched for the concert after waiting months for tickets.
I'm psyched about our upcoming vacation to the mountains.
