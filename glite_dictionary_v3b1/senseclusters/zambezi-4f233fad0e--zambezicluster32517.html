Zambezi

proper noun
major river in Africa
Pronunciation: / zæmˈbeɪzi / (AmE), / zæmˈbiːzi / (BrE)
a river in south-central Africa, the continent's fourth-longest, flowing east into the Indian Ocean.

Examples

The Zambezi River
After heavy rains, the Zambezi can flood large areas of Mozambique.
Many communities along the Zambezi depend on fishing for their livelihoods.
The Zambezi supports diverse wildlife and is famous for Victoria Falls.
