sweet balm

noun
a fragrant perennial herb; lemon balm
5.4
Pronunciation: / swit bɑːm / (AmE), / swiːt bɑːm / (BrE)
a bushy perennial herb of the mint family, with small white or yellowish flowers and fragrant, lemon-scented leaves, often grown in gardens; another name for lemon balm (Melissa officinalis)
