did not

interjection
emphatic denial
Pronunciation: / dɪd nɑt / (AmE), / dɪd nɒt / (BrE)
used as a short, emphatic spoken reply to deny or contradict a previous statement or accusation

Examples

She said 'did not!'
When accused of breaking the vase, she quickly replied, 'did not!'
He shouted 'did not!' when his friend claimed he had cheated in the game.
Even when everyone insisted he was late, he insisted, 'did not!'

Related Terms

  • antonym: did too - used as an emphatic reply to affirm or assert something in response to a denial
  • synonym: no - used to give a negative response or denial
