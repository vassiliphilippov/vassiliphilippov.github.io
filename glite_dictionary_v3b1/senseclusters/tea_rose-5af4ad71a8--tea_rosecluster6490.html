tea rose

noun
variety of hybrid rose with scented flowers
3.5
Pronunciation: / tiː roʊz / (AmE), / tiː rəʊz / (BrE)
a type of rose bush derived from a tea-scented Chinese rose, with large, fragrant pink or yellow flowers, widely grown in gardens

Examples

A tea rose bush
Many gardeners prize the tea rose for its elegant blooms and fragrance.
She planted a yellow tea rose bush along the garden path.
The delicate scent of the tea rose filled the garden at dusk.
