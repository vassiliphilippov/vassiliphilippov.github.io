protoplasmic

adjective
of cell protoplasm
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtəˈplæzmɪk / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəˈplæzmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of protoplasm, the living, jelly-like substance inside cells

Examples

Protoplasmic content
The protoplasmic content of the cell is responsible for many vital functions.
Scientists observed changes in the protoplasmic structure under the microscope.
Damage to the protoplasmic layer can affect the entire cell's health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cellular - relating to cells
  • antonym: nonliving - not alive or not containing living material
