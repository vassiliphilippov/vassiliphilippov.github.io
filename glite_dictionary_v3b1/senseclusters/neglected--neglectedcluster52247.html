neglected

adjective
not cared for or attended to
2.3
Pronunciation: / nɪˈɡlɛktɪd / (AmE), / nɪˈɡlɛktɪd / (BrE)
Not cared for or attended to; left without necessary attention or support.

Examples

Neglected garden
After months of being neglected, the garden was full of dead plants.
The old house looked neglected, with overgrown weeds and peeling paint.
The shelter takes in neglected animals who have nowhere else to go.
