fibroma

noun
benign fibrous tissue tumor
9.9
Pronunciation: / faɪˈbroʊmə / (AmE), / fɪˈbrəʊmə / (BrE)
A benign tumor composed mainly of fibrous or connective tissue.

Examples

A fibroma tumor
A dental X-ray revealed a small fibroma near the tooth root.
Most fibromas are harmless and do not require treatment.
The surgeon removed a fibroma from the patient's arm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sarcoma - a malignant tumor of connective tissue
