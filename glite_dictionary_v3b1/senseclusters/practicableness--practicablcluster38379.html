practicableness

noun
quality of being practicable or usable
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpræktɪkəblnəs / (AmE), / ˈpræktɪkəblnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being practicable; the condition of being capable of being done or put into practice.

Examples

The practicableness of plans
Engineers evaluated the practicableness of installing solar panels on the new building.
She admired the idea for its ambition but doubted its practicableness in real-world conditions.
The practicableness of his plan was questioned by several team members during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: practicability - the quality of being practicable or feasible
