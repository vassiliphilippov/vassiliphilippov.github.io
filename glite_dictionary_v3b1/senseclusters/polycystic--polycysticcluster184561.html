polycystic

adjective
having many cysts
7.2
Pronunciation: /pɑː.lɪ.ˈsɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /pɒl.ɪ.ˈsɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
having or characterized by many fluid-filled sacs, especially in an organ or tissue

Examples

A polycystic condition can affect the function of various organs in the body.
The patient was treated for polycystic ovary syndrome after experiencing irregular periods.
