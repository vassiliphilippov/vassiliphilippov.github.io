sandfly fever

noun
viral disease from sand fly bite
6.2 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsænd.flaɪ ˈfiː.vər/ (AmE), /ˈsænd.flaɪ ˈfiː.və/ (BrE)
A mild viral disease transmitted by the bite of the sand fly Phlebotomus papatasii, causing fever and often headache, muscle pain, or malaise.

Examples

Sandfly fever symptoms
Many cases of sandfly fever were reported among travelers to the affected region.
Symptoms of sandfly fever typically include fever, headache, and muscle pains.
Vaccines are not available for sandfly fever, so prevention focuses on avoiding sand fly bites.
