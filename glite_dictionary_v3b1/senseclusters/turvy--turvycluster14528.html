turvy

adjective
upside down

Synonyms

  • upside down
    2.0 7.83/m
    upside down is a common everyday phrase for having the top and bottom swapped, while turvy names that same inverted position.
  • inverted
    4.8 0.02/m
    inverted is a more formal term used to describe a reversed top and bottom, while turvy is a simple adjective for that position.
  • overturned
    3.6 0.00/m
    overturned means flipped over often after movement or force, while turvy describes the same upside-down state without implying how it happened.
  • upturned
    4.5 0.05/m
    upturned describes something whose lower side now faces up, while turvy means fully inverted so the top is at the bottom.
