reinterment

noun
burial again elsewhere
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈtɜːrmənt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈtɜːmənt / (BrE)
the act of burying a body again in a different place after it has been exhumed

Examples

The reinterment of a body
The reinterment of the famous poet attracted visitors from across the country.
After the old cemetery was closed, the reinterment of several families' ancestors was arranged.
A special ceremony was held for the reinterment of the soldiers' remains in the national memorial.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burial - the act of placing a dead body in the ground
  • antonym: exhumation - the act of digging up a buried body
