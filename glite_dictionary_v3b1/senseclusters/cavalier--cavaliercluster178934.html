cavalier

noun
mounted soldier
Pronunciation: / ˌkævəˈlɪr / (AmE), / ˌkævəˈlɪə / (BrE)
a horseman, especially a mounted soldier or knight, often associated with chivalry or nobility

Examples

A cavalier on horseback
The cavalier led his troops into battle with courage and skill.
At the festival, a cavalier in shining armor greeted the children on horseback.
During the parade, the proud cavalier waved to the cheering crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knight - a man who served his sovereign as a mounted soldier in armor
  • synonym: soldier - a person who serves in an army
