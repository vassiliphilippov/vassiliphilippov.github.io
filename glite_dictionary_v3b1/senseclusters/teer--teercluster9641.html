teer

noun
South Asian arrow
9.9
a pointed weapon, similar to an arrow, shot from a bow, especially in South Asian contexts

Examples

An archer's teer
The archer carefully placed the teer on the bowstring before taking aim.
In traditional festivals, skilled participants compete by shooting the teer at distant targets.
He collected each teer after the archery practice was finished.
