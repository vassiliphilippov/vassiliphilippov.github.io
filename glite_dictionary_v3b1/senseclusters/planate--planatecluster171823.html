planate

adjective
flattened or leveled
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪneɪt / (AmE), / ˈplæneɪt / (BrE)
(of a surface or object) having been flattened; level or flat.

Examples

A planate surface
In geology, a planate region is one that has been leveled by erosion.
The insect's wings are nearly planate, which aids in gliding flight.
The leaves have a distinctly planate surface, distinguishing them from their relatives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: convex - curved outward, opposite of flat
  • synonym: flattened - made or become flat
