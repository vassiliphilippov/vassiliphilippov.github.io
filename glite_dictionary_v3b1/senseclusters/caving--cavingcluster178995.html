caving

adjective
for cave exploration
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkeɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to the activity of exploring caves, especially describing equipment or practices used in caves

Examples

Caving helmet

The team checked their caving equipment before entering the underground tunnels.
Special caving boots are designed to provide grip on slippery rocks.

Related Terms
