outdoor game

noun
game played outdoors
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌdɔr ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌdɔː ɡeɪm / (BrE)
An athletic or physical game that is played outside rather than indoors.

Examples

Outdoor game of soccer
Children love playing outdoor games like soccer and hide-and-seek during summer.
Physical education classes often include various outdoor games on the school field.
The camp organized several outdoor games to keep everyone active and entertained.
