rose oil

noun
essential oil from rose petals
Pronunciation: / roʊz ɔɪl / (AmE), / rəʊz ɔɪl / (BrE)
a fragrant essential oil obtained from the petals of roses, used in perfumes, cosmetics, and aromatherapy

Examples

Pure rose oil
Pure rose oil is highly valued for its sweet and floral scent in luxury products.
She uses rose oil in her skincare routine for its soothing properties.
The perfumer added a few drops of rose oil to enhance the fragrance of the blend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attar of roses - another name for rose oil, especially in perfumery
