Baltic-Finnic

noun
subgroup of Finnic languages
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌbɔltɪkˈfɪnɪk / (AmE), / ˌbɔːltɪkˈfɪnɪk / (BrE)
Relating to a subgroup of the Finnic languages spoken around the Baltic Sea, including Finnish and Estonian.

Examples

Baltic-Finnic languages
Finnish is the most widely spoken Baltic-Finnic language.
Linguists have studied the differences between Baltic-Finnic and other Uralic languages.
The Baltic-Finnic group also includes Estonian, Karelian, and Votic.
