tubercle

noun
small projection on a bone
9.9
Pronunciation: / tuˈbɝːkəl / (AmE), / tjuːˈbɜːkəl / (BrE)
a small rounded projection or protuberance on a bone, especially one serving as an attachment point for a muscle or ligament

Examples

Bone tubercle
A small tubercle on the rib marks where the ligament connects.
The deltoid tubercle serves as an important attachment site for the shoulder muscle.
The doctor pointed out a tubercle on the X-ray of my vertebra.
