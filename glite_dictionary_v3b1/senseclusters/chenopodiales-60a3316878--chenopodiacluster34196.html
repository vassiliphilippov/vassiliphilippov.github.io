Chenopodiales

noun
order of flowering plants (botany)
Pronunciation: / ˌkɛnoʊˌpoʊdiˈeɪliːz /; / ˌkiːnəˌpoʊdiˈeɪliːz / (AmE), / ˌkiːnəˌpəʊdiˈeɪliːz /; / ˌkɛːnəˌpəʊdɪˈeɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of flowering plants that includes the goosefoot family and related families, formerly known as Centrospermae
