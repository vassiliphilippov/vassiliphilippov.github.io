transmittal

noun
accompanying document
6.5
Pronunciation: / trænzˈmɪtəl / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪtəl / (BrE)
a document or letter that is sent from one person or organization to another, often to accompany other materials

Examples

A transmittal letter
The project manager attached a transmittal to the set of blueprints sent to the client.
Please include a transmittal when you submit the final report to the office.
Each shipment requires a transmittal to document what is being sent and why.

Related Terms

  • synonym: correspondence - written communication between people or organizations
  • synonym: cover letter - a letter sent with documents to explain their purpose
