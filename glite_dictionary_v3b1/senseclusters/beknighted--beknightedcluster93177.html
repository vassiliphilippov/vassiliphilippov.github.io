beknighted

verb
to make a knight
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪˈnaɪtɪd / (AmE), / bɪˈnaɪtɪd / (BrE)
to make someone a knight, especially by a formal or ceremonial act

Examples

To beknight a warrior
The king beknighted the brave soldier after the battle.
In ancient times, a ruler might beknight a loyal subject for acts of valor.
The ceremony to beknight the young squire was attended by the entire court.
