electric car

noun
car powered by electricity
1.9 3.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk kɑr / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk kɑː / (BrE)
A car powered entirely or primarily by electricity, using an electric motor rather than a conventional internal combustion engine.

Examples

An electric car battery
Many cities are installing charging stations to support electric car drivers.
She decided to buy an electric car to reduce her carbon footprint.
The government offers incentives for people who purchase an electric car.
