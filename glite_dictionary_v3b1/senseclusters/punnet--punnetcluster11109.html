punnet

noun
small basket for fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌn.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈpʌn.ɪt / (BrE)
a small, light basket or container used for holding or selling fruits such as strawberries or raspberries, often serving as a measure

Examples

A punnet of strawberries
She bought a punnet of strawberries from the market for dessert.
The farmer arranged the punnets of raspberries neatly on the stall.
Would you like a punnet of blueberries with your order?
