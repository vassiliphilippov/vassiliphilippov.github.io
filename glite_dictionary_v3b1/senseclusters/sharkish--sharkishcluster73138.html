sharkish

adjective
resembling a shark
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑrkɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːkɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling a shark in appearance, especially having features like a pointed shape, fins, or sharp teeth.

Examples

Sharkish car design
The designer gave the car a sharkish look with its sleek, pointed front.
Her costume included a sharkish fin attached to her back for the play.
The mountain's sharkish peak stood out sharply against the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shark-like - having qualities or appearance similar to a shark
