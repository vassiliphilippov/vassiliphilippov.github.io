bromeosin

noun
red fluorescent dye used in biology and cosmetics
9.9
Pronunciation: / broʊˈmiːəsɪn / (AmE), / brəʊˈmiːəsɪn / (BrE)
a red fluorescent dye produced by treating fluorescein with bromine, used in cosmetics and as a stain in biological studies

Examples

bromeosin red dye
Scientists used bromeosin to highlight the cell structures under the microscope.
The manufacturer added bromeosin to the product for its cosmetic coloring properties.
When exposed to UV light, bromeosin emits a bright red fluorescence.
