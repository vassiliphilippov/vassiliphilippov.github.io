bromeosin

noun
red fluorescent dye used in biology and cosmetics
9.9
Pronunciation: /broʊ.ˈiː.sɪn/ /; / /broʊ.ˈiː.oʊ.sɪn/ (AmE), /brəʊ.ˈiː.sɪn/ /; / /brəʊ.ˈiː.ə.sɪn/ (BrE)
