ball

verb
form into a ball by winding or rolling
Pronunciation: / bɔl / (AmE), / bɔːl / (BrE)
to form (something) into a ball by winding or rolling it

Examples

To ball up socks
As she cleaned, she balled the socks together for the laundry.
He balls up the dough and lets it rest before baking.
She carefully balled the yarn before starting her knitting project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roll - to shape something by turning it over and over
  • antonym: unwind - to undo or untwist something that has been wound
