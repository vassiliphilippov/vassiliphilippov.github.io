yard goods

noun
fabrics sold by the yard
Pronunciation: / jɑrd ɡʊdz / (AmE), / jɑːd ɡʊdz / (BrE)
cloth or fabric that is sold by length, measured in yards.

Examples

Several yard goods

Special discounts are available on all yard goods this weekend.
