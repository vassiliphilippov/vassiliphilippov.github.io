Messiah

noun
Jesus Christ as the promised deliverer in Christianity
7.2
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈsaɪ.ə/ (AmE), /mə.ˈsaɪ.ə/ (BrE)
(in Christianity) Jesus Christ, regarded by Christians as the promised savior and deliverer of humankind.

Examples

Jesus the Messiah
Handel's oratorio 'Messiah' celebrates the birth, death, and resurrection of the Messiah.
In Christian theology, the Messiah is seen as the redeemer of humanity.
Many Christians believe that Jesus is the Messiah prophesied in the Old Testament.
