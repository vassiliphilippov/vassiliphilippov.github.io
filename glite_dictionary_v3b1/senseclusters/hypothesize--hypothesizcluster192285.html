to suggest or propose an explanation or theory based on limited evidence as a starting point for further investigation

Examples

To hypothesize a cause
Scientists often hypothesize about the origins of mysterious archaeological findings.

It is important for scientists to hypothesise and then test their ideas rigorously.
Some researchers hypothesise that climate change could increase the frequency of certain diseases.
