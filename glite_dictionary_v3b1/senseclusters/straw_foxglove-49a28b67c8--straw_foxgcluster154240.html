straw foxglove

noun
European yellow-flowered foxglove plant
Pronunciation: / strɔ fɑksˌɡlʌv / (AmE), / strɔː ˈfɒksˌɡlʌv / (BrE)
A European plant with yellow, tubular flowers, belonging to the foxglove family.

Unlike the common purple foxglove, the straw foxglove displays soft yellow flowers.
