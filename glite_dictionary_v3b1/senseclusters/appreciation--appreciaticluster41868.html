appreciation

noun
expression of gratitude
Pronunciation: / əˌpriʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a statement, action, or feeling that shows gratitude or recognition for something received or done

Examples

Words of appreciation
His speech was a heartfelt appreciation of the support he had received.
She sent a card to express her appreciation for their kindness.
Volunteers were given a small gift as a token of appreciation.
A small gift was given to express their appreciation for the volunteers' efforts.
His appreciation of her hard work was shown through a heartfelt letter.
The artist received an award in appreciation of his contribution to the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: admiration - a feeling of respect and approval
  • antonym: criticism - the expression of disapproval of someone or something
  • synonym: gratitude - the feeling of being thankful
  • antonym: ingratitude - lack of thankfulness
  • synonym: thanks - words or actions expressing gratitude
