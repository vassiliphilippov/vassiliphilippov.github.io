on course

adverb
progressing toward a goal
5.0
Pronunciation: / ɑn kɔrs / (AmE), / ɒn kɔːs / (BrE)
progressing as planned towards achieving a particular goal or result

Examples

On course for victory
Despite early setbacks, the team remained on course for victory in the championship.
If she keeps working this hard, she'll be on course to receive a promotion soon.
The government says the economy is on course to recover by next year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: off course - not progressing as planned or deviating from a path or goal
