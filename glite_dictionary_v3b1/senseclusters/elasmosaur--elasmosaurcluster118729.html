elasmosaur

noun
long-necked marine reptile
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlæzməˌsɔr / (AmE), / ɪˈlæzməsɔː / (BrE)
a large, long-necked marine reptile from the Cretaceous period, belonging to the family Elasmosauridae

Examples

A elasmosaur fossil

Children were fascinated by the long neck of the elasmosaur model at the natural history museum.
