concuss

verb
to injure the brain by a blow
4.1
Pronunciation: / kənˈkʌs / (AmE), / kənˈkʌs / (BrE)
to injure the brain by a blow or shock, typically causing a temporary loss of function or consciousness

Examples

Boxer concussed in fight
Boxers sometimes get concussed during matches if they're hit in the head.
The accident concussed him so badly he couldn't remember the event.
The player was concussed and had to leave the field immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concussion - a temporary unconsciousness or confusion caused by a blow to the head
