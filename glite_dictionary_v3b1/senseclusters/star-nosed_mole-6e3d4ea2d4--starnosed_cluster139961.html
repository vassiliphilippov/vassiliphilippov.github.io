star-nosed mole

noun
amphibious North American mole with star-shaped nose
Pronunciation: / stɑr noʊzd moʊl / (AmE), / stɑː nəʊzd məʊl / (BrE)
a small, amphibious mole of eastern North America, distinguished by the pink, fleshy tentacles surrounding its nose

Examples

A star-nosed mole
A star-nosed mole can detect and eat its prey with astonishing speed.
Biologists studied the habitat of the star-nosed mole in wetland areas.
The star-nosed mole is famous for its unusual nose covered in sensitive tentacles.
