strip

verb
remove cured leaves from a plant
Pronunciation: / strɪp / (AmE), / strɪp / (BrE)
to remove leaves from a plant or the last milk from a cow (in agriculture or animal care)

Examples

After curing, machines are used to strip the tobacco on large farms.
It takes skill to strip the leaves from the cured stalks efficiently.

It's important to strip the cow at the end of milking for hygiene.
She learned to strip the cows by hand on her family's dairy farm.
The farmer carefully stripped each cow to ensure no milk was left in the udder.
