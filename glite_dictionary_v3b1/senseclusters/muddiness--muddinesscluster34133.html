muddiness

noun
state of being muddy or dirty with wetness
6.1 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌd.ɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈmʌd.ə.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈmʌd.ɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈmʌd.ə.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality or state of being covered in or containing mud, especially with wet or dirty conditions

Examples

River muddiness
After the flood, the muddiness of the river made it impossible to see the bottom.
Players complained about the muddiness of the football field after last night's storm.
The heavy rain increased the muddiness of the hiking trail, making it difficult to walk.
