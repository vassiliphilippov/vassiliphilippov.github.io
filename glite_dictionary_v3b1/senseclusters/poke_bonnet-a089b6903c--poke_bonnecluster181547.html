poke bonnet

noun
women's hat with a projecting brim, tied under the chin
Pronunciation: / poʊk ˈbɑːnɪt / (AmE), / pəʊk ˈbɒnɪt / (BrE)
A woman's hat with a projecting brim that is tied under the chin, popular in the 19th century.

Examples

A poke bonnet hat
