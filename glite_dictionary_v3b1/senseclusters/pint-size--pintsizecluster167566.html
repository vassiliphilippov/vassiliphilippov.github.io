pint-size

adjective
very small; smaller than average
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪntˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpaɪntˌsaɪz / (BrE)
Very small in size or stature; smaller than average, especially in height.

Examples

A pint-size person
At only five feet tall, the pint-size actor still commanded attention on stage.
Don't underestimate the pint-size puppy—she's braver than she looks.
The coach nicknamed him 'the pint-size powerhouse' because of his extraordinary energy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diminutive - extremely or unusually small
  • antonym: gigantic - very large; enormous
  • synonym: tiny - very small in size or amount
