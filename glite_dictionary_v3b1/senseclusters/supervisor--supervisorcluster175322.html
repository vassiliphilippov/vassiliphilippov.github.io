supervisor

noun
person who oversees others' work
2.8 23/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsuÀêp…ôrÀåva…™z…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàsuÀêp…ôva…™z…ô / (BrE)
a person who oversees the work or tasks of others, ensuring that standards and objectives are met

Examples

Team supervisor
Each team has a supervisor who is responsible for maintaining safety protocols.
If you have any problems with your assignment, speak to your supervisor for guidance.
The supervisor walked around the factory floor to check on the workers' progress.

Synonyms

  • overseer
    4.8 1.89/m
    overseer watches and manages workers on the ground, while supervisor ensures standards and objectives are met across tasks and teams.
  • manager
    1.6 108/m
    manager controls resources and plans operations in an organization, while supervisor focuses on overseeing day-to-day work and meeting standards.
  • foreman
    3.1 6.76/m
    foreman leads and directs a team of manual workers on site, while supervisor checks overall work and enforces standards.
  • superintendent
    6.0 9.90/m
    superintendent runs a department or institution at a higher level, while supervisor oversees daily tasks and ensures specific standards.
  • boss
    1.0 68/m
    boss is the informal person in charge with authority over staff, while supervisor focuses on overseeing work and meeting standards.
