babi

noun
offensive insult (Malay)
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑː.biː/ (AmE), /ˈbɑː.biː/ (BrE)
an offensive term in Malay and Indonesian meaning 'pig', used as an insult to call someone rude or disgusting

Examples

Called a babi insultingly
He angrily called the rude driver a babi after being cut off in traffic.
Using the word babi in conversation can deeply offend someone from Malaysia or Indonesia.
She was shocked when someone shouted babi at her in the market.
