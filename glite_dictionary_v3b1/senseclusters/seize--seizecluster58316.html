seize

verb
capture someone’s attention or imagination
5.4 3.2
Pronunciation: /siːz/ (AmE), /siːz/ (BrE)
to capture and hold someone's attention, interest, or imagination strongly and suddenly

Examples

Seize the imagination

The dramatic news seized the public’s interest for weeks.
The gripping novel seized my attention from the very first page.
