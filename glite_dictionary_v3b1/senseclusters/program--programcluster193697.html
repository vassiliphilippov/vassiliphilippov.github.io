program

noun
political party's aims document
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
a document outlining the aims and principles of a political party or candidate

Examples

A party program
The opposition party released its program before the national election.
The party’s program focused on healthcare and education reform.
Voters analyzed the program to compare each candidate's policies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: platform - a statement of the aims and policies of a political party
