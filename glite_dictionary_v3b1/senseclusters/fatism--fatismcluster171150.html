fatism

noun
discrimination against overweight people
Pronunciation: / ˈfæt.ɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈfæt.ɪzəm / (BrE)
discrimination or prejudice against people who are overweight or obese.

Examples

Fatism against people
Her experiences with fatism made her determined to advocate for body positivity.
Many activists are campaigning against fatism in the workplace and in public health messaging.
The documentary exposed the subtle ways fatism appears in media and fashion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ableism - discrimination in favor of able-bodied people
  • synonym: sizeism - discrimination based on a person's size or weight
