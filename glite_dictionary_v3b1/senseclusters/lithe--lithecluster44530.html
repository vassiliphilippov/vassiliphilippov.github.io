lithe

adjective
gracefully thin and flexible
Pronunciation: / laɪð / (AmE), / laɪð / (BrE)
(of a person or their body) thin, supple, and able to move or bend easily and gracefully.

Examples

A lithe body
She admired the lithe movements of the young gymnast.
The cat stretched with a lithe grace as it awoke from its nap.
The dancer’s lithe body moved effortlessly across the stage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stiff - not easily bent or changed in shape; rigid
  • synonym: supple - bending and moving easily; flexible
