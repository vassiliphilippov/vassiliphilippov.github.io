Kevlar

proper noun
synthetic fiber brand
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛvˌlɑr / (AmE), / ˈkɛvˌlɑː / (BrE)
a strong, lightweight synthetic fiber used in products like bulletproof vests and protective gear

Examples

Kevlar material
Police officers often wear vests made from Kevlar for protection.
The lightweight Kevlar helmet saved the soldier's life during the mission.
Many racing tires are reinforced with Kevlar to increase their strength and durability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aramid - a class of strong, heat-resistant synthetic fibers
  • synonym: nylon - a synthetic polymer used to make fibers and fabrics
