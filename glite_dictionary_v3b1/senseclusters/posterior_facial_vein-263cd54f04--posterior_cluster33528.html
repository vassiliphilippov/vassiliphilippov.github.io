posterior facial vein

noun
posterior branch of the facial vein
8.4

the posterior branch of the facial vein, formed by temporal veins in front of the ear

Examples

The posterior facial vein near ear

The posterior facial vein drains blood from areas in front of the ear into the internal jugular vein.
