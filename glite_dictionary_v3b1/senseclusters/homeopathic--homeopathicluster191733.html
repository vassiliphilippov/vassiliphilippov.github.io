homeopathic

adjective
relating to homeopathy (alternative medicine)
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊmiəˈpæθɪk / (AmE), / ˌhəʊmiəˈpæθɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving homeopathy, a system of alternative medicine based on treating diseases with highly diluted substances that are believed to cause similar symptoms in healthy people

Examples

Homeopathic remedies
Many people seek homeopathic remedies for allergies instead of conventional treatments.
She visited a homeopathic practitioner to discuss her chronic pain.
There is ongoing debate about the effectiveness of homeopathic medicine.
She works at a clinic that specializes in homoeopathic treatments.
Some patients prefer homoeopathic remedies over conventional drugs.
There is ongoing debate about the effectiveness of homoeopathic medicine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: allopathic - relating to conventional medicine, especially as opposed to homeopathy
