unpinched

adjective
not constricted
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɪntʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɪntʃt / (BrE)
not pinched, squeezed, or constricted; free from tightness or tension

Examples

Calm unpinched face
After a week at the spa, her features appeared calm and unpinched.
The shoes were comfortable and left his toes feeling unpinched all day.
He smiled, his expression open and unpinched for the first time in months.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pinched - showing tightness or tension, especially in appearance
  • synonym: relaxed - free from tension or tightness
