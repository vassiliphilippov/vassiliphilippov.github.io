angiocardiogram

noun
X-ray of the heart after injection
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒioʊˈkɑrdiəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˌandʒiəʊˈkɑːdiəˌɡram / (BrE)
an X-ray image or series of images of the heart and its blood vessels taken after injecting a radiopaque substance

Examples

An angiocardiogram image
After the dye was injected, a detailed angiocardiogram was taken for diagnostic purposes.
Her angiocardiogram showed a blockage in one of the main coronary arteries.
The doctor reviewed the angiocardiogram to assess the patient's heart function.
