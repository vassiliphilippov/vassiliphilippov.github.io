famished

adjective
extremely hungry
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfæmɪʃt / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪʃt / (BrE)
extremely hungry; suffering from severe hunger.

Examples

To feel famished
After missing lunch, she felt absolutely famished by dinnertime.
He was so famished that he ate three helpings of pasta.
The famished children rushed into the kitchen as soon as the food was ready.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: hungry - feeling the need or desire for food
  • synonym: ravenous - extremely hungry; greedy for food
  • antonym: satiated - fully satisfied, especially with food
  • synonym: starving - suffering or dying from lack of food
