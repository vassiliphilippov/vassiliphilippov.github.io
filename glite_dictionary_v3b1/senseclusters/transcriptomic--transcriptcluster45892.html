transcriptomic

adjective
relating to RNA analysis
9.9
Pronunciation: / trænˌskrɪpˈtoʊmɪk / (AmE), / trænˌskrɪpˈtəʊmɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study or analysis of all RNA transcripts produced in a cell or organism

Examples

Transcriptomic analysis
Researchers use transcriptomic techniques to understand how cells respond to environmental changes.
A transcriptomic profile can help identify which genes are active in a disease state.
Advances in transcriptomic technology have improved our understanding of gene expression.

Related Terms

  • synonym: proteomic - relating to the study of all proteins in a cell or organism
