motherhouse

noun
main convent
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌðɚˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈmʌðəˌhaʊs / (BrE)
the main convent or headquarters of a religious order, where the central administration is located

Examples

The motherhouse convent
The sisters traveled from their small town to the motherhouse for a special ceremony.
Each year, new members are welcomed at the motherhouse before being assigned to other locations.
The motherhouse oversees the activities of all the order's convents around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: headquarters - the main office or center of operations
