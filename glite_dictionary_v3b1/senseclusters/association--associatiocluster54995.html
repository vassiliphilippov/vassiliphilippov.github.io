association

noun
mental connection of ideas or events
3.5
Pronunciation: / əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the mental process or result of connecting ideas, memories, or events in the mind

Examples

Mental association
Classical conditioning relies on the association of a stimulus with a particular response.
Piaget studied how children form associations between new and familiar objects.
The smell of fresh bread often triggers a strong association with childhood memories.
His association of thunderstorms with fear persisted long into adulthood.
The smell of baking bread always triggered a pleasant association from her childhood.
There is a strong association in his mind between that song and his summer holiday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connection - a relationship in which things are linked or associated
  • antonym: separation - the state of being apart or disconnected
