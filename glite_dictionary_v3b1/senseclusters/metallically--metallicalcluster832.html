metallically

adverb
in a metallic way
6.0
Pronunciation: / məˈtælɪkli / (AmE), / məˈtælɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that resembles or relates to metal or has metallic qualities, such as sound or appearance

Examples

To metallically sound
The old gate creaked metallically as the wind pushed it open.
Her voice echoed metallically in the empty warehouse, making the space feel even colder.
Coins clinked metallically as they fell onto the hard surface of the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sharply - in a way that is sudden or harsh
  • antonym: softly - in a gentle or quiet way
