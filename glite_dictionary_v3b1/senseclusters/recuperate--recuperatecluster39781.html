recuperate

verb
recover after illness or setback
8.5 5.1
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈkuː.pə.reɪt/ /; / /rɪ.ˈkjuː.pə.reɪt/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈkjuː.pə.reɪt/ /; / /rɪ.ˈkuː.pə.reɪt/ (BrE)
to recover health, strength, or well-being after illness, injury, or fatigue

Examples

To recuperate after illness
After her surgery, she needed time to recuperate before returning to work.
He spent two weeks at home to recuperate from the flu.
The team took a brief break to recuperate after the exhausting competition.
After a week in the hospital, he was able to recuperate at home.
It took several months for the community to recuperate after the devastating storm.
She took a few days off work to recuperate from her surgery.
