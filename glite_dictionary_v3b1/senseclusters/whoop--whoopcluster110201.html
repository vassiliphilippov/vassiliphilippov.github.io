whoop

verb
to defeat decisively
4.2
Pronunciation: / wuːpt / (AmE), / wuːpt / (BrE)

Examples

Our basketball team whooped their rivals last night, winning by a wide and unexpected margin.
They whooped the competition in the final round of the quiz show.
After months of practice, the chess club finally whooped their longtime opponents.
