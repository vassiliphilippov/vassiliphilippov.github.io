coiffe

noun
close-fitting cap
Pronunciation: / kwɑf / (AmE), / kwɒf / (BrE)
a close-fitting cap, especially one worn under a veil or helmet in the past

Examples

A knight's coiffe
The knight wore a metal coiffe beneath his helmet for extra protection.
In medieval times, a coiffe was often worn under a veil by noblewomen.
The museum displayed a delicate linen coiffe from the 14th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cap - a soft, close-fitting head covering
  • antonym: helmet - a hard protective head covering
