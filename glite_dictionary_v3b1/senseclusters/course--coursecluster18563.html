course

noun
area prepared for a sports event
3.0 32/m
Pronunciation: / k…îrs / (AmE), / k…îÀês / (BrE)
an area of land or water laid out and prepared for a sports event, such as golf, racing, or cross-country running

Examples

A golf course
After heavy rain, parts of the race course were too muddy for the horses.
She walked along the edge of the golf course, admiring the well-kept greens.
The championship was held on a challenging course with many sharp turns.

Synonyms

  • field
    1.0 36/m
    a field is an area of marked land used for team sports, while a course is a longer laid-out route or track for events.
  • playing field
    1.5 0.28/m
    a playing field is flat and marked for team games, while a course covers a longer route or varied terrain for races or golf.
  • playing area
    1.8 0.11/m
    a playing area is any marked space for games or sports, while a course is a prepared route across land or water for events.
