microloan

verb
to lend small amounts
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌloʊn / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌləʊn / (BrE)
to lend a very small amount of money to someone, typically to help them start or support a small business or meet personal needs

Examples

To microloan money
Many organizations microloan funds to entrepreneurs in developing countries to help them start businesses.
The charity decided to microloan small amounts to families needing help with school expenses.
Banks sometimes microloan to individuals who do not qualify for traditional loans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lend - to give something to someone for a period of time, expecting it to be returned
  • synonym: loan - to let someone use money or something temporarily with the expectation of repayment
