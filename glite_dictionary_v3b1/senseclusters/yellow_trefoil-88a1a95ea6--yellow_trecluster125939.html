yellow trefoil

noun
a small yellow-flowered European herb
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ ˈtriːfɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈtriːfɔɪl / (BrE)
A prostrate European herb with small yellow flowers and curved black pods, now naturalized in North America.

Examples

Yellow trefoil plant
Gardeners often plant yellow trefoil to improve soil health.
The curved black pods help to identify yellow trefoil in the meadow.
