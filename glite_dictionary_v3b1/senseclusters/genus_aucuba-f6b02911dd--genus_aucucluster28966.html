genus Aucuba

noun
genus of East Asian evergreen shrubs
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ɔːˈkuːbə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ɔːˈkjuːbə / (BrE)
A genus of hardy evergreen shrubs and small trees, native to East Asia, especially Japan, known for their glossy leaves and bright red berries.
