shaikh

noun
Muslim religious scholar
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪk / (AmE), / ʃeɪk / (BrE)
a respected religious scholar or community leader in Muslim or Arab societies

Examples

Respected shaikh leads prayers
The shaikh delivered a powerful sermon on compassion and justice in the community.
Students traveled from distant cities to study under the famous shaikh at the religious school.
A respected shaikh was invited to lead the discussion on Islamic philosophy.
The respected shaikh offered advice to the young members of the community.
After the ceremony, the shaikh greeted each visitor personally.
Many people traveled from distant villages to hear the shaikh speak.

Related Terms

  • antonym: follower - a person who supports or is guided by another
  • synonym: imam - a leader of prayer in a mosque or a Muslim religious leader
  • synonym: mullah - a Muslim learned in Islamic theology and sacred law
  • synonym: sheikh - a leader or elder in Arab or Muslim communities
