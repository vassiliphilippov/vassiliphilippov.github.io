long-suffering

noun
patient endurance of pain or hardship
4.1
Pronunciation: / lɔŋˈsʌfərɪŋ / (AmE), / lɒŋˈsʌfərɪŋ / (BrE)
Patient and enduring tolerance of pain, hardship, or unhappiness over a lengthy period.

Examples

Her long-suffering

His long-suffering finally paid off when he achieved his lifelong dream.
The refugees' long-suffering was evident in their weary faces.
