gustily

adverb
with strong bursts
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌstɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡʌstɪli / (BrE)
in a way that involves sudden, strong bursts, especially of wind or air

Examples

Wind blows gustily
The wind howled gustily through the trees, making the branches sway.
She laughed gustily at his joke, filling the room with her voice.
Smoke rose gustily from the chimney as the fire burned brightly inside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blustery - with strong, noisy, and gusty wind
  • antonym: calmly - in a quiet and peaceful manner
