taste bud

noun
sensory organ for tasting
5.5
Pronunciation: / teɪst bʌd / (AmE), / teɪst bʌd / (BrE)
A small sensory organ on the tongue that detects taste.

Examples

A taste bud on tongue
After burning his tongue, he temporarily lost sensation in his taste buds.
Children's taste buds are often more sensitive to strong flavors than adults'.
The chef explained how our taste buds recognize sweet, salty, sour, and bitter flavors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: papilla - small projections on the tongue that may contain taste buds
