Gulf of Bothnia

proper noun
northern arm of the Baltic Sea
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌlf əv ˈboʊθniə / (AmE), / ˈɡʌlf əv ˈbɒθniə / (BrE)
the northern arm of the Baltic Sea, situated between Sweden and Finland.

Examples

The Gulf of Bothnia

The ferry crosses the Gulf of Bothnia between Sweden and Finland.
