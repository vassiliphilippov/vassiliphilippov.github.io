uncalculated

adjective
not computed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈkælkjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkælkjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not determined by calculation, measurement, or estimation; not computed or figured out

Examples

Uncalculated amount

The report included some uncalculated figures that needed further review.
