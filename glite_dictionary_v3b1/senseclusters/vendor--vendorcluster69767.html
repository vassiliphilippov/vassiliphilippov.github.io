vendor

noun
person or company selling goods or services

a person or company that sells goods or services, especially in a market or as a supplier

Examples

A vendor sells goods

The food vendor on the corner sells the best hot dogs in the city.
We negotiated the price directly with the software vendor before signing the contract.

Synonyms

  • supplier
    2.7 18/m

  • merchant
    2.1 16/m
    a merchant buys and sells on a larger or retail scale in shops, while a vendor can be a small market or individual seller.
  • tradesperson
    3.5 0.00/m
    a tradesperson runs a shop and sells goods locally, while a vendor often means any seller at markets or suppliers.
  • purveyor
    6.7 2.00/m

  • salesperson
    2.2 2.21/m
    a salesperson actively sells products for a company in shops or by phone, while a vendor can be an independent market seller or supplier.
