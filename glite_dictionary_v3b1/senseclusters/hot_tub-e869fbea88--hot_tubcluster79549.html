hot tub

noun
large tub of hot water for bathing
Pronunciation: / hɑt tʌb / (AmE), / hɒt tʌb / (BrE)
A large tub filled with hot water, used for communal bathing or relaxation, typically able to accommodate multiple people.

Examples

A hot tub party
The hotel suite features a private balcony and a spacious hot tub.
They installed a new hot tub in their backyard for family gatherings.
We spent the evening relaxing in the hot tub after a long hike.
