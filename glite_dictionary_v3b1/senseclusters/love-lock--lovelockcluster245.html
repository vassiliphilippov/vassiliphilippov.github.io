love-lock

noun
padlock symbolizing romantic love
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌv ˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌlɒk / (BrE)
A padlock fastened to a public structure to symbolize the enduring love between two people, often with their names or initials inscribed.

Examples

A love-lock on bridge
Couples frequently attach a love-lock to the bridge before tossing the key into the river.
Last summer, we engraved our names on a love-lock and left it in Paris.
Many cities have banned the placement of a love-lock due to concerns about the weight on historic bridges.
