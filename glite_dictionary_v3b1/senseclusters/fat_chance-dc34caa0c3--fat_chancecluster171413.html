fat chance

noun
very unlikely event; no chance
Pronunciation: / fæt tʃæns / (AmE), / fæt tʃɑːns / (BrE)
(informal, sarcastic) used to say that something is very unlikely to happen

Examples

Fat chance of winning
Fat chance I'll win the lottery, but it's fun to daydream.
Do you think they'll finish on time? Fat chance!
He asked if she would forgive him, but she said, 'fat chance.'
Fat chance they'll let you into the club without a reservation.
He asked if I would work on my day off and I said, 'fat chance.'
You think the train will arrive on time? Fat chance!

Related Terms

  • degree higher: possible - able to happen or be done
  • synonym: slim chance - slight possibility that something will happen
