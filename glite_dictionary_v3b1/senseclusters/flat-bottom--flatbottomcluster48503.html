flat-bottom

noun
container with flat base
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈflæt ˌbɑtəm / (AmE), / ˈflæt ˌbɒtəm / (BrE)
a container or vessel with a level, flat base that allows it to stand steadily

Examples

Flat-bottom container
The chef prefers using a flat-bottom for even heating on the stove.
Make sure the flat-bottom is clean before placing it on the table.
A flat-bottom is ideal for mixing ingredients without tipping over.
