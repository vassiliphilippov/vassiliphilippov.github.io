general

adjective
of worldwide or universal scope
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛnɚəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnərəl / (BrE)
Relating to or affecting the whole world; having universal scope or applicability.

Examples

General concern
Climate change is a general concern that affects people in every country.
The treaty established general principles that apply worldwide.
There is a general consensus among scientists on the need for urgent action.

Related Terms

  • synonym: universal - Including or affecting all in a particular context; applicable everywhere
