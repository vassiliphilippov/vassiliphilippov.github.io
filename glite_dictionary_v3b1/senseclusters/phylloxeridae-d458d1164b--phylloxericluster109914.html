Phylloxeridae

noun
family of plant-parasitic insects
Pronunciation: / fɪl.ɒkˈsɛr.ɪˌdiː /; / fɪˌlɒksəˈrɪːdiː / (AmE), / fɪl.ɒkˈsɛr.ɪˌdiː /; / fɪˌlɒksəˈrɪːdiː / (BrE)
a family of small, plant-parasitic insects related to aphids, some of which are important agricultural pests
