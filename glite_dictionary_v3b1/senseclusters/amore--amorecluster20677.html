amore

interjection
affectionate address
Pronunciation: / əˈmɔːreɪ / (AmE), / əˈmɔːreɪ / (BrE)
an affectionate term used to address someone as 'darling' or 'my love', often in romantic contexts

Examples

Amore, my dear
He whispered 'amore' as he handed her a bouquet of roses.
Whenever they argued, she would soften his mood by calling him 'amore'.
At the restaurant, he surprised her by saying 'amore, this evening is for you.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: darling - a term of endearment for a loved one
  • synonym: dear - a loving or affectionate form of address
