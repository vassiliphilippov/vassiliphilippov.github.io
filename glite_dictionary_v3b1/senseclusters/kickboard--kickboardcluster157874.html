kickboard

noun
cabinet base panel
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkɪk.bɔːrd/ /; / /ˈkɪk.boʊrd/ (AmE), /ˈkɪk.bɔːd/ (BrE)
a narrow board or panel fixed at the base of cabinets or furniture to cover the gap between the unit and the floor

Examples

Cabinet kickboard
The carpenter installed a new kickboard to match the kitchen cabinets.
Dust often collects behind the kickboard if it is not sealed properly.
She chose a white kickboard to create a clean look at the base of the cupboards.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skirting board - a board covering the lowest part of an interior wall
