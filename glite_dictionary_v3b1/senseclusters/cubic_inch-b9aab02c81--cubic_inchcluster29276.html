cubic inch

noun
unit of volume (1 inch × 1 inch × 1 inch)
3.8 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkjuː.bɪk ɪntʃ/ /; / /ˈkuː.bɪk ɪntʃ/ (AmE), /ˈkjuː.bɪk ɪntʃ/ (BrE)
