unbreathable

adjective
not allowing airflow
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbriːðəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈbriːðəbəl / (BrE)
not allowing air or moisture to pass through, making it uncomfortable or unsuitable for breathing or ventilation

Examples

Unbreathable fabric
The plastic raincoat was so unbreathable that she felt hot and sticky after wearing it for an hour.
Some tents are made from unbreathable materials, causing condensation to build up inside.
He avoided unbreathable fabrics when shopping for summer clothes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airtight - not allowing air to enter or escape
  • antonym: breathable - allowing air or moisture to pass through
