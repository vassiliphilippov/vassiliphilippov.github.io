swag

verb
sway or move unsteadily
1.8 6.5
Pronunciation: /swæg/ (AmE), /swæg/ (BrE)
to sway or move heavily and unsteadily, especially from side to side or under a heavy load

Examples

To swag side to side
He watched the lantern swag back and forth as the ship rocked.
The old bridge began to swag under the weight of the heavy truck.
With every step, the overloaded donkey seemed to swag side to side.
