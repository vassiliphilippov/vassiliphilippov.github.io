Himalaya honeysuckle

noun
shrub with drooping purplish flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪməˈleɪə ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (AmE), / ˌhɪməˈleɪə ˈhʌniˌsʌkl̩ / (BrE)
a shrub with drooping spikes of purplish flowers, native to the Himalayas and adjacent regions, also known as Leycesteria formosa
