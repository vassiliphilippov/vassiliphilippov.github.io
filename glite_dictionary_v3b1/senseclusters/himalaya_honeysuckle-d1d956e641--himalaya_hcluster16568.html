Himalaya honeysuckle

noun
shrub with drooping purplish flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪməˈleɪə ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (AmE), / ˌhɪməˈleɪə ˈhʌniˌsʌkl̩ / (BrE)
a shrub with drooping spikes of purplish flowers, native to the Himalayas and adjacent regions, also known as Leycesteria formosa

Examples

The Himalaya honeysuckle shrub
Gardeners value the Himalaya honeysuckle for its resilience and attractive berries.
The Himalaya honeysuckle adds a splash of late summer color to many British gardens.
We planted a Himalaya honeysuckle near the fence to attract birds and bees.
