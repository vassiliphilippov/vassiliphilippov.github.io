pure

adjective
emphasizing something as complete or absolute
1.0
Pronunciation: / pjʊr / (AmE), / pjʊə / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is complete or absolute, often with a negative or pejorative implication

Examples

Pure nonsense
Her accusation was pure nonsense and had no basis in fact.
It was pure luck that we managed to catch the last train home.
The idea is pure folly and will never work.
