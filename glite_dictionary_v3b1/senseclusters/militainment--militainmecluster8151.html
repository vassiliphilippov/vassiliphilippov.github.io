militainment

noun
military-themed entertainment
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪlɪˈteɪnmənt / (AmE), / ˌmɪlɪˈteɪnmənt / (BrE)
Entertainment that prominently features military themes and often glorifies or celebrates the military, especially as influenced or endorsed by military organizations.

Examples

Militainment in movies
Critics argue that militainment blurs the line between genuine news and entertainment.
Many blockbuster action films today can be classified as militainment due to their celebration of military heroism.
The documentary explores how militainment shapes public perception of armed conflicts.
