Chokwe

proper noun
Central African people
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃoʊkweɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒkweɪ / (BrE)
a member of a Central African ethnic group or the Bantu language spoken by this group

Examples

Chokwe people
The Chokwe have a rich tradition of wood carving and mask making.
Many Chokwe people live in Angola, the Democratic Republic of the Congo, and Zambia.
She is studying the Chokwe language as part of her research on African cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Bantu - a group of related African peoples and languages
  • synonym: Luba - a Central African ethnic group and language
