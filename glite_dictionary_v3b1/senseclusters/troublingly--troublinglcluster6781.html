troublingly

adverb
causing concern
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌblɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtrʌblɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes worry, concern, or distress

Examples

Changed troublingly
The data has changed troublingly since the last report, raising new questions.
She noticed that his behavior had become troublingly unpredictable in recent weeks.
The city's air quality has troublingly declined over the past year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disturbingly - in a way that causes disturbance or distress
  • antonym: reassuringly - in a way that makes someone feel less worried
  • synonym: worryingly - in a way that causes worry or anxiety
