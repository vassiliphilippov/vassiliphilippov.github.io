brock

noun
badger (archaic)
Pronunciation: / brɑk / (AmE), / brɒk / (BrE)
an old or dialect word for a badger, a burrowing mammal with black and white markings

Examples

A brock badger
In the old stories, the wise brock was a friend to the woodland creatures.
Farmers once believed that seeing a brock near their fields was a sign of good luck.
The children spotted a brock disappearing into its burrow at dusk.
