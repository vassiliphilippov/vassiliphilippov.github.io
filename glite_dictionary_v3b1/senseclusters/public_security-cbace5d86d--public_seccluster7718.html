public security

noun
safety of people in public places
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk sɪˈkjʊrəti / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk sɪˈkjʊərɪti / (BrE)
The protection and safety of the general public in public places, maintained by authorities to prevent disorder or crime.

Examples

Public security measures
After several incidents, concerns about public security have increased among residents.
Maintaining public security is a primary responsibility of the police force.
The government has implemented new measures to improve public security in city centers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: law enforcement - the activity of ensuring obedience to the laws, especially by the police
  • synonym: public safety - the welfare and protection of the general public
