loft

verb
to store in attic
4.8 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / lÉ”ft / (AmE), / lÉ’ft / (BrE)
to lift or move something into a loft or attic for storage

Examples

To loft boxes
They spent the afternoon lofting old furniture into the attic before the renovation started.
After cleaning the garage, he began lofting boxes of winter clothes upstairs for storage.
We finished lofting the holiday decorations so the living room would be tidy again.
