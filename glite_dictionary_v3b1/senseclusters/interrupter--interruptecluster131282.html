interrupter

noun
one who breaks flow
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntəˈrʌptər / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈrʌptə / (BrE)
a person or thing that stops or breaks the flow or continuity of something

Examples

A noisy interrupter
The constant phone calls were a major interrupter of my concentration while studying.
During the presentation, a technical issue acted as an unexpected interrupter.
He was known as an interrupter because he often spoke over others in meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disruptor - someone or something that causes disruption
  • synonym: disturber - a person or thing that disturbs
  • antonym: facilitator - a person or thing that makes a process easier or smoother
