buzzy

adjective
tingling sensation
6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌ.ziː/ /; / /ˈbʌ.zɪ/ (AmE), /ˈbʌ.zɪ/ /; / /ˈbʌ.ziː/ (BrE)

The medicine made his fingers feel slightly buzzy and warm.
She described a buzzy sensation in her foot after sitting in one position too long.
