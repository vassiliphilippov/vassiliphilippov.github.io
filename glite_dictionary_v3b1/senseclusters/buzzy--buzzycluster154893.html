buzzy

adjective
tingling sensation
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌzi / (AmE), / ˈbʌzi / (BrE)
experiencing a slight buzzing or tingling physical sensation, often as a result of numbness or mild stimulation

Examples

Buzzy feeling
After the dentist's anesthetic wore off, my cheek felt buzzy for hours.
The medicine made his fingers feel slightly buzzy and warm.
She described a buzzy sensation in her foot after sitting in one position too long.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: numb - lacking normal sensation, often due to cold or anesthesia
  • synonym: tingly - having a slight stinging or prickling sensation
