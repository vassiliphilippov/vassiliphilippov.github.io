evergreen cherry

noun
California evergreen shrub or tree; wild plum
Pronunciation: / ˈɛvərˌɡrin ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌɡriːn ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
A small, spiny-leaved evergreen shrub or tree native to California, bearing white flowers and commonly known as the California wild plum.

Examples
