ice tea

noun
chilled tea drink served over ice
Pronunciation: / aɪs ti / (AmE), / aɪs tiː / (BrE)
Tea that is brewed and then served cold over ice, often with added sugar or lemon.

Examples

A glass of ice tea
On a hot summer day, nothing is more refreshing than a glass of ice tea.
She prepared a pitcher of ice tea with a slice of lemon for their guests.
Would you like sweetened or unsweetened ice tea with your meal?

Related Terms

  • synonym: iced tea - tea served cold over ice; alternate, more common spelling
