seafaring

adjective
connected with life or travel at sea
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsiˌfɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌfeərɪŋ / (BrE)
connected with traveling or working on the sea, especially regularly or as a profession.

Examples

Seafaring nations
Many seafaring nations built their economies around trade and exploration.
The island has a rich seafaring tradition dating back centuries.
The museum displays artifacts from the region's seafaring past.
