rhombus

noun
parallelogram with four equal sides
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːmbəs / (AmE), / ˈrɒmbəs / (BrE)
a parallelogram with all four sides of equal length and opposite sides parallel, but without right angles; an oblique-angled equilateral parallelogram

Examples

A rhombus shape
Artists often use the rhombus in mosaics and tiled designs for its distinctive symmetry.
In geometry class, we learned that a rhombus is a special kind of parallelogram.
The shape on the board looked like a rhombus with its four equal sides and opposite angles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diamond - a shape with four equal sides, also called a lozenge or rhombus in geometry
  • degree higher: square - a quadrilateral with four equal sides and four right angles
