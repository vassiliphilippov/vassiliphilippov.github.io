hardworking

adjective
putting in a lot of effort; diligent
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌhɑːdˈwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
Regularly putting in a lot of effort and persistence in one's work or tasks; diligent and industrious.

Examples

A hardworking person
His hardworking attitude helped him get promoted quickly.
She is known to be a hardworking student who never misses a deadline.
The company appreciates hardworking employees who go the extra mile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: industrious - diligent; hardworking and persistent
  • antonym: lazy - unwilling to work or put in effort
