Asturian

noun
language of Asturias
9.9
Pronunciation: / æˈstʊriən / (AmE), / æˈstjʊəriən / (BrE)
a Romance language spoken in the region of Asturias in northern Spain and nearby areas

Examples

Asturian language
She is fluent in both Asturian and Spanish after living in northern Spain for many years.
The university offers courses in Asturian to promote regional languages and culture.
Many traditional songs from Asturias are sung in Asturian rather than Spanish.
