unbruised

adjective
without bruises
4.5 0.03/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàbruÀêzd / (AmE), /  ånÀàbruÀêzd / (BrE)
not having any bruises or marks caused by injury or pressure

Examples

Unbruised fruit
The peaches arrived unbruised thanks to the careful packaging.
She checked her knee and was relieved to find it unbruised after the fall.
Despite the rough journey, most of the fruit remained unbruised and fresh.

Synonyms

  • unhurt
    1.0 0.53/m

  • uninjured
    1.8 0.47/m

  • unwounded
    4.8 0.05/m

  • unscathed
    6.3 1.87/m
    unscathed means left unharmed overall and often describes things, while unbruised means lacking physical bruises on the body.
