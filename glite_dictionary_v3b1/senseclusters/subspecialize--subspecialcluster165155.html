subspecialize

verb
to focus more narrowly
8.7
Pronunciation: /səb.ˈspeʃ.ə.laɪz/ /; / /sʌb.ˈspeʃ.ə.laɪz/ (AmE), /səb.ˈspeʃ.ə.laɪz/ /; / /sʌb.ˈspeʃ.ə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to focus on a narrower area within a specialized field, especially in a profession or academic discipline

Examples

To subspecialize in surgery
After becoming a general surgeon, she decided to subspecialize in pediatric surgery.
Many lawyers subspecialize in areas like environmental or intellectual property law.
He chose to subspecialize in marine biology after studying general biology at university.
