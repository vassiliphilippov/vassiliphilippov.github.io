formic acid

noun
a colorless pungent acid found in ants
2.7 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfɔːr.mɪk ˈæs.ɪd/ /; / /ˈfɔːr.mɪk ˈæs.əd/ (AmE), /ˈfɔː.mɪk ˈæs.ɪd/ (BrE)
a colorless, pungent, fuming liquid acid (HCOOH) found naturally in ants and some plants, or manufactured for use in textiles, paper, and insecticides
