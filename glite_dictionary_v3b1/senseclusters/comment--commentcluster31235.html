comment

verb
express an opinion or remark
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːˌmɛnt /; / ˈkɑːmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɛnt / (BrE)
to express an opinion or make a remark about something

Examples

To comment on news
He was asked to comment on the recent economic crisis.
Please comment if you have any suggestions or feedback.
She commented that the report was thorough and well-researched.
She often comments on current affairs in her blog.
The spokesperson refused to comment on the ongoing investigation.
When asked about the decision, he simply commented that it was confidential.

Related Terms

  • antonym: criticize - to express disapproval or find fault with
  • synonym: critique - to evaluate in a detailed and analytical way
  • synonym: remark - to say or write something as a comment
