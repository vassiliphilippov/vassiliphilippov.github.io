hang

verb
when a jury cannot reach a verdict
7.5
Pronunciation: / hæŋ / (AmE), / hæŋ / (BrE)
(of a jury) to be unable to reach a verdict, thereby preventing a decision in a trial.

Examples

The jury hangs
If the jury hangs, the judge may declare a mistrial.
It was the second time this year that a jury has hung in a high-profile case.
The jury hung after days of trying to reach a unanimous verdict.
