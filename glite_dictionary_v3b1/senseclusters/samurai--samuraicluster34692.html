samurai

noun
member of feudal Japanese military class
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmʊˌraɪ /; / ˈsɑː.məˌraɪ / (AmE), / ˈsæmʊˌraɪ /; / ˈsæm.ʊ.raɪ / (BrE)
a member of the military aristocracy or warrior class in feudal Japan, known for their code of honor and martial skill
