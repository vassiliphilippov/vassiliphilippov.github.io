gentleman-at-arms

noun
ceremonial guard for British sovereign
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛntlmən æt ˈɑrmz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntlmən ət ˈɑːmz / (BrE)
one of a group of forty ceremonial guards who attend the British sovereign on state occasions

Examples

A gentleman-at-arms guard

It is a great honor to be appointed as a gentleman-at-arms.
The gentlemen-at-arms are easily recognized by their ornate uniforms.
