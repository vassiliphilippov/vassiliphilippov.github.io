flip

noun
acrobatic movement; somersault
Pronunciation: / flɪp / (AmE), / flɪp / (BrE)
an acrobatic movement where the body turns over in the air, such as a somersault or dive

Examples

A back flip
He landed a back flip on his skateboard, impressing the crowd.
Learning how to do a front flip takes practice and courage.
The gymnast performed a perfect flip during her floor routine.
During the competition, every flip had to show clean form and a tight tuck.
His first attempt at a flip ended in a big splash, but he kept practicing.
She performed a perfect flip off the diving board, impressing the judges.
