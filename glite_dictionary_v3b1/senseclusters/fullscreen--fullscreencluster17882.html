fullscreen

noun
entire screen mode
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfʊl.skriːn/ (AmE), /ˈfʊl.skriːn/ (BrE)
a display mode in which content fills the entire screen, hiding window borders and other interface elements

Examples

Watch in fullscreen
You can watch the video in fullscreen for a more immersive experience.
Press F11 to switch your browser to fullscreen and hide the toolbars.
The game automatically starts in fullscreen but you can change this in the settings.
