Synonyms

  • stick out
    3.2 4.64/m
    stick out is a common, everyday way to say something extends beyond a surface, while poke out feels more casual and physical.
  • jut
    4.8 2.61/m
    jut means to extend sharply from a line or edge, while poke out suggests a small or sudden part sticking out.
  • protrude
    5.8 4.35/m

  • project
    4.3 2.43/m
    project means to extend outward and appears in technical descriptions, while poke out sounds more physical and informal.
