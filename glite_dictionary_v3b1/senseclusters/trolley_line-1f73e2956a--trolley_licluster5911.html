trolley line

noun
public transport route for streetcars or trolley buses
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑli laɪn / (AmE), / ˈtrɒli laɪn / (BrE)
a route or system of tracks along which streetcars or trolley buses operate, typically within a city

Examples

The trolley line route
After years of disuse, the trolley line was reopened as a heritage transit route.
Many historic neighborhoods still have an old trolley line running through them.
The city council proposed an extension to the trolley line downtown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bus line - a route regularly served by buses
  • synonym: tramway - a track for trams or streetcars, especially in a city
