nonmetallic

adjective
not resembling or containing metal
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.məˈtælɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.məˈtælɪk / (BrE)
not containing, made of, or resembling metal; lacking the properties of a metal.

Examples

Nonmetallic materials
Most plastics are considered nonmetallic materials and are used as electrical insulators.
Sulfur is a nonmetallic element commonly found in nature.
The surface had a dull, nonmetallic sheen, unlike the shiny silverware nearby.

Related Terms

  • antonym: metallic - having the characteristics of a metal
