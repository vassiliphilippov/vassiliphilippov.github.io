unvarnished

adjective
plain, not softened or disguised
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvɑrnɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈvɑːnɪʃt / (BrE)
expressed in a direct and honest way, without trying to soften or disguise the truth

Examples

Unvarnished truth
He appreciated their unvarnished opinions, even when the truth was difficult to hear.
She gave an unvarnished account of what happened during the meeting.
The documentary offered an unvarnished look at life in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candid - truthful and straightforward; frank
  • antonym: embellished - having added details or decoration, often to exaggerate or soften the truth
  • synonym: plain - clearly and simply stated; not elaborate
