get a look

verb
see something briefly
1.1
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ə lʊk / (AmE), / ɡɛt ə lʊk / (BrE)
to catch sight of something or someone for a short period of time

Examples

To get a look at someone
I tried to get a look at the famous actor as he walked by.
She managed to get a look inside the new restaurant before it opened.
The children climbed the fence to get a look at the parade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glimpse - a brief or quick view or look
