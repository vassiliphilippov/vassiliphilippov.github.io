acutely

adverb
in a sharp or highly sensitive manner
4.1
Pronunciation: / əˈkjuːtli / (AmE), / əˈkjuːtli / (BrE)
in a perceptive, shrewd, or highly sensitive manner

Examples

Acutely aware person
He acutely sensed that something was wrong, even before anyone spoke.
She was acutely aware of every nuance in his tone of voice.
The company acutely understood the market trends and adjusted its strategy accordingly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astutely - in a clever or perceptive manner
  • antonym: bluntly - in a direct and unsophisticated manner
