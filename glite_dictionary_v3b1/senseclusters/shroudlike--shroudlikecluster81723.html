shroudlike

adjective
resembling a shroud
8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈʃraʊd.laɪk/ (AmE), /ˈʃraʊd.laɪk/ (BrE)
resembling or similar to a shroud in appearance, effect, or quality, often suggesting something that covers or conceals

Examples

The old house was covered in a shroudlike layer of dust and cobwebs.
Her voice had a shroudlike quality, soft and mysterious in the dimly lit room.
