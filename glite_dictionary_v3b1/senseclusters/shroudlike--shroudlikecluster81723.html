shroudlike

adjective
resembling a shroud
Pronunciation: / ˈʃraʊdˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈʃraʊdˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or similar to a shroud in appearance, effect, or quality, often suggesting something that covers or conceals

Examples

A shroudlike fog
A shroudlike fog settled over the city, making it difficult to see the buildings.
The old house was covered in a shroudlike layer of dust and cobwebs.
Her voice had a shroudlike quality, soft and mysterious in the dimly lit room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: transparent - allowing light to pass through so that objects can be seen clearly
  • synonym: veiled - covered or concealed as if with a veil
