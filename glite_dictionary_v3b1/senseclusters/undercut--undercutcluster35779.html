undercut

verb
make a lower angled cut in felling a tree
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈʌndəkʌt / (BrE)
(of a person cutting a tree) to make an angled cut below the main cut on the side toward which the tree is intended to fall.

Examples

To undercut a tree
Properly undercutting a tree helps ensure it falls safely in the intended direction.
The lumberjack carefully undercut the tree before making the final saw cut.
Workers were trained to undercut each tree before felling it during the logging operation.
