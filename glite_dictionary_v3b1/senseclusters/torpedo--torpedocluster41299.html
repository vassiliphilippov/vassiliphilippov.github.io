torpedo

noun
large sandwich with meat and cheese
Pronunciation: / tɔrˈpiːdoʊ / (AmE), / tɔːˈpiːdəʊ / (BrE)
A large sandwich made from a long, crusty roll split lengthwise and filled with meats, cheese, and various toppings; also known as a submarine, hoagie, or grinder.

Examples

A meatball torpedo
I grabbed a meatball torpedo before heading to the game.
She ordered a turkey torpedo with extra lettuce and tomatoes for lunch.
The deli is famous for its gigantic Italian torpedo sandwiches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grinder - New England term for a similar sandwich
  • synonym: hoagie - a sandwich on a long roll, especially in Philadelphia
  • synonym: submarine sandwich - a large sandwich on a long roll with various fillings
