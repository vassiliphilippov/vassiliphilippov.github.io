Theotokos

noun
title for Mary
9.9
Pronunciation: / θiˈɑːtəˌkɑs / (AmE), / θiˈɒtəkɒs / (BrE)
a title or image of Mary, the mother of Jesus, especially as recognized as the mother of God in Christian tradition (mainly in Eastern Christianity)

Examples

Icon of Theotokos
The church displayed a beautiful icon of the Theotokos above the altar.
Many hymns in the Orthodox tradition honor the Theotokos for her role in salvation history.
Pilgrims traveled to the monastery to pray before the image of the Theotokos.
The church displayed a beautiful Theotokos above the altar for all to see.
Pilgrims traveled from afar to pray before the ancient Theotokos icon.
The artist spent years restoring the faded colors of the Theotokos in the monastery.
