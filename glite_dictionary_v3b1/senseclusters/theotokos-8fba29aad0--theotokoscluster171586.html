Theotokos

noun
title for Mary
9.9
Pronunciation: / θiˈɑːtəˌkɑs / (AmE), / θiˈɒtəkɒs / (BrE)
a title or image of Mary, the mother of Jesus, especially as recognized as the mother of God in Christian tradition (mainly in Eastern Christianity)
