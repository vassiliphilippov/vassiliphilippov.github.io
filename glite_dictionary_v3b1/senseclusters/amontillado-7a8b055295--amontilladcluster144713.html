Amontillado

noun
pale, medium-dry Spanish sherry
Pronunciation: / əˌmɑn.tiˈjɑː.doʊ / (AmE), / əˌmɒn.tɪˈjɑː.dəʊ / (BrE)
A pale, medium-dry sherry wine from Spain, known for its slightly nutty flavor and amber color.

Examples

A glass of Amontillado
