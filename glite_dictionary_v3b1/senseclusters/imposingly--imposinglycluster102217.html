imposingly

adverb
in a grand or impressive manner
4.4
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpoʊzɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpəʊzɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is grand, impressive, or striking in appearance or manner.

Examples

To speak imposingly
She spoke imposingly, commanding attention from everyone in the room.
The castle stood imposingly at the edge of the cliff, overlooking the valley below.
The statue loomed imposingly over the city square, drawing the awe of passersby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impressively - in a way that evokes admiration or respect
  • antonym: plainly - in a simple, unadorned manner
