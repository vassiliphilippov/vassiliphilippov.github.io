convergently

adverb
approaching a limit
7.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɝːdʒəntli / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːdʒəntli / (BrE)
in a way that approaches a single point, value, or limit, especially in mathematics or analysis

Examples

Numbers increase convergently
The series of numbers increased convergently toward a fixed value as more terms were added.
In evolutionary biology, unrelated species may develop similar traits convergently due to similar environments.
The algorithm works convergently, ensuring the solution becomes more accurate with each iteration.

Related Terms

  • antonym: divergently - in a way that moves apart or away from a point or limit
