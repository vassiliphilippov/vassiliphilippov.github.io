MiniDisc

noun
digital audio format
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪniˌdɪsk / (AmE), / ˈmɪniˌdɪsk / (BrE)
a small, portable optical disc and the technology for recording, storing, and playing digital audio, developed by Sony in the 1990s
