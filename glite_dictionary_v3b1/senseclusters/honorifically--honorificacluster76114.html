honorifically

adverb
with respect or title
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːnəˈrɪfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɒnəˈrɪfɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that shows respect or uses an honorific title or form of address

Examples

Honorifically called Doctor
The professor was honorifically addressed as Doctor during the conference.
In some cultures, elders are always spoken to honorifically as a sign of respect.
She was honorifically referred to as 'Madam President' throughout the event.
