Aghan

noun
ninth month of the Hindu calendar
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːɡɑːn / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡɑːn / (BrE)
The ninth month of the Hindu calendar, typically falling in November–December.

Examples

Month of Aghan
Harvesting often begins in Aghan after the monsoon season ends.
Many families perform religious rituals during Aghan each year.
The festival was celebrated in the month of Aghan according to the Hindu calendar.
