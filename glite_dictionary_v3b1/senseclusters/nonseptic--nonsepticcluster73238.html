nonseptic

adjective
without septic system
7.4
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɛptɪk / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɛptɪk / (BrE)
not using, connected to, or served by a septic sewage system or septic tank

Examples

Nonseptic system
The new development was designed to be nonseptic, connecting all homes to the city sewer system.
Because the property was nonseptic, waste management was handled by the municipal authorities.
They preferred a nonseptic solution to avoid the maintenance required by a septic tank.
