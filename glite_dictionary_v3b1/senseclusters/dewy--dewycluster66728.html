dewy

adjective
wet with dew; moist like dew
2.7 0.60/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàduÀêi / (AmE), / ÀàdjuÀêi / (BrE)
covered with or appearing as if covered with dew; wet with dew.

Examples

Dewy grass
He walked barefoot across the dewy lawn, enjoying the coolness.
Her dewy skin glowed softly under the sunlight.
The grass was dewy early in the morning.

Synonyms

  • bedewed
    8.2 0.01/m
    bedewed sounds literary and shows something was made wet by dew, while dewy is the simple adjective for a surface covered in dew.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dry - free from moisture or liquid; not wet
