split personality

noun
condition of having multiple independent personalities
1.0
Pronunciation: / splɪt pɚˌsɑːnˈæl.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / splɪt pɜːˌsɒnˈæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
a mental disorder in which an individual's personality is divided into two or more distinct identities, each with its own behaviors and memories; formally known as dissociative identity disorder

Examples

Split personality disorder
Many movies portray split personality disorder, though often inaccurately.
She read a case study about an individual struggling with split personality and memory loss.
The patient was diagnosed with a split personality after exhibiting two distinctly separate identities.
