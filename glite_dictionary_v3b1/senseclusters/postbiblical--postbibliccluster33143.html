postbiblical

adjective
after biblical times
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈbɪb.lɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈbɪb.lɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Occurring or coming after the period described in the Bible; relating to events or times subsequent to biblical times.

Examples

The postbiblical period
Many scholars study the evolution of Jewish traditions in the postbiblical era.
The book analyzes postbiblical interpretations of ancient laws.
This ritual developed during the postbiblical period of Jewish history.

Related Terms

  • antonym: biblical - relating to or contained in the Bible
