compartment

noun
partitioned section within a larger space
1.0
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / kəmˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a separate section or space within a larger container or area, used for storage or organization

Examples

Overhead compartment
Please place your bag in the overhead compartment before takeoff.
She stored her jewelry in a small compartment inside the box.
The train's sleeper car had a private compartment for each passenger.
Each suitcase has a separate compartment for shoes and toiletries.
He opened the glove compartment to retrieve his sunglasses.
The train carriage was divided into first-class and second-class compartments.
