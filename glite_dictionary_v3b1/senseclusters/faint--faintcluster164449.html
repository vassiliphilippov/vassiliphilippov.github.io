faint

adjective
timid; lacking courage or conviction
7.2
Pronunciation: / feɪnt / (AmE), / feɪnt / (BrE)
lacking conviction, boldness, or courage; timid or irresolute.

Examples

Faint heart
His faint attempts to oppose the decision were immediately dismissed.
She gave a faint smile, clearly uncertain about the plan.
Without faint hearts in the room, the proposal moved forward boldly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bold - showing a willingness to take risks; confident and courageous
  • synonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence
