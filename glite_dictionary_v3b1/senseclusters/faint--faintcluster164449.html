faint

adjective
timid; lacking courage or conviction
6.1 1.02/m
Pronunciation: / feÉªnt / (AmE), / feÉªnt / (BrE)
lacking conviction, boldness, or courage; timid or irresolute.

Examples

Faint heart
His faint attempts to oppose the decision were immediately dismissed.
She gave a faint smile, clearly uncertain about the plan.

Synonyms

    3.5 4.14/m

  • fainthearted
    7.7 0.12/m

  • weak-kneed
    7.1 0.03/m
    weak-kneed shows weak will or shaky courage under pressure, while faint is a general lack of boldness or determination.
