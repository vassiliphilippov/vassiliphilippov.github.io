bag of bones

noun
extremely thin person or thing
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɡ əv boʊnz / (AmE), / ˈbæɡ əv bəʊnz / (BrE)
a person or animal who is extremely thin, or an object in a very poor or skeletal condition

Examples

A bag of bones
After months of illness, the dog became just a bag of bones.
He’s nothing but a bag of bones after losing all that weight.
The abandoned house was filled with furniture that was practically a bag of bones.
