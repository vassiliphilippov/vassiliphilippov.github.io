fussiness

noun
the state of being easily upset or irritable
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌsinəs / (AmE), / ˈfʌsinɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being easily upset, irritable, or difficult to please

Examples

Child's fussiness at dinner
He apologized for his fussiness over the minor details of the plan.
Increased fussiness can sometimes be a sign that a child is getting sick.
The baby's fussiness made it difficult for her parents to get any sleep.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irritability - the state of being easily annoyed or angered
  • antonym: placidity - the state of being calm and not easily upset
