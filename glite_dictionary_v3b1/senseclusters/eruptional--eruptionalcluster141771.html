eruptional

adjective
skin eruptions
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrʌpʃənəl / (AmE), / ɪˈrʌpʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by eruptions on the skin, such as rashes or lesions.

Examples

Eruptional skin rash
The patient developed an eruptional rash after taking the new medication.
Doctors observed several eruptional lesions on his arms and legs.
An eruptional skin condition can be a sign of an allergic reaction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - free from skin eruptions or blemishes
  • synonym: eruption - a sudden appearance of a rash or lesion on the skin
