unlaid

adjective
not yet placed
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈleɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈleɪd / (BrE)
not having been laid, placed, or set in position

Examples

Unlaid carpet rolls
The new carpet rolls were still unlaid in the hallway.
Several bricks remained unlaid after the construction was paused for the day.
The foundation was ready, but the tiles were unlaid until the next morning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: laid - placed or set in position
  • synonym: unplaced - not put in a particular position
