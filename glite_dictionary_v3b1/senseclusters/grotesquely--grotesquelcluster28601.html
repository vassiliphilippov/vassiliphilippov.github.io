grotesquely

adverb
in a bizarre, unnatural manner
9.9 6.1
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊˈtɛskli / (AmE), / ɡrəʊˈtɛskli / (BrE)
In a way that is strange, distorted, or unnatural, often evoking a sense of absurdity or horror.

Examples

To smile grotesquely

The statue was grotesquely twisted out of shape after the fire.
The story was grotesquely exaggerated in the tabloid press.
