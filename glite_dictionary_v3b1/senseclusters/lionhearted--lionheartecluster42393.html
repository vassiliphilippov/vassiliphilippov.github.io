lionhearted

adjective
very brave; courageous
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪ.ənˌhɑr.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.ənˌhɑː.tɪd / (BrE)
Extremely brave and courageous; having the spirit or qualities of a lion.

Examples

A lionhearted hero

Her lionhearted actions inspired the entire rescue team to keep going.
The children's lionhearted defense of their friend left everyone in awe.
