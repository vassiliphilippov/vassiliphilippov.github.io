contraflow

noun
opposite-direction lane
6.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀêntr…ôÀåflo ä / (AmE), / Ààk…íntr…ôÀåfl…ô ä / (BrE)
a lane or route that allows vehicles or bicycles to travel in the opposite direction to the main flow of traffic, often as a permanent feature

Examples

A contraflow lane
The city installed a contraflow lane to help cyclists travel safely against the direction of traffic.
During roadworks, a temporary contraflow was set up to keep traffic moving in both directions.
Buses use the contraflow system to avoid congestion on the main road.

Synonyms

  • counterflow
    6.6 0.15/m
    counterflow describes any movement that goes the opposite way, while contraflow is a specific lane allowing vehicles to travel against traffic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bus lane - a lane reserved for buses
  • antonym: one-way street - a street where traffic is allowed in only one direction
