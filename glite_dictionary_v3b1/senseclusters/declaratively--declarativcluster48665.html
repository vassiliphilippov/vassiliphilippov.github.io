declaratively

adverb
in a stating manner
6.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˌklærəˈtɪvli / (AmE), / dɪˌklærəˈtɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that states facts or makes clear declarations

Examples

To speak declaratively
She spoke declaratively, leaving no doubt about her intentions.
The instructions were given declaratively so everyone understood what to do.
In programming, some languages allow you to write code declaratively rather than step by step.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assertively - in a confident and forceful way
  • antonym: interrogatively - in a questioning manner
