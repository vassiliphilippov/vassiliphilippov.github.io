Synonyms

  • clunk
    6.0 0.54/m
    clunk is a heavy, dull sound when a hard object hits another, while thumping describes that kind of heavy impact noise.
  • thud
    2.2 3.85/m
    thud is a dull heavy sound made by one heavy object striking, while thumping often refers to more forceful or repeated impacts.
  • thump
    3.0 3.09/m
    thump is a single heavy blow or sound from hitting something, while thumping suggests repeated or continuous heavy impacts.
  • clonk
    7.9 0.02/m

  • thunk
    8.9 0.41/m

  • whomp
    8.1 0.09/m
    whomp is a loud heavy hit often with sudden force or crash, while thumping means a steady heavy dull impact sound.
