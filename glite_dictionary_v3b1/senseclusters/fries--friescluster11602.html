fries

noun
deep-fried strips of potato
1.0
Pronunciation: / fraɪz / (AmE), / fraɪz / (BrE)
strips of potato that are deep-fried and typically served hot as a side dish or snack, especially in fast-food restaurants

Examples

A plate of fries
He ordered a large portion of fries and a soda for lunch.
The fries were crispy and golden brown, just the way I like them.
Would you like some fries with your burger?

Related Terms

  • synonym: french fries - deep-fried strips of potato, usually served as a side dish
