mental process

noun
a cognitive activity of the mind
4.2 0.60/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…õn.t…ôl Ààpr…ëÀê.s…õs / (AmE), / Ààm…õn.tl Ààpr…ô ä.ses / (BrE)
a cognitive operation or activity involving the mind, such as thinking, perceiving, remembering, or decision-making

Examples

A mental process of thinking
Learning a new language is an intricate mental process that requires practice and patience.
Meditation is said to slow down the mental process and enhance self-awareness.
Memory is a complex mental process involving various regions of the brain.
