ice

verb
apply ice to (an injury, etc.)
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: / aɪs / (AmE), / aɪs / (BrE)
to put ice or an ice pack on something to cool it, reduce swelling, or make it cold

Examples

To ice an injury
After twisting her ankle, she was told to ice it right away.
The coach advised the player to ice his sore knee after the game.
You should ice any swelling to prevent further injury.
She ices her drinks to keep them cold in the summer.
The roads iced up quickly after the sudden snowstorm.
They iced the rink overnight so it would be ready for skating.
