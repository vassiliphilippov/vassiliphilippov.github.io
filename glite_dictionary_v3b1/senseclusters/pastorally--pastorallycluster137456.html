pastorally

adverb
in a pastoral manner
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpæstərəli / (AmE), / ˈpɑːstərəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the duties, care, or spiritual guidance provided by a pastor or religious leader

Examples

Speak pastorally to people
The priest spoke pastorally to comfort the grieving family after the funeral.
She counseled the community pastorally, offering both support and practical advice.
The letter was written pastorally, focusing on encouragement and spiritual care.

  • synonym: spiritually - in a way relating to the spirit or religion
