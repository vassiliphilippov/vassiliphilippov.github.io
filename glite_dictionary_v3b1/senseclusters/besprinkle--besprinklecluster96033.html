besprinkle

verb
to sprinkle lightly with liquid
Pronunciation: / bɪˈsprɪŋkəl / (AmE), / bɪˈsprɪŋkl̩ /; / bɪˈsprɪŋkəl / (BrE)
to scatter drops of liquid or small particles lightly over something
