hellishness

noun
state of being extremely unpleasant or miserable
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈhɛlɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being extremely unpleasant, miserable, difficult, or terrifying, like hell.

Examples

The hellishness of fire
She never forgot the hellishness of being trapped in that building during the fire.
The hellishness of deadline week left him exhausted and on edge.
The hellishness of the conditions made survival almost impossible for the refugees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misery - a state or feeling of great unhappiness or suffering
