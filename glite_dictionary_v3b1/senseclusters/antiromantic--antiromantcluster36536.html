antiromantic

noun
person against romance
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiɹoʊˈmæntɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiɹəʊˈmæntɪk / (BrE)
a person who rejects or opposes romantic ideas, feelings, or conventions

Examples

An antiromantic person
As an antiromantic, he avoided sentimental films and refused to celebrate anniversaries.
The antiromantic in the group always questioned the value of grand gestures.
She was called an antiromantic because she disliked traditional love stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cynic - a person who believes people are motivated by self-interest
  • antonym: romantic - a person who believes in or pursues romance or idealized love
