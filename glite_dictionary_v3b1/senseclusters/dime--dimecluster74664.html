dime

noun
US coin worth 10 cents
Pronunciation: / daɪm / (AmE), / daɪm / (BrE)
A United States coin worth one tenth of a dollar (10 cents).

Examples

A dime coin
Back in the 1960s, a cup of coffee might cost just a dime.
He put a dime into the parking meter and waited for the light to change.
She found a shiny dime lying on the sidewalk.
