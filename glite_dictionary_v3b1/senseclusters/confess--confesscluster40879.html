Synonyms

  • own up to
    4.2 1.44/m
    own up to is a casual way to say you accept responsibility for a mistake, while confess is more formal and serious.
  • come clean
    4.1 2.46/m
    come clean is an informal phrase meaning to tell the truth about a hidden matter, while confess is often used for wrong acts.
  • admit
    2.0 109/m
    admit means acknowledge a fact often reluctantly, while confess usually implies admitting a moral wrong or crime.
  • make a clean breast of
    7.5 0.04/m
    make a clean breast of is a longer, old-fashioned phrase for fully revealing a fault, while confess is more common today.
