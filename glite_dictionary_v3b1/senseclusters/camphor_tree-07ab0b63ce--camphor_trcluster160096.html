camphor tree

noun
tree yielding camphor, native to East Asia
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkæm.fɚ ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈkam.fə ˌtriː / (BrE)
a large evergreen tree (Cinnamomum camphora) native to East Asia, known for its aromatic wood that yields camphor

Examples

A camphor tree
After the storm, several ancient camphor trees were found toppled along the old road.
The camphor tree in their garden filled the air with a distinct, spicy scent every summer.
Traditional medicines often use extracts from the camphor tree for their soothing properties.
