undatable

adjective
unsuitable for dating
8.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdeɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈdeɪtəbəl / (BrE)
(informal, slang) not considered suitable or attractive enough to date; unlikely to be chosen as a romantic partner.

Examples

Undatable person
On dating apps, many people quickly judge whether someone is undatable from their bio and photos.
He realized his jokes made him seem undatable after several matches stopped messaging him.
Calling a person undatable can be hurtful, so she tried to give gentler feedback instead.

Related Terms

  • antonym: datable - suitable or likely to be chosen as a romantic partner; opposite of 'undatable' in this slang sense
  • synonym: undateable - alternative spelling used with the same meaning; someone judged unsuitable for dating
