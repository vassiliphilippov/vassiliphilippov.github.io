on trial

adjective
being tested or evaluated
Pronunciation: / ɑn ˈtraɪəl / (AmE), / ɒn ˈtraɪəl / (BrE)
being tested or evaluated to determine quality, performance, or suitability for a particular purpose

Examples

On trial for use
The drug is still on trial and not yet approved for general use.
The new employee was hired on trial for three months before being offered a permanent position.
This software is currently on trial so users can try all its features before purchasing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: experiment - a test carried out to discover or demonstrate something
  • synonym: trial - a test of the performance or qualities of someone or something
