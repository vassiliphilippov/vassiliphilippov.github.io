trackway

noun
path of tracks
Pronunciation: / ˈtrækˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈtræk.weɪ / (BrE)
a path or series of marks left on the ground by the repeated movement of animals, people, or vehicles

Examples

Dinosaur trackway
Scientists discovered a fossilized trackway showing the movement of ancient reptiles across the mud.
The forest trackway was used by deer and other wildlife to reach the river.
A clear trackway led from the construction site to the main road.

  • synonym: trail - a path or track left by people, animals, or vehicles
