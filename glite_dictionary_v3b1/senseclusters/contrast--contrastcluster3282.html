contrast

noun
act of comparing to show difference
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.træst / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.trɑːst / (BrE)
the act of comparing two or more things in order to show differences between them

Examples

Contrast of ideas
The contrast between the two paintings made their differences clear to everyone in the gallery.
There was a sharp contrast between her cheerful tone and his somber expression.
Through careful contrast, the scientist identified unique characteristics of each species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comparison - the act of considering similarities or differences
  • antonym: similarity - the state of being similar
