zoophobia

noun
irrational fear of animals
Pronunciation: / zoʊ.əˈfoʊ.bi.ə / (AmE), / zəʊ.əˈfəʊ.bi.ə / (BrE)
an extreme or irrational fear of animals

Examples

Zoophobia fear animals
After being chased by a dog as a child, she developed zoophobia.
His zoophobia made it impossible for him to visit friends who had pets.
Therapy helped him gradually overcome his severe zoophobia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animal phobia - fear of animals (broader category)
  • synonym: phobia - an extreme or irrational fear of something
