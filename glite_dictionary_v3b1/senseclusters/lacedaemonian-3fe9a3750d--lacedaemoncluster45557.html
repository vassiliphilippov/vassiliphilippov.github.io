Lacedaemonian

noun
Spartan person or dialect
Pronunciation: / ˌlæsɪˌdiːməˈnoʊniən / (AmE), / ˌlæsɪˌdiːməˈnəʊniən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of ancient Sparta or the region of Laconia, or the Doric Greek dialect spoken there

Examples

Ancient Lacedaemonian soldier
The Lacedaemonian army was famous for its discipline and strength in battle.
Ancient historians often compared Lacedaemonians with Athenians in their writings.
Some inscriptions in the Lacedaemonian dialect have survived on old stone monuments.
