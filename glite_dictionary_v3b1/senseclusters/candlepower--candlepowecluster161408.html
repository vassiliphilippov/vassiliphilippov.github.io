candlepower

noun
measurement of luminous intensity
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkændlˌpaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈkændlˌpaʊə / (BrE)
a unit of measurement for luminous intensity, equal to the intensity of light emitted by one standard candle; measured in candelas

Examples

candlepower rating
Modern flashlights often advertise their candlepower to attract buyers.
Scientists measured the lamp’s candlepower to assess its suitability for the experiment.
The searchlight beam was rated at a million candlepower, illuminating the entire field.
