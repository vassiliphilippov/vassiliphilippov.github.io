persevere

verb
continue despite difficulty
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜrsəˈvɪr / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsɪˈvɪə / (BrE)
to continue trying to achieve something despite difficulties or discouragement

Examples

To persevere through problems
Even when faced with setbacks, she chose to persevere and eventually achieved her goals.
He had to persevere through months of physical therapy after the accident.
If you persevere, you will eventually master this challenging subject.

Related Terms

  • antonym: give up - to cease making an effort; stop trying
  • synonym: persist - to continue firmly in an opinion or course of action
