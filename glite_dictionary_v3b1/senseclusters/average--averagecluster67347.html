average

adjective
of ordinary or standard quality
Pronunciation: / ˈæv.ɚ.ɪdʒ /; / ˈævɚɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈæv.ər.ɪdʒ /; / ˈævərɪdʒ / (BrE)
ordinary or typical; not special or exceptional

Examples

An average student
Despite his average performance, he managed to pass the class.
Her singing was average, lacking the spark that makes a star.
The restaurant's food was just average, nothing to write home about.
He lives an average life, working nine to five and spending weekends with his family.
Most of the students scored at an average level on the final exam.
The average person drinks about eight cups of water per day.
His test scores were average compared to the rest of the class.
She is of average build for someone her age.
The city has an average temperature of 15°C in spring.
