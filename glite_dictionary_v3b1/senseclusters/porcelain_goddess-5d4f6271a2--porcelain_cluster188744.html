porcelain goddess

noun
toilet (euphemism); very beautiful woman
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrs(ə)lɪn ˈɡɑdəs / (AmE), / ˈpɔːsəlɪn ˈɡɒdəs / (BrE)
a humorous or euphemistic term for a toilet, especially when referring to vomiting into it; sometimes also used to describe a woman who is exceptionally beautiful and delicate

Examples

Worshipping the porcelain goddess
After a night of heavy drinking, he spent hours worshipping the porcelain goddess.
He joked that he needed to visit the porcelain goddess after eating too much.
She wore a flowing white dress and looked like a porcelain goddess at the event.
