light company

noun
company providing electricity
2.8
Pronunciation: / laɪt ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / laɪt ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company or public utility that supplies electricity, especially for lighting buildings, streets, etc.

Examples

The light company supplies electricity
During the blackout, the light company worked around the clock to restore electricity to the city.
She called the light company to set up a new account at her apartment.
The light company sent someone out to repair the downed power lines after the storm.
