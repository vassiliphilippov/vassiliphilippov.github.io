fatly

adverb
generously or richly
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfætli / (AmE), / ˈfætli / (BrE)
in a generous or abundant manner, especially regarding payment, reward, or provision

Examples

Fatly paid
The employees were fatly compensated for their hard work during the busy season.
He was fatly rewarded for his innovative ideas that boosted the company's profits.
The author was fatly paid for the rights to her bestselling novel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generously - in a way that shows a readiness to give more of something
  • synonym: richly - in a plentiful or abundant way
  • antonym: scantily - in a way that is barely sufficient or meager
