rifampin

noun
antibiotic drug for tuberculosis
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfæmpɪn / (AmE), / rɪˈfæmpɪn / (BrE)
an antibiotic drug used to treat tuberculosis and other bacterial infections

Examples

Rifampin antibiotic drug
Rifampin may interact with other drugs, so be sure to inform your healthcare provider about all medications you are taking.
Some bacteria can develop resistance to rifampin if the medication is not taken properly.
The doctor prescribed rifampin to help treat the patient's tuberculosis infection.
