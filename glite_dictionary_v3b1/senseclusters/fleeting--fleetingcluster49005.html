fleeting

adjective
lasting for a very short time
Pronunciation: / ˈfliːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfliːtɪŋ / (BrE)
lasting for only a very short time; brief and passing quickly

Examples

A fleeting moment
Happiness can sometimes feel fleeting when life gets overwhelming.
She caught a fleeting glimpse of her friend in the crowd before losing sight of him.
The beauty of the sunset was fleeting, disappearing within minutes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enduring - lasting or continuing for a long time
  • synonym: momentary - lasting for a very short time
