ihram

noun
pilgrimage clothing
Pronunciation: / ɪhˈrɑːm / (AmE), / ɪˈhrɑːm / (BrE)
a sacred state and special white clothing worn by Muslims during the pilgrimage to Mecca

Examples

The ihram clothing
Before entering Mecca, every pilgrim must put on the ihram and follow special rules.
The white garments of ihram symbolize purity and equality among all pilgrims.
Wearing ihram marks the beginning of the sacred rituals of the Hajj.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hajj - the annual Muslim pilgrimage to Mecca
  • synonym: pilgrimage - a journey to a sacred place for religious reasons
