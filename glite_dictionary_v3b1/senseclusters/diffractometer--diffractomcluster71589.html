diffractometer

noun
instrument for measuring diffraction
Pronunciation: / dɪˌfræktəˈmiːtər / (AmE), / dɪˌfræktˈɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure how beams of light or particles are spread out when they pass through a material

Examples

A diffractometer instrument
The laboratory installed a new diffractometer to improve their analysis of crystal samples.
Using a diffractometer, researchers can determine the structure of complex materials.
The technician carefully calibrated the diffractometer before starting the experiment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: microscope - an instrument used to see objects that are too small for the naked eye
  • synonym: spectrometer - an instrument for measuring properties of light over a specific portion of the spectrum
