hemicrania

noun
severe one-sided headache
7.3 9.1
Pronunciation: /he.mɪ.ˈkreɪ.niː.ə/ /; / /he.mə.ˈkreɪ.niː.ə/ (AmE), /he.mɪ.ˈkreɪ.niː.ə/ /; / /he.mə.ˈkreɪ.niː.ə/ (BrE)
A severe recurring vascular headache, typically affecting one side of the head and occurring more frequently in women than men.

Examples

Severe hemicrania pain
Her episodes of hemicrania made it difficult for her to concentrate at work.
She was diagnosed with hemicrania after experiencing repeated severe headaches on one side of her head.
The treatment for hemicrania often differs from that of other types of headaches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: migraine - a recurrent throbbing headache causing pain on one side of the head
