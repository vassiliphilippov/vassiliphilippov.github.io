boop

noun
short electronic sound
8.4 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / buÀêp / (AmE), / buÀêp / (BrE)
a short, high-pitched electronic or mechanical sound made by a device or toy

Examples

A boop from the microwave

Every time you press the button, the toy car responds with a cheerful boop.
She heard a quick boop from her phone when the message arrived.
