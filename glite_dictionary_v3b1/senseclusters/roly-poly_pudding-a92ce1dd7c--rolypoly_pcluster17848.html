roly-poly pudding

noun
British dessert of rolled suet pastry with jam or fruit
Pronunciation: / ˌroʊli ˈpoʊli ˈpʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌrəʊli ˈpəʊli ˈpʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
A traditional British dessert made from suet pastry spread with jam or fruit, rolled up, and baked or steamed.

Examples

Traditional roly-poly pudding
My grandmother always made roly-poly pudding for Sunday lunch when I was a child.
The roly-poly pudding was filled with raspberry jam and served warm with custard.
You can steam or bake roly-poly pudding depending on your preference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jam roll - a roll of suet pastry spread with jam and steamed or baked
