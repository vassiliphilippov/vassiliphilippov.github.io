pansexuality

noun
attraction to all genders
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpænˌsɛkʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌpænˌsɛkʃuˈælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being sexually or romantically attracted to people regardless of their gender

Examples

Pansexuality identity
She openly discussed her pansexuality with her friends.
Many people are learning more about pansexuality and what it means in modern society.
He explained that pansexuality is about attraction to people regardless of gender identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bisexuality - sexual or romantic attraction to both men and women
  • antonym: heterosexuality - sexual or romantic attraction to people of the opposite gender
  • antonym: homosexuality - sexual or romantic attraction to people of the same gender
