angular vein

noun
a vein at the side of the nose, joins facial vein
9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈæŋ.gjə.lər veɪn/ (AmE), /ˈæŋ.gjə.lə veɪn/ (BrE)
a short vein located near the root of the nose, formed by the union of the supraorbital and supratrochlear veins, which continues as the facial vein

Examples

The angular vein near nose
Blood from the forehead drains into the angular vein before reaching the facial vein.
The angular vein is vulnerable to injury during facial surgery.
The doctor explained how the angular vein connects the supraorbital and supratrochlear veins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facial vein - a vein receiving blood from the angular vein and draining into the internal jugular vein
