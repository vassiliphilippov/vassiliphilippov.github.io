unartful

adjective
lacking skill
7.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɑrtfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈɑːtfəl / (BrE)
lacking skill, cleverness, or artfulness in manner or execution

Examples

An unartful explanation
Her unartful explanation left everyone more confused than before.
The painting was charming despite its unartful brushstrokes and simple design.
He made an unartful attempt to fix the computer, which only made things worse.
