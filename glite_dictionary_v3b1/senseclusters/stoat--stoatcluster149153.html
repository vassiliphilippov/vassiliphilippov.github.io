stoat

noun
small mammal related to the weasel
9.9
Pronunciation: / stoʊt / (AmE), / stəʊt / (BrE)
a small carnivorous mammal, related to the weasel, with a brown summer coat and a black-tipped tail

Examples

A stoat animal
A stoat darted across the path, its brown coat blending with the dry grass.
In summer, the stoat has brown fur, but it turns white in winter in colder climates.
The stoat is often mistaken for a weasel because of their similar appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ermine - stoat in its white winter coat
  • synonym: weasel - small carnivorous mammal closely related to the stoat
