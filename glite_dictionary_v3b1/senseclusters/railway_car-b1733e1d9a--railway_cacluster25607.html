railway car

noun
vehicle running on railway tracks
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪl.weɪ kɑr / (AmE), / ˈreɪl.weɪ kɑː / (BrE)
A vehicle with wheels that runs on railway tracks, used for transporting passengers or goods.

Examples

A railway car wheel
Maintenance workers checked each railway car for damage after the day’s journeys.
Several passengers sat quietly in the railway car as the train sped through the countryside.
The freight railway car was loaded with logs for delivery to the mill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carriage - passenger railway car (especially in British English)
  • synonym: railroad car - vehicle running on railway tracks (American English)
