paleoecologist

noun
ancient ecosystem scientist
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪlioʊiˈkɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌpeɪlɪəʊiˈkɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies ancient ecosystems and how they functioned in the past

Examples

The paleoecologist studies fossils
The paleoecologist examined sediment layers to understand the climate of the Ice Age.
As a paleoecologist, she specializes in reconstructing ancient forests from fossil evidence.
A team led by a paleoecologist discovered how extinct animals shaped prehistoric landscapes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecologist - a scientist who studies the relationships between living things and their environment
  • synonym: paleontologist - a scientist who studies fossils and ancient life forms
