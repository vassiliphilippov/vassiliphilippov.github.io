unmistakably

adverb
without possibility of mistake
8.2 3.3
Pronunciation: /ʌn.mɪ.ˈsteɪ.kə.bliː/ (AmE), /ʌn.mɪ.ˈsteɪ.kə.bliː/ (BrE)
In a way that leaves no doubt; so clearly or obviously as to be impossible to mistake for anything else.

Examples

To speak unmistakably clearly
Her voice was unmistakably excited when she shared the news.
The aroma from the kitchen was unmistakably that of freshly baked bread.
The building was unmistakably of Victorian design, with ornate windows and brickwork.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ambiguously - in a way that is unclear or open to more than one interpretation
  • synonym: distinctly - in a way that is clear and unmistakable
