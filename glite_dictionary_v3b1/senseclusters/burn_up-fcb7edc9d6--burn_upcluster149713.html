burn up

verb
use up energy or fuel through activity
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: / bɝn ʌp / (AmE), / bɜːn ʌp / (BrE)
to use energy, especially as a result of physical activity; to expend (calories, fuel, etc.) through activity or use

Examples

To burn up calories
After climbing the hill, I could feel my body burning up energy quickly.
Intense cardio workouts can help you burn up more calories.
You burn up a lot of fuel walking several miles each day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expend - to use up (a resource, such as energy, time, or money)
