yarborough

noun
low bridge hand
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑːrbəroʊ / (AmE), / ˈjɑːbərə / (BrE)
a hand in bridge or whist containing no card higher than a nine

Examples

A yarborough hand
She was disappointed to be dealt a yarborough in the first round.
Getting a yarborough makes it almost impossible to win the game.
He laughed when he realized his entire hand was a yarborough.

Related Terms
