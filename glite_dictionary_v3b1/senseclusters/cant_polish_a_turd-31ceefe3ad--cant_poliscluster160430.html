(informal) Used to express that it is impossible to make something fundamentally bad or flawed appear good or of high quality, no matter how much effort is applied.

Examples

You can't polish a turd
Even with a new coat of paint, you can't polish a turd—the car is still in terrible shape.
He tried to edit the report, but realized you can't polish a turd when the data is this bad.
They spent hours redesigning the logo, forgetting that you can't polish a turd if the original idea is flawed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: you can't make a silk purse out of a sow's ear - it is impossible to turn something inferior into something of value
