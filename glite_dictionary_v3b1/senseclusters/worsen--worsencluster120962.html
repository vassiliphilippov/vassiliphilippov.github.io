worsen

verb
become or make worse
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrsən /; / ˈwɜːrsən / (AmE), / ˈwɜːsən / (BrE)
to make something worse or to become worse

Examples

To worsen the problem
Adding more debt will only worsen the company’s financial situation.
If tensions worsen, there could be further unrest in the city.
The patient's symptoms began to worsen after he stopped his medication.
Doctors fear the patient's condition may worsen overnight.
Heavy rainfall will worsen the flooding in some areas.
If you don't address the problem now, it will only worsen with time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggravate - to make a problem, injury, or situation worse
  • synonym: deteriorate - to become progressively worse
  • antonym: improve - to make or become better
