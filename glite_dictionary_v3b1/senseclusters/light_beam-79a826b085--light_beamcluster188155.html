light beam

noun
narrow column of light
5.6 1.9
Pronunciation: /laɪt biːm/ (AmE), /laɪt biːm/ (BrE)
a narrow column or stream of light, especially one that is visible as it passes through the air

Examples

A light beam shines
A single light beam pierced the darkness, illuminating the old path through the trees.
She adjusted the mirror so the light beam reflected toward the ceiling.
The lighthouse sent a rotating light beam across the sea to guide passing ships.
