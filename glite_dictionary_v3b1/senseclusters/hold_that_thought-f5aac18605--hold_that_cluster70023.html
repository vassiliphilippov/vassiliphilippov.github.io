hold that thought

verb
ask to pause an idea for later
Pronunciation: / hoʊld ðæt θɔt / (AmE), / həʊld ðæt θɔːt / (BrE)
used to ask someone to temporarily pause their idea or comment until the discussion can be resumed

Examples

Please hold that thought
Before I answer, let me hold that thought so I can get my notes.
I'm really interested in what you have to say, but could you hold that thought while I take this call?
She asked him to hold that thought until after the meeting, promising to return to the topic.
