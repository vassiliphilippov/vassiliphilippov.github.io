callosal

adjective
of the corpus callosum
Pronunciation: / kəˈloʊsəl / (AmE), / kəˈləʊsəl / (BrE)
relating to the corpus callosum, the structure connecting the two hemispheres of the brain

Examples

Callosal lesion
The neurologist noted a callosal lesion on the patient's brain scan.
Researchers are studying callosal development in children with learning difficulties.
Damage to callosal fibers can affect communication between the brain's hemispheres.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corpus callosum - the large band of nerve fibers connecting the two brain hemispheres
