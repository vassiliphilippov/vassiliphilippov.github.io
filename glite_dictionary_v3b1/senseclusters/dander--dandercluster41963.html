dander

noun
anger; irritation (esp. in idioms)
Pronunciation: / ˈdændɚ / (AmE), / ˈdændə / (BrE)
A feeling or state of anger or irritation, especially as expressed in the phrase 'get one's dander up'.

Examples

Dander up quickly
Don't let them get your dander up—they're just teasing you.
Her dander was clearly up after hearing the unfair criticism.
Whenever he feels slighted, his dander rises quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anger - a strong feeling of displeasure or hostility
  • antonym: calm - the absence of anger or agitation
