nonviral

adjective
not involving viruses
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈvaɪrəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈvaɪrəl / (BrE)
not caused by, related to, or involving a virus

Examples

Nonviral infection
The laboratory confirmed that the illness was nonviral in origin.
Doctors ruled out a nonviral cause after extensive testing.
This medication is used to treat nonviral infections such as those caused by bacteria.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bacterial - caused by or relating to bacteria
  • antonym: viral - caused by or relating to a virus
