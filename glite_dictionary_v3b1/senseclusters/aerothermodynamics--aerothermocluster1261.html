aerothermodynamics

noun
heat and air effects
Pronunciation: / ˌɛroʊˌθɜrmoʊdaɪˈnæmɪks / (AmE), / ˌeərəʊˌθɜːməʊdaɪˈnæmɪks / (BrE)
the study of how heat and air movement together affect objects, especially at high speeds

Examples

Aerothermodynamics study
Understanding aerothermodynamics is essential for designing safe and efficient spacecraft.
The team conducted experiments in aerothermodynamics to improve the heat shields on re-entry vehicles.
Advances in aerothermodynamics have made high-speed flight more reliable and safer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aerodynamics - the study of the movement of air and its effects on objects
  • synonym: thermodynamics - the study of heat and its relation to energy and work
