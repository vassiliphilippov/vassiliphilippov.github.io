girl

noun
female child or young woman
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɜrl /; / ɡɝl / (AmE), / ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a female child or young woman who is not yet an adult (sometimes used for a daughter)

Examples

A little girl
As a little girl, she loved reading fairy tales.
The girls played soccer together in the field after school.
Their first child was a beautiful girl with curly hair.
The girl in the red dress won the spelling bee last year.
Their neighbors have a little girl who loves to draw with chalk on the sidewalk.
When I was a girl, I wanted to be an astronaut.
After dinner, the girl helped her mother clean up the kitchen.
She took her girl to the park every Saturday afternoon.
The couple were overjoyed when their first girl was born.
