montage

verb
to combine images
5.8
Pronunciation: /mɑːn.ˈtɑːʒ/ (AmE), /mɒn.ˈtɑːʒ/ (BrE)
to create a composite image or sequence by combining several separate elements, such as photos or video clips

Examples

To montage pictures
She spent hours montaging photos for her art project.
The filmmaker is montaging different scenes to create a dramatic effect.
He enjoys montaging magazine cutouts to make creative posters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assemble - to put together separate parts to form a whole
  • synonym: edit - to prepare material by selecting and arranging parts
  • antonym: separate - to keep or set apart; not combine
