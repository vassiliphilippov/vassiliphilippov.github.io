undissipated

adjective
still present
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈdɪsɪˌpeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈdɪsɪpeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not dispersed, scattered, or lost; still present or retained.

Examples

Undissipated heat
The heat remained undissipated inside the insulated container for several hours.
Some of the excitement from the announcement was still undissipated the next morning.
In a perfect system, energy would stay undissipated and never be lost as heat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dissipated - scattered or lost, especially energy or resources
  • synonym: retained - kept or held and not lost
