sissy

adjective
showing traditionally feminine qualities (disapproving)
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪsi / (AmE), / ˈsɪsi / (BrE)
(of a boy or man) having characteristics regarded as traditionally feminine; lacking in traditional masculinity.

Examples

Sissy behavior
He was teased by his friends for his sissy mannerisms and voice.
Some boys were bullied at school for being too sissy to play football.
Wearing pink was once unfairly considered a sissy thing for boys to do.

Related Terms

  • synonym: effeminate - having characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly
  • antonym: manly - having qualities traditionally associated with men
