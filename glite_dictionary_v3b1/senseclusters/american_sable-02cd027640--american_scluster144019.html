American sable

noun
a rabbit breed prized for its fur
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈseɪ.bəl / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈseɪ.bəl / (BrE)
a breed of domesticated rabbit valued for its soft, dark brown fur

Examples

The American sable rabbit
Many breeders raise American sable rabbits due to the high demand for their pelts.
She admired the rich color of the American sable in the animal enclosure.
The American sable is often exhibited at rabbit shows for its luxurious fur.
