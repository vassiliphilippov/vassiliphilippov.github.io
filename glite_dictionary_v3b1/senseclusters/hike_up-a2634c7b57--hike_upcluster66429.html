hike up

verb
to climb up a hill or mountain
4.1
Pronunciation: / haɪk ʌp / (AmE), / haɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to walk or climb up a hill, mountain, or slope, often for exercise or enjoyment

Examples

To hike up a hill

She loves to hike up mountains whenever she travels to new countries.
We decided to hike up the trail early in the morning to avoid the heat.
