costochondritis

noun
inflammation of rib cartilage
Pronunciation: / koʊstəʊˌkɑnˈdraɪtɪs / (AmE), / kɒstəʊkɒnˈdraɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the cartilage that connects a rib to the breastbone, causing localized chest pain

Examples

Costochondritis chest pain
Many people with costochondritis experience tenderness when pressing on their chest.
Physical therapy and anti-inflammatory medication are often recommended for managing costochondritis.
The doctor diagnosed his chest pain as costochondritis after ruling out heart problems.
