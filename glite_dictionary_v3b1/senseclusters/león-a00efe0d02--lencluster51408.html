León

proper noun
city in Guanajuato, Mexico
Pronunciation: / leɪˈoʊn / (AmE), / leɪˈɒn / (BrE)
A city in central Mexico, in the state of Guanajuato, known for its leather industry and as a major economic center.

Examples

León leather city
León has grown rapidly due to its thriving shoe manufacturing industry.
The climate in León is warm and dry for most of the year.
They visited León to attend the annual leather fair held in the city.
