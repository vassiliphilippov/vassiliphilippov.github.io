gluelike

adjective
sticky or adhesive
Pronunciation: / ˈɡluˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡluːˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a sticky or adhesive quality similar to glue

Examples

Gluelike syrup
The syrup had a gluelike consistency that made it hard to pour.
After drying, the paint left a gluelike residue on the surface.
The sap from the tree was so gluelike that it stuck to my fingers for hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adhesive - able to stick to something
  • antonym: slippery - difficult to hold or stand on because it is smooth or wet
  • synonym: sticky - having a surface that sticks easily to things
