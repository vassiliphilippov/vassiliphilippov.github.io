milkfish

noun
tropical food fish
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a silvery tropical fish of the Indo-Pacific, widely farmed for food in Southeast Asia

Examples

A milkfish swims
The milkfish is a staple in many Southeast Asian cuisines.
Farmers raise milkfish in coastal ponds for both local markets and export.
Grilled milkfish is a popular dish served at family gatherings in the Philippines.
