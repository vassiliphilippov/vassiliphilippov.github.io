nerdy

adjective
like a stereotypical nerd
Pronunciation: / ˈnɜrdi / (AmE), / ˈnɜːdi / (BrE)
having qualities or interests typical of a stereotypical nerd, such as being very interested in academic or technical subjects, or appearing unfashionable or socially awkward
