harmonist

noun
musical harmony expert
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrmənɪst / (AmE), / ˈhɑːmənɪst / (BrE)
a person who is skilled in creating or arranging musical harmony, or who supports the use of harmony in music

Examples

Skilled harmonist in music
The talented harmonist transformed the simple melody into a rich choral arrangement.
As a skilled harmonist, she helped the band achieve a more balanced sound.
Every choir needs a harmonist to ensure the voices blend beautifully.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composer - a person who writes music
