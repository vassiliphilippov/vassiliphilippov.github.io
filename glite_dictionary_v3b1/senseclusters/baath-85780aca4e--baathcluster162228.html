Baath

noun
member of Baath party
Pronunciation: / bɑːθ / (AmE), / bɑːθ / (BrE)
a supporter or member of the Baath political party or movement, especially in certain Middle Eastern countries

Examples

A Baath supporter
He was arrested as a suspected Baath after the street protest last month.
The new government banned all former Baath members from holding public office.
Many Baath supporters attended the rally in the capital city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Baathist - a member or supporter of the Baath party
  • antonym: opponent - someone who opposes or is against the Baath party
