hulking

adjective
large and heavy; imposing
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌlkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʌlkɪŋ / (BrE)
Large and heavy in a way that seems awkward or imposing.

Examples

A hulking truck
A hulking truck blocked the entire lane during rush hour.
Despite his hulking appearance, he was surprisingly gentle.
The old barn was a hulking structure at the edge of the field.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tiny - very small in size
