tax obligation

noun
legal duty to pay taxes
1.0
Pronunciation: / tæks ˌɑːbləˈɡeɪʃn̩ / (AmE), / tæks ˌɒblɪˈɡeɪʃn̩ / (BrE)
a legal responsibility to pay taxes owed to a government or authority

Examples

Tax obligations of businesses
Businesses must be aware of their tax obligations in every country where they operate.
Failure to report income can result in serious tax obligations with penalties and interest.
She hired an accountant to help organize her tax obligations before the deadline.

Related Terms
