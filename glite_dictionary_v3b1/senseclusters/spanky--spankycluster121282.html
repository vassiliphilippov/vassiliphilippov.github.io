spanky

adjective
naughty or cheeky
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspæŋki / (AmE), / ˈspæŋki / (BrE)
behaving in a naughty, cheeky, or impertinent way, often deserving mild punishment or rebuke

Examples

Spanky child
The spanky child kept making faces at his older sister during dinner.
Her spanky attitude often got her into trouble with her teachers.
He gave a spanky grin after playing a harmless prank on his friend.

Related Terms

  • antonym: well-behaved - acting in a polite and proper manner
