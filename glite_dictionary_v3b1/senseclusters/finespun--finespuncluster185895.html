finespun

adjective
overly subtle or delicately detailed
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪnˌspʌn / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnˌspʌn / (BrE)
extremely delicate, subtle, or refined, especially in a way that is overly detailed or complicated
