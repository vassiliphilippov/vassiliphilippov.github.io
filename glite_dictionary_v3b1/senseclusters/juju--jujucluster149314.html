juju

noun
magical power or charm
9.9 8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒuː.dʒuː/ /; / /ˈdʒuː.dʒu/ (AmE), /ˈdʒuː.dʒu/ /; / /ˈdʒuː.dʒuː/ (BrE)
a magical power or charm, especially as believed in West African cultures; can also refer to an object thought to have such power or bring luck

Examples

Powerful juju charm
He wore an amulet said to possess powerful juju.
Some believe the team's recent wins are due to good juju brought by a lucky mascot.
The villagers feared the juju said to be protecting the sacred grove.
Some say the team’s unexpected victory was thanks to a lucky juju hidden in the locker room.
The old trader wore a juju around his neck for protection during long journeys.
Villagers believed the mysterious stone was a powerful juju that brought prosperity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amulet - an ornament or charm worn for protection against evil
  • degree lower: luck - success or failure apparently brought by chance rather than through one's own actions
  • synonym: mojo - magical power or charm, especially as believed to have supernatural influence
  • synonym: talisman - an object thought to have magical powers
