serration

noun
the state of being serrated
6.1 6.1
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /sə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the state or feature of having a notched or saw-toothed edge, or a row of tooth-like projections (such as on a knife or saw blade)
