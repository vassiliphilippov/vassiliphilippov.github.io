analysis of variance

noun
statistical method for comparing group means
Pronunciation: / əˈnæl.ə.sɪs əv ˈvɛr.i.əns / (AmE), / əˈnæl.ɪ.sɪs əv ˈveə.ri.əns / (BrE)
a statistical method used to compare the means of two or more groups to determine if there are significant differences among them

Examples

Analysis of variance test
After performing an analysis of variance, the researchers found a notable difference in test scores between the classes.
Learning how to conduct an analysis of variance is essential for many psychology students.
The scientist used analysis of variance to determine if the treatment groups differed significantly.
