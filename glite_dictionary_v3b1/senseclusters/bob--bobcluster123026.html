bob

verb
cut hair in the style of a bob
3.2 4.2
Pronunciation: /bɑːb/ /; / /bɔːb/ (AmE), /bɒb/ (BrE)
to cut hair in the style of a bob, a short haircut where the hair is typically cut straight around the head at about jaw-level, often with a fringe (bangs) at the front.

Examples

To bob hair
After years with long hair, Lisa is thinking about bobbing it this summer.
Many young women bobbed their hair in the 1920s as a statement of independence.
She decided to bob her hair for a fresh new look.
