Zeitgeist

noun
spirit or mood of a time period
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtsaɪtˌgaɪst / (AmE), / ˈtsaɪtɡaɪst / (BrE)
the defining spirit or mood of a particular period of history as shown by the ideas and beliefs of the time

Examples

The Zeitgeist of era
He argued that every era is shaped by its unique Zeitgeist.
Many artists attempt to express the Zeitgeist through their creative works.
The movie perfectly captures the Zeitgeist of the early 1980s, with its bold optimism and distinctive style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spirit of the age - the general cultural, intellectual, ethical, and political climate within a nation or group in a particular era
