underactivity

noun
reduced activity
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌʌndərækˈtɪvəti / (BrE)
a state in which something is less active than normal or expected

Examples

Thyroid underactivity
Doctors may check for thyroid underactivity if a patient feels unusually tired.
The underactivity of certain brain regions can affect mood and behavior.
Prolonged underactivity in children may be a sign of a medical condition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypoactivity - a condition of being less active than usual
  • antonym: overactivity - a state of being more active than normal
