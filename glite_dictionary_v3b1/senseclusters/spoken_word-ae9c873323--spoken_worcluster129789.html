spoken word

noun
a word that is spoken aloud
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈspoʊkən wɝd / (AmE), / ˈspəʊkən wɜːd / (BrE)
a word that is pronounced or uttered aloud, as opposed to being written or thought

Examples

Spoken word aloud
