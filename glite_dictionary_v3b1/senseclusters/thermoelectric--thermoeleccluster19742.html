thermoelectric

adjective
relating to thermoelectricity or heat-electric energy conversion
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜr.moʊ.ɪˈlɛk.trɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɜː.məʊ.ɪˈlɛk.trɪk / (BrE)
involving or relating to the direct conversion of heat into electrical energy, or vice versa, by means of thermoelectricity

Examples

Thermoelectric materials
Scientists are developing new thermoelectric materials to improve energy efficiency.
She explained the thermoelectric effect to her students using a simple experiment.
The thermoelectric device converted the waste heat into useful electricity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thermoelectricity - electricity produced by a temperature difference
