thermoelectric

adjective
relating to thermoelectricity or heat-electric energy conversion
6.3 7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜr.moʊ.ɪˈlɛk.trɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɜː.məʊ.ɪˈlɛk.trɪk / (BrE)

Examples
