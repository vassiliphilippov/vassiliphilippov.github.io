earthing

noun
body grounding for health
7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈɜːr.θɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈɜː.θɪŋ/ (BrE)
the practice of making direct physical contact between the body and the Earth's surface (for example by walking barefoot) with the intention of improving health or well-being

Examples

Earthing barefoot
Many people practice earthing by walking barefoot on grass to relieve stress and improve sleep.
After reading about grounding benefits, she tried earthing for a week and felt calmer.
The wellness podcast featured an expert explaining how earthing might reduce inflammation, though evidence is limited.
