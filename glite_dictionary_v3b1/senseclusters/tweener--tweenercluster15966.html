tweener

noun
person between stages
Pronunciation: / ˈtwiːnər / (AmE), / ˈtwiːnə / (BrE)
a person or thing that is intermediate between two recognized categories, especially between childhood and adolescence

Examples

A tweener age
At twelve, she felt like a tweener—not a child, but not a teenager yet.
The movie is popular among tweeners who are looking for stories about growing up.
He is a tweener in the basketball world, not quite a guard and not quite a forward.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: child - a young person who is not yet a teenager
  • synonym: preteen - a child who is not yet a teenager, usually aged 9 to 12
  • degree higher: teenager - a person aged between 13 and 19 years old
