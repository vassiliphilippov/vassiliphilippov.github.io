European bog asphodel

noun
yellow bog plant of western Europe
9.9
Pronunciation: / jʊˈroʊpiən bɑɡ ˈæsfəˌdɛl / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən bɒɡ ˈæsfədɛl / (BrE)
A yellow-flowered perennial plant (Narthecium ossifragum) of wet boggy areas in western Europe, from Scandinavia to northern Spain and Portugal.
