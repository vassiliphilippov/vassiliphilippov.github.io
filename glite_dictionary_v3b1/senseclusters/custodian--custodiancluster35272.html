custodian

noun
person in charge of maintenance or care
7.3 3.2
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈstoʊ.diː.ən/ (AmE), /kə.ˈstəʊ.diː.ən/ (BrE)
a person who is responsible for taking care of and protecting property, buildings, animals, or important assets (including digital data or records)

Examples

The custodian cleans
As a custodian, she takes pride in keeping the museum spotless and secure.
The custodian of the animal shelter ensured all the animals were well cared for.
The custodian unlocked the doors early every morning before the students arrived.
The company appointed a custodian to manage access to its patient records and backups.
During the audit, the IT team asked the custodian to provide logs showing who accessed the database.
Good custodians ensure data is stored securely and that backup procedures are regularly tested.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caretaker - a person employed to look after a building or someone else's property
  • synonym: data manager - a person who oversees the storage and organisation of data
  • synonym: data steward - a person responsible for data quality and governance policies
  • synonym: janitor - a person employed to clean and maintain a building
