Burmese

adjective
relating to Myanmar or its people
Pronunciation: / bɜrˈmiz / (AmE), / bɜːˈmiːz / (BrE)
of or relating to Myanmar (formerly Burma), its people, language, or culture.

Examples

Burmese culture
Many Burmese traditions celebrate the New Year with a water festival.
She cooked a delicious Burmese curry for dinner.
The Burmese language has a unique script and tone system.
