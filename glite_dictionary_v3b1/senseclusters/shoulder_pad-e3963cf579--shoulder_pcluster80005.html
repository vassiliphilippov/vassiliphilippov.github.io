shoulder pad

noun
protective pad for the shoulder (sports)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊldər pæd / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə pæd / (BrE)
a protective piece of equipment consisting of a hard, rounded pad worn over the shoulder, especially by football or hockey players, to prevent injury

Examples

Football shoulder pads
Coaches emphasize the importance of wearing proper shoulder pads during every practice and game.
Modern shoulder pads are designed to absorb impact and protect players from serious injury.
The football player put on his shoulder pad before heading out onto the field.
