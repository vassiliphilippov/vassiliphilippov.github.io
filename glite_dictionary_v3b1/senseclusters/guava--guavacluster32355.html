guava

noun
tropical fruit with yellow skin and pulp
Pronunciation: / ˈɡwɑvə / (AmE), / ˈɡwɑːvə / (BrE)
A tropical fruit with yellow skin and pink or white pulp, eaten fresh or used to make jellies and preserves.

Examples

Fresh guava fruit
She sliced a ripe guava for breakfast and sprinkled it with a pinch of salt.
The market was filled with the sweet aroma of fresh guavas.
They used guava to make a delicious homemade jelly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: papaya - A tropical fruit with orange-colored flesh and black seeds
