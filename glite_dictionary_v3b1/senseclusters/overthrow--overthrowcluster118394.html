overthrow

noun
removal of a ruler or government by force
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌθroʊ / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌθrəʊ / (BrE)
the removal of a ruler or government, especially by force

Examples

The overthrow of a leader
Civil unrest led to the overthrow of the monarchy.
Several leaders fled the country after the sudden overthrow.
The military staged an overthrow of the corrupt regime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coup - a sudden, violent, and illegal seizure of power from a government
  • antonym: restoration - the return of a ruler or regime to power after being overthrown
  • synonym: revolution - forcible overthrow of a government or social order
