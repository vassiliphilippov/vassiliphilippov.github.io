southwesterly

noun
wind from southwest
Pronunciation: / ˌsaʊθˈwɛstərli / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈwɛstəli / (BrE)
a wind that blows from the southwest direction

Examples

A southwesterly blows
The southwesterly brought cooler air to the coast after a hot day.
Fishermen waited for the southwesterly to calm before heading out to sea.
A strong southwesterly swept across the fields, bending the tall grass.

Related Terms

  • antonym: northwesterly - a wind that blows from the northwest direction
  • synonym: southerly - a wind that blows from the south direction
