Death

proper noun
personification of death
Pronunciation: / dɛθ / (AmE), / dɛθ / (BrE)
(in stories, art, and literature) the personification of death, often depicted as a figure representing the end of life.

Examples

Death in stories
In myths and legends, Death is often portrayed as a cloaked figure carrying a scythe.
Many artists have painted Death as a skeletal being, symbolizing the end of life.
The story describes Death visiting a village during a terrible plague.
