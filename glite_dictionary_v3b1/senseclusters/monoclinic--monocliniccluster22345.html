monoclinic

adjective
crystal system with oblique intersection
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑː.nəˈklɪn.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɒ.nəˈklɪn.ɪk / (BrE)
(of a crystal system) having three unequal axes, with one axis obliquely intersecting the others.

Examples

Monoclinic crystal system
Many pharmaceutical compounds are known to have a monoclinic crystal form.
Scientists observed a monoclinic structure in the newly formed crystal.
The mineral gypsum crystallizes in the monoclinic system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orthorhombic - crystal system with three unequal axes at right angles
  • degree higher: triclinic - crystal system with three unequal axes all obliquely intersecting
