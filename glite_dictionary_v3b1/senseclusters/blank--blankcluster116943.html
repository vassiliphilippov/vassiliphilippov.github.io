blank

adjective
ammunition without a bullet
3.7
Pronunciation: / blæŋk / (AmE), / blaŋk / (BrE)
(of ammunition) containing gunpowder but no bullet; not capable of discharging a projectile.

Examples

Blank ammunition
Even though it looked real, the pistol only contained blank rounds.
The prop gun was loaded with blank ammunition for the stage performance.
They used blank cartridges during the training exercise for safety.

Related Terms

  • antonym: live - (of ammunition) containing a bullet or projectile
