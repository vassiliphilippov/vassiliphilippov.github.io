unmuscular

adjective
not muscular
4.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmʌskjələr / (AmE), / ʌnˈmʌskjʊlə / (BrE)
lacking well-developed muscles or muscular strength

Examples

An unmuscular body
Despite his height, he was quite unmuscular and struggled to lift heavy objects.
The doctor advised the unmuscular patient to start a gentle exercise routine.
Compared to his athletic friends, Mark felt unmuscular and self-conscious at the gym.

Related Terms

  • antonym: muscular - having well-developed muscles or muscular strength
  • synonym: slender - thin in an attractive or healthy way
