capsule

noun
a structure enclosing a body part
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.suːl / (AmE), / ˈkæp.sjuːl / (BrE)
an anatomical structure that surrounds or encloses a body part, such as a joint or organ

Examples

A joint capsule
Damage to the liver's capsule can cause severe pain.
The joint is protected by a fibrous capsule that keeps it stable.
The kidney is surrounded by a tough capsule for additional protection.
