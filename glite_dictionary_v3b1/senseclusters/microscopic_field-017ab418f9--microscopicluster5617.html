microscopic field

noun
area seen through a microscope
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkrəˈskɑpɪk ˈfild / (AmE), / ˌmʌɪkrəˈskɒpɪk ˈfiːld / (BrE)
the area visible through the eyepiece of a microscope at a given magnification

Examples

The microscopic field view
