if

noun
possible condition
Pronunciation: / ɪf / (AmE), / ɪf / (BrE)
a possible condition or situation that could affect what happens or what is decided

Examples

Too many ifs
There are too many ifs to make a decision right now.
We discussed all the possible ifs before starting the project.
His plan depends on several ifs that are not guaranteed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: certainty - something that is sure to happen or be true
  • synonym: condition - a situation that must exist before something else can happen
