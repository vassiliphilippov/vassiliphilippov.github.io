Syrian pound

noun
currency unit of Syria
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriən paʊnd / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriən paʊnd / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Syria, equal to 100 piasters

Examples

The Syrian pound currency
He exchanged his dollars for Syrian pounds at the airport.
The Syrian pound has experienced significant devaluation in recent years.
The cost of dinner was 5,000 Syrian pounds at the local restaurant.
