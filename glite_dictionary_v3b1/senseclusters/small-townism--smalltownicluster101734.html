small-townism

noun
preference for small-town values or lifestyle
9.4
Pronunciation: / smɔl ˈtaʊn ɪzəm / (AmE), / smɔːl ˈtaʊn ɪzəm / (BrE)
a mindset or way of life characterized by the values, simplicity, and close-knit community typical of small towns
