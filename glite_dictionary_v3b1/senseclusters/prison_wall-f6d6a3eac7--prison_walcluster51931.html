prison wall

noun
wall enclosing a prison
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪzən wɔl / (AmE), / ˈprɪzən wɔːl / (BrE)
a wall that forms the boundary or enclosure of a prison

Examples

The prison wall
A mural was painted on the old prison wall as part of the restoration project.
Guards patrolled the perimeter along the top of the prison wall.
The escapee struggled to scale the prison wall under cover of darkness.
