Synonyms

  • diastrophism
    9.9 0.00/m
    diastrophism describes large-scale deformation of the Earth's crust that builds mountains and basins, while tectonism means broader crust formation and change.
  • tectonic movement
    4.9 0.03/m
    tectonic movement means the shifting of plates or crustal blocks that causes deformation, while tectonism refers to the whole set of crust-forming processes.
  • crustal movement
    7.7 0.00/m
    crustal movement is the actual shifting of crust pieces that can trigger earthquakes, while tectonism describes long-term crust formation and change.
