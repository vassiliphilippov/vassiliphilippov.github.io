nerve

verb
to steel oneself for difficulty
6.6 0.19/m
Pronunciation: / n…úrv / (AmE), / n…úÀêv / (BrE)
to prepare oneself mentally to face something difficult or unpleasant; to steel oneself

Examples

To nerve oneself
As the door creaked open, Anna nerved herself to face whatever was inside.
He nerves himself each time he has to deliver bad news to his team.
She nerved herself before stepping onto the stage for her speech.
