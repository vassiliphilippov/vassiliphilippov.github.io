lomatia

noun
evergreen ornamental plant genus
Pronunciation: / loʊˈmeɪtiə / (AmE), / lə(ʊ)ˈmeɪtɪə / (BrE)
Any of various evergreen shrubs or small trees of the genus Lomatia, grown as ornamentals for their attractive, fragrant flowers.

Examples

A lomatia shrub
A mature lomatia can add year-round greenery and color to any landscape design.
Botanists have identified over a dozen species within the lomatia genus, mainly native to Australia and South America.
The lomatia in our garden produces clusters of fragrant white flowers each spring.
