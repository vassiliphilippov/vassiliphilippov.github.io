headshake

noun
a side-to-side movement of the head
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌʃeɪk / (AmE), / ˈhɛdʃeɪk / (BrE)
a movement of turning the head from side to side to indicate denial, disbelief, or bemusement

Examples

A headshake of denial
His headshake was enough to tell me he wouldn't change his mind.
She gave a quick headshake to show she disagreed with the proposal.
With a headshake and a smile, he declined the offer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nod - a movement of the head up and down to indicate agreement or affirmation
