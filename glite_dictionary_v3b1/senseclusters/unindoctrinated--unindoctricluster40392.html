unindoctrinated

adjective
not influenced by beliefs
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈdɑktrəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈdɒktrɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having been taught, trained, or influenced by a particular set of beliefs, methods, or systems

Examples
