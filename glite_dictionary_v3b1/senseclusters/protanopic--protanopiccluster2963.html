protanopic

adjective
relating to red color blindness
8.1 8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtəˈnɑːpɪk / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəˈnɒpɪk / (BrE)
Unable to see the color red or to distinguish red and bluish-green, due to a type of color blindness.
