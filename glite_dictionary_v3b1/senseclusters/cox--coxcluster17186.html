cox

noun
person who steers a racing boat
7.5
Pronunciation: / kɑks / (AmE), / kɒks / (BrE)
the person who steers and directs a racing boat or a ship's boat, especially in rowing

Examples

Boat cox
During the regatta, the cox navigated the tightest turns perfectly.
The cox shouted encouragement as the crew pulled harder.
Without a skilled cox, the team struggled to maintain their rhythm.

Related Terms
