landfill

verb
to bury waste
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌfɪl / (AmE), / ˈlændˌfɪl / (BrE)
to dispose of waste by burying it in the ground, typically at a designated site.

Examples

To landfill waste
Most household garbage is landfilled rather than recycled in this region.
The company aims to reduce the amount of plastic that gets landfilled each year.
Hazardous materials should never be landfilled due to environmental risks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dump - to dispose of waste, often without proper management
  • antonym: recycle - to process waste so it can be used again
