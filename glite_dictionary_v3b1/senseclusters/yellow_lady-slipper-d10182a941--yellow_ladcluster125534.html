yellow lady-slipper

noun
orchid with yellow pouch-shaped lip
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ ˈleɪdi ˌslɪpər / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈleɪdi ˌslɪpə / (BrE)
a species of orchid with a yellow pouch-like lip and maroon to purple-brown petals, native to Europe, North America, and Japan

Examples

The yellow lady-slipper orchid
Gardeners often admire the unique shape and color of the yellow lady-slipper.
The yellow lady-slipper is protected in some areas due to its rarity.
We spotted a yellow lady-slipper blooming along the shaded forest path.
