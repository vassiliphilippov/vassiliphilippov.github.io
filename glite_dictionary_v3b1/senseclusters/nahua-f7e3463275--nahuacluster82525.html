Nahua

adjective
of the Nahua people
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːwɑː / (AmE), / ˈnɑːwɑː / (BrE)
relating to the Nahua people of central Mexico, their culture, or their language

Examples

Nahua culture
The Nahua language is still spoken by many communities in central Mexico.
Researchers studied Nahua traditions to better understand pre-Columbian history.
The festival featured Nahua music and dance performances.
