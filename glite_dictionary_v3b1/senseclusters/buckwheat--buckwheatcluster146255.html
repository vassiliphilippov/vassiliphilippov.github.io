buckwheat

noun
an annual plant with edible seeds used as food
5.3 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌk.wiːt/ /; / /ˈbʌk.hwiːt/ (AmE), /ˈbʌk.wiːt/ /; / /ˈbʌk.hwiːt/ (BrE)
An annual plant of the genus Fagopyrum, native to Asia, with small pinkish-white flowers and edible triangular seeds used for food and flour.
