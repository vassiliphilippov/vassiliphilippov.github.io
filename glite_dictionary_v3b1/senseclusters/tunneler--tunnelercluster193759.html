tunneler

noun
burrowing animal
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌnələr / (AmE), / ˈtʌnələ / (BrE)
an animal that digs tunnels or burrows underground, especially for shelter or searching for food

Examples

A mole tunneler
The mole is a well-known tunneler that spends most of its life underground.
Earthworms are important tunnelers because they help aerate the soil.
Some insects, like ants, are skilled tunnelers and create complex underground colonies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burrower - an animal that digs or lives in a burrow
