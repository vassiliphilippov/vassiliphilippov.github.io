Synonyms

    configuration shows how parts are arranged in a pattern, while conformation refers to the resulting overall shape or structure of those parts.
  • structure
    3.5 90/m
    structure is the way parts are organized into a system, while conformation describes the final shape created by that arrangement.
  • shape
    1.0 40/m
    shape is the outer outline or look you see, while conformation focuses on how parts' arrangement gives that form.
  • form
