trusty

adjective
reliable; deserving of trust
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌsti / (AmE), / ˈtrʌsti / (BrE)
reliable and deserving of trust; dependable.

Examples

A trusty friend
He always brings his trusty old camera on vacation.
She keeps a trusty notebook on her desk for jotting down ideas.
The hikers depended on their trusty map to navigate the mountain trails.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trustworthy - able to be relied on as honest or truthful
  • antonym: untrustworthy - not able to be relied on; not dependable
