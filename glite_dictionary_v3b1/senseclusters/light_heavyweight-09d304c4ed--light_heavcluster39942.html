light heavyweight

noun
weight division in combat sports
Pronunciation: / laɪt ˈhɛviˌweɪt /; / ˌlaɪt ˈhɛ.viˌweɪt /; / ˈlaɪt ˈhɛviˌweɪt /; / ˌlaɪt ˈhɛviˌweɪt / (AmE), / laɪt ˈhɛviweɪt /; / ˌlaɪt ˈhɛ.vɪˌweɪt /; / ˈlaɪt ˌhɛviweɪt /; / ˌlaɪt ˈhɛviweɪt / (BrE)
