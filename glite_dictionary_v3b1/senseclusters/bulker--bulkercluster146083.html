bulker

noun
person gaining muscle
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌl.kər/ (AmE), /ˈbʌl.kə/ (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is trying to gain muscle or body mass by eating more and training

Examples

A gym bulker
He became a dedicated bulker over the winter, focusing on eating more calories and lifting heavy weights.
Many gym-goers alternate between being a bulker and a cutter depending on their fitness goals.
As a bulker, she tracked her meals carefully to ensure she was gaining muscle, not just fat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bodybuilder - a person who builds muscle through weight training and diet
  • antonym: cutter - a person trying to lose body fat while keeping muscle
