dine

verb
to host or give a dinner for someone
Pronunciation: / daɪn / (AmE), / daɪn / (BrE)
to entertain someone with a dinner; to act as host for a dinner

Examples

To dine guests
Last weekend, he dined his colleagues at his home to celebrate the project's success.
She loves to dine important clients at her favorite restaurant.
They often dine friends and family during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entertain - to have someone as a guest, especially for a meal
