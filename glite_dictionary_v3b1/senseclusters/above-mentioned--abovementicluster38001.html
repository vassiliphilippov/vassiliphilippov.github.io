above-mentioned

adjective
mentioned earlier in a text
Pronunciation: / əˈbʌv ˌmɛnʃənd / (AmE), / əˈbʌv ˌmɛnʃənd / (BrE)
mentioned or referred to previously in the same document or text.

Examples

The above-mentioned person
All above-mentioned individuals should attend the meeting at noon.
For further details, see the above-mentioned chapter.
The above-mentioned items must be delivered by Friday.
