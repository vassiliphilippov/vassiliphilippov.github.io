polyphagous

adjective
eating various foods
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪfəɡəs / (AmE), / pɒˈlɪfəɡəs / (BrE)
eating or able to eat many different kinds of food, especially referring to animals or insects

Examples

Polyphagous insect feeding
The polyphagous caterpillar feeds on leaves from many different plant species.
Farmers are concerned about polyphagous pests that can attack several types of crops.
Because it is polyphagous, this beetle adapts easily to new environments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monophagous - eating only one kind of food
  • synonym: omnivorous - eating both plants and animals
