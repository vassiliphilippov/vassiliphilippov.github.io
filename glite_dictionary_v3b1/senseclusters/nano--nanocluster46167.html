nano

noun
nanotechnology field
Pronunciation: / ˈnænoʊ / (AmE), / ˈnænəʊ / (BrE)
the field of science and technology concerned with structures and devices at the nanometer scale, especially nanotechnology

Examples

Nano research
She is studying nano to develop new medical devices at the molecular level.
The conference brought together experts in nano from around the world.
Advances in nano have led to stronger and lighter materials for industry.
