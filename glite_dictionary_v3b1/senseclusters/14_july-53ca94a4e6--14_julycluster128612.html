14 July

noun
French national holiday; Bastille Day
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrˈtinθ dʒʊˈlaɪ / (AmE), / ˌfɔːˈtiːnθ dʒʊˈlaɪ / (BrE)
A national holiday in France commemorating the storming of the Bastille prison in 1789, seen as the start of the French Revolution.

Examples

14 July celebrations
Many tourists travel to Paris to witness the grand celebrations on 14 July.
On 14 July, fireworks light up the sky over the Eiffel Tower.
Schools and offices are closed throughout France on 14 July each year.
