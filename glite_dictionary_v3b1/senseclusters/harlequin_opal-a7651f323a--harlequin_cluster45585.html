harlequin opal

noun
opal with reddish base and brilliant color patches
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrlɪkwɪn ˈoʊpəl / (AmE), / ˈhɑːlɪkwɪn ˈəʊpəl / (BrE)
