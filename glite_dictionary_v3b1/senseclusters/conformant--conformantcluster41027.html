conformant

adjective
following rules
7.0
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɔrmənt / (AmE), / kənˈfɔːmənt / (BrE)
willing or inclined to follow rules, customs, or the behavior of a group

Examples

Conformant students
The new students were surprisingly conformant with the school's strict code of conduct.
In highly regulated industries, companies must remain conformant to all legal requirements.
Her conformant attitude made it easy for her to adapt to different social groups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compliant - willing to obey rules or requests
  • antonym: rebellious - resisting authority or control
