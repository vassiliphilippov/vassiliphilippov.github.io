Ezra Loomis Pound

proper noun
American modernist poet and critic
Pronunciation: / ˈɛzrə ˈlumɪs paʊnd / (AmE), / ˈɛzrə ˈluːmɪs paʊnd / (BrE)
(1885–1972) An American poet and critic who lived much of his life in Europe and was a major influence on the development of modernist literature.
