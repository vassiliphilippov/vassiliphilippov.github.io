misfire

verb
to fail as intended
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈfaɪə / (BrE)
to fail to work as intended or to produce the desired result

Examples

The plan misfired
The plan to attract more customers misfired and actually drove people away.
His attempt at humor misfired, leaving the audience silent and confused.
The engine misfired several times before finally starting properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fail - to not succeed in achieving a goal
  • antonym: succeed - to achieve the desired result
