hand-dye

verb
dye by hand
Pronunciation: / hænd ˈdaɪ / (AmE), / hænd ˈdaɪ / (BrE)
to dye (fabric, yarn, etc.) by hand rather than by machine, often resulting in unique color variations

Examples

To hand-dye fabric
Artists often hand-dye silk to create custom scarves and garments.
She prefers to hand-dye her yarns to achieve unique color effects.
The company specializes in fabrics that are hand-dyed using traditional techniques.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dye - to color a material, especially fabric, using a dye
