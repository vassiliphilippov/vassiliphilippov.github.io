risky

adjective
involving danger or hazard
2.3 3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪski / (AmE), / ˈrɪski / (BrE)
involving the possibility of loss, harm, or danger; hazardous (including financial or other risks)

Examples

A risky choice

Investing in that startup would be a risky move for our company.
Some people enjoy making risky bets on the stock market.
