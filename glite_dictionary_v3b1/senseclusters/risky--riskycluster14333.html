Synonyms

    5.2 5/m
    hazardous is often used for materials or situations that can cause harm, while risky highlights chance of loss or danger.
  • dangerous
    1.5 130/m
    dangerous means likely to cause harm in itself, while risky stresses the chance of loss or bad outcome.
  • unsafe
    1.1 4/m
    unsafe describes something not safe or ready to cause harm, while risky focuses on taking actions with possible loss.
  • precarious
    7.6 2/m
