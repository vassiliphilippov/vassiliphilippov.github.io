unstyled

adjective
plain or simple
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈstaɪld / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈstaɪld / (BrE)
lacking style, design, or decoration; plain or simple in appearance or formatting

Examples

An unstyled website
The website looked unstyled because no colors or fonts had been applied.
He preferred an unstyled report that focused only on the facts.
Many documents appear unstyled when viewed in plain text editors.
The document appeared unstyled because the template was not applied correctly.
After the update, some pages loaded as unstyled text without any colors or layout.
If the stylesheet fails to load, the website will display in an unstyled format.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decorated - having added design or ornamentation
  • antonym: formatted - arranged or styled according to specific rules or design
  • synonym: plain - simple and without decoration
