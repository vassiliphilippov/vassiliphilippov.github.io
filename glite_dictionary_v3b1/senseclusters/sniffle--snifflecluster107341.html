sniffle

noun
noisy breathing through the nose
3.9 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈsnɪf.əl/ (AmE), /ˈsnɪf.əl/ (BrE)
an act of breathing noisily through the nose, especially when the nose is blocked or one is about to cry

Examples

A quiet sniffle
A constant sniffle told us that he probably had a cold.
She wiped her nose after a loud sniffle interrupted the quiet room.
The child tried to hide her sniffle as she held back tears.
