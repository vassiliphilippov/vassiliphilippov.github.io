mired

adjective
trapped or hindered in difficulties
5.8 6.2
Pronunciation: /maɪrd/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.ərd/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.əd/ (BrE)

The company became mired in legal disputes that delayed the project for years.
