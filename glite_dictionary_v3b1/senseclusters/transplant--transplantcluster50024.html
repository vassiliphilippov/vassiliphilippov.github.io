transplant

noun
the act of moving something to a new location
Pronunciation: / trænsˈplænt / (AmE), / trænsˈplɑːnt / (BrE)
the act of removing something from one place and introducing it in another, especially moving a living thing to a new environment or position

Examples

Plant transplant
After the transplant of coral onto the reef, marine biologists monitored its growth closely.
Cultural adjustment can be difficult for any transplant to a new country.
The transplant of the old oak tree was successful, and it thrives in its new home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transplantation - the act or process of transplanting
