seam of a shirt

noun
stitched line joining fabric on a shirt
8.1
Pronunciation: / sim əv ə ʃɝt / (AmE), / siːm əv ə ʃɜːt / (BrE)
the stitched line where two pieces of fabric are joined together on a shirt

Examples

The seam of a shirt
She noticed that the seam of a shirt was coming undone near the sleeve.
The tailor reinforced the seam of a shirt to ensure it wouldn't tear again.
When ironing, be careful not to press too hard on the seam of a shirt.
