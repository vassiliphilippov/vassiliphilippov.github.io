business editor

noun
editor responsible for business news
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs ˈɛdɪtər / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˈɛdɪtə / (BrE)
the editor at a newspaper or other publication who is responsible for business news and coverage

Examples

Business editor at newspaper
After years as a reporter, she was promoted to business editor at the newspaper.
The business editor coordinates a team of journalists focusing on economics and finance.
The business editor oversaw the coverage of the recent stock market crash.

Related Terms

  • synonym: editor - person responsible for the content in a publication
