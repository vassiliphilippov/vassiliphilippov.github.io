nutritious

adjective
providing nourishment; healthy food
1.0
Pronunciation: / nuˈtrɪʃəs / (AmE), / njuˈtrɪʃəs / (BrE)
(of food) containing substances necessary for growth, health, and good condition; providing nourishment.

Examples

Nutritious meals
Children need nutritious meals to help them grow and stay healthy.
The soup was not only delicious but also nutritious, packed with vitamins and minerals.
This salad is especially nutritious because it contains a variety of vegetables and seeds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: healthy - promoting good health
  • synonym: nourishing - providing nourishment or sustenance
  • antonym: unhealthy - harmful to health; not good for you
