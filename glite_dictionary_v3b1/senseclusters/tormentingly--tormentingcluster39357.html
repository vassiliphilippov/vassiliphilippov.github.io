tormentingly

adverb
causing suffering
Pronunciation: / tɔrˈmɛntɪŋli / (AmE), / tɔːˈmɛntɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes someone pain, suffering, distress, annoyance, or frustration

Examples

The aroma of fresh bread drifted tormentingly from the kitchen while we waited for dinner.
