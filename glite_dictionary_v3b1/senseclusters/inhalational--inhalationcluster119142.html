inhalational

adjective
caused by inhaling
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnhəˈleɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnhəˈleɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by the act of breathing in a substance, especially a gas or vapor

Examples

Inhalational injury
The firefighters wore masks to prevent inhalational injuries from the smoke.
Doctors monitored the patient for signs of inhalational poisoning after the chemical spill.
Treatment for inhalational burns often requires specialized respiratory care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: respiratory - relating to breathing or the organs of breathing
