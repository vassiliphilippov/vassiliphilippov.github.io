tenderly

adverb
in a gentle or loving manner
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛndɚli / (AmE), / ˈtɛndəli / (BrE)

Examples

She spoke tenderly to her newborn baby, calming him with her soft voice.
The nurse tenderly dressed the patient's wounds, making sure not to hurt him.
