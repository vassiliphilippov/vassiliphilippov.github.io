Nuptse

proper noun
mountain in the Himalayas, near Nepal-Tibet border
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːptseɪ / (AmE), / ˈnʌptseɪ / (BrE)
a mountain peak in the central Himalayas on the border of Tibet and Nepal, with an elevation of 25,726 feet (7,861 meters)

Examples

Nuptse mountain peak
Expeditions to Nuptse require careful preparation due to its dangerous ridges.
From the Everest Base Camp, Nuptse is clearly visible to the southwest.
The climbers set out at dawn to reach the summit of Nuptse.
