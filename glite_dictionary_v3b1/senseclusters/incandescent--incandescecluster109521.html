incandescent

noun
type of light bulb
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkænˈdɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌɪnkænˈdɛsənt / (BrE)
a light bulb that produces light by heating a wire filament until it glows

Examples

Incandescent light bulb
Many homes still use incandescents for their warm and familiar light.
The old lamp required a new incandescent after the previous bulb burned out.
Some people prefer incandescents because they create a softer atmosphere than other bulbs.
