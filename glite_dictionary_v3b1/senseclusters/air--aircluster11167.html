air

noun
medium for radio or TV broadcasts
3.1
Pronunciation: / ɛr / (AmE), / eə / (BrE)
the medium through which radio and television broadcasts are transmitted; the state of being broadcast.

Examples

On the air
Millions of people listened to the announcement as soon as it hit the air.
The host returned to the air after a long commercial break.
The show has been off the air for over a year now.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airwaves - radio frequencies used for broadcasting
