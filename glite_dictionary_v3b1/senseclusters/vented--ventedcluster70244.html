vented

adjective
having a vent or vents
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈvɛntɪd / (BrE)
fitted with a vent or vents to allow the intake of air or the discharge of gases

Examples

A vented fireplace
A properly vented fireplace reduces the risk of smoke buildup.
She purchased a vented laptop stand to keep her computer cool.
The machine is vented to prevent overheating during continuous use.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unvented - without a vent or vents; lacking openings for air or gas
