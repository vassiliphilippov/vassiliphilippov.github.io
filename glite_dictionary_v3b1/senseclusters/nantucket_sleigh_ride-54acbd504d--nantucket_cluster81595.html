Nantucket sleigh ride

noun
exciting or dangerous wild ride, esp. at sea
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /næn.ˈtʌk.ɪt sleɪ raɪd/ (AmE), /næn.ˈtʌk.ɪt sleɪ raɪd/ (BrE)
