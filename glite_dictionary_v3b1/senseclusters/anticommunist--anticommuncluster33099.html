anticommunist

noun
opponent of communism
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈkɑːmjənɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈkɒmjənɪst / (BrE)

Examples

An anticommunist leader
During the Cold War, many anticommunists organized protests against leftist governments.
The senator was known as a strong anticommunist throughout his political career.
Some anticommunists believed that communism threatened national security.
