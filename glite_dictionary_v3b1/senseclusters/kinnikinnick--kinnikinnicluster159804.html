kinnikinnick

noun
evergreen groundcover shrub
Pronunciation: / ˌkɪnɪkɪˈnɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɪnɪkɪˈnɪk / (BrE)
a low-growing evergreen shrub, especially bearberry, with small leaves and red berries, often used as groundcover

Examples

Kinnikinnick groundcover plant
The hillside was covered in kinnikinnick, creating a lush green carpet dotted with red berries.
Gardeners often choose kinnikinnick for its ability to thrive in poor, rocky soil.
Wildlife such as birds and bears are attracted to the berries produced by kinnikinnick plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bearberry - a low-growing shrub with red berries, also called Arctostaphylos uva-ursi
