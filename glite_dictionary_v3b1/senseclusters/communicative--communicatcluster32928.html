communicative

adjective
willing to talk or share information
1.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunɪˌkeɪtɪv / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪkətɪv / (BrE)
willing or able to talk to people and share information or feelings; inclined to communicate.

Examples

Communicative person
After the meeting, he was unusually communicative about the company's future plans.
Children tend to become more communicative as they grow comfortable in their environment.
The new manager is very communicative with her team, always encouraging open discussion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reticent - not revealing one's thoughts or feelings readily
  • synonym: talkative - fond of talking; given to talking a lot
