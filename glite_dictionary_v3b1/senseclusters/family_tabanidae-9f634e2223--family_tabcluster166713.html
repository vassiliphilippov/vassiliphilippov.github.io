family Tabanidae

noun
family of horseflies (biting flies)
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli tæˈbænɪˌdi / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli tæˈbænɪdi / (BrE)
A family of large biting flies, commonly known as horseflies, that are known for their painful bites and are found worldwide.
