Synonyms

  • align
    3.5 9.37/m
    align places objects precisely in a straight or correct relationship, while line up means form an orderly row of people or things.
  • queue
    3.4 1.33/m
    queue means people form or join a line while waiting, whereas line up can mean arrange people or things into any orderly straight row.

  • antonym: scatter - to separate and spread out people or things over an area
  • degree lower: wait - remain in a place until an expected event happens
