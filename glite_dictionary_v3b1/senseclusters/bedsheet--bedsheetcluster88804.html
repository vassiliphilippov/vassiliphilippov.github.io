bedsheet

noun
cloth for mattress
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌʃit / (AmE), / ˈbɛdʃiːt / (BrE)
a large rectangular piece of cloth used to cover a mattress on a bed

Examples

A clean bedsheet
She folded the clean bedsheet and put it in the closet.
He changed the bedsheet every week to keep the bed fresh.
The hotel provided a crisp white bedsheet for each guest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blanket - a thick, warm covering for a bed
  • synonym: sheet - a large piece of cloth used on a bed
