repriming

noun
applying primer again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈpraɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈpraɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of applying a primer coat again to a surface before painting or finishing

Examples

Surface repriming
After sanding the old door, he started repriming it to prepare for a fresh coat of paint.
The workers spent the morning repriming the metal railings before applying the final color.
Proper repriming can help paint adhere better to weathered or previously painted surfaces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: painting - the act of applying paint to a surface
  • synonym: priming - the act of applying a primer coat to a surface
