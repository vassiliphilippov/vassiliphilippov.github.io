consignment

noun
delivery of goods for sale or disposal
1.0 4.2
Pronunciation: / kənˈsaɪnmənt / (AmE), / kənˈsaɪnmənt / (BrE)
the delivery of goods to an agent for sale, storage, or disposal, usually without transferring ownership until the goods are sold

Examples

Consignment sale
She sent the artwork on consignment to a gallery in Paris hoping for a good price.
The antiques arrived on consignment and will be sold next week.
The shop accepts clothes for consignment and pays the owner once they are sold.
