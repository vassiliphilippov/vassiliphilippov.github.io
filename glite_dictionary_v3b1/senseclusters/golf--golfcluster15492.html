golf

noun
outdoor game hitting a ball into holes
3.4
Pronunciation: / ɡɑlf / (AmE), / ɡɒlf / (BrE)
a game played on a large outdoor course in which players use clubs to hit a small ball into a series of holes in as few strokes as possible

Examples

A round of golf
He plays golf every Sunday, rain or shine.
Many business deals are discussed while playing golf.
The country club has an 18-hole golf course.

Related Terms

  • synonym: golf course - the area of land on which golf is played
  • degree lower: mini-golf - a miniature version of golf played on small courses with obstacles
