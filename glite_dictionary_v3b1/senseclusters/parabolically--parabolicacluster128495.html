parabolically

adverb
in a parabolic manner
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈbɑlɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈbɒlɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that resembles or relates to a parabola, especially in shape or movement

Examples

To move parabolically upward
The rocket ascended parabolically before reaching its highest point.
The artist drew the bridge so that it arched parabolically over the river.
The water sprayed parabolically from the fountain, creating a graceful arc.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curved - having a smooth, rounded shape
  • antonym: straight - not curved or bent
