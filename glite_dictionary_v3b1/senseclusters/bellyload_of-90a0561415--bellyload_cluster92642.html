bellyload of

noun
large quantity of food
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛliˌloʊd əv / (AmE), / ˈbɛliˌləʊd əv / (BrE)
an amount of food that is enough to fill one's stomach; a very large quantity of food

Examples

A bellyload of food
After the feast, everyone had eaten a bellyload of food and could barely move.
He managed to finish a bellyload of pancakes before noon.
She claimed she could easily handle a bellyload of spicy wings at the contest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: load of - a large amount, especially of something undesirable or impressive
  • synonym: ton of - a very large amount (informal)
