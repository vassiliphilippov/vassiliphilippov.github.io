craterlike

adjective
resembling a crater
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪtərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈkreɪtəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the appearance or shape of a crater

Examples

craterlike shape
The explosion left a craterlike depression in the middle of the field.
The surface of the old road was rough and craterlike after years of heavy use.
Scientists studied the craterlike formations on the surface of the moon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cratered - having craters or marked by craters
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; not rough or cratered
