unelected

adjective
not chosen by vote
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪˈlɛktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈlɛktɪd / (BrE)
not chosen or put in position by a vote or election

Examples

Unelected officials
The country was ruled by an unelected group of military leaders for several years.
Many citizens were frustrated with the influence of unelected officials in government decisions.
The board appointed an unelected director to fill the vacant position.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appointed - chosen for a position without an election
  • antonym: elected - chosen for a position by a vote
