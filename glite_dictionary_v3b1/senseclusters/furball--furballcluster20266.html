furball

noun
furry decorative ball
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈfɜːˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a small ball made of real or synthetic fur, often used as a decorative accessory or toy

Examples

A pink furball
She attached a pink furball to her purse for a playful touch.
The kitten chased the furball across the living room floor.
He gave his friend a keychain with a fluffy furball as a gift.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pom-pom - a small fluffy ball used as a decoration or accessory
