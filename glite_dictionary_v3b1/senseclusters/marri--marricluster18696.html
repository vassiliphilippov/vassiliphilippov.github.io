marri

noun
large Australian eucalyptus tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmæri / (AmE), / ˈmæri / (BrE)
A very large Australian eucalyptus tree (Corymbia calophylla), known for its rough bark and red gum.

Examples

The marri tree
