(of a substance, especially a chemical compound) containing no water; specifically, without water of crystallization.

Examples

Anhydrous chemical compound
Calcium chloride is often stored in an anhydrous form to ensure it does not introduce moisture.
The chemist used anhydrous sodium sulfate to absorb any remaining water from the solution.
The instructions specified the use of anhydrous ammonia, not the aqueous variety.

Synonyms

  • nonaqueous

  • waterless
