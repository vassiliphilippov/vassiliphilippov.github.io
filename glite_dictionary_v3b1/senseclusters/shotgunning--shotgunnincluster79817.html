shotgunning

noun
rapid beverage drinking
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑtˌɡʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɒtˌɡʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of quickly drinking a beverage, especially by puncturing a can and consuming it rapidly

Examples

To shotgun a drink
At the barbecue, everyone started shotgunning cans of soda to see who could finish first.
College students are often seen shotgunning drinks at parties for fun.
He won the contest by shotgunning his beverage faster than anyone else.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chugging - drinking a beverage quickly without stopping
