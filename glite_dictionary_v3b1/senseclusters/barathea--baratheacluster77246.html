barathea

noun
fine ribbed fabric
Pronunciation: / ˌbærəˈθiːə / (AmE), / ˌbærəˈθiːə / (BrE)
a fine, soft fabric with a ribbed or pebbled surface, commonly used for making suits and formal wear

Examples

Barathea fabric
The tailor recommended barathea for its elegant texture and durability.
His dinner jacket was made from black barathea, giving it a luxurious appearance.
Many formal suits use barathea because of its soft feel and subtle sheen.
