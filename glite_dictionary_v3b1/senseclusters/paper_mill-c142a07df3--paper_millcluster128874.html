paper mill

noun
company that sells academic papers
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpeɪ.pər mɪl/ (AmE), /ˈpeɪ.pə mɪl/ (BrE)
a company or service that produces academic or scholarly papers for clients, often selling fabricated, low-quality, or plagiarised work.
