Onobrychis

noun
a genus of Old World flowering herbs
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊ.nəˈbraɪ.kɪs /; / ˌoʊnəˈbraɪkɪs / (AmE), / ˌɒ.nəˈbraɪ.kɪs /; / ˌɒnəˈbraɪkɪs / (BrE)
a genus of herbs with pinnate leaves and pink or white flowers in racemes, followed by flat, unjointed seed pods; commonly known as sainfoin and used as animal fodder
