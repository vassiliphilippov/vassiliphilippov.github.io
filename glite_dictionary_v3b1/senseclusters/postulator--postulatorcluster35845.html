postulator

noun
one who puts forward an assumption or postulate
9.4 7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑːs.tjə.leɪ.tər/ /; / /ˈpɑːs.tə.leɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈpɒs.tjʊ.leɪ.tə/ /; / /ˈpɒs.tə.leɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person who assumes or proposes something as a basis for reasoning or argument

Examples

Logical postulator
A skilled postulator can make a weak argument appear stronger by framing the right assumptions.
As the postulator in the debate, she established the foundation for further discussion.
The postulator of the theory assumed several unproven premises.

Related Terms

  • synonym: postulate - to suggest or assume the existence or truth of something as a basis for reasoning
