telltale

adjective
revealing hidden information or evidence
6.3 1.80/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆtÉ›lËŒteÉªl / (AmE), / ËˆtÉ›lËŒteÉªl / (BrE)
(especially of a sign, object, or part) revealing or indicating something that is hidden or has happened, often unintentionally.

Examples

Telltale sign
A telltale sign of water damage is the musty smell in the basement.
Her telltale blush revealed that she was embarrassed.
The detective noticed the telltale fingerprints on the window.

Synonyms

  • revealing
    4.0 3.03/m
    revealing shows hidden facts or private details clearly, while telltale names a sign or small part that unintentionally shows what happened.
  • telling
    4.4 0.00/m
    telling gives a strong or striking clue, while telltale refers to a sign or part that quietly reveals a hidden truth.
