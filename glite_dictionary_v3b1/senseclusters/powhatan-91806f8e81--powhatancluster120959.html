Powhatan

noun
Algonquian language of the Powhatan
Pronunciation: / paʊˈhætən / (AmE), / paʊˈhætən / (BrE)
the extinct Algonquian language formerly spoken by the Powhatan people of eastern Virginia

Examples

The Powhatan language
Few written records of Powhatan remain, but some words were adopted into English.
Linguists have reconstructed parts of the Powhatan language from colonial records and word lists.
The museum exhibit included a display about the history of Powhatan and its speakers.
