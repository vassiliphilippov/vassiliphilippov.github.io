throne room

noun
ceremonial room in a palace for a monarch
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊn ˌrum / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊn ˌruːm / (BrE)
a large, formal room in a palace where a monarch sits on a throne and holds ceremonies or official audiences

Examples

The throne room ceremony
In the throne room, the king made his official proclamations to the assembled nobles.
The grand chandelier in the throne room sparkled above the queen's guests.
Tourists were awed by the gold decorations inside the throne room of the palace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audience chamber - a room where people have an audience with a monarch or important person
