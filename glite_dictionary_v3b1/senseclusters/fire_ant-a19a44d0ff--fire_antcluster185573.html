fire ant

noun
a stinging reddish ant of the Americas
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ ænt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ænt / (BrE)
a small, reddish-brown ant native to tropical and subtropical America, known for its painful stinging bite

Examples

A fire ant bite
A fire ant bite can cause intense burning and itching for several hours.
Children are advised to wear shoes outside to avoid fire ant stings.
The spread of the fire ant in southern states has become a major agricultural problem.
