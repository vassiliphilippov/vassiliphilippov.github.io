hee-haw

verb
donkey's braying sound
9.3 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàhiÀêÀåh…î / (AmE), / ÀàhiÀêÀåh…îÀê / (BrE)
(of a donkey) to make a loud, harsh braying sound.

Examples

Donkeys hee-haw loudly
As the children approached, the old mule hee-hawed in greeting.
The donkey began to hee-haw loudly at dawn, waking everyone nearby.
We heard the animal hee-haw from the other side of the field.

Synonyms

  • bray
    7.1 0.20/m
    bray is the common verb for a donkey's loud harsh cry, while hee-haw is the written sound that copies that braying noise.
  • heehaw
    9.3 0.03/m
    heehaw is a variant spelling used for a loud harsh laugh or donkey-like sound, while hee-haw is the form with a hyphen for donkey braying.
