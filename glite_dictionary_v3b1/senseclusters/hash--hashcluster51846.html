hash

noun
a drug from hemp with hallucinogenic effects
Pronunciation: / hæʃ / (AmE), / hæʃ / (BrE)
a purified resinous substance extracted from the hemp plant, used as a drug for its hallucinogenic effects

Examples

Some people smoke hash by mixing it with tobacco or rolling it into cigarettes.
The police found a small amount of hash in his backpack during the search.
