smartphone

noun
advanced mobile phone with computing features
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɑːrtˌfoʊn /; / ˈsmɑrtˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈsmɑːtˌfəʊn / (BrE)
a mobile phone with advanced computing features, internet access, and the ability to use apps, usually with a touchscreen
