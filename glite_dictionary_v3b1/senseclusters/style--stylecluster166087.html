style

noun
distinctive and stylish elegance
Pronunciation: / staɪl / (AmE), / staɪl / (BrE)
a distinctive way, manner, or trend in which something is done, created, or appears, especially in fashion, art, behavior, or design

Examples

Fashion style
He always dresses with such style that he is often mistaken for a celebrity.
She entered the room with remarkable style, turning every head as she passed.
Their wedding was simple but celebrated with great style and taste.
Flared jeans were the dominant style during the 1970s.
Minimalism remains a popular style among modern architects.
The restaurant's retro style attracts those who love vintage decor.
Each artist brings a different style to their performances.
She answered the questions in her usual style, calm and confident.
The interview was conducted in an informal style that put everyone at ease.
