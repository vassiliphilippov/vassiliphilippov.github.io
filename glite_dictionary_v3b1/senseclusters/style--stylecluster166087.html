Examples

Fashion style

She entered the room with remarkable style, turning every head as she passed.

Synonyms

  • fashion
    1.6 31/m

  • mode
    2.8 25/m
    mode is a particular or technical way used in a system or art, while style emphasizes a distinctive look or manner.
  • way
    1.0 1306/m
    way is a general method or path for doing something, while style focuses on a distinctive look, trend, or manner.
