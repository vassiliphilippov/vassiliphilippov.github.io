chronometric

adjective
absolute age dating
8.2
Pronunciation: /krə.nə.ˈmet.rɪk/ /; / /kroʊ.nə.ˈmet.rɪk/ (AmE), /krɒ.nə.ˈmet.rɪk/ /; / /krə.nə.ˈmet.rɪk/ (BrE)
relating to methods that measure or determine the absolute age of objects or events, especially in archaeology or geology

Examples

Chronometric dating methods
The team used chronometric analysis to establish the precise age of the ancient pottery.
Radiocarbon dating is a common chronometric technique in archaeological research.
Advances in chronometric methods have improved our understanding of early human history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chronological - arranged in order of time
  • antonym: relative dating - determining the age of objects or events in relation to each other
