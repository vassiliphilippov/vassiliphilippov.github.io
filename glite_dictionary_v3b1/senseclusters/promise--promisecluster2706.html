promise

noun
sign of future success or hope
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːmɪs / (AmE), / ˈprɒmɪs / (BrE)
a sign or indication that something good or successful is likely to happen in the future; grounds for hope or expectation

Examples

Promise for growth
Although the project has had setbacks, it still holds promise for the future.
His early work gave little promise of the masterpieces he would later create.
The young artist shows a great deal of promise with her latest paintings.
