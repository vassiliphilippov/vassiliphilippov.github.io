cabalist

noun
member of a secretive group
Pronunciation: / ˈkæbəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈkabəlɪst / (BrE)
A person who is a member of a secret group involved in plots or intrigue.

Examples

Secret cabalist
Historians debated whether the king was influenced by a cabalist within his council.
Rumors spread that a cabalist had infiltrated the organization, sowing distrust among its members.
The conspiracy was uncovered after authorities arrested a leading cabalist involved in the plot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cabal - a secret group or faction involved in intrigue
