pietist

noun
devout Christian movement
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪətɪst / (AmE), / ˈpaɪətɪst / (BrE)
a member of a religious movement that emphasizes personal faith, devotion, and heartfelt religious experience over formal rituals or doctrine

Examples
