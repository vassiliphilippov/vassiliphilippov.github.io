skunky

adjective
smelling like a skunk
Pronunciation: / ˈskʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈskʌŋki / (BrE)
having a strong, unpleasant smell or taste like a skunk

Examples

Skunky smell
The old cheese in the fridge had turned skunky and needed to be thrown away.
After the rain, the basement developed a skunky odor that was hard to ignore.
He wrinkled his nose at the skunky smell coming from the garbage can.
The beer tasted skunky after sitting in the sun for too long.
She wrinkled her nose at the skunky odor coming from the open bottle.
Some imported lagers can become skunky if they are not stored properly.

  • antonym: fresh - having a clean or pleasant smell
  • synonym: malodorous - having a very unpleasant smell
