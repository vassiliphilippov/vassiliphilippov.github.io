kitman

noun
equipment manager
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtmæn / (AmE), / ˈkɪtmæn / (BrE)
a person responsible for managing, preparing, and maintaining equipment and uniforms for a sports team or the armed forces

Examples

Football kitman
The kitman made sure every player had clean uniforms before the big match.
After training, the kitman collected all the equipment and checked it for damage.
In the army, the kitman is essential for keeping soldiers' gear in good condition.
The kitman arrived early to lay out the players' uniforms before the big game.
After the match, the kitman collected all the jerseys and washed them for the next day.
Every successful team relies on a dedicated kitman to keep their gear organized and ready.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coach - a person who trains and directs a sports team
  • synonym: equipment manager - a person who manages and maintains equipment for a team
  • synonym: quartermaster - an officer responsible for supplies and equipment in the military
