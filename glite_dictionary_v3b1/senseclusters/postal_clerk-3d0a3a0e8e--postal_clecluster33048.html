postal clerk

noun
worker in a post office
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstl ˌklɜrk / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstl ˌklɑːk / (BrE)
a person whose job is to handle mail and assist customers in a post office

Examples

A postal clerk helps
A friendly postal clerk greeted customers as they entered the post office.
She asked the postal clerk about the quickest way to send an urgent letter.
The postal clerk weighed my package and printed the shipping label.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: postmaster - person in charge of a post office
