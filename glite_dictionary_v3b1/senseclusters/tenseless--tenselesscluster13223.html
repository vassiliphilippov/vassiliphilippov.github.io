tenseless

adjective
lax in phonetics
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnsləs / (AmE), / ˈtɛnsləs / (BrE)
in phonetics, produced without muscular tension, describing vowels or consonants that are lax rather than tense

Examples

Tenseless vowel sound
In English, the vowel in 'bit' is considered a tenseless vowel.
Some languages make a clear distinction between tenseless and tense consonants.
The linguist explained how tenseless sounds are produced with less muscular effort.
