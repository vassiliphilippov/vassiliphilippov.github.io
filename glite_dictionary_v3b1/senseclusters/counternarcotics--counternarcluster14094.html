counternarcotics

adjective
against illegal drugs
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərnɑːrˈkɑːtɪks / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntənɑːˈkɒtɪks / (BrE)
relating to actions, policies, or efforts aimed at preventing or combating illegal drugs and drug trafficking

Examples

Counternarcotics operations
The agency launched a new counternarcotics program to reduce drug trafficking in the region.
International cooperation is essential for effective counternarcotics operations.
She works in a counternarcotics unit focused on intercepting illegal shipments.
