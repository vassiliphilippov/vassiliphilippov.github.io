datasheet

noun
information document
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪtəˌʃiːt / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtəˌʃiːt /; / ˈdeɪtəʃiːt / (BrE)
a document, often in table form, that provides detailed information or technical specifications about a person, product, or system

Examples

A datasheet document
The technician checked the datasheet to find the correct voltage for the device.
Please complete the datasheet with your personal and academic information before submitting your application.
Each product comes with a datasheet listing its technical specifications and safety instructions.
Before installing the new sensor, she carefully read the datasheet to understand its specifications.
The datasheet lists the maximum operating temperature and recommended voltage for the device.
Engineers often refer to the datasheet when designing electronic circuits.
The engineer reviewed the datasheet to check the specifications of the new component.
You can export the results as a datasheet for further analysis in a spreadsheet program.
Each datasheet in the database contains detailed information about the listed products.
