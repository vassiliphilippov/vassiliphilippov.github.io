payload

the amount of goods or cargo carried by a vehicle, especially by an aircraft, truck, or spacecraft

Examples

Truck payload
The airplane reached its maximum payload before taking off from the runway.
The truck was designed to transport a heavy payload across long distances.
This rocket can carry a scientific payload into orbit around the Earth.

Synonyms

  • cargo
    2.3 19/m
    cargo is the goods carried by a ship or plane, while payload is the amount a vehicle carries.
  • load
    1.1 17/m
    load is an amount carried at one time for any purpose, while payload refers to goods a vehicle carries, often by weight.
  • freight
    3.9 4.79/m
    freight is goods moved for business and cost, while payload is the amount carried by a vehicle regardless of sale.
  • shipment
    2.3 6.95/m
    shipment is a batch of goods sent together as an order, while payload is the total amount a vehicle carries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freight - goods transported in bulk by truck, train, ship, or aircraft
