maniac

noun
an insane, wildly reckless or obsessed person
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪniˌæk / (AmE), / ˈmeɪniˌak / (BrE)
a person who behaves in a wildly reckless or violent way, often considered insane or dangerously obsessed

Examples

A violent maniac
People called him a maniac because of his wild obsession with cleanliness.
She drives like a maniac when she's late for work.
The police warned the public about a maniac on the loose in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fanatic - a person filled with excessive enthusiasm or obsession
  • synonym: lunatic - a person who is insane (offensive, old use)
  • antonym: sane person - a person who is of sound mind
