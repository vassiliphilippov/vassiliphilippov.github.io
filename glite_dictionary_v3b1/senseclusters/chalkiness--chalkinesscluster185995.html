chalkiness

noun
dry powdery texture
6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɑː.kɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈtʃɔː.kɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɔː.kɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being like chalk, especially in taste, texture, appearance, or leaving a powdery residue

Examples

Related Terms

  • synonym: powderiness - the quality of being powdery or easily crumbled
  • antonym: smoothness - a texture that is even and free from roughness
