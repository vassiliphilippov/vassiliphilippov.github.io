weigh

verb
consider carefully; take into account
4.3
Pronunciation: / weɪ / (AmE), / weɪ / (BrE)
to consider carefully, especially by comparing different factors or possibilities before making a decision

Examples

To weigh the options
Judges are expected to weigh all the evidence presented in court.
She carefully weighed all her options before accepting the job offer.
The committee must weigh the potential risks against the benefits before approving the plan.
