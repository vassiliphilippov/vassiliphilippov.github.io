physiotherapist

noun
healthcare professional specializing in physical therapy
2.2 4.0
Pronunciation: /fɪ.zɪ.ə.ˈθe.rə.pɪst/ /; / /fɪ.zɪ.oʊ.ˈθe.rə.pɪst/ (AmE), /fɪ.zɪ.ə.ˈθe.rə.pɪst/ /; / /fɪ.zɪ.əʊ.ˈθe.rə.pɪst/ (BrE)

Examples

A physiotherapist helps patients
After her surgery, a physiotherapist helped her regain strength and mobility in her leg.
