casual

adjective
temporary or irregular; not regular
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæʒuəl / (AmE), / ˈkæʒjʊəl / (BrE)
Happening or done on a temporary, irregular, or occasional basis rather than regularly or permanently.

Examples

Casual work
Her job at the cafe was strictly casual, with no guarantee of regular hours.
Many students seek casual work during the summer to earn extra money.
They maintained a casual friendship, meeting only once in a while.

Related Terms

  • synonym: occasional - happening from time to time; not regular or frequent
  • antonym: regular - done or happening frequently, in an ordered way
