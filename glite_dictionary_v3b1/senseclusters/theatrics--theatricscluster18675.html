theatrics

noun
exaggerated behavior
Pronunciation: / θiˈætrɪks / (AmE), / θiˈætrɪks / (BrE)
dramatic or exaggerated behavior intended to attract attention or create an effect

Examples

Over-the-top theatrics
Her constant theatrics during meetings made it hard for others to focus on the agenda.
The actor's theatrics on stage captivated the entire audience.
He resorted to theatrics to get his friends' attention at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dramatics - exaggerated or theatrical behavior
  • synonym: histrionics - overly emotional or dramatic behavior
  • antonym: restraint - calm and controlled behavior
