dopaminergic

noun
dopamine-acting drug
8.5
Pronunciation: /doʊ.pə.mɪ.ˈnɜːr.dʒɪk/ (AmE), /dəʊ.pə.mɪ.ˈnɜː.dʒɪk/ (BrE)
