apple nut

noun
nutlike seed of a South American palm
Pronunciation: / ˈæp.əl nʌt / (AmE), / ˈap.əl nʌt / (BrE)
the nutlike seed of a South American palm tree; its hard, white shell is polished for making objects such as buttons

Examples

The apple nut seed
Before synthetics, apple nut was a popular material for making sewing accessories.
Many vintage coats feature apple nut buttons because of their smooth, polished look.
The craftsman carved a necklace out of apple nut shells collected from the forest.
