phenological

adjective
seasonal biological events
Pronunciation: / ˌfiːnəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌfiːnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study or observation of seasonal natural events in plants and animals

Examples

Phenological data collection
Researchers collect phenological data to monitor how climate change affects plant blooming times.
The phenological patterns of bird migration are shifting due to warmer temperatures.
Gardeners use phenological indicators, like leaf-out dates, to plan their planting schedules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecological - relating to the relationships between living things and their environment
  • synonym: seasonal - related to or affected by the seasons
