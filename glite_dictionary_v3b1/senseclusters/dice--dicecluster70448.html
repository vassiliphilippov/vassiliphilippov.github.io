dice

verb
cut into small cubes (food)
Pronunciation: / daɪs / (AmE), / daɪs / (BrE)
to cut food into small cubes, typically as part of food preparation

Examples

To dice vegetables
Carefully dice the onions before adding them to the pan.
She diced the tomatoes for the salsa.
The recipe instructs you to dice the potatoes into half-inch pieces.
