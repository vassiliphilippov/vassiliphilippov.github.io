saloon

noun
drinking establishment or bar
6.6
Pronunciation: / səˈlun / (AmE), / səˈluːn / (BrE)
a bar or public house where alcoholic drinks are served

Examples

A busy saloon
After work, they all met at the saloon for a few drinks and conversation.
Many small towns in the nineteenth century had at least one saloon on Main Street.
The old western saloon had swinging doors and a long wooden bar.
She ordered a sandwich and a beer at the local saloon after work.
Some saloons in the 19th century also offered gambling and live music.
The old western saloon was crowded with cowboys and travelers seeking a drink.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar - a place where alcoholic drinks are served
  • synonym: pub - a public house for drinking, especially in the UK
  • synonym: tavern - an establishment for the sale of beer and other drinks; sometimes also food
