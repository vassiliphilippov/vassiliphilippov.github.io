Hallowe'en

noun
evening festival before All Saints' Day
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌhæloʊˈin / (AmE), / ˌhæləʊˈiːn / (BrE)
The evening of October 31, the night before All Saints' Day, celebrated by children in costumes who go from house to house asking for treats, playing pranks, and attending parties.

Examples

Hallowe'en night
Many people decorate their houses with pumpkins and cobwebs on Hallowe'en.
On Hallowe'en, children dressed as ghosts and witches went door to door for candy.
We attended a costume party on Hallowe'en and had a lot of fun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Halloween - modern spelling of Hallowe'en, same holiday
