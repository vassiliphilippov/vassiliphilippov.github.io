forkful

noun
amount on a fork
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrkˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈfɔːkˌfʊl / (BrE)
an amount of food that can be held on a fork at one time

Examples

A forkful of food
He twirled a forkful of spaghetti before taking a bite.
She offered him a forkful of her salad to try.
After tasting the first forkful, he smiled in delight.
