logomania

noun
excessive display of brand logos
8.0
Pronunciation: /loʊ.goʊ.ˈmeɪ.niː.ə/ /; / /loʊ.goʊ.ˈmeɪ.njə/ (AmE), /ləʊ.gəʊ.ˈmeɪ.niː.ə/ /; / /ləʊ.gəʊ.ˈmeɪ.njə/ (BrE)
A trend or tendency to display brand logos excessively on clothing, accessories, or advertising, often as a visible sign of brand loyalty or fashion.

Examples

Fashion logomania trend
The designer's new line is unapologetic logomania, every jacket showing a giant emblem.
Fashion writers said the season favoured logomania as consumers embraced bold brand identities.
Streetwear culture has turned simple labels into status symbols, a kind of logomania.
