plainness

noun
lack of attractiveness; not beautiful
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪnnɪs / (AmE), / ˈpleɪnnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of having an appearance that is not attractive or beautiful

Examples

Plainness of appearance
Despite the plainness of the building, it served its purpose well.
Her plainness did not prevent her from having many friends and admirers.
Some designers intentionally highlight the plainness of certain models to challenge beauty standards.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beauty - the quality of being pleasing to the senses or to the mind
  • synonym: homeliness - the quality of being unattractive in appearance
