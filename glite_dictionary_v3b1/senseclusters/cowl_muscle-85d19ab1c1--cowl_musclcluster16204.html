cowl muscle

noun
muscle in shoulder and upper back
9.9
Pronunciation: / kaʊl ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / kaʊl ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
either of two flat triangular muscles of the shoulder and upper back responsible for moving the shoulders and arms
