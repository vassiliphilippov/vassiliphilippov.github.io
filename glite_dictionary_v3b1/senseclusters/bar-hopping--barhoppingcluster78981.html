bar-hopping

verb
to visit multiple bars
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑr ˌhɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑː ˌhɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
to visit several bars or pubs in succession during a single outing or night.

Examples

To go bar-hopping
We spent Friday night bar-hopping downtown with friends.
After dinner, they decided to go bar-hopping to celebrate the promotion.
He prefers bar-hopping to staying in one place all evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pub crawl - an event or activity of visiting several pubs or bars in one session
