wheelsman

noun
vehicle driver
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlzmən / (AmE), / ˈwiːlzmən / (BrE)
a person who drives or is responsible for steering a wheeled vehicle, especially in a formal or ceremonial context

Examples

Ceremonial wheelsman
The wheelsman skillfully guided the float through the crowded streets during the festival.
As the official wheelsman, she was responsible for driving the vintage fire truck in the parade.
Each wheelsman wore a uniform and cap to match the style of the classic vehicles they drove.
