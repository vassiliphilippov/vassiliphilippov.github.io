regalness

noun
royal quality
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈriːɡəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈriːɡəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being like a king or queen, showing dignity, elegance, or majesty

Examples

The regalness of a king
Her regalness impressed everyone at the ceremony.
The actor carried himself with such regalness that he seemed born to play a king.
Even in simple clothes, her regalness was unmistakable to all who met her.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humbleness - the quality of being modest or humble
  • synonym: majesty - impressive beauty or dignity
  • synonym: royalty - the status or power of a king or queen
