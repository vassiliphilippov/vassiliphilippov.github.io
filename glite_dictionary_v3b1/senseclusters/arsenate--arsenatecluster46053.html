arsenate

noun
salt or ester of arsenic acid
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrsəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈɑːsɪneɪt / (BrE)
A salt or ester of arsenic acid, typically containing the anion AsO4^3−.

Examples
