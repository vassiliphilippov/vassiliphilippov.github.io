m

adjective
symbol for one million
Pronunciation: / ɛm / (AmE), / ɛm / (BrE)
used after a numeral to mean one million (1,000,000), especially in financial, business, and journalistic contexts.

Examples

12m dollars
The startup raised 12m in funding to expand its platform across Europe.
Analysts expect the city budget to reach 300m dollars after the latest tax adjustments.
She sold her stake for 1.5m, which allowed her to buy a small house nearby.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: k - used as a symbol to denote one thousand (1,000), a lower numeric magnitude.
