pantothenic

adjective
containing vitamin B5
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpæn.təˈθɛn.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌpæn.təˈθɛn.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing pantothenic acid, a type of B vitamin important for metabolism

Examples

Pantothenic acid
Whole grains are a rich source of pantothenic acid, which helps your body use carbohydrates and fats.
A deficiency in pantothenic acid is rare because it is found in many foods.
The nutrition label lists the amount of pantothenic acid per serving.
