self-criticism

noun
judging one's own actions or faults
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈkrɪt̬ɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌselfˈkrɪtɪˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
the act of judging and criticizing one's own actions or character

Examples

Self-criticism habit
Her habit of self-criticism sometimes makes her doubt her own abilities.
The artist's self-criticism pushed him to improve his technique over the years.
Too much self-criticism can damage your confidence and motivation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-reproach - the act of blaming or criticizing oneself
