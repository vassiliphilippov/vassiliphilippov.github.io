stepchild

noun
child of one's spouse by previous relationship
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛpˌtʃaɪld / (AmE), / ˈstɛpˌtʃaɪld / (BrE)
A child of one's spouse from a previous marriage or relationship.

Examples

A stepchild in family
After her remarriage, Emily became a loving stepchild to her new father's family.
As a stepchild, he sometimes felt left out during family gatherings.
The school counselor provided support for each stepchild adjusting to blended family life.
