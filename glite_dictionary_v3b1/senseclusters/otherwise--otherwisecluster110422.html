otherwise

adverb
in other respects; apart from that
Pronunciation: / ˈʌðərˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈʌðəwaɪz / (BrE)
in a different way or in other respects; apart from that

Examples

Seemed otherwise normal
His behavior was strange, though his appearance was otherwise normal.
She was tired but otherwise in good health.
We would have gone to the park, but the weather was otherwise unpleasant.
