unmilked

adjective
not milked
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɪlkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɪlkt / (BrE)
not having been milked, especially referring to an animal such as a cow

Examples

Unmilked goats
The veterinarian checked the unmilked goats for any signs of discomfort.
Leaving cows unmilked for too long can cause health problems.
He quickly identified the unmilked animals by their full udders.

Related Terms

  • antonym: milked - having had milk removed from the udder
