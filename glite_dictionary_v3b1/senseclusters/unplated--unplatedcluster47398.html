unplated

adjective
not served on plate
6.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not arranged, decorated, or served on a plate, especially referring to food or dishes before presentation.

Examples

Unplated food presentation
The appetizers remained unplated until the guests arrived at the table.
She preferred to keep the desserts unplated so everyone could serve themselves.
The chef explained why the main course was intentionally unplated for a family-style meal.
