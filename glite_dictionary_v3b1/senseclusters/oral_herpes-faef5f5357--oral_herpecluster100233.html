oral herpes

noun
herpes infection of the mouth area
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrəl ˈhɜːrpiːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːrəl ˈhɜːːpiːz / (BrE)
A contagious viral infection characterized by sores or blisters around the mouth, caused by herpes simplex virus type 1 (HSV-1).

Examples

Oral herpes sores
During an outbreak of oral herpes, it's best to avoid sharing drinks or utensils.
Many adults are carriers of the virus that causes oral herpes.
She was diagnosed with oral herpes after noticing painful sores on her lips.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold sore - a small blister around the mouth caused by HSV-1
