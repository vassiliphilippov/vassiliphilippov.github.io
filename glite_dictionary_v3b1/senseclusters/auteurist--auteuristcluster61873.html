auteurist

adjective
director's unique style
Pronunciation: / oʊˈtɜːrɪst / (AmE), / əʊˈtɜːrɪst / (BrE)
relating to the distinctive style, vision, or influence of a film director as an auteur

Examples

Auteurist style
The film's auteurist approach was evident in its unusual camera angles and narrative style.
Critics praised the director's auteurist vision, which set the movie apart from mainstream releases.
Her auteurist influence could be seen in every detail of the production, from lighting to dialogue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cinematic - relating to movies or filmmaking
  • antonym: generic - lacking distinctive or individual characteristics
