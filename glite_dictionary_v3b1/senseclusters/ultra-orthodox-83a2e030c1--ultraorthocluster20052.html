ultra-Orthodox

noun
strict religious adherent
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrə ˈɔrθədɑks / (AmE), / ˌʌltrə ˈɔːθədɒks / (BrE)
a person who belongs to a very strict and traditional religious community, especially within Judaism, or members of such communities collectively

Examples

An ultra-Orthodox group
The ultra-Orthodox often maintain distinct customs and dress within their communities.
Many ultra-Orthodox live in neighborhoods where religious traditions are strictly observed.
Some ultra-Orthodox groups have their own schools and community organizations.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: Orthodox - a person who follows traditional religious beliefs and practices
  • antonym: secular - not connected with religious or spiritual matters
