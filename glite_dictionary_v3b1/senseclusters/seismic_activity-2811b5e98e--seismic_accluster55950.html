seismic activity

noun
earth movements caused by tectonic processes
6.1 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsaɪz.mɪk æk.ˈtɪv.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ˈsaɪz.mɪk æk.ˈtɪv.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈsaɪz.mɪk æk.ˈtɪv.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ˈsaɪz.mɪk æk.ˈtɪv.ə.tiː/ (BrE)
