yellow jacket

noun
small yellow-and-black social wasp
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ ˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
a small, aggressive, yellow-and-black social wasp, often nesting in the ground

Examples

A yellow jacket nest
Yellow jackets can become aggressive if their nest is disturbed.
He was stung by a yellow jacket while mowing the lawn.
We found a nest of yellow jackets in the backyard near the shed.
