ontological

adjective
relating to formal ontologies (AI, knowledge representation)
Pronunciation: / ɑnˈtɑlədʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ɒnˈtɒlədʒɪkəl / (BrE)
(in information science and artificial intelligence) relating to an ontology: a formal, explicit specification of concepts and relationships used to represent knowledge and enable automated reasoning or data integration.

Examples

Ontological framework
The research team built an ontological framework to standardize medical terms across different hospital databases.
She performed an ontological analysis of the dataset to identify core entities and their relationships.
Errors in the ontological model caused the AI to misclassify products in the catalog.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knowledge graph - a graph-based data model that represents entities and their interrelations for retrieval and reasoning
  • synonym: ontology (computing) - a formal representation of a set of concepts within a domain and the relationships between them
