voluntaryist

noun
advocate of voluntary association
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːlənˌtɛriɪst / (AmE), / ˈvɒlənˌtɛriɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that all human relationships and associations should be entered into voluntarily, without coercion or force

Examples

A voluntaryist thinker
The voluntaryist argued for a society without compulsory government.
As a voluntaryist, she believes that all charity should be given freely, not through taxes.
Many voluntaryists oppose laws that require participation in government programs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: libertarian - a person who advocates for minimal government and maximum individual freedom
  • antonym: statist - a person who supports significant government control or intervention
