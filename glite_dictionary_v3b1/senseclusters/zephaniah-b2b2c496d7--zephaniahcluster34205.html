Zephaniah

proper noun
Hebrew minor prophet (7th century BC)
Pronunciation: / zɛfəˈnaɪə / (AmE), / zɛfəˈnaɪə / (BrE)
A Hebrew minor prophet of the late 7th century BC, known for the biblical Book of Zephaniah.

Examples

Prophet Zephaniah
Zephaniah is traditionally regarded as the author of the Book of Zephaniah in the Old Testament.
Biblical scholars often discuss Zephaniah in the context of Israel's prophetic history.
The teachings of Zephaniah emphasized judgment and the hope of restoration.
