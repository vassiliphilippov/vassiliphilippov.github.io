Adonic

adjective
verse rhythm: dactyl + spondee/trochee
Pronunciation: / əˈdɑnɪk /; / əˈdɑː.nɪk / (AmE), / əˈdɒnɪk /; / əˈdɒn.ɪk / (BrE)
describing a verse or line with the Adonic meter (dactyl + spondee/trochee), or relating to Adonis, especially in beauty or attractiveness
