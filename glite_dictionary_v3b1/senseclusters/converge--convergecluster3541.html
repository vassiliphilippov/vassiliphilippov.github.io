converge

Synonyms

  • meet
    1.0 18/m
    meet is a general word for joining at a point, while converge suggests moving from different directions to the same spot.
  • come together
    2.1 2/m
    come together often describes people or things gathering closely, while converge focuses on paths or lines meeting from different directions.
