godawful

adjective
inspiring awe
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑdˌɔfəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌɔːfəl / (BrE)
inspiring awe or reverence; causing great respect, fear, or wonder because of power, size, or beauty (archaic)

Examples

A godawful sight
The ancient cathedral had a godawful presence that left visitors silent in admiration.
Early explorers described the canyon as a godawful sight, both beautiful and terrifying.
Standing before the godawful mountain, the climbers felt a mix of fear and respect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fearsome - causing fear or awe
