career man

noun
man focused on his career
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɪr mæn / (AmE), / kəˈrɪə mæn / (BrE)
a man who is devoted to his career or whose main focus is his professional advancement

Examples

A career man advances
As a dedicated career man, he quickly climbed the corporate ladder.
He was always known as a career man, rarely taking time off for holidays.
Unlike his brother, who preferred a quiet life, Tom was a true career man.

Related Terms

  • synonym: career woman - a woman whose main focus is her professional career
  • synonym: careerist - a person whose main concern is advancement in their career
