putback

noun
basketball rebound score
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊtˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈpʊtˌbæk / (BrE)
a basketball play in which a player immediately scores after rebounding a missed shot

Examples

A basketball putback
After missing the initial shot, he quickly scored a putback to tie the game.
The crowd cheered when she made a powerful putback following the rebound.
His ability to score on the putback gave his team a crucial advantage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: layup - a close-range shot in basketball, usually off the backboard
  • synonym: rebound - to gain possession of the ball after a missed shot
