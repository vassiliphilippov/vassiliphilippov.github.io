yellow star-thistle

noun
European weed with winged stems and hairy leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛl.oʊ ˈstɑr ˌθɪs.əl / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ ˈstɑː ˌθɪs.əl / (BrE)
A European weed with winged stems and hairy leaves, now found as an invasive plant in parts of the United States.
