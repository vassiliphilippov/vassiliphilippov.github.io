detainee

noun
person held in custody
1.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /diː.tə.ˈniː/ /; / /dɪ.tə.ˈniː/ (AmE), /dɪ.tə.ˈniː/ /; / /diː.tə.ˈniː/ (BrE)
