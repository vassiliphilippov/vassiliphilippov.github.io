red cedar

noun
tall aromatic tree of the Pacific Northwest
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈsiːdɚ /; / ɹɛd ˈsiːdɚ / (AmE), / rɛd ˈsiːdə /; / ɹɛd ˈsiːdə / (BrE)
a large evergreen tree of the Pacific coast or northwestern North America, with aromatic reddish wood (cypress family)
