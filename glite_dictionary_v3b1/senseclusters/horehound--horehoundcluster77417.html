horehound

noun
herbal-flavored candy
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔːrˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈhɔːhˌaʊnd / (BrE)
A hard candy flavored with an extract of the horehound plant, traditionally used as a soothing remedy for coughs.

Examples

Horehound candy
My grandmother always kept a tin of horehound in her purse for sore throats.
Some people find the taste of horehound candy a bit bitter, but others love it.
We visited an old-fashioned sweet shop that still sold horehound among other classic candies.
