reattempt

noun
second attempt
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈtɛmpt / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈtɛmpt / (BrE)
an act of trying to do something again after a previous failure or interruption

Examples

A second reattempt
After failing the exam, she made a reattempt the following month and passed.
The team's reattempt at breaking the record was more organized and successful.
Despite the initial setback, his reattempt showed determination and improvement.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: attempt - an effort to do something
  • synonym: retry - an additional attempt to do something
  • antonym: success - the achievement of a goal or purpose
