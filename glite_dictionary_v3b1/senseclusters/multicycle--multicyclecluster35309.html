multicycle

adjective
over multiple cycles
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌsaɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌsaɪkəl / (BrE)
involving or taking place over more than one cycle, especially in computing or engineering processes

Examples

Multicycle processor design
The engineers designed a multicycle processor to improve energy efficiency.
A multicycle operation can take several clock cycles to complete in a computer system.
The new chip supports both single-cycle and multicycle instructions for flexibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pipelined - using a series of processing stages to improve efficiency
  • antonym: single-cycle - involving only one cycle to complete an operation
