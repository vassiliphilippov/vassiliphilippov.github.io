myxoid

adjective
mucus-like tissue
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪk.sɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɪk.sɔɪd / (BrE)
containing or resembling mucus, especially in the texture of tissue

Examples

Myxoid tissue
The pathologist described the tumor as having a myxoid matrix under the microscope.
Certain soft tissue sarcomas display a myxoid appearance due to their gelatinous consistency.
The biopsy revealed myxoid changes in the connective tissue of the sample.
