anticholinergic

adjective

(of a drug or substance) inhibiting or blocking the action of acetylcholine at its receptor sites in the nervous system.

Examples

Anticholinergic effect
Doctors often prescribe anticholinergic medications to treat overactive bladder symptoms.
Elderly patients may be more sensitive to the effects of anticholinergic substances in medications.
Some anticholinergic drugs can cause side effects like dry mouth and blurred vision.

Synonyms

  • anticholinergic drug
    7.2 0.01/m
