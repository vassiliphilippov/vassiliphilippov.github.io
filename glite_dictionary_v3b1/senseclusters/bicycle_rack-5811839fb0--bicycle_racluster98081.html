bicycle rack

noun
framework for parking bicycles
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪsɪkəl ræk / (AmE), / ˈbaɪsɪkl̩ ræk / (BrE)
a metal or wooden framework designed for parking and securing bicycles

Examples

A bicycle rack near entrance
A new bicycle rack was installed near the main entrance of the building.
She locked her bike to the bicycle rack outside the library.
Students parked their bicycles neatly in the bicycle rack before heading to class.
