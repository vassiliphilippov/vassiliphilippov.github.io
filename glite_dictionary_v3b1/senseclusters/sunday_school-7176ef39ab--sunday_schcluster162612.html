Sunday school

noun
class for religious instruction on Sundays
1.8 2.24/m

A class held on Sundays, typically by a church, to teach children and sometimes adults about Christianity and the Bible.

Examples

Sunday school class
Many children attend Sunday school before the main church service.
She has taught Sunday school at her church for over ten years.
The church is organizing a special event for the Sunday school students this weekend.
