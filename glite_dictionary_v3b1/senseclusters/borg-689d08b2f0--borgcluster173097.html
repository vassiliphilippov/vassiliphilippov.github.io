Borg

proper noun
Swedish tennis champion
Pronunciation: / bɔːrɡ / (AmE), / bɔːɡ / (BrE)
Björn Borg, a Swedish former world No. 1 tennis player renowned for his dominance in the 1970s and early 1980s.
