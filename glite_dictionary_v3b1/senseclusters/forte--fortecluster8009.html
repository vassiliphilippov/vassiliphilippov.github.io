forte

noun
loud musical passage
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔr.teɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.teɪ / (BrE)
a passage or section in a musical work that is to be played or sung loudly

Examples

Forte section
After a gentle introduction, the choir launched into a powerful forte.
The composer indicated a sudden forte to heighten the drama of the piece.
The orchestra played the forte section with impressive energy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: piano - a musical passage to be performed softly
