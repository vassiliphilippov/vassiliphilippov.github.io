tenth cranial nerve

noun
the vagus nerve; major nerve of the body
7.2
Pronunciation: /tenθ ˈkreɪ.niː.əl nɜːrv/ /; / /tɪnθ ˈkreɪ.niː.əl nɜːrv/ (AmE), /tenθ ˈkreɪ.niː.əl nɜːv/ /; / /tɪnθ ˈkreɪ.niː.əl nɜːv/ (BrE)
