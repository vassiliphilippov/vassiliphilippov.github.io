homing

adjective
able to return home or to a target location
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmɪŋ / (BrE)
having the ability or function of finding or returning to a prescribed location, especially home or a specific destination.

Examples

A homing missile
A homing missile can track and strike a moving target with high accuracy.
Scientists studied the homing ability of carrier pigeons in various conditions.
The homing instinct helps migratory birds find their way over great distances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guiding - showing the way or directing
  • antonym: lost - unable to find the way
