Celestial City

noun
literary term for Heaven
3.4 8.7
Pronunciation: / səˈlɛstʃəl ˈsɪti / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛstɪəl ˈsɪti / (BrE)
A literary term for Heaven, especially as described as the ultimate destination of the righteous in John Bunyan's 'Pilgrim's Progress'.

Examples

The Celestial City in Heaven
In his sermon, the pastor spoke of the faithful entering the Celestial City after death.
Many readers find hope and comfort in the image of the Celestial City described by Bunyan.
She dreamed of walking through the gates of the Celestial City surrounded by light.
