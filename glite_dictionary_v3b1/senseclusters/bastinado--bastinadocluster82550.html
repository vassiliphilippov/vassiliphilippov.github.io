bastinado

noun
beating soles of feet as punishment/torture
Pronunciation: / ˌbæstəˈnidoʊ / (AmE), / ˌbæstɪˈnɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
a form of punishment or torture in which the soles of the feet are beaten with a stick, whip, or cudgel

Examples

A painful bastinado punishment
He recoiled at the mention of bastinado, knowing the pain it could inflict.
Historical records describe the use of bastinado in ancient judicial punishments.
The prisoner was subjected to bastinado as part of his interrogation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caning - punishment by beating with a cane
