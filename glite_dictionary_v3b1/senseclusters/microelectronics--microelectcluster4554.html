microelectronics

noun
study of miniature electronic components
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.kɹoʊ.ɪˌlɛkˈtɹɑn.ɪks / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.krəʊ.ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒn.ɪks / (BrE)
The branch of electronics that deals with the design, manufacture, and application of very small electronic components and circuits.

Examples

The field of microelectronics
Advances in microelectronics have made modern smartphones possible.
Research in microelectronics is crucial for developing faster and smaller computer chips.
She is pursuing a degree in microelectronics at the university.
