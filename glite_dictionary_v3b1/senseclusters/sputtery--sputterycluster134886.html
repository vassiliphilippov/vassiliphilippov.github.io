sputtery

adjective
hesitant or faltering
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspʌtəri / (AmE), / ˈspʌtəri / (BrE)
hesitating or faltering in speech or manner, often expressed in short, interrupted bursts

Examples

Sputtery speech
His sputtery explanation made it clear he was nervous about speaking in public.
The engine started with a sputtery noise before running smoothly.
She gave a sputtery response, pausing often as she searched for the right words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: halting - slow and hesitant, especially in speech
  • antonym: smooth - flowing easily and without interruption
