relearn

verb
learn again after forgetting or neglecting
4.1 1.0/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåriÀêÀàl…úrn / (AmE), / ÀåriÀêÀàl…úÀên / (BrE)

Examples

To relearn skills

Many adults decide to relearn a language they studied in school.
When his computer crashed, he was forced to relearn the basics of the software.
