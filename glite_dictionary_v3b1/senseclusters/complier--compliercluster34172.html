complier

noun
person who obeys
Pronunciation: / kəmˈplaɪər / (AmE), / kəmˈplaɪə / (BrE)
a person who obeys rules or follows instructions

Examples

A complier with rules
The company rewards every complier with a bonus for following safety procedures.
As a strict complier of the rules, she never missed a deadline.
Teachers appreciate a complier who listens carefully and does what is asked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: follower - a person who accepts the guidance or leadership of another
  • antonym: rebel - a person who resists authority or control
