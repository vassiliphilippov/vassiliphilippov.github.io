overexcited

adjective
excessively excited
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪkˈsaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪkˈsaɪtɪd / (BrE)
Feeling or showing more excitement than is reasonable or appropriate; excessively excited.

Examples

The overexcited children
The children became overexcited after eating too much sugar at the party.
The puppy was so overexcited to see its owners that it couldn't stop jumping around.
Try not to get overexcited before your big exam tomorrow.
