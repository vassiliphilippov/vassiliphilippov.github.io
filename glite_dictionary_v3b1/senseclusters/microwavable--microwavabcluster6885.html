microwavable

adjective
suitable for microwave
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈweɪvəbəl / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈweɪvəbəl / (BrE)
able to be safely cooked or heated in a microwave oven

Examples

Always check if the packaging is microwavable before heating your food.

She bought a set of microwavable containers for her lunches at work.
