evolutionist

noun
person who supports theory of evolution
4.9 4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌiːvəˈluːʃənɪst / (BrE)

Many evolutionists support the evidence presented by fossils and genetics.
