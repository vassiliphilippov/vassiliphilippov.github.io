noun phrase

noun
group of words acting as a noun
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnaʊn ˌfreɪz / (AmE), / ˈnaʊn ˌfreɪz / (BrE)
A group of words in a sentence that together function as a noun, typically acting as the subject, object, or complement of a verb.

Examples

A noun phrase example
A noun phrase can include adjectives, determiners, and sometimes prepositional phrases.
In the sentence 'The black cat sat on the mat,' 'the black cat' is a noun phrase.
Linguistics students often identify the noun phrase as the subject or object in a sentence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prepositional phrase - a phrase consisting of a preposition and its object
  • synonym: verb phrase - a phrase consisting of a verb and its complements or modifiers
