Examples

A noun phrase example
A noun phrase can include adjectives, determiners, and sometimes prepositional phrases.
In the sentence 'The black cat sat on the mat,' 'the black cat' is a noun phrase.
Linguistics students often identify the noun phrase as the subject or object in a sentence.
