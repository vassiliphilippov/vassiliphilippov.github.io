Brueghel

proper noun
Flemish landscape and genre painter (1525–1569)
9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈbruː.ɡəl/ (AmE), /ˈbruː.ɡəl/ (BrE)
(1525–1569) A Flemish painter renowned for his detailed landscapes and peasant scenes, considered one of the greatest artists of the Northern Renaissance.
