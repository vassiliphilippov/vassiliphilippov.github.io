intraperitoneal

adjective
within peritoneal cavity
8.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.trə.pɜːr.ɪ.tə.ˈniː.əl/ /; / /ɪn.trə.pə.rɪ.tə.ˈniː.əl/ (AmE), /ɪn.trə.pɜː.rɪ.tə.ˈniː.əl/ /; / /ɪn.trə.pə.rɪ.tə.ˈniː.əl/ (BrE)
located or occurring within the peritoneal cavity (the space in the abdomen that contains the intestines, liver, and other organs), especially as a route for giving drugs or fluids

Examples

Intraperitoneal injection
The doctor recommended an intraperitoneal injection to deliver the medication directly to the affected area.
Researchers studied the effects of intraperitoneal chemotherapy in treating abdominal cancers.
The procedure involved the intraperitoneal administration of fluids to manage the patient's condition.
The doctor recommended an intraperitoneal injection to deliver the medication directly into the abdominal cavity.
Researchers studied the effects of intraperitoneal chemotherapy in treating certain types of cancer.
The procedure involved placing a catheter for intraperitoneal fluid administration.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extraperitoneal - located or occurring outside the peritoneal cavity
  • antonym: intravenous - administered into a vein
  • synonym: peritoneal - relating to the peritoneum, the membrane lining the abdominal cavity
  • antonym: subcutaneous - administered under the skin
