spontaneous abortion

noun
medical term for miscarriage (natural pregnancy loss)
6.3
Pronunciation: / spɑnˈteɪniəs əˈbɔrʃən / (AmE), / spɒnˈteɪniəs əˈbɔːʃən / (BrE)
a natural loss of a pregnancy, occurring without intentional intervention; miscarriage

Examples

A spontaneous abortion event
After experiencing a spontaneous abortion, she received medical support and counseling.
Most spontaneous abortions occur during the first trimester of pregnancy.
The doctor explained that a spontaneous abortion is not caused by any intentional action.

Related Terms

  • synonym: miscarriage - the spontaneous loss of a pregnancy before the fetus can survive
