paratransit

noun
transport for disabled
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈtrænsɪt / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈtrænzɪt / (BrE)
a flexible transportation service for people who cannot use regular public transit, such as people with disabilities or special needs

Examples

Paratransit services
Many cities offer paratransit services to help people with disabilities travel independently.
She relies on paratransit to get to her medical appointments every week.
The new paratransit vans are equipped with wheelchair lifts for easier access.
The city expanded its paratransit services to help elderly residents get to medical appointments.
Many people with disabilities rely on paratransit for daily transportation needs.
Unlike regular buses, paratransit vehicles can be scheduled for door-to-door service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accessible transport - transportation designed to be usable by people with disabilities
  • antonym: public transit - regular transportation services like buses and trains for the general public
  • synonym: shuttle - a vehicle that travels frequently between two places
