stormily

adverb
in a violent or turbulent manner
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːrmɪli / (AmE), / ˈstɔːmɪli / (BrE)
In a way that is full of violent wind and rain, or with strong emotion or turbulence.

Examples

To stormily react
Clouds gathered stormily as thunder rumbled in the distance.
She left the meeting stormily, slamming the door behind her in anger.
The waves crashed stormily against the rocky shore during the night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful or tranquil manner
