tryptamine

noun
chemical compound
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪptəˌmiːn / (AmE), / ˈtrɪptəmiːn / (BrE)
a chemical compound found in plants and animals, related to neurotransmitters and used in chemical synthesis
