tutsan

noun

Pronunciation: / ˈtʌt.sən / (AmE), / ˈtʌt.sən / (BrE)
A deciduous Eurasian shrub (Hypericum androsaemum) with golden yellow flowers and reddish-purple fruits, sometimes used to make a soothing salve.
