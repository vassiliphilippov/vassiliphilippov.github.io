closet queen

noun
offensive term for closeted gay man
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑːzət kwiːn / (AmE), / ˈklɒzɪt kwiːn / (BrE)
Offensive term for a homosexual man who does not openly acknowledge his sexual orientation.

Examples

Closet queen secret
Calling someone a closet queen is disrespectful and can be deeply hurtful.
He struggled for years with being a closet queen, hiding his true identity from everyone.
Some tabloids tried to out him as a closet queen, which led to public outcry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: closet case - a person who hides their sexual orientation
  • antonym: out - openly acknowledging one's sexual orientation
