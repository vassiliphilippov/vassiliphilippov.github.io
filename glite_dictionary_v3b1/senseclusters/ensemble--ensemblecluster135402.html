ensemble

noun
a coordinated outfit of clothing
Pronunciation: / ɑnˈsɑmbəl / (AmE), / ɒnˈsɒmbəl / (BrE)
a complete outfit of clothing, especially one in which all pieces are matched or coordinated

Examples

A stylish ensemble
His new spring ensemble included a jacket, trousers, and matching shoes.
She arrived at the party in a stylish ensemble of navy blue and gold.
The designer showcased a chic ensemble perfect for an evening out.
