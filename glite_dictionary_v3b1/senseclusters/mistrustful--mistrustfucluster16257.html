mistrustful

adjective
lacking trust in others
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈtrʌstfəl / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈtrʌstfəl / (BrE)
Lacking trust in others; openly suspicious and unwilling to confide.

Examples

A mistrustful look
After being deceived in the past, she became mistrustful of strangers.
His mistrustful attitude made it hard for him to form close relationships.
The mistrustful dog growled at anyone who approached its owner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suspicious - having or showing a cautious distrust of someone or something
  • antonym: trusting - willing to trust others easily
