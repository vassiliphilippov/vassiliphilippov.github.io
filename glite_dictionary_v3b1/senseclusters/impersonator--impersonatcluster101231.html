impersonator

noun
person who pretends to be another
3.8
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɜrsəˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɜːsəneɪtə / (BrE)
a person who pretends to be another, especially to deceive or entertain

Examples

A impersonator of celebrities
That comedian is a talented impersonator of famous actors and politicians.
The bank was tricked by an impersonator using a stolen identity.
The police arrested the impersonator who was pretending to be a government official.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imitator - a person who copies the behavior or actions of another
  • synonym: mimic - a person skilled in imitating others
