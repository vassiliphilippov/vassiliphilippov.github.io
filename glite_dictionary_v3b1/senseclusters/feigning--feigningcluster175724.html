feigning

noun
the act of pretending
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of pretending or faking something, especially to deceive others

Examples

Feigning illness
He was criticized for his constant feigning of illness to avoid work.
Her feigning of surprise was convincing to everyone in the room.
The actor's skill at feigning emotions made his performance very believable.
His feigning of illness fooled everyone at the office.
She was skilled at feigning indifference when she was actually upset.
Their feigning of surprise seemed unconvincing to the audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: honesty - the quality of being truthful and sincere
  • synonym: pretending - the act of behaving as if something is true when it is not
