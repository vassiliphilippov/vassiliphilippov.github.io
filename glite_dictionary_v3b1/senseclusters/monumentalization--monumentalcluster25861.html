monumentalization

noun
making into a symbol
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnjəˌmɛntəlɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɒnjʊˌmɛntəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making a person, event, or idea into a monument or treating it as highly important and worthy of remembrance
