Bufo calamita

noun
a western European short-legged toad
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌbjuːfoʊ kəˈlæmɪtə / (AmE), / ˌbjuːfəʊ kəˈlæmɪtə / (BrE)
A common brownish-yellow short-legged toad native to western Europe, known for running rather than hopping.

Examples

The Bufo calamita toad
In western Europe, Bufo calamita can often be found in sandy heathlands.
The Bufo calamita is easily recognized by a yellow stripe running down its back.
Unlike most toads, Bufo calamita tends to run rather than hop.
