rationalizable

adjective
able to be justified
Pronunciation: / ˌræʃənəˈlaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˌræʃənəˈlaɪzəbl / (BrE)

Not every mistake is rationalizable by simply blaming a lack of information.
Her choice seemed odd at first, but it was rationalizable in light of her past experiences.
