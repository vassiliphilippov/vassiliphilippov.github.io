extratemporal

adjective
outside time
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəˈtɛmpərəl / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈtɛmpərəl / (BrE)
existing or occurring outside the normal flow of time; not affected by time or temporal order

Examples

Extratemporal reality

Some myths speak of gods who live in an extratemporal realm, untouched by history.
Art can sometimes create an extratemporal feeling, making moments seem to last forever.
