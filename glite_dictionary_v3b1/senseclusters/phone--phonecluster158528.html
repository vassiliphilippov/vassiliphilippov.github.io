phone

verb
contact by telephone
6.5
Pronunciation: / foʊn / (AmE), / fəʊn / (BrE)
to contact or try to contact someone by telephone

Examples

To phone a friend

I'll phone you when I arrive at the airport.
She forgot to phone her parents last night.
