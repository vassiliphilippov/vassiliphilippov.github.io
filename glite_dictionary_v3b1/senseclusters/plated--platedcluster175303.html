plated

adjective
covered with metal
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈpleɪtɪd / (BrE)
covered or fitted with a layer of metal or protective plates, often for strength or protection

Examples

Plated armor
The knight wore a plated suit to protect himself in battle.
Many luxury cars have plated bumpers for added durability and style.
The museum displayed a plated shield from the medieval period.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armored - protected with a hard covering or armor
  • antonym: bare - not covered or protected
