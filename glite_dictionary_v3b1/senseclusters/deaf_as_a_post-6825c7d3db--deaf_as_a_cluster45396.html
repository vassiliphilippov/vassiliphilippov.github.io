deaf as a post

adjective
completely deaf (idiom)
Pronunciation: / dɛf əz ə poʊst / (AmE), / dɛf æz ə pəʊst / (BrE)
completely deaf; unable to hear anything at all

Examples

Deaf as a post completely
Despite her hearing aids, Grandma is deaf as a post and needs us to speak up.
He was deaf as a post, so hand signals were often necessary for communication.
When the teacher called his name, Tim acted deaf as a post to avoid answering.
