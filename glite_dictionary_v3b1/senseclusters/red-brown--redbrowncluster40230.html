red-brown

adjective
brown with a reddish tint
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌbraʊn / (BrE)
Of a brown color tinged with red.

Examples

A red-brown color
His red-brown boots were worn but sturdy after years of use.
She chose a red-brown paint for the accent wall in the living room.
The fox’s coat was a beautiful red-brown color glinting in the sunlight.
