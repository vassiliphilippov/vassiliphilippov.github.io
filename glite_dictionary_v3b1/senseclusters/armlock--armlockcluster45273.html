armlock

verb
to restrain with arm hold
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrmlɑk / (AmE), / ˈɑːmlɒk / (BrE)
to hold and immobilize someone by applying a restraining grip to their arm, often in wrestling or martial arts

Examples

To armlock an opponent
The judo instructor taught us how to armlock an opponent safely during practice.
He managed to armlock his rival and win the match in under a minute.
If you armlock someone incorrectly, you could accidentally injure their shoulder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grapple - to seize or hold someone in a wrestling move
  • antonym: release - to let go or free from a hold
