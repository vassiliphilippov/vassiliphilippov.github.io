palp

noun
spider mating appendage
9.9
Pronunciation: /pælp/ (AmE), /pælp/ (BrE)
a specialized appendage of male spiders and some other arachnids, used to transfer sperm during mating

Examples

Spider palp mating
The male spider used his palp to transfer sperm to the female during mating.
Scientists observed the movement of the palp under a microscope to study spider reproduction.
In some species, the shape of the palp helps identify different types of spiders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pedipalp - a pair of appendages near the mouth of arachnids, often used for sensory or reproductive functions
