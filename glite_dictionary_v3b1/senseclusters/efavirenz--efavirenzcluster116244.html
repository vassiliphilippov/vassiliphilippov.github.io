efavirenz

noun
HIV treatment drug
Pronunciation: / ɛfəˈvɪrɛnz / (AmE), / ɛfəˈvɪrɛnz / (BrE)
a drug used in combination with other medications to treat HIV infection

Examples

Efavirenz HIV drug
The doctor prescribed efavirenz as part of her HIV treatment regimen.
Patients taking efavirenz should follow their medication schedule carefully.
Some people experience mild side effects when starting efavirenz therapy.
