Boston Tea Party

noun
1773 protest against British tea tax in Boston
5.4 0.24/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ëst…ôn ti Ààp…ërti / (AmE), / Ààb…íst…ôn ti Ààp…ëÀêti / (BrE)
