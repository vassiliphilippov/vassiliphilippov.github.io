Brihaspati

proper noun
Hindu god of ritual devotion and wisdom
9.9
Pronunciation: /brɪ.hə.ˈspɑː.tiː/ /; / /briː.hə.ˈspɑː.tiː/ (AmE), /brɪ.hə.ˈspɑː.tiː/ /; / /briː.hə.ˈspɑː.tiː/ (BrE)
In Hindu mythology, the personification of ritual devotion and the preceptor of the gods; regarded as the god of wisdom and eloquence.
