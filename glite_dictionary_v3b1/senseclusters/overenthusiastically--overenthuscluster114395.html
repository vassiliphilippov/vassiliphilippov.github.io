overenthusiastically

adverb
with excessive excitement
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪnˌθjuːziˈæstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that shows too much excitement or eagerness

Examples

Clap overenthusiastically
He clapped overenthusiastically at the end of every performance, drawing attention from the audience.
The students overenthusiastically volunteered for every activity, overwhelming the organizers.
She greeted her new coworkers overenthusiastically, which surprised some of them.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apathetically - in a way that shows little or no emotion or interest
  • degree lower: enthusiastically - in a way that shows excitement or eagerness
