bhut

noun
South Asian ghost
Pronunciation: / buːt / (AmE), / buːt / (BrE)
a ghost or spirit in South Asian folklore, often believed to haunt certain places or people

Examples

A bhut haunts
In many stories, a bhut is said to wander deserted roads at night.
Villagers believe the abandoned well is haunted by a bhut.
The children were afraid to enter the forest, fearing they might see a bhut.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ghost - the spirit of a dead person, believed to appear to the living
  • synonym: spirit - a supernatural being or essence, often invisible
