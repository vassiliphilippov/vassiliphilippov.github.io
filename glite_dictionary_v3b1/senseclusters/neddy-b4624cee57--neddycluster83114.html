Neddy

proper noun
nickname for Ned
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛdi / (AmE), / ˈnɛdi / (BrE)
a familiar or affectionate nickname for the male given name Ned, itself a diminutive of Edward

Examples

Neddy the shopkeeper
Everyone in the village knew Neddy as the friendly shopkeeper on the corner.
Although his real name was Edward, his friends always called him Neddy.
At family gatherings, Neddy would tell stories from his childhood.
