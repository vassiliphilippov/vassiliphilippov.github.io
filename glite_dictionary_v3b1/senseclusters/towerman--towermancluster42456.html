towerman

noun
control tower operator
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈtaʊəˌmæn / (BrE)
a person who operates or works in a control or signal tower, especially for railways or airports

Examples

Railway towerman
The towerman carefully monitored the tracks to ensure all trains moved safely.
As a towerman, he was responsible for coordinating signals at the busy railway junction.
The airport towerman communicated with pilots to guide them during takeoff and landing.
The towerman carefully monitored the planes taking off and landing at the busy airport.
As a towerman, she was responsible for ensuring the safe movement of trains through the station.
During the storm, the experienced towerman coordinated emergency procedures with the pilots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: controller - a person who manages or directs operations, especially in transport
  • synonym: signalman - a person who operates signals, especially on a railway
