Indian rosewood

noun
tree yielding dark hardwood, native to India

Pronunciation: /ˈɪn.diː.ən ˈroʊz.wʊd/ /; / /ˈɪn.dɪ.ən ˈroʊz.wʊd/ (AmE), /ˈɪn.dɪ.ən ˈrəʊz.wʊd/ /; / /ˈɪn.diː.ən ˈrəʊz.wʊd/ (BrE)
