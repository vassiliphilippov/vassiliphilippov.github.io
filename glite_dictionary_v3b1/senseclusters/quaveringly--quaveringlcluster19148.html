quaveringly

adverb
in a trembling or unsteady way
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkweɪvɚɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkweɪvərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a trembling or unsteady manner, as if with nervousness or emotion

Examples

To speak quaveringly
He replied quaveringly, betraying his uncertainty and fear.
Her voice rose quaveringly as she addressed the nervous crowd.
The candlelight flickered quaveringly on the old man's wrinkled face.
