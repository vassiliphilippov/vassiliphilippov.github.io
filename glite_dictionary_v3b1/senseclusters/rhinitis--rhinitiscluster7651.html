rhinitis

noun
inflammation of the nasal lining
Pronunciation: / raɪˈnaɪtɪs / (AmE), / raɪˈnaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the mucous membrane of the nose, typically causing nasal discharge and congestion

Examples

Chronic rhinitis
After catching a cold, he developed chronic rhinitis that lasted for weeks.
Allergy sufferers often experience rhinitis during the spring due to pollen in the air.
The doctor diagnosed her with allergic rhinitis and recommended antihistamines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hay fever - allergic rhinitis caused by pollen
  • synonym: sinusitis - inflammation of the sinuses
