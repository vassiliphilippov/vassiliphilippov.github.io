public restroom

noun
toilet facility open to the public
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk ˈrɛstroʊm / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈrɛstrʊm / (BrE)
A toilet facility that is available for use by members of the general public, often located in public buildings or spaces.

Examples

A public restroom
It's sometimes hard to find a public restroom in the city center.
Many travelers rely on public restrooms at train stations during long trips.
The museum had a clean public restroom near the entrance.
