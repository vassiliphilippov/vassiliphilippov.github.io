intracellular

adjective
inside a cell or cells
Pronunciation: / ɪntrəˈsɛljələr / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈsɛljʊlə / (BrE)
located or occurring within a cell or cells

Examples

Intracellular infection
An intracellular infection can be harder to treat than an extracellular one.
Scientists measured changes in intracellular calcium levels during the experiment.
The bacteria live and multiply in the intracellular environment of their host.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extracellular - located or occurring outside a cell or cells
