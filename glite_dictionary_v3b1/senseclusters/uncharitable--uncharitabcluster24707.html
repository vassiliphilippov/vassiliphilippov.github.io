uncharitable

adjective
not kind or generous; harsh in judgment
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃærɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɑːrɪtəbəl / (BrE)
not showing kindness, generosity, or sympathy towards others; harsh or critical in judgment.

Examples

Uncharitable comments
Her uncharitable comments made the situation even more uncomfortable for everyone involved.
It would be uncharitable to assume he acted out of selfishness alone.
The review was surprisingly uncharitable given the effort put into the production.

Related Terms

  • antonym: charitable - showing kindness and generosity
  • synonym: generous - showing a readiness to give more of something than is necessary or expected
  • synonym: kind - having or showing a friendly, generous, and considerate nature
