pass off

verb
disregard or dismiss as unimportant
2.5 3.3
Pronunciation: /pæs ɔːf/ /; / /pæs ɑːf/ (AmE), /pɑːs ɒf/ /; / /pɑːs ɔːf/ (BrE)
