popinjay

noun
vain, talkative person
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːpɪnˌdʒeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɒpɪndʒeɪ / (BrE)
a person who is vain and talkative, especially in a showy or boastful manner

Examples

A vain popinjay
Don't be such a popinjay—nobody likes a braggart.
He strutted about the office like a popinjay, boasting about his latest achievements.
The court was full of popinjays competing for the king's attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chatterbox - a person who talks a lot, especially about trivial matters
  • synonym: dandy - a man unduly concerned with style and appearance
