perigee

noun
point closest to Earth in orbit
8.1 8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈper.ə.dʒiː/ /; / /ˈper.ɪ.dʒiː/ (AmE), /ˈpe.rɪ.dʒiː/ /; / /ˈpe.rə.dʒiː/ (BrE)
