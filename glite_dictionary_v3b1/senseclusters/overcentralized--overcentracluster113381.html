overcentralized

adjective
excessively centralized
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈsɛntrəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsɛntrəlaɪzd / (BrE)
having too much control or authority concentrated in a single place or group

Examples

An overcentralized government
Many experts believe the government has become overcentralized, limiting regional decision-making.
The company's overcentralized management structure slowed down its response to local market needs.
Some argue that an overcentralized education system fails to address community-specific issues.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: centralized - controlled by a single authority or location
  • antonym: decentralized - distributed control among several locations or groups
