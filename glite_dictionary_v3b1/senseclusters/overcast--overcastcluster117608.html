overcast

adjective
cloudy or dull (weather)
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌkæst / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəkɑːst / (BrE)
(of the sky or weather) covered with clouds; dull or gloomy.

Examples

Overcast weather
It looks like rain is coming with such an overcast morning.
The sky was overcast all day, making everything look gray and dreary.
We postponed our picnic because the weather was so overcast.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - (of the sky) free from clouds or mist
  • synonym: cloudy - (of the sky) full of or covered with clouds
