Synonyms

  • cloudy
    1.0 2/m
    cloudy describes a sky with many clouds and light gloom, while overcast suggests thicker cloud cover and duller light.
  • leaden
    6.0 0.07/m
    leaden is dark, heavy, and low in the sky, while overcast can be more neutral cloud cover without heavy gloom.
  • cloud-covered
    3.9 0.01/m
    cloud-covered means simply hidden by clouds on a surface, while overcast suggests a sky-wide, gloomy cloud cover.
  • clouded
    2.1 0.00/m
    clouded often describes something made unclear by clouds or other things, while overcast refers specifically to dull, cloudy weather.
