corpse-to-be

noun
someone extremely tired or near death (humorous)
7.8 8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkɔːrps.tə.biː/ /; / /ˈkoʊrps.tə.biː/ (AmE), /ˈkɔːps.tə.biː/ (BrE)
a humorous term for someone extremely fatigued or appearing likely to die soon; a person who seems almost dead from tiredness or poor health
