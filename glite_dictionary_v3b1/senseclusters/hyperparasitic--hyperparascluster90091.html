hyperparasitic

adjective
parasitic on parasites
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˌpærəˈsɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˌpærəˈsɪtɪk / (BrE)
describing an organism that lives as a parasite on another parasite

Examples

Hyperparasitic fungus
The scientist discovered a hyperparasitic fungus that attacks other parasitic fungi.
Some wasps are hyperparasitic, laying their eggs inside the larvae of other parasitic insects.
Researchers study hyperparasitic relationships to understand complex food webs in ecosystems.
