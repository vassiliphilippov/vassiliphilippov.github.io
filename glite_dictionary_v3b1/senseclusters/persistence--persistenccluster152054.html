persistence

noun
continued effort despite difficulty
1.5 3.4
Pronunciation: /pər.ˈsɪs.təns/ (AmE), /pə.ˈsɪs.təns/ (BrE)
the quality of continuing firmly or steadily in an opinion, action, or task despite difficulty or opposition
