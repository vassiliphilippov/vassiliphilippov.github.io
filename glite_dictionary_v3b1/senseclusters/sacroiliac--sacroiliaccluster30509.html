sacroiliac

noun
pelvic joint
Pronunciation: / ˌsækrəʊˈɪliæk / (AmE), / ˌsækrəʊˈɪliæk / (BrE)
the joint between the sacrum and the ilium bones in the pelvis

Examples

The sacroiliac joint
The doctor examined her sacroiliac to determine the cause of her lower back pain.
Injuries to the sacroiliac are common among athletes who do a lot of lifting.
Physical therapy can help relieve discomfort in the sacroiliac joint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hip joint - the joint connecting the thigh bone to the pelvis
  • synonym: pelvis - the bony structure at the base of the spine
