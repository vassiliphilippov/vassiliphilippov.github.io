expansivity

noun
quality of being expansive
5.4
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌspænˈsɪvəti / (AmE), / ɪkˌspænˈsɪvɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being expansive, either in scale, generosity, or the tendency to expand.

Examples

Material expansivity
Engineers must account for the expansivity of materials when designing large structures.
She was known for the expansivity of her hospitality and generosity.
The expansivity of the mansion impressed all the visitors at the gala.
