ambit

noun
scope or limits of influence
Pronunciation: / ˈæmbɪt / (AmE), / ˈæmbɪt / (BrE)
the scope, range, or limits within which something acts, operates, or has influence or control

Examples

The ambit of law
The court ruled that the contract falls within the ambit of the new law.
The proposed changes lie outside the ambit of my responsibilities.
Topics of international trade are within the ambit of this report.

Related Terms

  • synonym: limit - the point or level beyond which something does not extend
  • synonym: range - the area of variation between upper and lower limits on a scale
  • synonym: scope - the extent of the area or subject matter that something deals with
