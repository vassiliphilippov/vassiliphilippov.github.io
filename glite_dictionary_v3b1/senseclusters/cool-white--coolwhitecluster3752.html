cool-white

adjective
white tinged with blue
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːl waɪt / (AmE), / ˈkuːl waɪt / (BrE)
(of white) having a slight blue tinge, giving a cooler appearance than pure white.

Examples

Cool-white light
She chose a cool-white paint to give the room a crisp, modern feel.
The cool-white tones of the dress contrasted nicely with her warm skin tone.
The new LED bulbs emit a cool-white light that works well in kitchens and offices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: warm-white - white with a slight yellow or red tinge
