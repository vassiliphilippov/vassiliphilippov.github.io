grading

noun
evaluating by assigning grades or scores
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of evaluating performance by assigning a grade or score, typically in academic settings

Examples

Test grading
Automation has made grading standardized tests much faster and more accurate.
He finds grading to be the most tedious part of his job.
The teacher spent her weekend grading essays for all her classes.

  • synonym: scoring - the act of assigning a score or points
