recognizably

adverb
in a way that can be recognized
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛk.əɡ.naɪ.zə.bli / (AmE), / ˈrɛk.əɡ.nʌɪ.zəb.li / (BrE)
To a degree that is able to be recognized or identified.

Examples

To be recognizably different
Each painting in the series is recognizably by the same artist.
Her accent was recognizably Canadian, even after all these years abroad.
The city center was recognizably different after the renovations.
