clout nail

noun
short nail with a flat head for attaching metal to wood
3.7
Pronunciation: / klaʊt neɪl / (AmE), / klaʊt neɪl / (BrE)
a short nail with a large, flat head, used for attaching sheet metal or similar materials to wood

Examples

A clout nail box
A box of clout nails was left in the shed after the repairs were done.
Always choose a clout nail when attaching thin sheet metal to prevent tearing.
The roofer used a clout nail to fasten the lead flashing securely to the timber.
