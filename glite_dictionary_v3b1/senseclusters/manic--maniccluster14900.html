manic

adjective
showing frenzied or manic energy
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪk / (AmE), / ˈmænɪk / (BrE)
Showing wild, frenzied energy or activity, often as a result of mania or extreme excitement.

Examples

A manic pace
He worked at a manic pace to meet the deadline.
She cleaned the whole house with manic energy after hearing the good news.
The comedian's manic style kept the audience laughing throughout the show.
