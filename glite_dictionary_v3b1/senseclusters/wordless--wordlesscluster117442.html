wordless

adjective
without words; silent or unspoken
4.1 1.41/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…ùÀêd.l…ôs / (AmE), / Ààw…úÀêd.l…ôs / (BrE)
expressed or occurring without the use of spoken or written words; silent or unspoken.

Examples

A wordless look
She gave him a wordless look that spoke volumes about her disappointment.
The wordless song carried deep emotion, moving everyone in the audience.
Through a wordless gesture, he offered his comfort to the grieving family.
