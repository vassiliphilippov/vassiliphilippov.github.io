socializing

noun
the act of meeting for social purposes
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəˌlaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəlaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of meeting and spending time with other people for pleasure and to strengthen relationships; the act of being sociable

Examples

Socializing with friends
Most college students spend a good deal of time socializing on campus.
She enjoys socializing with her coworkers after hours.
Too much socializing can distract from studying.
Many people find socialising at work events both enjoyable and beneficial for networking.
Online platforms have changed the nature of socialising in the modern world.
She values socialising with friends more than spending time alone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mingling - moving among and talking to people at a social event
  • synonym: networking - interacting with others to exchange information and develop contacts
  • synonym: socializing - the activity of spending time with others for pleasure (American spelling)
