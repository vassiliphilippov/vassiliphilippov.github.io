Examples

Internet bandwidth
If too many devices are connected, the available bandwidth may not be sufficient for everyone's needs.
Our office upgraded its bandwidth to improve download speeds for all employees.
Streaming high-definition videos requires a high bandwidth internet connection.

Synonyms

  • bitrate
    4.2 0.08/m
    bitrate is the measured speed of bits sent per second, while bandwidth is the maximum rate for a network connection.
  • data rate
    3.4 0.75/m
    data rate refers to how fast data moves or is processed, while bandwidth names the maximum transfer rate for a network connection.
  • channel capacity
    6.1 0.04/m
    channel capacity is the theoretical highest error-free data rate a channel can carry, while bandwidth is the practical maximum over a network.
