promulgation

noun
official enactment or announcement of a law
9.3 6.8
Pronunciation: /prɑː.məl.ˈgeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /prə.məl.ˈgeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /prɒm.əl.ˈgeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /prɒm.ʊl.ˈgeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the official public announcement or declaration of a new law, regulation, or decision, especially by an authority
