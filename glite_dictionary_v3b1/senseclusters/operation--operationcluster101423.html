operation

noun
military or naval activity or mission
5.4 2.7
Pronunciation: /ɑː.pə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɒ.pə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a planned or organized activity, especially by a group (such as military, police, or other coordinated actions) to achieve a specific goal

Examples

Military operation
After several weeks of planning, the joint operation between the air force and navy began.
He was awarded a medal for his bravery during the rescue operation.
The army launched a surprise operation at dawn to capture the enemy base.
After months of planning, the police launched a major operation against the drug cartel.
Running the restaurant is quite an operation, requiring careful coordination between the staff.
The rescue operation involved hundreds of volunteers and several helicopters.
