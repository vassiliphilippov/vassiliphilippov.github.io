howler

noun
monkey known for howling cries
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊlɚ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊlə / (BrE)
A large monkey found in the forests of Central and South America, known for its loud, howling calls.

Examples

A howler monkey
Researchers observed the sleeping habits of a young male howler in the rainforest.
The loud calls of the howler could be heard for several miles through the jungle.
We spotted a group of howlers swinging high up in the trees.
