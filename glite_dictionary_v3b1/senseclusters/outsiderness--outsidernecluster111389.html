outsiderness

noun
state of being an outsider
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtsaɪdərnəs / (AmE), / ˈaʊtsaɪdənɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of feeling or being an outsider, separate from a group or community

Examples

A feeling of outsiderness
His strong sense of outsiderness made it difficult for him to connect with his classmates.
The novel explores themes of outsiderness and acceptance in a small rural town.
Moving to a new country, she struggled with feelings of outsiderness for many months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alienation - the feeling of being isolated or estranged from a group
  • antonym: belonging - the feeling of being accepted as part of a group
