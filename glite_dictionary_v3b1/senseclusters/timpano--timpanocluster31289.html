timpano

noun
single kettledrum
Pronunciation: / tɪmˈpɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / tɪmˈpɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
a single kettledrum, especially one used in orchestras as the singular form of timpani

Examples

Orchestra timpano
The percussionist carefully tuned the timpano before the orchestra rehearsal began.
A thunderous roll on the timpano signaled the dramatic start of the symphony.
During the concert, the soloist performed an impressive passage on the timpano.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drum - a percussion instrument sounded by being struck
  • degree higher: timpani - a set of large kettledrums used in orchestras
