hellfire

noun
eternal fire as punishment for sinners
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɛlˌfaɪə / (BrE)
(in religion) a place or state of eternal fire, often depicted as a punishment for sinners after death.

Stories of hellfire were often used to instill fear and encourage moral behavior.
