admirable

adjective
deserving respect or approval
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.mɚ.ə.bəl /; / ˈæd.mə.rə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈæd.mɪ.rə.bəl / (BrE)
deserving respect or approval; inspiring admiration

Examples

An admirable person
His admirable honesty earned him the trust of everyone at work.
It is admirable how she remains calm under pressure.
She did an admirable job caring for her elderly parents.
He possessed an admirable sense of fairness that made him a trusted leader.
Her admirable dedication to her work has earned her the respect of her colleagues.
The rescue team showed admirable courage during the dangerous mission.

  • antonym: deplorable - deserving strong condemnation or disapproval
  • antonym: dishonorable - not deserving respect or honor
  • synonym: praiseworthy - deserving praise and approval
