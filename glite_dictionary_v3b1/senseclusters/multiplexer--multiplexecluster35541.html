multiplexer

noun

a device that selects and transmits one of several input signals onto a single output line, enabling multiple signals or activities to be combined and sent through a single channel.

Examples

A multiplexer device
A digital multiplexer is often used in telecommunications to streamline signal processing.
Engineers configured the multiplexer to switch between video feeds for the security system.
The multiplexer allows several data streams to share a single communication channel efficiently.

Synonyms

  • mux

  • data multiplexer
    5.9 0.00/m
