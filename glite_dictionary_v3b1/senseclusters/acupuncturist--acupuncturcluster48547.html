acupuncturist

noun
practitioner of acupuncture
Pronunciation: / ˈækjəˌpʌŋktʃərɪst / (AmE), / ˈækjʊˌpʌŋktʃərɪst / (BrE)
a person trained to treat pain or illness by inserting fine needles into specific points on the body

Examples

The acupuncturist treats pain
The acupuncturist carefully placed needles to help relieve my shoulder pain.
Many people visit an acupuncturist for alternative treatments when other methods do not work.
My friend became a licensed acupuncturist after years of study and training.
The acupuncturist placed needles in my back to help relieve my pain.
After several sessions with her acupuncturist, she noticed an improvement in her symptoms.
He became an acupuncturist after studying traditional Chinese medicine for many years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doctor - a person qualified to practice medicine
  • synonym: therapist - a person trained to treat physical or mental health problems
