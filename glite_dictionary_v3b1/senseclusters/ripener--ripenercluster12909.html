ripener

noun
substance for ripening
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪpənər / (AmE), / ˈraɪpənə / (BrE)
a substance or device used to make something become ripe or mature more quickly

Examples

A ripener substance
The bananas were treated with a ripener to ensure they were ready for sale.
A chemical ripener can help speed up the harvest process for some crops.
The use of a ripener is common in commercial fruit production.
