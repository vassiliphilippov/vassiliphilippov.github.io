review copy

noun
book sent for review
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvju ˈkɑpi / (AmE), / rɪˈvjuː ˈkɒpi / (BrE)
a copy of a newly published book sent to a reviewer or publication for evaluation and public review

Examples

A review copy of a book
Please send a review copy to our book editor for consideration.
She was thrilled to get a review copy of the novel before its release date.
The magazine receives dozens of review copies each month from publishers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advance copy - an early copy of a book provided before official release
