Maxim gun

noun
early water-cooled machine gun
Pronunciation: / ˈmæk.sɪm ˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈmaksɪm ˌɡʌn / (BrE)
an early type of fully automatic, water-cooled machine gun with a single barrel, invented in the late 19th century and now obsolete

Examples

A Maxim gun
A Maxim gun can still be seen on display at the history museum.
British colonial forces made effective use of the Maxim gun during their campaigns.
The Maxim gun revolutionized warfare at the turn of the 20th century.
