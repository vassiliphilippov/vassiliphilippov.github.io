degenerative disorder

noun
progressively worsening disease
3.1 4.7
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈdʒen.ə.rə.tɪv dɪs.ˈɔːr.dər/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈdʒen.ə.rə.tɪv dɪs.ˈɔː.də/ (BrE)
a medical condition characterized by progressive deterioration of tissue or function in the body, often worsening over time
