poles apart

adverb
completely different or opposed
5.4
Pronunciation: / poʊlz əˈpɑrt / (AmE), / pəʊlz əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
completely different or opposed to each other in every possible way

Examples

Poles apart views
Our views on ethics are poles apart, yet we still respect each other.
The siblings are poles apart in their approach to handling problems.
Though they work in the same industry, their design philosophies are poles apart.

Related Terms

  • antonym: like-minded - having similar opinions or interests
  • synonym: opposite - completely different or contrary
