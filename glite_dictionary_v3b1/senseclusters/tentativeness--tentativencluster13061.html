tentativeness

noun
uncertainty; hesitation
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈten.tə.tɪv.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈten.tə.tɪv.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being uncertain, hesitant, or not fully confident or decided
