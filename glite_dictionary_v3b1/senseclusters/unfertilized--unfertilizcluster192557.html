unfertilized

adjective
not made fertile; not fertilized
3.4 4.1
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈfɜːr.tɪ.laɪzd/ /; / /ə.ˈfɜːr.tɪ.laɪzd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈfɜː.tɪ.laɪzd/ /; / /ə.ˈfɜː.tɪ.laɪzd/ (BrE)
(of an egg, soil, etc.) not having been made fertile, especially not having undergone fertilization.

Examples

Unfertilized egg
Most flowers produce unfertilized seeds if pollination does not occur.
She studied the difference between fertilized and unfertilized samples under the microscope.
The chicken laid an unfertilized egg, so it will never develop into a chick.
Chickens can lay unfertilised eggs even without a rooster present.
It is safe to eat an unfertilised egg, as it does not contain a developing embryo.
The biologist studied the difference between fertilised and unfertilised eggs under the microscope.
