tab

noun
key for tabulation on keyboard
2.5 2.1
Pronunciation: /tæb/ (AmE), /tæb/ (BrE)
the key on a typewriter or computer keyboard that moves the cursor to a predetermined position, used for tabulation or indentation

Examples

Tab key
Many forms allow you to move to the next field by hitting the tab.
She pressed the tab to indent the first line of her paragraph.
To create a table, use the tab key to align the columns.
