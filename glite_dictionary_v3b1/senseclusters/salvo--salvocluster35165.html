salvo

noun
sudden outburst of cheers or applause
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsælvoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsalvəʊ / (BrE)
a sudden or simultaneous outburst of applause, cheers, words, or similar expressions

Examples

A salvo of applause
A salvo of applause erupted as the curtain fell on the final act.
She received a salvo of support after announcing her ambitious plans.
The speaker's comment was met with a salvo of laughter from the audience.
A salvo of applause erupted as the speaker concluded his speech.
She opened the meeting with a salvo of questions directed at the management team.
The politician's announcement was met with a salvo of criticism from the opposition.

  • synonym: burst - a sudden brief occurrence of something
