ectoproct

noun
sessile aquatic animal, bryozoan
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈek.tə.prɑːkt/ /; / /ˈek.toʊ.prɑːkt/ (AmE), /ˈek.tə.prɒkt/ /; / /ˈek.təʊ.prɒkt/ (BrE)
A sessile aquatic invertebrate of the phylum Ectoprocta, having the anus located outside the crown of tentacles; also called a bryozoan.

Examples

A ectoproct colony
Researchers discovered a rare ectoproct colony attached to the submerged rocks in the lake.
The museum's new exhibit showcases fossilized remains of several ancient ectoprocts.
Unlike some other marine invertebrates, the ectoproct has its anus outside the circle of tentacles.
