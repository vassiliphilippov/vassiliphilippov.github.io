glassily

adverb
in a clear, ringing way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is clear, high-pitched, and ringing, like the sound of glass.

Examples

Glassily ringing
The chandelier tinkled glassily as the wind blew through the open window.
Her laughter rang out glassily in the quiet room, echoing off the walls.
The old piano's notes sounded glassily after it was tuned.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dully - in a way that is not clear or resonant
  • synonym: shrilly - in a high-pitched and piercing manner
