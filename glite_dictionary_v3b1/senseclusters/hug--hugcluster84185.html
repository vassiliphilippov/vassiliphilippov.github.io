hug

noun
an affectionate embrace
1.0
Pronunciation: / hʌg / (AmE), / hʌg / (BrE)
an act of holding someone tightly in one's arms, typically as a sign of affection

Examples

A warm hug
A simple hug can make someone feel much better.
After the long trip, he greeted his mother with a big hug.
She gave her friend a warm hug before leaving.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embrace - an act of holding someone closely in one's arms
  • degree higher: kiss - an act of touching with the lips, often as a sign of affection
