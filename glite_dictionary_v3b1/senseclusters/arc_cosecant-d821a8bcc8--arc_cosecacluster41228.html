arc cosecant

noun
inverse trigonometric function of cosecant
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrk koʊˈsiːkənt / (AmE), / ˈɑːk kəʊˈsiːkənt / (BrE)
the inverse trigonometric function that gives the angle whose cosecant is a given number

The arc cosecant function
