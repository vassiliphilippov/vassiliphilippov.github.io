variegated prefix
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛəri / (AmE), / ˈveəri / (BrE)
a combining form meaning 'variegated' or 'having irregular patches or marks of different colors', used in botanical and descriptive terms

Examples

Vari-colored leaves
The prefix vari- is often used in plant names to indicate multicolored leaves.
In scientific terms, vari- helps describe species with irregular color patterns.
Gardeners look for the vari- prefix when searching for plants with unique foliage.
