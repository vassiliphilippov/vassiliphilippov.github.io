emotionlessly

adverb
without showing emotion
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊʃənlɪsli / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənləsli / (BrE)
In a manner showing no emotion or feeling.

Examples

Speak emotionlessly
He delivered the news emotionlessly, giving no hint of how he truly felt.
She stared emotionlessly out of the window, lost in thought.
Their faces remained emotionlessly blank during the entire meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coldly - in an unemotional or unfriendly manner
  • antonym: emotionally - with strong emotion or feeling
  • synonym: unemotionally - in an unemotional manner, without showing emotion
