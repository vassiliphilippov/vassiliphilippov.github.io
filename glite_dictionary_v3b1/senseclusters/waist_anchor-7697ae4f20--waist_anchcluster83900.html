waist anchor

noun
spare anchor used in emergencies
Pronunciation: / weɪst ˈæŋkɚ / (AmE), / weɪst ˈaŋkə / (BrE)
a spare anchor kept on a ship for use in emergencies

Examples

The waist anchor
During the storm, the captain ordered the use of the waist anchor to stabilize the ship.
Every vessel should carry a waist anchor for unexpected emergencies at sea.
The ship's crew quickly deployed the waist anchor when the main anchor failed.
