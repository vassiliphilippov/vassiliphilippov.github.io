chitchat

noun
casual informal conversation
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪtˌtʃæt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪtˌtʃæt / (BrE)
casual and light informal conversation about unimportant topics, typically to be sociable

Examples

Office chitchat
Before the meeting started, they exchanged a bit of chitchat about their weekends.
Her email was mostly chitchat with nothing important to report.
We enjoyed some friendly chitchat during our coffee break.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banter - playful and friendly exchange of teasing remarks
  • synonym: conversation - talk, especially informal exchange of news and ideas
  • antonym: discussion - the act of talking about something seriously
