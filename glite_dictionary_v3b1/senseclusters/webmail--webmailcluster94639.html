webmail

noun
browser-based email
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛbˌmeɪl / (AmE), / ˈwɛbˌmeɪl / (BrE)
an email service that can be accessed and managed through a web browser instead of a dedicated email program

Examples

Webmail service
Many people use webmail to check their messages from any device with internet access.
If you forget your laptop, you can still read your webmail at the library.
Some companies provide secure webmail accounts for their employees.

Related Terms

  • antonym: desktop email client - a program installed on a computer to manage email
  • synonym: email - messages sent electronically over a computer network
