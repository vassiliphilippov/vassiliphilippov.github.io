aquarist

noun
person caring for aquariums
Pronunciation: / əˈkwɛrɪst / (AmE), / əˈkwærɪst / (BrE)
a person who keeps, cares for, or studies fish and other aquatic life in aquariums

Examples

An aquarist cares for fish
The experienced aquarist set up a new tank for tropical fish in the classroom.
Many aquarists enjoy breeding rare species and sharing their knowledge online.
At the aquarium, a professional aquarist explained how to care for coral reefs.
The aquarist at the public aquarium gave a talk about coral reef conservation.
As a dedicated aquarist, she spends hours maintaining her tropical fish tanks.
Many aquarists join clubs to share advice and learn about new species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fishkeeper - a person who keeps fish as pets in aquariums
  • synonym: ichthyologist - a scientist who studies fish
  • synonym: zookeeper - a person who cares for animals in a zoo
