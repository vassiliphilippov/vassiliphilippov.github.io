riverway

noun
navigable river route
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvərˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɪvəˌweɪ / (BrE)
a navigable route or channel along a river, often used for transportation or travel

Examples

Busy riverway for boats
The city expanded along the riverway to take advantage of trade opportunities.
Boats travel up and down the riverway carrying goods between towns.
A new bridge was built to cross the busy riverway near the port.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canal - an artificial waterway for navigation or irrigation
  • synonym: waterway - a navigable body of water such as a river, canal, or channel
