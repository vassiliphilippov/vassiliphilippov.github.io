soaringly

adverb
in a rising manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːrɪŋli /; / ˈsɔrɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsɔːrɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that rises high, increases rapidly, or is grand and uplifting (physically or emotionally)

Examples

Fly soaringly high
The eagle flew soaringly above the mountain peaks, almost touching the clouds.
Profits have risen soaringly since the company launched its new product line.
Her confidence grew soaringly after she received the award for her hard work.
The music rose soaringly during the final scene, moving the entire audience to tears.
Her speech was soaringly optimistic, inspiring hope in everyone who listened.
The skyscraper stands soaringly above the city, visible from miles away.
