sensual

adjective
relating to physical or sexual pleasure
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn.tʃu.əl / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.sju.əl / (BrE)
relating to or derived from physical pleasure, especially sexual or involving the senses rather than the intellect

Examples

Sensual pleasure
His paintings often evoke a sensual appreciation for color, texture, and form.
She wore a sensual perfume that lingered in the air long after she left the room.
The chef prepared a meal that was a truly sensual experience for all the senses.
