mosaic

noun
artwork made of small colored pieces
Pronunciation: / moʊˈzeɪɪk / (AmE), / məʊˈzeɪɪk / (BrE)
a picture or pattern produced by arranging together small pieces of colored stone, glass, or other materials, used as decoration in art or architecture

Examples

A mosaic artwork
Many cathedrals feature a large mosaic above the altar depicting religious scenes.
She is learning to create a mosaic using colored glass tiles for her art class.
The ancient floor was decorated with a stunning mosaic of geometric designs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tessellation - an arrangement of shapes closely fitted together in a repeated pattern without gaps or overlapping
