tug

verb
strive or make an effort
Pronunciation: / tʌɡ / (AmE), / tʌɡ / (BrE)
to make a strenuous or persistent effort to achieve something; to strive hard

Examples

To tug toward success
Despite many setbacks, he kept tugging toward his dream of becoming a doctor.
She tugged for years to provide for her family before finally succeeding.
The team tugged to meet the tight project deadline, working late most nights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: relax - to become less tense or to stop striving
  • synonym: strive - to make great efforts to achieve or obtain something
  • synonym: struggle - to make forceful or violent efforts to get free or achieve something
