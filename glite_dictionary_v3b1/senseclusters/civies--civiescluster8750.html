civies

noun
civilian clothes (as opposed to uniform)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪviz / (AmE), / ˈsɪviːz / (BrE)
Clothes worn by civilians, especially as distinct from a military uniform; civilian clothes.

Examples

Civies clothes
After weeks in the field, the soldiers were happy to change into their civies.
He kept a set of civies in his locker for when he was off duty.
You could always tell who just got leave by the fresh look of their civies.
