ecosphere

noun
Earth's living system
Pronunciation: / ˈiːkoʊˌsfɪr / (AmE), / ˈiːkəˌsfɪə / (BrE)
the entire system of living organisms and their environment on Earth, considered as a whole

Examples

The Earth's ecosphere
Scientists study the ecosphere to understand how living things interact with their environment.
Climate change poses a serious threat to the balance of the ecosphere.
Efforts to reduce pollution help protect the delicate ecosphere of our planet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biosphere - the regions of the Earth where life exists
  • synonym: environment - the natural world, including air, water, and land
