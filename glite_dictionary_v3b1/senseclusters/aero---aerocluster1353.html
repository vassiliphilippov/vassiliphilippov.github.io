aero-

x
prefix: air, flight
Pronunciation: / ˈɛroʊ / (AmE), / ˈeərəʊ / (BrE)
prefix meaning 'air' or 'related to flight', used in words about aviation or the atmosphere

Examples

Aero-plane flight
The prefix aero- is found in words like 'aeroplane' and 'aerodynamics'.
In science class, we learned that aero- means something related to air or flight.
The term 'aerospace' combines aero- with 'space' to refer to air and space travel.
