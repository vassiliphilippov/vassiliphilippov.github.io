vasovesiculitis

noun
inflammation of vas deferens and seminal vesicles
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌveɪsoʊvəˌsɪkjəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌveɪsəʊvəˌsɪkjʊˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the vas deferens and seminal vesicles, usually occurring with prostatitis.

Examples

Chronic vasovesiculitis
