arrester

noun
device that stops movement
6.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈres.tər/ (AmE), /ə.ˈres.tə/ (BrE)
a device that stops or limits the movement or flow of something, especially in machinery or electrical systems

Examples

Surge arrester
The technician installed a arrester to prevent electrical surges from damaging the circuit.
A spark arrester is required on engines to stop sparks from escaping into dry grass.
The machinery was fitted with a safety arrester to halt movement in case of emergency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protector - a device that protects equipment from damage
  • synonym: stopper - a device that blocks or closes something
