paella

noun
traditional Spanish rice dish with saffron
7.0
Pronunciation: / paɪˈɛlə / (AmE), / paɪˈɛlə / (BrE)
A Spanish dish of rice, saffron, and various ingredients such as shellfish, chicken, and vegetables, cooked and served in a large shallow pan.

Examples

Traditional paella dish
His grandmother taught him the secret to making the perfect paella.
The festival featured a giant paella cooked over an open fire.
We ordered a seafood paella at the restaurant while enjoying the view of the harbor.
