anti-life

noun
opposition to life
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈæntiˌlaɪf / (AmE), / ˈæntiˌlaɪf / (BrE)
an attitude, belief, or state of being opposed to life or the value of living things

Examples

Anti-life belief
The villain's anti-life philosophy threatened the survival of the entire world.
Some critics accused the movement of promoting an anti-life agenda.
Her writings explored the dangers of an anti-life mindset in modern society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nihilism - the belief that life is meaningless
  • antonym: pro-life - supporting the value and protection of life
