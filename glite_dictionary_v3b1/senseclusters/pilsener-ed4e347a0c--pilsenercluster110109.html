Pilsener

noun
type of pale, hoppy lager beer
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪl.zə.nɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɪl.zə.nə / (BrE)
a pale, golden lager beer with a strong flavor of hops, originally brewed in the town of Pilsen, Bohemia

Examples

A cold Pilsener
He ordered a cold Pilsener at the bar after work.
Many breweries around the world now produce their own Pilsener.
The crisp taste of this Pilsener pairs well with spicy food.
