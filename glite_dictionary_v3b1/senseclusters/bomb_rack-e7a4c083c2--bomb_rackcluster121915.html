bomb rack

noun
device on aircraft for carrying bombs
Pronunciation: / bɑm ræk / (AmE), / bɒm ræk / (BrE)
a device mounted on an aircraft for holding and releasing bombs during flight

Examples

Bomb rack on aircraft
Each bomb rack is designed to release its payload at a precise moment.
The old aircraft's bomb racks had to be replaced due to rust and wear.
The technician inspected the bomb rack before the mission to ensure it was secure.
