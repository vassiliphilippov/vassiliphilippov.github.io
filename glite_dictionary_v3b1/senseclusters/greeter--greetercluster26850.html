greeter

noun
person who welcomes or greets others
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːtɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡriːtə / (BrE)
a person whose job or role is to greet people, especially guests or customers as they arrive

Examples

Store greeter
As a volunteer greeter, she helps new members feel comfortable during their first visit.
Many large stores employ a greeter to assist and guide visitors as they enter.
The greeter at the front door welcomed each customer with a warm smile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: host - a person who receives guests
  • synonym: welcomer - someone who gives a welcome
