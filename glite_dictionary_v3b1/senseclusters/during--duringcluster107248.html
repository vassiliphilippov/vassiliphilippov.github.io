during

preposition
within a period
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʊrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdjʊərɪŋ / (BrE)
at any time within the period of an event, activity, or situation

Examples

Talk during the show
Please do not talk during the performance so others can enjoy the show.
He fell asleep during the long train journey across the country.
Many people learned new skills during the lockdown period.

Related Terms

  • antonym: after - following in time; later than
  • antonym: before - earlier than a particular time or event
  • synonym: throughout - from the beginning to the end of a period
