Synonyms

  • notch
    4.1 1.40/m
    a notch is a small V-shaped cut often used to mark or measure an edge, while an indent is a larger recess or jagged cut.
  • nick
    3.5 1.05/m
    a nick is a small accidental cut or tiny notch on an edge, while an indent is a deliberate or larger depression in a surface.
  • dent
    2.2 1.68/m
    a dent is a rounded hollow caused by a hit or pressure, while an indent is often a cut, notch, or jagged depression.
