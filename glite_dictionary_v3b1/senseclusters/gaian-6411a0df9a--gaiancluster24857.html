Gaian

noun
follower of Gaia hypothesis
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪ.ən / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪ.ən / (BrE)
a person who believes in or follows the Gaia hypothesis, viewing Earth as a living system

Examples

A Gaian believer
As a dedicated Gaian, she believes that all living things are interconnected.
The Gaian movement encourages people to respect and care for the planet.
Many Gaians support renewable energy and sustainable living practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: environmentalist - a person who is concerned with protecting the environment
