commodities market

noun
exchange for trading raw materials and goods
5.7 3.5
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈmɑː.dɪ.tɪz ˈmɑːr.kɪt/ /; / /kə.ˈmɑː.də.tɪz ˈmɑːr.kət/ (AmE), /kə.ˈmɒd.ɪ.tɪz ˈmɑː.kɪt/ /; / /kə.ˈmɒd.ə.tɪz ˈmɑː.kət/ (BrE)

Examples
