redraw

noun
loan withdrawal facility
7.4
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈdrɔː/ /; / /rə.ˈdrɔː/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈdrɔː/ /; / /riː.ˈdrɔː/ (BrE)
a facility that allows a borrower to withdraw extra payments previously made into a loan account

Examples

A redraw facility
The bank offers a redraw option so customers can access extra funds they have paid into their mortgage.
After making several extra repayments, she used the redraw facility to pay for her car repairs.
Some loans include a redraw feature, allowing borrowers to withdraw surplus payments if needed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advance - a sum of money paid before it is due or needed
  • antonym: repayment - the act of paying back money previously borrowed
