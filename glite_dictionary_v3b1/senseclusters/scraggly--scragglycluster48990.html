scraggly

adjective
thin and uneven in appearance
4.3 4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈskræ.gliː/ /; / /ˈskræ.glɪ/ (AmE), /ˈskræ.glɪ/ /; / /ˈskræ.gliː/ (BrE)
thin, uneven, or untidy in appearance, often with a rough or irregular outline

Examples

A scraggly dog
A scraggly dog wandered through the alley, its ribs showing under matted fur.
Her scraggly hair fell across her face as she worked in the garden.
The old man’s scraggly beard made him look much older than he was.
A scraggly hedge bordered the neglected front yard.
He wore a scraggly scarf that was unraveling at the edges.
The cat had a scraggly tail and looked like it hadn't eaten in days.
