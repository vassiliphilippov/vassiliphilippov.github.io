subsector

noun
division of a sector
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌsɛktər / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌsɛktə / (BrE)
a smaller, specialized division within a larger sector or industry

Examples

A subsector of industry
The financial subsector includes banks, insurance companies, and investment firms.
Each subsector of the healthcare industry faces unique challenges and regulations.
Government policies can affect every subsector within the broader manufacturing sector.
