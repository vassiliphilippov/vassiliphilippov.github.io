astrodome

noun
transparent dome for navigation on aircraft
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈæstroʊˌdoʊm / (AmE), / ˈæstrəʊˌdəʊm / (BrE)
a transparent dome on the top of an aircraft, designed to allow navigators to make celestial observations

Examples

The astrodome on aircraft
Advancements in technology have made the astrodome largely obsolete in modern aircraft.
The B-17 bomber was equipped with an astrodome to aid in night navigation.
Through the astrodome, the navigator charted the plane's course using the stars.
