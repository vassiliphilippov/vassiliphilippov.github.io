Padre

noun
military chaplain or priest
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːdreɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːdreɪ / (BrE)
(especially in the armed forces) a Christian priest, particularly one serving as a military chaplain; sometimes used as a term of address for priests, especially in the Roman Catholic or Orthodox churches.

Examples

The Padre in uniform
After the service, the Padre stayed to chat with members of the congregation.
Many soldiers found comfort talking to the Padre during difficult times.
The Padre visited the troops every morning to offer support and prayers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chaplain - a member of the clergy attached to a private chapel, institution, or branch of the armed forces
  • synonym: priest - an ordained minister of the Catholic, Orthodox, or Anglican Church
