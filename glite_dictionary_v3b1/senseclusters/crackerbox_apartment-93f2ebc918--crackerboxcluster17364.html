crackerbox apartment

noun
small, cramped apartment
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkræk.ɚ.bɑks əˌpɑrt.mənt / (AmE), / ˈkræk.ə.bɒks əˌpɑːt.mənt / (BrE)
a small, cramped, and often poorly built apartment, typically considered uncomfortable or inadequate

Examples

A crackerbox apartment
After his divorce, he moved into a crackerbox apartment on the outskirts of town.
It was difficult to fit all their furniture into their tiny crackerbox apartment.
She dreamed of owning a house instead of renting a crackerbox apartment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: studio flat - a small apartment consisting of one main room
