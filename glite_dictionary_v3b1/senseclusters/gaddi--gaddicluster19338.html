gaddi

noun
cushion or seat on a throne in India
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːdi / (AmE), / ˈɡædi / (BrE)
A cushion or seat, especially one placed on a throne for a prince or ruler in India.

Examples

The gaddi cushion
Historians examined the ancient gaddi that had once supported generations of rulers.
The prince sat upon the gaddi during the elaborate coronation ceremony.
The royal gaddi was decorated with gold embroidery and precious stones.
