mythmaker

noun
creator of false stories
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪθˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈmɪθˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person or organization that creates or promotes myths, stories, or widely believed ideas, whether true or false

Examples

A mythmaker creates stories
The political strategist was known as a skilled mythmaker who shaped public opinion with powerful stories.
Some historians argue that every society has its own mythmakers who influence how history is remembered.
The film portrayed the media as a modern mythmaker, shaping reality through selective reporting.
The famous author was often described as a mythmaker for his imaginative novels.
Historians sometimes act as mythmakers when they interpret ancient legends.
Some politicians become mythmakers by shaping the stories people believe about their country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: storyteller - a person who tells or writes stories
  • antonym: truth-teller - a person who tells the truth
