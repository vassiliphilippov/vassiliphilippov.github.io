AOB

noun
dental condition
9.9
Pronunciation: / eɪ oʊ ˈbiː / (AmE), / eɪ əʊ ˈbiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'anterior open bite', a dental condition where the upper and lower front teeth do not touch when the jaws are closed

Examples

Dental AOB diagnosis
The dentist diagnosed the patient with an AOB after examining her bite.
Correcting an AOB often requires orthodontic treatment in children and teenagers.
An AOB can make it difficult to bite into certain foods like sandwiches or apples.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malocclusion - improper alignment of the teeth when the jaws are closed
  • antonym: overbite - a dental condition where the upper front teeth overlap the lower front teeth excessively
