leaner

noun
person or thing that leans
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈliːnər / (AmE), / ˈliːnə / (BrE)
a person or thing that leans or causes something else to lean.

Examples

A leaner against the wall
The old ladder was a dangerous leaner against the barn wall.
He became a habitual leaner, always resting against the nearest surface.
That fence is a real leaner and might fall over in a strong wind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: support - something that holds up or props another thing
