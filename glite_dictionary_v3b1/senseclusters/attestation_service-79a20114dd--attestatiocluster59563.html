attestation service

noun
CPA's professional opinion on reliability of statements
Pronunciation: / əˌtɛsˈteɪʃən ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / əˌtɛsˈteɪʃən ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a professional service provided by a certified public accountant (CPA) in which an independent opinion is given about the reliability of another party's written statement

Examples

CPA attestation service
A CPA may offer an attestation service to verify compliance with regulatory requirements.
Regulators often require businesses to obtain an attestation service for certain disclosures.
The company hired a third party to perform an attestation service on its financial report.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audit - an official inspection of an organization's accounts
  • degree lower: review - an evaluation, especially a less extensive examination than an audit
