pelt

verb
rain heavily and forcefully
Pronunciation: / pɛlt / (AmE), / pɛlt / (BrE)
(of rain) to fall very heavily and forcefully.

Examples

Rain pelts the roof
Sheets of rain pelted against the window during the storm.
The rain began to pelt down just as we left the house.
We got soaked when it started to pelt on our way home.
