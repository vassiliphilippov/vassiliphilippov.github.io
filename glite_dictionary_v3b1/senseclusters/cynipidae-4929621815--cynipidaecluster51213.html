Cynipidae

noun
family of gall wasps (insects)
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnɪp.ɪˌdi /; / sɪˈnɪpəˌdiː / (AmE), / sɪˈnɪp.ɪˌdiː /; / sɪˈnɪpɪdiː / (BrE)
