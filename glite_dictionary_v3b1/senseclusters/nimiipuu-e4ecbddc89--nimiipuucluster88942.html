Nimiipuu

proper noun
Nez Perce people
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪmiˌpuː / (AmE), / ˈnɪmiˌpuː / (BrE)
the self-designation of the Indigenous people known in English as the Nez Perce, native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States

Examples

The Nimiipuu people
The Nimiipuu have lived in the Pacific Northwest for thousands of years.
Many Nimiipuu traditions are still practiced by their descendants today.
The Nimiipuu language is being revitalized through community programs.
