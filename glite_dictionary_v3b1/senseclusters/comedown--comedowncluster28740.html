comedown

noun
feeling after excitement
6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌm.daʊn/ (AmE), /ˈkʌm.daʊn/ (BrE)
a period of feeling worse or less energetic after excitement, especially following drug use or a high point

Examples

Post-party comedown
After the concert ended, she felt a sudden comedown as the excitement faded.
Many people experience a comedown after using certain recreational drugs.
The team's victory was followed by a tough comedown when they lost the next game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: high - a period of intense excitement or euphoria
  • synonym: letdown - a disappointment or decrease in excitement
