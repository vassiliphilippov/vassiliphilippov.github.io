spirillum fever

noun
form of rat-bite fever in the Far East
Pronunciation: / spɪˈrɪləm ˈfiːvər / (AmE), / spɪˈrɪləm ˈfiːvə / (BrE)
A form of rat-bite fever prevalent in the Far East, caused by infection with bacteria of the genus Spirillum.

Examples

Spirillum fever infection
Outbreaks of spirillum fever have been reported in several Asian cities.
Prompt antibiotic treatment can effectively cure spirillum fever.
The doctor suspected spirillum fever after hearing about the patient's recent travel to the Far East.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rat-bite fever - infectious disease from rat bites, caused by bacteria
