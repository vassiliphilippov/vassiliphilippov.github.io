textuality

noun
quality of being a text
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛkstʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌtɛkstʃuˈælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or characteristics that define something as a text, especially in literature or linguistics

Examples

The textuality of a novel
The textuality of the novel was analyzed in the literary theory class.
Scholars debate the textuality of digital media compared to traditional books.
Her research focuses on the textuality present in ancient inscriptions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: literariness - the quality of being literary or related to literature
  • synonym: text - a written or printed work regarded as a connected piece of writing
