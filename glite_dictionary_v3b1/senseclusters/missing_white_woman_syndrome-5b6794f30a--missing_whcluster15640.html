missing white woman syndrome

noun
media bias in coverage of missing white women
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsɪŋ waɪt ˈwʊmən ˌsɪnˌdroʊm / (AmE), / ˈmɪsɪŋ waɪt ˈwʊmən ˌsɪn.drəʊm / (BrE)
the tendency of news media to give disproportionate attention to cases of missing white women, while neglecting missing persons from other demographic groups
