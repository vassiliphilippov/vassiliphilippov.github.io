oopsie

interjection
playful apology

an exclamation used to acknowledge a small mistake or accident in a playful or apologetic way

Examples

Oopsie after a spill
He dropped his ice cream on the floor and said oopsie with a grin.

The child giggled and exclaimed oopsie after knocking over the blocks.
