Abyssinian banana

noun
tropical African plant related to the banana
9.0
Pronunciation: /æ.bɪ.ˈsɪn.iː.ən bə.ˈnæn.ə/ /; / /æ.bə.ˈsɪn.iː.ən bə.ˈnæn.ə/ /; / /æ.bɪ.ˈsɪn.ɪ.ən bə.ˈnæn.ə/ (AmE), /æ.bɪ.ˈsɪn.ɪ.ən bə.ˈnɑː.nə/ /; / /æ.bə.ˈsɪn.ɪ.ən bə.ˈnɑː.nə/ (BrE)
a large evergreen plant with huge paddle-shaped leaves, related to the banana and bearing inedible banana-like fruit and edible young flower shoots, native to East Africa
