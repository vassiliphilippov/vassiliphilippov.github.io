overturn

verb
to turn upside down or tip over
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈtɜrn / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈtɜːn / (BrE)
to turn something over so that it is upside down or on its side

Examples

To overturn a box
If you don't secure the box, it might overturn during transit.
The boat overturned when the waves became too rough.
The strong wind caused the chair to overturn on the porch.
Be careful not to overturn the glass when you move it.
She accidentally overturned the chair while reaching for her bag.
The boat overturned after hitting a submerged log.

Related Terms

  • antonym: right - to restore to an upright or normal position
  • synonym: tip over - to fall or cause to fall over by turning
