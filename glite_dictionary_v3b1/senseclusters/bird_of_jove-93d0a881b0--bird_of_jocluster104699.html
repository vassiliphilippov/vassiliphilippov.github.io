bird of Jove

noun
poetic/literary term for eagle
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɝd əv dʒoʊv / (AmE), / bɜːd əv dʒəʊv / (BrE)
A poetic or literary name for an eagle, especially referencing its association with the Roman god Jupiter (Jove).

Examples

The bird of Jove symbol
In ancient Roman symbols, the bird of Jove often represented strength and authority.
She watched the bird of Jove as it glided effortlessly across the sky.
The poet described the bird of Jove soaring high above the mountains.
