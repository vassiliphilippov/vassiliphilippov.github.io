flowering onion

noun
European onion with white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊɚɪŋ ˈʌniən / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪŋ ˈʌnɪən / (BrE)
A European species of onion grown for its ornamental white flowers, often used in gardens and landscaping.

Examples

The flowering onion plant
Gardeners often choose the flowering onion for its ornamental value as well as its resilience.
She planted flowering onions along the border for their beautiful white blossoms.
The flowering onion adds a striking spherical bloom to the summer garden.
