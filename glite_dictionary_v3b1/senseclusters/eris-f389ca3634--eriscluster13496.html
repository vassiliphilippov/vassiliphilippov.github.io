Eris

proper noun
Greek goddess of discord
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr.ɪs / (AmE), / ˈɪər.ɪs / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, the goddess of discord and strife, sister of Ares.

Examples

Eris throws an apple
According to the myth, Eris threw the golden apple that led to the Trojan War.
In Homeric poetry, Eris is depicted as a bringer of chaos to the battlefield.
The story of Eris is often cited as an example of how minor events can cause major discord.
