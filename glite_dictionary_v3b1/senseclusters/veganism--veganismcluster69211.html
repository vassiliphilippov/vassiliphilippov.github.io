veganism

noun
avoiding animal products
Pronunciation: / ˈviːɡənɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈviːɡənɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice or philosophy of avoiding all animal products, especially in diet and lifestyle, often for ethical, environmental, or health reasons

Related Terms

  • antonym: carnivorism - the practice of eating only or mostly animal products
  • antonym: omnivorism - the practice of eating both plant and animal products
  • synonym: vegetarianism - the practice of not eating meat but sometimes consuming other animal products
