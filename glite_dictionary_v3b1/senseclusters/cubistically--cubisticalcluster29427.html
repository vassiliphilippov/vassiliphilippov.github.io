cubistically

adverb
in a Cubist style
9.9
Pronunciation: / kjuːˈbɪstɪkli / (AmE), / kjuːˈbɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or resembles the style of Cubism in art, often using geometric shapes and fragmented forms

Examples

Arranged cubistically in art
The artist arranged the objects cubistically to create a sense of abstraction.
Her drawings are composed cubistically, breaking the subject into angular shapes.
The cityscape was depicted cubistically, with overlapping planes and bold lines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geometrically - in a way that uses geometric shapes or forms
