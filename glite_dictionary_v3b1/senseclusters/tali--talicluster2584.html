tali

noun
ankle bones; ancient dice
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlaɪ / (AmE), / ˈtɑːlaɪ / (BrE)
the plural form of talus, referring to ankle bones or small bones used as dice in ancient games

Examples

Ancient tali dice
The museum displayed several ancient tali that were once used as gaming pieces.
Doctors studied the tali to understand more about the structure of the human ankle.
Archaeologists found tali among the artifacts at the Roman excavation site.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dice - small cubes used in games of chance
  • synonym: talus - the ankle bone in humans and some animals
