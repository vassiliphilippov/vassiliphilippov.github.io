qaida

noun
beginner reading textbook
Pronunciation: / ˈkaɪdə / (AmE), / ˈkaɪdə / (BrE)
a basic textbook or set of rules used to teach beginners how to read, especially in Arabic script

Examples

An Arabic qaida book
The teacher handed each student a qaida to help them learn the Arabic alphabet.
Many children start their education by reading from a qaida in the classroom.
Parents often buy a qaida for their children to practice reading at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: primer - an elementary textbook for teaching children to read
