yabber

verb
to chatter rapidly
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjæbər / (AmE), / ˈjæbə / (BrE)
to talk quickly and continuously, especially about trivial or unimportant matters

Examples

To yabber all day
The children were yabbering excitedly as they waited for the bus.
He tends to yabber on the phone for hours about nothing in particular.
We could barely concentrate with everyone yabbering in the background.
The children would yabber excitedly about their day as soon as they got home.
She tends to yabber on the phone for hours with her friends.
During lunch, the coworkers yabbered about their weekend plans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babble - to talk rapidly and continuously in a foolish way
  • antonym: be silent - to not speak or make noise
  • synonym: chatter - to talk quickly about unimportant things
