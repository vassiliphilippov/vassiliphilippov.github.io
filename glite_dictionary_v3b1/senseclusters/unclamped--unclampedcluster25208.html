unclamped

adjective
not fastened by clamp
5.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈklæmpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈklæmpt / (BrE)
not fastened or held in place by a clamp; released from being clamped

Examples

The unclamped device
After the device was unclamped, it could be safely removed from the table.
The technician checked that all parts were unclamped before starting the next step.
Make sure the wires are unclamped before you attempt to move the equipment.
