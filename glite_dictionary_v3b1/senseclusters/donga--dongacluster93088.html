donga

noun
steep-sided gully
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈdɒŋɡə / (BrE)
a steep-sided gully formed by water erosion, especially found in southern Africa

Examples

A deep donga
The cattle grazed near the edge of the donga after the rainy season.
Heavy rains last year caused a new donga to form near the village.
Children were warned not to play close to the deep donga behind the school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gully - a narrow channel formed by the action of water
  • synonym: ravine - a deep, narrow gorge with steep sides
