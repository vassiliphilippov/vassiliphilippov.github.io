buffy

adjective
soft and fluffy
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌfi / (AmE), / ˈbʌfi / (BrE)
having a soft, fluffy, or light texture or appearance, often resembling soft clouds or down

Examples

Buffy clouds
The buffy clouds filled the sky on a summer afternoon.
She wore a buffy sweater that felt soft against her skin.
The puppy's buffy fur made it look even more adorable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: downy - covered with soft, fine feathers or hair
  • synonym: fluffy - soft and light in texture or appearance
  • antonym: rough - having an uneven or coarse surface
