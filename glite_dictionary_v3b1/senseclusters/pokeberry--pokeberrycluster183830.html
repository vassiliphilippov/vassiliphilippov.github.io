pokeberry

noun
poisonous purple berry
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊkˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈpəʊkˌbɛri / (BrE)
a dark purple berry from the pokeweed plant, which is often poisonous and sometimes used to make dye or ink

Examples

A pokeberry bush
The children were told not to touch the pokeberry bushes in the backyard.
Birds sometimes eat pokeberries, but they can be dangerous for humans.
The deep purple stain from a crushed pokeberry is difficult to remove from clothing.
Children once used pokeberry juice to write secret messages on paper.
The artist created a natural paint using pokeberry as the main ingredient.
Be careful, as pokeberry is not safe to eat despite its attractive color.

Related Terms

  • synonym: berry - a small, round fruit
  • synonym: nightshade - a plant with poisonous berries
  • synonym: pokeweed - a plant with dark purple berries, some parts of which are poisonous
