frack

verb
to extract oil or gas
Pronunciation: / fræk / (AmE), / fræk / (BrE)
to extract oil or gas from underground rock by injecting liquid at high pressure to create cracks or fractures

Examples

To frack for gas
The company plans to frack several new wells in the region next year.
Many environmental groups oppose the decision to frack near protected areas.
After the land was fracked, the local water supply was closely monitored for changes.
The company plans to frack several new sites next year.
Many environmental groups oppose the decision to frack in protected areas.
Engineers developed safer methods to frack without contaminating groundwater.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drill - to make a hole in the ground to extract resources
  • synonym: hydraulic fracture - to break rock underground using pressurized fluid
  • synonym: hydraulic fracturing - the process of extracting oil or gas by injecting liquid at high pressure
