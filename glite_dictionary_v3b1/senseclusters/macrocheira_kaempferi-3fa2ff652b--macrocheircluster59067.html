Macrocheira kaempferi

noun
Japanese spider crab species
Pronunciation: / ˌmækroʊˈkaɪrə kaɪmˈfɛri / (AmE), / ˌmakrəʊˈkaɪərə kamˈfɛri / (BrE)
a very large deep-water Japanese crab, also known as the Japanese spider crab, distinguished by its long legs and found in the waters around Japan
