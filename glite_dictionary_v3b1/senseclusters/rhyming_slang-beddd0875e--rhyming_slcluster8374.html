rhyming slang

noun
slang replacing words with rhyming expressions
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪmɪŋ slæŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪmɪŋ slaŋ / (BrE)
a type of slang in which a word is replaced with a phrase or word that rhymes with it, often omitting the actual rhyming word

Examples

Classic rhyming slang
He taught me some classic rhyming slang expressions while we were in the pub.
Many phrases in rhyming slang are used in everyday speech in London.
The origins of rhyming slang date back to the 19th century.
