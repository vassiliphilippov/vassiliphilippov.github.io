belongings

noun
things owned by a person
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɔŋɪŋz / (AmE), / bɪˈlɒŋɪŋz / (BrE)
the things that a person owns, especially those that can be carried or transported

Examples

Personal belongings
He packed all his belongings into a suitcase before moving to a new city.
Please make sure you have collected your belongings before leaving the train.
Thieves broke into the apartment and stole several valuable belongings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: property - things owned by someone, especially land or real estate
