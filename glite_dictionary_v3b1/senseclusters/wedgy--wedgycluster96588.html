wedgy

noun
wedge-soled shoe
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛdʒi / (AmE), / ˈwɛdʒi / (BrE)
an informal term for a shoe with a wedge-shaped sole, especially a women's shoe.

Examples

Wedgy sandals
She bought a pair of wedgy sandals for her summer vacation.
Many people find a wedgy more comfortable than high heels for everyday wear.
The store displayed several colorful wedgy shoes in the front window.
