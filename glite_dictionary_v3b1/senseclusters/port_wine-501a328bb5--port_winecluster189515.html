port wine

noun
sweet, dark-red fortified wine from Portugal
Pronunciation: / pɔrt waɪn / (AmE), / pɔːt waɪn / (BrE)
a sweet, dark-red fortified wine originally made in Portugal, often served as a dessert wine

Examples

A glass of port wine
He enjoyed a glass of port wine with his cheese after dinner.
She bought a bottle of port wine as a gift for her friend.
The vintage port wine was stored carefully in the cellar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sherry - a fortified wine from Spain, usually lighter and drier than port
