mordant

adjective
sarcastically critical or biting
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrdənt / (AmE), / ˈmɔːdənt / (BrE)
expressing harsh criticism, especially in a sharply ironic or biting way

Examples

Mordant humor
His mordant comments often left his colleagues feeling uncomfortable.
She wrote a mordant review of the restaurant, pulling no punches about the service.
The comedian's mordant wit made the audience both laugh and cringe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biting - sharp or cutting in tone or expression
  • synonym: caustic - severely critical; capable of burning or corroding by chemical action (figurative sense)
