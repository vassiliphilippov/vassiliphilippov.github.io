Senecio jacobaea

noun
European plant; toxic weed called ragwort
Pronunciation: / səˈniːʃioʊ ˌdʒækəˈbiːə / (AmE), / sɪˈniːsiəʊ ˌdʒækəˈbiːə / (BrE)
A widespread European plant with yellow daisylike flowers, known as ragwort; sometimes considered an obnoxious weed and toxic to cattle if eaten in large amounts.
