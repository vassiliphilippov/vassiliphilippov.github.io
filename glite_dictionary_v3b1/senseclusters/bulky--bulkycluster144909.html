bulky

adjective
of large size for its weight
3.5 2.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌl.kiː/ /; / /ˈbʌl.kɪ/ (AmE), /ˈbʌl.kiː/ /; / /ˈbʌl.kɪ/ (BrE)
Having a large size or volume relative to its weight; taking up much space.

Examples

A bulky coat
Although the coat was bulky, it weighed almost nothing.
The package looked bulky, but it was surprisingly light to carry.
We had trouble fitting the bulky suitcase into the car's trunk.
