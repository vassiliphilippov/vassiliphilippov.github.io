St. Andrew

proper noun
Christian apostle; patron saint of Scotland
5.1

(In the New Testament) one of the twelve disciples of Jesus, brother of Peter, and the patron saint of Scotland.

Examples

St. Andrew's Day
Every November 30th, Scotland celebrates St. Andrew's Day as a national holiday.
Legend says that the remains of St. Andrew were brought to the town now known as St Andrews, Scotland.
