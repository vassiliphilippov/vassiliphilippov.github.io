sockpuppet

noun
fake online identity
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑkˌpʌpɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɒkˌpʌpɪt / (BrE)
a false online identity created to deceive or manipulate others

Examples

Fake sockpuppet account
The website administrator discovered that several comments were posted by the same sockpuppet.

Many online communities ban users who are caught operating a sockpuppet.
The politician was accused of using a sockpuppet to post supportive comments on social media.
Several sockpuppets were discovered promoting the company's products in online forums.
It is against the website's rules to create a sockpuppet to influence discussions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alias - a false or assumed identity
  • antonym: authentic account - an online identity that is genuine and not controlled by another person
  • synonym: front - a person or organization used to hide someone's real identity or purpose
  • synonym: impostor - a person who pretends to be someone else
  • antonym: real identity - a person's true identity online or offline
  • synonym: shill - a person who pretends to be independent but secretly promotes someone else's interests
