social

noun
an informal gathering or social event
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl / (BrE)
A party or informal gathering where people meet to socialize and enjoy communal activities.

Examples

A social gathering
Attending the social was a great way for newcomers to feel welcome.
She met many of her neighbors at the annual church social.
The community center is hosting a social this Friday evening.
