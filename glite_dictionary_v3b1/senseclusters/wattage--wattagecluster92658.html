wattage

noun
amount of electrical power (in watts)
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːtɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈwɒtɪdʒ / (BrE)
the amount of electrical power expressed in watts, calculated as the product of voltage and current

Examples

Wattage of a lamp
Always check the wattage before plugging in an electrical appliance.
Higher wattage generally means the device will consume more electricity.
The lamp has a wattage of 60 watts, making it suitable for reading.
