dent

noun
small hollow or impression in a surface
4.4
Pronunciation: / dɛnt / (AmE), / dɛnt / (BrE)
a small hollow or depression in a surface, usually caused by a blow or pressure

Examples

A dent in the car
A falling branch left a deep dent in the metal roof.
She was upset to find a dent in her new refrigerator door.
The car had a noticeable dent in the front bumper after the accident.
He accidentally made a dent in the wooden table with his hammer.
The hailstorm left a large dent in the car's hood.
There's a noticeable dent in the metal door after it was struck.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ding - a small dent or indentation, especially in a car or metal object
  • synonym: indentation - a recess or small depression in a surface
