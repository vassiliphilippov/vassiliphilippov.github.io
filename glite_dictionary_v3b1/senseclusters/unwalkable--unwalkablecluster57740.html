unwalkable

adjective
not suitable for walking
6.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɔkəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɔːkəbəl / (BrE)
not suitable or possible to walk in, especially referring to footwear or clothing that makes walking difficult or uncomfortable

Examples

Unwalkable shoes
The designer boots looked amazing but were completely unwalkable for a long day out.
She regretted buying shoes that turned out to be almost unwalkable after just a few steps.
Those high heels are stylish, but they are totally unwalkable on uneven pavement.
