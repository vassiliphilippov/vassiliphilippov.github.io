Père David's deer

noun
large Chinese deer species, now only in captivity
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɛr dəˈvidz dɪr / (AmE), / pɛə dəˈviːdz dɪə / (BrE)
a large species of deer from China, now surviving only in captivity, known for its unique antlers and named after the French missionary and naturalist Armand David
