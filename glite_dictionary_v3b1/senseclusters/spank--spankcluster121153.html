spank

noun
a slap with the flat of the hand
Pronunciation: / spæŋk / (AmE), / spæŋk / (BrE)
a slap given with the flat of the hand, especially as a punishment to a child

Examples

A quick spank
He gave his son a quick spank on the bottom for misbehaving.
Some parents argue that a spank is not an effective way to discipline a child.
The child started crying after receiving a spank.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: hit - to strike (someone or something) with force
  • synonym: slap - a quick blow with the flat of the hand or other flat object
