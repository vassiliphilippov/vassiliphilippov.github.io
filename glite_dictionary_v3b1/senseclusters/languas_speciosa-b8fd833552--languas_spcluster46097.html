Languas speciosa

noun
a tropical plant grown for ornamental leaves and flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈlæŋ.ɡwəs spɛkˈioʊsə / (AmE), / ˈlæŋ.ɡwəs spɛkˈiː.əʊsə / (BrE)
A tropical plant cultivated for its glossy oblong leaves and clusters of white, pink-shaded flowers with crinkled yellow and magenta-striped lips; commonly known as shell ginger.
