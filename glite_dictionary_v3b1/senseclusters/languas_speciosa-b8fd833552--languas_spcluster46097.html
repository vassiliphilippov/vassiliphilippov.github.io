Languas speciosa

noun
a tropical plant grown for ornamental leaves and flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlæŋ.ɡwəs spɛkˈioʊsə / (AmE), / ˈlæŋ.ɡwəs spɛkˈiː.əʊsə / (BrE)
