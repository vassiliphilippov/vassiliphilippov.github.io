basidiosporous

adjective
producing spores by basidia (fungal biology)
9.9
Pronunciation: /bæs.ɪ.diː.ə.ˈspɑːr.əs/ /; / /bæs.ɪ.diː.ə.ˈspɔːr.əs/ (AmE), /bæs.ɪ.diː.ə.ˈspɔː.rəs/ (BrE)
relating to or characterized by spores produced by basidia, as in certain fungi

Examples

Basidiosporous fungi
Many mushrooms are classified as basidiosporous fungi due to the way they produce their spores.
Some plant diseases are caused by basidiosporous fungi that infect crops.
The basidiosporous nature of certain molds makes them important for soil ecology.
