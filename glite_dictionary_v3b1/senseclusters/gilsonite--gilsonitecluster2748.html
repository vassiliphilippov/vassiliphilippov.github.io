gilsonite

noun
solid natural bitumen
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪlsəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɪlsəˌnaɪt / (BrE)
a naturally occurring, solid form of bitumen used in industrial applications such as road paving and inks

Large deposits of gilsonite can be found in certain regions of the western United States.
