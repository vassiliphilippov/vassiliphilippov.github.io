unslakable

adjective
impossible to extinguish
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsleɪkəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈsleɪkəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to satisfy, quench, or extinguish (such as a fire, thirst, or strong desire)

Examples

Unsakable thirst
The villagers watched in fear as the unslakable flames spread across the dry fields.
He had an unslakable thirst for knowledge that drove him to read every night.
Despite their efforts, the firefighters could not control the unslakable blaze.
She felt an unslakable curiosity about the mysteries of the universe.
His unslakable thirst for knowledge drove him to read every book he could find.
The team's unslakable desire to win pushed them to train harder than ever.
