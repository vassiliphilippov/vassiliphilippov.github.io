Pindaric

adjective
of Pindar's style
Pronunciation: / pɪnˈdærɪk / (AmE), / pɪnˈdarɪk / (BrE)
relating to the style, form, or works of the ancient Greek poet Pindar, especially his odes

Examples

Pindaric style poetry
The poet composed a Pindaric ode in honor of the champion.
Her writing style is highly Pindaric, filled with complex structures and vivid imagery.
The ceremony featured a Pindaric tribute celebrating the athletes' achievements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lyric - relating to poetry expressing personal emotions
  • synonym: ode - a type of lyrical stanza, often praising something
