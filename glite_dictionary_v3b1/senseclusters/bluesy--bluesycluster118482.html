bluesy

adjective
like blues music
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːzi / (AmE), / ˈbluːzi / (BrE)
having a style, sound, or feeling similar to blues music, often soulful or expressing sadness

Examples

Bluesy guitar solo
The guitarist played a bluesy solo that moved the entire audience.
Her bluesy singing style made every song sound heartfelt and emotional.
They added a bluesy touch to the classic rock song, giving it a new feel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - showing happiness or optimism
  • synonym: melancholic - having a feeling of sadness or sorrow
  • synonym: soulful - full of deep feeling or emotion
