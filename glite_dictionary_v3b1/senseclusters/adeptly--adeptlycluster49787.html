adeptly

adverb
skillfully, effectively
Pronunciation: / əˈdɛptli / (AmE), / əˈdɛptli / (BrE)
in a skillful and effective way

Examples

To adeptly manage
She adeptly managed the team through a difficult project.
The chef adeptly prepared the meal, impressing all the guests.
He adeptly handled the unexpected questions during the interview.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsily - in a way that lacks skill or grace
  • synonym: skillfully - in a way that shows skill
