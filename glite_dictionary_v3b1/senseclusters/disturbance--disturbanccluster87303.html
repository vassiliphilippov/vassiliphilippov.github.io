Synonyms

  • disruption
    4.1 11/m
    disruption is a sudden or major break in normal flow, while disturbance is a broader interruption of normal state or process.
  • interference
    4.1 11/m
    interference is active involvement that hinders a process, while disturbance names any interruption of normal state or function.
  • perturbation
    8.8 1/m
    perturbation is a small technical change in a system, while disturbance covers general interruptions or interferences in many contexts.
