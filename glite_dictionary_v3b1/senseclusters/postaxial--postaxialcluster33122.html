postaxial

adjective
away from central axis
9.9
Pronunciation: /poʊ.ˈstæk.sɪ.əl/ (AmE), /pəʊ.ˈstæk.sɪ.əl/ (BrE)
located behind or on the side away from the central axis of a limb or structure

Examples

The postaxial border
The postaxial border of the hand is the side with the little finger.
A postaxial polydactyly refers to an extra finger on the outer side of the hand.
Doctors examined the postaxial region of the limb for any developmental abnormalities.
