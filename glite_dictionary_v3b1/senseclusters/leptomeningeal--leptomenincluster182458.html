leptomeningeal

adjective
of brain membranes
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛptəˌmɛnɪnˈdʒiəl / (AmE), / ˌlɛptəˌmɛnɪnˈdʒiːəl / (BrE)
relating to the thin membranes (pia mater and arachnoid mater) covering the brain and spinal cord

Examples

Leptomeningeal disease
The patient was diagnosed with leptomeningeal disease after cancer cells spread to the brain membranes.
Doctors observed leptomeningeal enhancement on the MRI scan, indicating possible infection.
Treatment for leptomeningeal metastases often involves chemotherapy delivered directly into the spinal fluid.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meningeal - relating to the membranes covering the brain and spinal cord
