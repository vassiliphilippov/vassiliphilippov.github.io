grateful

adjective
feeling or showing thanks
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪtfəl / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtfəl / (BrE)

Examples

Grateful for help

I am extremely grateful to everyone who supported me through difficult times.
She was grateful for the help her neighbors offered during the storm.
