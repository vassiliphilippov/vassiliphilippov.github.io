bar girl

noun
woman paid to socialize with bar patrons
1.7
Pronunciation: / bɑr ɡɜrl / (AmE), / bɑː ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a woman employed by a bar to socialize with and act as a companion to male customers, often in exchange for drinks or tips

Examples

Bar girl at work
He mistakenly assumed the bar girl was interested in him, not realizing it was just her job.
Many bar girls rely on tips from customers for most of their income.
The bar girl smiled as she chatted with the tourists at the club.
