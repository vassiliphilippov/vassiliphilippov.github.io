snapshooter

noun
casual photographer
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæpˌʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈsnæpˌʃuːtə / (BrE)
a person who takes casual photographs, especially as a hobby and not professionally

Examples

Amateur snapshooter
As a snapshooter, she enjoyed capturing everyday moments with her phone.
The snapshooter wandered through the park, looking for interesting scenes to photograph.
Unlike a professional, a snapshooter often values spontaneity over technical perfection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amateur - a person who engages in a pursuit as a pastime rather than as a profession
  • synonym: photographer - a person who takes photographs, especially as a profession
  • antonym: professional - a person who does something as a paid occupation rather than as a hobby
