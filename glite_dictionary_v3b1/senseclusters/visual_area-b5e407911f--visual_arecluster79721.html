visual area

noun
cortical region processing visual information
3.8 6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒuəl ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒu.əl ˈeə.ri.ə / (BrE)
a region of the cerebral cortex that receives and processes visual information from the eyes, particularly from the lateral geniculate body of the thalamus

Examples
