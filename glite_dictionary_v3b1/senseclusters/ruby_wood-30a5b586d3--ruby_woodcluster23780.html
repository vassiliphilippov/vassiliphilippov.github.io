ruby wood

noun
hard red wood from red sandalwood tree
9.4 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàruÀêbi w äd / (AmE), / ÀàruÀêbi w äd / (BrE)
a hard, durable red wood obtained from the red sandalwood tree (Pterocarpus santalinus), valued for its use in cabinetwork and fine woodworking

Examples

A ruby wood piece
Due to overharvesting, ruby wood has become increasingly scarce in recent years.
Fine furniture makers often use ruby wood for its rich color and durability.
The intricate jewelry box was crafted from ruby wood and decorated with brass inlays.
