flagroot

noun
perennial marsh plant with aromatic roots
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈflæɡˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈflæɡˌruːt / (BrE)
A perennial marsh plant with swordlike leaves and aromatic roots, known for growing in wet habitats.

Examples

Aromatic flagroot plant
Herbalists often harvest flagroot for its aromatic and medicinal properties.
The swordlike leaves of the flagroot made it easy to identify among other marsh plants.
The wetland was filled with clusters of flagroot along the banks of the slow-moving river.
