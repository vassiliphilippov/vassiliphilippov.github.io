exportable

adjective
suitable for export
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɔrtəbəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɔːtəbəl / (BrE)
suitable for being exported to another country, especially in terms of goods, services, or achievements.

Examples

Exportable goods
Many agricultural products are considered highly exportable due to their global demand.
The country’s exportable resources include oil, coffee, and minerals.
The government is working to develop more exportable cultural achievements to share with the world.
