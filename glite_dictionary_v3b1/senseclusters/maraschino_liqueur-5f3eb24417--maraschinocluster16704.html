maraschino liqueur

noun
sweet cherry-based liqueur
Pronunciation: / ˌmærəˈskiːnoʊ lɪˈkɜːr / (AmE), / ˌmærəˈskiːnəʊ lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
A clear, sweet liqueur distilled from fermented juice of bitter wild marasca cherries, often used in cocktails and desserts.

Examples

A splash of maraschino liqueur
A splash of maraschino liqueur can add depth to many dessert sauces.
Classic recipes for the Aviation cocktail often call for maraschino liqueur.
The bartender poured a dash of maraschino liqueur into the cocktail for a unique flavor.
