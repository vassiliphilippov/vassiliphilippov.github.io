Anchusa

noun
a genus of rough Old World herbs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ænˈkjuːsə / (AmE), / anˈkjuːsə / (BrE)
a genus of rough and hairy Old World herbs in the borage family, with bright blue or violet flowers.

Examples

The vivid blue flowers of Anchusa are popular in cottage gardens.
