crumbling

noun
breaking apart process
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌmblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrʌmblɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of breaking into small pieces or gradually falling apart

Examples

The crumbling of buildings

Years of neglect led to the slow crumbling of the once-beautiful theater.
After the earthquake, the crumbling of several buildings made the area unsafe.
