stake

verb
tie or fasten to a stake
9.9
Pronunciation: / steɪk / (AmE), / steɪk / (BrE)
to tie or fasten (an animal or object) to a stake, typically in the ground, to restrain or support it

Examples

To stake a goat
She watched as he staked the restless goat to a post near the shed.
The farmer decided to stake his horse outside the barn so it could graze.
To protect the young tree, they staked it firmly into the ground.
