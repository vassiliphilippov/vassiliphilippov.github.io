low-pressure situation

noun
a relaxed, low-stress environment
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌloʊˈprɛʃər sɪˈtʃuːˌeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌləʊˈprɛʃə sɪˈtjuːˌeɪʃən / (BrE)
a circumstance or environment characterized by little stress, urgency, or demand

Examples

A low-pressure situation
He prefers to work in a low-pressure situation where mistakes are seen as learning opportunities.
Presenting in a low-pressure situation helped her build confidence for future challenges.
The retreat offered a low-pressure situation for new employees to get to know each other.
