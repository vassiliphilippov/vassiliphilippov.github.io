trishaw

noun
three-wheeled vehicle
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪˌʃɔ / (AmE), / ˈtraɪʃɔː / (BrE)
a three-wheeled, usually pedal-powered vehicle used for carrying passengers (and sometimes goods), especially in some Asian countries

Examples

The trishaw driver waited patiently while his passengers took photos.
In some Asian cities, a trishaw is a popular way for tourists to get around.
