pepperwood

noun
Pacific coast aromatic tree with hard wood
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpərˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈpɛpəˌwʊd / (BrE)
A tree native to the Pacific coast of North America, noted for its aromatic leaves, small cluster flowers, olivelike fruit, and hard, tough wood.

Examples

Pepperwood tree wood
Carpenters prized pepperwood for its durability and fine grain, often using it in furniture making.
During our hike, we came across a pepperwood with small, olive-like fruits hanging from its branches.
The forest was home to several mature pepperwood trees, their aromatic leaves scenting the air.
