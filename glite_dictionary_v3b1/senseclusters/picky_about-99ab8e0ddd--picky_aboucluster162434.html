picky about

adjective
very selective or particular about something
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪki əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈpɪki əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
colloquial phrase used to describe someone who is very selective or particular regarding a specific thing.

Examples

Be picky about clothes
He's quite picky about the clothes he wears and won't try anything new.
She became less picky about food after traveling abroad.
Some customers are extremely picky about the quality of the service they receive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fussy about - very concerned or particular about details
  • antonym: indifferent to - lacking concern or interest about something
