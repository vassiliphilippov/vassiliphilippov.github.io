araucaria

noun
tall coniferous tree with edible seeds
Pronunciation: / ˌɛrəˈkɛriə / (AmE), / ˌærɔːˈkɑːriə / (BrE)
a tall coniferous tree native to South America and Australia, with large cones and edible seeds, belonging to the genus Araucaria

Examples

A tall araucaria tree
Some species of araucaria produce seeds that are roasted and eaten as a snack.
The araucaria is a striking tree commonly found in southern Chile.
The tall araucaria trees lined the avenue, giving the area a distinctive appearance.
