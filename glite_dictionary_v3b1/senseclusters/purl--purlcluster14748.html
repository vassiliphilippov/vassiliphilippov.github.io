purl

verb
flow with a swirling motion (liquids)
7.8 7.4
Pronunciation: /pɜːrl/ (AmE), /pɜːl/ (BrE)
(of water) to flow with a soft, murmuring sound or swirling motion

Examples

A brook purls
As the brook purled through the meadow, birds sang nearby.
The stream began to purl gently around the mossy stones.

A little brook would purl along the edge of the garden, adding to the tranquil atmosphere.
As the boat drifted downstream, the water continued to purl over the smooth stones.
The mountain stream began to purl softly in the early morning light.
