soap opera

noun
serialized melodramatic drama series
5.4 2.0
Pronunciation: /soʊp ˈɑː.pə.rə/ /; / /soʊp ˈɑː.prə/ (AmE), /səʊp ˈɒp.ə.rə/ /; / /səʊp ˈɒp.rə/ (BrE)
