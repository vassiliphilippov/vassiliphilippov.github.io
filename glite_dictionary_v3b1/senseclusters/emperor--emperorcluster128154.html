emperor

noun
large Eurasian moth with transparent wings
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmpəɹɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛmpərə / (BrE)
a large moth of temperate forests of Eurasia, known for its heavily scaled transparent wings

Examples

Emperor moth
She spotted a rare emperor resting on a tree trunk during her hike.
The emperor moth is often seen in Eurasian woodlands during the early spring.
The wings of the emperor are covered with striking, heavy scales.
