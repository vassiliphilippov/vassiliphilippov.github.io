overclothe

verb
dress in too many warm clothes
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈkloʊð / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkləʊð / (BrE)
to dress someone or oneself in too many or too warm clothes

Examples

To overclothe babies
If you overclothe babies, they might become overheated and uncomfortable.
It's common for anxious parents to overclothe their children during winter.
Try not to overclothe yourself before going for a walk in the spring sunshine.
