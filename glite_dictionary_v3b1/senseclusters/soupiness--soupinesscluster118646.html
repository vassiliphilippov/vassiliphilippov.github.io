soupiness

noun
thickness like heavy cream
Pronunciation: / ˈsuː.pinəs / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pinɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being thick or having the texture of heavy cream, like soup

Examples

Creamy soupiness
Her recipe achieved just the right soupiness without becoming too thick or gloopy.
If the sauce lacks soupiness, try simmering it longer to improve the texture.
The soupiness of the chowder made it incredibly satisfying on a cold night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soupy - having a consistency like soup; thick or creamy
