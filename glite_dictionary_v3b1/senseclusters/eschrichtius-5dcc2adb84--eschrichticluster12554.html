Eschrichtius

noun
genus of baleen whales (gray whale)
Pronunciation: / ɛsˈkrɪktiəs / (AmE), / ɛsˈkrɪktɪəs / (BrE)
a genus of baleen whales, constituting the sole genus of the family Eschrichtiidae, including the gray whale

Examples

Eschrichtius gray whale
Eschrichtius is unique among whales for its benthic feeding habits.
Marine biologists studied the migration patterns of Eschrichtius last winter.
The gray whale, classified as Eschrichtius robustus, is found in the North Pacific Ocean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gray whale - a large baleen whale, the only living species in the genus Eschrichtius
