ACC

noun
adaptive cruise control (car safety system)
5.3
Pronunciation: / cce6accsid0c8sid0 / (AmE), / cce6accsid0c8sid0 / (BrE)
Abbreviation of adaptive cruise control: a vehicle system that automatically maintains a set speed and a safe following distance by adjusting the car's speed in response to other traffic.

Examples

Car ACC system
The new model's ACC automatically maintains a safe distance from cars ahead.
I disabled the ACC when traffic became too stop-and-go on the highway.
Drivers should still monitor the road even with ACC engaged, especially in heavy rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adaptive cruise control - a vehicle system that automatically adjusts speed to keep a safe distance from the vehicle ahead
