agonizingly

adverb
in a very painful or distressing way
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈæɡəˌnaɪzɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈæɡənʌɪzɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that causes great physical or mental pain or distress.

Examples

Moves agonizingly slowly
She moved agonizingly slowly after her injury, struggling with each step.
The negotiations dragged on agonizingly for hours before a decision was reached.
The wait for test results was agonizingly long and filled with uncertainty.
