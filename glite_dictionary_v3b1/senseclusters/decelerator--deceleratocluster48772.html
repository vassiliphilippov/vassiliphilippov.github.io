decelerator

noun
device that slows movement
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɛləˌreɪtər / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛləˌreɪtə / (BrE)
a device or mechanism that slows down movement or reduces speed

Examples

Car decelerator
The decelerator is an essential safety feature in modern vehicles.
Engineers tested the new decelerator to ensure it could handle emergency stops.
When the spacecraft re-entered the atmosphere, its decelerator deployed to slow it down.
The engineers installed a new decelerator to help the train stop more safely.
During reentry, the spacecraft deployed its decelerator to slow down before landing.
A parachute acts as a decelerator for skydivers, allowing them to land safely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accelerator - a device that increases speed or movement
  • synonym: brake - a device for slowing or stopping a moving vehicle
