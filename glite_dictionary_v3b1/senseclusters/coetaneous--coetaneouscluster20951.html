coetaneous

adjective
of the same period or age
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊ.ɪˈteɪ.ni.əs / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.ɪˈteɪ.ni.əs / (BrE)
belonging to or originating in the same period; contemporaneous

Examples

Several coetaneous manuscripts
Several coetaneous manuscripts provide insight into the cultural context of that period.
The artifacts were determined to be coetaneous with those found in the neighboring region.
Their findings suggest that both civilizations were coetaneous rather than successive.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anachronistic - belonging to a period other than that being considered
  • synonym: contemporaneous - existing or occurring in the same period of time
  • synonym: synchronous - occurring at the same time
