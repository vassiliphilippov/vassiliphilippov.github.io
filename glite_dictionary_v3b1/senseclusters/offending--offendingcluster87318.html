offending

adjective
violating a law or rule
Pronunciation: / əˈfɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈfɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
violating or breaking a law, rule, or accepted standard

Examples

The offending passages
Only the offending passages will be removed from the publication.
The company agreed to withdraw the offending advertisement after receiving numerous complaints.
The judge ordered all offending vehicles to be taken off the road.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illegal - contrary to law; not allowed by law
  • antonym: law-abiding - not breaking the law or rules
