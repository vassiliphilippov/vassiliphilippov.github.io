pineapple family

noun
plant family including the pineapple
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪˌnæpəl ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈpaɪˌnæpəl ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
A family of tropical American plants (Bromeliaceae) that includes the pineapple and related species, many of economic importance.

Examples

Many houseplants, such as bromeliads, belong to the pineapple family.
