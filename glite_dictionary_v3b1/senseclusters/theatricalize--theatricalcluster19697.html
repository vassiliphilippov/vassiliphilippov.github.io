theatricalize

verb
to act dramatically
Pronunciation: / θiˈætrɪkəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / θɪˈætrɪkəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something more dramatic or suitable for performance, often by exaggerating or adapting it for the theater
