storeship

noun
supply vessel
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔrˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈstɔːˌʃɪp / (BrE)
a ship used to store and supply provisions or equipment to other ships at sea

Examples

A storeship at sea
The navy sent a storeship to provide food and fuel to the fleet.
After weeks at sea, the crew welcomed the arrival of the storeship.
A storeship anchored in the harbor to resupply the smaller vessels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: supply ship - a ship that delivers supplies to other ships or locations
  • antonym: warship - a ship designed for combat
