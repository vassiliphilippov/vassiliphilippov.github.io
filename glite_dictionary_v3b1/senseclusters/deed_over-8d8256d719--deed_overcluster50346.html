deed over

verb
transfer property by legal deed
9.9
Pronunciation: / did ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / diːd ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to transfer ownership of property to another person or entity by means of a legal deed

Examples

To deed over property
After much discussion, they deeded over their shares to the new owner.
He decided to deed over the property to his daughter before retiring.
The company chose to deed over the land to a local charity.
