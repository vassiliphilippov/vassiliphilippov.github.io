thermostable

adjective
resistant to heat
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜrmoʊˈsteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊˈsteɪbəl / (BrE)
not easily changed, damaged, or destroyed by heat

Examples

Thermostable protein
This thermostable protein can function even at temperatures above 80 degrees Celsius.
Scientists prefer thermostable enzymes for industrial processes that require heat.
The vaccine remains effective because it is thermostable and does not degrade easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heat-resistant - able to withstand high temperatures
  • antonym: thermolabile - easily changed or destroyed by heat
