lyceum

noun
secondary school or cultural institution
8.8 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / la…™ÀàsiÀê…ôm / (AmE), / la…™ÀàsiÀê…ôm / (BrE)
A school, often secondary, for students between elementary school and college, typically covering grades 9 to 12; in some countries, an institution of higher education or a place of public lectures or cultural events.

Examples

A lyceum school
After finishing elementary school, she enrolled at the local lyceum to pursue her secondary education.
Many famous scientists and writers have given talks at the city's lyceum.
The town's old lyceum is now used as a community center for lectures and concerts.
