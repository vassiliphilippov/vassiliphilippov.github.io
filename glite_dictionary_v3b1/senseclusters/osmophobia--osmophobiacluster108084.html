osmophobia

noun
fear of smells
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːzmoʊˈfoʊbiə /; / ˌɑːzməˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌɒzməˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
an abnormal or strong negative reaction to certain smells, such as fear, aversion, or physical discomfort

Examples

Osmophobia to smells
People with osmophobia may avoid places with strong scents like perfume shops.
Her osmophobia made it difficult to attend social events where food was served.
Doctors sometimes see osmophobia in patients who suffer from migraines.
People with osmophobia may feel sick when exposed to strong perfumes or cooking smells.
Her osmophobia became worse during pregnancy, making it hard to be around certain foods.
Migraine sufferers often report osmophobia as a warning sign before their headaches begin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anosmia - the loss or absence of the sense of smell
  • synonym: hyperosmia - an increased sensitivity to smells
  • synonym: phobia - an extreme or irrational fear of something
