osmophobia

noun
fear of smells
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɑːz.moʊ.ˈfoʊ.bi.ə/ /; / /ɑːz.mə.ˈfoʊ.bi.ə/ (AmE), /ɒz.məʊ.ˈfəʊ.bi.ə/ /; / /ɒz.mə.ˈfəʊ.bi.ə/ (BrE)
an abnormal or strong negative reaction to certain smells, such as fear, aversion, or physical discomfort
