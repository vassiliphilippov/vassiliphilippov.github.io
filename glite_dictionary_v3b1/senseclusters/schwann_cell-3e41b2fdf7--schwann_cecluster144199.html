Schwann cell

noun
cell forming myelin in peripheral nerves
5.2
Pronunciation: /ʃwɑːn sel/ /; / /ʃwɔːn sel/ (AmE), /ʃwɒn sel/ /; / /ʃwɑːn sel/ (BrE)

Examples
