Synonyms

  • register
    2.8 32/m
    register records your details officially with an authority, while sign up is a more general everyday way to join activities.
  • enroll
    2.7 29/m
    enroll means officially join a course or program and sounds formal, while sign up is more informal and wider in use.
  • sign on
    3.4 4.44/m
    sign on agrees to work or join by contract, while sign up usually means simply registering to take part.
  • enter
    1.1 41/m
    enter often means take part in an event or competition, while sign up stresses registering to join or take part.
  • join
    1.0 180/m
    join means become a member or take part with others, while sign up emphasizes the act of registering first.
