filial love

noun
a child's love for their parent
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪliəl lʌv / (AmE), / ˈfɪlɪəl lʌv / (BrE)
the affection or deep love that a child feels toward their parent(s)

Examples

Filial love for parents
He expressed his filial love by visiting his mother every weekend.
Her filial love was evident in the way she cared for her aging parents.
Throughout history, stories often depict filial love as a virtue in many cultures.
