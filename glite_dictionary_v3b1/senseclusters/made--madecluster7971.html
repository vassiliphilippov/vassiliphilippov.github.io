made

adjective
describing a bed with covers arranged
2.0
Pronunciation: / meɪd / (AmE), / meɪd / (BrE)
(of a bed) having the sheets, blankets, and covers arranged neatly in place.

Examples

A made bed
A made bed instantly makes the whole room look tidier.
He always leaves his bed perfectly made before going to work.
She was pleased to find a made bed waiting for her at the hotel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unmade - of a bed, not having its covers arranged
