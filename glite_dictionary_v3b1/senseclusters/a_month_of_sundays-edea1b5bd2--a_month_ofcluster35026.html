a month of Sundays

noun
a very long time
Pronunciation: / ə mʌnθ əv ˈsʌndeɪz / (AmE), / ə mʌnθ əv ˈsʌndeɪz / (BrE)
An extremely long period of time; a duration so long that it seems unlikely ever to end.

Examples

A month of Sundays wait
He hasn't called in a month of Sundays, and I doubt he ever will.
It would take me a month of Sundays to clean out that old attic.
You won't finish this puzzle in a month of Sundays without some help.
