nonvisible

adjective
not visible
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈvɪzəbəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈvɪzəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be seen; not visible to the eye

Examples

Nonvisible radiation
Some types of radiation are nonvisible and require special instruments to detect.
The security system uses nonvisible lasers to monitor the area without being noticed.
Many animals can sense nonvisible light that humans cannot see.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invisible - impossible to see; not visible
  • antonym: visible - able to be seen
