dickcissel

noun
North American songbird
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪkˌsɪsəl / (AmE), / ˈdɪkˌsɪsəl / (BrE)
a small North American songbird with a yellow breast and a black throat patch, found in grasslands and fields

Examples

A dickcissel bird
We spotted a dickcissel singing in the tall grass during our morning walk.
The bright yellow breast of the dickcissel makes it easy to identify among other birds.
Farmers often hear the cheerful song of the dickcissel in their fields each summer.
