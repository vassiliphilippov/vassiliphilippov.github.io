shrimp butter

noun
butter blended or flavored with shrimp
Pronunciation: / ʃrɪmp ˈbʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ʃrɪmp ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
Butter blended with chopped shrimp or flavored with an extract made from shrimp shells, used as a spread or in cooking.

Examples
