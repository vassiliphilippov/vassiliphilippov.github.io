tertiary

adjective
third in order or rank
6.4 1.44/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…úr Éieri / (AmE), / Ààt…úÀê É…ôri / (BrE)
Third in order, rank, or level; coming next after the second and just before the fourth.

Examples

Tertiary care center
Each patient was referred to a tertiary care center for specialized treatment.
The museum contains material from the tertiary period in geological history.
This is her tertiary attempt at the exam, after failing twice.

Synonyms

  • tertial

  • 3rd
