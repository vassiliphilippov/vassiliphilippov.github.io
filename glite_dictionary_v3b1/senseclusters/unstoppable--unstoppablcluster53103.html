unstoppable

adjective
impossible to stop or prevent
4.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɑːpəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɒpəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to stop or prevent from continuing or achieving its aims

Examples

An unstoppable force
Her determination was unstoppable, and she achieved everything she set her mind to.
Technology sometimes feels like an unstoppable force shaping our future.
The team's offense proved unstoppable during the championship game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invincible - too powerful to be defeated or overcome
  • synonym: irresistible - too powerful or convincing to be resisted
  • antonym: stoppable - able to be stopped
