tetryl

noun
yellow explosive compound used in detonators
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛtrɪl / (AmE), / ˈtɛtrɪl / (BrE)
a yellow crystalline explosive compound used in detonators and as a booster charge in explosives

Examples

Yellow tetryl explosive
A small amount of tetryl was used to initiate the main charge in the device.
Due to its sensitivity, tetryl was replaced by safer compounds in many modern explosives.
The laboratory stores all tetryl in a secure, temperature-controlled facility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trinitrotoluene - another common explosive compound, also known as TNT
