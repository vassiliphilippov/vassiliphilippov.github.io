sand grouse

noun
pigeon-like bird of arid, Old World regions
4.1 7.5
Pronunciation: /sænd graʊs/ (AmE), /sænd graʊs/ (BrE)
a pigeon-like bird found in arid regions of the Old World, with long pointed wings and tail and young that are able to move soon after hatching
