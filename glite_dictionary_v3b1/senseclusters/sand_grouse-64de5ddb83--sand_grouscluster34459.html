sand grouse

noun
pigeon-like bird of arid, Old World regions
Pronunciation: / sænd ɡraʊs / (AmE), / sand ɡraʊs / (BrE)
a pigeon-like bird found in arid regions of the Old World, with long pointed wings and tail and young that are able to move soon after hatching

Examples

A sand grouse flock
A flock of sand grouse flew low over the dunes at dawn.
Researchers studied the nesting habits of the sand grouse in the Sahara Desert.
The sand grouse are well adapted to dry, desert environments.
