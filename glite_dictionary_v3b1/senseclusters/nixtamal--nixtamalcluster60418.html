nixtamal

noun
alkaline-treated corn
9.5
Pronunciation: /nɪk.ˈstɑː.məl/ /; / /nɪks.ˈtɑː.məl/ (AmE), /nɪk.ˈstɑː.məl/ /; / /nɪks.ˈtɑː.məl/ (BrE)
corn that has been soaked and cooked in an alkaline solution, typically used to make tortillas and other Latin American foods

Examples

Fresh nixtamal corn
Traditional tortillas are made from freshly prepared nixtamal rather than store-bought corn flour.
The process of making nixtamal gives the corn a unique flavor and texture.
She learned how to prepare nixtamal from her grandmother's recipe.
