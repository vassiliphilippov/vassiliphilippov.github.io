shitpost

verb
post provocative or low-quality content online
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈʃɪt.poʊst/ (AmE), /ˈʃɪt.pəʊst/ (BrE)
To post deliberately low-quality, provocative, or off-topic content online, typically to get a reaction, derail discussion, or amuse oneself.

Examples

To shitpost online
Some users shitpost in comment sections to derail debates and provoke heated reactions online.
During the livestream, several viewers shitposted memes and nonsense until the chat became unreadable.
Journalists warned that political accounts might shitpost false claims to sway public opinion before the vote.
