negrito

noun
indigenous Southeast Asian person
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɛˈɡriːtoʊ / (AmE), / nɛˈɡriːtəʊ / (BrE)
a member of various small-statured indigenous peoples of Southeast Asia and nearby islands, such as the Philippines and Andaman Islands

Examples

Negrito people in Asia
Anthropologists have studied the unique traditions of the negrito communities in Southeast Asia.
The negrito peoples are known for their deep knowledge of forest environments.
Some negrito groups maintain a semi-nomadic lifestyle in the Philippines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aeta - an indigenous Negrito people of the Philippines
  • synonym: andamanese - an indigenous Negrito people of the Andaman Islands
