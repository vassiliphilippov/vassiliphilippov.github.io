prickle

verb
cause a stinging or tingling sensation
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈprɪkəl / (BrE)
to cause a stinging or tingling feeling, as if touched by something sharp

Examples

Skin prickles with cold
A cold breeze made her skin prickle with goosebumps.
His words prickled her with embarrassment, and she blushed deeply.
The rough fabric prickled at my neck all day.
A sense of fear prickled him as he entered the dark forest.
Excitement began to prickle down her spine as the curtain rose.
The cold air made my skin prickle with goosebumps.
