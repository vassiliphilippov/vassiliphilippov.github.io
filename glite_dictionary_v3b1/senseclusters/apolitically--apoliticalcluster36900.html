apolitically

adverb
without political influence
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪpəˈlɪtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌeɪpəˈlɪtɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that is not influenced by or involved in politics

Examples

To act apolitically
The judge made her decision apolitically, focusing only on the facts of the case.
He prefers to remain apolitically involved in community projects, avoiding any party affiliations.
The report was written apolitically to ensure it would be accepted by all sides.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neutrally - in a way that does not support any side
  • antonym: politically - in a way that relates to politics
