vernier scale

noun
precision sliding measurement scale
5.8 6.3
Pronunciation: /və.ˈnɪr skeɪl/ /; / /və.ˈniːr skeɪl/ (AmE), /və.ˈnɪə skeɪl/ /; / /və.ˈniː.ə skeɪl/ (BrE)
A small movable scale that slides along a main scale, allowing for precise measurement of fractional divisions.

Examples
