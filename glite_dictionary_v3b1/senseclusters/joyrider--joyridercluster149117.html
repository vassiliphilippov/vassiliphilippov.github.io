joyrider

noun
car thief for fun
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪˌraɪdər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪˌraɪdə / (BrE)
a person who takes and drives a vehicle, usually without permission, for fun, often driving dangerously or recklessly
