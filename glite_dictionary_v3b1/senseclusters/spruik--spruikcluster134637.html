spruik

noun
public promoter
9.9
Pronunciation: /spruːk/ (AmE), /spruːk/ (BrE)
a person who publicly promotes or advertises goods, events, or ideas, often in a loud or enthusiastic manner

Examples

A market spruik
The market hired a spruik to attract more customers to the food stalls.
A lively spruik stood at the entrance, calling out special offers to passersby.
Every festival needs a good spruik to create excitement and draw a crowd.

  • synonym: barker - a person who stands outside and calls out to attract customers
