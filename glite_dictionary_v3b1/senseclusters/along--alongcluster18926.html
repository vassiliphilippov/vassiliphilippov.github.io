along

adverb
in line with a length or direction
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / əˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
in a line with or following the length or direction of something, especially beside it

Examples

Shops along the street
A row of shops stretches along the main street.
Children were playing along the sidewalk all afternoon.
We took a walk along the beach at sunset.

Related Terms

  • antonym: across - from one side to the other side of something
  • synonym: beside - at the side of; next to
