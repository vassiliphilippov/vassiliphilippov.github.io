nonplanar

adjective
not flatly drawable
8.0
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈpleɪ.nər/ /; / /nɑːn.ˈplæn.ər/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈpleɪ.nə/ /; / /nɒn.ˈplæn.ə/ (BrE)
not able to be drawn on a flat surface without edges or lines crossing, especially in mathematics or diagrams

Examples

Nonplanar graph drawing
The mathematician proved that the graph was nonplanar because its edges could not be arranged without crossing.
Some complex networks are inherently nonplanar and require three-dimensional representations.
In topology, a nonplanar diagram cannot be drawn on a flat sheet without overlapping lines.

Related Terms

  • antonym: planar - able to be drawn on a flat surface without any edges crossing
