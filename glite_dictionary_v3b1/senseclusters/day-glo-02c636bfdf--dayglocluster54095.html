Day-Glo

noun
bright fluorescent color or paint
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪ ɡloʊ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ ɡləʊ / (BrE)
a bright fluorescent color or paint that is highly visible, often used for safety or decoration
