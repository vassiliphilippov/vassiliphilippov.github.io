Examples

Synonyms

  • movable
    2.5 1.80/m
    movable describes things that can be shifted from place to place, while transportable implies design for carrying or easy transport.
  • portable
    2.9 13/m
    portable means light and small for easy carrying, while transportable refers broadly to something that can be moved between places.
  • carryable
    3.1 0.01/m
    carryable means able to be lifted and carried by a person, while transportable covers moving by any method or device.
  • relocatable
    4.2 0.01/m
    relocatable describes moving something to a new permanent place, while transportable simply means it can be moved or carried.
