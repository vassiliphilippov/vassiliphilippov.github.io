defense

noun
the part of a team that prevents scoring
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɛns / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns / (BrE)
(in sports) the part of a team or the players whose role is to prevent the opposing team from scoring points or goals.

Examples

Strong defense wins
If we want to win, our defense needs to be as strong as our offense.
Our defense managed to stop every attack in the second half of the match.
The coach praised the team's defense for their outstanding performance last night.
Our defence played exceptionally well in the second half of the match.
The coach praised the team's defence for holding off a strong attack.
This year, their defence has allowed fewer goals than any other team in the league.

Related Terms

  • antonym: offense - the part of a team that attempts to score points or goals
