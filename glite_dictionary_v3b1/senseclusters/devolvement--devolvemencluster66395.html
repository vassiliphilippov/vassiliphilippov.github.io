devolvement

noun
delegation of authority to regional bodies
2.8 6.5
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈvɑːlv.mənt/ /; / /də.ˈvɑːlv.mənt/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈvɒlv.mənt/ /; / /də.ˈvɒlv.mənt/ (BrE)
the transfer or delegation of power or responsibilities, especially from a central government to a regional or local authority

Examples

The devolvement of power
Debate continues over the devolvement of education policy from the national to the state level.
Many regions have benefited from the devolvement of certain administrative duties.
The new constitution called for the devolvement of power to local councils.

Related Terms

  • antonym: centralization - the concentration of control and authority in a central organization
  • synonym: devolution - the transfer of powers from a central to a regional authority
