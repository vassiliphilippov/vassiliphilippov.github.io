Anagasta kuehniella

noun
moth whose larvae infest stored grain
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈɡæstə kuːˈniːɛlə / (AmE), / ˌanəˈɡastə kuːˈniːɛlə / (BrE)
A small moth whose larvae infest and damage stored grain and flour.

Examples

Anagasta kuehniella larvae damage
Effective pest control is needed to prevent Anagasta kuehniella infestations in grain silos.
Stored flour was ruined by the larvae of Anagasta kuehniella.
The presence of Anagasta kuehniella is a serious concern for food storage facilities.
