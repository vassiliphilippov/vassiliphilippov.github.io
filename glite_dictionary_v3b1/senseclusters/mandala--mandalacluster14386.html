mandala

noun
circular geometric design in Hinduism and Buddhism
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.də.lə / (AmE), / ˈmæn.də.lə / (BrE)
A complex, often circular geometric design symbolizing the universe, chiefly used in Hinduism and Buddhism as an aid to meditation.

A mandala design
