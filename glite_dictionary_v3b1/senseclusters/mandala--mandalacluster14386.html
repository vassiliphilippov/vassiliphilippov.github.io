mandala

noun
circular geometric design in Hinduism and Buddhism
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.də.lə / (AmE), / ˈmæn.də.lə / (BrE)
A complex, often circular geometric design symbolizing the universe, chiefly used in Hinduism and Buddhism as an aid to meditation.

Examples

A mandala design
Meditators often focus on a mandala to deepen their concentration.
She admired the intricate patterns of the mandala in the temple.
The monks spent days creating a beautiful mandala out of colored sand.
