chypre

adjective
perfume style
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈʃiː.prər/ (AmE), /ˈʃiː.prə/ (BrE)
belonging to or characteristic of the chypre family of perfumes, typically with citrus top notes and a mossy, woody base.
