figureless

adjective
without clear shape
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪɡjərləs / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡjələs / (BrE)
lacking a distinct shape, form, or recognizable figure

Examples

Figureless painting
The artist painted a figureless scene, focusing on colors and light rather than people or objects.
In the early morning fog, the landscape appeared completely figureless and mysterious.
The story described a figureless shadow moving silently through the empty hallway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: formless - having no definite shape or form
  • synonym: shapeless - lacking a clear or definite shape
