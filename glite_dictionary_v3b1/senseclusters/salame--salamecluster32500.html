salame

noun
Italian cured sausage
Pronunciation: / səˈlɑːmeɪ / (AmE), / səˈlɑːmeɪ / (BrE)
a type of Italian cured sausage made from fermented and air-dried meat, similar to salami

Examples

A salame sandwich
He ordered a sandwich with salame and cheese for lunch.
The chef added slices of salame to the antipasto platter.
At the market, she bought fresh bread and spicy salame for a picnic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prosciutto - Italian dry-cured ham, usually thinly sliced
  • synonym: salami - a cured sausage made from fermented and air-dried meat
