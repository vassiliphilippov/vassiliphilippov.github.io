voder

noun
speech synthesizer device
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊdər / (AmE), / ˈvəʊdə / (BrE)
an early electronic device that could synthesize human speech sounds by imitating the human vocal tract

Examples

The voder machine
The voder amazed audiences with its ability to produce intelligible speech electronically.
Researchers studied the voder to understand how machines could mimic human voices.
At the 1939 World's Fair, the voder was demonstrated to the public for the first time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: speech synthesizer - a device or software that produces artificial human speech
  • synonym: vocoder - an electronic device that analyzes and synthesizes the human voice
