malady

noun
serious or desperate condition/problem
6.1 7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmæl.ə.di / (AmE), / ˈmælədi / (BrE)
a serious problem or condition, especially one that is unwholesome or difficult to remedy, often used in a figurative sense

Examples

Social malady
Corruption has been described as a malady affecting all levels of government.
Poverty remains a persistent malady in many regions of the world.
The economic malady has left many out of work and uncertain about their future.
