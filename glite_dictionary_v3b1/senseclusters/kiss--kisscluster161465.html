kiss

verb
touch with the lips as an expression
2.8
Pronunciation: / kɪs / (AmE), / kɪs / (BrE)

Examples

She leaned over to kiss her sleeping child gently on the forehead.
They kissed passionately before saying goodbye at the airport.
