anion

noun
negatively charged ion
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.aɪ.ˌɑn / (AmE), / ˈæn.aɪ.ɒn / (BrE)
A negatively charged ion; an atom or molecule that has gained one or more electrons.

Examples

A anion ion
Chloride is a common anion found in the human body.
During electrolysis, an anion moves toward the anode.
The solution contains both cations and anions.
