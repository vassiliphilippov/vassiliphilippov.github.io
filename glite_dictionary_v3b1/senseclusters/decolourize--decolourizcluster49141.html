decolourize

verb
make less colored or white

Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈkʌl.ə.raɪz/ /; / /diː.ˈkʌl.ə.raɪz/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈkʌl.ə.raɪz/ /; / /diː.ˈkʌl.ə.raɪz/ (BrE)

Examples

Sunlight can decolourize fabrics left outdoors for too long.
