overreach

verb
to exceed limits
6.2 0.87/m

to go beyond what is reasonable, allowed, or proper, especially in authority or action

Examples

To overreach authority
The government was criticized for trying to overreach its legal powers.
If you overreach in negotiations, you might lose the deal entirely.
She warned her team not to overreach and to stay within the project's scope.
