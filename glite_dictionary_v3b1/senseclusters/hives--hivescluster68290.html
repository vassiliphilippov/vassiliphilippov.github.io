hives

noun
itchy, allergic skin rash with welts
1.6
Pronunciation: / haɪvz / (AmE), / haɪvz / (BrE)
an allergic skin reaction characterized by raised, itchy, red welts on the skin's surface, often triggered by food, insect bites, or medication

Examples

Red hives on skin
She broke out in hives after eating strawberries.
Stress can sometimes cause hives to appear on the skin.
The doctor prescribed an antihistamine to relieve his hives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: urticaria - medical term for hives, the skin rash with red, itchy welts
