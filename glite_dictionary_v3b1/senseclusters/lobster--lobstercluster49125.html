lobster

noun
large edible marine crustacean
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑbstər / (AmE), / ˈlɒbstə / (BrE)
a large marine crustacean with a cylindrical body, stalked eyes, and large pincers, prized as seafood

Examples

A live lobster
She caught a lobster while snorkeling near the rocky coastline.
The chef prepared the freshest lobster for the evening special at the restaurant.
The price of lobster tends to rise during the holiday season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crab - a marine crustacean with a broad carapace and pincers
  • synonym: shrimp - a small, slender marine crustacean
