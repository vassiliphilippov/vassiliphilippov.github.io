rillette

noun
meat spread
9.9
Pronunciation: / riˈjɛt / (AmE), / riˈjɛt / (BrE)
a dish of meat, usually pork, cooked slowly in fat, shredded, and served as a spread

Examples

A rillette spread
They enjoyed a rustic rillette with pickles and fresh bread at the picnic.
The chef prepared duck rillette as a special appetizer for the evening.
You can store homemade rillette in the refrigerator for several days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pâté - a smooth spread made from finely ground meat or liver
  • synonym: terrine - a French meat dish cooked in a covered dish and served cold
