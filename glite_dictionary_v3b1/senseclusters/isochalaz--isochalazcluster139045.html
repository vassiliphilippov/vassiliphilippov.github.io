isochalaz

noun
line of equal hail storm frequency
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsoʊˈkeɪlæz / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəʊˈkeɪlæz / (BrE)
A line on a map connecting points having the same frequency of hail storms.

Examples

An isochalaz line
Drawing an isochalaz helps in understanding the spatial distribution of hailstorms.
Meteorologists use an isochalaz to study regions with similar hailstorm activities across a country.
The isochalaz on the weather map highlights areas prone to frequent hail.

  • synonym: isotherm - a line connecting points of equal temperature
