indebted

adjective
grateful for help or favors received
1.0 5.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɛtɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛtɪd / (BrE)
feeling grateful to someone because they have helped you, or owing someone a favor or obligation

Examples

Feeling indebted to someone
He left the company feeling indebted to his colleagues for their friendship and guidance.
I am indebted to you for your kindness during my illness.
She felt deeply indebted to her mentor for all his support over the years.
