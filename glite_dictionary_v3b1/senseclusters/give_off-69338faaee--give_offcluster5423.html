Synonyms

  • emit
    4.4 8.62/m
    emit is a neutral, often technical verb for sending out light, heat, or gas, while give off is common in everyday speech.
  • give out
    2.9 0.29/m
    give out often means produce or distribute and can also mean stop working, while give off specifically means emit smells, light, or heat.
  • emanate
    6.5 6.21/m
    emanate is more formal and shows something coming from a clear source, while give off is used for everyday smells, light, or heat.
