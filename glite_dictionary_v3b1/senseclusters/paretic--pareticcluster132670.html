paretic

adjective
partially paralyzed
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈrɛtɪk / (AmE), / pəˈrɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by partial paralysis or weakness of voluntary movement

Examples

Paretic muscles
After the accident, his left arm remained paretic for several months.
The doctor noted paretic symptoms in the patient's lower limbs.
Physical therapy helped improve the paretic muscles in her leg.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: paralyzed - unable to move or feel part or all of the body
  • degree lower: weak - lacking physical strength or energy
