pristine

adjective
spotlessly clean and unused
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪstin /; / ˈprɪstiːn, prɪˈstiːn / (AmE), / ˈprɪstiːn /; / ˈprɪstiːn, prɪˈstiːn / (BrE)
extremely clean, fresh, and in perfect condition; not spoiled or used
