uncomparable

adjective
not suitable for comparison
7.7 5.9
Pronunciation: / ʌn.kəmˈpɛr.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌn.kəmˈpær.ə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be compared because of lack of similarity or relevant features; unsuitable for comparison

Examples

An uncomparable experience
Some experiences are so unique that they remain uncomparable to anything else in life.
The data collected from different countries is uncomparable due to varying standards of measurement.
These two paintings are uncomparable because they belong to completely different artistic movements.
