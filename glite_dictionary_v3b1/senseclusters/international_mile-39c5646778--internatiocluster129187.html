international mile

noun
unit of length (1,760 yards; 1,609.344 meters)
6.4 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚˈnæʃənəl maɪl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl maɪl / (BrE)
a unit of length equal to 1,760 yards or 5,280 feet; exactly 1,609.344 meters, used especially in the United States and the United Kingdom

Examples

One international mile
A footrace of one international mile is equal to 1,609.344 meters.
Road distances in the UK are often marked in international miles instead of kilometers.
The marathon is officially measured in international miles for standardization purposes.
