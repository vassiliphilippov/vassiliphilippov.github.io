Council of Trent

proper noun
major Roman Catholic council, 1545–1563
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkaʊn.səl əv trent/ /; / /ˈkaʊn.səl ʌv trent/ (AmE), /ˈkaʊn.səl əv trent/ /; / /ˈkaʊn.səl ɒv trent/ (BrE)
the ecumenical council of the Roman Catholic Church held in Trento between 1545 and 1563, which aimed to address and condemn Protestant Reformation teachings, reform Church practices, and clarify Catholic doctrine
