currying

noun
leather finishing process
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜriɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌriɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of treating and finishing leather to improve its softness, flexibility, or appearance

Examples

Currying leather
The craftsman spent hours currying the leather to achieve a smooth and supple finish.
Traditional currying techniques are still used in some high-quality leather workshops.
Proper currying ensures that the leather remains flexible and resistant to water.
