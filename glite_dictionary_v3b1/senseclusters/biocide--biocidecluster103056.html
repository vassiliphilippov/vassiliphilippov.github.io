biocide

noun
organism-killing chemical
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪəˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəˌsaɪd / (BrE)
a chemical substance that kills or controls harmful living organisms such as bacteria, fungi, algae, or pests

Examples

A biocide chemical
The water treatment plant adds a biocide to prevent the growth of harmful bacteria.
Farmers sometimes use a biocide to protect crops from fungal infections.
Improper disposal of biocides can harm aquatic life in nearby rivers.
