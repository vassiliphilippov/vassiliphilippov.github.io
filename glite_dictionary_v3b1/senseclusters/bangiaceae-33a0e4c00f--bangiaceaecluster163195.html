Bangiaceae

noun
family of red algae
Pronunciation: / bænˈdʒiːəsi.iː /; / bæn.dʒiˈeɪ.siˌi / (AmE), / baŋˈɡiːəsɪ.iː /; / baŋ.giˈeɪ.sɪˌiː / (BrE)
a family of red algae (seaweeds) in the division Rhodophyta, found in marine environments
