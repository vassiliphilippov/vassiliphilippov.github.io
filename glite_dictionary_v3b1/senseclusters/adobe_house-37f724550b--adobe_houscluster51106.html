adobe house

noun
house made of adobe bricks
Pronunciation: / əˈdoʊbi haʊs / (AmE), / əˈdəʊbi haʊs / (BrE)
a house constructed from adobe bricks, typically made of sun-dried earth and straw

Examples

A adobe house
Many Southwestern villages are known for their distinctive adobe houses.
Restoring the old adobe house required careful attention to its original materials and design.
The family lived in a traditional adobe house that stayed cool during hot summers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adobe - sun-dried brick made from clay and straw
  • degree lower: mud hut - simple dwelling made of mud
