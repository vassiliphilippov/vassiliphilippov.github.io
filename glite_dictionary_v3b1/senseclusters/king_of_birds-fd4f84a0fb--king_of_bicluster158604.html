king of birds

noun
figurative term for the eagle
Pronunciation: / kɪŋ əv bɝdz / (AmE), / kɪŋ əv bɜːdz / (BrE)
a figurative term for the eagle, symbolizing power and nobility

Examples

The king of birds
Many national emblems feature the king of birds to represent strength and freedom.
The mighty king of birds soared above the mountain peaks, surveying its domain.
Throughout history, the eagle has been called the king of birds in literature and art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eagle - a large bird of prey with a massive hooked bill and long broad wings
