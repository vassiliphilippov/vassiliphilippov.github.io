mantled

adjective
covered or wrapped; enveloped
2.9 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmæn.təld/ (AmE), /ˈmæn.təld/ (BrE)
covered with or as if with a cloak, wrap, or covering; enveloped or concealed by a layer

Examples

The ancient castle stood mantled in ivy, giving it a mysterious appearance.
The mountain was mantled in a thick veil of mist, hiding its peak from view.
