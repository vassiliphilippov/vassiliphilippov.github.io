mantled

adjective
covered or wrapped; enveloped
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.təld / (AmE), / ˈmæn.təld / (BrE)
covered with or as if with a cloak, wrap, or covering; enveloped or concealed by a layer

Examples

Mantled in wool
Her shoulders were mantled in a soft woolen shawl to keep out the chill.
The ancient castle stood mantled in ivy, giving it a mysterious appearance.
The mountain was mantled in a thick veil of mist, hiding its peak from view.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bare - not covered or wrapped; without covering
  • synonym: cloaked - covered or concealed as with a cloak
  • synonym: shrouded - covered or enveloped, often with something that obscures
