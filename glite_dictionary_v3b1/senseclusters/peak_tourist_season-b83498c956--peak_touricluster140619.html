peak tourist season

noun
time with most tourists
1.0
Pronunciation: / pik ˈtʊr.ɪst ˈsi.zən / (AmE), / piːk ˈtʊə.rɪst ˈsiː.zən / (BrE)
the time of year when the greatest number of tourists visit a place

Examples

During peak tourist season
During peak tourist season, hotel rooms can be twice as expensive as usual.
It's best to book your tickets early if you plan to travel during peak tourist season.
Many locals take vacations themselves to avoid the crowds in peak tourist season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: off-season - the time of year when fewest tourists visit
