optimum

adjective
most favorable; best possible under conditions
Pronunciation: / ˈɑp.tə.məm / (AmE), / ˈɒp.tɪ.məm / (BrE)
the best or most favorable possible in a particular situation or under certain restrictions

Examples

Optimum temperature
It is important to maintain an optimum balance between work and rest.
She adjusted the settings to achieve optimum performance from the machine.
The engineers designed the system for optimum efficiency in hot climates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: optimal - most favorable or desirable; best
  • antonym: worst - least desirable or least favorable
