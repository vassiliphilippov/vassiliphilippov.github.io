Examples

The air passage system

Synonyms

  • airway
    4.5 0.04/m
    airway is a duct used to ventilate mines and tunnels, while air passage is a general channel for moving air.
  • air duct
    2.8 0.48/m
    air duct is a pipe-like passage in buildings and vehicles for moving conditioned air, while air passage covers any ventilation channel.
  • air shaft
    4.7 0.16/m
    air shaft is a mostly vertical passage that brings air up and down in mines or buildings, while air passage is any channel for ventilation.
  • vent
    2.7 7.48/m
