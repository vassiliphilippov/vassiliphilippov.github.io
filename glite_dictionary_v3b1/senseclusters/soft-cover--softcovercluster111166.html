soft-cover

noun
book with paper covers
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔftˌkʌvɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɒftˌkʌvə / (BrE)
A book with flexible paper covers, rather than a hard cover.

Examples

A soft-cover book
I prefer reading soft-cover editions because they're lighter to carry.
Most of the textbooks were available only in soft-cover.
The new novel was released in both hardcover and soft-cover formats.

Related Terms
