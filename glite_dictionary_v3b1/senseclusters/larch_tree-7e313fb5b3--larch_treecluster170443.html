larch tree

noun
deciduous coniferous tree (Larix genus)
8.5
Pronunciation: / lɑrtʃ tri / (AmE), / lɑːtʃ triː / (BrE)
A tree of the genus Larix, a conifer with needle-like leaves that are shed in autumn.

Examples

The larch tree needles
Every spring, the new needles on the larch tree emerge bright green and soft to the touch.
The forest was dominated by tall larch trees whose golden needles carpeted the ground in autumn.
Unlike most conifers, the larch tree loses its needles each winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: larch - a deciduous coniferous tree of the genus Larix
  • synonym: pine tree - an evergreen conifer of the genus Pinus
