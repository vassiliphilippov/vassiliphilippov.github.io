one of those things

noun
unavoidable or commonplace event
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌn əv ðoʊz ˈθɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈwʌn əv ðəʊz ˈθɪŋz / (BrE)
an unavoidable or unfortunate event or situation, often regarded as a normal part of life

Examples

One of those things in life
Getting caught in traffic is just one of those things you can't always avoid.
Missing the train was unfortunate, but it's one of those things that happens to everyone.
Sometimes plans fall through; it's simply one of those things in life.
