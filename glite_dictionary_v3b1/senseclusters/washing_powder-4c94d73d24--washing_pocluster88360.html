washing powder

noun
powdered detergent for washing clothes
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃɪŋ ˌpaʊdər / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃɪŋ ˌpaʊdə / (BrE)
A soap or detergent in powdered form used for washing clothes and other fabrics.

Examples

A box of washing powder
Don't forget to buy washing powder when you go to the supermarket.

Some washing powders are specially formulated for sensitive skin.
