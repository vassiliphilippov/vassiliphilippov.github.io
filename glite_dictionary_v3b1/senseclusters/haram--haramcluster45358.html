haram

noun
Islamic sanctuary
9.2
Pronunciation: / hɑːˈrɑːm / (AmE), / həˈrɑːm / (BrE)
a sacred or protected place, especially a sanctuary or holy site where certain actions are forbidden in Islamic tradition

Examples

The haram mosque
The Great Mosque of Mecca is considered the most important haram in Islam.
Visitors must follow strict rules when entering the haram to show respect for its sanctity.
Only Muslims are allowed inside the sacred haram during the annual pilgrimage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mosque - a Muslim place of worship
  • synonym: sanctuary - a sacred or safe place, especially in a religious context
