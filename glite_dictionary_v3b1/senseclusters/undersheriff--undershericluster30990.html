undersheriff

noun
sheriff's deputy
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌʃɛrɪf / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌʃɛrɪf / (BrE)

Examples

After the sheriff retired, the undersheriff temporarily took over the department's leadership.
The undersheriff attended the community meeting to answer questions about local law enforcement.
