inaugurate

verb
mark the beginning; usher in
5.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɔɡjəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈnɔːɡjʊreɪt /; / ɪˈnɔːɡjʊˌreɪt / (BrE)
to officially begin or introduce something, often with a ceremony or marking a significant change

  • synonym: open - to start or make accessible for use
  • antonym: terminate - to bring to an end or finish
