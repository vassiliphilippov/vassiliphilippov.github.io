sovereign

noun
hereditary monarch or supreme ruler
7.2 4.38/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…ëÀêv…ö…ôn / (AmE), / Ààs…ív…ôr…ôn / (BrE)
a monarch or other supreme ruler of a nation, especially one who holds the position by hereditary right

Examples

A sovereign king

Synonyms

    5.1 4.23/m

  • crowned head
    7.8 0.06/m
    crowned head is a formal, older phrase for a ruler with a crown, while sovereign focuses on supreme political authority.
