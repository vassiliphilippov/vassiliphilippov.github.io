supercool

adjective
below freezing, still liquid
7.6
Pronunciation: /suː.pər.ˈkuːl/ /; / /ˈsuː.pər.kuːl/ (AmE), /suː.pə.ˈkuːl/ /; / /ˈsuː.pə.kuːl/ (BrE)
