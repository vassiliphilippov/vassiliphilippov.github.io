powerset

noun
character's abilities
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊərˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈpaʊəˌsɛt / (BrE)
a set of abilities or special powers assigned to a character, especially in role-playing or video games

Examples

Superhero powerset
Each superhero in the game has a unique powerset that determines their strengths and weaknesses.
Players can customize their character's powerset to suit different play styles and strategies.
Unlocking a new powerset allows your character to use special abilities in battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skill set - a collection of skills or abilities a person or character has
  • synonym: superpower - an extraordinary ability or power, especially in fiction
