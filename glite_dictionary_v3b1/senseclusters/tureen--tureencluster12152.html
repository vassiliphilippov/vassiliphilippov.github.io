tureen

noun
large covered soup-serving dish
9.9
Pronunciation: / təˈrin / (AmE), / tjʊəˈriːn / (BrE)
a large, deep serving dish, typically with a lid, used for serving soups or stews

Examples

A tureen with lid
He ladled the stew from the tureen into each guest's bowl.
She inherited a silver tureen from her grandmother's collection of fine china.
The waiter brought the steaming tureen of soup to the table with a flourish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bowl - a round, deep dish used for holding food or liquid
  • antonym: plate - a flat dish used for serving or eating food
