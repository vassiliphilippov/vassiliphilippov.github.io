sawman

noun
saw operator
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈsɔːˌmæn / (BrE)
a person whose job is to operate a saw, especially in a sawmill or during logging

Examples

Experienced sawman cuts logs
The experienced sawman carefully cut the logs into even planks.
Each sawman in the mill must follow strict safety procedures while working.
After years as a sawman, he could judge the quality of wood just by its sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: logger - a person who works in logging, cutting down and preparing trees
  • synonym: sawyer - a person who saws wood, especially as an occupation
