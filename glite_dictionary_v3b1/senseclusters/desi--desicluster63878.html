desi

adjective
locally produced (India)
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪsi / (AmE), / ˈdeɪsi / (BrE)
originating or produced locally within the Indian subcontinent, not imported or foreign

Examples

Desi rice variety
Many farmers choose desi varieties of rice because they are better suited to the local climate.

She prefers desi products over imported ones for their authenticity and quality.
