megillah

noun
long, tedious account (informal)
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈɡɪlə / (AmE), / məˈɡɪlə / (BrE)
(informal) A lengthy and tediously detailed account or story, often perceived as boring or unnecessary.

Examples

The whole megillah
He told us the whole megillah when a simple answer would have sufficed.
I don't want to hear the entire megillah about your trip, just the highlights.
She launched into the megillah of how the meeting went wrong.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epic - a long narrative; an extended account
  • synonym: saga - a long, involved story, account, or series of incidents
  • antonym: summary - a brief statement or account giving the main points
