blueline

noun
proof copy of drawing
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈbluː.laɪn/ (AmE), /ˈbluː.laɪn/ (BrE)
a photographic copy of a drawing or document, usually with blue lines, used for checking or review before final printing

Examples

Blueline proof copy

A blueline of the floor plan was distributed to all contractors for reference during construction.
