blueline

noun
proof copy of drawing
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a photographic copy of a drawing or document, usually with blue lines, used for checking or review before final printing

Examples

Blueline proof copy
The engineer marked corrections on the blueline before sending it back to the design team.
We reviewed the blueline to ensure all measurements were accurate before final approval.
A blueline of the floor plan was distributed to all contractors for reference during construction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blueprint - a detailed technical drawing or plan, especially for architecture or engineering
  • antonym: original - the first or primary version of a document or drawing
