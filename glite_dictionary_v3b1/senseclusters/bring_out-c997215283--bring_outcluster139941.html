bring out

verb
launch or release something to the public
Pronunciation: / brɪŋ aʊt / (AmE), / brɪŋ aʊt / (BrE)
to make something available to the public, such as introducing, publishing, or launching a product, book, or performance

  • synonym: release - to make something available to the public
  • antonym: withdraw - to remove something from availability or the market
