butteriness

noun
smooth, rich quality
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtəri nəs / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəri nəs / (BrE)
a smooth, rich, or mellow quality, especially in sound, color, or style, resembling the texture of butter

Examples

Butteriness of her voice
The butteriness of her voice made the song soothing to listen to.
He admired the butteriness of the painting's colors, which gave it a warm feeling.
The butteriness in the speaker's tone helped calm the nervous audience.
