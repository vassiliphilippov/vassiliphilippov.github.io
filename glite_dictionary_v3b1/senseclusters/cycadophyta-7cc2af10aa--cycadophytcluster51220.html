Cycadophyta

noun
division of palmlike gymnosperms
Pronunciation: / saɪˌkædəˈfaɪtə / (AmE), / saɪˌkadəˈfʌɪtə / (BrE)
a group of palmlike gymnosperm plants, including living cycads and extinct related orders
