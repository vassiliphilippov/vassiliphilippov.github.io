inculpable

adjective
free from blame or guilt
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkʌlpəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈkʌlpəb(ə)l / (BrE)
not deserving blame or free from guilt; not liable to be blamed or considered guilty

Examples

Inculpable of guilt
Despite the accusations, the judge found the defendant inculpable of any wrongdoing.
Her reputation for being inculpable made her a trusted leader in the community.
The investigation concluded that all employees were inculpable in the loss of documents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blameless - not deserving blame or free from guilt
  • antonym: culpable - deserving blame; at fault
