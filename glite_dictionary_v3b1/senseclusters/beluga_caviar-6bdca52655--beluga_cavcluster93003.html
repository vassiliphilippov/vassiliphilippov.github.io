beluga caviar

noun
roe of beluga sturgeon; luxury delicacy
Pronunciation: / bəˈluːɡə ˈkæviɑr / (AmE), / bɪˈluːɡə ˈkævɪɑː / (BrE)
the roe of the beluga sturgeon, highly prized as a luxury delicacy, usually sourced from Russia or the Caspian Sea region

Examples

A serving of beluga caviar
He treated himself to a small serving of beluga caviar at the exclusive restaurant.
Only the wealthiest guests could afford beluga caviar at the charity gala.
The chef carefully placed a spoonful of beluga caviar on each canapé.
