spray paint

noun
paint applied in spray form
Pronunciation: / spreɪ peɪnt / (AmE), / spreɪ peɪnt / (BrE)
Paint that is dispersed in a fine mist and applied to surfaces using a spray gun or aerosol can.

Examples

A can of spray paint
He used spray paint to create a colorful mural on the wall.
The artist prefers spray paint for its smooth, even coverage.
Vandals covered the sign in red spray paint overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aerosol paint - Paint dispensed from a pressurized can in a fine spray
