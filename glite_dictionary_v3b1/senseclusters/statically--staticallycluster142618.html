statically

adverb
without movement
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstætɪkli / (AmE), / ˈstætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that does not change, move, or involve motion

Examples

Statically placed object
The sculpture was displayed statically in the center of the gallery.
Some websites load content statically rather than updating it in real time.
The car remained statically parked on the street for several days.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dynamically - in a way that involves movement or change
  • synonym: stationarily - in a way that remains in one place or does not move
