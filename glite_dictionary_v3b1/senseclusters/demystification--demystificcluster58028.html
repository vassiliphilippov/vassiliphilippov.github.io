demystification

noun
making clear or simple
5.6
Pronunciation: / diˌmɪstəfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / diˌmɪstɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of making something easier to understand or less mysterious, usually by explaining it clearly
