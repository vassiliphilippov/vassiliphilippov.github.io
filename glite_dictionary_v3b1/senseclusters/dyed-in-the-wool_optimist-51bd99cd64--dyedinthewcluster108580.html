dyed-in-the-wool optimist

noun
person who is always optimistic
5.8
Pronunciation: / daɪd ɪn ðə wʊl ˈɑptəmɪst / (AmE), / daɪd ɪn ðə wʊl ˈɒptɪmɪst / (BrE)
A person who is consistently and deeply optimistic, displaying an unwavering positive outlook.

Examples

A dyed-in-the-wool optimist always hopeful
As a dyed-in-the-wool optimist, she always expects everything to turn out well.
Even during tough times, Mark remains a dyed-in-the-wool optimist and lifts everyone’s spirits.
You need to be a dyed-in-the-wool optimist to see the bright side of this situation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: optimist - a person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future
  • antonym: pessimist - a person who tends to see the worst aspect of things
