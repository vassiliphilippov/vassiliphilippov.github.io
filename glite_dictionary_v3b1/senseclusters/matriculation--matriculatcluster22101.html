matriculation

noun
formal admission to a college or university
5.8
Pronunciation: / məˌtrɪkjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / məˌtrɪkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the formal process of being admitted to a group, especially enrollment in a college or university

Examples

College matriculation ceremony
He proudly attended the matriculation ceremony with his family.
Her matriculation at the university marked the beginning of a new academic journey.
The college requires evidence of matriculation before students can attend classes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enrollment - the act of officially joining a school or course
  • antonym: graduation - the successful completion of a course of study
