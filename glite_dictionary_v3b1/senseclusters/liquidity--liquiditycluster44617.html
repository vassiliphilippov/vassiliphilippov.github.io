liquidity

noun
the state of being liquid (readiness to flow)
6.1
Pronunciation: / lɪˈkwɪdəti / (AmE), / lɪˈkwɪdəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being liquid or able to flow easily; fluidity

Examples

High liquidity of water

Engineers test the liquidity of different chemicals when designing new products.
The liquidity of the metal increased after it was heated.
