create

verb
to invest with a new title or rank
Pronunciation: / kɹiˈeɪt / (AmE), / kɹiˈeɪt / (BrE)
to formally give someone a new official title, office, or rank, especially in a royal, noble, or religious context

Examples

To create a lord
He was created a peer for his years of service to the country.
The Queen will create him a baron tomorrow in the official ceremony.
The pope created several new cardinals last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appoint - to assign a job or position to someone
  • antonym: depose - to remove someone from a position of authority
