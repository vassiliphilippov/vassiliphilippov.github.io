rale

noun
abnormal rattling lung sound
8.1
Pronunciation: / reɪl / (AmE), / reɪl / (BrE)
an abnormal rattling sound heard in the lungs during breathing, usually detected with a stethoscope and associated with certain respiratory disorders

Examples

A rale in lungs
If you notice a rale, it's important to consult a healthcare provider promptly.
Persistent rales can indicate fluid in the lungs and may require further investigation.
The doctor heard a distinct rale in the patient's left lung during the examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crackle - a short, sharp sound, also used for certain lung sounds
  • synonym: rattle - a rapid succession of short, sharp sounds
