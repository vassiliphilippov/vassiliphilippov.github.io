avocado pear

noun
tropical fruit; also called avocado
Pronunciation: / ˌævəˈkɑdoʊ pɛr / (AmE), / ˌavəˈkɑːdəʊ peə / (BrE)
A pear-shaped tropical fruit with a rough, green or blackish skin, soft yellowish flesh, and a large seed in the center.

Examples

A avocado pear fruit
In some markets, avocado pear is the common name for the fruit known simply as avocado elsewhere.
She sliced an avocado pear for the salad, adding a creamy texture to the dish.
The avocado pear is rich in healthy fats and vitamins.
