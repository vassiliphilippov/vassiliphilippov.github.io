subcell

noun
cellular network subdivision
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌsɛl / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌsɛl / (BrE)
a smaller division within a cellular radio coverage area, used to manage network capacity, interference, or handovers

Examples

Network subcell
Engineers created a subcell to handle the increased traffic in the city center.
The network automatically assigned the call to a different subcell to avoid congestion.
By dividing the area into several subcells, the operator improved service quality during busy hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cell - a basic geographic area covered by a cellular network
