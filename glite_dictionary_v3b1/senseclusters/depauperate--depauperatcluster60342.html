depauperate

adjective
lacking diversity
9.9
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈpɑːr.ɪt/ /; / /dɪ.ˈpɑːr.ət/ /; / /dɪ.ˈpɔːr.ɪt/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈpɔː.rɪt/ /; / /dɪ.ˈpɒ.prɪt/ (BrE)
