Synonyms

  • sexually transmitted disease
    2.7 1/m
    sexually transmitted disease is the full phrase used in formal writing and health talk, while STD is the common abbreviation.
  • venereal disease
    3.7 0.3/m
    venereal disease sounds older and formal, used in medical or historical texts, while STD is the modern everyday term.
  • VD
    7.4 0.3/m

  • venereal infection
    5.8 0.01/m
