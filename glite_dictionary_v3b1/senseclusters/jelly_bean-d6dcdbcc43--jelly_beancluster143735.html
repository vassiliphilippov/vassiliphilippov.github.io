jelly bean

noun
bean-shaped sugar-glazed candy
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛli ˌbiːn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛli ˌbiːn / (BrE)
A small, bean-shaped candy with a soft jelly center and a sugar-glazed shell, typically available in various flavors and colors.

Examples

A jelly bean candy
My favorite flavor of jelly bean is cherry, but I also love the lemon ones.
She offered me a handful of jelly beans from the colorful glass jar.
We decorated the cupcakes with a single jelly bean on top of each.
