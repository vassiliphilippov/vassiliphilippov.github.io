translucently

adverb
letting light through
5.2
Pronunciation: /træn.ˈluː.sənt.li/ /; / /træns.ˈluː.sənt.li/ (AmE), /træn.ˈluː.sənt.li/ /; / /træns.ˈluː.sənt.li/ (BrE)
in a manner that allows light to pass through but not detailed shapes

Examples

Glass shines translucently
The frosted glass glowed translucently in the morning sunlight.
She painted the lampshade so the light would shine translucently through the colors.
The jellyfish drifted translucently in the clear blue water, almost invisible.
