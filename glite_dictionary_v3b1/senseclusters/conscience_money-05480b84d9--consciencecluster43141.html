conscience money

noun
voluntary payment to atone for dishonesty
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːnʃəns ˌmʌni /; / ˈkɑnʃəns ˌmʌni / (AmE), / ˈkɒnʃəns ˌmʌni / (BrE)
money given voluntarily to make amends for past dishonesty or wrongdoing, especially to relieve feelings of guilt
