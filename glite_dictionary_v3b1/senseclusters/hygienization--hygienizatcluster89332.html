hygienization

noun
making hygienic
Pronunciation: / haɪˌdʒiːnəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / haɪˌdʒiːnʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something clean and sanitary, especially by removing harmful microorganisms or improving hygiene

Examples

Room hygienization
Regular hygienization of public spaces helps prevent the spread of disease.
The hygienization of medical instruments is a critical step before surgery.
Strict hygienization procedures are followed in the food industry to ensure safety.
The government launched a hygienization program to improve public health in rural areas.
Proper hygienization of food preparation surfaces helps prevent the spread of disease.
Community workshops on hygienization taught families the importance of handwashing and clean water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disinfection - the process of eliminating most harmful microorganisms
  • synonym: sanitization - the process of making something clean and safe by removing germs
