antiballistic

noun
missile interceptor
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌænti bəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌænti bəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
a missile or weapon system designed to intercept and destroy incoming ballistic missiles

Examples

An antiballistic weapon
The military deployed an antiballistic to protect the city from potential missile attacks.
Engineers tested the new antiballistic under extreme weather conditions to ensure reliability.
An advanced antiballistic successfully intercepted the enemy missile before it reached its target.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antiballistic missile - a missile designed to counter incoming ballistic missiles
  • antonym: ballistic missile - a missile that follows a ballistic trajectory to deliver a warhead
