abdominally

adverb
through the abdomen
6.3
Pronunciation: / æbˈdɑːmənəli / (AmE), / æbˈdɒmɪnəli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or through the abdomen

Examples

Inserted abdominally needle
The doctor inserted the needle abdominally to collect a fluid sample.
Some surgeries are performed abdominally rather than through other entry points.
The medication was administered abdominally for faster absorption.
