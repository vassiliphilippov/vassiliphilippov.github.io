whites

noun
white clothing items
Pronunciation: / waɪts / (AmE), / waɪts / (BrE)
white clothes, especially those worn for sports or as part of a uniform

Examples

Tennis whites
After the tennis match, she put her whites in the laundry basket.
Cricket players are required to wear whites during official games.
He forgot to pack his whites for the tournament and had to borrow some.

Related Terms

  • antonym: colors - clothes of various colors, especially as opposed to white clothes
  • synonym: uniform - a set of standard clothing worn by members of an organization
