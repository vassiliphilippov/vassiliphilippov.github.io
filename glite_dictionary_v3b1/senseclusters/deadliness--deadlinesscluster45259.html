deadliness

noun
the quality of being deadly
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdlinəs / (AmE), / ˈdɛdlinəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being able to cause death or great harm; lethality.

Examples

The deadliness of poison
Many underestimate the deadliness of extreme weather events until it is too late.
The deadliness of the virus made it a significant threat to public health.
The documentary emphasized the deadliness of certain wild animals in their natural habitats.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harmlessness - the quality of causing no harm
  • synonym: lethality - the capacity to cause death
