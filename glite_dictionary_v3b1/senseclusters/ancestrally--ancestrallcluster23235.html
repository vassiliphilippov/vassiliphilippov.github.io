ancestrally

adverb
relating to ancestors
6.0
Pronunciation: / ænˈsɛstrəli / (AmE), / ænˈsɛstrəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to ancestors or previous generations

Examples

Owned ancestrally
The land was ancestrally owned by her family for centuries.
Many traditions are ancestrally passed down from one generation to the next.
The tribe claims an ancestrally significant connection to the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hereditarily - in a way that is passed down by inheritance
