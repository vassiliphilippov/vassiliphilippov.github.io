Corsican Army

noun
Corsican militant group (1999-)
Pronunciation: / kɔrˈsɪkən ˈɑːrmi / (AmE), / kɔːˈsɪkən ˈɑːmi / (BrE)
a militant organization founded in 1999 to resist links between Corsican nationalists and the Corsican mafia, known for acts of political violence in Corsica

Examples

The Corsican Army group
Authorities have linked the Corsican Army to attacks on businesses associated with organized crime.
The Corsican Army was responsible for several bombings targeting government buildings in the early 2000s.
The influence of the Corsican Army in local politics has been a subject of debate for years.
