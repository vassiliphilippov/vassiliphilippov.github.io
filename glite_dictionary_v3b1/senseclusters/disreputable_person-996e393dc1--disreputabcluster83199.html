disreputable person

noun
person with a bad reputation
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsˈrɛpjətəbl̩ ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˌdɪsˈrɛpjʊtəbl̩ ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is not respected by others because of their bad reputation or behavior

Examples

A disreputable person
He was considered a disreputable person because of his frequent run-ins with the law.
It's risky to associate with a disreputable person in a small community.
The company avoided hiring anyone known to be a disreputable person.

Related Terms

  • antonym: upstanding citizen - a person known for good moral character and respect in the community
