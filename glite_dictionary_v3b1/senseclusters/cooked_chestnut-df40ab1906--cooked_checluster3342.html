cooked chestnut

noun
chestnut prepared by boiling or roasting
Pronunciation: / kʊkt ˈtʃɛsˌnʌt / (AmE), / kʊkt ˈtʃɛs.nʌt / (BrE)
a chestnut that has been boiled, roasted, or otherwise heated until ready to eat

Examples

A cooked chestnut snack
After peeling the shell, the cooked chestnut was soft and sweet.
She scattered cooked chestnut pieces over the holiday salad for extra flavor.
The street vendor sold hot cooked chestnut snacks in paper bags.

Related Terms

  • antonym: raw chestnut - a chestnut that has not been cooked or processed
