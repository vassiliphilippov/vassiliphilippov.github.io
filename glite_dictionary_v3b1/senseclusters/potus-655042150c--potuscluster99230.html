POTUS

noun
US presidential office
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊtəs / (AmE), / ˈpəʊtəs / (BrE)
the office or position of the President of the United States, referring to the institution rather than the individual

Examples

The POTUS role
The POTUS is responsible for signing bills into law and leading the executive branch.
After the election, the new POTUS addressed the nation from the White House.
Many decisions made by the POTUS have a global impact.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Oval Office - the official office of the US president in the White House
  • synonym: White House - the official residence and workplace of the US president
