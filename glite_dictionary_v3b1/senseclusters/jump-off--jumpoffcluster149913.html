jump-off

noun
tie-breaker round
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmp ˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmp ˌɒf / (BrE)
a tie-breaking round in show jumping or other equestrian competitions used to determine the winner

Examples

Show jump-off round
The competition ended in a tie, so a jump-off was held to decide the champion.
Her horse performed flawlessly in the jump-off, securing her the gold medal.
Spectators cheered as the riders prepared for the exciting jump-off round.

Related Terms

  • synonym: final - the last round or match to determine the winner
  • synonym: playoff - an extra game or round to decide a winner after a tie
