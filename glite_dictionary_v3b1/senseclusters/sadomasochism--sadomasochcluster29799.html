sadomasochism

noun
pleasure from inflicting and receiving pain or humiliation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌseɪdoʊˈmæsəˌkɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌseɪdəʊˈmæsəkɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
The tendency to derive pleasure, especially sexual gratification, from both inflicting and receiving pain or humiliation.

Examples

Sadomasochism in culture
He wrote a book analyzing the roots and cultural impact of sadomasochism.
Some couples explore sadomasochism as part of their consensual relationships.
The documentary discussed the psychological aspects of sadomasochism in human behavior.
