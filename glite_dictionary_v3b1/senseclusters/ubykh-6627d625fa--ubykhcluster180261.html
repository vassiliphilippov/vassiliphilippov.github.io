Ubykh

noun
extinct Caucasian language of Turkey
Pronunciation: / ˈuːbɪk / (AmE), / ˈjuːbɪk / (BrE)
an extinct Northwest Caucasian language formerly spoken exclusively in Turkey by the Ubykh people
