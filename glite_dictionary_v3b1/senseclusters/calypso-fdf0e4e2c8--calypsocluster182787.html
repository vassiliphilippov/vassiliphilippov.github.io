Calypso

noun
genus of orchids (fairy slipper)
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪpsoʊ / (AmE), / kəˈlɪpsəʊ / (BrE)
A genus of orchids with a single species found in northern North America and Eurasia, commonly known as the fairy slipper.

Examples

Calypso orchid
Botanists were excited to discover a population of Calypso orchids in the new reserve.
The delicate flowers of the Calypso are easily overlooked due to their small size and hidden location.
The rare Calypso can be found in cool, mossy forests across northern regions.
