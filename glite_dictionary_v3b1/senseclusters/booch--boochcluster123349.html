booch

noun
informal word for kombucha drink
Pronunciation: / butʃ / (AmE), / buːtʃ / (BrE)
an informal term for kombucha, a fermented, slightly effervescent drink made from sweetened tea and a symbiotic culture of bacteria and yeast

Examples

A cold booch
After my run, I drank a cold booch to cool down.
Many health food stores now sell bottles of booch in various flavors.
She offered her guests a homemade booch with ginger flavor.

Related Terms
