Tonto

proper noun
Arizona place name
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːntoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɒntəʊ / (BrE)
the name of several places in central Arizona, especially a large national forest and nearby geographic features

Examples

Tonto National Forest
They hiked through the Tonto National Forest and set up camp beside the river.
The Tonto Basin is known for its beautiful desert landscapes and unique wildlife.
Many tourists visit the Tonto Natural Bridge to see the impressive rock formations.
