adventurous spirit

noun
willingness to take risks; love of adventure
1.0
Pronunciation: / ədˈvɛntʃərəs ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃ(ə)rəs ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
the quality of being willing to take risks and seek new experiences or adventures

Examples

An adventurous spirit
He was admired for his adventurous spirit and willingness to try new things.
Her adventurous spirit led her to climb mountains all over the world.
You need an adventurous spirit to travel solo in remote areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adventurous - willing to take risks or try out new things
  • antonym: timid - lacking in courage or confidence, not adventurous
