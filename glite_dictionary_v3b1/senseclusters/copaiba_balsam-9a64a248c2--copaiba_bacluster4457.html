copaiba balsam

noun
aromatic oleoresin from Copaifera trees
Pronunciation: / koʊˈpaɪbə ˈbælsəm / (AmE), / kəˈpaɪbə ˈbɑːlsəm / (BrE)
an aromatic oleoresin obtained from trees of the genus Copaifera, used in varnishes, perfumes, and medicinal ointments

Examples

copaiba balsam in varnishes
Art restorers sometimes use copaiba balsam as a component in varnishes for paintings.
Some herbal remedies include copaiba balsam for its anti-inflammatory properties.
The ointment contains copaiba balsam to help soothe skin irritation.
