try

verb
make an effort; attempt
Pronunciation: / traɪ / (AmE), / traɪ / (BrE)
to make an effort to do or achieve something; to attempt to do something, especially something difficult

Examples

To try to win
I always try to read before bed, even when I'm tired.
If you never try, you’ll never know what you can accomplish.
She decided to try cooking a new recipe for dinner last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attempt - to make an effort to achieve or complete something
  • antonym: fail - to be unsuccessful in achieving something
  • antonym: succeed - to achieve the desired aim or result
