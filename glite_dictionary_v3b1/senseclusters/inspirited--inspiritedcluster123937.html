inspirited

verb
to encourage
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈspɪrɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈspɪrɪtɪd / (BrE)
to fill someone with courage, enthusiasm, or a positive feeling

Examples

Inspirited the team
The coach's words inspirited the players before the important match.
Her success story inspirited many people to pursue their dreams.
The lively music inspirited the crowd at the festival.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discourage - to make someone less confident or enthusiastic
  • synonym: encourage - to give someone support or confidence
  • synonym: hearten - to make someone feel more cheerful or hopeful
