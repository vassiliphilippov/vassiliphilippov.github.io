flicky

adjective
quick, jerky movement
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪki / (AmE), / ˈflɪki / (BrE)
having a quick, light, or jerky movement or style

Examples

Flicky pen strokes
She drew a flicky line across the page with her pen.
His hair had a flicky style that made it look lively and fun.
The dancer's flicky movements added energy to the performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jerky - moving with sudden, uneven motions
  • antonym: smooth - moving in a steady, even way
