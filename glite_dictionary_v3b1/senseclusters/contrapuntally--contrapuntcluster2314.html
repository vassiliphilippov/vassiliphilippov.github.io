contrapuntally

adverb
in counterpoint style
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːntrəˈpʌntəli / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrəˈpʌntəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or resembles counterpoint in music, involving the combination of two or more independent melodies

Examples

In the fugue, the themes are developed contrapuntally throughout the piece.
