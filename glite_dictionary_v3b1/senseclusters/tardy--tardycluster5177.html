tardy

noun
record of lateness
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːrdi / (AmE), / ˈtɑːdi / (BrE)
an instance or record of being late, especially to school or work

Examples

A school tardy
If you have too many tardies, you may receive a detention.
The teacher marked three tardies on his attendance sheet this month.
Students with frequent tardies might need to meet with the principal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: absence - the state of being away or not present
  • synonym: lateness - the state or quality of being late
