stress

verb
pronounce with emphasis or accent
Pronunciation: / strɛs / (AmE), / strɛs / (BrE)
to pronounce a syllable or word with particular emphasis or an accent

Examples

To stress the first syllable
In poetry, it is common to stress certain syllables for effect.
She stressed the first syllable of the word in her pronunciation.
When learning English, beginners often stress the wrong syllable.
