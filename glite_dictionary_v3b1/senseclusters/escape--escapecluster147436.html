Synonyms

  • avoid
    1.0 152/m
    avoid is a common, deliberate action to keep away from harm, while escape focuses on not being affected or experiencing it.
  • evade
    4.4 3.50/m
    evade is to avoid something by being clever or tricky, while escape is simply to not be affected by it.
  • elude
    5.8 5.14/m
    elude often means slipping away or not being caught, while escape means avoiding being affected or harmed.
  • miss
    1.1 11/m
    miss means failing to experience something by chance, while escape means avoiding being affected on purpose or by luck.
