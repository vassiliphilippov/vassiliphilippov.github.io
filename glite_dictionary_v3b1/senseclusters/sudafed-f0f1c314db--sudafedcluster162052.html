Sudafed

noun
brand of decongestant medication
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːdəˌfɛd / (AmE), / ˈsuːdəˌfɛd / (BrE)
A proprietary name for a nasal decongestant medication containing pseudoephedrine or phenylephrine, used to relieve nasal congestion.

Examples

Sudafed nasal spray
Many people take Sudafed to relieve symptoms of a stuffy nose during allergy season.
The doctor recommended Sudafed as an over-the-counter option for nasal congestion.
You should consult a pharmacist before using Sudafed if you have high blood pressure.
