Sanctus

noun
Christian hymn of praise
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsæŋktəs / (AmE), / ˈsaŋktʊs / (BrE)
a hymn of praise that is part of the Christian Mass, beginning with the words 'Holy, Holy, Holy'

Examples

The Sanctus hymn
The choir sang the Sanctus beautifully during the Easter service.
Many composers have written their own versions of the Sanctus for the Mass.
As the congregation joined in the Sanctus, the church was filled with music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Agnus Dei - a liturgical prayer in the Christian Mass
  • synonym: Gloria - a hymn of praise in the Christian liturgy
