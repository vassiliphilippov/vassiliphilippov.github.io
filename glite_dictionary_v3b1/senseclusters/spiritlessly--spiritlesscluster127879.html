spiritlessly

adverb
without enthusiasm
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪtləsli / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtləsli / (BrE)
in a way that shows no enthusiasm, energy, or interest

Examples

To work spiritlessly
He spiritlessly completed his chores, barely paying attention to what he was doing.
The team played spiritlessly after losing their captain to injury.
She greeted her guests spiritlessly, making it clear she was not in the mood for company.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enthusiastically - in a way that shows excitement or eagerness
  • synonym: listlessly - in a way that shows a lack of energy or interest
