karyokinesis

noun
division of a cell's nucleus
9.9 8.8
Pronunciation: /kə.ri.oʊ.kɪ.ˈniː.sɪs/ /; / /kæ.ri.oʊ.kɪ.ˈniː.sɪs/ (AmE), /kə.ri.əʊ.kɪ.ˈniː.sɪs/ /; / /kæ.ri.əʊ.kɪ.ˈniː.sɪs/ (BrE)
