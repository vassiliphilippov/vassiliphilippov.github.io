pipestone

noun
red stone for pipes
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪpstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈpaɪpstəʊn / (BrE)
a soft, reddish stone traditionally used by Native Americans to make pipes and carvings

Examples

A pipestone pipe
The artist carved a beautiful pipe from pipestone found near the river.
Many Native American artifacts are made from pipestone because of its unique color and softness.
Visitors to the quarry can watch craftsmen shape pipestone into traditional objects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catlinite - a type of red pipestone used for Native American pipes
  • synonym: soapstone - a soft stone used for carving and making objects
