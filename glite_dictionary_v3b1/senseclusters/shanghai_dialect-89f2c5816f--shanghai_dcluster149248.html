Shanghai dialect

noun
Chinese dialect of the Shanghai region
9.3
Pronunciation: / ʃæŋˈhaɪ ˈdaɪ.əˌlɛkt / (AmE), / ʃæŋˈhaɪ ˈdaɪ.ə.lɛkt / (BrE)
the variety of Chinese spoken in the Shanghai region and surrounding areas of the Yangtze delta

Examples

Shanghai dialect speakers
Many elderly residents still prefer to speak Shanghai dialect at home.
She is fluent in both Mandarin and Shanghai dialect.
Studies of the Shanghai dialect reveal its unique pronunciation and vocabulary.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Mandarin Chinese - the most widely spoken Chinese dialect, different from the Shanghai dialect
  • synonym: Wu Chinese - the group of Chinese varieties including the Shanghai dialect
