filioque

noun
theological phrase
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪliˈoʊkweɪ / (AmE), / ˌfɪliˈɒkwi / (BrE)
the Latin phrase 'and the Son' added to the Nicene Creed, which became a major point of dispute in Christian theology
