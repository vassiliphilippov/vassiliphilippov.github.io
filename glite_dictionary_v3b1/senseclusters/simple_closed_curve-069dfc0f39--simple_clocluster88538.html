simple closed curve

noun
a non-self-intersecting closed curve
2.2 7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmpəl kloʊzd kɝv / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpəl kləʊzd kɜːv / (BrE)
a continuous closed curve in a plane that does not intersect itself, except at the endpoints, which coincide

Examples

A simple closed curve
A circle is an example of a simple closed curve in the plane.
Mathematicians study the properties of a simple closed curve to understand concepts like inside and outside.
The boundary of a region in geometry is often a simple closed curve.
