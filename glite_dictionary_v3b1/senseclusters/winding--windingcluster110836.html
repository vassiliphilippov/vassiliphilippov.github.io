winding

noun
the act of twisting or coiling
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪndɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of turning or twisting something around to coil it or to tighten it, especially as with a key or string.

Examples

Winding a clock
The winding of the old clock required a special key.
With each careful winding, the yarn became more tangled.
You can hear the clicking sound during the winding of the music box.

Related Terms

  • synonym: twisting - the act of turning something in a spiral or circular motion
