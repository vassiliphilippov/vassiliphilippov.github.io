jumping jack

noun
toy with moving limbs operated by strings
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ ˌdʒæk / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ ˌdʒæk / (BrE)
a toy figure with jointed limbs that can be made to move up and down by pulling attached strings

Examples

A jumping jack toy
As a child, she loved playing with her jumping jack and making it dance.
By pulling the strings, you can make the jumping jack kick its legs and flap its arms.
The old wooden jumping jack he found at the market brought back memories of his childhood.
