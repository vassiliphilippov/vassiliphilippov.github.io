fang

verb
to seize with fangs
Pronunciation: / fæŋ / (AmE), / fæŋ / (BrE)
to seize, grip, or bite with fangs or as if with fangs

Examples

To fang a rabbit
The wolf tried to fang the rabbit as it darted through the snow.
In the story, the vampire would fang its victims under the cover of darkness.
The dog attempted to fang the intruder but was called back by its owner.
