isotropically

adverb
uniformly in all directions
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪ.səˈtrɑ.pɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.səˈtrɒ.pɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a manner that is uniform in all directions; having identical properties in every direction.

Examples

To move isotropically
Light travels isotropically through certain transparent substances, unaffected by orientation.
The material expands isotropically when heated, maintaining the same properties in every direction.
The solution is designed to diffuse isotropically, ensuring even distribution throughout the medium.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anisotropically - in a manner not uniform in all directions
