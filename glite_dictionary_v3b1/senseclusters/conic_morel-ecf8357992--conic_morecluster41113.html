conic morel

noun
a species of morel mushroom with a cone-shaped cap
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ën…™k m…îÀêrÀà…õl / (AmE), / Ààk…ín…™k m…îÀêÀàr…õl / (BrE)
A species of morel mushroom with a cone-shaped, pitted cap that is attached to the stem without a free skirt; the cap is grey when young and turns black with age.

Examples

A conic morel mushroom
Please be cautious when foraging for conic morels to avoid confusing them with toxic lookalikes.
The conic morel is sought after by mushroom hunters due to its unique flavor.
When young, the conic morel appears grey, but darkens as it matures.
