brew

noun
fermented drink (especially beer)
Pronunciation: / bruː / (AmE), / bruː / (BrE)
a drink made by steeping, boiling, and fermenting ingredients, especially alcoholic beverages like beer, rather than by distilling

Examples

A cold brew
Many people enjoy a cold brew after a long day at work.
She tasted the homemade brew and smiled at its unique taste.
The local pub serves its own brew that has a rich and hoppy flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beer - an alcoholic drink made from yeast-fermented malt flavored with hops
  • antonym: distillate - a liquid product condensed from vapor in a distillation process
