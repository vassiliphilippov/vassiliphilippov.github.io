broke-ass lifestyle

noun
life marked by constant lack of money
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊk ˌæs ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊk ˌas ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
an informal way of describing a lifestyle marked by a lack of money and constant financial struggle

Examples

Living a broke-ass lifestyle
Even though I'm living a broke-ass lifestyle, I still find ways to have fun.
He wrote a blog about his broke-ass lifestyle after graduating from college with debt.
Their broke-ass lifestyle meant getting creative with every meal and every dollar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impoverished - lacking sufficient money, poor
  • antonym: wealthy lifestyle - a lifestyle characterized by abundance and affluence
