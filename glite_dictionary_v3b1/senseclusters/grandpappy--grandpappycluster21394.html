grandpappy

noun
originator; earliest example
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrænˌpæpi / (AmE), / ˈɡrænˌpæpi / (BrE)
an informal term for a person or thing considered the original or earliest example of a style, movement, or type

Examples

Grandpappy of rock music
Many historians call him the grandpappy of modern jazz because of his early recordings.
This old computer is the grandpappy of all the devices we use today.
She is often referred to as the grandpappy of contemporary dance in the region.

  • synonym: forefather - a person who originated or started something
