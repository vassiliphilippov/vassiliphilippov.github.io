Bhadrapada

noun
sixth month of the Hindu calendar
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːdrəˈpɑːdə / (AmE), / ˌbʌdrəˈpɑːdə / (BrE)
the sixth month of the Hindu calendar, typically falling in August–September of the Gregorian calendar

Examples

Month of Bhadrapada
In India, the monsoon rains often continue through Bhadrapada.
Many Hindu festivals are celebrated during Bhadrapada, including Ganesh Chaturthi.
The month of Bhadrapada usually corresponds to August or September in the Gregorian calendar.
