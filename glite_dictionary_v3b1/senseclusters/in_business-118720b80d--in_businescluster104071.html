in business

preposition
engaged in commercial activities
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ɪn ˈbɪznəs / (BrE)
actively engaged or involved in commercial activities, especially as the owner, manager, or employee of a business

Examples

In business today
Despite many challenges, our bookstore is still in business today.
He learned a lot from working in business before starting his own company.
My sister has been in business since she opened her café five years ago.

Related Terms

  • antonym: out of business - no longer operating as a business
