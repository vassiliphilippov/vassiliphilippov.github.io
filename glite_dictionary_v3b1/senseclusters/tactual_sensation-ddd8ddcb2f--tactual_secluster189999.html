tactual sensation

noun
sensation of touch or pressure
Pronunciation: / ˈtæk.tʃu.əl sɛnˈseɪ.ʃən /; / ˈtæktʃuəl sɛnˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈtæk.tʃʊ.əl sɛnˈseɪ.ʃən /; / ˈtæktʃʊəl sɛnˈseɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a physical feeling or perception you get through touch, such as pressure, heat, or pain, using the skin
