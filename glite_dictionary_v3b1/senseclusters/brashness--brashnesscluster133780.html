brashness

noun
offensively bold or showy behaviour
5.2 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈbræʃ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈbræʃ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being too bold, confident, or showy in a way that is rude, offensive, or lacking caution
