drop press

noun
machine for shaping large metal forgings
Pronunciation: / drɑp prɛs / (AmE), / drɒp prɛs / (BrE)
a machine used in metalworking to shape large forgings by dropping a heavy weight onto the material

Examples

A drop press machine
A skilled operator is required to ensure the drop press works safely and efficiently.
Modern factories often use a hydraulic drop press to manufacture engine parts.
The heavy drop press thundered as it forged the metal into its new shape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drop hammer - a similar machine that shapes metal by dropping a weight
