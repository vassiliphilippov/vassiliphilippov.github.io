preset

verb
to set in advance
3.9 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / priËËˆsÉ›t / (AmE), / ËˆpriËsÉ›t / (BrE)

Examples

To preset the timer

Before leaving, he preset the thermostat to turn on the heat at 6 a.m.
You can preset your coffee machine to start brewing automatically in the morning.
