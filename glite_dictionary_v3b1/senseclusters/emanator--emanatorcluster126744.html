emanator

noun
device that emits
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɛm.əˌneɪ.tər / (AmE), / ˈɛm.əˌneɪ.tə / (BrE)
a device or source that emits or gives off something, such as radiation, gas, or particles

Examples

Radiation emanator
The scientist adjusted the emanator to control the release of radioactive particles.
A new type of emanator was installed to disperse fragrance throughout the building.
Technicians monitored the emanator to ensure it was functioning safely during the experiment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: absorber - a device or material that takes in or absorbs something
  • synonym: emitter - a device or source that sends out something
