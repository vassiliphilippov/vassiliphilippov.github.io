hooray

interjection
exclamation of joy
Pronunciation: / hʊˈreɪ / (AmE), / hʊˈreɪ / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express excitement, joy, or approval

Examples

Hooray for you
When the final exam was canceled, the students all shouted hooray in relief.
The crowd erupted with a loud hooray as the fireworks lit up the sky.
After hearing the good news, she jumped up and exclaimed hooray.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boo - an exclamation expressing disapproval or dislike
  • synonym: hurrah - an exclamation of joy or approval
  • synonym: yay - an exclamation expressing happiness or triumph
