numerously

adverb
in large numbers
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːmərəsli / (AmE), / ˈnjuːmərəsli / (BrE)
in large numbers; in a way that involves many individuals or items

Examples

Awards numerously given
The awards were numerously distributed among the talented students at the ceremony.
Birds arrived numerously in the early morning, filling the trees with their songs.
Volunteers numerously signed up to help with the community clean-up event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abundantly - in large quantities; plentifully
  • antonym: sparsely - in small numbers or amounts; thinly scattered
