bring

verb
submit a case or charges in court
1.0 6.1
Pronunciation: /brɪŋ/ (AmE), /brɪŋ/ (BrE)
to advance or set forth (a formal action, such as charges or a case) in a court of law

Examples

To bring a case

The company will bring a lawsuit against its former employee for breach of contract.
The prosecutor decided to bring charges against the defendant after reviewing the evidence.
