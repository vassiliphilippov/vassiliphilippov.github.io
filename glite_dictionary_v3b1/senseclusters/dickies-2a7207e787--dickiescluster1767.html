Dickies

noun
false shirt-fronts
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪkiz / (AmE), / ˈdɪkiz / (BrE)
detachable pieces of fabric worn under a jacket or dress to give the appearance of a full shirt or blouse

Examples

A pair of Dickies under a jacket

Vintage fashion enthusiasts sometimes use Dickies to achieve a classic layered look.
The tailor recommended Dickies for a formal appearance without the bulk of a full shirt.
