confirmational

adjective
relating to Confirmation
8.3
Pronunciation: / kənˌfɜːrˈmeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / kənˌfɜːmˈeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the Christian rite of Confirmation or the ceremony of formally admitting someone to full church membership

Examples

Confirmational ceremony

The bishop delivered a special confirmational message to the new members.
