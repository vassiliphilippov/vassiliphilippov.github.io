maddening

adjective
extremely annoying or irritating
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmædənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmadənɪŋ / (BrE)
extremely annoying, irritating, or difficult to endure

Examples

A maddening habit

It was maddening trying to solve the puzzle with missing pieces.
The constant noise from the construction site was simply maddening.
