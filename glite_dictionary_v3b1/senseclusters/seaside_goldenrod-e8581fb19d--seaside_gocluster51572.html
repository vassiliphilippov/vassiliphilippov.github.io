seaside goldenrod

noun
showy coastal goldenrod species
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːˌsaɪd ˈɡoʊldənˌrɑd / (AmE), / ˈsiːsʌɪd ˈɡəʊldənˌrɒd / (BrE)
a strong, showy species of goldenrod (Solidago sempervirens) native to coastal areas of eastern and Gulf Coast North America

Examples

The seaside goldenrod plant
Birds and butterflies are often attracted to the seaside goldenrod in coastal gardens.
In late summer, the seaside goldenrod blooms with bright yellow flowers along the dunes.
The seaside goldenrod is tolerant of salty, sandy soils near the ocean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goldenrod - any plant of the genus Solidago, with clusters of small yellow flowers
