resuscitated

adjective
restored to life or consciousness
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsʌsɪˌteɪtɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈsʌsɪˌteɪtɪd / (BrE)
Restored to life or consciousness, especially after seeming dead or unconscious.

Examples
