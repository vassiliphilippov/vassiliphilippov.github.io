gymnastically

adverb
with gymnastic skill
Pronunciation: / dʒɪmˈnæstɪkli / (AmE), / dʒɪmˈnæstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that shows or relates to gymnastic skill or movement

Examples

To move gymnastically
The dancer moved gymnastically across the stage, impressing the audience with her flexibility.
He flipped gymnastically over the bar during the competition.
Children played gymnastically on the playground, swinging and tumbling with energy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: athletically - in a way that shows physical strength or skill
