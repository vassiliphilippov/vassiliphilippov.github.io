desalter

noun
salt-removal device
Pronunciation: / diːˈsɔltər / (AmE), / diːˈsɔːltə / (BrE)
a machine or device that removes salt from a substance, especially from crude oil or water

Examples

Oil desalter
The refinery installed a new desalter to improve oil quality.
A desalter is essential for removing salt from crude oil before further processing.
Modern water treatment plants often use a desalter to make water safe for use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filter - a device that removes unwanted material from a substance
  • synonym: purifier - a device that removes impurities from a substance
