Chrysophyllum

noun
genus of tropical evergreen trees or shrubs
Pronunciation: / ˌkraɪsoʊˈfɪləm /; / ˌkraɪsəˈfɪləm / (AmE), / ˌkrɪsəʊˈfɪləm /; / ˌkrɪsəˈfɪləm / (BrE)
a genus of tropical American evergreen trees or shrubs, some of which produce edible fruit such as the star apple
