pom-pom

noun
decorative ball of tufted material
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑm pɑm / (AmE), / ˈpɒm pɒm / (BrE)
a small ball made of tufted wool, silk, or other fabric, used as a decorative ornament, especially on hats or clothing

Examples

Cheerleader pom-pom
Many cheerleaders wave large, colorful pom-poms during their routines.
She sewed a blue pom-pom onto her scarf for a playful touch.
The child's winter hat was topped with a bright red pom-pom.
