retroreflector

noun
signal-reflecting device
8.0
Pronunciation: /re.troʊ.rɪ.ˈflek.tər/ (AmE), /re.trəʊ.rɪ.ˈflek.tə/ (BrE)
a device that reflects light or other signals back toward the direction from which they came

Examples

A retroreflector device
The surveyor placed a retroreflector on the target to help measure distances accurately.
Bicycle pedals often have a small retroreflector to increase visibility at night.
Scientists installed a retroreflector on the lunar surface during the Apollo missions.
