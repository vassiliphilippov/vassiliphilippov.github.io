moodily

adverb
in a moody or gloomy manner
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːdəli / (AmE), / ˈmuːdɪli / (BrE)
In a way that expresses a mood, especially one that is gloomy, sullen, or reflective.

Examples

To walk moodily
He walked moodily down the empty street after the argument.
She stared moodily out the window, lost in her thoughts.
The sky darkened as the clouds gathered moodily over the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a cheerful manner
  • synonym: sullenly - in a sullen or sulky manner
