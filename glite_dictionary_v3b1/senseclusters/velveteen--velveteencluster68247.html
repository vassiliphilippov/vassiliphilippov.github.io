velveteen

noun
cotton fabric imitating velvet
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛlvəˈtin / (AmE), / ˌvɛlvɪˈtiːn / (BrE)
a usually cotton fabric with a short, dense pile, made to imitate velvet

Examples

A velveteen jacket
A jacket made of velveteen offers a luxurious look at an affordable price.
She wore a beautiful velveteen dress to the party.
The upholsterer recommended velveteen for its durability and soft texture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: velvet - a soft, plush fabric with a thick, even pile
