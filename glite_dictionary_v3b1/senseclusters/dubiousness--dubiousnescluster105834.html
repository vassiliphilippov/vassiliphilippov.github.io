dubiousness

noun
state of being doubtful or uncertain
7.3 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈduː.biː.əs.nəs/ /; / /ˈdjuː.biː.əs.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈdjuː.biː.əs.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality or state of being uncertain, doubtful, or suspicious about something
