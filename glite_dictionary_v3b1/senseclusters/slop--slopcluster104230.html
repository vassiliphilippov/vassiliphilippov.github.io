slop

verb
serve or ladle carelessly
7.4 4.0
Pronunciation: /slɑːp/ (AmE), /slɒp/ (BrE)
to spill, pour, or serve liquid or semi-liquid food in a careless or messy way

Examples

To slop soup
He slopped milk all over the counter while pouring it into the glass.
She slopped the soup into my bowl, spilling some onto the table.
The server carelessly slopped the stew onto the plate, making a mess.
Be careful not to slop coffee all over the table.
If you slop paint on the rug, it will be hard to clean.
She accidentally slopped water onto the floor while filling the pot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spill - to cause or allow liquid to flow over the edge unintentionally
  • synonym: splash - to scatter liquid in drops or small quantities
