bob

noun
short, straight-across haircut
Pronunciation: / bɑb / (AmE), / bɒb / (BrE)
A hairstyle for women and children in which the hair is cut straight around the head at about jaw-level.

Examples

A bob haircut
Her daughter’s hair is cut in a neat bob, just above her shoulders.
She decided to get a bob for a fresh, modern look.
The classic bob became popular in the 1920s and remains stylish today.
