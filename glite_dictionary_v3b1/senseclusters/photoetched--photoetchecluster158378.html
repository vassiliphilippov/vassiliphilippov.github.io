photoetched

adjective
made by photographic etching
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊtoʊˌɛtʃt / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊˌɛtʃt / (BrE)
made or decorated by using a photographic process to etch a surface, especially metal or glass

Examples

Many modern circuit boards are photoetched for precise and detailed patterns.
