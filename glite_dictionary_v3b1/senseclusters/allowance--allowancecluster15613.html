allowance

noun
a sum or amount regularly given
3.7
Pronunciation: / əˈlaʊəns / (AmE), / əˈlaʊəns / (BrE)
an amount of money or quantity given regularly or for a specific purpose

Examples

A weekly allowance
Each member of the team has a monthly allowance for office supplies.
She receives a weekly allowance from her parents for doing chores around the house.
The company provides a travel allowance to cover accommodation and meals during business trips.
His parents give him a weekly allowance for his chores.
She receives a monthly allowance to cover her travel expenses.
The company offers an allowance for meals during business trips.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deduction - an amount taken away, often from a payment
  • synonym: grant - a sum of money given by an organization for a particular purpose
  • synonym: salary - a fixed regular payment, typically paid on a monthly basis
  • synonym: stipend - a fixed regular sum paid as a salary or allowance
