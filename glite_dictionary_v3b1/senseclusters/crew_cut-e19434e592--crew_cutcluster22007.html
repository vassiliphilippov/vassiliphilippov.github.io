crew cut

noun
very short men's haircut
Pronunciation: / ˈkru ˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈkruː ˌkʌt / (BrE)
A very short men's haircut in which the hair is cropped close to the scalp, typically the same length all over.

Examples

A crew cut hairstyle
Many athletes prefer a crew cut because it is easy to maintain.
My grandfather always wore a crew cut throughout his life.
When he joined the army, Tom got a crew cut for the first time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buzz cut - another very short haircut, usually shaved even closer than a crew cut
