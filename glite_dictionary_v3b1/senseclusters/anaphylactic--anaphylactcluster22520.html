anaphylactic

adjective
relating to severe allergic reaction (anaphylaxis)
7.5 7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌænəfəˈlæktɪk / (AmE), / ˌanəfəˈlæktɪk / (BrE)
relating to or caused by anaphylaxis, a severe and potentially life-threatening allergic reaction.

Examples

Anaphylactic shock
Doctors carry adrenaline injections for emergencies like anaphylactic shock.
She was rushed to the hospital after experiencing an anaphylactic reaction to peanuts.
The nurse quickly recognized the symptoms of an anaphylactic episode and acted promptly.
