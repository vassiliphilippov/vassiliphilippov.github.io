unquestioning

adjective
accepting without question
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ /; / ˌʌnˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ /; / ˌʌnˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ / (BrE)
accepting something without doubt or hesitation; not expressing doubt or uncertainty

Examples

Unquestioning support
He gave his unquestioning support to the leader, never expressing any doubt.
Her unquestioning loyalty impressed everyone in the company.
The child's unquestioning acceptance of the rules made things much easier.
Children often have an unquestioning faith in their parents' decisions.
He followed his mentor's advice with unquestioning obedience, never doubting the wisdom behind it.
Her unquestioning loyalty to the cause inspired everyone around her.

Related Terms

  • antonym: doubtful - feeling or showing uncertainty or lack of conviction
  • synonym: implicit - implied or understood though not directly stated; unquestioning
  • antonym: questioning - having or showing doubt or reluctance to accept something
  • antonym: skeptical - having doubts or reservations
