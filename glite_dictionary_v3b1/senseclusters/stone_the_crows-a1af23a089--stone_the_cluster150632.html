stone the crows

interjection
exclamation of surprise or disbelief
Pronunciation: / stoʊn ðə kroʊz / (AmE), / stəʊn ðə krəʊz / (BrE)
An exclamation used to express surprise, disbelief, or astonishment.

Examples

Stone the crows!

Stone the crows, I never thought I’d see the day he arrived on time!
