servilely

adverb
in an excessively submissive manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːr.vɪl.li / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.vaɪl.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows excessive willingness to serve or please others; in an obsequious manner.

Examples

To bow servilely
He bowed servilely before the prince, eager for approval.
She servilely agreed to all of her supervisor's unreasonable requests.
They acted servilely in the hope of getting a promotion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: independently - in a self-reliant or autonomous manner
  • synonym: obsequiously - in an overly obedient or submissive manner
