parastatal

adjective
state-like but unofficial
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈsteɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈsteɪtəl / (BrE)
(of an organization) owned, controlled, or operated by the government, but not an official part of the government

Examples

Parastatal organization
The security of the area was managed by a parastatal organization with close ties to the authorities.
Several parastatal agencies were created to oversee the new infrastructure projects.
Critics argued that the parastatal group operated without proper oversight or accountability.
The government appointed a new director to oversee the parastatal agency responsible for water supply.
Many parastatal companies operate in sectors like energy, transport, and telecommunications.
Critics argue that parastatal organizations can sometimes be less efficient than private firms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: governmental - relating to or belonging to the government
  • antonym: private - not owned or controlled by the government
  • synonym: quasi-governmental - having some but not all features of a government body
  • synonym: state-owned - owned by the government
