chaplet

noun
flower or foliage wreath for the head
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæplət / (AmE), / ˈtʃæplɪt / (BrE)
a garland or wreath made of flowers or foliage, worn on the head for ornament or as a mark of honor

Examples

A chaplet of flowers
A delicate chaplet of daisies crowned her hair at the festival.
Each dancer wore a chaplet crafted from ivy leaves and tiny white blossoms.
The bride completed her look with a handmade chaplet of roses and baby's breath.
