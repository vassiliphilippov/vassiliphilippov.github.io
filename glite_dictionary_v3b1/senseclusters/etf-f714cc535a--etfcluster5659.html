ETF

noun
exchange-traded investment fund
7.4 1.33/m
Pronunciation: / i ti …õf / (AmE), / iÀê tiÀê …õf / (BrE)
an investment fund traded on stock exchanges, similar to a mutual fund but with shares that can be bought and sold throughout the day

Examples

An ETF fund
Many investors choose ETFs for their low fees and liquidity.
The manager recommended diversifying my portfolio with an emerging markets ETF.
You can buy and sell ETFs on the stock market just like regular shares.

Synonyms

  • exchange traded fund
    6.1 0.20/m
    an exchange traded fund usually tracks an index and people buy and sell it like a stock during the day, while ETF is the short acronym investors use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mutual fund - an investment program funded by shareholders that trades in diversified holdings
