Synonyms

  • round off
    3.3 0.1/m
    round off reduces detail by changing numbers to the nearest whole or number ending in zero, while round adjusts numbers generally.
  • round out
    3.8 0.04/m
    round out expresses numbers to the nearest ten, hundred, or whole to make totals neater, while round refers to general numerical adjustment.
