Anatolia

proper noun
peninsula, Asian part of Turkey
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈtoʊliə / (AmE), / ˌanəˈtəʊlɪə / (BrE)
a large peninsula in southwestern Asia forming the Asian part of Turkey, historically significant as a crossroads of civilizations

Examples
