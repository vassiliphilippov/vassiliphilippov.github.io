lip-read

verb
understand speech by reading lips
1.0 7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪpˌrid / (AmE), / ˈlɪpˌriːd / (BrE)
to understand spoken words by visually interpreting the movements of a speaker's lips, especially when hearing is impaired or impossible

Examples

To lip-read words
He can lip-read very well in noisy environments.
She learned to lip-read after losing her hearing.
Watching the speaker closely, she managed to lip-read the key information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lipreading - the act or ability of understanding speech by visually interpreting lip movements
