patrol

noun
group performing security checks
5.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈtroʊl/ (AmE), /pə.ˈtrəʊl/ (BrE)
a group of people or vehicles assigned to move around an area to keep it safe or gather information

Examples

Border patrol

A police patrol regularly drives through the neighborhood to ensure safety.
The border patrol discovered several unauthorized crossings during the night.
