patrol

noun
group performing security checks
5.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈtroʊl / (AmE), / pəˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
a group of people or vehicles assigned to move around an area to keep it safe or gather information

Examples

Border patrol
A border patrol was dispatched to monitor the national frontier.
A police patrol drives through the neighborhood every hour to deter crime.
The security patrol reported no unusual activity during their rounds last night.
A patrol was sent out at dawn to survey the area for threats.
A police patrol regularly drives through the neighborhood to ensure safety.
The border patrol discovered several unauthorized crossings during the night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guard - a person or group that protects a place or people
  • synonym: sentry - a soldier stationed to keep watch
  • synonym: squad - a small organized group of soldiers, often for a specific task
