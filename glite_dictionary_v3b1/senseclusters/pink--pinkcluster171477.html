pink

verb
(of an engine) to knock or rattle
9.7
Pronunciation: / pɪŋk / (AmE), / pɪŋk / (BrE)
(of a car engine) to make a knocking or rattling sound caused by premature ignition of the fuel mixture.

Examples

The engine pinks
After adjusting the timing, the engine no longer pinked on uphill climbs.
If you use the wrong fuel, your car might pink when you accelerate.
It's important to tune the engine properly so it doesn't pink under load.
