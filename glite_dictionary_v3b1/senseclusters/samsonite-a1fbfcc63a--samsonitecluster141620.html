Samsonite

noun
brand of suitcase
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmsəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsæmsəˌnaɪt / (BrE)
a suitcase or piece of luggage made by the Samsonite company, or informally, any similar hard-sided suitcase
