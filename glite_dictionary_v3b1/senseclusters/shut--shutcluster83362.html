shut

verb
(of a door/window) become closed
Pronunciation: / ʃʌt / (AmE), / ʃʌt / (BrE)
to close something so that it is not open (such as a door, window, eyes, etc.)

Examples

To shut the door
As the bell rang, the elevator doors shut automatically.
If you don't hold the gate, it will shut on its own.
The door began to shut slowly when the wind picked up.
He shut his eyes for a moment to rest.
Please remember to shut the door quietly when you leave.
She shut the window because it was starting to rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: close - to move so as to block an opening or passage
  • antonym: open - (of a door, etc.) to become unfastened or no longer closed
