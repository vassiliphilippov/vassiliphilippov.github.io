black swan event

noun
unpredictable event with major impact
5.9 6.8
Pronunciation: /blæk swɑːn ɪ.ˈvent/ /; / /blæk swɔːn ɪ.ˈvent/ /; / /blæk swɑːn iː.ˈvent/ (AmE), /blæk swɒn ɪ.ˈvent/ (BrE)
An unpredictable or unforeseen event, typically one with extreme or wide-reaching consequences.

Examples

A rare black swan event
Many experts strive to predict the next black swan event, but by definition, they are nearly impossible to foresee.
The 2008 financial crisis is often cited as a black swan event that caught investors by surprise.
The emergence of the internet was a technological black swan event that transformed industries worldwide.
