unprofitability

noun
lack of profitability
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌprɑːfɪtəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌprɒfɪtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the state or condition of not making a profit or not yielding benefit or gain
