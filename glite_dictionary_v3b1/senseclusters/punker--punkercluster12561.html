punker

noun
petty criminal youth
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌŋkər / (AmE), / ˈpʌŋkə / (BrE)
a young person, often seen as rebellious or involved in petty crime or violence

Examples

A group of punkers
The police arrested a group of punkers who had been vandalizing cars in the area.
Many residents are concerned about punkers causing trouble in the neighborhood at night.
He used to hang out with punkers before turning his life around.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delinquent - a young person who regularly does illegal or immoral things
  • antonym: law-abider - a person who obeys the law
  • synonym: thug - a violent or criminal person
