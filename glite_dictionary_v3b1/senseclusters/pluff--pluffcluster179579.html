pluff

noun
soft mass of fibers
9.9
Pronunciation: /plʌf/ (AmE), /plʌf/ (BrE)
a soft, loose mass of material, especially cotton, lint, or similar fibers

Examples

A pluff of cotton
She brushed a pluff of cotton from her sleeve before leaving the house.
A small pluff of lint collected in the corner of the drawer.
The kitten played with a pluff of wool that had fallen on the floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lint - small fibers or pieces of thread that come off fabric
