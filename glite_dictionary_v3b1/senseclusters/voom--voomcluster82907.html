voom

interjection
sound of speed
9.9
Pronunciation: / vuːm / (AmE), / vuːm / (BrE)
an exclamation imitating the sound of something moving very fast, often used to express excitement about speed

Examples

Voom of a race car
With a loud voom, the race car zoomed past the cheering crowd.
The children shouted voom as they pushed their toy trucks across the playground.
He made a voom sound to show how quickly the rocket would fly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vroom - an exclamation imitating the sound of a revving engine
