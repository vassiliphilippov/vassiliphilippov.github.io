seasoned salt

noun
salt mixed with herbs and spices
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːzənd sɔlt / (AmE), / ˈsiːzənd sɒlt / (BrE)
a type of table salt mixed with herbs, spices, and other flavoring ingredients, often used to enhance the taste of food

Examples

A teaspoon of seasoned salt
My recipe calls for a teaspoon of seasoned salt instead of regular salt.
She sprinkled seasoned salt on the fries for extra flavor.
You can make your own seasoned salt at home using garlic powder and paprika.
