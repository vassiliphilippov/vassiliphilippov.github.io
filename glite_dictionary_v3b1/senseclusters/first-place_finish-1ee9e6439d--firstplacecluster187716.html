first-place finish

noun
winning a competition or race
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝst ˌpleɪs ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˌpleɪs ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
an achievement of finishing in first place in a competition, race, or contest

Examples

A first-place finish celebration
After months of hard training, she celebrated her first-place finish at the marathon.
His incredible performance led to a surprise first-place finish in the science fair.
Their team secured a first-place finish in the national championships last year.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: second-place finish - finishing in second place in a competition
  • synonym: victory - winning in a contest or competition
