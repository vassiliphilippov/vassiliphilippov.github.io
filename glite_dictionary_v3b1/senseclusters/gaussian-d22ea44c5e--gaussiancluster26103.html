Gaussian

adjective
relating to Carl Gauss or his theories
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaʊsiən / (AmE), / ˈɡaʊziən / (BrE)
Of or relating to the German mathematician Carl Friedrich Gauss or his mathematical theories, especially in fields such as probability, magnetics, electricity, or astronomy.

Examples

Gaussian theory
A Gaussian curve is often used to represent the distribution of test scores.
Many statistical models assume a Gaussian distribution of errors.
The noise in the image was modeled as Gaussian to simplify the analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: normal - relating to the normal (Gaussian) distribution in statistics
