Batrachia

noun
group of amphibians: frogs and toads
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈtreɪkiə / (AmE), / bəˈtreɪkɪə / (BrE)
A group of amphibians that includes frogs, toads, and their close relatives.

Examples

Batrachia group
Frogs and toads, members of Batrachia, can be found on every continent except Antarctica.
Many species within the Batrachia group are known for their vocalizations during mating season.
The evolutionary history of Batrachia has been studied extensively by herpetologists.
