neg

adjective
informal for negative
Pronunciation: / nɛɡ / (AmE), / nɛɡ / (BrE)
informal abbreviation for 'negative', used to indicate a test result or response is negative or not detected

Examples

Neg test result
The doctor said the COVID-19 test was neg, so I can return to work.
Her allergy results came back neg for all common triggers.
If the blood sample is neg, no further treatment is needed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: negative - showing that something is not present or not true
  • antonym: pos - informal abbreviation for 'positive', indicating a positive result
