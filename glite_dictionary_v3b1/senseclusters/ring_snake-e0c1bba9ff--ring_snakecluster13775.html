ring snake

noun
small North American snake with a neck ring
2.6
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ sneɪk / (AmE), / rɪŋ sneɪk / (BrE)
a harmless, nonvenomous snake with a bright ring or collar around its neck, found in North America or Europe

The ring snake is often spotted near ponds and slow-moving rivers in rural England.
