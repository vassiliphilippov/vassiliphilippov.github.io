damndest

noun
greatest effort
Pronunciation: / ˈdæmdɪst / (AmE), / ˈdamdɪst / (BrE)
the greatest possible effort that someone can make, used informally to emphasize trying very hard

Examples

Do your damndest
He did his damndest to solve the problem before the deadline.
They tried their damndest to win the championship, but fell just short.
You have to give your damndest if you want to succeed in this business.

Related Terms

  • antonym: minimum - the least or smallest amount possible
  • synonym: utmost - the greatest or most extreme amount or degree
