sky marshal

noun
armed officer on airplanes for security
6.5
Pronunciation: / skaɪ ˈmɑrʃəl / (AmE), / skaɪ ˈmɑːʃəl / (BrE)
A special law enforcement officer who travels on commercial airline flights to protect against hijackings and other threats.

Examples

A sky marshal on flight
A sky marshal was placed on the flight after authorities received a security threat.
During the investigation, the sky marshal discreetly monitored passenger behavior.
The presence of a sky marshal helps deter potential hijackers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: air marshal - a federal law enforcement officer on aircraft to prevent hijackings
