chain reaction

noun
series of linked chemical or nuclear reactions
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪn riˈækʃən / (AmE), / tʃeɪn riˈækʃən / (BrE)
A series of chemical or nuclear reactions in which the product of one reaction initiates the next in the sequence.

Examples

Chemical chain reaction
Polymerization often proceeds through a chain reaction mechanism in which active sites propagate the process.
Scientists carefully control conditions in reactors to maintain a safe chain reaction.
The nuclear explosion was the result of a self-sustaining chain reaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domino effect - a sequence of events where one event triggers the next
