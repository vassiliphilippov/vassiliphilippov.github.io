bacterize

verb
treat with bacteria
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbæk.təˌraɪz /; / ˈbæktəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈbæk.tə.raɪz /; / ˈbæktəˌraɪz / (BrE)
to subject (a substance or material) to the action or effect of bacteria, often in order to induce a chemical or biological change

Examples
