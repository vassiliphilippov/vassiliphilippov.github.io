gainer

noun
high-calorie weight supplement
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈgeɪ.nər/ (AmE), /ˈgeɪ.nə/ (BrE)
A high-calorie dietary supplement (usually a powder or ready-made shake) designed to help increase body weight and muscle mass.

Examples

A weight gainer
She started using a weight gainer to bulk up for the season.
Nutritionists warned him to choose a high-quality gainer rather than cheap, sugar-filled alternatives.
The gym sells several types of gainers, some designed for strength athletes and others for casual users.
