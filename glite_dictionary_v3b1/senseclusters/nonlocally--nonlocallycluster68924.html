nonlocally

adverb
at a distance
8.5
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈloʊkəli / (AmE), / nɒnˈləʊkəli / (BrE)
in a way that is not limited to a specific location, often involving action or influence at a distance

Examples

Act nonlocally in physics
In quantum physics, particles can interact nonlocally even when separated by great distances.
The system was designed to process data nonlocally across multiple servers.
Some theories suggest that information can be transferred nonlocally without a direct physical connection.

Related Terms

  • antonym: locally - in a way that is limited to a specific place
