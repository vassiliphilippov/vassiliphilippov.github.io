computer paper

noun
perforated continuous paper for computer printers
1.7
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpjuːtɚ ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
Continuous paper, usually with perforated edges and holes for feeding, used in computer printers such as dot-matrix printers.

Examples

Box of computer paper
She had to buy a box of computer paper for the dot-matrix printer.
Stacks of unused computer paper were found in the storage room.
The old office still uses computer paper with perforated sides for its reports.
