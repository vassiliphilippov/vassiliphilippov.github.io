computer paper

noun
perforated continuous paper for computer printers
1.7 2.0
Pronunciation: /kəm.ˈpjuː.tər ˈpeɪ.pər/ (AmE), /kəm.ˈpjuː.tə ˈpeɪ.pə/ (BrE)
