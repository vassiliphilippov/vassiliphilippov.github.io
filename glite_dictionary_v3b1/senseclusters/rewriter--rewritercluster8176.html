rewriter

noun
automated paraphrasing program
6.1
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈraɪ.tər/ /; / /rɪ.ˈraɪ.tər/ (AmE), /riː.ˈraɪ.tə/ /; / /rɪ.ˈraɪ.tə/ (BrE)
