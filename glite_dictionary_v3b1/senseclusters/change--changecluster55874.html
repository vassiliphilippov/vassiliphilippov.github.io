change

verb
(of voice) become deeper in tone
1.0
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
(of a person's voice) to become deeper in tone, especially during puberty.

Examples

A boy's voice changes
During adolescence, many boys notice that their voices change and become lower.
Some teenagers feel self-conscious as their voices change during puberty.
When he turned thirteen, his voice began to change unexpectedly.
