stickly

adjective
adhesive or gummy
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkli / (AmE), / ˈstɪkli / (BrE)
having a tendency to stick or be adhesive, often with a gummy or tacky texture

Examples

Stickly label
The label was so stickly that I couldn't peel it off without tearing the paper.
After spilling the soda, the floor became unpleasantly stickly underfoot.
She avoided touching the stickly residue left on the table after the craft project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: slippery - difficult to hold or stand on because of a smooth or wet surface
  • synonym: sticky - having the property of adhering or sticking to surfaces
