NEET

adjective
not in work or study
9.0
Pronunciation: / niːt / (AmE), / niːt / (BrE)
not in education, employment, or training; used to describe people, especially young adults, who are not engaged in these activities.

Examples

NEET status
The government launched a program to help NEET youth find jobs or return to education.
Many NEET individuals face challenges with social isolation and financial independence.
The survey revealed a rising number of NEET young adults in urban areas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: employed - having a paid job
  • antonym: student - a person who is studying at a school or college
  • synonym: unemployed - not having a paid job
