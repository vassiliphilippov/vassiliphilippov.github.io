unfavorableness

noun
quality of being unfavorable
3.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfeɪvərəbəlnəs /; / ˌʌnˈfeɪvərəblnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪvərəblnəs /; / ˌʌnˈfeɪvərəblnəs / (BrE)
the quality or condition of not being favorable, encouraging, or indicative of success

Examples

The unfavourableness of the weather forced them to cancel the picnic.
