para-legal work

noun
legal tasks by a non-lawyer
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈliːgəl wɜːrk / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈliːgəl wɜːk / (BrE)
legal tasks or activities carried out by someone who is not a qualified lawyer

Examples

Para-legal work tasks

The office hired an assistant to handle routine para-legal work and support the attorneys.
