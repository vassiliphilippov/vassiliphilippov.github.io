fresh-cut

adjective
recently cut or harvested
5.3
Pronunciation: / frɛʃ kʌt / (AmE), / frɛʃ kʌt / (BrE)
(of plants, flowers, vegetables, etc.) cut or harvested very recently and therefore still fresh.

Examples

Fresh-cut flowers
She placed the fresh-cut flowers in a vase on the kitchen table.
The store was advertising fresh-cut fruit salads for customers in a hurry.
You can smell the scent of fresh-cut grass throughout the neighborhood after mowing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fresh - recently made, obtained, or arrived; not stale or spoiled
