Alstonia

noun
genus of tropical trees or shrubs
Pronunciation: / ælˈstoʊniə / (AmE), / ælˈstəʊniə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical evergreen trees or shrubs with white funnel-shaped flowers and milky sap, native to Africa, southeastern Asia, and Polynesia
