suede cloth

noun
fabric resembling suede leather
Pronunciation: / sweɪd klɔθ / (AmE), / sweɪd klɒθ / (BrE)
a soft fabric made to imitate the appearance and texture of suede leather

Examples

Soft suede cloth
Her handbag looked like real suede, but it was actually suede cloth.
Many furniture covers use suede cloth for its soft texture and easy maintenance.
The jacket is made from suede cloth to give it a luxurious feel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suede - a type of soft leather with a napped surface
