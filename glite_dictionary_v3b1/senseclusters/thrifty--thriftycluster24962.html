thrifty

adjective
careful with money and resources
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɪfti / (AmE), / ˈθrɪfti / (BrE)
careful and economical in the use of money or resources; not wasteful

Examples

A thrifty person
Choosing a thrifty lifestyle helped them pay off their debt.
His thrifty habits allowed him to retire early.
She is very thrifty and always finds ways to save on groceries.
A thrifty shopper compares prices before making a purchase.
His thrifty habits helped him save enough money for a new car.
She has always been thrifty, looking for bargains at every opportunity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extravagant - spending or using resources freely and recklessly
  • synonym: frugal - using money or resources very carefully and sparingly
  • antonym: wasteful - using money or resources without care or restraint
