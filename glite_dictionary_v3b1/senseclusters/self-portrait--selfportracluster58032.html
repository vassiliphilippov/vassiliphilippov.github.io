self-portrait

noun
a portrait of oneself by oneself
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈpɔrtrɪt / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈpɔːtrɪt / (BrE)
A painting, drawing, photograph, or other representation of oneself created by oneself.

Examples

A self-portrait painting
He painted a self-portrait every year to document his changing style.
Her latest self-portrait captures a quiet moment of reflection.
The museum displayed a famous self-portrait by the artist.
