truthteller

noun
person who tells truth
Pronunciation: / ˈtruːθˌtɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˈtruːθˌtɛlə / (BrE)
a person who always tells the truth, even when it is difficult or unpopular

Examples

A truthteller always honest
Everyone respected her as a truthteller who spoke honestly, even when it was uncomfortable.
Being a truthteller can sometimes make you unpopular, but it builds trust over time.
He was known as a truthteller in the community, never afraid to share his real opinions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: honest person - someone who tells the truth and does not lie
  • antonym: liar - a person who does not tell the truth
