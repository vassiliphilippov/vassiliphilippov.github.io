shutoff

noun
device to stop flow
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌtˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtɒf /; / ˈʃʌtˌɒf / (BrE)
a device or action that stops the flow or operation of something, such as water, gas, electricity, or a machine, especially for safety or control

Examples

In case of emergency, you should know where the gas shutoff is in your house.

In case of emergency, press the shutoff button to stop the machine immediately.
The automatic shutoff engaged when the engine reached a dangerous temperature.
