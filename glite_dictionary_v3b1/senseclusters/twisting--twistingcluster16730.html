twisting

noun
distorting or misrepresenting meaning
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtwɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of distorting or misrepresenting something so that it appears to mean something unintended

Examples

Twisting of the truth
Her twisting of the facts made it hard to know what really happened.
Political debates often involve the twisting of opponents' statements to gain an advantage.
The lawyer accused the reporter of twisting his words to create a misleading headline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distortion - the act of twisting or altering something out of its true, natural, or original state
  • synonym: misrepresentation - the action of giving a false or misleading account of something
