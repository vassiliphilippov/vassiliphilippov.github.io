flashily

adverb
in a showy, ostentatious manner
Pronunciation: / ˈflæʃɪli / (AmE), / ˈflæʃɪli / (BrE)
in a showy or ostentatious way, often using bright colors or decorations to attract attention

Examples

To dress flashily
He always accessorizes flashily with large gold chains and rings.
She dressed flashily for the party, wearing sequins and bright colors.
The car was painted flashily, attracting everyone's attention in the parking lot.
She decorated her apartment flashily, using bright colors and sparkling lights everywhere.
The actor arrived flashily dressed at the awards ceremony, attracting everyone's attention.
The sports car was flashily customized, but its style wasn't to everyone's taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flamboyantly - in a flamboyant, showy, or flashy manner
  • synonym: ostentatiously - in a way that is showy or designed to impress
  • antonym: plainly - in a simple, unadorned, or unshowy manner
