Ketalar

noun
proprietary name for ketamine anesthetic
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛtəlɑr / (AmE), / ˈkiːtəˌlɑː / (BrE)
A proprietary name for ketamine hydrochloride, a general anesthetic and tranquilizer used mainly in veterinary medicine or for minor surgery.

Examples

Ketalar injection
During the operation, Ketalar was given intravenously to ensure the patient remained unconscious.
For pediatric procedures, some doctors choose Ketalar because of its rapid effect and safety profile.
The veterinarian administered Ketalar to sedate the injured dog before surgery.
