fawnlike

adjective
pale yellowish-brown
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɔːnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a pale yellowish-brown color or appearance similar to that of a young deer

Examples

Fawnlike color
The artist chose a fawnlike shade for the walls to create a calm atmosphere.
Her dress was a soft, fawnlike color that matched the autumn leaves.
The puppy's fawnlike fur made it look even more adorable.
