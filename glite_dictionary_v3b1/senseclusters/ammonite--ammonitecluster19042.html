ammonite

noun
fossil of a coiled marine mollusk
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæməˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈamənʌɪt / (BrE)
the coiled, chambered fossil shell of an extinct group of marine mollusks, typically found in sedimentary rocks

Examples

A ammonite fossil
She collects ammonite specimens from different geological periods.
The museum displayed a large ammonite fossil found in the cliffs nearby.
The spiral pattern of the ammonite is both intricate and beautiful.
