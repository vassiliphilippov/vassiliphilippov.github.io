assessment

noun
the act of judging or evaluating
1.2
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛsmənt / (AmE), / əˈsɛsmənt / (BrE)
the act of judging or evaluating the quality, value, or importance of someone or something

Examples

Job assessment
After the interview, a thorough assessment of the candidate's skills was conducted.
Teachers use regular assessment to track students' progress.
The manager's assessment of the team's performance was fair and detailed.
A careful assessment of the situation is necessary before making a decision.
After a full assessment of the damaged building, repairs were scheduled.
The teacher's assessment of the students' projects was thorough and fair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appraisal - the act of examining someone or something in order to judge their qualities, success, or needs
  • synonym: evaluation - the process of judging or calculating the quality, importance, amount, or value of something
  • synonym: judgment - the act of forming an opinion or decision after careful thought
