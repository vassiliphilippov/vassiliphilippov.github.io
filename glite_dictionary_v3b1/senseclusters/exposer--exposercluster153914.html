exposer

noun
person who reveals secrets
6.0
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspoʊzər / (AmE), / ɪkˈspəʊzə / (BrE)
a person who reveals something hidden, secret, or dishonest, especially to the public

Examples

Exposer of secrets
The journalist became famous as an exposer of government corruption.
As an exposer of secrets, she often faced criticism from those in power.
