push someone's buttons

verb
provoke a strong emotional reaction
Pronunciation: / pʊʃ ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈbʌtənz /; / pʊʃ ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈbʌtnz / (AmE), / pʊʃ ˈsʌmwənz ˈbʌtənz /; / pʊʃ ˈsʌmwənz ˈbʌtnz / (BrE)
to deliberately do or say something to make someone annoyed or angry

Examples
