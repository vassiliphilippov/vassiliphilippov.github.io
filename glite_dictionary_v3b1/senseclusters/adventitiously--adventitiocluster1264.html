adventitiously

adverb
abnormally or unusually
9.9
Pronunciation: /æd.vən.ˈtɪʃ.əs.liː/ /; / /əd.vən.ˈtɪʃ.əs.liː/ (AmE), /æd.vən.ˈtɪʃ.əs.liː/ /; / /əd.vən.ˈtɪʃ.əs.liː/ (BrE)
in a manner that arises from an unusual or abnormal place, especially in biological or medical contexts.

Examples

Roots grow adventitiously
The plant developed roots adventitiously after its stem was buried in the soil.
Some blood vessels may form adventitiously in response to injury or disease.
In certain species, leaves can grow adventitiously from unexpected parts of the stem.

  • antonym: normally - in a usual or expected way
