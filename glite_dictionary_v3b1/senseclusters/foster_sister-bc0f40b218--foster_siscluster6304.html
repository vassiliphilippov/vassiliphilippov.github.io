foster sister

noun
girl/woman raised by same foster parents
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑstər ˈsɪstər / (AmE), / ˈfɒstə ˈsɪstə / (BrE)
A girl or woman who is not biologically related to you but is raised by your parents as a member of the family.

Examples

My foster sister
Although Anna is my foster sister, we grew up together and are very close.
His foster sister just graduated from college last month.
She shares a bedroom with her foster sister in their new home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foster brother - boy/man raised by same foster parents
  • synonym: stepsister - a sister by the marriage of one's parent to another person
