tokenize

verb
to create digital tokens
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊkəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈtəʊkənaɪz / (BrE)
to convert an asset or right into a digital token on a blockchain so it can be traded or owned in smaller units

Examples

To tokenize real estate
The startup plans to tokenize works of art so more people can invest in them.
By tokenizing real estate, the company allows investors to purchase small shares of properties.
Many financial institutions are exploring ways to tokenize traditional assets for easier trading.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fractionalize - to divide something into smaller parts or shares
