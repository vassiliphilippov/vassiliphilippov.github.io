pitty-pat

adverb
with a quick, light, repeated sound
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtiˌpæt / (AmE), / ˈpɪtiˌpat / (BrE)
with a light, quickly repeated, rhythmic sound, like a heartbeat or light footsteps

Examples

She heard the pitty-pat of raindrops against the window as she fell asleep.
The children came running, their shoes making a pitty-pat along the wooden floor.
Her footsteps sounded pitty-pat down the empty hallway.
I could hear my heart go pitty-pat as I waited for the results.
The rain fell pitty-pat on the rooftop, soothing me to sleep.
