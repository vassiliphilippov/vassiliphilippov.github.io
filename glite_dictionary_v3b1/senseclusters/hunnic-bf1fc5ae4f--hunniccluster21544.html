Hunnic

adjective
of the Huns
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌnɪk / (AmE), / ˈhʌnɪk / (BrE)
relating to the Huns, a nomadic people who lived in Central Asia and Europe during late antiquity

Examples

Hunnic invasions
The Hunnic invasions changed the course of European history.
Archaeologists discovered Hunnic artifacts in the region near the Danube River.
The Hunnic language remains largely undeciphered by modern scholars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Hun - a member of a nomadic people from Central Asia and Europe
