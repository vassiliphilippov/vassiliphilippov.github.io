ultrasensitive

adjective
extremely sensitive
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrəˈsɛnsətɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈsɛnsətɪv / (BrE)
extremely sensitive to very small changes, signals, or influences

Examples

Ultrasensitive microphone
The ultrasensitive microphone can detect even the quietest whispers in the room.
Scientists used an ultrasensitive test to identify the presence of rare chemicals.
Her ultrasensitive skin reacts to even the slightest touch or change in temperature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypersensitive - very easily affected by small changes or stimuli
  • antonym: insensitive - not easily affected by changes or stimuli
