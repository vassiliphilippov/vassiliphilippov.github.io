spelling checker

noun
tool that checks spelling in text
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛlɪŋ ˌtʃɛkər / (AmE), / ˈspɛlɪŋ ˌtʃɛkə / (BrE)
a computer program or tool that checks the spelling in text by identifying words that are not in its dictionary, typically used in word processors

Examples

Use a spelling checker
Before submitting her essay, she used the spelling checker to review her work.
Many people rely on a spelling checker to avoid embarrassing typos in emails.
The word processor's spelling checker caught several mistakes in my document.
