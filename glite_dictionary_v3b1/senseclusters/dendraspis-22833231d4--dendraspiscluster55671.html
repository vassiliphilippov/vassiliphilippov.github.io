Dendraspis

noun
former genus of African mamba snakes
Pronunciation: / dɛnˈdræspɪs / (AmE), / dɛnˈdraspɪs /; / dɛnˈdrɑːspɪs / (BrE)
a genus (or former genus) of highly venomous African snakes known as mambas
