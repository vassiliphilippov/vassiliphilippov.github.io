holding paddock

noun
temporary livestock enclosure
9.2 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊld.ɪŋ ˈpæd.ək/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊld.ɪŋ ˈpæd.ək/ (BrE)
