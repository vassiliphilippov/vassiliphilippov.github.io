Having authority to make and enforce rules, laws, or decisions; exercising control over an organization, country, or group.

Examples

Governing body
Many countries have a governing body that oversees professional sports leagues.
The governing committee decided to update the organization's code of conduct.
The governing officials were responsible for implementing the new policy.

Synonyms

  • ruling
    3.9 0.00/m
    ruling is official control over a country or group, while governing focuses on making and enforcing rules and laws.
  • reigning
    4.9 0.01/m
    reigning describes someone who currently holds a title or power, while governing stresses the act of running and enforcing laws.
  • controlling
    4.2 0.00/m
    controlling means directing or influencing actions, often in business, while governing implies legal authority to make and enforce rules.
