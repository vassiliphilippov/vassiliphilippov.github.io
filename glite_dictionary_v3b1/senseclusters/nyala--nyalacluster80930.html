nyala

noun
spiral-horned African antelope
Pronunciation: / naɪˈɑːlə / (AmE), / naɪˈɑːlə / (BrE)
A spiral-horned antelope native to southern Africa, with a fringe of white hairs along its back and neck.

Examples

A nyala antelope
A male nyala has striking spiral horns and a distinct white fringe along its back.
The nyala is a shy creature often found near water in the forests of southern Africa.
We spotted several nyalas grazing in the reserve during our safari.
