Synonyms

  • rampart
    8.8 0.94/m
    A rampart is a broad earth or stone defensive wall with a top parapet, while a bulwark is any strong protective barrier.
  • bastion
    8.3 0.31/m
    A bastion is a part of a fort that sticks out and defends, while a bulwark is a general strong protective barrier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rampart - a defensive wall of a castle or walled city
