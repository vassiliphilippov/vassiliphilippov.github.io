irrepressibly

adverb
unstoppably
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˈprɛsəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈprɛsəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be controlled, restrained, or held back

Examples

Irrepressibly happy
The children cheered irrepressibly when their team scored the winning goal.
He smiled irrepressibly at the good news, unable to hide his excitement.
Her creativity flowed irrepressibly, inspiring everyone around her.

Related Terms

  • antonym: restrainedly - in a controlled or held-back manner
  • synonym: unstoppably - in a way that cannot be stopped
