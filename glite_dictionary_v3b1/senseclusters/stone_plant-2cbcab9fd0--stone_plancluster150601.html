stone plant

noun
succulent plant resembling a stone (genus Lithops)
Pronunciation: / stoʊn plænt / (AmE), / stəʊn plɑːnt / (BrE)
Any succulent plant of the genus Lithops, native to Africa, with thick leaves resembling stones and solitary yellow or white flowers.

Examples

Stone plant with flowers
I spotted a yellow flower emerging from a stone plant during my trip to Namibia.
Many collectors appreciate the beauty and resilience of the stone plant in their rock gardens.
The unique shape of the stone plant helps it blend in with the surrounding pebbles.
