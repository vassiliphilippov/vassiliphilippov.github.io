Pinus virginiana

noun
a shrubby pine tree native to eastern US
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪ.nəs vɚˌdʒɪn.iˈæn.ə / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ.nəs vɜːˌdʒɪn.iˈæn.ə / (BrE)
