tarry

adjective
resembling or containing tar
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑri / (AmE), / ˈtɑːri / (BrE)
resembling or containing tar; having the characteristics of pitch or tar.

Examples

Tarry residue
His shoes stuck to the tarry surface of the newly sealed driveway.
The air had a strong, tarry smell after the road was repaired.
The workers wore gloves to avoid the tarry residue left by the old roof.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pitchy - resembling pitch; black or sticky like tar or pitch
  • synonym: tar-like - having qualities or appearance similar to tar
