frontis

noun
book's title illustration
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌntɪs / (AmE), / ˈfrʌntɪs / (BrE)
an illustration or picture facing the title page of a book, especially in older publications

Examples

The frontis illustration
The frontis of the old novel featured a detailed engraving of the main character.
Collectors often value books with the original frontis still intact.
A beautiful frontis can make a rare edition even more special to bibliophiles.
