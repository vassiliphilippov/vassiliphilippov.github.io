fa la

noun
nonsense syllables in a song's refrain
Pronunciation: / fɑː ˈlɑː / (AmE), / fɑː ˈlɑː / (BrE)
A series of meaningless syllables used as a refrain, especially in partsongs such as madrigals.

Examples

A fa la refrain
Many English madrigals feature a catchy fa la refrain.
She hummed an old tune, filling the gaps with a light fa la.
The choir joyfully sang the fa la in each chorus.
