aloneness

noun
state or disposition of being alone
Pronunciation: / əˈloʊn.nəs / (AmE), / əˈləʊn.nəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being alone; a disposition or tendency to prefer solitude

Examples

The aloneness feeling
His aloneness was not a burden but a chosen way of life.
She sometimes enjoys her aloneness and finds peace in quiet moments by herself.
The writer valued aloneness as a source of creativity and reflection.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loneliness - sadness because one has no friends or company
  • synonym: solitude - the state of being alone, often by choice
