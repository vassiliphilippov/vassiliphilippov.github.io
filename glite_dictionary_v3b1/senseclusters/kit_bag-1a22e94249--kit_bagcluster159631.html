kit bag

noun
a soldier's bag for personal items
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪt bæg / (AmE), / ˈkɪt bæɡ / (BrE)
a sturdy bag, typically used by soldiers, for carrying personal belongings or equipment

Examples

A kit bag for soldiers
As a new recruit, he carefully packed his kit bag before basic training.
She found an old kit bag in the attic, filled with letters from the war.
The soldier slung his kit bag over his shoulder and marched towards the barracks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backpack - a bag with shoulder straps, carried on the back
  • synonym: duffel bag - a cylindrical canvas bag used for carrying personal belongings
