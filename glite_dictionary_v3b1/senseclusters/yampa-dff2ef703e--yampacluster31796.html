Yampa

proper noun
river in Colorado
Pronunciation: / ˈjæmpə / (AmE), / ˈjæmpə / (BrE)
a river in northwestern Colorado, United States, that flows into the Green River

Yampa River
The Yampa is known for its scenic canyons and whitewater rafting opportunities.
