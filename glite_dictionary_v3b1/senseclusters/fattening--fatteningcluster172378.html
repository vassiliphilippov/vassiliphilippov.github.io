fattening

adjective
causing weight gain
Pronunciation: / ˈfætənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfætənɪŋ / (BrE)
causing a person or animal to gain weight, especially by being high in calories or fat

Examples

Fattening food
Nutritionists warn that fattening snacks can make it harder to maintain a healthy weight.
He avoided fattening desserts while preparing for the competition.
Some people believe that bread is too fattening to eat regularly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caloric - containing a lot of calories
  • antonym: slimming - helping to reduce body weight
