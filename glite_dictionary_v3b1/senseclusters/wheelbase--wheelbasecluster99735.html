wheelbase

noun
distance between front and rear wheels
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlˌbeɪs / (AmE), / ˈwiːlbeɪs / (BrE)
the distance between the centers of the front and rear wheels of a vehicle

Examples

The wheelbase length
Race cars often feature a shorter wheelbase for increased agility on the track.
The wheelbase of the new sedan is longer than its predecessor, providing more interior space.
The designer adjusted the wheelbase to improve the vehicle’s stability and handling.
