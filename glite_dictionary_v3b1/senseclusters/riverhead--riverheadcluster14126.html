riverhead

noun
source of a river
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvərˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈrɪvəˌhɛd / (BrE)
the place where a river begins; the source or starting point of a river

Examples

The riverhead source
The explorers traced the river upstream until they reached the riverhead in the mountains.
Many small streams join together at the riverhead to form the main river.
We camped near the riverhead and enjoyed the fresh, clear water.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mouth - the place where a river flows into another body of water
  • synonym: source - the starting point of a river or stream
