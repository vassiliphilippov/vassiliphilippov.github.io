chef by trade

noun
professional chef by occupation
Pronunciation: / ʃɛf baɪ treɪd / (AmE), / ʃɛf baɪ treɪd / (BrE)
A person who works professionally as a chef, typically with formal training or experience.

Examples

As a chef by trade, Maria takes great pride in every meal she prepares.

Though retired, he remains a chef by trade at heart, always experimenting in the kitchen.
