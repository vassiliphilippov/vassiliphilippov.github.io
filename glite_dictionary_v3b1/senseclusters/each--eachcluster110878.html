each

determiner
every one individually (quantifier)
1.3
Pronunciation: / itʃ / (AmE), / iːtʃ / (BrE)
used to refer to every one of two or more people or things, regarded and identified separately.

Examples

Each student
Each of the rooms has a spectacular view of the city.
Each student received a certificate for completing the course.
Please take one apple each from the basket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: all - the whole quantity or extent of something
  • synonym: every - all considered as a group, without exception
