Anglo-Saxon deity

noun
a god worshipped by the Anglo-Saxons
Pronunciation: / ˌæŋɡloʊ ˈsæksən ˈdiːəti / (AmE), / ˌæŋɡləʊ ˈsæksən ˈdiːɪti / (BrE)
a god or goddess worshipped by the Anglo-Saxons in their religious beliefs and mythology

The mythology course covered the characteristics of each Anglo-Saxon deity in detail.
