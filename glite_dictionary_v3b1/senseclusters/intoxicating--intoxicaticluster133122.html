intoxicating

adjective
extremely exciting or exhilarating
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɑksɪˌkeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɒksɪˌkeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely exciting or exhilarating, as if producing the effects of alcohol or a narcotic.

Examples

Intoxicating excitement
His intoxicating laughter filled the entire room with energy and joy.
The view from the mountaintop was so intoxicating that she could hardly breathe.
There was an intoxicating sense of freedom as they raced along the coastline.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boring - not interesting or exciting
  • synonym: thrilling - causing excitement or exhilaration
