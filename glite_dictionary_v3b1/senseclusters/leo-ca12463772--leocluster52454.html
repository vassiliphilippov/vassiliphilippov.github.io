Leo

noun
person born under Leo (astrology)
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈliː.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈliː.əʊ / (BrE)
A person born between July 23 and August 22, when the sun is in the astrological sign of Leo.

Examples

A Leo person
As a Leo, she is known for her confidence and leadership skills.
Many people believe that being a Leo means you are energetic and outgoing.
My best friend, a Leo, always seems to take charge in any group.
