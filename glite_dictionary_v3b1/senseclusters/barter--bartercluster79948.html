barter

noun
exchange of goods without money
3.5 1.65/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ëÀêrt…ôr / (AmE), / Ààb…ëÀêt…ô / (BrE)
the act or system of exchanging goods or services directly for other goods or services without using money

Examples

A barter system
In ancient times, people would barter instead of using coins or bills.
The villagers relied on barter to obtain food and supplies during the economic crisis.
Without cash, the community turned to barter as the main form of exchange.

Synonyms

  • swap
    2.6 7.96/m
    swap is a simple exchange of items between people, often equal in value, while barter is a wider system of trading without money.

  • synonym: trade - the activity of buying, selling, or exchanging goods and services
