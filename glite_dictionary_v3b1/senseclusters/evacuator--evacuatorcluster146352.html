evacuator

noun
removal device or person
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvækjueɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˈvækjueɪtə / (BrE)
a device or person that removes air, gas, or fluid from a space or area

Examples

Dental evacuator
The dentist used an evacuator to keep the patient's mouth dry during the procedure.
A surgical evacuator quickly removed excess fluids from the wound.
The laboratory technician checked the evacuator to ensure it was functioning properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extractor - a device or person that takes something out
  • synonym: pump - a device used to move liquids or gases
