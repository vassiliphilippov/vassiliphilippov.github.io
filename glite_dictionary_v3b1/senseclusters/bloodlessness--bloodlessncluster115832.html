bloodlessness

noun
lack of blood or emotion
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈblʌdləsnəs / (BrE)
the state of having little or no blood, or lacking color, vitality, or emotion

Examples

Bloodlessness of the face
After the accident, the doctor noticed the bloodlessness of the patient's face.
The speech was criticized for its bloodlessness and lack of passion.
Her bloodlessness made her seem distant and unemotional during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anemia - a medical condition with a deficiency of red blood cells
  • synonym: pallor - an unhealthy pale appearance
  • antonym: vitality - the state of being strong and full of energy
