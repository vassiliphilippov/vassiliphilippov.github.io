baddest

adjective
most bad; worst
Pronunciation: / ˈbædɪst / (AmE), / ˈbædɪst / (BrE)
the superlative form of bad, meaning the most bad, worst, or of the lowest quality; often used informally or nonstandardly

Examples

The baddest storm
That was the baddest storm we have seen in years.
He claimed to be the baddest player on the team, but his performance was disappointing.
This is the baddest movie I have ever watched; the reviews were terrible.

  • synonym: worst - the most bad or undesirable
