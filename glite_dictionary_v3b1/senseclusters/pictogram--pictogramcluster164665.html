pictogram

noun
icon-based chart
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪk.tə.ɡræm/ (AmE), /ˈpɪk.tə.ɡræm/ (BrE)
a diagram or chart that uses repeated small images or icons to represent and compare numerical quantities

Examples

Pictogram chart
The teacher used a pictogram to help students understand the survey results easily.
Each symbol in the pictogram represents five people in the population.
A pictogram can make complex data more accessible to young children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diagram - a simplified drawing showing the appearance or structure of something
  • synonym: icon - a small image or symbol representing an object or idea
  • antonym: text - written words as opposed to images or symbols
