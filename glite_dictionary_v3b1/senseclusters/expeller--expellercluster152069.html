expeller

noun
device that expels
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛlɚ / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛlə / (BrE)
a device or person that forces something out, especially by mechanical means

Examples

Oil expeller
The oil expeller extracts oil from seeds efficiently in many factories.
A modern expeller can process large quantities of raw material quickly.
The technician repaired the expeller to ensure smooth operation of the machine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extractor - a machine or person that removes or takes out something
  • antonym: retainer - a device or person that keeps something in place
