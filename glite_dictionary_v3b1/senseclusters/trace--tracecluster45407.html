trace

noun
strap connecting a horse's harness to a vehicle
Pronunciation: / treɪs / (AmE), / treɪs / (BrE)
either of the two straps, chains, or lines by which a horse is attached to a vehicle and pulls it

Examples

Horse traces
She checked the traces to ensure the horses were securely harnessed before setting off.
The leather traces snapped under the strain of the heavy wagon.
With a quick adjustment to the trace, the driver signaled the team to move forward.
