myriapod

noun
terrestrial arthropod group: centipedes, millipedes
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪriəˌpɑd / (AmE), / ˈmɪriəˌpɒd / (BrE)
any terrestrial arthropod with an elongated body composed of many similar segments, such as centipedes and millipedes

Examples

A myriapod animal
Many myriapods play a vital role in breaking down decaying plant material.
Scientists discovered a new species of myriapod in the rainforest.
The forest floor was alive with myriapods crawling among the damp leaves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centipede - a myriapod with a flattened body and many legs
  • synonym: millipede - a myriapod with a long cylindrical body and two pairs of legs per segment
