dibutyl

adjective
with two butyl groups
9.3
Pronunciation: / daɪˈbjuːtɪl / (AmE), / daɪˈbjuːtɪl / (BrE)
containing or relating to two butyl groups in a molecule, especially in chemical compounds

Examples

Dibutyl ether
The chemist used dibutyl ether as a solvent in the reaction.
A common plasticizer is dibutyl phthalate, which improves flexibility in plastics.
Researchers tested the properties of dibutyl compounds in the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: monobutyl - containing one butyl group
  • degree higher: tributyl - containing three butyl groups
