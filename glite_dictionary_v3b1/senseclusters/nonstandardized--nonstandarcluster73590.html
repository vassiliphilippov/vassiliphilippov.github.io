nonstandardized

adjective
not following standards
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈstændɚdaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈstændədaɪzd / (BrE)
not made to conform to an official or widely accepted standard

Examples

Nonstandardized questionnaire used
The survey used a nonstandardized questionnaire, so the results were hard to compare.
Because the process was nonstandardized, each product turned out slightly different.
Researchers warned that nonstandardized methods could affect the reliability of the findings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irregular - not following a regular pattern or rule
  • antonym: standardized - made to conform to an official or widely accepted standard
