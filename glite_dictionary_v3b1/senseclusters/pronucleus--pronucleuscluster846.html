pronucleus

noun
nucleus of gamete before zygote formation
Pronunciation: / proʊˈnuː.kli.əs / (AmE), / prəʊˈnjuː.kli.əs / (BrE)
the nucleus of a sperm or egg cell during fertilization, before they merge to form the nucleus of a zygote

Examples

The pronucleus of sperm
After fertilization, the sperm pronucleus migrates toward the egg pronucleus for fusion.
Researchers studied the behavior of the male pronucleus using advanced microscopy techniques.
The presence of two pronuclei in the zygote confirms successful fertilization.
