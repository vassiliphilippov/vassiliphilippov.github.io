pronucleus

noun
nucleus of gamete before zygote formation
6.1 8.0
Pronunciation: /proʊ.ˈnuː.kliː.əs/ /; / /proʊ.ˈnjuː.kliː.əs/ (AmE), /prəʊ.ˈnjuː.kliː.əs/ /; / /prəʊ.ˈnuː.kliː.əs/ (BrE)
the nucleus of a sperm or egg cell during fertilization, before they merge to form the nucleus of a zygote
