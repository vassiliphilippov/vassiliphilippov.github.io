speed skate

noun
ice skate for racing
4.0
Pronunciation: / spiːd skeɪt / (AmE), / spiːd skeɪt / (BrE)
An ice skate with a long blade, designed for use in speed skating races.

Examples

A pair of speed skates
He strapped on his speed skates before the race began.
Professional racers use speed skates for their long, straight blades.
The design of a speed skate allows for faster gliding on the ice.
