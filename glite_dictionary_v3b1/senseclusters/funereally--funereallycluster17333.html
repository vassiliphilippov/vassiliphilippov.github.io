funereally

adverb
solemnly or mournfully
8.8
Pronunciation: / fjuˈnɪrəli / (AmE), / fjʊˈnɪərəli / (BrE)
in a manner that is solemn, mournful, or reminiscent of a funeral

Examples

To walk funereally
He walked funereally through the empty halls after hearing the sad news.
The music played funereally as the mourners gathered in the church.
She spoke funereally about the end of an era at the company.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy and positive way
  • synonym: solemnly - in a serious and formal way
