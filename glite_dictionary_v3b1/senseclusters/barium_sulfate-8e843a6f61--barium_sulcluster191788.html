barium sulfate

noun
white insoluble chemical compound used in imaging
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛriəm ˈsʌlˌfeɪt /; / ˈbɛriəm sʌlˌfeɪt /; / ˈbɛriəm səlˈfeɪt / (AmE), / ˈbeəriəm ˈsʌlfeɪt /; / ˈbæriəm ˈsʌlfɛɪt /; / ˈbeəriəm sʌlˈfeɪt / (BrE)
