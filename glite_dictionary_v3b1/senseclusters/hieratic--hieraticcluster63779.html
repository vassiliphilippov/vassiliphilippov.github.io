hieratic

adjective
relating to priests or priesthood
Pronunciation: / haɪ.əˈræt.ɪk / (AmE), / haɪ.əˈræt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to priests or the priesthood; priestly.

Examples

Hieratic priest
His speech was delivered with a hieratic solemnity befitting the occasion.
She wore hieratic robes that emphasized her spiritual authority.
The ceremony involved several hieratic rituals handed down over generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: priestly - relating to priests; characteristic of priests
  • antonym: secular - not connected with religious or spiritual matters
