weaving

noun
fabric or textile art
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwiːvɪŋ / (BrE)
a piece of fabric or artwork created by interlacing threads or fibers in a pattern

Examples

A colorful weaving
The artist displayed her latest weaving at the local craft fair.
Traditional weaving techniques have been passed down through generations in this village.
He admired the colorful patterns in the handmade weaving hanging on the wall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: knitting - the craft of making fabric by interlocking loops of yarn
  • synonym: tapestry - a decorative fabric with designs woven into it
