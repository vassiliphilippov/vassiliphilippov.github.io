back burner

noun
a position of low priority
Pronunciation: / ˈbæk ˌbɝːnər / (AmE), / ˈbæk ˌbɜːnə / (BrE)
A position of low priority or deferred attention compared to more urgent matters.

Examples

On the back burner
Environmental issues were unfortunately left on the back burner during the election campaign.
His dream of writing a novel remains on the back burner for now.
She decided to put her personal projects on the back burner while focusing on her new job.

  • antonym: front burner - a position of high priority or immediate attention
