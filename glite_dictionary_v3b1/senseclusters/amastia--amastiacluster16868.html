amastia

noun
absence of mammary glands
Pronunciation: / əˈmæstiə / (AmE), / əˈmæstiə / (BrE)
The absence of mammary glands, either due to surgical removal or a developmental defect.

Examples

Complete amastia condition
Some patients develop amastia following certain cancer treatments.
The surgeon explained that amastia can result from congenital conditions.
Treatment options for amastia include reconstructive surgery.
