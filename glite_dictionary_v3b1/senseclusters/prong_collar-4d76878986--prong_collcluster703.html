prong collar

noun
dog training collar with inward prongs
7.1
Pronunciation: / prɔŋ ˈkɑlər / (AmE), / prɒŋ ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
A type of dog collar made of chain links with blunted metal prongs that point inward toward the dog's neck, used in training.

Examples

A prong collar for dogs
Some trainers use a prong collar to discourage pulling during walks.
The prong collar should fit snugly but not too tightly around the dog's neck.
Veterinarians often caution against improper use of a prong collar due to potential injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: choke collar - dog collar that tightens around the neck when pulled
