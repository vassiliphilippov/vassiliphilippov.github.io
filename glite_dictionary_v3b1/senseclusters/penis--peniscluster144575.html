penis

noun
male sex organ
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpinɪs / (AmE), / ˈpiːnɪs / (BrE)
The male sexual organ, which is also used for urination in mammals, including humans.

Examples

Circumcision is a surgical procedure involving the removal of the foreskin from the penis.

The anatomy textbook provides a detailed diagram of the penis and its functions.
