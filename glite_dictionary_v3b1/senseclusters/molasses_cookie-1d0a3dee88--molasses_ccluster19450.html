molasses cookie

noun
spicy, molasses-sweetened cookie
Pronunciation: / məˈlæsəz ˈkʊki / (AmE), / məˈlæsɪz ˈkʊki / (BrE)
A soft, spicy cookie sweetened in part with molasses, often flavored with ginger, cinnamon, and cloves.

Examples

A molasses cookie with ginger
I love the chewy texture and spicy flavor of a good molasses cookie.
She baked a batch of molasses cookies for the school bake sale.
The rich aroma of molasses cookies filled the entire kitchen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gingersnap - a crisp, spiced cookie flavored mainly with ginger
