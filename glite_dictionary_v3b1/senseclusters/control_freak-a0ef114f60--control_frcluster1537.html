control freak

noun
person with a compulsive need to control
1.6
Pronunciation: / kənˈtroʊl ˌfrik / (AmE), / kənˈtrəʊl ˌfriːk / (BrE)
a person who feels a strong need to control people or situations, often excessively or obsessively

Examples

A control freak personality
Being a control freak can put a strain on relationships with friends and family.
My new boss is a total control freak who insists on checking every tiny detail.
She admitted that her control freak tendencies made group projects difficult.

Related Terms

  • synonym: micromanager - someone who controls every small detail of a task or job
