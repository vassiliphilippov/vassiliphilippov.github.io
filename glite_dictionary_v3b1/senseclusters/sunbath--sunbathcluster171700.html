sunbath

verb
to lie in sun
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌbæθ / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌbɑːθ / (BrE)
to sit or lie in the sun, usually to get warm or to tan the skin.

Examples

To sunbath on the beach
They decided to sunbath on the beach after swimming in the sea.
During the summer, many people sunbath in their gardens to relax and enjoy the weather.
He likes to sunbath by the pool whenever he has a free afternoon.
