step stool

noun
small stool with steps for reaching high places
Pronunciation: / stɛp stuːl / (AmE), / stɛp stuːl / (BrE)
A small stool with one or two steps, often foldable, used to reach higher places.

Examples

A step stool for reaching
He asked his daughter to stand on the step stool while helping bake cookies.
She used a step stool to reach the top shelf in the kitchen.
The step stool folds easily for convenient storage in the closet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ladder - a structure for climbing up or down, with a series of steps between two sidepieces
