rosebud orchid

noun
South American orchid with showy rose flowers
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊzbʌd ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzbʌd ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
An orchid native to central and northern South America, having 1- to 3-flowered racemes with large, showy, rose-colored blossoms; sometimes classified in the genus Pogonia.

The rosebud orchid flower
Several species were on display, but the rosebud orchid attracted the most attention.

We found a rare rosebud orchid growing deep in the rainforest.
