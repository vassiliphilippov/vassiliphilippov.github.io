cold hand

noun
emotionally distant gesture; symbol of death/danger
1.0
Pronunciation: / koʊld hænd / (AmE), / kəʊld hand / (BrE)
An unemotional or unwelcoming touch or gesture, or a symbol of death or danger.

Examples

A cold hand touch
I felt a cold hand on my arm and instantly sensed something was wrong.
She extended a cold hand, betraying her lack of warmth toward the guests.
The cold hand of fear gripped him as he entered the abandoned building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: icy touch - a touch or manner that is cold and unfeeling
  • antonym: warm hand - a hand that is welcoming or comforting
