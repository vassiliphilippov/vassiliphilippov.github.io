canteen

noun
water flask for soldiers or travelers
6.2
Pronunciation: / kænˈtin / (AmE), / kænˈtiːn / (BrE)
a small container or flask for carrying water or other drinks, typically used by soldiers, campers, or travelers

Examples

Metal canteen
Hikers are advised to carry a canteen of water on long trails.
She filled her canteen at the stream before setting up camp.
The soldier took a long drink from his canteen before continuing the march.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flask - a small container for liquids, often for carrying alcohol or water
