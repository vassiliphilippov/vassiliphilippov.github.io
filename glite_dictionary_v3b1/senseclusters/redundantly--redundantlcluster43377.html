redundantly

adverb
in an excessive way
4.6
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdʌndəntli / (AmE), / rɪˈdʌndəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is more than necessary, often by repeating the same information or actions

Examples

Redundantly repeated instructions
The instructions were given redundantly, making the meeting longer than necessary.
She redundantly checked the locked door several times before leaving.
The report redundantly included the same statistics in multiple sections.

Related Terms

  • antonym: efficiently - in a way that avoids waste or unnecessary repetition
  • synonym: repetitively - in a way that involves repeating the same thing
  • synonym: unnecessarily - in a way that is not needed
