sacredly

adverb
with deep reverence
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪkrɪdli / (AmE), / ˈseɪkrɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows deep respect or reverence, especially for something considered holy or solemn

Examples

To sacredly guard
The villagers sacredly guarded the ancient temple from outsiders.
He sacredly promised to uphold the traditions of his ancestors.
The ceremony was sacredly performed at dawn every year.
The ancient texts were sacredly preserved by generations of monks.
She sacredly honored her family's traditions during the ceremony.
The community sacredly guarded the site believed to be holy.
She sacredly promised to protect the ancient traditions of her family.
The oath was sacredly taken before the entire community.
He sacredly honored his commitment to serve those in need.

Related Terms

  • antonym: profane - showing disrespect for something sacred
  • synonym: reverently - in a way that shows deep respect
  • synonym: solemnly - in a serious and formal manner
