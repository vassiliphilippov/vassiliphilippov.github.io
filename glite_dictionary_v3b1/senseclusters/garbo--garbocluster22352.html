garbo

noun
rubbish collector
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːbəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person whose job is to collect household rubbish

Examples

The garbo collects bins
The garbo comes early every Tuesday morning to collect the bins.
When I was a child, I always waved at the garbo from our front window.
The garbo works hard to keep our streets clean and tidy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dustman - a person employed to remove household refuse
  • synonym: garbage collector - a person who collects household waste
