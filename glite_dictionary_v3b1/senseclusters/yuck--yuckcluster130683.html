yuck

noun
expression of disgust
Pronunciation: / jʌk / (AmE), / jʌk / (BrE)
an exclamation or expression showing strong dislike, disgust, or distaste for something

Examples

A loud yuck
The children shouted yuck when they saw the broccoli on their plates.
He made a loud yuck after tasting the sour milk.
Whenever she sees bugs, she always says yuck and turns away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ugh - an exclamation of disgust or dislike
  • antonym: yum - an exclamation expressing pleasure at the taste of food
