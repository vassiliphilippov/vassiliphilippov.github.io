hook up

verb
connect or link (devices, systems, etc.)
Pronunciation: / hʊk ʌp / (AmE), / hʊk ʌp / (BrE)
to connect or set up devices or systems so they work together, usually by attaching cables or making necessary links

Examples

The electrician will hook up the new stove to the power supply tomorrow.
They had to hook up the computer to the internet before starting the update.
Can you hook up the printer to my laptop for me?
I managed to hook up the speakers without any trouble.
The technician will hook up your internet connection tomorrow morning.
