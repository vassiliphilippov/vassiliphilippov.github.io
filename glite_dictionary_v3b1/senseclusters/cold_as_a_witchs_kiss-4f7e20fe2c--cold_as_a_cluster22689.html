cold as a witch's kiss

adjective
extremely unfriendly or distant
Pronunciation: / koʊld əz ə wɪtʃɪz kɪs / (AmE), / kəʊld əz ə wɪtʃɪz kɪs / (BrE)
(of a person's manner or behavior) extremely unfriendly or emotionally distant; icily unapproachable.

Examples

A cold as a witch's kiss tone
He spoke to me in a tone that was cold as a witch's kiss.
Her eyes were cold as a witch's kiss, betraying no sign of emotion.
The manager's response was cold as a witch's kiss, offering no comfort or support.
