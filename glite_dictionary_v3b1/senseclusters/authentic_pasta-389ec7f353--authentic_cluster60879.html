authentic pasta

noun
traditional-style pasta
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈθɛntɪk ˈpɑstə / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɛntɪk ˈpɑːstə / (BrE)
Pasta made using traditional methods or ingredients, regarded as true to its regional origins.

Examples

Authentic pasta from Italy
Gino showed us how to make authentic pasta from scratch.
Many restaurants in Italy serve only authentic pasta using age-old recipes.
She prefers authentic pasta because of the rich flavors and traditional techniques.
