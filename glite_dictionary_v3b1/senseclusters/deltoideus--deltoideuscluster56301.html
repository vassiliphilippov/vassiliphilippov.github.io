deltoideus

adjective
triangular-shaped
Pronunciation: / dɛlˈtɔɪdiəs / (AmE), / dɛlˈtɔɪdiəs / (BrE)
having a triangular or delta-shaped form, especially used in scientific or anatomical contexts.

Examples

Deltoideus leaf shape
The leaf was described as deltoideus due to its broad, triangular shape.
In anatomy, the deltoideus muscle covers the shoulder and is roughly triangular.
The species was named for its deltoideus petals, which resemble the Greek letter delta.

Related Terms

  • synonym: triangular - having three sides or shaped like a triangle
