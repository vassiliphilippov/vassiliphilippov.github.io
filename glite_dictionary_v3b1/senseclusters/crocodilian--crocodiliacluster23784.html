crocodilian

noun
large reptile of the crocodile family
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɑː.kəˈdɪl.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌkrɒ.kəˈdɪl.i.ən / (BrE)
any large, predatory reptile of the order Crocodylia, including crocodiles, alligators, caimans, and gharials, characterized by a long snout and a powerful tail

Examples

A crocodilian animal
Scientists studied the jaw strength of the crocodilian in various environments.
The Nile is home to many species of crocodilian, including both crocodiles and their relatives.
Young children were fascinated by the massive crocodilian basking on the riverbank.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crocodile - large predatory aquatic reptile; member of Crocodylidae
