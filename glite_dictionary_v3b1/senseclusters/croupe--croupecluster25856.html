croupe

noun
hindquarters of an animal
6.5
Pronunciation: / kruːp / (AmE), / kruːp / (BrE)
the part of a quadruped animal, especially a horse or similar animal, that corresponds to the human buttocks, located between the loins and the tail

Examples

The croupe of a horse
A well-sloped croupe is desirable in many horse breeds for both strength and appearance.
Groomers pay special attention to the croupe when preparing horses for a show.
The rider adjusted his seat on the horse's croupe to maintain better balance during the gallop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rump - the hind part of an animal's body or the buttocks in humans and animals
