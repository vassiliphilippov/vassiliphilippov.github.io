parklike

adjective
resembling a park
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of a park, especially in being spacious, green, and pleasant

Examples

Parklike green space
The hotel is surrounded by parklike gardens that are perfect for relaxing walks.
They enjoyed a picnic in the parklike setting near the river.
The new office complex features a parklike landscape with wide lawns and tall trees.

Related Terms

  • antonym: urban - relating to a city or town, not rural or green
