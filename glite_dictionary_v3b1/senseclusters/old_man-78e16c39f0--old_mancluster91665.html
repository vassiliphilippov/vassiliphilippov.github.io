old man

noun
herb used in absinthe or as a flavoring
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊld mæn / (AmE), / əʊld mæn / (BrE)
an aromatic herb (Artemisia abrotanum) of temperate Eurasia and North Africa, with a bitter taste, used in making absinthe and as a flavoring.

Examples

Old man herb
She crushed dried old man leaves to add a unique flavor to the stew.
The gardener planted a patch of old man next to the lavender for its strong scent.
Traditional recipes sometimes call for a pinch of old man in herbal liqueurs.
