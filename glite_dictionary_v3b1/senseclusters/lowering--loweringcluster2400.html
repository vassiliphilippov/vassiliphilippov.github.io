lowering

noun
the act of making something less
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of causing something to become less in amount, value, or degree

Examples

Lowering of prices
Environmental concerns have led to the lowering of emissions limits in many countries.
The doctor advised the lowering of salt in her diet.
There was a noticeable lowering of prices throughout the summer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: increase - the act of becoming greater or making something greater
  • synonym: reduction - the process or result of reducing
