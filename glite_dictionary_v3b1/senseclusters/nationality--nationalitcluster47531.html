nationality

noun
status of belonging to a nation
Pronunciation: / ˌnæʃəˈnæləti / (AmE), / ˌnaʃəˈnalɪti / (BrE)
the legal status or condition of belonging to a particular nation, either by birth or by naturalization

Examples

Nationality on a passport
His nationality was listed as Canadian on his passport.
She applied for Italian nationality after living in Rome for several years.
The school has students of many different nationalities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: citizenship - legal status of being a member of a country
