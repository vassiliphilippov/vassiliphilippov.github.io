drowsiness

noun
state of sleepiness or tiredness
Pronunciation: / ˈdraʊzi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdraʊzɪnɪs / (BrE)
A feeling of being very sleepy or tired, often leading to a desire to sleep.

Examples

Feeling drowsiness after medicine
Certain medications may cause drowsiness as a side effect.
He struggled to stay awake during the meeting due to overwhelming drowsiness.
The long, boring lecture filled the room with an air of drowsiness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alertness - the state of being alert or awake
  • synonym: sleepiness - the quality or state of being sleepy
