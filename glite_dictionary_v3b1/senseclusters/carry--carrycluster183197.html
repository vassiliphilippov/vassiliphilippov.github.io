carry

verb
to cover a distance (sports, esp. golf)
Pronunciation: / ˈkæri / (AmE), / ˈkæri / (BrE)
to move a ball or object a certain distance in sports, or to propel it forward

Examples

To carry the ball
His drive easily carried the distance required to reach the target.
The baseball carried over the fence for a home run.
The golf ball carried the water hazard and landed on the green.
She skillfully carried the puck past all her opponents before shooting.
The player managed to carry the ball across half the field without losing possession.
You are not allowed to carry the basketball without dribbling in official games.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dribble - to move a ball along by repeated small kicks, bounces, or touches
  • synonym: reach - to arrive at or extend to a place
