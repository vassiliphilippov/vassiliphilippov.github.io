laid-back friend

noun
a relaxed, easy-going friend
Pronunciation: / ˌleɪd ˈbæk ˌfrɛnd / (AmE), / ˌleɪd ˈbæk ˌfrɛnd / (BrE)
a friend who is relaxed, easy-going, and not easily upset or stressed

Examples

A laid-back friend
A laid-back friend can help you stay calm during stressful times and enjoy life more.
Everyone loves hanging out with Mark because he is such a laid-back friend who never gets stressed about plans.
Unlike my competitive classmates, my most laid-back friend always takes things in stride and never worries too much.
