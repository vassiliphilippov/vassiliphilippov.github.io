drop-seed

noun
a grass of the genus Sporobolus
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑpˌsid / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpˌsiːd / (BrE)
Any of several grasses of the genus Sporobolus, characterized by small, easily detached seeds.

Examples

A drop-seed grass
Many prairies contain species of drop-seed that provide habitat for wildlife.
Some gardeners plant drop-seed for its drought tolerance and delicate seed heads.
The botanist identified the tall drop-seed growing along the edge of the field.
