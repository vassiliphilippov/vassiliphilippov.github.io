intraepithelial

adjective
within epithelial cells
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˌɛpəˈθiːliəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˌɛpɪˈθiːliəl / (BrE)
located or occurring within the layer of epithelial cells

Examples

Intraepithelial lesions
The pathologist noted intraepithelial lesions during the microscopic examination.
Certain viruses can cause intraepithelial changes that may lead to cancer.
Researchers studied the intraepithelial immune response in the affected tissue.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subepithelial - located beneath the epithelial layer
