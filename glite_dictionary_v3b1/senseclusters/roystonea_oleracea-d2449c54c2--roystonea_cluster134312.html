Roystonea oleracea

noun
West Indian palm with edible buds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌrɔɪˈstiːniə oʊˌlɛˈreɪsiə / (AmE), / ˌrɔɪˈstiːniə əʊˌlɛˈreɪsiə / (BrE)
A tall West Indian palm tree whose young leaf buds are edible and often referred to as 'cabbage'.

Examples

Tall Roystonea oleracea palm
Chefs prize the tender leaf buds of Roystonea oleracea for use in local dishes.
Many gardeners cultivate Roystonea oleracea for its striking appearance and edible shoots.
The towering Roystonea oleracea is a familiar sight along Caribbean avenues.
