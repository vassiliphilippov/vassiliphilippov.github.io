screenlike

adjective
resembling a screen
Pronunciation: / ˈskriːnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈskriːnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or similar to a screen in appearance or function

Examples

Screenlike fabric
The artist used a screenlike fabric to divide the space without blocking the light.
A screenlike partition separated the two areas of the open-plan office.
The leaves formed a natural, screenlike barrier between the garden and the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: screeny - having the qualities or appearance of a screen
