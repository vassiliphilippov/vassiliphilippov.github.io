ehhh

interjection
hesitation sound
Pronunciation: / ɛ / (AmE), / ɛ / (BrE)
used as a hesitation sound when thinking, unsure, or searching for words in speech

Examples

Ehhh thinking
He said, 'ehhh, I am not really sure what to do next.'
During the interview, she paused and made an ehhh before answering the question.
Whenever he is uncertain, he often starts his sentences with ehhh.
