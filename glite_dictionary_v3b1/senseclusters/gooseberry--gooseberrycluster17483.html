gooseberry

noun
a tart, round berry or its shrub
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡusˌbɛri /; / ˈɡuːsˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈɡuːzbəri /; / ˈɡʊzb(ə)ri / (BrE)
a small, round, tart fruit (berry), or the bush that produces it
