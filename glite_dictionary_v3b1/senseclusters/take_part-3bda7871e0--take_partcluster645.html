Synonyms

  • participate
    2.5 99/m
    participate is used in formal and everyday contexts to join an activity with others, while take part is more casual.
  • join in
    1.1 9.68/m
    join in means to enter an activity that is already happening with others, while take part can mean joining activities generally.
  • partake in
    4.3 1.04/m
    partake in sounds more formal or old-fashioned and often appears in writing, while take part is common in everyday speech.
  • engage
    3.4 84/m
    engage is often used as 'engage in' to show formal involvement, while take part is more neutral and conversational.
