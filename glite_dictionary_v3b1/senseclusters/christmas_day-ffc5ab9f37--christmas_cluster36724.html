Christmas Day

noun
Christian holiday on December 25th
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪsməs deɪ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsməs deɪ / (BrE)
The annual Christian holiday celebrated on December 25th to commemorate the birth of Jesus Christ; in England, Wales, and Ireland, it is also one of the quarter days for legal and financial purposes.

Examples

Christmas Day celebration
Every year, our family gathers to celebrate Christmas Day with a festive meal.
In the UK, Christmas Day is considered one of the most important public holidays.
Many people exchange gifts on Christmas Day as part of their holiday traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Christmas - the festival or holiday celebrating the birth of Christ
  • antonym: Easter - Christian holiday celebrating the resurrection of Jesus
