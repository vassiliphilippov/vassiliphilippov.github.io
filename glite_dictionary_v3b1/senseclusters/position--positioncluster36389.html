position

noun
social or professional standing
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a person's social or professional rank, job, or role in society or an organization

Examples

A high position at work

Her new job gave her a higher position in the company hierarchy.
Some people judge others based solely on their social position.
After ten years, he was promoted to a senior position at the company.
She applied for a position in the marketing department last month.
The position of financial manager requires strong analytical skills.
