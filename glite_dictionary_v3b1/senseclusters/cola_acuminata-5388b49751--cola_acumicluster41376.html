Cola acuminata

noun
West African tree producing kola nuts
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkoʊ.lə ə.kjuː.mɪ.ˈneɪ.tə/ (AmE), /ˈkəʊ.lə ə.kjuː.mɪ.ˈneɪ.tə/ (BrE)
A tropical West African tree that produces large brown nuts (kola nuts) containing caffeine, used as a source of cola extract.

Examples

Cola acuminata tree nuts
Cola acuminata is widely cultivated in West Africa for its nuts, which are important in local ceremonies.
Soft drink manufacturers once used extracts from Cola acuminata as an ingredient in cola beverages.
The caffeine content of the kola nut from Cola acuminata makes it a traditional stimulant.
