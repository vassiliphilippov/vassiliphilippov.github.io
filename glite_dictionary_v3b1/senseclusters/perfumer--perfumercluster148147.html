perfumer

noun
person who creates perfumes
Pronunciation: / pərˈfjuːmər / (AmE), / pəˈfjuːmə / (BrE)
a person who makes and sometimes sells perfumes, especially as a profession

Examples

A perfumer makes scents
A skilled perfumer can identify hundreds of aromas with just a whiff.
She dreamed of becoming a perfumer and spent years studying the art of fragrance.
The perfumer created a new scent that instantly became popular with celebrities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fragrance - a pleasant, sweet smell; especially a perfume
  • synonym: perfume - a fragrant liquid typically made from essential oils, used to give a pleasant smell to the body
