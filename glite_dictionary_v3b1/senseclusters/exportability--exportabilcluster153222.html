exportability

noun
suitability for export
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkspɔːrtəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌɛkspɔːtəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being suitable or able to be exported to other countries

Examples

The exportability of products
The exportability of agricultural products depends on international regulations and quality standards.
Improving the exportability of local goods can boost a country's economy.
The company invested in packaging to increase the exportability of its products.

Related Terms

  • synonym: export - to send goods or services to another country for sale
  • antonym: importability - the quality or state of being suitable for import
