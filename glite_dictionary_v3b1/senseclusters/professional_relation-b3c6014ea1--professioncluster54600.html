professional relation

noun
relationship involving provision of professional services
4.6
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɛʃənl rɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənəl rɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a relationship established when a person seeks and receives professional help or services from a qualified individual or organization

Examples

A professional relation with clients
Doctors are expected to maintain clear boundaries in any professional relation with their patients.
The professional relation between a therapist and client must be based on trust and confidentiality.
The law regulates the professional relation between lawyers and their clients.
