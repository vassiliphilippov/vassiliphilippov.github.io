cheeriness

noun
happiness and optimism
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪriˌnəs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪəriˌnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being happy, lively, and optimistic

Examples

Her cheeriness at party
Her cheeriness made everyone feel welcome at the party.
Despite the rainy weather, his cheeriness never faded.
The teacher's cheeriness helped motivate the students on Monday mornings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheerfulness - the state of being noticeably happy and optimistic
  • antonym: gloominess - a feeling or appearance of sadness or depression
