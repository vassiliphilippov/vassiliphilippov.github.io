artificer

noun
fantasy game character who crafts magical devices
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈtɪfɪsər / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪfɪsə / (BrE)
(in fantasy role-playing games) a character class or type of character who designs, builds, and uses magical devices by combining practical engineering or crafting skills with arcane magic.

Examples

Fantasy artificer
The artificer engineered a magical staff that channels elemental power.
In our campaign, the party's artificer repaired the golem and reforged its enchanted gears.
Players enjoy playing artificers who combine engineering skills with arcane spellcasting to invent devices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inventor - a person who creates new devices or ideas
  • synonym: tinkerer - someone who repairs or invents things by making small changes or experiments
