safelight

noun
darkroom lamp
6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈseɪf.laɪt/ (AmE), /ˈseɪf.laɪt/ (BrE)
a dim light used in photographic darkrooms that does not affect light-sensitive materials

Examples

A safelight in darkroom
The photographer turned on the safelight before handling the undeveloped film.
A red safelight allowed her to see in the darkroom without ruining the photos.
Always check that your safelight is working properly to avoid accidental exposure.
