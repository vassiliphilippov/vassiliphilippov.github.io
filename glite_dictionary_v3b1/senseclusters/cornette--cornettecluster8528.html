cornette

noun
small flag or pennon
Pronunciation: / kɔrˈnɛt / (AmE), / kɔːˈnɛt / (BrE)
a small flag or pennon, especially one attached to a cavalryman's lance or used as a standard

Examples

Cavalry cornette
The knight rode with a colorful cornette fluttering from his lance during the parade.
Each cavalry unit carried a unique cornette to identify themselves on the battlefield.
The museum displayed an old cornette that once belonged to a famous regiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banner - a large flag or standard used as a symbol
  • synonym: pennon - a long, narrow flag, often used on lances
