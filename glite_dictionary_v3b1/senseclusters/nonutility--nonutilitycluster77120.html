nonutility

adjective
not from public utilities
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.juːˈtɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.juːˈtɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
not relating to or provided by a public utility service such as electricity, water, or gas companies

Examples

Nonutility energy projects
The government encouraged investment in nonutility solar power projects.
Private companies are developing nonutility energy solutions for remote areas.
She works for a startup focused on nonutility water purification systems.

Related Terms

  • antonym: utility - relating to a public service such as electricity or water
