alias

noun
false or assumed name
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.li.əs / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.li.əs / (BrE)
a false or assumed name used by a person, often to conceal identity

Examples

A secret alias
Many authors write under an alias to protect their privacy.
She registered for the competition using an alias rather than her real name.
The suspect was known by the alias 'John Smith' during his travels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pseudonym - a fictitious name, especially one used by an author
  • antonym: real name - a person's genuine or legal name
