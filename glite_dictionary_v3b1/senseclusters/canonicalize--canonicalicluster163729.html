canonicalize

verb
to make official canon
9.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈnæn.ɪ.kə.laɪz/ (AmE), /kə.ˈnɒn.ɪ.kə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to treat or declare something as part of an accepted, authoritative list or canon

Examples

To canonicalize a book
The committee decided to canonicalize several new works as part of the official collection.
Scholars often debate which texts to canonicalize in literary studies.
The museum's board voted to canonicalize the artist's early paintings in their permanent exhibit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authorize - to give official approval or recognition
  • antonym: exclude - to leave out or not include
