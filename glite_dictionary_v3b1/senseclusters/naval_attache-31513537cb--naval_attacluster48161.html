naval attache

noun
navy officer serving as a diplomatic attache
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪvəl ˌætəˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˈneɪvəl ˌætəˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
a military officer in a navy who is assigned as a diplomatic representative at an embassy or consulate.

Examples

A naval attache officer
A former naval attache shared insights about diplomacy and naval cooperation at the seminar.
As a naval attache, she was responsible for liaising with the host country's navy.
The embassy's naval attache attended the international maritime conference in Rome.
