frigid demeanor

noun
emotionally cold or unfriendly behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪdʒɪd dɪˈmiːnər / (AmE), / ˈfrɪdʒɪd dɪˈmiːnə / (BrE)
a manner or behavior that is emotionally cold, distant, or unfriendly

Examples

Frigid demeanor in meetings
Despite his frigid demeanor, he cared deeply about his family.
Her frigid demeanor kept everyone at a distance during the meeting.
The manager's frigid demeanor discouraged employees from sharing their ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aloof - not friendly or forthcoming; cool and distant
  • antonym: warm demeanor - friendly and welcoming manner
