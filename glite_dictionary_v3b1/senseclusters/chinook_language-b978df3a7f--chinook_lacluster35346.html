Chinook language

noun
indigenous language of the Chinook people
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃɪˈnʊk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / tʃɪˈnʊk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the indigenous language of the Chinook people, belonging to the Penutian language family, traditionally spoken in the Pacific Northwest region of North America

Examples

The Chinook language was traditionally spoken along the lower Columbia River.
