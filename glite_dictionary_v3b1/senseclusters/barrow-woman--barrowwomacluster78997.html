barrow-woman

noun
female street vendor (fruit/vegetables)
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbæroʊ ˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈbærəʊ ˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who sells fruit and vegetables from a barrow, typically in a street or market

Examples

A barrow-woman sells fruit
A local barrow-woman always had the freshest produce available each morning.
Historically, a barrow-woman would be a familiar sight in London markets.
The barrow-woman called out the price of her apples to passing shoppers.
