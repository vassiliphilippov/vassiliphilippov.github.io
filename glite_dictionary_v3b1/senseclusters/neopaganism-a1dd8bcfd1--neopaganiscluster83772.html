Neopaganism

noun
modern pagan religion
Pronunciation: / ˌnioʊˈpeɪɡənɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊˈpeɪɡənɪzəm / (BrE)
a modern religious movement that draws inspiration from or revives ancient pagan beliefs and practices

Examples

Neopaganism beliefs
Many people are drawn to Neopaganism because of its focus on nature and ancient traditions.
Modern festivals often include rituals inspired by Neopaganism and other earth-based religions.
She began studying Neopaganism to learn more about alternative spiritual paths.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Christianity - the religion based on the teachings of Jesus Christ
  • synonym: Wicca - a modern pagan religion with witchcraft traditions
  • synonym: paganism - ancient religious beliefs outside the main world religions
