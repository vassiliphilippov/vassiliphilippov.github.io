nitrify

verb
treat soil with nitrates
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtrəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtrɪfʌɪ /; / ˈnɪtrɪfʌɪ /; / ˈnɪtrɪfaɪ / (BrE)
