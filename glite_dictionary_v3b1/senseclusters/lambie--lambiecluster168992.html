lambie

noun
young lamb (affectionate)
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlæmbi / (AmE), / ˈlæmbi / (BrE)
an affectionate term for a small or young lamb, often used with children

Examples

Stuffed lambie
The little girl carried her favorite stuffed lambie everywhere she went.
At the farm, the children were excited to feed the newborn lambie from a bottle.
He gently stroked the soft wool of the tiny lambie in his arms.
