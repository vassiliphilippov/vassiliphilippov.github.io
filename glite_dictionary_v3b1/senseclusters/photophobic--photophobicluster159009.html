photophobic

adjective
sensitive to light
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtəˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
sensitive to light or tending to avoid light, especially due to discomfort or biological response

Examples

Photophobic animals
Some cave-dwelling animals are photophobic and stay away from sunlight.
After her eye surgery, she became photophobic and needed to wear dark glasses indoors.
The photophobic reaction in certain plants causes them to close their leaves in bright light.

Related Terms

  • antonym: photophilic - attracted to or thriving in light
  • synonym: photosensitive - reacting or sensitive to light
