indicate

verb
state or express briefly
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to state or express something briefly or clearly

Examples

To indicate a choice
He indicated his intention to resign in an email to staff.
She indicated her approval with a quick nod.
The instructions clearly indicate what steps to take next.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imply - to express indirectly or suggest without stating directly
  • synonym: suggest - to put forward as a possibility or consideration
