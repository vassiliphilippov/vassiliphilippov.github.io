votable

adjective
eligible for voting
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈvəʊtəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be voted on or put to a vote, especially in a formal setting

Examples

Votable proposal
The committee declared the proposal votable at the next meeting.
Only votable issues will be discussed during the annual shareholders' meeting.
The amendment became votable after the required signatures were collected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eligible - having the right to do or receive something
  • antonym: ineligible - not having the right to do or receive something
