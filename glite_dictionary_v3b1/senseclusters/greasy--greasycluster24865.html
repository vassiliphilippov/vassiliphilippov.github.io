greasy

adjective
covered with grease or oil
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːsi, ˈɡrisi /; / ˈɡrisi / (AmE), / ˈɡriːzi, ˈɡriːsi /; / ˈɡriːzi / (BrE)
covered with or containing a lot of grease or oil; oily or slippery to the touch

Examples

Greasy hands
His hands were greasy after fixing the car engine.
She wiped the greasy fingerprints off the kitchen counter.
The tools in the workshop are always greasy from regular use.
After working on the car, his hands were covered in greasy oil.
She wiped the greasy residue off the kitchen counter with a paper towel.
The fries were so greasy that my fingers became shiny after eating them.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dry - not containing moisture or oil
  • synonym: oily - covered in or resembling oil; containing oil
