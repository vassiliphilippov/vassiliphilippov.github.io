Abkhasian

noun
language spoken by the Abkhaz people
Pronunciation: / æbˈkeɪʒən / (AmE), / abˈkeɪziən / (BrE)
the language of the Abkhaz people, belonging to the Northwest Caucasian language family

Examples

Abkhasian language
Many works of folklore have been preserved in Abkhasian.
She is fluent in both Russian and Abkhasian.
The Abkhasian language uses an alphabet that is adapted from Cyrillic.

Related Terms
