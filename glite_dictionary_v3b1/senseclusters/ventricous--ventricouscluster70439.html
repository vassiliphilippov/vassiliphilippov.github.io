ventricous

adjective
swollen on one side (technical)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛn.trɪ.kəs / (AmE), / ˈvɛn.trɪ.kəs / (BrE)
(chiefly technical) Having a swelling or bulge on one side; swollen or inflated on one side.

Examples

A ventricous stem
Some fungi can be identified by their ventricous stems, which bulge noticeably on one side.
The botanist noted the ventricous base of the plant's seed capsule.
Under the microscope, the insect's ventricous gullet was clearly visible.
