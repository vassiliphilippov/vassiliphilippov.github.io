mangler

noun
person who mutilates or destroys
Pronunciation: / ˈmæŋɡlər / (AmE), / ˈmaŋɡlə / (BrE)
a person who mutilates, destroys, disfigures, or cripples something or someone, especially in a violent or damaging manner

Examples

A mangler of things
Many feared the forest at night, believing a mangler lurked in the shadows.
The notorious mangler was finally apprehended by the police after years of terrorizing the town.
The story described a gruesome mangler who destroyed priceless works of art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mutilator - a person who mutilates, especially the body
