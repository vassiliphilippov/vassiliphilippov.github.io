butt

noun
target of jokes or ridicule
Pronunciation: / bʌt / (AmE), / bʌt / (BrE)
A person or thing who is the target of jokes, ridicule, or pranks.

Examples

Butt of a joke
During the meeting, her mistake made her the butt of everyone's laughter.
He was always the butt of their cruel jokes at school.
Nobody likes to be the butt of a prank, especially in public.

Related Terms

  • synonym: target - person who is the focus of attack or ridicule
