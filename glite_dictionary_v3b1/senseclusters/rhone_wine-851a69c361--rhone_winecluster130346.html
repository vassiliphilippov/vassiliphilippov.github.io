Rhone wine

noun
wine from the Rhone valley, France
6.3
Pronunciation: / roʊn waɪn / (AmE), / rəʊn waɪn / (BrE)
Any of various wines produced in the Rhone River valley of France, known for their distinctive regional characteristics.

Examples

Rhone wine region
He prefers Rhone wine with his steak because of its rich, complex flavors.
The restaurant offers a fine selection of Rhone wine from both northern and southern vineyards.
We toured several vineyards in France and brought home a case of Rhone wine.
