forty-second cousin

noun
a very distant relative (42 generations removed)
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔr.ti ˌsɛk.ənd ˈkʌz.ən / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.ti ˌsɛk.ənd ˈkʌz.ən / (BrE)
a relative who shares a common ancestor with you 42 generations back; often used to refer to a very distant relation

Examples

A forty-second cousin
He's practically a stranger; he's my forty-second cousin on my father's side.
I only discovered last year that Emma is my forty-second cousin through a genealogy website.
She always jokes about being related to royalty as someone's forty-second cousin.
