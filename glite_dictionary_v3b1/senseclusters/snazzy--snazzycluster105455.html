snazzy

adjective
flashily stylish or attractive
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæzi / (AmE), / ˈsnæzi / (BrE)
Brightly stylish or attractive in a flashy way.

Examples

A snazzy outfit
His snazzy new car turned heads on the street.
She wore a snazzy red jacket to the party.
The restaurant has a snazzy design with bold colors and modern lighting.
