purchase order

noun
commercial document for ordering goods/services
3.2 0.43/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ùÀê.t É…ôs Àå…îÀêr.d…ö / (AmE), / Ààp…úÀê.t É…ôs Àå…îÀê.d…ô / (BrE)
a commercial document issued by a buyer to a seller, indicating the products, quantities, and agreed prices for products or services the seller will provide to the buyer

Examples

A purchase order form
After reviewing the purchase order, the supplier confirmed that all specifications would be met.
Please include the purchase order number with your shipment to ensure proper processing.
The company placed a purchase order for new office supplies last week.

Synonyms

  • order form
    2.0 0.33/m
    an order form is a brief request to a supplier for goods or services, while a purchase order is a formal buyer document that confirms quantities and prices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: invoice - a bill sent by a seller to a buyer listing goods or services provided and stating the amount due
