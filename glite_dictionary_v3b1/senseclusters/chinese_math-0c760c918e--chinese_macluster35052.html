Chinese math

noun
colloquial term for difficult mathematics
5.1 8.1
Pronunciation: /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz mæθ/ (AmE), /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz mæθ/ /; / /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz mɑːθ/ (BrE)
(colloquial) Very difficult or complex mathematics; any task or problem perceived as being extremely hard to solve, likened metaphorically to mathematics as taught in China.

Examples

Difficult Chinese math
Figuring out tax forms can seem like Chinese math to people unfamiliar with accounting.
She joked that her new job involved more Chinese math than she could handle.
The instructions were so complicated, it was like trying to understand Chinese math.
