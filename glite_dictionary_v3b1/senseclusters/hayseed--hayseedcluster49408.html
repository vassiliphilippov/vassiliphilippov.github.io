hayseed

noun
unsophisticated or rural person
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪˌsid / (AmE), / ˈheɪˌsiːd / (BrE)
an unsophisticated or uncultured person, typically from a rural area; a country bumpkin

Examples

A hayseed person
Don't let their country accents fool you—they're not just hayseeds.
He was treated like a hayseed by his city cousins when he visited them.
The movie poked fun at the local hayseed who got lost in New York.

Related Terms

  • antonym: city slicker - a sophisticated or urban person, especially in contrast to someone rural
