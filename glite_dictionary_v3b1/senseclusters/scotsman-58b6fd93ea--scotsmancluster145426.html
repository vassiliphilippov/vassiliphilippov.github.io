Scotsman

noun
male native or inhabitant of Scotland
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑtsmən / (AmE), / ˈskɒtsmən / (BrE)
a man who is a native or inhabitant of Scotland.

Examples

A Scotsman from Scotland
As a Scotsman, he celebrated Hogmanay with great enthusiasm.
Every Scotsman has a unique story about life in the Highlands.
The famous engineer was a proud Scotsman who contributed much to his homeland.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Scot - a person from Scotland, regardless of gender
  • antonym: Scotswoman - a female native or inhabitant of Scotland
