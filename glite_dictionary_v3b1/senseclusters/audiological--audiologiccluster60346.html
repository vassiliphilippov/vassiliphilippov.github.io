audiological

adjective
hearing and balance
Pronunciation: / ˌɔdiəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɔːdiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the scientific or medical study, assessment, or treatment of hearing (and balance) disorders

Examples

Audiological assessment
The clinic offers audiological assessments for children with suspected hearing loss.
She decided to pursue a career in audiological research after studying neuroscience.
Modern audiological equipment can detect even mild forms of hearing impairment.
The doctor recommended an audiological test to determine the cause of his hearing problems.
She attended an audiological conference to learn about the latest research in hearing loss.
An audiological assessment is often the first step in diagnosing hearing disorders.
