readable

adjective
easy to read or decipher
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈriːdəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of writing or text) able to be easily read or deciphered; clear and legible.

Examples

A readable text
She rewrote her notes to make them more readable for the entire class.
The font size on this label is so small that it's barely readable.
The old manuscript was damaged, but most of the text remained readable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: legible - clear enough to be read easily
