Pellicularia koleroga

noun
fungus causing disease in coffee plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛlɪˈkjuːlɛriə ˌkoʊləˈroʊɡə / (AmE), / ˌpɛlɪˈkjuːlɪəriə ˌkɒlɪˈrəʊɡə / (BrE)

Examples
