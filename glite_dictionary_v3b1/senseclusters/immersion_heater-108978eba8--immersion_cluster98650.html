immersion heater

noun
device for heating liquid by immersion
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɜr.ʒən ˌhiː.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜː.ʃən ˌhiː.tər / (BrE)
a device with a heating element designed to be placed directly in liquid, especially to heat water in a tank or container

Examples

An immersion heater in water
If the central heating fails, you can still use the immersion heater to heat water.
She switched on the immersion heater to make sure there would be enough hot water for a bath.
The electrician installed a new immersion heater in the hot water tank.
