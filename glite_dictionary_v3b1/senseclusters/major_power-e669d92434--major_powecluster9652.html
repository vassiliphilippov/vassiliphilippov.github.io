major power

noun
influential country on a global scale
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdʒɚ ˈpaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdʒə ˈpaʊə / (BrE)
a country that is influential enough to influence events globally, especially in political or military matters

Examples

A major power country
After the war, the country emerged as a major power on the international stage.
Being a major power comes with significant responsibilities in maintaining world peace.
Negotiations between major powers often shape the direction of global policies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: great power - a nation with significant global influence
  • degree lower: minor power - a country with limited regional or global influence
  • degree higher: superpower - an extremely powerful state with dominant influence worldwide
