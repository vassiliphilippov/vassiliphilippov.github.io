crotch goblin

noun
slang for mischievous child
Pronunciation: / krɑtʃ ˈɡɑblɪn / (AmE), / krɒtʃ ˈɡɒblɪn / (BrE)
(slang, humorous, derogatory) A mischievous or unruly child, especially a young one.

He joked that his three-year-old niece was a certified crotch goblin at family gatherings.
Their new babysitter seemed unfazed by the chaos their crotch goblins created every afternoon.
