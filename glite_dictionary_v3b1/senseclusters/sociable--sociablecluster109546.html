sociable

noun
informal party or gathering
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəbl / (BrE)
a social gathering or informal party designed to encourage friendly interaction and communal activity

Examples

A sociable event
Everyone brought food to share at the annual spring sociable.
Our church is hosting a sociable next Friday evening for the whole community.
She enjoys organizing a sociable every month to bring her neighbors together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: party - a social gathering of invited guests
  • synonym: social - relating to society or its organization; an event involving people gathering together
