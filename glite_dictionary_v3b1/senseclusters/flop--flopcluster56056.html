flop

noun
act of collapsing or falling down
Pronunciation: / flɑp / (AmE), / flɒp / (BrE)
an act of falling, collapsing, or throwing oneself down with suddenness or force

Examples

A sudden flop
She made a dramatic flop onto the grass to watch the clouds.
The fish landed on the dock with a loud flop.
With a tired flop, he collapsed onto the sofa after work.
