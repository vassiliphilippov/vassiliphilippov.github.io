Synonyms

  • bundle up
    3.0 0.36/m
    bundle up means to wrap or tie several items together tightly, while bundle means to gather or group things into one package.
  • bunch
    1.2 2.52/m
    bunch means to gather things into a loose hand-held group, while bundle usually implies tying or wrapping them into one compact package.
  • cluster
    3.5 6.08/m
    cluster means things come or are placed close together, often naturally or in groups, while bundle suggests actively tying items together.
  • clump
    4.4 0.91/m
    clump describes things forming a thick, compact lump often by themselves, while bundle means deliberately tying or grouping items into one unit.
