cry cupboard

noun
private metaphorical space for emotions
Pronunciation: / kraɪ ˈkʌbɚd / (AmE), / kraɪ ˈkʌbəd / (BrE)
a private, metaphorical space used for storing emotions or as a place for emotional release

Examples

A cry cupboard space
After a tough day, she retreated to her cry cupboard to let out her feelings.
Everyone needs a cry cupboard to process life's challenges.
His cry cupboard is full of memories he finds hard to share.
