fascicle

noun
installment of a printed work
Pronunciation: / ˈfæ.sə.kəl / (AmE), / ˈfæs.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
one of the separately published installments of a book or other printed work

Examples

A fascicle of a book
Scholars eagerly awaited each new fascicle of the historical dictionary over several years.
The novel was originally released in monthly fascicles before being bound as a complete volume.
This fascicle contains chapters five through eight of the textbook.

Related Terms

  • synonym: volume - a book forming part of a larger work
