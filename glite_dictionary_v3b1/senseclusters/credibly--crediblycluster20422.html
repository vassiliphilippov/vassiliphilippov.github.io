In a way that can be believed or accepted as true; plausibly or convincingly based on evidence.

Examples

To credibly explain reasons
She credibly explained the reasons for her late arrival, so nobody doubted her story.
The company credibly claimed that its product was environmentally friendly.
The witness was able to answer every question credibly during the trial.

Synonyms

  • plausibly

  • believably
