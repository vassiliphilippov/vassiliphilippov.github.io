nightline

noun
overnight helpline
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a telephone service that provides help or support to people during the night, often for emotional or mental health needs

Examples

Nightline support call
She called the nightline when she felt lonely and needed someone to talk to.
The university offers a nightline for students who need support outside regular hours.
Volunteers answer calls on the nightline to help people through difficult nights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: helpline - a telephone service offering advice or support
  • synonym: hotline - a direct and immediate telephone line for emergency or support
