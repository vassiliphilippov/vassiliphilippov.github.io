quoth

verb
to say (archaic)
Pronunciation: / kwoʊθ / (AmE), / kwəʊθ / (BrE)
said; used in old stories and poems to indicate that someone spoke.

Examples

Quoth the raven
‘Nevermore,’ quoth the raven in the famous poem.
‘I will return by dawn,’ quoth the knight before riding into the darkness.
‘All is well,’ quoth the wise old man to the gathered villagers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: said - expressed in words; spoke
