proprietarian

adjective
favoring private property
9.9
Pronunciation: / prəˌpraɪəˈtɛriən / (AmE), / prəˌprʌɪəˈtɛːrɪən / (BrE)
relating to or supporting the belief that private property and ownership should be central to social or economic organization

Examples

Proprietarian ideas
The party's proprietarian stance influenced its policies on land distribution and taxation.
Many economists debate the merits of proprietarian principles in modern society.
Her proprietarian beliefs led her to oppose government intervention in private business.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collectivist - favoring collective ownership and control over property
  • synonym: libertarian - supporting individual freedom and minimal government intervention
