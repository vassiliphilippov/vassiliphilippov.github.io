overbright

adjective
excessively vivid
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈbraɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈbraɪt / (BrE)
having colors or tones that are unnaturally vivid or excessively intense

Examples

Overbright colors
The overbright lights in the store made it hard to see the true color of the clothes.
Some digital photos appear overbright when the saturation is set too high.
He found the overbright paint on the walls distracting and unnatural.
