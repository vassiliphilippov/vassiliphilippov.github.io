Combined DNA Index System

noun
FBI-maintained DNA database for forensics
Pronunciation: / kəmˈbaɪnd ˌdiːɛnˈeɪ ˈɪndɛks ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / kəmˈbaɪnd ˌdiːɛnˈeɪ ˈɪndɛks ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a computerized DNA database maintained by the Federal Bureau of Investigation for storing and comparing DNA profiles in criminal investigations
