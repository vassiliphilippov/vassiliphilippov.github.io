moo

verb
make a cow-like low sound
6.9
Pronunciation: / muː / (AmE), / muː / (BrE)
(of a cow or similar animal) to make a long, low sound characteristic of bovines.

Examples

Cows moo loudly
As we walked past the farm, a calf suddenly mooed at us.
She could hear the herd mooing contentedly in the fields.
The cows often moo loudly in the early morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: low - (of a cow) to make a deep sound
