musical time

noun
the beat or timing in music
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuzɪkəl taɪm / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl taɪm / (BrE)
the underlying pattern or beat of rhythm in music that organizes the timing of notes and measures

Examples

Musical time pattern
He nodded his head in musical time as he listened to the song.
If you lose the musical time, the whole performance can fall apart.
Learning to keep musical time is essential for playing in an orchestra.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rhythm - a strong, regular, repeated pattern of movement or sound in music
