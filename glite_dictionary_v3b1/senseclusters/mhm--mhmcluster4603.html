mhm

interjection
hesitation sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / m̩hm̩ / (AmE), / m̩hm̩ / (BrE)
a low vocal sound used to indicate thinking, hesitation, or that one is not yet ready to answer

Examples

Mhm while thinking
She paused for a moment and said mhm as she considered the question.
After hearing the suggestion, he replied with a thoughtful mhm before giving his opinion.
There was a long mhm on the phone before she finally answered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: um - a vocal sound expressing hesitation or uncertainty
