Louis Jacques Mande Daguerre

proper noun
French inventor of the daguerreotype
9.9
Pronunciation: / luːˈi ʒæk ˈmɑːnd deɪˈɡɛər / (AmE), / luːˈiː ʒak mɑ̃d daɡɛʁ / (BrE)
(1789–1851) French artist and inventor who developed the daguerreotype, the first practical photographic process.
