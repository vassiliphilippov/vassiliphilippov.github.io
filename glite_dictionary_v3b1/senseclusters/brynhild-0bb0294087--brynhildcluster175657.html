Brynhild

proper noun
Valkyrie or queen in Germanic legend
9.9

in Norse and Germanic mythology, a Valkyrie or queen who fell in love with Siegfried, later causing his death and taking her own life

Brynhild in mythology
In the epic poem, Brynhild is deceived by Siegfried, leading to a tragic sequence of events.

The story of Brynhild has inspired operas, novels, and countless retellings throughout European literature.
