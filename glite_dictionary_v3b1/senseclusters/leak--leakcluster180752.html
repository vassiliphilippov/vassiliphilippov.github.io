leak

noun
unauthorized disclosure of information
4.8 6.76/m
Pronunciation: / lik / (AmE), / liÀêk / (BrE)
an act of making secret or confidential information known to the public, especially without official approval

Examples

A news leak
After the leak of sensitive negotiations, the company launched an internal investigation.
He denied any involvement in the leak of personal information from the database.
The government was embarrassed by a leak of classified documents to the press.

Synonyms

  • disclosure
    4.1 13/m
    disclosure often means an official or planned revealing of information, while leak is an unauthorized secret release to the public.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cover-up - an attempt to prevent people from discovering information
  • synonym: disclosure - the action of making new or secret information known
  • synonym: revelation - the act of revealing or disclosing something, especially information
