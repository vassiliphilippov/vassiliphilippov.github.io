unabating

adjective
not decreasing
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈbeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈbeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
continuing without becoming weaker or less intense

Examples

Unabating enthusiasm
Despite the late hour, her unabating enthusiasm kept the team motivated.
The unabating noise from the construction site made it hard to concentrate.
He faced unabating pressure to finish the project on time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diminishing - becoming less or weaker
  • synonym: persistent - continuing firmly or for a long time
  • synonym: relentless - never stopping or becoming less intense
