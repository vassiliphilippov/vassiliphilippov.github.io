molding

noun
forming by casting in a mold
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊl.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈməʊl.dɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of forming a material or object by casting it in a mold

Examples

Molding process
Advances in technology have made the molding process faster and more precise.
The factory specializes in the molding of plastic components for automotive parts.
Their company has revolutionized the molding of lightweight metal structures.

Related Terms
