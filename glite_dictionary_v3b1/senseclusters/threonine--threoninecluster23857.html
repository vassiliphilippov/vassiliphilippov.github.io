threonine

noun
an essential amino acid found in proteins
Pronunciation: / ˈθriːəˌniːn / (AmE), / ˈθriːəniːn / (BrE)
an essential amino acid that is a constituent of most proteins and is necessary for human nutrition

Examples

Dietary sources rich in threonine include eggs, fish, and dairy products.
People with threonine deficiency may experience problems with their immune system.
