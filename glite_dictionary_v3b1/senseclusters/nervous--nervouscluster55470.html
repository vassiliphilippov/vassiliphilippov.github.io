nervous

adjective
relating to the nervous system
4.2 9.15/m
Pronunciation: / Ààn…úrv…ôs / (AmE), / Ààn…úÀêv…ôs / (BrE)

Examples

Nervous system

Synonyms

    4.9 8.51/m

  • neurological
    6.3 5.53/m
    neurological is used in medicine for the study and disorders of the nervous system, while nervous is a general word about the body's nerves.
