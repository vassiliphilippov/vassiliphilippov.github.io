terraform

verb
to make land habitable
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrəˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈtɛrəˌfɔːm / (BrE)
to change the environment of a place (such as a planet, moon, or area on Earth) to make it suitable for human life
