written symbol

noun
character or mark used in writing
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtən ˈsɪmbəl / (AmE), / ˈrɪtən ˈsɪmbəl / (BrE)
a mark, letter, or character used in writing or printing to represent a sound, word, or meaning

Examples

A written symbol in alphabet
Every language has its own unique set of written symbols in its alphabet.
The ampersand is a written symbol that represents the word 'and'.
The mathematician used a special written symbol to denote infinity.

Related Terms
