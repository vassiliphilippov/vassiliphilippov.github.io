sugar bomb

noun
high-sugar food or drink

Pronunciation: /ˈʃʊ.ɡər bɑːm/ /; / /ˈʃʊ.ɡər bɔːm/ (AmE), /ˈʃʊ.ɡə bɒm/ (BrE)
