extensity

noun
degree of extent
8.8
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛnsəti / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnsəti / (BrE)
the degree, range, or scope to which something extends or covers an area, often used figuratively

Examples

Extensity of the law
The extensity of the new law surprised many citizens across the country.
Researchers measured the extensity of the forest to assess its environmental impact.
She was impressed by the extensity of his knowledge on the subject.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extent - the area covered by something; the range or scope
  • antonym: limitation - a restriction or boundary on the range or scope of something
  • synonym: scope - the range of something's coverage or influence
