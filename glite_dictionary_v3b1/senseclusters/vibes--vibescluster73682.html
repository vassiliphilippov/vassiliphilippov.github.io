vibes

noun
percussion instrument like a vibraphone
Pronunciation: / vaɪbz / (AmE), / vaɪbz / (BrE)
A percussion instrument similar to a xylophone, but with metal bars and resonators containing rotating disks to produce a vibrato effect; short for vibraphone.

Examples

Playing the vibes
She has been practicing the vibes for years and can play difficult pieces fluently.
The jazz solo featured a brilliant passage on the vibes that captivated the audience.
You can hear the mellow sound of the vibes in many classic jazz recordings.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: xylophone - A percussion instrument with wooden bars struck by mallets
