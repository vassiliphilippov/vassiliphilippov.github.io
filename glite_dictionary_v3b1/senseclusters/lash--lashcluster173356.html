lash

noun
a quick blow with a whip
6.8
Pronunciation: / læʃ / (AmE), / læʃ / (BrE)
A quick, sharp blow with a whip or a whiplike object.

Examples

A sharp lash
The harsh lash left a bright red mark on the table.
The prisoner winced as another lash struck his back.
With every lash of the whip, the horse became more agitated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stroke - a single unbroken movement, especially when hitting or striking
