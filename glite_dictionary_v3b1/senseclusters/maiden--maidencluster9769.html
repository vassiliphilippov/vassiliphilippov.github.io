maiden

adjective
happening for the first time
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdn̩ / (BrE)
(of an action or event) happening for the first time; serving to set something in motion.

Examples

Maiden flight launched
She gave her maiden speech at the annual conference last year.
The artist's maiden exhibition attracted a lot of attention from critics.
The ship's maiden voyage was cause for much celebration.

Related Terms

  • antonym: final - occurring at the end; last in a series
  • synonym: inaugural - first in a series, marking the beginning of something
