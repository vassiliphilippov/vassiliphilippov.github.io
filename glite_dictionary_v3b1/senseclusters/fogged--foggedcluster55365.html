fogged

adjective
obscured by fog or condensation
1.0
Pronunciation: / fɔgd / (AmE), / fɒgd / (BrE)
(of a surface, especially glass) made difficult to see through by condensation or fog.

Examples

Fogged mirror
He wiped the fogged mirror after his shower to see his reflection.
She pressed her face to the fogged glass, trying to glimpse the garden outside.
The fogged windshield made it hard to drive safely in the morning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - easy to see through, not obscured
  • synonym: misty - blurred or made unclear by mist
