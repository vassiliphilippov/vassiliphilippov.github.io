bling

verb
decorate in a flashy way
8.9
Pronunciation: /blɪŋ/ (AmE), /blɪŋ/ (BrE)
(informal, slang) to decorate, outfit, or upgrade something in a showy or ostentatious way, often using shiny or expensive-looking accessories.

Examples

To bling out a car
They bling out their car with chrome rims and neon lights for the weekend show.
For the festival she blinged her phone case with rhinestones and tiny metal studs.
Local artists enjoy blinging old guitars to create one-of-a-kind stage instruments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: customize - to change or add to something to make it suitable for a particular person or purpose
  • synonym: embellish - to decorate or add detail to make something more attractive or showy
