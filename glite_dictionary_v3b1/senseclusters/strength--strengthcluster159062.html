strength

noun
military personnel and equipment capacity
Pronunciation: / strɛŋkθ / (AmE), / strɛŋθ / (BrE)
the number of personnel and amount of equipment and resources that determine the fighting capacity of a military force

Examples

Military strength
Allied forces combined their strength to launch a major offensive.
At full strength, the navy could deploy over a hundred ships in the region.
The army increased its strength by recruiting thousands of new soldiers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: force - a group of people organized for a particular purpose, especially military action
