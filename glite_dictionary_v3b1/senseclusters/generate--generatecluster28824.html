generate

verb
produce offspring by reproduction
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛnəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnəreɪt / (BrE)
(of a person, animal, or plant) to produce offspring by reproduction.

Examples

Generate new offspring
In rare cases, a single plant may generate a new lineage through self-fertilization.
Some species of fish are able to generate hundreds of offspring in a single season.
The ancient myth claimed that gods could generate children with mortals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beget - (of a man) to father or produce offspring
  • synonym: procreate - to produce young; to reproduce
  • antonym: sterilize - to make (a person or animal) unable to produce offspring
