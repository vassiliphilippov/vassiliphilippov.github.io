charter member

noun
original member of an organization
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːr.tɚ ˈmɛm.bɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑː.tə ˈmɛm.bə / (BrE)
one of the original members at the founding of an organization, club, or society

Examples

A charter member
As a charter member, she helped establish the group's original mission and values.
He was proud to be a charter member of the local art society.
The club presented awards to its remaining charter members at the anniversary celebration.
