pilotless

adjective
without a human pilot
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪ.lət.ləs / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ.lət.ləs / (BrE)
(of a vehicle, especially an aircraft or spacecraft) operating or designed to operate without a human pilot onboard.

Examples

Pilotless aircraft
Modern technology has led to the development of pilotless cargo planes.
Scientists launched a pilotless spacecraft to study the comet.
The military tested a new pilotless drone during the exercise.

  • synonym: unmanned - (of a machine or vehicle) operated without a person on board
