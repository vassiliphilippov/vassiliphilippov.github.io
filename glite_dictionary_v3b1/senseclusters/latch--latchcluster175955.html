latch

noun
spring-loaded doorlock requiring a key
1.0
Pronunciation: / lætʃ / (AmE), / lætʃ / (BrE)
a device for fastening or securing a door, gate, or window

Examples

Door latch
He fumbled with the key, trying to open the latch on the front door.
Make sure that the latch is locked before you leave the house.
The old garden gate has a rusty latch that sticks in cold weather.
Before leaving, she slid the latch into place to secure the gate.
He heard the latch click as the door closed behind him.
The old wooden chest had a rusty latch that was difficult to open.
