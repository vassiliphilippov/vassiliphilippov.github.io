sponge mushroom

noun
edible morel mushroom with a pitted head
Pronunciation: / spʌndʒ ˈmʌʃrʊm / (AmE), / spʌndʒ ˈmʌʃrəm / (BrE)
an edible morel with a rounded or elongated head marked by an irregular pattern of pits and ridges
