unsustained

adjective
not upheld
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsəˈsteɪnd / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəˈsteɪnd / (BrE)
not supported, proven, or upheld after examination or investigation

Examples

Unsustained claim
The judge ruled that the allegations were unsustained due to lack of evidence.
Her claim remained unsustained after a thorough investigation by the authorities.
The report was dismissed as unsustained because it could not be verified.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sustained - supported or upheld, especially after review
  • synonym: unsubstantiated - not supported by evidence or proof
  • synonym: unsupported - not given support or backing
