reemphasis

noun
renewed emphasis
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɛm.fə.sɪs / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɛm.fə.sɪs / (BrE)

Examples

The reemphasis on rules

After the incident, there was a clear reemphasis on following safety procedures.
The campaign included a reemphasis on the importance of healthy eating habits.
