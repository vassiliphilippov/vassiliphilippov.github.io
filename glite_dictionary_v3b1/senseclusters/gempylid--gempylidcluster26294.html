gempylid

noun
a snake mackerel; fish of the Gempylidae family
Pronunciation: / dʒɛmˈpɪlɪd / (AmE), / dʒɛmˈpɪlɪd / (BrE)
A marine fish of the family Gempylidae, known as snake mackerels, characterized by their elongated bodies and oily flesh, resembling mackerels; found worldwide.

Examples

A large gempylid fish
A large gempylid was caught by scientists during a deep-sea expedition.
Research on the gempylid family has revealed several newly discovered species.
The gempylid is prized in some countries for its rich, oily flesh.
