bikini

noun
woman's two-piece bathing suit
Pronunciation: / bɪˈkini / (AmE), / bɪˈkiːni / (BrE)
A woman's two-piece bathing suit that reveals the midriff and much of the body.

Examples

A bikini swimsuit
He bought his sister a new bikini for her vacation.
Models walked the runway in designer bikinis during the fashion show.
She wore a bright red bikini to the beach party.

Related Terms

  • antonym: one-piece - a type of swimsuit made from a single piece of fabric covering the torso
  • synonym: swimsuit - a garment worn for swimming
