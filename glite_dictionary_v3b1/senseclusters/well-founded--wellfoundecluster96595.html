well-founded

adjective
supported by evidence or sound reasoning
5.8 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈwel.faʊn.dɪd/ /; / /wel ˈfaʊn.dɪd/ (AmE), /ˈwel.faʊn.dɪd/ /; / /wel ˈfaʊn.dɪd/ (BrE)

Examples
