endocrine system

noun
hormone-producing bodily gland system
3.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈen.dəʊ.kraɪn ˈsɪs.təm/ /; / /ˈen.doʊ.krɪn ˈsɪs.təm/ (AmE), /ˈen.dəʊ.krɪn ˈsɪs.təm/ /; / /ˈen.dəʊ.kraɪn ˈsɪs.təm/ (BrE)
