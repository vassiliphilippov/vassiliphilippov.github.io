prewarming

adjective
heated in advance
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈwɔrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈwɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to warming something in advance, especially to prepare it before a main procedure or use

Examples

Prewarming blankets
The prewarming process helps reduce the risk of hypothermia during surgery.
A prewarming oven ensures that food cooks evenly from the start.
Using a prewarming blanket, the medical team prepared the patient for the operation.
