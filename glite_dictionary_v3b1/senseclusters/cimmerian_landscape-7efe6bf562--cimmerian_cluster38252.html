Cimmerian landscape

noun
very dark or gloomy landscape
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˈmɪriən ˈlændˌskeɪp / (AmE), / sɪˈmɪəriən ˈlændskeɪp / (BrE)
A landscape that is extremely dark, gloomy, or shadowy, often evoking an atmosphere of mystery or foreboding.

Examples

A Cimmerian landscape of shadows
As the sun set, the valley transformed into a Cimmerian landscape of endless shadows.
Fog drifted over the Cimmerian landscape, hiding the crumbling ruins from view.
The novel's setting is a Cimmerian landscape that perfectly captures the protagonist's despair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shadowy - full of shadows; dark; indistinct
