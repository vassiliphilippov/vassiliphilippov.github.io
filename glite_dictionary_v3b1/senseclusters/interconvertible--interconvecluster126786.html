interconvertible

adjective
mutually changeable
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərkənˈvɝːtəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəkənˈvɜːtəbəl / (BrE)
able to be changed or converted into each other

Examples

Interconvertible tickets
The two types of tickets are interconvertible and can be exchanged at the counter.
In some countries, the local currency and the US dollar are interconvertible at a set rate.
These chemical compounds are interconvertible under certain laboratory conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convertible - able to be changed into a different form or use
  • synonym: exchangeable - able to be replaced or traded for something else
  • antonym: fixed - not able to be changed or exchanged
