chez

preposition
among or with (French)
9.7
Pronunciation: /ʃeɪ/ /; / /tʃez/ (AmE), /ʃeɪ/ (BrE)
used before a person or group to mean 'among', 'with', or 'in the home or opinion of' that person or group, especially in reference to customs or beliefs.

Examples

Customs chez the family
The tradition is still strong chez the Smith family, who celebrate every year.
This style of cooking is popular chez the French.
The idea was well received chez her colleagues at the university.

Related Terms

  • synonym: among - in the company of; surrounded by
  • synonym: with - accompanied by; in the presence of
