pycnium

noun
fungal reproductive structure
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɪkˈniːəm / (AmE), / pɪkˈniːəm / (BrE)
a flask-shaped structure in certain fungi where reproductive cells are produced

Examples

A pycnium structure
The researcher identified a pycnium on the infected plant tissue under the microscope.
Each pycnium contains specialized cells that play a role in fungal reproduction.
The formation of a pycnium is an important stage in the life cycle of rust fungi.
