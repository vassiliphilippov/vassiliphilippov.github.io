upper-class

adjective
of the highest social or economic class
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpɚ klæs / (AmE), / ˌʌpə ˈklɑːs / (BrE)
belonging to or characteristic of the group in society with the highest social or economic status

Examples

Upper-class society
He grew up in an upper-class neighborhood with exclusive schools and private clubs.
Her upper-class accent set her apart at the dinner party.
The upper-class lifestyle often includes luxury cars and expensive holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aristocratic - relating to the aristocracy or noble class
  • antonym: lower-class - having a low social or economic status
  • degree lower: middle-class - having a moderate amount of wealth and status
