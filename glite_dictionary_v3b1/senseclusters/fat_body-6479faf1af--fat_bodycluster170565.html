fat body

noun
overweight or plump body
3.6
Pronunciation: / fæt ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / fæt ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
a body that is noticeably overweight or plump; a physique with excess body fat

Examples

A fat body
His fat body made it difficult for him to run long distances.
She was self-conscious about her fat body but learned to embrace it.
The puppy's fat body wobbled as it played on the grass.

Related Terms

  • antonym: slim body - a body that is slender and not overweight
  • antonym: thin body - a body that is lacking in fat or is underweight
