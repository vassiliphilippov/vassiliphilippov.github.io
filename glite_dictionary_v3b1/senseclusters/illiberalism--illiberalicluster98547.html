illiberalism

noun
intolerance of differences
8.9
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈlɪ.bər.ə.lɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈlɪ.bə.rə.lɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
an attitude or practice of being unwilling to accept different opinions, ideas, or ways of life; intolerance or narrow-mindedness

Examples

Social illiberalism
The rise of illiberalism in some countries has led to restrictions on free speech.
Many activists spoke out against the illiberalism shown by the new government policies.
Educational institutions should guard against illiberalism to encourage open discussion and learning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intolerance - unwillingness to accept views, beliefs, or behavior that differ from one's own
  • antonym: liberalism - willingness to accept different opinions and ways of life
  • synonym: narrow-mindedness - lack of willingness to consider alternative ideas or opinions
