soundman

noun
audio technician for media productions
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊndˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈsaʊndˌman / (BrE)
A person responsible for managing and amplifying sound or producing sound effects, especially for television, radio, or live events.

Examples

The soundman adjusts equipment
During the live broadcast, the soundman quickly fixed an issue with the audio feed.
She thanked the soundman for ensuring the band sounded great on stage.
The soundman adjusted the microphones before the concert began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sound engineer - person responsible for recording, mixing, and reproducing sound
