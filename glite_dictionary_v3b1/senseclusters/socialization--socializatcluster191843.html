Synonyms

  • nationalization
    7.0 1.51/m
    nationalization is taking private businesses into state ownership, often for public or economic reasons, while socialization follows socialist principles of state control.
  • collectivization
    9.6 0.35/m
    collectivization is organizing farms or industry for collective ownership, often in agriculture or communities, while socialization means state ownership under socialist principles.
