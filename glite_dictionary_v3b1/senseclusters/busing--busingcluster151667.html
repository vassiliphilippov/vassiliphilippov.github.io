busing

noun
transporting by bus
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʌsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of transporting people, especially students, by bus from one place to another

Examples

School busing
Busing is provided for all students who live more than two miles from the school.
The city expanded busing services to help reduce traffic congestion during rush hour.
Many parents rely on busing to ensure their children arrive at school safely each morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transportation - the act of moving people or goods from one place to another
