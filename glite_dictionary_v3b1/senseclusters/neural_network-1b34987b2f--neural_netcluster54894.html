neural network

noun
computer system modeled on the brain
4.8 0.32/m
Pronunciation: / Ààn är…ôl Ààn…õtÀåw…úrk / (AmE), / Àànj ä…ôr…ôl Ààn…õtw…úÀêk / (BrE)
A computer system inspired by the structure of the human brain, consisting of interconnected processors that can learn and make decisions.

Examples

Artificial neural network
A neural network can identify patterns in large sets of data far more efficiently than traditional algorithms.
Modern voice assistants rely on complex neural networks to understand and respond to speech.
Scientists use a neural network to recognize handwritten characters on scanned documents.
