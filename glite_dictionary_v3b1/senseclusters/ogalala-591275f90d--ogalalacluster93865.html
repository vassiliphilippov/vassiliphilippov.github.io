Ogalala

noun
member of a Teton Sioux people
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊɡəˈlɑːlə / (AmE), / ˌəʊɡəˈlɑːlə / (BrE)
A member of the Siouan people forming a division of the Teton Sioux, historically living in the Black Hills region of western South Dakota.

Examples

An Ogalala elder
An Ogalala elder shared traditional stories with the children of the tribe.
Many Ogalala have worked to preserve their language and customs.
She traced her ancestry back to the Ogalala who once roamed the Black Hills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Sioux - a group of Native American peoples, including the Ogalala
