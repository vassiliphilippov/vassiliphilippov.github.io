madtom

noun
small North American catfish
Pronunciation: / ˈmædˌtɑm / (AmE), / ˈmædˌtɒm / (BrE)
a small North American freshwater catfish of the genus Noturus, often found in streams and rivers

Examples

A madtom fish
The scientist identified the small fish as a madtom after examining its fins.
Anglers sometimes catch a madtom while fishing in rocky streams.
The madtom is known for hiding under stones during the day.
