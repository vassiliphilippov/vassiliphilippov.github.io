blood sausage

noun
sausage made with animal blood
5.7
Pronunciation: / blʌd ˈsɑsɪdʒ / (AmE), / blʌd ˈsɒsɪdʒ / (BrE)
A type of sausage made by cooking and mixing animal blood (usually pig's), fat, and other ingredients, typically having a dark color.

Examples

A blood sausage dish
In many European countries, blood sausage is served as part of a hearty breakfast.
My grandmother makes homemade blood sausage every winter using a traditional family recipe.
Some people are hesitant to try blood sausage because of its unusual ingredients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: black pudding - a type of blood sausage, especially in Britain and Ireland
