releasing hormone

noun
hormone from hypothalamus that stimulates pituitary hormone release
2.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlisɪŋ ˈhɔrˌmoʊn / (AmE), / rɪˈliːsɪŋ ˈhɔːməʊn / (BrE)
a hormone produced by the hypothalamus that stimulates the release of specific hormones from the anterior pituitary gland
