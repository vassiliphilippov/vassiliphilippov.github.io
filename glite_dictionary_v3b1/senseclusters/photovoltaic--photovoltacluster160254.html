photovoltaic

adjective
producing electricity from light
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtəˌvoʊlˈteɪɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊvɒlˈteɪɪk / (BrE)
(of a device or material) producing a voltage or electric current when exposed to light or other radiant energy.

Examples

Photovoltaic cell
In a photovoltaic cell, sunlight is converted directly into usable electricity.
Many new homes are being built with photovoltaic panels on their roofs to generate power.
The photovoltaic effect is key to the operation of solar energy systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: solar cell - a device that converts light directly into electricity
