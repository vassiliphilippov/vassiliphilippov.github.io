red-tapism

noun
excessive bureaucracy causing delays
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛd ˌteɪˌpɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈrɛd ˌteɪ.pɪ.zəm / (BrE)
The practice or tendency of adhering excessively to official rules and formalities, often causing unnecessary delay or hindrance.

Examples

Reduce red-tapism
Efforts to reduce red-tapism have aimed to streamline administrative procedures.
Many foreign investors are discouraged by the red-tapism plaguing the country's bureaucracy.
The new company struggled to get its license approved due to endless red-tapism.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: inefficiency - lack of efficiency, especially in organizations
  • synonym: obstruction - something that impedes or blocks progress
