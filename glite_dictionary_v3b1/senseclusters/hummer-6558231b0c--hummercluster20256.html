Hummer

proper noun
SUV brand
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmər / (AmE), / ˈhʌmə / (BrE)
a brand of large, rugged sport utility vehicles known for their size, off-road ability, and distinctive appearance

Examples

Hummer SUV
He proudly parked his new Hummer in front of the house for everyone to see.
The Hummer was designed to handle rough terrain and challenging conditions.
Many people recognize a Hummer by its boxy shape and loud engine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Jeep - a brand of rugged vehicles often used for off-road driving
  • synonym: SUV - a sport utility vehicle, a large car designed for both on-road and off-road use
