migration

noun
seasonal movement of animals
Pronunciation: / maɪˈɡreɪʃən / (AmE), / maɪˈɡreɪʃən / (BrE)
the regular movement of groups of animals from one region to another, particularly for feeding or breeding purposes

Examples

Bird migration
Scientists are studying the migration patterns of Arctic terns.
The annual migration of monarch butterflies covers thousands of miles.
Whale migration brings these creatures close to the shore every winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emigration - the act of leaving one place to settle in another, especially of people
  • synonym: immigration - the act of coming to live permanently in a foreign region or country
