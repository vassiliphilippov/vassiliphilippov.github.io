Tejano

noun
Texan of Mexican descent
Pronunciation: / teɪˈhɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / teɪˈhɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
a person of Mexican descent living in Texas, or relating to the culture of such people

Examples

Tejano community in Texas
The museum features art created by Tejano artists from across the state.
Many Tejanos celebrate their heritage with traditional music and dance.
She is proud to be a Tejano and shares her family's history with her children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Chicano - a person of Mexican origin or descent living in the United States
  • synonym: Texan - a person from Texas
