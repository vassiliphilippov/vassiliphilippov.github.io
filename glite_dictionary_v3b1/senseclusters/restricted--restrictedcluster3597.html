restricted

adjective
lowest level of document classification
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈstrɪktɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈstrɪktɪd / (BrE)
limited or controlled in amount, access, or availability; not freely available (sometimes used officially for documents or information)

Examples
