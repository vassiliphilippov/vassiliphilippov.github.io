scalloper

noun
scalloping tool
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈskæləpər / (AmE), / ˈskæləpə / (BrE)
a tool or machine used to cut or shape a scalloped decorative edge on materials such as fabric, paper, metal, or food

Examples

A scalloper tool
The tailor used a scalloper to create a decorative edge on the dress hem.
A scalloper can add a beautiful finish to paper invitations for special events.
Chefs sometimes use a scalloper to give vegetables an attractive, wavy edge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cutter - a tool used for cutting materials
  • synonym: pinking shears - scissors with sawtoothed blades for cutting decorative edges
