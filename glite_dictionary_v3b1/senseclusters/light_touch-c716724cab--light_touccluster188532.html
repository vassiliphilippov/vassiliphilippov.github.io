light touch

noun
gentle or brief contact
Pronunciation: / laɪt tʌtʃ / (AmE), / laɪt tʌtʃ / (BrE)
a gentle or brief physical contact

Examples

A light touch on keys
A light touch on the piano keys produced a delicate sound.
He woke up at the light touch of the morning sun on his face.
She felt a light touch on her arm as he walked by.
