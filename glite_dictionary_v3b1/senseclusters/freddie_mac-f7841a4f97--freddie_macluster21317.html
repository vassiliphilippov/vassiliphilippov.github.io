Freddie Mac

proper noun
US mortgage corporation
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfre.dɪ mæk/ (AmE), /ˈfre.dɪ mæk/ (BrE)
A US government-sponsored corporation that purchases residential mortgages and resells them as securities to investors.
