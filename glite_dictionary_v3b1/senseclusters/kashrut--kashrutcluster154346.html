kashrut

noun
Jewish dietary laws
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɑʃˈrut / (AmE), / kæʃˈruːt /; / kɑːʃˈruːt / (BrE)
the Jewish dietary laws that determine which foods are allowed and how they must be prepared and eaten (the system of what is kosher)
