kashrut

noun
Jewish dietary laws
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkæ.ʃruːt/ /; / /ˈkɑː.ʃruːt/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.ʃruːt/ /; / /ˈkæ.ʃruːt/ (BrE)
