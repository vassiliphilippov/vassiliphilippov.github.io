systematist

noun
a person who organizes things systematically
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstəmətɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəmətɪst / (BrE)
a person who arranges or organizes things into a system or order

Examples

A systematist of ideas
As a systematist, he brought structure to what had previously been a chaotic collection of ideas.
Marie was a systematist who always created clear outlines for every project.
Throughout history, the most influential scientists have often been effective systematists.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disorganizer - a person who creates disorder, the opposite of a systematist
  • synonym: organizer - a person who arranges or structures things
