say

verb
speak or pronounce words
Pronunciation: / seɪ / (AmE), / seɪ / (BrE)
to express, state, or communicate something in words (to speak, declare, recite, or give instructions)

Examples

To say a word
Can you say all the letters in your name clearly?
I cannot say this tongue twister without stumbling.
She tried to say the new French word correctly several times.
Experts say that regular exercise can help improve your mood.
She says the package arrived yesterday afternoon.
The government spokesperson said that new policies would be introduced next year.
If you don’t understand the instructions, just say so.
She said ‘Hello’ to everyone in the office.
The teacher asked us to say our names aloud.
