Synonyms

  • tell
    1.0 686/m
    tell is used when speaking directly to someone to give information, while say is more general and needs no listener.
  • state
    1.6 122/m
    state is for clearly or formally presenting facts in speech or writing, while say is more casual and wide in use.
  • express
    3.9 1.52/m
    express often shows feelings or ideas and can be nonverbal, while say means putting words into speech.
