boatload

noun
amount a boat can carry
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊtˌloʊd / (AmE), / ˈbəʊt.ləʊd / (BrE)
The amount of cargo or people that can be carried by a boat or ship.

Examples

A boatload of passengers
A boatload of tourists arrived at the island each week during the summer.
The company imported a boatload of steel from overseas.
They brought in a boatload of fresh fish early this morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carload - the amount of cargo that can be carried by a freight car
