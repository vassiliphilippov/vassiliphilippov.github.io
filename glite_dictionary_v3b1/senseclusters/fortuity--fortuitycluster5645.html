fortuity

noun
an event happening by chance
7.1
Pronunciation: / fɔˈtuːəti / (AmE), / fɔːˈtjuːəti / (BrE)
an event that happens by chance, especially one that is unexpected or without apparent cause

Examples

Sheer fortuity
It was sheer fortuity that they met at the airport after so many years.
Some discoveries in science result from deliberate effort, while others come about through fortuity.
The success of the project was a remarkable fortuity that no one had anticipated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accident - an unfortunate event that happens unexpectedly and unintentionally
  • antonym: certainty - something certain to happen or a state of being definite
  • synonym: coincidence - a remarkable concurrence of events or circumstances without apparent causal connection
