nomina

noun
scientific names (plural)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːmɪnə / (AmE), / ˈnɒmɪnə / (BrE)
the plural form of 'nomen', referring to scientific names used in biological classification, especially in taxonomy

Examples

Animal nomina
Researchers compared the nomina of several related animal species to clarify their classification.
The database contains thousands of nomina for plants, fungi, and microorganisms.
Taxonomists must follow strict rules when creating new nomina for discovered species.
