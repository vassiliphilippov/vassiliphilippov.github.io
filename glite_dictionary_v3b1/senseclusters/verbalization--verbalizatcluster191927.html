verbalization

noun
AI-generated natural-language explanations
8.1
Pronunciation: /vɜːr.bə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /vɜː.bə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
(in computing and AI) the generation of human-readable natural-language explanations or descriptions by a system to communicate its data-driven reasoning, decisions, or outputs.

Examples

AI verbalization of results

Automated verbalisation of analytics enables nontechnical staff to understand model outputs.
