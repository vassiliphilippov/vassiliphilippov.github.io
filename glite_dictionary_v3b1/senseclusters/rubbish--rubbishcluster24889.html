rubbish

noun
waste or unwanted material
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌb.ɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrʌb.ɪʃ / (BrE)
waste material; things that are no longer wanted or are considered worthless and are to be thrown away.

Examples

Throw out rubbish
It's amazing how much rubbish people throw away every day.
Please put all your rubbish in the bin before you leave.
The park was cleaned up and all the rubbish was collected by volunteers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: garbage - waste material, especially food waste; unwanted material to be disposed of
  • synonym: trash - waste material; things that are thrown away
