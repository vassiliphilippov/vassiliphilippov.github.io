trichion

noun
anatomical point at the hairline midpoint on the forehead
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪkiˌɑn / (AmE), / ˈtrɪkɪɒn / (BrE)
the point on the forehead where the hairline meets its midpoint, used as a reference in anatomy
