regular maintenance

noun
routine scheduled upkeep
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛgjələr ˈmeɪntənəns / (AmE), / ˈrɛgjʊlə ˈmeɪntənəns / (BrE)
the routine and scheduled upkeep of equipment, machines, or a system to ensure proper functioning and prevent problems

Examples

Regular maintenance schedule
All vehicles require regular maintenance to run efficiently and safely.
Scheduling regular maintenance helps prevent unexpected equipment breakdowns.
The landlord is responsible for regular maintenance of the building's heating system.
