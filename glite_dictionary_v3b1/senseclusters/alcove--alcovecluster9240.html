alcove

noun
recessed area in a room
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈælˌkoʊv / (AmE), / ˈæl.kəʊv / (BrE)
a small recessed section of a room, often used for a bed, seating, or shelves

Examples

A alcove in room
Bookshelves filled the alcove next to the fireplace.

They placed the bed in a cozy alcove near the window.
