Battle of Solferino

proper noun
1859 battle in Italy between French/Sardinians and Austrians
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬l əv ˌsoʊlfəˈriːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˌsɒlfəˈriːnəʊ / (BrE)
an indecisive battle fought in 1859 between the French and Sardinian armies under Napoleon III and the Austrians under Francis Joseph I, crucial in the Second Italian War of Independence

Examples

The Battle of Solferino 1859
Many soldiers lost their lives during the Battle of Solferino in 1859.
The Battle of Solferino was a turning point in the struggle for Italian unification.
The horrors witnessed at the Battle of Solferino inspired the creation of the Red Cross.
