Erignathus barbatus

noun
species of Arctic bearded seal
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛrɪɡˈneɪθəs bɑːrˈbeɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌɛrɪɡˈnɑːθəs bɑːˈbɑːtəs / (BrE)
A species of seal, commonly known as the bearded seal, found in the Arctic Ocean, medium-sized and grayish to yellow with bristles on each side of its muzzle.
