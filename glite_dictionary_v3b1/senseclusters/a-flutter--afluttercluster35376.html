a-flutter

adjective
in a state of excitement or nervousness
5.2
Pronunciation: / əˈflʌt.ɚ / (AmE), / əˈflʌt.ə / (BrE)
Feeling excited or nervous; in a state of quivering anticipation or agitation.

Examples

Heart a-flutter
Her heart was a-flutter as she waited for the results.
My stomach was a-flutter before the big presentation.
The crowd was a-flutter with anticipation as the band took the stage.
