Evernia

noun
perfumery ingredient (oakmoss extract)
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈev.ər.nɪ.ə/ /; / /ˈev.ər.niː.ə/ (AmE), /ˈev.ə.nɪ.ə/ /; / /ˈev.ə.niː.ə/ (BrE)
(in perfumery) an extract or ingredient obtained from the lichen Evernia prunastri, used to give fragrances oakmoss, mossy, or woody notes.

Examples

Evernia extract in perfume
Perfume houses often add Evernia to give fragrances a deep, earthy oakmoss note.
Regulations now limit the use of Evernia extracts in some perfumes because of allergen concerns.
The perfumer blended amber and bergamot with Evernia to create a classic chypre accord.
