sailing adventure

noun
recreational journey by boat
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlɪŋ ədˈvɛntʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈseɪlɪŋ ədˈvɛntʃə / (BrE)
a recreational journey made by boat, typically involving exploration or travel for pleasure rather than competition

Examples

A sailing adventure trip
After months of planning, their epic sailing adventure finally began at dawn.
Her first sailing adventure taught her the basics of navigation and teamwork at sea.
They dreamed of a sailing adventure around the Greek islands during their summer vacation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruise - a journey on a ship for pleasure
  • synonym: voyage - a long journey by sea or in space
