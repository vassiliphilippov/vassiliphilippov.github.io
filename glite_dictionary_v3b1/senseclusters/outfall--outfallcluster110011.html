outfall

noun
the outlet of a river or drain
2.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈaʊt.fɔːl/ /; / /ˈaʊt.fɑːl/ (AmE), /ˈaʊt.fɔːl/ (BrE)
the place where a river, drain, or sewer discharges its water into another body of water

Examples
