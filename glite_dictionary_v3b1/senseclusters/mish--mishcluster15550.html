mish

noun
informal mistake
Pronunciation: / mɪʃ / (AmE), / mɪʃ / (BrE)
an informal term for a mistake, mess, or confusing situation

Examples

A big mish
There was a huge mish with the travel plans, and we missed our flight.
After the mish in the kitchen, dinner was delayed by an hour.
He laughed about the mish during the presentation and quickly fixed the error.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunder - a careless or serious mistake
  • synonym: mess - a situation that is confused or full of problems
  • antonym: success - the achievement of a desired result
