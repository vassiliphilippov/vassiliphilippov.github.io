crowd

noun
informal group of friends
1.0
Pronunciation: / kraʊd / (AmE), / kraʊd / (BrE)
a group of friends or acquaintances with whom someone regularly spends time

Examples

His usual crowd

In college, she joined a new crowd who shared her interests.
