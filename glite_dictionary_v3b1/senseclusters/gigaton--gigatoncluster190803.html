gigaton

noun
mass unit, billion tons
4.4 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈgɪg.ə.tən/ (AmE), /ˈgɪg.ə.tən/ (BrE)
a unit of mass equal to one billion (1,000,000,000) metric tons, often used to measure very large quantities such as carbon emissions, ice loss, or energy
