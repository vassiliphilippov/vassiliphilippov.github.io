bouffy dress

noun
voluminous, puffy style of dress
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːfi drɛs / (AmE), / ˈbuːfi drɛs / (BrE)
A dress with a voluminous, puffy shape, often featuring layers or fabric that create a rounded silhouette.

Examples

A bouffy dress style
In the photograph, the bride smiled joyfully in her vintage bouffy dress from the 1950s.
She wore a bouffy dress to the party, which made her feel like a princess.
The designer's latest collection features a signature bouffy dress in pastel colors.
