casing

noun
frame around a door or window opening
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪ.sɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.sɪŋ / (BrE)
the frame or trim that encloses and finishes the edges around a door or window opening

Examples

Door casing
After years of exposure to moisture, the casing on the back door had to be replaced.
The carpenter carefully measured the opening before installing the new casing.
The wooden casing around the window was painted white to match the walls.
