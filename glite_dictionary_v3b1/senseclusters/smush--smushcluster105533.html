smush

noun
soft, messy mass
Pronunciation: / smʊʃ / (AmE), / smʊʃ / (BrE)
a soft, shapeless mass or a messy mixture of crushed or mashed material

Examples

A sticky smush
The dropped cake turned into a sticky smush on the kitchen floor.
After the rain, the playground was covered in a muddy smush of leaves and dirt.
She found a smush of melted chocolate at the bottom of her bag.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mash - a soft mass made by crushing or mixing
  • synonym: sludge - a thick, soft, wet mixture
