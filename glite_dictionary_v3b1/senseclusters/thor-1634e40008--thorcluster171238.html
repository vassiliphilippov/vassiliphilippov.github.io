Thor

proper noun
Norse god of thunder and rain
Pronunciation: / θɔr / (AmE), / θɔː / (BrE)
In Norse mythology, the god of thunder, rain, and fertility, often depicted wielding a hammer that symbolizes lightning.

Examples

Thor god of thunder
In Norse legends, Thor is famed for protecting humanity from giants.
Many Norse festivals include stories about Thor and his magical hammer, Mjölnir.
The Marvel movies have introduced Thor to a new generation of fans worldwide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Odin - supreme god in Norse mythology, Thor's father
  • synonym: Zeus - in Greek mythology, god of thunder, similar to Thor
