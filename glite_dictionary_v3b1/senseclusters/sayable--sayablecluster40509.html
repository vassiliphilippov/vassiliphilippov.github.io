sayable

adjective
able to be expressed
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪəbəl / (AmE), / ˈseɪəbəl / (BrE)
able to be spoken or expressed in words

Examples

Sayable in words
Some feelings are not easily sayable and remain hidden inside.
He struggled to find the sayable words for his complicated emotions.
Not every thought is sayable in a public setting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expressible - able to be expressed or communicated
  • antonym: unspeakable - too bad or shocking to be expressed in words
