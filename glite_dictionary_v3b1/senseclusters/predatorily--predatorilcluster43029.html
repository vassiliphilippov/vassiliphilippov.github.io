predatorily

adverb
like a predator
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛdətɔˌrəli / (AmE), / prɪˈdætərɪli / (BrE)
in a manner that hunts, captures, or kills other animals for food, or behaves like a predator

Examples

To move predatorily
The lion moved predatorily through the tall grass, watching its prey.
He glanced predatorily at the food on the table, eager for his turn.
Wolves often hunt predatorily in packs to catch larger animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggressively - in a forceful or attacking way
  • antonym: prey - an animal that is hunted and killed for food
