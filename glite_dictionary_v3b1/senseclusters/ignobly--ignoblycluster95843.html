ignobly

adverb
in a dishonorable or base way
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈnoʊbli / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnəʊbli / (BrE)
In a manner that is dishonorable, base, or lacking in nobility.

Examples

To act ignobly
Despite an opportunity to help, she chose to behave ignobly and walked away.
He acted ignobly by betraying his closest friends during the crisis.
The politician was criticized for responding ignobly to his opponent's honest questions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nobly - in a noble or honorable manner
