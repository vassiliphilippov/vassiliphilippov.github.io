onside

adjective
within allowed sports area or position
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈɒn.saɪd / (BrE)
(of a player or play in certain sports) not offside; within the allowed area or position as specified by the rules.

Examples

An onside player
The referee checked the replay to see if the forward was actually onside during the play.
The striker made sure he was onside before receiving the pass.
They scored a goal after confirming the player was clearly onside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: offside - in a position not allowed by the rules; not within the prescribed area of play
