two-lane

adjective
having two lanes, one in each direction (of a road)
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːˌleɪn / (AmE), / ˈtuːˌleɪn / (BrE)
(of a road) having one lane for traffic moving in each direction.

Examples

A two-lane road
Driving on a two-lane street requires extra caution when passing other vehicles.
The accident happened on a two-lane highway in a rural area.
They plan to widen the two-lane road to reduce congestion.
