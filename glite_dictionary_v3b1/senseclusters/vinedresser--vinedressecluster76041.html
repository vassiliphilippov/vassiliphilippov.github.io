vinedresser

noun
grapevine caretaker
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪnˌdrɛsər / (AmE), / ˈvaɪnˌdrɛsə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to care for and cultivate grapevines, especially in a vineyard

Examples

The vinedresser prunes vines
The experienced vinedresser carefully pruned each vine to encourage healthy growth.
During harvest season, the vinedresser works long hours in the vineyard.
A skilled vinedresser can improve the quality of the grapes significantly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gardener - a person who tends and cultivates a garden
  • synonym: viticulturist - a person who studies or practices the cultivation of grapevines
