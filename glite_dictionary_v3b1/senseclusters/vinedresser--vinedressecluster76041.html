vinedresser

noun
grapevine caretaker
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈvaɪn.dres.ər/ (AmE), /ˈvaɪn.dres.ə/ (BrE)

Examples

The vinedresser prunes vines

During harvest season, the vinedresser works long hours in the vineyard.
A skilled vinedresser can improve the quality of the grapes significantly.
