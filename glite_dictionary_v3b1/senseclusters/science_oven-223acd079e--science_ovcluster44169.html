science oven

noun
laboratory oven for experiments
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪəns ˌʌvən / (AmE), / ˈsaɪəns ˌʌvən / (BrE)
an oven specially designed for conducting scientific experiments, typically used in laboratories to heat or process materials under controlled conditions

Examples
