innholder

noun
inn manager or owner
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person who owns or manages an inn, especially in historical contexts

Examples

The innholder welcomes guests
The weary travelers were relieved when the innholder offered them a warm meal and a bed.
In old English towns, the innholder was often a respected member of the community.
The innholder kept the fire burning late into the night for his guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: innkeeper - a person who runs or manages an inn
  • synonym: landlord - a person who owns or manages a rented property, especially a pub or inn
