vervet

noun
African monkey with black face and hands
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜr.vət / (AmE), / ˈvɜː.vɪt / (BrE)
A medium-sized African monkey with a black face and hands and a greyish body, commonly found in savanna regions.

Examples

A vervet monkey
A group of vervet monkeys gathered near the riverbank, grooming each other.
Researchers observed the behavior of a young vervet in its natural habitat.
The safari guide pointed out a vervet resting on the acacia tree branch.
