Bartlett pear

noun
type of juicy yellow pear
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrtlɪt pɛr / (AmE), / ˈbɑːtlɪt peə / (BrE)
A juicy, yellow-skinned variety of pear, widely grown for its sweet flavor and smooth texture.

Examples

A ripe Bartlett pear
After dinner, he enjoyed a juicy Bartlett pear for dessert.
She bought a basket of Bartlett pears from the local market.
The ripest Bartlett pear was chosen for the fruit salad.
