ambulator

noun
church walkway
Pronunciation: / ˈæmbjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈæmbjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
an ambulatory or covered walkway, especially one that curves around the apse of a church

Examples

Church ambulator
The monks processed quietly along the ambulator during the evening prayers.
Visitors admired the stained glass windows lining the ancient ambulator of the cathedral.
Restoration work revealed original stonework in the church's ambulator.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ambulatory - a covered passage around the apse or choir of a church
