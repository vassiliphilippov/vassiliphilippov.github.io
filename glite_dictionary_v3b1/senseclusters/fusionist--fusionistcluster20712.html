fusionist

noun
supporter of combining ideas
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːʒənɪst / (AmE), / ˈfjuːʒənɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or practices the combination of different ideas, groups, or styles

Examples

Creative fusionist thinker
As a fusionist, she encouraged collaboration between artists from different backgrounds.
The new leader was known as a fusionist who brought together rival political factions.
Many chefs today are fusionists, blending culinary traditions from around the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: separatist - a person who supports separation from a group
  • synonym: unifier - a person who brings different people or things together
