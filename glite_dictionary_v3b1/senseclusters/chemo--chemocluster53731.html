chemo

noun
cancer drug treatment
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkiːməʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for chemotherapy, a treatment for cancer using drugs to destroy cancer cells

Examples

A round of chemo
After her third round of chemo, she started to lose her hair.
He missed several days of work because he was undergoing chemo treatments.
The doctor explained the possible side effects of chemo before starting the therapy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemotherapy - the use of drugs to treat cancer
