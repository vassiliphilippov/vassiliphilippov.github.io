multilamellar

adjective
having many layers
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌlti.ləˈmɛlər / (AmE), / ˌmʌlti.ləˈmɛlə / (BrE)
consisting of or having multiple thin layers or sheets

Examples

Multilamellar cell membrane
The cell membrane was found to be multilamellar under high magnification.
Researchers developed a multilamellar vesicle for drug delivery applications.
The multilamellar structure improved the material's strength and flexibility.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monolayer - a single, one-layered structure
