heaviness

noun
thickness or boldness of a line or mark
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvinəs / (AmE), / ˈhɛvinɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being thick or bold, especially as applied to a line or mark in drawing or printing.

Examples

Heaviness of the line
She adjusted the heaviness of the pen to make the heading stand out.
The heaviness of the pencil strokes gave the sketch a dramatic look.
Varying the heaviness of your lines can add depth and emphasis to your drawing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boldness - the quality of being bold or prominent (of lines)
  • synonym: thickness - the quality of being thick
