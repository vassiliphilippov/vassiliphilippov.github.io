otomycosis

noun
fungal ear infection
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊtəmaɪˈkoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌəʊtəmaɪˈkəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a fungal infection of the external ear canal

Examples

Otomycosis ear infection
The doctor diagnosed the patient with otomycosis after examining his ear.
Treatment for otomycosis usually involves antifungal ear drops and keeping the ear dry.
People who swim frequently are at higher risk of developing otomycosis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dermatophytosis - a fungal infection of the skin
  • synonym: ear infection - an infection affecting the ear
