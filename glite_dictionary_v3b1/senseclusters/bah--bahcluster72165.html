bah

noun
expression of contempt
9.8
Pronunciation: / bɑ / (AmE), / bɑː / (BrE)
a short exclamation used to express contempt, disgust, or dismissal.

Examples

A loud bah
When he heard the idea, he just muttered bah and left the room.
She dismissed the rumors with a loud bah and refused to discuss them further.
Every time someone mentions dieting, he responds with a sarcastic bah.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pshaw - an exclamation expressing disbelief or contempt
  • synonym: ugh - an exclamation expressing disgust
