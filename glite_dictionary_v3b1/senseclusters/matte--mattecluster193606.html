matte

verb
make or become matted and felt-like
8.1 6.7
Pronunciation: /mæt/ (AmE), /mæt/ (BrE)
to change the texture of a material so that it becomes matted, dense, and felt-like, often as a result of moisture or friction

Examples

To matte wool
If you wash this wool sweater on hot, it will matte and shrink.
Repeated washing can cause delicate fabrics to matte over time.
The dog's fur began to matte after playing in the rain for hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: felt - to mat together fibers into a dense fabric
