phosphoryl

noun
chemical group with phosphorus
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑsfəˌrɪl / (AmE), / ˈfɒsfərɪl / (BrE)
a chemical group consisting of a phosphorus atom bonded to oxygen atoms, found in many biological and chemical compounds

Examples

A phosphoryl group
The enzyme transfers a phosphoryl group during the reaction.
A phosphoryl group is essential in many metabolic processes in living cells.
