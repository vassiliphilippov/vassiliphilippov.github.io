underappreciate

verb
to not value enough
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərəˈpriːʃiˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˌʌndərəˈpriːʃieɪt / (BrE)
to value, recognize, or show appreciation for someone or something less than is deserved

Examples

To underappreciate artists
Many artists feel that society has underappreciated their contributions to culture.
The team's hard work was underappreciated by upper management, leading to low morale.
She realized she had underappreciated the importance of regular exercise until she became ill.

Related Terms

  • antonym: appreciate - to recognize the full worth of something
  • synonym: undervalue - to assign too low a value to something
