blase

adjective
unimpressed or indifferent from overexposure
Pronunciation: / blɑːˈzeɪ / (AmE), / blɑːˈzeɪ / (BrE)
Unimpressed, unconcerned, or indifferent to something because of frequent exposure or excessive indulgence.

Examples

Blase about travel
After years of traveling, she felt rather blase about visiting yet another famous landmark.
He sounded blase about the upcoming concert, having performed countless times before.
The critics' blase attitude shocked the eager young artist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apathetic - showing or feeling no interest, enthusiasm, or concern
  • synonym: indifferent - having no particular interest or concern
