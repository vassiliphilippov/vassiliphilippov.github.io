Ceres

proper noun
largest asteroid and dwarf planet
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriːz / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriːz / (BrE)
the largest object in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter, classified as both an asteroid and a dwarf planet

Examples

Ceres dwarf planet
Ceres was the first object in the asteroid belt to be discovered and is now also classified as a dwarf planet.
NASA's Dawn spacecraft orbited Ceres to study its surface and composition.
Scientists have discovered water ice on Ceres, suggesting the possibility of past or present life.
