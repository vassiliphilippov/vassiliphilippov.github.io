gumshoe

noun
waterproof rubber overshoe
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmˌʃu / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmˌʃuː / (BrE)
A waterproof overshoe made of rubber, worn to protect shoes from water or snow.

Examples

Rubber gumshoe
During the heavy rain, he put on his gumshoes to keep his feet dry.
In winter, gumshoes were a common sight on city streets.
She wiped her gumshoe on the mat before stepping inside.
