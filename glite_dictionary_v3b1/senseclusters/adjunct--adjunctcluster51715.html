adjunct

noun
assistant or subordinate person
7.3 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈædʒ.ʌŋkt/ (AmE), /ˈædʒ.ʌŋkt/ (BrE)
a person who is an assistant or subordinate to someone else, often in a supporting or auxiliary role

Examples

An adjunct staff member
In some companies, an adjunct may have a part-time or temporary position.
She worked as an adjunct to the director, handling administrative duties.
The professor was assisted by an adjunct during the research project.
