imprinter

noun
attachment figure in imprinting
8.3
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɪntər / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɪntə / (BrE)
an animal or person that becomes the main object of attachment for a young animal during imprinting

Examples

Mother imprinter
The scientist acted as the imprinter for the ducklings during their first days of life.
Without a natural parent, the puppy saw its human caretaker as its imprinter.
In some experiments, a moving object can become the imprinter for newly hatched chicks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caregiver - a person who provides care and protection
  • synonym: parent - a mother or father of an animal or person
