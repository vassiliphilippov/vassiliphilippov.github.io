principal

noun
original sum of a loan or investment
5.0 3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪnsəpəl / (AmE), / ˈprɪnsɪpəl / (BrE)
the original sum of money invested or borrowed, on which interest is calculated

Examples

Principal amount

She invested the principal and used the interest for her expenses.
The loan must be repaid with interest on the principal.
