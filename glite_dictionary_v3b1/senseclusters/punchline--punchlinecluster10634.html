punchline

noun
the key point of a joke
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌntʃˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈpʌntʃˌlaɪn / (BrE)
the sentence or phrase that gives the point of a joke or humorous story, usually causing laughter

Examples

The punchline of a joke
Everyone laughed when he finally revealed the punchline to his long story.
She always forgets the punchline and ruins the joke.
The comedian paused dramatically before delivering the punchline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tagline - a memorable phrase or slogan often used in advertising
