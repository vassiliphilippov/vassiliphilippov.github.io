spurge

noun
plant of the genus Euphorbia
9.9
Pronunciation: / spɝdʒ / (AmE), / spɜːdʒ / (BrE)
any of numerous plants of the genus Euphorbia, typically having milky, often poisonous sap

Examples

Spurge plant
Some species of spurge are considered weeds because they spread quickly and crowd out other plants.
