fed up with

adjective
annoyed or bored by something persistent
2.0
Pronunciation: / fɛd ʌp wɪð / (AmE), / fɛd ʌp wɪð / (BrE)
annoyed or bored with something that has continued for too long

Examples

To be fed up with delays

I'm fed up with the amount of paperwork I have to do every week.
Many workers are fed up with the lack of communication from management.
