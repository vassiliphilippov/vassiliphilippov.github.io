April 14

noun
Pan American Day, unity among American nations
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.prəl ˈfɔrˌtinθ / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.prəl ˈfɔːˌtiːnθ / (BrE)
Pan American Day; a day commemorating political and economic unity among the countries of the Americas.

Examples

April 14 Pan Day
Many countries in the Americas observe April 14 as Pan American Day each year.
On April 14, leaders of the Americas often reaffirm their commitment to unity and progress.
Schools held special assemblies on April 14 to honor hemispheric cooperation.
