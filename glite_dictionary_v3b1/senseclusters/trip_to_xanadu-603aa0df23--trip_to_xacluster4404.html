trip to Xanadu

noun
idealized or luxurious vacation
Pronunciation: / trɪp tu ˈzænədu / (AmE), / trɪp tə ˈzænəduː / (BrE)
a journey or vacation to an imaginary place of great luxury and beauty; an idealized or fantastical getaway

Examples

A trip to Xanadu dream
After months of hard work, their cruise felt like a trip to Xanadu.
She described her week at the resort as a trip to Xanadu.
Winning the lottery let him take a trip to Xanadu he had always dreamed of.
