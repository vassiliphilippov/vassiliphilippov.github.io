wavery

adjective
uncertain or hesitant
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪvəri / (AmE), / ˈweɪvəri / (BrE)
showing uncertainty, hesitation, or lack of firmness in action or speech

Examples

Wavery voice
His wavery voice made it clear he was unsure about the decision.
The committee's wavery response left everyone confused about the next steps.
She gave a wavery smile, uncertain whether to accept the invitation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: resolute - determined and unwavering
  • synonym: uncertain - not sure or confident
