Fenian

adjective
of Irish legends
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːniən / (AmE), / ˈfiːniən / (BrE)
relating to the Fianna, the legendary band of Irish warriors, or to the Fenian Cycle of Irish mythology

Examples

Fenian legends
The Fenian legends are an important part of Irish cultural heritage.
She wrote her thesis on Fenian tales and their influence on modern literature.
Many poems celebrate the bravery of the Fenian warriors in ancient Ireland.

Related Terms

  • antonym: historical - relating to history or past events
  • synonym: mythological - relating to myths or mythology
