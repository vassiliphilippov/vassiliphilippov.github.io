glossy magazine

noun
magazine on shiny, high-quality paper
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɑːsi məˈɡæzˌin / (AmE), / ˈɡlɒsi məˈɡæziːn / (BrE)
a magazine printed on shiny, high-quality paper and featuring attractive images and layouts

Examples

A glossy magazine cover
A photo of the celebrity appeared on the cover of every glossy magazine last month.
She bought a glossy magazine to read during her flight.
The waiting room had a table stacked with glossy magazines about fashion and travel.
