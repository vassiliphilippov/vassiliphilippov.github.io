Benedict

noun
egg dish with sauce
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛnədɪkt / (AmE), / ˈbɛnədɪkt / (BrE)
a dish of poached eggs served on toast or an English muffin, often with ham or smoked salmon and hollandaise sauce

Examples

Eggs Benedict
For brunch, he decided to try the classic Benedict with a side of fresh fruit.
The restaurant offers a vegetarian Benedict made with spinach instead of ham.
She learned how to make a perfect Benedict at home for her family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eggs Benedict - a dish of poached eggs, ham, and hollandaise sauce on an English muffin
