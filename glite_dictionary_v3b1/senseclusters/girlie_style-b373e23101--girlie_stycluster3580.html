girlie style

noun
decorative style with feminine features
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɝːli staɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːli staɪl / (BrE)
a decorative style characterized by features commonly associated with femininity, such as pastel colors, floral patterns, or playful details

Examples

Girlie style decorations
Although he preferred a minimalist look, his sister insisted on a girlie style for the party decorations.
She decorated her bedroom in a girlie style with pink curtains and heart-shaped pillows.
The shop's girlie style attracts customers who love bows and pastel colors.
