conformability

noun
ability to adapt
7.4
Pronunciation: / kənˌfɔrməˈbɪləti / (AmE), / kənˌfɔːməˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being able to fit, adapt, or adjust to a surface, shape, or standard

Examples

Bandage conformability
The conformability of the bandage allows it to wrap smoothly around joints.
Engineers tested the conformability of the new material on different surfaces.
High conformability is important for products that must seal tightly against uneven edges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexibility - the ability to bend or adapt easily
  • antonym: rigidity - the quality of being stiff and not able to adapt
