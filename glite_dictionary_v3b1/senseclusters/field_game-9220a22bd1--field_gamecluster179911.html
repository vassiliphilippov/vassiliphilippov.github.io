field game

noun
outdoor game played on a field
1.0
Pronunciation: / fiːld ɡeɪm / (AmE), / fiːld ɡeɪm / (BrE)
An outdoor game played on a field of specified dimensions, such as football, rugby, or field hockey.

Examples

A field game match
Many schools require students to participate in at least one field game each year.
Rain forced the cancellation of today's field game at the local sports complex.
Soccer is a popular field game enjoyed by millions of people around the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indoor game - a game played indoors rather than on a field
  • synonym: team sport - a sport where individuals are organized into teams
