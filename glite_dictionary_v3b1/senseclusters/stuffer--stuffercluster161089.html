stuffer

noun
person who stuffs and mounts animals
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌfər / (AmE), / ˈstʌfə / (BrE)
a person who stuffs and mounts the skins of animals for display, especially as part of taxidermy

Examples

Animal stuffer

The stuffer carefully mounted the bird for the museum display.
The old shop was run by a well-known stuffer in the town.
