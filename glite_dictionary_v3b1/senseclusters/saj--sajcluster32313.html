saj

noun
Middle Eastern griddle
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɑːdʒ / (AmE), / sɑːdʒ / (BrE)
a round, flat metal griddle used in Middle Eastern cooking, or the flatbread baked on it

Examples

Hot saj bread
The chef heated the saj before placing the dough on it to bake.
Fresh bread cooked on a saj is a staple in many Middle Eastern households.
She learned to make traditional flatbread using a saj during her trip to Lebanon.
