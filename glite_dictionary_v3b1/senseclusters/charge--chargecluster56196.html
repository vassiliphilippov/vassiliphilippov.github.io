charge

verb
to aim or ready a weapon/device
Pronunciation: / tʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / tʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
to fill or load a device with explosives or ammunition; to aim or ready a weapon

Examples

To charge a weapon
Before entering the building, the police officer charged his weapon and checked the safety.
Make sure to charge your stun gun before going out at night.
She charged the tranquilizer gun before aiming at the animal.
The workers charged the ship's hold with barrels of wine.
They charged the truck with boxes before sunrise.
We need to charge the cart with supplies for the journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aim - to point or direct (a weapon) at a target
  • antonym: disarm - to remove or make (a weapon) not ready for use
  • synonym: load - to put a large amount of something into or onto something else
