bourbon

noun
American whiskey made from corn
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrbən / (AmE), / ˈbɜːbən / (BrE)
a type of American whiskey made mainly from corn and aged in charred oak barrels

Examples

A glass of bourbon
