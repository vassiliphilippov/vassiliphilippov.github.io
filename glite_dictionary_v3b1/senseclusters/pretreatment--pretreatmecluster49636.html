pretreatment

noun
preparatory process
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈtriːtmənt / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈtriːtmənt / (BrE)
a process, chemical, or agent used to prepare something before the main treatment or procedure

Examples

A pretreatment process
The technician applied a pretreatment to the metal to prevent rust before painting.
Using a stain remover as a pretreatment can help get tough marks out of clothes.
Some hair salons recommend a special pretreatment before coloring to protect your hair.
The doctor recommended a pretreatment to reduce the risk of side effects from the medication.
Proper pretreatment of the wood helps prevent damage from insects and moisture.
Before dyeing the fabric, a thorough pretreatment is necessary to ensure even color.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preparation - the act of making something ready for use or action
  • degree higher: treatment - the process of dealing with or applying something to achieve a result
