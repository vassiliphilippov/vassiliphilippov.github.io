producer

noun
someone or something that manufactures goods
2.7 18/m
Pronunciation: / pr…ôÀàdus…ôr / (AmE), / pr…ôÀàdjuÀês…ôr / (BrE)
a person, company, or country that makes goods for sale, especially in large quantities

Examples

A producer of goods
As a leading producer of textiles, the country exports goods worldwide.
Farmers are the primary producers of food in our economy.
The region became a major producer of wine after the new vineyards opened.

Synonyms

  • manufacturer
    3.1 46/m
    manufacturer makes goods in large factories with machines, while producer refers to a person, company, or country that makes goods for sale.
  • maker
    1.6 32/m
    maker often means a person or small company who makes things by hand, while producer makes goods for sale, often in large amounts.
