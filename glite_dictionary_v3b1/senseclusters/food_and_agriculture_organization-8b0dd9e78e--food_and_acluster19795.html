Food and Agriculture Organization

proper noun
UN agency for food and agriculture
Pronunciation: / fuːd ənd ˈæɡrɪˌkʌltʃər ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / fuːd ənd ˈæɡrɪˌkʌltʃə ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an agency of the United Nations that coordinates international efforts to improve nutrition and food security, and to advance agriculture worldwide

Examples
