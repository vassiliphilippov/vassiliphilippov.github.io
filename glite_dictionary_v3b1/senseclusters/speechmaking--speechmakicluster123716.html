speechmaking

noun
public speaking; delivering speeches
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːtʃˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspiːtʃˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of delivering a formal spoken address to a public audience

Examples

Effective speechmaking
Effective speechmaking requires careful preparation and a clear delivery.
The politician's speechmaking captivated the crowd for over an hour.
They attended a workshop aimed at improving their speechmaking skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debate - a formal discussion in which opposing arguments are put forward
  • synonym: oratory - the art of public speaking, especially formally and persuasively
  • antonym: silence - the absence of speech or sound
