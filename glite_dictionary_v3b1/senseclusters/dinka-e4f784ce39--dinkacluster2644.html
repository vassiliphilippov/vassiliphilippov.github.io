Dinka

proper noun
South Sudanese ethnic group
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪŋkə / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋkə / (BrE)
a member of a major ethnic group of South Sudan, or the Nilotic language spoken by this group

Examples

A Dinka person
The Dinka have a rich tradition of cattle herding and storytelling.
Many Dinka people live along the Nile in South Sudan.
She is learning to speak Dinka to communicate with her neighbors.
