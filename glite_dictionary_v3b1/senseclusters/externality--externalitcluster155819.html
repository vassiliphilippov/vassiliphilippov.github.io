externality

noun
outward feature
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstɚˈnæləti / (AmE), / ˌɛkstɜːˈnælɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being external, or an outward feature or aspect of something

Examples

Externality of the surface
The externality of the sculpture made it stand out in the gallery.
She was more interested in the externality of the product than its function.
The designer focused on the externality of the building to create a striking first impression.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appearance - the way that someone or something looks
  • antonym: interiority - the quality of being internal or inside
