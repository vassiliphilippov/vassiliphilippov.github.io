hissing hot

adjective
extremely hot; emitting a hissing sound
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪsɪŋ hɑt / (AmE), / ˈhɪsɪŋ hɒt / (BrE)
extremely hot, especially referring to food or objects that are so hot they emit a hissing sound

Examples

Hissing hot pizza
He took a bite without realizing the pizza was still hissing hot.
Steam rose from the hissing hot pot of stew as she carefully stirred it.
The hissing hot pan warned everyone not to touch it right out of the oven.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scalding - extremely hot, especially liquid
