metathetic

adjective
chemical exchange reaction
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
describing a chemical reaction in which parts of two molecules are exchanged to form new compounds

Examples

Metathetic reaction
The chemist observed a metathetic reaction that produced a white precipitate in the solution.
Certain industrial processes rely on metathetic methods to synthesize new materials efficiently.
A metathetic exchange can result in the formation of entirely different chemical compounds.

  • synonym: exchange reaction - a reaction involving the exchange of parts between molecules
