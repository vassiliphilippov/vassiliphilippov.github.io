unsampled

adjective
not digitally sampled
8.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsæmpəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɑːmpəld / (BrE)
not converted into digital samples or not created from pre-recorded samples, especially in audio or signal processing

Examples

Unsampled audio tracks
The musician preferred using unsampled instruments for a more authentic sound.
Some audiophiles argue that unsampled recordings capture more detail than digital versions.
The unsampled signal was preserved to maintain the original quality of the audio.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analog - existing in a continuous, non-digital form
  • antonym: sampled - converted into digital samples or created from pre-recorded samples
