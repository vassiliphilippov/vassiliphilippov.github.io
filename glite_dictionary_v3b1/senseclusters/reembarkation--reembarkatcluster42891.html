reembarkation

noun
boarding again
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪmˌbɑːrˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːɪmˌbɑːkˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of boarding a ship, plane, or vehicle again after having previously disembarked

Examples

The reembarkation of passengers
After the storm passed, the reembarkation of passengers was carefully supervised by the crew.
The tour guide announced that reembarkation would take place at 3 p.m. sharp.
Strict security checks delayed the reembarkation process at the airport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embarkation - the act of boarding a ship, plane, or vehicle
