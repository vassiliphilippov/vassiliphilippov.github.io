in turn

adverb
in the proper order or sequence
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪn tɝn / (AmE), / ɪn tɜːn / (BrE)
in the proper order or sequence; each after the other or as a result of a previous action

Examples

Speak in turn
Each member of the group spoke in turn during the meeting.
Success brings confidence, which in turn leads to greater achievements.
The children took the toys in turn so everyone had a chance to play.
