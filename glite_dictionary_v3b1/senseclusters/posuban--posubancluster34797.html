posuban

noun
Ghanaian shrine structure
Pronunciation: / poʊˈsuːbæn / (AmE), / pəˈsuːbæn / (BrE)
a traditional shrine or structure in some Ghanaian communities, often used for ceremonies or as a symbol of protection

Examples

The posuban shrine
The community elders met at the posuban to discuss important matters.
Tourists admired the colorful decorations on the village posuban during the festival.
Each year, offerings are made at the posuban to honor the ancestors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monument - a structure built to commemorate a person or event
  • synonym: shrine - a place regarded as holy or used for religious purposes
