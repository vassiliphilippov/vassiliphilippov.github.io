nonreturn

noun
not returnable
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈtɜrn / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
an item or situation that cannot be sent back, exchanged, or refunded after purchase

Examples

Nonreturn item
Please note that this product is a nonreturn and cannot be refunded or exchanged.
The store clearly marked the clearance items as nonreturn to avoid confusion.
If you buy a nonreturn item, make sure you are certain about your choice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: returnable - able to be sent back or refunded
