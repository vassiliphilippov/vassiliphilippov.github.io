blue vitriol

noun
hydrated blue crystalline copper sulfate
Pronunciation: / bluː ˈvɪtriəl / (AmE), / bluː ˈvɪtriəl / (BrE)
a blue crystalline compound, copper(II) sulfate pentahydrate, used in electroplating, as a fungicide, and in chemistry
