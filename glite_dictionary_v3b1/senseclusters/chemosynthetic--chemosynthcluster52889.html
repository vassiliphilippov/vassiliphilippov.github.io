chemosynthetic

adjective
using chemical energy
Pronunciation: / ˌkiːmoʊsɪnˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɛməʊsɪnˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
describing organisms that produce food by using chemical energy instead of sunlight

Examples

Chemosynthetic bacteria
Certain bacteria living near hydrothermal vents are chemosynthetic and do not rely on sunlight.
Scientists study chemosynthetic organisms to understand life in extreme environments.
The ecosystem around the vent is supported by chemosynthetic microbes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: photosynthetic - using sunlight to produce food
