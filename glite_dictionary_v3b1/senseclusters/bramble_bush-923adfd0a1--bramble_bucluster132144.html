bramble bush

noun
prickly bush with edible fruits (e.g. blackberries)
Pronunciation: / ˈbræmbəl bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈbræmbəl bʊʃ / (BrE)
a prickly shrub of the genus Rubus, typically bearing edible aggregate fruits such as blackberries

Examples

A bramble bush with berries
A tangle of bramble bushes lined the edge of the old garden.
She scratched her arm on a bramble bush while trying to reach the fruit.
The children picked ripe berries from the bramble bush along the footpath.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: blackberry - edible fruit produced by some bramble bushes
  • synonym: bramble - prickly shrub of the genus Rubus
