inseam

noun
inside trouser seam
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌsiːm / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌsiːm / (BrE)
the seam on the inside of a pair of trousers or similar garment, running from the crotch to the hem

Examples

Pants inseam
He measured the inseam of his jeans to make sure they would fit properly.
The tailor asked for her inseam length before altering the pants.
A short inseam can make trousers look too high above the ankles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: outseam - the seam on the outside of a pair of trousers, running from the waistband to the hem
