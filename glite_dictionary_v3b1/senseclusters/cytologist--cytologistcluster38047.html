cytologist

noun
scientist specializing in cell studies
5.6
Pronunciation: / saɪˈtɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / saɪˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A scientist who specializes in the study of the structure, function, and chemistry of cells.

Examples

cytologist studies cells
A team led by a cytologist discovered a new cellular process.
As a cytologist, she contributed to important cancer research.
The cytologist examined the specimen for abnormal cell formation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cell biologist - a scientist who studies cells and their functions
