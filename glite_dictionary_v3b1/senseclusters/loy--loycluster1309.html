loy

noun
Irish digging spade
Pronunciation: / lɔɪ / (AmE), / lɔɪ / (BrE)
a long, narrow spade traditionally used in Ireland for digging soil

Examples

A loy spade

In rural Ireland, a loy was often preferred for digging potatoes.
