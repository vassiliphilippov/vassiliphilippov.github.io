raw

adjective
unpleasantly cold and damp weather
Pronunciation: / rɔ / (AmE), / rɔː / (BrE)
(of weather) unpleasantly cold and damp.

Examples

Raw weather
People hurried indoors to escape the raw chill of November.
She shivered in the raw morning air waiting for the bus.
The rain and wind made the afternoon feel especially raw.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chilly - uncomfortably or unpleasantly cold
  • synonym: damp - slightly wet, often in a way that is unpleasant
