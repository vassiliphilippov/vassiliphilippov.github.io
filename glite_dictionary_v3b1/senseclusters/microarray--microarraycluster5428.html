microarray

noun
gene analysis tool
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ.reɪ/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ.ə.reɪ/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ.reɪ/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ.ə.reɪ/ (BrE)
a laboratory device with many tiny spots of DNA or other molecules, used to analyze gene expression or detect biological molecules

Examples
