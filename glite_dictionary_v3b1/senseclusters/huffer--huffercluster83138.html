huffer

noun
heavy breather
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌfər / (AmE), / ˈhʌfə / (BrE)
a person who breathes heavily, especially after physical effort or exertion

Examples

A tired huffer
After climbing the steep hill, the last huffer finally reached the top, breathing loudly.
The coach encouraged the huffer to slow down and catch his breath after the sprint.
During the intense workout, she became a real huffer and needed a break.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gasp - to take a quick, deep breath due to exertion or surprise
  • synonym: pant - to breathe quickly and heavily
