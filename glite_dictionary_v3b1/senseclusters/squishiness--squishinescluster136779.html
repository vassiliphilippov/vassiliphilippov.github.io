squishiness

noun
soft, yielding quality
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɪʃinəs / (AmE), / ˈskwɪʃinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being soft and easily pressed or squeezed

Examples

The squishiness of a pillow
The squishiness of the new mattress made it very comfortable to sleep on.
Children love the squishiness of playdough as they shape it into different forms.
He tested the squishiness of the fruit to see if it was ripe enough to eat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: firmness - the quality of being solid or resistant to pressure
  • synonym: softness - the quality of being soft or gentle to the touch
