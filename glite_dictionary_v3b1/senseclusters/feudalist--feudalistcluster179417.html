Examples

Feudalist society
The country maintained a feudalist structure long after most of Europe had modernized.
Some critics argue that the organization operates in a feudalist manner, with power concentrated at the top.
The novel describes a feudalist society where loyalty to local leaders is more important than national laws.

    9.9 0.01/m
    seigneurial relates to the rights and land of a feudal lord, while feudalist refers to the wider feudal system and its loyalty networks.
