ah

interjection
hesitation sound
2.4
Pronunciation: / ɑː / (AmE), / ɑː / (BrE)
used to express hesitation, doubt, or to pause while thinking in speech.

Examples

Ah, thinking pause
He said, 'ah, let me think about that for a moment before I answer.'
During the interview, she paused and said 'ah' while searching for the right words.
'Ah, I see what you mean now,' he replied after considering the explanation.
