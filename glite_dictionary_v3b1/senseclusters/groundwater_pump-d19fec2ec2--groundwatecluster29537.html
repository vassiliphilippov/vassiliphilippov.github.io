groundwater pump

noun
device for extracting underground water
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊndˌwɔtɚ pʌmp / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊndˌwɔːtə pʌmp / (BrE)
a device designed to extract or sample water from underground sources
