accent

noun
diacritical mark for pronunciation or stress
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.sɛnt / (AmE), / ˈæk.sənt / (BrE)
a mark placed above a letter, especially a vowel, to indicate how it should be pronounced or stressed

Examples

Accent mark
Many French words use an accent mark over vowels.
The acute accent in 'é' shows how the letter should be pronounced.
The dictionary explains the meaning of each accent used in Spanish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diacritic - a sign added to a letter to alter its pronunciation or distinguish it from similar words
