felicitous

adjective
aptly or suitably expressed; appropriate
Pronunciation: / fəˈlɪsɪtəs / (AmE), / fəˈlɪsɪtəs / (BrE)
pleasantly and appropriately expressed or suited to the occasion; well-chosen or apt in manner or style.

Examples

Felicitous phrase
Her felicitous choice of words made the speech memorable for everyone.
His felicitous remarks eased the tension in the meeting.
The decor was felicitous and matched the joyful mood of the event perfectly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inept - not suitable or appropriate; awkward
