dream

verb
to daydream or indulge in fantasies
2.7 29/m
Pronunciation: / driÀêm / (AmE), / driÀêm / (BrE)
to experience a series of thoughts or images while awake, often indulging in pleasant or fanciful ideas; to daydream or fantasize

Examples

To dream of success
He would often dream of becoming a famous musician while staring out the window.
Rather than work, she preferred to dream about a life of adventure.
Sometimes I dream about traveling the world during boring meetings.

Synonyms

  • daydream
    2.3 1.89/m

  • fantasize
    4.5 4.87/m
    fantasize means imagining things that are unlikely or ideal, while dream means enjoying pleasant, fanciful thoughts while awake.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
