Examples

To inter a body

Synonyms

    1.7 33/m

  • inhume
    9.9 0.00/m

  • entomb
    8.1 0.65/m

  • lay to rest
    4.0 0.83/m
    lay to rest is a gentle idiom used at funerals, while inter is a direct formal verb for burying.
