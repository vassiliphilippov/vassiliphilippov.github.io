Tragopogon

noun
genus of herbs; salsify, goatsbeard
Pronunciation: / ˌtræɡəˈpoʊɡən /; / træˈɡɑpəˌɡɑn / (AmE), / ˌtræɡəˈpɒɡən /; / træˈɡɒpəɡɒn / (BrE)
a genus of Old World herbs with narrow, grass-like leaves and yellow or purple flower heads, commonly known as salsify or goatsbeard
