sastrugi

noun
wind-shaped snow ridges
Pronunciation: / ˈsæstruːɡi / (AmE), / ˈsæstruːɡi / (BrE)
wavelike ridges of hard snow formed by wind on the surface of polar regions

Examples

Hard sastrugi ridges
The sled slowed as it crossed the uneven sastrugi stretching across the frozen plain.
Photographers captured the dramatic patterns of sastrugi sculpted by the Antarctic winds.
Walking over the sharp sastrugi made the expedition much more difficult for the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snowdrift - a mound or bank of snow formed by the wind
