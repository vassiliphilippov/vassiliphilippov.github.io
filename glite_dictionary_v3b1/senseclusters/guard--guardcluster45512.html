guard

noun
device to prevent injury or accidents
3.1
Pronunciation: / ɡɑrd / (AmE), / ɡɑːd / (BrE)
a device or barrier that is intended to prevent injury or accidents, often by protecting a person from moving parts, sharp edges, or other hazards

Examples

Blade guard
Always make sure the blade guard is in place before operating the machine.
The hockey player wore a mouth guard to avoid dental injuries.
The saw has a safety guard to protect your hands while cutting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protector - a device or covering that protects
  • synonym: shield - a broad piece of protective equipment; something that protects
