hematological

adjective
pertaining to blood or hematology
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːməˈtɑːlədʒɪkəl /; / ˌhiːməˌtɑːˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈtɒlədʒɪkəl /; / ˌhiːməˌtɒlˈɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of blood, blood-forming organs, or blood diseases; pertaining to hematology.

Examples

Hematological tests
Her hematological tests came back within the normal range.
Recent advancements in hematological research have improved treatment options for anemia.
The patient was referred to a specialist for further hematological evaluation.
A wide range of haematological conditions can affect both children and adults.
She presented with multiple haematological abnormalities indicating a possible blood disorder.
The patient was referred to a haematological specialist for further tests.
