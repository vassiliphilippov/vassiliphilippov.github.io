gyrocar

noun
car with gyroscopes
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪroʊˌkɑr / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪərəʊˌkɑː / (BrE)
a car that uses gyroscopes to maintain balance, especially allowing it to run on two wheels

Examples

A gyrocar balances
The engineer explained how the gyrocar could stay upright even when stationary.
A prototype gyrocar attracted attention at the international auto show.
He read an article about a historic gyrocar that balanced perfectly on two wheels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automobile - a road vehicle powered by an engine, typically with four wheels
  • synonym: monorail - a vehicle that runs on a single rail, often balanced by gyroscopes or other means
