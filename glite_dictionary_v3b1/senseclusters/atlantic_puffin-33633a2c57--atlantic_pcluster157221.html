Atlantic puffin

noun
seabird of the northern Atlantic
6.6 5.5
Pronunciation: /ət.ˈlæn.tɪk ˈpʌf.ɪn/ /; / /æ.t.ˈlæn.tɪk ˈpʌf.ɪn/ (AmE), /ət.ˈlæn.tɪk ˈpʌf.ɪn/ (BrE)
a seabird of the auk family (Fratercula arctica), found in the northern Atlantic, with a black back, white underparts, and a multicolored bill
