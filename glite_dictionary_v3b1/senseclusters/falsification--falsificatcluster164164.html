falsification

noun
deliberate misrepresentation of facts
4.8 6.1
Pronunciation: /fæl.sɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /fæl.sə.fə.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /fɔːl.sɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /fæl.sɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of deliberately making something false, especially by altering or misrepresenting information or documents
