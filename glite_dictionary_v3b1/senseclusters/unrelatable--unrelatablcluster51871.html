unrelatable

adjective
not connectable
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈleɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈleɪtəbəl / (BrE)
not easy to connect with, understand, or relate to (especially emotionally or personally)

Examples

An unrelatable character
The characters in the novel were so unrealistic that they felt completely unrelatable to most readers.
Her experience was so unique that it seemed unrelatable to anyone else in the group.
Because the formats differ, the new dataset was largely unrelatable to our historical records.
The film's plot was so unrealistic that most viewers found it unrelatable.
Her experiences seemed unrelatable to people from different backgrounds.
Some students thought the textbook was unrelatable and hard to engage with.
