low-voltage

adjective
designed for use at low electrical voltage
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌloʊ ˈvoʊltɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌləʊ ˈvəʊltɪdʒ / (BrE)
Designed to operate at or involving a relatively low electric voltage, typically for safety or specific technical purposes.

Examples

Low-voltage system
Schools often use low-voltage systems to minimize the risk of electrical injury.
The electrician installed low-voltage lighting in the garden for safety.
This device is only compatible with low-voltage power sources.
