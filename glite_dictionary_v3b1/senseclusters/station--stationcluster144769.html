station

verb
assign to a place or position
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsteɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
to assign to a specified position or place, especially for a period of duty or work

Examples

To station guards
Security guards are stationed at the entrance to check visitor passes.
She was stationed abroad for two years as part of her job.
The army decided to station its troops near the border for added security.
