switchback

verb
to zigzag on a slope
6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈswɪtʃ.bæk/ (AmE), /ˈswɪtʃ.bæk/ (BrE)
to follow or make a path that zigzags with sharp turns, especially when going up or down a slope

Examples

The trail switchbacks uphill
The mountain trail switchbacks up the hillside, making the climb easier for hikers.
To reach the summit, you must switchback several times along the narrow path.
The road switchbacks down the steep canyon, offering breathtaking views at every turn.
