blam

interjection
sound of impact
9.8
Pronunciation: / blæm / (AmE), / blæm / (BrE)
an exclamation or written sound effect used to represent a sudden loud impact, explosion, or gunshot

Examples

Blam in comics
The comic book showed the superhero punching the villain with a big blam across the page.
Suddenly, there was a loud blam as the door slammed shut in the wind.
He described the fireworks as going off with a series of blams and flashes of light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bang - a loud, sudden noise or exclamation representing it
  • synonym: boom - a deep, loud sound, often from an explosion
