nonoffensive

adjective
not aggressive
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˈfɛnsɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəˈfɛnsɪv / (BrE)
not intended to attack, harm, or provoke; not aggressive or hostile

Examples

Nonoffensive tactics
The peacekeepers were given strict orders to use only nonoffensive tactics during the mission.
The new policy encourages nonoffensive strategies to resolve conflicts in the workplace.
All military equipment deployed in the area was strictly nonoffensive and intended for defense.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aggressive - ready or likely to attack or confront
  • synonym: defensive - intended to protect rather than attack
