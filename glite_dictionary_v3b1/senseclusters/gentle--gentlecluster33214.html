gentle

adjective
easily handled or managed (docile)
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛn.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.tl̩ / (BrE)
(of an animal or person) easily handled or managed; docile and obedient.

Examples

Their gentle manner made them ideal for caring for nervous patients.
