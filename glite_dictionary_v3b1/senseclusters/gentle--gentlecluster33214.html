gentle

adjective
easily handled or managed (docile)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛn.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.tl̩ / (BrE)
(of an animal or person) easily handled or managed; docile and obedient.

Examples

Gentle dog
She preferred to work with gentle dogs because they were easier to train.
The gentle pony stood quietly as the children petted him.
Their gentle manner made them ideal for caring for nervous patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: docile - easily taught or managed; submissive
  • synonym: obedient - willing to comply with orders or requests
  • antonym: wild - not domesticated or not easily controlled
