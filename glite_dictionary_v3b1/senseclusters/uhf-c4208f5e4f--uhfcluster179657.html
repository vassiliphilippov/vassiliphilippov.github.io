UHF

noun
ultra high frequency radio range
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌjuː ˌeɪtʃ ˈɛf / (AmE), / ˌjuː ˌeɪtʃ ˈɛf / (BrE)
the range of radio frequencies from 300 to 3000 megahertz, used in television broadcasting, mobile phones, and other communications

UHF radio frequencies
