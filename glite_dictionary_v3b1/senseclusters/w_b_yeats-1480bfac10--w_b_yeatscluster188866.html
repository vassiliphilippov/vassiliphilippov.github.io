W. B. Yeats

proper noun
Irish poet and dramatist
Pronunciation: / ˌdʌbəl ˈju ˌbi ˈjeɪts / (AmE), / ˌdʌbl juː ˌbiː ˈjeɪts / (BrE)
(1865–1939) Irish poet and dramatist, a leading figure of 20th-century literature and a founder of the Irish Literary Revival.
