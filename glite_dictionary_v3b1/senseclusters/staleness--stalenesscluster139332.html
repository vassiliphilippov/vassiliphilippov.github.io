staleness

noun
the quality of being stale or no longer fresh
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsteɪl.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality of having lost freshness, purity, or interest as a result of aging or being exposed to air.

Examples

Staleness in the air
He could detect the staleness in the air of the abandoned house.
Her jokes had a certain staleness after being repeated so many times.
The staleness of the bread made it unappetizing for breakfast.

Related Terms

  • antonym: freshness - the state of being fresh
