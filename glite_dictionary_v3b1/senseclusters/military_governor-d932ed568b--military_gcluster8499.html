military governor

noun
head of military-established government
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪtɛri ˈɡʌvənɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈɡʌvənə / (BrE)
the head of a government established and administered by the military, especially in an occupied or defeated country

Examples

A military governor appointed
After the coup, a military governor was appointed to oversee the region.
All official decisions had to be approved by the military governor.
The military governor imposed strict curfews during the occupation period.

Related Terms

  • synonym: governor - the official head of a region, state, or colony
