rich

adjective
causing indignation due to hypocrisy or irony
8.3
Pronunciation: / rɪtʃ / (AmE), / rɪtʃ / (BrE)
(of a statement or action) causing indignation because of hypocrisy or irony, especially when someone criticizes others for faults they themselves possess.

Examples

Rich coming from you
Her calling me untrustworthy is rich considering all the lies she's told.
It's a bit rich for him to complain about lateness when he's always late himself.
That's rich coming from someone who never helps anyone else.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypocritical - pretending to have moral standards one does not have
