animatistic

adjective
relating to animatism (religious belief)
3.2 9.9
Pronunciation: /æn.ɪ.mə.ˈtɪs.tɪk/ /; / /æn.ə.mə.ˈtɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /æn.ə.mə.ˈtɪs.tɪk/ /; / /æn.ɪ.mə.ˈtɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
of or relating to animatism, the belief that all objects, places, and creatures possess a supernatural force or power.

Examples

Animatistic belief system
An animatistic worldview sees mountains and rivers as sources of supernatural energy.
Anthropologists have documented animatistic practices in several indigenous societies.
The tribe's rituals are deeply animatistic, emphasizing the spirits believed to inhabit natural objects.
