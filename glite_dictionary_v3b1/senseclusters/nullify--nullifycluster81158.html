Examples

Synonyms

  • cancel out
    4.0 1.0/m
    cancel out neutralizes two opposing effects so they have no impact, while nullify removes something's legal or practical force.
  • null
    7.8 0.06/m
    null makes a value or rule have no effect, while nullify is a more common verb that cancels or voids something.
  • neutralize
    5.6 2/m
    neutralize makes a dangerous or active thing harmless by acting against it, while nullify cancels its effect or value.
  • negate
    5.3 5/m
    negate makes a claim or effect not true or effective, while nullify focuses on canceling effect or legal power.
  • counteract
    4.8 4/m
    counteract acts directly against a force to reduce its power, while nullify removes the effect completely or makes it void.
  • countervail
    9.3 0.9/m
    countervail balances or offsets an effect, often in policy or trade, while nullify cancels or removes the effect entirely.
  • undo
    2.6 3/m
    undo reverses an action to restore a previous state, while nullify makes the action's result lose its effect or value.
