backing

noun
supporting material added at the back
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbækɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbækɪŋ / (BrE)
a layer or piece of material forming the back of something, added to give support or strength

Examples

Cardboard backing
The frame had a sturdy backing to help it stay upright.
The rug's rubber backing prevents it from slipping on the floor.
We glued a sheet of cardboard as a backing for the photograph.
