response time

noun
interval between stimulus and response
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈspɑːns taɪm/ /; / /riː.ˈspɑːns taɪm/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈspɒns taɪm/ (BrE)

Examples
