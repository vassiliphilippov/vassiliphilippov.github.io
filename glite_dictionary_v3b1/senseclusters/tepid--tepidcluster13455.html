tepid

adjective
moderately warm; lukewarm
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛpɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɛpɪd / (BrE)
(of a liquid or temperature) only slightly warm; lukewarm.

Examples

Tepid water
After the power outage, the shower was only tepid instead of hot.
She poured herself a glass of tepid water because she didn't like it cold.
The soup was served tepid, much to the customers’ disappointment.

  • synonym: lukewarm - slightly warm; not hot or cold
