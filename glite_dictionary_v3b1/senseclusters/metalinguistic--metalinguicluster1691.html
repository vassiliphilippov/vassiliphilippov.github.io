metalinguistic

adjective
about language itself
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˌlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˌlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to language that refers to, discusses, or analyzes itself, especially its form or use

Examples

Metalinguistic awareness
The teacher encouraged metalinguistic awareness by asking students to think about how words are formed.
In her essay, she used metalinguistic comments to explain her choice of vocabulary.
Children develop metalinguistic skills as they learn to recognize puns and jokes about language.
Teachers use metalinguistic activities to help students think about how language works.
Her metalinguistic skills improved as she learned to compare different languages.
A metalinguistic comment is one that talks about the language being used.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: linguistic - relating to language or linguistics
  • synonym: self-referential - referring to itself or its own category
