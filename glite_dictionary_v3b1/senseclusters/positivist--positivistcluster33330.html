positivist

adjective
relating to positivism (philosophy)
9.0 0.65/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ëÀêz.…™.t…™.v…™st / (AmE), / Ààp…íz.…™.t…™.v…™st / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of positivism, a philosophical theory asserting that only factual knowledge gained through observation and experience is meaningful.

Examples

Positivist approach
Her views on law are firmly rooted in the positivist tradition.
Many positivist thinkers argue that only observable phenomena should be studied in science.
The university course explores the influence of positivist ideology on social research.

Synonyms

  • positivistic
    9.3 0.20/m
    positivistic describes an approach that emphasizes observable facts and scientific method, while positivist names a person or position that holds that belief.
  • positive
    8.4 0.44/m
    positive is a common, broader adjective often meaning certain or optimistic, while positivist specifically refers to the philosophical approach.
