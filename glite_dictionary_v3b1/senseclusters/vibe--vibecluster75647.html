vibe

noun
distinctive emotional atmosphere or feeling
Pronunciation: / vaɪb / (AmE), / vaɪb / (BrE)
a distinctive emotional quality, mood, or atmosphere that you sense about a place, situation, person, or music

Examples

A cool vibe
His apartment gives off a creative vibe with all the artwork on the walls.
She didn't like the vibe at the party, so she decided to leave early.
The new café had a relaxed vibe that made everyone feel welcome.
Her music creates a mellow vibe that helps people relax.
The café had such a welcoming vibe that we stayed for hours.
There's a fun vibe at the summer festivals every year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ambience - the character and atmosphere of a place
  • synonym: atmosphere - the pervading tone or mood of a place or situation
  • synonym: aura - a distinctive feeling or character that seems to surround something
