dominium

noun
supreme authority
9.9
Pronunciation: / doʊˈmɪniəm / (AmE), / dəˈmɪnɪəm / (BrE)
the supreme power or authority held by a ruler, state, or governing body; sovereignty

Examples

Royal dominium
The king's dominium was recognized by all the neighboring kingdoms.
After the revolution, the new government asserted its dominium over the entire region.
The treaty marked the end of foreign dominium in the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domination - the exercise of control or influence over someone or something
  • synonym: sovereignty - supreme power or authority over a territory
  • antonym: subordination - the state of being under the authority or control of another
