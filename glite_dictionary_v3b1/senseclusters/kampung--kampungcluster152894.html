kampung

noun
traditional village
Pronunciation: / kɑmˈpʊŋ / (AmE), / kæmˈpʊŋ / (BrE)
a traditional village or rural settlement, especially in Malaysia or Indonesia

Examples

A coastal kampung
She grew up in a small kampung near the coast.
Many families in the kampung gather together for community celebrations.
The kampung was surrounded by rice fields and coconut trees.

Related Terms

  • antonym: city - a large and densely populated urban area
  • synonym: village - a small community or group of houses in a rural area
