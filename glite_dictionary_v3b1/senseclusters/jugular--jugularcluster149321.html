jugular

adjective
of or pertaining to the neck or throat
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌɡjələr / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌɡjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the region of the neck or throat, especially referring to veins in that area

Examples

Jugular vessel damage
Injury to a jugular vessel can cause serious bleeding.
The nurse checked for a pulse in the patient's jugular area.
The surgeon carefully examined the jugular vein during the operation.
