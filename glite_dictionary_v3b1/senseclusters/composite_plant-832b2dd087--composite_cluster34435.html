composite plant

noun
plant with clustered florets (Asteraceae)
6.3 5.0
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɑːzɪt plænt / (AmE), / kəmˈpɒzɪt plɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant belonging to the family Asteraceae, characterized by dense clusters of small florets that resemble a single flower

Examples

A composite plant flower
Many composite plants are valued in gardens for their long-lasting blooms and variety of colors.
Sunflower and dandelion are both types of composite plants found in many temperate regions.
The daisy is a well-known example of a composite plant with its characteristic flower structure.
