glassmaker

noun
person who makes glass
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes glass, especially by hand or in a workshop

Examples

A glassmaker at work

The new museum exhibition showcases the work of local glassmakers from the 18th century.
