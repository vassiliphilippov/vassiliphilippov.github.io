easternmost

adjective
farthest to the east
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstərnˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈiːstənˌməʊst / (BrE)
Farthest towards the east or most easterly in position or location.

Examples

The easternmost point
The easternmost point of the island is marked by a lighthouse.
The city is situated at the easternmost edge of the country.
They traveled to the easternmost province in search of rare wildlife.
