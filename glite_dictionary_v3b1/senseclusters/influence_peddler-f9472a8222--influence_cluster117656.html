influence peddler

noun
person gaining favors for payment, often illegally
3.4 7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈɪn.fluː.əns ˈpɛd.lər/ (AmE), /ˈɪn.fluː.əns ˈpɛd.lə/ (BrE)
a person who uses their position or connections to gain favors or advantages for others in return for payment, often through improper or illegal means
