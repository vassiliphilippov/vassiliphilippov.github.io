Leibnitzian

adjective
relating to Leibniz or his philosophy
Pronunciation: / laɪbˈnɪtsiən / (AmE), / laɪbˈnɪtsiən / (BrE)
of or relating to Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz or to his mathematics or philosophy

Examples

Leibnitzian philosophy
His metaphysical arguments closely follow Leibnitzian principles.
The book provides an excellent introduction to Leibnitzian logic and its influence on modern philosophy.
The scientist adopted a Leibnitzian view of the universe, emphasizing reason and harmony.
