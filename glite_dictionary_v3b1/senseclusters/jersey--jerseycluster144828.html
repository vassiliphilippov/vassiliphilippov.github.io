jersey

noun
close-fitting pullover shirt
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɝzi / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːzi / (BrE)
A close-fitting pullover shirt, typically made of knitted fabric, worn as part of a uniform or for sports.

Examples

A football jersey
Each player wears a numbered jersey on the field.
He framed his favorite team's jersey and hung it on the wall.
Her new jersey is made of lightweight, breathable material.
