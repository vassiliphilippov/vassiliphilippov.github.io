spayed

adjective
female animal sterilized by ovary removal
9.9 3.6
Pronunciation: /speɪd/ (AmE), /speɪd/ (BrE)
(of a female animal) having had the ovaries surgically removed so as to prevent reproduction.

Examples

Spayed female pets
All pets must be vaccinated and spayed or neutered prior to adoption.
Our cat was spayed at the animal shelter before we adopted her.
The veterinarian recommended having your dog spayed to prevent unwanted litters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intact - (of an animal) not having been sterilized or had reproductive organs removed
  • synonym: neutered - (of an animal) having reproductive organs removed or rendered inoperative, often used for males but also as a general term
