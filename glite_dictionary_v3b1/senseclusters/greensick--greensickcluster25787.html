greensick

adjective
affected by chlorosis (archaic)
9.6 9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgriːn.sɪk/ (AmE), /ˈgriːn.sɪk/ (BrE)
(archaic) affected by or relating to chlorosis, a form of anemia, especially in young women, characterized by a pale, greenish complexion.

Examples

A greensick woman
In Victorian novels, a young woman might be called greensick if she appeared pallid and listless.
Poets once described pale, greensick maidens wasting away in solitude.
The doctor diagnosed her as greensick and prescribed a change of diet.
