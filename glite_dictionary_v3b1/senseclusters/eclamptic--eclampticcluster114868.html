eclamptic

noun
person with eclampsia
Pronunciation: / ɪˈklæmptɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈklæmptɪk / (BrE)
a person, especially a pregnant woman, who is affected by eclampsia

Examples

An eclamptic patient
The eclamptic was closely monitored by the medical team throughout her labor.
Doctors acted quickly when the eclamptic began to show signs of severe complications.
After treatment, the eclamptic gradually recovered and was able to return home.
