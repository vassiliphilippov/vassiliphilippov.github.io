mandibular joint

noun
joint between lower jaw and skull
7.3
Pronunciation: / mænˈdɪbjələr dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / mænˈdɪbjʊlə dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
the joint connecting the lower jawbone (mandible) to the temporal bone of the skull

Examples

The mandibular joint pain
An injury to the mandibular joint may lead to chronic discomfort if not treated properly.
Problems with the mandibular joint can cause difficulty in chewing and speaking.
The dentist examined her mandibular joint after she complained of jaw pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: temporomandibular joint - the hinge joint connecting the jaw and skull, also known as TMJ
