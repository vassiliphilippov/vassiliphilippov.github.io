eastern pipistrel

noun
a tiny North American bat species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstɚn pɪˈpɪstrəl / (AmE), / ˈiːstən pɪˈpɪstrəl / (BrE)
a very small bat native to eastern North America, known for its rapid flight and insect-eating habits

Examples

The eastern pipistrel bat
Despite its small size, the eastern pipistrel plays an important role in controlling insect populations.
Scientists studied the hibernation patterns of the eastern pipistrel in several states.
The eastern pipistrel is often seen fluttering around streetlights at dusk.
