unendurably

adverb
intolerably
7.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnɪnˈdʊrəbli / (AmE), / ʌnɪnˈdjʊərəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is impossible to tolerate or endure

Examples

An unendurably painful feeling
The pain became unendurably intense as the night went on.
It was unendurably hot in the crowded subway car during rush hour.
She found the silence in the empty house unendurably long after her family left.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bearably - in a way that can be tolerated or endured
  • synonym: intolerably - in a way that cannot be tolerated
