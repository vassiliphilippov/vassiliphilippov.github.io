musical

adjective
talented in or devoted to music
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːzɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl / (BrE)
having a natural talent for music or showing a great interest in music; devoted to music.

Examples

A musical child
As a child, he was extremely musical and could play several instruments by ear.
She comes from a very musical family, with both parents being professional musicians.
The school encourages musical students to join the orchestra and choir.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unmusical - not having talent or interest in music
