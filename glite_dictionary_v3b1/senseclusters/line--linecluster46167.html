line

verb
reinforce inside with material
2.3
Pronunciation: / laɪn / (AmE), / laɪn / (BrE)
to cover or reinforce the inside surface of something with another material

Examples

To line a bag
She decided to line her handbag with soft fabric for added durability.
Some publishers line their books with special paper to protect the cover.
The tailor will line the coat with silk to make it more comfortable.
She decided to line the drawers with scented paper to keep them fresh.
The workers will line the tunnel with concrete to reinforce it.
You should line the baking pan with parchment paper before pouring in the batter.
