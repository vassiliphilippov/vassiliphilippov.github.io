earthlike

adjective
of a color resembling earth or soil
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɜːθ.laɪk / (BrE)
Having a color similar to that of soil or earth; earth colored.

Examples

Earthlike colors
She wore a dress of earthlike tones that blended with the landscape.
The artist used earthlike colors to evoke a sense of natural beauty in his painting.
The walls were painted in an earthlike hue that gave the room a warm feel.
