float

noun
flotation therapy session
7.7
Pronunciation: / flo8at / (AmE), / fl598at / (BrE)
a session of flotation therapy in which a person lies in a dark, quiet tank of highly salted water to relax and reduce sensory input.

Examples

A therapy float
She booked a one-hour float at the wellness center to relax.
After a stressful week he tried his first float and found it deeply calming and restorative.
The spa's new package includes three floats and guided breathing exercises for beginners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flotation therapy - a wellness treatment in which a person floats in saltwater inside a dark tank to reduce sensory stimulation
  • synonym: sensory deprivation tank - an enclosed tank filled with heavily salted water used for flotation to reduce external sensory input
