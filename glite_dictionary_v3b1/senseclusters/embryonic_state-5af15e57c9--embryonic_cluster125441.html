embryonic state

noun
early or undeveloped stage
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛm.briˈɑnɪk steɪt / (AmE), / ˌɛm.brɪˈɒnɪk steɪt / (BrE)
An early or undeveloped stage in the development of something.

Examples
