The number of musical beats occurring in one minute, used to measure the tempo of a piece of music.

Examples

Song beats per minute
Most running playlists feature tracks with around 120 beats per minute to keep a steady pace.
She set the metronome to 80 beats per minute to help her practice the piece slowly.
The song has a rapid tempo of 140 beats per minute, making it popular in dance clubs.

Synonyms

  • bpm
    3.6 0.34/m
    bpm is a short form used on recordings and charts to show tempo, while beats per minute is the full unit.
  • metronome marking
    6.6 0.00/m
    metronome marking is a notation on the score that gives tempo as a number, while beats per minute is the unit.
  • M.M.
    8.2 0.00/m
    M.M. is a score abbreviation composers use to mark tempo with a number, while beats per minute names the numeric rate.
