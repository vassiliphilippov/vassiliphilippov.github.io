Noruz

noun
Persian New Year holiday at the spring equinox
9.7
Pronunciation: / noʊˈruːz / (AmE), / nɔːˈruːz / (BrE)
The traditional New Year holiday celebrated at the vernal equinox in Iran, Azerbaijan, Afghanistan, Pakistan, parts of India, and among the Kurds.

Examples

Noruz holiday
Noruz marks the arrival of spring in many countries across Central and South Asia.
Families gather to celebrate Noruz with special meals and traditional rituals.
The city held a festival in honor of Noruz, featuring music, dance, and fireworks.
