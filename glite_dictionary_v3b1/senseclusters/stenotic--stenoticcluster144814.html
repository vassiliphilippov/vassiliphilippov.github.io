stenotic

adjective
abnormally narrowed canal or passage (medical)
Pronunciation: / stəˈnɑː.tɪk / (AmE), / stəˈnɒ.tɪk / (BrE)
(of a body canal or passage) abnormally narrowed or constricted.

Examples

A stenotic airway
A stenotic airway can cause breathing difficulties and should be treated promptly.
Radiologists detected a stenotic segment in the patient's carotid artery.
The patient was diagnosed with a stenotic mitral valve, requiring surgical intervention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stenosis - abnormal narrowing of a bodily passage
