exchange

noun
mutual discussion, often heated
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
a dialogue or conversation, especially one that is heated or unpleasant, in which people express their views to each other

Examples

Heated exchange
During the meeting, there was a tense exchange between the two colleagues.
The candidates engaged in an animated exchange about education policies.
They had a brief but sharp exchange before leaving the room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agreement - harmony or consensus, opposite of a heated discussion
  • synonym: argument - an exchange of diverging or opposite views, typically heated or angry
  • synonym: disagreement - a lack of consensus or harmony, often expressed in discussion
