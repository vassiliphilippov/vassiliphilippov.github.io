prayer book

noun
book of prayers for worship or devotion
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛr ˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈpreə ˌbʊk / (BrE)
a book containing a collection of prayers, often used in religious worship or personal devotion

Examples

A prayer book
He opened his prayer book to recite the evening prayers.
She carried her prayer book to church every Sunday morning.
The old family prayer book was passed down through generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hymnal - a book of hymns and religious songs
  • synonym: missal - a book containing the texts used in the Catholic Mass
