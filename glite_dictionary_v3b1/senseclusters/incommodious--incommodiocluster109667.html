incommodious

adjective
uncomfortably small or cramped
9.0 9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkəˈmoʊdiəs / (AmE), / ˌɪnkəˈməʊdiəs / (BrE)
(of a room, building, or space) uncomfortably or inconveniently small or cramped.

Examples

We found the incommodious apartment difficult to live in for a family of four.
