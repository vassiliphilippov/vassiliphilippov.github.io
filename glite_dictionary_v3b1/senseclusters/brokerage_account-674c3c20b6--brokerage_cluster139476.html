brokerage account

noun
account for trading securities through a broker
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊkəɹɪdʒ əˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkərɪdʒ əˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
an account held with a securities brokerage that allows a customer to deposit funds and trade financial assets such as stocks and bonds

Examples

A brokerage account online
A brokerage account enables users to buy and sell shares directly online.
She opened a brokerage account to start investing in the stock market.
To access certain investment options, you must have a brokerage account.

Related Terms

  • antonym: savings account - a bank account that earns interest
  • synonym: trading account - an account for buying and selling financial instruments
