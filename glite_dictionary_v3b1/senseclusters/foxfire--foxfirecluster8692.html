foxfire

noun
faint marsh light
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑksˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɒksˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a faint, natural greenish light seen at night, produced by certain fungi growing on decaying wood
