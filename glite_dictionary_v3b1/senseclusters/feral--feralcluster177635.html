feral

noun
wild former pet
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪərəl / (BrE)
an animal that lives in the wild after having been domesticated or kept in captivity

Examples

A feral dog
The park is home to several ferals that once belonged to local families.
Animal control officers are working to manage the population of ferals in the city.
Many ferals struggle to survive without regular food or shelter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domesticated - an animal tamed and kept by humans
  • synonym: stray - a domestic animal that has wandered away or is lost
