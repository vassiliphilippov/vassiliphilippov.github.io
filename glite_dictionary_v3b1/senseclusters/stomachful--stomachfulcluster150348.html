stomachful

noun
amount to fill stomach
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌməkˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈstʌməkˌfʊl / (BrE)
an amount of food or drink that fills the stomach completely

Examples

A stomachful of food
After eating a stomachful of pasta, she felt too full to move.
He drank a stomachful of water after running in the heat.
It is not healthy to eat more than a stomachful at one meal.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bellyful - as much as the belly can hold; a large amount
  • degree lower: mouthful - a small amount of food or drink that fills the mouth
