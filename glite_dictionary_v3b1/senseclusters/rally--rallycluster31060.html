rally

noun
recovery of health or spirits during illness
4.2 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈræ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈræ.liː/ (BrE)
a period of recovery, renewed effort, or improvement after a decline or weakness

Examples

Stock rally
After several days of decline, there was a brief rally in her condition before she relapsed.
The doctor's optimism grew when he saw the rally in his patient's spirits and strength.
The patient showed a surprising rally late in the afternoon, giving hope to his family.
Despite trailing most of the game, the team mounted an impressive rally in the final minutes.
The doctor's words inspired a rally of hope among the patient's family.
There was a sudden rally in the stock market after weeks of decline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recovery - the process of becoming healthy again
  • synonym: revival - a process of becoming strong, active, or popular again
