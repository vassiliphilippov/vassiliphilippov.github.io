metaphorically

adverb
in a metaphorical, not literal, way
1.0 5.4
Pronunciation: /me.tə.ˈfɔːr.ɪ.kliː/ (AmE), /me.tə.ˈfɔː.rɪ.kliː/ (BrE)
In a way that uses or relates to metaphor; not literally.

Examples

Used metaphorically
She described the challenge metaphorically as climbing a mountain.
The author often speaks metaphorically to express deeper truths.
When he said his hands were tied, he meant it metaphorically, not that he was actually restrained.
