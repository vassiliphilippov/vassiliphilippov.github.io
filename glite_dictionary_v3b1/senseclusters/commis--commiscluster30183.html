commis

noun
junior kitchen assistant
Pronunciation: / kəˈmi / (AmE), / kɒˈmi / (BrE)
a junior cook or kitchen assistant in a professional kitchen, responsible for basic food preparation tasks

Examples

Kitchen commis
The commis arrived early to help set up the kitchen before the restaurant opened.
As a commis, she learned how to prepare ingredients and follow recipes from the head chef.
Many famous chefs started their careers as a commis in busy hotel kitchens.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: chef - the main cook in a professional kitchen
  • degree lower: kitchen porter - a person who assists with cleaning and basic tasks in a kitchen
