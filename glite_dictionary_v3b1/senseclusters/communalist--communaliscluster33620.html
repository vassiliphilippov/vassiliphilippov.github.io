communalist

adjective
supporting communalism
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunəlɪst / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnəlɪst / (BrE)
relating to or supporting communalism, especially by promoting the interests of a specific religious or ethnic community, sometimes causing division between groups

Examples

Communalist politics
The party's communalist agenda was criticized for deepening social divisions.
Some leaders adopted a communalist approach, focusing on the needs of their own community.
Critics argue that communalist policies can undermine national unity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inclusive - not limited to one group; including all people
  • synonym: sectarian - relating to or promoting the interests of a particular group, often causing division
