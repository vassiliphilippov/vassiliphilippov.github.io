rubbernecker

noun
person who stares inquisitively, often at accidents
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbərˌnɛkər / (AmE), / ˈrʌbəˌnɛkə / (BrE)
a person who stares with excessive or inquisitive curiosity, especially at accidents or unusual events

Examples

A rubbernecker at accident
At parades, a dedicated rubbernecker will jockey for the best view of the festivities.
The highway slowed to a crawl because of every rubbernecker trying to see the accident scene.
The police criticized the rubberneckers for blocking emergency vehicles by watching the fire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bystander - a person who is present at an event but does not take part
