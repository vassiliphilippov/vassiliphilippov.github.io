beach pancake

noun
coastal plant with wine-red flower heads
9.9
Pronunciation: / bitʃ ˈpænkeɪk / (AmE), / biːtʃ ˈpænkeɪk / (BrE)
A North American coastal plant with hemispherical heads of dark red flowers, found in dunes from California to Mexico.
