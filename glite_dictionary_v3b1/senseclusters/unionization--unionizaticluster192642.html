unionization

noun
the process of forming a labor union
8.0 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːniənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌjuːniənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of forming, joining, or organizing workers into a labor union; the act or policy of organizing workers into unions

Examples

Unionization of workers
Concerns about unionization surfaced during contract negotiations with management.
Rapid unionization of factories led to better working conditions for many laborers.
The company resisted unionization efforts by its employees for many years.
Debates over unionisation continue to shape labor policies worldwide.
Management opposed the unionisation of their employees, fearing increased wage demands.
The company faced a wave of unionisation efforts among its warehouse staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collectivization - the process of organizing people or things into a collective group, often in agriculture or labor
  • antonym: deunionization - the process of dissolving or reducing the influence of labor unions
  • synonym: unionization - the process of forming or joining labor unions (American spelling)
  • synonym: unionize - to organize workers into a labor union
