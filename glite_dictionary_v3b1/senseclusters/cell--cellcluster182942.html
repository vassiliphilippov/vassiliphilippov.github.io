cell

noun
small compartment or enclosed space
2.5
Pronunciation: / sɛl / (AmE), / sɛl / (BrE)
a small compartment or enclosed space, especially one forming part of a larger structure

Examples

Honeycomb cell
Each cell in the honeycomb contains a drop of honey.
The battery consists of multiple cells connected together.
The ice cube tray has twelve separate cells for making ice.
