röntgen

noun
unit of radiation exposure
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈrɜːnt.gən/ (AmE), /ˈrɜːnt.gən/ (BrE)
a unit used to measure exposure to X-rays and gamma rays, especially in air

Examples

Rontgen value
The technician recorded the patient's exposure in röntgen units.
A single röntgen is a relatively large amount of radiation for humans.
Scientists used the röntgen to compare different X-ray machines' outputs.
