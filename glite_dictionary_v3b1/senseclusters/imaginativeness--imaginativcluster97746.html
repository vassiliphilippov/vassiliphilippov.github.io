imaginativeness

noun
creative ability to form new ideas
5.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪtɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪˌmadʒɪˈneɪtɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having or showing a creative ability to form new ideas, images, or concepts that are not present to the senses

Examples

Creative imaginativeness
Children tend to approach tasks with remarkable imaginativeness.
His imaginativeness made mundane stories come alive with vivid detail.
The imaginativeness of her writing has inspired many young authors.
