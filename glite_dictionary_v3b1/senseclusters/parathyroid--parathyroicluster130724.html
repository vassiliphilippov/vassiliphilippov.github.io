parathyroid

noun
small endocrine gland near the thyroid
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpær.əˈθaɪ.rɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌpær.əˈθaɪ.rɔɪd / (BrE)
any one of the four small endocrine glands located near or within the thyroid gland, which regulate calcium levels in the body

Examples

The parathyroid gland
An ultrasound was performed to check for enlargement of the parathyroid.
Disorders of the parathyroid can lead to abnormal calcium levels.
The surgeon carefully identified each parathyroid gland during the procedure.
