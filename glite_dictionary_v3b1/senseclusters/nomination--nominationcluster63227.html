nomination

noun
the act of officially naming a candidate
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːməˈneɪʃən /; / ˌnɑː.məˈneɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃən /; / ˌnɒ.mɪˈneɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
