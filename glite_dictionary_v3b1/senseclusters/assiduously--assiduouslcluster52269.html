In a way that shows great care, attention, and persistence in carrying out a task.

Examples

To assiduously study
He prepared assiduously for his exams, reviewing all his notes and practice tests.
She completed her research assiduously, paying close attention to every detail.
The gardener assiduously tended to his plants, ensuring they flourished throughout the season.

Synonyms

  • diligently
    4.1 3.40/m
    diligently means working steadily with effort and persistence, while assiduously emphasizes care, close attention, and sustained effort.
  • painstakingly
    6.0 2.16/m
    painstakingly means doing something with great care and thorough detail, while assiduously stresses both careful attention and steady persistence.
  • meticulously
    8.3 3.21/m
    meticulously describes very exact, detailed work, while assiduously adds long-term persistence and broad attention to the whole task.
  • conscientiously
    5.4 0.72/m
    conscientiously means doing work from a sense of duty and care, while assiduously highlights careful attention plus steady persistence.
