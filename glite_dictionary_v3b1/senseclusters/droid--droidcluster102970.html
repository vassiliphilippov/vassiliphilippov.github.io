droid

noun
Android smartphone
4.7
Pronunciation: / drɔɪd / (AmE), / drɔɪd / (BrE)
an informal term for a smartphone that runs the Android operating system, sometimes referring to devices sold under the DROID brand

Examples

Android droid phone
She checked her droid for directions before leaving the house.
Many people prefer a droid because of its customizable features.
His old droid finally stopped working after years of use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Android phone - a smartphone using the Android operating system
  • antonym: iPhone - a smartphone made by Apple using the iOS operating system
