requisitory

noun
formal written demand
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkwəzɪˌtɔri / (AmE), / rɪˈkwɪzɪtəri / (BrE)
a formal written demand or request for goods, money, or resources, especially in official contexts

Examples

Requisitory for funds
The committee issued a requisitory to the treasury for additional funding.
Before supplies could be delivered, a formal requisitory had to be submitted.
The officer prepared a requisitory detailing the resources needed for the expedition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: request - an act of asking for something to be given or done
  • synonym: requisition - an official order demanding the supply of something
