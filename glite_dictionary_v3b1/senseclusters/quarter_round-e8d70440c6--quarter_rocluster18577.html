quarter round

noun
convex molding, quarter-circle shape
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrdər raʊnd / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə raʊnd / (BrE)
a convex molding with a cross section in the shape of a quarter of a circle or ellipse, typically used where floors meet walls

Examples

A quarter round molding
After refinishing the hardwood, she replaced the old quarter round to match the new floor color.
The carpenter installed a quarter round along the base of the wall for a neat finish.
To cover the gap between the floor and the molding, they used a strip of quarter round.
