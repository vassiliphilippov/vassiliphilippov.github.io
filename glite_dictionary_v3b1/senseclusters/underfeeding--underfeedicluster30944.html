underfeeding

noun
insufficient supply to machine
5.5
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dər.ˈfiː.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.ˈfiː.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
the insufficient supply of material, fuel, or input to a machine or process, leading to faults or reduced performance

Examples

Underfeeding of fuel
The engine malfunctioned due to underfeeding of fuel during the test run.
Consistent underfeeding of raw materials can cause a drop in production quality.
Technicians monitored the system to prevent underfeeding and ensure smooth operation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: overfeeding - the excessive supply of material or input to a machine or process
  • synonym: starvation - a severe lack of necessary input or resources
