Jumbotron

proper noun
large stadium screen
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌm.boʊˌtrɑn / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌm.bəʊˌtrɒn / (BrE)
a very large video display screen, especially used at sports stadiums or public events to show images, video, or information to the audience
