guzzling

noun
drinking greedily and rapidly
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌz.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌz.lɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of drinking large amounts of something quickly and greedily.

Examples

Beer guzzling contest
At the festival, the beer guzzling contest drew a large crowd.
He was caught guzzling soda straight from the bottle in the kitchen.
Their guzzling of the expensive wine shocked the party's host.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gulping - the act of swallowing something quickly
  • antonym: sipping - the act of drinking in small quantities
