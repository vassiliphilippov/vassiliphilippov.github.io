bewildering

adjective
very confusing
Pronunciation: / bɪˈwɪldərɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪldərɪŋ / (BrE)
very confusing or difficult to understand

Examples

A bewildering puzzle
The plot of the movie was so bewildering that many viewers left the theater confused.
She found the new tax regulations absolutely bewildering and needed help to understand them.
The maze was bewildering to navigate, with paths twisting in every direction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - easy to understand
  • synonym: confusing - difficult to understand or follow
