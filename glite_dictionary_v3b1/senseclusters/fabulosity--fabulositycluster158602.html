fabulosity

noun
impressive style or glamour
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌfæbjəˈlɑːsəti / (AmE), / ˌfæbjʊˈlɒsəti / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely impressive, stylish, or fabulous

Examples

Fashion fabulosity
The designer's new collection was praised for its sheer fabulosity on the runway.
He walked into the party with an air of fabulosity that turned everyone's head.
Social media influencers often showcase their fabulosity through bold fashion choices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dullness - the quality of being uninteresting or lacking excitement
  • synonym: glamour - an attractive or exciting quality that makes something seem special
