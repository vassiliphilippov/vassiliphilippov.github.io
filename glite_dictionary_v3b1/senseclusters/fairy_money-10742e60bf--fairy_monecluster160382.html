fairy money

noun
illusory or easily spent money
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛri ˈmʌni / (AmE), / ˈfeəri ˈmʌni / (BrE)
money that is easily spent, seems unreal, or is illusory, as if it might disappear like magic

Examples

Fairy money disappears
After winning the lottery, it all felt like fairy money in his bank account.
Bonuses can seem like fairy money, easy to spend without a second thought.
She treats every tax refund as if it’s just fairy money and quickly splurges it.
