ramp up

verb
increase or strengthen
Pronunciation: / ræmp ʌp /; / ræmp / (AmE), / ræmp ʌp /; / ræmp / (BrE)
to increase or make something stronger, especially in amount, speed, or intensity (often used in business or technical contexts)

They will ramp up their marketing efforts ahead of the product launch.

The company decided to ramp up production to meet rising demand.
