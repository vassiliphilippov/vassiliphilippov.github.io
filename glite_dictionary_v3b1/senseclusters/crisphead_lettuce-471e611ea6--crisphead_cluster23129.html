crisphead lettuce

noun
lettuce with tightly packed crisp leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪspˌhɛd ˈlɛtɪs / (AmE), / ˈkrɪspˌhɛd ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
A type of lettuce with crisp, tightly packed light-green leaves forming a firm head, commonly known as iceberg lettuce.
