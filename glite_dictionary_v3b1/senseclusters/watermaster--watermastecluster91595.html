watermaster

noun
water resources official
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəməstə / (BrE)
an official responsible for managing and distributing water resources, especially in regions where water rights are regulated

Examples

The watermaster allocates water
The watermaster allocated water rights during the drought to ensure fair distribution.
Farmers met with the watermaster to discuss their irrigation needs for the upcoming season.
A new watermaster was appointed to oversee the river basin's water usage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrator - a person responsible for managing an organization or system
  • synonym: water bailiff - an official who supervises the use of water, especially in the UK
