plumper

adjective
more rounded or full
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌmpər / (AmE), / ˈplʌmpə / (BrE)
having a more rounded, fuller, or thicker shape or appearance than something else.

Examples

Plumper cheeks
After a week of rain, the berries looked plumper and juicier than before.
She chose the plumper cushion for her chair because it was more comfortable.
The cat became plumper over the winter months from eating extra food.
