dissenter

noun
one who disagrees with established views or authority
5.9 5.7
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈsen.tər/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈsen.tə/ (BrE)
a person who disagrees with or refuses to conform to an established policy, belief, or authority

Examples

A dissenter disagrees
As a dissenter, she was often at odds with her colleagues on major issues.
During the meeting, one dissenter stood up to voice his opposition to the proposed policy.
The government faced criticism from every dissenter in the assembly.
