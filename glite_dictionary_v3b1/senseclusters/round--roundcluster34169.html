round

noun
chair leg crosspiece
Pronunciation: / raʊnd / (AmE), / raʊnd / (BrE)
A crosspiece connecting and strengthening the legs of a chair or similar item of furniture.

Examples

Chair round
Carpenters often replace a broken round to restore the sturdiness of an old chair.
She tightened the joints where the round connected to each leg.
The antique chair was missing a round, which made it unstable.
