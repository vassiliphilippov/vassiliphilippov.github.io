next door

adverb
in the adjacent residence or building
1.0 16/m
Pronunciation: / Àån…õkst Ààd…îr / (AmE), / Àån…õkst Ààd…îÀê / (BrE)
in or at the house, apartment, or building that is immediately adjacent to one's own.

Examples

The next door neighbour
Someone has parked their car right next door for the whole week.
The new neighbors who moved next door are very friendly.
When I was a child, my best friend lived next door.

Synonyms

  • neighboring
    1.9 0.01/m
    neighboring describes things or areas that touch or are near each other, while next door refers specifically to the house or building beside yours.
