Examples

To convey goods

Synonyms

  • transport
    1.4 18/m
    transport moves people or goods, often by vehicle or company, while convey means to carry something from one place to another.
  • take
    1.0 655/m
    take means to carry or lead someone or something away, while convey focuses on moving items from one place to another.
  • bring
    1.0 317/m
    bring means to carry something toward a place or person, while convey is a general word for moving things between places.
  • deliver
    1.0 51/m
    deliver means to take goods to a person and complete the handover, while convey is simply to move something between places.
  • transfer
    2.7 32/m
    transfer means to move someone or something between roles, places, or groups, while convey mainly means to carry things from one place to another.
  • send
    1.0 126/m
    send means to cause someone or something to go, often by order or message, while convey means to carry physically from place to place.
