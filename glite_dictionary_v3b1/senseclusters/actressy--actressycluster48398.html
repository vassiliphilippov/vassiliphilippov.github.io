actressy

adjective
overly theatrical
Pronunciation: / ˈæktrəsi / (AmE), / ˈæktrəsi / (BrE)
behaving in an exaggerated, theatrical, or affected way, like an actor or actress

Examples

An actressy performance
Her reaction to the news was a bit too actressy for my taste.
He gave an actressy speech that felt more like a performance than a genuine message.
The director asked her to be less actressy and more authentic in the next scene.

Related Terms

  • antonym: natural - not affected or artificial; genuine
  • synonym: theatrical - exaggerated or overly dramatic in manner or style
