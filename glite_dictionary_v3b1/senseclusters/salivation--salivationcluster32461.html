salivation

noun
the production of saliva
3.5
Pronunciation: / sæləˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / salɪˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the production and release of saliva in the mouth

Examples

Excessive salivation
Excessive salivation can be a symptom of certain medical conditions.
Some medications list salivation as a possible side effect.
When he smelled the food, his salivation increased automatically.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drooling - letting saliva fall from the mouth uncontrollably
