hotbed

noun

A bed of earth, covered with glass and heated by fermenting manure, used to encourage the growth of plants.

Examples

A hotbed for seedlings
Gardeners built a hotbed to start their vegetables before transplanting them outdoors.
The seedlings thrived in the hotbed during the early spring months.
Using a hotbed can significantly advance the growing season for many plants.
