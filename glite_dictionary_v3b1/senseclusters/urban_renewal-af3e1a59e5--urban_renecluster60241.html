urban renewal

noun
redevelopment of city slum areas
3.6 0.60/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ùÀêb…ôn r…™ÀànuÀê…ôl / (AmE), / Àà…úÀêb…ôn r…™ÀànjuÀê…ôl / (BrE)
the process of clearing, rebuilding, and redeveloping areas of a city that are considered run-down or slums, often to improve housing and infrastructure

Examples

Urban renewal projects
After years of urban renewal, the downtown area became more attractive to businesses and tourists.
Many residents opposed the urban renewal plan because it threatened to displace longtime communities.
The city launched an urban renewal project to replace old buildings with modern housing.

Synonyms

  • redevelopment
    3.5 1.52/m
    redevelopment is the process of rebuilding or making major physical changes to an area, while urban renewal focuses on clearing and improving run-down city districts.
  • regeneration
    5.8 1.95/m
    regeneration means renewing or restoring both buildings and communities, while urban renewal refers specifically to clearing and rebuilding poor city areas.
  • revitalization
    5.8 1.85/m
    revitalization brings energy, new businesses, and public life to areas, while urban renewal mainly involves clearing and rebuilding housing and roads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gentrification - the process of changing an area so that it is more appealing to people with money
  • antonym: urban decay - the process whereby a city falls into disrepair and dilapidation
