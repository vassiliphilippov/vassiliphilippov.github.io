stridently

adverb
in a loud and harsh manner
4.8 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈstraɪ.dənt.liː/ (AmE), /ˈstraɪ.dənt.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that is loud, harsh, and unpleasant to listen to; in a forcefully assertive or strident manner.

Examples
