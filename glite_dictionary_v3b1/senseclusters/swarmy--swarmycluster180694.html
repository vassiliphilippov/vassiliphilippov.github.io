swarmy

adjective
overly flattering
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːrmi / (AmE), / ˈswɑːmi / (BrE)
behaving in an overly flattering, ingratiating, or excessively smooth manner

Examples

Swarmy compliments
His swarmy compliments made everyone uncomfortable at the meeting.
People often distrust someone with a swarmy attitude in business negotiations.
She found his swarmy behavior insincere and off-putting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genuine - honest and sincere, not pretending or flattering
  • synonym: obsequious - excessively eager to please or obey someone
  • synonym: sycophantic - behaving in a way that flatters someone powerful for personal gain
