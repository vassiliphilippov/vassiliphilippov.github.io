poplar

noun
type of tall temperate tree
4.4 1.39/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ëÀê.pl…ö / (AmE), / Ààp…íp.l…ô / (BrE)
a tall tree of northern temperate regions, typically with light, soft wood and catkin flowers, belonging to the genus Populus

Examples

Tall poplar tree
He used poplar wood to build lightweight furniture for his workshop.
Many artists have painted landscapes featuring clusters of poplar in the background.
The avenue was lined with tall poplar trees swaying gently in the breeze.

Synonyms

  • poplar tree
    3.0 0.17/m
    poplar tree is the everyday name for the tall, fast-growing tree, while poplar is the shorter, standard noun used in gardening and books.

Related Terms

  • synonym: willow - deciduous tree with slender branches, related to poplars
