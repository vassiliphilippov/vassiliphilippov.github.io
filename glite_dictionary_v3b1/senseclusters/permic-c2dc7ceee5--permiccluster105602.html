Permic

noun
Finnic language group of the northwest Urals
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrmɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɜːmɪk / (BrE)
a group of closely related Finnic languages spoken in the northwest region of the Ural Mountains, principally including Udmurt and Komi.

Examples

Permic languages group
Researchers have traced several linguistic similarities among the Permic languages.
The Permic languages are still spoken by indigenous peoples in the Urals.
Udmurt and Komi both belong to the Permic branch of the Uralic language family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Komi - a language belonging to the Permic branch
  • synonym: Udmurt - a Permic language spoken in Russia
