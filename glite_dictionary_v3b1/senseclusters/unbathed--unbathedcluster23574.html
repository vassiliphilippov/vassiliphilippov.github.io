unbathed

adjective
not covered or soaked
5.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbeɪðd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbeɪðd / (BrE)
not covered, soaked, or filled with a particular substance, quality, or light; not bathed in something.

Examples

Unbathed in sunlight
The old painting hung unbathed in sunlight, its colors fading in the dim room.
After the rain, the street remained unbathed in the golden glow of the evening.
The garden was unbathed in dew, leaving the leaves dry and crisp at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - not covered or filled with anything
  • antonym: bathed - covered or filled with a substance, quality, or light
