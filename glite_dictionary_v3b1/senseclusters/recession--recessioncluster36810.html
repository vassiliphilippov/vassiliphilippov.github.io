recession

noun
the act of becoming more distant
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɛʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛʃən / (BrE)
the act of moving back or becoming more distant from a previous position

Examples

Recession of the glacier
Scientists observed the recession of the comet as it moved away from the sun.
The recession of the coastline over centuries has left the old port far inland.
The recession of the ice sheets changed the landscape dramatically.

Related Terms

  • synonym: retreat - the act of withdrawing or moving back
