steering committee

noun
group organizing and guiding activities or agendas
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪrɪŋ kəˈmɪti / (AmE), / ˈstɪərɪŋ kəˈmɪti / (BrE)
a group of people chosen to organize, direct, or make decisions about the activities or agenda of a larger body or event

Examples

The steering committee meeting
As chair of the steering committee, she coordinated all preparations for the conference.
The steering committee meets monthly to set the agenda for the board meetings.
The university formed a steering committee to oversee the development of its new research center.
