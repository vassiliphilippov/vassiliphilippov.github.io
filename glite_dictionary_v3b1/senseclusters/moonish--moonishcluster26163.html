moonish

adjective
changeable in mood
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈmuːnɪʃ / (BrE)
changeable in mood or temperament, like the phases of the moon

Examples

Moonish moods
Her moonish moods made it hard for her friends to know what to expect.
The artist was known for his moonish temperament, sometimes cheerful and sometimes withdrawn.
Because of his moonish nature, his decisions often surprised everyone around him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fickle - likely to change frequently, especially in mood or opinion
  • antonym: steady - not changing or fluctuating; stable
