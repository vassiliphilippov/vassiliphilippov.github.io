yard marker

noun
marker indicating football yard line
4.9
Pronunciation: / jɑrd ˈmɑrkər / (AmE), / jɑːd ˈmɑːkə / (BrE)
a marker placed on or alongside a football field to indicate the position of a specific yard line

Examples

Football yard markers
Broadcast cameras often zoom in on the yard markers to show the team's field position.
Fans in the stands could clearly see that the ball was past the yard marker.
The referee placed the yard marker on the 20-yard line before the next play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yard line - a line marked on a football field showing number of yards from either end
