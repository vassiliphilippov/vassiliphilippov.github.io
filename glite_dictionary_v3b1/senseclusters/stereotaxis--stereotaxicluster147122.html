stereotaxis

noun
precise medical navigation
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛri.oʊˈtæksɪs / (AmE), / ˌstɪəri.əʊˈtæksɪs / (BrE)
a medical technique or process for precisely locating and guiding instruments or beams to specific positions in the body, especially the brain, often using spatial cues

  • synonym: stereotaxy - a method of locating points within the body using a three-dimensional coordinate system
  • synonym: taxis - movement of an organism in response to a stimulus
