garbage carter

noun
person who collects and disposes of garbage
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ ˈkɑrtər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ ˈkɑːtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to collect and dispose of garbage or refuse

Examples

A garbage carter collects trash
In the past, a garbage carter would use a horse-drawn cart for the day's collection.
Residents thanked the garbage carter for keeping the neighborhood clean.
The garbage carter arrives early every Monday to collect the rubbish from our street.
