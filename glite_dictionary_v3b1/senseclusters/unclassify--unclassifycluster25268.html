unclassify

verb
to remove from category
7.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈklæsɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ʌnˈklɑːsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to remove something from a particular category or classification, making it no longer part of that group

Examples

To unclassify documents
The government decided to unclassify several old documents for public access.
After the review, the committee voted to unclassify the report and share it with the media.
The museum chose to unclassify the artifact, allowing it to be displayed in a general collection.
