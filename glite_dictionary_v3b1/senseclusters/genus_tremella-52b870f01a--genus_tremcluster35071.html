genus Tremella

noun
genus of gelatinous fungi
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs trəˈmɛl.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs trɪˈmiː.lə / (BrE)
A genus of fungi characterized by yellowish, gelatinous fruiting bodies with convolutions resembling those of the brain.

Many members of the genus Tremella have a distinctive, brain-like appearance.
