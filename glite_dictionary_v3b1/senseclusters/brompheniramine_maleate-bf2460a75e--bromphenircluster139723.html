brompheniramine maleate

noun
antihistamine drug for allergies
Pronunciation: / broʊmˌfɛnɪˈræmɪn məˈleɪət / (AmE), / brəʊmˌfɛnɪˈræmɪn məˈleɪət / (BrE)
an antihistamine drug used to relieve symptoms of allergic reactions such as hay fever and rhinitis
