place

noun
situation or condition someone is in
Pronunciation: / pleɪs / (AmE), / pleɪs / (BrE)
a situation, condition, or role that someone has, especially in relation to others

Examples

In your place
If I were in your place, I would make the same decision.
It's hard to judge someone until you've been in their place.
She tried to imagine herself in his place before giving any advice.
He always knows his place and behaves respectfully toward his elders.
In Victorian society, people were expected to stay within their place.
She felt that it was not her place to comment on the decision.
