rebuy

noun
tournament re-entry fee
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌbaɪ / (AmE), / ˈriːˌbaɪ / (BrE)
an additional purchase of chips or a fee paid to re-enter a gambling tournament after losing or being eliminated

Examples

A rebuy in poker
After losing all his chips, he made a rebuy to continue playing in the tournament.
The poker event allows one rebuy per player during the first hour.
She decided not to use her rebuy option and left the game early.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buy-in - the initial amount paid to enter a gambling tournament
