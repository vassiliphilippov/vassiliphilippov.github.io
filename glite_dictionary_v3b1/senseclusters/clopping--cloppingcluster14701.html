clopping

noun
sound of horse's hooves on hard surface
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
the rhythmic sound made by a horse's hooves striking a hard surface

Examples

The clopping of hooves
She could hear the distant clopping as the carriage approached.
The clopping of hooves echoed down the cobblestone street.
The stable was filled with the steady clopping of the horses being led outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clip-clop - the sound made by a horse's hooves on a hard surface
