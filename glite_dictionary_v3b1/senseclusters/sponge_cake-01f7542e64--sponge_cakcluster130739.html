sponge cake

noun
light, airy cake made with eggs, flour, and sugar
4.0
Pronunciation: / spʌndʒ keɪk / (AmE), / spʌndʒ keɪk / (BrE)
a light, airy cake made with eggs, flour, and sugar, usually without added fat like butter or oil

Examples

A sponge cake dessert

She baked a delicious sponge cake for the family gathering.
The recipe calls for three eggs to achieve a fluffy sponge cake.
At the bakery, I bought a strawberry-filled sponge cake for dessert.
She baked a sponge cake for his birthday celebration.
The recipe calls for three eggs in the sponge cake mixture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genoise - an Italian sponge cake that uses melted butter
  • degree higher: pound cake - a heavier cake made with equal weights of flour, eggs, butter, and sugar
