nonjury

adjective
without a jury
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈdʒʊri / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈdʒʊəri / (BrE)
decided by a judge without the involvement of a jury

Examples

Nonjury trial
The case was heard in a nonjury trial due to its complexity.
Some civil disputes are resolved through nonjury proceedings.
The judge delivered the verdict in the nonjury hearing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bench trial - a trial decided by a judge without a jury
  • antonym: jury - a group of people who decide the outcome of a trial
