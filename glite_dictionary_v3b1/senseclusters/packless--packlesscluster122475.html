packless

adjective
without a group
Pronunciation: / ˈpækləs / (AmE), / ˈpækləs / (BrE)
not belonging to or accompanied by a pack or group; solitary, especially referring to animals or people

Examples

Packless wolf
After the alpha's death, many wolves were left packless and wandered alone across the valley.
The packless dog avoided other animals and preferred to roam by itself.
He felt packless in the new city, missing the friends he had left behind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lone - being alone; without others
  • antonym: social - enjoying the company of others; living in groups
  • synonym: solitary - existing or living alone; without companions
