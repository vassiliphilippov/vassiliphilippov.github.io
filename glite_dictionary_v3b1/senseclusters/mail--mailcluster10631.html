mail

noun
electronic mail (email messages)
1.0
Pronunciation: / meəl / (AmE), / meəl / (BrE)
messages sent or received electronically over the internet (electronic mail or email).

Examples

Check your mail online
I checked my mail and found several new messages from colleagues.
She deleted the spam from her mail before reading the important notifications.
After the meeting he forwarded the report to everyone's mail and saved a copy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: email - electronic messages sent and received over computer networks; often used interchangeably with 'mail' in digital contexts.
