common limpet

noun
a European marine mollusk with a conical shell
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ˈlɪmpɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈlɪmpɪt / (BrE)
a small, conical marine mollusk (Patella vulgata) with a shell that clings tightly to rocks along European coasts
