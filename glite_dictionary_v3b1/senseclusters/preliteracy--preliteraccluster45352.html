preliteracy

noun
stage before reading
Pronunciation: / priːˈlɪtərəsi / (AmE), / priːˈlɪtərəsi / (BrE)
the stage in a child's development before they have learned to read and write

Examples

Preliteracy stage
Many educational programs focus on preliteracy skills in preschool children.
Parents can encourage preliteracy by reading stories aloud at home.
Developing preliteracy abilities is important for later academic success.

Related Terms

  • antonym: literacy - the ability to read and write
