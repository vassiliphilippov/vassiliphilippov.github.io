fly

noun
opening in trousers closed by zipper or buttons
4.5
Pronunciation: / flaɪ / (AmE), / flaɪ / (BrE)
an opening in the front of a garment, typically trousers, that is closed with a zipper or buttons concealed under a fold of fabric

Examples

Open fly on pants
He forgot to zip up his fly before leaving the restroom.

The button on my fly came loose, so I had to fix it.
