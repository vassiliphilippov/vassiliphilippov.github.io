Pachyrhizus tuberosus

noun
tropical edible-rooted climber
Pronunciation: / ˌpækɪˈraɪzəs tuːˈbɛrəsəs / (AmE), / ˌpakɪˈrʌɪzəs tjuːˈbɪərəsəs / (BrE)
A climbing plant native to the Amazon basin, known for its large, edible tuberous roots.
