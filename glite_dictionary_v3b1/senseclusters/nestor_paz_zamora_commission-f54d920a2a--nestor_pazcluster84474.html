Nestor Paz Zamora Commission

proper noun
Bolivian militant Marxist-Leninist organization
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛstɔr ˈpɑs zəˈmɔrə kəˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈnɛstɔː pæz zəˈmɔːrə kəˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
a left-wing militant organization in Bolivia that serves as an umbrella for various small indigenous subversive groups, reviving Marxist-Leninist ideologies originally established by Che Guevara in the 1960s.
