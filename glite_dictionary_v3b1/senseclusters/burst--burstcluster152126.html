burst

verb
release suddenly and forcefully
Pronunciation: / bɝst / (AmE), / bɜːst / (BrE)
to suddenly force out or release something held inside, often with force or emotion

Examples

To burst out laughing
After the long meeting, he finally burst out in frustration.
She burst into laughter when she heard the joke.
Tears burst from his eyes as the memories overwhelmed him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: erupt - to burst forth suddenly and violently
  • synonym: explode - to burst apart with force and noise
