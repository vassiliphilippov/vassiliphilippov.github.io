skin elasticity

noun
ability of skin to stretch and recover
Pronunciation: / skɪn ɪˌlæstɪˈsɪti / (AmE), / skɪn ɪˌlæstɪˈsɪti / (BrE)
the ability of the skin to stretch and return to its original shape

Examples

Skin elasticity level
Collagen supplements claim to improve skin elasticity over time.
Daily moisturizing can help maintain skin elasticity as we age.
Factors such as sun exposure and smoking may reduce skin elasticity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elasticity - the ability of an object or material to resume its normal shape after being stretched or compressed
