logit

noun
statistical function
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊdʒɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɒdʒɪt / (BrE)
a mathematical function that expresses the logarithm of the odds, commonly used in statistics and logistic regression

Examples

Logit function
The logit function is essential in logistic regression analysis.
Researchers often use the logit transformation to interpret odds ratios.
Understanding the logit helps in modeling binary outcomes in statistics.
