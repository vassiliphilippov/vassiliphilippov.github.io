antifeminist

noun
someone opposed to gender equality
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tiˈfɛm.ɪ.nɪst / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈfɛm.ɪ.nɪst / (BrE)
a person who opposes or rejects the idea of social, economic, or political equality between men and women

Examples

Antifeminist views
Debates between feminists and antifeminists often become heated.
Many critics accused the author of being an antifeminist because of her views on women's rights.
The politician's antifeminist comments sparked outrage among progressive groups.

Related Terms

  • antonym: feminist - a person who supports feminism
  • synonym: misogynist - a person who dislikes or is prejudiced against women
