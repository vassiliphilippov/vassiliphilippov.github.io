zodiac

noun
astrological diagram of 12 signs
1.0 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈzoʊ.di.æk/ (AmE), /ˈzəʊ.di.æk/ (BrE)
the set of 12 signs or constellations used in astrology, often shown as a circular diagram or as an imaginary belt in the sky

Examples

The zodiac signs
She studied the zodiac to learn more about her star sign.
The zodiac is divided into twelve equal parts, each named after a constellation.
The astrologer drew my zodiac on a piece of parchment.
Astrologers often consult the zodiac when making predictions about the future.
Many people believe their zodiac sign influences their personality and fate.
The constellation Leo is one of the twelve zodiac signs.

  • synonym: constellation - a group of stars forming a recognizable pattern
  • synonym: horoscope - astrological chart based on the positions of celestial bodies at a particular time
