mousetrap

verb
to catch with trap
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊsˌtræp / (AmE), / ˈmaʊsˌtræp / (BrE)
to catch an animal, especially a mouse, by using a mousetrap.

Examples

To mousetrap a mouse
We mousetrapped the pantry last night and found three dead mice this morning.
The farmer mousetraps his barn every autumn to keep rodents away from the grain.
After noticing droppings, she quickly mousetrapped the kitchen to solve the problem.

Related Terms

  • antonym: release - to set an animal free from confinement
  • synonym: trap - to catch an animal using a device or method
