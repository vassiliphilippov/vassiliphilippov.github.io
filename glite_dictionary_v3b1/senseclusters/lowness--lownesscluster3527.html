lowness

noun
a state of feeling low in spirits
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊnəs / (AmE), / ˈləʊnəs / (BrE)
a state of feeling sad, depressed, or lacking in spirits

Examples

A feeling of lowness
After the breakup, he experienced a period of deep lowness that lasted several weeks.
Her friends noticed her lowness and tried to cheer her up with a surprise visit.
The film captures the lowness people feel during moments of loss and isolation.
