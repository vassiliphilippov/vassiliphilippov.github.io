Luciferian

noun
follower of Luciferianism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌluːsəˈfɪəriən / (AmE), / ˌluːsɪˈfɪəriən / (BrE)
a person who follows or identifies with Luciferianism, a belief system that venerates Lucifer as a symbol of independence, knowledge, or rebellion

Examples

Modern Luciferian
The author described herself as a Luciferian who values personal freedom and critical thinking.
Some people misunderstand what it means to be a Luciferian and confuse it with other belief systems.
He joined a Luciferian group to discuss philosophy and the symbolism of light and knowledge.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Satanist - a person who follows Satanism, a different belief system often associated with the veneration of Satan
