Gentiana lutea

noun
European perennial plant with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / dʒɛnˈtiː.ənə luˈtiː.ə / (AmE), / dʒɛnˈtɪə.nə luːˈtiː.ə / (BrE)
A robust European perennial plant with clusters of yellow flowers, commonly known as the great yellow gentian.

Examples

Yellow Gentiana lutea flowers
Bright yellow clusters of Gentiana lutea can be seen on mountain meadows during summer.
Herbalists value Gentiana lutea for its bitter compounds used in tonic preparations.
The roots of Gentiana lutea are used in traditional medicine for digestive problems.
