Kabuli

noun
person from Kabul
Pronunciation: / kəˈbuːli / (AmE), / kəˈbuːli / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from Kabul, the capital city of Afghanistan

Examples

A Kabuli student
The young Kabuli applied for a scholarship to study engineering abroad.
As a proud Kabuli, she often shared stories about her hometown with her classmates.
Many Kabulis celebrate traditional festivals with music and dance unique to their city.
