cowfish

noun
tropical trunkfish with hornlike spines
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊ.fɪʃ / (BrE)
A type of tropical trunkfish with hornlike spines above its eyes, related to boxfishes.

Examples

A cowfish with horns
A cowfish can be recognized by the distinctive horns above its eyes.
Marine biologists recently discovered a new species of cowfish in the Pacific Ocean.
The brightly colored cowfish swam gracefully among the coral reefs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boxfish - a type of sea fish with a box-shaped body, closely related to cowfish
