voicelessly

adverb
without vocal cord vibration
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔɪsləsli / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪsləsli / (BrE)
in a way that is produced without vibration of the vocal cords, as in certain speech sounds

Examples

Say voicelessly in class
The teacher demonstrated how to pronounce the sound voicelessly for the class.
She whispered voicelessly so that no one else could hear her.
In phonetics, some consonants are articulated voicelessly while others are voiced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: silently - in a way that makes no sound
  • antonym: voiced - produced with vibration of the vocal cords
