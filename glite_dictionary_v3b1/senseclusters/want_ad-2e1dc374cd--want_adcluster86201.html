want ad

noun
classified advertisement for something wanted
8.4
Pronunciation: / wɑnt æd / (AmE), / wɒnt æd / (BrE)
an advertisement in a newspaper or similar publication stating something that is wanted, such as a job, item, or service

Examples

Place a want ad
He placed a want ad to sell his old bicycle.
Many employers still post job openings in the want ads.
She found her new apartment through a want ad in the local paper.
