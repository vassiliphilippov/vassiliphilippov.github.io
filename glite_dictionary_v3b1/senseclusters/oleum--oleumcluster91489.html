oleum

noun
oil (medical term)
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.liː.əm/ /; / /ˈoʊ.lɪ.əm/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.liː.əm/ /; / /ˈəʊ.lɪ.əm/ (BrE)
a term used in medicine and pharmacology to refer to an oil, especially in the names of specific oils.

Examples

Oleum of peppermint
The prescription included oleum menthae piperitae, which is peppermint oil.
In older medical texts, you may find oleum used to describe various plant-based oils.
The pharmacist prepared a mixture containing oleum ricini for the patient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oil - a viscous liquid derived from plants, animals, or minerals
