bonelet

noun
small bone, esp. in ear
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊn.lət / (AmE), / ˈbəʊn.lət / (BrE)
a small bone, especially one located in the middle ear

Examples

A bonelet in ear
Damage to a bonelet can affect hearing significantly.
The ossicles are the three bonelets found in the middle ear.
The surgeon carefully examined the bonelet for signs of infection.
