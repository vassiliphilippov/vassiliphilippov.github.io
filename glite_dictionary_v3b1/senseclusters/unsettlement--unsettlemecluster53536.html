unsettlement

noun
state of unease
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛtəlmənt / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛtəlmənt / (BrE)
a state or feeling of being disturbed, uneasy, or not stable

Examples

A feeling of unsettlement
After the leadership change, there was a period of unsettlement in the company.
The sudden move caused a sense of unsettlement among the children.
Political events often bring unsettlement to the financial markets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - a state of peace and lack of disturbance
  • synonym: instability - the state of being unstable or not firmly fixed
  • synonym: uncertainty - the state of being unsure or not certain
