incantation

noun
ritual magic spell or chant
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.kænˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.kænˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A series of words or sounds spoken or chanted as part of a ritual or magic spell, believed to have special or magical power.

Examples

A incantation chant
During the ceremony, the priestess led the group in a powerful incantation.
Legends say the incantation must be spoken at midnight for the spell to work.
The sorcerer whispered an ancient incantation to unlock the hidden door.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chant - repeated rhythmic phrase, especially as part of ritual
  • antonym: curse - magical words intended to invoke harm
  • synonym: spell - words or formula having magical effect
