quencher

noun
substance that stops reaction
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkwen.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈkwen.tʃə/ (BrE)
a substance that reduces or stops a chemical or physical process, especially by preventing fluorescence or a reaction

Examples

Chemical quencher
The scientist added a quencher to the solution to stop the reaction.
A quencher is often used in experiments to prevent unwanted fluorescence.
Without a quencher, the chemical process would continue and affect the results.

  • synonym: inhibitor - a substance that slows down or prevents a chemical reaction
