embassy

noun
virtual/online diplomatic presence
4.6 0.21/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u025bmb\u0259si / (AmE), / \u02c8\u025bmb\u0259si / (BrE)
a virtual or online diplomatic presence that represents a country on the internet, offering information and consular services to citizens and visitors.

Examples

Virtual embassy website
Many governments now maintain a virtual embassy to provide consular information to citizens abroad.
During the crisis, refugees used the country's online embassy portal to request emergency travel documents.
The foreign office launched a digital embassy where citizens can book consular appointments and find advice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consulate - a government office that provides consular services to citizens abroad, similar in function to an embassy's services in certain contexts.
  • synonym: diplomatic mission - an organization that represents a country's government in a foreign country; includes embassies and consulates.
