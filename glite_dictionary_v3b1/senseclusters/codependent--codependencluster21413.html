codependent

adjective
unhealthy mutual reliance
7.3
Pronunciation: /koʊ.dɪ.ˈpen.dənt/ /; / /koʊ.də.ˈpen.dənt/ (AmE), /kəʊ.dɪ.ˈpen.dənt/ /; / /kəʊ.də.ˈpen.dənt/ (BrE)
describing a person or relationship where someone relies too much on another, often in unhealthy or excessive ways

Examples

Codependent relationship
Their codependent behavior made it difficult for either of them to make decisions alone.
A codependent relationship can prevent personal growth and independence.
Therapists often help clients recognize codependent patterns in their relationships.
He became codependent in his relationship, always putting his partner's needs before his own.
Therapists often help codependent individuals set healthy boundaries in their lives.
Her codependent behavior made it difficult for her to make decisions without her friend's approval.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dependent - relying on someone or something else for support
  • antonym: independent - not relying on others; self-sufficient
