beluga

A small white whale found in Arctic and sub-Arctic waters, known for its distinctive color and lack of a dorsal fin.

Examples

A beluga whale
Many belugas migrate long distances each year in search of food.
The beluga is easily recognized by its white color and rounded forehead.
We saw a beluga swimming near the edge of the ice floe.
