North Asia

proper noun
Russian part of northern Asia
3.9
Pronunciation: / nɔrθ ˈeɪ.ʒə / (AmE), / nɔːθ ˈeɪ.ʃə / (BrE)
the northern part of Asia, comprising primarily the Asian portion of Russia (Siberia and the Far East).

Examples

North Asia region
Many people think of North Asia as a region dominated by forests and permafrost.
The Trans-Siberian Railway crosses much of North Asia on its journey from west to east.
Wildlife in North Asia includes Siberian tigers, reindeer, and brown bears.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Siberia - a vast region in Russia, extending eastwards from the Urals to the Pacific
