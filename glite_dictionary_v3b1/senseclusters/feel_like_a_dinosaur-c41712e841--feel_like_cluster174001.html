feel like a dinosaur

verb
to feel old-fashioned or outdated
Pronunciation: / fil laɪk ə ˈdaɪnəˌsɔr / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈdaɪnəsɔː / (BrE)
to feel old-fashioned or out of date, especially in comparison to newer trends or technologies
