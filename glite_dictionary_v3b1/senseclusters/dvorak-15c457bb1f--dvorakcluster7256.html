Dvorak

proper noun
keyboard layout or surname
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdvɔːræk / (AmE), / ˈdvɔːræk / (BrE)
a keyboard layout designed for efficient typing, or the surname of a famous Czech composer

Examples

Dvorak keyboard
She switched her computer to the Dvorak layout to type faster.
Many people find the Dvorak keyboard easier to use after some practice.
Antonín Dvorak composed some of the most famous classical music of the 19th century.
