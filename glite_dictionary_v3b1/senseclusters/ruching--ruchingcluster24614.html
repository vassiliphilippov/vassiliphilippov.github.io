ruching

noun
gathered fabric decoration
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈruːʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈruːʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a sewing technique or decorative effect where fabric is gathered or pleated into ruffles or folds
