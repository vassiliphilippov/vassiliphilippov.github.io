mitotic

adjective
relating to mitosis
5.5
Pronunciation: / maɪˈtɑtɪk / (AmE), / mɪˈtɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving mitosis, the process of cell division.

Examples

The mitotic phase
During the mitotic phase, chromosomes align in the center of the cell.
Errors in mitotic division can lead to genetic disorders.
Researchers observed a mitotic process in the dividing cells under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mitosis - the process of cell division in which the nucleus divides
