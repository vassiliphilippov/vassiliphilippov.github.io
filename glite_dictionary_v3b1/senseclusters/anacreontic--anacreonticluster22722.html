anacreontic

noun
lyric poem of pleasure
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈkriːɒntɪk / (AmE), / ˌænəˈkriːɒntɪk / (BrE)
a short lyric poem in the style of the ancient Greek poet Anacreon, usually celebrating love, wine, or pleasure

Examples

An anacreontic poem
The poet composed an anacreontic to celebrate the joys of spring and friendship.
Her latest collection features an anacreontic that praises the pleasures of good company and fine wine.
In literature class, we read an anacreontic inspired by the ancient Greek tradition.
