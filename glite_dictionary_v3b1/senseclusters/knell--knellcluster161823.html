knell

noun
slow bell sound for death or funeral
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɛl / (AmE), / nɛl / (BrE)
the sound of a bell rung slowly, especially to announce a death, a funeral, or the end of something

Examples

Funeral knell
Many saw the announcement as the knell for the old company, marking the end of an era.
The church bell's knell rang out at midnight, spreading a sense of foreboding over the town.
The solemn knell echoed through the silent village as the funeral procession began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toll - the sound of a large bell struck slowly to mark a death or event
