Thanksgiving Day

noun
North American harvest and gratitude holiday
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˌθæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ deɪ / (AmE), / ˌθæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ deɪ / (BrE)
In the United States, a national holiday celebrated on the fourth Thursday of November to give thanks and commemorate the harvest, traditionally associated with a historic feast shared by Pilgrims and Wampanoag people in 1621. In Canada, observed on the second Monday in October.

Examples

Thanksgiving Day feast
In Canada, Thanksgiving Day falls in early October and also marks the harvest season.
Many Americans travel home to celebrate Thanksgiving Day with loved ones.
We host a large family dinner every Thanksgiving Day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Independence Day - a U.S. national day celebrating independence
