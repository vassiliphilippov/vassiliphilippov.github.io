reishi

noun
medicinal mushroom
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪʃi / (AmE), / ˈreɪʃi / (BrE)
a type of mushroom used in traditional East Asian medicine, believed to have health benefits

Examples

Reishi mushroom tea
Many people add reishi to tea for its supposed health benefits.
The herbalist recommended reishi as a supplement to support the immune system.
Dried reishi mushrooms are often sold in health food stores and Asian markets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lingzhi - another name for the reishi mushroom, especially in Chinese contexts
  • synonym: shiitake - an edible mushroom commonly used in East Asian cuisine
