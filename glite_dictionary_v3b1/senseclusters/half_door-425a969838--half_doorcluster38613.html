half door

noun
door divided into two horizontal parts
3.9 4.0
Pronunciation: /hæf dɔːr/ (AmE), /hɑːf dɔː/ (BrE)
an exterior door divided horizontally into two sections, allowing each half to be opened or closed independently

Examples

A half door opens
Opening just the top of the half door let in the summer breeze but kept the dog inside.
She leaned over the lower part of the half door to greet her neighbor.
The old farmhouse still has a charming half door that opens onto the garden.
