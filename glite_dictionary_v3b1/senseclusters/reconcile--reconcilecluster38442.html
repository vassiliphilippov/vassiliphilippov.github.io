reconcile

verb
to bring into agreement or harmony
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛk.ən.saɪl / (AmE), / ˈrɛk.ən.saɪl / (BrE)
to make different things, ideas, or situations compatible or bring them into agreement

Examples

To reconcile ideas
It is often difficult to reconcile work commitments with family life.
She struggled to reconcile her beliefs with the harsh reality.
The project aims to reconcile environmental protection with economic development.
It was difficult to reconcile his beliefs with the facts.
She struggled to reconcile her memories of home with the reality she faced.
We must reconcile our budget constraints with the project's requirements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conflict - be incompatible or at odds
  • synonym: harmonize - to bring into agreement or harmony
