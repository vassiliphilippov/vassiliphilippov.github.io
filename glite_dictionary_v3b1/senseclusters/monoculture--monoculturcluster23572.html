monoculture

noun
dominance of a single cultural or technological model

a situation in which a single cultural model, technology, platform, or set of ideas dominates a society, industry, or ecosystem, reducing diversity and variety.

Examples

Cultural monoculture
Critics warned that the platform's global reach had created a cultural monoculture, stifling local voices and innovation.
Security experts worry a single operating system's dominance produces a technological monoculture vulnerable to widespread attacks.
School curricula focused on one set of ideas can lead to an intellectual monoculture that discourages critical thinking.
