herb garden

noun
garden for growing herbs
1.5
Pronunciation: / hɜrb ˈɡɑrdn̩ / (AmE), / hɜːb ˈɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)
A garden where herbs are cultivated for use in cooking, medicine, or decoration.

Examples

A herb garden plot
She spends every morning tending her herb garden to keep the basil and mint healthy.
The fresh thyme from their herb garden made the soup especially fragrant.
They designed a small herb garden in their backyard to grow culinary plants.
