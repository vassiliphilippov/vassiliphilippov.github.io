pitch-blackness

noun
total darkness; absence of light
4.6
Pronunciation: / pɪtʃˈblæknəs / (AmE), / pɪtʃˈblaknəs / (BrE)
The state of being completely dark; total absence of light.

Examples

Complete pitch-blackness
She stumbled through the pitch-blackness after the power outage hit the entire neighborhood.
The narrow hallway was shrouded in pitch-blackness, making it impossible to see a thing.
When we turned off the flashlight, pitch-blackness surrounded us inside the cave.

  • synonym: darkness - the partial or total absence of light
