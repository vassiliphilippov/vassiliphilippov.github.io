tee-off

noun
start of play in golf by hitting from the tee
Pronunciation: / ˈti ˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈtiː ˌɒf / (BrE)
the act of hitting a golf ball from the teeing ground to start play on a hole

Examples

The tee-off shot
His tee-off set the pace for the rest of the game.
She waited patiently for her tee-off on the third hole.
The tournament will begin with the first tee-off at 8 a.m.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drive - a long shot in golf, especially from the tee
