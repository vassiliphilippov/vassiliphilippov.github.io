hidden gem

noun
something excellent yet little-known
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪdən dʒɛm / (AmE), / ˈhɪdən dʒɛm / (BrE)
a valuable or outstanding person, place, or thing that is not widely known or recognized

Examples

A hidden gem place
Reviewers call the tiny museum a hidden gem filled with fascinating artifacts.
That old park is truly a hidden gem in the city, overlooked by most tourists.
We stumbled across a hidden gem of a bakery tucked away in the alley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jewel - a very valuable object, often used metaphorically
