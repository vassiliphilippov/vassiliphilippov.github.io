weight

verb
to influence or bias towards one side
Pronunciation: / weɪt / (AmE), / weɪt / (BrE)
to influence or present something in a way that gives preference to one side over others; to bias

Examples

To weight results
Some reports are deliberately weighted to favor certain interests.
The committee tried not to weight its findings toward any particular group.
When analyzing data, ensure you do not weight the results with your own opinions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bias - to show prejudice for or against something
