bottom-dwelling

adjective
living at the bottom of a body of water
8.8 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑː.təm ˈdwel.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbɒt.əm ˈdwel.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
(of a fish or marine animal) living on or near the bottom of a body of water.

Examples

The bottom-dwelling crabs burrow into the sand to hide from predators.
