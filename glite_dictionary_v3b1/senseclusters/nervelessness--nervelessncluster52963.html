nervelessness

noun
fearless composure, lack of nervousness
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnɝːv.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈnɜːv.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
calm and confident self-possession, especially in dangerous or stressful situations; lack of nervousness or fear

Examples

Her nervelessness in a crisis impressed everyone in the team.
The pilot's nervelessness saved many lives during the emergency landing.
