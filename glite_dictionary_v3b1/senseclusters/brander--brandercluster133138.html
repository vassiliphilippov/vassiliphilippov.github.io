brander

noun
person or tool for marking
Pronunciation: / ˈbrændər / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːndə / (BrE)
a person or tool that marks something, especially livestock, with a hot iron to show ownership

Examples

A brander with iron
The brander carefully pressed the hot iron onto the calf's side.
Every ranch employed a skilled brander during the annual roundup.
The old brander was displayed in the museum as a piece of ranching history.
