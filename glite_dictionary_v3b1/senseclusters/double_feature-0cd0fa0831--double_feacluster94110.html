double feature

noun
two games or films shown together
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbl ˈfiːtʃər / (AmE), / ˈdʌbl ˈfiːtʃə / (BrE)
An occasion when two games or two films are presented in succession, especially when the same two teams play two games on the same day.

Examples

A double feature movie
Back in the 1950s, it was common to see a double feature at a drive-in.
The baseball league announced a double feature for Saturday due to the rainout.
They attended a double feature at the movie theater last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doubleheader - two sports games, usually baseball, played by the same teams on the same day
