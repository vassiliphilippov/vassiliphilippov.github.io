superfecta

noun
wager to predict first four finishers
8.4
Pronunciation: / suː.pɚˈfɛk.tə / (AmE), / suː.pəˈfɛk.tə / (BrE)
a bet in which the bettor must correctly predict the first four finishers in a race in the exact order

Examples

A superfecta bet
After placing a superfecta bet, she watched the last race with growing excitement.
He won a huge payout by correctly selecting the superfecta at Churchill Downs.
The superfecta is considered one of the most difficult bets in horse racing.
