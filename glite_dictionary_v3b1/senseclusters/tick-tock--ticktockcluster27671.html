tick-tock

noun
sound made by a clock
1.5
Pronunciation: / tɪk tɑk / (AmE), / tɪk tɒk / (BrE)
the regular sound produced by a clock, often symbolizing the passage of time

Examples

Clock tick-tock sound
She found the steady tick-tock soothing as she tried to fall asleep.
The tick-tock of the grandfather clock filled the silent room.
With every tick-tock, he felt the deadline approaching.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tick - a short, light, clicking sound, like that made by a clock
