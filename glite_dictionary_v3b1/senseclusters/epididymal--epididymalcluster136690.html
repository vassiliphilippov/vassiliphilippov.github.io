epididymal

adjective
of the epididymis
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈdɪdəməl / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈdɪdɪməl / (BrE)
relating to or associated with the epididymis, a tube in the male reproductive system where sperm matures and is stored

Examples

Epididymal cyst
The ultrasound revealed an epididymal cyst that required further evaluation.
He was diagnosed with epididymal inflammation after experiencing pain in his groin area.
Doctors often check for epididymal abnormalities during a routine physical examination.
