teary-eyed

adjective
with eyes full of tears
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪriˌaɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɪəriˌaɪd / (BrE)
Having eyes filled with tears, usually because of emotion or irritation.

Examples

A teary-eyed look
After watching the emotional film, he became teary-eyed and tried to hide it from his friends.
She gave her friend a teary-eyed smile as they said goodbye at the airport.
The bride's father looked teary-eyed as he walked her down the aisle.
