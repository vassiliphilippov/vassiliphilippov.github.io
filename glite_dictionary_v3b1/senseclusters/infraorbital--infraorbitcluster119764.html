infraorbital

noun
facial sensory nerve
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfrəˈɔrbɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnfrəˈɔːbɪtəl / (BrE)
the infraorbital nerve, a branch of the maxillary nerve that provides sensation to the cheek, lower eyelid, upper lip, and side of the nose

Examples

Infraorbital nerve
The dentist carefully injected anesthetic near the infraorbital to numb the patient's upper lip.
Damage to the infraorbital can cause numbness in the cheek and upper lip area.
Surgeons must identify the infraorbital during facial procedures to avoid nerve injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facial nerve - a nerve that controls muscles of facial expression and provides some sensation
  • synonym: maxillary nerve - a major nerve in the face that the infraorbital nerve branches from
