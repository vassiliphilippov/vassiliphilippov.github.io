bivalent

adjective
chromosomes paired in meiosis
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪ.və.lənt / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ.və.lənt / (BrE)
(of homologous chromosomes) paired together during synapsis in meiosis.

Examples

Bivalent chromosomes pair
During meiosis, each bivalent consists of two homologous chromosomes joined together.
Improper pairing of chromosomes can result in an abnormal bivalent configuration.
The scientist observed the formation of a bivalent under the microscope.
