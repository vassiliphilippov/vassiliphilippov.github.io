intraspecific

adjective
within a single species
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.trə.spəˈsɪf.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trə.spəˈsɪf.ɪk / (BrE)
Occurring or arising within a single species; involving members of the same species.

Examples

Intraspecific competition
Aggression in birds often results from intraspecific disputes over nesting sites.
An intraspecific variation in color was observed across different populations of butterflies.
The scientists studied intraspecific competition among wolves to understand how they share territory.

Related Terms

  • antonym: interspecific - arising or occurring between different species
