painted

adjective
having makeup applied
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪntɪd / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd / (BrE)
(of a person) having makeup applied, especially conspicuously or excessively.

Examples

She arrived at the party with a painted face, her eyes outlined in sparkling blue.
The actress was described as a painted lady, her makeup thick and colorful.
