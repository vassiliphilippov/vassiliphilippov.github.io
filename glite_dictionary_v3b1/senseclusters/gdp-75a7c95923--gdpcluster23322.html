GDP

noun
total value of goods/services produced in a country
3.9 20/m
Pronunciation: / d íi di pi / (AmE), / d íiÀê diÀê piÀê / (BrE)
Gross Domestic Product: the total market value of all goods and services produced within a country's borders in a specific period, usually a year.

Examples

The GDP growth
A sharp decline in GDP can signal a looming recession.
Economists use GDP as an indicator of national economic performance.
The country's GDP grew by 3% last year, reflecting a strong economy.
