light cream

noun
cream with at least 18% butterfat
Pronunciation: / laɪt krim / (AmE), / laɪt kriːm / (BrE)
A type of cream containing at least 18% butterfat, used in cooking and as a topping.

Examples

Light cream in coffee
Many dessert recipes call for light cream to add richness without being too heavy.
She poured a little light cream into her coffee for a smoother taste.
The café offers both light cream and whole milk for your tea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: single cream - British term for cream with similar fat content
