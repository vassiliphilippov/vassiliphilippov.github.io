leatherback turtle

noun
large marine turtle with leathery shell
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛðɚˌbæk ˈtɝt̬əl / (AmE), / ˈlɛðəbæk ˈtɜːtəl / (BrE)
a large marine turtle with a leathery, flexible shell, known for being the largest living turtle

Examples

The leatherback turtle
A single leatherback turtle can weigh over 900 kilograms, making it the largest turtle species.
Scientists are working to protect the leatherback turtle from threats like plastic pollution.
The leatherback turtle migrates thousands of miles across oceans each year.
