UTC

noun
international time standard (Coordinated Universal Time)
4.0 2.53/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåjuÀê tiÀê ÀàsiÀê / (AmE), / ÀåjuÀê tiÀê ÀàsiÀê / (BrE)
the time standard commonly used across the world, based on the mean solar time at the Prime Meridian, adjusted with leap seconds; Coordinated Universal Time

Examples

Coordinated UTC time
All time differences in the report are expressed in UTC for consistency.
Satellites synchronize their onboard clocks with UTC to ensure accurate positioning.
The online meeting is scheduled to start at 14:00 UTC tomorrow.

Synonyms

    4.9 0.00/m
