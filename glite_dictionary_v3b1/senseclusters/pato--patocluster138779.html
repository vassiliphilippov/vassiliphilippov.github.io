pato

noun
Argentine horseback sport
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpætəʊ / (BrE)
a traditional Argentine team sport played on horseback in which players try to score by throwing a ball with handles into a goal

Examples

Argentine pato game
During his visit to Buenos Aires, he attended a thrilling pato match at a local ranch.
Learning the rules of pato helped her appreciate Argentina's unique sporting traditions.
The players raced across the field on horseback, competing fiercely in the game of pato.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polo - a team sport played on horseback with a small ball and mallets
