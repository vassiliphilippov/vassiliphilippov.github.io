tiki

noun
Polynesian-inspired style
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːki / (AmE), / ˈtiːki / (BrE)
a style and cultural trend from the mid-20th century inspired by Polynesian motifs, especially in bars, restaurants, and decor

Examples

Tiki culture trend
The bar was decorated in a tiki style, with bamboo furniture and carved masks.
He collects vintage tiki mugs from famous mid-century restaurants.
Many people enjoy hosting tiki parties with tropical drinks and themed decorations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Polynesian - relating to the islands and cultures of Polynesia
  • synonym: retro - imitative of a style from the recent past
