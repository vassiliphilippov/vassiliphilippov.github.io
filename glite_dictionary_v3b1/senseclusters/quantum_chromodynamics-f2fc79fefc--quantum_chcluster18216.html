quantum chromodynamics

noun
theory of strong interactions in particle physics
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkwɑːn.təm kroʊ.moʊ.daɪ.ˈnæm.ɪks/ /; / /ˈkwæn.təm kroʊ.moʊ.daɪ.ˈnæm.ɪks/ (AmE), /ˈkwɒn.təm krəʊ.məʊ.daɪ.ˈnæm.ɪks/ (BrE)
the theory in particle physics that describes the strong interaction binding quarks together within protons, neutrons, and other hadrons, mediated by gluons
