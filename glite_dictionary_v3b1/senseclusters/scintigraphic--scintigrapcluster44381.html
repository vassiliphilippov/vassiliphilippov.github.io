scintigraphic

adjective
from scintigraphy
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪntɪˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɪntɪˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or obtained by scintigraphy, a medical imaging technique using radioactive tracers

Examples

A scintigraphic scan
The scintigraphic scan revealed abnormal activity in the patient's thyroid gland.
Doctors rely on scintigraphic techniques to detect certain types of cancer early.
A scintigraphic image can help assess blood flow to the heart muscle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nuclear - relating to the use of radioactive substances in medicine
  • synonym: radiographic - relating to images produced by X-rays
