unmanfully

adverb
in a way lacking traditional manly qualities
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmænfəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈman.fəli / (BrE)
In a way that lacks qualities traditionally regarded as befitting a man, such as strength, courage, or determination.

Examples

To act unmanfully
He retreated unmanfully in the face of danger, abandoning his friends.
She criticized him for speaking unmanfully instead of with confidence.
The knight, accused of acting unmanfully, hung his head in shame.
