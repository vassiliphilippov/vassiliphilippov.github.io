lottery

noun
event decided by chance
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːt̬əri / (AmE), / ˈlɒtəri / (BrE)
an event, process, or situation whose outcome is determined by chance rather than by planning or control

Examples

Lottery for places
Admission to the program is a lottery, with places assigned randomly.
Life can sometimes seem like a lottery, where luck plays a huge part.
Many people feel that getting a good job these days is a lottery.
