guanabana

noun
tropical fruit with spiny skin and tart pulp
7.8 9.0
Pronunciation: /gwə.ˈnæ.bə.nə/ /; / /gwɑː.ˈnæ.bə.nə/ (AmE), /gwə.ˈnɑː.bə.nə/ /; / /gwɑː.ˈnɑː.bə.nə/ (BrE)
