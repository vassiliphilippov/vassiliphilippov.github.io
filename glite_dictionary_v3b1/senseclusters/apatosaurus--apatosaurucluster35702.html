apatosaurus

noun
large herbivorous Jurassic dinosaur
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˌpæt̬əˈsɔr.əs / (AmE), / əˌpæt.əˈsɔː.rəs / (BrE)
A large, four-legged herbivorous dinosaur that lived in North America during the late Jurassic period.

Examples

A apatosaurus skeleton
A museum exhibit displays a full skeleton of an apatosaurus for visitors to admire.
Experts believe the neck of the apatosaurus helped it reach food high in the trees.
The apatosaurus roamed the plains of North America millions of years ago.
