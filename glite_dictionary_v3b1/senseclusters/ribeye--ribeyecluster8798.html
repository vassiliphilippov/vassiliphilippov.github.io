ribeye

noun
beef cut from rib
2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪb.aɪ/ (AmE), /ˈrɪb.aɪ/ (BrE)

Examples

The chef recommended the ribeye because of its marbling and rich flavor.
She cooked a juicy ribeye on the barbecue for her family.
