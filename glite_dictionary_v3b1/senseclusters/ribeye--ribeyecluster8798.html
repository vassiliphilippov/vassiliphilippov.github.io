ribeye

noun
beef cut from rib
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪbˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɪbˌaɪ / (BrE)
a tender, flavorful cut of beef taken from the rib section of a cow

Examples

Grilled ribeye steak
He ordered a grilled ribeye for dinner at the steakhouse.
The chef recommended the ribeye because of its marbling and rich flavor.
She cooked a juicy ribeye on the barbecue for her family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sirloin - a cut of beef from the back of the animal, behind the ribs
  • synonym: tenderloin - a very tender cut of beef from the loin
