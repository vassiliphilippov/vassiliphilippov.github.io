sectional

adjective
made of sections; divided into parts
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃənəl / (BrE)
consisting of or divided into sections; made up of distinct parts that can be arranged or used separately or together

Examples

Sectional sofa pieces
The architect drew a sectional view of the building to show the interior structure.
The museum displayed a sectional model of the ship, allowing visitors to see the inside.
We decided to buy a sectional sofa for the living room because it can be rearranged easily.
