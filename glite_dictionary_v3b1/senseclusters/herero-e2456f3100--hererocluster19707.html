Herero

proper noun
Namibian ethnic group
9.9
Pronunciation: / həˈrɛroʊ / (AmE), / həˈrɛərəʊ / (BrE)
a member of a Bantu ethnic group from Namibia, or the language spoken by this group

Examples

The Herero people
The Herero have preserved many of their traditional customs despite historical challenges.
She is studying the Herero language as part of her research on African cultures.
Many Herero people live in central Namibia and celebrate their heritage with colorful festivals.
