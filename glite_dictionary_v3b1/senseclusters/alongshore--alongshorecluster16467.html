alongshore

adjective
parallel to shore
5.5
Pronunciation: / əˈlɔŋʃɔr / (AmE), / əˈlɒŋʃɔː / (BrE)
lying, moving, or extending parallel to the shore or coastline

Examples

Alongshore winds
The alongshore winds made it easier for the boats to travel parallel to the beach.
Scientists measured the alongshore movement of sand caused by the ocean currents.
A series of alongshore structures were built to protect the coastline from erosion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coastal - located on or near a coast
  • antonym: offshore - situated at sea some distance from the shore
