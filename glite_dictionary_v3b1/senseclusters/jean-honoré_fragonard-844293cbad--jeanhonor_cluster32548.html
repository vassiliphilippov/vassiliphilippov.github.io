Jean-Honoré Fragonard

proper noun
French Rococo painter (1732–1806)
Pronunciation: / ʒɑn ɒnɔːˈreɪ fræɡəˌnɑːrd / (AmE), / ʒɑ̃ ɔnɔːˈʁe fʁaɡɔnaʁ / (BrE)
(1732–1806) A French painter known for his exuberant and sensuous Rococo style, particularly depicting scenes of aristocratic life and love.
