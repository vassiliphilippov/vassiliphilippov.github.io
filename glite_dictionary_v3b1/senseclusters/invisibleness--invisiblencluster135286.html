invisibleness

noun
the quality of being invisible
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɪzəbəlnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɪzəbəlnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being impossible to see or perceive by the eye

Examples

The invisibleness of objects
He marveled at the invisibleness of the magician's trick.
Some animals rely on their invisibleness to hide from predators.
The invisibleness of the object made it difficult for scientists to detect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invisibility - the quality of not being visible
  • antonym: visibility - the quality of being able to be seen
