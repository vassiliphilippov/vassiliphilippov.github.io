livestock

noun
farm animals raised for profit
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪvˌstɑk / (AmE), / ˈlʌɪvstɒk / (BrE)
animals, such as cattle, sheep, and pigs, kept on a farm for use or profit

Examples

Livestock animals
Disease outbreaks among livestock can have a significant impact on food supply.
Modern technology has improved the way livestock are managed and cared for.
The farmer sold some of his livestock at the market to make extra money.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cattle - large ruminant animals, typically domesticated
  • synonym: poultry - domesticated birds kept for eggs or meat
