yarmelke

noun
skullcap worn by Jewish men
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑrməkə / (AmE), / ˈjɑːməkə / (BrE)
a small round skullcap worn by Jewish men, especially during prayer or religious observance

Examples

A yarmelke cap
He wore a yarmelke throughout the ceremony.
Many boys received their first yarmelke at their bar mitzvah.
Visitors were offered a yarmelke before entering the synagogue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kippah - a skullcap worn by Jewish men or boys, especially during prayer
