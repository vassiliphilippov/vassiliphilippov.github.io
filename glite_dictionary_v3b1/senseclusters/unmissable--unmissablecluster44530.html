unmissable

adjective
must-see or do
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɪsəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɪsəbəl / (BrE)
so good, important, or noticeable that you should not or cannot miss it

Examples

An unmissable event
The new movie is an unmissable experience for anyone who loves action films.
This concert is unmissable and tickets are selling out quickly.
The chef's signature dish is truly unmissable for food enthusiasts.
The museum's new exhibition is truly unmissable and has attracted visitors from all over the city.
Her performance on stage was so powerful that it was simply unmissable.
The bright red sign made the restaurant unmissable from the street.
