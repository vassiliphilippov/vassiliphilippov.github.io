skinsuit

noun
realistic body costume
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪnˌsuːt / (AmE), / ˈskɪnˌsuːt / (BrE)
a realistic full-body costume, often made of latex or similar materials, used to change a person's appearance in film, theatre, or performance

Examples

Movie skinsuit
The actor wore a detailed skinsuit to portray the alien creature in the science fiction movie.
For the stage play, the designer created a custom skinsuit to help the performer look like a statue.
The special-effects team spent hours fitting the realistic skinsuit before filming the transformation scene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bodysuit - a close-fitting one-piece garment covering the torso and sometimes limbs
  • synonym: costume - clothing worn to represent a character or type of person
