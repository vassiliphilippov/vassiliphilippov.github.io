preseptal

adjective
in front of a septum
Pronunciation: / priːˈsɛptəl / (AmE), / priːˈsɛptəl / (BrE)
located or occurring in front of a septum, especially in medical contexts

Examples

Preseptal cellulitis
The patient was diagnosed with preseptal cellulitis, which required prompt treatment.
Swelling in the preseptal area of the eyelid can indicate an infection.
Doctors must distinguish between preseptal and postseptal conditions for proper management.

Related Terms
