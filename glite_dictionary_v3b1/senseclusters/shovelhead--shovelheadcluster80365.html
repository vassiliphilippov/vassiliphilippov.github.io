shovelhead

noun
small hammerhead shark with spade-shaped head
8.7 7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈʃʌv.əl.hed/ (AmE), /ˈʃʌv.əl.hed/ (BrE)
A small species of hammerhead shark with a spade-shaped head, found abundantly in bays and estuaries.
