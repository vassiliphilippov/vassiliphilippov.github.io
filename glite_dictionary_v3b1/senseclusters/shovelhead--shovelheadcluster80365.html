shovelhead

noun
small hammerhead shark with spade-shaped head
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌvəlˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈʃʌv.əl.hɛd / (BrE)
A small species of hammerhead shark with a spade-shaped head, found abundantly in bays and estuaries.

Examples

The shovelhead shark
Marine biologists study the habits of the shovelhead to better understand estuarine ecosystems.
Several shovelhead sharks were spotted swimming close to the shore during low tide.
The wide, flattened head of a shovelhead helps it forage for food in shallow waters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hammerhead - a type of shark with a distinctive hammer-shaped head
