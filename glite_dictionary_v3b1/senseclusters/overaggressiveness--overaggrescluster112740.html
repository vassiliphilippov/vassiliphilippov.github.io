overaggressiveness

noun
excessive aggression
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərəˈɡrɛsɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərəˈɡrɛsɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being excessively forceful, hostile, or combative in behavior or attitude

Examples

The overaggressiveness of manager
The manager's overaggressiveness during meetings made some employees uncomfortable.
His overaggressiveness on the basketball court often led to unnecessary fouls.
Parents worried that overaggressiveness in video games might influence their children's behavior.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: aggressiveness - the quality of being forceful or ready to attack
  • antonym: calmness - the quality of being peaceful and not aggressive
  • synonym: hostility - unfriendly or antagonistic behavior
