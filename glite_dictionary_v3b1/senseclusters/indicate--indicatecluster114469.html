indicate

verb
to point out or show something
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.dɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.dɪ.keɪt / (BrE)
to point out or show a place, direction, person, or thing, either physically or in a figurative sense

Examples

To indicate the way
He indicated the correct route on the map for us.
She indicated her friend by nodding in his direction.
The professor indicated which chapters we needed to read for the exam.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conceal - to hide or keep something secret
  • synonym: point - to indicate a place, direction, or thing by extending a finger or object
  • synonym: show - to cause or allow something to be seen; display
