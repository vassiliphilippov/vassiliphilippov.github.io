Examples

To indicate the way

Synonyms

  • point out
    1.8 94/m
    point out draws attention to something clearly and carefully, while indicate can be more general and simply show or hint.
  • point
    1.0 170/m
    point uses a physical gesture to show a place or thing quickly, while indicate works more broadly and in formal contexts.
  • show
    1.0 167/m
    show makes something visible or obvious to others, while indicate often points or hints at it without revealing everything.
  • designate
    4.6 4.66/m
    designate names or appoints something officially, whereas indicate simply points to or shows a place, direction, person, or thing.
