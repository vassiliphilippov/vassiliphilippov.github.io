Synonyms

  • unselfish
    2.0 0.91/m
    unselfish is willing to put others' needs before your own, while selfless focuses on caring more for others than oneself.
  • altruistic
    6.7 2.16/m
    altruistic shows general concern for others' welfare, often as a moral choice, while selfless simply means putting others first.
  • self-giving
    7.3 0.32/m
    self-giving is ready to give time or care to others in generous ways, while selfless is broader concern for others' needs.
  • self-sacrificing
    5.5 0.30/m
    self-sacrificing is ready to give up personal comfort or safety for others, while selfless may simply mean kind concern.
