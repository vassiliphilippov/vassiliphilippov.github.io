selfless

adjective
unselfishly concerned for others
1.5 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsel.fləs/ (AmE), /ˈsel.fləs/ (BrE)
concerned more with the needs and wishes of others than with one's own; unselfish

Examples

Selfless help
Despite her busy schedule, she gave selfless support to her friends in times of need.
His selfless actions during the crisis earned him the respect of everyone.
The volunteers worked selflessly to help the community after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: altruistic - showing a disinterested and selfless concern for others
  • synonym: generous - willing to give more of something than is strictly necessary
  • antonym: selfish - concerned chiefly with one's own advantage
