epicentral

adjective
at the focal point
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈsɛntrəl / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈsɛntrəl / (BrE)
relating to or located at the central or most important point of an event or phenomenon, often used figuratively.

Examples

Epicentral role
The city became the epicentral location for the international conference on climate change.
Media attention was epicentral to the small village after the discovery.
Her home was epicentral to the community's relief efforts during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: central - being in the center or most important position
  • antonym: peripheral - on the edge or outer part, not central
