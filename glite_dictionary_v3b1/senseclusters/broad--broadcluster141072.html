broad

adjective
open-minded; tolerant
3.6 5.22/m
Pronunciation: / br…ëd / (AmE), / br…îÀêd / (BrE)
Having or showing willingness to consider different ideas or opinions; open-minded or tolerant.

Examples

Broad mind
His broad approach to politics made him popular with voters from different backgrounds.
She has broad sympathies and accepts people from all walks of life.
The committee took a broad view of the community's needs.
