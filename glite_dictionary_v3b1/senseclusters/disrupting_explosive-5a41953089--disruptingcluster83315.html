disrupting explosive

noun
high explosive used to damage a target
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈrʌptɪŋ ɪkˈsploʊsɪv / (AmE), / dɪsˈrʌptɪŋ ɪkˈspləʊsɪv / (BrE)
