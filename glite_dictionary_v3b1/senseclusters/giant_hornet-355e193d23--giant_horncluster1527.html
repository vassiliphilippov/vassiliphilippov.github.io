giant hornet

noun
large species of hornet
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈhɔrnɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈhɔːnɪt / (BrE)
a large species of hornet, especially the European hornet (Vespa crabro) introduced into the United States

Examples

A giant hornet nest

The giant hornet is known for its painful sting and aggressive behavior.
