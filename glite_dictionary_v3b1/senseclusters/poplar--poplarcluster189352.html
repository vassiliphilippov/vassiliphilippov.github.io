poplar

noun
soft, light wood from a poplar tree
6.5 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑː.plər/ (AmE), /ˈpɒ.plə/ (BrE)
The soft, lightweight, pale wood obtained from the poplar tree, often used for making furniture, plywood, and paper but not very durable.

Examples

Poplar wood furniture
He learned that poplar is not suitable for outdoor construction because it is not durable.
Some musical instruments use poplar for their bodies due to its light weight.
The cabinet was made from poplar because of its smooth finish and affordable price.
