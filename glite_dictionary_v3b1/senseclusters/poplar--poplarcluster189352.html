poplar

noun
soft, light wood from a poplar tree
3.8 0.19/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ëÀêpl…ö / (AmE), / Ààp…ípl…ô / (BrE)
The soft, lightweight, pale wood obtained from the poplar tree, often used for making furniture, plywood, and paper but not very durable.

Examples
