fireboard

noun
fireplace covering panel
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a decorative or protective panel used to cover the opening of a fireplace when it is not in use

Examples

Decorative fireboard
During the summer, a painted fireboard covered the empty fireplace in the living room.
The antique fireboard featured a floral design that matched the room's decor.
She removed the fireboard to prepare the fireplace for use in the winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firescreen - a screen placed in front of a fireplace to protect from sparks or heat
