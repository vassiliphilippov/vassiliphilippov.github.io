tympanic vein

noun
vein from tympanic cavity (ear)
7.7
Pronunciation: /tɪm.ˈpæn.ɪk veɪn/ (AmE), /tɪm.ˈpæn.ɪk veɪn/ (BrE)
A vein draining blood from the tympanic cavity and emptying into the retromandibular vein.

Examples

Tympanic vein drains blood

The tympanic vein plays an important role in draining blood from the middle ear.
