negligent

adjective
failing to take proper care or attention
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛɡlɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡlɪdʒənt / (BrE)
failing to take proper care or show enough attention to something, especially when this causes harm or risk.

Examples

Negligent attitude
His negligent attitude toward deadlines led to several missed opportunities.
She was criticized for being negligent in her duties as a caretaker.
The company was found negligent in failing to provide safety equipment for its workers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attentive - paying close attention to something
  • synonym: careless - not giving sufficient attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
