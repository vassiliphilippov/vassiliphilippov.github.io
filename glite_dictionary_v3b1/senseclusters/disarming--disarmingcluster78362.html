disarming

noun
act of removing weapons
2.3
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɑrmɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪsˈɑːmɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of reducing or depriving of weapons, especially as part of a military or political process.

Examples

Disarming of weapons
International observers monitored the disarming of rebel groups after the ceasefire agreement.
Successful disarming operations often require cooperation from all parties involved.
The country's disarming of its nuclear weapons was seen as a major step toward peace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disarmament - the act of reducing or eliminating military forces or weapons
