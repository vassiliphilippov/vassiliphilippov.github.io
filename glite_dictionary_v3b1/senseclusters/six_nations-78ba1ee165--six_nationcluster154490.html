Six Nations

noun
annual European rugby union championship
3.5
Pronunciation: / sɪks ˈneɪʃənz / (AmE), / sɪks ˈneɪʃənz / (BrE)
an annual European rugby union championship contested by six national teams (England, France, Ireland, Italy, Scotland, and Wales).

Examples

Six Nations rugby
England's team aims to win the Six Nations title again this year.
The match at Twickenham could decide who tops the Six Nations this season.
Fans from Ireland and France traveled together to watch the final Six Nations match.
