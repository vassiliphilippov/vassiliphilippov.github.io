lamellibranch

adjective
bivalve mollusk (clam, oyster, etc.)
8.2
Pronunciation: / ləˈmɛl.əˌbræŋk / (AmE), / ləˈmɛl.ɪˌbraŋk / (BrE)
A mollusk of the class Bivalvia, such as a clam, oyster, or mussel, having a shell consisting of two hinged parts.
