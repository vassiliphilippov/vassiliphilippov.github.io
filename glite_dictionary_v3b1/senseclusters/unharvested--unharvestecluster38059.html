unharvested

adjective
not collected
4.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɑrvəstɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɑːvɪstɪd / (BrE)
not collected or gathered, especially referring to crops or natural resources remaining in place

Examples

Unharvested crops
After the heavy rains, many acres of unharvested corn remained in the fields.
The orchard was filled with unharvested apples at the end of the season.
Wild animals often feed on unharvested crops left behind by farmers.
