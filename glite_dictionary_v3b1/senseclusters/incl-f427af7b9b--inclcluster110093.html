incl.

adjective
abbreviation for inclusive
5.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈkluː.sɪv/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈkluː.sɪv/ (BrE)
abbreviation for 'inclusive', used after dates or numbers to show that the endpoints are included

Examples

March 10-15 incl.
The conference will be held from March 10 to March 15 incl., so both dates are part of the event.
The offer is valid for ages 18 to 25 incl., meaning anyone in that range can apply.
The store is open Monday to Saturday incl., but closed on Sundays.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exclusive - not including the stated limits or endpoints
  • synonym: inclusive - including the stated limits or endpoints
