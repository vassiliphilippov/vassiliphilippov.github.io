extrathoracic

adjective
outside the chest
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəθəˈræsɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəθəˈræsɪk / (BrE)
located or occurring outside the chest cavity

Examples

Extrathoracic tumor
The scan revealed an extrathoracic tumor near the patient's upper abdomen.
Doctors ruled out any extrathoracic causes for her breathing difficulties.
An extrathoracic obstruction can affect airflow outside the chest area.
