Nephite

adjective
of the Nephites
Pronunciation: / ˈniːfaɪt / (AmE), / ˈniːfaɪt / (BrE)
relating to the Nephites, their culture, history, language, or records as described in the Book of Mormon

Examples

Nephite language
The ancient text was written in a Nephite script that scholars are still trying to decipher.
Many traditions in the region are believed to have Nephite origins according to local legends.
The museum displayed artifacts from a Nephite settlement described in the Book of Mormon.
