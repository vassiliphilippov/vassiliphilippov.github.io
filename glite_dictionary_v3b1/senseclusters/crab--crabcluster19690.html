crab

noun
pubic louse; parasitic insect
Pronunciation: / kræb / (AmE), / kræb / (BrE)
a louse that infests the pubic region of the human body, causing irritation; also called a pubic louse

Examples

Pubic crab
Crabs can be treated effectively with a special shampoo from the pharmacy.

The doctor confirmed that his itching was caused by crabs.
