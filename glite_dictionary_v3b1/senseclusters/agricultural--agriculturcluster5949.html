agricultural

adjective
relating to farming or agriculture
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /æ.ɡrɪ.ˈkʌl.tʃər.əl/ (AmE), /æ.ɡrɪ.ˈkʌl.tʃə.rəl/ /; / /æ.ɡrɪ.ˈkʌl.tʃʊə.rəl/ (BrE)
connected with or relating to farming, agriculture, or the production of crops and animals

Examples

Agricultural activities
Many rural areas still depend on agricultural activities as their main source of income.
The conference focused on agricultural technology and sustainable farming practices.
The government announced new subsidies for agricultural producers to help them modernize their equipment.
Innovations in agricultural technology have increased food production worldwide.
She attended an agricultural college to study crop management.
The country’s economy depends heavily on its agricultural sector.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farming - activity of cultivating crops and raising animals for food
  • antonym: industrial - relating to industry rather than agriculture
