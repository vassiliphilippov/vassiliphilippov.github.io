trialist

noun
supporter of trialism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈtraɪəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates trialism, the division of a state or power among three groups or nations

Examples

Political trialist
The leading trialist argued for equal representation of all three national groups in the new government.
Historians debate the influence of trialist ideas on the political reforms of the early twentieth century.
As a prominent trialist, she published articles supporting the division of power among the three regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: federalist - a supporter of a federal system of government
  • antonym: unionist - a supporter of political union between groups or nations
