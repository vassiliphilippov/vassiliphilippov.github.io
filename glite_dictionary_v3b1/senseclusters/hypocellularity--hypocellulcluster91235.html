hypocellularity

noun
state of having abnormally few cells
9.9
Pronunciation: /haɪ.pə.sel.jə.ˈlær.ɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /haɪ.pə.sel.jə.ˈlær.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
the condition of having an abnormally low number of cells in a tissue or organ

Examples
