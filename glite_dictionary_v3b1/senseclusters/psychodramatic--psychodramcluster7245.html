psychodramatic

adjective
relating to psychodrama
8.9
Pronunciation: /saɪ.koʊ.drə.ˈmæt.ɪk/ (AmE), /saɪ.kəʊ.drə.ˈmæt.ɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of psychodrama, especially as a method of therapy using role play and acting

Examples

Psychodramatic therapy session
The therapist used psychodramatic techniques to help clients explore their feelings.
Her psychodramatic approach encouraged participants to act out real-life situations.
In a psychodramatic session, group members take on different roles to express emotions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: therapeutic - relating to the treatment of illness or emotional problems
