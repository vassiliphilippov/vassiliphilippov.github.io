unreferred

adjective
not directed elsewhere
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈfɜrd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈfɜːd / (BrE)
not referred or directed to someone or something, especially not sent by another person or authority.

Examples

Unreferred patient
The unreferred patient arrived at the hospital without a doctor's note.
Many unreferred cases are handled directly by the emergency department.
She was surprised to see so many unreferred applicants for the program.
