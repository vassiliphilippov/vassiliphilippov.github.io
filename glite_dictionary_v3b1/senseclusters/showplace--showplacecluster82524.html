showplace

noun
notable place, often exhibited
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊˌpleɪs / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌpleɪs / (BrE)
a place or building that is impressive, beautiful, or interesting and is shown to visitors

Their new home is a real showplace with its modern design and beautiful gardens.
The renovated theater is now a showplace that attracts visitors from all over the region.
