fine

adjective
showing elegance or high quality
Pronunciation: / faɪn / (AmE), / faɪn / (BrE)
very good in quality or appearance; showing elegance, refinement, or skill

Examples

A fine painting
His performance on the piano was the work of a truly fine musician.
She wore a fine silk dress to the gala, attracting many compliments.
The restaurant is known for its fine cuisine and impeccable service.
