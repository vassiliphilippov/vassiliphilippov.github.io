alien

noun
extraterrestrial being
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.li.ən / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.li.ən / (BrE)

Examples

A green alien
Many science fiction movies feature an alien visiting Earth with advanced technology.
Some people claim to have seen an alien spaceship in the night sky.
