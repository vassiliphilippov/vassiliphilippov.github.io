subway

noun
underground city railway system
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.weɪ / (BrE)
an underground railway system in a city, operated by electric trains

Examples

City subway train
She takes the subway to work every morning because it's faster than driving.
The city has invested in expanding its subway system to reduce traffic congestion.
Tourists often get confused by the subway map during their first visit to New York City.
