caudex

noun
woody stem base of certain perennial plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːdɛks / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdɛks / (BrE)
the thickened, woody stem base of certain plants, usually at or near ground level, from which new growth can emerge
