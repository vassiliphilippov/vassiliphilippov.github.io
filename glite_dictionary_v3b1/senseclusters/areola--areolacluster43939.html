areola

noun
small circular area (esp. around the nipple)
6.5
Pronunciation: / əˈriː.ə.lə / (AmE), / əˈriː.ə.lə / (BrE)
a small circular area of different color or texture from its surroundings, especially the ring of pigmented skin surrounding a nipple

Examples

Breast areola
A red areola formed around the insect bite on his arm.
Doctors examined the areola for any unusual changes in size or color.
The baby's mouth was positioned over the mother's areola during breastfeeding.
