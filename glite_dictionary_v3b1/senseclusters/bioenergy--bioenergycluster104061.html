bioenergy

noun
vital life force
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊˈɛnərdʒi / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
a supposed life force or vital energy believed by some healing traditions to affect health and well-being

Examples

Healing bioenergy
Some alternative therapies claim to balance the body's bioenergy to promote healing.
The practitioner explained that blocked bioenergy could lead to physical or emotional problems.
Many cultures have concepts similar to bioenergy as a source of life and health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chi - the vital life force in Chinese philosophy and medicine
  • synonym: prana - the vital energy believed in Indian traditions to sustain life
