backflip

noun
backward somersault
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈbæk.flɪp/ (AmE), /ˈbæk.flɪp/ (BrE)
an acrobatic movement in which a person jumps and rotates backward in the air, landing on their feet

Examples

A perfect backflip
The gymnast performed a flawless backflip during her floor routine.
He practiced the backflip for weeks before finally landing it safely.
Crowds cheered as the skateboarder landed a daring backflip off the ramp.

Related Terms

  • synonym: somersault - an acrobatic movement where the body rolls forward or backward in a complete revolution
