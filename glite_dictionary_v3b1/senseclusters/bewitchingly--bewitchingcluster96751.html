bewitchingly

adverb
in an enchanting or captivating way
Pronunciation: / bɪˈwɪtʃɪŋli / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪtʃɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is powerfully or charmingly attractive or enchanting.

Examples

She smiled bewitchingly
She smiled bewitchingly, making it impossible for anyone to refuse her request.
The garden was bewitchingly beautiful under the full moon.
The music played bewitchingly throughout the evening, enchanting all the guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enchantingly - in a charmingly attractive way
