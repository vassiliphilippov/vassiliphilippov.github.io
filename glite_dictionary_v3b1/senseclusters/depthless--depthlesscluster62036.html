depthless

adjective
without depth
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛpθləs / (AmE), / ˈdɛpθləs / (BrE)
lacking depth, either physically or in meaning

Examples

Depthless story
The artist's work was criticized as depthless and lacking true emotion.
Despite its beautiful appearance, the lake was surprisingly depthless and safe for children.
Some conversations feel depthless when people only discuss the weather.
