beef burrito

noun
tortilla filled with beef and other ingredients
Pronunciation: / bif bəˈritoʊ / (AmE), / biːf bəˈriːtəʊ / (BrE)
A Mexican-style tortilla rolled around a filling of seasoned beef, often with rice, beans, and other ingredients.

Examples

A beef burrito meal
I made homemade beef burritos for my family last night.
She ordered a beef burrito with extra cheese and salsa for lunch.
The restaurant's signature dish is a spicy beef burrito served with guacamole.
