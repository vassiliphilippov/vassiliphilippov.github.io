flange

noun
projecting rim or collar for attachment or strength
Pronunciation: / flændʒ / (AmE), / flændʒ / (BrE)
A projecting flat rim, collar, or rib on an object, serving to strengthen or attach it to another object.

Examples

Metal flange
A broken flange can compromise the structural integrity of the machinery.
The metal pipe has a flange at each end for bolting to adjacent pipes.
The wheel was held in place by a flange that fit snugly into the groove.
