tetch

adjective
easily irritated
Pronunciation: / tɛtʃ / (AmE), / tɛtʃ / (BrE)
easily annoyed or irritable; quick to take offense.

Examples

A tetch person
He grew tetch when the meeting ran longer than expected.
After a sleepless night, she was noticeably tetch with her coworkers.
The children knew to avoid their tetch uncle when he was tired.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not easily upset or excited
  • synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or angered
  • synonym: touchy - easily upset or offended
