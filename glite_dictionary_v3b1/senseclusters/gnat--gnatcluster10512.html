gnat

noun
small biting flying insect
8.8
Pronunciation: / næt / (AmE), / nat /; / næt / (BrE)
a very small flying insect, especially one that bites people or animals

Examples

A gnat bite
A gnat bit my arm while I was hiking in the forest.
Clouds of gnats hovered over the pond at sunset.
Swatting away a single gnat can be surprisingly difficult.
After the rain, clouds of gnats hovered over the marshland.
In summer, I often get bitten by gnats near the river.
The tent was filled with gnats until we zipped it up tightly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: midge - small fly similar to a gnat, often biting
  • synonym: mosquito - a small flying insect that bites and sucks blood
