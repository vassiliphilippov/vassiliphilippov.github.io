huapango

noun
Mexican dance party
9.9
Pronunciation: / wɑːˈpɑːŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / wɑːˈpaŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a social event or party where people dance to traditional Mexican huapango music

Examples

Village huapango party
We were invited to a lively huapango in the village square last Saturday night.
At the huapango, couples performed traditional dances in colorful costumes.
The music at the huapango kept everyone on their feet until the early morning.
