psychometry

noun
clairvoyant object reading
Pronunciation: / saɪˈkɑːmətri / (AmE), / saɪˈkɒmətri / (BrE)
the supposed ability to discover information about an object or its owner by touching or holding the object

Examples

Psychometry by touch
The psychic claimed to use psychometry to learn about the object's history.
During the demonstration, she practiced psychometry by holding a stranger's watch.
Many people are skeptical about the reality of psychometry and similar abilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clairvoyance - the claimed ability to gain information about an object or person by extrasensory perception
  • synonym: telepathy - the supposed communication of thoughts or ideas by means other than the known senses
