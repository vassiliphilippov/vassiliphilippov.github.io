andesitic

adjective
like andesite rock
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌændəˈsɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌændɪˈsɪtɪk / (BrE)
describing rocks or lava that have a composition similar to andesite, an intermediate volcanic rock

Examples

Andesitic volcanic rock
The geologist identified the rock as andesitic based on its mineral content.
Many volcanoes in the Andes Mountains produce andesitic lava flows.
The andesitic composition of the ash affected the surrounding soil fertility.

Related Terms

  • antonym: basaltic - describing rocks or lava similar to basalt
  • antonym: rhyolitic - describing rocks or lava similar to rhyolite
