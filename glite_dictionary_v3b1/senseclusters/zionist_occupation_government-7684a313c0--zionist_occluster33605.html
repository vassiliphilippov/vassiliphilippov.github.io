Zionist occupation government

noun
pejorative term for Israeli government over occupied territories
Pronunciation: / ˈzaɪ.ə.nɪst ˌɑː.kjəˈpeɪ.ʃən ˈɡʌv.ərn.mənt / (AmE), / ˈzaɪ.ə.nɪst ˌɒk.jəˈpeɪ.ʃən ˈɡʌv.ən.mənt / (BrE)
(sometimes offensive) A term used by critics to refer to the government associated with Israeli control over Palestinian territories, often used in a pejorative or polemical context.

Examples

The Zionist occupation government policies
International reactions to policies attributed to the Zionist occupation government have been deeply divided.
Some media outlets criticized protesters for their repeated use of the phrase Zionist occupation government.
The term Zionist occupation government is frequently used in anti-Israel rhetoric by certain activist groups.
