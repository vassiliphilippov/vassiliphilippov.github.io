bus depot

noun
place where buses are kept and operated
3.1
Pronunciation: / bʌs ˈdiː.poʊ / (AmE), / bʌs ˈdiː.pəʊ / (BrE)
A building or area where buses are kept, maintained, and operated from, often including passenger waiting facilities.

Examples

The bus depot building
After his shift, the driver parked the bus at the bus depot for the night.
The city plans to renovate the old bus depot to improve commuter services.
We waited at the bus depot for the next departure to the city center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bus station - a place where buses regularly stop for passengers
  • synonym: terminal - a station at the end of a transportation line
