Book of Obadiah

proper noun
Old Testament book of prophecies
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊk əv oʊˈbeɪdiə / (AmE), / ˈbʊk əv əʊˈbeɪdɪə / (BrE)
A book of the Old Testament containing the prophecies of Obadiah; the shortest book in the Christian Bible.
