bean tostada

noun
Mexican dish with tortilla and beans
Pronunciation: / bin toʊˈstɑːdə / (AmE), / biːn tɒˈstɑːdə / (BrE)
A Mexican dish consisting of a flat, crispy tortilla topped with refried beans, and often other ingredients such as lettuce, cheese, and salsa.

Examples

A bean tostada dish
For a quick dinner, she made herself a bean tostada with lettuce and tomato.
I ordered a bean tostada topped with cheese and fresh salsa at the restaurant.
The menu featured several varieties, but the classic bean tostada remained my favorite.
