gum ball

noun
ball-shaped chewing gum with sugar coating
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌm ˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈɡʌm ˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a small, round piece of chewing gum with a hard, colored sugar coating, typically sold in vending machines

Examples

A gum ball machine
Children lined up eagerly, hoping to get their favorite flavor gum ball.
She put a bright red gum ball into her mouth and started to chew.
The old vending machine dispensed a colorful gum ball for just a quarter.
