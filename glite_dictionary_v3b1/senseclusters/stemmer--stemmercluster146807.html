stemmer

noun
person who stems artificial flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛmər / (AmE), / ˈstɛmə / (BrE)
a worker who makes or attaches stems to artificial flowers as part of their production.

Examples

Flower stemmer
Experienced stemmers can attach stems to flowers much faster than new trainees.
She worked as a stemmer in the artificial flower factory during the summer.
The stemmer crafted delicate wire stems for hundreds of silk blooms each day.
