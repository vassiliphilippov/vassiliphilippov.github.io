anchorage

noun
emotional or psychological security or support
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋ.kɚ.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈæŋ.kər.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
a feeling or source of security or stability, especially emotional or psychological support

Examples

Emotional anchorage
After his mentor retired, he felt the loss of a steady anchorage in his professional life.
Her friendship became an emotional anchorage during difficult times.
Many children find their primary anchorage in loving parental relationships.

Related Terms

  • antonym: instability - lack of stability; the state of being unstable
  • synonym: security - the state of feeling safe and free from worry
