metalworking vise

noun
tool for holding metal objects tightly
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛt̬əlˌwɝːkɪŋ vaɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəlˌwɜːkɪŋ vaɪs / (BrE)
a heavy tool with two parallel iron jaws and a wide opening below, used for holding metal objects firmly during metalworking processes

Examples

A metalworking vise tool
A sturdy metalworking vise is essential in any metal fabrication workshop.
He tightened the metalworking vise to hold the pipe in place while he filed its edges.
The machinist securely clamped the metal sheet in the metalworking vise before drilling holes.
