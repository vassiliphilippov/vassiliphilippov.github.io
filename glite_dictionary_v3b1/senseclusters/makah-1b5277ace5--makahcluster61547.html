Makah

proper noun
Native American people
Pronunciation: / məˈkɑː / (AmE), / məˈkɑː / (BrE)
a member of a Native American people from the Pacific Northwest coast of the United States

Examples

A Makah person
The Makah have a rich tradition of whaling and fishing.
A Makah elder shared stories about their ancestors' journeys along the coast.
Many Makah continue to preserve their language and cultural practices today.
