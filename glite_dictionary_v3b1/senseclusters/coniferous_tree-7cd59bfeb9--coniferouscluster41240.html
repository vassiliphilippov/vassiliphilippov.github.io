coniferous tree

noun
a tree or shrub that bears cones
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɪfərəs tri / (AmE), / kəˈnɪfərəs triː / (BrE)
a tree or shrub, typically evergreen, that produces cones and needle-like or scale-like leaves; a member of the conifer group

Examples

A coniferous tree with cones
A coniferous tree can usually be identified by its needle-like leaves and cones.
Many wildlife species depend on coniferous trees for shelter and food.
The forest was dominated by coniferous trees such as pine and spruce.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conifer - a tree that produces cones, usually evergreen
  • antonym: deciduous tree - a tree that sheds its leaves annually
