bump up

verb
increase or raise (something)
7.4
Pronunciation: / bʌmp ʌp / (AmE), / bʌmp ʌp / (BrE)
to increase or raise something, especially by a noticeable amount

Examples

To bump up prices
The company bumped up our salaries this year to match inflation.
They're planning to bump up the ticket prices next season.
We should bump up our advertising budget to reach more customers.

Related Terms
