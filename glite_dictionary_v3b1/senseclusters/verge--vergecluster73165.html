verge

noun
edge or boundary region
3.0 3.1
Pronunciation: / vɝdʒ / (AmE), / vɜːdʒ / (BrE)
the edge, margin, or brink of something; the point at which something is about to happen or change

Examples

On the verge of tears
Dandelions were growing on the grassy verge beside the country road.
He stood on the verge of the cliff, gazing down at the sea below.
The company is on the verge of a major breakthrough in cancer research.
After months of drought, the crops were on the verge of dying.
She felt she was on the verge of discovering something important.
The company is teetering on the verge of collapse.
