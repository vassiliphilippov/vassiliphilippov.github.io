overtiredness

noun
excessive tiredness
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈtaɪərdnəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈtaɪədnəs / (BrE)
a state of being extremely or excessively tired, often causing difficulty sleeping, restlessness, or irritability

Examples

Child's overtiredness
The child's overtiredness made it impossible for her to settle down at bedtime.
After a long day of travel, his overtiredness led to restlessness and irritability.
Parents often struggle with their children's overtiredness after busy days or missed naps.
His overtiredness made it difficult for him to concentrate during the meeting.
Children often become irritable due to overtiredness after missing their afternoon nap.
After several long shifts, her overtiredness was obvious to everyone at work.
After staying up all night to finish her project, she felt a wave of overtiredness in the morning.
Children often show signs of overtiredness by becoming fussy or unable to fall asleep.
Long hours at work can lead to overtiredness and difficulty concentrating.

  • synonym: exhaustion - a state of being extremely tired
  • synonym: fatigue - extreme tiredness or exhaustion
