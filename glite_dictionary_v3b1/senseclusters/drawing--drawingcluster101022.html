drawing

noun
making pictures or diagrams
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔɪŋ /; / ˈdrɔɪɪŋ /; / ˈdrɔɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːɪŋ /; / ˈdrɔː.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or skill of making pictures or diagrams, or a picture made by hand with a pen, pencil, or similar tool

Examples

A drawing of a cat
His drawing of the landscape won first prize in the competition.
She spent the afternoon working on a drawing of her cat.
The art class focuses on basic drawing techniques.

The book contains several beautiful drawings by famous illustrators.
He spent hours perfecting his drawing of a horse for his art class.
She framed the drawing her son made at school and hung it in the hallway.
The exhibition featured several pencil drawings of the city skyline.

Related Terms

  • antonym: painting - a picture made with paint rather than pen or pencil
  • synonym: sketch - a rough or unfinished drawing or painting, often done quickly
  • synonym: sketching - the act of making a rough or unfinished drawing
