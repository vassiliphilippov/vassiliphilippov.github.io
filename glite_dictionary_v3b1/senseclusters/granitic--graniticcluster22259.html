granitic

adjective
like granite
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈnɪtɪk / (AmE), / ɡrəˈnɪtɪk / (BrE)
made of, containing, or resembling granite, a very hard, coarse-grained rock

Examples

Granitic rock
The granitic cliffs rose sharply above the valley, creating a dramatic landscape.
Scientists studied the granitic composition of the ancient mountain range.
Her determination was as granitic as the stone itself, unbreakable and strong.

Related Terms

  • synonym: granite - a very hard, coarse-grained igneous rock
  • synonym: rocky - full of or resembling rock
