cohesively

adverb
in a unified way
Pronunciation: / koʊˈhiːsɪvli / (AmE), / kəʊˈhiːsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that forms a united or consistent whole

Examples

To work cohesively together
The group presented their findings cohesively, making the report easy to understand.
All the parts of the design fit cohesively to create a beautiful final product.
The students worked cohesively to solve the complex problem during the competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: independently - in a way that is separate or not connected
  • synonym: together - in a united or joined way
