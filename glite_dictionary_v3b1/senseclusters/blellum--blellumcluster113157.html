blellum

noun
talkative fool
Pronunciation: / ˈblɛləm / (BrE)
a person who talks excessively or behaves foolishly, especially in Scottish dialect

Examples

A noisy blellum
Everyone knows him as the village blellum who never stops chatting.
She was called a blellum for telling stories all afternoon at the market.
Don't be such a blellum when the teacher is talking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chatterbox - a person who talks a lot
  • synonym: fool - a person who behaves in a silly or unwise way
  • antonym: silent - not speaking or making noise
