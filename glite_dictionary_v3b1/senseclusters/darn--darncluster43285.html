darn

verb
repair fabric by sewing
1.9 4.3
Pronunciation: / dɑrn / (AmE), / dɑːn / (BrE)
to mend a hole in fabric, especially knitted material, by weaving yarn or thread across the gap

Examples

To darn a sock
I need to darn these gloves before winter arrives.
My grandmother taught me how to darn socks using a special needle.
She patiently darned the hole in his favorite sweater.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mend - to repair something that is broken or damaged
  • synonym: patch - to repair with a piece of material sewn over a hole
