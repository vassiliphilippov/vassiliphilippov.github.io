lister plow

noun
double-moldboard farm plow
9.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪs.tər plaʊ/ (AmE), /ˈlɪs.tə plaʊ/ (BrE)
a type of moldboard plow with a double moldboard designed to move soil to either side of a central furrow, commonly used in farming to prepare seedbeds

Examples

A lister plow tool
After attaching the lister plow to his tractor, he began working the field in preparation for spring.
Modern lister plows help reduce soil erosion by directing water flow along the furrows.
The farmer used a lister plow to create deep furrows for planting potatoes.
A lister plough creates two ridges of soil with a deep furrow between them.
Modern lister ploughs are designed for efficient row crop preparation.
The farmer used a lister plough to prepare the field for planting corn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plough - a large farming implement used for turning over the soil
  • synonym: plow - a farm implement used for cutting, lifting, turning over, and partly pulverizing soil
