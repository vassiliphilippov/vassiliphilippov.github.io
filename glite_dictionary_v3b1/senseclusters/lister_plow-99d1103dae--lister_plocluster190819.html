Synonyms

  • lister
    9.9 0.01/m
    lister is the common short name farmers use for the double-moldboard plow, while lister plow is the fuller technical name.
  • middlebreaker
    9.9 0.00/m
    middlebreaker opens a central furrow and spreads soil to both sides for planting, while lister plow prepares seedbeds more generally.
  • middle buster
    9.9 0.00/m
    middle buster moves soil to each side to form planting rows, while lister plow focuses on making wider seedbeds.
  • two-furrow plow
    8.0 0.00/m
    two-furrow plow turns over two furrows at once for fast field work, while lister plow centers soil around a middle furrow for seedbeds.
  • double-furrow plow
    8.3 0.00/m
    double-furrow plow simply refers to having two moldboards to turn soil, while lister plow often shapes soil into a raised seedbed.
