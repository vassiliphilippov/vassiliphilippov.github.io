pewterer

noun
maker of pewter items
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuːtərər / (AmE), / ˈpjuːtərə / (BrE)
a person who makes or sells objects made of pewter

Examples

The pewterer sells bowls
The pewterer displayed his handcrafted bowls and candlesticks at the local fair.
In the old town, a skilled pewterer was known for his intricate designs.
Many museums feature the work of a famous pewterer from the eighteenth century.
