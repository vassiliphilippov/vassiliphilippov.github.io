stove

noun
heating or cooking apparatus
1.4 1.0
Pronunciation: / stoʊv / (AmE), / stəʊv / (BrE)
A device or apparatus used for providing heat, especially for warming a room or cooking.

Examples

Wood-burning stove
Many old cabins still rely on a wood-burning stove for warmth in winter.
She turned on the stove to heat up the room during the chilly evening.
The broken stove made it difficult to cook dinner or keep the house warm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oven - an enclosed compartment for cooking, baking, or heating food
