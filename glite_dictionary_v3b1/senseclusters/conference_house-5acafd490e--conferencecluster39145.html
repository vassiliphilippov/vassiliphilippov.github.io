conference house

noun
building for holding conferences
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.fɚ.əns haʊs / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.f(ə).r(ə)ns haʊs / (BrE)
a building or center designated for holding conferences and meetings

Examples

A conference house building
Many corporations have their own conference house for internal meetings and workshops.
The annual summit was held at a newly built conference house on the outskirts of the city.
The university's conference house is equipped with state-of-the-art technology for international seminars.
