aphthoid

adjective
like a mouth ulcer
Pronunciation: / ˈæfθɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈæfθɔɪd / (BrE)
resembling or related to an aphtha, a small ulcer or sore in the mouth

Examples

aphthoid mouth sores
The dentist noted several aphthoid spots on the patient's gums during the examination.
Treatment was started for the aphthoid lesions found inside her mouth.
Doctors often see aphthoid ulcers in patients with certain immune conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ulcerous - having or relating to ulcers
