mano

noun
Spanish word for hand
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
a hand, especially as used in English speech borrowed from Spanish

Examples

Raise your mano
She waved her mano at the crowd during the parade.
He raised his mano to signal for silence in the room.
The teacher asked the students to raise their mano if they knew the answer.

Related Terms
