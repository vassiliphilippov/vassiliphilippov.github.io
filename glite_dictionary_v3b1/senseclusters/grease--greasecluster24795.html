grease

noun
a thick oily lubricant
Pronunciation: / ɡris / (AmE), / ɡriːs / (BrE)
A thick, oily or fatty substance, especially one used to lubricate machinery or parts.

Examples

Engine grease
Heavy grease is needed to lubricate the moving parts of this engine.
The mechanic wiped his hands, stained with grease, on a rag.
You should apply grease to the gears to keep them running smoothly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lubricant - a substance used to reduce friction between surfaces
  • synonym: oil - a viscous liquid used for lubrication or fuel
