nyah

noun
mocking chant
Pronunciation: / njɑː / (AmE), / njɑː / (BrE)
a teasing or mocking sound or chant, often repeated, especially by children to taunt others

Examples

A loud nyah
The children sang a loud nyah after winning the game on the playground.
He stuck out his tongue and let out a playful nyah to tease his friend.
Their repeated nyah made the losing team feel even more embarrassed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheer - a shout of encouragement or praise
  • synonym: taunt - a remark or sound made to mock or provoke someone
