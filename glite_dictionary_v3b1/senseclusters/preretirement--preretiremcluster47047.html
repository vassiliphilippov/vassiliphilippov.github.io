preretirement

noun
stage before retirement
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːrɪˈtaɪərmənt / (AmE), / ˌpriːrɪˈtaɪəmənt / (BrE)
the period or stage in a person's life before they retire from work

Examples

The preretirement period
Many people use the preretirement years to save extra money for the future.
During the preretirement phase, she attended several financial planning seminars.
He started thinking about travel plans in his preretirement period.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preparation - the process of getting ready for something
  • antonym: retirement - the period after a person stops working permanently
