semiperipheral

adjective
partly on the edge
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˌpəˈrɪfəriəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˌpəˈrɪfəriəl / (BrE)
partly but not fully on the edge or margin of something, especially in economic or social systems

Examples

Semiperipheral countries
In world-systems theory, some nations are classified as semiperipheral due to their intermediate economic status.
The city occupies a semiperipheral position between the urban center and the rural outskirts.
Researchers studied the influence of semiperipheral regions on global trade patterns.
