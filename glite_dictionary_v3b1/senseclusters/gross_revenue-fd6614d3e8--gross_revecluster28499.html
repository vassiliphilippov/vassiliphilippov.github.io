gross revenue

noun
total income before deductions
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊs ˈrɛvənjuː / (AmE), / ɡrəʊs ˈrɛvənjuː / (BrE)
The total amount of money received for goods and services before any deductions, such as costs or expenses, over a specific period.

Examples

Total gross revenue
Before expenses are deducted, all the money collected counts towards the gross revenue.
Last year, their gross revenue exceeded $5 million for the first time.
The company's gross revenue increased significantly last quarter due to strong sales.

Related Terms

  • antonym: net revenue - income remaining after deductions such as expenses or taxes
