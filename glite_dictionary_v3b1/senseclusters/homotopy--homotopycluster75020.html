homotopy

noun
topological deformation theory
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊ.mə.tə.piː/ (AmE), /ˈhɒ.mə.tə.piː/ (BrE)
a concept in mathematics (topology) describing how one shape or function can be continuously changed into another without cutting or gluing
