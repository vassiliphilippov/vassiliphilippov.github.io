tuberculoma

noun
tuberculosis mass
Pronunciation: / tuːˌbɜːrkjəˈloʊmə / (AmE), / tjuːˌbɜːkjʊˈləʊmə / (BrE)
a mass or lump of tissue formed by tuberculosis infection, usually found in the brain or lungs

Examples

A tuberculoma mass
The MRI scan revealed a tuberculoma in the patient's brain.
Doctors treated the lung tuberculoma with a combination of antibiotics.
A tuberculoma can sometimes be mistaken for a tumor on imaging tests.

Doctors diagnosed the lung mass as a tuberculoma after further testing.
Treatment for a tuberculoma often involves a long course of antibiotics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tumor - an abnormal mass of tissue
