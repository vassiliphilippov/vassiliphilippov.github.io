carcinomatosis

noun
widespread cancer spread
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːrsɪnoʊməˈtoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌkɑːsɪnəʊməˈtəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a condition where cancer has spread widely throughout the body or within body cavities

Examples

Carcinomatosis spreads widely
The doctor explained that carcinomatosis made treatment options more limited.
After several tests, the diagnosis of carcinomatosis was confirmed by the oncology team.
Patients with carcinomatosis often require specialized care due to the extent of cancer spread.
The CT scan revealed carcinomatosis affecting the patient's abdominal cavity.
Doctors explained that carcinomatosis often indicates an advanced stage of cancer.
Treatment options for carcinomatosis are limited due to the extensive spread of tumors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: localized cancer - cancer that remains in its original place and has not spread
  • antonym: localized tumor - a tumor confined to one area and not spread
  • synonym: metastasis - the spread of cancer from one part of the body to another
