doggishly

adverb
in a servile way
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔːɡɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡɪʃli / (BrE)
in a servile, fawning, or overly eager-to-please manner

Examples

Follows orders doggishly
He followed his boss doggishly, always eager to do whatever was asked of him.
She laughed doggishly at every joke, hoping to win their approval.
The assistant waited doggishly for instructions, never daring to speak up.

  • antonym: proudly - in a way that shows self-respect or dignity
  • synonym: servilely - in a way that shows excessive willingness to serve or please others
