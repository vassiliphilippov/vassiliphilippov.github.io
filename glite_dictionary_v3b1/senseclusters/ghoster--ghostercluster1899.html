ghoster

noun
person acting as ghost
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊstər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊstə / (BrE)
a person who pretends to be or acts as a ghost, especially to entertain or frighten others

Examples

Haunted house ghoster
During the haunted house event, she was the main ghoster who scared visitors with her spooky costume.
The children laughed when their friend played the ghoster and jumped out from behind the curtain.
In the play, the ghoster wore a white sheet and floated across the stage to surprise the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: actor - a person who performs in plays or movies
  • synonym: ghost - the spirit of a dead person, often imagined as appearing to the living
