light-bulb moment

noun
sudden moment of realization
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪt bʌlb ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt bʌlb ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
A sudden realization, insight, or understanding; a moment when something becomes clear.

Examples

A light-bulb moment
After hours of confusion, he finally had a light-bulb moment and understood what was wrong with the code.
She experienced a light-bulb moment during the meeting when everything finally made sense.
The teacher smiled as her student had a light-bulb moment while solving the math problem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epiphany - a sudden and powerful moment of realization or insight
