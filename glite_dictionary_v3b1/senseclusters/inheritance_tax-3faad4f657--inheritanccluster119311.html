inheritance tax

noun
tax on inherited assets
4.9 3.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈher.ɪ.təns tæks/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈher.ɪ.təns tæks/ (BrE)
a tax levied on the estate or assets inherited from a deceased person

Examples

Inheritance tax payment
After her aunt passed away, she was required to pay inheritance tax on the money she received.
Many families are concerned about the impact of inheritance tax on their estate planning.
The government revised the laws regarding inheritance tax last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: estate tax - a tax levied on the net value of the estate of a deceased person before distribution to the heirs
  • degree lower: gift tax - a tax on money or property given to someone while the giver is alive
