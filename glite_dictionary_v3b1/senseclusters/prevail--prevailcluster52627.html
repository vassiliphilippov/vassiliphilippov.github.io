prevail

verb
persuade someone after effort

to persuade someone to do something, often after repeated effort (usually used as 'prevail upon/on someone')

Examples

To prevail upon a friend
After much discussion, he finally prevailed upon his friends to change their travel plans.
It took a lot of effort to prevail on her to accept the job offer.
She prevailed upon her parents to allow her to study abroad.
