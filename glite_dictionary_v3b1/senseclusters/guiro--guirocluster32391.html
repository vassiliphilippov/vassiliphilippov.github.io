guiro

noun
Latin percussion instrument
9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈgwiː.roʊ/ /; / /ˈgwɪ.roʊ/ (AmE), /ˈgwaɪ.rəʊ/ /; / /ˈgwiː.rəʊ/ (BrE)
