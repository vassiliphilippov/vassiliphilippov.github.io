guiro

noun
Latin percussion instrument
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡwiːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡwiːrəʊ / (BrE)
a Latin American percussion instrument made from a hollow gourd with ridges, played by scraping with a stick

Examples

A guiro instrument
The musician played the guiro to add a unique rhythm to the salsa song.
During the festival, children learned how to use a guiro in a music workshop.
She bought a handmade guiro as a souvenir from her trip to Cuba.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cabasa - a percussion instrument with loops of steel ball chain wrapped around a cylinder
  • synonym: maraca - a Latin American percussion instrument shaken to make sound
