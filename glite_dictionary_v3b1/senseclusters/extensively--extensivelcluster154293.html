In a way that covers a large area or includes a great deal; widely or thoroughly.

Examples

To extensively farm land
Most of the land in this region is extensively farmed for crops.
The policy was extensively discussed during the meeting before any decision was made.
This species has been extensively studied by researchers around the world.

Synonyms

  • widely
    1.0 45/m
    widely occurs in many places or among many people, while extensively implies wide or full coverage across areas or topics.
  • comprehensively
    4.9 1.20/m
    comprehensively covers all or nearly all parts, while extensively means covering large areas or many parts but not always every detail.
  • thoroughly
    3.5 24/m
    thoroughly means done with great detail and care, while extensively emphasizes wide or large-scale coverage rather than deep detail.
  • broadly
    2.7 3.46/m
    broadly means in a general, wide way, while extensively suggests wide or thorough coverage across specific areas.
  • to a great extent
    3.5 1.32/m
    to a great extent means mostly or largely in degree, while extensively describes wide coverage or thorough treatment across areas.
