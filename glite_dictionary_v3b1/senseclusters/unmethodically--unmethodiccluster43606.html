unmethodically

adverb
without order
7.5
Pronunciation: /ʌn.mə.ˈθɑː.dɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /ən.mə.ˈθɑː.dɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /ʌn.mə.ˈθɒ.dɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /ən.mə.ˈθɒ.dɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a way that lacks order, system, or a planned method

Examples

Packed unmethodically
He packed his suitcase unmethodically, tossing clothes in without folding them.
The files were scattered unmethodically across the desk, making it hard to find anything.
She searched unmethodically through the pile of papers, hoping to find her missing receipt.
