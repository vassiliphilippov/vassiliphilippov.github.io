underbaked

adjective
not fully developed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈbeɪkt / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈbeɪkt / (BrE)
not fully developed, planned, or thought through; lacking sufficient preparation or detail.

Examples

Underbaked idea
The committee rejected the underbaked plan because it lacked important details.
Her argument seemed underbaked and failed to convince the audience.
We should not present an underbaked idea to the client without more research.

Related Terms

  • synonym: half-baked - poorly planned or not fully thought out
  • synonym: unfinished - not completed or fully developed
  • antonym: well-developed - fully planned or thoroughly thought out
