sheerly

adverb
steeply or abruptly
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪrli / (AmE), / ˈʃɪəli / (BrE)
in a very steep, abrupt, or nearly vertical manner; directly or sharply downward

Examples

Cliff drops sheerly
The mountain rose sheerly from the valley floor, making the climb extremely challenging.
The road drops sheerly at the edge of the plateau, so drivers must be careful.
Waves crashed against the rocks where the land ended sheerly above the sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abruptly - suddenly or unexpectedly, often with a sharp change
  • synonym: vertically - in a straight up-and-down direction
