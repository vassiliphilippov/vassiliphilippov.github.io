splurgy

noun
brief spending spree
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsplɝːdʒi / (AmE), / ˈsplɜːdʒi / (BrE)
a brief period or act of spending money extravagantly, often on indulgent or unnecessary things

Examples

A weekend splurgy
Her weekend splurgy left her wallet much lighter but her spirits high.
After getting his bonus, he went on a little splurgy at the electronics store.
Sometimes a small splurgy can make a stressful week feel worthwhile.

Related Terms

  • antonym: saving - the act of keeping money instead of spending it
  • synonym: splurge - an act of spending money freely or extravagantly
  • synonym: spree - a short period of doing something in an excessive way
