pesa

noun
money; cash
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪsə / (AmE), / ˈpeɪsə / (BrE)
money or cash, especially as used in East African English, borrowed from Swahili.

Examples

Borrow some pesa
He didn't have enough pesa to buy the bus ticket and had to walk home.
Can you lend me some pesa until I get paid next week?
Vendors at the market prefer to be paid in pesa rather than by card.
