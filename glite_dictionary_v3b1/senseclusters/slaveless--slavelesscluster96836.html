slaveless

adjective
not permitting or having slavery
Pronunciation: / ˈsleɪvləs / (AmE), / ˈsleɪvləs / (BrE)
(of a region, state, or society) not permitting or having slavery; free from slavery.

Examples

A slaveless society
He grew up in a slaveless community where equal rights were valued.
The constitution defined the territory as a slaveless land.
The northern states were declared slaveless following the abolition of slavery.
