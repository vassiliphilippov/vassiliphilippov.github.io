Synonyms

  • divide
    1.1 52/m
    divide means to split something into parts or groups in many situations, while separate covers putting things apart generally.
  • split
    1.3 25/m
    split is to break or cut something into two or more pieces, while separate is a broader act of putting things into parts.
  • partition
    4.0 1.83/m
    partition means to create fixed sections or barriers within a space, while separate means to put or move things into parts.
  • detach
    4.3 1.74/m
    detach means to take a part off or away from something larger, while separate means to divide or move things apart.
  • isolate
    4.2 16/m
    isolate means to keep someone or something alone and cut off contact, while separate means to divide or set apart.
