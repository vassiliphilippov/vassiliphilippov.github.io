decon

noun
decontamination facility
7.7 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàdiÀêk…ën / (AmE), / ÀàdiÀêk…ín / (BrE)
a place or facility where people, equipment, or materials are decontaminated, especially after exposure to hazardous substances

Examples

A decon station
After the chemical spill, all workers had to pass through the decon before leaving the site.
The emergency team quickly set up a decon to handle the exposed equipment.
Medical staff directed patients to the decon for thorough cleaning after the incident.
