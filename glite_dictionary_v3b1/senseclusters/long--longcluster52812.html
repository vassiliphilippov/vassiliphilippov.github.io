long

verb
desire strongly; yearn
5.2
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ / (AmE), / lɒŋ / (BrE)
to have a strong, often persistent desire for something; to yearn deeply

Examples

To long for home
He often longs to see his friends again after a long absence.
In the quiet of the night, she longed for the comfort of home.
Many people long for a sense of purpose in their lives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crave - to have a powerful desire for something
  • antonym: despise - to feel a strong dislike for
  • synonym: yearn - to have an intense feeling of longing for something
