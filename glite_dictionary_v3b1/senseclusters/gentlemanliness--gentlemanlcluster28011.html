The qualities, conduct, or characteristics befitting a gentleman, such as courtesy, honor, and refinement.

Examples

Gentlemanliness and respect
His gentlemanliness was evident in every interaction, from his polite speech to his respectful gestures.
Many admired the gentlemanliness of old-fashioned movie stars and wished society still valued it.
She was impressed by his gentlemanliness, especially when he helped the elderly woman across the street.
