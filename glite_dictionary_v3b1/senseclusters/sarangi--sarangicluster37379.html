sarangi

noun
South Asian string instrument
9.9
Pronunciation: / səˈrɑːŋɡi / (AmE), / səˈrʌŋɡi / (BrE)
a traditional bowed string instrument from South Asia, especially used in Indian and Nepali classical music

Examples

Play the sarangi
The musician played a haunting melody on the sarangi.
She learned to play the sarangi from her grandfather, who was a master musician.
The unique sound of the sarangi adds depth to traditional Indian music performances.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sitar - a plucked string instrument from India
  • synonym: violin - a bowed string instrument used in Western classical music
