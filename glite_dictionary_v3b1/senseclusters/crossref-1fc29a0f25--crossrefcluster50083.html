Crossref

proper noun
DOI registration organization
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌrɛf / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌrɛf / (BrE)
an organization that provides digital object identifiers (DOIs) for scholarly publications to facilitate citation and access

Examples

Crossref DOI provider
Many academic publishers use Crossref to assign DOIs to their journal articles.
Researchers can easily find and cite papers thanks to Crossref services.
The conference proceedings were registered with Crossref for better discoverability.

  • synonym: ORCID - an organization providing unique researcher identifiers
