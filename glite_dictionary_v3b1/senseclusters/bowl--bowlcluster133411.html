bowl

noun
part of a pipe holding tobacco
6.2
Pronunciation: / boʊl / (AmE), / bəʊl / (BrE)
The rounded part of a smoking pipe, open at the top, used to hold tobacco.

Examples

Pipe bowl
A cracked bowl can make a pipe difficult to smoke.
He carefully packed the bowl of his pipe with fresh tobacco.
The old man tapped the ashes out of the bowl before refilling it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pipe - a tube used for smoking tobacco
