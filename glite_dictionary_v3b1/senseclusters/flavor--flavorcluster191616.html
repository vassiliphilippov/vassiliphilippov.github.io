flavor

verb
to give taste to food or drink
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪvər /; / ˈfleɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvə / (BrE)
to give a particular taste or quality to food or drink by adding seasoning or another substance

Examples

To flavor the soup
A squeeze of lemon can flavor the grilled fish beautifully.
She likes to flavor her soups with a pinch of paprika.
The chef flavored the sauce with fresh basil before serving.
The bread was lightly flavoured with rosemary, giving it a distinct aroma.
The chef expertly flavoured the soup with fresh herbs and garlic.
You can flavour the yogurt with honey or fruit to make it sweeter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: season - to add salt, herbs, or spices to food for flavor
  • synonym: spice - to add spices to food
