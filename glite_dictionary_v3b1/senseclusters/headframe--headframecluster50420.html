headframe

noun
mine shaft structure
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhed.freɪm/ (AmE), /ˈhed.freɪm/ (BrE)
a tall structure built above a mine shaft to support the equipment used for lifting people and materials in and out of the mine

Examples

Mine headframe structure
The old headframe still stands at the entrance to the abandoned mine.
Workers repaired the damaged headframe to ensure the safety of the hoisting operations.
A new steel headframe was installed to replace the wooden one destroyed in the fire.
