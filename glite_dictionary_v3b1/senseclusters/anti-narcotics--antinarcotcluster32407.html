anti-narcotics

adjective
against illegal drugs
Pronunciation: / ˌænti nɑrˈkɑtɪks / (AmE), / ˌænti nɑːˈkɒtɪks / (BrE)
relating to actions, policies, or measures intended to prevent illegal drug use or trafficking

Examples

Anti-narcotics policy
The police established a special anti-narcotics unit to combat drug trafficking in the city.
International cooperation is essential for effective anti-narcotics operations across borders.
The new law introduced stricter anti-narcotics measures to reduce illegal drug use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drug enforcement - the act of enforcing laws against illegal drugs
  • antonym: pro-narcotics - supporting or promoting the use of narcotics
