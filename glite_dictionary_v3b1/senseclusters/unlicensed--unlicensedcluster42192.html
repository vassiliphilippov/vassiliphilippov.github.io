unlicensed

adjective
without official approval or permit
5.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlaɪsənst / (AmE), / ʌnˈlaɪsənst / (BrE)
not having official approval, authorization, or a legal permit; without a required license.

Examples

An unlicensed street vendor
Authorities shut down the unlicensed street vendors for safety reasons.
He was caught driving an unlicensed vehicle and fined heavily.
The police raided the unlicensed bar operating downtown.

Related Terms

  • antonym: licensed - having official permission or approval
