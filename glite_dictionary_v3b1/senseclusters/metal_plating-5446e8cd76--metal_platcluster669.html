metal plating

noun
thin metal coating on a surface
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛt̬.əl ˈpleɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmet.əl ˈpleɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
a thin layer of metal deposited on the surface of an object, usually for protection or decoration

Examples

Metal plating layer
He inspected the metal plating on the antique lamp for signs of wear.
Many jewelry items achieve their shine and durability through metal plating.
The process of metal plating can help prevent corrosion on steel parts.
