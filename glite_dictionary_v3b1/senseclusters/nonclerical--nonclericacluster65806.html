nonclerical

adjective
not clerical work
6.7
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈklɛrɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈklɛrɪkəl / (BrE)
not relating to office or administrative work typically performed by clerks; not involving routine clerical tasks

Examples

Nonclerical job
The company is seeking candidates for several nonclerical positions in its research department.
Her responsibilities are entirely nonclerical, focusing on project management and client relations.
Unlike his previous job, his new role is strictly nonclerical and involves no paperwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrative - relating to the organization and running of a business or office
  • antonym: clerical - relating to office or administrative work
