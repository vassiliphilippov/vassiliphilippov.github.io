suction pump

noun
a pump that draws fluids by suction
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌkʃən pʌmp / (AmE), / ˈsʌkʃən pʌmp / (BrE)
a pump used to raise or draw fluids by creating a partial vacuum that causes suction

Examples

A suction pump device
A suction pump is commonly found in laboratories for handling chemicals.
He repaired the old suction pump to irrigate the garden.
The water was removed from the basement using a suction pump.
