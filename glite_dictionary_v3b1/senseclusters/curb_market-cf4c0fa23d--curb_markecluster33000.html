curb market

noun
informal outdoor street market
Pronunciation: / kɝb ˈmɑrkət / (AmE), / kɜːb ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
an informal outdoor market, often held on sidewalks or curbs, where vendors sell goods directly to the public

Examples

A curb market for produce
Local farmers sell their produce at the curb market every weekend.
She enjoys browsing handmade crafts at the city curb market.
The curb market offers fresh vegetables and flowers at affordable prices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: street market - a regular market set up outdoors on streets
