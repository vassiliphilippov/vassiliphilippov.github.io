welder's mask

noun
protective mask for welding
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛldɚz mæsk / (AmE), / ˈwɛldəz mɑːsk / (BrE)
a protective mask worn to shield the face and eyes from sparks, intense light, and heat during welding

Examples

A welder's mask
Always wear a welder's mask to avoid injury while working with metal.
She forgot her welder's mask and had to borrow one from a coworker.
The welder's mask protected his eyes from the bright flashes of the arc.

Related Terms

  • synonym: welding helmet - a rigid protective head covering used in welding
