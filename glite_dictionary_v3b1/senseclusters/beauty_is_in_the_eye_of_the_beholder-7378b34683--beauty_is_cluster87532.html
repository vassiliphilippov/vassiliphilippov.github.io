beauty is in the eye of the beholder

x
beauty is subjective
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːti ɪz ɪn ði aɪ əv ðə bɪˈhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈbjuːti ɪz ɪn ði aɪ əv ðə bɪˈhəʊldə / (BrE)
used to express the idea that different people have different opinions about what is beautiful or attractive

Examples

Saying beauty is in the eye of the beholder
He believes that beauty is in the eye of the beholder when people question his taste in music.
To some, modern art is stunning, but true beauty is in the eye of the beholder.
Whether you like this dress or not, remember that beauty is in the eye of the beholder.
