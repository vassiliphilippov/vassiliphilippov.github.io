wine sauce

noun
savory sauce made with wine, for fish or meat
Pronunciation: / waɪn sɔs / (AmE), / waɪn sɔːs / (BrE)
A savory sauce made with wine and stock, often seasoned with onions and herbs, served with fish or meat.

Examples

A wine sauce recipe
Many French recipes call for a classic wine sauce as an accompaniment to seafood.
She prepared a delicate wine sauce to serve with the poached chicken.
The grilled salmon was topped with a rich wine sauce that enhanced its flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: velouté - a type of white sauce made with stock and roux
