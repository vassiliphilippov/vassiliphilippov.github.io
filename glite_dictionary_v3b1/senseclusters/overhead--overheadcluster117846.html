overhead

noun
top interior surface on a ship
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvɚˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌhɛd / (BrE)
(nautical) The top interior surface or ceiling of an enclosed space on a ship.

Examples

Ship overhead
He bumped his head on the overhead as he walked through the narrow passage.
Storage compartments are often installed along the overhead of a ship's cabin.
The cabin's overhead was painted white to brighten the enclosed space.
