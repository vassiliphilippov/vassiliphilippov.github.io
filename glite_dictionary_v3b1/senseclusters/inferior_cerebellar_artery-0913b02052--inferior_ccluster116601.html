inferior cerebellar artery

noun
artery to lower cerebellum
6.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈfɪr.ɪ.ər ser.ə.ˈbel.ər ˈɑːr.tə.riː/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈfɪə.ri.ə ser.ə.ˈbel.ə ˈɑː.tə.ri/ (BrE)
an artery that supplies blood to the lower part of the cerebellum in the brain

Examples

The inferior cerebellar artery
An MRI revealed an aneurysm in the patient's inferior cerebellar artery.
Damage to the inferior cerebellar artery can result in serious coordination problems.
The neurosurgeon identified a blockage in the inferior cerebellar artery during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cerebellar artery - any of the arteries supplying the cerebellum
  • degree higher: superior cerebellar artery - artery supplying the upper part of the cerebellum
