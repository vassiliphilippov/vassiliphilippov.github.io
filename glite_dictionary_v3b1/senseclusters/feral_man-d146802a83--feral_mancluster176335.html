feral man

noun
man not socialized or raised among humans
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛr.əl mæn / (AmE), / ˈfɪə.rəl mæn / (BrE)
A man who has grown up without socialization, often in isolation from human society.

Examples

A feral man in forest
He behaved more like a wild animal than a human, leading people to call him a feral man.
The story of a feral man surviving without human contact fascinated anthropologists.
The villagers were shocked to find a feral man living alone in the forest for decades.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wild child - a child who has lived isolated from human contact
