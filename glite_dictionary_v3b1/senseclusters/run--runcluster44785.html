run

verb
publish or broadcast (content)
2.6
Pronunciation: / rʌn / (AmE), / rʌn / (BrE)
to publish, broadcast, or express something, especially in media

Examples

To run an article

They ran a commercial for the new car during the Super Bowl.
His argument runs that all people deserve equal opportunities.
The law runs as follows: no vehicles are allowed in the park after dark.
The story runs that she vanished without a trace many years ago.
