Norwich terrier

noun
small English terrier breed

Pronunciation: /ˈnɔːr.ɪtʃ ˈte.rɪ.ər/ /; / /ˈnɔːr.ɪdʒ ˈte.rɪ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈnɒr.ɪtʃ ˈte.rɪ.ə/ /; / /ˈnɒr.ɪdʒ ˈte.rɪ.ə/ (BrE)
A small English breed of terrier with short legs, a wiry straight coat that can be red, grey, or black-and-tan, and erect ears.

The Norwich terrier is known for its friendly and fearless temperament.
