catmint

noun
aromatic perennial herb attractive to cats
Pronunciation: / ˈkætˌmɪnt / (AmE), / ˈkæt.mɪnt / (BrE)
A perennial herb of the mint family with aromatic leaves, white or purple-spotted flowers, and a strong attraction for cats, often used in herbal remedies.

Examples

Dried catmint leaves
Dried catmint was traditionally used in herbal teas for its calming effects.
The aromatic smell of catmint fills the air whenever the wind rustles its leaves.
When she planted catmint in her garden, neighborhood cats began to visit her yard every day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catnip - an aromatic herb (Nepeta cataria) with similar effects on cats
