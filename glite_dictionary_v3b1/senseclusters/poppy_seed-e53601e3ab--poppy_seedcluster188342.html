poppy seed

noun
small edible seed of the poppy flower
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑpi ˌsid / (AmE), / ˈpɒpi ˌsiːd / (BrE)
The small, edible, greyish seed of the poppy flower, used whole or ground in baking and cooking.

Examples

A poppy seed muffin
A lemon and poppy seed muffin is her favorite treat.
She sprinkled poppy seeds on top of the bread before baking.
The recipe calls for two tablespoons of ground poppy seed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sesame seed - small, edible seed from the sesame plant, used in cooking and baking
