family Isoetaceae

noun
a family of aquatic plants (quillworts)
Pronunciation: / ˌfæməli ˌaɪsoʊ.ˈɛtə.siː / (AmE), / ˌfæmɪli ˌaɪsəʊ.ˈɛtə.siː / (BrE)
