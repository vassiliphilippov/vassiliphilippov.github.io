lacking in depth

adjective
not detailed or thorough; superficial
4.1 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈlæk.ɪŋ ɪn depθ/ (AmE), /ˈlæk.ɪŋ ɪn depθ/ (BrE)
not providing enough detail, insight, or thoroughness; superficial.

Examples

Somewhat lacking in depth
Her explanation was clear but somewhat lacking in depth on the technical aspects.
The report was criticized for being lacking in depth and failing to address key issues.
While the movie was enjoyable, many found its storyline lacking in depth compared to the original.
