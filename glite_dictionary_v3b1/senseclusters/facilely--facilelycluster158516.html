facilely

adverb
easily or simply
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfæsɪlli / (AmE), / ˈfæsɪlli / (BrE)
in a way that is easy or effortless, sometimes suggesting a lack of depth or thoroughness

Examples

To facilely answer questions
He facilely answered all the questions, impressing the interviewers with his quick thinking.
The team facilely completed the project ahead of schedule, but some details were overlooked.
Arguments made too facilely can sometimes miss important complexities in the issue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: easily - without difficulty or effort
  • synonym: effortlessly - in a way that requires no effort
  • antonym: laboriously - in a way that requires a lot of effort
