oriel window

noun
projecting bay window supported by corbels or brackets
Pronunciation: / ˈɔriəl ˈwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɔːrɪəl ˈwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
a bay window that projects from the wall of a building and is supported by a corbel or bracket, rather than reaching the ground

Examples

An oriel window on wall
Sunlight streamed through the oriel window, casting patterns on the floor.
The house features a beautiful oriel window overlooking the garden.
They restored the old oriel window to preserve the building's Victorian charm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bay window - a window that projects outward from the main walls of a building forming a bay in a room
