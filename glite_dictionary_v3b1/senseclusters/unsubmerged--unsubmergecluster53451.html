unsubmerged

adjective
not underwater
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsəbˈmɜrdʒd / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəbˈmɜːdʒd / (BrE)
not covered or flooded by water; remaining above the surface of water

Examples

Unsubmerged land
After the floodwaters receded, only a few houses remained unsubmerged on the hill.
The rescue team searched for unsubmerged areas to set up their equipment.
During the storm, the bridge stayed unsubmerged and provided a safe crossing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dry - not wet or covered with liquid
  • antonym: submerged - covered or flooded by water
