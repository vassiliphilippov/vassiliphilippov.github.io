wittily

adverb
in a witty manner
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtɪli / (AmE), / ˈwɪtɪli / (BrE)
In a witty manner; in a way that shows quick and inventive verbal humor.

Examples

To wittily reply
He wittily deflected the criticism with a clever remark.
She wittily responded to every challenge thrown at her during the debate.
The author wittily describes the absurdities of modern life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dully - in a dull manner, lacking brightness or interest
  • synonym: witty - showing quick and inventive verbal humor
