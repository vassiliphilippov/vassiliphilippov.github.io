Examples

Synonyms

  • detractor
    7.1 3.02/m
    a detractor strongly criticizes someone's ideas or work in public, while a knocker is more a casual, informal critic.
  • disparager
    8.5 0.01/m
    a disparager criticizes or belittles someone's worth directly, while a knocker is an informal critic who speaks down about things.
  • denigrator
    8.6 0.01/m
    a denigrator attacks someone's reputation unfairly and strongly, while a knocker simply speaks badly about value or quality.
  • depreciator
    8.8 0.00/m
    a depreciator lowers the stated value of something, often in figures or speech, while a knocker tends to speak down about value informally.
  • mocker
    7.1 0.13/m
    a mocker laughs at or copies someone to show scorn, while a knocker mainly criticizes or belittles value or quality.
