fuelwood

noun
wood for burning
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːəlˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəlˌwʊd / (BrE)
wood that is used as fuel for heating or cooking

Examples

Dry fuelwood
The villagers collected fuelwood from the nearby forest to cook their meals.
During the cold months, families rely on fuelwood to heat their homes.
Sustainable management of fuelwood resources is important for the environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charcoal - a black substance made by burning wood slowly, used as fuel
  • synonym: firewood - wood that is burned in a fire for heat
