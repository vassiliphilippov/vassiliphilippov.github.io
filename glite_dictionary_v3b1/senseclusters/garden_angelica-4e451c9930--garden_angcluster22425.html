garden angelica

noun
a herb grown for edible stems and medicinal roots
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdən ænˈdʒɛlɪkə / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˈændʒlɪkə / (BrE)
a biennial herb (Angelica archangelica) cultivated for its edible candied stems and roots used in medicine

Examples

Fresh garden angelica
Cultivating garden angelica requires moist soil and partial shade for best growth.
The stems of garden angelica are often candied for use in desserts and baking.
Traditional medicine sometimes employs the roots of garden angelica to treat digestive disorders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angelica - any plant of the genus Angelica, especially those used for flavoring and medicine
