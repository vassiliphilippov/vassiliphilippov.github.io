tote up

verb
add up a total; calculate the sum
8.0
Pronunciation: / toʊt ʌp / (AmE), / təʊt ʌp / (BrE)
to calculate the total of several numbers or amounts by adding them together

Examples

We need to tote up the votes before announcing the winner.
