follow in someone's footsteps

verb

to do the same thing as someone else, especially a parent or mentor, or to emulate their actions or career

Examples

To follow in someone's footsteps
After graduating from college, she decided to follow in her father's footsteps and join the family business.
He hopes his son will follow in his footsteps and pursue a career in law.
Many young athletes dream of following in the footsteps of their sporting heroes.
