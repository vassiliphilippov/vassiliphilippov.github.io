co-principal

adjective
sharing main responsibility
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊˈprɪnsəpəl / (AmE), / ˌkəʊˈprɪnsəpəl / (BrE)
sharing the main responsibility or authority for something with one or more others

Examples

Co-principal investigator
The two co-principal investigators led the research project together.
As co-principal authors, they shared equal credit for the publication.
The school appointed co-principal administrators to manage the new campus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joint - involving two or more people together
  • antonym: sole - being the only one; not shared
