stingray

noun
cell-phone surveillance device
9.3

a portable surveillance device used by law enforcement or intelligence agencies to locate or track mobile phones by imitating a cell tower and collecting device identifiers.

Examples

Police use a stingray
The police used a stingray to locate the suspect's phone.
Journalists raised privacy concerns after learning prosecutors deployed a stingray during the investigation.
Our city passed rules requiring warrants before law enforcement can use a stingray.

Related Terms

  • synonym: IMSI-catcher - a device that collects International Mobile Subscriber Identities and other data from nearby mobile phones
  • synonym: cell-site simulator - a device that simulates a mobile phone mast to make phones connect and reveal identifying information
