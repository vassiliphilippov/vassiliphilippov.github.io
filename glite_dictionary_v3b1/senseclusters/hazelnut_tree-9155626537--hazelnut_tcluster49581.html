hazelnut tree

noun
tree producing edible hazelnuts
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪzəlˌnʌt triː / (AmE), / ˈheɪzəlnʌt triː / (BrE)
A shrub or small tree of the genus Corylus, which produces edible nuts known as hazelnuts, typically enclosed in a leafy husk.

Examples

The hazelnut tree nuts
Squirrels often gather around the hazelnut tree to collect its tasty nuts.
The hazelnut tree in our backyard produces a generous crop each autumn.
We planted a hazelnut tree in the garden to attract more wildlife.
