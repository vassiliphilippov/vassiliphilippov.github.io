luminosity

noun
quality of emitting or reflecting light
5.8 5.8
Pronunciation: /luː.mə.ˈnɑː.sɪ.tɪ/ /; / /luː.mɪ.ˈnɑː.sɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /luː.mə.ˈnɒ.sɪ.tɪ/ /; / /luː.mɪ.ˈnɒ.sɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)

Examples
