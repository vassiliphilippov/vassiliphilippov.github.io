thro'

preposition
archaic 'through'
9.9
Pronunciation: / θroʊ / (AmE), / θrəʊ / (BrE)
an old or poetic spelling of 'through', used especially in poetry or archaic texts

Examples

Flow thro' valley
The poet wrote of the river flowing thro' the silent valley at dawn.
In old letters, you might find the word thro' instead of 'through'.
The cold wind whistled thro' the cracks in the ancient stone wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: through - from one side or end to the other
