Ophrys muscifera

noun
a European orchid with fly-like flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːfrɪs ˌmʌsəˈfɛrə / (AmE), / ˈɒfrɪs ˌmʌsɪˈfɪərə / (BrE)
A European orchid species whose flowers resemble flies, commonly known as the fly orchid.
