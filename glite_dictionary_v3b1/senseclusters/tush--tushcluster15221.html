tush

noun
animal's pointed tooth
9.3
Pronunciation: / tʌʃ / (AmE), / tʌʃ / (BrE)
a long, pointed tooth, especially one found in animals such as boars or elephants

Examples

A boar's tush
The wild boar's tush was sharp enough to injure a predator.
Hunters admired the size of the elephant's tush after a long expedition.
In some animals, a tush can be used for digging or fighting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tusk - a long, curved, pointed tooth of certain animals, such as elephants or walruses
