NP

interjection
texting abbreviation: “no problem”
Pronunciation: / ən pi / (AmE), / ən pi / (BrE)
Informal texting or chat abbreviation for 'no problem', used as a casual reply to thanks or an apology.

Examples

Replying NP
When I thanked her, she replied with NP, showing it really was no problem.
He texted NP after I apologised, so I knew the mistake wasn't important.
Friends often answer messages with NP when helping each other, casually signalling it's fine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: no problem - a casual phrase meaning that something was not an inconvenience
  • synonym: you're welcome - a polite response to 'thank you', indicating that the help was gladly given
