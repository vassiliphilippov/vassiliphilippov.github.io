echoed through

verb
reverberated in a space
4.9 0.49/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…õko äd Œ∏ru / (AmE), / Àà…õk…ô äd Œ∏ruÀê / (BrE)
(of a sound) reverberated or resounded throughout a space, typically creating a prolonged or repeated effect.

Examples

Voice echoed through hall
Her voice echoed through the grand cathedral, reaching every corner of the vast hall.
The sound of laughter echoed through the old, empty theater after the show ended.
Thunder echoed through the mountains, making the night feel even more dramatic.
