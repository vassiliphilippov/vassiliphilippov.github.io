anti-foreign

adjective
opposed to foreigners
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈfɔrən / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈfɒrən / (BrE)
opposed to people, ideas, or things from other countries

Examples

Anti-foreign remarks
The politician's anti-foreign remarks sparked controversy across the country.
Some groups protested the anti-foreign policies introduced by the government.
Critics argue that anti-foreign attitudes harm international cooperation and understanding.

Related Terms

  • antonym: welcoming - friendly or accepting toward foreigners
  • synonym: xenophobic - having a fear or dislike of foreigners
