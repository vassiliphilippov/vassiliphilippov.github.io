suck down

verb
eat or drink quickly and greedily
4.8
Pronunciation: / sʌk daʊn / (AmE), / sʌk daʊn / (BrE)
To eat or drink something quickly or greedily, often with enthusiasm or without pause.

Examples

To suck down a drink
After the marathon, she was eager to suck down a bottle of water.
He managed to suck down his coffee in less than a minute before the meeting.
They would suck down burgers and fries while watching the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gulp down - to swallow food or drink quickly
  • synonym: wolf down - to eat food very quickly
