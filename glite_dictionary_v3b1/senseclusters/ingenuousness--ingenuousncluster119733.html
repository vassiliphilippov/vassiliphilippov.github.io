ingenuousness

noun
openness or frankness

Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈdʒen.juː.əs.nəs/ /; / /ɪn.ˈdʒen.jʊ.əs.nəs/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈdʒen.jʊ.əs.nəs/ /; / /ɪn.ˈdʒen.juː.əs.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being open, honest, and innocent; showing frankness and lack of deceit
