Nigeria

proper noun
country in West Africa
1.0
Pronunciation: / naɪˈdʒɪriə / (AmE), / naɪˈdʒɪəriə / (BrE)
A country in West Africa, bordering the Gulf of Guinea, which gained independence from Britain in 1960 and is the most populous country in Africa.

Examples

Nigeria country
Nigeria is known for its diverse cultures and rich history.
She was born in Nigeria and moved to the United States as a child.
