figure

noun
a person as seen or regarded by others
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡjə /; / ˈfɪɡə / (BrE)
a person, especially one who is notable, important, or seen in a particular way

Examples

A famous figure

Nelson Mandela was a prominent figure in the fight against apartheid.
She became a central figure in the local community after founding the charity.
The speaker is a leading figure in the tech industry.
