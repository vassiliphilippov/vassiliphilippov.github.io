soever

particle
emphatic particle
9.9
Pronunciation: /soʊ.ˈe.vər/ (AmE), /səʊ.ˈe.və/ (BrE)
used after pronouns or adverbs to emphasize any amount or degree, often in formal or legal expressions.

Examples

Any person soever
The law applies to any person whomsoever is found guilty of the offense.
No reason soever was given for the sudden cancellation of the event.
He claimed he had no knowledge soever of the missing documents.

Related Terms
