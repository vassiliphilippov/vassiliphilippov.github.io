beefalo

noun
hybrid of cattle and buffalo
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːfəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbiːfəˌləʊ / (BrE)
A hardy breed of cattle created by crossing domestic cattle with the American buffalo, valued for its leaner beef.

Examples

A beefalo breed
A beefalo typically combines the hardiness of buffalo with the manageability of cattle.
Many consumers seek out beefalo as a healthier alternative to traditional beef.
The ranch specializes in raising beefalo for their flavorful, lean meat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buffalo - large wild bovine, specifically the American bison
  • synonym: cattle - large domesticated bovines, typically raised for beef or milk
