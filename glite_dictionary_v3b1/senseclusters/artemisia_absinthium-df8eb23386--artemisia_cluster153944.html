Artemisia absinthium

noun
aromatic herb used in absinthe
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑːr.təˈmɪʒ.i.ə æbˈsɪn.θi.əm / (AmE), / ˌɑː.tɪˈmɪz.i.ə æbˈsɪn.θi.əm / (BrE)
an aromatic perennial herb native to Eurasia and North Africa, with strongly bitter leaves, used in making the liqueur absinthe and in herbal medicine
