paulownia

noun
fast-growing tree
Pronunciation: / pɔːˈloʊniə / (AmE), / pɔːˈləʊniə / (BrE)
a fast-growing tree with large leaves and pale purple flowers, valued for its lightweight wood

Examples

Paulownia tree blossoms
The paulownia is often planted for its beautiful spring blossoms.
Carpenters appreciate paulownia wood because it is both strong and lightweight.
A row of paulownia trees lined the edge of the park, providing ample shade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: empress tree - a common name for paulownia, especially Paulownia tomentosa
