cephalalgia

noun
medical term for headache
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛfəˈlældʒiə / (AmE), / ˌsɛfəˈlældʒiə / (BrE)
pain in the head; headache, especially as a result of vascular dilation, muscle contractions, or reactions to drugs

Examples

Some medications may cause cephalalgia as an adverse side effect.
The patient reported persistent cephalalgia following her migraine episode.
