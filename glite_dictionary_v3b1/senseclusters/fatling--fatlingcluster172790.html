fatling

noun
plump child (humorous)
Pronunciation: / ˈfæt.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfæt.lɪŋ / (BrE)
a small, plump person, especially a child, often used humorously or insultingly

Examples

A plump fatling
The aunt laughed and picked up the plump fatling as he reached for the balloon.
At the family picnic, everyone doted on the youngest fatling with extra slices of cake.
He teased his little brother by calling him a fatling after dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chubby - slightly fat in a pleasant or attractive way
  • antonym: thin - having little fat on the body
