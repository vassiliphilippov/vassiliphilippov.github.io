pterygoid

noun
palate bone in skull
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrɪˌɡɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɛrɪɡɔɪd / (BrE)
a bone in the palate of many vertebrates that forms part of the skull structure

Examples

A pterygoid bone
The fossil showed a fractured pterygoid that clarified the reptile's palate structure.
In some animals, the pterygoid helps support the roof of the mouth.
Researchers examined the pterygoid to understand the evolution of skull shapes in early vertebrates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maxilla - the upper jawbone in vertebrates
  • synonym: palatine - a bone in the roof of the mouth in vertebrates
