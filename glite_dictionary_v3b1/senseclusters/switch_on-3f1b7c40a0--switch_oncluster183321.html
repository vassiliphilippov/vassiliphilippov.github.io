Synonyms

  • turn on
    1.0 23/m
    turn on is the everyday verb for causing a device to begin working, while switch on often highlights pressing a button or switch.
  • switch
    1.0 9.26/m
    switch means moving a control to change a device's state, while switch on focuses on making the device start working.
  • start up
    3.0 1.68/m
    start up is used for engines or systems when they begin operating, while switch on highlights pressing a button or switch.
  • fire up
    3.5 1.68/m
    fire up is informal and energetic for starting engines or software, while switch on is more neutral and fits many devices.
  • flip the switch
    3.5 0.36/m
    flip the switch means moving a switch quickly to turn something on, while switch on simply means making a device start working.
