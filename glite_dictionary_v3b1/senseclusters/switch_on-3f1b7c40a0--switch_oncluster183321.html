switch on

verb
activate a device by switching
Pronunciation: / swɪtʃ ɑn / (AmE), / swɪtʃ ɒn / (BrE)
to make a device or system start working by pressing a button or moving a switch

Examples

To switch on the heater
Could you switch on the heater? It's getting cold in here.
He switched on the computer and waited for it to start up.
She always switches on the lights as soon as she enters the room.
Could you switch on the lights? It's getting dark in here.
He forgot to switch on his phone after the meeting.
She always switches on her computer as soon as she gets to work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: turn on - to cause to operate by flipping a switch
