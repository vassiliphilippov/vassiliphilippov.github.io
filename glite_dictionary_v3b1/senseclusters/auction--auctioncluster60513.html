auction

noun
public sale to highest bidder
2.8 20/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…îk. É…ôn / (AmE), / Àà…îÀêk. É…ôn / (BrE)
a public sale in which goods or property are sold to the person who offers the highest price

Examples

A house auction
He bid on several items during the auction but didn't win any.
The painting was sold at an auction for a record price.
They decided to hold an auction to raise money for charity.

Synonyms

  • auction sale
    3.2 0.06/m
    auction sale is a formal two-word phrase often used in notices or adverts, while auction is the common single-word term.
  • vendue
    9.9 0.02/m
    vendue is an old-fashioned term once used for public sales, while auction is the common modern word used today.

Related Terms

  • antonym: purchase - the act of buying something
  • synonym: sale - the exchange of goods for money
