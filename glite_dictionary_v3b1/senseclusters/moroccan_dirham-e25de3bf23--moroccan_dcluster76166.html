Moroccan dirham

noun
currency of Morocco
Pronunciation: / məˈrɑːkən ˈdɪrˌhæm / (AmE), / məˈrɒkən ˈdɪəˌhæm / (BrE)
the official currency of Morocco, subdivided into 100 centimes

Examples

The Moroccan dirham currency
I exchanged my euros for Moroccan dirhams at the airport.
The price of the carpet was listed as 2,000 Moroccan dirhams.
Tourists found it easy to withdraw Moroccan dirhams from local ATMs.
