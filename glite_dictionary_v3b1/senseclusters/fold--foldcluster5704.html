fold

noun
group of church members or congregation
Pronunciation: / foʊld / (AmE), / fəʊld / (BrE)
a group of people who share a common faith, especially those who regularly attend a particular church; the members of a congregation

Examples

Back into the fold
After many years away, she was welcomed back into the fold by her old church friends.
Several families left the fold after disagreements over church policy.
The pastor encouraged new believers to join the fold and find spiritual support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: congregation - the people who regularly attend religious services in a church
