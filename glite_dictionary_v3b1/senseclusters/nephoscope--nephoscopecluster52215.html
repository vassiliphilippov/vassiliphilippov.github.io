nephoscope

noun
cloud movement measuring instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛfəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈnɛfəskəʊp / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the altitude, direction, and velocity of cloud movement, usually employing a grid

Examples

A nephoscope instrument
A nephoscope can provide valuable data for weather prediction and cloud studies.
Students learned how to use a nephoscope during their atmospheric science fieldwork.
The meteorologist used a nephoscope to determine the speed and direction of the passing clouds.
