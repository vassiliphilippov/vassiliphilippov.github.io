proestrus

noun
pre-estrus stage
9.9
Pronunciation: / proʊˈɛstrəs / (AmE), / prəʊˈɛstrəs / (BrE)
the stage in the reproductive cycle of female mammals just before estrus, when hormonal changes prepare for ovulation

Examples

The proestrus phase
The veterinarian explained that the dog's proestrus phase would last about nine days before she became fertile.
During proestrus, female dogs may show swelling and a bloody discharge as their bodies prepare for ovulation.
Careful observation of proestrus signs helps breeders determine the optimal time for mating.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: anestrus - the period of reproductive inactivity in female mammals
  • degree higher: estrus - the period of sexual receptivity and fertility in female mammals
