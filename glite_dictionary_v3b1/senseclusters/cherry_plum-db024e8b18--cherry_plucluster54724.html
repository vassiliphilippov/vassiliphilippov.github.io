cherry plum

adjective
deep red–purple color
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛri ˌplʌm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri ˌplʌm / (BrE)
Describing a deep red–purple color, like the skin of a cherry plum; used for paint, fabric, cosmetics, and other materials.

Examples

Cherry plum color
She chose a cherry plum sofa to add a rich, elegant touch to the living room.
The designer suggested cherry plum curtains to complement the gold detailing in the hotel lobby.
For autumn, he prefers cherry plum nail polish because it looks sophisticated with warm coats.
