sibling

noun
a brother or sister
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪblɪŋ / (BrE)
a brother or sister; one of two or more children having one or both parents in common

Examples

A sibling relationship
Having a sibling can help children learn to share and cooperate.
My siblings and I grew up in the same town.
She is the youngest sibling in her family.
