nitrohydrochloric acid

noun
mixture of nitric and hydrochloric acids
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnaɪtroʊˌhaɪdrəkləˈrɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / ˌnɪtrəʊˌhaɪdrəkləˈrɪk ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
a yellow, fuming, highly corrosive mixture of nitric and hydrochloric acids, used especially for dissolving metals such as gold and platinum
