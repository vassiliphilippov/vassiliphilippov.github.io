boule

noun
round French loaf
Pronunciation: / bul / (AmE), / buːl / (BrE)
a round loaf of bread, typically French in style, with a thick crust and soft interior

Examples

A crusty boule
She bought two crusty boules from the bakery to serve with soup.
The baker shaped the dough into a perfect boule before baking.
A traditional French boule has a thick, golden crust and a chewy crumb.

Related Terms

  • antonym: baguette - a long, thin French loaf of bread
  • synonym: batard - an oval-shaped French loaf of bread
