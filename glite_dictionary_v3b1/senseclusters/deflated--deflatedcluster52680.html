deflated

adjective
lacking confidence or hope
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfleɪtɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈfleɪtɪd / (BrE)
feeling less confident or less hopeful; brought low in spirit

Examples

Feeling deflated after loss
He looked visibly deflated when he heard the disappointing news.
She felt deflated after her idea was rejected at the meeting.
The team's morale was deflated by their unexpected loss.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confident - feeling or showing assurance; opposite of lacking confidence
  • synonym: discouraged - having lost confidence or enthusiasm
