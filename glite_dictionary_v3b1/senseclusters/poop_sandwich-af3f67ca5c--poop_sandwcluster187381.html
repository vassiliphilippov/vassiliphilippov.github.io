poop sandwich

noun
an unpleasant situation (humorous, slang)
Pronunciation: / pup ˈsænwɪtʃ / (AmE), / puːp ˈsænwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a humorous and informal term for a very unpleasant or undesirable situation or experience

Examples

A poop sandwich situation
He described dealing with the paperwork as a poop sandwich he couldn't avoid.
Sometimes life hands you a poop sandwich and you just have to get through it.
The morning shift at the restaurant turned into a total poop sandwich when three employees called in sick.
