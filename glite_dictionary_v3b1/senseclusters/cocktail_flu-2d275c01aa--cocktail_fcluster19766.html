cocktail flu

noun
slang for hangover from mixed drinks
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑkˌteɪl flu / (AmE), / ˈkɒkˌteɪl fluː / (BrE)
A humorous term for hangover symptoms or mild illness experienced after excessive consumption of alcoholic mixed drinks.

Examples

Recovering from cocktail flu
After the wedding, he spent all Sunday recovering from cocktail flu.
Many people experience cocktail flu after a night of heavy partying.
She texted her coworkers that she was staying home with a case of cocktail flu.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hangover - the unpleasant physical effects following heavy drinking
