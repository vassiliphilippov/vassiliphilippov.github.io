swab

noun
absorbent cleaning implement with a handle
7.6
Pronunciation: / swɑb / (AmE), / swɒb / (BrE)
a cleaning implement consisting of absorbent material attached to a handle, used for cleaning floors or other surfaces

Examples

A floor swab
After mopping, she rinsed the swab thoroughly and left it to dry.
He dipped the swab in the bucket before wiping the kitchen floor.
The janitor used a large swab to clean up the spill in the hallway.
