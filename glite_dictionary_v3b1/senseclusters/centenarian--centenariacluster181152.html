centenarian

noun
person aged 100 or over
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛn.təˈnɛr.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌsɛn.tɪˈneə.ri.ən / (BrE)
a person who is at least 100 years old

Research shows that many centenarians live active and fulfilling lives.
