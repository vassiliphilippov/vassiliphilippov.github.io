vote with one's wallet

verb
express preference by spending money
4.9
Pronunciation: / voʊt wɪð wʌnz ˈwɑːlɪt / (AmE), / vəʊt wɪð wʌnz ˈwɒlɪt / (BrE)
to express one's preferences or opinions by choosing where or how to spend money

Examples

To vote with their wallets
After the company raised prices, customers voted with their wallets and stopped buying its goods.
Many environmentally conscious shoppers vote with their wallets by purchasing green products.
More and more people are voting with their wallets to support fair trade producers.
