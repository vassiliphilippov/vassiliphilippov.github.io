Russian cactus

noun
prickly Eurasian weed plant
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌʃən ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃən ˈkæktəs / (BrE)
A prickly, bushy plant native to Eurasia that has become a troublesome weed in parts of the central and western United States.

Examples

The Russian cactus plant
I learned how to identify Russian cactus during our botany field trip.
Many ranchers consider Russian cactus an invasive weed on their property.
The field was overrun with Russian cactus, making farming difficult.
