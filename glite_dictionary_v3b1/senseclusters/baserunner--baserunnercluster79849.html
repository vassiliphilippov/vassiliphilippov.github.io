baserunner

noun
player on base
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsˌrʌnər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsˌrʌnə / (BrE)
a player in baseball who is on base and attempting to advance or score a run

Examples

The baserunner steals base
The baserunner waited for the perfect moment to steal second base.
After hitting a single, she became the only baserunner in the inning.
The pitcher kept a close eye on the baserunner at first base.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fielder - a player who tries to get baserunners out
  • synonym: runner - a player advancing around the bases in baseball
