xeroma

noun
dryness of the eye's conjunctiva and cornea
9.9
Pronunciation: / ziˈroʊmə / (AmE), / zɪəˈrəʊmə / (BrE)
Abnormal dryness of the conjunctiva and cornea of the eye, often caused by vitamin A deficiency.
