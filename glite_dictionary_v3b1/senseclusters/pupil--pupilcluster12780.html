pupil

noun
school-aged child or student
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuːpəl / (AmE), / ˈpjuːpɪl / (BrE)
a student, especially a child, who goes to school

Examples

The teacher praised every pupil for their hard work during the semester.
