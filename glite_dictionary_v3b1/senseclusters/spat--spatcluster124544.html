spat

verb
have a brief, petty quarrel
9.7
Pronunciation: / spæt / (AmE), / spæt / (BrE)
To engage in a short and trivial argument or quarrel, especially over something unimportant.

Examples

To spat over chores
After spatting in the hallway, the friends quickly made up and laughed together.
The siblings spat over who would get the last cookie, but soon forgot about it.
They often spat about trivial matters, but it never lasted long.
