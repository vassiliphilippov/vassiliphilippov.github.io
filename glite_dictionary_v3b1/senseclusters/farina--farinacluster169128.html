farina

noun
fine meal from cereal grains, especially wheat
Pronunciation: / fəˈriːnə / (AmE), / fəˈriːnə / (BrE)
A fine meal or flour made from cereal grains, especially wheat, used as a cooked cereal or in puddings.

Examples

A bowl of farina
Many families enjoy a bowl of farina as a warm breakfast cereal on cold mornings.
She remembered her grandmother making farina with milk and sugar on chilly days.
The recipe calls for a cup of farina to add texture to the pudding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flour - fine powder made by grinding cereal grains
  • synonym: semolina - coarse, purified wheat middlings used in pasta and porridge
