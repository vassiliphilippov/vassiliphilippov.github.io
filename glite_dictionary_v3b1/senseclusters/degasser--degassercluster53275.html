degasser

noun
gas-removal device
7.4
Pronunciation: / diˈɡæsər / (AmE), / diˈɡɑːsə / (BrE)
a machine or device used to remove dissolved gases from liquids

Examples

A degasser machine
The laboratory uses a degasser to prepare samples for analysis.
Before bottling, the beverage passes through a degasser to remove unwanted gases.
A degasser is essential in water treatment plants to ensure water quality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: purifier - a device that removes unwanted substances from something
