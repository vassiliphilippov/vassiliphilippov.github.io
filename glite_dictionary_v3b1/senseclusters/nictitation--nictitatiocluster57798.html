nictitation

noun
the act of blinking (reflex)
Pronunciation: / nɪk.təˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / nɪk.tɪˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of rapidly closing and opening the eyelids; blinking, especially as a reflex.

Examples

Rapid nictitation
Excessive nictitation may indicate eye irritation or dryness in patients.
Some animals exhibit a third eyelid movement known as the nictitating membrane, which is a form of nictitation.
The frequency of nictitation can vary depending on one's alertness and environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blink - to close and open the eyes quickly
