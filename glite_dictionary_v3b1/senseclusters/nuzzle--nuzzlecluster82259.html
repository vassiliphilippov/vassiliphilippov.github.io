nuzzle

verb
rub or push with nose/face
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌzəl / (AmE), / ˈnʌzəl / (BrE)
to gently rub or push with the nose or face, often as a sign of affection

Examples

To nuzzle a cheek
He nuzzled his face into her hair, seeking comfort.
She gently nuzzled her baby's cheek, making him giggle.
The puppy likes to nuzzle against my arm when it’s sleepy.
