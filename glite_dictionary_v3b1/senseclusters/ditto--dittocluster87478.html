ditto

adverb
same as before
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɪtəʊ / (BrE)
used to show that the same thing mentioned before also applies in this case

Examples

Ditto in reply
She said she loved the concert, and I replied, 'ditto.'
If you are tired after the hike, ditto for me.
He ordered a coffee, and I said, 'ditto,' to get the same.

Related Terms

  • synonym: likewise - in the same way; also
  • synonym: same - identical; not different
