hog millet

noun
an annual grass grown for grain or forage
4.5 9.3
Pronunciation: / hɑɡ ˈmɪlət / (AmE), / hɒɡ ˈmɪlɪt / (BrE)
an annual grass (Setaria italica) cultivated in Europe and Asia for its grain, and in the United States sometimes grown for animal forage

Examples

Field of hog millet
Farmers planted acres of hog millet after the demand for animal feed increased.
In some regions, hog millet is fed to livestock during the winter months.
The grain harvested from hog millet can also be used in certain human foods.
