expressible

adjective
able to be expressed
2.7
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛsəbəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛsəbəl / (BrE)
able to be expressed in words or by other means

Examples

An expressible feeling
Her gratitude was barely expressible after the incredible kindness she received.
Some feelings are more easily expressible through art than through words.
The concept is clearly expressible in mathematical terms.
