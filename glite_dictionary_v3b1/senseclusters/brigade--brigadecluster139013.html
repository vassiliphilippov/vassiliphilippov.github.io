brigade

verb
organize into a brigade
2.7
Pronunciation: / brɪˈɡeɪd / (AmE), / brɪˈɡeɪd / (BrE)
to form people or things into a brigade; to unite or organize as a brigade

Examples

To brigade volunteers
During the emergency response, volunteers were quickly brigaded to tackle different tasks.
The commander decided to brigade the new recruits for more efficient training.
The event organizers brigaded the participants by region to improve coordination.
