Cali cartel

proper noun
Colombian drug trafficking organization
9.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkæ.liː kɑːr.ˈtel/ /; / /ˈkɑː.liː kɑːr.ˈtel/ (AmE), /ˈkæ.li kɑː.ˈtel/ /; / /ˈkɑː.li kɑː.ˈtel/ (BrE)
