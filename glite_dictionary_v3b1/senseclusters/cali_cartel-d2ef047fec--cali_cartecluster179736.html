Cali cartel

proper noun
Colombian drug trafficking organization
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkæli kɑrˌtɛl / (AmE), / ˈkæli kɑːˌtɛl / (BrE)
a powerful Colombian drug trafficking organization that controlled much of the world's cocaine trade in the 1980s and 1990s, noted for its sophisticated operations and influence over politicians

Examples

The Cali cartel organization
Many law enforcement agencies collaborated internationally to dismantle the Cali cartel.
The Cali cartel rose to power after the decline of the Medellín cartel in the early 1990s.
The leaders of the Cali cartel were eventually arrested and brought to justice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drug cartel - criminal organization involved in illegal drug trade
