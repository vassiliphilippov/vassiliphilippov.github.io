longshore

noun
dock worker
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋˌʃɔr / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌʃɔː / (BrE)
a dock worker who loads and unloads ships at a port; short for longshoreman

Examples

A longshore worker
The experienced longshore directed the crew as they unloaded the cargo ship.
After years as a longshore, he knew every part of the busy harbor.
A group of longshore workers gathered at the dock before sunrise to begin their shift.

Related Terms

  • synonym: longshoreman - a person employed at a dock to load and unload ships
  • synonym: stevedore - a worker who loads and unloads cargo from ships
