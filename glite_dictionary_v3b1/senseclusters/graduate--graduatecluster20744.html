graduate

noun
person who has received a degree or diploma
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrædʒuˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡrædʒuːət / (BrE)
a person who has successfully completed a course of study at a school, college, or university and received a degree or diploma

Examples

A college graduate
After four years of hard work, she is now a proud graduate of Harvard University.
As a recent graduate, he is seeking his first full-time job in engineering.
Every graduate received a diploma during the commencement ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alumnus - a male former student or graduate of a particular school, college, or university
  • antonym: student - a person who is studying at a school or college
