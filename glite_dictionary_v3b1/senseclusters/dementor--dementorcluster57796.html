dementor

noun
Harry Potter creature
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɛntər / (AmE), / dɪˈmɛntə / (BrE)
a fictional dark creature from the Harry Potter series that drains happiness and causes despair in humans

Examples

A dementor attacks
A dementor hovered above the lake, making everyone nearby feel cold and hopeless.
The presence of a dementor on the train terrified the students as the lights flickered.
Harry learned to defend himself against a dementor using the Patronus Charm.
