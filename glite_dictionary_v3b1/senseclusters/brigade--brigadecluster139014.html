brigade

verb
organise coordinated online action
7.9
Pronunciation: /brə.ˈgeɪd/ /; / /brɪ.ˈgeɪd/ (AmE), /brɪ.ˈgeɪd/ (BrE)
to organise or mobilize people online to act together—by posting, commenting, voting, or sharing—in order to support or attack a person, idea, or post.

Examples

To brigade comments
Supporters brigaded the comments to defend the creator after the false accusation.
They warned users not to brigade other communities because it breaks site rules.
During the campaign, volunteers brigaded several threads to draw attention to the cause.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coordinate - to arrange or harmonize actions among people to achieve an effect
  • synonym: mobilize - to organise or prepare a group to take collective action
