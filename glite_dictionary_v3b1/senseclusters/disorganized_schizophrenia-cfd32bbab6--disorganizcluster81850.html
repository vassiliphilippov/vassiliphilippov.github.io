disorganized schizophrenia

noun
type of schizophrenia with erratic behavior
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsˌɔːrɡəˈnaɪzd skɪtˌsoʊfrəˈniːə / (AmE), / ˌdɪsˌɔːɡəˈnaɪzd skɪtˌsəʊfrəˈniːə / (BrE)
a form of schizophrenia marked by severe personality disintegration, disorganized speech, childish mannerisms, and bizarre behavior, typically beginning in adolescence

Examples

Disorganized schizophrenia symptoms
He was admitted for treatment after presenting clear signs of disorganized schizophrenia in his late teens.
Patients diagnosed with disorganized schizophrenia often exhibit fragmented thoughts and unpredictable behavior.
The symptoms of disorganized schizophrenia can significantly disrupt daily functioning and social interactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catatonic schizophrenia - a subtype of schizophrenia with disturbances in movement and behavior
  • degree lower: schizophrenia - a severe mental disorder characterized by distorted thinking, perceptions, and emotions
