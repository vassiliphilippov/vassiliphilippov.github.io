wye

noun
Y-shaped electrical circuit
Pronunciation: / waɪ / (AmE), / waɪ / (BrE)
a three-phase electrical connection where three components are joined at a single point, forming a Y-shaped circuit

Examples

Wye electrical connection
The engineer explained how a wye connection distributes power evenly in a three-phase system.
Many industrial machines are wired using a wye configuration for safety and efficiency.
Switching from a delta to a wye connection can help reduce electrical faults.

Related Terms

  • antonym: delta - a three-phase electrical connection forming a triangle-shaped circuit
