sciatic

adjective
relating to the hipbone or sciatic nerve
Pronunciation: / saɪˈætɪk / (AmE), / saɪˈatɪk / (BrE)
relating to the sciatic nerve, the hip area, or sciatica (pain or condition involving the sciatic nerve)

Examples

Sciatic nerve pain
Doctors examined the imaging to check for sciatic nerve compression.
She suffered from intense sciatic pain after the injury.
The sciatic nerve is the longest and widest single nerve in the human body.
He was suffering from a sciatic nerve injury that made it painful to walk.
Physical therapy helped alleviate his sciatic discomfort after several weeks of treatment.
The doctor diagnosed her symptoms as sciatic pain originating from her lower back.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ischial - relating to the ischium (the lower, rear part of the hip bone)
  • synonym: sciatica - pain affecting the back, hip, and outer side of the leg, caused by compression of a spinal nerve root
