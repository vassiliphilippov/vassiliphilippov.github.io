exculpate

verb
declare someone not guilty
7.9 8.3
Pronunciation: /ek.ˈskʌl.peɪt/ /; / /ek.ˈskəl.peɪt/ (AmE), /ek.ˈskʌl.peɪt/ /; / /ek.ˈskəl.peɪt/ (BrE)
