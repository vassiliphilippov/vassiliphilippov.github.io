Synonyms

  • exonerate
    8.2 4/m
    exonerate clears someone officially of blame after review or new evidence, while exculpate declares a person not guilty of charges.
  • acquit
    7.4 0.9/m
    acquit is a court verdict that finds someone legally not guilty, while exculpate can also mean clearing from blame outside court.
  • discharge
    7.2 0.2/m
    discharge formally removes criminal charges or duty from a person, while exculpate focuses on declaring them not guilty.
  • absolve
    7.2 2/m
    absolve frees someone from moral blame or guilt, often in religious tone, while exculpate is a legal declaration of not guilty.
