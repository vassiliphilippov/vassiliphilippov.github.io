misdescribe

verb
to describe incorrectly
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsdɪˈskraɪb / (AmE), / ˌmɪsdɪˈskraɪb / (BrE)
to describe something incorrectly or inaccurately

Examples

To misdescribe events
The article misdescribes the events that took place during the meeting.
If you misdescribe the product, customers may be disappointed when it arrives.
He realized he had misdescribed the location and quickly corrected himself.

Related Terms

  • antonym: describe - to give an account of something in words
  • synonym: misrepresent - to give a false or misleading account of something
