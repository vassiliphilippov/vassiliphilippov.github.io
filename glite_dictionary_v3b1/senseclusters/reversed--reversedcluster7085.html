reversed

adjective
turned opposite in order or direction
2.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɜrst / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːst / (BrE)
turned the opposite way or in the opposite order to what is normal or expected

Examples

Reversed order
Reading a reversed sequence can change the meaning entirely.
The engineer discovered that the wires had been reversed during installation.
The magician showed a card with the image reversed to confuse the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inverted - turned upside down or inside out
  • antonym: normal - not changed from the usual order or direction
