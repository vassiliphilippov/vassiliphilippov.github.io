chromogen

noun
compound convertible to a pigment
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkroʊ·məˌdʒɛn / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊ·məˌdʒɛn / (BrE)
a compound that can be converted into a pigment, often during a chemical reaction or biological process.

Examples

A chromogen compound
