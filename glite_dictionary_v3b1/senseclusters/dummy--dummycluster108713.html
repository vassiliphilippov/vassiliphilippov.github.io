dummy

adjective
appearing real but non-functional
5.1 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈdʌ.miː/ /; / /ˈdʌ.mɪ/ (AmE), /ˈdʌ.miː/ /; / /ˈdʌ.mɪ/ (BrE)
having the appearance of being real or genuine but lacking the ability or capacity to function; not real.

Examples

Dummy camera
He replaced the real camera with a dummy one to deter thieves.
The company set up a dummy corporation to hide its financial activities.
The exhibit included a dummy missile that could not actually be fired.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fake - not genuine; imitation or counterfeit
  • antonym: genuine - actually being what it is claimed to be; authentic
