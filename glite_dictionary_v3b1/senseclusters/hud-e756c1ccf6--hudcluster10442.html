HUD

noun
heads-up display
7.6 0.4/m
Pronunciation: / e…™t É juÀê ÀàdiÀê / (AmE), / e…™t É juÀê ÀàdiÀê / (BrE)
a transparent or projected display that shows information (such as speed, navigation, or status) in a user's field of view, often used in aircraft, vehicles, and video games.

Examples

Car HUD display
The car's HUD projected speed and navigation instructions onto the windshield at night.
During the training flight the pilot relied on the HUD to keep altitude and course stable.
Gamers customize the game's HUD to show health, map, and mission objectives on screen.
