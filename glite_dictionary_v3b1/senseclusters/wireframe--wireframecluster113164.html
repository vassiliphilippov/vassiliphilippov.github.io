wireframe

noun
wire structure frame
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪərˌfreɪm / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəˌfreɪm / (BrE)
a physical frame or supporting structure made of wire, used as the basic shape for objects like lampshades, models, or cages

Examples

A wireframe sculpture
The artist shaped a wireframe before covering it with paper to make a sculpture.
A sturdy wireframe is essential for building a durable lampshade.
They constructed a model airplane using a lightweight wireframe as the base.

Related Terms

  • synonym: framework - a basic supporting structure of something
  • synonym: skeleton - the supporting structure or framework of an object
