psychographic

noun
marketing profile type
8.8
Pronunciation: /saɪ.kə.ˈgræf.ɪk/ /; / /saɪ.koʊ.ˈgræf.ɪk/ (AmE), /saɪ.kə.ˈgræf.ɪk/ /; / /saɪ.kəʊ.ˈgræf.ɪk/ (BrE)
a profile or classification of people based on psychological characteristics such as attitudes, values, interests, or lifestyle, often used in marketing

Examples

A psychographic report
The marketing team created a psychographic to better understand their target audience's interests and values.
Advertisers use psychographics to tailor messages that appeal to specific personality types.
A detailed psychographic can reveal what motivates customers to choose certain brands.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demographic - a profile or classification of people based on statistical data such as age, gender, or income
  • synonym: lifestyle segmentation - dividing people into groups based on their way of living
