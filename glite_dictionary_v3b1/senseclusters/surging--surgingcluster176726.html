surging

adjective
rising or moving powerfully
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
characterized by powerful, swelling movement or waves; moving or rising with strong, forceful motion

Examples

The surging waves
Her voice rose above the surging emotions in the tense room.
The surging crowd pushed toward the stage as the band began to play.
We watched the surging waves crash against the rocky shoreline during the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: billowing - characterized by swelling out or up, like waves or clouds
