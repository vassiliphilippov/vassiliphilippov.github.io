photomural

noun
large photographic mural
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊtoʊˌmjʊrəl / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊˌmjʊərəl / (BrE)
a very large photograph or photographic image used to cover a wall or large surface as decoration
