hypochondriacal

adjective
excessively worried about illness
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpəˌkɑnˈdraɪəkəl / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəˌkɒndrɪˈakəl / (BrE)
Suffering from or relating to hypochondria; excessively worried about having a serious illness.

Examples

Hypochondriacal worries
His hypochondriacal tendencies made him anxious about even the smallest symptoms.
She became increasingly hypochondriacal, visiting the doctor for every minor ache and pain.
The novel’s protagonist is portrayed as both witty and hypochondriacal throughout the story.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypochondriac - a person affected by or exhibiting hypochondria
