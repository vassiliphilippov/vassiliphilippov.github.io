milometer

noun
meter showing miles traveled (vehicle)
8.1
Pronunciation: / maɪˈlɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / maɪˈlɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument in a vehicle that shows the distance it has traveled in miles

Examples

Car milometer reading
After their road trip, they checked the milometer to see how far they'd gone.
Please note down the reading from the milometer before returning the vehicle.
The old car’s milometer showed it had traveled over 100,000 miles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: odometer - an instrument for measuring the total distance traveled by a vehicle (miles/kilometers)
