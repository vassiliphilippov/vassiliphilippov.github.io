Synonyms

  • unpaid
    1.8 2/m
    unpaid describes money that has not been paid, while outstanding refers to sums that are still owed and waiting payment.
  • owing
    2.4 0.00/m
    owing describes a specific debt that someone owes, while outstanding describes money still unpaid on an account.
  • due
    1.3 20/m
    due means money that must be paid now or is owed by a set date, while outstanding means still owed and unpaid.
  • undischarged
    8.1 0.01/m
