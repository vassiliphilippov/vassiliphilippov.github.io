superheavy

adjective
above heavyweight class
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈhɛvi / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈhɛvi / (BrE)
relating to a weight class above heavyweight, especially in combat sports like boxing or wrestling.

Examples

Superheavy category
He competed in the superheavy category at the national wrestling championship.
The new rules introduced a superheavy division for athletes over 110 kilograms.
Only a few boxers qualify for the superheavy weight class due to its strict requirements.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: heavyweight - a weight class below superheavy in combat sports
