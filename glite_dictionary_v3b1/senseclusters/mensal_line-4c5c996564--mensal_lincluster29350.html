mensal line

noun
crease on palm indicating emotions (palmistry)
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛn.səl laɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.səl laɪn / (BrE)
A crease on the palm of the hand, thought in palmistry to indicate one's emotional nature.

Examples

The mensal line on palm
In palmistry, the mensal line is believed to reveal a person's sensitivity to emotion.
Some say a deeply curved mensal line suggests a highly passionate nature.
The fortune-teller carefully examined the mensal line on his hand before making her prediction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heart line - a line on the palm said to be associated with emotional matters in palmistry
