Natrix sipedon

noun
species of North American water snake
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪ.trɪks saɪˈpiː.dɑn / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.trɪks sɪˈpiː.dɒn / (BrE)
A species of nonvenomous water snake native to North America, often found near water; also called the northern water snake.
