to order someone to appear, especially in a court of law or in an official situation

Examples

To summons a witness
He was formally summonsed to give evidence before the magistrate.
If you are summonsed to jury duty, you must attend.
The court summonsed all witnesses to appear next week.

Synonyms

  • summon
    5.3 9.88/m
    summon is the base verb used to order someone to appear in person, while summons is the present-tense form often used for he or she.
  • cite
    6.4 1.84/m
    cite is a legal term used to officially require someone to attend court, while summons is a general order to appear.
  • call
    2.0 81/m
    call is a general verb meaning to request someone to come, while summons is a formal legal order to appear.
