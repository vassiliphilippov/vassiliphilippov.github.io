summons

verb
order to appear, especially in court
1.5 4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌ.mənz/ (AmE), /ˈsʌ.mənz/ (BrE)
to order someone to appear, especially in a court of law or in an official situation

Examples

To summons a witness
He was formally summonsed to give evidence before the magistrate.
If you are summonsed to jury duty, you must attend.
The court summonsed all witnesses to appear next week.
