unsingable

adjective
not able to be sung
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɪŋəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪŋəbəl / (BrE)
impossible or very difficult to sing, especially because of musical complexity or awkwardness

Examples

An unsingable song
The composer wrote an unsingable aria that challenged even the most skilled vocalists.
Many found the national anthem's new arrangement nearly unsingable due to its high notes.
The choir struggled with the unsingable melody during rehearsal.
