uncareful

adjective
not careful
5.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɛr.fəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɛə.fəl / (BrE)
not careful; lacking attention, caution, or thought in actions or behavior

Examples

An uncareful mistake
An uncareful mistake in the report led to confusion among the team.
He was uncareful with the fragile vase and accidentally broke it.
Being uncareful while crossing the street can be very dangerous.

Related Terms

  • antonym: careful - giving attention to avoiding harm or errors
  • synonym: careless - not giving enough attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
  • synonym: reckless - showing a lack of concern for danger or consequences
