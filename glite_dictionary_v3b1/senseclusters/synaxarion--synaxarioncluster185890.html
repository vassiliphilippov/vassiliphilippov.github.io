synaxarion

noun
Eastern Christian liturgical book
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪnəkˈsɛəriən / (AmE), / ˌsɪnækˈsɑːrɪən / (BrE)
a liturgical book in Eastern Christianity that contains the lives of saints and information about feast days, or a short account of a saint's life read from it
