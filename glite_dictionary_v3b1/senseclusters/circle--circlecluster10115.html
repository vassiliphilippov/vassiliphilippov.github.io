circle

verb
draw or form a circle around
2.0 3.91/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…ùÀê.k…ôl / (AmE), / Ààs…úÀê.k…ôl / (BrE)
to draw or form a circle around something; to surround or encircle

Examples

To circle a word
He quickly circled the errors in red ink.
Please circle all the correct answers on the test.
She circled the spot on the map where they would meet.

Synonyms

  • encircle
    3.4 3.90/m
    encircle surrounds something completely in a circular way, while circle often means to draw or form a ring around it.
  • ring
    2.5 3.62/m
    ring forms a circle around something or encloses it tightly, while circle often means to draw or mark a round shape around it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encircle - to form a circle around; surround
  • synonym: surround - to be all around; enclose on all sides
