tremblingly

adverb
with visible shaking
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛmblɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtrɛmblɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows shaking, usually from fear, cold, or weakness

Examples

To tremblingly reach out
He reached out tremblingly to accept the award, overwhelmed by emotion.
The child answered tremblingly when the teacher called on her unexpectedly.
She held the letter tremblingly, afraid of what it might say.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shakily - in a way that is unsteady or trembling
  • antonym: steadily - in a way that is firm and does not shake
