docking

noun
securing a ship to a dock
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of securing a ship or boat to a dock by attaching it with ropes or other means

Examples

The docking of a ship
After docking, the crew quickly secured the vessel with thick ropes.
The docking of the cruise ship went smoothly despite the strong winds.
The harbor workers prepared for the docking of three large cargo ships this morning.
