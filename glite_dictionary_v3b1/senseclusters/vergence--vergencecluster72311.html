vergence

noun
eye movement for focus
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààv…úrd í…ôns / (AmE), / Ààv…úÀêd í…ôns / (BrE)
the movement of both eyes in opposite directions to maintain single binocular vision

Examples

Eye vergence
Proper vergence is necessary for reading without eye strain.
Children with poor vergence may have trouble focusing on close objects.
Eye doctors test vergence to diagnose certain vision problems.

Related Terms
