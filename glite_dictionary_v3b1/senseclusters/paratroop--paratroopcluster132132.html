paratroop

noun
parachuting military unit
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpær.ə.truːp/ (AmE), /ˈpær.ə.truːp/ (BrE)
a soldier or group of soldiers trained to parachute into combat, or a military operation involving such soldiers
