armband

noun
band worn around the upper arm
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrmˌbænd /; / ˈɑːrmˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈɑːmˌbænd /; / ˈɑːmbænd / (BrE)
a band of cloth or metal worn around the upper arm, often as a symbol of rank, mourning, identification, or affiliation

Examples
