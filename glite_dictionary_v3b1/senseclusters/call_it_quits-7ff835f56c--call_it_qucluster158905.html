call it quits

verb
stop an activity; quit
4.9 3.2
Pronunciation: /kɔːl ɪt kwɪts/ /; / /kɑːl ɪt kwɪts/ (AmE), /kɔːl ɪt kwɪts/ /; / /kɔːl ət kwɪts/ (BrE)
to stop doing something, especially because you feel you have done enough or cannot continue
