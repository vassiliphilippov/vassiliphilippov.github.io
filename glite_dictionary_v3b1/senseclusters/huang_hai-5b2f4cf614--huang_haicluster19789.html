Huang Hai

proper noun
the Yellow Sea; sea between China and Korea
Pronunciation: / ˈhwæŋ ˈhaɪ / (AmE), / ˈhwæŋ ˈhaɪ / (BrE)
the Yellow Sea, a shallow part of the Pacific Ocean off the east coast of Asia, between China and Korea
