Examples

Explain parsimoniously
The theory was developed parsimoniously, relying on only a few basic principles.
She decorated her apartment parsimoniously, choosing only essential furniture and simple designs.
The report was written parsimoniously, avoiding unnecessary details and focusing on the main findings.
