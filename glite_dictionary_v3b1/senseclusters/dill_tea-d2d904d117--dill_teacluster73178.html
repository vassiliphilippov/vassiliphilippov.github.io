dill tea

noun
herbal infusion of dill
Pronunciation: / dɪl ti / (AmE), / dɪl tiː / (BrE)
a beverage made by infusing dill leaves or seeds in hot water, often consumed for its flavor and potential health benefits

Examples

A cup of dill tea
Many people drink dill tea to aid digestion after a heavy meal.
She served a calming cup of dill tea at the garden party.
Some herbalists recommend dill tea for its mild soothing effect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herbal tea - an infusion of leaves, seeds, or roots of plants other than tea
