striped marlin

noun
Pacific fish with blue stripes
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪpt ˈmɑrlɪn / (AmE), / ˈstraɪpt ˈmɑːlɪn / (BrE)
a large Pacific game and food fish (Kajikia audax) with a pointed snout and distinctive dark blue vertical stripes along its body

Examples

The striped marlin fish
Commercial fisheries often target the striped marlin for its valuable meat.
The striped marlin is a prized catch among sport fishermen in the Pacific Ocean.
We saw a massive striped marlin leap out of the water during our boat trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: black marlin - a closely related marlin species of the Indo-Pacific region
  • synonym: blue marlin - a related large marlin species of the Atlantic and Pacific oceans
