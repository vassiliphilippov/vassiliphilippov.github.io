incontrovertibleness

noun
quality of being indisputable
4.0 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.kɑn.trəˈvɝː.tə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˌɪn.kɒn.trəˈvɜː.tə.bəl.nəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being unquestionable or impossible to dispute

Examples

The incontrovertibleness of facts
Historians often debate the incontrovertibleness of certain so-called facts.
She spoke with such conviction and incontrovertibleness that no one dared challenge her conclusions.
The incontrovertibleness of the evidence made acquittal impossible for the defendant.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disputableness - the quality of being open to dispute or argument
  • synonym: incontrovertibility - the quality of being incontrovertible, used more commonly than 'incontrovertibleness'
