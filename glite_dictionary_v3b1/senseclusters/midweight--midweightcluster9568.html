midweight

noun
intermediate weight class
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌweɪt / (BrE)
a weight division in combat sports for competitors whose weight is between lightweight and heavyweight, or a competitor in that division

Examples

Midweight champion
She won the midweight championship after a close and exciting match.
Many athletes move up from lightweight to midweight as they gain muscle.
The midweight division often features a mix of speed and power in its fighters.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: heavyweight - a weight class above midweight in combat sports
  • degree lower: lightweight - a weight class below midweight in combat sports
