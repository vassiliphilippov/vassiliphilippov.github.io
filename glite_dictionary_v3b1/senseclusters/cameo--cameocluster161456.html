cameo

noun
brief celebrity appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmioʊ / (AmE), / ˈkæmiəʊ / (BrE)
a brief appearance or role by a well-known person in a film, TV show, or other performance

Examples

A cameo appearance
The famous singer made a surprise cameo in the comedy movie.
Fans cheered when the author had a brief cameo in the television adaptation of her book.
The actor's cameo lasted only a minute, but it was a highlight of the film.
The famous actor made a surprise cameo in the comedy, delighting the audience.
Fans were excited to spot the singer's cameo during the movie's opening scene.
The director often includes a personal cameo in each of his films.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guest appearance - a short role by a guest actor in a show or film
  • antonym: lead role - the main part in a film or show
