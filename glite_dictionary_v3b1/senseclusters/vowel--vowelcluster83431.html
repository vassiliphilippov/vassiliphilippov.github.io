noun

A letter of the alphabet that represents a vowel sound, such as 'a', 'e', 'i', 'o', or 'u'.

Examples

Vowel letter
English has five main vowel letters: a, e, i, o, and u.
The word 'apple' begins with a vowel in both spelling and pronunciation.
When writing a plural, add 'es' if the noun ends in a vowel followed by 'y'.
