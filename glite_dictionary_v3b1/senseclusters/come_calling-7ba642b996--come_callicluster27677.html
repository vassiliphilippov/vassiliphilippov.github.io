come calling

verb
arrive or occur unexpectedly
Pronunciation: / kʌm ˈkɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / kʌm ˈkɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
to arrive or appear unexpectedly, often bringing consequences or seeking attention

Examples

Success comes calling to those who persist through adversity.
When opportunity came calling, she did not hesitate to take a risk.
