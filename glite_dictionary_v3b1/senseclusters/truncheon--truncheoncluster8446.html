truncheon

noun
police officer's club
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌn.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈtrʌn.tʃən / (BrE)
a short, thick stick carried by police officers as a weapon

Examples

Police truncheons
During the protest, police raised their truncheons to form a barrier.
In many countries, the truncheon is a standard part of a police officer's uniform.
The officer gripped his truncheon tightly as he walked down the dark alley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baton - a stick carried as a weapon or symbol of authority
  • synonym: nightstick - a police officer's short stick used as a weapon
