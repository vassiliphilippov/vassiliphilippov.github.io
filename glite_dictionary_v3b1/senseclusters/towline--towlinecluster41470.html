towline

noun
rope used for towing
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈtəʊˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a rope or line used for towing a vehicle, ship, or other object

Examples

A strong towline
A strong towline is essential when assisting another vessel in rough seas.
Please check the towline for any signs of wear before towing the barge.
The ship attached a towline to the stranded boat to pull it to safety.
