scandium

noun
a soft, silvery-white metallic element
Pronunciation: / ˈskændiəm / (AmE), / ˈskandɪəm / (BrE)
a soft, silvery-white metallic element, symbol Sc, that is used in some alloys and occurs naturally in minerals such as thortveitite.
