unrecalled

adjective
not withdrawn
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈkɔld / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈkɔːld / (BrE)
not withdrawn, called back, or removed from a position, duty, or sale.

Examples

Unrecalled product
The faulty medication remained unrecalled despite reports of adverse effects.
Several products with safety issues were left unrecalled on store shelves.
The official continued in his role, his appointment still unrecalled by the committee.

Related Terms

  • antonym: recalled - called back or removed from sale or service
  • antonym: withdrawn - removed from use, sale, or position
