run

verb
be affected by; experience (risk, temperature, etc.)
5.2
Pronunciation: / rʌn / (AmE), / rʌn / (BrE)
to be affected by or experience (something, especially something negative, such as a risk, temperature, or debt)

Examples

To run a risk
Children who play outside in the rain may run a risk of catching a cold.
He began to run a fever after returning from his trip.
Investing in new businesses always means you run the possibility of loss.

Related Terms

  • antonym: avoid - to keep away from or prevent the occurrence of something
  • synonym: incur - to become subject to something unwelcome or unpleasant
