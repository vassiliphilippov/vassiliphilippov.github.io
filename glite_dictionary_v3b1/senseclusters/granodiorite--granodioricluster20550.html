granodiorite

noun
igneous rock type
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɡrænoʊˈdaɪəˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˌɡranəʊˈdʌɪəraɪt / (BrE)
a coarse-grained igneous rock similar to granite but with a higher proportion of plagioclase feldspar

Examples

A granodiorite rock
The ancient statue was carved from a block of granodiorite.
Geologists discovered a large deposit of granodiorite beneath the mountain range.
The museum displayed a sample of granodiorite next to other igneous rocks for comparison.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diorite - a coarse-grained igneous rock with a composition between granite and gabbro
  • synonym: granite - a common coarse-grained igneous rock composed mainly of quartz and feldspar
