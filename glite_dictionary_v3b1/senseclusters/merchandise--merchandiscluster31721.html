merchandise

noun
branded goods for promotion (e.g. films, bands)
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜrtʃənˌdaɪz /; / ˈmɜːrtʃənˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɜː.tʃən.daɪz /; / ˈmɜːtʃənˌdaɪz / (BrE)
