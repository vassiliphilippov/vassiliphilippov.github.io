Synonyms

    1.0 200/m

  • article of commerce
    4.2 0.03/m
    article of commerce is a formal term for an item offered for sale in trade, while merchandise is everyday goods sold or used for promotion.
  • ware
    3.7 5/m
    ware is a word for a type or group of manufactured goods, often in compounds, while merchandise means general goods offered for sale.
