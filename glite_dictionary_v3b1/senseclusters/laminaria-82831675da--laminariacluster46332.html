Laminaria

noun
genus of brown seaweeds (kelps)
Pronunciation: / ˌlæməˈnɛriə / (AmE), / ˌlæmɪˈneəriə /; / ˌlæmɪˈnɛəriə / (BrE)
a genus of large brown seaweeds (kelps) in the family Laminariaceae, found in cold marine waters
