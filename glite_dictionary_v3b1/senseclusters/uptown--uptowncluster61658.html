uptown

noun
residential area of a city away from downtown
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˈʌp.taʊn / (BrE)
The residential part of a city or town, typically located away from the central business district.

Examples

A quiet uptown neighborhood
Many people prefer living uptown rather than in the bustling city center.
Property values in uptown have risen significantly over the past decade.
They moved to uptown to enjoy a quieter environment.
