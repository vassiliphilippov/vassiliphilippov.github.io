crematory

noun
furnace for burning corpses to ashes
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛməˌtɔri /; / ˈkriːməˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈkriːmətri /; / ˈkriːməˌtəri / (BrE)
a place or furnace where dead bodies are cremated
