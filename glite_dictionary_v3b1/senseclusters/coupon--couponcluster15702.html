coupon

noun
detachable voucher or certificate for redemption
1.7 16/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkuÀêÀåp…ën / (AmE), / ÀàkuÀêp…ín / (BrE)
a detachable certificate, voucher, or ticket that can be exchanged for a financial benefit or discount, often as part of a bond, offer, or promotion

Examples

A discount coupon
Each bond includes a coupon that can be redeemed for interest at regular intervals.
She handed the cashier a coupon for five dollars off her groceries.
You can use this coupon to get a free drink with any meal.

Synonyms

  • voucher
    3.1 7.07/m
    voucher is a document often used as proof of payment or as prepaid credit, while coupon is usually a detachable promotional ticket for discounts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: voucher - a small printed piece of paper that entitles the holder to a discount or exchange for goods or services
