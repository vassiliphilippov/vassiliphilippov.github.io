lowercase

verb
convert text to lower-case
3.8 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8lo\u028a\u0259r\u02ccke\u026as / (AmE), / \u02c8l\u0259\u028a\u0259ke\u026as / (BrE)
(computing) to convert letters in a piece of text so that they are all in lowercase (small) form.

Examples

To lowercase text
The program will lowercase all usernames before storing them to ensure case-insensitive login.
Before comparing the emails, she lowercased every subject line to avoid mismatches.
To clean the dataset, the script will read each field and lowercase any mixed-case entries automatically.

Synonyms

  • uncase
    7.6 0.00/m
    uncase is a computing verb that changes only uppercase letters to small form, while lowercase is the general conversion to small letters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: downcase - to convert text to lower-case letters (term used in some programming languages).
  • antonym: uppercase - to convert text to uppercase (capital) letters; opposite action to lowercase.
