scintilla of regret

noun
a tiny amount of regret
Pronunciation: / sɪnˈtɪlə əv rɪˈɡrɛt / (AmE), / sɪnˈtɪlə əv rɪˈɡrɛt / (BrE)
A very small or scarcely detectable amount of regret.

Examples

A scintilla of regret
Despite the consequences, she showed not a scintilla of regret for her actions.
During the interview, he denied feeling even a scintilla of regret about the decision.
There wasn't a scintilla of regret in her voice as she announced her departure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ounce of regret - a small amount of regret
  • synonym: trace of regret - a very slight sign of regret
