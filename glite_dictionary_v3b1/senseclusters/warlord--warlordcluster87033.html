warlord

noun
military leader ruling a region by force
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrˌlɔrd / (AmE), / ˈwɔː.lɔːd / (BrE)
a military leader who rules a region by force, especially when the central government is weak or ineffective, and is not accountable to any higher authority

Examples

A warlord controls region
After the collapse of the government, a local warlord took control of the area.
Several rival warlords fought over the territory for years.
The film depicted the rise of a ruthless warlord in a lawless land.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dictator - a ruler with total power over a country, typically one who has obtained control by force
  • antonym: peacemaker - a person who brings about peace, especially by reconciling adversaries
