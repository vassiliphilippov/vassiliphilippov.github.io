stamp

noun
machine for crushing materials
3.0
Pronunciation: / stæmp / (AmE), / stæmp / (BrE)
a machine with a heavy bar that moves up and down to pound or crush materials, especially ores

Examples

A stamp mill
Historic gold mines often used a stamp to break down rock for further processing.
The engineer inspected the old stamp to assess whether it could be restored.
The ore was fed into the stamp for initial crushing at the mine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crusher - a machine designed to break large rocks or ore into smaller pieces
