skywatcher

noun
astronomy observer
Pronunciation: / ˈskaɪˌwɑtʃər / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌwɒtʃə / (BrE)
a person who observes the sky, especially to watch astronomical events or phenomena

Examples

A skywatcher at night
Many skywatchers gathered in the park to view the rare lunar eclipse.
As a dedicated skywatcher, she tracks meteor showers and planetary alignments throughout the year.
The news reported that skywatchers could see the comet with the naked eye tonight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astronomer - a scientist who studies celestial bodies and the universe
  • synonym: stargazer - a person who observes stars or celestial events
