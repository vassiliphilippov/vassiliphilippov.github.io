Pediculus capitis

noun
human head louse species
6.4
Pronunciation: / pɛˈdɪkjʊləs ˈkæpɪtɪs / (AmE), / pɛˈdɪkjʊləs ˈkapɪtɪs / (BrE)
a species of louse that infests the human scalp, commonly known as the head louse

Examples

Pediculus capitis head louse
Pediculus capitis is most common among school-aged children due to close contact.
An infestation of Pediculus capitis can cause intense itching and discomfort in children.
Doctors use special shampoos to eliminate Pediculus capitis from the scalp.
