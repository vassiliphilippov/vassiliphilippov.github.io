agreeability

noun
quality of being pleasant or agreeable
3.8
Pronunciation: / əˌɡriːəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / əˌɡriːəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being pleasant, friendly, or easy to get along with; a tendency to agree with others

Examples

Friendly agreeability
He was admired for his agreeability and willingness to listen.
Her natural agreeability made her popular in every social group.
The team's success was partly due to the agreeability of its members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agreeableness - the quality of being agreeable, pleasant, or likable
  • antonym: disagreeability - the quality of being unpleasant or difficult to get along with
