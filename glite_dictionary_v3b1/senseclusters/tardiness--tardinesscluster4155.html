tardiness

noun
habit or quality of being late
4.1 0.63/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…ëÀêrdin…ôs / (AmE), / Ààt…ëÀêd…™n…ôs / (BrE)
The quality or habit of being late or not arriving at the expected or scheduled time.

Examples

Repeated tardiness
His repeated tardiness resulted in a warning from his supervisor.
She blamed traffic for her tardiness to the meeting.
The school has a strict policy regarding tardiness and absences.

Synonyms

  • lateness
    1.6 0.49/m
    lateness is the fact of arriving after the expected time, while tardiness describes a regular habit of being late.
  • belatedness
    6.1 0.06/m
    belatedness is a less common, formal word for being delayed, while tardiness means a habitual state of being late.

Related Terms

  • antonym: punctuality - The quality of being on time
