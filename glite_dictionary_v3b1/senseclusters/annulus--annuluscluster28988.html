annulus

noun
ring-shaped structure on a mushroom stem
8.2 8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.jʊ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈan.jʊ.ləs / (BrE)
A ring-shaped remnant of the partial veil that encircles the stem of a mature mushroom.

Examples

Mushroom annulus
After the cap expands, the annulus often remains as a ring on the stalk.
Some species can be identified by the presence or absence of an annulus.
The annulus is clearly visible around the stem of the mushroom.
