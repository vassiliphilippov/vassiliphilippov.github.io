distensible

adjective
capable of being stretched or expanded
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɛnsəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈstɛnsɪbəl / (BrE)
capable of being stretched or expanded by pressure; able to distend.

Examples

A distensible blood vessel
A vein is a distensible blood vessel that can accommodate varying blood volumes.
Some snakes have highly distensible jaws to swallow large prey.
The distensible walls of the bladder allow it to store urine until release.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expandable - able to increase in size or volume
  • antonym: inflexible - incapable of bending or stretching
