preflight

noun
aircraft safety check
4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpriː.flaɪt/ (AmE), /ˈpriː.flaɪt/ (BrE)
an inspection or set of checks performed before an aircraft takes off to ensure safety and readiness

Examples

A preflight inspection
The pilot completed the preflight before boarding the passengers.
A thorough preflight can help prevent technical issues during the journey.
Every crew member participates in the preflight to ensure all systems are working properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inspection - a careful examination or check
  • antonym: postflight - an inspection or check performed after a flight
