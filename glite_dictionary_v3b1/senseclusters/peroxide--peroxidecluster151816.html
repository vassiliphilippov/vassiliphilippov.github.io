peroxide

noun
powerful oxidizing liquid, bleaching agent
Pronunciation: / pəˈrɑːkˌsaɪd /; / pəˈrɑksaɪd / (AmE), / pəˈrɒk.saɪd /; / pəˈrɒksaɪd / (BrE)
a chemical substance (such as hydrogen peroxide) used for bleaching, disinfecting, or as an oxidizer
