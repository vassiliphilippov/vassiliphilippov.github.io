prog

noun
informal progressive supporter
Pronunciation: / prɑɡ / (AmE), / prɒɡ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who supports progressive political ideas or policies, sometimes used in a derogatory way

Examples

A young prog
Some people called the outspoken council member a prog because of her support for environmental reforms.
The debate became heated when a prog challenged the traditional views of the panel.
He was labeled a prog after advocating for universal healthcare in his community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservative - a person who favors traditional values and is cautious about change
  • synonym: progressive - a person who advocates for social reform or new ideas
