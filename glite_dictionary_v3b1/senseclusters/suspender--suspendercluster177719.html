suspender

noun
strap for holding up trousers
Pronunciation: / səˈspɛndɚ / (AmE), / səˈspɛndə / (BrE)
An elastic strap, usually one of a pair, worn over the shoulders to hold up trousers.

Examples

A pair of suspenders
He wore suspenders to keep his trousers from slipping down.
Her grandfather always dressed in a suit with suspenders and a bow tie.
Some people prefer belts, while others find suspenders more comfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braces - elastic straps worn over the shoulders to hold up trousers (British English)
