templet

noun
model or standard for comparison
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛm.plɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɛm.plɪt / (BrE)
A model or pattern used as a standard for making comparisons or copying objects.

Examples

A templet pattern
The architect used a templet to ensure each window was identical.
