rebase

verb
to move base
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbeɪs / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbeɪs / (BrE)
to move an operation, unit, or equipment to a different base or headquarters

Examples

To rebase a unit
The company decided to rebase its logistics center closer to the main shipping port.
After the merger, the military planned to rebase several units to the new headquarters.
The airline will rebase several aircraft at its new Mediterranean hub next month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relocate - to move to a new place or position
  • synonym: station - to assign to a specific place or position
