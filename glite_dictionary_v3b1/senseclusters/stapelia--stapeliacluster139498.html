stapelia

noun
a genus of succulent, foul-smelling plants
Pronunciation: / stəˈpiːliə / (AmE), / stəˈpiːlɪə / (BrE)
a genus of succulent, leafless plants with toothed stems and large, often star-shaped, foul-smelling flowers, native to southern Africa

Examples

The stapelia flower
Botanists are fascinated by the stapelia's scent, which resembles that of rotting meat to attract flies.
I bought a stapelia because I was intrigued by its strange appearance and reputation for foul-smelling blooms.
The stapelia in the greenhouse produced several unusual, star-shaped flowers this summer.
