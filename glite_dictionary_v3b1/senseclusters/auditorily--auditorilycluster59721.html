auditorily

adverb
by hearing
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːdəˈtɔːrəli / (AmE), / ˌɔːdɪˈtɒrɪli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to hearing or the sense of sound

Examples

To learn auditorily
The teacher gave the instructions auditorily so that students could practice their listening skills.
Some people learn better auditorily than through reading or writing.
The warning was delivered auditorily through a loudspeaker system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aurally - in a way that relates to hearing
  • antonym: visually - in a way that relates to seeing
