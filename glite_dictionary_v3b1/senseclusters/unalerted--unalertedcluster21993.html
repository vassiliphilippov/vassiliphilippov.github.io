unalerted

adjective
not attentive
5.8
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ə.ˈlɜːr.tɪd/ /; / /ə.nə.ˈlɜːr.tɪd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ə.ˈlɜː.tɪd/ /; / /ə.nə.ˈlɜː.tɪd/ (BrE)
not aware or not having been warned about something that requires attention

Examples

An unalerted guard
The security guard was completely unalerted to the suspicious activity outside the building.
Because she was unalerted to the announcement, she missed the important meeting.
The hikers remained unalerted to the approaching storm until it was too late.
The unalerted residents did not evacuate before the storm arrived.
Because the team was unalerted, they were caught off guard by the announcement.
An unalerted driver may not react quickly to sudden road hazards.
