italic

adjective
slanting style of type or letters
2.5 3.2
Pronunciation: /aɪ.ˈtæl.ɪk/ /; / /ɪ.ˈtæl.ɪk/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈtæl.ɪk/ /; / /aɪ.ˈtæl.ɪk/ (BrE)
(of type or lettering) having letters that slant to the right, used especially for emphasis or distinction.

Examples

Italic letters
All book titles in the list should be printed in italic type.
Her name appeared in italic letters for emphasis.
The software allows you to switch between italic and bold styles easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oblique - slanted typeface similar to italic but without the design features of true italic
  • antonym: roman - upright standard style of type as opposed to italic
