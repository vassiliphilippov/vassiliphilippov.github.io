physicalist

adjective
supporting physicalism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzɪkəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəlɪst / (BrE)
relating to or supporting the philosophical view that everything is physical or depends on physical processes

Examples

Physicalist theory
The author presents a physicalist explanation for consciousness in the final chapter.
Her physicalist perspective shapes her arguments about the nature of reality.
Many scientists adopt a physicalist approach when studying the mind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dualist - supporting the view that mind and body are separate
  • synonym: materialist - supporting the view that only physical matter exists
