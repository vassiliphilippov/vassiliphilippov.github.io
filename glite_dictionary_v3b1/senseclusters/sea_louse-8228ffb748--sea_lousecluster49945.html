sea louse

noun
marine isopod crustacean
Pronunciation: / siː ˌlaʊs / (AmE), / siː ˌlaʊs / (BrE)
a small marine isopod crustacean that parasitizes fish or feeds on organic debris in the ocean

Examples

A sea louse parasite
Researchers are developing new methods to control sea louse populations in aquaculture.
Salmon farming operations often struggle with infestations of sea louse that can harm the fish.
The presence of sea louse was detected on the gills of several marine fish species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: louse - a small wingless parasitic insect
