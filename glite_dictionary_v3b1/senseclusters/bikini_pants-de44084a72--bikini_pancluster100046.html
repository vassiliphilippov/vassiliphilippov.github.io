bikini pants

noun
small, tight-fitting women's underpants
Pronunciation: / bɪˈkini pænts / (AmE), / bɪˈkiːni pænts / (BrE)
A pair of women's underpants that are small, tight-fitting, and cut in a style similar to a bikini bottom.
