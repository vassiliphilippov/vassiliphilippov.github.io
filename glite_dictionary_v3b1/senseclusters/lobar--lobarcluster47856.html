lobar

adjective
relating to a lobe of an organ
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ.bɑr / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.bɑː / (BrE)
relating to or affecting a lobe, especially of an organ such as the brain, lungs, or liver.

Examples

lobar pneumonia
A CT scan revealed a lobar infarction in the brain.
The patient was diagnosed with lobar pneumonia, which affects a single lobe of the lung.
The study focused on lobar distribution of disease in the liver.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lobular - relating to a lobule
