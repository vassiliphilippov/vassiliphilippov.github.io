Synonyms

  • innovative
    5.5 20/m
    innovative creates or uses new ideas and methods in many areas, while groundbreaking stresses a major, first change that reshapes a field.
  • pathbreaking
    6.3 0.05/m
    pathbreaking brings new and important ideas that open a way forward, while groundbreaking focuses on bold first steps that change practice.
  • trailblazing
    5.6 0.1/m
    trailblazing starts new ways and shows others the route, while groundbreaking refers to very notable first work that changes a field.
  • revolutionary
    5.2 9/m
