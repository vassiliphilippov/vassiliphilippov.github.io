chon

noun
North Korean monetary subunit
Pronunciation: / tʃʌn / (AmE), / tʃɒn / (BrE)
A monetary unit of North Korea, equal to one hundredth of a won.

Examples

North Korean chon coin
Historically, the chon was used for small transactions in North Korea.
The souvenir cost 50 chons, a fraction of a North Korean won.
Tourists are rarely allowed to use the North Korean chon during their visits.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: won - the basic monetary unit of North Korea and South Korea
