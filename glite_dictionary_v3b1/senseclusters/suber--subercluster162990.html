suber

noun
cork oak tree
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːbər / (AmE), / ˈsuːbə / (BrE)
a Mediterranean evergreen oak tree (Quercus suber) whose thick bark is harvested to produce cork

Examples

Suber tree
The suber is valued for its bark, which is carefully stripped to make cork products.
Farmers in Portugal have cultivated suber for centuries to support the cork industry.
A single mature suber can provide cork every nine years without harming the tree.

Related Terms
