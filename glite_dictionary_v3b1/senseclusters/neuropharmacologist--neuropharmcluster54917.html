neuropharmacologist

noun
drug effects scientist
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˌfɑːrməˈkɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˌfɑːməkəˈlɒdʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies how drugs affect the nervous system

Examples

A neuropharmacologist studies drugs
The neuropharmacologist explained how the new medication interacts with brain cells.
After years of study, she became a leading neuropharmacologist in her field.
A neuropharmacologist may work with pharmaceutical companies to develop treatments for neurological diseases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neurologist - a doctor who specializes in the nervous system
  • synonym: pharmacologist - a scientist who studies the effects of drugs
