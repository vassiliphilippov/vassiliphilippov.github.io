thriftshop

noun
shop selling secondhand goods cheaply
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɪftˌʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈθrɪftʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop that sells secondhand goods, often to raise money for charity, at reduced prices.

Examples

A thriftshop sale
Many students furnish their apartments with items bought from a thriftshop.
She found a vintage leather jacket at the local thriftshop.
Volunteers sort donations every morning at the thriftshop downtown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charity shop - British term for a thrift shop, especially one run by a charity
