Boer War

noun
two wars between the British and Boers in South Africa
7.9
Pronunciation: /bɔːr wɔːr/ /; / /boʊr woʊr/ (AmE), /bɔː wɔː/ /; / /bʊə wɔː/ /; / /ˈbəʊ.ə wɔː/ (BrE)
either of two wars fought in South Africa between the British and the Boers (descendants of Dutch settlers), specifically the First Boer War (1880–1881) and the Second Boer War (1899–1902).

Examples

Her great-grandfather fought in the Boer War and shared many stories from that time.
Many soldiers from across the British Empire served in the Boer War.
The Boer War significantly influenced the development of modern warfare tactics.
