Jagannath

proper noun
avatar of Vishnu in Hinduism
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈgɑːnəθ / (AmE), / dʒəˈɡʌnəθ / (BrE)
A form or incarnation of the Hindu god Vishnu, revered especially in the eastern regions of India.

Examples

Jagannath festival
Devotees celebrate the annual Ratha Yatra festival in honor of Jagannath.
Many stories in Hindu mythology recount the miracles of Jagannath.
The temple of Jagannath in Puri is a major pilgrimage site for Hindus.
