tergite

noun
arthropod dorsal plate
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrɡaɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɜːɡaɪt / (BrE)
the dorsal (upper) plate or segment of an arthropod's body, such as in insects or crustaceans
