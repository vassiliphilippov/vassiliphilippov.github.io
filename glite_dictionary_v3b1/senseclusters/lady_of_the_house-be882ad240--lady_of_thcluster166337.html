lady of the house

noun
woman who manages a household
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdi əv ðə haʊs / (AmE), / ˈleɪdi əv ðə haʊs / (BrE)
the woman, especially the wife, who is in charge of managing a household

Examples

The lady of the house
As the lady of the house, she made sure everything was perfectly organized for the dinner party.
The lady of the house greeted us warmly at the door.
Whenever there was a problem, the children turned to the lady of the house for guidance.
