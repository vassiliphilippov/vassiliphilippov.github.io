individualistically

adverb
in an individualistic manner
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪvɪdʒuəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪvɪdjʊəˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows independence and self-reliance, or prioritizes individual interests above those of a group.

Examples

To act individualistically
Employees are encouraged to think individualistically and propose innovative solutions to company challenges.
In his art, he expresses himself individualistically, never imitating anyone else's style.
She approached the project individualistically, preferring to rely on her own ideas rather than group consensus.
