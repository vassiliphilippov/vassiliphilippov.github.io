Mount Carmel

proper noun
mountain range in northwestern Israel
Pronunciation: / maʊnt ˈkɑrməl / (AmE), / maʊnt ˈkɑːməl / (BrE)
a mountain range in northwestern Israel, near the Mediterranean coast, noted for its biblical and historical significance

Examples

Mount Carmel mountain range
According to the Bible, the prophet Elijah performed miracles on Mount Carmel.
Many pilgrims visit Mount Carmel for its religious significance each year.
The city of Haifa is located on the slopes of Mount Carmel.
