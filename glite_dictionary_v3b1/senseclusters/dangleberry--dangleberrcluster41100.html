dangleberry

noun
a North American huckleberry shrub
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdæŋɡəlˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈdaŋɡəlˌbɛri / (BrE)
A North American shrub (Gaylussacia frondosa) of the heath family, with pink flowers and sweet blue fruit, also known as a type of huckleberry.

Examples

Sweet dangleberries
Birds often feed on the dangleberries in eastern woodlands.
The dangleberry bush produces sweet blue fruit in late summer.
We found several dangleberries growing along the forest path during our hike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: huckleberry - a small edible blue-black berry; also the plant bearing the berry
