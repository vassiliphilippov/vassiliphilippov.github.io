sparklingly

adverb
very clean or bright
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːrklɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈspɑːklɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely clean, bright, or impressive, often used as an intensifier.

Examples

Sparklingly clean room
The windows were sparklingly clean after she finished washing them.
He delivered his speech sparklingly, captivating everyone in the audience.
The lake looked sparklingly clear under the midday sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brilliantly - in a very bright or impressive way
  • antonym: dully - in a way that is not bright or impressive
