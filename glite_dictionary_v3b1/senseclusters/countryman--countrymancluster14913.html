countryman

noun
man living in the country
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌn.tri.mæn / (AmE), / ˈkʌn.tri.mən / (BrE)
a man who lives in the countryside, especially one with traditional rural habits or ways

Examples

Old countryman
As a lifelong countryman, he preferred fresh air and open fields to city crowds.
The countryman greeted us warmly as we walked past his farmhouse.
The old countryman knew every trail and stream in the valley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: countrywoman - a woman who lives in the country and has country ways
