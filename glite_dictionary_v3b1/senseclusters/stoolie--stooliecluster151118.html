stoolie

noun
police informer (slang)
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːli / (AmE), / ˈstjuːli / (BrE)
a person who acts as an informer or decoy for the police, especially in criminal investigations

Examples

A stoolie informer
No one trusted him after he was exposed as a stoolie in court.
The detective relied on a stoolie to break the case wide open.
The gang suspected there was a stoolie among them feeding information to the police.

Related Terms

  • synonym: informer - a person who provides information to the police, often secretly
  • synonym: snitch - a person who betrays others by informing on them
