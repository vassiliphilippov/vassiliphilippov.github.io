yuppity crowd

noun
group of young urban professionals
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjʌpɪti kraʊd / (AmE), / ˈjʌpɪti kraʊd / (BrE)
A group of young urban professionals who are often perceived as elitist or pretentious.

Examples

The yuppity crowd at events
Some locals felt out of place among the yuppity crowd at the art gallery.
The city's nightlife is often dominated by the yuppity crowd looking for trendy spots.
The new restaurant quickly attracted a yuppity crowd on opening night.
