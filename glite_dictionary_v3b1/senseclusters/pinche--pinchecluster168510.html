pinche

noun
South American tamarin with tufted head
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪntʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɪntʃeɪ / (BrE)
A small South American tamarin monkey (Saguinus oedipus), known for its distinctive tufted white head.

Examples

A pinche monkey
In the rainforest, researchers spotted a group of noisy pinches swinging through the branches.
The pinche is critically endangered due to habitat loss and illegal pet trade.
The pinche is easily recognized by the distinctive white hair on its head.
