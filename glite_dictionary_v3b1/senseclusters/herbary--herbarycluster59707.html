herbary

noun
herb garden or collection
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜːrbəri / (AmE), / ˈhɜːbəri / (BrE)
a place where herbs are grown or a collection of dried herbs, especially for medicinal or culinary use

Examples

Medicinal herbary garden
The ancient herbary contained rare plants used for traditional remedies.
Students visited the herbary to learn about different medicinal herbs.
She carefully labeled each specimen in her personal herbary at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: garden - a piece of ground for growing plants
  • synonym: herbarium - a collection of preserved plant specimens
