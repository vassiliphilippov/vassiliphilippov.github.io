blink

verb
quickly close and open the eyes
1.0
Pronunciation: / blɪŋk / (AmE), / blɪŋk / (BrE)
to quickly close and open the eyes, often involuntarily or briefly

Examples

To blink your eyes
He didn't blink once throughout the shocking announcement.
She tried not to blink during the staring contest.
The nurse asked him to blink several times to test his reflexes.

  • synonym: wink - to close and open one eye quickly
