cereal bowl

noun
bowl for breakfast cereal
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriəl boʊl / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəl bəʊl / (BrE)
a bowl used for serving or eating breakfast cereal

Examples

A cereal bowl
Each guest was given a colorful cereal bowl at the breakfast table.
He accidentally dropped his cereal bowl and it shattered on the kitchen floor.
She poured milk into her cereal bowl before adding the cornflakes.
