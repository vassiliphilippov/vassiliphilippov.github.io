Golgi's cell

noun
type of cerebral cortex neuron
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldʒiz sɛl / (AmE), / ˈɡɒldʒiz sɛl / (BrE)
a neuron in the cerebral cortex characterized by short dendrites and an axon that may be long or short, often found branching in the grey matter
