producer

noun
something that produces or creates
2.4 3.54/m
Pronunciation: / pr…ôÀàdus…ôr / (AmE), / pr…ôÀàdjuÀês…ô / (BrE)
an organism, person, or thing that produces or creates something; especially one that generates or manufactures goods, substances, or outcomes

Examples

A producer of food
Maine is a leading producer of potatoes in the United States.
The new factory is a significant producer of solar panels for export.
This species of algae is an important producer in the aquatic ecosystem.

Synonyms

  • maker
    1.6 32/m
    a maker is a person or company that builds or manufactures goods, while a producer is any person, organism, or thing that creates products.
  • creator
    2.8 15/m
    a creator makes or invents new things, while a producer often makes goods or manages production for sale.
  • generator
    3.9 1.06/m
    a generator produces energy or causes something to start, while a producer mainly makes goods, substances, or other products.
