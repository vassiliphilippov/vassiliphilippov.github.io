producer

noun
something that produces or creates
1.0
Pronunciation: / prəˈdusər / (AmE), / prəˈdjuːsə / (BrE)
an organism, person, or thing that produces or creates something; especially one that generates or manufactures goods, substances, or outcomes

Examples

A producer of food
Maine is a leading producer of potatoes in the United States.
The new factory is a significant producer of solar panels for export.
This species of algae is an important producer in the aquatic ecosystem.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consumer - an organism or person that consumes goods or services
