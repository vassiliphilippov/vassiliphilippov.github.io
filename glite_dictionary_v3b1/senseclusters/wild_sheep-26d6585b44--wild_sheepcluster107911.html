wild sheep

noun
undomesticated sheep
Pronunciation: / waɪld ʃiːp / (AmE), / waɪld ʃiːp / (BrE)
A species or individual sheep that has not been domesticated and lives in a natural, untamed environment.

Examples

A wild sheep species
Many species of wild sheep are found in mountainous regions across Asia and North America.
The wild sheep in this reserve are rarely seen by visitors due to their shy nature.
Unlike their domesticated cousins, wild sheep have adapted to survive harsh environments.
