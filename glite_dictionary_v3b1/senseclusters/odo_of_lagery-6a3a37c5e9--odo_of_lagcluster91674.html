Odo of Lagery

proper noun
French pope who initiated the First Crusade
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊdoʊ əv læˈɡɛri / (AmE), / ˈəʊdəʊ əv læˈɡɛri / (BrE)
(c. 1042–1099) French pope, known as Urban II, who initiated the First Crusade with his sermons.

Examples

Odo of Lagery pope
Odo of Lagery is best remembered for launching the First Crusade at the Council of Clermont.
Medieval histories often credit Odo of Lagery with changing the course of European and Middle Eastern affairs.
The Papal Bull issued by Odo of Lagery had far-reaching consequences throughout Christendom.
