storable

adjective
able to be stored
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːrəbəl / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrəbəl / (BrE)
able to be kept or preserved for future use without spoiling or becoming unusable

Examples

Storable food
These grains are highly storable and can be kept for years if sealed properly.
The new packaging makes the product more storable in small kitchens.
Frozen vegetables are convenient because they are both nutritious and storable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: perishable - likely to spoil or decay quickly
  • synonym: preservable - able to be preserved for later use
