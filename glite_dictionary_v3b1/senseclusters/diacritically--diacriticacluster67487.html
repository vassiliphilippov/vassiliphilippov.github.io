diacritically

adverb
with diacritical marks
8.9
Pronunciation: /daɪ.ə.ˈkrɪ.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /dɪ.ə.ˈkrɪ.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /dɪ.ə.ˈkrɪ.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /daɪ.ə.ˈkrɪ.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)

Examples

Some languages are written diacritically to distinguish between similar sounds.
