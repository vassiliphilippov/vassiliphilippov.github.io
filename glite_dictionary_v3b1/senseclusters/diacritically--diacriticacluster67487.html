diacritically

adverb
with diacritical marks
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəˈkrɪtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈkrɪtɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to or involves the use of diacritical marks in writing or printing

Examples

To diacritically mark vowels
The vowels in the text were diacritically marked to clarify their pronunciation.
Some languages are written diacritically to distinguish between similar sounds.
The teacher explained how to read words that are diacritically annotated in the textbook.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orthographically - in a way that relates to spelling or writing systems
