polarizer

noun
light-filtering device
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊləˌraɪzər / (AmE), / ˈpəʊləˌraɪzə / (BrE)
a device or filter that allows only light waves of a specific polarization to pass through

Examples

Camera polarizer filter
The scientist used a polarizer to study the properties of polarized light.
A polarizer can help reduce reflections when taking photographs outdoors.
By rotating the polarizer, the photographer was able to enhance the colors in the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filter - a device that removes unwanted parts from something passing through it
