astrobiologically

adverb
in relation to life in space
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌæstroʊˌbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌæstrəʊˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the scientific study of life in the universe, especially beyond Earth

Examples

The planet is astrobiologically interesting
The planet is astrobiologically interesting because it may support life.
Researchers are searching for astrobiologically significant signs on Mars and other planets.
Water is considered astrobiologically important when looking for life beyond Earth.

Related Terms
