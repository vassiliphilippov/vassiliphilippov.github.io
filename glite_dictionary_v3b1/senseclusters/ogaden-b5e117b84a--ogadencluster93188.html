Ogaden

proper noun
region in Ethiopia
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊɡəˌdɛn / (AmE), / ˈəʊɡədɛn / (BrE)
a region in eastern Ethiopia inhabited mainly by ethnic Somalis, known for its distinct history and culture

Examples

The Ogaden region
The Ogaden is home to a large Somali-speaking population in eastern Ethiopia.
Many humanitarian organizations have worked in the Ogaden due to recurring droughts.
The history of the Ogaden includes periods of conflict and cultural resilience.
