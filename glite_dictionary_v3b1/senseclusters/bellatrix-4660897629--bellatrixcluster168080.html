Bellatrix

proper noun
star in Orion
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛləˌtrɪks / (AmE), / ˈbɛlətrɪks / (BrE)
a bright star in the constellation Orion, also known as Gamma Orionis

Examples

Bellatrix star in Orion
The star Bellatrix is easily visible in the night sky as part of Orion's shoulder.
Astronomers have studied Bellatrix to learn more about the life cycle of massive stars.
Many stargazers use Bellatrix as a reference point when locating other stars in Orion.
