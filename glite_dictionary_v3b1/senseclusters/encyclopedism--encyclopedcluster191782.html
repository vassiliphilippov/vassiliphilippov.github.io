encyclopedism

noun
profound scholarly knowledge
Pronunciation: / ɛnˌsaɪkləˈpidɪzəm /; / ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpidɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪzəm /; / ɪnˌsʌɪklɪəˈpiːdɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
the possession of profound, comprehensive scholarly knowledge; encyclopedic learning
