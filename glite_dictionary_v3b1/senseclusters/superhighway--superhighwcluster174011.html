superhighway

noun
electronic data network; the internet
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈhaɪweɪ / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈhaɪweɪ / (BrE)
an extensive electronic network that enables rapid transfer of digital data, especially sound, video, and graphics; often used to refer to the internet

Examples

Digital superhighway
As technology advanced, the superhighway became crucial in business, education, and entertainment.
Government policy aims to expand the superhighway to rural communities, ensuring high-speed internet for all.
The information superhighway has transformed the way we share and access data worldwide.
