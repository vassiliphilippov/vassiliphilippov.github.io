angler

noun
person who fishes with a hook and line
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡlər / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡlə / (BrE)
a person who fishes with a rod, line, and hook, especially as a hobby rather than for commercial purposes

Examples

Amateur angler
As an amateur angler, she was thrilled to catch her first fish on holiday.
Every Saturday morning, the angler patiently waits by the lake for a bite.
The skilled angler landed a large trout after hours of casting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fisherman - a person who catches fish for a living or as a hobby
  • antonym: netter - a person who catches fish using nets
