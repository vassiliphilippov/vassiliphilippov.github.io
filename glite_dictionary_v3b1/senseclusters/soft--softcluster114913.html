soft

adjective
declining in value (finance)
6.3 2.90/m
Pronunciation: / sÉ‘ft / (AmE), / sÉ’ft / (BrE)
(of a commodity, market, or currency) experiencing or likely to experience a fall in value; weak or declining in price.

Examples

Soft market
Investors are cautious when the soft market shows little sign of recovery.
Prices for agricultural products have turned soft due to oversupply.
The currency remained soft against the dollar throughout the month.

Synonyms

  • weak
    3.3 4.06/m

  • synonym: weak - lacking strength or stability, especially in value or price
