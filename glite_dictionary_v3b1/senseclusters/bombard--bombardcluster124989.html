bombard

noun
large bass shawm (musical instrument)
6.5
Pronunciation: / bämˈbɑrd / (AmE), / bɒmˈbɑːd / (BrE)
A large shawm; the bass member of the shawm family, used as an early musical wind instrument.

Examples

A bombard instrument

Music historians are fascinated by the deep, reedy sound of the bombard.
The medieval band featured a musician playing the bombard at the center.
