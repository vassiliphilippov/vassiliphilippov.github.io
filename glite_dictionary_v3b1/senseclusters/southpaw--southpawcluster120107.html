southpaw

noun
a left-handed person (esp. in sports)
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊθˌpɔ / (AmE), / ˈsaʊθˌpɔː / (BrE)
a person who is left-handed, especially in sports such as baseball or boxing

Examples

The team signed a veteran southpaw to strengthen their pitching lineup.
The young southpaw delivered a fastball that stunned the crowd.
