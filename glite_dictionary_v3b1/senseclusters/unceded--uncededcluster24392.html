unceded

adjective
not given up
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsiːdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsiːdɪd / (BrE)
not surrendered, transferred, or given up, especially in reference to land or rights.

Examples

The unceded territories
The treaty did not include the unceded territories still controlled by the local people.
Many indigenous groups continue to live on unceded land that was never formally transferred.
The government acknowledged the area as unceded, recognizing the community's ongoing claim.

Related Terms

  • synonym: retained - kept or continued to have
  • antonym: surrendered - given up or handed over, especially under pressure
