saxophone

noun
single-reed woodwind instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈsæksəˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈsæksəfəʊn / (BrE)
a musical instrument of the woodwind family with a single reed and a conical metal tube, typically used in jazz and dance music

Examples

A saxophone player
He bought a vintage saxophone from a music store downtown.
She played the saxophone in the school jazz band.
The sound of the saxophone filled the smoky nightclub.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarinet - a woodwind instrument with a single reed and cylindrical bore
