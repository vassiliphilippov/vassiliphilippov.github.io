GI series

noun
X-ray test of the gastrointestinal tract
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiː ˈaɪ ˈsɪriːz / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː ˈaɪ ˈsɪəriːz / (BrE)
a diagnostic procedure involving X-ray examination of the gastrointestinal tract after swallowing a contrast medium such as barium sulfate

Examples

A GI series X-ray
A GI series can help reveal ulcers, tumors, or other abnormalities in the digestive tract.
After drinking the barium solution, the patient underwent a GI series at the hospital.
The doctor ordered a GI series to investigate the cause of her abdominal pain.
