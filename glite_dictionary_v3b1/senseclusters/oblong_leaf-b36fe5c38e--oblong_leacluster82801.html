oblong leaf

noun
a leaf with parallel sides and rounded ends
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːb.lɑːŋ liːf / (AmE), / ˈɒb.lɒŋ liːf / (BrE)
a simple leaf whose sides are nearly parallel and ends are rounded, longer than it is wide

Examples

An oblong leaf
I found an oblong leaf on the forest floor, different from the others nearby.
The rubber plant has an oblong leaf with a glossy surface.
The textbook illustrates an oblong leaf next to an oval-shaped one.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elliptical leaf - a leaf shaped like an ellipse, broader than an oblong leaf
