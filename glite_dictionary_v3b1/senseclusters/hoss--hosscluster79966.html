hoss

noun
big, strong person
Pronunciation: / hɑs / (AmE), / hɒs / (BrE)
an informal term for a big, strong horse or a large, strong man

Examples

A big hoss
He is a real hoss when it comes to lifting heavy things.
The football team relied on their biggest hoss to break through the defense.
Everyone admired the hoss that pulled the wagon at the county fair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: giant - a very large or strong person
  • synonym: horse - a large domesticated animal used for riding or work
