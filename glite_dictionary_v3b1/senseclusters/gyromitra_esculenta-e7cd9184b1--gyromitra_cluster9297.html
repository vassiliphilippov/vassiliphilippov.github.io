A species of poisonous fungus, known as the false morel, with a wrinkled, convoluted cap ranging in color from dull yellow to brown.

Examples

Gyromitra esculenta mushroom
Despite its species name meaning 'edible,' Gyromitra esculenta is highly toxic and should not be consumed.
Many mushroom foragers mistake Gyromitra esculenta for edible morels, which can lead to poisoning.
The wrinkled, brain-like appearance of Gyromitra esculenta distinguishes it from true morels.
