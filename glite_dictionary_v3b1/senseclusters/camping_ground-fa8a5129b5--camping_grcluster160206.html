camping ground

noun
site for pitching tents or camping
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmpɪŋ ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈkæmpɪŋ ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
an area of land set aside for people to pitch tents or park recreational vehicles, typically for holiday use

Examples

Many families return to the same camping ground every summer for their holiday.
The camping ground offers showers, toilets, and a small shop for campers.
We set up our tent at the camping ground near the lake for the weekend.
