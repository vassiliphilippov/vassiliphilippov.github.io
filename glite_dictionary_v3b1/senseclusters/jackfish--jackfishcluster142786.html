jackfish

noun
North American pike
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒækˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒækˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a name for various predatory freshwater fish, especially the pike, found in North America

Examples

A jackfish in the lake
He was excited to catch his first jackfish during their summer trip to Canada.
The lake is known for its large population of jackfish that attract many anglers.
After cleaning the jackfish, they cooked it over an open fire by the shore.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pickerel - a small, predatory freshwater fish related to the pike
  • synonym: pike - a large, predatory freshwater fish with a long body and pointed snout
