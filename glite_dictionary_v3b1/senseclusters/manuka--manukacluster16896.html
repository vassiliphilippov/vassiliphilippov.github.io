manuka

noun
honey from manuka shrub
7.9
Pronunciation: / məˈnuːkə / (AmE), / məˈnuːkə / (BrE)
a type of dark, strong-flavored honey made from the nectar of the manuka shrub, valued for its antibacterial properties
