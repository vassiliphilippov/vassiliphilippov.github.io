ministry

noun
government department building
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnɪstri /; / ˈmɪnəstri / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪstri / (BrE)
a government department and its building, responsible for a particular area of public affairs

Examples

Ministry of Health
All official documents are handled within the main ministry in the capital.
Security has been tightened around the ministry following recent protests.
The new ministry was inaugurated by the prime minister last week.
A new policy was introduced by the health ministry to improve public hospitals.
She works at the ministry of education, focusing on school reform.
The foreign ministry issued a statement regarding the international summit.
