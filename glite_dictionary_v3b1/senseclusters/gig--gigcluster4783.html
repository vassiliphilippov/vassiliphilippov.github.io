gig

noun
short-term paid job (freelance)
Pronunciation: / ɡɪɡ / (AmE), / ɡɪɡ / (BrE)
a short-term paid job or task, often freelance and sometimes arranged through an online platform or app.

Examples

A weekend gig
She accepted a weekend gig driving for a delivery app to earn extra money.
Many students do small gigs like tutoring or dog walking between classes.
The platform connects freelancers with short gigs and pays immediately after task completion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freelance job - a paid piece of work done independently rather than as a permanent employee
  • antonym: permanent job - a regular, ongoing job with long-term employment
