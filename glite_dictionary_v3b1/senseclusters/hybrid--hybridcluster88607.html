hybrid

adjective
produced by crossbreeding
1.0 3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪ.brɪd / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.brɪd / (BrE)
(of an animal or plant) produced by crossbreeding different species, varieties, or breeds.

Examples

Hybrid plants
Many garden flowers are hybrid forms, created to enhance color and fragrance.
Some dog breeds, such as the Labradoodle, are intentionally hybrid to combine desirable traits.
This new variety of corn is a hybrid developed for greater resistance to disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crossbred - produced by breeding two different varieties or species
  • antonym: purebred - bred from parents of the same breed or variety
