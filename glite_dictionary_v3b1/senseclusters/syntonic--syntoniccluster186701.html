syntonic

adjective
in harmony with group
Pronunciation: / sɪnˈtɑnɪk / (AmE), / sɪnˈtɒnɪk / (BrE)
having a personality or attitude that is in harmony with one's environment or social group

Examples

Syntonic personality
Her syntonic nature made her popular and well-liked in every group she joined.
A syntonic attitude helps people adapt smoothly to new social environments.
The therapist described him as syntonic because he easily fit in with his peers.
