blockage

noun
an obstruction in a pipe or passage
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑkɪdʒ /; / ˈblɑːkɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈblɒkɪdʒ / (BrE)
an obstruction or something that blocks a passage, pipe, or tube, preventing movement or flow
