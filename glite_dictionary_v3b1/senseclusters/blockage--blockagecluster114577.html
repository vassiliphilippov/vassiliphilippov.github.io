blockage

noun
an obstruction in a pipe or passage

Synonyms

  • obstruction
    4.4 2.61/m
    obstruction is something solid that blocks a path or flow, while blockage is the common word for any blocked passage.
  • occlusion
    8.7 0.49/m
    occlusion usually describes a medical or technical blocking inside a vessel or organ, while blockage is the everyday word for a blocked passage.
  • clog
    3.5 0.44/m
    clog is a stuck mass or object that blocks a pipe or drain, while blockage is the general term for any stop of flow.
  • stoppage
    5.0 0.02/m
    stoppage is a halt of flow or work, often temporary, while blockage is a physical obstruction that stops movement in a pipe.
