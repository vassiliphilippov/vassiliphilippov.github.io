aporetic

adjective
expressing doubt
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˈrɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌapəˈrɛtɪk / (BrE)
expressing or involving doubt, puzzlement, or an unsolvable contradiction, especially in philosophical discussions
