wilted

adjective
limp or drooping (of plants)
2.9 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪl.tɪd/ (AmE), /ˈwɪl.tɪd/ (BrE)
(of plants) limp and drooping, usually as a result of heat, loss of water, or disease.

Examples

Wilted flowers
After a week without rain, the wilted flowers drooped sadly in the garden.
Please remove any wilted spinach leaves before serving the salad.
The lettuce had become wilted from sitting out in the heat too long.
