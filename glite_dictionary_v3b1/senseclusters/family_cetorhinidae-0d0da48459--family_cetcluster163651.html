family Cetorhinidae

noun
taxonomic family for basking shark
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛtəˌraɪnɪˌdi / (AmE), / ˈsiːtəʊˌraɪnɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of large sharks, containing only the basking shark, sometimes recognized in older taxonomic classifications

Examples

The family Cetorhinidae
In earlier zoological texts, the basking shark was classified within the family Cetorhinidae.
Some scientists debate the validity of family Cetorhinidae as a distinct shark family.
The family Cetorhinidae includes only one living species, the basking shark.
