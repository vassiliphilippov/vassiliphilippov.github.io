lord

noun
man with power or authority; ruler or noble
1.0 3.9
Pronunciation: / lɔrd / (AmE), / lɔːd / (BrE)
A man who has power and authority over others, especially as a ruler, noble, or powerful figure.

Examples

A powerful lord

Local farmers paid taxes to their lord in exchange for protection.
The lord of the manor held great influence over the local villagers.
