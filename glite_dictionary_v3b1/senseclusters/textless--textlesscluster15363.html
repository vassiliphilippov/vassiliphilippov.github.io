textless

adjective
without written words
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛkstləs / (AmE), / ˈtɛkstləs / (BrE)
lacking text or not containing any written words

Examples

Textless version
The textless version of the poster focused only on the artwork.
Some silent films are completely textless, relying only on visuals to tell the story.
He submitted a textless presentation, using only images to communicate his ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blank - having nothing written or printed
  • antonym: textual - containing or relating to text
  • synonym: wordless - without words or written language
