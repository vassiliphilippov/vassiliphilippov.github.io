signal tower

noun
building for controlling railway signals
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡnəl ˌtaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnəl ˌtaʊə / (BrE)
a building from which signals are sent to control the movements of railway trains

Examples

Railway signal tower
A new electronic system replaced the function of the original signal tower.
From the signal tower, operators managed train traffic along the main line.
The old signal tower near the station has been converted into a museum.
