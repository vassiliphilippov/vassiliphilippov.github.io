copayment

noun
fixed medical fee
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌpeɪmənt / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌpeɪmənt / (BrE)
a fixed amount of money that a person pays for a medical service, with the rest usually covered by insurance

Examples

A copayment amount
Each time I visit the doctor, I have to pay a copayment at the front desk.
Some insurance plans require a higher copayment for specialist visits than for general checkups.
The pharmacy asked for a small copayment when I picked up my prescription.
Each time you see a specialist, you must pay a copayment at the clinic.
Her health insurance plan requires a small copayment for every prescription.
The copayment for emergency room visits is higher than for regular doctor appointments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coinsurance - a percentage of costs a patient pays after meeting the deductible
  • antonym: deductible - the amount a patient must pay before insurance covers costs
  • synonym: premium - the amount paid regularly for insurance coverage
