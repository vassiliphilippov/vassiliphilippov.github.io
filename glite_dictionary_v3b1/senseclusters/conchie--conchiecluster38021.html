conchie

noun
slang for objector
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑntʃi / (AmE), / ˈkɒntʃi / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who refuses military service on moral or religious grounds, especially during wartime

Examples

A conchie in wartime
During World War I, many people looked down on conchies for refusing to fight.
He was proud to be a conchie, standing by his beliefs despite criticism.
Some families supported their conchie relatives even when others did not understand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conscientious objector - a person who refuses military service for moral or religious reasons
