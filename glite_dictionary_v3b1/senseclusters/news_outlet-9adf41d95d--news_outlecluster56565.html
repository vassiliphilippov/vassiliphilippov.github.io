news outlet

noun
organization that provides news
Pronunciation: / nuz ˈaʊt.lət / (AmE), / njuːz ˈaʊt.lət / (BrE)
An organization or platform that provides news to the public, such as newspapers, websites, or television stations.

Examples

Local news outlet
Many people rely on their local news outlet for updates about community events.
She works as a journalist for an international news outlet.
The news outlet reported live from the scene of the incident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: media outlet - organization that disseminates information to the public
  • synonym: news source - origin from which news is obtained
