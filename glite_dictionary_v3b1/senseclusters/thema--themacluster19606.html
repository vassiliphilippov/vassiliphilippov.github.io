thema

noun
Byzantine province
Pronunciation: / ˈθiːmə / (AmE), / ˈθiːmə / (BrE)
an administrative district or province in the Byzantine Empire, governed by a military official

Examples

Byzantine thema region
The thema system was crucial for organizing the Byzantine Empire's military and administration.
Each thema was governed by a strategos who managed both civil and military affairs.
Historians study the thema structure to understand how the empire maintained control over its territories.
