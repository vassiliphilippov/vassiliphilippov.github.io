clinical

adjective
emotionally detached; dispassionate
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪnɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈklɪnɪkəl / (BrE)
showing no emotion or personal involvement; scientifically detached or dispassionate

Examples

Clinical approach
He gave a clinical account of the events, never allowing emotion to influence his words.
Her analysis of the situation was so clinical that it seemed devoid of any human warmth.
The report's clinical tone made it difficult for readers to empathize with the victims.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detached - not involved emotionally; aloof or objective
  • synonym: dispassionate - not influenced by strong emotion; rational; objective
  • antonym: emotional - displaying strong feelings; not detached or clinical
