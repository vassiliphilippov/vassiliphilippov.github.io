the Tempter

noun
Satan; one who tempts to evil
9.9
Pronunciation: /ðə ˈtemp.tər/ /; / /ðiː ˈtemp.tər/ (AmE), /ðə ˈtemp.tə/ /; / /ðiː ˈtemp.tə/ (BrE)
A name for Satan or the Devil, regarded as the chief spirit of evil and adversary of God, especially as one who tempts or leads humans into sin.
