release the handbrake

verb
disengage a vehicle's handbrake
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlis ðə ˈhændˌbreɪk / (AmE), / rɪˈliːs ðə ˈhændbreɪk / (BrE)
to disengage the handbrake of a vehicle, allowing it to move

Examples

To release the handbrake gently
After parking on a slope, make sure to release the handbrake gently to prevent rolling.
Before starting the car, remember to release the handbrake to avoid damaging the brakes.
She forgot to release the handbrake and wondered why the car would not move.
