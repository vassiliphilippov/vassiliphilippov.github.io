Examples

Deep inhalation of air

Synonyms

  • inspiration
    4.1 0.13/m
    inspiration means drawing air into the lungs during breathing, while inhalation is the neutral medical term for the inward breath.
  • breathing in
    1.0 0.75/m
    breathing in is the everyday phrase for taking air into the lungs, while inhalation is the formal or medical word.
  • intake
    3.5 0.83/m
    intake often names the amount or act of taking air into the lungs, while inhalation focuses on the actual inward breath.
  • aspiration
    5.9 0.50/m
    aspiration often means drawing breath but also refers to inhaling something accidentally, whereas inhalation usually means the normal inward breath.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exhalation - the act of breathing out air from the lungs
  • synonym: inspire - to draw air into the lungs
