diastrophic

adjective
relating to rare bone disorder
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəˈstrɑːfɪk / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈstrɒfɪk / (BrE)
relating to diastrophic dysplasia, a rare inherited disorder affecting cartilage and bone development, causing short stature and joint deformities
