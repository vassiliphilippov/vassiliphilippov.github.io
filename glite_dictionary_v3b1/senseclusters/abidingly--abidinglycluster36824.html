abidingly

adverb
enduringly
6.2
Pronunciation: / əˈbaɪdɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈbaɪdɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that lasts for a long time or shows enduring quality

Examples

To be abidingly loyal
He remained abidingly loyal to his childhood friends throughout his life.
The town is abidingly proud of its rich cultural heritage.
She was abidingly interested in learning about different world religions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: temporarily - for a short time only
