meeter

noun
person who measures
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈmiːtə / (BrE)
a person or device that measures quantity or amount, especially in an official capacity (archaic or dialectal)

Examples

Official meeter of grain
The village appointed a meeter to ensure fair distribution of grain.
In old records, the meeter was responsible for checking the amount of harvested crops.
A skilled meeter was essential for trade in the local market.
