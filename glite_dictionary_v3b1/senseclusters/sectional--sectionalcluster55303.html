sectional

adjective
relating to a section, as if cut through
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkʃənəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or based on a section, especially as if cut through by an intersecting plane; showing a view of the inside as if exposed by a cut.

Examples

Sectional view drawing
A sectional view of the building revealed hidden support beams.
The engineer studied the sectional drawing of the machine to understand its internal structure.
The textbook includes several sectional diagrams to illustrate how the device works.
