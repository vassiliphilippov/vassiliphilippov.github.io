Synonyms

    3.9 2/m
    puncture is to make a small hole that lets air or liquid escape, while pierce is to cut or go through something.
  • perforate
    6.1 0.6/m
    perforate means making one or many holes through material, often in a line, while pierce means cut or go through in general.
  • bore
    4.9 0.2/m
    bore means make a hole by drilling or digging with a tool, while pierce means cut or go through with a sharp object.
  • penetrate
    4.1 8/m
    penetrate means enter or pass into something often with force or effort, while pierce means make a hole or cut through material.
