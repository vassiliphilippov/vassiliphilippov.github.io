festival

noun
celebratory day or period
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛstəvəl / (AmE), / ˈfɛstɪvəl / (BrE)
A day or period marked by gatherings, celebrations, and often feasting in honor of a particular event, occasion, or tradition.

Examples

A summer festival
During the summer, many tourists visit for the famous music festival.
Families gather together to enjoy food and games during the spring festival.
The city hosts an annual festival to celebrate its rich cultural heritage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holiday - a day of festivity or recreation when no work is done
