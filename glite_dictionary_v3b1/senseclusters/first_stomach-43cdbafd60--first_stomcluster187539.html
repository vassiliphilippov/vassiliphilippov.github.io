first stomach

noun
first compartment of a ruminant's stomach
6.0 6.6
Pronunciation: /fɜːrst ˈstʌ.mək/ (AmE), /fɜːst ˈstʌ.mək/ (BrE)
the first compartment of the stomach in a ruminant animal, where food is initially collected and later returned to the mouth for chewing as cud
