ignominious

adjective
deserving or causing shame or disgrace
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪɡ.nəˈmɪn.i.əs / (AmE), / ˌɪɡ.nəˈmɪn.i.əs / (BrE)
deserving or causing public disgrace or shame; humiliating.

Examples

An ignominious defeat
He resigned in ignominious circumstances after the scandal became public.
The general's ignominious defeat ended his once-stellar military career.
Their cheating was discovered, resulting in an ignominious expulsion from the competition.
