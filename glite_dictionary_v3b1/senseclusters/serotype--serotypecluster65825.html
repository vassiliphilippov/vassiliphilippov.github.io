serotype

verb
to identify serotype
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪrəˌtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈsɪərəˌtaɪp / (BrE)
to determine the specific serotype of a microorganism by testing its surface antigens.

Examples

To serotype bacteria
The laboratory will serotype the bacteria to determine which strain caused the outbreak.
Researchers serotyped the virus samples to track the spread of the disease.
It is important to serotype pathogens accurately for effective treatment and prevention.
