cobwebby

adjective
covered with or like cobwebs
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑbˌwɛbi / (AmE), / ˈkɒbˌwɛbi / (BrE)
covered with or resembling cobwebs; very thin, light, delicate, or filmy like a cobweb

Examples

She wore a cobwebby scarf that looked weightless around her shoulders.
The curtains in the old house were cobwebby and let the morning light filter through.

  • antonym: opaque - not able to be seen through; not transparent or translucent
  • synonym: spidery - thin and delicate like a spider's web
