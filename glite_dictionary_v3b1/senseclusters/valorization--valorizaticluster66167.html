valorization

noun
making waste valuable
Pronunciation: / ˌvælərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvælərʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of converting waste, by-products, or low-value materials into more valuable products, especially in industry or recycling

Examples

Valorization of waste
The company invested in new technology for the valorization of plastic waste into fuel.
Government policies now encourage the valorization of industrial by-products to reduce environmental impact.
Researchers are studying the valorization of food waste to create sustainable packaging materials.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recycling - the process of converting waste into reusable material
  • antonym: waste - materials that are not wanted and are thrown away
