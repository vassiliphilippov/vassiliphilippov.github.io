turbocompressor

noun
engine air booster
6.7
Pronunciation: /tɜːr.boʊ.kəm.ˈpre.sər/ /; / /ˈtɜːr.boʊ.kəm.pre.sər/ (AmE), /tɜː.bəʊ.kəm.ˈpre.sə/ /; / /ˈtɜː.bəʊ.kəm.pre.sə/ (BrE)
a machine that uses a turbine to increase the pressure of air or gases, often to improve the efficiency of engines or industrial systems

Related Terms

  • synonym: supercharger - a device that increases the pressure of air supplied to an engine
  • synonym: turbocharger - a device that increases engine power by forcing extra air into the combustion chamber
