boresome

adjective
causing boredom
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔrsəm / (AmE), / ˈbɔːsəm / (BrE)
causing boredom; dull, tedious, or uninteresting

Examples

A boresome movie
The movie was so boresome that I struggled to stay awake until the end.
He found the long meeting boresome and kept checking his watch.
Many students thought the textbook was boresome compared to the interactive lessons.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exciting - causing great enthusiasm and eagerness
  • synonym: tedious - too long, slow, or dull; tiresome
