aberrant

adjective
deviating from normal or accepted standards
Pronunciation: / əˈbɛr.ənt / (AmE), / əˈbɛr.ənt / (BrE)
deviating from what is normal, usual, or acceptable, typically in an unwelcome way

Examples

Aberrant behavior
Her aberrant choices set her apart from the rest of her family.
His aberrant behavior at the meeting surprised his colleagues.
The scientist was concerned about the aberrant results from the experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deviant - departing from usual or accepted standards, especially in social or sexual behavior
  • antonym: normal - conforming to a standard; usual, typical, or expected
