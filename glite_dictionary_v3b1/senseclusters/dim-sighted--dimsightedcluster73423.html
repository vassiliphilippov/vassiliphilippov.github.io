Synonyms

  • visually impaired
    3.9 1.60/m
    visually impaired is a neutral medical term for reduced sight used by professionals, while dim-sighted is an informal description of poor vision.
  • visually challenged
    5.8 0.01/m
    visually challenged is a polite, euphemistic phrase people use to soften disability, while dim-sighted is more direct and descriptive of poor sight.
  • near-blind
    6.3 0.03/m
    near-blind means almost unable to see with very little sight left, while dim-sighted means generally having reduced or unclear vision.
