lineman

noun
American football player on line of scrimmage
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪnmæn /; / ˈlaɪnˌmæn /; / ˈlaɪn.mæn / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnmən /; / ˈlaɪn.mən / (BrE)
a player in American or Canadian football who is positioned on the line of scrimmage, responsible for blocking or defending
