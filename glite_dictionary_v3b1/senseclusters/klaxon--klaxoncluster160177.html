klaxon

noun
loud warning horn, often for vehicles
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈklæk.sən / (AmE), / ˈklæk.sən / (BrE)
a type of loud electric horn, typically one formerly used on motor vehicles or as a warning signal

Examples

A klaxon horn
A sudden blast from a klaxon warned workers to evacuate the building.
During the parade, someone pressed the klaxon, startling the crowd with its loud sound.
The old car still had its original klaxon mounted on the front fender.
