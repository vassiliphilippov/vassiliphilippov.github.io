cradle

verb
to separate gold or gravel using a cradle
8.4 7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkreɪ.dəl/ (AmE), /ˈkreɪ.dəl/ (BrE)
(of gold or gravel) to wash or separate in a cradle, a rocking mining device used especially for gold prospecting.

Examples

To cradle gravel
During the gold rush, prospectors would cradle gravel to find tiny nuggets.
Old miners still demonstrate how to cradle gold at historical sites.
She learned to cradle the sediment, separating gold from the sand efficiently.
