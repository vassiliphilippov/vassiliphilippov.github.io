hangtag

noun
retail display label
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋˌtæɡ / (AmE), / ˈhæŋˌtæɡ / (BrE)
a small label or tag attached to a product or item, showing information such as price, brand, care instructions, or a message

Examples

Before buying the dress, she checked the hangtag for the price and fabric details.
