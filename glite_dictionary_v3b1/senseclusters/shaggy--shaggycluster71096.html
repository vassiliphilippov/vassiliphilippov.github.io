shaggy

adjective
having long, thick, or untidy hair or material
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæɡi / (AmE), / ˈʃaɡi / (BrE)
having long, thick, and untidy hair, fur, or material; looking rough or messy

Examples

A shaggy dog
Her shaggy hair blew wildly in the wind as she ran down the hill.
The old dog had a shaggy coat that needed frequent brushing.
They placed a shaggy rug in front of the fireplace for added comfort.
A shaggy dog ran through the muddy yard, its fur sticking out everywhere.
She wore her shaggy hair loose around her shoulders.
The old man's shaggy beard made him look like a hermit.
