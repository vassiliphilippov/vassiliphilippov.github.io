replot

verb
to graph again
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈplɑt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈplɒt / (BrE)
to make a new plot or graph of data, especially after changes or corrections have been made

Examples

To replot the data

If the scale changes, you should replot the graph to reflect the new values.
The students replotted their results to show the updated trends more clearly.
