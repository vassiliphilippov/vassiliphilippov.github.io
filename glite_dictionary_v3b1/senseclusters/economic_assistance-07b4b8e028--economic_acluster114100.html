economic assistance

noun
financial or material help for support or development

help in the form of money or other resources given to support a person, group, or country in financial need or to promote economic development.

Examples

Economic assistance program
Following the disaster, neighboring countries offered economic assistance to help with recovery efforts.
Many developing nations rely on economic assistance from international organizations to improve infrastructure and services.
The government announced new economic assistance programs to help small businesses survive the recession.

Synonyms

  • economic aid
    3.2 0.87/m
    economic aid is often emergency or grant help given to countries, while economic assistance sounds more formal and program-focused over time.
  • financial assistance
    3.3 2.79/m
    financial assistance is money or material help for immediate needs, while economic assistance aims to boost long-term economic development.
  • financial support
    2.1 4.86/m
    financial support can fund a project or activity over time, while economic assistance focuses on helping economies or people in need.
  • financial aid
    2.6 4.41/m
    financial aid helps students or individuals with living or study costs, while economic assistance targets wider economic needs or development.
