overhappy

adjective
excessively happy
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈhæpi / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈhæpi / (BrE)
feeling or showing more happiness than is usual or expected

Examples

An overhappy smile
He was overhappy when he heard the news, almost unable to contain his excitement.
Some people thought she was overhappy about her promotion, celebrating for days.
The children became overhappy after receiving so many gifts at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecstatic - feeling or expressing overwhelming happiness
  • antonym: unhappy - not happy; sad or dissatisfied
