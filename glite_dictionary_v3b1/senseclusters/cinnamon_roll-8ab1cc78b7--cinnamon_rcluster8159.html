cinnamon roll

noun
very sweet, innocent person (slang)
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪn.ə.mən roʊl/ /; / /ˈsɪn.ə.mɪn roʊl/ (AmE), /ˈsɪn.ə.mən rəʊl/ /; / /ˈsɪn.ə.mɪn rəʊl/ (BrE)
informal slang for a person who is extremely kind, innocent, or emotionally pure; someone whom others view as sweet and unlikely to cause harm.

Examples

He is a cinnamon roll

They liked how the actor played a vulnerable father — truly a cinnamon roll in the film.
