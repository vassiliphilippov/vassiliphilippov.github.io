pantomimist

noun
actor performing using gestures only
Pronunciation: / ˈpæn.tə.mɪ.mɪst / (AmE), / ˈpæn.tə.mɪ.mɪst / (BrE)
an actor who performs and conveys meaning using gesture and facial expression without spoken words

Examples

A pantomimist actor
As a skilled pantomimist, she brought the story to life with just her movements.
The famous pantomimist captivated the audience without uttering a single word.
The festival featured a renowned pantomimist known for his expressive performances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mime - an actor who only uses movement and expression to perform
