popliteal vein

noun
vein behind the knee
6.7
Pronunciation: /pɑːp.lɪ.ˈtiː.əl veɪn/ /; / /pɑːp.lɪ.ˈtɪ.əl veɪn/ (AmE), /pɒp.lɪ.ˈtɪ.əl veɪn/ /; / /pɒp.lɪ.ˈtiː.əl veɪn/ (BrE)
