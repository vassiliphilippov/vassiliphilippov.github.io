tournant

noun
kitchen relief cook
Pronunciation: / tuːrˈnɑːnt / (AmE), / tʊəˈnɑ̃ / (BrE)
a cook in a professional kitchen who fills in at different stations as needed, acting as a relief or floater

Examples

Kitchen tournant
The tournant quickly adapted to each station, helping wherever the kitchen needed extra hands.
When the grill chef called in sick, the tournant stepped in to keep service running smoothly.
A skilled tournant must be familiar with every part of the kitchen operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: floater - a worker who fills in at various positions as needed
  • antonym: station chef - a chef responsible for a specific area of production in a kitchen
