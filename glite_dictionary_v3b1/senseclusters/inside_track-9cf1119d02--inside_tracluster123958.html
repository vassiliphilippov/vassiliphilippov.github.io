inside track

noun
inner side of a racecourse curve
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.saɪd træk / (AmE), / ˈɪn.saɪd træk / (BrE)
the inner side of a curved racecourse, especially where the distance is shortest

Examples

The inside track of the race

She started the race in lane one, closest to the inside track.
The inside track often gives runners an advantage on curved sections of the course.
