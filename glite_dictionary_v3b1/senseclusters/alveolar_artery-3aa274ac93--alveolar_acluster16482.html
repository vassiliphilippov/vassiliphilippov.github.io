alveolar artery

noun
artery supplying the jaw's alveolar process
7.3
Pronunciation: /æl.ˈviː.ə.lər ˈɑːr.tə.riː/ /; / /æl.ˈvɪə.lər ˈɑːr.tə.riː/ (AmE), /æl.ˈviː.ə.lə ˈɑː.tə.ri/ /; / /æl.ˈvɪə.lə ˈɑː.tə.ri/ (BrE)
an artery, branching from the maxillary artery, that supplies blood to the alveolar process of the jaw

Examples
