undelivered

adjective
ineffective or incomplete
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈlɪvərd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈlɪvəd / (BrE)
not having produced the intended effect, result, or emotional impact; ineffective or incomplete.

Examples

Undelivered message
The speech was powerful, but the final message remained undelivered to the audience.
His apology felt undelivered, leaving everyone unsure of his sincerity.
The emotional impact of the film was undelivered due to weak acting in the last scene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ineffective - not producing the intended result
  • antonym: successful - achieving the intended result
