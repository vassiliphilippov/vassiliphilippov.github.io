Synonyms

  • tear
    1.0 10/m
    tear is a common word for a ripped opening in cloth, while rent is a slightly older, formal term for such damage.
  • rip
    1.9 1.11/m
    rip means a sudden, forceful pull that makes a hole in fabric, while rent names a tear made by pulling.
  • split
    1.1 1.11/m
    split is a general break or crack often in hard things, while rent refers to a pulled tear in soft fabric or material.
