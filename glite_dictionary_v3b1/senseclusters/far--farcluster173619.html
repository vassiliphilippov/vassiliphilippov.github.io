far

adverb
remote in time
1.0 38/m
Pronunciation: / f…ër / (AmE), / f…ëÀê / (BrE)
at a remote point in time, either in the past or future

Examples

Far in the future
Few people can accurately predict events far in advance.
It's difficult to imagine life far into the next millennium.
Those traditions began far back in the twelfth century.

Synonyms

  • further
    1.1 0.04/m
    further implies a greater distance or later time relative to something, while far means a remote point in time.
