miseducation

noun
teaching incorrect information
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or result of teaching or being taught incorrect, misleading, or harmful information or values

Examples

The miseducation of students
The documentary highlights the miseducation of students in underfunded schools.
Years of miseducation can be difficult to correct later in life.
Activists are working to address the miseducation that leads to harmful stereotypes.
The miseducation of children can have long-lasting effects on their beliefs and opportunities.
He realized that years of miseducation had shaped his views about the world.
Efforts to correct miseducation often require changing deeply held misconceptions.

  • synonym: misinformation - false or inaccurate information
