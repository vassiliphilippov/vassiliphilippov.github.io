Strep pneumo

noun
abbreviation for Streptococcus pneumoniae bacterium
5.3
Pronunciation: / strɛp ˈnuːmoʊ / (AmE), / strɛp ˈnjuːməʊ / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for Streptococcus pneumoniae, a type of bacteria that can cause pneumonia and other respiratory infections

Examples

Strep pneumo bacteria
Many cases of bacterial pneumonia are caused by Strep pneumo.
The doctor ordered a blood test to check for Strep pneumo infection.
Vaccination against Strep pneumo is recommended for young children and older adults.
