cranker

noun
prank caller
Pronunciation: / ˈkræŋkər / (AmE), / ˈkraŋkə / (BrE)
a person who makes annoying, obscene, or threatening telephone calls, especially as a prank or harassment

Examples

Obscene call cranker
The police warned residents after a persistent cranker made several threatening calls overnight.
She was annoyed by a cranker who kept calling her late at night.
Authorities are trying to identify the cranker responsible for the series of obscene phone calls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crank caller - a person who makes prank or harassing phone calls
  • synonym: prankster - a person who plays practical jokes or pranks
