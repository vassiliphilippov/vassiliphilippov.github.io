heartbeat

noun
vital force or core of something
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrtˌbit / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtˌbiːt / (BrE)
the essential core or vital force of something, especially as the main source of activity or energy

Examples

Cultural heartbeat
London is considered the cultural heartbeat of the country.
Silicon Valley is often described as the heartbeat of the tech industry.
The volunteers are the heartbeat of our community organization.
