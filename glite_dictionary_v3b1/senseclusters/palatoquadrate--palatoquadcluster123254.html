palatoquadrate

noun
upper jaw cartilage
Pronunciation: / ˌpælətəʊˈkwɑːdreɪt / (AmE), / ˌpælətəʊˈkwɒdreɪt / (BrE)
a cartilage in the upper jaw of some vertebrates, especially fishes, that supports the jaw structure
