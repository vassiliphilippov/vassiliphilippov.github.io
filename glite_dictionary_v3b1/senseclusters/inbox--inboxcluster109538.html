inbox

verb
to send a message
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌbɒks / (BrE)
to send a private electronic message to someone, typically through a social media or messaging platform.

Examples

To inbox a friend
If you have any questions, just inbox me and I'll reply as soon as I can.
She asked him to inbox the photos instead of posting them publicly.
You can inbox your feedback directly to our support team for a faster response.
