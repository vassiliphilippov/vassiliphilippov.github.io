unhydrated

adjective
lacking body water
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhaɪdreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhaɪdreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having enough water in the body or lacking the normal amount of bodily water needed for healthy function

Examples

Unhydrated body
After hiking all day in the sun, she was still unhydrated despite drinking some water.
Doctors warn that being unhydrated can lead to headaches and fatigue.
He realized he was unhydrated when he started feeling dizzy during the workout.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dehydrated - having lost too much water from the body
  • antonym: hydrated - having enough water in the body
