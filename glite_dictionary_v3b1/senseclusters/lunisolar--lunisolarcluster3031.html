lunisolar

adjective
of both the sun and the moon
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌluː.niˈsoʊ.lɚ / (AmE), / ˌluː.niˈsəʊ.lə / (BrE)
Relating to or calculated by the combined movements or positions of the moon and the sun.

Examples

Lunisolar calendar system
Astronomers studied the lunisolar influences on tidal patterns.
Many ancient calendars were based on lunisolar cycles that combined the movements of the moon and the sun.
The lunisolar year prevents holidays from drifting far from their appropriate seasons.
