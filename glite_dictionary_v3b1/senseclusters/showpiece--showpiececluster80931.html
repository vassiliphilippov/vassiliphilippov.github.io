showpiece

noun
outstanding item in a collection
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊˌpis / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌpiːs / (BrE)
The most outstanding or featured item in a collection, exhibition, or display.

Examples

The showpiece dessert
The chef prepared a special dessert as the showpiece of the evening's menu.
The painting was clearly the showpiece of the entire exhibition.
Their new electric car served as the showpiece at the auto show.
