muscadine

noun
a grape variety from the southern U.S.
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌs.kəˌdaɪn /; / ˈmʌskəˌdaɪn / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.kəˌdaɪn /; / ˈmʌskəˌdaɪn / (BrE)
a type of grape native to the southern United States, often used for making wine, jelly, juice, and preserves
