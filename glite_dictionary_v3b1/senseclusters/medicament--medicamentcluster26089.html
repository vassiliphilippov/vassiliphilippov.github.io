medicament

noun
substance treating or preventing disease
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪkəmənt / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəmənt / (BrE)
A substance used to treat, prevent, or relieve the symptoms of a disease or medical condition.

Examples

A medicament for pain
Pharmacists must ensure the medicament is stored at the correct temperature.
The doctor prescribed a new medicament to help control her symptoms.
This medicament should be taken twice daily after meals.
