Saint Johns

proper noun
river in northeastern Florida
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈdʒɑnz / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈdʒɒnz / (BrE)
a river in northeastern Florida that flows northward through Jacksonville and empties into the Atlantic Ocean

Examples

Saint Johns River
