hyperlink

verb
to add a clickable link
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪpərˌlɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈhaɪpəlɪŋk / (BrE)
to add a clickable link to text, an image, or a document that connects to another webpage or resource

Examples

To hyperlink a word
She hyperlinked each reference in her report to the relevant website for easy access.
Before publishing the blog post, he hyperlinked all the product names to their official pages.
The teacher asked students to hyperlink their sources in the online assignment.
