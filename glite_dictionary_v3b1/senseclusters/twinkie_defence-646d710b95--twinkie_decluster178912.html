Twinkie defence

noun
legal defense citing diminished responsibility
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪŋki dɪˌfɛns / (AmE), / ˈtwɪŋki dɪˌfɛns / (BrE)
a legal defense in which a defendant claims that an unusual circumstance, such as consumption of junk food or emotional distress, diminished their responsibility for a crime
