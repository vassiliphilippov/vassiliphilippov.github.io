silkworm

noun
caterpillar that produces silk
2.1 3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlkwɜrm /; / ˈsɪlkˌwɝm / (AmE), / ˈsɪlkwəːm /; / ˈsɪlkˌwɜːm / (BrE)
the caterpillar (larva) of a moth, especially Bombyx mori, that produces silk used for making textiles

Examples

The silkworm caterpillar

Farmers in China have been raising silkworms for thousands of years.
The silkworm spins a cocoon from a single thread of raw silk.
The children watched a silkworm eating mulberry leaves in their science class.
