metrist

noun
poetry meter expert
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtrɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies, analyzes, or writes about the rhythmic structure of poetry

Examples

Poetry metrist
The metrist explained how the poem's rhythm created a musical effect.
As a skilled metrist, she could identify subtle changes in meter across different verses.
Literary critics often consult a metrist when analyzing complex poetic forms.

Related Terms
