to shine with a bright, shimmering, reflected light, as if wet or covered in small sparkles

Examples

Stars glitter at night
Dewdrops glittered like tiny jewels on the grass.
Her diamond ring glittered on her finger, catching everyone's eye.
The lake began to glitter under the afternoon sun.

Synonyms

  • glisten
    4.1 6.59/m
    glisten is a soft, wet-looking shine on surfaces like leaves, while glitter is brighter and shows many small sparkles.
  • shimmer
    4.3 6.31/m
    shimmer is a soft, wavering light that seems to move, while glitter is brighter and gives many sharp sparkles.
  • gleam
    1.9 9.49/m
    gleam is a clear, smooth reflection of light from one area, while glitter breaks light into many tiny sparkles.
  • shine
    1.0 24/m
    shine is a general state of being bright or glossy, while glitter is a specific, sparkly, shimmering kind of shine.
