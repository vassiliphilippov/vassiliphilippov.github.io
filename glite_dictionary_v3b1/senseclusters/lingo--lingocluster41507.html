lingo

noun
group-specific jargon or language
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
the characteristic language, jargon, or vocabulary used by a particular group, profession, or community, often difficult for outsiders to understand

Examples

Technical lingo
I couldn't understand the technical lingo the doctors were using.
She picked up some local lingo during her travels abroad.
Teenagers often invent their own lingo that baffles their parents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jargon - specialized language used by a particular group
  • synonym: slang - very informal language specific to groups
