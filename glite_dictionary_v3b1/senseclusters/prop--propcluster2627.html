prop

noun
object used to support something
6.5 2.8
Pronunciation: /prɑːp/ (AmE), /prɒp/ (BrE)
an object placed under or against something to support it and prevent it from falling or shaking

Examples

A metal prop
A metal prop held the heavy stage scenery in place during the performance.
The worker placed a prop under the ladder to make sure it was stable.
They used a wooden prop to keep the door open during the summer breeze.
