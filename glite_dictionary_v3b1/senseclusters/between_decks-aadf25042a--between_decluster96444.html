between decks

adverb
in the space between a ship's decks
Pronunciation: / bɪˈtwin dɛks / (AmE), / bɪˈtwiːn dɛks / (BrE)
in the space between the decks of a ship, usually referring to the area below the main deck and above the lowest deck.

Examples
