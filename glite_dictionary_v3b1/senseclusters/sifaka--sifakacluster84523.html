sifaka

noun
leaping Madagascan lemur
Pronunciation: / sɪˈfɑːkə / (AmE), / sɪˈfɑːkə / (BrE)
a type of lemur from Madagascar with a long tail, known for its distinctive leaping movement between trees

Examples

The sifaka lemur
The sifaka uses its strong legs to leap gracefully from branch to branch.
Tourists in Madagascar are often excited to spot a wild sifaka in the forest.
A group of sifakas rested in the shade after a morning of energetic jumping.

Related Terms
