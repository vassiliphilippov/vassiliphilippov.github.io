mineral

noun
naturally occurring inorganic solid
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnərəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪnərəl / (BrE)
a naturally occurring solid substance that is not organic and has a definite chemical composition and structure

Examples

A mineral crystal
Quartz is a common mineral found in many types of rocks.
The geologist identified several rare minerals in the sample.
Water dissolves some minerals from the soil, enriching its content.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rock - a naturally occurring solid aggregate of minerals
