Dendroica

noun
genus of New World warblers
Pronunciation: / dɛnˈdroʊɪkə /; / dɛnˈdrɔɪkə / (AmE), / dɛnˈdrɔɪkə / (BrE)
a former genus of New World warblers (small songbirds) in the family Parulidae, now usually included in the genus Setophaga
