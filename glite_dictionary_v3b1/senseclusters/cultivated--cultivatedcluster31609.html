cultivated

adjective
refined and polished in taste or manners
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltɪˌveɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd / (BrE)
having or showing good education, manners, and refinement in taste

Examples

Cultivated manners
He spoke with a cultivated accent that impressed everyone at the dinner party.
Her cultivated manners made her stand out in any social gathering.
The author's cultivated taste in literature was evident from his eloquent reviews.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cultured - having good education and refined tastes
  • synonym: genteel - polite, refined, or respectable in an affected or ostentatious way
  • antonym: uncouth - lacking good manners, refinement, or grace
