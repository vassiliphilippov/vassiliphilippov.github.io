rhapsodist

noun
composer of rhapsodies
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈræpsədɪst / (AmE), / ˈræpsədɪst / (BrE)
a musician, composer, or performer who creates or performs rhapsodies, which are free-form and expressive musical pieces

Examples

Rhapsodist at the piano
The talented rhapsodist captivated the audience with his passionate piano performance.
As a renowned rhapsodist, she was known for her improvisational style and emotional depth.
The festival featured a rhapsodist who composed original pieces inspired by folk melodies.

  • synonym: performer - a person who presents music or art to an audience
