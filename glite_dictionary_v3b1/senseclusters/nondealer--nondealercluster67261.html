nondealer

noun
not a dealer
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈdiːlər / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈdiːlə / (BrE)
a person or entity that is not acting as a dealer in a transaction, game, or activity

Examples

Nondealer waits
In this round, the nondealer must wait until the dealer finishes shuffling the cards.
The rules state that the nondealer cannot place bets until their turn comes.
During the transaction, the nondealer observed the process but did not participate directly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dealer - a person who distributes cards or manages transactions
  • synonym: player - a participant in a game who is not the dealer
