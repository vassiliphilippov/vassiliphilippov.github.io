impedance

noun
electrical resistance to current flow
7.9 0.89/m

The opposition that a material offers to the flow of an electric current, measured in ohms.

Examples

Impedance of speaker
A speaker with incorrect impedance may cause damage to the amplifier.
High impedance materials are often used as insulators in electronic devices.
To ensure optimal performance, the engineers measured the impedance of each circuit component.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conductance - the ease with which electricity flows through a material
  • synonym: resistance - the opposition to current flow in an electrical circuit
