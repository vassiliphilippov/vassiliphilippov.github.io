weather side

noun
side facing the wind
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛðɚ saɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə saɪd / (BrE)
the side of something, especially a ship or building, that is facing or exposed to the wind

Examples

The weather side of a boat
Always check the weather side of the boat for signs of approaching squalls.
He positioned the tent with the entrance away from the weather side for shelter.
The trees on the weather side of the hill appeared more windswept and stunted.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lee side - the side sheltered from the wind
