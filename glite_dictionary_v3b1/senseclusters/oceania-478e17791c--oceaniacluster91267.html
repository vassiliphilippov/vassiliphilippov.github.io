Oceania

proper noun
region of islands in the South Pacific
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊsiˈæniə / (AmE), / ˌəʊsiˈɑːniə / (BrE)
a region consisting of many islands in the South Pacific Ocean, including Melanesia, Micronesia, and Polynesia, and sometimes Australasia and the Malay Archipelago
