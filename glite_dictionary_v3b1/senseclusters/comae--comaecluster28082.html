comae

noun
plural of coma (astronomy)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊmiː / (AmE), / ˈkəʊmiː / (BrE)
the plural form of coma, referring to the nebulous envelopes surrounding the nuclei of comets

Examples

Comet comae
Astronomers observed several comae in the images of distant comets.
The brightness of the comae made it difficult to see the comet nuclei clearly.
Different comae can vary in size and composition depending on the comet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coma - the nebulous envelope around the nucleus of a comet
