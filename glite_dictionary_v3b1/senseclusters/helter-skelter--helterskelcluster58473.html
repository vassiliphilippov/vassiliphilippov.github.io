helter-skelter

adjective, adverb
in disorder or confusion
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛltɚˈskɛltɚ /; / ˌhɛltərˈskɛltər / (AmE), / ˌhɛltəˈskɛltə / (BrE)
in a disorderly, confused, or chaotic way or state
