graceful

adjective
elegant and beautiful in movement or form
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪs.fəl / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪs.fəl / (BrE)
having elegant, smooth, and attractive movement, style, or manner

Examples

A graceful movement
A swan glided graceful across the still surface of the lake.

Her graceful gestures made her seem perfectly at ease in high society.
The graceful lines of the mansion spoke of old money and refined taste.
The restaurant's graceful decor attracted wealthy patrons seeking an elegant atmosphere.
