acarophobia

noun
fear of small insects or mites
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˌkɛrəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / əˌkærəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
An extreme or irrational fear of small insects, mites, or worms.

Examples

Acarophobia of mites
After seeing a documentary about mites, his acarophobia grew worse.
Cognitive therapy helped him overcome his acarophobia.
Her acarophobia made her avoid gardens and parks altogether.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entomophobia - fear of insects
  • degree lower: phobia - an extreme or irrational fear of something
