piezo

adjective
pressure-generated electricity
9.9
Pronunciation: / piˈeɪzoʊ / (AmE), / piˈɛzəʊ / (BrE)
relating to the generation of electricity by applying pressure to certain materials

Examples

Piezo material sensor
The engineer installed a piezo device to convert vibrations into electrical signals.
Many modern lighters use a piezo element to create a spark.
A piezo sensor can detect even slight changes in pressure on the surface.
