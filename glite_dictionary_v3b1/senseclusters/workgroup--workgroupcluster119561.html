workgroup

noun
networked computer group
3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈwɜːrk.gruːp/ (AmE), /ˈwɜːk.gruːp/ (BrE)
a group of computers on the same local network that share files, printers, or other resources

Examples

Computer workgroup
Each department set up a workgroup to make sharing documents and printers easier.
The IT manager created a new workgroup for the marketing team's computers.
Joining the workgroup allowed all the laptops to access the shared office printer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domain - a collection of computers managed as a unit in a network
  • synonym: network - a group of interconnected computers
