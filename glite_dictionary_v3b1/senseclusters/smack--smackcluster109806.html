smack

noun
distinctive flavor or taste
Pronunciation: / smæk / (AmE), / smæk / (BrE)
a strong or distinctive taste, especially one imparted by a condiment or seasoning

Examples

A smack of lemon
She detected a smack of lemon zest in the salad dressing.
The soup had a smack of garlic that lingered pleasantly on the tongue.
There was just the right smack of saltiness in the sauce.
