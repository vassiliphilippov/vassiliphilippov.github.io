adenopathy

noun
disease or swelling of glands
Pronunciation: / ˌæd.əˈnɑp.ə.θi / (AmE), / ˌad.ɪˈnɒp.ə.θi / (BrE)
a disease or swelling of glandular tissue, especially of the lymph nodes

Examples

Adenopathy of lymph nodes
Doctors investigated the cause of her persistent adenopathy to rule out infection or malignancy.
Enlarged lymph nodes are often a sign of adenopathy in the neck or armpit.
The patient was diagnosed with adenopathy after an ultrasound revealed swollen lymph nodes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lymphadenopathy - disease of the lymph nodes
