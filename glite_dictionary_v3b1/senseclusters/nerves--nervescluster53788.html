nerves

noun
anxiety; nervousness
Pronunciation: / nɝːvz / (AmE), / nɜːvz / (BrE)
feelings of anxiety or nervousness, or the ability to stay calm in stressful situations

Examples

Control your nerves
Deep breaths can help you control your nerves in stressful situations.
Just before the interview, his nerves started to get the better of him.
She always gets a case of nerves before giving a presentation.
Despite the pressure, he managed to keep his nerves under control.
She showed remarkable nerves during the crisis.
The surgeon needed steady nerves to perform the delicate operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry or unease
  • antonym: calm - a state of being relaxed and free from anxiety
  • synonym: composure - calmness and self-control, especially in a difficult situation
  • synonym: self-control - the ability to control one's emotions and actions
