triumphalist

noun
one who boasts victory
8.3
Pronunciation: / traɪˈʌmfəlɪst / (AmE), / traɪˈʌmfəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who proudly celebrates or proclaims their own or their group's victory or superiority, often excessively

Examples

A triumphalist leader
After the election, the triumphalist could not stop talking about his party's overwhelming victory.
Critics accused the coach of being a triumphalist after his team won the championship.
Her triumphalist attitude made it hard for others to share in her success.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braggart - a person who boasts about achievements or possessions
  • antonym: modest person - someone who does not boast about their achievements
