tourism

noun
the travel business for leisure
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊr.ɪ.zəm /; / ˈtʊrɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈtʊə.rɪ.zəm /; / ˈtʊərɪzəm / (BrE)
the activity or business of traveling to different places for pleasure, leisure, or learning, and providing services for travelers

Examples

Tourism industry
Tourism makes up a significant part of the economy in many coastal resorts.
High levels of tourism can sometimes cause environmental challenges for local communities.
The government launched initiatives to promote sustainable tourism in rural areas.
Tourism is a major source of income for many countries around the world.
The city has invested in new attractions to boost tourism and attract more visitors.
Sustainable tourism aims to protect the environment while supporting local communities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: commuting - regular travel between home and work
  • synonym: travel - the act of going from one place to another, especially over a distance
