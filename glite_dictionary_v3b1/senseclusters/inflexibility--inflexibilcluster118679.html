inflexibility

noun
rigid or unyielding quality
6.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn.flek.sə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɪn.flek.sɪ.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ɪn.flek.sə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɪn.flek.sɪ.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
