liquidly

adverb
with financial liquidity
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪdli / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪdli / (BrE)
in a manner that is easily converted into cash or shows financial liquidity.

Examples

Liquidly traded assets
The assets in the fund can be traded liquidly on the open market.
She prefers investments that can be sold liquidly if needed.
Because the shares are liquidly held, investors can exit at any time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: illiquidly - in a way that is not easily converted into cash
  • synonym: readily - quickly and easily
