charge up

verb
restore electrical power to a device
Pronunciation: / tʃɑrdʒ ʌp / (AmE), / tʃɑːdʒ ʌp / (BrE)
to restore electrical power to a battery or electronic device by connecting it to a power source

Examples

Charge up the phone
Don't forget to charge up the camera batteries before the hike.
I forgot to charge up my electric toothbrush last night, so the battery died this morning.
She needs to charge up her phone before leaving for the day.
