earner

noun
person who earns money
2.0 5.10/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ùÀên…ö / (AmE), / Àà…úÀên…ô / (BrE)
a person who earns money, typically by working for wages or a salary

Examples

A earner of money
As a part-time earner, he supplements the family's income.
She is the highest earner in her department.
The company rewards every top earner with a bonus at the end of the year.

Synonyms

    2.8 1.39/m

  • employee
    1.3 144/m
    an employee is hired and paid by an organization, while an earner can be anyone who earns money, not only employees.
