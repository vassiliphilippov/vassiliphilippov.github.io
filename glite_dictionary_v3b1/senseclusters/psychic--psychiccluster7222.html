psychic

noun
person claiming supernatural perception
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪkɪk / (AmE), / ˈsʌɪkɪk / (BrE)
a person who claims to have abilities to perceive information beyond the normal human senses, such as telepathy or clairvoyance

Examples

A psychic reader
She consulted a psychic to ask about her future career.
Skeptics often challenge the claims made by self-proclaimed psychics.
The TV show invited a well-known psychic to predict the winner of the election.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medium - a person who claims to communicate with spirits of the dead
