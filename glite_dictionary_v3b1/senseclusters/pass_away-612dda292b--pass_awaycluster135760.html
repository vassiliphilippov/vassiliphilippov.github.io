pass away

verb
to die (euphemistic)
Pronunciation: / pæs əˈweɪ / (AmE), / pɑːs əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to die; to cease living, especially expressed in a gentle or respectful way

Examples

Pass away peacefully
Her beloved pet passed away peacefully in its sleep at the age of fifteen.
Many people gathered to honor him after he passed away last month.
When his grandmother passed away, he inherited the family home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: die - to stop living; to cease to exist as a living being
  • synonym: expire - to die; to come to an end of life
  • synonym: kick the bucket - to die (informal, idiomatic)
