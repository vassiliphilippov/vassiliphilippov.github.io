semimetallic

adjective
partly metallic
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˌməˈtælɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˌmɛˈtælɪk / (BrE)
having a surface appearance, color, or sheen that is somewhat like metal but not fully metallic

Examples

Semimetallic paint finish
The artist chose a semimetallic paint to give the sculpture a subtle shine.
Her dress shimmered with a semimetallic blue that caught the light beautifully.
The car's semimetallic finish made it stand out without being too flashy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: matte - having a dull, non-shiny surface
  • degree higher: metallic - having the appearance or qualities of metal
