myxolipoma

noun
benign mixed tumor
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪksoʊlaɪˈpoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌmɪksəʊlɪˈpəʊmə / (BrE)
a benign tumor made up of both myxoid (gelatinous) and fatty tissue

Examples

A myxolipoma tumor
The surgeon removed a myxolipoma from the patient's upper arm.
A myxolipoma is usually non-cancerous and grows slowly.
The MRI scan showed that the mass was a myxolipoma rather than a malignant tumor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lipoma - a benign tumor of fatty tissue
  • synonym: myxoma - a benign tumor of myxoid (gelatinous) tissue
