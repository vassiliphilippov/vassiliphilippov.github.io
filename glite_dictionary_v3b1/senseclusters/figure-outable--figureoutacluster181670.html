figure-outable

adjective
able to be figured out or solved
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪɡjɚˈaʊɾəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌfɪɡjəˈaʊtəbl / (BrE)
able to be figured out or solved; capable of being understood or resolved with effort

Examples

A figure-outable problem
Despite the complexity of the instructions, she believed the project was figure-outable.
Don't panic—anything is figure-outable when you take a deep breath and think logically.
Most everyday problems are figure-outable if you break them into smaller parts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: incomprehensible - impossible to understand
  • synonym: solvable - able to be solved
