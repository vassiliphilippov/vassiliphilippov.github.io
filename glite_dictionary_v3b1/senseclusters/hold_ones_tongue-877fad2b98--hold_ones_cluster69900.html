hold one's tongue

verb
remain silent; refrain from speaking
2.6 4.4
Pronunciation: /hoʊld wʌnz tʌŋ/ /; / /hoʊld wənz tʌŋ/ (AmE), /həʊld wʌnz tʌŋ/ /; / /həʊld wənz tʌŋ/ (BrE)
