snapper

noun
party favor that pops when pulled
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæpər / (AmE), / ˈsnæpə / (BrE)
A party favor in the form of a paper roll, typically containing candy or a small gift, that makes a popping sound when pulled at both ends.

Examples

Party snapper toy
Each child at the party received a snapper filled with a tiny toy and some sweets.
She pulled her snapper and laughed at the loud pop and surprise inside.
The table was decorated with colorful snappers for everyone to enjoy.
