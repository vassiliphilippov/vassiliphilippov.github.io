snowberry

noun
shrub with pink flowers and white berries
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌbɛri / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub native to western North America, with spikes of pink flowers and clusters of round white berries.

Examples

The snowberry shrub
Birds are often attracted to the snowberry because of its clusters of berries in autumn.
The garden was filled with snowberry bushes, their white fruit standing out against the green leaves.
We planted a snowberry to add winter interest to the backyard.
