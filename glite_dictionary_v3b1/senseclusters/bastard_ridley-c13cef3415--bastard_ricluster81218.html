bastard ridley

noun
a small sea turtle of North America
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbæ.stərd ˈrɪd.liː/ (AmE), /ˈbɑː.stəd ˈrɪd.liː/ (BrE)
A small grey sea turtle (Lepidochelys kempii) found along the Atlantic and Gulf Coasts of North America, also known as Kemp's ridley.

Examples

The bastard ridley turtle
Conservationists are working to protect the nesting sites of the bastard ridley along the Gulf coast.
It is rare to see a bastard ridley turtle, as their population has drastically declined.
The bastard ridley is currently the most endangered sea turtle species in the world.
