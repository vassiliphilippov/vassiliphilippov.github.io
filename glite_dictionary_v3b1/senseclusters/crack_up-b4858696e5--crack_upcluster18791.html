crack up

verb
to laugh uncontrollably
Pronunciation: / kræk ʌp / (AmE), / kræk ʌp / (BrE)
to laugh loudly and uncontrollably, especially because something is very funny

Examples

To crack up laughing
I cracked up so hard I could barely breathe.
She always cracks up when she watches that movie.
The comedian’s joke made the whole audience crack up with laughter.
Every time he tells that story, I just crack up.
She tried to keep a straight face, but ended up cracking up anyway.
The movie was so funny that the whole audience cracked up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burst out laughing - begin to laugh suddenly and loudly
  • degree lower: giggle - to laugh lightly in a silly way
