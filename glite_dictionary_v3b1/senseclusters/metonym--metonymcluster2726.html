metonym

noun
word used for something related
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛt̬.ə.nɪm / (AmE), / ˈmɛt.ə.nɪm / (BrE)
A word or expression used to refer to something by naming something else closely associated with it.

Examples

Metonym for Hollywood
'Hollywood' is often used as a metonym for the American film industry.
In the phrase 'The White House issued a statement,' 'White House' is a metonym for the president and staff.
Using 'wheels' as a metonym for 'car' is common in everyday conversation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: metaphor - figure of speech involving comparison for effect
  • synonym: synecdoche - a figure of speech in which a part is used for the whole or vice versa
