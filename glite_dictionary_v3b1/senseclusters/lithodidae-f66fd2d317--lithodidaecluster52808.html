Lithodidae

noun
family of deep-sea king crabs

Pronunciation: / lɪˈθɑdəˌdiː / (AmE), / lɪˈθɒdɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of large, deep-sea crabs, commonly referred to as king crabs, found in cold waters.
