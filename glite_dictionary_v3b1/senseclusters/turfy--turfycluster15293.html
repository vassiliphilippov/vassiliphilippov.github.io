turfy

adjective
like turf or grass
4.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…ùÀêfi / (AmE), / Ààt…úÀêfi / (BrE)
covered with turf, or having the appearance, texture, or smell of turf or grass.

Examples

Turfy ground
After the rain, the garden had a pleasant turfy aroma that reminded her of spring.
They spread their picnic blanket on the soft, turfy ground near the lake.
The golf course was famous for its lush, turfy fairways and well-kept greens.

Synonyms

  • grassy
    1.0 3.85/m
    grassy is covered in lots of living grass and often describes a green look or smell, while turfy means a turf-like feel and scent.
  • grass-covered
    2.6 0.09/m
    grass-covered describes a surface actually covered by grass, while turfy suggests a turf-like feel, smell, and denser texture.
