ebullient

adjective
cheerfully enthusiastic, exuberant
Pronunciation: / ɪˈbʊliənt / (AmE), / ɪˈbʌl.i.ənt / (BrE)
full of energy, enthusiasm, and cheerful excitement; joyously unrestrained

Examples

An ebullient smile
He greeted us with an ebullient smile that brightened the whole room.
Her ebullient personality made her the life of every party.
The ebullient crowd cheered wildly as the band took the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exuberant - full of energy, excitement, and cheerfulness
  • antonym: restrained - characterized by reserve or moderation
