teletype

verb
to send by teletype
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌtaɪp / (BrE)
to send or write a message using a teletype machine or similar device

Examples

To teletype a report
The operator will teletype the weather report to all stations every morning.
Before email, companies would teletype important updates to their overseas offices.
They teletyped the urgent message to the newsroom as soon as it arrived.
The operator teletyped the weather report to all stations along the route.
Before email, news agencies often teletyped urgent updates to their offices worldwide.
She teletyped the official announcement to the embassy late at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fax - to send a document electronically over a telephone line
  • synonym: telegraph - to send a message using a telegraph system
