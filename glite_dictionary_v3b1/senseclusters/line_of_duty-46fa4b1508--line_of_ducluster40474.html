line of duty

noun
scope of one's official duties
4.3
Pronunciation: / laɪn əv ˈduːti / (AmE), / laɪn əv ˈdjuːti / (BrE)
the range of tasks or responsibilities that are normally required as part of one's job or official role

Examples

In the line of duty
Many firefighters have lost their lives in the line of duty.
She always goes beyond the line of duty to help her colleagues.
The officer was injured in the line of duty during a rescue operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duty - something that one is required to do as part of a job or position
