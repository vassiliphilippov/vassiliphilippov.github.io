genus Agathis

noun
a genus of kauri pine trees
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs əˈɡæθɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs əˈɡæθɪs / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen coniferous trees known as kauri pines, native to the southern hemisphere, especially Australasia and Southeast Asia.

Examples

Botanists discovered a new member of the genus Agathis in Southeast Asian forests.
Several species within the genus Agathis are valued for their high-quality timber and resin.
The genus Agathis includes some of the most ancient trees in the world.
