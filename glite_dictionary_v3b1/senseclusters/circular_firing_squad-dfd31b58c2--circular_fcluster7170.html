circular firing squad

noun
group undermining itself through infighting
8.8 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…ùÀê.kj…ô.l…ö Ààfa…™.…π…™≈ã skw…ëd / (AmE), / Ààs…úÀê.kj ä.l…ô Ààfa…™…ô.r…™≈ã skw…íd / (BrE)
a situation in which members of a group harm or undermine each other, often through mutual criticism or blame, instead of working together against a common challenge

Examples

A circular firing squad meeting
During the meeting, the committee turned into a circular firing squad, with members blaming each other for the failure.
Political parties sometimes form a circular firing squad when facing a scandal, damaging their own chances.
We can't afford a circular firing squad if we want this project to succeed; we need to cooperate.
