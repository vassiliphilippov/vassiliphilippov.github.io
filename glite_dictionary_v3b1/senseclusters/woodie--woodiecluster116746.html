woodie

noun
golf club with wooden head
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdi / (AmE), / ˈwʊdi / (BrE)
a golf club with a wooden head, traditionally used for long-distance shots, now mostly replaced by metal clubs

Examples

A woodie golf club
He found an old woodie in his grandfather's golf bag and decided to try it on the course.
Many modern golfers have never used a woodie because metal clubs are now standard.
The vintage woodie was displayed as part of the golf club's history exhibit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: driver - a type of golf club used for long-distance shots
  • antonym: iron - a golf club with a metal head used for shorter shots
