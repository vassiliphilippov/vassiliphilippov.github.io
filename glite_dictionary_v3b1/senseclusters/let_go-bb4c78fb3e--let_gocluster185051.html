let go

verb
relax, stop worrying
2.3 3.7
Pronunciation: / lɛt ɡoʊ / (AmE), / lɛt ɡəʊ / (BrE)
to stop worrying, relax, or stop being emotionally attached to something

Examples

To let go of stress
After a long week, she tried to let go and have fun at the party.
It's important to let go when you're on vacation and enjoy yourself.
Sometimes you just need to let go of your fears and trust the process.
After months of worrying, she finally decided to let go of her fears and move forward.
It was a difficult decision for the manager to let go several employees during the restructuring.
You'll feel better once you learn to let go of past mistakes.
