stopblock

noun
safety barrier
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑpˌblɑk / (AmE), / ˈstɒpˌblɒk / (BrE)
a block or barrier placed to prevent something, such as a vehicle or object, from moving beyond a certain point

Examples

Railway stopblock
The train slowed as it approached the stopblock at the end of the track.
A heavy stopblock was installed to prevent carts from rolling off the platform.
Maintenance workers checked the stopblock regularly to ensure it remained secure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buffer - a device that reduces impact or prevents movement beyond a point
