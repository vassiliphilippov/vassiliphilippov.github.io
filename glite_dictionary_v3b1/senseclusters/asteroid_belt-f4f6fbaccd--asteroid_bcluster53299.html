asteroid belt

noun
region in space with many asteroids
Pronunciation: / ˈæstəˌrɔɪd bɛlt / (AmE), / ˈæstərɔɪd bɛlt / (BrE)
the region of the Solar System between Mars and Jupiter where most asteroids are found orbiting the Sun

Examples

The asteroid belt
Most asteroids in our Solar System are located within the asteroid belt.
Scientists study the asteroid belt to learn about the early Solar System.
The asteroid belt contains thousands of rocky bodies orbiting the Sun.
