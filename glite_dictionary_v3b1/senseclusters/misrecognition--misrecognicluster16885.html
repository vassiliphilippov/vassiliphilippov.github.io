misrecognition

noun
failure to acknowledge
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˌrɛkəɡˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˌrɛkəɡˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
the failure to acknowledge or respect someone's identity, status, rights, or contributions

Examples

Misrecognition of achievements
The misrecognition of her achievements led to her feeling undervalued at work.
Social movements often arise in response to the misrecognition of certain groups.
Repeated misrecognition of his efforts eventually caused him to leave the organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disregard - lack of consideration or respect for something
  • antonym: recognition - the act of acknowledging or respecting someone or something
