unbeholden

adjective
not under obligation or indebted
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnbɪˈhoʊldən / (AmE), / ˌʌnbɪˈhəʊldən / (BrE)
Not owing anything to anyone; not under moral or social obligation to someone.

Examples

Completely unbeholden to others
His vote was seen as completely unbeholden, free from outside influence or pressure.
She prides herself on being unbeholden to any political party.
The independent artist remained unbeholden to record labels, funding her own projects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beholden - under obligation or indebted to someone
