MSL

noun
NASA Mars mission
6.2
Pronunciation: / ɛm ɛs ɛl / (AmE), / ɛm ɛs ɛl / (BrE)
the Mars Science Laboratory, a NASA mission that sent the Curiosity rover to explore Mars

Examples

MSL mission
The MSL mission greatly expanded our understanding of the Martian surface and climate.
Scientists analyzed data sent by MSL to search for signs of past water on Mars.
After landing in 2012, MSL began its exploration of Gale Crater.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Curiosity - the rover sent to Mars by the Mars Science Laboratory mission
  • antonym: Perseverance - a NASA rover sent to Mars after Curiosity
