coast live oak

noun
evergreen oak tree native to western North America
9.9 6.6
Pronunciation: /koʊst laɪv oʊk/ /; / /koʊst lɪv oʊk/ (AmE), /kəʊst laɪv əʊk/ /; / /kəʊst lɪv əʊk/ (BrE)
