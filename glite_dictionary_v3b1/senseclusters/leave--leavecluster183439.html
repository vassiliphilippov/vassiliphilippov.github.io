leave

verb
entrust to someone
Pronunciation: / liv / (AmE), / liːv / (BrE)
to put something or someone into the care or responsibility of another person

Examples

To leave a child with
I will leave my dog with my neighbor while I am on vacation.
Parents often leave their children in the care of relatives during emergencies.
She left her responsibilities to her assistant before going on leave.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entrust - to assign the responsibility for doing something to someone
  • antonym: relinquish - to give up control of something
