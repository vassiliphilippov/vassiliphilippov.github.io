figure of eight

noun
shape resembling the number eight
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪɡjər əv eɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡə əv eɪt / (BrE)
a shape or pattern resembling the number eight, typically made by a continuous line crossing itself

Examples

Figure of eight pattern
She tied the rope into a figure of eight before securing it to the post.
The children enjoyed drawing a figure of eight in the sand with a stick.
The skater traced a perfect figure of eight on the ice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: figure-eight - the same shape, chiefly American spelling
