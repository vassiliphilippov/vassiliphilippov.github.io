wash

noun
thin coat of water-based paint
Pronunciation: / wɑʃ / (AmE), / wɒʃ / (BrE)
a thin layer or coating of paint or color applied to a surface (especially in art or painting)

Examples

A blue wash
The artist added a pale wash of blue to suggest the sky.
The mural was finished with a transparent wash to soften the colors.
You can create subtle shading by applying a light wash over the drawing.
A subtle wash gives the background its gentle, misty appearance.
He used several overlapping washes to build depth and richness in the painting.
The artist applied a pale wash of blue across the sky to create a sense of atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glaze - a thin, transparent layer of paint used in art
