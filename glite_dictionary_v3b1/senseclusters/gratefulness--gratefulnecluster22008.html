gratefulness

noun
state of feeling or showing gratitude
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪtfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtfəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of feeling or showing thanks and appreciation for something received or done

Examples

Gratefulness for help
His gratefulness for the kindness of strangers became clear in his heartfelt speech.
She expressed her gratefulness to everyone who had supported her during difficult times.
The child's eyes lit up with gratefulness when he received the unexpected gift.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gratitude - the feeling of being thankful and appreciative
  • antonym: ingratitude - lack of thankfulness; ungratefulness
