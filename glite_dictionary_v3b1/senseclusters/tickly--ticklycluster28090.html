tickly

adjective
slightly itchy sensation
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkli / (AmE), / ˈtɪkli / (BrE)
causing a slight itching or tingling sensation, often making you want to scratch or cough

Examples

Tickly cough
She had a tickly cough that kept her awake all night.
The wool sweater made his neck feel tickly and uncomfortable.
A tickly sensation in my nose made me want to sneeze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irritating - causing slight discomfort or annoyance
  • synonym: itchy - having or causing an uncomfortable feeling on the skin that makes you want to scratch
