logicist

adjective
strictly logical
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːdʒɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˈlɒdʒɪsɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by strict logical reasoning or formal logic.

Logicist thinking style

She admired his logicist thinking, even if it sometimes seemed cold or detached.
