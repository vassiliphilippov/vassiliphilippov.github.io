preovulatory

adjective
before ovulation
7.9
Pronunciation: / priːˈɑːvjələtɔːri / (AmE), / priːˈɒvjʊlətəri / (BrE)
occurring or existing before ovulation in a biological cycle

Examples

The preovulatory phase
The preovulatory phase is marked by the growth of ovarian follicles.
Doctors monitor hormone changes during the preovulatory period to assess fertility.
Certain symptoms may appear in the preovulatory stage of the menstrual cycle.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postovulatory - occurring after ovulation
