outcross

noun
cross between genetic lines
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈaʊt.krɑːs/ /; / /ˈaʊt.krɔːs/ (AmE), /ˈaʊt.krɒs/ (BrE)
an act of mating between individuals from different genetic lines, or the offspring produced by such a cross

Examples

A genetic outcross
The scientist recommended an outcross to increase genetic diversity in the population.
Introducing an outcross can help reduce inherited health problems in purebred animals.
The new puppy was the result of an outcross between two different dog breeds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crossbreed - an animal or plant resulting from the crossing of different breeds or varieties
  • antonym: inbreeding - the breeding of closely related individuals
