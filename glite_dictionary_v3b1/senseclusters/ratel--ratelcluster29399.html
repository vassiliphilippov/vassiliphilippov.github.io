ratel

noun
badger-like carnivorous mammal of Africa and Asia
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈrɑːtəl / (BrE)
a nocturnal, badger-like carnivore found in wooded regions of Africa and southern Asia, also known as the honey badger
