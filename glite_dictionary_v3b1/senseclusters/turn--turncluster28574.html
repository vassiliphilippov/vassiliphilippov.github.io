turn

Pronunciation: / t…ùn / (AmE), / t…úÀên / (BrE)
to refuse admittance or send away; to dismiss or cause to leave a place

Examples

To turn away guests
Security turned away several people who had no tickets to the event.
The embassy turned him away because he lacked the necessary documentation.
When the restaurant was full, they turned customers away at the door.
