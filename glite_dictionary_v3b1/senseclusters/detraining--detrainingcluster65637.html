detraining

noun
getting off a train
4.1
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ/ /; / /də.ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ/ /; / /də.ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of getting off a train, especially when passengers disembark at a station or in an emergency

Examples

Detraining at the station
