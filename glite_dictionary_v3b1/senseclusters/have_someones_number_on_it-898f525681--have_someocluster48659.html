have someone's number on it

verb
be meant to target or trouble someone
5.5
Pronunciation: / hæv ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈnʌmbɚ ɑn ɪt / (AmE), / hæv ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈnʌmbə ɒn ɪt / (BrE)
(of something) to be destined or likely to affect or bring trouble to someone in particular.

Examples

It has his number on it
He avoided the falling rock, convinced it had his number on it.
If that new policy has your number on it, you'd better be prepared.
This warning letter definitely has your number on it after your recent mistakes.
