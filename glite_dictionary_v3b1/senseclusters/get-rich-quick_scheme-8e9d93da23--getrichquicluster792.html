get-rich-quick scheme

noun
plan promising quick and easy riches
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɡɛt rɪtʃ ˈkwɪk skiːm / (AmE), / ˌɡɛt rɪtʃ ˈkwɪk skiːm / (BrE)
a plan or scheme that promises to make someone rich quickly and with little effort, often with dubious legitimacy

Examples

A get-rich-quick scheme
After reading about another get-rich-quick scheme in the news, she became more cautious with her money.
Don't fall for any get-rich-quick scheme advertised online; they're usually too good to be true.
He lost thousands of dollars in a get-rich-quick scheme that promised instant profits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: con - a trick or swindle to deceive someone
  • antonym: investment - the action or process of investing money for profit
  • synonym: scam - a dishonest scheme; a fraud
