painted-leaf begonia

noun
decorative begonia with patterned leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪntɪd liːf bɪˈɡoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd liːf bɪˈɡəʊniə / (BrE)
a type of ornamental begonia with distinctive, colorful, patterned leaves, often grown as a houseplant
