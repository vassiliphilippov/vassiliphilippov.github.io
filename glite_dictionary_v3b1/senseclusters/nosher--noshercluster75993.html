nosher

noun
someone who snacks between meals
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑʃər / (AmE), / ˈnɒʃə / (BrE)
a person who eats small amounts of food or snacks between meals, especially rather than eating full meals

Examples

A nosher eats snacks
As a dedicated nosher, she prefers chips and fruit to big meals.
If you’re a regular nosher, try to keep healthy snacks on hand.
My brother is a notorious nosher who always raids the kitchen for cookies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snacker - someone who eats snacks frequently or between meals
