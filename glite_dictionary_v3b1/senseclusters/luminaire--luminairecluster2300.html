luminaire

noun
complete electric light fixture
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌluː.məˈnɛr / (AmE), / ˌluː.mɪˈneə / (BrE)
a complete electric light unit, consisting of the fixture, lamp holder, and other parts needed to produce artificial light

Examples

A luminaire unit
Each luminaire in the gallery is individually controlled to highlight different artworks.
The architect specified a modern recessed luminaire for the conference room ceiling.
You must choose a luminaire that meets safety standards for wet environments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: light fixture - an electrical device used to create artificial light, including all its components
