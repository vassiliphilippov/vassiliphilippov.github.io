imagist

adjective
using vivid imagery
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɪm.ə.dʒɪst/ (AmE), /ˈɪm.ə.dʒɪst/ (BrE)
relating to a style that uses clear, precise, and vivid imagery, especially in poetry or art.

Examples

Imagist poetry style
Her imagist poems are known for their sharp, clear descriptions of everyday scenes.
The artist's imagist approach brought a new level of detail to his paintings.
Critics admired the imagist qualities in his writing, especially the vivid sensory language.
