Synonyms

  • relationship
    1.7 160/m
    relationship often describes ongoing personal or social bonds, while relation refers to a general connection between people, groups, or things.
  • connection
    1.9 70/m
    connection is a direct link between people, ideas, or things, while relation focuses on how they interact or are related.
  • interrelationship
    5.9 0.7/m
    interrelationship shows mutual influence among parts or people in a system, while relation names a general connection without that mutual emphasis.
  • interrelation
    5.8 0.5/m
    interrelation describes how two or more things affect each other closely, while relation is a more general term for their connection.
  • association
    3.0 17/m
    association often means a formal group or a casual link between people, while relation is a broader term for their connection or interaction.
  • tie
    2.4 30/m
    tie usually implies a personal, emotional, or business bond between people or groups, while relation is a neutral word for any connection.
