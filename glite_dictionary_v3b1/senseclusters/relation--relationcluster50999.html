relation

noun
connection or dealings between people or groups
Pronunciation: / rɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the way in which two or more people, groups, countries, or things are connected or interact with each other

Examples

International relations
Diplomatic relations were restored between the two countries after decades of hostility.
International relations is a subject that examines interactions between nations.
The company is committed to maintaining strong relations with its partners and suppliers.
In mathematics, a function is a special kind of relation between sets.
The relation between cause and effect is fundamental to scientific study.
Their research explores the relation of language to thought.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connection - a link or association between things
  • antonym: disconnection - a lack of connection or association
  • synonym: relationship - the way in which two or more people or things are connected
