to describe or explain something thoroughly by giving the individual facts, features, or items; to provide details for

Examples

To detail each step
Please detail all of the steps involved in the process when you write your report.
The spokesperson will detail the company's plans for the upcoming year at the press conference.
The witness detailed what happened during the night of the incident in her testimony.

Synonyms

    6.8 1.54/m

  • particularize
    7.8 0.22/m
    particularize means to name or describe specific parts with close attention, while detail means to give each small fact or item.
  • expound
    7.3 2.16/m
    expound means to explain something fully and at length, while detail means to give focused individual facts or items.
  • describe
    1.0 213/m
    describe means to give an account of how something looks or works, while detail means to list its separate small facts or features.
  • explain
    1.0 217/m
    explain means to make an idea clear and easy to understand, while detail means to give many small facts or specific items.
