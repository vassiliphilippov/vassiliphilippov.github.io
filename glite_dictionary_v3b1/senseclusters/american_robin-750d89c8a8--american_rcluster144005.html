American robin

noun
North American songbird, rust-red breast
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛɹɪkən ˈɹɑbɪn / (AmE), / əˈmɛɹɪkən ˈɹɒbɪn / (BrE)
a large North American thrush with a rust-red breast and abdomen, widely known as a common songbird

Examples

The American robin bird
An American robin built its nest under our porch roof this year.
Every spring, I look forward to seeing the first American robin in my backyard.
The American robin is often one of the earliest birds to start singing at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: robin - a small bird with a red or orange breast found in Europe or North America
  • synonym: thrush - a songbird of the family Turdidae, to which robins belong
