laryngologic

adjective
of the larynx
Pronunciation: / ˌlærɪnɡəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌlarɪŋɡəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the medical study or treatment of the larynx (voice box)

Examples

Laryngologic treatment
The hospital has a specialized laryngologic department for treating voice disorders.
She attended a conference on the latest laryngologic surgical techniques.
Advances in laryngologic research have improved outcomes for patients with throat cancer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laryngeal - relating to the larynx
  • synonym: otolaryngologic - relating to the medical study of the ear, nose, and throat
