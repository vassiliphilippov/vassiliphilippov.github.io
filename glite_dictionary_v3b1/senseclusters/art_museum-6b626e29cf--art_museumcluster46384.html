art museum

noun
museum for displaying artworks
3.6
Pronunciation: / ɑrt mjuˈziəm / (AmE), / ɑːt mjuːˈziːəm / (BrE)
A building or institution where works of art are displayed for public viewing and preservation.

Examples

The art museum
Many tourists visit the art museum to admire its impressive collection of paintings and sculptures.
She volunteers at the art museum every Saturday afternoon.
The city opened a new art museum featuring works by local artists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gallery - a room or building for the display of art; sometimes used as a synonym for art museum
