kittenish

adjective
playfully lively or flirtatious
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtənɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɪtənɪʃ / (BrE)
(of a person or their manner) playfully lively or flirtatious, like a kitten.

Examples

She gave him a kittenish grin before darting away.
The actress is known for her kittenish wit and playful personality.
