appetizing

adjective
stimulating the appetite; appealing in smell or appearance
5.1 2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæpəˌtaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈapɪtʌɪzɪŋ /; / ˈapɪtaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
having an appearance or smell that makes you want to eat

Examples

An appetizing smell
The aroma of grilled chicken is extremely appetizing when you're hungry.
The chef decorated the dish in an appetizing way with colorful vegetables.
The freshly baked bread looked so appetizing that I couldn't resist having a slice.
Everything on the buffet looked so appetising that I couldn't decide what to try first.
She arranged the fruit platter in a very appetising way.
The aroma of freshly baked bread was truly appetising.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appetizing - appealing to or stimulating the appetite (American spelling)
  • synonym: tasty - having a good taste; flavorful and pleasant to eat
  • antonym: unappetising - not appealing to the appetite
  • antonym: unappetizing - not appealing or stimulating to the appetite
