harbormaster

noun
port operations manager
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑːr.bər.mæs.tər/ (AmE), /ˈhɑː.bə.mɑː.stə/ (BrE)
a person in charge of the daily operations, safety, and management of a harbor or port

Examples

The harbormaster manages harbor
The harbormaster ensured that all vessels followed the safety regulations while docking.
After a storm, the harbormaster inspected the port for any damage.
The harbormaster assigned berths to the incoming fishing boats each morning.
