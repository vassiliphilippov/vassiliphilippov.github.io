diptych

noun
artwork made of two joined panels
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪp.tɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɪp.tɪk / (BrE)
a work of art, especially a painting or carving, made of two panels that are usually hinged together and can be folded like a book

Examples

A diptych painting
She created a modern diptych combining two contrasting themes.
The diptych was carefully restored to preserve its original colors.
The museum displayed a beautiful diptych from the Renaissance period.
