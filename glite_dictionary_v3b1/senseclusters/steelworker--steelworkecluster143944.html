steelworker

noun
person making steel
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːlˌwɝːkɚ / (AmE), / ˈstiːlˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make steel, especially in a steel mill or factory

Examples

A steelworker in factory
Every steelworker must wear protective gear while working near the furnace.
My grandfather was a proud steelworker in Pittsburgh during the 1950s.
The company hired dozens of new steelworkers to meet the increasing demand.
