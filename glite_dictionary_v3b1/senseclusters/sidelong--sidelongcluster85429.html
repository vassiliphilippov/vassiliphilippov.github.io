sidelong

adjective
inclined or directed to one side
2.4 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌlɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌlɒŋ /; / ˈsaɪdlɒŋ /; / ˈsaɪd.lɒŋ / (BrE)
to the side or indirect; slanting or sideways (especially of looks, glances, or position)

Examples

He gave a sidelong look at the exit, thinking about leaving early.
She cast a sidelong glance at her friend, unsure whether to speak.
