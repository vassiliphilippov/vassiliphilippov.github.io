Synonyms

  • sideways
    2.1 8.04/m
    sideways shows a clear, literal movement or position to the side, while sidelong often suggests a slight or indirect angle.
  • slantwise
    6.8 0.01/m
    slantwise goes at a noticeable slant or diagonal, while sidelong is more often a small, sideways or indirect look.
  • asquint
    9.9 0.00/m
    asquint describes a glance directed to one side, often doubtful or jealous, while sidelong covers general sideways looks.
  • askance
    9.8 0.01/m
    askance means looking with doubt or disapproval, often sideways, while sidelong can mean simply turned to the side.
  • oblique
    7.5 0.96/m
    oblique is a formal, often geometric word for something slanted, while sidelong usually refers to a glance or slight sideways position.
  • laterally
    5.0 1.18/m
    laterally describes physical movement or position to the side, usually neutral and technical, while sidelong also implies glance or indirectness.
