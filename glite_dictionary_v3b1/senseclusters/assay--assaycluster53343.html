assay

verb
attempt or try (rare, literary)
Pronunciation: / əˈseɪ / (AmE), / əˈseɪ / (BrE)
to attempt or try to do something, especially in a careful or tentative way (now rare or literary)

Examples

To assay a task
Before you judge his motives, consider how few have ever assayed such a task.
She assayed a reply, but her words failed in the silence of the hall.
The young knight assayed to lift the heavy sword, though it far outweighed him.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abstain - to deliberately refrain from trying or doing something
  • synonym: attempt - to try to do something
  • synonym: essay - to try or attempt (in a literary context)
