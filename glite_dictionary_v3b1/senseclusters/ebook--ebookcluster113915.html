ebook

noun
digital book
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈiːbʊk / (AmE), / ˈiːbʊk / (BrE)
a book in digital form that can be read on electronic devices such as computers, tablets, or e-readers

Examples

Read an ebook
Many people download an ebook to read while traveling because it is convenient and lightweight.
The library offers thousands of ebooks that can be borrowed online for free.
He published his first novel as an ebook before releasing a printed version.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digital book - a book available in electronic format
  • antonym: hardcover - a book with a rigid protective cover
  • antonym: paperback - a book with a flexible paper cover
