basso profundo

noun
very deep bass singing voice
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːsoʊ prəˈfundoʊ / (AmE), / ˌbæsəʊ prəˈfʊndəʊ / (BrE)
A very deep bass male singing voice, the lowest range of the bass voice types.

Examples

A basso profundo voice
