Quileute

proper noun
Native American people
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪliˌjuːt / (AmE), / ˈkwɪliˌjuːt / (BrE)
the Quileute people (a Native American tribe of western Washington State), a member of this group, or their language

Examples

The Quileute people
The Quileute have a rich tradition of storytelling and art.
Many members of the Quileute community live near the Pacific coast in Washington.
The Quileute language is unique and unrelated to most other Native American languages.
The Quileute have lived along the Pacific coast for thousands of years.
Many members of the Quileute community participate in traditional ceremonies each year.
The Quileute reservation is located near the mouth of the Quillayute River in Washington.
