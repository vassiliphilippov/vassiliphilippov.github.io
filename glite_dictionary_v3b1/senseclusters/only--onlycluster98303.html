only

adverb
except that; with an exception or contrast
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊnli / (AmE), / ˈəʊnli / (BrE)
used to introduce an exception or contrast to what was previously said; except that.

Examples

Only I can't go
I would come to the party, only I'm feeling a bit unwell tonight.
She wanted to buy the dress, only it was too expensive for her.
They enjoyed the concert, only the seats were a little uncomfortable.
