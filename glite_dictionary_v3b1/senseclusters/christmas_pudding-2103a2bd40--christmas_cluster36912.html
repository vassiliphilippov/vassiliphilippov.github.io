Christmas pudding

noun
traditional rich steamed Christmas dessert
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪs·məs ˌpʊd·ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.məs ˌpʊd.ɪŋ / (BrE)
A rich, dense, steamed or boiled pudding made with dried fruit and spices, traditionally served at Christmas in Britain and some other countries.

Examples

A Christmas pudding dessert
Every year, my grandmother makes a delicious Christmas pudding for our family dinner.
The chef flamed the Christmas pudding with brandy before serving it to the guests.
Traditionally, a coin is hidden inside the Christmas pudding for good luck.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plum pudding - an older term for Christmas pudding
