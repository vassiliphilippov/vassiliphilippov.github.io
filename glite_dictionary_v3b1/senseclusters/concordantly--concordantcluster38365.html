concordantly

adverb
in agreement
Pronunciation: / kənˈkɔrdəntli / (AmE), / kənˈkɔːdəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is in agreement or harmony with something or someone

Examples

Concordantly written reports
The two reports were concordantly written, showing no differences in their findings.
All members of the committee acted concordantly to reach a unanimous decision.
The scientists concordantly agreed on the interpretation of the data.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consistently - in a way that is always the same or in agreement
  • antonym: contrarily - in a way that is opposite or in disagreement
  • synonym: harmoniously - in a way that shows harmony or agreement
