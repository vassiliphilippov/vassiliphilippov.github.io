hag

noun
ugly or evil old woman
Pronunciation: / hæɡ / (AmE), / hæɡ / (BrE)
An old woman who is ugly or regarded as evil, often featured in folklore or myth.

Examples

An old hag
Local legends spoke of a hag who cursed anyone who dared to cross her path.
She was mistaken for a hag because of her ragged clothes and hunched back.
The fairy tale described the old woman as a hag living deep in the forest.
