half-bred

adjective
animal with one purebred parent
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhæfˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfˌbrɛd / (BrE)
(of animals) having only one purebred parent; not of pure breed.

Examples

Half-bred dog
Many pet owners prefer half-bred dogs because they are believed to have fewer health issues.
The farmer bought a half-bred colt to add to his stable.
This particular horse is half-bred, with its mother being a thoroughbred and its father a mixed breed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crossbred - an animal resulting from the crossing of different breeds
  • antonym: purebred - an animal of unmixed lineage
