remittal

noun
decrease in intensity, especially of symptoms
9.5
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɪt.əl / (AmE), / rɪˈmɪt.əl / (BrE)
a reduction or decrease in the intensity or degree of something, especially the symptoms of a disease

Examples

Remittal of symptoms
Doctors monitored the remittal of symptoms to assess the effect of the experimental treatment.
Some chronic diseases are characterized by periods of remittal and relapse.
The patient's fever showed a marked remittal after the new medication was administered.
