flour weevil

noun
insect that infests flour and grains
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊər ˈwiːvəl / (AmE), / ˈflaʊə ˈwiːvəl / (BrE)
a small beetle that infests flour and stored grains, often causing damage and contamination

Examples

A flour weevil infestation
A flour weevil can quickly infest a pantry if flour is not stored properly.
The bakery struggled with a recurring flour weevil problem last summer.
We had to throw away the whole sack because it was full of flour weevils.
