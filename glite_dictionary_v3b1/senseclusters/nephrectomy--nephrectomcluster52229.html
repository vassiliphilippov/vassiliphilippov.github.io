nephrectomy

noun
surgical removal of a kidney
9.3 8.1
Pronunciation: /nə.ˈfrek.tə.miː/ (AmE), /nə.ˈfrek.tə.miː/ (BrE)
a surgical procedure to remove a kidney

Examples

A nephrectomy surgery
A nephrectomy may be performed if the kidney is severely damaged by injury or disease.
After being diagnosed with a tumor, the patient underwent a nephrectomy to remove her left kidney.
The surgeon explained the risks associated with the nephrectomy before the procedure.

Related Terms
