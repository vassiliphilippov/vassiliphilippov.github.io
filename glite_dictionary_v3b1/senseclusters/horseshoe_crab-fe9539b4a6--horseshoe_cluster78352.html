horseshoe crab

noun
marine arthropod with horseshoe-shaped shell
6.8 7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌʃu ˌkræb / (AmE), / ˈhɔːʃuː ˌkræb / (BrE)
a large marine arthropod found along the Atlantic coast of North America, with a domed horseshoe-shaped shell and a long, pointed tail; considered a living fossil, distantly related to spiders and scorpions
