seminarist

noun
student at a (religious) seminary
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛməˌnɑrɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɛmɪˌnɑːrɪst / (BrE)
a student who studies at a seminary, especially in preparation for priesthood in the Roman Catholic Church

Examples

A seminarist student
As a seminarist, he often participated in community outreach organized by the church.
The bishop spoke to a group of seminarists about their future roles in the clergy.
The young seminarist devoted himself to hours of prayer and study every day.
