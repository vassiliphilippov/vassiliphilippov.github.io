Russian mayonnaise

noun
mayonnaise with horseradish, onion, and chili sauce or ketchup
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌʃən ˌmeɪəˈneɪz / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃ.ən ˌmeɪ.əˈneɪz / (BrE)
A type of mayonnaise flavored with horseradish, grated onion, and chili sauce or ketchup, sometimes with caviar added.

Examples

Russian mayonnaise with horseradish
I prefer to add zest to my sandwiches by using Russian mayonnaise instead of plain condiments.
She served the shrimp cocktail with a generous dollop of Russian mayonnaise on top.
The recipe calls for Russian mayonnaise to enhance the flavor of the potato salad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mayonnaise - a thick, creamy sauce made of egg yolks, oil, and vinegar or lemon juice
