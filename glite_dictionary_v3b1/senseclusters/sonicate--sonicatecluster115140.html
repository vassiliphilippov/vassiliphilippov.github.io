sonicate

verb
to treat with sound waves
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːnɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɒnɪˌkeɪt / (BrE)
to treat a substance with sound waves, especially to break it up, mix it, or disperse particles

Examples

To sonicate a solution
The lab technician decided to sonicate the solution to ensure all particles were evenly dispersed.
Researchers often sonicate samples to break up cell membranes before analysis.
After you sonicate the mixture, check if it has become homogeneous.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mix - to combine or blend substances together
  • synonym: ultrasonicate - to treat with ultrasonic sound waves
