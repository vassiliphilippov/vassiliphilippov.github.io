strap fern

noun
fern with long, narrow leaves
Pronunciation: / stræp fɝn / (AmE), / stræp fɜːn / (BrE)
A fern with long, narrow, strap-shaped leaves, commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Examples

Strap fern leaves
Botanists identified several species of strap fern growing along the rainforest floor.
Many gardeners appreciate the unique appearance of the strap fern as a decorative houseplant.
The strap fern thrives in humid, shaded environments where its long leaves can absorb moisture from the air.
