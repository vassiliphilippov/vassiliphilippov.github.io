platting

noun
land division process
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈplætɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈplætɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of mapping or dividing land into plots or lots, especially for development or sale

Examples

Land platting
The city council discussed the platting of farmland for a new housing development.
Proper platting ensures that each lot has access to roads and utilities.
After platting, the land was ready to be sold to individual buyers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subdivision - the act of dividing land into smaller lots
  • synonym: surveying - the measurement and mapping of land
