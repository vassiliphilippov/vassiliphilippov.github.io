Edwardian

noun
person from the era of Edward VII
Pronunciation: / ɛdˈwɔrdiən / (AmE), / ɛdˈwɔːdiən / (BrE)
a person who lived during the reign of Edward VII of the United Kingdom, or who has the characteristics or attitudes associated with that period (1901–1910)

Examples

An Edwardian gentleman
As an Edwardian, he valued formality and etiquette above all.
Many Edwardians enjoyed afternoon tea and elaborate garden parties.
The grand house was inherited by an old Edwardian who remembered its heyday.
