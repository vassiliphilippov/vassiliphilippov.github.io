luminaria

noun
festival of lights
8.4
Pronunciation: /luː.mə.ˈnɑːr.iː.ə/ /; / /luː.mə.ˈnɑː.riː.ə/ (AmE), /luː.mə.ˈnɑː.riː.ə/ /; / /luː.mɪ.ˈnɑː.riː.ə/ (BrE)
a public event or festival featuring many lights, candles, or illuminated decorations, often held at night

Examples

Luminaria festival
The city organizes a beautiful luminaria every winter, lighting up the streets with thousands of candles.
Families gather in the park to enjoy the annual luminaria and admire the glowing decorations.
During the luminaria, children walk along the illuminated paths, singing songs and carrying lanterns.
