ambidextrous skill

noun
ability to use both hands equally well
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəs skɪl / (AmE), / ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəs skɪl / (BrE)
the ability to use both hands with equal skill or proficiency

Examples

Ambidextrous skill in painting
Her ambidextrous skill allowed her to switch hands effortlessly while painting.
Only after years of practice did he achieve ambidextrous skill at playing the drums.
The guitarist's ambidextrous skill impressed the audience with rapid fingerwork on both hands.
