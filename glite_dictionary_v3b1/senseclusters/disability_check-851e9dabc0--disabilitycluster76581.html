disability check

noun
monthly payment to disabled person
4.0 3.0
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː tʃek/ (AmE), /dɪs.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː tʃek/ (BrE)
a monthly payment provided to a person who is disabled and unable to work, usually by a government or insurance program

Examples

His application for a disability check was approved after months of paperwork.
Many families depend on a disability check to cover basic living expenses.
