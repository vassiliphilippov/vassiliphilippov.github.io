studier

noun
person who studies
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌdiər / (AmE), / ˈstʌdiə / (BrE)
a person who spends time learning or researching a subject

Examples

A studier of history
As a dedicated studier, he always reviews his notes before every exam.
The library is often quiet except for the occasional studier working late into the night.
Every studier develops their own methods for remembering important information.
The most dedicated studier in the class always finished her assignments early.
As a lifelong studier, he enjoys reading about history in his free time.
Every studier has their own method for preparing for exams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: researcher - a person who carries out academic or scientific investigation
  • synonym: student - a person who is studying at a school or college
  • antonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school
