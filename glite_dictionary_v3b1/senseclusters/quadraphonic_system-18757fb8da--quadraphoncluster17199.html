quadraphonic system

noun
system with four audio channels
Pronunciation: / kwɑː.drəˈfɑː.nɪk ˈsɪs.təm / (AmE), / kwæd.rəˈfɒn.ɪk ˈsɪs.təm / (BrE)
a sound recording or playback system using four separate audio channels to create a more immersive audio experience
