tin god

noun
arrogant person with power or authority
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɪn ɡɑd / (AmE), / tɪn ɡɒd / (BrE)
A person who has or thinks they have a lot of authority or power and behaves in an arrogant or self-important way.

Examples

A tin god attitude
He strutted around like a tin god, enjoying the sense of control it gave him.
Many employees avoided the new supervisor because he acted like a tin god in meetings.
The principal became a tin god, refusing to consider any suggestions from staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autocrat - a person with absolute power or authority
  • synonym: despot - a ruler with absolute power, often oppressive
  • synonym: tyrant - a cruel and oppressive ruler
