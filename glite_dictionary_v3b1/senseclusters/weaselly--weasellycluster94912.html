weaselly

adjective
sneaky or evasive
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːzəli / (AmE), / ˈwiːzəli / (BrE)
sneaky, dishonest, or sly (like a weasel), either in behavior or appearance

Examples

A weaselly excuse
His weaselly excuses made everyone doubt his story.
The politician's weaselly response avoided giving a clear answer.
I don't trust his weaselly behavior when it comes to money.
His weaselly grin made it hard for anyone to trust him.
The suspect gave a weaselly explanation that did not convince the police.
With his weaselly build and quick movements, he slipped through the crowd unnoticed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: honest - truthful and not hiding information
  • synonym: sly - clever in a dishonest or secret way
