preponderance

noun
superiority in number or amount
9.9 6.7
Pronunciation: /priːp.ˈɑːn.drəns/ /; / /prə.ˈpɑːn.drəns/ /; / /prə.ˈpɑːn.dərəns/ (AmE), /prɪ.ˈpɒn.drəns/ /; / /prɪ.ˈpɒn.dərəns/ (BrE)
the state of being greater in number, amount, or importance than something else
