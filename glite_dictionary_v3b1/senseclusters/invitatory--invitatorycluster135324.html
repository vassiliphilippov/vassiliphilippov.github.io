invitatory

adjective
conveying an invitation
6.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɪtəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɪtətəri / (BrE)
Serving as or conveying an invitation; inviting.

Examples

An invitatory note
He penned an invitatory note, hoping to encourage people to join the meeting.
The conference began with an invitatory address, warmly welcoming all the participants.
The host sent an invitatory message to all her friends, asking them to attend the celebration.
