steam jet ejector

noun
device using steam to create a vacuum
6.4
Pronunciation: / stim dʒɛt ɪˈdʒɛktɚ / (AmE), / stiːm dʒet ɪˈdʒɛktə / (BrE)
a device that uses steam to create a vacuum by removing non-condensible gases, especially from surface condensers in industrial systems

Examples

steam jet ejector device
Routine maintenance of the steam jet ejector is essential to ensure efficient vacuum production in power plants.
The steam jet ejector was used to maintain the required pressure in the condenser during the experiment.
Upgrading the steam jet ejector improved the overall efficiency of the refrigeration system.
