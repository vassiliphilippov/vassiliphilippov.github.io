ephemeral

adjective
lasting a very short time
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛmərəl / (AmE), / ɪˈfiːmərəl / (BrE)
lasting for only a very short time; fleeting or transitory.

Examples

Ephemeral beauty
Fashion trends are often ephemeral, quickly replaced by the next popular style.
He realized that youth is ephemeral and should be cherished while it lasts.
The beauty of the sunset was ephemeral, lasting only a few moments before fading away.

Related Terms

  • antonym: permanent - lasting forever or for a very long time
  • synonym: temporary - lasting or meant to last for only a short time
  • synonym: transient - lasting only for a short time
