someway

adverb
by some unspecified means
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌm.weɪ / (BrE)
In some unspecified way or by some unspecified means.

Examples

In someway or another
He knew he would reach his goal someway, even if the path was unclear.
The problem seemed impossible, but someway they found a solution.
We must try to fix this someway, even if we don't know how yet.
