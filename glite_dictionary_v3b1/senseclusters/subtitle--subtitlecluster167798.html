subtitle

noun
secondary or explanatory title
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌtaɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌtaɪtəl / (BrE)
a secondary title below the main title of a book, article, or work, often giving additional information or clarification

Examples

Book subtitle
She suggested adding a subtitle to clarify the purpose of her report.
The subtitle of the book explained its focus in more detail.
The magazine article's subtitle drew readers in with an intriguing question.

Related Terms

  • synonym: title - the name of a book, composition, or work
