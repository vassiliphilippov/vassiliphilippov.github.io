vagility

noun
ability to move
Pronunciation: / vəˈdʒɪlɪti / (AmE), / vəˈdʒɪlɪti / (BrE)
the ability of an organism to move or disperse within its environment

Examples

High vagility species
Species with high vagility can adapt more easily to changing environments.
The vagility of insects allows them to colonize new habitats quickly.
Researchers measured the vagility of different mammals to study their migration patterns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mobility - the ability to move easily from one place to another
  • antonym: sedentariness - the state of being settled and not moving much
