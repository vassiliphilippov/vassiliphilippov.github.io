Abies lasiocarpa

noun
a Rocky Mountain fir tree species
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.bi.iːz ˌlæ.si.oʊˈkɑːr.pə / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.bi.iːz ˌlæ.si.əˈkɑː.pə / (BrE)
A medium to tall evergreen tree of the Rocky Mountains, also known as subalpine fir, characterized by its narrowly conic to columnar crown.
