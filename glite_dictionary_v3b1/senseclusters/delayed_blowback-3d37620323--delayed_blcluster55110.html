delayed blowback

noun
postponed negative consequences
Pronunciation: / dɪˈleɪd ˈbloʊˌbæk / (AmE), / dɪˈleɪd ˈbləʊˌbæk / (BrE)
Postponed negative consequences or unintended adverse reactions that result from an action, decision, or policy and appear after some time.

Examples

Delayed blowback from sanctions
The new sanctions risk delayed blowback for years, impacting allies and markets.
Experts warned that hasty decisions could produce delayed blowback long after the policy ends.
The company's cost-cutting measures led to delayed blowback when customer trust finally eroded.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blowback - unintended adverse consequences of an action or policy
  • synonym: repercussion - an unintended consequence or effect, often negative
  • synonym: unintended consequence - an outcome not foreseen or intended by a purposeful action
