ngoma

noun
African communal ceremony
9.9
Pronunciation: / ŋˈɡoʊmə / (AmE), / ŋˈɡəʊmə / (BrE)
a traditional African ceremony or gathering featuring drumming, dancing, and often ritual, or the drum or dance itself used in such events

Examples

A ngoma ceremony
The villagers gathered for a lively ngoma to celebrate the end of the rainy season.
During the ngoma, elders shared stories and young people performed traditional dances.
Tourists were invited to join the ngoma and experience local music and customs.
The villagers gathered to play the ngoma at the harvest festival.
She learned the traditional ngoma dance from her grandmother.
The sound of the ngoma echoed through the night during the celebration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dance - a series of movements set to music
  • synonym: djembe - a West African drum played with bare hands
  • synonym: festival - a celebration or series of events for a special occasion
  • synonym: ritual - a ceremony with a set of fixed actions, often religious or cultural
