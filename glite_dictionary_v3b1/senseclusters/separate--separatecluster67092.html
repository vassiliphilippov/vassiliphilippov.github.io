separate

noun
individual garment for mix-and-match outfits
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛpɚət / (AmE), / ˈsɛpərət / (BrE)
a garment sold individually, intended to be worn with other items rather than as part of a matched set

Examples

A clothing separate
During the sale, all separate items were marked down by twenty percent.
She prefers buying separates because they offer more flexibility in creating different outfits.
The store features a large selection of separates, including blouses, skirts, and trousers.
