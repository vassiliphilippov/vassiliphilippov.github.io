knocker

noun
boastful, self-important person
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːkər / (AmE), / ˈnɒkə / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly US) A person who is boastful and self-important; someone who shows off their success or status, often with arrogance.

Examples

A loud knocker
Everyone in the office avoided the knocker who bragged constantly about his achievements.
She didn't want to be seen as a knocker, so she stayed humble despite her success.
The club's reputation suffered because its president was such a notorious knocker.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braggart - a person who boasts about achievements or possessions
  • antonym: modest person - someone who does not boast or show off
  • synonym: show-off - a person who behaves in a way intended to attract attention or admiration
