telework

verb
to work remotely
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌwɜːk / (BrE)
to work from a location outside the office, typically using computers and the internet to communicate

Examples

To telework from home
Many employees now telework several days a week to avoid long commutes.
She prefers to telework because it allows her to balance her job and family life.
During the storm, the entire team was asked to telework until the office reopened.

Related Terms

  • antonym: commute - to travel regularly to and from work
  • synonym: telecommute - to work from home using electronic communication
