opening

noun
available job or opportunity
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənɪŋ / (BrE)
a position or opportunity, especially for employment or advancement, that is available and can be filled

Examples

A job opening
She applied as soon as she heard about the opening in the marketing team.
The company has an opening for a software engineer in its IT department.
There was fierce competition for the opening at the prestigious law firm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vacancy - a job or position that is unfilled and available
