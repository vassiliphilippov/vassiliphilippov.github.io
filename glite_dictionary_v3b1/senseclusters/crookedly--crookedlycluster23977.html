crookedly

adverb
in a lopsided or twisted way
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʊkɪdli / (AmE), / ˈkrʊkɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that is not straight, level, or symmetrical; in a lopsided or twisted manner.

Examples

Arranged crookedly on wall
He arranged the picture crookedly on the wall, so it looked uneven.
She smiled crookedly, revealing a hint of mischief.
The line was drawn crookedly across the page, making the diagram look odd.

Related Terms

  • antonym: straight - in a straight or direct way
