Champs Elysees

proper noun
famous avenue in Paris
Pronunciation: / ʃæ̃z eɪˈliːzeɪ / (AmE), / ʃɒ̃z eɪˈliːzeɪ / (BrE)
A broad and famous avenue in Paris, renowned for its elegant shops, cafes, and as a major tourist attraction.

Examples

The Champs Elysees avenue

The parade marched proudly along the Champs Elysees during Bastille Day celebrations.
