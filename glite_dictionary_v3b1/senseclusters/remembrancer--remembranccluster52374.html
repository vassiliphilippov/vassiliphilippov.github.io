remembrancer

noun
City of London official
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɛmbrənsər / (AmE), / rɪˈmɛmbrənsə / (BrE)
an official of the City of London Corporation who represents the city's interests to Parliament and acts as its parliamentary adviser
