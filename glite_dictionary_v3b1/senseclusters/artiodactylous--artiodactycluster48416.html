artiodactylous

adjective
relating to even-toed mammals
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.tɪ.oʊ.ˈdæk.tɪ.ləs/ /; / /ɑːr.tɪ.ə.ˈdæk.tɪ.ləs/ (AmE), /ɑː.tɪ.ə.ˈdæk.tɪ.ləs/ /; / /ɑː.tɪ.əʊ.ˈdæk.tɪ.ləs/ (BrE)
relating to or belonging to mammals of the order Artiodactyla, which have an even number of toes on each foot

Examples

Artiodactylous mammals
Most domesticated livestock, such as sheep and pigs, are artiodactylous mammals.
Scientists studied the hooves of artiodactylous species to understand their movement in different terrains.
The camel is an artiodactylous animal adapted to desert life.
