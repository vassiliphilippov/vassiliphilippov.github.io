uninflated

adjective
not inflation-adjusted
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈfleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈfleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not adjusted for inflation; expressed in current nominal terms rather than real, inflation-adjusted terms

Examples

Uninflated prices
The report presents uninflated figures, which may not reflect the actual change in value over time.
When comparing salaries, it is important to distinguish between uninflated and inflation-adjusted amounts.
The uninflated cost of the project appears lower, but inflation must be considered for an accurate comparison.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nominal - expressed in current prices, not adjusted for inflation
  • antonym: real - adjusted for inflation to reflect true value
