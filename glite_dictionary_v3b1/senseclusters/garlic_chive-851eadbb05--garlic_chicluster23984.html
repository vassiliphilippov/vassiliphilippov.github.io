garlic chive

noun
herb with flat leaves and garlic flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrlɪk tʃaɪv / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːlɪk tʃaɪv / (BrE)
a perennial herb with flat leaves and a mild garlic flavor, used in cooking like chives
