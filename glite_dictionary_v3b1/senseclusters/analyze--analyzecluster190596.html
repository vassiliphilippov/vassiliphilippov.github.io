analyze

verb
to examine in detail for understanding
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈænəˌlaɪz /; / əˈnælaɪz /; / əˈnæl.aɪz / (AmE), / ˈænəlaɪz /; / ˈæn.əl.aɪz /; / ˈæn.ə.laɪz / (BrE)
to examine something carefully and in detail to understand it or find its essential features

The scientist will analyze the results of the experiment before drawing any conclusions.
