dud

adjective
explosive device that fails to detonate
9.9 5.7
Pronunciation: /dʌd/ (AmE), /dʌd/ (BrE)
(of an explosive device) failing to ignite or detonate as intended.

Examples

Dud bomb
During training, soldiers sometimes encounter a dud round that doesn't fire.
Luckily, the shell turned out to be a dud and caused no harm.
The bomb squad carefully removed a dud grenade from the construction site.
