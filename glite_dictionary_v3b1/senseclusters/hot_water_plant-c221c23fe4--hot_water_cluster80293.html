hot water plant

noun
tropical plant with showy bell-shaped flowers
7.3 8.1
Pronunciation: /hɑːt ˈwɔː.tər plænt/ (AmE), /hɒt ˈwɔː.tə plɑːnt/ (BrE)
A tropical plant of the genus Achimenes, grown for its bright, bell-shaped flowers resembling those of the gloxinia.

Examples

Flowering hot water plant
Gardeners prize the hot water plant for its vibrant, bell-shaped blooms during the summer.
The hot water plant thrives in bright, indirect sunlight and well-drained soil.
You can propagate a hot water plant easily from its small underground rhizomes.
