virulent backlash

noun
strong, hostile negative reaction or criticism
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪrjələnt ˈbækˌlæʃ / (AmE), / ˈvɪrjʊlənt ˈbæklæʃ / (BrE)
an extremely strong, hostile, and often widespread negative reaction or criticism against someone or something

Examples

A virulent backlash
After the book's publication, the author received a virulent backlash from critics and readers alike.
The CEO's statement triggered a virulent backlash on social media and among customers.
There was a virulent backlash from the community following the new policy announcement.
