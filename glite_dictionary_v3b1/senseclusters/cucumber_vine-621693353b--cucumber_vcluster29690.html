cucumber vine

noun
vine that produces cucumbers
3.0 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkjuÀêk åmb…ö va…™n / (AmE), / ÀàkjuÀêk…ômb…ô va…™n / (BrE)
a climbing or trailing plant of the genus Cucumis, cultivated for its cylindrical green fruit known as the cucumber

Examples

The cucumber vine grows
After planting the seeds, the cucumber vine began to spread across the sunny patch in the backyard.
Farmers carefully water the cucumber vine to ensure a healthy crop of cucumbers.
The cucumber vine climbed rapidly along the garden fence, its yellow flowers attracting bees.

Synonyms

  • Cucumis sativus
    6.0 0.01/m
