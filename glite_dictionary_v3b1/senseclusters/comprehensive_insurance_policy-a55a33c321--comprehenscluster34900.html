comprehensive insurance policy

noun
insurance covering many risks
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːm.prəˈhɛn.sɪv ɪnˈʃʊr.əns ˈpɑː.li / (AmE), / ˌkɒm.prɪˈhɛn.sɪv ɪnˈʃɔː.rəns ˈpɒl.ɪ.si / (BrE)
an insurance policy that covers a broad range of risks, often including damage, theft, and loss, rather than just specific events

Examples

A comprehensive insurance policy
A comprehensive insurance policy usually covers theft, fire, and accidental damage to your vehicle.
After purchasing her new car, Maya decided to get a comprehensive insurance policy for extra peace of mind.
The landlord required tenants to have a comprehensive insurance policy before moving in.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comprehensive coverage - insurance coverage that protects against a wide variety of risks or damages
  • antonym: third-party insurance - insurance covering claims by others, not by the insured themselves
