payback

noun
revenge or act of retaliation
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈpeɪˌbæk / (BrE)
the act of punishing or harming someone in return for something they have done, especially as revenge

Examples

Sweet payback
After years of being bullied, he finally got his payback when he stood up for himself.
Many movies revolve around the theme of payback for betrayal or injustice.
She believed that fate would deliver payback to those who wronged her family.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgiveness - the action of stopping blaming or being angry with someone for something wrong
  • synonym: retribution - punishment inflicted as vengeance for a wrong or criminal act
  • synonym: revenge - the action of inflicting hurt in return for harm received
