Synonyms

  • vengeance
    6.8 12/m
    vengeance is a strong desire to punish someone for a wrong, while payback often feels more personal and informal.
  • getting even
    7.4 0.3/m
    getting even is an informal phrase about personal revenge, while payback is often used for causing harm in return.
  • retaliation
    5.2 5/m
    retaliation is an act of responding with harm in return, while payback usually implies personal revenge and intent to hurt.
  • reprisal
    8.3 2/m
    reprisal is an official or armed response to an attack, while payback is any personal act of revenge.
  • retribution
    7.5 4/m
    retribution is punishment seen as deserved or lawful, while payback often means personal revenge without formal justice.
  • tit for tat
    5.8 0.5/m
    tit for tat describes equal, often quick responses in kind, while payback focuses on punishing someone for a wrong.
  • requital
    9.5 0.01/m
    requital can mean returning a favor or harm in kind, while payback specifically means seeking revenge or punishment.
