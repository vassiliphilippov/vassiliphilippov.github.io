Platycerium

noun
genus of epiphytic tropical ferns
Pronunciation: / ˌpleɪtɪˈsɪriəm /; / ˌplætɪˈsɪəriəm / (AmE), / ˌplætɪˈsɪəriəm / (BrE)
a genus of tropical ferns, often growing on trees, native to the Old World and commonly known as staghorn ferns
