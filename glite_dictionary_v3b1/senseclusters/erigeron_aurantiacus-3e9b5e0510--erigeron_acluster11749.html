Erigeron aurantiacus

noun
mat-forming herb with orange-yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrɪdʒərəˌn ɔˌræntiˈeɪkəs / (AmE), / ɪˈrɪdʒərɒn ɔːˌræntiˈeɪkəs / (BrE)
