cava

noun
Spanish wine cellar
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːvə / (AmE), / ˈkɑːvə / (BrE)
a cool underground cellar, especially in Spain, used for storing and aging wine

Examples

Wine cava
The old house had a hidden cava where the family kept their finest wines.
During the tour, visitors admired the cool air inside the ancient cava beneath the vineyard.
They celebrated the harvest by opening a special bottle from the cava.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cellar - a room below ground level used for storage, especially of wine
  • synonym: wine vault - an underground room for storing wine
