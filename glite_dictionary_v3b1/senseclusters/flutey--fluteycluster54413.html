flutey

adjective
like a flute
Pronunciation: / ˈfluːti / (AmE), / ˈfluːti / (BrE)
having a clear, high, and sweet sound similar to that of a flute

Examples

Flutey voice
The bird sang a flutey melody that echoed through the forest.
She spoke in a flutey voice that was both gentle and cheerful.
The musician played a flutey tune on his recorder during the concert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flutelike - resembling the sound of a flute
  • antonym: harsh - having an unpleasant, rough, or grating sound
