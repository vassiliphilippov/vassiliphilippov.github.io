dolly

noun
wheeled platform for moving objects
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːli / (AmE), / ˈdɒli / (BrE)
a small platform on wheels, used for moving heavy objects easily

Examples

A dolly cart
The crew placed the camera on a dolly to film the tracking shot.
The movers used a dolly to transport the refrigerator into the kitchen.
You can rent a dolly to help move heavy boxes from the truck.

Related Terms
