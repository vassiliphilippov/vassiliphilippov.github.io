salvific

adjective
having power to save or redeem
9.6
Pronunciation: / sælˈvɪfɪk / (AmE), / salˈvɪfɪk / (BrE)
having the power to bring about salvation or redemption; relating to salvation.

Examples

Salvific power
Many theologians believe that faith is intrinsically salvific.
Some traditions emphasize the salvific power of sacred texts.
The ritual had a salvific significance for its participants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: redemptive - having the quality of redeeming or saving
