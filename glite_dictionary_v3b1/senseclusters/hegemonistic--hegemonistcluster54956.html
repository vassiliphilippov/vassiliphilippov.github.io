hegemonistic

adjective
relating to dominance
Pronunciation: / hɪˌdʒɛmənˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / hɪˌdʒɛmənˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or supporting the dominance or leadership of one group, country, or person over others

Examples

Hegemonistic foreign policy
The new administration adopted a hegemonistic approach to foreign policy.
Critics argue that hegemonistic attitudes can lead to international conflict.
Some historians describe the empire's expansion as hegemonistic in nature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dominant - having power and influence over others
  • antonym: submissive - willing to obey or be controlled by others
