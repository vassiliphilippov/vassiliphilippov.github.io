epidemiologist

noun
expert in disease patterns and control
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɛp.ɪˌdi.mi.ˈɑ.lə.dʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛp.ɪˌdiː.miˈɒl.ə.dʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies the transmission, distribution, and control of diseases within populations

Examples

An epidemiologist studies diseases
As an epidemiologist, he specializes in identifying the sources of infectious diseases.
During the pandemic, the government consulted an epidemiologist to develop effective public health strategies.
The epidemiologist presented her findings on the recent outbreak at the scientific conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epidemiology - the branch of medicine dealing with the incidence and control of diseases
