string quartet

noun
musical group with two violins, viola, and cello
Pronunciation: / strɪŋ kwɑrˈtɛt / (AmE), / strɪŋ kwɔːˈtɛt / (BrE)
a musical ensemble consisting of two violins, a viola, and a cello; or a piece of music composed for such a group

Examples

A string quartet group
Many famous composers have written music specifically for string quartet.
She joined a string quartet during university, playing the viola in local recitals.
The string quartet performed a beautiful rendition of Beethoven's late works at the concert hall.
