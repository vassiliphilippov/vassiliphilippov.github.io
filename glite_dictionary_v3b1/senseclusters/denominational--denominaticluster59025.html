denominational

adjective
relating to a religious denomination
Pronunciation: / dəˌnɑməˈneɪʃənəl /; / dəˌnɑːməˈneɪʃənəl / (AmE), / dɪˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənəl / (BrE)
connected with or characteristic of a particular religious denomination or sect
