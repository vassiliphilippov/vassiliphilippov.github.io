intercrossing

noun
crossing over each other
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈkrɔsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈkrɒsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or result of two or more things crossing each other, such as lines, cables, or paths

Examples

Intercrossing of lines
The intercrossing of cables behind the server rack made it hard to trace individual connections.
The artist's design featured the intercrossing of colorful lines to create a sense of movement.
At the busy junction, the intercrossing of roads required careful attention from all drivers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intersection - the point or area where two or more things cross
  • synonym: overlap - the area where two things cover the same space
