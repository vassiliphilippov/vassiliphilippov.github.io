Humulus lupulus

noun
plant used to flavor beer (common hop)
8.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàhjuÀêmj…ôl…ôs luÀêÀàpjuÀêl…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàhjuÀêmj äl…ôs ljuÀêÀàpjuÀêl…ôs / (BrE)
