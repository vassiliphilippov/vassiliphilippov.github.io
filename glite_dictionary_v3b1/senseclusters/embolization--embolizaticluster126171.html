embolization

noun
blockage by embolus
Pronunciation: / ˌɛmbələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛmbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process in which an embolus, such as a blood clot or air bubble, blocks a blood vessel and causes an obstruction

Examples

Blood embolization
The doctor explained that embolization can lead to serious complications if not treated quickly.
A sudden embolization of the artery resulted in severe pain and loss of function.
Advanced imaging techniques help detect embolization in patients with unexplained symptoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embolism - the blockage of a blood vessel by an embolus
  • synonym: thrombosis - the formation of a blood clot inside a blood vessel
