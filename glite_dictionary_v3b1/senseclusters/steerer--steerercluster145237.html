steerer

noun
one who leads others into danger by trickery
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪrər / (AmE), / ˈstɪərə / (BrE)
a person who cunningly leads someone into danger, typically as part of a scheme or plot

Examples

A cunning steerer
Police arrested the steerer who had lured several victims to the scam artists.
She realized too late that the charming man was actually a steerer leading her into a trap.
The unsuspecting tourist was targeted by a steerer working with local thieves.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: guide - a person who leads or directs others
