Nabataean

proper noun
ancient Arab people
Pronunciation: / ˌnæbəˈtiːən / (AmE), / ˌnæbəˈtiːən / (BrE)
a member of an ancient Arab people known for their trade and for building the city of Petra

Examples

A Nabataean trader
The Nabataean civilization is famous for building the city of Petra.
Archaeologists discovered ancient pottery made by the Nabataeans in the Jordanian desert.
Many tourists visit Petra to learn about the Nabataean culture and history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Arab - a member of a Semitic people from the Arabian Peninsula
  • antonym: Roman - a citizen of ancient Rome
