pericarp

noun
the wall of a ripened fruit ovary
6.3 7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrɪˌkɑrp /; / ˈpɛr.ɪˌkɑrp / (AmE), / ˈpɛrɪkɑːp /; / ˈpɛr.ɪˌkɑːp / (BrE)
the part of a fruit formed from the ovary wall that surrounds and protects the seed or seeds

Examples

The pericarp layer

Botanists study the structure of the pericarp in various fruit species.
The orange pericarp is thick and leathery, protecting the juicy segments inside.
