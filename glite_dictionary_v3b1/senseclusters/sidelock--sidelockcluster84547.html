sidelock

noun
firearm side mechanism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌlɒk / (BrE)
a type of firearm mechanism where the lock or firing system is mounted on side plates, commonly found in high-quality shotguns
