Fourth of July

noun
US Independence Day holiday
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrθ əv dʒəˈlaɪ / (AmE), / ˌfɔːθ əv dʒəˈlaɪ / (BrE)
The national holiday in the United States commemorating the adoption of the Declaration of Independence on July 4, 1776.

Examples

Fourth of July fireworks
Fireworks are a popular way to celebrate the Fourth of July across the United States.
Many families have barbecues on the Fourth of July to mark the occasion.
The city hosts a parade every Fourth of July to honor Independence Day.
