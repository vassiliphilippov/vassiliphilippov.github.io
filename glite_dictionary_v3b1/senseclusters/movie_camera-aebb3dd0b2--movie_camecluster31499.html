movie camera

noun
camera for recording moving images
Pronunciation: / ˈmuvi ˌkæmərə / (AmE), / ˈmuːvi ˌkæmərə / (BrE)
a camera designed for taking a sequence of photographs on film or digitally, creating the illusion of motion when played back rapidly

Examples

A movie camera lens
Early filmmakers used a hand-cranked movie camera to record silent films.
She bought a vintage movie camera to start making short films.
The director inspected the movie camera before shooting the next scene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: camcorder - a portable video camera with a built-in recorder
