bad manners

noun
impolite or rude behavior
Pronunciation: / bæd ˈmænərz / (AmE), / bæd ˈmænəz / (BrE)
rude or impolite behavior; lack of social politeness or courtesy.

Examples

Bad manners at table
Chewing with your mouth open is considered bad manners in many cultures.
Interrupting someone while they are speaking shows bad manners.
It is bad manners to check your phone at the dinner table.

Related Terms

  • antonym: good manners - polite or considerate behavior
  • synonym: rudeness - the quality of being rude or impolite
