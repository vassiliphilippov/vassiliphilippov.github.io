humic

adjective
relating to humus or decomposed organic matter
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuː.mɪk / (AmE), / ˈhjuː.mɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or derived from humus, the organic component of soil formed by the decomposition of plant material.

Examples

Humic acids
Humic acids play an important role in retaining moisture in agricultural soils.
Scientists analyzed humic compounds to assess soil fertility.
The soil was rich in humic substances, providing nutrients for plant growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humus - organic component of soil formed from decomposed plant material
