carriage

noun
machine part that supports/moves another component
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkærɪdʒ / (BrE)
the part of a machine that supports and moves another component, especially in devices such as typewriters or printers

Examples

Printer carriage
Technicians replaced the carriage to restore proper function to the plotter.
The printer jam was caused by an obstruction in the carriage assembly.
The typewriter's carriage moved smoothly as she typed each letter.
