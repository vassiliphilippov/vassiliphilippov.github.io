third-rate service

noun
inferior or poor service
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜrd ˈreɪt ˈsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / ˌθɜːd ˈreɪt ˈsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
service of very poor quality; inferior or substandard service.

Examples

Third-rate service quality
The restaurant closed soon after customers complained about its third-rate service.
We were disappointed to receive third-rate service during our stay at the hotel.
You shouldn't expect more than third-rate service from such a poorly managed company.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: second-rate service - service of mediocre but not the worst quality
