boatful

noun
amount filling a boat
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊtfʊl / (AmE), / ˈbəʊtfʊl / (BrE)
an amount or number of people or things that fills a boat

Examples

A boatful of fish
They caught a boatful of fish during their trip.
A boatful of tourists arrived at the island early in the morning.
The storm made it difficult for the boatful of supplies to reach the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carload - an amount that fills a car
  • synonym: truckload - an amount that fills a truck
