smartness

noun
quality of being quick-witted and intelligent
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɑːrtnəs / (AmE), / ˈsmɑːtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being quick-witted and intelligent, especially in conversation or behavior

Examples

Smartness in conversation
Her smartness impressed the interviewers during the challenging questions.
His smartness in handling difficult situations made him a valued team member.
The comedian's smartness kept the audience laughing throughout the show.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dullness - lack of intelligence or quickness of mind
  • synonym: wit - mental sharpness and inventiveness; quick understanding
