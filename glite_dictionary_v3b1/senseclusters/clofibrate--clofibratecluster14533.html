clofibrate

noun
drug for lowering blood lipids
Pronunciation: / kloʊˈfaɪbreɪt / (AmE), / kləʊˈfɪbreɪt / (BrE)
a drug used to lower lipid levels in the blood and to treat certain cardiovascular diseases
