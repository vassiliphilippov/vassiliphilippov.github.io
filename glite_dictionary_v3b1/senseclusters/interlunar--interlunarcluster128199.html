interlunar

adjective
moon not visible
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈluːnər / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈluːnə / (BrE)
relating to the period when the moon is not visible from Earth, especially between the old and new moon

Examples

The interlunar period
The sky was pitch black on the interlunar night, making the stars appear even brighter.
Ancient cultures often held special ceremonies during the interlunar period.
Travelers found it difficult to navigate by night in the interlunar darkness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: full moon - the phase of the moon when it is fully illuminated
  • synonym: new moon - the phase of the moon when it is not visible from Earth
