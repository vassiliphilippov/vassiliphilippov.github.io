death seat

noun
front passenger seat (dangerous position)
5.5 6.7
Pronunciation: /deθ siːt/ (AmE), /deθ siːt/ (BrE)
the front passenger seat in a car, believed to be the most dangerous place to sit in case of an accident

Examples
