death seat

noun
front passenger seat (dangerous position)
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛθ ˌsiːt / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ ˌsiːt / (BrE)
the front passenger seat in a car, believed to be the most dangerous place to sit in case of an accident

Examples

The death seat in car
During the crash, the person in the death seat suffered the most serious injuries.
He nervously buckled up as he settled into the death seat for their road trip.
Some people refuse to sit in the death seat because they think it's bad luck.

Related Terms

  • synonym: passenger seat - the seat next to the driver in a car
