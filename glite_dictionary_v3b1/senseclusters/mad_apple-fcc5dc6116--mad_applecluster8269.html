mad apple

noun
old name for eggplant (Solanum melongena)
Pronunciation: / ˈmæd ˌæpəl /; / mæd ˈæpəl / (AmE), / ˈmad ˌapəl /; / mad ˈapəl / (BrE)
an old or alternative name for the eggplant, a plant grown for its large, shiny, edible fruit used as a vegetable

Examples

In 18th-century texts, the mad apple is frequently mentioned as a curious imported plant.
