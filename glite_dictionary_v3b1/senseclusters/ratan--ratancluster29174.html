ratan

noun
cane or switch from rattan palm
8.6 8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈræt.ən/ (AmE), /ˈræt.ən/ (BrE)
A thin switch or cane made from the stems of rattan palms, often used for whipping or as a walking stick.
