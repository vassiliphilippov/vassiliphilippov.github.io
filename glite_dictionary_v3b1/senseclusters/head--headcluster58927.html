head

noun
rounded end of a bone
Pronunciation: / hɛd / (AmE), / hɛd / (BrE)
the rounded, top, or end part of an object, such as a tool, pin, match, drum, coin, foam on a drink, vegetable, muscle, or bone

Examples

The head of the femur fits into the pelvis to form the hip joint.
The socket receives the head of the bone to create a ball-and-socket joint.
Each head of cabbage in the garden was large and tightly packed.
She bought a fresh head of lettuce for the salad.
The recipe calls for one head of cauliflower, cut into florets.
Damage to the distal head of the muscle can impair movement of the joint.
The biceps brachii has two heads that originate from different parts of the shoulder.
The surgeon identified the head of the muscle before proceeding with the procedure.
