Dead Sea

proper noun
salt lake between Israel and Jordan
Pronunciation: / dɛd siː / (AmE), / dɛd siː / (BrE)
a large salt lake between Israel and Jordan, notable for its extremely high salt content and being the lowest point on Earth's surface above water

Examples

The Dead Sea region
Many tourists visit the Dead Sea to experience floating effortlessly in its salty waters.
Scientists study the unique minerals found in the Dead Sea for possible health benefits.
The Dead Sea is shrinking due to water diversion and environmental changes.
