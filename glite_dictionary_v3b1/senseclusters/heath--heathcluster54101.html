heath

noun
a small evergreen shrub with pink or purple flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / hiθ / (AmE), / hiːθ / (BrE)
a low-growing evergreen shrub of the family Ericaceae, with small bell-shaped pink or purple flowers, especially found in heathland or moorland areas

Examples

Heath plant
Many insects and birds rely on heath for food and habitat.
She collected a sprig of heath and pressed it between the pages of her book.
The heath was covered with vibrant purple flowers in late summer.
