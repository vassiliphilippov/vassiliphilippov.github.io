cinéaste

noun
artistic filmmaker
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪniˌæst / (AmE), / ˈsiːneɪˌæst /; / ˈsiːniːˌæst / (BrE)
a person who is passionate about cinema or is a filmmaker, especially one with a strong artistic vision

Examples

Famous cinéaste director
The acclaimed cinéaste was known for his unique visual style and storytelling.
Aspiring filmmakers often study the works of a master cinéaste to learn about artistic direction.
Her reputation as a cinéaste grew after her films won several international awards.
As a dedicated cinéaste, she attends every film festival in the city.
The club was founded by a group of young cinéastes eager to discuss classic movies.
Every aspiring cinéaste dreams of making a film that will be remembered for generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auteur - a filmmaker whose personal influence and artistic control are so great that they are regarded as the author of the movie
  • synonym: director - a person who supervises the actors and directs the action in the making of a film
  • synonym: filmmaker - a person who makes films or movies
  • synonym: movie buff - a person who is very interested in movies
