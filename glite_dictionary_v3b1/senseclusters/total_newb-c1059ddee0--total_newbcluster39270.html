total newb

noun
inexperienced newcomer (slang, especially online/gaming)
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊ.t̬əl nub / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl njuːb / (BrE)
An inexperienced person; someone new to a particular activity or field, especially in online communities or gaming.

Examples

A total newb player
Don't worry if you feel like a total newb in your first coding class; everyone starts somewhere.
She joined the photography group as a total newb, but quickly learned the basics.
When I started playing chess online, I was a total newb and lost nearly every game.
