muriate

noun
chloride compound
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmjʊriˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈmjʊərɪeɪt / (BrE)
a chemical compound that is a chloride, especially potassium chloride or any salt of hydrochloric acid (an old term)
