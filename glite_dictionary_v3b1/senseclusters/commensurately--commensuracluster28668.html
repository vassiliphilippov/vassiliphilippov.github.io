commensurately

adverb
in proportion
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɛnʃərətli / (AmE), / kəˈmɛnʃərətli / (BrE)
in a way that matches or corresponds in size, degree, or extent

Examples

Increase commensurately
As the project grew in complexity, the budget increased commensurately to cover additional costs.
Her responsibilities expanded commensurately with her promotion to manager.
If the risk rises, the potential reward should also increase commensurately.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disproportionately - in a way that is not in proportion
  • synonym: proportionally - in a way that is in proportion
