gator

noun
informal term for alligator
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪ.tə / (BrE)
An informal term for an alligator, a large semi-aquatic reptile with a broad, rounded snout.

Examples

A gator animal
The wildlife park has several gator feeding shows each day.
Watch out for gators if you're swimming in that lake.
