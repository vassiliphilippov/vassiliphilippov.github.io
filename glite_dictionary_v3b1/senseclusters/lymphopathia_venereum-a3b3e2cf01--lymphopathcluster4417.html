lymphopathia venereum

noun
a sexually transmitted infection affecting lymph nodes
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪm.foʊˈpeɪ.θi.ə vəˈnɪr.i.əm / (AmE), / ˌlɪm.fəʊˈpæθ.i.ə vɪˈnɪə.rɪ.əm / (BrE)
a sexually transmitted infectious disease caused by Chlamydia bacteria, characterized by genital sores and swollen lymph nodes in the groin

Examples

lymphopathia venereum infection
Early diagnosis of lymphopathia venereum is important for effective treatment.
Recent cases of lymphopathia venereum have been reported in several urban centers.
