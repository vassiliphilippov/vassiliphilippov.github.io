light-hearted way

noun
in a cheerful and carefree manner
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪt ˌhɑrtɪd weɪ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt ˌhɑːtɪd weɪ / (BrE)
in a cheerful and relaxed manner; free from seriousness or worry.

Examples

In a light-hearted way
Even though the subject was difficult, he approached it in a light-hearted way.
Let's discuss the issue in a light-hearted way to ease the tension.
She told the story in a light-hearted way to make everyone smile.
