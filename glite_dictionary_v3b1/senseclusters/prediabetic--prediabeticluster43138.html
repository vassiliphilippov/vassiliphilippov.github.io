prediabetic

adjective

4.8

having blood sugar levels higher than normal but not high enough to be diagnosed as diabetes

Examples

Prediabetic condition
People who are prediabetic should monitor their diet and exercise regularly.
A prediabetic condition can often be reversed with healthy lifestyle changes.
