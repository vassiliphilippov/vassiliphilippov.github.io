prediabetic

adjective
at risk for diabetes
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːdaɪəˈbɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌpriːdaɪəˈbiːtɪk / (BrE)
having blood sugar levels higher than normal but not high enough to be diagnosed as diabetes

Examples

Prediabetic condition
People who are prediabetic should monitor their diet and exercise regularly.
A prediabetic condition can often be reversed with healthy lifestyle changes.
Doctors may recommend regular blood tests for prediabetic patients to track their glucose levels.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: diabetic - having diabetes
  • degree lower: normoglycemic - having normal blood sugar levels
