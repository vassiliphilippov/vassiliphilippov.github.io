multitool

noun
compact multi-function tool
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌtuːl / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌtuːl / (BrE)
a compact hand tool that combines several different functions or implements in one device

Examples

A pocket multitool
He always carries a multitool in his backpack for emergencies.
The multitool includes pliers, a knife, and a screwdriver.
With her multitool, she quickly fixed the broken bicycle chain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toolkit - a set of tools kept together for a specific purpose
