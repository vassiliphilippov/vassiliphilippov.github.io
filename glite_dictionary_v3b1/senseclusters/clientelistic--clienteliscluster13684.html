clientelistic

adjective
political exchange system
Pronunciation: / ˌklaɪəntəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌklaɪəntɪˈlɪstɪk /; / ˌklaɪəntəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to a system where goods, services, or favors are exchanged for political or personal support, often outside formal institutions

Examples

Many developing countries struggle with clientelistic practices in their political systems.

Many political systems struggle with clientelistic networks that undermine fair competition.
