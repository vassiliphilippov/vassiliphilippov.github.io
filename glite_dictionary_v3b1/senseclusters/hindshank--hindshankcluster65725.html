hindshank

noun
cut of meat from back leg
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪndˌʃæŋk / (AmE), / ˈhaɪndˌʃæŋk / (BrE)
a cut of meat taken from the upper part of the rear leg of an animal, especially cattle or lamb

Examples

A hindshank cut
Lamb hindshank is often used for slow-cooked stews and hearty dishes.
She asked the butcher for a hindshank to prepare a traditional Italian osso buco.
The chef braised the hindshank for hours until the meat was tender and flavorful.
