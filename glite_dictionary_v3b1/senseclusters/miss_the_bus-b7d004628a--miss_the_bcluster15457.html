miss the bus

verb
fail to catch a bus; miss an opportunity
Pronunciation: / mɪs ðə bʌs / (AmE), / mɪs ðə bʌs / (BrE)
to fail to get on a bus before it leaves; (figurative) to miss an opportunity.

Examples

To miss the bus stop
He realized he had forgotten his wallet just as he was about to miss the bus.
If you miss the bus in the morning, you might end up being late for school.
She always feels anxious that she'll miss the bus on her way to work.
