automatic pistol

noun
handgun that fires automatically while trigger is held
4.4 4.2
Pronunciation: /ɔː.tə.ˈmæt.ɪk ˈpɪs.təl/ /; / /ɑː.tə.ˈmæt.ɪk ˈpɪs.təl/ (AmE), /ɔː.tə.ˈmæt.ɪk ˈpɪs.təl/ (BrE)
