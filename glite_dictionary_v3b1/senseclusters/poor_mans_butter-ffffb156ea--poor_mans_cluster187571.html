poor man's butter

noun
a cheap substitute for butter
Pronunciation: / pʊr mænz ˈbʌtər / (AmE), / pɔː mænz ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
a cheap alternative or substitute for butter, such as margarine or peanut butter, used when butter is considered too expensive or unavailable

Examples
