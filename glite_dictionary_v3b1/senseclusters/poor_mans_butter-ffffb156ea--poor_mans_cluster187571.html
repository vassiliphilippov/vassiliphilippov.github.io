poor man's butter

noun
a cheap substitute for butter
5.2
Pronunciation: / pʊr mænz ˈbʌtər / (AmE), / pɔː mænz ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
a cheap alternative or substitute for butter, such as margarine or peanut butter, used when butter is considered too expensive or unavailable

Examples

Poor man's butter substitute
During the war, people resorted to using margarine as poor man's butter.
Peanut butter has long been considered the poor man's butter in many households.
She topped her toast with avocado, calling it a poor man's butter.
