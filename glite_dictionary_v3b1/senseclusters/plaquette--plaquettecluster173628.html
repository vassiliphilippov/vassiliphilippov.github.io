plaquette

noun
small brochure
9.4
Pronunciation: / plæˈkɛt / (AmE), / plæˈkɛt / (BrE)
a small printed brochure or pamphlet, usually created to provide information or publicity

Examples

Museum plaquette
The museum provided a plaquette to guide visitors through the main exhibits.
Each conference attendee received a plaquette with the event schedule and speaker bios.
A colorful plaquette advertised the new community center opening next month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: booklet - a small, thin book with a paper cover
  • synonym: brochure - a small book or magazine containing information about a product or service
  • synonym: leaflet - a small printed sheet of paper for distribution
