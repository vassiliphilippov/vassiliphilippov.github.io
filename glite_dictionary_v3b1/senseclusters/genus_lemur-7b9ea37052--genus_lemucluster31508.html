genus Lemur

noun
type genus of Lemuridae (lemurs)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈliːmjər / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈliːmjʊə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Lemuridae, comprising several species of lemurs found in Madagascar.

Examples

Conservation efforts are crucial for the survival of animals in the genus Lemur.

The genus Lemur includes several species that are native to Madagascar.
