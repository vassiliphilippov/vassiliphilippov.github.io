slow lane

noun
lane for slower-moving vehicles
Pronunciation: / sloʊ leɪn / (AmE), / sləʊ leɪn / (BrE)
the lane on a road or highway designated for vehicles moving at slower speeds than other traffic

Examples

Drive in the slow lane
During rush hour, the slow lane was backed up for several miles.
He drove in the slow lane so he could enjoy the scenery at a relaxed pace.
Trucks are required to stay in the slow lane on this stretch of highway.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fast lane - lane for vehicles moving at faster speeds
