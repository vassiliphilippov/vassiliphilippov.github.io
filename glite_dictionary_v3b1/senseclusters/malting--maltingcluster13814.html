malting

noun
malt production facility
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔltɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɔːltɪŋ / (BrE)
a building or facility where grain is converted into malt for brewing or distilling

Examples

Old malting building
The old malting was converted into a museum about the town's brewing history.
Workers at the malting processed tons of barley every week for the local distillery.
After the fire, the owners rebuilt the malting with modern equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brewery - a place where beer is produced
  • synonym: malt house - a building where malt is made
