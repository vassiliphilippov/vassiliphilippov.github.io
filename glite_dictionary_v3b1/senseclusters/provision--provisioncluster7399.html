Synonyms

  • stipulation

    stipulation is a specific condition written into an agreement, while provision is any requirement stated in a legal document.
  • term
    3.3 150/m
    term often refers to a named condition within an agreement, while provision means a requirement stated in a legal document.
  • proviso
    8.4 1/m

  • clause
    5.3 13/m
