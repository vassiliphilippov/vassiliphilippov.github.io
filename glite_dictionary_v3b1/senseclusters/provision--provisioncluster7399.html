Examples

A provision in a contract
According to the new provision, all employees must complete safety training annually.
The contract contains a provision that allows either party to terminate with notice.
They added a provision to the agreement to cover unexpected expenses.

Synonyms

  • stipulation
    6.6 1.57/m
    stipulation is a specific condition written into an agreement, while provision is any requirement stated in a legal document.
  • term
    3.3 154/m
    term often refers to a named condition within an agreement, while provision means a requirement stated in a legal document.
  • proviso
    8.4 1.56/m
    proviso gives a specific exception or restriction in a document, while provision names a general condition in an agreement.
  • clause
    5.3 13/m
    clause is a distinct section or paragraph in a contract, while provision is the condition or requirement stated inside that section.
