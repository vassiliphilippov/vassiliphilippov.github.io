smuggled

adjective
illegally imported or distributed
Pronunciation: / ˈsmʌgəld / (AmE), / ˈsmʌgəld / (BrE)
brought into or distributed within a country illegally, especially to avoid taxes or restrictions.

Examples

Smuggled goods
Customs officials seized a shipment of smuggled electronics at the border.
He was arrested for selling smuggled cigarettes in the market.
The museum displayed artifacts that were later found to be smuggled into the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contraband - goods that are imported or exported illegally
  • antonym: legal - permitted by law, not forbidden
