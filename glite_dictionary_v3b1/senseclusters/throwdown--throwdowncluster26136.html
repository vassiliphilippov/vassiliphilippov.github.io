throwdown

noun
violent fight
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a sudden and intense physical fight or brawl, often involving violence

Examples

A parking lot throwdown
The heated argument quickly escalated into a throwdown in the parking lot.
Security had to break up a throwdown between rival fans after the game.
Rumors spread that there would be a throwdown at the party later that night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brawl - a noisy and violent fight, usually involving several people
  • antonym: peace - a state of calm and absence of conflict
  • synonym: scuffle - a short, confused fight or struggle
