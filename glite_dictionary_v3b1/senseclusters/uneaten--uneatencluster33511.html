uneaten

adjective
not consumed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈiːtən / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːtən / (BrE)

Examples

Uneaten food
There was a plate of uneaten vegetables left on the table after dinner.
She wrapped the uneaten cake and put it in the fridge for later.
The picnic ended with plenty of uneaten sandwiches remaining in the basket.
