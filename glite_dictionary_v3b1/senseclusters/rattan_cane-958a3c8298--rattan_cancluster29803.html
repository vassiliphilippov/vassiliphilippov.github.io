rattan cane

noun
stem of rattan palm used in furniture and crafts
Pronunciation: / rəˈtæn keɪn / (AmE), / rəˈtæn keɪn / (BrE)
the long, flexible stem of certain climbing palms, especially of the genus Calamus, used to make furniture, baskets, and walking sticks

Examples

A rattan cane chair
He bought a beautiful chair made from rattan cane at the market.
Many antique walking sticks feature handles crafted from rattan cane.
The artisan skillfully wove the rattan cane into a sturdy basket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cane - the hollow stem of various plants, especially grasses like bamboo or sugarcane, used for furniture and sticks
  • synonym: wicker - pliable twigs, typically of willow, used for weaving baskets or furniture
