white-faced hornet

noun
North American black-and-white hornet
6.7 6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪt feɪst ˈhɔrnɪt / (AmE), / ˈwaɪt feɪst ˈhɔːnɪt / (BrE)
A large black-and-white hornet native to North America, known for its distinctive pale face and aggressive behavior when threatened.

Examples
