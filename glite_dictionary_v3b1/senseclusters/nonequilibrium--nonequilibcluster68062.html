nonequilibrium

adjective
not in balance
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪˈkwɪlɪbriəm / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪˈkwɪlɪbriəm / (BrE)
not in a state of balance or steady condition, especially in scientific or technical contexts.

Examples

Nonequilibrium conditions
The chemical reaction was observed under nonequilibrium conditions to study its unique properties.
Many natural systems exist in a nonequilibrium state due to constant energy input.
Researchers analyzed the nonequilibrium dynamics of the particles after the sudden temperature change.

Related Terms

  • antonym: equilibrium - a state of balance or stability
  • synonym: unstable - likely to change or not firmly fixed
