throw things at the wall

verb
try different ideas to see what works
5.2
Pronunciation: / θroʊ θɪŋz æt ðə wɔl / (AmE), / θrəʊ θɪŋz æt ðə wɔːl / (BrE)
to try out a number of ideas or methods to see which one is successful or effective; to experiment without a clear plan in the hope that something will work

Examples

To throw things at the wall to test
During brainstorming sessions, we often throw things at the wall to encourage creativity.
If you throw things at the wall, you might discover an unexpected solution.
The team decided to throw things at the wall until they found a winning strategy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: experiment - a test done to discover whether something works
  • synonym: trial and error - the process of experimenting to find a solution
