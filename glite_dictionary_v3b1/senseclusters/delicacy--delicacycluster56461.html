delicacy

noun
rare or luxurious food
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛlɪkəsi / (AmE), / ˈdɛlɪkəsi / (BrE)
a rare or luxurious food that is considered highly desirable or special, often because of its unique flavor or limited availability

Examples

A local delicacy
Caviar is considered a delicacy in many parts of the world.
Insects are a common delicacy in some Asian cuisines.
The restaurant specializes in local delicacies served with modern flair.

Related Terms

  • antonym: staple - a basic or regular food item
  • synonym: treat - something special to eat or drink; often sweet or rare
