Urtica pipulifera

noun
a species of European nettle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɚˈtaɪkə pɪˌpjulɪˈfɪrə / (AmE), / ɜːˈtɪkə pɪˌpjuːlɪˈfɪərə / (BrE)
An annual European nettle with stinging leaves and small clusters of green flowers.

Examples

Urtica pipulifera plant
Urtica pipulifera thrives in disturbed soils throughout southern Europe.
Gardeners should handle Urtica pipulifera with care because of its stinging hairs.
The small green flowers of Urtica pipulifera appear in dense clusters during early summer.
