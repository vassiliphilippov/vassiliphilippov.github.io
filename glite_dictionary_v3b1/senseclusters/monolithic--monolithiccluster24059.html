monolithic

adjective
large and solid in size or form
5.7 6.2
Pronunciation: /mɑː.nə.ˈlɪ.θɪk/ (AmE), /mɒ.nə.ˈlɪ.θɪk/ (BrE)
Imposing in size, bulk, or solidity; massive and uniform in form or structure.

Examples

The skyscraper's monolithic façade dominated the city skyline.
