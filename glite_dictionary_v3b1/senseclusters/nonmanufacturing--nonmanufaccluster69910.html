nonmanufacturing

adjective
not involving production
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnmænjuˈfæktʃərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnmænjuˈfæktʃərɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to industries, activities, or sectors that do not involve the production of goods in factories

Examples

Nonmanufacturing industries
The company expanded into nonmanufacturing sectors such as finance and consulting.
Many jobs in the nonmanufacturing industries require strong communication skills.
Government policies are encouraging growth in nonmanufacturing areas of the economy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manufacturing - relating to the production of goods in factories
  • synonym: service - relating to providing services rather than producing goods
