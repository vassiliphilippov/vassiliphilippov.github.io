pantryman

noun
male household servant in charge of wines and table
1.1 8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpæntrɪˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈpæn.tri.mæn / (BrE)
a male servant, typically in a large household, who is responsible for managing wines and overseeing the dining table, often acting as the head of pantry-related service

Examples

The pantryman manages wine
As the household's pantryman, he was trusted with the keys to the wine cellar.
During grand banquets, the pantryman supervised the staff as they set the table.
The pantryman ensured that the finest wines were served at every dinner.
