Examples

Synonyms

    3.1 50/m
    indicate points to something directly or by a sign, while show is broader and can display or make things known.
  • point
    1.0 170/m
    point uses a finger or object to direct attention, while show can be physical or more general in making something known.
  • point out
    1.8 94/m
    point out carefully directs attention to a detail or fact, while show is more general about making things visible or known.
  • designate
    4.6 4.66/m
    designate formally names or marks a person or place, while show is more common and simply makes something clear or visible.
  • signify
    3.7 9.56/m
    signify means to mean or be a sign of something, while show is to point out or make a thing or place known.
