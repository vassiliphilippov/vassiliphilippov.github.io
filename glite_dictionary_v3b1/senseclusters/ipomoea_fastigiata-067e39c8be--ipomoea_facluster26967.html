A tropical American prostrate or climbing herbaceous perennial plant with a large starchy root; sometimes regarded as a source of the sweet potato.

Examples

Some botanists believe that Ipomoea fastigiata could be an ancestor of the modern sweet potato.
The large root of the Ipomoea fastigiata is often used as a food source in some regions.
