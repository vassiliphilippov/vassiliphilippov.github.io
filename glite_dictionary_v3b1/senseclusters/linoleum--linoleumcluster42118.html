linoleum

noun
durable floor covering material
6.5 3.7
Pronunciation: /lɪ.ˈnoʊ.liː.əm/ /; / /lə.ˈnoʊ.liː.əm/ (AmE), /lɪ.ˈnəʊ.li.əm/ /; / /lə.ˈnəʊ.li.əm/ (BrE)
A type of durable, washable floor covering made from solidified linseed oil and other materials, often used in kitchens and bathrooms.
