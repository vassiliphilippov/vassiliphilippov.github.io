unrecoverably

adverb
irreversibly
7.8
Pronunciation: /ʌn.rɪ.ˈkʌv.ər.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.rɪ.ˈkʌv.ə.rə.bəl/ (BrE)

Examples

Once trust is broken unrecoverably, it is very difficult to rebuild a relationship.
