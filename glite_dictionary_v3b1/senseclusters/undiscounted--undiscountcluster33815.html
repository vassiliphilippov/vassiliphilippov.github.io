undiscounted

adjective
full price
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪsˈkaʊntɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪsˈkaʊntɪd / (BrE)
not reduced in price or value; at the full original amount or rate

Examples

Undiscounted price
The undiscounted price of the new phone is higher than most people expected.
If you pay the undiscounted rate, you will not receive any special offers.
Tickets purchased at the door are usually undiscounted and more expensive than advance sales.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discounted - sold at a reduced price
  • synonym: full-price - sold at the original price without any reduction
