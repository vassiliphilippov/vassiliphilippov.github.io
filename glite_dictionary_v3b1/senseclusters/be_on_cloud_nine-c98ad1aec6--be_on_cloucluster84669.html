be on cloud nine

verb
be extremely happy
3.7
Pronunciation: / bi ɑn klaʊd naɪn / (AmE), / bi ɒn klaʊd naɪn / (BrE)

Examples

She was on cloud nine after hearing about her promotion.
They were on cloud nine all weekend after winning the championship.
