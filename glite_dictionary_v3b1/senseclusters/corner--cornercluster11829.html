corner

verb
gain exclusive control over a market
7.7 0.93/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…îrn…ôr / (AmE), / Ààk…îÀên…ô / (BrE)
to gain exclusive control over the supply or trade of a particular commodity or market

Examples

To corner the market
He managed to corner the local market for organic vegetables through strategic partnerships.
Speculators tried to corner silver in the 1980s, causing prices to soar.
The company attempted to corner the coffee market by buying up all available stock.

Synonyms

  • corner the market
    7.2 0.43/m
    corner the market means gaining control of supply to set prices and shut out rivals, while corner is the verb for taking exclusive trade control.

  • synonym: monopolize - to obtain exclusive possession or control of something
