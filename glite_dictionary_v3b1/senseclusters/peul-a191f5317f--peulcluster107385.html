Peul

noun
West African Fulani language
Pronunciation: / pɜl / (AmE), / pɜːl / (BrE)
A West African language belonging to the Fulani people, used widely as a lingua franca from Senegal to Chad.

Examples

Peul language
He is fluent in Peul and often acts as a translator in his village.
Many official documents in the region are published in both French and Peul.
The Peul language is spoken across many countries in West Africa.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Fula - The Fula language, an alternative name for Peul spoken in West Africa
  • synonym: Fulani - An alternative name for the language and people known as Peul
