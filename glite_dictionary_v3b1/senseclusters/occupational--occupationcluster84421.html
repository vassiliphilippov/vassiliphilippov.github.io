occupational

adjective
relating to a person's job or profession
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑː.kjəˈpeɪ.ʃən.əl / (AmE), / ˌɒ.kjəˈpeɪ.ʃən.əl / (BrE)
relating to a job, profession, or occupation, especially the activities or risks involved in one's work.

Examples

Occupational training
Her occupational training prepared her for a career in nursing.
Several health problems in the factory were found to be occupational in nature.
Wearing safety goggles reduces the risk of occupational hazards.
