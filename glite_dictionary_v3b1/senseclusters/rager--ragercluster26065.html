rager

noun
energetic partygoer
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈreɪdʒə / (BrE)
a person who attends or enjoys wild, loud parties and parties energetically

Examples

A rager at parties
Everyone knew that Mark was a rager who could keep the party going all night.
At college, she quickly gained a reputation as a rager who never missed a big event.
The group invited a well-known rager to make sure the celebration would be lively.

Related Terms

  • antonym: introvert - a person who prefers quiet environments and avoids parties
  • synonym: party animal - a person who loves going to parties and social events
