carrying

adjective
for holding or transport
Pronunciation: / ˈkæriɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkæriɪŋ / (BrE)
intended or suitable for holding, supporting, or transporting objects from one place to another

Examples

Carrying case
She bought a new carrying case to protect her laptop during travel.
The backpack has a special carrying handle for easy transport.
He designed a carrying bag that fits all his camera equipment safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: portable - able to be easily carried or moved
  • antonym: stationary - not intended to be moved or carried
