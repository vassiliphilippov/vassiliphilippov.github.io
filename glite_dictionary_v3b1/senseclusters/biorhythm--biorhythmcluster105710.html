biorhythm

noun
natural biological cycle
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪoʊˌrɪðəm / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəʊˌrɪðəm / (BrE)
a regular, natural cycle in the biological processes of a living organism, such as sleep–wake or hormonal cycles

Examples

Daily biorhythm cycle
Your biorhythm can affect how alert you feel at different times of the day.
Scientists study the biorhythm of animals to understand their sleep patterns.
Traveling across time zones can disrupt your natural biorhythm and cause jet lag.
