thundershower

noun
brief storm with thunder and lightning
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌndərˌʃaʊər / (AmE), / ˈθʌndəˌʃaʊə / (BrE)
A brief rainstorm accompanied by thunder and lightning.

Examples

A thundershower storm
A sudden thundershower forced the hikers to take cover under the trees.
After the intense heat, a thundershower brought welcome relief to the city.
The afternoon thundershower cooled the air and left puddles in the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thunderstorm - a storm with thunder, lightning, and usually heavy rain
