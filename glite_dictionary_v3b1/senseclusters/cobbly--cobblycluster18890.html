cobbly

adjective
uneven like cobblestones
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑbli / (AmE), / ˈkɒbli / (BrE)
covered with or resembling cobblestones; uneven or rough like cobbles

Examples

A cobbly road
The old road was cobbly and hard to walk on.
We cycled along a cobbly path that wound through the village.
Her suitcase rattled loudly over the cobbly street in the historic district.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bumpy - having many raised areas or bumps
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
  • synonym: uneven - not level or smooth
