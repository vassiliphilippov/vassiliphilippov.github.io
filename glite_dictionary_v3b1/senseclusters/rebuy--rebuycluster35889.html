rebuy

verb
to buy again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbaɪ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbaɪ / (BrE)
to purchase something again after having lost, sold, or given it away

Examples

To rebuy a phone
After losing his wallet, he had to rebuy all of his identification cards.
She decided to rebuy the book she had given away years ago.
Many players choose to rebuy chips after being eliminated from the tournament.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repurchase - to buy something again
  • antonym: sell - to give something to someone else in exchange for money
