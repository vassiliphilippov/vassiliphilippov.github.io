rose geranium

noun
fragrant southern African plant with pink flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊz dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˈrəʊz dʒəˈreɪniəm / (BrE)
a southern African plant of the geranium family with fragrant three- to five-lobed leaves and pink flowers, widely grown for its scent

Examples

A rose geranium plant
He planted a rose geranium in the herb garden to enjoy its beautiful pink flowers and fragrance.
She added a few fresh rose geranium leaves to the potpourri for a pleasant scent.
The essential oil of rose geranium is often used in perfumes and aromatherapy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geranium - any plant of the genus Geranium, with lobed leaves and showy flowers
