float

verb
propose an idea tentatively
2.3
Pronunciation: / floʊt / (AmE), / fləʊt / (BrE)
to suggest, circulate, or propose an idea or plan for consideration, often tentatively

Examples

To float an idea
Before making any changes, he plans to float the proposal with the board.
She floated the idea of extending deadlines, but the team was hesitant at first.
The company decided to float a new strategy during the meeting to gauge reactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: propose - to put forward an idea or plan for discussion
  • synonym: suggest - to mention an idea for consideration
