antidiarrheal

noun
drug that treats diarrhea
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌdaɪəˈriəl / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌdaɪəˈrɪəl / (BrE)
A drug used to control or stop diarrhea.

Examples

An antidiarrheal drug
It is important not to overuse antidiarrheal drugs without medical advice.
Many travelers carry antidiarrheal medications when visiting countries with different diets.
Your doctor may recommend an antidiarrheal to manage symptoms of acute diarrhea.
