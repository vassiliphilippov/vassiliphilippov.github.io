unhealthy

adjective
detrimental to health
1.0 1.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛlθi / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛlθi / (BrE)
not good for your health; likely to cause illness, injury, or harm

Examples

An unhealthy diet
Doctors warn that eating too much junk food is unhealthy for children.
She gave up her unhealthy habit of smoking last year.
Working long hours in a stressful environment can be very unhealthy for your mind and body.
She developed an unhealthy obsession with dieting.
The air in the crowded room quickly became unhealthy to breathe.
Years of smoking had left him unhealthy and weak.
Doctors warn that an unhealthy lifestyle can lead to serious medical problems.
She realized her frequent consumption of soda was an unhealthy habit.
The city’s air quality has reached unhealthy levels due to pollution.
