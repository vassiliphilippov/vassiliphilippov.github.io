motu

noun
Papuan ethnic group
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtuː / (AmE), / ˈməʊtuː / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous ethnic group living near Port Moresby on the Papuan coast of Papua New Guinea

Examples

Motu people
The motu have a rich tradition of canoe building and fishing along the coast.
Many motu people still speak their native language in daily life.
A motu elder shared stories about their ancestors with the younger generation.
