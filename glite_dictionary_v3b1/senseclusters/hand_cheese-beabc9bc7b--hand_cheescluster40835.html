hand cheese

noun
cheese molded by hand
8.2
Pronunciation: / hænd tʃiːz / (AmE), / hænd tʃiːz / (BrE)
a semi-hard cheese originally shaped or molded by hand, typically with a strong flavor and aroma

Examples

A hand cheese piece
In some regions, hand cheese is enjoyed with a splash of vinegar and a sprinkle of herbs.
She served slices of hand cheese with onions and bread for a traditional appetizer.
Unlike mass-produced cheeses, hand cheese is shaped by craftsmen using only their hands.
