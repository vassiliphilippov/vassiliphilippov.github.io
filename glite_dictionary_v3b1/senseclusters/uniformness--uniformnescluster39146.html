uniformness

noun
lack of diversity or variation
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːnɪfɔːrmnəs / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪfɔːmnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being uniform; lack of diversity or variation

Examples

The uniformness of projects
She criticized the uniformness of the students' projects, hoping for more creativity next time.
The uniformness in the company’s dress code ensured a professional appearance.
The uniformness of the landscape made the long drive monotonous.
