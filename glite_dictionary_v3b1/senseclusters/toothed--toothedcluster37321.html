Synonyms

    5.4 0.00/m

  • serrate
    7.5 0.03/m
    serrate describes a saw-like margin with teeth pointing toward the tip, while toothed is a more general tooth-like edge term.
  • saw-toothed
    6.4 0.01/m
    saw-toothed has sharp, regular notches like a saw's blade in tools or roofs, while toothed can mean any tooth-like edge.
  • dentate
    9.4 0.00/m
    dentate names toothlike edges in biology and botany, while toothed is the common, general word for such edges.
  • jagged
    3.6 1/m
    jagged means rough, sharply uneven edges often irregular, while toothed describes a repeated tooth-like pattern along an edge.
  • notched
    3.9 0.00/m
    notched has one or more cuts or small indentations along an edge, while toothed suggests many tooth-like projections.
