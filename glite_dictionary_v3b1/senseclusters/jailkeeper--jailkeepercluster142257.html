jailkeeper

noun
prison guard
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒeɪlˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪlˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person responsible for guarding and managing a jail or prison and its inmates

Examples

The jailkeeper guards prisoners
The jailkeeper made sure all the inmates were accounted for before locking the doors.
As a jailkeeper, she was responsible for maintaining order and safety in the facility.
The jailkeeper greeted the new staff member and explained the daily routines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jailer - a person in charge of a jail and its prisoners
  • synonym: prison guard - a person who supervises prisoners in a prison
  • antonym: prisoner - a person who is kept in prison as punishment
