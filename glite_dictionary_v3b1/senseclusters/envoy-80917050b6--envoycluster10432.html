Envoy

noun
open-source proxy for microservices
Pronunciation: / ˈɛn.vɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛn.vɔɪ / (BrE)
(computing) an open-source edge and service proxy used in cloud-native architectures to route, observe, and secure network traffic between microservices and services.

Examples

Envoy proxy software
We deployed Envoy as a sidecar to route and monitor traffic between our microservices in production.
During the outage, Envoy logs helped the team identify which service was failing to accept connections.
They configured Envoy to enforce TLS and collect metrics for every request across the cluster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: proxy - a server or program that intermediates requests between clients and other servers
  • synonym: reverse proxy - a proxy that routes client requests to backend servers and can provide load balancing or security
