marked

adjective
singled out for attention or fate
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkt / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkt / (BrE)
singled out for special attention or especially for an imminent, usually negative, outcome

Examples

Marked man
After testifying, he became a marked man in his old neighborhood.
His actions at work have made him a marked employee among management.
The witness knew she was marked after revealing the secret.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anonymous - not identified by name; not singled out
  • synonym: targeted - made a target of; singled out for negative attention
