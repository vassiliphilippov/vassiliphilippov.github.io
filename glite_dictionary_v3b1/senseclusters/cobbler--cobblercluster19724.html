cobbler

noun
fruit dessert with biscuit topping
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑblɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒblə / (BrE)
a baked fruit dessert with a thick, biscuit-like topping, typically without a bottom crust

Examples

Peach cobbler dessert
Blueberry cobbler is a summer favorite in many Southern homes.
She baked a delicious peach cobbler for dessert last night.
The restaurant's apple cobbler came with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crumble - a baked fruit dessert with a crumbly flour-butter topping
