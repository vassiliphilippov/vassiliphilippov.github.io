bind

verb
to provide with a binding (esp. books)
Pronunciation: / baɪnd / (AmE), / baɪnd / (BrE)
to provide (a book or similar item) with a protective cover or binding

Examples

To bind a book
Many publishers bind special editions using high-quality materials.
She learned how to bind her own notebooks during the bookmaking workshop.
The library decided to bind the rare manuscripts in leather for preservation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rebind - to bind (a book) again, usually with a new cover
