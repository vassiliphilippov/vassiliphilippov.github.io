bride and groom

noun
couple being married
Pronunciation: / braɪd ənd ɡruːm / (AmE), / braɪd ənd ɡruːm / (BrE)
the couple who are getting married at a wedding ceremony

Examples

The bride and groom at wedding
As the bride and groom exchanged vows, their families watched with joy.
Friends decorated the car for the bride and groom to drive away in after the reception.
The photographer asked the bride and groom to pose for pictures after the ceremony.
