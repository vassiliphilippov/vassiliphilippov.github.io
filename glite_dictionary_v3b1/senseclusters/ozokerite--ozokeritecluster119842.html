ozokerite

noun
waxy mineral used in candles
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊzəˈkɛraɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊzəˈkɛəriːt / (BrE)
A naturally occurring, waxy mineral composed of a mixture of hydrocarbons, found with petroleum and used in making ceresin and candles.

Examples

Natural ozokerite mineral
Geologists discovered a rich vein of ozokerite near the old oil field.
Pure ozokerite is often refined to make ceresin for industrial use.
The factory imports ozokerite to manufacture high-quality candles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceresin - a refined wax made from ozokerite or similar mineral waxes
