self-contemplation

noun
examination of one's own thoughts and feelings
2.1 6.3
Pronunciation: /self.kən.tem.ˈpleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /səlf.kən.tem.ˈpleɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /self.kɒn.tem.ˈpleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /səlf.kɒn.tem.ˈpleɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)

Examples
