proboscis

noun
long flexible snout or trunk of an animal
9.9
Pronunciation: / proʊˈbɑːsɪs / (AmE), / prəˈbɒsɪs / (BrE)
the long flexible trunk or snout of an animal, especially that of an elephant or certain insects

Examples

Elephant proboscis
His comically long nose resembled the proboscis of an anteater.
Scientists observed the butterfly unfurling its proboscis to sip nectar from the flower.
The elephant used its proboscis to reach the high branches laden with fruit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snout - the projecting nose and mouth of an animal
