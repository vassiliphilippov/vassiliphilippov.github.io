containment

noun
the act of restraining or controlling something
6.1
Pronunciation: / kənˈteɪnmənt / (AmE), / kənˈteɪnmənt / (BrE)
the action of keeping something harmful under control or within limits; the act of preventing the spread of something

Examples

Containment of fire
Firefighters focused on the containment of the blaze before it reached the nearby woods.
International organizations worked towards the containment of the outbreak to protect neighboring countries.
The government's policy emphasized the containment of extremist groups within its borders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restraint - the action of keeping something under control
  • antonym: spread - the process of extending over a larger area
