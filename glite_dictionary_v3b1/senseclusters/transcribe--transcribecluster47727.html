transcribe

verb
write out spoken words or notes
Pronunciation: / trænˈskraɪb / (AmE), / trænˈskraɪb / (BrE)
to write or type out a spoken message, notes, or other material in full form

Examples

To transcribe a lecture
He spent the afternoon transcribing his professor's lecture notes for the class.
Researchers transcribed the interviews to analyze the participants' responses.
The secretary will transcribe the meeting minutes from the audio recording.

Related Terms

  • synonym: record - to set down in writing or some other permanent form
  • antonym: translate - to express words in another language
