intellectual property

noun
legal rights over creative works
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.t̬əˈlɛk.tʃu.əl ˈprɑː.pɚ.ti / (AmE), / ˌɪn.tɪˈlɛk.tjʊ.əl ˈprɒ.pə.ti / (BrE)
Legal rights that protect the creations of the mind, such as inventions, literary and artistic works, designs, and symbols.

Examples

Protect intellectual property
Artists are often concerned about unauthorized use of their intellectual property.
Patents, copyrights, and trademarks are all forms of intellectual property.
The company invested millions to protect its intellectual property from competitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: copyright - exclusive legal right to creative works
  • synonym: patent - exclusive right granted for an invention
  • synonym: trademark - distinctive sign legally registered for business
