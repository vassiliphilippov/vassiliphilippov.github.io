gall

noun
deep and bitter anger or resentment
6.6
Pronunciation: / ɡɔl / (AmE), / ɡɔːl / (BrE)
a feeling of deep and bitter anger or resentment, often directed at someone's actions

Examples

A feeling of gall
His harsh words left a sense of gall lingering between them for days.
She couldn't conceal the gall she felt after being unfairly passed over for promotion.
The decision was met with gall by those who felt betrayed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bitterness - anger and disappointment at being treated unfairly
  • synonym: resentment - a feeling of persistent ill will at a perceived insult or injury
