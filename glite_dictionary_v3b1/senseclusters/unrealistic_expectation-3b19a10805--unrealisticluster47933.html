unrealistic expectation

noun
impractical or unachievable expectation
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnriːəˈlɪstɪk ˌɛksˌpɛkˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌʌnriːəˈlɪstɪk ˌɛkspɛkˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
an expectation that is not practical, reasonable, or likely to be achieved

Examples

Unrealistic expectations about salary
Many people enter the workforce with unrealistic expectations about their starting salary.
Teachers warn students against having unrealistic expectations about how quickly they will master the material.
The company apologized for setting unrealistic expectations during the product launch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expectation - a strong belief that something will happen or be the case
  • antonym: realistic expectation - an expectation based on what is possible or likely
