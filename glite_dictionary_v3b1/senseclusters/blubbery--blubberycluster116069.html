blubbery

adjective
swollen or thick with fat
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌbəri / (AmE), / ˈblʌbəri / (BrE)
(of a part of the body or a person) swollen with or full of soft, thick fat; resembling or covered with blubber.

Examples

The blubbery lips
He pressed his blubbery lips together in disapproval.
She was tired of being teased for her blubbery cheeks as a child.
The seal's blubbery body helped it survive the freezing water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fat - having a large amount of excess flesh
  • antonym: skinny - very thin, lacking fat
