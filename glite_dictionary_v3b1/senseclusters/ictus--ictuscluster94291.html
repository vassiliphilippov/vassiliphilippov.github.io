ictus

noun
sudden occurrence of a disease
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪk.təs / (AmE), / ˈɪk.təs / (BrE)

Examples

After the initial ictus, the patient was rushed to the emergency room.
Doctors monitored her closely after she experienced a cardiac ictus during the procedure.
