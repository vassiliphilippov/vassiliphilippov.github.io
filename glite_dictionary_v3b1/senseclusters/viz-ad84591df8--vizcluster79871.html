viz.

adverb
namely; that is to say (abbreviation)
Pronunciation: / vɪz / (AmE), / vɪz / (BrE)
namely; that is to say; used to introduce examples or further explanation.

Examples

Used viz. to explain
He listed his favorite hobbies, viz. reading, hiking, and cooking.
Several factors must be considered, viz. cost, time, and labor.
The solution includes two key ingredients, viz. hydrogen and oxygen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: namely - used to introduce specific examples or details
