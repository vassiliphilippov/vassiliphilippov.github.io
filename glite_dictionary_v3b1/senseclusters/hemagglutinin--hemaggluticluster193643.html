hemagglutinin

noun
viral surface protein
9.9
Pronunciation: /hiː.mə.ˈglʌ.tɪ.nɪn/ /; / /he.mə.ˈglʌ.tɪ.nɪn/ (AmE), /hiː.mə.ˈglʌ.tɪ.nɪn/ /; / /he.mə.ˈglʌ.tɪ.nɪn/ (BrE)
a protein (especially on the surface of some viruses) or other substance that causes red blood cells to clump together and helps viruses attach to and enter host cells
