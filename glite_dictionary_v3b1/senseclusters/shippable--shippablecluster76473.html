shippable

adjective
able to be shipped
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpəbəl / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpəbəl / (BrE)
able or ready to be sent or delivered to another place, especially by shipping services

Examples

We cannot mark the order as shippable until the packaging is complete.
Once the software update is shippable, customers will receive a notification.
