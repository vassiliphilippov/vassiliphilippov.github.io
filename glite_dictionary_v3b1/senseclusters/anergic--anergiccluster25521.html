anergic

adjective
lacking energy
9.1
Pronunciation: / əˈnɜrdʒɪk / (AmE), / əˈnɜːdʒɪk / (BrE)
lacking physical or mental energy; inactive or listless

Examples

Anergic patient
After the long illness, she felt unusually anergic and struggled to get out of bed.
The patient remained anergic despite several days of rest and medication.
He became anergic during the hot summer, barely able to complete his daily tasks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: energetic - having or showing a lot of energy
  • synonym: lethargic - feeling sluggish and lacking energy
