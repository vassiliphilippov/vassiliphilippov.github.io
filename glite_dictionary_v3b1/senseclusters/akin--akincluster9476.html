akin

adjective
similar in quality or character
Pronunciation: / əˈkɪn / (AmE), / əˈkɪn / (BrE)
having a similar quality or character; related in nature, feeling, or appearance

Examples

Akin to happiness
A sudden sense of joy, akin to relief, washed over her.
His remarks were akin to an apology, though he never actually said sorry.
The siblings' interests are akin, especially their love for classical music.

Related Terms

  • antonym: different - not the same as another; unlike
  • synonym: similar - having a resemblance in appearance, character, or quantity
