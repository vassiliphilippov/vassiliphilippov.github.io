lose

verb
to suffer the loss of a person
Pronunciation: / luːz / (AmE), / luːz / (BrE)
to suffer the loss of a person, especially through death or removal from one's life

Examples

To lose a friend
After a long illness, he lost his mother last year.
Many parents fear they could lose their children in a custody battle.
She never truly recovered from having lost her best friend so suddenly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gain - to acquire or obtain something, especially after a loss
