dissipater

noun
device dispersing energy
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪsɪˌpeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdɪsɪpeɪtə / (BrE)
a device that spreads out heat, energy, or force to prevent buildup or damage

Examples

Heat dissipater
The dissipater in the engine prevents overheating during long drives.
Engineers installed a new dissipater to manage excess energy in the power plant.
A small dissipater helps protect sensitive electronics from heat damage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accumulator - a device that stores energy for later use
  • synonym: heat sink - a device that absorbs and disperses heat from another object
