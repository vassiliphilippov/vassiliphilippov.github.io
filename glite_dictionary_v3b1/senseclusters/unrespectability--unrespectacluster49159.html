unrespectability

noun
lack of respectability; poor reputation
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˌspɛk.təˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˌspɛk.təˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the state or quality of lacking respectability or a good reputation; dishonorableness

Examples

The unrespectability of actions
Political opponents often accuse each other of unrespectability to undermine public trust.
She worried that her cousin's unorthodox lifestyle would bring an air of unrespectability to the family name.
The company's history of financial scandals contributed to its unrespectability among investors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disreputableness - the quality of having a bad reputation
  • antonym: respectability - the quality of being respectable or having a good reputation
