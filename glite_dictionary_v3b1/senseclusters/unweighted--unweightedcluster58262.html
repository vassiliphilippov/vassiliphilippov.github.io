unweighted

adjective
without added weight
3.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈweɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈweɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having extra weight added or attached; performed or measured without additional load.

Examples

Unweighted squats
The athlete performed several unweighted squats before starting her main workout.
In the experiment, the results from the unweighted samples were compared to those with added mass.
He recommended starting with unweighted exercises to learn proper form and prevent injury.
