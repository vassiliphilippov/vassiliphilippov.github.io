Nelson Mandela

proper noun
South African anti-apartheid leader and president
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛl.sən mænˈdɛl.ə / (AmE), / ˈnɛl.sən mænˈdɛl.ə / (BrE)
(1918–2013) South African anti-apartheid leader and statesman, who became the country's first Black president after 27 years in prison
