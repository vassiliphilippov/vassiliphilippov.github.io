rambunctiously

adverb
noisily and energetically
7.5
Pronunciation: / ræmˈbʌŋkʃəsli / (AmE), / ræmˈbʌŋkʃəsli / (BrE)
in a noisy, energetic, and uncontrolled manner

Examples

To rambunctiously play
The puppies ran rambunctiously around the living room, knocking over pillows and toys.
Fans cheered rambunctiously as their team scored the winning goal in the final seconds.
During recess, the students played rambunctiously on the playground, laughing and shouting with excitement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boisterously - in a loud and lively way
  • antonym: calmly - in a quiet and peaceful way
