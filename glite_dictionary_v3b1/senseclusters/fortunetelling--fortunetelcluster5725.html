fortunetelling

noun
practice of predicting the future
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrtʃənˌtɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtʃənˌtɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of predicting a person's future, often as a service for payment

Examples

Fortunetelling with tarot cards
Local fairs often feature booths for tarot readings and fortunetelling.
Many people are skeptical about the accuracy of fortunetelling.
She went to a small shop that specializes in fortunetelling and had her palm read.
