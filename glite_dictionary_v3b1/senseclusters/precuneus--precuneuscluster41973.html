precuneus

noun
brain region
9.9
Pronunciation: / priˈkjuːniəs / (AmE), / priˈkjuːniəs / (BrE)
a region of the brain located in the medial part of the parietal lobe, involved in memory and visual processing

Examples

The precuneus region
The precuneus plays a key role in recalling personal memories.
Researchers found increased activity in the precuneus during visual imagination tasks.
Damage to the precuneus can affect a person's ability to process spatial information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cuneus - a neighboring region of the brain in the occipital lobe
  • synonym: parietal lobe - the part of the brain where the precuneus is located
