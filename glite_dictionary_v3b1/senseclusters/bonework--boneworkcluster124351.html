bonework

noun
working with bones
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊnwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnwɜːk / (BrE)
the process or skill of preparing, carving, repairing, or studying bones, especially for art, display, or scientific purposes

Examples

Bonework skills
The museum displayed ancient bonework tools created by early humans.
She specializes in bonework, restoring fragile skeletons for scientific study.
Intricate bonework can be found in many traditional art forms around the world.
