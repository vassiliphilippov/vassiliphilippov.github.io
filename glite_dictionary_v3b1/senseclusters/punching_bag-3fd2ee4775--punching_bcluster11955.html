punching bag

noun

Pronunciation: /ˈpʌn.tʃɪŋ bæg/ /; / /ˈpən.tʃɪŋ bæg/ (AmE), /ˈpʌn.tʃɪŋ bæg/ /; / /ˈpən.tʃɪŋ bæg/ (BrE)
A person who is a frequent target of another's anger, criticism, or abuse.

Examples

Team's punching bag

I refuse to be your punching bag just because you had a bad day at work.
She always treats her assistant like her personal punching bag whenever she's stressed.
