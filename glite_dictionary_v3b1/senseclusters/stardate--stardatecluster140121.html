stardate

noun
fictional time system
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌdeɪt / (BrE)
a fictional system of timekeeping used in science fiction, especially in the Star Trek universe

In the show, each mission is recorded with a unique stardate to mark the time.
Fans often debate how the stardate system actually works in the Star Trek universe.
