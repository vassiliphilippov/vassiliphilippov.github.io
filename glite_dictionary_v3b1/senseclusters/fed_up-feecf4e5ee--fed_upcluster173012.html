  • tired of
    1.9 50/m
    tired of is a common phrase meaning you have had enough and feel annoyed or bored, while fed up covers the same ongoing feeling.
  • sick and tired
    5.3 5/m
    sick and tired is an emphatic phrase showing strong frustration or exhaustion, while fed up is a more general state of having had enough.
  • browned off
