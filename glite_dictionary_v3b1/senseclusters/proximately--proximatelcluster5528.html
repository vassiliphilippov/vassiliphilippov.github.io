proximately

adverb
near in position or time
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːksɪmətli / (AmE), / ˈprɒksɪmətli / (BrE)
in a way that is near in space, time, or order

Examples

Proximately in time
The two events occurred proximately in the same week.
The houses are proximately situated, making it easy for neighbors to visit each other.
The chapters in the book are proximately arranged to show the progression of ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: closely - in a way that is near in space, time, or relationship
  • antonym: remotely - in a way that is far in space, time, or relationship
