secpar

noun
astronomical distance unit (1 sec of parallax)

Pronunciation: /ˈsek.pɑːr/ (AmE), /ˈsek.pɑː/ (BrE)
A unit of astronomical distance equal to the distance at which a star would have a parallax of one second of arc, approximately 3.262 light-years.
