hospita

noun
female host or guest
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːspɪtə / (AmE), / ˈhɒspɪtə / (BrE)
an archaic term for a female host or guest

Examples

The hospita welcomes guests
In the ancient manor, the hospita welcomed travelers with warmth and generosity.
The letter described a gracious hospita who cared for her guests during their stay.
Old literature sometimes mentions a hospita as the lady of the house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guest - a person who is invited to visit or stay
  • synonym: hostess - a woman who receives or entertains guests
