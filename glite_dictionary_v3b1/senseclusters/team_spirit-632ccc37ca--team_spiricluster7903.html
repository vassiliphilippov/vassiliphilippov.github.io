team spirit

noun

Examples

Good team spirit
Good team spirit can make a big difference in workplace productivity.
Our coach always encourages team spirit before every big game.
The volunteers showed amazing team spirit during the charity event.

Synonyms

  • esprit de corps
    7.9 0.20/m
    esprit de corps is a formal phrase for shared pride, loyalty, and unity, while team spirit is the everyday feeling that binds group members.
  • morale
    3.8 8.17/m
    morale is the current level of confidence and energy in a group, while team spirit is deeper pride and mutual support among members.

  • antonym: selfishness - lack of care for others or group needs
