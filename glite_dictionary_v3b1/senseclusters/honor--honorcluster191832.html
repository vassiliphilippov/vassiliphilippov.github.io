honor

noun
moral integrity; good reputation
2.2 32/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ëÀên…ôr / (AmE), / Àà…ín…ô / (BrE)
the quality of having strong moral principles and a good reputation; the respect that a person receives because of this

Examples

A man of honor
He is known throughout the community as a man of honor.
His actions brought great honor to his family.
She felt it was her honor to be chosen as the team captain.
Serving his country was a matter of honour for him.
She accepted the award with great honour and humility.
The guests rose to their feet to pay honour to the retiring professor.
He valued his honour above material wealth and refused to compromise his principles.
Her actions brought great honour to her family's name.
The soldier was awarded a medal in recognition of his honour and bravery.
