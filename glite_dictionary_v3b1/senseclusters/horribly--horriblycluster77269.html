horribly

adverb
in a dreadful or shocking way
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrəbli / (AmE), / ˈhɒrəbli / (BrE)
In a dreadful, shocking, or extremely unpleasant manner.

Examples

To feel horribly embarrassed
She felt horribly embarrassed when she realized her mistake.
The food was horribly overcooked and almost inedible.
The scene was horribly violent, leaving many witnesses in shock.

Related Terms

  • synonym: terribly - in an extremely bad or serious way
  • antonym: wonderfully - in a way that inspires delight or admiration; very well or impressively
