divider

noun
vertical structure that separates spaces
1.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaɪdər / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪdə / (BrE)
a vertical structure or object that separates areas or spaces, such as a wall or partition between rooms

Examples

Room divider
A bookshelf can serve as a stylish divider between the dining and living areas.
Temporary dividers were installed in the hall for the exhibition.
The office uses a divider to create separate workspaces for staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partition - a structure dividing a space into parts
  • synonym: wall - a continuous vertical brick or stone structure dividing a room, building, or area of land
