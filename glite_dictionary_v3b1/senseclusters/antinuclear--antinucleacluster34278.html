antinuclear

adjective
against cell nuclei
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈnuːkliɚ / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈnjuːkliə / (BrE)
directed against the nuclei of cells, especially referring to antibodies that react with cell nuclei in autoimmune disorders

Examples

Antinuclear antibody test
The doctor ordered an antinuclear antibody test to help diagnose the patient's symptoms.
A positive antinuclear result can indicate the presence of certain autoimmune diseases.
Researchers study antinuclear antibodies to better understand conditions like lupus and rheumatoid arthritis.
