artificial kidney

noun
dialysis machine; kidney replacement device
7.7 3.4
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.tɪ.ˈfɪʃ.əl ˈkɪd.niː/ /; / /ɑːr.tə.ˈfɪʃ.əl ˈkɪd.niː/ (AmE), /ɑː.tɪ.ˈfɪʃ.əl ˈkɪd.niː/ /; / /ɑː.tə.ˈfɪʃ.əl ˈkɪd.niː/ (BrE)
