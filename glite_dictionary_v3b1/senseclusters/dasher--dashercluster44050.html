dasher

noun
churn-stirring tool
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdæʃ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈdæʃ.ə/ (BrE)
a tool used for stirring, beating, or agitating substances (especially in food preparation, like churning butter or making ice cream); also, a person or thing that dashes

Examples

The old ice cream maker had a wooden dasher inside to mix the ingredients.
She used a dasher to beat the cream until it thickened.
As a child, he was known as a real dasher on the playground, always running ahead of everyone.
