bandanna

noun
large, colorful handkerchief
Pronunciation: / bænˈdænə / (AmE), / bænˈdanə / (BrE)
A large, brightly colored handkerchief, often worn around the neck or head as a decorative accessory or for practical purposes.

Examples

A red bandanna
During the concert, he wore a blue bandanna as a stylish accessory.
She tied a bright red bandanna around her head before heading out into the sun.
The cowboy used his bandanna to wipe the sweat from his brow.

Related Terms
