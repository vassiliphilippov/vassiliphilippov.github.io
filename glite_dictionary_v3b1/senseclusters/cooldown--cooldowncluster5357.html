cooldown

noun
pause to calm down
3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkuːl.daʊn/ (AmE), /ˈkuːl.daʊn/ (BrE)
a short period of time taken to pause and calm down after intense activity or emotion.

Examples

Team cooldown break
After the heated debate, the team took a cooldown before continuing the meeting.
He suggested a quick cooldown so everyone could collect their thoughts.
Sometimes a short cooldown helps people avoid saying things they regret.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rest - a period of relaxing or stopping activity
