plump up

verb
make fuller or rounder by shaking
5.9
Pronunciation: / plʌmp ʌp / (AmE), / plʌmp ʌp / (BrE)
to make something fuller or rounder by shaking, fluffing, or patting it

Examples

To plump up a duvet
Every morning, I plump up my duvet to keep it nice and fluffy.
He paused to plump up the seat cushions to make them more comfortable.
She likes to plump up the pillows before guests arrive.
