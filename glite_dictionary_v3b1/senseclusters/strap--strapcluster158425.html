strap

noun
loop for holding onto in buses or trains
1.3 3.2
Pronunciation: / stræp / (AmE), / stræp / (BrE)
a loop of leather or similar material hanging from the ceiling of a bus or train for passengers to hold onto while standing

Examples

Bus strap

Many commuters rely on the strap for balance during rush hour.
The old train cars still had leather straps instead of metal bars.
