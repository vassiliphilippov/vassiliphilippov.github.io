unmanifested

noun
hidden reality
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnmænɪˈfɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnmænɪˈfɛstɪd / (BrE)
the aspect of reality or existence that is not expressed, visible, or perceivable in the physical world.

Examples

Many spiritual traditions speak of the unmanifested as the source of all creation.
Through deep meditation, some people claim to experience the unmanifested directly.
Philosophers have long debated whether the unmanifested can ever be truly known.
