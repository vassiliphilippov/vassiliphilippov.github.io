pellet

verb
to form into pellets
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛlɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɛlɪt / (BrE)
to form or compress a substance into small, rounded balls or pellets

Examples

To pellet plastic
The machine pellets recycled plastic to make it easier to transport.
Farmers often pellet animal feed to ensure even distribution of nutrients.
Some companies pellet wood waste for use as an eco-friendly fuel.
The factory uses a special machine to pellet sawdust for use as fuel.
Farmers often pellet animal feed to make it easier for livestock to eat.
Some recycling plants pellet plastic waste before processing it further.

  • synonym: granulate - to form into grains or small particles
