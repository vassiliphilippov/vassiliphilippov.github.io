bean trefoil

noun
shrub with trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers
8.6
Pronunciation: / bin ˈtrɔɪfɔɪl / (AmE), / biːn ˈtrɔɪfɔɪl / (BrE)
a shrub with trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers, producing backward curving seed pods; the leaves have a strong, unpleasant smell when crushed

Examples

The bean trefoil shrub
After flowering, the bean trefoil develops distinctive backward curving seed pods.
Crushing the leaves of bean trefoil releases a strong, unpleasant odor.
The small bean trefoil in the garden caught my attention with its bright yellow flowers.
