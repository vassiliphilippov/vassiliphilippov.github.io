narrational

adjective
about storytelling
Pronunciation: / nəˈreɪʃənəl / (AmE), / nəˈreɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the act, process, or style of telling a story or giving a narrative

Examples

A narrational style
The director used a unique narrational approach to present the story from multiple perspectives.
Her narrational style kept the audience engaged throughout the entire novel.
Critics praised the film's narrational techniques for making the complex plot easy to understand.

Related Terms

  • antonym: descriptive - giving details about how something looks, feels, or works
  • synonym: narrative - relating to the telling of a story
