render unto Caesar

verb
give the government its due
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛndər ˌʌnˌtu ˈsiːzər / (AmE), / ˈrɛndə ˌʌnˌtuː ˈsiːzə / (BrE)
to give what is due or owed to authorities or the government, especially in the context of taxes or civic obligations

Examples

To render unto Caesar taxes
During audit season, it's best to render unto Caesar and file your taxes honestly.
While she disagreed with the new law, she chose to render unto Caesar and comply.
You may protest in other ways, but you must still render unto Caesar when it comes to civic duties.
