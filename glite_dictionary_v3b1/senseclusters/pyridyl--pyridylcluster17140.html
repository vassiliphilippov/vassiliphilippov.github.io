pyridyl

adjective
from pyridine
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪrɪˌdɪl / (AmE), / ˈpɪrɪˌdɪl / (BrE)
relating to or containing a group derived from pyridine, especially as a substituent in chemical compounds

Examples

Pyridyl compound
The chemist identified a pyridyl group attached to the aromatic ring.
Adding a pyridyl substituent can change the properties of the compound.
Researchers studied the effects of pyridyl derivatives in pharmaceutical development.
