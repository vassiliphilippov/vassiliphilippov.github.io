vivandière

noun
female tavern worker
Pronunciation: / viˌvɑnˈdjɛr / (AmE), / viˌvɒnˈdjɛə / (BrE)
a woman who sells food and drink to the public, especially in a tavern or inn, often in a historical context

Examples

Tavern vivandière
The vivandière greeted travelers warmly as they entered the bustling tavern.
In historical novels, a vivandière often provides food and comfort to weary soldiers.
Locals remembered the old vivandière who served hearty meals to everyone in the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: innkeeper - a person who manages or owns an inn
  • synonym: waitress - a woman who serves food and drink in a restaurant or cafe
