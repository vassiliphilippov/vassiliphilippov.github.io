goombay

noun
Bahamian music or drum
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːmbaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡuːmbaɪ / (BrE)
a style of Bahamian music featuring rhythmic drumming, or the drum used to play this music

Examples

Goombay drum rhythms
The lively rhythms of goombay filled the festival air.
He learned to play the goombay drum from his grandfather in Nassau.
Tourists enjoyed dancing to traditional goombay music at the beach party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calypso - a style of Caribbean music with rhythmic and harmonic vocals
  • synonym: junkanoo - a Bahamian street parade with music, dance, and costumes
