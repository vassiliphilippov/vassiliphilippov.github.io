glop

noun
overly sentimental writing or music
Pronunciation: / ɡlɑp / (AmE), / ɡlɒp / (BrE)
writing, music, or other artistic work that is excessively sweet, sentimental, or lacking in seriousness or depth

Examples

Sentimental glop
Her latest novel is popular, but critics have dismissed it as sentimental glop.
I can't listen to that glop they call love songs on the radio these days.
The movie was nothing but predictable glop designed to make audiences cry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kitsch - art or writing considered to be in poor taste due to excessive sentimentality
  • synonym: schmaltz - excessively sentimental music, art, or writing
