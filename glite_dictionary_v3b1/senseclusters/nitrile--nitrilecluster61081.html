nitrile

noun
organic compound with a cyano group
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtraɪl / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtraɪl / (BrE)
a class of organic compounds containing the cyano group (–CN)

Examples

Nitrile group
Many pharmaceutical drugs include a nitrile group to enhance their biological activity.
Polyacrylonitrile is a common type of synthetic nitrile used in manufacturing fibers.
The chemist synthesized a new nitrile during the experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cyanide - an anion containing a carbon triple-bonded to a nitrogen atom
