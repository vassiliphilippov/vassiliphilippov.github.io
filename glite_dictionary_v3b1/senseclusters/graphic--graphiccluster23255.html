graphic

adjective
vividly explicit, often about sex or violence
1.6 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgræ.fɪk/ (AmE), /ˈgræ.fɪk/ (BrE)
describing something in very clear, vivid, or explicit detail, especially about sex, violence, or lifelike scenes

Examples

Graphic details
Her memoir provided a graphic account of her experiences during the war.
The documentary was criticized for its graphic depiction of real-life medical procedures.
The movie contains several graphic scenes of violence that may disturb viewers.
Her graphic description of the scene made it easy to picture every detail.
The novel contains several graphic portrayals of battle that are not suitable for children.
The reporter gave a graphic account of the disaster, leaving the audience horrified.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - lacking vividness or interest
  • synonym: explicit - clearly and openly expressed, leaving no doubt about meaning, especially relating to sex or violence
  • synonym: lifelike - very similar to the person or thing represented
  • synonym: vivid - producing powerful feelings or strong, clear images in the mind
