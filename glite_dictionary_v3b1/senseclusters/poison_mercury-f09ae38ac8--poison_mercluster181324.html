poison mercury

noun
plant causing rash on contact
3.7 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪzən ˈmɜrkjəri / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzən ˈmɜːkjəri / (BrE)
a climbing plant found in eastern and central North America with three-part leaves, greenish flowers, and clusters of white berries; produces an oil that causes a skin rash on contact
