seedcase

noun
the vessel containing a plant's seeds
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsidˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈsiːdkeɪs / (BrE)
the part of a plant that encloses and protects the seeds; a vessel containing seeds

Examples

A seedcase protects seeds
After flowering, the plant develops a protective seedcase around its seeds.
Each seedcase contains dozens of tiny black seeds ready to grow.
The seedcase split open, scattering seeds across the garden bed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capsule - a dry fruit that releases seeds when ripe
  • synonym: pod - the outer shell or vessel of seeds in some plants
