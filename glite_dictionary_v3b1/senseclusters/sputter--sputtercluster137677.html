sputter

verb
speak with spitting sounds, esp. in anger
Pronunciation: / ˈspʌtər / (AmE), / ˈspʌtə / (BrE)
to speak or utter words with spitting or explosive sounds, often out of anger or excitement

Examples

To sputter with anger
"But—but that's not fair!" he sputtered angrily.
He began to sputter with outrage when he heard the accusation.
She sputtered an excuse, unable to hide her embarrassment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mutter - to speak in a low or barely audible voice, often in dissatisfaction
  • synonym: stammer - to speak with involuntary pauses or repetitions
