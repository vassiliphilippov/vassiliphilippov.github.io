rectal vein

noun
veins draining the rectum and anal canal
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛktəl veɪn / (AmE), / ˈrɛktəl veɪn / (BrE)
any of several veins that drain blood from the walls of the rectum and anal canal

Examples

The rectal vein drains
Blood from the lower part of the digestive tract is carried away by the rectal vein.
Hemorrhoids occur when a rectal vein becomes swollen and inflamed.
The surgeon carefully ligated the rectal vein to prevent excessive bleeding during the operation.
