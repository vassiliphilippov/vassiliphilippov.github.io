salaryman

noun
Japanese office worker
Pronunciation: / ˈsæləriˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈsæləriˌman /; / ˈsæləriˌmæn / (BrE)
a (usually male) office worker or company employee in Japan who works long hours and is loyal to the company
