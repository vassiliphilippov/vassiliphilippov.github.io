swindle

verb
cheat by deceit or fraud
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪn.dl̩ / (AmE), / ˈswɪn.dl̩ / (BrE)
to cheat someone out of money or property by deceit or fraud

Examples

To swindle money
He tried to swindle investors by selling shares in a fake company.
She warned her friends not to swindle others, no matter how tempting the opportunity.
The couple was arrested for attempting to swindle elderly homeowners out of their savings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defraud - to illegally obtain money from someone by deception
