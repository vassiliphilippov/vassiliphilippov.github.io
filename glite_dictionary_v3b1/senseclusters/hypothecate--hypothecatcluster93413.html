hypothecate

verb
pledge property without transferring possession
9.9
Pronunciation: / haɪˈpɑːθəˌkeɪt / (AmE), / hɪˈpɒθɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to pledge property as security for a loan without giving up possession or title to it

Examples

To hypothecate property
Banks sometimes hypothecate securities on behalf of their clients to secure additional funding.
Under the agreement, the borrower may hypothecate the car as collateral while continuing to use it.
You should never hypothecate your house unless you fully understand the terms of the loan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pledge - to formally promise or give something as security for a debt
