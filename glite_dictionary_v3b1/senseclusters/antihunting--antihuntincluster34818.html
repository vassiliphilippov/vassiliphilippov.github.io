antihunting

noun
opposition to hunting
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈhʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈhʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
opposition to hunting, or a person, group, or movement that campaigns against hunting or seeks its prohibition

Examples

Antihunting movement
The antihunting movement gained momentum after several wildlife documentaries aired on television.
Many antihunting activists attended the rally to demand stricter laws against animal cruelty.
Local authorities responded to the antihunting group's petition by reviewing hunting regulations in the area.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hunting - the activity of pursuing and killing wild animals
  • synonym: protest - an organized public demonstration expressing strong objection
