hybridist

noun
creator of hybrids
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪbrɪdɪst / (AmE), / ˈhaɪbrɪdɪst / (BrE)
a person who creates hybrids, especially by cross-breeding plants or animals

Examples

Plant hybridist
The hybridist spent years developing a new strain of disease-resistant wheat.
As a skilled hybridist, she introduced several unique flower varieties to the market.
Many gardeners admire the work of a talented hybridist who can combine the best traits of different plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breeder - a person who breeds plants or animals
  • synonym: geneticist - a scientist who studies genetics
