couple

noun
two things considered together
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌpəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌpəl / (BrE)
Two things of the same kind, considered together.

Examples

A couple of apples
Could you hand me a couple of pencils, please?
He needs a couple of minutes to finish his work.
I bought a couple of apples at the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pair - two corresponding or matching things; a set of two
