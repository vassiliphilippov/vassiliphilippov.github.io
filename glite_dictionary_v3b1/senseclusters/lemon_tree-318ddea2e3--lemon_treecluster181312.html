lemon tree

noun
tree producing lemons
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛmən tri / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən triː / (BrE)
A small evergreen tree of the citrus family, native to Asia and widely grown for its yellow, acidic fruit.

Examples

A lemon tree blossom
Every spring, the lemon tree is covered in fragrant white blossoms.
She planted a lemon tree in a large pot on her patio.
The lemon tree in our backyard produces dozens of fresh lemons every year.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: citrus tree - any tree of the citrus genus
  • synonym: lemon - the yellow, acidic fruit of the lemon tree
