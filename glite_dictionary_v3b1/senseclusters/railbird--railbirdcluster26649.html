railbird

noun
racing fan near the outer rail
8.9 8.5
Pronunciation: /reɪl bɜːrd/ (AmE), /reɪl bɜːd/ (BrE)
a person who watches horse races or gambling games (especially poker) from the sidelines, often without participating and sometimes offering advice
