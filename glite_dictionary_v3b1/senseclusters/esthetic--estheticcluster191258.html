esthetic

adjective
relating to beauty or aesthetics
1.2 4.8
Pronunciation: /es.ˈθet.ɪk/ /; / /əs.ˈθet.ɪk/ (AmE), /es.ˈθet.ɪk/ /; / /əs.ˈθet.ɪk/ (BrE)
relating to beauty or the appreciation of beauty; pleasing in appearance or showing good taste
