bulimia nervosa

noun
an eating disorder involving binging and purging
8.4
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɪmiə nɚˈvoʊsə / (AmE), / bjʊˈlɪmiə nəˈvɜːsə / (BrE)
A serious eating disorder characterized by episodes of excessive eating followed by fasting, purging, or self-induced vomiting to prevent weight gain.

Examples

Bulimia nervosa disorder
Many young women with bulimia nervosa struggle in silence due to stigma and shame.
Repeated cycles of bingeing and purging are typical symptoms of bulimia nervosa.
The psychologist specialized in treating patients with bulimia nervosa and other eating disorders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anorexia nervosa - an eating disorder characterized by lack or loss of appetite and extreme weight loss
