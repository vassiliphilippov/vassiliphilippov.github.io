bulimia nervosa

noun
an eating disorder involving binging and purging
5.8 0.22/m
Pronunciation: / b…ôÀàl…™mi…ô n…öÀàvo äs…ô / (AmE), / bj äÀàl…™mi…ô n…ôÀàv…úÀês…ô / (BrE)
A serious eating disorder characterized by episodes of excessive eating followed by fasting, purging, or self-induced vomiting to prevent weight gain.

Examples

Bulimia nervosa disorder
Many young women with bulimia nervosa struggle in silence due to stigma and shame.
Repeated cycles of bingeing and purging are typical symptoms of bulimia nervosa.
The psychologist specialized in treating patients with bulimia nervosa and other eating disorders.

Synonyms

  • bulimia
    6.0 1.36/m
    bulimia is the common short name people and doctors use for the condition, while bulimia nervosa is the full medical diagnosis.
  • binge-purge syndrome
    7.4 0.00/m
    binge-purge syndrome is a clinical phrase that describes cycles of bingeing and purging, while bulimia nervosa refers to the diagnosed disorder.
  • binge-vomit syndrome
    9.9 0.00/m
    binge-vomit syndrome names cycles of overeating followed by self-induced vomiting, while bulimia nervosa is the medical name for that disorder.
  • bulimarexia
    9.9 0.00/m
    bulimarexia shows alternating bingeing and food avoidance like anorexia, while bulimia nervosa focuses on bingeing then purging to prevent weight gain.
