cubitus

noun
elbow joint (anatomical term)
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːbɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbɪtəs / (BrE)
the elbow joint, especially as referred to in anatomical or zoological contexts; the hinge joint between the forearm and upper arm, or the corresponding joint in a quadruped's forelimb

Examples

Cubitus joint movement
In some mammals, the cubitus is adapted for digging or running.
Swelling around the cubitus may indicate inflammation or trauma.
The doctor examined the patient's cubitus for signs of injury.

Related Terms
