lively tune

noun
cheerful or spirited piece of music
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪvli tuːn / (AmE), / ˈlaɪvli tjuːn / (BrE)
a cheerful and spirited piece of music, typically with an upbeat tempo

Examples

A lively tune plays
Our spirits lifted instantly when a lively tune started playing in the café.
She composed a lively tune to celebrate the arrival of spring.
The musicians played a lively tune that had everyone dancing around the room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dirge - a mournful musical composition, often for funerals
  • synonym: melody - a sequence of musical notes that are perceived as a single entity
