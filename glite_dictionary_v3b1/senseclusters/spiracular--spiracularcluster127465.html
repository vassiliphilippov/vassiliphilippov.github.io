spiracular

adjective
of a spiracle
Pronunciation: / spaɪˈrækjələr / (AmE), / spaɪˈrækjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to a spiracle, an external respiratory opening in some animals such as insects or fish

Examples

Spiracular opening
The scientist examined the spiracular structures on the side of the insect's body.
Fish larvae often have visible spiracular openings used for breathing.
Damage to the spiracular system can affect an insect's ability to survive underwater.
