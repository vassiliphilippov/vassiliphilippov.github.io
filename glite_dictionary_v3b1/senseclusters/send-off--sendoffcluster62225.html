send-off

noun
farewell event or celebration
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnd ˌɔf /; / ˈsɛndˌɑf / (AmE), / ˈsɛnd ɒf /; / ˈsɛndˌɒf / (BrE)
an event or ceremony held to wish someone well or to mark the beginning of a journey, competition, or new venture

Examples

A send-off party
Her friends organized a surprise send-off at the airport before she left for college.
The company gave him a warm send-off on his last day at work.
We threw a small send-off party before they moved overseas.
Everyone cheered the runners at the official send-off ceremony.
Friends and family organized a send-off party when she joined the Peace Corps.
The team gathered for a special send-off before the championship game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farewell - an instance of parting or saying goodbye
  • synonym: kickoff - the start of an event, especially in sports
  • antonym: welcome - a greeting or reception when someone arrives
