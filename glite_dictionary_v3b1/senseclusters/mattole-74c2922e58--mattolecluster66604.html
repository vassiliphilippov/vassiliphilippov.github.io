Mattole

noun
Indigenous people of northwestern California
Pronunciation: / məˈtoʊl / (AmE), / məˈtəʊl / (BrE)
A member of an Athapaskan-speaking Indigenous people living in northwestern California.

Examples

The Mattole people
Researchers have worked to preserve the language and culture of the Mattole.
Some descendants of the Mattole still live in their ancestral homelands today.
The Mattole have a rich tradition of storytelling and basket weaving.
