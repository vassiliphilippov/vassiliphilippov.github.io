golden wattle

noun
Australian tree with golden yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈgoʊldən ˈwɑtəl / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈwɒtəl / (BrE)
a shrubby Australian tree (Acacia pycnantha) with clusters of fragrant golden yellow flowers, widely cultivated as an ornamental plant

Examples

Bright golden wattle flowers
Clusters of golden wattle bloom brilliantly in early spring.
Many gardens feature golden wattle for its striking color and fragrant flowers.
The golden wattle is Australia's national floral emblem.
