noter

noun
person who takes notes
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊtər / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtə / (BrE)
a person who takes notes or makes written comments, especially during meetings or lectures

Examples

Meeting noter
The diligent noter recorded every detail of the meeting.
During the lecture, the noter quickly wrote down all the important points.
As the official noter, she was responsible for keeping accurate records of the discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recorder - a person who writes down information or events
  • synonym: scribe - a person who copies or writes documents by hand
