patella

noun
the kneecap bone
Pronunciation: / pəˈtɛl.ə / (AmE), / pəˈtel.ə / (BrE)
the small, flat, triangular bone at the front of the knee joint, commonly known as the kneecap

Examples

The patella bone
An injury to the patella can significantly affect a person's ability to walk.
During the examination, she noticed swelling just above her patella.
The doctor explained that the patella plays an important role in knee movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kneecap - the round, flat bone forming the front of the knee joint
