drawknife

noun
woodworking tool for shaving wood
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔˌnaɪf / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːnaɪf / (BrE)
A hand tool used by woodworkers, consisting of a blade with handles at both ends, used to shave off thin slices of wood.

Examples

A drawknife tool
Before sandpaper, craftsmen would smooth wood with a drawknife.
Sharpening the drawknife made it easier to peel bark from the log.
The carpenter used a drawknife to shape the legs of the chair.
