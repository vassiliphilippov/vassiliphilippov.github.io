hydrol

noun
aromatic floral water
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdrɒl / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrɒl / (BrE)
a fragrant water produced by steam distillation of plants, used in cosmetics and aromatherapy

Examples

Rose hydrol spray
She sprayed the rose hydrol on her face to refresh and tone her skin after cleansing.
Lavender hydrol is often used as a gentle facial mist in natural skincare routines.
Many aromatherapists recommend using a hydrol as a soothing room spray or linen freshener.

Related Terms

  • antonym: essential oil - a concentrated oil extracted from plants, often used in aromatherapy
  • synonym: hydrosol - a water-based solution containing plant extracts from distillation
