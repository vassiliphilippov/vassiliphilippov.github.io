frizziness

noun
quality of being frizzy
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪzinəs / (AmE), / ˈfrɪzinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being frizzy, especially when hair is dry, curly, and not smooth or straight

Examples

Hair frizziness
The frizziness of her hair made it difficult to style on humid days.
Many people use special shampoos to reduce frizziness and keep their hair smooth.
After swimming in the ocean, he noticed an increase in the frizziness of his hair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curliness - the quality of having curls or being curly
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of being even and free from roughness
