Judaization

noun
conversion to Judaism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒuːdəaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdʒuːdəaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of converting people to Judaism or causing someone to adopt Jewish beliefs or practices

Examples

Judaization of people
The historian studied the Judaization of certain communities in the ancient world.
Some leaders resisted the Judaization of local customs after the migration.
Debates about Judaization shaped religious policies in the early centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Christianization - the process of converting people to Christianity
  • synonym: conversion - the act of changing one's religion or beliefs
