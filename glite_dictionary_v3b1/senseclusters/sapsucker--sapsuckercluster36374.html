sapsucker

noun
American woodpecker that feeds on sap
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsæpˌsʌkər / (AmE), / ˈsæpˌsʌkə / (BrE)
A small American woodpecker that feeds on sap from trees such as apple and maple.

Examples

A sapsucker bird
A sapsucker drilled neat rows of holes on the maple tree outside our window.
Ornithologists observed a red-breasted sapsucker feeding on apple tree sap during the spring.
The presence of sapsuckers often indicates a healthy, diverse woodland ecosystem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: woodpecker - bird that pecks at wood to find insects or make nests
