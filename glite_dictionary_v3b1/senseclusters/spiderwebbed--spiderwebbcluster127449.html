spiderwebbed

adjective
marked with web-like lines
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪdərˌwɛbd / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdəˌwɛbd / (BrE)
covered with or marked by thin, web-like lines or patterns resembling a spider's web

Examples

Spiderwebbed glass
The windshield was spiderwebbed after the hailstorm hit the car.
She noticed the old mirror was spiderwebbed with fine cracks across its surface.
The ice on the pond became spiderwebbed as the temperature dropped overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cracked - having lines or breaks on the surface
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and unmarked surface
