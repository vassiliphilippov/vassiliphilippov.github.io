brawl

noun
a noisy public fight
1.9
Pronunciation: / brɔl / (AmE), / brɔːl / (BrE)
a noisy and disorderly fight, especially involving a group of people in a public place

Examples

A street brawl
A massive brawl broke out after the soccer match, forcing police to intervene.
He was injured in a brawl outside the stadium last weekend.
The bar was known for the occasional brawl on Friday nights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fight - a violent confrontation between individuals or groups
  • synonym: melee - a confused struggle or skirmish involving many people
  • antonym: peace - absence of violence or conflict
