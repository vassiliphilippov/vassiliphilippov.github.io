chidingly

adverb
scolding manner
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪdɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪdɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that expresses mild disapproval or scolding

Examples

Looked chidingly at
The teacher looked chidingly at the students who were talking during the lesson.
He shook his head chidingly when his friend forgot her umbrella again.
She smiled chidingly as she reminded him to finish his homework on time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: approvingly - in a way that shows approval or praise
  • synonym: reprovingly - in a way that shows disapproval or criticism
  • synonym: scoldingly - in a way that scolds or rebukes
