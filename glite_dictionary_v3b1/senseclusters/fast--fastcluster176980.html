fast

adjective, adverb
securely fixed in place
7.6
Pronunciation: / fæst / (AmE), / fɑːst / (BrE)
firmly fixed in place or held securely; not able to move

Examples

Fast to the wall
After the repairs, the shelves remained fast to the wall.
Make sure the ladder is fast before you climb up.
The bolts were tightened so the door was held fast against the wind.
She gripped the handlebars fast as the bike went over the bump.
The lid was screwed on fast, making it difficult to open.
The poster was stuck fast to the wall and wouldn't come off easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firm - solidly fixed or stable
  • antonym: loose - not firmly fixed in place
  • antonym: loosely - in a way that is not firmly or tightly fixed or held
  • synonym: securely - in a way that is fixed or fastened firmly
