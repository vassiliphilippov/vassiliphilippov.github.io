jag

noun
spree of drinking or drug use
9.8
Pronunciation: / dʒæɡ / (AmE), / dʒaɡ / (BrE)
A period or spree of excessive drinking or drug use.

Examples

A drinking jag
After a three-day jag, he needed time to recover.
His drinking jag worried his friends and family.
She went on a drug jag that lasted all weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: binge - an episode of excessive indulgence (especially in food or drink)
  • synonym: spree - a sustained period of excessive or indulgent activity
