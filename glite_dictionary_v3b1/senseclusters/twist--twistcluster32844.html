twist

noun
a muscle or ligament strain
1.0
Pronunciation: / twɪst / (AmE), / twɪst / (BrE)
a sudden strain or injury to a muscle or ligament, often caused by stretching or turning it awkwardly

Examples

A knee twist
A quick twist of the knee can result in days of swelling and discomfort.
She suffered a painful muscle twist after slipping on the stairs.
The soccer player's ankle twist forced him to leave the match early.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sprain - an injury to a ligament caused by being stretched too far
