cricoarytenoid

noun
laryngeal muscle
Pronunciation: / ˌkraɪkoʊˌærɪˈtɛnɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌkraɪkəʊˌærɪˈtiːnɔɪd / (BrE)
a muscle or structure connecting the cricoid and arytenoid cartilages in the larynx, involved in moving the vocal cords
