sheepfold

noun
enclosure for sheep
Pronunciation: / ˈʃipˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈʃiːp.fəʊld / (BrE)
an enclosure or pen for confining sheep

Examples

A sheepfold enclosure
A low stone wall surrounded the old sheepfold in the meadow.
He checked the sheepfold every morning to count the animals.
The flock was safely driven into the sheepfold before nightfall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pen - an enclosure for confining livestock, especially smaller animals
