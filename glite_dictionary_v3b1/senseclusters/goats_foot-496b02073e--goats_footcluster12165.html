goat's foot

noun
a South African plant with bluish flowers
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊts fʊt / (AmE), / ɡəʊts fʊt / (BrE)
A short-stemmed South African plant with bluish flowers, named for the shape of its leaves or flowers resembling a goat's foot.

Examples

The goat's foot plant
Local healers often use extracts from the goat's foot plant for traditional remedies.
The bluish flowers of the goat's foot make it a favorite among gardeners in South Africa.
The wild goat's foot blooms along the rocky hillsides in early spring.
