goat's foot

noun
a South African plant with bluish flowers
8.8 8.4
Pronunciation: /goʊts fʊt/ (AmE), /gəʊts fʊt/ (BrE)
A short-stemmed South African plant with bluish flowers, named for the shape of its leaves or flowers resembling a goat's foot.

The goat's foot plant
