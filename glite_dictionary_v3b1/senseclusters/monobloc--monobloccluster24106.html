monobloc

adjective
single-piece construction
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑː.nə.blɑːk/ (AmE), /ˈmɒn.ə.blɒk/ (BrE)
made or formed as a single piece or unit, especially from metal or plastic, rather than assembled from parts

Examples

Monobloc chair
The new chair is a monobloc design, molded from a single piece of plastic.
Engineers chose a monobloc cylinder head to improve durability and reduce leaks.
Many outdoor furniture sets now use monobloc construction for strength and simplicity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: one-piece - made from a single piece or unit
