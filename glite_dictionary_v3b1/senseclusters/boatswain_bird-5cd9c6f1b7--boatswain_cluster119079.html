boatswain bird

noun
tropical seabird (tropicbird)
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊtsən bɜrd / (AmE), / ˈbəʊtsən bɜːd / (BrE)
a mostly white web-footed tropical seabird, often found far from land; commonly refers to a tropicbird

Examples

Researchers spotted a flock of boatswain birds nesting on the remote island.

The graceful boatswain bird soared effortlessly above the ship's mast.
