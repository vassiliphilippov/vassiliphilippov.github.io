coordination compound

noun
chemical substance with a central atom bonded to ligands
4.3 5.6
Pronunciation: /koʊr.də.ˈneɪ.ʃən ˈkɑːm.paʊnd/ /; / /koʊ.ɔːr.də.ˈneɪ.ʃən ˈkɑːm.paʊnd/ (AmE), /kəʊ.ɔː.dɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən ˈkɒm.paʊnd/ /; / /kəʊ.ɔː.də.ˈneɪ.ʃən ˈkɒm.paʊnd/ (BrE)
