new

adjective
harvested before full maturity (crops)
5.1 36/m
Pronunciation: / nu / (AmE), / njuÀê / (BrE)
(of crops) harvested before reaching full maturity; picked at an early stage of development.

Examples

The chef prefers to use new carrots for their tender texture.
