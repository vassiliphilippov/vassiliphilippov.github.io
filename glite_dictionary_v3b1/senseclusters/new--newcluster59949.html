new

adjective
harvested before full maturity (crops)
Pronunciation: / nu / (AmE), / njuː / (BrE)
(of crops) harvested before reaching full maturity; picked at an early stage of development.

Examples

New potatoes
Farmers market stalls were filled with baskets of new peas.
The chef prefers to use new carrots for their tender texture.
We enjoyed a plate of new potatoes with dinner last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: young - (of food/plants) not fully mature, recently sprouted or picked early
