unredressed

adjective
not treated (wound)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈdrɛst / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈdrɛst / (BrE)
not treated, bandaged, or attended to, especially referring to a wound or injury

Examples

Unredressed wound
The unredressed wound became infected because she had delayed treatment.
If a cut is left unredressed, it may take longer to heal and could become serious.
Medical staff warned that unredressed injuries can lead to complications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: untreated - not given medical care or attention
