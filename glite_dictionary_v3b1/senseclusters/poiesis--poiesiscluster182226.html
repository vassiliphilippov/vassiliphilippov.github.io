poiesis

noun

the process of formation or production, especially used as a suffix in biological and medical terms.

Examples

Cell poiesis
The term poiesis is often used in biology to describe the creation of new cells.
In medical textbooks, poiesis appears in words like hematopoiesis and erythropoiesis.
Understanding poiesis helps students grasp how different tissues are formed in the body.
