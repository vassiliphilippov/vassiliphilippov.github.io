pedunculated polyp

noun
polyp with a stalk
6.7 7.0
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈdʌŋ.kjə.leɪ.tɪd ˈpɑː.lɪp/ /; / /pɪ.ˈdʌŋ.kjʊ.leɪ.tɪd ˈpɑː.lɪp/ (AmE), /pə.ˈdʌŋ.kjʊ.leɪ.tɪd ˈpɒl.ɪp/ /; / /pɪ.ˈdʌŋ.kjʊ.leɪ.tɪd ˈpɒl.ɪp/ (BrE)
