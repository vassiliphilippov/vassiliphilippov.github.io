preclassic

adjective
before classical era
Pronunciation: / priːˈklæsɪk / (AmE), / priːˈklæsɪk / (BrE)
relating to the period before the classical era in a particular culture or civilization

Examples

Preclassic period
Archaeologists discovered preclassic pottery at the ancient settlement site.
The museum's exhibit focuses on preclassic art from early Mesoamerican cultures.
Many preclassic structures were built before the rise of the great city-states.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: classical - relating to the most developed or highest stage of a culture
  • degree higher: postclassical - relating to the period after the classical era
