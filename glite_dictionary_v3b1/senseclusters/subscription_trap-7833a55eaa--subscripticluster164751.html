subscription trap

noun
deceptive ongoing payment scheme
4.3 7.6
Pronunciation: / səbˈskrɪp.ʃən træp / (AmE), / səbˈskrɪp.ʃən træp / (BrE)
a commercial practice or situation in which a consumer is unknowingly locked into recurring payments, often due to misleading terms or unclear cancellation procedures

Examples

A subscription trap online
Many people inadvertently sign up for a subscription trap when they accept a free trial online.
New regulations aim to protect consumers from falling prey to a subscription trap.
She discovered she was caught in a subscription trap after months of unnoticed charges.
