crackhead

noun
wildly obsessive person
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkræk.hed/ /; / /ˈkræk.əd/ (AmE), /ˈkræk.hed/ /; / /ˈkræk.əd/ (BrE)
an informal and offensive term for a person who behaves in a wild, irrational, or obsessive way, often used figuratively for someone extremely enthusiastic about something

Examples

Video game crackhead
He became such a crackhead about video games that he played for hours every night.
Some people call her a fashion crackhead because she follows every new trend obsessively.
The group of friends joked that only a true crackhead would camp out for concert tickets.

  • antonym: rationalist - a person who bases opinions and actions on reason and knowledge
