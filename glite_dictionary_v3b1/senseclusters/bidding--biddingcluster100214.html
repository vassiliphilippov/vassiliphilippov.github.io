bidding

noun
placing bids at an auction
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of making offers (bids) to buy an item or contract, especially at an auction or in online real-time bidding systems.

Examples

Auction bidding
At the charity auction, she won the painting after furious bidding from several collectors.
Online bidding by advertisers pushed the price for that keyword to an all-time high.
After hours of patient bidding, the antique vase finally sold well above estimate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bid - an offer of a price for something, especially at an auction
