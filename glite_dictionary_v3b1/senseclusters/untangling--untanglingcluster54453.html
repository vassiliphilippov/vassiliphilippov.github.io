untangling

noun
the act of removing tangles
2.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtæŋɡlɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈtaŋɡlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of freeing something from a tangled or complicated state

Examples

The untangling process
She focused on the untangling of her thoughts before making a decision.
The untangling of the fishing nets took several hours.
The untangling of wires behind the desk was more difficult than he expected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: untangle - to make something free from tangles
