fribble

noun
silly or frivolous person
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈfrɪbəl / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a person who is silly, frivolous, or lacking seriousness

Examples

A silly fribble
In the play, the wealthy fribble spends his days gossiping and avoiding any real work.
She dismissed her brother as a fribble because he never took anything seriously.
The old novel describes the town's mayor as a harmless fribble who loved parties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or lacks good sense
  • antonym: serious person - someone who is thoughtful and not frivolous
  • synonym: simpleton - a person lacking in common sense
