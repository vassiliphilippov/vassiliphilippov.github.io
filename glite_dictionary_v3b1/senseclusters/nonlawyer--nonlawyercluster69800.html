nonlawyer

adjective
not a lawyer
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈlɔjər / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈlɔːjə / (BrE)
not trained, qualified, or licensed as a lawyer; used to describe someone who is not a lawyer

Examples

Nonlawyer staff member
The firm hired a nonlawyer consultant to help with administrative tasks.
Only a nonlawyer staff member can answer general questions about scheduling.
As a nonlawyer advisor, she cannot provide legal advice to clients.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lawyer - a person who is qualified to practice law
  • synonym: layperson - a person without professional or specialized knowledge in a particular subject
