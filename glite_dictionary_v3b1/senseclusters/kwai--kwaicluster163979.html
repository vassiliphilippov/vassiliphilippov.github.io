kwai

noun
Chinese unit of currency (yuan), informal
Pronunciation: / kwaɪ / (AmE), / kwaɪ / (BrE)
a colloquial term for the basic unit of money in China (yuan)

Examples

Twenty kwai
A meal in Beijing can cost as little as twenty kwai.
He exchanged a hundred dollars and got about six hundred kwai in return.
She only had fifty kwai left in her wallet after shopping.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yuan - the base unit of currency in China
