phylum Rotifera

noun
phylum of microscopic aquatic animals (rotifers)
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪləm roʊˈtɪfərə / (AmE), / ˈfaɪləm rəʊˈtɪfərə / (BrE)
A phylum of microscopic aquatic animals known as rotifers, characterized by a wheel-like ciliated structure at their front end.

Members of the phylum Rotifera are important for studying aquatic ecosystems.

The phylum Rotifera includes more than 2,000 described species of rotifers.
