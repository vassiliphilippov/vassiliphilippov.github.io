wild goose never laid a tame egg

x
proverb about unrealistic expectations
9.6
Pronunciation: / waɪld ɡus ˈnɛvɚ leɪd ə teɪm ɛɡ / (AmE), / waɪld ɡuːs ˈnɛvə leɪd ə teɪm eɡ / (BrE)
used to express the idea that it is unreasonable to expect a wild or unruly person or thing to produce calm or orderly results; unrealistic expectations lead to disappointment.

Examples

Wild goose never laid a tame egg
Trying to find order in chaos is futile; after all, wild goose never laid a tame egg.
When her risky scheme failed, he shrugged and said, 'wild goose never laid a tame egg.'
You can't expect discipline from someone who never learned it—a wild goose never laid a tame egg.
