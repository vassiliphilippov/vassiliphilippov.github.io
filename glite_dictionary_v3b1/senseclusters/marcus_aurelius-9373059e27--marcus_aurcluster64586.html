Marcus Aurelius

proper noun
Roman emperor and Stoic philosopher
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkəs ɔːˈriːliəs /; / ˈmɑrkəs ɔːˈriliəs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkəs ɔːˈriːliəs / (BrE)
Roman emperor (121–180 AD) and Stoic philosopher, known for his leadership and philosophical writings during the decline of the Roman Empire
