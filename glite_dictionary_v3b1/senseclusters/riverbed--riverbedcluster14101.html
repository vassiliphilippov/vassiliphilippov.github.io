riverbed

noun
bottom of a river channel
4.7 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪv.ər.bed/ (AmE), /ˈrɪv.ə.bed/ (BrE)
The ground at the bottom of a river, typically the channel where the river flows or once flowed.

Examples

The riverbed bottom
Fishermen searched the riverbed for hidden rocks before boating downstream.
Floodwaters carved a new path through the old riverbed.
The drought left the riverbed dry and cracked for miles.
