geochemist

noun
Earth chemistry scientist
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːoʊˈkɛmɪst / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəʊˈkɛmɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies the chemical composition and processes of the Earth
