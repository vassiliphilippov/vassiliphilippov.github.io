Gothic

adjective
relating to the Goths (ancient people)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑθɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡɒθɪk / (BrE)
of or relating to the Goths, an ancient Germanic people who played a major role in the history of the Roman Empire.

Examples

Gothic tribes
Historians are fascinated by the Gothic invasions that transformed Europe.
Some of the oldest texts in the Gothic language have been preserved.
The museum has a rare collection of Gothic jewelry from the 4th century.
