lobola

verb
to pay bride price
Pronunciation: / loʊˈboʊlə / (AmE), / ləʊˈbəʊlə / (BrE)
to pay a bride price to a bride's family as part of traditional marriage arrangements, especially in some African cultures

Examples

To lobola a bride
He decided to lobola his fiancée as part of their traditional wedding ceremony.
Many families expect the groom to lobola before the marriage can take place.
After he lobolaed her, the couple celebrated with both families.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dowry - property or money brought by a bride to her husband on marriage
