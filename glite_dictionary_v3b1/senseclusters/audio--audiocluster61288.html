audio

noun
sound in the audible range
1.0 1.6
Pronunciation: /ˈɑː.di.oʊ/ /; / /ˈɔː.di.oʊ/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.di.əʊ/ (BrE)
the sound that is recorded, transmitted, or played back, especially as part of media or broadcasts, as opposed to the visual part
