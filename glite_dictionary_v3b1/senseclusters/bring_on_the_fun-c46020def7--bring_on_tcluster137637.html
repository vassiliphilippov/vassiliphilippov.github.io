bring on the fun

verb
initiate enjoyment or entertainment
1.0
Pronunciation: / brɪŋ ɑn ðə fʌn / (AmE), / brɪŋ ɒn ðə fʌn / (BrE)
to start or create an atmosphere of enjoyment or entertainment; to encourage people to have fun

Examples

To bring on the fun with music
After finishing their work early, the team decided to bring on the fun with music and games.
As soon as the barbecue is ready, it's time to bring on the fun and enjoy the evening.
Whenever we travel together, someone always says, "bring on the fun!" as we start the trip.
