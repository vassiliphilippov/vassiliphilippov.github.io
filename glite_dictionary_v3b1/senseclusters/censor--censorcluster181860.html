censor

verb
remove or suppress content deemed objectionable
1.0 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsen.sər/ (AmE), /ˈsen.sə/ (BrE)
to examine and remove or suppress parts of books, films, letters, or other media considered objectionable or offensive
