orderly

noun
soldier serving as a personal attendant to an officer
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdərli / (AmE), / ˈɔːdəli / (BrE)
a soldier assigned to serve as a personal attendant to a superior officer, performing tasks such as carrying messages and handling personal chores

Examples

Army orderly
After the battle, the orderly helped the colonel clean and repair his uniform.
An experienced orderly can anticipate his officer's needs without being told.
The general's orderly woke him at dawn and brought his coffee.
