fiendishness

noun
extreme cruelty
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːndɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈfiːndɪʃnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely cruel, wicked, or devilish

Examples

The fiendishness of a villain

She was shocked by the fiendishness displayed in the villain's elaborate plan.
Historians still discuss the fiendishness of certain medieval punishments.
