ripply

adjective
wavy surface
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪpli / (AmE), / ˈrɪpli / (BrE)
having small waves or a wavy, undulating appearance on the surface (such as water, fabric, or muscles)

Examples

A ripply surface
The pond became ripply when the wind picked up in the afternoon.
She admired the ripply pattern on the sand left by the receding tide.
The fabric had a ripply texture that made the dress look unique.
After months of training, his arms looked more ripply than ever before.
The lake's surface became ripply as the wind picked up in the afternoon.
She wore a dress that showed her ripply abs at the fitness competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smooth - having an even and flat surface without waves
  • synonym: wavy - having a series of curves or undulations
