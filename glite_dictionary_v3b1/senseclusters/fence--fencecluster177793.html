fence

noun
structure that encloses or divides an area
2.4
Pronunciation: / fɛns / (AmE), / fɛns / (BrE)
A structure serving as an enclosure, barrier, or boundary, typically made of wood or wire, erected to surround or divide an area of land.

Examples

A wooden fence
A white picket fence surrounded the charming cottage.
After buying the dog, they built a fence around their yard.
The old wooden fence at the back of our house needs repair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hedge - a fence or boundary formed by closely growing bushes or shrubs
  • synonym: wall - a solid vertical structure that encloses or divides an area
