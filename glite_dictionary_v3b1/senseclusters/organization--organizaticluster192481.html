organization

noun
the act of organizing a business or activity
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrɡənəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌɔrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌɔːrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌɔːr.ɡən.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌɔː.ɡə.naɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act, process, or result of arranging or structuring things or people in an orderly way
