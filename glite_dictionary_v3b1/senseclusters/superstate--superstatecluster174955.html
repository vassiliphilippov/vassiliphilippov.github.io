superstate

noun
large unified country
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌsteɪt / (BrE)
a very large and powerful country or political entity formed from the union of several smaller states

Examples

A European superstate
Some fear that a superstate would reduce the independence of its member countries.
The science fiction novel describes a future ruled by a global superstate.
Debates continue about whether the European Union could become a true superstate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: city-state - an independent city with its own government
  • synonym: confederation - an alliance of independent states
  • synonym: federation - a group of states with a central government
