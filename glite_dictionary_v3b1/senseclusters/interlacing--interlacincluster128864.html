interlacing

noun
weaving together
5.3
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈleɪ.sɪŋ/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈleɪ.sɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act or process of weaving or crossing things together so that they are linked or intertwined

Examples

Interlacing of branches
The interlacing of vines created a dense canopy over the garden path.
Traditional baskets are made by the careful interlacing of reeds or grasses.
The artist admired the interlacing patterns in the ancient stone carvings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: weaving - the act of forming fabric by interlacing threads
