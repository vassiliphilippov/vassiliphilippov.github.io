VLSI

adjective
microchip integration technology
9.7
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.el.es.aɪ/ /; / /ˈviː.əl.əs.aɪ/ (AmE), /ˈviː.el.es.aɪ/ /; / /ˈviː.əl.əs.aɪ/ (BrE)
relating to or using very-large-scale integration, a technology for placing thousands of transistors on a single microchip.

Examples

VLSI chip design
The new smartphone uses VLSI technology to improve performance and reduce power consumption.
Engineers attended a conference on VLSI design and manufacturing techniques.
Advances in VLSI have enabled the creation of smaller and faster electronic devices.

Related Terms
