sexual assault

noun
unwanted, forced sexual act; criminal offense
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkʃuəl əˈsɔlt / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl əˈsɔːlt / (BrE)
Unwanted sexual contact or activity, especially one that involves the use or threat of force, and is considered a criminal offense.
