in secret

adverb
in a hidden or secret manner
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈsiːkrət / (AmE), / ɪn ˈsiːkrɪt / (BrE)
in a way that is hidden or not intended to be known by others; secretly

Examples

To study in secret
She revealed that she had been studying in secret for the big exam.
The committee met in secret to discuss the sensitive issue.
The two leaders arranged to speak in secret to avoid public attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: openly - in a way that is not hidden or secret
  • synonym: secretly - in a secret or private way
