strong relationship

noun
close or significant connection
Pronunciation: / strɔŋ rɪˈleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / strɒŋ rɪˈleɪʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
a close, significant connection between people or groups, marked by trust, affection, or importance

Examples

A strong relationship with clients
Maintaining a strong relationship with clients is vital for business success.
The team built a strong relationship based on mutual respect and communication.
They credit their long marriage to the strong relationship they have developed over the years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: weak relationship - a connection that is not close or significant
