trim

verb
decorate with ornaments
6.1
Pronunciation: / trɪm / (AmE), / trɪm / (BrE)
to decorate something by adding ornaments or attractive items (such as objects, food, or clothing)

Examples

To trim a tree with lights

We always trim the Christmas tree together as a family.
At the banquet, the platters were trimmed with colorful vegetable flowers.
She likes to trim her cakes with slices of strawberries and mint leaves.
The chef trimmed the roast with fresh herbs for a beautiful presentation.
