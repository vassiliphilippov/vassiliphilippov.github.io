provincial

noun
person from the provinces, often unsophisticated
6.1
Pronunciation: / prəˈvɪnʃəl / (AmE), / prəˈvɪnʃəl / (BrE)
a person from a province or rural area, regarded as lacking urban sophistication or narrow-minded

Examples

A provincial arrives
He regarded the newcomers as mere provincials with little knowledge of the world.
The city was filled with provincials seeking work and adventure.
They accused her of being a provincial who did not understand urban life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: countryman - a person from the countryside
  • antonym: urbanite - a person who lives in a city, especially one with a modern and cosmopolitan attitude
