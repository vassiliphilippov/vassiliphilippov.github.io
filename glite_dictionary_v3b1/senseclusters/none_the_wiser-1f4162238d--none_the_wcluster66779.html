none the wiser

adjective
lacking increased understanding
Pronunciation: / nʌn ðə ˈwaɪzɚ / (AmE), / nʌn ðə ˈwaɪzə / (BrE)
not having gained any additional knowledge or understanding, especially after something has been explained or revealed

Examples

Still none the wiser
After three hours of research, she was still none the wiser about the cause of the error.
He explained the entire process, but I was none the wiser when he finished.
They told him their version of the story, yet he remained none the wiser.
