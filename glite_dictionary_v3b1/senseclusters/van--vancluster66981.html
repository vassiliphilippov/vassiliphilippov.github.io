van

noun
foremost part of a moving group, esp. army
6.5
Pronunciation: / væn / (AmE), / van / (BrE)
the foremost part or leading units of a moving group, especially at the head of an army

Examples

Van of the army
In the van of the procession, the flag bearers led the way.
The van of the army pushed forward into enemy territory ahead of the main force.
The general marched at the van of his troops as they entered the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vanguard - the forefront of an action or movement, or those leading it
