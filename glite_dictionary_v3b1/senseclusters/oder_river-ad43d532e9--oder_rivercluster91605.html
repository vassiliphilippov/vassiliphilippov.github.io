Oder River

proper noun
major river in Central Europe
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊdər ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈəʊdə ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A major river in Central Europe, originating in the Czech Republic, flowing through western Poland, and emptying into the Baltic Sea.

Examples

The Oder River flood
Flooding of the Oder River in 1997 caused widespread damage in Poland.
The Oder River forms part of the border between Poland and Germany.
Trade along the Oder River has historically been important for Central European commerce.
