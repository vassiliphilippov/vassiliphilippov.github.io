unoffendable

adjective
inoffensive
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈfɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈfɛndəbəl / (BrE)
not likely to offend or upset anyone; inoffensive in nature or content

Examples

Unoffendable comments
The teacher made sure her comments were unoffendable to avoid upsetting any students.
They chose an unoffendable design for the public campaign to appeal to everyone.
His jokes are always unoffendable, so no one ever feels uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inoffensive - not likely to offend anyone
  • antonym: offensive - causing someone to feel hurt, upset, or angry
