corporally

adverb
physically, bodily
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːrpərəli / (AmE), / ˈkɔːpərəli / (BrE)
in a bodily or physical manner, rather than spiritually or mentally

Examples

To be corporally present
The actor was corporally present on stage, captivating the entire audience.
Some believe that ghosts can appear corporally, while others think they are only spiritual.
He felt corporally exhausted after the long hike through the mountains.

Related Terms
