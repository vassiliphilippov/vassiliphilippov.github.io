Pushtun

noun
a member of an ethnic group in Afghanistan and Pakistan
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpæʃ.tuːn/ /; / /ˈpʌʃ.tən/ (AmE), /ˈpæʃ.tuːn/ /; / /ˈpʌʃ.tən/ (BrE)
a member of an ethnic group native to the mountainous regions of eastern Afghanistan and northwestern Pakistan, also known as Pashtun or Pathan

Examples

A Pushtun person
He identified himself as a Pushtun from eastern Afghanistan.
Many Pushtuns speak both their native Pashto and local dialects.
The Pushtun people have a long and rich tradition of oral storytelling.
