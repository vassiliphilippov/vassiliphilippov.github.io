unpenetrated

adjective
not entered or affected
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈpɛnɪˌtreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈpɛnɪtreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having been entered, passed through, or affected by something

Examples

Unpenetrated cave walls

Much of the rainforest is still unpenetrated and home to unknown species.
The fortress walls were so strong that they stayed unpenetrated during the siege.
