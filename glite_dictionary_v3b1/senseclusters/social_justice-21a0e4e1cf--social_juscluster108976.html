social justice

noun
fairness in society and policy
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ˈdʒʌstɪs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ˈdʒʌstɪs / (BrE)
the fair distribution of wealth, opportunities, and privileges within a society; fairness in social policy and rights

Examples

Social justice movement
Her commitment to social justice inspired her to become a human rights lawyer.
Many activists emphasize social justice as essential for building a more equitable society.
The organization works to promote social justice through education reform and equal opportunity initiatives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: equity - the quality of being fair and impartial
  • antonym: injustice - lack of fairness in treatment or policy
