worksite

noun
place of work
2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈwɜːrk.saɪt/ (AmE), /ˈwɜːk.saɪt/ (BrE)
a place where work, especially construction or industrial work, is carried out

Examples

A worksite area
All employees must wear safety helmets while on the worksite.
The construction worksite was busy with workers and heavy machinery.
Inspectors visited the worksite to ensure all regulations were being followed.
All workers must wear safety gear at the worksite.
The manager inspected the worksite to ensure all safety regulations were being followed.
Heavy machinery was delivered to the worksite early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jobsite - a place where a specific job or project is carried out
  • synonym: office - a room or building where people work, usually at desks
  • synonym: workplace - a location where people are employed to work
