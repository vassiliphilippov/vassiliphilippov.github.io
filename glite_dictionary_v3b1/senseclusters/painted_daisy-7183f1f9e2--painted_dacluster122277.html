painted daisy

noun
ornamental Asiatic flowering plant
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪntəd ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
a perennial garden plant native to Asia, with finely divided aromatic leaves and white, pink, or purple flowers, sometimes used as a source of insecticide

Examples

Painted daisies in garden
Some gardeners plant painted daisies to help repel insects naturally.
The painted daisy brightened up the garden with its vibrant shades of pink and purple.
The florist suggested adding a painted daisy to the bouquet for a splash of color.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pyrethrum - a plant extract used as an insecticide, made from certain daisies
