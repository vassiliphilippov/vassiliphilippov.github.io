queenly

adjective
having qualities or appearance of a queen
5.6 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkwiÀênli / (AmE), / ÀàkwiÀênli / (BrE)
having the rank, qualities, or appearance of a queen; regal or dignified

Examples

Queenly manner
His mother always handled family matters in a wise and queenly manner.
She walked into the room with a queenly grace that caught everyone's attention.
The actress wore a queenly gown at the award ceremony.

Synonyms

  • queenlike
    7.3 0.00/m
    queenlike looks and suits someone as if they were a queen, while queenly suggests actual rank, dignity, or regal bearing.
  • regal
    5.3 1.85/m
    regal looks grand and royal, often used for kings or queens, while queenly focuses on a queen's rank and dignity.
  • majestic
    4.3 3.43/m
    majestic shows great beauty and grandeur, often very impressive, while queenly points to royal rank and calm dignity.
  • stately
    5.7 2.70/m
    stately is formal, dignified, and slow in style, often used for buildings or people, while queenly implies royal rank and graceful authority.
