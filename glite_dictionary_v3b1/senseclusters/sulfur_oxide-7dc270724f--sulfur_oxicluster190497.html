sulfur oxide

noun
oxide compound of sulfur and oxygen
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌlfɚ ˈɑːksaɪd /; / ˈsʌlfɚ ˈɑkˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsʌlfə ˈɒksaɪd / (BrE)
any of several chemical compounds consisting of sulfur and oxygen, especially sulfur dioxide (SO₂) and sulfur trioxide (SO₃)
