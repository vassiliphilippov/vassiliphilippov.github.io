tin man

noun
metal character in Oz
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪn mæn / (AmE), / ˈtɪn mæn / (BrE)
a fictional character made of metal who desires a heart, from 'The Wizard of Oz'

Examples

Tin man from Oz
The tin man joined Dorothy on her journey to see the Wizard.
Children often dress up as the tin man for costume parties.
In the story, the tin man wishes for a real heart to feel emotions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cowardly lion - a lion character from 'The Wizard of Oz' who seeks courage
  • synonym: scarecrow - a straw-filled character from 'The Wizard of Oz' who wants a brain
