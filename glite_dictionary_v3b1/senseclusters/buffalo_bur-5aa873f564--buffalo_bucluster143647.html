buffalo bur

noun
prickly North American nightshade plant
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌfəloʊ bɜr / (AmE), / ˈbʌfələʊ bɜː / (BrE)
A North American nightshade plant (Solanum rostratum) with prickly leaves and stems and yellow, racemose flowers.

The buffalo bur plant
