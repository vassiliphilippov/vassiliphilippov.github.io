aleuronic

adjective
relating to aleurone (seed layer)
5.2
Pronunciation: / əˌlɔːˈrɑːnɪk / (AmE), / əˌljʊəˈrɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of aleurone, the protein-containing outer layer of some seeds.

Examples

Aleuronic layer
Changes in the aleuronic cells can affect the nutritional value of cereals.
Researchers studied the distribution of aleuronic proteins in wheat grains.
The aleuronic layer is important in the germination process of seeds.
