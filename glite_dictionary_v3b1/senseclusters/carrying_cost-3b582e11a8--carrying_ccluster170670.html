carrying cost

noun
cost of holding an asset
5.8 5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkæriɪŋ kɑst / (AmE), / ˈkæriɪŋ kɒst / (BrE)
The cost associated with holding an asset, including expenses such as storage, maintenance, insurance, and the opportunity cost of capital tied up in the asset.

Examples

The carrying cost of assets
Investors must consider the carrying cost of holding real estate over long periods.
Reducing inventory levels can help minimize carrying costs for a business.
The company's high carrying cost for unsold goods affected its profit margins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holding cost - costs incurred for storing or holding inventory
  • synonym: opportunity cost - the loss of potential gain from other alternatives when one alternative is chosen
