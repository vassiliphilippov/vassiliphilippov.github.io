religious mystic

noun
someone seeking direct spiritual connection beyond ordinary understanding
2.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈmɪstɪk / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈmɪstɪk / (BrE)
