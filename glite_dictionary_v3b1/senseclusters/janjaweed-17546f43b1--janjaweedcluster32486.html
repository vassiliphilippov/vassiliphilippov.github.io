Janjaweed

noun
Sudanese militia member
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑːndʒəˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈdʒændʒəˌwiːd / (BrE)
a member of an armed militia group active in western Sudan, especially known for involvement in the Darfur conflict

Examples

A Janjaweed fighter
The government was accused of supporting the Janjaweed during the conflict in Darfur.
Many villages were attacked by the Janjaweed in the early 2000s.
International organizations have documented human rights abuses committed by the Janjaweed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: militiaman - a member of an armed group not part of the regular army
  • synonym: rebel - a person who rises in opposition or armed resistance
