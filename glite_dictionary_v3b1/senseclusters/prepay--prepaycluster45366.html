prepay

verb
pay for in advance
4.1
Pronunciation: / priˈpeɪ / (AmE), / priːˈpeɪ / (BrE)
to pay for something before it is received or due

Examples

To prepay the bill
Customers are required to prepay for gas at this station.
She decided to prepay the entire loan to avoid interest charges.
You must prepay for your order before it can be processed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postpay - pay for something after receiving it
