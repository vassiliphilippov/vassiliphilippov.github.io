day-to-day basis

noun
regularly; as part of the daily routine
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌdeɪ tə ˈdeɪ ˈbeɪsɪs / (AmE), / ˌdeɪ tə ˈdeɪ ˈbeɪsɪs / (BrE)
used to describe something that happens as part of the daily routine; regularly, every day

Examples

On a day-to-day basis
Her symptoms are monitored on a day-to-day basis for any changes.
Managing the store requires flexibility because challenges arise on a day-to-day basis.
We review project goals on a day-to-day basis to ensure we stay on track.
