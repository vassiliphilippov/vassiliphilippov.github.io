Yucateco language

noun
Mayan language of the Yucatec people
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːkəˈteɪkoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌjuːkəˈteɪkəʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
