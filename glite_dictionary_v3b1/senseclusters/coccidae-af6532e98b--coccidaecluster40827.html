Coccidae

noun
family of scale insects
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːkɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˈkɒsɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of small insects, commonly known as scale insects, that infest plants and feed on their sap.

Examples

Coccidae scale insects
Many species in the Coccidae family can cause significant damage to agricultural crops.
Members of the Coccidae are often controlled using natural predators in integrated pest management.
The gardener discovered an infestation of Coccidae on her houseplants.
