undersaturated

adjective
pale or muted colors
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈsætʃəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈsætʃəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
having less color saturation than normal, so that colors appear pale or muted

Examples

Undersaturated colors
The colors in the old photograph appeared undersaturated compared to the original scene.
If your print looks undersaturated, try increasing the color intensity in your editing software.
The artist chose an undersaturated palette to create a calm and subdued atmosphere in the painting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: desaturated - having reduced color intensity or vividness
  • degree higher: saturated - having the maximum possible color intensity
  • antonym: vivid - having strong, bright, or intense colors
