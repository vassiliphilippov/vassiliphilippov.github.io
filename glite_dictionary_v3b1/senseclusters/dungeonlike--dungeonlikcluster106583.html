dungeonlike

adjective
dark and gloomy
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌndʒənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdʌndʒənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a dungeon, especially in being dark, gloomy, or oppressive

Examples

A dungeonlike room
The old library had a dungeonlike feel with its thick stone walls and narrow windows.
We entered a dungeonlike cellar that was cold and barely lit by a single bulb.
His office was so dungeonlike that he brought in extra lamps to brighten it up.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - bright and pleasant, creating a happy atmosphere
  • synonym: gloomy - dark or poorly lit, especially in a way that creates a depressing atmosphere
