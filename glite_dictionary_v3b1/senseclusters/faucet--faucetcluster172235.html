faucet

noun
website that gives small crypto rewards
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfɔː.sət/ /; / /ˈfɔː.sɪt/ (AmE), /ˈfɔː.sɪt/ /; / /ˈfɔː.sət/ (BrE)
(in cryptocurrency) a website or online service that dispenses very small amounts of cryptocurrency to users, often for completing simple tasks or as a promotional reward.

Examples

Crypto faucet
Many users try a faucet that awards small Bitcoin rewards for simple tasks.
The security team warned students against crypto faucet sites that ask for private keys or seed phrases.
After the promotion ended, the company shut down its popular faucet that had distributed small tokens daily.
