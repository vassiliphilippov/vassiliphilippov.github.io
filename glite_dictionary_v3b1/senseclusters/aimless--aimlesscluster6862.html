aimless

adjective
without purpose or direction
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪmləs / (AmE), / ˈeɪmləs / (BrE)
without purpose, direction, or a clear plan; lacking a goal

Examples

An aimless life
After graduation, he spent a year living an aimless life, not sure what to do next.
She walked aimless through the park, lost in thought.
The conversation soon became aimless and wandered from topic to topic.
After college, he spent an aimless year traveling through Europe with no fixed plans.
She felt her life was aimless after losing her job and was unsure which direction to take.
The conversation drifted in an aimless way, never settling on any particular topic.
