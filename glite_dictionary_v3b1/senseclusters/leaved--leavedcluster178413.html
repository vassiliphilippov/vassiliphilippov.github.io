leaved

adjective
having leaves; used in combinations
7.0
Pronunciation: / livd / (AmE), / liːvd / (BrE)
(of a plant) having leaves, or leaves of a specified kind (usually used in combination).

Examples

Broad-leaved plant
Many houseplants are broad-leaved, making them attractive additions to home décor.
She found a rare four-leaved clover in the field.
The forest was filled with tall, leaved trees that provided plenty of shade.
