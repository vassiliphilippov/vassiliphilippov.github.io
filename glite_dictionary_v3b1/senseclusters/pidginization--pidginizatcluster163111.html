pidginization

noun
creation of a pidgin
9.1
Pronunciation: /pɪdʒ.ɪ.nɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /pɪdʒ.ɪ.naɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /pɪdʒ.ɪ.nɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /pɪdʒ.ɪ.naɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process by which a simplified language, or pidgin, develops when speakers of different languages need to communicate

Examples

The pidginization process
The pidginization of language often happens in multicultural trading centers.
Researchers studied the pidginization that occurred during early contact between settlers and indigenous people.
Rapid pidginization can result when groups with no common language must work together.
