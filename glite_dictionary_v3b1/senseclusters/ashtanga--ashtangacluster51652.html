ashtanga

noun
yoga style sequence
8.6
Pronunciation: / ɑːʃˈtɑːŋɡə / (AmE), / ɑːʃˈtæŋɡə / (BrE)
a style of yoga that follows a set sequence of postures combined with specific breathing techniques

Examples

Ashtanga yoga class
She attends an ashtanga class every Saturday to build strength and flexibility.
Many people find ashtanga challenging because of its fast-paced, structured sequence.
After practicing ashtanga for a year, he noticed significant improvements in his balance and focus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hatha - a general category of yoga focusing on physical postures
  • synonym: vinyasa - a style of yoga linking movement and breath
