snowball

noun
ball of snow for play
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌbɔːl / (BrE)
A ball of snow pressed together, typically for throwing in play.

Examples

Throw a snowball
He was hit by a snowball during the neighborhood snowball fight.
She packed a tight snowball and aimed it at her brother.
The children raced outside to make a snowball as soon as the snow started falling.
