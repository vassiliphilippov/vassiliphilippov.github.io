pincer

noun
grasping claw of a crustacean or arthropod
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnsər / (AmE), / ˈpɪnsə / (BrE)
a grasping claw or structure on the limb of a crustacean or other arthropods, used for holding or manipulating things

Examples

A crab's pincer
A lobster's large pincer can deliver a surprisingly strong grip.
The crab used its pincer to defend itself from predators.
The scientist carefully examined the beetle's pincer under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: claw - a curved pointed horny nail on each digit of the foot in birds, lizards, and some mammals
