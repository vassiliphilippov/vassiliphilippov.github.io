mycelium

noun
sustainable fungal biomaterial
7.4
Pronunciation: /maɪ.ˈsiː.lɪ.əm/ /; / /maɪ.ˈsɪ.lɪ.əm/ (AmE), /maɪ.ˈsiː.lɪ.əm/ /; / /maɪ.ˈsɪ.lɪ.əm/ (BrE)
A sustainable biomaterial grown from fungal mycelium and processed into panels, foams, or leather-like sheets for packaging, insulation, or products.

Examples

Mycelium packaging material
The company produces lightweight mycelium packaging that biodegrades quickly after use.
They wrapped the fragile lamp in panels of grown mycelium instead of foam for shipping.
Designers developed a durable, leather-like mycelium for vegan shoes and furniture upholstery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biomaterial - a material produced from living organisms, used in manufacturing or design
  • synonym: mushroom leather - a leather-like material made from fungal mycelium used as a vegan alternative to animal leather
