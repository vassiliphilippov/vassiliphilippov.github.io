First Epistle of John

proper noun
a New Testament letter attributed to John
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrst ɪˈpɪsəl ʌv dʒɑn / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ɪˈpɪsl ɒv dʒɒn / (BrE)
the first of three New Testament letters traditionally attributed to Saint John the Apostle, addressing themes of love and faith
