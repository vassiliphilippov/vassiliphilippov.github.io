freeloader

noun
person who exploits others' generosity
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːˌloʊdər / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌləʊdə / (BrE)
a person who takes advantage of others' generosity without giving anything in return

Examples

A freeloader at parties
She was tired of being taken advantage of by freeloaders at her parties.
The house was full of freeloaders who never contributed to the rent or groceries.
Tom is such a freeloader—he never pays for his share of dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parasite - a person who lives at the expense of others
  • synonym: sponger - a person who habitually borrows or lives off others
