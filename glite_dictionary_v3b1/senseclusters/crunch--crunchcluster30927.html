crunch

noun
noisy crushing or chewing sound
3.8 1.2
Pronunciation: /krʌntʃ/ (AmE), /krʌntʃ/ (BrE)
the sound or act of crushing or chewing something noisily

Examples

A loud crunch
She bit into an apple, enjoying the satisfying crunch.
The only thing he could hear was the crunch of dry leaves beneath his boots.
There was a loud crunch as the car drove over the gravel.
She winced at the crunch of the car accident outside.
The crunch of popcorn echoed in the quiet theater.
There was a loud crunch as he stepped on the frozen leaves.

Related Terms
