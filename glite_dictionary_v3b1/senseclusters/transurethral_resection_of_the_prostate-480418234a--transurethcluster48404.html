transurethral resection of the prostate

noun
surgical removal of prostate tissue via the urethra
Pronunciation: / ˌtræn.sjʊˈriː.θrəl rɪˈsɛk.ʃən əv ðə ˈprɑː.steɪt / (AmE), / ˌtranz.jʊˈriː.θrəl rɪˈsek.ʃən əv ðə ˈprɒs.teɪt / (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which tissue from the prostate gland is removed through the urethra, often to treat benign prostatic hyperplasia
