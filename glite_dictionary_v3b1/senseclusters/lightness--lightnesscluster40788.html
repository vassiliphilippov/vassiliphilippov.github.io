lightness

noun
graceful nimbleness or agility
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of moving with grace and nimbleness; ease and agility in physical movement

Examples

Lightness of movement
Her lightness on the basketball court made her difficult to defend.
The lightness of the dancer's movements captivated the entire audience.
The cat crossed the garden wall with remarkable lightness and ease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agility - the ability to move quickly and easily
  • antonym: clumsiness - lack of grace or agility in movement
