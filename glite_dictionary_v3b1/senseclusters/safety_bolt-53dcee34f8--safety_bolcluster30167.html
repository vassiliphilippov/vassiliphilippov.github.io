safety bolt

noun
bolt for securing doors from inside
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti boʊlt / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti bəʊlt / (BrE)
a bolt used on a door or gate that cannot be operated from outside, providing additional security

Examples

A safety bolt on door
He slid the safety bolt into place, securing the garden gate against intruders.
Make sure the safety bolt is locked before you go to bed.
The apartment is equipped with a safety bolt that can only be operated from the inside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deadbolt - a locking bolt moved by turning a knob or key; commonly used for extra security
