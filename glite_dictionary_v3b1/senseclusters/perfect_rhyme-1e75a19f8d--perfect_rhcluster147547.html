perfect rhyme

noun
exact rhyming of final sounds
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrfɪkt raɪm / (AmE), / ˈpɜːfɪkt raɪm / (BrE)
a rhyme in which the sounds at the end of lines are an exact match, both in vowel and following consonant sounds

Examples

A perfect rhyme
It's not always easy to find a perfect rhyme for every word in English.
Many classic nursery rhymes rely on perfect rhyme to create a pleasing sound.
She was pleased to discover a perfect rhyme for her poem's final line.
