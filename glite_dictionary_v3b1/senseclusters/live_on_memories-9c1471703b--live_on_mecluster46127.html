live on memories

verb
find comfort in past experiences
1.0
Pronunciation: / lɪv ɑn ˈmɛməriz / (AmE), / lɪv ɒn ˈmɛməriz / (BrE)
to continue to derive emotional comfort or sustain oneself by recalling past experiences

Examples

To live on memories happily
After her husband passed away, she tried to live on memories of the happy years they spent together.
He didn't make new friends in the city and instead chose to live on memories from his hometown.
Many retired athletes live on memories of their glory days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dwell on the past - to spend a lot of time thinking or talking about past events
  • antonym: move on - to stop thinking about or being affected by something from the past
