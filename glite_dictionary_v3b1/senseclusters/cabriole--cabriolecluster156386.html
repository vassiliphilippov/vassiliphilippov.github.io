cabriole

verb
to leap with a kick
Pronunciation: / ˈkæbriˌoʊl / (AmE), / ˈkæbriəʊl / (BrE)
to leap or spring into the air with a kicking motion, especially as performed by a horse or dancer.

Examples

To cabriole in dressage
The horse would cabriole gracefully during the dressage performance, impressing the judges.
In ballet class, the students practiced how to cabriole with perfect timing and form.
Spectators watched as the stallion began to cabriole energetically around the ring.
