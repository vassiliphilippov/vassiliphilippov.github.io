comedown

noun
a fall to lower status or level
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈkʌmˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a situation in which someone or something declines to a lower status, position, or level, often disappointing compared to previous circumstances

Examples

A career comedown
After years as a CEO, working as a consultant was quite a comedown.
Losing the championship was a real comedown for the team.
Moving from a spacious house to a small apartment was a bit of a comedown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fall from grace - a loss of status, respect, or prestige
  • antonym: promotion - the act of rising to a higher position or status
