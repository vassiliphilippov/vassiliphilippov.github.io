endocarditis

noun
inflammation of heart lining/valves
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.doʊ.kɑːrˈdaɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛn.dəʊ.kɑːˈdaɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
A serious medical condition involving inflammation of the inner lining of the heart (endocardium) and its valves.

Examples

Bacterial endocarditis
Antibiotic treatment is often required to successfully manage bacterial endocarditis.
If left untreated, endocarditis can lead to severe heart valve damage.
The patient was diagnosed with endocarditis following complaints of fever and chest pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: myocarditis - inflammation of the heart muscle
  • synonym: pericarditis - inflammation of the membrane surrounding the heart
