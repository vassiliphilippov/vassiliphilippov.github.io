hostless

adjective
without a host
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊstləs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊstləs / (BrE)
not having or lacking a host to live in or on.

Examples

Hostless bacteria
The bacteria became hostless after the animal died.
Some viruses cannot survive in a hostless environment for long.
Scientists studied the hostless stage of the parasite's life cycle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: free-living - not dependent on a host for survival
  • antonym: parasitic - living in or on another organism (a host)
