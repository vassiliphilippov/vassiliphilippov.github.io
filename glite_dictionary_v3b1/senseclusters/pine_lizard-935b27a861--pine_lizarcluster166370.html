pine lizard

noun
a small North American lizard
4.5
Pronunciation: / paɪn ˈlɪzɚd / (AmE), / paɪn ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
a small, active lizard native to the United States and southern Canada, typically found in pine forests

Examples

A pine lizard in forest
A pine lizard darted across the path as we hiked through the forest.
Biologists are studying how the pine lizard adapts to changes in its habitat.
The children were delighted to spot a pine lizard basking on a fallen log.
