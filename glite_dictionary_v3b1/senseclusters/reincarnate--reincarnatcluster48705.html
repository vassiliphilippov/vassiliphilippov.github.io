reincarnate

adjective
born again in a new body
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈɪnkɑrˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɪŋ.kɑː.neɪt / (BrE)
(of a person or soul) having been born again in a new body after death.

Examples

A reincarnate soul
In the novel, the main character discovers she is a reincarnate soul with memories of her past life.
Many people believe the Dalai Lama is a reincarnate spiritual leader continuing his legacy across generations.
They searched for the reincarnate child who would one day lead their community.

Related Terms
