cavewoman

noun
prehistoric woman living in a cave
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪvˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈkeɪvˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A prehistoric woman, especially one imagined as living in a cave during the Stone Age.

Examples

A cavewoman costume
For the costume party, she dressed up as a classic cavewoman with a fur dress and wooden club.
Some anthropologists study what daily life was like for a cavewoman in the Stone Age.
The film portrayed a fierce cavewoman defending her family from wild animals.
