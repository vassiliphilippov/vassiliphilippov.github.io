foreign legion

noun
military unit of foreign volunteers
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrən ˈliːdʒən / (AmE), / ˈfɒrən ˈliːdʒən / (BrE)
a military unit made up of foreign volunteers who serve in the armed forces of a country, especially associated with the French Foreign Legion.

Examples

The foreign legion unit
He served in the foreign legion for five years before returning home.
Stories about the foreign legion often describe its mix of nationalities and backgrounds.
The foreign legion became famous for accepting volunteers from around the world.
