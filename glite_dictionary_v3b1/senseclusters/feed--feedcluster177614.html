feed

verb
(of animals) eat food
1.0 22/m
Pronunciation: / fid / (AmE), / fiÀêd / (BrE)
(of an animal) to eat or take in food.

Examples

Lions feed on prey
Lions feed on their prey after a successful hunt.
Some birds feed throughout the night when insects are plentiful.
The horse begins to feed at sunrise every day.

Synonyms

  • eat
    1.0 309/m
    eat means putting food into the mouth, chewing, and swallowing, while feed means an animal takes in food.
  • feed on
    2.9 5.21/m
    feed on means eating a particular item or living on it regularly, while feed means to eat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eat - to put food into the mouth, chew, and swallow it
