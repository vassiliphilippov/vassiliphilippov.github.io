herewith

adverb
by means of this (formal)
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪrˈwɪθ / (AmE), / ˌhɪəˈwɪθ / (BrE)
(formal) By means of this document, letter, or communication; with this letter or statement.

Examples

I herewith submit
I am herewith submitting my resignation, effective immediately.
Please find herewith the documents requested for your review.
The revised contract is herewith enclosed for your signature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hereby - by this means (especially in official or legal contexts)
