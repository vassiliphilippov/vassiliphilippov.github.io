pottage

noun
a thick vegetable (and sometimes meat) stew
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑː.tɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɒt.ɪdʒ /; / ˈpɒ.tɪdʒ / (BrE)
a thick soup or stew made from vegetables and sometimes meat, often traditional or historical
