bark

noun
the sound a dog makes
1.0
Pronunciation: / bɑrk / (AmE), / bɑːk / (BrE)
the sharp, loud sound made by a dog or a similar noise

Examples

The bark of the dog could be heard all night long.

The engine made a harsh bark before starting up.
Every evening, I am greeted by the familiar bark of my neighbor's terrier.
Suddenly, the bark of a dog startled the sleeping cat.
The bark of a large dog echoed through the empty street.
