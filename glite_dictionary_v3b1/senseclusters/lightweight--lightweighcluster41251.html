lightweight

noun
an unimportant or insignificant person
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌweɪt / (BrE)
a person or thing that is not important, influential, or serious

Examples

A business lightweight
Despite his big talk, he's considered a lightweight in the business world.
Don't underestimate her—she may seem like a lightweight, but she's quite determined.
Many critics dismissed the new senator as a political lightweight with little experience.
He was considered a lightweight in the world of professional boxing.
The new laptop is a real lightweight, making it easy to carry around campus.
In the debate, she was dismissed as a political lightweight by her opponents.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: featherweight - a person or thing lighter than a lightweight, especially in sports
  • antonym: heavyweight - a person of great importance, influence, or ability
  • synonym: light - having little weight; not heavy
  • synonym: nobody - a person of no importance or influence
