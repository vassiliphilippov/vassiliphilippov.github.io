goose-stepper

noun
person who marches with goose step
Pronunciation: / ˈɡusˌstɛpər / (AmE), / ˈɡuːsˌstɛpə / (BrE)
a person, especially a soldier, who marches using the goose step, a formal military marching style characterized by straight-legged steps

Examples

A goose-stepper soldier
During the parade, one goose-stepper lost his balance and broke formation.
Reporters praised the goose-stepper for his discipline and attention to detail.
The goose-stepper maintained perfect synchronization with the rest of the marching unit.
