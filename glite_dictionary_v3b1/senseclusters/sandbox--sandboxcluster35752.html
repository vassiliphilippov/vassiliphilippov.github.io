sandbox

noun
box or area of sand for children
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsændˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈsændˌbɒks / (BrE)
a container or area filled with sand, especially for children to play in

Examples

Children's sandbox
Her favorite playground activity is digging tunnels in the sandbox.
The children spent the afternoon building castles in the sandbox.
We put a new cover on the sandbox to keep the neighborhood cats out.

Related Terms
