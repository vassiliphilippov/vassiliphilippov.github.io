stressless

adjective
without stress
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɛsləs / (AmE), / ˈstrɛsləs / (BrE)
not causing or experiencing stress; free from worry, tension, or anxiety

Examples

Stressless weekend
After finishing her exams, she finally enjoyed a stressless weekend at home.
The company aims to create a stressless work environment for all employees.
He prefers a stressless lifestyle, avoiding unnecessary worries and pressures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or anger
  • synonym: relaxed - free from tension or anxiety
  • antonym: stressful - causing a lot of worry or tension
