monkeytail

noun
hairy trailing cactus
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋkiˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋkiˌteɪl / (BrE)
a type of trailing cactus with long, hairy cylindrical stems, often grown as an ornamental houseplant

Examples

Monkeytail cactus plant
The monkeytail cactus is popular for its unique, furry stems that hang over the sides of pots.
She placed her monkeytail in a sunny window to help it grow long and healthy.
Gardeners admire the monkeytail for its unusual appearance and easy care indoors.
