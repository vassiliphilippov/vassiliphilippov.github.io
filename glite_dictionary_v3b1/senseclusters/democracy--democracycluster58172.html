democracy

noun
rule by majority within a group
2.7 5/m

The principle that the numerical majority of an organized group has the power to make decisions binding on the whole group.

Examples

Majority democracy
Critics argue that pure democracy can overlook the rights of minorities within the group.
In a democracy, policies are often decided by what most people in the group want.
The club adopted more democracy in choosing its new leaders, allowing all members to vote.

Synonyms

    3.6 0.9/m

  • majoritarianism
    9.1 0.08/m
    majoritarianism is a political view that gives the majority's will priority, while democracy values majority rule but also protects minorities.
