Examples

Practice of self-denial
He showed great self-denial by refusing luxury in pursuit of his goals.
Her years of self-denial enabled her to care for her ailing parents.
Monks practice self-denial as part of their spiritual discipline.
Her self-denial during the fasting period was admired by all her friends.
Practicing self-denial can help you achieve long-term goals despite short-term temptations.
Years of self-denial allowed him to save enough money to start his own business.
His self-denial allowed him to save money for years despite many temptations.
Practicing self-denial is often considered a virtue in many religious traditions.
She showed great self-denial by refusing dessert at every family gathering.

  • abnegation
