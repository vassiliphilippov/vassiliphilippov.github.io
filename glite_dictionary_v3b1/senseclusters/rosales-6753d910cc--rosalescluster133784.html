Rosales

noun
an order of flowering plants
Pronunciation: / roʊˈzeɪliːz / (AmE), / rəˈzeɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of flowering plants that includes the rose family (Rosaceae) and several related families; in some classifications, it does not include Leguminosae (the legume family)

Examples

Rosales plant order
Recent classifications of Rosales differ on whether the legume family should be included.
Scientists studied the evolutionary relationships within Rosales to better understand flowering plant diversification.
The order Rosales includes well-known families such as the Rosaceae and Urticaceae.
