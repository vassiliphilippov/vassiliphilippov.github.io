Rosales

noun
an order of flowering plants
9.1
Pronunciation: /roʊ.ˈzeɪ.liːz/ /; / /roʊ.ˈseɪlz/ (AmE), /rəʊ.ˈzeɪ.liːz/ /; / /rəʊ.ˈseɪlz/ (BrE)
