electorally

adverb
in relation to elections
6.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktərəli / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktərəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to elections or the process of voting

Examples

To act electorally
The party was electorally successful in several key districts during the last election.
Certain policies are designed to be electorally appealing to a broad range of voters.
He made decisions that were not always electorally popular but were considered necessary.

Related Terms
