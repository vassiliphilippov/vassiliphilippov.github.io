pleated

adjective
with narrow folds
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈplitɪd / (AmE), / ˈpliːtɪd / (BrE)
having narrow folds or creases, especially as a design in fabric or clothing

Examples

Pleated skirt
She wore a pleated skirt to the party.
The curtains in the living room were beautifully pleated to add texture.
He ironed his pleated trousers carefully before the interview.

Related Terms
