Synonyms

  • respect
    1.0 48/m
    respect is deep admiration for someone's qualities or actions, while regard is a more general feeling including respect, admiration, or affection.
  • esteem
    3.6 21/m
    esteem is formal high respect often used in writing or speech, while regard covers respect, admiration, or warm feeling.
  • admiration
    1.8 9.30/m
    admiration is warm approval for someone's skill or action, while regard is a broader feeling that can include admiration or affection.
  • affection
    1.4 23/m
    affection is a gentle, personal feeling of liking or love, while regard often means respect or a more formal feeling.
  • fondness
    2.8 2.65/m
    fondness is a warm, often simple liking used in close relations, while regard can mean formal respect, admiration, or affection.
