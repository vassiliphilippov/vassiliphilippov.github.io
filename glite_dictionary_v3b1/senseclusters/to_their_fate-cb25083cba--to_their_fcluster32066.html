to their fate

adverb
left without help or support
5.6
Pronunciation: / tə ðɛr feɪt / (AmE), / tə ðɛə feɪt / (BrE)
left without help or support, allowing circumstances to determine what happens to them

Examples

The explorers were left to their fate when the rescue team failed to arrive.
