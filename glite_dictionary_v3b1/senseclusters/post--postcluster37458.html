post

noun
a job or position in an organization
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /poʊst/ (AmE), /pəʊst/ (BrE)
a job, position, or place where someone is assigned to work or carry out duties, especially in an organization or the military

Examples

A senior post
After years of service, she was finally promoted to a senior post within the company.
He applied for the vacant post of marketing manager last month.
She resigned from her post as ambassador after three years abroad.
After completing basic training, he was assigned to a remote post in the desert.
Families living on the post have access to various facilities and services.
The army plans to build a new post near the border.
During the night, he patrolled between the different posts along the perimeter.
No one is allowed to leave their post until they have been relieved by another guard.
The guard took his post at the main gate just before sunrise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: position - a particular job or role within an organization
  • synonym: station - a place where someone is assigned to work or stand guard
  • antonym: unemployment - the state of not having a job
