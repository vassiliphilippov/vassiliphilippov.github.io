majordomo

noun
chief steward
9.1
Pronunciation: /meɪ.dʒər.ˈdoʊ.moʊ/ (AmE), /meɪ.dʒə.ˈdəʊ.məʊ/ (BrE)
a person who manages the operations and staff of a large household, estate, organization, or event; a chief administrator

Examples

The majordomo manages staff
The majordomo coordinated the daily activities of everyone working in the estate.
As majordomo, she ensured that every event at the villa ran smoothly.
The wealthy businessman hired a majordomo to manage his household staff and affairs.
The majordomo ensured that every detail of the gala was perfectly organized.
As the majordomo of the estate, she supervised all the staff and daily operations.
During the conference, the majordomo handled logistics and coordinated the speakers.
