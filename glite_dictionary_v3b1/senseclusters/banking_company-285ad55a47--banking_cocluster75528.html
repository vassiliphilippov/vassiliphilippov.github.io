banking company

noun
financial institution accepting deposits and lending money
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋkɪŋ ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈbæŋkɪŋ ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
A financial institution that accepts deposits and channels the money into lending or investment activities.

Examples

A banking company
Each banking company must comply with strict rules regarding customer deposits.

The government introduced new regulations for every banking company operating within its borders.
