phreak

noun
telephone system hacker
9.9
Pronunciation: / friːk / (AmE), / friːk / (BrE)
a person who hacks or manipulates telephone systems, especially to make free calls or explore networks

Examples

A phreak hacker
In the 1970s, a phreak could make free long-distance calls using homemade devices.
The documentary explored how early phreaks experimented with telephone networks.
He became a well-known phreak by discovering new ways to access restricted phone lines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cracker - a person who breaks into computer systems, often with malicious intent
  • synonym: hacker - a person who uses computers to gain unauthorized access to data
