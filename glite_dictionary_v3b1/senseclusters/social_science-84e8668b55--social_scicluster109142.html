social science

noun
the study of society and social relationships
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
The scientific study of society, human relationships, and social behavior.

Examples

Study of social science
He decided to major in social science at university.
Many careers in government and education require knowledge of social science.
The conference brought together experts from various social science fields to discuss global challenges.
