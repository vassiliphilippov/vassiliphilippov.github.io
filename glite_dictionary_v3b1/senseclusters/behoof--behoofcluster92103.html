behoof

verb
to benefit or help
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪˈhuːf / (AmE), / bɪˈhuːf / (BrE)
to be of use, benefit, or advantage to someone; to help or profit (archaic or literary)

Examples

To behoof others
The wise counsel of his elders would greatly behoof him in his new responsibilities.
It would behoof you to listen carefully to the instructions before starting the task.
Her generosity did much to behoof the poor in the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benefit - to be helpful or advantageous to
  • antonym: harm - to cause damage or injury to
