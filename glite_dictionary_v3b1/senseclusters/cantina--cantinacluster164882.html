cantina

noun
institutional cafeteria
3.4
Pronunciation: / kænˈtinə / (AmE), / kænˈtiːnə / (BrE)
a cafeteria or canteen, especially in a school, factory, or military setting, where meals are served to groups of people

Examples

School cantina
The students gathered in the cantina for lunch after their morning classes.
Every day, the factory workers enjoy a hot meal in the company cantina.
During the break, soldiers relaxed and chatted in the base cantina.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cafeteria - a restaurant or dining area where people serve themselves food
  • synonym: canteen - a place in a school, factory, or office where people eat meals
