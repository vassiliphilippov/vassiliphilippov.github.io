subject matter

noun
topic or content of discussion or work
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbdʒɪkt ˌmætər / (AmE), / ˈsʌbdʒɪkt ˌmætə / (BrE)
the topic or content addressed by a discussion, work of art, book, etc.

Examples

The subject matter of a book
Before choosing a thesis topic, he considered several possible areas of subject matter.
Some viewers found the subject matter of the painting disturbing.
The subject matter of her novel deals with social inequality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: content - the subjects or topics covered in a book or document
  • synonym: topic - the subject of a discussion or piece of writing
