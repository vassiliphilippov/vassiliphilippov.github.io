Relating to or advocating an economic system in which industries are owned and operated by individuals or companies for profit, rather than by the state.

Examples

Private-enterprise system
Critics claim that unregulated private-enterprise systems can lead to income inequality.
Many analysts argue that a private-enterprise approach leads to greater innovation in the technology sector.
The new government favors a private-enterprise model over state control of major industries.

Synonyms

    3.9 1.71/m

  • free enterprise
    3.8 2.80/m
    free enterprise emphasizes competitive markets and limited state control, while private-enterprise highlights private ownership and profit as the system's core.
