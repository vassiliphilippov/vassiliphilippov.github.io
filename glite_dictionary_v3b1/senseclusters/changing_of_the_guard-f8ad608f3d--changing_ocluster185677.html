changing of the guard

noun
ceremony marking shift of guards or responsibilities
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ əv ðə ɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ əv ðə ɡɑːd / (BrE)
a ceremonial event in which the responsibility for a post or duty is handed over from one group or individual to another, especially that involving uniformed guards at a palace or other significant location

Examples

The changing of the guard ceremony
Many people consider the changing of the guard to be a symbol of tradition and continuity.
The changing of the guard is performed daily during the summer months.
Tourists gathered early to see the changing of the guard outside Buckingham Palace.
