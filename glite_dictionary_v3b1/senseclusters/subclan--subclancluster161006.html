subclan

noun
smaller group in clan
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌklæn / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌklæn / (BrE)

Examples

A subclan group

The festival brought together members from every subclan in the region.
Leadership of the subclan passed from father to eldest son.
