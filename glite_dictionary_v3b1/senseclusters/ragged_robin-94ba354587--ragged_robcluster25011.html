ragged robin

noun
a plant with pink, ragged-petalled flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈræɡɪd ˈrɑbɪn / (AmE), / ˈræɡɪd ˈrɒbɪn / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to Europe and western Asia, known for its deeply divided pink flowers with ragged-edged petals.
