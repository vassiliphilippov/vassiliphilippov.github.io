voidness

noun
emptiness; lack of content
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪdnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being empty, lacking content, or having nothing inside

Examples

A feeling of voidness
After the guests left, a sense of voidness filled the empty house.
The artist tried to capture the feeling of voidness in her abstract painting.
He struggled with the voidness that followed the end of his long career.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emptiness - the state of containing nothing
  • antonym: fullness - the state of being full or containing a lot
  • synonym: nothingness - the state of not existing or of being nothing
