decad

noun
group of ten
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛkæd / (AmE), / ˈdɛkæd / (BrE)
an old term for a group or set of ten things

Examples

A decad of rules
The philosopher divided his teachings into several decads for easier study.
Each decad in the collection represented a different aspect of ancient knowledge.
The book was organized into decads, each containing ten related essays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decade - a period of ten years or a group of ten
