ambisexual

adjective
attracted to both sexes
Pronunciation: / ˌæmbiˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌæmbɪˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
attracted to both men and women; having sexual attraction to people of both sexes
