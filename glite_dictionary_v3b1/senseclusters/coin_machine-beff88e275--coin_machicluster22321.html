coin machine

noun
machine operated by inserting a coin
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔɪn məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈkɔɪn məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine operated by inserting a coin into a slot to activate its function or service

Examples

Coin machine slot
Older parking meters were often a type of coin machine requiring change to use them.
She bought a snack from the coin machine in the office lobby.
The laundromat has a coin machine for exchanging bills into quarters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vending machine - machine that dispenses items when a coin is inserted
