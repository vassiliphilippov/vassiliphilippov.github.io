spindler

noun
textile worker
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪndlɚ / (AmE), / ˈspɪndlɚ / (BrE)
a person who operates a spinning machine or spins thread, especially in a textile mill

Examples

Factory spindler spins thread
The spindler carefully monitored the spinning machines throughout her shift.
In the 19th century, a skilled spindler was essential to every textile factory.
He learned the trade of a spindler from his father, who worked in the same mill.
