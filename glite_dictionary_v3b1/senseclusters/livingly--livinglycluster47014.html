livingly

adverb
in a lively way
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋli / (BrE)
in a lively, animated, or spirited manner

Examples

To speak livingly
She described her childhood adventures so livingly that everyone could picture them.
The actor performed the role livingly, bringing the character to life on stage.
He painted the scene livingly, using bright colors and energetic brushstrokes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dully - in a way that lacks liveliness or excitement
  • synonym: vividly - in a way that is very clear, bright, or lively
