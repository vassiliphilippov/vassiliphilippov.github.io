belt-like

adjective
resembling a belt in form or shape
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛltˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɛltˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling a belt in shape, position, or appearance, especially by forming a band or encircling part.

Examples

A belt-like band
A belt-like scar encircled his upper arm where the burn had healed.
The fungus grew in a belt-like pattern around the trunk of the tree.
The satellite image showed a belt-like cloud formation stretching across the continent.
