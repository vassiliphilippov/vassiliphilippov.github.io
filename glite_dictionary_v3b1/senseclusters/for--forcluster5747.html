for

preposition
rate or ratio
1.0
Pronunciation: / fɔr / (AmE), / fɔː / (BrE)
used to indicate a rate, proportion, or ratio, often meaning 'per' in contexts of quantity or measurement

Examples

Miles for each hour
The speed limit is sixty miles for every hour on this highway.
You need one egg for every two cups of flour in this recipe.
The company charges a fee for each transaction processed.
