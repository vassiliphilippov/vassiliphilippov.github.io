rotor blade

noun
rotating blade of a helicopter
5.2 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊ.tər bleɪd/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊ.tə bleɪd/ (BrE)
A long, shaped blade that rotates to provide lift for a helicopter or similar aircraft.

Examples
