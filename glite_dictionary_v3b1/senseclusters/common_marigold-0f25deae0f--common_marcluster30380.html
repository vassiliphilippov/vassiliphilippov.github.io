common marigold

noun
a yellow or orange European annual flower (calendula)
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑm.ən ˈmær.iˌɡoʊld / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ən ˈmær.ɪˌɡəʊld / (BrE)
a species of annual plant native to Europe, with bright yellow or orange flowers, widely grown as an ornamental and used in herbal remedies; also known as calendula
