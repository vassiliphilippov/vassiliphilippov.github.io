recounting

noun
an act of narrating events
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkaʊntɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈkaʊntɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of telling or describing events or experiences in detail

Examples

A recounting of events
Her vivid recounting of the accident helped the police understand what had happened.
His endless recounting of childhood adventures amused his grandchildren.
The book is a recounting of his travels through South America.

Related Terms

  • synonym: narration - the act of narrating a story or events
  • synonym: storytelling - the activity of telling stories or describing events
