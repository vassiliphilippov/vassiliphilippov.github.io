turn up

verb
bend or fold so one part covers another
4.5
Pronunciation: / tɝn ʌp / (AmE), / tɜːn ʌp / (BrE)
to bend or fold a part of something so that one part covers another, especially the edge or end

Examples

To turn up sleeves
Before going outside, she turned up her coat collar to keep out the wind.
He turned up the cuffs of his trousers before wading into the water.
She turned up the edge of the blanket to make it neater.

Related Terms
