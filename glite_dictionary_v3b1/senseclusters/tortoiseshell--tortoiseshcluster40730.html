tortoiseshell

noun
a brightly colored butterfly
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔr.t̬əsˌʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈtɔː.tə.səl / (BrE)
a brightly colored butterfly, especially one whose larvae feed on nettles, such as the small tortoiseshell of Eurasia (Aglais urticae)

Examples

Tortoiseshell butterfly
Several tortoiseshells were seen fluttering along the hedgerows, their larvae feeding on nettles nearby.
She identified the butterfly as a small tortoiseshell because of its vivid orange and black pattern.
The tortoiseshell alighted gracefully on the purple thistle flowers in the meadow.
