inappropriate

adjective
not suitable or proper
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnəˈproʊpriət / (AmE), / ˌɪnəˈprəʊpriət / (BrE)
not suitable or proper in a particular situation or for a particular purpose

Examples

An inappropriate comment
His comment was highly inappropriate given the seriousness of the situation.
The teacher quickly stopped the inappropriate behavior in class.
Wearing jeans to a formal wedding would be considered inappropriate.
His remarks were considered inappropriate by everyone at the meeting.
It would be inappropriate to wear jeans to a formal wedding ceremony.
The teacher warned the students not to use inappropriate language in class.

Related Terms

  • antonym: appropriate - suitable or proper in the circumstances
