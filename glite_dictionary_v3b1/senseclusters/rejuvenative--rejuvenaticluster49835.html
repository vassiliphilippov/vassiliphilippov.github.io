rejuvenative

noun
youth-restoring treatment
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdʒuːvənətɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒuːvənətɪv / (BrE)
a substance, treatment, or procedure that restores youthfulness, appearance, or vitality

Examples

A rejuvenative treatment
The spa offers a special rejuvenative that promises to make your skin look younger.
Many people seek a rejuvenative to regain their energy and youthful appearance.
After a long week, she treated herself to a rejuvenative at the wellness center.

  • synonym: tonic - a medicine or substance that gives a feeling of vigor or well-being
