Theodoric

proper noun
male given name
Pronunciation: / θiˈɑdərɪk / (AmE), / θiˈɒdərɪk / (BrE)
a male given name, especially known for Theodoric the Great, king of the Ostrogoths in the 5th and 6th centuries

Examples

Theodoric the Great
Theodoric was crowned king of the Ostrogoths in 493 AD.
Many historians study the reign of Theodoric for its impact on early medieval Europe.
The name Theodoric is still used in some European countries today.
