roast

verb
cook with dry heat (in oven or over fire)
Pronunciation: / roʊst / (AmE), / rəʊst / (BrE)
to cook food, especially meat, using dry heat in an oven or over an open fire

Examples

To roast vegetables
She likes to roast vegetables to bring out their flavor.
The chef will roast the lamb for several hours until it is tender.
We plan to roast a chicken for Sunday dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bake - cook food with dry heat, usually in an oven
  • antonym: boil - cook in water at 100°C / 212°F
  • synonym: broil - cook by direct exposure to heat, especially from above
