grieve

verb
cause great sadness
4.1 3.84/m
Pronunciation: / …°riv / (AmE), / …°riÀêv / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel great sadness or sorrow

Examples

To grieve a friend
His absence deeply grieved his family during the holidays.
It would grieve her to know how upset you are.
The sudden changes in policy grieved employees across the company.
