close ranks

verb
unite for support or a common purpose
4.8 3.6
Pronunciation: /kloʊz ræŋks/ (AmE), /kləʊz ræŋks/ (BrE)
to unite or come together in order to support each other, especially during times of difficulty or challenge

Examples

To close ranks tightly
After the scandal, party members were urged to close ranks to protect the party's reputation.
In the face of criticism, the organization decided to close ranks and present a united front.
When team morale was low, the coach encouraged everyone to close ranks and work together.
