bergère

noun
French armchair
Pronunciation: / bɛrˈʒɛr / (AmE), / bɛˈʒɛə / (BrE)
an upholstered French armchair with closed sides and an exposed wooden frame

Examples

A bergere chair
The living room was decorated with a classic bergère upholstered in floral fabric.
She inherited a beautiful bergère from her grandmother's antique collection.
A pair of elegant bergères flanked the fireplace in the Parisian apartment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armchair - a comfortable chair with side supports for a person's arms
  • antonym: sofa - a long upholstered seat with a back and arms
