overweeningly

adverb
excessively arrogantly
9.9
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈwiː.nɪŋ.iː/ /; / /ə.vər.ˈwiː.nɪŋ.iː/ (AmE), /əʊ.vər.ˈwiː.nɪŋ.iː/ /; / /ə.vər.ˈwiː.nɪŋ.iː/ (BrE)
in an excessively proud, arrogant, or self-confident manner

Examples

To act overweeningly proud
He overweeningly dismissed the suggestions of his colleagues, believing only his ideas mattered.
The CEO spoke overweeningly about the company's success, ignoring the team's contributions.
She overweeningly assumed she would win the competition without much effort.
He was overweeningly confident, ignoring the advice of his experienced colleagues.
The proposal was overweeningly complex for such a simple problem.
She overweeningly believed in her own abilities, often underestimating the challenges ahead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogantly - in a proud and self-important way
  • synonym: excessively - to a degree that is more than is necessary or reasonable
  • antonym: moderately - to a reasonable or average degree
  • antonym: modestly - in a humble or unassuming way
