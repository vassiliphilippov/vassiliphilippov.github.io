pietist

adjective
excessively devout
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpaɪ.ə.tɪst/ /; / /ˈpiː.ə.tɪst/ (AmE), /ˈpaɪ.ə.tɪst/ /; / /ˈpiː.ə.tɪst/ (BrE)
showing or expressing strong religious devotion, often in a way that seems excessive or self-righteous

Examples

Pietist attitude
His pietist attitude sometimes made others feel uncomfortable in social situations.
The novel features a pietist character who is always quoting religious texts.
Some found her pietist approach inspiring, while others thought it was too extreme.
