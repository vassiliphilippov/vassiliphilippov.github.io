flatmate

noun
person sharing a flat or apartment
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈflætˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈflætˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a person who shares a flat or apartment with one or more others

Examples

My flatmate from Spain
I had a flatmate from Spain during my first year at university.
It's important to communicate well with your flatmate to avoid conflicts.
My flatmate usually takes out the trash on Mondays.
