Quidditch

noun
real-world broomstick team sport
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪdɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈkwɪdɪtʃ / (BrE)
(in sport) a real-world team game adapted from the fictional Harry Potter sport, played on the ground with players running while holding a broom between their legs and competing to score goals and complete specific roles.

Examples

College Quidditch match
The university now hosts an intercollegiate Quidditch tournament featuring broomstick play and mixed-gender teams.
She joined the local Quidditch club, where players sprint with brooms between their legs during matches.
After practice, the Quidditch team celebrated their win at a nearby café.
