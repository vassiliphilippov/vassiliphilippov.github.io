adiposity

noun
state of being fat; body fat
Pronunciation: / ˌæd.əˈpɑː.sɪ.t̬i / (AmE), / ˌæd.ɪˈpɒs.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being fat; the amount of fat in the body, especially as a health measure

Examples

Body adiposity level
Increased adiposity is a major risk factor for cardiovascular disease.
Studies show that diet and exercise can help reduce adiposity.
The doctor was concerned about the patient's level of adiposity.
