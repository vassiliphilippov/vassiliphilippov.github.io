rotary harrow

noun
soil-tilling tool with spiked rotating wheels
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊ.t̬ər.i ˈhær.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.tər.i ˈhær.əʊ / (BrE)
an agricultural implement with rotating, spiked wheels used to break up and smooth soil surfaces

Examples

A rotary harrow tool
After several passes with the rotary harrow, the soil was fine and level.
Modern rotary harrows are designed to minimize soil compaction while tilling.
The farmer used a rotary harrow to prepare the field before planting.

Related Terms
