discrepantly

adverb
inconsistently
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskrɛpəntli / (AmE), / dɪˈskrɛpəntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a difference, inconsistency, or lack of agreement between things

Examples

Reported discrepantly
The results were discrepantly reported by different research teams.
She noticed that the data was discrepantly presented in the two documents.
The students' test scores were discrepantly high in one class and low in another.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inconsistently - in a way that is not consistent or uniform
  • antonym: uniformly - in a way that is the same throughout
