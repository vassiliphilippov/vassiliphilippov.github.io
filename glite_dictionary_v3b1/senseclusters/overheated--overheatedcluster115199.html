overheated

adjective
heated beyond safe or desirable point
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈhitɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈhiːtɪd / (BrE)
Heated to a level beyond what is safe or desirable.

Examples

Feeling overheated indoors
He removed his jacket because he felt overheated in the crowded room.
The overheated debate caused tempers to flare among the participants.
The car engine became overheated after driving for hours without a break.
