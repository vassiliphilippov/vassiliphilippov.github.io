misandry

noun
hatred of men
Pronunciation: / mɪˈsændri / (AmE), / mɪˈsændri / (BrE)
hatred of, or prejudice against, men or boys

Examples

Misandry in society
The documentary examined the roots of misandry in certain social movements.
Some critics accused the film of promoting misandry through its negative portrayal of male characters.
Discussions about misandry often arise in debates about gender equality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: misogyny - hatred of, or prejudice against, women or girls
  • synonym: sexism - prejudice or discrimination based on sex
