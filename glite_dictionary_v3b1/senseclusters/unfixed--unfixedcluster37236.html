unfixed

adjective
not firmly fixed or attached
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪkst / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪkst / (BrE)
not firmly placed, set, or attached; not fixed in position

Examples

Unfixed shelf
Because the date was still unfixed, attendees struggled to make travel plans.
The painter left the canvas unfixed until she was satisfied with her work.
The shelves remained unfixed, so they wobbled every time someone brushed past them.
