bento

noun
Japanese lunch box
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛnˌtoʊ /; / ˈbɛntoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɛntəʊ / (BrE)
a Japanese meal served in a box, usually with rice, fish or meat, and vegetables; also, the box itself

Examples

A bento box
She packed rice and vegetables in her bento before heading to work.
The children opened their bento boxes during the school picnic.
He admired the colorful arrangement of food in his homemade bento.
She brought a homemade bento to school, filled with rice and vegetables.
At the Japanese restaurant, I ordered a salmon bento for lunch.
His mother carefully arranged the food in his bento every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lunchbox - a container for carrying a packed meal
  • synonym: packed lunch - a meal prepared at home and taken to be eaten elsewhere
