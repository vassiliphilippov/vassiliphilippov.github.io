reconnaissance plane

noun
military aircraft used for intelligence gathering
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɑnəˌsəns pleɪn / (AmE), / rɪˈkɒnɪsəns pleɪn / (BrE)
A military aircraft designed and used for obtaining information about enemy forces or positions by flying over the area of interest.

Examples

A reconnaissance plane flies
A reconnaissance plane spotted several ships approaching the coastline in the early morning.
During the Cold War, the use of reconnaissance planes was critical for gathering intelligence.
The military deployed a reconnaissance plane to survey the enemy's movements along the border.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spy plane - an aircraft used for surveillance, often at high altitude or with stealth technology
