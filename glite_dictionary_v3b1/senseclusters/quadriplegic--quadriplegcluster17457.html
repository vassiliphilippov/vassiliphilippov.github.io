quadriplegic

noun
person paralyzed in all four limbs
9.9 6.4
Pronunciation: /kwɑː.drə.ˈpliː.dʒɪk/ /; / /kwɑː.drɪ.ˈpliː.dʒɪk/ (AmE), /kwɒ.drɪ.ˈpliː.dʒɪk/ /; / /kwɒ.drə.ˈpliː.dʒɪk/ (BrE)
a person who is paralyzed in both arms and both legs, usually as a result of spinal cord injury or disease

Examples

A quadriplegic patient
After the accident, he became a quadriplegic and required round-the-clock care.
As a quadriplegic, she advocated for better accessibility and disability rights.
The hospital specialized in rehabilitation for quadriplegic patients.
