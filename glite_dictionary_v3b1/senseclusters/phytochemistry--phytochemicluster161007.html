phytochemistry

noun
plant chemistry; study of plant compounds
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪtoʊˈkɛmɪstri / (AmE), / ˌfʌɪtəʊˈkɛmɪstri / (BrE)
the branch of chemistry concerned with the chemical processes and compounds found in plants

Examples

Plant phytochemistry
A background in phytochemistry is essential for developing plant-based pharmaceuticals.
He specializes in phytochemistry, focusing on the chemical properties of medicinal herbs.
Recent advances in phytochemistry have helped scientists discover new medicinal compounds from rainforest plants.
