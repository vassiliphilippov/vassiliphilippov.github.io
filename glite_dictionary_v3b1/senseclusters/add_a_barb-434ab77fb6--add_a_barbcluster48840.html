add a barb

verb
make a cutting or hurtful remark
Pronunciation: / æd ə bɑːrb / (AmE), / ad ə bɑːb / (BrE)
to include a sharp or hurtful remark, typically to make a statement more cutting or impactful

Examples

To add a barb in debate
During the debate, he couldn't resist adding a barb to his opponent's argument.
Instead of staying neutral, he added a barb that soured the conversation.
She often adds a barb to her feedback, making it sting more than necessary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insult - speak to or treat with disrespect or scorn
  • synonym: sarcasm - witty language used to convey insults or scorn
