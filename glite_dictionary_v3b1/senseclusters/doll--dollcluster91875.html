doll

noun
informal term for a young woman
5.8
Pronunciation: / dɑl / (AmE), / dɒl / (BrE)
(informal) A term used to refer to a young woman, often in a playful, familiar, or affectionate way.

Examples

A pretty doll
Back in the 1950s, it was common to call a pretty woman a doll.
She’s a real doll, always cheerful and helpful to everyone.
Thanks for helping me out, you’re such a doll!
