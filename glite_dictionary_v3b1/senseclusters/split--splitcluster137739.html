split

noun
gymnastic or dance position with legs extended apart
Pronunciation: / splɪt / (AmE), / splɪt / (BrE)
a position in which the legs are extended in opposite directions at right angles to the trunk, typically with one leg forward and the other back, often used in gymnastics or dancing

Examples

Do the split
After weeks of practice, he finally managed to do the split.
She performed a perfect split during her gymnastics routine.
The dancer slid gracefully into a split on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: straddle - a position with the legs wide apart
