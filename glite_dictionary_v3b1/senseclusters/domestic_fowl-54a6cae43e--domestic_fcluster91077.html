domestic fowl

noun
bird kept by humans for eggs or meat
8.0
Pronunciation: / dəˈmɛstɪk faʊl / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk faʊl / (BrE)
A bird of a species that has been tamed and bred by humans, especially for eggs or meat, such as a chicken or duck.

Examples

A domestic fowl bird
Early morning sounds on the farm include the calls of various domestic fowl.
Many rural families keep domestic fowl such as chickens and ducks in their backyard.
The spread of disease among domestic fowl can have serious consequences for the poultry industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chicken - a common type of domestic fowl kept for eggs or meat
  • synonym: duck - a waterbird often kept as domestic fowl
