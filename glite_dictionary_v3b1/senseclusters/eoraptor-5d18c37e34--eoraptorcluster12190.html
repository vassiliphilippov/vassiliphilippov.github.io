Eoraptor

noun
early theropod dinosaur genus
Pronunciation: / iˈoʊˌræptər / (AmE), / iˈəʊˌræptə / (BrE)
a small, lightly built early theropod dinosaur of the genus Eoraptor, which lived in the Late Triassic period

Examples

A small Eoraptor dinosaur
Fossils of the Eoraptor have provided valuable insights into early dinosaur evolution.
Paleontologists consider Eoraptor to be one of the earliest known theropod dinosaurs.
The Eoraptor was a swift, bipedal predator that inhabited South America around 230 million years ago.
