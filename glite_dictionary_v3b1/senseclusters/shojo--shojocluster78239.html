shojo

noun
mythical Japanese creature
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊˌdʒoʊ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌdʒəʊ / (BrE)
a mythical Japanese creature with a red face, known for its fondness for alcohol and joyful dancing

Examples

Dancing shojo spirit
The legend describes a shojo who appears at festivals to dance and drink sake.
In traditional art, a shojo is often shown with a bright red face and long hair.
The villagers believed that a shojo brought good fortune to those who shared their wine.
