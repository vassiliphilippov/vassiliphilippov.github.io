humanistically

adverb
with compassion
Pronunciation: / hjuːˌmænɪˈstɪkli / (AmE), / hjuːˌmænɪˈstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that shows concern for people's or animals' welfare, emphasizing compassion and humanity

Examples

Humanistically help others
The organization responded humanistically to the disaster, providing food and shelter to those in need.
He always tries to act humanistically, considering the feelings and rights of others.
The teacher handled the conflict humanistically, listening to each student's concerns with patience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compassionately - in a way that shows sympathy and concern for others
  • antonym: cruelly - in a way that causes pain or suffering to others
