rustic

adjective
relating to the countryside or rural life
4.3 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌ.stɪk/ (AmE), /ˈrʌ.stɪk/ (BrE)
having the qualities, appearance, or simplicity typical of the countryside; simple, rough, or unsophisticated in a rural way

Examples

Rustic wooden house
His manners were somewhat rustic, but he was sincere and friendly.
They chose furniture with a rustic charm for their cottage.
We enjoyed a rustic meal by the fireplace in the old farmhouse.
Her decor style blends modern furnishings with rustic elements like reclaimed wood and vintage signs.
The old farmhouse had a charming rustic feel, with exposed wooden beams and stone floors.
They chose a rustic venue for their wedding, surrounded by fields and wildflowers.
He spoke with a rustic accent that revealed his country origins.
Her rustic manners made her seem awkward at the formal dinner.
The old farmhouse had a rustic charm that made city dwellers feel out of place.
