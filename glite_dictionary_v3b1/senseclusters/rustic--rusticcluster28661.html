rustic

adjective
relating to the countryside or rural life
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌs.tɪk /; / ˈrʌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈrʌs.tɪk /; / ˈrʌstɪk / (BrE)
having the qualities, appearance, or simplicity typical of the countryside; simple, rough, or unsophisticated in a rural way

Examples

Her rustic manners made her seem awkward at the formal dinner.
The old farmhouse had a rustic charm that made city dwellers feel out of place.
