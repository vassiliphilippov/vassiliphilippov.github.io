fresh coffee

noun
newly brewed coffee
1.0
Pronunciation: / frɛʃ ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / frɛʃ ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
Coffee that has been freshly brewed and has not been sitting for long, often characterized by a strong aroma and full flavor.

Examples

Fresh coffee aroma
Nothing beats the taste of fresh coffee on a cold winter day.
She always serves guests a pot of fresh coffee with breakfast.
The smell of fresh coffee woke me up instantly this morning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stale coffee - coffee that has been sitting for a long time and lost its flavor
