transportive

adjective
emotionally moving
7.9
Pronunciation: / trænˈspɔrtɪv / (AmE), / trænˈspɔːtɪv / (BrE)
causing someone to feel as if they have been carried to another place or state, especially emotionally or mentally

Examples

Transportive music experience
The artist's transportive paintings made viewers feel as if they were in another world.
Her storytelling was so transportive that the audience forgot they were in a theater.
Listening to the transportive melody, he imagined himself walking through a distant land.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evocative - bringing strong images, memories, or feelings to mind
  • synonym: immersive - deeply involving or absorbing the senses or mind
