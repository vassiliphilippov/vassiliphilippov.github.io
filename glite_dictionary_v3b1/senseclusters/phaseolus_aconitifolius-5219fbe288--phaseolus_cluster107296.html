Phaseolus aconitifolius

noun
East Indian legume plant (moth bean)
Pronunciation: / fəˈsiː.oʊ.ləs əˌkɒnɪˈtɪfəliəs / (AmE), / fəˈsiː.li.əs əˌkɒn.ɪ.tɪˈfəʊ.li.əs / (BrE)
an East Indian legume plant with hairy leaves and small yellow flowers, cultivated for food, forage, and as a soil conditioner, especially in India; also known as moth bean
