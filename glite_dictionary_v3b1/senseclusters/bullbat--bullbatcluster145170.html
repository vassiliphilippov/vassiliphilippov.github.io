bullbat

noun
nocturnal North American bird (nightjar)
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌbæt / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌbæt / (BrE)
A mainly nocturnal North American bird in the nightjar family, also known as the common nighthawk.

Examples

The bullbat bird
At dusk, the bullbat swooped low over the field, catching insects in mid-air.
Farmers often welcomed the bullbat for its appetite for crop pests.
We watched a lone bullbat as it darted under the evening sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nighthawk - a nocturnal North American bird with a distinctive call and erratic flight
  • synonym: nightjar - a nocturnal bird of the family Caprimulgidae
