First Epistle of Paul the Apostle to Timothy

noun
New Testament letter from Paul to Timothy
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝst ɪˈpɪsəl əv ˈpɑl ði əˈpɑsəl tu ˈtɪməθi / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ɪˈpɪsəl əv ˈpɔːl ði əˈpɒsl̩ tuː ˈtɪməθi / (BrE)
the first of two letters in the New Testament traditionally attributed to Saint Paul and addressed to Timothy, offering guidance on pastoral matters and church leadership
