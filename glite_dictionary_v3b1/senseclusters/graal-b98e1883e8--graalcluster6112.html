Graal

noun
old term for Holy Grail
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪl / (AmE), / ɡreɪl / (BrE)
an old or literary term for the Holy Grail, the legendary cup sought by knights in medieval legend

Examples

The Holy Graal
In the old tales, the knights embarked on a quest for the Graal.
Legends describe the Graal as a vessel of great spiritual power.
The search for the Graal inspired many stories in medieval literature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Holy Grail - the legendary cup said to have been used by Jesus at the Last Supper
