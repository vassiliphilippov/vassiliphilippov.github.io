top

noun
upper-body garment, often for women
Pronunciation: / tɑp / (AmE), / tɒp / (BrE)
a piece of clothing that covers the upper part of the body, typically from the shoulders to the waist or hips, worn especially by women

Examples

A new top
He accidentally spilled coffee on his favorite top.
She bought a new top to wear to the party.
This top is made of cotton and is perfect for summer weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blouse - a woman's upper garment resembling a shirt
  • synonym: shirt - a garment for the upper body, typically with sleeves and a collar
