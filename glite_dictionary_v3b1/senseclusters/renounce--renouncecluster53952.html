Synonyms

  • abjure
    9.9 0.1/m
    abjure is a formal refusal of a belief, often given under oath, while renounce means formally giving up a belief or claim.
  • relinquish
    7.2 3/m
    relinquish is choosing to give up a right, possession, or role voluntarily, while renounce feels more formal and public.
  • resign
    3.7 20/m
    resign is when someone officially leaves a job or office by notice, while renounce can mean formally abandoning many roles or beliefs.
  • release
    2.6 3/m
    release is giving up ownership or control of something, often legal or physical, while renounce is to formally abandon rights or beliefs.
  • repudiate
    8.2 2/m
    repudiate is formally rejecting or denying association or responsibility, while renounce is formally giving up a belief, claim, or position.
