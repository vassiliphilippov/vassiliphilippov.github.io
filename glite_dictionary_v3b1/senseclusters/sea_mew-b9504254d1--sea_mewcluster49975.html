sea mew

noun
another name for the common gull
Pronunciation: / si mjuː / (AmE), / siː mjuː / (BrE)
a name for the common gull found in Eurasia and northeastern North America

Examples

A sea mew bird
A solitary sea mew perched on the rocky outcrop, calling out to its flock.
The children tried to feed the sea mew scraps of bread at the harbor.
We watched the sea mew gliding above the crashing waves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: common gull - a medium-sized gull (Larus canus) found in Eurasia and North America
  • synonym: seagull - a general term for various species of gulls, especially those found on coasts
