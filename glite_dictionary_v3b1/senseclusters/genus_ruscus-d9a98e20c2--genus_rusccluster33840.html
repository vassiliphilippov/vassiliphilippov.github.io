genus Ruscus

noun
genus of European evergreen shrubs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈrʌs.kəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈrʌs.kəs / (BrE)
A genus of European evergreen shrubs, sometimes classified in the family Asparagaceae, known for its small, tough leaves and red berries.
