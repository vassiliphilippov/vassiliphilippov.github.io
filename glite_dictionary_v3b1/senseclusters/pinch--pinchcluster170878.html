pinch

verb
cut the tip off a plant
Pronunciation: / pɪntʃ / (AmE), / pɪntʃ / (BrE)
to cut off the tip or top of a plant, especially to encourage bushier growth or more flowers

Examples

To pinch a shoot
Gardeners often pinch young shoots to encourage more flowers.
He carefully pinched the tops of the tomato plants to promote healthier growth.
If you pinch the tips of the basil plants, they will grow bushier.

  • synonym: top - to cut off the top part of a plant
