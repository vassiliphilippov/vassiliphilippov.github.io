kief

noun
powdered cannabis resin
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / kiÀêf / (AmE), / kiÀêf / (BrE)
A powder made from the resinous trichomes of cannabis, traditionally collected and used as a concentrated form of cannabis, especially in North Africa.

Examples

Powdered kief
Legislation about kief varies greatly from country to country.
Some people sprinkle kief over their joints to increase potency.
The vendor showed us a small container filled with kief collected from the cannabis plants.

Synonyms

  • kif
    9.9 0.14/m
    kif is a North African cannabis product often smoked or made into concentrates, while kief is the fine powder of resin trichomes used as concentrated cannabis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hashish - a drug made from the resin of cannabis plants, often formed into blocks or cakes
  • synonym: marijuana - dried leaves and flowers of the cannabis plant, used as a drug
