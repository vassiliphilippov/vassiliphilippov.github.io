wood ant

noun
European reddish-brown woodland ant
Pronunciation: / wʊd ænt / (AmE), / wʊd ænt / (BrE)
a reddish-brown European ant that typically builds anthills in woodland areas

Examples

A wood ant colony
A wood ant colony can contain hundreds of thousands of worker ants.
Scientists observed a wood ant nest deep in the forest.
The wood ant is known for constructing large, dome-shaped anthills from plant material.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ant - a small insect living in complex social colonies
