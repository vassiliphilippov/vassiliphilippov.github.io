tannoy

noun
public loudspeaker system
Pronunciation: / ˈtænɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈtænɔɪ / (BrE)
a loudspeaker or public address system used to make announcements in public places such as stations, airports, or shops

Examples

The tannoy system
An important message was broadcast on the tannoy in the supermarket.
Could someone call for assistance using the tannoy?
The train's delay was announced over the tannoy at the station.
Passengers were asked over the tannoy to proceed to gate 23.
The tannoy announced the next train departure time.
There was a crackling noise from the tannoy before the announcement began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: public address system - an electronic amplification system used to address a large audience
