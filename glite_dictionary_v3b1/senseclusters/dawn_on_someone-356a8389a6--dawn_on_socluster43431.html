dawn on someone

verb
become clear to someone; realize
Pronunciation: / dɔn ɑn ˈsʌmwʌn / (AmE), / dɔːn ɒn ˈsʌmwʌn / (BrE)
(of an idea or realization) to become clear or apparent to someone, often suddenly.

Examples

It dawned on me
It dawned on me that I had left my keys at home only after reaching the office.
It suddenly dawned on him that he had forgotten his wedding anniversary.
The solution dawned on her while she was taking a shower.

Related Terms

  • synonym: occur to someone - to (suddenly) come into someone's mind
  • synonym: realize - to become aware of something clearly
