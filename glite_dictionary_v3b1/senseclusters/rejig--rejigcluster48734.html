rejig

verb
rearrange or re-equip a factory or plant
Pronunciation: / riˈdʒɪɡ / (AmE), / riˈdʒɪɡ / (BrE)
to rearrange or re-equip the machinery or processes of a factory or plant, often to improve or update operations

Examples

To rejig factory machines
After the merger, they had to rejig the entire manufacturing plant to produce the new product line.
Engineers worked through the night to rejig the production line in response to changing customer demands.
The company plans to rejig its factory layout in order to boost efficiency.
