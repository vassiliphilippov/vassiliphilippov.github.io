stalked

adjective
having or growing on a stalk
9.9
Pronunciation: / stɔkt / (AmE), / stɔːkt / (BrE)
(of a plant or organism) having or growing on a stalk or peduncle.

Examples

A stalked plant
A stalked barnacle is attached to the surface by a fleshy stalk.
Many flowers in this genus are stalked, standing well above the leaves.
The microscope revealed small, stalked glands on the leaf underside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sessile - (of a plant or organism) attached directly by its base without a stalk
