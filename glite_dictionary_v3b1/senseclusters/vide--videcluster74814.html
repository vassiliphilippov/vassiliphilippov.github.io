vide

verb
to refer elsewhere
9.9
Pronunciation: /vaɪd/ /; / /ˈviː.deɪ/ /; / /ˈvɪ.deɪ/ (AmE), /vaɪd/ /; / /ˈviː.deɪ/ /; / /ˈvɪ.deɪ/ (BrE)
to refer the reader to another place in the text for more information, used in formal or academic writing.

Examples

The document states, 'vide section 5 for further clarification on this rule.'
In legal texts, authors often use vide to direct readers to related cases.
