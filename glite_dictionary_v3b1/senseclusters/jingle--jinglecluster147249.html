Synonyms

    7.5 0.01/m

  • tink
    9.5 0.02/m
    tink makes a short high-pitched bell-like sound, while jingle is a gentler metallic ring from small objects.
  • ching
    8.8 0.01/m

  • clink
    5.1 1/m
    clink makes a short sharp metallic or glass sound from two pieces striking, while jingle is a softer, ringing metal sound.
