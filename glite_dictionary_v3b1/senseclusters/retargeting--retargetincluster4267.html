retargeting

noun
animation data transfer
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈtɑːrɡətɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈtɑːɡɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of transferring or adapting motion-capture data or animations from one character skeleton to another so movements appear natural

Examples

Retargeting animation data
The animation team used retargeting to apply the dancer's moves to a digital character.
Advances in retargeting have made animated films look more realistic than ever before.
By using retargeting, developers can reuse motion data for different characters in a video game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animation - the process of creating moving images
  • synonym: motion capture - the recording of movement for digital animation
