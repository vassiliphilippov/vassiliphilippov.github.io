low

noun
lowest point or level
2.8
Pronunciation: / loʊ / (AmE), / ləʊ / (BrE)
a point or level that is lower than others, especially the lowest that something has reached

Examples

A new low
After weeks of steady decline, the company's profits hit a new low.
Many people feel at their emotional low during the dark winter months.
The temperature reached its low for the month last night, dropping below freezing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: high - a point or level that is higher than others, especially the highest that something has reached
