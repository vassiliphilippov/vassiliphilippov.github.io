shimmeringly

adverb
with flickering light
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪmərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈʃɪmərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shines with a flickering or wavering light

Examples

Stars shine shimmeringly
The stars shone shimmeringly in the clear night sky above the mountains.
Her dress sparkled shimmeringly under the ballroom lights as she danced.
The surface of the ocean appeared shimmeringly bright in the midday sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gleamingly - in a way that shines brightly and steadily
