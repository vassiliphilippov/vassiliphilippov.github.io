haft

verb
to attach a handle
Pronunciation: / hæft / (AmE), / hɑːft / (BrE)
to attach a handle to a blade, tool, or weapon

Examples

To haft a blade
The craftsman carefully hafted the axe with a strong piece of oak.
After forging the blade, she hafted it to complete the tool.
It takes skill to properly haft a knife so it is balanced and safe to use.
The blacksmith spent hours hafting new axes for the villagers.
Careful hafting ensures that the blade will not come loose during use.
Archaeologists found evidence of ancient tools hafted with plant fibers and resin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detach - to remove or separate something from what it is attached to
  • synonym: handle - to put a handle on something
  • synonym: mount - to fix or attach something firmly in place
  • antonym: unhandle - to remove a handle from something
