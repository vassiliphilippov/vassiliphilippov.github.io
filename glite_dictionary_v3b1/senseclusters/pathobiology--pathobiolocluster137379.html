pathobiology

noun
study of disease biology
Pronunciation: / ˌpæθoʊbaɪˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌpæθəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of biology that studies the biological mechanisms and processes underlying diseases

Examples

Pathobiology of diseases
She decided to specialize in pathobiology to better understand how diseases develop at the cellular level.
The university offers advanced courses in pathobiology for students interested in medical research.
Recent discoveries in pathobiology have led to new treatments for chronic illnesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pathology - the study of the causes and effects of diseases
