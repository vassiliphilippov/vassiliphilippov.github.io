lithiasis

noun
formation of stones in the body
9.9
Pronunciation: / lɪˈθaɪəsɪs / (AmE), / lɪˈθaɪəsɪs / (BrE)
the formation of stones (calculi) in an internal organ, such as the kidney or gallbladder

Examples

Lithiasis in kidney
Cholecystic lithiasis can sometimes require surgical intervention if gallstones block the bile duct.
Proper hydration may help reduce the risk of developing renal lithiasis.
The patient was diagnosed with lithiasis after experiencing severe abdominal pain.
