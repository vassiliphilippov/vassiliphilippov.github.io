civvy

noun
slang for civilian
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvi / (AmE), / ˈsɪvi / (BrE)
a slang term for a civilian, especially someone who is not in the military

Examples

A civvy among soldiers
After years in the navy, he struggled to fit in as a civvy.
The veterans joked about how easy life was for a civvy.
She left the army and became a civvy again, starting a new job in the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: soldier - a person who serves in the military
