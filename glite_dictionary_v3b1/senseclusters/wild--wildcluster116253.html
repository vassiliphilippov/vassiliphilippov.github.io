wild

adjective
intensely enthusiastic or obsessed
Pronunciation: / waɪld / (AmE), / waɪld / (BrE)
intensely enthusiastic about or strongly preoccupied with someone or something

Examples

Wild about music
He's absolutely wild about old jazz records and spends hours collecting them.
She went wild for the artist's new painting and couldn't stop talking about it.
The children are wild about their new puppy and play with it all day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crazy - intensely enthusiastic or obsessed (informal)
  • synonym: mad - very enthusiastic about something (British informal)
