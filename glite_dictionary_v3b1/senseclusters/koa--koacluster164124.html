koa

noun
Hawaiian hardwood tree
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊ.ə / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.ə / (BrE)
a large tree native to Hawaii, known for its strong, attractive wood used in furniture and crafts

Examples

Koa wood furniture
The craftsman carved a beautiful bowl from koa wood.
Many traditional Hawaiian canoes were made using strong koa trees.
The rich color of koa makes it popular for making musical instruments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mahogany - a tropical hardwood tree valued for its reddish-brown timber
  • antonym: pine - a common softwood tree used for timber
