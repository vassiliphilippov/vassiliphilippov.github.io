Parkinson's syndrome

noun
degenerative disorder causing tremors and impaired movement
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑːr.kɪn.sənz ˈsɪn.droʊm/ (AmE), /ˈpɑː.kɪn.sənz ˈsɪn.drəʊm/ (BrE)
A degenerative disorder of the central nervous system, marked by tremors and impaired muscular coordination.

Examples

Parkinson's syndrome symptoms
Early treatment can help manage the symptoms of Parkinson's syndrome and improve quality of life.
Many people with Parkinson's syndrome experience difficulty with balance and coordination.
The neurologist diagnosed her with Parkinson's syndrome after observing her muscle rigidity and tremors.
