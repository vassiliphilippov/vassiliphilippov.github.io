Danio

proper noun
aquarium fish genus
Pronunciation: / ˈdæniˌoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdæniəʊ / (BrE)
a genus of small, brightly colored freshwater fish often kept in home aquariums

Examples

Danio fish tank
Many beginner aquarists choose Danio species because they are hardy and easy to care for.
The aquarium was filled with colorful Danio swimming among the plants.
Researchers often use Danio in scientific studies due to their transparent embryos.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minnow - a small freshwater fish of the family Cyprinidae
  • synonym: zebrafish - a small striped freshwater fish, Danio rerio, popular in aquariums
