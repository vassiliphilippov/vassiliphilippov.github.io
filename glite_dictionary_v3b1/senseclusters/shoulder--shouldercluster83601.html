shoulder

noun
strip of land beside a road
9.3 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈʃoʊl.dər/ (AmE), /ˈʃəʊl.də/ (BrE)
a narrow strip of land, often unpaved, along the side of a road, used especially in emergencies or for stopping vehicles.
