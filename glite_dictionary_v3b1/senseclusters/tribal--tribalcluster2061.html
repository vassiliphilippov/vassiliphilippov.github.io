tribal

adjective
relating to a tribe or tribes
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪ.bəl / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ.bəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a tribe or tribes, especially in terms of customs, traditions, or social organization.

Examples

Tribal customs
Several tribal leaders attended the conference to discuss community issues.
The region is known for its rich tribal heritage and traditional dances.
Their artwork features patterns inspired by tribal symbols and mythology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tribe - a social group consisting of people of the same race, beliefs, or customs
