exhaustion

noun
state of being used up
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɔs.tʃən / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːs.tʃən / (BrE)
the act of using something completely so that nothing remains

Examples

Exhaustion of resources
After hours of intense debate, there was a sense of exhaustion in the room.
Continuous use can lead to the exhaustion of the device's battery.
The exhaustion of natural resources is a growing global concern.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depletion - reduction in the number or quantity of something
  • antonym: repletion - the act of being filled or replenished
