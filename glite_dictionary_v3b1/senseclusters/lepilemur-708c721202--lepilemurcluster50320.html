Lepilemur

noun

a small, nocturnal lemur of the genus Lepilemur, known as the sportive lemur, native to Madagascar

Examples

A Lepilemur in trees
The Lepilemur spends most of its life in the trees, feeding on leaves at night.
Researchers observed a Lepilemur leaping between branches in the Madagascan forest.
Unlike other lemurs, the Lepilemur is usually seen alone rather than in groups.
