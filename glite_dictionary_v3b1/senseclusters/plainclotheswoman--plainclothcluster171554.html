plainclotheswoman

noun
female detective in civilian clothes
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪnˌkloʊðzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈpleɪnˌkləʊðzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female police officer or detective who wears ordinary clothes instead of a uniform while on duty
