hipped roof

noun
roof with all sides sloping down
6.8 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / h…™pt ruÀêf / (AmE), / h…™pt ruÀêf / (BrE)
A roof that slopes downwards on all four sides, with both the ends and sides sloping toward the walls.

Examples

A hipped roof design
Many traditional houses feature a hipped roof to better withstand strong winds.
The architect recommended installing a hipped roof for its durability and classic look.
The cottage was topped with a red-tiled hipped roof that gave it a distinctive appearance.
