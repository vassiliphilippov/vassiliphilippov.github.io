meringue

noun
sweet topping/dessert of egg whites and sugar
1.7 5.5
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈræŋ/ /; / /mə.ˈrɪŋ/ (AmE), /mə.ˈræŋ/ /; / /mə.ˈrɪŋ/ (BrE)
A sweet, light topping or dessert made from beaten egg whites and sugar, often used on pies or baked into crisp shells.

Examples

Sweet meringue topping
She topped the lemon pie with a generous layer of meringue.
The meringue was light, airy, and perfectly sweet.
You need to beat the egg whites until they form stiff peaks for the meringue.
