railway man

noun
male railway employee
3.6 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈreɪl.weɪ mæn/ (AmE), /ˈreɪl.weɪ mæn/ (BrE)
A man who works for a railway company, especially as an employee involved in the operation or maintenance of trains and tracks.

Examples

An experienced railway man
After thirty years as a railway man, he retired with fond memories of his colleagues.
His father was a dedicated railway man who worked long hours on the tracks.
The railway man greeted passengers as the train arrived at the station.
