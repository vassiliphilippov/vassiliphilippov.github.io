kingsnake

noun
nonvenomous North American constrictor snake
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋˌsneɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋˌsneɪk / (BrE)
A nonvenomous North American constrictor snake that often feeds on other snakes and small mammals.

Examples

A kingsnake pet
Many people keep the kingsnake as a pet because it helps control rodent and snake populations.
The kingsnake is well known for its immunity to the venom of other snakes.
We saw a brightly colored kingsnake slithering through the grass in the national park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: milk snake - a species of North American kingsnake with distinctive coloring
