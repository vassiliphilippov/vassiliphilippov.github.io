drillmaster

noun
drilling supervisor
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈdrɪl.mæ.stər/ /; / /ˈdrɪl.mə.stər/ (AmE), /ˈdrɪl.mɑː.stə/ (BrE)

The company hired an experienced drillmaster to oversee the mineral exploration project.
