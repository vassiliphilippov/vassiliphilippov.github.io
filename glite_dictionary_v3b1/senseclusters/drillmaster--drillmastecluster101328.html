drillmaster

noun
drilling supervisor
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪlˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈdrɪlˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person in charge of supervising or operating mechanical drilling work, especially in fields like oil, water, or mineral extraction

Examples

Oil drillmaster
The drillmaster inspected the rig carefully before the team started drilling the new well.
As a drillmaster, she ensured all safety procedures were followed on the drilling site.
The company hired an experienced drillmaster to oversee the mineral exploration project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foreman - a person in charge of a group of workers
  • synonym: operator - a person who controls machinery or equipment
