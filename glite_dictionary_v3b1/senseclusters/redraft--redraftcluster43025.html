redraft

noun
draft for dishonored draft plus costs
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈdræft / (AmE), / ˌriːˈdrɑːft / (BrE)
a draft issued for the sum of a previously dishonored draft plus any additional charges or costs incurred for issuing it again

Examples

Bank redraft fee
After the original check bounced, a redraft was drawn for the total amount owing.
International transactions sometimes require a redraft if the initial payment is dishonored.
The bank issued a redraft to recover the unpaid sum and the additional fees.
