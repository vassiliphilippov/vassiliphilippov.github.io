Synonyms

  • surroundings
    2.5 11/m
    surroundings are the things and area immediately around a person or place, while environment includes those things plus wider conditions.
  • conditions
    1.4 0.08/m
    conditions are the factors that affect how people live or things work, while environment means the whole place and its surroundings.
  • environs
    7.1 0.00/m
