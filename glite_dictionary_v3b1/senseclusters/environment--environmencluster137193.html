environment

noun
surrounding conditions or influences
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈvaɪərənmənt /; / ɪnˈvʌɪərənmənt / (BrE)
the surroundings or conditions that affect or surround a person, animal, or thing

Examples

The environment around us
A supportive environment can help students learn and succeed.
He longed for the peaceful environment of his home after a busy day at work.
The restaurant creates a relaxing environment for its diners.
Children need a safe and nurturing environment to learn and grow.
The company is trying to create a positive work environment for its employees.
The rainforest provides a unique environment for thousands of plant and animal species.
