precalculate

verb
to calculate in advance
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈkælkjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈkælkjʊleɪt / (BrE)

Examples

Engineers often precalculate values to avoid delays during real-time operations.
You should precalculate the total cost before starting the project.
