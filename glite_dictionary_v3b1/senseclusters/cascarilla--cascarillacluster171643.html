cascarilla

noun
West Indian aromatic shrub
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌkæskəˈriːjə / (AmE), / ˌkæskəˈrɪlə / (BrE)
A West Indian shrub (Croton eluteria) with aromatic bark, used medicinally and for flavoring.

Examples

The cascarilla shrub
Herbalists value cascarilla for its medicinal properties and pleasant aroma.
The bark of cascarilla is often used to add a subtle flavor to liquors.
The plantation cultivated cascarilla for export to European perfumeries.
