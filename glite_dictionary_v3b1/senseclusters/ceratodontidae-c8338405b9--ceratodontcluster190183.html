Ceratodontidae

noun
family of lungfishes with hornlike tooth ridges
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛr.ə.toʊˈdɒn.tɪˌdi /; / sɛˌræt̬əˈdɒn.tɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˌsɛr.ə.təʊˈdɒn.tɪˌdiː /; / sɛˌræt.əˈdɒn.tɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of lungfishes with hornlike ridges on their teeth, found in freshwater habitats (including both ancient and living species)
