background

noun
scenery at back of stage
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈbækɡraʊnd / (BrE)
the area or scenery behind the main subject in a picture, on a stage, or on a computer screen

  • synonym: wallpaper - an image or pattern used as the background of a computer screen
