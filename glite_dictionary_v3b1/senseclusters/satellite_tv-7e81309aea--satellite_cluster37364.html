satellite TV

noun
television system using satellites for broadcasting
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsæt.ə.laɪt tiː.ˈviː/ /; / /ˈsæt.ə.laɪt ˈtiː.viː/ (AmE), /ˈsæt.ə.laɪt tiː.ˈviː/ /; / /ˈsæt.ə.laɪt ˈtiː.viː/ (BrE)
