sarcopenia

noun
age-related muscle loss
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑːrkoʊˈpiːniə / (AmE), / ˌsɑːkəʊˈpiːniə / (BrE)
a medical condition characterized by the loss of muscle mass and strength, especially as a result of aging

Examples

Sarcopenia muscle loss
Older adults are at higher risk of developing sarcopenia if they do not stay physically active.
Doctors recommend resistance training to help prevent sarcopenia in aging populations.
A balanced diet rich in protein can slow the progression of sarcopenia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cachexia - weakness and wasting of the body due to severe illness
  • synonym: muscle atrophy - the wasting or loss of muscle tissue
