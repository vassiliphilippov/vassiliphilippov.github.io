hand-held computer

noun
portable computer small enough to hold or carry
Pronunciation: / ˌhændˈhɛld kəmˈpjuːtər / (AmE), / ˌhændˈhɛld kəmˈpjuːtə / (BrE)
a small, portable computer that can be held in one hand and carried easily, typically battery-powered and often small enough to fit in a pocket

Examples

A hand-held computer device
He pulled a hand-held computer from his pocket to check his schedule.
Many field researchers rely on a hand-held computer for data collection in remote areas.
The new hand-held computer model boasts a longer battery life and a touch screen interface.

Related Terms

  • antonym: desktop computer - a personal computer designed to fit on or under a desk
  • synonym: personal digital assistant - an early type of hand-held computer for personal organization
