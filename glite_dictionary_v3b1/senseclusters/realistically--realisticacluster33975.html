realistically

adverb
in a realistic manner
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌrɪəˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows an awareness of the actual facts or situation, avoiding wishful thinking or illusion.

Examples

Realistically speaking
Realistically, not everyone will agree with the new policy.
Realistically, we can't finish the project by next week.
She approached her goals realistically, knowing that success would take time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unrealistically - in a way that is not realistic; idealized or impractical
