flasher

noun
device for turning lights on and off automatically
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈflæʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈflæʃə / (BrE)
an electrical device that automatically turns a lamp on and off, often used in advertising displays or signal lights

Examples

A sign flasher
A malfunction in the flasher caused the sign to stay lit instead of blinking.
Electricians replaced the broken flasher in the traffic signal.
The neon flasher in the store window caught everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blinker - a device that flashes lights on and off, especially in automobiles
