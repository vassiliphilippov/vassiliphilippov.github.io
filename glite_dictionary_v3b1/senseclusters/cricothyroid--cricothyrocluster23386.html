cricothyroid

adjective
neck cartilage area
Pronunciation: / ˌkraɪkoʊˈθaɪrɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌkraɪkəʊˈθaɪrɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to both the cricoid and thyroid cartilages in the neck

Examples

Cricothyroid membrane incision
The surgeon made an incision at the cricothyroid membrane to secure the airway.
Damage to the cricothyroid muscle can affect a person's ability to change pitch while speaking.
The cricothyroid ligament is an important landmark in emergency procedures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laryngeal - relating to the larynx or voice box
