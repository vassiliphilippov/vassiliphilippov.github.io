abominate

verb
to intensely dislike or detest
5.1 7.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈbɑː.mɪ.neɪt/ /; / /ə.ˈbɑː.mə.neɪt/ (AmE), /ə.ˈbɒm.ɪ.neɪt/ /; / /ə.ˈbɒm.ə.neɪt/ (BrE)
to feel intense hatred or loathing for something; to detest strongly
