riprap

noun
erosion control stones
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪpˌræp / (AmE), / ˈrɪpˌræp / (BrE)
a layer of large stones or broken rock placed along shorelines, riverbanks, or slopes to prevent erosion
