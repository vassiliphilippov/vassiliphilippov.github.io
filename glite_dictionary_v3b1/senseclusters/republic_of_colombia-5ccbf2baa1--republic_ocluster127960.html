Republic of Colombia

proper noun
country in northwestern South America
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv kəˈlʌmbiə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv kəˈlɒmbiə / (BrE)
The official name of Colombia, a country in northwestern South America with coastlines on the Pacific Ocean and the Caribbean Sea, independent from Spain since 1821.
