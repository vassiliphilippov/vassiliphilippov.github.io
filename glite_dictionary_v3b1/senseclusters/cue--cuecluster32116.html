cue

noun
stick for striking ball in billiards or pool
4.4 2.02/m
Pronunciation: / kjuÀê / (AmE), / kjuÀê / (BrE)
a long, tapering rod used to strike the cue ball in billiards, pool, or snooker

Examples

A cue stick
A good cue is essential for playing professional billiards.
He chalked his cue before taking the shot.
She bought a new cue with a smooth, polished finish.
