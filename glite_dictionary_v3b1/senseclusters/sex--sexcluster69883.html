sex

noun
biological category (male or female)
Pronunciation: / sɛks / (AmE), / sɛks / (BrE)
either of the two main categories (male or female) into which living things are divided according to their reproductive functions

Examples

Sex of the baby
Knowing the sex of the animal is important for breeding programs.
Some bird species cannot be easily distinguished by sex based on appearance alone.
The doctor informed them of the baby's sex during the ultrasound.
Some animals can change their sex in response to environmental factors.
The application form asks you to indicate your sex as either male or female.
The company was praised for promoting equality between the sexes.
