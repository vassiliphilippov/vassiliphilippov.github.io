transit line

noun
public transportation route or system
Pronunciation: / ˈtræn.sɪt laɪn / (AmE), / ˈtræn.zɪt laɪn / (BrE)
a route or system along which public transportation vehicles regularly operate

Examples

Main transit line
During rush hour, the main transit line is often crowded with commuters.
Plans are underway to expand the existing transit line to serve more neighborhoods.
The city recently opened a new transit line to connect the suburbs with downtown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: train line - a railway route for passenger trains
