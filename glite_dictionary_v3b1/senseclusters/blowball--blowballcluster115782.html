blowball

noun
dandelion seed head
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈbləʊˌbɔːl / (BrE)
the round, fluffy seed head of a dandelion or similar plant, easily dispersed by the wind

Examples

A blowball seed
Children love to pick blowballs and watch the seeds float away on the breeze.
In the meadow, countless blowballs stood where dandelions had flowered just weeks before.
She made a wish and gently blew on the blowball, scattering its seeds into the air.
