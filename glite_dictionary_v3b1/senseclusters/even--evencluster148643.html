even

adverb
used to emphasize contrast or surprise
Pronunciation: / ˈivən / (AmE), / ˈiːvən / (BrE)
used to emphasize something surprising, extreme, or unexpected

Examples

Even he smiled
He smiled even as he delivered the bad news.
She finished the race even though she was exhausted.
The party went on even with the heavy rain outside.
Do you even know how to fix this problem?
Nobody believed him, not even his closest friends.
She was so tired she couldn't even walk upstairs.
