isolator

noun
person or device kept apart
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈaɪsəleɪtə / (BrE)
a person or device that is kept apart from others to prevent the spread or reception of infection or disease

Examples

Medical isolator
The hospital used a special isolator to keep the patient separated from others.
After the outbreak, each suspected case was placed in an isolator for observation.
Doctors checked the isolator daily to monitor the patient's recovery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carrier - a person or animal that transmits a disease
  • synonym: quarantine - a period or place of isolation to prevent disease spread
