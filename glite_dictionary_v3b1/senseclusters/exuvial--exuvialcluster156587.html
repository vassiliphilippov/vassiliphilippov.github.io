exuvial

adjective
relating to cast-off animal skins
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪgˈzuːviəl / (AmE), / ɪgˈzjuːviəl / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of the cast-off skins or cuticles of animals, especially invertebrates after molting

Examples

Exuvial material
Scientists collected exuvial material to analyze for environmental toxins.
The exuvial stage provides important clues about an insect's growth.
The ground was littered with exuvial remains after the cicadas had molted.
