compulsive

noun
person with uncontrollable habits
7.3
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpʌlsɪv / (AmE), / kəmˈpʌlsɪv / (BrE)
a person who is unable to control their repeated actions or habits, often to an unhealthy degree

Examples

A compulsive shopper
Many compulsives struggle to change their daily routines even when they want to.
Support groups can help compulsives manage their repetitive behaviors.
Some compulsives develop rituals that interfere with their work or relationships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addict - a person who cannot stop doing or using something, especially a harmful substance or activity
  • antonym: impulsive - a person who acts without thinking or planning
