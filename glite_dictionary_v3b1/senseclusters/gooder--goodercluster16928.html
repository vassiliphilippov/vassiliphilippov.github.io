gooder

noun
person who helps others
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʊdər / (AmE), / ˈɡʊdə / (BrE)
an informal or dialectal term for a person who does good deeds or helps others

Examples

A real gooder helps
After volunteering at the animal shelter all summer, people started calling him a gooder in the community.
She became known as a gooder for always helping her neighbors with their groceries.
The teacher praised the gooder in class who organized a fundraiser for charity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benefactor - someone who gives help or money to others
  • synonym: do-gooder - a person who tries to help others, sometimes in a self-righteous way
