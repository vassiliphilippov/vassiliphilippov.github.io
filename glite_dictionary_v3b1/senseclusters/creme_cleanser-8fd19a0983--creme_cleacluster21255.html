creme cleanser

noun
thick liquid skincare cleanser
Pronunciation: / krɛm ˈklɛnzɚ / (AmE), / krɛm ˈklɛnzə / (BrE)
A thick, lotion-like liquid used for cleansing the skin, particularly the face.

Examples

A gentle creme cleanser
A gentle creme cleanser can help maintain the skin's natural moisture balance.
My dermatologist suggested using a creme cleanser for my sensitive skin.
Unlike foaming cleansers, a creme cleanser hydrates while removing makeup.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleanser - a substance for cleaning the skin
  • synonym: face wash - a liquid or gel used to clean the face
