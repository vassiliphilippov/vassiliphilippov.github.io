chloroplatinate

noun
salt of chloroplatinic acid
Pronunciation: / ˌklɔːroʊˈplætɪneɪt / (AmE), / ˌklɔːrəˈplætɪneɪt / (BrE)
a salt formed from chloroplatinic acid, containing the complex chloroplatinate anion

Examples

A chloroplatinate salt
A chloroplatinate is often used to test the presence of potassium ions in solution.
Ammonium chloroplatinate is a yellow, crystalline compound.
The laboratory synthesized several types of chloroplatinate for analysis.
