Iris virginica

noun
a species of iris native to eastern US
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrɪs vərˈdʒɪnɪkə / (AmE), / ˈaɪrɪs vəˈdʒɪnɪkə / (BrE)
A species of iris with blue or violet flowers, native to the eastern United States, also known as the Southern blue flag.
