shot

noun
metal ball for shot put event
Pronunciation: / ʃɑt / (AmE), / ʃɒt / (BrE)
an attempt to score or hit something in a sport by throwing, striking, or kicking; or the object used in shot put

Examples

The weight of the shot varies between men's and women's events.

After taking a shot to the ribs, he struggled to keep fighting.
She staggered backward after receiving a sharp shot to the stomach.
The boxer delivered a powerful shot to his opponent's jaw in the final round.
