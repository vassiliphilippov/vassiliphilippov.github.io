Diospyros ebenum

noun
species of ebony tree; valuable for its dark wood
9.9
Pronunciation: / daɪˌɑːspɪˌrɒs ˈɛbɪnəm / (AmE), / dɪˌɒspɪˌrɒs ˈiːbɪnəm / (BrE)
A tropical tree native to southern Asia, valued for its hard, dark-colored heartwood (ebony) used in fine cabinetmaking and woodwork.

Examples

Diospyros ebenum tree
Conservationists are concerned about the overharvesting of Diospyros ebenum in its native regions.
Many antique cabinets from India are crafted from Diospyros ebenum due to its strength and beautiful dark color.
The wood of Diospyros ebenum is highly prized for making fine furniture and musical instruments.
