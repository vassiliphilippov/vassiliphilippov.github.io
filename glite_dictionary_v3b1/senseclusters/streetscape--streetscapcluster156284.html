streetscape

noun
street's visual design
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːtˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈstriːtˌskeɪp / (BrE)
the overall visual appearance and design of a street, including its buildings, trees, and other features

Examples

Modern streetscape design
The city council hired architects to improve the streetscape in the downtown area.
Colorful murals and new benches transformed the streetscape into a more welcoming place.
Trees and lighting play a big role in shaping the streetscape of modern neighborhoods.
The city council hired an architect to redesign the streetscape for better pedestrian access.
Colorful murals and benches added character to the neighborhood's streetscape.
Trees and lighting play a big role in creating an inviting streetscape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cityscape - the visual appearance of a city or urban area
  • synonym: landscape - the visible features of an area of land
