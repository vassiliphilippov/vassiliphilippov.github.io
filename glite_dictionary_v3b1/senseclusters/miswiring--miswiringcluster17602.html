miswiring

noun
incorrect neural connection
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈwaɪərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈwaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
an abnormal or incorrect connection, especially between neurons or circuits, leading to impaired or atypical function
