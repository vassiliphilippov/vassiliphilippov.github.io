heteronym

noun
word spelled same, pronounced differently
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛtəˌnɪm / (AmE), / ˈhɛtərəʊnɪm / (BrE)
A word that is spelled the same as another but has a different pronunciation and meaning.

Examples

English heteronym examples
In English, heteronyms like "lead" and "tear" often confuse learners due to their differing pronunciations.
She explained to her students that a heteronym is a word with the same spelling but different sounds and meanings.
The word bow is a classic example of a heteronym because it can be pronounced in two different ways.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homograph - A word spelled the same as another, with a different meaning (pronunciation may or may not differ)
  • synonym: homonym - A word spelled and pronounced the same as another, but with a different meaning
