anticensorship

adjective
opposed to censorship
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈsɛnsərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈsɛnsəʃɪp / (BrE)
opposed to the suppression or restriction of information or expression

Examples

Anticensorship laws
The group launched an anticensorship campaign to protect freedom of the press.
Many artists support anticensorship efforts to defend creative expression.
The new law was praised by anticensorship advocates around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: free speech - the right to express opinions without censorship
  • antonym: pro-censorship - supporting the suppression or restriction of information or expression
