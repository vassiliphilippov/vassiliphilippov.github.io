hologram

noun
life-sized 3D stage projection
5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhɔː.loʊ.græm/ /; / /ˈhɑː.lə.græm/ (AmE), /ˈhɒ.lə.ɡræm/ /; / /ˈhəʊ.lə.ɡræm/ (BrE)
A three-dimensional, often life-sized projected image of a person or object produced using holographic or related projection techniques, especially one shown on stage or in a live performance.

Examples

Stage hologram show
The hologram of the late star appeared on stage to perform an encore.
Festivalgoers gasped when a lifelike hologram of the singer moved across the arena.
Technicians adjusted the lights until the three-dimensional hologram looked convincingly real to everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holography - the technique of producing holograms and holographic images
  • synonym: projection - the act or result of projecting an image or light to create a visible display
