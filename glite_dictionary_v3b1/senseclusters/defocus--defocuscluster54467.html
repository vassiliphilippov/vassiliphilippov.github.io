defocus

noun
lack of sharpness
7.4
Pronunciation: / diˈfoʊkəs / (AmE), / diˈfəʊkəs / (BrE)
a state in which an image or view lacks sharpness or clarity due to being out of focus

Examples

Image defocus
The defocus in the photograph made it difficult to recognize the faces.
Adjusting the lens can correct any defocus caused by movement.
Scientists studied the effects of defocus on the accuracy of microscope images.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blur - a lack of clear or sharp definition in an image
  • antonym: focus - a state of clear and sharp definition in an image
