dihedral angle

noun
angle between two planes
7.8

Examples

The dihedral angle between planes
Geometric problems often require calculation of the dihedral angle between two surfaces.
In chemistry, the dihedral angle between two bonds affects the molecule's properties.
The dihedral angle of an airplane wing helps determine its stability in flight.
