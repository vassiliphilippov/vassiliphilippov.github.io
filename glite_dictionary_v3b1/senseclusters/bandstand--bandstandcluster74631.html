bandstand

noun
outdoor platform for band performances
Pronunciation: / ˈbændˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈbænd.stænd / (BrE)
a raised platform, typically in a park or public area, where musical bands perform outdoors

Examples

A bandstand in park
Families picnicked near the bandstand as the music played.
Renovations to the Victorian bandstand made it the centerpiece of the park once again.
The local brass bandstand hosted free concerts every Sunday afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stage - a raised area for performances, especially indoors
