streetwalking

noun
walking in streets
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːtwɔːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstriːtwɔːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of walking along public streets, especially for leisure, exercise, or as a pedestrian

Examples

Leisure streetwalking
On weekends, they enjoyed streetwalking through the city center to discover new cafes.
Many tourists spend hours streetwalking in historic neighborhoods, admiring the architecture.
After dinner, we went streetwalking to get some fresh air and see the lively streets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hiking - walking long distances, especially in the countryside
  • synonym: strolling - walking in a slow, relaxed way
