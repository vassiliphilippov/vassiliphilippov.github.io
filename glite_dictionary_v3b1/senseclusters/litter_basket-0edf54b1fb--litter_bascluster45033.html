litter basket

noun
bin for public rubbish disposal
3.5 1.3
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪt.ər ˈbæs.kət/ /; / /ˈlɪt.ər ˈbæs.kɪt/ (AmE), /ˈlɪt.ə ˈbɑː.skɪt/ /; / /ˈlɪt.ə ˈbɑː.skət/ (BrE)

Examples
