myenteric plexus

noun
nerve network in the gut wall
8.3
Pronunciation: / maɪˌɛnˈtɛrɪk ˈplɛksəs / (AmE), / maɪˌɛnˈtɛrɪk ˈplɛksəs / (BrE)
a network of nerve fibers and ganglia located between the layers of the muscular wall of the gastrointestinal tract, controlling gut motility
