belt

noun
linked strip holding ammunition for a machine gun
Pronunciation: / bɛlt / (AmE), / bɛlt / (BrE)
A strip of flexible, linked material holding ammunition, designed to feed cartridges into a machine gun.

Examples

A belt of bullets
After several minutes of firing, the belt was nearly empty.
Technicians inspected each belt for damaged or misaligned cartridges before combat.
The gunner reloaded the machine gun with a new belt of ammunition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cartridge belt - a belt worn around the waist with pockets for cartridges
