self-analysis

noun
examination of one's own thoughts and motives
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf əˈnæləsɪs /; / ˌself əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
the examination and evaluation of your own thoughts, feelings, and motives, often to better understand yourself

Examples
