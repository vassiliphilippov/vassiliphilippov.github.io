Myxinikela siroka

noun
extinct fossil hagfish species
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɪkˌsɪnɪˈkeɪlə sɪˈroʊkə / (AmE), / mɪkˌsɪnɪˈkiːlə sɪˈrəʊkə / (BrE)
An extinct species of fossil hagfish from the Pennsylvanian period (c. 300 million years ago) that resembled modern hagfishes.
