Examples

To speak articulately
If you want your message heard, present your ideas articulately and with confidence.
She spoke articulately during the debate, impressing both the judges and the audience.
The professor explained the complex topic articulately so that everyone could understand.
Despite her nerves, she answered every question articulately and thoughtfully.

Synonyms

    eloquently is fluent and often used to persuade listeners, while articulately means speaking clearly and effectively without implying persuasion.
  • intelligibly
    4.9 0.13/m
    intelligibly is clear enough to be understood by listeners, while articulately adds fluent, effective expression beyond plain clarity.
  • perspicuously
    8.8 0.02/m
    perspicuously is written or spoken in a very clear, formal way, while articulately means clear and fluent in normal speech.
  • pellucidly
    8.7 0.01/m
    pellucidly paints ideas with bright, literary clarity, while articulately focuses on clear, natural speech for everyday listeners.
  • clearly
    1.0 65/m
    clearly describes facts in a simple, direct way, while articulately adds fluent, careful expression that sounds polished when spoken.
