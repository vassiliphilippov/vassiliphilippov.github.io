excitonic

adjective
about excitons
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛksaɪˈtɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛksaɪˈtɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving excitons, which are bound states of an electron and a hole in a material, especially in physics or chemistry

Examples

Excitonic states

Strong excitonic effects can influence the optical properties of thin films.
Understanding excitonic interactions is important for designing efficient solar cells.
