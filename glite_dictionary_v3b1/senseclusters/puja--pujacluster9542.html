puja

noun
Hindu worship ceremony
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpuː.dʒə/ (AmE), /ˈpuː.dʒə/ (BrE)
a Hindu religious ceremony that involves prayers, offerings, and rituals to honor a deity

Examples

Morning puja ceremony
Every morning, the family gathers to perform puja at home.
During the festival, hundreds of people attended the temple for a special puja.
She prepared flowers and sweets as offerings for the evening puja.

  • synonym: worship - the act of showing reverence and adoration for a deity
