plaintively

adverb
in a sad, mournful manner
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪntɪvli / (AmE), / ˈpleɪntɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that sounds or appears sad and mournful.

Examples

To speak plaintively
He looked plaintively at his friend, hoping for some comfort.
She called out plaintively for her lost cat as night fell.
The violin's melody rose plaintively through the quiet auditorium.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plaintive - sounding sad and mournful
