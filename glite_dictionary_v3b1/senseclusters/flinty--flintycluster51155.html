flinty

adjective
unfeeling; emotionally hard
8.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈflɪn.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈflɪn.tiː/ (BrE)
(of a person or their expression) very hard, unfeeling, and showing no emotion or sympathy

Examples

Flinty attitude
Despite his flinty demeanor, he cared deeply for his family.
Her flinty eyes betrayed no sign of pity during the interview.
The judge remained flinty even as the defendant pleaded for mercy.
Despite the pleas for help, his flinty heart remained unmoved.
Her flinty expression made it clear that she had no sympathy for his excuses.
The judge's flinty demeanor intimidated everyone in the courtroom.

  • synonym: hard-hearted - lacking sympathy or compassion
  • antonym: tender - showing gentleness, kindness, and affection
