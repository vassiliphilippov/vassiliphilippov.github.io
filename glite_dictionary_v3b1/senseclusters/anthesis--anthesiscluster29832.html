anthesis

noun
flowering period or process
1.7
Pronunciation: / ænˈθiːsɪs / (AmE), / ænˈθiːsɪs / (BrE)
the period or process during which a flower bud opens and the blossom unfolds

Examples

Anthesis period
Accurate timing of anthesis is important for plant breeders studying pollination.
During anthesis, flowers are at their most attractive to pollinating insects.
The anthesis of the magnolia tree is an impressive sight each spring.
