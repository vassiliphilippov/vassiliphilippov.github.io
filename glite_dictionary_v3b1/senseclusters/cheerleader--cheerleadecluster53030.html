cheerleader

noun
enthusiastic public supporter
5.5 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɪr.liː.dər/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɪə.liː.də/ (BrE)
A person who enthusiastically and publicly supports a cause, idea, or group, often in a vocal or visible way.

Examples

A cheerleader for change
Despite criticism, he acted as a cheerleader for the team's decision.
She has always been a prominent cheerleader for environmental reform.
The senator became a vocal cheerleader for the education bill.
