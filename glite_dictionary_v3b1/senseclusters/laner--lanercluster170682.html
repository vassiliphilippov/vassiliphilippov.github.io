laner

noun
player in a game lane
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪnər / (AmE), / ˈleɪnə / (BrE)
a player who is responsible for controlling a specific lane in certain multiplayer online games

Examples

Top laner player
As the top laner, he focused on defending his tower and farming minions.
The team relied on their experienced laner to gain an early advantage in the match.
She became a well-known laner in the competitive gaming community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: jungler - a player who moves through the jungle area in multiplayer online games
  • synonym: support - a player who assists teammates in multiplayer online games
