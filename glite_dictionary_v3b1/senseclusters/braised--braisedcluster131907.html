braised

adjective
cooked by browning then simmering
3.4
Pronunciation: / breɪzd / (AmE), / breɪzd / (BrE)
(of food) cooked by browning in fat and then simmering slowly in a closed container.

Examples

Braised short ribs
Our menu features braised short ribs cooked in red wine sauce.
She prefers braised cabbage to raw salads in the winter.
The chef served delicious braised lamb shanks with roasted vegetables.
