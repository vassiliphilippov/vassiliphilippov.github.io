the worm turns

x
when fortunes unexpectedly reverse; the underdog's turn
Pronunciation: / ðə wɝm tɝnz / (AmE), / ðə wɜːm tɜːnz / (BrE)
used to indicate that a person who has been treated badly or oppressed begins to resist or take action in their own favor, or when fortunes unexpectedly reverse, especially for the underdog.

Examples

The worm turns suddenly
After months of losing, suddenly the worm turns and the team starts winning every game.
In many classic stories, the worm turns at the most unexpected moment, surprising everyone.
She used to let her colleagues take credit for her work, but finally the worm turns and she stood up for herself.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comeuppance - a deserved reversal of fortune or retribution
  • synonym: turn the tables - to reverse a situation to one's advantage
  • synonym: underdog - a person expected to lose in a contest or struggle
