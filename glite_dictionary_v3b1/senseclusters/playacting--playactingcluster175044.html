playacting

noun
theatrical role-playing or pretending
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪˌæktɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌæktɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of performing a role in a play or pretending to be someone else, especially as an actor

Examples

Playacting in a play
Children often engage in playacting when they mimic their favorite movie characters.
She dismissed his apology as mere playacting meant to gain sympathy.
The drama class assignment involved improvisational playacting based on real-life scenarios.
