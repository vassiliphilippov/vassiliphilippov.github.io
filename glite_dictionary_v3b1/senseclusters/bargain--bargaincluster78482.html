Synonyms

  • buy
    1.0 8/m
    a buy is a purchase judged to be especially good value, while a bargain emphasizes paying less than true value.
  • great deal
    1.8 1/m
    a great deal is a particularly large saving or offer, while a bargain can be any purchase below its true value.
  • steal
    3.7 1/m
    a steal is an item bought at an unusually low price, while a bargain is less extreme but still gives good savings.
  • good value
    1.7 1.0/m
    good value means something worth its price, while a bargain stresses that the item costs much less than its true value.
