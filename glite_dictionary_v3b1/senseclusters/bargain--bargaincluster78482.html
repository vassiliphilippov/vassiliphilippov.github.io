bargain

noun
something bought cheaply; good deal
3.4 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑːr.gən/ /; / /ˈbɑːr.gɪn/ (AmE), /ˈbɑː.gən/ /; / /ˈbɑː.gɪn/ (BrE)
