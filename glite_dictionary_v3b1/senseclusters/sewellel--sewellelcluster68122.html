sewellel

noun
a primitive North American burrowing rodent
Pronunciation: / suˈwɛləl / (AmE), / suˈwɛləl / (BrE)
a burrowing, nocturnal rodent native to the uplands of the Pacific coast of North America, considered the most primitive living rodent

Examples

The sewellel animal
Although rarely seen, the sewellel is an important part of its upland ecosystem.
Some scientists consider the sewellel a living fossil due to its primitive characteristics.
The sewellel creates extensive burrow systems in the moist forests of the Pacific Northwest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mountain beaver - an alternative common name for the sewellel
