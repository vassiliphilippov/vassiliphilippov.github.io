dysmenorrhea

noun
painful menstruation
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsˌmɛnəˈriə / (AmE), / ˌdɪsˌmɛnəˈriːə / (BrE)
painful menstruation, typically involving abdominal cramps or discomfort during a woman's period

Examples

Severe dysmenorrhea pain
Many women experience dysmenorrhea during adolescence, which can disrupt daily activities.
Severe dysmenorrhea may be a sign of an underlying condition that requires medical attention.
She takes medication to relieve her dysmenorrhea every month.
