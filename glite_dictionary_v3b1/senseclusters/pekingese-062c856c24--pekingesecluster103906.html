Pekingese

noun
small Chinese dog breed
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːkɪˈniz / (AmE), / ˌpɛkɪˈniːz / (BrE)
A breed of small dog originating in China, with a long silky coat, short legs, and a broad flat muzzle.

Examples

A Pekingese dog
Grooming a Pekingese requires regular brushing to maintain its long, silky coat.
Her grandmother always had a loyal Pekingese by her side.
The Pekingese won best in show at the prestigious dog competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Shih Tzu - a small Chinese breed of dog with a long silky coat
