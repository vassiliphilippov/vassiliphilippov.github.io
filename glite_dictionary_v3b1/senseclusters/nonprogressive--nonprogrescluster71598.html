nonprogressive

adjective
not worsening
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnprəˈɡrɛsɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnprəˈɡrɛsɪv / (BrE)
not increasing, advancing, or worsening over time; remaining stable in condition or state

Examples

Nonprogressive disease
The doctor explained that the condition was nonprogressive and would not get worse over time.
A nonprogressive disorder means the symptoms remain the same rather than deteriorating.
Her nonprogressive hearing loss allowed her to adapt without fear of further decline.

Related Terms

  • antonym: progressive - increasing or advancing over time
  • synonym: stable - not changing or likely to change
