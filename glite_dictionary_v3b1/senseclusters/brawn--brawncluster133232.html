brawn

noun
muscular strength
Pronunciation: / brɔn / (AmE), / brɔːn / (BrE)
Physical strength, especially as opposed to intelligence.

Examples

Strong brawn
Despite his impressive brawn, he was gentle and soft-spoken.
The job requires more brawn than brains.
They relied on their brawn to move the heavy furniture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brains - intellectual ability; intelligence
  • synonym: muscle - the physical tissue or strength that gives power to move and lift
