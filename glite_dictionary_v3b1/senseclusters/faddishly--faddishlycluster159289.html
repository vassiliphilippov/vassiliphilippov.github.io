faddishly

adverb
in a manner typical of a fad
9.9 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfæ.dɪ.ʃ.liː/ /; / /ˈfæ.dɪ.ʃ.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈfæ.dɪ.ʃ.liː/ /; / /ˈfæ.dɪ.ʃ.lɪ/ (BrE)
In a way that is influenced by or characteristic of a fad; in a manner that follows short-lived trends.

Examples

Dress faddishly often
He dressed faddishly, always seeking the latest styles regardless of practicality.
Some diets are adopted faddishly without considering long-term health effects.
The company's products were marketed faddishly to attract trendy consumers.
