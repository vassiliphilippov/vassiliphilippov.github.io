reconsent

noun
renewed permission
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriːkənˈsɛnt / (AmE), / ˌriːkənˈsɛnt / (BrE)
the act of giving or obtaining consent again, especially after changes to conditions or information

Examples

User reconsent required
After updating the privacy policy, the company requested reconsent from all its users.
The clinical trial required reconsent when new procedures were introduced.
Parents were asked for reconsent before their children participated in the revised study.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consent - permission for something to happen
  • antonym: refusal - the act of saying no or not giving permission
