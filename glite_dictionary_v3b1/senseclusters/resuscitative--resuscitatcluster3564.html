resuscitative

adjective
reviving or restoring
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsʌsɪˌteɪtɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈsʌsɪtətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or having the effect of reviving someone or something that is unconscious, inactive, or failing
