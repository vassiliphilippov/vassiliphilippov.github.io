fendering

noun
wall protection strips
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛndərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɛndərɪŋ / (BrE)
protective strips or guards installed on walls, doors, or corridors to prevent damage from carts, wheelchairs, or equipment

Examples

Wall fendering
The hospital installed vinyl fendering along the corridors to protect walls from gurneys and carts.
Maintenance staff checked the fendering regularly to ensure it was securely attached to the walls.
New fendering was added to the loading area to prevent damage from heavy equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bumper - a protective bar on vehicles or objects to absorb impact
  • synonym: guardrail - a rail or barrier to protect against impact or damage
