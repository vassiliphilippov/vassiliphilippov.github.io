pantheress

noun
female panther
Pronunciation: / ˈpænθərɛs / (AmE), / ˈpænθərɛs / (BrE)

A fierce pantheress

In the rare book, a pantheress was mentioned as the leader of the pride.
The explorer believed he had spotted a pantheress near the river at dawn.
