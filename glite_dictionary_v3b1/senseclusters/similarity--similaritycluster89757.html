similarity

noun
psychological principle of grouping by likeness
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪm.əˈlɛr.ə.ti /; / ˌsɪməˈlɛrɪti / (AmE), / ˌsɪm.ɪˈlær.ɪ.ti /; / ˌsɪməˈlarəti / (BrE)
