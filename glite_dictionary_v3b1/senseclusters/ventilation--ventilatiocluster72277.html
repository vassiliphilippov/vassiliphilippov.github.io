ventilation

noun
process of breathing
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛn.t̬əˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌvɛn.tɪˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of breathing, in which air is inhaled into the lungs and carbon dioxide is exhaled

Examples

Lung ventilation
Adequate ventilation is essential for maintaining healthy blood oxygen levels.
Mechanical ventilation may be required for patients who cannot breathe on their own.
The patient's ventilation was monitored carefully throughout the surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: respiration - the process of breathing; taking in oxygen and releasing carbon dioxide
