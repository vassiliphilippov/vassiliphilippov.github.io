the process of breathing, in which air is inhaled into the lungs and carbon dioxide is exhaled

Examples

Lung ventilation
Adequate ventilation is essential for maintaining healthy blood oxygen levels.
Mechanical ventilation may be required for patients who cannot breathe on their own.
The patient's ventilation was monitored carefully throughout the surgery.

Synonyms

  • breathing
    1.0 21/m
    breathing is the everyday action of inhaling and exhaling air, while ventilation is the medical term for that lung process.
  • respiration
    3.4 1.61/m
    respiration is a scientific word for taking in oxygen and sending out carbon dioxide, while ventilation emphasizes moving air into and out of the lungs.
  • external respiration
    4.6 0.02/m
    external respiration is gas exchange between air and blood in the lungs, while ventilation names the airflow into and out of the lungs.
