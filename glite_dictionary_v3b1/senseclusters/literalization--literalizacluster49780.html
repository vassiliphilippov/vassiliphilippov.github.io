literalization

noun
interpreting literally
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪtərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌlɪtərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of interpreting or expressing something in a strictly literal or non-figurative way

Examples

Literalization of a joke
The literalization of jokes often ruins their humor for the audience.
His literalization of the poem missed its deeper symbolic meaning.
Teachers warn against the literalization of idioms when learning a new language.

Related Terms

  • antonym: figurative - not literal; using metaphor or symbolism
  • synonym: interpretation - the act of explaining or understanding the meaning of something
  • synonym: literalism - strict adherence to the exact meaning of words
