mudpack

verb
to apply mud treatment
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌdpæk / (AmE), / ˈmʌdpæk / (BrE)
to apply a layer of mud to the skin as a cosmetic or therapeutic treatment

Examples

To mudpack the skin

At the spa, clients often mudpack their faces for a refreshing treatment.
Some athletes mudpack their legs to help with muscle recovery after intense exercise.
