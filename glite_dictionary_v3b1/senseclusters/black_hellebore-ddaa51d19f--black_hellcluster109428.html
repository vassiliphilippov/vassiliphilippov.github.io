black hellebore

noun
a perennial plant with leathery leaves and purplish-green flowers
8.2 9.0
Pronunciation: /blæk ˈhel.ɪ.bɔːr/ (AmE), /blæk ˈhel.ɪ.bɔː/ (BrE)
a European evergreen plant (Helleborus niger) with leathery leaves and white or purplish flowers that bloom in winter; all parts are toxic if eaten
