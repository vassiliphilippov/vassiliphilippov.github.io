protocol

noun
diplomatic rules and etiquette
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊt̬əˌkɑl /; / ˈproʊ.təˌkɑl / (AmE), / ˈprəʊtəkɒl /; / ˈprəʊ.tə.kɒl / (BrE)
the official or formal system of rules and procedures, especially for ceremonies, diplomacy, or other official situations

Examples

Diplomatic protocol
Breaking diplomatic protocol, the minister arrived late to the state banquet.
Official protocol required that guests be formally introduced before meeting the president.
The ambassador followed strict protocol during the signing ceremony.
All visitors to the laboratory must follow strict protocol to ensure safety.
In diplomatic meetings, protocol dictates where each official should sit.
The university has a detailed protocol for addressing academic misconduct.
