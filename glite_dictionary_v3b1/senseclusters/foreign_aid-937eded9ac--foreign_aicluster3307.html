foreign aid

noun
economic or military assistance given by one nation to another
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔɹən eɪd / (AmE), / ˈfɒɹɪn eɪd / (BrE)
money, resources, or assistance (such as economic or military help) given by one country to another, especially to support development or recovery.
