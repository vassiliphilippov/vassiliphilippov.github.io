advert

noun
advertisement; public promotion
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.vɝːt / (AmE), / ˈæd.vɜːt / (BrE)

Examples

TV advert

I found my first job through an advert in the local newspaper.
They're running a clever advert in all the magazines this month.
