staminate

adjective
having stamens; male (in botany)
6.9 8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈstæ.mɪ.neɪt/ /; / /ˈstæ.mɪ.nət/ (AmE), /ˈstæ.mɪ.neɪt/ /; / /ˈstæ.mɪ.nət/ (BrE)
(of a flower or plant) having stamens and capable of producing pollen, but lacking functional pistils; male in reproductive function.

Examples
