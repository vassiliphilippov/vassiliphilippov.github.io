omnidirectional

adjective
sending/receiving in all directions
2.9 6.3
Pronunciation: /ɑːm.nɪ.daɪ.ˈrek.ʃən.əl/ /; / /ɑːm.nɪ.dɪ.ˈrek.ʃən.əl/ (AmE), /ɒm.nɪ.daɪ.ˈrek.ʃən.əl/ /; / /ɒm.nɪ.dɪ.ˈrek.ʃən.əl/ (BrE)
(of a device or signal) sending or receiving equally in all directions; not limited to a specific direction.

Examples

Omnidirectional microphone
Some microphones are omnidirectional, meaning they pick up sound from all sides equally.
The omnidirectional antenna provides coverage in every direction around it.
The security camera uses an omnidirectional lens to monitor the entire room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unidirectional - operating in or moving only in one direction
