arteria nutricia

noun
artery supplying a bone's medullary cavity
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪəriə nuˈtrɪʃiə / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə njuˈtrɪʃiə / (BrE)
an artery that supplies blood to the medullary cavity of a long bone

Examples

arteria nutricia of bone
Damage to the arteria nutricia can result in impaired bone healing.
During orthopedic surgery, the arteria nutricia must be carefully preserved.
The arteria nutricia is essential for delivering nutrients to the inner part of the bone.
