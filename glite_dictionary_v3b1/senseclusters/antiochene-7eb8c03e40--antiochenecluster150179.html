Antiochene

noun
person from Antioch
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiəˈkiːn / (AmE), / ˌæntiəˈkiːn / (BrE)
a person who is an inhabitant or native of the ancient city of Antioch

Examples

Ancient Antiochene merchant
The ancient Antiochene merchants were known for their trade along the Silk Road.
Artifacts found in the ruins belonged to a wealthy Antiochene family.
Many early Christian scholars were Antiochenes who contributed to theological debates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Antioch - an ancient city located in what is now Turkey
