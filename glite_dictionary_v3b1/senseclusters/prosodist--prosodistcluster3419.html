prosodist

noun
expert in poetic rhythm
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːsədɪst / (AmE), / ˈprɒsədɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or is skilled in the patterns of rhythm, sound, and intonation in language, especially in poetry

As a skilled prosodist, she could identify subtle patterns in spoken English.
