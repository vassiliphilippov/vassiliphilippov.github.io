trigram

noun
three-word sequence
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪɡræm /; / ˈtraɪˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈtraɪɡræm / (BrE)
a group or sequence of three words, letters, or symbols, especially used in language analysis or computational linguistics
