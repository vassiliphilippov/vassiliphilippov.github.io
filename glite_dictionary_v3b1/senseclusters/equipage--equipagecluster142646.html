equipage

noun
military equipment and supplies
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkwɪpɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪpɪdʒ / (BrE)
the equipment and supplies that are used by a military force or expedition

Examples

Military equipage
His responsibilities included overseeing all military equipage before the march.
Much of the regiment's equipage was lost during the difficult crossing of the river.
The army's equipage was transported in dozens of heavy wagons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: equipment - the necessary items for a particular purpose
