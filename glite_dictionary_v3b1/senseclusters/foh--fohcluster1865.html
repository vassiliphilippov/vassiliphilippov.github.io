foh

interjection
slang for disbelief
Pronunciation: / foʊ / (AmE), / fəʊ / (BrE)
an informal interjection used to express disbelief, dismissal, or contempt toward someone or something

Examples

Foh, no way
When he claimed he could run a mile in four minutes, she just said foh and laughed.
Someone tried to cut in line, and the crowd shouted foh in unison.
He bragged about his new car, but his friends responded with a quick foh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whatever - an informal way to dismiss or show indifference
