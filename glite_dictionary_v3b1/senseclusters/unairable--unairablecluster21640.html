unairable

adjective
not ventilatable
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɛrəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈeərəbl / (BrE)
not able to be ventilated or provided with fresh air; stuffy or badly ventilated.

Examples

Unairable basement
The old basement was so unairable that it always smelled musty.
After the flood, the room became unairable and needed professional cleaning.
They found the attic unairable during the hot summer months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stuffy - lacking fresh air and unpleasant to breathe in
  • antonym: ventilated - provided with fresh air
