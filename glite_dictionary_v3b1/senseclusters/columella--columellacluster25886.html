columella

noun
small column-like structure in biology
Pronunciation: / ˌkɒl.juˈmɛl.ə / (AmE), / ˌkɒl.juˈmɛl.ə / (BrE)
a small column or column-like structure found as a part of certain plants or animals, especially in biological anatomy

Examples
