os pubis

noun
front section of hipbone
Pronunciation: / ɑs ˈpjuːbɪs / (AmE), / ɒs ˈpjuːbɪs / (BrE)
the front section of the hipbone in humans and other vertebrates, forming the anterior part of the pelvis

Examples

The os pubis bone
Pain in the os pubis can sometimes result from overuse during sports activities.
The surgeon examined the fracture near the os pubis to determine the extent of the injury.
X-rays showed that the os pubis had healed properly after the operation.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: ilium - uppermost and largest bone of the pelvis
  • degree lower: ischium - the lower and back part of the hipbone
  • synonym: pubic bone - the same bone, alternative name
