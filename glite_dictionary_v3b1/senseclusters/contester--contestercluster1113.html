contester

noun
someone who challenges a result
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɛstər / (AmE), / kənˈtɛstə / (BrE)
a person who formally challenges or disputes the outcome of a competition, election, or other event

Examples

Contester files an appeal
Each contester must provide evidence to support their claim during the appeals process.
The court decided in favor of the contester after reviewing the competition's rules.
The defeated candidate became a contester after alleging irregularities in the vote count.

Related Terms

  • synonym: challenger - a person who challenges another, especially in a contest
  • synonym: contestant - a person who takes part in a contest or competition
