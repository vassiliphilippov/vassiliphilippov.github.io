stroma

noun
fungal tissue mass with spore structures
Pronunciation: / ˈstroʊmə / (AmE), / ˈstrəʊmə / (BrE)
A mass of fungal tissue in which spore-bearing structures are embedded or on which they are borne.

Examples

Fungal stroma
Microscopic examination revealed that the spore sacs were embedded in the stroma.
Researchers studied the morphology of the stroma to classify the new fungal species.
The fungus developed a dense stroma that supported the formation of fruiting bodies.
