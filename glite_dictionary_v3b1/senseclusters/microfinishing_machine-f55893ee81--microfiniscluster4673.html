microfinishing machine

noun
machine for fine surface finishing
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A machine tool used to enhance surface smoothness and precision of workpieces by removing a thin amorphous surface layer left by prior processes, typically using an abrasive stone or tape.

Examples

A microfinishing machine tool
Before reaching the final inspection, each part passes through a microfinishing machine for enhanced smoothness.
Operators must regularly calibrate the microfinishing machine to maintain precise surface tolerances.
The automotive plant installed a new microfinishing machine to improve the quality of engine components.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lapping machine - machine for polishing surfaces to a high degree of smoothness
