being a bitch

noun
acting unpleasantly or harshly (offensive, vulgar)
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːɪŋ ə bɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbiːɪŋ ə bɪtʃ / (BrE)
acting in an unpleasant, harsh, or mean-spirited way, especially in a manner considered unkind or difficult; generally offensive or vulgar

Examples

Being a bitch to friends
He apologized for being a bitch about the plans last night.
She admits to being a bitch sometimes when she feels stressed at work.
Some people mistake her confidence for being a bitch, but she’s actually very kind.
