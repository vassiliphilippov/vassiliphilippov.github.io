WHOIS

verb
to look up domain info
Pronunciation: / ˈhuː ɪz / (AmE), / ˈhuː ɪz / (BrE)
to search for or obtain the registration details of a domain name or IP address using the WHOIS protocol or service

Examples

To WHOIS a domain
Before purchasing the website, she decided to WHOIS the domain to verify the owner's details.
IT professionals often WHOIS suspicious domains to check their registration information.
You can WHOIS an IP address to find out who controls it.
