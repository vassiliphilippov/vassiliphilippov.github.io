lady-slipper

noun
a wild orchid with a pouchlike lip
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdi ˌslɪpər / (AmE), / ˈleɪdi ˌslɪpə / (BrE)
a wild orchid of North America and Eurasia with a large, inflated pouchlike lip, often difficult to cultivate

We spotted a rare lady-slipper blooming along the forest trail.
