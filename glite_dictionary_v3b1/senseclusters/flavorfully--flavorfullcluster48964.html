flavorfully

adverb
with strong taste
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪvɚfəli / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvəfəli / (BrE)
in a way that is full of flavor or has a strong, pleasant taste

Examples

Flavorfully cooked vegetables
The chef prepared the vegetables flavorfully with a mix of spices and herbs.
She always cooks flavorfully, making every meal a delight for the senses.
The sauce was flavorfully rich, adding depth to the entire dish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blandly - in a way that lacks strong flavor or taste
  • synonym: tastily - in a tasty or appetizing manner
