insinuation

noun
subtle method to gain favor
4.0 6.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.sɪ.njuː.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪn.sɪ.njuː.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
