flying robin

noun
tropical fish with gliding fins
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈflaɪ.ɪŋ ˈrɑː.bən/ /; / /ˈflaɪ.ɪŋ ˈrɑː.bɪn/ (AmE), /ˈflaɪ.ɪŋ ˈrɒb.ɪn/ /; / /ˈflaɪ.ɪŋ ˈrɒb.ən/ (BrE)
