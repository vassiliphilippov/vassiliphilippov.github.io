nouvelle

noun
short novel
8.2
Pronunciation: / nuːˈvɛl / (AmE), / nuːˈvɛl / (BrE)
a short novel or a long short story, especially in French literature

Examples

A nouvelle story
The author released a nouvelle that explored the complexities of family relationships.
In her literature class, she analyzed a famous French nouvelle from the nineteenth century.
He prefers reading a nouvelle because it is longer than a short story but not as long as a novel.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: novel - a long work of fiction, longer than a novella
  • synonym: novella - a short novel, longer than a short story but shorter than a novel
  • degree lower: short story - a brief work of fiction, shorter than a novella
