butanol

noun
a flammable alcohol used as a solvent
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːtəˌnɔl / (AmE), / ˈbjuːtənɒl / (BrE)
a flammable alcohol, derived from butane, used as a solvent and sometimes as a fuel or in chemical manufacturing

Examples

Butanol fuel
Due to its flammability, butanol must be stored away from heat sources.
Scientists are researching the potential of butanol as an alternative biofuel.
The laboratory uses butanol as a solvent in several chemical processes.
