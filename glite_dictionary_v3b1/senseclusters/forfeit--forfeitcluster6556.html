forfeit

noun
penalty involving loss or giving up something
8.0 5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔr.fɪt /; / ˈfɔːrfɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.fɪt /; / ˈfɔːfɪt / (BrE)
something that is lost, given up, or taken away as a penalty for breaking a rule or law
