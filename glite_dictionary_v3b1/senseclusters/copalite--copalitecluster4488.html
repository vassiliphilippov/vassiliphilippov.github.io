copalite

noun
partly mineralized copal resin from the ground
9.9 9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkoʊ.pə.laɪt/ (AmE), /ˈkəʊ.pə.laɪt/ (BrE)
A partly mineralized form of copal resin that has been buried and dug from the ground, often considered an intermediate stage between copal and amber.

Examples

A piece of copalite
Copalite is often confused with true amber due to its appearance and origin.
Fossil hunters discovered a rare piece of copalite while excavating in East Africa.
The ancient copalite samples were analyzed to determine their age and composition.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: amber - fossilized tree resin used as a gemstone
  • synonym: copal - a natural resin from tropical trees
