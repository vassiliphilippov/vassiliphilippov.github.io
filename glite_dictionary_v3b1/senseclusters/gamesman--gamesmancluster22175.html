gamesman

noun
gambler or player
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪmzˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmzˌmæn / (BrE)
a person who plays games, especially for money or as a gambler

Examples

Professional gamesman
The skilled gamesman always seemed to know when to place his bets.
As a professional gamesman, he traveled to casinos around the world.
Many admired the gamesman for his calm strategy and quick thinking at the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gambler - a person who bets money on games of chance
  • synonym: player - someone who participates in a game or sport
  • antonym: spectator - a person who watches a game but does not play
