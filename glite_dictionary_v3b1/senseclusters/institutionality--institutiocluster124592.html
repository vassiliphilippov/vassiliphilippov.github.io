institutionality

noun
state of being institutional
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.stɪˌtuː.ʃəˈnæl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌɪn.stɪˌtjuː.ʃəˈnæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being an institution or having the characteristics of an institution

Examples

The institutionality of the university

The institutionality of the university shapes its policies and traditions.
Critics questioned the institutionality of the new committee and its connection to established organizations.
Understanding the institutionality of government agencies helps explain their decision-making processes.

  • synonym: institutionalization - the process of making something an institution
