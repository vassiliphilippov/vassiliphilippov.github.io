christless

adjective
without belief in Christ
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkraɪst.ləs / (AmE), / ˈkraɪst.ləs / (BrE)
Not believing in, acknowledging, or connected to Christ; lacking Christianity.

Examples

A christless society
He lamented what he saw as a christless society losing its moral foundation.
The author described the community as christless and untouched by Western traditions.
The missionaries traveled to many christless regions to spread their message.

Related Terms
