nonet

noun
group of nine
Pronunciation: / noʊˈnɛt / (AmE), / nɒˈnɛt / (BrE)
a group or set of nine people or things considered together

Examples

A group nonet
The art class was divided into a nonet to work on a group project.
A nonet of scientists collaborated on the groundbreaking research paper.
For the final performance, a nonet of dancers took the stage together.
