golden-green

adjective
green with a golden tint
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɡoʊldənˈɡriːn / (AmE), / ˌɡəʊldənˈɡriːn / (BrE)
Of a green color that is tinted with gold; a shade of green with a yellowish or golden hue.

Examples

A golden-green dress
She chose a golden-green dress that beautifully complemented her complexion.
Sunlight made the golden-green grass shimmer softly in the afternoon breeze.
The bird’s feathers shone a vivid golden-green in the morning light.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: emerald-green - a bright green color like that of an emerald
  • degree lower: olive-green - a brownish-green color
  • degree lower: yellow-green - a color between yellow and green
