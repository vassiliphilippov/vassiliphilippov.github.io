developmentalist

noun
development specialist
7.9
Pronunciation: /dɪ.vəl.əp.ˈmen.təl.ɪst/ /; / /də.vəl.əp.ˈmen.təl.ɪst/ (AmE), /dɪ.vəl.əp.ˈmen.təl.ɪst/ /; / /də.vəl.əp.ˈmen.təl.ɪst/ (BrE)
a specialist who studies the physical, cognitive, and social development of humans or other organisms, especially in early life stages

Examples

Child developmentalist
The school hired a developmentalist to assess the children's learning progress.
A developmentalist may focus on how language skills emerge in early childhood.
Parents often consult a developmentalist if they have concerns about their child's growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: child psychologist - a psychologist specializing in the mental development of children
  • synonym: developmental psychologist - a psychologist who studies how people grow and change over time
