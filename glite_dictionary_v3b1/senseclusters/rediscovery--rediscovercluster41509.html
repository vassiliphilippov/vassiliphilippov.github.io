rediscovery

noun
act of discovering again
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.dɪˈskʌv.ɚ.i / (AmE), / ˌriː.dɪˈskʌv.ər.i / (BrE)
the act or process of discovering something again, especially after having forgotten or lost awareness of it

Examples

The rediscovery of history
The rediscovery of the lost manuscript excited scholars around the world.
The city's recent rediscovery of its cultural heritage has attracted many tourists.
With each rediscovery of the artist's work, her legacy grows stronger.

Related Terms
