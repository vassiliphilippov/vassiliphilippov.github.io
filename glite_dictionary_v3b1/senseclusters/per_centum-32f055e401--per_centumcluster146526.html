per centum

noun
proportion per hundred; percent

Pronunciation: /pɜːr ˈken.təm/ /; / /pɜːr ˈsen.təm/ (AmE), /pɜː ˈsen.təm/ /; / /pɜː ˈken.təm/ (BrE)

Examples
