Chrysanthemum

noun
genus of flowering plants (chrysanthemums)
9.4
Pronunciation: / krɪˈsænθəməm / (AmE), / krɪˈsanθəməm / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the daisy family, known for their large, brightly colored blooms; in some classifications, several related genera were formerly included here.

Examples

A Chrysanthemum flower
In botanical terms, the Chrysanthemum genus includes many popular ornamental flowers.
Recent classifications have moved some species out of the Chrysanthemum genus into separate groups.
The garden was filled with colorful chrysanthemums in full bloom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Dendranthema - genus of flowering plants formerly included in Chrysanthemum
