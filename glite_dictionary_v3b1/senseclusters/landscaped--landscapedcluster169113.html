landscaped

adjective
improved land by gardening or design
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlænd.skeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈlan.skeɪpt / (BrE)
(of an area of land) improved or designed with gardens, plants, paths, or other features, typically by a professional landscaper.

Examples

A landscaped garden
After construction was complete, the park was carefully landscaped to attract more visitors.
The hotel grounds are landscaped to blend in with the surrounding countryside.
Their new house has a beautifully landscaped backyard with a pond and several flowerbeds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: wild - (of land) not managed or altered by humans; in a natural state
