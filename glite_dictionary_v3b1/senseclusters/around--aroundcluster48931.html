around

adverb
approximately (quantity)
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈraʊnd/ /; / /ə.ˈraʊn/ (AmE), /ə.ˈraʊnd/ (BrE)
used with numbers or quantities to mean approximately, not exactly but close to the stated amount

Examples

Around fifty people
He weighs around seventy kilograms.
There were around twenty people at the meeting.
This phone costs around $500, depending on the model.

  • antonym: exactly - precisely; without approximation
