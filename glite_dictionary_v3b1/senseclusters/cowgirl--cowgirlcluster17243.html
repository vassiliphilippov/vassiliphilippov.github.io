cowgirl

noun
female ranch worker; woman cowboy
4.5 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkaʊ.gɜːrl/ (AmE), /ˈkaʊ.gɜːl/ (BrE)
a woman who works with cattle and performs traditional cowboy skills, either on a ranch or in rodeos

Examples

A cowgirl rides horses
As a cowgirl, she could ride, rope, and wrangle cattle as well as any man.
Dressed in boots and a hat, the cowgirl led the herd across the open plains.
The young cowgirl won several awards at the annual rodeo for her impressive skills.
Dressed in boots and a hat, the cowgirl took first place in the barrel racing competition.
She grew up on a ranch and dreamed of becoming a professional cowgirl.
The talented cowgirl wowed the crowd with her impressive roping tricks at the rodeo.

  • synonym: rancher - a person who owns or works on a ranch
