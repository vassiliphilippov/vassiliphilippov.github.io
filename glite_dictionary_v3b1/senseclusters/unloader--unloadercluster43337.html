unloader

noun
person or machine removing goods
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈloʊdər / (AmE), / ʌnˈləʊdə / (BrE)
a person or machine that removes goods or materials from a vehicle or container

Examples

Container unloader
The unloader carefully removed each box from the shipping container.
At the port, a mechanical unloader transfers grain from ships to storage silos.
After the truck arrived, the unloader started working immediately to empty it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loader - a person or machine that puts goods or materials into a vehicle or container
  • synonym: remover - a person or thing that takes something away
