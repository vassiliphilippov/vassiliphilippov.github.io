holdup man

noun
male armed robber
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊldʌp mæn / (AmE), / ˈhəʊldʌp mæn / (BrE)
a man who commits armed robbery, especially by threatening with a weapon

Examples

A holdup man with a gun
Police arrested a suspected holdup man near the scene of the crime.
The holdup man demanded cash from the terrified cashier.
Witnesses described the holdup man as tall and wearing a black mask.

Related Terms

  • antonym: burglar - a person who illegally enters buildings to steal things
  • synonym: robber - a person who takes property unlawfully, especially by force or threat
