rive

verb
split or tear apart forcibly
7.1
Pronunciation: / raɪv / (AmE), / raɪv / (BrE)
to split or tear apart forcibly, especially with a sharp instrument

Examples

To rive a log
A bolt of lightning rived the old oak tree in the storm.
Centuries of conflict have riven the nation.
The powerful axe rived the log into two pieces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleave - to split or sever (something), especially along a natural line
  • synonym: tear - to pull or rip (something) apart or to pieces
