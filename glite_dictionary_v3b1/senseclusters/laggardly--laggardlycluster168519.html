laggardly

adjective, adverb
slow or delayed
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlæɡərdli / (AmE), / ˈlæɡədli / (BrE)
slow or delayed in action or response; in a slow or sluggish way

Examples

A laggardly response
The committee's laggardly decision-making process frustrated everyone involved.
His laggardly approach to completing assignments often resulted in missed deadlines.
Despite reminders, the team remained laggardly in updating the project files.
The committee responded laggardly to the urgent requests for assistance.
He finished his homework laggardly, distracted by the television in the background.
The train moved laggardly through the countryside after the heavy snowfall.
