rock rose

noun
flowering plant of the genus Helianthemum
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑk ˌroʊz / (AmE), / ˈrɒk ˌrəʊz / (BrE)
any of various small flowering plants of the genus Helianthemum, bearing rose-like yellow, white, or reddish flowers and typically growing in rocky places.
