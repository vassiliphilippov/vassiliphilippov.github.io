Picturephone

proper noun
AT&T video telephone
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪktʃɚˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃəfəʊn / (BrE)
the trademarked name of AT&T's commercial video-telephone service and device introduced in the 1960s, allowing users to see and hear each other during calls

Examples

Picturephone video call
The Picturephone was a groundbreaking invention showcased at the 1964 New York World's Fair.
Few people could afford a Picturephone when it was first released due to its high cost.
The concept behind the Picturephone paved the way for modern video calling technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: videophone - a telephone with a video display for two-way visual communication
