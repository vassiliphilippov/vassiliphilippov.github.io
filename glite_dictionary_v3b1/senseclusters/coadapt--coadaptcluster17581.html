coadapt

verb
to adapt together
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊəˈdæpt / (AmE), / ˌkəʊəˈdæpt / (BrE)
to change or adjust together so that two or more organisms, people, or systems work better with each other

Examples

As their partnership grew, both companies coadapted their strategies for mutual benefit.
