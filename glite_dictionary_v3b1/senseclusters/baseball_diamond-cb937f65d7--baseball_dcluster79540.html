baseball diamond

noun
the infield area of a baseball field
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsˌbɔl ˈdaɪmənd / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl ˈdaɪəmənd / (BrE)
the square-shaped area of a baseball field formed by the three bases and home plate, where much of the action occurs during a game
