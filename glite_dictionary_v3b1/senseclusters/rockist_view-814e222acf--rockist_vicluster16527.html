rockist view

noun
belief prioritizing rock music over others
7.9 7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈrɑː.kɪst vjuː/ /; / /ˈrɑː.kɪst vuː/ (AmE), /ˈrɒk.ɪst vjuː/ /; / /ˈrɒk.ɪst vuː/ (BrE)
An attitude or belief that regards rock music as superior to other genres, often dismissing pop, electronic, or other styles as less authentic or valuable.

Examples

A rockist view on music
Her rockist view often leads to heated debates about the merits of modern pop.
In the essay, he challenges the rockist view that only guitar-based bands make authentic music.
Many critics accuse the magazine of promoting a rockist view by seldom reviewing hip-hop albums.
