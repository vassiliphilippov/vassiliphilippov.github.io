nonconference

noun
informal meeting
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈkɑnfərəns / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkɒnfərəns / (BrE)
an informal meeting or gathering that is not organized as an official conference

Examples

Team nonconference
The team decided to hold a nonconference to brainstorm ideas in a relaxed setting.
Instead of a formal event, the organizers planned a nonconference for community members to share experiences.
Many researchers prefer a nonconference because it encourages open discussion and networking.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conference - a formal meeting for discussion, often with many participants
  • synonym: workshop - a meeting for discussion or practical work on a particular subject
