Judaize

verb
to adopt Jewish customs
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːdəˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːdəˌaɪz / (BrE)
to adopt Jewish customs, beliefs, or practices, or to make something more Jewish in character

Examples

To Judaize festivals
The community began to Judaize their festivals by incorporating traditional Jewish rituals.
Some leaders tried to Judaize the local cuisine by introducing kosher food laws.
Over time, the group chose to Judaize their practices to align more closely with Jewish traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convert - to change one's religion or beliefs
