mailbag

noun
bag used for carrying mail
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪlˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈmeɪlˌbaɡ / (BrE)
a large, strong bag used for carrying and transporting mail

Examples

A heavy mailbag
After the holiday rush, the mailbag was noticeably heavier than usual.
She reached into the mailbag to sort through the letters and packages.
The postal worker slung the mailbag over his shoulder before heading out on his route.
A torn mailbag was found near the side of the road after the robbery.
After collecting the morning post, he emptied the mailbag onto his desk.
The letter carrier slung a mailbag full of letters over her shoulder.
