deglaze

verb
dissolve pan juices with liquid
Pronunciation: / diˈɡleɪz / (AmE), / diˈɡleɪz / (BrE)
to dissolve the browned bits of food left in a pan after cooking by adding liquid and stirring, typically to make a sauce

Examples

To deglaze the pan
After sautéing the chicken, she added wine to deglaze the pan and create a flavorful sauce.
Chefs often deglaze pans with broth or vinegar to capture every bit of flavor.
The recipe instructs you to deglaze the skillet before adding the vegetables.
