contentless

adjective
empty; lacking contents
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑntɛntləs / (AmE), / ˈkɒntɛntləs / (BrE)
having no contents; empty or lacking any material or items inside.

Examples

Contentless folder
The folder was completely contentless after I deleted all the files.
She handed me a contentless envelope, which made me suspicious.
After the move, the shelves in the living room were contentless and bare.

Related Terms

  • synonym: empty - containing nothing; not filled or occupied
  • antonym: full - containing as much as possible; not empty
