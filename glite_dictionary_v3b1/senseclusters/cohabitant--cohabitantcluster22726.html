cohabitant

noun
unmarried live-in partner
6.1
Pronunciation: / koʊˈhæbɪtənt / (AmE), / kəʊˈhæbɪtənt / (BrE)
a person who lives with another in a romantic or sexual relationship without being married, often sharing a household.

Examples

Unmarried cohabitant
After five years as a cohabitant, he felt ready to discuss marriage with his partner.
The law now recognizes the rights of a cohabitant in property disputes.
She and her cohabitant share all household expenses equally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partner - a person with whom one shares a romantic relationship
  • antonym: spouse - a husband or wife in a marriage
