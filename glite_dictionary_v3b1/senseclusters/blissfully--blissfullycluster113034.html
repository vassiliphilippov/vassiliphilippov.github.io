blissfully

adverb
in a blissful, joyful way
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪsfəli / (AmE), / ˈblɪsfəli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows great happiness or joy; without awareness of trouble or danger.

Examples

Blissfully happy
After the wedding, they were blissfully happy on their honeymoon.
He remained blissfully unaware of the chaos unfolding around him.
She lay blissfully in the sun, listening to the birds singing in the trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joyfully - in a joyful manner
  • antonym: miserably - in a miserable or unhappy manner
