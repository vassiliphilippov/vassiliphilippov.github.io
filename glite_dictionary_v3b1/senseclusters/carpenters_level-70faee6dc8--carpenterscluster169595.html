carpenter's level

noun
tool with a built-in spirit level
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːr.pən.tɚz ˌlɛv.əl / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pən.təz ˌlɛv.əl / (BrE)
a straight, usually rectangular tool with a built-in spirit level, used to check if surfaces are horizontal or vertical

Examples

A carpenter's level tool
A carpenter's level is an essential tool for framing walls and doors.
Before installing the shelf, she checked the position with a carpenter's level.
He placed a carpenter's level on the beam to make sure it was perfectly horizontal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spirit level - tool for indicating whether a surface is horizontal or vertical
