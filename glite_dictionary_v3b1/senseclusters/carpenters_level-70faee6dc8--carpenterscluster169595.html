carpenter's level

noun
tool with a built-in spirit level
3.7 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀêr.p…ôn.t…öz Àål…õv.…ôl / (AmE), / Ààk…ëÀê.p…ôn.t…ôz Àål…õv.…ôl / (BrE)
a straight, usually rectangular tool with a built-in spirit level, used to check if surfaces are horizontal or vertical

Examples

A carpenter's level tool
A carpenter's level is an essential tool for framing walls and doors.
Before installing the shelf, she checked the position with a carpenter's level.
He placed a carpenter's level on the beam to make sure it was perfectly horizontal.

Synonyms

  • spirit level
    4.1 0.05/m
    a spirit level is a sealed tube with a bubble that shows exact horizontals, while a carpenter's level is the straight tool housing that tube.
  • level
    3.2 13/m
    a level is the common bubble tool used to check horizontals, while a carpenter's level is the larger straight tool with that bubble built in.
