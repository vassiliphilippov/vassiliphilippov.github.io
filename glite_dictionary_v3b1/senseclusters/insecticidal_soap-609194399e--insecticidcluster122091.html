insecticidal soap

noun
soap solution for killing insects
5.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌsɛktɪˈsaɪdəl soʊp / (AmE), / ɪnˌsɛktɪˈsaɪdəl səʊp / (BrE)
a soap-based solution used to control or kill insects, especially pests on plants
