frontman

noun
band leader
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌntˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈfrʌntˌmæn / (BrE)
the main singer or leader of a band or musical group, often the public face of the group

Examples

Band frontman
The frontman of the band greeted the audience before the first song.
After the concert, fans lined up to meet the charismatic frontman.
She dreamed of becoming the frontman for a famous rock group one day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bandleader - the leader of a musical group
  • synonym: lead singer - the main vocalist in a band
  • antonym: member - a person who belongs to a group or band
