board foot

noun
unit of volume for timber
Pronunciation: / bɔrd fʊt / (AmE), / bɔːd fʊt / (BrE)
a unit of volume for timber, equal to a piece of wood 1 foot long, 1 foot wide, and 1 inch thick

Examples

One board foot
