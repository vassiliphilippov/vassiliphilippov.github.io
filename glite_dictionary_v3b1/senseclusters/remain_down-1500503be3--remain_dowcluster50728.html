remain down

verb
stay down during referee's count (boxing)
3.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmeɪn daʊn / (AmE), / rɪˈmeɪn daʊn / (BrE)
(of a boxer) to stay on the ground without standing up while the referee counts to ten, resulting in being counted out of the match.

Examples

To remain down after count
After the heavy blow, the boxer chose to remain down as the referee counted to ten.
Despite the crowd's encouragement, he decided to remain down and lose the match by knockout.
The fighter could not get up and remained down until the count was finished.

Related Terms

  • antonym: get up - to stand up from the ground in boxing
