fludarabine

noun
chemotherapy drug
Pronunciation: / fluˈdærəˌbiːn / (AmE), / fluˈdærəˌbiːn / (BrE)
a drug used in chemotherapy to treat certain types of cancer, especially blood cancers like leukemia

Examples

Fludarabine chemotherapy drug
The doctor prescribed fludarabine to help treat the patient's leukemia.
Some patients may experience side effects when taking fludarabine as part of their chemotherapy.
Researchers are studying how fludarabine can be combined with other drugs for better results.
