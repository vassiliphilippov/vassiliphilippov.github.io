noninstructional

adjective
not related to teaching
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪnˈstrʌkʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪnˈstrʌkʃənəl / (BrE)
not related to teaching or the act of giving instruction

Examples

Noninstructional staff
The school hired more noninstructional staff to help with administration and maintenance.
Budget cuts affected several noninstructional programs, such as after-school care and transportation.
Her duties are strictly noninstructional, focusing on office management rather than classroom teaching.
