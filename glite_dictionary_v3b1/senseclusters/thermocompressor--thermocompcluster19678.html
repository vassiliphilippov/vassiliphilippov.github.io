thermocompressor

noun
device for compressing vapor or gas using steam
4.5 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜrmoʊkəmˈprɛsər / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməkəmˈprɛsə / (BrE)
A device that uses high-pressure steam or gas to entrain and compress a lower-pressure vapor, commonly used in industrial and engineering processes to increase pressure or recover energy.

Examples

A thermocompressor device
A thermocompressor works by mixing high-pressure steam with low-pressure vapor to boost overall pressure.
Engineers chose a thermocompressor for the process to recover energy from waste steam.
The factory installed a new thermocompressor to improve the efficiency of its steam system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ejector - a device that uses a jet (usually of steam or air) to remove or move fluids or gases
  • synonym: injector - a device that injects fluid using the velocity of another fluid
