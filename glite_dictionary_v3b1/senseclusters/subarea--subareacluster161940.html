subarea

noun
smaller division
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈɛəriə / (BrE)
a smaller part or division within a larger area, field, or subject

Examples

A subarea of park
Each subarea of the park has its own unique features.
The research project was divided into several subareas to make the work more manageable.
Students were assigned to a specific subarea of the library for their group study.
Her thesis explored a subarea of cognitive psychology that had not been widely studied.
Each subarea of the park is managed by a different team of rangers.
The conference included sessions on every major subarea of artificial intelligence.
