thunderstruck

adjective
extremely surprised or shocked
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌndɚˌstrʌk / (AmE), / ˈθʌndəˌstrʌk / (BrE)
Extremely surprised or astonished; so shocked that one is unable to react.

Examples

To be thunderstruck
He stood thunderstruck, unable to believe the good news he had just heard.
She was thunderstruck when she saw her missing dog at the door after weeks.
The whole audience was thunderstruck by the magician's final trick.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astonished - greatly surprised or impressed
  • synonym: dumbfounded - unable to speak because of shock or surprise
  • antonym: unfazed - not perturbed or affected by surprise or shock
