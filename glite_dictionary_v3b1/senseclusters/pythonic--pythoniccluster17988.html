pythonic

adjective
snake-like qualities
9.8
Pronunciation: / paɪˈθɑnɪk / (AmE), / paɪˈθɒnɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a python, especially in movement or appearance

Examples

Pythonic movement
The dancer's pythonic movements captivated the audience with their slow, winding grace.
A pythonic pattern was painted along the length of the ceremonial staff.
He watched the rope fall in a pythonic curve to the ground.
