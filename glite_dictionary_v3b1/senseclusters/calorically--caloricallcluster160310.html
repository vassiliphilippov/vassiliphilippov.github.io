calorically

adverb
relating to calories
6.7
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɔrɪkli / (AmE), / kəˈlɒrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the amount of calories or energy in food or drink

Examples

Calorically dense food
The dessert is calorically rich, so it should be eaten in moderation.
Some drinks are calorically dense even though they seem light.
He tries to eat calorically balanced meals to maintain his weight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nutritionally - in a way that relates to nutrition or nutrients
