portmanteau

noun
word formed by blending two words
Pronunciation: / pɔrtˈmæntoʊ / (AmE), / pɔːtˈmæntəʊ / (BrE)
a word formed by blending parts of two other words and combining their meanings, such as 'brunch' from 'breakfast' and 'lunch'

Examples

A portmanteau word
'Blog' is a portmanteau of 'web' and 'log', now widely used online.
She explained that 'brunch' is a classic portmanteau blending 'breakfast' and 'lunch'.
The word portmanteau combines two existing words to express a new idea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blend - a word created by combining parts of two words
