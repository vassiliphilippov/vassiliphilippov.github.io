portmanteau

noun
word formed by blending two words
9.9 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑːr.mæn.toʊ/ /; / /ˈpɔːr.mæn.toʊ/ (AmE), /ˈpɔːt.mæn.təʊ/ (BrE)
