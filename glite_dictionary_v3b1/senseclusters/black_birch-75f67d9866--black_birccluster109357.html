black birch

noun
birch tree with reddish-brown bark
6.9 5.4
Pronunciation: / blæk bɝtʃ / (AmE), / blæk bɜːtʃ /; / blak bɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a species of birch tree native to eastern North America, known for its dark or reddish-brown bark, aromatic oil, and hard wood
