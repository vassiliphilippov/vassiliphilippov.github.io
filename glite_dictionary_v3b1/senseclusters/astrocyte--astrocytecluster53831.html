astrocyte

noun
type of neuroglial (glial) cell in the nervous system
Pronunciation: / ˈæstroʊˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈæstrəʊˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a star-shaped cell of the nervous system that supports and protects neurons, found especially in the brain and spinal cord

Examples

A astrocyte cell
An astrocyte helps regulate the balance of chemicals in the brain's environment.
Researchers observed that astrocytes play a crucial role in repairing neural tissue after injury.
The unique star-shaped structure of an astrocyte is essential for its function in the central nervous system.
