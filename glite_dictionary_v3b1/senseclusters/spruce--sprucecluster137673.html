spruce

noun
soft wood from spruce trees, used in timber
6.7
Pronunciation: / spruːs / (AmE), / spruːs / (BrE)
the light, soft, and moderately strong wood of spruce trees, especially used for timber and millwork

Examples

Spruce wood panel
Most guitars use spruce for the soundboard due to its resonant qualities.
The beams in the cabin were made from spruce because of its light weight and durability.
They decided to use spruce for the window frames as it is easy to work with.

Related Terms

  • antonym: oak - wood from oak trees, known for hardness and strength
  • synonym: pine - the wood from pine trees, also used for timber
