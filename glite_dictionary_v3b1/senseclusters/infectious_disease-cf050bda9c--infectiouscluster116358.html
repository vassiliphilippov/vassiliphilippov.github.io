infectious disease

noun

Examples

Synonyms

  • communicable disease
    3.6 0.4/m
    communicable disease is a term for any illness that spreads between people or animals, while infectious disease adds the idea of germs causing it.
  • contagious disease
    3.2 0.4/m
    contagious disease spreads easily by contact between people, while infectious disease focuses on illnesses caused by germs that can also spread.
  • infection
    3.1 50/m
    infection is the process or condition of germs in the body, while infectious disease names the illness that can be passed to others.
