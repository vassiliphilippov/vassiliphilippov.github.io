sabino

noun
Mexican cypress tree
Pronunciation: / saˈbinoʊ / (AmE), / saˈbiːnəʊ / (BrE)
a large cypress tree (Taxodium mucronatum) native to Mexico and the southwestern United States, known for its massive trunk

Examples

Ancient sabino tree
The ancient sabino near the plaza was taller than the nearby church and drew many visitors.
Many people gather under the shade of the old sabino during local festivals.
The sabino is famous for its wide trunk and long lifespan in Mexican folklore.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cypress - a type of coniferous tree with scale-like leaves
