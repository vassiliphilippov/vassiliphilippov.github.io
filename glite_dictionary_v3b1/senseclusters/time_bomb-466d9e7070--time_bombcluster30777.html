time bomb

noun
problem that will become dangerous
5.7
Pronunciation: / taɪm bɑm / (AmE), / taɪm bɒm / (BrE)
a situation or problem that will become very serious or dangerous in the future if not dealt with

Examples

An economic time bomb
Environmental neglect is a time bomb for future generations.
The lack of regular maintenance is a time bomb waiting to explode.
Unchecked inequality could turn the city into a social time bomb.
Many experts warn that climate change is a time bomb for future generations.
Rising tensions between the groups have turned the city into a time bomb.
Unchecked financial debt can be a time bomb for businesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: powder keg - a situation likely to become violent or dangerous
  • synonym: ticking time bomb - a situation that is about to become very dangerous
