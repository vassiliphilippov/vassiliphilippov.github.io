Nephelium mutabile

noun
East Indian fruit tree; close to rambutan
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɪˈfiliəm mjuˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / nɪˈfiːliəm mjuːˈteɪbəli / (BrE)
An East Indian fruit tree of the soapberry family, bearing fruit similar to but sweeter than that of the rambutan.

Examples

Nephelium mutabile fruit
Growers are starting to cultivate Nephelium mutabile as a premium export crop due to its unique flavor.
The Nephelium mutabile yields a fruit that is prized for its sweetness across Southeast Asia.
Unlike the rambutan, Nephelium mutabile produces fruit that many consider even more delicious.
