blackly

adverb
in a grim way
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈblækli / (AmE), / ˈblækli / (BrE)
in a dark, grim, or gloomy manner, often suggesting bitterness or irony

Examples

To smile blackly
He smiled blackly as he recounted the misfortunes that had befallen him.
The comedian joked blackly about topics that made the audience uncomfortable.
She stared blackly at the empty street, lost in her somber thoughts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy or optimistic manner
  • synonym: grimly - in a serious, harsh, or forbidding manner
