súgán

noun
straw rope (Irish)
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːɡɑːn / (AmE), / ˈsuːɡɑːn / (BrE)
a rope made by twisting straw, traditionally used in Ireland for making seats, baskets, or bindings

Examples

A súgán rope
The craftsman wove a sturdy súgán to repair the old kitchen chair.
In rural Ireland, people often used súgán for making baskets and tying bundles.
She admired the traditional súgán seat on the antique wooden stool.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rope - a strong, thick line made by twisting together strands of fiber
