postsecondary

adjective
after high school
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈsɛkəndɛri / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈsɛkəndəri / (BrE)
relating to education that takes place after high school, such as college or university studies

Examples

Postsecondary education
She plans to attend a postsecondary institution after graduating from high school.
Many jobs now require some form of postsecondary education or training.
The government increased funding for postsecondary programs to help more students access college.

Related Terms

  • antonym: secondary - relating to education at the high school level
  • synonym: tertiary - relating to third-level or higher education after secondary school
