postsecondary

adjective
after high school
3.7
Pronunciation: /poʊst.ˈsek.ən.driː/ (AmE), /pəʊst.ˈsek.ən.driː/ (BrE)

Examples

Postsecondary education

Many jobs now require some form of postsecondary education or training.
The government increased funding for postsecondary programs to help more students access college.
