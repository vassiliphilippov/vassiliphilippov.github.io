catapult into the spotlight

verb
suddenly become widely noticed
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkædəˌpʌlt ˈɪntu ðə ˈspaɪtˌlɑɪt / (AmE), / ˈkatəˌpʌlt ˈɪntuː ðə ˈspaɪtˌlaɪt / (BrE)
to cause someone or something to suddenly become the center of public attention or interest

Examples

To catapult into the spotlight
Controversy can catapult a brand into the spotlight, for better or worse.
Her breakthrough role in the movie would catapult her into the spotlight overnight.
The viral video catapulted the unknown musician into the spotlight within days.
