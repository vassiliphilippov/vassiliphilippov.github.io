involved

adjective
enveloped or surrounded by
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɑlvd / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɒlvd / (BrE)
surrounded or enveloped by something, especially in a way that is thick or difficult to see through; wrapped up in

Examples

Involved in mist
A sense of mystery clung to the old house, involved in mist at dawn.
His intentions were involved in secrecy, hidden from everyone around him.
The mountain peak was involved in a thick blanket of clouds all morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enveloped - completely surrounded or enclosed
  • antonym: exposed - not covered or protected; visible
