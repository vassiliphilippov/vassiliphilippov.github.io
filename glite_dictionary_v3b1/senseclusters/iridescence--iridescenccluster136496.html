iridescence

noun
rainbow-like appearance of shifting colors
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˈdɛsəns / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈdɛsns / (BrE)
a quality of showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles, often resulting in a rainbow-like or shimmering effect

Examples

Beautiful iridescence colors
Peacock feathers are admired for their beautiful iridescence under sunlight.
The artist tried to capture the iridescence of the insect's wings in her painting.
The soap bubble shone with an exquisite iridescence as it floated through the air.
