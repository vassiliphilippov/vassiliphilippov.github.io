offworld

noun
space settlement
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfˌwɜrld / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌwɜːld / (BrE)
a settlement, facility, or colony located in outer space or on a planet other than Earth

Examples

A Mars offworld
The first permanent offworld was established on Mars in the late 21st century.
Scientists are studying how life adapts in an offworld environment far from Earth.
Supplies are regularly shipped from Earth to the offworld orbiting the moon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colony - a group of people living in a new territory
  • synonym: space station - a large structure in space where people can live and work
