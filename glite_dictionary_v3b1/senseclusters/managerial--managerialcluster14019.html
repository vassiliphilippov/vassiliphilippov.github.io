managerial

adjective
related to management or managers
Pronunciation: / ˌmænəˈdʒɪriəl / (AmE), / ˌmænəˈdʒɪəriəl / (BrE)
relating to the activities, responsibilities, or duties of managers or management.

Examples

Managerial duties
A managerial position requires strong decision-making abilities.
She developed her managerial skills during her time as a team leader.
The promotion meant an increase in managerial responsibilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrative - relating to the organization and running of a business, school, or other institution
