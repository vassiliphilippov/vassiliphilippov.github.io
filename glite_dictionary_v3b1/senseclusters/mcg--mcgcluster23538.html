mcg

noun
abbreviation for microgram (µg)
Pronunciation: / ɛm si dʒi / (AmE), / ɛm siː dʒiː / (BrE)
a microgram; a unit of mass equal to one millionth (1/1,000,000) of a gram, used especially in medicine and science

Examples

Total mcg amount
Label the container with the total mcg of the chemical present.
The doctor prescribed a daily dose of 500 mcg of vitamin B12.
This medication contains 25 mcg of the active ingredient per tablet.
