reoccur

verb
to happen again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈkɜr / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈkɜː / (BrE)
to happen again, especially after some time has passed

Examples

Symptoms reoccur often
If the symptoms reoccur, you should contact your doctor immediately.
Flooding may reoccur in the area if the drainage system is not improved.
The same issue tends to reoccur every few months despite our efforts.

The same issue reoccurred after the software update was installed.

If the pain should reoccur, please contact your doctor immediately.
Flooding may reoccur in the area if heavy rains continue.
The same technical issue could reoccur if the software is not updated.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: occur - to happen or take place
  • synonym: recur - to happen again, especially at regular intervals
  • synonym: repeat - to do or happen again
