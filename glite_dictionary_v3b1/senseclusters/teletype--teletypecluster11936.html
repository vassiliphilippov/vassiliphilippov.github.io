teletype

noun
teletyped message
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌtaɪp / (BrE)
a message sent or received using a teletype machine, especially in the form of printed text over long distances

Examples

A teletype message
The newsroom received a teletype with breaking news from overseas.
He read the urgent teletype aloud to the entire office staff.
Before email, important updates often arrived by teletype late at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fax - a message sent by facsimile machine
  • synonym: telegram - a message sent by telegraph
