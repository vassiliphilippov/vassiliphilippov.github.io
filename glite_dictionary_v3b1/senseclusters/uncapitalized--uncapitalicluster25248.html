uncapitalized

adjective
without financial capital
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈkæpɪtəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkæpɪtəlaɪzd / (BrE)
lacking sufficient financial capital or investment to operate or expand a business or project

Examples

Uncapitalized business
The new company remained uncapitalized and struggled to pay its employees on time.
Many small businesses fail because they are uncapitalized from the start.
After the investors withdrew, the project was left uncapitalized and could not continue.

Related Terms

  • antonym: capitalized - provided with sufficient financial capital
  • synonym: underfunded - not having enough money to operate effectively
