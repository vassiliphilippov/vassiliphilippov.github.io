laceleaf

noun
plant with lacy leaves
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪsˌliːf / (AmE), / ˈleɪsˌliːf / (BrE)
a plant with finely divided, delicate leaves that resemble lace, often grown for decorative purposes

Examples

Decorative laceleaf plant
The delicate laceleaf stood out among the other plants in the conservatory.
Gardeners often choose laceleaf for its attractive, finely cut foliage.
A single laceleaf in a vase can add elegance to any room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthurium - a tropical plant sometimes called laceleaf
  • synonym: fern - a plant with feathery, divided leaves
