motley

noun
diverse or varied collection
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑtli / (AmE), / ˈmɒtli / (BrE)
A collection or group consisting of many different types or kinds that are often varied or incongruous.

Examples

A motley of objects
On her desk was a motley of trinkets, books, and half-finished sketches.
The club attracts a motley of locals, students, and curious tourists each night.
The festival gathered a motley of artists, performers, and food vendors from around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assortment - a collection or variety of different things
  • synonym: medley - a varied mixture of people or things
  • synonym: variety - the quality or state of being diverse; a number of different types
