glace

verb
to coat with glaze
8.4
Pronunciation: /gleɪs/ /; / /glæ.ˈseɪ/ (AmE), /gleɪs/ /; / /glæ.ˈseɪ/ (BrE)
to coat food with a glossy sugar or syrup glaze, or to reduce a liquid until it forms a thick, shiny coating

Examples

To glace carrots
The chef will glace the carrots with a sweet syrup before serving them.
To finish the dessert, she carefully glaced the fruit with a thin layer of sugar.
After reducing the sauce, he used it to glace the roasted vegetables for extra shine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frost - to cover a cake or pastry with icing
  • synonym: glaze - to cover food with a smooth, shiny coating
