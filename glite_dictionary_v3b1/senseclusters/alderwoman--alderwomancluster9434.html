alderwoman

noun
female member of a city council
Pronunciation: / ˈɔldɚˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈɔːldəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A female member of a municipal legislative body, such as a city council.

Examples

An alderwoman in council
After serving as an alderwoman for two terms, she ran for mayor.
Several residents contacted their alderwoman to discuss the proposed changes to the park.
The city council elected its first alderwoman this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alderman - a member of a municipal legislative body, traditionally male
