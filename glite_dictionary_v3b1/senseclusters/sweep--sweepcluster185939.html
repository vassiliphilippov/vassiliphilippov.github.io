sweep

verb
clean a surface with a broom
1.2
Pronunciation: / swip / (AmE), / swiːp / (BrE)

Please sweep the kitchen floor before our guests arrive.
She swept the dust into a pile and threw it away.
Could you sweep the kitchen before our guests arrive?
He is sweeping the classroom floor after school.
She swept the front porch to remove the fallen leaves.
