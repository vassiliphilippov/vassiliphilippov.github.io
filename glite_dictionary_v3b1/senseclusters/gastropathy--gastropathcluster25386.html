gastropathy

noun
stomach lining disease
8.8
Pronunciation: / ɡæsˈtrɑːpəθi / (AmE), / ɡæsˈtrɒpəθi / (BrE)
a disease or disorder affecting the lining of the stomach

Examples

Gastropathy symptoms
The endoscopy revealed signs of gastropathy in the patient's stomach lining.
Chronic use of certain medications can lead to gastropathy over time.
Doctors monitor patients with diabetes for possible gastropathy symptoms.
The doctor diagnosed the patient with gastropathy after reviewing the endoscopy results.
Chronic use of certain painkillers can lead to gastropathy over time.
Unlike gastritis, gastropathy does not involve significant inflammation of the stomach lining.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gastritis - inflammation of the stomach lining
  • synonym: gastrosis - any disease of the stomach
  • synonym: peptic ulcer - a sore in the lining of the stomach or upper intestine
