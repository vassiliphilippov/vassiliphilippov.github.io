firefighting

adjective
relating to fire control
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌfaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌfaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to the equipment, methods, or work used to put out fires or control fire emergencies

Examples

Firefighting equipment
The city invested in new firefighting equipment to improve emergency response times.
She attended a firefighting training course to learn how to handle fire emergencies.
Modern firefighting techniques have greatly reduced the risk of large urban fires.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fireproof - resistant to catching fire
  • antonym: flammable - easily set on fire
