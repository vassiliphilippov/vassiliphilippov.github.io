externa

noun
outer anatomical layer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛkˈstɜrnə / (AmE), / ɛkˈstɜːnə / (BrE)
the outer layer or external part of an organ or structure, especially in anatomy

Examples

The externa layer
The externa of the artery provides structural support.
Damage to the externa can affect the function of the entire blood vessel.
In some organs, the externa is composed mainly of connective tissue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adventitia - the outermost connective tissue covering of an organ or vessel
  • antonym: interna - the inner layer or internal part of an organ or structure
