give the shirt off someone's back

verb
be extremely generous or selfless
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv ðə ʃɜrt ɔf ˈsʌmwʌnz bæk / (AmE), / ɡɪv ðə ˈʃɜːt ɒf ˈsʌmwənz bæk / (BrE)
to be extremely generous or selfless, willing to give away almost everything to help others

Examples

To give the shirt off someone's back
If I ever needed support, I know Tom would give the shirt off his back for me.
My grandfather would give the shirt off his back to someone in need.
She's so kind that she'd give the shirt off her back if you asked for help.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generous - willing to give more of something than is strictly necessary or expected
  • antonym: selfish - lacking consideration for others; concerned chiefly with one's own personal profit or pleasure
  • synonym: selfless - concerned more with the needs and wishes of others than with one's own; unselfish
