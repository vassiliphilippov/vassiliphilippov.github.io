nuttery

noun
place for nut trees
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtəri / (AmE), / ˈnʌtəri / (BrE)
a place where nut trees are grown or where nuts are processed

Examples

A nuttery garden
The old estate included a large nuttery filled with chestnut and hazelnut trees.
They visited the local nuttery to learn about how different nuts are harvested and processed.
A new nuttery was established to supply fresh walnuts to the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grove - a small group of trees, especially one planted for cultivation
  • synonym: orchard - a piece of land planted with fruit or nut trees
