Second Adventist

noun
member of a denomination expecting Christ's imminent return
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛk.ənd ædˈvɛn.tɪst / (AmE), / ˌsek.ənd adˈvɛn.tɪst / (BrE)
a member of a Christian denomination holding the belief that the imminent return of Christ is expected

Examples

A Second Adventist believer
Her grandfather was a devoted Second Adventist who often spoke of the coming of Christ.
Many Second Adventists gather annually to discuss their faith and the expectation of Christ's return.
The history curriculum included a chapter on the rise of the Second Adventist movement in America.
