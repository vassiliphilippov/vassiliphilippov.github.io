psychophysical

adjective
mind-body relationship
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊˈfɪzɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊˈfɪzɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the connection or interaction between mental and physical processes

Examples

Psychophysical responses
The study explored the psychophysical responses of participants to different types of music.
Yoga is believed to have many psychophysical benefits for both mind and body.
Researchers measured the psychophysical effects of sleep deprivation on cognitive performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychosomatic - relating to the influence of the mind on the body
