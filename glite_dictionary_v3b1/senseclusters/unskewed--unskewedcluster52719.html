unskewed

adjective
symmetric distribution
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskjuːd / (AmE), / ʌnˈskjuːd / (BrE)
having no skew; being roughly symmetric around the mean, especially in a statistical distribution or dataset

Examples

Unskewed distribution
The data appeared unskewed, making it suitable for standard statistical analysis.
An unskewed distribution is often easier to interpret and model.
After removing outliers, the results were nearly unskewed and centered around the mean.

Related Terms

  • antonym: skewed - having a distribution that is not symmetric around the mean
  • synonym: symmetric - having balanced or identical parts on both sides of a central line
