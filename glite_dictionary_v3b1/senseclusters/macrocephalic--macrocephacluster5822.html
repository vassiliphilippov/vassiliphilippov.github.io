macrocephalic

adjective
having an unusually large head
Pronunciation: / ˌmækrə səˈfælɪk / (AmE), / ˌmakrəʊsɪˈfalɪk / (BrE)
having an unusually large head, often due to a medical condition

Examples

A macrocephalic head
A macrocephalic measurement can indicate an underlying neurological issue.
Patients with certain genetic disorders may have a macrocephalic appearance.
The doctor noted that the infant appeared macrocephalic during the initial examination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: microcephalic - having an unusually small head
