pigeon pea

noun
tropical shrub with edible seeds
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪdʒən pi / (AmE), / ˈpɪdʒɪn piː / (BrE)
a tropical shrub (Cajanus cajan) with yellow flowers and edible seeds, widely grown for food and as a forage crop in warm regions

Examples

Pigeon pea plant
Farmers in India often plant pigeon pea as a drought-resistant crop.
Intercropping with pigeon pea helps enrich the soil with nitrogen.
The seeds of the pigeon pea are used in many traditional stews and curries.
