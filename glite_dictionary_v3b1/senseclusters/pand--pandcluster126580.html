pand

noun
item given as security
9.9
Pronunciation: / pænd / (AmE), / pænd / (BrE)
an item given as security for a loan; a pledge or pawn, chiefly in Scottish or archaic usage

Examples

A pand for a loan
The jeweller kept the necklace as a pand until the loan was repaid.
He left his watch as a pand with the moneylender in exchange for a small sum.
In old Scottish law, a pand was often required before granting a loan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pawn - an item given as security for a loan
  • synonym: pledge - something given as security for repayment of a loan
