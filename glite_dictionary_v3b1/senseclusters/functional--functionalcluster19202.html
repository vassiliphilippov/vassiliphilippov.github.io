functional

adjective
relating to how something operates or functions
1.3 3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋkʃənl / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋkʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the way something works or operates, especially in contrast to its physical structure or anatomy.

Examples

Functional impairment
Researchers observed a functional impairment that did not correspond to observable tissue damage.
Some hearing loss is classified as functional rather than structural.
The doctor diagnosed the patient with functional blindness despite normal eye anatomy.
