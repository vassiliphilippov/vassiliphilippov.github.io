phrasemaker

noun
creator of clever phrases
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪzˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈfreɪzˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who is skilled at inventing clever or memorable phrases, especially in speech or writing

Examples

A clever phrasemaker
The journalist was a brilliant phrasemaker whose headlines caught everyone's attention.
As a skilled phrasemaker, she could turn any idea into a catchy slogan.
Many admired the author's talent as a phrasemaker in his witty essays.
