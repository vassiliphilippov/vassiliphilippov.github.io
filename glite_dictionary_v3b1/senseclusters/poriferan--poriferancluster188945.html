poriferan

noun
primitive marine animal (sponge)
Pronunciation: / pɔˈrɪfərən / (AmE), / pɒˈrɪfərən / (BrE)
a primitive multicellular marine animal consisting of a porous body supported by a fibrous skeleton, belonging to the phylum Porifera; commonly known as a sponge

Examples

A poriferan sponge
A poriferan filters tiny particles from water by passing it through its porous body.
Marine biologists discovered several new species of poriferan on the coral reef.
The poriferan is among the simplest of all multicellular animals, lacking true tissues and organs.
