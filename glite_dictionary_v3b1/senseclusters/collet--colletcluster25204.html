collet

noun
band or collar holding a stone in jewelry
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.lɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒl.ɪt / (BrE)
A band or collar that securely holds an individual stone in a jewelry setting.

Examples

Jewelry collet
The diamond was set in a gold collet to enhance its brilliance.
The jeweler inspected the collet to ensure the stone was held firmly.
Vintage rings often feature a distinctive collet setting for the gemstone.
