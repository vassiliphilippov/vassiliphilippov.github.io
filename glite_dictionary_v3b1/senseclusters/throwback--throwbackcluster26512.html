throwback

adjective
resembling an earlier type or era
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊˌbæk / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) resembling an earlier type or displaying characteristics from a previous time; atavistic.

Examples

Throwback style
Her fashion style is a true throwback to the 1970s.
His manners are a throwback to a more formal era.
The building has a throwback charm reminiscent of Victorian architecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atavistic - relating to or characterized by reversion to something ancient or ancestral
