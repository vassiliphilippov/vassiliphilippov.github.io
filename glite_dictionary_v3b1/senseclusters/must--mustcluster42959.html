must

verb
to be required
1.0
Pronunciation: / mʌst / (AmE), / mʌst / (BrE)
to be required or necessary to do something according to rules, laws, or strong advice

Examples

To must follow rules
You must show your ID to enter the building.
All passengers must fasten their seat belts during takeoff and landing.
Students must submit their assignments by Friday to receive credit.
