functionalism

noun
psychological theory: mental processes serve adaptation
4.3 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfʌŋk.ʃə.nəl.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈfʌŋk.ʃə.nəl.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
