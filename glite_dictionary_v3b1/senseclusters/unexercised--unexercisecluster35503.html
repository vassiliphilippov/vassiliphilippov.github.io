unexercised

adjective
not physically trained
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈɛksərˌsaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈɛksəsaɪzd / (BrE)
not used or developed through physical activity or training, especially referring to muscles or fitness.

Examples

Unexercised muscles
After months of illness his leg muscles remained unexercised and weak.
The unexercised dog became restless and bored in the apartment.
Years of a sedentary lifestyle left his body unexercised and out of shape.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fit - in good physical condition due to exercise
  • synonym: inactive - not engaging in physical activity
