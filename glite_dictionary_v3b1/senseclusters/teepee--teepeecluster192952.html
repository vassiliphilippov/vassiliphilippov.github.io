teepee

noun
cone-shaped Native American tent
6.5 0.58/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆtiË.pi /; / ËˆtiËŒpi /; / ËˆtiËpi / (AmE), / ËˆtiË.piË /; / ËˆtiËpiË /; / ËˆtiËpi / (BrE)
A cone-shaped tent traditionally used by some North American Indigenous peoples, especially the Plains tribes, typically made of animal skins or fabric draped over wooden poles.

Examples
