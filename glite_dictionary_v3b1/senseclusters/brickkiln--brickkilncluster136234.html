brickkiln

noun
kiln for baking bricks
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪkˌkɪln / (AmE), / ˈbrɪkˌkɪln / (BrE)
a kiln used for firing and hardening bricks

Examples

A brickkiln oven
After collecting the clay, workers stacked it near the brickkiln to dry.
Smoke rose steadily from the brickkiln as the bricks were baked at a high temperature.
The old factory still had a brickkiln in the back, used for making handmade bricks.
