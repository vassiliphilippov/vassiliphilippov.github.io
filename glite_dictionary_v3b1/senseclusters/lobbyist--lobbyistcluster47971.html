lobbyist

noun
person who seeks to influence legislators
6.8 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈlɑː.biː.əst/ /; / /ˈlɑː.bɪ.ɪst/ (AmE), /ˈlɒb.iː.əst/ /; / /ˈlɒb.ɪ.ɪst/ (BrE)
