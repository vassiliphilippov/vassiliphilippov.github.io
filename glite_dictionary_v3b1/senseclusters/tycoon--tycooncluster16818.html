tycoon

noun
powerful businessperson
Pronunciation: / taɪˈkun / (AmE), / tʌɪˈkuːn / (BrE)
a person of great wealth, power, and influence in business

Examples

A tycoon in business
After years of hard work, he became a respected tycoon in the tech industry.
Many young entrepreneurs dream of becoming a tycoon in their chosen field.
The film stars an oil tycoon who will stop at nothing to expand his empire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magnate - a wealthy and influential businessperson, especially in a particular industry
  • synonym: mogul - an important or powerful person, especially in the media industry
