cast

noun
hard bandage for broken bones
1.8
Pronunciation: / kæst / (AmE), / kɑːst / (BrE)
A hard protective covering, typically made from plaster or fiberglass, applied to immobilize a broken bone during healing.

Examples

A broken arm cast
After breaking his arm, the doctor put a cast on it to keep it stable.
His friends signed their names on his cast with colorful markers.
She had to wear a cast for six weeks before the bone had healed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: splint - a rigid device used to prevent movement of a broken or injured limb
