circumfuse

verb
to pour or spread around
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜrkəmˈfjuz / (AmE), / ˌsɜːkəmˈfjuːz / (BrE)
to pour or spread around something; to surround with a substance

Examples

To circumfuse the air
A gentle perfume seemed to circumfuse the air, making the entire room fragrant.
Mist circumfused the valley at dawn, blurring the outlines of trees and hills.
The golden light circumfused the ancient hall, lending it an ethereal glow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diffuse - to spread widely
  • synonym: envelop - to surround completely
