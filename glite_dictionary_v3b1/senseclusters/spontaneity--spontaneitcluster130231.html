spontaneity

noun
the quality of being spontaneous
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌspɑːn.təˈneɪ.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌspɒn.təˈneɪ.ə.ti / (BrE)
the quality of being spontaneous, acting on impulse or natural feeling without constraint or premeditation

Examples

Natural spontaneity
Her spontaneity made every gathering lively and unpredictable.
I admire the spontaneity of small children as they play and explore.
Sometimes a little spontaneity can turn an ordinary weekend into a memorable adventure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deliberation - the quality of being carefully considered or planned
  • synonym: impulsiveness - the tendency to act on impulse rather than careful thought
