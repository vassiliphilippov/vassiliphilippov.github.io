rumble

noun
low, continuous deep noise
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌm.bəl / (AmE), / ˈrʌm.bəl / (BrE)
a loud, low, continuous, and deep sound or noise

Examples

Rumble of thunder
The distant rumble of thunder signaled an approaching storm.
There was a low rumble coming from the furnace in the basement.
We could feel the rumble of the train as it passed beneath our feet.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: hum - a low, steady continuous sound
  • synonym: roar - a full, deep, prolonged loud noise
  • synonym: thunder - a loud rumbling sound associated with storms
