americano

noun
Campari cocktail
6.3
Pronunciation: / əˌmɛrɪˈkɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / əˌmɛrɪˈkɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
a cocktail made with Campari, sweet vermouth, and soda water, usually served over ice with a slice of orange or lemon

Examples

A classic americano cocktail
She ordered an americano to enjoy while waiting for her friends at the bar.
The bartender prepared a classic americano with a slice of orange on top.
On a warm evening, he preferred a refreshing americano instead of a stronger cocktail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: martini - a cocktail made with gin and vermouth, garnished with an olive or lemon twist
  • synonym: negroni - a cocktail made with gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth
