Bundist

adjective
of the Bund movement
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊndɪst / (AmE), / ˈbʊndɪst / (BrE)
relating to the Jewish socialist Bund movement, its ideology, or its activities

Examples

Bundist ideas
The Bundist newspaper published articles in Yiddish to reach the Jewish working class.
Her Bundist beliefs shaped her activism for labor rights and cultural autonomy.
Many Bundist organizations promoted secular Jewish education in Eastern Europe.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Zionist - supporting the movement for the re-establishment of a Jewish homeland in Israel
  • synonym: socialist - supporting or relating to socialism
