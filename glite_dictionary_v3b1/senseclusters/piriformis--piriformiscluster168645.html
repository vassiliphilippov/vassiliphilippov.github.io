piriformis

noun
buttock muscle
9.9
Pronunciation: /pɪ.rɪ.ˈfɔːr.mɪs/ (AmE), /pɪ.rɪ.ˈfɔː.mɪs/ (BrE)
a small, pear-shaped muscle in the buttock that helps rotate the hip joint

Examples

The piriformis muscle
The physical therapist showed me stretches for my piriformis to relieve hip pain.
Injury to the piriformis can sometimes cause discomfort in the lower back or leg.
Runners often experience tightness in the piriformis after long training sessions.
