nonuniform

adjective
not homogeneous or consistent
1.0
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈjuː.nəˌfɔrm / (AmE), / nɒnˈjuː.nɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
not having the same form, quality, or character throughout; not homogeneous or consistent

Examples

A nonuniform pattern
Scientists observed a nonuniform concentration of chemicals in the solution.
The distribution of heat in the room was nonuniform, making some areas warmer than others.
The fabric’s color appeared nonuniform under different lighting conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homogeneous - of the same or similar kind throughout
  • antonym: uniform - having the same form or character throughout
