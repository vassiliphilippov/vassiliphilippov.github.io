favored

adjective
preferred and specially treated
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪvərd / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvəd / (BrE)
preferred above others and given special treatment or advantages

Examples

The favored son
He was always the favored son, receiving more attention than his siblings.
Of all the players, she was the favored candidate for the team captaincy.
The king's favored advisor enjoyed many privileges.
Certain students seem to get favoured treatment from the teacher.
She was always the favoured candidate for promotion among her colleagues.
The sunny side of the hill is the favoured spot for picnics.
