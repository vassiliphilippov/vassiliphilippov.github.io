pressing

noun
the act of applying pressure
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of applying pressure to something, especially by pushing or squeezing

Examples

Gentle pressing
A gentle pressing helped stop the bleeding from the wound.
At the pressing of a button, the machine started to operate.
The pressing of grapes is an important step in winemaking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pressure - the continuous physical force exerted on or against an object
  • antonym: release - the act of letting go or removing pressure
