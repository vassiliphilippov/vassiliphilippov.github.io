practice session

noun
period of training with repeated exercises
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpræk.tɪs ˌsɛʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈpræk.tɪs ˌseʃ.ən / (BrE)
a period of time set aside for systematic training by repeating an activity, often to improve skills or performance

Examples

A practice session
Before the concert, the orchestra held a final practice session to rehearse their pieces.
She improved quickly by attending each practice session with dedication and focus.
The coach scheduled a practice session every afternoon to prepare the team for the match.

Related Terms
