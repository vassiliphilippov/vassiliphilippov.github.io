jokingly

adverb
in a joking or playful manner
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒoʊkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊkɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is meant as a joke; not seriously

Examples

To jokingly say
He jokingly called himself the king of the office after getting a promotion.
She jokingly suggested we take a trip to the moon for the weekend.
The teacher jokingly warned the students that homework would be doubled if they kept talking.
He called his friend a genius, but only jokingly.
If I say something rude, please know that I mean it jokingly.
She jokingly suggested that we should all wear costumes to the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facetiously - in a way that treats serious issues with deliberately inappropriate humor
  • antonym: seriously - in a serious manner
