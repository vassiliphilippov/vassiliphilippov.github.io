asymbolia

noun
inability to interpret symbols
9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.sɪm.ˈboʊ.liː.ə/ /; / /eɪ.sɪm.ˈboʊ.liː.ə/ (AmE), /ə.sɪm.ˈbəʊ.liː.ə/ /; / /eɪ.sɪm.ˈbəʊ.liː.ə/ (BrE)
