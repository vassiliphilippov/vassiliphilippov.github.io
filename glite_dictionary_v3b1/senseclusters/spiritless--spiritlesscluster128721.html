spiritless

adjective
lacking energy or enthusiasm
1.5 4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtlɪs /; / ˈspɪrɪtləs / (BrE)
lacking energy, enthusiasm, or liveliness; showing little spirit or determination

Examples
