afforestation

noun
creating forest on non-forested land
5.4 6.0
Pronunciation: /ə.fɔːr.ɪ.ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.fɑːr.ɪ.ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ə.fɒr.ɪ.ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.fɔːr.ɪ.ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of planting trees or sowing seeds on land that was not previously forested, to create a forest

Examples

Active afforestation efforts
Active afforestation efforts can help reduce carbon emissions over time.
Environmentalists advocate for large-scale afforestation to restore biodiversity.
The government has launched an afforestation program to combat desertification in the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deforestation - the removal of trees from a forested area
  • synonym: reforestation - restoration of forest to land that was recently deforested
