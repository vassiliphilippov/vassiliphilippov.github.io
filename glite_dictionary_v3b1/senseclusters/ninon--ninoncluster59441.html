ninon

noun
fine sheer silky fabric
Pronunciation: / nɪˈnɑn / (AmE), / nɪˈnɒn / (BrE)
a fine, strong, sheer fabric with a silky appearance, made from silk, rayon, or nylon, used especially for curtains and evening wear

Examples

Light ninon fabric
She chose pale pink ninon curtains to give the living room an airy feel.
The delicate gown was made from ivory ninon that draped beautifully.
The designer combined lace and ninon to create an elegant evening dress.
