drop off

verb
fall asleep unintentionally
Pronunciation: / drɑp ɔf / (AmE), / drɒp ɒf / (BrE)
to fall asleep, often unintentionally or for a short period

Examples

To drop off while reading
He tends to drop off if he's reading late at night.
I must have dropped off for a minute because I missed the start of the film.
She dropped off on the sofa while waiting for his call.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fall asleep - to enter into the state of sleep
  • antonym: wake up - to cease sleeping and become awake
