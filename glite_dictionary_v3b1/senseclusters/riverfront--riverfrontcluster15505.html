riverfront

adjective
beside a river
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvərˌfrʌnt / (AmE), / ˈrɪvəˌfrʌnt / (BrE)
located on or next to the land beside a river, especially describing buildings or areas along a riverbank

Examples

Riverfront hotel
They enjoyed dinner at a riverfront restaurant with a beautiful view of the water.
The city built a new riverfront park for families to relax and play.
Many tourists choose riverfront hotels for easy access to boat tours.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inland - away from the coast or a river
  • synonym: waterside - situated beside a body of water
