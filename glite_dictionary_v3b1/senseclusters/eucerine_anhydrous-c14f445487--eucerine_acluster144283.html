eucerine anhydrous

noun
water-free ointment base for skin treatments
9.1
Pronunciation: / juːˈsɛrɪn ˌænˈhaɪdrəs / (AmE), / juːˈsɛrɪn ˌanˈhʌɪdrəs / (BrE)
A water-free ointment base, commonly used for incorporating other medications for topical treatment of irritated or inflamed skin.

Examples

Eucerine anhydrous ointment base
Eucerine anhydrous is frequently used as a base in dermatological preparations.
Doctors may recommend eucerine anhydrous for patients with sensitive skin who cannot use standard creams.
You should store eucerine anhydrous in a cool place to maintain its effectiveness.
