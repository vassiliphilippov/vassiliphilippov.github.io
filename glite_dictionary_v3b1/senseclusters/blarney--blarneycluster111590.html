blarney

noun
flattering or persuasive talk
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑːrni / (AmE), / ˈblɑːni / (BrE)
talk that flatters or deceives in order to gain favor or advantage; smooth, persuasive flattery.

Examples

Sweet blarney
Don't let his charming blarney fool you into trusting him too quickly.
She tried to win the job with a bit of blarney during the interview.
The politician was full of blarney, making promises he couldn't keep.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flattery - excessive or insincere praise, especially to further one's own interests
