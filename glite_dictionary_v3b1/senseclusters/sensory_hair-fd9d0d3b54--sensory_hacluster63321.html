sensory hair

noun

A hairlike artificial sensor, often used on robots or electronic skin, that detects touch, airflow, vibration, or chemical cues by mimicking biological whiskers.

Examples

Robot sensory hair sensor
The robot's sensory hair detects subtle air flow changes to adjust its grip.
Researchers fitted the prosthetic hand with several sensory hairs to improve object detection.
When the drone flew close to foliage, its sensory hair warned the control system of nearby branches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whisker - a long projecting hair or bristle used by animals to sense the environment; inspiration for biomimetic sensors
