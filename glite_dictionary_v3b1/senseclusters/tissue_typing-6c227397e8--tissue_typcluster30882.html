tissue typing

noun
testing tissue compatibility before transplant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪʃu ˌtaɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪʃuː ˌtʌɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of testing tissue compatibility between a donor and a recipient before an organ transplant

Examples

Tissue typing test
Advances in tissue typing have greatly improved transplant success rates.
Before the kidney transplant, doctors performed tissue typing to ensure compatibility.
The lab specializes in tissue typing for bone marrow donors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crossmatch - a test to check compatibility between donor and recipient blood or tissue
  • synonym: organ transplant - surgical operation to transfer an organ from one person to another
