smut

verb
become infected with a fungal plant disease
Pronunciation: / smʌt / (AmE), / smʌt / (BrE)
to infect or be infected with smut, a fungal disease that produces black, powdery spores on plants

Examples

To smut corn plants
Farmers inspect their fields carefully so their corn does not smut after heavy rains.
If the barley smuts, it can ruin the entire harvest.
Much of the wheat crop smutted last year due to humid conditions.
If your corn becomes smutted, you must remove the infected plants immediately.
The damp weather caused the wheat fields to smut, ruining much of the harvest.
The farmer worried that the moisture would smut his crops before harvest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blight - suffer from a plant disease causing damage or death
  • synonym: wilt - to become limp or drooping, often due to disease or lack of water
