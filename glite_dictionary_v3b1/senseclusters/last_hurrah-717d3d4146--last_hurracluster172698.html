last hurrah

noun
a final effort or performance
Pronunciation: / læst həˈrɑ / (AmE), / lɑːst həˈrɑː / (BrE)
a final effort, event, or celebration before retirement, a big change, or the end of a period

Examples

The last hurrah party
After working in the industry for 40 years, his retirement party was truly his last hurrah.
She decided to run one more marathon as a last hurrah before turning fifty.
The band reunited for one last hurrah before disbanding for good.
Graduating seniors organized a last hurrah before everyone moved away for college.
The concert was the band's last hurrah before announcing their split.
We all wanted one last hurrah together before the end of summer.

  • synonym: swansong - a final act or performance before ending one's career
