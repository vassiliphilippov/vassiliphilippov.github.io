neoteric

noun
supporter of new ideas
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌniːəˈtɛrɪk / (AmE), / ˌniːəˈtɛrɪk / (BrE)
a person who supports or introduces new ideas, methods, or styles, especially in art or literature

Examples

Neoteric in art
As a neoteric, she dismissed traditional rules and experimented with free verse.
The art gallery featured works by several young neoterics challenging old conventions.
He was known as a neoteric in the field, always eager to try innovative techniques.
