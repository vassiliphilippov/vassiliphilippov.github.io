overexcitable

adjective
easily overstimulated
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪkˈsaɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪkˈsaɪtəbəl / (BrE)
easily becoming too excited or overly stimulated, often reacting strongly to minor events.

Examples

An overexcitable child
The overexcitable child found it hard to sit still during the quiet story time.
Our overexcitable dog barks at every visitor who comes to the door.
He became overexcitable whenever he heard news about his favorite sports team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not easily excited or upset
  • synonym: excitable - easily excited or stirred up
