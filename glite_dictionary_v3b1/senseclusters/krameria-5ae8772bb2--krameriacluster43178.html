Krameria

noun
medicinal plant root
9.9
Pronunciation: / krəˈmɪəriə / (AmE), / krəˈmɪəriə / (BrE)
the dried roots or herbal preparations from plants of the genus Krameria, used as an astringent or in traditional medicine

Examples

Krameria root powder
The herbalist recommended Krameria to help treat minor mouth irritations.
Traditional healers often use Krameria for its astringent properties in various remedies.
A tea made from Krameria roots is sometimes used to soothe sore throats.
