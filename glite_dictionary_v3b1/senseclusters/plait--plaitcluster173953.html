plait

noun
a braid or twist of hair
Pronunciation: / plæt / (AmE), / plæt / (BrE)
A length of hair that has been divided into three or more sections and twisted or braided together.

Examples

A hair plait
He admired the neat plait that kept her hair out of her face.
She wore her hair in a single long plait down her back.
The little girl's mother expertly tied her plaits before school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braid - a length of hair made by weaving three or more strands together
