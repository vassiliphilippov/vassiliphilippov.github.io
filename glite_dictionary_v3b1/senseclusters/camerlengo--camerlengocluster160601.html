camerlengo

noun
papal financial official
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkæmərˈlɛŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˌkæməlˈleŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a high-ranking official in the Roman Catholic Church responsible for managing papal property and finances, especially during a papal interregnum

Examples

Vatican camerlengo
The camerlengo played a crucial role in organizing the Vatican's finances after the pope's death.
During the papal interregnum, the camerlengo is responsible for the administration of the Holy See.
The new camerlengo was appointed to oversee the transition between popes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chamberlain - an official who manages the household of a sovereign or noble
