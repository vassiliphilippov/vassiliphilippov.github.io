biogeographically

adverb
by geographic distribution
9.8
Pronunciation: /baɪ.oʊ.dʒiː.ə.ˈgræf.ɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /baɪ.əʊ.dʒiː.ə.ˈgræf.ɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a way that relates to the geographic distribution of living organisms or ecosystems

Examples

To biogeographically limit species
The plant is biogeographically limited to certain mountain ranges in South America.
Researchers analyzed how species are biogeographically distributed across different continents.
The island is biogeographically unique due to its isolated location and rare species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geographically - in a way that relates to the physical features or location of an area
