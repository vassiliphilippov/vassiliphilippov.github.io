fearlessness

noun
lack of fear
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪrləsnəs /; / ˈfɪr.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfɪələsnɪs /; / ˈfɪə.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of not being afraid; a lack of fear, especially in difficult or dangerous situations

Examples

Remarkable fearlessness
He approached the daunting challenge with remarkable fearlessness.
Her fearlessness in the face of danger inspired everyone around her.
Many admired her fearlessness when she spoke out against injustice.
He admired her fearlessness when speaking out against injustice.
Her fearlessness in the face of adversity inspired everyone on the team.
The fearlessness of the firefighters saved many lives during the blaze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bravery - brave behavior or actions
  • synonym: courage - The ability to do something that frightens one; bravery.
  • antonym: cowardice - lack of bravery; excessive fear
  • antonym: timidity - Lack of courage or confidence; shyness.
