yaqona

noun
plant for kava drink
Pronunciation: / jɑːˈkoʊnə / (AmE), / jɑːˈkɒnə / (BrE)
a tropical plant (Piper methysticum) whose root is used to make the traditional kava drink in the Pacific Islands

Examples

Yaqona plant
Farmers in Fiji grow yaqona for both local use and export.
The ceremony began with the preparation of yaqona for the guests.
He explained the cultural importance of yaqona in island traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kava - a traditional drink made from the root of the yaqona plant
