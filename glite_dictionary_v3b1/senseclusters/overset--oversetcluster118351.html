overset

verb
to overturn
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈsɛt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsɛt / (BrE)
to overturn, upset, or cause something to fall over

Examples

To overset a boat
The strong wind overset the small boat in the harbor.
A careless bump overset the vase, causing it to shatter on the floor.
If you lean too far, you might overset the chair and fall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overturn - to turn something over so that it is upside down
  • antonym: stabilize - to make something steady or prevent it from falling
  • synonym: upset - to knock something over or disturb its position
