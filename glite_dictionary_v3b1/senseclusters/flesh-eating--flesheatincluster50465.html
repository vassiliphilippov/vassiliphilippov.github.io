flesh-eating

adjective
carnivorous; eating flesh
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛʃ ˌitɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflɛʃ ˌiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of animals) feeding on the flesh of other animals; carnivorous.

Examples

Flesh-eating animal
Many large predators, such as lions and tigers, are flesh-eating animals.
Scientists discovered a new flesh-eating species in the rainforests of South America.
The documentary focused on the hunting strategies of flesh-eating birds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carnivorous - feeding on other animals; meat-eating
  • antonym: herbivorous - feeding on plants
