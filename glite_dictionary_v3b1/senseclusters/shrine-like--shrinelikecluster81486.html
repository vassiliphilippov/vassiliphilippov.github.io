shrine-like

adjective
resembling a shrine
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʃraɪnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈʃraɪnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having qualities of a shrine, especially in being sacred, quiet, or reverent

Examples

Shrine-like quiet
The old study had a shrine-like stillness that made everyone speak in hushed tones.
Candles and flowers gave the memorial a shrine-like appearance.
Her bedroom was arranged in a shrine-like manner, honoring her favorite musician.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ordinary - not special or distinctive
  • synonym: sacred - regarded with great respect and reverence
