satisfiable

adjective
able to be fulfilled
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsætɪsˌfaɪəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsætɪsˌfaɪəbəl / (BrE)
able to be satisfied, fulfilled, or made true, especially in logic or mathematics

Examples

Satisfiable logical formula
A logical formula is satisfiable if there is at least one assignment that makes it true.
The contract terms are satisfiable within the given budget and timeline.
Mathematicians proved that the system of equations is satisfiable under certain conditions.
