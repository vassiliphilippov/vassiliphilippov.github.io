political boss

noun
a leader who controls a political party's decisions
1.8
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪtɪkəl bɑs / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl bɒs / (BrE)
a powerful leader in a political party who controls votes and often dictates appointments or decisions, typically exercising influence behind the scenes

Examples

The political boss controls votes
Many reforms were introduced to reduce the power of the political boss in urban politics.
The political boss wielded significant influence over city hall for decades.
Voters accused the political boss of favoring friends in government appointments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: machine politics - political system controlled by a party organization and its leaders
  • synonym: party boss - leader who controls a political party's votes and appointments
