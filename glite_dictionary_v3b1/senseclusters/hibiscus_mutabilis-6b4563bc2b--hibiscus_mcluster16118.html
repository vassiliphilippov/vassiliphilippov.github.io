Hibiscus mutabilis

noun
Chinese shrub with color-changing flowers
9.2
Pronunciation: /hɪ.ˈbɪ.skəs mjuː.ˈtæ.bɪ.lɪs/ /; / /haɪ.ˈbɪ.skəs mjuː.ˈtæ.bɪ.lɪs/ (AmE), /hɪ.ˈbɪ.skəs mjuː.ˈtæ.bɪ.lɪs/ /; / /haɪ.ˈbɪ.skəs mjuː.ˈtæ.bɪ.lɪs/ (BrE)
