nonhedonic

adjective
not pleasure-related
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.hɪˈdɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.hɪˈdɒnɪk / (BrE)
not related to pleasure or the pursuit of pleasure

Examples

Nonhedonic reasons
The research examined nonhedonic reasons for people choosing to donate their time.
Some activities are motivated by nonhedonic values such as duty or responsibility.
Her nonhedonic approach to work focused on helping others rather than personal enjoyment.
