Manchego

noun
person from La Mancha
Pronunciation: / manˈtʃeɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / manˈtʃeɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of La Mancha, a region in central Spain

Examples

A Manchego villager
The Manchego welcomed visitors to his village with stories about local traditions.
As a proud Manchego, she often spoke about the beauty of La Mancha.
Many Manchegos still celebrate ancient festivals unique to their region.
