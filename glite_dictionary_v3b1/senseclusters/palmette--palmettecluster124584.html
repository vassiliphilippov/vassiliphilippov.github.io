palmette

noun
stylized palm motif
Pronunciation: / pælˈmɛt / (AmE), / pælˈmɛt / (BrE)
a decorative design or motif in art and architecture that resembles a stylized palm leaf

Examples

A palmette pattern
The ancient vase was adorned with a repeating palmette pattern around its rim.
Architects often use the palmette motif to decorate columns and friezes in classical buildings.
A row of palmettes decorated the border of the mosaic floor in the historic villa.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthemion - a classical decorative motif based on a radiating cluster of leaves
  • synonym: rosette - a decorative motif resembling a rose or circular flower
