stretch

adjective
able to be easily stretched; elastic
Pronunciation: / strɛtʃ / (AmE), / strɛtʃ / (BrE)
(of fabric or material) able to be easily stretched without damage; elastic.

Examples

Stretch fabric
Athletes often choose stretch material for their workout clothes.
She prefers to wear stretch jeans for comfort and flexibility.
The new sofa is covered in a durable stretch fabric that resists tears.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elastic - able to return to original shape after being stretched
