giggle uncontrollably

verb
laugh in an uncontrollable, silly way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪɡəl ˌʌn.kənˈtroʊləbli / (AmE), / ˈɡɪɡl ˌʌn.kənˈtrəʊləbli / (BrE)
to laugh in a silly and uncontrolled way, often because something is very funny or embarrassing
