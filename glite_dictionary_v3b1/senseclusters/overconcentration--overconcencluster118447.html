overconcentration

noun
excessive focus
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərkənˌsɛnˈtreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəkɒnˌsɛnˈtreɪʃən / (BrE)
excessive focus on a single task, idea, or detail that causes neglect of other important aspects or reduces effectiveness

The team's overconcentration on one strategy left them unprepared for unexpected challenges.
