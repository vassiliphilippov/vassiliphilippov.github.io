cataphasia

noun
speech disorder causing repetition of words
Pronunciation: / ˌkæt̬.əˈfeɪ.zi.ə / (AmE), / ˌkat.əˈfeɪ.zi.ə / (BrE)
A speech disorder characterized by the repeated utterance of the same word several times in succession.

Examples

Cataphasia speech disorder
Patients with cataphasia often repeat the same word involuntarily during conversation.
Researchers are studying treatments to help individuals affected by cataphasia improve their speech patterns.
The neurologist explained that cataphasia might occur after certain types of brain injury.
