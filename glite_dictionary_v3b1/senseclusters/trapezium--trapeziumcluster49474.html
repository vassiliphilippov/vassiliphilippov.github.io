trapezium

noun
quadrilateral with no parallel sides
5.3
Pronunciation: / trəˈpiːziəm / (AmE), / trəˈpiːziəm / (BrE)
A quadrilateral with no sides parallel to each other, especially in British usage.

Examples

A trapezium shape
In British mathematics, a trapezium has no parallel sides at all.
The student drew a trapezium and labeled each of its angles.
Unlike a parallelogram, a trapezium does not have any parallel sides.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: trapezoid - a quadrilateral with exactly one pair of parallel sides
