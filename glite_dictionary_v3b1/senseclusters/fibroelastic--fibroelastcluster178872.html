fibroelastic

adjective
containing fibrous and elastic tissue
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbroʊɪˈlæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌfɪbrəʊɪˈlæstɪk / (BrE)
made of or containing both fibrous and elastic tissue

Examples

Fibroelastic tissue
The fibroelastic properties of the tissue allow it to stretch and recover its shape.
Doctors examined the fibroelastic membrane that supports the organ's structure.
A fibroelastic layer in the artery wall helps maintain blood flow under pressure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elastic - able to return to original shape after stretching
  • synonym: fibrous - containing or resembling fibers
