royal mast

noun
mast above the topgallant mast
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔɪ.əl mæst / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪ.əl mɑːst / (BrE)
the mast on a sailing ship that is immediately above the topgallant mast

Examples

The royal mast above
On older ships, the royal mast was the highest of all the masts.
The crew climbed the royal mast to adjust the sails during the high winds.
With the royal mast set, the ship could catch every bit of the available breeze.
