Cetus

proper noun
constellation near Pisces and Aquarius
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːtəs / (AmE), / ˈsiːtəs / (BrE)
A large constellation on the celestial equator, located near Pisces and Aquarius.

Examples

Cetus constellation
Astronomers locate Cetus to the south of Pisces and east of Aquarius in the night sky.
Many bright galaxies have been identified within Cetus by astronomers.
The constellation Cetus is often depicted as a sea monster in star charts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Pisces - constellation near Cetus, representing the fish
