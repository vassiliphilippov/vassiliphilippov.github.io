dermographia

noun
skin reaction to scratching
Pronunciation: / ˌdɜːrməˈɡræfiə / (AmE), / ˌdɜːməˈɡræfiə / (BrE)
a skin condition in which light scratching causes raised, red lines to appear on the skin

Examples

Dermographia skin reaction
After lightly scratching her arm, red lines appeared due to dermographia.
People with dermographia may notice their skin reacts strongly to even gentle contact.
The dermatologist explained that dermographia is usually harmless but can be uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: urticaria - a skin reaction causing itchy, raised welts
