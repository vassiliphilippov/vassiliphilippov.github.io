monoski

noun
single wide ski
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑnoʊˌski / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəʊˌski / (BrE)
a single wide ski designed for skiing with both feet side by side on the same ski

Examples

A monoski ride
He learned to balance quickly on the monoski during his first lesson.
The monoski allows both feet to be placed side by side, unlike traditional skis.
She enjoyed the unique experience of gliding down the mountain on a monoski.
He learned to balance on a monoski during his first winter in the Alps.
The athlete won a gold medal in the monoski event at the championship.
Using a monoski requires a different technique than traditional skiing.
