twinberry

noun
evergreen North American plant with scarlet berries
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪnˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈtwɪnˌbɛri / (BrE)
a plant with paired berries, especially one of several North American species called 'twinberry'
