autarch

noun
absolute ruler
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːtɑrk / (AmE), / ˈɔːtɑːk / (BrE)
a ruler who has absolute power and authority over a state or people

Examples

Absolute autarch ruler
The autarch imposed strict laws to maintain his control over the kingdom.
Citizens feared the unpredictable decisions of their autarch.
After years of unrest, the autarch was finally overthrown by the people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autocrat - a ruler with unlimited power
  • antonym: democrat - a supporter of democracy or democratic government
  • synonym: dictator - a leader who has complete control over a country
