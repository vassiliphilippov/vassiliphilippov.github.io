Phragmites

noun
genus of tall marsh reeds
Pronunciation: / fræɡˈmaɪtiːz / (AmE), / fræɡˈmaɪtiːz / (BrE)
a genus of tall perennial grasses (reeds) found in marshes and along rivers, especially in tropical and temperate regions
