undercaptain

noun
officer below captain
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌkæptɪn / (AmE), / ˈʌndəkæptɪn / (BrE)
an officer who holds a rank just below that of captain, acting as a subordinate to a captain

Examples

The undercaptain officer
The undercaptain relayed orders from the main commander to the troops.
After the captain was injured, the undercaptain took temporary command of the unit.
Each squad was led by an undercaptain who reported directly to the captain.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: captain - an officer in command of a company or ship
  • synonym: lieutenant - an officer ranking below a captain
