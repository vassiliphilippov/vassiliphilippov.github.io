cinch

noun
something very easy to do
Pronunciation: / sɪntʃ / (AmE), / sɪntʃ / (BrE)
a task or undertaking that is very easy to do

Examples

A cinch to do
Cooking that simple recipe was a cinch even for someone with little experience.
Finishing the project by Friday should be a cinch with this extra help.
The exam was a cinch for anyone who had studied the notes carefully.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breeze - a task that is very easy to do
  • antonym: challenge - something that is difficult to do
  • synonym: pushover - something easily accomplished or someone easily influenced
