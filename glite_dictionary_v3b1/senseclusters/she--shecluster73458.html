she

pronoun
female pronoun
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʃi / (AmE), / ʃi / (BrE)
used to refer to a female person or animal that has already been mentioned or is known

Examples

She is happy
She arrived at the airport early to catch her flight.
When the teacher called her name, she stood up and answered confidently.
The cat is very friendly; she loves to sit on my lap.

Related Terms

  • antonym: he - male pronoun for a person or animal
  • synonym: her - object or possessive form of 'she'
