puzzlement

noun
confusion due to not understanding
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌzəlmənt / (AmE), / ˈpʌzlmənt / (BrE)
a feeling or state of being confused because something is difficult to understand or explain

Examples

Clear puzzlement
His puzzlement was clear as he stared at the complex equation.
Much to her puzzlement, the instructions made no sense at all.
There was a look of puzzlement on their faces when the announcement was made.

  • synonym: confusion - the state of being bewildered or unclear
