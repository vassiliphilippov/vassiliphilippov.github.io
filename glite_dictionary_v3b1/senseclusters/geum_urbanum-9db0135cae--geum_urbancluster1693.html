Geum urbanum

noun
a Eurasian plant with yellow flowers and astringent root
Pronunciation: / ˈɡiəm ɜrˈbeɪnəm / (AmE), / ˈɡiːəm ɜːˈbɑːnəm / (BrE)
A hairy Eurasian plant with small yellow flowers and an astringent root formerly used in medicine.

Examples

Geum urbanum plant
Geum urbanum can often be found growing in shady woodlands and hedgerows.
Gardeners sometimes plant Geum urbanum for its attractive yellow flowers and hardy nature.
In herbal medicine, the root of Geum urbanum was valued for its astringent properties.
