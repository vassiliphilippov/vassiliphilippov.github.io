jump

noun
an act of springing off the ground
Pronunciation: / dʒʌmp / (AmE), / dʒʌmp / (BrE)
an act of springing off the ground or another surface by pushing oneself upward or forward

Examples

A long jump
After a running start, he took a long jump across the stream.
She made a spectacular jump over the fence during the competition.
The cat startled me with a sudden jump onto the windowsill.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: hop - a quick short jump, usually on one foot
  • synonym: leap - an act of jumping a long way or to a great height
