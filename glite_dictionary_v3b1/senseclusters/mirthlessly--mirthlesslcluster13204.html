mirthlessly

adverb
without joy
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜrθləsli / (AmE), / ˈmɜːθləsli / (BrE)
in a way that shows no joy, amusement, or genuine happiness

Examples

To laugh mirthlessly
He laughed mirthlessly at the joke, but his eyes remained cold.
She nodded mirthlessly when she heard the disappointing results.
The team accepted their defeat mirthlessly, knowing they had done their best.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a way that shows happiness or good spirits
  • synonym: joylessly - in a way that lacks joy or happiness
