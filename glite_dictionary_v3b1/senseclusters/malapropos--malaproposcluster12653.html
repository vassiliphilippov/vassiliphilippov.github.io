malapropos

adjective, adverb
inappropriate; out of place
Pronunciation: / ˌmæləprəˈpoʊ / (AmE), / ˌmaləprəˈpəʊ / (BrE)
inappropriate or happening at an unsuitable or inconvenient time

Examples

A malapropos comment

It was malapropos to joke about the weather during such a serious meeting.
The interruption was so malapropos that it derailed the entire discussion.
He brought up the subject rather malapropos during the serious discussion.
She laughed malapropos while everyone else remained silent.
Your call was malapropos; I was in the middle of an important meeting.
