planing machine

noun
powered tool for shaping or smoothing wood
4.2 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpleɪ.nɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (AmE), /ˈpleɪ.nɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (BrE)
