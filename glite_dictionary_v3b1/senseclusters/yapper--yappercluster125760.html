yapper

noun
noisy small dog
Pronunciation: / ˈjæpər / (AmE), / ˈjæpə / (BrE)
an informal term for a small dog that barks frequently and noisily

Examples

Noisy yapper
The little yapper next door barks at everyone who walks by the house.
Every time the mail carrier arrives, that yapper makes a huge fuss.
Despite his size, the yapper acts like he owns the entire street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barker - a dog that barks a lot
  • synonym: lapdog - a small pet dog, often kept for companionship
