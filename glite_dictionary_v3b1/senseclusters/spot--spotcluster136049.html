spot

verb
notice; catch sight of
2.1 30/m
Pronunciation: / spÉ‘t / (AmE), / spÉ’t / (BrE)
to notice, see, or identify someone or something, especially when it is difficult

    4.6 7.03/m

  • discern
    5.8 9.48/m
    discern means detect or recognize something with careful sensing, while spot is a common word for noticing or seeing something.
  • espy

  • spy
    3.1 2.98/m

  • mark

    mark means notice or pay attention to something carefully, while spot focuses more on suddenly seeing or identifying a person or thing.
