outfit

noun
set of clothes worn together
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌfɪt / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.fɪt / (BrE)
a set of clothes, often including accessories, worn together, especially for a particular occasion or purpose

Examples

A stylish outfit
His outfit included a matching hat and shoes.
She chose a stylish outfit for the party tonight.
That outfit is perfect for hiking in the mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attire - clothes, especially fine or formal ones
  • synonym: costume - clothing worn to portray a particular style or for a special occasion
