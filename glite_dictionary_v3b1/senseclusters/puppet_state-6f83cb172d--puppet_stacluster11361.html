puppet state

noun
state controlled by an outside power
7.6 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆpÊŒpÉªt steÉªt / (AmE), / ËˆpÊŒpÉªt steÉªt / (BrE)
A government that is formally independent but is actually controlled and directed by an outside power, often serving that power's interests rather than its own population.

Examples

A puppet state
During the war, the occupying power set up a puppet state to legitimize its control.
Many historians argue that the regime was nothing more than a puppet state serving foreign interests.
The citizens resented living under a puppet state that did not represent their will.

Synonyms

  • puppet government
    5.8 0.39/m
    puppet government is an administration set up and controlled by outsiders, while puppet state is the whole country under that outside control.
  • puppet regime
    8.4 0.16/m
    puppet regime is an often harsh ruling system imposed by foreigners, while puppet state is the formally named country they control.

Related Terms

  • synonym: satellite state - a country that is formally independent but under heavy influence or control by another country
