gibbet

noun
structure for hanging criminals
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪbɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪbɪt / (BrE)

Examples

A wooden gibbet

The old gibbet still stands at the edge of the village as a grim reminder of the past.
