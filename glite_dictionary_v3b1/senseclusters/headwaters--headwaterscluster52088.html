headwaters

noun
river source
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈhed.wɔː.tərz/ (AmE), /ˈhed.wɔː.təz/ (BrE)
the source or beginning part of a river or stream, where it originates

Examples

Headwaters of the river
The headwaters of the Amazon are located high in the Andes mountains.
Pollution in the headwaters can affect the entire river downstream.
Scientists hiked for days to reach the remote headwaters of the stream.
