differential gear

noun
gear system allowing wheels to rotate at different speeds
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəl ɡɪr / (AmE), / ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəl ɡɪə / (BrE)
a gear system, usually with bevel gears, that allows two shafts to rotate at different speeds, especially used in the rear axle of vehicles

Examples

The differential gear system
If the differential gear fails, it can cause serious problems with handling the vehicle.
Most modern vehicles are equipped with a differential gear to improve stability and traction.
The differential gear helps each wheel turn at the right speed when the car goes around a corner.

  • synonym: differential - mechanism allowing different rotational speeds, especially in vehicles
