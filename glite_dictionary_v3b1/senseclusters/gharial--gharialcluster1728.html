gharial

noun
Indian crocodilian
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈriːəl /; / ˈɡɛriəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːrɪəl /; / ˈɡɑːriɑːl / (BrE)
a large, fish-eating crocodilian with a very long, narrow snout, native to rivers in India and nearby regions

Examples

The gharial crocodile
The gharial is easily recognized by its extremely long and narrow snout.

Conservationists are working to protect the remaining wild gharial populations from extinction.
