front

noun
forward-facing side; most prominent side
1.0 189/m
Pronunciation: / frÊŒnt / (AmE), / frÊŒnt / (BrE)
the part or side of something that is forward, most prominent, or nearest to the viewer or entrance

Synonyms

  • face
    1.0 26/m
    face is the main outer surface of an object or building, while front means the side nearest the viewer or entrance.
  • front end
    3.6 0.27/m
    front end is the part that faces forward or leads a vehicle or machine, while front names the general forward side.
  • forepart
    7.5 0.02/m
    forepart is the front section of an object or animal in older use, while front is the common word for the forward side.
  • forefront
    4.4 0.96/m
    forefront is the leading or most prominent position in a field, while front refers to the physical side nearest the viewer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: back - the side that is opposite to the front; the rear side
  • synonym: face - the front part of the head; also the surface of something that is seen most
