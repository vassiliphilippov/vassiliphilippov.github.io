axletree

noun
fixed axle with spindles for wheels
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈækslˌtri / (AmE), / ˈaksəlˌtriː / (BrE)
A fixed axle on a carriage or wagon, with projecting ends (spindles) on which the wheels turn.

Examples

The axletree of cart
A blacksmith was hired to repair the damaged axletree on the cart.
Old carriages often had a sturdy wooden axletree between the wheels.
The front axletree of the wagon snapped under the heavy load.

Related Terms

  • synonym: axle - a rod or spindle passing through the center of a wheel
