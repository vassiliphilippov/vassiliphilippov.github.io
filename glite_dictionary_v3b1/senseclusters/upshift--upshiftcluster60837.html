upshift

noun
change to higher gear
5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈʌp.ʃɪft/ (AmE), /ˈʌp.ʃɪft/ (BrE)
the act of changing to a higher gear in a vehicle or machine to increase speed or efficiency

Examples

A smooth upshift
A smooth upshift can help save fuel when driving on the highway.
He performed a quick upshift as the car entered the passing lane.
Learning the right time for an upshift is important for new drivers.
