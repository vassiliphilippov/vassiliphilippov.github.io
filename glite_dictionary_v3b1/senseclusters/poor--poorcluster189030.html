poor

adjective
lacking quality or substance
4.0
Pronunciation: / pɔr /; / pʊr / (AmE), / pɔː / (BrE)
not good enough in quality or amount; lacking or insufficient

Examples

Poor soil quality
Despite beautiful scenery, the soil there is poor and unsuitable for farming.
Students complained that the cafeteria meals were poor in both taste and nutrition.
The region is poor in natural resources, with little timber or mineral wealth.
His knowledge of French is poor, so conversation was difficult.
The company's poor performance this quarter was due to a lack of funding.
The refugees had to survive on a poor diet for weeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deficient - lacking in some necessary quality or element
  • synonym: inadequate - lacking the quality or quantity required; insufficient
  • synonym: inferior - of lower quality or standard
  • antonym: rich - containing much of something, especially nutrients or quality
  • antonym: sufficient - enough to meet the needs
