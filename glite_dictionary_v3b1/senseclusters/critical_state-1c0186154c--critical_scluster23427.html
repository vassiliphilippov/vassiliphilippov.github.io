critical state

noun
urgent or important situation
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪtɪkəl steɪt / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtɪkəl steɪt / (BrE)
a situation or condition of great importance or urgency, often requiring immediate attention or action

Examples

A critical state condition
If the server enters a critical state, all systems must be checked immediately.
The negotiations reached a critical state before an agreement was finally made.
The patient's health is in a critical state and needs constant monitoring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crisis - a time of intense difficulty or danger
