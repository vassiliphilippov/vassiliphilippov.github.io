The act of treating something in an overly emotional, romantic, or idealized way, often ignoring realism or practicality.

Examples

The sentimentalization of history
Some historians criticize the sentimentalization of past events in popular media.
The film's sentimentalization of childhood glossed over its darker realities.
There is a risk of sentimentalization when personal stories are presented without context.
Her sentimentalisation of the past made her overlook its many difficulties.
The sentimentalisation of childhood often ignores the hardships many children face.
The movie's sentimentalisation of war was criticized by reviewers.
