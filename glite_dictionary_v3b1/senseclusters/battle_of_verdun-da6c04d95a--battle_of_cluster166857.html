Battle of Verdun

proper noun
major 1916 WWI battle at Verdun, France
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈbæt.əl əv vər.ˈdʌn/ /; / /ˈbæt.əl əv vɜːr.ˈdʌn/ (AmE), /ˈbæt.əl əv vɜː.ˈdʌn/ /; / /ˈbæt.əl əv və.ˈdʌn/ (BrE)
a major World War I battle fought in 1916 near Verdun, France, between French and German forces, known as one of the longest and bloodiest battles in history
