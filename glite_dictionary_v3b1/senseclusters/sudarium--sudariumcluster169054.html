sudarium

noun
funeral face cloth
9.9
Pronunciation: / suˈdɛəriəm / (AmE), / suˈdɛəriəm / (BrE)
a cloth placed over the face of a dead person during funerals or burials

Examples

Funeral sudarium
The priest placed a sudarium over the deceased's face as part of the burial ritual.
A white sudarium was carefully folded and laid on the body before the family arrived.
Historians studied the ancient sudarium found in the tomb to learn about burial customs.
