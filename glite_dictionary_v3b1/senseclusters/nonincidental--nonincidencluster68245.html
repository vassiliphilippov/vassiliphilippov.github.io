nonincidental

adjective
intentional or significant
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɪnsɪˈdentəl / (BrE)
not happening by chance or as a minor part; intentional or significant in relation to something

Examples

A nonincidental effect
The judge ruled that the damages were nonincidental and directly related to the contract breach.
Her contributions to the project were nonincidental and played a key role in its success.
The company faced penalties for nonincidental violations of environmental regulations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: incidental - happening by chance or as a minor part
  • synonym: intentional - done on purpose; deliberate
