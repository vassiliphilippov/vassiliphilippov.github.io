just about

adverb
almost; approximately
1.2
Pronunciation: / dʒʌst əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / dʒʌst əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
Almost or approximately, but not exactly; nearly correct or true.

Examples

Just about finished
He finished the marathon in just about four hours, which was faster than he expected.
It’s just about time to leave for the train station.
There are just about twenty people waiting in the lobby right now.

Related Terms

  • synonym: almost - very nearly but not quite; close to
  • synonym: approximately - close to a particular number or value but not exactly that number or value
