Arabize

verb
to write in Arabic script
Pronunciation: / ˈærəˌbaɪz / (AmE), / ˈærəbaɪz / (BrE)
to represent or write words using Arabic letters or script, especially by transliterating them into Arabic

Examples

To Arabize words
The software can Arabize English text so it appears in Arabic script.
Many foreign place names are Arabized for use on local maps in the Middle East.
The teacher explained how to Arabize scientific terms for Arabic-speaking students.

Related Terms

  • antonym: romanize - to write words using the Roman (Latin) alphabet
  • synonym: transliterate - to write words from one script in the letters of another
