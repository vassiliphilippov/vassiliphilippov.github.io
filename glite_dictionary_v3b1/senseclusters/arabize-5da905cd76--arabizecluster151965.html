Arabize

verb
to write in Arabic script
8.8
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈræ.baɪz/ /; / /ˈær.ə.baɪz/ (AmE), /ə.ˈræ.baɪz/ /; / /ˈær.ə.baɪz/ (BrE)
