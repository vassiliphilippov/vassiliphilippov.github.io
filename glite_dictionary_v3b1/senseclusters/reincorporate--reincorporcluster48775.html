reincorporate

verb
to form again as corporation
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈkɔːrpəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈkɔːpəreɪt / (BrE)
to form or register again as a corporation, or to integrate something again into a whole

Examples

To reincorporate a company
After moving to another state, the business chose to reincorporate under new regulations.
The nonprofit organization decided to reincorporate to better serve its members.
Sometimes a company must reincorporate to comply with updated legal requirements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dissolve - to officially end the existence of a corporation or organization
  • synonym: reorganize - to arrange or form again in a new way
