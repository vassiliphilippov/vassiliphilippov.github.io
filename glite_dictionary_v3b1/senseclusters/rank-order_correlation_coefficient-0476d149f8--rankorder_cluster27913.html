rank-order correlation coefficient

noun
statistical measure of ranks correlation
8.6
Pronunciation: /ræŋk ˈɔːr.dər kɔːr.ə.ˈleɪ.ʃən koʊ.ə.ˈfɪʃ.ənt/ (AmE), /ræŋk ˈɔː.də kɒr.ə.ˈleɪ.ʃən kəʊ.ə.ˈfɪʃ.ənt/ (BrE)
a statistical measure used to assess the relationship between the ranks of scores on two variables

Examples

The rank-order correlation coefficient is often used in psychology to compare test rankings.
