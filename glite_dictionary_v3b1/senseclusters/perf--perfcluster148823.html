perf

noun
stamp perforation
9.7
Pronunciation: /pɜːrf/ (AmE), /pɜːf/ (BrE)
an informal short form for 'perforation', referring to the row of small holes or cuts that allow paper or stamps to be separated easily

Examples

Stamp perf
Collectors often check the perf of a stamp to determine its authenticity.
The perf was uneven, making the stamp harder to separate cleanly from the sheet.
He measured the perf to compare it with other rare stamps in his collection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perforation - a row of small holes that make it easy to tear paper or stamps
