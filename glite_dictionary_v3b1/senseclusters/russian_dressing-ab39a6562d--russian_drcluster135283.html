Russian dressing

noun
a creamy salad dressing with chili sauce or ketchup
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌʃən ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃ(ə)n ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
a salad dressing made with mayonnaise, horseradish, onion, and chili sauce or ketchup, sometimes with caviar added

Examples

Homemade Russian dressing
Homemade Russian dressing is easy to make and tastes fresher than store-bought versions.
She topped her salad with Russian dressing for extra flavor.
The sandwich comes with a generous portion of Russian dressing spread inside.
