crepey

adjective
thin and wrinkled
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪpi / (AmE), / ˈkreɪpi / (BrE)
having a thin, wrinkled, or finely crinkled texture, especially resembling crepe fabric

Examples

Crepey skin texture
As people age, their skin can become crepey and lose its firmness.
The scarf had a delicate, crepey texture that felt soft to the touch.
She applied moisturizer daily to help reduce the crepey appearance on her neck.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
  • synonym: wrinkled - having small lines or folds in the surface
