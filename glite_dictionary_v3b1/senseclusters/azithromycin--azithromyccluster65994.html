azithromycin

noun
antibiotic medication
Pronunciation: / əˌzɪθrəˈmaɪsən / (AmE), / əˌzɪθrəˈmaɪsɪn / (BrE)
a broad-spectrum antibiotic used to treat various bacterial infections in the body

Examples

Azithromycin antibiotic treatment
Azithromycin is often used for respiratory tract infections.
Some patients may experience mild side effects when taking azithromycin.
The doctor prescribed azithromycin to treat her sinus infection.
