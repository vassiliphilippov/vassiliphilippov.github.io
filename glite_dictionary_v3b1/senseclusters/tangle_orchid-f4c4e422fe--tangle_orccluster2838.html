tangle orchid

noun
orchid with tangled roots of genus Plectorrhiza
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtæŋɡəl ˌɔr.kɪd / (AmE), / ˈtæŋɡəl ˌɔː.kɪd / (BrE)
