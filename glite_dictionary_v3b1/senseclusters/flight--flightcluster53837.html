flight

noun
unit in air force smaller than a squadron
4.1 4.5
Pronunciation: /flaɪt/ (AmE), /flaɪt/ (BrE)
a unit in an air force or a group of aircraft flying together as a formation

Examples

A flight of jets
Each flight consists of several aircraft as well as ground crew members.
He was promoted to lead the flight during his second year in the air force.
The flight was tasked with conducting patrols along the eastern border.
A flight of bombers crossed the horizon at dawn, heading toward their target.
The airshow featured a spectacular flight of jets performing synchronized maneuvers.
The lead pilot gave the signal, and the entire flight changed formation midair.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: squadron - a principal division of an air force, larger than a flight
  • degree higher: wing - a large unit in an air force, usually composed of two or more squadrons
