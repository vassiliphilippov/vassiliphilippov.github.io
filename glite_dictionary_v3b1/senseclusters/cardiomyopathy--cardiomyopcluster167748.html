cardiomyopathy

noun
heart muscle disease
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːrdioʊmaɪˈɑːpəθi / (AmE), / ˌkɑːdiəʊmaɪˈɒpəθi / (BrE)
A disease of the heart muscle that reduces its ability to pump blood effectively, often of unknown cause.
