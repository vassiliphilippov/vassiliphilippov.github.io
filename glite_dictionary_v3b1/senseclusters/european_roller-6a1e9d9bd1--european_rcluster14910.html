European roller

noun
a brightly colored Eurasian bird species
Pronunciation: / jʊˌroʊpiən ˈroʊlər / (AmE), / jʊəˌrəʊpiən ˈrəʊlə / (BrE)
a Eurasian bird (Coracias garrulus) with bright blue and green plumage and a reddish-brown back, found in open countryside and known for its acrobatic flight displays.

Examples

The European roller bird
During their migration, flocks of European rollers can be seen gliding over southern Europe.
The European roller is easily recognized by its vibrant blue feathers and distinctive flight.
Wildlife enthusiasts travel great distances to observe the rare courtship rituals of the European roller.
