multitouch

noun
touchscreen technology
3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌl.tɪ.tʌtʃ/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tiː.tʌtʃ/ (AmE), /ˈmʌl.tɪ.tʌtʃ/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tiː.tʌtʃ/ (BrE)
a technology that enables a touchscreen to detect and respond to multiple points of contact at the same time

Examples

Multitouch technology
Most modern smartphones support multitouch gestures like pinch-to-zoom.
The new tablet features advanced multitouch capabilities for a smoother user experience.
With multitouch technology, you can use two fingers to rotate or zoom images easily.
