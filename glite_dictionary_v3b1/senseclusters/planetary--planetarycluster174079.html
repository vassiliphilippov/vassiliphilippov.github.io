Synonyms

  • global
    2.7 133/m
    global describes issues or effects that reach the whole world across nations and markets, while planetary means relating to Earth as a planet.
  • planetwide
    3.7 0.07/m
    planetwide means affecting the entire planet at once, whereas planetary is used for things relating to the Earth or other planets.
  • worldwide
    2.1 16/m
    worldwide describes events or effects that exist across many countries and people, while planetary stresses relation to the Earth itself.
  • terrestrial
    5.5 0.89/m
    terrestrial describes life or land on Earth, often versus air or sea, while planetary means relating to the Earth or whole world.
