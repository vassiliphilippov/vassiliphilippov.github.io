planetary

adjective
relating to Earth or its inhabitants
Pronunciation: / ˈplænəˌtɛri /; / ˈplænəˌter.i / (AmE), / ˈplænɪtəri /; / ˈplæn.ɪ.tər.i / (BrE)
relating to the planet Earth or to the whole world; global

Examples

Planetary crisis
Climate change poses a significant planetary challenge that affects all nations.
Our planetary resources are not limitless and must be managed wisely.
The scientists discussed the planetary consequences of deforestation at the conference.
Our organization focuses on planetary health, studying how human activity affects the earth as a whole.
Recent advances in technology have led to planetary changes in communication and information exchange.
The climate crisis is a planetary issue that requires cooperation from every nation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: global - relating to or involving the entire world
  • antonym: local - relating to a particular place; not global
  • synonym: terrestrial - relating to the Earth; earthly
