rub-a-dub

noun
a drumming or rhythmic sound
Pronunciation: / ˌrʌb ə ˈdʌb / (AmE), / ˌrʌb ə ˈdʌb / (BrE)
a repetitive, rhythmic sound like that made by beating a drum

Examples

The rub-a-dub sound
All morning, we heard the rub-a-dub from the festival parade.
The distant rub-a-dub of drums echoed across the valley.
The poem described the rub-a-dub of the soldiers' march.
