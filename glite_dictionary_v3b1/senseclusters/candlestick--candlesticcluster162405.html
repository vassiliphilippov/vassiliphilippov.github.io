(in finance) a single bar on a candlestick chart that shows the open, high, low and close prices for a specified period.

Examples

Candlestick chart bar
The trader noted a bullish candlestick on the daily chart, suggesting a price rise.
Analysts compared several candlesticks to identify a reversal pattern after the long downtrend.
A green candlestick with a long lower wick indicated buyers stepped in during the session.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candlestick chart - a financial chart that displays price movements using candlesticks, each showing OHLC data for a period.
  • synonym: price bar - a representation of open, high, low and close prices for a period (often used interchangeably with candlestick in charting).
