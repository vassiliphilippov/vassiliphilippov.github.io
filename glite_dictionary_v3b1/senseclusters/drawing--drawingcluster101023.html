drawing

noun
event of choosing winners by chance
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrɔː.ɪŋ / (BrE)
an event in which winners are selected by chance, often from among people who have bought tickets or entries

Examples

Lottery drawing
Every Saturday, the lottery drawing attracts thousands of hopeful participants.
The charity held a raffle drawing to raise money for the local hospital.
You need to buy a ticket to be eligible for the next drawing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lottery - a means of raising money by selling numbered tickets and giving prizes to the holders of numbers drawn at random
  • synonym: raffle - a competition in which people buy numbered tickets in the hope of winning prizes, the winners being chosen by chance
