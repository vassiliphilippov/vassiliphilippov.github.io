impacted fracture

noun
bone fracture where ends are jammed together
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpæktɪd ˈfræktʃər / (AmE), / ɪmˈpæktɪd ˈfræktʃə / (BrE)
a type of bone fracture in which one broken end is driven into the other broken end, causing the bone fragments to be jammed together

Examples

An impacted fracture of femur
The X-ray revealed an impacted fracture of the femur after the skiing accident.
Treating an impacted fracture may require surgery to separate and realign the bone ends.
Unlike a simple break, an impacted fracture occurs when bone fragments are jammed together.

  • synonym: fracture - the cracking or breaking of a hard object or material, especially a bone
