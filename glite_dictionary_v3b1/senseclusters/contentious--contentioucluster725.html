contentious

adjective
causing controversy or argument
5.4
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɛnʃəs /; / kənˈtɛn(t)ʃəs / (AmE), / kənˈtɛnʃəs /; / kənˈtɛn.ʃəs / (BrE)
causing or likely to cause disagreement or argument; often argumentative
