takfiri ideology

noun
radical Islamic doctrine sanctioning violence
Pronunciation: / tækˈfiːri aɪˌdɪɑːˈlɑdʒi / (AmE), / takˈfɪəri aɪˌdɪəˈlɒdʒi / (BrE)
A radical Islamic doctrine that sanctions violence against those deemed apostate or non-believers.
