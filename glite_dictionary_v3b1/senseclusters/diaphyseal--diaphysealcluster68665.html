diaphyseal

adjective
of the shaft of a long bone
Pronunciation: / daɪˈæfɪsiəl / (AmE), / daɪˈafɪsiːəl / (BrE)
Relating to the diaphysis, or shaft, of a long bone.

Examples

Diaphyseal bone shaft
A diaphyseal bone tumor typically requires careful surgical intervention.
Pediatric patients may experience growth disturbances from diaphyseal injuries.
The fracture was located in the diaphyseal region of the femur.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diaphysis - the shaft or central part of a long bone
