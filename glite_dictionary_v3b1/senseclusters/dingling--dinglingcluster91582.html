dingling

verb
to ring lightly
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪŋlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋlɪŋ / (BrE)
to make a light, repeated ringing or bell-like sound.

Examples

To dingling in the wind
The wind chimes dingled softly in the evening breeze outside the window.
Every time someone entered the shop, a small bell dingled above the door.
She smiled as the tiny silver bells on her bracelet dingled with each movement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clang - to make a loud, resonant metallic sound
  • synonym: jingle - to make a light, metallic ringing sound
