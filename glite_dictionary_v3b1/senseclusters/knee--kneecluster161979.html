knee

verb
to strike with knee
Pronunciation: / niː / (AmE), / niː / (BrE)
to hit or strike someone or something with your knee

Examples

To knee a player
During the match, he kneed his opponent in the side by accident.
She kneed the door open when her hands were full.
The player kneed the ball to pass it to his teammate.
The referee gave a warning after the player kneed his opponent in the second half.
She accidentally kneed the table while standing up quickly.
During the self-defense class, we learned how to knee an attacker if necessary.
