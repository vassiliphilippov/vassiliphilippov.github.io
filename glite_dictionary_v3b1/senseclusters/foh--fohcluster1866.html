foh

noun
sound mixing position
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛf oʊ eɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɛf əʊ eɪtʃ / (BrE)
the area in a live venue where the main sound mixing is done, or the person operating sound there

Examples

Foh sound desk
The foh was set up in the center of the audience for the best sound control.
She worked as the foh for several major concerts last year.
If you have any sound issues, talk to the foh during the break.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monitor engineer - the person responsible for mixing sound for performers on stage
