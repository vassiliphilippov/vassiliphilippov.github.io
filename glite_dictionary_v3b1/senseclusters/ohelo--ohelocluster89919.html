ohelo

noun
Hawaiian berry shrub
Pronunciation: / oʊˈhɛloʊ / (AmE), / əʊˈhɛləʊ / (BrE)
a low-growing Hawaiian shrub that produces small edible red or yellow berries, or the berry itself
