crystal microphone

noun
microphone using a piezoelectric crystal
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstəl ˈmaɪkrəˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstl ˈmaɪkrəˌfəʊn / (BrE)
a microphone that uses a piezoelectric crystal to convert sound waves into an electrical signal

Examples

A crystal microphone
Because of its sensitivity, a crystal microphone was chosen for the recording session.
Many vintage tape recorders used a crystal microphone due to its reliability and low cost.
The old radio was equipped with a crystal microphone for voice transmission.
