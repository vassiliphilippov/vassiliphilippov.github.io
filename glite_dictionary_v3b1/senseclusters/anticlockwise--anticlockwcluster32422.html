anticlockwise

adjective, adverb
in the opposite direction to a clock's hands
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tiˈklɑːk.waɪz /; / ˌæn.tiˈklɑːkˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈklɒk.waɪz /; / ˌæntiˈklɒkwaɪz / (BrE)
in or towards the direction opposite to the way the hands of a clock move (counterclockwise)

Examples
