virucidal

adjective
destroying viruses
Pronunciation: / ˌvaɪrəˈsaɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌvɪərəˈsaɪdəl / (BrE)
Capable of destroying or inactivating viruses.

Examples

Virucidal agents
Laboratories used special virucidal agents to sterilize their equipment.
Proper handwashing with a virucidal soap helps reduce the risk of infection.
The new disinfectant is highly virucidal and effective against multiple strains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bactericidal - capable of killing bacteria
  • degree higher: virulent - extremely harmful or poisonous (often regarding viruses)
