pineland

noun
area with pine trees
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪnˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈpaɪnˌlænd / (BrE)
an area of land where pine trees are the main or dominant type of vegetation

Examples

A pineland area
The wildfire spread quickly through the dry pineland.
Many animals make their homes in the pineland near the river.
We hiked for hours through the dense pineland before reaching the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pine forest - a forest where pine trees are the main species
  • synonym: woodland - land covered with trees and shrubs
