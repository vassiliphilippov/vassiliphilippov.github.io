fountainhead

noun
source of a stream or river
Pronunciation: / ˈfaʊntənˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈfaʊntənˌhɛd / (BrE)
the original source of a stream or river; the point from which a flow of water originates

Examples

River fountainhead
Protecting the fountainhead ensures clean water for all the towns downstream.
The mountain spring is the fountainhead of the river that winds through the valley.
They hiked for hours to reach the hidden fountainhead of the stream.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mouth - the place where a river flows into another body of water
  • synonym: source - a place, person, or thing from which something originates
