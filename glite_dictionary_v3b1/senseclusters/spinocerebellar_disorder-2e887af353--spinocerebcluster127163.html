Any of a group of congenital disorders characterized by progressive degeneration of the cerebellum and spinal cord, resulting in symptoms such as spasticity and ataxia.

Examples

A spinocerebellar disorder diagnosis
Genetic testing is often used to diagnose various types of spinocerebellar disorder in affected families.
Symptoms of a spinocerebellar disorder typically include difficulties with coordination, balance, and muscle control.
The neurologist suspected a spinocerebellar disorder after observing the patient's gait abnormalities and muscle stiffness.
