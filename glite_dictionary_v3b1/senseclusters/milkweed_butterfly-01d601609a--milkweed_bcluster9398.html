milkweed butterfly

noun
large orange American butterfly; larvae eat milkweed
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkwid ˌbʌtɚˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkwiːd ˌbʌtəflaɪ / (BrE)
a large American butterfly, typically with deep orange wings and black and white markings, whose larvae feed on milkweed plants; includes the monarch butterfly

Examples

Children often observe the transformation from caterpillar to milkweed butterfly in school science projects.
