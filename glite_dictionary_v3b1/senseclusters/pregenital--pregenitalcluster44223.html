pregenital

adjective
before genital development
Pronunciation: / priːˈdʒɛnɪtəl / (AmE), / priːˈdʒɛnɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to a stage before the development of the genital organs or sexual functions, especially in psychoanalytic theory

Examples

Pregenital stage
In Freudian theory, certain childhood behaviors are considered pregenital stages of development.
The psychologist discussed the influence of pregenital experiences on adult personality.
Some disorders are thought to originate in the pregenital phases of psychosexual growth.
