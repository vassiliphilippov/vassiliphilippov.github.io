paired

adjective
connected via pairing (e.g., Bluetooth)
3.7
Pronunciation: /perd/ (AmE), /peəd/ (BrE)
(of an electronic device) connected to another device through a pairing process (for example Bluetooth), indicating an established wireless or authorized link between the devices.

Examples

Paired devices connected
Please ensure your phone is paired with the car's Bluetooth system before you drive.
After the update, the speaker showed as paired but produced no sound during normal playback.
The technician saw the new thermostat listed as paired but still had to authorize it on the app.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connected - joined or linked so that information or power can be exchanged; similar state for devices.
  • antonym: unpaired - not linked to another device; the opposite of being paired in a technical context.
