nonprotractile

adjective
unable to be extended outward
4.1
Pronunciation: / nɑn.prəˈtræk.taɪl / (AmE), / nɒn.prəˈtræk.taɪl / (BrE)
(of a body part) not able to be extended or protruded outward.

Examples

Nonprotractile claws
Certain cats have nonprotractile claws that are always exposed.
In some fish, the jaws are nonprotractile and cannot extend forward to catch prey.
The frog has a nonprotractile tongue, unlike many other species.

Related Terms

  • antonym: protractile - able to be extended or protruded outward
