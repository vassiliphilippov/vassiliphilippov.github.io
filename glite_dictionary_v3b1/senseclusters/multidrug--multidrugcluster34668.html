multidrug

adjective
involving several drugs
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌdrʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌdrʌɡ / (BrE)
involving or using more than one type of drug or medicine at the same time

Examples

Multidrug treatment
Doctors often recommend multidrug regimens to prevent resistance in treating infections.
The hospital developed a multidrug protocol for patients with complicated illnesses.
Researchers are studying the effects of multidrug combinations on cancer treatment outcomes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monotherapy - treatment using a single drug or medicine
  • synonym: polypharmacy - the use of multiple medications by a patient
