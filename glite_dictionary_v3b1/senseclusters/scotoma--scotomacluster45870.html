scotoma

noun
partial loss of vision; blind spot
Pronunciation: / skoʊˈtoʊmə / (AmE), / skəʊˈtəʊmə / (BrE)
a partial loss of vision or a blind spot in an otherwise normal visual field

Examples

A scotoma in vision
Migraine sufferers sometimes experience a temporary scotoma before the headache begins.
The patient reported a scotoma in her peripheral vision after the accident.
Visual field tests revealed a central scotoma in the left eye.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blind spot - an area where vision is absent or reduced in the visual field
