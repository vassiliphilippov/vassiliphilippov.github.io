Booklist

proper noun
book review publication
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌlɪst / (BrE)
the title of a publication by the American Library Association that reviews and recommends books

Examples

The Booklist publication
The librarian recommended reading the latest Booklist review before buying copies for the class.
Many teachers use Booklist to find new titles for their students.
The award-winning novel was highly praised in Booklist last month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Library Journal - a magazine providing reviews and news for librarians
