knop

noun
small cluster
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɑp / (AmE), / nɒp / (BrE)
a small rounded cluster or bunch, especially of flowers or similar growths

Examples

A knop of flowers
She picked a single tight knop of blossoms and placed it in the vase.
The rose bush was covered in each knop just before the flowers opened.
A delicate knop of berries hung from the end of the branch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bud - a small swelling on a plant that develops into a flower or leaf
  • synonym: cluster - a group of similar things growing or held together
