Examples

Musical overtone
By adjusting her breath, the singer was able to isolate a clear overtone above the main note.

Synonyms

  • harmonic
    6.7 0.21/m

  • partial
    8.6 0.03/m
    partial is a component of a musical sound and can be harmonic or inharmonic, while overtone refers only to higher harmonic tones above the basic note.
