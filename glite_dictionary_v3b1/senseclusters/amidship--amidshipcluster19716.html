amidship

adjective, adverb
in the middle of a ship
Pronunciation: / əˈmɪdˌʃɪp / (AmE), / əˈmɪdʃɪp / (BrE)
in or toward the middle part of a ship or vessel; located in the middle part of a ship or aircraft
