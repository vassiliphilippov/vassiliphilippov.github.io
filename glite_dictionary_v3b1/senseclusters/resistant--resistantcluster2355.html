resistant

adjective
not affected by disease, drug, or force
1.6 4.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɪstənt / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪstənt / (BrE)
(of people, animals, or organisms) not affected by, or able to withstand, a disease, infection, drug, or other damaging agent.

Examples

Resistant to disease
People who are vaccinated are generally more resistant to flu viruses.
Some bacteria have become resistant to antibiotics, making infections harder to treat.
The new crop variety is resistant to drought and several common plant diseases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immune - protected or exempt from a disease or the effects of something
  • antonym: susceptible - likely or liable to be influenced or harmed by a disease or agent
