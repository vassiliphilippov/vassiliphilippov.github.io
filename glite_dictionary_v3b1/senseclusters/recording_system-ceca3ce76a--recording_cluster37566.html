recording system

noun
system for recording sound
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɔrdɪŋ ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːdɪŋ ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
An assembly of equipment used for recording sound, especially in audio or music production.

Examples

Audio recording system
Before the concert, technicians checked every component of the recording system.
Modern musicians often use a digital recording system for their home studios.
The studio installed a new recording system to improve sound quality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: playback system - system for reproducing recorded audio or video
  • synonym: recording equipment - devices or apparatus used to record sound or images
