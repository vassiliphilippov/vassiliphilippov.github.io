monoski

verb
to ski on one ski
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑnoʊˌski / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəʊˌski / (BrE)
to ski using a monoski, a single wide ski on which both feet are placed side by side

Examples

To monoski downhill
He decided to monoski for the first time during his trip to the Alps.
After years of traditional skiing, she wanted to monoski for a new challenge.
Many people find it easier to monoski on gentle slopes when learning the basics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ski - to travel or glide over snow on skis
  • synonym: snowboard - to ride over snow on a snowboard
