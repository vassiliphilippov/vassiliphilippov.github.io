ignorer

noun
person who ignores
8.5
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈnɔrər / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnɔːrə / (BrE)
a person who deliberately pays no attention to someone or something

Examples

The ignorer ignores questions
The ignorer in the group refused to respond to any of the questions.
As an experienced ignorer of distractions, she finished her work quickly.
He was labeled an ignorer after repeatedly failing to reply to messages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bystander - a person who is present but does not take part
  • antonym: observer - a person who watches or notices something
