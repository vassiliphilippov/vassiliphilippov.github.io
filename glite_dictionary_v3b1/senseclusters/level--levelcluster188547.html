level

noun
a floor or story in a building
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈlɛvəl / (BrE)
a floor or story in a building, or a flat horizontal surface

Examples

The top level

Related Terms

  • synonym: floor - one level of a building's interior, often used synonymously with 'level'
  • antonym: slope - a surface that is not level but inclined
  • synonym: story - a level part of a building with rooms, usually between two floors
