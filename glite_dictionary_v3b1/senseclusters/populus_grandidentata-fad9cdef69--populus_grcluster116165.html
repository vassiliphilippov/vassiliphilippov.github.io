Populus grandidentata

noun
species of aspen tree in eastern North America
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːpjələs ˌɡrændˌaɪdɛnˈteɪtə / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊləs ˌɡrandɪdɛnˈtɑːtə / (BrE)
A species of aspen tree with a narrow crown, native to eastern North America, noted for its large, toothed leaves.
