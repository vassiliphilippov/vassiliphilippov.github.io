atmospherically

adverb
in a mood-creating way
Pronunciation: / ˌætməsˈfɛrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌætməsˈfɛrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the atmosphere or creates a particular mood or feeling

Examples

Atmospherically lit room
The room was atmospherically lit with candles, creating a sense of mystery.
The novel is atmospherically rich, drawing readers into its unique world.
Clouds moved atmospherically across the sky as the storm approached.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moodily - in a way that creates a particular mood or feeling
  • antonym: plainly - in a simple or unadorned way
