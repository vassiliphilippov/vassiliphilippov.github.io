cop out

verb
avoid responsibility (due to fear)
8.7 6.2
Pronunciation: /kɑːp aʊt/ (AmE), /kɒp aʊt/ (BrE)
to avoid doing something you should do or avoid taking responsibility, often by making excuses or withdrawing
