osteoarthritic

adjective
affected by osteoarthritis
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstioʊɑrˈθrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒstiəʊɑːˈθrɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by osteoarthritis, a disease that causes joint pain and stiffness

Examples

Osteoarthritic joints
The doctor recommended exercises to help with her osteoarthritic joints.
Many elderly people suffer from osteoarthritic pain in their knees and hips.
He found it difficult to walk long distances because of his osteoarthritic condition.
