pas de trois

noun
ballet dance for three people
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɑ də ˈtrwɑ / (AmE), / pɑː də ˈtrwɑː / (BrE)
A dance in ballet choreographed for three dancers.

Examples

A pas de trois dance
In tonight’s performance, the highlight was the challenging pas de trois featuring the principal dancers.
She was thrilled to be cast in her first pas de trois as part of the company’s spring season.
The dancers performed a beautiful pas de trois in the second act of the ballet.
