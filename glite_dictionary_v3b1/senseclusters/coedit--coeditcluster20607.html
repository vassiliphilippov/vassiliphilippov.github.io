coedit

verb
to edit jointly
Pronunciation: / koʊˈɛdɪt / (AmE), / kəʊˈɛdɪt / (BrE)
to edit something together with one or more other people

Examples

To coedit a journal
They coedit a journal on environmental science.
The two authors decided to coedit the anthology to combine their expertise.
Several professors coedited the textbook to ensure accuracy and clarity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaborate - to work together with others on a project
  • synonym: edit - to prepare written material for publication by correcting or revising it
