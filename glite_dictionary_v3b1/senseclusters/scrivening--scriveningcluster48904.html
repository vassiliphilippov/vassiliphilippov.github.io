scrivening

noun
manual document copying
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɪvənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskrɪvənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of writing or copying documents by hand

Examples

Daily scrivening work
The scribe devoted his day to scrivening important legal documents for the court.
Before the invention of the printing press, scrivening was a highly valued skill in monasteries.
She studied the art of scrivening to better understand medieval manuscripts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transcription - the act of making a written copy of spoken or written material
  • antonym: typing - the act of writing documents using a typewriter or computer
