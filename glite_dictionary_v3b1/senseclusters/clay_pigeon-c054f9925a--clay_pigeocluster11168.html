clay pigeon

noun
disc-shaped shooting target
Pronunciation: / kleɪ ˈpɪdʒən / (AmE), / kleɪ ˈpɪdʒɪn / (BrE)
a disc-shaped target made of baked clay, used as a moving target in shooting sports such as skeet or trapshooting

Examples

A clay pigeon target
He loaded his shotgun and waited for the next clay pigeon to be released.
Skeet shooting involves aiming at a clay pigeon launched into the air.
The shooter hit every clay pigeon during the final round of the competition.
