hipsterism

noun
hipster lifestyle
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪpstərɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈhɪpstərɪzəm / (BrE)
the attitudes, lifestyle, or practices associated with hipsters, often emphasizing alternative culture and trends

Examples

Hipsterism culture
The new art gallery became a symbol of hipsterism in the neighborhood.
Many young people are drawn to hipsterism for its focus on creativity and individuality.
Critics argue that hipsterism sometimes values style over substance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bohemianism - the unconventional lifestyle of artists and free spirits
  • antonym: mainstream - the ideas, attitudes, or activities regarded as normal or conventional
