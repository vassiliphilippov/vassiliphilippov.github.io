tin whistle

noun
a simple metal fipple flute
Pronunciation: / tɪn ˈwɪsəl / (AmE), / tɪn ˈwɪsl̩ / (BrE)
a simple, inexpensive fipple flute, typically made of metal, commonly used in folk and traditional music

Examples

A tin whistle player
He collects vintage tin whistles from around the world.
Many children learn to play the tin whistle as their first musical instrument.
She played a lively tune on her tin whistle at the street festival.
