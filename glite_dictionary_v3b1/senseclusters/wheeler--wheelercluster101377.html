wheeler

noun
vehicle by wheel count
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlər / (AmE), / ˈwiːlə / (BrE)
a vehicle or person described by the number of wheels they have or use, often in compounds like 'two-wheeler' or 'four-wheeler'

Examples

A two-wheeler
He bought a new two-wheeler for his daily commute.

He bought a new wheeler for off-road adventures in the mountains.
The company manufactures both two-wheeler and four-wheeler vehicles.
As a skilled wheeler, she could ride any type of bike with ease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: four-wheeler - a vehicle with four wheels, such as a car or ATV
  • synonym: two-wheeler - a vehicle with two wheels, such as a bicycle or motorcycle
