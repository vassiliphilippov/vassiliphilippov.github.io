all-terrain bike

noun
bicycle for rough/off-road use
7.9 2.8
Pronunciation: /ɔːl.tə.ˈreɪn baɪk/ /; / /ɔːl tə.ˈreɪn baɪk/ (AmE), /ɔːl.tə.ˈreɪn baɪk/ /; / /ɔːl tə.ˈreɪn baɪk/ (BrE)
