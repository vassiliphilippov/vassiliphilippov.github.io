combine

verb
to have two or more things together
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /kəm.ˈbaɪn/ (AmE), /kəm.ˈbaɪn/ (BrE)
to have or possess together; to exhibit two or more qualities, attributes, or things at the same time

Examples

Combine skill and effort
He combines intelligence and determination in his approach to work.
Her art combines modern design with traditional techniques.
The new leader combines charisma and experience, making her ideal for the role.
