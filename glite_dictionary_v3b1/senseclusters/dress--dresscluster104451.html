Synonyms

  • dress up
    2.7 5.99/m
    dress up means putting on special or costume clothes for an event, while dress covers wearing or arranging clothes more generally.
  • put on
    3.0 1.76/m
    put on means placing a specific garment on your body, while dress often means wearing clothes or preparing a whole outfit for an occasion.
  • garb
    7.9 0.38/m
    garb means to clothe someone in a particular style or uniform, while dress is more common and covers normal clothing choices.
  • clothe
    3.2 0.95/m
    clothe means to provide or cover someone with clothes, often in a formal or figurative way, while dress is the everyday verb for wearing clothes.
