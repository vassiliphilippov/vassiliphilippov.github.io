blushy

adjective
slightly pink color
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌʃi / (AmE), / ˈblʌʃi / (BrE)
having a pink or rosy color, especially in a way that suggests a gentle flush or slight redness

Examples

Blushy pink sky
The sky turned a blushy pink as the sun began to set over the horizon.
She wore a blushy scarf that brightened her entire outfit.
His cheeks looked blushy after running in the cold morning air.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pale - having little color or a light shade
  • synonym: rosy - having a pink or reddish color
