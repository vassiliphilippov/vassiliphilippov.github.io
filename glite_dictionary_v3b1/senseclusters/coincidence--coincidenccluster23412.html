coincidence

noun
accidental occurrence; seeming chance event
1.1 2.9
Pronunciation: /koʊ.ˈɪn.sɪ.dəns/ (AmE), /kəʊ.ˈɪn.sɪ.dəns/ (BrE)
an accidental or surprising occurrence of events at the same time or in a way that seems planned

Examples

A strange coincidence
By a strange coincidence, they both wore identical outfits to the party.
It was pure coincidence that we met at the airport on the same day.
Their birthdays falling on the same date was just a happy coincidence.
A rare coincidence occurred when the alarms all rang at the exact same moment.
The coincidence of thunder and lightning is often just a matter of timing.
The experiment measured the coincidence of the two signals within microseconds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accident - an event that happens by chance, without apparent cause
  • antonym: plan - something arranged or intended in advance
  • synonym: simultaneity - the quality of occurring at the same time
