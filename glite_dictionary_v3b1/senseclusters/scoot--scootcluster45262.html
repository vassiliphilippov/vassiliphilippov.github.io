scoot

verb
move or go quickly
2.8 2.13/m
Pronunciation: / skuÀêt / (AmE), / skuÀêt / (BrE)
to move or go quickly or suddenly, especially in a hurried or playful manner

Examples

To scoot quickly
If you scoot over a bit, there will be enough room for everyone on the bench.
She scooted across the street to catch the bus.
The children scoot down the hallway as soon as the bell rings.
