protea family

noun
family of shrubs/trees in the order Proteales
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊti.ə ˈfæm.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈprəʊti.ə ˈfam.ɪ.li / (BrE)
a large family of Australian and South African shrubs and trees with leathery leaves and clustered mostly four-parted flowers, constituting the order Proteales
