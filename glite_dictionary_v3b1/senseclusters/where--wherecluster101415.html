where

adverb
at that place
Pronunciation: / wɛr / (AmE), / weə / (BrE)
at or in the place, point, or situation that has been mentioned or is understood from context

Examples

The place where we met
This is the restaurant where we first met three years ago.
He reached a point where he could no longer continue working.
The city where she grew up has changed a lot over the years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: here - at or in this place
  • synonym: there - at or in that place
