misclassify

verb
to assign incorrectly
5.7
Pronunciation: /mɪs.ˈklæs.ə.faɪ/ /; / /mɪs.ˈklæs.ɪ.faɪ/ (AmE), /mɪs.ˈklɑː.sə.faɪ/ /; / /mɪs.ˈklɑː.sɪ.faɪ/ (BrE)
to put something into the wrong group or category by mistake

Examples
