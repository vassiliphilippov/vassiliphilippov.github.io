last call

noun
final chance to order drinks at a bar
Pronunciation: / læst kɔl / (AmE), / lɑːst kɔːl / (BrE)
the final announcement or opportunity to order drinks before a bar or pub closes

Examples

The last call announcement
If you want another drink, you'd better order now—it's last call.
The bartender announced last call, so everyone hurried to buy their final round.
We decided to leave after last call was announced at midnight.
