counterbore

verb
to enlarge hole opening
8.3 0.01/m

Examples

To counterbore a hole
The carpenter needed to counterbore the holes for the bolts.
Before inserting the screws, she carefully counterbored each hole to ensure a smooth finish.
You should counterbore the wood so the screw heads do not stick out.
