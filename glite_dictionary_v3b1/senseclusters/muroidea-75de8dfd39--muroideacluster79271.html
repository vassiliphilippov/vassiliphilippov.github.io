Muroidea

noun
superfamily of rodents (mice, rats, hamsters, etc.)
9.9
Pronunciation: / mjuːˈrɔɪdiə / (AmE), / mjʊəˈrɔɪdɪə /; / mjʊəˈrɔɪdiə / (BrE)
