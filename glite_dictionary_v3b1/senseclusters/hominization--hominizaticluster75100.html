hominization

noun
human evolutionary process
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑːmənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌhɒmɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the evolutionary process by which humans developed from earlier primates

Examples

Hominization of primates
The study of hominization helps scientists trace the origins of modern humans.
Debates about hominization often focus on when early primates became recognizably human.
New fossil discoveries have provided more evidence about the stages of hominization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropogenesis - the origin and development of humans
  • synonym: human evolution - the process of change by which humans developed over time
