bottomless pit

noun
something requiring endless resources
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑtəm.ləs pɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɒt.əm.ləs pɪt / (BrE)
a situation or thing that seems to consume endless resources or has an insatiable need or demand

Examples

A bottomless pit of money
His constant need for attention felt like a bottomless pit that could never be filled.
The company's budget for repairs became a bottomless pit after a year of unexpected problems.
Trying to renovate that old house turned out to be a bottomless pit for their finances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: money pit - a project or thing into which one keeps pouring money, often without end
