undetailed

adjective
lacking details
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdiːteɪld / (AmE), / ʌnˈdiːteɪld / (BrE)
lacking specific, thorough, or complete details

Examples

An undetailed description
The instructions were so undetailed that I could not assemble the furniture properly.
Her explanation was undetailed, leaving us with many unanswered questions.
An undetailed summary can make it hard to understand the main points of a report.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detailed - including many specific facts or features
  • synonym: vague - not clearly expressed or lacking detail
