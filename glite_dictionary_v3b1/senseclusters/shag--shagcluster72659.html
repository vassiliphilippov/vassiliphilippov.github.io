shag

noun
matted tangle of hair or fiber
4.6 5.7
Pronunciation: /ʃæɡ/ (AmE), /ʃæɡ/ (BrE)
a thick, coarse, or matted mass of hair, fiber, or a fabric with a long, rough surface (often used for carpets or rugs)

Examples

Shag carpet
A layer of dusty shag collected at the base of the old carpet.
He ran his fingers through the dog's curly shag and smiled.
The sheep's thick shag needed to be carefully combed before shearing.
He vacuumed the shag rug twice a week to keep it clean.
Her toes sank deep into the soft pile of the shag as she walked barefoot.
The living room was decorated with a bright orange shag carpet popular in the 1970s.
