Examples

To oxidize metal
If you leave apple slices out in the air, they quickly oxidize and turn brown.
Some metals oxidize more rapidly than others, affecting their durability.
The iron fence began to oxidize after years of exposure to rain.
Certain chemicals can oxidize pollutants in water, making them less harmful.
