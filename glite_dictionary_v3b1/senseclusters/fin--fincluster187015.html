fin

verb
show fins above water while swimming
7.3
Pronunciation: / fɪn / (AmE), / fɪn / (BrE)
(of aquatic animals) to show the fins above the water while swimming.

Examples

Dolphins fin near surface
As we watched the ocean, several dolphins began to fin near our boat.
Sharks often fin close to the surface during feeding time.
The orcas were seen finning along the coastline in the early morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surface - to come up and break the surface of water
