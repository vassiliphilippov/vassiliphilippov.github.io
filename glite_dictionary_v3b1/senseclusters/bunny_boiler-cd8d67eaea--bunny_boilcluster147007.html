bunny boiler

noun
dangerously obsessive or unstable person
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌni ˌbɔɪlər / (AmE), / ˈbʌni ˌbɔɪlə / (BrE)
An obsessive or emotionally unstable person, especially someone who reacts in an extreme or dangerous way to the end of a romantic relationship.

Examples

A bunny boiler ex
After the breakup, he feared his ex-girlfriend might turn into a bunny boiler.
Nobody wants to be labeled a bunny boiler in a new relationship.
She was jokingly called a bunny boiler after sending dozens of unanswered messages to her date.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: psychopath - a person suffering from chronic mental disorder with abnormal or violent social behavior
  • synonym: stalker - a person who illegally follows and watches someone over a period of time
