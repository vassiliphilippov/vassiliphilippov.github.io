autoinhibited

adjective
automatically prevented
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtoʊɪnˈhɪbɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊɪnˈhɪbɪtɪd / (BrE)
automatically prevented from operating by an internal safety or control feature

Examples

Autoinhibited machine
The machine remains autoinhibited until all safety checks are complete.
If a fault is detected, the system becomes autoinhibited to avoid damage.
Some medical devices are autoinhibited when not in use to ensure patient safety.

Related Terms

  • antonym: activated - made active or started operation
  • synonym: disabled - made unable to function or operate
