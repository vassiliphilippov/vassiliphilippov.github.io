(computing) The process of transferring data, programs, or other resources into a computer's memory or into an application for execution or use; often indicated by a progress or 'loading' screen or icon.

Examples

Loading screen
The game showed a spinning loading screen while textures were fetched.
When she opened the app, a small icon said loading as her profile information synced.
During startup, the laptop froze on a loading bar that never reached the end.
