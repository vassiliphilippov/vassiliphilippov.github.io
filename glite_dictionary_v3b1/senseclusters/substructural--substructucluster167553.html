substructural

adjective
supporting structure
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbstrʌkˈtʃʊrəl / (AmE), / ˌsʌbstrʌkˈtʃʊərəl / (BrE)
relating to or forming part of a supporting structure or underlying system

Examples

Substructural support beams
The engineers examined the substructural components of the building for signs of wear.
Damage to the substructural supports can compromise the safety of the entire bridge.
Regular maintenance of substructural elements is essential for long-term stability.
