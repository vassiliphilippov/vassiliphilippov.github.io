interleague

noun
game between leagues
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌliːɡ / (AmE), / ˈɪntəˌliːɡ / (BrE)
a game, match, or series played between teams from different leagues, especially in professional sports

Examples

An interleague game
The baseball season features several interleague games that attract large crowds.
Fans eagerly awaited the annual interleague series between the rival teams.
This year, the interleague schedule was expanded to include more teams from different regions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intra-league - a game played between teams within the same league
  • synonym: match - a sports contest between two teams or players
