Arcadia

noun
ideal rural paradise
8.6
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈkeɪdiə / (AmE), / ɑːˈkeɪdiə / (BrE)
an idealized place or state of simple, peaceful happiness and natural beauty

Examples

A peaceful Arcadia
The artist dreamed of escaping the city to find his own Arcadia in the countryside.
Many stories describe a lost Arcadia where people live in harmony with nature.
For her, the quiet village represented a personal Arcadia far from the noise of modern life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dystopia - an imagined place of great suffering or injustice
  • synonym: paradise - a perfect place of happiness and beauty
  • synonym: utopia - an imagined perfect society or place
