albuminous

adjective
containing or resembling albumin
Pronunciation: / ælˈbjumɪnəs / (AmE), / alˈbjuːmɪnəs / (BrE)
containing, relating to, or resembling albumin (a type of protein found in blood, egg whites, and other substances)

Examples

An albuminous substance
Egg whites are known for their highly albuminous content, providing valuable protein.
The albuminous nature of the solution was crucial for the laboratory experiment.
The doctor noted the presence of albuminous fluid in the sample.

Related Terms

  • synonym: albumin - a simple protein found in blood plasma, egg white, and other substances
