ostensorium

noun
vessel for displaying the consecrated Host
6.3 9.9
Pronunciation: /ɑːs.ˈten.sɔːr.iː.əm/ /; / /ɑːs.ˈten.sər.iː.əm/ (AmE), /ɒs.ˈten.sə.riː.əm/ /; / /ɒs.ˈten.sɔː.riː.əm/ (BrE)
