iconological

adjective
about image interpretation
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪkənəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌaɪkənəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the interpretation of images and symbols, especially in works of art

Examples

Iconological analysis of art
The museum guide offered an iconological explanation of the ancient mural's hidden meanings.
Her research takes an iconological approach to understanding Renaissance paintings.
Students learned to use iconological methods to analyze religious symbols in medieval art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iconographic - relating to the visual images and symbols in art
  • synonym: symbolic - serving as a symbol or representing something else
