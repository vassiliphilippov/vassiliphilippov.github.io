remonstrant

noun
protester or objector
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɑnstrənt / (AmE), / rɪˈmɒnstrənt / (BrE)
a person who protests or formally objects, especially in a public or official way

Examples

A remonstrant speaks out
The remonstrant stood before the council and explained his objections to the new law.
As a known remonstrant, she often spoke out against unfair treatment in her community.
During the meeting, a single remonstrant raised concerns about the proposed changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protester - a person who publicly expresses strong objection
  • antonym: supporter - a person who approves of or backs an idea or cause
