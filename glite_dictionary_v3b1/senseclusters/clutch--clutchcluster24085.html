clutch

noun
mechanical device connecting/disconnecting power
5.8
Pronunciation: / klʌtʃ / (AmE), / klʌtʃ / (BrE)
a device or pedal in a vehicle used to connect or disconnect the engine from the transmission system

Examples

Clutch pedal
Press the clutch pedal fully before starting the engine.
She had trouble shifting gears because the clutch was worn out.
The mechanic replaced the clutch in my car last week.
He pressed the clutch before shifting into second gear.
She accidentally stalled the car by releasing the clutch too quickly.
The old truck's clutch was so worn that it slipped during every gear change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accelerator - a pedal that controls the speed of a vehicle
  • synonym: brake - a device for slowing or stopping a vehicle
