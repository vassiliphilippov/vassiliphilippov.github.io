normothermia

noun
normal body temperature
Pronunciation: / nɔːr.moʊˈθɜːr.mi.ə / (AmE), / nɔː.mɔːˈθɜː.mi.ə / (BrE)
The state of having a normal body temperature.

Examples

Normal normothermia state
After the surgery, doctors worked to restore the patient to normothermia as quickly as possible.
Maintaining normothermia during the procedure reduced the risk of complications.
The nurse monitored her temperature closely to ensure she remained in normothermia throughout the night.

  • antonym: hypothermia - abnormally low body temperature
