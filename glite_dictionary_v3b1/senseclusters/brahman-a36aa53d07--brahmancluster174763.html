Brahman

noun
universal spirit in Hinduism
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑːmən / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːmən / (BrE)
the ultimate reality or universal spirit that is the foundation of all existence in Hinduism

Examples

The Brahman spirit
In Hindu philosophy, Brahman is considered the source of all creation.
Many sacred texts discuss the nature of Brahman and its relationship to the universe.
Meditation is often practiced to realize one's unity with Brahman.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Atman - the individual soul or self in Hindu philosophy
  • synonym: nirvana - a transcendent state free from suffering and individual existence
