at all costs

adverb
no matter what; whatever it takes
1.0
Pronunciation: / ət ɔl kɔsts / (AmE), / ət ɔːl kɒsts / (BrE)
no matter what the expense, effort, or risk may be; whatever happens or is required.

Examples

At all costs protect
He promised to protect his family at all costs.
The rescue team was determined to reach the stranded climbers at all costs.
You should maintain your integrity at all costs, even when it is difficult.
