badman

noun
tough, admired man
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbæd.mən/ (AmE), /ˈbæd.mən/ (BrE)
an informal term, especially in the Caribbean, for a man who displays toughness, style, or sexual confidence and is admired in his community

Examples

A stylish badman
He walked into the club like a true badman, turning heads with his confidence.
In many Caribbean songs, the badman is celebrated for his bold attitude.
Everyone in the neighborhood respected him as a badman who stood up for his friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gangster - a member of a gang, often projecting toughness
  • antonym: gentleman - a polite and refined man
  • synonym: player - a man who is confident and admired, often for his style or romantic success
