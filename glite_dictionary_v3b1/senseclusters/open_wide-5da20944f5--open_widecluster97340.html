open wide

verb
to open the mouth fully
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊpən ˈwaɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊpən ˈwaɪd / (BrE)
to open one's mouth as far as possible, often as instructed by a doctor or dentist

Examples

To open wide mouth
"Open wide, please," the doctor said before looking at the boy's throat.
The dentist asked me to open wide so she could examine my teeth.
When I told the children there was a treat inside, they all opened wide.
