water shrew

noun
small semiaquatic shrew near streams
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ʃru / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ʃruː / (BrE)
a small semiaquatic shrew that typically inhabits areas near fast-flowing streams

Examples

The water shrew near streams
The water shrew feeds on insects and small aquatic animals.
Unlike many other shrews, the water shrew is an excellent swimmer.
We spotted a water shrew darting along the bank of the stream.
