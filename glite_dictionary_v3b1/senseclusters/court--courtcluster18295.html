court

noun
respectful deference or attention
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɔrt / (AmE), / kɔːt / (BrE)
respectful attention or deference, especially as given to someone of high status in hopes of favor

Examples

Pay court
He paid court to the distinguished guests with elaborate courtesy.
Many nobles sought to pay court to the king in hopes of gaining his favor.
Throughout the evening, admirers paid court to the famous poet, eager for her attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disrespect - lack of respect or courtesy
  • synonym: homage - special honor or respect shown publicly
