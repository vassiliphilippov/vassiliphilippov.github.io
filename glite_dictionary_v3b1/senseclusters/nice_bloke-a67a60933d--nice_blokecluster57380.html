nice bloke

noun
pleasant or agreeable man (British, informal)
4.3
Pronunciation: / naɪs bloʊk / (AmE), / naɪs bləʊk / (BrE)
an informal way of referring to a pleasant or agreeable man, commonly used in British English

Examples

A nice bloke friend

Everyone says Tom is a nice bloke who will always lend a hand.
Sarah introduced me to a really nice bloke at her birthday party.
