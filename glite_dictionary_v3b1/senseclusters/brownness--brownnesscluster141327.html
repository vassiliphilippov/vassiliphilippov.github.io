brownness

noun
quality of being brown
Pronunciation: / ˈbraʊnnəs / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being brown in color

Examples

The brownness of leaves
The brownness of the autumn leaves made the park look warm and inviting.
She admired the brownness of his eyes in the sunlight.
The brownness of the bread's crust showed it was well baked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: darkness - the quality of being dark in color
  • antonym: palenness - the quality of being pale in color
