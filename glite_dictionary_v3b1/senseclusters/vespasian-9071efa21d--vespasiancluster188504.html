Vespasian

proper noun
Roman emperor, founder of Flavian dynasty
Pronunciation: / vɛˈspeɪʒ(i)ən /; / vɛˈspeɪʒən / (AmE), / vɛˈspeɪzɪən / (BrE)
Roman emperor (AD 9–79), founder of the Flavian dynasty, who restored stability to the Roman Empire, reformed the army, improved finances, and began building the Colosseum
