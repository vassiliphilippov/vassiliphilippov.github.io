inconsonant

adjective
not in harmony
9.8
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑnsənənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒnsənənt / (BrE)
not in agreement or harmony; inconsistent or incompatible with something else

Examples

Inconsonant behavior
His actions were inconsonant with his stated beliefs.
The new policy is inconsonant with the company's core values.
Their opinions remained inconsonant despite hours of discussion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consonant - in agreement or harmony
  • synonym: discordant - not in agreement; conflicting
  • synonym: incompatible - not able to exist or work together
