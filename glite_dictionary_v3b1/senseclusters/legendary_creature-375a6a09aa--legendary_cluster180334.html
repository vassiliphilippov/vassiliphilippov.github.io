legendary creature

noun
mythical or folkloric being
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛdʒ.ən.der.i ˈkri.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɛdʒ.ən.dər.i ˈkriː.tʃə / (BrE)
an animal or being from myth or folklore, whose existence is not proven but is widely accepted in stories or tradition

Examples

Legendary creatures like dragons
Children love reading tales about legendary creatures such as dragons and unicorns.
Many cultures have stories about legendary creatures that protect forests or rivers.
The Loch Ness Monster is one of the most famous legendary creatures in the world.
