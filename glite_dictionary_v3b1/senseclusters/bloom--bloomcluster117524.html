bloom

noun
period of flourishing or prosperity
4.5
Pronunciation: / bluːm / (AmE), / bluːm / (BrE)
a period of greatest freshness, beauty, or success (for a person, thing, or activity)

Examples

In full bloom
Some believe classical music was in full bloom during the late eighteenth century.
The artist created her best work while in the bloom of her career.
The company enjoyed its greatest bloom during the 1990s tech boom.
His writing career reached its bloom during his thirties.
She was still in the bloom of youth when she left the country for the first time.
They looked back fondly on the bloom of their student days.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decline - a period when something weakens or becomes less successful
  • synonym: peak - the highest point or greatest level
  • synonym: prime - the period in life when a person is at their most active or successful
