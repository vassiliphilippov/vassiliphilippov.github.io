Pope Leo X

proper noun
pope (1513–1521), excommunicated Luther
8.8
Pronunciation: /poʊp ˈliː.oʊ ðə tenθ/ /; / /poʊp ˈliː.əʊ ðə tenθ/ (AmE), /pəʊp ˈliː.əʊ ðə tenθ/ (BrE)
