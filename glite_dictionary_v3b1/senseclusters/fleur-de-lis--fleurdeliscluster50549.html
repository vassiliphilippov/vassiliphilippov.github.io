fleur-de-lis

noun
stylized lily symbol, esp. heraldry
9.8
Pronunciation: / flɜr də ˈliː / (AmE), / flɜː də ˈliː / (BrE)
A stylized lily or iris used as a decorative design or symbol, especially in heraldry.

Examples

Royal fleur-de-lis symbol
Many churches in France are decorated with the fleur-de-lis as a symbol of purity.
The knight's shield was emblazoned with a fleur-de-lis to signify his allegiance.
The royal banner displayed a golden fleur-de-lis at its center.
