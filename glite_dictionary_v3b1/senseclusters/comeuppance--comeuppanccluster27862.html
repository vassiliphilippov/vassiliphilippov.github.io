comeuppance

noun
deserved punishment or fate
9.9
Pronunciation: / kʌmˈʌpəns / (AmE), / kʌmˈʌpəns / (BrE)
a punishment or outcome that someone rightly deserves, especially as a consequence of their actions

Examples

Get comeuppance
After years of cheating his business partners, he finally got his comeuppance in court.
Many believe that dishonest politicians rarely get the comeuppance they deserve.
The villain receives her comeuppance at the end of the movie, much to the audience's satisfaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: desert - something deserved, especially a reward or punishment
  • synonym: justice - the quality of being fair and reasonable; the administration of deserved punishment or reward
  • antonym: reward - something given in return for good or evil done
