cremate

verb
burn a body to ashes (funeral)
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkriː.meɪt / (AmE), / ˈkriː.meɪt / (BrE)
to dispose of a dead body by burning it to ashes, typically as part of a funeral rite

Examples

To cremate a body
After the service, they cremated his remains and scattered the ashes at sea.
It was her wish to be cremated rather than buried.
Many families choose to cremate their loved ones for cultural or practical reasons.
