cinnabar

noun
red mineral, ore of mercury
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnəˌbɑr / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəbɑː / (BrE)
a heavy, bright red mineral (mercuric sulfide) that is the main ore of mercury

Examples

Natural cinnabar mineral
Artists once used ground cinnabar to create vivid red pigments.
Pure cinnabar is toxic due to its high mercury content.
The miners extracted cinnabar from deep within the mountain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vermilion - a bright red pigment made from cinnabar
