gadfly

noun
large fly that annoys livestock
Pronunciation: / ˈɡædˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡæd.flai / (BrE)
a large fly that annoys or bites livestock, such as a horsefly or botfly

Examples

A biting gadfly
A single gadfly can cause cattle to stampede if it continues to bite.
Farmers often take measures to protect their animals from gadflies during the summer months.
The presence of gadflies made the cows restless in the pasture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: botfly - a parasitic fly that lays eggs on mammals
  • synonym: horsefly - a type of biting fly that torments horses and cattle
