Warthin tumor

noun
benign salivary tumor
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːrθɪn ˈtuːmər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːθɪn ˈtjuːmə / (BrE)
a benign tumor of the salivary gland, most commonly found in the parotid gland

Examples

A Warthin tumor
The surgeon explained that the Warthin tumor was not cancerous and could be removed safely.
After the biopsy, the patient was relieved to learn it was a Warthin tumor rather than something malignant.
A Warthin tumor typically appears as a painless lump near the jaw or below the ear.

Related Terms

  • antonym: malignant tumor - a cancerous growth that can spread to other parts of the body
  • synonym: pleomorphic adenoma - a common benign tumor of the salivary glands
