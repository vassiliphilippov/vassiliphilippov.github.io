unswaddle

verb
to unwrap a baby
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈswɑːdəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈswɒdəl / (BrE)
to remove the swaddling cloth from a baby; to unwrap a newborn from a blanket or cloth.

Examples

To unswaddle a baby
The nurse gently unswaddled the baby to check her temperature.
After feeding, she unswaddled her son so he could stretch his arms and legs.
They unswaddle newborns before giving them their first bath in the hospital.

Related Terms

  • antonym: swaddle - to wrap a baby tightly in cloths or blankets
  • synonym: unwrap - to remove a covering or wrapping from something
