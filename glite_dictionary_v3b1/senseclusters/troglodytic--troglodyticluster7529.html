troglodytic

adjective
primitive or backward
Pronunciation: / ˌtrɑːɡləˈdɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌtrɒɡləˈdɪtɪk / (BrE)
describing behavior or attitudes that are extremely primitive, uncivilized, or socially backward

Examples

Troglodytic thinking
His troglodytic views on gender roles shocked everyone at the conference.
The film depicted a troglodytic society untouched by modern technology.
She refused to accept such troglodytic attitudes in her workplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbaric - cruel or uncivilized
  • antonym: civilized - polite and socially advanced
