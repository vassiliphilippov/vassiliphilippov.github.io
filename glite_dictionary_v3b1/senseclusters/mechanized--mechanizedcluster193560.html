mechanized

adjective
operated by machinery
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛk.əˌnaɪzd /; / ˈmɛkəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈmɛk.ə.naɪzd /; / ˈmɛkənaɪzd / (BrE)
operated or equipped with machinery, especially instead of being done by hand (can refer to vehicles, processes, or activities)

Examples

The mechanised infantry advanced quickly across the open terrain.
With the introduction of mechanised farming, agricultural productivity increased dramatically.
