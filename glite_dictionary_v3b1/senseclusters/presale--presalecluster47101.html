presale

noun
early purchase period
3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpriː.seɪl/ (AmE), /ˈpriː.seɪl/ (BrE)
a period when products or tickets are available for purchase before the official sale begins

Examples

A presale period
The presale for the new smartphone starts next week for registered customers.
Members received a special code to access the presale tickets before the public.
During the presale, fans could buy limited edition merchandise ahead of everyone else.

Related Terms

  • antonym: general sale - the main period when products or tickets are available to everyone
  • synonym: preorder - an order placed for a product before it is available
