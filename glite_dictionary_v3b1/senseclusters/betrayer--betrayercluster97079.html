betrayer

noun
one who is disloyal or unfaithful
1.8 5.1
Pronunciation: /bɪ.ˈtreɪ.ər/ (AmE), /bɪ.ˈtreɪ.ə/ (BrE)
a person who is disloyal or betrays the trust of others, for example by revealing secrets
