delight

noun
something that gives great pleasure
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlaɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈlaɪt / (BrE)
great pleasure or something that brings pleasure or happiness

Examples

A delight to see

This chocolate cake is an absolute delight for anyone with a sweet tooth.
It was a pure delight to listen to their beautiful music.
She expressed her delight at winning the first prize in the competition.
The children's faces lit up with delight when they saw the birthday cake.
