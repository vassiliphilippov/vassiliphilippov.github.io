agamic

adjective
reproducing without sexual fusion
Pronunciation: / əˈɡæmɪk / (AmE), / əˈɡamɪk / (BrE)
(of reproduction) not involving the fusion of male and female gametes; occurring without sexual union.

Examples

Agamic reproduction
Many plants are capable of agamic reproduction, producing offspring without seeds.
Some lizards can breed agamically, without needing a mate.
The scientist studied how certain insects developed through agamic methods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asexual - not involving sexual reproduction
