face mask

noun
cosmetic facial treatment
2.7
Pronunciation: / c8fe6as ccm6sk / (AmE), / c8fe6as ccm51d0sk / (BrE)
A cosmetic product applied to the face to improve skin condition or appearance; may be a sheet, clay, cream, or peel-off formulation.

Examples

Clay face mask
After the long flight, she put on a cooling face mask to hydrate her skin.
The esthetician recommended a clay face mask to reduce oiliness and unclog pores.
Teenagers often use peel-off face masks before parties to improve skin appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facial - a beauty treatment for the face, often including cleansing and masks
  • synonym: sheet mask - a disposable cloth or paper mask soaked in serum and applied to the face
