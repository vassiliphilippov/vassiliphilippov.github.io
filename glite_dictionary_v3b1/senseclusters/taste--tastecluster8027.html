taste

verb
experience or enjoy briefly
Pronunciation: / teɪst / (AmE), / teɪst / (BrE)
to experience or enjoy something briefly, especially something new or desirable

Examples

To taste adventure
He wanted to taste adventure just once before settling down.
She finally got to taste freedom after years of confinement.
They will taste victory if they win the next match.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sample - to try a small amount of something
  • synonym: savor - to enjoy the taste or experience of something
