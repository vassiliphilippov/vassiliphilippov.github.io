goalpost

noun
post forming part of a goal in sports
Pronunciation: / ˈgoʊlˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊlˌpəʊst / (BrE)
one of the two upright posts that form part of the goal in sports such as football, rugby, or hockey, usually joined by a crossbar

Examples

The goalpost and crossbar
After hitting the goalpost, the ball bounced back onto the field.
He scored by sending the ball just inside the left goalpost.
The referee checked to see if the goalpost was properly secured before the match began.

  • synonym: crossbar - the horizontal bar connecting two goalposts
