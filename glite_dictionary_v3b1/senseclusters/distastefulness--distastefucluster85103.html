distastefulness

noun
quality of being offensive or unpleasant
2.0
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈteɪstfəlnəs / (AmE), / dɪsˈteɪstfəlnəs /; / dɪsˈteɪstfʊlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being unpleasant, offensive, or causing dislike (in general or in taste/experience)

Examples
