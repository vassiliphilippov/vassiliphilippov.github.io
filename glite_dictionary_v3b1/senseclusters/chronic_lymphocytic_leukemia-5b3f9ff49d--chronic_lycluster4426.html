chronic lymphocytic leukemia

A type of cancer in which the bone marrow makes too many lymphocytes, a kind of white blood cell; most common in older adults.

Examples

Chronic lymphocytic leukemia diagnosis
Chronic lymphocytic leukemia often progresses slowly and may not cause symptoms for years.
He was diagnosed with chronic lymphocytic leukemia after a routine blood test showed abnormal results.
Recent advances in treatment have improved the prognosis for patients with chronic lymphocytic leukemia.
