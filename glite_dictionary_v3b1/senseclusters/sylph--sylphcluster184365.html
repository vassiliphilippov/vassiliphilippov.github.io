sylph

noun
a slender, graceful young woman
7.0
Pronunciation: / sɪlf / (AmE), / sɪlf / (BrE)
a slender and graceful young woman, especially one considered ethereal or delicate in appearance

Examples

A graceful sylph
At the party, the hostess was a sylph in a flowing silver gown.
He described the dancer as a sylph with the grace and lightness of the wind.
She moved across the stage like a sylph, capturing everyone's attention with her elegance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nymph - a beautiful, graceful young woman, often in mythology
  • synonym: waif - a thin, often fragile-looking young woman
