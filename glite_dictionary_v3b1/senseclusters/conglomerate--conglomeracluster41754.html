conglomerate

noun
large corporation with diverse subsidiaries
7.6
Pronunciation: / kənˈɡlɑː.mɚ.ət / (AmE), / kənˈɡlɒm.ər.ət / (BrE)
a large corporation formed by the merging of several different companies, often operating in various industries, under a single ownership or management

Examples

A business conglomerate
A conglomerate may operate in industries as varied as food production, energy, and entertainment.
Over the past decade, the company has grown into a global conglomerate through mergers and acquisitions.
The media conglomerate owns television stations, newspapers, and magazines across the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corporation - a large company or group of companies authorized to act as a single entity
