play the fool

verb
act in a silly or foolish manner
Pronunciation: / pleɪ ðə fuːl / (AmE), / pleɪ ðə fuːl / (BrE)
to behave in a deliberately silly or foolish way, often to amuse others or avoid seriousness

Examples

To play the fool
Stop trying to play the fool; we know you understand what's going on.
Though she liked to play the fool at parties, she was actually very clever.
Whenever he gets nervous, he tends to play the fool to lighten the mood.
