cedilla

noun
diacritical mark under c
9.9 7.8
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈdɪl.ə/ (AmE), /sə.ˈdɪl.ə/ (BrE)
A diacritical mark ( ¸ ) placed below the letter c to indicate it is pronounced as s, as in the French word 'façade'.

Examples

The cedilla mark
He asked his teacher what the cedilla in the word meant.
In French, a cedilla changes the pronunciation of 'c' before a, o, or u.
The word 'façade' contains a cedilla under the letter c.
