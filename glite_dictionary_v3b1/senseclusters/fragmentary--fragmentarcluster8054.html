fragmentary

adjective
consisting of incomplete or disconnected parts
9.1 5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfræɡ.mənˌtɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈfræɡ.mən.təri / (BrE)
Consisting of small or incomplete, disconnected parts; not whole or continuous.

Examples

A fragmentary memory

The archeologists could only analyze the fragmentary evidence found at the site.
The document survives only in a fragmentary form after centuries of deterioration.
