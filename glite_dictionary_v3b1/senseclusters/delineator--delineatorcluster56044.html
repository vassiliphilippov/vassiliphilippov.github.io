delineator

noun
one who outlines
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪniˌeɪtər / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪniːeɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that describes, outlines, or marks the boundaries of something

Examples

A delineator of borders
The delineator carefully marked the boundaries of the new property on the map.
As a skilled delineator, she was able to outline the main features of the proposal clearly.
The road delineator posts help drivers stay within their lanes at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: definer - a person or thing that states or explains the meaning or limits of something
  • synonym: describer - a person who gives an account or representation of something
