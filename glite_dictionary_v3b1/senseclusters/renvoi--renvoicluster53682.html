renvoi

noun
referral back for action
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈren.vwɑː/ (AmE), /ˈren.vwɑː/ (BrE)
the act of sending a matter back to another person, body, or authority for further consideration or action, especially in legal contexts

Examples

Renvoi to a court
The judge ordered a renvoi so the case could be reconsidered by the lower court.
In international law, a renvoi may occur when a legal issue is referred back to another jurisdiction.
After reviewing the evidence, the committee decided on a renvoi to the original panel for further analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: referral - the act of directing something to another person or authority
  • synonym: remand - to send a case back to a lower court for further action
