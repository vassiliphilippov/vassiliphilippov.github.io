filarial

adjective
relating to filaria worms
5.1
Pronunciation: / fɪˈlɛriəl / (AmE), / fɪˈlɑːrɪəl / (BrE)
Relating to or caused by filaria, a type of parasitic worm; or affected by such parasites.

Examples

Filarial worm infection
Mosquitoes are common vectors for transmitting filarial diseases in tropical regions.
Researchers are working on new drugs to combat filarial parasites in humans.
The patient showed symptoms of a filarial infection after returning from abroad.
