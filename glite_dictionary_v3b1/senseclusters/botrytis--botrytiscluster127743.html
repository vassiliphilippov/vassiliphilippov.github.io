botrytis

noun
grape-infecting fungus
9.3
Pronunciation: /bə.ˈtrɪ.tɪs/ (AmE), /bə.ˈtrɪ.tɪs/ (BrE)
a type of fungus (mold) that infects plants, especially grapes, sometimes causing 'noble rot' used in making sweet wines
