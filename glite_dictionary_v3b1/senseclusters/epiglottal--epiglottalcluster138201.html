epiglottal

adjective
of the epiglottis
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈɡlɑtəl / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈɡlɒtəl / (BrE)
relating to or produced with the epiglottis, especially in the context of speech sounds

Examples

Epiglottal sound
An epiglottal sound is produced by using the epiglottis in the vocal tract.

The doctor explained that epiglottal inflammation can affect a person's voice.
