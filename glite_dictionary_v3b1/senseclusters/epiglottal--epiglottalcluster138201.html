epiglottal

adjective
of the epiglottis
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈɡlɑtəl / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈɡlɒtəl / (BrE)
relating to or produced with the epiglottis, especially in the context of speech sounds

Examples

Epiglottal sound
An epiglottal sound is produced by using the epiglottis in the vocal tract.
Linguists study rare epiglottal consonants found in some languages of the Caucasus.
The doctor explained that epiglottal inflammation can affect a person's voice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glottal - relating to the glottis or the space between the vocal cords
  • synonym: pharyngeal - relating to the pharynx, especially in speech sounds
