Roman arch

noun
a semi-circular architectural arch
7.0 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊ.mən ɑːrtʃ/ /; / /ˈrəʊ.mən ɑːrtʃ/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊ.mən ɑːtʃ/ (BrE)
An arch with a semi-circular shape, formed by drawing a curve from a single center point, characteristic of Roman architecture.
