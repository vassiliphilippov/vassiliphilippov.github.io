standup comedian

noun
solo performer who tells jokes on stage
Pronunciation: / ˈstændˌʌp kəˈmiːdiən / (AmE), / ˈstandʌp kəˈmiːdɪən / (BrE)
A comedian who performs solo, typically standing on stage and delivering jokes directly to an audience.

Examples

A standup comedian show
Many aspiring performers move to big cities to become a standup comedian.
She started her career as a standup comedian before acting in films.
The standup comedian entertained the audience with witty observations about daily life.
