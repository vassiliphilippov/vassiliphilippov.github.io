conceptualism

noun
idea-focused theory
8.2
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈsep.tʃuː.ə.lɪ.zəm/ /; / /kən.ˈsep.tʃʊə.lɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /kən.ˈsep.tʃʊə.lɪ.zəm/ /; / /kən.ˈsep.tʃuː.ə.lɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a theory or artistic style that emphasizes the importance of ideas over physical objects or forms

Examples

Modern art conceptualism
Many modern artists are inspired by conceptualism and focus on the meaning behind their work.
The exhibition explores the influence of conceptualism on contemporary sculpture.
Some critics argue that conceptualism challenges traditional definitions of art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conceptual art - an art movement where the idea is more important than the finished object
  • antonym: realism - an artistic style that aims to represent subjects as they appear in real life
