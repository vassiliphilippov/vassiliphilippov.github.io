unflanked

adjective
not bordered
8.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈflæŋkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈflæŋkt / (BrE)
not having anything on either side; not flanked or bordered by something.

Examples

Unflanked castle
The castle stood unflanked on the hill, exposed to the wind from all directions.
Unlike the other streets, this avenue was unflanked by buildings or trees.
The monument appeared lonely and unflanked in the wide open square.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bordered - having a border or edge along the side
  • antonym: flanked - having something on either or both sides
