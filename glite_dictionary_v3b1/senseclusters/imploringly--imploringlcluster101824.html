In a way that shows someone is begging or earnestly asking for something; in a beseeching manner.

Examples

Look imploringly at
He raised his hands imploringly toward the group, begging them to listen.
She looked at him imploringly, hoping he would understand her desperate need.
The child reached out imploringly for his mother's hand after falling down.

Synonyms

  • beseechingly
    7.8 0.09/m
    beseechingly is a very strong, urgent way to ask with serious emotion, while imploringly is earnest but slightly softer.
  • pleadingly
    4.3 0.25/m
    pleadingly shows an emotional, urgent request often in speech, while imploringly feels more intense and desperate.
  • entreatingly
    7.4 0.01/m
    entreatingly sounds formal or old-fashioned and polite, while imploringly is a direct, more emotional plea.
  • suppliantly
    8.4 0.00/m
    suppliantly shows humble, submissive begging often before authority, while imploringly is urgent and focused on strong need.
