imploringly

adverb
in a pleading or begging way
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈplɔrɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔːrɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows someone is begging or earnestly asking for something; in a beseeching manner.

Examples

Look imploringly at
He raised his hands imploringly toward the group, begging them to listen.
She looked at him imploringly, hoping he would understand her desperate need.
The child reached out imploringly for his mother's hand after falling down.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demandingly - in a manner expressing a strong insistence, not begging
  • synonym: pleadingly - in a manner showing that one is begging or appealing
