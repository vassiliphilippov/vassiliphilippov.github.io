Jenga

noun
stacking and removing block game
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈdʒeŋɡə / (BrE)
a game in which players take turns removing wooden blocks from a stacked tower and balancing them on top, with the object of not causing the tower to collapse

Examples

Jenga game tower
He lost the Jenga game when the tower toppled over.
She carefully pulled a block from the Jenga tower, hoping it wouldn't fall.
We played Jenga all evening and the tension was intense.
