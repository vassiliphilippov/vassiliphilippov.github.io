holding

noun
act of retaining something
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of retaining or keeping something in one's possession or control

Examples

Holding of power
His holding of the documents was questioned during the investigation.
Prolonged holding of your breath can be dangerous.
The holding of power by a single individual often leads to conflict.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keeping - the act of retaining possession or custody
  • antonym: letting go - the act of releasing something held or retained
