civetlike

adjective
resembling a civet
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvɪtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɪvɪtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a civet, a small nocturnal mammal with a long body and spotted fur

Examples

Civetlike animal
The mysterious creature had a civetlike tail and moved silently through the undergrowth.
Her pet's civetlike markings made it stand out among the other animals at the shelter.
Scientists noted the animal's civetlike features during their expedition in the rainforest.
