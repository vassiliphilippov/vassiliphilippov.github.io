aper

noun
person who mimics or imitates others
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈeɪpə / (BrE)
a person who imitates or mimics someone else's words or actions, often unoriginally

Examples

An aper of others
Rather than being creative, he acted as an aper of his more talented peers.
She was called an aper because she repeated every joke he made.
The critics dismissed him as a mere aper of famous artists, lacking any originality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imitator - a person who copies the behavior or actions of others
  • antonym: original - a person who creates or originates, rather than imitates
