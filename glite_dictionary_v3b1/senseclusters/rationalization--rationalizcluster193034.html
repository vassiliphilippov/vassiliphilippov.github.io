rationalization

noun
systematic reorganization for efficiency
2.6 5.1
Pronunciation: /ræ.ʃə.nəl.ɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ræ.ʃ.nə.lɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ræ.ʃə.nəl.ɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ræ.ʃ.nə.lɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of organizing or improving a process, business, or system to make it more logical or efficient
