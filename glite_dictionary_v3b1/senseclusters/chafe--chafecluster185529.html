chafe

verb
to warm by rubbing
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪf / (AmE), / tʃeɪf / (BrE)
to make or become warm by rubbing, especially with the hands

Examples

To chafe cold hands
After coming inside from the cold, he began to chafe his hands together by the fire.
She chafed her fingers briskly to restore feeling.
The little boy chafed his arms to warm up after playing in the snow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rub - to move something over the surface of another with pressure
