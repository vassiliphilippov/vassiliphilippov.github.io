stocker

noun
store shelf worker
2.0
Pronunciation: /ˈstɑː.kər/ (AmE), /ˈstɒ.kə/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to put products on store shelves and keep them stocked

Examples

Grocery stocker
The stocker worked overnight to ensure all the shelves were full by morning.
As a stocker, she organizes products and checks for expired items every day.
Many students take part-time jobs as a stocker in local supermarkets.
