role player

noun
participant in role-playing games
3.9
Pronunciation: /roʊl ˈpleɪ.ər/ (AmE), /rəʊl ˈpleɪ.ə/ (BrE)
a person who adopts and plays a fictional character to participate in tabletop or online role-playing games or role-play communities, often collaboratively and for entertainment.

Examples

Online role player
She is a well-known role player in several online tabletop RPG communities.
After work, he meets friends to be a role player at the weekly D&D session.
Her writing impressed the community; as a role player, she creates vivid characters and backstories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: player - a person who takes part in a game
