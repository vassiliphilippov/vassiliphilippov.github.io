Leboyer method

noun
childbirth method minimizing trauma
8.4
Pronunciation: /lə.ˈbɔɪ.eɪ ˈmeθ.əd/ /; / /lə.ˈbɔɪ.ər ˈmeθ.əd/ (AmE), /lə.ˈbɔɪ.eɪ ˈmeθ.əd/ /; / /lə.ˈbɔɪ.ə ˈmeθ.əd/ (BrE)
a method of childbirth that aims to reduce trauma for the newborn by delivering the baby in a quiet, dimly lit room without pulling the head and encouraging immediate bonding with the mother
