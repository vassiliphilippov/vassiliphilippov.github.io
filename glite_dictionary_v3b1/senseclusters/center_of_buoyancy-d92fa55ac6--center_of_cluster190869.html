center of buoyancy

noun
center of mass of submerged part of a floating body
4.9 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsen.tər əv ˈbɔɪ.ən.siː/ /; / /ˈsen.tər ə ˈbɔɪ.ən.siː/ (AmE), /ˈsen.tə əv ˈbɔɪ.ən.siː/ /; / /ˈsen.tə ə ˈbɔɪ.ən.siː/ (BrE)
the point representing the center of mass of the submerged part of a floating body, through which the buoyant force acts
