Lablab

noun
genus of flowering plants (hyacinth bean)
Pronunciation: / ˈlæb.læb / (AmE), / ˈlæb.læb / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the pea family, with one species, the hyacinth bean (Lablab purpureus), widely cultivated for food and ornamental purposes.
