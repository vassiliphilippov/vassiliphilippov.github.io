automatic

noun
self-operating device
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
a machine or device that operates by itself with minimal human intervention, especially an automatic transmission in a car.

Examples

Car automatic
Many drivers find an automatic easier to use than a manual transmission.
The new washing machine is an automatic that adjusts settings by itself.
He bought an automatic because he wanted a car that was simple to drive.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manual - a machine or device operated by hand or human control
