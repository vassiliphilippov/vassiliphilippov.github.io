plod

noun
slow, heavy walk
Pronunciation: / plɑd / (AmE), / plɒd / (BrE)
a slow, heavy, and laborious walk

Examples

A slow plod
After hours of hiking, their plod became even slower.
She watched his lonely plod across the muddy field.
The unmistakable plod of boots echoed down the hallway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trudge - to walk slowly and with heavy steps, typically from exhaustion or harsh conditions
