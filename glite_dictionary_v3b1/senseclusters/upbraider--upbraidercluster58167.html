upbraider

noun
person who reproaches or blames
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈbreɪdər / (AmE), / ʌpˈbreɪdə / (BrE)
a person who reproaches or criticizes someone severely

Examples

A strict upbraider
He acquired a reputation as an upbraider, never missing an opportunity to chastise others.
No one was surprised when the notorious upbraider voiced her disapproval at the meeting.
The teacher became known as an upbraider of any student who failed to complete their assignments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: admirer - a person who regards something with respect or approval
  • synonym: critic - a person who expresses unfavorable opinions
