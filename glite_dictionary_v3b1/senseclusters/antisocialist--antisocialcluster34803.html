antisocialist

noun
opponent of socialism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈsoʊʃəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈsəʊʃəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who opposes socialism or campaigns against socialist parties or policies

Examples

An antisocialist leader
The candidate was labeled an antisocialist because of his strong opposition to government-run programs.
Many antisocialists attended the rally to protest the new economic reforms.
Her views as an antisocialist often sparked debates with her colleagues who supported socialist policies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anti-communist - a person who opposes communism or communist parties
  • antonym: socialist - a person who supports socialism or socialist policies
