orally

adverb
by mouth; via the mouth
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.ə.liː/ /; / /ˈɑːr.ə.liː/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.rə.liː/ (BrE)
By mouth; in such a way that something is taken through the mouth, especially medicine.

Examples

Take medicine orally

Certain antibiotics can be administered orally or by injection, depending on the severity of the infection.
The medication should be taken orally once daily with water.
