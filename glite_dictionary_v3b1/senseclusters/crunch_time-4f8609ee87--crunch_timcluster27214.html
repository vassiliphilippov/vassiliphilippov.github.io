crunch time

noun
critical period for urgent action
9.4 3.4
Pronunciation: /krʌntʃ taɪm/ (AmE), /krʌntʃ taɪm/ (BrE)
a critical period when decisive action or intense effort is required, especially to meet a deadline or achieve a goal

Examples

During crunch time
Coaches often say that crunch time is when true champions rise to the occasion.
It's crunch time now—the exam starts in less than an hour!
The team pulled several all-nighters during crunch time to finish the report.
