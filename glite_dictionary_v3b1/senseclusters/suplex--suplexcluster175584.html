suplex

noun
wrestling throw
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːplɛks / (AmE), / ˈsuːplɛks / (BrE)
a wrestling move in which a competitor lifts and throws their opponent backward onto the mat

Examples

A wrestling suplex
The wrestler executed a powerful suplex that brought the crowd to its feet.
After practicing for weeks, she finally mastered the suplex in her training session.
He surprised his opponent with a sudden suplex during the championship match.

Related Terms

  • synonym: body slam - a wrestling move where an opponent is lifted and slammed to the mat
  • antonym: pin - to hold an opponent's shoulders on the mat in wrestling
