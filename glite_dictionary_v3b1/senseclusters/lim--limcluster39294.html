lim

sym
limit symbol
9.6
Pronunciation: / lɪm / (AmE), / lɪm / (BrE)
the mathematical symbol used to indicate the limit of a function or sequence as a variable approaches a particular value

Examples

The lim symbol
In calculus, the lim symbol is used to express the concept of a limit.
The equation included the lim notation to show what happens as x approaches zero.
