precision rifle

noun
high-powered, long-range accurate rifle
3.6 6.3
Pronunciation: /prɪ.ˈsɪʒ.ən ˈraɪ.fəl/ /; / /priː.ˈsɪʒ.ən ˈraɪ.fəl/ (AmE), /prɪ.ˈsɪʒ.ən ˈraɪ.fəl/ (BrE)
a high-powered rifle designed for accuracy at long ranges, often used in military or law enforcement operations to engage distant targets, including lightly armored vehicles and aircraft.

Examples

A precision rifle shot

The new precision rifle is capable of hitting targets more than a mile away with remarkable accuracy.
