precision rifle

noun
high-powered, long-range accurate rifle
3.6
Pronunciation: / prɪˈsɪʒən ˈraɪfəl / (AmE), / prɪˈsɪʒən ˈraɪfəl / (BrE)
a high-powered rifle designed for accuracy at long ranges, often used in military or law enforcement operations to engage distant targets, including lightly armored vehicles and aircraft.

Examples

A precision rifle shot
He spent months training to master the use of a precision rifle for long-range competitions.
The military deployed a precision rifle team to monitor the area from a distant hillside.
The new precision rifle is capable of hitting targets more than a mile away with remarkable accuracy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sniper rifle - a high-precision rifle designed for sniping at targets from a concealed position
