annihilate

verb
destroy or kill completely
1.6
Pronunciation: / əˈnaɪ.ə.leɪt / (AmE), / əˈnʌɪ.ɪ.leɪt / (BrE)
to destroy completely or kill in very large numbers

Examples

To annihilate entire communities
If left unchecked, the virus could annihilate entire communities in a matter of weeks.
The invading army threatened to annihilate the entire city if they did not surrender.
The powerful earthquake annihilated several villages in the remote area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eradicate - to destroy or remove completely
  • synonym: exterminate - to destroy completely, especially a group of living things
