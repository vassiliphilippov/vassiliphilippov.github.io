planisphere

noun
adjustable star chart
Pronunciation: / ˈplænɪˌsfɪr / (AmE), / ˈplænɪsfɪə / (BrE)
a circular star chart that can be adjusted to show the visible stars for any date and time

Examples

Star planisphere
She used a planisphere to identify constellations in the night sky.
A planisphere is a helpful tool for learning about the stars visible on any given night.
Before going stargazing, he set his planisphere to match the date and time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: star chart - a map showing the positions of stars and constellations
