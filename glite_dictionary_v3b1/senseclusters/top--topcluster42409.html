top

noun
circus tent
5.3 1.10/m
Pronunciation: / tÉ‘p / (AmE), / tÉ’p / (BrE)
a large tent, especially one used to house the audience at a circus performance, often called a 'big top'.

Examples

Circus top
After the rain, the top had to be dried before the next show could begin.
Children rushed into the top to find the best seats for the circus.
The acrobats performed under the top while the audience watched in awe.

Synonyms

  • big top
    3.5 0.15/m
    big top is the informal circus name for the main show tent, while top is the same circus tent word used more widely.
  • circus tent
    2.5 0.15/m
    circus tent is the general term for any large tent used for shows and performers, while top names the main circus tent.
  • round top
    8.1 0.00/m
    round top is a canvas tent shape often used for audiences at circuses, while top is the common circus name for it.
