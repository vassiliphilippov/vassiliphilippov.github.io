embarrassment

noun
state of financial difficulty
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbɛrəsmənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈbærəsmənt / (BrE)
a state of financial difficulty or inadequacy; the condition of being short of money or resources

Examples

Financial embarrassment
After his investments failed, Michael was left in a state of embarrassment.
The company faced an acute embarrassment when it could not meet its payroll obligations.
To avoid financial embarrassment, she took out a modest loan from the bank.

Related Terms

  • synonym: difficulty - a situation that makes achieving something hard
  • antonym: prosperity - the state of being successful or flourishing financially
