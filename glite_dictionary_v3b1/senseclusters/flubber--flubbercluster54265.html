flubber

noun
stretchy toy material
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌbər / (AmE), / ˈflʌbə / (BrE)
a soft, stretchy, and sticky toy material, similar to slime, often made from glue and borax

Examples

Green flubber toy
The children made green flubber in science class and played with it for hours.
She stretched the flubber as far as she could before it snapped back into shape.
Parents often supervise when kids play with flubber to avoid a sticky mess.

Related Terms

  • synonym: putty - a soft, malleable substance used as a toy or for repairs
  • synonym: slime - a soft, sticky, and stretchy toy material popular with children
