breaker point

noun
contact in ignition distributor
5.9 8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkər pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkə pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a movable contact in a vehicle's distributor, which opens and closes as the cam rotates, controlling the flow of current to the spark plugs

Examples
