eau de vie

noun
clear fruit brandy
6.4 9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊ də ˈvi / (AmE), / əʊ də ˈviː / (BrE)
A clear, colorless fruit brandy distilled from fermented fruit juice or mash.

Examples

A glass of eau de vie
After dinner, she offered her guests a small glass of eau de vie made from plums.
Bartenders sometimes use eau de vie to add a subtle fruit flavor to cocktails.
The distillery is famous for producing high-quality eau de vie from local cherries.
