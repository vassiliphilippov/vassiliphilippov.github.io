halibut

noun
large flatfish used as food
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhæləbət / (AmE), / ˈhælɪbət / (BrE)
the lean, edible flesh of a very large flatfish of the Atlantic or Pacific, valued as food

Examples

Halibut fillet dish
Fresh halibut is often considered a delicacy in coastal restaurants.
He bought a large halibut at the fish market for the weekend barbecue.
We grilled the halibut and served it with a lemon butter sauce for dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flounder - another type of flatfish
  • synonym: sole - a different but similar flatfish valued as food
