pleasing

adjective
pleasant; agreeable to the senses
Pronunciation: / ˈpliːzɪŋ /; / ˈplizɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpliːzɪŋ / (BrE)
giving a sense of enjoyment, satisfaction, or being attractive or agreeable

Examples

A pleasing aroma
A pleasing aroma filled the kitchen as the bread baked.
He spoke in a pleasing tone that instantly put her at ease.
The painting had a pleasing effect on everyone who saw it.
He brought home a pleasing piece of news for the whole family.
Her work received many pleasing comments from the judges.
The combination of colors creates a visually pleasing effect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: displeasing - not pleasant; causing dissatisfaction
  • synonym: pleasant - giving a sense of happy satisfaction or enjoyment
