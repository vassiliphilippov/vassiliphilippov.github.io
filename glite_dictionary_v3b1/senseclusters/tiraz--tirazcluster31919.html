tiraz

noun
Islamic textile workshop
9.9
Pronunciation: /tɪ.ˈrɑːz/ /; / /tə.ˈrɑːz/ (AmE), /tɪ.ˈrɑːz/ /; / /tə.ˈrɑːz/ (BrE)
a medieval Islamic royal or state workshop where embroidered textiles and inscribed bands were produced, often for rulers or officials
