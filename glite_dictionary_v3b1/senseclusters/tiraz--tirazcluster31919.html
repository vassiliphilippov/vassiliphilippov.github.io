tiraz

noun
Islamic textile workshop
Pronunciation: / tiˈrɑz / (AmE), / tiˈrɑːz / (BrE)
a medieval Islamic royal or state workshop where embroidered textiles and inscribed bands were produced, often for rulers or officials

Examples

Royal tiraz workshop
The caliph ordered new robes from the royal tiraz to show his authority at the ceremony.
Many luxurious garments produced in the tiraz were given as gifts to high-ranking officials.
Historians study textiles from the tiraz to learn about medieval Islamic art and culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atelier - an artist's or designer's workshop
  • synonym: manufactory - a place where goods are manufactured
