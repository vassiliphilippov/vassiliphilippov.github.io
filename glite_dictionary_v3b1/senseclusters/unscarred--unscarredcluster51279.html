unscarred

adjective
not emotionally harmed
4.9 0.03/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàsk…ërd / (AmE), /  ånÀàsk…ëÀêd / (BrE)
not harmed or marked, especially emotionally or psychologically, by traumatic or damaging experiences

Examples

Unscarred by trauma
After years of hardship, he emerged surprisingly unscarred by his experiences.
The children seemed unscarred by the difficult events they had gone through.
She faced many challenges but remained emotionally unscarred.
