nondeviant

noun
conforming person
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈdiːviənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈdiːviənt / (BrE)
a person who behaves according to accepted rules, standards, or expected behavior

Examples

A nondeviant student
The study compared the behavior of nondeviants and those who broke the rules.
As a nondeviant, she always followed the school's code of conduct.
Researchers observed how nondeviant participants responded to peer pressure in group settings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conformist - a person who conforms to accepted behavior or established practices
  • antonym: deviant - a person who does not follow accepted rules or standards
