Hydnum

noun
genus of fungi (Hydnaceae)
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪd.nəm / (AmE), / ˈhɪd.nəm / (BrE)
a genus of fungi in the family Hydnaceae, known for their spiny or toothed fruiting bodies
