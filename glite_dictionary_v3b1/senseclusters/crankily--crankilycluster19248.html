crankily

adverb
in an irritable way
Pronunciation: / ˈkræŋkɪli / (AmE), / ˈkræŋkɪli / (BrE)
in a bad-tempered, irritable, or grouchy manner

Examples

Crankily complains
He crankily complained about the noise coming from the street.
After a long day, she responded crankily to her friend's questions.
The child crankily refused to eat his vegetables at dinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy and positive way
  • synonym: grouchily - in a grumpy or complaining manner
  • synonym: irritably - in an easily annoyed or angered way
