house guest

noun
guest staying in someone’s home
1.4
Pronunciation: / haʊs ɡɛst / (AmE), / haʊs ɡɛst / (BrE)
A person who is invited to stay and be entertained in someone’s home.

Examples

A house guest visiting
Please remember to treat every house guest with respect and kindness.
Their home was always open to a house guest in need.
We enjoyed having her as a house guest during the holidays.
