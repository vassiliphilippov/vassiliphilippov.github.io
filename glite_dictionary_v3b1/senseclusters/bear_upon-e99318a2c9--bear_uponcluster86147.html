bear upon

verb
to affect or relate to
5.3
Pronunciation: / bɛr əˈpɑn / (AmE), / beə əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to have an effect on or relate to something; to concern or influence

Examples

To bear upon a matter
His comments have little to bear upon the main issue we are discussing.
Recent discoveries may bear upon our understanding of the disease.
The lawyer argued that the evidence did not bear upon the case.

  • synonym: affect - to have an effect on something
