favus

noun
contagious fungal scalp infection
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪvəs / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvəs / (BrE)
A contagious fungal infection of the scalp, characterized by scaly lesions, occurring mainly in Africa and the Middle East.

Examples

Favus scalp infection
In some regions, favus remains a public health challenge among children.
The characteristic honeycomb-like crusts are a hallmark of advanced favus.
The doctor confirmed that the persistent scalp condition was favus, requiring antifungal treatment.
