protean

adjective
able to change forms easily; versatile
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊtiən, proʊˈtiːən / (AmE), / ˈprəʊtɪən, prəʊˈtiːən / (BrE)
Able to change quickly and easily into many different forms; versatile or variable.

Examples

A protean talent
His protean talents made him a valuable addition to any creative team.
She admired his protean ability to play very different characters on stage.
The protean nature of the virus makes it difficult to develop a lasting vaccine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: versatile - Able to adapt or be adapted to many different functions or activities
