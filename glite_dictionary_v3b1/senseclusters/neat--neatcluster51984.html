neat

adjective
excellent; very good (informal)
Pronunciation: / nit / (AmE), / niːt / (BrE)
(informal, mainly North American) Very good, excellent, or impressive.

Examples

Neat trick
He got a neat new bike for his birthday.
That was a neat trick you did with the cards!
Your design for the website looks really neat.
