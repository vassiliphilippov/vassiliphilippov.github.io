overcoat

noun
long heavy winter coat
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvɚˌkoʊt / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌkəʊt / (BrE)
A long, heavy coat worn over other clothes, typically for warmth in cold weather.

Examples

A wool overcoat
During winter, a thick overcoat is essential for staying warm.
He put on his overcoat before stepping outside into the cold.
She left her overcoat hanging by the door.
