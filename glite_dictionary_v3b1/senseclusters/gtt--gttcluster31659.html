gtt

noun
glucose tolerance test
8.4 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / d íi ti ti / (AmE), / d íiÀê tiÀê tiÀê / (BrE)
a medical test called a glucose tolerance test, in which blood sugar is measured after consuming a sugary drink to assess how the body processes glucose

Examples

GTT for diabetes
The doctor ordered a gtt to check for signs of diabetes.
After fasting overnight, she went to the clinic for her gtt appointment.
A gtt can help diagnose problems with how your body handles sugar.
