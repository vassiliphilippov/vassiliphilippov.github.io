flighting

noun
advertising in intervals
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a marketing strategy of scheduling advertising in intervals with periods of no activity in between

Examples

Flighting an ad campaign
The marketing team decided that flighting would be the most cost-effective approach for their new campaign.
By using flighting, the company ensured their ads appeared during the most important shopping seasons.
Many brands prefer flighting to avoid wasting their advertising budget during slow periods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pulsing - advertising that combines continuous and flighting strategies
