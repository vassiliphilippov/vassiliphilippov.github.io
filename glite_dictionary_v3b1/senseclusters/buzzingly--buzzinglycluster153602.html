buzzingly

adverb
with a buzzing sound
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌzɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbʌzɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that produces or resembles a buzzing sound

Examples

Bees fly buzzingly
The bees flew buzzingly from flower to flower in the summer garden.
The old neon sign hummed buzzingly above the quiet street at night.
She spoke buzzingly with excitement about her new project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noisily - in a loud or noisy manner
  • antonym: quietly - in a quiet or silent manner
