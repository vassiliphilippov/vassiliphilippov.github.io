Arcady

proper noun
ideal rural paradise
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrkədi / (AmE), / ˈɑːkədi / (BrE)
a poetic or literary name for an idealized, peaceful rural paradise, often used to evoke simplicity and harmony with nature

Examples

Dream of Arcady
The poet longed to escape the city and find peace in Arcady.
In her novel, the countryside is described as a modern Arcady untouched by time.
Artists of the Romantic era often painted scenes inspired by the idea of Arcady.

  • synonym: utopia - an imagined perfect society or place
