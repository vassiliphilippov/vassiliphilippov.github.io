methodically

adverb
in an organized, systematic way
6.9
Pronunciation: / məˈθɑːdɪkli / (AmE), / məˈθɒdɪkli / (BrE)
In a methodical manner; in an organized and systematic way.

Examples

To work methodically
He approached his studies methodically, following a strict timetable every day.
She packed her suitcase methodically, making sure nothing was forgotten.
The detective methodically examined each piece of evidence at the scene.
