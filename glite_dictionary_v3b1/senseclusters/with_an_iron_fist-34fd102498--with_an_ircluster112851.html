with an iron fist

adverb
with severe or strict control
Pronunciation: / wɪð ən ˈaɪrn fɪst / (AmE), / wɪð ən ˈaɪən fɪst / (BrE)
with very strict or oppressive control; in a harsh or authoritarian manner

Examples

With an iron fist rule
She managed the department with an iron fist, leaving little room for flexibility.
The dictator governed with an iron fist, silencing all opposition.
Under his leadership, the company was run with an iron fist and strict discipline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: with a heavy hand - in a strict or harsh manner
  • antonym: with a velvet glove - with a gentle or lenient manner
