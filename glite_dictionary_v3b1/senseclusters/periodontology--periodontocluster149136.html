periodontology

noun
study of gum health
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪriˌɑːdənˈtɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌpɪəriəʊdɒnˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of dentistry that studies and treats the gums and other tissues supporting the teeth

Examples

Periodontology gum treatment
After years of study, she became an expert in periodontology and helped many patients with gum disease.
Advances in periodontology have greatly improved treatments for supporting tissues of the teeth.
He attended a conference on periodontology to learn about the latest research in gum health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dentistry - the medical field concerned with the care of teeth and mouth
  • antonym: orthodontics - the branch of dentistry dealing with the correction of teeth alignment
