romancer

noun
writer of romances
7.2
Pronunciation: / roʊˈmænsər / (AmE), / rəʊˈmænsə / (BrE)
a person who writes romances or romantic fiction, especially stories involving love, adventure, or chivalry

Examples

A famous romancer

As a skilled romancer, she brought passion and adventure to every story she wrote.
Many aspiring writers dream of becoming a successful romancer in the world of fiction.
