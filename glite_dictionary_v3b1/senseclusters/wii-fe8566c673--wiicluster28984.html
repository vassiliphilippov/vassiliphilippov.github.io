Wii

noun
Nintendo game controller
Pronunciation: / wiː / (AmE), / wiː / (BrE)
the wireless handheld controller used to play games on the Nintendo Wii console, often called a Wiimote

Wii controller

Please hand me the Wii so I can select the next game.
After charging the Wii, we played tennis together in the living room.
