psylla

noun
small jumping plant louse insect
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlə / (AmE), / ˈsɪlə / (BrE)
A small, active insect resembling a cicada, with hind legs adapted for leaping, that feeds on plant juices; also known as a jumping plant louse.

Examples

A psylla insect
A heavy infestation of psylla left the leaves curled and discolored.
Orchardists keep an eye out for the psylla, which can damage fruit trees.
Pesticides are often required to control psylla populations in commercial crops.
