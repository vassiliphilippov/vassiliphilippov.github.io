depiction

noun
representation in picture or portrait
6.3 3.3
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈpɪk.ʃən/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈpɪk.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or result of showing or representing something in a picture, painting, drawing, or other visual form

Examples

The artist's depiction of the battle was both dramatic and detailed.
The museum featured a stunning depiction of the city skyline at sunset.
This novel offers a vivid depiction of life in nineteenth-century Paris.

The artist's depiction of the landscape captured every detail beautifully.
This book includes a famous depiction of the historical event.

Her notebook contained a depiction of the city skyline, outlined in pencil.
The artist made a depiction of the landscape with just a few carefully drawn lines.
