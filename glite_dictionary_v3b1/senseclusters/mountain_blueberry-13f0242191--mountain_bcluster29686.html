mountain blueberry

noun
a western U.S. blueberry shrub
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən ˈbluˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈbluːˌbɛri / (BrE)
an upright shrub of the western United States with solitary flowers and somewhat sour edible berries

Examples

Wild mountain blueberry
Hikers often pick mountain blueberry fruits during late summer in the Rockies.
The mountain blueberry thrives at higher elevations where the soil is acidic.
The leaves of the mountain blueberry are smaller than those of other blueberry species.
