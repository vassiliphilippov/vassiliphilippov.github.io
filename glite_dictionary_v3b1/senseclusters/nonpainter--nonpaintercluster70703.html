nonpainter

noun
person who does not paint
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈpeɪntər / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈpeɪntə / (BrE)
a person who does not paint, especially as an artist or hobbyist

Examples

A nonpainter
As a nonpainter, he found the art class challenging.
The workshop welcomed both experienced artists and complete nonpainters.
Even a nonpainter can appreciate the beauty of a well-crafted mural.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artist - a person who creates art, especially professionally
  • antonym: painter - a person who creates art by painting
