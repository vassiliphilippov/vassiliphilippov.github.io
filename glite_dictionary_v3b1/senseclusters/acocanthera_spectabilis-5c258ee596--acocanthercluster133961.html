Acocanthera spectabilis

noun
South African shrubby tree species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌækoʊˈkænθərə spɛkˈteɪbɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˌækəʊˈkænθərə spɛkˈteɪbɪlɪs / (BrE)
A medium-sized shrubby tree native to South Africa, with thick, leathery evergreen leaves, white or pink flowers, and typically two-seeded, purplish-black globe-shaped fruits.
