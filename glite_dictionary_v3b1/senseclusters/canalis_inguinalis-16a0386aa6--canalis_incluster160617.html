Examples

The canalis inguinalis passage
During fetal development, the testes descend through the canalis inguinalis into the scrotum.
In females, the round ligament passes through the canalis inguinalis to reach the labia majora.
The surgeon carefully examined the canalis inguinalis for signs of a hernia.
