parasitization

noun
exploitation for gain
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˌsaɪtəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpærəˌsaɪtɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of exploiting or taking advantage of someone or something for one's own benefit, often without giving anything in return

Examples

Parasitization of resources
The parasitization of public resources by private companies sparked outrage among citizens.
Activists spoke out against the parasitization of local charities by those seeking only publicity.
Some critics argue that the parasitization of creative work online is becoming more common.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abuse - the improper or harmful use of something or someone
  • synonym: exploitation - the act of using someone or something unfairly for personal gain
  • antonym: support - the act of helping or giving assistance
