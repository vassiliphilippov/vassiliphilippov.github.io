Panama hat

noun
a straw sun hat with a flat crown
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpænəˌmɑ hæt / (AmE), / ˈpænəmə hæt / (BrE)
a light, wide-brimmed hat made from plaited straw, traditionally handwoven in Ecuador, and typically worn in warm weather for sun protection

Examples

A Panama hat style
A genuine Panama hat can be quite expensive due to its craftsmanship.
He wore a Panama hat to shield himself from the tropical sun.
She bought a stylish Panama hat while visiting Ecuador.
