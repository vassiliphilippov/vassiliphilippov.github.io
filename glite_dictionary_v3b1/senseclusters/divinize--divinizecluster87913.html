divinize

verb
to make divine
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪvəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈdɪvɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to make someone or something divine, or treat as a god

Examples

The ceremony was meant to divinize the statue, making it worthy of worship.

Related Terms

  • antonym: profane - to treat something sacred with disrespect
  • synonym: sanctify - to make holy or sacred
