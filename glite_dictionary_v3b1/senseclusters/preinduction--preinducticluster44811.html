preinduction

adjective
before induction
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːɪnˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːɪnˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
occurring or used before an induction, especially referring to checks, procedures, or medications immediately prior to induction

Examples

Preinduction checklist
The nurse completed the preinduction checklist before the patient entered the operating room.
Doctors reviewed the preinduction medications to ensure the patient was ready for anesthesia.
A thorough preinduction assessment helps prevent complications during surgery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postinduction - occurring or used after an induction
  • synonym: preoperative - occurring or performed before a surgical operation
