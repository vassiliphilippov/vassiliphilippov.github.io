play a role

verb
to participate or influence
Pronunciation: / pleɪ ə ɹoʊl / (AmE), / pleɪ ə rəʊl / (BrE)
to participate in or have a significant influence on a particular situation or outcome

Examples

To play a role in decisions
Her experience will play a role in the committee's decision.
Teachers play a role in shaping the values of society.
We all play a role in protecting the environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contribute - to help to cause or bring about something
  • synonym: play a part - to be involved in or have an effect on a situation
