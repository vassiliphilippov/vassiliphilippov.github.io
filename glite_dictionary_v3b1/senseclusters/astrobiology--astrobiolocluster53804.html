astrobiology

noun
study of life in the universe
Pronunciation: / ˌæstroʊbaɪˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌastrəʊbʌɪˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of life in the universe, including the effects of outer space on living organisms and the search for extraterrestrial life

Examples

Astrobiology research
Advances in astrobiology could help us understand whether life exists elsewhere in the universe.
She decided to specialize in astrobiology after reading about the search for life on Mars.
The university launched a new course in astrobiology for students interested in space sciences.

  • synonym: exobiology - the study of life beyond Earth
