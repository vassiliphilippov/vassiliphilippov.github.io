CID

noun
customer ID code
Pronunciation: / si aɪ di / (AmE), / siː aɪ diː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for customer identification, used to uniquely identify a customer in a system or database

Examples

Bank CID number
Each customer receives a unique CID when they register for online banking.
Please provide your CID so we can access your account information quickly.
The system uses the CID to track all transactions made by a customer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ID - a code or number used to identify someone or something
  • synonym: account number - a unique number assigned to a customer account
