country-style

adjective
in the style of the countryside
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌntri staɪl / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri staɪl / (BrE)
characteristic of or typical of the countryside, especially in appearance, taste, or manner

Examples

Country-style chicken
She served a delicious country-style chicken stew at the party.
The restaurant is famous for its hearty country-style breakfasts.
They decorated their home with country-style furniture to create a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rustic - having a simple, rural, or country-like charm
  • antonym: urban - relating to a city or town, as opposed to the countryside
