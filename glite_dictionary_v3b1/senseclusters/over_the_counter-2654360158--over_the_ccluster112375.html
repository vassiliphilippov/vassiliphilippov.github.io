over the counter

adjective
available without a prescription
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvər ðə ˈkaʊntər / (AmE), / ˌəʊvə ðə ˈkaʊntə / (BrE)
(of medication) available for purchase without a doctor's prescription.

Examples

Over the counter medicine
Many allergy drugs are available over the counter in pharmacies.
Some people choose over the counter pain relief instead of visiting a doctor.
You can buy that medicine over the counter.
