roof mushroom

noun
mushroom with a roof-shaped cap
Pronunciation: / ruf ˈmʌʃˌrum / (AmE), / ruːf ˈmʌʃˌruːm / (BrE)
A type of mushroom with a cap shaped like a roof, belonging to the family Pluteaceae and often found early in the summer.

Examples

A roof mushroom cap
Early summer is the best time to spot a roof mushroom in damp woodland areas.
The roof mushroom is easily identified by its pointed cap that looks like a rooftop.
We found several roof mushrooms growing in the shade near the old barn.
