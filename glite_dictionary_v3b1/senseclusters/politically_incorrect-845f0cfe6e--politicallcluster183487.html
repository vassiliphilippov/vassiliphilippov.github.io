politically incorrect

adjective
offensive/discriminatory to certain groups
2.2 5.2
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈlɪ.tɪ.kəl.iː ɪn.kə.ˈrekt/ (AmE), /pə.ˈlɪ.tɪ.kəl.iː ɪn.kə.ˈrekt/ (BrE)
describing language, ideas, or behavior that may offend or exclude certain groups of people, especially those who have experienced discrimination or disadvantage

Examples

Politically incorrect language
Calling her by an outdated term was seen as highly politically incorrect.
He was criticized for making a politically incorrect remark during the meeting.
Some comedians deliberately use politically incorrect jokes to provoke their audience.
