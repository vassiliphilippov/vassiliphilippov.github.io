proportionality

noun
harmonious arrangement of parts
8.1 5.9
Pronunciation: /prə.prɔːr.ʃən.ˈæl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /prə.prɔːr.ʃɪn.ˈæl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /prə.pɔː.ʃən.ˈæl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /prə.pɔː.ʃɪn.ˈæl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
the quality of achieving a harmonious relationship among the parts of something, so that they fit together well as a whole

Examples

Good proportionality in design
Artists often strive for proportionality to create visually appealing compositions.
The proportionality of the columns and arches contributed to the elegance of the cathedral.
The architect admired the building's proportionality, noting how every part seemed to belong to the whole.
