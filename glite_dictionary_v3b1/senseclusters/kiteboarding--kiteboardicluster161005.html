kiteboarding

verb
to ride board with kite
4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkaɪt.bɔːr.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkaɪt.bɔː.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
to ride on a board over water while being pulled by a large controllable kite powered by the wind

Examples

To kiteboard on water
We spent the afternoon kiteboarding along the coast, enjoying the strong winds and waves.
She learned to kiteboard during her vacation in Brazil and now practices every summer.
Many people travel to this beach specifically for kiteboarding because of its ideal wind conditions.
