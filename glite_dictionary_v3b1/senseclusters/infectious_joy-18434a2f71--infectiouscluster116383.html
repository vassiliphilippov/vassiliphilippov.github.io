infectious joy

noun
joy that spreads to others
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɛkʃəs ˈdʒɔɪ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɛkʃəs ˈdʒɔɪ / (BrE)
A feeling of delight or happiness that is so strong it spreads to others and makes them feel happy too.

Examples

Infectious joy spreads
Her infectious joy lit up the entire room, making everyone smile despite the gloomy weather.
The children played together with such infectious joy that their parents couldn't help but join in.
There was an infectious joy in his voice that made even strangers feel welcome.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contagious laughter - laughter that easily spreads among people
  • antonym: misery - a feeling of great unhappiness
