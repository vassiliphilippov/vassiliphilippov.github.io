tipping point

noun
critical threshold for major change
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪpɪŋ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈtɪpɪŋ pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the critical moment or threshold at which a significant or irreversible change occurs

Examples

The tipping point moment
Her resignation was the tipping point that led to a series of policy changes at the company.
Public outrage hit a tipping point after the scandal became widely known.
We may soon reach a tipping point in the adoption of renewable energy technologies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: point of no return - the stage at which reversal is no longer possible
  • synonym: turning point - a time at which a decisive change in a situation occurs
