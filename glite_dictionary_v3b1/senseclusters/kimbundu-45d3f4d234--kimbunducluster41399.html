Kimbundu

proper noun
Angolan language or people
Pronunciation: / kɪmˈbʊndu / (AmE), / kɪmˈbʊndu / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken in Angola, or a member of the Kimbundu ethnic group

Examples

The Kimbundu language
She is studying Kimbundu as part of her research on African languages.
Many people in Luanda speak Kimbundu at home and in their communities.
The Kimbundu have a rich cultural heritage in central Angola.
