heller

noun
former Czech monetary subunit
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɛlə / (BrE)
a former monetary unit, equal to one hundredth of a koruna, used in the Czech Republic and Slovakia

Examples

A Czech heller coin
He collected old heller coins from the time before they were discontinued.
The price was so low that it was quoted in hellers rather than korunas.
When the Czech Republic switched to the euro, the heller ceased to be used as currency.
He still has a collection of old hellers from his trip to Slovakia.
Prices were sometimes listed in korunas and hellers in old shop receipts.
When the currency was reformed, the heller was gradually phased out.
