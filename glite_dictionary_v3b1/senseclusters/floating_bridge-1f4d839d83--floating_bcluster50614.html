floating bridge

noun
temporary bridge on floating pontoons
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊ.tɪŋ brɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈfləʊ.tɪŋ brɪdʒ / (BrE)
A temporary bridge supported by a series of pontoons or floating platforms placed on a body of water.

Examples

A temporary floating bridge
A temporary floating bridge was installed for pedestrians while the main bridge was being repaired.
During the flood, engineers quickly constructed a floating bridge to allow rescue vehicles to reach the isolated village.
The army crossed the river using a floating bridge assembled from pontoons.
