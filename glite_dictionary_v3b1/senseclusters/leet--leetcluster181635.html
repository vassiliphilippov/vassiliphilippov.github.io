leet

adjective
in leetspeak style
Pronunciation: / liːt / (AmE), / liːt / (BrE)
written or expressed in leetspeak, a style of writing that replaces letters with numbers or symbols

Examples

Leet message style
The hacker left a leet message on the website that only insiders could read.
Her username was written in a leet style, using numbers instead of letters.
Some online forums ban leet writing to keep messages easy to understand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leetspeak - an alternative alphabet using numbers and symbols to replace letters
