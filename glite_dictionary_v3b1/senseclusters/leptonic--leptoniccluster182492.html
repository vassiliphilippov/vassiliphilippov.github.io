leptonic

adjective
about leptons
Pronunciation: / lɛpˈtɑnɪk / (AmE), / lɛpˈtɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to leptons, which are a type of elementary particle in physics

Examples

Leptonic particle physics
The scientist explained the leptonic properties of neutrinos during the lecture.
Researchers observed a rare leptonic interaction in the particle accelerator experiment.
The paper discusses both hadronic and leptonic decay processes in detail.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hadronic - relating to hadrons, another type of subatomic particle
  • synonym: lepton - a type of elementary particle such as an electron or neutrino
