with a flourish

adverb
in a stylish or showy way
Pronunciation: / wɪð ə ˈflɝɪʃ / (AmE), / wɪð ə ˈflʌrɪʃ / (BrE)
in an extravagant or stylish manner, especially to attract attention or impress others

Examples

With a flourish of style
He opened the envelope with a flourish, eager to see what was inside.
She handed in her resignation letter with a flourish, making sure everyone was watching.
The magician revealed the final card with a flourish, astonishing the audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modestly - in a way that is not intended to attract notice or show off
  • synonym: ostentatiously - in a way that is showy or designed to attract notice
