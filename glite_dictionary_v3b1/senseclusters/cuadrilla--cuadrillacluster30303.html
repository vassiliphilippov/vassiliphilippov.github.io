cuadrilla

noun
bullfighting team
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈdriːjə / (AmE), / kwæˈdrɪljə / (BrE)
the team of assistants who support a matador during a bullfight, including banderilleros and picadors

Examples

A cuadrilla at a bullfight
The cuadrilla entered the arena before the matador to prepare for the bullfight.
Each member of the cuadrilla has a specific role in supporting the matador.
Without his skilled cuadrilla, the matador would face much greater danger in the ring.

Related Terms

  • antonym: matador - the main bullfighter who kills the bull
  • synonym: team - a group of people working together
