risk of metastasis

noun
probability of cancer spreading
Pronunciation: / rɪsk əv məˈtæstəsɪs / (AmE), / rɪsk əv mɛˈtastəsɪs / (BrE)
the likelihood or probability of cancer cells spreading from their original site to other parts of the body

Examples

The risk of metastasis increases
Genetic factors can influence the risk of metastasis in breast cancer cases.
Smoking increases the risk of metastasis in patients with certain types of cancer.
The treatment plan was designed to lower the risk of metastasis after surgery.
