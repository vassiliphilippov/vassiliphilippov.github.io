strangely

adverb
in an unusual or unexpected way
Pronunciation: / ˈstreɪndʒli / (AmE), / ˈstreɪndʒli / (BrE)
In a way that is unusual or unexpected.

Examples

To strangely behave
Strangely, the house was completely silent despite being full of people.
She felt strangely calm even though the situation was very tense.
The painting was strangely familiar to me, though I had never seen it before.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normally - in a usual or expected way
  • synonym: oddly - in an unusual or peculiar way
