love lotion

noun
romantic skin care product
Pronunciation: / lʌv ˈloʊʃən / (AmE), / lʌv ˈləʊʃən / (BrE)
A scented cream or oil marketed for enhancing romantic attraction or used for skin care with a romantic or sensual emphasis.

Examples

Love lotion scent
After a warm bath, she enjoyed applying love lotion for its soothing and romantic fragrance.
He surprised her with a bottle of love lotion that had a delicate rose scent.
The advertisement claimed that their love lotion would ignite passion and soften your skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: massage oil - oil used to facilitate massage and skin care
