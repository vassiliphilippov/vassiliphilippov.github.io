unburnable

noun
non-combustible waste
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɜrnəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɜːnəbəl / (BrE)
waste or items that cannot or should not be burned, often collected separately as non-combustible refuse
