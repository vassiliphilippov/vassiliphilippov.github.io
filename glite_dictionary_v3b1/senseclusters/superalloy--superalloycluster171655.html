superalloy

noun
high-performance metal
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌælɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌælɔɪ / (BrE)
a high-performance metal alloy engineered to maintain strength and stability under extreme temperatures and stress

Examples

A superalloy metal
The turbine blades in jet engines are often made from superalloy to withstand intense heat.
Researchers are developing a new superalloy for use in next-generation power plants.
Because of its durability, superalloy is essential in the aerospace industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alloy - a mixture of two or more metals
  • degree lower: steel - a strong, hard metal made from iron and carbon
