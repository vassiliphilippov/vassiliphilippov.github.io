transrational

noun
beyond rational thought
9.9
Pronunciation: / trænzˈræʃənəl / (AmE), / trænzˈræʃənəl / (BrE)
a concept, experience, or quality that goes beyond or cannot be fully explained by rational thought or logic

Examples

The transrational in art
Some artists believe that true creativity comes from the transrational rather than logical analysis.
Meditation can sometimes lead people to a transrational state of awareness.
The philosopher wrote about the importance of the transrational in understanding human experience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irrational - not logical or reasonable
  • synonym: mystical - relating to spiritual or mysterious experiences beyond ordinary understanding
