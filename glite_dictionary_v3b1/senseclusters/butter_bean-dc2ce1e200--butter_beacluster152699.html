butter bean

noun
a type of edible flat bean
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ɚ ˌbiːn /; / ˈbʌt̬ɚ ˌbin / (AmE), / ˈbʌt.ə ˌbiːn /; / ˈbʌtə ˌbiːn / (BrE)
a type of bean plant, especially grown in the southern United States, that produces small, flat, edible beans (also refers to the beans themselves, similar to lima beans)

Examples

Cooked butter beans
My grandmother cooked a pot of butter beans for Sunday dinner.
The recipe calls for one cup of fresh butter beans.
You can plant butter beans in late spring for a summer harvest.
He added chopped butter beans to the stew for extra flavor and texture.
My grandmother always served butter beans with cornbread during family dinners.
The canned butter beans were on sale at the grocery store this week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lima bean - a similar type of legume with flat, edible seeds
