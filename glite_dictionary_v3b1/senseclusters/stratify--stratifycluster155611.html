stratify

verb
form into social classes or levels
4.0 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈstræt.ə.faɪ/ /; / /ˈstræt.ɪ.faɪ/ (AmE), /ˈstræt.ɪ.faɪ/ /; / /ˈstræt.ə.faɪ/ (BrE)
to divide or organize people or groups into different social levels, classes, or castes

Examples
