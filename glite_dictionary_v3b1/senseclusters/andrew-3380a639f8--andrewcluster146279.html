In the New Testament, one of the twelve disciples of Jesus, the brother of Peter, and considered the patron saint of Scotland.

Examples

Andrew the disciple
Andrew is mentioned in the Gospel of John as one of the first disciples to follow Jesus.
Many churches in Scotland are named after Andrew, the country's patron saint.
On St Andrew's Day, people across Scotland celebrate their national heritage.
