algidity

noun
prostration with cold, clammy skin and low blood pressure
Pronunciation: / ælˈɡɪdəti / (AmE), / ælˈɡɪdəti / (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by cold, clammy skin and low blood pressure, often resulting in severe physical weakness or prostration.

Examples

Severe algidity symptoms
Doctors treated the algidity promptly to prevent further complications.
Symptoms such as algidity can indicate a dangerous drop in blood pressure.
The patient's sudden algidity was a sign of severe shock during the operation.
