nerve-racking

adjective
extremely stressful or worrying
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnɝːv ˌrækɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɜːv ˌrækɪŋ / (BrE)
causing a lot of stress or worry; extremely stressful or irritating to the nerves

Examples

A nerve-racking experience
Job interviews can be nerve-racking, especially if it's for your dream job.
The final minutes of the game were particularly nerve-racking for the fans.
Waiting for the test results was a nerve-racking experience for everyone involved.
