Synonyms

    a historical account is a written narrative that describes past events in detail, while history is the broader study and story of those events.
  • historical record
    3.3 2.06/m
    a historical record is a written document seen as reliable evidence of past events, while history is the wider account and interpretation.
  • chronicle
    5.8 6.30/m
    a chronicle is a factual, ordered account of events written over time, while history examines and explains their causes and meaning.
  • narrative

  • account
