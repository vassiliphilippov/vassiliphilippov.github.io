sword dancing

noun
traditional dance involving swords
3.7
Pronunciation: / sɔrd ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / sɔːd ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
A traditional dance, typically performed by men, involving the agile stepping over or brandishing of swords.

Examples

Traditional sword dancing
In Scotland, sword dancing is an important part of local heritage and traditional celebrations.
The art of sword dancing requires great skill, agility, and precise choreography.
The village festival featured a group of men performing sword dancing in colorful costumes.
