shnorr

verb
to ask for or cadge something persistently
Pronunciation: / ʃnɔr / (AmE), / ʃnɔː / (BrE)
to obtain or try to obtain something, such as money or goods, by persistently asking or wheedling; to cadge

Examples

To shnorr drinks
Every time we go out, Sam ends up shnorring drinks from strangers at the bar.
He always tries to shnorr a few dollars from his coworkers for lunch.
She shnorred her way into the event, even though she didn't have a ticket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mooch - to ask for or obtain something without paying or giving anything in return
