spice

noun
quality of being highly spiced or flavored
1.5 1.35/m
Pronunciation: / spaÉªs / (AmE), / spaÉªs / (BrE)
The quality of being seasoned with spices, resulting in a highly flavored or pungent taste.

Examples

Strong spice in food
Her cooking is known for its generous spice and bold flavors.
The sauce added an unexpected spice to the otherwise plain dish.
You can increase the spice of the curry by adding more chili flakes.

Synonyms

  • spiciness
    2.3 0.08/m
    spiciness refers to a strong, hot or pungent taste from spices, while spice means the general quality of being seasoned.
  • spicery
    9.9 0.01/m
    spicery is an older, rarer term for being flavored with spices, while spice is the common modern word used by speakers.
