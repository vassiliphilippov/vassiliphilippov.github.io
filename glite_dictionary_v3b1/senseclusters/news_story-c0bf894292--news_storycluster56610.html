news story

noun
article reporting news
Pronunciation: / nuːz ˈstɔːri / (AmE), / njuːz ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
a written or broadcast article that reports recent events or information as news

Examples

A news story report
She spent all night working on her next news story for the local paper.
Social media has changed the way a news story spreads around the world.
The front page featured a news story about the mayor's resignation.
