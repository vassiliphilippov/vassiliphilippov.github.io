unidiomatic

adjective
not typical usage
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌɪdiəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌɪdiəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
not natural or typical in the way native speakers use the language; not following the usual idioms or patterns
