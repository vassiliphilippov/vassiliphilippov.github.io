epizootic

adjective
animal epidemic; affecting many animals in a region
9.9 8.8
Pronunciation: /e.pɪ.zoʊ.ˈɑː.tɪk/ /; / /e.pɪ.zə.ˈwɑː.tɪk/ (AmE), /e.pɪ.zə.ˈɒ.tɪk/ /; / /e.pɪ.zə.ˈwɒ.tɪk/ (BrE)
(of animal diseases) occurring as an outbreak affecting many individuals of one species in a particular area; epidemic among animals.

Examples

Veterinarians worked tirelessly to control the epizootic disease spreading among wild deer.
