untell

verb
to retract a statement
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɛl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɛl / (BrE)
to try to retract or take back something that has already been said or revealed

Examples

To untell a story
After realizing his mistake, he wished he could untell what he had just said.
Sometimes you say things you want to untell the moment they leave your mouth.
She tried to untell the rumor, but it had already spread.

Related Terms

  • synonym: retract - to withdraw a statement or accusation
