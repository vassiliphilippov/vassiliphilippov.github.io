leatherette

noun
synthetic material resembling leather
6.9 4.2
Pronunciation: /le.ðə.ˈret/ (AmE), /le.ðə.ˈret/ (BrE)
a synthetic material made to look like leather but typically less expensive

Examples

A leatherette jacket
He wore a leatherette jacket that looked almost like genuine leather.
Many car interiors use leatherette instead of real leather for a more affordable option.
The new sofa was upholstered in leatherette to give it a stylish appearance.
