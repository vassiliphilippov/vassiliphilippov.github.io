unblur

verb
to make clear
5.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈblɜr / (AmE), / ʌnˈblɜː / (BrE)
to make an image or visual clear or sharp by removing blur.

Examples

To unblur a photo
The software can unblur old photographs to reveal hidden details.
After adjusting the settings, she managed to unblur the image on her computer screen.
He unblurred the video so the faces could be recognized more easily.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blur - to make something less clear or more difficult to see
  • synonym: sharpen - to make an image or picture clearer or more defined
