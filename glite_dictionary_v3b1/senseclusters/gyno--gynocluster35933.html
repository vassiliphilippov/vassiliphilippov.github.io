gyno

x
female-related prefix
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪnəʊ / (BrE)
a combining form used in scientific terms to refer to female or woman, especially in biology and medicine

Examples

Gyno prefix in biology
The term gynoecium refers to the female reproductive organs of a flower.
In medical contexts, gyno is often used as a prefix for words related to women.
The word gynophobia describes an irrational fear of women.

Related Terms

  • antonym: andro- - prefix meaning man or male, especially in scientific terms
  • synonym: gyneco- - prefix meaning woman or female, especially in medical terms
