philanthropist

noun
person who donates to charitable causes
6.0
Pronunciation: / fəˈlænθrəpɪst / (AmE), / fɪˈlanθrəpɪst / (BrE)
a person who seeks to promote the welfare of others, especially by donating money to good causes

Examples

A philanthropist donates money
As a philanthropist, she established several scholarships for underprivileged students.
Many people admire Bill Gates not just as a businessman, but as a leading philanthropist.
The well-known philanthropist donated millions to support medical research.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benefactor - a person who gives money or help to a person or cause
  • antonym: misanthrope - a person who dislikes or distrusts humanity
