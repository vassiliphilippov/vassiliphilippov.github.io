bound

adjective
secured with a cover (of books)
7.4
Pronunciation: / baʊnd / (AmE), / baʊnd / (BrE)
(of a book) secured within a protective cover, especially when specified by a material (e.g. leather-bound, cloth-bound).

Examples

Leather-bound book
All the reference materials were neatly bound and labeled for the library.
He loves collecting leather-bound books with ornate spines.
The old cloth-bound edition was passed down through generations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unbound - not secured within a cover (of books)
