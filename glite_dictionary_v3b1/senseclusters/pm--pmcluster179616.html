pm

adverb
between noon and midnight (time)
1.0 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / piÀê Àà…õm / (AmE), / piÀê Àà…õm / (BrE)
used to indicate the time between noon and midnight in the 12-hour clock system.

Examples

6 pm sharp
I usually have dinner around 7 pm every evening.
The meeting will start at 3 pm and end by 5 pm.
The store closes at 10 pm on weekdays.

Synonyms

  • PM
    1.0 0.00/m
    PM is the capitalized form often seen in schedules and formal writing, while pm is the common lowercase used in casual notes.
  • post meridiem
    5.3 0.01/m
    post meridiem is the full Latin phrase used mainly in formal time notation, while pm is the short, common clock mark.
