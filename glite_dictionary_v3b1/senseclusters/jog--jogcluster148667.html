jog

noun
a slight push or shake
Pronunciation: / dʒɑɡ / (AmE), / dʒɒɡ / (BrE)
a slight push or shake, often to get someone's attention or to remind them of something

Examples

A gentle jog
A sudden jog in the crowd made me drop my phone.
He gave his friend a gentle jog to wake him up.
She needed a quick jog of her memory to recall his name.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nudge - a gentle push, especially with the elbow
