klezmer

adjective
Jewish folk music style
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈklɛzmər / (AmE), / ˈklɛzmə / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of klezmer music, a traditional Jewish musical style from Eastern Europe

Examples

Klezmer melodies
The lively klezmer rhythms filled the hall with energy and excitement.
She wore a traditional dress to the klezmer concert last night.
Many people danced to the joyful sounds of the klezmer band at the festival.
