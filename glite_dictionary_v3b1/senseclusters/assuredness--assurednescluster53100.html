assuredness

noun
confidence and composure under stress
6.1 5.0
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈʃʊrd.nəs/ (AmE), /ə.ˈʃʊəd.nəs/ (BrE)
confidence and composure, especially in difficult situations; the state of being sure of oneself or calm under pressure.

Examples

With assuredness and calm
He faced the crisis with remarkable assuredness and clarity.
Her assuredness during the interview impressed the hiring committee.
Public speaking requires a certain level of assuredness that comes from experience.
