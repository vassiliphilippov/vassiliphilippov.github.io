service bureau

noun
company offering specialized services for other businesses
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːvɪs ˌbjʊroʊ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs ˌbjʊərəʊ / (BrE)
a company that provides specialized services or facilities to other businesses for a fee, often to achieve economies of scale

Examples

A service bureau company
Many small companies rely on a service bureau to handle their payroll processing efficiently.
The law firm outsourced its printing needs to a specialized service bureau.
Using a service bureau allows businesses to access high-end equipment without investing in their own.
