ladyhood

noun
state of being a lady
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdiˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈleɪdihʊd / (BrE)
the state or quality of being a lady, especially in terms of maturity or social status

Examples

Many traditions mark the passage from girlhood to ladyhood in different cultures.
