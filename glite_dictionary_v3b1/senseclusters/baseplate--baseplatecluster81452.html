baseplate

noun
supporting metal plate
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsˌpleɪt / (BrE)
a flat, usually rigid plate at the bottom of a structure, machine, or device that provides support or stability

Examples

Metal baseplate
The circuit board is screwed onto the baseplate for stability and heat dissipation.
Engineers designed a thicker baseplate to improve the device's cooling performance.
Before assembling the machine, make sure the baseplate is clean and free of debris.
The metal baseplate keeps the entire machine stable.
Before assembling the model, attach the wheels to the baseplate for support.
The sculpture was mounted on a heavy baseplate to prevent it from tipping over.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chassis - the frame or base structure of a device or machine
  • synonym: foundation - the lowest load-bearing part of a building
  • synonym: mounting plate - a plate used to attach or support components
  • synonym: platform - a flat surface that supports something
