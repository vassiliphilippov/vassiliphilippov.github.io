despotic

adjective
tyrannical or oppressive; like a despot
8.4 6.7
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈspɑː.tɪk/ /; / /de.ˈspɑː.tɪk/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈspɒ.tɪk/ /; / /də.ˈspɒ.tɪk/ /; / /de.ˈspɒ.tɪk/ (BrE)
