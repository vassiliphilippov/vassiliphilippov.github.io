don't

noun
prohibited action
4.6
Pronunciation: / doʊnt / (AmE), / dəʊnt / (BrE)
a rule or piece of advice about what should not be done in a particular situation

Examples

A big don't
One big don't at the interview is arriving late or appearing unprepared.
The teacher listed several don'ts for the science lab to keep everyone safe.
When traveling abroad, it's important to learn the local don'ts to avoid offending anyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: do - an action or behavior that is recommended
  • synonym: taboo - something that is forbidden or not allowed
