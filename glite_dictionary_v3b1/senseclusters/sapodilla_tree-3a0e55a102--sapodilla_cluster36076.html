sapodilla tree

noun
tropical tree producing chicle and fruit
Pronunciation: / ˌsæpəˈdɪl.ə tri / (AmE), / ˌsæpəˈdɪl.ə triː / (BrE)
a large tropical American evergreen tree that produces chicle gum and edible brown fruit with sweet flesh

Examples

The sapodilla tree fruit
Chicle, once used in chewing gum, is harvested from the sapodilla tree.
Her backyard boasts a flourishing sapodilla tree laden with sweet, brown fruit.
The sapodilla tree thrives in warm, tropical climates with well-drained soil.
