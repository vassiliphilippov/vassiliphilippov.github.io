flooding

noun
water covering land
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflʌdɪŋ / (BrE)
a situation in which water covers land that is usually dry, often due to heavy rain or overflowing rivers

Examples

Severe flooding in streets
The heavy rain caused severe flooding in the city.
After days of storms, flooding forced many families to leave their homes.
The government issued warnings about possible flooding along the riverbanks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drought - a long period with little or no rain
  • synonym: inundation - an overwhelming flow of water over land
