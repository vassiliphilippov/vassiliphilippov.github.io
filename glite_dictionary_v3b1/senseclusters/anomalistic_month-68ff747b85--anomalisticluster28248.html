anomalistic month

noun
lunar period from perigee to perigee
9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈnɑː.məl.ɪs.tɪk mʌnθ/ /; / /ə.nə.ˈmæl.ɪs.tɪk mʌnθ/ (AmE), /ə.ˈnɒ.məl.ɪs.tɪk mʌnθ/ /; / /ə.nə.ˈmæl.ɪs.tɪk mʌnθ/ (BrE)
the period taken by the Moon to return to the same point of perigee in its orbit, about 27.5546 days

Examples
