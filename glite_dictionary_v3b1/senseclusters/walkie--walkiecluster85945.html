walkie

noun
handheld radio
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔki / (AmE), / ˈwɔːki / (BrE)
an informal term for a handheld two-way radio used for communication over short distances

Examples

A walkie talkie
We brought our walkies on the camping trip to stay in touch.
Each security guard carries a walkie to communicate with the team.
The children used a walkie to talk to each other from different rooms.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: radio - a device for receiving or transmitting sound wirelessly
  • synonym: walkie-talkie - a portable two-way radio
