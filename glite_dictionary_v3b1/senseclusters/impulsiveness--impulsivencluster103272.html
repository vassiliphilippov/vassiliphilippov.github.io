Synonyms

  • impetuosity
    8.9 0.10/m
    impetuosity is sudden energetic action driven by a strong urge, while impulsiveness is a general tendency to act without careful thought.
  • rashness
    5.6 0.18/m
    rashness is acting or speaking without thought in a quick, careless way, while impulsiveness is a broader tendency to act without careful consideration.
  • recklessness
    5.2 1.27/m
    recklessness ignores danger and shows no regard for outcomes, while impulsiveness emphasizes acting quickly without thinking through consequences.
  • hastiness
    5.8 0.04/m
    hastiness means deciding or doing things too quickly, often from rush or lack of care, while impulsiveness is driven by sudden urges.
  • flightiness
    8.1 0.02/m
    flightiness is being changeable and led by whims, while impulsiveness is acting quickly without careful thought about consequences.
