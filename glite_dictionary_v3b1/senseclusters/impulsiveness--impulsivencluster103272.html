impulsiveness

noun
tendency to act without thinking
1.0 4.8
Pronunciation: /ɪm.ˈpʌl.sɪv.nəs/ /; / /əm.ˈpʌl.sɪv.nəs/ (AmE), /ɪm.ˈpʌl.sɪv.nəs/ /; / /əm.ˈpʌl.sɪv.nəs/ (BrE)
the tendency to act quickly without careful thought or consideration of the consequences

Examples

His impulsiveness showed
Despite his impulsiveness, he was admired for his enthusiasm and spontaneity.
He regretted his impulsiveness after making an expensive purchase on a whim.
Her impulsiveness often got her into trouble when she acted without thinking.

  • degree higher: rashness - the quality of acting hastily or without due consideration; higher degree
  • antonym: thoughtfulness - the quality of thinking carefully before acting
