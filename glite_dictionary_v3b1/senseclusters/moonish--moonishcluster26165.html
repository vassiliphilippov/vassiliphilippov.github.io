moonish

adjective
like the moon
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈmuːnɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling the moon in appearance, especially in being pale, round, or softly luminous.

Examples

Moonish light
The garden was bathed in a moonish light that made everything look magical.
Her dress had a moonish sheen, soft and pale under the evening lamps.
He admired the moonish glow of the pearl in the jewelry store window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lunar - relating to or resembling the moon
  • antonym: sunny - bright with sunlight
