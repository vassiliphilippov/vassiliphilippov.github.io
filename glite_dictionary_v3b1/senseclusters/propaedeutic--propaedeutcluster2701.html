propaedeutic

adjective
preparatory or introductory instruction
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊpɪˈdjutɪk / (AmE), / ˌprəʊpɪˈdjuːtɪk / (BrE)
preparatory or introductory, serving as a preliminary instruction before further study or practice

Examples

Propaedeutic training

The course is propaedeutic for students beginning their studies in philosophy.
The text serves as a propaedeutic introduction to more advanced mathematical concepts.
