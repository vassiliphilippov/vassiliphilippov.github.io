lungworm

noun
parasitic lung worm
8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈlʌŋ.wɜːrm/ (AmE), /ˈlʌŋ.wɜːm/ (BrE)
a parasitic worm that lives in the lungs of animals or humans and can cause respiratory disease
