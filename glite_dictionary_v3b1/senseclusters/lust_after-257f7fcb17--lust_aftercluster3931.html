lust after

verb
intensely desire (non-sexual)
Pronunciation: / lʌst ˈæftə / (AmE), / lʌst ˈɑːftə / (BrE)
to feel a very strong desire or craving for something (often abstract or material), used figuratively and not limited to sexual desire.

Examples

To lust after power
The ambitious leader seemed to lust after power and prestige more than helping others.
She lusted after fame for years, accepting roles she didn't enjoy to stay in the public eye.
Many collectors are lusting after rare first editions that have skyrocketed in value.

Related Terms

  • synonym: covet - to strongly desire something belonging to someone else
  • synonym: crave - to have an intense desire for something
  • synonym: desire - to strongly wish for or want something
