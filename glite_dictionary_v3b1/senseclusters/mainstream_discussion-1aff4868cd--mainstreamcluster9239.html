mainstream discussion

noun
widely accepted conversation
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪnˌstriːm dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / ˈmeɪnstriːm dɪˈskʌʃən / (BrE)
a conversation or discourse that is common, widely accepted, or part of popular opinion and thought

Examples

Mainstream discussion topic
Climate change is a frequent topic in mainstream discussion today.
Mental health issues are finally receiving attention in mainstream discussions.
Some beliefs remain controversial and are rarely mentioned in mainstream discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mainstream - the ideas, attitudes, or activities that are regarded as normal or conventional; the dominant trend
  • synonym: public discourse - discussion or debate that takes place in public forums or mainstream media
