submarine

noun
large sandwich on a split long roll
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌb.məˌrin / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.mə.riːn / (BrE)
A sandwich consisting of a long, split roll filled with meats, cheese, and salad vegetables, also known as a sub, hoagie, or hero in different regions.

Examples

A submarine sandwich
I ordered a turkey submarine with extra cheese for lunch.
She brought a homemade submarine to the picnic, stuffed with meats and fresh vegetables.
The deli is famous for its giant Italian submarine sandwiches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hero - US regional term for a large sandwich on Italian bread
  • synonym: hoagie - another US regional term for a large sandwich on a long roll
