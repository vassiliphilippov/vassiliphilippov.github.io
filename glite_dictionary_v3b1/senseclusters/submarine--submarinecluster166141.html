submarine

noun
large sandwich on a split long roll

Pronunciation: /ˈsʌb.mə.riːn/ /; / /sʌb.mə.ˈriːn/ (AmE), /ˈsʌb.mə.riːn/ /; / /sʌb.mə.ˈriːn/ (BrE)
