kniphofia

noun
a flowering plant with tall spikes of bright flowers
Pronunciation: / nɪˈfoʊfiə / (AmE), / nɪˈfəʊfiə / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Kniphofia, known for its long, grasslike leaves and tall spikes of red or yellow drooping flowers.
