pororoca

noun
Amazon tidal wave
Pronunciation: / ˌpɔːrəˈroʊkə / (AmE), / ˌpɒrəˈrɒkə / (BrE)
a powerful tidal wave that travels up a river from the ocean, especially on the Amazon River, moving against the river's current

Examples
