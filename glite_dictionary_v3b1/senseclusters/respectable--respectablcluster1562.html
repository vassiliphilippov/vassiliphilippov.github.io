respectable

adjective
having acceptable morals or behaviour
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɛktəbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈspɛktəbəl /; / rɪˈspɛktəbl̩ / (BrE)
regarded by society as good, proper, or having acceptable moral standards; deserving of respect

Examples

A respectable person
Despite her modest background, she became a respectable member of the community.
His behavior at the event was considered perfectly respectable by everyone present.
They want their children to marry someone respectable and hardworking.
He has always tried to lead a respectable life despite his difficult circumstances.
Only respectable businesses are allowed to operate in that district.
She comes from a respectable family known for their charity work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disreputable - not considered to be respectable; having a bad reputation
  • synonym: honorable - deserving of honor and respect
  • synonym: honourable - deserving of honor or respect; having high moral standards
  • synonym: reputable - having a good reputation
