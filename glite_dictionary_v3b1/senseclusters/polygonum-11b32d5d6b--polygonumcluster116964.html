Polygonum

proper noun
plant genus including knotweeds
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɑːɡənəm / (AmE), / pəˈlɒɡənəm / (BrE)
A genus of herbs or woody subshrubs found chiefly in north temperate regions, including knotweeds and related plants.
