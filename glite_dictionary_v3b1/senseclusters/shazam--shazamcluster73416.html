shazam

interjection
surprise exclamation
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈzæm / (AmE), / ʃəˈzæm / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express surprise or to accompany a sudden or magical action

Examples

Shazam, it appears
He waved his hand and, shazam, the lights turned on.
She opened the box and, shazam, a rabbit jumped out.
Just press this button and, shazam, your photo appears on the screen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abracadabra - an exclamation used when performing a magic trick
  • synonym: wow - an exclamation expressing surprise or admiration
