Lemuridae

noun
family of typical lemurs
Pronunciation: / lɪˈmjʊrɪˌdi / (AmE), / lɛˈmjʊərɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of primates comprising the typical lemurs, native to Madagascar.

Examples

The Lemuridae family
Modern members of the Lemuridae family exhibit a range of social behaviors.
Scientists have discovered several new species within the Lemuridae during recent expeditions.
The Lemuridae are unique to the island of Madagascar, where they play crucial ecological roles.
