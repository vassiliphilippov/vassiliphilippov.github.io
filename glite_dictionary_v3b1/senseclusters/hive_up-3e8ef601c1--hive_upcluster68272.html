hive up

verb
store up for later use
9.7
Pronunciation: / haɪv ʌp / (AmE), / haɪv ʌp / (BrE)
to store up or accumulate something for future use, especially in preparation for times when it may be needed

Examples

To hive up supplies
Farmers often hive up supplies before the harsh winter begins.
Like squirrels with nuts, he likes to hive up food just in case of an emergency.
She decided to hive up her savings instead of spending them on unnecessary things.

Related Terms
