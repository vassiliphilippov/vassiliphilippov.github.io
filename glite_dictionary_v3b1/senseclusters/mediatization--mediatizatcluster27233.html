mediatization

noun
media influence process
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmiːdiətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmiːdiətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which media shapes, influences, or transforms society, institutions, or practices

Examples

Mediatization of politics
The mediatization of politics has changed how leaders communicate with the public.
Social networks have accelerated the mediatization of everyday life.
Many scholars study the mediatization of culture in the digital age.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digitalization - the process of converting information into digital form
  • synonym: mediation - the act of intervening or acting as a medium
