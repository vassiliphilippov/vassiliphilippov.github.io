thump

verb
beat rhythmically (heart, pulse)
5.3 2.9
Pronunciation: /θʌmp/ (AmE), /θʌmp/ (BrE)
(of a heart or pulse) to beat or move with a steady, heavy, rhythmic sound.

Examples

Heart thumps fast
Her heart thumped loudly in her chest as she waited for the results.
His heart thumps faster whenever he gets nervous.
I could feel my pulse thumping through my veins after the intense workout.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beat - to strike repeatedly; (of a heart) to pulsate
