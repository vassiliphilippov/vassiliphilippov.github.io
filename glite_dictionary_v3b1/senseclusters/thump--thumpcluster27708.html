thump

verb

(of a heart or pulse) to beat or move with a steady, heavy, rhythmic sound.

Examples

Heart thumps fast

I could feel my pulse thumping through my veins after the intense workout.

Synonyms

  • throb
    3.1 0.80/m
    throb beats strongly and often feels or vibrates with pain or emotion, while thump is mainly a heavy steady heartbeat sound.
  • pulse
    4.2 2.35/m
    pulse moves or expands in a regular rhythm like a heartbeat, while thump suggests a louder, heavier beat.
  • pulsate
    4.1 1.14/m
    pulsate means to move or flow with a steady rhythm in a soft way, while thump is loud and heavy.
  • beat
