alleluia

noun
praise chant in worship
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌæləˈluːjə / (AmE), / ˌælɪˈluːjə / (BrE)
a chant or exclamation of praise, especially used in Christian worship services or songs

Examples

A joyful alleluia
The choir sang a joyful alleluia at the end of the Easter service.
During the ceremony, everyone joined in the alleluia with enthusiasm.
The organist played a beautiful alleluia as the congregation entered the church.
The congregation responded with a heartfelt alleluia after the prayer.
During the festival, the choir sang several beautiful alleluias.
He shouted alleluia when he heard the good news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amen - a word used to express agreement or affirmation in worship
  • synonym: hallelujah - an exclamation or song of praise to God
