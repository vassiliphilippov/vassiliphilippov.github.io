condottiere

noun
opportunistic leader
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːndəˈtjɛreɪ / (AmE), / ˌkɒndɒtˈtjɛəreɪ / (BrE)
a leader who pursues power or action mainly for personal gain or profit, often acting opportunistically

Examples

Opportunistic condottiere
The ambitious condottiere quickly changed sides when a better offer was made.
Some critics accused the politician of being a modern condottiere who cared only for personal advancement.
In history, a condottiere often led armies for whoever paid the most.
