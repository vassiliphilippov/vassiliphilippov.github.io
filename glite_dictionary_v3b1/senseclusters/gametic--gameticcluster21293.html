gametic

adjective
of reproductive cells
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈmiːtɪk / (AmE), / ɡəˈmiːtɪk / (BrE)
relating to gametes, the reproductive cells such as sperm and eggs in animals and plants

Examples

Gametic cells
The researcher focused on gametic differences between male and female plants.
Certain diseases can be passed on through gametic mutations.
The process of fertilization involves the fusion of two gametic cells.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reproductive - relating to reproduction or the process of producing offspring
  • antonym: somatic - relating to the body cells other than reproductive cells
