dignified

adjective
having or showing dignity and self-respect
1.3 4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪɡnəˌfaɪd /; / ˈdɪɡnɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɪɡnɪfaɪd / (BrE)
having a calm, serious, or formal manner that shows self-respect and earns respect from others

Examples

His dignified response to the criticism won him the respect of his colleagues.

The ambassador gave a dignified speech that impressed everyone present.
Even in defeat, he remained calm and dignified.
She walked into the room with a dignified air that demanded attention.
The ambassador maintained a dignified silence throughout the heated debate.
