Caucasoid

noun
outdated racial category
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔkəˌsɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːkəsɔɪd / (BrE)
a term formerly used in anthropology to refer to people of European or related descent, now considered outdated and inappropriate

Examples

A Caucasoid person
The classification of people as Caucasoid is no longer accepted in modern anthropology.
Many textbooks have removed references to Caucasoid due to its outdated and problematic nature.
The term Caucasoid was once used in scientific literature but is now considered inappropriate.
