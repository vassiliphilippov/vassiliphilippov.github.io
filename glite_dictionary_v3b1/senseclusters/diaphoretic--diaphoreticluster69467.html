diaphoretic

adjective
promoting sweating (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪ.ə.fəˈrɛt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʌɪ.ə.fəˈrɛt.ɪk / (BrE)
(of a drug or substance) causing or promoting perspiration.

Examples

Diaphoretic medicine
After taking the diaphoretic tea, she began to sweat profusely.
Ginger is often used in herbal medicine as a natural diaphoretic.
The doctor prescribed a diaphoretic to help manage the patient's fever.
