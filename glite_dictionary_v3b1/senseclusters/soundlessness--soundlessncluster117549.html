soundlessness

noun
the state of making no sound
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊnd.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsaʊn(d)ləsnɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being without sound; complete silence

Examples

Complete soundlessness
He was struck by the soundlessness that followed the sudden power outage.
In the early morning, the soundlessness of the city streets is remarkable.
The soundlessness of the snow-covered forest was both calming and eerie.

Related Terms

  • antonym: noise - a sound, especially one that is loud or unpleasant
  • synonym: noiselessness - the quality of making no noise
  • synonym: silence - the absence of sound
