Rollerblade

noun
type of in-line skate
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlɚˌbleɪd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊləbleɪd / (BrE)
A type of in-line skate with wheels arranged in a single line, originally a trademark but commonly used for any such skate.

Examples

A pair of Rollerblades
He put on his Rollerblades and glided down the sidewalk.
Her parents bought her a new pair of Rollerblades for her birthday.
People often use Rollerblades for exercise in the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: in-line skate - a skate with wheels set in a single line
  • degree lower: roller skate - a shoe with four wheels for skating
