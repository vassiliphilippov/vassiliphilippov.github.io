depone

verb
give evidence under oath
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpoʊn / (AmE), / dɪˈpəʊn / (BrE)
to give evidence or make a formal statement under oath, especially in a written deposition

Examples

To depone in court
Several experts will depone to the authenticity of the document during the trial.
The witness was called to depone before the court regarding the events of that night.
Under Scottish law, a person may depone a written statement as evidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swear - make a solemn statement or promise undertaking to tell the truth
  • synonym: testify - give evidence as a witness in a law court
