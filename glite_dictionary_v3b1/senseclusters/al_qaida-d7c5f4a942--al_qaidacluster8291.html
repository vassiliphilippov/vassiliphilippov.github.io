al Qaida

proper noun
militant Islamic terrorist organization
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɑl kaɪˈiːdə / (AmE), / æl kaɪˈiːdə / (BrE)
A militant Islamic terrorist organization, founded by Osama bin Laden, known for orchestrating global attacks and opposing the United States and its allies.
