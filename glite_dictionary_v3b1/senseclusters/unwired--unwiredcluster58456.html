unwired

adjective
without electrical wiring
6.8
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈwaɪrd/ /; / /ʌn.ˈwaɪ.ərd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈwaɪəd/ /; / /ʌn.ˈwaɪd/ (BrE)
not equipped with electrical wiring or lacking internal wiring for power in a building or structure

Examples

Unwired building
The old barn was completely unwired, making it difficult to use any modern equipment.
They enjoyed a weekend in an unwired cabin, relying on candles and a wood stove for light and heat.
Because the shed was unwired, they had to run an extension cord from the house for power tools.
