linn

noun
waterfall or deep pool
Pronunciation: / lɪn / (AmE), / lɪn / (BrE)
a waterfall or a deep pool in a river, especially in Scottish or northern English dialect

Examples

A linn waterfall
The hikers stopped to rest beside the linn hidden in the forest.
A narrow path led down to a beautiful linn at the base of the hill.
Local legends say the linn is home to mysterious creatures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pool - a small area of still water in a river or stream
  • synonym: waterfall - a place where water flows over a vertical drop in a river
