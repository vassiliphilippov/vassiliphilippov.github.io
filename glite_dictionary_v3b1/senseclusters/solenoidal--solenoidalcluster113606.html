solenoidal

adjective
like a solenoid
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊləˈnɔɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌsəʊlɪˈnɔɪdəl / (BrE)
relating to or having the properties of a solenoid, especially in producing a uniform magnetic field

Examples

Solenoidal coil
The engineer designed a solenoidal coil to create a strong, uniform magnetic field for the experiment.
A solenoidal field is often used in physics to describe certain types of magnetic configurations.
The device relies on a solenoidal structure to function efficiently in the laboratory.
