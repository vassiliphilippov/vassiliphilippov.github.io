flare

noun
short fly ball in baseball
Pronunciation: / flɛr / (AmE), / fleə / (BrE)
(baseball) A fly ball that is hit a short distance into the outfield, typically landing between the infielders and outfielders.

Examples

A flare single
He managed to loop a flare into shallow left for a single.
The batter hit a flare that dropped just in front of the center fielder.
The pitcher was frustrated when the hitter got a hit on a lucky flare over second base.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blooper - (baseball) a softly hit fly ball that barely goes over the infield
