incomplete

adjective
not finished or lacking parts
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkəmˈpliːt /; / ˌɪn.kəmˈplit / (AmE), / ˌɪnkəmˈpliːt /; / ˌɪn.kəmˈpliːt / (BrE)
not finished or missing some necessary parts or steps

Examples

An incomplete puzzle

The project remains incomplete due to a lack of funds.
Because the puzzle was incomplete, we couldn't finish it.
The medical form was returned because it was incomplete.
