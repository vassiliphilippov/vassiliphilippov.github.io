zen

noun
state of calm
Pronunciation: / zɛn / (AmE), / zɛn / (BrE)
a state of calm attentiveness and peacefulness, often achieved through meditation or mindfulness

Examples

A feeling of zen
After a long walk in the forest, he felt a deep sense of zen.
She tries to maintain her zen even during stressful meetings at work.
Listening to gentle music helps me find my zen before bedtime.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry or unease
  • synonym: tranquility - a state of peace and calm
