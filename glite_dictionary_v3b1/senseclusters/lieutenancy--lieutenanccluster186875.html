lieutenancy

noun
the position of a lieutenant
Pronunciation: / luːˈtɛnənsi / (AmE), / lɛfˈtɛnənsi / (BrE)
the office or position of being a lieutenant, especially in the military or police

Examples

His lieutenancy in army
After his lieutenancy, he decided to pursue a career in civilian life.
He held the lieutenancy for three years before being promoted to captain.
The lieutenancy carries significant responsibilities within the regiment.
