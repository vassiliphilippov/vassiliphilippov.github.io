stegosaur

noun
herbivorous dinosaur with bony back plates
9.9 7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsteɡ.ə.sɔːr/ (AmE), /ˈsteɡ.ə.sɔː/ (BrE)
a large plant-eating dinosaur, characterized by upright bony plates along its back and a spiked tail, that lived during the Jurassic period
