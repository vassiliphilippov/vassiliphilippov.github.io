stegosaur

noun
herbivorous dinosaur with bony back plates
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛɡəˌsɔr / (AmE), / ˈstɛɡəsɔː / (BrE)
a large plant-eating dinosaur, characterized by upright bony plates along its back and a spiked tail, that lived during the Jurassic period

Examples

A stegosaur dinosaur
Children are often fascinated by the distinctive plates found on the back of a stegosaur.
Scientists believe the tail spikes of the stegosaur were used for self-defense against predators.
The fossilized skeleton of a stegosaur was discovered in Colorado last year.
