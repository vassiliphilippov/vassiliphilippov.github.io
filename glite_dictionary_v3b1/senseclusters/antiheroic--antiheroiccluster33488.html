antiheroic

adjective
opposing heroism
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌhɪˈroʊɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌhɪəˈrəʊɪk / (BrE)
deliberately lacking or rejecting the qualities of a traditional hero, especially in literature or film

Examples

Antiheroic character
The film's antiheroic protagonist refuses to act bravely or selflessly in dangerous situations.
Her antiheroic attitude made her question the value of traditional heroes in literature.
The novel offers an antiheroic perspective, focusing on flawed and ordinary characters instead of champions.
The film's antiheroic protagonist made choices that were morally ambiguous.
Her antiheroic behavior set her apart from the other characters in the novel.
Critics praised the antiheroic qualities of the main character for making the story more realistic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antihero - a central character who lacks traditional heroic qualities
  • antonym: heroic - having the qualities of a hero
  • synonym: unheroic - not displaying qualities of a hero
