analphabetic

adjective
not alphabetic or alphabetical
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.æl.fəˈbɛt.ɪk /; / ˌænˌæl.fəˈbɛ.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌan.alfəˈbɛt.ɪk /; / ˌanˌal.fəˈbɛ.tɪk / (BrE)
not using or based on an alphabet; relating to writing systems or arrangements that do not use an alphabet

Examples

Analphabetic script
Some phonetic scripts are analphabetic, as they do not correspond to any alphabet.

Some early scripts were analphabetic, preceding the invention of alphabets.
