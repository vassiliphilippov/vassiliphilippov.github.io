martin

noun
type of swallow (bird)
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑɹtɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtɪn / (BrE)
a type of swallow with a squarish or slightly forked tail and long pointed wings, found in many parts of the world

Examples

A martin bird
A flock of martins darted over the lake at sunset, catching insects in midair.
Each spring, martins return to nest in the boxes hung by local birdwatchers.
We watched a purple martin swoop gracefully above the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swallow - a small migratory bird with a slender body and long pointed wings
