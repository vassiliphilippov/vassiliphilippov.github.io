misdistribution

noun
incorrect allocation
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən / (BrE)
the incorrect or unlawful distribution of property, money, or assets, especially by not following a will, law, or agreement

Examples

Misdistribution of funds

He was accused of misdistribution after failing to divide the assets according to the agreement.
Proper procedures must be followed to avoid misdistribution when handling an estate.
