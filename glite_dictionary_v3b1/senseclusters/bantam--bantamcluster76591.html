bantam

noun
small breed of domestic fowl
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbæn.təm / (AmE), / ˈbæn.təm / (BrE)
any of several small breeds of domestic fowl, especially chickens, noted for their diminutive size and lively disposition

Examples

A bantam chicken
Many people choose a bantam breed because of their small size and vibrant personalities.
The little bantam strutted confidently among the larger chickens.
They keep a few bantams in their backyard for eggs and companionship.
