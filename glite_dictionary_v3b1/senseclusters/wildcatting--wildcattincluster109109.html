wildcatting

noun
risky oil exploration
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪldˌkætɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldˌkætɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of drilling for oil or gas in areas that have not been proven to contain reserves

Examples

Oil wildcatting
Many fortunes were made and lost through wildcatting during the early days of the oil industry.
He invested heavily in wildcatting, hoping to discover a new oil field.
The risks of wildcatting are high, but the rewards can be enormous if oil is found.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exploration - the act of searching for resources such as oil or minerals
  • antonym: production - the process of extracting oil or gas from proven reserves
  • synonym: prospecting - searching for mineral deposits, especially by drilling or excavation
