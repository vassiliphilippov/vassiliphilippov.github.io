artlessly

adverb
in a natural or ingenuous way
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrtləsli / (AmE), / ˈɑːtləsli / (BrE)
in a natural, ingenuous, or simple manner, without cunning or pretense

Examples

Artlessly accept advice
He dealt with the criticism artlessly, accepting it without defensiveness or guile.
She artlessly confessed her feelings, surprising everyone with her honesty.
The child spoke artlessly, unaware of the impact of her words.
