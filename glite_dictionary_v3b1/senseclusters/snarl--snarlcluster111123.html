snarl

verb
make or move with a snarling noise
5.0 1.41/m
Pronunciation: / sn…ërl / (AmE), / sn…ëÀêl / (BrE)
to make a low, harsh sound or to move while making such a sound, like an animal showing anger or aggression

Examples

Engines snarl loudly

The wolves began to snarl when they sensed danger nearby.
