CD drive

noun
device for reading compact discs
6.1
Pronunciation: / siː diː ˈdraɪv / (AmE), / siː diː ˈdraɪv / (BrE)
A device that reads compact discs, often as part of a computer or audio system.

Examples

A CD drive device
Many laptops today come without a built-in CD drive due to the decline of physical media.
My computer no longer has a CD drive, so I use an external one for old discs.
To install the software, insert the disc into the CD drive and follow the prompts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: CD player - device for playing audio compact discs
  • synonym: DVD drive - device for reading and writing DVDs
