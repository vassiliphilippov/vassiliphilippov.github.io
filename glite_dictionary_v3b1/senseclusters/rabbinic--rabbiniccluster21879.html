rabbinic

adjective
relating to rabbis or their teachings
Pronunciation: / rəˈbɪnɪk / (AmE), / rəˈbɪnɪk / (BrE)
relating to rabbis, their teachings, or Jewish religious law.

Examples

Rabbinic teachings
He enrolled in a rabbinic studies program to become a scholar of Jewish law.
Her thesis focused on the influence of rabbinic authority in medieval Europe.
The rabbinic tradition places great emphasis on the interpretation of sacred texts.
