mythically

adverb
in a legendary way
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪθɪkli / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely large, impressive, or extraordinary, as if belonging to myth or legend

Examples

Mythically tall mountains
The mountain rose mythically above the clouds, inspiring awe in all who saw it.
Her achievements were mythically impressive, setting records that lasted for decades.
The city grew mythically fast, transforming into a major metropolis almost overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: legendary - in a way that is famous or remarkable like a legend
  • antonym: ordinarily - in a usual or normal way
