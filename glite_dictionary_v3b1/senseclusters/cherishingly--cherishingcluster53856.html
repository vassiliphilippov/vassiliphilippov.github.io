cherishingly

adverb
with deep affection
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛrɪʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛrɪʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows deep affection, care, or love for someone or something valued

Examples

To cherish memories

He held the old letter cherishingly, remembering the person who wrote it.
The artist gazed cherishingly at her finished painting, proud of her work.
She held the old photograph cherishingly, remembering the happy days it captured.
He spoke cherishingly about his childhood memories during the family gathering.
The teacher cherishingly encouraged her students to pursue their dreams.

  • synonym: tenderly - in a gentle and caring way
