diplomatic service

noun
body of government officials in diplomacy
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
the government department or body consisting of officials who represent their country in foreign affairs and at international missions

Examples

The diplomatic service
After years in the diplomatic service, she was appointed ambassador to France.
Competition for a position in the diplomatic service can be extremely fierce.
Many members of the diplomatic service are stationed overseas for much of their careers.
