pontine

adjective
relating to bridges
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑnˌtaɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɒnˌtiːn / (BrE)
relating to bridges or the construction of bridges.

Examples

Pontine bridge design

A pontine structure must be both strong and flexible to withstand environmental stress.
They studied ancient pontine techniques to restore the historic bridge.
