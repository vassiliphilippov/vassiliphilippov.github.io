bidimensional

adjective
lacking depth
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪdɪˈmɛnʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪdɪˈmɛnʃənəl / (BrE)
simple or lacking emotional or intellectual depth; flat and not fully developed, especially when describing characters or ideas

Examples

A good story needs more than just bidimensional heroes and villains to keep readers engaged.
