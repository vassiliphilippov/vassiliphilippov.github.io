short

adverb
not reaching a target or goal
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt / (AmE), / ʃɔːt / (BrE)
to a point or distance less than a goal or target; not as far as expected or intended

Examples

Fall short of goal
Her answer fell short of what was required to pass the test.
The missile landed short of its intended target.
They stopped short of accusing him directly during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: completely - fully; to the greatest extent
  • synonym: insufficiently - to an insufficient degree; not enough
