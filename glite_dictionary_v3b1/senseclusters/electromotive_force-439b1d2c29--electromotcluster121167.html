electromotive force

noun
source voltage causing electric current
6.4 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / …™Àål…õk.tr…ôÀàmo ä.t…™v f…îrs / (AmE), / …™Àål…õk.tr…ôÀàm…ô ä.t…™v f…îÀês / (BrE)

Examples
