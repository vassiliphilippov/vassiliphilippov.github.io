big boy

noun
grown or mature person (informal)
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ bɔɪ / (AmE), / bɪɡ bɔɪ / (BrE)
an informal term for a grown or mature person, or someone acting with new independence or responsibility, often used playfully

Examples

A big boy now
Ever since he got his own apartment, he feels like a big boy now.
The doctor said he was a big boy and didn't cry during the shot.
You're a big boy—it's time to start doing your own laundry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adult - a fully grown person
  • antonym: child - a young person not yet mature
