India rubber

noun
natural rubber from tree sap
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiə ˈrʌbər / (AmE), / ˈɪndi ə ˈrʌbə / (BrE)
a natural, elastic material obtained from the sap of certain tropical trees, especially Hevea and Ficus, used in products like tires and erasers

Examples

Natural India rubber
Bouncing balls were once made entirely from India rubber extracted from South American trees.
In the 19th century, India rubber was commonly used to make waterproof coats and shoes.
The eraser on the end of this old pencil is made of India rubber.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rubber - a tough, elastic polymer used in various products
