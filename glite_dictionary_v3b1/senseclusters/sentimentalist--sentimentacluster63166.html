sentimentalist

noun
person given to excessive sentimentality
3.7 6.0
Pronunciation: /sen.tɪ.ˈmen.tə.lɪst/ /; / /sən.tɪ.ˈmen.tə.lɪst/ (AmE), /sen.tɪ.ˈmen.tə.lɪst/ /; / /sən.tɪ.ˈmen.tə.lɪst/ (BrE)

Examples

A sentimentalist person

The author was criticized as a sentimentalist for his overly emotional writing style.
