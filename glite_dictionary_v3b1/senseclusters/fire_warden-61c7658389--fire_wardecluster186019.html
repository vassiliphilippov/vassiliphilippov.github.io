fire warden

noun
official managing forest fire safety
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ ˌwɔrdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌwɔːdn̩ / (BrE)
an official responsible for managing and protecting a forest area from fire hazards

Examples

Fire warden duties
Our community appointed a fire warden to oversee emergency procedures in case of wildfire.
The fire warden coordinates with local firefighters during major forest fires.
The fire warden patrols the park daily during the dry season to prevent any outbreaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firefighter - a person whose job is to put out fires
  • synonym: forest ranger - an official responsible for protecting and maintaining forests
