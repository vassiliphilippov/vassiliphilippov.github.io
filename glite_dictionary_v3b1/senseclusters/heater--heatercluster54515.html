heater

noun
device for providing heat
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːtər / (AmE), / ˈhiːtə / (BrE)
a device for supplying heat or warmth, especially to a room or for heating water

Examples

Electric heater
Please turn on the heater before you shower to warm up the bathroom.
The heater broke last night, so the room was freezing this morning.
They bought a portable heater to keep the office warm during winter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: air conditioner - device that cools and sometimes heats air
  • synonym: radiator - a device, typically metal, that emits heat into a room
