dot

verb
to mark with a dot
4.2
Pronunciation: / dɑt / (AmE), / dɒt / (BrE)
to make or place a small round mark or spot on something

Examples

To dot the i
Make sure you dot your i's and cross your t's in the application.
Please dot each 'i' carefully when writing your name.
The teacher asked the students to dot the correct answers on the test sheet.
He dots his paintings with bright, vivid colors for emphasis.
She used a marker to dot the worksheet with answers.
Tiny fireflies dotted the night sky above the trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spot - to mark with small spots or patches
  • synonym: stipple - to cover with dots or small spots, especially in art
