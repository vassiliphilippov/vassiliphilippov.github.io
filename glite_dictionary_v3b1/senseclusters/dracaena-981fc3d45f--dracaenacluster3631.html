Dracaena

noun
genus of tropical plants with sword-shaped leaves
8.3
Pronunciation: /drə.ˈsiː.nə/ /; / /drə.ˈkeɪ.nə/ (AmE), /drə.ˈsiː.nə/ /; / /drə.ˈkeɪ.nə/ (BrE)
A genus of tropical plants native to the Old World, known for having branches ending in tufts of sword-shaped leaves; some species are popular as ornamental houseplants.

Examples

Dracaena houseplant

Several species of Dracaena are valued for their striking sword-shaped leaves.
The Dracaena marginata is a popular houseplant for homes and offices.
