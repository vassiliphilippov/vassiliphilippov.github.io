Synonyms

  • back
    1.0 238/m
    back is the common everyday word for the side behind something, while rear is more formal and used in writing.
  • back end
    3.8 0.21/m
    back end is used for the far side of a machine or vehicle, while rear is a general, neutral word.
  • backside
    1.6 2.01/m
    backside often means a person's buttocks or a casual back surface, while rear is neutral and used in formal contexts.
