rose-red

adjective
deep bluish-red color
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊz ˌrɛd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊz ˌrɛd / (BrE)
of a deep, slightly bluish-red color, resembling that of some roses

Examples

Rose-red color
He painted the sunset with shades of rose-red and gold.
The rose-red petals stood out vividly against the green foliage.
The dress was a striking rose-red that caught everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crimson - a rich deep red color, inclining to purple
  • degree lower: scarlet - a brilliant red color with a slightly orange tinge
