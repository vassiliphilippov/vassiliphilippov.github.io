ladle

verb
serve or remove with a ladle
9.9 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈleɪ.dəl/ (AmE), /ˈleɪ.dəl/ (BrE)
to serve or transfer a liquid or soft food using a ladle or similar deep spoon

Examples

To ladle soup
He ladled water from the well to fill the bucket.
She carefully ladled the soup into each bowl.
The volunteer ladled hot stew to the waiting guests.
He ladled sauce over the pasta before serving it at the table.
She carefully ladled the hot stew into each guest's bowl.
The chef ladled soup into cups for the waiting customers.
