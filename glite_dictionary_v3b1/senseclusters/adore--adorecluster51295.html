adore

verb
love deeply and intensely

to love someone or something very much; to regard with deep, often rapturous affection

Examples

To adore someone
Many fans adore the singer for his generosity and kindness.
She absolutely adores her grandchildren and spends as much time with them as possible.
When they first met, he instantly adored her cheerful personality.

Synonyms
