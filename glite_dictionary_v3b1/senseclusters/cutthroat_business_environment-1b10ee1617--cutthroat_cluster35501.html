cutthroat business environment

noun
ruthlessly competitive business setting
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌtˌθroʊt ˈbɪznəs ɪnˌvaɪrənmənt / (AmE), / ˈkʌtˌθrəʊt ˈbɪznəs ɪnˌvaɪərənmənt / (BrE)
a business context characterized by intense, ruthless competition where individuals or companies will do almost anything to succeed

Examples

Many startups fail due to the relentless pace of the cutthroat business environment.

Surviving in a cutthroat business environment requires resilience and strategic thinking.
