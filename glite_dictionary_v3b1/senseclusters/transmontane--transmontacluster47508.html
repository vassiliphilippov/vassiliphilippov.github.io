transmontane

adjective
on/from the other side of mountains
Pronunciation: / trænzˈmɑːnˌteɪn / (AmE), / trænzˈmɒn.teɪn / (BrE)
Located on or coming from the other side of the mountains, especially relative to the speaker's location.

Examples

Transmontane regions
Several rare plant species are found only in transmontane regions.
The transmontane winds brought a dry heat to the valley.
The settlers crossed the range into transmontane territory in search of new land.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tramontane - on or coming from the far side of the mountains (especially in Italy or southern Europe)
