billet

noun
entry ticket or token
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪlɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɪlɪt / (BrE)
a small, usually rectangular piece of material, or a ticket giving entry or allocation to a place or event

Examples

Entry billet
He handed the guard his billet to enter the concert.
Each visitor received a billet at the entrance to the museum.
You must show your billet before you can take your seat in the theater.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pass - an official document or card that allows entry or access
  • synonym: ticket - a printed piece of paper that allows entry to an event or place
