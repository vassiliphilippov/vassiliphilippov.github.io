Synonyms

  • coastside
    3.7 0.00/m
    coastside refers to places right by the sea or coast and sounds local and informal, while coastal means near or relating to the coast.
  • shoreside
    4.1 0.02/m
    shoreside sits near the edge of a shore and suggests very close water access, while coastal covers the broader land by the sea area.
  • onshore
    3.5 0.84/m
    onshore means on or moving toward the land from the sea and often contrasts with offshore, while coastal simply means near the sea.
  • coastwise
    7.7 0.01/m
    coastwise describes moving or traveling along the coast for transport or trade, while coastal simply means located near the sea.
