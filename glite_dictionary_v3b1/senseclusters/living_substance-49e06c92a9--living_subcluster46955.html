living substance

noun
the material of a living cell
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˈsʌbstəns / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˈsʌbstəns / (BrE)
the material that makes up a living cell, including both the cytoplasm and the nucleus

Examples

The living substance of cells
Microscopists study the living substance to understand cellular processes.
The living substance of the cell is essential for its growth and development.
The nucleus is a part of the living substance within each cell.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protoplasm - the living content of a cell that is surrounded by a plasma membrane
