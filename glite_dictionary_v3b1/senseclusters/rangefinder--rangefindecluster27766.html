rangefinder

noun
device for measuring distance to an object
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪndʒˌfaɪndər / (AmE), / ˈreɪndʒˌfaɪndə / (BrE)
an instrument that measures the distance from the observer to a target, using acoustic, optical, or electronic methods

Examples

Laser rangefinder
A laser rangefinder can quickly determine how far away a golf hole is.
Soldiers used a portable rangefinder to calculate the distance to the target.
The photographer adjusted the rangefinder before taking the shot to ensure proper focus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: telemeter - an instrument for measuring distance, especially by electronic means
