maw

noun
mouth or jaws (informal, often figurative)
4.9 7.3
Pronunciation: /mɔː/ (AmE), /mɔː/ (BrE)
(informal) the mouth or jaws, especially of a voracious animal or figuratively as an opening that consumes.

Examples

The maw of a beast
He tossed the old files into the maw of the shredder, never to be seen again.
She stuffed the last bite of cake into her maw and grinned mischievously.
The hungry beast opened its maw wide, revealing rows of sharp teeth.
