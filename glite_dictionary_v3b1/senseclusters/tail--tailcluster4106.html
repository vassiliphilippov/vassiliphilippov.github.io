tail

noun
person employed to follow and observe someone
Pronunciation: / teɪl / (AmE), / teɪl / (BrE)
a person, especially a spy or detective, employed to follow someone and report their movements, typically in secret

Examples

The detective assigned a tail to keep watch on the suspect's house.
