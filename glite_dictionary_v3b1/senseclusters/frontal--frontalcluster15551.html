frontal

adjective
relating to the front of an advancing air mass
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌn.t̬əl / (AmE), / ˈfrʌn.təl / (BrE)
(of weather or meteorology) relating to the front of an advancing mass of air, especially where two air masses meet and weather changes occur.

Examples

Frontal weather system
A frontal system moved through last night, bringing heavy showers.
Meteorologists recorded a sharp drop in temperature as the frontal boundary passed.
The forecast predicts frontal rainfall in the region tomorrow.
