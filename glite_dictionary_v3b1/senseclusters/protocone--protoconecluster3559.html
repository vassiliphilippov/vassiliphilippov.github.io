protocone

noun
main upper molar cusp
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊtəˌkoʊn / (AmE), / ˈprəʊtəˌkəʊn / (BrE)
the main cusp on the upper molar teeth of some mammals, especially in early mammalian species
