protocone

noun
main upper molar cusp
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊtəˌkoʊn / (AmE), / ˈprəʊtəˌkəʊn / (BrE)
the main cusp on the upper molar teeth of some mammals, especially in early mammalian species

Examples

The protocone cusp
The paleontologist identified the protocone as a key feature of the ancient mammal's tooth.
Damage to the protocone can affect how an animal chews its food.
Researchers compared the size of the protocone in different fossil species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypocone - a cusp on the upper molar teeth of mammals, located behind the protocone
  • synonym: paracone - a cusp on the upper molar teeth of mammals, located beside the protocone
