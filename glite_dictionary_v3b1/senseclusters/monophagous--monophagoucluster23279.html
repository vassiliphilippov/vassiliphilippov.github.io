monophagous

adjective
eating one food type
Pronunciation: / məˈnɑːfəɡəs / (AmE), / məˈnɒfəɡəs / (BrE)
feeding on or consuming only one specific type of food or host

Examples

Monophagous animal
The panda is a monophagous animal, eating almost exclusively bamboo.
Some insects are monophagous and depend on a single plant species for survival.
Because it is monophagous, the beetle population declines if its only food source disappears.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: oligophagous - feeding on a few closely related types of food
  • antonym: polyphagous - feeding on many different kinds of food
