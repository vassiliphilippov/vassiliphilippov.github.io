corruptly

adverb
in a dishonest or immoral way
Pronunciation: / kəˈrʌptli / (AmE), / kəˈrʌptli / (BrE)
In a dishonest or immoral way, especially for personal gain.

Examples

Behave corruptly
She was accused of behaving corruptly during the investigation.
The official acted corruptly by accepting bribes from the company.
They believed the system had been manipulated corruptly from within.

Related Terms

  • antonym: honestly - in an honest manner
