revet

verb
to cover and support with protective facing
6.3 8.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɛt / (AmE), / rɪˈvɛt / (BrE)
to cover or support a wall, embankment, or similar structure with a layer of stone, concrete, or other material to strengthen or protect it
