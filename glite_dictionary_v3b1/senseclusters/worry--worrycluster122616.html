worry

verb
touch or rub repeatedly; fidget with
8.3 0.22/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆwÉœri / (AmE), / ËˆwÊŒri / (BrE)

Examples

She absentmindedly worried the ring on her finger during the meeting.
The child worried the loose thread on his sweater until it came off.
