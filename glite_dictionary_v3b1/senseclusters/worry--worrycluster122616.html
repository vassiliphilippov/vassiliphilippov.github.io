worry

verb
touch or rub repeatedly; fidget with
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜri / (AmE), / ˈwʌri / (BrE)
to touch or rub something repeatedly and habitually, especially in a fidgeting or restless manner

Examples

To worry a pencil
He worried his pencil as he tried to think of an answer.
She absentmindedly worried the ring on her finger during the meeting.
The child worried the loose thread on his sweater until it came off.
