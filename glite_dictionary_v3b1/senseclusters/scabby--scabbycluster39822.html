scabby

adjective
covered with scabs
Pronunciation: / ˈskæbi / (AmE), / ˈskæbi / (BrE)
covered with scabs or rough, crusted patches caused by healing wounds or skin disease.

Examples

A scabby knee
After falling off his bike, his knees looked scabby for a few weeks.
He wore long sleeves to hide his scabby arms.
The scabby dog scratched constantly at its sore patches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scabrous - having a rough surface, often used for skin or surfaces
