past tense

noun
grammatical tense for past actions
1.2 1.0
Pronunciation: /pæst tens/ (AmE), /pɑːst tens/ (BrE)
a verb tense that refers to an action or state that occurred in the past

Examples

The past tense form
In the sentence 'He walked to school,' 'walked' is in the past tense.
Regular verbs in English usually add '-ed' to form the past tense.
She always confuses the past tense with the present tense when speaking English.
