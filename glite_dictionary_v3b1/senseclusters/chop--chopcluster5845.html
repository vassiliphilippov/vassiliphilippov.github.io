chop

verb
move suddenly or abruptly
Pronunciation: / tʃɑp / (AmE), / tʃɒp / (BrE)
to move suddenly, quickly, or abruptly; to shift direction or position with a sudden motion

Examples

To chop direction
Her mood chopped from cheerful to irritable in an instant.
Stock prices chopped up and down throughout the volatile trading session.
The speedboat suddenly chopped to the left, startling everyone on board.
