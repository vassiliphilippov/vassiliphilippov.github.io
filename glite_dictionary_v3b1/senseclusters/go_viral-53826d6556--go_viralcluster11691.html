go viral

verb
spread rapidly online
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˈvaɪɹəl / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈvaɪərəl / (BrE)
(of information, images, videos, etc.) to spread very quickly to a large number of people on the internet.

Examples

To go viral online
Her tweet about climate change went viral after celebrities started sharing it.
It's every marketer's dream that an advertisement will go viral and attract huge attention.
The video of the dancing dog went viral overnight and gained millions of views.

  • synonym: trend - to become popular or widely discussed online
