Republican River

proper noun
river in the central United States
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌblɪkən ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪkən ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in the central United States, flowing from eastern Colorado eastward through Nebraska and Kansas, where it joins the Kansas River

Examples

Republican River in Kansas
During their road trip, they crossed the Republican River just west of the Kansas border.
Many small towns along the Republican River rely on its waters for agriculture and recreation.
The Republican River often experiences flooding during the spring due to snowmelt and heavy rainfall.
