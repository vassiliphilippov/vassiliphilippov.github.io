nonnative

noun
person not native speaker
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈneɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈneɪtɪv / (BrE)
a person who does not speak a language as their first language

Examples

Nonnative speaker
Many nonnative speakers find pronunciation to be the most difficult part of learning English.
The class was designed specifically for nonnative students to improve their writing skills.
As a nonnative, she worked hard to master the local accent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: learner - a person who is learning a language
  • antonym: native - a person who speaks a language as their first language
