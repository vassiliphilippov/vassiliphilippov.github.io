historical linguistics

noun
study of language change over time
4.4
Pronunciation: / hɪˈstɔrɪkəl lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪkəl lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks / (BrE)
the study of how languages change over time, including the development and evolution of languages and dialects

Examples

Historical linguistics study
Many researchers in historical linguistics analyze sound changes across different eras.
Recent advances in historical linguistics have helped trace the origins of several language families.
She developed an interest in historical linguistics after studying ancient texts in college.
