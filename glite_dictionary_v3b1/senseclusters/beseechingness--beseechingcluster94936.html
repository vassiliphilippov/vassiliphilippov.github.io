beseechingness

noun
quality of imploring or pleading
9.8
Pronunciation: / bɪˈsiːtʃɪŋnəs / (AmE), / bɪˈsiːtʃɪŋnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of expressing an urgent or earnest appeal; the state of imploring or pleading fervently.

Examples

With beseechingness in voice
He spoke with a beseechingness that made it impossible to refuse his request.
The beseechingness in her eyes moved everyone in the room to help.
The beseechingness of his letter revealed his desperation and hope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entreaty - an earnest or humble request
  • synonym: imploring - earnestly asking or begging
