tenantless

adjective
without occupants
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnəntləs / (AmE), / ˈtɛnəntləs / (BrE)
not occupied by a tenant; having no one renting or living in it

Examples

A tenantless house
The apartment remained tenantless after the previous family moved out.
They lowered the rent because the building had been tenantless for months.
A tenantless cottage stood at the edge of the quiet village.

Related Terms

  • antonym: occupied - being lived in or used by someone
  • synonym: vacant - not occupied; empty
