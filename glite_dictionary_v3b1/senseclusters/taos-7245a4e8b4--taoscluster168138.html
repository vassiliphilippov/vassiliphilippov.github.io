Taos

noun
member of Pueblo people of northern New Mexico
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊs / (AmE), / ˈtaʊs / (BrE)
a member of the Pueblo people native to northern New Mexico, especially one from Taos Pueblo.

Examples

Taos Pueblo people

The Taos maintain many traditional ceremonies and crafts.
Visitors to New Mexico often seek to learn more about the history of the Taos.
