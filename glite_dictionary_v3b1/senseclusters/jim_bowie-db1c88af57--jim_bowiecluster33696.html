Jim Bowie

proper noun
American pioneer, Alamo defender
Pronunciation: / dʒɪm ˈboʊ.i / (AmE), / dʒɪm ˈbəʊ.i / (BrE)
(1796–1836) An American pioneer and soldier who became a hero of the Texas Revolution, noted for his role in the defense of the Alamo.

Examples

Jim Bowie hero
Jim Bowie is remembered as one of the legendary defenders of the Alamo.
Many books have been written about the life and exploits of Jim Bowie.
The famous Bowie knife is named after Jim Bowie, who popularized its use.
