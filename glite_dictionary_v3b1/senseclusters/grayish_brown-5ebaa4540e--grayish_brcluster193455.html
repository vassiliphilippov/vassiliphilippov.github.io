grayish brown

noun
a light grey-brown color
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪɪʃ braʊn /; / ˈɡreɪɪʃ ˈbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪɪʃ braʊn /; / ˈɡreɪɪʃ ˈbraʊn / (BrE)
a color or pigment that is a mixture of grey and brown, resulting in a light dull brown shade

Examples

A grayish brown color
His jacket was a faded grayish brown, showing years of use under the sun.
She chose a grayish brown paint for the living room to create a warm atmosphere.
The bird's feathers were a soft grayish brown that helped it blend with the woodland floor.
Her scarf was a lovely shade of greyish brown that matched her boots perfectly.
The walls were painted a subtle greyish brown that gave the room a warm feeling.
They chose a greyish brown fabric for the new sofa to suit their neutral decor.
