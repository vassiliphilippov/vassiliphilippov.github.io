booter

noun
person who clamps cars
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːtər / (AmE), / ˈbuːtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to attach wheel clamps to vehicles to enforce parking regulations or collect fines

Examples

Parking booter
The booter placed a clamp on the illegally parked car early in the morning.
After receiving several complaints, the city hired a new booter to patrol the downtown area.
Drivers were careful to avoid the booter when parking near the stadium.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parking attendant - a person who supervises parking and enforces parking rules
