kidnappee

noun
person abducted
Pronunciation: / ˌkɪdnæpˈiː / (AmE), / ˌkɪdnæpˈiː / (BrE)
a person who has been kidnapped and is held against their will

Examples

The kidnappee rescued
The kidnappee was rescued by the police after several days in captivity.
Negotiators worked tirelessly to ensure the safe return of the kidnappee.
The family was relieved when the kidnappee finally came home unharmed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: kidnapper - a person who takes someone away by force to demand something
  • synonym: victim - a person harmed or affected by a crime or event
