wheel-like

adjective
resembling a wheel in shape
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːl ˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈwiːl ˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the shape or appearance of a wheel; round and flat like a wheel

Examples

Wheel-like shape
A strange, wheel-like pattern appeared in the crop field overnight.
Some jellyfish have a wheel-like structure that helps them move efficiently through the water.
The ancient shield was distinctly wheel-like in both size and decoration.
