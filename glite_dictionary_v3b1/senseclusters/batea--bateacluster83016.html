batea

noun
shallow wooden basin
Pronunciation: / bəˈteɪə / (AmE), / bəˈteɪə / (BrE)
a shallow wooden basin or dish traditionally used for washing, kneading dough, or serving food

Examples

Bread in a batea
She brought a carved batea to the table with fresh bread and olives.
The baker used a batea to knead the dough for the morning's bread.
After washing the vegetables, he placed them in a batea to drain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basin - a wide, open container used for holding liquids or food
  • synonym: bowl - a round, deep dish used for food or liquid
