bookroom

noun
room for selling books
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌruːm / (BrE)
a room or area in a shop, school, or institution where books are stored, displayed, sold, or managed

Examples

School bookroom
The school's bookroom was filled with new textbooks for the upcoming semester.
She spent her afternoon browsing the bookroom for interesting novels to read.
Volunteers organized the donated books neatly in the community center's bookroom.
The school keeps all its extra textbooks in the bookroom for easy access.
Each morning, the staff organizes the bookroom before students arrive to collect their materials.
After the renovation, the bookroom had more shelves and better lighting for sorting books.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bookshop - a shop where books are sold
  • antonym: library - a place where books are kept for reading or borrowing
  • synonym: storeroom - a room used for storing things
