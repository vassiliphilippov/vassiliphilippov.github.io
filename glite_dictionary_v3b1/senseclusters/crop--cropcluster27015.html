crop

noun
bird's food-storage pouch
5.6
Pronunciation: / krɑp / (AmE), / krɒp / (BrE)
a pouch in the esophagus of many birds and some other animals, used for storing and softening food before digestion

Examples

Bird's crop
Many birds regurgitate food from their crop to feed their young.
Seed stays in the crop of a pigeon before passing into the stomach.
The robin’s crop was swollen after it had eaten its fill of berries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craw - the crop of a bird; a bird's stomach or gullet
