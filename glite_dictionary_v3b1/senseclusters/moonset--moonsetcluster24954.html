moonset

noun
moon going down
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌsɛt / (BrE)
the time or event when the moon disappears below the horizon

Examples

The moonset time
We woke up early to watch the moonset over the mountains.
Photographers gathered on the shore to capture the beautiful moonset before dawn.
The sky turned a deep blue as the moonset marked the end of the night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: moonrise - the time when the moon appears above the horizon
  • synonym: sunset - the time when the sun goes below the horizon
