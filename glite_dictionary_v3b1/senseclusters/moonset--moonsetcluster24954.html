moonset

noun
moon going down
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmuːn.set/ (AmE), /ˈmuːn.set/ (BrE)

Examples

The moonset time

Photographers gathered on the shore to capture the beautiful moonset before dawn.
The sky turned a deep blue as the moonset marked the end of the night.
