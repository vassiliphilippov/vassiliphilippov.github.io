loll

verb
to lounge or be lazy
9.9 5.6
Pronunciation: /lɑːl/ (AmE), /lɒl/ (BrE)
to sit, lie, or stand in a lazy, relaxed way, often with little energy or purpose

Examples

Cats often loll in the sun for hours at a time.
He likes to loll on the sofa and watch television after work.
