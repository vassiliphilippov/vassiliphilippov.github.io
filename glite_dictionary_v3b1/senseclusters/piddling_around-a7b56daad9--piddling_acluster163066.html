piddling around

verb
waste time; be unproductive
7.2 6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪd.əl.ɪŋ ə.ˈraʊnd/ /; / /ˈpɪd.lɪŋ ə.ˈraʊnd/ (AmE), /ˈpɪd.əl.ɪŋ ə.ˈraʊnd/ /; / /ˈpɪd.lɪŋ ə.ˈraʊnd/ (BrE)

Examples
