physiognomist

noun
face-reading expert
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪziˈɑːnəmɪst / (AmE), / ˌfɪziˈɒnəmɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or judges character or personality from facial features

Examples

The physiognomist studies faces
The physiognomist examined each guest's face before making his predictions.
In the 19th century, a physiognomist might have been consulted to judge a person's trustworthiness.
She read about a famous physiognomist who claimed to reveal secrets from a single glance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phrenologist - a person who studies skull shapes to determine character
