miraculousness

noun
quality of being miraculous
Pronunciation: / mɪˈrækjələsnəs / (AmE), / mɪˈrækjʊləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being very surprising, extraordinary, or seeming to result from a miracle

Examples

The miraculousness of rescue
The miraculousness of the rescue left everyone speechless.
Scientists could not explain the miraculousness of the plant's sudden recovery.
He spoke about the miraculousness of life with genuine awe.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ordinariness - the quality of being normal or usual
  • synonym: wonder - a feeling of great surprise and admiration
