drizzle

verb
to rain lightly
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈdrɪzəl / (BrE)
to rain lightly, with very small drops falling slowly and steadily

Examples

It drizzles outside
Clouds gathered and soon it started drizzling over the quiet city.
It began to drizzle just as we left the house for a walk.
The weather forecast said it might drizzle all morning.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: rain - water falling in drops from the clouds
