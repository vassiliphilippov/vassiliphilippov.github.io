arnica

noun
a plant used in herbal medicine
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrnɪkə / (AmE), / ˈɑːnɪkə / (BrE)
a plant with yellow, daisy-like flowers, used in herbal medicine, especially for treating bruises and minor injuries
