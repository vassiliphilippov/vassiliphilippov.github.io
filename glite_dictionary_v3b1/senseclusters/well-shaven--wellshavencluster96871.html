well-shaven

adjective
closely and recently shaved
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛlˈʃeɪvən / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈʃeɪvən / (BrE)
having been closely shaved recently so that little or no hair is visible, especially on the face or head.

Examples

A well-shaven face
A well-shaven appearance is often preferred in professional settings.
Despite a long journey, the ambassador looked perfectly well-shaven and composed.
He arrived at the interview well-shaven and neatly dressed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clean-shaven - with all facial hair shaved off
