happily

adverb
in a joyous manner
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhæpəli / (AmE), / ˈhæpɪli / (BrE)
In a joyous or cheerful manner; with happiness.

Examples

She smiled happily
She accepted the invitation happily, eager to see her friends again.
The children ran happily down the hill into their mother's arms.
They laughed happily as they played in the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joyfully - in a manner full of joy
  • antonym: sadly - in a sorrowful or unhappy manner
