Deinocheirus

noun
a genus of lightly built theropod dinosaur
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪnəˈkaɪrəs /; / daɪˌnəʊˈkaɪrəs / (AmE), / ˌdaɪnəʊˈkaɪərəs /; / daɪˌnəʊˈkʌɪrəs / (BrE)
a large theropod dinosaur genus from the Late Cretaceous period, known for its long limbs and massive forelimbs
