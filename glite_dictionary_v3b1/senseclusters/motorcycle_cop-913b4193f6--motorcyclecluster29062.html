motorcycle cop

noun
police officer on motorcycle
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtərˌsaɪkəl kɑp / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəˌsaɪkl kɒp / (BrE)
A police officer who patrols on a motorcycle, often monitoring motorists' speeds and enforcing traffic laws.

Examples

Motorcycle cop patrol
A motorcycle cop escorted the parade through the busy streets.
The motorcycle cop pulled over several drivers for speeding on the highway.
We saw a motorcycle cop waiting near the intersection to catch drivers running red lights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: police officer - a member of the police force
  • synonym: traffic cop - a police officer who directs and controls traffic
