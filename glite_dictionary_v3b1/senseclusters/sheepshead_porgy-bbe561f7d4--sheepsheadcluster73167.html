sheepshead porgy

noun
a tropical Atlantic fish with striped body
Pronunciation: / ˈʃipsˌhɛd ˈpɔrɡi / (AmE), / ˈʃiːpsˌhɛd ˈpɔːɡi / (BrE)
a tropical Atlantic fish, Archosargus probatocephalus, found from Florida and the Bahamas to Brazil, known for its toothy mouth and vertical stripes
