Dwight D. Eisenhower

proper noun
34th U.S. President and general
4.3
Pronunciation: /dwaɪt diː ˈaɪ.zən.haʊ.ər/ (AmE), /dwaɪt diː ˈaɪ.zən.haʊ.ə/ (BrE)
(1890–1969) The 34th President of the United States and a five-star general in the U.S. Army who supervised the Allied invasion of Normandy and the defeat of Nazi Germany.

Examples

Dwight D. Eisenhower as president
Dwight D. Eisenhower served as the Supreme Commander of the Allied forces in Europe during World War II.

Many historians regard Dwight D. Eisenhower's presidency as a time of economic prosperity and growth.
