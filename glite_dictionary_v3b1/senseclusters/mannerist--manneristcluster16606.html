mannerist

adjective
affected or artificial
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmæn.ər.ɪst/ (AmE), /ˈmæn.ər.ɪst/ (BrE)
showing affected, artificial, or exaggerated manners, gestures, or style rather than natural expression

Examples

Mannerist acting style
The actor's mannerist delivery made the dialogue sound forced and unnatural.
Critics described the painting as mannerist because of its exaggerated poses and stylized forms.
Her speech became increasingly mannerist, with elaborate gestures and dramatic pauses.
