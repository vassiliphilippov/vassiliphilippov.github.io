attitude

noun
deliberate pose or posture (art/theater)
4.8 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈæt.ə.tuːd/ /; / /ˈæt.ɪ.tuːd/ (AmE), /ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd/ /; / /ˈæt.ə.tjuːd/ (BrE)
