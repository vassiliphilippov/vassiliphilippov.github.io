comparable to

adjective
as good as or similar to
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmp(ə)rəbəl tu / (AmE), / ˈkɒmp(ə)rəbəl tʊ / (BrE)
worthy of being compared with; as good as or similar in quality, value, or amount to (something else)

Examples

Comparable to the best
His achievements are comparable to those of the greatest athletes in history.
Nothing she had ever tasted was comparable to her grandmother’s cooking.
The quality of this handmade work is comparable to that of famous designers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: equal to - being the same in value or amount as something
  • antonym: inferior to - of lower quality or value than something
  • antonym: superior to - of higher quality or value than something
