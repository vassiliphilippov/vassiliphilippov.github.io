swanky

adjective
fashionable and elegant (showy)
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈswæŋki / (AmE), / ˈswæŋki / (BrE)
Imposingly fashionable and elegant, often in a way that draws attention or suggests ostentation.

Examples

A swanky hotel
He arrived at the awards ceremony in a swanky sports car.
Her swanky new handbag turned heads at the party.
They stayed in a swanky downtown hotel during the conference.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modest - not showy or ostentatious; humble
  • synonym: stylish - fashionably elegant
