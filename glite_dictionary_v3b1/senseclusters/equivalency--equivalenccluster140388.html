equivalency

noun
official qualification match
6.3
Pronunciation: /ɪ.kwɪ.ˈvæl.ən.siː/ (AmE), /ɪ.kwɪ.ˈvæl.ən.siː/ (BrE)
an official recognition or certificate that one qualification or course is equal to another for purposes such as admission or credit

Examples

Equivalency certificate
She obtained a high school equivalency to apply for college.
Many employers accept a GED as an equivalency to a traditional diploma.
International students may need to prove equivalency of their qualifications for admission.
