escarole

noun
broad-leaved variety of endive
Pronunciation: / ˈɛskəˌroʊl / (AmE), / ˈɛskərəʊl / (BrE)
a variety of endive with broad, flat leaves and irregular, frilled edges, often used in salads and cooking

Examples

Fresh escarole leaves
At the market, he chose a head of escarole instead of romaine lettuce.
She added chopped escarole to the soup for extra flavor and texture.
The salad was topped with fresh escarole and a light vinaigrette.
