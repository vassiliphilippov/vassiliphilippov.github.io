dog robber

noun
pet thief or petty thief
Pronunciation: / dɑɡ ˈrɑbər / (AmE), / dɒɡ ˈrɒbə / (BrE)
a thief who steals dogs or takes items of little value

Examples

The dog robber steals pets
In some cities, the problem of the dog robber has become a serious concern for pet owners.
Police caught a known dog robber trying to lure pets with treats in the park.
The dog robber sneaked into the yard at night, but the neighbors spotted him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cat burglar - a burglar who enters buildings by climbing to steal, usually valuables
  • synonym: pet thief - someone who steals pets, especially dogs or cats
  • synonym: petnapper - a person who kidnaps or steals pets
