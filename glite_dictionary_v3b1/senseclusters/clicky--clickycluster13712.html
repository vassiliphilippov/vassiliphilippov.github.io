clicky

adjective
exclusive, unfriendly
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪki / (AmE), / ˈklɪki / (BrE)
unfriendly or exclusive in a way that suggests belonging to a small, closed group

Examples

Clicky group
The new students found the art department unfriendly and clicky, so they struggled to make friends.
Her coworkers were so clicky that it was hard for her to feel welcome in the office.
Some clubs at school can be very clicky, making it difficult for outsiders to join.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cliquish - tending to form or favor a small, exclusive group
  • antonym: inclusive - open to everyone; not exclusive
