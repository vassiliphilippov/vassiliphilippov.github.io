baseplate

noun
dental support plate
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbeɪs.pleɪt/ (AmE), /ˈbeɪs.pleɪt/ (BrE)
a temporary wax or plastic plate used in dentistry to support artificial teeth or record jaw position during denture construction

Examples

Dental baseplate
The dentist carefully adjusted the baseplate to ensure a comfortable fit for the patient.
A wax baseplate is often used to record the position of the jaws before making dentures.
Technicians use a baseplate as a foundation when constructing artificial teeth in the lab.
