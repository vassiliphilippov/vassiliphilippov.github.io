unchristian

adjective
lacking Christian values
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈkrɪstʃən / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkrɪstʃən / (BrE)
not showing qualities or behavior considered typical of a good Christian, such as kindness or compassion

Examples

Unchristian behavior
Many people thought his actions were completely unchristian and went against the values of the community.
It is considered unchristian to judge others harshly without understanding their situation.
Her refusal to help those in need was seen as unchristian by her friends.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Christian - showing qualities or behavior typical of a good Christian
  • synonym: cruel - causing pain or suffering to others
  • synonym: unkind - not kind or considerate
