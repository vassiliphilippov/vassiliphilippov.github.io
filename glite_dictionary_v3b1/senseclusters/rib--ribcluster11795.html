rib

noun
cut of meat containing one or more ribs
Pronunciation: / rɪb / (AmE), / rɪb / (BrE)
a cut of meat that includes one or more ribs of an animal, typically beef or pork, often cooked or served as a dish

Examples

A rib steak
He ordered a juicy rib at the steakhouse for dinner.
Pork rib is one of the highlights of their barbecue menu.
The chef seasoned each rib before placing it on the grill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chop - cut of meat from near the rib, often including the bone
