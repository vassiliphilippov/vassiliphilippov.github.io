Examples

Synonyms

  • relief
    1.0 34/m
    relief is the feeling of comfort when pain ends, while alleviation names both the act and that feeling.
  • assuagement
    9.9 0.01/m
    assuagement is the quiet feeling of comfort after pain lessens, while alleviation covers both the act and feeling.
  • easing
    4.0 0.33/m
    easing is the process of making things less strong or painful, while alleviation can mean that process or the relief.
  • mitigation
    5.6 3.77/m
    mitigation is a formal act to reduce severity in law or policy, while alleviation is used more for personal relief.
  • easement
    7.9 0.01/m
    easement is an uncommon or legal word for making pain less strong, while alleviation is the common general term.
