alleviation

noun
the act of making pain or suffering less severe
6.5 4.2
Pronunciation: /ə.liː.vi.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.lɪ.vi.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ə.liː.vi.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.lɪ.vi.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
