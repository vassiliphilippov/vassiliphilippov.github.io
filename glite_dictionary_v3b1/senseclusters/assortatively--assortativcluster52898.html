assortatively

adverb
by similarity
8.6
Pronunciation: / əˈsɔrtətɪvli / (AmE), / əˈsɔːtətɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves grouping or pairing based on similarity or shared characteristics

Examples

To mate assortatively
In many animal species, individuals mate assortatively according to size or color.
The study found that people tend to form friendships assortatively based on shared hobbies.
Communities often organize assortatively, with members grouping by language or culture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: randomly - in a way that lacks any pattern or method
  • synonym: selectively - in a way that involves careful choice
