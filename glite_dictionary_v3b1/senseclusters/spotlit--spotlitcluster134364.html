spotlit

verb
to illuminate with spotlight
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑtˌlɪt / (AmE), / ˈspɒtˌlɪt / (BrE)
to illuminate with a strong, focused beam of light, especially using a spotlight.

Examples

Spotlit stage
The dancer was spotlit as she performed her solo on stage.
A single painting was spotlit in the otherwise dimly lit gallery.
He felt nervous when he was suddenly spotlit during the presentation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: darken - to make or become dark
  • synonym: illuminate - to light up something
