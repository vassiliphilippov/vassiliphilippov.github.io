Bermuda buttercup

noun
yellow-flowered South African bulbous plant
Pronunciation: / bɚˈmjuːdə ˈbʌtərˌkʌp / (AmE), / bəˈmjuːdə ˈbʌtəkʌp / (BrE)
A South African bulbous plant of the wood sorrel family, bearing bright yellow flowers; widely grown as an ornamental and sometimes considered a weed.

Examples

Bright Bermuda buttercup flowers
Farmers often struggle to control Bermuda buttercup because of its invasive growth.
She planted Bermuda buttercup bulbs along the path for a cheerful splash of color.
The garden was covered in bright yellow Bermuda buttercup blossoms each spring.
