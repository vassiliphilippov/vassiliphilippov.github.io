Schinus molle

noun
Peruvian evergreen tree with pink fruit
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàskiÀên…ôs Ààmo äli / (AmE), / ÀàskiÀên…ôs Ààm…íle…™ / (BrE)
a small evergreen tree native to Peru, with a broad, rounded crown, slender, drooping branches, clusters of greenish flowers, and rose-pink fruit; also known as the Peruvian pepper tree.
