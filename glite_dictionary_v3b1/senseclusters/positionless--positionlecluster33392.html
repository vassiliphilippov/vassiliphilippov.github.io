positionless

adjective
without a clear stance
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɪʃənləs / (AmE), / pəˈzɪʃənləs / (BrE)
not having or expressing a clear opinion, stance, or policy on an issue

Examples

Positionless on an issue
During the meeting, he remained positionless and did not contribute to the discussion.
Voters were frustrated by the candidate's positionless responses to important questions.
Her positionless attitude made it difficult for others to know where she stood on the issue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indecisive - not able to make decisions quickly or firmly
  • synonym: neutral - not supporting or favoring either side in a conflict or dispute
  • antonym: opinionated - expressing strong beliefs or judgments
