Lincoln

noun
breed of long-wooled sheep
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋkən / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋkən / (BrE)
a breed of large, long-wooled sheep originally from Lincolnshire, raised for mutton and wool

Examples

Lincoln sheep breed
Lincoln sheep are valued for their heavy fleece and calm temperament.
She specializes in breeding Lincoln sheep for agricultural shows across the country.
The farmer introduced a new flock of Lincoln sheep to improve wool quality on his farm.
