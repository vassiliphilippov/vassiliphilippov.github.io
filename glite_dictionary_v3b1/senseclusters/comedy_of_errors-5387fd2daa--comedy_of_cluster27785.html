comedy of errors

noun
series of blunders; mishap-filled situation
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmədi əv ˈɛrərz / (AmE), / ˈkɒmədi əv ˈɛrəz / (BrE)
a situation or event marked by a series of blunders, misunderstandings, or mishaps, often resulting in chaos or unintended humor

Examples

A comedy of errors
The entire move was a comedy of errors, from the broken truck to the missing keys.
Their vacation turned into a comedy of errors with lost luggage, missed flights, and hotel mix-ups.
What was meant to be a simple dinner party became a comedy of errors as each guest arrived at the wrong house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debacle - a sudden and ignominious failure; a fiasco
  • synonym: fiasco - a complete failure, especially in a ludicrous or humiliating way
