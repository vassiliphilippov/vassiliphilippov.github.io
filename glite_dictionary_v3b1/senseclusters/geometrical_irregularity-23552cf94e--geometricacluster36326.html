geometrical irregularity

noun
uneven or asymmetrical shape or pattern
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪkəl ɪˌrɛɡjəˈlærɪti / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪkəl ɪˌrɛɡjʊˈlarɪti / (BrE)
an uneven or unevenly distributed shape or spatial pattern; a lack of symmetry in geometry
