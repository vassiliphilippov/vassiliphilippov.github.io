mess about

verb
spend time idly or purposelessly
Pronunciation: / mɛs əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / mɛs əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to spend time in a place doing nothing in particular; to be somewhere without any real purpose

Examples

To mess about at home
He spent the weekend messing about at home instead of working.
The children used to mess about in the park after school.
There were a few teenagers messing about near the playground all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hang around - spend time in a place without doing anything special
  • synonym: loiter - stand or wait around without apparent purpose
