uttermost

adjective
the most distant or extreme
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈʌt̬.ɚˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈʌt.ə.məʊst / (BrE)
at the greatest possible degree, distance, or extremity; the most extreme or utmost

Examples

Uttermost point
The uttermost branch of the river was barely visible from where they stood.
The explorers pushed on to the uttermost boundary of the desert.
They reached the uttermost regions of the Arctic after weeks of travel.
In his uttermost despair, he found comfort in his friends’ support.
She made the uttermost effort to resolve the issue before the deadline.
The expedition reached the uttermost limits of the country.
