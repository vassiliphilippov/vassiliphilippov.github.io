yellow pond lily

noun
a North American water lily with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ pɑnd ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ pɒnd ˈlɪli / (BrE)
a common water lily of eastern and central North America, with broad leaves and globe-shaped yellow flowers, typically found in sluggish fresh or slightly brackish water
