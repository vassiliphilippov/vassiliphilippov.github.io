Guarani

noun
member of a South American indigenous group
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɡwɑːrəˈniː / (AmE), / ˌɡwɑːrəˈniː / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous people living chiefly in Paraguay and Bolivia

Examples

A Guarani community

Many Guarani maintain their traditional customs despite modern influences.
The Guarani have a rich oral history passed down through generations.
