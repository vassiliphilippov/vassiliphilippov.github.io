vomeronasal

adjective
of the vomeronasal organ
Pronunciation: / ˌvoʊməroʊˈneɪzəl / (AmE), / ˌvəʊmərəʊˈneɪzəl / (BrE)
relating to the vomeronasal organ, a sensory organ that detects pheromones in some animals

Examples

The vomeronasal organ
The vomeronasal system is important for detecting pheromones in many reptiles and mammals.
Scientists study vomeronasal receptors to understand animal communication through chemical signals.
Some animals rely on vomeronasal cues to identify potential mates.
