cathole

noun
outdoor waste hole
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkætˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈkæthəʊl / (BrE)
a small hole dug in the ground outdoors for burying human waste

Examples

A cathole outdoors
When camping in remote areas, it is important to dig a cathole for proper waste disposal.
Each hiker was instructed to use a cathole at least 60 meters from any water source.
The guide demonstrated how to cover a cathole after use to protect the environment.
