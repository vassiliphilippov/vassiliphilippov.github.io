evacuator

noun
emergency rescuer
6.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvækjueɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˈvækjueɪtə / (BrE)
a person, vehicle, or organization that moves people from a dangerous place to safety during an emergency

Examples

Emergency evacuator
The evacuator arrived quickly to help families escape the burning building.
During the hurricane, a military evacuator transported dozens of people to a safe shelter.
Each evacuator was trained to handle emergencies calmly and efficiently.

Related Terms

  • antonym: evacuee - a person who is removed from a dangerous place
  • synonym: rescuer - a person who saves someone from danger
