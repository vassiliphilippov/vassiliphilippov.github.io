Alcyone

proper noun
mythical woman turned into kingfisher
Pronunciation: / ælˈsaɪəni / (AmE), / ælˈsaɪəni / (BrE)
A woman in Greek mythology who was transformed into a kingfisher, often associated with the Halcyon days myth.

Examples

Alcyone in mythology
In Ovid's Metamorphoses, Alcyone mourns for her lost husband Ceyx.
Legend says the gods showed mercy by transforming Alcyone into a kingfisher.
The story of Alcyone explains the origin of the term 'halcyon days.'
