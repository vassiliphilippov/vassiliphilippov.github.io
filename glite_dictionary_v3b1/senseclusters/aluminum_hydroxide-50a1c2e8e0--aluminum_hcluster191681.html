aluminum hydroxide

noun
white crystalline compound; mineral gibbsite
2.0 5.1
Pronunciation: / əˌluːmɪnəm haɪˈdrɑksaɪd /; / əˌluːˌmɪniəm haɪˈdrɑksaɪd / (AmE), / ˌaljʊˈmɪnɪəm haɪˈdrɒksaɪd /; / ˌaljʊˈmɪniəm hʌɪˈdrɒksʌɪd / (BrE)
a white crystalline compound, Al(OH)₃, that occurs naturally as the mineral gibbsite and is used in antacids, medicine, water purification and industry
