permeameter

noun
permeability measuring device
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜːrmiˈæmɪtər / (AmE), / ˌpɜːmiˈæmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the permeability of a material, such as soil or rock

Examples

A permeameter device
The laboratory technician calibrated the permeameter before starting the experiment.
A permeameter is essential for testing how easily water moves through different soils.
Researchers used a permeameter to compare the permeability of various rock samples.

Related Terms

  • synonym: permeability - the ability of a material to allow fluids to pass through it
  • synonym: porosimeter - an instrument used to measure the porosity of a material
