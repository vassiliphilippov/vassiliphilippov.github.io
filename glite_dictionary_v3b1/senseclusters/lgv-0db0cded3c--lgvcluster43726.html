LGV

noun
sexually transmitted chlamydial infection; genital ulcers, swollen lymph nodes
9.9 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / …õl d íi vi / (AmE), / …õl d íiÀê viÀê / (BrE)
an infectious disease caused by a species of chlamydia bacteria, transmitted by sexual contact, and characterized by genital lesions and swelling of lymph nodes in the groin

Examples

LGV infection
Early diagnosis of LGV can prevent serious complications if treated promptly.
Testing for LGV is recommended in patients presenting with genital ulcers and swollen lymph nodes.
