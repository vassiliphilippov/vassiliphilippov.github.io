swiper

noun
touchscreen user
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈswaɪpər / (AmE), / ˈswaɪpə / (BrE)
a person who uses a touchscreen device by making swipe gestures to move through or select items, especially in apps.

Examples

Phone swiper
As a frequent swiper, she quickly browses through hundreds of photos on her phone every day.
The dating app rewards each swiper with new matches based on their preferences.
Many young people have become expert swipers thanks to social media and mobile games.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tapper - a person who selects items by tapping on a touchscreen
  • synonym: user - a person who uses a device or service
