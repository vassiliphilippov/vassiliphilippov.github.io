frenum

noun
tissue fold in body
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːnəm / (AmE), / ˈfriːnəm / (BrE)
a small fold of tissue that restricts the movement of an organ in the body

Examples

Tongue frenum
The dentist checked the frenum under the boy's tongue for any abnormalities.
A short frenum can sometimes cause speech difficulties in children.
During the examination, the doctor noted that the patient's frenum appeared normal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frenulum - a small fold of tissue that supports or restricts the movement of a body part
