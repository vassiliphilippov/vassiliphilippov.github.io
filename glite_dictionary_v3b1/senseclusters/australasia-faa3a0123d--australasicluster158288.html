Australasia

noun
region including Australia and New Zealand
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstrəˈleɪʒə / (AmE), / ˌɒstrəˈleɪziə / (BrE)
A region comprising Australia, New Zealand, and neighboring islands in the South Pacific.

Examples

Australasia region
European exploration of Australasia began in the 17th century and dramatically changed the region.
Many unique species of birds can only be found in Australasia due to its geographic isolation.
The conference brought together scientists from countries across Australasia to discuss environmental challenges.
