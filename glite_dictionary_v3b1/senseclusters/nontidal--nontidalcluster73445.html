nontidal

adjective
not affected by tides
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈtaɪdəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈtaɪdəl / (BrE)
not affected by tides or not subject to tidal movement

Examples

A nontidal river
The upper part of the river is nontidal and maintains a steady flow throughout the year.
Unlike the estuary, this section of the canal is completely nontidal.
Many freshwater fish thrive in nontidal waters where the current is more predictable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tidal - affected by or relating to tides
