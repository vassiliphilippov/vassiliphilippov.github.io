reexamination

noun
second review
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪɡˌzæmɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːɪɡˌzæmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a second or further examination or review of something or someone, often to reconsider or check details

Examples

Case reexamination
After new evidence was found, the court ordered a reexamination of the case.
The teacher's reexamination of the test papers revealed several grading errors.
A reexamination of the company's finances showed some unexpected expenses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inspection - a careful examination or scrutiny
  • antonym: neglect - failure to care for or pay proper attention to something
  • synonym: review - an act of examining or assessing something again
