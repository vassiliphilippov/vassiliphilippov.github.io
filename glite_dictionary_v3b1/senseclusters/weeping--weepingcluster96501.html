Synonyms

  • tearful
    1.8 1.70/m
    tearful often shows quiet sadness with tears, while weeping describes visible sorrow with active tears on the face.
  • in tears
    2.0 3.74/m
    in tears means crying openly and deeply upset, while weeping shows sorrow often with a sad, tearful face.
  • teary-eyed
    3.2 0.51/m
    teary-eyed describes eyes that fill with tears from emotion, while weeping means full sorrow with visible crying or sad expression.
  • teary
    2.0 1.48/m
