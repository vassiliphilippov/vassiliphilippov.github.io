kindergarten

noun
preschool for young children
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪndɚˌɡɑːrtən / (AmE), / ˈkɪndəˌɡɑːtən / (BrE)
a school or class for very young children, usually aged four to six, that prepares them for primary school

Examples

A kindergarten class
Attendance in kindergarten helps children develop important social skills before entering primary school.
The teacher decorated the kindergarten classroom with colorful posters.
When Lily started kindergarten, she made many new friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preschool - an educational establishment for children too young for primary school
