buckteeth

noun
protruding front teeth
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkˌtiːθ / (AmE), / ˈbʌkˌtiːθ / (BrE)
teeth, especially the upper front ones, that stick out noticeably from the mouth

Examples

Prominent buckteeth
The dentist recommended braces to help correct her buckteeth.
He was self-conscious about his buckteeth when he smiled in photos.
Some cartoon characters are drawn with exaggerated buckteeth for a humorous effect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overbite - a dental condition where the upper front teeth extend over the lower teeth
