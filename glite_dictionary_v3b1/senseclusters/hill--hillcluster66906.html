hill

noun
naturally raised area of land
Pronunciation: / hɪl / (AmE), / hɪl / (BrE)
A naturally raised area of land, not as high or craggy as a mountain.

Examples

Climb a hill
A small white cottage sat at the foot of the hill.
Children love to roll down the grassy hill in the park.
She hiked up the hill to get a better view of the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knoll - a small, rounded hill
  • degree lower: mound - a small natural or artificial hill
  • degree higher: mountain - a large natural elevation of the earth's surface
  • antonym: valley - a low area of land between hills or mountains
