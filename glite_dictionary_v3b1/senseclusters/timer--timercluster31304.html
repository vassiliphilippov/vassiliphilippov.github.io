timer

noun
device controlling time-based activation
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪmɚ /; / ˈtaɪmər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmə / (BrE)
a device that measures a set period of time and signals when the time is up, often by sounding an alarm or turning something on or off

Examples

Kitchen timer
Please set the timer for 20 minutes before putting the cake in the oven.
She used a timer to remind herself to take breaks while working.
The lights in the garden are connected to a timer so they turn on automatically at dusk.
He bought a new digital timer to help him manage his workouts.
Please set the timer for 20 minutes so the cake doesn't burn.
The kitchen timer beeped loudly, telling us that the pasta was ready.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alarm - a device that gives a warning at a set time
  • synonym: alarm clock - a clock that sounds an alarm at a set time
  • synonym: stopwatch - a device used to measure the exact time elapsed
