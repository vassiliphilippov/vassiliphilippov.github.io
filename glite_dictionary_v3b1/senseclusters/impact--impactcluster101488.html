impact

noun
strong effect or influence
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmˌpækt /; / ˈɪm.pækt / (AmE), / ˈɪmpækt /; / ˈɪm.pækt / (BrE)
a powerful effect or influence that something has on a situation, person, or thing

Examples

Impact on society
Her speech made a lasting impact on the audience.
The new policy had a significant impact on the company's profits.
We are still assessing the environmental impact of the construction project.
His speech had a powerful impact on the audience, inspiring many to take action.
The new law is expected to have a significant impact on small businesses.
We are studying the impact of climate change on global agriculture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cause - something that produces an effect or result
  • synonym: consequence - a result or outcome, especially one that is unwanted or significant
  • synonym: effect - a change that is caused by something
  • synonym: influence - the capacity to have an effect on someone or something
