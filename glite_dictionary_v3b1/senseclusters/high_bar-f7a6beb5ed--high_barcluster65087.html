high bar

noun
horizontal gymnastics apparatus
Pronunciation: / haɪ bɑr / (AmE), / haɪ bɑː / (BrE)
a gymnastic apparatus consisting of a horizontal bar supported by uprights at both ends, used for gymnastic exercises and routines

Examples

Olympic high bar
He won a gold medal for his high bar performance at the Olympics.
Proper grip is essential for safety when swinging on the high bar.
The gymnast performed an impressive routine on the high bar during the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horizontal bar - another term for the high bar; same apparatus used in gymnastics
