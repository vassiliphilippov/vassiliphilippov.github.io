auk

noun
northern seabird, family Alcidae
Pronunciation: / ɔk / (AmE), / ɔːk / (BrE)
A black-and-white, short-necked, web-footed diving bird found in northern seas, belonging to the family Alcidae.

Examples

A auk bird
Many species of auk nest on the steep cliffs of northern islands.
The great auk was hunted to extinction in the 19th century.
We spotted an auk diving for fish near the rocky shoreline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guillemot - another type of auk of the northern seas
  • synonym: puffin - a distinctive seabird in the auk family with a colorful beak
