uncustomarily

adverb
not in usual way
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈkʌstəmɛrəli / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkʌstəmərɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is not customary or usual

Examples

Speak uncustomarily little
He spoke uncustomarily little during the entire dinner, surprising everyone at the table.
The weather was uncustomarily warm for this time of year, so we went to the beach.
She was uncustomarily generous, offering to pay for everyone's lunch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: customarily - in a way that is usual or traditional
  • synonym: unusually - in a way that is not usual or expected
