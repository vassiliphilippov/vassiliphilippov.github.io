longshore

adjective
along the shore
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋˌʃɔr / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌʃɔː / (BrE)
located or occurring along or near the shore of a sea or ocean

Examples

Longshore current
The longshore winds carried the salty air far inland.
Scientists studied the longshore drift to understand how sand moves along the beach.
A longshore current can change the shape of the coastline over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coastal - situated on or near a coast
  • antonym: inland - situated away from the coast or shore
