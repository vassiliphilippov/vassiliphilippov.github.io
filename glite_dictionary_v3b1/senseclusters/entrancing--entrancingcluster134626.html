entrancing

adjective
captivating; holding full attention
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈtrænsɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈtrɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
so beautiful or fascinating that it holds your complete attention, as if under a spell

Examples

An entrancing story
Her entrancing storytelling style captivated listeners of all ages.
The ballerina gave an entrancing performance that left the audience speechless.
We spent hours lost in the entrancing beauty of the mountain landscape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bewitching - so beautiful or attractive as to seem magical
  • antonym: boring - not interesting; tedious
  • synonym: captivating - attracting and holding the interest; charming
  • synonym: enchanting - delightfully charming or attractive
