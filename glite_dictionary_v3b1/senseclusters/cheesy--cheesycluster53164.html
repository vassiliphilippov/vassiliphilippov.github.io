cheesy

adjective
of poor quality; shoddy
4.3 1.16/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt ÉiÀêzi / (AmE), / Ààt ÉiÀêzi / (BrE)
Of very poor quality or cheaply made; unsubstantial or shoddy.

Examples

Cheesy plastic watch
He wore a cheesy plastic watch that looked like it came from a toy store.
The film was criticized for its cheesy special effects and predictable plot.
The hotel room felt cheesy and rundown, far from what the website had promised.
