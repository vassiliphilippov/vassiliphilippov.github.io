shamefacedness

noun
state of being ashamed or embarrassed
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪmˈfeɪstnəs / (AmE), / ʃeɪmˈfeɪsɪdnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being ashamed or embarrassed.

Examples

His shamefacedness showed
He tried to hide his shamefacedness after being caught cheating on the test.
Her shamefacedness was clear when she realized her mistake in front of everyone.
There was a certain shamefacedness in his apology, as though he wished he could disappear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embarrassment - a feeling of self-consciousness, shame, or awkwardness
  • synonym: shyness - the quality of being shy; discomfort in social situations
