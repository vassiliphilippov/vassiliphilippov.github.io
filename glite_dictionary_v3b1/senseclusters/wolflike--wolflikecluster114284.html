wolflike

adjective
resembling or characteristic of a wolf
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊlfˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈwʊlfˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of a wolf, especially in appearance or behavior

Examples

Wolflike behavior
He gave her a wolflike grin, sharp and predatory.
The animal’s wolflike howls echoed through the forest at night.
The dogs in the film were bred for their wolflike appearance and behavior.
