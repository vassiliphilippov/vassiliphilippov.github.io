tauri

proper noun
of Taurus (stars)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊraɪ / (AmE), / ˈtaʊraɪ / (BrE)
the Latin genitive form of Taurus, used in star names to mean 'of the constellation Taurus'

Examples

Beta Tauri star
The star known as Beta Tauri is one of the brightest in the Taurus constellation.
Astronomers often refer to stars as Alpha Tauri or Gamma Tauri based on their position.
We identified several Tauri stars while studying the night sky through the telescope.
