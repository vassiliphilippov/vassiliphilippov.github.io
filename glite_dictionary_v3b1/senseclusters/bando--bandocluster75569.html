bando

noun
official proclamation
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːndoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbændəʊ / (BrE)
a public proclamation or official order issued by authorities, especially in historical or formal contexts

Examples

Official bando announcement
The mayor read the bando aloud in the town square to inform everyone of the new law.
During the festival, a bando was posted at the city gates announcing the rules for visitors.
In medieval times, a bando would often be used to declare a state of emergency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decree - an official order issued by a legal authority
  • synonym: edict - an official order or proclamation issued by a person in authority
