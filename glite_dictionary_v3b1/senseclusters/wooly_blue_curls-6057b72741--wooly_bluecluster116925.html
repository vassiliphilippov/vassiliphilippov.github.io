wooly blue curls

noun
aromatic plant of California and Mexico
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊli blu ˈkɝlz / (AmE), / ˈwʊli bluː ˈkɜːlz / (BrE)
A perennial aromatic plant with woolly leaves and blue to lavender curls of flowers, native to southern California and Mexico.

Examples

Wooly blue curls plant
Gardeners in California sometimes plant wooly blue curls to attract bees and butterflies.
She admired the delicate blooms of the wooly blue curls as they waved in the afternoon breeze.
The hillsides in spring are often covered in wooly blue curls, filling the air with their pleasant scent.
