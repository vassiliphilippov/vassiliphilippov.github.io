design

verb
plan for a specific purpose
1.0 2.2
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈzaɪn/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈzaɪn/ (BrE)
to plan, create, or invent something, especially how it will look or work

Examples

To design a building
The new stadium was designed to withstand severe weather conditions.
The software was designed to be easy for beginners to use.
This kitchen is designed for both style and functionality.
She was hired to design a marketing plan that would increase sales.
The engineers will design a new energy-efficient engine for the company.
They worked late into the night to design a solution to the technical problem.
He is currently designing a custom website for a local business.
She designed a beautiful new logo for the company.
The architect designed the house to maximize natural light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: build - to make or construct something
  • synonym: devise - to invent or plan by careful thought
  • synonym: invent - to create or design something not existing before
  • synonym: plan - to decide on and arrange in advance
