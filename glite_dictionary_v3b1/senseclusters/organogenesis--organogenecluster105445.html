organogenesis

noun
formation of plant organs
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrɡənoʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡənəʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs /; / ˌɔːɡənəʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs / (BrE)
the process by which organs are formed in a living thing
