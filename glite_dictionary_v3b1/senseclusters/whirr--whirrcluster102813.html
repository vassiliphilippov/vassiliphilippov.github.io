whirr

noun
buzzing or humming sound from rapid movement
Pronunciation: / wɝː / (AmE), / wɜː / (BrE)
a continuous buzzing or humming sound produced by something moving rapidly

Examples

A soft whirr
The whirr of the ceiling fan was soothing as she drifted off to sleep.
There was a sudden whirr as the drone lifted off the ground.
We listened to the constant whirr of machinery in the background.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buzz - a low, continuous humming or murmuring sound
  • synonym: hum - a low, steady, continuous sound
