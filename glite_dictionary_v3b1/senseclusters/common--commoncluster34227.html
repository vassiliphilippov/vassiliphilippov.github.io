Synonyms

  • common land
    3.5 0.10/m
    common land is land open to public use often with legal grazing rights, while common is a town open area for recreation.
  • commons
    3.2 0.79/m
    commons are shared open spaces used by a community, while common refers to a single town or village open area for grazing or play.
  • green
    1.0 0.88/m
    green is a small grassy area in a town kept mainly for recreation, while common can mean larger grazing land near a village.
