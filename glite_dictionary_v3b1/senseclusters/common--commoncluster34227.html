common

noun
public open area of land
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑm.ən / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ən / (BrE)
an area of open land in or near a town, used by the public for recreation or grazing

Examples

Village common
Children were playing football across the green common behind the houses.
Let's meet for a picnic on the common this Saturday afternoon.
Many English villages still retain a central common where locals can gather.
