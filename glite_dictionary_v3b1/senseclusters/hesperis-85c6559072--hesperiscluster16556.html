Hesperis

noun
genus of fragrant-flowered herbs
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛspərɪs / (AmE), / ˈhɛspərɪs / (BrE)
a genus of flowering plants with fragrant flowers that smell strongest at night, commonly called dame's violet or night-scented stock
