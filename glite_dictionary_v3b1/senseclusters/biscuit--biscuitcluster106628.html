biscuit

noun
small, soft leavened bread
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪs.kɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɪs.kɪt / (BrE)
A small, round, soft bread, typically leavened with baking powder or baking soda rather than yeast.

Examples

A warm biscuit with butter
I like to eat a warm biscuit with butter and honey for breakfast.
She baked a batch of biscuits for the holiday dinner.
The restaurant serves their fried chicken with homemade biscuits on the side.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scone - A similar baked good, often sweeter and more common in British English
