higgle

verb
to bargain or wrangle over terms or price
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈhɪɡl / (BrE)
to bargain or wrangle persistently, especially over the price or terms of a purchase or agreement

Examples

To higgle over price
Farmers in the market higgled among themselves over the value of each harvest.
She refused to higgle and agreed to pay the full price at once.
The vendors would often higgle with customers for hours over their goods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: haggle - to argue or bargain persistently over the cost of something
