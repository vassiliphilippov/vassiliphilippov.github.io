neuritic

adjective
nerve inflammation
Pronunciation: / nʊˈraɪtɪk / (AmE), / njʊəˈraɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by inflammation or degeneration of nerves

Examples

Neuritic pain
The doctor noted several neuritic symptoms during the neurological examination.
Researchers are studying the causes of neuritic changes in Alzheimer's disease.
The patient experienced pain due to neuritic inflammation in the arm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: healthy - not affected by disease or inflammation
  • synonym: neuropathic - relating to disease or dysfunction of nerves
