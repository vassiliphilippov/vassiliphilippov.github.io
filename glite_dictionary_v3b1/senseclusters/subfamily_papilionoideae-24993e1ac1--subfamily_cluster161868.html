subfamily Papilionoideae

noun
a subfamily of legumes (Fabaceae)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˌfæməli ˌpæpɪliˌoʊnɔɪˈdiːiː / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˌfæmɪli ˌpapɪlɪəʊnɔɪˈdiːiː / (BrE)
A major subfamily of the legume family Fabaceae, comprising the majority of species formerly placed in the family Papilionaceae.

Examples

The subfamily Papilionoideae plants

Many important crops belong to the subfamily Papilionoideae due to their nitrogen-fixing abilities.
The subfamily Papilionoideae includes familiar plants such as beans, peas, and lupins.
