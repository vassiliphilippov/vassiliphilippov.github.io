areole

noun
area around nipple
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛriˌoʊl / (AmE), / ˈɛəriːəʊl / (BrE)
the small, pigmented circular area of skin surrounding the nipple on the breast of a mammal

Examples

Breast areole
The doctor checked the areole for any signs of irritation or unusual color changes.
Some people have a larger areole while others have a smaller one, and both are normal.
During pregnancy, the areole often becomes darker and more noticeable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nipple - the small projection in the center of the breast through which milk can flow
