Examples

Mushy peas
After boiling the vegetables too long, they turned out mushy and unappetizing.

The bananas became mushy after sitting out in the heat all day.

Synonyms

  • pulpy
    4.5 0.38/m
    pulpy describes soft, wet flesh like overripe fruit, while mushy suggests a more shapeless, wet and soupy texture.
  • pappy
    7.6 0.00/m
    pappy has a porridge-like, very soft feel often lacking structure, while mushy is more generally pulpy or soggy.
  • squishy
    3.0 1.48/m
    squishy is soft and easy to squeeze with a springy or spongy bounce, while mushy is pulpy and shapeless.
  • doughy
    4.6 0.49/m
    doughy feels thick, sticky and raw like uncooked dough, while mushy means pulpy, soft and without firmness.
