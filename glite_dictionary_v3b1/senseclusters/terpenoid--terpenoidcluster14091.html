terpenoid

noun
organic plant compound
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrpəˌnɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɜːpɪnɔɪd / (BrE)
a naturally occurring organic compound related to terpenes, often found in plants and essential oils

Examples

Plant terpenoid
Scientists study terpenoids for their potential medicinal properties in treating diseases.
Many essential oils owe their distinctive scents to the presence of terpenoids.
The leaves of some plants are rich in terpenoids that help protect them from insects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: terpene - a large class of organic compounds produced by plants
