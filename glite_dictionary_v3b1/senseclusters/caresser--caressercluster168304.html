caresser

noun
person who strokes gently
4.6
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɛsər / (AmE), / kəˈrɛsə / (BrE)

Examples

He became known as a skilled caresser of fine fabrics in the textile shop.
A true caresser knows how to calm a child with just a touch.
