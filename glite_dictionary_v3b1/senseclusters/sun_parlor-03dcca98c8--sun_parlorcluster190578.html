sun parlor

noun
room enclosed with glass to enjoy sunlight
Pronunciation: / sʌn ˈpɑrlɚ / (AmE), / sʌn ˈpɑːlə / (BrE)
A room, typically enclosed largely with glass, designed to allow occupants to enjoy sunlight while being sheltered indoors.
