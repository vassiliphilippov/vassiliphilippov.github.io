duty-free

adjective
exempt from customs duty
3.3 0.57/m
Pronunciation: / ËŒduËtiËˆfriË / (AmE), / ËŒdjuËtiËˆfriË / (BrE)
Exempt from payment of customs duty, especially used to describe goods bought at airports or on international journeys.

Examples

Duty-free goods
He purchased a bottle of duty-free whiskey before his flight.
Passengers are allowed a limited amount of duty-free goods when entering the country.
The airport shop sells duty-free perfumes and cosmetics.
