tray

noun
flat container for holding or serving
1.0 20/m
Pronunciation: / treÉª / (AmE), / treÉª / (BrE)
A shallow, flat, usually rectangular or oval container used for holding, displaying, or serving food or other articles.

Examples

A tray of food

She carried the drinks on a silver tray to the guests in the living room.
The nurse brought in lunch on a tray and placed it by the patient's bed.
