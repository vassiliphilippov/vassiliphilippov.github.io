Canadian English spelling

noun
English spelling conventions in Canada
6.1
Pronunciation: / kəˈneɪdiən ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈspɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / kəˈneɪdiən ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈspɛlɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

Canadian English spelling rules
He was confused by the mixture of British and American rules in Canadian English spelling.
