vitamin

noun
essential organic nutrient for metabolism
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪ.t̬ə.mɪn / (AmE), / ˈvɪt.ə.mɪn / (BrE)
any of a group of organic substances that are essential in small amounts for normal metabolism and good health, usually obtained from the diet

Examples

Essential vitamins
Some breakfast cereals are fortified with extra vitamins to improve their nutritional value.
Vitamin C is an important vitamin that helps support the immune system.
You should eat a variety of fruits and vegetables to get enough vitamins.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mineral - an inorganic substance needed for health and growth
  • synonym: nutrient - a substance that provides nourishment essential for growth and life
