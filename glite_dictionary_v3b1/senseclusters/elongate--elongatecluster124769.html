elongate

adjective
long and slender in shape
3.5 6.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɔŋˌɡeɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɒŋˌɡeɪt /; / ɪˈlɒŋ.ɡeɪt / (BrE)
having a shape that is much longer than it is wide; long and narrow or slender
