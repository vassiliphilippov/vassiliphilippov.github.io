Synonyms

  • linear
    6.1 0.89/m
    linear describes very long, narrow shapes with nearly parallel sides, while elongate means long and generally slender in form.
  • oblong
    4.6 0.84/m
    oblong means longer than wide with parallel or nearly parallel sides, while elongate focuses on being long and especially slender.
  • prolate
    9.9 0.03/m
    prolate describes an object stretched along one axis like a rugby ball, while elongate means simply long and narrow.
  • elliptic
    6.5 0.03/m
    elliptic means shaped like an oval or ellipse with rounded ends, while elongate stresses being long and narrow.
  • oval
