kingdom Monera

noun
former biological kingdom of prokaryotes
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkɪŋ.dəm mə.ˈnɪr.ə/ (AmE), /ˈkɪŋ.dəm mə.ˈnɪə.rə/ (BrE)
a former classification of organisms consisting of prokaryotic microorganisms, such as bacteria and blue-green algae, characterized by the absence of a distinct nucleus and membrane-bound organelles.

Examples

Kingdom Monera classification
Advances in genetics have led to the breakdown of the kingdom Monera classification.
In older biology textbooks, kingdom Monera is used to classify all prokaryotic organisms.
Many scientists now place organisms formerly in kingdom Monera into separate domains.

  • synonym: prokaryote - organism without a distinct nucleus
