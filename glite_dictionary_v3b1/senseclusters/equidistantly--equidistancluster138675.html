equidistantly

adverb
at equal distances
Pronunciation: / ˌiːkwɪˈdɪstəntli / (AmE), / ˌiːkwɪˈdɪstəntli / (BrE)
at equal distances from two or more points or locations

Examples

Trees planted equidistantly
The trees were planted equidistantly to create a symmetrical appearance in the garden.
Markers were placed equidistantly along the hiking trail for easy navigation.
The lights are spaced equidistantly around the stadium to ensure even illumination.

  • synonym: evenly - in a regular or equal way
