center

noun
basketball position; player in the center
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntər /; / ˈsɛnɚ /; / ˈsɛntɚ /; / ˈsɛn.tɚ /; / ˈsɛnɾɚ /; / ˈsɛn.tər /; / ˈsɛnər /; / ˈsɛnɾər / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə /; / ˈsɛn.tə / (BrE)
