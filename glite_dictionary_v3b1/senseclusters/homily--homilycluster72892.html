homily

noun
sermon on a moral or religious topic
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːməli / (AmE), / ˈhɒmɪli / (BrE)
a talk or sermon given on a moral or religious subject, especially one offered as part of a church service

Examples

Sunday homily
During the service, she listened attentively to the Sunday homily.
His homily inspired the congregation to reflect on their daily actions.
The priest delivered a thoughtful homily on kindness and charity.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: lecture - a formal talk on a subject, often instructive but less often religious
  • synonym: sermon - a talk on a moral or religious subject given in church
