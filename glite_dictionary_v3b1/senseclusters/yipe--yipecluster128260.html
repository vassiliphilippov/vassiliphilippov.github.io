yipe

noun
sharp cry or exclamation
8.9
Pronunciation: / jaɪp / (AmE), / jaɪp / (BrE)
a short, sharp cry or exclamation expressing surprise, fear, or excitement

Examples

A sudden yipe
The puppy let out a sudden yipe when it stepped on a thorn.
A loud yipe from the child startled everyone in the room.
She gave a quick yipe of excitement when she saw her birthday present.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shriek - a loud, high-pitched cry of fear or excitement
  • synonym: yelp - a short, sharp cry, especially by a dog
