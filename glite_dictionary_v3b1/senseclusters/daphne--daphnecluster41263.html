daphne

noun
evergreen shrub with fragrant bell-shaped flowers
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdæfni / (AmE), / ˈdæfni / (BrE)
a shrub with shiny, mostly evergreen leaves and clusters of small, often fragrant, bell-shaped flowers, grown as an ornamental plant

Examples

The daphne shrub
The daphne bloomed early this year, filling the air with a sweet scent.
The garden center recommended daphne as a low-maintenance ornamental shrub.
We planted a daphne in the front yard for its beautiful fragrance in spring.
