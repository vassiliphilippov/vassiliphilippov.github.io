dip

noun
exercise lowering and raising body with arms
Pronunciation: / dɪp / (AmE), / dɪp / (BrE)
a gymnastic exercise performed on parallel bars or similar equipment, in which the body is lowered and raised by bending and straightening the arms

Examples

Ten dips on bars
He performed ten dips on the parallel bars as part of his workout.
If you want stronger arms and chest muscles, try adding dips to your routine.
The coach demonstrated the correct form for dips to avoid shoulder injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pull-up - an exercise involving raising the body with the arms while hanging
  • synonym: push-up - an exercise performed by raising and lowering the body using the arms while lying face down
