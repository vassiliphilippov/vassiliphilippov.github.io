afire

adjective
burning; on fire
Pronunciation: / əˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / əˈfaɪə / (BrE)
burning; on fire or set alight by fire or flame

Examples

Afire with ideas

The crowd watched as the fireworks set the night sky afire.
The old barn was suddenly afire after the lightning struck the roof.
