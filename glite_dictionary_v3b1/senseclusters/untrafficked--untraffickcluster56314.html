untrafficked

adjective
not illegally traded
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtræfɪkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈtræfɪkt / (BrE)
not involved in illegal trade or movement of people or goods; not smuggled or sold unlawfully

Examples

Untrafficked goods
The authorities confirmed that the goods were untrafficked and entered the country legally.
Many remote areas remain untrafficked by smugglers due to strict border controls.
The organization works to ensure that all workers are untrafficked and employed fairly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: legal - permitted by law; not illegal
  • antonym: trafficked - illegally traded or moved, especially people or goods
