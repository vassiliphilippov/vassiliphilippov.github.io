Examples

Synonyms

  • twitch
    2.8 5.23/m
    twitch is a small, quick, often harmless muscle movement, while spasm is a sudden involuntary contraction of a muscle.
  • cramp
    3.2 0.69/m
    cramp is a sudden painful tightening of a muscle that lasts longer, while spasm is usually brief and less painful.
  • jerk
    2.2 1.07/m
    jerk is a quick sharp movement or pull, often not from muscle contraction, while spasm is an involuntary muscle contraction.
