phagocytize

verb
to engulf and digest
9.9
Pronunciation: / fəˈɡɑːsəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / fəˈɡəʊsɪtaɪz / (BrE)
to engulf and digest particles, cells, or bacteria, especially by a phagocyte

Examples

To phagocytize bacteria
Certain immune cells can phagocytize harmful bacteria to defend the body.
The microscope showed how the cell phagocytized the invading particle.
During infection, white blood cells are actively phagocytizing pathogens.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expel - to force out or eject
  • synonym: ingest - to take in or absorb
