Versed

noun
brand name for sedative drug (midazolam)
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜːrsɛd / (AmE), / ˈvɜːsɛd / (BrE)
A brand name for an injectable form of the drug midazolam, used for sedation and to reduce pain during medical procedures.

Examples

Patients often feel relaxed and drowsy after receiving Versed during surgery.
The nurse administered Versed before the patient underwent a minor procedure.
