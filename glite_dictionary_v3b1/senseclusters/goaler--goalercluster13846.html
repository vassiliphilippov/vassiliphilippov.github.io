goaler

noun
jailer or goalkeeper
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgoʊ.lər/ (AmE), /ˈgəʊ.lə/ (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a person who guards or is in charge of a jail or prison (also an old spelling for 'goalkeeper' in sports, but very rare)

Examples

Prison goaler
The goaler made sure no one escaped from the prison.
In the old match reports, the term goaler was used instead of goalkeeper.
The goaler was respected for his strict discipline and fairness.
The goaler checked each cell before locking the doors for the night.
In the old novel, the goaler was both feared and respected by the inmates.
The goaler brought food to the prisoners every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goalkeeper - the player who guards the goal in sports like soccer or hockey
  • synonym: jailer - a person in charge of a jail and its prisoners
  • synonym: prison guard - a person who guards prisoners in a prison
  • antonym: prisoner - a person who is kept in prison as a punishment
