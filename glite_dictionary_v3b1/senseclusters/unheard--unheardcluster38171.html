unheard

adjective
not heard or noticed by the ear
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɝd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɜːd / (BrE)
not heard; unperceived by the ear; not having been listened to or noticed.

Examples

Almost unheard words
He spoke so softly that his words were almost unheard in the noisy room.
Her warnings went unheard by the council members.
The song remained unheard outside her hometown until years later.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heard - perceived by the ear; listened to
  • synonym: inaudible - impossible to hear; not capable of being heard
