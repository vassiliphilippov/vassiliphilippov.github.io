criterium

noun
standard for judging
9.3
Pronunciation: / kraɪˈtɪriəm / (AmE), / kraɪˈtɪəriəm / (BrE)
a standard or rule used to judge or evaluate something, though less commonly used than 'criterion'

Examples

A criterium for success
The main criterium for the award was originality in design.
Applicants must meet every criterium listed in the job description.
Experience is often considered the most important criterium when hiring new staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criterion - a standard or principle for judging or evaluating
  • synonym: standard - an accepted level of quality or achievement
