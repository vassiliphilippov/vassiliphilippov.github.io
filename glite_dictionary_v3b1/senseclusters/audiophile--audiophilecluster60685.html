audiophile

adjective
for high-fidelity sound
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔdiəˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈɔːdiəʊfaɪl / (BrE)
designed for or appealing to people who are enthusiastic about high-quality sound reproduction

Examples

Audiophile headphones
He invested in an audiophile speaker system to enjoy his favorite albums in the best quality.
The store specializes in audiophile headphones for customers who demand superior sound.
Many audiophile products are designed with advanced materials to reduce distortion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: high-fidelity - producing very accurate sound reproduction
  • antonym: lo-fi - having lower sound quality, often intentionally
