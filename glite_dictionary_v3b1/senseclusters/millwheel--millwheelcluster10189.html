millwheel

noun
water wheel powering mill machinery
7.7 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪl.wiːl/ (AmE), /ˈmɪl.wiːl/ (BrE)
a large water wheel that provides power to drive the machinery in a mill

Examples

The millwheel turns
The millwheel powered the grindstones inside the ancient flour mill.
The old millwheel creaked loudly as water rushed over its paddles.
Tourists admired the picturesque millwheel beside the river.
