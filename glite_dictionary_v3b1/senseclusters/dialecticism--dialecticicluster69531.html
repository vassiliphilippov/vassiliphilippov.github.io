dialecticism

noun
dialectic reasoning
Pronunciation: / daɪəˈlɛktɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / daɪəˈlɛktɪˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice or theory of dialectic reasoning, especially the use of argument to resolve contradictions in philosophy or rhetoric

Examples

Philosophical dialecticism
The philosopher's dialecticism allowed her to address complex contradictions in her theories.
In academic debates, dialecticism is valued for its ability to clarify opposing viewpoints.
His approach to problem-solving was rooted in dialecticism, always seeking synthesis between conflicting ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dialectic - a method of argument involving contradiction and resolution
  • synonym: rhetoric - the art of effective or persuasive speaking or writing
