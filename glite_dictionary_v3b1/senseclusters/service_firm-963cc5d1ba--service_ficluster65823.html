service firm

noun
business providing services for a fee
2.8 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsɜːr.vɪs fɜːrm/ /; / /ˈsɜːr.vəs fɜːrm/ (AmE), /ˈsɜː.vɪs fɜːm/ /; / /ˈsɜː.vəs fɜːm/ (BrE)

Examples

The law office is a service firm specializing in corporate clients.
