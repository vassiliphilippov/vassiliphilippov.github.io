genus Ploceus

noun
type genus of weaver birds (Ploceidae)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ploʊˈsiː.əs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs pləʊˈsiː.əs / (BrE)
the type genus of the family Ploceidae, comprising several species of weaver birds found in Africa and Asia
