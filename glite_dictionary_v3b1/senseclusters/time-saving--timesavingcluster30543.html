time-saving

noun
reduction in time needed
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪmˌseɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmˌseɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the reduction in the amount of time needed to complete a task, or something that helps save time

Examples

A big time-saving
The new washing machine offers a significant time-saving compared to older models.
Using public transport can be a real time-saving during rush hour.
The chef recommended a time-saving technique for preparing vegetables quickly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: delay - a period of time by which something is late or postponed
  • synonym: efficiency - the ability to do something with minimal waste of time or resources
  • synonym: shortcut - a quicker way of doing something
