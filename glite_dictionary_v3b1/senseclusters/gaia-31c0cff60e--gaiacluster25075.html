Gaia

proper noun
Earth as organism
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪ.ə / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪ.ə / (BrE)
the Earth and its ecosystems considered as a single, living, self-regulating organism

Examples

Gaia theory
Some scientists study Gaia to understand how living things and the environment interact as a whole.
The concept of Gaia suggests that the Earth acts like a single, living system.
Many environmental movements are inspired by the idea of Gaia as a living planet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Earth - the planet we live on
  • synonym: biosphere - the global sum of all ecosystems
