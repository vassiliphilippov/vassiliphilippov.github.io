Gaia

proper noun
Earth as organism
8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈgaɪ.ə/ /; / /ˈgeɪ.ə/ (AmE), /ˈgaɪ.ə/ /; / /ˈgeɪ.ə/ (BrE)

Examples

Gaia theory
Some scientists study Gaia to understand how living things and the environment interact as a whole.
The concept of Gaia suggests that the Earth acts like a single, living system.
Many environmental movements are inspired by the idea of Gaia as a living planet.
