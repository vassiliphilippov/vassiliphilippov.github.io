forceps delivery

noun
childbirth using forceps assistance
3.5 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfɔːr.seps dɪ.ˈlɪv.ər.iː/ /; / /ˈfɔːr.səps dɪ.ˈlɪv.ər.iː/ (AmE), /ˈfɔː.səps dɪ.ˈlɪv.ər.iː/ /; / /ˈfɔː.seps dɪ.ˈlɪv.ər.iː/ (BrE)
a medical procedure in which forceps are used to assist the delivery of a baby by grasping its head and guiding it out through the birth canal

Examples

Forceps delivery procedure
Advances in obstetrics have reduced the frequency of forceps deliveries in modern hospitals.
Due to complications, a forceps delivery was performed to assist the birth.
The doctor recommended a forceps delivery after labor had stalled for several hours.
