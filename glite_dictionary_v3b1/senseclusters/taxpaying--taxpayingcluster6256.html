taxpaying

adjective
required to pay taxes
Pronunciation: / ˈtæksˌpeɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtæksˌpeɪɪŋ / (BrE)
required to pay taxes; not exempt from taxation.

Examples

Taxpaying business owner
As a taxpaying business owner, she carefully files her returns each year.
He is proud to be a productive, taxpaying member of society.
The city depends on its taxpaying residents to fund public services.
