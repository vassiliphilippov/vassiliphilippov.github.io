electrical contact

noun
point where electric current passes between conductors
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛk.trɪ.kəl ˈkɑn.tækt / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛk.trɪ.kəl ˈkɒn.tækt / (BrE)
a point or surface that allows electric current to pass from one conductor to another

Examples

A electrical contact point
A faulty electrical contact in the circuit board caused intermittent power loss.
The switch failed because the electrical contact became corroded over time.
To ensure a stable connection, the technician cleaned each electrical contact thoroughly before assembly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conductor - a material or object that allows electricity to flow through it
