sign-off

noun
final approval
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪn ˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈsaɪn ˌɒf / (BrE)
the act of formally ending something or giving final approval to a document, project, or process

Examples

Manager's sign-off
The manager's sign-off was required before the project could move forward.
We cannot publish the report until we receive the final sign-off from the client.
After months of work, the team celebrated when the sign-off was finally given.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approval - the act of officially agreeing to something
  • synonym: authorization - official permission or power to do something
  • antonym: rejection - the act of refusing to accept or approve something
