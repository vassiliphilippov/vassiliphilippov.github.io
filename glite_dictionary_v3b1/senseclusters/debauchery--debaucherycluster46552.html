debauchery

noun
excessive indulgence in sensual pleasures
8.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈbɑː.tʃə.ri / (AmE), / dɪˈbɔː.tʃəri / (BrE)
excessive indulgence in sensual pleasures, especially involving drinking and sexual activity

Examples

Life of debauchery
He rejected a life of debauchery in favor of quiet routine and discipline.
Rumors of debauchery at the celebrity's party quickly spread through the town.
The novel depicts a world of debauchery and moral decay during the Roaring Twenties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decadence - moral or cultural decline as characterized by excessive indulgence
  • synonym: dissipation - overindulgence in sensual pleasures; self-indulgence
  • antonym: temperance - moderation or self-restraint, especially regarding alcohol or sensual pleasures
