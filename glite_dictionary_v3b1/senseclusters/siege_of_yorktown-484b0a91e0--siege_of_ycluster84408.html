siege of Yorktown

noun
final battle of American Revolutionary War (1781)
8.2
Pronunciation: /siːdʒ əv ˈjɔːrk.taʊn/ /; / /siːdʒ ə ˈjɔːrk.taʊn/ (AmE), /siːdʒ əv ˈjɔːk.taʊn/ /; / /siːdʒ ə ˈjɔːk.taʊn/ (BrE)
the final major battle of the American Revolutionary War in 1781, in which British forces under Cornwallis surrendered to American and French troops after a three-week siege
