frowning

adjective
showing a displeased or angry expression
Pronunciation: / ˈfraʊnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfraʊnɪŋ / (BrE)
Showing a facial expression of displeasure or anger, characterized by a furrowed brow.

Examples

Frowning face
He sat at his desk, frowning in concentration.
She gave him a frowning look when he interrupted her.
The teacher's frowning face made the students fall silent.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smiling - showing a pleased, kind, or amused facial expression
