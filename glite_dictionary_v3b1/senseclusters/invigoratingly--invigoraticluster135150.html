invigoratingly

adverb
energetically, refreshingly
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɪɡəˌreɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɪɡərətɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that gives energy or makes someone feel refreshed and lively

Examples

To feel invigoratingly fresh
The cold shower woke him up invigoratingly after a long night.
She breathed invigoratingly crisp air during her morning walk in the park.
The coach spoke invigoratingly to motivate the team before the big game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dully - in a way that lacks energy or excitement
  • synonym: energetically - in a way that shows energy and enthusiasm
  • synonym: refreshingly - in a way that makes someone feel fresh or cool
