flight line

noun
area where aircraft are parked and serviced
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪt laɪn / (AmE), / ˈflaɪt laɪn / (BrE)
an area at an airport or airbase where aircraft are parked, maintained, and prepared for flight, but excluding runways and taxiways

Examples

The flight line area
Crews worked late into the night servicing planes on the flight line.
Security around the flight line was increased following the incident.
The mechanics inspected each jet on the flight line before takeoff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hangar - a large building for housing aircraft
