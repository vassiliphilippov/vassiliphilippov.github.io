drop

verb
hang or be suspended loosely
3.2
Pronunciation: / drɑp / (AmE), / drɒp / (BrE)
to hang or be suspended loosely, usually downward

Examples

A light dropped down
A single bulb dropped from the old warehouse ceiling, barely lighting the room.
Long vines dropped from the highest branches of the tree.
Tinsel dropped from the edge of the shelf, glinting in the sunlight.
