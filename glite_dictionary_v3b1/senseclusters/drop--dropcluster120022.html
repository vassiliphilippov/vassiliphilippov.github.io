drop

verb
hang or be suspended loosely
1.0 2.12/m
Pronunciation: / drÉ‘p / (AmE), / drÉ’p / (BrE)
to hang or be suspended loosely, usually downward

Examples

A light dropped down
A single bulb dropped from the old warehouse ceiling, barely lighting the room.
Long vines dropped from the highest branches of the tree.
Tinsel dropped from the edge of the shelf, glinting in the sunlight.

Synonyms

  • dangle
    2.3 13/m
    dangle hangs loosely and often swings in the air, while drop means to hang or be suspended downward more generally.
  • hang
    2.0 23/m
    hang usually drapes over fabric or clothing without movement, while drop describes hanging loosely usually pointed downward.
  • suspend
    3.5 6.57/m
    suspend is a formal word for fastening something from above, while drop is a casual term for loose hanging downward.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dangle - to hang or swing loosely, especially with a swaying motion
