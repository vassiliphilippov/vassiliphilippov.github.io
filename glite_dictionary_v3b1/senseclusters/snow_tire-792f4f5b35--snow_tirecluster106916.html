snow tire

noun
tire for driving in snow
Pronunciation: / snoʊ ˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / snəʊ ˈtaɪə / (BrE)
a tire for vehicles with deep tread designed to provide better traction on snow-covered roads

Examples

A snow tire tread
Her car handled well on icy roads thanks to the snow tires.

Many drivers switch to snow tires to improve safety during the winter months.
