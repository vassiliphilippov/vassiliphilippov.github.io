headman

noun
male leader of a tribe or clan
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈhɛd.mæn / (BrE)
the male leader or chief of a tribe or clan

Examples

Tribal headman
As headman, he was responsible for resolving disputes within the clan.
The headman welcomed the visitors to the village and presided over the meeting.
The villagers gathered around the headman to listen to his advice.
