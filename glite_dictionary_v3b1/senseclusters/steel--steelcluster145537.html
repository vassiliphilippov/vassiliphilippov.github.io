steel

noun
strong alloy of iron and carbon
1.0
Pronunciation: / stil / (AmE), / stiːl / (BrE)
A strong, hard alloy of iron with a small amount of carbon, widely used in construction and manufacturing.

Examples

Steel beams
Modern skyscrapers would not be possible without steel frameworks.
She polished the steel surfaces until they gleamed.
The beams were made of high-quality steel to support the new bridge.
