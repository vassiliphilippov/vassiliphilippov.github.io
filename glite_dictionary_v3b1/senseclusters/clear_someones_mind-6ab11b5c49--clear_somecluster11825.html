clear someone's mind

verb
make free from distracting thoughts
Pronunciation: / klɪr sʌmwʌnz maɪnd / (AmE), / klɪə ˈsʌmwʌnz maɪnd / (BrE)
to make oneself free from distracting thoughts or worries in order to focus or think calmly

Examples

To clear my mind
A long walk always helps me clear my mind after a stressful day.
Listening to music is his favorite way to clear his mind when he's overwhelmed.
She meditated for twenty minutes to clear her mind before the meeting.
