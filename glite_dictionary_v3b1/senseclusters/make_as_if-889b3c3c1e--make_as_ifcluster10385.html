make as if

verb
appear to begin an action
7.1
Pronunciation: / meɪk æz ɪf / (AmE), / meɪk æz ɪf / (BrE)
to begin or appear to begin to do something, especially when not actually intending to do it

Examples

To make as if to leave
He made as if to leave, but stopped at the door.
She made as if to answer, then changed her mind.
The dog made as if to bite, but only barked.
