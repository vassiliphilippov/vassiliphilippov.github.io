an abbreviation for standard cubic feet per minute, a unit measuring the flow rate of gases under standard conditions

Examples

SCFM air flow
The air compressor is rated at 10 SCFM at 90 psi.
Engineers use SCFM to compare the performance of different ventilation systems.
Always check the SCFM requirement before connecting pneumatic tools.
