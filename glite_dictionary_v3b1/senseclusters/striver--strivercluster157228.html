striver

noun
person who works hard toward a goal
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈstraɪvə / (BrE)
a person who works very hard to achieve something or to improve their situation

Examples

A striver for success
As a natural striver, she never gives up, no matter how tough the challenge.
He was known as a striver who worked his way up from humble beginnings.
Many successful entrepreneurs are former strivers who never stopped believing in themselves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hard worker - a person who works diligently and puts in great effort
  • antonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
