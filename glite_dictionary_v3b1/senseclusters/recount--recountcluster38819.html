Synonyms

  • relate
    2.9 3/m
    relate often means telling a clear, personal account of events to someone, while recount focuses on giving full detail.
  • narrate
    4.0 3/m
    narrate is a structured, storytelling way to describe events for readers or listeners, while recount is more factual and detailed.
  • tell
    1.0 600/m
    tell is a general spoken way to share news or stories in everyday speech, while recount means to give full details.
  • retell
    2.8 0.5/m
    retell means to tell a story again to a new audience or with changes, while recount is to give original, detailed facts.
  • describe
    1.0 110/m
    describe focuses on the look, feel, or parts of something, while recount focuses on the sequence and facts of an event.
  • recite
    3.3 1/m
    recite means to say words or lists from memory, often without personal detail, while recount involves telling personal or event details.
