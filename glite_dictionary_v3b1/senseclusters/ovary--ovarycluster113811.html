ovary

noun
flower organ containing ovules
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.və.ri / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.vəri / (BrE)
the part of a flower that contains the ovules and develops into the fruit after fertilization

Examples

Flower ovary
In botany class, we dissected a lily to study its ovary and ovules.
The ovary is a crucial organ involved in the plant's reproductive process.
The ovary of a flower swells and becomes the fruit after pollination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pistil - the female reproductive part of a flower, including the ovary, style, and stigma
  • antonym: stamen - the pollen-producing reproductive organ of a flower
