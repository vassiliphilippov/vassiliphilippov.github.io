numismatic

adjective
about coins or currency
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnuːməˈsmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌnjuːməˈsmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to coins, paper money, or medals, especially their collection or study

Examples

Numismatic collection
He joined a numismatic society to learn more about rare coins and their history.
The museum displayed a numismatic collection featuring ancient Roman and Greek coins.
Her interest in numismatic artifacts led her to travel to many different countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monetary - relating to money or currency
  • antonym: philatelic - relating to the collection or study of stamps
