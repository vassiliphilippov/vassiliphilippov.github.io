parcel

noun
a package of wrapped items
1.1 1.97/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ërs…ôl /; / Ààp…ër.s…ôl / (AmE), / Ààp…ëÀês…ôl /; / Ààp…ëÀê.s…ôl / (BrE)
a package or object that is wrapped or boxed, especially for sending or delivery

Examples

A parcel arrives
He opened the parcel to find several books inside.
She sent a parcel to her parents for their anniversary.
The parcel arrived earlier than expected and was neatly wrapped.
Carefully wrapping the parcel, he addressed it to his friend overseas.
She was excited to receive a parcel from her grandmother in the post.
The delivery man left the parcel on the front porch.

Synonyms

  • packet

  • bundle
    2.3 8.94/m
    a bundle is several things tied or grouped together, often for carrying, while a parcel is a single wrapped item sent for delivery.
