outdrive

noun
boat propulsion unit
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌdraɪv / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌdraɪv / (BrE)
the external propulsion unit at the back of a boat that contains the propeller and gears for steering and movement

Examples

Boat outdrive repair
The mechanic replaced the damaged outdrive after the boat hit a submerged log.
Regular maintenance of the outdrive is essential for smooth boating performance.
He upgraded his boat's outdrive to improve speed and fuel efficiency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outboard - an external engine mounted on the outside of a boat's stern
  • synonym: sterndrive - a type of boat propulsion system mounted at the stern
