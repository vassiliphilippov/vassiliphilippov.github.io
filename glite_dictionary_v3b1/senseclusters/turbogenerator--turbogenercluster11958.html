turbogenerator

noun
steam turbine-driven electric generator
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.boʊˌdʒɛn.əˌreɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.bəʊˌdʒɛn.ər.eɪ.tə / (BrE)
A generator consisting of a steam turbine directly coupled to an electric generator, used to produce electric power.

Examples

A turbogenerator unit
Engineers inspected the turbogenerator to ensure it was functioning at full capacity.
Modern power plants rely on a turbogenerator to efficiently convert steam energy into electricity.
The turbogenerator was installed to supply electricity to the entire factory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generator - machine that produces electricity
  • synonym: turbine - machine that converts fluid energy into mechanical energy
