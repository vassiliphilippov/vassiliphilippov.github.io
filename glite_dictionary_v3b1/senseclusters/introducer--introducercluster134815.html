introducer

noun

5.3
Pronunciation: /ɪn.trə.ˈduː.sər/ /; / /ɪn.trə.ˈdjuː.sər/ (AmE), /ɪn.trə.ˈdjuː.sə/ /; / /ɪn.trə.ˈduː.sə/ (BrE)

Examples

Business introducer

As an introducer, she earned a commission for every customer she referred to the insurance firm.
Many banks work with an introducer network to expand their customer base.
