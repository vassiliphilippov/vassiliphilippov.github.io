Crossref

verb
to register with Crossref
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɑːs.ref/ /; / /ˈkrɑːs.rəf/ /; / /ˈkrɔːs.ref/ /; / /ˈkrɔːs.rəf/ (AmE), /ˈkrɒs.ref/ /; / /ˈkrɒs.rəf/ (BrE)
to register or link a scholarly work in the Crossref system, usually by assigning a DOI or adding metadata

Examples

To Crossref an article
The journal will Crossref all its new articles to ensure they are easily discoverable.
Authors are encouraged to Crossref their datasets for better visibility and citation tracking.
Before publication, the editor asked the team to Crossref the conference proceedings.
