testa

noun
outer seed covering in plants
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛstə / (AmE), / ˈtɛstə / (BrE)
the protective outer covering of the seed of a flowering plant

Examples

The testa of seed
Botanists examined the structure and thickness of the testa under a microscope.
In many seeds, the testa is hard and impermeable to water.
The testa protects the seed from physical damage and disease.
