hodgepodge

noun
a mixed jumble of things
8.6 5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːdʒˌpɑːdʒ /; / ˈhɑdʒˌpɑdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhɒdʒˌpɒdʒ / (BrE)
a confused or jumbled mixture of different things, ideas, or elements

Examples

A hodgepodge of leftovers
For dinner, we ended up eating a hodgepodge of leftovers from the fridge.

The museum's new exhibition is a hodgepodge of modern and classical artwork.
The report was a hodgepodge of statistics, opinions, and personal anecdotes.
