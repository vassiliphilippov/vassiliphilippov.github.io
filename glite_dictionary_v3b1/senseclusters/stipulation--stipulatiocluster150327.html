stipulation

noun
specified condition in an agreement
4.2 5.3
Pronunciation: /stɪp.jə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /stɪp.jə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a condition or requirement that is specified in an agreement or contract

Examples

Stipulation in a contract
Every rental agreement includes a stipulation about the deposit refund procedure.
The company agreed to the merger, subject to the stipulation that no jobs would be lost.
There is a stipulation in the contract that requires employees to give two weeks’ notice.
He agreed to the deal, but only with the stipulation that his expenses would be covered.
The contract included a stipulation that work must be completed within six months.
Without the necessary stipulation, the agreement would not be legally binding.

  • synonym: proviso - a condition attached to an agreement
  • synonym: term - a condition or provision in an agreement
