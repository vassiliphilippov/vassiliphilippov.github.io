rest

noun
musical symbol for a silence
8.0
Pronunciation: / rɛst / (AmE), / rɛst / (BrE)
a symbol in musical notation that indicates a period of silence of a particular length

Examples

A rest in music
Each rest has its own symbol to show how long the silence should last.
The composer inserted a rest in the third measure to create a dramatic pause.
You must count the beats during every rest to stay in time with the music.
