unconference

noun
participant-led meeting
7.3
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈkɑːn.fɜːr.əns/ /; / /ʌn.ˈkɑːn.frəns/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈkɒn.fɜː.əns/ /; / /ʌn.ˈkɒn.frəns/ (BrE)
a meeting or conference where participants create the agenda and lead discussions themselves, often informally

Examples

A tech unconference
At the unconference, attendees suggested topics and led sessions themselves.
Unlike a traditional conference, an unconference allows everyone to participate equally.
The unconference format encouraged open discussion and spontaneous collaboration among participants.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conference - a formal meeting for discussion, often with a set agenda
  • synonym: workshop - a meeting where people engage in intensive discussion or activity on a particular subject
