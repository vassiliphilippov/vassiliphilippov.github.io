positivity

noun
quality of being optimistic or encouraging
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːzəˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌpɒzɪˈtɪvəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being optimistic, encouraging, or confident that a good result will occur

Examples

Her positivity at work
Her positivity inspired the whole team to work harder and believe in their success.
Maintaining positivity in challenging situations can make a big difference in your outlook.
We appreciated the positivity he brought to every meeting, even during difficult projects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: negativity - the quality of being pessimistic or unhelpful
  • synonym: optimism - belief that things will turn out well
