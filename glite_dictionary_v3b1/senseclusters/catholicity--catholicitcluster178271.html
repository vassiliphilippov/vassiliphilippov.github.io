catholicity

noun
universality; comprehensive scope
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌkæθəˈlɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌkæθəˈlɪsəti / (BrE)
the quality of being universal or comprehensive in scope; the state of extending to or being present everywhere

Examples

Intellectual catholicity
Her proposal was praised for its catholicity, as it included ideas from every department.
The catholicity of his interests made him knowledgeable in a wide range of subjects.
The catholicity of the language ensures that it is spoken around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: universality - the quality of being universal; existing everywhere
