rocking

adjective
moving back and forth
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
moving or causing to move repeatedly backward and forward or from side to side in a gentle or rhythmic way

Examples

Rocking chair
The rocking chair creaked softly as she sat by the window reading her book.
He found the rocking motion of the boat soothing during the long journey.
The baby's crib had a gentle rocking movement that helped her fall asleep.

Related Terms

  • antonym: still - not moving; motionless
  • synonym: swaying - moving slowly or rhythmically backward and forward or from side to side
