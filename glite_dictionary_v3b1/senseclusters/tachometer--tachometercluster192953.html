tachometer

noun
instrument for measuring rotational speed
8.4 6.1
Pronunciation: / təˈkɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / təˈkɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument used for measuring the speed of rotation of a shaft or disk, typically in a motor or other machine.

Examples

Car tachometer gauge
If the tachometer reading is too high, it could indicate a problem with the engine.
She glanced at the tachometer to check the revolutions per minute before shifting gears.
The tachometer on the dashboard shows how fast the engine is running.
