bleep of surprise

noun
a sound expressing surprise
9.8
Pronunciation: / bliːp əv səˈpraɪz / (AmE), / bliːp əv səˈprʌɪz / (BrE)
a short high-pitched sound or exclamation made to express shock or surprise

Examples

A sudden bleep of surprise
A sudden bleep of surprise escaped him as the lights flickered back on.
She let out a bleep of surprise when she saw the unexpected result.
There was an audible bleep of surprise in the audience when the winner was announced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gasp of surprise - an audible intake of breath showing shock or surprise
