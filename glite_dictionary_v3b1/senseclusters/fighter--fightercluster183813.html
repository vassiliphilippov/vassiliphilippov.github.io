fighter

noun
military airplane designed to destroy enemy aircraft
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈfaɪtə / (BrE)
A high-speed military aircraft designed to destroy enemy aircraft in the air.

Examples

A fighter jet
A new generation of stealth fighters was unveiled at the air show.
Each fighter is equipped with advanced radar and missile systems.
The air force deployed a fighter to intercept the unidentified aircraft.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bomber - a military aircraft designed to drop bombs
  • synonym: interceptor - a fighter aircraft designed to intercept and destroy enemy aircraft, especially bombers
