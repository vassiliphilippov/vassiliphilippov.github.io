thuddingly

adverb
with a heavy sound
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌdɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈθʌdɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that produces or resembles a heavy, dull sound, like a thud

Examples

Drums beat thuddingly
The drums beat thuddingly throughout the night, echoing across the valley.
He walked thuddingly down the hallway in his heavy boots after a long day.
The hail struck the car roof thuddingly, making conversation difficult inside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heavily - with great weight or force
  • antonym: softly - in a gentle or quiet manner
