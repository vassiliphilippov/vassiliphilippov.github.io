signature dish

noun
specialty dish representing a chef or restaurant
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡnətʃɚ dɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnətʃə dɪʃ / (BrE)
a dish that is regarded as the most representative or distinctive specialty of a particular chef or restaurant

Examples

The signature dish
If you visit this bistro, you must try the signature dish recommended by the staff.
The chef's signature dish attracted visitors from all over the city.
Their signature dish, a slow-cooked lamb stew, is famous throughout the region.
