demonic

adjective
intensely driven
6.1
Pronunciation: / deɪˈmɑnɪk / (AmE), / diːˈmɒnɪk / (BrE)
having an intense, often frightening or obsessive energy or inspiration, as if driven by a powerful inner force

Examples

Demonic energy
The writer's daemonic creativity led him to work for days without rest.
Her daemonic determination made her unstoppable in achieving her goals.
Some described the artist's daemonic passion as both inspiring and a little frightening.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apathetic - showing or feeling no interest or enthusiasm
  • synonym: demonic - having characteristics of a demon; fiercely energetic or evil
  • synonym: inspired - filled with creative or intense energy
