bushbaby

noun
nocturnal African primate with large eyes and ears
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊʃˌbeɪbi / (AmE), / ˈbʊʃˌbeɪbi / (BrE)
An agile, long-tailed, nocturnal African primate with dense, woolly fur and large eyes and ears.

Examples

A bushbaby animal
Bushbabies are known for their distinctive cries and excellent night vision.
The bushbaby uses its strong hind legs to leap from tree to tree at night.
We spotted a bushbaby with large, shining eyes during our safari in Tanzania.
