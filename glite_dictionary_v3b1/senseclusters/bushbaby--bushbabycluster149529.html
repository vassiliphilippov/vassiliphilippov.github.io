bushbaby

noun
nocturnal African primate with large eyes and ears
8.9 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈbʊʃ.beɪ.biː/ (AmE), /ˈbʊʃ.beɪ.biː/ (BrE)
An agile, long-tailed, nocturnal African primate with dense, woolly fur and large eyes and ears.

Examples

A bushbaby animal
