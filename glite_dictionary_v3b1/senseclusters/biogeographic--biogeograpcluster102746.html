biogeographic

adjective
of or relating to biogeography
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˌdʒi.əˈɡræf.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊ.dʒi.əˈɡraf.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to the geographical distribution of living organisms and ecosystems.

Examples

Unique biogeographic regions
Australia has unique biogeographic characteristics due to its long isolation.
Researchers are mapping biogeographic boundaries to understand species dispersal.
The biogeographic regions of the world were shaped by ancient land movements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biogeographical - alternative form meaning relating to biogeography
  • synonym: geographic - relating to geography
