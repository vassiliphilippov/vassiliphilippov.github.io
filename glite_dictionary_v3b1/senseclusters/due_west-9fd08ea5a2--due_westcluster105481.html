due west

noun
the direction directly west
1.2 0.41/m
Pronunciation: / du Ààw…õst / (AmE), / djuÀê Ààw…õst / (BrE)
the direction corresponding to 270 degrees on a compass; directly west.

Examples

Due west direction
The compass needle pointed due west as they walked along the desert trail.
The hikers followed the path due west until they reached the old lighthouse.
We set out at dawn, keeping the boat headed due west across the lake.
