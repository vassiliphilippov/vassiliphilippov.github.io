maddeningly

adverb
extremely annoyingly
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmædənɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈmædənɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely annoying or frustrating

Examples

Maddeningly slow traffic
The instructions were maddeningly vague, making it hard to assemble the furniture.
He arrived maddeningly late to every meeting, frustrating his colleagues.
The computer froze maddeningly just as she was about to save her work.
