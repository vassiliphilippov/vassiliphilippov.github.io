whortleberry

noun
European blueberry-like shrub and its berries
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhwɜr.təlˌbɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈwɜː.tl̩ˌbɛr.i / (BrE)
A small, erect European shrub of the heath family with solitary flowers and blue-black edible berries, similar to a blueberry.

Examples

Whortleberry bush
Many birds feed on whortleberries during the harvest season.
She picked a handful of whortleberries to add to her pie.
The woods were filled with ripe whortleberries in late summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bilberry - a similar European shrub producing edible blue-black berries
  • synonym: blueberry - a North American shrub with sweet blue berries
