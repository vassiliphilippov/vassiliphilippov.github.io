whortleberry

noun
European blueberry-like shrub and its berries
9.3 8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈwɑːr.təl.be.rɪ/ /; / /ˈwɔːr.təl.be.rɪ/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.təl.be.rɪ/ /; / /ˈwɜː.təl.be.rɪ/ (BrE)
