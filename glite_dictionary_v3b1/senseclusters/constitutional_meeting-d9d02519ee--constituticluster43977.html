constitutional meeting

noun
meeting to address or amend a constitution
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑn.stəˈtuː.ʃən.əl ˈmiː.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.stɪˈtjuː.ʃən.əl ˈmiː.tɪŋ / (BrE)
a formal meeting held to discuss, create, or amend a constitution

Examples

A constitutional meeting
A constitutional meeting is required before any amendments can be made to the bylaws.
The delegates gathered for the constitutional meeting to propose significant reforms.
The organization scheduled a constitutional meeting to draft their new governing document.

Related Terms

  • synonym: constitutional convention - a formal gathering to draft or revise a constitution
