convulsive

adjective
characterized by sudden, involuntary muscle movements
3.7
Pronunciation: / kənˈvʌl.sɪv / (AmE), / kənˈvʌl.sɪv / (BrE)
(of movements or actions) characterized by sudden, violent, and involuntary muscle contractions; resembling or causing convulsions.

Examples

Convulsive movements
He was taken to the hospital after a convulsive fit left him unable to stand.
Her laughter became convulsive, shaking her entire body uncontrollably.
The patient experienced convulsive movements after the medication was administered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: involuntary - not under conscious control
  • synonym: spasmodic - occurring in sudden brief bursts, like a spasm
