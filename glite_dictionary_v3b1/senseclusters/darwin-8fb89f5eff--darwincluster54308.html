Darwin

proper noun
English naturalist; theory of evolution
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑr.wɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɑː.wɪn / (BrE)
Charles Darwin (1809–1882), an English naturalist known for developing the theory of evolution by natural selection.
