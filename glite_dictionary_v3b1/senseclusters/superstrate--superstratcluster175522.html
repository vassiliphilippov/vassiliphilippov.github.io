superstrate

noun
dominant invading language influencing another
9.5 9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsuː.pər.streɪt/ (AmE), /ˈsuː.pə.streɪt/ (BrE)
a layer or stratum that is placed above another, especially a language imposed on a community that influences its original language
