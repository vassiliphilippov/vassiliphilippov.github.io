mudslinging

noun
unfair accusations
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌdˌslɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmʌdˌslɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making unfair or malicious accusations or comments about someone, especially in politics

Examples

Political mudslinging
The election campaign was filled with mudslinging and personal attacks.
Instead of discussing real issues, the candidates resorted to mudslinging during the debate.
Many voters are tired of the constant mudslinging in politics these days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defamation - the act of damaging someone's reputation by false statements
  • antonym: praise - the act of expressing approval or admiration
  • synonym: slander - the action of making false spoken statements that damage someone's reputation
