yakka

noun
Australian grass-tree
Pronunciation: / ˈjækə / (BrE)
an Australian grass-tree of the genus Xanthorrhoea, with a thick trunk and long, stiff leaves

Examples

A yakka plant
The yakka is a common sight in the dry regions of South Australia.
Bushwalkers admired the ancient yakka growing along the rocky hillside.
Fire can stimulate new growth in a yakka after a long drought.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grass tree - an Australian plant with a woody trunk and long leaves
