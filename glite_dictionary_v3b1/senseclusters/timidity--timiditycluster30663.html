timidity

noun
shyness or lack of courage
5.1 4.7
Pronunciation: /tə.ˈmɪ.dɪ.tiː/ /; / /tɪ.ˈmɪ.dɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /tɪ.ˈmɪ.dɪ.tiː/ /; / /tə.ˈmɪ.dɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
a lack of courage or confidence; shyness or fearfulness in new or challenging situations

Examples

Natural timidity
Despite his natural timidity, he decided to introduce himself to the group.
Her timidity kept her from volunteering for the new project at work.
The timidity of the kitten made it hide under the couch during the party.
He struggled to overcome his timidity when meeting new people.
Her timidity prevented her from speaking up during the meeting.
The child's timidity faded as he grew more comfortable in his new environment.
