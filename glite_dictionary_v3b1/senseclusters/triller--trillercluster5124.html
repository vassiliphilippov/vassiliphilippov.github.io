triller

noun
small trilling songbird
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪlər / (AmE), / ˈtrɪlə / (BrE)
a small insect-eating songbird of the genus Lalage, found in Australasia and Asia, known for its trilling call

Examples

A triller bird
The ornithologist identified the noisy triller perched in the mangrove trees near the shore.
A triller sang its distinctive song from the top of the eucalyptus tree.
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a rare triller during their trip to northern Australia.
