genotypically

adverb
by genetic makeup
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːnəˈtɪpɪkli / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːnəˈtɪpɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the genetic makeup or genotype of an organism

Examples

To be genotypically similar
The two bacteria are genotypically similar but show different resistance to antibiotics.
Although the animals look different, they are genotypically almost identical.
Scientists compared the plants genotypically to understand their inherited traits.

Related Terms

  • antonym: phenotypically - in a way that relates to the observable characteristics of an organism
