disjointed

adjective
lacking logical or orderly connection
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈdʒɔɪntɪd / (AmE), / dɪsˈdʒɔɪntɪd / (BrE)
lacking a clear, logical, or orderly connection; not well organized or coherent

Examples

Disjointed speech
He presented his ideas in a disjointed manner, jumping from one topic to another.
Her explanation was so disjointed that no one could follow her argument.
The film's plot felt disjointed and confusing, making it hard to understand the story.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coherent - logically connected and consistent
  • synonym: disconnected - lacking a logical or meaningful connection
