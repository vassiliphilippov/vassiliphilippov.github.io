Synonyms

  • assist
    1.0 55/m
    assist is used for helping with a task or serving someone, while help is a general act of making things easier.
  • aid
    1.0 21/m
    aid is often formal support or help given in serious situations, while help is a general, everyday way to make things easier.
  • facilitate
    4.9 37/m
    facilitate means to make a process or action run more smoothly, while help covers direct or simple assistance.
