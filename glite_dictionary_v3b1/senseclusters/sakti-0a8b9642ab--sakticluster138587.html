Sakti

noun
Hindu female divine principle; consort of Siva
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːkti / (AmE), / ˈʃæk.ti / (BrE)
the female divine principle in Hinduism, personified as the consort of Siva and a benevolent form of Devi

Examples

Sakti power
Devotees celebrate the power of Sakti during major festivals like Navaratri.
In Hindu belief, Sakti is worshipped as the embodiment of creative energy.
The goddess Sakti is revered in many temples across India.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Devi - the supreme goddess in Hinduism, representing the female aspect of the divine
