gold-tail moth

noun
European moth with yellow tail tuft
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊld teɪl mɑθ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊld teɪl mɒθ / (BrE)
a European moth with a white furry body and a yellow tuft at the end of its tail

Examples

The gold-tail moth
Gardeners often spot a gold-tail moth resting on leaves during the summer months.
She took a photograph of a gold-tail moth for her insect collection.
The gold-tail moth is easily identified by its distinctive yellow tail tuft.
