Great Barrier Reef

proper noun
largest coral reef off Australia
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt ˈbæriər riːf / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈbæriə riːf / (BrE)
the world's largest coral reef system, located in the Coral Sea off the northeastern coast of Australia
