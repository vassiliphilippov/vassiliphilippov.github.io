biometric

noun
identifying body feature
5.1
Pronunciation: /baɪ.ə.ˈme.trɪk/ /; / /baɪ.oʊ.ˈme.trɪk/ (AmE), /baɪ.ə.ˈme.trɪk/ /; / /baɪ.əʊ.ˈme.trɪk/ (BrE)
a measurable physical or behavioral characteristic, such as a fingerprint or voice pattern, used to identify a person

Examples

A biometric scan
The security system scans your biometric to allow access to the building.
Each employee must register a unique biometric such as a fingerprint or iris scan.
Modern smartphones often use a biometric for unlocking instead of a password.

Related Terms

  • antonym: password - a secret word or phrase used to access something
