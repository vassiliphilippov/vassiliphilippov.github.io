putting to death

noun
termination of a life
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊt̬ɪŋ tə dɛθ / (AmE), / ˈpʊtɪŋ tə dɛθ / (BrE)
the act of causing someone or something to die, especially as a form of punishment or mercy.

Examples

The putting to death of animals
Many animal shelters face ethical questions regarding the putting to death of unadopted pets.
The law at that time allowed the putting to death of criminals convicted of certain crimes.
Throughout history, societies have developed various methods for the putting to death of individuals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: euthanasia - the act of killing someone painlessly, especially to relieve suffering
  • synonym: execution - the carrying out of a death sentence
