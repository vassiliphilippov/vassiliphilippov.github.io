densitometry

noun
measurement of optical density
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛn.səˈtɑː.mə.tɹi / (AmE), / ˌdɛn.sɪˈtɒm.ə.tri / (BrE)
the measurement of the optical density of a material, typically by passing light through it and measuring transmission
