particularist

noun
person favoring specifics
Pronunciation: / pərˈtɪkjələrɪst / (AmE), / pəˈtɪkjʊlərɪst / (BrE)
a person who focuses on specific cases or individual circumstances rather than general rules or principles

Examples

A particularist thinker
As a particularist, she prefers to consider each situation on its own merits.
The judge was known as a particularist who valued the details of every case.
In debates, a particularist often challenges broad generalizations with specific examples.

Related Terms

  • synonym: individualist - a person who values individual circumstances or independence
  • antonym: universalist - a person who applies general rules or principles to all cases
