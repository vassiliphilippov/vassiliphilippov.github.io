bird's nest fern

noun
a tropical epiphytic fern with nest-like fronds
4.5
Pronunciation: / bɝdz nɛst fɝn / (AmE), / bɜːdz nɛst fɜːn / (BrE)
a tropical Old World or Australian epiphytic fern (Asplenium nidus) that often grows in tree crotches and has broad, arching fronds forming a nest-like cluster
