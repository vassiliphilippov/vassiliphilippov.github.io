kerplunk

verb
to fall with a thud
9.1
Pronunciation: / kərˈplʌŋk / (AmE), / kəˈplʌŋk / (BrE)
to fall or cause something to fall with a sudden heavy splash or thud

Examples

To kerplunk on the floor
The book slipped from her hands and kerplunked onto the wooden floor.
He dropped the stone into the well, and it kerplunked loudly at the bottom.
Please don't kerplunk your backpack on the table; it might break something.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plop - to fall or drop with a soft sound
  • synonym: thud - to fall or hit with a dull sound
