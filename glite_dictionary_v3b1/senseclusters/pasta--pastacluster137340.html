pasta

noun
Italian noodle-based dish
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːstə /; / ˈpɑstə / (AmE), / ˈpɑːstə /; / ˈpæstə / (BrE)
a type of food made from wheat flour and water or eggs, formed into various shapes (such as spaghetti or macaroni), usually boiled and often served with a sauce

Examples

Boiled pasta dish
Her favorite pasta is spaghetti with meatballs.
I cooked some pasta for dinner with tomato sauce and cheese.
Many Italian restaurants offer a wide selection of pasta dishes.
She prefers whole wheat pasta because it's healthier than the regular kind.
The restaurant serves a wide variety of pasta dishes, including lasagna and ravioli.
We made homemade pasta for dinner last night, and it tasted delicious.
