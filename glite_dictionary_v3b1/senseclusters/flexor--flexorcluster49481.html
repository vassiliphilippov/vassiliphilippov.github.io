flexor

noun
muscle that bends a joint
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛk.sər / (AmE), / ˈflɛk.sə / (BrE)
A muscle that contracts to bend a joint in the body.

Examples

The flexor muscle
After an injury, his finger flexor was too weak to grip objects properly.
Physical therapy helped strengthen the flexor muscles in her legs.
The biceps brachii is an important flexor of the elbow joint.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extensor - a muscle whose contraction straightens a joint
