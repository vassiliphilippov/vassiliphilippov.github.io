musculus sphincter ductus pancreatici

noun
muscle controlling main pancreatic duct
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌs.kjə.ləs ˈsfɪŋk.tər ˈdʌk.təs ˌpæn.krɪˈæ.tɪ.saɪ / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.kjʊ.ləs ˈsfɪŋk.tə ˈdʌk.təs ˌpæn.krɪˈæ.tɪ.saɪ / (BrE)
the smooth muscle sphincter that surrounds and controls the opening of the main pancreatic duct

Examples

The musculus sphincter ductus pancreatici muscle
A dysfunction of the musculus sphincter ductus pancreatici can lead to digestive problems or pancreatitis.
Surgeons must be careful not to damage the musculus sphincter ductus pancreatici during pancreatic procedures.
The musculus sphincter ductus pancreatici regulates the flow of pancreatic secretions into the duodenum.
