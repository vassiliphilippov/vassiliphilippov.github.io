rabbit-eared bandicoot

noun
small Australian marsupial with rabbit-like ears
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈræbɪt ɪrd ˈbændɪˌkut / (AmE), / ˈræbɪt ɪəd ˈbændɪˌkuːt / (BrE)
a small Australian marsupial with long, leathery ears resembling those of a rabbit
