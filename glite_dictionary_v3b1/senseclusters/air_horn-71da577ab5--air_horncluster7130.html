air horn

noun
loud pneumatic horn for signaling
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr hɔrn / (AmE), / ˈeə hɔːn / (BrE)
A horn that produces a loud sound using compressed air, commonly used for signaling, especially on vehicles and ships.

Examples

Blow an air horn
He startled everyone by suddenly blowing an air horn during the celebration.
Many trucks are equipped with a loud air horn for emergencies.
The ship blasted its air horn as it entered the harbor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: klaxon - an electric horn that produces a loud warning sound
  • synonym: siren - a device that makes a loud warning sound
