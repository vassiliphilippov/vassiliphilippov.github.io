gooseneck loosestrife

noun
herbaceous plant with arching white flower spikes
5.6 8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈguːs.nek ˈluːs.traɪf/ /; / /ˈguːs.nek ˈluːs.straɪf/ (AmE), /ˈguːs.nek ˈluːs.traɪf/ /; / /ˈguːs.nek ˈluːs.straɪf/ (BrE)
a perennial herbaceous plant (Lysimachia clethroides) with arching spikes of white flowers, named for its distinctive curved flower spikes resembling a goose’s neck

Examples

Gooseneck loosestrife plant
Gooseneck loosestrife thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is popular in perennial gardens.
She planted gooseneck loosestrife along the border for its unique, swan-like blooms.
The arching flower spikes of gooseneck loosestrife give the plant its common name.
