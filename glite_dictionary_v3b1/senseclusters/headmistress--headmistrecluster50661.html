headmistress

noun
female head teacher of a school
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌmɪstrəs / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌmɪstrɪs / (BrE)
a woman who is the head teacher or principal of a school, especially in a private or girls' school

Examples

The headmistress of school
After being appointed headmistress, she became a role model for the students and staff.
The new headmistress introduced several reforms to improve discipline in the school.
The parents were invited to meet the headmistress during the open evening.
