player

noun
person who dates several partners at once
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who pursues a number of different romantic or sexual partners at the same time, often without serious intentions

Examples

A smooth player
After his last breakup, he decided he didn't want to be a player anymore.
Everyone knows he's a player, so I wouldn't take him too seriously.
She warned me that Mark had a reputation for being a player at college.
