at length

adverb
in detail; for a long time
Pronunciation: / æt lɛŋθ / (AmE), / ət lɛŋθ / (BrE)
for a long time; in great detail or thoroughly, especially in speech or writing.

Examples

Talk at length
He described the events at length in his letter.
She spoke at length about her experiences living abroad.
The report was discussed at length during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lengthily - in a lengthy or verbose manner
