tendril

noun
slender climbing plant stem for support
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛn.drəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.drɪl / (BrE)
a slender, thread-like stem or part of a climbing plant that attaches itself to objects for support

Examples

A tendril of plant
She gently unwound a tendril from the trellis to avoid damaging the plant.
The cucumber plant sent out a long tendril searching for support.
The ivy used a tendril to grip the garden fence tightly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vine - a plant with a long, thin, flexible stem that climbs or trails
