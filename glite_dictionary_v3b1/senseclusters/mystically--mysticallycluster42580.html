mystically

adverb
in a mystical or spiritual manner
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˈmɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that relates to mysticism, spiritual experience, or supernatural insight.

Examples

To mystically see visions
She gazed mystically into the crystal ball, lost in her visions.
The dancers moved mystically, creating an atmosphere of otherworldliness.
The priest spoke mystically about the secrets of enlightenment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mystical - having a spiritual meaning that transcends human understanding
