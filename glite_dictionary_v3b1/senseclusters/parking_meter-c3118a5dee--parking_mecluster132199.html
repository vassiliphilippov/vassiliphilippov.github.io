parking meter

noun
coin-operated device for parking time
2.6 2.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑːr.kɪŋ ˈmiː.tər/ (AmE), /ˈpɑː.kɪŋ ˈmiː.tə/ (BrE)
a device next to a parking space, operated by coins, cards, or electronically, used to collect payment and indicate how long a vehicle may legally park there

Examples

A parking meter machine
All the parking meters on this street now accept credit cards.
Make sure to put enough coins in the parking meter or you'll get a ticket.
There was already a car at the parking meter when she arrived.
