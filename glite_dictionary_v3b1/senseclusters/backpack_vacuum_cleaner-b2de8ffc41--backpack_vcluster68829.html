backpack vacuum cleaner

noun
vacuum cleaner worn on the back
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌpæk ˈvæk.juːm ˈkliː.nər / (AmE), / ˈbæk.pæk ˈvæk.juːm ˈkliː.nə / (BrE)
