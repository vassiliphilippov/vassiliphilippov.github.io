untheatrical

adjective
not suited to theater; lacking theatrical quality
9.9 5.5
Pronunciation: /ʌn.θiː.ˈæ.trɪ.kəl/ /; / /ʌn.θɪ.ˈæ.trɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.θiː.ˈæ.trɪ.kəl/ /; / /ʌn.θɪ.ˈæ.trɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
not suited to or characteristic of the stage or theater; lacking the qualities typical of theatrical performances

Examples
