untheatrical

adjective
not suited to theater; lacking theatrical quality
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnθiˈætrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnθɪˈætrɪkəl / (BrE)
not suited to or characteristic of the stage or theater; lacking the qualities typical of theatrical performances

Examples

An untheatrical style
Despite her talent, her acting style was considered too untheatrical for the demanding director.
He gave a speech in an untheatrical manner, avoiding any dramatic flair or gestures.
The film impressed critics with its subtle, untheatrical approach to storytelling.
