pedogenesis

noun
soil formation process
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛdəˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌpiːdəˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (BrE)
the process by which soil is formed and develops through physical, chemical, and biological factors

Examples

Soil pedogenesis
Scientists study pedogenesis to understand how different soils develop over time.
Climate, vegetation, and topography all influence the process of pedogenesis.
Understanding pedogenesis is essential for sustainable land management and agriculture.
