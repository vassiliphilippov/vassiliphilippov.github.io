Convallariaceae

noun
former plant family (lily-of-the-valley, etc.)
9.9
Pronunciation: / kənˌvælər.iˈeɪ.siˌi /; / kənˌvælərɪˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / kɒnˌvæl.ər.iˈeɪ.siː.iː /; / kɒnˌvælərɪˈeɪsɪˌiː / (BrE)
a plant family (including lily-of-the-valley and related plants) that was sometimes considered separate from the Liliaceae family, but is now usually included in it
