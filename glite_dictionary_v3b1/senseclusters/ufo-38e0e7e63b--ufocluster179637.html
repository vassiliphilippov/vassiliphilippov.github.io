UFO

noun
unidentified flying object
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌjuː ɛf ˈoʊ / (AmE), / ˌjuː ɛf ˈəʊ / (BrE)
an unidentified flying object, especially one thought to be of extraterrestrial origin

Examples

A UFO sighting
Scientists remain skeptical about the existence of extraterrestrial life despite numerous UFO sightings.
Several eyewitnesses reported seeing a UFO hovering above the forest last night.
The military pilot claimed to have spotted a UFO during his routine flight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flying saucer - an alleged type of UFO, typically described as disk-shaped
