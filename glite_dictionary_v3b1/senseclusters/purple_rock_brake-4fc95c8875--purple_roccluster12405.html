purple rock brake

noun
North American fern with dark glossy stems
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpəl rɑk breɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl rɒk breɪk / (BrE)
A small, shallowly creeping North American fern, typically found on cliffs or walls, with dark, glossy leaf stalks.
