toupeed

adjective
wearing a toupee or hairpiece
Pronunciation: / tuˈpid / (AmE), / tuːˈpiːd / (BrE)
wearing a toupee; having a small hairpiece to cover partial baldness.

Examples

Toupeed man
His toupeed head was hardly distinguishable from a natural hairline under the stage lights.
Many comedians have poked fun at the obvious toupeed news anchor.
The toupeed actor sat nervously in the makeup chair before his scene.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bald - having little or no hair on the scalp
