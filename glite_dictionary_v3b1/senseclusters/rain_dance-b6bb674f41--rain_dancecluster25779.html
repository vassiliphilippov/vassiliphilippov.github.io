rain dance

noun
ritual dance to bring rain

A ritual dance performed to invoke rain, especially among certain indigenous peoples.

Examples

A rain dance ritual
Anthropologists have studied the rain dance as part of traditional agricultural societies' rituals.
Many Native American tribes performed the rain dance in hope of ending droughts.
