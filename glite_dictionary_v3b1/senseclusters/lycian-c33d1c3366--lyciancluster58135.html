Lycian

adjective
of ancient Lycia
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪsiən / (AmE), / ˈlɪsiən / (BrE)
relating to Lycia, an ancient region in what is now southwestern Turkey, or its people, language, or culture

Examples

Lycian tombs
The Lycian tombs carved into the cliffs are a popular tourist attraction.

The exhibition featured Lycian jewelry and pottery from archaeological sites.
