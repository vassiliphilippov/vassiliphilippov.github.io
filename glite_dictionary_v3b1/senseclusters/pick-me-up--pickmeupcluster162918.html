pick-me-up

noun
energetic or restorative drink
3.2 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪk.miː.ʌp/ /; / /ˈpɪk.mə.ʌp/ (AmE), /ˈpɪk.miː.ʌp/ /; / /ˈpɪk.mə.ʌp/ (BrE)
a drink or other substance (or sometimes an activity) that makes you feel more lively, refreshed, or energetic, especially when you are tired or feeling low
