unconscious

adjective
not awake or aware; insensible
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈkɑːn.ʃəs/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈkɒn.ʃəs/ (BrE)
not awake or aware of and responding to one's environment; lacking consciousness, as if asleep or dead

Examples

Unconscious patient
During surgery, patients are kept unconscious with anesthesia.
She was found unconscious by the side of the road after the accident.
The boxer was knocked unconscious in the final round of the match.
