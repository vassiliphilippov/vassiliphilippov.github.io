extinguishable

adjective
able to be put out or ended
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbəl / (BrE)
Able to be put out, destroyed, or brought to an end.

Examples

An extinguishable fire
Some types of hope are fragile and extinguishable in difficult times.
The chemical foam made the flames quickly extinguishable by the firefighters.
The fire in the wastebasket was easily extinguishable with just a cup of water.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inextinguishable - impossible to extinguish or put out
