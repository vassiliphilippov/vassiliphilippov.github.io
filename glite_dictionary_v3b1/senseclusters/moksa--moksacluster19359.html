moksa

noun
Hinduism: liberation from rebirth
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊkʃə / (AmE), / ˈməʊkʃə / (BrE)
(in Hinduism) freedom from the cycle of birth and death; spiritual liberation.

Examples

Achieving moksa
Achieving moksa is considered the ultimate goal of human existence in Hindu belief.
Many Hindus seek to attain moksa through spiritual practice and self-realization.
The concept of moksa is central to several Indian philosophies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nirvana - Buddhist term for spiritual liberation and release from the cycle of rebirth
