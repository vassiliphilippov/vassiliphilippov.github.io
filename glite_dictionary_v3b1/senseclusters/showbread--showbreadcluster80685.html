showbread

noun
Temple offering bread
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌbrɛd / (BrE)
bread placed on a table in the ancient Jewish Temple as an offering to God

Examples

The showbread on table
The priest arranged the showbread on the sacred table in the Temple.
Each week, fresh showbread was placed before the altar as part of the ritual.
The tradition of showbread is described in ancient religious texts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offering - something presented to a deity as a religious act
  • synonym: sacrifice - an act of giving up something valuable for a religious purpose
