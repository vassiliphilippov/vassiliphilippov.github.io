Synonyms

    impair makes something weaker or less able to work, while damage causes more general harm or loss to it.
  • harm
    1.0 27/m

  • spoil
    2.8 2.79/m
    spoil ruins food, fun, or small things so they are not enjoyable, while damage covers any harm that lowers value or function.
  • injure
    1.6 3.05/m
