Synonyms

  • scenarist
    8.3 0.03/m
    scenarist is a rare, formal name for someone who writes screenplays for film or television, while screenwriter is the common modern term.
  • scriptwriter
    4.0 0.31/m
    scriptwriter writes scripts for plays, films, or broadcast shows, while screenwriter refers mainly to film and television screenplays.
  • film writer
    3.1 0.08/m
    film writer writes scripts mainly for feature films, while screenwriter often covers both film and television screenplay writing.
