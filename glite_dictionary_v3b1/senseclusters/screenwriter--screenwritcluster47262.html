screenwriter

noun
person who writes screenplays
Pronunciation: / ˈskrinˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈskriːnˌraɪtə / (BrE)
a person who writes screenplays for films or television

Examples

A screenwriter writes scripts
Many screenwriters start their careers by writing short films.
She dreams of becoming a successful screenwriter in Hollywood.
The film's screenwriter won an award for best original screenplay.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scriptwriter - person who writes scripts, especially for radio, television, or film
