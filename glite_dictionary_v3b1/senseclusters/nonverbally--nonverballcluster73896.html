nonverbally

adverb
in a nonverbal manner

In a manner that does not involve the use of words; by means other than speech or writing.

Examples

To communicate nonverbally
During the meeting, she signaled her agreement nonverbally by nodding.
Some animals communicate nonverbally using body language and sounds.
The children were able to express their excitement nonverbally through gestures and facial expressions.

Synonyms

  • nonlexically
    9.9 0.00/m

  • wordlessly
    3.9 1.19/m
    wordlessly means doing something without speaking or using words, often in silence, while nonverbally covers other nonverbal actions like gestures.
