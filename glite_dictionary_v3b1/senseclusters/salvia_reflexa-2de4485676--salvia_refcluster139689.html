Salvia reflexa

noun
a sage species with violet-blue flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈsæl.vi.ə rɪˈflɛk.sə / (AmE), / ˈsal.vɪ.ə rɪˈflɛk.sə / (BrE)
A species of sage native to western North America and Central America, known for its violet-blue flowers and common use in cultivation.
