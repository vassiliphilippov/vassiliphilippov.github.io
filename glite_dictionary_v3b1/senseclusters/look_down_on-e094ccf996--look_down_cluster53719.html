look down on

verb
regard with contempt
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /lʊk daʊn ən/ /; / /lʊk daʊn ɑːn/ (AmE), /lʊk daʊn ən/ /; / /lʊk daʊn ɒn/ (BrE)
