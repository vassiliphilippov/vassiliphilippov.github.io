look down on

verb
regard with contempt
1.0
Pronunciation: / lʊk daʊn ɑn /; / lʊk ˈdaʊn ɑn / (AmE), / lʊk daʊn ɒn /; / lʊk ˈdaʊn ɒn / (BrE)
to think you are better than someone and treat them with contempt or as inferior
