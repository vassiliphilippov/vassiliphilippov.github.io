smell-o-vision

noun
technology for experiencing scents in media
Pronunciation: / smɛl ə ˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / smɛl ə ˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
a fictional or speculative technology that allows viewers to experience scents while watching visual media
