administratively

adverb
in an administrative manner
1.0 5.0
Pronunciation: /æd.mɪ.nɪ.ˈstreɪ.tɪv.liː/ /; / /ə.ˈmɪn.ɪ.strə.tɪv.liː/ (AmE), /ə.ˈmɪn.ɪ.strə.tɪv.liː/ /; / /æd.mɪ.nɪ.ˈstreɪ.tɪv.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that relates to the running of a business, organization, or government; by or for an administrator.

Examples

Handled administratively by managers
Her transfer to another department was handled administratively rather than through formal channels.
Many changes in policy are made administratively, with little input from staff.
The new guidelines were implemented administratively without requiring a board meeting.
