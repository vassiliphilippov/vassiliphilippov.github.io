arroz con pollo

noun
Spanish rice and chicken dish
Pronunciation: / əˌroʊs kɔn ˈpoʊjoʊ / (AmE), / əˌrɒs kɒn ˈpɒljəʊ / (BrE)
A Spanish or Latin American dish of rice and chicken cooked together and highly seasoned, often with saffron.
