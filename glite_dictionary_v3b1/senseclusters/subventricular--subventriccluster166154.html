subventricular

adjective
beneath a ventricle
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbvɛnˈtrɪkjələr / (AmE), / ˌsʌbvɛnˈtrɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
located or occurring beneath a ventricle, especially in the brain

Examples

The subventricular zone
The subventricular zone is a key area for neural stem cell activity in the brain.
Researchers observed increased cell growth in the subventricular region after the treatment.
Damage to subventricular tissue can affect brain development and function.
