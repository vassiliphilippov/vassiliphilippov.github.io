offworld

adverb
outside Earth
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfˌwɜrld / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌwɜːld / (BrE)
in or to a place outside the planet Earth, especially in space or on another planet

Examples

Travel offworld
The scientists sent a probe offworld to collect samples from Mars.
Many science fiction stories imagine humans living offworld in distant colonies.
After years of training, she was finally selected for an offworld assignment.

Related Terms
