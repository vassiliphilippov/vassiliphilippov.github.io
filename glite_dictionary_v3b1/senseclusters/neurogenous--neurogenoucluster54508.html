neurogenous

adjective
from nervous tissue
9.9
Pronunciation: / nʊˈrɑdʒənəs / (AmE), / njʊəˈrɒdʒɪnəs / (BrE)
originating from or caused by nervous tissue or the nervous system

Examples

Neurogenous disorder
The patient was diagnosed with a neurogenous disorder that affected muscle function.
Some skin conditions are considered neurogenous because they are triggered by nerve activity.
Researchers studied the neurogenous origins of certain chronic pain syndromes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neurogenic - arising from or produced by the nervous system
