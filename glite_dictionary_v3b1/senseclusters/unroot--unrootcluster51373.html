unroot

verb
to remove root access
8.1 0.00/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàruÀêt / (AmE), /  ånÀàruÀêt / (BrE)
to remove administrator (root) access or superuser privileges from a computer, phone, or other device

Examples

To unroot a phone
You should unroot your phone before sending it in for repairs to avoid warranty issues.
After deciding to sell his tablet, he chose to unroot it and restore the original software.
Some apps will not work unless you unroot your device first.
