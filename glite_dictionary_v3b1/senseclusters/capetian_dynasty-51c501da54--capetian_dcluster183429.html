Capetian dynasty

noun
Frankish royal family ruling France, 987–1328
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈpiːʃən ˈdaɪnəsti / (AmE), / kəˈpiːʃən ˈdɪnəsti / (BrE)
the Frankish royal family founded by Hugh Capet that ruled France from 987 to 1328

Examples

The Capetian dynasty established a foundation for the modern French state.
The end of the Capetian dynasty led to a succession crisis in France.
