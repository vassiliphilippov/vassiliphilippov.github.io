Turnerian

adjective
of Victor Turner's theories
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɜrˈnɪriən / (AmE), / tɜːˈnɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the theories or methods of Victor Turner, especially concerning ritual and liminality in anthropology

Examples

Turnerian theory of ritual
The professor's analysis of ritual was distinctly Turnerian in its focus on liminality.
Many scholars have adopted a Turnerian approach to studying social transformation.
Her research applies Turnerian concepts to modern community festivals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropological - relating to the study of humans and societies
