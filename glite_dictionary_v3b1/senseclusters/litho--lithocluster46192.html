litho

x
combining form: stone
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪθoʊ / (AmE), / ˈlɪθəʊ / (BrE)
a combining form meaning 'stone' or 'rock', used in scientific and technical compound words

Examples

Litho- prefix
The prefix litho is found in words like 'lithography' and 'lithosphere'.
In geology, litho is used to refer to rocks or stone layers.
Many scientific terms use litho to indicate a connection to stone.
