congenially

adverb
in a friendly or pleasant way
2.4
Pronunciation: / kənˈdʒiː.ni.əli / (AmE), / kənˈdʒiː.ni.əli / (BrE)
In a pleasant, friendly, or agreeable manner.

Examples

To congenially chat
He chatted congenially with his neighbors at the community event.
She greeted each new guest congenially, putting everyone at ease.
The two colleagues worked congenially on their joint project.
