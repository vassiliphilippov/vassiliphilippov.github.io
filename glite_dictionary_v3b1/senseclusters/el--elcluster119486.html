el

noun
elevated railway system
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛl / (AmE), / ɛl / (BrE)
a railway powered by electricity that runs on tracks elevated above street level, especially in a city

Examples

Chicago el train
Chicago is famous for its el, which circles the city center.
