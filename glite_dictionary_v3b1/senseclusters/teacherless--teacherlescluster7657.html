teacherless

adjective
without a teacher
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtiː.tʃər.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈtiː.tʃə.ləs/ (BrE)
without a teacher; done or existing without the presence or guidance of a teacher

Examples

The course is completely teacherless, allowing students to learn at their own pace.
