teacherless

adjective
without a teacher
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːtʃərləs / (AmE), / ˈtiːtʃələs / (BrE)
without a teacher; done or existing without the presence or guidance of a teacher

Examples

Teacherless course
The course is completely teacherless, allowing students to learn at their own pace.
Many modern language apps offer a teacherless approach to learning.
He preferred a teacherless environment where he could study independently.
The students worked quietly in a teacherless classroom until the substitute arrived.
A teacherless study group can encourage students to take more responsibility for their learning.
During the teacherless period, the class discussed the assignment among themselves.

Related Terms

  • antonym: instructor-led - guided or taught by a teacher or instructor
  • synonym: self-taught - having learned something without a teacher
  • antonym: supervised - watched over or managed by someone in authority
  • synonym: unattended - not supervised or watched over
