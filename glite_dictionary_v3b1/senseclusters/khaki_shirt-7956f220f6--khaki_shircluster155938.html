khaki shirt

noun
shirt made of khaki fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈkæki ʃɝt / (AmE), / ˈkɑːki ʃɜːt / (BrE)
A shirt made from khaki-colored, often durable cotton fabric, typically associated with military, casual, or uniform wear.

Examples

A khaki shirt
During the summer, a lightweight khaki shirt is comfortable for hiking trips.
He paired his khaki shirt with blue jeans for a laid-back look.
The company uniform includes a khaki shirt with matching trousers.
