incense tree

noun
tree yielding aromatic resin for incense
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌsɛns ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈɪn.sens ˌtriː / (BrE)
any of various tropical trees in the family Burseraceae that produce fragrant resins or gums burned as incense

Examples

A incense tree species
Botanists have identified several species of incense trees in Southeast Asia.
The resin from the incense tree is valued in religious ceremonies.
Villagers collected sap from the incense tree to sell at the local market.
