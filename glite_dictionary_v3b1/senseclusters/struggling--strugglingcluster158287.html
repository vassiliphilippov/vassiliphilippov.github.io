struggling

adjective
trying to overcome hardship or lack of success
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstrʌɡəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstrʌɡlɪŋ / (BrE)
trying hard to overcome difficulties, especially lack of money, success, or recognition

Examples
