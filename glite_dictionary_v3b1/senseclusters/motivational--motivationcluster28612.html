motivational

adjective
relating to motivation or encouragement
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmoʊ.tɪˈveɪ.ʃən.əl / (AmE), / ˌməʊ.tɪˈveɪ.ʃən.əl / (BrE)
providing or relating to the reason or encouragement for someone to do something; connected with motivation

Examples

Motivational speech
He listened to a motivational speech that inspired him to change his habits.
Many people buy motivational books to help them achieve their goals.
The coach gave a motivational talk before the match to boost team morale.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discouraging - causing someone to lose confidence or enthusiasm
  • synonym: inspirational - providing inspiration or encouragement
