telerobotics

noun
remote control of robots
4.9 6.4
Pronunciation: /tel.ə.roʊ.ˈbɑː.tɪks/ (AmE), /tel.ə.rə.ˈbɒt.ɪks/ (BrE)
The branch of robotics concerned with the control and operation of robots from a distance.

Examples

Telerobotics control system
Recent advances in telerobotics have made remote surgery possible in many hospitals around the world.
Scientists use telerobotics to control exploration vehicles on Mars from laboratories on Earth.
The military has invested heavily in telerobotics to keep personnel out of dangerous environments.
