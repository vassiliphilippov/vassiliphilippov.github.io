isobaric

adjective
constant pressure
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsəˈbærɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈbærɪk / (BrE)
describing a process or condition that occurs at constant pressure

Examples

Isobaric process
The chemical reaction was studied under isobaric conditions to ensure accurate results.
In an isobaric process, the pressure remains unchanged while the volume may vary.
Engineers often analyze isobaric transformations when designing pressure vessels.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adiabatic - describing a process with no heat exchange
  • synonym: isothermal - describing a process occurring at constant temperature
