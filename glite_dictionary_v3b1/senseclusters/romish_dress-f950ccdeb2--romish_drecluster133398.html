Romish dress

noun
clothing style linked to Roman Catholic tradition
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊmɪʃ drɛs / (AmE), / ˈrəʊmɪʃ drɛs / (BrE)
A style of clothing, often elaborate or traditional, associated with Roman Catholic practices, ceremonies, or clergy.

Examples

Romish dress style
During the play, the lead actress appeared in a Romish dress to portray her character's devout faith.
She admired the ornate embroidery on the Romish dress displayed in the museum.
The priest wore a Romish dress during the traditional mass.
