intercounty

noun
county-level match
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈkaʊnti / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈkaʊnti / (BrE)
a sports match or competition between teams representing different counties

Examples

Intercounty match
The annual intercounty attracted thousands of fans from neighboring regions.
She was selected to play in the prestigious intercounty for her outstanding performance.
Rivalries often intensify during the intercounty matches each summer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intracounty - a competition or event within the same county
