ring up the curtain

verb
begin a performance; start something
8.2
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ ʌp ðə ˈkɝːtən / (AmE), / rɪŋ ʌp ðə ˈkɜːtən / (BrE)
to begin a performance or event, especially in a theater, by raising the curtain; to start something

Examples

To ring up the curtain on a show
It's time to ring up the curtain on tonight's performance.
They will ring up the curtain at the new community center opening.
When the orchestra is ready, we'll ring up the curtain and begin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bring down the curtain - to end a performance or an event by lowering the stage curtain
  • synonym: raise the curtain - to start a performance by lifting the stage curtain
