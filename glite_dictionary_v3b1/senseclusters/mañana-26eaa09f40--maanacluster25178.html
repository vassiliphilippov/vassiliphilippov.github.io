mañana

noun
habit of postponing
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɑːˈnjɑːnə / (AmE), / maˈnjɑːnə / (BrE)
an attitude or habit of delaying tasks or decisions until a later time; procrastination

Examples

Mañana attitude
His mañana approach to work often led to missed opportunities.
The team's mañana attitude frustrated their manager, who valued prompt action.
If you keep living with a mañana mindset, important tasks will never get done.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diligence - careful and persistent work or effort
  • synonym: procrastination - the act of delaying or postponing tasks
