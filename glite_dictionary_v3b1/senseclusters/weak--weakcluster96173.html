weak

adjective
diluted or insipid (of liquids/flavors)
1.3 1/m
Pronunciation: / wik / (AmE), / wiÀêk / (BrE)
(of a liquid or flavor) overly diluted or lacking in strength; thin and insipid.

Examples

Weak tea
I'd rather have strong tea than the weak stuff they served.
She complained that the soup was too weak and lacked flavor.
The weak coffee tasted more like water than a proper drink.
