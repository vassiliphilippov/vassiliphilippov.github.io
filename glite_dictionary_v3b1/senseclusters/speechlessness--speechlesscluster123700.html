speechlessness

noun
state of being unable to speak
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːtʃləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈspiːtʃləsnɪs / (BrE)
the state of being unable to speak, especially as a result of strong emotion or shock

Examples

In speechlessness from shock
He stared at her in speechlessness, unable to find the right words.
She listened in speechlessness as the shocking news was delivered.
The announcement left the entire team in stunned speechlessness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: eloquence - the ability to speak with fluency and persuasion
  • synonym: silence - the absence of sound or speech
