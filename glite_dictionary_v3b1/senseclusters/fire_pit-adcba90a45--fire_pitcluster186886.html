fire pit

noun
outdoor fire container
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪər pɪt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə pɪt / (BrE)
a structure or area designed to safely contain an outdoor fire for warmth, cooking, or social gatherings

Examples

Outdoor fire pit
We gathered around the fire pit to tell stories and enjoy the evening air.
The new house has a stone fire pit in the backyard for summer parties.
After dinner, they roasted marshmallows over the fire pit with their friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbecue - a grill or area for cooking food outdoors over a fire
  • synonym: campfire - an open fire used for cooking or warmth outdoors
