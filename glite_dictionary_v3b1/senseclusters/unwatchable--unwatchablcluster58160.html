unwatchable

adjective
impossible to watch
5.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɑtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɒtʃəbəl / (BrE)
not possible or practical to watch because of very poor quality, technical problems, or an obstructed view

Examples

Unwatchable video quality
The old VHS tape was so damaged that the movie became unwatchable after the first ten minutes.
Many critics called the film unwatchable due to its confusing plot and poor camera work.
Heavy rain made the football match almost unwatchable for fans in the stadium.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unviewable - not able to be seen or watched
  • antonym: watchable - pleasant or possible to watch
