flirtation

noun
playful romantic or sexual behavior
6.0
Pronunciation: / flɝːˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / flɜːˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
playful or amorous behavior intended to attract sexual interest or romantic attention.

Examples

Casual flirtation with colleagues
His casual flirtation with colleagues sometimes caused awkward situations.
There was obvious flirtation between them at the party last night.
What started as harmless flirtation soon turned into a serious relationship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flirting - the act of behaving in a playful or amorous way to attract attention
