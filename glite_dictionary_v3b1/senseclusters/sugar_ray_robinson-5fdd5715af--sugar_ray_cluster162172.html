Sugar Ray Robinson

proper noun
famous American champion boxer (1921–1989)
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːɡər reɪ ˈrɑːbɪnsən / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə reɪ ˈrɒbɪnsən / (BrE)
(1921–1989) An American professional boxer, regarded as one of the greatest fighters in history, who won the world middleweight championship five times and the world welterweight championship once.

Examples

Sugar Ray Robinson boxer
Sugar Ray Robinson is often cited as one of the greatest boxers of all time.

The legendary career of Sugar Ray Robinson spanned from the 1940s to the 1960s.
