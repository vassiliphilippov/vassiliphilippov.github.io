unsprung

adjective
not triggered
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsprʌŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsprʌŋ / (BrE)
not having been triggered, released, or set off, especially referring to a trap or spring-loaded device

Examples

Unsprung trap
The old bear trap remained unsprung after a week in the woods.
He checked the mousetraps every morning, but most were still unsprung.
The device was left unsprung to avoid accidentally injuring anyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sprung - having been triggered or released
  • synonym: untriggered - not set off or activated
