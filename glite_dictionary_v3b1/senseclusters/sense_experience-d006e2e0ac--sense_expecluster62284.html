sense experience

noun
basic perception via the senses
Pronunciation: / sɛns ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / sɛns ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
an elementary perception or awareness produced by sensory stimulation, without analysis or interpretation

Examples

A sense experience of touch
A gentle breeze on her skin was her first sense experience of the morning.
Each sense experience contributes to our understanding of the world.
The infant's earliest sense experience comes from touch and sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sensation - a physical feeling or perception resulting from something that happens to or comes into contact with the body
