information processing

noun
methods/sciences of handling information
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfɚˈmeɪʃən ˌproʊsɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɪnfəˈmeɪʃən ˌprəʊsɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
the methods or sciences involved in collecting, manipulating, storing, retrieving, and organizing information, especially by computer systems

Examples

Information processing methods
Advances in information processing have dramatically increased the speed of data analysis in science.
Modern businesses rely on efficient information processing to make better decisions.
The university offers a degree in information processing and management systems.
