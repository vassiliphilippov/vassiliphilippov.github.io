Alexandrian

adjective
of Alexandria
Pronunciation: / ˌælɪɡˈzændriən / (AmE), / ˌælɪɡˈzændriən / (BrE)
relating to the ancient city of Alexandria, its people, or its culture

Examples

Alexandrian culture
The Alexandrian school of philosophy greatly influenced later thinkers.
Many Alexandrian scholars contributed to the advancement of science in the ancient world.
Artifacts from the Alexandrian period are displayed in the museum.
