dargah

noun
saint's tomb complex
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɑrˈɡɑ / (AmE), / dɑːˈɡɑː / (BrE)
a shrine or complex built around the tomb of a Muslim (especially Sufi) saint, used for worship and gatherings
