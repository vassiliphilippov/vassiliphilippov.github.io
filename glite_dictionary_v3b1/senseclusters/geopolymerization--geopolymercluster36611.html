geopolymerization

noun
formation of geopolymers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːoʊˌpɑːlɪmərɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəʊˌpɒlɪməraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a chemical process in which aluminosilicate powders react with alkaline solutions to form solid, stone-like materials

Examples

Geopolymerization process

Engineers used geopolymerization to create a durable, eco-friendly pavement for the new road.
