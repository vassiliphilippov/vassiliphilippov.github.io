drink

verb
propose a toast; celebrate with a drink
1.0 5.1
Pronunciation: /drɪŋk/ (AmE), /drɪŋk/ (BrE)
(intransitive, usually with 'to') To propose a toast to someone or something, often by raising a glass and wishing them well before drinking.

Examples

To drink to success
Everyone gathered to drink to the newlyweds at the reception.
Let us all drink to the success of our new project!
They raised their glasses to drink to the team's hard work and dedication.

Related Terms
