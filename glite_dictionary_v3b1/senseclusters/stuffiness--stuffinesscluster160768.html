stuffiness

noun
pompous or overly formal quality
6.1 6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌfinəs / (AmE), / ˈstʌfinɪs / (BrE)
a quality of being excessively formal, pompous, or lacking liveliness, often making an atmosphere seem dull or oppressive

Examples

Social stuffiness
He disliked the stuffiness of the boardroom, where everyone took themselves too seriously.
Her speech was refreshing, breaking through the stuffiness that usually filled such formal events.
The party was spoiled by the stuffiness of the guests, who refused to relax and have fun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: formality - the quality of being formal or conventional
  • antonym: informality - the quality of being informal
