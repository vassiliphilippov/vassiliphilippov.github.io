Washingtonia

noun
fan-leaved palm tree
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌwɑːʃɪŋˈtoʊniə / (AmE), / ˌwɒʃɪŋˈtəʊniə / (BrE)
a tall palm tree of the genus Washingtonia, known for its fan-shaped leaves and often used as an ornamental plant

Examples

A Washingtonia palm

Birds often nest in the thick crowns of Washingtonia trees in urban parks.
