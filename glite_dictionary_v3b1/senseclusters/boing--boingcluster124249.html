boing

interjection
sound of bouncing
9.6
Pronunciation: /bɔɪŋ/ (AmE), /bɔɪŋ/ (BrE)
an exclamation imitating the sound of a bounce, used to express a sudden springing movement or surprise

Examples

Boing of surprise
The cartoon character jumped on the trampoline and shouted boing as he bounced high.
When the spring snapped back into place, it made a loud boing that startled everyone.
Kids love to say boing when their toys bounce across the floor.
