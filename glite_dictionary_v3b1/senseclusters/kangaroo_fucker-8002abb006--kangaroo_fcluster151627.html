kangaroo fucker

noun
vulgar insult, often used jokingly
Pronunciation: / ˈkæŋɡəˌru ˈfʌkər / (AmE), / ˈkaŋɡəˌruː ˈfʌkə / (BrE)
(Vulgar slang) A jocular, self-deprecating term intended as an insult or self-mockery, especially referencing someone from Australia or pretending to be Australian.

Examples

A kangaroo fucker joke
He often jokingly called himself a kangaroo fucker to make his friends laugh.
She laughed off the insult, replying that all Aussies are born kangaroo fuckers.
The comedian referred to Australians as kangaroo fuckers in his edgy routine.
