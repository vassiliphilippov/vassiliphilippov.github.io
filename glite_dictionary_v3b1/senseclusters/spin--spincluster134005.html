spin

verb
produce thread to make a web
5.0
Pronunciation: / spɪn / (AmE), / spɪn / (BrE)
to produce thread or webs by drawing out and twisting fibers, as done by spiders or when making yarn

Examples

To spin thread
Some caterpillars spin webs for protection during their pupal stage.
Spiders spin intricate webs to catch their prey.
The silkworm spins its cocoon from a single thread of silk.
In the past, people would spin flax into linen for clothing.
She learned to spin wool by hand using a traditional spindle.
Silkworms spin fine threads that are harvested to make silk fabric.

Related Terms

  • synonym: weave - to form (a fabric or web) by interlacing threads
