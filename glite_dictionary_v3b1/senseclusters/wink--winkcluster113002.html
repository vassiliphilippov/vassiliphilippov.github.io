wink

verb
keep back tears or emotion by blinking
Pronunciation: / wɪŋk / (AmE), / wɪŋk / (BrE)
to try to keep back or suppress (tears or emotion) by blinking repeatedly

Examples

To wink away tears
He managed to wink away his frustration before anyone noticed.
Lucy winked away the stinging sensation in her eyes after the news.
She tried to wink back her tears during the emotional speech.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blink - to shut and open the eyes quickly
