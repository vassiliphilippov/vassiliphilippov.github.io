north-seeking pole

noun
the end of a magnet pointing toward the north
5.4 5.0
Pronunciation: /nɔːrθ ˈsiː.kɪŋ poʊl/ (AmE), /nɔːθ ˈsiː.kɪŋ pəʊl/ (BrE)
the end of a magnet that points toward the north when the magnet is suspended freely, also known as the north pole

Examples

The north-seeking pole

The compass needle's north-seeking pole aligns with Earth's magnetic field.
When suspended, the north-seeking pole of a magnet turns toward geographic north.
