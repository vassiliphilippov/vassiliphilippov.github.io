chantry

noun
endowed chapel for Masses for the dead
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæntri /; / ˈtʃæn.tɹi / (AmE), / ˈtʃæntri /; / ˈtʃæn.tri / (BrE)
a chapel or fund in a church, especially in medieval times, for saying Masses for the soul of the donor
