mentalistic

adjective
about mind-reading acts
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛntəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛntəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to mentalism, especially the performance art of creating the illusion of mind-reading or psychic abilities.

Examples

Mentalistic magic show
The magician's mentalistic tricks left the audience wondering if he could really read minds.
She developed a mentalistic routine that involved predicting numbers chosen by volunteers.
Many mentalistic performances rely on clever observation and psychological techniques.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychic - relating to supernatural mental abilities
  • antonym: skeptical - doubting or questioning the truth of something
