only

adjective
single; sole; unique
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊnli / (AmE), / ˈəʊnli / (BrE)
being the single one or thing; existing alone or exclusive of all others

Examples

The only person
He was the only person to arrive at the meeting on time.
She cherished being the only child in a large extended family.
This is the only known specimen of its kind left in existence.
For years, this was his only source of income.
It was the only hotel open during the winter months.
She is their only daughter, and they cherish her deeply.

  • antonym: multiple - having or involving several parts, elements, or members
  • synonym: single - being only one or not more than one
  • synonym: sole - being the only one; exclusive
