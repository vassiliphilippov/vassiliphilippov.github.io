sublittoral

adjective
relating to the continental shelf or its marine life
8.7 8.8
Pronunciation: /sʌb.ˈlɪt.ə.rəl/ /; / /səb.ˈlɪt.ə.rəl/ (AmE), /sʌb.ˈlɪt.ə.rəl/ /; / /səb.ˈlɪt.ə.rəl/ (BrE)
of or relating to the region of the continental shelf between the seashore and the edge of the continental shelf, or to marine organisms living there

Examples

Seaweed diversity is especially rich in the sublittoral regions just offshore.
