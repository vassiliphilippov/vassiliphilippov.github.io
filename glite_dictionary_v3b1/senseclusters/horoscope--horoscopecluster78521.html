horoscope

noun
astrological chart of planets and zodiac signs
3.9 5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔr.əˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈhɒr.əˌskəʊp / (BrE)
A diagram or chart showing the positions of the planets and signs of the zodiac at a particular time and place, used in astrology to predict a person's future or character.

Examples

Birth horoscope chart
In ancient times, kings often consulted their horoscope before making important decisions.
She created a detailed horoscope for her friend's birthday using their exact place and time of birth.
The astrologer explained how the horoscope reflects the alignment of the planets at the moment you were born.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astrology - the study of how the positions of celestial bodies supposedly influence human affairs
