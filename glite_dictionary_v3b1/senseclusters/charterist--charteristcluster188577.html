charterist

noun
charter school supporter
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːrtərɪst / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːtərɪst / (BrE)
a supporter or advocate of charter schools or the charter-school movement

Examples

A charterist teacher
The charterist argued that more school choice would benefit students in the district.
As a proud charterist, she attended every meeting to support new charter school proposals.
Many charterists believe that innovation in education comes from independent public schools.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reformer - a person who works to change and improve a system
  • antonym: traditionalist - a person who supports established customs or schools
