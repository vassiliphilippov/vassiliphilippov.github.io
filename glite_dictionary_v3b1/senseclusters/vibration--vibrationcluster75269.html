Synonyms

  • oscillation
    7.0 2/m
    oscillation is a regular back-and-forth motion often used in science, while vibration is a rapid shaking of objects in general use.
  • shaking
    1.8 4/m
    shaking is a strong, uncontrolled movement you can see or feel, while vibration is a rapid repeated motion, often small.
  • trembling
    2.7 0.07/m
    trembling is a slight shaking often caused by fear, cold, or weakness, while vibration is a physical repeated movement.
  • quiver
    3.9 0.6/m
    quiver is a tiny, often emotional tremble you might feel in your lips or hands, while vibration is usually physical movement.
