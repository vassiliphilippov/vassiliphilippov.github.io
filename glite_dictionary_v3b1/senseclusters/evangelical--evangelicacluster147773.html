evangelical

adjective
ardently enthusiastic about a cause
Pronunciation: / ˌiːˌvænˈdʒɛlɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌiːˌvænˈdʒɛlɪkəl / (BrE)
showing passionate or zealous enthusiasm for a particular cause or belief, often with a missionary fervor.

Examples

Evangelical enthusiasm
He spoke in evangelical tones about the need for change within the organization.
Her evangelical commitment to environmental protection inspired everyone around her.
The volunteers approached the project with an almost evangelical zeal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: passionate - showing or caused by strong feelings or beliefs
  • synonym: zealous - having or showing great energy or enthusiasm in pursuit of a cause
