Alytes obstetricans

noun
European toad species with egg-carrying males
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈlaɪtiːz ɒbˌstɛt̬rɪˈkænz / (AmE), / əˈlaɪtiːz ɒbˌstɛt.rɪˈkænz / (BrE)
A European toad in which the male carries fertilized eggs wrapped around its hind legs until they hatch.
