anionic

adjective
relating to anions
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.aɪˈɑ.nɪk / (AmE), / ˌæn.aɪˈɒn.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of anions, especially in chemistry.

Examples

Anionic compounds
Laboratory tests were conducted to measure anionic concentrations in water samples.
Many dyes in laundry detergents are anionic and interact with fabrics.
The soap contains anionic surfactants that help remove dirt and oil.
