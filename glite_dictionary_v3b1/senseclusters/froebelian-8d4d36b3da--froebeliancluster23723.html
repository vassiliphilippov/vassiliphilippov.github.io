Froebelian

noun
follower of Froebel's methods
9.9
Pronunciation: /froʊ.ˈbiː.li.ən/ /; / /ˈfroʊ.bə.li.ən/ (AmE), /frəʊ.ˈbiː.li.ən/ /; / /ˈfrəʊ.bə.li.ən/ (BrE)
a person who supports or practices the educational methods developed by Friedrich Froebel, especially play-based early childhood education

Examples

A Froebelian teacher
As a dedicated Froebelian, she encouraged children to learn through guided play and exploration.
The new teacher identified herself as a Froebelian and introduced creative activities in her classroom.
Many early childhood educators consider themselves Froebelians because they value play-based learning.
