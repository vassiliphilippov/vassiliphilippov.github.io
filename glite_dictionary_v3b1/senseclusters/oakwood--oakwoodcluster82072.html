oakwood

noun
wood from oak tree
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊkˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈəʊkˌwʊd / (BrE)
the hard, durable wood obtained from an oak tree, commonly used in furniture and construction

Examples

Oakwood furniture
The craftsman selected oakwood for the cabinet because of its strength and beauty.
Many old houses feature floors made from solid oakwood that last for generations.
She admired the rich color of the oakwood dining table in the showroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oak - a type of tree known for its strong wood
  • degree lower: pinewood - wood from a pine tree, softer than oakwood
  • degree lower: teak - a durable hardwood used in furniture and shipbuilding
