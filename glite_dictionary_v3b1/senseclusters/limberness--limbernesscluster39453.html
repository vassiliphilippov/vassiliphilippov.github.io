limberness

noun
flexibility
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmbɚnəs / (AmE), / ˈlɪmbənəs / (BrE)
the quality of being flexible and able to move or bend easily

Examples

The limberness of body
Her limberness allowed her to perform complex yoga poses with ease.
The dancer's limberness impressed everyone during the audition.
Regular stretching can improve your limberness and prevent injuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexibility - the ability to bend or move easily
  • antonym: rigidity - the quality of being stiff and not bending
