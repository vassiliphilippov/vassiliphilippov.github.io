lightering

noun
cargo transfer between ships
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of transferring cargo from one ship to another to reduce weight, often to allow entry into shallow ports

Examples

Lightering cargo transfer
Because the harbor was too shallow, lightering was required before the vessel could dock.
The crew began lightering the oil tanker to reduce its draft for safe passage.
Efficient lightering operations helped the port handle larger ships than usual.

  • synonym: unloading - removing cargo from a ship or vehicle
