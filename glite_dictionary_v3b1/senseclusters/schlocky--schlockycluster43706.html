schlocky

adjective
cheap and low-quality
Pronunciation: / ˈʃlɑki / (AmE), / ˈʃlɒki / (BrE)
cheap, poorly made, or lacking in good taste

Examples

A schlocky movie
The movie was so schlocky that we laughed at the special effects.
He decorated his room with schlocky posters and plastic furniture.
Many critics called the new TV show schlocky and unoriginal.
The decorations at the party were a bit schlocky, but everyone still had a great time.
Critics called the new horror film schlocky because of its exaggerated special effects and predictable plot.
He enjoys watching schlocky TV shows that are so bad they're entertaining.

Related Terms

  • antonym: classy - stylish and of high quality
  • synonym: shoddy - badly made or of poor quality
  • synonym: tacky - showing poor taste or quality
