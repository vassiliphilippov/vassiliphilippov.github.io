rudderfish

noun
a fish known for following ships
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌdərˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrʌdəˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a fish found in American coastal waters, known for following ships and sometimes caught for food or sport
