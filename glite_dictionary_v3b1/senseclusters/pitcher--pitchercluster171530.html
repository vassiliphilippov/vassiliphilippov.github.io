pitcher

noun
baseball player who throws to batter
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃə / (BrE)
the player on a baseball team who throws the ball to the batter

Examples

Baseball pitcher
After years of playing shortstop, she finally became the team's pitcher.
Every baseball team relies on its pitcher to control the pace of the game.
The pitcher threw a fastball that struck out the batter.
She wants to become a professional pitcher when she grows up.
The pitcher threw a perfect game, striking out every batter.
The team's starting pitcher injured his shoulder during the first inning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: batter - the player who tries to hit the ball with a bat in baseball
  • antonym: catcher - the baseball player who catches pitches thrown by the pitcher
