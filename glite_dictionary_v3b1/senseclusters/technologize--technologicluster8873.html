technologize

verb
to apply technology
Pronunciation: / tɛkˈnɑlədʒaɪz / (AmE), / tɛkˈnɒlədʒaɪz / (BrE)
to interpret, represent, or treat something mainly in technical or technological terms

Examples

To technologize a problem
Some educators worry that schools may technologize learning at the expense of personal interaction.
The company decided to technologize its customer service by introducing automated chatbots.
Critics argue that we should not technologize every aspect of daily life without considering the consequences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: computerize - to convert to a system operated or controlled by computers
  • synonym: modernize - to make something more modern or up to date
  • antonym: simplify - to make something easier or less complex
