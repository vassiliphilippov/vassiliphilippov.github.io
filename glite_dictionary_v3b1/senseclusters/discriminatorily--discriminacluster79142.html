discriminatorily

adverb
with unfair bias
Pronunciation: / dɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪtərɪli / (AmE), / dɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪtərɪli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows unfair or prejudiced treatment toward different groups of people

Examples

To act discriminatorily
The company was accused of hiring discriminatorily based on age and gender.
Policies that are enforced discriminatorily can harm minority groups.
He argued that the rules were applied discriminatorily in his case.

Related Terms

  • antonym: impartially - in a way that treats all people equally
  • synonym: unfairly - in a way that is not just or equitable
