bibliog

noun
bibliography entry
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪbliˌɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈbɪbliˌɒɡ / (BrE)
an individual bibliographic reference or entry in a list of sources, especially in academic writing

Examples

A bibliog entry
Each bibliog must include the author's name, title, and publication date.
The professor asked us to double-check every bibliog in our final paper.
She forgot to add a bibliog for one of her main sources.

Related Terms

  • synonym: citation - a formal mention of a source in academic writing
  • synonym: reference - a mention of a source of information in academic work
