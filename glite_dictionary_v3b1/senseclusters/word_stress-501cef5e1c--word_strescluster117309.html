word stress

noun
emphasis on a syllable in a word
4.2 3.2
Pronunciation: /wɜːrd stres/ (AmE), /wɜːd stres/ (BrE)
the emphasis given to a particular syllable within a polysyllabic word when pronounced

Examples

Word stress pattern
In English, word stress can change the meaning of a word, as in 'record' and 'record'.
Misplacing word stress can make pronunciation difficult for listeners to understand.
Teachers often use visual cues to help students learn proper word stress.
