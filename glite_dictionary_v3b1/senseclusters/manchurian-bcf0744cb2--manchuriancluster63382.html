Manchurian

noun
Indo-Chinese dish
5.4
Pronunciation: / mænˈtʃʊriən / (AmE), / mænˈtʃʊəriən / (BrE)
a popular Indo-Chinese dish of fried vegetables or meat served in a spicy, tangy sauce

Examples

Vegetable Manchurian
We ordered vegetable Manchurian as a starter at the new Indo-Chinese restaurant.
Chicken Manchurian is often served with fried rice or noodles.
Many people enjoy the spicy flavor of Manchurian at family gatherings.

  • synonym: sweet and sour - a dish featuring a sweet and tangy sauce, often with meat or vegetables
