arrest

noun
the act of detaining someone by law
5.8
Pronunciation: / əˈrɛst / (AmE), / əˈrɛst / (BrE)
the act of detaining someone, especially a criminal, by legal authority

Examples

Police arrest
He was placed under arrest for driving without a license.
The arrest of the suspect was broadcast on the news.
The police made an arrest after a lengthy investigation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detention - the act of keeping someone in custody
  • antonym: release - the act of freeing someone from custody
