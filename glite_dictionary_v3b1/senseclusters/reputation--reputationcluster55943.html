reputation

noun
online trust score
4.0 1.14/m
Pronunciation: / ccr5bpj59c8te6a8359n / (AmE), / ccr5bpj8ac8te6a8359n / (BrE)
On online platforms, a numeric or system-generated measure of a user's trustworthiness, standing, or reliability, often shown as a score or rating and used to determine privileges.

Examples

Online reputation score
Her reputation on the marketplace rose to 4.9, unlocking seller privileges.
New users with low reputation cannot post reviews until they complete identity verification.
Stack Overflow's scoring system awards points to build a user's reputation and access moderation tools.

Synonyms

  • karma
    4.6 0.52/m
    karma is a visible points total earned from community votes, while reputation is the platform's system score showing trust and privileges.
  • rep
    7.9 1.96/m
    rep is an informal short form used in casual talk for someone's standing, while reputation is the platform's numeric system score showing trust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rating - a measured score indicating quality, performance, or reliability on a scale or system
  • synonym: trustworthiness - the quality of being reliable and deserving of trust; often estimated by platform reputation systems
