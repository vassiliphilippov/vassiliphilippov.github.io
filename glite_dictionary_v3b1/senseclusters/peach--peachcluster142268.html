peach

noun
a pale pinkish-yellow color
Pronunciation: / pitʃ / (AmE), / piːtʃ / (BrE)
a pale pinkish-yellow color, resembling the skin of a ripe peach

Examples

Peach color dress
He handed me a folder in a pale peach shade, different from the usual office colors.
She chose a peach dress for the summer party, which complemented her skin tone beautifully.
The walls of the nursery were painted a soft peach to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere.
