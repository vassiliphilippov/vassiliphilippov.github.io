vascular

adjective
relating to fluid-carrying vessels
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvæskjələr / (AmE), / ˈvæskjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or having vessels that conduct and circulate fluids, such as blood or sap, in animals or plants.

Examples

Vascular system
Patients with heart problems may have issues with their vascular system.
The surgeon specializes in vascular surgery, repairing arteries and veins.
Trees have vascular tissues that transport water and nutrients from roots to leaves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circulatory - relating to the circulation of fluids, especially blood
  • antonym: nonvascular - not having vessels or ducts to conduct and circulate fluids
