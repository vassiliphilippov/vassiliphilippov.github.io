evergreen

adjective
retaining leaves year-round (plants)
Pronunciation: / ˈɛvɚˌɡrin / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌɡriːn / (BrE)
(of a plant) retaining green leaves throughout the year, not shedding leaves seasonally.

Examples

Evergreen leaves
The evergreen pine trees looked vibrant even during the winter months.
Unlike many deciduous varieties, evergreen shrubs provide privacy and color all year long.
We planted a row of evergreen bushes to keep the garden looking lively through every season.
