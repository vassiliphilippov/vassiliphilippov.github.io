undertrained

adjective
lacking sufficient training
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈtreɪnd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈtreɪnd / (BrE)
not having received enough training or preparation for a particular task or job

Examples

Undertrained staff
The undertrained staff struggled to handle the emergency effectively.
Many undertrained workers found it difficult to operate the new machinery safely.
An undertrained team can make costly mistakes during critical situations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inexperienced - lacking practical experience or training
  • synonym: unqualified - not having the necessary skills or knowledge for a job
  • antonym: well-trained - having received thorough or sufficient training
