unfloured

adjective
not coated with flour
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈflaʊərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈflaʊəd / (BrE)
not coated or dusted with flour before cooking or baking

Examples

Unfloured fish
The chef preferred to serve the fish unfloured for a lighter texture.
Some recipes call for unfloured chicken to achieve a crispier skin.
She baked the potatoes unfloured to keep the dish gluten-free.

Related Terms

  • antonym: floured - coated or dusted with flour
