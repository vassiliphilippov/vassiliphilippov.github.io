deep-chested

adjective
having a large or prominent chest
Pronunciation: / dipˈtʃɛstɪd / (AmE), / diːpˈtʃɛstɪd / (BrE)
(of an animal or person) having a large or prominent chest that extends downward, often indicating strength or stamina.
