natural language processing

noun
field of computer science for language understanding
1.0 5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnætʃɚl ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˌproʊˈsɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnætʃrəl ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˌprəʊˈsɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
