sperm bank

noun
facility for storing and preserving sperm
Pronunciation: / spɝm bæŋk / (AmE), / spɜːm baŋk / (BrE)
a facility where sperm is collected, stored, and preserved for future use, typically for artificial insemination.

Examples

A sperm bank facility
Many couples rely on a sperm bank when facing fertility challenges.
She decided to visit a sperm bank to discuss her options for having a child.
The sperm bank maintains detailed records about every donor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryobank - a facility that stores biological samples at very low temperatures
