goldentop

noun
yellow-flowered plant
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈgoʊl.dən.tɑːp/ (AmE), /ˈgəʊl.dən.tɒp/ (BrE)
a common name for several herbaceous plants in the aster family with clusters of small yellow flowers, often found in North American meadows and wetlands
