goldentop

noun
yellow-flowered plant
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldənˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldənˌtɒp / (BrE)
a common name for several herbaceous plants in the aster family with clusters of small yellow flowers, often found in North American meadows and wetlands

Examples

Wild goldentop flowers
The field was covered in goldentop that glowed brightly under the afternoon sun.
Hikers admired the clusters of goldentop growing along the edge of the wetland.
In late summer, bees are often seen visiting goldentop for its nectar.

Related Terms
