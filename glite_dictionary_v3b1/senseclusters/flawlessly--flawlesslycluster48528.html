flawlessly

adverb
without faults or imperfections
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔləsli / (AmE), / ˈflɔːləsli / (BrE)
In a manner that is without any faults, mistakes, or imperfections; perfectly.

Examples

To perform flawlessly
He completed the complicated puzzle flawlessly, without making a single mistake.
The chef presented a meal that was flawlessly executed and beautifully arranged.
The dancer performed her routine flawlessly in front of the judges.

Related Terms

  • antonym: imperfectly - in a faulty or flawed way
  • synonym: perfectly - in a way that is perfect; without error or defect
