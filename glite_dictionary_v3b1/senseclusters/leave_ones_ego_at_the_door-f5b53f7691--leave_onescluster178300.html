leave one's ego at the door

verb
set aside pride or arrogance
Pronunciation: / liv wʌnz ˈiɡoʊ æt ðə dɔr / (AmE), / liːv wʌnz ˈiːɡəʊ æt ðə dɔː / (BrE)
To set aside one's pride or sense of self-importance, especially before entering a situation where cooperation or humility is required.

Examples

To leave your ego at the door
If you want honest feedback, be ready to leave your ego at the door.
She learned to leave her ego at the door when collaborating with others.
To succeed in this workshop, everyone needs to leave their ego at the door.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humble oneself - to make oneself humble or modest
  • synonym: swallow one's pride - to suppress feelings of pride or self-importance
