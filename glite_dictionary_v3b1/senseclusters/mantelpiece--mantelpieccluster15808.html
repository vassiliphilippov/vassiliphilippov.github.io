A shelf above a fireplace, often projecting from the wall and sometimes decorated.

Examples

A mantelpiece shelf

He hung the Christmas stockings from the mantelpiece every year.
She placed the family photographs on the mantelpiece above the fire.

Synonyms

  • mantel
    4.4 2.82/m
    mantel is the short, common word for the shelf above a fireplace, while mantelpiece is the fuller, sometimes more formal name.
  • mantel shelf
    3.9 0.04/m
    mantel shelf names the shelf used to show photos or ornaments above a fireplace, while mantelpiece is the general decorated feature.
  • chimneypiece
    8.2 0.04/m
    chimneypiece is a slightly older, architectural word for the decorated area around a fireplace, while mantelpiece is the common everyday shelf term.
