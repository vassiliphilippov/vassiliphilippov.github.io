mantelpiece

noun
shelf above a fireplace
6.6 4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.t̬əlˌpis / (AmE), / ˈmæn.tl̩.piːs / (BrE)
A shelf above a fireplace, often projecting from the wall and sometimes decorated.

Examples

A mantelpiece shelf
A clock ticked quietly on the mantelpiece as the flames danced below.
He hung the Christmas stockings from the mantelpiece every year.
She placed the family photographs on the mantelpiece above the fire.
