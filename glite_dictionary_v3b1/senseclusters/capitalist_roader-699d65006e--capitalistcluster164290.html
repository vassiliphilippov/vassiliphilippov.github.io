capitalist roader

noun
advocate of capitalist policies within socialism
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.ɪ.t̬əl.ɪst ˈroʊ.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæp.ɪ.təl.ɪst ˈrəʊ.də / (BrE)
a person who advocates or adopts capitalist practices or policies, especially within a socialist or communist system

Examples

A capitalist roader person
During the Cultural Revolution, being labeled a capitalist roader could result in serious consequences.
Some party members accused him of being a capitalist roader after he proposed economic reforms.
The newspaper article denounced senior officials as capitalist roaders who betrayed socialist principles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capitalist - a supporter of capitalism
  • antonym: socialist - a supporter of socialism, often opposed to capitalists
