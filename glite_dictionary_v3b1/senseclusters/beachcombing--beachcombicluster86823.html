beachcombing

noun
searching beach for items
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtʃˌkoʊmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃˌkəʊmɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of searching along a beach for valuable, interesting, or useful items washed ashore

Examples

A morning of beachcombing
They spent the morning beachcombing and found several colorful shells.
On vacation, she enjoyed beachcombing for sea glass and driftwood.
After the storm, many people went beachcombing to see what had washed up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scavenging - searching for and collecting discarded or useful items
  • synonym: treasure hunting - searching for hidden or valuable objects
