ponytailed

verb
to tie hair back
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊniˌteɪld / (AmE), / ˈpəʊniteɪld / (BrE)
to fasten hair into a ponytail by tying it back with a band or similar item

Examples

To ponytail her hair
Before going for a run, she ponytailed her hair to keep it out of her face.
He quickly ponytailed his daughter's hair before school started.
Many dancers ponytailed their hair for the performance to look neat and tidy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: let down - to allow hair to fall freely instead of tying it
  • synonym: tie back - to pull hair away from the face and secure it
