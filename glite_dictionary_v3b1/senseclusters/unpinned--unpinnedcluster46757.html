unpinned

adjective
not fastened with pins
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɪnd / (BrE)
not fastened, secured, or held in place with a pin or pins

Examples

Unpinned paper
After the meeting, he removed the unpinned notes from the bulletin board.
The tailor found the unpinned fabric pieces scattered on the table.
She noticed her scarf was unpinned and about to fall off her shoulder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loose - not firmly fixed in place
  • antonym: pinned - fastened or held in place with a pin or pins
