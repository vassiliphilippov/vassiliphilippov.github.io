heterotic

adjective
showing hybrid vigor
9.9
Pronunciation: /he.tə.ˈrɑː.tɪk/ (AmE), /he.tə.ˈrɒ.tɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or showing hybrid vigor, especially as a result of crossbreeding different varieties or species

Examples

Heterotic corn variety
The heterotic corn variety produced higher yields than either of its parent strains.
Researchers observed that heterotic animals often have greater resistance to disease.
Plant breeders aim to develop heterotic hybrids for improved agricultural performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hybrid - an organism produced by crossing different varieties or species
  • antonym: inbred - produced by breeding closely related individuals, often lacking hybrid vigor
