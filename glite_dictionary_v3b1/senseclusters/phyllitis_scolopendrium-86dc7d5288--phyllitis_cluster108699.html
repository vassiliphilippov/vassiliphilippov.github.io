Phyllitis scolopendrium

noun
species of Eurasian fern; hart's-tongue fern
Pronunciation: / faɪˈlaɪtɪs ˌskoʊləˈpɛndrɪəm / (AmE), / fɪˈlɪtɪs ˌskɒləˈpɛndrɪəm / (BrE)
