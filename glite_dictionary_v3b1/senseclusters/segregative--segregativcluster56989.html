segregative

adjective
causing separation
8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈse.ɡrə.ɡə.tɪv/ (AmE), /ˈse.ɡrə.ɡə.tɪv/ (BrE)
causing or tending to cause separation or division into groups, especially by distinguishing differences

Examples

Segregative policies
The new law was criticized for its segregative impact on local schools.
Some argued that the policy was inherently segregative and would harm community relations.
Urban planning should avoid segregative designs that separate neighborhoods by income.
