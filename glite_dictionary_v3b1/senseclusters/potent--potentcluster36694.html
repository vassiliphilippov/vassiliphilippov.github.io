potent

adjective
(of a male) sexually capable
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊtənt / (AmE), / ˈpəʊtənt / (BrE)
(of a male) capable of copulation; able to achieve an erection and perform sexual intercourse.

Examples

Potent man
As he grew older, he was pleased to remain potent and healthy.
Some medications may impact a man's ability to stay potent.
The doctor's examination confirmed that the patient was still potent.
