lichenous

adjective
resembling lichen
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪkənəs / (AmE), / ˈlɪkənəs / (BrE)
having the appearance, texture, or characteristics of lichen

Examples

lichenous layer
The ancient tree trunk was covered in a lichenous layer that gave it a greenish hue.
She studied the lichenous patches growing on the damp rocks by the river.
The artist painted a lichenous texture to mimic the look of old stone walls.
