asymmetric

adjective
not symmetrical; lacking symmetry
1.6 5.0
Pronunciation: /eɪ.sɪ.ˈme.trɪk/ /; / /ə.sɪ.ˈme.trɪk/ (AmE), /eɪ.sɪ.ˈme.trɪk/ /; / /ə.sɪ.ˈme.trɪk/ (BrE)
having two sides or parts that are not the same in size, shape, or position; lacking symmetry.

Examples

Asymmetric shape
Because of its asymmetric shape, the building looks different from every angle.
The artist created an asymmetric design that drew everyone's attention.
The butterfly's wings appear asymmetric due to an injury.
