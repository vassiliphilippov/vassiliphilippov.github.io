ductus

noun
body tube or channel
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌktəs / (AmE), / ˈdʌktəs / (BrE)
a tube or channel in the body that carries fluids such as blood or bile

Examples

Body ductus
The doctor explained that the ductus was responsible for transporting bile to the intestine.
A blockage in the ductus can cause serious health problems if not treated promptly.
During the operation, the surgeon carefully avoided damaging the ductus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duct - a tube in the body that carries fluids
  • synonym: vessel - a tube in the body for carrying blood or other fluids
