woodbine

noun
European honeysuckle with fragrant flowers
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdˌbaɪn / (AmE), / ˈwʊd.baɪn / (BrE)
a European climbing honeysuckle with fragrant red and yellow-white flowers, also known as Lonicera periclymenum.

Examples

Fragrant woodbine flowers
A beautiful woodbine twined itself around the old garden gate.
In early summer, the scent of the woodbine filled the evening air.
They planted woodbine along the fence to attract bees and butterflies.
