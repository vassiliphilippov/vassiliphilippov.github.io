upswell

noun
sudden upward movement
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌswɛl / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌswɛl / (BrE)
a sudden upward movement or swelling, especially of water or another surface.

Examples

River upswell
A sudden upswell in the river made the bridge tremble.
The upswell of the ocean caught the swimmers by surprise.
After the storm, an upswell of water flooded the lower fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surge - a sudden powerful forward or upward movement
  • synonym: wave - a moving ridge or swell on the surface of water
