balletomania

noun
passionate enthusiasm for ballet
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌbæl.ə.toʊˈmeɪ.ni.ə / (AmE), / ˌbæl.ə.təʊˈmeɪ.ni.ə / (BrE)

Examples

During the 1920s, balletomania swept through Paris, drawing crowds to every performance.
Her balletomania led her to attend every major production in the country.
Some critics attribute the growth of dance festivals to a resurgence of balletomania.
