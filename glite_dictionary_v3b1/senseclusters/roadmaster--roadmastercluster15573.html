roadmaster

noun
road maintenance supervisor
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊd.mæ.stər/ /; / /ˈroʊd.mə.stər/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊd.mɑː.stə/ (BrE)
a person responsible for the maintenance and supervision of roads or railways

Examples

The roadmaster supervises repairs
The roadmaster scheduled repairs after the heavy storm damaged several sections of the highway.
As a roadmaster, she was responsible for inspecting the tracks every morning.
The team reported directly to the roadmaster about any issues they found on the route.
The roadmaster inspected the tracks after the heavy storm to ensure they were safe for trains.
After noticing a problem with the rails, the roadmaster quickly organized a repair crew.
Every week, the roadmaster reviews reports from maintenance teams along the railway line.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conductor - a railroad employee in charge of a train's operation and crew
  • synonym: superintendent - a person who manages or oversees a place or activity
  • synonym: track inspector - a person who examines railway tracks for safety and maintenance
  • degree lower: worker - a person who does physical work, especially in maintenance
