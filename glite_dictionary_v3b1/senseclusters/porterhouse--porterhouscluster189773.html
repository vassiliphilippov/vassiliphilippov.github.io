porterhouse

noun
large T-bone steak cut
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔːɾɚˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtəˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a large steak cut from the thick end of the short loin, containing a T-shaped bone and a large section of tenderloin

Examples

A large porterhouse steak
A well-marbled porterhouse is often large enough for two people to share.
He ordered a juicy porterhouse for dinner at the steakhouse.
The chef expertly grilled the porterhouse to a perfect medium rare.

Related Terms

  • synonym: T-bone steak - steak cut from the short loin including a T-shaped bone
