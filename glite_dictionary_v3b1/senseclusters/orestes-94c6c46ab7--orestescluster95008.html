(in Greek mythology) the son of Agamemnon and Clytemnestra, who, persuaded by his sister Electra, avenged his father's murder by killing his mother and her lover Aegisthus.

Examples

Orestes and Electra
In art and literature, Orestes is often depicted alongside his sister Electra.
Many Greek tragedies revolve around Orestes and his quest for revenge against his mother and her lover.
The story of Orestes inspired numerous plays by Aeschylus, Sophocles, and Euripides.
