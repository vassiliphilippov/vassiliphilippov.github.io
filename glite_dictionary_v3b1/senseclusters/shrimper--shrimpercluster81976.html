shrimper

noun
shrimp harvester
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrɪmpər / (AmE), / ˈʃrɪmpə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to catch or harvest shrimp, especially professionally

Examples

Professional shrimper
The experienced shrimper set out before dawn to make the most of the tide.
Many families in the coastal town rely on shrimpers for their main source of income.
After a long day at sea, the shrimper sold his catch at the local market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fisherman - a person who catches fish for a living or as a hobby
  • synonym: shrimp - a small shellfish with a long body and legs, eaten as food
