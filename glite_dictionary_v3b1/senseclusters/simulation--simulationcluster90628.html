simulation

noun
act of pretending; false appearance
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪmjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪmjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of pretending or giving a false appearance, especially in order to deceive

Examples
