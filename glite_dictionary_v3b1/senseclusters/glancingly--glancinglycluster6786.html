glancingly

adverb
briefly or casually
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlænsɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːnsɪŋli / (BrE)
in a brief or indirect way; mentioning or referring to something only quickly or casually

Examples

Mentioned glancingly
She mentioned the topic glancingly before moving on to the main subject of her talk.
The article only glancingly addressed the environmental impact of the project.
He looked glancingly at his watch, not wanting to seem impatient.
The report glancingly addressed the environmental impact but did not go into detail.
She mentioned her previous job only glancingly during the interview.
The novel glancingly refers to historical events without focusing on them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: briefly - for a short time; quickly
  • synonym: casually - in a relaxed or informal way
  • antonym: directly - in a way that is straightforward or without deviation
  • synonym: superficially - in a way that is not thorough or detailed
  • antonym: thoroughly - in a detailed and complete way
