adjoint

noun
assistant or deputy
9.5
Pronunciation: / æˈdʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / æˈdʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
a person who assists or acts as a deputy to someone in a position of authority

Examples

Mayor's adjoint
The mayor relied on her adjoint to coordinate meetings and manage her schedule.
As an adjoint, he was responsible for assisting the director with daily operations.
The new adjoint quickly became an essential part of the leadership team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assistant - a person who helps someone in a job or task
  • synonym: deputy - a person appointed to act as a substitute with power to act
  • antonym: superior - a person of higher rank or authority
