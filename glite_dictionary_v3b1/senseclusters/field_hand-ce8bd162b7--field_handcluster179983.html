field hand

noun
manual farm worker
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld ˌhænd / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌhænd / (BrE)
a person employed to do manual work on a farm, typically in the fields

Examples

A field hand worker
After the harvest, the field hand helped load the wagons with fresh produce.
He found employment as a field hand on a large cotton plantation.
Many field hands worked from sunrise to sunset during the busy season.

  • synonym: farmhand - a person who works on a farm
