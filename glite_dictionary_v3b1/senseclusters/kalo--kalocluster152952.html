kalo

noun
Hawaiian taro plant
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːloʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːləʊ / (BrE)
a tropical plant (taro), especially in Hawaii, whose starchy root is eaten as food and which is important in Hawaiian culture and tradition
