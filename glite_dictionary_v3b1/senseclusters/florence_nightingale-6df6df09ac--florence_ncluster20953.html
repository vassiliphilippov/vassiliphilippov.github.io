Florence Nightingale

proper noun
pioneering English nurse (1820–1910)
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔːrəns ˈnaɪtənˌɡeɪl /; / ˈflɔːrəns ˈnaɪtɪnˌɡeɪl / (AmE), / ˈflɒrəns ˈnaɪtɪnɡeɪl / (BrE)
