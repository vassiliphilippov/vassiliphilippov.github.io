durn

adjective
mildly emphatic, informal
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɝːn / (AmE), / dɜːn / (BrE)
used informally to express mild annoyance or emphasis, as a humorous or softened form of 'damn' or 'darn'

Examples

That durn thing
I can't believe I lost my durn wallet again.
That durn cat keeps knocking things off the table.
He forgot to bring the durn map on our hike.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: damn - used to express anger or emphasis
  • synonym: darn - a mild, informal substitute for 'damn'
