elating

adjective
causing joy or upliftment
Pronunciation: / ɪˈleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
making someone feel very happy or joyful; uplifting in spirit

Examples

An elating effect
The good news had an elating effect on everyone in the office.
There was something elating about the freedom of being outdoors.
Winning the award was an elating moment in her career.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depressing - making someone feel very sad or disheartened
