handbarrow

noun
rectangular carrying frame with handles
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌbæroʊ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌbærəʊ / (BrE)
a rectangular frame with handles at both ends, carried by two people for transporting loads

Examples

A handbarrow frame
In the past, coal was often moved using a simple handbarrow.
The workers lifted the heavy stone onto the handbarrow and carried it across the yard.
They needed two people to operate the handbarrow because of its size and weight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wheelbarrow - a cart with a single wheel and two handles for carrying loads by hand
