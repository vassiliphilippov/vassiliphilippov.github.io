bolson

noun
enclosed desert basin
Pronunciation: / boʊlˈsoʊn / (AmE), / bɒlˈsɒn /; / bɒlˈsəʊn / (BrE)
a large, flat-floored basin in a desert or arid region that collects water but has no outlet to the sea
