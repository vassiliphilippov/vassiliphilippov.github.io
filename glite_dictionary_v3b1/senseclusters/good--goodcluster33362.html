good

adjective
skilled; proficient or competent
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd / (AmE), / ɡʊd / (BrE)
having or showing skill, knowledge, and proficiency at something

Examples

Good at math
She is a good pianist who can play complex pieces beautifully.
The chef is good at creating delicious dishes from simple ingredients.
You need a good lawyer to handle such a complicated case.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adept - very skilled or capable at something
  • antonym: incompetent - not having enough skill or knowledge to do something well
  • synonym: proficient - skilled or competent at something
