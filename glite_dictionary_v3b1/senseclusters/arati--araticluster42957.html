arati

noun
Hindu worship song
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrəti / (AmE), / ˈɑːrəti / (BrE)
a Hindu devotional song or hymn sung during a ritual in which lamps are waved before a deity

Examples

Arati devotional song
The priest led the arati as worshippers offered prayers and flowers to the deity.
During the evening arati, the temple was filled with music and the glow of lamps.
Visitors were invited to join the arati and experience the spiritual atmosphere of the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bhajan - a Hindu devotional song expressing love for the divine
  • synonym: chant - a repeated rhythmic phrase, often sung in religious ceremonies
