phylum Nemertina

noun
a phylum of proboscis worms
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪləm nɛˌmɜrˈtaɪnə / (AmE), / ˈfʌɪləm nɛˌmɜːˈtiːnə / (BrE)
A taxonomic phylum comprising the proboscis worms, a group of mostly marine, elongated, unsegmented invertebrates known for their eversible proboscis.
