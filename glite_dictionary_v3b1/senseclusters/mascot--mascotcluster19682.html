mascot

noun
symbolic figure for a group or team
Pronunciation: / ˈmæs.kɑt / (AmE), / ˈmæs.kɒt / (BrE)
a character, animal, or object adopted by a team, group, or organization as a symbolic figure, often believed to bring good luck

Examples

The mascot dances
During the parade, the team's mascot danced and posed for photos with fans.
Our university's mascot is a fierce-looking eagle that energizes the crowd at every game.
The company chose a cartoon rabbit as its official mascot to appeal to children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emblem - a design or object symbolizing a quality, type, group, etc.
  • synonym: symbol - something that stands for or represents something else
