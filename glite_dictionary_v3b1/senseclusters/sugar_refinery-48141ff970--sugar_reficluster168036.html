sugar refinery

noun
factory for refining sugar
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʊɡɚ rɪˈfaɪnəri / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə rɪˈfaɪnəri / (BrE)
a factory where raw sugar is processed and purified into refined sugar

Examples

A sugar refinery factory

The new sugar refinery in town provides jobs for hundreds of local workers.
