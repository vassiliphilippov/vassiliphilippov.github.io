ugly

noun
unattractive person or thing
Pronunciation: / ˈʌɡli / (AmE), / ˈʌɡli / (BrE)
a person or thing that is considered unpleasant or unattractive in appearance

Examples

The uglies at school
Some people in the group called the outcasts the uglies, which was very hurtful.
The old building was often referred to as one of the city's uglies.
He felt left out when others labeled him among the uglies at school.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beauty - a person or thing that is very attractive
  • synonym: eyesore - something very unpleasant to look at
