Spanish War

noun
war between the U.S. and Spain in 1898
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspænɪʃ wɔr / (AmE), / ˈspænɪʃ wɔː / (BrE)
the war fought between the United States and Spain in 1898, resulting in the U.S. gaining territories such as Puerto Rico, Guam, and the Philippines
