apron

noun
protective garment worn over clothes
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.prən / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.prən / (BrE)
A garment, typically made of cloth, leather, or plastic, tied around the waist and worn over other clothes to protect them from dirt or stains.

Examples

A kitchen apron
Please remember to wear an apron in the workshop to protect your clothes.
She tied her apron around her waist before starting to cook.
The artist's apron was covered in splotches of paint.
