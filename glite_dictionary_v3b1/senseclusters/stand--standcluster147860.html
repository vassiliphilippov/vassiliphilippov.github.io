Synonyms

  • stand up
    1.0 1.12/m
    stand up is a phrase for putting something upright, like a vase on a table, while stand can mean being upright or holding a place.
  • place upright
    3.1 0.00/m
    place upright is to put an object in a vertical position, often by hand, while stand can mean being upright or occupying a place.
