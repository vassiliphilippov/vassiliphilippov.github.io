antidrug

adjective
against drug use
Pronunciation: / ˈæntiˌdrʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈæntiˌdrʌɡ / (BrE)
designed to prevent, oppose, or reduce the use of illegal drugs or drug addiction

Examples

An antidrug campaign
The school launched an antidrug campaign to educate students about the dangers of substance abuse.
Many parents support antidrug policies to keep their communities safe.
The government funds several antidrug initiatives each year to combat addiction.
The government launched a new antidrug initiative to educate young people about substance abuse.
Many parents support antidrug programs in local schools to keep their children safe.
The city organized an antidrug march to raise awareness about the dangers of narcotics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drug-free - not involving or using drugs
  • antonym: prodrug - supporting or promoting drug use
