unwish

verb
to remove from wishlist
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɪʃ / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɪʃ / (BrE)
to remove something from a wishlist or list of desired items, especially on an online platform

Examples

To unwish an item
After changing her mind, she decided to unwish the shoes from her online shopping list.
You can easily unwish any item if you no longer want it on your wishlist.
He unwished several books after realizing he already owned similar titles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: add - to include something on a list or record
  • synonym: delete - to remove something from a list or record
