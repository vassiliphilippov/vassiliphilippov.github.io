bell captain

noun
supervisor of hotel bellboys
Pronunciation: / bɛl ˈkæp.tən / (AmE), / bɛl ˈkæp.tɪn / (BrE)
the person in a hotel responsible for supervising bellboys and coordinating their work, assisting guests with luggage and errands

Examples

The bell captain supervises bellboys
The bell captain assigned two bellboys to help with the guests' luggage.
The bell captain made sure all arrivals and departures were handled smoothly.
Whenever I have a special request, I speak directly to the bell captain at the front desk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bellboy - a hotel attendant who helps guests with luggage
