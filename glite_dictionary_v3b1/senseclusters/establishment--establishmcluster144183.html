Examples

Establishment of a species

The establishment of invasive species can drastically alter local ecosystems.
Tree establishment is critical to the success of reforestation projects.

Synonyms

  • ecesis
    9.9 0.00/m
    ecesis is the technical ecology term for a species becoming established in a new habitat, while establishment is the general term.
  • naturalization
    6.1 0.02/m
    naturalization involves introducing nonnative species and their success in a new region, while establishment refers only to becoming established in that habitat.
