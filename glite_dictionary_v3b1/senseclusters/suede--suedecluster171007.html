suede

noun
soft napped fabric imitating leather
6.5 3.4
Pronunciation: /sweɪd/ (AmE), /sweɪd/ (BrE)
a soft material with a napped (fuzzy) surface, either real leather or fabric made to look like leather, used for clothing and accessories

Examples

Suede jacket
She bought a pair of suede boots for the winter season.
The jacket is made of suede and feels incredibly soft to the touch.
This couch is covered in synthetic suede that’s easy to clean.
Because suede is delicate, it can be easily stained by water.
She wore a stylish jacket made of suede to the party.
The suede shoes felt soft and luxurious to the touch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nubuck - top-grain cattle leather sanded or buffed on the grain side
  • synonym: suede leather - leather with a soft, napped finish, usually made from the underside of animal skin
