wood frog

noun
a brown frog found in North American woodlands
Pronunciation: / wʊd frɑɡ /; / wʊd frɑg / (AmE), / wʊd frɒɡ /; / wʊd frɒg / (BrE)
a small, light-brown frog found in moist woodlands of North America, especially in forests with spruce trees
