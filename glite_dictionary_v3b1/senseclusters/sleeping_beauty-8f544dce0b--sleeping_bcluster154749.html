Sleeping Beauty

noun
princess in a fairy tale; passive or beautiful sleeper
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈslipɪŋ ˈbyuti / (AmE), / ˈsliːpɪŋ ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
A princess who falls into a deep sleep and is awakened by a prince, from a famous fairy tale; also used figuratively to describe someone strikingly beautiful and passive or someone sleeping soundly.

Examples

She is a Sleeping Beauty
He nicknamed his sister Sleeping Beauty because she could sleep through anything.
Many consider the old mansion a kind of Sleeping Beauty, waiting to be restored to its former glory.
The young actress played Sleeping Beauty in the school's annual fairy tale production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beauty - a quality of being pleasing to the senses; synonym for 'Sleeping Beauty' in the figurative sense
