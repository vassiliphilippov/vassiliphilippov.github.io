positive identification

noun
definitive confirmation of identity
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑzətɪv ˌaɪˌdɛntəfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpɒzətɪv ˌaɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
definitive evidence or confirmation that a person is who they claim to be, often used in legal, security, or technical contexts

Examples

Positive identification of personnel
Entry to the secure facility is only permitted after positive identification of all personnel.
The bank requires positive identification before allowing you to access your account.
Witnesses provided positive identification of the suspect at the trial.
