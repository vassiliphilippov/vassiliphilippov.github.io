lightning bug

noun
nocturnal beetle with a glowing abdomen
3.5 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪt.nɪŋ bʌg/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪt.nɪŋ bʌg/ (BrE)
A nocturnal beetle, especially common in warm regions, that produces light from special organs in its abdomen; also known as a firefly.

Examples

A lightning bug flying
Kids love chasing lightning bugs on summer evenings in the backyard.
The soft glow of a lightning bug lit up the darkness of the field.
We used jars to catch lightning bugs and watch them flicker before letting them go.
