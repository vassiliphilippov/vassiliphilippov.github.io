colony

noun
group of microorganisms from one parent cell
2.9 2.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.lə.niː/ /; / /ˈkɔː.lə.niː/ (AmE), /ˈkɒ.lə.niː/ (BrE)
a group of the same kind of living things (such as animals, plants, or microorganisms) living or growing together

Examples

An ant colony
