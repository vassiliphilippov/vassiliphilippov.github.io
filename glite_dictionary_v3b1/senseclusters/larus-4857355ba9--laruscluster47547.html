Larus

noun
genus of gulls (birds)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛə.rəs /; / ˈlɛərəs / (AmE), / ˈlɑː.rəs /; / ˈlar.əs / (BrE)
the genus of gulls that is the type genus of the family Laridae (includes most typical gull species)
