contrastingly

adverb
in a contrasting manner
7.1 5.0
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈtræ.stɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /kən.ˈtræ.stɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that shows a sharp difference or contrast between two or more things.

Examples

Used contrastingly in speech
She is outgoing and sociable; contrastingly, her brother is quiet and reserved.
The living room was decorated in light colors; contrastingly, the bedroom featured dark, rich tones.
The weather was sunny yesterday but, contrastingly, today it is cold and rainy.
