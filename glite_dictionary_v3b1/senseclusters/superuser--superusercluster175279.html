superuser

adjective
with full system privileges
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌjuːzər / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌjuːzə / (BrE)
having or relating to the highest level of administrative privileges on a computer system.

Examples

Superuser access
You need superuser access to change these system files.
The software must be installed in superuser mode to work properly.
Only superuser accounts can modify the network settings on this server.

Related Terms

  • antonym: restricted - limited in access or privileges
