consumerist

adjective
focused on consumption
6.3
Pronunciation: / kənˈsuːmərɪst / (AmE), / kənˈsjuːmərɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by an emphasis on buying and owning goods and services

Examples

Consumerist culture
The advertisement reflects a consumerist attitude that encourages people to buy more than they need.
Some critics argue that a consumerist society leads to environmental problems.
Her consumerist values made her focus on shopping and collecting the latest products.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anti-consumerist - opposed to the emphasis on buying and owning things
  • synonym: materialistic - focused on material possessions and physical comfort
