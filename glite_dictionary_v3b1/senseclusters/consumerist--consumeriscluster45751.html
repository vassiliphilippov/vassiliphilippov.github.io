consumerist

adjective
focused on consumption
6.3 0.39/m

relating to or characterized by an emphasis on buying and owning goods and services

Examples

Consumerist culture
The advertisement reflects a consumerist attitude that encourages people to buy more than they need.
Some critics argue that a consumerist society leads to environmental problems.
Her consumerist values made her focus on shopping and collecting the latest products.

Synonyms

  • materialistic
    6.1 1.96/m
    materialistic cares more about owning things and comfort, while consumerist focuses on buying and using goods and services.
  • acquisitive
    7.9 0.36/m
    acquisitive wants to gain or possess things, while consumerist describes a wider culture or habit of buying and owning goods.

Related Terms
