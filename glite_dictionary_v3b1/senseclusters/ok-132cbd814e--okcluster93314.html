OK

adverb
expression of agreement
1.0
Pronunciation: / oʊˈkeɪ / (AmE), / əʊˈkeɪ / (BrE)
used to show agreement or acceptance, often at the beginning of a sentence.

Examples

OK, let's go
OK, I understand what you mean now.
OK, I will see you tomorrow at 8 o'clock.
OK, let's get started with today's meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: all right - used to express agreement or understanding
  • antonym: no - word used to express refusal or disagreement
