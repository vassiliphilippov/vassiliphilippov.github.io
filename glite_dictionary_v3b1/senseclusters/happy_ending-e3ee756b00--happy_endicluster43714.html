happy ending

noun
a positive conclusion or outcome
Pronunciation: / ˈhæpi ˈɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhæpi ˈɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
a positive or satisfying conclusion to a story, situation, or series of events

Examples

A happy ending to a story
After years of struggle, the couple finally got their happy ending.
Not all real-life stories have a happy ending.
The fairy tale promised a happy ending for its main characters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tragic ending - a negative or sorrowful conclusion to a story or event
