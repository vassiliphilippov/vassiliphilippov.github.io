jeweler's glass

noun
magnifying lens for jewelers
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːələrz ˌɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːələz ˌɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a small magnifying lens used by jewelers to closely examine gemstones and other fine details

Examples

A jeweler's glass lens
A jeweler's glass helps reveal the tiny details in precious stones.
