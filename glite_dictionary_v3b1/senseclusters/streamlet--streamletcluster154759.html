streamlet

noun
a small stream
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːmlɪt / (AmE), / ˈstriːmlɪt / (BrE)
a small narrow stream of water; a tiny brook or rivulet

Examples

A small streamlet
A thin streamlet wound its way through the grassy meadow.
After the heavy rain, a tiny streamlet formed beside the path.
The children followed the streamlet until it disappeared into the woods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brook - a small stream, especially one that flows through the countryside
  • synonym: rivulet - a very small stream or brook
