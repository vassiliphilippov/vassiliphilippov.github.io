cozy

adjective
pleasantly warm and comfortable
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊzi / (AmE), / ˈkəʊzi / (BrE)
warm, comfortable, and pleasant, especially in a small or friendly place or atmosphere

Examples

A cozy room

Related Terms
