nonpalpable

adjective
cannot be felt
8.1
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈpælpəbəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈpælpəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be felt by touch, especially during a physical examination

Examples

Nonpalpable tumor
The tumor was nonpalpable and could only be detected through imaging tests.
During the examination, the physician found the lymph node to be nonpalpable.
A nonpalpable mass may require further diagnostic procedures to identify.

Related Terms

  • antonym: palpable - able to be touched or felt
