tugger

noun
one who tugs or pulls
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌɡər / (AmE), / ˈtʌɡə / (BrE)
a person or thing that pulls, tugs, or drags something, especially with effort

Examples

A tugger at rope
Each tugger at the rope was determined to win the game of tug-of-war.
That old engine is a real tugger when it comes to hauling boats upriver.
The little tugger struggled to move the heavy sled across the snow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puller - a person or thing that pulls
