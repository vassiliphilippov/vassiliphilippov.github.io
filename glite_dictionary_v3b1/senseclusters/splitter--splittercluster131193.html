splitter

noun
device for dividing
Pronunciation: / ˈsplɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsplɪtə / (BrE)
a tool or device that divides something into two or more parts

Examples

Audio splitter cable
He used a splitter to connect two headphones to his phone.
The technician installed a cable splitter to provide internet to both rooms.
A Y-shaped splitter allows you to share audio from one device with multiple people.
He used a splitter to connect two sets of headphones to his phone.
The wood splitter made it easy to prepare firewood for the winter.
A cable splitter allows you to send the signal to multiple devices at once.

Related Terms

  • antonym: combiner - a device that joins two or more things together
  • synonym: divider - a device that separates or splits something into parts
