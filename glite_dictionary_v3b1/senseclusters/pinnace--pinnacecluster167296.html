pinnace

noun
small boat for ship-to-shore transport
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪn.əs / (AmE), / ˈpɪn.ɪs / (BrE)
a small boat, originally one used as a tender for communication between ship and shore, or as a light sailing vessel

Examples

A small pinnace boat
During the storm, a small pinnace brought urgent messages from the harbor to the ship.
The captain sent a pinnace ashore with supplies for the stranded sailors.
The explorers disembarked from their pinnace onto the unknown coast.
