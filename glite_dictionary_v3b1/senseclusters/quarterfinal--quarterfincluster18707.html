quarterfinal

noun
one of four rounds before semifinals
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌkwɔrtɚˈfaɪnəl / (AmE), / ˌkwɔːtəˈfaɪnəl / (BrE)
one of four matches or rounds in a tournament from which the winners progress to the semifinals

Examples
