believable

adjective
able to be believed; plausible
1.0
Pronunciation: / bɪˈlivəbəl / (AmE), / bɪˈliːvəbl / (BrE)
Able to be accepted as true or real; plausible.

Examples

A believable lie
He told a believable lie that almost fooled everyone.
Her explanation was so detailed that it sounded completely believable.
The movie's plot was more believable than most science fiction stories I've seen.
