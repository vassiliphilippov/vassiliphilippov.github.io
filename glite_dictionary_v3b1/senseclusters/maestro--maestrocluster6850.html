maestro

noun
master artist or musician
5.4
Pronunciation: / maɪˈstroʊ / (AmE), / maɪˈstrəʊ / (BrE)
A distinguished artist, especially a master conductor, composer, or musician of great skill.

Examples

A maestro conductor
Her dedication to her craft earned her the reputation of a true maestro in the art world.
Many young musicians aspire to reach the level of a maestro one day.
The orchestra played beautifully under the direction of maestro Giordani.

Related Terms

  • synonym: master - a person who has acquired complete knowledge or skill in a subject
