ledgeman

noun
quarry worker who splits stone blocks
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈledʒ.mæn/ /; / /ˈledʒ.mən/ (AmE), /ˈledʒ.mən/ /; / /ˈledʒ.mæn/ (BrE)
A male quarry worker whose job is to split off blocks of stone from a ledge.

Examples

A ledgeman at quarry
As a ledgeman, he worked long hours breaking stone in the quarry.
The experienced ledgeman skillfully separated the granite blocks from the rock face.
