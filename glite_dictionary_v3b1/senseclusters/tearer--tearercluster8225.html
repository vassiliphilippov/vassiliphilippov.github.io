tearer

noun
person who cries
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪrər / (AmE), / ˈtɪərə / (BrE)
a person who sheds tears, especially someone who cries easily or often

Examples

A tearer at movies
He is such a tearer that even happy endings make him cry.
During the emotional speech, several tearers in the audience reached for tissues.
As a known tearer, she avoids watching sad movies with friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crybaby - a person who cries or complains easily
  • synonym: weeper - someone who weeps or cries
