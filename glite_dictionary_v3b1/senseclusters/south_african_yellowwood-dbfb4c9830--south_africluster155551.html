South African yellowwood

noun
southern African tree; timber source
9.4
Pronunciation: /saʊθ ˈæ.frɪ.kən ˈjel.oʊ.wʊd/ /; / /suːθ ˈæ.frɪ.kən ˈjel.oʊ.wʊd/ (AmE), /saʊθ ˈæ.frɪ.kən ˈjel.əʊ.wʊd/ /; / /suːθ ˈæ.frɪ.kən ˈjel.əʊ.wʊd/ (BrE)
