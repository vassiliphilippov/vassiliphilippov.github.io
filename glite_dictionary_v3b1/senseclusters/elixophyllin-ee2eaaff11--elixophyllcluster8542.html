Elixophyllin

noun
theophylline-based bronchial dilator (trade name)
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɪksəˈfɪlɪn / (AmE), / ɪˌlɪksəˈfɪlɪn / (BrE)
A proprietary preparation of theophylline, used in medicine to dilate the bronchi and treat breathing disorders such as asthma.
