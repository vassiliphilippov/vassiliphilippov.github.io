stipulative definition

noun
definition given by stipulation, not standard usage
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪpjələtɪv ˌdɛfəˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈstɪpjʊlətɪv ˌdɛfɪˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
a definition assigned to a word or phrase by an individual, which may not reflect standard or established usage

Examples

A stipulative definition of success
Her stipulative definition of 'success' differed greatly from its everyday meaning.
The philosopher introduced a stipulative definition to clarify how he would use the term in his argument.
Whenever creating a new technical term, it is helpful to provide a stipulative definition at the start.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lexical definition - a definition that reflects standard usage
  • synonym: precising definition - a definition intended to make a term more precise
