clink

noun
a short, light metallic sound
7.1
Pronunciation: / klɪŋk / (AmE), / klɪŋk / (BrE)
a short, light, metallic sound, such as that made by two glasses or metal objects striking each other

Examples

A clink of glasses
The clink of coins in his pocket was unmistakable as he walked into the room.
The clink of keys on the metal hook echoed in the empty hallway.
We heard the clink of glasses from the dining table as the toast began.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: clang - a resonant, prolonged, metallic sound
  • degree higher: clank - a loud, sharp, metallic sound
