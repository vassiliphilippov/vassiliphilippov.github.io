review

noun
summary at the end of a discussion
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvju / (AmE), / rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)
a short summary at the end of a lecture, discussion, or document that repeats the main points or substance of what has been presented

Examples

Meeting review
At the end of the meeting, the chair provided a review of the key decisions made.
Please stay for the review so we can be sure everyone remembers the main points.
The final review helped the students recall everything that was taught during the semester.

Related Terms

  • synonym: summary - a brief statement of the main points
