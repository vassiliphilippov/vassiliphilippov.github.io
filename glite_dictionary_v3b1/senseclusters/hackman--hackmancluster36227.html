hackman

noun
driver of hired carriage
Pronunciation: / ˈhækˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈhæk.mən / (BrE)
a driver of a hired carriage or cab, especially in the past

Examples

The hackman waits
The hackman waited patiently outside the theater for his next passenger.
In Victorian London, a hackman was a common sight on busy streets.
She hailed a hackman to take her home after the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cabdriver - a person who drives a taxi for hire
  • synonym: coachman - a person who drives a horse-drawn coach
