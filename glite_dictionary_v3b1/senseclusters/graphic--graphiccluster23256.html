graphic

adjective
relating to graphs or visual presentations
2.4 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈgræ.fɪk/ (AmE), /ˈgræ.fɪk/ (BrE)
relating to visual representation, such as graphs, symbols, images, or the graphic arts

Examples

Graphic design
A graphic display makes complex information easier to understand.
Our report includes several graphic representations of sales growth over the past year.
The results were explained using a graphic chart that highlighted key trends.
A graphic symbol is easier to recognize than a written word.
Computer interfaces rely on graphic icons for navigation.
The document uses several graphic signs to illustrate the instructions.
His graphic designs blended traditional techniques with modern aesthetics.
She curated an exhibition featuring the artist's stunning graphic work.
The museum's collection of graphic prints is renowned worldwide.
