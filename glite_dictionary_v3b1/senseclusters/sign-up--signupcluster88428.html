sign-up

noun
registration form
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnˌʌp / (BrE)
a sheet, form, or online page where people write their names to register for an activity or event

Examples

A sign-up sheet
The sign-up for the summer camp is available at the front desk.
Students filled out the sign-up to join the after-school club.
You must complete the online sign-up before attending the workshop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: application - a formal request to join or participate
  • synonym: registration - the process of signing up for something officially
