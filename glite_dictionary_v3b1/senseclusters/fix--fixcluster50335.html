fix

noun
dose of something craved, often drugs
Pronunciation: / fɪks / (AmE), / fɪks / (BrE)
an amount or dose of something craved, especially an injection of a narcotic drug

Examples

A morning fix
After quitting smoking, he still craved his morning fix of caffeine.
She admits she needs a daily fix of exercise to feel balanced.
The addict was desperate for his next fix and became increasingly anxious.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dose - a measured quantity of a drug taken at one time
