fastback

noun
car with sloping rear
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæstˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈfɑːstˌbæk / (BrE)
a car or car design with a roofline that slopes continuously down to the rear of the vehicle, often giving a sporty appearance
