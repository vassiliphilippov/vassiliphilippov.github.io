tyken

noun
Scottish bedding
9.9
an old term for bedding or bedclothes, especially used in Scotland

Examples

A thick tyken
The old inn provided a thick tyken to each guest on cold nights.
He remembered his grandmother sewing a new tyken every winter.
In the Scottish Highlands, a tyken was often filled with straw for warmth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bedclothes - sheets, blankets, and covers used on a bed
  • synonym: blanket - a large piece of soft material used to keep warm in bed
