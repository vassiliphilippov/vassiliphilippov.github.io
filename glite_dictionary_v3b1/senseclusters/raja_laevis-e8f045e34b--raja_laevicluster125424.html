Raja laevis

noun
a large species of skate (fish)
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdʒə ˈliːvɪs / (AmE), / ˈrɑːdʒə ˈliːvɪs / (BrE)
a large species of skate, growing up to 5 feet long, known for being active and easy to catch

Examples

The Raja laevis skate
Biologists studied the habitat preferences of Raja laevis in the Atlantic Ocean.
Fishermen were excited when they caught a Raja laevis off the coast.
The Raja laevis is renowned for its size and agility compared to other skates.
