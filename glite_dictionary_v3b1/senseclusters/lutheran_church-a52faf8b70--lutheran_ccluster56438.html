Lutheran Church

noun
Protestant denomination based on Luther's teachings
Pronunciation: / ˈluːθərən tʃɝːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈluːθərən tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a Protestant Christian denomination based on the teachings of Martin Luther

Examples
