Heliothis zia

noun
a crop-damaging moth species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːliˈɒθɪs ˈzaɪə /; / ˌhiːliˈoʊθɪs ˈzaɪ.ə / (AmE), / ˌhiːlɪˈɒθɪs ˈzaɪə /; / ˌhiːlɪˈɒθɪs ˈziː.ə / (BrE)
a species of moth whose larvae (caterpillars) are pests that damage crops such as corn, cotton, and tomatoes
