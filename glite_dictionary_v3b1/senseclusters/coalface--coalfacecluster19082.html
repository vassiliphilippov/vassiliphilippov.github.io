coalface

noun
frontline; hands-on work
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkoʊl.feɪs/ (AmE), /ˈkəʊl.feɪs/ (BrE)
the most practical, hands-on, or frontline part of an activity, organization, or situation where real work or problems are encountered.

Examples

Work at the coalface
Reporters returned from the coalface to show the crisis's day-to-day struggles.
Our volunteers work at the coalface helping refugees find shelter and legal advice.
She moved from strategy meetings to the coalface to see how the new policy worked in practice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: front line - the most active or immediate position in an activity or operation; where direct work or conflict takes place
  • antonym: head office - the central administrative or managerial part of an organization, as opposed to direct operational work
