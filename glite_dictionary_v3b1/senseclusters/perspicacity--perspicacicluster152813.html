perspicacity

noun
keen insight or perceptiveness
7.6 7.7
Pronunciation: /pɜːr.spɪ.ˈkæs.ə.tɪ/ (AmE), /pɜː.spɪ.ˈkæs.ə.tɪ/ (BrE)
