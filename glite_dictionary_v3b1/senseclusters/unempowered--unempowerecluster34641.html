unempowered

adjective
lacking power or confidence
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪmˈpaʊərd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˈpaʊəd / (BrE)
not having power, authority, or the ability or confidence to act or influence things

Examples

Feel unempowered to act
Many residents felt unempowered to challenge the policy because they lacked money and information.
The team was unempowered and unable to make important decisions on their own.
She often felt unempowered at work due to a lack of support from her manager.
The committee members felt unempowered to influence the final decision.
Many students are unempowered when it comes to shaping school policies.
She remained unempowered despite her years of experience in the company.

Related Terms

  • antonym: empowered - having the ability, confidence, or authority to act
  • synonym: helpless - unable to take action or protect oneself
  • synonym: powerless - without the ability to control or influence events
