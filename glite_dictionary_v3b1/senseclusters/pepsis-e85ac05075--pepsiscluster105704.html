Pepsis

proper noun
genus of spider wasps
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpsɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɛpsɪs / (BrE)
a genus of large spider wasps known for hunting tarantulas, especially in the Americas

Examples

Pepsis wasp sting
The Pepsis wasp is famous for its extremely painful sting.
Scientists observed a Pepsis dragging a tarantula back to its burrow.
Many people are surprised by the size and vivid colors of the Pepsis.
