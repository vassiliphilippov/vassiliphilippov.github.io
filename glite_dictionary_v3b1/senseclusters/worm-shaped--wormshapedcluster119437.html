worm-shaped

adjective
resembling a worm in shape
Pronunciation: / wɜrm ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / wɜːm ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the form or appearance of a worm; long, thin, and cylindrical in shape.

Examples

A worm-shaped tunnel
He was fascinated by the worm-shaped tunnels left in the wood.
The bacteria appeared as worm-shaped rods under the microscope.
The larvae are worm-shaped and burrow into the soil for protection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cylindrical - having the shape of a cylinder
  • synonym: wormlike - similar to a worm in form or movement
