SVR

noun
support vector regression (machine learning)
Pronunciation: / ɛs vi ˈɑr / (AmE), / ɛs vi ˈɑː / (BrE)
(in machine learning) support vector regression: a supervised learning method that uses support vectors and kernel functions to predict continuous numerical values.
