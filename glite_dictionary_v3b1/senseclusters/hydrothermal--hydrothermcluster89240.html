hydrothermal

adjective
relating to hot water
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdroʊˈθɜrməl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdrəʊˈθɜːməl / (BrE)
relating to the action or presence of hot water, especially within the earth's crust

Examples

Hydrothermal vents
Scientists study hydrothermal vents to learn about life in extreme environments.
The area is known for its hydrothermal springs, which attract many tourists.
Minerals are often deposited by hydrothermal fluids moving through rock layers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geothermal - relating to the heat of the earth's interior
