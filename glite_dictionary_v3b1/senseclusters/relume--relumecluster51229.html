relume

verb
to restore brightness
9.9
Pronunciation: / riˈluːm / (AmE), / rɪˈluːm / (BrE)
to restore brightness, energy, or hope to something, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

To relume hope
The teacher's encouragement helped relume the student's passion for learning.
After the long winter, the arrival of spring seemed to relume the whole town.
A heartfelt speech can sometimes relume hope in a discouraged group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extinguish - to put an end to something, especially hope or light
  • synonym: rekindle - to revive or renew something, especially feelings or interest
