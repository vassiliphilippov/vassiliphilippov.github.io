spotted hemlock

noun
a highly poisonous North American water hemlock
7.0 8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈspɑː.təd ˈhem.lɑːk/ /; / /ˈspɑː.tɪd ˈhem.lɑːk/ (AmE), /ˈspɒt.ɪd ˈhem.lɒk/ /; / /ˈspɒt.əd ˈhem.lɒk/ (BrE)
