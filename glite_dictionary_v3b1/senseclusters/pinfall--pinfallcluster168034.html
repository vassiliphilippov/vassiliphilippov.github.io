pinfall

noun
bowling pins knocked down
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌfɔl / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌfɔːl / (BrE)
the total number of pins knocked down in a single roll, frame, or game of bowling

Examples
