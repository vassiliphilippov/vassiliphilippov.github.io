diaristic

adjective
like a diary
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a diary or journal, especially in style or content

Examples

Diaristic writing style
The author's diaristic approach gives readers a personal glimpse into her daily experiences.
His paintings have a diaristic quality, capturing moments from his everyday life.
Many blogs today are written in a diaristic style, sharing thoughts and events as they happen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autobiographical - relating to a person's own life or autobiography
  • synonym: journalistic - relating to the style or content of journalism
