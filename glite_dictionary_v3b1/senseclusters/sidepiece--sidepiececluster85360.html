sidepiece

noun
secret romantic partner
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌpiːs / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌpiːs / (BrE)
a person with whom someone is romantically or sexually involved in addition to their main partner, often secretly

A secret sidepiece

Some people disapprove of having a sidepiece while being in a committed relationship.
The magazine published a story about a celebrity and his alleged sidepiece.
