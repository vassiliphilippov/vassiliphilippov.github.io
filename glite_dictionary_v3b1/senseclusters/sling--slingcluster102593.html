sling

noun
supporting bandage for arm or hand
6.2
Pronunciation: / slɪŋ / (AmE), / slɪŋ / (BrE)
A bandage or piece of cloth suspended from the neck to support an injured arm or hand.

Examples

Arm in a sling
After his fall, emergency workers placed his arm in a sling before taking him to the hospital.
She wore her broken arm in a sling for several weeks after the accident.
The doctor showed him how to use the sling properly to avoid strain.

Related Terms
