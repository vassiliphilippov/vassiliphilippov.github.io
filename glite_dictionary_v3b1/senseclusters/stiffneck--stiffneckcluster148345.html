stiffneck

noun
painful, rigid neck
5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈstɪf.nek/ (AmE), /ˈstɪf.nek/ (BrE)
a condition in which the neck feels sore and is difficult or painful to move

Examples

Painful stiffneck
After a long flight, she developed a stiffneck and could barely turn her head.
Using a proper pillow can help prevent a stiffneck in the morning.
He missed work because his stiffneck made it hard to drive.
