Synonyms

  • note
    3.7 20/m
    note is a small specific quality in speech, writing, or behavior, while tone is the overall way someone's expression shows feeling.
  • tone of voice
    3.1 2/m
    tone of voice is the sound and emotion in how someone speaks, while tone covers voice, writing, and actions more widely.
  • tenor
    5.8 2/m
    tenor is the general meaning or character of what is said or written, while tone shows a speaker's or writer's feeling.
  • feeling
    1.4 15/m
    feeling is the mood or atmosphere that people sense in a place or situation, while tone is how someone expresses emotion.
