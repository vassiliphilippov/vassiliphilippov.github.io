running

noun
managing or administering something
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌn.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrʌn.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of organizing, managing, or being in control of something

Examples

Running a business
He took over the running of the family business after his father retired.
She is responsible for the day-to-day running of the school office.
The running of such a large organization requires a lot of skill and experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administration - the act of managing duties, responsibilities, or a business
  • synonym: management - the process of dealing with or controlling things or people
