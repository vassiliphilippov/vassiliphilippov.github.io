interschool

adjective
between two or more schools
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈskuːl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈskuːl / (BrE)
Occurring or existing between two or more schools, especially secondary schools, usually in reference to competitions or events.

Examples

Interschool sports competition
Rivalries often develop between teams in interschool athletic meets.
She won an award at the interschool science competition.
The annual interschool debate attracted participants from across the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intramural - occurring within a single institution, especially a school
