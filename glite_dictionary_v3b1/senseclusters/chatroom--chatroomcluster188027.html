chatroom

noun
online space for real-time chatting
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃætˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈtʃætˌruːm / (BrE)
A website or online space where users can communicate with each other in real time, usually about a particular topic.

Examples

A chatroom online
He met several of his closest friends in a technology chatroom years ago.
Many teenagers spend hours in their favorite chatroom discussing music and games.
The website hosts a chatroom dedicated to new parents seeking advice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forum - an online message board for discussions
  • synonym: message board - a website for posting and reading messages
