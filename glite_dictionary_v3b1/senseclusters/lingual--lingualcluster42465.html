lingual

adjective
relating to the tongue or its vicinity
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋɡwəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋɡwəl / (BrE)
relating to, situated near, or resembling the tongue, especially in medical or anatomical contexts.

Examples

Lingual nerve
A lingual frenulum connects the tongue to the floor of the mouth.
Researchers observed lingual movements during speech production.
The dentist noted inflammation on the lingual surface of my lower teeth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: buccal - relating to the cheek, especially the side of teeth next to the cheek
  • synonym: tongue - the muscular organ in the mouth used for tasting and speaking
