tax

verb
set or determine the amount (esp. of a fine or charge)
3.1
Pronunciation: / tæks / (AmE), / tæks / (BrE)
to set or determine the amount to be paid, especially of a payment such as a fine or charge

Examples

To tax the amount
Courts have the authority to tax the amount of compensation in certain cases.
The council decided to tax the landowner for the environmental damage caused.
The judge will tax the costs of the lawsuit against the losing party.
