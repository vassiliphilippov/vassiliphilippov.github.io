dichotomously

adverb

In a way that divides or classifies into two distinct and opposing groups or parts.

Examples

To think dichotomously
In his argument, he viewed society dichotomously, as being either progressive or traditional.
Plants may branch dichotomously, with each branch splitting into two equal parts.
The data can be analyzed dichotomously, with responses classified as either positive or negative.
