Bofors gun

noun
automatic antiaircraft gun
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊfɔːrz ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈbəʊfɔːz ɡʌn / (BrE)
an automatic, usually double-barreled antiaircraft gun designed by the Swedish company Bofors and widely used in military defense systems
