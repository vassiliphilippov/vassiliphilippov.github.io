dove

noun
emblem of peace
Pronunciation: / doʊv /; / dʌv / (AmE), / dəʊv /; / dʌv / (BrE)
a small pigeon, often white, that is a symbol of peace

Examples

A white dove
Artists often depict a dove carrying an olive branch to convey messages of harmony.
She wore a necklace with a small silver dove, a token of her beliefs in peace.
The white dove released at the ceremony represented hope for lasting peace.
A white dove flew above the crowd, symbolizing hope and peace.
She watched a dove build its nest outside her window.
The garden is often visited by various species of dove.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peace - freedom from disturbance; tranquility
  • synonym: pigeon - a bird of the same family, generally larger and stockier
