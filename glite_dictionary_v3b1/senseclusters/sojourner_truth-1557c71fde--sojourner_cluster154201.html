Sojourner Truth

proper noun
U.S. abolitionist and feminist (1797–1883)
6.1
Pronunciation: / soʊˈdʒɝːnɚ ˈtruːθ / (AmE), / səʊˈdʒɜːnə ˈtruːθ / (BrE)
