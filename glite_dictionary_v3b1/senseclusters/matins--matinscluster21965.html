matins

noun
first canonical hour, morning prayer
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmætɪnz / (AmE), / ˈmatɪnz / (BrE)
the first canonical hour of Christian liturgy, typically celebrated in the early morning or at daybreak

Examples

Matins prayer service
Matins mark the beginning of the daily cycle of prayers in many Christian traditions.
She woke at dawn to attend matins at the cathedral.
The monks gathered in the chapel for matins before sunrise.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: lauds - the second canonical hour following matins
