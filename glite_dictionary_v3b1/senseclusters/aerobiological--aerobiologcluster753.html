aerobiological

adjective
airborne biology
Pronunciation: / ˌɛəroʊˌbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌeərəʊˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of biological particles, such as pollen or spores, that are carried in the air

Examples

The aerobiological study
The aerobiological team collected air samples to monitor seasonal pollen levels.
Researchers used aerobiological methods to track the spread of fungal spores in the region.
An aerobiological analysis can help predict allergy outbreaks during spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aerobiology - the study of airborne biological particles
  • synonym: biological - relating to living organisms
