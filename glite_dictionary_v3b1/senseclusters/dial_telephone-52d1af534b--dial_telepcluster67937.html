dial telephone

noun
telephone with a rotary dial
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəl təˈloʊˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəl tɪˈləʊ.fəʊn / (BrE)
A telephone with a rotary dial used to input the number to be called.

Examples

A classic dial telephone
Many museums display a classic dial telephone as a piece of technological history.
My grandmother still has a dial telephone in her living room.
You need to rotate the wheel on the dial telephone to make a call.

Related Terms

  • antonym: push-button telephone - telephone operated by pressing buttons, not a dial
  • synonym: rotary phone - telephone operated by a rotary dial
