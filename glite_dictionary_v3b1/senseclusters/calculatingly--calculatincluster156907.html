calculatingly

adverb
in a scheming or strategic manner
Pronunciation: / ˈkælkjəˌletɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkalkjʊˌleɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows careful, clever, and often selfish thought and planning to achieve one's own aims.

Examples

To calculate carefully
He calculatingly devised a plan to gain his colleague's trust.
She smiled calculatingly, clearly weighing the benefits of each option before responding.
The villain watched calculatingly from the shadows, waiting for the perfect moment to act.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cunningly - in a clever and deceitful way
  • antonym: innocently - in a harmless or naive way
