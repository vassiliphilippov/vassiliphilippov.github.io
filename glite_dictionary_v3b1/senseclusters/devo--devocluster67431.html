devo

noun
slang for fan
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːvoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdiːvəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a devoted fan, especially of a particular band or music genre, or a member of the band Devo

Examples

Devo of a band
He has been a devo of punk music since the 1980s.
Many devos gathered at the concert to celebrate their favorite band.
She proudly calls herself a devo of classic rock.

Related Terms

  • synonym: devotee - a person who is very interested in and enthusiastic about someone or something
  • synonym: fan - a person who admires and supports someone or something
