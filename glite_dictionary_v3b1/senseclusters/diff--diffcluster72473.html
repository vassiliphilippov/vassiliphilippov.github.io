diff

noun
informal difference
Pronunciation: / dɪf / (AmE), / dɪf / (BrE)
an informal term for a difference between two things, especially in comparison or analysis

Examples

A big diff
There is a big diff between the two versions of the report.
Can you show me the diff so I can see what changed?
The software highlights the diff between the original and the updated document.

Related Terms

  • synonym: difference - the way in which two or more things are not the same
  • antonym: similarity - the state of being similar or alike
