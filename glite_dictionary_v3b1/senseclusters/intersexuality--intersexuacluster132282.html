intersexuality

noun
mixed sexual traits
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˌsɛkʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌɪntəsɛkʃuˈælɪti /; / ˌɪntəˌsɛkʃuˈælɪti / (BrE)
the condition of having physical or biological traits that do not fit typical definitions of male or female, including having both male and female characteristics

Examples

Biological intersexuality
Researchers observed intersexuality in several species of amphibians exposed to environmental pollutants.
The study focused on the causes and effects of intersexuality in marine organisms.
Cases of intersexuality have been reported in plants as well as animals.
Medical professionals are learning more about intersexuality and how to support people with diverse bodies.
Discussions about intersexuality often include both scientific and social perspectives.
Some activists work to raise awareness of intersexuality and promote acceptance in society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hermaphroditism - the condition of having both male and female reproductive organs
  • synonym: intersex - a person whose physical sex characteristics do not fit typical definitions of male or female
  • antonym: sexual dimorphism - the existence of two distinct sexes with different characteristics
