TST

sym
tuberculosis test
Pronunciation: / ti ɛs ti / (AmE), / tiː ɛs tiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'tuberculin skin test', a medical test used to determine if someone has been exposed to tuberculosis

Examples

TST medical test
The nurse administered a TST to check for possible tuberculosis infection.
A positive TST result may indicate exposure to tuberculosis bacteria.
Before starting the job, employees must have a TST as part of the health screening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: PPD test - another name for the tuberculin skin test
  • synonym: tuberculin skin test - a test for tuberculosis using an injection of tuberculin
