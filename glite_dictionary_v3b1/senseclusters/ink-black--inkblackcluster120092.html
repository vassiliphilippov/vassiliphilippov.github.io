ink-black

adjective
very dark black color
Pronunciation: / ɪŋk blæk / (AmE), / ɪŋk blak / (BrE)
Having the extremely dark color of black ink; very black.

Examples

Ink-black feathers
Ravens have ink-black feathers that shine in the sunlight.
She wore an ink-black dress to the evening gala.
The ink-black sky was filled with sparkling stars.

  • synonym: pitch-black - completely black; as dark as pitch
