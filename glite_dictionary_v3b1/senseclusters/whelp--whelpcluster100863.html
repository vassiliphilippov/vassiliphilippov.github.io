whelp

verb
give birth to puppies (animal verb)
7.9
Pronunciation: / wɛlp / (AmE), / wɛlp / (BrE)
(of a female dog or other carnivorous mammal) to give birth to young, especially puppies.

Examples

To whelp puppies
She whelped four adorable puppies last spring.
The old shepherd watched as his prized collie whelped a healthy litter of pups.
The veterinarian assisted while the fox whelped in the den.
