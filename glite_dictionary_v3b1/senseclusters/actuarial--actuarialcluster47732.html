actuarial

adjective
relating to actuaries or risk assessment
3.7 6.5
Pronunciation: /æk.tʃuː.ˈer.iː.əl/ (AmE), /æk.tʃuː.ˈeə.riː.əl/ (BrE)
relating to the work, methods, or calculations of actuaries, especially for assessing risk in insurance and finance.

Examples

Actuarial science
She is studying actuarial science at university to become an actuary.
The company relied on actuarial data to determine the costs of its new health plan.
The insurer used complex actuarial models to predict life expectancy and set premiums.
