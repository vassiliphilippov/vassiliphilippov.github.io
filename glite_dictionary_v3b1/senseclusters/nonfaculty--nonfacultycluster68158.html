nonfaculty

adjective
not teaching staff
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈfækəlti / (AmE), / nɒnˈfækəlti / (BrE)
not belonging to the teaching staff of a school or university

Examples

Nonfaculty staff members
The university provides parking permits for nonfaculty employees as well as professors.
Many nonfaculty members work in administration, maintenance, or student services.
The new policy affects both nonfaculty and faculty staff at the college.

Related Terms

  • antonym: faculty - the teaching staff at a school or university
  • synonym: staff - all employees of an organization, including non-teaching roles
