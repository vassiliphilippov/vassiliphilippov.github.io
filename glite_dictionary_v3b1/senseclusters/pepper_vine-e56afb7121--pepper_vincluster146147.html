pepper vine

noun
a shrubby or climbing plant of genus Piper
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpər vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɛpə vaɪn / (BrE)
any of several shrubby or climbing plants of the genus Piper, including varieties used for culinary spices or ornamental purposes.
