West Pakistan

proper noun
former western region of Pakistan (1947–1971)
6.1
Pronunciation: / wɛst pəˈkɪstæn / (AmE), / west pəˈkɪstæn / (BrE)
The western provincial region of Pakistan from 1947 to 1971, constituting the territory now known as Pakistan after the separation of East Pakistan (now Bangladesh).

Examples

West Pakistan region
Before 1971, West Pakistan and East Pakistan were separated by more than a thousand miles of Indian territory.
Many government offices were headquartered in West Pakistan during its existence.
The history of West Pakistan is crucial to understanding the creation of modern Bangladesh.
