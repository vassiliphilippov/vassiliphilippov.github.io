fix-up

noun
small repair
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪksˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈfɪksˌʌp / (BrE)
a small repair or improvement made to something, especially to make it more presentable or functional

Examples

Quick fix-up
We gave the kitchen a quick fix-up before our guests arrived.
The car only needed a minor fix-up to pass inspection.
After a weekend fix-up, the apartment looked much better.
