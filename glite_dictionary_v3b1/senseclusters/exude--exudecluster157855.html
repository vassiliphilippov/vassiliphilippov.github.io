exude

verb
show (a quality or feeling) openly
3.1 5.2
Pronunciation: /ɪg.ˈzuːd/ /; / /ɪks.ˈuːd/ (AmE), /ɪg.ˈzjuːd/ /; / /ɪg.ˈzuːd/ (BrE)
(of a person) to display a quality or feeling strongly and openly through mood, behavior, or appearance.

Examples

Exude confidence
He exudes warmth and kindness whenever he speaks to his colleagues.
She exudes great confidence even in challenging situations.
The team exuded enthusiasm throughout the project.
