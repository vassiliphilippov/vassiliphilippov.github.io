holoku

noun
Hawaiian formal dress
9.9
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈloʊkuː / (AmE), / həʊˈlɒkuː / (BrE)
a long, loose Hawaiian dress worn by women, especially for formal occasions

Examples

A holoku dress
She wore a beautiful white holoku to the wedding ceremony.
The traditional holoku is often made from fine fabrics and decorated with lace.
During the festival, many women dressed in a holoku to celebrate their heritage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: muumuu - a loose, brightly colored Hawaiian dress
