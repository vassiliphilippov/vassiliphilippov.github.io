inversion

noun
act of turning upside down
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɜrʒən / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɜːʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of turning something upside down, inside out, or reversing its normal position, order, or direction

Examples

A sudden inversion
A sudden inversion of the cake left it scattered all over the floor.
During yoga, certain poses involve the inversion of the body.
The inversion of the bottle made it easier to pour out the last drops.
Doctors performed an inversion of the eyelid to check for infection.
Meteorologists studied the inversion of air layers in the atmosphere.
The inversion of the bag revealed its hidden contents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reversal - the act of reversing or the state of being reversed
  • antonym: upright - in a vertical position; not inverted
