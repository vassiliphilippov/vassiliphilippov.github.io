Saint Crispin

proper noun
patron saint of shoemakers
9.9
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈkrɪspɪn / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈkrɪspɪn / (BrE)
A Christian saint and the patron saint of shoemakers, martyred with his brother for trying to spread Christianity in the 3rd century.

Examples

Saint Crispin the shoemakers
He referenced Saint Crispin in his speech to honor the dedication of craftsmen.
The festival of Saint Crispin is still celebrated by shoemakers in some countries.
The legend of Saint Crispin inspired many in the leatherworking trades.
