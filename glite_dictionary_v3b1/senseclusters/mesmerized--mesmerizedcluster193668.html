mesmerized

adjective
fascinated; held spellbound
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛzməˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈmɛzməraɪzd / (BrE)
Having one's attention completely absorbed or transfixed, as if under a spell.

Examples

To be mesmerized by flames
He became mesmerized by the flickering flames in the fireplace.
She was mesmerized by the dancer's graceful movements on stage.
The children sat mesmerized as the magician pulled a rabbit from his hat.
He watched the flames, completely mesmerised by their flickering shapes.
She sat mesmerised by the dancer's graceful movements.
The audience was mesmerised throughout the entire performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bored - feeling weary and uninterested
  • synonym: entranced - so captivated as to be unaware of surroundings
  • synonym: fascinated - intensely interested or absorbed
  • synonym: spellbound - held as if by a magic spell
