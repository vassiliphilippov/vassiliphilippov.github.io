zoanthropy

noun
delusion of being an animal
Pronunciation: / zoʊˈænθrəpi / (AmE), / zəʊˈænθrəpi / (BrE)
a rare psychological condition in which a person believes they have turned into an animal

Examples

Zoanthropy disorder
Cases of zoanthropy are extremely rare, but have been documented throughout history.
He exhibited signs of zoanthropy, insisting he should eat and behave like a horse.
The patient was diagnosed with zoanthropy after claiming she had become a cat.
