front yard

noun
area of land before a house
2.5
Pronunciation: / frʌnt jɑrd / (AmE), / frʌnt jɑːd / (BrE)
the area of land between the front of a house and the street, usually belonging to the house

Examples

The front yard grass
Children were playing catch in their front yard after school.
The front yard was neatly mowed and decorated with garden gnomes.
We planted new flowers in the front yard last weekend.
