caper

verb
leap or jump about playfully
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪ.pɚ / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.pə / (BrE)

Examples

To caper around

She couldn't help capering with laughter after hearing the good news.
The children began to caper around the playground, full of excitement.
