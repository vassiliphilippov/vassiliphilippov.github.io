leg bone

noun
bone in the leg
4.8 2.7
Pronunciation: / lɛɡ boʊn / (AmE), / lɛɡ bəʊn / (BrE)
any of the bones forming the human or animal leg, such as the femur, tibia, or fibula.

Examples

A leg bone fossil
Paleontologists discovered a fossilized leg bone belonging to a prehistoric animal.
She injured a leg bone during the soccer match and needed a cast.
The X-ray revealed a fracture in his leg bone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: femur - the bone of the thigh or upper hind limb
  • synonym: fibula - the outer and usually smaller bone of the leg between the knee and the ankle
  • synonym: tibia - the inner and usually larger bone of the leg between the knee and the ankle
