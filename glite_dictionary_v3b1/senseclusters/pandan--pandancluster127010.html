pandan

noun
aromatic flavoring extract
Pronunciation: / ˈpændæn / (AmE), / ˈpændæn / (BrE)
a fragrant green extract or concentrate made from pandan leaves, used as a flavoring and coloring in food and drinks

Examples

Pandan flavor in cake
The chef used pandan to give the cake a unique aroma and bright green color.
In Southeast Asian desserts, pandan is a common ingredient for both flavor and color.
She bought a bottle of pandan extract to try making traditional rice cakes at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: matcha - a green tea powder used as a flavoring and coloring
  • synonym: vanilla - a popular flavoring extract from vanilla beans
