Brahui

noun
Dravidian language of Pakistan
Pronunciation: / brəˈhuː.i / (AmE), / brəˈhuː.i / (BrE)
an isolated Dravidian language spoken by the Brahui people in Pakistan and nearby regions

Examples

Brahui language
