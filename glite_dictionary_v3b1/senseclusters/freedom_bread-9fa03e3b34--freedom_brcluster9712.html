freedom bread

noun
bread symbolizing freedom or liberation
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːdəm brɛd / (AmE), / ˈfriːdəm brɛd / (BrE)
A type of bread symbolizing freedom or liberation, often baked or served during celebrations with symbolic significance.

Examples

A slice of freedom bread
A slice of freedom bread was placed at each setting during the liberation feast.
During the independence ceremony, families brought freedom bread to share with the community.
The tradition of baking freedom bread dates back to times of political upheaval.
