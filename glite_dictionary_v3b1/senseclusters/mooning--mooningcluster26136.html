mooning

noun
romantic daydreaming
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmuː.nɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈmuː.nɪŋ/ (BrE)
a state of dreamy or lovesick longing, often involving romantic daydreaming or yearning for someone.

Examples

Mooning over a crush
Her constant mooning over her crush distracted her from her studies.
He spent the afternoon mooning by the window, lost in thoughts of his first love.
Friends teased him about his mooning after the girl who moved away.
