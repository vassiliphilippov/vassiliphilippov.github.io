hopper

noun
plant-eating insect that jumps
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːpər / (AmE), / ˈhɒpə / (BrE)
a small plant-eating insect, especially in its immature stage, with long hind legs adapted for jumping

Examples

A green hopper
A tiny green hopper landed on my arm while I sat in the garden.
Birds often feed on hoppers during the summer months.
The field was filled with hoppers leaping from blade to blade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grasshopper - a plant-eating insect with long hind legs for jumping
  • synonym: locust - a migratory grasshopper-like insect
