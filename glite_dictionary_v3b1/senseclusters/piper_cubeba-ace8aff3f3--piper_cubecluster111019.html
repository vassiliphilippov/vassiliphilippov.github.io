Piper cubeba

noun
spicy fruit-bearing tropical vine
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪpər kjuːˈbiːbə / (AmE), / ˈpaɪpə kjuːˈbiːbə / (BrE)
A tropical Southeast Asian shrubby vine, also known as cubeb, that bears pungent, pepper-like fruits used as a spice and in traditional medicine.

Examples

Piper cubeba spice
Historically, Piper cubeba was traded from Java to the Middle East and Europe as a valuable spice.
Some traditional remedies include Piper cubeba fruits to help treat respiratory problems.
The essential oil extracted from Piper cubeba is prized in both culinary and medicinal applications.
