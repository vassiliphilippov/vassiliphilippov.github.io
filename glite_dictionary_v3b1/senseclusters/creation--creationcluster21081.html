creation

noun
the act of making something
2.3
Pronunciation: / kriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / kriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of bringing something into existence, inventing, or starting something new

Examples

The creation of a company
Every novel is a unique creation of its author.
The artist's latest creation was unveiled at the gallery opening.
Through careful planning and teamwork, the creation of the new company became possible.
Her creation of a new recipe impressed all her friends.
The creation of the charity marked a turning point in the community.
The company's creation of a new division was announced last week.
Many companies struggled in the years following their creation.
The creation of the law marked a turning point for citizens.
The new foundation was managed carefully from its creation.

  • synonym: inception - the beginning of something, especially an institution or activity
  • synonym: initiation - the action of beginning something or admitting someone to a group
  • synonym: invention - the creation of something for the first time as a product of one's own ingenuity
  • synonym: origination - the process or fact of originating or being originated
