merge

verb
to combine or unite into one
Pronunciation: / mɜrdʒ /; / mɝːdʒ /; / mɝdʒ / (AmE), / mɜːdʒ / (BrE)
to combine or join two or more things so that they become one

Several rivers merge at this delta before flowing into the sea.
The traffic lanes slowly merge into one as you enter the tunnel.
Her ideas merged seamlessly with those of the other team members.
The colors on the canvas merge to create a striking sunset effect.
The two rivers merge just south of the city.
