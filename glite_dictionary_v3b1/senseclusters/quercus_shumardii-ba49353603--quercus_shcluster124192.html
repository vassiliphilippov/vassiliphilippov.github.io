Quercus shumardii

noun
a species of red oak tree in the U.S.
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɜrkəs ʃuːˈmɑrdi / (AmE), / ˈkwɜːkəs ʃuːˈmɑːdiː / (BrE)
A large deciduous red oak native to the southern and eastern United States, characterized by large elliptical leaves with seven to nine lobes, large acorns, and medium hard, coarse-grained wood.
