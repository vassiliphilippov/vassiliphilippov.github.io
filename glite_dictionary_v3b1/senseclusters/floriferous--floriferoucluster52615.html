floriferous

adjective
abundant in flowers
Pronunciation: / flɔˈrɪfərəs / (AmE), / flɒˈrɪfərəs / (BrE)
producing many flowers or bearing flowers abundantly

Examples

Floriferous garden
The floriferous meadow was covered in colorful blossoms throughout the spring.
Gardeners often choose floriferous plants to create a vibrant and lively landscape.
This floriferous variety of rose blooms several times each season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barren - not producing flowers or fruit
  • synonym: flowering - producing flowers; in bloom
