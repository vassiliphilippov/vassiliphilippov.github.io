rain out

verb
have an event canceled or stopped due to rain
7.1 3.8
Pronunciation: /reɪn aʊt/ (AmE), /reɪn aʊt/ (BrE)
(of rain) to cause an event, especially a sports game, to be cancelled or interrupted.

Examples

To rain out a game
Our picnic plans were rained out at the last minute.
The baseball game was rained out due to a heavy downpour.
They had to rain out the concert after the storm started.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wash out - to cause an event to be canceled due to heavy rain or flooding
