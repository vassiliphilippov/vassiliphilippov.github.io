so

adverb
subsequently; soon afterward
Pronunciation: / soʊ / (AmE), / səʊ / (BrE)
used to introduce a result, consequence, or logical conclusion

Examples

So it rained
She read her book for a while, so she fell asleep.
The show ended, so everyone started leaving the theater.
We finished our dinner and so went out for a walk.
It started to rain, so we decided to stay indoors.
She didn’t study hard, so she didn’t pass the exam.
The store was closed, so he went home empty-handed.

  • synonym: therefore - for that reason; consequently
