polyclonal

adjective
from many cell types
8.0
Pronunciation: /pɑː.li.ˈkloʊ.nəl/ (AmE), /pɒl.ɪ.ˈkləʊ.nəl/ (BrE)
consisting of or derived from many different types of cells or antibodies, rather than a single type

Examples

Polyclonal antibodies
The laboratory produced polyclonal antibodies to help detect a wide range of viruses.
A polyclonal response means the immune system is using many different cells to fight the infection.
Researchers compared the effects of polyclonal and monoclonal antibodies in the experiment.
