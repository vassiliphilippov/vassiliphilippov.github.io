White Russian

noun
a cocktail with vodka, coffee liqueur, and cream
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌwaɪt ˈrʌʃən / (AmE), / ˌwaɪt ˈrʌʃən / (BrE)
a cocktail consisting of vodka, coffee liqueur, and milk or cream, usually served over ice

Examples

A White Russian cocktail

She brought a tray of White Russians to the guests during the party.
The recipe for a classic White Russian calls for vodka, Kahlúa, and cream.
