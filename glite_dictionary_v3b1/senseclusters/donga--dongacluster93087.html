donga

noun
temporary shelter
Pronunciation: / ˈdɒŋə / (AmE), / ˈdɒŋə / (BrE)
a prefabricated or temporary shelter, cabin, or portable building used especially on remote work sites

Examples

A donga cabin
The construction crew stayed in a donga while working on the remote highway project.
Each donga was equipped with basic beds and a small heater for the cold nights.
After a long day, the workers returned to their donga to rest and eat dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barracks - a building or group of buildings used to house workers or soldiers
  • synonym: cabin - a small, simple house or shelter
