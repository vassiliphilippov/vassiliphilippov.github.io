mother country

noun
country of one's birth or origin
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌðɚ ˌkʌntri / (AmE), / ˈmʌðə ˌkʌntri / (BrE)
the country in which a person was born or with which a person feels a strong national connection

Examples

Return to the mother country
After living abroad for several years, she returned to her mother country.
Immigrants often have strong emotional ties to their mother country.
The traditions of my mother country continue to influence my daily life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fatherland - one's native country (often used in patriotic contexts)
  • synonym: homeland - a person's native land
