reregulate

verb
to restore regulation
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈrɛɡjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈrɛɡjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to adjust or restore the regulation or control of a machine, device, system, or process to return it to proper operation

Examples

To reregulate a machine
The engineer needed to reregulate the system after the unexpected power surge.
If the temperature fluctuates too much, you may have to reregulate the thermostat.
After repairs, the technician carefully reregulated the device to ensure it worked correctly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: destabilize - to make something less stable or controlled
  • synonym: readjust - to adjust again or differently
  • synonym: regulate - to control or maintain the operation of a process or system
