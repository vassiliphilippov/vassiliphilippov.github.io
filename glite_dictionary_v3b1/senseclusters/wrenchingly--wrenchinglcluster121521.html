wrenchingly

adverb
with emotional pain
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛntʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈrɛntʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes strong emotional pain or distress

Examples

Wrenchingly honest story
The story was wrenchingly honest about the struggles faced by the main character.
She spoke wrenchingly about her experiences during the war.
The letter was wrenchingly sad, revealing the depth of his loneliness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comfortingly - in a way that provides comfort or relief
  • synonym: painfully - in a way that causes pain or distress
