gasometer

noun
instrument for measuring gas flow
Pronunciation: / ɡæˈsɑːmətər / (AmE), / ɡəˈsɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument for measuring the amount of gas passing through a pipe

Examples

Gasometer reading
A faulty gasometer can lead to incorrect billing for gas usage.
Regular maintenance of the gasometer ensures accurate gas measurements.
The engineer used a gasometer to monitor the flow rate in the pipeline.
