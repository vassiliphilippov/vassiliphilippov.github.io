feature

verb
to include as a prominent part
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːtʃɚ /; / ˈfiːtʃər / (AmE), / ˈfiːtʃə / (BrE)
to show, include, or highlight something as important or noticeable

Examples

To feature a guest
The film festival features works by directors from around the world.
The new museum features interactive exhibits for children and adults alike.
Tonight's concert will feature several up-and-coming artists from the local area.
He often features bold ties to make a statement at work.
She loves to feature her vintage handbag at every party.
The singer featured a dazzling diamond necklace on the red carpet last night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exclude - to not include something
  • synonym: include - to contain as part of the whole
  • synonym: sport - to wear or display (something) in a way that attracts attention
