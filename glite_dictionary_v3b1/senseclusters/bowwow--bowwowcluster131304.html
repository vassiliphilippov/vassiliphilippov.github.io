bowwow

noun
dog's bark sound
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊˌwaʊ / (AmE), / ˈbaʊˌwaʊ / (BrE)
an imitative word for the sound made by a dog when barking

Examples

A happy bowwow
The puppy greeted us with a cheerful bowwow as we entered the house.
Children often imitate a dog's bowwow when playing with their toys.
We heard a loud bowwow coming from the neighbor's yard late at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bark - the sharp sound made by a dog
  • synonym: woof - the sound a dog makes when barking
