Io

proper noun
maiden in Greek mythology transformed by Zeus
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, a maiden loved by Zeus who was transformed into a white heifer to hide her from Hera.

Examples

Mythical maiden Io
In Ovid's 'Metamorphoses', Io is transformed into a heifer to escape Hera's wrath.
Many ancient sculptures and paintings depict Io alongside a white cow.
The story of Io illustrates the jealousy of Hera and the cunning of Zeus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Europa - another maiden in Greek mythology seduced by Zeus
  • synonym: Zeus - king of the gods in Greek mythology
