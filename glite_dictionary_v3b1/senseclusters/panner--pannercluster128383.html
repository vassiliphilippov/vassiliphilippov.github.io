panner

noun
camera operator
Pronunciation: / ˈpænər / (AmE), / ˈpænə / (BrE)
a person who moves a camera horizontally to follow action or capture a wide scene

Examples

Camera panner operator
The panner skillfully tracked the actor as she walked across the stage.
During the live broadcast, the panner kept the moving players in the frame.
A good panner can make action scenes look smooth and dynamic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cameraman - a person who operates a camera
  • antonym: director - a person who supervises the making of a film or broadcast
