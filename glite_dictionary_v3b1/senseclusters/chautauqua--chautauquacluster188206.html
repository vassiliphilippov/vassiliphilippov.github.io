chautauqua

noun
educational community event
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈtɔkwə / (AmE), / ʃəˈtɔːkwə / (BrE)
a community event, especially in the U.S., featuring lectures, music, and cultural programs for public education and entertainment

Examples

A chautauqua event
The town hosted a week-long chautauqua with lectures, concerts, and art workshops for all ages.
Many people traveled from nearby cities to attend the annual chautauqua in the park.
The chautauqua brought together educators, musicians, and community members for a celebration of learning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: festival - a series of performances or events, often cultural or artistic
  • synonym: seminar - a meeting for discussion or training on a particular topic
