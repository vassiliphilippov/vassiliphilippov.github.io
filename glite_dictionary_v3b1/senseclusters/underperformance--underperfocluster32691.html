underperformance

noun
below expected results
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərpərˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / ˌʌndəpəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
the act or situation of performing below the expected or usual standard or level (can refer to people, organizations, or financial assets)
