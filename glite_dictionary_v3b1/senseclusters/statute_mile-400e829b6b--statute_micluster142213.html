statute mile

noun
British/American mile, 1,609.344 meters

Pronunciation: /ˈstæt.ʃuːt maɪl/ /; / /ˈstæt.juːt maɪl/ (AmE), /ˈstæt.juːt maɪl/ /; / /ˈstæt.ʃuːt maɪl/ (BrE)
