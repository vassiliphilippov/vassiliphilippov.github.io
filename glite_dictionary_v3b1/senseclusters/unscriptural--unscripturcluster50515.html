unscriptural

adjective
not in scripture
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskrɪptʃərəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈskrɪptʃərəl / (BrE)
not supported by or not in accordance with religious scripture

Examples

Unscripural beliefs
Many church leaders argued that the new tradition was unscriptural and should be avoided.
He refused to accept any teaching he considered unscriptural.
The debate centered on whether the practice was unscriptural or had biblical support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unbiblical - not in accordance with the Bible
