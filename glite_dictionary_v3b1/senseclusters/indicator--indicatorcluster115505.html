indicator

noun
sign or signal
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪkeɪtə / (BrE)
something that shows, points out, or gives information about a condition or fact

Examples

Indicator of change
The color change is an indicator of a chemical reaction.
Rising unemployment is often seen as an indicator of economic problems.
The fuel gauge acts as an indicator of how much gas is left in the tank.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evidence - facts or signs that show something exists or is true
  • synonym: sign - something that shows that something exists or is happening
  • synonym: signal - an action or event that shows what someone feels or what is happening
