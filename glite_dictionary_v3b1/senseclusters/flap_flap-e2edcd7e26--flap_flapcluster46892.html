flap flap

noun
sound/action of flapping wings
Pronunciation: / flæp flæp / (AmE), / flæp flæp / (BrE)
the repeated sound or action made by flapping wings

Examples

The flap flap sound
The bird’s sudden flap flap startled the quiet forest.
The only sound in the cavern was the persistent flap flap of bat wings overhead.
We heard a gentle flap flap as the heron took flight from the riverbank.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flap - a movement or sound made by something broad and flat, especially wings
