Oprah

proper noun
Oprah Winfrey's media brand
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊprə / (AmE), / ˈəʊprə / (BrE)
the television talk show hosted by Oprah Winfrey, and by extension, the media brand associated with her, including the show, network, and magazine

Examples

The Oprah show
Millions tuned in every week to watch Oprah interview actors, politicians, and authors.
The Oprah brand expanded to include a magazine and a television network.
Winning a spot on Oprah could turn an unknown author into a bestseller overnight.
