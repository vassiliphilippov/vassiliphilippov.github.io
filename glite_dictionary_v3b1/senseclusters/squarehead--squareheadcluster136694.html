squarehead

noun
square-shaped fastener head
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɛrˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈskweəˌhɛd / (BrE)
a bolt, screw, or fastener with a head shaped like a square for turning with a wrench or tool.

Examples

Squarehead bolt
The mechanic used a wrench to loosen the squarehead on the old machine.

The heavy gate was held in place by a large squarehead bolt.
