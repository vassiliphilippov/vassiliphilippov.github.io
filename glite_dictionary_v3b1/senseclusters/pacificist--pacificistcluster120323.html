pacificist

noun
person opposed to violence
6.6 6.5
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈsɪf.ɪ.sɪst/ (AmE), /pə.ˈsɪf.ɪ.sɪst/ (BrE)
a person who opposes violence as a way of resolving conflicts or disputes

Examples

A pacificist person
As a self-declared pacificist, she refused to participate in any violent protests.
He was criticized for his pacificist beliefs during a time when many were calling for military action.
The group, mostly pacificists, advocated for peaceful negotiations between the two sides.
