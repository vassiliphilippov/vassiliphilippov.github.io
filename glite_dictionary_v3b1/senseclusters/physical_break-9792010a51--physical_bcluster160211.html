physical break

noun
pause for physical activity or rest
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzɪkəl breɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl breɪk / (BrE)
a short pause from work or activity taken for physical movement, exercise, or rest

Examples

Take a physical break
