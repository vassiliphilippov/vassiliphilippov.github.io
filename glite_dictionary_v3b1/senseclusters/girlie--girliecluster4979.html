girlie

noun
playful term for girl
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɜːrli / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːli / (BrE)
a young girl or a female friend, used in a playful or affectionate way

Examples

My girlie friends
She invited all her girlies to the birthday party.
The girlies spent the afternoon laughing and sharing stories at the café.
Every summer, the girlies plan a weekend trip together.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boy - a male child or young man
  • synonym: gal - an informal term for a girl or young woman
