nomarch

noun
Greek regional governor
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈnəʊˌmɑːk / (BrE)
the elected or appointed governor of a prefecture (nomos) in modern Greece, serving as the chief local administrative official

Examples

Greek nomarch
The nomarch announced emergency measures after the earthquake damaged several towns in the prefecture.
Each nomarch is responsible for implementing national policies at the local level in Greece.
After the election, the new nomarch promised to improve public services in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mayor - the elected head of a city or town
