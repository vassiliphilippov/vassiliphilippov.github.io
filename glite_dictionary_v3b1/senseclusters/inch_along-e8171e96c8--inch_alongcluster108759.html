inch along

verb
move slowly and carefully
7.5 2.9
Pronunciation: /ɪntʃ ə.ˈlɔːŋ/ (AmE), /ɪntʃ ə.ˈlɒŋ/ (BrE)
to move very slowly and carefully, usually by small amounts at a time

Examples

To inch along slowly
The line outside the store began to inch along as the doors finally opened.
We had to inch along the icy sidewalk to avoid slipping.
With the traffic jam, we could only inch along for miles.

Related Terms
