religious ceremony

noun
a formal ritual with religious meaning
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈsɛrɪməni / (BrE)
a formal event or ritual performed as part of a religion

Examples
