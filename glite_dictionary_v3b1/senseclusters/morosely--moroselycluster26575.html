morosely

adverb
in a gloomy or sullen manner
6.8
Pronunciation: / məˈroʊsli / (AmE), / məˈrəʊsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a gloomy or sullen mood; in a morose manner.

Examples

To morosely stare
He stared morosely out of the window after hearing the bad news.
She morosely shuffled her papers, clearly upset by the situation.
The dog sat morosely by the door, waiting for its owner to return.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy or joyful manner
  • synonym: gloomily - in a sad or depressed manner
