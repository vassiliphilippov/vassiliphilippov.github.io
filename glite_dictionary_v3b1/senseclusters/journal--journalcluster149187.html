journal

verb
to write in a diary
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɝːnəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːnəl / (BrE)
to write or record personal experiences, thoughts, or events in a journal or diary

Examples

To journal daily events
Every night, she journals about her day before going to bed.
He started to journal regularly to help process his thoughts and feelings.
Many people find it helpful to journal during stressful times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diary - a daily written record of personal experiences
  • antonym: forget - to fail to remember or record
  • synonym: record - to write down information or events
