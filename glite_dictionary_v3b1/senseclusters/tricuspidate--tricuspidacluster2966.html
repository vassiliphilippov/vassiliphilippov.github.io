tricuspidate

adjective
having three cusps or points
Pronunciation: / traɪˈkʌspɪˌdeɪt / (AmE), / traɪˈkʌspɪdeɪt / (BrE)
having three cusps or points, especially describing teeth or valves

Examples

Tricuspidate leaf shape
Certain plant seeds have a tricuspidate shape, aiding in their dispersal.
In mammals, the tricuspidate valve separates the right atrium and right ventricle.
The dentist noted that the patient's tricuspidate molar was in excellent condition.
