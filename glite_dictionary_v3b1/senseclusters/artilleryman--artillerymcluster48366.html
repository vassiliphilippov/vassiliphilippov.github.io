artilleryman

noun
soldier serving artillery weapons
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪləriˌmæn / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪl.ər.i.mən / (BrE)
A soldier who operates or serves artillery weapons in the military.

Examples

An artilleryman fires guns
After years of service, he became the lead artilleryman in his regiment.
The army recognized the bravery of every artilleryman stationed on the front line.
The experienced artilleryman quickly adjusted the cannon's aim during the battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gunner - a member of the military trained to operate artillery guns
