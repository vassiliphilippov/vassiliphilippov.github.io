glasses

noun
lenses in a frame for vision correction
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsɪz / (BrE)
A pair of lenses set in a frame and worn in front of the eyes to correct defective vision.

Examples

Reading glasses
His glasses fell off during the basketball game and broke.
I can't see the board without my glasses on.
She put on her glasses so she could read the tiny print.
