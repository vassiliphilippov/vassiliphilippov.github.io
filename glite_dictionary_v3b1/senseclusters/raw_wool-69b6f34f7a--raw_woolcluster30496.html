raw wool

noun
unprocessed wool; natural state
Pronunciation: / rɔ wʊl / (AmE), / rɔː wʊl / (BrE)
Wool in its natural state before it has been refined or processed.

Examples

Raw wool exports
Exports of raw wool are an important part of the country's economy.
She learned how to spin raw wool into yarn by hand.
The factory purchases raw wool directly from local farmers before beginning the cleaning process.

Related Terms

  • antonym: processed wool - wool that has been cleaned and refined
