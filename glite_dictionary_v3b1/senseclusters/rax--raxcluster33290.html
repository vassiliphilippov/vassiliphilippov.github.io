rax

noun
drying or storage rack
9.9
Pronunciation: / ræks / (AmE), / ræks / (BrE)
a frame or rack used for drying or storing items such as clothes, herbs, or hay, especially in dialect or archaic usage

Examples

A clothes rax
They placed the freshly washed linens on the rax to dry in the warm breeze.
The old cottage still had a wooden rax hanging above the fireplace for storing herbs.
Farmers would use a rax to keep hay off the damp ground during rainy seasons.
