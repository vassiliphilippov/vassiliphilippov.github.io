flighting

noun
short bursts of flight
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the action of flying in short flights or bursts, especially by birds or insects

Examples

Bird flighting
The sudden flighting of sparrows startled the children playing nearby.
We watched the flighting of butterflies as they moved from flower to flower.
At dusk, the flighting of bats was visible against the evening sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fluttering - moving or flying with quick, light movements
  • antonym: soaring - the act of flying or rising high in the air for a long time
