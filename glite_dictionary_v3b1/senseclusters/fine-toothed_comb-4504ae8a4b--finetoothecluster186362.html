fine-toothed comb

noun
careful, detailed examination
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪn ˌtuðt ˈkoʊm / (AmE), / ˈfaɪn ˌtuːθt ˈkəʊm / (BrE)
a method or process of examining something in minute detail, especially to find errors or problems

Examples

Check with a fine-toothed comb
Every application is reviewed with a fine-toothed comb before it is accepted.
She read over the manuscript with a fine-toothed comb, searching for any spelling or grammatical mistakes.
The auditors examined the company's accounts with a fine-toothed comb to ensure there were no discrepancies.
