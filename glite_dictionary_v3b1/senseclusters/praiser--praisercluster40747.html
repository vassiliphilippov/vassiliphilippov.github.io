praiser

noun
person who praises
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpreɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈpreɪ.zə/ (BrE)
a person who expresses approval or admiration for someone or something, especially in a general or public way

Examples

A generous praiser
As a generous praiser, she always encouraged her friends to do their best.
The teacher became known as a praiser of students' creativity and hard work.
He was never a harsh critic, but rather a constant praiser of others' achievements.
The mayor thanked the praiser for his kind words during the award ceremony.
As a well-known praiser of local artists, she was invited to speak at the gallery opening.
The praiser delivered a heartfelt speech honoring the achievements of the retiring teacher.

Related Terms

  • synonym: admirer - a person who admires someone or something
  • antonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or finds fault
  • synonym: panegyrist - a person who writes or delivers formal praise
