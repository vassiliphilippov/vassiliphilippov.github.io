pilon

noun
lower tibia in ankle
Pronunciation: / piːˈlɒn / (AmE), / ˈpiːlɒn / (BrE)
the lower end of the tibia that forms part of the ankle joint, often discussed in orthopaedics

Examples

Pilon fracture treatment
The surgeon explained that the pilon was fractured and would need careful reconstruction.
A severe injury to the pilon can affect the stability of the ankle joint.
After the accident, X-rays revealed damage to the pilon requiring immediate medical attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ankle joint - the joint connecting the foot with the leg
  • synonym: tibia - the larger bone of the lower leg, also called the shin bone
