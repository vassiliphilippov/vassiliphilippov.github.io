response

noun
result produced by an action or event
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
a result or outcome produced by a particular action, event, or situation

Examples

A direct response
His statement came as a direct response to the allegations made earlier in the week.
The company's profits increased in response to changes in consumer demand.
The new policy was introduced in response to growing environmental concerns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reaction - an action performed or a feeling experienced in response to a situation or event
  • synonym: result - the outcome of an action or process
