night watcher

noun
nighttime guard or watchman
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪt ˌwɑtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt ˌwɒtʃə / (BrE)
A person employed to keep watch or guard during the nighttime hours.

Examples

A night watcher on duty
After the incident, a night watcher was hired to ensure the building's security.
During his shift as a night watcher, he often listened to the quiet sounds of the sleeping city.
The night watcher made his rounds through the empty corridors every hour.
