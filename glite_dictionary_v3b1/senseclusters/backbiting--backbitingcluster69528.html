backbiting

noun
gossiping negatively
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌbaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbækˌbaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of saying unpleasant or harmful things about someone who is not present

Examples

Office backbiting
Constant backbiting among colleagues can create a toxic work environment.
She was hurt when she discovered the backbiting going on behind her back.
Leaders should address backbiting quickly to maintain team trust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gossip - casual talk about other people's private lives
  • antonym: praise - express approval or admiration for someone
  • synonym: slander - false spoken statements that damage someone's reputation
