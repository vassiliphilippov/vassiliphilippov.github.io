ad interim

adverb
temporarily; for the time being
Pronunciation: / æd ˈɪntəˌrɪm / (AmE), / ad ˈɪntərɪm / (BrE)
for the intervening time; temporarily

Examples

Ad interim period
Rules established ad interim will be reviewed at the end of the year.
She was appointed ad interim manager until a permanent replacement could be found.
The president named an ad interim treasurer to oversee financial matters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: temporary - lasting for a limited time
