ratio analysis

noun
evaluation of financial relationships using ratios
1.0 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈreɪ.ʃoʊ ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ /; / /ˈreɪ.ʃɪ.oʊ ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ (AmE), /ˈreɪ.ʃəʊ ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ /; / /ˈreɪ.ʃɪ.əʊ ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ (BrE)
the examination of the relationships between different financial figures in order to assess an organization's performance, often by comparing them with past results or with other firms.

Examples
