seed

noun
male reproductive fluid (semen)
Pronunciation: / sid / (AmE), / siːd / (BrE)
the whitish liquid produced by male animals, containing sperm that can fertilize an egg; semen

Examples

Human seed
In older texts, seed is often used to refer to a man's progeny or descendants.
Some ancient rituals symbolically honored the seed as a source of fertility.
The poet used the word seed as a metaphor for the continuation of life.
