open arms

noun
warm, enthusiastic welcome
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpən ɑrmz / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən ɑːmz / (BrE)
in a way that is very welcoming and enthusiastic; with warmth and eagerness to receive someone or something

Examples

With open arms
She was unsure at first, but the team accepted her with open arms.
The company received the new partnership proposal with open arms.
The villagers greeted the returning soldiers with open arms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: warm welcome - a greeting or reception that is very friendly and enthusiastic
