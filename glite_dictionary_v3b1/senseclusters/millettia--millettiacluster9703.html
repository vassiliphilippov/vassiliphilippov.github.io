millettia

noun
a genus of tropical trees/shrubs with dark streaked wood
Pronunciation: / mɪˈlɛtiə / (AmE), / mɪˈlɛtɪə / (BrE)
Any of several tropical trees or shrubs of the genus Millettia, known for their showy, streaked, dark reddish or chocolate-colored wood.
