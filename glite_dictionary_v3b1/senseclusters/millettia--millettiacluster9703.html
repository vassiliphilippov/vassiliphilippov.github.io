millettia

noun
a genus of tropical trees/shrubs with dark streaked wood
Pronunciation: / mɪˈlɛtiə / (AmE), / mɪˈlɛtɪə / (BrE)
Any of several tropical trees or shrubs of the genus Millettia, known for their showy, streaked, dark reddish or chocolate-colored wood.

Examples

millettia tree wood
Many millettia species are valued for both their timber and ornamental flowers.
Some musical instruments are crafted from the dense, streaked wood of millettia trees.
The craftsman used millettia wood to make a beautiful, dark-hued cabinet.
