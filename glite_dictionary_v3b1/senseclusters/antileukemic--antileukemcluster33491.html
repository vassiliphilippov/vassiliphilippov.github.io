antileukemic

noun
leukemia treatment agent
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌluːˈkimɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌluːˈkiːmɪk / (BrE)
a drug or agent used to prevent or treat leukemia

Examples

An antileukemic drug

Researchers are developing a new antileukemic that targets cancer cells more effectively.
Some children respond well to the latest antileukemic drugs during treatment.
