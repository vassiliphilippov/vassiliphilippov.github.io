whites

noun
brightest image areas
3.4
Pronunciation: /waɪts/ /; / /hwaɪts/ (AmE), /waɪts/ /; / /hwaɪts/ (BrE)
the lightest, brightest areas or tones in a photograph or image, close to pure white

Examples

Adjust the whites
Photographers often adjust the whites to make sure the image looks natural and balanced.
If the whites are too bright, important details in the photo can be lost.
Editing software allows you to control the whites separately from the midtones and shadows.
