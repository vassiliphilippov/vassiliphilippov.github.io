rammer

noun
compacting tool
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈræmər / (AmE), / ˈræmə / (BrE)
a tool or machine used to pack or compact material tightly, especially soil or concrete

Examples

Soil rammer
The construction crew used a rammer to compact the gravel before pouring concrete.
He bought a new rammer to make his landscaping work easier and more efficient.
Before laying the bricks, she made sure the soil was firm by using a rammer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compactor - a machine that compresses or compacts material
  • synonym: tamper - a tool used to press or pack down material
