pathophysiology

noun
study of disease effects
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpæθoʊˌfɪziˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌpæθəʊˌfɪziˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of how diseases or injuries change the normal functions and processes of the body
