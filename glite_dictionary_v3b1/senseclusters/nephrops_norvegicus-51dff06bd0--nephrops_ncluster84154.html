Nephrops norvegicus

noun
edible European lobster species
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɛfrɑps nɔrˈvɛdʒɪkəs / (AmE), / ˌnɛfrɒps nɔːˈvɛdʒɪkəs / (BrE)
