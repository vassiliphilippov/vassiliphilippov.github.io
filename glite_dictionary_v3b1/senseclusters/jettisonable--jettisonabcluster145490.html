jettisonable

adjective
able to be discarded
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛtɪsənəbl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛtɪsənəbl / (BrE)
able to be released, discarded, or dropped, especially from a vehicle or aircraft during operation

Examples

Jettisonable fuel tanks
The aircraft was equipped with jettisonable fuel tanks for emergency situations.
Engineers designed the satellite with a jettisonable cover to protect it during launch.
The escape pod is jettisonable in case of a critical system failure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixed - firmly attached and not able to be removed
