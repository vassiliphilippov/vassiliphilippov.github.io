deoxycytidine monophosphate

noun
a nucleotide in DNA, also called dCMP
9.8
Pronunciation: / diˌɑksɪˈsaɪtədin ˌmɑnəˌfɑsˌfeɪt / (AmE), / diˌɒksɪˈsɪtɪdiːn ˌmɒnəˌfɒsˌfeɪt / (BrE)
a nucleotide that is one of the four building blocks of DNA, consisting of a deoxycytidine molecule bound to a single phosphate group; also called deoxycytidylate or dCMP
