varimax

verb
to rotate factor loadings
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛrɪˌmæks / (AmE), / ˈvɑːrɪˌmæks / (BrE)
to apply the varimax rotation to factor or component loadings to make them easier to interpret in statistical analysis

Examples

To varimax the factors
The researchers decided to varimax the factors to simplify the interpretation of their results.
Before presenting the findings, the team varimaxed the component loadings for greater clarity.
It is common practice to varimax the data in exploratory factor analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orthogonal rotation - a type of rotation in factor analysis that keeps factors uncorrelated
  • synonym: rotate - to turn or adjust the orientation of something
