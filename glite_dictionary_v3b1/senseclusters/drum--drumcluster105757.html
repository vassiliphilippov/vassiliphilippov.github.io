drum

noun
musical percussion instrument with stretched membrane
Pronunciation: / drʌm / (AmE), / drʌm / (BrE)
a musical percussion instrument made of a hollow cylinder with a membrane stretched tightly across one or both ends, played by striking with the hands or sticks

Examples

Play the drum
He played the drum in the school band during the concert.
She tapped the drum lightly to create a soft rhythm for the song.
The sound of the drum echoed through the hall as the parade marched by.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drumstick - stick used to play a drum
  • synonym: tambourine - a percussion instrument with a drumhead and jingles
  • synonym: xylophone - musical percussion instrument with wooden bars
