avalanche

noun
sudden overwhelming quantity or influx
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈæv.əˌlæntʃ / (AmE), / ˈæv.əl.ɑːnʃ / (BrE)
A sudden and overwhelming quantity of things occurring or appearing at once, especially in large numbers.

Examples

An avalanche of emails
After posting his job advertisement, he received an avalanche of applications.
The celebrity was met with an avalanche of fan mail after her performance.
There was an avalanche of complaints following the company’s announcement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flood - an overwhelming quantity or stream of things
