Satyendra N. Bose

proper noun
Indian physicist, namesake of bosons
Pronunciation: / səˈtjɛndrə ɛn ˈboʊs / (AmE), / səˈtjɛndrə ɛn ˈbəʊs / (BrE)
(1894–1974) Indian physicist whose work with Albert Einstein on quantum statistics led to the concept of bosons, fundamental particles in physics.

Examples

Satyendra N. Bose physicist
Satyendra N. Bose was instrumental in developing the field of quantum statistics.
Many physicists regard Satyendra N. Bose as one of the pioneers of modern particle physics.
The term 'boson' is derived from the name of Satyendra N. Bose.
