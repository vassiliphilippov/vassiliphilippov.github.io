glutton for punishment

noun
person who seeks out unpleasant or tough tasks
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlʌt̬ən fər pəˈnɪʃmənt / (AmE), / ˈɡlʌtn̩ fə pəˈnɪʃmənt / (BrE)
a person who willingly undertakes unpleasant, difficult, or demanding tasks, often repeatedly or to an excessive degree

Examples

A glutton for punishment
Even after failing twice, Mark proved he was a glutton for punishment by applying to the program again.
If you volunteer to organize the office party again, you're really a glutton for punishment.
Sarah must be a glutton for punishment to sign up for two back-to-back fitness bootcamps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: masochist - a person who takes pleasure in their own pain or humiliation
  • synonym: workaholic - a person who compulsively works hard and long hours
