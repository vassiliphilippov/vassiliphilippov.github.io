tranq

verb
to sedate with tranquilizer
9.9
Pronunciation: / træŋk / (AmE), / træŋk / (BrE)
to give a tranquilizer to a person or animal in order to sedate them, often by injection or dart

Examples

To tranq an animal
The veterinarian had to tranq the bear before treating its wound.
They quickly tranqed the aggressive dog to prevent anyone from getting hurt.
Wildlife officers sometimes tranq animals to relocate them safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sedate - to calm or put to sleep with a drug
  • antonym: stimulate - to make more active or alert
  • synonym: tranquilize - to make calm or unconscious with a tranquilizer
