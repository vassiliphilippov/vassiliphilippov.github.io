Khmer

noun
the language of Cambodia
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɛr / (AmE), / kəˈmɛə / (BrE)
the official language of Cambodia, belonging to the Mon-Khmer branch of the Austroasiatic language family

Examples

Khmer language
Many Cambodians speak Khmer as their first language.
She is taking a course to learn how to read and write Khmer.
The ancient temple inscriptions are written in classical Khmer.
