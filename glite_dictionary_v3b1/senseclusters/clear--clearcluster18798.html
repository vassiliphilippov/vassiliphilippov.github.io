clear

noun
freedom from suspicion or blame
6.1
Pronunciation: / klɪr / (AmE), / klɪə / (BrE)
a state in which someone is free from blame, suspicion, or guilt

Examples

In the clear
After a thorough investigation, the police declared that she was in the clear.
He finally felt clear after months of being wrongly accused.
The audit put the company's reputation in the clear once and for all.
