till

verb
prepare land for crops
Pronunciation: / tɪl / (AmE), / tɪl / (BrE)
to prepare land for crops by ploughing, harrowing, and fertilizing it

Examples

To till the land
After the flood, the community worked together to till their damaged soil.
Early settlers had to till the land by hand before they could plant crops.
The farmer uses modern machinery to till the fields every spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cultivate - to prepare and use land for growing crops
