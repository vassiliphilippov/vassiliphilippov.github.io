gules

adjective
heraldic red
9.9
Pronunciation: /gjuːlz/ /; / /guːlz/ (AmE), /gjuːlz/ (BrE)
heraldic term for the color red, used to describe a red area on a coat of arms

Examples

Gules shield
The family crest displayed a lion rampant in gules on a silver background.
In heraldry, gules represents courage and is often used for shields.
The knight's banner was decorated with a bold stripe of gules across the center.
