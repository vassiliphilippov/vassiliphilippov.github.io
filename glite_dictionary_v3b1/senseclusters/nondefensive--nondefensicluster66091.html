nondefensive

adjective
not self-protective
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.dɪˈfɛnsɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dɪˈfɛnsɪv / (BrE)
not intended to protect against attack or criticism; not reacting with self-protection or resistance

Examples

A nondefensive response
She gave a nondefensive response to the feedback and listened carefully.
His nondefensive attitude made it easier for others to share their opinions.
During the meeting, Mark remained nondefensive even when his ideas were challenged.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defensive - reacting to criticism with self-protection or resistance
  • synonym: open - willing to consider new ideas or accept criticism
