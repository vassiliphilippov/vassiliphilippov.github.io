immatureness

noun
state of not being mature
8.7
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmætʃərnɪs / (AmE), / ɪˈmatjʊənɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of not having reached full development or maturity.

Examples

The immatureness of a child
His immatureness was evident in the way he handled criticism.
Some critics pointed out the immatureness of the artist's first novel.
The company's immatureness resulted in several avoidable mistakes early on.
