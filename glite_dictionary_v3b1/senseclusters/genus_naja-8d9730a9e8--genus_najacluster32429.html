genus Naja

noun
genus of cobras (snakes)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈnɑː.dʒə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈnɑː.dʒə / (BrE)
A genus of venomous snakes in the family Elapidae, commonly known as cobras, native to Africa and Asia.
