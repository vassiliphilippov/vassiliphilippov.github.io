Synonyms

  • prickle
    4.0 0.61/m

  • needle
    5.3 0.11/m
    needle means to pierce with a thin point, while prick can mean a quick sharp sting without naming the tool.
  • sting
    2.3 4.14/m
    sting is a sudden sharp pain from an insect or plant, while prick covers causing either sharp or tingling sensations.
  • sting
    3.3 1.44/m
    sting is a sharp emotional hurt that makes you feel upset, while prick also covers physical pokes and small tingling feelings.
