pat-down

verb
to flatten gently
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpætˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈpætˌdaʊn / (BrE)
to press or flatten something gently with repeated light touches, usually using the hands.

Examples

To pat-down soil
After planting the seeds, he pat-down the soil to keep it in place.
She pat-down her hair to smooth out any stray strands before the meeting.
The baker pat-down the dough to make it even before baking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: press - to apply pressure to something
  • synonym: smooth - to make a surface even or flat
