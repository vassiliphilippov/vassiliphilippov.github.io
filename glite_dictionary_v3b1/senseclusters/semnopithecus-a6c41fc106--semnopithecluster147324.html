Semnopithecus

noun
a genus of Asian langur monkeys
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɛmˌnɒpɪˈθiːkəs / (AmE), / sɛmˌnɒpɪˈθiːkəs / (BrE)
A genus of Asian monkeys known as gray langurs or Hanuman langurs, characterized by long tails and slender bodies.

Examples

A Semnopithecus langur
A Semnopithecus langur sat quietly on a branch, watching the passing tourists below.
Researchers observed a group of Semnopithecus displaying complex social behaviors in the forest canopy.
The Semnopithecus genus includes several species of gray langur found across South Asia.
