Anglesey

proper noun
island off the coast of Wales
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡəlˌsi / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡləsi / (BrE)
an island off the northwest coast of Wales, forming a county and known for its rural landscapes and historical sites.
