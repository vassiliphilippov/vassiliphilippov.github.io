Synonyms

  • mix
    1.0 13/m
    a mix means putting different things together in no set order, while fusion makes them join into one new combined form.
  • combination
    2.3 30/m
    a combination is joining items into a set or result, while fusion suggests a deeper merging into a single new entity.
  • mixture
    1.2 3/m
    a mixture keeps different parts together in one mass, while fusion usually makes those parts form a single new whole.
  • amalgamation
    7.5 0.3/m
    an amalgamation is a formal joining often used for companies, while fusion is a broader joining in art, food, or style.
  • coalescence
    7.8 0.07/m

  • unification
    4.3 1/m
    unification is bringing groups or states into one political unit, while fusion is a broader idea of joining people, styles, or things.
