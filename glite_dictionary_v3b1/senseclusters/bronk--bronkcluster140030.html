bronk

noun
informal for bronchitis
9.9
Pronunciation: / brɑŋk / (AmE), / brɒŋk / (BrE)
an informal term for bronchitis or the bronchial tubes, often used to refer to a chest illness

Examples

The bronk illness
He missed work for a week because he had a bad case of the bronk.
My doctor said the bronk should clear up with rest and fluids.
After coughing all night, she worried she might have caught the bronk from her coworker.
