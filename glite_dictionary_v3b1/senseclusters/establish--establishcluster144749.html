establish

verb
prove or demonstrate the truth of something
Pronunciation: / ɪˈstæblɪʃ / (AmE), / ɪˈstæblɪʃ / (BrE)
to prove or show the truth or validity of something, especially through evidence, reasoning, or demonstration

Examples

To establish the truth
It took years to establish that the painting was indeed a genuine work by Van Gogh.
She used several experiments to establish her theory beyond doubt.
The study helped establish the effectiveness of the new drug.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disprove - to show that something is false or incorrect
  • synonym: prove - to demonstrate the truth or existence of something by evidence or argument
