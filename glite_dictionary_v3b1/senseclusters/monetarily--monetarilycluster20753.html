monetarily

adverb
in terms of money
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnɪˈtɛrəli / (AmE), / ˌmɒnɪˈtɛərəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to money or finances

Examples

To be monetarily valuable
Although the event was not monetarily rewarding, it brought the community together.
The company benefited monetarily from the new government policy.
She was not monetarily motivated but wanted to help others.
