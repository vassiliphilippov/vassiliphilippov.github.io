accommodator

noun
person who adapts
Pronunciation: / əˈkɑːməˌdeɪtər / (AmE), / əˈkɒməˌdeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who adapts to the needs or wishes of others to make situations easier or more comfortable

Examples

Flexible accommodator
As an accommodator, she always tried to make everyone feel comfortable.
The team valued having an accommodator who could help resolve conflicts smoothly.
Being an accommodator sometimes meant putting others' needs before his own.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adapter - a person who adjusts to new conditions
  • antonym: resister - a person who opposes or refuses to adapt
