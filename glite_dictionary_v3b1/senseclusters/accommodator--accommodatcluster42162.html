accommodator

noun
person who adapts
6.7
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈkɑː.mə.deɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ə.ˈkɒ.mə.deɪ.tə/ (BrE)

Examples

Being an accommodator sometimes meant putting others' needs before his own.
