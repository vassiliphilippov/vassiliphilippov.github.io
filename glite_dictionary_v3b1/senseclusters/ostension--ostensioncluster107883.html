ostension

noun
demonstrative explanation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑːˈstɛnʃən / (AmE), / ɒˈstɛnʃən / (BrE)
the act of showing or demonstrating something, especially to explain its meaning

Examples

Teaching by ostension
The teacher relied on ostension by pointing to real objects to teach new vocabulary.
In early childhood education, ostension helps children connect words with physical items.
He used ostension to clarify the meaning of the gesture by performing it himself.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demonstration - the act of showing how something works or is done
  • synonym: explanation - a statement that makes something clear
