oxalate

noun
salt or ester of oxalic acid
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːksəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒksəˌleɪt / (BrE)
a salt or ester of oxalic acid, commonly found in plants and used in chemistry and medicine

Examples

Calcium oxalate
Calcium oxalate is a common cause of kidney stones.
Many leafy green vegetables naturally contain oxalate.
The presence of oxalate crystals in urine may indicate a kidney disorder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oxalic acid - a strong organic acid found in many plants
