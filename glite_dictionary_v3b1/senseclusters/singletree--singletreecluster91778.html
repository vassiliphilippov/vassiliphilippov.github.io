singletree

noun
draft animal harness bar
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəlˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡəlˌtriː / (BrE)
a horizontal bar, usually in a harness, used to attach a draft animal to a vehicle or implement and to balance and distribute the pull of a load
