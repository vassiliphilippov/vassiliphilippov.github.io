Sphyraena

noun
genus of marine fish (barracudas)
Pronunciation: / sfɪˈriːnə / (AmE), / sfɪəˈriːnə / (BrE)
A genus of predatory marine fish known as barracudas, being the only genus of the family Sphyraenidae.

Examples

The Sphyraena fish
Many anglers hope to catch a Sphyraena because of its formidable size and speed.
Marine biologists observed a school of Sphyraena near the coral reef.
The genus Sphyraena includes several species commonly known as barracudas.
