primary syphilis

noun
first stage of syphilis
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪˌmɛri ˈsɪfəˌlɪs / (AmE), / ˈprʌɪməˌri ˈsɪfɪlɪs / (BrE)
the initial stage of syphilis, marked by the appearance of a chancre at the site of infection

Examples

The primary syphilis sore
A painless sore, or chancre, is the hallmark of primary syphilis.
Early detection and treatment of primary syphilis can prevent serious health complications.
If primary syphilis is left untreated, it may progress to the next phase of the disease.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: secondary syphilis - the second stage of syphilis, marked by skin rashes and mucous membrane lesions
