unemotional

adjective
not showing emotion; cool in manner
3.6 3.6
Pronunciation: /ʌ.nɪ.ˈmoʊ.ʃə.nəl/ /; / /ʌ.nə.ˈmoʊ.ʃə.nəl/ (AmE), /ʌ.nɪ.ˈməʊ.ʃə.nəl/ /; / /ʌ.nə.ˈməʊ.ʃə.nəl/ (BrE)
not showing or affected by strong emotions; calm and not emotional
