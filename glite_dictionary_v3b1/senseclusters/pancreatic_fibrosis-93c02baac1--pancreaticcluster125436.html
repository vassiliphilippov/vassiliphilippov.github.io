pancreatic fibrosis

noun
fibrous tissue formation in the pancreas
1.9 6.0
Pronunciation: /pæn.kriˈæt.ɪk faɪ.ˈbroʊ.sɪs/ /; / /pæŋ.kriˈæt.ɪk faɪ.ˈbroʊ.sɪs/ /; / /pæn.kriː.ˈæt.ɪk faɪ.ˈbroʊ.sɪs/ (AmE), /pæn.kriˈæt.ɪk faɪ.ˈbrəʊ.sɪs/ /; / /pæŋ.kriˈæt.ɪk faɪ.ˈbrəʊ.sɪs/ (BrE)
A medical condition characterised by the development of fibrous tissue in the pancreas, often resulting in impaired pancreatic function.
