multi-color

adjective
having patches of different colors
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌkʌlər /; / ˌmʌltiˈkʌlɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌkʌlə /; / ˌmʌltiˈkʌlə / (BrE)
Having sections or patches of different, often bright, colors.

Examples
