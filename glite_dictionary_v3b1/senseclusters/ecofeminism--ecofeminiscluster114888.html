ecofeminism

noun
environmental-gender movement
Pronunciation: / ˌiːkoʊˈfɛmɪnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌiːkəʊˈfɛmɪnɪzəm / (BrE)
a movement or theory that connects environmental protection and gender equality, exploring how the treatment of women and the environment are related

Examples

Ecofeminism movement
Many scholars believe that ecofeminism offers a unique perspective on the relationship between humans and nature.
The conference included a panel on ecofeminism and its impact on global environmental policies.
She wrote her thesis on how ecofeminism addresses both ecological and social justice issues.
The conference featured a panel on ecofeminism and its role in shaping environmental policy.
Many activists believe that ecofeminism offers a unique perspective on social and ecological justice.
Her research in ecofeminism examines how environmental issues affect women differently around the world.
