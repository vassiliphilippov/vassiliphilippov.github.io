crawly

adjective
like insects creeping
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔli / (AmE), / ˈkrɔːli / (BrE)
moving or feeling as if small insects are creeping on the skin or surface

Examples

Crawly skin feeling
After walking through the old basement, he had a crawly feeling on his arms.
The movie gave her a crawly sensation, as if something was moving on her skin.
He brushed his leg, thinking a crawly bug was climbing up his trousers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creepy - causing an unpleasant feeling like insects crawling on the skin
