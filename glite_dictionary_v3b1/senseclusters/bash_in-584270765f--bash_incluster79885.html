bash in

verb
hit violently; damage by striking
6.1
Pronunciation: / bæʃ ɪn / (AmE), / bæʃ ɪn / (BrE)
to hit someone or something violently, often causing serious damage or injury

Examples

To bash in a window
He threatened to bash in his rival's window if he wasn't paid.
She was afraid someone might bash in her car windows if she left it there overnight.
The angry mob tried to bash in the door with a battering ram.
