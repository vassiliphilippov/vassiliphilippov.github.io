Eastertide

noun
the Easter season in Christianity
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstərˌtaɪd / (AmE), / ˈiːstətaɪd / (BrE)
the period of the Christian year beginning with Easter and lasting until Pentecost, celebrating the resurrection of Jesus Christ.

Examples
