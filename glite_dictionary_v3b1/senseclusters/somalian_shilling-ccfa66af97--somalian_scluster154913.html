Somalian shilling

noun
currency of Somalia
Pronunciation: / soʊˈmɑːliən ˈʃɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / səʊˈmɑːliən ˈʃɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Somalia, equal to 100 cents

Examples

The Somalian shilling currency
He exchanged his dollars for Somalian shillings at the local bank.
Prices in Mogadishu are usually listed in Somalian shillings.
The value of the Somalian shilling has fluctuated significantly over the years.
