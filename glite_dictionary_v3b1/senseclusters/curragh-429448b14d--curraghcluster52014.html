Curragh

proper noun
plain in Ireland
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜrə / (AmE), / ˈkʌrə / (BrE)
a large flat open plain in County Kildare, Ireland, known for horse grazing and military training

Examples

The Curragh plain
