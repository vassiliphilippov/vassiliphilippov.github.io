lithely

adverb
gracefully and flexibly
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪðli / (AmE), / ˈlaɪðli / (BrE)
in a flexible, graceful, and agile manner

Examples

To move lithely
The gymnast moved lithely across the balance beam, impressing the judges.
She slipped lithely through the crowd, barely touching anyone as she passed.
The cat jumped lithely onto the windowsill to watch the birds outside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsily - in an awkward or uncoordinated way
  • synonym: gracefully - in a smooth and elegant way
