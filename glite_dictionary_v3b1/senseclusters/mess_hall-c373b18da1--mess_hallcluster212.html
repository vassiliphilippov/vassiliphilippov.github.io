mess hall

noun
military dining room
Pronunciation: / mɛs hɑl / (AmE), / mɛs hɔːl / (BrE)
a large dining room on a military base or other institution where service personnel eat their meals

Examples

The mess hall building
After a long day of training, the soldiers gathered in the mess hall for dinner.
The base commander held a meeting in the mess hall to address all personnel.
The smell of fresh coffee filled the mess hall each morning.
