resultative

adjective
expressing a result
8.6
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzʌltətɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈzʌltətɪv / (BrE)
expressing or indicating the result of an action or process

Examples

Resultative adjective
In the phrase 'She wiped the table clean,' 'clean' is a resultative adjective.
Linguists study how resultative constructions show the outcome of an action.
The word 'flat' in 'hammered the metal flat' is used in a resultative way.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consequential - resulting as an effect or outcome
