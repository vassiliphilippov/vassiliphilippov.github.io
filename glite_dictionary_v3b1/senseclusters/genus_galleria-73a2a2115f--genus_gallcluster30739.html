genus Galleria

noun
genus of moths including the greater wax moth
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs gəˈlɪəriə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ɡəˈlɪəriə / (BrE)
A genus of moths in the family Pyralidae, which includes the greater wax moth or honeycomb moth (Galleria mellonella).
