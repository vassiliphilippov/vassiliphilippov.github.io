tender

noun
rail car for locomotive's fuel and water
6.3 7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈten.dər/ (AmE), /ˈten.də/ (BrE)
a railroad car attached to a steam locomotive for carrying its fuel and water supply

Examples

A train tender
A classic train set often includes a small tender right behind the engine.
The steam engine was followed by a tender filled with coal and water.
Without a tender, the locomotive would quickly run out of fuel on a long journey.
