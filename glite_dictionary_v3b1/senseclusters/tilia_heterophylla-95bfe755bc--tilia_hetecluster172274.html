Tilia heterophylla

noun
a basswood tree of the Allegheny region
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪliə ˌhɛtərəˈfaɪlə / (AmE), / ˈtɪlia ˌhɛtərəˈfɪlə / (BrE)
A species of basswood tree native to the Allegheny region of the United States, also known as white basswood or American basswood.
