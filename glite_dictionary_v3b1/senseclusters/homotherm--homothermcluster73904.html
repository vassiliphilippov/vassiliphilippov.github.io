homotherm

noun
animal with constant body temperature
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊməˌθɜrm / (AmE), / ˈhəʊməˌθɜːm / (BrE)
an animal that maintains a relatively constant body temperature regardless of the environmental temperature

Examples

A homotherm animal
A mammal's ability to be a homotherm helps it stay active in cold environments.
Birds are classified as homotherms because they regulate their body temperature internally.
Unlike reptiles, homotherms can thrive in a wider range of climates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homeotherm - an animal able to maintain a constant body temperature
  • antonym: poikilotherm - an animal whose body temperature varies with the environment
