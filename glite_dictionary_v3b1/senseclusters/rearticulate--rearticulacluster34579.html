rearticulate

verb
to express again
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɑːrˈtɪkjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːɑːˈtɪkjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to express or state something again, often in a new or clearer way
