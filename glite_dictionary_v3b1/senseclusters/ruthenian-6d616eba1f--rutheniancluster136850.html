Ruthenian

noun
Byzantine Catholic member
Pronunciation: / ruːˈθiːniən / (AmE), / ruːˈθiːniən / (BrE)
a member of the Eastern European community historically associated with the Ruthenian (Byzantine) Catholic Church

Examples

Ruthenian Catholic
The local Ruthenian community celebrates traditional Byzantine Catholic holidays every year.
She traced her ancestry back to a proud line of Ruthenians from Eastern Europe.
After joining the church, he became an active Ruthenian parishioner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Russian - a person from Russia or of Russian descent
  • synonym: Ukrainian - a person from Ukraine or of Ukrainian descent
