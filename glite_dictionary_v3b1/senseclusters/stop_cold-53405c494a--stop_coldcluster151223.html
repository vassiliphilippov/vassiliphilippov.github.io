stop cold

verb
to stop abruptly or completely
Pronunciation: / stɑp koʊld / (AmE), / stɒp kəʊld / (BrE)
to stop suddenly and completely, often because of shock, surprise, or fear

Examples

To stop cold suddenly
He stopped cold when he heard his name called across the busy street.
She stopped cold at the sight of the snake slithering across the path.
Traffic stopped cold after the sudden accident on the highway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freeze - to become motionless from shock or fear
