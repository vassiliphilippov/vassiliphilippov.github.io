blow bubbles

verb
to create bubbles with gum by blowing
3.8
Pronunciation: / bloʊ ˈbʌbəlz / (AmE), / bləʊ ˈbʌbəlz / (BrE)
to form bubbles by expelling air through gum or another substance held in the mouth

Examples

To blow bubbles with gum
He became the champion at his school for being able to blow bubbles larger than anyone else's.
She tried to blow bubbles, but her gum wasn't stretchy enough.
The children love to blow bubbles in the park with their bubble gum.
