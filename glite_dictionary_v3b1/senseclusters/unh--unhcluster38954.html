unh

interjection
sound of exertion
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌn / (AmE), / ʌn / (BrE)
a short sound made to express effort, exertion, or strain, especially when lifting or pushing something heavy

Examples

Unh lifting a box
He let out a loud unh as he tried to move the heavy sofa.
With a quick unh, she pushed the stuck door open.
The weightlifter gave an unh as he lifted the barbell above his head.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oof - a sound expressing pain or effort
  • synonym: ugh - a sound expressing disgust or discomfort
