snuff

verb
to extinguish flame
5.7
Pronunciation: /snʌf/ (AmE), /snʌf/ (BrE)
to extinguish a flame, especially by pinching or covering it

Examples

To snuff a candle
She gently snuffed the candle before going to bed.
The wind snuffed out the lantern as we walked through the forest.
He tried to snuff the match with his fingers after lighting the stove.
