optoelectronic

adjective
light and electronics
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːptoʊɪˌlɛkˈtrɑːnɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒptəʊɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to devices or systems that convert light into electrical signals or electrical signals into light

Examples

Modern optoelectronic devices
Modern fiber optic networks rely on optoelectronic components to transmit data efficiently.
The engineer designed an optoelectronic circuit to detect changes in light intensity.
Many security systems use optoelectronic sensors to monitor movement and light levels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electronic - relating to devices that use electricity to function
  • synonym: photonic - relating to the use of light (photons) in technology
