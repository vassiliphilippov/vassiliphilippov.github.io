upturned

adjective
having the bottom facing upwards
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌtɜrnd / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌtɜːnd / (BrE)
turned so that the bottom is facing upwards; upside-down or inverted.

Examples

Upturned boat
During the storm, several boats were left upturned on the shore.
She found an upturned chair in the middle of the room.
The child used an upturned bucket as a drum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overturned - turned over so the bottom is now on top
  • antonym: upright - in a vertical position, not overturned
