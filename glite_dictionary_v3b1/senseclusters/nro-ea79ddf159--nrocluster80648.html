NRO

noun
US National Reconnaissance Office (intelligence agency)
Pronunciation: / ɛn ɑr oʊ / (AmE), / ɛn ɑːr əʊ / (BrE)
abbreviation for the National Reconnaissance Office, a United States government agency within the Department of Defense responsible for space-based intelligence gathering and satellite reconnaissance.

Examples

The NRO agency
Much of the NRO's work remains highly classified.
Officials from the NRO briefed Congress on recent intelligence from space.
The NRO operates some of the world's most advanced spy satellites.

Related Terms

  • synonym: CIA - Central Intelligence Agency; a US civilian intelligence agency
  • synonym: NSA - National Security Agency; a US signals intelligence and cybersecurity agency
