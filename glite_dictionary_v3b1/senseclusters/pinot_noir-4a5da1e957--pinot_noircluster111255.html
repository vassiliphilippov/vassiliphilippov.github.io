Pinot noir

noun
a type of dry red wine or its grape variety
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːnoʊ ˈnwɑr /; / piˈnoʊ ˈnwɑr / (AmE), / ˌpiːnəʊ ˈnwɑː /; / ˈpiːnəʊ ˈnwɑː / (BrE)
