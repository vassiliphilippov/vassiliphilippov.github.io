foster family

noun
a family caring for a foster child
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑstɚ ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈfɒstə ˈfæməli / (BrE)
A family that provides care and accommodation to a child who is not their biological child, usually on a temporary basis.

Examples

A foster family
After living with his foster family for a year, Marcus was reunited with his mother.
Her foster family supported her through many difficult moments during her teenage years.
The agency carefully matches children with the right foster family to ensure their well-being.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adoptive family - a family that has legally adopted a child
