pebbling

noun
bumpy surface texture
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɛblɪŋ / (BrE)
a surface texture or pattern marked by small, rounded bumps resembling pebbles

Examples

Pebbling on leather
The pebbling on the basketball provides a better grip for players.
She admired the pebbling on the leather handbag, which made it unique.
The old road's surface showed signs of pebbling after years of weathering.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grain - the texture or pattern on a material's surface
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of having an even, flat surface
