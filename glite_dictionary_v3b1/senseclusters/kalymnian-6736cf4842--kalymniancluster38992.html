Kalymnian

adjective
of Kalymnos island
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪmnɪən / (AmE), / kəˈlɪmnɪən / (BrE)
relating to the Greek island of Kalymnos, its people, culture, or things originating from there

Examples

Kalymnian culture

Many Kalymnian dishes feature fresh seafood caught around the island.
