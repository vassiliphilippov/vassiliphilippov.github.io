collateral

noun
marketing or promotional materials
5.0 0.11/m

Marketing or promotional materials used to support sales and communications, such as brochures, white papers, case studies, presentations, and digital assets.

Examples

Sales collateral
The sales team circulated new collateral to potential clients at the conference.
She designed digital collateral such as infographics and videos for the product launch campaign.
Before the meeting, he printed branded collateral to hand out during the client presentation.
