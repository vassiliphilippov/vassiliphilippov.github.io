bepimpled

adjective
covered with bumps
Pronunciation: / bɪˈpɪmpəld / (AmE), / bɪˈpɪmpəld / (BrE)
covered with small raised bumps or protuberances, especially on objects or surfaces rather than skin.

Examples

Bepimpled statue surface
The ancient statue was bepimpled with patches of moss and rough stone.
After years of neglect, the metal surface became bepimpled with rust and corrosion.
The cake's icing was accidentally bepimpled with small air bubbles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bumpy - having many raised areas or uneven spots
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and flat surface without bumps
