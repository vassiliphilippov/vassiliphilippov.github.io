tablespoon

noun
large spoon for serving or measuring
3.1 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪbəlˌspoʊn / (AmE), / ˈteɪbl̩ˌspəʊn / (BrE)
a large spoon, larger than a dessert spoon, used for serving food or for measuring an amount of an ingredient
