tablespoon

noun
large spoon for serving or measuring
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪbəlˌspoʊn / (AmE), / ˈteɪbl̩ˌspəʊn / (BrE)
a large spoon, larger than a dessert spoon, used for serving food or for measuring an amount of an ingredient

Examples

A tablespoon for serving
Add one tablespoon of sugar to the mixture and stir well.
She used a tablespoon to serve the soup into each bowl.
The recipe calls for two tablespoons of olive oil.
