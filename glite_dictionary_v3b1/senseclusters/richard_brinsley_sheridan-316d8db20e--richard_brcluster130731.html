Richard Brinsley Sheridan

proper noun
Irish playwright and satirist (1751–1816)
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃərd ˈbrɪnzli ˈʃɛrɪdən / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃəd ˈbrɪnzli ˈʃɛrɪdən / (BrE)
