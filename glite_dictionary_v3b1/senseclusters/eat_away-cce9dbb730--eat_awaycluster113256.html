eat away

verb
gradually erode; wear away
9.4 3.7
Pronunciation: /iːt ə.ˈweɪ/ /; / /ɪt ə.ˈweɪ/ (AmE), /iːt ə.ˈweɪ/ /; / /ɪt ə.ˈweɪ/ (BrE)

Examples

Over time, the constant flow of water ate away the riverbank.
