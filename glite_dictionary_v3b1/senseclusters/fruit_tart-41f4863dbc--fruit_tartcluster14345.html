fruit tart

noun
pastry dessert topped with fruit
Pronunciation: / fruːt tɑrt / (AmE), / fruːt tɑːt / (BrE)
A dessert consisting of a pastry base filled with custard or cream and topped with fruit.

Examples

A fruit tart dessert
He chose the fruit tart for dessert because it looked light and refreshing.
She served a homemade fruit tart at the dinner party.
The bakery's signature fruit tart features an arrangement of fresh berries.
