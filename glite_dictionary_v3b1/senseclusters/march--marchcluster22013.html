march

noun
steady advance; ongoing progress
Pronunciation: / mɑrtʃ / (AmE), / mɑːtʃ / (BrE)
a gradual and continuous advance or development, especially over time or in a process

Examples

The march of time
Nothing can halt the march of time, no matter how hard we try.
The march of history is filled with unexpected turns and landmark events.
The march of technology has transformed the way we live our lives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: progress - forward or onward movement toward a destination or goal
  • antonym: retreat - the act of withdrawing or moving back
