HMO

noun
health insurance organization offering network-based services
4.3
Pronunciation: /eɪtʃ em oʊ/ (AmE), /eɪtʃ em əʊ/ (BrE)
an organization providing or administering health insurance, offering healthcare services to members through a network of designated providers for a fixed, prepaid fee

Examples

An HMO plan
Many employers offer an HMO as part of their health benefits package.
The HMO requires referrals for specialist visits.
You must select a primary care physician within your HMO network.

Related Terms

  • antonym: PPO - Preferred Provider Organization, another type of health insurance plan
