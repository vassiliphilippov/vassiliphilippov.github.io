Habsburg

proper noun
dynasty of European rulers (1440–1806)
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈhæps.bɜːrɡ/ /; / /ˈhæbz.bɜːrɡ/ (AmE), /ˈhæps.bɜːɡ/ /; / /ˈhæbz.bɜːɡ/ (BrE)
a European royal family that ruled several states, including Austria and Spain, and held the Holy Roman Emperor's crown from 1440 to 1806.

Examples

The Habsburg dynasty
