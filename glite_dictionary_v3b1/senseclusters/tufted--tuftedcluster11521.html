tufted

adjective
growing in dense clumps (of plants)
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌftɪd / (AmE), / ˈtʌftɪd / (BrE)
(of plants) growing in small, dense clumps or tufts.

Examples

Tufted grass
Several tufted plants thrive in rocky, dry environments where water is scarce.
The tufted grasses made the hillside look soft and uneven.
Their garden features many tufted species, which add interesting texture to the landscape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clumped - growing in clusters or masses
  • antonym: sparse - thinly scattered or distributed
