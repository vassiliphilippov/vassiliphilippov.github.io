stockholding

noun
stocks or shares owned
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑkˌhoʊldɪŋ /; / ˈstɑːkˌhoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɒkˌhəʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
the shares that a person or organization owns in a company, or the fact of owning such shares

Examples

Institutional stockholding can influence company decisions significantly.
