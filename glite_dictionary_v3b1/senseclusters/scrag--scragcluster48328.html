scrag

noun
very thin, scrawny person
3.9
Pronunciation: / skræɡ / (AmE), / skræɡ / (BrE)
a person who is unusually thin and scrawny

Examples

A skinny scrag

She hated being called a scrag by her classmates.
The bullies called him a scrag because of his bony frame.
