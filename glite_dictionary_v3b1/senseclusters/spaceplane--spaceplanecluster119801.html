spaceplane

noun
atmosphere-space vehicle
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈspeɪs.pleɪn/ (AmE), /ˈspeɪs.pleɪn/ (BrE)
a vehicle designed to fly in both Earth's atmosphere and outer space

Examples

A spaceplane vehicle
The engineers unveiled a new spaceplane capable of reaching orbit and returning safely.
A spaceplane can take off like an airplane and then travel into space.
Scientists are developing a reusable spaceplane to make space travel more affordable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airplane - a powered flying vehicle with fixed wings
  • synonym: rocket - a vehicle propelled by rocket engines, often used for space travel
  • synonym: spacecraft - a vehicle designed for travel in outer space
