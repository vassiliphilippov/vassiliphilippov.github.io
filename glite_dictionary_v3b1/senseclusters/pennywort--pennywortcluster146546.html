pennywort

noun
plant with round leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛniˌwɝt / (AmE), / ˈpɛniˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a small plant with round leaves, often found in wet, damp, or rocky places

Examples

Wild pennywort plant
We found pennywort growing along the edge of the pond.
The round leaves of pennywort make it easy to identify in wet meadows.
Some people use pennywort in salads or herbal remedies.
Tiny pennywort plants grew in the cracks of the castle wall, their leaves round and glossy.
The old stone bridge was covered in patches of pennywort after the rainy season.
She identified the plant as pennywort by its distinctive round leaves and fleshy texture.

  • synonym: navelwort - a plant with round leaves, also called pennywort
  • synonym: water pennywort - a type of pennywort that grows in water
