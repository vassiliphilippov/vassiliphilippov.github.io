Synonyms

  • successive
    4.3 2/m
    successive describes items that follow one another without any break, while consecutive stresses an unbroken continuous sequence.
  • sequential
    4.9 0.8/m
    sequential refers to things arranged in a clear order, while consecutive emphasizes a tight, uninterrupted run in that order.
  • serial
    3.7 14/m
    serial often describes parts of a series or episodes over time, while consecutive means immediately following without gaps.
