upend

verb
become turned on end
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈɛnd / (AmE), / ʌpˈɛnd / (BrE)
to turn or be turned so that the end is upright or vertical

Examples

To upend a boat
After the storm, several boats upended along the shoreline.
During the turbulence, the model airplane upended and crashed to the ground.
The vase accidentally upended and water spilled everywhere.
Before cleaning, upend the container to remove any remaining crumbs.
He accidentally upended the glass, spilling water across the table.
The movers had to upend the sofa to fit it through the doorway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invert - to turn something upside down or reverse the position
  • antonym: right - to put something back in its correct upright position
