duck

noun
a web-footed swimming bird
2.5 1.0
Pronunciation: /dʌk/ (AmE), /dʌk/ (BrE)
a small wild or domesticated bird with webbed feet, a broad bill, a short neck, and usually a stout body, found in both freshwater and saltwater habitats
