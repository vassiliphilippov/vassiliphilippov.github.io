Carica papaya

noun
species of papaya tree
6.5
Pronunciation: / kəˈɹiːkə pəˈpaɪə / (AmE), / kəˈriːkə pəˈpʌɪə / (BrE)
A tropical American shrub or small tree with large deeply lobed leaves, known for its large oblong yellow fruit called papaya.

Examples

Carica papaya tree
Carica papaya is cultivated in many tropical regions for its sweet fruit.
Botanists classified the papaya tree under the species Carica papaya.
The leaves of Carica papaya are often used in traditional medicine.
