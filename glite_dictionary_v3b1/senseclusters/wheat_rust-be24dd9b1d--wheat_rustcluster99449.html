wheat rust

noun
fungal disease affecting wheat
Pronunciation: / wiːt rʌst / (AmE), / wiːt rʌst / (BrE)
A fungal disease of wheat caused by various rust fungi, resulting in reddish or brownish spots on leaves and stems and often reducing yield.

Examples

wheat rust disease
Farmers worry about wheat rust destroying their crops during wet seasons.
Scientists have developed new wheat varieties that are resistant to wheat rust.
The outbreak of wheat rust severely reduced the region's grain harvest last year.
