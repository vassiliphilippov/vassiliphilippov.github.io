overgo

verb
to happen to (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈɡəʊ / (BrE)
to happen to or befall someone or something, especially as an event or fate (archaic)

Examples

Strange events overgo a town
A strange fate seemed to overgo the small town every winter.
No one could have predicted the hardships that would overgo the travelers on their journey.
Legends tell of curses that may overgo those who disturb the ancient ruins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: occur - to happen or take place
