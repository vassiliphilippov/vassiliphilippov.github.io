contactor

noun
electrical switch device
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːnˌtæktər / (AmE), / ˈkɒnˌtæktə / (BrE)
an electrical device used to switch a power circuit on or off, typically by remote control

Examples

An electrical contactor
The electrician installed a new contactor to control the building's lighting system.
A faulty contactor can cause the motor to stop working unexpectedly.
Before servicing the machine, always disconnect the contactor to ensure safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relay - an electrical device that opens or closes a circuit automatically
  • synonym: switch - a device for making and breaking the connection in an electric circuit
