chelated

adjective
involving chelation (chemistry)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkiːleɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound or molecule) relating to or involving chelation, especially in which a metal ion is bonded to an organic molecule at multiple points.

Examples

Chelated iron supplement
Iron in this supplement is present in a chelated form for better absorption.
Some soils are not compatible with chelated fertilizers.
The chelated compound showed greater stability in biological systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chelate - a compound in which a metal ion is bonded to an organic molecule at multiple points
  • antonym: non-chelated - not involving or produced by chelation
