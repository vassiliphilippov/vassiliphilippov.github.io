cannonball

verb
to jump and splash
Pronunciation: / ˈkænənˌbɔl /; / ˈkæn.ənˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈkænənˌbɔːl /; / ˈkæn.ənˌbɔːl / (BrE)
to jump into water with your knees pulled to your chest, making a big splash

He loves to cannonball into the lake and make the biggest splash.
