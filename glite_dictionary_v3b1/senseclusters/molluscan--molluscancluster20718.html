molluscan

noun
mollusk animal
7.9
Pronunciation: / məˈlʌskən / (AmE), / məˈlʌskən / (BrE)
an animal belonging to the phylum Mollusca, such as a snail, clam, or octopus

Examples

A molluscan animal
The scientist studied a rare molluscan found in the deep ocean.
Shell collectors often search beaches for interesting molluscan specimens.
A fossilized molluscan can reveal much about ancient marine environments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arthropod - an invertebrate animal with an exoskeleton, such as an insect or spider
  • synonym: mollusk - an invertebrate animal of the phylum Mollusca
