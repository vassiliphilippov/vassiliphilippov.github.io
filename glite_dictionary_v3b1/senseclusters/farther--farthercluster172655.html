farther

adjective
more distant in space or time
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrðər / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðə / (BrE)
at a greater distance in space, time, or degree (literal or figurative)

Examples

Farther point
As the evening went on, we drifted farther from the main topic of discussion.
She moved farther away to attend college in another city.
The train station is farther down the road than the grocery store.
His ideas were even farther from our expectations than we had feared.
Nothing was farther from her mind than returning to her old job.
This statement could not be farther from the truth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: further - at a greater distance or more advanced point
