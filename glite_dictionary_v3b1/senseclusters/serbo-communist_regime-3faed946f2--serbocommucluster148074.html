Serbo-Communist regime

noun
political regime linked to Serbo-Communism
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜrboʊ kəˈmjunɪst rɪˈʒim / (AmE), / ˌsɜːbəʊ kəˈmjuːnɪst rɪˈʒiːm / (BrE)
A political regime characterized by or associated with Serbo-Communism, typically referring to governments in former Yugoslavia influenced by both Serbian and Communist ideologies.

Examples
