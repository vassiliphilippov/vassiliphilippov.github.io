seeress

noun
female prophet
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪrɛs /; / ˈsɪrɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɪərɪs / (BrE)
a woman who claims to see the future or is believed to have prophetic powers or exceptional foresight (sometimes used figuratively for someone who predicts future events or trends)

Villagers believed the seeress could predict storms and disasters.

In ancient stories, a seeress would guide heroes with her visions of the future.
Her uncanny ability to anticipate changes in technology earned her the nickname seeress among her peers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prophetess - a female prophet
  • synonym: seer - a person who claims to see the future
  • antonym: skeptic - a person who doubts or questions claims of prophecy
