noncondensable

adjective
cannot become liquid
9.8
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.kən.ˈden.sə.bəl/ /; / /nən.kən.ˈden.sə.bəl/ (AmE), /nɒn.kən.ˈden.sə.bəl/ /; / /nən.kən.ˈden.sə.bəl/ (BrE)
not able to be changed from a gas to a liquid by cooling or compression

Examples

Noncondensable gas problem
In refrigeration systems, air is considered a noncondensable gas that can reduce efficiency.
Helium is a noncondensable gas under normal atmospheric conditions.
