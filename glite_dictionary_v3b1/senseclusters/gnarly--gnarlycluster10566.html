gnarly

adjective
slang: extremely impressive or extreme
8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈnɑːr.liː/ /; / /ˈnɑːr.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈnɑː.liː/ /; / /ˈnɑː.lɪ/ (BrE)
informal slang: (of an event, action, or thing) extremely impressive or exciting; more generally, intensely extreme (can be positive—excellent—or negative—dangerous or severe—depending on context).

Examples

After the fall, his legs looked gnarly and the medics rushed to stabilize him.
