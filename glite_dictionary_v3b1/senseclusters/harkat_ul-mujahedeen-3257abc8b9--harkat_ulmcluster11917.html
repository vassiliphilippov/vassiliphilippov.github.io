Harkat ul-Mujahedeen

proper noun
Islamic militant group active in Kashmir and Pakistan
Pronunciation: / hɑːrˈkɑːt ʊl muːˈdʒɑːhədin / (AmE), / hɑːˈkɑːt ʊl mʊˈdʒɑːhədiːn / (BrE)
an Islamic militant group based in Pakistan, originally active in fighting the Soviet Union in Afghanistan in the 1980s, now considered a terrorist organization primarily operating in Kashmir and seeking the region's accession to Pakistan
