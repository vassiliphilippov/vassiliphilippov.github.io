cotyloid cavity

noun
cup-shaped part of the hip bone
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊ.t̬ɪ.lɔɪd ˈkæv.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.tɪ.lɔɪd ˈkæv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the cup-shaped hollow in the hip bone that receives the head of the femur to form the hip joint

Examples

The cotyloid cavity of hip
Cartilage within the cotyloid cavity helps cushion the femoral head during movement.
Fracture of the cotyloid cavity may disrupt the stability of the hip joint.
The surgeon examined the cotyloid cavity to ensure it could support a hip replacement.
