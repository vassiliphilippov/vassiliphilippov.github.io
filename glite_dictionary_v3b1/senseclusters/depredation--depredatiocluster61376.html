depredation

noun
destructive or plundering action
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛprəˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛprɪˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
an act of attacking, plundering, or causing destruction, especially resulting in damage or loss

Examples

Wildlife depredation

For centuries, coastal towns guarded against the depredations of pirates.
The village suffered repeated depredations by invading armies during the war.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pillage - the act of looting or violently robbing
  • synonym: plunder - the act of stealing goods, typically using force
  • synonym: ravage - an act of violent destruction
