spermatheca

noun
sperm storage organ
9.9
Pronunciation: /spɜːr.mə.ˈθiː.kə/ (AmE), /spɜː.mə.ˈθiː.kə/ (BrE)
a sac or organ in female animals (especially insects and invertebrates) that stores sperm after mating until it is needed for fertilization

Examples
