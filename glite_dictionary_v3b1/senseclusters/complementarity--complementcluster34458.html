complementarity

noun
the state of mutually completing or balancing opposites
5.2 7.0
Pronunciation: /kɑːm.plə.mən.ˈtær.ɪ.ti/ /; / /kɑːm.plə.mən.ˈteə.rɪ.ti/ (AmE), /kɒm.plə.men.ˈtær.ɪ.ti/ /; / /kɒm.plə.men.ˈteə.rɪ.ti/ (BrE)
the state or principle that two different things can complete or supplement each other, making a whole
