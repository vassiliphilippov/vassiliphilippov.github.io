numb

adjective
so shocked or afraid as to be unable to move
4.1 2.94/m
Pronunciation: / nÊŒm / (AmE), / nÊŒm / (BrE)
unable to feel or show emotion; emotionally unresponsive or stunned

Examples

Numb with fear
He was numb with terror as the shadow approached.
I felt so numb with fear that I could not move a muscle.
The terrible news left her numb and unable to speak.
After years of stressful work, she became numb to criticism from her boss.
He was so overwhelmed by tragedy that he felt numb to all emotion.
Many people become numb to reports of violence in the news.

Synonyms

  • dead
    4.1 20/m
    dead has no feeling or response in a strong, often permanent way, while numb suggests a temporary emotional unfeeling.
  • dead inside
    8.5 0.24/m
    dead inside feels empty and deeply without feeling, while numb often means not able to feel or show emotion.
  • unresponsive
    4.7 0.84/m
    unresponsive does not react to people or feelings and can be physical, while numb focuses on lost emotional feeling.
  • stunned
    3.0 1.38/m
    stunned is shocked and unable to react for a short time, while numb can mean a steady lack of emotional feeling.
  • emotionless
    5.5 0.81/m
    emotionless shows no feeling in face or voice, while numb means you cannot feel or show emotion inside.
  • impassive
    7.1 1.50/m
    impassive keeps a still, calm face and shows no reaction, while numb means you do not feel or respond emotionally.
  • unemotional
    4.4 0.52/m
    unemotional stays calm and rarely shows strong feelings, while numb means you are unable to feel or show emotion.
  • unmoved
    5.1 0.99/m
    unmoved is not affected outwardly by events or stories, while numb means you cannot feel those emotions inside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alert - fully aware and able to respond quickly
  • synonym: callous - showing or having an insensitive and cruel disregard for others
  • antonym: empathetic - showing an ability to understand and share the feelings of others
  • synonym: insensitive - not showing concern for others' feelings
  • synonym: petrified - so frightened that one is unable to move; paralyzed with fear
  • synonym: stunned - so shocked or surprised as to be temporarily unable to react
