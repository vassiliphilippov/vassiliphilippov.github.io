charter

noun
official document creating an organization
4.0 4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːrtər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːtə / (BrE)
an official document that formally creates an organization, such as a company, and defines its rights and purposes

Examples

A company charter
A company must present its charter when registering legally.
The new city operated under a special charter granted by parliament.
The university received its charter from the state in 1852.

Related Terms

  • synonym: constitution - a set of fundamental principles or established precedents according to which an organization is governed
  • synonym: deed - a legal document regarding the ownership or rights of property
