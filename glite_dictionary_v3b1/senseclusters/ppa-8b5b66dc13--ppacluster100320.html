PPA

noun
power purchase contract
Pronunciation: / pi pi eɪ / (AmE), / piː piː eɪ / (BrE)
a contract between an electricity producer and a buyer that sets the terms for selling and purchasing generated power

Examples

Energy PPA contract
The company signed a PPA to secure renewable energy for its operations.
A long-term PPA can help stabilize electricity costs for both producers and buyers.
Many solar projects rely on a PPA to guarantee a market for their power.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agreement - a negotiated and typically legally binding arrangement
  • synonym: contract - a legally binding agreement between parties
