so much for

x
expression of disappointment or failure
Pronunciation: / soʊ mʌtʃ fər / (AmE), / səʊ mʌtʃ fə / (BrE)
used to indicate that something has failed or has not happened as expected, often expressing disappointment or resignation

Examples

So much for plans
So much for an early night—he called and kept me up talking for hours.
So much for my confidence; the test was much harder than I expected.
So much for our beach day; it started pouring rain as soon as we arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: never mind - used to indicate that something is not important or will be ignored
  • synonym: that's that - used to indicate a final decision or the end of a matter
