offertory

noun
offering of gifts at a religious service
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.fɚˌtɔːr.i / (AmE), / ˈɒf.ə.tər.i / (BrE)
the offerings of money or other gifts presented by the congregation during a religious service, especially in Christian churches

Examples

Offertory collection basket
During Sunday service, the offertory was collected in woven baskets passed down each row.
He placed an envelope with his weekly offertory inside the church's collection box.
The choir sang a hymn while the congregation presented their offertory at the altar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collection - money collected during a religious service
  • synonym: donation - something that is given to a charity or religious institution
