textualize

verb
to convert to text
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛkstʃuəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈtɛkstjʊəlaɪz / (BrE)
to convert or represent something (such as speech, images, or ideas) into written or printed text

Examples

To textualize voice messages
The new app can textualize voice messages so users can read them later.
Researchers used software to textualize hundreds of handwritten letters for analysis.
It is helpful to textualize images with captions for accessibility purposes.
The researcher aimed to textualize the interviews for easier analysis.
Folklorists often textualize oral stories to preserve them for future generations.
It can be challenging to textualize complex emotions in a short essay.

  • antonym: oralize - to express or interpret something in spoken form
