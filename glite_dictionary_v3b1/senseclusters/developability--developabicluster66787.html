developability

noun
surface can be flattened
Pronunciation: / dɪˌvɛləpəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / dɪˌvɛləpəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the property of a surface that allows it to be unfolded or flattened into a plane without stretching or tearing

Examples

Surface developability
The engineer analyzed the developability of the metal sheet for use in the new design.
Understanding the developability of complex surfaces is important in architecture and manufacturing.
The developability of the material made it ideal for creating smooth, curved panels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexibility - the quality of being able to bend easily without breaking
  • antonym: rigidity - the quality of being stiff and not able to be bent or changed
