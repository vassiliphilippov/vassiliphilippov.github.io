lyric

adjective
relating to poetry expressing emotion
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪrɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɪrɪk / (BrE)
(of poetry, art, or writing) expressing deep personal emotions in a beautiful or songlike way

Examples
