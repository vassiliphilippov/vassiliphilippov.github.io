farm horse

noun
horse used for farm work
Pronunciation: / fɑrm hɔrs / (AmE), / fɑːm hɔːs / (BrE)
A horse kept and used for performing agricultural work on a farm, typically sturdy and calm in temperament.

Examples

A farm horse works
After a long day, the farm horse was rewarded with fresh hay and water.
The old farm horse patiently pulled the plow across the field.
They relied on their loyal farm horse for many years before getting a tractor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: workhorse - a horse used for hard, routine work; also, a dependable person or machine
