paleogeography

noun
study of ancient physical geography
2.9 9.2
Pronunciation: /peɪ.li.oʊ.dʒiː.ˈɑː.grə.fiː/ (AmE), /peɪ.li.əʊ.dʒiː.ˈɒ.grə.fiː/ (BrE)
The study of the physical geography of the Earth in ancient times or during geological epochs.

Examples
