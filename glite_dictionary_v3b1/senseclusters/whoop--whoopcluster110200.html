whoop

verb
to cough spasmodically
Pronunciation: / wuːp, hwuːp / (AmE), / wuːp, hwuːp / (BrE)
to cough with a spasmodic gasping intake of breath, especially as a symptom of whooping cough

Examples

To whoop with cough
He began to whoop uncontrollably after inhaling the irritant.
Patients with pertussis may whoop violently during coughing fits.
The child continued to whoop late into the night, alarming his parents.
