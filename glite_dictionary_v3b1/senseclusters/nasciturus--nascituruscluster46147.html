nasciturus

noun
unborn child (legal)
Pronunciation: / næsɪˈtʊrəs / (AmE), / næsɪˈtjʊərəs / (BrE)
a child conceived but not yet born, especially as considered in legal matters

Examples

The nasciturus child
In some countries, the law protects the rights of a nasciturus in inheritance disputes.
The court considered whether the nasciturus should be included as a beneficiary.
Legal documents sometimes refer to a nasciturus when discussing unborn heirs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fetus - an unborn offspring in the later stages of development
  • antonym: infant - a very young child or baby
