too-greedy

adjective
excessively greedy or gluttonous
2.0
Pronunciation: / tu ˈɡridi / (AmE), / tuː ˈɡriːdi / (BrE)
Excessively greedy or gluttonous; showing more greed than is acceptable or reasonable.

Examples

A too-greedy person
He became too-greedy and ended up losing everything he had gained.
Investors who were too-greedy ignored the risks and suffered heavy losses.
The child's too-greedy appetite worried his parents at dinner.

Related Terms
