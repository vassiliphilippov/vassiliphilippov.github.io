Examples

A compound sentence with conjunction
A compound sentence typically uses words like 'and', 'but', or 'or' to join clauses.
She wanted to go for a walk, but it started to rain is a compound sentence.
Students are often asked to write a compound sentence to show their understanding of sentence structure.
