iodize

verb
treat with iodine to prevent infection
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.əˌdaɪz /; / ˈaɪəˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.ə.daɪz /; / ˈaɪədaɪz / (BrE)
to treat or add iodine to something, such as to prevent infection or to fortify food

Examples

Many countries iodize salt to prevent iodine deficiency in the population.
