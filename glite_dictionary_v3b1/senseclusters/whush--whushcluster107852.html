whush

noun
soft rushing sound
9.1
Pronunciation: / wʊʃ / (AmE), / wʊʃ / (BrE)
a soft rushing or rustling sound, especially made by moving air or fabric

Examples

A whush of wind
A sudden whush of wind blew the papers off the table.
She heard the gentle whush of her dress as she walked across the room.
The door closed with a soft whush, barely making a sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rustle - a soft, light sound made by things gently rubbing together
  • synonym: whoosh - a sudden rushing sound, especially of air or water
