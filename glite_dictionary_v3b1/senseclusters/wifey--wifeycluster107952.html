wifey

adjective
like a wife
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪfi / (AmE), / ˈwaɪfi / (BrE)
behaving in a way considered typical of a wife, especially being domestic, responsible, or caring

Examples

Feeling wifey today
She felt extra wifey as she cooked dinner and tidied the house for her partner.
His friends joked that he was acting wifey after he started baking every weekend.
After moving in together, she became more wifey and enjoyed organizing their home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domestic - relating to home or family life
  • antonym: unconventional - not conforming to traditional roles or expectations
