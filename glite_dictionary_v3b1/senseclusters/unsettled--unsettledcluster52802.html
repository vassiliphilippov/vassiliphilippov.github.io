unsettled

adjective
not yet resolved or decided
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛtld̩ /; / ʌnˈsɛt̬əld / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛtld /; / ʌnˈsɛtəld / (BrE)
not resolved, decided, or stable; lacking certainty or final state

Examples

Much of the region was still unsettled during the early nineteenth century.

Her finances were unsettled, making it difficult to plan for the future.
The region remained politically unsettled for decades after the revolution.
