mana

noun
magical energy in games
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnə / (AmE), / ˈmænə /; / ˈmɑːnə / (BrE)
a supernatural or magical power or energy, especially one used to perform special abilities or cast spells (in spiritual beliefs or games)

Examples

Mana for spells

The ancient artifact was said to be filled with powerful mana that could heal or destroy.
Some cultures believe that certain people possess more mana than others, giving them special status.
