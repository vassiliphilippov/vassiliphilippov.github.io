mana

noun
magical energy in games
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnə / (AmE), / ˈmænə /; / ˈmɑːnə / (BrE)
a supernatural or magical power or energy, especially one used to perform special abilities or cast spells (in spiritual beliefs or games)

Examples

Mana for spells
The wizard could not cast another spell because he had no mana left.
Players often collect potions to restore their mana during long battles.
She managed her mana carefully to use her strongest abilities at the right moment.
In many video games, characters use mana to perform special abilities or cast spells.
The ancient artifact was said to be filled with powerful mana that could heal or destroy.
Some cultures believe that certain people possess more mana than others, giving them special status.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magic - supernatural power used to perform spells or feats
