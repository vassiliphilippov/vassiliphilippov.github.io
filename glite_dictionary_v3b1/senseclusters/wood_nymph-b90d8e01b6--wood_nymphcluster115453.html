wood nymph

noun
mythological female spirit of the woods
4.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /wʊd nɪmf/ (AmE), /wʊd nɪmf/ (BrE)
a mythological female spirit or minor deity associated with forests or woods, especially in Greek and Roman mythology
