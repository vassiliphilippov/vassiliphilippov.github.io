erotic

adjective
sexually arousing; related to sexual pleasure
2.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrɑːdɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈrɒtɪk / (BrE)
giving sexual pleasure or intended to arouse sexual desire

Examples

Erotic paintings
She found the paintings surprisingly erotic in their depiction of the human body.
The dance performance had an unmistakably erotic energy that captivated the audience.
The novel contained several erotic scenes that explored the characters' desires.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chaste - abstaining from sexual activity, pure or morally restrained
  • synonym: sensual - relating to or involving gratification of the senses and physical pleasure, especially sexual pleasure
