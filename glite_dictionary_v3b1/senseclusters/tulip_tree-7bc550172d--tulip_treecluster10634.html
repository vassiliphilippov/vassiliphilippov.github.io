tulip tree

noun
North American tree with tulip-shaped flowers
9.9 5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːlɪp ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈtjuːlɪp ˌtriː / (BrE)
a tall North American deciduous tree (Liriodendron tulipifera) with large tulip-shaped greenish-yellow flowers and soft white wood used for cabinet making
