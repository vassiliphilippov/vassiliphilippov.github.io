urologic

adjective
urinary system medical
Pronunciation: / ˌjʊrəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the medical study or treatment of the urinary system

Examples

Urologic specialist
The patient was referred to a urologic specialist for further evaluation.
Advances in urologic surgery have improved recovery times for many patients.
He attended a conference on the latest urologic treatments for kidney stones.
