thermoformable

adjective
can be molded by heat
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜːrmoʊˈfɔːrməbəl / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊˈfɔːməbəl / (BrE)
able to be shaped or molded when heated, especially referring to certain plastics or materials

Examples

Thermoformable plastic
This type of plastic is thermoformable, making it ideal for packaging applications.
Engineers chose a thermoformable material to create lightweight car parts.
The thermoformable sheet can be heated and pressed into complex shapes for industrial use.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rigid - not able to be bent or shaped easily
