millenarist

noun
person believing in an imminent millennium
Pronunciation: / mɪˈlɛn ər ɪst / (AmE), / mɪˈlɛn ər ɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes in the coming of a millennium, especially a period of great peace and prosperity often with religious connotations

Examples

A millenarist believer
As a millenarist, he preached that society was on the verge of a dramatic transformation.
Historians have studied various millenarist groups from the Middle Ages.
The millenarist movement gained followers who hoped for a new era of peace.
