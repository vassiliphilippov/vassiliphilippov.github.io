reedlike

adjective
slender and upright like a reed
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈriːdlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling a reed in being tall, slender, and upright.

Examples

A reedlike shape

Tall, reedlike plants swayed gently in the breeze by the lake.
The dancer's reedlike grace was evident in every movement she made.
