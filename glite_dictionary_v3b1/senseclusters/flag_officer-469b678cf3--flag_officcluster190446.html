flag officer

noun
senior naval officer above captain
5.3
Pronunciation: / flæɡ ˈɑfəsər / (AmE), / flæɡ ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a senior officer in a navy above the rank of captain, entitled to fly a flag to denote command

Examples
