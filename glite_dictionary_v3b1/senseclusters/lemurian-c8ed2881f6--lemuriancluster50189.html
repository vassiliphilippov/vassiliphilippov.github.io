Lemurian

noun
mythical Lemuria inhabitant
9.9
Pronunciation: / lɪˈmjʊriən / (AmE), / lɪˈmjʊəriən / (BrE)
a person associated with Lemuria, either as a mythical inhabitant of the lost continent or as someone who believes in or follows its spiritual or mystical ideas
