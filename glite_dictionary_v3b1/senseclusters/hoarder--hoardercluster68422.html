hoarder

noun
a person who accumulates and stores things excessively
5.5 0.68/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…îrd…ôr / (AmE), / Ààh…îÀêd…ô / (BrE)
a person who collects and stores large quantities of things, often secretly or unnecessarily, for future use

Examples

A hoarder of things
Neighbors complained about the hoarder on their street who never threw anything away.
She became a hoarder after the war, afraid she might run out of essentials.
The show follows a hoarder whose home is filled with newspapers from floor to ceiling.

Synonyms

  • packrat
    8.4 0.09/m
    a packrat is someone who keeps many small useless items at home, while a hoarder stores large quantities, often secretly, for future use.
  • stockpiler
    5.3 0.01/m
    a stockpiler gathers and saves large amounts of supplies for future needs, while a hoarder keeps many things often without clear need or reason.
  • magpie
    8.1 0.07/m
    a magpie collects odd or shiny items people throw away, while a hoarder stores large quantities, often secretly, and may refuse to let go.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collector - a person who collects things, often as a hobby
  • antonym: spender - a person who spends resources freely or lavishly
