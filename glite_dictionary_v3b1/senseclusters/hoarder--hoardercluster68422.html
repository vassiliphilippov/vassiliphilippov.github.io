hoarder

noun
a person who accumulates and stores things excessively
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːdə / (BrE)
a person who collects and stores large quantities of things, often secretly or unnecessarily, for future use

Examples

A hoarder of things
Neighbors complained about the hoarder on their street who never threw anything away.
She became a hoarder after the war, afraid she might run out of essentials.
The show follows a hoarder whose home is filled with newspapers from floor to ceiling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collector - a person who collects things, often as a hobby
  • antonym: spender - a person who spends resources freely or lavishly
