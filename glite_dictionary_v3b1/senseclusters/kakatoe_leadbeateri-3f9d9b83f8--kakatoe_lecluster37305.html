Kakatoe leadbeateri

noun
a white Australian cockatoo with roseate plumage
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkækəˌtoʊ liːdˈbiːtəri / (AmE), / ˈkækətəʊ liːdˈbiːtəri / (BrE)
