backlot

noun
outdoor studio area
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbæklɑt / (AmE), / ˈbæklɒt / (BrE)
an outdoor area on a movie studio's property used for filming scenes, often with sets resembling streets or buildings

Examples

A backlot area
The famous western scene was shot on the studio's backlot to create an authentic town atmosphere.
Tourists enjoyed walking through the backlot and seeing the sets from their favorite movies.
Many outdoor scenes are filmed on a backlot instead of real city streets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: set - a place where a movie or TV scene is filmed
  • antonym: soundstage - an indoor area in a studio for filming scenes
