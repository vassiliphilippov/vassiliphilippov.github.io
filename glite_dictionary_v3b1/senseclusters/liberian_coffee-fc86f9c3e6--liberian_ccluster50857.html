Liberian coffee

noun
West African coffee tree species
5.5
Pronunciation: / lɪˈbɪriən ˈkɔfi / (AmE), / lɪˈbɪəriən ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
a small tree native to West Africa, cultivated for its coffee beans

Examples

Liberian coffee tree
Due to its hardiness, Liberian coffee is sometimes used in hybrid crops.
The farmer decided to plant Liberian coffee alongside his other coffee varieties.
Unlike Arabica, Liberian coffee is less common in global coffee production.
