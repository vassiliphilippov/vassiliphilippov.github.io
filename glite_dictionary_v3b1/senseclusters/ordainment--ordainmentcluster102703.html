ordainment

noun
religious appointment
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈdeɪnmənt / (AmE), / ɔːˈdeɪnmənt / (BrE)
the act of officially making someone a priest or minister in a religious ceremony

Examples

After her ordainment, she began leading the congregation in weekly prayers.
Family members traveled from afar to witness his ordainment last Sunday.
