typist

noun
a person who types documents for work
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪ.pɪst / (AmE), / ˈtaɪ.pɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to type documents, especially using a typewriter

Examples

Office typist
Before computers became common, every large office hired at least one typist.
During the interview, the company tested each typist for speed and accuracy.
Jane worked as a typist in a busy law office for five years.

Related Terms
