engrossingly

adverb
captivatingly
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡroʊsɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡrəʊsɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that completely captures and holds your attention

Examples

To engrossingly hold attention
The documentary was engrossingly presented, making it easy to watch for hours.
She spoke so engrossingly that the entire audience listened in silence.
The movie unfolds engrossingly, keeping viewers on the edge of their seats.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boringly - in a way that fails to interest or excite
  • synonym: captivatingly - in a way that attracts and holds interest
