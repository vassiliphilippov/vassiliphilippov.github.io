pustulous

adjective
covered with pustules
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌstʃuˌləs / (AmE), / ˈpʌstjʊləs / (BrE)
covered with or resembling pustules, especially as a result of infection or disease

Examples

Pustulous skin rash
The doctor noted that the rash was pustulous and required immediate treatment.
After several days, the area became red and pustulous, indicating a possible infection.
A pustulous eruption appeared on his arm following the allergic reaction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - free from spots or blemishes
  • synonym: pustular - relating to or marked by pustules
