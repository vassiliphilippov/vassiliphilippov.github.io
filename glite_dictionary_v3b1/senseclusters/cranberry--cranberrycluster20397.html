cranberry

noun
small, tart red berry used for food
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrænˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈkrænˌbəri / (BrE)
a small, very tart red berry, commonly used to make sauces and juice

Examples

Dried cranberry
Dried cranberry is often added to salads for extra flavor.
She made a delicious sauce with fresh cranberry for the Thanksgiving dinner.
The juice from the cranberry is both tart and refreshing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blueberry - a small, sweet blue-black berry
  • degree lower: currant - a small, tart berry, usually red or black
