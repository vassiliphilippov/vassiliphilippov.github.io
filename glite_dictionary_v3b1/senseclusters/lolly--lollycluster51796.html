Synonyms

  • lollipop
    1.0 0.06/m
    lollipop is a frozen flavored treat on a small stick, while lolly is a general name for sweet snacks on sticks including this.
  • ice lolly
    2.0 0.01/m
    ice lolly is a frozen flavored ice on a small stick often called a popsicle, while lolly covers both frozen and other stick sweets.
  • popsicle
    1.4 1.29/m
    popsicle is a frozen sweet on a stick common in American English, while lolly is a broader word for stick sweets in general.
