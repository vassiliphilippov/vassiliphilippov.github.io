polydispersity

noun
polymer weight variation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːlidɪˈspɜːrsəti / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪdɪˈspɜːsəti / (BrE)
the degree of variation in size or molecular weight among particles or molecules in a sample, especially in chemistry or materials science
