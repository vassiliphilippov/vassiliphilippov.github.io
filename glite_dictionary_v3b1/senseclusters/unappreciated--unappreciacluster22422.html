unappreciated

adjective
not valued or rewarded
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈpriʃieɪtɪd /; / ˌʌn.əˈpri.ʃi.eɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈpriːʃieɪtɪd /; / ˌʌn.əˈpriː.ʃi.eɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
not valued, recognized, or given the praise or reward deserved
