wig out

noun
emotional outburst
Pronunciation: / wɪɡ aʊt / (AmE), / wɪɡ aʊt / (BrE)
an episode of extreme agitation, panic, or emotional loss of control.

Examples

A sudden wig out
After the argument he had a sudden wig out and smashed the lamp in anger.
Her unexpected wig out during the meeting surprised everyone in the office.
The stress of the exam caused him to have a major wig out in the hallway.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - a state of being relaxed and not agitated
  • synonym: meltdown - a sudden loss of control due to stress or emotion
  • synonym: panic attack - a sudden episode of intense fear or anxiety
