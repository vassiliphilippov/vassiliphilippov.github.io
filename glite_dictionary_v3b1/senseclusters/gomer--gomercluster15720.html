gomer

noun
slang for foolish person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊmər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊmə / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who is silly, foolish, or acts in a clumsy way

Examples

Clumsy gomer
He forgot his wallet again—what a gomer!
Don't be such a gomer and remember to lock the door next time.
Everyone laughed when the gomer tripped over his own shoelaces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence
  • synonym: goof - a silly or foolish person
  • synonym: idiot - a person who acts in a very stupid way
