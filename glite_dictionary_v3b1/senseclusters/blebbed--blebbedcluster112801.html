blebbed

adjective
marred by small bubbles or particles (esp. in glass or quartzite)
6.1
Pronunciation: / blɛbd / (AmE), / blɛbd / (BrE)
(of glass or quartzite) marred by small bubbles or small particles of foreign material.

Examples

Blebbed glass marbles
Collectors often reject blebbed glass marbles because of their visible flaws.
The quartzite surface appeared blebbed after exposure to intense heat.
The scientist noted that the sample was blebbed due to imperfections in the cooling process.
