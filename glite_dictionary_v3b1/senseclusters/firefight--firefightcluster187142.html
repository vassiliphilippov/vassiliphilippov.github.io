firefight

verb
to exchange gunfire
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfaɪr.faɪt/ /; / /ˈfaɪ.ər.faɪt/ (AmE), /ˈfaɪ.ə.faɪt/ (BrE)

They were trained to firefight effectively in urban environments.
