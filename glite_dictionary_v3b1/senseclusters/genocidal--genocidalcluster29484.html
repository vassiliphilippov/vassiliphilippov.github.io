genocidal

adjective
relating to genocide
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛnəˈsaɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛnəˈsaɪdəl / (BrE)
relating to genocide or actions intended to destroy a national, ethnic, racial, or religious group

Examples

Genocidal violence

International organizations work to prevent genocidal violence in conflict zones.
The documentary exposed the genocidal intentions behind the regime's policies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: peaceful - not involving violence or conflict
