gin

noun
machine separating seeds from cotton fibers
7.7
Pronunciation: / dʒɪn / (AmE), / dʒɪn / (BrE)
a machine for separating seeds from raw cotton fibers, especially one used in cotton processing

Examples

A cotton gin

Farmers relied on the gin to process large quantities of cotton quickly.
