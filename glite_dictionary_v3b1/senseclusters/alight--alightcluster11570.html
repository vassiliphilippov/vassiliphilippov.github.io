alight

adjective
lit up or burning
3.9
Pronunciation: / əˈlaɪt / (AmE), / əˈlaɪt / (BrE)
lit up by or as if by fire or flame; illuminated or burning

Examples

Eyes alight with joy
Her eyes were alight with excitement as she told the story.
The candles were alight on every table, creating a warm atmosphere.
The whole building was alight when the firefighters arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ablaze - burning brightly, on fire
  • synonym: aflame - burning or on fire; brightly lit
