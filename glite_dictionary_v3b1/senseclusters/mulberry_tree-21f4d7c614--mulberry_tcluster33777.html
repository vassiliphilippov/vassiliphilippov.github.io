mulberry tree

noun
tree producing blackberry-like edible fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌlˌbɛri triː / (AmE), / ˈmʌlbəri triː / (BrE)
A tree of the genus Morus that produces edible fruit resembling blackberries.

Examples

The mulberry tree fruit
Silkworms feed primarily on the leaves of the mulberry tree.
The mulberry tree in our backyard is heavy with fruit this summer.
They planted a row of mulberry trees along the edge of the orchard.
