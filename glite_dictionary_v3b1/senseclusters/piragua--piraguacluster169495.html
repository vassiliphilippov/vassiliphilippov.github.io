piragua

noun
small boat or ice dessert
Pronunciation: / piˈrɑːɡwə / (AmE), / piˈrɑːɡwə / (BrE)
a small, narrow boat, or in some regions, a dessert made of flavored shaved ice

Examples

Wooden piragua boat
The fishermen used a piragua to reach the far side of the lake.
On hot days, children line up for a sweet piragua from the street vendor.
We rented a piragua and explored the calm waters of the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canoe - a narrow boat pointed at both ends and moved by paddles
  • synonym: shaved ice - a dessert made of finely shaved ice flavored with syrup
