chain

noun
blockchain; distributed ledger
7.0
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪn / (AmE), / tʃeɪn / (BrE)
(in cryptocurrency and distributed-ledger technology) a blockchain; a particular distributed digital ledger made of cryptographically linked blocks that records transactions and is maintained by a decentralized network.

Examples

Ethereum chain
Developers often deploy their dApp on the Ethereum chain to access its smart-contract features.
Before transferring tokens, check which chain the wallet supports to avoid losing funds.
The transaction was recorded on a public chain, viewable by anyone on the network.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blockchain - a distributed ledger of cryptographically linked blocks that records transactions
  • synonym: distributed ledger - a database that is shared and synchronized across multiple sites or institutions
