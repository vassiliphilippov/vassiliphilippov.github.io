oiler

noun
a ship for carrying crude oil
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɪlər / (AmE), / ˈɔɪlə / (BrE)
a cargo ship specifically built to transport crude oil in large quantities.

Examples

A large oiler ship
Several oilers were anchored off the coast, waiting for clearance to enter the harbor.
The company invested in a new oiler to expand its global shipping operations.
The massive oiler docked at the port to unload its shipment of crude oil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tanker - a large ship for transporting liquids in bulk, especially oil or gas
