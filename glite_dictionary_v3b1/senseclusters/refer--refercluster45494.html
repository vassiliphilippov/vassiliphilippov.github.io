refer

verb
use a word/name to designate
Pronunciation: / rəˈfɜr /; / rəˈfɝː / (AmE), / rɪˈfəː /; / rɪˈfɜː / (BrE)
to use a word, name, or symbol to describe, indicate, classify, or mean something

Examples
