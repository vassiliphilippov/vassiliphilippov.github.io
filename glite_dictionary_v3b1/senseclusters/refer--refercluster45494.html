refer

verb
use a word/name to designate
Pronunciation: / rəˈfɜr /; / rəˈfɝː / (AmE), / rɪˈfəː /; / rɪˈfɜː / (BrE)
to use a word, name, or symbol to describe, indicate, classify, or mean something

Examples

Refer to as friends
He refers to his colleagues as friends rather than just coworkers.
Many people refer to the capital city as the heart of the country.
The planet is often referred to as the 'Blue Planet.'
Linguists often refer such words to a broader category of loanwords.
Some botanists refer this plant to a different genus.
The symptoms can be referred to several related diseases.
In linguistics, the word 'dog' refers to a domestic canine animal.
In this context, the color red refers to danger or warning.
The prefix 'auto-' refers to something related to oneself or automatic processes.
