perspicuousness

noun
clarity; ease of understanding
7.2
Pronunciation: / pərˈspɪkjuəsnəs / (AmE), / pəːˈspɪkjuəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being clear and easy to understand, especially in style or expression

Examples

Clear perspicuousness
He valued perspicuousness in scientific writing above all else.
The perspicuousness of her argument made it easy to follow and convincing.
Writers are often praised for the perspicuousness of their prose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarity - the quality of being clear, in particular in expression
  • antonym: obscurity - the state of being difficult to understand
