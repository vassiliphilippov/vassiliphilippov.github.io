holystone

noun
sandstone block for scrubbing ship decks
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊliˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈhəʊliˌstəʊn / (BrE)
a block of soft sandstone used for scrubbing and cleaning the wooden decks of ships

Examples

A holystone block
A worn holystone lay abandoned by the rail after the cleaning was done.
Sailors once used a holystone to clean the wooden decks every morning.
The captain inspected the crew as they scrubbed the ship with holystones.
