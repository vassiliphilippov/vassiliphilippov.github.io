remorsefully

adverb
in a manner showing regret or guilt
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɔrsfəli / (AmE), / rɪˈmɔːsfəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows deep regret, guilt, or sorrow for a wrongdoing.

Examples

Look remorsefully
After realizing the damage he caused, he looked remorsefully at his friend.
She remorsefully apologized for her harsh words during the argument.
The employee remorsefully confessed to making the accounting error.

  • synonym: ruefully - in a mournful or regretful manner
