cow pie

noun
piece of cow feces
6.9
Pronunciation: / kaʊ paɪ / (AmE), / kaʊ paɪ / (BrE)
A piece of cow feces, typically found in fields or pastures.

Examples

A cow pie in field
She used a shovel to clear away the cow pies from the barnyard.

Watch your step in the field, or you might step in a cow pie.
