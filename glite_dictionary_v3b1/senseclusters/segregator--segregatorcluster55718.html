segregator

noun
person who supports separation of groups, esp. by race
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛɡrɪˌɡeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsɛɡrɪˌɡeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who supports or promotes the separation of people, especially on the basis of race

Examples

A segregator in schools
A known segregator, he opposed efforts to integrate the local schools.
She studied the history of segregators and their impact on society.
The governor was criticized as a staunch segregator during the civil rights era.

Related Terms

  • antonym: integrationist - a person who supports the combining of different racial or ethnic groups
  • synonym: segregationist - a person who supports and enforces segregation
