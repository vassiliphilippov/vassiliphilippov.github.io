strip miner

noun
person who does strip mining
2.4
Pronunciation: / strɪp ˈmaɪnər / (AmE), / strɪp ˈmaɪnə / (BrE)
a person who extracts minerals or coal from the surface of the earth by strip mining, which involves removing large strips of soil and rock covering the mineral deposits

Examples

Strip miner worker
After years of work as a strip miner, he developed a deep understanding of surface mining techniques.
Environmental groups often criticize the practices of the strip miner for the damage caused to the landscape.
The strip miner operated heavy machinery to remove large sections of earth from the site.
