laid up

adjective
confined by illness or injury
Pronunciation: / leɪd ʌp / (AmE), / leɪd ʌp / (BrE)
Confined to bed or unable to work due to illness or injury.

Examples

Be laid up in bed
After his surgery, he was laid up in the hospital for several days.
I'm sorry I missed your party; I was laid up with a terrible cold.
She was laid up with the flu for over a week.
