historical account

noun
narrative or record of past events
1.7 3.0
Pronunciation: /hɪ.ˈstɔːr.ɪ.kəl ə.ˈkaʊnt/ (AmE), /hɪ.ˈstɒr.ɪ.kəl ə.ˈkaʊnt/ (BrE)

Examples
