whupping

noun
decisive defeat
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwʌpɪŋ / (BrE)
a strong beating or a decisive defeat, especially in a competition or fight

Examples

A big whupping
The underdog team gave their rivals a real whupping in the championship game.
After the argument, he joked that he deserved a whupping for his stubbornness.
Fans were shocked by the whupping their favorite boxer received in the ring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thrashing - a severe beating or overwhelming defeat
  • antonym: victory - the act of winning or defeating an opponent
