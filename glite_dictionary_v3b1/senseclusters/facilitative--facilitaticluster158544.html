facilitative

adjective
making a process easier; removing difficulties
Pronunciation: / fəˈsɪlɪˌteɪtɪv / (AmE), / fəˈsɪlɪtətɪv / (BrE)
making a process easier or less difficult; helping to remove obstacles or difficulties.

Examples

Facilitative role
Leadership training plays a facilitative role in team development and communication.
The new policy takes a facilitative approach to innovation in the technology sector.
The organization's facilitative changes have helped streamline the application process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facilitating - making something easier; helping progress
  • antonym: impeding - making progress more difficult; hindering
