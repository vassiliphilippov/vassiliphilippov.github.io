neuropsychiatric

adjective
relating to neuropsychiatry
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˌsaɪkiˈætrɪk / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˌsaɪkiˈætrɪk / (BrE)
Relating to neuropsychiatry or to disorders with both neurological and psychiatric features.

Examples

Neuropsychiatric disorders
Some viruses can cause neuropsychiatric complications such as confusion and mood changes.
The patient showed significant neuropsychiatric symptoms after the head injury.
Treatment of neuropsychiatric disorders often requires a multidisciplinary approach.
