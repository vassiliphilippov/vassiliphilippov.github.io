craquelure

noun
fine surface cracks
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkræk.əl.jʊr/ /; / /ˈkræk.əl.ʊr/ (AmE), /ˈkræk.əl.jʊə/ /; / /ˈkræk.əl.ʊə/ (BrE)
a network or pattern of fine cracks on the surface of a painting, varnish, or ceramic glaze, usually from aging or sometimes as a decorative effect
