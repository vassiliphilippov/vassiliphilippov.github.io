bead

noun
rounded molding in furniture or architecture
4.1 1.57/m
Pronunciation: / bid / (AmE), / biÀêd / (BrE)
A small, rounded decorative molding used for edging or ornamenting furniture, doors, or windows.

Examples

A bead molding
A narrow bead was carved along the windowsill to match the room's trim.
The cabinet features a delicate bead along its edges for added elegance.
The craftsman carefully sanded the bead to ensure a smooth finish on the table.

Synonyms

  • beading
    5.4 0.02/m
    beading is a narrow row of small round moldings used to edge furniture or doors, while bead is a single rounded molding.
  • beadwork
    6.1 0.00/m
    beadwork is decoration made from many beads on a surface, while bead is a single small rounded molding used for edging.
