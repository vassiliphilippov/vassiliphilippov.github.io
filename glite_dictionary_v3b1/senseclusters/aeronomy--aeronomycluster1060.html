aeronomy

noun
study of upper atmosphere
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛˈrɑːnəmi / (AmE), / ɛəˈrɒnəmi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the upper layers of a planet's atmosphere, especially their physical and chemical properties

Examples

Aeronomy study
Researchers in aeronomy analyze how solar winds interact with the Earth's upper atmosphere.
Advances in aeronomy have improved our understanding of the ozone layer and its changes.
The field of aeronomy is important for predicting satellite behavior in space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atmospheric science - the study of the Earth's atmosphere and its processes
  • synonym: meteorology - the scientific study of weather and climate
