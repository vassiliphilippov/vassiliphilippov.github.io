blurriness

noun
the state of being blurry
4.5 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / Ààbl…úÀêri.n…ôs / (AmE), / Ààbl år.i.n…ôs / (BrE)
the quality of being indistinct or lacking sharpness in detail or outline

Examples

Eye blurriness
Eye strain can sometimes cause temporary blurriness in your vision.
The blurriness of the photo made it hard to recognize who was in it.
The scene was filled with a dreamy blurriness that gave it a surreal quality.

Synonyms

    6.7 0.00/m
    unsharpness is the general lack of visual focus or clarity in images, while blurriness describes soft, unclear edges.
  • fuzziness
    5.8 0.30/m
    fuzziness shows soft, fuzzy edges or unclear texture in an image, while blurriness refers to overall loss of sharp detail.
  • indistinctness
    7.0 0.02/m
    indistinctness means things are hard to see or name because they lack clear form, while blurriness means low visual sharpness.
  • blur
    4.2 8.90/m
    a blur is a vague shape or smeared mark you can notice, while blurriness is the general loss of image detail.
  • fogginess
    4.4 0.03/m
    fogginess describes low visibility like from weather or mental confusion, while blurriness is about lack of sharp visual detail.
  • furriness
    5.4 0.00/m
    furriness means an image looks like it has soft, hairlike texture, while blurriness means edges and details are not sharp.
