Sonoran Desert

proper noun
desert in southwestern US and Mexico
Pronunciation: / səˈnɔɹən ˈdɛzɚt / (AmE), / səˈnɔːrən ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
a vast desert region in southwestern Arizona, southeastern California, and northwestern Mexico, known for its arid climate and unique flora and fauna

Examples
