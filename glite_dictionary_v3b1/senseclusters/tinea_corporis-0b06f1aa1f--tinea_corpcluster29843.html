tinea corporis

noun
fungal skin infection (not scalp/groin)
6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtɪ.niː.ə/ /; / /ˈtɪ.niː.ər/ (AmE), /ˈtɪ.niː.ə/ (BrE)
