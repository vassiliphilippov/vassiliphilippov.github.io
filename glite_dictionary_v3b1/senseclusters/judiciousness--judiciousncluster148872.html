judiciousness

noun
the quality of good judgment
5.5 5.8
Pronunciation: /dʒuː.ˈdɪʃ.ə.nəs/ /; / /dʒuː.ˈdɪʃ.nəs/ (AmE), /dʒuː.ˈdɪʃ.ə.nəs/ /; / /dʒuː.ˈdɪʃ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of having or showing good judgment, especially in making decisions or forming opinions

Examples

As a manager, his judiciousness helped him resolve conflicts fairly and efficiently.
