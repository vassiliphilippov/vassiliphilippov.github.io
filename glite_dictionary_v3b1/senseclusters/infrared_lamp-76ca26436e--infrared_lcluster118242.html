infrared lamp

noun
electric lamp emitting infrared radiation
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfrəˌrɛd læmp / (AmE), / ˌɪnfrəˌrɛd lamp / (BrE)
an electric lamp that produces infrared radiation, especially for heating or therapeutic purposes

Examples

An infrared lamp heater
In physiotherapy, an infrared lamp is commonly applied to relieve pain and stiffness.
She used an infrared lamp to keep her muscles warm after the workout.
The infrared lamp helped speed up the paint drying process in the workshop.
