St. Christopher

proper noun
Christian patron saint of travellers
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈkrɪstəfər / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈkrɪstəfə / (BrE)
A Christian martyr and the patron saint of travellers, believed to have lived in the 3rd century.

Examples

St. Christopher medal
Legend says that St. Christopher carried a child across a dangerous river.
Many people carry a St. Christopher medal for protection when they travel.
The church dedicated to St. Christopher is a popular pilgrimage site.
