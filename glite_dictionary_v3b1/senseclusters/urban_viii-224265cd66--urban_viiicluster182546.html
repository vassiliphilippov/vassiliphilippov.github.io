Urban VIII

proper noun
Italian pope (1623–1644) who condemned Galileo
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɜrbən ðə ˈeɪtθ / (AmE), / ˌɜːbən ði ˈeɪtθ / (BrE)
(1568–1644) Italian pope from 1623 to 1644, known for sanctioning the condemnation of Galileo and later freeing him.
