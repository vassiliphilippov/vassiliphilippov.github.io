chop shop

noun
illegal garage for dismantling stolen cars
7.1
Pronunciation: / tʃɑp ʃɑp / (AmE), / tʃɒp ʃɒp / (BrE)
an illegal garage or workshop where stolen vehicles are dismantled so that their parts can be sold separately

Examples

A chop shop
He suspected that his missing car might have ended up in a chop shop.
Many stolen vehicles are quickly stripped for parts at a chop shop.
The police raided a chop shop that had dozens of stolen cars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: garage - a place for repairing or keeping motor vehicles
