underfeeding

noun
insufficient nourishment
4.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dər.ˈfiː.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.ˈfiː.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of providing too little food to a person, animal, or plant.

Examples

Child underfeeding
Chronic underfeeding can weaken the immune system and slow growth in children.
The shelter rescued several animals suffering from underfeeding and neglect.
Farmers must avoid underfeeding their crops to ensure a healthy harvest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malnutrition - a condition caused by not getting enough nutrients from food
  • antonym: overfeeding - the act of giving too much food
  • degree higher: starvation - extreme suffering or death caused by lack of food
