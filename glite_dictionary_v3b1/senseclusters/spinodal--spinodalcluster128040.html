spinodal

adjective
unstable phase region
Pronunciation: / spaɪˈnoʊdəl / (AmE), / spaɪˈnəʊdəl / (BrE)
relating to a region or condition in which a mixture becomes unstable and separates into different phases

Examples

Spinodal region
The scientist explained the spinodal region where the solution would separate into two phases.
During rapid cooling, the material entered a spinodal state and began to decompose.
Researchers studied the spinodal decomposition process in polymer blends to improve material properties.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stable - not likely to change or break apart
  • synonym: unstable - likely to change or break apart
