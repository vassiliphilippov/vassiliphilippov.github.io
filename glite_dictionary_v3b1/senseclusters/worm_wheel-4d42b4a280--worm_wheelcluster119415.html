worm wheel

noun
gear meshing with a worm in machinery
8.1 8.4
Pronunciation: / wɜrm wiːl / (AmE), / wɜːm wiːl / (BrE)
a gear in a worm gear mechanism that meshes with the thread of a worm to transmit motion at a right angle

Examples

A worm wheel gear
A worm wheel typically transmits motion at a right angle to the worm.

The worm wheel in the gearbox helps change the direction of the rotating force.
