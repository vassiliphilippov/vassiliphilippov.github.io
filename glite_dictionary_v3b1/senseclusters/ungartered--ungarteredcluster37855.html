ungartered

adjective
without a garter
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡɑːrtərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡɑːtəd / (BrE)
not held up or fastened with a garter.

Examples

Ungartered stocking

The old photograph showed a woman with ungartered socks slipping down her legs.
He noticed her ungartered hosiery as she adjusted her shoes.
