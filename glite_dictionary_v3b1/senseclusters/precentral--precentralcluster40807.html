precentral

adjective
in front of central sulcus
Pronunciation: / priːˈsɛntrəl / (AmE), / priːˈsɛntrəl / (BrE)
located in front of the central sulcus of the brain

Examples

The precentral gyrus
The precentral gyrus plays a key role in voluntary motor control.
Damage to the precentral area can affect a person's ability to move certain muscles.
Researchers studied the precentral cortex to understand how the brain initiates movement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postcentral - located behind the central sulcus of the brain
