affiliative

adjective
friendly; social bonding
6.9
Pronunciation: / əˈfɪliˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / əˈfɪliətɪv / (BrE)
showing friendliness or a desire to form social bonds and connections with others

Examples

Affiliative behavior
The teacher used an affiliative approach to build trust with her students.
Animals often display affiliative behaviors to strengthen group bonds.
His affiliative nature made him popular among his colleagues.
