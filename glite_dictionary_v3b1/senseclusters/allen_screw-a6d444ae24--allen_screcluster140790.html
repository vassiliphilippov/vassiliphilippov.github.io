Allen screw

noun
screw with hexagonal hole in head
6.6 5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈælən skuː / (AmE), / ˈælən skuː / (BrE)
A screw with a hexagonal hole in the head, designed to be driven by a hex key or Allen wrench.

Examples

An Allen screw head

Many modern electronics are held together using small Allen screws instead of traditional screws.
The furniture assembly instructions recommend tightening each Allen screw with the provided hex key.
