Fourierist

adjective
of Fourier's theories
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊriərɪst / (AmE), / ˈfʊəriərɪst / (BrE)
relating to the ideas, theories, or followers of Charles Fourier, especially his social and communal philosophies.

Examples

Fourierist ideas
The community was organized according to Fourierist principles of shared labor and resources.
Historians studied the influence of Fourierist thought on 19th-century social movements.
A Fourierist approach to urban planning emphasized cooperation and communal living.

Related Terms

  • synonym: socialist - relating to or supporting socialism
  • synonym: utopian - relating to ideal or perfect societies
