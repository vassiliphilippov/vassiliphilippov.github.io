moray

noun
a large, brightly colored eel
8.3 0.30/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…îÀêre…™ / (AmE), / Ààm…íre…™ / (BrE)
A large, brightly colored eel found in warm coastal waters, known for its ferocity and often distinctive patterning. Morays are generally not aggressive to humans, but larger species can be dangerous if provoked.

Examples

A large moray eel
A large green moray suddenly emerged from its crevice, startling the snorkelers nearby.
Morays are often admired for their striking appearance and vibrant colors.
The diver spotted a moray hiding between the rocks of the coral reef.
