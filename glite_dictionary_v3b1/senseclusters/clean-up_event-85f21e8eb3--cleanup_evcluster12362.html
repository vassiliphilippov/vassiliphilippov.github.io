clean-up event

noun
event to remove litter from a place
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈklin ʌp ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈkliːn ʌp ɪˈvent / (BrE)
an organized activity in which a group of people gather to remove litter or waste from a public place

Examples

Community clean-up event
After the festival, a clean-up event helped restore the area to its original condition.
Dozens of volunteers attended the clean-up event at the city park last Saturday.
The school organized a clean-up event to teach students about environmental responsibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: litter pick - an event to pick up trash from an area
  • degree higher: volunteer activity - an event where people volunteer to do work for the community
