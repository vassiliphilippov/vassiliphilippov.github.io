rockist purist

noun
someone rigidly devoted to rock music standards
5.9 9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːkɪst ˈpjʊrɪst / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪst ˈpjʊərɪst / (BrE)
a person who strictly adheres to traditional standards of rock music, often dismissing other genres as inferior

Examples

A rockist purist fan
As a rockist purist, he refused to listen to any hip-hop or electronic music.
Many critics argue that being a rockist purist limits one’s appreciation for modern music innovation.
The debate between rockist purists and pop enthusiasts is common in music forums.
