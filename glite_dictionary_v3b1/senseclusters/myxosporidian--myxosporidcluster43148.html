myxosporidian

noun
parasitic protozoan affecting fish
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪk.soʊ.spəˈrɪd.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌmɪk.səʊ.spəˈrɪd.i.ən / (BrE)
a protozoan of the class Myxosporea, mostly parasitic in fish and including various serious pathogens

Examples

A myxosporidian parasite
A myxosporidian parasite can cause significant damage to the gills of salmon.
Scientists are studying how myxosporidians affect aquaculture and wild ecosystems.
The disease was traced to a myxosporidian infecting the fish population in the pond.
