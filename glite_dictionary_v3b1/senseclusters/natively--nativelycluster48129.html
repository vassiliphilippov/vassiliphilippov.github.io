natively

adverb
in a natural way
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪtɪvli / (AmE), / ˈneɪtɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that is natural, original, or inherent to a person, place, or thing

Examples

Speaks natively
She speaks French natively because she was born and raised in Paris.
Some plants grow natively only in tropical regions and cannot survive cold climates.
The software runs natively on both Windows and Mac without needing an emulator.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artificially - in a way that is not natural or is made by people
  • synonym: naturally - in a way that is normal or expected for someone or something
