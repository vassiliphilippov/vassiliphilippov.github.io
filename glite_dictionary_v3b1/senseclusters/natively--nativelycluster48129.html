natively

adverb
in a natural way
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪtɪvli / (AmE), / ˈneɪtɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that is natural, original, or inherent to a person, place, or thing

Examples

Speaks natively

The software runs natively on both Windows and Mac without needing an emulator.
