firmness of purpose

noun
determined commitment to a goal
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrmnəs əv ˈpɜrpəs / (AmE), / ˈfɜːmnəs əv ˈpɜːpəs / (BrE)
the quality of being resolutely committed to achieving a goal or following a course of action

Examples

Firmness of purpose in action
He faced criticism with a firmness of purpose that impressed even his opponents.
Her firmness of purpose helped the team complete the project despite numerous setbacks.
Many admired her firmness of purpose in pursuing justice for her community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: determination - the quality of being determined; firmness of resolve
  • antonym: indecisiveness - the trait of being unable to make firm decisions
  • synonym: resoluteness - the trait of being firm and unwavering
