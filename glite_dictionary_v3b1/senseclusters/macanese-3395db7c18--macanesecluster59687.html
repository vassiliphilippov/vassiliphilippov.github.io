Macanese

noun
Portuguese-based creole
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmækəˈniːz / (AmE), / ˌmækəˈniːz / (BrE)
the Portuguese-based creole language traditionally spoken by the Macanese community in Macau, also known as Patuá

Examples

Macanese language

Few young people today are fluent in Macanese, making efforts to preserve the language even more urgent.
The unique vocabulary of Macanese reflects influences from Portuguese, Cantonese, and other languages.
