bugbane

noun
perennial plant of the Cimicifuga genus
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌɡˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈbʌɡˌbeɪn / (BrE)
a perennial plant of the genus Cimicifuga, having white or cream flowers in long spikes and reputed to repel insects

Examples

Bugbane repels insects
Herbalists sometimes recommend bugbane for its traditional use in repelling insects.
In midsummer, the delicate plumes of bugbane add a graceful touch to woodland gardens.
The gardener planted bugbane along the shady border to help deter mosquitoes.
