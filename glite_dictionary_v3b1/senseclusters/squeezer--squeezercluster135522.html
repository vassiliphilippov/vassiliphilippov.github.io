squeezer

noun
utensil for extracting juice from fruit
1.8 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààskwiz…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàskwiÀêz…ô / (BrE)
A kitchen utensil used to extract juice from fruit by pressing or squeezing.

Examples
