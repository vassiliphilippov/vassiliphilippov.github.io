mornin'

interjection
informal morning greeting
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrnɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪn / (BrE)
an informal greeting used to say 'good morning' to someone, especially in casual conversation

Examples

Friendly mornin' greeting
He waved at his neighbor and called out, "mornin'!" as he left for work.
With a big yawn, she greeted her family at the table, saying, "mornin' everyone."
The shopkeeper smiled and said, "mornin', how can I help you today?"

Related Terms

  • antonym: evening - a greeting used later in the day
  • synonym: good morning - a polite greeting used in the morning
