Examples

  • bloodline
    4.8 1.74/m

  • ancestry
    4.1 4.86/m

  • family line
    3.2 0.28/m

  • descent
    4.6 7.06/m
    descent describes origin or family background across generations, while pedigree adds formal records and often a claim of purity or distinction.
  • parentage
    5.3 0.80/m

  • stock

  • line
