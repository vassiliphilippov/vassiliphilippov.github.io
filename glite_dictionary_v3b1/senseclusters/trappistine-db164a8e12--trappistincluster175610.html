Trappistine

noun
female Trappist nun
9.9
Pronunciation: / træpˈiːstiːn / (AmE), / træpˈiːstiːn / (BrE)
a nun who is a member of the Cistercian order of Trappists, devoted to a life of prayer and work

Examples

A Trappistine nun
The Trappistine rose early each morning to begin her prayers in the quiet chapel.
Visitors to the abbey were welcomed by a friendly Trappistine who guided them through the gardens.
After many years of service, the elderly Trappistine became a respected figure in her community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Trappist - a monk of the Cistercian order of Trappists
  • antonym: monk - a man who has taken religious vows and lives in a monastery
  • synonym: nun - a woman who has taken religious vows and lives in a convent
