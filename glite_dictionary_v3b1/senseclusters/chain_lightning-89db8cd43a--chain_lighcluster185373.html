chain lightning

noun
magical attack hitting multiple targets
9.1
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪn ˈlaɪtnɪŋ / (AmE), / tʃeɪn ˈlaɪtnɪŋ / (BrE)
(in fantasy, gaming, and fiction) a magical or special attack that strikes one target and then arcs to hit additional nearby targets in sequence.

Examples
