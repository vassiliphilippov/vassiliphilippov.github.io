alternative lifestyle

noun
nontraditional way of living
Pronunciation: / ɔlˈtɝːnətɪv ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (AmE), / ɔːlˈtɜːnətɪv ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
a way of living that is noticeably different from what is considered conventional or typical in society

Examples

An alternative lifestyle choice
After college, she chose to pursue an alternative lifestyle focused on sustainability and community living.
Some parents worry when their children express interest in an alternative lifestyle rather than following tradition.
The documentary highlighted people who have embraced an alternative lifestyle outside of city life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mainstream lifestyle - a conventional or typical way of living in a society
