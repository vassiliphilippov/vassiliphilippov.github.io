bawk

interjection
chicken alarm call
Pronunciation: / bɔk / (AmE), / bɔːk / (BrE)
an exclamation imitating the clucking sound of a chicken, used to express alarm or attract attention

Examples

Bawk of surprise
Suddenly, a loud bawk echoed from the chicken coop, startling everyone nearby.
The children laughed as they tried to imitate the hen's bawk during the farm visit.
Hearing a sharp bawk, the farmer rushed outside to check on his flock.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cluck - the sound made by a chicken
