submit an affidavit

verb
formally present a sworn written statement
Pronunciation: / səbˈmɪt ən ˌæfəˈdeɪvɪt / (AmE), / səbˈmɪt ən ˌæfəˈdeɪvɪt / (BrE)
to formally present a written statement under oath, usually for use as evidence in legal proceedings

Examples

To submit an affidavit
After the incident, she decided to submit an affidavit detailing everything she had witnessed.
If you cannot appear in person, you may submit an affidavit explaining your absence.
The witness was required to submit an affidavit before the court.
