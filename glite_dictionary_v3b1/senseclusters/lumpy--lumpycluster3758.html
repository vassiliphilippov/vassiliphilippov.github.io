lumpy

adjective
full of lumps or small clumps
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌm.pi /; / ˈlʌmpi / (AmE), / ˈlʌm.pi /; / ˈlʌmpi / (BrE)
having small solid pieces or lumps in it, so it is not smooth

Examples

A lumpy porridge
After sitting overnight, the porridge became lumpy and thick.
She didn't like the lumpy texture of the mashed potatoes.
The sauce turned out lumpy because I forgot to stir it properly.
After sitting in the fridge overnight, the soup became thick and lumpy.
He tried to smooth out the lumpy mashed potatoes before serving them.
The cake batter turned out lumpy because I didn't mix it well enough.
