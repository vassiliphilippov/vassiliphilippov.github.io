lumpy

adjective
full of lumps or small clumps

Examples

A lumpy porridge
After sitting overnight, the porridge became lumpy and thick.
She didn't like the lumpy texture of the mashed potatoes.

Synonyms

  • clumpy
    4.7 0.18/m

  • chunky
    3.5 1.10/m
    chunky has larger, noticeable pieces or a thick texture, while lumpy means many small bumps that make a surface not smooth.
