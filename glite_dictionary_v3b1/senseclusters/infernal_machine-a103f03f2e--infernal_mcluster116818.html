infernal machine

noun
timed bomb, often for sabotage
6.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɜrnəl məˈʃin / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɜːnəl məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a bomb fitted with a timed detonating mechanism, especially one designed for sabotage or assassination
