hemiparetic

noun
person with hemiparesis
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛmiˌpəˈrɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɛmiˌpəˈrɛtɪk / (BrE)
a person who has hemiparesis, meaning weakness or partial paralysis on one side of the body

Examples

A hemiparetic walks
The physical therapist designed exercises specifically for the hemiparetic to improve mobility.
A hemiparetic may need assistance with daily activities due to weakness on one side.
Support groups can help a hemiparetic adjust to changes in their physical abilities.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: hemiplegic - a person with complete paralysis on one side of the body
