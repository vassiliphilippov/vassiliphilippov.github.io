heeler

noun
political henchman
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːlər / (AmE), / ˈhiːlə / (BrE)
a political worker who performs loyal, often unpleasant or underhanded tasks for a political boss or organization

Examples

A political heeler
The mayor's trusted heeler handled all the campaign's behind-the-scenes negotiations.
Rumors spread that the heeler was responsible for intimidating rival supporters during the election.
Every political boss seemed to have a loyal heeler to do the dirty work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boss - a person in charge, especially in politics or crime
  • synonym: henchman - a loyal supporter who does unpleasant or illegal work for a powerful person
  • synonym: operative - a worker, especially one involved in secret or political activities
