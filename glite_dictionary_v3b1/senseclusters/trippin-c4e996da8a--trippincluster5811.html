trippin'

verb
to act irrationally
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪpɪn / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpɪn / (BrE)
acting in a confused, irrational, or overly emotional way, often in a surprising or exaggerated manner

Examples

You are trippin' out
You are trippin' if you think I would forget your birthday.
He started trippin' when he saw the surprise party waiting for him.
She was trippin' over nothing, worrying about things that might never happen.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm down - to become less upset or excited
  • synonym: overreact - to respond more emotionally or forcefully than necessary
