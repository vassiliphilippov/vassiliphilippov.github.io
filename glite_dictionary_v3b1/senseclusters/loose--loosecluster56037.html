loose

adjective
not possessed or controlled (sports ball)
3.5 1.7
Pronunciation: /luːs/ (AmE), /luːs/ (BrE)
free, not contained or restrained; not in anyone's possession or control

Examples

Loose ball
A loose ball near the baseline led to a frantic fight for possession.
After the rebound, players scrambled for the loose puck on the ice.
The defender rushed to recover the loose ball in front of the goal.
The children were thrilled to see so many horses running loose in the field.
The police warned that several dangerous animals were loose in the area after the storm.
Two convicts remained loose for days before being recaptured.
