chachalaca

noun
Central American game bird
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃɑː.tʃəˈlɑː.kə / (AmE), / tʃa.tʃəˈlɑː.kə / (BrE)
A slender, tree-dwelling bird native to Central America and Mexico, resembling a wild turkey and valued as a game bird.

Examples

The chachalaca bird
Many hunters in Mexico consider the chachalaca a prized game bird.
The chachalaca is often heard before it is seen, thanks to its loud, distinctive call.
