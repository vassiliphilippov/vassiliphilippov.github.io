prospect

noun
chance of future success
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.spɛkt /; / ˈprɑːspɛkt / (AmE), / ˈprɒs.pɛkt /; / ˈprɒspɛkt / (BrE)
the chance or possibility that something will happen in the future; an expectation or prediction about what might occur
