roux

noun
a fat and flour mixture used as a sauce base
9.9
Pronunciation: / ruː / (AmE), / ruː / (BrE)
a mixture of fat and flour cooked together and used as a base for thickening sauces and soups

Examples

A roux base
A dark roux gives gumbo its distinctive flavor and color.
She whisked the roux until it turned a light golden color before adding the milk.
To make the perfect béchamel, start by preparing a roux with butter and flour.
