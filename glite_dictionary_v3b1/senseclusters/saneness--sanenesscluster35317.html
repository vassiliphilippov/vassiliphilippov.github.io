saneness

noun
soundness or normality of mind
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪn.nəs / (AmE), / ˈseɪn.nəs / (BrE)
The condition of having a normal, healthy state of mind; mental soundness.

Examples

The saneness of mind
Her saneness was questioned after a series of strange decisions.
In times of stress, meditation can help preserve one's saneness.
There was a calm saneness to his approach, even under pressure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insanity - the state of being seriously mentally ill; madness
  • synonym: sanity - sound mental health; the normal condition of the mind
