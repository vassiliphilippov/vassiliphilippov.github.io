floor

noun
level or storey in a building
1.0 46/m
Pronunciation: / fl…î…π / (AmE), / fl…îÀê / (BrE)
a level of a building that forms one area or set of rooms at the same height; a storey

Examples

Fifth floor
Our office is on the fifth floor of the building.
She pressed the button for her floor in the elevator.
The hotel has twenty floors, with a restaurant on the top level.

Synonyms

    1.3 33/m
    story names one level of a building in descriptions, while floor also refers to the room surface inside a building.
  • level
    1.0 63/m
    level can mean a flat surface or a building story in many contexts, while floor is the specific area or storey inside a building.
