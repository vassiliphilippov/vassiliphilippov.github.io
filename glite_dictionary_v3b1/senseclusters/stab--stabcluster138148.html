stab

noun
attempt or effort
8.6 3.7
Pronunciation: /stæb/ (AmE), /stæb/ (BrE)
An attempt or effort to do something, especially one that is not certain to succeed; a try.

Examples

Take a stab at it
He decided to take a stab at painting, even though he had no experience.
She made a stab at answering the difficult question, but wasn't completely sure.
This is my first stab at baking bread, so it might not turn out perfect.
