backstabbing

noun
secret betrayal
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌstæbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbækˌstæbɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of betraying someone secretly or unfairly, especially by pretending to be friendly while acting against them

Examples

Office backstabbing
Office politics often involve unexpected backstabbing that can damage trust among coworkers.
He was hurt by the backstabbing of someone he thought was a close friend.
Rumors and backstabbing made the workplace environment very stressful for everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: betrayal - the act of being disloyal or unfaithful to someone
  • antonym: loyalty - the quality of being faithful and supportive
