thalamic

adjective
of the floral receptacle
8.8
Pronunciation: / θəˈlæmɪk / (AmE), / θəˈlæmɪk / (BrE)
relating to the thalamus, especially the floral receptacle or torus that supports the organs of a flower in botany.

Examples

Thalamic region of flower
The thalamic region of the flower supports the petals and stamens.
Researchers studied the thalamic structure to understand flower development.
A mutation in the thalamic tissue affected the arrangement of the floral organs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: receptacular - relating to the receptacle of a flower
