overweighting

noun
excessive loading
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈweɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈweɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of adding too much weight to something, making it heavier than is safe or appropriate

Examples

Overweighting the elevator
The overweighting of the elevator led to a safety alarm being triggered.
Repeated overweighting of the shelves caused them to collapse unexpectedly.
Strict regulations prevent the overweighting of commercial vehicles on highways.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overloading - placing too much load or weight on something
  • antonym: underweighting - adding less weight than is needed or expected
