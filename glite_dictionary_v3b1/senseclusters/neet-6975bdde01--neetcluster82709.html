NEET

noun
unemployed youth
8.7
Pronunciation: / niːt / (AmE), / niːt / (BrE)
a young person who is not in education, employment, or training

Examples

A NEET youth
The government launched a program to help NEET youth find jobs.
Many NEET individuals face challenges re-entering education or the workforce.
The report highlighted a rise in the number of NEET young adults in urban areas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: student - a person who is studying at a school or college
  • synonym: unemployed - not having a paid job
  • antonym: worker - a person who is employed
