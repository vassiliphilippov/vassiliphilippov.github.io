sugar snap pea

noun
variety of pea with edible, crisp pods
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʊɡɚ snæp pi / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə snæp piː / (BrE)
a type of pea with a sweet taste and a crisp, rounded, edible pod that can be eaten whole, raw or cooked
