mi

noun
third note of major scale (solfège)
Pronunciation: / mi / (AmE), / mi / (BrE)
the syllable used to name the third note of a major scale in solmization (solfège)

Examples

Mi note
On the piano, the note mi corresponds to E in the C major scale.
She sang the scale from mi up to do with perfect pitch.
The children practiced the sequence: do, re, mi, fa, so.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: do - first note of the major scale (solfège)
  • degree higher: fa - fourth note of the major scale (solfège)
  • degree lower: re - second note of the major scale (solfège)
  • degree higher: so - fifth note of the major scale (solfège)
