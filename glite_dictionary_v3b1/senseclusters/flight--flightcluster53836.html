flight

noun
an instance of air travel
4.7
Pronunciation: / flaɪt / (AmE), / flaɪt / (BrE)
an act or instance of traveling by air, or a scheduled trip by aircraft

Examples

A flight to Paris
He booked a last-minute flight for his business trip.
Her first flight was both thrilling and a little scary.
The flight to Paris was delayed due to bad weather.
I booked a flight to Paris for next month.
Our flight was delayed due to heavy fog at the airport.
She took the morning flight and arrived in New York before noon.
