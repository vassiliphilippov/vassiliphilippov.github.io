red flag

noun
emblem of socialist revolution
Pronunciation: / rɛd flæɡ / (AmE), / rɛd flaɡ / (BrE)
A red banner or emblem used as a symbol of socialist revolution or left-wing political movements.

Examples

Red flag of revolution
During the revolution, the red flag became a powerful symbol of change.
The red flag has been associated with socialist movements since the 19th century.
The protesters marched through the streets, waving a red flag as a sign of solidarity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banner - a long strip of cloth bearing a slogan or design, often used in protests
  • synonym: symbol - something that stands for or represents an idea or movement
