elongate

verb
make or become longer by stretching
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɔŋɡeɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɒŋɡeɪt / (BrE)
to make something longer, especially by stretching or pulling it

Examples

To elongate a rope
If you pull the dough too hard, it will elongate into a thin strand.
Scientists use a device to elongate metal wires for testing.
The muscles will elongate when stretched regularly during exercise.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shorten - to make or become shorter
  • synonym: stretch - to make something longer or wider without tearing it
