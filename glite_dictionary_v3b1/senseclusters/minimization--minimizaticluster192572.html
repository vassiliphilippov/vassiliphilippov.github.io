minimization

noun
the act of making as small as possible
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪnɪməˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌmɪnəməˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪnɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of reducing something to the smallest possible amount, degree, or level

Examples

Careful minimization of risk
Careful minimization of risk is essential when making financial decisions.
Her work contributed to the minimization of noise pollution in urban areas.
The company focused on the minimization of waste to improve efficiency.
Effective minimisation of risks is crucial in financial management.
The company's new policy focuses on the minimisation of environmental impact.
The procedure led to a significant minimisation of errors in the system.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maximization - the act of making something as large or great as possible
  • synonym: minimization - the act of reducing to the smallest degree (American spelling)
  • synonym: reduction - the action or fact of making something smaller or less
