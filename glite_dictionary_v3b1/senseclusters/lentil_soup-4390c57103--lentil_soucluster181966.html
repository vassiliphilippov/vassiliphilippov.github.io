lentil soup

noun
soup made with lentils and vegetables
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛn.təl suːp / (AmE), / ˈlɛn.təl suːp / (BrE)
A soup made from lentils cooked in stock, often with onions, carrots, and celery.

Examples

A bowl of lentil soup
On cold winter days, I love to make a big pot of lentil soup for my family.
She garnished her homemade lentil soup with fresh parsley and a squeeze of lemon.
The restaurant's vegetarian lentil soup is one of the most popular items on the menu.
