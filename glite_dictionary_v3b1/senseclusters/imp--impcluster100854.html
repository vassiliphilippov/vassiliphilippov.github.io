imp

noun
mischievous supernatural being in folklore
Pronunciation: / ɪmp / (AmE), / ɪmp / (BrE)
In folklore, a small, mischievous supernatural being, often considered a minor demon or sprite.

Examples

A mischievous imp
In old fairy tales, a cunning imp would often play tricks on villagers.
She described her little brother as an energetic imp who was always up to mischief.
The garden statue depicted a grinning imp perched on the edge of the fountain.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: demon - evil supernatural entity
  • synonym: sprite - small, elusive supernatural being
