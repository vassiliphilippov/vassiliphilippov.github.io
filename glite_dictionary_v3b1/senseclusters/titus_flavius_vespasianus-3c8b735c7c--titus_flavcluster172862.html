Titus Flavius Vespasianus

proper noun
Roman Emperor, son of Vespasian
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtaɪ.təs ˈfleɪ.viː.əs ve.sˈpeɪ.ʒə.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈtaɪ.təs ˈfleɪ.viː.əs ve.sˈpeɪ.ʒə.nəs/ (BrE)
(39–81) Roman Emperor, son of Vespasian, who ruled from AD 79 to 81 and is known for completing the Colosseum and dealing with the eruption of Mount Vesuvius.
