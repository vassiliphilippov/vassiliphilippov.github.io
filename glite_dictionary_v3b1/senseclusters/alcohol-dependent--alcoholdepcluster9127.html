alcohol-dependent

adjective
addicted to alcohol
Pronunciation: / ˌæl.kəˌhɑl.dɪˈpɛn.dənt / (AmE), / ˌælk.əhɒl.dɪˈpen.dənt / (BrE)
Unable to control the use of alcohol because of a physical or psychological need for it; addicted to alcohol.

Examples

Alcohol-dependent person
He became increasingly alcohol-dependent after losing his job.
Living with an alcohol-dependent parent can be very challenging.
The clinic offers support for people who are alcohol-dependent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alcoholic - a person addicted to alcohol
  • antonym: teetotal - choosing not to drink alcohol
