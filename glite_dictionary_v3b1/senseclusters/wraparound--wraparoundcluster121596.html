wraparound

adjective
extending all around
Pronunciation: / ˈræpəˌraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈræpəˌraʊnd / (BrE)
going or extending all the way around something, especially to cover or enclose it completely

Examples

Wraparound sunglasses
She wore a stylish wraparound skirt to the party.
The house features a wraparound porch that offers views from every side.
He bought wraparound sunglasses to protect his eyes from the sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encircling - surrounding or forming a circle around something
  • antonym: partial - not complete or not covering the whole
