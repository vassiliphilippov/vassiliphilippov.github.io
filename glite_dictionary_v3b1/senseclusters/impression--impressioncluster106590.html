Synonyms

  • imprint
    4.2 1/m
    imprint is usually the mark left by pressure or the tool that makes it, while impression is the general name for such a mark.
  • indentation
    3.7 0.6/m
    indentation is a hollow or notch pressed or cut into a surface, while impression is any mark made by pressure.
  • print
    2.0 20/m
    print is a clear mark left by an object like a hand or paw, while impression is any mark made by pressing.
  • depression
    4.2 0.3/m
    depression is a dip or the act of pressing down, while impression is a visible mark left by pressure on a surface.
