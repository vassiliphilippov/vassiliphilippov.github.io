peltast

adjective
light skirmishing tactics
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛlˌtæst / (AmE), / ˈpɛltɑːst / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of peltasts, especially their light equipment or skirmishing tactics in ancient warfare

Examples

Peltast tactics
The army relied on peltast units to harass the enemy before the main battle began.
Their peltast tactics allowed them to move quickly and avoid direct confrontation.
Historians often discuss the effectiveness of peltast formations in ancient Greek warfare.
