bouton

noun
skin pimple or boil
Pronunciation: / buːˈtɒn / (AmE), / buːˈtɒn / (BrE)
a small inflamed spot on the skin, such as a pimple or boil

Examples

A skin bouton
She was embarrassed by the sudden bouton on her chin before the interview.
The doctor examined the red bouton that had appeared overnight on his arm.
He tried not to touch the bouton on his forehead to avoid making it worse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boil - a painful, swollen bump on the skin filled with pus
  • synonym: pimple - a small inflamed spot on the skin
