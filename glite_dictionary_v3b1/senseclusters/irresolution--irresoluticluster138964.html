irresolution

noun
lack of decisiveness or firmness
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrˌrɛzəˈluːʃən /; / ˌɪrəˈzuːlʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪrɛzəˈluːʃən / (BrE)
the state of being uncertain or unable to decide; lack of determination

Examples

His irresolution in choice
Faced with two equally attractive offers, his irresolution was understandable.
Her irresolution led her to miss several important opportunities.
The committee's irresolution frustrated those who wanted swift action.
He struggled with irresolution before finally making up his mind.
Her irresolution caused her to miss several good opportunities.
Moments of irresolution are normal when faced with life-changing decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indecision - inability to make a decision quickly
  • antonym: resolution - the quality of being firmly determined; decisiveness
  • synonym: uncertainty - the state of being unsure
