jobbing

noun
doing small jobs
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒbɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of doing small jobs or tasks, often for different people or employers

Examples

Jobbing for neighbors
He supports his family by jobbing as a painter and gardener for local residents.
Many people turn to jobbing when they cannot find full-time work.
After retiring, she kept busy by jobbing for friends and neighbors.
