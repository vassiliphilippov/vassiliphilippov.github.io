comfortingly

adverb
in a reassuring or consoling way
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmfərtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkʌmfətɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that provides comfort or reassurance.

Examples

Speak comfortingly
He spoke comfortingly, reassuring his friend that everything would be alright.
She smiled comfortingly at the nervous child, helping him to relax.
The old armchair looked comfortingly familiar after a long journey.

Related Terms

  • antonym: uncomfortably - in a way that causes discomfort
