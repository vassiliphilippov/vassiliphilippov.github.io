revolter

noun
source of disgust
8.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvoʊltər / (AmE), / rɪˈvəʊltə / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes strong disgust or distaste in others

Examples

A revolter at dinner
The spoiled food was a real revolter for everyone at the picnic.
Many found the graphic images in the documentary to be a revolter.
His rude behavior at the dinner table made him a revolter in the eyes of the guests.

  • synonym: offender - a person who causes offense or displeasure
