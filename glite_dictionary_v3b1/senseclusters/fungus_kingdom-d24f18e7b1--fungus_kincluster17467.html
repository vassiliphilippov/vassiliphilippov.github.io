fungus kingdom

noun
group of organisms including fungi
5.6 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfʌŋ.gəs ˈkɪŋ.dəm/ (AmE), /ˈfʌŋ.gəs ˈkɪŋ.dəm/ (BrE)
