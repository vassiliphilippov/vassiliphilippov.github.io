cupidinous

adjective
desiring wealth or pleasure
9.9
Pronunciation: / kjuːˈpɪdɪnəs / (AmE), / kjuːˈpɪdɪnəs / (BrE)
having strong desires, especially for wealth or pleasure; lustful or covetous

Examples

A cupidinous person
The cupidinous merchant was never satisfied with his riches.
Her cupidinous ambitions led her to pursue wealth at any cost.
Critics described the character as cupidinous, always seeking pleasure and luxury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: covetous - having a strong desire for possessions or wealth
  • antonym: generous - willing to give more of something than is strictly necessary or expected
  • synonym: greedy - having an intense and selfish desire for something, especially wealth or power
