Synonyms

  • commercialization
    6.3 2.17/m
    commercialization turns ideas or products into businesses for profit, while commodification emphasizes treating things as sellable commodities.
  • commoditization
    8.1 0.18/m
    commoditization makes goods and services common and interchangeable, while commodification focuses on turning things into items people can buy.
  • marketization
    7.4 0.34/m
    marketization introduces market rules like competition and private ownership, while commodification means making specific things into products to sell.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commercialization - the process of managing or running something mainly for financial gain
  • antonym: decommodification - the process of removing something from the market or making it not for sale
