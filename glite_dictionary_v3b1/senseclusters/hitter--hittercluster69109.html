hitter

noun
a person who hits
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪtər / (AmE), / ˈhɪtə / (BrE)
a person who hits, especially in sports or other activities involving striking

Examples

Strong hitter
She has a reputation as a powerful hitter on the volleyball court.
The baseball team needs a strong hitter to drive in runs.
The coach praised him for being the hardest hitter in the league.

Related Terms

  • antonym: non-hitter - a person who does not hit (rare, opposite sense)
  • synonym: striker - a person who strikes, especially in sports
