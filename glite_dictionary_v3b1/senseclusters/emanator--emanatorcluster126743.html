emanator

noun
source of emanation
Pronunciation: / ˈɛm.əˌneɪ.tər / (AmE), / ˈɛm.əˌneɪ.tə / (BrE)
a being or principle from which something, especially spiritual or metaphysical qualities, flows or originates

Examples

Supreme emanator
In some philosophies, the highest emanator is considered the ultimate source of all existence.
The ancient text refers to the sun as an emanator of life and energy.
Mystics often debate the nature of the emanator and its relationship to the material world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: originator - a person or thing that creates or initiates something
  • synonym: source - the origin or starting point of something
