tomato sauce

noun
sauce made from tomatoes and seasonings
1.8
Pronunciation: / təˈmeɪtoʊ sɔs / (AmE), / təˈmɑːtəʊ sɔːs / (BrE)
A sauce made primarily from pureed or strained tomatoes, often combined with savory vegetables and seasonings, typically used with pasta and other dishes.

Examples

Homemade tomato sauce
He prefers homemade tomato sauce rather than the store-bought kind.
Many recipes call for a can of tomato sauce as a base ingredient.
She poured tomato sauce over her spaghetti before serving it.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: ketchup - tomato-based condiment, sweeter and thicker than typical tomato sauce
  • synonym: marinara sauce - simple Italian tomato-based sauce with garlic and herbs
