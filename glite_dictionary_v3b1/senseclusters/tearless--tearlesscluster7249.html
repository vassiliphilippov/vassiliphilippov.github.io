tearless

adjective
not showing or containing tears
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪr.ləs / (AmE), / ˈtɪə.lɪs / (BrE)
Not crying or showing tears; free from the presence of tears.

Examples

A tearless face
Despite the sad news, her face remained tearless throughout the entire meeting.
His tearless goodbye surprised everyone who knew how close he was to his friend.
The child gave a tearless apology, showing little emotion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tearful - full of tears or likely to cry
