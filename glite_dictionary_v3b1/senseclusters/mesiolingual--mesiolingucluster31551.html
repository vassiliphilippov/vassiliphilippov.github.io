mesiolingual

adjective
tooth surface location
Pronunciation: / ˌmiːzi.oʊˈlɪŋɡjuəl / (AmE), / ˌmiːziəʊˈlɪŋɡjuːəl / (BrE)
relating to both the mesial (front) and lingual (tongue-facing) surfaces of a tooth

Examples

Mesiolingual tooth surface
The dentist found a small cavity on the mesiolingual surface of her molar.
A mesiolingual filling was required to restore the damaged tooth.
Plaque often accumulates on the mesiolingual areas if not brushed carefully.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distolingual - relating to the distal and lingual surfaces of a tooth
  • synonym: mesiobuccal - relating to the mesial and buccal surfaces of a tooth
