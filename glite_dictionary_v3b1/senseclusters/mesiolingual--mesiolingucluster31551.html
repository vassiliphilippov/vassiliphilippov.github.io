mesiolingual

adjective
tooth surface location
9.9
Pronunciation: /miː.zi.oʊ.ˈlɪŋ.gwəl/ /; / /miː.si.oʊ.ˈlɪŋ.gwəl/ (AmE), /miː.zi.ə.ˈlɪŋ.gwəl/ /; / /miː.si.ə.ˈlɪŋ.gwəl/ (BrE)
relating to both the mesial (front) and lingual (tongue-facing) surfaces of a tooth
