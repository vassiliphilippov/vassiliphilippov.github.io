rollercoaster

noun
situation with rapid changes, especially emotional
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlɚˌkoʊstɚ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊləkəʊstə / (BrE)
a situation, event, or experience that involves many rapid or extreme changes, especially in emotions or circumstances

Examples

A rollercoaster ride
My career has felt like a rollercoaster, full of unexpected twists and turns.
The past year has been a rollercoaster for the entire team, with many highs and lows.
Their friendship turned into an emotional rollercoaster after they moved to different cities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emotional rollercoaster - a situation with quickly changing emotions
  • synonym: up and down - subject to frequent or sudden changes
