A lawsuit brought to protect a private civil right or to obtain a civil remedy, rather than to prosecute a criminal offense.

Examples

A civil action lawsuit
A civil action often results in financial compensation rather than criminal punishment.
She became involved in a lengthy civil action against her landlord.
The company filed a civil action to recover damages from the breach of contract.
