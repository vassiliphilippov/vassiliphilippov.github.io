wizarding

noun
practice of magic
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪzərdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪzədɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice or activity of using magic, especially as performed by wizards

Examples

Wizarding school
She dreamed of attending a famous wizarding school to learn spells and potions.
The wizarding world has its own unique customs and traditions.
Books about wizarding adventures are popular among young readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sorcery - the use of magic, especially evil magic
  • synonym: witchcraft - the practice of magical skills, spells, and abilities
