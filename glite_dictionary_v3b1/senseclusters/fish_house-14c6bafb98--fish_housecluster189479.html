fish house

noun
ice fishing shelter
3.4
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ haʊs / (AmE), / fɪʃ haʊs / (BrE)
a small shelter or hut used by anglers for ice fishing, typically placed on a frozen lake

Examples

Ice fish house
They set up their fish house early in the morning to get the best spot on the lake.
A portable fish house makes ice fishing much more comfortable during cold weather.
After drilling a hole, the group gathered inside the fish house to wait for a catch.
