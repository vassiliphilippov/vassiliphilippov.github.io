mashup

noun
combined web application
Pronunciation: / ˈmæʃˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈmæʃˌʌp / (BrE)
a web or software application that combines content, data, or functions from multiple sources into a single service or interface

Examples

Web mashup
The new travel mashup lets users compare flight prices and book hotels on one platform.
Many music fans enjoy a mashup that blends songs from different artists into a single track.
Our team created a mashup that displays weather updates alongside local news headlines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggregation - the process of collecting and combining information from different sources
  • synonym: integration - the act of combining different systems or data into one
