equatorially

adverb
in an equatorial way
Pronunciation: / ɪˌkweɪtəˈriəli / (AmE), / ɪˌkweɪtəˈrɪəli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or situated at the equator or an equator

Examples

Located equatorially
The climate is more humid equatorially than in the northern regions.
Rainfall is distributed equatorially across the continent, leading to dense forests.
The satellite orbits the Earth equatorially to maximize coverage of tropical areas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: polar - relating to or situated at the poles
  • synonym: tropically - in a manner relating to the tropics
