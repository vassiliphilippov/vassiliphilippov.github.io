cruncher

noun
machine that crushes
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌntʃər / (AmE), / ˈkrʌntʃə / (BrE)
a machine or tool that crushes, compacts, or breaks material into smaller pieces or a denser form

Examples

Can cruncher machine
The recycling plant used a powerful cruncher to compress empty cans before shipping them away.
Workers fed old cardboard boxes into the cruncher to make them easier to transport.
A new cruncher was installed to break down large plastic containers efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compactor - a machine that presses material into a smaller, denser form
  • synonym: crusher - a machine that breaks things into smaller pieces
