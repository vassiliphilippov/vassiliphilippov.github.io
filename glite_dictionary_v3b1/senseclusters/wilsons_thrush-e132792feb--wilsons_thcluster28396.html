Wilson's thrush

noun
North American songbird (veery)
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪlsənz θrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɪlsənz θrʌʃ / (BrE)
A tawny-brown North American songbird of the thrush family, noted for its melodious song; another name for the veery.

Examples

Wilson's thrush song
Researchers studied the migration patterns of Wilson's thrushes in eastern North America.
The lovely flute-like call of the Wilson's thrush echoed across the wetland.
We saw a Wilson's thrush singing in the maple forest at dawn.
