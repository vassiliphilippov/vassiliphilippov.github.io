oogamy

noun
egg-sperm reproduction
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈuː.gə.miː/ /; / /ˈoʊ.ə.gə.miː/ (AmE), /ˈuː.gə.miː/ /; / /ˈəʊ.ə.gə.miː/ (BrE)
a form of sexual reproduction in which a large, non-motile egg is fertilized by a small, motile sperm

Examples

Oogamy reproduction
Many plants and animals reproduce through oogamy.
In oogamy, the egg is much larger than the sperm and does not move.
The evolution of oogamy allowed for greater specialization of reproductive cells.
