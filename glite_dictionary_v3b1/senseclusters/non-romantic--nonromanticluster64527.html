non-romantic

adjective
not Romantic style
Pronunciation: / nɑn roʊˈmæntɪk / (AmE), / nɒn rəʊˈmæntɪk / (BrE)
not related to or characteristic of the Romantic movement or its style in art, music, or literature

Examples

Non-romantic art style
The artist's non-romantic approach focused on realism rather than emotion.
Many critics praised the non-romantic elements in the new symphony.
Her writing style is distinctly non-romantic, avoiding flowery language and dramatic themes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Romantic - relating to the Romantic movement in art, music, or literature
  • synonym: classical - relating to the style of ancient Greece and Rome or to classical music and art
