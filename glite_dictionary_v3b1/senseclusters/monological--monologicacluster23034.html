monological

adjective
single viewpoint
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a monologue; involving only one point of view or voice

Examples

Monological speech style
The professor's monological lectures left little room for student participation or discussion.
Critics argued that the film's monological narrative failed to present alternative perspectives.
Her writing style is often monological, focusing on a single character's inner thoughts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dialogical - involving dialogue or multiple viewpoints
  • synonym: unilateral - done or decided by one side only
