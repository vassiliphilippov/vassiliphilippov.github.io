outsideness

noun
state of being outside
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtsaɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈaʊtsaɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being outside or external to something

Examples

Physical outsideness
The outsideness of the sculpture made it stand out in the open plaza.
He felt a sense of outsideness when he moved to a new city.
The architect emphasized the outsideness of the space by using large glass walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: externality - the state of being external or outside
  • antonym: insideness - the quality or state of being inside
