flat tire

noun
a tire that has lost air
2.0
Pronunciation: / flæt ˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / flæt ˈtaɪə / (BrE)
a tire that has lost all or most of its air and cannot function properly

Examples

A flat tire
Driving on a flat tire can damage your wheel and is dangerous.
She learned how to change a flat tire during her first driving lesson.
We had to pull over on the highway because of a flat tire.
Driving with a flat tire can damage the wheel and make the car unsafe.
He was late to work because he got a flat tire on the way.
We had to pull over and change the flat tire before continuing our road trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puncture - a small hole that lets air out of a tire, often causing a flat tire
