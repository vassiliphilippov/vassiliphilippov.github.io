flip over

verb
turn upside down or reverse quickly
1.0 2.1
Pronunciation: /flɪp ˈoʊ.vər/ /; / /flɪp ˈəʊ.vər/ (AmE), /flɪp ˈəʊ.və/ (BrE)
to turn something so that the side that was facing up is now facing down, or to reverse the position of something quickly.

Examples

To flip over a page
After writing his name, he flipped over the exam paper to start the test.
Carefully flip over the steak to cook the other side.
The wind was so strong that it flipped over the patio table.
