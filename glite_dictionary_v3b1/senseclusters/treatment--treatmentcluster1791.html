treatment

noun
manner of managing or dealing with
2.6 34/m
Pronunciation: / Ààtrit.m…ônt / (AmE), / ÀàtriÀêt.m…ônt / (BrE)
the process or manner of dealing with or managing a person, group, or thing

Examples

Fair treatment
Proper treatment of confidential information is critical for maintaining trust.
The treatment of all employees should be fair and consistent in the workplace.
The new recycling policy changed the treatment of household waste in the city.

Synonyms

  • handling
    3.0 8.33/m
    handling focuses on the practical way of managing or moving someone or something, while treatment refers to the general manner of dealing.
  • care
    1.0 350/m
    care is looking after someone's needs or keeping something in good condition, while treatment is the broader way of managing or dealing with them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handling - the act of managing, controlling, or dealing with something
  • antonym: mismanagement - the process of handling something badly
