bull tongue

noun
heavy plow with wide blade
7.4
Pronunciation: / bʊl tʌŋ / (AmE), / bʊl tʌŋ / (BrE)
a type of heavy plow with a single wide blade, used mainly in cotton fields

Examples

A bull tongue plow
Old barns in the South sometimes still house a rusty bull tongue plow.
Replacing the blade on a bull tongue can be difficult due to its unique shape.
The farmer used a bull tongue to break the tough soil before planting cotton.
