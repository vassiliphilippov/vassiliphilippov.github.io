high

adjective
having a high pitch (of sound or voice)
Pronunciation: / haɪ / (AmE), / haɪ / (BrE)
(of a sound or voice) having a high pitch or frequency.

Examples

High voice
Canaries are known for their high and cheerful songs.
Her voice was surprisingly high for someone of her stature.
The alarm emits a high beeping sound when triggered.
