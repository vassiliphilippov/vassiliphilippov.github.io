uterotonic

adjective
induces uterine contractions
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːtəroʊˈtɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌjuːtərəʊˈtɒnɪk / (BrE)
causing or increasing contractions of the uterus

Examples

Uterotonic effect
The doctor prescribed a uterotonic medication to help manage postpartum bleeding.
Certain uterotonic agents are used to induce labor in pregnant women.
A uterotonic effect is important for controlling bleeding after childbirth.
