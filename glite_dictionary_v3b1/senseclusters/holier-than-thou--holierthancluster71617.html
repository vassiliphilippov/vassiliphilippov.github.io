holier-than-thou

noun
self-righteous person
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊ.lɪ.ər ðən ðaʊ/ /; / /ˈhoʊ.lɪ.ər ðæn ðaʊ/ /; / /ˈhoʊ.lɪ.ər ðən ðoʊ/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊ.lɪ.ə ðən ðaʊ/ /; / /ˈhəʊ.lɪ.ə ðən ðəʊ/ /; / /ˈhəʊ.liː.ə ðən ðaʊ/ (BrE)
A person who displays an attitude of moral superiority, often in a smug or judgmental way.

Examples

Holier-than-thou person
Many fans accused him of being a holier-than-thou who lectured others without offering help.
She refused to invite any holier-than-thou to the discussion, fearing their criticism would shut people down.
At the meeting, a holier-than-thou made several judgmental remarks about the volunteers' motives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modest - a person who has a humble or low view of their own importance; opposite of self-importance
  • synonym: self-righteous - a person who believes they are morally superior to others
