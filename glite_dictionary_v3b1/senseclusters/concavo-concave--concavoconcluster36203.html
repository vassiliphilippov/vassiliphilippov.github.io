concavo-concave

adjective
concave on both sides
Pronunciation: / kənˌkeɪvoʊ kənˈkeɪv / (AmE), / kɒnˌkeɪvəʊ kɒnˈkeɪv / (BrE)
having both surfaces or sides concave; curved inward on both sides, typically referring to lenses or mirrors.

Examples

A concavo-concave lens
A concavo-concave mirror can be used to create unique optical effects in scientific instruments.
Red blood cells have a distinctive concavo-concave shape in their normal state.
The concavo-concave lens was used to focus the light more precisely in the experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biconcave - concave on both sides, especially of a lens or disk
  • antonym: concavo-convex - concave on one side and convex on the other
