starlight

noun
light from stars at night
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌlaɪt / (BrE)
the light that comes from the stars, especially as seen at night

Examples

Starlight at night
Poets have often described the beauty of starlight on a clear night.
The desert was so remote that only starlight illuminated the path ahead.
Through the window, she admired the gentle starlight filling the garden.
