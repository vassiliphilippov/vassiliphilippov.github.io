pyromaniacal

adjective
provocative or inflammatory
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪroʊməˈnaɪəkəl / (AmE), / ˌpaɪərəʊməˈnaɪəkəl / (BrE)
deliberately provocative or inflammatory, showing a strong eagerness to cause conflict or chaos, often in a destructive way

Examples

Pyromaniacal speech

Social media can amplify pyromaniacal statements, making conflicts escalate quickly.
