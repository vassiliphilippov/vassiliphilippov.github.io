thoroughbred race

noun
race between thoroughbred horses
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜːroʊˌbrɛd reɪs / (AmE), / ˈθʌrəbɹɛd reɪs / (BrE)
A horse race in which only thoroughbred horses are allowed to compete.

Examples

A thoroughbred race event
She has always dreamed of attending a famous thoroughbred race at Churchill Downs.
The annual thoroughbred race attracted top horses and jockeys from around the country.
Winning a thoroughbred race requires skill from both the horse and the rider.

  • synonym: horse race - a competition of speed between horses
