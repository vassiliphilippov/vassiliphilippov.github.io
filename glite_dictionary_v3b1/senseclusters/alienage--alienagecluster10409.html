alienage

noun
condition of being an alien or foreigner
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪliənɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈeɪliənɪdʒ / (BrE)
the state or condition of being an alien, especially in relation to legal rights and status in a country

Examples

The alienage status
Discrimination based on alienage can be challenged in some legal systems.
Her alienage prevented her from voting in national elections.
The law distinguishes between alienage and citizenship when granting certain rights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: citizenship - the status of being a citizen of a particular country
