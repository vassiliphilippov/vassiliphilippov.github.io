phlegmasia alba dolens

noun
painful femoral vein thrombosis after childbirth
9.9
Pronunciation: / flɛɡˈmeɪziə ˈæl.bə ˈdoʊ.lɛnz / (AmE), / flɛɡˈmeɪziə ˈal.bə ˈdəʊ.lɛnz / (BrE)
a painful condition characterized by thrombosis of the femoral vein in the leg, often occurring after childbirth, leading to swelling and whiteness of the limb

Examples

Painful phlegmasia alba dolens

The doctor diagnosed her sudden leg swelling as phlegmasia alba dolens following childbirth.
