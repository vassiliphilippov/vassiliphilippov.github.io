oculus

noun
circular architectural opening
8.6 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ëÀêkj…ôl…ôs / (AmE), / Àà…íkj äl…ôs / (BrE)
a circular opening or window, especially one at the top of a dome in architecture

Examples

Dome oculus
The ancient temple featured a large oculus that allowed sunlight to illuminate the interior.
Rain sometimes falls through the oculus at the top of the dome, creating a dramatic effect.
Architects admired the way the oculus framed the sky above the grand hall.

Synonyms

  • oxeye
    9.9 0.00/m
    an oxeye is a small oval dormer or round roof window seen on houses, while an oculus is a larger circular opening high in a dome.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dome - a rounded roof or ceiling, often with an oculus at the top
  • synonym: window - an opening in a wall or roof to let in light or air
