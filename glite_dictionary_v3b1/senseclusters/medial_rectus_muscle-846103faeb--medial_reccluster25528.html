medial rectus muscle

noun
eye muscle that turns eyeball inward
8.1 5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːdiəl ˈrɛktəs ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiəl ˈrɛktəs ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
the muscle located on the medial side of the eyeball whose contraction moves the eye inward toward the nose
