prover

noun
one who proves
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːvər / (AmE), / ˈpruːvə / (BrE)

Examples

A prover of facts

In mathematics, a computer can act as a prover to check complex theorems.
As a skilled prover, she convinced the jury with clear and logical arguments.
