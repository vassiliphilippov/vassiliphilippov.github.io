granulocytic leukemia

noun
type of blood cancer with excess myelocytes
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɡrænjəloʊˈsɪtɪk lɪˈkiːmiə / (AmE), / ˌɡranjʊləˈsɪtɪk lɪˈkiːmiə / (BrE)
a malignant cancer of blood-forming tissues characterized by an excessive increase of myelocytes in the blood
