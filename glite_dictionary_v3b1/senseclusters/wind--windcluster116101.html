wind

noun
musical instrument played by blowing air
Pronunciation: / wɪnd / (AmE), / wɪnd / (BrE)
a musical instrument in which sound is produced by a column of air set in motion by blowing, typically by breath or bellows

Examples

Wind instrument
Maintenance is crucial to keep a wind in good playing condition.
She played a wind in the school orchestra, preferring flute to brass instruments.
The concert featured a variety of wind and percussion instruments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brass - a family of wind instruments made of brass or other metal
  • antonym: string - a musical instrument in which sound is produced by vibrating strings
  • synonym: woodwind - a family of wind instruments made of wood or metal
