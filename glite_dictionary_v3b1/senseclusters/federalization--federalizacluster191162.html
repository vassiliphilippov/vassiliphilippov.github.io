federalization

noun
process of coming under federal control
4.7 7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛdərələˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌfɛdərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfɛdərələˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌfɛdərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of bringing a country, region, or organization under the control of a federal government or adopting a federal system
