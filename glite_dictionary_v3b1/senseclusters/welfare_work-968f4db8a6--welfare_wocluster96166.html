welfare work

noun
activities to improve well-being of disadvantaged people
3.5 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…õl.f…õr Àåw…ùÀêk / (AmE), / Ààw…õl.fe…ô Àåw…úÀêk / (BrE)
organized activities carried out to improve the well-being of disadvantaged people in society, especially through social services or charitable organizations.

Examples

welfare work in community
After graduation, Maria decided to dedicate her life to welfare work in her local community.
The organization specializes in welfare work for children living in poverty.
Volunteers play a crucial role in supporting welfare work throughout the country.

Synonyms

  • social service
    3.3 7.27/m
    social service is a formal organized program run by agencies to help vulnerable people, while welfare work is broader, including community or charity activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: social work - work to improve the welfare of individuals and communities, often by trained professionals
