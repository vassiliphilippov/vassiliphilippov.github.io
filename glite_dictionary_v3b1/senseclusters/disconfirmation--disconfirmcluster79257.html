disconfirmation

noun
failure to meet expectations
8.3
Pronunciation: /dɪs.kən.fər.ˈmeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /dɪs.kən.fɜːr.ˈmeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /dɪs.kən.fə.ˈmeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the failure of an outcome or experience to meet expectations, resulting in a mismatch between what was expected and what actually occurred

Examples

Customer disconfirmation
The survey revealed a significant disconfirmation between what customers expected and what they actually received.
A disconfirmation of her hopes left her feeling frustrated after the meeting.
Researchers study disconfirmation to understand how unmet expectations affect satisfaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disappointment - a feeling of sadness when expectations are not met
