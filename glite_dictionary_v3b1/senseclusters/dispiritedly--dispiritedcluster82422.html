dispiritedly

adverb
in a discouraged or hopeless manner
6.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspɪrɪtɪdli / (AmE), / dɪˈspɪərɪtɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of hope or enthusiasm; in a discouraged manner.

Examples

To dispiritedly pack belongings
He dispiritedly packed his belongings, feeling the weight of disappointment.
She walked dispiritedly home after failing the exam.
The team left the field dispiritedly after their defeat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy and optimistic way
  • synonym: dejectedly - in a sad or depressed manner
