factually

adverb
in a way based on facts
2.5 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfæk.tʃuː.ə.liː/ /; / /ˈfæk.tʃə.liː/ (AmE), /ˈfæk.tʃʊə.liː/ /; / /ˈfæk.tʃə.liː/ (BrE)

Examples
