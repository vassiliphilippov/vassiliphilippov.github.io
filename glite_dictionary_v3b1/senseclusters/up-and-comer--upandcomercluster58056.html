up-and-comer

noun
a rising, successful person
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌp ənd ˈkʌmɚ / (AmE), / ˌʌp ənd ˈkʌmə / (BrE)
a person who is gaining success, popularity, or prominence, often quickly or recently

Examples

An up-and-comer in business
As an up-and-comer in the startup world, she quickly built a reputation for innovation.
Several established players are watching the up-and-comer closely as he advances in the league.
The up-and-comer impressed the judges with a fresh perspective and strong skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rising star - a person who is quickly gaining fame or success
  • antonym: veteran - a person with long experience in a field
