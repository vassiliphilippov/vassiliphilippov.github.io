savagism

noun
state of being uncivilized
Pronunciation: / ˈsævədʒɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈsævɪdʒɪzəm / (BrE)
the state or condition of being uncivilized, primitive, or lacking social development

Examples

Ancient savagism
The historian wrote about the perceived savagism of ancient tribes in the region.
Many early explorers misunderstood local customs as signs of savagism rather than different cultures.
The film challenged stereotypes by showing that so-called savagism was often a matter of perspective.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbarism - the condition of being uncivilized or primitive
  • antonym: civilization - an advanced state of human society with culture and organization
