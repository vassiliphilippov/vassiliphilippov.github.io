scarring

noun
formation of scars
3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈskɑː.rɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈskɑː.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process or result of forming a permanent mark or scar on the skin or tissue after injury or healing

Examples

Skin scarring
The accident caused severe scarring on his left cheek.
Laser treatments can help reduce the appearance of scarring after surgery.
Some people are more prone to scarring than others due to their skin type.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cicatrization - the process of scar formation in healing
  • synonym: healing - the process of becoming healthy again after an injury
  • degree higher: regeneration - the process of renewal or restoration of tissue
