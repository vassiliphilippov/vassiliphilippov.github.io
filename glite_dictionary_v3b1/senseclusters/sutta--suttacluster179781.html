sutta

noun
informal cigarette
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌtə / (BrE)
an informal word for a cigarette, especially used in Australian, British, and New Zealand English

Examples

A quick sutta
He stepped outside for a quick sutta before the meeting started.
Do you have a sutta I could borrow? I left my pack at home.
After dinner, they all went out to share a sutta in the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cigarette - a small roll of paper filled with tobacco for smoking
