hooey

noun
nonsense; foolish talk
Pronunciation: / ˈhuː.i / (AmE), / ˈhuː.i / (BrE)
Nonsense; senseless or foolish talk.

Examples

Complete hooey
All that advertising hype is complete hooey.
Don't believe his story—it's just a load of hooey.
She dismissed the superstition as pure hooey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - spoken or written words that have no meaning or make no sense
  • synonym: rubbish - informal: words or ideas without value or meaning
  • antonym: sense - that which is reasonable or makes sense
