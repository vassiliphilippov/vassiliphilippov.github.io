burgeon

verb
grow or develop rapidly
7.6 4.16/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…úrd í…ôn / (AmE), / Ààb…úÀêd í…ôn / (BrE)
to begin to grow or develop rapidly; to flourish or expand quickly

Examples

To burgeon quickly
Scientific interest in renewable energy is burgeoning around the world.
Technology companies continue to burgeon as demand for innovative products rises.
The city's population has burgeoned in the past decade, transforming the local economy.
