minuteness

noun
great precision; attention to detail
8.3
Pronunciation: / maɪˈnuːtnəs / (AmE), / mɪˈnjuːtnɪs / (BrE)

Examples

Minuteness of detail

The minuteness of his measurements impressed all the scientists at the conference.
