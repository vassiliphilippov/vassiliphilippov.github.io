gasolene

noun
liquid fuel for engines
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæsəliːn / (AmE), / ˈɡasəliːn / (BrE)
a volatile flammable liquid derived from petroleum, used chiefly as fuel for internal-combustion engines

Examples

A gasolene engine

Many rural areas still use generators powered by gasolene.
The old car wouldn't start because it was out of gasolene.
