last straw

noun
final irritation before a breaking point
Pronunciation: / læst strɔ / (AmE), / lɑːst strɔː / (BrE)
the final problem or annoyance that makes a situation unbearable or causes someone to lose patience

Examples

The last straw
After months of delays and empty promises, the missed deadline became the last straw.
The constant noise was annoying, but the broken heater was the last straw for him.
When she was blamed for the error she didn't make, it was the last straw and she quit her job.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breaking point - the moment of greatest strain; when someone can no longer cope
