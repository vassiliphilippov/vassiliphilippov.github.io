single cream

noun
cream with about 18% butterfat (British)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəl kriːm / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl kriːm / (BrE)
a type of cream containing about 18% butterfat, commonly used in British cooking

Examples

Single cream poured

In the UK, single cream is often used in place of milk to enrich coffee.
The recipe calls for 200ml of single cream to make the sauce extra smooth.
