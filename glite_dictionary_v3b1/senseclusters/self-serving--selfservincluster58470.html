self-serving

adjective
concerned only with one's own interests

Concerned only with one's own interests; having or showing concern for one's own welfare and advantage at the expense of or without regard for others.

Examples

Self-serving statements
Critics accused the company of making self-serving statements to boost their image.
His self-serving attitude quickly made him unpopular among his colleagues.
They dismissed the politician's apology as insincere and self-serving.

Synonyms

  • self-seeking
    6.9 0.02/m
    self-seeking seeks personal gain often at others' expense, while self-serving describes acting to protect or promote one's own advantage.
  • selfish
    1.0 16/m
    selfish thinks only about personal comfort and feelings, while self-serving focuses on actions that advance one's own interest or advantage.
  • egoistic
    7.4 0.47/m
    egoistic puts self-interest first in thought or choice, while self-serving refers to actions intended to gain personal advantage.
  • self-centered
    3.9 2.69/m
    self-centered is wrapped up in one's own needs and view, while self-serving describes behaviours meant to gain personal benefit.
  • egocentric
    7.4 0.92/m
    egocentric thinks from their own perspective and may not see others' views, while self-serving plans actions for personal gain.
