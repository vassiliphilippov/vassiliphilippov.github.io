Utug

proper noun
Sumerian sun god
Pronunciation: / ˈuːtʊg / (AmE), / ˈjuːtʊg / (BrE)
A sun god in Sumerian mythology, regarded as the counterpart of the Akkadian god Shamash.

Examples

Utug sun god
In mythology, Utug was believed to bring justice to the world each day.
Stories about Utug appear in several cuneiform tablets from ancient Mesopotamia.
The ancient Sumerians worshipped Utug as the powerful sun god.
