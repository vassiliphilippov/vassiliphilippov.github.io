dirty trick

noun
underhanded or deceitful act
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdɝːti trɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti trɪk / (BrE)
an act intended to harm, cheat, or deceive someone in an unfair or dishonest way

Examples

A dirty trick played
He was angry when he realized someone had played a dirty trick on him.
Her resignation was forced by a dirty trick orchestrated by her rivals.
Politicians are sometimes accused of using dirty tricks during campaigns.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: fraud - deception intended to result in financial or personal gain
  • degree lower: prank - a playful trick or joke
  • synonym: trick - an action intended to deceive or outwit someone
