stroganoff

noun
creamy meat dish
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɔːɡəˌnɔf / (AmE), / ˈstrɒɡənɒf / (BrE)
a Russian dish of sautéed meat in a creamy sauce, often served with noodles or rice

Examples

Beef stroganoff dish
She prepared a delicious stroganoff for the family dinner last night.
At the restaurant, I decided to try the chicken stroganoff with rice.
The cookbook includes a simple recipe for classic beef stroganoff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beef stew - a dish of beef cooked slowly in liquid with vegetables
  • synonym: goulash - a stew of meat and vegetables, seasoned with paprika
