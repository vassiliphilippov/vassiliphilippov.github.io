biography

noun
life story of a person
Pronunciation: / baɪˈɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / baɪˈɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
an account or detailed written description of a person's life, including important events and experiences

Examples

A biography book
His biography was published to critical acclaim last spring.
She spent years researching the biography of the famous artist.
The library hosts a large collection of political biographies.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: autobiography - an account of a person's life written by themselves
  • synonym: memoir - a historical account or biography written from personal knowledge
