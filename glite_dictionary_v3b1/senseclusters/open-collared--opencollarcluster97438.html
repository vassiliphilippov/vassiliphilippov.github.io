open-collared

adjective
shirt with collar unbuttoned
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊ.pənˈkɑː.lɚd / (AmE), / ˌəʊ.pənˈkɒ.ləd / (BrE)
(of a shirt) worn with the collar unbuttoned at the neck.

Examples

Open-collared shirt
He looked relaxed in his open-collared shirt and khaki trousers.
Many people prefer an open-collared look in the summer heat.
The invitation suggested business casual, so I wore an open-collared blue shirt.
