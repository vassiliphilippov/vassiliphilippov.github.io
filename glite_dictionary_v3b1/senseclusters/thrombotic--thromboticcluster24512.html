thrombotic

adjective
relating to blood clots
Pronunciation: / θrɑmˈbɑtɪk / (AmE), / θrɒmˈbɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or caused by a blood clot in a blood vessel

Examples

A thrombotic event
The doctor explained that a thrombotic event had blocked the patient's artery.
After surgery, the risk of thrombotic complications increases if the patient is immobile.
A thrombotic stroke occurs when a blood clot obstructs a vessel in the brain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hemorrhagic - relating to bleeding or the escape of blood from a vessel
  • synonym: thrombus - a blood clot formed in a blood vessel
