thrombotic

adjective
relating to blood clots
6.8
Pronunciation: /θrəm.ˈbɑː.tɪk/ /; / /θrɑːm.ˈbɑː.tɪk/ (AmE), /θrɒm.ˈbɒt.ɪk/ /; / /θrəm.ˈbɒt.ɪk/ (BrE)

Examples

A thrombotic event
