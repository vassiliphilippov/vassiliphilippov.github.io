prickly shield fern

noun
evergreen North American fern with scaly fronds
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪkli ʃild fɝn / (AmE), / ˈprɪkli ʃiːld fɜːn / (BrE)
A North American fern with evergreen, leathery fronds that are covered in pale brown, chafflike scales.
