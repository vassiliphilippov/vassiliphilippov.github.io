kalumpang

noun
tropical tree with oil-rich seeds
Pronunciation: / kəˈlumpɑŋ / (AmE), / kaˈlʌmpaŋ / (BrE)
a large tree of the Old World tropics with foul-smelling orange-red flowers and red pods containing oil-rich seeds, sometimes used as food

Examples

The kalumpang tree
Botanists studied the kalumpang for its potential uses in sustainable oil production.
In some regions, villagers harvest the seeds of kalumpang to add to traditional dishes.
The kalumpang tree is known for its distinctive red pods filled with valuable oil-rich seeds.
