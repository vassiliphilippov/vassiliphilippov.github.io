anteflexion

noun
forward organ bending
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈflɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈflɛkʃən / (BrE)
a forward bending or curvature of an organ, especially the uterus

Examples

Uterus anteflexion
The ultrasound showed that the uterus was in anteflexion, which is considered normal.
Doctors often note anteflexion when describing the position of the uterus in medical reports.
Mild anteflexion of the organ does not usually cause any health problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexion - the act of bending or the condition of being bent
  • antonym: retroflexion - a backward bending or curvature of an organ
