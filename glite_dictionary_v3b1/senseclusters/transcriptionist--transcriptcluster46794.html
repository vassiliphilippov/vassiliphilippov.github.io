transcriptionist

noun
music notation writer
7.2
Pronunciation: /træn.ˈskrɪp.ʃən.ɪst/ /; / /træns.ˈkrɪp.ʃən.ɪst/ (AmE), /træn.ˈskrɪp.ʃən.ɪst/ /; / /træns.ˈkrɪp.ʃən.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who listens to music and writes it down in musical notation from a performance or recording

Examples

Music transcriptionist
The transcriptionist listened to the recording and created sheet music for the orchestra.
As a skilled transcriptionist, she could quickly notate complex jazz improvisations.
The band hired a transcriptionist to write out the arrangements for their new album.
