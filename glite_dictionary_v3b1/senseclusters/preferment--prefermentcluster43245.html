preferment

noun
promotion or advancement in position
Pronunciation: / prɪˈfɝːmənt / (AmE), / prɪˈfɜːmənt / (BrE)
the act of giving someone a higher position or advantage, especially a promotion to a more important job or office

Examples

A preferment in job
After years of hard work, she finally received the much-anticipated preferment.
He was rumored to have secured his preferment through influential connections.
Rumors of preferment often cause rivalry among colleagues.
