thunder

verb
to produce a loud noise in the sky (weather)
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌndɚ / (AmE), / ˈθʌndə / (BrE)
(of thunder or the weather) to produce a loud noise in the sky, usually during a storm.

Examples

To thunder in the sky
It began to thunder just as we reached the top of the hill.
It had thundered all night, keeping everyone awake.
Whenever it thunders, the children run to their parents' room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rumble - make a deep, resonant sound, especially like distant thunder
