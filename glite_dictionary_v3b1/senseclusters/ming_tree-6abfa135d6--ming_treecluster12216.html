ming tree

noun
ornamental artificial bonsai
Pronunciation: / mɪŋ tri / (AmE), / mɪŋ triː / (BrE)
An artificial plant, often made of plastic, stone, or wire, designed to resemble a miniature bonsai tree.

Examples

An artificial ming tree
A jade ming tree stood next to the window, sparkling in the afternoon light.
She collects rare ming trees made from precious stones.
The living room was decorated with a ming tree on the coffee table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bonsai - a tree or shrub grown artificially small by pruning and training
