Brother

noun
monk's title in Catholic Church
1.0 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbrʌ.ðər/ (AmE), /ˈbrʌ.ðə/ (BrE)
A title used for a monk or male member of a religious order, often used as a form of address in the Roman Catholic Church and some other Christian denominations.

Examples

Brother Michael greeted the visitors at the monastery gate each morning.
After taking his vows, he was known as Brother Thomas within the abbey.
