Georgian

adjective
relating to the former British colony of Georgia
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔːrdʒən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒɪən / (BrE)
relating to the former British colony of Georgia in North America or its people, especially before it became a U.S. state

Examples

Georgian colony
The Georgian settlers played a key role in the early history of the South.
The museum features artifacts from the Georgian colonial period.
This document provides insight into life under Georgian rule in the 18th century.
