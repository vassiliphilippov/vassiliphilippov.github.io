Australian honeysuckle

noun
Australian tree with yellow nectarous flowers
Pronunciation: / əˌstreɪliən ˈhʌniˌsʌkl̩ / (AmE), / ɒˌstreɪliən ˈhʌniˌsʌkl / (BrE)
A shrubby tree native to Australia, known for its silky foliage and spikes of cylindrical yellow nectarous flowers.

Examples

The Australian honeysuckle plant
Gardeners prize the Australian honeysuckle for its vibrant yellow blooms and attractive foliage.
The Australian honeysuckle attracts many native birds with its nectar-rich flowers.
We spotted a beautiful Australian honeysuckle growing near the edge of the national park.
