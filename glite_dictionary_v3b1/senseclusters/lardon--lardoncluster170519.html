lardon

noun
bacon cube or strip
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑrdɒn / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdɒn / (BrE)
a small strip or cube of bacon or pork fat used to add flavor to dishes in cooking

Examples

Crispy lardons in salad
The chef sprinkled crispy lardons over the salad for extra flavor.
She added a handful of lardons to the soup to make it richer.
French quiche often contains lardons for a savory taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bacon - salted and cured meat from the back or sides of a pig
  • synonym: pancetta - Italian cured pork belly used in cooking
