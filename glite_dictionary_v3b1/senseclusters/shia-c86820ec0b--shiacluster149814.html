Shia

noun
main branch of Islam
4.7 0.95/m
Pronunciation: / Àà ÉiÀê…ô / (AmE), / Àà ÉiÀê…ô / (BrE)
One of the two main branches of Islam, whose followers believe that Ali, the cousin and son-in-law of Muhammad, was the rightful successor to Muhammad; mainly practiced in Iran and some other countries.

Examples

Shia Muslims
Many Muslims in Iran identify as Shia rather than Sunni.
Religious festivals important to Shia communities are observed throughout the world.
The history of the Shia branch dates back to the earliest years of Islam.

Synonyms

  • Shiism
    8.5 0.08/m
    Shiism is the name for the belief system and branch of Islam, while Shia often names the people who follow that branch.
  • Shiah Islam
    5.8 0.00/m
    Shiah Islam is a fuller phrase for the Shia branch of Islam, while Shia is the shorter name for its followers and identity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Sunni - the other main branch of Islam
