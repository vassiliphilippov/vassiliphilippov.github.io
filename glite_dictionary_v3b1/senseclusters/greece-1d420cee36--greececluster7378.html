Greece

proper noun
ancient country of city-states in classical times
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡris / (AmE), / ɡriːs / (BrE)
An ancient country of city-states, especially Athens and Sparta, that reached its peak of civilization in the fifth century BCE.
