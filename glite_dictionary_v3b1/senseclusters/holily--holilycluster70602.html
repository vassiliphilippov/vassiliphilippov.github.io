holily

adverb
in a devout way
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊlɪli / (AmE), / ˈhəʊlɪli / (BrE)
in a holy, devout, or pious manner

Examples

To live holily
She tried to live holily, always helping those in need and attending church regularly.
The monk prayed holily in the quiet of the early morning.
They acted holily by forgiving those who had wronged them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: devoutly - in a deeply religious or sincere way
  • synonym: piously - in a way that shows religious devotion
  • antonym: sinfully - in a way that is morally wrong or wicked
