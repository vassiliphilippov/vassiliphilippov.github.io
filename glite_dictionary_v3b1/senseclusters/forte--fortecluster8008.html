forte

adjective, adverb
loudly (in music)
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrteɪ /; / ˈfɔr.teɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːteɪ /; / ˈfɔː.teɪ / (BrE)
(in music) loud; used as a direction or indication to play or sing loudly

Examples

Look for the forte markings in your sheet music and remember to increase your volume there.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: mezzo-forte - (in music) moderately loud; a musical direction for medium-high volume
  • antonym: piano - in music: softly or quietly
