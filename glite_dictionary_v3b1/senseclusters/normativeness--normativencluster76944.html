normativeness

noun
degree of normality
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔːrmətɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˈnɔːmətɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality or degree of being considered normal, typical, or accepted within a particular group or society

Examples

Cultural normativeness
The normativeness of certain customs varies greatly between cultures.
Teachers discussed the normativeness of using digital devices in the classroom.
Researchers examined the normativeness of family structures in different societies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: normality - the state of being normal or typical
