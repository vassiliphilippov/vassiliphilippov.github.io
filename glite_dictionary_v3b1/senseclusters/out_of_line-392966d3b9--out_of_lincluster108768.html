Synonyms

  • over the line
    8.2 1/m
    over the line means crossing a clear limit in speech or action and upsetting others, while out of line means behaving inappropriately.
  • unbecoming
    6.7 0.7/m
    unbecoming describes formal speech or dress that looks improper, while out of line refers to wrong or unacceptable behavior in general.
  • beyond the pale
    9.1 0.5/m
    beyond the pale describes actions people see as morally wrong or shocking, while out of line can mean simply inappropriate.
  • out of place
    3.5 3/m
    out of place feels awkward or like it does not belong in a setting, while out of line means clearly unacceptable behavior.
