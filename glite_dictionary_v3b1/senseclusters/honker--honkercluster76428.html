honker

noun
informal term for Canada goose
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑŋkər / (AmE), / ˈhɒŋkə / (BrE)
(North American, informal) a Canada goose, known for its loud, trumpeting call.

Examples

A wild honker

The lake is a favorite resting place for migrating honkers.
We spotted a flock of honkers flying south for the winter.
