haw

noun
thorny shrub or tree (Crataegus)
Pronunciation: / hɔ / (AmE), / hɔː / (BrE)
a spring-flowering shrub or small tree of the genus Crataegus, typically with thorny branches, white or pink flowers, and red berries

Examples

Haw tree berries
Birds often feed on the red berries produced by the haw tree.
Gardeners sometimes plant haw for its attractive flowers and dense thorns.
The hedgerows were filled with blossoming haw in the early spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hawthorn - another name for the Crataegus shrub or tree
