bone spavin

noun
bony growth disorder in a horse's hock
9.3
Pronunciation: / boʊn ˈspævɪn / (AmE), / bəʊn ˈspævɪn / (BrE)
A disease of horses in which a bony growth develops on the hock joint, causing lameness.

Examples

Bone spavin disease
Early detection of bone spavin allows for better management of the horse's condition.
Many older horses suffer from bone spavin, which can lead to chronic lameness if untreated.
The veterinarian diagnosed the horse with bone spavin after noticing a hard swelling on the hock.
