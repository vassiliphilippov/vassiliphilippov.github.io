fire department

noun
organization that fights fires
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ dɪˌpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
A public service organization that is responsible for preventing and extinguishing fires in a particular area.

Examples

The fire department
If you see smoke or flames, call the fire department immediately.
The fire department responded quickly to the alarm and extinguished the blaze.
The city is planning to hire more firefighters for the fire department this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fire brigade - organization of people trained to put out fires (esp. British English)
