plant a seed of doubt

verb
introduce uncertainty or suspicion
4.1
Pronunciation: / plænt ə siːd ʌv daʊt / (AmE), / plɑːnt ə siːd ɒv daʊt / (BrE)

Examples

To plant a seed of doubt

Rumors can plant a seed of doubt that damages trust in an organization.
The detective tried to plant a seed of doubt in the jury's minds.
