for two pins

adverb
very tempted to do something
9.9
Pronunciation: / fər tuː pɪnz / (AmE), / fə tuː pɪnz / (BrE)
used to say that you are very tempted or willing to do something, often something you are hesitating to do

Examples

For two pins I would quit
For two pins, I’d quit my job and move abroad.
He was so fed up that for two pins he would have walked out.
She looked so angry that for two pins she would have shouted at him.
