tomboyishness

noun
quality of behaving like a tomboy
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːmˌbɔɪɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈtɒmˌbɔɪɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of behaving in a manner considered typical of boys, especially in girls or young women

Examples

Tomboyishness in girls
Her tomboyishness was evident from her love of football and climbing trees.
Many people encouraged her tomboyishness rather than making her conform to traditional gender roles.
The teacher admired the girl's tomboyishness and confidence in sports.

Related Terms
