unprimed

adjective
without a primer
8.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpraɪmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpraɪmd / (BrE)
not supplied with a primer needed to start a chemical or biological reaction, such as DNA replication or PCR.

Examples

Unprimed DNA sample
The experiment failed because the DNA sample was unprimed and could not be replicated.
Researchers discovered that unprimed templates do not allow the reaction to proceed efficiently.
It is important to avoid using unprimed material when setting up a PCR assay.
