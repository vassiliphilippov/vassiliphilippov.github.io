heehaw

noun
donkey's bray
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhiˌhɔ / (AmE), / ˈhiːˌhɔː / (BrE)
the loud, braying sound made by a donkey

Examples

Donkey's heehaw
The farmer laughed when he heard the heehaw echo across the fields.
A sudden heehaw startled the children as they walked past the stable.
Every morning, the donkey's loud heehaw woke up the entire village.
