beggar-ticks

noun
barbed seeds of bur marigolds
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛɡər tɪks / (AmE), / ˈbɛɡə tɪks / (BrE)
the barbed seeds of bur marigold plants (genus Bidens) that easily cling to clothing or animal fur

Examples

Beggar-ticks seeds on clothes
After our walk through the field, my socks were covered in beggar-ticks.
Children sometimes call beggar-ticks 'hitchhikers' because they cling to clothing so easily.
The sticky beggar-ticks can be difficult to remove from a dog's fur.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bur - a seed or dry fruit with hooks or teeth
