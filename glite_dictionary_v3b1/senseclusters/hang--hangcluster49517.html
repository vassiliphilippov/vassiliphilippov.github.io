hang

verb
hold on tightly; cling
4.6 2.3
Pronunciation: /hæŋ/ (AmE), /hæŋ/ (BrE)
to hold on tightly or tenaciously to something or someone, especially so as not to let go

Examples

The climber managed to hang from the ledge until help arrived.
