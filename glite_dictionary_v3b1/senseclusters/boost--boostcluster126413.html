boost

noun
an increase in cost or rate
1.1
Pronunciation: / bust / (AmE), / buːst / (BrE)
an increase or improvement in amount, rate, effectiveness, or support; something that helps or encourages

Examples

A boost in sales
Gas companies announced a further boost in prices for the coming winter.
The school fees saw a significant boost this semester.
The workers protested against yet another boost in rent.
A kind word can provide a much-needed boost to someone's morale.
The team needed a boost after their recent losses.
Winning the award gave her a real boost in confidence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discouragement - the act of making someone less confident or optimistic
  • synonym: encouragement - the act of giving someone support, confidence, or hope
  • synonym: increase - an instance of growing larger or greater in amount
  • antonym: reduction - the action or fact of making something smaller; a decrease
  • synonym: support - help or assistance provided to someone
