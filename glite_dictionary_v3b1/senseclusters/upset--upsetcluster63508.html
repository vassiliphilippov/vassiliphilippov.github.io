Examples

  • worried
    1.0 63/m
    worried shows clear anxiety about a specific problem, while upset can mean general distress or being emotionally unsettled.
  • disturbed
    3.8 1.53/m

  • agitated
    4.1 2.20/m

  • perturbed
    6.4 0.04/m
    perturbed sounds more formal and shows mental unease, while upset is common everyday language for being troubled.
  • disquieted
    7.0 0.04/m
    disquieted means quietly uneasy and unsettled inside, while upset is the usual word for feeling worried or distressed.
  • dismayed
    4.3 0.10/m
    dismayed feels shocked and disappointed by an event, while upset is a wider feeling of worry or emotional disturbance.
  • bothered
    1.6 0.00/m

  • unhappy
    1.0 19/m
