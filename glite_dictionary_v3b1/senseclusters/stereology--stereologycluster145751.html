stereology

noun
3D structure analysis
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛriˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌstɪəriˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of three-dimensional structures based on analysis of two-dimensional images or sections

Examples

Stereology study method
Researchers use stereology to estimate the number of neurons in a brain region.
Advances in stereology have improved the accuracy of tissue volume measurements.
The laboratory specializes in stereology for analyzing complex biological structures.
