Synonyms

  • putative
    7.9 2.19/m
    putative is a formal word for something widely thought true without proof, while supposed is more general and neutral.
  • reputed
    5.6 0.00/m
    reputed is used when many people believe someone or something is a certain way, while supposed is less specific.
  • alleged
    4.2 0.14/m
    alleged is used for claims often about wrongdoing and needs proof, while supposed is a more neutral claim.
  • conjectural
    8.2 0.27/m
    conjectural describes ideas formed from little evidence or guesswork, while supposed merely means believed or claimed.
  • so-called
    5.0 35/m
    so-called marks doubt about a name or label and often sounds critical, while supposed refers to belief about a fact.
  • hypothetical
    6.9 8.97/m
    hypothetical presents an imagined or possible situation for thinking or testing, while supposed implies people may actually believe it.
