facepiece

noun
mask covering part
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪspiːs / (AmE), / ˈfeɪspiːs / (BrE)
the part of a mask or respirator that covers and fits over the face

Examples

Protective facepiece
The technician checked the facepiece for any damage before using the protective equipment.
A proper seal between the facepiece and the skin is essential for safety.
He replaced the old facepiece on his mask to ensure a better fit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mask - a covering for all or part of the face
  • synonym: respirator - a device worn over the mouth and nose to protect from inhaling harmful substances
