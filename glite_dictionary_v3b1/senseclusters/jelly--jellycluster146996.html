jelly

noun
gelatin-based dessert or coating
1.0 1.55/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆdÊ’É›li / (AmE), / ËˆdÊ’É›li / (BrE)
A soft, semifluid, often transparent food made with gelatin and flavoured with fruit juice or other ingredients, eaten as a dessert or used as a glaze.

Examples

Fruit jelly dessert
For the party, they made colorful fruit jelly in fun shapes.
She served strawberry jelly with whipped cream for dessert.
The chicken was coated with a savory jelly glaze before roasting.

Synonyms

  • gelatin dessert
    2.8 0.01/m
    a gelatin dessert is a formal, set sweet made from dissolved gelatin, while jelly is used as a dessert or glaze.
  • jello
    1.0 0.44/m
    jello is the informal, common name for a fruit-flavored, chilled gelatin dessert, while jelly can mean dessert or glaze.
  • Jell-O
    2.8 1.08/m
    Jell-O is a brand name for powdered, fruit-flavored gelatin sold commercially, while jelly is a general food term.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jam - a sweet spread made from fruit and sugar, often confused with jelly
