slob

noun
messy or obnoxious person
3.1 1.71/m
Pronunciation: / slÉ‘b / (AmE), / slÉ’b / (BrE)
a person who is messy, untidy, or careless about their appearance or habits

Examples

A slob person
After the party, the house looked like a group of slobs had moved in.
He can be such a slob when it comes to keeping his room tidy.
She refused to date him because he was a total slob about his appearance.
Don't be a slob; clean up after yourself once in a while.
Even though she works hard, her desk makes her look like a total slob.
He's such a slob that his room is always a mess and he never puts things away.

Synonyms

  • sloven
    9.6 0.01/m
    sloven describes someone habitually dirty and untidy in daily life, while slob is a more casual, insulting word for a messy person.
  • schlub
    9.9 0.30/m
    schlub looks lazy, socially awkward, and a bit unkempt, while slob mainly means a person who is untidy and messy.

Related Terms
