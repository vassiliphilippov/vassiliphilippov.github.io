seminal duct

noun
tube carrying sperm from testis
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmɪnəl dʌkt / (AmE), / ˈsɛmɪnəl dʌkt / (BrE)
the tube that carries sperm from the testis in males; also called the vas deferens or ductus deferens

Examples

The seminal duct tube
Blockage of the seminal duct can lead to infertility in men.
The seminal duct is essential for the transportation of sperm from the testes.
The surgeon carefully repaired the damaged seminal duct during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vas deferens - the main duct conveying sperm from the testicle to the urethra
